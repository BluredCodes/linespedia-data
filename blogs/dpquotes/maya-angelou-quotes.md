30+ Best Maya Angelou Quotes to Inspire Strength &amp; Confidence

## 
Maya Angelou (1928–2014) remains one of the most beloved voices of wisdom, dignity, moral depth, and human resilience. Her writings, poetry, and speeches continue to inspire millions across cultures, faiths, and generations—including readers who value kindness, integrity, humility, compassion, and purpose-driven living. Because her teachings focus on universal values such as courage, gratitude, patience, empathy, forgiveness, and personal growth, her quotes resonate with people seeking uplifting guidance that aligns with moral and value-based principles.

Throughout her remarkable life, she overcame adversity with grace and turned her pain into poetry that uplifted generations. Her work celebrates resilience, hope, and the beauty of being unapologetically yourself. From literature to public speaking, Maya Angelou became a global symbol of strength, compassion, and wisdom.

Her timeless words remind us to rise above fear, embrace kindness, and live with purpose — lessons that remain as powerful today as ever.
