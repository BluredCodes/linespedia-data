## What Makes a Quote Truly “Deep”?
In a world full of “toxic positivity” and cliché slogans, a truly deep quote does three things differently:

- It Honors the Struggle: It doesn’t tell you to “just be happy.” It acknowledges that growth is usually uncomfortable.
- It Rewards Reflection: You might read it once and get the gist, but when you read it again three months later, it feels different because you are different.
- It Connects the Personal to the Universal: It reminds you that the “unique” fear or sadness you’re feeling has been felt by millions of people throughout history. You aren’t alone in it.
