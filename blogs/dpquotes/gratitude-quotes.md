Why Gratitude Improves Your Life (Scientifically Proven Benefits)

Practicing gratitude isn’t just a feel-good habit — research from leading psychology and wellness institutes in the USA shows that it creates measurable improvements in:

✔️ Mental health

✔️ Physical health

✔️ Relationships

Gratitude boosts dopamine and serotonin — the “feel-good hormones.”

It helps you stay strong during difficult times and shift focus toward hope.
