American poet, memoirist, and civil rights activist. Her writings on courage, love, and human dignity are celebrated globally.

Rumi (1207–1273)

Persian poet, jurist, and spiritual scholar. His timeless wisdom touches themes of love, gratitude, humility, and spiritual reflection.

William Shakespeare (1564–1616)

English playwright and poet often considered the greatest writer in the English language. His works include profound insights on human emotions and relationships.

Ralph Waldo Emerson (1803–1882)

American essayist and philosopher known for his teachings on self-reliance, nature, and personal growth.

Marcus Aurelius (121–180)

Seneca (4 BC–65 AD)

Leo Tolstoy (1828–1910)
