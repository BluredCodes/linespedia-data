title: "Love’s not a word"
writer: "Hanger"

Love’s not a word
Or a state of mind
Love is a feeling you can’t leave behind
I Look in her eyes what can I see
Two eyes of loving look back at me
A tingling kiss of lips so sweet
The feeling two souls in the middle meet
Warmth and tenderness in her embrace
A smile of contentment there on her face
A stroll on the beach holding hands
No time to notice the shifting sands
Out to dinner with a glass of fine wine
Others are envious of your hand in mine
Home for the evening the music turned low
We dance close together ever so slow
Where next the mood takes us who can say
A night of sweet magic rolling in hay
kissing and cuddling right through the night
Candles burned low as the darkness they fight
Sleep close together content with our love
We wake simultaneously to smiles from above
For the angels are pleased with what they have seen
I am glad that this time it wasn’t a dream
