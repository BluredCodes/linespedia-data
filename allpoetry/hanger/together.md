---
title: "Together"
writer: "Hanger"
slug: "together"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8826-Together-by-Hanger"
---

Together there’s love been there from the start 
Together there’s harmony beating in my heart
Together there’s understanding from words never spoken 
Together there’s life filled with pure emotion
Together there’s tranquility swimming in my head
Together there’s a whirlwind flying through my bed
Together there’s forgiveness if we made a mistake
Together there’s heart filled joy no need to fake
Together there’s two souls joined as one
Together there’s joyful play and much more fun
Together there’s beauty in sunsets every night
Together there’s happiness never need to fight
Together there’s song and dance
Together there’s sweet romance
