---
title: "Your Love and Mine"
writer: "Hanger"
slug: "your-love-and-mine"

Inside beats love
Here in my heart
Perhaps it's been there
Right from the start
Our friends said they saw it
Such a long time ago
It took quite some time
Till I saw the rainbow
My love now growing
From strength to strength
I would be near you
Go to any length
Sometimes saying nothing
Just holding your gaze
The vision is clear 
Through all of the haze
Talking for hours
Till the moon starts to fall
Lips silently speak
With a gesturing call
Desperate passion
Begging time to stand still
Our bodies so close
Reach emotional thrill
Drained and exhausted
Yet not satisfied
Hearts pounding together
Silently confide
This is a love
Built on friendship and trust
Stronger than steel
This love never shall rust
Held here in my heart
For the rest of time
Truth and knowledge
Your love and mine
