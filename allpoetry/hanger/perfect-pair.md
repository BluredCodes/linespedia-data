---
title: "Perfect Pair"
writer: "Hanger"
slug: "perfect-pair"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

When I hold you in my arms so tight
A loving sensation that feels just right
My skin alive and tingling me
Senses aroused try to get free 
This feeling like a magical potion
The warmth and trust of a loving devotion 
Body trembling, though I'm not cold
Feverish passion burns uncontrolled
Tracing the curves of your face so light
My love again at an all-new height
Our eyes a flame with love and desire
I swear I can hear an angelic choir
Then pressing your lips gently to mine
Surely I'm dreaming it feels so fine
Running my fingers through your silken hair
All can see we're a perfect pair
