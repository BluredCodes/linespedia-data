---
title: "Someone Special"
writer: "Hanger"

Now I’ll try to describe herThough I know it won’t be easyAlready as I start to thinkMy stomach goes all queasyFor me the most impressive thingThose eyes of emerald greenThey glisten and they sparkleHer love through them I’ve seenI love her perky little lipsThat cheeky little grinIf I was not to kiss themYou’d be witnessing a sinHer hands so soft and gentleAs they intertwine with mineHer silk touch makes me tingleSends a shiver down my spineHer body so curvaceousShe always looks so smartJust one more of the reasonsShe’s imbedded in my heartHer minds as fast as lightningShe could cut me down real quickShe could not do it betterIf she hit me with a stickIf I am being moody Or just a tad unfairIn an instant she can change my moodWith just a certain stareShe knows what I am feelingWithout me uttering a soundYet we could talk for hoursShe’s the best friend I have foundTo me she is so perfectI would not change a thingIt is her I always think ofShe’s the one makes my heart singI’ve tried here to describe herSo that all of you can seeOne day I hope you all can findSomeone special, just like me.
