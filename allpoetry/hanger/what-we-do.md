---
title: "What We Do"
writer: "Hanger"
slug: "what-we-do"
source: "AllPoetry"

The smile on your face 
Makes this such a happy place
The sparkle in your eye
Is so much brighter than the sky

This is what I see
When you're looking at me

The happiness inside
Is what I cannot hide
My hearts rapid beat
Makes me fidget in my seat

This is what I do 
When I look at you

The glow in your cheek
As you steal a little peek
A cheeky little grin
Cause you know you always win

This is what I see
When you're looking at me

I fiddle & I wriggle
I squirm & I squiggle
I have a happy face
You know I always pace

This is what I do 
When I look at you
