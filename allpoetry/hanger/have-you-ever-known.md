---
title: "Have you ever known"
writer: "Hanger"
slug: "have-you-ever-known"
source: "AllPoetry"

Have you ever known a loving kiss
Held wonder from a state of bliss
Enjoyed a moment soft and true
Stood in awe of a sky so blue

Have you ever known a loving heart
Felt true love that will never depart
Watched the sun rise over the sea
Skipped trough a park in a moment of glee

Have you ever known a love so pure
Beheld a smile with a wondrous lure
Been Held transfixed by a bright starry night
Cuddled for hours in full moon light

Have you ever known loves sweet embrace
Looked at your love and felt your heart race
Tasted sweetness from a pair of lips
Witnessed beauty surpassing, a lunar eclipse

Though I have no money and some say I’m poor
My lady has given me all this and more
I hold countless riches beyond compare
I have found a love so true and rare
