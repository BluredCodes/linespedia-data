---
title: "A Lovers Prayer"
writer: "Hanger"
slug: "a-lovers-prayer"

Just a moment spent with you
Used to be enough
A glimpse of your eyes
Enough to satisfy
Yet now my soul grows hungry 
My heart yearns to beat with yours
Skin that longs to touch you
Lips screaming out for more
It’s bad enough I want you
Knowing you want me too
To have a little taste of you
Then have to let you go
Dreams and nightmares fill my head
Yearning desires of love divine
Passion setting blood a boil
Lonely sunsets watched in thought
Crashing waves call out your name
Emotions twisted and confused
Souls linked in need like sun and moon
I pray we’ll be together soon
