My life is so confusing.
Lonely not amusing.
In love and still alone.
My happiness a phone.

Resting with her decision.
Deliberate with precision.
Now patiently I wait.
For she controls my fate.

With every twist and turn.
My heart and body yearn.
To hold her snug and tight.
My emotions struggle and fight.

In her eyes I see the sorrow.
My strength she can only borrow.
A family full of strain.
Yet still she must remain.

If I could find away.
I’d stop this pain today.
Then she could rest and smile.
Guess I’ll have to wait awhile.
