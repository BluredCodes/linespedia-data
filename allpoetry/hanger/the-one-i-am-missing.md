---
title: "The One I am Missing"
writer: "Hanger"
slug: "the-one-i-am-missing"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/9086-The-One-I-am-Missing-by-Hanger"
---

I stand by a lighthouse on a cliff by the sea.
All that is missing is you here with me.

Through the door to the stable as I gaze at a horse.
I ponder our lives as they plot out their course.

I see the young people running free on the beach.
I wish you were here within my arms reach.

Under the stars on a night of full moon.
I long to be with you I hope it is soon.

I was thinking of you as I entered a shop.
My heart started to gallop with a clippity clop.

I drove up the mountain through the mist and the rain.
I miss you so much that my heart starts to pain.

When I dream of you as I sleep late at night.
I want you I need you to hold you so tight.

So I picture your face and your warm tender smile.
I know that I'll see you in just a short while.

When you say that you love me my pulse starts to race.
I know that you notice the smile on my face.

So till we're together hugging and kissing.
Remember that YOU are the one I am missing.
