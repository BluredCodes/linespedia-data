---
title: "Two Souls"
writer: "Hanger"
slug: "two-souls"

Walking, Talking
Gazing, Praising
Listening, Hearing
Caring, Sharing
Yearning, Desiring
Needing, Heeding
Taking, Giving
Living, Forgiving
Helping, Understanding
Passionate Feeling
Just some of the things we share together
Like mist on the locks near Scottish heather
Neither one able to survive on its own
Sometimes were together other times we just phone
Near or far we are never apart
Two souls joined together as one loving heart
