---
title: "Red Rose"
writer: "Hanger"
slug: "red-rose"
source: "AllPoetry"

A single Red rose should say it all.
I love you,I need you,
Help me stand tall.
If you've been Good or you've been bad.
Or maybe if you're feeling sad.
I'll give this little gift to you.
And tell you that I love you too.
