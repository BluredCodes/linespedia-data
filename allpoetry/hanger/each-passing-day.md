---
title: "Each Passing Day"
writer: "Hanger"
slug: "each-passing-day"
source: "AllPoetry"

Eyes that show the world just who I am 
A mouth that lets all know that I can feel 
Arms designed to hold you, so very close to me 
A Beating chest, a heart for only you 
 
Within the shadows of that heart there lies a child 
Frightened of all the things that lie ahead 
Through the doorway to my soul a secret a mirror 
Reflections of a past torment and pain 
 
Stairs spiral from within an inner chamber 
To the essence flowing round within my veins 
That which makes me who I am 
What I think, what I believe 
 
Feelings deep and strong with love that forms a song 
I can't explain how loved you make me feel 
Passion overflows whenever you are near 
Silent tears flow every time you leave 
 
Freindship from the time when we first met 
Grew and blossmed into love with just one kiss 
Fingers tingle, with your touch so soft and gentle 
Your smile that warms me, even when I'm wrong 
 
Two hearts formed and forged by the same mould 
Finally meeting and realising where they both belong 
For saftey with each other to bond forever together 
I will always love you more with each passing day
