Can you kill a person, who has lost their soul?
Can love be freely given, if you’re drowning in a hole?
Can a friend reach out a hand, if your mad dog growls?
Can a child contain its fright, while a lone wolf howls?

Can faith be regained, when you lose a child?
Can you stop emotions, when you’re feeling wild?
Can anyone write poetry, if they're feeling numb?
Can you pinch yourself, if you have no thumb?

Can the earth evolve, without the human brain?
Can water clear the streets, without a drain?
Can mankind survive, if we cant find peace
Can you make a woollen jumper, if you have no fleece?

Can my heart continue beating, without your love?
Can a magician pull from a hat, a pristine white dove?
Can anyone make sense, of what I write today?
Can I have back my youth, and dreams of yesterday?
