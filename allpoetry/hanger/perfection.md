---
title: "Perfection"
writer: "Hanger"
slug: "perfection"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/9793-Perfection-by-Hanger"
---

If we always told the truthOr we never were aloofPut our rubbish in the binNever ever did we sinIf we never had a fightLoved each other more each nightIf we never went to warAnd never ever sworeIf we grin and give a smileHelp each other for a whileTell our mother that we love herSwap all knowledge we discoverShare a moment of our timeThen this world would work out fine.
