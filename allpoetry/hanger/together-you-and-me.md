---
title: "Together You and Me"
writer: "Hanger"

You lay on my bedPillow cradles your headGazing deep in your eyesFeelings cannot disguiseNo need for a wordAll is silently heardLips softly meetingFeel pulses beatingPassionately kissed Emotions not missedOn the nape of your neckSuch a gentle peckSafe from all harmsHeld close in your armsLove and romance our needPure passion we feedOur sweats now combineSweet salt tastes so fineHearts thumping in tuneRising heat fills the roomThe touch of your skinSends my head in a spinWith a need now dispersedFeelings never reversedPassion slowly unwindsThe clock harshly remindsIt's time you must leaveFrom our chest sighs heaveThough my heart feels great sorrowI thank heaven for tomorrowWhen again we can beTogether You and Me
