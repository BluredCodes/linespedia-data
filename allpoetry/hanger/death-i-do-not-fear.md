---
title: "Death I do not fear"
writer: "Hanger"
slug: "death-i-do-not-fear"
source: "AllPoetry"

Whisper to me now
Sweet nothings in my ear
Sing for me now
For words I cannot hear

Paint for me now
With oils in colours hue
Describe them too me clearly
For visions I cannot view

Caress me now
With fingers soft and tender
Caring for me always
For feelings I cannot render

My mind is glazed with darkness
For my time is surely near
Yet cry for me not
For death I do not fearThese are thoughts I had in a very weird dream.
