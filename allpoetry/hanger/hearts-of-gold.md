Within each chest a heart of gold
Beating steady strong and true
Take trust away then behold
The fabric of a soul

Emotions boiling spilling free
Words spoken never meant
Cruel in painful reality
Yet the knife drives deeper still

Sunrays fail to warm ones blood
Like loves own boiling cauldron
From deep within adrenalin floods
Minds spark with instant fusion

A personal toll taken with each fight
Stripping away to the core
No time for strength to give respite
Hidden distant behind each lie

Only then will truth be fully revealed
How strong will this love be?
Can it survive within its shield?
More of this misery

More lies will foster further doubt
Until the dam walls break
Then finally the mouth will shout
Enough of this for meA little different from what I normally write. But I thought I would try something new.
