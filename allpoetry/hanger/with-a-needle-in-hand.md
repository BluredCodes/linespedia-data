title: "With a needle in hand"

With a needle in hand
Without Reprimand
Time to get high
Time to fly
Search for a vein
Feel no pain
Stick it inside
No need to hide
Society tolerates all that they do
All you have they’ll steal from you
To pay for a habit stricken with death
If it’s someone you know best hold your breath
They take their friends and family along
For a painful ride lets hope were strong
For the Families misery stops just round the bend
When this life full of trouble suddenly ends
The cost not in dollars and can’t measure so
It’s all our emotions taking a blow
The love ones that tragedy leaves behind
If you ever feel the urge should remind
With a needle in hand
A strong Reprimand
Not the way to get high
Love lets you fly
