---
title: "What Else Matters"
writer: "Hanger"
slug: "what-else-matters"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8842-What-Else-Matters-by-Hanger"
---

Tears and sobs
Silence shatters
Mind so cloudy
Heart in tatters
Comforting words
Mumbling chatters
Sound advice
Cohesion scatters
All alone
Footstep patters
Loving kisses
Blushingly flatters
Taking risks
Life clatters
Time to go
Emotion splatters
Love so strong
What else matters
