title: "Swept Off My Feet"
writer: "Hanger"

We went for a walk … you and I
On a day without a cloud in the sky
I needed to tell you a secret I kept
An emotion so raw that away I was swept

We walked round the block, for nearly an hour 
We walked thru the park, past the shop with the flower
We talked about work and all manner of things
Yet still how to tell you my mind would not bring

Then you asked me what I want to say?
I wanted to hide for the rest of the day
My pulse raced, my throat went dry
Internally I wanted to cry

A fear of rejection or a slap in the face
My heart was pounding and I quickened the pace
Then I took a deep breath and started to speak
I told you I loved you as my fear reached its peak

I dared not to look as the moments went bye
When you said you loved me I let out a sigh
With a feeling like just winning a prize
I looked at you and saw love in your eyes

Then we walked in silence the rest of the way
Neither one knowing quite what to say
How could we known what was to follow
A love that just grew like the birth of a swallow

But obstacles here in our path right away
I struggle with life need to see you each day
For our lives intertwine like a climbing rose
The thorns a reminder not to get to close

But for now I will love you and wait by your side
From the view of others you know I must hide
So a kiss tides me over until we next meet
When again I know I’ll be swept off my feet
