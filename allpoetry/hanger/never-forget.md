---
title: "Never Forget"
writer: "Hanger"
slug: "never-forget"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8671-Never-Forget-by-Hanger"
---

There's a smile on my faceThinking of another placeBuilt just for you and meFull of happiness and gleeNo argument or fight hereJust loving care for you dearSuch wonders you can seeOur most blissful place to beBlue lagoon with a water slideTwo tire swings for us to rideBetween giant palms a hammock liesOur place for rest, no prying eyesA parquetry dance floor in here tooCandle light, music, slow dancing with youCheek to cheek such grace on our feetA tango, a waltz, just go with the beatHours we spend in such sweet caressNo time to worry no time for stressAlas now time to leave this placeLet it fade away without a traceBack to the lives that we normally leadTill next I come get you atop my black steedPlease don't worry do not fretFor this we will never ever forget
