---
title: "We all took flight"
writer: "Hanger"

Child hood memories 
Nights of fear
Mothers screaming
Ring still in my ear

Bruised black and blue
From head to toe
No where to run
Wish he would go

Shouting yelling
Smashing glass
Now she’s crying
Time will not pass

Now my turn
To feel the heat
Leather strap
On my backside beats

Must be tough 
Big strong man
Inflicting pain 
Because he can

Sad and lonely
Now his plight
Stupid old man
We all took flight
