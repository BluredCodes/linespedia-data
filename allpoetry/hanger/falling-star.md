---
title: "Falling Star"
writer: "Hanger"
slug: "falling-star"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Shouting yelling accusing abusingYou make his life completeYou………….. Never get it rightYou………….. Cause every fightWhy do you stop and waitTime to seal his fateYou gave him every chanceNo time for another glanceYou bury your true feelingHe sends your emotions reelingNo matter where you hideHe still hurts you deep insideI hate the way it makes me feelI’ll make it right your love I’ll stealYou say you need to do what’s rightYet still you stifle every fightHe screams and shouts upsets the kidsThe house will smile when of him it ridsI should not feel the way I doBut it’s because I love you trueAnd when at last you see the light Show him the door not another fightI’ll be there to comfort youThen you will know what you went throughYour friends can see a falling starThey notice it even from a farThey know what he puts you throughPlease don’t let your heart be blueToo your own self time to be trueTell him now his lot is thruEnjoy your life, as now you shouldWe all would help if we only couldYet this decision you must makeTime to go and seal his fate
