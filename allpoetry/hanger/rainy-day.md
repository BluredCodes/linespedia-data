title: "Rainy Day"
writer: "Hanger"

If I had my way on a rainy day 
We'd be soaking wet with no dismay

We'd walk together hand in hand
Bare of foot through grass and sand

Sing a song together out of key
As long as you are here with me

That smile I witness in your eye
Makes all my troubles whistle bye

If I had my way on a rainy day 
I'd kiss you sweetly if you would stay

We'd be dancing together cheek to cheek
Without a care everyday of the week

Watch the world go bye, so carefree
That's how it is, as it's meant to be

Massage your neck take the tension away
That's how I see things everyday

If I had my way on a rainy day
I'd simply love you in everyway
