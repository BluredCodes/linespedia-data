---
title: "Secrets"
writer: "Hanger"
slug: "secrets"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Secrets locked inside your heart
Love for another, kept in the dark
No one to talk to, no one can know
Yet still your love for me will show

Eyes betray the way you feel
Fleeting moments, your heart must steal
Torn between love and a moral dilemma
Love will still cause, your heart to tremor

In your heart the conflicts rise
When you see the pain in my eyes
Only a few, know my love for you
You keep from others this secret too

Still I am here to comfort you
Whenever your heart is feeling blue
When tears start to well and run down your face
Head on my shoulder, your resting place

Here I can hold you, show you I care
Relieve you of pain, that shouldn’t be there
Returning with pleasure a smile to your face
Looking and witnessing beauty and grace

With you in my arms nothing else is around
Nothing to hear but my own heart pound
Secure in the knowledge, My Love for You
Eternally lasting strong and true.
