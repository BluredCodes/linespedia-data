---
title: "An Angel Perfect as a Dove."
writer: "Hanger"

I know my heart it cannot lie.
You know with you I'm never shy.
You are the sparkle in my eye.
My guiding star up in the sky.

When I see your smiling face.
It lifts me to a happy place.
My heart it pounds and starts to race.
My love it grows at a rapid pace.

The taste of your lipstick oh so sweet.
Whenever our two lips do meet.
I get a tingle through to my feet.
My heart again it skips a beat.

Now it comes the time to part.
A shallow stillness fills my heart.
My mind begins to flit and dart.
Until the morning again will start.

When morning comes I am not glum.
I start to sing I start to hum.
For soon I know I'll see my love.
An angel perfect as a dove.
