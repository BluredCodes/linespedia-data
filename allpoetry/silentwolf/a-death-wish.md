A beautiful sunset stays in view
The orange, yellow, and pinkish hue
You sit alone on the sandy shore
Wishing for the days before
The angels sing from up above
Showering you with feelings of love
Your health is fading day by day
Only doctors can find a way
Death seems closer with each day
You know not much longer on earth will you stay
Sleep is now a thing of the past
You want to make your final days last
They’re not quite sure what’s causing you to be ill
They’ve already tried every pill
You would rather have death come to you
Then sit in pain when the days are new
You hear the children playing outside
How you long to be there with them to guide
You understand how god has reason in his way
But how you wish you could just stay
And be healthy again, searching for reason
Watching the snow fall with the new season
You warp yourself with your coat snug
And walk off at the pace of a slug
Enjoying Earth’s beauty while you’re still here
Somehow brings peace and cheer
How you wish you could tell everyone in this world
Come out of your homes, don’t lay there all curled
When your hiding yourself within your home
You can’t see the sunshine and miracles shown
Every day is a gift, you’ve been shown
If only earlier you would have known
But now as you walk down the street
All the people you have yet to meet
To tell them your sorrowful story
Hoping to teach, not make them feel sorry
Perhaps the message you wish most to go on
