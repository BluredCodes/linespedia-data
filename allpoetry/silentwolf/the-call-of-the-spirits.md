Inside a soul the black wolf ran
Completely untouchable by that of man
With paws swiftly gliding across the snow
Nowhere in particular did this wolf have to go
Just running with the wind so swift
Mother earth showing her beautiful gift
The spirits howled in the midnight air
Knowing the captor was running down there
As the wind hit her fur she howled out loud
Into the night and among the clouds
The moon was so full and covered with lust
The stars seemed brighter than golden dust
Kicking up snow her spirits were high
Side by side with spirits she seemed to fly
Eyes wide open to take in the sight
Of the grandest most beautiful midnight
The trees seemed to bow in awe of the beauty
The snow cleared a path through which she could flee
Climbing a hill with spirits so fast
The future now seemed a thing of the past
Beyond wisdom and beauty lies the soul
And at least once in life you must answer the call
The call of the wild, the call of the earth
The call of Mother Nature each time she gives birth
And once you have answered the wilderness call
You will find the truth in one and all
