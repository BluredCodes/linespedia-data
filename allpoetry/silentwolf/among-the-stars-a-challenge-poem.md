While we live our lives here on earth 
Out in space there is a grand birth 
A hatchie slowly peeks out of his shell 
There's fire all over, but this is no hell 
His first feast of his life is the goo that surrounds him 
This is the way it is, and always has been 
His two tiny wings are not of much use 
But soon the young dragon will with them find muse 
His meeps loudly echo around cavern walls 
Outside it is heard, mother and father return calls 
Meeping happily at the sight of them he trembles with glee 
They help him out of the shell knowing he is the key 
He scatters about as his parents stay close 
This would be the one who is treasured the most 
All the dragons hear a loud horn 
And all of them know the new prince has been born 
They gather around, the little one cries 
He hides behind his parents and fear fills his eyes 
His mother nudges him out from beneath her 
All around the dragons know him for sure 
This tiny silver dragon has the mark of the one 
For upon his scaled hide is a bright golden sun 
He yawns a small yawn, curls up tiredly 
To a baby dragon the biggest struggle is getting free 
His belly is full and he shall be watched over 
For they know the young prince will be their lucky clover 
So Clover he was dubbed, as the dragons flew free 
Around the hidden planet that no earthling could see 
They knew that soon enough their time would come 
When they would join the people of earth again as one 
The knights may have killed many, but they never could destroy 
The power and might of one little boy 
Who upon his own doing took a dragon's egg home 
Which he nurtured and cared for until it had flown 
Then looking at the sky, knowing the safest place 
He told it to fly at a very fast pace 
Up into the sky where it could live a full life 
Not having to worry about being slain with a knight's knife 
Then as the dragon produced a golden ray 
More dragons came and held safe in it's bay 
The river of stars shone brightly above 
And the dragon never forgot the boy's love 
The young prince dragon must go and make haste 
To where the great grandson of the boy was placed 
There he would find the key to peace 
And man would no longer call dragons a beast 
So right now as earth goes about, and the pure water gleams 
The young dragon, Clover flies soundly in dreamsThis is a challenge poem I have written today.  I don't know extreame information on Dragons, but I am slowly learning.  Hope you like it :)
