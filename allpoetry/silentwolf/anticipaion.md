Another day passes by
Not fast enough does time fly
But patient I must be
For soon it is you I will see
I watch the clock slowly tick
It seemingly moves backwards, an awful trick
Anticipation is a mistake
For it seems the clock will forsake
But still I wait for you my love
You bring peace, much like a dove
As time grows closer to the day
For your safe trip I pray
I just can’t wait to see you
You make my life feel so new
I hope you understand
How happy you made me by asking for my hand
I can’t wait for teen years to be done
So that by marriage we are one
I’ll love you until my last breath
For my breath itself is what you take away
