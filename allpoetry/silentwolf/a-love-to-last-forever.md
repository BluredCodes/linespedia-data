title: "A Love to Last Forever"

Strife, heartache, misery
All these come with being lonely
Wishing for the time when you felt whole
Instead of putting happiness on hold
Waiting for the moment when no longer will you be alone
Waiting for the day when you'll finally feel at home
Slumber doesn't come easy to souls apart
Being alone leaves you with but half a heart
The darkest hours of the night only seem worse
Loneliness is much worse than a curse
Wanting to feel for once in place
Wanting to feel angelic wings brush your face
As you fall asleep on fallen feathers of love
With the ONE you love, surely sent from above
Reaching for the day when things will be ok
Reaching for the one with which you will stay
Sharing your heart involves loyalty
While you're apart, faithful you must be
Neglect the rules and prepare for hurt
And you can't blame someone else for you being a flirt
Stay true to the one you love, never forget
For if you do, you'll never regret
In time spent together every day
Make sure to show your love in some way
For I have grown to appreciate time
When I am his and he is mine
It's not very often we are together
But every time we are, I wish it to last forever
Sometimes love hurts, but if you believe
You will find one who will never make you grieve
If you chance upon one whom is true and kind
Realize that this is one great find
Share your emotions, your joys and your love
And you will be blessed from the angels above
