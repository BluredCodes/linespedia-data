Angel

Darkness forms from deep within
Life now seems so sad and grim
Memories from a past forgot
You try not to remember, but you cannot
A scream echoes in your ears
Your face is stung with silent tears
Whatever heart inside you flees
You whimper out ‘just kill me, please!’
The midnight air rushes in
Your soul trembles, your skin so thin
You lay on the floor asking yourself why
But it only gives you more reason to cry
You’ll never fully forgive yourself
For memories you put on back of the shelf
The dark night sky is black and blue
It feels your soul is the same dark hue
Inside you cry out in anger
But once again the pain grows stronger
The cold and lonely night wages on
Seemingly forever away is dawn
The scars on your heart are so damn deep
And all you can do is lay there and weep
It seems in this world you are all alone
The majestic darkness your only home
You hear a noise that drowns your tears
Strong steps now awaken your fears
You lift your head with tear blurred eyes
Your vision finds a shocking surprise
A dark figure steps in the door
And picks you up off the cold hard floor
He holds you safe within his grasp
Between your tears you take air in gasps
You cry so hard burying your face
He holds you tighter as you hear his heart race
He pets your hair and rubs your back
You feel your heart and soul come back
He still holds you safely in his arms
And whispers softly, his voice like charms
“Its ok, I’d never leave
I’m sorry I died, but please don’t grieve
As long as you’re here I will stay
All I ask is that for me you pray
I love you so much, I feel your pain
I didn’t want to make life for you a strain”
You sob quietly sighing
But now you hear that he is crying
After all that happened he loves you still
Quietly he walks with you on his arms to sit on the window sill
As you look outside with a slight frown
Angelic wings so soft with down
Brush your face wiping your tears
Releasing each and every one of your fears
The beautiful sun begins to peek out
The dark figure still sits about
He wraps you safe within his wings
Then softly he sings
The same song from years before
Suddenly you’re not alone anymore
You look at him and he smiles at you
Tears pelting the air then vanishing through
He disappears and you sit there
A single white feather at which you stare
You feel his presence within the room
The angel of your fallen groom
You pick yourself up and brush off the dirt
Looking around your senses alert
‘it’s a new day’ you say as you walk out the door
