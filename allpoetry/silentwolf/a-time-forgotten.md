title: "A Time Forgotten"

A time forgotten long ago
When forests would flourish and pure water flow
Creatures lived that man has killed
A time of now forever lost skill
Land untouched no barren land
No fire broke out by human hand
Animals lived in peace and harmony
Back then all of them were free
The wolves roamed lands not to fear
That death by shotgun would be near
There weren't fur coats or traps set
Because nobody had thought cruelty up yet
The rolling hills so soft and green
Reflections of sunlight shone off clear streams
The wind would whistle through the trees
Sending about the wonderfully cool breeze
The skies were blue with puffy clouds flying
Everything was alive and nothing was dying
The springtime rains were warm and pure
If you were sick that was the cure
The power to heal was in the earth
And bestowed upon creatures at their birth
The now barren lands were filled with flowers
The earth itself held immaculate powers
In the night stars filled the skies
Everywhere one could hear the wolves' and coyotes' cries
Oh how I wish I could go back to that time
When my true wolf form was once mine
And bask in the glow of the harvest moon
Sleeping well past the afternoon
Time didn't matter, there was no stress
All around was happiness
But the time has been forgotten by people today
Except in my heart where it shall stayI tend to go back to this time in my mind when I'm feeling stressed about the day.  Oh how I wish it was like that again where you didn't have to worry about school or work and didn't have to wake up at five in the morning just to get ready to go to someplace where they don't even care.  *sigh*...
