Sometimes time will go to fast
Leaving hopes back in the past
Sometimes time will go to slow
Having tears painstakingly flow
Time can be happy, time can be sad
Time can be good, time can be bad

Contradiction of time, what a pain
Nothing to lose, nothing to gain
Time is confusing sometimes too
You can't figure out just what to do
Time can be sweet, time can be sour
Time can go by in a flash, or seconds can last for hours

Time plays game throughout our life
Sometimes we're well off, others we strife
When we're not watching, time can fly
But not if you're watching the second tick by
Time can glide, time can stall
Time can mean everything, or nothing at all

Contradiction of time, oh how confusing
Are we winning, or are we losing
How can we appreciate time on our hands
When it goes so slow or ruins our plans
Time can be slow, or fast we may find
