title: "Captor of the Storm"
writer: "SilentWolf"

Wind whistles through the night
Calling spirits all around
Snow flies through the ebony sky
Gathering in mounds around a dark figure
Curled up in the snow against the wailing wind
A wolf lies, bright eyes glowing
It stands slowly anchoring it's paws firmly
Looking out over the land that is now covered
The forest seems desolate and quiet 
As all inhabitants seek shelter from the storm
All the powers of nature gather together
Inpowering the spirit of the wind
Sending more snow flying through the air
The wolf stands unmoving, not a muscle twitches
Winds swirl around it's face
It's eyes remain open, unblinking
A rage hits the storm and sends piles of snow from on trees
Falling to the ground in huge bunches
Not a sound comes from the wolf
Nature's fury grows sending ice shreds through the air
Going in between the fur and stinging the skin 
The wolf still stands, not so much as one hair moves
The wind confused and growing even more angry hurls itself
Into the body and soul of the wolf trying to defeat it
Chilling to the bone the creature that stands
As if a challenge to the mighty wind and snow
Yet he still stands, without a sound undying
The wind gets weary, slows it's pace and the snow falls peacefully
All look out to see what the fight was
The wolf moves, eyes glare into the sky
It releases a howl sending the last snowflakes to the ground
The spirit of the storm throws itself at the mercy
Of the creature who stood against it 
In the darkest hour, in the most painful place
The wolf once again
Is the captor of the storm*laughs* I have captured storms such as this in the past... just a thought
