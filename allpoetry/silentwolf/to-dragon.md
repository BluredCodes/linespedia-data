title: "To Dragon"

Feeling your hand pressed hard against mine
Feeling your lips so soft and sublime
Falling asleep close under the moons night
Waking up next to you in mornings bright light
Knowing you love me and hearing it often
Hearing your voice when you talk to me soften
The scent that belongs only to you
The comfort it gives me when I am blue
The future of us that is so close to being
The knowledge that away from me you shall never go fleeing
Holding you when tears fill your eyes
Hoping it’s not me for which you cry
The trust I have only in you
The promise you give you will always stay true
Looking deep into your eyes
Seeing your soul is more than a prize
The anticipation of seeing you again
Knowing I won’t be alone again
All that you give me in every way
Even the things which for me you have given away
If I could give you everything in a heartbeat I would
I’d promise you joy forever if I could
Leave you, you know I wouldn’t
Love you less I know I couldn’t
I may not have much to offer you
But this I swear is forever true
