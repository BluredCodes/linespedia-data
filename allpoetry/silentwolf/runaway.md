How can you claim you do not feel?
How can you deny what you know is real?
Throw away your thoughts and heart
Scrape up the pieces that have fallen apart 
You try to run, you try to hide
Deep in your soul you know you lied
You’re in love but your afraid
Love always has the price to be paid
Thinking it always ends in hurt
All you have known is for them to be flirts
Too many people have come and gone
Some stay months
And some not long
But you were always hopeful that one would stick around
The one true love you thought you had
Packed up and left with all that they had
Leaving you down on your knees
Crying out the seven seas
Leading you to close your heart
You won’t allow another to tare it apart
And now you deny 
The feeling inside
But you know its true
You need him
And for the first time
He needs youKinda how I felt ... when I met my mate.  *sniffle*
