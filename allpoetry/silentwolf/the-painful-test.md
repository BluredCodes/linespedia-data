I came home early from school today
Because of the pain I could not stay

I shouldn't have gone today at all
But my Drivers Ed class always calls

You need so much time just to pass
So in the classroom I sat eyes of glass

Waiting for the test I had to take to be finished
So I could go home and sleep trying to deminish

This cold that I have that hurts me so bad
My muscles so sore my brain's going mad

I write this not for sympathy
But to my school I wish to plea

Not to set restrictions on days you can miss
For going to school sick is no damn bliss

I had to sit through three classes before
I could finish the test and walk out the door

Now I'm home and tired as #ell
My stomach hurts badly an feels like it swelled

I think for now I am going to rest
Stupid f*cking Driver's Ed test!Well.. at least I got an 89% on the test... not too bad.  Want... sleeep...
