---
title: "I'm Leaving"
writer: "SilentWolf"

I cannot come here anymore for personal reasons.  I apologize for any inconvienance this may cause anyone.  When I say I will not be returning I do mean it, I have to delete this site off of all of my browsers/cpu/etc.  Well *sighs* goodbye everyone. *walks off into the darkness tail swaying behind her*
