A rather odd couple steps into view
A wolf and a dragon, yes it is true
Sometimes people find it funny
But their love they wouldn't trade for all the earth's money
The dragon is white and looks rather strong
The wolf is black with fur soft and long
By looking at these two and knowing their bond
One would know how much of eachother they're fond
The dragon's winter element shows
As he stands by his mate he makes it snow
The wolf and the shadows are as one
They can make day into night faster than the sun
No matter where they go now and forever
You can be sure they'll be seen together
So why, you would say, do these two get along
I say it's easy, if your love is this strongOh come on, I HAD to write something about a wolf and a dragon, otherwise it wouldn't make my poetry page right!
