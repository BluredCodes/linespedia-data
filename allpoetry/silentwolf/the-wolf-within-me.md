A soft wind blows across the lands
Brushing through fur as though it were hands
Of a fallen god from long ago
Bestowed upon the winter snow
The wolf within me cries

The harvest moon shines bright in the sky
Looking for a place to bellow my cry
Gliding swiftly through the trail
The night is short and spirit frail
The wolf within me strives

Finding place beneath a hill
Brothers come forth fresh from kill
All together we are as one
And howl against the midnight sun
The wolf within me sings

The midnight hours fly by fast
The dawn has come and night has past
Moving slowly to my place
Walking with a steped up pace
