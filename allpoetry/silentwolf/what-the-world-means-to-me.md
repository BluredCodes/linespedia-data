Lies distinct
Trust failed
Hope extinct
Body frailed
Happiness gone
Depression stays
Days too long
The game still plays
Love a joke
Hatrid abundant
Red hot pokers poke
Evil is triumphant
Living life
In a pool of lies
Everywhere strife
More hope dies
Sleep dissappear
Tiredness burns
Awaken fear
The world still turns
Pain passes through
Lives are lost
Lives that are new
Will pay the cost
Sadness flows
Through open eyes
And if them you close
Then it'll eat your insides
There's no way out
This world is cruel
Your pain, your tears
Give evil it's fuel
And so it lives on another day
You are scared no doubt
And on your fears it will prey
It whispers "Go ahead sit there, let it allll out"I am currently BEYOND depressed
