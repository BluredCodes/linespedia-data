title: "A Dragon Tear"
writer: "SilentWolf"

Look upon a dragon's face
The long slender muzzle and strong jaw bone
Look at a dragon's frame
Strongly built armor proud pose and leather wings
Look into a dragon's eyes
Glitter swirls in pools of deep beauty
Look deep inside a dragon's soul
Loyalty, spirituality, love, and hurt
Hurt for those who have killed it's kind
Hurt for it can no longer roam free
Look into a dragon's heart
Purity at it's finest, not a speck of evil lingers here
A dragon gives his heart to all who are willing to be accepting
He asks not for a thing in return, but love
Undying, unconditional, and true
We have all denied the dragon it's right
It's right to live free, it's right to survive
And because of that the dragon has left us
Gone to a far away galaxy where people cannot harm it
The price of a dragon's hide was high
And so they went on slaying it
Did they stop for once to look into it's soul
Not one would, for they feared it
They never got a chance to see the true beauty
I have, believe me I have
My mate is a dragon, and there is not a more perfect match
He is loyal, caring, and loving
Just as he was in the past when his true dragon form
Would fly up into the night carrying me safe
And dreams unfold before me in this life
Of nights we spent together under the stars
Our love was unconditional
Yet, people still call dragons a beast
Evil, never pure, and killers
Perhaps if they took the time to look into a dragon's eyes
We would fly with them, hand in paw
Paw in paw, into the night
Soaring above the stars, a glimpse of heaven
Upon fallen hope's regained wings
Look into the dragon's eyes
Peer past his heart and into his soul
You will see tears of parting ways
Because until we can understand and live in peace
A dragon shall fly free of the harm we did unto itDon't know.. miss my mate
