title: "One for the Elements"

The accent of the spirited sky tells me it is time
The gentle breeze upon the land whistles like a chime
I lower myself to the ground and prowl out of the cave
Careful not to wake my mate I stumble and freeze, a close shave
He mumbles and turns over, hugging the shadows I screened around rocks
I did so for in this occasion, when at my door the spirits knock
Walking over snow covered lands in the forest so vast and still
I lower my head and duck a branch, to my ears even the snap of a twig kills
I make no mark in the snow, for the shadows dust it away
And the same for when I shall return in the early day
I leave no scent behind, for it filters through the night
Because in this special time, I control all with new might
My aura seems to shine, my senses know the way
For this is the sacred ground, where my ancestors once stayed
They know of my power, and use it to help me find the spot
The wind blows me in the direction, get lost I cannot
The sky is alive with stars, all of them dressed up so fine
It seems you can see the heavans, between the bright star shine
I walk a little further, then suddenly I see
The river of deep blue purity and the spirited wolf shaped tree
My memories serve me well, for when I was born this was where I stay
I can almost see myself, curled up on a branch during the naptime of the day
I stand before the tree, as it lifts itself to the sky
It faces the bright full moon, as if like a wolf it can cry
I stand myself strong and firm on the sacred ground
In an instant I feel a presance, and turn myself around
Five of the ancient wolves, elements are flared
Earth, Fire, Wind, Water, and Light, all of the ancients are there
Earth the grass' green, Fire the sunlight's red, Wind the bright sky's blue
Water of the deepest river's color, and Light, the purest white hue
All stand before me, I am the one of the shadowy dark
With the deepest black fur, and on my chest the cresent moon's mark
All of them step forth, their eyes a glow with bliss
They shower me with their power, was their something that I missed
Now I remembered, before their time had come
They knew when I grew older, I would be the one
I now held their power, the tattoos of elements on my skin
Under my fur they must lay, for nobody must know I was kin
Then the power hungry creatures, all around the land
Would throw themselves on me, and make my sudden end
They gave me immortality, the greatest gift of all
When I split with my mate, we knew we would never fall
I stood there looking at them, and they looked back into my eyes
Then we all lifted our muzzles, and let out the song to the skies
Slowly then they faded, each adding wisdom to mine
And if I should ever need them, I could find them any time
Just look into the sky, the water, the sun, the dirt, the light
It may seem odd to think this, but they're there alright
With the sun peeking over the forest, and night turning into day
I turned and headed back, my heart knew the way
I was shocked to find when I returned my mate was sitting awake and strong
He confessed and told me, he knew all along
For winter is his power, and beeing one with cold
He confided in the wind, and knowing him as my mate was told
I thought he might be angry, but wasn't in the least
Actually he was proud of me, and on sleep we did feast
With all these powers, carried on through time
