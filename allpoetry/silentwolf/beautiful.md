Let us thank god for another beautiful day
And as we pray
Let us hope that it ain’t gonna be like yesterday
Yesterday (heh)
The day of the dead
The day of the unknown
The day that we dread
Yesterday seems much like a dream
One where you wake up in mid scream
The sweat pours down your frightened face
And your heart beats at a steadfast pace
But this is'nt a dream
There’s no waking up
You can scream all you want
But they don’t give a fuck
How could they understand the pain that you feel
They are too ignorant to see what is real
Oh, its depression, it ain’t nothin new
They give you some pills they think you’ll get through
There is no replacing the loss that you had
How can they sit there and say that you’re mad?
They just don’t care
They always have to have some bullshit notion
You’re pulling out your hair
They’re conjuring a new potion
People are ignorant
They’re in it for the money
They don’t know shit
Cuz for them life’s all honey
I know different
They can’t be goin through what I am
Put me up on a table
See how many pills they can cram
For those whose lives are ‘normal’
This is so you can know
All the pain that we feel
And it only can grow
But you fuck with us through pills
Artificial medications
It gives me the chills
To feel my heart chasin
Racin me for the chance to live
Will I win
Or will my heart give?
Life isn’t fair is too sweet of words
More like life kills
And our souls flee like caged birds
Off to where they can finally have peace
You may think this is really grim
But your medications
