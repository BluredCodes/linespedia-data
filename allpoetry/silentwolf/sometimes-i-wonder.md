If I died would you be sad
If I cried would you get mad
If I stayed would you leave
If I left would you grieve
If I smiled would you frown
If I was sad would it bring you down
Does my life even matter
Is my heart destined to shatter
Do you still care about me
Does my soul set you free
Is my life a disgrace
To you am I just another face
In the crowd passing by
Anymore would I catch your eye
Or would you turn away
Regretting we met that day
Can I make you smile
Am I worth your while
Do I even make you happy anymore
Or more depressed than ever before
This sometimes I wonder
Does it make you ponder
I'm asking your answer truthfully
Do you even... still love me?I wrote this a long time ago.  Don't remember why, perhaps the reason for not remembering is because it was a painful time.  Oh well.
