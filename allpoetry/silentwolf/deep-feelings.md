The feeling of nothingness deep within me
The knowing that you I shall never see
The darkness that lies deep within
The deathly fear that is so grim
The moon up in the midnight sky
The crow that sits and bellows a cry
The park where children no longer play 
The morning hour when night gives into day
The discouraging lies that keep us down
The fluffy clouds that seem to drown
The happy faces all turned sad
The happy people all turned mad
The frightened children in the dark
The morning so frail
The nighttime so dark
The nagging feeling of loneliness
The fear of feeling no more nor less
The laughter drown by crying screams
The once sparkling eyes that no longer gleam
The wanting to sleep forever more
The feeling that death is just outside your door
All this adds up to what I feel
And you can’t say different 
I know that it’s real
But I know one more thing 
And I swear that it’s true
