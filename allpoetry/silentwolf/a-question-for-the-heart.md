title: "A question for the heart"
writer: "SilentWolf"

Have you ever felt a shaking deep inside
Has the one you trust most ever hurt your pride
Has your heart ever been shattered into a million parts
Has it ever been shot by a million darts
Have you ever been shushed when you needed to talk most
Has your mate ever turned into a heartless ghost
Have you given out trust only to recieve pain
Has it ever made you think so hard that it hurt your brain
Has the daylight sky so true and blue
Ever turned into a dark midnight hue
Has your day been taken by someone so low
You sat in the dark letting tears flow
Have you ever let your world fall apart at the seams
Has your beautiful music been drown out with screams
If this is true then theres something you should know
You're not alone, by all means no
Stand up for yourself and always be true
And in the end you will get through
