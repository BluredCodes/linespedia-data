The wind brushes softly against your face
The sense of warmth with your coats embrace
The sun shining brightly on your cheek
The sigh you give so soft and meek
Can you feel me?

The taste of snowflakes on your lips
The comfort of your lover’s kiss
Soothing tea on winters night
Or hot coca that can make things right
Can you taste me?

Aromatic candles special made
Fresh airs smell that never fades
The scent of your mate you always know
That special smell of winters snow
Can you smell me?

The beautiful sunset with the sky’s praise
The moonlight’s power in full moon phase
The vast tundra of arctic lands
The sand prints left from children’s hands
Can you see me?

The soft whisper of a lovers voice
Him telling you he wants to marry you, it’s your choice
Listening to his heartbeat as it lulls you to sleep
When he sings with feelings so deep
Can you hear me?

Knowing life is always a gift
Moving with each and every shift
Getting past the bumps that come
Waking up looking at the morning sun
