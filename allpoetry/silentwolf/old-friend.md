---
title: "Old Friend"
writer: "SilentWolf"

It's snowing outside my senses tell me
As I step over to the window and look to the soft white flakes
Pelting the ground binding together to form a blanket over earth
I am reminded of past lives, past adventures
As I stood tall in the forest peeking between the canopy of trees
And the snow hit my muzzle clustering and clinging to it
I could only smile and let out a snort 
Sending the snow aiming itself at me scurrying away
Ahh, my old friend, a welcome visitor
I walk slowly through it tail swaying behind
Knocking more snow off the trees to the ground where it scatters once more
My paws equipped snow shoes, my fur coat works wonders
I speed up my steps a bit smelling the air
It smells like a barbaque... mmm 
I flicker the snow here and there across vast lands covered with trees
The snow falling brings a peace throughout the forest
Each distinct snowflake makes an attempt at tackling me
So I begin to run, ears flattened back
I play with the snow "Try and catch me!" I say
It swirles around trying to get in my ears
"Hey no fair that tickles!"
I try to flick them not watching where I'm going
Straight into a bush, snow plops down on my head and I laugh
The time my spirit was free without restrictions
Then, remembering I get up and shake off "you win again old friend"
I come upon a cave looking quite dark
It's warm inside and a creature is asleep
Cautiously I gather the white fluff off the ground and step into the cave
My shadow vision does not fail me, I come upon the creature my heart racing
Slowly I lift the snow in the air... then quickly... quietly..
I RELEASE IT!  "AHA GOTCHA DRAGON!"
We chase eachother until the sun comes up then retire for sleep
I sigh returning to my bed letting the curtain fall
Memories of the past... how I wish I could have that time back
