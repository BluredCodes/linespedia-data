Sneeze sniffle cough cough
Body why can't you fight this off?
Sniffle sneeze sniffle sneeze
Damnit kill this virus please!
A war inside my body, so it seems to go on
And all I can do is sit here, like a weary pawn
Try to sleep all day, but sleep I just cannot
My nose is running, my throat is sore, my head is just too hot
I try to take some medicine, but man it tastes like crap
Choke it down through watering eyes, greatfully put back on the cap
With another cough my stomach cramps
My eyelids feel like hot lamps
My throat feels like it's burning
The room looks like it's turning
I think "maybe i'll go to bed"
But then I sit there much to dread
Sleep just will not come
So I sit there until one
Or two or three or four
I wish to sleep forever more
Or at least until I'm well again
So I don't have to take this medicine
I guess I can't complain
I have had much worse pain
I just need to get some sleep
But outside my window damn birds peep
Keeping me awake some more
Till my little brother comes to my door
Nobody can leave me alone today
Why can't they just go away!
Sneeze sniffle cough cough
