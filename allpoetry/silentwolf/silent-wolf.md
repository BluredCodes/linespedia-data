Silent Wolf, is that just a name 
Is it just a character from some roleplay game
How does she write from a wolf's point of view
Why doesn't she come up with a character that's new
We don't even know what the wolf's looks are
So here I am to set the bar
In my past life Silent Wolf was me
The shadows were home, wherever they may be
My body was sleek and powerful too
My fur was so thick black nothing could get through
My eyes were yellow and oh so bright
They looked like the moon on it's harvest night
My claws were silver and sharp as knives
Those who didn't attack me were the wise
A crescent moon dawned upon my chest
At fighting I was the very best
But I didn't fight much, so you see
I believed whatever will be will be
My muscles rippled in the moon light
Was I fast?  Does the fact nobody could match me suit you right
In the moonlight you could often find me
Up on a branch of a low lying tree
Staring up into the star filled sky
Or ears flat back bellowing my cry
Beside my mate I stood tall
Others stared, but I paid no heed at all
The shadows and elements were my powers
I would practice with them as a pup for hours
They always obeyed my every command
Wherever I tred they were close at hand
The nightime was a great friend to me
Through the nights I wandered free
Smelling the forest air so pure
If one could taste it they'd surely want more
All the powers of the ancestors past
Would stay in my soul and through me would last
If you wish to know more, why not ask me
I'll say this for sure, just a character Silent Wolf will never beJust figured I would write a poem about myself in my past life since people tend to wonder why I dawn the title 'Silent Wolf'.. (btw the full name is Silent Shadow Wolf) This probably won't get many stars since people tend to go for the poetic flow and such... actually I don't know what they really go for anymore.  This is what was in my soul and that is how it shall stay.  For one cannot rate a person's soul on grammer and such ;)
