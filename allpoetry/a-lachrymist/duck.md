---
title: "Duck"
writer: "a lachrymist"

I gaze upon these blacken and green colours,
with a understanding of what should be.
Scattering across the riverbed,
my comprehension fades from me.
Hoping for a quick return,
they sing out with their most graceful voices
reminding me of the colours.
The understanding of their culture reaches deafen ears,
But for a moment opened with the clearest of attentions.
