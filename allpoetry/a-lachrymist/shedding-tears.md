---
title: "Shedding Tears"
writer: "a lachrymist"
slug: "shedding-tears"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/7679-Shedding-Tears-by-a-lachrymist"
---

Betrayed by hope and wanting,
I  lay here broken hearted 
and shedding tears.
Moans of sorrow cross my lips,
I cradle with my warm embrace.
Shedding tears and bleeding here.

Eyes wide shut from broken thoughts,
desires of wanting have broken me.
Wanting of silence kills me.
Knees held tight and shedding tears,
the stale air reaches me, but I hide from her.
Warmth surrounds me, with the cold only to recur.

silently killing me with thoughts
so I lay wounded and bound tight
yet you stay, keeping me doused with misery
and I'm shedding tears and pissing blood.

expecting something from nothing
 so I lay hoping for a  bit of nothing
for all you see is my shadow
blind to the fact of me.
shedding tears and wanting you/something.
