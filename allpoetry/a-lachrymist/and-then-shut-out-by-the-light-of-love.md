---
title: "And Then Shut Out By The Light Of Love"
writer: "a lachrymist"

This here, brought on by convenience.
A feeling of love, lost in time.
Now this here, sees the convenience 
in everything present and lost.
annoyances brought on by this convenience.
something tells it that love is absent
from his conception of all  that he knows.
So I look in disbelief, In disgust of you.
His present unwanted, and abandoned by love.
Trying to clear away this feeling, 
this memory of you and your convenience.
