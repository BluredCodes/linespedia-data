---
title: "chimera"
writer: "a lachrymist"
slug: "chimera"

my voice softens, your watchful eye penetrates my every undying need. It waits to express itself to this harshful world. Pulling at the tongue, leaving me speechless and emotion-less. I scream out to my conditions, hoping for some release. Escape from me. Leave me softened and soaked from desperation. Down on my knees, I surrender. Letting this pull me down, holding me free. Wrap and hold me firm in this unknown chimera. Let this go, free me from this harsh nightmare you call life.
