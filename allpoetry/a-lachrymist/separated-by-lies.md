title: "Separated By Lies"
writer: "a lachrymist"

The poem that should have been called 'Fuck Your God'.With these cold hearted accusationsyour love feels less to me now.But you betrayed my expectationsof my morals and codes.My actions do no harm, but you feel hurt.Respectful and curtsey to your every need,aren't I good?Hand your bullshit to someone else.You lied to me, and cheated my soul.But you stole my everything,and your love feels so much colder now.My home is no longer,for you have taken what's mine,so I toy with your emotions.But only for the sake of revenge.For nothing else matters,but your rules and that lie.You and your God lock me out.these rules of yours and hisonly cause hatered.Words seem so terrible to you,so you lock them upwith your close mindedness.With your judgement, You have sinned.with your hatred, you have hurt.with your expectations, you have failed.Fuck your everything.It's nothing.Fuck those lies.break free from what you knew.It means nothing.It is nothing.Now I'm left in a place with nowhere to go.Will you stick to your lies, to your God?Or lend a hand, and guide me back home.Where I feel safe, where I belong.
