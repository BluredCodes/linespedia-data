There is a little biscuit,
Staring back at me,
I told it not to worry.
I`m cutting down you see.

Its got a lovely chocolate coat,
But I still refuse to try it,
I can`t be giving in so soon,
Its the first day of my diet.

So go ahead and look at me,
You`re a biscuit not a lover
Triumphantly I turn my back,
And eat its little brother!
