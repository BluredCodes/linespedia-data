We`re putting on a childrens play
A nursery Rhymning twist
But I`m feeling very anxious,
As I have a dreadful lisp.

With certain words you take your time
And think before you say,
Like Mice and Mouth and sausages 
create a lot of spray.

Your tongue and teeth get knotted up
th and s words are the worst
but guess what lines they`ve given me!
I think I must be cursed.

So give a thought to the front two rows
of our willing  audience,
as they dodge the spittle as I recite
Sing a Song of Sixpence.bibs all round I think
