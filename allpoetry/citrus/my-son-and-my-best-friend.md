title: "My son and my best friend"

Dirty Pants,
Ladish chants
Dandruff and grease,
Bitten nails
Emails
Anything for peace.

Calls from girls
Chocolate whirls
Growing at a pace,
MSN
ICQ,
Spots upon the face.

The word is cool 
The friendships rule,
Homework always late,
Hairy chin,
Cheeky grin
my son and my best mate!give me some pointers how to set this out..I wasn`t too sure.
