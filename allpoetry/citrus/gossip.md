title: "Gossip"
writer: "Citrus"

I heard he doesnt really love her,
And some say he has another,
Oh, but I heard it was the wife
who had herself a secret life.
Ooo and do you know she dyes her hair?
Well, at her age it would be rare.
That car they have, is much too flash,
I didn`t think they had the cash,
They must have everything on credit,
Well, that how Mrs Godfrey said it.
She said she heard someone say,
wont be long until they move away.
You know I wouldn`t really blame them dear,
There are some horrid gossips hereof course I dont do this.............
