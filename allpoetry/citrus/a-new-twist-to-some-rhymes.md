Pussy Cat Pussy Cat
Where have you been?
While you were away,
A mouse ate the Queen.

Doctor Foster went to Gloucester
Hoping it wouldn`t rain,
But to his digust
He started to rust
So never went there again!

Incy Wincy Spider
Climbed up the spout,
But someone forgot to
Take the bath plug out.
"Oh just forget it"
Old Incy wincy said
No more in the bath,
I`ll have a shower instead.

10 Green bottles,
hanging on the wall
no its 9 green bottles
cos, one has had a fall.
But that green bottle,
Didn`t accidently fall,
For I can see Johnathon
Right behind the wall.I make these silly variations up, for the children who I work with...they love it.
