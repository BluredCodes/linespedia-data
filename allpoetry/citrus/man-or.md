title: "Man or......."
writer: "Citrus"

You must have crawled in late last night,
I may have been asleep
But I felt your soft caress`s
As you went beneath the sheet.

Those sweet and tender touches,
I start to sigh and stir,
Up and down my spine you go,
Your fingers feel like fur.

Ok, you are forgiven,
You know how to make me feel,
My breath begins to quicken
and my senses start to reel

I need to look into your eyes
Your love I need to see
But from underneath the bedclothes
A spider winks at me!
