"What do you want for Christmas"?
He asks me every year,
"Oh just get me anything" I say,
It`s up to you my dear

But then again, I don't want rings
and don't buy any clothes
Oooo and please don't get me tickets
for any of those shows.

Oh and I don't want any perfume,
no underwear or socks,
and I just don't have the room 
left in my jewellery box!

I never wear those slippers
Just can`t abide a hat,
I don't want cd or video`s
I just wouldn`t like all that.

So whatever is your problem?
Don't give me that look of fear
just go out there with confidence
and surprise me for once this year!My poor hubby.....bless him
