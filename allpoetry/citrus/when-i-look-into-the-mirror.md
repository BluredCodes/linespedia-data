When I look into the mirror
Its not really me that I see,
But a girl, so young and vibrant
Such poise, a rare beauty

Those full and rounded ruby lips
Those big green sparkling eyes
She`s got exactly what it takes
To tease and tantalize

The freckles scattered on her nose
The sunlight in her hair,
That little mole upon her neck
her skin so clear and fair.

But as I take a second look,
Those eyes dont shine like stars!
Her face looks like that scary thing
in "The Alien from Mars"!

Can you hear that bump? 
its only me, 
coming back down
to reality!a girl can dream cant she?
