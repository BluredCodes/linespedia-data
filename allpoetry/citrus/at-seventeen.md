title: "At seventeen"
writer: "Citrus"

At seventeen and so in love
to a man who`s twice her age,
her friends are in an uproar
her family in a rage.

He told her it would be alright
he said they couldn`t linger,
the only way to seal their love
was a ring upon her finger.

At nineteen, a lovely son,
another on the way,
days spent all alone in tears
her husband out all day.

He tells her "stop the nagging"
thats all he ever hears,
she thinks about her friends advice
and to all her families fears.

At twenty one the love all gone
her husband has another,
she dreams of men in magazines
and longs to be their lover.

Now the happy wedding photo,
shakes within her tearful grip
as carefully and deliberately
she slowly starts to rip.
