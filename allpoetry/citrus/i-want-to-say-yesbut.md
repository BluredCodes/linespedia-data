title: "I want to say yes..but...."

I want to say yes
But I have to say no
I want to say stay
But I have to say go

I tell you don't call me
But you know its a lie
I tell you its over
But I can`t say goodbye

I say I don't love you
But you know that I do
I say that I`m happy
But you know that I`m blue

I wish it had ended
but I want it to start
I wish I was stronger
But you`ve broken my heart
