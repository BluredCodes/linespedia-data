How do you get a fake Tan?
it`s not as easy as they say
I`ve tried the creams, the mousse, the gel
I`ve even tried the spray

The say you must exfoliate
and moisturize with care
then smooth it on and rub it in
though streaking`s very rare

So I`m fully exfoliated
so smooth I`ve applied the cream
I now sit back and wait four hours
for the best tan  ever seen.

Well time is up, but oh dear me
I`ve done it wrong I guess,
for when I look down at my legs
Its just an orange mess!
