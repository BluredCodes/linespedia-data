---
title: "His Penis"
writer: "Citrus"
slug: "his-penis"

It`s a structure of great beauty,
it`s remarked on every day,
It`s probably the finest
And has to lie a certain way

It stands up every morning
it`s more than just perfect
it makes the others jealous
it demands the girls respect

And now of course my life`s complete
with my lovely son and hubby
I listen to their manly chats 
about their penis buddy.
