God, oh please, god, oh
please, god, please,
I'm sorry. I'm so sorry.
Can't stop crying.
Lost in my own head,
can't say any words,
oh god, I'm so sorry.

I hurt you. I'm sorry.
I hurt you and I can't fix it
I lost you, I'm sorry
I lost you and I can't fix it
I can't
believe
can't
I
I
can't
stop crying

I promised I know I said
I wouldn't I said I know
I couldn't I know oh god

I need
to sleep
now and hope
that no one else
hurts when I wake

I can't stop crying.

I'm sorry.I did something stupid. I hurt her, and didn't mean to. And now I have to live with myself. I don't know if I can. This was written at the poem addition page. It is inadequate. I'm sorry.
