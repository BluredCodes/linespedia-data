In the valley of my dreams
The life I seek
Fondles its way along my chest
Probing its tongue into my breath-

Seduced by an overpowering resolve 
To fornicate with infinity,
I find myself perched upon the Tree of Life
Above the mist that fears no folly
Hovering away from shadows and scenes of hate...

Skin of truth smooth and sanctimonious

Tears of beauty transformed from joy

The mind of god archetypal benevolence

Galaxies born and turned to ash...

As I feel for the contours of the invisible kingdom 
As I dare to venture into an unknown quest,
I seduce the whims of my evolving madness...

Forsaken me not
For I know the way
Into the heart resounding with eternal fury...
