(The primal ooze of time’s emotion
Flows down my leg and into your garden:
Where the chatter of past lifetimes
Seeps into the present before us...)

Your poppy red lips intoxicate me-
As I see the blue moon outside my window
Listening for your voice across the midnight wind...

Your poetic excellence seduces me
As I filter into your astral kingdom 
And taste your melting metaphors
And touch you illuminating imagery
And recite your voice within my mind...

Timeless tales of truth and beauty
Come to me in an montage of images
Only seen before in a meditative trance
Only heard by my will to secure you...

The fire in your eyes passionately reaches across to me
Offering a refreshing and inspiring focus,
Ventilated by my growing force to become you,
Fueled by the desire to know your inner details and desires...

And as I merge into your solidifying assurance
And as you project into my heated form
We begin to form from the pyre of dreams,
Seductive and secret and sumptuously divine-

Here, we play in the parameters of paradise,
In the beginning of creation
Like an evolving embryo
We become a nebulous new specie
Rising from the ashes of our burning passion...
