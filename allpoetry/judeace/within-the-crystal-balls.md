title: "Within the Crystal Balls"

Gazing, I can’t help gazing into my crystal ball
On my desk next to the statue of the wizard,
Who’s also holding a crystal ball within his hand...

Gazing, I am gazing into the vaporous 
Echo of a faint image
Slowly revealing a paragon of beauty
Set before my eyes by an unknown force
By an astral creature seducing me further inside... 

I’m peeping into the window of my mind
And yet I am outside the doorway to your heart
Into the envelop of entry 
Being sucked up by the power of your will...

You are an oracle of my oral whims
Forcing me to speak in tongues
In tones of metaphor I have never expressed before...

I am totally fascinated by your image
A gorgeous ghost in an electric orb of glass
Pulsating, riveting, translucent perfection... 

And I, I am all wound up 
Within an image I see within myself...

(Except for the wizard he seems to be smiling also)
