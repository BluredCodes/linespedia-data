I want to protect you...
Protect you from the demons
Lurking within torrid dimensions-

From mad jealous, deeply cynical 
Portrayals of diminished superficial emotion-

From the fear, anger and frustration
Designed by the great manipulator
Of our third global alter-

I want to shield you from wanton madness
Spilling over from the source of commonplace ideals-

From prefabricated conceptions of life and death
Flowing within hearts growing with pain...

I want to cover you...
With astral wings spread over your firmly-

With my smell and taste 
In your eyes 
I need to secure you-

I need to ensure my cosmic concern-

This is a world of unknown potential
A whole other lifetime of modulating fantasy...

And when I slay all possible dragons
There is my deep need
For you, to protect me.
