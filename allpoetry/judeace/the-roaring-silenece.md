Listen deep
Listen deeper

Can you hear that?

It fills the air with strange emotions
Telling tales of lost devotion-

It is deafening but strangely inaudible
Tireless and indifferent
Not open to suggestion-

With it, wild winds are brought
Shifting worlds around my skull
Sending chills into my spine
Bending me over 
Shifting my focus...

Listen close
Listen closer

Can you feel that?
Overwhelming isn’t it?

When you’ve offered all that is humanly possible
Given the time to listen and initiate
Given all that is godly available
But have missed the mark 
For you have not heard
The call of love as it fills your ears...
