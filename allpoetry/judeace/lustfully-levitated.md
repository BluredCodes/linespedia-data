I meditate on the twilight
As long strands of clouds become visible,       
Superimposed on a blue sky basked with orange glow...
This is a magical time
                        Wandering within worlds
Of light and darkness,
Tapping into the powers of creation
As the approaching sun’s emerges,
Its scent fondling my senses,
Consuming me with your extravagant face...

Hail light of nighttime fantasy! 
Soothe me into the horizon of creative whim,
The paradigm of all that is essential...
Filling me with the fit of motivated madmen,
Into the cluster of evolving egos...

Free me from the clutch of despondent daydreamers- 
Release me into the sea of tomorrow’s mantra
As I wink at you kneeling in front of me
Your face decorated with scripts of fascination,
With voluptuous verses of deep pleasured fixation,
With charismatic avatars exploding ceremoniously before me...

Uplift me above the commonplace
Ceremony of planetary persistence...

Levitate me with lips
Speaking libraries of peppered emotion...

Look into my eyes
See the heart of heaven

Witness the smile of sensation
As you surround me
With the enigmatic essence of your uplifting endurance...
