title: "Wizard's Window"

Imagine the stars dancing above you
Not fixed or twinkling
But alive and pulsating
Red and blue and green and orange
Like a symphonic spectrum of light and color…

Imagine the sound of worlds colliding
Not as a grand explosion
But gently merging 
Like astral bodies embracing 
In an syncopated rhyme
Of time and space, 
Like an inter-dimensional portal:
Sliding your membrane within its grasp…

Visualize the fury of mighty oceans
As they unleash unbridled energy 
Into the center of your heart...

Let your secret creative juices
Flow onto my thoughts and passions
Like a wonderful waterfall 
Winking- 
Where I welcome a wandering worshipper 
Of your wondrous beauty
Into my wizard’s waiting window…
