title: "Beneath the surface"

The time is right, 
To immerse myself into your heart
Like a solar sub submerging my thoughts
Into the depths of your deep soothing space: 
You are mysterious and unseen
Until twilight reveals your sparkling form...

This moment is unfolding,
Stars screaming for attention:
Their distance and solitude overwhelming,
Faint yet alive 
Like your elusive scent
Across the expanding night:
Almost dawn
In the glimmer you of consecrated face...

This night has revealed 
Your voice and your lips
Expressing visions and ideas 
Through life’s supernal veil-

You have finally given me 
Your thought’s peripheral embrace
So I can taste the motion
Surrounding your wishes...

And as I approach closer to you
I am orbiting around your smile
Like a perihelion in my quest to know you...
