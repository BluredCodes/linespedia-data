Dig your fingers into my dreams
Dazzle me with your anointed beauty 
As I drift across the dreamscape of our dawn-

Gather me into your harvest
Into your basket of fine seduction
As I wander the morning mist
Watching the skies
Listening for your symphonic splendor

I am surrounded by your solicitous sonata
As you allow me to conduct 
Our impulse for fortuitous event- 
 
Raise your body like a sail
With your arms extended to the night sky…

Catch my auspicious breeze 
Filter into your respiring virtuosity
So we can head into the saintly horizon…

You are inside my heart
Growing like a splendid phantasmagorical fruit 
That has ripened 
And released juices 
Assimilating into my blood 
Throughout my frenzied flesh

I can taste you in my mouth
Lured by your sweet embrace
Cured by your powerful potion
Your fingers altering my form…
