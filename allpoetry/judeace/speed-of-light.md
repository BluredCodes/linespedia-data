---
title: "Speed of Light"
writer: "JudeAce"
slug: "speed-of-light"
source: "AllPoetry"

I do not remember my poems-
Orgasmic ideas explode deep within me
As images, words, meters spontaneously unfold,
Tasty emotions refined through a cosmic veil...

I’m living real hard 
But that’s my living devotion
To truth and knowledge
And audacious dreams...

I’m too caught up in anonymous emotions
At least that’s what they say to me

But wondrous worlds of metaphoric splendor 
So do embrace me
I become a sieve inside a mystical garden
Watering ideas inside new brains...

Precious drops of delirious splendor
Inside my wandering eye
Release spectrums of surreal and spacious syntax  
Into cool winds soothing this evening’s muse...

I listen to opinions inside gray matter-
I speak to whims of ethereal appearance-
I ride the invisible stallion of freedom
To a river where I can refresh my burden
And clear the dust from my weary views
Tired but not malnourished,
For a creative urge controls my way...

So I wait and wonder on this wonderful evening
What do my poems really imply?
And when is their time about to come-

But I know that’s just a rhetorical question
And that time is only an illusion
When living at the speed of light...
