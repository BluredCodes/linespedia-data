My life has been changed.
I once had not a care in the world,
Now I care so much for you I can't think.
You are the first thing I think of when I get up
and the last thing before i go to bed.
My life has changed.
Is it your hugs?
Your kisses?
The way you make love to me?
The way you look after?
My life has been changed
All because of your love.I don't know if this is a very good one, I wrote it 2 days before my boyfriend broke up with me. I'm sure it needs work just let me know.
