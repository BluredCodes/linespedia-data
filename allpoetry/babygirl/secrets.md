We are enemies,
We who used o be so in love.
I stand staring at you,
You stand Challenging me.
Neither of os speaks,
Neither of us cries.
We simply stand apart,
Thinking,
Remembering,
And Reliving.
You shake your head,
Then turn and walk away.
A lump arises in my throat
Tears fill my eiyes.
So I simply stand there astonished
And watch as you slowly close the door.
