---
title: "[You were so nice,]"
writer: "babygirl"
slug: "you-were-so-nice"
source: "AllPoetry"

You were so nice,
You held me like no other had before.
Your kiss was so gentle and sweet. 
We said it couldn't happen again,
We said we wanted to be friends.
We didn't want anything to happen to that.
I tried enough though,
You never come around.
Now I'm saying it's your turn,
If you want me,
You know where to find me!
