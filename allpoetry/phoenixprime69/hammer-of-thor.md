---
title: "Hammer of Thor"
writer: "phoenixprime69"

The thunder crashes
lightning smashes
all who oppose
storm clouds roll
roll over me
roll over me
drowning my sorrows
drowning my sins

chorus:

hammer of thor!
hammer of thor!
save me save me! (s)
Hammer of thor
hammer of thor
hammer of thor!

guitar solo

the thunder crashes
feel the power
feel the power
of my soul
lightning crashes
burns all that I see
with his strength
I am mighty
I destroy all!!!

repeart chorus, guitar solo to fade.

(s) denotes lead vocals only

phoenix
