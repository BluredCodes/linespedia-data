In the heat of the night 
I just can't believe 
That you've gone away 
And left me so cold (so cold) 
I can still feel the heat 
Of your touch on me 

Oh my Sophia 
What have I done? 
Oh my Sophia 
What can I do? 
What can I do? 
To bring you home to me

Can't live without you_
