qui-tas the word for beloved only in my little world that I carry with me deep inside my heart i visit there, in shadows in dreams you've been there as always in my heart waiting for me loving me i am so lost my worlds have collided what is real? what is fantasy? can this be true? do I touch you? or is it Him? do I feel your words inside my mind? or do I float on clouds of gold? I longed for your touch wrapped in fantasy cloaked in darkness hearing your heart call to me hearing your lips push me away dragons danced across the sky the phoenix rose from the sun now I stand on the threshold of dream... Is it real? Then I don't wish to wake up. Kalehel Arielstha Ki-Tharan, Tel' Doloethe Mar-Kola Qui-tas. (It means, in elven, 'I am Prince Arieyl Goldmoon, but without you my beloved, I am nothing.') phoenix (c) Charles P.
