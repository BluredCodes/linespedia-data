The Forest 

sounds of nature 
songs of mother earth 
the cat purrs in the forest 
birds sing songs of nature 
birds never weep 
weep goes the willow 
willow who chokes on air 
airplane flies over 
birds sing their songs 
songs of happiness 
happiness is the forest 
forest who chokes on air 
air which we breathe 
breathe what we pollute 
songs the birds sing 
songs echo through the forest 
silence falls on the forest 
the cat stalks the bird 
no bird sings happy songs 
happiness is the forest 
airplane flies overhead 
axe cuts the trees 
bulldozer chases the animals 
birds sing of sorrow 
of death 
technology is great 
technology eats nature 
man cannot live without 
tv, car, kmart 
forest cannot live without air 
birds cannot live without trees 
technology eats nature 
progress takes the land 
death follows technology 
dogs barks at the birds 
tells them to fly away 
far away from man 
dog coughs; to late for him 
he is bound to man 
technology taken his breath 
silence falls over the forest 
death is silent
