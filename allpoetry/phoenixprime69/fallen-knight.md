warm summer sun 
glints off my steel 
shining brightly 
hair flowing in the wind 
my horse, white and strong 
stands ready 

my heart beats quickly 
lance poised ready 
the crowd cheers my name 
I spur, ride to you 
lowering the lance 

to accept your blessing 
our eyes lock 
my mind whispers 
"I love you" 

garter stays in hand 
you tossed a rose 
black, like the night 
and turned away 

the crowd gasped 
they knew what it meant 
my armor felt heavy 
world started spinning 

my horse fell dead 
I am crushed beneath it 
my sword rusted 
armor split open 

I lay defeated. 


Disgraced, dishonored 
I moved to the shadows 
watching you 
wondering 
watching 
waiting 
knowing your heart 

had not changed. 

angry, bitter 
saddened 
my honor lost 
my reason lost 

I wandered 
staying in darkness 
darkness was cool, soothing 
hid my pain 
from me 

and now, 
a lifetime later 
you again call my name 
I come, broken, destroyed 

not the Knight you once knew 
bitter, angry, strong, 
evil has its advantages 

But I will serve 
willingly, honorably 
I am yours now and forever 
sworn on my sword 
I shall defend you 

Now again I ride 
with honor, reason, 
dignity 
I long to fight for you 
slay your demons 

I am your Knight 
now and forever.Well it's my first one here, what do you think?
