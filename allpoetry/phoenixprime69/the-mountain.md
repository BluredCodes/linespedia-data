I am the mountain 
standing tall 
defiant. 

I am the mountain 
who never sways in the wind 
eternal. 

I am the mountain 
whose caverns are deep 
safe. 

I am the mountain 
I hold up the sky 
forever. 


I am the mountain. 
I provide shelter. 
I am strength. 
I am eternal. 
I am shelter in the night. 

I am alone. 



phoenix
