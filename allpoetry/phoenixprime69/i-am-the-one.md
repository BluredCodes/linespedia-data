I am the fool perfection attained slips away Godhood attained slips away how I run from myself afraid to try doomed to fail i look in the mirror see what i am I see your tears what I have become I am the One whose hand destroyed my fate I am the One shattered dreams I am the One whose heart lays empty shattered broken lost failure useless I am That One phoenix (c) Charles P.
