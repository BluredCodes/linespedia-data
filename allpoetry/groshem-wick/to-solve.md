title: "To  solve..."

A stem from a branch,
is what makes of it.
Two ways of paths,
and how we come to it..


Gold was the ferver then,
tangable and valuable-
gold is the ferver now,
high upon a mountain.
The combination of these outcome
is the path for a stone.

Seek it deep within the earth;
cleanse it with the waters,
utter upon it with breath,
exalt it to the sun.
