To believe that one has the right to the " lofty " idea
that they may address critic to anothers heart is rubbish.
Fame, fortune, and ego are man made devises to procure
a higher place.
Thus making your heart and emotions and thoughts of the
least value; compared...


Impaired, would be the proper.
