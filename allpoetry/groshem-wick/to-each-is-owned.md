She was a bone diggin daddy
crochin through the bush -

He was a cherry poppin mamma
lookin  through the door -

There eyes met, there hearts aswept,
the bodies was a tingle, tangle, angled, dripp.
Emanations should be so grand as two
adorned could rule the land.
Fortell the futur gone is now,
that we may atwist the key,
and rule the sand in time.

She was a dog diggin bitch -

He was a pussy whippin whore -
