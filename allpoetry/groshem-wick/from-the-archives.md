Eva,

My sweet, my love.

This moment is a consicration of my 
divinity with yours,
in the hope that all these demons
forget there deeds and cleanse us.

The past is gone now,
it is the present that we succeed.
Perhaps our bonds be risen
and we claim the adorned Eden once more.

Our Love is the first Eva
and I keep it eternal.

          
                A. Kadmon
