title: "Flyaway"

Darling, you're an innocent falcon
I can't stop you 
From wandering with the wind.
But every time 
You come and then leave me
I wish I could
Tie you to the ground
See for myself if a caged bird cannot sing.

So now you're flying high
I can't see you 
For the sunlight reflected in my eyes.
You're brilliant,
Icarus,
You'll never fall
Though the sun may melt the wax
You're flying far too high
For that to matter anymore.

Darling, you're a will-o-the-wisp
You fly through me
Like a ghost remembered in a dream.
You still hurt me
I love but can't leave you
I wish I could
finally let you go
see for myself if I can live without you there.It's meant to be put to music...
