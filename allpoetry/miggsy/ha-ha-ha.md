title: "ha ha ha"
writer: "Miggsy"
slug: "ha-ha-ha"
source: "AllPoetry"

drip drip
screaming mouth
laying on the bed
walls breathing in and out
want to get up
maggots on the floor
now crawling up my legs
steady scream
coming, suppressed
ah did it
sshhhh funny
no one knows
calm once more
red and green 
flashing in my head
what does it mean
stop or go 
what do i do
down i go once again
laying on the bed
trip trip
