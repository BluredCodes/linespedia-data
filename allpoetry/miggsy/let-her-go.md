title: "Let Her Go"

I saw your forgotten future
Do you hold it dear?
The declarations of love 
and the sketches of hurt
Clear them, you need to
There is no room in your heart, why?
Look, look close to you
I am here, waiting
Let me in, 
Let me in, you will see
we can be one... feel my strengths
Yes, I do, I have them, you bring them out
'Forever' will be ours
Just want it as much as meI am ready for any critical comments, I am new to this and want to learn how to express my thoughts so they make sense.
I write from the heart and sometimes there is so 'much' flying around in there it is hard to put it down logically.
