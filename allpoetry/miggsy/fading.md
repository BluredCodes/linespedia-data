---
title: "Fading"
writer: "Miggsy"
slug: "fading"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/26574-Fading-by-Miggsy"
---

I want to sing out of tune 
and not care what you hear

I want to scream my head off 
and not care what you think

I want to torture my body
and not care your concern

I want to lose touch with reality
and not care at all
