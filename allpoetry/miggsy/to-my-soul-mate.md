---
title: "To My Soul Mate.."
writer: "Miggsy"
slug: "to-my-soul-mate"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/25763-To-My-Soul-Mate..-by-Miggsy"

I had a dream and you were in it
It was a dream of love
A dream of a journey
The other half of yours

I have been looking for you
since we were separated 
at a moment in time
when it was decided 
the essence who would be

Longing and knowing you were there
somewhere
we had to be divided
to endure different lives
so as to truly give to each other,

Fate was always there
as we grew
we lay whispering to each other
beckoning...
we made it 
together we are 
Soul and Soul
