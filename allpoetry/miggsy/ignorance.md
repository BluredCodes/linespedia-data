title: "Ignorance"
writer: "Miggsy"

A cute little smile
The anger within
Pink pouty lips
With their dangerous grin

Who would know
if they don't look
inside the cover 
of this 'innocent' book

Think they are smart 
but have no idea
can only SEE
what they want to HEAR!!

To ignorant to notice
Something is wrong
Will all be suprised
When 'someone' is gone
