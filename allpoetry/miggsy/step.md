our fingers entwine
as we stand together before our future

as i dream this dance...

I imagine a million red hearts
with tiny wings
fluttering around my head
and as I close my eyes
I hear a our  angels
singing our song
their melodic voices 
calming my nervous excitment
I see your smile 
and a look in your eyes
tells me you hear them too

like a far off whisper I hear a voice
and to this I take my line
without taking a breath 
and from the depths of my soul
I say to you  "I do"
