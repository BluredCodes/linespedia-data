---
title: "Try It"
writer: "Miggsy"
slug: "try-it"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/26172-Try-It-by-Miggsy"
---

how do you like it 
with a twist?
or are you to good 
and stupidly sweet
me 
i like it differently
in a way you have never had it
or even wanted it
cos' you don't know
and what you don't know, you... (you know how it goes)
you are missing it, 
deeper
come with me 
try it out
come over and give it a go
you won't go back 
i promise
