---
title: "Getting There"
writer: "Miggsy"
slug: "getting-there"

it's been a long time
since i saw you
you said i have changed
i see you have to 
but only in someways
i will never forgive you
as unhealthy as that is
i will always hate you 
but love you... 
in a strange sort of way
you are ugly in your heart 
and you too often let it show
when you abused me with your words
without remorse,without a thought
why did you think you could
who gave you the right
no-one, you did, and it was not yours to have
you changed me, twice, once for the worse 
but now for the better
i left and closed the door
sometimes it sneaks opens
and slams on my fingers
but not as much anymore
