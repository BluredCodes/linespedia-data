---
title: "I don't want to"
writer: "Miggsy"
slug: "i-dont-want-to"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/26142-I-dont-want-to-by-Miggsy"
---

tonight yet again
i put on my face
and go out with you
to 'your' place
sometimes it's hard
to act this way
when it is not what i do
nor what i say
put on the smiles
put on the fake
pretend and lie
bullshit make
one day i'll tell you
and you will know
that sometimes
i don't want to go


but then again 
you already do
