---
title: "Figment"
writer: "Miggsy"
slug: "figment"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/26107-Figment-by-Miggsy"
---

i don't understand
why 
we are so different 
i feel it
more than you
do you just ignore it
or worse
don't you notice it at all
there is part of me 
you will never understand
and I you
do you even want to
how will we make it
when i resent 
leaving inside
the part of me 
hidden 
to sit and fester
one day it will come out
and you won't believe
i know it will 
for now
 I will 
stay frustrated and burning
