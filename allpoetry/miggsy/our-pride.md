title: "Our Pride"
writer: "Miggsy"

the moment i've been waiting...
we will soon be acting out
only been in my dreams
up until now
soon you will be beside me
and our 'Forever' will be true
a family for real 
our son will be there to
our boxed gift
and this, the ribbon to complete
diamonds and gold 
wrapping around my heart
where it will always be
