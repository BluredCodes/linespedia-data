title: "Soon I will forget (Or Will I?)"

while he was there 
in person
you were in my heart
he is my destruction
and always will be
you are my saviour
my hope my 'Forever'
he hurt me deeply
but so do you (sometimes)
I hate him and his bastard words
you...you will always say sorry
difference!!!
you are mine and I am yours
WE help me forget
it does come to the surface
but more and more, it's
LESS and LESS
