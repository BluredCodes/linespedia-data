---
title: "Me...Not You"
writer: "Miggsy"
slug: "menot-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/26070-Me...Not-You-by-Miggsy"
---

why aren't you there
where i want you to be 
when i need to get to the other side
not the 'sweet' side
but the dark side

i know
because you don't belong there
only i do
