---
title: "Good Intentions Missed"
writer: "Miggsy"
slug: "good-intentions-missed"

The fragrence of an opening flower
released, dispersing wildly
Longing to reach some welcoming place
in which to fullfill its destiny
The innermost depths of someone 
who will appreciate it's every intention
Lost, weightless holding onto everything 
it wants to give
Floating until it finds this appreciation
Moving futher from it's protective shelter

The perfume starts to fade
it's determination waning
There is no one to embrace this precious desire for giving
It disappears,never to reach the purpose for which its birth was meant.
