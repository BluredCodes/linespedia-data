title: "no better than me"
writer: "Miggsy"
slug: "no-better-than-me"

how are you
you superficial man
why do you think you are 
any better than me
driving in your expensive car
living in you rich home
i would not notice  
except you look down on me
i feel your judgement burning,
your comments stinging
just be careful what you do 
don't put yourself to high
cos one day when you are sitting 
at your mahogany table
eating from your wedgewood plate
you could be choking 
while i am sitting there
