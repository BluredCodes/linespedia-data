title: "Simple Wish"
writer: "Miggsy"

i dream of a time
when i can be true to my heart
not
to the drone of 'expectation'
my dreams are so simple...
to sit in a breeze, listening 
as it plays whispering tunes in my ear
hearing my son's stories of 
dinosaurs and dragons...laughter so loud
to ponder, over books and photos
not emails and catostrophies
no stress, no deadlines, 
just time...
to cook in my kitchen
wonderful aromas 
and the sight of love
when you both walk through the door
how elusive my simple dreams seem to be
but they are mine 
and if i close my ears to the 'Dream Stealers' 
no one will be able to take them from me
