title: "So Called"
writer: "Miggsy"

Here I am Stupid Clown
Fingers up fingers down
Pushing on idiot keys
Here's the boss!...
On my knees

Stupid me living 'Dreams'
Never never as it seems
Sitting in a rotten chair
Breathing in man-made air

Think I have it
But I don't
Want to leave
But I won't
