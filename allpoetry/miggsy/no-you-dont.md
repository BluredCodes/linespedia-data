---
title: "No You Don't"
writer: "Miggsy"
slug: "no-you-dont"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/26572-No-You-Dont-by-Miggsy"

how do we fall into these ' normal lives'
to which we don't belong
i am loosing my depth 
in all the do's and don'ts 
i need purpose and reason to comment 
and to join your conversation, of 
shallow and minor and mirrored dribble
do you have any idea
did you hear
did you see
oh what about this and that
get over yourself and open your eyes
it's not what you think
regurgitated words
straight from the black and white
read the words that aren't there
listen to the words that aren't spoken
open you small peanut brain 
and you will soon see
you don't have what you think you have
...any freakin' idea
