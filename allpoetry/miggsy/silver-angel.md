---
title: "Silver Angel"
writer: "Miggsy"
slug: "silver-angel"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/33725-Silver-Angel-by-Miggsy"
---

Memories come back to haunt me
of a time when I was young...
and you weren't
innocence was mine to have
and yours to protect
but you didn't 

Memories come back to haunt me
of a time when I was sweet 
and you were cruel 
it seemed innocence was mine to give
and yours to take

Memories come back to haunt me
of a time we were playing your "games"
demons flew over us
and called my name

I felt their  hearts so black
mocking my pale face
while it was covered in shame
I could feel the grass beneath me
stabbing needles into my flesh
and the crawling, biting monsters 
leaving their sting in my palms
as I gripped and ravaged the dirt
trying to claw my way into the protection of the earth

you looked at me and smiled 
and I dreamed...
of the silver angel in my pocket

Memories come back to haunt me
of a time when I took control
and you screamed 
as my silver angel wrapped it's wings of wrath 
around your blackened demon heart

Memories come back to haunt me
of a time when my innoncence was gone
and your last breath of pleasure expelled 
in the space between our mouths

Memories come back to haunt me 
of time when I was free
and you took that from me
