title: "A Plea for Darkness"
writer: "Miggsy"

darkness come here
for all I want to feel is nothing but the silence
nothing but you...
lie here beside me
i want to look at you through this night
and see your soul
i want to know what you know
i want to move with you as none before
own you and yours
i want to close my eyes and smell you
your sweet sweet musky perfume
a scent that drives me crazy
us, as the hands of time stand still
i want to feel my heart beat hard, against yours
we know each other as no one else has ever
our secret combination
take us to another intensity
and surround us as we float
a glimmer of the stars above 
we don't want to reach
the coolness of the night carrying tickles over my skin 
goosebumps exploding with your every whisper in my ear
i feel your senses and excitement 
our bodies move and touch and melt together
there is only our music 
and the darkness and peace
