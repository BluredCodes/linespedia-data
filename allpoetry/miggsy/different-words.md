---
title: "Different Words"
writer: "Miggsy"
slug: "different-words"

Where have my words gone...
once full of passionate remorse
erupting from my broken heart
so not long ago
easily spewing from the depths of me

I must have healed somewhere
my wounds must have closed
for now my painful words 
are nowhere to be found

I search my soul.... and rip and tear
looking for a resemblence of hate and anger 
words of shame and regret
but nothing do I feel
nothing strong anyway

Only thoughts of confusion
find their way to my pen
and in desperation I write only of my plight
what now will I become
what will I do
for my heart that was so dark and empty
is now wonderfully full of you
