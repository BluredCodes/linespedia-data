---
title: "Lovers Secrets"
writer: "Miggsy"
slug: "lovers-secrets"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/35260-Lovers-Secrets-by-Miggsy"
---

i  believe in secrets 
you're an open book
unless you have the biggest secret
and I, to shallow to look
undo your secrets and let me in to share
i will keep them to myself
i will tell no one, not even dare


i don't think you have any
how can this be so
doesn’t it make you human
to know things only you know
it makes for life exciting 
a secret together, in our hearts
a knowing of each other
our lips it never parts

so trust in me my darling
and let your secret out
to me and me alone
tell me what it’s about
you will ease your burden 
and I can share the load
and together we will travel
down the lovers road
