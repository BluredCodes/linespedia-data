title: "Hmmph"
writer: "Miggsy"
slug: "hmmph"

---

I can't concentrate
my thoughts are everywhere but nowhere
I am listening to everything
but hearing nothing
I take it on board
but push it away
part of your words 
are here to stay
I am useless and stupid
but know I am not
I am shit and disgusting
but beautiful as well
I am weak and gullible 
but as strong as they come
I am cold and heartless
but warm as the sun
who am I now
I have no quality
but it will come
I know...
and you will always be
NOTHING TO ME!!!!
