burning sensation
slice my skin
instant elimination
held back within
heated blood streaming down the land that is my arm
fuck how i have missed my self induced bodily harm
poisons of alcohol stinging my wrist
my only release from shit like this
razers down
its scissors time to cut through  me this time
ive recently lost my balance and im on a fine line
downing all the pills
step into the tub
sad to know im gonna die without ever being loved
water turning pink
stained with all my blood
refuse to stop the flow as i smile at the flood
far too lazy to give a damn
and just a bit too tired to hide
i say goodbye to those i love
and i welcome my suicide
