---
title: "would you"
writer: "AngelzBaby"
slug: "would-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/44602-would-you-by-AngelzBaby"
---

if i told you i was going to die
would you hold me until tomorow
would you kiss away all of my tears
would you take away the sorrow

if i told you i was dying
would you stay with me until the time came there
would you hold my hand and stroke my face
remind me that you care

if i told you that i was hurting
would you take away the pain
and if i was going crazy
would you stay until i was sane

if i told you that i was ending my life
would you tell me to live on
would you hold me up and keep me whole
until i was strong

if i told you i was going to die
would you tell me that you need me
im not sure of all the answer
but i know im sure we'll see
