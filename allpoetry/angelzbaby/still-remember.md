You raised your hand and then raised your voice
What you left me with was no choice
You brought a knife up to my neck
And said, "C’mon baby, what the heck?"
I tried my best to get you off
But all you did was restrain me
You hit me,	
You hurt me,
You made me cry,
Did anything to obtain me,
You took me down, 
And tied me up,
My hands were bound,
My mouth gagged shut,
I screamed and cried but no one heard
And then suddenly it occurred
You were slowly stripping me 
Of my dignity and my clothes
I was so young and so unaware
It wasn't the path I chose
You held me down and continued
To strip me of my youth
The rest continue with your mind
I swear it's all the truth
I was so young, and so innocent,
But this fate I could not escape,
I remember that night like yesterday,
The night of that horrible rape
