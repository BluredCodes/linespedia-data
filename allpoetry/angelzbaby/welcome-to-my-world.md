title: "Welcome To My World"
writer: "AngelzBaby"

Welcome to this world 
Torn apart you
Torn apart by your words
And all you put me through
Welcome to this river
The one in which I drowned
So my feelings and my hurt
Would be concealed and bound
Welcome to this forest 
The one in which im lost 
In thoughts and contemplations
About my very life cost
Welcome to this shadow
The one you left behind
The one that you've forgotten
So you would never have to find
Welcome to this cave
Filled of hopes and dreams
All destroyed by you
At least that’s what it seems
Welcome to this tear
Of course caused by you
For all your words of hatred 
And everything untrue
Welcome to this heart
The one you played so long
Welcome to this spirit
That’s already dead and gone
