incognito
such a pain
put it on my shoulders
who else to blame
vast disasters
all my fault
all the pain
all the assault
prostitution
illegal drugs
its my problem
my boys are thugs
my girls are whores
stupid sluts
no defense
wouldnt dare say but
the illusion
its our doing
for all the ODing 
all the screwing
mirror image
were all the same
were all fuckers
we should be ashamed
high school shootings
bomb threats too
wouldnt dare say its partially because of you
adolescents 
blame it on us
its our fault
just all because
wheres the proof
the evidence
now your scared
your getting tense

your the adults
you can do no wrong
it sucks to know it was you all along
