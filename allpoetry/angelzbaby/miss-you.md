---
title: "Miss You"
writer: "AngelzBaby"
slug: "miss-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/43593-Miss-You-by-AngelzBaby"
---

Let the lord take me
Into his loving arms
Don’t let the devil break me
And deliberately do me harm
Let me escape from this depression
Into a life of happiness
Don’t let me taste these salty tears
Instead let me sample bliss
Let me lie in shadows
And come out when it feels right
Don’t leave me with confusion so I can’t sleep all night
Let me lie in peace
With no voices of despair
Let me live in my world
Where people treat me like they care
I can hear the whispers
Even those behind my back
And u can’t seem to see that my soul's 
Slowly turning black
Let me for once realize
This world on my own
Let me walk through it
But don’t make me go alone
Hold me as my eyes close shut
And as my flesh turns blue
And remember no ifs, ands or buts
My whole world was all of you.
