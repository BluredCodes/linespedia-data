---
title: "Lies"
writer: "AngelzBaby"
slug: "lies"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

lies
sick demented horrible lies
they're tearing up my heart
and still you won't head to my cries
tell me when did this start

lies
all that you can say
nothing else can make me smile
swear it is the truth 
or were you lying all the while

lies
thats all you gave to me
except when you said no one would love me
but it seems that the tables may turn
and it's your turn to see

lies
they can go both ways
trust me on this one
i can make your very soul rip
if you know where i'm coming from

lies
you started all of them
you made me fall apart
but babe it's time you know the truth
this time I"M gonna break YOUR heart
