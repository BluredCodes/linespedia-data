title: "SHE"
writer: "Hex Venus"

How i would love
to scar your
pretty, porcelan, picture
Perfect Face
Perfect Hair
Perfect Body
with my nails, sharp
and bloody thirsty.

but OOPS!, it seems
i have bitten them all off
in a frenzied anxiety attack 
insecurity raging.

I hate the way you look
at the angel sent for me
as if he was sent for you
because HE WASN'T!

But i hate the way
he looks at you more
the subtle longing in his eyes
ocean blue and lightening struck.

The way he talks and touches
your lips, hips
hair - black
his smile when your near
his laughter when you joke
(and more so when you dont)

He was sent for ME 
this one
not you
so please dont test me here
or i will grow my nails back
just for you.
