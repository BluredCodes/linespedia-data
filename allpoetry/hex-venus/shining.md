writer: "Hex Venus"

The amount of time
I've loved you
Outway the time
I've hated you.

But it seems I
spend so much time
in tears over you
and over others...

You either love me
or your dont...
and if you do
you must respect me

You must allow me
to be wrong
to make mistakes
to be imperfect.

You must recognise
that there will be NO others
for you or for me!!
You must promise!

You must care for me, my love
Be kind to me..
be loving and sweet
and ALWAYS be my knight in shining armour
to rescue me 
and protect me
and carry me off
when i can no longer walk
