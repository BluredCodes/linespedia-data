One day I wondered
What it would be like 
to EAT
the hair on your toes
and now, today 
while i watch you 
ASLEEP
I wonder what it would have accomplished.I know this is really off beat, and i wasnt REALLY thinking about eating his toe hari - it just worked well as a quirky poem!
