Embrace
Let me face
And let me fall
Down my spiral declination of life
I will open my tattered wings and soar
To summer land
Across from hell
Far from heaven
Down to the floor
Endure
I will fight
I will shed my tears of blood in the river of joy
And I will wander
I will roam
I will find a place to break my bones
I will cry
Let me sleep
Let me rest
Let me shiver and let me moan
Try and break me
I do confess
My sins
My evils
My human nature
And I will die
I will give up
I cannot lie
I will fall
I will crash
I will embrace
