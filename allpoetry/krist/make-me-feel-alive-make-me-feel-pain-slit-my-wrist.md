make me feel alive, make me feel pain, slit my wrist
and enojoy the rain Let it drip drip drip... like poetry 
on the floor and i'll dance, Oh how i'll dance
with such joy on my face till the very last drop
Such true poetry flowing from my veins
from me to you so you can see
that i am all right
that it is okay to bleed
and this thing called life
  
         i dont Truely need
