humans are strange
whos rite? whos wrong?  is it in our Beliefs?
Who knows? Death, Subject to control. Life. How strange
Explain, i dont understand, Dont know how. Dont know how, dont know why
just like everyone, can be fun, its not a sin
Lets have a shot, fly around
sad creature
Broken Rule pain-tinted pool, fucking fools
swelling bruises, Hide the Scars, HIDE the Scars
i smiled today, I died tomorrow. Dont let go
Keep your choke hold, dont let go, until i fold
silence now, the storm has passed. Somewhere far, Somewhere quiet,
keep it real, shit that steals
My treasured white, lost over there, up and in 
Travel in my vein He said Big Cheese, Make Me. Hope you found your peace
The child and the mother fought. What a waste of skinl. god says its rite, How can we be wrong, cause i think im here, i smile cause i think they'll see 
i act cause im not here
let them see
maybe we'll find peace
just maybe we will
