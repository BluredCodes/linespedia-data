---
title: "Be Proud"
writer: "meganmb2004"
slug: "be-proud"

Look into the mirror,And see who's staring back.Think of those qualities she has,And not of those she lacks.Her personality is great,She  always has something interesting to say.Just her presence in my life,Has brightened the bleakest of my days.She should have so much confidence,Because there's so much she can do.But if ever there's a time she needs me,I'll be here to help her through.She's a person of true beauty,The person looking back at you.She has so many great features,Even though she may not think it's true.You're a great friend,And I can be myself around you.And without you in my life,I don't know what I would do.So look into the mirror,And be proud of who's staring back.Just think of those great qualities she has,And not of those she lacks.
