It started with a smile,
A simple little "Hi."
After six years of friendship,
We sadly said goodbye.

We used to be inseparable,
We were always side by side.
I always had a shoulder,
Whenever I needed to cry.

You were my strength,
When I felt too weak.
You were always there,
When no one else was willing to be.

Now I am to feeble
To even want to speak.
And sadly I am blinded
By truth and reality.

The reality that you're gone,
It's shattering my heart.
Who would've thought after six years,
Best friends would drift so far apart?This isn't one of my recent poems, but tell me what you think.  :)
