When I need someone 
I turn to you.
You tell me you'll always be there for me,
And that I can always count on you.

You've been my shoulders,
When I needed to cry.
You've let me lean on you
Through all the tough times.
You're my ears
When I need to vent.
You listen without judging,
And give me much needed words of encouragement.
You give me words of strength
When I can no longer fight.
You're there to l ift me to my feet,
And tell me things will be alright.

Konny and Sam,
The two angels in my life.
I thank God everday for you two,
And I thank you for ending all my strifes.

Konny,
Your smile brightens up the the bleakes of days.
And Sam,
Your words warm my heart.
Thank you, God, for allowing our paths in life to cross,
I pray they never part.I wrote this poem for two of my best friends.
