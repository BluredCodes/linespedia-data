title: "Don't End Your Life"
writer: "meganmb2004"

Before you decide to do it,
To end your own life,
Just think of those that love you.
Just please, drop that knife.

You have so much to live for,
Please believe it's true.
If you, my friend, were no longer living,
I don't know what I'd do.

You bring so much joy to my life,
I just think you need to know.
So please, before you kill yourself,
Know I dearly love you so.

I know things seem hard right now,
And it seems everyone's against you.
Just know that whatever obstacle comes your way,
I'll be here to help you through.

The tears are gathering in my eyes.
Just please, listen to the words I say.
If you don't want to live for yourself,
Live for me, I beg you, take on another day.

I need you here with me,
To share my laughter and my cries.
Before you decide to do it, just think of me;
I pray I'm enough to save your life.
