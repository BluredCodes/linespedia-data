---
title: "Our Time to Part"
writer: "meganmb2004"

I feel as if I'm hurting you,
With the words that I say.
I've told you that I love you,
And then I just took that away.

Why do you still say you love me,
If I can't respond the same way?
Why do you tell me you always want to be with me,
If I'm not sure we'll be together for another day?

I do care for you,
And hopefully that's something you'll always know.
We were just not meant to be,
So eventually we'll just have to let eachother go.

I need to be true to myself,
And I need to be true to my heart.
I'm sorry I put you through this,
I think it's our time to finally part.

Hopefully this isn't too hard on you,
Because I know it is for me.
Just know you have a place inside my heart,
And there you'll remain to be.
