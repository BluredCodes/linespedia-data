Why do I keep on hurting you,
If it just keeps hurting me?
Why is it that when I talk to you,
I'm not the person I want to be?

Everything is my fault,
I've known that from the start.
I'm sorry I've caused you so much pain,
I'm sorry I've crushed your heart.

I wanted this to work,
Believe me, I would've given anything.
It just takes two hearts to have true love,
And my heart's not in it, I don't think.

I don't mean to hurt you with these words,
I'm not writing this to be cruel.
I just need to show you the reality of things,
And to my heart I must be true.

I'm sorry things aren't working out,
I would have loved them to.
I'm sorry for everything I've done,
For all the pain I may be causing you.
