Your sky is the limit,
And I can see yours is never ending.
You have the potential inside of you,
All you have to do is keep trying.

Never quit,
Even when you feel as if your just going to fail.
You might win, you might not,
But you can never tell.

Sure, things may seem difficult,
They will from time to time.
When you overcome a great challenge,
That will be your time to shine.

Never give up,
Always see things through.
Your sky is the limit,
