Follow your heart,
Do what you want to do.
Reach for the stars,
Let your true light shine through.

Nothing is impossible,
No matter what it may seem.
I know you can do anything,
From the very easy to the extreme.

No matter how difficult it may be,
You should always see it through.
You should always do your best,
And the best will come to you.

Follow your dreams,
And don't let aynone stand in your way.
Just stay focused on your goal,
Don't listen to the negative that people say.

I know you can do it,
I have complete faith in you.
Just reach for the stars,
And let your true light shine through.2001, Tell me what you think.  :)
