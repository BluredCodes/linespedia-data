Mommy, why is the world fighting?Why is brother going to war?I know I said I'd be strong for him,But Mommy, I don't want him to go anymore.Mommy, where is Daddy?Why didn't he ever come back from work?Can we visit him and make sure he's okay?I remember he works in that big tower.Mommy, who are all those people,The one's yelling on t.v.?Mommy, why is there so much hate in their voice?How come so many people are dieing from disease?Mommy what's wrong?Mommy, please don't cry.I'm sure Daddy will be home soon,Mommy, I'm sure big brother will be fine.Tell me what you think :)
