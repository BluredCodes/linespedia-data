Your happiness comes first,
Don't ever think differently.
Whatever you do in life,
Make sure you do it happily.

If a decision lies before you,
And with something you need to part,
Make your decision wisely,
Do what's in your heart.

Do what you really want,
Listen carefully to what's inside.
When your head and your heart are saying the same thing,
That's when you'll know it's right.

Just ease your pain,
Do whatever you need to do.
Your decision is your choice,
Remember, no one can take that away from you.
