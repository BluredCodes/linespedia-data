I want to see your heart,
With just one glimpse into your eyes.
I want to show you the feelings,
That I harbor for you inside.

I long to fall asleep by your voice,
And wake up to your smile.
I long to tell you your the one,
That makes my heart beat worth while.

I dream of the day I know your truly mine,
The day that our heart beats as one.
I dream of growing old with you,
And loving you until my last breath is done.

I pray that your mine to stay,
That our love was truly meant to be.
I pray that our love is strong enough to outlast anything,
And that it will never cease.
