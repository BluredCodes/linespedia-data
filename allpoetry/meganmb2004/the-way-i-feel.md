---
title: "The Way I Feel"
writer: "meganmb2004"

How can I feel this way,
About someone I'm not suppose to?
If I follow my heart there's so much to gain,
Yet, if I do, there's so much I can loose.

I feel betrayed by you,
And still feel love for you.
My mind doesn't know what to think anymore,
And my heart doesn't know what to do.

I want to be in your arms,
But then, I want to push you away.
I don't want you to be in my life,
But then, I want you to stay.

You hurt me in so many ways,
You're one person who I thought would never.
You said you wouldn't ever do that to me,
And yet, I am here deciding if we should stay together.

You've put so many smiles on my face,
And have given the gift to love again to me.
You've caused me to forget about the past,
But now I have to decide if we were really meant to be.

It's funny how you can fill my life with so much pain,
Still, so much joy in one instance.
It's funny how you can leave me so confused,
And make me decide if I still want your presence.
