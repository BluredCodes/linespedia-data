---
title: "I Can't Bare to Hurt You"
writer: "meganmb2004"

I don't want to hurt you any longer,
So I need to let you go.
I can't stand to hear the pain in your voice,
So I need to leave you alone.

Just please walk away.
Don't make this harder than it already is.
Don't tell me you love me,
And that having me is the only way you can live.

I can tell it's hurting you;
Every 'I love you' I can't say.
I know you want to hear it,
But I can't keep saying things I'll just take away.

You're a great person,
So I know you'll find someone who's good to you.
I'm sorry we weren't meant to be,
And I'm sorry I couldn't love you the way I was supposed to.

I need to be strong.
The decision I've made is what I need to do.
I need to let you go,
Because I can no longer bare to hurt you.
