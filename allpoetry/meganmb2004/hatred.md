The world is filled with hatred,
And blinded by fear.
We have to fight to survive,
If we want to see another year.

People die here every second
In this disease stricken place.
Babies without a chance to live,
Leave their mothers with tears on her face.

War kills brave people,
The ones that promised to come back home.
A family is now left without their father,
And their mother must raise her kids alone.

Violence has so many victims,
Even the innocent are now dead.
A little girl pierced with a bullet
While she was innocently lying in bed.

Fear is blinding children's eyes,
Until they can't see what they want to become.
Instead of bettering themselves with knowledge,
They feel they must protect themselves with guns.

It gets worse everday,
And still the hated won't end.
When will all the violence stop?
And when will the peace begin?
