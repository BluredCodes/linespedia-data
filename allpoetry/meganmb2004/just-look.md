Look past her face,
And into her heart.
She's a girl of beauty.
She's filled with hopes and dreams,
And she can fulfill them
If she just reaches for the stars.

Look deep into her eyes,
And into her soul,
The happiness trapped within,
The sadness she can't control.

Listen beyond the laughs.
The sadness that is covered.
The sorrow that's in her voice,
And the happiness that has been taken from her.This is one of my older poems when i first started writing a lot.
