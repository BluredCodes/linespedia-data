title: "The Road Towards Your Dream"

Everyone takes a path in life,A path that decides their success.The road you choose you must stay on,So hopefully you'll choose the best.You've chosen the road few could travel,And have chosen not to quit.You can finally reach the end,If you push yourself a bit.When the road seems dark and winding,And you feel you just can't win,Just remember in your heart and mindThere's a light waiting at the end.I know the road you're trudgingSeems to be all uphill.I believe you can climb it,And I know you eventually will.I know the road seems long,A lot longer than it really may be.But keep taking that same path,Keep walking towards your dream.
