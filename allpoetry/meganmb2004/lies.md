He took her heart,
He left her tears.
She's loved him forever,
He just didn't care.

He said he'd never leave her,
He said he'd always care.
He said a lot of lies,
Just to keep her near.

He thought the games were fun,
She thought he was sincere,
And when the games were done,
He left her standing there.This is one of my earlier poems when i first started writing.
