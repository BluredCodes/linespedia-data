Everything I've accomplished,
I owe it all to you.
You've been there through the good and bad,
And have helped to guide me through.

Your words are the strength I need,
To gain my second wind.
When I think I can no longer do it
I hear your encouraging words in my head.

I know I put you through a lot,
And it's not always easy raising me.
But you've done a great job,
And I've grown up happily.

You're two of the best people I know,
Or have ever come across.
I'm previlaged to have you as parents,
To teach me from right and wrong.

Thank you for everything,
And I just want you to know,
I love you and I appreciate you,
Even though it may not always show.tell me what you think :)
