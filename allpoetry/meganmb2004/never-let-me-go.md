---
title: "Never Let Me Go"
writer: "meganmb2004"

With one glimpse into your eyes,
I'm floating with the stars.
Every time that you hug me,
Your loving touch warms my heart.

In the comfort of your arms,
Is where I know I'll always be safe.
And I know I'll always find security,
With just one look upon your face.

With every smile you share with me,
I fall more and more for you.
Your words explain your love for me,
And I trust these words to be true.

With one gentle kiss,
I slowly melt away.
I want to be like this forever,
In your arms is where I want to stay.

With every warm embrace,
I can feel your gentle soul.
Just hold me and tell me you love me,
And never let me go.Tell me what you think.  :)
