Life's flower is something delicate.If sheltered too much it withers with sorrow,When able to absorb the sunshine it stands high with happiness.Live in the sunshine and never in darkness.Live each day in your life so it won't wither away.Your life's flower may go through storms,It may be picked and stepped upon,But remember, it will be admired for all its worth and beauty.Live among the sunshine and green grass around you.Absorb all the light and joy possible.Life's flower is something delicate,Allow it to stand high with happiness.
