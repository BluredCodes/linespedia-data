True friends are a precious gift,
And you've been a great gift through the years.
We've made it through the laughs strongly,
And we've been even stronger though the tears.

Friends like you are hard to find,
The kind that will lend their shoulder when you need to cry.
And will ease your pain just with silent presence,
And will push you along when you can no longer try.

You're such a great person,
With a beautiful heart and soul.
You have such a generous nature,
And you're someone I'm honored to know.Tell me what you think :)
