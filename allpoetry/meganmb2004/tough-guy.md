You think you're tough?
And that your words can phase me?
Well think again,
I see the things in you the rest of the world sees.

Yeah, you may think you're smooth,
Yelling words at me that aren't true.
The people that you call your friends,
Are simply the people who have the most pity for you.

Are you proud of yourself?
And the things that you do?
Don't you know that the way you act,
Only allow people to see the real jerk in you?

That night you passed the line,
And brought the people I love most in the fight.
Just to keep myself from killing you,
I held back with all my might.

Yeah, you're a real tough guy,
Trying to hurt girls with words that aren't true.
Just remember if you ever cross my path again,
