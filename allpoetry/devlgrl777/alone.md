aLoNethe overwhelming feeling of lonelinessseeks truth, which lies in the darkness of someplace unknownforgetting the beauty of passion and love for lifecare leaves all words behindnothing is shard or saidunwillingness fills the world before mewhispers flow threw my earslies......inside...you....revolve around lies to hide the truthfor fear of optimistic eyeswaiting for the mortifying moment to lash outthe temptation of death can conceive unreal thoughts of insanity oblivious to the mindimpeccable to the outside worldwhispers dragging on in the mindless tunnels of the heartrippng memories from their places shattering any speck of happiness leftleaving dark...black..hatred houghtsinner fear escpes threw the eyesi wish i could just be awayi'm tired......always isolatedwaiting...wanting the empty moments to end feeling alone....again....closed eyes see the vastness of darkimerging demands...lies told to truthbelieving only words from the mindseen to be real...but are not
