To All the Girls

To all the girls I loved before
Fuck you.
Fuck you a million ways
A million times over
You’re a picture of beauty
In the ratholes
And dives you now haunt
Six screaming bastard brats
In the background
A check from whatever state 
You happen to be living off of
Many nights drunk and raging 
At me?
For not being a willing victim
Not caving in and saying meekly
Yes I will bow to you
And all those years of pain
Sleep now in your lumpy beds
And drift into memories
Dark vengeful night.
