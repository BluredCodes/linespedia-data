---
title: "To Never Know What Love Is"
writer: "Michael Dennis Rivers"
slug: "to-never-know-what-love-is"

Later you see that those big bright “elations”
Are not all you thought they were.
Their “shine” dims after a while
And eventually becomes dark, even black
Like the rot on your teeth, so close
Yet so abhorred.  Time… time buddy,
That’s all it takes and then it takes you.
And all the love you think you ever had 
In your pissy little life turns out to be just
So much leaky goo oozing out of your 
Sorry corpse.  A corpse that, while it stalked 
This sad planet, never, never knew what love was
Or ever had it...  Sad, sad thing… to beat about
A drum and never hear the boom, boom, boom.
Never know what the moving hands and heavy
Strikes on the skin were all about.
