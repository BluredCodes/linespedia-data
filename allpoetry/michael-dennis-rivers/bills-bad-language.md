Bill’s (Offensive language)
(Barbed wire fences, buried in trees,
only hurt when the wind blows)

Each day, when all the work is done,
We mechanics must drive 
The cars out front and park them
So they spell  “Bill’s.”

It’s a fucking pain in the ass,
It takes a long time 
And we all want to go home.

Why do we do it?
Because Bill, the owner, wants it done.
Somebody told him
It’s good advertising.

Well why doesn’t he do it;
Or get the son-of-a-bitch
Who thought it up to do it?
