The Bowler and The Strike (a poem for two voices)

She was a dear thing;_________You’re up!
His head jerked,______________He stepped to the line
He coughed,___________________and exhaled.
He took a drag._______________He inhaled deeply
“I miss her”__________________and tried to set himself
He coughed again,_____________every muscle relaxed
Releasing a cloud of smoke____second –nature,
He continued:_________________the intense concentration
“Things was touch and go.”____Lungs slowly filled
“They coulda been different”__The ball fell into its arc.
“She might be here today,”____Sliding feet
“She worked too hard__________Quivering mass of muscle
“She never let up.”___________Ambling toward the foul line.
“Then the breakdown."_________The pendulum peaked and fell.
“Her body just let go.”_______The release; nice follow through.
“I didn’t know what to do,”___A frozen finger tracked the ball
“I just watched her fade.”____As it carved a path to the pins.
“Her heart gave out.”_________SMASH! Good action, good spin.
It pumped one last time,______One last pin in a drunken wobble
outward,______________________slowed to a teetering finish
inward,_______________________and came to an impossible stop.
the muscle tightened__________May have rocked back and stood
It tried______________________or by pure chance, fallen.
“She’d be with me yet today."_Fate made the difference… 
But it didn’t,________________It hesitated…
