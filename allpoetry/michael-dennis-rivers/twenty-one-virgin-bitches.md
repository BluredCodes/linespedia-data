title: "Twenty-one Virgin Bitches"

Old Bill, my neighbor,
Fought in the Pacific Theater,
New Guinea or someplace?
Up in the mountains,
In the caves, like in Afghanistan.
They tied dynamite on the dogs,
like Muslim martyrs and sent them
In to find the Japs.
I wonder if they told the dogs,
German Shepherds, that they
Were going to each get
Twenty-one virgin bitches?Hell no, I won't go!
