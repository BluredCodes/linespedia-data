I started a new job 
And wanted to know
How long it would take me
To come and to go. 
I started my stopwatch
And drove my car so
I was going ‘bout average
Not fast and not slow, 
But I was distracted,
I’d only pressed “GO.”
And wound up later
With nothing to show
For my still running stopwatch
From three days ago. 
I looked at the numbers
Their pulsating flow
And said to myself...
"Fuckit
I don’t want to know.”
