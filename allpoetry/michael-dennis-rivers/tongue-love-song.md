Tongue Love Song

Hey baby,
I wanna stick my tongue
Down your throat,
Up your nasal passage
And out your nose.

I wanna tie it in a knot
So I can keep your face
Close to mine.

I wanna stick it in one ear
And out the other 
And tie it in a pretty bow
On top of your head.

I wanna run my tongue 
Through your digestive track
And out your rear
And tie it in a knot
‘Round your leg.

I wanna stick my tongue 
Under your fingernail
And out your toenail

I’ll synch it tight
‘Til you’re a little ball.
Then I’ll swallow you
So you’ll always be part of me
And I won’t have to worry
No more.I did this a long time ago... I'm so silly!
