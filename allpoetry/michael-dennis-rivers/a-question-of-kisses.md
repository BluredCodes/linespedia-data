(“It is not hard to see, all
that is needed is patience.”
Saramaga)

How can the so nice music
Be created by such evil people?
Maybe the deep look takes time?
How is it that some voices,
Some faces that excite the heart
Come forth from ugliness?
Is the first kiss blindness?
Is it only after kiss after
Kiss after kiss that the lips
Quiver and shy away from
The mole and wrinkle?
In the end does only true beauty out?
Is the rest buried in gravity’s settling?
When does the beast give up,
Lie still and submit?
