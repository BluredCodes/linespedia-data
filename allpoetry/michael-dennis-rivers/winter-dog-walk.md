Winter Dog Walk

Nightly after work
We go,
The wife and I, 
With dogs in tow,
Or tow us ‘til
We come on ice
And snow.

Then she to me:
Hey, Give us an arm.”
Once by the ice
She’ll say,
“Thanks.”

And I’ll reply
“No…tis I 
Who must thank you!
Ask your dogs, 
For stability,
Four legs 
Are twice 
As good
As two.”
