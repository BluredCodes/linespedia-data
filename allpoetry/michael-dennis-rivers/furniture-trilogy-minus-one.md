I Round Table With Knights

Merlin built the table,
Not the knights.
They were vain and faulted
And what a table!
Huge hardwood segments
perfectly put together,
oiled and polished,
flawless.
Knights of the round table???
No, it should be called:
"Round Table with Knights."


II Desk Pictures

She was a prolific breeder;
as were her children.
So the little photos
on her desk
of births, birthdays,
weddings and the like
multiplied in a
geometric progression.
Finally she had to quit
because there was no room 
on her desk 
for her work.Two eliminated by popular demand!
