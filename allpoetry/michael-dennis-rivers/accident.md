Accident

There was a girl crying
In muted hysteria.
I stopped my motorcycle
Beside the fatal car,
Looked in

And saw a woman die.
I saw life leave her lips.
She was slouched against the driver’s door,
Eyes wide open,
Blood trickled from her ear.

I saw the man who killed her
Walking ‘round his Jeep
In a circle of confusion.
She had pulled out of a side street
As he came fast around the curve.
