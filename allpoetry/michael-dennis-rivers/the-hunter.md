The HunterIn early spring,Orion stalkingfrom his winter prominence,down low and dimin eastern sky,takes with himhis bright dog, Sirius.There is a March windin my face.'Soon,' I think,'we will save daylightand the bears will dominate the northern sky.'The wind blows harderas I rush west to tell Orionwhere the bears are.But it's too late,he's gone down and my nightfacesees only his milky path.
