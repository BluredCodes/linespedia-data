We drank a drunk of drinks
And sang a juke of songs.
We danced and danced the night
Then fucked ‘til nearly dawn.

You loved me dearly then
And now you’re clearly gone.
What happened to our love?
What happened to our song? 

I s’pose like dance-a-thons,
And like some jukebox songs,
The ones we play too long…
When all the liquor’s gone… 
Some things that seem so strong
Just never last that long.
