Keeping the Young Wife in Bananas

It’s hard, you know, being old 
With a young wife
Whose young life
Keeps me bold.
I work at it,
Keeping the bananas firm for her
They have a tendency,
As they ripen, to become soft.
Their skin darkens and they shrivel
But I work at it.
