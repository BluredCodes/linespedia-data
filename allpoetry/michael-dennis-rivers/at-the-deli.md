At the Deli

I went to the market
With my wife the other day.
She sent me to the deli counter
To get some sliced ham and turkey.
I said to the woman behind the counter:
“A pound of ham and a pound of turkey please.” 
She took a block of ham off the shelf
And put it on the slicing machine.
She grabbed the handle and jerked it
Back and forth, back and forth, back and forth.
She did the same to the turkey.Men!
