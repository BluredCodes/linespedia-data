---
title: "Love Again"
writer: "Michael Dennis Rivers"

To love and lose and pain
And love and lose again
And again and pain.

Better this to choose
Than never love again
For fear to lose.This is not about people, it's about loving animals that have a shorter life span that humans so you lose them but I guess it could be about people too?
