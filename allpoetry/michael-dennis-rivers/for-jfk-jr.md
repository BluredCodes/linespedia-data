For JFK Jr.

The mirror slipped
From her hand
As she walked along.
It rolled and flipped
Reflecting all around it
And hit the ground
And broke into a million pieces.
Each piece reflected something different.
She stopped momentarily,
Look down at the spectacle,
Then continued along.
