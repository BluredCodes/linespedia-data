Bug God

Who is this bug,
Why is he here,
And where did he come from?

What does he want,
Ugly thing,
Crawling across my vision
Affront to my eyes.
I shall chuff him
With my foot
And jeet him into eternity.

Then, Godlike, 
In my munificence,
I let him live.
