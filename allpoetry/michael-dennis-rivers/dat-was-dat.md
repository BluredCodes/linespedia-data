Dat Was Dat

“Who dat?”

“Oh, dat da fat cat.”

“Da fat cat
Dat splat dat rat
Dat sat on dat mat?”

“Yah, dat da cat.”

“Why dat fat cat splat dat rat?”

“Well, dat rat got a bat
and  splat dat knat
dat shat on his hat.”

“Ohhh... who tat dat rat 
ta use da bat?”

“Hummmm? I tink da Cat did dat?”

“You mean da Cat done tat the rat
to use da bat  before he didem splat?”

“Yah, I tink da fat cat did dat?”

“But how ‘bout da Knat?
Who tat da knat ta shat
In da rat’s hat?”

“Hummm, I tink da Cat did dat too?
Before he gibbem da gat.”

“You mean da Cat gib da knat a gat!
Why he do dat?”

“Well so da knat can go rat-a-tat tat
At da bat.”

“You mean da bat dat da rat go splat wit?”

“No, no, da udder bat, da flyin bat.
Da flyin bat dat was at da londreymat.”

“Da Londreymat! 
What da bat doin at da Londreymat?”

“Well he was washin da hat 
dat da knat shat on.
Yah, dat and da mat dat had da splat rat on.”

“Well when da knat go rat-a-tat tat wid da gat
did he get da bat?”

“No, he miss da whole londreymat 
And kill da fat cat
An dat was dat...
Yah, dat was dat.”I didn't check all the words in the dictionary that end in "at" for more animals.  Maybe I'll add on to this later.
