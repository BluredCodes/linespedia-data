“Queer,” I think, 
On dog walk
In this lonely field,
“Why are all these Binkies here?”
The morning paper
Makes the answer clear;
A Rave,
Ecstasy.
All night music plays 
While druggie girlz
With grinding teeth
Suck baby Binkies.

They sway and dance
With boyz in baggy pants
Who later, in this dark and lonely field, 
Have them spit their Binkys 
For something dinky.
