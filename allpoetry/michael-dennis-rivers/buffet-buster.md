title: "Buffet Buster"

He parked his DeVille conspicuously 
In front of the Uptown Grille and
Sneaked over to the Hong Kong Buffet
Which was hard to do because he was
Such a very big man.  He could eat a lot too.
Sometimes he wouldn’t eat for days
Before he made these weekly trips.
He liked the Buffet and had a 20% off
Coupon that he’d cut from the newspaper.
He didn’t have to tip anybody either.
It was unusual for him not to make 
At least ten or twelve trips up for the food.
He’d return with his plate overflowing.
You knew when he was getting done,
He’d make funny noises and puff up his cheeks.
The owner, Harry, blanched when he came in,
He was single-handedly putting him out of business.
