Morning of spring,
Evening of fall,
The quiet light,
Not so dark,
Not so bright,
Not so hot, not so cold,
Like the faces we see
With forgotten names.
Vague memories of passing people
In the painful cold of winter,
In the suffering heat of summer.

It is the very good 
And very bad 
That we best remember, 
Not the not so good, 
Nor the not so bad.
