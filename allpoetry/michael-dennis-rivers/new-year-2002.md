New Year 2002 
(genus: Homarus Americanus)

For New Year’s
Celebration
A boiled live
Crustacean.

We sit with covered eyes
His claws from bag are pryed
He screams, “I doanwannadie,
Repatriation!”

In seconds he is dead
In minutes he is red
In hours we are fed,
Mastication.

With baked potato,
Corn and bread,
The year in born in
Libation.

And every year, 
When the day arrives, I say
“Never again, such cruelty, boiled alive!”
Expiation.
