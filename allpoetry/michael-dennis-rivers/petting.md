Petting

It was the beginning of love,
With a Catholic upbringing, 
No sex education, 
And a fear of the unknown.

How I got such a nice girl 
or why she liked me,
I’ll never know.
I knew I loved her, 
I must have, 
all those long walks 
from the Girl’s Club Dances 
all the way to her house 
in the freezing cold.  

I had an ulterior motive.  
She was a pretty girl 
with budding breasts 
and long blond hair. 

We walked up the hill 
in the crisp dark,
holding hands 
and lightly chatting.  
She was a quiet girl.   

When we reached her house, 
we reached the moment 
we were both waiting for, 
the good night kiss.  

It was dark in front of her house, 
no street lights. 
a lonely street, 
few car went by.  
We stood facing each other, 
holding hands.  
I pulled her to me and 
our lips met in a little kiss.  
A car passed; we parted.  

Now we stood again 
Wrapped together 
drawing warmth 
from each other 
after the long walk. 
There was no talk,  
we just looked at each other. 
I gave her a smile,
She laughed nervously.
 
I couldn’t believe 
she was holding me to her 
as closely as I held her.  
We kissed again for a longer time.  
I pressed her to me, 
At first with
my butt pulled back 
so my little “stiff thing” 
wouldn’t touch her.  
  
Eventually I rationalized, 
she had a thick coat 
that came between us 
so she probably 
wouldn’t mind me 
pressing.  

No feeling her up.  
I didn’t grope her in any way.  
She was my girlfriend, 
not some slut.  
She was my first girlfriend.  

I manage to slip my hands 
inside her coat after a while.  
I don’t remember how 
I opened her coat.  
Maybe I just boldly unbuttoned it.  
Now I was warmer and closer.
  
We squeezed each other and kissed 
and hugged and pressed against each other 
until eventually the evening culminated  
in a little “Ohhh,” 
in sticky underwear,
in a last goodnight kiss,
in a long, cold walk home 
with dreamy eyes,
in a goofy face
