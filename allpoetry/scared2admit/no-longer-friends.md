title: "No Longer Friends"

I sit directly behind you
Watching as you read
I wonder what your life is like now,
Without a best friend in me

You seem really happy
But I've seen the way you look at me
And I wonder if you're remembering
All the laughter we once shared

Sometimes you'll talk to me
And I get  really confused
Cause you've made it clear we're no longer friends
But then you act like we are

I wish you would just talk with me
As I hate not knowing
What it was exactly
That ruined the friendship we once had.
