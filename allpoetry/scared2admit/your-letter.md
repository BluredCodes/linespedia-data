Your letter came today
As i read it, i cried
As my thoughts were invaded
By memories of our past

I still think of you,
Each and everyday
The letter made me sad
Knowing you were never to come back...
Never returning to what our lives once were

Your letter made me realize
You are now forever gone
Now someone else can dream of you
For i've finally given up

I cried because i cant remember
Your voice, your smell, your face...
You're now only a distant memory
Of the best time of my life.
