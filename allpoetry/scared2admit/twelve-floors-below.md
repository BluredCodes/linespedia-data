title: "Twelve Floors Below"
writer: "scared2admit"

As I look down through
The frosted glass
Tears begin to fill my eyes
And quietly roll down my checks
My heart starts to beat
That much faster,
My hand starts to sweat and shake
And I can barely write.
I shiver, even though I'm not clold
Trying to forget the pictures
Flashing through my mind
I look down once again
The building feels like it's moving
And I start to feel dizzy
Twelve floors below my feet
Cold, hard concrete awaits,
I don't contemplate jumping
Just think of a time,Not too long ago,
When i would of been desperate to fall
I can't get the images out
They sufforcate me
For a minute I return to my old hell
And I break down and cry
Cause it's just too much to take.
