---
title: "Why Me?"

I heard your voice
and my knees started to shake.
I was nervous.
I filled my head with wonder
as to why you would call me.
As I talked more with you,
it became easier 
to say what I wanted to. 
You made me laugh
and filled my heart with joy.
But then my hands started to shake.
My palms became clammy and sweaty
Chills went flowing down my spine.
Questions exploded in my mind.
Could I ever learn to trust
or love again?
Would he be able to understand,
and help me through the hurt
and pain, lingering from the past?
Is this just the start
of a beautiful friendship,
or is this the start of a love I never thought I'd find?
