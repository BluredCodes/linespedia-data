I feel like trash.
The kind of trash
only rats eat.
I scream for
somebody...
anybody...
to hear me.
Droplets of water,
well up in my eyes.
People wait, and watch,
for the first tear drop.
This time...
I won't.
This time...
I can't.
I'm all dried out.
There's nothing left
of me now.
