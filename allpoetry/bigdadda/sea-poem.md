title: "Sea Poem"

The waves are crashing and smashing 
Still waiting there for me, 
for once you belong to the sea 
She'll never set you free.

I remember her subtle beginnings, 
Soft waves on a moonlit night, 
Carrying me far from my origins 
While sampling her ancient delights, 
That had captured so many before. 
And as you slowly sail from the shore 
Gently behind you, she closes the door.

I left her on the quay 
Waving hands and tear filled eyes, 
Shouting golden promises that would never come to be. 
"My Darling" she cried"Your life is here with me!, 
Never give it to that other one, The Sea." 
I was filled by the moment and blown by the winds, 
Not knowing how soon 
I would have to wait on her whims.

She gave me foreign places 
With a myriad of different faces. 
So much to see and feel 
While all the time my wounds healed. 
Till I'd forgotten what I'd left behind, 
Spurred on by her relentless pace 
The other one was sent from mind, 
I couldn't even remember her face.

Then as if to show her great strength 
She took me back to my journey's start. 
Saying go on leave me, if you have the heart, 
But remember upon you, I've put my mark, 
Try something else, if that's what you desire. 
Try if you can, to put out my fire. 
But it can never the same be 
For a man who has worked upon the sea.
