When I have died
And there comes an end
Will I have lost
My best friend?

If I knew the date 
Would it change my lifestyle?
And would I just greet it
With a happy smile?

If the days are full
And the nights content
Should I meet death 
Full of resent?

When I have died
And there comes an end
Will I have lost 
My best friend?
