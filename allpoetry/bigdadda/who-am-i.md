---
title: "Who am I"
writer: "BigDadda"
slug: "who-am-i"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I’m in your head for questions unknown,
I’m in your bed when you’re all alone.
I walk with you though you don’t know I’m there,
I talk with you with words unheard.

You ignore me and deny my existence
Then you fight me with all your resistance.
You’d prefer it if I just went away,
Left you alone to go your own way.

I cannot make your journey easier to travel
But when you arrive, all the mysteries will unravel.
I’m with you till the end, as enemy or friend
We’ll always be together – you cannot change it ever.

I hope you can find me before its too late
Come to your senses and learn to relate
Feel with me and learn to see
Be with me and it all becomes real

I never was or ever will
I’m never here or ever there.
But when it’s all over, said and done
I’ll be with you my friend – because we are one.
