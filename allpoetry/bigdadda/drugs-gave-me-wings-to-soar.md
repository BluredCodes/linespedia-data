Drugs gave me wings to soar
Then it took away the sky,
It gave lots of beautiful things
Then it made me cry.
I was sleeping in the forest
I was swimming in the falls,
Lost in the land of Never Never 
And did not hear you call.

Like Pegasus with broken wings
The horse that cannot fly,
Drugs gave me wings to soar
Then it took away the sky.

And when the music sang to me
Her voice was sweet and clear.
She filled the night with melody
And held me very near.
Now the bells toll that awful sound
And I am all alone
Lost, afraid, I n a dark room
Nowhere to be found.

I am drowning in a sea of Shit
The stench is driving me mad
Searching hard for my next hit 
And feeling oh so bad
My blood is sick, The Flesh is dead
The needle marks are there,
The sad eyes hang out my head
And I wish sometimes I cared.

Like Pegasus with broken wings 
The horse that cannot fly,
Drugs gave me wings to soar
Then it took away the sky.
