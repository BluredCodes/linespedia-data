Peggy Peggy I am in love with you
Peggy Peggy Now you are gone and I'm blue

It used to be such fun with you,
You made the sun shine thru and thru.

Peggy Peggy I am in love with you
Peggy Peggy Now you are gone and I'm blue

I loved the touch of your embrace, 
I love the memory of your face.

Peggy Peggy I am in love with you
Peggy Peggy Now you are gone and I'm blue.

A Mothers love can never die
It resides with you all the time,
On the wings of Eagle she does fly
Soaring right across the Sky.

Peggy Peggy I am in love with you
Peggy Peggy Now you are gone and I'm blue
