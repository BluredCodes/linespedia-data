My old friend darkness greets me
As my new mother embraces me
Revitalizing my body and mind
With the coldness of her touch.

Her immenseness envelops me
As she draws me deep down into her womb.
Glazing back to the surface – disappearing
The moon flickers – two worlds removed.

Freed of all thought
I glide deeper – deeper into her soul
Senses awed
Peace absolute

On the bottom now
Sister tide rocks me like a newborn child
Gently to and fro
Like the sands on the ocean bed – my bed

Four bells – come up
FOUR BELLS – SHUT UP.
Ascent now, 
Time to leave – time to grieve.

A fight to 80 feet
Stop for a second and she pulls you back down
Down to rest – down to sleep
Arms to the side – look to the surface

60 feet now – gently rising – no effort required.
The hull of the boat,
The eye of the moon,
The world is waiting.

Break the surface – thumb in the air
Danger everywhere.
“Diver on the surface indicating well”  
Diver on the surface – back into hell.I am trying to express the personal relationship you can develop with the ocean/sea 
four bells is a miltary signal sent to the diver  to come up immedately.

Other expressions and actions are also military use.
