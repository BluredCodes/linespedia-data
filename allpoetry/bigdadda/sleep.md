title: "Sleep"
writer: "BigDadda"

I was sleeping one day
When I awoke in the middle of a life.
And tried to find value 
In the things I did that day.

The way the hours took me
The company I kept.
Food consumed by me
And places where I slept.

To find merit in my actions
And reason in my being
I found the place where Angels sleep
Is not all that is seems.

The stupor of gratification
Had come to live with me,
And I must learn to control it
If I am to be free.

Free from sleeping the day
Of my one and only life.
To feel the pain of accomplishment
No matter what the price.

And I sometimes wonder,
If I slept till my death.
What people would miss my touch?
What memories would I have left?
