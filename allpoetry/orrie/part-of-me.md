Dark thoughts spilling over
Caressing my skin
Whispering softly
...let me in
Intruding and pulsing
It's fingers  now spread
Down through my body
Filling my head
Torrents of pain
Wash over my eyes
Kissing my eyelids
With soft gentle sighs
The darkness I am
And always shall be
It has always been
A part of me
Feeling so helpless
I reach out for the pain
And pull it towards me
Again..and againI seem to have a problem finishing a piece now. I used to be able to get my feelings out the way I wanted . But now? I get halfway through and I find it sooo hard to finish it properly. Does anyone else see it in this piece?
