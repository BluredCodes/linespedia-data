title: "Love in Darkness"

Love in darkness
Shine your light.
Rid me of sorrow,
Rid me of strife.
Love in darkness
Set me free.
Remove my shackles,
Let me out of this cage.
Love in darkness
Shine your light.
Keep me warm
On this cold night.
Hold my hand
Tell me it'll be all right.
Love in darkness
Set me free.
Stop the hurting,
Fill this empty space.
Calm my anger,
Soothe my rage.
Love in darkness
I cannot see you
Please, shine your light.
Come to me
Let me lie in your arms.
Love in darkness
I give you my heart.
Please make yourself known.
End my lonliness
And love me, please love me
Like all my dreams have shown.
