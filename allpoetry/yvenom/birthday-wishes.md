Your special day has come again.
A very special time, my friend.
For today, you are a queen,
But a beauty you've always been.
I'm sorry to say I couldn't bake
For you a birthday cake.
So I say to you let's just pretend
That I actually did, instead.
A ring of icing roses and beautiful swirls
Are on this cake surrounded by pearls.
And on the top with a fiery blaze
Are the birthday candles to catch your gaze.
Pretend they're there, and blow them out
And I'll try to grant you your birthday wish.
Do this now, and then we'll see
If I can grant what you wish of me.
If you want, I'll grant you two.
Tell me now what I can do.
But mind you, I don't do dishes,
So make some special birthday wishes.
Birthday wishes to take you to the stars.
Look, there's Mercury, Venus, and Mars.
Birthday wishes of spectacular dreams,
So many things to wish for, it seems. 
Birthday wishes of true love's kiss
But I'm unable to grant such bliss.
Birthday wishes of summer's dream,
Birthday wishes of moonlight's gleam.
Birthday wishes from the heart
Because that's where all wishes start.
Birthday wishes to have such fun
And a huge smile when it's done.
Birthday wishes of happiness' tears
To wash away all your fears.
Birthday wishes to fly like a dove
And to have all the things that you love.
Birthday wishes strong and true
And I promise I'll try
To make your every wish come true.I wrote this for a friend on her 16th birthday. I think, though, that she took it out of context cause she acted kinda weirded out. I was just trying to be nice.
