Bold Charger was my horse's name.
Ahh! Such a splendid fellow.
Imagine my undying shame.
When folks named him marshmellow.

Its really sad, but I see why,
They took that horse away.
He ate a whole marshmellow pie,
And then refused to pay.

They caught him in the deli isle,
His saddlebags were stuffed.
You should have seen the people smile,
To see my old pal cuffed.

Can,t take that pony to the store.
I made the judge a deal.
I'll keep him tied outside the door,
Pending his appeal.

Alas poor horse, His twig was bent.
Now he's a crooked tree.
Good thing that pony wasn't sent,
To the penitentiary.With Love for the legendary Gracie Bumble
