title: "Come to me Love."

Joyous does love find me here.
I wait beneath your window dear,
As if I were at heavens gate,
With rapture I anticipate,
The warmth of your angelic grace,
Enfolding me in glad embrace.
The spark you lit becomes a fire,
Burning in me ever higher.
Come to me love as darkness falls,
The moon is high and romance calls.

Deep in my heart the secret flame,
Warms me and I call your name.
Out side your window, waiting there,
Come to me love, my angel fair.
Within its cage of flesh and bone,
My wanting heart calls to your own.
Far past this night our future lays,
For I will love you all my days.
My very souls at your command,
Come to me love and take my hand.

I long to rest, upon your breast,
The very one I have caressed,
In love I bide here in the night,
Just beyond your windows light.
Awaiting your oppertunity,
To slip away and come to me.
To share these silver moonlit hours,
Lost in this joyous love of ours.
I love you more than words can tell.
Come to me love for all is well.Come to me love.......
