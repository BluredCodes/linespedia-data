Upon this day, I make a vow.
Upon this ring I promise now.
Upon your heart I shall rely.
And love you til the day I die.

Within this church, we've just begun,
Within our hearts to be as one.
Within this veil I soft reply,
I'll love you til the day I die.

In that I give my heart to you.
In that I am this day made new.
In that you make my heart to fly,
I'll love you til the day I die.

So that you never know a doubt,
So that you never go without.
So that you need not ask me "why?".
I'll love you til the day I die

That in your every waking act.
That testifies to love as fact.
That finds your heart contains no lie.
I'll love you til the day I die.

Say that we are man and wife,
Say it now for all our life.
Say what no one can deny,
I'll love you til the day I die.
                   
             By grace our love be sweetly sealed.
             By hope our loving hearts revealed
             By all thats sacred, you and I,
             Shall love until the day we die....ANOYHER wedding poem (for latosha, who asked for it)
