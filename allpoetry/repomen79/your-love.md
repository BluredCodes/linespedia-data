Write your rainbow colored prose,
Oh gentle blooming yellow rose.
From the passion of your pen,
I draw the strength to rise again.
For by your gentle loving word,
Is my happiness secured.
And love suprises me once more.
But thats what poetry is for.

Long life, long time, long and ever longer.
By your love I find that I'm, made ever so much stronger.
We are more than petals in the wind.
We are love and loving isn't ever going to end.
I send to you a grateful prayer,
That joy be gathered bout you there,
And all the Blessings love can be.
Be yours through out eternity.

Darlin how the time flies past,
Love and only love can last.
Your love has made you precious dear,
Your grace is given without fear,
It leaves a man not much to say,
But I've loved you so much today.
And I will treasure all you bring, 
and hear the loving song you sing.
                  
                      Theres a secret restive feeling in the darkness of a heart
                      Everything put together comes apart,
                      Theres an absence of redemption in the sorrow of a soul.
                       Only loving has the power to make whole.
                       What a blessing I recieve,
                       From your love, and I believe.
                       In your love I will abide.
                       Til all sorrows put aside.For Tamara88..... Thank you so very much darlin
