title: "Her Heart did not forget"

She moved me with her tender touch,
Where I had never been,
I count myself so very much,
As blessed above all men.
Even I, though years go  by,
Do love her greater yet.
I am fullfilled and she is why,
Her heart did not forget.

The shadows of the evening fall,
Where sunlight once did shine.
So the years diminish all.
Yet do not threaten mine.
Her light will shine throughout the years. 
As long as heaven let.
I hold her close with tender tears,
Her heart did not forget.

Within my soul, serenity,
Without her, I am not.
I held her grace with majesty,
Yet still my heart forgot.
The corner stone Of all we are,
In wich our love is set.
Her joy thats taken me so far,
Her heart did not forget.

We will not fear the storms that blow,
Nor dread the dark of night.
For I am sheltered in the glow,
Of her warm and loving light.
I've heard that couples grow apart,
With time, We haven't yet.
She's still my life, my strength, my heart,
Cause her heart did not forget

Run to shore oh angry sea,
Rage futile, howling storm.
I reckon with eternity,
Her love shall keep me warm.
And if the mountain fade and fall,
We will not be upset.
For in this house, our love is all,
And her heart did not forget.If only........
