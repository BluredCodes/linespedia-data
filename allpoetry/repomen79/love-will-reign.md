---
title: "LOVE Will Reign!"

Although it seems a dark road with a future much in doubt.
A world of wealth beyond all count where the poor must do without.
In a life thats filled with wanting, trouble, pain, and tears,
The future can seem daunting, bound to justify your fears.
But the sun shines on the poor man,
In its early morning gold.
That no amount of money can,
Allow a man to hold.
Isn't it a wonder how,
Love can change the here and now?
Bring such comfort to your soul,
Mend your heart and make it whole.
Love and let no cares decieve.
For love will reign, I do believe.

Within that mornings golden light,
Is proof against the darkest night.
Awaiting but the eye to see,
Heart believe and be set free.
Much more than animated clay,
You are the reason for the day.
And every loving thing you do,
Will find its way right back to you.
Drink deep of joy, and know rebirth.
Shout your love to the weary Earth.
That you sing a newer happier song,
For tears may fall, but nothings wrong.
They shall be dried and love remain.
I promise you, that Love will reign.JOY to the world.........The wonder of his love.
