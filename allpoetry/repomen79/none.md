EAR! why do you listen?
                 Did you think,
   that just the hoping, was a
                            song?
       
           FOOL! Hold your breath
                         Until the whole
                                     World sings along.
TEAR! did you roll your way
                     Unbidden,
                        Thinking there was sympathy?
           The stars will turn to darkness
                       Before such a thing shall be.
