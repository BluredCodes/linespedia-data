title: "number One Fan"
writer: "repomen79"

In my minds eye, I can see you as you write your poetry.
Of love and loss and winds that toss you with uncertainty.
I mourn for what I'll never own,
For darling I have never known,
A loving heart so true,
As the one I sense in you.
And every thing I write, In the stillness of the night.
Is that you might delight, in laughter love and light.

The very stars shine on us both alike.
I could no more help but love you than the clock forget to strike.
I take your every sadness to my heart.
What I wouldn't give to take your part.
But you are going your own way.
I love you but I cannot stay.
A greater heart has willed, 
That our love be unfullfilled.

Would that for one moment I behold.
The beauty that I so long to enfold.
Please know, I hear the song you sing,
And know the gladness that you bring.
To this wanting heart out there.
That speaks your name in prayer.
And loves you much more so,
Than you will ever know.
