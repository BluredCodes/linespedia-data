Blue Ocean Flowers,
For hours and hours,
I float in the sea.
That is inside of me.
Til this world disappears.
No one hears.
And the years,
Go by in slow motion.
When you float in the ocean.
And life with its tears,
Is only a wave.
The only way to behave,
Is lacksadaisicle.
Its a dreamsicle, really quite comical,
How all the small fish,
Share the same wish, that the big fish,
Don't take a bite of us.
Oh we fuss.
And put on a show.
So the big fishs know,
That we're bad.
Worse meal ya ever had,
So don't even try.
I'll tell ya why.
Cause I'm a tiny bit distasteful,
And I'm blessed with super powers.
To eat me would be wasteful,
Cause I taste like Ocean flowers.
And yeah' their blue.
They grow in the ocean, so whats it to you?
Okay, be that way.
Please don't think I care.
I'll say what I want to say.
Then float right out of there.
Yes, blue ocean flowers fine and free.
Here for a time then gone.
I make a home in the stormy sea,
For a flower carries on.
What else can save you from the wave?
Like flowers we are tossed.
Just relax, we'll float acrost.
Like gentle flowers, this life of ours.
Is filled with great and loving powers.
Look at them.
They try to swim.
Or build a boat. But you can float.
And on that note.
I'll say goodbye.
I think I see the reason why.
The ocean flower that I am,
Is as happy as a clam.Hugs
