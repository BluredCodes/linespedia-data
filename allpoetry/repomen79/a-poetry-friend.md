Oh for the joy in laughing eyes,
Of women taken by suprise,
In the essance of the lilly of the valley as it grows,
And the warmth of love and light that every happy women knows.
It would mean so very much,
For her life to be as such.
Bring her to a kinder place,
Crown her golden hair in lace.
Hold the mirror to her gaze,
As the rainbow color plays,
Acrost a face devoid of pain,
Lillies do not bloom in vain

The silence in the darkness crying out for your sweet song.
We only pass this way once, and we've waited our lives long,
For the joy that you will give,
And the life that you must live.
All of it a symphony,
A harmony of poetry.
For precious is the lilly thus revealed.
Our love for you shines brightly unconcealed.
We send our passions power,
Even in your darkest hour.
And our love for you shall form,
