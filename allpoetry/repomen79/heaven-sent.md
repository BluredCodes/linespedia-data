Of golden lifetime,shinning on.

How sweet, even the pain.

To know when all of fear is gone.

That love shall yet remain.

Don't weep my dear, our journey here,

Was given that we see.

That sorrows pass to ,disappear.

And one day we'll be free.



He see's you're tears, you're deepest fears,

And waits outside the door.

To hold you dear through all your years.

Both now and ever more.

Let not your precious love be lost.

To those who deeply need.

To be assured at any cost.

That they are loved indeed.



Climb your mountain, be a fountain.

That your gladness overflow.

Spend your love without accounting,

So in the end you know.

That joy will last when sorrows past.

And you made straight the way,

Our time upon the Earth goes fast.

And nothing gold can stay



I dreamed I met you in the night.

I was lost and so alone.

Your loving arms did so invite,

And break this heart of stone

But what of me? that I should see.

An angel on my way.

Could it truly be, that eternity,

Is the life I live today?Joy to the world. for love shall reign over all.
