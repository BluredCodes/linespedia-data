The sun is low and evening falls and tomorrow never knows.
Within her graceful garden walls, My rapture only grows.
I take this cameo to keep,
I know that we must one day sleep.
How brightly does her love shine in my eyes.
I send my own to comfort where she lies.
Run my love! all this is yours.
To one day pass, but love endures.
Yes go to the ancient of age to tell,
She tends her garden very well.
I've loved her so this very day.
And rapture walks her garden way

There will for everything a time and place.
one day I might gaze upon her face.
On an ever after morning with the air so clean and clear.
I will bring the flowers adorning my most precious angel dear.
Oh for the dreams that shall not be.
Fair take the very heart of me.
Though its offered gladly, with good will.
My love is ever with you still.
I wrote this to make sure you knew.
How much I still care for you.
And I,ve loved you a lot today.
Rapture walks your garden way.For Lurie, who can't write an insulting limerick. your precious to me darlin.
