The Tick tock man is right behind,
I hear his footsteps and I find,
That life is so much sweeter now,
And all of you more dear somehow.
Do you love? or do you not?
Loves the only wealth you got.
Tick Tock, Tock Tick,
Its enough to make you sick.
Tock tick, Tick Tock,
All of us are on the clock.
Love was beauty, beauties grand,
All of it is in your hand.
Dont let the hours slip away,
Love somebody new today,
Give the comfort that you need,
It comes back to you indeed.
Cast your cares into the wind,
Be a lover, be a friend.
Deep in your heart, believe you can,
Run faster than the Tick Tock man.With love for Tina Brannon. Thank you darlin for all your lovely comments.
