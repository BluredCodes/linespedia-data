Deeply dream your shinning hour.
Beneath the mighty sky,
For all the boasts of pomp and power.
Await their day to die.
Cherish beauty, breifly held,
But given not to keep.
For all abides to be compelled,
to likewise one day sleep.

Have for yourself this moment dear.
And all that it can be.
How glad I find you loving here.
This is eternity.
All the things you never knew.
were given that you ponder.
the joy that was begifted you.
To live in loving wonder.

How so much more than Earth or stone.
How vast and never ending.
The precious heart you call your own.
In blessed comprehending.
That all of us recieved the same.
with love and hope and prayer.
How every joy you'll ever claim.
Was also my affair.

Pin your heart upon a star.
And ride your magic dream.
For we must go so very far.
And are more than we seem.
Let me take your precious hand.
Let us call forever ours.
Its quite enough to understand.
We all are  fragile flowers.>>> Its a beautiful day! >>
