Upon each one, the rain must fall.
The mystery awaits us all.
Nothing guarentees the morrow.
All our days are spent in sorrow.
No one can know the tears you've cried.
What other will stand at you're side?

His light is set upon the hill.
And all is all by his good will.
Him who died between two thieves.
See's you're deepest pain and grieves.
What hope shall make you justified?
What other will stand at you're side.

In that we love in silent prayer.
In that we seek for comfort there
In that we hope that heavon hears.
In that the heart of man appears.
To be weak and secretly terrified.
What other will stand at you're side?

Behold the king who rode an ass.
Behold his teachings come to pass.
Behold in him are men made new.
Behold the one who died for you.
Behold that death itself has died.
What other will stand at you're side?

Who has declared the wise man blind ?
Who rules the heart that rules the mind?
Whose love for you shall ever be?
Whose love is all eternity?
Whose care for you does ever bide?
What other will stand at you're side?

You walk about uprightly here.
With strength destined to disappear.
And see you'r self looking at them.
So whom among us would condem?
The aching words you have to write.
The scream from a dream in the dead of night.
The hurting heart you cannot hide.
What other will stand at you're side?I want you to be.
free from anger and rage.
That you would be free.
And not in a cage.
