Does your deep soul speak of secret things?
Neath the sounds of a far off train.
In the reminesce that the midnight brings,
Is your dear heart filled with pain?
Number the stars, that your wanting knows,
The depths of eternity.
Sail your ship where the current flows,
For things that cannot be.

Is there anywhere, that does not share,
The grief of midnights doubt?
Hide the care, and the dark despair,
Tell yourself it will work out.
My love you lie, alone like I,
Seems like true love never learns.
The long soft sigh, that will never die,
For the fire ever burns.

There'll be no rest, In this aching breast,
For the heart will not be still.
Having once been blessed, with the very best,
This heart forever will,
Wake in the night to live again,
In a happy time since passed.
To feel your glad arms bout me then,
But the feeling does not last.

Here in a place that knows no sun, 
Dreams the man I should have been.
Here where only shadows run,
In patterns over lonely men.
Do you some times alike me wake?
Beneath a midnight cloud?
And feel as though your heart will break?
And call my name out loud?NOT about me.....(I'm a HAPPY guy!!!!)
