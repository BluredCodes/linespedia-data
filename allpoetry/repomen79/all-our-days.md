title: "All Our Days"

I can hear your heartbeat even though you arent near.
I can feel the comfort, of your love upon me here,
Could it be the weather, or the sadness of this day?
Binding us together, though you are so far away.
                  Seeing is believing if you only have the eye.
                  Giving is recieving and your hearts the reason why.
                  warm within your love as real,
                  As the joy you make me feel.
 Deserts would be flowers from the tears, I'll never weep,
 All of passions powers, I would give for you to keep.
 In the rapture of your being, as the sundown turns to gold.
 My soul knows what its seeing in the angel I enfold.
                  I can hear your heart beat, even as I hear my own.
                  Sounding like the sweetest song, a man has ever known.
                  Would that I could take your hand, walk a way with you.
                  Rapture walk beside me in the early morning dew.
   The lord said,"laugh children laugh" for there is born.
   A new world every loving morn.
   My angel rapture always stays,
   So let us love for all our days*****************************************
