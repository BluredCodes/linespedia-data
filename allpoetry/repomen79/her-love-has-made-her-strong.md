She's cast aside the chains that bound.
Her heart to memories.
For in her gladness she has found,
The strength that truly frees.
So she no longer questions why?
Nor feels she has done wrong.
Faith has taught her heart to fly,
And love has made her strong.

No more the battered inoccent.
That did in fear abide.
Your foolish anger can't prevent,
Her from slapping you aside.
Those days are done at last tough guy.
Better stay where you belong.
Or you'll wear the blackened eye.
For love has made her strong.

You took the gift of precious care,
As though it was your due.
Abused it til it was no longer there,
Now theres no room for you.
Now you ask, what have I done?
You tried to kill her song.
But now she sings a whole new one, 
For love has made her strong.For all the ones who give the abuser the bums rush. GOOD FOR YOU!!!
