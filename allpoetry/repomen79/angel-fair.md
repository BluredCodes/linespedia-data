Her gift was a loving heart so wide.
That all the world could fit inside.
The poor, the ones whose hearts cried out.
found in her grace, to conquer doubt.
So every baby was her child.
And every hurt was reconsiled.
The fairest flower of the field,
Was to these wondering eyes revealed.
Oh precious angel tell me why.
That such as you love such as I?


Moonlight paints her beauty shades,
Of silver, night time seranades,
a Goddess trailing golden hair,
Picture of an answered prayer.
And She has made the blind to see.
The kindness of serenity.
Somber does the blood flow in the vein,
passion is the power and the power shall remain.
And we shall love in fields of light.
Til our imortal hearts delight

She is stardust, she is moonglow,
And forever will be so.
She is joy and overflowing as she sings.
I have to look behind her, check for wings.
How could I have all my dreams?
Come true at once, but so it seems.
Count myself a most lucky man.
Love her so much greater than.
All the things I once held dear.
Come angel walk beside me here.Your all angels, aren't you?
