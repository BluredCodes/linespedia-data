---
title: "A Million Miles Away"
writer: "repomen79"
slug: "a-million-miles-away"

Of  quantum foam and coming home.
And shaking off the curse.
I set my dreaming mind to roam,
The distant multiverse.
For fabric rent, causal event,
And "time" another way.
Just next door where the black hole went,
A million miles away.

Of tesserect and ressurect
And probability.
To where all of space and time connect.
In singularity.
The super strings that are all things.
Are the fabric of you're day.
These are the things that the universe sings,
A million miles away

We meet in a hole thats filled with a soul.
Infinate in every direction.
A dream will go where the wave will flow.
So the dreamer makes connection.
To places far,that really are,
Right where the dreamer lay.
Yet distant as the furthest star.
A million miles away

Of unified feild, and scepter weild,
In the hand of a coming king.
The very breath of life revealed,
As such a precious thing.
Within his hand, we take our stand,
Where peace and mercy stay.
To fly one day, at His command,
A million miles away.
