The wicked worm of warfare turns,
And once again, the Reichstag burns.
A stone age country torn by war,
Is bombed and blasted even more.
Because all "parriots" agree, 
Its a question of security.
What cares the mighty U S A .
If arab chidren die that way?
How is it that so many can?
Suffer so for just one man?

The sun falls gold on rugged walls,
In the mountains as the evening falls.
The spirit of the mujhadeen,
Cries satan stalks our land again.
Imans of insanity.
In robes of christianity.
Whose very children go unfed,
To buy the bombers overhead.
Steeped in evil superstitions,
Bigotry and cluster munitions
Allah grant your children peace.
Their burden does so great increase.

To the fabled land of the khyber pass,
America brings death in mass.
And not an american voice is raised.
In fact the wicked monsters praised.
And all America stands in awe,
At the coming of the anti law.
Fallen, fallen, the land of the free.
Just how the nazi's came to be.
The last great hope of every man,
Has perished in Afghanistan.our cage getssmaller and smaller. find any plain clothes officers under your bed yet?
