From father to father, and son to son,

Its more than I can tell.

Our God is a most benevolent one.

And he knows you very well.



Lovers become mothers, in the blink of an eye.

And your world is made brand new.

your future a question, with no reply.

So much is depending on you.



In an infants life is only grace.

Let it multiply unto you.

In his love for you, you'll find the place.

Of innocence you once knew.



Oh for the joy you will see in his eyes.

The same that you boght me.

Now for your life, do you realise?

How fragile life can be?



There will come  times of bitter tears.

Yet even the darkest night,

Must pass away as dawn appears.

You are always in his sight.



The eye that notes a sparrows fall,

Will surely see your need.

The loving hand that holds us all.

Lays on your heart indeed.



I give you my love, my hope and prayers.

And all the joy we've known.

To hope where reason would despair.

You do not walk alone.



Through a child you relearn childhood.

And the healing trust of surity.

Too find once more that God is good.

And goodness is security.



In short I wish, what you were to me.

Your child be to you the same.

Your wealth your heart, your destiny.
