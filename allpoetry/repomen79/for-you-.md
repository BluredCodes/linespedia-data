In a place of desperation, dark as night without a sound,
To know you was salvation, or as close as could be found.
That you would have a hug for me,
Was as good as it can be,
In my heart I hug you yet.
Darlin I will not forget.
You were flowers in december, in the winter of my heart.
And the hours dont remember where my love for you did start.
Angels watch you as you sleep.
Heaven hold your soul and keep,
All her beauty gathered there.
Where you lay my angel fair.

Hallway walker, Has no claim,
Yet you seem to know his name.
Bring to me dear your living fire,
That I once more might know desire.
For I am taken from the anteroom of the semiMobile dead.
With my heart upon a kite string, sailing way off overhead.
I send you joyous laughter from the canyons of the sky.
Drop that weight and follow after, there is time enough to fly.
Let love and light surround you,and all manner of good things.
I'm so happy to have found you, and the Gladness your love brings.
And though I do as I always do,In hopes my words might touch.
This time love its all for you.You meant so very much.For Ladonna, who huged me when I felt old and ugly. And was generaly good to me whenever she was able.
   Thank you so much for everything darlin.
