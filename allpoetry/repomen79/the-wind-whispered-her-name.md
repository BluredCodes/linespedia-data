The night be winter dark and deep.
The searching heart will find no sleep.
For memory that gathers here.
Of beauty once so very dear.
Do you like me sometimes return?
To fires that no longer burn?
Did love command the things you do?
do you weep when day is through.
This hurting heart can find no blame.
The very wind whispered your name.

Upon your heart so rare and kind,
I send a prayer and hope you find.
That every hope your heart conceal.
Be found to be completely real.
Within your home I found the best,
Of grace and was so truly blessed.
And if the years take me away.
I've winter nights in wich to pray.
That one day things might be the same.
The very wind whispers your name.

The wind blows my sadness grows,
For all the ways a lifetime goes.
For all the laughter love and tears,
Beneath their winter coat of years.
Come blessed memory,
Lead unto tranquility.
Life is lived by what one does,
Pain is born for what once was.
I find my tears bring me no shame,
The very wind whispers your name.FOR BINGOBABE69, Whose love I have sorely missed and for all her family. God Bless you all, this is for YOU.
