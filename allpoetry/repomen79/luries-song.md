title: "Lurie's  Song"
writer: "repomen79"

Lurie let us come ,
To you're house and stop.
And you're mom
Can buy us soda pop.
And we all can eat an easter treat.
On a bright April day.

Lurie look at us.
babies bearing tales.
making such a fuss.
we're all wearing bells
How fun to play, on a sunny day.
Do you like us this way?

lurie lick a spoon.
Mammas baking.
Mamma sings a tune.
As shes making.
A pretty birthday cake for Lurie's sake.
on a happy birthday.

Lurie likes to perch.
On her daddy's chair.
When their home from church.
And he's sitting there.
He tells her things, about the king of kings.
And together they pray.

Lurie loves the Lord,
And she want's to know.
How he was restored.
When they hurt him so.
She wants to hear that he's always near.
And always will stay.

Lurie look around.
All the world you see.
Was lost and found.
When He set it free.
And He loves you so, wants you to know.
Everything is okay.

Lurie life is long.
there are many cares.
But He is strong.
And he hears you're prayers
He'll hold you in ,His hand again.
At the end of the day.

Lurie light will shine.
In you're darkest hour.
He'll send a sign.
Of his loving power.
You're every need will be met indeed.
Thats all I can say.Love like a child my angel,for such is the kingdom of heavon.
    I love you too
