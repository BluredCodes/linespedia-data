The Earth and sky are not to last,
For all shall be made new.
The hope of all the ages past,
Still brightly shines for you.
All the stars did joyful sing,
To sparkle as a sign.
That unto every living thing,
His son will always shine.

The very hill that once did hold,
His form in agony.
Was put there for in days of old,
To set Gods people free.
The flesh of him as living bread.
The blood of him as wine.
By such are weary spirits fed.
His son will always shine.

Although His face was turned away,
From the goodness of His will.
And darkness fell in the midst of day,
Upon that blessed hill.
Now shines upon the path we trod.
On yours as well as mine.
The mighty mighty lamp of God,
His son will always shine.For Tina's Heart,  YES YES YES JOY TO THE WORLD..........
