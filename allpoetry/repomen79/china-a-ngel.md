-In the least of the greater impression.
Is the joy that will ever atone.
For the pain in its purest expression.
Of a child who has lived all alone.
In a creation of endless curiosity.
Comes a wisdom much older than years.
And the lack of anothers generosity.
Brings no consolation in tears
Til unexpectedly one day.
A china angel on his way.

Of all the days that went befor.
how easy it is to ignore.
the one who muddles on the side.
who never cared and never tried.
To break free of his inner walls.
And then a china angel falls.
Upon his way, at first its odd.
A puzzle from a loving God.
He could have looked and turned away.
But something in his heart said stay.

Unearthly beauty cast aside.
This china angel did abide.
Upon this young boys way to school.
In such a way does heavon rule.
Fragile and so very fair.
China angel lying there.
All her children heavon holds.
His heart her china wing enfolds.
His day will not go as he planned.
A miracle is in his hand.

Where yesterday he had no friends.
Today his isolation ends.
For every heart so deeply yearns.
For beauty with a pain that burns.
Even the most tiny hearts.
For a little boy acceptance starts.
He's bought his miracle to share.
They see him now at last they care.
And bless the child who came to find.
That beauties grace, and grace is kindIs there any one out there?
