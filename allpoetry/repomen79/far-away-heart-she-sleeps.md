title: "Far Away Heart  (She sleeps)"

Oh indigo skies and autumn moon,
Fair take the breath of me.
Wax full to vanish all to soon.
So ever must it be.
With tender sighs, and searching eyes,
And angels where you sleep.
I pray to where my lady lies.
to hold and bless and keep.
How silent be my lonely night.
If only you were here.
How precious in my loving sight,
Does your sweet soul appear.

How fragile be the spark that glows,
How infinate the being,
The eye that looks within you knows,
exactly what its seeing.
Across the void of time and space,
my heart bids your's be free.
For in my dream I see your face,
You're beautiful to me.
To where you lay my love I pray,
you joy forever after.
That angels guide you on your way,
With love and light and laughter.


My love, the pain will not remain,
One day we'll be made new.
And ever after love will reign,
On me as well as you.
When comes that day, we both shall say,
How bright this loving life shone.
That we should meet upon our way,
And did not walk alone.
So dream a dream, let concience stream,
to places far and wide.
I'm not as far as it might seem,
I'm right here by your side.Sleep in heavonly peace.
