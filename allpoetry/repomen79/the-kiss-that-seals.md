---
title: "The Kiss that Seals"
writer: "repomen79"
slug: "the-kiss-that-seals"

Call forth a child to bear this ring,
And magic shall be done.
Today her loving heart shall sing,
As two become as one.
At the flower bedecked alter play,
The joyous wedding song.
That their shinning spirit on this day,
Shall last their whole lives long.

Let the precious hearts of love they hold,
Comfort them in time of trials.
On this blessed day of sunlights gold,
All of Earth and Heaven smiles.
OH for the trembling hands that touch,
As the loving eye reveals.
The faith that grace conffers on such,
Til at last the kiss that seals.

Joy to the world for love shall reign,
They'll be a resting place,
Unto one another in times of pain,
And for each other, grace.
Like lillies bending with the wind,
The joyous couple kneels.
To make a vow without an end,
And kiss the kiss that seals.


Call for the flute and the tamborine,
Let a song of two as one begin,
For everlasting love is seen,
right Here in the sight of men.
May her loving light forever shine,
With the gladness that she feels.
Its her wedding day and she's made divine,
As they kiss the kiss that seals.JOY to the world.......
