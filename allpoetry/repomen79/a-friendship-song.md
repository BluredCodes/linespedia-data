There are large hearts,
under little words.
None much larger than your own.
In my need your great indeed,
So much greater than you've known.

I have come to,
Be in love a bit,
With all the people that I meet.
You impress me, God has blessed me,
Somehow made me more complete.

Sometimes life is long,
we all need a friend,
One who'll try to understand,
That living is for giving,
Thank you for your helping hand.

Tender touching hands,
Loving large heart,
May all blessings come to you.
Your an angel, and I'll never part,
From the love I hold for you.


And if troubles come,
And you feel pain.
May it quickly pass away.
You are sunny skies, not a drop of rain,
You are Christmas every day.

Lifes a mystery,
sometimes you just can't know,
And sometimes theres just no answer.
The beauty of your soul, sets your face aglow,
Gentle pretty tiny dancer.

Everybody dreams,
But in a womens heart,
Is a hope thats ever growing.
You are magic but thats just a part,
Of the women I am knowing.

What could I say?
That would thank you.
And not leave you in dismay?
A prayer above you, Heaven love you,
And it always be that way.Written for Aileen Rodrigues, but applies to so MANY others.
Friendship is heavens way of keeping us company.
