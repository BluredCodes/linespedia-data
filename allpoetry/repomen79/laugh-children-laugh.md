title: "Laugh children laugh"

Let laughter ring out like a bell,
Laugh beloved all is well.
The very air caresses you,
Your every loving hope is true.
The veil of sorrow disappears.
Joy has come to dry your tears.
The faceless crowd has turned to gold,
It offers you its heart to hold.
Laugh children laugh, oh can't you see?
This moment IS eternity.

Oooh, for the glad smiles, returning to the faces.
Succor the trials in so many places.
A joyful shout above the noise,
That all might hear the fathers voice.
Today is the peace of the snow white dove.
Today is the day when my heart knows love.
Today is the day that my pen employ,
The laughter in my heart, and the loving joy.
For all of them do so amaze,
In all the beauty of their ways.

Laugh children laugh, for heaven knows,
How sweet your loving kindness grows.
Having been with you in the dead of night,
Weeping for the words you write.
The hopes that vanished with the wind,
Shall live again and never end.
And you shall speak a joyous word,
In that your kindness has secured,
The love you bring,that is your craft,
