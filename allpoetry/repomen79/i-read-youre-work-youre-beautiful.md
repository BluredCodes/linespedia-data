I know the dark, I've known it well.
And the hunger that abides.
For beauty in the face of hell.
And the comfort it provides.

Oh Lord, but for an eye to see.
Somewhere a light must shine.
For another hearts prosperity,
To somehow prosper mine.

So I swim in the wonder that you're poems weave.
Even shed a grateful tear.
For you're great love, and I do believe,
Heavon holds us very dear.

Take my thoughts, my love my prayer.
Take the time to say hello.
The endless love that we can share,
Is the best thing that I know.

Speak you're dreams to all man kind,
You loved, you were,and you'll be.
A light for such as me to find.
And you're love has set me free.

I have been troubled greatly.
As you are sometimes too.
But I've been alright lately.
For having heard from you.

You're tender touch of pen to word.
The gentle souls you are.
Have joy my darling, I have heard,
And love you from afar.
Don't let them tell you no one cares.
You've nothing new to show.
I've loved you're gentle hoping prayers.
More than you'll ever know.

Rain rain, toil and pain.
Beloved bear you're weight.
You're love shall count it all as gain,
And make you're pathway sraight.

So in the end, my poet friend,
Who makes my heart to shine.
Write on, theres other hearts to mend.
As you have mended mine

Like Frost upon that snowy night.
We've promises to keep.
And many lives to fill with light,
Before our time to sleep.

We live a miracle its true.
I've read you're work and cried.
And wish for love and light for you.
