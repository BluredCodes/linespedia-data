My friend would you sit here and play me a somg?
Just play me some sad old tune.
Cause its saterday night for the rest of my life.
And I'm going away with the moon.

The radio doesn't remember the song.
The words have all drifted away.
The nurse comes to ask if theres anything wrong?
And I find that I've nothing to say.

Hear how the train makes its faraway sound.
The very air whispers amen.
Life seems much sweeter when you are around.
Won't you come here and see me again?

Let burn the candle til at last it ends'
For the light of your love doe's not die.
Here I abide in the love that she sends.
As the universe turns slowly by.

When all of creation is measured and weighed.
And the final accounting falls due.
I'll walk to to my destiny with joy unafraid.
And grateful for having known you.

Life was a gamble' the luck of the draw.
So favored this fortunate son.
All I can do is thank Him in awe.
Our God is a most loving one.

At times in my heart I hear old voices calling,
Beloved companions I'd thought passed away.
How golden this life shines, as evening is falling.
How I long for the end of the day.

I find oh precious beloved one..
His loving gift to me.
