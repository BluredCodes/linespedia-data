No bells, no snow, no eight reindeer.
But all the same its Christmas here.
Within this heart did shadows form,
To dissappear as love does warm.
As by her word I am assured,
that love and joy and hope endured.

Within this room no mistletoe,
Yet I send kiss' even so,
Unto your far and shinning light,
For you have made this Christmas night.
Such a joy your loving draws,
I have no need of santa clause.

Upon this day of springtime breeze,
My life is turned to Christmas tree's.
I wish you merry christmas dear,
Every day throughout the year.
The shadows fade, and storm clouds lift,
You've given me your greatest gift.

Silent night, is made so bright,
You are so precious in my sight.
All is calm, all is well,
More, much more than I can tell.
Sleep in Heavenly peace, my dear,
Silent night and I am near.Thank you so much, Tamara, Myra, Mindy and always Laura
 I am made small in the face of your loving kindness. ..(in other words, THANKS I NEEDED THAT!!!) always laura
