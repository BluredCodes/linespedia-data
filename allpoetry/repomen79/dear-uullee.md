Dear Jullee for the life of me.
I bid you grace and peace.
Poor powers of reason that I be.
I reason love won't cease.
Let not you're good heart be dismayed.
My spirit touchs your's
Despite the love we haven't made.
My love for you endures.

Time is kind and you will find.
you're every dream was real.
Twa's only doubt that made you blind.
And fear that sought to steal.
The comfort and security.
That loves without regret.
Dear Jullee for the life of me.
I love you better yet.

The tiny storms that living forms.
Blow cold and then are gone.
Faith abides and loving warms.
And courage carries on.
Don't fear my dear for you are all,
That beautiful can be.
I hear you're loving spirit call.
Oh for the life of me.With love to my first freind on the net. for the  comforting voice of her far away heart.
