---
title: "Never Really Gone"
writer: "repomen79"
slug: "never-really-gone"
source: "AllPoetry"

My love that you be hurting so,
And feeling left alone.
Please know I did not want to go,
The choice was not my own.
You saw this body, laid away,
beneath an emerald lawn.
This loving spirit comes to say,
I'm never really gone.

Oh dear that you, who were so true.
Should shed such bitter tears.
Please take the view, I hope you do,
That my loving heart still hears.
You did not put me in the ground.
My love for you go's on.
If you want me just look around,
I'm never really gone.

That I would bring, such a sad thing,
To you, who made my life shine.
I say to death, where is thy sting?
For you are ever mine.
And comes the day, at heavens way,
We meet at that good dawn.
We'll love forever and a day.
I'm never really gone.For anyone whose lost someone dear to them.
