Here in the room that our love has built,
A place of joy, devoid of guilt.
once more I take my grateful rest.
Upon your warm familiar breast.
To find myself made again complete,
Just listening to your heart beat.
Though days be long and comforts few,
I find my strength in knowing you,
No matter how my spirits tried,
Will be there standing at my side.
The hand I took holds tightly still.
The love we've made forever will.
Be there at the break of day,
To sooth the worlds cares away
Here in this room where we retreat,
I listen to your heartbeat.
This happy man has never found,
Nothing as lovely as that sound.
Sleep softly love I hold you tight.
And hear your heartbeat in the night.
Til morning finds me once again,
More blessed than ordinary men.c2000 small words
