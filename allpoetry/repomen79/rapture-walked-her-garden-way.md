Seek out the ancient of age to say.
Rapture walked her garden way.
Fragile flicker in the wind.
Midnight bloomer soon to end.
tell me do the roses weep.
Dreaming in their autumn sleep?
We only have this summer day.
And rapture walked her garden way
I look around.
and she is there
the sound.
Of music everywhere.
All my prayers came true today.
Rapture walked her garden way.

I sat up in the night in tears.
All thats precious disappears.
Tasted her poetic art.
Touched her wild and wanting heart.
She spoke to where my passion lay
And rapture walked her garden way.
Oh isn't it a pity theres so very little time?
Isn't it a wonder that our passion is sublme?
Didn't it seem right to live?
giving all  that I could give?
In that I just couldn't stay.
And rapture walked her garden way.

All in a dream,
Or did it seem?
That her light fell on my darkness like a kiss.
How I want her very badly to know this.
That I don't know her at all.
As I climb her garden wall
To taste forbidden fruit.
She is love and absolute. 
all the happy creatures say. 
Rapture walks her garden way.For the lovely and precious Lurie(Laura Daniels) who likes love poems.
