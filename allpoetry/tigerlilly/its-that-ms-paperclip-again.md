It appears that I’m having a thing with,
shhhhh…
The MS paperclip again.
I may start calling it an affair,
We’re meeting more often
Now than then.

Sly little nag, just sits there
Raising his brows…
I write down a few words
He cringes, pretending not
To have heard
Then I swear that he gags.

I’m thinking this writing’s
So stinking… 
I make a paperclip rolf!
Wonder then, should I consider
Backgammon, tole painting
Or golf?

He keeps telling me, dear,
If you feed
me one more smelly byte
I fear, I’ll toss
all my cookies at you
disable Word, too...
and lock down notepad.stuck again...
