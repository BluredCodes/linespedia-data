---
title: "love's command"
writer: "tigerlilly"

wee kitten moments old
pushed aside
struggling silently
still caught in tattered
birth sac
no breath sounds
do i hear

mother cat's decision made
dare i think
then to open heart
inviting
that one inside
with whom even
she will have 
no part

as these thoughts
fly 
warm rough cloth
cleans sleek little
body feather-light
in my hand

clearing all obstructions
breathing into it
love's command
come back little one
don't go away

there's room 
in my cat world
for another yet to play
with all the catnip
furrymice 
take breath
then mew
for me

success at last
air rushes into lungs
expanding
first cry
the exhalation
hunger
so demanding

back among her siblings
she is gently placed
scrabbling now
for sustenance
i know she'll win
this racethis one is now a fine fluffy feline named minute :)
