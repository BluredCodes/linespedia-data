title: "love's prime factor"

i count up at days end
all the little things you do
pack the kid's lunch
wash the car
buy the dinner for two

and as i see the total acts
that number our love
i thank you darling from my heart
my number one
i'm thinking of

you know i'm not keeping score
but we've had our bad days
when problems subtracted
from the sum of loving ways

but we drew close and held on
stood together side by side
though the cold winds rushed past
our house we did not divide

now i'm counting the blessings
remembering joys and tears
as our love multiplied
bringing children through the years

and as i see the total acts
that number our love
i thank you darling from my heart
my number one
i'm thinking of

i'll stop now the growing list
for no matter what the path
i can see my number one
as the prime factor
