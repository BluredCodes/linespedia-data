shhhhhh!

just a whisper

speak of love
near
my ear

aaaaaah!

sweet tangle

of your hands
in my hair

mmmmmmm!

this kiss

that's
what these lips
were made for

ohhhhhhh!

turn out
the lights

V
V
V

lock
the door...all comments and critiques welcomed
