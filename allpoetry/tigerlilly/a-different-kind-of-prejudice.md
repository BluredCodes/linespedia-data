prejudice comes in many forms
color alone is not the norm
chosen first i suppose
for to eyes it's apparent
but others are there
just as inherent

a house may be old but clean
some call that condition mean
not seeing a home bound with love
held together with pride
no great importance are things
but the people inside

as for the matter of clothes
some may turn up their nose
at home-sewn recycled items
without that designer label
money wastes in the closet
let's put food on the table

the question is career
what field are you in my dear
a housewife in the country
oh one of those
it must be nice not to work
i suppose

where did you take your degree
and what is G.E.D.
how nice theres a program
for people like you
who haven't a goal
to pursue

i've heard all of this you see
as it was applied to me
these comments were born
from all colors and kinds
from those who placed wallets
