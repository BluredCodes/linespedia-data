my friend
i come to you 
with open eyes
heart in hand
no great surprise
my soul is known

reflected 
i can hear it
in your voice
singing stories
at day's end

our lives
meet and touch
on many pathways
in these tangled woods
of life

companions
sharing laughter
tears and strife
forever after

on the avenues
we choose
to use
