Bad news, breaking into the sphere 
of consciousness, heralds change.
A weighty word that snares my spirit,
sends it plunging like a stone
to unfathomed depths.

These are uncertain times, 
but I've walked this way before,
and the darkness is familiar.
almost fitting, somehow...
though not affording comfort.

So it is that I must stay here...
vulnerable and open, for awhile at least, 
'til my fearful casing splits...
and I emerge a soft-winged creature
made to rise and soar
once more above the trees,
until it's time to grow again.all comments and critiques welcomed
