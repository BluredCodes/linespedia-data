Red silk
Banners waving,
Celebrate New Leap Year!
Black horse hurdles one extra month,
Assuring much more Spring Festival cheer.
Happy Valley tracked its entrance,
Coursing yang water horse,
Rewarding with 
Red silk.a late greeting, but then i am always behind in things... :)
