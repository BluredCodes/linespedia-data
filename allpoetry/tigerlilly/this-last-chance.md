---
title: "this last chance"
writer: "tigerlilly"
slug: "this-last-chance"
source: "AllPoetry"

night zephyrs
found
my desert oasis
when life was settled

heart becalmed
in the barrens
of my own
choosing

this 
world held
camels tied
date palms
fruited heavy
overhead
with the efforts
of saying no 
all these
years

one
evening's
dusk brought
cool refreshing
air

curling
taunting
tempting fingers  
rushed
into my tent

visions formed
where 
sand shifted
into shapes of pillows
men and stallions
running free
into
darkest night

i listened closely
to the wind

then
i saw
the scorpion
with his curved tail
from the corner
of my mind

deadly sting
i know...

but 
with the night
a song has come
old yet timeless
sing the words
play the music

i hear
 
then dance
before the firelight
knowing
the risk i take
of dying
before dawn

i beg
this final moment
give me innocent
and dark

at the same time

set me down
before i fall
into the hourglass
where
my own sands
run out
before my eyes

i plead
the wish
to have once more
the time of youth
not caution blinded
to such
light as this 


shining
on one
who 
slays the sun

rising
with the moon
unknown
into my well of comfort
when i sleep
each night
beside still
waters

before my eyes
images move faster
wind current
carries
me along

clouds 
of stormtime
raise and gather

thunder roars 
lightening strikes
the faithful saber
that i carry
charged
with good and evil
forces

offered here
my own place
to sit among
the kings 
dreaming nomad
honored

paying homage
where multitudes
have knelt
before this
altar

pressing 
to my senses
crying
wind images say
stop
rest here
no need to mind
the moment
given

i can't walk
away
from this
last chance
to see
the desert blossom
