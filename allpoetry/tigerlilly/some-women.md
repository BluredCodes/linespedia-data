Women’s equality
Concept
Simple as A-B-C

We all stand up for equal pay
For equal work
Call the cops
Drag all to Family Court
For those no-goods
Who would shirk
The duties of a mom
Or dad
Without The Court’s
Permission
Cleaning up the mess we make
Is in fact 
Part
Of the commission
In real equality

Women’s Liberation 
Is a done deal ‘till some babe
Hits that cold glass ceiling
Ouch!   
Yes, it’s still real…
But a woman 
With a fine mind
Just won’t waste her time
When the world is full of better
Deals, when she makes up
Her mind that liberation
Is a woman’s right 
to Choose
Her own damn
Course in life

Feminists
Please tell me
What is the definition
Of your purpose in society
Do you truly have a mission?
Or is it just that you enjoy
Living fast and free…
That’s good if all
Your choices only
Touch YOU
Individually

My complaint
Is with those females
Who forget their tender parts
Bitterness you’ll often find
Hidden 
Deep within their hearts
Inside silken undies
No surprise-surprise
If you even looked at all
You’d find two inflated
Twisted blue 
Wishes
For some balls

I say…INFLATED
Because
This is where
Some women find
THEIR self-worth
In being imitation copies
Of their own
Image and idea
Their hope and panacea
Of what a MAN
Should be

I say…TWISTED
Because
These women

(SISTER, YOU  KNOW  WHO  I  MEAN)

Whose egos and whose psyches
Are bent way out of shape
What with mood-swings
Mars and Venus
Passages and PMS
All running in the contest
For Oprah’s 
Bitch of the week

Win it every time
I guess
However, I digress

BLUE…I say
Because
These ARE the ones
Who walk around
As if they’re
Pissed at all the world 
(AND YOU)
Their problem is they weren’t
Born MEN
Now they don’t know 
What to do…

BUT  GOD  FORBID!!!  

(YOU  SHOULD  NEVER  FORGET  THIS)

THEY  INSIST…

THAT YOU  ~~MUST~~  STILL

TREAT   THEM  LIKE  WOMEN!!!

Or  THEY  will be the ones
Who’ll yell loudest
Scream the longest
Nag the worst
Making miserable
Life in general
For all MEN
Concerned
And for
Most other WOMEN
Who’ve been gifted
With some sense

By the way
These wicked bitches
Impersonating humans
Are the same who sit around
And say
“Gee, I wonder why there
Aren’t any REAL MEN
Around today?”i am not out to make anyone angry... but in my personal life, i have run into one too many of these women lately, and i just had to vent a biti feel sure most could think of someone whom this description fits...
