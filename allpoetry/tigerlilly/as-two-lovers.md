---
title: "As Two Lovers..."
writer: "tigerlilly"

All alone in the darkness,
thinking of you...
if I could just sleep,
I'd be dreaming too.

Those few moments we had,
earlier tonight...
are hard to hold onto,
'till morning's light.

This longing is building,
and; oh, how i yearn...
for your love's fulfilling,
how my body burns.

The phone's ringing cuts
through silence and gloom,
your voice comes to me,
lights up my room...

with the presence of love.
Warm glow in the dark,
you say you want me...
kindling that spark.

Flash fire, leaping flames,
with words heart's embrace;
distance no longer matters,
the miles are erased.

I feel your arms so warm,
your breath on my cheek,
no longer alone, now...
as two lovers, we speak.all comments and critiques welcomed :)
