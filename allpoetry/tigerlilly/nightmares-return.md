at 3:00 am
nightmares return
waking me
shaking me
from coccoon
of blankets warm

sweating 
cold bullets
fear drenched
trembling 
in this storm
that sweeps
through heart
and soul

my dreaming mind
unkind
to bring forth
images of loss

grating
fractured bone-ends
of grief
to tear me 
from healing sleep
where closed eyes
and memory
still could keep
the thought 
of your sleeping form
beside my own

now lost
in this waking
aching tide
of waters dark
pain so deep
crashing 
against
broken heart
as i weepall comments and critiques welcomed
