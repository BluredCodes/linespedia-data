---
title: "stolen dream"
writer: "tigerlilly"
slug: "stolen-dream"
source: "AllPoetry"

ruby wine in crystal glasses
glowing in the candlelight
a lover's assignation
taking place
deep in the night

soft swelling strains
Rachmaninoff
began to fill our ears
as violins intimated
*uplifting*
passions
here

scent of roses filled the room
noses found themselves
in tune
with the kiss
held as we danced
'cross the room
ahhh...sweet romance

THEN

insidious intrusive noise
shattered wine glass
dripping red
i woke to find myself
clutching pillows
in my bed

alarm clock cutting
buzz-saw edges
through
sensibilities

brain 
scattering romantic dreams
like dry leaves
before a rain

hitting "snooze five minutes"
i tried to grasp
this vision fine
but it slipped from 
memory's finger tips
onto broken glass
spilled wine

no use i see as ears
pick up
the news and world report
violins dissolve
to sports scores
broadcasters
wry retorts

scent of roses turns to coffee
as i further wake
the kids want favorite cereals
one's crying for
some cake

with bleary eye
and heartfelt sigh
i bid adieu to dreamland's
lover...
wishing
for a little more time
here
beneath the covers

glaring at the clock's smug face
thinking of my love's embrace
cursing silently
time's machinery
that stole this dream
