Long before dawn,
on a cold winter's morn,
a half-whispered question slowly parted
the sleeping curtains of my eyelids...
"Honey, you awake?"

My mind, 
dream-mingled with half-realities,
answered words,
dimly-heard... with feeling's
ache of anticipation.

I was swimming
in the pleasing warmth of cover's
ocean, sensing changes in the sea, tide’s pull
was just beginning...

then experienced, slow deliberateness,
my Neptune,
exploring depths of this kingdom
that he owned.

Amphitrite,
rising from these Morpheus wanderings,
adds
her own undulations to the current
of building waves...

where
impassioned gods and goddesses
worship in the deep,
his trident strikes a quaking
by
