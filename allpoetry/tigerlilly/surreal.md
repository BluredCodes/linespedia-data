waking this morning
sinking back
into
pillow's cradle

crying
for womb
sheltered
silence
fractured
only by
heart beats
once more

thought marbles
clatter 
loud
on waxed floors
shattered
dream pieces
slow not 
scattered
fallings
away

muffled life cries
pouring out
over
memories lost
or evaded
some
tossed
tearing tears
from sad
eyes

cold blue steel
emanates
visions of brass
chased 
allure
worshipped high
on temple
hill

last
sight chaos
loud
sound
no great
loss

it's all
