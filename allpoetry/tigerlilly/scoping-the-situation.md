Peering into this deep well
of Self...
using introspection's narrow scope.
 00
Darkness is all I see.
Can't tell...
if what I lost is at the bottom
here...
but I peer,
intently.
    00
Evidently,
it would help to have light
(motivation)
(inspiration)
illuminating the murk.
       00
Where in this abyssmal
place might you be?
   00
Oh Muse...
why have you hidden
yourself away
from me?all comments and critiques welcomed :)
