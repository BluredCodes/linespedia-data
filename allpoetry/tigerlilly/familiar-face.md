title: "familiar face"

this bed 
that once saw slumber
witnessed
passisve sleeping scenes
has become
a place of torment
sheet shrouded
tossing dreams

soft pillow
where i once rested
this blithe
untroubled head
is crushed
by lonely arms now
in my absent
lover's stead

old quilts
that seemed so cozy
with colors
bright and bold
bring to mind
night passions
desire played out
in it's folds

each dim
shadowed corner
holds memories 
in the gloom
of small talked 
laughing moments
that we shared here 
in this room

brass clock 
sits on the table
ticks long hours
through empty space
i just can't help
wondering
who's enjoying
that sweet
