Part I

Wild animal soul,
feeling cornered,
trapped.

Heart
lies pressed against 
the bony bars
of this CAGE,
which represents...
a BODY failing.
ME!

Of all people,
but then
why not me?
I am only one of many
who liked to run,
roaming free...

making no consessions
to aches and pains...
till stopped,
dead...
almost;
by the sudden blow,
so unexpected,
undetected.

(Mercy
would have been
the killing...
but, not so.)

My world seen now
through
wire-meshed window
in this cell
of cautions,
here and there,

till
not one space
is free of someone
saying
NO!

The eyes that WATCH
you daily,
(passing...
always passing through),
like vistiors
at the zoo...

for signs,
for new symptoms.
 
Close the gates!
Go away...
why stand
looking
while pain tears
with jagged teeth, 
the edges
of my life.

Trapped.
