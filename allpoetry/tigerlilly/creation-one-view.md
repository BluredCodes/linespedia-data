title: "Creation ~ One View"
writer: "tigerlilly"

one day before days had come to be measured
all that existed was a mass of seething energy
in the cauldron of emptiness

The Divine One then dipped His finger
into this mixture of chaos
tasted the results
declared that it was good and it was ready

He tipped the pot a bit and our Universe flowed out
before Him

while it was still warm He shaped with His own Hands
this Earth
loving it a little more because something
about this Creation appealed to Him

dipping once again into that lifeforce
He sprinkled trees and forests
mountains and plains
oceans and deserts
and all that lives in
or on them

He felt that this was good and while
all things were beautiful
a delight to His eye
something struck Him as...

YES! the idea came to His mind and before
another thought passed through
He dribbled starlight substance mixed with blackest
night then breathed on it...cooling
this lump that began to shape itself into a Man

for all it was worth then
He felt He could sit back and rest
but it was not to be, for while the Man appeared
to be doing a fine job tending to all He had made
and the last of the animals
right down to Zebra
were named...that Man had a downcast look
and it was then that the Divine One devised
the word 'loneliness'
applying it to the hearts
of all His creatures

passing His hand across the eyes of Man...The Divine One
soothed him into sleep, and while that first brush
with Morpheus occurred
He reached inside the Man's body
under his arm
near his heart and drew out Woman
placing her by Man's side

now, it appeared that He would get the rest He sought
but like a bad dream held in the back of His mind
He knew that trouble was stirring in Paradise
looking down, He saw bigger than life and twice as true
that Serpent, with his false light
shining through

too late to intervene it seemed for Eve was already
extending that apple to Adam in the hand of invitation
and because he had fallen hard for this woman
he took and ATE OF IT

at that moment...a storm swept through mankind
and rolled black clouds down through the ages far
into the future, and the garden became a forbidden place
for man and woman

Adam and Eve were cast out and clothed with their
brother's skins, guilty as charged...compounding
further still their sins
hurting... 
the Divine One saw them struggle in want and need
fighting pain destruction death
and took pity

giving them hope in the form of a Saviour one day
to come, to deliver their hearts back to the state
of perfect innocence He had created them in
wiping clean the black night quality that
had stained their souls so deeply

with the whitest of white this One would come
and the reddest of all blood would spill out
to cleanse what man had done

and so it happened
