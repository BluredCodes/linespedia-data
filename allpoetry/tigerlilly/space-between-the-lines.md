title: "space between the lines"
writer: "tigerlilly"

some lovers
have the luxury
to share a lifetime's
span of years
for others
life is cut short
leaving one
who's wreathed in tears

most will fall
into another picture
of the lot
ordinary people
with a love
not cold or hot

it's here
that fate begins
to slip
somewhere 
between the lines
looking for the ones
who know
their love
is in decline

it always happens
that these two
will meet
along the way
starting out
as good friends
growing closer
every day

they build grand worlds
to live in
because there's nothing
left to lose
they're living
in the spaces
that their spouses
just won't use

so if you'd like
to keep your love
at the center
of your heart
make sure you leave
no vacancies
for another's love
to start

close the ranks with care
keep a match 
to your lover's fuse
or he may slip one day

between the lines

through some space
