---
title: "this morning's call"
writer: "tigerlilly"
slug: "this-mornings-call"
source: "AllPoetry"

good morning
love
you say
it's not

i'm listening
i hear

wishing i could
hold you
tight
in this
early morning
light

damn these miles
between us
when you need
me there

you know
there's nothing
in your life
that i wouldn't
share  
                  
even now
in your voice
hearing pain
from wounds dealt 
just by living
every day

words of love
you say
are like
a cooling balm
spread
on old sunburn

it stings
anyway

i hear you say
your demons were
all waiting
there
when you opened
up your eyes

don't be surprised

they 
learn your 
weakest hour
but together
we 
can turn 
this thing
around

we'll
slay the dragons
sending bad things
back to hell and gone
never fear

but until then
 
i'll damn
these miles between us
when all i want to do
is hold you
hold you
