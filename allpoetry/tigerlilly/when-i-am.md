when i am...

thinking 
of you

that soft look, the sparkle
i see in your eyes
when you
look at me
that way

and
i remember with more than
mind, that smile that
dances, playing
on your lips

oh
how
i love to trace
those contours, lovely
curving planes
with my fingertips

and 
think of loving you
again

but
even more i dream
of moments when i press
my lips to yours

and 
then it seems like
paradise
as you paint my skin
with your warm breath

and
open gates
where within
the tickling touch
of tongue
