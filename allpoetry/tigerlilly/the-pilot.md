one day Hope went sailing
on the sea of Love
whispering prayers unfailing
to the Captain up above

Hope's ship was stout and true
sails billowed full of air
waters calm and blue
the day so bright and fair

Faith came on board a sailor
Patience answered as first mate
the smiling cook was Humor
but the pilot's running late

at last the harbormaster
said it's clear to sail
and now we face disaster
but not from storm or gale

Courage is the pilot's surname
his middle title Care
without his given...Laughter
couples see Posiedon's lair

at last the pilot's boarded
misplaced but not forgot
and the Trust that Hope afforded
makes up our happy lot

all hands on deck, up anchor
now we are on our way
late but with no rancor
Hope's ship will sail todayall comments and critiques welcomed
