---
title: "winter's wedding"
writer: "tigerlilly"

morning brings first snowfall 
still so new and white
soft down plucked from sky geese
fresh-fallen through the night

contented Mother Earth's breasts 
once chilled by winter's cold
clothed now in a white gown 
hills and valleys to enfold

faithful Ice King taking pity
has gifted her with cover
consumating nuptuals
as this season's lover

graceful swells and contours 
her body once more celebrated
she'd lain so long in nakedness
wondering why he'd waited

she'd cried so with her longing
'till he heard her plaintif wail
rewarding then by sending
snowflakes ending her travail

against the sky bare trees
set the boundaries of their bower 
while white burdened evergreens
canopied the wedding hour

still in darkest night
he played up all her tender features
with trailing kisses evidenced 
in paw-prints of night creatures

her bright anticipation 
reflected in her lover's eyes
wind songs poured soft into his ears
Earth's whited virgin sighs

with like voice the Ice King 
genltly soothed away her fears
vowing promises of snowfalls
forever through the coming years

yielding then to sweet adoring
season's circle a wedding ring
all his moisture she's absorbing
Mother Earth's conceiving Springall comments and critiques welcomed
