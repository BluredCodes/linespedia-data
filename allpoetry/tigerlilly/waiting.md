darkness
wraps
around us
as we cradle
in the night

thunder rolls
and rain falls
while
lightning flashes 
bright

sky tears sweep
across
the blackened
face of
window panes

and
sheeted
kissing water
drops

trickle 
down
the 
glass
it
never
stops

cold winds        
can't
reach us
lying 
here

waiting 
for the storm
to passall comments and critiques welcomed
