title: "Step into the Shadows"
writer: "tigerlilly"

"I didn't think you'd understand"
he said,
while smiling...I just lied.

Relief came through in his voice,
my tears
stayed locked inside.

"You know I love you"
he said,
as he backed away,
and the funny thing...
I know he does,
it just doesn't feel that way.

So I'll step into the shadows
here on lonely street...
duck into the house of pain
where aching hearts retreat.

Someday he'll call me
I know;
but, until he speaks my name,
I'll just count myself a loser
in this cheating game.

"You're really quite a woman"
he said,
I smiled and just agreed.

"You know that when I have time"
you're
everything I need.

So I held him close
and felt
my heart break
as he pushed it
to the side,

then kept on smiling up at him,
with the tears
locked deep inside.

Well, I know I love him all the same
though our space
is growing small,
but i'll keep my hand
in this cheating game...

step
from the shadows when he calls.all comments and critiques welcomed :)
