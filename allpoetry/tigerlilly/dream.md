i know
it's a dream
yet it seems
so real 
that i feel
your warm breath
on my face

as your arms 
hold me
tightly
so close
an embrace
that desire
starts to burn
and my body
yearns

for your touch
and the kiss
that follows
a moment
sweet moment
like this
when you
half wake me
then take me

while i'm still
caught up
in my
