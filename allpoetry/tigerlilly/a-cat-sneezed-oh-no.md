title: "A cat sneezed?  OH NO!!!"
writer: "tigerlilly"
slug: "a-cat-sneezed-oh-no"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/42757-A-cat-sneezed---OH-NO----by-tigerlilly"

One cat, then two, oh no,
three more...
have sniffles, sneezes,
throats so sore.

It's been the best
of winters yet
'cause no one's 
had to see the Vet.

But now this cold germ
does conspire,
racing through my bunch
...viral wildfire!

So grab the carriers,
chase a cat...
one's still hiding,
now look at that...

'twould work, my dear,
if only tail...
did not protrude
past bedrail!

Into the car, crates
stacked so high...
we're on our way,
appointment nigh;

and oh! how they express
despair...yowling
meowling...
ten miles there.

At last this circus
does arrive,
where techs and Doctor
now will strive...

with compact fur-bundled
teeth and claws,
fright well advanced
and without pause.

These gentle little 
babies dear,
have turned to tigers
now i fear.

At last, all is done,
back into cages...
soothe the last,
out of wildcat rages.

Back to the car,
then within house...
give to one and all
new catnip mouse.

They glare at me,
green and yellow eyes...
"It's all your fault,
and we despise...

the Vet and shots,
that we find there...
thought you loved us,
thought you cared!"

This is what they said
i know,
read it in their
faces so...

how can i say this?
Oh, what a plight!
