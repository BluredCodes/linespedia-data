Silent sun-sabers
slashing slanting slits slightly
sundering sound sight.all comments and critiques welcomed, as the evening sun pours through the mini-blinds... which happen to be tilted at just the right angle for the light to enter these dark-room expanded pupils, thereby piercing the optic nerves with calculated intensity.
