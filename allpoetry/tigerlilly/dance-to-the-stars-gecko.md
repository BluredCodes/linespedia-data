Your soul
can reach afar,
for the simplest pleasure.
Grab the brass ring of hope, when life
denies fullness of measure.  Touch the hands
of friends and loved ones who would each
dance you to the stars… reach
out, bring joy to
your soul.gecko, you are the dearest lil lizard i know...now, how did that tape from the cam corder turn out the night ye wuz clingin' to the ceiling on 'no peeking'? run that one back, as anything with me in it should give ya a laugh, my friend... :) then we shall share another orange... *hugs*
