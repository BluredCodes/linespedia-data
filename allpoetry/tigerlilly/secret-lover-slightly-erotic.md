title: "secret lover (slightly erotic?)"

streamers
of moonlight
cutting
through blinds

slight
illimunation
that only defines

this sense
of peace
found
cradled here

close 
as a heartbeat
we're so near

perceiving
your smile
as you
stroke my hair

still trembling
emotions
laying so bare

my soul
is thus opened
to the love
pressing
there

once more
slow entry
sought

yet 
speedily
gained

hardly move
for some moments
this feeling
sustained

'till the wave
rolls and swells
to crest once
again

not yet
you would have me
mix pleasure
with pain

hold back
a little more
before desire
breaks
on the shore

i've lost sight
and now sound
my ears
cannot hear

just sensation
in a vortex
centering near

pulling us
both down
in one another 
we drown

his
for the taking

shaking

now waking

from dreams
rumpled
cover

flees
my night's
