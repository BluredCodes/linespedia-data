Part II

Emotions bound to one
who stopped caring
"that way"
long ago.

When that comforting,
could soothe the ache
of every hurt
and all the trials
life can put you
through.

Honor binds the holes
where memory
brings thoughts
of youth and promises,
long dead.

Two that once
made one,
did not divide,
but fractioned 
by the years
into pieces,
now rolled away.
Our feet
were clay.

Each reached out,
in different ways,
to mend the tears
that split
anyway...
we're still here.

Fears of lonliness,
ate up time
in ragged bites,
when neither
asked the other,
who should go?

Trapped.
