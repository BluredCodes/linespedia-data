title: "inviting you"
writer: "tigerlilly"

may i come 
to dance with
you?

it's true
the steps are words
and music comes from verse 
rhythm from 
a metered line
so new

please
take 
my hand

then listen
while we wait
as speech
will orchestrate
the tune we sing
in this
merry poet's
band

sweet muse

springs forth
fresh like strains
from violin 
resting  
happy near
my ear
as we whirl 
across 
the page

letters form
this great theater
wrapped around
the stage
where houselights
dim and 
word ballet
begins

upright
feathered
quills 

inked 
and ready
flow with life 
loving steady
racing onward
penning
words to
sing

brief
phrases
turn
to sentences 
clear mountain waters
tumbling down
to decorate
the bare rock 
face 
of life

wanton
spirit
that
i am
come dance
with me

the words
await 
for you
the book is
open
still
blank leaves
to see

lined out
steady
ever ready
longing
always longing
with words
of love 
and laughter
to be
filledall comments and critiques welcomed
