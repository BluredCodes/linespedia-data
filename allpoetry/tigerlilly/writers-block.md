I’ve spent the night conversing
With the MS paperclip…
Blank document in Word waits,
He says, “Let her rip!”

I told him that I would indeed,
Dance my fingers 'cross these keys,
Filling pages with sweet words;
But inspiration flees.

Well, he scratched his head,
Raised his brows, blinked his beady eyes;
Then sat looking sideways,
As if this was no surprise.

He’s seen me sitting mind-blank,
So many times before…
Giving me a look like “Gee whiz!
Wish I could help you more."

"There’s that folder on your desktop,
You’ve filled with false starts, dim hope,
When lack of inspiration…
Made you wish for hangman’s rope."

I see you shrug your shoulders,
Like me, ideas... you have none.
It must be time to go to bed,
