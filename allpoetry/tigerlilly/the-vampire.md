the call of darkness
the sense of danger
you're reaching out 
to touch a stranger
in the night
he's waiting for you
waiting only for you

there stands another
waiting for someone
for someone like you
who walks in darkness
who flirts with danger
in the night
darkness calls to you

is it your destiny?
to walk in darkness
with some one stranger
flirting with danger
in the night
he's watching for you
so crimson and ivory

bare your throat 
spill your life's blood
deep in the night
seek immortality
he's waiting for you
is this your destiny?
he's watching for you

a heart's stake driven
lies in your choice
flirt with darkness
live in the nightshape's
curse of death
is he waiting for you?
blood roses in bloom to see

destined to darkness
a hazy shadow
a tall dark stranger
the call of darkness
flirting with danger
is this for you?
