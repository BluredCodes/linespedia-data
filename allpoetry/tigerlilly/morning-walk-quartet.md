Gray wispy fingers
Leave swirling graceful streamers
Trees in morning fog

Dewdrops meet in pools
On leaves of vining vinca
Wash flower faces

Scattered corn attracts
Doves from their flight to landing
Breakfast on the wing

Koi in the pond
Nudge frogs aside to hasten
