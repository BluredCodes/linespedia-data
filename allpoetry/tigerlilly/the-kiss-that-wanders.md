title: "The Kiss That Wanders"

Candle-lighted moisture
Gleams on the curving
Planes of lips...
Contours invite fine
Tracings of a lover's
Fingertips.

Warm breath flows gently
Past these slightly
Parted gates...
Soft tempting tongue
Inside resides,
Coyly awaits...

That first and gentle
Pressing touch, tender
In the night...
Promising so much more,
As hours
Pass in flight.

In sweetest explorations,
Two enjoy mouth's
Honey wine...
Tongues caressing, 
Playing,
In a kiss divine.

Then whispered words
Of passion escape
Into the air...
As this kiss begins
To wander, wander…
