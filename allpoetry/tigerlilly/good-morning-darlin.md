good morning, darlin'
time to get up
hope you slept well
last night

hoping also
when i held you
that i loved 
you just right

we can keep away
together
winter's knocking
at our door

your arms
give me the shelter
that i was
once searching for

this magic spell 
called loving you
keeps me
out of the rain

next time
life thunders
i'll run into
your arms
again.all comments and critiques welcomed
