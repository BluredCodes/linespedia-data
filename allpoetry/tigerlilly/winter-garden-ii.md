philodendron pales
	from enforced hibernation
	   kitchen windowsills

	     in terra cotta
        pots aloe yearns for sol's
	    heated summer kiss

	   ivies twine and cling
	   to surfaces just within
	   their seeking grasp

           blades of green herald
         hyacinths forced appearance
	   for it's sweet perfumeall comments and critiques welcomed
