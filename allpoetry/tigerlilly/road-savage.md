---
title: "road savage"
writer: "tigerlilly"

black satin ribbon winds before us
a rain-slicked highway in the night
red sabers clash there with green broadswords
flashing reflected traffic lights

ice cold wind swarms 'round the leathers
into your back i press my face
we've made this harley home sweet home now
our world is hurtling us through space

no roof above, no walls, no baggage
not one thing left to tie us down
we're on our own with this road savage
just said goodbye to our home town

my arms wrap 'round and hold him tighter
while road signs measure out the miles
though my heart skids on old ice patches
his love is warming up my smiles

roaring sound masks danger's klaxon
blind to all barriers in sight
bound in lust to this dark stranger
dreaming harley highway flight.all comments and critiques welcomed
