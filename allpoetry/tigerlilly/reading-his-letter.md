words in a letter written by him
step off white pages
into my open arms

breath hastens as I read
his desriptions
of need

kisses feeding desires
banked each time
we’re apart

first he would satisfy
every thirst that I own
in my soul

then again for he says
giving me joy makes
his heart whole

he hasn’t forgotten
laughs, jokes, tickles
and smiles

playing waiter with only
a bottle, a wine glass
while wearing a towel  

then the playing is done

we join two as one

with love

closing my letterall comments and critiques welcomed
