Just a little ordinary… 

L
O
V
I
N
G

Is all I ever need…

I won’t ask for fireworks
Or for shooting stars
I’ll look for comfy coziness
Kisses sweet as candy bars

This is what I want to share
With some special one…

That I can call sweetheart
And we can walk together
Know we’ll never part
…Never part

Just an ordinary heart…

R
E
A
D
Y

To give and to receive… 

A little special warmth and fire
Long talks of faith and love
Throw in a few moonlight walks
With all the joys above

This is what I want to share
With some special one…

That I can call sweetheart
And we can walk together
Know we’ll never part
…Never part

Here’s to… 

H
O
P
I
N
G

One more heart…

Will

‘till then…
