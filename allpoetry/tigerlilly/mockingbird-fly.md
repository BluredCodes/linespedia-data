Mockingbird
Singing outside my door today,
Spread your wings little one…
Take my troubles away.

When your song is done,
And you’re ready to fly…
Turn my tears into raindrops,
As you head toward the sky.

If your next stop…
Brings you to the sycamore tree,
Sing a long lonesome love song,
Please sing it for me.

But hedge apple’s your next perch,
You’ve come back to my door…
If you need some new sad songs,
I’ll teach you some more.all comments and critiques welcomed :)
