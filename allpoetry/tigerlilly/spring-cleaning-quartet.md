Dust motes in sunlight
Lemon oil scents busy air
Time for Spring cleaning

Tangle of hangers
Attack with moth balls sharp bite
Winter coats hung up

Expose oak floor boards
Rugs rolled up and stored away
Bedrooms greet Summer

Trade cookbooks on shelf
With hearty soups and good bread
Bid Autumn goodbyeall comments and critiques welcomed
