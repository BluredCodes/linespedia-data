title: "the woman breaks through"
writer: "tigerlilly"

you said you liked my denim jeans
the old chambray shirt with worn-out seams

you said there's nothing you would suppose
like all the freckles on my turned up nose

you said my black hair shone raven in sun
when we brought our paired horses out for a quick run

you said my soft voice caused you to sigh
but there's something now that you're puzzled by

we've been together all of these years
and you've never ever seen me trailing tears

down the tom-boy face you're so used to
never once saw a sad look or me feeling blue

now here at the last when you haven't a clue
my lashes are wet as the woman breaks through

it was i guess something you overlooked
through all the years that i cleaned and cooked

till you came home from a night on the town
and found sweet joy in a pink cotton gown

and the satin came through on my skin
pressed close to you so warm and then

the silk was the feel of my lips on your brow
that's how i'd like to be kissing you now

if you hadn't just told me goodbye
caused these sad tears that now fill my eyes

ending all thoughts of me and of you
and right about here the woman breaks through

boots scuff the dirt on the old country lane
as i watch you leave my world fills with  pain

this tomboy heart won't know what to do
