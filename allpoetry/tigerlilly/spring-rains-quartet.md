Rivulets of words
Condensing punctuation
Rain’s letter on glass

Guttered waterspout
Rain barrel overflowing
Lawn drinks in excess

Roof leak drips in pan
Forgotten on sunny days
Neglect’s metronome

Earths renewing state
Nature’s amniotic flux
