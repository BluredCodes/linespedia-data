diamonds of spring
are sunshined dewdrops strung 
on blades of grass

graceful curving reeds
bending over water's face
shade the summer light

autumn leaves falling
from the frosty branch of home
reveal persimmons

torpid morning thoughts
swimming slowly like chilled koi
in a winter pondall comments and critiques welcomed
