My New Year’s resolution was to watch my weight,
and so the foods I love too much have been banished 
from my plate. And though I broil and grill with pride 
and steam the veggies, give up the sides, I’d throw
that new size 8 away for something southern fried
today. Slim for summer is my goal, but oh, how 
it does take it's toll when every night is filled
with dreams of covert trips to the Krispy 
Kreme.  One Sonic Blast, thought
divine, but I couldn’t hide it on
my waistline. I have been told it
does get easier, as the days go 
by, to pass up all your favorite foods
and not even blink an eye.  I just wonder
if the truth in fact, is that we’re too weak to care,
from cycling, jogging, aerobics class, all efforts to
arrange the body mass in a shape that’s pleasing to
the eye, but oh! for a slice of chocolate pie. I think as
the eighteenth glass of water I drink sits in the space 
where a Big Mac should be and I cannot in good faith
possibly see how I can swallow one more helping of 
broccoli, and I’ve gone orange from all the carrots
I’ve eaten, and I know thoughts like this could be
self defeating, so then I must keep on repeating..
good work, keep it up, finish your greens,and 
smile as you slip on those 
junior size jeans.all comments and critiques welcomed
