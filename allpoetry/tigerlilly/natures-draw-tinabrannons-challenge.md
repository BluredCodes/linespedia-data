The lake
Reflects to sky,
Portraitures of Nature
Passing by.  Each season
Stops to talk in pictures, ambling
Slowly on it’s walk.  In this mirror, all
Are seen, in colors crisp and clean.
These shaded forest hues
Draw my eye to
The lake.all comments and critiques welcomed :)
