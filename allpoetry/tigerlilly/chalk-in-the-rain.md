with 
lights turned down
 to dim glow
music plays our song's 
 refrain
we hold each other 
tightly
circling dance steps
sighing names

just 
outside our window
worlds are
 passing in the night
while here
 in our hotel room
love and kisses
seem so right

hours 
stretch before us
though we're always 
wanting more
secret city rendezvous
“do not disturb”
 sign
on the door

pressing 
close in tenderness
like 
lovers everywhere
caresses 
lead to passion
melting all
our cares

as 
 raindrops beat
against dark glass
our hearts still
 sound as one
with
minutes 
now remaining
after
 loving’s done

 though
 staging our performance
 brings us
 both some happiness

reality rolls in
 like fog

city hotel room
then
dissolves

like chalk 
in
 the rainall comments and critiques welcomed
