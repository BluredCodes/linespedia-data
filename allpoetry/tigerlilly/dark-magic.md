my elusive far off dream man
so many miles away
can still work his dark magic
with memories that play

across the barren deserts
in the lonesome's of my mind
then take me to the center
of the heat i want to find

on this journey in the moonlight
his fingers are a caravan
seeking sweet oasis
striking wetness in the sand

my mind runs to shelter
in the arms i'm longing for
nights once were so endless
till his shade came to my door

now he's tracing satin skin
trekking over mountains bare
down smooth slopes he's sliding 
a hunter near a valley there

strong bow taut and ready
he enters forested expanse
lets fly his heated arrow
i haven't got a chance...

i won't run and hide from passion
even in a memory
although i'll cry tomorrow
