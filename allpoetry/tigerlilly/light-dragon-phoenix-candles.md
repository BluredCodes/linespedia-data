Pumelo waters 
Summer evils cleansed away
Bathed in new garments

Red silk veil covers
Shy blushing face of new bride
Married woman’s hair

Coverings removed
Two gold goblets of honey wine
Lives linked with red thread

In bridal chamber
Encouraged by visitors
Love’s cups are exchangedDragon-Phoenix = man-woman
pumelo = similar to grapefruit, very fragrant
