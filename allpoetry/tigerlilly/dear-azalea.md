Dear 
Azalea
I’m so sorry,
Took your cover
Off too soon…
You were a full
Hedge standing there
Waiting for your
Chance to bloom.

I thought the cold
Was over,
But freezing rains
Draped you 
With ice;

And
Now
I’ve
Had
To
Trim
The
Damaged
Branches
