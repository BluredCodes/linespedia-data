Over time, so many fallen, on the fields that rage,
today some names, now in the news…
how many stop to read the page?

To say a prayer, to thank them, for standing in our stead…
to understand, how many, in standing for us,
are now dead.

They stood where you and I were not,
alone in death and danger…
not knowing you or I
they stood prepared to die.

If they can give so much for us…
please say a prayer for each stranger.so many have fallen for our nation through all the years of strife... men and women of courage...i thank you.
