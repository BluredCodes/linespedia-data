Fooling myself.
Look into the mirror,
Stare into my eyes,
Know down in my heart
That I’m telling…
I’m telling lies.

Fooling myself.
Waiting for his call,
Sitting by the phone,
Know he’s with another
While I’m here…
All alone.

Lying to myself,
He doesn’t care at all.
She’s flying double
With him,
And I…
I’m just in freefall.all comments and critiques welcomed
