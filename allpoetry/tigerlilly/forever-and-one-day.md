title: "forever and one day"
writer: "tigerlilly"
slug: "forever-and-one-day"
source: "AllPoetry"

on life's calendar my special love
rates a bold red-letter day

when Cupid on his cloud above
holds our hearts in sway

it's not Christmas nor Valentine's
nor a birthday filled with cheer

just an ordinary day at random
when we meet just once each year

like the movie from so long ago
two romantics build their sphere

a shining world unto themselves
including all that they hold dear

calls and letters fill three sixty four
but on that one day we stand at a door

leading to our garden of delight
sharing one day making love all night

it may seem strange if you don't know
of this special place where we go

here our hourglass is full at last
we dream of future cherish past

then through storms of the coming year
hope is given in lessened fears

as time brings us back to replay
