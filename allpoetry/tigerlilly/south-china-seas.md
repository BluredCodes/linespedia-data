junked 

not trashed 
but boated 
shipped out
today
setting sail 
i'm so free 

time

unrelated 
antiquated 
threw my 
watch
in the sea 

life 

bends 
itself 
to the shape
of the next wave 
in sight 

chances
 
are 
yang peak
sees
the jade door
tonight

freed 

inhibitions 
and more 
left them
all on the shore 
sailing 
south china seasall comments and critiques welcomed
