silence

no calls
e-mails or IM's

there's nothing 
from him

icy cold fingers
of dread
grip tightly
chilling
last night's
warm heart

rank tendrils
of worry
creep
into frayed edges
of thoughts

stop
 
startled

mirrored glance
showing
how pale a color
fear is

ticking clock

sounding of
anvils and hammers
shivers
the chill dawn's
measure
of minutes

but 
still

only 
silencecomments and critiques welcomed
