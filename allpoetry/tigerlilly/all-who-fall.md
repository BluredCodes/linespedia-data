gliding figures
skating on thin ice
reality

frozen hearts
too numbed for pain
unfeeling

widening cracks
dark waters seeping
into life

gaping hole
claiming all who fall
to strife

frigid air
would warm
the breathless

bloodied hand
slips out of sight
ice caresseswell...it just kinda felt like how things get near the end of anything...i don't know what to make of it...'twas just there.
