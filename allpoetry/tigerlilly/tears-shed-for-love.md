tears shed for love

tears are the measure of a hearts true reach,
when someone in love would so beseech...
with prayers that our cries would strike the right chord
of the fates
who control these moves on the board.

celestial harps are strummed by passing bright wings,
gathering tears of lovers...these silvery things.
the count then is tallied by a heavenly host,
who sends along a message
by cupid's own post.

and this is a secret, between me and you...
cupid's arrows are tipped with your tears silver dew.
so now may your heart be at it's peaceful best,
the dart has been dispatched, 
love does the rest.all comments and critiques welcomed
