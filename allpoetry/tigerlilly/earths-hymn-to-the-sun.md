Golden orb in azure skies,
your rays absorbed
to quicken life…
conceived and waiting
in earth's womb.

Warm finger-rays so gently
tug the emerald shoots
to worlds above,
well received your nurturing
light on newborn leaves.

Unfurling, then behold,
rainbow’s reflected glow, 
unfolding flower faces,
raised to azure skies…
golden orb passing by.all comments and critiques welcomed  :)
