when darkness rules
night is queen
her angels step
from shadows

and she speaks
to men

soothing voice
small warm hand
inviting
closeness
speaking
sin

she speaks
louder then

breath sighs
open up your
night eyes
look at me
before
i'm gone

moving in a dream
it seems
to him

stirring closer
next to them

angels seeking
honey in the shadows

die
at dawnall comments and critiques welcomed
