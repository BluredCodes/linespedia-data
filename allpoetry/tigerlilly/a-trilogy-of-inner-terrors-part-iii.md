Part III

More promises,
bound to keep...
tired steps
move through fogbanks,
lost sleep...
one more minute,
one more day,
moving on.

Trapped in isolation,
this time by love.
Giving over all
that's left inside,
to those
who carried me,
led me when blinded...
helped me stand
when weak legs
collapsed.

Trapped.
