title: "bind me..."
writer: "tigerlilly"

close my eyes
       bind my touch 
       away
       that i might
       better hear words
       spoken
       
       to heightened
       captured senses
       pouring
       honey
       in the sweet spot
       of my mind

       stray thought breezes
       blow gently cross
       love-dewed
       silky raven
       cleft
     
       warm dark
       breath sounds
       heard by my woman
       crying hungry
       in the night

       restless winds
       released
       impassioned
       cries
       escape

       lust's bondage
       scattered
       ties
       beat against
       fierce thrust

       fall back
       unravelled
       well travelled
       by your
       manall comments and critiques welcomed
