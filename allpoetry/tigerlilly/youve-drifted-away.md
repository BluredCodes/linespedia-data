---
title: "you've drifted away"
writer: "tigerlilly"
slug: "youve-drifted-away"
source: "AllPoetry"

like autumn's leaf
on a pond
gentle breezes
pushed you on
past my reach
beyond
all recall

winter's snowflake
melts so fast
on my tongue
it didn't last
just a chill
where
it fell
now remains

spring's dandelion
fluff in the air
wind currents
swirling
you away 
to my despair
i didn't
know

summer's red rose
petals i see
fallen
faded
like your love
for me
now it's gone
and i miss
you so

you gave a year
in the garden
of loving care
i didn't notice
the damage
season's passage
brought to us
there

like a fool
i closed my eyes
hoping you
would stay
but from love's
paradise
you've simply
drifted
