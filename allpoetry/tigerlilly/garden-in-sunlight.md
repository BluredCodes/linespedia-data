Golden spring pollen
Nestled in fragrant needles
Promise of pinecones

Forsythia frames
Spring’s wakening garden with
Yellow bloom arches

Dogwood’s swelling buds
Chase spring’s partner in bloom time
Catch pussy willow

Chives grapple with leeks
Pushing upward to sunlight
