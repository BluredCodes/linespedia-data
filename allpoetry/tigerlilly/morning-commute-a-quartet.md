yarn drawn from a bag
mind tracking to next station
knitter drops stitches

glass reflects student
schoolbook's pages left unturned
absent from classroom

baby's breath milky
bubbles marking rosy lips
speaks of laughing cows

speeding morning train
man covers last night's daydreams
under newspaperall comments and critiques welcomed
