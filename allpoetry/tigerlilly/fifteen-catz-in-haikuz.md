title: "fifteen catz in haikuz"

fluffy gray tabby
portioned across sliding books
precarious nap

the tuxedo cat
brought city notions to a
patchwork crazy quilt

with dark eyes glowing 
the cat measured the length of
her bath with pink tongue

hisssssss sister meows
loudly while treed on the shelf
brothers all alike

water heater topped
with black cat draped copper pipes
warm on winter nights

lapwarmer purrrsues
mouse dreams while chasing cursor's 
trails through cyberspace

a cat sentry sphinx
silently guarding elder 
mistress in the night

white aproned doll cat
monitors housekeeping from
her self-claimed domain

old tom reflecting
many years of better days
on sun-warmed brick path

the matriarch queen
rules all with iron-clawed edicts
dispatched with swift skill

crossing darkened room
black cat fastens self around
unwary ankles

radar ears detect
tuna-tins opening sound
orchestrated treat

she-devil leader
of mischievious forays
unguarded fish-tanks

frightened fuzzy gray
furbie static-sparking through
southern thunder storm

missing tail presents
tall order for short tabby
a balancing actall comments and critiques welcomed
