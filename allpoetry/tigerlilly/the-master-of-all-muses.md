In our everyday ordinary lives
beyond all human schemes,
great forces come together
to guide our hopes and dreams.

If left to us our visions
would not take us far...
but with inspiration's guidance
we can hope to catch a star...

look into the fragile moment
when a miracle is born,
whether daffodil or rosebud
or a baby unicorn.

Impossible, you say, i know
it seems hard to believe...
but the heart can picture
anything that our mind conceives.

It comes pouring out in love songs
in verse and rhyming word,
paintings by the masters,
the greatest symphony's we've heard...

because we look beyond ourselves,
with faith the planted seed...
the Master of all Muses,
