---
title: "the shepherd's love"
writer: "tigerlilly"
slug: "the-shepherds-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

from heaven's mount sweet waters flow
in a river of love coursing here below

near sunset's hour we two will meet
wading in the shallows, me and my sweet

yes, he...my precious turtledove
a gift i know from One above

for when he smiles and takes my hand
music swells from angel bands

his cheek near mine when we're together
feels like the brushing of angel's feather

and in Sol's fading golden glow
when the nightingale sings soft and low

with his arms around me holding tight
we watch the first stars of twilight

lips pressed to mine do make me sigh
his hand then drops from breast to thigh

scent of grass and flowers in the air
crushed by our weight as we lay there

with the song of water's dancing by
we gaze into each other's eyes

a question was asked but no words said
simply answered by a nod of the head

helping one another with latchet and lace
the barrier of garments we did erase

moonrise did dapple and pearl our skin
as we loved one another and felt no sin

for the riverbank was our wedding bed
sheep our witnesses in clergy's stead

the shepherd's love consumed the shepherdess
