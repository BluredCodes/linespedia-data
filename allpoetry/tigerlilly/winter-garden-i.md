purple crocus wrapped
 in green spears it's way through an
        ice field's formation
	
	against the white snow
    cardinal's wings flash red stop
	 for sunflower seeds
	
     frost-bleached winter grass
     creates contrast for glossy
        leaves on evergreens
	
        then southern winter
    warm spells call daffodils up
        into a false spring.all comments and critiques welcomed
