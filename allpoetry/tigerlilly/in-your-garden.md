title: "In Your Garden"

Each morning, I brush your hair,
look through the window...
tell you what I see there,
in your garden...
how it misses
your tender love and care.

The old tom cat you saved
last winter, looks
fat and sassy now...
I tell you this,
as I wash your face...
then kiss your precious brow.

The bird bath and the feeder,
I tend to these things
for you...
we never know beforehand
what trials
life will bring us to.

But one thing is as certain
as crocus
pushing through the sod...
while we speak of your garden,
Mama,
we're both holding hands with God.all comments and critiques welcomed :)
