A CHILD

A child's laughter while at play

The sweetest sound around

Their innocence precious

Unconditional love

Is the greatest to be found



Such little hands 

That work so hard

New worlds they do explore

Their great desire

To love and learn

They are to be adored



Those little legs

That run so fast

In a world so grand and new

Their splendor awe

Inquisitive

About what all things do



Great big smiles

Eyes so bright

Wonderous sweet and mild

Trusting loving

No prejudice

There is nothing 

More precious than a child
