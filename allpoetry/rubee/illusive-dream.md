ILLUSIVE DREAM

It was just a dream that lasted a while
But I could have sworn I saw him smile

But then again, that's what dreams do
Feels like you're there, just isn't true

You can see his face, look into his eyes
Hear his voice, it's just in your mind

His breath so warm, his hand in your hair
You know it's love but he's not really there

A touch so gentle as he holds your hand
You need him now, you can't understand

A love so strong, you feel his touch
Who is this man you love so much?

In your heart you can feel him near
But he's miles away, he can't be here

Should you awake and try to forget
This dream of love, no one you've met?

The tears will fall, your heart will break
The pain is real, the dream is fake

Do you hang on or do you let go?
You're just not sure, you just don't know

For hours on end you sit and wait
He doesn't come, is this your fate?

Maybe not now, things aren't what they seem
So you hang on......to this illusive dream.
