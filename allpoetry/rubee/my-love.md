MY LOVE

He rides down from the mountains

On a steed so fine and strong

He looks so noble and dashing

Ladies swoon as he strides along

But he only rides to be by my side

For the time has been much too long

Since we last shared our passion

But this night, to us, will belong



As he dismounts his faithful steed

Approaching, lifts me off the ground

Taking me in his strong, loving arms

And twirling me all around

We share a love so strong, eternal

No sweeter man could there be found

For me there would be no other

My heart to him, forever bound



He carries me to our chamber

Gently placing me on the bed

I can see a glimmer in his eyes

Sweet thoughts racing through my head

His absence had felt like forever

Now the loneliness that was, has fled

For this night will be spent together

Filled with passionate love instead



The candlelight dances upon the walls

As he gently brushes his lips on mine

I feel his soft touch upon my breast

A sensation so sweetly divine

He slowly descends to lay by my side

Our bodies and souls entwine

In sweet embrace, I look into his face

And know everything now, will be fine



~PennyLynne~
