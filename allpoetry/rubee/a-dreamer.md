A DREAMER

I use to be a dreamer
I dreamed a lifetime ago
Of times gone by and yet to be
Unknown lands yet to see
A universe yet to unfold
Many stories yet untold.

Until I met a realist
Who taught me all he knew
That dreams and such were for fools
Life was meant to live by rules
Dreams were just a fantasy
Not like life, reality.

I use to be a dreamer
And music filled my soul
Living songs that I had heard
My life revealed in every word
Melodies from the heav'ns above
Brought my world a peaceful love.

So now I've been a realist
Living life as planned
For many years on end
No childish pretend
Until I met a dreamer
Now I will dream again.
