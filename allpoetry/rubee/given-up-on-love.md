GIVEN UP ON LOVE

I had given up on love
Many years ago
It didn't seem meant for me
He never said "I love you so"

But in spite, we made a life
And had a family
Though I yearned to have his love
It could never be

I had given up on love
Too many years ago
And now I see how sad it's been
Not to feel or know
How love can make your life complete
I have missed it so

Then one day you came along
I'm sure it was meant to be
It felt that strong, we do belong
For all eternity

~*~*~*~*~*~

Could it be 
The Angels did see
I needed love
And sent you to me!
