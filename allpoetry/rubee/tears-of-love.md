TEARS OF LOVE
       Have you ever shed a tear
        Not because you were sad
          But because something
      Touched your heart and soul so
           strong, so deeply
    Its beauty overwhelmed your senses
    It's essence over powered all you
    had ever known up until that very
                 moment
    Have tears ever streamed down your
    face because something touched the
          very center of your soul
    You wanted to touch it, caress it
    Hold it ever so tenderly for always
            Have you ever cried
       Because you were truely happy
      Not because you gained wealth or
              material things
     But because you finally knew what
    true happiness really felt like deep
        inside the very heart of you
    Feeling the love that surrounds and
        encompasses us and each and
          everything we hold dear
  All the real treasures in this world are
                  free
      And all that much more beautiful
      If you could share them with me
