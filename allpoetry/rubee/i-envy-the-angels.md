I ENVY THE ANGELS



I envy the angels

as they are able to gaze upon you while you sleep

I long to be a treasure 

something you hold close to your heart and keep



I envy the oceans

for I know that is where your heart belongs

I long to be the music

for I know you feel the rhythm of the songs



I envy the rains

they are able to touch your face as you walk by

I long to be a gentle breeze

it has the power to dry the tears that you might cry  



I envy the moon and stars

for you watch them with such wondrous delight

I long to be the dreams

you dream that make everything seem alright



I envy the rainbow

that captures your joy, amazing you so much

I long to be a flower 

for it is blessed to feel your gentle touch



I envy the sunset

as you look upon it in its splendor

I long to be a poem

To hear your voice that speaks so tender



I envy the universe

For she holds you in her arms and gives you life

I long to be the sun

It gives you warmth and eases all your strife



I envy all that you adore

for you are as wonderous as you could be

I long to be your love

for Love, you are a part of me.



~PennyLynne~
