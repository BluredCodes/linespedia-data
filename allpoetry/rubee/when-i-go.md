WHEN I GO

When you lose me
You will cry for your loss
You will then understand the pain
That was in my heart for so long
You will wish for the hours that were wasted
The times you neglected to think were important
You will feel the emptiness 
That I've felt many times without you
You will wish for those 
Crazy days when I made you laugh
You will miss my child-like ways
And my innocence
You will long for my gentle touch
And sweet caresses
You will miss the passion that lurks
Inside the woman that is in me
I have not yet gone
But only give you fair warning
For it won't be much longer
Then I will have to be on my way
And though I'll love you til my end
I can no longer pretend
The spaces don't hurt my very soul
If you fail to see it now
There will be no reason
For me to stay
