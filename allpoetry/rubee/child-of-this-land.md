A CHILD OF THIS LAND

Mothers teach your children
Please, please do not be sad
You were born a native
A child of this land

Now some will try to sway you
Some will make you feel so bad
That you were born a native
A child of this land

I know there was a time
But my child things have changed
We were once proud people
Til our lives were rearranged
Please don't be discouraged
For we can be again
You were born a native
A child of this land

Mothers teach your sons
That they can still be strong
And when this world seems bitter
They will get along
For no matter what some people say
It doesn't make it right
You're a child of this land
And we must all unite

Mothers teach your sons
An answer can be found
But not in a bottle
Or lying neath the ground
Teach him that proud and tall
Is the way that he must stand
For he is a native son
A child of this land

Mothers teach your children
There are no rules for race
You are no worse or better
By the color of your face
We are all God's children
And together we must stand
For we're all sons and daughters
Children of God's land

My child please remember
And try to understand
Life isn't always easy
But you're a child of this land
