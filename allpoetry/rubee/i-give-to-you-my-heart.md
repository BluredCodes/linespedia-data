I GIVE TO YOU MY HEART

I give to you my heart, Love
I place it gently in your hand
But there are a few conditions
You must first understand

It is a very fragile heart
It's been dropped a time or two
It took a long time to mend
Now I'm entrusting it to you

Please don't neglect it
Forgetting that it's there
Whatever you do, don't drop it
For it would break beyond repair

I pray that you will hold it strong
Keep it close and warm
For if you do these things Love
It will last a long, long time

I give to you my heart, Love
Please take tender care.
