CONQUISTADOR



Conquistador you've travelled the world

Searching for your Eldorado

Your journey is done, for I am the one

That you have been searching for



You may have thought that it was gold

That led you far and wide

Searching the corners of the earth

Until I was at your side



Through many battles you have fought

Losing more than you had won

Always searching but never finding

Golden treasures in the sun



Sailing the seas to other lands

Have all taken it's toll

The lasting scars of wounds so deep

Have cut right to your soul



Many years have passed but still you search

When will you let it go?

Your journey is done, for I am the one

I am your Eldorado



Conquistador, search no more

For you have found your Eldorado



~PennyLynne~
