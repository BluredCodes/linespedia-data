SATURDAY NIGHT

She's ready for love
On a Saturday night
With her hair done up
Her makeup just right

A splash of perfume
To add to the mood
Silk stockings and heels
And she's looking good

Sexy and stunning
In her black neglige
She stands seductively
In the doorway

Soft music is playing
The lights are down dim
Her hand on her thigh
As she motions to him

A playful smile
On her ruby red lips
She gives him a wink
How could he resist?

She's ready for love
And feeling just right
But this is Canada
And damn.......
It's hockey night!!!
