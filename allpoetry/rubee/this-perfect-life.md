THIS PERFECT LIFE

This perfect life
Yet void of love
I count my blessings
From above

A home, a family
Life's daily needs
Save for love
As my heart bleeds

It seems I can only
Have it one way
This perfect life
Here, do I stay?

Should I choose love
All else must die
How do I choose?
My soul does cry

A love I yearn
But cannot obtain
Do I have a right
To even complain?

To love I lose
To lose I gain
This perfect life
With so much pain!
