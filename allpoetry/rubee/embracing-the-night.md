EMBRACING THE NIGHT

I embrace the nighttime
A time of calm and peace
Safely in the darkness
That's when I feel at ease

My world becomes so quiet
Movement for the day is done
No demands are placed on me
And I can be alone

My thoughts are much clearer then
I can think at last
The hustle and the bustle
Of the day are finally passed

I can sit and ponder
Reflect on who I am
See where I need improving
And hope tomorrow that I can

I truely am grateful
For this time and space
I embrace the nighttime
It is my saving grace.
