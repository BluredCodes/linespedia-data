I'M OKAY

There was a time
I was free as a breeze
Now I am plagued
With anxieties

A feeling of doom
Pangs of fear
Ever aware
If an exit is near

Please be patient
While I try to decide
If I should forge forward
Or just run and hide

Someday I'll be better
I know that I will
But for the meantime
I'll just pop this pill

So when you see me
And I look somewhat dazed
That's just the panic
I'm really okay.
