One day we were bound to fade away 	
		Like a broken boat in the bay	
It happened very soon
	AND BOY WAS IT A BOOM !
It hurt really bad 	
	And may us both really sad!
I cried for weeks 
	But just told you I was weak
		You said the same 	
			But wait…. This must be FATE!
I knew you were worth my tears 	
		And my smiles
	When we first met 
The sleepover s that w had weren’t 
			So bad 
We went to many different places and 
		We saw a lot of different faces 
