my mind is clouded
my senses weaken 
others are speaking in close proximity
and what on earth can the matter be
they lower their voices
stand with their heads bowed
unaware that i stare with unseeing eyes
it is unwise to assume 
the reason for my pain
i have nothing to gain
but more pain
please dear friends make amends and when you speak of me understnd my situation
accept my gratification of peace 
of sleep
sleep is so sweet
so soothing
so complete a rest
it is best that i part with a happy soul donated to another 
let him or her have my a life with the help of a surgeons knife
please let that person be enhanced with a second chanceto live
i give only the part that i know i no longer need take heed stranger
no danger ahead 
this is the end for me 
the beginning for you
i know what i have to do ;
my last gift will come unwrapped
bared for all to see but my soul remains
forever in eternity,
no i can leave in a while 
i know that my last breath forms a smile
