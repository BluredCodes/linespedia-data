there was a day when i was happy 
  there was a day that i had no worries 
  there was a day that i thought that i could do anything that i wanted 
then the day came when i was on the beach and that  asshole got a hold of me 
 i got dragged 
i screamed i did not want to get hurt
  i was so scared 
  i was so weak 
          the whole day is so bleak to me 
               every day i think about it 
                 every day i  cry 
i need help to get through my days  help from some one who understands my anger and my sadness
im a mess 
   the day was so horrible 
 that guy did not read his bible 
he must have been on sick bastard
  maybe even a retard 
   i hate that guy he is off now 
  he took his bow 
o wow
i loved my day before that 
i am scared of the beach at night 
i feel like i have been seen by the devil 
the evil that has taken me in just makes my back shivver
