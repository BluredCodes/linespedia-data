title: "ALL  OF YOU"

ALL MY LIFE I THOUGHT THAT I WAS MISSING SOMETHING 
THEN I LOOKED AT YOU AND I REALIZED THAT I HAD EVERYTHING THAT I WOULD EVER NEED

ALL THIS YEAR I THOUGHT THAT I WOULD NEVER LOVE AGAIN
I THOUGHT THAT I WOULD GET OVER YOU 
AND I DID 

ALL THIS TIME MY LOVE FOR YOU WAS SO REAL 
AND YOURS WAS FAKE

I LOVE YOU CAME OUT OF YOUR MOUTH 
AS DID IT MINE 
BUT I MEANT IT AND YOU DIDNT 

YOU BROKE IT OFF AND THEN I BLEW MY BRAINS 
THANK GOD I DID NOT DIE 
CUZ IN MY WILL I TOLD THEM THAT IT WAS YOUR FAULT

THEN I FOUND OUT THAT YOU STILL CARED 
AND I WAS DONE WITH THE HEART ACHE
SO NOW BECAUSE I CAN NEVER TRULY LOVE AGAIN
