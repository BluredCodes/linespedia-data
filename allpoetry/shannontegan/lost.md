i was in a tunnel
and could not see the light
and whenever i'd look up
i couldnt see the sky

sometimes when im standing 
i feel like i have been walking for miles
and my heart could be crying
dead in the middle of a smile

but then i climbed the hills
and wow i saw the mountains
i yelled help cuz'
i was lost

then i felt the strong wind
heard a small voice say 
the storm is over 
and i can see the sunshine 
some where beyond 
the clouds
i feel heaven 
and heaven is over me

i tell my god to come down
come down and set me free

but now in the midst of my battle
all hope is gone
down town in a rushed crowd

i felt all alone
and every now and then i felt like i would lose 
i have been racing for years but still no finish line 

some how my begining 
stepped right in 
the faith became my friend
but my enemy 
at the same time
and now i can depend on the voices of the wind
