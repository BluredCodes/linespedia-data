Between the searching and the need to work it out 
I stop believing everything will be alright
I'm walking uphill being turned around and round
Secret in motion when my feet are on the ground 
In my mind's eye
One little girls anger one womans cries
Funny how time flies
It seems so over that there is no more beginning and there will be no end
It seems like the weed is smoke is gone
It seems like life is too over to be over 
It seems like im in kindegarten again 
My lies are not even there any more 
There is too much anger to lie 
She makes my life miserable and she makes me wanna die
So many times I have tried and there some one is trying to save me frome something that icrave
Something so needed 
Something so intense
That would upset so many 
But what if that happened 
Would you cry?
Or would you sigh?
What exactly would come out of it ?
