Too Crazy even for you !

						By  Shannon Tegan


Last nite was so crazy..
I smoked weed that was just awesome
I was so out of control 
It was magical 
I lit up my bone
And just laid there

I was so drunk 
And you made it ok

You made it seem like there was more to think about then HIM
I love you but I  am too crazy even for you..
I think that I love you 
I think that I will be friends with you forever
I think that you will be there for me for ever
My mind is weak and drugged
My life is wild and crazy 
But you are too 

 Still I believe that I am too crazy even for you.

I walk  in the night with the moon
And I think about ways to get through my life ?
Is that possible at all ? or is it completely insane ?
Its insane I know but come on just take it in one day at a time take like it was a wad of money given to you from god for you to do anything with?
What would you do ?
I would go out and buy myself something but I don’t know what I would buy I 
I would love everyminute that I spent with you if I could spend time with you ?
I think that I am out of control
You?
I think that I will be like this forever
I think that I will be my only friend
That is true to my feelings
