Warning I could crack and day 
   And who knows when ?
Not me that’s for sure 
WARNING! WARNING! WARNING!
I am a girl.
 I am a teen. 
I am 17 
WARNING! WARNING! WARNING! 
I cry.
I am in love. 
And I am lost and confused.

WARNING! WARNING! WARNING!
I have flown in the night.
 And I have cried in the night.
WARNING! WARNING! WARNING!
