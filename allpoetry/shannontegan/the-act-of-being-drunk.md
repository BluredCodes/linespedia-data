the act of being drunk 
it is supposed to be only for 21 year olds right?
well im 17 and im drunk
im like an old rug 
torn to pieces b/c of all this liquor i intake
and rake in 
its fucked up 
i can walk in a store and buy a beer and walk out with it
not like a guy 
you cant walk in to a store and buy a nice guy with nice hair and 
a perfectdick 
nope you cant you can only walk in and buy beer
as if the perfect guy is not a necessity 
b/c it so is..
i need a perfect guy but where is that angel tha will 
take my life and turn it into the life that i wanna live
i would drop that beer in a minute to find that perfect guy
