ALL THIS TIME 
I KNEW THAT SOMEDAY YOU'D HAVE TO FIND 
SOMETHING THAT YOU LEFT BEHIND
ALL THESSE TEARS 
AND LIKE A LIGHT LOVE DISAPPEARS 
BUT HEARTS ARE GOOD FOR SOUVENEIRS
AND MEMORIES ARE FOREVER
ALL THIS TIME 
ALL IN ALL I HAVE NO REGRETS 
THE SUN STILL SHINES
AND THE SUN STILL SETS 
THE HEART FORGIVE AND THE HEART FORGETS 
BUT WHAT WILL I DO WITH THIS TIME 
ONE MORE KISS
EVEN THOUGH IT ITS COME TO THIS 
ILL CLOSE MY EYES AND MAKE A WISH 
HOPING THAT YOU REMEMBER
