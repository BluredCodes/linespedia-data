You Don't have to go out 
Lookin' for trouble because
Trouble will find you
it's just a  matter of time
That's when you're gonna need someone behind you
A true friend isn't easy to find

 You're gonna find out
You're gonna find out who your true friends are
There will come one at  a time
Somewhere down the line
 

 A Room full of people 
 start talkin' 
 only Talkin' about you when you're not there
Who'll be the one who won't play that game?
Who's gonna stand up for your good name?

 

I'll be the one that
That will pull you back
When you've taken one step too far
That's when you'll know who
Who really loves you
You're gonna find out who your true friends are

Now people say
Almost anything
There's no way to see inside their hearts
But they you'll see who runs away
And who's there with you when the trouble starts

so what if I’m a little nutty..
im a cool shit.
Im a good person to have on your side

You ARE LOSING ME THOUGH 
Ever time you are mean to me 
I will get mad
And will forgive you ..

Y ? I ask you ?
Maybe b/c I like you as a friend
Or maybe b/c I wanna be forever friends
I guess you’ll never know.

Until that day comes where you say 
That you hate me 
I will be there for you to cry on my shoulder
To punch in the face when you are mad
And to tell your problems too..

But I cant stop those people who talk 
That’s not my style.
I’ll just give them a look that “says dude.”
Get a life
That girl is my best friend
She always will be till the day I die.
