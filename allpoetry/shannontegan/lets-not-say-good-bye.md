lets not say good bye 
because it only leave me 
drying my eyes
i know we willnever meet again 
but everytime your not around
i'll just pick one of our many pictures 
and look at your smiles
you are wonderful people
in and out 
i would never want to say good bye
not even for a little while
so when we are gone from outside of these doors

you will all forever and always have a special place in my heart


*************to all my friend i love you with all of my heart
