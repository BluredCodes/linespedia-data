naked desperation glares behind
eyes that lie so irrationally quiet.
                   Come seeking an unbearable emotion
                 harboring in a self piteous fear::
        Give away the throne,
        for all our laughter is gone.

        Horrified that change explodes,
destroying a carefully constructed mold
so delicate, one breath collapsed--

                   Take the desire to heal
        And Breathe warmth into this glass.
