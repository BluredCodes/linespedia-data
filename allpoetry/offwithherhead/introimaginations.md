it is crucial to stay focused.

inhale,...

       exhale,...

Begin.

Only now is my paradise so vivid

Blackest blue of water, 

                       sky-

    twisting winds, salty, livid

  A castle lighting behind my 

                       eye.

            Breathe in,...one

                             two

                               Breathe out.

Gazing across this vast raging ocean

      suffocated by the enchantment alone possessed

Diving from cliff into this swirling potion

   Elixir of midnight liquid infests

                    inhale      one

                                  two

                              three

                                  four

            exhale     five

                          six

                      seven

                          eight.

Standing mesmerized on rocky shore

           Chanting with an aquatic grace

                   eerie hypnotic voices sing for, 

I, who am one with the sea in this place.



FOCUS inhale

           exhale  again

one two three four

                   breathe in five six breathe out.

My eyes are clouded, but mind is free

Body is lost in meditative dream

        I've built my world for only I to see

    I've lost myself in meditative dream.

Inhale,...

Exhale,...

Breathe.
