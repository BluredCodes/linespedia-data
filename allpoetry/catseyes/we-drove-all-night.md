title: "we drove all night"
writer: "catseyes"

we drove all night.

the city lights made our eyes
temperamental, disapproving

but we kept moving
cracked the windows
listened 
looked to heaven
to give us direction
watched the sky turn purple
and streams of grey
running through the middle
to get away from the state lines

in the thick of conversation
we missed wisconsin
by over thirty minutes

almost forgot what our names were
as each rest stop 
rested without us
and our destination ran 
as fast as we were driving

like a bonnie and clyde
getaway, like runaways in hiding

our escape, a blinding love
and perfect timing,
us, eloping today
