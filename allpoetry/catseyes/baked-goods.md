---
title: "baked goods"
writer: "catseyes"
slug: "baked-goods"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/31069-baked-goods-by-catseyes"
---

Like yeast, I rise
above the humility 
of becoming an ingredient

the mixture
beaten a hundred strokes
to remove all lumps
of imperfection

the baker, never satisfied
with the combination

more spice
more sugar
a dash of this
a dash of that
a dash of me

a bitter taste 
turns over
on his tongue
when one ingredient
fails to blend
with the others

the flavor of betrayal
the aroma of deception
permeates within his kitchen

damaged taste buds 
can’t appreciate

the flavor of cinnamon
is still the flavor of cinnamon
