my love,
I cannot take you
where you don’t want to go
and no matter how hard I try
no matter how much I want
I know you’re just not ready

there’s something unsteady
within your words
believe me, I know
I’ve sat for hours listening

and maybe this thing
this thing we share
will still be there a week,
a month, a year from now

or maybe we’ll both change
and move on to other things
we have no way of predicting

but for now, this very minute
I will cherish that you’re in it
and promise you
I’ll always be your friend
