title: "If Ever The Moon"
writer: "catseyes"

If ever the moon was silver
it was when your words 
touched my lips.
when my thoughts fell unto,
over and around
and soon I was laying
on the wet ground 
staring up,
soaking you in,
beginning to understand
how a man could reach me.

If ever a mind could blend with mine,
yours was capable.
And I laid there listening,
in a glistening glow,
with a knowing, unfamiliar.
