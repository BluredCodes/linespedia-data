---
title: "Motion Sickness"
writer: "catseyes"

There is a queasiness in my stomach,
where calm once paddled at a steady pace.
Now below me, all is rough and uneasy,
an unsettling storm of emotion
like a sucking undertow.

It is the shifting and swaying,
the constant rocking,
an unanswered knocking,
tilting my world upward,
then drowning it in a pool of 
afterthoughts and forget-me-nots.

But I keep on rowing, fighting
the current that wants to swallow 
everything.
And as the water pours in, I bale
faster than it can consume me.

I bale, because if i can't breathe
I die.
