So you went away
And graduated 
Into silence.
She left the lamp alight they say
In case the Golden Land
Deported you
And left your suitcase
By it's quay. 

So shone the lamp
A little duller
Through each day
At night her tears
Like pinpricked stars
On velvet black
Did play.
Instead you'll burn in shadows
A portion of the night
The flicker giving you
The only light.
