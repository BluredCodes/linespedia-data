Stolen moments out of time 
Are all I have that is mine to rhyme 

I know it does not seem like much 
However, to me it is like a healing touch 

My writings are not read at home 
Therefore, I post them on the net to roam 

Out there, I know they will be seen 
Of all the comments, only a few are mean 

I have learned to take the good with the bad 
Now when I see a negative reply, I do not get mad 

I hope each writing reaches at least one person in a special way 
To help them through a situation or just to brighten their day 

Some times getting through daily life can seem quite hard 
When it is, reading a poem can be like getting a greeting card 

It may be custom tailored to ease that certain need 
Or to remind us that as humans, when hurt, we all bleed 

Try harder to be more tolerant and caring for others 
Remember under God, we are all sisters and brothers 

THE VOICE
