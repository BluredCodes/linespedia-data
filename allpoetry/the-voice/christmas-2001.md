title: "Christmas 2001"

This year as I look over my list 
Of everyone for whom I should get a gift 
I notice that this year the list is so long 
The reason is because in the past I approached it all-wrong 
As I reflect back over the events of the past year 
My cheek becomes wet from a rolling tear 
This year after great meditation and prayer 
I have decided to give the gift that everyone can share 
I am giving the that keeps on giving 
What I am giving is eternal living 
When God gave us the gift of his only son 
He was sent to earth to save everyone 
This year to honor my lord I am departing from the commercial hype 
Instead I would like to introduce the lost to everlasting life 
The ultimate gift God gave to all of mankind 
I hope this year in your heart that same gift you will find 

Merry CHRISTmas, 

THE VOICE
