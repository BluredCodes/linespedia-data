Inside this box is a ringWith it I promise you everythingIn hopes one day our soles will cling And our love will be a memoriable thingI will take care of you And keep our love a new I promise to never yell at youAnd never judge you on anything you doI'll be here for youAnything I will do for you I promise this all to you With this ring I know it is a simple thing But I hope it will mean everythingThis ring it will mean an everlasting thing This circle of love ever lasting
