I WOULD WALK A MILLION MILESI WOULD CRACK A THOUSAND SMILESI WOULD SING YOU A SONG ALL NIGHT LONGANYTHING TO BE WITH YOUI WOULD SWIM AN OCEAN WIDEI WOULD TRAVEL ACROSS THE SKYI WOULD TELL A THOUSAND STORIESANYTHING TO HOLD YOUI WOULD SLEEP IN THE COLDI WOULD SELL MY PRIZE POSETIONSI WOULD GIVE YOU MY WORLDANYTHING TO BE WITH YOUTHERE IS SO MUCH IN THIS WORLD I WOULD DO JUST TO BE WITH YOUSOME MAY CALL IT OBSESTIONBUT I SEE IT FROM DIFFERENT PROSOECTIONWANTING TO BE WITH YOUAND SINCE THIS FEELING COMES FOR MY HEART I KNOW THIS IS TRUEI'M IN LOVE WITH YOU
