I WISHMOST OF THE DAY I THINK OF YOUNOTHING ELSE MAKES ME AS HAPPY AS YOUHOW I WISH I COULD SEE YOU DAILYI WISHI WISH, I COULD SEE YOU EVERY DAYI WISH, I COULD HOLD YOU IN MY ARMSI WISH, I COULD PROTECT YOU FROM HARMI WISH, I COULD BE THERE WHEN YOU NEED MEI WISH, I COULD COME HOME TO YOUI WISHI WISH, I COULD SAY YOU WERE MINEI WISH OF YOU ALL THE TIME I WISH
