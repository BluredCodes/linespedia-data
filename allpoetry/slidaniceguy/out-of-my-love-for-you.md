---
title: "Out Of My Love For You"
writer: "SliDaNiceGuy"
slug: "out-of-my-love-for-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/9007-Out-Of-My-Love-For-You-by-SliDaNiceGuy"

If you asked for money it would be yours out of my love for youIf you asked for a place to stay I'd look all day out of my love for youIf you asked me to walk miles I would out of my love for youIf you asked me to hate I would out of my love for you If you asked me kill I would out of my love for youAnd if you asked me to love another I would out of my love for you, but I'd never be able to love them they way I love you
