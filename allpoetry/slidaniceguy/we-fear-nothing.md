---
title: "we fear nothing"
writer: "SliDaNiceGuy"
slug: "we-fear-nothing"

Fear is a midnight black echo
Screaming in my head
Voice after voice

Dark, vast, Little is known about fear
Little is known about our fears
Lost, shaken in a night of black
No one there
Just me and my fear
Thoughts paralyze
Frozen in time till taken
Afraid to speak
Afraid to move
Afraid

Fear is a midnight black echo
Dripping into my mind
Oozing into my nerves
Who's there
What's that
Why me

Manifestation of objects, people, death
Seeing what's not really there
Hearing what has not been said
Engulfed in black nothingness
We fear

Phobias of the mind
Trickery to the sole
Not going but there
Along the fine line of sanity

Fear is a midnight black echo
Heavy breathing
Sweat
Wide eyed
Goose bumps
We fear
FEAR
