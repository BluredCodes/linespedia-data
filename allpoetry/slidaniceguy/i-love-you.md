You set my heart afire with love devoting desireI walk, talk, think youWhat would i do with out youI would carry on everyday feeling the same wayTalking to you as if you as though your still thereBig brown eyes and red-brown hairI would stairWith out a careWith no one as fairYou set my heart afire with love devoting desireMy love for you will never tireMy eternal desireYou give my goose bumpsAnd make me perspireWhen I think of your touchYour gentle kiss You set my heart afire with love devoting desireWith a childs smileAnd twinkling eyesThere is no surpriseI LOVE YOU!
