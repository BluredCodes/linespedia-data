title: "Lonely Valentine"
writer: "shar bear"

he hands her the rose, so beautiful and pure
she takes it with a smile, just before he kisses her
love is a candle, with its bright burning flame
to be treated like a gift, not like a game


when in love nothing else matters, because it takes you 
when out of love your lonely, and ure misery breaks you

i love, but am not loved, im hated, but do not hate
dont try and rescue me, because your far too late
i gave my heart once, only to have it broken
so many tears i cried, so many words unspoken

sitting in silence, thinking of the love that was once mine
no further have i gotten, ill soon be forgotten
lonely valentine
