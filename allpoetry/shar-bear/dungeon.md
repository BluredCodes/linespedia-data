back against the wall, in the corner of her dungeon
silent lips for she is afarid
afarid to open her mouth and taste the fear
so she sits, alone, thinking about the past
remembering the ones she lost
then hating herself for letting it all happen

why did you walk away?? when i told you everything
i told you i loved you,i swear i meant it

but still she sits, thinking about the loss
confused by all the scattered words, and blurry images
never knowing why you walked away, without a goodbye

lack of faith, lack of strength, lack of the love ive lost
lack of the heart you took with you, when you walked off
if there were words i could say, to make you come back
i would put them into song, and sing every word to you

all alone, in the corner of this dungeon
back against the wall, should i make that call??
and tell you one more time
that i love you
