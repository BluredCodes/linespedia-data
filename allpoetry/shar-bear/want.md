title: "want"
writer: "shar bear"

i want to run away and never return
i want to dance on fire, and not get burned
i want to love you, but i need you to love me
i want to give you my eyes, so you that can see
that it isnt easy being me
i want to fall, but never hit the ground
i want to get lost, and never be found
i want to love you, but i cant find the words to say

i want to die, i want to die, i want to die, i want to die
I WANT TO STOP LIVING THIS FUCKING PATHETIC LIE
that is my life

i want to die forever, i want to trip and fall
because life without you, isnt worth living at all
