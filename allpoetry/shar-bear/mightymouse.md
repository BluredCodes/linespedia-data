like any other day you slammed the door in my face
called me a discrace, now i pace and i pace
trying to think back to that day when you said things were ok
but you lied when you tried to deny that you cried
stayed up the whole night, with tears in your eyes
and still i belived you were real, the way you make me feel
is just to good to be real, but yet you treat me like shit
dont you understand, that i am your lady, and you be my man
im doin all that i can, and still i get no praise
so i go another day, fighting, writing, trying
to be the best i can be, so you can see, that all i can be, is me
and if you dun like it, then you can say to ma face
that you dont want a place, in my heart
beat me up, tear me apart
big strong man, show yourself, petty little bitch
u nuttin but a mighty mouse
