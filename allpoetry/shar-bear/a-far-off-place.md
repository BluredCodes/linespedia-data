---
title: "a far off place"
writer: "shar bear"
slug: "a-far-off-place"
source: "AllPoetry"

everyone always tells me im such a good friend
one that will be there till the very end
they say im sweet, they say i make them happy
they tell me i put a smile on their face
but how can someone so sweet, someone so fun
think everyday, about how there gonna run
run away from everything that makes her cry
and makes her feel hurt, and want to die
slitting her wrists, just brought pain to her heart
made her feel abandoned, like a broken shopping cart
all she wants to do is fly amongst the birds
wild and free
with no-one to tell her what to do, what to say
where she can be herself, each and everyday
never would she stop and think, of what will become
of the sweet, funny, gurl they though they always knew
because she knows it would bring back painful memories
with no wings to fly, and no-one to set her free
all she can do is wait
and waste her life away, for her whole life is a lie
told to make the lives of others more appealing, more happy
to help them through their days of hate, days of pain, of hurt and heartache
dont cry for her, for she will one day be where she belongs
in a place where noone knows................................
of those sad love songs
