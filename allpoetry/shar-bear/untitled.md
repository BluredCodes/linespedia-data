untitled


i cant keep this inside me, not for any longer
i need to get it out
i miss you, i want you, like it was before
back the way it was before you walked out.....
before you walked out on me and left me cold
even after all the pain, and heartache
i was willing to take you back..........
why did you do this to me?/
you told me that you loved me, or was that just a lie
life without you, makes me want to die
never will i find someone, someone like you
and if i ever find another, they wont be like you
1 in a million, needle in a hey stack
maybe i need something??
like your love that i lack
you killed me inside, you broke my heart
picked me uip and tossed me, tore me apart
this is for you, my confession
you will always be, my only obsession
