everything is changing, everyone is different
back in the day when things were easy, i remember
now nothing is easy, nothing is free
open your eyes and forever be
lost in the beauty, of the world, and the soul
thinking of choices ive made
make me laugh, sometimes cry
things that i discouvered and things that passed me by
everyday is a new day
full of whatever you make it
outerspace is now like an everyday thing
nothing is amzing anymore, but wait
for futher in your life, later on
the times you'll have, you'll see
kudos to those who get there
and fly so far away
distinct, relate, but dont get lost
so forever love 
life is like a journey, lives like a book
so finish yours
with a crazy cover;)
