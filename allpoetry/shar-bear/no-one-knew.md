no-one knew

lonely, because there is noone there
sad, because she thinks that noone cares
to herself, most of the time
so i give to her, this small rhyme

pop your pills, everyday
write ure poems, their all so great
drink till you cant drink anymore
smoke weed till you pass out cold on da floor

someone so to herself, and mysterios
so full of love and life
living everyday, by the roll of the dice

shes like my older sister
the one i never knew
this poem is just to say
dat ill always love u:):):)

for the one and only....naz
