the silence falls over her and she is now mute
her lack of converstaion, and inspiration
has finally taken over
she needs some one to show her the way
be obiliviuos to all of her crys, and hold her
hold her until the end of time
someone in which to dance, dance among the stars
forever turning, spinning
this jaded gurl, with sad eyes, and broken dreams
wants nothing but to be loved, to see beyond
the hurt, and the pain, that inflicts her heart
stabbing herself with blame
open wounds, suduce the flesh,making it want more
yet she trys so hard to make them see
what they are truly looking for
focused on too much at once, could cause her to 
lose it all, her mind and body will disseaper
forever, never returning for anything for there is nothing to return for
her love had flown, raped to the bone
for her there is no more, to live for, to die for
kudos to those who see through sickness
