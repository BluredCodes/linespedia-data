i want to take you by the hand and fly away
take you away from all you fear, and all that causes you pain
you mean to much to me, for me to see you cry
i love you so much, for you i would die
smoke me a spliff, drink me a 40
sing with you at the top of my lungs, nirvana,
rape me, rape me my friend
rape me, rape me again

whenever i hear that song, i see your face, and ure cute little smile
u brighten my days with ure tee hees, and ure o-tays
and please dont ever ask me to kill you babe

my 18 year old lesbian that goes to collage(jks)
my older sister, without the relation
my one, my only
