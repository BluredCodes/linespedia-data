the last goodbye

for all the world's offered 
and all the love you gave me
i have nothing to show
maybe i wasnt worth it
i wasnt worth any of your time
thats why i sit here
with this 40 and this vile
the world is a crazy place
filled with psycho's,bloodshed and hate
of all the things i can think of right now
all i can think of is you
and the way i felt when you held me
in your arms i was safe, safer then ever before
but not now, nor ever again, never more
the day you went away was the lasy day i cried
the day you left was like the end of my life
u left me with nothing, i got no warning
and it wont get better tomorrow morning
this is for the lovers tightly holding each other
this is for the haters that only love their mothers
love is like the sun, bright, and full of life
but when out of love.... your just the psycho with the knife
