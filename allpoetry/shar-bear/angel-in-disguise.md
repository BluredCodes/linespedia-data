title: "angel in disguise"
writer: "shar bear"

ive come to a desision, that i just cant make
and i want so bad, what i cannot take

it seems like im lost, when i look into your eyes
a whole new world, with this angel in disguise

if i told you i loved you, would you spread your wings
and we could fly away, high amongst the trees
to a far off land, where we can be free
none else but you and me
