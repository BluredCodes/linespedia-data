title: "Brokedown Palace"

brokedown palace, cold dark cage
a place for me to let out
all of this rage
the rain falls down, wets my hair to the tips
runs down my cheeks, onto my muted lips
i take a secound and close my eyes
thinking about the days when the sun used to rise
on me, almost telling me i have got another chance
another chance at escaping this hell, that wont set me free
darkness and shadows cluster my vision
more confusion to my indesision
i need to escape, i have to, i must
before i lose it, spontaniously combust
i find it in myself to lift my head from my knee
and through the twisted bounded metal, i see you reaching for me
have you come to help me, set me free
i hear your footsteps getting closer, i see your face
but all i smell is fear, and hate is all i taste
your so close, just reach for my hand, and we can run away
but its too late, you dissepeared before my own eyes
once again i got my hopes up, just to get burned by lies
back to the wall,knees to my chest
im going back to doing what i do best
waste away, all alone
in this brokedown palace
that has become my home
