writer: "shar bear"

when i was a little girl, i used to dream
of my true love, comming to rescue me
to take me away, to a far off land
away from all the hurt, the pain, and the greed
i remember when i used to be happy
and when i used to smile
used to belive in myself, but it's been awhile
for so long i thought i would make it
thought i would get through this thing called life
but its so hard to think
when your holding a knife
