---
title: "who knew?"
writer: "shar bear"
slug: "who-knew"
source: "AllPoetry"

always on time, with a smile on her face
she does her own things, walks at her own pace
straight "A" student, first class friend
someone who'll be there, until the very end
everybody knows her, shes loved by all
rather stay home and make lables, then go to the mall
but how can she seem so happy, each and every day
i bet theres lots of hurt and pain, safely hidden away
she goes all out to help out friends, and sometimes they treat her like shit
pick on her, call her dumb, even say shes a ditz
but she'll prove them all wrong when her time comes
and thats when they'll all see
that that gurl they used to call a ditz
just discouvered the cure for H.I.V
full of potential, full of life, never last in line
full of love, full of hate, but everything will turn out fine
maybe someday she'll realize, that shes one of a kind
and amazing people like herself, are truly hard to find
