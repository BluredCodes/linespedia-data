title: "fake"
writer: "shar bear"
slug: "fake"
source: "AllPoetry"

your a fake and i hate you
bullshit, lies, FAKED IT ALL
you blamed it all on me, how the fuck could you do that
little bitch, you dont even know me
only 3 years we spent, laughing, friendship
FUCK YOU AND YOUR FAKE FRIENDSHIP
you faked the love
you faked the smiles
you faked EVERYTHING and for what?? a little satisfaction
did it make you happy??? did it turn you on???
how  does it feel to be shallow and vain????
GET OUT OF MY HEAD, AND TAKE YOU FAKE LIFE, YOU FAKE EVERYTHING
and leave, and dont come back, I HATE YOU
i hate your face, i hate your eyes, i hate your lips
which you speak all your lies
can u belive i actually put trust in you, i loved you once??
I LOVED A FAKE, I LOVED A FAKE
HOW WAS I SO FUCKING BLIND
to love someone who didnt even know what love was
you dont know me, i dont know you,
an old sad, lonely woman is what will become of you
lonely for you r shallow
sad for you faked it all
old because you wasted away
so take all your lies, all your empty promises
and take them away from me, far away where i cant hear you
where i cant see you, where i cant feel you
a fake is all you are, and all you will ever be
