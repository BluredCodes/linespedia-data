---
title: "liar"
writer: "shar bear"
slug: "liar"
source: "AllPoetry"

all alone, no one to keep me safe
no one even cares
the blackest heart, the shattered dreams
no one is ever gonna be there
you waste your precious time
by telling me different
cant you see, you'll never be, able to get through to me
i dont belive the words you say, when youve lied in the past
so dont ever again tell me what to do
cause i always get the last laugh
eyes of brown, from the burns
heart with holes, from all the hurt
so dont tell me you understand, cause you live in a world
where everything is perfect
my world is full of hate, lies, pain, death, abuse
the list goes on
dont say your sorry, cause i dont need your sympathy
i dont need people like you
thinking that your taking care of me
so find another, that will let you in their heart
and maybe they will fall for your shit
ure fake personality, and your forced smile
belongs in the bottom of a ditch
