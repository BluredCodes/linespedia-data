on a sun-derful day
in a sleek car
with a pretty woman
on an endless highway
to futures bright with hope
he was there...but
he took a wrong turn
and wound up in 
the ovens of August
in a rent-a-wreck special
now a dirt road lies ahead
to a dead end
nothing but dust behind him
nothing but empty beside him
on an aching, twisted rut
to a hapless future
========================================
be very careful 
when you make
Concrete decisions
that you are headed
in the right direction
there is only 
one off ramp in life
and it's always
your final exit
"Godspeed!"
============================================
================================================Artis
