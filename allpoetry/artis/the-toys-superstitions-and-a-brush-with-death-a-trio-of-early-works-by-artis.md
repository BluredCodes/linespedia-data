The Toys.(to be etched on a marble shaped teddy bear for a six year old.)
~
The toys sit sadly
untouched on the shelf
unmoved from the spot
where he left them himself
~
now no longer played with 
they grow dusty and old
and they wonder each day 
how he could be so cold
~
but they'll stay in their places
growing rusty and bored
Timmy drowned in the river
and he's home with the lord

Superstitions

My eyes keep drifting to that glaring bulb
sixty watts of salvation
keeping watch over my soul
for I cannot forget what that 
old fortune teller whispered in my ear
this morning past
"Don't turn out the light tonight!"
"Death waits in the corner for you!

it's four a.m. and the bulb above just flickered...
"Hail Mary, full of grace, the Lord be with me."

"A Brush with Death."

It fell
the brush tip first
when they said Mom had died
staining white linen like red tears
splashed hard.
