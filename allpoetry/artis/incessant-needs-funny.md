Coffee doesn't do it anymore
liquid demorol is great
but it requires surgery 
for a side effect
candy was dandy yet....
liquor was quicker 
but unfortunatley
vodka gives me headaches
after four hours of a 
liquid mental void
water has to be bottled 
to be any good
green tea is just 
a watered down version
of something you wish it was instead
coke is a temporal fix
but things seldom go better with it
whether up your nose
or right out of the bottle
adding glug to your mug
qualludes are............
damn...I can't remember??????
pot was often potluck
and my grass was always greener
then what the other guy was fencing
and it's been made offensive
on a federal level
friends are bending over
and turning their "other" cheeks
to real criminals
simply because they failed to
stash their stash
five years for a ziploc
full of smoke
from material they use for rope 
beers been canned now with 
a larger chug hole
but it still gives lousy head
sex is out there but 
you need an afffadavit
of good health 
to score a healthy fix
true love is serios stuff
and you have to be committed
or all the world disdains you
heroin was never heroic
it steals all you have
and then leaves you 
with a jones you can 
never keep up with
nitrous oxide is a gas!
but laughing till it hurts
defeats the purpose
speed kills but at least 
it's quick about it
hash was always better with 
corned beef added
love was always my biggest high
but it never casme 
in the same strength
it can't be bought
and it's gift can often cause 
you to take up all the things
that I've listed above
cigarettes are breathtaking
literally
but oh, what a rush
when you gaze into 
those fires of Hades
blazing on the tip of your 
nicotine buzz
just after making love
or waking up for that first puff
happiness can't be injected
ingested, or inhaled
for any longer then 
a brief oasis in your day
wheaties never made me
Olympic material
LSD once turned a hamburger
I was frying into
a mooing cow
with third degree burns
on my stovetop
I could taste the bristles 
and the hide and hoofs
I swore of meat for months
religion promises much
but it carries a lot of "wait"
Vacations always require you
to come back
and leave you wishing 
you had just left
chocolate makes me smile
while it slowly chokes 
my arteries to death
ladies are nice
but I'm having trouble 
getting into them lately
fri-ends always have-ends
stuck about halfway through
the word itself
Marriage is an (I)
surrounded by Marr, and age
children still brighten my world
untouched by its vices
they sing and dance and laugh
the greatest natural highs
we leave behind as we get older
So poetry is my refuge
from the sober world 
in which I now exist
creative juices are 
my drink/drug of choice
yet still I am haunted
taunted and sometimes flaunted
and not always undaunted
by those old...Incessant needs
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~Artis
