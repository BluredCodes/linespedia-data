You know, there's been a lot of words between us since we met
and we have shared some happiness, I never will forget
but there are things a man can't say, and a poet cannot write,
Like unsung songs, and undreamnt dreams, that whisper in the night
~~~~
Unreachable expressions
that are  r u  n  n  i  n g 
wild and free
and words unsaid to tell you
just what I feel in me
~~~~
I've tried to write a song that would tell what you mean to me
but this old guitar, and my cracking voice, are not a symphony
I'd need a million instruments, and ten thousands voices true
to try and capture the beauty of what I've found in you...
~~~~
and so, 
I'll Have to be content
with what I've said befpre
a love like yours is all I need
now and forevermore
sweet love like yours
is all I'll need
now and forevermore
