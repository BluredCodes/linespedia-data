Snow angels are nothing more 
then special moments
shared by two
in the winter residue
the angel itself
and the mortal creating it
************(
but they cannot cure disease
or bring upon us world peace
feed the hungry
shelter the homeless
or make all hatred cease
they are only the impressions
we have formed
they are only what we think
a higher being might be
               
              
"Boy, talk about a winter depression!!"

If hearts can fly
then mine is 
winging its way
past the rings of Saturn
and...............beyond
as the sun breaks 
over the mountain
and the butterfly
sails it's way 
cross fields of love
~/
 <3
did you ever feel
as though you 
were a balloon
I am the ballon
and you're the air
you keep on filling me
with your happiness
and I keep growing 
with your love
I've touched the skies
as the moon sets 
over the mountains
and the luna moth
spreads it's wings
cross fields of love.
