This is a simple song
and meant for only one
whose smile, and gentle eyes
have made my whole world new
can loving you be wrong
you are so very young
the years between us number
seven and a month or two
I don't know why it is
I feel they way I do
so many things are keeping us apart
but feelings soft, and warm
are telling me it's true
you feel the same way too
inside your heart
this is a simple song
and meant for only you.
