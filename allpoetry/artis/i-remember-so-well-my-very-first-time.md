I remember so well 
my very 
first time
arms and legs
entangled
awkwardly
attempting 
to find 
a more
comfortable position
bending 
and lurching
to find that 
perfect spot
in that tight, 
little space
trying so hard
to make 
the opening bigger
so that I could 
slip easily inside
zippers grating
sweat and 
heavy breathing
intermingling
with the frosty air
as I used my feet
to scootch
and slide
to hump
and glide
further
and deeper
inside
till I was 
snugly
wrapped 
in a cocoon
of warmth
with an 
"Oh, so...."
satisfied smile
and a long sigh 
of contentment
I closed my eyes
and just basked
in the sheer 
joy of 
my first
fresh smelling
warmly embracing
snooze inducing
Sears and Roebuck
sleeping bag
camping out 
when you are eight
is such an 
unforgettable
experience
