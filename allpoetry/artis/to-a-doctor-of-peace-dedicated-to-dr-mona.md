I paint you with warm colors
pastel tints that soften 
the worlds harshness
your hope is splashed in white
it blots out the darkness
I am inspired
therefore 
I will exhibit it to others
you are a cool valley
of rain-fed, spring greens
wherein your shade protects
the tiny toddlers
dancing on stubby legs
bare toes flexing
sired by all nations
giggles echoing heavenward
they find shelter
in your crimson smile
from the burning masses
that linger as flaws
to life's beauty all
that which I struggle to portray
from the bleak palette before me
your healing hands
are comforting vines that grow
connecting living cells
that create flowers
that will soon rebloom
your eyes are moist pools
fed by tears in the storms
but they nurture
by their deeper awareness
your soft lips beckon me
to leave a brief touch
two opposite souls entwined 
in total togetherness
I will stroll with you
on the banks of 
the cleansing waters
and tarry long after
we find seperate paths again
I paint you as a friend 
the first step 
to a masterpiece
unfolding between us
may all the world
see the beauty 
of two different
souls enjoined.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~Much Peace-Artis
