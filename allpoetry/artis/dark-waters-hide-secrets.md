The ocean at night
without light
is breathtaking
but ominous
all of its
ebony liquids
unseen 
only whitecaps
are visibly faint
in the glow
of a fingernail moon
a leftover clipping
of God's  
snipped off 
by the angels
who faithfully
groom him
this remnant 
most sadly
illuminates little
in the dark
void of space
in the waters below
while above
just a few stars
deposit their richness
diamond-like
in the Cloud Banks    )
                 
                  
////////////////////
thus the 
only stars left
are the starfish 
who splay
their five points
beneath waves
as they reach
for their prey
hungry mouths
open wide
and all manner
of predators
prowl these 
dark waters
as they feed 
on their own
the surf's roar
hides the screams
: 0 O : O : : o :
And though it's 
probably quite safe
to takes dips
in this cauldron
of life and 
death struggles 
not only at noontime
but deep in the night
there is always 
the chance as
you cut through 
black waters
that your strokes
executed with 
very small splashes
will attract the 
attention of
a creature
who hungers
whose brain is 
the size of
a scrotum 
quite chilled
from the cold 
salty waters
and it just
might mistake you
for something it
normally 
thinks of 
as food
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~^~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
~~~~~~~^~~~~o/~~~~~~~~~~^~~~~~~~
So I'll stay 
in the hollow
depression 
my buttocks have
formed in this 
sand dune
and dream of
cool waters
washing sweat from 
my brow as 
the fingernail
moon scrapes
its trail cross
the sky.
