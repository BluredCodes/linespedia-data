title: "Behold..... the children of tomorrow."
writer: "artis"

Behold the Children of Tomorrow
standing at it's door
scoffing at the yesterdays
that brought them where they are
mocking those who fought to free
this world where they now dwell
they can right what was done wrong
make heaven out of hell
they can do it better
better than before
blinded by their egos
hastened by their lust
thier feet high off the floor
no reason to share trust
they never stop to think about
all those who've tried and fell
~
the Children of the Yesteryears
who stood at this same door
at the entrance to "their" futures
with great dreams to do much more
till they were staggered by the slamming
of the many doors to come
and forced to change directions
dissillusioned, old, and numb
hoping now in one last effort
to guide younger souls away
from the same paths where they stumbled
sneering faces turn away
one more final door now slamming
on each wrinkled...dreamless face
~
as the Children of Tomorrow
pay no heed to past mistakes
moving on without its wisdoms
as together they embrace
the valiant struggle to do better
that is ended with disgrace
becoming those despised
when younger hopefuls 
take their place
as the Children of the Future
with new goals 
begin their race
it's an endless vicious cycle
generations lost in space.
