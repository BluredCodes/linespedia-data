I met a man 
star-spangled
and he murdered me
in Vietnam
in a heated rush
through the tangled brush
I was just nineteen
and he was Uncle Sam
under heavy fire 
I tripped a wire
and it mangled me
into a jigsaw man
they shipped me home
almost 80 lbs.
all very organ-nized
whatever they could find
chunks of flesh and bone
bagged and boxed up tight
with a U.S. Flag
the rest left behind
they buried me with full honors
near where I grew up
I got a blackjack salute
and the rest of me
dissappeared with time
back where I blew up
under jungle roots
but what that war
pulled assunder
God will soon restore
I'll be rejoined 
and then.........
ressurection day
will find me complete
like I was before
forever nineteen again
