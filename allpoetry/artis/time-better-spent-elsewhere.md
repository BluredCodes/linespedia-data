Nothing to write
little to share
pencil is broke
paper is bare
computer is down
pen's out of ink
head holds no thoughts
empty I think
hours go by 
still I don't care
butt's getting tired 
just sitting there
hope the phone rings
pray for a knock
some interruption 
to my writer's block
power goes out
for the third time tonight
here in the dark
I have nothing to write
running my useless hands
up through my hair
I guess that my time is 
better spent now 
e l s e w h e r e
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~Artis
