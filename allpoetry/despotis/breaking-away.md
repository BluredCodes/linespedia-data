Breaking Away

I'm a bit weary from constantly changing 
and remaining the same. 
It's a vicious cyclists nightmare.
The sun is playing hide and go seek with my happiness. 
But, it's all for the best, isn't it? 
I don't recall putting in a request for an instant replay. 
Maybe the button is just stuck. 
I have late fees, and shrink fees, 
all for the love of you, or ivy. 
I feel a pull toward the road. 
I won't black out in Idaho. 
I simply need yet another escape route. 
I need to find peace, so I can ultimately resolve
