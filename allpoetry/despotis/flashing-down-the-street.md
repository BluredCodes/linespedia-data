Flashing Down The Street


  And we're walking through popsicle skies.
  Building dreams and bridges from stained sticks. 
  Maybe it's always going to be this way.
  You stop.  I stumble.
  Reaching for a can I've been kicking for a block.
  Mother Nature's angelic disciple.
  Bank shot into the bin with the swastika like arrows.
  Environmentally conscious Nazis.
