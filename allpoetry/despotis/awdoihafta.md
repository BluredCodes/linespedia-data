AWDOIHAFTA

The peace negotiations have come to an end.
Allow me to indulge in a footnote before we adjourn
A sparkling grape whine, if you will
The mediator is  retiring
I've had a good run, but it's time to hop ahead
Maintaining balance between the boiling blood 
of my incessantly asinine not so distant ancestors 
Has driven me into psychological seclusion
Game over, no credits, insert two quarters
The treaties will burn, 
Invalid from the start
Drawn up without the letter A
Hawthorne phobia, perhaps?
Cryptic samples of tabloid talk show confessions 
tossed around at family gatherings
Bitter, cruel, laughter to accompany 
the twisting of the knife
The volume level increases as I leave the room
I am the epitome of anti-social?
True words spoken by a sociopath.
This conference seems far from being over
The plans have all failed miserably
Have we burned them yet?
Fuck NAFTA
Fuck SISTA
And everything else that ends with a TA
Just for good metric measure
Swallowing the evolutionary conflict of personalities
The mirrors behind me could never have guessed
The mirrors ahead have been covered
Draped in a black sheath that may have been too costly
It all seems to fall under 
the strange death ritual category
If there is such a thing
There should be
This siding, suffering, solitary, 
systematically self sacrificing safety net 
Has reached its holding capacity
I'm sending the troops home
Any glimpse of victory was surely a mirage
They're stranded in the desert of communication breakdown
