Tour of Duty


  I've been called upon once, as I'm called upon now
  to restore peace and order in a replenishing cycle
  My duty, dear followers, is to eliminate 
  honor, pride, and all that was once sacred 
  Until the chaos is balanced 
  like a rickety teeter-totter with the bolts removed
  I will never fail to succeed to relinquish my self respect 
  for the good of my people
  I would skip barefoot in a windstorm, 
  through a field of push pins,
  so that the sun may shine down upon your home and mind,
  The Hoover Village of The Damned and choice blood relatives.
