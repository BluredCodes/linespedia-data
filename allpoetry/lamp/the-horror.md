~*~

If only
that
hadn't been said
from that mouth . . .
*sigh*
I could have
been content
with uncertainty.
But 
that
from that mouth . . .
*ouch*
I'll get over it.
I'm fine.
Even if my dreams
Don't come true,
I can handle it.
But to have 
Those words
Uttered from
That mouth...
Smashing
My dream
By those words
From that mouth.
*ouch*
Why did
I have to be told
Those words
From that mouth?
I never asked
Or questioned
That future . . .
Because I knew
It would kill me
If those words
Were spoken
From that mouth.

~*~
