~*~

Do you not know me?
Then how do your words reflect
My life's own essence? 

I bear my burden.
The load is common for me.
Is it hard for you?
 
When I find True Love
I sacrifice all for it.
Everything else pales. 

If love is the goal
And if you mean to hold me,
Don't ever let go.

~*~
