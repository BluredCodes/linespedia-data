~*~

I wonder how you're doing & what your mood is.
I'm doing fine & my mood is up.
I wonder what you're thinking.
I'm thinking how much you would enjoy this world that I'm exploring.
I wonder if you miss me.
I miss you, but we'll survive it and flourish, and you're with me in my heart, anyhow.
I wonder how we'll begin.
We'll glide through it well, I'm sure, like a dance.
I wonder how life got so full of precious possibility
And how the world became so splendiferously wondrous.
I wonder how we found each other.
I'm glad we did.

~*~
