~*~

No maps.
No shoulds.
No promises.
No scripts.
Just gravity.
Catch me.
And I'll be yours.

~*~an apoiul poem (an apoiul is an "Abstract Painting Of Ideas Using Language" with no rhyme, no restriction to concrete subject matter, and natural flow)
