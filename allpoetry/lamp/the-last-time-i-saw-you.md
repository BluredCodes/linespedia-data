~ * ~ 

The last time I saw you, 
We spoke not a word, 
For it was forbidden. 
Your back was turned to me, 
But I could feel your awareness gazing at me, 
And I know you could feel mine. 
You stood tall and straight, 
Unable to turn around, 
And me, unable to reach out and touch you. 
And yet, in that simple moment, 
I know you were alive, breathing, 
And that was enough to make me happy.

~ * ~
