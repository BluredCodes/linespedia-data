~*~

“Why do I love this man?”
It’s a question I often ask God,
In my total bewilderment.
”Because you do.”
Comes as God's reply.

“Why do I love you?”
God offers by way of explanation,
Recognizing that I am created in God's image.
”Because I do.”
Comes as God's reply.

~*~Sometimes there is no explanation for why I love someone, except that I'm following God's example in loving me, for which there is no explanation. God just loves me. I just love someone. That's just the way it is. There is no other explanation for either God's love or for my love. Because God made me to love, just like God loves. The love just is.
