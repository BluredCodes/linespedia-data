~*~ 

I have spent all day
Trying to figure out 
What it is that I want,
Who it is that I want.
Not who wants me,
Not who I should want,
But who I do want.
The answer might surprise you,
But it shouldn't.

I seem to have the spirit of
A moderately intelligent butterfly
Of fragile, easily broken sensibilities.
So, while I appreciate butterfly keepers,
I want to fly free 
With a twin-souled butterfly.

~*~
