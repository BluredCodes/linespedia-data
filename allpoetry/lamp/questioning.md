~*~

I love you.
What more can I say?
Particularly if you’re not here for me to look
in the eye, face-to-face.
But you haven’t told me what you want.

Do you want me now?
Do you want me later?
Do you want me at all?
In my life, do you want me to see you 
as just a benevolent figure?
Or as just a lover?
Or as a husband?
Or as just a friend?
Or what?

You need to be explicit.
Do you love me back at all?
Are you even there for me?
If so, where are you?

Because I don’t see you here,
And I don’t see you sending for me.
So, why are you stalling?
Are you perhaps playing games with me?
Are you not being sincere with me?

~*~
