~*~

It's not the hair,
You have or that you don't have,
It's how you work it.

It's not the clothes,
You have or that you don't have,
It's how you work it.

It's not the age,
You have or that you don't have,
It's how you work it.

It's not all that
You have or that you don't have,
It's how you work it.

~*~
