Everyday we step farther away from the hell we used to live in. 
Everyday we grow farther apart.
Everyday my memory fades a little more.
Everyday we get a little older, a little wiser.
Everyday we learn things we don't want to know.
Everyday we get a little closer to the truth.
Everyday we get a little closer to tomorrow.
Everyday we get a little further from yesterday.
Everyday the pain gets a little easier to bare.
Everyday we ignore each other a little more.
Every day seems a little more lonely without you.
