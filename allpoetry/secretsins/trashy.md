---
title: "Trashy"
writer: "SecretSins"

You can't see these scars inside of me,
I'm burnt and broken.
You left a funny after-taste of 
stale cigarettes and cheap beer. 
Now I'm left to walk around in this solitude,
carrying the weight you gave me.
It's heavy on my back and I can't 
seem to manage anymore. 
The stares of these faceless people 
are burning holes through my skin. 
I'm so trashy now,
and I don't know how to move on, 
after all of this.
