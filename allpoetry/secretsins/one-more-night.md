Let's make this final moment last,
cuz everything that's set in stone
is set in the past.

There really isn't much left to do,
just sit and reminisce 
about all the times we've been though.

None of us knows what will come tomorrow,
so we'll stay here today
wait out the pain and maybe some sorrow.

It's so scary, we've come so far.
We've shared so much,
first kisses and our first car.

I don't know what I'd have done without you,
my very best friends
and you seem to good to be true.I wrote this to my five best friends when we all graduated from high school.
