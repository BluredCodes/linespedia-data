title: "Crumbled At Your Feet"

Don't give me your false glory,or your superioristic views.You know your just like me,the only difference is I gave a damn.You're trying to push me down with your lies,while you hide behind your pretty little face. So angelic you seem now,but you're dirty little secret shows through.And I'm crumbled here,broken woman lying on the floor.And you're standing over me,all of the men you've had,gather in your eyes,as you try to throw them on me.But I'm not like you,cuz behind your innocent eyes,hids a sinful whore.And between my broken tears,is your flawless image,only without the lies.When I wrote this, I thought is was me looking at you, Lex!  But now I know it's you just looking at me and all of the things I've promised not to be.
