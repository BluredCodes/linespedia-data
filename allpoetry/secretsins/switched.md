---
title: "Switched"
writer: "SecretSins"
slug: "switched"

You seem to forget the ways it used to be.How I was the one to worry,and I was the one to care. Do you remember all those stories you told us?Wild parties, older men?Do you remember lying,making mistakes?(Now you're perfect)Now you call me a whore, a hypocrite.You say I've got to learn,But let me ask you,how many times have you cried for me?(I've cried a million tears for you)Do you really care like I did?Please stop pretending.I'm not as weak as you think,I'm not as weak as you,and that is what you're afraid of.Out of all the things I've learned about my 'best friend' I've learned that I'm not the only hypocrite.  Saying you care and then beating me down, is not respect, it's power.
