I was too young to feel that way,
well, at least that's what they say.

I was too young to love you so much,
well, they said you used me as a cruch. 

I was too young to have a broken heart,
well, at least they thought I shouldn't care that we were apart. 
 
Now I'm older, and I still felt the way I did,
well, they really were the only ones who looked at us as kids.This isn't that good, but today I saw my first love.  Everyone thought that it was fake but looking back I know I truly loved him, no matter what they say.  I just had to get that out really quick like :)
