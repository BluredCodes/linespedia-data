title: "High On Yourself"

You're so shallow,superficial little girl.Ballerina you think you're perfect,go ahead do your fancy little twirl. But don't forget where you came from,don't forget your past.Don't forget the ones you walked on,from your lover to that worthless outcast. You'd slit his throat and not look back.You'd do it all,to make up for the qualities you lack. You want everyone to think you're perfect,to build you up, make you high.But you've got another thing coming,and I'm the one who's gonna show you, you can't fucking fly.
