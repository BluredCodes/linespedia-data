---
title: "Because I Never Have"
writer: "SecretSins"
slug: "because-i-never-have"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/30816-Because-I-Never-Have-by-SecretSins"

I never understood what those words meant. 
I never understood how standing by a flag, 
could bring tears to someones eyes. 
I never understood what the price of freedom was.
I never understood the baggage you all carry,
from those days of hellish war. 
I know I'll never truly understand
the power of what you did,
but I'm free and I'm alive,
and now I want to thank every one of you for that,
because I never have.
