Now what do you want me to do?
You're pushing me away from everything you ever taught me. 
You told me to stand up strong, 
hold on to what I believe in.
And now here I am standing up,
not backing down,
and there you are. 
You're telling me to let go,
to come home and ask forgiveness. 
But, mother you taught me well. 
So I'm not going to back down,
I'm just going to sit here and wait for you,
and if you choose not to find me,
then don't find me. 
I'm fine here without you,
I can make it on my own, 
I have so far.
