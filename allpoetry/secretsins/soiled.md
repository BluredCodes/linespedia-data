---
title: "Soiled"
writer: "SecretSins"
slug: "soiled"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I'm so confused,
I'm lost and out of control.
It's so hard to watch you friends fall.
It's so hard not to crumble 
under the secrets you have to keep.
It seems like all the things 
we used to be are so far away.
We all seem so soiled,
like we've given up on all the things
we used to want.
We live in this dirty little town
and try to move on from 
the things that made us who we are.
We're running away,
but not getting very far.
