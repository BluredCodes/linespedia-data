title: "Another"
writer: "SecretSins"

Another day gone by,
with you on my mind. 
Another sleepless night,
closed eyes, making me blind. 

Another dream of you,
with a nightmare ending.
Another day to live through,
without a love pending. 

Another heart to break,
for you today. 
Another lover to hate,
but it's my price to pay.Sometimes.....
