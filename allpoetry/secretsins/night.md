You slide your fingers down his bare chest,
you're kissing all of the places that make him want you.
You're lips are teasing him,
as he pulls your shirt over your head.
Nimble fingers unhook your black lace bra.
Matching panties soon fall to your ankles. 
All night long you travel with him.
You say he takes you places you've never been.
But you've been living in those places forever. 
In the morning you wake,
look at what you've done. 
He lays asleep, naked beside you.
You dress yourself,
and stand outside,
smoke a ciggarette, 
ease away the pain. 
Last night you saw all of him,
but how can you look at him now?
Cast away your eyes,
swallow your tears. 
Drive yourself home,
take a shower, get some rest,
try and forget.
Wallow in your lifetime of regret.
But not too long,
cuz come the night,
you'll do it all again.
