title: "Funny Little Girls"

Funny little girls,you think you're so smart. That you know everything,but everything you think you know about me,isn't real.But how could you know that?All you ever do is hide,behind your dirty looks and power. But if you really have the balls you say you have,step into my part of our dirty little world. Then say those things about me.Then try to stand your ground,lets see how quickly you crumble.
