---
title: "Walk Away"
writer: "SecretSins"

You think you're so much better than me,
but bitch don't cross lines that you can't handle. 
Don't talk words that you can't back up.  
I'm done apologizing and I'm done giving a damn.
You've pushed me down long enough,
don't be surprised when I stand up 
and slap you right back.  
I'm gonna wipe my wounds 
and walk away from you,
but you, you're stuck here,
and you're gonna find that you can't 
wash your bloody hands clean.
