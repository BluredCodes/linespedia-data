title: "Not What I Asked For"

I'm standing here alone,
all these eyes are placed on me.
What did I do,
how far must I run 
to escape this shit?
I know I put it all on me
but isn't there a point
when enough is enough?
I've learned that I can handle
far more than I thought I could.
You keep pushing me forward,
but I've got no place to go.
