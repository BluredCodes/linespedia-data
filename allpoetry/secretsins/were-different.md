You always say that we are so different,
well what's that supposed to mean?
You shudder like it isn't worth it anymore. 
Are you avoiding me on purpose?
I used to be what you lived for,
at least that's what you said. 
Well, what has changed so much since then?
We weren't as worried about our differences before. 
When I told you months ago it wasn't working,
you told me to hold on. 
What I thought was breaking us apart,
was really holding us together. 
Where did that all go?
I can't breath without you,
I don't fell like falling apart today.
