---
title: "Do You?"
writer: "SecretSins"
slug: "do-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/35552-Do-You--by-SecretSins"
---

Is it really all that hard to keep up?
I don't run that far ahead. 
When you spend so much time alone,
do you ever miss me?
When you're so tired from never loving anyone,
does your heart break for me?
When you're so happy with her,
do you ever say my name?
