title: "Hide"
writer: "SecretSins"

I thought that we were bigger than this.
I thought we were grown up.
But we're just scared little girls,
hiding behind the only things we can find.
I hide behind my lies,
you hide behind your silence and bitter stares. 
It hardly even matters anymore.
In a year it will all be over,
and we won't even remember the promises 
we've broken. 
Or will we?
