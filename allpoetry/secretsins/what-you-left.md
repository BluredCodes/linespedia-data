---
title: "What You Left"
writer: "SecretSins"

It only hurts when I'm by myself,
when I had you to think about 
I never felt this alone.
I thought I was doing the right thing.
How long could I stay with you?
How long can I breath without you?
My chest is caving in.
How can something this right,
feel so wrong?
I'm short of breath,
I can't hold on anymore.
Maybe I'll get over this,
maybe you'll move on after me.
Maybe he'll make me happy.
But will he ever erase the stains 
of love and hate you left on me?Loosing him was the hardest thing I've ever had to do, but I had no choice.
