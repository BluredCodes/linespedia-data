---
title: "All I've Never Meant"
writer: "SecretSins"
slug: "all-ive-never-meant"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I never meant for it to turn out this way,
I never meant to push you away.
I never meant to hurt you like this,
I never meant to sell my worth for a kiss.
I never meant to fall apart,
I never meant to end it, before we could even start.
I never meant to put it all on you,
I never meant to pass my lies along as true.
I never meant to lay any blame,
I never meant for us to play this game.  
I never meant to turn out like I did,
I never meant for it to change, but who am I to kid?
