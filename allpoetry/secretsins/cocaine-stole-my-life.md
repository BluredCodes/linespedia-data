---
title: "Cocaine Stole My Life"
writer: "SecretSins"
slug: "cocaine-stole-my-life"
source: "AllPoetry"

I'm standing across this smoke filled room,
you're shriveled in the corner, 
snorting up your doom.

Take a hit, hide it from us all,
no knows about you,
but, God, they will when you fall.

You think it's nothing to me,
you think it's not painful,
but now there isn't much you see.

You told me you would stop it,
you told me it was over,
that you were finished with that shit.

But it isn't the first lie you told,
it's not the first promis you broke,
but now your lies are getting old.This isn't very good, I was just upset and started rambling.  It doesn't seem to flow very well, I guess it's just my raw emotions, it's hard to watch someone you love fall so hard.
