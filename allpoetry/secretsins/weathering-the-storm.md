What is it now that you want me to do,
when you know I belong to another?
You know that I can't, I have to stay true,
but it hurts to see you with your lover.  

Something happens when you say you love me,
the words that you say have me so confused.
When you speak to me, it's like I'm set free.
You take away the pain of being used.

You two are so diff'rent, like day and night,
he makes me feel safe, but you make me real.
I know you won't lose me without a fight,
you're not like him you're not afraid to feel.

We both know that we can't be together,
I guess this storm, we just have to weather.I wrote this for my senior english class, we were writing sonnets.  It's about my boyfriend and the guy I was in love with.
