What's the point of this anymore?
Why don't we just give up?
We constantly lie, say it's going to get better.
What goes up, must come down.
Why do we feel like lying 
about the people we've become?
Are we really that terrible?
Are we that ashamed?
Have we become as dirty 
as we make ourselves out to be?12-30-01,
