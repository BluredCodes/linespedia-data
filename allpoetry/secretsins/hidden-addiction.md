You spent so long, hiding it from us all.
How'd you do it?
Lesser would have stumbled under the pressures of her addiction.
You hid it so well,
that even now, seven months later,
I find myself wondering if it was all just a bad dream.
Then I remember the phone call,
the look on your face when I confronted you. 
I remember the hurt in your voice,
when you asked how I could do that.
I remember it all every time I look at your picture,
cuz it's all I have left.
