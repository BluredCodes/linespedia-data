---
title: "The Rain"
writer: "SecretSins"
slug: "the-rain"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I've always loved the rain,
it washes away the filth you put on me.
I've hiden from you for all this time,
but I can't seem to forget.
Your days of cheap thrills and lies 
linger in my mind.
All those days you said you loved me,
the times you cried when I told you
I'm in love with someone else. 
You can call me a whore for cheating on you,
but baby, what did you expect??
I've always loved the rain, 
it washes away the filth of you.
And at the end of the storm,
I see my rainbow,
and he is all I need to dry from the rain.
