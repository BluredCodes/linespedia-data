---
title: "Let Me Grow Up"
writer: "SecretSins"

Everything is so confusing now, 
I don't know what to do.
Everytime I think of running away,
you say "I Love You."

If you love me so much,
stop treating me like a child,
I grew up long ago,
I'm not meek and I'm not mild. 

You say I lost all your trust,
but I never had it anyway.
You say eventually I'll understand,
I laugh, that'll be the day.

I know something you don't know,
you think I'm so niave,
but I've dealt with things you couldn't imagine,
I've seen things you couldn't concieve.
