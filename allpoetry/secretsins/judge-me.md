---
title: "Judge Me"
writer: "SecretSins"

source: "AllPoetry"

---

I've never judged you for a thing you did. 
When you needed me I was there. 
Do you have any idea how many times I've defended you?
I believed in you,
trusted you.
But we grew apart,
most people do eventually.
I guess I figured we all just grew up.
I was wrong.
We've ALL made mistakes,
but I guess mine are the only ones you see.
But it's okay,
it does lead me to wonder though,
of all the things you've said behind my back.
