You're laying there,
wearing nothing,
but everything just the same.
You're eyes undressing me, 
take away all of my problems. 
All because of those eyes,
and how my bare skin feels rubbing against yours. 
You're my perfect tomorrow, 
and my cure for yesterday.
There isn't a place in this world I'd rather be,
than laying naked in this bed,
next to you.
