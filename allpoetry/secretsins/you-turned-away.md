Let me ask a question, cuz it's been on my mind.
When you needed help,
did I turn my back on you?
When you felt "cheap after a one night stand", 
did I call you a whore and say that I couldn't believe
how you've changed?
When you were all messed up on LSD and Coke and God knows what,
did I say you dissapointed me?
Did I say I couldn't look at you?
Well I'm not on drugs, 
YOU taught me better than that. 
And I've never felt cheap,
even after that night.
But I've been hurt and I've been scared. 
Just like you!!
But I don't have a shoulder to lean on,
cuz you turned your back on me and my mistakes, 
and I never turned my back on you.I know that this sounds sort of conceited, I don't mean it to!! I know I'm not perfect, I've made plenty of mistakes. This is just how I feel at this particular moment.
