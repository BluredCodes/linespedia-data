No matter how hard I try,
     you still wont understand.

     You say you wont to get to know me,
     but yet you wont take the time.
 
     I got you the papers,
     and you just wouldn't read.
   
     I've did all I could,
     to help you see,
     who I am but you just don't wont to see.

     So now is the time to leave my feeling,
     to someone who cares.

     I just don't see why you didn't care.to john
