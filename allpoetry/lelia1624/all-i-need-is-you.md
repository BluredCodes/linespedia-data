title: "All I need is you"

All I need is your love,  to keep me going day by day.    All I need is to see your smile,  it keeps me going for miles.    All I need is you trust,  and for that I am free.      All I need is your faith,  to keep me going because I know you believe in me.     All I need is your understanding,  for that I know you care.     All I need is what I have,  in you and for that I love you.This is about Paul like most of the others are about he has stood by me and loved me and me kids even though they aren't his.
