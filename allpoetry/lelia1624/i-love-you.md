title: "I love you"

When I look into your eyes,   I see what lies ahead.   When you hold me in your arms,   I feel safe from anyone.   When you kiss me so soft,   it feels like we are in heaven taking a walk.     When you say I love you,   I believe it is true.   When I close my eyes and see your smile,   it makes me wont to run a thousand mile to be with you.   When we are together,   I wont time to stop forever.   When we are apart,   I wont to lock myself in the dark.   When you said give my boy a kiss from daddy,   I didn't know what to say.     when you listen about the way i feel,   it shows me you really care.   When you said wait it is to soon,   it made me see the real you.   When I say I love you,   I hope you know it is true.   I love you unconditionally and I know you love me to.
