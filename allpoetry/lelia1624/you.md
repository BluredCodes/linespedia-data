You act like it is all Okay,
   but you wont look deep inside.
 
    My heart is broke,
   like this rose beside.

    You say you love me,
   but love is not sex or money.

    Love comes from the heart,
   and I'm just now seeing you don't have one.

    My love is fading fast,
   but you can't seem to see.

    What is going on inside of me.



    To John
