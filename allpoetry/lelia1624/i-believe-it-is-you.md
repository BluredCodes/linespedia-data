---
title: "I believe it is you"
writer: "lelia1624"

When I feel the wind upon my face I believe it is you softly touching my face.    When the wind blows the trees, I can hear your voice telling me over and over again how much you love me and will till the end.    When I feel the sun upon my face, I believe it is you brighting up my day.      When the moon light hits my face, I see you smiling down, with you bright blue eyes so filled with love.    When the stars shine so bright, I can feel the warmth of your arms, as if you were here holding me tight.    When I close my eyes to dream, your always there with a smile and the look of love in your eyes.
