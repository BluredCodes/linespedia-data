---
title: "I Fight"
writer: "Petra"
slug: "i-fight"

My eyes burn from the rays of the sun.  I didn't know it back then but now that I look back on that day I see it clearly.  My thirst for the energy it produced, ruled me.  I'm different now mentally and physically.  I looked normal enough, but if you looked closer you could see I wasn't.  It ruled my thoughts, my dreams, my fantasies, and even my emotions.  My hatred was growing every minute I thought of it, I would have killed for it.  My heart kept its beat just at the thought of obtaining its power and strength.  But things are different now.  Now fight to destroy it.  I fight to destroy it because of its power over me, over everything in my life.  I fight for life...This is the first time I've put poetry on the internet so I'm sorry if it's not really great.
