---
title: "Jane"
writer: "Petra"
slug: "jane"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

She haunts my dreams, 
and tortures my friends.
She's malicious and evil.

She kills for pleasure,
exhilerating she finds it.
She's graceful and seductive.
That is where her power lies,
within her beauty.

Jane, the evil demon of my dreams.
The night is her cloak and her lover.
I'm frightened to speak her name,
for fear she'll come.

She'll tempt me with eternal life.
I'm afraid I won't deny,
while in a state of delirium.

I would be damned to a life without God,
without my savior.

I'll be damned because of her,
I'll be damned because of me.
I'd live in the darkness, 
and she'd have me forever.

Condemned to the eternal abyss,
I would be as she is, 
and live as she does.

I will pray she never comes,
I will pray for my soul, 
and I will speak her name without fear.
Here I know I am protected, 
and safe from her presence.Jane was a vampire that was in one of my dreams a while back and she was so different I decided to write about her.
