---
title: "My Best Friend"
writer: "Petra"
slug: "my-best-friend"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

She is dark,
she is mysterious,
she has the face of a doll,
and the heart of a tiger,
she has innocent eyes,
you'd think she were an angel,
she is a magnetic tease.

She is a sexual goddess,
though a man couldn't touch her,
she is cunning and brilliant
with a touch of insanity.

She is beautiful.

She is talented.

And she is my best friend.
