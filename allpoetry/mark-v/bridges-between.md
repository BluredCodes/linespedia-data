title: "Bridges Between"
writer: "Mark V"

Twinkle, twinkle, little StarShining far, yet in my HeartWith Rays of Greenand Rays of BlueForming Bridges BetweenMy Soul and YouThough traveling lightCannot be seenThis Light's so BrightForming Bridges BetweenNor Darkness, nor a Thundery CloudCould Kill this Light, or Dim it OutThis Light is shining through the StarsBack and ForthBetween Our Hearts
