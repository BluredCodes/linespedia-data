Have I lost it?
Is it gone?
or do I fear the power?

Words transcend but
rapidly fall down
for loss of meaning

The worry, the doubts
took away their wings

Words can touch
but cannot mend
what is broken

Your beautiful gift 
has left me
stuttering in awe

How I wish to find
just the right words
to say ...

I Love You
