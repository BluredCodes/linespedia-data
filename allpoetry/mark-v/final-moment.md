---
title: "Final Moment"
writer: "Mark V"
slug: "final-moment"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/5405-Final-Moment-by-Mark-V"
---

Rustling Wings .. Restless
Eminent Lights their Guidance
Spirits Soar .. Wafted
into Bliss .. Paradise Found
Final Moment of Goodbye
