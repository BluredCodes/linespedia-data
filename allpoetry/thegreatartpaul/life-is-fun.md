Life is fun
Dancing or run
Singing or listening
Winter or spring
Being with a friend
Talking with them
Or having a quiet time alone with a book and some pretty music

Life is fun
Chewing on some gum
Reading a joke
Drinking a coke
Studying nature
Thinking hopefully about the future
Sleeping in extra long so you won't get sick

So ski
Or don't ski
Be
Who you want to be
To see
More inventively freeMaybe today you should try something new ?
