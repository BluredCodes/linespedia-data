SmileSince we will be here for a whileJesus said be like a childYou might just as well make the best out of the tripDress up kind of hipListen to some cool jazzAnd smile while your enjoying the razz man tazzOf the happy paradeMake sure you try Grandma's LemonadeWho said life had to be dullThough there is surely to be lowsYou go fishing but you don't get a bluegillEven still happyness growsWhere ever your smile goesSo let other's knowThe joy within your soulThat God is the real goldAnd when life is going slowSmile dispite it allGod's hope is eternalJesus rocks your worldNo matter how dark it seems Smile and dream dreamsThere are better days aheadSmile don't dreadBecause you have a friendWho's friendship never endsSo Smile again and againHave you ever lost a love one ?God understands because he to had a son who died.
