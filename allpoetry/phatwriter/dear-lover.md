Dear lover
I have not much in this world
but wat little i was born with 
and what little i can scrape together from time to time
I love you with all my heart
I will be around as long as you want me to be
you are the sunshine of my life
the bright light that wakes me in the morning 
im sorry that i dont have the money to buy you whatever you want
but all i can really give you
is my heart, 
and my undieing affection for as long as you want it 
"this worlds an ugly place, but your so buetiful to me"

yours forever,
PHATwriter
