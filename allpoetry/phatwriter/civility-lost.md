Courtesy and kindness
in a pool
of ignorance and blindness
centuries of hateful words
rivers and lakes flood
loneliness and bullies
years of abuse and noogies
sticks and stones may break his bones
but civility never hurt anyone
walking down the street with a monkey on my back
a lifetime of hateful works stack and stack 
on my back 
Courtesy and kindness 
in a pool 
of ignorance and blindness
Tommy boy and tesla coil
nothing stops a lifetime toil
so count your blessings
before they hatch
and savor that last catch
civil s a dream
just like bliss
another day of ignorance
a rotting planet
a hate filled city
help us remember
when things were pretty
so eat your eggs and your pancakes too
but look back just once 
and say 
thank you
with this I part 
unto the night
until then I say 
goodnight
do not fear a little kindness
because no matter what or how blind he is
a little courtesy
never hurt
