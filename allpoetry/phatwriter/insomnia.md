everynight at my pillow drenhced in tears
i think about people
 people and fears
i wonder whos talking and sleeping like me 
i wonder who drinking or trying to see
to see the moonlight and the dawning sun fight over the sky
so as this fight continues, i wonder where i am , 
thinking about memories and where we all stand
the sun creeps in after his victory
shining on my face, "go away" i shout "and leave me be"
this withered garden i call a mind is shorter now then rain or time
time to wake for a day
a day of happines u ask?
no not quite, 
a day of mothers milk and quiet rest
of cartoons and toys 
girls and boys
of many naps and fathers laps
another night awaits
another night of pondering 
looking through my cage, through the cracks
