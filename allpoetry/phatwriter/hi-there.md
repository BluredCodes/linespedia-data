Hey there 
how ya doin?
Im fine 
Thanks for askin

Dream about you every night
where you goin i didnt mean to scare you

Hello there
You look so pretty
 tonight
your welcome

Dream about you every night
where you goin i didnt mean to scare you

Good evening
Ill take two please
thank you 
I will

Good night
I had fun
I hope you did too
Thank you for being so wonderful
