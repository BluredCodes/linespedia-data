Buetiful blue eyes
prettier than the bluest skys
when i look into those pretty eyes
I promise ill never tell any lies

Bright red hair 
I would never dare
to hurt you

Soft Lips
Kissing them
takes me away 
to another place

a place where your love is the wind 
and your eyes are the sky
and your red hair is like the rain
and your soul is the sun

I hope this wind never dies
Coming from the blueest skys
with the brightest sun
in the brightest rain i have ever seen

i hope the weather never changes
this sun shower engulfs my soul into its buety 
and it possess me 
but i would not have it any other way 
so again i thank you sweet sun shower
