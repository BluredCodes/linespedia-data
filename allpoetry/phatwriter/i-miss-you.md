---
title: "I miss you"
writer: "PHATwriter"
slug: "i-miss-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

If i could catch a star and place it in your hand
I d swim a thousand oceans to meet you on the sand

If i could catch a rainbow and place it in your room
I d plant you a million flowers just to watch them bloom

If i could catch the love that flows from my heart 
id give it all to you and we d never be apart

If i could love you forever and never see you hurt
id slay a million dragons and bury them in the dirt

if i could write a poem to show you how much i care
it would win a million prizes and everyone would stare

If  something i could say could make you be happy
id say it a thousand times when things get crappy

Just remember how much i love you , you shine like the sun
and when i see your pretty face , i know that your the one
