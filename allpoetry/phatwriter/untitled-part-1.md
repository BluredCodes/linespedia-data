When we were kids
everything was great
we played for endless hours
but never really late

baseball and basketball 
hockey and lacrosse
in the summer sun 
or in the winter frost

riding bikes and
 swimming holes
fighting imaginary goblins
and trolls

now that we are older things have changed a bit
friends come and go 
but our fire stayed lit

through thick and thin
rough and old
 middle school
unfold

freshman year brought new faces
new ides and different places

high school started the other day
we made new friends and went to play

making music all day long until she came along
i called your house , you weren't home
your mom said you rode you bike alone

i rode with friends of ancient lost 
through our old old frost

i saw you there oh friend of friends
standing where the sidewalk ends

talking laughing , talking still
who is that person? who is that girl?

i walked over to say hello
you walked away 
and i got mellow

i went home and wondered why 
our old friendship made me cry

until that i made a choice
to listen to that inner voice

i kicked and screamed
cried and dreamed 
back when things were perfect

we had each other everyday
ran and played

through think and thin 
friends and kin

all the things we planned for now are over here
gone somehow 
ended by an evil face
never again in this place

love is strong and friend stay long
until it becomes a song

a song of sorrow 
a song of fright
into the brainwashed night

whatever happen to those days
the days i miss and always praise

until the day we meet again
in my mind i love you my friend

so when she leaves ill be here
crying tears of hope and fear
