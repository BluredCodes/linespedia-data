three little words can change a lifetime
three little words can make people a past time
some call it luck
 some call it sport
some leave it alone
 and out of thier heart
how can three words mean so much u ask?
more damage than the biggest car crash
well let me tell you
let me inform
those three little words 
are out of the norm
under the covers many lay and cry
 when hearts turn to cold
and love starts to die, 
so no matter how bad things start to get 
hold your head up high and never regret
by regret i mean i leave no clue 
just dont say these three little words
"i hate you"
