---
title: "My denial"
writer: "PHATwriter"

many an enemy
and few a friend
broken lifes 
left to mend



unwilling air
divided years
nuthing left 
but you and tears



was what we had just a dream?
what was left but self esteem?


they go short
friends stay long
broken hearts
left a song


was what we had just a dream?
what was left but self esteem?

long awaiting the arival
its been so long in my denial
a crooked man and evil find 
all take place in my mind





\once was life
 in these big green eyes
my ignorance
my life denies


was what we had just a dream?
what was left but self esteem?



long awaiting the arival
its been so long in my denial
a crooked man and evil find 
all take place in my mind
