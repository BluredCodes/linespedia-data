Anger loathe and hatred fear
Brandishing a tiny tear
I wish the world a little box 
Runny away like a fox
Under hills and inside holes
Dragons toes and ugly foes
Leave me be ex lover lost
Leave me be or youll feel the cost
Ill let my anger lose
And who will  be in my knoose?
I have a clue of who it will be
Anger pulling and grabbing me 
So stop this worthless escopade
Stop this stupid fight today
Im tired of your little world
And all the people in it 
No one boasts there ingnorance
So stuff it and stop it 
Ive had enough of angry days 
If lovers lost and faded ways 
Ways of love and ways of hate
Anger rage and loathing ways
I cant forgive the way you are 
It will always leave a scar
But one things sure of all of this
Ive become stronger through expierience
