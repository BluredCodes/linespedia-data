I can remember countless nights that I had hoped for someone to hold
Last night,the wish came true and you kept me warm from the cold
Oh the sweet sensation of your body touching mine
You're gentle kisses made it all devine
The feeling between us was something true
And in my heart my love for you grew
You've proven to me that you care and you love
I belive you and love you back my sweet little dove
Never more shall the cold dark wind blow in my face
Because you've wraped me with your wings and now I am safe
This to me will always be 
My most cherished memorie
Thank you for steping into my life
And thank you for giving me that moment "last night..."
