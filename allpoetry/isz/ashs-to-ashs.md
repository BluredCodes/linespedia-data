---
title: "Ash's to Ash's"
writer: "Isz"
slug: "ashs-to-ashs"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/29419-Ashs-to-Ashs-by-Isz"
---

As a seed of new
Planted in fresh soil

Slowly growing out of the dirt
Growing tall in the spring breeze
To flower it's paddles in the sun
It does grow.

But just as fast as the seed was planted
The buds turn black as night
And in seconds the flower is nothing but ash
To be blown around in the storms of summer

And never again can love bloom.
For ash's to ash's there is no hope.

By: Isz
