---
title: "Caged and Free"
writer: "Isz"
slug: "caged-and-free"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

With nothing to hold me here
I'm beginning to stray
Looking to the horizon
My imagination begins to race
My Sagittarius soul has begun to stir
I need to find out more
I need the new experience
Few can hold me down
But only one can hold me back
A person with a title only I can give them
And there is no person to give it to
So restless I have become
But now my world is too small
I can't stay at home and wander from my doorstep
I must set forth to find new things
My hopes and dreams no decaying
Own the pedestal I had put them on
For Gavyn is caged and Isz is free
Once again I must travel
And show people my face
For the soul others have seen
Was never meant to be

By: Isz
