---
title: "Lost love"
writer: "Isz"
slug: "lost-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/31013-Lost-love-by-Isz"
---

Why is it I do everything for you
You don't even know that I do
And you would not care if you did

I remember the times in the past
When you would run into my arms
And I would hold you tight
You told me you never felt so save

I remember the dreams we had planned
The white picked fence dreams
Those romantic fun filled dreams
That dream of ours forever

When you left with out a reason
It killed something inside me I cannot bring back
You brought out the best in me
I still do everything for you

Maybe one day you will understand
Find the sparkle in my eye you saw
I am a hopeless romantic but you loved that
Maybe you can find what you saw when I return

I will always think of you
Till I return
Good-bye my lost love
May this poem find it's way to you someday

By: Isz
