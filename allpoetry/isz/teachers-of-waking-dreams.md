---
title: "Teachers of Waking Dreams"
writer: "Isz"
slug: "teachers-of-waking-dreams"
source: "AllPoetry"

Oh teachers in my waking dreams
I called to you as a child to teach me
All the lessons of the world
To make me wiser then those around me
But I the human world this knowledge failed
I am told I am wise be many
Many from all over the world
But this knowledge came at a price
You did warn me my dear teachers
And I know I said I could take it
But I yield I am to mortal
For in this vessel I am not as strong
Please stop the torture
Bring me into the light
In which I stood when I first asked you
This old wise man is to wise for his wishes
I don't want to learn more about pain
Or the sorrow that blankets this world
Let me lose myself in the daydreams
Those dreams that everyone uses to get by
Day by day the problems they don't see
Take back my gifts if in turn you will let me smile
If you will let me love
And be loved in return
I give up my wisdom free to you teachers
Everything from knowledge of the magic energies
To the abilities to read dreams so clearly
Even down to my dreams of things not past
Take it all away
If in the end I am happy with my love
Take it all away

By: Isz
