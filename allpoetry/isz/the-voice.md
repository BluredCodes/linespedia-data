I walk to my place of time wasting
It is bitter cold outside
The rain begins to fall
Thoughts of things long past fill my head
A voice that used to cheer my on
Was yelling at me
"You fucked up"
"YOU lost your chance"
"YOU will NEVER be HAPPY"
"YOU WILL NEVER BY LOVED AGAIN!"
I yell back
Get out of my head
People stare at me
I continue walking
It will not shut up
I get to my place of time wasting
I walk in the doors
Muddling to myself as I walked down the stairs
The voice became a whisper
I began to talk to my friends
And then she came down the stairs
I went white
The voice yelled even louder
"you... will... N-E-V-E-R... E-V-E-R... be love... AGAIN!"
I got up and left
I stood in the rain
In the bitter cold
I could not see her cold eyes
I could not be remembered of what I lost
Time flowed by
I did not know how long I was out there
I turned around
Look at the door to my place of time wasting
"NEVER AGAIN"
I yelled out into the night
GET OUT OF MY HEAD
People stared at me again
I turned
Ran for the bus
When I got home
It still was yelling
I could not make it stop
I sat down
And only when my cousin came to see me
Did the voice stop
I am at peace
For now

By: IszSorry this poem is in story formate. But it is the words of my heart body mind and soul. Thou they may not be mine anymore. They do still talk to me.
