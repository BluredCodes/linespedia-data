---
title: "Cocktail of Tears"

Tears of my soul did spill
To join tears of my fallen body
Pondering the pain I felt
Collecting my cocktail drops
With clouded thoughts I walked
I could not stand still
With drink in hand
I walked off
Vision blurred
Lost my glasses
Could not see
No sleep 
Did drained me so
I walk with the wind
For it is my sight
To cloudy to think
Cold sinks in
Pulled my cocktail
Up to my face
The glass was full
My head was light
What should I do now
Tossed the glass
To the middle of the street
May my tears of the soul
Keep me strong
May my tears of the body
Set me free

By: Isz
