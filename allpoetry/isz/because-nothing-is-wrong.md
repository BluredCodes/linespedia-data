---
title: "Because Nothing Is Wrong"
writer: "Isz"
slug: "because-nothing-is-wrong"

With the open wound
I show to know one
I wonder the night
Bitter cold is it around me
The snowfall does not help
Someone walks up to me
I smile
They start talking to me
Nothing wrong
No nothing at all
Have a nice day
And another meaningless face
Fades from my mind
I wish I could help myself
But I just get even more lost
I don't take other's advice
For I must give up to much
This wound is killing me
But I wonder on
Something must be able to help me
I must find it
Before I am consumed

By: Isz
