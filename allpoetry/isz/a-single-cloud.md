---
title: "A single Cloud"
writer: "Isz"
slug: "a-single-cloud"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/36737-A-single-Cloud-by-Isz"
---

Separated by friend old and young
In either group I am left alone
Everyone has his or her life’s to live
They’re "others" to be with
And I am stuck
In an ageless gap
To many problems to be so care free
Not enough solutions to be steady
Like a single cloud
I drift, floating ever so slowly
Following the morning fog
And chancing the afternoon shower
Yet always to late to join in
But just in time for the stories
On the times I had missed
I am just a poorly mixed drink
Of emotions, actions, and bad timing
Nothing can come good of it
And so I drift on
Chasing a new flock of birds

By: Isz
