---
title: "We will soar"
writer: "Isz"
slug: "we-will-soar"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/29847-We-will-soar-by-Isz"
---

Walking free of my form
In dreams I am not human

To soar up high amongst the clouds
Back when the world was unscarred to mankind

To feel free of my binds
With my great wings stretch wide
My white scales shining as brightly as ever
I soar free

Knowing that I must maintain the balance
The energies of magic run strong
And the battles continue
Waiting for the great war

Seeing what would happen
I must rely on the humans
The others are getting old
But knowing they are my downfall
I protect them
House there sick
Train there wise
For they must win the war

To the great guardian kin I hope
We have learned are lesson
So we may return to claim or world
And leave these vessels behind
We will be great again
Be strong my kin
For we will soar

By: Isz
