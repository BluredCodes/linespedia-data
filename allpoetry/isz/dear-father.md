Dear father
I will follow you soon

The drink is bitter but yet warms me up
Is this what is destined for the Lakeman family
Can we not bear to deal with the problems of the world?

Father I wish you where here. I wish I could see you
Even just once more. To have the chance to talk to you
To ask you for advice like I should

We are left alone as always
Father please come home soon
Or give me the strength to follow you

I am so tired of this world already
And my life has only just begun

Oh father I wish you could have seen me in my better days
But above and beyond I wish you could see me now

I know your kind words would help me
I know your arms would hold your child
Even for a second... I need you here

I wish I were not such a spineless bitch
I wish I had ran to you and brought you home
Mom still loves you even if she hides it well
And I miss you more and more ever day
It may have been years that we have been apart
But please father, show me the way

I want to make you proud
But still greatest of all
I want you back
Selfish as it might seem dad
But if I had you here I would have my strength to be happy

You and me again
Off to a movie outside our apartment
I have few memories
Wish I had so many more

I know you have passed on long ago
But even now I cannot let you go
This world is to troubled and filled with pain

Oh father please come home
Wipe the tears off your childs face
And show him the kind heart you are well known for

I drink to you
In memory of the 'Wonderers' father
May he rest peacefully for all time in the company of his loved ones
Awaiting the day his son comes back to him
Forever
Here's to you dad.
