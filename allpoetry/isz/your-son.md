---
title: "Your Son?"
writer: "Isz"
slug: "your-son"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/25103-Your-Son--by-Isz"
---

As I look at you
Hatred roles over the hills
You do not have the right
Judgement is not yours to give
You through me away
I am not your son
I will not smile because you say so
I laugh only at your failure
At myself in your eyes
Every year you're surprised
Surprised I'm alive
I am sick of your yelling
I am not your son

By: Isz
