Old friend

We have spent years together
And months apart
We have gone through it all together

You know me better then I know myself
As I know you better then you do
Yet we can't help each other
But we are always there
When ever we have needed each other

As I go on in my life
You go on in yours
Problems come and go
Even though we do not open up
We know what is wrong

I cannot hide from you
And you will never be able to hide from me
For our friendship grows with time
And year have unfolded
To show a friendship that will never end

May we continue to live on
Find are way's to stable ground
For we will be there together
At some point
We will see the world
As we would like

My old friend

By: Isz
