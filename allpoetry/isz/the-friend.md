---
title: "The 'Friend'"
writer: "Isz"
slug: "the-friend"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/36019-The-Friend-by-Isz"
---

You cry and I'm there
Always giving you a hug
Makes you smile
Aren’t I a great friend
Don't worry
I will always be there
Go run and play
If the time comes
I will always step out
Of those shadows I call home
To pick you up
And put you back on the path
Aren’t I a great friend
I watch everything I do
To make sure to please you all
And though I may slip
I turn around and pick up those pieces
For I am there for you
Even when I can't be there for myself
Aren’t I a great... am I really
I don't know anymore
Sometimes I just can't see myself
Everyone says I can be the greatest friend
But I'm told that I can't help others
If I can't help myself
And well time shows
I just can't solve my problems anymore
So go run and play
I will always by there when you need me
Being some one to you
That I can't be to myself

By: Isz
