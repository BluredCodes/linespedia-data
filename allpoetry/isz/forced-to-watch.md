title: "Forced to Watch"
writer: "Isz"

I fall from your world
I sit watching
Watching as your world crumbles
I can't get back in
You will not let me

All I do is sit
Sit and watch as you fade
Why am I forced to stay
I had been there for so long
I want to be there

The smiles of childern
Hunt my dreams
The dreams that can't ever be
For I am sitting
Watching your world fall apart

By: Isz
