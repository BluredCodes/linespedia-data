My eyes half shut
I pear around the room
Know one to be found
I am alone at work
Just like always
Another 14 hours of this...
Phone rings none stop

I do computers
Not phones
Hello
Yes boss
No boss
Will you just leave me alone

13 hours 50 mins to go
Phone still ringing

Hello
Yes boss
No boss
Let me do my work
Yell at me
See if I stay
You sicken me
Back off

The day goes on just like the last
I hate this job
But I need the money
So I work the hours
Day in day out
He wonders why I snap
The phone rings again

Hello
Yes boss
No boss
Lisson, your on my list now
BACK OFF
Come on boss
Fire me
You no one in the world works like I do
No one will take your shit like me
I work for one third my min pay
I did not continue my schooling for this
You heared me
Now what are you sceaming
No I will not work better if anything changes
I will be the same
You should leave me alone
Mr. I know computers
Mr. I can do it all
Then do it
Mr. accountent
That can't even turn on his box
Try me

The day continues
More and more I wish
I wish for someway to get out
Find something better
But then the tables turn
With out this job I must go home
Phone rings

Hello
Yes boss
No boss
What was that boss... click

He will call back
My ears will ring
I know he needs me
Everyone else quits
Phone rings

Hello... Click

He knows he's pissing me off
Thats why he does it
I should just leave again
He should learn...
This time...
May be...
Nope...
Oh well

I return to my work at hand
Phone rings
I lose track of my thoughts

Grumble...
Hello
Yes boss, No boss
Yes boss, Yes boss
No boss
Grumble
Click

Now where was I...
Phone rings... ... ...
