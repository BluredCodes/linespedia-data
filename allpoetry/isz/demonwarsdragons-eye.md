With red scales
I armoured myself
With feathered wings
I dressed myself
With staff in hand
I walk through my forest
I took my last look around
Made sure my forest was to be safe
And with the memory of my lost love
The memory that turn this dragon to rage
I left the safety of my forest
I turned my back on my councle
They wanted to sit and watch the end
From there high peeks they would watch
For the laws spoke out for my kin
Dragons must not take part
Your time has come and gone
May you have guarded the mortals well
And trained them to survive
My love who I searched for
To many long years I did search for
Was gone
Betrayed by her own mortal friends
I was filled with rage
I would never see her flowing white scales
Those light blue eyes filled with hope
I would never fly above are land with her at my side
I had lost a dragon's love
And it can not be replaced
I charge into there keep
These mortals I had trained
With the thoughts that I was the reason she died
I unleased the mystic powers of the dragon kin
Not till the last mortal fell
Did I see the demons that surrounded my love
I had spilt the blood of the pure and possessed alike
I had failed in my ways
My councle turn on me
They would not help there kin
With my love in my claws
I took flight to depths unknown
To fight off the pledge that took my love from me
I would not fight on the mortal realm
For my rage consumed all life
And to spill more blood of the pure
Would only end in the lose of there home
I fought till my scales cracked
And a pain dragons where never ment to feel
Filled my body
I was dieing
I retreated to my forest
I placed my love by my river of life
I gave my love the life they took from her
But as my curse I would live as a mortal
Doomed to search forever for my lost love

By: IszThis is a child's version of the tellings of the demon wars seen through this dragon's eyes. I will write more on the battles which took place as I chased down my love's lost soul.
