I open my eyes
All around are fears
Things I wish I would never see
I wish I would never have to live
Things that I feared
More then anything physical
I look up at the way things where
I have fallen so far
I see all the times I fell
And how I stood back up
Tried to climb back to here I was
Just to fall even farther
What do I do now?
I dear not look down
For I find
If I fear it
It will be
I look around me
Harden my shell
Harder then I have ever
Nothing gets at me
And as long as I avoid
The only thing that can smash me
I 'WILL' be fine
One last look around
I 'WILL NEVER' fall this low again
My strength grows with in
And I prepare for the long journey
For I am Isz
Here me screech

By: Isz
