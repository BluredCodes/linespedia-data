Blessed with death every night
Cursed with birth every morning
Cry yourself to sleep little child
For the morning comes soon
And the pain will only grow
So cry yourself to sleep
And harden your shell
For tomorrow has more pain to add
Sweat dreams my child
The morning comes soon
