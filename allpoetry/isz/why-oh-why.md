title: "Why oh Why"
writer: "Isz"

Though I continue to walk alone down this road
I am always pulled back into time
Still doing everything for her
Yet with no smile to add
I can't turn away from this past
I wish it would all come back at times
Others I am left to ponder what if?
No mater how much I harden my shell
I am left like a small child
Who has wondered from mother's sight
Lost in a mall 
But I walk on
Lost in my mind
What am I left with?
What can I bring back to this life?
And What if's?
I pull my hat down and continue on
When I next look beyond the brim
May my world have changed
May I once again
Wear that smile I miss so much

By: Isz
