---
title: "The Return"
writer: "Isz"
slug: "the-return"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Peace returns to the minds of few
I am left wondering what have I become
So kind and caring I once was
What happened to those days?
I have finally found myself again
I ask myself what had happened
With a blank face I got no answer
But a solution I was told
Now I am complete once again
To think of how life is grand
In all its misery, how the sun shines
May those that think poorly of me
Continue on their lives with out me
For the person they did know is dead
And I have returned to my childhood youth
I smile for you my child
May the haunting dreams fade to memory
And those who will meet you be filled with joy
The joy you spread, before the changing
Before I decided to give all to one
And none to all
Those who wish to stay
Please forgive me past
For the past did not run to deep
Before it was a joyful time
I have returned
May this smile never fade

By: Isz
