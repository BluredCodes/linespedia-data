---
title: "The Spoiled Children"
writer: "Isz"
slug: "the-spoiled-children"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/24562-The-Spoiled-Children-by-Isz"
---

Like children in a field
They play to make all yield

Like children out to win
They will pop all dreams with a pin

For these few as old as they seem
Are nothing but children, spoiled and mean

By: Isz
