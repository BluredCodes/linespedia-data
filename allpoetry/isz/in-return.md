---
title: "In Return"
writer: "Isz"
slug: "in-return"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

In the end it does not matter
No matter how nice I am
The actions I take
Nothing I can say or do
I am myself and still, it does not matter
To move on is impossible
For there is nothing else there
I do this all for you
I have given up everything for you
I have even put my life down
Just to save yours
In return I am left
Standing in the cold
Under the night sky
By my own free will it seems
I live on only because of you
This body is failing
My mind is to tainted
A shattered heart now lies
Where once it grow with out bounds
And a soul empty and hollow
Left with nothing
I only continue on for you

By: Isz
