title: "The Reason Why"
writer: "Isz"

The more and more I say good bye
The more and more the reasons are clear
For it is she that drives me to leave
I failed her in love for reasons unknown to me
Everyone around has something bad to say
But with my own eyes I see they smile at her
Who is being lied to I don't know
What can I do to show her the kind side again?
Nothing so far has worked
I must leave to find myself
But will I return
And if I do would she take me back
I fear not
I am lost in memories of times past
She will not even talk to me now
Repent all I have done
Now only one person is still mad
But it is still she
So I turn my back and leave

By: Isz
