---
title: "Tear to the Streets"
writer: "Isz"

My travels through life lead to dead ends
Once again I am forced to turn to the cold streets
Just to stay close to those I hold dear
Though my family is far I do hold the dear as well
But I will not be a burden to them ever again
So I cannot go back to there warm homes
I am too stubborn to turn to my friends with my problems
They’re doing to well now to have to deal with my shit
To many bad choices made to fix them
My problems have gone from I'm late for school
And who would be attracted to me
To where can I sleep safely and is there food today
I must have done terrible things to find myself here
I was the one with the promising future
Now I am the one doomed to live in boxes
At the side of the roads I used to run by
And as my friends find out my problems
The more they are angry at my choices
It does help me at this point in time
I just feel unwanted by all

           @-\--
A rose to guide my path
As I continue my journy
Alone in the dark
I walk the streets

By Isz
