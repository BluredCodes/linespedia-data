---
title: "The Write"
writer: "Isz"
slug: "the-write"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Comments surround my voice
Ways they think
Cloud my mind
These are words of my soul
My style is of my heart
The writings are written be this body
And the stories
Are emotions felt by this mind
When I write
My teachings fade
For I am set free
No reason to comment
For I believe I am true
A real poet
And my writings are words
Words of my heart, mind, body, and soul
There is no correct way to follow
No wrong way to steer clear
I am no longer boxed
I am free
To write the words of me

By: IszThis was written to explain to those that don't like my style of writting. We are or can be poets. The words and thoughts are there. Set them free in anyway you'ed like.
