---
title: "To those in need"
writer: "Isz"

These things an Isz has promised
Forever till the ends of time

To offer a shoulder to all
To be there to wipe away every tear
To make you laugh when you had to fall
To show you how to smile in the face of fear

To stay at your side every day
To all ways be strong
To never falter as others may
To rush to your side no matter who long

To be the best friend you have ever had

And most of all to give the best hugs ever given

For these are the promises I have made
And the reason why I will always be...
Be a friend to all for all time...

Dedicated to those that need a friend...
I am always here for all... Just give me a call...

By: Isz
