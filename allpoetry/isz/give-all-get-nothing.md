---
title: "Give all, Get Nothing"
writer: "Isz"
slug: "give-all-get-nothing"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/24360-Give-all--Get-Nothing-by-Isz"
---

To have given up all you know
To have given up everything for just one
And to know that you still will never be happy
Searching for something you know
But yet it is not possible to find
To get to the point where only one person knows
Knows what makes you happy and yet
That person will never decide to make you happy
Because you have given up all for them
You have nothing to give to anyone else
To love one that you can never have
And to lose the choice to love another
The touchier that they put you through
To be teased and always let you feel the pain
Knowing that you can never let yourself let them down
And that you have sworn to be there for them till the end of time
These bad mistakes I have made
Shall hunt me till the end of my days
Doom to love one I can not have
And cursed not to love any other
