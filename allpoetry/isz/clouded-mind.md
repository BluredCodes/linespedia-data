---
title: "Clouded Mind"
writer: "Isz"

Looking into your eyes
My spin ripples
Your eyes are so cold
Your presence in the same room
Makes me tremble
You where the only one
Who can hurt me?
To say you did
Would leave out so much pain
Even though you are not in the position too
You still cause me so much trouble
I tiptoe around you
But why
Why can't I just stop this?
You don't want me
Why can't I, not want you
What is it you have over me
No one else sees anything in you
Why is it that I do?
But that does not matter to you
I feel nothing I ever did has
You will never read these
You don't even know I continued to write
But I write on
If there was a higher beginning
You would be told of these
But I don't even know what to make of them
Please don't hate me
For I can't stop loving you
And my pain only grows

By: Isz
