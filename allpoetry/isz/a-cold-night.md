title: "A cold night"
writer: "Isz"

Lost in a town so far away
Can not get home for I can not pay
Cold and alone
With no sign of home
To wander on in the dark
Or stand alone and make my mark
Paths wind left and right
Travels continue in search for a light
Something to keep me warm
I wish you would take form
Guide me home from the cold
For my love will never grow old

By: Isz
