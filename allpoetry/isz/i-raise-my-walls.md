title: "I Raise My Walls"

Through and through
I can not see
My vision is blurred
I care not to believe
I have lost my path
No warmth in the cold
To stiff to run
To numb to feel
The walls raise up around me
The masks are worn
Nothing in
No one will see
Be happy for I am not sad
At least as far as you know
Walk away like the others have
Return do not
For nothing lives in this body
My soul has bled it'self free

By: Isz
