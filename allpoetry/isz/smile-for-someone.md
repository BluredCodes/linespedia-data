---
title: "Smile For Someone"
writer: "Isz"
slug: "smile-for-someone"
source: "AllPoetry"

It is worth being happy
For if it does not
Then it should not be
Nothing is worth being unhappy
And sometimes it is up to us
We must find joy in all
Smile for someone
For someone smiles for you

By: Isz
