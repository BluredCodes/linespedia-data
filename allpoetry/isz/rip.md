The pain and torcher of a memory
Those that will not leave my life
And the pain they gave still stings
When is it my turn to smile
To run through fields of joy
I wait for my time
Others find there loves
Wether in the eyes or arms of others
Sitting in the corner
No eyes to look into
Arms rapped around others
To alone to see the pain inside
Poetry is my only vent
No others see me as I am
Energies to strong to control
Dreams over powering
Stroies apon stories
May my mind rest
Rest I do need
I wish to sleep
To finaly rest in peace

     Here lies Isz
     May he rest in peace
     For he is finaly happy
     At rest away from the pain

The day will come when I will happy
One way or another I will be happy

By: Isz
