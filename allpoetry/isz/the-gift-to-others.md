title: "The Gift to Others"

Now as he walks down his darkened path, light did shine
For he had learned how to shine for others
But as this was a gift for others
Inside he all but curled up and died
In the darkness’s he had lived in for so long
Was what controlled his soul and mind
His body twisted by the travels
Nothing lingering of the proud man he once was
The loneliness is all he knows
Given up on that which made him happy
So he gives his greatest gift at all cost
He turns away from all his problems and shines
A false light it maybe
But in the dark it shines for others
Showing them the way to go
The way to be happier then they ever where
But at the cost of his own happiness
To laugh a heart filled laugh
Or to smile with joy
These are just a few things given
For the darkness has taken it's finally unbreakable hold

By: Isz
