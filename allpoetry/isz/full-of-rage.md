When things are never right
Time passes so very slowly
I have become what I have hated
I never ment to hurt anyone
Those that remember me before
Never want me to leave
No matter what I do
Those that did not
Hate me so
I was never hated before
Although this has happen over years
I only stepped into the light now
I see who I am
And how far I have fallen
I will leave
Hoping that i can come back
So that they will not turn
For this is not me
I am kind to all
I am not full of rage

By: Isz
