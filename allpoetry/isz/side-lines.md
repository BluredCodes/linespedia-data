As some find there happiness
And others have it taken away
I stand by
Picking up there pieces
They are happy
One way or another
I have made them happy
As for me
I have given up
So many I would like to know
To take a chance with
But I will stay here
On the side lines
For they all need friends
And that’s my lot
I will be a best friend to all
For happiness is not in my cards
When I laugh this way
May it continue to make you smile
When I make an ass of myself
May you always laugh
For I have mastered this act
And you will never see
My hidden tears
And bottle emotions
So ask me again
What's wrong?
Nothing, just thinking
Come on what's wrong
Oh nothing, come on smile you, nothing's wrong
Now you’re happy
And I may continue Lost in my world
Standing at the side lines of life

By: Isz
