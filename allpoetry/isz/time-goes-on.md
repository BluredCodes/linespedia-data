---
title: "Time Goes On"
writer: "Isz"

With opened eyes
I watched
From my perch
I sat
With wings unfolded
I stretched
The world changed around me
But I lived on
So many things changed
People have come and gone
I feel this body getting old
So many more years
Will anyone remember
Times long passed
Still haunt my mind
Good or bad
The thoughts do linger
People that I know
I try to make happy
And guard them
Making sure I'm there
To bring a smile
Even in the darkest hour
From my perch
Where I sit
With my opened eyes
I watch
As time goes on

By: Isz
