Once Again I find myself tangled
          Stuck in webs of words untrue
       I refuse to speak them
  Yet rapped am I in there webs
                 I pray to be left out
                   But more tangled I become
                                     Break the web
                                       And people get hurt
                                  Why do they lie so
                              I will never know
                     They seem inconsiderate
                  They take my writing
                Swear it is there's
                     Remove me from my room
                           Do I not exist
                                 To lie to me
                                Is to just lose me
                                To lie to others about me
                                    Is worse then sin
                           The wounds have become so deep
                                Then even time 
                      May never heal them

                                  By: Isz
