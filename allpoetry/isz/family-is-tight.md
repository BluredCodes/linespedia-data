---
title: "Family is Tight"
writer: "Isz"
slug: "family-is-tight"
source: "AllPoetry"

Brought from one world to another
For what I can never understand
I am talked down too
Look upon as a black sheep
Why do they bring me along
When all they do is put me down
A holiday away from troubles
This is what a pray for
A break in life
A chance at being one
I move father away
Yet they still find me
Family is tight
Like a noose around my neck

By: Isz
