---
title: "Ageless Soul"
writer: "Isz"

Years pass on
Many see this young vessel
But inside you must look
For this soul is older then time
Broken memories of times long past
Things I could not know, yet do
As I train my soul, I remember more
The ways of the old
The ways of the free minded
I have flown over the great mountains
Stood in forest's of tree's older than man
To swim in the oceans crystal clear
To depths of until distance
These are some of my clouded memories
So much wisdom to be found
I travel my mind and explore
Things that happened, that I should not know
Views I claim to understand, that are so possible
I am of the few
Open-minded and free souled
For I am older then time
Look inside passed the bitter pains
Back past before you where human
Remember what you where
When you where free
Bring back those memories
Remember peace
And know, to even hate one
Is to be hated
Do onto others, as you would have them do onto you
NOT
Do onto others as others do onto you
We are bound by human emotions
Some are great
But most lead to pain
My greatest lesson learned
If they hate you, be kind to them
If they are kind to you, love them
If they love you, then you are truly happy
And all the surviving is justified
Learn the lessons of time
And be kind to all
These are the words of a young child
Molded by surviving the pain, yet being happy
A young child, with an ageless soul
Wondering free in open minds

By: Isz
