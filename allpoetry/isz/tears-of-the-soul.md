---
title: "Tears of the Soul"
writer: "Isz"
slug: "tears-of-the-soul"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/22933-Tears-of-the-Soul-by-Isz"

Travels made to see which was I
It was believed to be true
As the night trickled by on and on
The truth, which I saw, was little of that
For the time which was supposed to be spent
Was spent in times alone on the coach
Things I was told to say could not be said
I have lost once again what was found
And the bitter thoughts did sink in
Back to the path I do walk
Alone forever alone
This lesson I have learned
Painful was the truth
But this lesson I did learn
Finally I did learn
And in the darkness it did begin
The tears that I feel
Crying inside as to not hurt others
The pain I have come so accustom to
No one and nothing should change this
For in the past it was all taken away
To many times for me to believe
So on I go
Blade in hand
Hand out stretched
Not for people to grab
But in anticipation
For the time when the warm tears will fall
But not of the body but of the soul
All red and warm as they fall
This is my end and nothing more
No meaning is left
No reasons can be found

By: Isz
