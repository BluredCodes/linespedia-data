Is it You: 

When I close my eyes, 
I see the curves of your face.
I hear soft melodies playing.
Is it the memories of this place.

At night I dream of your taste.
It's like your there with me
I can feel your soft breath.
I was wondering is it me you see.

Some how I know your out there.
I feel a connection that is so tender.
The need to sleep for love.
Dreaming of you to surrender.

Will you sweep me off my feet.
It is a feeling, that is so new.
I see you every night,
I am wondering, is it you.I wrote this one trying to find out if I will ever be in real love.
