If you only knew, My Love:

I give you my number,
Hoping you'll return my calls.
I missed everyone that day.
Terrified of the dead air between us

Everyday my heart leads me to you.
You don't see me standing there,
but I am glancing at you from a far
Just hoping I'm on your mind.

I want to fall head over heals,
in love with your words.
That sweet smell of you,
I want locked in my heart.

Your glossy blue eyes are a maze, 
I can't help but to find my self lost, 
like a liltle child, listening for your tone.
Everytime our eyes come across each other.

I wish there was an easier way,
I want you to know whats on my mind.
I wish you would aproach me, 
Only if you knew how I felt.

Untill then I must dream of your tender touch.
I have to hear your voice before I sleep.
So those long phone calls at night are dear to me. 
Untill then sweet dreams and sleep tight my love.
