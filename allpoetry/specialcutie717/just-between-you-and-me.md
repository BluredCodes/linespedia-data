Just between You and Me:To my loving mother:What would a holiday be,without all the toys and stress.Just between you and me,money spent became more and more less.To make all those joy full memories.Time spent with a stress free nogan.Spinning records, playing melodies,and a frosted hot coco,in a tabogan.Engaged in a detailed discussion,thinking of those embracing moments.Just talking, no need of translation,silence speaks of no unremultfull coments.To believe that what we have now,is all we'll need in days to come.Faith in love, no questions how.Trusting faith until the day is done.Now only to remember the things we have learn.Leave us yearning for a tale you've once told.Taking the untaken path, ready for that turnHolidays come rare, so take it all, and beholdSara Elizabeth Santos
