Just a Whisper :Your soft breath,upon my ear.As you whisper,I will always be here.That tender voice.Brings a new feeling.My heart stands on end...Is that love what your stealing.Within every breath,I know your there.It sits heavily on my shoulder.It’s the weight of your stare.It’s love what you fear,but yet you want mine.it’s just a whisper, that stops the hands of time.
