title: "Leave me"
writer: "Elfgiva"

source: "AllPoetry"

I want you to die 
So you can not bring me any more pain
No Please dont leave me
But my heart aches for you
Why oh please tell me why 
I love you so
I want you to be with me
I want to feel your touch once more
Leave for good this time
Leave me to my sad songs and my cookie dough
Don't turn and stare at me when you leave
so my heart wont ache seeing your beautiful face
I'll be fine I'm sure..
NO dont leave me I love youI know its bad
