I hate you all 
You walk around in your conformists circles
A homegenous mixture
I can see you are living with inner disgust
of being a carbon copy of the worlds veiw of perfection
You make me sick
Why dont any of you try to differentate from the others 
WHy I want to know are you happy trying constanly to be the way "they" think you should be?
Think about itI know its mean Please dont think that Im streotypising people Im  just saying what i think Also I know it horrible and doesnt deserve to be called poetry
