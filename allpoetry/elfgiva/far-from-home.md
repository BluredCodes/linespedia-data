I stopped and sat on a long deserted beach
The sand between my baretoes
The wind whistled and chilled 
I pulled my cape closer
I close my eyes
Clicked my heels
I wish I were home I reapeated thrice
I squinted my eyes closed tighter
I hoped with all my heart 
And..
I opened my eyes tentatively
Still at the beach far from home
I got up and walked into the sea...Hey it just came to me in asecond it still not all thought out
