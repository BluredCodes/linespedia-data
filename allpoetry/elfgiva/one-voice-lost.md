I am unique
I am not some lone voice in the crowd that noone hears
Or do I just delude myself
Am I truely different or am I just the same 
a person that will never make a difference
I shout but does any one hear my woes?my insights?my wit?
Am I just lost in in this homogenous trudge that we call conformity?
Will anyone ever hear me 
Is my one voice un cared about
Will I ever be known for my work 
Will anyone cares when I die
I try to make a difference 
But does it mean anything?This sucks I know!
