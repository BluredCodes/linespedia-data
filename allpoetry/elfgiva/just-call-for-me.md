Just call for me
And I'll be there
over the world 
I would travel 
for your sweet kiss 
I would
swim the icy seas
climb the highest mountains
brave the fiery pits of hell
just to see your smile once more
I would 
run until my legs crumbled under me
until the barriers would not permit me through
until the world end 
and the angels come for us 
I will come
I wont stop until I feel your sweet embrace once moreI have no talent I am sorry for wasting your time to read my poetry.
