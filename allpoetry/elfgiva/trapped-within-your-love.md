Im trapped in a whirl of darkness and light
Your face is a swirl in the deepest recesses of my soul and mind
Im trapped within the world of your love
Love me
I would give everything 
to stay with you in this ethreal world of fantasy
I Love You so completly your face haunts me 
when I'm away from your embrace
I want to stay just like this life after lifeI know it doesnt make sense
