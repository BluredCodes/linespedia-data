In the throes of utter despair
I see your face everywhere
When I look into the sky the clouds form your face
and the stars spell out your name
I sit alone my heart weepingfor your love
I cry out into the wandering winds 
But I am  unheard 
unimportant
I love you 
dont leave me
here
