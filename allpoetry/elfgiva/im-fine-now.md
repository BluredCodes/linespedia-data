Leave me alone 
Let me be in peace 
I am hurting
you broke my heart
I loved you
Leave me alone
I dont need you 
I want to fly away
from the pain and misery you caused
I came to see the light 
of your betrayal
when you couldnt look into my adoring eyes
why did you lie to me?
Leave me alone
Dont apologize for your shortcomings
I can see them now
Im fine
I know the truth so I can liveI know it doesnt make any sense but i had an idea
