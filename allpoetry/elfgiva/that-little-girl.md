When I look in the mirror 
I see 
That little girl
Who wanted every one to like her
All she wanted was to be popular
Just not to be that little geek who sat in the front of class and raissed her hand for every question
She was miserable

Am I still like that sad little girl
No I am not her I'm different 
I think....

I am happy now being myself
But I still keep the little girl memorys dear
because she is still apart of who I am today

If not for that sad little girl i wouldnt be me.Please dont be to critical on it . It waasnt a poem written for you it was written for me . It is my personal life that you willl be commenting on. And if you dont like it i dont care. Thnis is for me.
