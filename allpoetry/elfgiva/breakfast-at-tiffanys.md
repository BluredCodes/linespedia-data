Can I have breakfast at Tiffany's
Ther is the most wonderful place
I can drink my coffee and not worry about my next paycheck comes from
Or what my life is worth
I just want to have breakfast at Tiffany's 
The world can be forgotton in front of Tiffany's window
I can not worry 
I can be free from doubt
Breafast at Tiffany's
