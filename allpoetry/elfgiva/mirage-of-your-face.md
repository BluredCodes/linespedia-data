Happiness is a mirage 
As I run I cannot catch it
Your beautiful face is nestled in unreality
I still cant believe that you were ever with me
The love and happiness you brought with you only lasted a few days
Maybe hours,weeks who count when one is in love
Then you dissepeared with the sweeping winds
The only things left to say to you are thank you 
and goodbye...Im sorry I just wrote this in a second .
