Lets dream about places around the world
So snuggle in with your quilt
You never know you might find out
Where the Eiffel Tower was actually built.

We start our Journey in Italy
Where the flag is red and white and green
They eat a lot of pizza there
And you can see the tower of Pisa lean.

Its time to go to England now
Where they have a clock they call Big Ben
The Queen lives in a Palace
And the Prime Minister at number Ten.

Now we move to France
To see the Eiffel Tower
A man named Gustave Eiffel designed it
It took a lot of time
And a lot of manpower.

In the United States of America
There is so very much to see
From the West Coast to the East Coast
To the Statue of Liberty.

In Egypt you`ll find the Sphinx
And also the River Nile.
The Pyramids are so amazing
They will surely make you smile.

Fuji is a sacred mountain
That can be found in Japan
The ladies wear Kimonos
And often carry a fan.

Koala Bears and Kangeroos
Boomerangs and more
With the Surf up in Austrailia
The surfrs really score.

Onto to Central America now
Puerto Rico, Antigua and Barbuda
Barbados, Bahamas and Guatemala
Costa Rica, Panama and Cuba.

Mexico has beautiful beaches
Take a holiday in this paradise
Eat some delicious tacos
Or try some Mexican rice.

Venezuela is in South America
Caracas is the capital City
Just north of there is Los Roques
Which is a small island thats extremely pretty.

A Safari is an adventure
Which makes Africa a place you must see
Watch out for lions and Tigers
And also the Chimpanzee.

Now close your eyes and go to sleep
And dream of where you would like to go
And if you choose Austria
Then take your skies becasue there, they have snow.
