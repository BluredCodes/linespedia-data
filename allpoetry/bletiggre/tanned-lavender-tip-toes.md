What might have been....but will never be.For your in love with someone....and someone's in love with me.With mirrored souls....unexpectedly colliding.Deep dark secrets....precariously hiding.A taste of fruits....forbiddingly sweet.Passionate whispers....from web of deciet.What could have been....we might never see.Two are as one....though, neither are free.All too aware....of how much we can lose.Destined paths cross....whether or not we choose.Helpless to resist....the flame of our fire.Tip-toeing cautiously....through tanned, lavendar desire.What should have been....but can never be.Cherished times....forever held in our memories.this ones for you dream hope you enjoy. I actually like this one. though the lavender theme is one i have used before but there is a meaning to it.
