---
title: "Soul's and Hearts"
writer: "Bletiggre"
slug: "souls-and-hearts"

Sadness presses hard .... against my heart
My mind struggles to comprehend this fight
Obviously,  you're more than my best friend
Where did it all start?? Where will it end??

Unknowingly,  your words strike deep inside
Little wounds so fresh,   tears I cannot hide
Is there more to your story than you tell
Your heaven seems so far away, from inside my hell.

What should I have said, could I have done
to make you realize you are my only one?
I adore everything you dream,  everything you are,
My Angel,  My Princess,   My shining star.

I battle each day to push away what I feel
Grasping at fantasies, ignoring others ignorant spiel.
Can you deny the magic that we share??
Can you try to explore what is or is not there??

Soul to Soul,  Heart to Heart,
Once so close together, now, seemingly so far apart.
I Encourage your dreams,  the life we could live
For you .... all of me .... I am willing to give.

My final offer, My final plea,
Please understand, you are everything to me.
I love you more than anyone could dream
and I am not one to say what I do not mean.

So search your heart, search our souls,
You'll probably find,  we share common goals.
To dance through life to the music we make,
to love, to cherish, to receive what we take.

We are not that different .... You and I
both believe that love deserves a try.
Take my hand and walk into the future with me
As best friends and lovers ...Together ..... there is --
a whole new world to see.
