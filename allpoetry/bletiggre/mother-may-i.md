title: "Mother May I"

Mother May I .... take my first stepyou were there to soften the fall.Mother May I .... learn to speakA.B.C.'s, you taught me it all.Mother May I .... succeed at schoolyou were always my favorite teacher.Mother May I .... go to churchyou are my 'GOD' and my preacher.Mother May I .... go to the promyou talked me through my first date.Mother May I .... use the carfor me, you'd always stay up and wait.Mother May I .... go away to collegewe both cried when I finally left home.Mother May I .... call and tell you my fearsyou never let me feel as if I was alone.Mother May I .... become my own manwith your advice I have tasted life.Mother May I .... give you Grandkidsbut be patient, until I find a wife.Mother May I .... be strong and courageousthrough the times when you were ill.Mother May I .... provide you hope and warmth,love and respect you, I always will.Mother May I .... wish you Happy Birthdayand pray you're around another 50 years.Mother May I .... say you are my hero and best friendthrough all the times of happiness and times of tears.
