Of French vanilla flowers,rolling oceans,  emerald green.Delicate, lacy-lavender showers,and rainbow reaching dreams.A gentle, sultry-satin grace,Carefree blue-sky aspirations.Pink cotton-candy taste,and hypnotic conversations.The early morning sunrise,Key West's finest sunsets.Hidden mysteries which surprise,and priceless art that one collects.As strong as Mother Nature's hand,though, Father's little girl.Dreaming of clean white sand,and playing with dolphins in the water.Kaleidoscope colored fantasies,nights of diamond tomorrow's.Angelic soul in which I believe,soothes the demons of my sorrows.descriptive poem about someone for which I once cared. Each line has a meaning which only she could understand. Is there a way to make it flow better or a better use of descriptive words??
