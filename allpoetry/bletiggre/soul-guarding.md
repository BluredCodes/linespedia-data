Tender is one's heart,hiding behind false composure.Tender are one's emotions,swirling around, without closure.Silent is the night,chosen to be spent alone.Silent are the hopes,of someone pretty well known.Guarded is the soul,by a solid stone wall.Guarded are the feelings,which don't allow you to fall.Strong is the desire,to care for and respect.Strong are these arms,wanting to hold and protect.Patient is the man,standing firmly by your side.Patient are the dreams,when our fears will subside.Patient is the clock,for you are worth the time.Patient are the days,'till one day you are mine.this is for you Jaclyn Denise. Patience is a virtue you brought out of me.
