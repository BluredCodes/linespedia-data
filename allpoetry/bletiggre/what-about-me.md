Lying naked beside her
emotions turn cold
I reach out to touch her
as truths start to unfold

Tells a story of her past
the hurt from before
says "we're going to fast"
we just can't be this close anymore

I say:
Chorus # 1
What about me ...
where do I stand
in this mess that's come to be
a decision of choice or demand
What about us...
and the magic we share
or are you in lust.
LIFE JUST ISN'T FAIR!

her words start to blur
as the memories stir

I Remember our laughter
and wiping away your tears
Dreaming of hapilly ever after
conquering our fears

Taking risky chances
traveling roads we could regret
Under the circumstances 
Am i that easy to forget??
AM I THAT EASY TO FORGET??

Chorus #2
And what about me...
where do I stand
why can't you see
I am a special man
What about you...
the person I depend
between the old and the new
between lovers and friends
What about us..
CAN'T WE BE MORE THAN JUST FRIENDS?
what about me

She stops what she's saying
sees the tears in my eyes
holds me close and starts praying
that it will all be alright
What about me..
Chorus# 3
Thought we were special
a delightful surprise
Thought we were special
when you looked in my eyes
What about me...
and all that I feel 
Why can't you see..
I AM HERE AND I AM REAL
What about us
I CAN'T FAKE WHAT I FEEL
repeat chorus # 1 and # 2
