“Anything and Everything”I love you more than anything ---My heart flutters and swirlsMy soul dances and twirlsIt’s hard to keep my feet on the groundWhenever you are around.I want you more than everything ----Your dreams and silent wishesYour tender moist kissesThe happiness your love bringsThe dorky, little, silly thingsI need you more than anything ----Confidence, continuously strongArms in which I belongA love that could have grownYour eyes,------ my heart called home.I miss you more than everything ----When we were loversAbove and under coversThe way it was beforeYou chose to close our door.My heart aches more than anything ---Sorrows I cause myselfIn love with someone elseLonging becoming unhealthyAs I face harsh reality.I am sad more than everything ----Love you too damn muchYour heart, your soul, your touchTrying to be just friendsIs this where it all endsI am sorry more than anything ---I now must say goodbyeCan’t convince my heart to lieShared so many dreamsBut it’s tearing me apart at the seams.I don’t know everything or anything ----I don’t know how to let the feeling goDon’t know how to tell myself noDon’t want to ruin what we shareI will always love youAnd in my heart ------ you will always be there.
