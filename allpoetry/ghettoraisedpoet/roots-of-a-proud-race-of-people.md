THROUGHOUT HISTORY
MY PEOPLE HAVE BEEN INFLUENTUAL TEACHERS,PHILOSOPHERS AND INVENTORS
AND PEOPLE WHO HAVE FOUGHT FOR WHAT THEY BELIEVED IN
NO MATTER HOW HARD OR TRIBULANT SEEMED THEIR VENTURES

HARRIET TUBMAN WAS A WOMAN
OF TRUTH COURAGE AND STRONG CONVICTION
SHE FOUGHT FOR FREEDOM AND JUSTICE FOR HER PEOPLE
IN TIMES WHEN WE WERE KEPT DOWN AND BINDED
SORROWFULLY BOUND IN A GREAT DEAL OF RESRTICTION

MALCOLM X
IN THE 1950'S AND 60'S HELD
HELD THE HOPES AND DREAMS OF AN ENTIRE RACE IN THE PALM OF HIS HAND
AND SO HE TAUGHT US IF WE ARE BEING UNJUSTLY TRIED
TO STAND ON OUR OWN TWO FEET AND MAKE A RIGHTOUS STAND

MARTIN LUTHER KING JR.,
A MAN WHO PREACHEDTHAT NO MATTER WHAT CREED OR SKIN COLOR WE ARE
STILL INSIDE WE REMAIN THE SAME
AND BECAUSE OF A DREAM AND HIS FAITH IN GOD
HE WORKED TIRELESSLY
AND BELIVED THAT ALL HIS WORK WOULD NEVER GO IN VEIN

SO HERE WE ARE IN THE PRESENT
AND BECAUSE OF THESE ICONS AND MANY MORE
MANY OF OUR DREAMS WE ARE FINALLY ABLE TO MAKE COME TRUE
AND SO WE SHOULD NEVER FORGET 
THE DOORS OF HOPE AND OPPURTUNITY THEY OPENED THAT WAS CLOSED FOR THEM
SO THAT NOW IN TODAY'S TIME WE WOULD BE ABLE TO WALK THROUGH
