---
title: "Timeless Love"
writer: "GhettoRaisedPoet"

If you asked me to stop breathing
I would gladly stop my heart from beating
At this very second in this world your love is all I care about
And if it took me laying down my life to save yours 
I would hurrily meet my demise for you without a doubt

We started as childhood companions
Whenever you saw one the other wasn't far behind
Then as our bodies grew and we grew older
We found that throughout all our adventures as children
True Love was our greatest find

We shared our first kiss together
On that warm summer's night as we lay beside the lake
And when our lips parted and we stared into each other's eyes
We knew that what we had felt all along wasn't fake

The day we were married
Was like none other I'll ever forget and will forever remember
Because the moment you said I do
My heart felt an overwhelming tremor

We had our first child together
And nothing in this world felt better
When I held my son for the first time
I knew that we had a bond so deep
That no creature on this earth could break or sever

So now we sit here still together after so much time has passed
Knowing that it was all worth it
Never letting it cross our minds that this was ever wrong
Because since the tender age of five
We have sung Love's Timeless Song
