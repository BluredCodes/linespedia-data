---
title: "Dream Chaser"
writer: "GhettoRaisedPoet"

Alone I stand against the world
Knowing that I must survive on my own
That as of now a new day has begun
And the time to fulfill my destiny has come

There's no turning back now
No time to become a coward and quit
I've wanted to be here for so long
And now it's time to see how bad I really want this

The prize is mine and mine alone 
And I will be the one to claim it
This is my time and no one else's 
To finally become what I was born to be

My mind is clear and focused
I'm willing to knock down and destroy anything that stands in my path
And if I lose trying to chase my dream
At least I'll know in my heart 
That I gave it all I could
