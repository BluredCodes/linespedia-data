My Love,I wish to make a promise to you
One that will last forever
It's a vow to love you and you only 
And to form a bond of love between us
That will never be made to sever

I loved you from my eyes first sight
And I prayed that this moment would one day come
The day that we would become one for all times
Under a beautiful setting sun

All my possessions in this world 
I would gladly lay at your feet
Because an existence without you
Would only cause me to forever cry and weep

So as I kneel here at your feet
I will ask you to be my wife
As I await your answer and wonder
If you will allow me to hold your heart forever
Until both our souls go asunder
