I admire those who have passed on before me
Because their existence is better than that of my own
They are in a much better and joyous world
While I remain here on this earth 
Feeling hopelessly alone

What is my true role 
In this never ending play we have come to call Life
The evil prosper and grow richer by the minute
While the good continue on in their everyday struggles and strife

Love contiuously flies by my door
And never seemd to acknowledge that I'm home
While a sence of belonging in this world
I hungrily search for
As I wander about and roam
