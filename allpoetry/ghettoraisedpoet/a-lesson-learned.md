I asked my mother one day
Why are we made to suffer so?
And she looked at me with tenderness in her eyes and said
Son without some pain in our lives
How would we ever grow?

So I then asked her
Mother why is growth so important 
And what is it meant to to?
Then she looked at me with those same loving eyes and said
Son only God can answer that question for you

So she took me by the hand outside
And pointed in the sky for me to see
Then she said just like the birds in the sky my son
You and I will live amongst the clouds in heaven one day
Ever so free

So my mother looked down upon my young face
And asked if I understood
Then I said mother if we are all made to walk
Among the clouds one day
Why do some of us act as if inside us 
We have nothing that is Pure and Good

At that moment
My mother's tears began to flow
And then she held me close and said
Son,a lot of people's pain
They have yet to learn to let go

So as she held me closer
She kissed me gently on my forehead and foretold
That,Son as you travel through life
All of your questions will be answered
As you learn and grow old
