title: "A Lover's Pain"
writer: "GhettoRaisedPoet"

I sometimes lay awake at night and wonder
If true love is really meant to happen to me
Or will my lonliness and despair 
Ulitimately turn out to be my destiny?

As I have traveled through my life
I have met many people whose hearts are as pure as gold
But it's unlucky for me
That their hearts belong to someone else
And yet I have none of my own to hold

I sometimes dreams if
I have already met my soulmate
And not known that it was true love that I just missed
Because all of the times I thought I was in love and was not
Are to great and far to many to list

A friend of mine once said That the Heart i slike an open book
Each with a story to tell and with a critical ending to surely follow
All you have to do is not tear out the pages
Or it will be you and not the book
Whose insides will be left empty and hollow
