title: "A Confused Soul's Prayer"
writer: "GhettoRaisedPoet"

God,
Can you see us?
Your sinful children here on earth?
Wreaking havoc and hurting each other
As if to one another
We do not even have a penny's worth

God,
Satan is in full work around us
And he needs to be stopped with the swiftest of speed
He's caused so much hurt and sorrow
That's why I'm praying to you
In hopes that my plea for help you will graciously heed

God,
Sometimes I sit and wonder
Why you even created us at all
The evil continue to grow rich and stronger
While the good one by one
Continue to drop and fall

God,
We need you now more than ever
In this tragic and trying time
Nothing on this earth can heal our pain and scars
Except for your grace that is forever endless and divine
