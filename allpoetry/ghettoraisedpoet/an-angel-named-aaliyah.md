title: "An Angel Named Aaliyah"

I will begin to speak from my heart
Because words from my mouth at this time fail me to express
About how I came to know and love and angel
That is with God now and forever
And who's spirit from now on we will continue to bless

Aaliyah,Our hearts broke 
When we found out our angel fell from the sky
They say that fate determines what happens to us
But it seems now all we can say is WHY?

We loved everything about you
From your slanted hair over your mysterious eyes
To your loving heart and beautiful smile
You taught me to dust myself off and try again if I fail
Because in the end it will all be worthwhile

But I believe God placed you here for a reason
And when you accomplished your goal he called you home
God Bless You Aaliyah
Because you are in safe hands forever
Next to God's Eternal Throne
