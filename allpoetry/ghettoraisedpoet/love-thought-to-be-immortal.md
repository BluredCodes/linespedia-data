title: "Love Thought To Be Immortal"

Fate became the Thief of my HappinessFor now my heart has lost the will to continue on livingThey say time heals all woundsBut for now this act remains UnforgivenThe body I held close to me day in and day outSeems to me now to have only been a vessel holding my one and true love in it's portBut the dream we had of one day sailing off togetherSeems to have now been prematurely cut shortYour eyes were once what my heart's lightRose and set upon by every glimmer shone therefore inside themBut it appears now that the pain of never gazing into them againWill forever prick holes in my heart with thornsJust like those upon a rose's stemPeople say when we are born we should cryAnd when we pass on we should be happy and rejoiceBut what do you do when the ability to love you were born with inside you dies?And you will never again be able to hear it's voice
