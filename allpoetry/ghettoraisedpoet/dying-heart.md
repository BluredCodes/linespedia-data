Broken And Dying
Never able to function the same as before
Busted open and bleeding heavily
All of what was once True Love onto the wide open floor

At one perfect moment in time
Vivarent and Full Of Life
Compassion seeming to flow though every vein and artery
But now Love's Door has closed by Hurt and Pain
And has been shackled by it's feet
Never able to again be free

Flatlining has occured
And it has now passed on
As many other's before it have done just the same'
Sunshine and Joy once present there
But now all that remains is Dark Clouds and Rain

What was once thought to be Unbreakable
Has now been proven to be just that of a mere joke
And has been thrown into its eternal grave
Because Pain laid it's hands upon Love's neck
And never let go of it's Unrelenting Choke
