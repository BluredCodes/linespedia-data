title: "They call me Beast"
writer: "GhettoRaisedPoet"

I appear to the world around me
As just a burden to all their lives
All men who lay eyes upon me wish me dead
Including women and their every child

I live in the shadows and corners
Because from society I am seen as an outcast
When I brush against so called 'Perfect People'
Rapidly they wash their skin
As if I was some sort of terrible disease or rash

These 'Perfect People' around me
Have labeled me a freak of nature
And just an outright beast
Because my pain they love so much to feed upon
As if it were some gigantic feast

Why don't they realize 
That in many ways I'm just like them
Except for having the hate in my soul
To look upon something differen from me
And to a lifetime of hurt
So easily upon it's head condemn
