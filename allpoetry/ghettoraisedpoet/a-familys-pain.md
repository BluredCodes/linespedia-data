title: "A Family's Pain"
writer: "GhettoRaisedPoet"

Where's my father?
He's nowhere to be found
We haven't heard from him in two days 
Since he struck my mother
And left her laying on the ground

The rent's two months past due
And my father isn't anywhere around
He's probably out getting high
In some alley halfway across town

The refrigerator is empty
Momma dosen't know what to do
Because daddy has stolen the grocery money 
And now momma is crying
Wishing she had never uttered the words I do

Sometime's I wonder what me as his seed could have done wrong
Is there something wrong that i did or said?
To make my father not wanna be with his wife and child
And make momma wish that she was dead 

This situation I see all to often
But hopefully one day this sad truth will turn into a memory
Of something never to happen or come about again
And only love and caring for theie familes to experience and see
