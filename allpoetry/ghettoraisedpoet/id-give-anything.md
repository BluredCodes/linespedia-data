I'd Give Anything.....

To wake up in the morning of the one who unconditionally loves me
To feel them breathe a sigh of relief because they are as happy as I am
To finally know what Real Love is
To Be able to look in to my love's eyes and soul and to know that it's all worth it
To love with reckless abandon 
To give up everything I hold dear for a chance at a future filled with passion
To be able to hold her hand and through her fingertips hear her heart beat
To know that to the world  I may be just a person
But to that person I may be their world
To look into the future and see if any of these things are to ever come to be
Amd most of all I'd give anything 
To finally be in love everlasting
Instead of being of just a fool for Love's cruel games
