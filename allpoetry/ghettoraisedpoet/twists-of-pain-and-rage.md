My Life is like a tornado.
With all it's spinning and turning.
My thoughts rage on like a fire.
That never ceases from burning.

My soul sometimes feels like it's trapped.
Between heaven and hell.
It's filled with so much chaos and fury.
That where it will end up I can't tell.

My spirit feels bound.
Like a bird without wings.
Because the world I live in seems to have it wrapped up
In all the pain and agony that it brings.

So I'm stuck in today.
And don't know if I'll see tommorrow.
How will I know what to do when it comes?
By listening to the things 
That my heart says I should follow.


Shun Justin Williams
