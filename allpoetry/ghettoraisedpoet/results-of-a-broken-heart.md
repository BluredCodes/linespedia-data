THE SECOND YOU UTTERED THE WORD GOODBYEMY HEART CEASED TO EXISTIT SEEMED TO HALT TO ALL SHOCK AND PAINTHE LOVE I THOUGHT WE SHARED WAS LIKE NONE OTHER RECORDED THROUGHOUT HISTORYAND AT THIS VERY MOMENTIF I'LL EVER RECOVER FROM THIS TRAGEDY REMAINS TO ME A MYSTERYMY SOUL APPEARS TO HAVE LOST TOUCHWITH EVERYTHING JOYOUS THAT HAS EVER INHABITED MY BEINGAND IT'S INEVITABLE DEATH AND DEMISE IS ALL THAT I AM FOR SEEINGTHROUGHOUT MY BLOOD LINE AND VEINSALL THAT WAS EVER PRESENT WAS TRACINGS OF LOVE,JOY AND HAPPINESSBUT NOW THAT YOU HAVE LEFT MY HEART TO SURVIVE ON IT'S OWNALL THAT INHABITS MY SOUL IS PAIN,LONELINESS AND TORMENTTHE WAY YOU MADE ME FEELI SWORE TO MYSELF ONCE WAS HEAVEN SENTBUT IT'S APPARENT NOW THAT I LOVED YOU MORE THAN YOU DID METO A FAR DEEPER AND FAR GREATER EXTENTA BREACH OF LOVE AND TRUSTIS WHAT WAS VIOLATED HEREBECAUSE FROM NOW UNTIL THE END OF TIMEI HAVE LOST ALL THAT I HAVE EVER HELD DEAR
