Have you ever loved to the point where  waking up next to the one you love just to hear themm breathe would give you a piece and joy like no other?

Love is when no matter what you would lay down your life for the one you love without doubt and without question.

Passion is an undying wanting and needing for a love so deep that it shakes the foundation of your very soul.

True and Pure Love is when you lose the one you love and even though they aren;t with you all you want is for them to be happy.

True Pain is realized when you give your heart and soul to someone and they play with them and toss them aside as if they were a toy that a child had gotten bored with.

Absolute Joy is when the one you love tells you for the first time I love you and your heart feels as if it could take wings and fly.

Ultimate and Undying Passion is achieved when you have found the one for whom you love unconditionally and wish to spend the rest of your life with basking in each other's essence.

These are The Emotions Of Love.
