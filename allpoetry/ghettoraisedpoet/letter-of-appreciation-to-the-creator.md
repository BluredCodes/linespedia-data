Lord,
I am your child
One not without sin or fault
So that is why I turn to you
When my Strength and Love
Have seemed to come to a crashing halt

I know I have not always served you
In a way that is righteous and true
But I'd like to ask for your forgivness
And to say Thank You
For guiding me through all the pain
That I have had to endure

You're Grace and Mercy
Are like no other
And is rivaled by no other being
That is why I am overjoyed you have remained
Here with me in times good and bad
When from my problems I have felt like fleeing

So Lord I praise you
Until another breath my body is unable to make
And will continue to come to you and you only
In times where Life's trials and tribulations
Have become to unbearable for me to take

Amen
