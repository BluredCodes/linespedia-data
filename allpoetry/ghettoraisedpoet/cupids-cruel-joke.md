title: "Cupid's Cruel Joke"

Cupid played a cruel joke on me
He led me to believe that my loved loved me in return
But all he really did was stab me right in the core of my heart
As he maniacally laughed and said Fool next time you'll learn

Cupid played me for a fool
But in love aren't fools the one's who are supposed to win?
Instead of his love being a blessing and heaven sent
What he did should be added as one of the world's deadliest sins

Cupid loved to play games with me
And he mocked my pain as he had target practice upon my heart
While he looked at my soul to paint with the blood of my heart
As if it were already some mangled piece of art

Cupid was supposed to strike you when you least expected it
And Boy did he catch me offguard!!
But I never thought when Cupid came to visit me
That his price for love with be so diffucult to pay and so hard
