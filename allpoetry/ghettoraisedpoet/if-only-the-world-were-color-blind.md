Ignorance and Hate 
Why do some human beings covet these things so?
They are passed down from one generation to the next.
And the Chain Of Close-Mindedness
They will never seem to let it go.

Do you think our children are born to hate
Something that is so different from them
But in so many ways just the same?
No I think it is the parents that instill in that child's mind
That mixing of different colors is a deadly sin
And anything different from them
Should be made to return back
To the place from which it first orginated and came.

If we are pricked or cut
Do we not bleed just the same and the color exactley alike?
But it is the thought of having to accept this truth 
That give most humans a terrible fright.

If only the world were color blind.
People could finally open their eyes up and see.
That the world would be a more joyous place to live in.
If we embraced all colors and creeds 
And became the family that God invisioned intended for us to be.
