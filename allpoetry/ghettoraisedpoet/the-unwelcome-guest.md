Depression has become a companion of mineFor longer than I can rememberIt saddens my heart and confuses my mindAnd doesn't realize nor does it care that it's presence is not at all welcomeIt first showed up on my doorstepBack in my younger days when I was a little more than an infantBut now I am a manAnd this unfeeling guest has decided to remain with me for all timesIt brought into my home Confusion and DoubtAnd a no longer wanting to remain here on earth It whispers in my earBe rid of yourself!!Because no one loves you now and no one ever willLeast of all me
