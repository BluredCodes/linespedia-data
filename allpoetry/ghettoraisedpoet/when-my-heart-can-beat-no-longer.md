When my heart can beat no moreAnd all strings holding it together have been cut and severedI will be prepared to meet my final demiseAnd whatever  beyond Deaths never ending reachI look into the sky above my headAnd wish for that existence before my time Why the feelings have come upon meI have yet to find a reason or rhymeLove is absent from the place I have reserved for it inside my heartMaybe it was never meant for me to have in the first placeBecause even in my life it has never playe a strong partMaybe beyond this lifeIs a place made out just for meBut the answer to that question I will have to await God to find out and see
