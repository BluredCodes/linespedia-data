As I sit here alone in silence
I think to myself and wonder
Why have you left me with a broken Heart and Soul
And a spirit that's like a sinking ship going under

I entrusted to you my secrets and dreams
And you swore to me you's never fail
That each and every day
The words I Love You
You'd never forget to me to tell


But now it seems apparent
And I can't believe it's happened again
That another person has violated my Loyalty and Trust
And once more commited this unholy sin

The crime of being unfaithful
The sin of being untrue
But the only wrong I think I've become guilty of
Is the Unconditional Love of you
