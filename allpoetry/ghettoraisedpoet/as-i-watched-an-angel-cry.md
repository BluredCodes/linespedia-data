AS I WATCHED THE ANGEL CRY
I COULD SEE THE PAIN ETCHED ACROSS HER FACE
AND HOW HER PRECIOUS TEARS FELL TO THE GROUND
AT SEEING HOW THE WORLD HAD BECOME SUCH A HATEFUL AND SINFUL PLACE

I CONTINUED TO WATCH AND GAZE
AS THIS MAJESTIC FIGURE'S WINGS STARTED TO LOSE THEIR ONCE PROUD SHAPE AND LOOK
IT SADDENED MY HEART SO
BECAUSE I HAD NEVER HEARD OF AN ANGEL CRYING
NOT EVEN IN THE GOOD AND HOLY BOOK

HER ONCE BEAUTIFUL FACE OF GOLD
WAS NOW A CRIMSON MASS OF RED
I WANTED TO RUN AND CRY OUT TO HER
BUT MY BODY COULD NOT MOVE
NOR MY MOUTH FORM ANY WORDS TO BE UTTERED OR SAID

THEN I SAW HER FALL TO HER KNEES 
AS THE EARTH TREMBLED UNDER HER FEET
IT WAS A SIGHT TO BEHOLD
AT HOW ONE BEING'S LOVE COULD RUN SO DEEP

THEN SHE COLLAPSED TO THE GROUND
AND WHEN SHE TOOK HER FINAL BREATH
I REALIZED WHAT I HAD FEARED FROM THE VERY START
THAT THE SINFUL CREATURES OF EARTH
HAD KILLED ONE OF GOD'S MOST PRECIOUS CREATIONS WITH THE DEADLIEST WEAPON OF ALL
A BROKEN HEART
