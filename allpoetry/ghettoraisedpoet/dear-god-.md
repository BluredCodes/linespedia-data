Dear God,
I am one of many of your lost children
Wandering like a nomad from place to place
Never really knowing where I can call home                   
So that is why I am seeking your guidance and grace

Dear Lord,
There are times when my heart aches so bad
Because of loneliness and pain
But I know that if I trust in you
Then my heart will definitely outlast the Devil's Evil Rein

Oh God,
I sometimes am plagued with a fear
Of not knowing what great tradgedy tommorow will bring
But you have instructed me to trust in you
Because peace you will bestow upon my soul
And provide a wonderful song for my heart to sing

Lastly God,
As I take a deep breath
And turn out the lights and light my candle
I am calmed by your words that say
I will never put more on you my child than you can handle

Amen
