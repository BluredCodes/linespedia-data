title: "Trials of my life"

MY WORLD IS A SERIES
OF TWISTED CORNERS AND MAZES
MY MEMORIES ARE FILLED WITH
BROKEN PROMISES AND MISSING FACES

MY PAST IS CLUTTERED
WITH MANY A MISSING PART
SOMEDAY I HOPE TO FEEL THESE VOIDS
BECAUSE IT OFTEN FEELS AS IF THEY ARE EATING AWAY AT MY HEART

I FEEL A CALM COOL BREEZE
BRUSH AGAINST MY FACE
AND WONDER WHY MY LIFE CANNOT BE AS GENTLE AND CALM
AS THE EVER PRESENT WIND
INSTEAD OF DANGEROUSLY CRASHING AT SUCH AN ALARMING PACE

SO I LOOK AT THE SUNRISE
AND I KNOW A NEW DAY HAS BEGUN
ANOTHER CHAPTER IN MY LIFE HAS STARTED
AND ANOTHER BATTLE HAS STARTED TO BE WON
