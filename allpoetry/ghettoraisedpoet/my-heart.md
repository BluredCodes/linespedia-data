MY HEART IS A PLACEWHERE ONLY PURE LOVE CAN LIVEAND ONE DAY I HOPE TO FIND MY ONE TRUE LOVETO WHICH ALL OF THE SECRETS OF MY HEART I WILL GIVEMY HEART LOVES UNCONDITIONALLYAND NOT MANY PEOPLE CAN UNDERSTAND AND SEETHAT THE PASSION THAT RUNS THROUGH THE CORE OF ITWOULD BE A WONDER FOR ALL OF THE WORLD TO SEEMY HEARTS IS FILLED WITH HONESTYAND NEVER WANTS TO BE TOLD A LIEBECAUSE WHEN MY HEART FEELS DECEIVEDALL IT CAN DO IS BREAK OPEN AND CRYMY HEART WILL GO ON FOREVERAND I HOPE THE LOVE INSIDE IT WILL NEVER DIEBECAUSE THE DAY IT CEASES TO FEEL LOVE AND JOYI'LL FINALLY KNOW THAT IT IS MY TIME TO DIE
