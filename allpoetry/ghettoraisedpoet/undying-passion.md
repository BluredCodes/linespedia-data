Love was once my steadfast companionIn the days before tragedy struckNow misery has taken its placeAs my heart is now in a terrible and lonesome rutWe all know that death will eventually befall each one of usBut what I fail to understandIs why you before me?I always thought that we would drift into the great beyond togetherInto a ppassionateand blissful eternityMy Love,you were a friend and ccounsler to allAnd to every problem you had an answerUntil a walnut sized problem started growing in your headThat was the result of a deadly cancerThe doctor looked you and me in the eyeAnd said the news  am about to give is all but goodAnd said it's to far along and there's nothing more we can doBecause we've done all earthly possible that we couldFour months went swiftly byAs your eyes grew weary and your body rackked with painThe only thing that gave you peaceWas the gentle tapping on  the window of the Autumn RainMy heart ached To see my angel so jaded slipping away from me with every breathBut I vowed to remain by your side faithfullyUntil a breath in my own body I was unabe to takeThen one eveningAs the tempreture was frigid outsideYour fever inside was buildingAs you gasped for breath sweating largelyWith your eyes bulging wideI quickly called the paramedicsWithout a singl moment's wasteAnd as I rode to the hospital with youI could tell on yur face that the pain was becoming to unbearable for you to takeWe when arrivedTwo hours I worrily waitedUntil the doctor walked in and saidTen minutes go into shock from coughing up her own blood she slippedAnd out of this world she fadedI went into a stag of shock myselfAs my heart broke there on the spotAnd as a nurse wiped my tearsShe said she was lucky because you obviusly loved her alotSome time has passed nowSince I with great sorrow in my heartLaid my beloved to her final restBut the only thing that makes me smile and gives my heat solaceIs knowing that I passed True Love's Test
