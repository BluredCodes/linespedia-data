title: "Where do I go from here"
writer: "GhettoRaisedPoet"

Your arms were once a safe haven to me
But now that shelter is gone
An now that my heart has lost it's home in your eyes
A drastic change for the worst it has undergone

The bed we made love in 
Was once a volcano of passion
But now that I wake up on this cold surface alone
I search for traces of your smile in the smallest ration

I look into the mirror
And imagine the person I used to be
I was once at the height of love's greatest peak
But now my heart wails endlessly like a waling banshee

I can no longer bring myself to eat
For my soul has lost the will to live
Another day I do not care to see
Because to another being 
I can never give my heart to them eternally
