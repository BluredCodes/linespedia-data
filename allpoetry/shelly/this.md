it’s so simple to think that nothing exists but
	this moment
	this smile
	these eyes

	so easy to imagine nothing better than this, that
	this moment
	this smile
	these eyes
	are the reason that 
	I am here to begin with.

	How could I even contemplate anyone else?
	
	This moment
	This smile
	These kind blue eyes
	
	are the reason 
	for me, and the birthplace of my own
	warmth
	laughter
	vision.
	
	This is what I hold in my hands--
	and it’s all that I need.
