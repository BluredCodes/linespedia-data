title: "Images of pain"
writer: "me e.m"

My mind is working as quickly as ever
rational thoughts speed through my head like a runaway train.
I think about the future and the past however the present 
evades my consciousness as if it is a dangerous predator.

My emotions are blocked off by an invisible barrier that 
pain has built up over time, to keep my feelings out.
A sharp numbness takes over my body, then suddenly a burning
sensation in my throat can only mean.....

My barricade is not yet strong enough to keep those pesistant
feelings out, the pain swells in my chest like a heavy weight.
It then rushes out spewing tears and choking sobs from the deep
chasms of my heart and soul.

My thoughts alternate from memories and images, imprinted
in my brain to questions that i know are unanserable.
I am angered by my narrow perspective as i selfishly think of the
implications this will have on me, rather than the needs of others.

My body aches for he who is gone but it also bears the sorrow of 
those who are still here, who must live on without him.
Then a calm comes over me like an invisible spirit who provides lyrics 
for my song and simply and truly i believe he is safe. 

The pain relinquishes its power as i feel peace take contol.i am coming to terms with the death of a loved one, i recommend writing poety for anyone who is going through what i am - it is good therapy.
