How many times have i asked
what is the answer?

For i am a craving soul with a vicious appetite.

How many different answers
have i heard?

For i am a yearning ear with selective hearing.


How many times have i
thought i'd found the answer?

For i am a enslaved tongue hoping to tell the world.

How many times ........
has the question been asked?
How many times ........
has the answer been found?

Infinite times, just enough to discover 
that the answer.....................
has forgotten the question!all opinions r welcomed
