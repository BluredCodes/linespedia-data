---
title: "You make me wanna spew"
writer: "me e.m"
slug: "you-make-me-wanna-spew"

What i thought was so inspiring
has left me inquiring,

What the fuck did i see in that existance?
now all i know is my resistance.

Look at you all standing there
nice clothes and fancy hair.

Yeah maybe it would be pretty cool
to be so popular that the boys all drool.

Now i know i don't wanna be like you
you are so obviously fake that i wanna spew.
