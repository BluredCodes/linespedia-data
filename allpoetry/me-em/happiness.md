title: "Happiness??????"
writer: "me e.m"

Anger, hatred, jealousy, depression
it grows like cancer 
eventually all consuming
still you wonder why you're not happy.

Maybe happiness is just accepting 
what you have and choosing to enjoy life,
disregarding the expectaions and limits
imposed on you by yourself and others.
