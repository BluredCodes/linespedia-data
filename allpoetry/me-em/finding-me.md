---
title: "Finding me"
writer: "me e.m"

Hey i feel like writing 
expressing what i'm fighting, 
coz i feel so surreal 
like a bananna without its peel. 
Like whats my prob? 
i dunno, 
i feel so outa control 
like i'm being carried by a wave 
to a destiny i can't pave. 
I try, you know to live life good 
but maybe i'm trying to be someone else and not who i should. 
But it's really hard when you just don't know 
who the hell you are and how far you should go. 
I want some things i've never had before so bad 
everyone keeps telling me to be myself otherwise i'll get mad. 
What i'm trying to say is; i wanna live without expectation, 
to strive for things that come from the soul 
not from some tv star who has never had a mole. 
How do i know if my dreams and desires are true? 
How do i know if i'm just trying to look good for you? 
The only answer that i can find 
lies deep within my heart, soul and mind. 
I'll search the length of the earth until i can see
why the hell i'm here and who i must be,
i might be happy when i discover..... 
ME!
