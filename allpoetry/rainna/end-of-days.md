The End of Days has shed its light,
But as it dawned I felt no fright.
For the grace of God has saved us all,
Satan again has seen the fall.
No fear I felt as the clock tolled the hour,
For with strength our Father did us shower.
And so the day of doom has passed,
Another thousand years we shall last.
So we give thanks to the Father above,
Worshiping him with our faith, trust, and love.
