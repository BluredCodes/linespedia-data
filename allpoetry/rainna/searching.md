Every day of my life I feel a certain emptiness,
A black void inside me that I don't know how to fill.
I search for whatever it is; longing to find it, 
     And search 'til I tire.
I rest myself, then set forth again.
I travel through strange and mysterious places, sailing treacherous seas.
I climb snow-capped mountains and brave killer bees.
I stop to eat and play awhile, then set out again,
     Down the majestic Nile.
I stop to gaze into a little pond,
While my mind wanders to things beyond.
Then something catches my eye.
     I look and there it is!
The center of my searches, the reason for my travels.
I've found it, the reason I live!
