Where are you going? Where might life lead you?
Before you go, I just want to say I'll miss you.
All these days we've had together,
Throughout my life I will treasure.
Through the good times and the bad,
You're the best friend I've ever had.
Well you and I have done our talking,
But now you better get to walking.
There are new opportunites for you ahead,
And I don't want to find you lazing in bed!
So go on. Get out there. Make a good life.
Please do not become a docile housewife.
So, so long, farewell, adieu,
Goodbye, goodluck, I'll miss you.This was written for my friend, Dorothy, who was graduating and leaving for college soon. She helped shape who I am and she saved my butt too many times to count. I hope you like this poem as much as she did.
