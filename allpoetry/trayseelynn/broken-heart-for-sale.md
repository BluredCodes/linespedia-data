One Broken Heart For Sale
needs to be glued or mended
signs of love were found but no longer remain
some scaring, tears, cracks and slight dust
looks as though a few stitches would fix it
previous owners' now seperated
was once guarded by chains and walls
to a good home, warm hands and love -a must
sitting on floor to be picked up ASAP
Thank You!

WANTED: Unbroken Heart!
no pieces or parts please
must be whole, without hurt
scars or wounds
owner will take very good care of it
will be guarded by chains and walls
please contact the princess locked in the tower.
if you can't find her, look for her note
found on the floor next to her broken heart.
"Happily ever after"'s need not apply!
