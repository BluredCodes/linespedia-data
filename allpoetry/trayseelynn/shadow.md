---
title: "Shadow"
writer: "trayseelynn"
slug: "shadow"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/39141-Shadow-by-trayseelynn"
---

the shadows that surround me
they sound a lot like you
they tell me that they love me
and i have to believe its true

the shadows, they engulf me
my heart, they want to win
the darkness mist around me
i take them, breathe them in

the shadows that surround me
thier scent calls my heart
they know i can't ignore it
even though that we're apart

the shadows, they keep calling
they draw me closer in
they try to take your place
but i won't let them win

the shadows keep me lonely
they know i can not fight
they know i miss you more and more
especially at night

the shadows love this darkness
where you use to be
the place i held you closest
is now a memory
