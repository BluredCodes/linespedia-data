---
title: "Hand Of A Friend"
writer: "trayseelynn"
slug: "hand-of-a-friend"
source: "AllPoetry"

You understand my heart,
You know me inside and out
You listen to my non-sense
You know what I'm all about
I can not lie to you
I can not keep things hidden
But I know when I make mistakes
I'll always be forgiven
I hold you near me always
I think of you so dearly
I know not matter where I am
Your presence is always with me
You're the one person in my life
That never lets me down
I can't think of anyone else
I'd rather be around
When things in my world shatter and fall
And everything seems so dark and grey
I know that you'll be there to catch me
All I have to do is get on my knees and pray.
I come to you in good times or bad
WHen I feel like I just need a friend
Thank you Lord for every minute
Cause all you do is just reach out your hand.
