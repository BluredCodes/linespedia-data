Sitting here waisting away
I feel like my life is over.
These four walls are closing in
And my life is getting tougher
When you were by my side
I felt like I could take on the world
But hang on, my love, life's a bumpy ride
I can't get you out of my dreams
and your memories still on my mind.
Life's not as bad as it seems
'Cause you know I have nothing to hide
As long as you know I love you
I'll cry at night holding on
No more will I fight this misery
I know I've got to be stong.
Your memory keeps haunting me
I know I did you no harm
Sitting here I feel like a stranger
And you just don't know me at all
I've tried to explain how my heart feels
Can't you see by my face that this love is for real?
Don't know know I love you so,
Now I ask just one more time, why the hell did you go?
Can't you believe in us one more time?
They say the third time's a charm
Enough is enough with these childish rhymns
What I am trying to say is... I love you, honestly.
