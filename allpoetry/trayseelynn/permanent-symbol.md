title: "Permanent Symbol"

outlined in black and filled in with color
the lines are continuously connected

the wings spread apart wanting a hug
gazing into them i see a sunset

the colors of violet, pink and yellow
fade into eachother like the cold into the wind

a bit of excitment filled me
i was very anxious to see the picture

i looked in the mirror and the colors didn't show
a red raised place where once there was peach skin

applying some love and compassion
i made it love me and show me itself

like a face that has been crying finaly turned a smile
the beauty shined through from the darkened scabs

as i look at my beautiful butterfly i will remember
a friend who was there for me when i needed her

a permanent symbol of friendship that will last forever
