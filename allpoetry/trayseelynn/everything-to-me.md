---
title: "Everything To Me"
writer: "trayseelynn"

just when i thought 
everything was complete

everything is broken
makes me lifeless, makes me weak

just when i thought
we would last forever

i awake from this dream
and we are no longer together

why did it have to end this way
i will probably never know

this hurt and emptiness inside me
will make me stronger as i go

i'll miss you and i'll love you
inside, you know i will

although it wasn't meant to be
my love for you was real

so learn from this as i did
just give your heart time to see

that the love you have inside you
meant everything to me
