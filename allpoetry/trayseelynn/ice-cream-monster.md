title: "Ice Cream Monster"

I look upon my spoon so nice and shiney
I'm thinking "OH NO! MORE FAT ON MY HINEY"
I'm dipping, diving, into this gallon container
If I eat this whole thing, getting fatter... no brainer

God this is tempting, almost half is eaten
This ice cream I'm downing, it has me beaten
It's so creamy cold, I can't stop at all
But if I do, Into this chocolate I'll fall

Whipped cream and syrup, is good as a topping
God, I'm gonna hurl, so the floor I'll be mopping
Stop me before I eat the whole thing now
God, I'm so fat, I look like a cow

Chocolate Chip Mint, Mocha Fudge Coffee
Top it with nuts, whipped cream, and toffee
I don't really care what kind I've got
I love ice cream, with fudge that is hot

Gosh, I'm getting brain freeze again
Is eating this much ice cream considered a sin?
Ice cream monster, he has gotten to me
It's making me eat it, HELP ME, SET ME FREE

Please take this cold spoon out of my hand
My fingers are frozen, so hurry if you can
Frostbite has set in, I'm losing this battle
Set me out in the pasture with the rest of the cattle

Just don't let me see, the deep freezer again
Because I know the Ice Cream Monster will win!I got the munchies... H E L P  M E !!!!
