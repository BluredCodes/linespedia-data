i am a person, 
who needs a person, 
to be a person.

i am a heart, 
who needs a heart, 
to make it beat.

i am a soul, 
who needs a soul, 
to make it whole.

i am a lover, 
who needs a lover, 
to be loved.

i am a friend, 
who needs a friend, 
to be there.

i am a dreamer, 
who needs a dreamer, 
to dream about.

i am a thinker, 
who needs a thinker, 
to share my thoughts.

i am one, 
who needs one, 
to make us one.

i am an individual, 
who needs an individual, 
to be undivided.

i am a soulmate, 
who needs a soulmate, 
to be a soulmate.

i am me, 
and you are you, 
together... we could be "us"

because i am a person, 
who needs a person, 
to be a person...

and without you, 
i just wouldn't be me....
