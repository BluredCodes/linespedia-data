title: "When Love Was Lost...."
writer: "trayseelynn"

When Love was lost, I had to find it.
Missing pieces of my heart, trying to bind it.
When Love was lost, I was very hurt.
Hurt from the emptiness, being tossed in the dirt.
When Love was lost, It wasn't free.
It kept me in the dark, wouldn't let me be.
When Love was lost, I missed it so,
I wouldn't ever let it go.
When Love, was lost, I only cried,
For once I wish he would have lied.
When Love was lost, I believed he found it.
I couldn't stand to be around it.
Suffocating from a Love that was Lost
please help me find it, my love, that he tossed...
Tossed it away, into a darkened sea, 
for someone to find it, someone but me.
Help me to find it, I want it returned
I think finally, a lesson was learned
When Love is Lost, set it free
Let it be what it wants to be
Let Love find someone who deserves it more
who knows what love is really for
Love's not for me, not anymore
Not from him, the one I adored
He'll find another, and so will I
My Love that was lost, was just a white lie.Love is lost when you see the person you loved with someone else. Love is lost when he tells you that you didn't love him as much as he loved you, although you know in your heart you did. Love is lost when nothing else seems to matter to him but himself:) Love is Lost and I can't find it anymore...
