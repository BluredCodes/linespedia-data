---
title: "Almost.... Hurts"
writer: "trayseelynn"
slug: "almost-hurts"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/42365-Almost....-Hurts-by-trayseelynn"
---

almost made it to forever
almost made it last
almost lasted till the end of time
it just all ended so fast

almost had the perfect life
almost made it through
almost had everything
what am i going to do without you

almost was the happiest
almost had it all
almost was who you wanted me to be
i stumble and i fall

almost loved you enough
almost held you forever
almost kept you in my arms
but now we aren't together

almost, doesn't count anymore
almost, didn't make you stay
almost couldn't keep you here
i really hate feeling this way

almost, isn't good enough
almost, isn't always
almost, won't make you come back
almost, in so many ways,

.... hurts
