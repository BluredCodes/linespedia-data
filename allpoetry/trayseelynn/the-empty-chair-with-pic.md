there all alone, in a dark room it sits
it's master away, not paying attention to it
it's taken for granted, the comfort it brings
he uses his chair for miriad of things

but here on the screen all i see is this chair
hoping its master will give it great care
poor thing, all deserted, the lights are all dim
no one around it, not even "him"

in front of the desk that holds all the work
shouldn't this poor chair always come first
it holds up it's master, with comfort and grace
hoping it not to faulter or else fall on his face

the seat and it's cushion, greatly are needed
for when the project is just not right and must be repeated
late nights turn to days, the seat must be worn
but greatfully, the master is keeping it warm

i look upon his face and i see
a lonely empty chair calling out to me
please, while your watching me, come take a seat
kick back and relax and pick up your feet

although it's inviting, i know i can't help it
his master's away, and alone he must sit
for hours and hours, poor chair cries its tears
the darkness and coldness, it's biggest fears

soon, the master, will return to the chair
sit upon, the seat that he earlier left there
no longer alone, all warm, soft and comfy
hey, master, is there enough room there for me?

don't worry, dear friends, this chair will be fine
because it belongs to a dear friend of mine
all night as he works, this chair is his friend
keeping him sitting upright again and again!the picture is the actual chair:)
