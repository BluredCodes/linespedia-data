---
title: "Missing You"
writer: "trayseelynn"
slug: "missing-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

I'll miss you, I'll miss you
With all of my heart
The longer you're away
The more I fall apart.

I love you, I love you
More than words can say
I love you every minute
Of every passing day.

I need you, I need you
In everything I do
I need you here to hold me
When I lay here missing you!
