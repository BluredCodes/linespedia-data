---
title: "Pathway Left Behind"
writer: "trayseelynn"
slug: "pathway-left-behind"
source: "AllPoetry"

the road i'm walking is cold and long
i'm hoping somehow it will make me strong

alone in the dark, i struggle to see
hoping that somehow, someone will find me

arms outreached, i feel my way around
hoping i don't trip and fall to the ground

scared and afraid, leaving clues behind
emtpiness fills me,  i'm out of my mind

noises surround me, taunting me more
they want to hurt me, settle the score

i'm stumbling down this pathway of life
fighting the pain, the anquish and strife

a light, over there, i see it, it's bright
it's cut through the darkness, broken the night

i'm alive and i'm breathing, i'm finally free
the warmth of this light, inside of me

wrapping it's arms around me with care
gently and softly, it's always been there

this love that i feel, it's perfect and whole
lighting this darkness deep in my soul

a love like no other, someday i will find
because the darkness, it's over, i've left it behind
