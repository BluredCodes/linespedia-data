---
title: "Today, Ten Months Ago"
writer: "trayseelynn"
slug: "today-ten-months-ago"

Today would have been ten months
since you came into my heart
but since you are not here
Today, I fall apart

Today would have been ten months
since the day we started this
but since you are not here
the more of "us" I miss

Today would have been ten months
2 more would be a year
it's the nightmares I wake up from
when I notice you're not here

Today would have been ten months
my day is going down hill
I just want you to know I still love you
and I know I always will

Today would have been ten months
you would have been with me
but instead you're somewhere else
you're ten months of memories

Today would have been ten months
I'll try and make it through
today's just another day
that I'm spending without you"Happy Ten Month Anniversary, Baby... I miss you and love you! Wherever you are and whatever you do today, just know this!"
