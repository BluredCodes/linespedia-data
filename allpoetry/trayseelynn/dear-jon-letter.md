Dear "Jon":

I would like to thank you, dearest "Jon"
For the night I had, the date I went on
Perfume, you bought me, still lingers on his chest
The love we made, it was simply the best
He enjoyed my hands, caressing his face
As I laid there trapped in his warm embrace
We kissed so deeply, he stole my breath
I feel renewed, reborn, refreshed.
This game you played, you haven't won
I'll have you know, it's just begun
I'm still playing, can't you see
You just THOUGHT you were over me
I can't believe the lies you told
Your love for me, it just turned cold
I believed it all, I believed you cared
Cheat on you? I wouldn't dare.
But you made your choice, you made your bed
Now lay with her, because to me, you're dead
I wanted to thank you, for the things that I have
With a new heart, and life, the one I took back
You thought you had me under your thumb
But I hate to tell you, you haven't won
I was a fool, to give you my all
But I just realized, it was my call
I didn't have to live with that hurt
I wasn't the one left in the dirt
So you take your lies, and all your "I love you's"
I'm no longer playing by your shitty rules
This is the end, the finish line is near
I'm done with you; I'm over you, dear
You had your fun, now leave me alone
Don’t page me, email, or call on the phone
I could care less who's sleeping in your bed
In the end, their love, is all in your head
You’ll get hurt, the way you hurt me
And you'll realize, I’m gone, just a memory
You can't turn back, you can't make it up
It’s over, it's done and YOU gave it up
So I thank you, again, for this man in my arms
And he'll keep me safe now, allow me no harm
He’ll lay with me until I fall asleep
And be there in the dreams that we keep
Thank you for the nights we're alone
Making love for hours, when no one is home
All because of things you gave me
Is making me forget your memory
In another man's arms, I found my heart
The one you stepped on and tore apart
He’ll be the one to love me instead
And I’ll be the one who lies in his bed
All because of what we had
You lost the game, love, too bad so sad!!


Lovingly Not Yours Anymore,
