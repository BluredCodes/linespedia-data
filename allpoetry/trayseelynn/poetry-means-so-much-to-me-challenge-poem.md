Contentment and Courtesy if you please 
The poems I write are both of these 
They make me feel better about myself 
I hope they do this, also, to someone else 

Poetry makes me happy when I'm sad 
Makes me cheerful when I am mad 
Good poetry keeps me smiling all day 
and keep me in good spirits when I'm in dismay 

Good poetry speaks from it's heart 
Keeps us together, no matter how far apart 
Speaks to the person it was meant to touch 
That's why good poetry means so much 

A writer tries to express the way that they feel 
Sometimes the poetry can be fictitous or real 
Written from the heart of each poet on here 
We are all friends, we have nothing to fear 

Closeness bring us to a new kind of place 
Good Poetry brings a smile to my face 
Friendships are made from poets galore 
You never know what you are in store for 

Read the ones you like cuz I'm sure you will be touched 
cuz Good Poetry, to the writer, means so much! 
I'm writing this poem to help you understand 
that good poetry to me is wonderfully grand!Challenge Description: I believe that all poetry is good. But write a poem on what a good poem is TO YOU. What would you like to see in a good poem? How would you want to feel while and after you read a good poem? Or you can write a how-to poem (how to write good poetry). Be specific. Make it funny or interesting. Any style. But the number one rule is safety. Whatever you write, please respect everyone on this site. Thank you---naninina ps. NO FIGHTING ON THESE--only peace.
