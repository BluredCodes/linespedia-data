---
title: "His Love For Me"
writer: "trayseelynn"
slug: "his-love-for-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/39109-His-Love-For-Me-by-trayseelynn"
---

his love for me
it broke me
a million pieces scattered

his love for me
it held me
and nothing else would matter

his love for me
it warmed me
a heater in the storm

his love for me
it kissed me
it made me new, reborn

his love for me
it touched me
made me quiver, made me shake

his love for me
i miss it
i long for it, i ache

his love for me
is gone now
no longer will i see

his love for me
remembered
until eternity
