title: "daddy didn't come home"

daddy did not come home with me
he's not in his chair drinking his tea
he's not in his bed where he use to be
because daddy did not come home with me

daddy does not call home anymore
he doesn't call mom like he did before
doesn't ask what's for dinner or what to get from the store
because daddy doesn't call home anymore

daddy does not live in this home
he lives somewhere else by himself, all alone
i don't get to see him, just talk on the phone
because daddy does not live in this home

daddy is not out mowing the lawn
he's not in the shower singing a song
i don't really know what went so wrong
i really hope daddy isn't gone for too long

daddy i miss you :(
