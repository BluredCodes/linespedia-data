---
title: "Love, Me!"
writer: "trayseelynn"

Have I ever told you that I love you?
Of course I have, but you didn't hear me.

Have I ever told you that I need you?
Of course I have, but you didn't listen.

Have I ever told you that I'd die for you? 
Of course I have, but you didn't care.

Have I ever told you that I dream your dreams?
Of course I have, but you didn't see me.

Have I ever told you that I hear your thoughts?
Of course I have, but you didn't know what you were thinking.

Have I ever told you that I love you?
Of course I have, all in all I always will.
