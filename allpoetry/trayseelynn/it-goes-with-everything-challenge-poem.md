i take a bite 
it's heaven sent 
it's cheese it's cheese 
smell it's scent 

some have holes 
and some do not 
eat a little 
eat a lot 

smooth and creamy 
i eat it in my sleep 
tasty and dreamy 
tons of it, i keep 

cheese on my pizza 
oh look at the strings 
melting in my mouth 
oh the joy it brings 

i live it, breathe it, love it 
it's the most wonderful thing 
this cheese is most inviting 
it goes with everythingEat some cheese today! it goes with everything!
