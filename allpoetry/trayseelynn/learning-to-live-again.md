---
title: "Learning To Live Again"
writer: "trayseelynn"

When darkness falls upon my heart
our closeness now feels worlds apart
the love I had for only you
has never proven to be untrue

I know that our love was not a lie
as much as I have thought and cried
My love for you did not fail
but you weren't happy and I could tell

I thought our lives would soon be one
as our time together had just begun
My time with you would soon run out
And, you, I would have to learn to live without

Broken hearts belong to two
Love only spares a chosen few
Unfortunatly, we were not those
No longer together, that's how it goes

No one's at fault, not you nor I
we just weren't equally satisfied
I'm obviously not who you want me to be
And now I have to set "Us" free.

When my heart has to be strong
Our lives must now, just go on
I won't say Goodbye, however,
I treasured all our times together

Although it's over, I'm now renewed
Learning to live again, without you.
