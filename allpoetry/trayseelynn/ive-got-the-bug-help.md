title: "I've Got The Bug! HELP!!!!"

i have snot running out of my ears
drowning in it, is what i fear
i've blown and blown and blown my nose
nothing comes out, isn't that how it goes?
my nostrils are so extremely raw
i feel like i'm breathing through a straw
i've run out of kleenex, now what do i do?
blow my poor nose on my shirt? EWWW!
i feel like there's no skin left on my nose
from blowing it so much, the bone's starting to show
nothing seems to help, not pepper, not sprays
i've really had a problem, blowing it these days
i'm sick, at home, alone in bed
eating the chicken soup i've been fed
trying to exterminate this bug that i've got
pills, syrups and OJ can't make it stop
please oh please bug, just go away
so i can sleep and have a better day
i cough and i wheeze, i feel like shit
i feel like i've been thrown in a pit
run over by a truck, hit by a plane
this darn old bug, is being a real pain
advil don't help me, neither does tylenol
i'm beginning to think i won't get better at all
maybe tomorrow will be a better day
and i will no longer feel this way
gallons and gallons of gatorade have come and gone
i've peed so much, i've sang the "Pee Pee Fairy" song
sprays to make my throat fill better
but i can't help that i feel under the weather
so bring me some antibiotics and ice
a nice cold rag on my eyes, might feel nice
oh no! there my nose goes again
