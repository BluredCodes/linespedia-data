---
title: "Runaway Bird"
writer: "trayseelynn"
slug: "runaway-bird"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/41191-Runaway-Bird-by-trayseelynn"
---

i want to take off from this place
like a bird i want to fly
spread my wings like eagles
and soar against the sky
~
the wind will run its fingers
through my crimson hair
leaving everything behind
not a worry, not a care
~
the wind will carry me softly
like a mother will a child
keep me safe from darkened storms
i think i'll stay here for a while
~
i day dream while i'm flying here
i see everything so clearly
i live within this perfect world
because it's all below me
~
i do not have to fight or cry
i do not have to live or die
i do not have to laugh or love
because of my home up above
~
when i want to get away
from all this mixed up madness
i smile and take off from this place
and behind i leave the sadness
