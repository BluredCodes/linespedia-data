---
title: "eventually"
writer: "trayseelynn"
slug: "eventually"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/42370-eventually-by-trayseelynn"
---

the pain of my broken heart
the tears that fall
the emptiness you left with me
you should have kept it all

the darkness that engulfs me
the sadness that leaves me drained
the hurting that keeps killing me
yet your memory remains

the haunting of your love for me
the ghost of you keeps tourmenting me
the lifelessness of my body
why can't you let me be

the cause of this could be fixed
the need for me to get over you
the hurting can be filled with love
if i just knew what to do

the need for me to be strong
the need for me to make it through
the time i spend in my life
i'll eventually get over you

the heartache will end soon
the darkness filled with light
the pain filled with love again
i'll try with all my might

the more i learn to love
the more i learn to live
the more the heart will be renewed
i'll have more of myself to give

eventually....
