---
title: "Keep Dreamin'"
writer: "trayseelynn"
slug: "keep-dreamin"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/51131-Keep-Dreamin-by-trayseelynn"

Keep dreamin'
of getting me back
in your bed where you lay

keep dreamin'
of holding me close
in your arms all day

keep dreamin'
of kissing my lips
touching me your finger tips

keep dreamin'
of making love to me
as night turns to day

keep dreamin'
of having me there
when you wake

keep dreamin'
of bathing with me
and drying us off

keep dreamin'
of time after time
you held me and cried

keep dreamin'
of all the things we had
all the time we shared

keep dreamin'
of looking into my eyes
and seeing the sun rise

keep dreamin'
because now thats all you have
to remember us by

keep dreamin'
and soon you will awake
to find out your mistakes

keep dreamin'
of getting me back
in the bed where you lay

keep dreamin'Nightmares suck don't they?!?!
