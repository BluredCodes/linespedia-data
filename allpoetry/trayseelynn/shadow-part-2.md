title: "Shadow (part 2)"

the shadows call me softly
whispering words you've said
makes my heart beat faster
down a path of heartache i'm led

the shadows know no boundries
they take me to places unseen
hoping you're there to greet me
co-dependant, on them, i lean

the shadows, at night, they haunt me
thier arms keep me in this dream
wrapped around my body
i wake up, and i scream

the shadows know i miss you
they rip my heart out more
they can not stand the thought
that it's you that i adore

the shadows, they sit and curse me
they want my love as well
they keep hanging on to nothing
they know my heart so well

the shadows keep me waiting
telling me that you'll return
they know that if i listen
the more and more i'll yearn

the shadows keep me hoping
when i go to bed at night
i'll wake up from this nightmare
and it's you that's holding me tight

but the shadows can not love me
the way that you always will
they want my love so badly
they have a void to fill

the shadows will not keep me
from loving you this way
i'll love you always, forever
