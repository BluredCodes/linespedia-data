---
title: "When I'm Alone"
writer: "trayseelynn"
slug: "when-im-alone"
source: "AllPoetry"

alone i sit and wonder
how things are gonna be
because i no longer have him
and he no longer has me

alone i sit and remember
when i looked into his eyes
the nights we stayed up till morning
and watch the sun set and rise

alone i sit and i'm thinking
how things might have been
i would love him forever
from the beginning till the end

alone i sit and i miss him
and alone i sit and cry
alone i want to be with him
alone as time goes by

alone, one tear is falling
its trails outlines my cheeks
as it silently drips downward
no finish line it seeks

it knows when it hits bottom
another one will come
until a puddle gathers
until my crying's done

my heart mops up the wetness
and tries to hide the pain
cuz alone i sit in my bedroom
knowing my tears are not in vain

alone i sit and ponder
alone i try to see
there was a time when i loved only him
and he loved only me
