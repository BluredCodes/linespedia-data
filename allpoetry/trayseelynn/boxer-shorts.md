Butt huggers and Bikini briefs aren't what I crave
Thongs, to wear those, a man has to be brave
I want a man who leaves some to the imagination
Boxer shorts, are my fixation

I don't want a man who's pants are too tight
or too thin that through them, you see daylight
I want something I can fit my hands in
a man, with boxers, is a definate win!

No time to fight to take them off of him
just slide them down, step out of them
ooh baby, you know, i love you in those
better than when you're wearing no clothes

Boxers are my natural high
to see him in those, i could almost die
my heart beats so fast, i can't hardly breath
it turns me on so, its hard to believe

They have them with colors and pictures galore
i buy him several when i go to the store
he has so many pairs, i can't pick or chose
the ones i want him wear when we snooze

But he knows the ones that turn me on
the ones with my name, but they soon are gone
I take them off gently with my teeth
he has no time to even speak!

Baby, those boxers, do me in good
you turn me on like a man should
when you wear your boxers around the room
they're sure to be my final doom!

So when you're trying to make me hot
butt huggers and thongs, definatly do not!
get you some boxers, any color will do...
and soon I will be all over you!My inspiration for this poem will not be named but he knows who he is :))
