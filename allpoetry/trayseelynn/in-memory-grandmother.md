---
title: "[In Memory] Grandmother"
writer: "trayseelynn"
slug: "in-memory-grandmother"

Her hair with the smell of perfume
Brings her presence to my mind
The way she made toast every morning
And not a crumb was left behind

Her paintings, reflections of life
Bring the past to our present time
Visions of her heart you see
Is what she left behind

Her home, a place to gather together
family, young and old
Bring to mind a happy time
Memories that can't be bought or sold

A sister, a mother, a daughter, a friend
She was a gentle soul
A life lived by no other person
Her heart was made of gold

Grandmother, I love you
You'll always live in my heart
Through life or death, you live with me
We'll never be apartThis was written for my Grandmother, may her memory live on through the lives she touched and remembered in her paitings:)
