---
title: "Love Is Forever"
writer: "trayseelynn"
slug: "love-is-forever"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/37566-Love-Is-Forever-by-trayseelynn"
---

Love doesnt come from money
Love isnt made from gold
Love cant be purchased 
Be borrowed, be sold.
Love doesnt come from anger
Love never comes from hate
But Love is something, they both 
Can never penetrate.
Love is something you give
To someone for free or in need
Love is something you share
Because you know it will always be there
Love is the greatest gift you can give
Love helps us get through the life that we live
Love is there in trails, in sorrow, in pain.
Love must never be handled or given in vain.
Love has it rules that we never choose
Love is there to catch us,
Come win or come lose.
Love is a shadow, always around,
Love will never let us down.
Love is an anchor- it holds us in place
Loves there with us, through problems we face.
Love is eternal, Love is forever
Love is the glue that holds us together.
Love is unchanging, its unwilling to lose
Its always there no matter the directions we choose.
Love is unending, it goes on and on
Love has the power to make our lives strong
Loves never broken, in pieces or apart.
Love is the feeling that comes from the heart!
