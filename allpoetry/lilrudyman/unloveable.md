title: "''Unloveable''"
writer: "lilrudyman"

You said that you loved me,
And I believed it was true.
I opened up my heart,
Only becasue I loved you.
I can't belive what I've done!
I was being such a fool.
I opened up my legs,
'Cause I was trying to be cool.
Now look what you did?
Were you being smart?
You got me twisted around my own head,
'Cause you was playing games from the start.
You hurt me, yo.
You hurt me deep inside.
You made me bleed from the heart,
And that caused me to die.
I'm laying on the floor,
With blood pouring from my head.
But you never came to my rescue,
And now, I am dead.
