You are falling towards the earth,
But you cannot stick.
My mom said we can't play around,
'Cause the snow is not thick.
The tempurature says it's 60,
Then sun says it's not.
I look out the window,
And I only see one spot.
I wonder each day,
If the snow will stick to the ground.
I look out the window the next day,
Amd I begin to frown.
I see the sun,
Gleaming on my window each day.
And when I walk outside,
It feels like it's the end of May.
The season says it's winter,
The sun says it's not.
The thermometer says it's freezing,
The sun says it's hot.
