---
title: "\"I'm Born To Die\""
writer: "lilrudyman"
slug: "im-born-to-die"

I'm born to die.
I wake up only to close my eyes.
I say hello only to wave good-bye.
Like I said,
I'm born to die.

I'm born to die.
I open my eyes just so I can cry.
I get in the shower only to make myself dry.
As I said before,
I'm born to die.

I'm born to die.
I go through so much only to let out a sigh.
I get shot 9 times just before I die.
I said it before, and I'll say it again.
I close my eyes, I wave good-bye,
I start to cry, I begin to dry,
I let out a sigh only because,
I'm born to die.
