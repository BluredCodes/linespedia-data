Just you and me,
In the bedroom together.
I promised you,
That I'll make this memory last forever.
I slowly take off your clothes,
As I message your back.
I rub on your legs,
And kiss on your neck.
I hear you moan,
That means that's your spot.
I begin to tease you,
And you tell me not to stop.
You say that it's hurting,
But you love the way it feels.
You tell me to put on a condom,
So we won't make a big deal.
We keep on going,
Just as fast as we can.
AS I finally let it out, you scream,
"Oh I love you my man!"
