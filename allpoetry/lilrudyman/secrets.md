title: "''Secrets''"

You have a lot on your mind,
But you don't speak a word.
You remain silent, and you keep still.
But this is a song I've already heard.
Your secrets are no strangers.
Your actions are so loud.
You hold everything inside you,
But manage to walk so proud.
You walk around everyday,
Tryin' to be so tough.
You try so hard to hide from people,
And now your hiding from yourself.
You can't hide what's in your heart,
Because it speaks through your eyes.
Girl, why you tryin' to be somebody you ain't?
Girl why you tryin' to lie?
I see you tryin' to hide your secret,
And it's taring you apart.
You go around pretending every day,
But you can't pretend what's in your heart.
