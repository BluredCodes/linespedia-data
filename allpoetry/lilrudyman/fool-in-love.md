Your just a fool,
You don't understand.
Why he treat you like he do,
If he's such a good man.
He slaps you around the house,
And beats up on your face.
Then you say it'll all get better,
If you just use your amazing grace.
You say your in love,
And I don't know how.
This man says your lovely and beautiful,
But in public, calls you a cow.
You say you know he's sorry,
And he won't beat on you again.
But you said that the other times,
When he left you cryin' in the din.
Your just a fool,
You don't understand.
Why he treat you like he do,
If he's such a good man.
