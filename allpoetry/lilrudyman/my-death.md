title: "'My Death'"

I look at this earth,
And how it has made me cry.
So I looked up above,
And asked God why.
I'm sick of this earth,
And how it has brought my down.
And on some occasions,
I wish I'd just lay in the ground.
Sometimes I am happy,
Other times I am sad.
I wake up each morning,
Just to get mad.
So I brought me some drugs,
The ones that'll put me to sleep.
So I can finally rest in peace,
And lay 6 feet deep.
I swalloed the pill,
And took my last breathe,
I closed my eyes,
And accepted my death.
