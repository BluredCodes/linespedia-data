---
title: "'I'm Cool Like That'"
writer: "lilrudyman"

I'm cool like that.
I got enemies and friends giving me respect.
I have plenty of friends cause people love me like that.
Basically, what I'm saying is,
I'm cool like that.

I'm cool like that.
I can go for days without school, and still pass my test.
I can have the worst grades, and still be the best.
Don't be mad, that's just how I am,
'Cause I'm cool like that.

I'm cool like that.
I'm just who I am, cause I'm cool like that.
Don't ever try to hurt me, cause you'll never hit me back.
What I'm saying is that,
You can never hold me back.
So ma' be easy,
'Cause I'm cool like that
