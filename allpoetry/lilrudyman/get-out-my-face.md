---
title: "''Get Out My Face''"
writer: "lilrudyman"
slug: "get-out-my-face"
source: "AllPoetry"

Yo "tough man,"
Don't come around here.
Don't wanna see your face,
You betta disappear.
You think you tough,
Tryin' do all you can.
I suggest you start runnin',
'Cause you ain't no macho man.
You need to get out my face,
Before I smack you with this spear.
You betta run for your life!
Am I perfectly clear?
You tryin' to show me,
That you ain't scared.
Yo, you playin' wit your life,
This ain't no truth or dare.
You shakin' in ya boots,
But you still think you the stuff.
MAN GET OUT MY FACE!,
'Cause you ain't nowhere near tough.I'm not mad at anyone,just needed to address the situation that all boyz ain't tough as you think. No anger in my heart, so don't jump to conclusions. I still gotz luv for all ya'll.
