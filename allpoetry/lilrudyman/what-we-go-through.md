I love you mom,
Through the good times and bad.
Even though on occasions,
I can make you feel very sad.
For you to worry about me,
Fills my heart with pride and joy.
Despite the fact when I come in late,
You scream,"Where you been at boy?"
Putting you through those emotional obstacles,
I see that they hurt you.
But they only make you a stronger person inside,
And that's why you never cry boo hoo.
Just to see you cry mom,
Makes me very mad.
My anger gets the best of me,
And I end up hitting anyone in my path.
From a son to a mother,
I have a few words to speak.
I will always love you,
Even when your 6 feet deep.
