2 hours of pleasure,
12 hours of pain.
The documents was passed your way,
And you signed your name.
Then it was official,
That you were my dad.
And as I gazed into your eyes,
I saw that you were mad.
3 days later,
You ran away.
I guess you couldn't handle the fact,
That you had 3 children, and 2 on the way.
You ran so far,
So far far away.
And I never saw you again,
Even until this very day.
So listen up "dad",
I have a few words for you.
I never wanna see you again.
Forget you, we're THROUGH!
