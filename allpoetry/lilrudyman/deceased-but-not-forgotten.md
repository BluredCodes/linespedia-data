---
title: "'Deceased, but not forgotten'"
writer: "lilrudyman"

You are my brother,
You are my friend.
Through drugs nd alcohol,
Through thick and thin.
You was the 2nd child,
Out of three little boys.
You was always friendly,
And always shared your toys.
Even though I got on your nerves,
You still hung around.
And when I fell off my bike,
You helped me off the ground.
It was about 2:00,
When you came home from school.
You went over some friend's house,
And played Nintendo with some dude.
Then another guy came downstairs,
And shot you in head,
And 6 hours later,
My brother Kevin was dead.*This is dedicated to my brother who was murdered on December 8th, 1993. I LOVE YOU KEV!
