You have a secret,
But I cannot tell.
You made me promise,
Or I'll go to hell.
But I cannot hold back,
What lies on my chest.
You are my best friend,
So I must put you through this test.
Your secret lies within,
Deep down in your soul.
And I am the only person,
The only soul that knows.
You are a quiet soul,
And I don't know why.
You are a nice person,
But your feelings hide deep inside.
You have an open heart,
But no one really knows.
You must open yourself up,
