---
title: "''Crying Earth''"
writer: "lilrudyman"

Look at this earth,
And how it remains.
If we continue doing this,
It'll never be the same.
Listen to this earth.
Look at how it can't take no more.
Everyday,
It sinks deeper into it's core.
Diseases and fumes,
Caused mainly by us.
C'mon now, all of that!
It is just too much.
We walk around each day,
With a smile on our face.
But the earth moves everyday,
And it has become a dying place.
What we need to do is get it it together,
And get our minds right.
Before the earth decides on day,
There will no longer be any life.
