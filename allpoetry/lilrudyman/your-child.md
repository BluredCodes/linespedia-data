You think you're a father,
Even though it's not true.
That little girl named Brenda,
Has never loved you.
She did something so sneaky,
Something the devil wouldn't do.
She said you was the father,
And that is not true.
She said she hasn't been with anyone,
After the sex.
Because her stomach started hurting,
And she decided to take the test.
I have to admit though,
She set you up good.
She had you thinkin' you were a father,
That you finally had your manhood.
After you finish reading this,
You'll probably be raging in the wild.
But that cute little baby,
Is not your child.
