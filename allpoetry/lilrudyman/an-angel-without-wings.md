title: "''An Angel Without Wings'"
writer: "lilrudyman"

When I first saw your physique,
I felt so ashamed,
Because you were my gaurdian angel.
An angel without wings.
You said you could fly,
Because you were the "special one."
But as I looked at your figure,
I knew it couldn't be done.
How can you be an angel,
Or even a child of God?
How can you be a faithful servant,
If you cannot fly?
You're missing one little feature,
That makes your appearance complete.
But that one special necessity,
Has damaged your physique.
You say you are an angel,
And that is a shame.
But how did you even become,
My angel without wings.
