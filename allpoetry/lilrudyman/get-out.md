I'm sick of people,
Always having something to say.
Sometimes I wish,
They will just go the hell away.
You read my stuff,
Just to say your's is better.
Well if your feelings are that strong,
Than continue reading this letter.
I'm 'bout sick of your mouth,
And your stuck-up ways.
'Cause I'm about tired of you,
Downing my work every day.
"Your poems aren't worth reading,"
"Your poems aren't like mine."
If you don't like it, move on.
Don't waste my time.
Don't read my stuff,
If you don't like what I do.
Don't ever read my work,
