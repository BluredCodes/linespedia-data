Brown skin,
You know I love your brown skin.
It makes you even more beautiful within.
Your brown skin stretches from end to end.
Oh darling, I love your brown skin.

Brown skin,
You know I love your brown skin.
We have so much history because of brown skin.
My sweet heart, I adore your brown skin.

Brown skin,
You know I love your brown skin.
It has a special tone that we all commend.
So lovely, shiny,  and beautiful within,
That when we make love,
We will have beauteous brown children.
My angel, oh how I cherish your brown skin.
