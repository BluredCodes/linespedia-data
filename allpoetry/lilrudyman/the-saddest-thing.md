---
title: "\"The Saddest Thing\""
writer: "lilrudyman"
slug: "the-saddest-thing"

Him and her,
Well in their youth.
He said that he loved her,
But it wasn't the truth.
She thought that he loved her,
At least that's what he said.
But those words in translation meant,
"I want you in bed!"
So the belly started rising,
They now had a child to share.
And when he found out,
He didn't even care.
"Yo, the baby ain't mine," 
Was the first thing he said.
And nine months later,
Out came the head.
"We had love, romance, and each other," she cried.
Remembering what he said, it's too bad, he lied.
What he did was wrong,
What he did was mean.
He should've known better,
After all, he's 16.
But her childhood and memories,
Are put on the shelf.
But the saddest thing is,
She is only 12.
