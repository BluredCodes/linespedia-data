title: "'My Time Has Come'"
writer: "lilrudyman"

The storm is almost over now.
I can see beyond the clouds.
Heaven is shining on me.
And I am ready to be set free.
I lived my life to the fullest,
With a smile each day.
And now that I'm dead,
I am happy to see Heaven's gateway.
The devil sees me flyingm
And he tries to pull me down.
But I'm almost at the top,
So I can't stop now.
I'm almost there now,
The clouds are getting clearer,
I can see Heaven's rope,
And how it pulls me nearer.
The storm is over now.
I am higher than the clouds.
Heaven is over me.
And God has finally set me free.
