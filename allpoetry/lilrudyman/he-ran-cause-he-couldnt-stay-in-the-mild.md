I thought you and me being together,Made us strong.But after the way you played me,I found out I was wrong.You left me out in the street,Left me out in the cold.Then you had the nerve to stroll away,As if you were so bold.For you to leave me by the river,Was oh so mean.And you say you did this because,You were only 16.You had no right,To make loneliness my new life.All of this because,You forgot to 'wrap it up' last night.Since you found out, that you had a child.You decided to run away,Because you couldn't stay in the mild.
