I seen her in the streets,
Walking with her friends.
Talking about touhing up her split ends.
But what caught my eye,
Was her beautiful smile.
Those lovely eyes, and the shape of her thighs.
Those lushes lips and those lovely hips,
Just makes me wanna give her a kiss.
When our eyes met, it was love at first sight.
Ao I asked her for her number,
And she said alright.
Ever since then, we did a lot of things.
Like making love in the shower,
And buying each other diamond rings.
The next thing we know,
Marriage came.
Ever since the first day I saw her,
Everyone said I was stunned.
All because,
I knew she was the one.
