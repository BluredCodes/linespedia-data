---
title: "\"Before The Water Runs Dry\""
writer: "lilrudyman"
slug: "before-the-water-runs-dry"

I understand,
What you were tryin' to say.
I know how it feels,
To be that way.
Just keep your head up high.
And keep aiming for the sky.
Never let go, or you'll fall down.
You'll fall, all the way to the ground.
If you fall, just get back up again.
And keep on tryin',
Until the very, very end.
But watch out for, those deadly roads.
The ones that are filled with,
Those dirty, nasty toads.
But let those toads know, that you ain't scared.
Or they will put you through their most dangerous dare.
But remember what I said,
"Keep your head up high."
And keep on goin',
Before the water runs dry.
