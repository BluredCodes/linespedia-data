title: "'You Are My Friend'"

You are my friend,
And not just some fool.
Ever since the 3rd grade,
You've walked me home from school.
You've kept me in your company, 
When no one was around.
And you picked me up,
When I was down.
Your just like my brother,
Even though it's not true.
And as many times as we fought,
I still cared about you.
We've been through hell,
And together we fought back.
Even though jealousy got in our way,
We still stayed in tack.
Our friendship is evolving,
Higher than the trees.
And we'll make it to heaven,
Together, you and me.
