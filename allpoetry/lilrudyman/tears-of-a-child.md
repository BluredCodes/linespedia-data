I am a human being, 
But I don't have a brain.
I live without a heart,
And I wander without a name.
I am the only child,
Growing up under her hood.
But my mother screams and yells at me,
And says that I am no good.
If you didn't want me mom,
Why did you make that choice.
Don't you know that I am a human being,
And nor some little toy.
You're playing with fire mom,
And fumbling with God's grace.
And you're thinking things will be perfect,
If you just kill this "little" mistake.
But later on in life,
When you she your tears of regret.
Just remember half the tears are mine,
That will make your pillow wet.
