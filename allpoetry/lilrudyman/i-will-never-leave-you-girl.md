I will never leave 
You girl!
Your my brightest star
In this whole wide world.
So I dedicate my life
To you!
And I'll do all the things,
You want me to do.
Your my rising sun,
And my shining star.
And I''ll be right there,
Whether I'm near or far.
Your my blowing wind,
And my setting sun.
And I'll stay by your side,
Cause your the one.
I will never leave you girl.
Your the only girl I see in this world.
So I put you first in my life.
So would you take the honor of being my wife.
