By night one way,
By day another.
You'll forever remain this way,
Until you find your true lover.

By night you'll be one way,
By day another.
And this is the way you'll have to stay,
Until you find your true lover.

You'll be beautiful in the light,
And ugly in the dark.
And this is the spell I cast upon you,
Until you're shocked by love's spark.

This spell was bestowed upon me,
Ever since I was born.
And this is the way I have to stay,
Until love takes it's true form.Yes, this poem is inspired by the movie, "Shrek". Only the quote "By night one way, by day another" is what I took from the movie. Everything else is completely mine. Thanks for reading.
