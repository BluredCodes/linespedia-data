Why did you do this to me?
What did I do?
All I ever wanted,
Was to be friends and care about you.
But you chose a different path,
And threw me in the street.
You played me in front of everybody,
And left me there to weep.
My intentions were good,
And this is what I get from you.
I always said hello,
And never disrespected you.
I guess it was the popularity,
That got into your head.
'Cause when I was sick,
You never called or cared.
Now that I have spoken my piece,
It is time for me to part.
Good-bye, so long,
And I'll always love you from the heart.
