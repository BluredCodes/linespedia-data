I've been writing for a long time 
So this comes easy for me.
But I can't seem to focus right now,
Oh how can this be?
I'm supposed to be a skillful poet.
So for me, rhyming is pretty easy.
But I am fixed on writer's block right now,
Lord....why is this happening to me?
Writing poetry is pretty easy for me,
Probably because I'm a kid.
And I have so much to write about,
Or at least I thought I did.
My writer's block is escalating,
I haven't much time.
What can I possibly do,
That will help me to rhyme?
My skills are fading away from me,
And I haven't much to speak.
My mind is getting blurry,
My rhymes.......... are getting weak.
