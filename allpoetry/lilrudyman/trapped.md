---
title: "''Trapped''"
writer: "lilrudyman"

How can I love someone,
That hurts me so bad.
Even though his intentions were good.
But that was all he ever had.
It seems like I'm loving you for nothing,
Because it's been that long.
But I can't just give up that easy,
And tell you to get gone.
Giving up something like this,
Can cease my lifetime.
But the love I feel for you,
Is nothing but a hopeless cry.
In the beginning things were great,
And the love I sensed will go long.
But how can something so great,
Go so drastically wrong.
How do I let go when I loved him for so long.
And I gave him all I can.
But the love I once had for you,
Was nothing but a sin.No, this poem is not about me. It's about some of my friends.
