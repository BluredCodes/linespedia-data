Life is like an ocean
Growing up as a child
Sweet and innocent
Playful...free flowing
And waves form slowly
Above the surface
As the gentle wind
Blows gradually
Calm and easy

But then, times of
Dramatic changes
One reaches adolescence
Broken heart
Shattered dreams
Confused and baffled
By life itself
Feeling lonely
Just want to cry
Just want to shout
Want love...want everything
Once in awhile
Comes a storm
A thunderstorm
Heavy rain pounding
Against each other
Twisting and turning every
Water particle inside out

Time to be an adult
Learning from past mistakes
Life is together now
Feeling great
New hope
New future
New goals
The storm has just passed
Things are back to normal
The waves sit tranquilly
Smooth...a line of flat surface
Perfect view

Life is like an ocean
Now repeating itself
Unpredictable
Sometimes it's up
And sometimes it's down
Life is full of joy
Yet, it's filled with sorrow too
A calm ocean can be
Fun to swim in
Yet, it can be dangerous
And destroy everything
It works both ways
Don't know why
Don't know how
It just happens, unpredictablyWhat can you feel from this poem?
