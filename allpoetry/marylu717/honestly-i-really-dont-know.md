title: "Honestly, I Really Don't Know"

Love is something I cannot explain
I just feel it within my heart
I don't know why
Or how it happens...but it just does

You never knew because you never asked
Do I love you?...Yes, I do
But I really don't know why

My girl friends might ask
What is it that I see in you
That brings a smile to my face
My guy friends  might ask
What is it that you've got that they don't have
What is it that makes me praise highly
of you in my diary
That makes my writing seem untrue
My parents might ask
What is it that makes me think you're so special
That every now and then I get a little clumsy.

I would reply I really don't know
I would tell them
So you're different than other guys
straight and warm inside
Maybe you're not.....
As rich as Bill Gates
As buff as Arnold Shwartzeneggar
As tall as Joel West
As funny as Jim Carey
As handsome as Leon Lai

But so what
I love you for who you are
A guy who's not afraid to share your feelings
A guy who admits your fear of being more than friends
A guy who listens to my daily crisis
A guy who's really smart in your own way
Because you try your best

Now, you know
Yes, I love you so
Why?
Honestly, I really don't know.Ever had this type of feeling inside of you and didn't know how you got it?  Is it like a virus that attacks on every human who loves to love the people around us?  Honestly, I Really Don't Know.
