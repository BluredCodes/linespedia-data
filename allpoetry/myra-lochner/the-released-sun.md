title: "The Released Sun"

Oriental poppy dance ...
a delicate aquarelle,
recreating brushstrokes of Sheerness:
a Clarity that suddenly wélls
from the Waters of Oblivion

The Golden Sun is being released:
an Eastern Flower
painting brown cinnamon
yellow saffron
sweet amber chochola ...

Passion 
drifting on an aromatic glow
Westwards

The Angel of the Myrtle Trees
blends the distillate of fruit
and seed;
the dew of youth renews
the Ecliptic Journey ...

the Eternal Poem begins ...

myra
