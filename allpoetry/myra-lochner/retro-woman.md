title: "Retro-woman"

Woman wearing horns of deer; stone;
wood; shell; metal; bone -
for enthusiasm: smouldering azure
for fertility: the amber fire ...
amulet for benediction:
smooth scallop of a pearly heart

Woman wearing chains of gold - linking
circles ... ring-in-ring;
friendship's quiet rosen quartz
may mend a lonely broken heart;
Kashmir sapphire's cornflower blue
oh, noble Serenity: for you!

Woman wearing Fleur-de-lys
bracelets of filigree;
for passion: Burma's ruby-red mogok
for texture: pearls of baroque ...
need purification from afar?
shield tender breasts with a polar-star!

myraRetro: moving from geometrical designs back to antique jewellery
