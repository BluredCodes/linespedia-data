title: "Sight  of Sound"

Im-
mortality  
closes wings  
to honour us 
by treading  
on our earthly path  
mere wounded mortal beings  
then hear the sound of Love: soft, 
soft trailing of a feathery veil 
faintly audible 
Whiteness
of soul's
gentle
Dove
