For my pleasure and in my purpose
I saw something else today
that was so good:

In the waiting room
a mother was sitting
with her baby 
asleep 
on her lap.

It started to rain outside ...
the temperature dropping sharply
and then
another woman took off her jacket,
covering the sleeping child,
saying:

"So that he may sleep soundly"

It was so sweet:

mother's instinct
and this baby --
knowing nothing
: about this interaction
: or the rain
: or the waiting

just sleeping soundly
embraced by a stranger's 
jacket


myra
