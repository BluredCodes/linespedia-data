title: "Crippled"

I was crippled by your love
which taught me to mistrust
the pureness of my heart
I was cut off by the coldness
of your voice
which swished like sharp swords
through your eyes

then

I was caged by your loneliness
and your pain
which tainted your being 
with darkened flesh and frowns

I could not leave ...

myra 2002
