Be my Tree:

I want to lean
against you ...
feeling 
your rough 
touch
with my smooth
tender
skin

Make your mark
upon my soul
deep
impressions
rooted in moist
soil:

soft
whispers
of Eternity

Let me climb
up to the seventh
Heaven
searching
my way 
through 
evergreen leaves
to the sweetness
of your
Fruit

be my Tree:

drip
your Myhrr
into my open 
wounds

heal
this feeble
heart ...

 
myra
