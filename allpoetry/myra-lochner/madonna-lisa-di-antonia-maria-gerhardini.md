your soul was lingering
at the tip of his brush
your cool eyes cautioned:
"do not rush"

his artist's heart warned him:
"careful - the mix is close! 
touch the corners of the mouth"
but you stayed aloof; slightly morose

he turned away from your gaze
the task tedious; his temper vile ...
then Mockery slyly stroked your face
shading your lips with its famous Smile

myra
