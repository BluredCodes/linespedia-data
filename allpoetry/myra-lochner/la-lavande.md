Like Van Gogh
I discovered that the lilac 
whispers of lavender
is needed to soften
the yelling yellow Light

But also that the transposed Sun
could dry the purple tears
of loneliness and fears

I framed mý painting
with my eyes
and exhibited it
in the Gallery
of the Heart

but unlike Van Gogh
I could always hear
with my inner ear

and my bedroom
remains white  
and is not at Arles 
...

myra
