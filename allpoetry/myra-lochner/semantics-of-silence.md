wrestling 
in the underclay
sweet Silence is slowly emerging
spindling towards seasonal fullfillment

the forest floor starts to unravel root words;
imperishable the wonderment
of Genesis multiplies
in colourful syllabic
cries


elsewhere
there is the anguish 
of drought, flood and root-rot
the imperfect tense of a painful heritage

tasting the semantics of a spoken Silence
I carefully cultivate protection 
from the bitter fruit:
the ruthless
Muteness


myra
2002The tree shall be known by its fruit ... and the growth determined by the root ...
