I shall speak with Silence
the keys of many words
and syllables of secrets
shine within me 
muted stars
against the black velvet
of my sacred desires

Do you not know
that my voice
is locked in the Vault
of Sweet Sighs?

To hear me 
you must visit the Hermit
dwelling in your own heart
writers of farces
are not welcome here

Purification palpitates
: the rap of the earthy drums
turns to chimes
and eyes
begin to whisper

My beloved silver star

I shall speak with Silence
echoed by Eternity

and then
I shall speak
no more

myra
