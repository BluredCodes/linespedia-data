title: "Keepsake Bouquet"

i cherish petals of affection
tenderly capturing it
in a potpourri of dreams

colourful declarations of love
trimmed with the ribbons 
of devotion

my dainty pansies
~ slowly turning
into antique pearls ~
line sweet rememberance


cup your hands:

my tears
are not destined 
for this forever thirsty Earth

an enchanted melody
evaporates from your fingertips ~
the Essence of Eternity 

myra
