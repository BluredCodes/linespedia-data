title: "Morir en ti"

... and then the Universe
stops breathing ... 
:
names burst forth from bodies
-  newborn light -
the present 
of a past 
of passion
in the arms 
of a transparent
tomorrow

darkness dies with sighs
of Night
:
son of desire
daughter of delight

purified
