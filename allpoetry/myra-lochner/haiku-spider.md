crippled spider's pain
my compilement of love-poems
hides two hairy legs

myra
2002I found two hairy spider legs in my compilement of lovepoems ... whatever happened to the poor spider, I do not know ... What I do know is, in Afrikaans we say "Hy is been-af", (madly in love) ... perhaps the my potion was too strong for such a small creature ...
