title: "Meyou Cat"

What do I do
with this recurring sound:
my meyou meyou cat ...
sleeping in silence;
awakening in a dream?

My meyou meyou cat ...
spinning silk
from yearning yarn;
sleeping on a bed
of illusions?

Shush!  do not wake
my meyou meyou kitty:
milky dreams are better
creamed within slumber.

myra
