arrange my flowers
in vases of love's poetry
murals of music 

myra

2002This senryu is applicable on all visions of beauty:  a flower addressing a poet; a lover addressing a lover ... I tried to stick to the original form, but to resound a timeless voice.
We are all but shadows,  drawn as murals of love's music ...
