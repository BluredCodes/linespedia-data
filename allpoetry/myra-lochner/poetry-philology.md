POETRY PHILOLOGY is a poetry style which stimulates language creativity.  The poem is constructed by the use of single words, nouns or verbs, which are linked as follow: suffix of first word, becoming the prefix of the following word, and so further.

It is important to plan according to a definite theme. The title may be structurally part of the body of the poem. A more "free floating" approach may lead to serindipity/ interesting effects. 

This chain poem lends itself well towards collaboration. It may also be used in small groups for competitive language creativity. 

Enjoy!

Myra Lochner

Example of Poetry Philology:


CHAUVINISM

 pi-jaw
    jawbone
       bonehead

headstrong
    strongman
          man-age-ment
