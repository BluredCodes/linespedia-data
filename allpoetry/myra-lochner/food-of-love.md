---
title: "Food of Love"
writer: "Myra Lochner"

I sit at my table

I say grace

I am eating my breakfast
 
 apple
 peach
seedless grape

during an act of compromise
I am also drinking with my eyes
the saline wine
of ocean skies

I am not devouring
as if starved:
I am eating my breakfast
on the plate of Love

I finish my breakfast

Love says grace

I sit at my table

I say grace


myra
