("...please pick for me the first flower you see...")

Following  the wine route
of the Little Karoo
I found your flowers ... awaiting
my approach with  splendid  perseverance
yearning for release
from the mortal stem
of barrenness and drought

Let me present  to you:

Nymania Capensis ... Chinese Lantern ... Klapperbos
after the insignificant bloom
this rose-pink paper pod —

with its 4 chambers like the human heart
and the 4 seeds 
imbedded in the 4 corners 
of the transparent leaves
longing for  the 4 Winds
and their Destiny ...
the 4 corners of this Earth 
 
and

Bougainvillea X Buttiana
with its South American roots —

with its 3 crimson leaves
caressing  the tentative seeds 
of 3 sanctituded styles 
symbol of Trinity
and Humanity
growing without  the passionate hand
or the admiring glance

I am sending you
this poetical gift of reason
this perfect 7
rhyming Earth and Heaven

I send it
flying over the seas
moved by the Spirit 
to a new Beginning
in the earnest Soul 
of Eternity 

myra
