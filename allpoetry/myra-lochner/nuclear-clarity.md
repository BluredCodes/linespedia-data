(for Thor, who carries the End of the World
in his name)


I cannot dissociate me
from Thor:
His distant discussion
splits my emotion
like the wonder
of atomic fission

With every peal of Thunder
I see my innermost secrets
bared before the Heavens:

Why do I try
to lit my warm ray
at his Fire of Ice?

The answer is written
by the Holy Promise:

The end of this World
will not come by Water
but by the Flash
of Fire ...

Oh Thunder!
do not forget that Sun
also awaits combustion

Do honour to my prenatal
desire:

Let me enlight the Waters
of this Earth
whilst you inflame
the Universe!

myra
