This Labial …  called Love
(At the Touch of Love, everyone becomes a Poet – Plato)

♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥  Love is laboratory and labyrinth  ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥

♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥  Love is lavender and lava  ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥
♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥ Love is laughter and lamentation ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥
♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥  Love is laurel and lazar  ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥
♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥  Love is lute and lancet  ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥
♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥  Love is laud and loathe  ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥
♥♥♥♥♥♥♥  Love is light and lead  ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥
Love is
♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥  Lecturer  ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥
♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥  and learner  ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥
Love is
♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥  Lord  ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥♥
♥♥♥♥♥♥♥  and  ♥♥♥♥♥♥♥
♥♥♥♥♥  loafer  ♥♥♥♥♥

♥♥♥♥ Love  ♥♥♥♥
♥♥♥♥  is  ♥♥♥♥
♥♥   Lark  ♥♥
♥  Lamb  ♥
♥  Lion  ♥
♥
myra
