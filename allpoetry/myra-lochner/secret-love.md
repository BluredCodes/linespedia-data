HE:

If I kiss you in the dark
would you know its me?
If I sign without my name -
would you yet know me just the same?
If you were blind, across the sea,
to you come in dreamtimes shaded secrecy...
and I kiss you in the dark
would you know it's me?


SHE:

Love which in secrecy declares,
do you consider that as fair?
And words which merely words caress,
would I dare put it to the test?
As poems kiss lips muted by earth,
light opens eyes, blinded by birth.
Thus, I may answer boldly, YES:
I would know you, none the less!

myra & secret admirer
