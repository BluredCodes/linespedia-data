(challenge submission)

I love to moon away the day 
with poem and serenade; 

I love to drape myself in black 
silk brassière and mourning jade; 

I love to turn loud whispers mute 
outwit chaotic illusion; 

I love to smile those sensual tears 
which add to the confusion; 

I love to whip unbridled lust 
and keep passion at bay; 

But... I can't hide my purple pain: 
my pen gives me away ... 


myra
