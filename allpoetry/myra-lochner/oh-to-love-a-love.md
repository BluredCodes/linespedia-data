title: "Oh! to love a  Love"

Oh! 
To sing a fountain
in a holy heart 
to reap a river  
and to send the sound 
of gurgling glee   
to four seasons  

of this Uni-verse! 

Oh!
to hear a sun
in honest eyes
to smell lush fields
in a ready smile
to taste adoration
in a lover's cries

Oh! 
To love a Love 
as clear as this: 
... 
...
to write Oblivion 
with its very own Verse

I 
love 
you
