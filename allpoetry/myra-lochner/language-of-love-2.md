This Labial  XXXXXXX called Love
(At the touch of  Love everyone becomes a Poet – Plato)

Love is lace and lancet   XXXXXXX     Love is light and lead
Love is lavender and lava  X Love X is laughter and tears shed

Love is laureation and lamentation  X  Love is law and lie
XXXXXXX Love is lucidity and lunacy XXXXXXX
Love is the language of lord and lady
Love is the language of the lamb
the language of the lark
the lion

Love is Light
Love
is
Lord
X

myra
