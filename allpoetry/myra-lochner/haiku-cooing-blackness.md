sad cooing choirs
one dove harmonizing trees
veld fire ashes

myraAfter a veld fire (brush fire, as gercko pointed out), I was awakened one morning by a joyous cooing of, what sounds to me as many doves returning.  To my astonishment it was only one dove, filling in for all the others in their absence, while perched on the stump of a burnt tree.
