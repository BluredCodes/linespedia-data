janke was presented
with a silver gift
by Neptune

now he strolls speechless
on the glistening sand dune
mesmerized by this
shimmering silver fish

lifeless and coy
and free

in his hand

this wonderful unblemished fish
unaware of the ecstasy
of a little boy
and of eternity
and decay

will soon
die again
