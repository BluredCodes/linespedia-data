---
title: "Dance of  the Hours"
writer: "Myra Lochner"

ponchielli must have known
something
about the composition
of creature and creation

whilst time is gliding
over seasons
heart's passion
sounds its sharp staccato

the Dance turns timeless
turns da capo

myra
