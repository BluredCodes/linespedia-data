Do not be bored 
by this unpolished Stone 
- this Cosmic Jewel! 

For steelblue Hope 
or the garish canary yellow 
of Tiffany's scope, 
follow this formula 
of Divine accuracy: 

Add to one atom 
of either nitrogen or boron 
one million atoms of carbon 
and the Pure White Crystal 
sparkles color fantasy ... 

Define herafter Femininity 
the Ultimate Fancy 
on the axiom fire-ice: 
see the glistenings of golden sun 
unmeasurable in carat, smiles of fun; 
the silver whispers of the moon 
not appraisable in sterling's swoon. 

So magnificent this Journey 
but oh! so lonely ... 

Warmth against 
the Futuristic Freeze --
far-off Stars within reach:
capturing 
the eyes of God 
set in claws of Gold 

myraColoured diamonds (fancies): the Hope Diamond (steelblue, 1669, Galconda, India) and the Tiffany Diamond (canary yellow, 1878, Kimberley, South Africa). The formula by the Creator is magnificent. It reminds me of the dualism of True Femininity: fire and ice. She wears her jewels with flair. 

I am thrilled being a woman!
