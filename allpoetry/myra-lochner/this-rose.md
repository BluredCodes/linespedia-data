---
title: "This Rose"
writer: "Myra Lochner"
slug: "this-rose"
source: "AllPoetry"

See this royalty; this rose -
queen amongst queens:
this contradiction of emotions
tenderness and passion twirling
around a stem of yearning
and of dreams

See this romance; this rose -
poem amongst poems:
this everlasting love born
amidst the crucifying thorn
listen to her fragrant flute
to the rhythm of her mood

See this promise; this rose -
invitation amongst Invitations:
this secretive bond of souls
which unfolds without motion
which dwells upon the spell
of pure visualization

See this royalty; this rose -
enter into the kingdom
of her heart

myra
