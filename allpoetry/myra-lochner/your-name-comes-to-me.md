(FUN: I wrote this 
when I was 16 years old:
a poem for puppy lovers)



*   ~   *   

Your name comes to me
like a far away song
my Love ... Beloved ... my Love
Your name speaks to me
of a love gone so long
gone like a lost melody

I reach out to you
to grasp but my pain
my Love ... Beloved ... my Love
I cry out to you
all my yearning in vain

our music an ended song

myra

*   ~   *I did not really know what I was writing LOL; actually, I wrote this piece about paper love: my first love, kissed only twice by him (one kiss in an elevator going to the 15th floor!), moved away and left me with all these new feelings!
