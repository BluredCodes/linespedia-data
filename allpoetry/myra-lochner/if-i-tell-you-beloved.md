---
title: "If I tell you, Beloved"
writer: "Myra Lochner"
slug: "if-i-tell-you-beloved"

If I tell you,
Beloved,
that my eyes landscape
a Dream,
perhaps I must first gather
the feelings
fondling the Thoughts
behind these words ...
lest you miss the Heart
of the Scene:

My magic Pen
Precision,
- this precious gift -
sketches powerful 
littoral land swells,
with speechcraft of springtide ...

Sketching sensually,
Beloved,
an exercise in Repetition
of Birth, Death, and again Birth,
Passion and profound Intimacy:

Such Splendour 
capturing a split-second 
of Perfection:

Our Dream

Feel me tremble,
Beloved, 
when I sketch this Landscape
against the backdrop of Expectation;
waves of Rapture 
patiently
await us ...

myra
18.02.2002
