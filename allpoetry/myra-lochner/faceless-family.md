I entered here 
under my own flag 
expectantly, I did not lack 
the honesty, forgiveness and the trust 
that is needed for a family 
also patience, hope 
and endless love 

but oh! 
you treated me like dirt! 
like a prostitute; a shallow flirt - 
you sent me porno, virusses and scams 
you cracked my passwords; hacked my screen 
booted me and called me names 

i did not care 
that my privacy is bared: 
i live according to transparent rules  
but oh! it hurts when family become calculating 
fools playing innocent, but acting 
behind friendly masks 
cowardly; unfair 

these acts of using 
and deliberate abusing 
become unbearably annoying 
cultivating hate i did not know 
i would ever contemplate 

for you 
my forcible faceless family 
this is merely routed fun and toying: 
these ruthless deeds of dark destroying 
you are thoroughly enjoying! 

but beloveds, 
can't you see? you are cracked, too 
within me
