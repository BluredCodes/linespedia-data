As soon as breath the body left 
starts sweet decomposition; 
but on the Last Dream it is fed 
returns for recognition ... 

myra
2002With inversion, the atmosphere of the Olde Worlde is being reconstructed ... I am an incurable chamber poet: oh, but to walk with Keats! Content: lost love/death
