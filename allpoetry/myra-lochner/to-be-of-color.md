---
title: "To be of Color"
writer: "Myra Lochner"
slug: "to-be-of-color"
source: "AllPoetry"

i am a social worker

cultivated into denotation
and connotation
defining group dynamics
by words such as 
"isolate", "rejectee" "neglectee"

but i never knew the meaning
of the phrase
"to be of color"
 
i know about diagnosis
prognosis
modus operandi
abuse reports court cases 
Mother Teresa and Ghandi

but i never knew the meaning
of the phrase
"to be of color"

i look at people through my eyes

withdrawing regressing
coping growing 
fighting raping murdering
dying

but i never knew the meaning
of the phrase
"to be of color"

i hear people with my ears

grumbling denying 
projecting suppressing
scolding defending 
lying offending

but i never knew the meaning
of the phrase 
"to be of color"

i  sense people
with words of wonder:

beautiful sculptures 

formed lovingly 
from precious shades of clay
by a Sensual Artist

for His Grand Finale
