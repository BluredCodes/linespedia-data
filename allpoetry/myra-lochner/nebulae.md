---
title: "Nebulae"

when we go away
we shall return as nebulae
in deeper dimensions of dreams

we shall breathe
our satisfied yearning
through the leaves
of evergreens

we shall bleed
our settled pain
in the red of poinsettia

we shall shed
our tranquil tears
in hyaline streams

when we go away
we shall return
to stay

galaxy of glory

myra
