title: "Silent Scream"

words wafted from morning mist:

look what you have done
seekers of pleasure -
drifting on the frowning tide
morsels of weekend waste


screaming seagulls
feeding
on discarded lust

I turn away
I shall return
when Neptune has cleansed
the day

myra
