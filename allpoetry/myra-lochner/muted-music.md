*
 
(oh, my desperate souls!)

to lose Love completely: 
blocked out from the memory ... 

nothing nothing that remains: 

not the breath of butterflies 
not the bells of silver lies 
not the braille of blinding pain 
not the blue-blue flags 
of Hope's inviting skies 

muted music ... silent cry 
nothing but this inner Death 

nothing 
nothing 

nothing 
left 

myra

*
