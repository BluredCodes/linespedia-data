say: "God"
and all is said

... but unto us shall come 
His Holy Flames, 
like the Swords of the Angels, 
Protectors of the Gates 
of the Forgotten Garden ... 
and that Fire shall cleanse 
us and gild us ... 
and we will be burnt 
into His Shimmering Face ... 


...and from soft flesh 
the heart be ripped 
and planted into Soil 
to grow a Tree 
its branches  reaching 
up to Him ... 
its Blooms be Love 

and there:
at last -
no - not the Shadow ...
no, no: it's Dove ...

myra
