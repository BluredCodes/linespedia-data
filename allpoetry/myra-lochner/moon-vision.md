title: "Moon Vision"

I am a visitor
to this wonderland of words
I use my borrowed cosmic view
dumbfounded
like an eager child

I am a star-gazer
amidst a meteoric shower
leaving my constellation
step by step
nearing Eternity

I am an blind exegete
searching this pageant
of Love
spellbound
by interrelated senses:

I see the music
of Fulfilment

I hear the colour
of Victory

I taste the tactile
deliverance of Truth 

I smell the warm
halo of Hope ...

Moon vision
at the tender tips
of my fingers

myra
