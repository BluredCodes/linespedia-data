Who was waiting here before me?
Waiting for the call 
from convicting Chambers
be it Life or Death?

This chair still carries
the imponderable vibrations
of all invisible souls
nearing that Vast Land
of Dusk ... or Dawn

Oh! I hear a poignant plea:

Mercy is needed -
for both these paths bring pain ...
Mercy ... 
and some Landmark
on this winding journey
through gorges of grief
Courage too - 
if beckoned
by the Raven of the Dark ...

My soul shall not be shrouded
by black cloak!
I thus unshadow my sad eyes
and wish a pair of longer wings

For not a single soul 
can travel very far
in this redeeming Rain of Stars
blinded
or with fettered feet ...

myra
