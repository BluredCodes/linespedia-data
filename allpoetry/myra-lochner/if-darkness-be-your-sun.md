If Darkness be your Sun
and Sun your Darkness
I am crying tears
precious one:

not spilled like pearls
downwards
but projected passionately
upwards ...

Radiant Rays
a tautology of sun

embracing you
within the Sun

myraI am forever crying tears of laughter ...
