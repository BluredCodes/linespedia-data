title: "Words for Walt"

I am written by your poems ... 
see me: i am the gnome 
of your thoughts ... 
slipping away 
with your earthy words
becoming water
and wind
and pliable clay
becoming memory
moon
everlasting day 

I am the tree 
with your evergreen eyes
hearing and smelling
and touching blue skies
I breathe through your leaves
this longing for rest
so carry on
writing 
in quest of the sun

myra 2002We write our universal self with the repetition of wonder ...
