title: "The Fullness of Emptiness"

Even though I feel empty

this Emptiness
embraces me 
like an unfulfilled Lover:

stroking me
with His sacred sorrow

(I understand)

nagging me 
with His neglected needs

(I understand)

decorating me 
with His desolated dreams

(I understand)

Talk to me

from your fullness,
Emptiness!

(I am listening)

Fill my lonely hours
with your longing -

I'll fill you 
with my Being

myra
