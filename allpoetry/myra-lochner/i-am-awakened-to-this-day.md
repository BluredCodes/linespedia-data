I am awakened to this day
wearing my soft satin-smooth
silk-woven Dream

this Dream I wear

spun from the unreachable skies
scaly trees
soft leaves
murmers of water
and laughter of sun

this Dream I wear

woven by senseless
bashful worms
into fragile thread
into musical light
playing my wants

this Dream I wear

stroking my soul
this tender kisses
of prolonged awareness
tracing the lines
of flesh I wear

look!
everything it caresses
turns to gold

until I am shining like a sun

myra
