His delicate fingers trace confessions of centuries
his lips seal secrets of sanctuaries 
his breath pipes legends of lost
the Wind his musical Master
guiding him sensitively
in playing the panflute
playing the themes 
of Love and Life
playing the 
panflute ...
listening ...
playing
the pan-
flute...
.....
myra 
...
01. 
26. 
20021. Of all musical instruments, the panflute is the most hauntingly beautiful sound of sensitivity ... Delicate, and ancient, and beautifully sad.
