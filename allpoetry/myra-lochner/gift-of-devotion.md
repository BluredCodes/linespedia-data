Should I come to you 
by aeroplane 
or ship 
or by foot 
it would be tiresome 
crossing mountains 
oceans 
and continents 

Now I send you instead 
these flowers 
from soft, singing soil: 

white jasmine 
for sweet purity; 
yesterday-today-tomorrow 
shading purple and white 
melancholy and hope; 
impatient in the pink 
for the persistent heart; 

and for an overdose: 

that intense blue billet-doux 
of the potato-plant ... 

I send it 
on silk and silver 
memories
of tenderness 

I show 
my deepest love 
effortlessly
