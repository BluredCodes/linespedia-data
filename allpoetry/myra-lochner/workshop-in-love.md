for Ernest, 
who reminded me that Love 
is a verb

felt-tip fingers verbs of blood
writing riddles, songs and poems
and whispers ... feathering 
naked souls 
tiptoe-ing nevertreaded paths
of doves 
nesting in shadows
and in dreams
