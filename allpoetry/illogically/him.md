she misses him |&|
told him so
he misses her
she's one of his "closest friend"
or so he said
she replied,"yea...friends we'll always be"
the words she really wanted to say
were whispered silently
to herself but not him
"and thats all we'll be"
or so she thinks
a tearcrept down her cheek
as she turned her back
and slowly walked a w a y
words left unspoken
secrets left untold
chances left unknown
