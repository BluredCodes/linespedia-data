just by a random hey and
a friends was what I gained

sure you’re a bit insane
but than so am I 

and when I’m just feeling shitty
or when I wanna cry

you’re right there by my side
always knowing what to do

and for this I thank you
you’re the reason im still here

you’re always somewhere near,
helping me get through my fears

you’re the one setting me free
and always trying to protect me

from all this pain
so for you I try to maintain

but if the worse happens
don’t be sadden

because you still got those memories
and you and me…together forever we shall be
