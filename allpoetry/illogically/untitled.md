time no longer exist
everythin dead frozen
no movement detect
     cold
     still
     silence
aroma of death
floats...spreads
bodies lies scattered
upon the blood-stained ground
moans and cries
are all that's heard
survivors...just moving shadows
in the fogged atmosphere
searching for a breathing soul
---
while the "others" celebrate
their ever-so-happy victory
