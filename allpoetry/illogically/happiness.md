ever heard of the girl who all she craves is a bit of happiness?? 
--
she sits there alone
in that four-walled room
darkness is all that's seen
and is all that's felt

that shiny blade glistens
as the fragile skin get sliced
open it goes...
and the river of red trails

she watch and listens
as it drips and drips
wanting for that moment
when it'll stop completely
(when she'll stop completely)

silence merges
she is no longer watching
nor is she listening
she is truely happy
--
the river of red has stopped flowing
