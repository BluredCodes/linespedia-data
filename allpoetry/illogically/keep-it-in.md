shhhhhhhhh do you hear that??
or is it just me??
open your ears and listen
yes yes i hear it

the mocks and words
the laughter and taunts
clear as a crystal 
those words enters my mind

make no replys
just be quiet
bottled it all in
keep it inside...

----
burst goes something
----

everythin comes pouring out
tears wont stop
screams incessantly
let it all out
