i need you
i love you
--[n/e/e/d | m/e]
--[l/o/v/e | m/e]
i want you
i crave you
--[w/a/n/t | m/e]
--[c/r/a/v/e | m/e]
this burning desire
for you dwells
--[t/o/u/c/h | m/e]
--[h/u/r/t | m/e]
hold me in your arms
kiss me ever so gently
--[s/e/d/u/c/e | m/e]
--[c/a/r/e/s/s | m/e]
i love you
