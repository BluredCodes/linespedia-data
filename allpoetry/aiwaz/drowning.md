title: "drowning"

the sea is heavy around me

but i am weightless in its grip

heroin is the cold around me

as the currents surge and shift...

i share my breath with the fishes

pearls in glowing swirls

from my lips to them

as my body twists the silt...

to a diver of the deeps

death comes in a rapture

of the slow endless drift

of watery dreams

to the heavy black hereafter...
