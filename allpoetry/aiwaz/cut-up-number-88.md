melt banana zipper spy
flip and hit
the shiny bright splat, 
and the 4:04 pm daydreams
free all the bees
or at least an eyeball
lost in mirror...
squeaky hinges
speak and spell code
bumble bee beats
fom hard funk verbs
new ground
for the warp, 
backspin the icky shiny
herds...props to the chics that rock
