---
title: "if and then?"
writer: "aiwaz"
slug: "if-and-then"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/39290-if-and-then--by-aiwaz"
---

magnesium shock
chakra salt
crescent scar
tattered drifts
of drum skin thrifts
each of us has
our own wind
to cast our seed
at a whim
like a thought
deflected from
more sombre dreams
of windless scorching
summmer droughts
of fire catastrophes
each horizon each night
smoking within, without
ravenous thirst
for childish firsts
and victorys without
doubts?
