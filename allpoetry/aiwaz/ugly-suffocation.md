---
title: "ugly suffocation"
writer: "aiwaz"

you breathe the air of my words
but dont know it
i suffocate you softly
unseen inside yr head
i see you when yr ugly
i see you when you cut yr nails
and dream of other men

i feel you in my ribs
gnawing, devouring
eating me inside out
you scream with my breath
weep with my blood
but i wont let you surface inside of me
down, down
deeper down
ill hold you til you drown
hold you til yr voice is gone
scream now without a sound...

you are my fish in a bowl
you circle yr dreary life
as i watch you from abovethis is not about me!
