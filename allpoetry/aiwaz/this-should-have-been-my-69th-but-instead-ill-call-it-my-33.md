vien ici ma cheri
come to me slinky smooth
slippery silk
of feline moves
come to me for yr milk
let me nuzzle yr ear
for you my affections i can not bare
help me lighten the load
come to me and let me smooth yr fur
in you i see a glimpse of dream
that is the first step on our road
vien ici ma belle
j'ai jamais penser ca peut etre comme ca
vien ici ma minou vien a mois comme une chathehehe...beg...hehe
