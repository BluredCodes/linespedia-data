title: "flood me every night"
writer: "aiwaz"

she moves to him like a monsoon storm
and takes him softly in her her hands
trembling with unforgiven yearning
of stinging embers, blood bite kiss
he trickles hotly in her mouth
as she folds him into the flesh of her lips
and pulls her down her ladder
rung by rung
frantic feast of tooth and tongue
inch by quivering inch
surging sigh by screaming sigh
for liquid sex tropical storms
when theres nowhere left
...to hide...
