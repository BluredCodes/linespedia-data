junkyard alabama adolfscents
with special guests
                 capone and altman
werewolf of washington flesh feast inferno
      blood inquisition distortion
of royal albert descendents
of christian
            death
                 kennedys
accused of acursed liberty-

matando guerros
        a priest called to exorcise
a dishwasher that
         bleeds and laughs
   black grave straw dogs
buried alive after death....

beware of lust and terror
       the xploited and crippled by society
the day the earth stood still
       degenerate flipper dwarves
of desperate living
        danced in and smashed the door...

spooky xorcismo
                santo tigresa negro
suspira y la kong
                 the brood of howling gore
hot as fresh death
                  are scratching through 
the walls

of haunted hillbilly death curse freaks
who dance tarrantula
          pygmy circus sleep
and pink flamingo insanity
of opium composers
and thrill kill killers
          of blood and lace...

el attaque de los muertos sin ojos
le vampire noir
           who came to ride the wild pink horse
of government issue murder by hatchet
            in shocking colour
at the gates
            of a germ religion


please xcuse this cryptic brain massacre
but i see these words everydayfor the punx
