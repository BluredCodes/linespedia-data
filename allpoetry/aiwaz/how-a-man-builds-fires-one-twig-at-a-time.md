title: "how a man builds fires, one twig at a time"

trestle snakes of the weight of light
smoke nozzles the wings of a moth
the sun flies screaming into night
like a midnight flood of fire
echos of hard steps linger in the mosque

hurricanes wither the heart's loss
the constellations cuts the sky in half
cinders and crickets in the bell tower
send all the spiders running
swarming in frantic mobs
as apples fall from trees
to the ocean moans darkly blue
where crabs nest in yellowing skulls

the gulls all flee from here
the lighthouse never cuts this fog
soon the moon will rise
cast patterns on yr eyes
and winds like the breath of god
sing yr name whistling
despite the dry heat of my thoughts

the tarantulas 
dance with saints again
a bat flies drunkenly into mirrors tonight
woodsmoke lingers with a wisp of dawn
rain down heavy pollen like meteors
from my flowering thoughts of you
and the ache you carry 
from another weary fight

clouds make love on this barren land
the seeds of our souls
make green desert sands
and orchards bloom 
granite doldrums of smoldering sighs
frogs drone the end of night
i watched a snail crawl across
a straight razor
like an assassin stalking assassins
from the valley the echos of evil laughs
where crocodiles rip babies in half
and chew them languidly like pears
and the drums and pipes 
of pan tear tears 
off the face of children
like the smoke of fires
from the tents of a wandering tribe

one more cry before 
my heart is swallowed
by all the hours between us
uncounted
both our hands reaching for something

all these thoughts dance in veils
to keep me from howling tonight
bite down on an olive branch
i tried not to think of this
i sat down over the poppy fields
and fell into a half dream
of flowers in bloom
and raging jungles of flame
despite the turbulence
of scattered darkness
and whispered vows 
i will never forget
promises made are promises kept
in case i wasnt clear
i wont ever leave you
i cant ever leave you
i dont even try
our fate has already been set
