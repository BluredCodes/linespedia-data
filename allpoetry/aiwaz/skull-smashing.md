---
title: "skull smashing"
writer: "aiwaz"
slug: "skull-smashing"
source: "AllPoetry"

dig yr heels into me
my skull is fresh lettuce in yr hands
tear me open
let me beg for yr demands
kiss me with yr razor lips
fuk me with crack and lash
wear my skin
as yr bleeding disguise
rapture comes
when my wounds open
blossoming
salvation flows
with bloody rhymes
