infrared roses
of galaxy blooms
constellation flowers
ultraviolet moons

astronomy infinity
invisible eyes
in microwave wombs
blink for hours
in morse code our echos
yr thoughts
in waves consume me
x rays shiver 
from me to you

my heart flickers 
radio red to blue
radiation in a spectrum
you reflect my frequency
and now i laugh
like just having heard
yr smile 
from the centre 
of all my galaxies
