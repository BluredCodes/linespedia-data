talking to you
im a kitten chasing
a butterfly in the grass
right before i catch
yr fluttering wings
in my fluttering jaws
you say something else
and im a child
with a kite on a string
runnning, running,
trying to catch up
and laughing all the way
but then you say...
and yr not a kite
and yr the sun rising
in a glorious day
and im an old man
who burns his eyes out
as you shine down on me
today...dedicated to all internet lovers..
