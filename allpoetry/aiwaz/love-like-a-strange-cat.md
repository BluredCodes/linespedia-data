title: "love like a strange cat"

love brushs softly
in a dream
like a cat
rain dances on the roof
every light is out
the floors are creaking
like mice
and the mice are creaking
like floors
a late night movie
is flickering
as i exhausted
from a day of yr beauty
fall asleep
with my head in yr lap
hands warm between yr knees
as you let yr mind wander
and trace circles on my cheek
