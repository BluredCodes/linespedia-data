---
title: "funeral sex"
writer: "aiwaz"
slug: "funeral-sex"

you crush my lips

like a spent cigarette

yr tongue is a cherry flame

that flicks ashes on my breath-

yr nails dig like shovels

and leaves graves

to bury what i can not forget-

flesh to flesh

sweat to sweat

the solemnity of funeral sex-

you were my love

now yr a scar on my back

that i cant even see any

more...
