the ceiling
is literaly
caving in
i hear dead
draggy steps
someone watches
the sleepin guests
and fogs the windows
between us
with his clouds of
steaming breath.
footsteps in the snow
long deep strides
from our door
to the road
and the great intermingling of lives.
