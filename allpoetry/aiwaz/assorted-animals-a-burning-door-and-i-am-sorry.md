yr words are hyenas
snickering verses among the tombs
no, from you
is the hard boot
echoing against
the groan of the oak
of the door slaming shut-
yes, is the crack of kindling
broken door
burning in the hearth-
maybe, unfurls like a cape
if this keeps up
i just might stop
until turtles rain down like sheep.
