dark as gunpowder

cold as stainless steele

silent like consolation

as quiet as i feel

torn like violent seizing

dirty as an old dollar bill

innocent as tiny palms

bleeding from the kill

we kamikazee pilots 

go diving for the veins

the glory of our sunsets

is drowned in deep blue pain
