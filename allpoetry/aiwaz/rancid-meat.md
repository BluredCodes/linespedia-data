title: "rancid meat"
writer: "aiwaz"
slug: "rancid-meat"

butcher sex 
lick the cum from yr blade
carve me like meat 
to bleed in the shade
hang me to dry 
rot in the darkness of yr thighs
feed me to starving mouths
chew me raw with yr bleeding sighs
from this hook 
my body hangs naked and cold
boil me in soup
salt my meat in yr hole
