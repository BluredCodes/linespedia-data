if i sit still
all will pass beyond me
move me
or move right through
but let me move with you tonight.
let me feel yr breath in my ear
spill yr blood in whispers
trace yr heart with yr tongue on my palm
let me taste the sweat of yr fear.
hold me when the dark comes howling.
boiling suck of molten sex
lava virgin
volcano slave
priestess of fire breath
sacrifice yrself to me.
come to me naked and pure
lie down before me
like a new world
to conquor and burn.
come to me
yr body is not yr own
it lies here sanctified
by painted charms of blood and cum.
come to me
yr secrets are my prayers
yr curses are the holy rhymes
kneel for me
the fire of yr body trembling
are the psalms of yr ressurection.
burn for me
cry out like a whores choir
beg for yr impalement
desire redemption by fire.
