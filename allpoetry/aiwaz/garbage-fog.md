title: "garbage fog"

merchandise of flesh and bone
styro foam conscience
of exasperated heaps 
sacrcastic neon halo
drunks of hangover faith
and yellow skin.
rusted over unused eyes
meat rotting on the hook
i took two steps forward
and ended up
somewhere on the left
where the machines grind down
mangled gears
of prosperity chance
in an elephant graveyard
of twisted metal
gleaming flesh
decaying gore
of cancer sores
that weep into this fog
that hangs over my house
like an uninvited guest.
