title: "halloween is every day"
writer: "aiwaz"
slug: "halloween-is-every-day"
source: "AllPoetry"

perfume like memory
of narcotic skin, 
firecracker eyes,
seven veiled smoke
penis hallucinating prey
starvation reverberation
of tibet bass,
painted mud,
oak mask,
it is to you
i scarifice this face.
primative ego
jack o lanterns
pumpkin skulls
of glaring, burning
smiles distorted,
shadow contortions
all catch flames
to warm yr hands
and light yr way
as the world beyond
petrifies
to flat ash gray...

every sacrifice begins with a dance
first step
          anguished tearing
second step
          ego blaring
third step
          is none at all
fires rage where my feet left off...
