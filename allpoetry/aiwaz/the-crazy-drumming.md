---
title: "the crazy drumming"
writer: "aiwaz"
slug: "the-crazy-drumming"
source: "AllPoetry"

adrenalin queen of deep reaction
summon thee all the crazy drummings
of my swollen heart of trouble.
a simple merging of flesh
the salty trickle of love
amongst two 
sharing one body
in the furnace of  sex
incinerating the miles
between us.. 
poetry is our sex
we tickle our hearts
with lovers grace
rhyme verses
with trembling tongues
until yr almost
here....

    we would light the world on fire....of course its for you...
