---
title: "sunday morning diner"
writer: "aiwaz"
slug: "sunday-morning-diner"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/39949-sunday-morning-diner-by-aiwaz"

i want to lie with you
sleeping tangled flesh
awake you on a sunday morning
duvet shelter of cuddled bliss
bite yr nose
lick yr ear
tickle yr neck
til yr a giggling mess
so that on monday you could say
sunday was a feast
and not just a taste
