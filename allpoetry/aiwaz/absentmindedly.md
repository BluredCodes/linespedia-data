instant pornography
she spells her name with her hips
as she crosses the room
my mind cums at the thought of her
the flat of her stomach
the tender fist of her womb.
pull me inside of you
dangle me from the tip of yr tongue
let me trace the taste of yr tooth
paint my fire on yr belly
smear me into yr skin
think of me
as i do
absentmindedly.
