under the hoofs 
of this unsteady phantom
i see you amongst the
rogues of meth amphetamine lanterns
i can see you spinning round
eyes closed 
feet 3 inches off the ground
through narcotic fog 
and angel smog
twitch my brain, 
with firecracker thoughts
my third eye burns
where spirits lock
electric overload
dancing strobes 
this distance implodes
as i fall into my hole
dug into the neurons
of this wrongly wired mind
psychic fits our hearts entwine
for an episode i am in yr skin
as round and round the dancefloor you spin
