my feet locked in stone
acid kicked in
im caught wading, daring,
during
the instant petrification
of an ocean...
amid short bursts of suffocation
a rattling in my lungs
as if of weapons
rolling their chambers to fire
the acid ignites
and plagues my calm resolve
and enflames my kindling mind
with a bassnarcotic call
madness strangled
this bird in my palm
that slept wreathed in flowers
in the nest of my hand
suffocate these patient designs
resistance means widerstand
stunt rock bleeds for our sins tonight
make way for the junk beat voloptuaries
and austrian digital fuck gimps
hecate will eat nazis tonight
abelcain and his tornado of halos
a doormouse beckons the start of night
let the sign of our coming
be wisconsin mud
blood beer and cum
of yellow nausea refugees
who duck from the hideous sight
of the all powerful all seeing
medusa eye
that blinks only at its own 
wretched lie
but somehow
through this venetian snare
and a scottish ass
in this run down barn
in old east troy
it just feels 
like we might have
a chance tonightfor my austrian friend
