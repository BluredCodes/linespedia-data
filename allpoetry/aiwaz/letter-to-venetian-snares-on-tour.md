in order to get yr cats to come up stairs i threw on "songs about my cats"
hoping that the beats would remind them of you....it worked...each cat came
upstairs when their song was playing. chinaski came in belly dancing shaking
maracas when he heard his song, kakarooki was a little shy but she did
little tap routine bowed elegantly the way a feline should....fluf
couldnt wait to dance, she was jennifer beal in flashdance, like a
maniac with lights and smoke.....bobo came late, like it was no big deal but made up for it with
this psychotic tango, complete with a rose in his whiskers and a broken
bottle in his drunken paw....its all true even if it isnt...
                                                     pattaking care of the kitties while their dad is rocking the shit out of europe
