---
title: "yr softest fur"
writer: "aiwaz"
slug: "yr-softest-fur"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/47900-yr-softest-fur-by-aiwaz"

i seen frogs 
dancing drunkenly
but they were too sober
to satisfy me
hop to me bunny rabbit
i like it
when
you shake yr tail
i need to pet
yr softest fur
let me burrow
with you
tonight
