baba yaga
was the hell born on an undigested apple pit
treebark hands wither leave cloak
children follow with bloody hands
come for a bleeding
this house was burnt to the ground 
pitchfork courage
a century past
she leaves no tracks in the ash
come into the woods
children hang
like fruit on the vine
rotting in cold blood
upside down in parody
