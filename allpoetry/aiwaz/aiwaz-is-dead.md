this is just a note to say im retiring, no more posts for me. thank you for yr comments and friendship but ive decided that my art is too precious for me to commit to mass consumption...i have become too concerned or flattered by the opinions of strangers, it has inflated my ego and my work has suffered. my self esteem must be derived from myself and the one i love with all my heart, i cheapen myself when i share my love like a whore to anyone who passes my way....i know what matters now...thank you
