i found this rug 
in a cubist castle 
of spanish
orange blooms
its a persian rug
from the fifteenth century
her silk threads were woven
by anais nin
born before
as the caliph whore
i found this rug
or rather 
it found me
the mountain comes 
to mohammud
threaded in orange
red and royal blue
reserved for 
the safe return 
of wild eyed 
desert heretics
their skulls cleaned
and used as bowls
to console their 
grieving families
