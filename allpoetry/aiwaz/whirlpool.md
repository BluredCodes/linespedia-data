my soul tonight is as a whirlpool
churning the sea into clouds of salt white foam.
swirling about the edge of abyss
a ship now approaches
notice its broken mast
it sails torn and tattered
its course fixed on the appetite
that feeds the greed of the jaws
of void.
sailors now line the decks
and utter prayers under curses
the circular motion has already begun
into the heart of the ocean.
