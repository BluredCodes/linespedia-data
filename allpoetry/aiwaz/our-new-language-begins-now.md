title: "our new language begins now"

let us find a new language
there are no words that i could say
to tell you how i feel today
let our love be the new dictionary
each word i say reminds me of you
and how they fail to say what is true
all word are spent on cheap emotions
whisper a new one, only i can hear
come close and kiss it to my ear
iravu ivaru iravu ivaru iravu ivaru
my meager eloquence can not define
drink these words deep, with them you
are mine...
