every wickeday evening
at lightning up o clock
when buzzing circuits whir and pray
and summon ghosts  of heartbeat dubbing
and all yr world is a bird
and all yr words its feathers
cast out yr liquick music call
peel me with yr letters
a thread of vision
bound and knot
entwined        remind
        expertly
