i was the tree singing
in the woods
chopped down by chainsaw
one afternoon
dragged and hauled to the stinking mill
cut into planks
with uniform flanks
and sold to the store on the hill
bought by a carpenter
with sure strong hands
lovingly cut shaped and polished 
painted with orange and blue
pure varnish
carved into the horse 
that you became
back and forth
back and forth
rocking my wood
as i squeak my hearts wish
