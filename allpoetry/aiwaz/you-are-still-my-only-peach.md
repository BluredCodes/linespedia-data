Sent: Dec 07, 2001 - 05:37 PM  





i can not keep up with you tonight

you the face that left its mirror

to prowl the woods

with ash grey wolves

for tender trembling deer-



how then can i keep up yr pace?

with each word, each stride,

monuments collide

like yr arched lips

moist for sweet embrace-



what trees stand over you?

ive seen them bend and bow

and lay themselves down

for the love of yr pens grace-



if all i am is words on yr screen

how then can i express

the wonder in yr words ive seen

of a darkening beauty i can only guess-



you offer me concrete dandelions

and they feast on the bones that i lay

at yr feet with mute humility

their flesh stripped by the words you say-



instead of clouds of ragged dreams

or words that tremble, beg, beseech,

its better far,

with dumbstruck tongue

simply to give you a peach.
