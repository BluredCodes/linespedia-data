---
title: "sometimes hello is enough"
writer: "aiwaz"
slug: "sometimes-hello-is-enough"

clocks run backwards

as you enter

the furniture

orbits you

around the room

every other

voice is lost

in a hurricane howl

when you come

to say

hello



towers

are built

stone by

stone

but

are

toppled

with

a single

clap

things are always changing

sometimes just like that...
