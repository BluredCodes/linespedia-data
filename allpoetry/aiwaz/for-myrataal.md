the buddha said:
           if you seek the teaching of a thief
           you will recieve the blessings of a thief
           if you seek in the heart of an honest soul
           you will recieve the blessings of an honest soul
           if you seek truth in a master
           so ye shall nourish from his mind
           but if you see the buddha and seek the ways
           so ye shall be blessed by a buddha
           for all yr twisting days....
you never know where the rain will fall or the sun will shine
but i have seen the beauty of the darkness around by the light
of yr soul and the words you entwine...
bless me buddha of african rose
awake me with yr grey wisdom
conjur in me dawns of gold
and twilight poetry
of ancient rhythms
