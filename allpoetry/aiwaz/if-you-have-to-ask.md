---
title: "if you have to ask"
writer: "aiwaz"
slug: "if-you-have-to-ask"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/39935-if-you-have-to-ask-by-aiwaz"

quiet say nothing
let yr space be yr breath
    words tire at the marathon wire
   and crash in blind despair
say nothing, bite yr tongue til it bleeds
you weild it
            like a hammer of lies
pound each one
             as a nail in yr hand
and one for each of yr
                      eyes
camoflage introspection
stuttering yr feeble rejection
the neon wild
of yellow nausea
ego mirrors
           of opaque poets
observation
           of no reflection.
