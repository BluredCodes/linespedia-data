---
title: "heiroglyphs"
writer: "aiwaz"
slug: "heiroglyphs"
source: "AllPoetry"

cleopatra has blessed us
tender face
      flower heart

do you burn like  coal
buried in sand
or do we  plant our fire
        orange flower
on rocky ashen land
impossible power
flower face
here we grow
        our future in 
heiroglyphs
        two cups full
or one cup shattered
spilling, spilling
        royal blessings
what the queen giveth
the queen can taketh away....
a black asp smiles 
as they approach

in the temple of the cat
one starving bull
feasts on one fat calf
a scorpion dances 
       on the grave of kings
here we lie blessed
       trembling in fever
 our redemption stings

here we will grow, here we will wither
a dark cloud rolls
perhaps a change in weather...
