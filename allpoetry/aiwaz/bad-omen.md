---
title: "bad omen"
writer: "aiwaz"
slug: "bad-omen"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/39265-bad-omen-by-aiwaz"
---

yellow sulphur rot of fevered words
like a black fly halo round yr putrid skull
polished horn of sneering burning
eyes removed with corkscrew thumbs
from feast of worm,
corpse of nothing
yr laughter like a dead eye stare
cactus hands of longest drought
may the wheel leave you where
hollowed and scattered
withered and tattered
yr scarecrow soul
stands decayed
before all you ever shattered...
