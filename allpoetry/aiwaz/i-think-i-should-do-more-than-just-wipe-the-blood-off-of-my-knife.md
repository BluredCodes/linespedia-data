my love has black hair
i know it because 
i have felt in my hands
let it hang from my fingertips
felt her naked toe on mine
like two snakes we wind
i have felt her heart beat
i know it because 
i slept with my ear to her breast
drum me softly to dreams
i feel her in my skin
i know it
i have her perfume on me still
i bear her scratches on my back and neck
i know it so much id turn around 
if i could figure out
something better i could say
         im sorry i understand
i see painfuly suddenly
that which you could not say
that which i should have seen
i know my love is blind
i cant see through a veil of tears
realization comes suddenly
with bladed cuts
that bleeds
of failures of a friend.
