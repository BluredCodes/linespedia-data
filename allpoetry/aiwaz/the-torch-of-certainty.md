---
title: "the torch of certainty"
writer: "aiwaz"

the torch of certainty, 
lamp of burning tender oil
shines down on me 
to melt this mortal coil
bathe yrself, my love, 
let its light give life to yr eyes
to banish darkness, fear and doubt, 
give blood to realize
from this sphere of light, 
i see the depths of darkness beyond
from this veil i hear the echos of our laughter song
i feel the warmth that melts glaciers 
and oceans flood our shore
this light is you my love 
and you carry it with you for ever more
