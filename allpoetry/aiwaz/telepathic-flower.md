telepathic flower
spy with nectar honey
and leaves of erotic dreams
that sap you each night
dark hair swirling
leather hardened in the grip
garden shears
to the stems fine hairs
bright leaves ripen and fall
a rosebud
blossoms with blood
black soil sex weed
tangled roots
in potted mud.
