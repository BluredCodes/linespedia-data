tear a piece of the sky like kleenex
stripmine the the green grass for coal
smoke, smoke, forests burn
blue becomes grey like the clouds
that hang in permanent eminence
of sudden drunk
black quick
ligntning strikes
thunderclaps boom constantly
in the furnace burning rooms
black coal red to fire
another mold of melting steele
and a crane circles endlessly
with nowhere close to land...
