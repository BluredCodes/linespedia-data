---
title: "everything is true, nothing is forbidden"
writer: "aiwaz"
slug: "everything-is-true-nothing-is-forbidden"
source: "AllPoetry"

nothing is true
everything is forbidden
ive seen the face of the prophet
reflected in the dervish blade
who whirls away darkness and doubt
nothing is true
everything is permitted
i hear the voice of all in the drums
that which can not be departed from
anything is true
nothing is forgiven
i bathe my sins away
with giant handfuls of sand
everything is true
everything is forbidden
i have built temples
so children would understand
i have come to flocks of shepherds
trembling like a lamb
i have been wriggling in nets
bewitched by fishermans lines
i was born of a diamond lotus
that once was coal
burning in my mind
everything is true
nothing is forbidden
not now, not ever
read this now and understand
the blessing has been given...
