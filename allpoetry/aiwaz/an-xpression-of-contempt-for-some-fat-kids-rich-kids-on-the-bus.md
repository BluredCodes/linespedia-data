i saw them boarding their ships
built by slaves in enslaved lands
while they kiss champagne with their lips
i followed their traffic stream
head raised like a periscope
submarine mind of arctic stealth
ice blue tears of drowning hope
on the surface
on which their conversation steams
i leave no wake
sports weather political farce
no light is lit
but i let my torpedos surge
to target their vessels
of self adulation
and well fed comfort
to sink their certainty
until lost and forgotten
a moss eaten carcass
of their fat pink bellys
that digest the withered bones
of all these crying fish
sink now or sink later
the luck of yr birth 
is not yrs to keep
i will marvel at the beauty
of yr ships going down
scuttled on the floor
of the ocean deepwhatever, half baked idea..but im stuck right now, so this will have to do until i get a good idea
