title: "dostoyevsky idiot riddles"

i carry the weight of my world in my jaw
each word smothered strangled in my paw
each letter gnash tooth gnaw
chew me small swallow me raw
cancer of my inky claw
demonic engines
of backwoods crossroad
scratch thorns
tangled nerves woven
burning fuse tripwires
landmines seed 
the soil of my screams
each step blossoms fires
and blows winds to
this twitching behind my eyes
spasms of dostoyevsky idiot 
riddles
elecrical chemical impulse shivers
searing chasms of blinding light
give me caeser bleeding on the senate floor
let me bite down on the moon
see it hang in the blueminous gel
worn and rusted like a burnt out spoon
give me a whole set of bones to hang
let me chew each one to a nub
lightning bottles 
of egg yolk eyes
that crack and fry inside my skull
peel my skin off like a luxury grape
feed me my flesh, make me chew it slowly
i carry the weight of my world in my jaw
