sleep napoleon
 history needs yr corpse
 for the morgue of poetry
 to pull yr 
 b o n e s a p a r t
 and bottle yr organs
 for creaking shelves
 heavy with a million hearts
 the crops of yr greatness
 was planted with severed hands
 harvested with reaper smoke
 from this frozen barren land
 yr granite tomb
 is sealed with ice
 condensed moisture from 
 the dying breaths
 mathematic holocaust
  stone garden
 reminds us
 to forgetbonus points for whoever can place the quote i used for the title
