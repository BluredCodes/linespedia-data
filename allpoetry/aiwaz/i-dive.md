---
title: "i dive"
writer: "aiwaz"
slug: "i-dive"

you dive
   yr body is the perfect arc
arch yr back for me
i plunge deeper 
when you scratch for dark
   hold onto that moment
     suspended weightless 
in mid air
    half a breath
  from the surface
i feel the tips of yr hair
yr eyes widen
in fraction of a second
   i will touch you
 gravity pulls me into you
    in a moment i will swim you...

sweet ocean i never fall
i dive
when i hear you whimper
yr organ call
