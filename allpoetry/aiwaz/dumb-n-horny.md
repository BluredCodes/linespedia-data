---
title: "dumb n horny"
writer: "aiwaz"
slug: "dumb-n-horny"
source: "AllPoetry"

i like the long slow grind of swivelling hips
i like spending hours sleeping in yr lips
i like quenching myself on yr secret juice
i like yr scream when you have nothing left to lose
drip
    drip
        drip

             please one more trip

lips
   lips
      lips

            i love it when i 

slip
    slip
        slip

into yr hipslipsdrips
