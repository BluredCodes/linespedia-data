title: "crane at the hearts well"
writer: "aiwaz"

deep in a well

where the blind fish swim 

i found a song

echoing from the black with in

i caught the fish that sang it clear

silver and smooth

and flipping with fear-

you are a crane she says

and i am the fish of yr beak

i know but its not like that

i says,

i wont break yr neck,

or spill yr blood

i live to hear you speak.

all these fish

with their cold dead eyes,

ill let swim to their dreary lives

if only once you sing that song 

that baited my hearts sigh...

with these words i opened my beak 

and down to the drink she fell.

as she swam away i thought

of feathers and scales

and the foolish depths

this hearts well.
