indigo sky
of soapy bubbles
e x t e n d e d
trees of math dreams
line the river miles
bridges of hands 
e x t e n d e d
spiral out from this point
you know where you are 
because
of where you are not
abscence sets the stage
for lovers drama
with all hearts props
sugar glass smash
ritual fights are fought
pain of without
not within
you hold all of my heart
