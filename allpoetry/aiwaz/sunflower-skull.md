title: "sunflower skull"
writer: "aiwaz"

drifting through the sunflowers
they bow beneath my outstretched hands
as i pass over a dirty skull
of a long forgotten man-
careless stumble
these stone keep my upright
bones washed clear
of trembling fear
tarpits of the longest night
forget nothing
in these fields yr memories grow
love forlorn and tangled
and arranged neatly row by row-
anger lust and hatred
motions that once seemed so violent
now laid out
in vegetable calm
green and gold enlighted-
oh so greedy at birth
generous in death
yr dreams now seeds
cracked under my tooth
and chewed slowly to rest
if all you ever were
was moment of mine
to slowly digest
than let them build
a monument
and let none
yr fruitful life contest...
