---
title: "taxadermy"
writer: "aiwaz"
slug: "taxadermy"

paint his white cheeks rouge
slip pennies under his eyes
fold his waxy flesh to a smirk
bestow peace like a swarm of flies
pinned like a frog in a lab
entrails carefully removed
catalogued and weighed
discarded as trash
and stuffed with a pink balloon
