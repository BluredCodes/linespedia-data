the day has turned inside out
   the plants lay tangled in clouds
the fountain is cracked and nauseated
   and the candle lights itself.
corpses shame the graveyard
   fresh ash chokes the tombstones
the fathers prayer breaks into song
   but stangely out of tune.
             the 
birds refuse to flap their wings
   and fall like bombs from the sky
while burrowing moles 
   leap from the cliffs
in a pathetic attempt to fly-
   the deere feed on the wolves
foxes offer themselves
   to a pack of pecking hens
that lay eggs with no shells-
   the cat lays still
     as the mice feed
       on his belly
         pink
alone in the den
     the snake fed up with his
        image of sin
gets up and walks away...
the sun cooks the rotting fruit
   balls bounce with no children in sight
good old boy salt of the earth
  cowboys are found
             naked, hogtied
             to their land humiliated
             barbwire corpses
of rapists who blow whistles
in hysterical panic of
             masked women who stalk
  dark alleys with knives
           casterate slice to the fists in life
but bleed milk so the little bits grow.dedicated to all men who  try to crush their women...predators will be prey
