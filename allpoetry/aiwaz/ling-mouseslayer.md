---
title: "ling, mouseslayer"
writer: "aiwaz"
slug: "ling-mouseslayer"
source: "AllPoetry"

a starving mouse
is crawling through the ceiling
a tiny cat
sits by the hole and waits
jaws quivering, tail swishing
eyes wide open for the kill
the mouse is ready to drop
like a paratropper
the cat waits on the highest shelf
til his body wriggling
half way between here
and there
dangles like fruit on a tree
to be plucked-
for a split second-
the mouse catches a scent
scurries to retreat
but awkwardly
and he dies afraid, aware
severed in half
empty belly spilling
mangled into a kind of divine gore
reserved for artists
with burning minds
