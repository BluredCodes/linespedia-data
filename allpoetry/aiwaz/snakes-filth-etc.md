title: "snakes filth etc"

yr heart is a nest
with snake trax all around
there are no eggs
just cracked pieces
of shell lying on the ground
yr mind is like a sewer pipe
weeping fumes
and smoking filth
you pollute the purest springs
to relieve the stench within
keep yr uglieness to yrself
we have enough already
consume yrself with that
you create
and let others wall yr tomb
cold black alligator blood
ideas drowned in mud
burning flesh of finer things
like blossom bruise
of silence sin...
