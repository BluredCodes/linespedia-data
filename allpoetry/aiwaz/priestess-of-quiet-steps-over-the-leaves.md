title: "priestess of quiet steps over the leaves"

words so sinister
meaning half inflected
boiled tender
in a cauldron
of alchemical rhymes
that warn me like the
fat men skinned pink hanging
from a tree in the bruise of night
a tiger devoured my parents
and made dessert of my
childish mind
and i said
i like following
yr tracks through
the woods
but i never thought
it would lead to this
and she said with a spoon
in her mouth
the best traps
are always
baited with a kiss
