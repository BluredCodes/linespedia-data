---
title: "naught but what"
writer: "Enraptured"
slug: "naught-but-what"
source: "AllPoetry"

a feigned desire.

naught

yet true and undecided. 

waves of truth
and hidden again.

comings and goings

forever left to wander

Ask me again when I have a breath to spare

To return and feel the gift you give with every word.

To just enjoy from afar.

Fear of that departure.
Indecision 

if you would be so kind as to 
just tell me the answers.

now perhaps so the time doesn't have to go away

Have I permission to just be

To embrace, without a kiss.
To hold dear
the gentle warmth 
you bring
to my hearthyes hearth. not heart
