title: "passed days to come"

Be ready strike this
and writ and write and wrote indeed

But of what
of whom 

and time and space.
and love and lust.

Comings and goings
and here and there, 
then and now and then again.

Of what?

When will it be known? And clear?

Inpatience takes its gentle toll 
it holds firm
and makes its presence known


And of our sweet passed days, of blessed things to come?
What then,
and how.
