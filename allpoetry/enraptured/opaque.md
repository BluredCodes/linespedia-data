And as I walk, my thoughts remain
And as I breath, my thoughts remain
And as I sleep, my dreams remain
Unclear.

Unclear this hope.
Unclear this desire.
Unclear this need.
Unburnt this fire.

And as my days fade into night
And as my body refrains from light
And as my soul searches this life

My needs remain, 

Unclear.Here is an older one...
