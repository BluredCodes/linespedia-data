---
title: "to reveal at once"
writer: "Enraptured"
slug: "to-reveal-at-once"

To look one does not always see
But to find oneself putting down that cracking mask

Truly unusual

To want and share and know

There is so much to be revealed and I want it all out
To remove all hesitation
 And remain
   Comfort.
