---
title: "Contradiction in terms"
writer: "Enraptured"
slug: "contradiction-in-terms"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/32140-Contradiction-in-terms-by-Enraptured"

These words can mean nothing but their definitions.
And yet,
Strung together on this page
They hold a meaning, 
Bring a thought,
Envoke a response.
