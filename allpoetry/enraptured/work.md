title: "Work"
writer: "Enraptured"
slug: "work"
source: "AllPoetry"

Work.
Last day.
Off.
Work.
Three days.
Soon.
Long drive.
Five hours.
Away.
Friday.
Saturday.
Home Sunday.
I know.
Work. 
Four o’clock.
Oh well.
Sleep soon.
Dance.
Eight o’clock.
No work.
Good.
