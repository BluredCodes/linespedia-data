---
title: "Just a passing thought"
writer: "Enraptured"
slug: "just-a-passing-thought"
source: "AllPoetry"

I wait with bated breath 
to hear the next whisper 
of your ever so pleasant voice 
upon my ears.

Your breath upon my neck.
The thought still lingers.

The hairs prick even as I write these lines.

Duality.
Always within me.
