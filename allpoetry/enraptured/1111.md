It's just a time
    Why do I assign
       Such meaning
 Can it stand as a sign
   In a rhyme
     A time
       A Line
     But why, does this line in time assign 
   So much to me.
 It is november
   On Sunday it will be the day.
 The eleventh month
 The eleventh day
 The eleventh hour
 The eleventh minute
      I will count the seconds
         All will be alligned.
             Then I will ask for my sign.See, 11:11 is a time of day that I am obsessed with. I always watch my clock for that time to come around. It means something to me that I can't explain...
