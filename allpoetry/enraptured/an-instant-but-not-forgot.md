title: "an instant, but not forgot"
writer: "Enraptured"

But where then?

So often here and taken for granted.
And suddenly before I have noticed, it has been days.
An instant.

As she and I wonder. 
Why and where?

Did you go.
How are the depths...

Please friend. 
Come forth
Be the strength you are

Our communication of simplicity

Is missed.
