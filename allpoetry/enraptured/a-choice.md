---
title: "A Choice"
writer: "Enraptured"
slug: "a-choice"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Faced with life, I choose death
Faced with love, I choose lust
Faced with food, I choose drink
What is it that I want?
Do I want fulfillment
Do I want these endless days?
The world can not be enough
Faced with cure, I choose the drug
Faced with the marker, I choose the pen
Faced with emotion, I choose stone.
Should I live this way?
Do I want this life?
