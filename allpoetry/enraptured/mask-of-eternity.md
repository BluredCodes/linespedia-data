title: "mask of eternity"
writer: "Enraptured"

This mask has been worn from then 'til now
Then 'til now
And now from then

When then did I hide?
Behind this
Never a truth was known
Cold and frightened 
I hid from you, 
I hid 

Opaque was the world.
Seemingly carefree.

And yet, a cry for beautiful darkness...

I was caught in your words, your way.
Nearly let down was my guard. 
My mask, began to crack.

And then, I realized and returned. 
To my cave in the depth of my path. 
My masked eternity alone.
