title: "Word"
writer: "Enraptured"

So many, and yet
The simplest of words,
They prove the point.
The terms used so smoothly
Convey that which may be
Difficult to learn. A thought
Described to the simplest of words.
Why delve into the extensive vocabulary.
Thought, feeling, so simply.
Need not these fairies, 
This art in terms,
Simple words. Simple thought.
Thought does not come laced 
With this art of speech, 
This multi-syllable hell of confusion.
Thought, and image or a feeling.
An extensive collaboration
Of phrases, grammar and
Enormous words.
Simple words.
