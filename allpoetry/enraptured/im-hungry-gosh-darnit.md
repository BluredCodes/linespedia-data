title: "I'm hungry gosh darnit"

I can not even eat
My stomache swirls and twists.
  It calls for food, but when it comes time,
    My stomache is so wrought by the twisting and turning of this new.

A butterfly perhaps
Or an elephant, trodding around inside

Just let me have my spare ribs you silly animals...
