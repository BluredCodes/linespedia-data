Running a stop sign distracted

That's what I remember of one day 
With you.

Charm and wit... talent for words.
Caught by your mystery.
Entangled in your eyes...

Oh no... I am losing the memeory.
The colour of your eyes.

You told me that I drive with one finger.
I pretend to not care, even though you know I do.

I asked if I was allowed to kiss you. 

And for the first time,
I saw you,
Lost for words.For Z.T. aka A.K. just remembering...
