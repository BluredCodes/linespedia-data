title: "What seems to be..."

And this new found hope.

Unexpected, but more than welcome
Certainly holds her interest.

So blatent, and honest.
Un-edited.

Very refreshing.
She wants to look forward 
To remove the impressions
To see the unfeigned
Remove all pretense.

Take fault along with strength

Enjoy the time, and take all that you need.

Please, do not be afraid to enter in, this world is open to more than she.
As cold as she first may seem, she does need. 
Warmth.
