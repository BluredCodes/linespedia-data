---
title: "to  follow the people surround"
writer: "Enraptured"

Hatred of the people surround

disown

disassociate

from the ideas and the value

Only to follow along with the knowledge
of the wrong direction

Beating into your skull
as a nail being hammered into a stud.

You know, but you refuse to acknowledge

To take a look the other way
the direction
the path

Trod by so many, so few.
