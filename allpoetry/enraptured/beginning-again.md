And now this new chapter. 
A life away from all I've known.
Frightened and masked I begin 

And now to adjust,
And who to know.

What to do.

She smiled politely and invited me in
To her world.
We joke and connect
Make fun out of life.
This uncertainty, calmed by her support

Truly a friend I hope to always know.
A constant in this new beginning, 
Our friendship will continue to grow.
