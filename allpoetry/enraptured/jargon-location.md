All that she meant
You'll arrive
I know 
when I don't wait
any longer
so broken
was good

but to write is to her 
as peaches are to cream

growing 
and persuing

distgusting to crash

the middle afternoon

look in that direction
and feel what you think
tinker in the vanishing stop of the heart

scratch and breathe 
dizz in the head 
and weak in the rest
completely

unhealable?
I hope not.
To find that fixer
The filler

The rapture.???
