They Question
She passes, avoids eye contact.
Some smile shyly
Others laugh,
or ask why.
How do they feel?
They question our motives.
Awkward they feel.
They look and try to understand.
The man, green and yellow plaid
He passes, each time
Seeming to ignore.
Puzzled the noodle passes, her golden locks, curls.
She questions.
They all question.
Some of us feel awkward.
But why?
The same boys pass.
They try to impress.
Stone, expression
Stiff bodies.
They arrive from other years.
They grin, they've grown since here.
Still, they question
