shiver these cold hands.
thesis. 
the vast lake and walk across... will the ice break with each step. 
wonder as the walk continues. 

basement. and domain. remain comfortably cold.

clown painting

celebrated
ancient
hard
loved

the art is lost.
art in words
art in motion
communication

built a city of lies
and of truth?

what random keystroke 
shall cause the next line?

inspire, inspite

someone lack the ability to look beyond here.

Do you stay?
social responsibility
