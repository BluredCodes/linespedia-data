Take some time to ponder existence and you will find that the everyday gains importance when you realize that the being does not always have to include the future and the thoughts that remain of the past in the present take time to enjoy some thought as it passes through your heart and mind on its way to the past to your soul be kind and see the energy that comes between the force of you.There's the form it was written in.
