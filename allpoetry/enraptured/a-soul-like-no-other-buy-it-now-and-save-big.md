She's cartoony.  Bounces.
Kermit, Ernie, Bert, Her. All alike.
A Muppet.
Indeed. A puppet, who tries so 
desperately for depth. It doesn't matter
Who she offends. Self involved.
Full of tension. Hatred. It stems from nowhere.
It stems from the attempt of depth.
She looks to me for assurance. Why?
She does not respect me, why does she ask support?
To grow from me?
Or to burn out?
