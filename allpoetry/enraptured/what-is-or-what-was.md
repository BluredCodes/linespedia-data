How does one differentiate between the connections
How does one choose

1. Inspiration, flow of words.
   I know you have cared.
   I love to be in your mind
   To be spoken of, written of
   Loved silently, loved at all.

2. Content and joyful.
   Undiscovered depths.
   To bear deep into your existence.
   To learn all I can.
   And also to teach.

Paths crossing, 
Lives, 
Redirection

One written of this beautiful darkness.
Another.
To which am I drawn...
