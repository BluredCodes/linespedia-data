---
title: "that torn slip of paper"
writer: "Enraptured"
slug: "that-torn-slip-of-paper"
source: "AllPoetry"

in love with 
a pen name

and words
hollow but filled 

the song, and the script.
which shall I follow

to add a line... improvise

fill'd to the brim with the unknown answer

you give me desire

a dance 
your hands would shake

and 'hope' 

if you recall... 

outside

the snow fell upon my nose

you went south and I north

i stare at it still
a memory in a box for strawberry tea...Can I really love you from afar when I want so to be near, but to know that to fulfill would just take away the want?
