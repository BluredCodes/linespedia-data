---
title: "Para ti..."
writer: "Enraptured"
slug: "para-ti"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/32474-Para-ti...-by-Enraptured"
---

I want you to feel safe in my arms
To find solace in my eyes.

I want to pry my way into your soul
To walk some unknown plains.

I want to sit by your side
To feel your energy surround.

I want to  know you inside
To know your depths.

I want you to know all that there is
To look at me and see eternal rapture.
