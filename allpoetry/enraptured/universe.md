---
title: "Universe"
writer: "Enraptured"
slug: "universe"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

These thoughts betray.
This heart.
This mind.
They do not think together.
Sometimes, I do not know what to think.
Shall I let the universe work for me?
But how is this achieved?
Believe, I am told.
Believe things are.
Believe in everything and nothing.
Accept.
Manifest.
This thought, object, emotion.
But how?
