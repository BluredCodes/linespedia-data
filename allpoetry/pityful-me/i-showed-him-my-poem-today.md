Showed him "Chained To A Married Man" today
My latest poem I had written
No words were spoken
I have to ask him
"Well? What do you think?"
"Ha Ha" he says "pretty good"
"I'll write YOU one sometime I'll blow YOU away!"

"Huh? That's it?"
Okay didn't expect critical kudos
From a grave digger
YES I SAID GRAVE DIGGER
But, damn what's to understand the word of morality

"What? Couldn't read my pain?"
It was laid bare on the page
Waiting for your response
All I get was "pretty good"
No comment on context
"What? Did you only see the words Sex, Pussy and Morality?"
"ASSSSSSHOLE!!!!!!!!!"

Write me a poem indeed
Perhaps yours more chained
By you illiterate
Thinking only with your penis's mind 
You are to your bloody wife  AHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHH!!!
"TAKE THIS PROSE AND SHOVE IT!!"
