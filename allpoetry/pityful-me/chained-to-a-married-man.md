He is "c~h~a~i~n~e~d" and so am I
She gets to fix his dinner
I get to sell him coffee
She gets to hear his snores
I get stolen moments

My morality screams whore, whore, whore
My pussy screams more, more, more
My heart beats Love-Love, Love-Love, Love-Love

He was "unhappy" and so was I
She gets to welcome him home at night
I only see him leave
She gets to see him as he really is in all emotions
I am only allowed glimpses, yet to see him mad

My morality
My sex
My heart

Who wins?
Who loses?
Who breaks?
