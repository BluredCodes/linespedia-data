Shouts ~ Screams ~ Yell
Our language
What a way to speak
Whoops! We don't speak
We Shreech!

Taunt ~ Tease ~ Torment
Our quality time
What a way to bond
UH OH! We don't bond
We scatter!

Argue ~ Fuss ~ Fight
Our dialogue
What a way to chat
OH AHAHAH! We don't chat
We rumble!
