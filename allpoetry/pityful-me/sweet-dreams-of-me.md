title: "Sweet Dreams Of Me"
writer: "Pityful Me"

Laughter sings to the air
Sweet lightens the heart
Children grow
Damn 
While I blink 

Fingers float over the skin
Eases the lust
Good husband works
Hard labor
While I fuck

Razor glides over the skin
Little brother snorts 
Eases the pain
White powder
While I bleed

Big Brother drinks
Little sister long gone
While father stares in space
Alcoholic haze
Off on a Bi~polar downslide

That is what happens
When no one to see
Release
Sweet distraction
Where is my director please?
