---
title: "Choices What Choices?"
writer: "Pityful Me"

Married "MAN"
Wedded "WOMAN"
But not to each other

The choice is made but...
How, when, where to go
"SHE" knows the "WHY"

Love for the first time
In "HIS" life that be the "WHY"
That's also the easy part

She found a man "WHO"
Makes "HER" laugh, and feel...
Free and safe

But "SHE" has children
Teenagers, loveable
Mood altering in a split second teenagers

How to do "THIS"
Without fucking up "THEIR" heads...
"ANYMORE" than they already are

To keep the love solid with "THEM"
"THEY" love their parents
They also like the "MARRIED MAN"

So if "SHE" runs
Will "HE" be friend or foe to her children?
Will love turn to blame?

It's not "HER" fault she'll cry
But will "THEY" hear her?

To divorce and the wait..
For "HIM" till they are settled 
Will split them               down the middle

One year "WE" debate
"Will" that be enough time?

War within "HERSELF"
Wanting the best for her children
Yet wanting "HIS" arms around "HER"
Not sure which choice is fair for "ALL"
