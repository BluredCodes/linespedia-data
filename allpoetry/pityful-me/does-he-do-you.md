Does he tell you he loves you still?
He tells me

Does he blow you secret kisses?
He does me

Does the warmth and strength of his arms make you feel safe?
They do me

Does he give you a look that makes your nipples hard?
He does me

Does the sight of him make you all tingley?
I get wet

Do you admire the strength of his convictions?
I do

Do you know that he is warm, generous and kind and somewhat shy?
I do

Do you know he knows and remembers the child he was?
I do

Does he make you want to laugh, cry, and scream all at once?
He does me

Does he make you have a big orgasm two times in fifteen minutes?
He has me

Does he make you feel pretty?
He does me

Does he amaze you with his memory, wit and charm?
He does me

Does the sound of his laughter make your heart jump?
Mines does

Do you know he asked me to marry him today?
I DO  ~~~~~~~  I DO!
