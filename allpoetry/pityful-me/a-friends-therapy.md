Lee taught me something today
"Write" she says
"Just write"
"Wrong or right"
"Write damn you write"...

I scratch I scribble
It flows so fast
How? When? Where? Does it come from?
I ask her...

"Write she says" 
"You will soon discover
The rhythm, the rhyme, the why's"
"Write write" she screams...

My fingers are cramped and tired
Brain frizzled short circuiting
Damned jammed won't flow
Backed up like a toilet
Clogged up with shit
Shit called emotions

Life 
Deal with it
Plunge deep into the feces
Deeper Deeper
Won't flow can't deal
Twisted and turns like the pipes underground
But the words won't stop

Would I have done it without her?
No Way!
I wouldn't have done it without her
"Come see - Come see"
"See how your prose just flows"
Rated on the internet
Poems/Comments by the dozen

Lee taught me something today
"Write write let the angst out"
she says
"Release - Surrender to the pen"
After all Lee cackles
"It's FREE THERAPY"!!!!
