---
title: "Mixture Of Tears"
writer: "Pityful Me"
slug: "mixture-of-tears"

He cried today
As we held each other
Tears - a mixture of
Joy, Longing, and pain

Said he'd never felt
Anything like this before
Me either and I know
I never will again

This affair is not
All sex you see
There is a mystical
Force involved

If it is love or loneliness
I do not know for sure
The heart knows only
That we can not do without it

His touch, his scent
Laughter and tears
The same he says is felt
By him towards me

I cried today
Alone by myself
Tears - a mixture of
Joy, Longing, and Pain
