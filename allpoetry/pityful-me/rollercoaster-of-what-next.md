Anger, Joy, Pain
These are the seats of my roller coaster
I put myself in line
But you pick the cars I ride

I wonder if I'm just another sucker
Following endless line of females
Why you? Why me?
If I just had the will-power
To holler "Stop!"
But, I can't take this anymore

Then, I look back at the summit
Feel your arms around, as you pick me up and spin me around
All in jest, just for kicks
And in turn two
I felt the wind
I came alive

And below the crest
I wallow in self hate
Disgust, and remorse
What are we doing?
Rending apart not only of friendship 
But your mileage checker, your friend, your now enemy

What happens when this ride ends?
When this is all said and done
As I stand in line for another
Who'll be standing beside me?
You, him, someone new?
If I have any wisdom
I will stand in line by myself... 
Choosing the next car of this crazy ride of what next?
