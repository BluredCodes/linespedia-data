title: "What Am I Worth?"
writer: "Pityful Me"

What worth am I to you?
My offerings be
Minimum wage
Three teenagers
"Basically Just Me"

No material things to speak of
Nothing to pawn
Sell on a barter
Old stands and knick-knacks
"Basically Just Me"

A broken down Ford
Two cats and a dog
A bad habit or two
Or maybe I have three
"Basically Just Me"

Arms to hold you
My heart to love
The best pussy... 
You said you ever had
"Basically Just me"
