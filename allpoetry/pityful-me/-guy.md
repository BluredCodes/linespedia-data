Where is "my guy"?
My long legged

S
K
I
N
N
Y

With Buns  (_x_) "guy"
They are just perfect "guy"

My John Deere Driving
Prank Lovin with eyes
That just make me melt "guy"

The one that believes he loves me
In spite of my faults "guy"
The one I'm risking 
My children's love for "guy"

Heaven help me
The one I can't stay away from "guy"
The guy who dries my tears "guy"

Oh never mind...
He's the one that's at home
With his wife tonight "guy"

~Sobs~
