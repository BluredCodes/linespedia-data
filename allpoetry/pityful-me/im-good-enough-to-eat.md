Your bra is blue
Your nipples are hard
Your ass like...
Two little round apples

 ~@~

He'd better be
Thinking "Golden Delicious"

~@~

Two ripe handfuls
He gestures
Sparkling eyes
Chambray shirt
Grin that lights me up
Curls my t~o~e~s 

~@~

My response to him
   WOULD
      YOU
        LIKE
          TO
            TAKE
               A
                 BITE?
