Those sweet cheeks

MMMMMMMMMMMMMMMMM

Those sweet cheeks

They arouse me

Standing

Walking

Leaning

OWWW those sweet cheeks

High

Little

Plush

Mmmmmm

Mmmmmm

Mmmmmm

How perfect they fit the curve of my palm

Warm

Smooth

Twin humps of delight

Awwwww Those sweet cheeks

Oh, those sweet cheeks of his!
