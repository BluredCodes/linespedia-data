I know her
That one who sits
In her chair
With lights off
Chain smoking
Thinking
Crying
Knowing......

Knowing your secrets
Our secrets
Innate knowledge
Instinct
"A WOMAN KNOWS"

Go to her comfort her
Kiss as she weeps
Hug her as she screams

Release me
Release her
Release MY guilt!
