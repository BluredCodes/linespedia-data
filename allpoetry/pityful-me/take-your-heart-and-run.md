I had a friend give me some advice today
"Hold your heart tight and run"
But where do I go?
Everywhere I see you
Feel, hear, and taste you
"Take your heart and run," she says
And so I run holding fast

~~~
But...
~~~

Every turn
Every pass 
Every mountain
Bridge and Byway
You are there

~~~
Sometimes I don't see you ahead of me
Then I'll turn and see you gaining on me
Following the tears
Flowing between my fingers and dropping on the ground
Straight from the heart
I try so hard to hide from you
~~~

If this one thing be said that is true
"THE HEART BETRAYS ITSELF"
"No matter how fast and furious we run!"
This I say in response to my friend.
