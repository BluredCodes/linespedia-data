title: "Jealousy Equals...."
writer: "Pityful Me"

Jealousy = Green
No = Black
Swamp mire Black
OOZING, STINKING
HATRED!!!

Suffocating of both self
And others
Ends of friendship
TRAPPING, CHOKING
KILLS LOVE!!!

Jealousy = Green
No = Red
Stabbed heart red
SEEPING, ROTTING, LOATHING
Wounding of both self
And Others
STABBING, PIERCING
By words and action
KILLS LIFE!!!
