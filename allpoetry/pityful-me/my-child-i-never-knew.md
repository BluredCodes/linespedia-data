My child I never knew
The one I never hugged
I love you, you know
How you ask?

I love you because....
I remember you
Your weight in my womb
Your twists and turns
Sometimes I imagine I heard your cries
Did you hear mine?
While you were snuggled inside

My child I never knew
The one I never kissed
I still cry today
Why you ask?
I cry because
My child who was never really mine
Just on loan short nine month term
I pray you would keep a small fetal memory with a shaky hand
The hand that signed the papers was filled with love
It believed it was the only safe thing for you
The hand was connected to a heart 
A heart beating
Karen became your mommy
I sometimes imagine I was her
