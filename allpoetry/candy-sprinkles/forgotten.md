---
title: "Forgotten"
writer: "Candy Sprinkles"
slug: "forgotten"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/520-Forgotten-by-Candy-Sprinkles"
---

Dreamers of another world
followers of a different kind
You didnt seem to mind 
the abuse of weaker ones.
You care only for yourself
and to me you were never kind.
When the love died,
you went with it.
No more abuse,
now who is weak?
You, your time to lose
its not a game,
its not an object,
it is my soul
and you had it in your hands.
But now your gone.
No more lipstick stains
on your collar,
you were wrong.
No more bruises, scrapes 
or burns, you said thatI needed to learn.
Learn what?
That you were stonger
and bigger than I?
Learn that with each blow I cry?
How does it feel
to be overpowered?
You were nothing but a coward!
Crimson all along the walls
and all around my toilet
How does your head feel
Now that you ate that bullet?
Be gone with you and all of this
because you were, but now is,
forgotten shit.
