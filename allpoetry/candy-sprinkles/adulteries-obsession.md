The moment I hear
the deepness in your voice,
begging to be your pet
completely by choice.
Making love in your wifes bed.
Youre so hot, 
all I see is red.
Fucking in her bed,
honest words,thats all you've ever said.
Taking my body, my temple
into her home.
On all fours, in her
four poster mahogany dome.
Thats where she rests her head
having sweet dreams of you.
Knowing her Prince Charming 
is always true.
But I know the difference.
Therefore I am that difference.
All the lies,
we feel no guilt for
Only wanting you inside me
that much more
On the floor,
Im such a whore,
when Im down on all fours.
Its my imagination you kissed
Because of your power
I couldnt resist.
