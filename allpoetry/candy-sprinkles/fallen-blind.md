---
title: "Fallen Blind"
writer: "Candy Sprinkles"
slug: "fallen-blind"
source: "AllPoetry"

Fallen Blind
into the lost garden

Fallen Angel 
into the fiery hell

Fallen interest
into the arms of another

Fallen thoughts
into the world of colour

Fallen tear
into the ear of confusion

Fallen picture
kept to remind me

Fallen blind
into the sea of colour
