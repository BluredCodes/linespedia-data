---
title: "Pure Indication"
writer: "Candy Sprinkles"

Do you want to come?
Well,see
what I mean...

Do you want to feel
your explosion inside me?
Oh,ok...not what I mean!

Can you discover
my energy?
Without noticing 
my advances?
Can you touch me,
the way that I need?
Wait,not what I mean.

Can you handle me
the way I need?
I want you to handle me.
Mannipulate my innermost thoughts.
Can you handle them?
Thats exactly what I mean.
