---
title: "Changed Man"
writer: "Candy Sprinkles"

You were what seemed to be
the sweetest I ever knew.
In what I found was the truth,
theres nothing you can do.
Someone out there you will find,
that is strong enough inside,
to open up your good heart
and never make her cry.
I propose this must end
between the two of us.
Cause darling I do fear
its each other we do not trust. 
In one fine day
if we shall meet again,
I would love you from that moment,
only as a changed man.
