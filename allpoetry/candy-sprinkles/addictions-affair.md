title: "Addictions Affair"

A cannibalistic sickness
twisted with his kiss.
I love him so much now,
So much I cant resist.
His intellect is touching.
His texture, so lean.
Not like violent.
Not like mean.
Where do you see me,
ten minutes from now?
You see me screaming
for him out loud.
Its simple like equations.
Perhaps quaint like dreams.
Without him I'm dirty,
with him I'm clean.
How do I return 
to life,living without?
Without his reassurance,
without a reasonable doubt.
Loneliness it pangs me
death defies all that is real
its relieving to know
only my curiosity he has killed.
