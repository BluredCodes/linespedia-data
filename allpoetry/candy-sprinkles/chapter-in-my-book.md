---
title: "Chapter in my Book"
writer: "Candy Sprinkles"
slug: "chapter-in-my-book"

When you've come 
to realize
your being a lonely soul,
its easy to build a wall
for then your body 
pays the toll.
Some carry regret,
Some live in fear.
Some become heartless,
and never shed a tear.
Living the rest of life,
living it apart.
Only left with a scar, 
branded into your heart.
Not saying to scout,
Out for the next,
but because of him
dont blame the rest.
So fill another chapter 
into your book,
but remember when he left
its your heart that he took.
