---
title: "Simply Love"
writer: "Candy Sprinkles"
slug: "simply-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

Beauty a quality of must
if not physical,inner at most.
I see through each of you,
all wanting me like new shoes.
No price on my head.
No tag at my side.
Only thinking of my in your bed.
A coward and beggar
I would be,
to fall in your web of deciet.
I see more than in your pants
I see more than wallet.
Your spirit and mind.
Your being and if you are kind.
True love Ive never had,
because of the world
Im rather glad.
You look at life
like a one way mirror,
I being on the other side
see right through you.
I think feelings are dearer.
To me and my mother,
we both loved one man.
Two kinds of love,
from mine as a daughter
to hers as a lover,
his best friend
his meaning of life.
Ill wait forever 
to be one mans wife.
I see through life
quite saddened by poverty and death,
I want to live,love and live again
and never die.
Unless its after fifty years of marriage,
and good man to stand by.
