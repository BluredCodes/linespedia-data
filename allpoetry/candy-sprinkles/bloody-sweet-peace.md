---
title: "Bloody-Sweet Peace"
writer: "Candy Sprinkles"
slug: "bloody-sweet-peace"
source: "AllPoetry"

The heavens are peaceful,
the road ahead is long.
My destination,not yet known.
Seeing the sunset quiver,
the chill it sends...
cold.

The blood was everywhere,
after the preformance.
And you.
Then there was you.
I mourned.
I deplore.
I have vexation.

I laughed especially.
