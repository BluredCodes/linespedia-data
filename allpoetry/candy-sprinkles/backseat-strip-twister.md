Little Italy 
and two Friday's of fuck. 
Losing my picture, 
and wanting you there. 
Passionate kisses. 
Sex on the stairs. 
Biting my nipples, 
pulling my hair, 
underwear, 
your game 
knowing only your 
last name. 
The moment you came, 
cum,
it was warm 
all on me, 
my sweater- I'm wetter 
it's better, this backseat 
strip twister, I scream out 'Mr.' 
Harder, now fast... 
Dont hold back..
