---
title: "Mother's Curse?"

Silently I weep
My heart pounds so soft
Pain fills my body
But it was so worth the cost
Angel in my arms
Treasure I do hold
My heart feels fresh and new
Though my body feels so old
A miracle they do call it
To me it’s nothing less
A child of my blood
An angel of my flesh
Sweetly you lay sleeping
Upon your father chest
And I am wide-awake
Though I greatly need the rest
And each I will treasure
As if it your very first
Until the day should come
You carry on the “Mother’s curse”
I hope you have child
Like you in everyway
Then you too will know
When I will always treasure this dayFor my three angels... a blessing from the start
