Why does it still ring in my ears
The betrayal of life
The calious way you spoke
"You have another choice"
You couldn't see that "I" didn't
"I have had 2"
You tell me with no emotion
So calm, no regret
As I look toward my belly and wonder
How?
How do you kill and walk away?
When you have another
One you felt grow inside you
One you held in your arms
One you have raised for years
"I had two and I was fine"
Two times you weren't ready
Two times you thought,
"Nah, not yet"
I didn't understand then
3 childern later
