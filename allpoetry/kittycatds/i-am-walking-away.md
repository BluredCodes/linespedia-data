title: "I am walking away"
writer: "kittycatds"

I have to walk away
Can’t you see?
All the pain and anguish
You’re causing me?
The pain you feel
Cuts me deep
But you are no longer
Mine to keep
I cannot help 
If you won’t help you
And I see no better
Thing for me to do
But walk away 
And hope inside
That you can open yourself
And walk outside
Let yourself see
There is so much more
But it’s up to you
 To open the door
I have watched for years
Your cycle of pain
And talking with you
Is driving me insane
When I try to help
You turn on me
And it’s only the bad
You care to see
I may be stuck 
Right here for now
But I PLAN to leave
Someday, somehow
But you, you hide
And mope alone
More stubborn
Than mountain of stone
I have tried to help
And be there for you
I have said my say
The rest is up to you
But remember this
Whenever you see
There is a world
Of opportunity
I will be here
To lend a hand
And by your side
I shall always stand
But until the day
You open your heart
I have to pull
Myself apartTo a dear friend, whom I love dearly
