Dance under the moon
Set your spirit free
Laugh with your beauty
But never forget me
For it was I who burnt your soul
It was me that free your heart
And while it is her that holds you
My love can never part
Dance under the moon
Its light your lonely guide
Remember on that bridge
Of the love that died
For you to find joy
It was I that passed away
And while your fairy holds you
On the ground my tears will lay
So dance now my beloved
Your joy I cannot share
But when the wind does blow
It's my voice you will hear thereLove is the greatest thing in the world
