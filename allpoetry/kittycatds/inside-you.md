Find the fairy by the gold tree
Find the elf hidden in me
Find the dream you had to let go
Find the talent you had want to show
Find the magic hidden in you
Find the mystery in all you do
Listen to the beauty of the child that cries
Listen to the splendor of the million lies
Listen to your heart as it reaches for more
Listen to your heart; its dream is your cure.Changed some words, and delete two lines, hopefully it sounds better
