---
title: "For now"
writer: "kittycatds"
slug: "for-now"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/5144-For-now-by-kittycatds"
---

so wrong 
so lost
so all together not me
so cold 
so distance
so all together not you
just freeze
just die
just lose yourself to me
just burn
just turn
just lose myself in you
in you
in me
in all we didn't do
in us
in trust
in all we have to do
right here
right now
right where we are meant to be
right there
right time
right where we are meant to go
