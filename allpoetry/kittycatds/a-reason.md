I want out
But I feel lost here
I want freedom
But I am scared to move
I want magic
But I stopped believing
I want love
But I push it away
I want peace
But I yell and scream
I want happiness
But I look for the bad in things
I want so much
But I feel so little
I want …
A reason to keep on going…
