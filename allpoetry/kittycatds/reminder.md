Every phone call is a reminder
Every whispered word
Your ups and downs
Mood swings and pain
And your denial
How great can that be?
It hurts that you can’t see it
Reminds me of her
Reminds me of a past
A past I thought I was over
A past I need to forget
I want to walk away
But blood binds us deep
Your words hurt me
You don’t know
Your actions cut
You can’t see
That I want to scream out
Tell you to get better
Tell you to not be HER
To not follow that path
The reminders kill me
It’s something I need to forget
I don’t want to know
I don’t want to see
I need to deny
Because she too is in me
