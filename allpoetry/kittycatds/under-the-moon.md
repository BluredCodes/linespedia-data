Under the moon
With the crystal star
Under the trees
With soft water
In each touch
In each word
With the cold wind
With the quiet night
Upon my children’s face
Upon my heart tears
While wishing on a star
While singing to the wind
Every time I cry
Every time I laugh
When everything is right
When everything is wrong
Nights the wind blows hard
Nights the wind blows not at all
In each and everything
In everything and nothing at all
Its in this I see you
Its in this I need you
And it’s in this that I remember
That I cannot love you anymore…
