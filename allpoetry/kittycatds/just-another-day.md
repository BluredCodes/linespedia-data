It was just another Friday
It was just another visit home
It didn’t mean too much to me
It was just another day there
And moment she was stoned
Or half out of it from drugs
Needing them to survive
She had forgot any other way
It was just another Friday
It was just another visit home
It was just another day that I didn’t say hello
It didn’t mean I hated her
I didn’t want it to mean that much to me
I just couldn’t handle seeing her that way
Mad and unsure how to act
It was just another pain pill
(Or maybe two or three)
It was just another day for her
It was just another trip to the hospital
It was just another drug run
It didn’t mean she wanted to die
Or maybe in a way it did
She had forgotten how to live
It was just another Tuesday
It was just the time to pick up my brother
It was just another D&D day
I didn’t think I would see the cops there
Or hear what my father would say
Not allow to go in my parents home
It was just another death scene
It was just another one down
It was just my mother lying in there
It was just her very last day…i am not sure if i like it...it seems to missing something or too much... maybe?
