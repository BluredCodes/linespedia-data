title: "And still"
writer: "kittycatds"

I can not touch you
I can not feel you
And yet you are still there
I can not see you
I can not taste you 
And yet you are still there
You are not a figment of my imaging
You are not an illusion or ghost
Yet no one knows it but me
You are not a trick of the eye
You are not a mirage or hallucination
Yet no one knows it but me
I can not hide from you
I can not run away
Still you follow me
I curse you and damn you
I hate you and despise you
Still you follow me
And still I am here…
