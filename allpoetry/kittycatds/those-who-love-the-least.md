“Those who love the least have the most control...”
A quote I heard once, and smirked at
I alone know that truth hidden in there
The power that comes from guarding your heart
That certain something of knowing, of controlling
I smirked when I read it to you, because I knew
I learned from hard experience
That to not love you as much
To not let you that close
Would guarantee love returned
For eternity
I learned that you want most 
That which you can never have
And when that love is lost
You want it that much more
I have never been dumped
Never had someone break my heart
(Or so you think)  
To show you that hurt 
Would let you in…
A risk I can't take, or else I lose control
I held the power than…
I hold the power now…
But what you don’t see…
What you can’t know
Is the truth…
Those who loves the least, feels the least in love…It funny the things you do to keep from getting hurt... and end up hurting yourself anyway...
