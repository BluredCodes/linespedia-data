Again I forget, as I rush outside
The sound of the truck reminding me
The chilling wind surrounding me
As I hurry to unlock the gate
My coat flys open, sending chills through me
As I struggle to move the cans 
That weight more than me
Or so it seems
The truck pulls closer as the can starts to spill
I curse to myself, how stupid can I be?
Everyday its the same
Rushing to hurry
Things I should have remembered
Twenty-five and still I need a mommy
Day in and day out
Last mintue reminders
Last mintue feedings
Last mintue cleaning
Last mintue life
What was that you say?
I forgot what?
Damn, not again...
