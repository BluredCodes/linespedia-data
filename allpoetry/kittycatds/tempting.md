Glimpses of you
Only pieces here and there
Interested but feeling shunned
Calm and cool
Laid back
Quick tongued
But never at me
Disappointment hurts so 
Short conversations
Lead me no way
Never closer
If I push
You don’t push back
If I insult
You take it
Hold your hand
You don’t let go
But never a hint
Is it you tolerating me 
Or tempting?
