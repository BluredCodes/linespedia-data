Wrap me up in silken lace
Hold me close your face
Whisper nothings in my ear
Just your voice I need to hear
Talk for hours with no forethought
Forget the rules we’ve both been taught
No need for love to interfere
No need to worry if you’ll be here
Come morning light, I’ll have strayed
But you’ll have the memories on which we laid
Side by side, upon your bed
One kiss given upon your head…There is going to be more to this if I can figure out what I want to say... but I thought I would see what people thought of what I have
