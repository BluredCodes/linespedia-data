title: "Touch me"
writer: "kittycatds"

touch me on that spot
you know the one
the one that fills me up
and burns me down
kiss it gently
bite it soft
make me melt
til I've had enough
touch me every which where
light me aflame
make me moan
and scream out your name
touch me softly
barely a caress
make me beg
leave me a mess
touch me, bite me
tease me some more
up against the wall
crawling on the floor
please don't stop
its never enough
touch me on that spot
