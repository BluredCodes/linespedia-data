He thinks me a slut
She thinks me a saint
They think I inspire
They think that I taint
Neither really know me
Some don't really care
Others judge from a distance
My few friends are rare
For I know who I am
Only I can truely decided
Just what is the mystery
In my heart I choose to hide
         ~~....~~
I am a mother
A sister, a friend
I make many a mistake
But I am there in the end
I am poet
A lover, a child
With my childern, patient
With my man, wild ;)
         ~~....~~
I shouldn't have to
Explain my actions
But when you attack
Except explosive reactions
My past is mine
My mistakes are too
I don't sit and judge
What give you the right to?
No longer shall I sit and pretend
That what you say 
Doesn't offend
        ~~....~~
I am a person
I am real
I have thoughts and feelings
I, too, can feel
I am a person
Right here to see
So before you judge
Get to know ME!
