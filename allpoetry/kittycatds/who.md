Who are you?
Looking at me
Who are you? 
Pretending to be
Hiding behind
That fake smile
Lying each day
Have for a while
So who are you?
To be looking at me
Who are you? 
In this mirror I seesomething I wrote because i think everyone pretends to be something else once in a while and regrets it
