title: "For just this once"
writer: "kittycatds"

I hold my head high
But wonder why
Each day I say
This will be the week
This time my bills will be paid
This time my frig will be full
This time I will gain weight
This time my kids will be fine
This time…
But each time I am knocked down
Plan after plan
Day after day
And I wonder why
But still I hold my head high
Each time I get knocked 
I cry, but then dry my eyes
Each time I get behind
I sigh, but plan again
Each time I lose faith
I pray, and than hope again
This time, this time I ask
For things to go right
For plans to follow through
For life to be simple
For my children to be feed
For my bills to be paid
For it not to hurt so much
For my family
For me
For just this once…
