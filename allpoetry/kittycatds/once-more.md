---
title: "Once more"
writer: "kittycatds"

When I close my eyes
it's still there
The long lost dream
with you hidden in the corner
Deep in the shadows
of my forgotten memories
Still wandering through my mind
Laughing and Joking
Darting between daydreams
and nightmares
Teasing me with your smile
Mocking me with your eyes
Your eyes so bright blue
Magical, alive
And when I open my eyes
You disappear, fade
And I sigh, so afraid
Cuze I know you're there
If I close my eyes
Or blink for just a second
You're still there
Ready to haunt me once more!
