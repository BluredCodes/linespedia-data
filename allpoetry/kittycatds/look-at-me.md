Why do I try?
Screaming for attention
"LOOK AT ME!!"
Am I invisible?
Am I mute?
Can you not see me
Over here
Jumping up and down
Screaming for attention
Perhaps I need blonde hair
to go along with my blue eyes
Or being model thin
is not thin enough?
Am I not well versed enough?
Or just too outspoken?
As you nod and smile at me
and turn away 
to chat it up with some girl
who doesn't even know what a dipstick is
Or that she is one...I don't know... just remembering highschool a bit too much
