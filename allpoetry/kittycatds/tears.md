It
Hurts
So very much
I'm trying not to cry
The tears keep flooding back
I don't know if I keep doing this
But each day it’s something new
Each "obligation" a reason to leave
Each moment here a chance to fight
Each word I say is the wrong one
Each and every gesture is flawed 
And it’s always me
AlwaysWas attempting to make a teardrop... did it work?
