Starving children
Need to be feed
But I lay here
I feel half dead
No reason to move
No reason to stay
I close my eyes
Ignoring the day
The children, they cry
For my attention once more 
I yell at them
To close the door
The covers I pull
Up over me
Pray to the world
To let me be
I will not move
I will not fight
I shall here
Until the night
And in the darkness
I can cry once more
Just like I did 
The night before…Don't worry, my morning don't completely happen this way... but there are some morning...
