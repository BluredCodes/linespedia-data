title: "Us"
writer: "kittycatds"

You prompt me to talk
And than shot me down
My words mean nothing to you
My thoughts are naught
My feelings are nil
And yet my silence angers more
I try to please
Carefully chosen words
But the efforts too much to bare
With each smiling face
With each pleasant tone
I know I lie to myself and you
Yet each livid word
Each hidden belief
Destroys what is barely there
I don’t know how
I don’t know when
I don’t know what to do
Slowly we die
Steadily we wilt
A relationship no longer true
