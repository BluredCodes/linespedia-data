title: "Your Desire"

Speak of your Desire,
and your beauty
and speak it well
For never shall I fall for such petty things again
I never shall let love hold my heart
I never shall let beauty control me
Never I say
And why you ask?
For beauty held me,
and loved me
But then... let me go
So keep to your dreaming of making your heaven
and having your desire...
Me, I shall stay here
In hell and weep
