The sorrow slips away
Though the heart still burns
The memory doesn't fade 
The soul still yearns
You reach out empty handed
And receive nothing more
The anger's still there
The tears still pour
You ask the world 'WHY?'
Scream questions in the air
Nothing answers back
But the truth is still there
You can not change decisions
That were not yours to make
Though the end result still hurts
It's a pain you must take
I can not offer comfort
I can not change the past 
I can not mend your heart
But the sorrows sure to pass
And with everyday your learning
With every break you know
The sorrow soon will fade
And with the pain, You grow
