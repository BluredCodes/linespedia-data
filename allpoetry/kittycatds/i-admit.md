title: "I admit"

I admit I have my doubts
Moments I wonder
Moments I worry
I admit there are times
When I think what if
Or why me
I admit it’s not all roses
And things aren’t perfect
The flaws are there
I admit it not like the movies
No perfect happy ending
No sonnets or songs in my honor
I admit he is crazy
Temper fits and fights
Immature moments I want to scream
I admit it’s not what I dreamed
No shining armor
No horse to ride off on
I admit …
But its also quiet moments
And laughing fits
Trips to nowhere 
For no reason
It’s little gifts and perfect hugs
It’s understanding and knowing there’s more
I admit I have my doubts…
But than I see him
And realize I AM in love…
