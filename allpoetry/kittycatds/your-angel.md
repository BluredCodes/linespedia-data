---
title: "Your Angel"
writer: "kittycatds"

You can not see me
You can not feel me
But I am here, 
Holding your hand
When nights are cold
And you feel warm
When you want to weep
And suddenly smile
When you are afriad
And suddenly feel safe
When you are alone
But feel secure
When you feel beaten
But struggle on
When times are tough
But learn to make due
When it seem everything and anything 
Is holding you down...
I am there...
It was always me...
Always there for you...
And on your last day
When there is fear in your heart
I will be the one
Holding out my hand
To lead you home...There are moments when for some reason, despite everything I manage to go on and even as a child I KNEW I had a guardian angel... This is for her... The only way I know to say thank you, whom ever you are...
