You are the one I have never had
And for that I want you more
I have not spoken to you in months
And somehow, you creep into my mind
How do you do that?
I use to think I could just wait
Find the right moment
When I was free
But than again
I never am
I was never good at being alone
So you have a special role
And I have a new plan
YOU can be my fantasy
The one I dream of at night
The one who makes me secretly smile
While others dream of movie stars
I will be dreaming of youOK, I know this completely SUCKS but I need to keep writing or else this sleeplessness seems wasted!
