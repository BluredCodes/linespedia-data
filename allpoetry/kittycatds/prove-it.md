You say that you love me
Prove it with a kiss
Make me feel things 
Things I never felt before
You say that you love me
Prove it with your arms
Make me feel a warmth
A warmth I never felt before
You say that you love me
Prove it with my heart
Make it skip a beat
A beat its never skip before
You say that you love me
Prove itthis one finally just came out... and I didnt want to fight with it too much...
