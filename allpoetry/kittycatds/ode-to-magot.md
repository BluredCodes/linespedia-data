Silent and sweet 
My pride grows deep
To my eyes a woman
To my heart a child
I remember when
Though no one would believe
How you cried for a cut
Barely enough to bleed
How you would run and hide
And were afraid of me
Oh I remember
What no one else can see
All grown up now
Standing on your own
A child to my heart
Oh the things I’ve known
The locus shells
And that plastic spoon
How your birthday meant
Mine was soon
How mother would cheer you
Through all our fights
But I would win
Do you remember those nights?
And under our blankets
We would hide in fear
Because of a closet
We wouldn’t go near
But now you’re older
And better than me
So there is one thing left
I need you to see
YOU are my angel
All the strength I need
All my hopes and dreams
You continue to exceed
So never forget
No matter what you say
You are more beautiful
In every way…
