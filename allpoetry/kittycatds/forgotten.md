Have you forgotten me?
Am I a long lost memory?
Just a line from a book
A quote you can’t remember
A story no longer true
Or do you recall
Like I can
Each kiss stolen in the trees
Each moment sacred and forbidden
Each embrace more important than the last
Do you still dream of me at night?
Remember each line of my face
Each expression I would make
Each tear that would fall
Or am I forgotten?
