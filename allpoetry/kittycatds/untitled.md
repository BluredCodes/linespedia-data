If I stare at the screen long enough
If I think about you for a minute more
If I force myself to keep typing
If I imagine you are right here
Than maybe I can find it
All those words that are hiding
All the anger that I felt
All the distain I still feel
All the confusion and fright
All the pain I went through
All the people you hurt
But you are not here
And I can’t yell at you
And I can’t tell you
And I can’t forgive you
Not to your face
Your not here
Though I want to still yell
I want you to know
I need you to see
Most of what I am
Most of what I do and did
Are because of how you were
Are because of how you shaped me
But most of all I want to show you
That I can be better
That I can let go
That I can remember 
And I can see
That were you failed, Mommy
