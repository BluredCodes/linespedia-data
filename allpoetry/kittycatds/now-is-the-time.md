There was a time
The mirror was my enemy
There was a time
I didn’t like what I’d see
There was a time
My reflection killed me
There was a time
I couldn’t stand the pain
There was a time
My thoughts were of sorrow
There was a time
My actions were wrong
There was a time
I hated myself
There was a time…
~~---*---~~
Now is the time
To rejoice in my presence
Now is the time
To find myself
Now is the time
To smile in the mirror
Now is the time 
To laugh at myself
Now is the time
I find my image clearer
Now is the time
I let go and forgive myself
Because
Now is MY time!
