So now these words escape me, 
Like the blood that floods from my heart
To hold someone so close 
to truely love them as, as
As what?
So hard to explain
This undying, complete love felt for those
that are of you
Two set of blue eyes
Two adoring face
Two completely trusting hearts
You are me and yet so different
You both are so endearing, so perfect
Like having two mirrors in front of you
that show everything you had hope to be
One so calm and gentle
the other wild and honest
perfect blends of poetry
Magic in the palm of my hands
Angels that I can one day watch
FLY!
