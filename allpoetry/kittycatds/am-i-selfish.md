title: "Am I selfish"

First I was hiding
because it was safe
Then I went crazy
alone in this place
I tried to get out
but ties held me close
I stared out the window
what do I want most?
My mother heart
It needs to be here
My child mind screams
"This so isn't fair"
I look toward you
You always know right
Lord, how do I escape
From this world tonight
Am I being selfish
People have told me before
While in the same breath
They say I need more
Where is the line
That people draw tight
Am I selfish or needy
to want to leave tonight
I'm scared and alone
everday in this home
This last thing I want
is another day alone
So tell the answer 
To my wondering plea
Am I doing this for good
or just for me?
