title: "To believe"

Pray to the stars
My happiness there
Waiting amongst the dreams
Pray to the moon
Its face my friend
Joy hiding in each and every beam
The lights, they dance
I feel the joy
Of happiness waiting for me
I won't give up
Nor ever give in
My belief stronger than pain
Pain trys to stab
I smile anyway
My life is mine to choose
Pulling myself up
Standing strong
No wind shall knock me down
And toward your face
I slowly turn
My prayers answered in you...To those that are going to hard times on here... Though I am mostly silent, I still read and feel... and hope the best for you...
