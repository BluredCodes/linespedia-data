Behind me
In my past
Slowly sneaking
A quiet smile,
Of not so long ago
A dream
A hope
A purpose
One I had forgotten 
Until it was gone
Fate brought me not here
But my own selfish lies
My disillusions
My ideals of more
Of something bigger
More intense
Passion means nothing now
Now that I am lonely
And you have her…
