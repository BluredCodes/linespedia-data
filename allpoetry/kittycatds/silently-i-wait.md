---
title: "Silently I wait"
writer: "kittycatds"

It’s alone and quiet
The children are in bed
The soft clicking of the keyboard
Its keeps me from going crazy
My nights are always like this
Me alone, you out there
I spend the night worrying
But you’ll never know
I’ll never say
This is life I never wanted
A promise to myself you could say
When that pager goes off
And you go running
Throwing on clothes you keep by the bed
A hasty kiss as you dart away
Love makes us do foolish things
And foolishly I keep quiet
Never share my fears 
About your job
About your choice
Your love 
A job with no glory
Except the life you save
The child you help
People you come to know and worry about
If you were a cop or firefighter
Parades would be in your honor
But everyone forgets the EMT
A lesson I have sadly learned
Unaware of the dinner you left
Only to eat later cold
The woman you have at home
Hoping you make it home safe
But I keep quiet
In silent awe of you
And the strength you hold
As you lose another child
Because someone was drinking and driving
How you have seen the worst of people
And save their lives anyway
No, you’ll never have the glory
But you’ll always have the love
And you’ll always have me
Silently at home
Hoping and praying
Worrying and wondering
And loving you that much more for itIts not really all I wanted it to be...Its hard to put into emotion how I feel when he is gone
