---
title: "Room's Cold"
writer: "kittycatds"

Room’s cold
No heat, no phone
The baby cries to be fed
Window’s broke
Curtain’s torn
Three children in the bed
Mother cries
Father’s gone
Hope no longer alive
Job’s lost
Money gone
Forsaken family of five
She cries
She prays
She wonders what to do
4 kids
1 woman
Family without a clue
The gun
The guilt
The only way she knew
The fear
The regret
The gun on the man she drew
Her children
Her fear
The food she couldn’t buy
The tears
The sorrow
The man saw her start to cry
The ground
She lies
The gun she couldn’t aim
She plead
She hurt
The man saw her shame
The miracle
The angel
That man on the street
Her bruises
Her pain
He knew that she’d been beat
And there
In her
He saw what no one could
He promised
He helped
He brought he what he could
The love
The faith
The fondest slowly grew
His trust
Her hope
A love that was so true
One woman
One man
A new family born
New home
New life
A past no longer torn
