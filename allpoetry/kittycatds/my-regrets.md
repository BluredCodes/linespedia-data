You don’t see 
You can’t know
The truth I mask inside
The teasing words
The feigned laughter
All to shelter you
Our shoddy past
Your excelling future
Reminders to only me
Regrets I hide
Happiness I fake
I know it was all me
My decision 
My mistake
This is who I am
And each word
Each fleeting glance
Is one more tear…for memy writing isn't what it use to be... I am fighting through a block...
