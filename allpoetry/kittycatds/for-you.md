---
title: "For you"
writer: "kittycatds"
slug: "for-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

My words
stale
my thoughts
unfinished
my dreams
half lies
my desires
hidden
all of it
for you
and for whatever
it is that you want
that you need
that you desire
and hope
from a distance
out of my reach
with 
HER...
