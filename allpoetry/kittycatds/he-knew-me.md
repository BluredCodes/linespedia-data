title: "He knew me"

I thought he knew me
When his lips pressed mine
When he said I was his
I thought he knew me
When his world was mine
When our dreams were one
I thought he knew me
When we fought for no reason
When we made up so quick
I thought he knew me
When he bought things for fun
When we fall so quick
I thought he knew me
When he almost cried at my pain
When I gave birth to our child
I thought he knew me
So I let him be free
So I tried not to complain
I thought he knew me
Til he wanted to be free
I thought he knew me
I thought he knew me...it changed it quite a bit... which is a first for me... hope it flows better now
