The way we argue
The way we fight
The way you say 
My name at night
The way we play
And wrestle about
The sound of your voice
Even when you shout
The way you hold 
Our child up high
The smile you give
Makes me want to cry
Your many assets
Though I point out few
Your many faults
Those make you seem true
Your smile, your kiss
Your taunting tease
Your annoying habits
Put me at ease
All that you are
And all you can be
The way you found
The strengths in me
I don’t think you know
Just how much I do care
I guess you don’t see
The times that I stare
Everything you are
Everything you do
These are the reasons
