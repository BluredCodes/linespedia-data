I see
I know
Your heart still bleeds
I listen
I cry
Reaching out too much
I try
You need
Wishing your heart to stone
Old soul
Young chi
One learning from the other
Teacher
Student
When does the mentor learn?
Infantile
Aged
Can you tell the difference anymore?
Ying
Yang
Two pieces, one purpose
Give 
Take 
Unequal balance anymore
Trying
Pushing
Too blind to understand
Teacher
Student
Child
Elder
Young
Old
Titles
Just titles
Maybe one day you will finally see…
