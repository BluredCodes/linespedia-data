---
title: "In the dark"
writer: "kittycatds"

She sits there in the cold
Alone
Afraid
She sees him in the distance
Ominous
Foreboding 
She curls herself tight, holding her knees
Praying
Pleading
She hopes he doesn’t notice, can not sense her
Fearing
Dreading
He moves in closer, within reach
Slowing
Sensing 
He feels something in the air
Sorrow
Torment
She holds her breath, pulls herself tighter
Anxious 
Apprehensive
He slows his steps, listens to the wind
Blowing
Dancing
He hears a tear drop, and turns his head
Silence
Screaming
She closes her eyes, overtaken with fear
Petrified
Frozen
He reaches down, touches her face
Puzzled
Perplexed 
Slowly she looks up, sees him
Surprise
Relief
He takes her in his arms
Caring
Concern
They walk off together
Understanding
Forgiving
