---
title: "You and me"
writer: "kittycatds"
slug: "you-and-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

You shame me
Then hate me
Turn from me
Then laugh at me
You mock me
Then stalk me
You fear me
Then scare me
Believe in me
Then criticize me
Yet all the while
You say you love me.Its an old one I found...
