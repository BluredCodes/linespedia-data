title: "Two"
writer: "kittycatds"

I can see eyes shining in my mind
Bright, blue, burning
I can feel to smiles dancing on my heart
Alive, magical, free
I can taste the tears so bitter shed
Alone, afraid, in need
Two hearts, two lives, two perfect angels
Mine to love and care
Two smiles, two babies, two sweet devils
In my arms, to teach and guide
And when the time comes to let them go
I shall stand back and watch them 
FLY!
