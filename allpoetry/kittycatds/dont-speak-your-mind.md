title: "Don't speak your mind"
writer: "kittycatds"

And it is this that kills me
Drawing lines to make it better
Have your beliefs but never speak them
Freedom of speech but don't step on toes
Be wary of those more senstive
Those uncapable of turning their heads
"Shh, you are offending those who do not agree"
But MY thoughts and feelings are less?
Don't offend but when you are offended just turn your head
"So he didn't agree with you, couldn't you just walk away?"
Why? Because I was willing to debate 
And HE was upset, offended?
I am suppose to back down
Because HE is closed-minded?
HE who has never been there
He who knows more, with the silver spoon in his mouth?
He who has never lived without
Never a day on the brink of starving
Never a fear that his child may starve
Never made a bad mistake in his life
I should back down?
Why?
Because I AM poor?
Because I DO understand?
Because I HAVE been there?
I am the wrong one
Because I couldn't justify killing my child
Because I didn't think the ends justified the means?
I am unworthy of a child, because HE thinks I am not rich enough
"Don't breed what you can not feed"
Never any expections
Never any doubts
I am poor and I am at fault
In capable of raising healthy happy childern
Because I will never be able to buy them that fancy sports car
Your right...
What was I thinking...
I should be shot for speaking my mindThis is in reference to a few guys that apparently think I am unworthy of childern because I am too low on the income scale and when I tried to debate it, calmly they got pissed and angry and "I" was told to just chill
