Cold hard steel 
In her hand
If he didn’t get it
He would now understand
Aiming high
Trembling lip
All her weight
Resting on her hip
He thinks she bluffing
Smile unfold
He doesn’t believe
She could be that bold
Her hand is shaking
But her mind unyielding
She knows the power
Her hand is wielding 
Staring her down
“Take your best shot”
The room is quiet
And suddenly hot
She watches him
And remembers all
The teasing words
The names he would call
Determined now
She fixes her aim
Upon the man she wants to maim
Just one squeeze
Just a simple deed
No hesitation 
On her face to read
She looks at him
And smiles in reply
And with the whip cream
Shoots him straight in the eye…
