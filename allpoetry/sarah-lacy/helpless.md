Helpless

Exhausted eyes watch
    as my head spins
into another restless night
     I'm falling...
up into the vast memories
among masses of unfading regret,
  suspended in midair
by the wings of confusion,
and all I want is to fall
gently into my white cotton sheets.

       ~Sarah Lacy~
