Although this does not apply to my life now I still have those dark places

My life is in a dark place
Though I've fought so hard to reach the light
Day by day I struggle
With fearful dreams at night

How can I find the peace
I so long to embrace
When daily visions run rampant
I still see his face

It hovers over me
It sneers a horrible grin
Where can I find the strength
Will it come from within

The damp floor of his soul 
Sprouts evil like toadstools
Playing all his evil games
He made us all the fools

Fools for obeying
Because of utter fear
Fools for the silence
That no one can hear

The fungus replicating
It goes on and on
I am so grateful
That now he is gone

Gone to meet his maker
Denied at the gate
He sits with satan
He has fulfilled his fate

DBenson 9/27/01
