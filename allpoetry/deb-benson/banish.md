---
title: "Banish"
writer: "Deb Benson"
slug: "banish"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/39233-Banish-by-Deb-Benson"
---

Banish the touch of lingering dreams
Free me from reminders of the past
Banish my fear of repeated events
Free me from those childhood traumas
Banish the hate I sometimes feel
Free my soul--let me soar
Banish the touch of lingering dreams

DBenson 11/8/01
