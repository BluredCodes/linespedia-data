With a strand of sadness threaded
through the afterglow of lovemaking
I lay still and quiet in my bed
The air is full of noise
But no one is here
He had rushed to leave me
I pondered his leaving
And knew I could not welcome him again
For who wants a man that clearly
Cares more for his pleasure
Than the joy of sharing

DBenson7/18/01
