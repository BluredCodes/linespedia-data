This is a poem I wrote on Sept 12th 2001 and wanted to post for the 6 month anniversary of the tragedy that struck America

The echo of music was stilled
When our countrymen were horribly killed
The religious fanatics did their dastardly deed
With no thought of life-only their greed
Wanting show that our borders they could cross
No matter the manner-no matter the loss
How heavy our hearts, how painful our souls
Will our country ever feel safe, ever feel whole
We will not allow ourselves to face defeat
Our freedom and democracy they cannot unseat
The terrorists will be found-justice to be done
No more lives lost-we pray-peace will come
Please no bombs-bullets or others to be killed
The world has had enough
When
The echo of music was stilledLet us all hope that this will be over soon and peace will come.
