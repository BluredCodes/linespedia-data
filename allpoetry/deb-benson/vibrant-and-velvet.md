I have taken Robert Burns line  Oh my love is like a red, red rose
and put a little twist on it

Our love is like a red, red rose
Vibrant and velvet
Carrying us skyward
Wrapping us in softness
Sprinkling our souls with 
Dew kissed petals
Thornless by design
Our love is like a red, red rose
Vibrant and Velvet

DBenson 1/31/02
