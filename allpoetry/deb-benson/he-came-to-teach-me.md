title: "He Came to Teach Me"

He came to teach me
What I needed to know

Chestnut hair
Sky matching eyes
Full moon smile
Too beautiful for a man
What a package he made
Blind love

Golden wrapped
With black heart
Betrayer of trust
Untrue to me
Untrue to self

He came to teach me
What I needed to know 
How to love
How to lose
How to come out SANE

I judged his book
Not by content
But by cover
He came to teach me
