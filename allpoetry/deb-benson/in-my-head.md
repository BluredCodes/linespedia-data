This line "I shall never get you put together entirely" by Sylvia Plath inspired me to write this

"I shall never get you put together entirely"
I tell myself in my head
Too many things have occurred in life
Taking another piece of me
Making it hard to find the lost
And scattered pieces of my soul
Wanting to feel whole again
Knowing
"I shall never get you put together entirely"
Echoeing in my sad heart
