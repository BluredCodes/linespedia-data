---
title: "With Another"
writer: "Deb Benson"
slug: "with-another"
source: "AllPoetry"

Tonight your face passes in front of my eyes
Even tho I am with another
Your sweet smell fills my nostrils
Making me hunger for your taste
Even tho I am with another
I feel your hands caressing me
With your gentle manliness
Even tho I am with another
Your soft mouth makes love to me
A quickening fire
Even tho I am with another
I see your face
Half closed eyes speak
The words your lips refuse
Even tho I am with another
I feel your breath quicken
Your murmurs of desire
As we rise to unite
Even tho I am with another
Reaching the ones of us
I come crashing down--unsated
Because I am with another
