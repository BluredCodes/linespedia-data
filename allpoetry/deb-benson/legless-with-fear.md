title: "Legless With Fear"
writer: "Deb Benson"

We wanted to run when
Our predators came near
Unable to move
Legless with fear
We wanted to yell 
We wanted to scream
Voiceless with fear
Invading our dreams
We wanted to escape
All of the pain
We were numb with fear
Again and Again
We went to other places
In our mind
To help us escape
No safety to find
We all lost our childhood
We've shed rivers of tears
Haunting us 
The rest of our years
We all have traveled
Thru this loney past
Fughting for our sanity
How long will it last
Before we finally 
Find ourselves
