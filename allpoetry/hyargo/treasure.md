title: "Treasure"
writer: "hyargo"
slug: "treasure"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I am open,
My heart is a door with no locks,
And you are welcome in.
Passage is free,
But not without responsibility.
Have a care,
This heart is fragile,
It's been broken and torn,
Tiny pieces long in their repair.
To enter is to treasure,
And to know without worry,
That yours is treasured, as well.
