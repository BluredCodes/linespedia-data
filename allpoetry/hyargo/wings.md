---
title: "Wings"
writer: "hyargo"
slug: "wings"
source: "AllPoetry"

Your smile inspires me,
Lifted, am I, above this world,
Each time you take my hand, 
My soul takes flight.

I am carried on wings
of emotion and passion,
of joy and smiles and laughter,
I am floating on a dream.

You lift me and give me life,
You overwhelm me,
You envelop me in warmth,
You give me wings.This is for Bill Hearne.  My love, my lover, my rescuer.
