title: "Quick To Blush"

I have blue eyes that often hide
Beneath long lashes and 
I am quick to blush.
My smile is shy and I laugh
When I am nervous,
My hands are restless, 
So I fold them in my lap.
My face lights up
When I am excited,
I shiver when 
Given to passion.
My heart I wear on my sleeve,
A silly heart it sometimes is.
I wish I were more mysterious,
But my honesty surrounds me
Like an aura.
This is who I am.Be kind.
