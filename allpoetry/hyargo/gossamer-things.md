title: "Gossamer Things"
writer: "hyargo"

I fell asleep with a smile
Gracing my lips
And my heart.

I've been touched deeply
By simple words,
I am stirred.

Dreams are but gossamer things,
Delicate and gilt,
With tangible anticipation.
