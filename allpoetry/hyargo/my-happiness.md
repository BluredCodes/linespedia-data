---
title: "My Happiness"
writer: "hyargo"

The sweet newness of you
Touches my heart
Each time you touch my skin.

My daydreams are of you
My nights filled with your image
My soul consumed with joy.

My lips wear a smile
That you renew with each kiss
Happiness is truly mine.

In all this world
So glad am I
To have found you, somehow.
