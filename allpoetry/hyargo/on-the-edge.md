Standing at the edge of lucidity and repast,
I contemplate many new things,
Wondrous and frightening,
Brilliant, though not as to blind.
My mind toils with such possibility,
The sound of your voice,
Reaching to take my hand.
I look on with cautious hesitation,
Yet, I find nothing to fear,
Waking thoughts bring clarity,
But my answer unfolds slowly, still.
