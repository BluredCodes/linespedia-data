---
title: "Missing You"
writer: "hyargo"
slug: "missing-you"

My mouth misses the sweet taste of your kiss,
My skin feels incomplete without your touch,
My brain knows it will not be long,
My heart hurts as if it has been forever.
Counting hours until your return,
I breathe deeply to steady myself,
And I wait for you with patient love,
And I anticipate the rediscovery of you.For Capt. Bill Hearne.
