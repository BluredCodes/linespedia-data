title: "Free"

I can't carry that burden anymore,
The one that pulls me under,
The one that drowns.
I can't keep alive, any longer,
that which has been long dead.
The cost is great, and all the pain,
It keeps my head down,
And my shoulders buckle
Beneath such awesome weight.
I've had to crawl from under
That pressing bulk.

I am light and I am free.Alas, poor Dan.  You just weren't the one.
