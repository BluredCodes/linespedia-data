---
title: "Kneal With Me"
writer: "hyargo"
slug: "kneal-with-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/47719-Kneal-With-Me-by-hyargo"
---

Hush your worried mind,
Rest your fears on my shoulders,
Just for a moment, let them go,
And kneal with me.

We will pray to God,
Ask Him to protect you,
Not out of selfish desire,
But for all who need you.

Smooth your furrowed brow,
Kneal with me, here,
I will pray with you,
And you will safely return.For John Z., my friend.
