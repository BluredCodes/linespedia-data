The first touch
Was but a word,
Embracing them both.

Breath put to sound,
Wrapping itself around them,
A cozy blanket of rapport.

A soft blush
Illuminating her face,
His smiling eyes felt.

The first touch
Was but a word,
The single word “Hello.”Don't you ever feel like the first words you shared with a particular person were every bit as moving and powerful as a physical touch?
