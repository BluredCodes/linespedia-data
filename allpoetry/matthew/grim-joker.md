---
title: "Grim Joker"
writer: "Matthew"

Ashes to ashes, dust to dust
Fight your battles and do what you must
Make your fortune and gamble on time
Don't you dare try to waste any of mine
We are all connected in our minds
Only the thinnest walls we hide behind
You can say my existence is pointless
You can say that I am faceless

Wearing a mask to cover up my face
But what you say makes no difference to me
Your ramblings and your gambling's have brought you to this place
You fear the darkness within and so you flee
I laugh at your antics and you think me mad
My mild amusement brings no real joy
And thus some think my life is sad
But don't waste your pity on this poor boy

You refuse to believe that we are anything alike
You deny the shadow within your self 
And you can't see the humor of our lives
Why can't you see that I won't bite? 
I just want you to see what you can be if you take your feelings off the shelf
Don't worry about the danger I know that you will survive

Embrace your darkness and set your self, free
Hop in next to the Grim Joker and take a ride with me
