---
title: "Oblivion"
writer: "Matthew"
slug: "oblivion"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

What was has no meaning
what will be holds no weight
what we were was the begining
what we are has no fate
the only truth to which we are tied
is that eventually everything dies

Your mother gave you morals
and your honor keeps you bound
but the the laws of survival govern all
so what hope have you found

Your father taught you discipline
and how to face the end
but a greater purpose you will not find
and to the the darknes you must bend

Generals will gamble with your life
and the rightous, for your soul
entire cites will be consumed in fire 
and the people cry for more

What was can never be again
while what may be keeps vain hope strong
and blinds us from the here and now
but what may be can never be again
so find hope in sweet oblivion
