---
title: "Dreams of Madness"
writer: "Matthew"

The dream begins
The black wind howls
The cold wind bites to the bone
The flinty eyes of death are hard

The dream goes on
The souls of many cry in vain
They all reside in the house of pain
The black cloak descends on all
Eternal rest asleep we fall
Familiar faces no hiding places

The dream goes on
My face reflected back at me
A tingling feeling no time to flee
I see a monsters face 
But I am not afraid
For I have seen the eyes of death

The dream goes on
And I hold to what hope that I can find
For my face is looking back at me
And I am not afraid
For I have heard the black wind howl

The dream goes on
And as I look into the creatures eyes
I see a soul like yours or mine
And I recognize this soul for it is not afraid
