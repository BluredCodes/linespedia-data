---
title: "The Devil Wears Lip Gloss"
writer: "Matthew"
slug: "the-devil-wears-lip-gloss"

I move through the world like a ghost.
No soul no reason no remorse for you or me.
Are you blind? are you lost?
You do what you can to stay free.
But what is the point to your madness and the reason for mine?
Where is the happiness that you want to find?

Sugar in the side of the liver sick man
Shadow by the smile in the snow
The Blood and the mud by the fire in the pan
Don't say a word because you don't know

You dance like you dance on nails and glass
And you sing like you sing when your throats on fire
But you can't take back-lost love from the past
And pretty words on paper wont bring you any higher

I've got no soul and I feel no pain
And you can't make me out against the pouring rain
But a can of peaches and a bottle of pills 
Can make a hell of a difference on this bloody hill

How can you laugh and how can you cry?
Take me to the river so that I might see
What started the lie?
And, who told it to me?

Free the genie and burn the witch
Down the bottle and slap the bitch
Run and scream and cower and flee
It's all a joke because you can't hide from me

You're running scared and your life is at a loss
But you can't do a damn thing because the Devil wears lip-gloss
