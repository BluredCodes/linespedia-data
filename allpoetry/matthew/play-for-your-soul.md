title: "Play For Your Soul"

I was higher than a kite when I O.D'd that night
And they came for me the very next day
An angel and a demon and the red one a screaming
Telling me that there was hell to pay 
The Angel said hold on red this mans path aint set 
His soul is in a balancing way
And in order to see where his soul is a heading 
For it we're going to have to play
Well I was a sitting and that demon was a grinning 
As he pulled out that bass in bad ass'd black
Then I saw the golden strings on a pearly white strat 
Pulled out from behind that angels back
Then the demon began to play
Well that demon could a rock and a roll as he played for my soul
With that low rumbling sound of a hell bound hound
My body started shaking and my mind started quaking
And the demon shrieked and he squealed 
At the steal of a deal that the angel had been fool to make
But then the angel began to play
Well that explosion of sound it shook me around as my soul was torn in two
But that angel kept a strumming and that strat kept a humming
And the sound blew my mind away
