---
title: "Mirror"
writer: "Matthew"
slug: "mirror"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I look at you and what do I see
I see the world reflected back at me
Your shining eyes, your smiling face
When you’re around you brighten up the place
What little I know what little I see
The fullness of life reflected back at me
Reflection, the image lives the mirror shows
Rich green fields where you run free
Or the beauty of the evergreen draped in winter snows
Look once look twice look three times even
Reflection, the image lives the mirror shows
Life’s too short to sit there grieving
Life and time intertwined, where do they go so quickly?
No one knows
You don’t know and you don’t care
Reflection, the image lives the mirror shows
I don’t know and I despair
But I only need to look to you 
And see the mirror standing there
