---
title: "Lights in the Sky..."
writer: "Matthew"

In the quiet of the dark night 
You can hear them
Sweetly calling
In the heat of the good fight
You can hear them 
As you're falling
Nowhere to run
Nowhere to go
I hope you have fun
but I just don't know

light in the sky do you know?
Where we're from and where we are to go
light in the sky do you understand?
about the darkest of souls of man
light in the sky where are you from?
Are you a messenger from above?
Light in the sky I just don't know
But I hope that I have a place to go

I don't know where they're from
But I can hear them
I know they're coming
But I don't know when
I know they're out there
In the sky above
But I don't care
Just as long as they come
