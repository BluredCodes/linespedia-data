---
title: "Angel's Wings"
writer: "Nickie"
slug: "angels-wings"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/51289-Angels-Wings-by-Nickie"
---

We have created our own hell
We have destroyed life
And the meaning of happiness.
We have not been misled
The answers are in front of us
But we are too blind to see them,
Fixed only on ourselves.
We have remorse
But only for a short time.
Then we go and sin again,
Forgetting lessons taught.
This is our hell
And Angel's wings are useless here.

2-20-02
