writer: "Nickie"

I never want to hear those words
Come from your lips again.
Words that gave me hope
And made me alive once again.
You said you would always be there
That I would always have a place
But i was foolish to believe
Those words you spoke with such grace.
They've only brought me pain
For you see I never took them (or you) for granted
And now it hurts me so
To know that you've let go.
Now I am afriad
To pass on those words you once gave me
For i know all too well
What pain it brings.
