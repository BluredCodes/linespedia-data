---
title: "My Angel"
writer: "Nickie"
slug: "my-angel"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I look at the moon
And I know something's not right
I had to know soon
Before the morning light.

I told myself this
He's and angel, don't try
Let ignorance be bliss
And live in my lie.

My heart was too strong
You had to be mine
I waited for so long
To cross my line.

I have yet to be blue
And my heart does not lie
I will love you
Until the day I die.

3-20-02
