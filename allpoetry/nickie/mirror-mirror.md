Mirror mirror on the wall
What purpose do i serve
If any at all?

Mirror Mirror do not lie
Aren't these the tears
Which you've made me cry?

I look at you
Looking back at me
You seem to know something
Something i cannot see.

Please tell me now
What you hold inside
I can take it
And promise not to cry.

I look at you
Looking back at me
And i know you will never tell me
What you see.

3-19-02
