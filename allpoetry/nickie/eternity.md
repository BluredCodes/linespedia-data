Imagine you as a bird
And your beak so small
So small that it can only hold one grain of sand.
Now imagine all the sand
Gathered in one large pile and an ocean.
the land across the ocean 
Empty.
You must fill it with the sand
One grain at a time.
Now that you have carried 
Every grain of sand
You know what one day
Of eternity is like.
Choose how you want to spend
Eternity, my friend
For it never ends.
