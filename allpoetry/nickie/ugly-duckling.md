I used to be ugly
I used to be shy
I used to keep my whole world inside.

I had no boyfriends
Or friends for that fact
The only thing i had was the clothing on my back.

The tables have turned
And I have seen
That God sprinkled a little beauty on me.

I now have a boyfriend
And enough friends indeed
But my tears are enough to feed a small tree.

Change me back to me
For I have seen beauty's side
And as an ugly duckling never once did I cry.

3-19-02
