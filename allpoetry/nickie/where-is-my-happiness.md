---
title: "Where is my happiness?"
writer: "Nickie"
slug: "where-is-my-happiness"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/53190-Where-is-my-happiness--by-Nickie"
---

Why are so many people around me happy? 
I wish I could be as happy as them 
They laugh and talk to friends 
I sit alone and think about my life, 
Only being able to wish 
That one day I could be that happy. 
Seeing them laugh makes me want to cry 
Where is the joy that I used to have?

3-21-02
