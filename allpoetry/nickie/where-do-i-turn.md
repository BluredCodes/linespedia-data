---
title: "Where do I turn?"
writer: "Nickie"
slug: "where-do-i-turn"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/53191-Where-do-I-turn--by-Nickie"

Seeing what i have done is hard 
but I deserve it. 
I hurt you 
And for that i must pay. 
Seeing you look past me 
Or not looking at all. 
Wondering if you will see me again 
I guess not. 
I hide my feelings from the world 
So that I don't cry. 
I cry alone 
I have no shoulder to cry on 
Not anymore. 
I can only hope 
That you will forgive me one day. 
Please forgive me soon. 
The depression is killing me inside.
