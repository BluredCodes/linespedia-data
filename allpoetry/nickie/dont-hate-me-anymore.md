---
title: "Don't hate me anymore"
writer: "Nickie"
slug: "dont-hate-me-anymore"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/53193-Dont-hate-me-anymore-by-Nickie"
---

You tell me you don't hate me 
And yet you act like you do. 
You don't talk 
Well to me at least. 
You are happy when i'm not there 
And unhappy when i am. 
You tell me that you are fine 
But are you? 
I don't know what to do anymore, 
And i am confused, 
Confused by you.

3-21-02
