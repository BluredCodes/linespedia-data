---
title: "I remember"
writer: "Nickie"
slug: "i-remember"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I remember the beatings
I took so young.
I remember the healing 
That took so long.
I remember your face,
Eyes bloodshot red.
I remember long nights
Alone in my bed.
You shattered my dreams
And told me I wouldn't survive.
I look back now
At this time gone by.
You never loved me
And I remember this.
There was never a day
I recieved a kiss.

3-16-02
