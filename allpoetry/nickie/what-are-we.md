---
title: "What are we?"
writer: "Nickie"
slug: "what-are-we"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/51375-What-are-we--by-Nickie"
---

What will become of you and I?
Will we rise to the heavens
Or sink to the devil's ground?
Will we find truth,
And if we do will we be ready?
Some say that we know truth,
That truth is based on what is real, 
What we can see and touch.
But if truth is based on reality,
What is God?
We cannot touch Him or see Him
And yet we find Him to be
The creator of truth and life.
So if truth is based on reality 
And reality based on truth
And God is truth
Then what are we?
