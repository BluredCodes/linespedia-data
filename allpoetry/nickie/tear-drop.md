---
title: "Tear Drop"
writer: "Nickie"

Tears
One after the other.
Each holding a part of you
Now slowly being released from my body.
Each tear is a lie you have told me.
I dry my eyes and one tear falls.
It is the biggest of them all
Your "I love you"
Now gone.

3-16-02
