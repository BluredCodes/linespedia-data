Taken in on the deal that we madeIt felt a sore improper tradeFor me to give and you to rakemy soul across a vapid lakeof utter disregarding tonesthat seered my skin and played my boneslike violins and death knell drumsI never came until you cumbut it never was as good enoughso I'm leaving youI call your bluff
