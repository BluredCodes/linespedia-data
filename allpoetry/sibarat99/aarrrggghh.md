Sometimes I feel like Snoopy, dancing away on Schroder's piano while he plays.And Lucy wastes her time watching Schroder,while linus wastes his time waiting for a big pumpkin.And Pigpen wonders why the world can't accept him the way he is,while Peppermint Patty wonders why she can't admit the way SHE is (gay).And Charlie Brown pays a nickel to his shrinkto find out what we already know.That it's Snoopy dancing away on the piano,who ALWAYS steals the show.
