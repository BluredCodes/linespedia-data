Why can't I have thisWhy can't I have thatMy wallet's too thinMy stomach's too fatMy job is too hardMy car is too oldHow did my heart get so goddamned coldThis house is too smallYou talk to muchMy ravaged skin crawls at the very thought of your touchWhat happened to usWhat happened to herI'd give up everything to be like we wereI can remember our souls wired as onethere's too much selfishness under the sun
