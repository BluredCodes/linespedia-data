---
title: "Web Love"
writer: "sibarat99"

May I have this dance, said the spider to the flyMay I spin you around and gaze into your eyesMay we tiptoe along the threads I have madeMay I hold you so close in my arm barricadeMay I kiss you with love till the day that you dieMay I have this dance,said the spider to the fly
