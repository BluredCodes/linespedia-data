I will go full circleI have come to believeThe lift of a thoughtthe might of the seaThe strength of ideasthe roll of the landThe emotion in mindthe sun on the sandThe pride in my soul,is the womb and the seedThis flesh I am inseems so foreign to meI am trapped in the earthMy body roots on a treeAnd I pray for its deathnearer God unto thee
