Window of love
I spread my bruised untamed heart
I encircle the arch of your arm
as you lift the flute to your mouth
you smile
as your masculine cold hands,
embrace the curve of my body
your forehead brushes mine
extending your lips
and I am so in love with you 
that
it burns the crevices of my heart
and when you touch me electricity
from the tips of your fingers
flowing to the left of my shoulder
increases in speed to the corner of my tongue
and this is love,baby
L-O-V-E, 
here is your red carpet moving upward
to in between 
the sheets
you and I
this is marriage
why does it hurt like this,
as you uncover all the pain buried under bushes
hide under dirt in the treasure beneath my Chasity belt
which is secured with lock and key
I suppose I will learn to play the role as your love slave,
right?
