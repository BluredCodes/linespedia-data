fathershould i release the feelings of yester-yearand forgive you the things you did, saidfallshould crumble misguided love and add you back into my prayer listinto my phone book of return callsall 'cause of your glory seekingcould not allow another man to step on your gucci shoessoon my wedding dayyou embraced mecouldn't kiss meto say you look beautifullike your ma-mayou skipped overgrowing into a young ladybut mama said it oncethen twicekissing me on my foreheadand had a stream of tearsdown her face'cause she was seeing the babythat she rocked for 21 yearswalk down the aislesomething that was once robbedbut like a puzzle piece you fit inunwantedun-neededfor meso i had to suppress all these feelingand love you again
