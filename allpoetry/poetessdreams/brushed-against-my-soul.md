---
title: "Brushed Against My Soul"

arched fingers on hand
as legs entwines
and water droplets
tear up my robe seam
the slipping, sliding of movement
as you brush against my soul
as your touch elongates
my joyous moan
the beauty of your touch
is why i love you so much
your touch,
on my mocha skin
and your chocolate wonder
breathing in
as it brushes against my soul
my head rests upon your chest
