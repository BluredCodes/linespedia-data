I am a seng pot of possibilitiesWhen reality bites the corner of my assThe slanted walls that are caved inMay be I should be Juliet and drink the serum To my death, for the thought of loveAnd I can kiss myself a thousand timesStill that doesn’t take the emotionsThe candy-coded dreams that are left in submissionWhat will be my strength to pick my heavy breast to move onTo take the string of corruptness from my beingTo open the door that is so far away in my brain which has been shut offThe years of abuseMust I reject all that I am, So I can be forgivenTo be a good wife   a good woman           a good personI refuse to be the government’s dummyTo be the product of their emotionI wish my life was easyAnd I could push the clouds from my eyes give myself a plastic heartIt will be the pace-makerThe dream setter  free me!!! Can I keep trucking? move on again, I saymove on, I sayloudly and clearlyfor the strength of overcoming all that maybe ignored~
