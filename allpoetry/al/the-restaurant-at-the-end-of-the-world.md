The Restaurant At The End Of The WorldI hope some day we will all meetIn the Restaurant At The End Of The World.Where we will gather to eatA final Millennial feast,A beginning eternity to greet.And when you smile at my smileI will feel your hand upon my cheek.As friendship is served beyond measureBeside the generous creek.Where one will thirst no moreOr look for the incompleteCup of happiness, peace, and joy.There will be psalms to reapAnd after the last courseSlowly fold our napkins, tidy, and neat.Alan W. Jorgensen - 23 September 2001Dedicated to Princess DebSmile and Silent Stormme
