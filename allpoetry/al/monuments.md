Monuments

The points in each life
The patterns and memories
All the laughter
All the tears
All the dreams
And all the fears
Constant flowing voices
Sing the mighty moment
But cheap as grass
The sum of each
The embers of the night
The ashes of the day
The children are coming
Give way, give way.

19 September 2001
Al
