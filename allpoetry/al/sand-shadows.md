Sand Shadows

Down the sea foamed
By I walked only
For the sand to run 
Between my toes
Thought I the sun
Was mine only 
To cast shadows.
But shadows lie
Form false images
Of what we are
Afraid to face
To face only
Few can do.
True to the eyes
Truth can’t hide
Except from those only
Looking at common
Shadows that lie.An old piece, from the sixties
