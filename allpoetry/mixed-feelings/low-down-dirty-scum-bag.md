You had the balls to tell me you love me.
You dick.
I can't believe you. you treat me like a princess then acted the way you did.
You tell me to call and instant message,
then turn around and complain to your brother.
How could you.
You say I call 24/7.
I don't.
You say I instant message you to much...
Well you are the one who tells me to get on in the first place.
So there.
I can't believe you.
You could treat people like this,
more importantly ...me.
I loved you from my heart,
not my head.
You should have accepted that,
but you didn't cause you don't know what love is.
Do you know what I am saying?
I hope.
I fell in love with you and then this.
Do you know how hard it is to hate you?
Do you know how much I love you?
Do you know how much it hurts to even as much look at your picture?
No, and you know why. 
Cause you don't care about girl's feelings.
You treat us right then turn around and hurt us..
The only thing you care about is satisfying your ouwn no good,dumb needs.
No they aren't needs.
You know what they are?
Nothing but no good, lame,low down dirty wants. 
Well you know what!!??
People in hell want ice water!!!!!!
