writer: "mixed feelings"

I know you know what it is like to be afraid.You lay in your bed shivering, Crying and thinking all at the same time.You're wishing that is never happened,and that he was still here.You're wishing he had never gotten a hold of it and lost control.You're wishing you were there to stop it from ever happening.You're wishing that the ambulance had only gotten there to him faster.You're wishing that God had only givin him a second chance.But your wishing didn't do much at all.Because now he is gone and there is nothing you could have done about it.
