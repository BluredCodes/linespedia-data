Poem pick me up when I am down.You always know how to make me smile.Even when times are hard.When I look into your eyesmy knees go weak. Your sparkling eyes,eye candy smile,and that sly smirk you do,just make me wanna die.I melt under my own flesh.If only you knew.Those days when you don't call.or the weeks when your gone.My mind cries. Your sweet voice,sarcasism remarks. ohhh my skin just gets goose bumps.Chills up and down my spine. And my stomach gets tighter and tighter. Your soft dark skin.I love to touch.Your sparkling white teeth. oh it makes me smile.No matter how korny the jokes,they still manage make me laugh.Oh what more could a girl ask for?Nothing,but...but...YOU!
