Sea Dream


Have you ever had the urge to follow a notion?
To sail in a boat and sit on the ocean.
If you pick a day that's calm and clear
And you stop the boat.  What do you hear?
Nothing!  Yes there's nothing to be heard
No slap of wave on boat, not even a bird.

If you go out on a calm dark night
When there isn't a star anywhere in sight
You turn out the lights and just sit back
Tell me - What do you see on this night so black?
Nothing!  Yes there's nothing to be seen
Not even a sparkle of phosphorescent sheen.

So in this world of turmoil and trouble
And pressures at work that will burst your bubble
Should I dare to offer some advice that's free?
Take it if you want or leave it be.
For you can lighten your burdens when
You take your mind to the sea again.


The Swagman
