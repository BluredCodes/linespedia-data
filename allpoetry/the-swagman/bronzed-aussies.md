Bronzed Aussies

Thousands of Bronzed Aussies upon a wall
Never again will any one fall
Looking down on this land they call home
Waiting patiently for their Kingdom to come.

They went away to trudge another land
Or sailed the seas to some distant sand
And flew like birds on a wing and a prayer
Or just went along to provide some care.

Men and women from all walks of life
Stood up and were counted in our times of strife.
In aid of their country they came to the fore
Now they’re Bronzed Aussies fallen in war

On War Memorial plaques all over this land
Thousands of Bronzed Aussies continue their stand,
So hail to you heroes wherever you lie
I hope you’ll be the last of our soldiers to die.



Dedicated to all Australians who fought and fell in war.

The Swagman
