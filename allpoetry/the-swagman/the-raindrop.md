---
title: "The Raindrop"
writer: "The Swagman"
slug: "the-raindrop"
source: "AllPoetry"

Sun shining brightly on the ocean wide
Nowhere a cloud the blue to hide,
Sun shining brightly on the ocean top
Giving birth to a little raindrop.

Breeze gently blowing over the sea
Carries the vapour as high as can be,
Breeze gently blowing over the shore
Clouds forming where they weren’t before

Clouds are gathering over the land
Vapours together are starting to band,
Clouds are gathering over the hills
The raindrop starts falling to fill the rills

The raindrop is falling to the earth below
Giving it moisture making it grow,
The raindrop is falling to the mountain stream
The end of its life it would seem.

The stream is flowing through the slopes
Carrying the water, renewing life’s hopes,
The stream is flowing through the plains
Into the creeks and the rivers it drains.

The river flows gently towards the coast
An artery of life for the raindrop to boast,
The river flows gently towards the tide
The sun shining brightly on the ocean wide.
