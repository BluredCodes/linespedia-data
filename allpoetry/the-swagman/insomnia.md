Insomnia

Again sweet sleep you have taken flight
And left me here alone this night
Now comes the pain to swoop and bight
Is there nothing to ease my plight?

The pain in my legs the pills doth fright
But the pain in my mind continues to fight
On and on no sleep in sight
Again shall I greet the morning's light?

Having trouble finding the words which might
Ease my mind to its slumber filled right
I wonder if the future is so bright
And if everything will be alright.

Now my mind's at a loftier height
In someone else it's taking delight
Of the future I have no insight
But Sleep cometh - From this day I must alight.

by  The Swagman
