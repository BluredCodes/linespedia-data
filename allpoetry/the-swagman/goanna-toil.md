Goanna Toil


For those of you who are not sure
A goanna is a large monitor
Its patterned skin looks like lace
And it gets along at a rapid pace

In days gone by we used their oil
In liniments for after toil
No longer is that done today
For now it’s made another way

How’d we get the oil you ask?
Well I’m here to tell you it was a task
To get this oil that’s smooth as silk
We’d find a goanna we could milk.

To find them was no problem at all
We just used a lizard call
We’d round them up into the yard
Up to now it hasn’t been hard

Have you ever tried putting a goanna in a bail?
It kicks and scratches and swishes its tail
With that accomplished we think we can cope
But now its time for the leg rope

Success at last - Oh what a treat
But what’s this I see - There isn’t a teat
Then Pop comes over and calm as can be
Says “You silly fool that’s a he not a she”


By The Swagman
