You’re Beautiful

Your lips are as soft as the velvet dew
And as sweet as the morning mist
Over wildflowers blooming anew
Under the morning sky gently kissed
By the dawn’s light slowly creeping
On the stars as they’re softly sleeping.

Your hands are as gentle as a butterfly
And as warm as a golden ray
Of the sun as it crosses the sky
Searching for the end of the day
With a passion which burns so bright
Then fades giving way to the night.

Your eyes are as blue as the oceans deep
And as bright as the brightest star
In the night sky where angels sleep
On soft white clouds drifting from far
Beyond where our minds can measure
The joys of their own sweet pleasure

Your heart is pure like new fallen snow
And loving like a gentle sea breeze
As it kisses the moon’s soft glow
Over rolling waves on steel grey seas
As they caress a sandy beach
On an island just our minds can reach.


                                        By The Swagman
