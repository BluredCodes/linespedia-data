water music

'look up' i said and he tilted his head to stare up into
 the sky 'what' 'i'd love to swim in it' 'what the sky'
'yeah especially when it's like this'
[it's real dark i can barely make out the line of his
 body against the blackness the moon and attendant
 constellations are spread out like a map of my most
 beautiful dreams]
'wouldn't you' 'what' 'like to swim in it' 'yeah sure
 whatever' 'your excitement overwhelms me' 'sorry'
'that's okay it was a stupid question' 'want to go for
 a ride' 'sure' 'okay come on my car's parked over
 there' we walk through the field to his car and i wait
 for him to unlock the doors
[oh god you're so beautiful i love you i love you
 i love you]
 inside the car it's dark and smells like his house
'kiss me' i said 'kiss me please'
