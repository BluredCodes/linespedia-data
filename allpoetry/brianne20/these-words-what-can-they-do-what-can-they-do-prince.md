these words what can they do what can they do prince

intricate stars fall  little
sparks   give me my romeo
fireflies breathe kisses
crown burnt lips  heart
angel  in love with  light
etoile  turn the night into
water  air  juliet dreams
brianne falls  beautiful
beautiful  prince1999,
