I would like to thank
my hair for being so limp
today –
and so grossly unattractive.
every greasy strand
flat against my head
pushes him further and
further away from me –
the way that things should be.
I’d also like to thank my
legs – you never looked fatter
or flabbier than you did
this morning, and for that
my sincerest appreciation.
And of course, my butt –
where would I be without you?
probably being kissed
and having lovely lovely rough sex –
and really,
who wants that?
