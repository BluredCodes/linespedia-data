skin

my heart beats
  feebly like a dying
bird
against the caging
  of my ribs
and when the raging sea
    I can never
   call love
threatens to break free
  and explode
     implode
 burn with all the heat of 
      a million
   tiny stars
my skin subdues me
  the translucent 
 thinness
     the pale blue veins
   delicate curves
    and folds
it contains me
  I am held in place
    with fragility
   and the waves
   crash harmlessly
        against the
      sea wall1999,
