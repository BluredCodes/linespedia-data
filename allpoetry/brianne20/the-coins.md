The Coins

you were young
   and now you are old
 from eighty-nine to
    ninety
 (the new decade)
    your profile sharpened
 your chin started to
    sag
 and you began to look
    infinitely more forbidding
   more fierce
       more tyrant-ish
 but princesses don't age,
           do they?
   you know it and I know it
they simply smile, and hand
         in their
               tiaras
    and turn into queens
in ninety-nine you bought
      a new crown
 you aged to a 
       fat old lady
 and they had to change the coins
             again
to accomodate
       your increased
                      girth
