I am the lover's worst nightmare.
His tongue is dry and
swells up to fill his mouth
like a bloated sponge
between the cracked lips
that once spelled
DESIRE and
WANT
He comes to me
a little boy begging, and asks
for his drop of water.
My blood dried up years ago
I tell him.
My bones are hard as rock.
The only water in this desert
are your tears, if you can
make yourself cry.
It's alright, he says.
I LOVE YOU LIKE SALT.2000,
