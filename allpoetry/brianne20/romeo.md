Romeo

I woke up to find that I had
 become overly sentimental.
Poetry fell from my lips
 and I could not stop
 myself from saying I love
    you in more than
 words.  The day passed on
   with its coincidences and
 misplaced trusts, and when
evening fell the final scene
    was stripped of
 sensibility and god the pain
was suddenly so real.
  I felt devoid of my self.
I could only hold you and cry.1999,
