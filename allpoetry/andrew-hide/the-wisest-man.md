Around the island a stories told
of the wisest man,
in the days of olde.

He tested many, all those he could,
remove the sword
from the stone it stood.

When Arthur came and passed the test,
he crowned him king
to rule the rest.

He had no title or medals on his chest,
his job from evil
to protect the rest.

For centuries to come
the tale will be told,
of Merlin and the days of olde.





Andrew A Hide
1992
