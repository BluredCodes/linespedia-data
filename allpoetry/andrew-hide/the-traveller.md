I travel through this land of mine,
a freeman through and through,
in every town I have a home,
I always find a view.
I travel north, south, east and west,
happy to be where I am,
and to every person that I meet
I am a different man,
they don't know where I'm going,
or from where I came,
only that I'm here now,
one day, may come again.



Andrew A Hide
2001
