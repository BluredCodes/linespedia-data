title: "My Time Here"
writer: "Andrew Hide"

T'was spring when I was born,
and to the sky did reach,
I grew so tall and strong,
before I ever knew it,
spring had been and gone.

Summer then did follow,
a young girl I did meet,
and though we were happy
and days were ever-long,
before we ever knew it
summer had been and gone.

Autumn soon did follow
but for what did I care,
I watched our children growing
tall and very strong,
then I soon did realise,
autumn had been and gone.

Now I am in winter,
I sit here in my chair,
my limbs have all grown spindly,
the wind has claimed my hair,
but sorrow is not in me,
before me lies a door.

So please don't mourn my passing,
for now I know there's more,
and as I go to enter,
further can I see,
behind this strange barrier,
a new spring waits for me.



Andrew A Hide
2001
