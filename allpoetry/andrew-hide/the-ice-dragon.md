An almighty crash of thunder,
as the glacier rips apart,
a steaming, smoking Dragon
rises slowly from its heart.
Her mighty wings unfurling
as she reaches for the sky,
silver, frosty blue, 
the colour of her eye,
she bellows out a screech
as she arcs across the sky,
an amazing sight of wonder,
as the ice spirit starts to fly.
