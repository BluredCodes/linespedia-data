A Dragons Pride
is hard to hide
for wisdom is within.

A thousand years 
he has been through
and this just to begin.

he's sailed through 
the winds of time
upon a golden wing.

The children gather
evermore and with
their song they sing.

A Dragons Pride
is hard to hide
for wisdom is within.For those who LOVE Dragons.
