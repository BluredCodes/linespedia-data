This is a place I hardly know,
I've barely been before,
I haven't walked this ground,
since I was three or four.

Everything is different now,
but one thing stays the same,
the feeling that I get
from being on this land.

My father worked the land here
for many a year and then,
we moved onto another place
and started over again.

This is where I was born,
and learnt to stand and walk,
here I became a person,
here I learnt to talk.

Now I am in adulthood
and life for me is grand,
but no matter where I live now,
this is my homeland.
