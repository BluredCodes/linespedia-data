title: "A Monument in Time."

The Stones, they stand 
in a circle, proud,
weathering the storm,
the sun and the cloud.

Why they were built,
we do not know,
for the gods above?
or the demons below?

But they will stand 
for centuries more,
for scientists to study
and mystics to explore.
