Deep in this darkened water,
where the sun can hardly shine,
I see a glimpse of silver,
treasure from another time.

Gold and silver trinkets,
tumblers and doubloons,
beautifully crafted jewellery,
from a ship once marooned.

And from this darkened hiding place,
to the surface of the sea,
back to the land and museum,
for other people to see.just a bit of fun.
