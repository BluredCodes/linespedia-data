My Mother she created me
and brought me to the earth,
all through my life encouraged me
and taught me of my worth.

My Wife I chose from many,
my partner she would be,
and she shall stand beside me
for all the world to see.

My Daughter she's an angel
and what more can I say
she brings me joy and pleasure
every minute of the day.

Now these three Women I adore,
and one day hope to find,
men all around the world
appreciate woman kind.



Andrew A Hide
1992
