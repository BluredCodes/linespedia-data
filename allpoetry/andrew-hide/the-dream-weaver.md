Deep within a cavern,
in some far off distant place,
a little man is hard at work,
with a smile on his face.

He uses pallets full of colour,
and buckets of emotion,
with stories from his library,
he adds a magic potion.

The Dream Weaver's hard at work,
all through-out the day,
when bed-time comes, on magic wings,
he sends them on their way.

He has made the essence,
the emotion and the theme,
you add your personality,
and that completes the dream.
