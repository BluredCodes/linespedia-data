"Do you believe in Magic?"
I was often asked as a boy,
I wanted to believe in magicians
and magic a brand new toy.

Now thirty years later,
I look back on my life,
remember walking down the aisle
and marrying my wife.

The birth of my five daughters,
their wetting on my knee,
their screaming laughter and giggles,
the joy they bring to me.

Yes, I believe in Magic
from the miracles I've seen
the wonders of creation,
