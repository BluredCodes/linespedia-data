I have given everything,
for now there's nothing more,
I am nothing but a shadow,
as you walk out of that door.

You have taken everything,
all that I adore,
my hopes, my dreams, my memories,
you've striped me to the core.

You say that I'm not good enough,
like the man I was before,
you say your leaving anyway,
as I cry upon the floor.

You've blamed me for everything,
you say I've been a jerk,
but it was you who was seeing others,
whilst I was away at work.
