title: "A fairy secret."

I know a Fairy secret,

don't know if I should tell,

if I told to many,

it may just break their spell.



OK then I'll tell you,

it may come as a surprise,

you'll have to take my word for it,

I wouldn't tell you any lies.



A pixie once did tell me,

as he sat upon a stool,

if you look for fairies in day light,

then your nothing but a fool.



At night is when you see them,

down in the wooded glen,

far away from city lights,

cats, dogs and of course men.



Fairies only exist at night,

like star-light and the moon,

they change themselves at day break,

to avoid man and his doom.



As the first rays touch the ground

to start a brand new day,

they all turn into bumble bees,

and carry on their way.



So you won't see a fairy,

during the hours of day light,

and you'll never see a bumble bee

in the middle of the night.



Now this secret,

I have give to you,

and you must swear forever,

that you will keep it true.
