title: "The Fairie's Dance."

The Fairies danced, for all they could

amongst the trees, deep in the wood,

and if you sit, patient and true,

you too may find they'll dance for you.



So when you enter, the forest, dark,

no-one around, but if you hark,

the scamper from the bush you hear

is not a monster, wolf or deer.



But Fairies dancing once again,

with no fear, they have no bane,

so if your good and do no wrong,

you too may hear the Fairie's song.







Andrew A Hide

1992
