The way I have acted
I deserve this
The way I treated you
I deserve this
The way I deceived you
I deserve this
The way I lied to you
I deserve this
The way I fought with you
I deserve this
The way I tried to help
I deserve this
The way I didn’t give you your space
I deserve this
For everything I have done to you 
and for everything I didn't
I’m sorry
I'm sorry for treating you the way I did
You didn’t deserve to be treated like this
I am truly sorry 
I know saying sorry wont change anything but
There isn't anything else I can say to change 
What I have done
All I can say is that I deserve what came to me
and I really didn’t mean to or want to hurt you
I got scared
Scared to get to close
This doesn't excuse what I did 
and I know that
Je m'excuse...
