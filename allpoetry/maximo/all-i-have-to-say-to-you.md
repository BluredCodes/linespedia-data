title: "All I Have To Say To You..."

I hope you learn what it feels like
I hope you suffer as I did and still do
I hope your life is destroyed like you destroyed mine
I do not wish you the best
I do not wish you happiness and safety
I wish you pain and sorrow
For what you have done to me
I wish I could be sure you don't do this to anyone else
I've lost all the trust I once had
I can never get it back
I lost that feeling of being safe
That feeling that I’ll be okay to go outside
The feeling that I won't get hurt going out for a smoke
I'm scared of the world cause of you guys
I'm scared to let anyone in
I'm scared because of you...Just getting it all out...
