And you 
The one who broke my heart
The one who distorted my happiness forever
The one who beat me
The one who left me bruises
Left me scars
Scars that will never heal
Bruises that will never go away
The way you took your anger out on me
The way you told me you were sorry
The way you tried to make it all up to me
I have problems now
Trust problem 
Emotional problems
You have scarred me internally and externally
I still have all the memories
All the pain you caused
Were you never taught it was wrong?
Wrong to hit others
Wrong to abuse you girlfriend
What the hell was wrong with you?
Don't you know right from wrong?
And all that time 
I thought it was my fault
I thought I was the one making you do this
I thought I was doing something wrong
I know now it had nothing to do with me
You’re the one who needs the help 
And you’re the one who needs to learn 
Not me
Maybe one day you'll get the help you need
As for me I never want to see you again
Stop dropping by unexpected
Stop showing up at my house
Stop pretending everything is okay
Stop apologising
IT WON"T HELP.Just clearing my mind after 2 long years...
