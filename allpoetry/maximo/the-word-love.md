Telling someone you LOVE then when you really don't 
is just about the worst thing you can do.

Make sure you know what you are saying before you say it
It's the least someone can do.

For it's not a word that should be thrown around.It require a lot of thought and truth.Just a little quote i wrote... please give me your opinion on it! Thanxs...
