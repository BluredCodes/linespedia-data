---
title: "Fear"
writer: "maximo"

I hate you
For what you did to me
The way you abused me
The way you hurt me
The way you took advantage of me
The way you treated me as a whore
The way you drugged me so I didn't know what was going on
You think you saved me from the pain you were causing
From putting me under
I still have all that pain
I still have the vision of the faceless man who did this
I still have nightmares about you
What you did to me will never go away
I will never me able to be the way I was before
I will never be able to do the things I did
I will never be able to go places I once went
Out of fear of meeting you
Out of fear of seeing you face
Out of fear of this happening again
Out of the fear that you've caused me
