I went to a club expecting to have a good time
Have a night out on the town
Just me and my girlfriends
We got to this dance club
We were having a blast
I meet someone 
We started drinking together
We had some good laughs
We had fun, we danced, and we talked.
We were getting along well.
We continued to drink.
Then I woke up 
Not knowing where I was or how I got there
I was in a hotel room
Lying in a bed
My head hurt
My body ached
I realised what happened
I went to the doctors
I was fine
It was true what had happened
I was raped
And it's something I can’t get past.
I can’t get past the pain 
I can’t get past the feeling of waking up there
I still have nightmares about it
I can't trust anyone anymore
I'm sorry...Just letting go...
