I've pushed everyone away
Pushed them out of my life
Got them mad at me
so they wont be around
I've pushed family away and all my friends
I just want to be left alone to deal with my life
I want to put my life together before anyone enters it
I want to start from stratch
I need to do this for me
For myslef
I need time to heal
I need time to put the puzzle back together
I need to be able to handle my own life
Before i can deal with others
I'm sorry 
And this may seem selfish
But it is something i must do
To help myself survive this world
And keep myself from....
