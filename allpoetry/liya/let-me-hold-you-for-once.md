title: "Let Me Hold You For Once"
writer: "Liya"

Let me hold you for once
Let me show you the tenderness I feel
Let me take your face in my hands
and show you the heaven that it brings

Your kisses evoke so many emotions in me
in my dormant secluded soul
Let me utter your name over and over again
until it's etched in my being like a hole

Never before had I felt this passion before
this passion to never let go
To hold your hands forever
To be able to lean on you for long

I want to be a sanctuary for you
an oasis in a desert land
From all your troubles, all your sorrows,
To provide a refuge with embracing hands

Let the future bring what it may
Let all our woes stand in queue
I won't let this moment pass away
to get lost in oblivion

..will treasure this moment for eons and eons
Even when my spirit has passed away

To remember what you gave me today
To remember the love that nothing could sway



I love you darling..
despite my fears
despite the angst
