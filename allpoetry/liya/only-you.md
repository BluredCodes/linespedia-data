---
title: "Only You"
writer: "Liya"
slug: "only-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Never can show you what you gave me
Never can tell you what you mean
Never can guess how my life would have been if you didn't come along
yes I had a vision that I had seen...

It was a life without passion, a life without love
a passing of days as a lifeless being 
You gave me a zest that I didn't know I had inside me
You gave me laughter and you make me sing    
 
You are really a shoulder I can cry on
You are truly someone I can share my sorrows with
I myself may not be able to give you all that you deserve
But just know that I want to, and always will

You came to my life like a shooting star
a star that I never dreamt of seeing
Yes I had my doubts, I had my fear
But your love outshined all inside my being

You'll never know what you mean to me
or how much your presence I cherish
You are the person who resides closest to my heart 
and there are boundless emotions that you unleash...


I don't think I can live a life without you anymore
not now..
not everFor the man in my life
