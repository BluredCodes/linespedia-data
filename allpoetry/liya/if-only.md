---
title: "If only"
writer: "Liya"
slug: "if-only"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/24888-If-only-by-Liya"
---

Must life always be a path full of cruel choices
Must life always be a hazy confusion
Must it always lead to disaster and catastrophe
Must it always make you lost

Lost in the mist of love, confusion and hate
Lost in the mist of fear, distress and pain
Lost in the realm of no sensation at all
Lost in the arena where you can't see a thing

Had so many dreams about this one life I have
so many wishes out of this crazy paradox
only to see them get crushed in the realm of reality
only to see them murdered by the hands of fate

If only I could turn back the hands of the clock
If only I could go back to that long lost past
If only I could get back those dreamy childhood days
If only I could touch that innocence again
I'd have given my all

To rest in peace
and never to wake up again
