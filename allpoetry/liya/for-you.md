---
title: "For you"
writer: "Liya"
slug: "for-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

Why did you vow about your love to me
Why did you promise me so many things
Why did you make my heart grow weak
Why did you make me dream

I was well and fine in my solitary world
with my solo dreams to cushion my pain
Going along my own way
bearing the dark clouds, thunderstorms and rain

I am so afraid to let go of my heart 
so afraid to become vulnerable
so afraid to face the music of life
in case I'm not able
to bear the uncertainty, confusion and bewilderment
along with hope, excitement and enjoyment
that you bring along in my world
that you add to the fickle world

My demons will never leave me I tell you
I will always question fate
I have seen the shattering of dreams
Are you sure it's for each other we are made

Yes I know you don't wanna hurt me
I know you don't wanna ever see me cry
But when the illusion is broken...and the chemistry is olden
you WILL eventually see me die

just don't try to hold on to it then 
just say a nice goodbyeIt's for you
You know who you are
