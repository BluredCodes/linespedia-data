---
title: "Where are you"
writer: "Liya"
slug: "where-are-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

When I gaze at the stars every night..trying so hard to lose the pain in their glory..I feel a presence beside me.. avenging my solitude.. 
Is that you?
When I cry like crazy in the dark depths of the night..I feel a presence beside me.. soothing me.. 
Is that you?
When from within the misery of the night a new dawn emerges..with new hopes and dreams..I feel a presence beside me.. urging me to go on.. 
Is that you?

who are you..
where are you..
tell me..
