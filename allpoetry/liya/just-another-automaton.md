title: "Just another Automaton"

Survival ---that's the name of the game
Ruthlessness -- guess that's the same
Nothing's forever
The present is all you can take
There's nobody for you
You are the only one for your sake

Days will go by, nights will pass
Drawn in shades of grey
On bleak empty canvas
Regrets will come haunting by
Resignedness will reign
But what the heck, that's life
Not the fairy tale of the insane

Yes..still I hold on to it, still I cry
But hey I'm getting over it - just let me try!



cheers to all you automatons out there
I'm one of you.
