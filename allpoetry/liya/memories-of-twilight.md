---
title: "Memories of Twilight"
writer: "Liya"

So many memories fly pass
so many memories touch me by
  not a tempest, not a pandemonium
    just a feather light touch and a sigh
  
Of a lost world woven in melody
Of times in a dreamy haze
  
  of people long gone
  of laughter turning fade



I see a hawk gliding by...



no more the playful shrieks we were so chastised for
nowhere a word you can hear
but the whispers in the air, in the rustlings of the leaves
they are so near...
they don't leave me by

My breath catches in midway
Is someone standing beside?
  no...it's just the breeze playing silly...
I rather push it aside

I see some faces in the walkway
  they are not the ones I seek
My loved ones...my sacred ones...
are they anywhere to be seen..?

Those kids are playing hide & seek
  but that's not the game to play
You can just climb the tiled roof over there
and it will reveal the hide-away

You should rather play catch-me-if-you-can here
that's what I used to say 
Ron used to disagree
  he was always a stubborn kid anyway
  (I wonder what his kids now might have to say)

hey I swear I heard a sigh!
what might it be..?
  ..'cause the person that that sigh belonged to
is no more to be seen

I have the telephone I can call her up
the time zone doesn't matter
  but what's the use...why bother
    she might just ask my name



Memories of midnight
Memories of the twilight
Memories woven in melody
  memories to be left aside



Dawn shines over this bungalow as it did before
sunlight flickers through the mangroves in the same way
  only the person who used to stand here watching it with me
  is some couple of thousand miles away

the avalanche of memories
the calligraphies of the past...
  
  why bother...why pursue
  they weren't meant to last



I might as well get a move on
a minute of my precious time is already gonemy ever-precious childhood days...
