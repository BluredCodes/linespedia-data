---
title: "Solitude"
writer: "Liya"
slug: "solitude"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/24204-Solitude-by-Liya"
---

So many nights have gone by
gazing at the stars
so many days..
spent in a haze
looking for you
wishing you were here

at least beside me
if not for me
as a semblance of sanity, as a means of escape
from the realm of my world
from the realm where dreams shatter
where hopes fade away
and where days pass by

Tears have fallen heedlessly
like dew drops on concrete floor
I've learnt to live with it
there's no other way really
But I have not learnt to live without you---
the vision for my survival..
wishing you were here
wishing you'd come

Wishing so many wishes...
And listening to the ridicule of fate---
No one will come
No one will be there
in this hard pebbled path
to be walked alone
