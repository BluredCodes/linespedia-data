Pain is power
Pain is strength
Hatred, Horror, Angst, Disgust
Feel the tremor in their lust!

Eject the pain in my nerves
Feel the high in the sigh!
Ethereal feeling of infiniteness
The world awaits to be MINE!

Kill me to make me live
Slash me to see the blood
Pour me to drink from the hollow
What sensuality in the divinity of this flood!

To reach the peak of inhuman strength!
To devour the doom of day
Dancing with the devils
Drunk with their evils
Feeling the fear within 
--of death to thrive
NEVER BEEN SO ALIVE

Shake the core of my existence
Reset the arteries of my being
Spill the contents on greasy mud
Trod to kill the worms being seen

Bring on the EXORCISM
Bring on the LIGHT
Call the charms of Aurora
To glow in this plight!

No more under the spell of Morpheus

Give me an eternity of vigilant sightthe reality of darkness....:)
