---
title: "Dont Like Em'"
writer: "Rapid"
slug: "dont-like-em"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/47276-Dont-Like-Em-by-Rapid"
---

Do I care what someone thinks?
And if I did would I break?
Make no mistake,
I am who I wanna be.
What somebody says
it aint gonna phase me.

I dont care if I'm hated,
cause my life is already r-rated.
Liars, I dont like 'em,
Fakies, arent real, and
Me, this is who I'm gonna be.
This is the real me.
