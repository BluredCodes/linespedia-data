I tried to run,
                     I really did.
                                 But I never could,
                     outrun my sins.

             Drugs,
                   I thought they were the way out.
                                   That was not true,
                    cause still myself I fought.

       Anger,
            it almost killed me several times.
                    My mind seemed to be,
                           an endless, black sky.

       I've always wondered,
                 who is my worst enemy?
                        I can answer that,
            cause that person is me.
