Written in the sands of time,
    is our signatures, yours and mine.
    As the last light of dusk is shed,
    winds are whirling in our heads.
    Though our love is a strong thing,
    me must learn to endure the pain.
    The last bit of sand will be uncovered,
    revealing how we love eachother.
    So hold on tight here comes the wind,
    we'll be together until the end.Oh, yeah by the way, I changed my name to Rapid/ was poet0angel
