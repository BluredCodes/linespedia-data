drip
   drop
drip
   drop
3 a m
the dripping
the dropping
  water from the faucet
hypnotizing me
 with tired 
 bloodshot eyes
i stare at the pine branch 
outside my window
  the wind punches it around
little white flecks
  are scattered among the pine needles
atop the bark
 the first snow of the year
         i am the first to witness it
in all its slushy glory
 because i can not sleep
     this pinch in my chest
 this disgusting feeling in the pit of my stomach
keeps me awake
   awake and scared
what ifs
are spinning a web
  a web of terror around me
a web only i could have gotten myself into
   only i
only me
     me and him
im stuck in this web
   this web that is suffocating me 
in the morning
    ill know once and for all
  ill know if i shall kill myself
before i strangle in my web
or ill know
   if i can breathe again
breathe and live again
   a plus or a minus
one little pink line
   will decide my fate
