title: "[hold my face in your hands]"
writer: "tickledpink"

hold my face in your hands
kiss me like you mean it
tell me you love me
tell me i matter most
you dont need it as long as you have me
i wont ask
i wont tell
its up to you
you say its just who you are
its not who you are
its something you do
so you say if i wont ask
if i wont tell
you'll do it for yourself
you'll stop
so hold my face in your hands
kiss me like you mean it
tell me you love me
say you'll stop

liar
your pathetic
you last a split second
then slide around behind my back
slither around and think i cant see
keep on thinking im blind
but dont forget 
i feel
i feel you stabbing me in the chest 
every time 
so have your fun
and pretend i cant see
but know, just know
that i know the things you do
and i know how you can lie
i know how slick you are

you know you can turn me with one look
i cant stay mad at you
i cant stay upset
i can just look in those eyes
and your gaze pushes my hurt away
but dont be fooled
you cant make it go away like that
you just mask it for a while
and make me think you care
but everytime you turn around
it comes right back 
it hurts

but dont worry about it
hurt me all you want
have your fun 
im blind
im unfeeling
do what you want
cause someday ill be gone
and you'll realize 
which door you should have opened
and which one you should have let close
slowly
slowly
until you hear that click
and its gone
im behind door number one
and your slamming it on me in slow motion
