title: "[i hate the way you love me]"

i hate the way you love me
nothing but neglect
hurt is all i feel
ignore me
then love me
confusion
i am nothing to you in your company
i am everything to you alone
afraid of the truth
use others for your deeds
make me cry
a single tear i shed
your not worth the grief
