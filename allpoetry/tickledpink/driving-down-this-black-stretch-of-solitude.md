driving down this black stretch of solitude
every glaring set of headlights
are yours
walking blindly through this imaginary crowd
every blond haired head
is yours
lonely strolls down these over-populated streets
every stranger that brushes my arm
is you
i'm desperately searching for you
any little piece of you
digging deep into myself
flipping through the pages
every memory
is of you
