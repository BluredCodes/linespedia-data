title: "[dont worry]"
writer: "tickledpink"

dont worry
i could never
    kill
myself
im too weak
 for that
too weak 
to even make the suffering
stop
  just frail enough 
to lie in my sorrows
 maybe ill get lucky
maybe ill drown
