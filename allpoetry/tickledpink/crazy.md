crazy
together
what are we
what's this between us
crazy 
together
wild touch
soft
whats love
just a word
no meaning
like a title
no meaning
just a word
to describe
the indescribable
thats us
indescribable
crazy 
together
dont need a word
to describe what cant be 
we know it between us
