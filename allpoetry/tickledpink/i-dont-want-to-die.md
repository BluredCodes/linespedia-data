title: "[I dont want to die]"

I dont want to die
I just want this shit to stop
I want it all to end
and leave me alone
i want to be left alone
but not by myself
i cant be by myself
i need you
your not here
i cant go to you
because i cant get out
i cant move
you cant hear me crying for you
because your party is so much fun
my head is aching 
my eyes are burning
i am bleeding
my wrists are stinging
my life is staining the tile floor
a red river twists its way
over my hands and down my legs
through my salty blurr 
i search for you
but your not there
why cant you hear me
why wont you come
put down your drink
throw away your addiction
and listen to me screaming 
i need you
please
your strength is the only strength
i have
your hand is the only one
that can stop the bleeding
please hear me
