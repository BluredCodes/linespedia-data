thats fine
do what you want
see if i care
mock me for caring
so see if i care
its not up to me
these things you do
so do what you want
hurt me with every 
hit you take
but keep on 
its your life
its none of my business
your none of my business
dont worry
i wont bother you anymore
its more important to you 
than ill ever be
so go ahead
take another hit
toke it up with them
and laugh about the way i care
it doesnt matter
its not important
just like me
