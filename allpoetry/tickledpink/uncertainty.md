uncertainty
   angxiety
never been so scared in my life
          could i be...
no way
   not possible
 not me
    twist, turn
what if...
 making me sick
  freaking me out
      driving me insane
   terrified
oh 
   my
      god
what would i do
      stupid
what were you thinking
be more careful
please 
   god
spare me
    i cant handle that
