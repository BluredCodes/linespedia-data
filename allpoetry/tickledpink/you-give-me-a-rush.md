---
title: "[you give me a rush]"
writer: "tickledpink"
slug: "you-give-me-a-rush"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/16208-you-give-me-a-rush-by-tickledpink"

you give me a rush
intoxication
a barely there kiss
intoxicates me
im drunk off of you
your sanity in this world
that drives me far from sane
your color in this life
that blinds me with its vibrance
you are my drug
my feel good
intoxication
