---
title: "[you]"
writer: "tickledpink"
slug: "you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/16209-you-by-tickledpink"
---

you
     dont
   love
 me
dont say you do
dont say you'll stop
for me
if
  you
    wont
you can say you love me
or you can show me
you can say it
or you can mean it
         
    if you dont mean it
  dont say you do
