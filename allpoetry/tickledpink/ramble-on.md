title: "[ramble on]"
writer: "tickledpink"

ramble on
about this and that
spontaneous thoughts
glide off your tongue 
and bounce around the room
i love your mind
and the things it thinks
you always have a point
to go to war with a point of mine
they slide from our thoughts
and out of our mouths
ready for combat

you speak of things
ive never once thought
and you speak of things
i know all too well
you embrace the things i say
my ramblings
and i hold every word
that rushes past your lips
close to my heart
because its your soul
condensing
and dripping into my hands

i love your words
even if i hate what your saying
i love them because they are yours
