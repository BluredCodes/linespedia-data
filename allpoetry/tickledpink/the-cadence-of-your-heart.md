title: "[the cadence of your heart]"
writer: "tickledpink"

the cadence of your heart
lulls me
i drift off into limbo
eternal ecstasy
rise and fall
with each breath
sleepy eyes
look down upon me
sleepy smile
glows my way
a sleepy kiss
lying here 
in limbo
