ignorance
it really is bliss
for months
i lived in bliss
i thought that light bulb
of realization had flickered
that i was what you wanted
and you didnt need anything else
i thought you would be wholly mine
so i walked
with that wool down over my eyes
so thick that all i could see
was what you wanted me to see
i stumbled into things
and never saw the bruises 
because you 'just didnt want me to be upset'
moths of time have eaten a tiny whole trough the wool
and my bruises hurt me more than you could know
im black and blue
and stained with salty disappointment
there's a winding road to the end
and ive hit every sign on the way
never knowing there was a brick wall
waiting for me at the finish
please
dont let me hit that wall
