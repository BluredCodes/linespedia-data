title: "[cramped in this little cage]"

cramped in this little cage
tear stained enclosure
held by these shackles
your words push me to run
run away from you
these sad shackles digging into my skin
into me
holding me here still
excruciatingly
my wrists begin to bleed
cut by the shackles your words 
twist around them
these things you say
carve my soul
piece by piece
with every word
another piece falls to the ground
and whithers
