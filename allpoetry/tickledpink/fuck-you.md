---
title: "[fuck you]"
writer: "tickledpink"
slug: "fuck-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

fuck you
who are you
i dont need you
to teach me to ride
my bike
and i dont need you to
teach me this 
and that
i dont want you around
i hate you
you're callous
unfeeling
pushing me into the 
mouth of hell
flaming arms reaching 
to pull me in
and your pushing me there
get away from me
dont come near me
selfish bastard
just leave me alone
i dont need you
