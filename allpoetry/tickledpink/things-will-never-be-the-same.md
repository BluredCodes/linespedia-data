title: "[things will never be the same]"

things will never be the same
you screwed up...
or was it me
you dropped me and left me
my soul flew away with my heart
following you in your glory
i was alone with nothing.
empty.
tears of sadness
rage
sheer confusion
tears of happiness...
i am still in your heart
you want me there with you
rushing to hold you
paralyzed by confusion
am i in your heart still?
or are you just masquerading
tell me your thoughts
tell me your feelings
emotions gone wild
nothing in my eyes but confusion
hurt
nothing in your eyes
a little light in the depths still shines
tell me your thoughts.
