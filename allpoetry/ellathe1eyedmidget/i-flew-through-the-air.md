I flew through the air with the greatest of ease,In the bedroom I was such a tease,until the damned chandelier broke,landed on Rob and gave him a stroke.You can take the midget out of the circus,but you'll never take the circus out of the midget.Oh wait, I'm ramb'ling again.What was I doing.....okay I was...no, maybe...Oh yeah, No..wait,UHM.....Shit!
