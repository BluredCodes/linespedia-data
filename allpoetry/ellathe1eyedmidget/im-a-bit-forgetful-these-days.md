It's seems i'm always forgettingwhere I put my specks,I can never find my hearing aid,why are my pants so wet?Rob! Where are my car keys?Did you hide them again?!Help me find my boots,I think it's gonna rain!I lost my dentures....helpme find them now!I can't see where i'm going..OW! Damned table leg! Stubbed my toe!Rob! Where are you,you old fart!?HELP! ROB!
