---
title: "Small Brain"
writer: "EllaThe1EyedMidget"
slug: "small-brain"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/21023-Small-Brain-by-EllaThe1EyedMidget"
---

That nasty little man on here yesterday,
he was callin' us girls names,
small brained little boy,
playin' childish games!
His Mamma should have taught him
to have more respect,
I suppose that he must be,
a child of neglect.
He will never learn,
nor will he ever change,
because he does not care who he hurts,
because of that small brain.
