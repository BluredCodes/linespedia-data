---
title: "I'm Short"
writer: "EllaThe1EyedMidget"

I'm short,I'm short,Yeah, i'm short!Poeple like to point,I just say i'm short,yeah, i'm short!I'm 2 foot 4,my boyfriend is a bore,I can't even remember his name.I'm short,yeah, i'm short!
