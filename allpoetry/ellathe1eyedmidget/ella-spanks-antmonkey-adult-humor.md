Yank'n,
wank'n,
Monkey spankin'
Ant you are a card!
I'm just afraid
you'll get to excited
and yank it much
too hard!
Dislocated,
relocated,
disjointed,
a hellish pain,
Don't you know
that you'll go blind,
Ant you are insane!
Stains on the ceiling,
sticky on the floor,
it's hanging off the ceiling fan
and dripping off the door!
Ella will spank it for ya,
ya know that I adore ya....
Let me get my rubber gloves.This one's for you Antmonkey!!!!!!:P
