title: "Misguided"
writer: "cathymc"

The light shines through the face of the clouds 
The blue is clear in your translucent eyes 
The showers cannot blind this heavenly embrace 
The tears cannot stain this crystallized face. 

Bear not the pain, close not the mind 
Teardrops are falling, leaving me blind 
But always I can draw from you, from love. 

If by chance your balloon is released from captivity 
Do not place blame upon fate or destiny 
Catching the bubble might cause it to break 
Bursting the bubble would be a fatal mistake 
For even the clouds are jealous of the chained 
Too free they are, traveling unordained. 

Trace the photograph outlined for the child 
Destiny searches for the meek and the mild 
Fill in the empty faces with a few forgotten phrases 
Duplicate and then proceed.
