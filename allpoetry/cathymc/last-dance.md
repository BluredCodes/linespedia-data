Holding your picture in my arms 
Dancing together across the floor 
Almost the way it could have been 
If only you had only loved me more 

Close enough to feel your heartbeat 
Swirling together in an imaginary cloud 
Painting the world with a brilliant sheen 
Whispering your name outloud 

Dancing, though the music has ended 
With tears streaming down my face 
For I have seen what could have been 
And lost it without a trace.
