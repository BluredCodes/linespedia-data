---
title: "Make her Cry"
writer: "Leylia"

Go for it,
Do what you can,
Say what you can,
Just make sure she's in tears before you leave,
Because then you've won.

If she is crying so hard that she can't control her breathing,
Then you've won.
If she's collapsed on the floor, unable to stand up,
Then you've won.
If she tells you she's given up on you,
Then you've won.

If you can honestly tell her that you hate her,
Then you've won.

Do you really think she broke down before you did?
Do you really think she gave up before you?
Has it occured to you that she has done
EVERYTHING she can to ensure your happiness?
Of course it hasn't.

You don't realize the sacrifices she's made for you.
You don't realize that she's let me down to help you.
You don't realize that we all still love you, no matter what.

But we really don't like you, bro.
So make your mother cry, baby.
We expect nothing less from you.
