---
title: "Three months and Sixty Years"
writer: "Leylia"

Three months, 
Sixty years,
Passing by,
Standing here.

Ever present,
Ever gone,
Always lessons,
Scoolboy's yawn.

Doesn't matter,
Wasn't there,
Never saw them,
Doesn't care.

Sees the pictures
On the news.
Wandering victims,
Rescue crews.

Children somber,
Grown-ups cry.
Kids remember,
Adults die.

Passing by,
Standing here,
Three months 
To sixty years.
