title: "Snow"
writer: "Leylia"

Perfection in its finest form
Is the unexpected winter storm,
When fields of green that never knew
The lovelines of frozen dew
Find themselves quite suddenly
Engulfed by sweetest purity.

Perfection at its greatest height
Is breath that comes in clouds of white,
Drawing faces in the snow;
People stop to say hello.
Friendliness abounds today;
Everyone's in love today.

I feel that "sweet, euphoric high"
Your form against the cloud-rimmed sky,
To feel your frozen fingertips,
The warmth I still find in your lips.
I couldn't feel the cold at all,
When in your arms I'd gladly fall.

I know enough of love to say,
