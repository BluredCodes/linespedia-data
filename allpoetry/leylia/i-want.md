You told me all you wanted was the truth,
I told you all I wanted was the same.
You thought my friends were horribly uncouth,
I thought yours were villainous and vain.
All I wanted was to be your friend.
Foolishly I thought you'd want that too.
You never wanted what we were to end,
You wanted me to stay a part of you.
"Want" is far too strong, I must confess.
Over "love" and "need" it stands above.
Power to destroy, and to undress,
Power to prolong such empty love.
Now our future eats away our past,
