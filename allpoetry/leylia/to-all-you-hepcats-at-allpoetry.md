Well, my friends...
The time has come.
Spring break beckons,
The beach and the sun
Await my arrival,
I have to comply.
And so I bid to you
A fond goodbye.

I know I am known
To only a few,
But that doesn't mean
That I won't miss you.
This site has been quite
A life-saver for me.
Without you I'm not certain
Where I would be.

You've all been supportive,
Just wanted to say,
I'm so glad Chameleon
Sent me here that day.
This isn't forever,
So don't get me wrong
I'll see you in one week,
Which isn't that long.

IndyDom, you rock.
What more can I say?
Your comments have helped me
So much on my way.
Mooseman, the same gratitude
Goes to you.
(Expect that cash in 
The next day or two.)
ZeusDawg, your rhythm
And style make me giddy.
(Tina got roses
Because she's so pretty!)

Chasingtheday
And gecko and nam,
humanimal,
Chameleon
malevolent angel,
And Blushfulmoon,
And Doc...
