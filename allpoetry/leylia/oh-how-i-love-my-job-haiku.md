Just give me one page!
Control-P, Print!  Control-P,
PRINT, GOD DAMN YOU, PRINT!WHAT KIND OF CRACK IS THE DAMN THING SMOKING AND WHERE CAN I GET SOME?  (when frustration takes hold and won't let go until the page prints correctly...oh well, at least i get overtime)
