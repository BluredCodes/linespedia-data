---
title: "III (Siren's Song)"
writer: "Leylia"
slug: "iii-sirens-song"

Beginning as a faint and distant hum,
I heard my nightly lullabye begin.
I crawled up out of bed to watch them come,
Anticipation prickly on my skin.
I pressed my face against the window pane
'Till I could see the spinning, flashing lights
Glancing off the glassy specks of rain,
One thousand kisses, one thousand goodnights.
Their voices changed their tone and started to fade,
I settled back in bed and closed my eyes.
While other children might have been afraid,
I felt safe, almost cradled by their cries.
'Cause I knew they'd bring back their soothing light,
Singing me to sleep tomorrow night.
