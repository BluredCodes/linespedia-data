---
title: "Lost and alone"
writer: "psychoblue23"
slug: "lost-and-alone"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/36713-Lost-and-alone-by-psychoblue23"
---

Waking up already lost, 
who needs life to make me miserable.
I have love.

Wondering around alone,
who needs hate to drive me to the edge.
I have love.

I know of of love, so I am subjected to pain.
I know of desire so I am subjected to loneliness.

I know of love.
And I know of suffering.
