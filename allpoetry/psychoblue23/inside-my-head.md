It's all the voices, the sounds in my head, I dont wanna listen anymore, the constent fear of being mad. . . . .Is it a fear or is a reality just in my head? 

Waking up during the night. . . another 2 hrs of sleep. The fear of life passing me by wakes me and says get going.

It's all the things I see that no one understands and cant realize the truth about when I know what's right but not the best thing to do.

Faces and voices. . . then more voices then faces, some that are real and fake to others but seem to be my only friends on this twisted uneventfull journey to the end. . . 

crazy. . . .
