Your lips are divine
Like a rose that is just about to bloom
Your smile
Is like crystal and diamonds
Shinning in a cave
Your eyes are like the sea
And are pure when close to me
Your face reminds of perfection
Because you are the one I lovethis is my most recent and favoirite
