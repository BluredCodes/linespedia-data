For the first time
In a long time
I feel really happy
For the first time
In a long time
I feel beatiful
And loved
For the first time
In a long time
Someone told me
That I was beautiful
Just a little compliment
But it went so far
So very far
So now I smile
And I drink
My gingerale
And I feel happy
For the first time
In a long timeThank you Jen...
