Does the stain your sheet
As you lay crumpled on the floor?
Do you think that if you reached out
We would simply slame the door?

Does the pain give you comfort
While you're lying there at night?
Does the thought that we might know you
Make you shake with fright?

Do the tears soak your pillow
As you try to fall asleep?
Is it us, trying to help you
That's causing you to weep?

Do your thoughts come back to haunt you
As you're lying there in bed?
Do consuming thoughts of darkness
Fill your pretty head?

Do my ramblings make you queasy
As you're wondering what to do?
Do my thoughts and words both scare you
Simply 'cause they're true?

Does the blood stain your carpet
When you're all alone at night?
Do you think that if you'd let us
We would try to make things right?For a friend...if she chooses to listen...
