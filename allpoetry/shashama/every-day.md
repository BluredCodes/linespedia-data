writer: "Shashama"

Every day that I see you
I fall a litlle deeper in love
I wish that I could tell you
To your face. 
Because I know that you know
And I know that
I have told you before
But every time
That I see you face
I realize just how much
I want you to love me back
I wish that I could be sure
That you know the WHOLE truth
And not just a fragment
Because I can't manage to tell you
Not just yet anyhow
But sometimes
When I look into your eyes
Or when we are talking
I know that you know
And I hope with all of my might
That every day
When you see me
You feel the same way

Every day
I love you a little more
