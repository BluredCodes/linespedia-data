---
title: "It's Times Like These"
writer: "Shashama"
slug: "its-times-like-these"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Its times like these 
That I think about you
Wondering
If you ever think about me
And its times like these
When I just want to cry
And I just want you
To tell me that its ok
Its times like this
When I wish
I had someone
To  hold me
And its times like these
When, more then ever,
I want you to smile
Its times like these
That I want to smile with you
And its times like these
When you make me smile
And you light up the room
With your laughter
And I can't
Be depressed anymore
Yes, its times like these
When I love you most
