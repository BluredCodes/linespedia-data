My chest hurts
And I think I'm gonna puke
My heart skips another beat
This is how I feel
Right now

Check the pulse
Fast then slow
Back to fast again
Skip a beat
I'm scared

Yet they don't take
Me seriously
Its just nerves
Well fuck you
This isn't nerves

They wont even check
If I died right now
Of a heart attack
Would they care?
Or be sorry?

Would they say
"We should have
Checked that out
Oh well! Our bad"
Gee thanks mom

I know what they'll say
You're faking it
How the fuck
Can I fake my 
My heart beat?

But it's getting worse
It hurts me
And I will admit it
I am pretty damn
ScaredStupid fucking parents, how can I fake my heart skipping beats? Stupid people...
