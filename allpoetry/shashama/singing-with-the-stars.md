---
title: "Singing with the Stars"
writer: "Shashama"
slug: "singing-with-the-stars"
source: "AllPoetry"

I want to be taken into the dark blue sky
Just wraped up and swept away
Where I can't here the words anymore
And I can sing to the stars, and listen to them sing
I want to visit the dark side of the moon
And fly off the ground and around to the light
I want to see the Earth rise, and bask in it's glory
I want to fly through the sky with the wind at my face
Where nothing can bind me, and nothing tries
I want to get away, to where hope is abundant
And no one could drag me down if they tried
But they don't try, because I am free
This is where I want to be, where I want to go
And the tears swell in my eyes because I know that I can't
The sky can't take me away, and the stars can't hear me
But I don't care, because in my dreams, I still go
I still fly into the dark dark sky and sleep on the moon
I still sing my songs to the ever present audiance
And love every single second of it
And loathe my alarm as it jars me awake for another day
Another day of pain, where my friends turn against me
But at night I can still dream
And during the day I can still hope
I can still hope for the sky to take me awayI feel like this a lot...
