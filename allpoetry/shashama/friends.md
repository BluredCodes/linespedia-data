---
title: "Friends"
writer: "Shashama"
slug: "friends"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/41383-Friends-by-Shashama"
---

You say that we are friends
I'm not so sure
'Cause you hurt me
And don't seem to care

Have you ever said sorry
And meant it at all
After you slash at me
With all of your words?

Do you mean what you say?
Or is it a joke?
Well, joke or not,
It still hurts me

In fact, it hurts a lot
More than you may think
Or maybe more than you know
But still, its painful

You say that we are friends
But I'm not sure
'Cause you're mean to me
And don't seem to care
