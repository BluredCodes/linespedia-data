Slut
Apparently this is the word used to describe me
Slut
Nice. Very catchy. Has a good ring
Slut
What do I do? Sleep around? Nope
Slut
Apparently I can't even stop myself from being one
Slut
Apparently I am just the biggest flirt you ever saw
Slut
Well I am NOT a slut. I am not even a maneater
Slut
I don't play with their hearts, then stomp them to the ground
Slut
I don't constantly flirt, and no matter what you say
Slut
I am not a
Slut
