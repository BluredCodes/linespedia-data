Challenge Description: write a poem about fire, no longer than eight lines. good luck! (17 points)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The fire burns bright tonight, as it hungrily eats the wood 
Like water washing over the smooth oak, it flows dutifully 
Even from an ember, persistant fire can be brought back 
Returning to its full glory, as high as the wind can take it 
Pass through quickly, don't stay too long, you'll be burnt 
The fire may give much, yet it may also take much away 
It's untamed beauty, wild, yet somehow it's so calm 
Be wrapped in my dear fire, and forever warm you shall be
