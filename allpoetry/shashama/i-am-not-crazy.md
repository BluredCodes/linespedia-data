title: "I am not crazy..."
writer: "Shashama"

I am not crazy
Do you remember when
You told me that I was?
To fucked up for you,
To messed in the head?
Do you remember?

I am not insane
I am not a toy for you
I am not something
That you can drop and run
I am alive
Did you know this?

I am not mad
But I still know your face
I still know those words
I still have it
That letter you wrote
Or did you forget?

I am not wild
I am here now
I can forgive you
Maybe I still love you
Maybe not
Do you care?

I am not an animal
I am human
I am whole
I am loved
I am better than you
Did you know?He will never read this....but I still hope that he knows how I feel...
