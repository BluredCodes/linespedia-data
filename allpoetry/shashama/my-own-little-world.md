---
title: "My Own Little World"
writer: "Shashama"
slug: "my-own-little-world"
source: "AllPoetry"

There is a place
Hidden in my mind
Where I can go
To escape

There is a world
So far away
Where I can hide
From you all

There is a land
Beneath the waves
Where I can live
For all time

My own little world
With no pain
Where we can stay
Together

If you knew
This little world
Would you have
Me put away?

My world runs
So close to your
They intermix
At random now

But which is real?
And which is mine?
I have to tell
For myself

This is what I mean
When I say
That I am not sure
What is real











What is real?
