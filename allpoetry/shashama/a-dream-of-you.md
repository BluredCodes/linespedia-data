A dream
Of you
Was all
It took
For me
To know
How much
I love
To see
Your face
And feel
The warmth
You send
To me

A dream
Of you
Was all
It took
For me
To see
How much
I want
For you
To say
Its true
Dear Shay
And I
Can't hide
Once more

A dream
Of you
Was all
It took
To know
That I
Still need
Your words
And I
Still want
You here
And want
You near
To me

A dream
Of you
Is all
It takes
For you
To get
Me trapped
And for
Me to
Know that
I just
Want you
To stay
With me

A dream
Of you
Was all
It took
For me
To know
How much
I love
To see
Your face
And feel
The warmth
You send
