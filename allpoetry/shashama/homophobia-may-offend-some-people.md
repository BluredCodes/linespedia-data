I don't understand homophobia
I don't get how someone could hate someone
For something like that
I mean, something important, like, this guy killed 20 children
I could see hating without knowing the person
But hating for what gender the person sleeps with at night
Its beyond me
I just don't understand it
I wish I could just stand up and scream
At all the closed minded people
And wake them up
And make them see
Sadly, I cannot
So I am stuck fighting every homophobic I come across
I am sorry
If our views conflict
If what I say goes against what you believe
This is what I think
This is how I feel

Homophobia is Gay-_-
