I think I might cry. 

So unusual for me. 


I don't feel like having to deal with any of this any more....


Someone take me away....


To someplace


Where I don't have to do this anymore


I'm so sick of this cycle


Make a friend, lose a friend


Over and over


Till you curl up and die


Left alone


Friendless


When you're old and gray


Will you wish that you had changed things?


Way back when


Will you wish that you would've done something


Something to change things


As they are now


Well you can't change things


So stop trying


I told you it's over


I meant it


GoodbyeNice thoughts, eh?
