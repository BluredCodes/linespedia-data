---
title: "Sory, i kant spel"
writer: "Shashama"
slug: "sory-i-kant-spel"
source: "AllPoetry"

Im terably sory i kant spel
Meye pomes showe thiss
i downt gott goed grammer niether
im sory
i downt putt appostrafes wer theyy ar soposed too gow
and i relly kant spel
i trie too fex meye erers
butt it downt worrk
so im sory
i rely kant rite goed pome niether
im sory
i thinck thaht i wil ennd thiss pome noew
sory
