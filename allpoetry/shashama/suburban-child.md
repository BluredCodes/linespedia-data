I don't want to be a Suburban Child
Where action is low and talk kept mild
I want to go where I can be wild
And not be a drab old Suburban Child

I don't want to be a Country Gal
And have to date some guy named Hal
And instead of "I do" say I "Shall"
Not for me, this Country Gal

I'm not going to be a Suburban Child
Where all my actions are watched and filed
But underneath the bodies are piled
No, not I, no Suburban Child

I really want to be a City Girl
Where I can just let my hair unfurl
And not be bound by rubies and pearls
Just to live as a City Girl
