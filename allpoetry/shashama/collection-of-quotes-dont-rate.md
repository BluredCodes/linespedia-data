“I wish I could fly
From this building
From this wall
And if I should try
Would you catch me if I fall?”
~When I Fall, Barenaked Ladies (BNL)

“Everyone here reminds me
That there’s no point
In going home
When everything there reminds me
That I’m still alone”
~Do You Miss Me?, Lucky Boy’s Confusion (LBC)

“Look at my mouth, a thin painted line
Look at my limbs, bent up and bundled in twine
Forever, ever mine
Form of a tree, shape of a child
I wish I could cry, stuck in a permanent smile
Forever, ever mine
I know you must have loved me sometime
But now I'm just a toy”
~Just a Toy, BNL

“When I leave I won't make a sound 
Make sure no one's around 
Where am I headed? I'm headed westward bound 
I can't breathe this Midwest air tonight… 
Feels like this masquerade should end 
And I'll miss my friends”
~City Lights, LBC

“Why did they pave the lawn?
Why did they change the locks?
Why did I have to break it, I only came here to talk
This is where we used to live
How is the neighbor downstairs?
How is her temper this year?
I turned up your TV and stomped on the floor just for fun”
~The Old Apartment, BNL

“I don’t want to be your other half
I believe that one and one makes two”
~Not the Doctor, Alanis Morsette

“You can call it anything you want the fact remains the same 
I never got to be your Fred Astaire 
You can lie to yourself and all your friends and pretend that you don't care 
But circumstance gets in the way”
~Fred Astaire, LBC

I gained all this weight out of love, not hate
I've got so much love to give
~Stomach vs. Heart, BNL

“I’m not gonna drop my life and run
Just because you ain’t having fun
As if I didn’t have a choice”
~??, Majik

“Why did you paint the walls?
Why did you clean the floor?
Why did you plaster over the hole I punched in the door?”
~The Old Apartment, BNL

“When you dream, sometimes you remember. When you wake up, you always forget”
Dream, in Brief Lives

“I've got this crazy feeling
You've been trying to get to me
When all you have to do is call me”
~Call Me Calmly, BNL

“Watered down connections in time 
Killing songs and bracing fears 
He can't erase cause it sends her to a place 
Where the feelings are sincere”
~Not about Debra, LBC

“That’s the trouble with walls
They’re only mirrors on the inside”
~Mirrors, Majik 

“Well half my friends are crazy and the others are depressed
And none of them can help me study for my math test”
~Grade 9, BNL

“Today I think we’re all insane
For playin’ this world’s sorta game
Take a look at what’s around
Ah, destruction goin’ down”
~Beyond this Curtain, Majik

"It won't rain all the time, the sky won't fall forever"
~It Can't Rain Forever, Jane Siberry

"People always say how you should be yourself, like 'yourself' is the definite thing, like a toaster or something. Like you can know what it is even. But every so often, I'll have like, a moment, when just
being myself in my life, right where I am, is like, enough."
~My So-Called Life

"I judge people by what they might be,-not are, nor will be."  
Robert Browning

"Of all the paths [that] lead to a woman's love
Pity 's the straightest. "
~Unknown

"After the time when you disappeared from me
I began my journey searching for you
The start is on the map, the stenciled picture of an angel
The destination it points to, a waiting dark colisseum
In my trembling heart, the secret kiss of that day
However hard destiny may be
I will keep following it"
~Serra Starr Song, Naoko Takeuchi
