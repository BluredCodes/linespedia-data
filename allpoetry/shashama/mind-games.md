You wanna play mind games?
Go ahead, I can’t hear you.
You don’t control me, you never will.
You think you know me, 
But you don’t.

So you’re gonna play mind games,
Then put up your walls?
Well I have walls too
And no wooden horse will fool me.
Do you hear?

Now you’re playing your mind games
Break me down, cut me up
Into little chunks that you can handle
I am not a statistic, not just a toy.
Can you tell?

Stop playing your mind games
Don’t attack me when I fall
I am not helpless, not useless
Don’t over analyze what I am trying to say
Am I clear?
