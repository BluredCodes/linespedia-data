The Elf
The Sacred Elf
Lives in my heart
And gives me strength

The Elf
The Sacred Elf
Is my uprising
And my downfall

The Elf
The Sacred Elf
Is consuming
My mind

The Elf
The Sacred Elf
Gives me hope
And aids my soul

The Elf
The Sacred Elf
Is my one
My true

The Elf
The Sacred Elf
Lives in my heart
And gives me strength
