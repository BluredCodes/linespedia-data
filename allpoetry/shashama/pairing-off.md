As we walk down the hall
We are all friends
But I can't help noticing
And I can't help feeling sad
When I see you all
Pairing off
Somehow
I am always left out
I look in front of me
And I see
The captain
And his slave
I look behind me
And I see
The shepard boy
And his sheep
And, on one side
I see
The untrusting
And his worshiper
Its starting to bug me...
I can't explain why
I just wish
That I was one of the ones
