The Whore
Surveyed her catch
And stalks the grounds
Looking for me
Die Bitch
I need not see you
You need not 
Tell me what to do
Ugly fuck
Selfish cunt
Bitch LEAVE ME ALONE
Die...leave...go away
The Concubine
With the voice from hell
Laughs away upstairs
Let her laugh...whore
Soon we will fight again
Me and the Bitch
And this time, I wont be back
I wont tolerate someone like her
I cant stand that WHORE
Not anymore
No one should be able to do
The things she does to me
Ugly as hell
With a voice like nails
On a chalkboard
I flinch just hearing it
Another thing for me to do?
Lazy ASS DO IT YOURSELF
