I would like to thank all my friends
Without them, I would not be here
They have helped me in so many ways
I cannot begin to describe how much I love them
 -----------------------
Scotty, you're my dearest friend
You know that it is true
I will love you till the very end
And help you when you're blue

Vix, you saved my life this time
So please, don't be so sad
We always where partners in crime
Although I make you mad

Sarah, you are a very new bud
I really do admire you so
Even though I am a drawing dud
You helped me get the flow

Jess, words cannot describe
What you mean to me
You make me glad I am alive
If only you could see

Paul, you helped me out again
What would I do without you?
You've carried me through thick and thin
I would do the same too

Nam you made me laugh again
With all your silly comments
How you do it, I don't understand
But my mind is in fragments..

The friends that are on this page
Are MOST important to me
My love for them, I cannot gage
As you might be able to see

I do have other friends you know
But verses for all I cannot give
I am just going with the flow
Just realize that they live...I don't know what I would do without my friends...I love them so much...thank you guys, you have saved my life more than once...
