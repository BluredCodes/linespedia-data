title: "Again"
writer: "Shashama"

I sigh more deeply
Than I have in years
For I am slipping
Again

I laugh more weakly
Than I have before
For I am falling
Again

I smile more fakely
Than I wish to
For I'm loosing hold
Again

I cry more often
Than is good for me
For I am ripping
Again

I scream more harshly
Than my voice can handle
For I've stopped singing
Again

I sigh more deeply
Than I have in years
For I am slipping
AgainSigh...
