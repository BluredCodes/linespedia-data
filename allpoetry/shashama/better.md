Sitting in quiet sorrow
With hands over my eyes
Waiting for tomorrow
For the sun to once more rise

Its dark here in my mind
Where no light is ever shining
Where hope is nowhere to find
And sin is ever pining

Sitting here I wonder
If life is really good
To sex and loot and plunder
Is really what I should

I know I'm not so happy
But that will change in time
Right now I'm felling crappy
But to stay would be a crime

So I am feeling better
For the moment at least
I'm making myself better
And that is no small featThanks guys...you helped...
