---
title: "Salt"
writer: "Shashama"
slug: "salt"
source: "AllPoetry"

You're like salt

Pour into my wounds

And sting like hell

Every day, it's something new

To fight about

So you became salt

Because even though

You sting when you

Cut me

And then crawl inside

To spread the pain

Salt is still needed

To make

Most things

Taste better
