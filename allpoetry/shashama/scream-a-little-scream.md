title: "Scream a Little Scream"

Scream a little scream for me
Rip your soul to shreds
Pick up all the parts and flee
You know you're better off dead.

Don't turn off the lights just yet
Stay and cry a little more.
Sob untill you're soaking wet
Then get up, go on, walk out that door.

Do a little dance of death
Move with all your glory
Gasp and take your last slow breath
The rest...you know the story

Cut a little deeper, dear
Don't let the pain outside.
Keep on going, don't show fear
And hang in for the ride.

Scream a little scream for me
Rip your soul to shreds
Pick up all the parts and flee
You know you're better off dead.So angry...so very very angry...
