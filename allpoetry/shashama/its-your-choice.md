Your future is what you make it. 
Your friends are you. 
Your life is now. 
Your past is done.
Your hope is here. 
You are beautiful. 
Your mind is how you see it. 
Your body is what you think of it. 
Your soul is how you keep it. 
Your love is how you give it. 
Your heart is always whole. 
Your beauty is everlasting.
Your spirit is what you take from it.
Your eyes are mesmerizing.
Your intelligence is not everything.
Your pain is optional.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
No one choses who you are.
It's your choice.
Make the right one.

To the ones I love...If you know who you are...
