---
title: "Five of Cups"
writer: "Shashama"

She doesn't see
Just how beautiful she is
And she doesn't see
How much we love her
All she sees
Is a warped view
Of herself
She doesn't see
How good she is
She doesn't see
How much we care
And she doesn't see
That so many people
Would do the things
That she wanted
If she only let them
If she only opened up
She doesn't see
How good she is
And she doesn't see
How talented she is
All she sees
Are her faults
All she sees
Are the things
She doesn't have
But she can't see
What she does have
She can't see
She has been blinded
For far too long
And she won't
Let us help her
To seeI named this "Five of Cups" because that card in the Tarot represents looking at all the things you don't have, and not the things you do...
