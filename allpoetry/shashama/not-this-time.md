---
title: "Not This Time"
writer: "Shashama"

I'm sorry that I  lied
And broke my word to you
Let me tell you, at the time
I thought it would be true

But you weren't there for me
You turned your back and ran
I forgave you, many times in fact
But I'm telling you, not again

You tried to tell me "sorry"
If it was sincere, we'd be fine
I wish I could forgive you
But sorry, not this time

I had made a promise
That I wouldn't be like them
That I wouldn't leave you standing
But I just can't be your friend

This isn't what I wanted
But it's the end of the line
I wish I could forgive you
But sorry, not this time
