Ah! Thy blade!
Tis far to precious for thine hands
What becomith of thy courage?
Dost thou not wishith to use thy blade?
Yet thine hand darest not move
To cut thy frail skin

Thine eyes strayith from thy wrist
Canst thou not cut thine self?
Not even one cut, canst thou make
Pain is thy enemy
Pain is thy fear
Not one cut
Must thou be so weak?

*Yes, yes I must be so weak*

Dost thou lie to thine own reflection?

* I do not lie, not to you*
