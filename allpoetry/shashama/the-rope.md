It hangs as a symbol before me
Tempting my darkest regions
A dark reminder of my attempts
Yes, my attempts at suicide
I left The Rope up there
It was unconquered, unchallenged even
It hung there for years, mostly unused
But every once in a while...the Knot was tied again
And The Rope was used once more
I couldn't take It down
I still needed, no, wanted It up there
I still longed for the feel around my neck
My friends kept me going
Kept me away from that rope
But It still hung
Waiting for the day, when I would try again
Waiting to be used
Hoping to be used
And my Friends...I love you all
You kept me up again
And I thank you
