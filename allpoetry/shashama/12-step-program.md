When you are lost, feel free to come to the meeting of Love Anon. We, like all other Anon programs, have a 12 step program, somewhat like the original AA program. It goes as follows:

1. We admitted we were powerless over love - that our lives had become unmanageable.

2. Came to believe that we did not need to be returned to sanity.

3. Made a decision to turn our will and our lives over to the care of love, as we understand it.

4. Made a searching and fearless moral inventory of ourselves.

5. Admitted to God (as we see him), to ourselves and to another human being the exact nature of our love.

6. Were entirely ready to come to terms with our love.

7. Humbly asked Him to preserve our love.

8. Made a list of all persons we had harmed, and became willing to make amends to them all.

9. Made direct amends to such people wherever possible, except when to do so would injure them or others.

10. Continued to take personal inventory and when we were wrong promptly admitted it.

11. Sought through prayer and meditation to improve our understanding of the nature of our love, praying only for knowledge of ourselves.

12. Having had a spiritual awakening as the result of these steps, we tried to carry this message to those lost in love and to practice these principles in all our affairs.

Have a happy lifetime, and good luck in love...
