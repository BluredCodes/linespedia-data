Hey, don't forget
We are the future
Knowing this
I'd start running
But hey, that's me
Don't worry
We'll follow in your footsteps
Destroy the world
And all her creatures
Star wars
Pollute the air
And the water
Kill people
Lots and lots of people
In the name of God
And Goodness
And Freedom
Funny, isn't it?
The things you do
To get those things
Actually destroy them
Do you think
To yourself
"Next generation,
Will right our wrongs"?
Well did you also think
Maybe thats what
Your parents thought
About YOUR generation
Can you really put
All of your hopes
On us?
We are the children
We are the future
But you raise us
To be what
You couldn't be
That didn't work before
Why would it now?
We try to warn you
But hey
We're just children
What do we know?
