If ever you find need
To just talk a while
Please feel free
To call me

If ever you think
That nothing is right
And you can't last
Just call me

If ever you want
To share a misfortune
Or just to talk
Please call me

If ever you feel alone
Like no one is there
I'll be right here
So call me

If ever you are lost
And you cannot find the way
Don't hesitate
Simply call me

If ever you find need
To just talk a while
Please feel free
