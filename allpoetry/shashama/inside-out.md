Inside out and upside down
Hopeless dreams smashed to the ground
Inside out and upside down
Someone please help me turn my life around

I want you now, I want you here with me
I want your love, don't want your sympathy
Give me your heart, I'll give you mine
Think it won't work out? All we need is time.

So come on, have a little faith
Come on, join in the dance!
I want you, to love to hold me tight
Join me, just give love one more chance!

I know you sense me, guess what, I sense you too
I know you feel me, and all I feel is you
We can turn our lives around
We can spread our wings

So come on, have a little faith
Come on, join in the dance!
I want you, to love to hold me tight
Join me, just give love one more chance!

Inside out and upside down
Hopeless dreams smashed to the ground
Inside out and upside down
Someone please help me turn my life around

Inside out and upside down
With angel's wing, lift off the ground
Inside out and upside down
I know we can make it, we can turn our lives around

So come on, have a little faith
Come on, join in the dance!
I want you, to love to hold me tight
