The dragon
In her lair
Surrounded by diamonds
And other gems
Her fourtune
Is vast
And bountiful
And everyone
Fears her name
And even though
She has all of this
She isn't happy
For she is judged
Before she is known
And she can't find
Anyone to love her
And so she sits
Surrounded by
Her precious gems
This poor dragon
Cries out
For someone
To look past
Her fangs
She just wishes
To find another
Dragon like herself
And she searches
For another
Who will care
But she'll sit
In her nest
Of diamonds
And she'll cry
Until she finds
Her dragonDedicated to all the dragons out there...
