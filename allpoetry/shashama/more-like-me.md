Why is it
That every time
I meet someone new
Who just happens
To be timid
Or shy
I always make them
More like me
Don't get me wrong
I don't mean to
It just happens
Every time
And every time
The parents
Of this person
Tend to
Hate me for it
I'm kinda sick of it
It's not my fault
Maybe I should put up
Posters
With my picture
Saying:
Keep you children
Away from this
Monster
For she might just
Change them
And make them
More outgoing
And self confident
And spunky
And she just might
Make them think
That they don't
Have to fit in
With the populars
And they don't have
To dress like everyone
Else to be liked
By her
And she might
Convince them
To break out
Of the mold
And not automatically
Just go along
With how things are
So beware
Dear parents
She might just destroy
Everything you have
Tried so hard
To createSeriously, it seems like I am always making people more like me....their parents hate me for it, but oh well...
