I wish to walk with the Elves
My heart breaks because I know that I cannot
I yearn for the old times, when there were things left to be discovered
I want to fight the old ways, hand to hand, with swords
I wish to learn the ancient art of archery
I crash down, because this cannot happen
I'd love to learn the old tongues
Adventure is what I want
The tears flow, because this is not available to me
I need to live in ancient times
I am broken because I cannot
I wish to walk with the Elves
Wise and beautiful they are
And I know that I can't
