Shipwrecked
On an island
With no hope
Of Escape
Tattered clothing
No water
Despiration
No contact
No one here
To listen
To me
Whining
Or singing
My songs
So loney
Full of pain
Maybe I'll
Just end it
It might be beter
But It might
Be worse
So I'll just
Keep on
Breathing
Keep on 
Living
For today
Maybe next time
I'll be rescued
Maybe next time
