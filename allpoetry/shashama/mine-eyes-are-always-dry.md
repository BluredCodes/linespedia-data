---
title: "Mine Eyes Are Always Dry"
writer: "Shashama"
slug: "mine-eyes-are-always-dry"

Mine eyes are always dry
For I cannot seem to cry
No matter how hard I try
Mine eyes are always dry

My heart is always still
For It is without thrill
It cannot get its fill
My heart is always still

My mind is always messed
For I am not blessed
And lately I've been stressed
My mind is always messed

My thoughts are always dark
For my parents left their mark
Love as tiny as a quark
My thoughts are always dark

Mine eyes are always dry
For I cannot seem to cry
No matter how hard I try
Mine eyes are always dry
