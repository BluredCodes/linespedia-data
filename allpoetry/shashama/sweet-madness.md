Sweet Madness
Comes and takes me away
Again, oh Sweet Madness
Trapped in your swirling
Pulled down by the Chaos
My Sweet Madness
Oh enemy of sanity
But friend of mine
Precious Madness
No genius can live without
My Sweet Madness
Come to me, oh Madness
Take me away my dear
I am waiting for you
You took me once
Not too long ago
Dear Madness
Do you remember?
You left me then
Sweet Madness
You return to me now
And try to overcome me
Take away who I am
Sweet Madness
Shall I let you this time
Dear Madness?
I think not
