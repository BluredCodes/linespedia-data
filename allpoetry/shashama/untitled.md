A mother
Holds a child
And looks
At this child
With a love
I'll never know

A father
Throws a ball
And the son
Hit this ball
With a strength
I'll never have

An aunt
Sings a song
And smiles
At this song
With a passion
I can't imagine

An uncle
Tells a joke
And laughs
At this joke
With happiness
I never had

A friend
Squeezes a hand
And presses
Against this hand
With a friendship
I can't haveHelp with a title?
