I have nothing to prove
Not to you, not anybody
I am what I am
And thats what I'm gonna be
Why are they always trying
To change who I am?
I am happy with me
Why not you?
I will NOT be something else
I will NOT change for you
Simply because thats 
What you think you want
Call me a poser
Call me what you will
I don't care anymore
I don't need your approval
Thanks anyway, I'll pass
You think that I am just
Going to give in to you?
I'm not, I never will
You certainly don't know me
If thats what you think
You think I care so much
That you call me names
And badmouth me
You try to cut me down
But you will never succeed
I have nothing to prove
To you or anyone
For I am who I am
And thats who I want to be
I am me
And I love itI can't write a poem to save my life lately...oh well
