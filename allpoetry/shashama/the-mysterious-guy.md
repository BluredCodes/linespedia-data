title: "The mysterious guy"

I don't know his name
I don't know his age
I have never once spoken to him
Yet I like the mysterious guy

It was "love" at first sight
When I heard him sing
He looked into my eyes
I knew I liked this mysterious guy

A little obsessed?
Ok, yeah I am...
And I don't know why
But I like the mysterious guy

My friends think I'm nuts
They are probably right
But they didn't see his eye
I like the mysterious guy

I'll find out his name
I'll learn how old he is
I will have a conversation
Will I still like the mysterious guy?True story...go figure...
