---
title: "Sing My Heart Out..."
writer: "Shashama"
slug: "sing-my-heart-out"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/24715-Sing-My-Heart-Out...-by-Shashama"
---

I'm going to do what I want
And what I want to do is sing
I want to not have to expose my midriff
Just so that I can be liked

I'm going to do what I want
And I want to sing my heart out
Pure, crystal clean notes
Thats how I want to sound

I'm going to do what I want
And I want to entertain you all
My voice can reach every one
Once I get the chance

I'm going to do what I want
And I want to sing for you
No matter what the people think
I'm going to sing my heart out

I'm going to do what I want
And what I want to do is sing
And I'll sing just what I want
And I'll sing my heart out...
