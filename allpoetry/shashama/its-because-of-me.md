title: "Its because of me"
writer: "Shashama"

Its because of me, you were sent away.Its because of me you're gone.I'm sorry that it came to that.I'm sorry that I am who I am.But because of me, you are far away.I apologise to all my friendsEspescially the one we lost.They will tell me, that its not my faultBut I know the truth.Have you forgotten all the times we spent?All of the jokes we shared?Have new friends replaced us now?How are you? We miss you.I speak for all of your friends when I say this.We loved you then, we love you still.Our hearts are with you forever, wherever you are.When you come back, we will rejoice.Nothing can keep us apart.Torn away by savage cruelty.Ripped from us by hate.I'm hoping you still think of us.I'm hoping you still care.For the 'love' of a 'god', you were taken awayFor the 'protection' of your faith, you are gone.We miss you so much...we love you SO much.Come home.My friends know who I am talking about...and I cried when I wrote this.
