---
title: "Preppy little Bitches"
writer: "Shashama"
slug: "preppy-little-bitches"
source: "AllPoetry"

I may not be pretty...
I may not be popular
But I'm

sick

and

tired

of all these

preppy

little

bitches

all the time

complaining

and

whining

about their

pathetic

little

lives

when they have

nothing

to

complain

about because their

lives

are

fine



argles....I wrote this at camp after some of these types were comparing butt sizes...honest...but sizes...arg
