title: "How it is for me..."
writer: "Shashama"

Dreams manifest and lies take overSlipping in and out of the flow of timeDaydreams consume, almost realConfusion settles, clouding visionsLost in my world, lost in timeWhat is real and what was a dream?I can't even tell anymoreYou tell me something, I am sure to forgetIts not like I try, if I try to forget, I rememberThat is how it has always been.I am rotting in the closet, to suppressed to be realI am hiding inside this outer shell I have madeNobody knows me and nobody will...Misty veils cover my eyes...Darkened visions...I can't see, but I can smell youYou linger near me, you try to get closeI won't let you, I won't let anybodyIf no one is near me, no one can hurt meAnd I can't hurt themThat's how it is
