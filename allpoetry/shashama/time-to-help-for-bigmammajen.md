---
title: "Time to Help (for Bigmammajen)"
writer: "Shashama"
slug: "time-to-help-for-bigmammajen"

The lonely girl
Sits in the corner
Waiting for AM
Waiting for morning
When the sun will shine again
When the chains will be lifted
And the wounds
Will start to heal
Even as she goes
Through this ordeal
She manages
To help everyone else
She manages
To give out her heart
To everyone around
And she shines
When she smiles
And when she walks in
The room lights up
And the people smile
Showing her their true beauty
And just a few words
From her lips
Will show them
That life is still worth living
And there is something about her
That draws people to her
But I don't even wonder
What this thing could be
Because I know
That naturally beautiful people
Attract everyone to them
And she is certainly
A beautiful person
Both inside and out
And I just wish to thank her
For being there
To listen to me whine
And complain
And I would like to thank her
For showing me
That some people do care
And I would like to thank her
For being who she is
For being herself
And for being beautiful
And for being there
For your AM will come
For your sun will shine
But even while you are chained
In the dark
Not free from your captor just yet
You still found time
To help me
And to encourage me
So thank you
I love ya hun...:)
