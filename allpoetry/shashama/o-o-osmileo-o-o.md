A simple smile
Can go a long way
And travel miles
In just seconds
A smile
Can be heard
Or seen
Or sensed
Or felt
And any way
It is recieved
It is with good wil
For a smile
Is a great gift
To give
To anyone
So don't frown
For as they say
Someone
May be falling in love
With your smile
