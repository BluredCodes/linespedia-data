I just try to breathe
And I choke on the air
Just breathing, I screw up
What is this? Why?
Maybe I am being a pity whore
I don't care
This is how I feel
So this is what I write
I need to be held
I need to be touched
I want to kiss for love
Just once
Thats all I ask
But no
I choke
Every breath I take
I cough
And choke
Maybe one breath
I'll die
