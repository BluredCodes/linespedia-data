---
title: "Little Girl"
writer: "Shashama"
slug: "little-girl"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/22198-Little-Girl-by-Shashama"
---

Little girl,
Reach out
Your hand
And I
Will pick 
You up

Little girl,
You are
Lost
Let me
Find
Your way

Little girl,
Hold on
Tight now
For fear
Of losing
You

Little girl,
I know
Who you
Are and
I am 
scared

Little girl,
You are
The
Mysterious
One
I know

Little girl,
Please help
I fear
I've lost
My way
Again

Little girl,
Why do
You seem
So
Familliar
To me

Little girl,
I know
That you
Are me
And I
Am you

Little girl,
I wish 
You have
Luck
On your
Journey
