title: "My Closest Friend"

I hold you dear,
My closest friend
I will be with you
Until the very end

I'll go to hell for you
And come back  again
Just to see your face
You, my closest friend

My loyalty never fades
You should know its true
I'll be here, my closest friend
To help you when your're blue

I love you so
My closest friend
I will be here for you
Untill the bitter end

I hope you know
Just who you are
My closest friend
And thats by farBackground later....gtg now
