*No, you are not me, you are a reflection, there is a difference*

Thou shalt not defy me

*Mirror Mirror on the wall, you do not scare me, not at all*

Canst thou cut thine self?
So weak, thou art

*Not weak, I have strength, it is you who is weak*

Dost thou dare disobey thine self?
Dost thou flaunt thine imputance?

*Only to you*

Why must thou taunt?
Tis a sad state when one taunts oneself.

*I am not you. I am so much stronger. I will live my life out without you, and I will survive through it. I do not  need you, and I do not want you*

For all time, will thou need me.
For all time...

*Never will I need you, ever*

Leave now
But thou must comeith back

~*~Every day, my mirror taunts me, every day, I shove it away.~*~


~*~Every day, it gets harder to do~*~
