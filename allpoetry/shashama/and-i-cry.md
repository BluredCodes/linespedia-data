title: "And I Cry"

Teardrops fall
Shatter like crystal
I am screaming
Screaming
As the blood stains my hands
Running a little further
Wounds opening wide
Drifting away
On a boat of angst
Made for me
By my "friends"
Giant hands churn the water
Looking up to see
The people I love
As my heart bleeds
And I fall
Down deeper

D

O

W

N

I try to yell for help
And am slapped in the face
And I cry
For those that I have lost
And the tempest rages
And behind my back
They talk
They think I don't know
And I scream at them to stop
But they can't hear
They can't hear me
To wrapped up in what I've done wrong
To care about anything
That I may have done right
And I cry
Because I thought that I could trust
But I couldn't
I was wrong once again
I tried the benifit of the doubt
It failed me again
And I cry
Because they don't get another try
Because I am hurt
And because I am leaving them
Once and for all
I won't stand the boat ride
I won't take the tempest
And I won't be slapped
And stabbed in the back
And then ridiculed
For all of my faults
And I cry
Because I am through with them
And I am leaving
So I guess
To these people
That I onced called friends
That this
Is goodbye
And I cry
