---
title: "Binge"
writer: "Shashama"
slug: "binge"
source: "AllPoetry"

Bad day
Come home
And eat
Sit down
Tube on
And start
To binge
Popcorn
Chicken
Even beef
Tofu
Meatloaf
Ice cream
Sandwich
Apples
Bannanas
Every time
I get
Depressed
I binge
I want
To fix
This thing
I do
I'm not
Sure how
I've gotten
Used to
The idea
Of food
As an
Escape
From things
And people
But mostly
Situations
I don't
Want to
Deal with
So I
Binge
