It’s the little things you do
That make me fall for you
Those tiny things you say
That makes my heart stay

It’s the way you look at me
That really sets me free
The way you give me hope
That proves I’m not a dope

And whenever you’re around
My heart does leaps and bounds
And when you touch my hand
All the taller I stand

It’s the way I feel for you
That keeps me staying true
I’ll never leave your side
I’ll match your every stride

I don’t care who else knows
My love will always grow
For you make me feel
That I can be realI tried to write this a while ago, got a little ways on it, then my inspiration kinda fizzled...it was rekindled recently so I finished the poem....
