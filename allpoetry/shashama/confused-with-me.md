---
title: "Confused With Me"
writer: "Shashama"

I really am surprised to see 
Some people are confused with me 
They seem to know just how I feel 
And like to say: "It can't be real" 

This really is an icky mess 
And I'm angry, I must confess 
For all these people seem to know 
How I should walk, where I should go 

For now I feel I cannot write 
Because my words might cause some spite
For if I write just what I know 
They'd only say: "It can't be so" 

They are not me, how can they know 
If my heart should sway to or fro? 
I know I know, that's all there is 
And they need to mind their own biz 

'Cause they aren't me, they cannot feel 
They cannot judge what's fake, what's real 
Why do they think themselves like me 
Because they aren't, why can't they see?
