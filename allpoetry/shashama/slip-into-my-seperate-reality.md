Slip into my seperate reality
Step right in, have a drink
What color do you want?
Yes, we drink colors here
Is that so strange?

Slip into my seperate reality
Sit down here, have some lunch
I'll sing for you, but only if you ask
Here, the sky is green and the grass is blue
And some of us have powerful magic

Slip into my seperate reality
Hold on tight, its spinning again
The outer shell must be failing
Thats ok, she'll get a grip soon
But for now, hold on to me

Slip into my seperate reality
Where I can fly and you can dream
And those dreams will come true
If you want it bad enough
Do you?

Slip into my seperate reality
You should have the red
It's in season in 2 minutes
Taste good? I hope it does
No, no I am not on drugs

Slip into my seperate reality
When we dance, we'll leave the ground
And pop like bubbles
To come back and be built again
And laugh and do it over

Slip into my seperate reality
Night time is here, the sun is out
Are you enjoying your stay?
Now try the blue, its starting to ripen
Yummy isn't it?

Slip into my seperate reality
Walk on the sky and jump to the ground
Play with the clouds
Skip and sing and dance
Don't leave yet, I'm just started

Slip into my seperate reality
The real world? Just a myth
This is real to me, how 'bout you?
We can stay here forever
In my seperate realityEr...I'm really not on drugs...
