My mother wasn't there to teach me how to use makeup
My father wasn't there to tell my first boyfriend off
My mother wasn't there to get my ready for the dance
My father wasn't there to lift me on his shoulders
My mother wasn't there the first time I was dumped
My father wasn't there to take us all out camping
My mother wasn't there to paint my nails and gossip
My father wasn't there to teach me how to throw
My mother wasn't there to hold me when I cried
My father wasn't there to say that it's ok
My mother wasn't there to give me stern lectures
My father wasn't there to say "be back by ten"
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
I feel kinda abandoned
Kinda left out
Like I missed something
That I really should have had
My parents
