title: "I know you (warning)"
writer: "Shashama"

I know you
You're that guy
The guy
That told me
That I'm too
Fucked up
For you

Well, thats ok
Cause look
At me now
So much better
Than you
So now what
Do you think?

Just so you know
I haven't
Forgoten
Or forgiven
So don't
Get too near
To me

Just a warning
For your safety
Cause when I
See you
I might just
Kick
Your ass

"There are things
Inside your head
That I don't
Want to deal with
You're too 
Fucked up
For me"

At least,
Thats what you
Said to me
Thats what you 
Told me
What do you
Think now?

I know you
You're that guy
That I thought
That I loved
How wrong
Can one person
Be?

I know you
You're that guy
The one who
Killed me
But I'm back
From the dead
What now?

I laugh
And spit
On your grave
As I dance
Upon the one
That made
Me this way

I know you
You're that guy
The guy
That told me
That I'm too
Fucked up
For you
