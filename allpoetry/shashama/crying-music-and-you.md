---
title: "Crying, Music, and You"
writer: "Shashama"
slug: "crying-music-and-you"

I almost cried today
Just sitting there
On my couch
Listening
To the music
Thinking
About what
It used to be like
Just laying there
I almost cried
Music
Has been doing
Just that lately
It brings up the tears
Then they go away
Maybe I just emote
Very quickly
But I still wish
That I could cry
I bet my tears
Are getting stale
Bottled up in there
But all the same
I love my music
Its kept me alive
More than once
Its my dream
My love
My life
I don't know
What
I would've done
Without the thought
Of Madrigals
Just a few months ago
I would have lost
All conciousness
From that rope
Around my neck
I was already
Starting to
My vision blurred
And my breath shortened
My eyes rolled back
And my knees collapsed
But I stopped
I could've died
But I didn't
Music and you
Kept me alive...You in this case reffers to my friends...
