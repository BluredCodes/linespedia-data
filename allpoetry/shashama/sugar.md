title: "Sugar"

You're so sweet to me

Like sugar

It didn't used to be this way

We used to fight

I don't remember the last time we fought

I can't remember

It was too long ago

I want you to know

That I'll be here forever

Cause you're like sugar to me

So sweet

That's why I love you

You're like sugar

And I am

And always have been

Addicted to sugar

I guess that means I'm addicted to you
