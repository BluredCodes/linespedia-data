Its the simple things
Like putting gingerale
In really fancy wine glasses
And eating Doritos
While chatting with your ex
Who still thinks that you
Are beautiful
And you find yourself
Falling for him
Again
How do these things happen?

But still, 
There's nothing quite like
Drinking gingerale
In wine glasses
While eating Doritos
And you realize
That things are ok
Cause someone
Someone out there
Thinks that you are beautiful
And that's all that matters
At the moment
And you think to yourself
That things are gonna be ok
And that you will survive
So here's a toast
To surviving
And I lift up my gingerale
In my wine glass
To you
