Sometimes, you pick up your pen
and the words just flow.
Sometimes, like now, they don't.

Damn the cursed writer's block!
I am sure we all have had it.
Poets like me, we know the horror
Of the dangerous writer's bock.

Of course, maybe math class
Is not the best place for poetic inspiration
Maybe I should write this in French.

Je n'ai pas les choses que parle!
Or did I say that wrong?
I think that it is time to end
This stupid writer's block poem....I had writer's block. Who would've known...
