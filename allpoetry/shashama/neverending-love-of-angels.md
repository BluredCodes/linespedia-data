---
title: "Neverending love of Angels"

What would you do if the Angels turned their backs on you?
Would you turn and run away?
If their wings just started molting.
Would you turn, and close your eyes?
Angel's wings are fragile things,
So soft and full of love
You broke those perfect Angel wings.
With your hate, you tore them apart.
You do not deserve it,
But the Angels, they still love you.
Undying, never ending love.
That only they can give.
Everyone gave up, yet they stayed.
Thats what made them Angels.
I stayed too.

Am I an Angel?



No.
