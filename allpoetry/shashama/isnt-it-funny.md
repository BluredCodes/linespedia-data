---
title: "Isn't it funny?"
writer: "Shashama"
slug: "isnt-it-funny"
source: "AllPoetry"

Isn't it funny
How when
I ran away
Crying
Only one
Person
Had the
Compassion
To follow me?
Or how
The ones
Who have
Claimed
To be my
Friends
Are the ones
That stayed
Behind?
Isn't it great
When you're
Pressured
To do
Something
That you don't
Want to do
Just yet
Cause you're
WAITING
For the
Right time
But then
Your "friend"
Tries to
Make you
Do what
You don't
Want to
Do
Just then?
Highly
Amusing
Is it not?
