---
title: "The Old Harmonies"
writer: "Shashama"
slug: "the-old-harmonies"

There was a time
When the world was new
When hate was inside
So very few

The people all sang
The old harmonies
And joining in chorus
Were the birds and the trees

The Earth was unshadowed
It's glory not gone
And all of it's creatures
Sang the same song

Hate was unknown then
Brothers still cared
Everything was important
And everything was shared

Me, I still long for
Those times, so long past
It really is too bad
That they didn't last

There are few among us
Who sing the old songs
Some are still with us
They're not quite all gone

So keep your eyes open
For the old singers few
You don't believe me?
I'm telling you, it's true

One day, I know
You'll ask a fair bird
About the old harmonies
He'll say what he's heard

When you hear his answer
You'll know that its true
The singer of the harmonies
Has now become you...My name...
