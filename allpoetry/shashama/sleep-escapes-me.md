title: "Sleep Escapes Me"

Sleep escapes me
Losing precious hours
Thinking
Wondering
Or maybe hoping

Sleep avoids me
I am so very tired
Tossing
Turning
Or maybe listening

Sleep shuns me
I am awake, restless
Looking
Hoping
Just to fall asleep

Sleep hides from me
Forsaking what I need
Dreaming
Waking

Sleep escapes me
Losing precious hours
Thinking
Wondering
Or maybe hoping
And starting againSigh...can't sleep much lately...
