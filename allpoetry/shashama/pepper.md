You're like pepper

Get into my nose

Try to make me cry

I don't like pepper

And I don't feel sorry

When it gets ground 

To pieces

Take this as a warning

I don't like pepper

And I wont

Feel sorry

When you get ground

To piecesDon't tempt me....
