Oh women
Frail and weak we are
Unable to carry on
Unable to match up
To men
Meant to stay
Indoors
Cooking and cleaning
Property
Objects
Toys for pleasure
Meant to be dumb
Frail women
Dirty unclean things
Only there for sex
And children
Trodden down
Beaten into place
Oh Women
Rising frim the darkness
Fighting
Stand proud oh, women
For your time
Will come
