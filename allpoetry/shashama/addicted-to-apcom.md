I think I am a little obsessed
For, here is the first place that I go
When I get up (after I'm dressed)
No matter, wind or rain or snow

To think, this page has got me hooked
It really is quite grand
My eyes are shot, my back is crooked
This site's the best in all the land!

You know I love all of the writings
I wouldn't come here otherwise
Some of the poets do need smitings
And the best should get a prize

Something's wrong inside my head
Look at these silly rhymes
Soon enough, I'll go to bed
So bear with me, just one more time

I really am addicted
To allpoetry.com
My typing's been affected
But all the "pro's" outweigh the "con's"

It's time to end this stupid thing
About my little obsession
Next I'll write about a summer fling
That I caught with some other girl (messin')It needed to be done....I write too many depressing poems...I needed a happy one...
