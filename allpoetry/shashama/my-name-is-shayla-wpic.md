My name is Shayla
But thats not who I am
I am the devil child
Who soars through the night
On silver wings
I am the black cat
Silently stalking
And pouncing in the dark
I am the unicorn 
Standing tall and proud
Pure and beautiful
I am the dark mistress
Blending into the shadows
Visible only to those
Who are willing to look
I am the tiger
Weaving through the trees
Slender and graceful
I am the vampire
Crouching, waiting
For my prey to arrive
I am the angel
There to protect you
And hold you when you cry
I am the hunted
Escaping every time
Just to be hunted again
I am the chosen
Destined to be great
To be something better
I am the singer
Bringing the world to harmony
Trying to make the song work
My name is Shayla
But thats not who I am
