I'm sick of being hated
For who I used to be
And loathed
For how I used to act
'Cause people don't realize
Just how much I've changed
It wouldn't be so bad
If I was hated
For who I am now
Or how I act now
But that's not the case
I've done some bad things
I've messed up pretty bad
But I am so much different
I'm not who I was
I wouldn't do now
What I did then
No one sees past the past
I guess
I'm just sick of being hated
For what I used to be*sigh*
