title: "Ramen noodle, my hearts desire"

Oh my sweet ramen noodle
How I long for thee
But the walk to the store
Is far too long for me

Oh loving ramen noodle
You I wish to eat
So I will go to the store
Put my coat on, cover my feet

Oh dear ramen noodle
I walk up to the store
Where I am always hit on
After walking through the door

Oh sweet ramen noodle
This guy, he freaks me out
But for my ramen noodle
I will be strong and stout

Oh now ramen noodle
You are cooking in the pot
And soon I will be eating
Once you're good and hot

Oh my dear ramen noodle
Oh how my hunger grows
The scent that you do give
Is as sweet as yonder rose

Oh yummy ramen noodle
What I go through for you
But you are so much better
Than any soup or stew

Yummmm
