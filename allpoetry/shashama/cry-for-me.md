Cry for me
Those who know my pain
Live for me
I cannot live again

Love for me
Treasure every kiss
Hope for me
I will never know the bliss

Sing for me
For once I had a voice
Dance for me
For now I have no choice

Breathe for me
My air is almost gone
Believe in me
Forgive others when they're wrong

Cry for me
Those who know my pain
Live for me
I cannot live againWritten about the same time as Dance of Death...
