Drive that sword a little deeper
A little deeper into my heart
Now twist so the wound wont close
Another hole, another empty space

Smile for the nice man dear
I don't want to smile
I am not your toy 
I am not here for your amusement

Spin, spin like a top
Get dizzy and puke
Stop faking bitch
Get up and get back to work

Throw yourself away
Cut until you die
Dance in the streets naked
Until you are raped and beaten

Fall, down down
No get up and play some more
Sing until you cough up blood
And can't sing anymore

Worthless piece of shit
Fucking squirrels again are we?
Not good for your health you know
Hysterical laughter can kill

Pick up a kitten
Throw it against the wall
Simply because it's cute
Gut a puppy alive

Don't forget to pay your whore
Never do that
Its how I ended up dead
In the gutter off of 355

Scream for me
As I tickle you with the knife
Serves you right
Told you  not to fuck the squirrel

I dance in her head
Pushed to a corner of her mind
Not to be oppressed am I?
No, not I

Tick tock tick tock
Someone kill the clock
Fuck it all, I'll do it
Clock no go tick tock no more

Scream....LOUDER WHORE
La de da, what do we have here?
Fiesty one, doesn't want to DIE
HEHE, that can be fixed

Bees sting you know...
But I sting worse
I AM A 5' BEE
HA!! There you go

Sleep deprivation will do this to you
Too bad I got enough sleep last night
Oh well..to bad so sad
Sing a lullaby now...ha!
