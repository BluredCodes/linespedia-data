---
title: "Let Go (Let me fall)"
writer: "Shashama"
slug: "let-go-let-me-fall"
source: "AllPoetry"

Sweep me away
Out of this place
Just let me leave
Let me disappear

I'm so tired
I want to go home
I want to sleep
And not wake up

Please let me sleep
Please let me die
Just let go
So I can slip away

Crouching in a corner
Thats how I feel
Alone and crying
In the dark of my mind

So please
Let me go
Don't hold on
Cause I'll just drag you too

Stay away from me
I'll just hurt you
That may be why
I push you away

Or maybe I'm afraid
That you will
Wake me up when
I try to sleep

Maybe thats why
I just block people off
When they get to close
Maybe thats why

But please
Just let me sleep
Just let me die
Just let me go

Don't hold on
I don't want you
To fall with me
So just let go

Why can't you let go?
Why can't I fall?
I'm bad for you
I'm bad for me too

Let go
Let me fall
I do it for you
I do it for love

Please....
