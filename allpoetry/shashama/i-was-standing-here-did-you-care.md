title: "I Was Standing Here, Did You Care?"

I was standing here
But you didn't seem to know
So I just turned my back
And then started to go

You really should have listened
You really should have cared
And not ignored the secrets
That I had trust to share

I thought you were my friend
It turned out, I was wrong
But now, it lasts no longer
And slowly fades my song

I was hopeful for this friendship
I thought that it would last
But all the times and memories
Have faded to the past

But I was standing here
All this time, no lie
But you didn’t seem to notice
So now I say “Goodbye”

I was here for you
But you weren’t here for me
I’m sorry that it’s over
But it just had to be
