Stumbling throughout the woods at night
Its to dark to see where I am going
Branches scratch and bugs bite
I am bleeding, can you see me?
Trip and fall, stub my toe
Over something heavy
I lay there for minutes, maybe hours
Waiting for some light
But there is not light, not anywhere around
So I lay there, where I fell
Sitting up, I look around
What was it that I tripped over?
A branch? A root? A rock?
It seems to be glowing from the inside
Its not a rock or root or branch
Its far to big for that
Its too soft too...
But its glowing, I can see it
It is a body, lying there, dead...
I want to look away, but I can't
Leaning closer, I inspect the features
A girl, a human girl
A little closer I get to her
And I can see her
She is me



And the last thing I hear is my screamScary thoughts....
