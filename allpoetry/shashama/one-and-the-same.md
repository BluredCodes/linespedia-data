---
title: "One and the Same"
writer: "Shashama"
slug: "one-and-the-same"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/22230-One-and-the-Same-by-Shashama"
---

You are me
And I am you
And we are one
And the same

I am fire
And you are water
I am air
And you are land
We have hopes
And we have dreams
And we are one
And the same

I am black
And you are white
You are good
And I am bad
And we both want
Different things
But we are one
And the same
