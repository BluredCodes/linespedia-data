I fear I'm falling again
The path I walk on has frozen over
And suddenly turned downward
So steep I can't walk

I've fallen once again
I'm torn and bleeding
And the journey
Isn't close to over

I feel like crying once again
Or dying, can't tell which
Few things are keeping me alive
Few things, but they're there

My friends, I love you
Please do not doubt me
But hard times have come
And I don't want to go on

I'm sorry for this trouble
Probably just venting hot air
I might get through it
But I am just really sick of everything

Sick of life
Sick of "love"
Sick of fear
Sick of "hope"

Tired...
Physically and emotionally
How am I supposed to keep going?
How am I supposed to survive?
