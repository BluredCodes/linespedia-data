title: "Devil's Tongue in My Mouth"
writer: "Shashama"
slug: "devils-tongue-in-my-mouth"
source: "AllPoetry"

Devil's tongue
In my mouth
Driving farther
Into my soul

Devil's tongue
In my mouth
Getting harder
To gain control

Devil's tongue
In my mouth
Slashing deeper
Into my heart

Devil's tongue
In my mouth
Dancing faster
Than before

Devil's tongue
In my mouth
Pressing harder
Than is needed

Devil's tongue
In my mouth
Driving farther
Into my soul
