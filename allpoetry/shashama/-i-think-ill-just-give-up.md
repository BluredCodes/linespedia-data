---
title: "( I think I'll Just) Give Up"
writer: "Shashama"
slug: "-i-think-ill-just-give-up"

I am in over my head
Its like quicksand
The harder I try to get out
The more I am stuck fast

I can still breathe
But breathing doesn't matter
When I can't live
How I want to live

I am in way to deep
I'm just trying to keep
My head up
But no use

I sink deeper into the web
There is nothing to hold on to
It doesn't matter
I think I'll just give up
