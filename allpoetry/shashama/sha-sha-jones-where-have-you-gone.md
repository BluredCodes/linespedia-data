title: "Sha Sha Jones, where have you gone?"
writer: "Shashama"

Sha Sha Jones
Is no more
She got up and left
And slammed the door

Sha Sha Jones
She ran away
She couldn't bear
The price she had to pay

Sha Sha Jones
Used to care
Now she doesn't
Have time to spare

Sha Sha Jones
Where have you gone?
Why did you stop
Singing your song?

Sha Sha Jones
Did you want her then?
Do you want her now?
Want her back again?

Sha Sha Jones
She was me
But then one day
She turned to flee

Sha Sha Jones
I miss her so
I still wonder
Why she had to go

Sha Sha Jones
Was she your friend?
She may come back
In the end

Sha Sha Jones
Maybe one day
She will come back
This time to staySha Sha Jones was my name last year....
