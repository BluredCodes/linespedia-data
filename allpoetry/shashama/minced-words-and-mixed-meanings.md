title: "Minced words and mixed meanings"

Too many times, I have thought I loved
Too many times, I was wrong.
All those times, I thought it would be forever,
Well, forever's not over yet, but is he here?
Forever, what a funny word
So dead, it has no meaning.
When they say forever, they mean "Just a month"
Interesting, I thought forever meant always...
Unless "always" means, "Yeah, two weeks"
I used to think "always" meant "for all time"...
I realise that that means "Just today"
Typical, english is full of mixed meanings
But I never thought this drastic...
I thought I wouldn't fall into this trap again...
But I know that "not ever" means "Sometime later"
I thought that I couldn't be hurt again
But hey, that just means "could"
Maybe I should...
