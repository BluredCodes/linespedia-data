I am getting so MAD again
I want to kick and SCREAM again
I want to start to HURT people
I want to yell and just SWEAR now

But 
NO
I

Have

LENT

Not even Christian
Go on, ask why
Not only because
I was forced into it
But because
I truly want
To make myself

BETTER

People cannot
SEEM
To understand
This concept
Not even
My own
MOTHER
Got it
She ridiculed
And started swearing
And offered
To get me
Some chocolate
Nice mother, nice
How very sweet
But I can make it
Without swearing
Until LENT is over
But ah ha...you doubt me
Don't you?
You have read my poems
And know what I can do
Even my own
FRIENDS
Laughed at me
And told me
That I couldn't do it
But I can
And I can prove it
I will make it
I will be fine
And when this is
OVER
I will be
The better
PERSON
For all of my
EFFORTS


AAAAGGGGGHHHHHH THIS IS HARD!!!!
