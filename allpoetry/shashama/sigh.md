title: "*s*i*g*h*"
writer: "Shashama"
slug: "sigh"
source: "AllPoetry"

Simply
Involving
Guys'
Hearts

Spoken
Insensitive
Guessing
Haughtily

Sensitve
Inqueries
Getting
Hate

Smiling
Inconspicuous
Greatly
Hurt

Smoke
Inflamable
Guts
Hated

Spining
Incompitant
Grieving
Heartily

*S*I*G*H*
