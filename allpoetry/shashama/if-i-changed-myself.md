title: "If I changed myself..."

If I changed my hair
Would you look my way?
IF I lost my style
Could I make you think twice?
If I hid my dark side
Could I make you mine?
If I wore makeup
And became "bubbley"
And lost
My identity
Of who I am
Simply to get you
Would it work?
If I painted my nails
And became a cheerleader
And smiled all the time
Would you be with me?
Maybe
But I'm sorry
I can't do that
As much as I love you
I have to be me
And if *YOU*
Don't like *ME*
For who *I* am
Then I don't need *YOU*...yeah...
