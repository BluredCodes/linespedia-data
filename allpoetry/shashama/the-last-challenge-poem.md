Challenge Description: Write a poem about the last unicorn, how you visualize him or her, where you see it, and what this means to you. (55 points)



--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Whiter than the eye can even see 
And more beautiful than the mind can comprehend 
The last unicorn gallops through the fields 
She shimmers in the sun 
As she tosses her mane 
And as I see her 
I feel suddenly sad 
For I know that someday 
She too will be gone 
And I feel mad 
That humans were part 
Of her race's destruction 
And I am sad 
For I will never see something 
Quite so beautiful 
As the last unicorn 
But at the same time 
I feel hopeful 
For she has survived 
For so very long 
Through thick and through thin 
And she has stayed tall 
And glistening 
And beautiful 
Even through all the hard times 
And as I see her 
I swell with happiness 
For not everyone 
Gets the chance to see something 
As perfect as the unicorn 
And as she looks at me 
She seems to smile 
And tell me that everything 
Is going to be ok 
And I feel better 
About her and myself 
And I feel my legs 
Beging to lengthen 
And my nose stretch out 
And I fall to my hands and feet 
And my hair grows out 
And as the unicorn dies 
I become her 
Free to run 
And glisten in the sun 
And she smiles one last time 
And then dies 
But I am still happy 
Because I, 
As the last unicorn 
Get to pass the happiness 
Onto someone else
