You're like a lemon

Yet don't get me wrong

I love lemons

So sour

Yet somehow sweet

Thats you to a "T"

Lemons aren't bitter

To my tongue, they are sweet

Even if not to anyone else's

So, little lemon

Even when everyone leaves

Because you are too sour

You'll still be sweet to meFYI, all these flavor poems are for one of my friends each, like Sugar was for one, Lemon, Sal, ect...the only one that is not for a friend is Pepper...
