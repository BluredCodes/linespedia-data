I know you believe, 
That I’m everything you want me to be.
And I know that you think 
That you were always there to be with me.
But I’m sorry,
You Weren't’t there for me.
And I’m sorry,
That I have to let you free!
Cause I love you,
Yeah, I do.
But I know I cannot live
Here with you.
Cause I love you,
You know it's true.
You know it's true.
You were always
Playin' games
Though I warned you
You never changed.
You were always' me
I asked
But you just never let me be.
So I’m sorry
That I have to go away
Yeah I’m sorry
But I have to go away
So I’m sorry
Cause you know I cannot stay
Yeah I’m sorry
Cause I love you,
Yeah, I do.
But I know I cannot live
Here with you.
Cause I love you,
You know it's true.
You know it's true.
Yeah
You know it's true.
