Nervous fright
Dizzying height
But nothing can beat
The Actor’s Rush

Walk upstage
Feigning rage
All of it for
The Actor’s Rush

Everyone’s eyes
No more tries
Here it comes now
The Actor’s Rush

Raucous applause
Worthy cause
‘Cause it was for
The Actor’s Rush

Flattering bow
What to do now?
Coming down from
The Actor’s Rush

Nervous fright
Dizzying height
But nothing can beat
The Actor’s RushOnly the entertainers know the true quality of the Actor's Rush....written partially for a friend of mine who knows the feeling....
