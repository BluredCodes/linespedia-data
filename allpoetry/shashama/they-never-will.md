"And then I open up and see, the person falling here is me." ~"Dreams" The Cranberries

Me
I fell
Again
I really have to stop that
Once again
Unfufilled
Once again
Left in the dust
Wondering

Why?

Why now?

Why again?

I am so easily swayed
I didn't think I could be
I'm losing again
I fight
These losing battles
Every time
And every time
I think
That things will change
They never do
They never will
