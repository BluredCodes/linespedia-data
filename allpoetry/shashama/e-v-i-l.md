E          V          I          L

A pure evil entity slithers through my mind
Leaving ewy guey slimy trails behind
Does he love me, does he not?
The question lingers there to rot.

E          V          I          L

Like a worm, bellycrawl through my brain
To ask it out loud, I'll try to refrain
Loathing this feeling, with all of my might
Scared that maybe, just maybe, you're right

E          V          I          L

Scorch where it touches, this evil little worm
My position on this, I will be quite firm
This worm scares me much, not sure why
Mayhaps it's the cunning, this thing is quite sly

E          V          I          L

Slime trails all crisscrossed inside my head
I won't be surprised if I turn up dead
It implants evil thoughts, in an evil mindset
And as to reactions, I can only frett

E          V          I          L

Slip slide little worm, in all of your glory
The outcome, well, we only know the story
This worm must have come form somewhere above
The thought that is evil, implanted, is love

E          V          I          LThis isn't really about anyone or anything...go figure...
