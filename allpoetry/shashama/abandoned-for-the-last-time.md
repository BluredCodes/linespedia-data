title: "Abandoned for the last time"

Too many times, I have sat on this stoop.
Waiting for you to come get me
Two hours late. Yet, do I complain?
If I'm 10 minutes late, you scream at me.
I want to tell you, that I am fed up.
I'm sick of excuses and lies
I am here to say, that I am sick of it
I have been abandoned for the last time.
So goodbye, my dear sir!
Farewell, his stupid wench!
Blame yourself, I hope that you do/
You could at least have picked me up on time.
So, solong! Oh punctuel one!
Au revoir! Oh whore of my nightmares
I have been abandoned for the last timeWhat can I say? They never pick me up on time...
