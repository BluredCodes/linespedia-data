title: "Second Best"
writer: "Shashama"

I want to be the very best
I wont settle for less
I can't stand to be average
I can't stand to be low
I am very competitive
And until you came
I was the best
At least in my eyes
Then you showed up
And suddenly
In everything I do
I'm somewhere down the list
I don't want to be down there
I want to be on top
Call it selfish
Call it what you will
I don't care
You wonder why
You stress me out
Because you make me
Second best
And I can't stand that
