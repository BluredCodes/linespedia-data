---
title: "Tired"
writer: "Shashama"
slug: "tired"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/23915-Tired-by-Shashama"
---

Sick
And Tired
Of everything
Around me

Sick
And tired
Of all
That I see

Sick
And tired
Of people
And of life

Sick
And tired
Of schools
And assholes

Sick
And tired
Of living
And breathing

Sick
And tired
Of everything
Around me

Sick
And tired
Of you...I am falling again...falling beyond belief....I just want to rest...I am tired...
