title: "Good To Know"
writer: "Shashama"

It's nice to know
That some will be there
When I need them most
And when I don't

It's nice to think
I have some friends
That will stick by me
No matter what

It's nice to have
At least one friend
To stand by you
When you're hurt

It's nice to lean
On that one friend
When she knows
That you need suport

It's nice to know
That some will be there
Even if you don't think
That you need them

Even though you really do....Thanks Vix...
