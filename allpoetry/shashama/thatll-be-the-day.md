title: "That'll Be the Day"
writer: "Shashama"

Mother Nature wraps her arms around her children
Protecting them from harm, from us
We destroy, without creating
Without caring, or compassion
For all the lives that we've ruined
Mother Nature does her best
To save all of her children
From the monsters that we have become
Mother Earth tries to help her
But she's dying anyway
I wish all the creatures and Nature that we're destroying
Had lifeboats to escape this sinking ship
If I'm alive when the time comes for us to be destroyed
You know what I am going to do? I'm gonna laugh
For years I have waited for us to all die off
So I'll laugh. A good hard laugh...the kind the crazy people do
That'll be the dayDon't ask don't ask don't ask....
