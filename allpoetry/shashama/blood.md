title: "Blood"

Drip

Another stain

On the carpet

Another spot

To conceal

Drop

One more tear

Making splashes

Growing puddles

On the floor

Drip

More life

Leaving my body

More love

Thats been misused

Drop

More happiness

That never came

More hope

That I never had

Drip

Another stain

On the carpet

Another spot

To concealDrip...
