Don't call me a poser
When you are more then me
You come in
Steal our sayings
Our jokes
Even our friends
And you wonder
Why we get so mad?
Open your eyes
You're not as great 
As you think you are
Get off your horse
Because pretty soon
You will fall
While all the time
Calling me the poser
Its so funny
To watch you take my place
And turn around
And criticise me
For being something fake
When its YOU
Tha'ts being fake
Not me
I can't stand it
But I put up with it
You know why?
Because I know
That without ME
You would be nowhere
Its MY FAULT
That you are here
Yet you condone me
When I am responsible
For all the friends you have
Don't take me for granted
Because pretty soon
People are going to realize
That YOU are the poser
And not meThats why I "hate" you
