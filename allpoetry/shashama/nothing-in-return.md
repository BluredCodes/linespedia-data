Crouched in fetal position
Low in a corner
Crying

~~~~~~~~~~~~

This is how I feel
Not from school
Or anger
Or fear
Not from her
Or you
Or them
But from me
Feeling the way I do
About you
And getting nothing in return
From me
Pouring my soul into something
And hoping so hard
And getting nothing in return
From me
Baring my feelings
Opening my heart
And getting nothing in returnCheesey to be true
