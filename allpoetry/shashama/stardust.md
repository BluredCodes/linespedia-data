title: "Stardust"

Stardust
Sprinkles on my face
As I look to the sky
For my answers
Stardust
Sparkles in the sky
Such a lovely sight
I want to take it in
Stardust
Gets into my throat
And chokes me
As I fall to the ground
Stardust
Killing me slowly
But all that I wanted
Was to look
Stardust
