I sit here, tearless
Unable to cry
I sit here, soulless
Unable to die

I am here, dreamless
Unable to love
I am here hopeless
Shunned from above

I walked here, lifeless
Unable to care
I walked here, deathless
So people beware

I came here, sleepless
Unable to snooze
I came here, loveless
Unable to lose

I am nothing....
