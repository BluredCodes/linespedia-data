title: "Same"
writer: "Shashama"
slug: "same"

You know what's strange?
Tears and blood make the same sound
As they fall

You know what's odd?
Life and death hold the same roads
As they go on

You know what's messed?
Falling in and out of love feels the same
As I know

You know what's sad?
Friends and foes will say the same things
As they yell

You know what hurts?
When you turn your back and yell the same words
As you did before

You know what's strange?
Tears and blood make the same sound
As they fallI wrote this when I was mad at a friend of mine. That friend shall go nameless because he/she is sure to read this...
