---
title: "I really do love you..."
writer: "Shashama"
slug: "i-really-do-love-you"

I may deny it
I may fight it
But I cant hide the fact that I love you
I try so hard to keep it from you
I cant help but wonder if you know
I watched as all those years you loved her
And then she hurt you so
I winced, but I just kept on walking
For I knew she held your heart
Shes gone, and you are broken
But I still love you
You are everything to me, but do you know it?
I wonder if you know?
I could never hide it with anyone else...
Did I succeed with you?
If you know, please tell me...
If you dont, you cant
But let me just tell you now...
I really do love you...I'm not sure if this person knows who he is, but hey...
