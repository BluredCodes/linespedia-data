The Killer of Songs
Hunts me down
Hasn't gotten me yet
I hope he never does
He lives in most of us
Destroying the music
That all of us posses
Some of us can't fight
And so we lose our voice
No more singing for us
Once he has gotten you
There is no turning back
You will not get back
You beautiful voice
You will not be able to
Join in with harmonies
You will be forced
To sit and watch us sing
And to envy us all
But The Killer of Songs
Wants to get me
He wants to wreck me
And stop my singing
He trys to take me
And kill all hope inside
And leave me crumpled
And lying on the floor
This Killer of Songs
Wants to fill me with
All his evil words
They have no melody
They do not appeal to me
I try to force him away
Its getting so hard lately
For he wants to take my mind
He wants to leave me used
And beaten without friends
I will not let him take me
For I will not let his message
Consume my mind and soul
For this Killer of Songs
Is a common thing
We use the word every day
We use HIS word every day
This Killer of Songs
Wants us to believe
This Killer of Songs
Wants us to hate
