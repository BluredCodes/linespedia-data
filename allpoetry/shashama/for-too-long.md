---
title: "For Too Long"
writer: "Shashama"

For too long, I have loved you
It's a love I can't deny anymore
Yet can't admit it yet
How to tell you?
How to let you know?
I love you more than I think you know.
More than anyone in the world
Talking to you makes me happy
Just seeing your face brightens my day
For too long, for too long I have watched you suffer
For too long, I have seen you hurt.
I wish that I could just hold you
And make you believe that it will be ok
I wish that I could show you how good life can be
For too long, I have wished for you
For too long, I have held back my feelings
I love you...can't you see?
