How to die?
How to die?
Will I fall or will I fly?
Will the cops come rushing 'round?
When I flop and hit the ground?

How to kick?
How to kick?
When I pull, will it go click?
Will the news bang on the door?
When my blood has stained the floor?

How to pass?
How to pass?
Will the edge wear from the glass?
Will the teachers have a fit?
When they've found my wrists are slit?

How to croak?
How to croak?
Will I breathe, or will I choke?
Will my parents say 'Oh no!'
When I'm swaying to and fro?

How to die?
How to die?
Will I live or will they cry?
Will the see, they're hands are red?
When they've found I've turned up dead?Explintation: (btw, I wouldn't really kill myself...)First two lines: ways to say 'Die' basically...Pass=pass away, kick=kick the bucket. Third lines: Botched suicide, me livingFourth and fith lines: Someone finding out once I've done it...
