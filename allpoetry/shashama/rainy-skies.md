Depression slips in, like a cloud over skies
Intoxicates my mind with thoughts of suicide
Death seems welcome, a good alternative
But I must move on, I must survive

Dismal days and rainy skies blur my vision
Soaking me, I stand on the road I have made
Rainwater in my eyes, so acidic it burns
I have to recover, I have to go on

Time slips past me, I'm not sure where I am
I don't know who I am or why I am here
The Old Harmonies fade away, and slowly die

Standing, facing the crying sky
My indurance shatters as I burst into tears
So cruel, this unjust world
But I must push on. I will survive.Eh...yet again, the depressing yet must survive theme, go figure...
