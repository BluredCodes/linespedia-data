Daddy was a drugee, guilty yet carefree
Mommy was a bitch, who ran away from me.
Stepmom was a whore, and so she slept with men
Stepdad, he drank alcohol, keeping with the trends.

Daddy was abusive, beating us at whim,
Mommy was afraid, and so she hid from him
Stepmom, she got raped, because she had her job
Stepdad used to slap me, calling me a slob.

Daddy used to yell, at mom to get a life
Mommy used to scream back "Get a diff'rent wife!"
Stepmom used to make me, pleasure all her clients.
Stepdad used to rape me, 'cause to me he was a giant.

Daddy, he died strangely, hung up from a tree.
Mommy, she got shot, the bullets counted three.
Stepmom was so lonely, till her throat was slit.
Stepdad, yeah he killed himself, 'cause he cut his wrists.

Ok, I will admit it, 'twas me who killed them all,
I couldn't stand the torture, or the constant calls.
I really dont regret it, not one single mite.
And if anyone asks you, I did it out of spite...Scary....
