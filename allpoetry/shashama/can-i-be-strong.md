---
title: "Can I be Strong?"
writer: "Shashama"
slug: "can-i-be-strong"

Some          Times
Times         Change
For           The
Good          Will
We            All
Must          Care
Try           After
Some          Time
When          Now
We're         That
Alone         You
Again         Have
Been          Gone
Thinking      I
Can           Weep
I             Almost
Be            Come
Strong        AgainIt reads in three different ways...from left to right, from top to bottom on the left, and from top to bottom on the right...
