Challenge Description: Write a poem in cinquain form. A cinquain is a short, 5 lined, unrhyming poem that has 22 syllables distributed 2, 4, 6, 8 ,2....If you are still unsure, look at my cinquains for more help. (50 points)



--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Something 
Was once unsure 
But now it lives again 
In harmony with everything 
Alive
