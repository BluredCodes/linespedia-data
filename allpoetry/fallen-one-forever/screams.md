I scream and scream....
I scream and still no one comes.
I scream from my heart...
My mind; My lungs; My soul..
I scream as loud as I can!!!!!!
Yet, no one hears me... no one sees me...
A nightmare I suppose...
My bestfriend showered in a curtain of blood.
My brother gone; disappeared; vanished...
I wake up screaming...
I try to drown out the images I see.
Yet, no one hears my screams?!
What? Are they deaf?
I screamed so loud...
The windows shook and pictures fell.
I screamed so loud...
They probably heard it in hell!
I start to cut; to make it all go away..
I start to cry and to scream again.
I am so afraid; so distraught...
As I lay here soaked in my own blood...
I pass from dark to light...
Ending this painful and frightening night.This actually was a dream I had... I really freaked me aout and the weird thing is i woke up screaming but No one heard me scream at all.. they slept right thro it!
