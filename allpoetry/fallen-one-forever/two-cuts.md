Sometimes I wonder, 
Why I am here.
To me it makes no difference,
I hold nothing dear.

My heart is a stone,
That seems to be hard and cold.
My face is like hardened clay,
In its stony silence; just waiting to unfold.

My emotions are calm and collected,
Without the happiness of life.
I see earth as a graveyard,
With everyone living pain and strife.

I’m dead down deep in side, 
I have no soul to save.
Please just make two cuts,
