I wish, I wish,
That I had a wish to wish.
Pointless as it seems,
I live my life through dreams.

Dreaming of confusion I created,
Unentending my failure to be set free.
Dreaming of dreams that make no sense,
That tell of things yet to come.

Whether they be ill or fair,
I see things that make me cry.
Wanting only to die,
Cutting in to my soul.

Forever scareing it,
Without a painful day to do so.
Wishing only for it's end,
As it ends so do I.

And so I end ,
And I die.
Yet it seems pointless,
For anyone to cry.2001, I just write what I feel If this is upset in or fasinateing or what ever leave me a message.
