Everyone keeps telling me I'm so bad,
All the teachers say,
I'm the worst student they've had.
So keep on saying,
I'm the worst that you've seen.
Look at this kid,
She's so vulgar and mean.
Listen up bitch, I don't give a fuck what you think.
All the bullshit you talk about me,
Why don't you just mind your own business please!

All boy bands should commit mass suicide.
And line up single file just to kiss my ass.
They make me want to scream and shout.
They make me want to blow my brains out.
N*sync and Backstreet are all fags.
In a few years they'll all be in rags.
No little bitch will know their name.
Or scream it out to them in vain.
Even if that doesn't happen,
They will all still die.
I'll cut their asses up,
And scream bye bye bye.ok so i hate pop its not really a crime is it?
