Red tears,
For the dear.
Black tears,
For the fear.

Blue tears,
For the sad and broken hearted.
Gray tears,
For the morbid and lifeless.

Pink tears,
For the happy and loved.
Purple tears,
For the passionate and infatuated.

White tears,
For the odd and misunderstood.
Clear tears,
For all of us.

So many different,
Shades of tears.
We use them all,
To hide our discomfots and fears.Ok so i RAN OUT OF COLORS! lol
