I dream of a time,
When I drempt life away.
Living in my fantasy world,
A million miles away from me.

Dreams so sureal,
It's like I'm emersed in darkness.
Calming all my pain and fears,
Melting away my shame and distrust.

I dream so much it's hard,
To tell real life from the dream.
I cut to shock me back to reality,
It's just a sudden rush for me.

It's perfecr bliss,
To know I'm really here.
Knowing that this isn't,
Just a dream within a dream.

I dream so much that nothing seems real.
I'm starting to believe,
Nothings real and nothing matters anymore.
I am never to awaken from this dream?

Never to feel or love,
Never to hate or fear;
Never to dispise or care about anything.
