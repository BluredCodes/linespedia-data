The sky is dark,
As is my soul.
There is no light,
Down in this deep; dark hole.

My eyes are shadowed,
Away from the light.
There are many tunnels,
Away from them I run in fright.

I run hard and fast,
Straight across the street.
Where men run and skate,
Where god and satan meet.

Where demons rule over angels,
Where we are forced to go.
We are pushed out into life,
And there is still so much we don't know....comment if you want I was just stateing the obvious.
