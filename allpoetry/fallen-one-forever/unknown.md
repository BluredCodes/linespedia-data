It’s strange,
To think someone understands me.
Knowing that there,
Is someone like me.

Who knows how I feel,
What I’ve been through.
What I think,
What I might possibly need.

A person; so identical to me,
It’s hard to tell where;
My thoughts end,
And where his begin.

Confusing I suppose,
To those who can’t see.
That he understands,
That he’s almost me.Inspired by the first person who ever really understood how I felt and how I precieved things... BRS thanks bubba
