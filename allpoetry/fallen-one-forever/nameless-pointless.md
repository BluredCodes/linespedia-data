So strange...
I am lost.
Unusual for me,
I'm concious of everything.

So fucked up am I,
I've lost my mind.
Living through insanity,
Or not at all; life just passes.

I know not what I think,
Nor what I've done.
I just used to think,
That you were the one.

You were the on;y thing I needed,
The only person I ever wanted.
You were all I had,
You were the first person I loved.

"Please?" I begged of you.
"Please" just return this feeling.
"Please, please just love me."
Love me back the same as I loved you.This was a but the first guy I ever thought I loved, I lost what innocence I had to him, and he like all guys left.
