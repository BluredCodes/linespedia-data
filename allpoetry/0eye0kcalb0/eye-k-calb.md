Is this person real

Or are they just a dream



Is that really air that you are breathing

Or is it something else, 

Something without a name



Am I feeling the wind

Or is it just your laughter



How do I know the difference 

Between your smile and your frown



I feel the fire from your heart

Inside my mouth



Is this a friendship?

Is this love?



Or is this just another

Something without a name
