The town
Filled with people
Of such ignorance
The town
Filled with people
Of such lonely hearts

The time
I visited Simpletown
The time 
I loathed my visit
To Simpletown

The days were long
The nights were short 
The people visited 
With simplicity overpowering life

Thoughts were not thought of
Feelings were not felt out
Minds did not matter
They loved to gossip
But tried to avoid
Being a victim of it
For if you were, 
It was the end of yourself

Oh dear, I am too complicated
For Simpletown
I think too much and express all to well
For the hell that is
Simpletown
I bring up things that could get me killed
So they all thought, in that town

But no one can deny
That I heard all of their cries,
Knew all of the loneliness,
And felt the oppression of life
In a simple, yet complicated town

I am allowed to come and go as I please
While they are stuck in a confusing community
The solution to their problems
Is quite...
Simple

They must pack up and leaveThis is a story/poem about a small town that should not exist
