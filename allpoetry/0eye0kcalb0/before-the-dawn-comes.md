Please

I beg you to let me stay here

Forever

Let me stare over at your

Peaceful

Face and just dream



Dreaming

Of things that will never come true,

Knowledge

And acceptance that we will rise,

Awake

Open our eyes

Seperate

Never speak again

Part...



Forever



Before the dawn comes

Please

Let me live in this dream

Please

Just allow me to 

Pretend

Allow me to 

Fake

Before we both

Awake

From a "shameful" night

Please!

Before the dawn comes
