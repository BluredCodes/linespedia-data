From time to time
I wonder if I will ever go blind
Then I start to really think
What would be worse-
Going blind or being deaf-
I can't seem to find
A balance between the two

I can either stop looking at beauty
Or stop hearing it

What if I were both?
I think I would watch a movie
And listen to all of my records
One last time
If I were both
