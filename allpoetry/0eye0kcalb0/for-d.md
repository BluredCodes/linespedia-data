Sorry
I forgot what day it was
It's just that I have been 
Boozing so long
Sorry
I had forgotten
That I love you
It's just that I have been
Dreaming so long
So many, many excuses
For my bad parenting

Please keep your blood from boiling
Try to learn how to thin it
Please control your emotions
Or just throw them away

Sorry
I forgot to call you
I don't feel like keeping you company
It's just that my life keeps rolling along
I'm so sorry
I had forgotten
That I am related to you
It's just that I am so tired
Of dreaming for so long
So many, many excuses
For your bad parenting

I've learned to keep my blood from boiling
I've learned how to thin it
I can now control my emotions
Or just throw them away
