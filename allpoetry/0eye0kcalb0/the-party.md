I hold my glass
Wishing you would pass
My way

I sip the wine
Feeling fine
Hoping you would come 
My way

Another glance
Does it mean I have a chance
To have things go 
My way

Who started this all
Who made me fall
For your smile today

And now it's time to go
A blur of tonight
Where did all the time go
Do you need a ride

I don't know you but...
Do you need a ride
I want you to say
I want you to say
You want to be rescued from life

Oh, here we go
Another party
Oh, here we go 
With my imagination running wild
Another stranger for me to store
