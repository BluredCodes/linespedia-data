I need someone to take me away
So I can rest and
Clear my mind

I want someone to take me 
Somewhere hidden
Maybe in a cabin
In unknown parts of a forest

I want to sink into a soft bed
Swim in a sea of satin sheets
I want to fall out of reality
And listen to the buzz of silence

I wish there was beauty in my mind
Instead of knives and violence
I wish I could think flowers
And sniff out love

If I could dance out a song
Maybe this can all come true...
Maybe
