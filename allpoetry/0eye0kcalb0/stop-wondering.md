There's a burning feeling in my stomach

Energy in my legs,

But not enough to get me up

And tackle the hurt in my heart



I like to put things off

Hope they'll go away

I know that they won't

But I still do it anyway



Is patience still waiting

For me to make a move

Is patience still waiting

For me to take a stand



Will it wait forever

Or will it just give up



I can't ignore my clammy hands

I can't keep procrastinating

And putting my dreams on a distant land

I can't ignore my racing heart

It is about to  burst 

I want to rip it apart

Just give me the worst



Should I just give up?

Should I just give it all up?

Or should I just hope for the best

But I always wonder



If patience is still waiting

Will it wait on me forever

Why can't we both end this now

And stop wonderingFor someone I can't forget
