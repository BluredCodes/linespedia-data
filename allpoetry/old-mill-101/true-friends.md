True friends are the ones who are supposed to be there when u really need them the most.
 
 True friends dont talk about you behind your back and when u get near them they pretend everything is alright.

 True friends help you through the most troublesome times in your life, not give them to you.

 True friends stay with you for hours on end and talk with you when your drunk and make sure that you are alright.

 When you have found that true friend it is your duty to thank them for so much that they have done so thanks Chellsea.
