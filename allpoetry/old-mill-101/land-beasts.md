I knew this kid who will remain nameless
Who made out with this girl in his car
She liked to drink in a bar

He told people that he did her 
It was her time of the month
so they dident fuck

Now that he is home he still talks to her online
But has no intentions of seeing her
He justs says that he wants to Fuck her 

He doesent want a relationship
Just friends with benefits
She thinks its a bunch of shit
While he sits home and eats chips
