So we awokeapart, but togethersoft touches in the hint of dawn. Sleep-warmed bodies curlingagainst one anothereyes yet closedbut minds awakeand seeking the other. I felt you pull me neardid you hear me whispermy ascent? Around my midriffyour arm captured meyour lips buried in my hairas you drew me onto yourself. Sweetly softened passionsopening to one anotherlonging for more than physicalitypresented itselfin your firm insistence, my welcoming warmth. 'Make love with me'you murmuredthough we already were. Like pulled taffywe played with one another'sdesires.  Luxurioustempting, slowly relishing. Even fulfillment was more like a spilling overof a slowly poureddelicacy.  Deliciously flooding our senseswith the others desire. You pulled me atop you.'I want to see your eyes.' and I opened mine.  You were here. Was I there?Marsha Rose ©'01
