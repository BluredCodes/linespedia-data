title: "My fRIEND"
writer: "kama"

A merry young ladyMeaningful and brightBehind her mask of shynessErupts a playful delightRemember her golden hairLeave her not in the wrong wayEvery moment cherish her beautySo simple is made complexSo short yet so strong willedAll around you would love herRather you wouldhad i not loved her first
