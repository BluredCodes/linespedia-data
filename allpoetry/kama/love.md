loveit hurts you when your aloneit stings you when your in doubtit gives you more pain when you can take no moreyou open up your heartit pierces it like a cold steel bladeloveit can give you strengthit can give you the hope you needit burns like a fire insideit can turn dreams into a realityyou can swing it like a fiery steel bladelovethe double-edged swordsometimes it cuts youwhen you use it
