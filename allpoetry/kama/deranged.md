as i walk into the light, i start to fight yes to fightbreaking down the barriers within, and feeling no remorse for my sinthe voices call from inside my head, screaming you should all be deadwhy must they yell into my ear, is it because they sense my feari know that i can not be stopped,the blood runs down suddenly i'm stoppedi fear that this could be the end, never ever did i defendsimply put into words, they hunted me down like the buffalo herdsrhyme and reason have all but left, i feel as though their has been some theftbe it so away from here, remember i am always wandering neari promise it will be quick if you like to be beat with a stickslowly but surely you will die, and when you die i won't cry cause i am deranged
