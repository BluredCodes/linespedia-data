My gentle Lady, I feel you near me now,
I await your tender caress across my naked back,
I long for you to feel my face,
And take me into your loving embrace.

The darkness has come now,
But you calm my nerves.
You speak to me and your tender voice,
Reminds me that it will be alright.

I can not see you and I never have,
But your touch is all I know.
It chills my spine when the room is warm,
And dulls the bleed they can not see.

My gentle Lady, I long for you to smother me,
To take me where all your lovers go.
You are my one and only,
My gentle Lady Oblivion.
