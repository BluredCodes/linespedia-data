You look so very weary now,
My dear.
The effort of not being asleep
Has worn you down
And washed you up,
My dear. 

Have you been walking an untrodden route,
My dear?
Each step forward taken as a step blind
Knowing only that
You know nothing of the path,
My dear. 

Or perhaps a path you know too well,
My dear.
Is it that you know the journey's end
But are unable to step back,
Unwilling to go forth,
My dear? 

Come rest, your travel is over for now,
My dear.
Come lie by me and it will be right
Like you I wait for the dark
And a dreamless sleep to hide me from
