---
title: "Forest and Field"
writer: "h2whoa"

I know that where I am
Now,
Is not where I shall always
Be,
In a forest of naked giants reaching
Out their wooden bones,
Embracing the charcoal sky
Of the night.

I lost the path when the
Thorny fingers clutched my naked
Skin,
Tearing my face as they
Pulled me in.

But you've come to lead me back
To an open field,
Away from the jealous monsters
Who would hold me with their shackle-roots.
The sun will pour down on me
It's glorious honey, as a gentle breeze
Kisses my cheek.

I am cold and have in my mouth
A stone of fear,
But I will be warm and have in my mouth
The fruit of freedom.

Be patient.
