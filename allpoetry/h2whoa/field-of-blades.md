I am swaying.



The breeze that you are,

Sweeps through the field,

Looking for the non-believers,

The losers and the lost.



You will find me.



I shoot out my roots,

Try to grip a little tighter.

Why won't you leave me be?!

You scream your terrifying howl.



I'll pretend I don't exist.



Others around me are unafraid,

Their individuality grants a conformity.

I am singled out. I choke in fear,

You pluck me up.



A helpless weed.
