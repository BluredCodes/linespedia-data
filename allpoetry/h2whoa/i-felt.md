title: "I felt."

I felt the sun reach out to touch me.
I felt the trees embrace my body.
I felt the glory of the world
I felt the anger in your words.

Golden cotton brushed past my cheek,
And I felt it slip away.
I reached out to touch the sun,
And I felt my hand ablaze.

I felt that I no longer belonged,
So I took away my essence.
I wandered the woods with the Blizzard King,
And I felt his loving-hate.

And I lie on this shore and I
Feel
Barbed wire waves fondling my body.
I feel the sand beneath my back
As clocks melt on my face.
