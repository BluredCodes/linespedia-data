You asked me to come down,
From my hail-stone tower,
But your voice was butchered
By the mourning gale.

The King of the Blizzard was staring at me.

I thought I had my sword to hand,
To defend myself from His
Malevolent, firey-snow gaze.

What I actually held was my heart,
And I realised it was made of wax
With the wick burning through.

You asked me to come down,
And I flew.
