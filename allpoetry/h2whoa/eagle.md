title: "Eagle"

Soar, my eagle,
Visit a world I can never see.
Let the cotton-wool clouds
Caress your face,
Like I used to long ago.

And allow the winds of liberty,
To run their fingers across
Your powerful body as
You glide unthinkingly;
Sail away from me.

Soar, my eagle,
Away from the pain,
Away from the fires below.
The blizzard has covered what you used to love,
Which is everything you know.

You are my eagle,
And I am a moth.
