title: "A Friend's Suicide"
writer: "h2whoa"

I don't suppose they'll
Ever know why you did
What you did.
You were barely past the start,
The finish was out of sight,
When you decided you'd
Had enough,
You decided to throw in the towel.

You were 24, man!
What the fuck were you thinking,
Before you decided to end
All thought
With the sharpest blade:
An overdose?

To all your friends I would like to say:
"I know it is painful that he has gone away,
It pains us all to see him embrace the night
I know that nothing can ever seem right.
This foolish act has caused such pain
That I know it must hurt to open your eyes again.
But appreciate that for all your tears
And all your overwhelming fears,
You did not cause this deed.
And time will soothe your burning bleed."

You've left them hollow,
Screaming in pain.
But for all of that,
They want you back.
A desire
Left unfulfilled.

I only hope that
Death
Has offered you the peace,
That you couldn't find in
Life.
