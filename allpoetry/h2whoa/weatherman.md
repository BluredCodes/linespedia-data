title: "Weatherman"

I have angered you?
I wear my cloak of waves,
As I stand on the cliff.

You hail my coming with
Angry tears from
Angry clouds,
As I stand on my cliff.

I raise my arms and
My gale reaches a frenzy.
A crown of tear-drops upon
My head.
A mask of indifference on
My face.

Did you see me?
I flew.
I flew!
