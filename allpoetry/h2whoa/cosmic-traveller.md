Take me up to float among the stars,
Engulf me in your darkness,
Silhouette me in your nebulous glory,
I am not a star.

Let me feel the ending cold,
As I escape the gravity of this rock.
Absorb me into your unseen
Unforgiving darkness.

I can not breathe.

I can not even dream.
