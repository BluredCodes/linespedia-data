title: "Without you"
writer: "h2whoa"

Be my anchor
And hold me down.
Be my wings and help me
Fly.

Without you I am not
Me,
Not the me that you proved
I can be.

I know I am hollow,
I am Death searching
For my scythe.
I am the rage-driven rain
Smashing the ground into
Submission.

You pour your light
Into this hungry beast.
You soften my blade and
Pad out my bones.
I am the love-filled breeze
Caressing the world with
Peace.

Without you
I can not
Be.
