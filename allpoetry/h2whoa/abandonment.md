title: "Abandonment"

Please don't leave me here, 
Don't leave me on my own. 
I can't complete this journey, 
If you make me walk it alone. 

Where are you all going?! 
I thought you were my friends, 
Please don't leave me here, 
Or here my journey ends. 

I'll do anything to make you stay, 
Just tell me for what you long. 
I'll do whatever you ask, 
If when I finish you're not gone. 

I can't believe they abandoned me, 
Their faces contorted with hatred deep. 
I just want to lie here, 
And enter an endless sleep.
