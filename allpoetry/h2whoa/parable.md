You told me when I looked in the sand,

And saw only one set of footprints,

That this meant not that you'd abandoned me,

But that you had carried me on your back.



Why did you lie to me, when I know you were gone?

How can our affair continue when you leave me,

And then lie?

I trod that path alone and you left me to struggle.



I did not ask for your sympathy,

And I certainly did not get it.

So why do you pretend my battle was yours?

They were my footprints in the sand.



You lied to me.
