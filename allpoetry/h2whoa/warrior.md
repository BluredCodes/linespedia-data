How must it feel to stand
As tall as he?
The iron warrior
Has battled with Time
Itself,
And made a pact with
Fate.

Now, the iron warrior for the
First time in a thousand
Millenia
Has been stirred into life.
The abyss of his mouth opens
And his roar splits reality
In two.
The storm clouds weep in terror
And gently brush his face
As lighthning lamely crashes
Against his armour.
Lamenting an age.

With a giant heave
The iron warrior is free
From the mountainside that
Protected us from his vicious
Blade,
And deprived us of his patient
Love.

The rocks cry out in desparation:
Their Lord Protector has abandoned them,
And the iron warrior pounds the earth.

I can only hide for so long.
What chance has the
Insignificant worrier
Against the
Iron Warrior?
