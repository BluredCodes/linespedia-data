The Blizzard-King has my heart.

A flash of unwelcome light,
Sears through my conciousness:
I know you have come at last.
A paper door
And a steel bolt,
Are all that come between.

Eyes weep red,
Your sword-teeth exposed,
You breathe the steam of
Righteousness.

Purge the world of
A parasite.

I offer you my throat
