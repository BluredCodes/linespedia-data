title: "Snowman"
writer: "h2whoa"

I shiver.

I lie where I fell.
I feel the dirt beneath me.
The gritty wood stabs at my bare flesh.

My fingers are numb.

I taste the warm blood as it leaks into my mouth.
Each snowflake that touches me is no longer unique.
They are all tainted by my polluting blood.

A blizzard is screaming at me.

I can not stand.
Nor do I wish to.
I can hear you.

My stomach tightens.

Your feet crush the untainted snow around my form.
You look at me, your eyes chill the blizzard.
I am a white-powder lump.

I float.
