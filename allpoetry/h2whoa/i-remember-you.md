title: "I remember you"

You are a reminder of an age gone by,
But I know we have met before.
I do not remember your face,
And no longer feel your touch,
But I know we have met before.

You are just a fading memory,
Of a fading life;
A shadowy recollection,
A shade of someone I once loved.
But you don't remember me.

I am not surprised that you look at me,
And the pyre of recognition is not ignited.
I held a funeral for you,
Not long after you had left me for dead.
Such a long time ago.

Indeed I have died a thousand times over,
Since we parted company an infinity ago.
And I look at you now not with love and longing,
But with hatred and bitterness.
