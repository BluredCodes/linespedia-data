---
title: "I Realised"
writer: "h2whoa"

I am not deserving
Of anything,
I expect
Nothing.

A glacier in my head.

I am not real,
I realised,
Barely even an
Essence.

I want to be a mountain,
But can barely manage
A whisper.

You are a monument
To all I am not.
You are a fire
Blazing in a frozen desert
Against the backdrop of
A starless night.

You are my everything.
You take my nothing
And give it form.
I can't exhale
When you breathe in,
I can find no joy
When you dance with
Melancholy.

I realised
I love you.
