I was dreaming.

I walked through a silent land.
I knew I was the
Sacrificial lamb.
I arrived at the base of
The overpowering temple.
The darkness hurt my eyes.

You laid me down upon the
Altar.
Fireflies wrote my name,
And the wind called
For my blood.

I thought the blade was
My Lover.

I do not dream.
