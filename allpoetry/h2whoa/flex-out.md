Flex out and touch me,
I know you'll only recoil,
Again.

You tell me I'm a kaleidoscope,
When really I'm just
Soil.

I am lost,
Glow on me.
I didn't want to lose,
The glow I had.
An echo
Is not a shout.

Can I crumble?
