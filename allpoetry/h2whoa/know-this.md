A silent echo ripples
Across the blackened waters
Of my conciousness
And the curls of smoke
From a forgotten fire
Emerge from a ring of trees.

The ice shatters already
Broken limbs,
As one by one
The wistful wooden wonders
Succumb to the Blizzard King's
Fiery, choking grip.

A collective cry as we hit the ground,
The stars weep with forgiving compassion,
Whilst your steel talons
Disfigure my face.
There is a fork in the road now
But I hover.

Know this:
I am reality.
