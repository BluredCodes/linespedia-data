I am watching me.

Don't turn your back,
Boy,
That would be a mistake.
I'm hunting you,
Stalking you,
I'm ready to strike.

I'm watching me, Boy.

I'm creeping in the darkness,
Embracing the freezing air.
I'm ready to get you,
I need to bite.
I'll take you out,
Boy.

For both of us it'll be

Goodnight.
