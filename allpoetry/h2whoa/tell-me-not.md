Tell me not that

You'll pick me up.

Tell me not that

Revenge will be mine.



The ice creeps closer

To my bleeding throat

And the cold stills

My once thrashing body.



Tell me not that

I'll be OK

And tell me not that

You love me.



If I'd been merely drowning

I could have fought.

I'm stuck in a solid-water tomb

At last the Moon is sated.



Tell me not that

You are fire.

I know the ice

Is here to stay.
