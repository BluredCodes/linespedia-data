I thought you'd stay there,
When the curtain went up.
You told me not to fret,
That I am a whisper,
You are the shout.

I thought I could stay there,
Where the Snow Demon sleeps.
I wanted to embrace his glory,
Find salvation in the uniformity,
Of his Ice Age grip.

I thought you were a flame,
A passionate roar.
You told me you would sear away
The splinter-sins embedded
Deep within my dream.

I thought I was a butterfly,
And you showed me that
I'm not.
