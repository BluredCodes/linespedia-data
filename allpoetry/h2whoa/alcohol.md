A whirlwind spins my brain,
And my eyes are lying
To me.
They tell me a thousand yards is
Within my reach,
Yet you are
Beyond my grasp.

I sing out loud because
I don't care.
I know I can sing better than you.
Who needs notes anyhow?
And lyrics are an unwanted
Restraint.

A fog of synthetic well-being
Narrows my veiw,
And the inexpensive anaesthetic
Broadens my horizons.

I love you.
You're my truest friend.

I need to pee.Just to let you know, I wrote this when I was drunk!
