---
title: "I am, You are."
writer: "h2whoa"

I am a broken man,
I am an imposition.
I am a plastic man,
And I'm looking

At you.

You are a real person,
You are not trouble.
You are happy to be,
Loving and caring to all

But not me.

I am a defeated man,
I am sitting in my box.
I am looking out from a plastic window,
And I'm calling out

For anyone.

You are ignoring me,
I am in disbelief.
You are giving me a plastic smile,
And I'm remaining

Unseen.
