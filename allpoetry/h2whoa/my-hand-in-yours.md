title: "My Hand in Yours."
writer: "h2whoa"

I was blinded,
And you were there for me.
You took my hand and said,
"Be calm it'll be alright".

You soothed my fear.

Your gentle clutch engulfed my hand,
You tentatively led me forward.
I could feel the love in what you did,
As you led me from the battle.

I fell in love at that moment.

You guided me past the obstacles,
The holes, the rocks and
The great searching roots,
To a place of sanctuary.

You saved me.

But we carried on walking,
You let go of my hand,
You had led me to the largest cliff and said,
"You're on your own".

You destroyed me.
