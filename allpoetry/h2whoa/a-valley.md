Silence.

I am an intruder here,
In this Valley of Shadows.
Blackness shines blindingly brightly upon me,
As I crush impossibly
Hard
Stones beneath my feet.

I
Slowly
Press on.

I see the eyes,
That can not be seen,
Watching me,
Though I am an invisible shade.

The stones become silky,
Black rose petals
That rise up,
Unseen but heard,
To meet my face.

And I turn my face to avoid,
Your angry glare.

Embrace me.
