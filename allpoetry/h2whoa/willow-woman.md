title: "Willow Woman"

Whisper wistfully, Willow Woman,
You know that I can hear your song.
Let the golden tide bring like fire
Warmth to the Ice Man's face;
Silently seeking soft seduction,
Let the thaw begin.

Know that when I lay there
In the blackened back-alley,
Consuming the filth of a thousand
Civilisations,
I couldn't even bleed.
As the rats ran over to view their bounty,
The sewers hissed their contempt,
I looked up at the harsh neon-glare
Of "Viva" and "Osiris" signs
Flickering at me.

And as the mist engulfed the Ice Man's body
When the neon "Viva" flickered out,
Know now, Willow Woman,
That he thought lovingly of you.
Your wistful song entered his mind,
And turned his form to water.
