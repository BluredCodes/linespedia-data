---
title: "Please go"
writer: "h2whoa"

Just to look into your eyes,
Fills my heart with such pain,
You do not realise what you do to me,
The tears you cause me to hide.

Every time I see you,
The strength goes from my knees.
I need to sit in the dust,
And cry the cry of pain.

Why do you stay with me?
Can't you see what I am?
A fraud, a fool, inadequate,
I don't call myself a man.

Just to look into your loving eyes,
Reminds me what I do.
You deserve so much more than me,
I can't stand that I hold you down.

A stranded angel deserves the world.
