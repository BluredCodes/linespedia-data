I got to thinking when out walking,
Over the hills of the mystical realm
Of Life.
I sensed a thousand woodland aromas
Assault my nose in a tender way,
And I felt the spongy mattress of leaves
And moss massage my feet
As I continued on my quest.

I looked up to the Heavens and they glared
Down on me. I walked on through the rain
And I came to realise it was pain.
And as the eyes of forest animals and magical
People watched me from the trees,
I reached a cross in the road and I
Lay down and wept.
A monster grinned at me.

At that point the Blizzard King came
Stumbling through the trees.
He gripped my heart with gentle brutality
And whispered in my ear.
"I will lead you on my child,
Out of the torrential pain.
You can dry your clothes and go to sleep
And never wake again".

I started to go with Him, to trust this deceitful beast.
I wanted to give in to him and believe that He knew best.
But I saw the sun hiding behind a cloud
And I turned to the Blizzard King.
"This trek may well be arduous
And I might have lost heart.
But so long as I can FEEL the rain,
Then I can at least
Anticipate
The relief of journey's end".

And at that point a beam of sun
Burst open on my head.
The Blizzard King vanished with a terrible scream
And I could breathe anew.
