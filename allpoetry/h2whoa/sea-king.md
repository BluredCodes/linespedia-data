Neptune wants my flesh.



The waves smash the cliff,

In a violent frenzy,

Eating the Earth with

Outrageous greed,

As the Sea-King cries out for my soul.



The gulls overhead,

Royal harbringers of doom,

Scream for my presence in his

Near-weightless court,

As he entices me down with the promise of peace.



I thought of you,

When I saw the sun submerged,

Before I realised

The sun was

Me.
