Lost, alone, afraid, where are you?

Why do you hide?

I need you, you need me

Running like a coward

Helplessly falling to the ground

Drowning in your sorrows

Where did you go?

When did you lose yourself?

You wear a mask of many colors

But I can still see inside

Rise to the surface

Free yourself

From the vanity of your own mind

Find your way back to me
