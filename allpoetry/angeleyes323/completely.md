I gave my heart, my soul

I gave to you my everything

Completely

You made me want to love you

I held nothing back

Words you needed to hear

Music to your ears

I learned a thousand ways to say "I love you"

The feeling deep inside of me

I gave to you endlessly

The chemistry felt so wonderful

The whole world broke into a smile

Nothing else mattered 

Now you decide to throw it all away

Didn't hold on tight enough

One day you will wake up

Notice something is missing

That something will be me

You could have had me

           Completely
