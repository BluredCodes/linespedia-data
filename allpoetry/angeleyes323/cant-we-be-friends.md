Where do I go to start all over?



When all is said and done



You promised me forever



I would always be the one



Now you walk away a fool



Broken promises and lies



Heartless in your emotions



Leaving me alone to cry



You said you loved me 



 Would be with me ‘til the end



So, how the Hell can you ask?



Can we still be friends?
