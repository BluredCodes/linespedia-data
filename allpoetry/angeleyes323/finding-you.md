Beyond the dimensions of time 



You stepped into my life 



Emerging from a senseless place 



Without rhyme or reason 



Souls entwined as one 



Minds of the same nature 



Did you find me or did I find you? 



Seasons of change or what is meant to be? 



Shapeless faces, but familiar spirits 



May I be the one you turn to 



Throughout the storms of life? 



Against all odds, I'll search your heart 



And keep it in a safe place 



Hidden from the damage of the world 



that you have known 



Trust your soul, you'll find the truth
