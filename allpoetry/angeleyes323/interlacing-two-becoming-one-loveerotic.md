The great spiral dance of love begins

Finding their direction by candlelight

Words whispered to her like fragments of a song

Unintelligible sounds harmonic with each breath

Ecstatic secrets of desire beyond the physical

Another entity formed, together they become whole

Inexpressible, metered intensity combined with warmth and meaning

Remitting way, towards a place they had never been

Kissing her between phrases of love

Wound around so tightly, a mixture of freedom and suffocation

In a space where illusion meets reality

Loving profoundly and completely

His body so closely molded to hers

Holding the very essence of who she is

Touching not only her body, but her mind and soul

Unbridled passion, a spiritual entwinement

He could see through her, around her, inside her

Holding her prisoner with the depths of his gaze

A deep well of longing and need

Two glistening and wanting bodies

Rhythmic dance ensuing thrusts of passion

Silhouette of one, pulses accelerating

Belly to belly pushing his love into her

Hunger deep as fire, waving like rivers flow

Taking possession of her, all of her dimensions

Her sinuos body arching towards him

Pulling her into him, him into her

Holding his manhood like a tether of some kind

His gentle hands slithering across her breast

Titillating deeply into her velvet mound

Dulcet, synchronized cadence of ecstacy

Forcing their way to peaks of bliss

Knowing that they had not fallen for each other

But had fallen towards each other

Interlacing two, becoming one
