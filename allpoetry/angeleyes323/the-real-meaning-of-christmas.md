What is the meaning of Christmas

Some still do not know

It's not about all the presents,

the trees, or mistletoe

It's about the Holy Night and

the star that shined above

That led the wisemen to Baby Jesus

Who gave the Gift of Love

His love is never ending

With us he'll never part

Be forever near us, residing in our hearts
