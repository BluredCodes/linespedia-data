You said you are hooked on me



But "drugs" are your addiction



Bringing pain and misery



Getting high is for kicks



Drugs are your first love



Love me? You make me sick



Snort it, smoke it, shoot them in



Saying you love me



Is a mortal sin



Your playground exists in your mind



Go on and play



If it's thrills you long to find



Don't look back, I'm saying goodbye



You have nothing



No emotions, everything has died
