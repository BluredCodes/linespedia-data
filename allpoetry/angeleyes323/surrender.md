My wounded heart bleeds intensely



Profoundly affected more as I see myself



inside of you . . .



My flesh is weakened by the memory of your touch



My spirit is damaged, purging my soul



Cutting deep like a razor's edge



I bleed with a riveting coldness



Your promises lash out without meaning



Are they lost in a sea of timeless infinity



Your actions speak of an unsettled mind



Are all your feelings dead?



Mourning the past?



In my dreams I encompass your being



Thirsting with desire to carry your burdens



Transcending shadows from your broken heart



Diluting the poisons from your misery



Wash your hands of all regrets, look no further



Surrender your love to me . . .
