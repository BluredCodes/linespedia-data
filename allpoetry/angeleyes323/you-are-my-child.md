My love is unconditional 



You can turn away and forget me 



Curse my name and even let me go 



But you can't stop my love for you 



For you are my child 



I loved you before I held you 



I loved you even more when I did 



I look at you only through love's eyes 



Forgiving you before you ask 



I walk with you through the darkest storms 



When my days on earth are done, my love will remain 



I will be your guardian angel, watching from heaven above 



My love is unconditional



For you are my child
