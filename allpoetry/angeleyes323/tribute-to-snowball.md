Today I lost a friend of mine

One with four legs and fur that shined

Stayed with him 'til he took his last breath

He'll never leave my heart, not even through death

Full of energy, mischief and fun

He loved to play outside chasing leaves in the sun

He loved to sleep, cuddled up beside of me

Beautiful blue eyes, sparkling as can be

Purrs, whiskers, innocent little feline

Snowball, the best friend you could ever findA tribute to my "little buddy" Snowball who passed away.
