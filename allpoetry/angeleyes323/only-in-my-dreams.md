title: "'only in my dreams'"

Feeling your touch, your warm embrace

Despite never being face to face

Your voice, gently whispers in my ear

Riveting a desire to be near

Passion pouring out across the miles

Making the wait all worth while

An eternal longing it seems

Will I only live it in my dreams?
