I don't want to keep you from flying



I dare not hold you back



I can't hold you prisoner for my own



Soaring above the horizon to find your dreams



I give you wings to reach that place you desire



Remember me when you go, but don't look back



In the darkest storm, my arms will be wrapped around you



I will be the bridge you cross when standing at the water



My tears will quench your thirst when you run dry



When you feel you can't go on



I will be the breath that gives you life



You don't go alone, I follow 



Tempting the fruits of what was



I will be the wind beneath your wings



As you soar like an eagle



Reaching beyond the limits of your being



Ride the waves, breaking through the tide



I set you free, to follow your dreams
