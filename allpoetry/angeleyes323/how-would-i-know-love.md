How would I know love?





Would it be whispered in a bated breath

Words rendering my heart's desire



Would it be an unfathomable beauty

Unconquered destinations, within my grasp



Would the stars descend from heaven

Surging out of endless space



Would cherubs take wings of flight

A thousand words echoing sweet rapture



Would my lips be pursed with sultry utterance

Articulately orchestrated without chasm



Would it sound like the nightingale

Impaling itself in superlative song



Would the whole world still to listen

In a stifled splendour



Would magenta winds gust

Cutting across the veins of the mesas and arroyos



Would symphonies circle through rhythmic spheres

Melodious prelude ascending with the aurora



Would the warm, crimson sun

Draw the spring into my heart



Would a shaded monochrome paint

Not only the flowers, but the shadow of roses



Would love survive death into eternity

Enclosed in a sanctuary of elysium





The answers became clear as your soul touched mine
