If only you could see yourself through my eyes

You would see the man that you want to be

You're my strength, the star that guides me

You think your dreams have passed you by

Not believing in yourself, a discourse of thoughts

Look at my smile, feel my heart beating

You are my solace, I am lost in your magic

One brief moment, a new meaning to my life

Your love holds me prisoner, I surrender to you

Close out the world and listen to our song

A heart whose love is innocent beats for you

Stand tall again like a statue

Formed by a great artisan

Paint the colors of the rainbow with my love

Sail to the clouds of your emotions

Let the wind carry you to the height of your passions

Like a vessel across the sea with no arrival

Trade the memory of your past

Step out of the darkness

Mellow to the tender light

See your beauty reflected through my eyes
