They say what goes around, comes around



If that is true, you have hell to pay



One's life is measured by how they live



You are certainly doomed



A demon in human form



Innocent hearts victimized



Cold-hearted fool, without remorse



What goes up, must come down



You're going

Trapped in a body out of control



I know snakes and how they slither



Your venom runs like arsenic through the vein



Enhancing your game, in the depths of other's misery



Walking through darkness backwards blind



Copiously flowing with vengeance



Your tank is running on empty



Soon you will be drained
