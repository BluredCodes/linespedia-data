---
title: "Fantasy of love"
writer: "AngelEyes323"
slug: "fantasy-of-love"

Somewhere in a distant place and time

I lived a fantasy of love divine

A fleeting glimpse of moonlight on your hair

Blue eyes dancing without a care

Moonlit walks, gazing at the stars above

Hand in hand, sparked by true love

I awake to reality out of my trance

Mirrors of your memory in every glance
