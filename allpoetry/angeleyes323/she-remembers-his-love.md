Greying hair, wrinkled hands

Memory fading away

Looking through the window

Not knowing where she is

Seasons come and go

Fleeting memories of her life

Forgotten names, forgotten places

But she remembers his love

Memorizing his every touch

Those deep brown eyes

Whispers in the night

How he swept her off her feet

Like it was yesterday

Faded roses, his words of love

Kept in her memory box

Keeping his love alive

Locked in her heart forever

The passion never died

Many thoughts forgotten

But she remembers his love
