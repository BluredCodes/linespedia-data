Go ahead, walk away

Vanish into the night

Leave it all behind

Forget what we were

Put yourself first

You always have

I will move on

Better days will come

A lonely fool, not to see

What you are losing

I will stand tall

While you fall to the ground

A cold heart

Never to return

To what you had

Go ahead, walk away
