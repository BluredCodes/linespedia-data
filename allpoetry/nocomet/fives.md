we hide ourselves in extremes
5 letter words yes fives
drowning self pity poor soul
clapping cleverly like butterflies do
reserving one for titlement time
staying on the only path
4u
steel yourself for truth
locked in your cage
blinded by your wants
bleeding with your needs
at least 3
options with anything
learn what then?
what you want
what you need!
what you know
2b
so
