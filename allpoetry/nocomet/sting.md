the vanity, the vanity
coupled with insanity

why are you afraid?
what scares you?

appearances
the cover of books

take a look
then open your eyes

go to nowhere you have been
come home and know that it is no more

the wonder of our lives
in with love and fear

the web of life
with the sting of death
