in the name of the love within me
in the name of divine truth
and in the name of divine love
for my highest and greatest good
and for the highest and greatest good of all
i release all of my fears and negativity
from my past in this life
and from all my past incarnations
and replace these fears and negativities
with divine love
from our blessed creator

i intend to ascend
i intend to achieve full consciousness
in this incarnation on earth

so be it
