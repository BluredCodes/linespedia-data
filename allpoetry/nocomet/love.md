oh to wish upon a star
to find out who we really are
dreams and wishs do come true
not only for me, also for you.

for your self to climb higher
all you need is a strong desire
do un to others as you'd have done to you
may peace and happiness be bestowed upon you

do not save money for a rainy day
you should for good purposes give half away
this action may seem bold
your returns will be 3 or 4 fold

when realisation that past, present and future are one
the mysteries of the universe will have already begun (to unfold)

for coincidences are not for us to debate
they are here to remind us of destiny and fate

as for the meaning of de ja vu
i have been here before, how about you?

all is love and love is all
this is universal law.
