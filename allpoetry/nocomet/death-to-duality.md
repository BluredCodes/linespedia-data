loving the good
hating the bad

makes our
worlds oh so sad

sick to my core
of this and that

two sides to a coin
what a load of crap

2 sides for the narrow minded
only believing what they see

those who are blinded
cannot let things Be

look and wonder
as people blunder

unaware
