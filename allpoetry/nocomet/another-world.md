title: "another world"
writer: "noComet"

an original thought
dreams pass by
hopes of ..
.. everlasting

streams of golden bays
shallow in may or june
piles of feathered broomsticks
crossing pennies without me
arrow the harp to loganbar sway
noteworth with y
do we die?
(in the)
end
