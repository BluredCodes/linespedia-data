the light of love
pure as snow
a moment of eternity
time waits for no-one
time eternal
the power of suggestion
the flickering .. a whisp of flame
the grace of a beautiful woman
silence
stillness
peace
the heartbeat of life
a breath
a sigh
echo, echo ..

silence
