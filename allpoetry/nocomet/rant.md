things that happen that go beyond explanation
words that babble and mean nothing
content in the silence of everything that does not matter
not pretending to be anything
knowing you can be everything
smiling to yourself at those who judge a book by its cover
for they will be a fucker
not a lover

as soft as silk
as hard as steel
time to make the dream real
