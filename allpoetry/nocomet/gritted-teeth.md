dealing with the anger within me
the hatred toward humanity i feel
unquenchable fires of rage for the stupidity
for accepting nothing that is worth something
..complaining, with no action to justify it ..
loathing gutless wonders
afraid of death
yet dying for metaphors all the time
sadness reigns in helltown
cesspools of grief
those with no belief
crying out for more
no key to unlock
