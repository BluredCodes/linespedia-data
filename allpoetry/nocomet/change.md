inspired by change
random directions
new things

change

amused and bewildered
frustrated and confused
slippery straws swaying seductively, seemingly an anonymous source
the discovery of one original thought
needs and wants
happiness, the fickle host
of hope for fulfillment

joy stolen by fear
shedding a tear
who knows where

growing in layers of wisdom as moments pass
complexities beyond rational ways

one night, one day
it will be our last ..
as we know it.
