is it true?
that I've fallen for you
am I confused?
my fears amused

stumbling with doubt
looking for a way out

to pine over what may never be
to be afraid that when you get what you want
you do not want it anymore

shades of grey
confusion caused by uncertain circumstance

meanings half said
powerful imaginations

suffering from our attachment to things

to have no expectations of the other
accepting our misguided actions

to be free to be

all is definitely not as it seems
a bubble about to burst

in awe of the webs we weave
undercurrents

masks coming unstuck .. who is there?
soulmates, friends, ships in the night
calmness in the heart
to listen without judgement
opening the mind

equality

stating your case
with little haste

aware of the sharp tongue
trying to bring you down

a way to figure
who is right?
what is right for you?

INSTINCTS

why explain that which is impossible

quietly ..
we go on our way.
