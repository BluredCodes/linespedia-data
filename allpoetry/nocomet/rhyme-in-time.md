title: "rhyme in time"

who needs T.V.?
imagine life without it
can you?
the days of old
before we lost ourselves
have we lost our marbles?
or not know any better
business destroys
a child plays with toys
adulthood - a child in bigger clothes

bless the soul on an innocent babe
in awe of the wonderment

shelves
pigeon holes
categories
judgement

free from restriction
free from fear
the fools will mock you
blind to their fate
veins that flow hate
