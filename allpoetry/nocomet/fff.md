hello to chaos
oh partner in my mind
shed your skin and hide for me
one more time
sip once more from my cup of lies
it is my ego i hope, that soon will die.

fortitude for the fortunate
who sup with the one of all

patience
wisdom
virtues of merit

misunderstood by non-believers
of faith in our selves.
