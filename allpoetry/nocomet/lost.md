i am lost and confused
with little love
gripped with my material being
seized with emptiness
repressing the anger of my inaction
wallowing in my toxic sludge
it is me whom i judge

welcoming food for the soul
when it arises
distraught when it fades
i tire of these melancholy accolades

my spirit is poor
distractions galore

i know there is more

food for the soul
dig a hole

gentle is the breeze
that caresses my face

a moment in time
time for a moment.
