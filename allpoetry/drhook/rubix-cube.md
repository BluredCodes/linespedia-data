Rubix Cube You dirty Rubix Cube I've been up all night trying one side at a time and I always lose I get red then white but not at the same time and it's still not right Rubix Cube I hate you Rubix Cube I try yellow then blue but there's still so much to do, screw it, I'm through I sit and cry boo-hoo cause I'm still not through cause I can break you in two Rubix Cube YOU Crazy RUBIX CUBE I pry you apart, screaming out my heart, stressing so hard I put you in order, back quarter by quarter, then ask, like a reporter Rubix Cube Why'd I bother, Rubix Cube? The only way I beat you was to completely break and defeat you and I had to cheat, too I turned you and turned you and all I got was burned through, but I earned that too
