title: "Tomorrow is so far away"

From the first time we kissed
I felt you were the one
I promised I would devote
The rest of my life to our love
But it seems as though we got lost 
Somewhere along the way.

Tonight was a blur to me
it felt as though you were waiting for this
Moment to tell me what hid deep inside you.
I didn't feel it coming on
But I realized it when you were gone.

First I was angry
I didn't know what I did
When I realized what you were saying
I realized you didn't trust me
Anger became sadness
I felt what I was up against
Though I didn't understand
