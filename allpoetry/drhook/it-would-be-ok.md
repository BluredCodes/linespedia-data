Standing by my mailboxa picture of your faceyour words are very special to mebrought a smile to this placeI don't know you yetyou're very far awayI wanna get the chancecloser than yesterdayWhen you're diving for pearlsyou gotta dive deeptake a good breath, now, honeymy hearts to keepSitting on my bikethinking you'll fit inholding me so tightsoulmates thick and thinNothing more brilliantin the midday sunthoughts of you and memoving as oneWaiting for the timeto touch your handto know if your minewaiting for the dayto hear your voice and the words you'll sayCouldn't stand to be so far awaycouldn't wait another endless dayRead your thoughtsI cherish what you sayFar across the oceanFar across the seaFar across the endless oceanI knewIt would be OK
