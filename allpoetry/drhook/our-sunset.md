I remember a sunset I once sat to watch
On a quiet and lonely summer's night
The wind came to a slow, soft whisper
As the sun cast the last of its golden light.

This was so different from any other I'd seen
I knew it would never leave my mind
For as I turned to walk away
I felt as thought those moments were meant to be mine.

And when I look at those moments I cherish
I begin to think of you as well --
For you have also placed a certain hold on me
One I know will never leave my mind...
