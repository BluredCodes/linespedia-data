---
title: "Past Love"
writer: "drhook"

We have grown together
Since our memories of the past
Letting us now accept one another
For who we are
We both have the strength
To work together
For behind our Love
We have a special friendship...
With all we feel
There is no room for doubt
Only encouragement
To keep each other above ground
We grow together
Without taking away freedom
And we can Love each other
Without invading our own peace of mind...
We both have the strength 
To work together
For behind our Love 
We have a special friendship...
