title: "Because of You"

Because of you loving is so much fun
More exciting than it ever was
Unable to love anyone else but you
And I’m positively sure that it’s all because
My rainy day are now filled with sunshine
And with a rainbow so beautiful in the sky
And my life’s blooming like a rose
But yet and still I wonder why
Because of you my life is more distinguished
More delightful than it has ever been before
You’ve turned my life into a garden of love
With hearts that are red and with friends that are true blue
Having nothing but the best of times
And it’s all because of you
