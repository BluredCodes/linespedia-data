In a state of euphoric solitude 
Almost like being in love 
With an untouchable dream 
And it's so cold 
When I can't see the light 
You told 
Me 
Secrets in that night 
Pretending they meant more than 
Words 
Wishing they were more than 
Words 
Am I alone Now 
Am I alone Now 
Am I even here 
You've got the best of me 
You've seen the worst of me 
Now I'm standing here waiting 
Am I alone now 
Leave me helpless 
Am I even here 
Where is my mind 
Is it 

LOST 

Is it 

lonely 

When will it all dissappear 
When I've lost the will to 
Feel 
Lost the reason to 
Breathe 
Lost the hope to 
Dream 
Lost the strength to 
Leave 

Find me helpless 
Leave me helpless 

Let's runaway 
And screw this world 
Let's runaway 
With nothing left to gain 

Let's fuck this life 
With these 

Words. 

am i alone now?
