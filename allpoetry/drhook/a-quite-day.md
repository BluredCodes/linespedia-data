title: "A Quite Day"

The quietness creeps in
like the sound of the clunker 
being started at the house next door
it’s abrupt but its nothing I haven’t heard before
finding myself in the company of no one
except maybe for the occasional cricket
rubbing his legs together trying to lure a mate for the evening
yet he rubs all night
the darkness covers me like
that three thousand dollar full-length leather coat I saw in Macy’s window
but I still tremble because it offers no warmth from the cold of my current state
I’m left to the vast complexity of mind
my reflection gives no console
my thoughts give no solace 
I am again…alone
