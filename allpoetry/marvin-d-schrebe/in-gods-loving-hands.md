title: "In God's Loving Hands"
writer: "Marvin D. Schrebe"
slug: "in-gods-loving-hands"
source: "AllPoetry"

We may not have the power to heal;But we know the one who does;God our great physician;As he is and always was.All things are in God’s power;And that includes disease;God can heal if it’s in his will;If not our pain he’ll ease.Man has seen some terrible things;None of us lives a sheltered life;The day will always come around;When we see life’s toil and strife.God knows exactly what he’s doing;Even when we don’t understand;Let us remember that all we endure;Is in God’s loving hand.
