---
title: "To My Son"
writer: "Marvin D. Schrebe"
slug: "to-my-son"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/2736-To-My-Son-by-Marvin-D.-Schrebe"
---

You are such a wonderful gift;
The best little boy on Earth;
You are not really my child;
But I've helped raise you from your birth.

I love you as if you were my own;
In fact I call you son;
I would literally die for you;
And it's been that way since your life begun.

I wouldn't trade you for the world;
You mean that much to me;
I'll be here for you no matter what;
Just watch me and you will see.

Nobody could ever take your place;
Those are shoes nobody could fill;
You will always be my son;
In a hundred years I'll be loving you still.

Always take care of yourself;
And always know you make me proud;
Of all the little boys in the world;
You'll be my pick in any crowd.
