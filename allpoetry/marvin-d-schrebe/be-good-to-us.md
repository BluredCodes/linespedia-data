---
title: "Be good to us"
writer: "Marvin D. Schrebe"
slug: "be-good-to-us"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

When others hurt us we forgive them;
So why not forgive ourselves;
Instead we lay tons of abuse;
Upon our poor mind’s shelves.

The way beat up on ourselves;
Really should be a crime;
The slightest little mistake;
And we ridicule every time.

How often do we look in the mirror;
 And like what we see;
How often do we treat ourselves;
How often am I good to me?

Our goals for us are often high;
More than we can reach;
When we fail to meet those goals;
Self-condemnation is what we preach.

Let take some time to be good to us;
For we take us everywhere we go;
If all we do is beat ourselves;
Our self-esteem will always be low.
