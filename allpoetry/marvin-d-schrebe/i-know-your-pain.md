---
title: "I know your pain"
writer: "Marvin D. Schrebe"
slug: "i-know-your-pain"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/2821-I-know-your-pain-by-Marvin-D.-Schrebe"
---

Your best friend took your boyfriend;Your pain must be intense;It’s at times like these we realize;Life doesn’t always make sense.Those you trusted have betrayed you;And you are so very young;There are no words to heal your hurt;No song that can be sung.Just know that time will heal your pain;You’ll get through this and move on;The pain you suffer will subside;And your battle will be won.I know that it’s hard to trust;You’re angry and your hurt;How could they when you trusted them;How could your best friend be such a flirt?Just know there is good in everything;And this will help you grow;I don’t need to have your pain described;Because you see I’ve been there and know
