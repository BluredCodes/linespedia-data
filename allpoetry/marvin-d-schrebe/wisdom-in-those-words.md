---
title: "Wisdom in those words"
writer: "Marvin D. Schrebe"
slug: "wisdom-in-those-words"

Our lives on Earth are short at best;
Sooner or later we all must go;
While we’re here let us live our life;
In a way that helps us grow.

We have but one purpose in life;
To be the best that we can be;
When we look back on our life;
Many good things may we see.

Let us strive to live a life;
That is happy and full;
When things start to go wrong;
Let acceptance be the rule.

Let us not complain and cry;
When things don’t go our way;
Let us accept it and move on;
Serene and peaceful let us stay.

Life is what we make of it;
We’ve all heard old timers say;
There is wisdom in those words;
From that wisdom let us not stray.
