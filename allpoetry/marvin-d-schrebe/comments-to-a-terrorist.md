---
title: "Comments to a Terrorist"
writer: "Marvin D. Schrebe"
slug: "comments-to-a-terrorist"
source: "AllPoetry"

How could you be so cruel;And lack respect for human life;You kill people in a cowardly way;And spend your time creating strife.People like you have ice water for blood;You thrive on other’s misery;You will never get away with it though;Just watch and you will see.I hope that when they find you;They send you straight to hell;For it ‘tis what you deserve;It would serve you oh so well.You are such a miserable creep;Satan must be your brother;You enjoy killing so well;You’d probably kill your mother.You know exactly who you are;Our government soon will too;At that point may they take your life;And that would be too good for you.
