When life seems confusing;Mysterious and dark;When we need inspiration;And can’t find a spark.Let us remember;The wisdom of old;The tidbits of truth;Old timers have told.Problems are for us;What manure is to a plant;They help us to grow;When it seems we can’t.Gold is not pure;‘Til its been through the fire;Impurities are gone;The muck and the mire.Some of our greatest gifts;Are tribulations you see;For through trials come wisdom;They shape you and me.Hi Everyone.
