title: "Loss of true love"
writer: "Lawrence"

I may not be the person that you wanted me to be,
But I was someone who cared for you forever endlessly.

I listened when you needed me to,
and I tried to understand.
I held you when you were feeling down,
I took your heart in my hands.

Now eventhough you've gone astray,
and pushed me far from you.
I'm glad I had a chance to be there,
to help you make it through.

When you were down and feeling blue,
I wiped away your tears.
You trusted me with everything,
I took away your fears.

So eventhough we no longer speak,
And I never see you around.
Please remember me for the person I was,
And think about me when your feeling down.

By Lawrence MacKenzie
