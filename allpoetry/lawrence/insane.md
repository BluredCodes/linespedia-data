Confusion becomes a part of me.It eats away my brain.My head is full of garbage.I am slowly going insane.I don't know what to do next.I don't know where to go.I try to ask someone for help.But my reflexes are to slow.By the time I get the words out.No one is around to hear.I look around in a daze.I scream out loud with fear.They put me in a little room.They forget that I exist.They assume that no one cares for me.They assumn I won't be missed.I say 'I may be crazy!''But I'm still a human being!'.I don't think your right to say,I should not be heard or seen.By Lawrence MacKenzie
