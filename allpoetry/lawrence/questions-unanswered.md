We were so good as a couple and everyone agreed.
Then one day you just up and left and brought me to my knees.
I begged you to try a little harder because I knew we were meant to be.
But you didn't want to listen you just did not want to see.
You shut out every sign of doubt and only listened to your fear.
You started hating me without reason and never cried a single tear.
You took every memory of me and did your best just to forget.
You figured it was best because then you would not have regret.
Then I seen you and I wanted to smile, but you just looked away.
You got upset when you seen me cause it ruined the rest of your day.
All I wanted was to see you smile and possibly say hi.
But your face was filled with so much hate I felt like I would cry.
I don't understand just why you'd want to be so cold.
I gave my heart and soul to you and treated you like gold.
I never did a single thing to hurt you in any way.
But you hate every part of me and why you will not say.
All I'm looking for is answers because I am so confused.
I never ever thought that you'd be someone I would loose.
Everypart of how we met was put together in angels hands.
It was so perfect in every way, I just don't understand.

By Lawrence MacKenzie
