Do I fall into your thoughts?
Do I come gracefully rolling into your memory
when you think of that jar of sorrows
locked within your chest

Can you speak to me
and not remember how your gaze
said everyting our tounges were to afarid to let go

Did i make you blue?
Never really could make sense of it anyway
Maybe theres no sense to be found in it
The reality of it all existed in our dreams
In those round shapes
that swirl beneath the fluttering lids
Puzzle pieces that when put together
only show theres nothing left to say
Our silence says it all.
