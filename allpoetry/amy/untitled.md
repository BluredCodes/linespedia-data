title: "untitled"
writer: "Amy"

I sit, an amber haired ghost
watching a bunch of girls stream outside to smoke.

They waste there words between quick puffs
filling each other with toxic conversation
of clothing and men.

I wonder how it is to walk through ones day
so unseeing.
I wonder just the same
if they know how it feels to be unseen.
