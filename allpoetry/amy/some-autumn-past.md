title: "Some autumn past"
writer: "Amy"

once was a time
when you were the young brave warrior
begging for my heart.

Once was a time
when i had slim slinking hips
and thought i could see everything
that rested between the folds 
of your pale brow.
Dreams ad sweet whispers
were all we needed to feed on.

Once was a time
when nimble fingers could turn the pages
and pens were not so afarid to tell the words.
Our future was a slumbered prose
scribbled carelessly upon each others youthful soul.
