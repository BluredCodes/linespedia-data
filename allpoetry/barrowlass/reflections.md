There's a mirror in my mind
look in there and you'll find
thoughts which, when first testing the air,
die, or others which spread wings and fly
to nameless recesses.

Other things you'll see reflected
highly polished or sadly neglected,
but, I fear, there'll never clearly be,
an image of what's meant to be "Me"
