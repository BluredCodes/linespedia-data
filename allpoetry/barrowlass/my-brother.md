My brother's bigand strongand musclywith tattoos on his armsand a hairy chest.My brother'll bash anyonewho picks on mehe's the best is my brother.He used to fight bears,catch whalesbut now that's not allowed.Somy brother doesn't work anymorejust sleeps and goes to the puband eats mealsthat fill the tableand he wearssize 20 bootsmy brother's the best'cos he said soso there!
