---
title: "An old woman's abacus"
writer: "barrowlass"

source: "AllPoetry"

A year for each womb-bound child,
A year for each child who died,
A year for the husband gone off to war,
A year for the nights she cried.

A year for the worries,
A year for the songs,
A year for the joys,
A year for the wrongs.

A year for the hugs,
A year for the kisses,
A year for the memories,
which she never misses.
