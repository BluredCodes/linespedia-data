Innsbruck's history paved streets
were soft on our visiting feet.

The Golden Roof glowed
as it had for 500 years.

The Stadtturm Tower allowed us
to view the city's beauty.

But far more wonderful
were the Nordkette 

Those mountains which protect and guard
the secretive Inn Valley.

Farewell, Dear Innsbruck
with tears I leave.

Maybe with joy I will return
bringing the remnants of my heart.memories of a wonderful visit earlier this year
