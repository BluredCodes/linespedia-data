---
title: "Amnesia"
writer: "barrowlass"
slug: "amnesia"
source: "AllPoetry"

They don't remember when
ripped from warm dark wombs
they first saw the sky

they don't remember when
sound was born
from their unused throats

they don't remember when
the cord was severed
and unity disappeared

they dimly remember when
warm milky breasts
pillowed their infant-heavy heads
