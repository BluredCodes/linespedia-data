---
title: "A model's prayer"
writer: "barrowlass"

Supersonic, aquatonic
please - make my face
a little less chronic.
The lines are encroaching
day by day
and soon the bloom of youth
will die away.

The years come round
with certainty
and leave their footprints
all over me.
So, please Mr Surgeon
with your magic knife,
please give my face
a few hours' more life!
