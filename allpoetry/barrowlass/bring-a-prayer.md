When you lay the flowers
bring a prayer

When you grieve in saddening hours
bring a prayer

When you live in endless limbo
bring a prayer

When you feel your life is zero
bring a prayer

When your heart hurts from endless weeping
bring a prayer

Please
bring a prayer

for you
for me
for our children

bring a prayer
for our world

please.
