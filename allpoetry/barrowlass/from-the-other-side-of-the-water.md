Two o'clock
"Breaking news
An aircraft strikes the World Trade Centre, New York.  No further details"

I continue baking bread, preparing vegetables for the evening meal.

Three o'clock
"It's believed terrorists are involved in the plane crash.  In the past hour another plane has struck the Centre which has collapsed."

Four o'clock
I am glued to the TV, watching in disbelief 
the dreadful events unfold.

I am frightened.
For me
For my family
For my baby grandson
For the world
For mankind.

I am 
alone
yet united
with all the fearful ones.

And the others sing and dance
on Godless streets.a not very coherent, but heartfelt account of how we received the news in the UK
