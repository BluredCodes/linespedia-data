---
title: "Thoughts about thoughts"
writer: "barrowlass"
slug: "thoughts-about-thoughts"
source: "AllPoetry"

I thought about you today.
Just a little thought -
a speck of dust on the surface of my mind.

It was there, then it had gone
to wherever dead thoughts go.
