It happened again
that black Tuesday
when human rain
fell from the WTC

It's happened before
in London
in Coventry
in Barrow (my home)

The dreadful price
of man's megalomania
and man's ignobility
is death

But
from the rubble 
and dust strewn streets
will grow
the flower

of courage and hopeJust a view from across the water in UK
