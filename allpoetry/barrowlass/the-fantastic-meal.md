Edward wanted the extra special
fabberoonie, zillionistic midday meal.

He asked for:

Fried fonsanka with side salad - gootchis.
Grilled galumbo with suckabilly sauce.
Poached pittimi, lightly done,
with catchem on the side.

And for dessert:

Sugared skootchi fruit,
laid on a bed of yum-yum nuts,
mixed with zoomis,
and covered with moo-milk.

Edward was full (almost) until tea time.
