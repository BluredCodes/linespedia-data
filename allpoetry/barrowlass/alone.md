title: "ALONE"
writer: "barrowlass"
slug: "alone"

Standing apart
fast-beating heart.

Can I play?
Can I play?

Kids all around
bodies of sound.

Can I play?
Can I play?

Standing alone
want to go home.

I can't play
I can't play

Kids catcalling
shrieking, bawling.

Won't let me play
not any day
