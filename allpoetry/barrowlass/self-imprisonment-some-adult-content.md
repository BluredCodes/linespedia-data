I stare from the window
A prisoner in this place
that isn't home.

His key in the door
wakens snakes of fear.

I have few teeth
His "dentistry" saw to that.
"No one will look at you now"

He calls me
"Ugly, toothless cow"

Last night we had sex
after he'd tattooed my body
with bruises.

Pleasuring isn't in him -
Only vengeful lust.

I want to go, fly free
from this cesspit.
But fear is the prison.

I can't find the key,
I'm bound, blinded, by my cowardice.
