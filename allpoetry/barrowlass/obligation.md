There was a time
when, shoulders bowed,
I took on the world

There was a time
when, heart overcome
sadness unfurled.

No longer.

I have found
that I am me
and I am free.

I have found
that I can do
whatever I choose.

But, do not fear,
I haven't turned to stone,
merely grown stronger.

For my strength
is needed
as is your strength
as is our all.
