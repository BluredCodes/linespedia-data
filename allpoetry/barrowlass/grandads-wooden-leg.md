title: "Grandad's wooden leg"

My Grandad had a wooden leg,
he used it to prop up the bed.
He and Grandma got frisky,
which was rather risky,
and now poor Grandad is dead.no disrespect to Grandad - he'd have seen funny side of this limerick, particularly as he DIDN'T have a wooden leg.
