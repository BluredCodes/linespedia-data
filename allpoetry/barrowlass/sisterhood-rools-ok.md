I've got my bona fide membership
of the sisterhood of life.
I'm a mother, I'm a lover,
I'm also a wife.

I HAVE got a clitoris (somewhere)

I do have boobs, contrary to first impressions,
though you do have to - what's the expression?
Excavate?  Still, I'm a member of the sisterhood
held by married and spinsterhood.

I'm sure I've got a vagina (somewhere)

Right on I'm not, but I'll tell you now
don't ever call me a stupid cow.
You can do lots of damage with emery board faces
particularly in tender places.

I don't suffer from penis envy (honestly)

I'm a woman with XY
and I often ask "Why
is XX more valuable than XY?"

Could it be that this biological action
gives nature a malevolent satisfaction?getting it off my chest - or somewhere else!
