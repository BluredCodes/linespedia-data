You make me feel
So special inside
You stand so still
With your arms open wide
You listen when needed
You talk when I need it
I love you so much
I feel like a prick
I don’t think enough
About you
And your feelings
If you wanted to
You call it quits
And I couldn’t blame you
Why do you stay?
“Oh, I’ll see it through.”
Thank you
I need that right now
You are the kindest person
The pain hurts…ow
You are always there
For me when I need you
Thank you thank you
Thank you…
