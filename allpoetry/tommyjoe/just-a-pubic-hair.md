I am just a pubic hair
There really is no where
That I can go for emotional support
I ask the other pubic hairs
And they come up with nasty retorts.
My days are long and difficult
Nothing much occurs
The only time I receive attention
Is shower time, and it hurts
I see a lot of things around
But nothing really happens much
I don’t get any exercise
But as a pubic hair, I don’t need it as such
I get a good view of my owner getting pleasure
And sometimes I get to cop a feel
But mostly it’s just pressure
It can be fun and sometimes it is
But after play time
I get all covered by jizz
It’s not a glorious life style
But it’s a job that’s needed
Oh, wait…nevermind, it’s not
“Please let me go!” I pleaded
I don’t want to be a pubic hair
I would like to be much more
But most people don’t hire pubic hairs
I really would be quite poor…
I can’t commit suicide either
Over nothing do I have control
I can just stay here and wait
For the root to scream “Oh, no!”
When I hear the cry
I know it’s almost over
I will just fall away and die
The next time he bends over.Maybe I really am on crack...
