title: "Oblivious"

She just stands there
Her back to me
Oblivious to all that I do
And say
And think
She knows I exist…
It’s not that bad
But I want more
But I’m scared to approach her
She doesn’t know what I want
And I can’t expect her to
But I wish she would
Somehow hear my thoughts
My desires
I have talked to her once
And seen her many times
She’s nice and funny
And very good looking
I would like to get to know her
Know what she thinks
What she wants
But she is oblivious
To all
To me…this is about a girl in one of my classes...i was thinking all of this at a photography gallery with that class
