“Follow me
I am your savior
I am here for you
Follow my behavior.”

How easy it is
To start something
Get up on a rock
And say anything
People crowd up
They point and stare
All you have to do is ask
And they get completely bare

People follow anyone
Big
Tall
Short
Small
Looks don’t matter
Neither does style
You’ll always have followers
All of the while

What I did was simple
I stood up and shouted
I proclaimed I was god
They cried and pouted
I quoted some verses
They stared in disbelief
I was here at last
They all sighed in relief

Stupid ants in a crowd
Follow one, follow all!
Start up
Wait…there’s a fall
There’s always a catch
You can’t guess what it is
I’ll get you to do
You are about to hatch
A new life of lies
One that I create
I hold you up in the open
You are the bait
You lure them to me
One at a time
You will me to be
All that I say
I am what you want
What you need
Me to be

You need people to follow
Be it false or it true
You need people to follow
So I will be that for you
