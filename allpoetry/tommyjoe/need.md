I need you
All of you
Your laugh
Your smile
I am just a half
And you are my other
You complete me
And I complete you
Don’t be scared of love
Because you are young
Love strikes hard
And sudden
It is a great thing:
Love
Everyone should have it
So if we have found ours?
Why shy away?
I have been good to you
We are always together
Why do you run
From love?
But not me…
You tease…
Let me think there’s a chance
And then off you are
Dashing away
Dashing my hopes
My dreams
