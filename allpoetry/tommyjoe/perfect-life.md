Get up at 6
Commute at 9
Coffee at the café
Lunch at the grill with wine
Window office with a good view
Trees, parks and water ways
Look out
Stare quick
And little people like ants
Below you like always

Step on people
Walk on them all
So mighty you are
Never have to take the fall
Other obstacles in front of you
Are made to pay
Move fast
Push hard
Everything is removed
Nothing is let to sit and stay

Merge with traffic
Merge with life
Pull in quickly
Causing nothing but strife
Life is yours for the taking
Here’s the silver platter
Hold it
Take it
Everything here
Nothing matters
