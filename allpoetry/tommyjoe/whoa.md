I was swinging through the jungle
Just like Tarzan.
Limb to limb, vine to vine
I swung without a care in the world
Objects hit me in the face
Hit me in the crotch
But I was steel baby
100\\% stee---ooooh!!
Okay, that one hurt a bit
But I’ll be fine.
Just let me sit over here for a second…
Whoooh….whoooh….oookay…
Almost ready…
Whoa!  Who is that?
A half naked lady swinging through
The trees, casually going along
And I thought I was the only indecent
One here in these woods
Oh, well…time to go
Chase her along…gotta get some of that
It’s not everyday you see an ass like that
Especially of the same species
Lions and tigers and bears, oh my
They get old, believe me…
No moans with the lips…so sensual
No more forest…it’s buildings now.
Hmmm…drugs are bad, how did that happen?
Okay…sidetracked…why am I here?
Oh, the girl…hmmm.
Where could that piece of tail have gone?
Shit…over there…I’ve got her now
Crap!  An overly-large and burly man
Has just blocked my way!  Oh, no!
Shit, that suck…but this is my reality…
Kick his ass even without SEAL training
Haha I love this!  Why fight?  Why chase?
I don’t have to
This is MY reality, bitch!  Mmmmm
I got you now!   Hooo-eeeey!!
5 star hotel here we come
No shabby motel for this lay…
I’m wakin’ up in style.

Nevermind…I was wrong.
I just wake up the same old way:
Hot and sweaty with a massive hard on.
Damnit…This is an actual dream of mine...except the last part.  That was all too real...LOL
