“Think for yourself,
Question authority”
		-Tool, “Third Eye”

Think for yourself
A great line for our society
Of followers
And non-thinkers.
In this day and age
Most people don’t think
They follow
They follow what’s on TV
What’s on the radio
Whatever medium they enjoy,
They follow
“Buy this…”
“Buy that…”
We are a society of consumers
We buy what they tell us to
The shampoo that makes our hair magically soft
And gives us an orgasmic experience while washing
The cola that Britney Spears drinks
The make-up that Tyra Banks wears
We listen to these people
Who have no place to talk
If you drink Pepsi, do you begin to look like Britney Spears?
Tyra Banks looks great without the make-up, too
Think for yourself
Buy what you need
Question authority
Is it really an authority?
Should it be an authority?
Is it your authority?
Tyler Durden knows what he is talking about
We buy what we don’t need…
And listen to those that put themselves up on the pedestal
Think for yourself
Question authority.
