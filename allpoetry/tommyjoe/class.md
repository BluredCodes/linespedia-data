I am so bored
I hate this class
My teacher sucks
I’ll kick his ass
I’m so damn tired
I want to sleep
Shut up you fool
Don’t make a peep
I wrote the paper
But it is not due
That pissed me off
Shut up you toolI wrote this during my science lecture class after finding out the professor delayed the due date because he didn't want to deal with all the papers until the weekend.  I had stayed up almost all night to finish it.  I was not the only one unhappy with him.
