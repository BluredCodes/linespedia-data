Spacing out
Bored as hell
Passing notes
I might as well

Who the hell is Ayer?
I really don’t care
Do his ideas have effect?
Please show me where

I get nothing from this
A waste of time
Piece of shit class
I don’t mean to whine

Philosophize my ass
I’d get more use outta that
Something to do with me
You stupid useless prat

I wake up early
Drag my ass outta bed
I’m way too tired right now
This class is messin’ with my headUmm...I wrote this during my Philosophy class in between drawing charicatures of the TA who was lecturing and writing profane things on my roommate's notebook, who is also in that class.
