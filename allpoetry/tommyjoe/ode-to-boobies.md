I like boobies
Boobies are fun
I like boobies
I wish I had one
Boobies are great
Boobies are cool
I swim with boobies
In the middle of my pool
I like a good boob
But two are the best
More than that is strange
But, hey, it’s your chest
Round and firm
But soft all the while
I want a lot of boobies
In a large pile
An orgy, you say?
Sure, it suits me
An orgy of boobies
Is fine with me!i'm a guy...what can i say?
