Why are people never real?
They always put on a show
Put on their masks
We never know
The real them
All we see is their mask
They don’t take it off
They just bask
And become comfortable
In their new skin
You always lose
You never win
You need to open up
And tell me your thoughts
Your body decays
But your mind never rots
Deep inside of you
Really deep in there
The true you awaits
You may ask “Where?”
I don’t have the answer
I just know it’s there
I found mine
Yes, I asked “Where?”
But not of you
Or him
Or her
But looked in
I looked inside
And truly seeked
I found me
Now you find you
Don’t believe me?
You should…it’s true
