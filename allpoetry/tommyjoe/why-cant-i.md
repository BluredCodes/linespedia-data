---
title: "Why Can't I?"

Why can’t I get up
And walk over to her?
She seems very nice
She talks
Smiles
Laughs
Looks over…
Her eyes stop on me
But just for a second
But that second delivered so much
It is a second longer
Than anyone else got
She wants me
I know it…
Then why can’t I just get up
And walk over to her?
She seems very nice
