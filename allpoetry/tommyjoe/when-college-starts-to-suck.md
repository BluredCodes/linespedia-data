College is great
You can wake up late
Go to bed late
Do whatever you want
Your parents aren’t there
There’s nobody to tell you what to do
You have almost complete freedom
Almost, but not complete
There are some rules
In the dorms,
You can’t be loud too late
Or too early
You can’t piss the crap out of the RA
You can’t pee on vans
Especially when drunk off your ass
At a party
And then don’t lie to the cop
He’ll find out
He’ll chase you down and catch you
And that’s when college starts to sucksome people don't like that most of my poems don't have a beat...but i really don't care, so make up your mind on the content and try to see what i meant by it (be it stupid or serious)
