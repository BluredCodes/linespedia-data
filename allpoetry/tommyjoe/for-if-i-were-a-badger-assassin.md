I wish I were a Badger
A Badger Assassin to be precise
For if I were a Badger Assassin
I would be as cool as ice

The Badger Assassins are my heroes
I dream to be one every night
For if I were a Badger Assassin
It would make everything all right

I would be a part of something great
Something larger than myself
For if I were a Badger Assassin
I would keep my mental health

I could have keep my official Bandersnatch
Up on top of my fireplace
For if I were a Badger Assassin
The Bandersnatch would not go to wasteI hope I get in, I hope I get in, I hope I get in...etc., etc....
