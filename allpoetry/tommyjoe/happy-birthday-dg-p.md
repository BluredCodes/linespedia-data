This poem is to say
Happy birthday to Stephanie
She’s such a great girl
Although a little whiney

Just kidding Stephanie!
You know I love you
Even though you are a dork
A nerd, and a doody doo

You are a really funny person
You always make me laugh
Your stupid little jokes
About me not taking a bath

Well, okay, I have to admit
I laugh at you more than a bit
At the way you ramble and blabber
Crack is bad Steph, you really should quit

You know I’m just kidding
I really really am truly!!
You are a great friend
Even though just a wee bit unruly

This is taking longer than I thought
Just to say happy birthday
I hope you like 19
May this be a great turning dayHappy birthday Steph.  You really are a great friend.  :)  Thanks for everything
