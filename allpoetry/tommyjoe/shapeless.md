I am always on a rollercoaster
A coaster of emotion
The last thing I see
Embeds deeply in me

A movie I watch
The music I hear
It all comes inside
I can’t help, I open wide

Anything you want
Deep
Shallow
Always malleable

Shape me
Mold me
Cut me
Fold me

Play a game
Come on
Have fun
I am here, the one

I am the paper
Use me like origami
A plain little sheet
Take me, take me you creep

I am whatever you want
I’ll make you smile
I’ll be whatever you need
The object of your greed

Use me
Abuse me
Hurt me
Throw me

I do not matter
I am the tree
Screaming and she is cut down
A silent scream, without a sound

I don’t complain
I just sit still
I’m here, just use me
