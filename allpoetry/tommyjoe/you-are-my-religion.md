Webster’s Dictionary defines “religion” as “a supernatural constraint, sanction…”
In following this definition:

You are my religion.
You have supernatural control over me
You constrain me when you feel the need
You control me when you feel the greed
You use me when you need the weed

A sanction is an agreement
I agreed to be with you
I am held to you
By my own free will
I believe in you
Through my own faults
And through my faults alone
You are my religion
