Than you are offering
I want somebody close
Somebody to hold and care for
If I ever need to just get laid
I’ll come back to you
But until then
Stay away
We can be friends
But keep on those pants
I don’t want to fuck you
I don’t just want what’s between your legs
I want your personality too
If that is what you’re offering
Then I will definitely accept
But none of this cheating shit
I’ve heard too much about it already
Change your ways
And I’ll be yours
But as you are now
