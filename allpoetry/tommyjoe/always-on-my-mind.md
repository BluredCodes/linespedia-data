Always on my mind
The pink carnation of my soul
Every morning I wake up
Every night I lie down to sleep
Every blink of my eye
Every turn of my head
I see you in everyone
Every face is yours
Every body is yours
Every thought is yours

I am consumed by love
You are the beautiful althea in the garden of my life
The scent you carry with you
The way you wear your hair
The way you smile and look at me
The way you walk
Your voice transmits through my mind
I listen to your syllables
Your name…and the way it can be saidI love you Brenna Jean, and this poem only begins to describe how I feel.  There is so much more I wish I could put into words.
