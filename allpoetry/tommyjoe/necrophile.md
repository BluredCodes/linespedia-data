Fucking the dead
A disturbing thought
Fucking the dead
A pile of rot
Fucking the dead
Who would do such a thing?

Some people are gross
Some people are twisted
While lying in your grave
You don’t want to be fisted

The sick fuck sneaks
The sick fuck peeks
The sick fuck fucks
The sick fuck reeks

I don’t understand this race
In which such things happen
A graveyard is a holy place
These things shouldn’t be occurin’

Fucking the dead
A disturbing thought
Fucking the dead
A pile of rot
Fucking the dead
Who would do such a thing?
