Why does she do this?
Play with my mind?
One or the other please
No more games for me.

I love you a lot
But cannot just stand around.
I need to have somebody
And don’t need the confusion
If you love me
And I love you
Why can’t we be together?
There is nobody but yourself opposed to this
Your mom loves me
And has always said we would be together
I just don’t understand you
The reasons for your confusion.

She said she loves me
She said she wants to get married
She said a lot of things
But most negate the previous

Why can’t you make up your mind?
If you want to be with me
Then be with me.
If you don’t, then tell me
And let me move on.
I don’t want to stand here
And wait for your confusion
To subside.
Good bye, until your minds conclusion.
