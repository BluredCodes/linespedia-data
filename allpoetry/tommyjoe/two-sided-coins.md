title: "Two Sided Coins"

I’ve seen a rich man ask
I’ve seen a tough man cry
I’ve seen a grown man snap
I’ve seen an honest man lie

I’ve seen an officer break the law
I’ve seen a holy man swear
I’ve seen a doctor work drunk
I’ve seen a married man stare

I’ve seen a sad man smile
I’ve seen a poor man give
I’ve seen a bad man help
I’ve seen a dead man live

I’ve seen a perfect man fault
I’ve seen the devil swear to the lord
I’ve seen everything there is to see
I’ve seen a politician keep his wordThis is about the different sides of people that are usually hidden.  Most like to show one side, but we all have the other side, too.
