I’ve already said that I like boobies a lot
You understand that point
It’s not that I’m on pot
Well, maybe I am
But that’s not the point
I am just a guy
I like things that go yoink
I have a thing between my legs
And I enjoy the space between yours
I have heard such great stories
And partook in the lore
I really like the lower extremities
They are a thing of great fun
Both womens’ and mine
From the others I run
So many tools can be used
To please a woman’s lower extremities
The traditional method
Plus our own special remediesThis one's for you JustPlainJoe!
