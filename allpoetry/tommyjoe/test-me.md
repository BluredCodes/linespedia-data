I was right here in class,
Sitting right flat on my ass,
Not listening or paying attention at all.
My grade just went down,
Flew straight into the ground,
I wonder ‘bout my reasons I am here at all.
I failed the test, I flunked the quiz.
I must be close to retarded, but gee wiz!
I do not care; why am I here at all?Hahaha...written during a math lecture...
