Three Worlds
Trapped between the hate of three worlds,
And the struggle of two made one,
You've lived your life with the battle of two worlds as one. 
You've had to learn how to look straight into their eyes for the truth. 
You understood them like others never did,
and have a connection of a lifetime with them. 
You see deeper than others and you know more. 
The friction of these two worlds,
just to have someone understand, care, recognize their identity. 
Then there is another world, completely opposite of the other two.
When the three worlds meet it will be world War III and it will kill me,
because with their passionate hate, they will destroy each other.
This is your secret between the hate of three worlds.
