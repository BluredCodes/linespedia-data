---
title: "My Little Feet"
writer: "Linda4ever"
slug: "my-little-feet"
source: "AllPoetry"

A pair of shoes that wouldn't fit
That are so big, my feet swim.
To small for shoes, with so much room
In kids shoes its all I need.

Instead I see big shoes
That stay and look at me 
While I past them by.

From store to store I go
All I see are shoes that are not me
And that are way too big for little feet.
