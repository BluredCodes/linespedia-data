I know your gone, but I want to know why?
I didn't even get to say goodbye. 
Or even ask you why? 
In my dreams I see you, and in reality I've lost you.
Nevertheless, you will always be in my heart. 
All I knew is that I love you, 
and I felt comfort inside. 
Now that your gone I have so many 
questions without any answers.
