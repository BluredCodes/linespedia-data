What happens when I lose my only friend? Who was always there for me, who gave me strength to succeed.Who said, give me a smile, your beautiful. The one that never lost faith in me, and always believed.This is a true friend, who I have learned so much, and will never forget.It's the memories and experiences I will cherish for a life time.I will miss my friend, when its time to say goodbye.But there always be a place for this special friend in my heart. So it really isn't a goodbye. But another way to say, thanks for being there.
