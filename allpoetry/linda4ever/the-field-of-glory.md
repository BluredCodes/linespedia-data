Have you ever seen fields full of glorious flowers blooming.
How wind gently goes through your hair. 
Watch the beauty of nature and its wonderful mysteries.
Realizing that you are one with nature. 
Know how you wouldn't be able to survive with out it.
Yet, why we are destroying the most beautiful thing in our life?
Without it we are only destroying ourselves.
Its like one big field of Glory.
For the world is to enjoy, not to destroy.
