---
title: "Homesick"
writer: "Linda4ever"

I look around me in my surroundings, 
and it reminds me of an unresolved past, 
in my unconcious. Memories of what was 
once there, when I was there.
Reality hits me, with spontaneous emotions,
towards our troubled society. Where is home?
I'm so far away from Home.
