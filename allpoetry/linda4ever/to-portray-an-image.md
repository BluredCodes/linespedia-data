title: "To Portray An Image"
writer: "Linda4ever"

To portray an image that is not me.
To live a life that is not mine.
Trying to please those the want more, realizing that you can't do more. 
You'll always be wrong in their eyes, they'll never be satisfied. 
I thought it was acceptance, yet I was wrong.
Yet, I don't have the problem, I'm fine just
the way I am. It's not right to portray an image. 
Just be an image of your own.
