Once you've met a special person. 
Who makes you laugh and smile. 
Who lends a hand and comforts you. 
Between the thoughts we cherish, the Love we gave, 
and the happiness we had. 
Yet you know when its good something must go wrong. 
You must be brave and understand, 
its all from fear of falling in love. 
For caring and the desire burning through your heart and soul. 
Evil stands between to shatter everything you've had. 
Know you'll never love the same or replace a part of you that misssing. So no matter what, there will always be place for 
him in my heart, no matter how much love hurts.
