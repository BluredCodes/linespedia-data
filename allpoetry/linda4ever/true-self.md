You meet guys that appear to be sweet. 
Yet, don't let experience fool you.
Since, their not as mature as their suppose to be. 
So, you can be young and know more. 
Even though the truth hurts, one must be 
mature and strong to accept it. 
They might think they'll never hurt you physically.
Yet, verbally it hurting inside.
To think they're afraid to let their guard down,
so what's inside we can't see. 
Realization hits that they loved, lost and forgot in the end. 
However, they will never find that same love. 
So, no one will really know, who they really were
except the ones that saw inside.
