A scrunchy is different colors,
Shades, and all sorts of sizes.
Some are as small as bugs,
And others are very similar to
Pumkins even tight, and loose.
This scrunchy feels moods
of up and down, or maybe both.
A scrunchy get thrown all 
Over the place, and pulled, with 
Mercy. The pain it goes through, 
as it sees whats all around me,
And more
