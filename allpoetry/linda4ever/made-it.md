Imagine just starting school, and having to go through changes,
of classes, teacher's, as well as friends. 
As you reflect back to the good times, and the bad times. 
It seems that you've grown up and learned things about yourself.
As for other's some don't even change.
All the studying, testing, homework, uniforms, books, and etc.
It seems so much easier now that it's coming to an end.
Hey sometimes you realize that maybe you could have done better
in something, but at least you've learned from your mistakes.
Finally it's coming to an end, and having so much information
use it to follow your dreams.
