My man is the charming fellow, with the great sense of humor, you have to Love. His daring, in his adventures. Delicious better than cookies. Desiel he can carry the world. His naughty but not an animal. Also Lovable and irrestitable,HubbyI Love You XOXOXOXOXOXOXO * MAUH *
