I do not wish to say goodbye, but instead I'll see you later. 
If you think about it, you leave one life, and go into another. 
I started a new life, and beginning. 
Oh, but what's the difference, isn't it the same. 
No its not, I would never forget the memories, good and bad times. 
In the end I have new and old friends.
