I finally found my Love, the one I always dreamed of. 
Being with you, makes me realize how much I Love You, 
and how I am very lucky to have a man, like you. 
I Love you for who you are, and who you want to be. 
I will always be here for you. 
My darling my Love for you is eternal. 
You are my inspiration, and I think of you always 
in the morning as I wake up, 
in the evening, and at night as I dream of you in my sleep. 
You make me feel so good, everytime you touched me, 
kiss me, knowing that you want me. 
I hope to do the same, if not I hope you show me how. 
Knowing you have changed my life for the better. 
I've learned so much from you and I've grown very attach to you. 
There will always be a place in my heart for you only and forever. 
I Love everything about you and I wouldn't change anything in the whole wide world. 
* Muah*
