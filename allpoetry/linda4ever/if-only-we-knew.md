In the beginning, we were afraid.We learned that these big furry creatures were bad.Then I wondered, how can they be bad if these creatures, are one with nature.Their rage comes from survival, just as we put up are guards, and have tempers.Yet, if you look into their eyes, you notice their not these horrible creatures.Now that time has past, we know these creatures, ascute, funny, interesting, loving, and caring.Their know as Monkey's, Gorrila's, Chimpanzee's, and Bamboo's.Yet, there is always a sad part to this story.There are evil men, who are called pochers.They kill them for fun, fur, and money.Their full of greed with no remorse.One day it will be the other way around.They are smarter than you think.Soon pocher's will be hiding. To all my furry friends,they are the one's that I think when I am sad, they make me happy.
