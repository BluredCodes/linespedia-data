A room of wonder, and memories. A room that describes who you are. This same room carries the memories throughout your life. As your eyes look around the room. You have noticed all the wonderful things you have that has a meaning and brings memories. This room becomes your own world, its where your thoughts and dreams come true. A room which carries your belongings, and memories.
