title: "Wanting more..."

I love the way your hands roam through my body, the way you grab and feel me. How I closed my eyes and our lips touch as we kiss and never stop. So we are both tied up in lust and desire for more. Wanting more, yet being grateful for having someone there, being able to be satisfied.
