I have a fear, that scares me so, you wonder, what this fear could be.
The fear of knowing my past.
How would I react, what will happen?
This makes me confused and scared.
I want to know but I'm afraid.
What happens if I get hurt, or don't understand.
My life would change, cause part of that was never there appears.
But then you ask what's so bad about your past?
My answer is it's the fact I don't know it.
Its a part of me that's missing.
When someone asks do you know who you are?
My response is I know only half myself, but not the rest.
This is my biggest fear, because I lived my life without my past.
