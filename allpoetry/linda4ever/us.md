When I close my eyes, I enter into my own world.
One where the two of us become one.
A world where our love is everlasting.
I feel our hearts beat and our palms sweat.
The heat rises.
Is it Love, Lust, or Both?
I open my eyes to find
what I dreamed is happening at this very moment.
I figured out what it was.
The passionate desires in our hearts made real.

Dedicated to my Hubby
