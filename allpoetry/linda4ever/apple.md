Apple is not just a fruit you eat, it's so much more. An apple can come in different sizes and color.They are juicy, and quite good for you.Some have a sour taste and are green or yellowish. Also, apple's smell good, especially if it's baked or cooked. Hmmmmmmmmmmmm.....Like as in apple sauce, or apple pie, bet the guys enjoy that one.
