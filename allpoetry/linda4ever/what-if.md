I wanted him to know, but I didn't tell him. 
He thought it was the best, but I knew it wasn't. 
He wanted to hold me, but he didn't do it. 
I wanted to kiss yet I knew he would never.
We were standing together and stared at each other in the eyes. 
Yet we both knew we loved each other.
But we turned the other way.
So that we won't hurt each other and this is True Love.
Forever.
