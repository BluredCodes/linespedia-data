This is a DustinIt lives in dungeons, dumpsters, driveways, and dugouts.A Dustin eats dimples, dreams, Danish, and diamonds.It likes digging, dancing, dazzling dares, and drawing.It wears dark dresses, dines, and does love dandelions and daffodilsone day a Dustin, Dangerously drove and drank my delicious dreams.
