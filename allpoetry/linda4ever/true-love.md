I finally found my Love, my knight in shining Armor
The one that always be there, the one that never leave. 
The one who says he loves me just the way I am. 
His the one that says I'm sexy and makes me feel so good. 
His gentle touch and tender care, the Love he gives to make me happy. 
This is my Hubby, the Love of my life. 
The one that knows I love him, just the way he loves me. 
This is the guy I'll never forget, the one I'll always love, with all my heart. 
Hubby my heart will always be yours and no one elses.
The love we have is True Love. 
And I'll never regret any moment I spent with you, by your side. 
Love you always and forever. Your girl Melissa  * MUAH *  
Hey your the best thing thats ever happened to me. Te amo
