There is nothing wrong with being friends. 
It's better then not having any.
It's someone you can talk and laugh with.
There's all type of friends:
True Friends...
Best Friends...
Distance friends...
Close friends... 
Distant friends...
and real close friends.
Someone that's there for you and, you can do the same.
Then there's that fear of losing a friend, if it goes
beyond friends, or just not working.
Sometimes you might get hurt, yet it's a great experience.
Plus you tend to learn a lot about eaxh other.
So when you come to think about it.
Is it just friends, or more.
