My Lost Love I Found You
I lost the only man I loved. 
Yet, it was for the best, 
since he wasn't the same man I fell in love with. 
When he left, it felt like someone tore my heart out, 
I felt crushed. 
Yet it seemed no matter what I did to stop the pain, and forget. 
It would always come, one way or another. 
Never did stop loving him, 
I always said it was the medicine. 
Yet, I knew I had to go on with my life. 
Then one day I got a phone call it was my Love. 
He said I want to see you before you go. 
So we did and he said I love You 
and I never stopped Loving you. 
He felt speechless, 
and all that happen was tears running down my face. 
As our lips touched, 
I knew we had True Love. 
So in the end I did find my Lost Love.
