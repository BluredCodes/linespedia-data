title: "Is Spring Beautiful?"

Spring cannot be compared to the beauty in our hearts.The sun is setting and April Showers are falling down on the grass. Which makes you think how your lover is more beautiful than the flowers blooming.When May flowers bloom they are alive and filled with beauty. Yet they die when it is Winter. To that special someone in your heart. So long as beauty is there I will always be filled with care.
