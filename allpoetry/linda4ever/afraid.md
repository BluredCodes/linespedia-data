Why is it so wrong, to be afraid of the unknown? 
The feeling you get when you want to know, but there's no where to go. 
The pain you feel when you can't take it anymore. 
Why even bother, or care, its just going to hurt even more. 
What if I know, what will happen. 
Maybe I can't take it, or its to late to except it.
