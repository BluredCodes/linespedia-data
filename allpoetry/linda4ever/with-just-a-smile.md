With just a smile, you can make someone's day better.
This is the smile you face life with.
It's what you need to go on when things go wrong.
A special quality that makes you feel good.
In reality, when you pass someone you don't need to 
say anything, just smile, and that means more than words.
Besides you get cute nicknames like smiley and giggles.
You can help someone else smile, or be happy.
Showing a grin from cheek to cheek.
So remember never forget to smile.
 does this make sense?
