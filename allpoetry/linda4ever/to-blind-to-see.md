Let's see how does it start, you become friends, and take it from there.  

Then, there are guys who will do anything to make you happy.   

Yet, your to blind to see therefore its to late.  

Even with friends you must be cautious and careful with your actions. 

Well, if you want to be more than friends, you take a risk on friendship. 

However, if you don't take that chance. You'll never know what might of been and could of been. 

If someone loves you so much, but it doesn't work out, they would still want to be friends.
