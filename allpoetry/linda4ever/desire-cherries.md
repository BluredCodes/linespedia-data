---
title: "Desire Cherries"
writer: "Linda4ever"
slug: "desire-cherries"
source: "AllPoetry"

Even though they're small,
round, and a luscious red.
I have a cravening 
that I can't hide.

Maybe one or two,
hey how about three.
I try to savor in my mouth
for an everlasting taste.
Please forgive my desire
For cherries.
