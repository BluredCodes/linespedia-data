As I dance with you, with your arms around me. 
Everything seems so far away, it's only us dancing. 
When I look at you and, our lips touch I feel that I'm in heaven, 
and that you are everything to me. 
Even when you sing it's so sexy and romantic. 
Like your voice, as you smile. 
This is the night I'll never forget, it's being with you. 
Wanting you with desire that only increases as we get closer. 
It's not just being aroused, 
or getting turned on by any movement, or words. 
It's so much deeper than that, it's what I dream of.
