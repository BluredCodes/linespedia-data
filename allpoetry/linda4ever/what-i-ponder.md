Imagine how wondrous it isto ponder to your fullest. Enteringa world of your own with thoughtsof you wondering about. What youwant, need, feel, dream, desire andof course fantasia about. This isa place where your thoughts go wild and come loose, yet one must becautious that they don't loose tractof where they are and what they'redoing. But when you come back to reality and go thinking I can dowhat my thoughts and desires want,but its not right. If I do I mustface the consequences, yeahI might enjoy the heat of the moment, it was good. But what happens next? What would my world turn into? Yet no matter what Iknow I will have someone always there to comfort meand love me with all his heart.
