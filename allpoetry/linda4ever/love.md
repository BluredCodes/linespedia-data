One touch can change everything one kiss can mean a lot.
They say not to fall in love with him. 
But they were to late.
He came into my heart and left without saying goodbye. 
Everyday I waited for a call on the phone, or even a letter.
I try to forget him, but I can't get him out of my mind and heart. 
I wonder if he feels the same. 
I miss him so much.
He made the choice, his last choice.
He will not come into my heart, to hurt me. 
Its already broken, and shattered. 
I loved, still do and I will love forever.
