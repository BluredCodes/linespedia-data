title: "The Struggle Of Two Loves"

One of the biggest struggles is life's love.  
Especially when its two types of love.  
Theirs the lust, and passionate love. 
Then there's that love in caring for the person, for what they've done. 
Yet in the end someone will get hurt. 
So, yes you can love two guys but not in the same way.  
Usually guys don't understand so they make you pick between them. 
In other words they become jerks. 
In the end you don't pick any, and the one that gets hurt is the girl. 
The hardest thing is having two guys loving you with all their heart.
