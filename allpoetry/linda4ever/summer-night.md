Summer Night
One Summer Night we ran away for a while.
Laughing on the wet grass underneath the stars reminiscing about.
The love we shared and how much you cared.
Being with you always makes my dream feel like a Summer Night.
My day is not complete if I don't see the moonlight shining on your face.
No one can break our love because love is hard to find.
I am yours and you are mine.
