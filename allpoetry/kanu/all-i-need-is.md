title: "All I need is..."

All I need is love;
And you in my heart,
For ever.

All I need is joy;
And those loving moments,
For ever.

All I need is a song;
And the dancing rhythem,
For ever.

All I need is a pain;
And that in my heart to remain,
For ever,

All I need are flowers;
And the fragrance in it,
For ever.

All I need is strength;
And a helping hand,
For ever.

All I nead is a freedom;
And still to be under trail,
For ever.

All I need is an open sky;
And no one to catch me,
For ever.
