My wife my partner
The one who has lived with me
Bearing me and mine behaviour
I love her.

I am in her,she is in me
We both are not apart.
We both are one
Not two
Not away
But stay
In each other's heart.

I mean it ,I feel it
Like a star,
That is in the sky,
Always and ever,
Not far from it
But most nearer and nigh.

My wife I love her
Than anything in this world. 
My wife I need her
Like any poem needs words.
