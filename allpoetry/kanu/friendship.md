Friendship is like a newly made,
A bubble in the water.
That remains for a while in a parade,
And then gets vanished for ever.

Its like a handsome shape,
Of the cloud that floats in the sky.
And all atonce it gets changed,
With twinkling of an eye.

Never true, never right,
Like a bubble and a cloud.
It tempts and remains for a night
And then leaves us to cry aloud.

We know this friendship's never true,
But still we wander in search of new.
