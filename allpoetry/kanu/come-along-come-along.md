title: "Come along, Come along."

Come along, come along;
O' my darling,
Every moment is precious.

No time to spare;
This only unit's that scarce,
Every moment is precious.

Every day, every hour;
Every minute, every second,
Every moment is precious.

Waste no time in quarrels;
That hurts the tender heart,
Every moment is precious.

Heart speaks, I want to love;
Grace this moment with it O' beloved,
Every moment is gracious.

Brings no joy and happiness along;
That moment shall no way long,
Every moment is precious.
