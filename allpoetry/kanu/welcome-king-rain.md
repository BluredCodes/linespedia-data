title: "Welcome, King Rain"

O' Cuckoo;
I want to sing with you the sweet songs of rain.
O' Peacock;
I want to dance with you to attract the mighty rain.

You Open your colourful feathers wide,
You throw in front and then take it back.
You then lift your tiny toes,
And speedup your dancing steps in glee.
O' the dancing Dude.

The Cuckoo up in the sky fly,
Above the tree branches rest.
With full of enthusiasium  sings,
Songs of rain in 'coo-coo' two fold 
O' the mystic Guest.

Then together they both meet ,
The dancing dude and the mystic guest.
The rain has started thundering and lighting,
Making that forest a Live Opera Concert.

And I be there watching all this in 
And along with you Welcome our King Rain.
