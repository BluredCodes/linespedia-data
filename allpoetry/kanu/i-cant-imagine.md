---
title: "I can't imagine"
writer: "Kanu"
slug: "i-cant-imagine"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

My wife, my life is
My heart and my soul.
She is my heart beat,
She is my whole.

All microscopic cells and all my arteries,
All my veins, and thin haired capillaries.
The flow of blood to and fro,
Its all her magic that tends to grow.
Every minute and every second,
That i live is due to her,
And is for her.
Even every microsecond.

I cant imagine
She not being in me.

Every breath that I take,
I take her name in it.
I would never breath ever,
If she is not in it.
And if i never took a breath
And never made air flow in me.
Never will any cell survive
Nothing will live in me.

I walk and she is there,
I talk and she is there,
I work and she is there,
I laugh and she is there,

Place me in fire,
And she is with me.
Place me in water,
And she is with me.

I cant imagine,
She not being with me.

For me she is everywhere,
No place that without her.
She resides in me,by me,
No act is without her.

What I wear is for her,
What I do is for her,
What I dream is for her,
What I get is for her.

I cant imagine,
She not being for me.

My heart beats and says,
I love you my dear wife.
My every art speaks,
I love you my dear wife.

I cant imagine,
She not being by me.
