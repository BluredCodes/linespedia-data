title: "I am the messenger of love"

I am the messenger of Love,
I am the messenger of joy,
I am here to spread,
All love and all joy.
I am the Santaclaus.

I love everyone.

Black and white,
Heavy and light.
 Young and Old,
 Weak and bold.
Men and Women,
Boys and Girls,
 All the humans,
 And all the animals.

I love everyone.

I love you all,
 My job's just that.
I can't at all bring,
 The feeling of Hatred.

Merry Christmas I go singing,
 Red and white my dress.
Red for joy that i give,
 And White for peace, I bless.
