---
title: "The Diamond and my Heart"
writer: "Kanu"
slug: "the-diamond-and-my-heart"
source: "AllPoetry"

The diamond in your finger,
And the heart inside me.
Both as same and all equal,
My eyes to them do see.

Both do shine and glitter bright,
Saying they are pure and clean.
One hundred percent in their way,
They do this really mean.

Not for any sake of joy,
Shall they be far from you.
Both are here to promise, they will,
Lifelong  be with you.
