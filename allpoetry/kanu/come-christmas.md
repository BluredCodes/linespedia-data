Come Christmas, Come Christmas;
O' the happiest fest.
Come Christmas, Come Christmas;
O' the happiest fest.

Santaclause that day all the way,
Visits each an every place.
And with him brings toys and games,
With children he plays.
Come Santaclause, Come Santaclause;
O' our dearest friend.

Santaclause when he comes along,
All the friends sit together.
And they sing and dance and sing;
That old song together.

"Jingle bells Jingle Bells;
Jingle all the ways,
Santaclause is coming along;
Riding on the sledge."
