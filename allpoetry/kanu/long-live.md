Long live long live; my love, with love and passion.
No hatred no chaos nor any sort of detestation.

With laughter and merry mind, goes on,
Thou every day passes in cheerful mood.
With drums of delight, and blissful bassoon,
The harmonious harpsichord and fun filled flute.

The vivacious violin and the gleeful guitar,
Pleasant sounding piano and the base voiced bass.
The cheerful clarinet it's sounds play at par.
And the full set of proud percussions give them applause.

All these joyous, buoyant instruments get together,
To form a musical orchestra.
Where my love sings the songs of joyful weather, 
Like a live, happy concert of an opera.
