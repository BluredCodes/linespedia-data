Sit by my side,O' mine Lady,
Mine heart calls ye along.
To shower all the love it holds,
In the form of a song.

The song that has a beautiful rhyme,
And the tune to sooth ye heart.
That teaches every time it plays,
"LOVE" , The wonderful art.
