---
title: "Fighter, Fighter"
writer: "Kanu"
slug: "fighter-fighter"
source: "AllPoetry"

Fighter fighter,get up and fight,
No more can you be a coy.
Set the mark for yourself right,
And turn up in to a strong boy.

No more can you get afraid,
From this cowardly world around.
Do and let this world know,
That you are not with frighting, bound.

Fighter fighter, get up and fight,
The injustice that you face.
Bring out all your strength you have,
And dont get suppressed in this race.

Get up and prove your self,
And then spread love and smile.
Remember that God is with you,
Always and all the while.
