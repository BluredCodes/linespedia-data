title: "Far Away, Far Away"

Far Away Far Away I see the rising sun,
The rays are soothing enough
the light is cool enough
The joy is wonderful enough.

Near by me I watch, a sweet baby born,
In my arms , cozy and calm.
lifting hands and legs in wish 
With its shining face in charm.

Far Away Far Away, I see the burning sun,
The rays are hot to bear,
The light is bright to bear,
The joy is great to bear.

Near by me I watch , this same baby,
Turing in to a strong man.
Ready to fight and be a rebel,
With this whole world 

Far Away Far Away I see the setting sun,
The rays are partly cool,
the light is partly cool
The joy is partly cool.

Near by me I watch that man,
Graying in to an old ,laden ,mature person.
With all his past he walks along
the silent and the lonely lane.

Far Away Far Away I see no sun,,but yes,
Its rays are still I can see.
Its light is still I can see,
Its joy is still I can feel.

Similarly the man that's gone,
Is not a forgotten past.
He lives on in the hearts of those,
He has changed their lives, and will last.
