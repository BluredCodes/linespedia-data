title: "Sing The Songs Of Joy"
writer: "Kanu"

O' sing the songs of joy,
As joyous days are back again.
I want not to remain a moment,
In the world of agony and pain.

Utter to me the words of love,
"I love you", " I will never leave you".
I want not to hear anything else,
As I know,"I cannot live without you".
