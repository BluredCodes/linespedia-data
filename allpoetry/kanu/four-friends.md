Four friends stand,
Under a tree,
Laugh, sing, dance, talk,
All gossip,
Then they are happy.
They keep meeting.
Thier friendship tightens.
Few days back,
One who were mere strangers.
Now fast friends,
Who cant live without each other .

Seperation time,
Four of then go away,
One in south,
One in North,
One in East,
One in West.
That tree is lonely,
That place barren.
But before parting all four,
Had promised to meed one day here.

After years they decided,
To meed same place.
But no could'nt meet.
Again they decided,
But still some reason,
And they could'nt meet.
Atlast the death struck,
To one of their friends.
News spreads, all know,
But still those three could'nt meet.

Time was the reason,
No time was the reason.
