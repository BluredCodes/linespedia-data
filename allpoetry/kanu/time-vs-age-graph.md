Time Vs Age Graph.

On the X axis;
Sleeps the Time,
Ever incrementing ahead.
On the Y axis;
Stands the Age,
Rising with time always.

The line of the graph,gradually rises,
Starts from 0 point, and increases.
Diagonally in that even graph,
That's same for every individual.

Some understand some dont,
They waste this time, unthoughtfully.
They repent but later for what,
When no way that they can change it.

Thinketh not, while that time,
Flying near thee eyes, go.
Uncaring they let go let go.

Time rise means age rise,
Age rise means maturity.
Every moment you remember,
You are elder to your previous moment.

Remember the life always is,
To learn and then to teach others.
To rise in every aspect,
By every passing moment.
To understand the experience,
And then to implement it in the other.
To make a point to achieve,
That you wanted to but couldn't in your past moment.
To remember every moment,
Teaches us a lesson to change,
positively in the coming moment.

No use if you think,
And cry on a small thing,
And be discouraged.
