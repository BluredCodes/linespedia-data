title: "Existing (sad humor?)"
writer: "dianes"

Existing is what I do best,
Everyday is another test,
Don’t turn on the TV to watch,
I’m already depressed, now where is the Scotch?

Would be so easy to just drown my sorrows,
But, what happens when I awake tomorrow,
I would only feel worse, than I do today,
So it won’t help to drink my sorrows away.

Yes, I could always pop a pill,
To simply swallow away all of my ills,
But instead of feeling better,
I would end up sicker then ever.

I could always go out and find someone to play,
Hop in bed and stay all day,
Yes how that would help my self-esteem,
To feel like a hooker wouldn’t be too keen.

I could get down on my knees and pray,
Will he give me answers to my life’s disarray,
I try to listen, to hear,
Please grant me the answers that I need so dear.

Well doesn’t seem like any of these work,
So I just keep existing like some dumb jerk,
Maybe one day I won’t just exist,
I don’t know how long I can live like this!"little frustrated with my life!" lol
