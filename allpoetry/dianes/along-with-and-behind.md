---
title: "Along With and Behind"
writer: "dianes"
slug: "along-with-and-behind"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/53388-Along-With-and-Behind-by-dianes"

Along with the darkness
Comes the light of day

Behind the tears of sadness
Comes the smiles as they play

Along with all the heartbreak
Comes the hope of new love

Behind all the hurt and pain
Comes the memories of love's remains

Along with the youthfullness
Comes decreptiness with age

Behind all the masks we wear
Comes a heart filled with fear

Along with all our friends
Comes the enemy in wait

Behind all our lives
It all ends up in the hands of fate
