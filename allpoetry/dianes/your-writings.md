---
title: "Your Writings"
writer: "dianes"

I read the words you penned today,
Written across the page,
You expressed so many emotions,
Left me in a daze.

Your thoughts so well written,
Phrased oh so well,
As I was reading,
I was caught up in your spell.

The places that you take me,
Along your ink stained page,
Simply do astound me,
They get better more with age.

So keep writing on my friend,
I can’t wait till my next trip,
Who knows where your thoughts will take me next,
With your emotions your writes have expressed.

Many writes have taken me,
To places I have never been,
Some I can relate to,
Whether in happiness or sin.

Then there are the fantasies,
Filled with love and lust,
Others with pain and agony,
Tissues are a must.

Amazing how these writes I read,
Can touch a simple heart,
And on that last thought,
On to read, I will depart...written for all the writers that i have read here who have touched me in one way or another with their writes...some more then others but each in their own unique way...
