---
title: "You are Still with Me!"
writer: "dianes"
slug: "you-are-still-with-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

It’s amazing to me,
After all these years,
Even though you're gone,
You are still with me.

You creep into my thoughts,
Enter into my dreams,
I thought I cast you out,
You are still with me.

My life was a living hell with you,
One day happy, next day blue,
Total confusion, never understanding,
You are still with me.

The mind games you played,
The sick love you gave,
Even now from your grave,
You are still with me.
