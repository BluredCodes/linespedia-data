---
title: "You Walked In"
writer: "dianes"
slug: "you-walked-in"

I wish i had a transporter,
To transport me straight to you,
You already have my heart,
Now take the rest of me too..

Frustration at the situation,
That I've gotten myself in,
I really wasn't looking,
How did you walk in...

You didn't give me any old lines,
You became my friend,
Lots of words thru time,
Your spirit did transcend...

Before I even knew it,
You made yourself at home,
Propped your feet up,
Upon my heart of stone...

I laugh now at how I think,
That I had hardened up my heart,
How I would never let myself feel,
The pain love imparts...

My soul and my heart scoff at me,
Because they both know,
No matter how I tried to deny it,
A love did grow...

For now you have a home,
Transport me into yours,
I thought I had the door locked,
How did you just walk in...."don't know how, don't know why, all i know is i know"
