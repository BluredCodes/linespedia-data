---
title: "How Much Longer Can I  Pretend"
writer: "dianes"
slug: "how-much-longer-can-i-pretend"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/48259-How-Much-Longer-Can-I--Pretend-by-dianes"
---

H-how much longer can I pretend,
O-our feelings aren't real,
W-when I know how we feel..

M-many nights and days of talking,
U-unsung melodies,
C-chasing our dreams,
H-how much we have sighed..

L-longing to be together,
O-only we have to wait,
N-now isn't our time,
G-good things have to wait,
E-even though we both know,
R-real life is a hassle...

C-can we hold on,
A-and if so for how long,
N-not knowing the answer...

I-I love you...

P-please know,
R-reasons keep us apart,
E-even though our hearts are,
T-together,
E-eventually,
N-no one will keep us apart,
D-dreams for now will keep us together..."sigh"
