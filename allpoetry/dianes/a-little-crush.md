I thinks me got a little crush,
On someone, maybe you…
I can’t divulge the name,
I’m too embarrassed too…

I know it seems so silly,
For a woman of my age,
To act like a teenager,
And sigh, at the mention of his name…

It’s just an infatuation,
The mystery you see,
But it’s ok,
Cuz, it’s all innocent you see!
