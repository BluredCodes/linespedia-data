---
title: "It's Where I Belong"
writer: "dianes"
slug: "its-where-i-belong"

Gather me in your arms,
Where I long to be,
Whisper all those sweet words,
You’ve longed to say to me.

Press your body,
Up against mine,
The taste of your sweet kiss,
Gentle against mine.

Look into my eyes,
As I look into yours,
The touching of our souls,
Opening from within.

I’ve longed for this moment,
For so very long,
To be in your arms,
It’s where I belong…luv yah!
