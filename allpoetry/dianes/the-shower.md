Let’s go get a shower,
Get dirty while we get clean,
I’ll soap you all up,
And wash all in between.

I’ll cover you with kisses,
From your head down to your toes,
Maybe I will make some stops,
We’ll see how it goes.

Bodies pressed together,
Nothing in between,
Except for the trickle of water,
Between my cleavage and the steam.

I will lather up my body,
All soapy just for you,
Slide up and down your skin,
It’s Ms. Bubbles to you.

Press me up against the tiles,
Let me wrap my legs round you tight,
Baby let’s live out our fantasy,
Make me yours tonight.

We can be in a waterfall,
Water coursing down,
I can hear those jungle drums beating,
But it's my heart pounding the sound.

I love the feel of you inside me,
Let me envelope you in my heat,
I can feel your passion rising,
Let’s make our lust complete ...
