Blessed peace has descended
you are finally at rest
Body, mind and soul
done with life's test

Your body fought so valiantly
to keep you in this world
While your mind and soul had left us
about two weeks ago

You were such a proud woman
always dressed the best
If you didn't like something
your words would never rest

It was hard to see you lying there
so fragile and so weak
I thank the Lord above
