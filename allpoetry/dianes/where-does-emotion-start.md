---
title: "Where Does Emotion Start?"
writer: "dianes"
slug: "where-does-emotion-start"

The mind is a funny thing,
Especially the thoughts it brings.
You never know what will pop in your mind,
At any given space of time.

So many thoughts,
So many dreams,
Good ones, bad ones, 
In betweens.

It seems when I try to concentrate,
It’s when I lose myself in thought...making me distraught.
My mind makes up its own mind bout what I will say or do.
I wonder am I just a vessel for my mind to tell me what to do.

Feelings and emotions…
They say comes from the heart,
But in the end it’s your mind,
That makes the decisions you must impart.

They never say you broke your mind,
You always break your heart.
You can lose your mind...You can lose your heart...
But tell me I wonder where does emotion start?
