title: "Absence Makes the Heart Grow Fonder"

Absence makes the heart grow fonder,
Now I know it's true,
For every day that we don't talk,
The more that I miss you...

When I wake up in the morning,
Immediately I think of you,
I want to curl back up on the bed,
Pretend that you are here too...

I know that you are sleeping,
As I dress to go to work,
I can picture your little grin,
What are you dreaming of with that look...

A smile begins to creep upon my face,
Imagining your toussled hair,
A blush forms upon my face,
Picturing you lying there...

I try to push you from my thoughts,
I miss you way too much,
But you never stray very far,
Because it's my heart you've touched..."sigh, miss yah"
