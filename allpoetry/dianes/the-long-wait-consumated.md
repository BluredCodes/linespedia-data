She was so nervous,
He was so excited,
After months of talking,
Finally they would meet.

With plans set in motion,
Reservations now done,
All that was left was,
The anticipation.

So no one else they would meet.

She must be insane.

Finally, she sees his face,
His eyes search for hers,
Instantly in each others arms,
Feeling like their hearts will burst.
 
Straight out to the car,
Finally alone,
All she can smell,
Is the call of his cologne.

She tries to ask about his trip,
Playing it as cool as can be,
Noticing the way he's looking at her,
Completely devouring her, you see.

They check into the hotel,

And tired of acting patiently.
