Your voice it sends shivers,
Up and down my spine,
It brings a smile to my face,
I record it in my mind.

I play back the recording,
Because you are not near,
Remembering your sweet words,
Wishing you were here.

You have a way of speaking,
Straight to my heart,
It’s not so much the words you say,
But the tone they do impart.

You have become so special,
Though society says it’s wrong,
My heart can’t tell the difference,
Our friendship is growing strong.

If nothing ever happens,
If never do we meet,
I thank the Lord up above,
