title: "I Was with You Today"

I was with you today
did you feel me walk beside you
did you hear my voice
whispering I love you...

When you showered
did you feel my touch
upon your body...

As your heart beat
did you feel mine 
beating along with yours...

Did you have that feeling
of someone watching you 
it was me...hoping you would see
I was with you today..."xxoo"
