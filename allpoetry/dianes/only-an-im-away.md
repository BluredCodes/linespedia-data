---
title: "Only an IM Away"
writer: "dianes"
slug: "only-an-im-away"
source: "AllPoetry"

Isn't it amazing the people you can find,
Who share the same tastes you do,
Who can relate and are so kind.
and make you happy too.

You never have to meet them,
You talk to them online,
They make you laugh,
They give you hugs,
They are only an IM away.

It's funny how you get so close,
Without ever seeing their face,
They get to know you inside out,
Doesn't matter what your race.

They can read in between the lines,
Know when you are sad and blue,
They share in your happiness,
and they cry with you too.

I think we are so lucky,
especially those homebound,
That we can reach out and touch a heart,
without ever making a sound.

It's all in the words we type,
In the way we feel.
Sometimes it can be wonderful.
Almost surreal.Just a little ditty bout being online and how I've been so lucky in the friends that I find.
