---
title: "You Took My Heart With You"
writer: "dianes"

You took my heart with you,
When you said goodbye,
I struggled so hard,
To keep the tears from my eyes.

I wanted to keep you
All unto myself,
Wanting to be selfish,
Share you with no one else.

Frustrated and mad,
I let you leave,
Knowing you would come back,
If I just believed.

I've never been possesive,
Nor jealous in my life,
But when I know you have to go,
It always causes me strife.

How I wish that I could keep you,
Only just for me,
I know that isn't fair,
But I long for your company.

So you took my heart with you,
As you left me here to stay,
I will remain here waiting,
Missing you today..."Sigh, hate goodbyes"
