title: "You Tell Me You Love Me"

You tell me you love me,
My God, you’re a fake!
You stab at my heart,
Why don’t I just hand you the stake?
You curse me out,
You knock me down,
But in front of others,
I wear the smile of a clown.

No, you don’t beat me physically,
I could heal from that you see.
But your words cut like a knife.
How can you treat me like that…
I’m your wife!
