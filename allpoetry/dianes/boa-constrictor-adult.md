Think of me as a boa constrictor,
Beautifully coiled,
Waiting to feel your warmth,
As I slowly unfurl,
I slither up upon your body,
Slinking inch by inch,
Wrappin myself around you,
My muscles wrappin you within,
My eyes start to hypnotize,
I pull you into my grasp,
You feel my grip upon you,
As you tighten up your ass,
I have totally engulfed you,
With my strong desires,
You are a willing victim,
But a victim you are not,
You love the feel of my constrictions,
This boa is filled with more then lust,
Coiled together tightly,
We writher in such motion,
You are the snake charmer,
