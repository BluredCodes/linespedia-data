title: "With Thoughts in Mind"

With thoughts in mind you stop to jot down a line or two,
Always ending every message with remember I love you...

You let me know that you are there and how much you care,
You tell me of your simple needs and comfort me in my despair...

I read your words upon the page, can almost picture your smile,
Damn this heart of mine for not knowing distance miles...

For wanting to be with you, to be held in your arms,
You know that you have suceeded, I have succumbed to your charms...

I know you didn't plan it, and you know neither did I,
But when a connection is made, this you can't deny...

You gentle words of love, have taken me under your spell,
What will become of us, time will only tell...
