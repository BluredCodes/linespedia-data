As I lay me down to sleep,
I pray dear Lord please help me keep,
Perspective when I need it so,
Into trouble I don’t go.

I have this problem Lord you see,
I meet people who appeal to me,
They have some interests the same as mine,
And they always seem so kind.

I have so much fun,
I learn so much,
I lose myself,
I say too much.

So Lord help me keep perspective today,
Let me have fun,
Let me learn,
But keep perspective in return.
