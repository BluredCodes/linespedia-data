I sit waiting for my mate,
Looks like he's running late,
It's a twist of fate,
Gonna have to break our date.

Have to go to work,
Face my bosses and jerks,
When I'd rather stay,
Just to talk away.

Well maybe later today,
I can come back to play,
Maybe by eight,
He will be awake.

If he wouldn't stay up all night,
He'd learn how to get up right,
But can't be mad you see,
He's my honeybee.

Oh I know this is silly,
But I'm just a silly willy,
He brings the sunshine to me,
I just enjoy his company!just feelin in a silly mood.....so i wrote a silly ditty.....i know its not to witty.....
