---
title: "Is It Wrong to Love"
writer: "dianes"
slug: "is-it-wrong-to-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

Is it wrong to love,
To share your feelings,
Does it matter to whom you feel this way for,
How do you tell your heart to just simply ignore…

Your heart can’t tell the difference,
At least mine can’t, I know,
Once I tell someone I love them,
My feelings can’t help but grow…

So how can someone say it’s wrong,
To love another person,
How can you love just one,
It doesn’t make rhyme or reason…

Many people cross our path,
Over our time here on earth,
Am I supposed to ignore them,
Bury my feelings beneath the dirt…

Tell me I’m wrong,
That you’ve never loved another,
Whether it be friendship or romance,
My heart will always wonder…

I’m not saying to be unfaithful,
If committed, your love should stay strong,
But to deny your heart love’s feelings,
Please tell me, I’m not wrong.

So if I say I love you,
I mean it from my heart,
No matter where life may lead you,
In my heart we'll never part.
