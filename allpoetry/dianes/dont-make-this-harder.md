title: "Don't Make this Harder"
writer: "dianes"

Don't make this harder 
than it is...

Don't give me those looks
that get under my skin...

Your sad eyes and pouting lips
That you aim my way have missed...

The mark I was to your aim
is now nothing more then a game...

You waited to long
to change your ways...

You lost my love
now it's all in vain..."sigh"
