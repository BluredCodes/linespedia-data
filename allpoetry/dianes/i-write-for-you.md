---
title: "I Write for You"
writer: "dianes"
slug: "i-write-for-you"

I write for you,
A poem of longing,
One that speaks of my wish,
For the impossible.

To see your smile,
To hear your laugh,
To see your eyes shine,
To hold you in these arms of mine.

You may be far,
But in my heart you are near,
When I say goodbye,
Sometimes I shed a tear.

I can only hope that one day,
That we could talk away,
The clock would stop,
We could play all day.
 
You’ve opened my heart,
To feel once more,
You are my friend,
I can’t ask for more.
