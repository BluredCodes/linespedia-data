title: "Can't Let You Back in my Heart Again"

I’m so sorry,
I can’t let you back…into my heart again,

I gave you all my love,
Two beautiful children too,
All I ever wanted was a family and you.

I’m so sorry,
I can’t let you back…into my heart again,

It’s not like I never told you,
How unhappy I had become,
You chose to ignore it…now look what you’ve done,

I’m so sorry,
I can’t let you back…into my heart again,

Counseling and therapists didn’t help one bit,
No one was going to tell you what to do,
To you it was all bullshit,

I’m so sorry,
I can’t let you back…into my heart again,

So after many years of prayers and tears,
Of wanting to make it work,
It all fell on deaf ears,

I’m so sorry,
I can’t let you back…into my heart again,

It’s not like I didn’t warn you see,
Now you left my heart,
Feeling nothing for you…empty,

I’m so sorry,
I can’t let you back…into my heart again.
