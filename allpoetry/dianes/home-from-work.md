He comes in from work 
Tired from his long day
Looks for something to eat
Picks up the paper to read 
Wonders what else happened today...

Gets done eating
Stretchessssssssssssss
Yawwwwwwwwnsssssssssss
To tired to even turn the tv on...

Grabs a shower
Heads for bed
Fluffs up the pillow
Lays down his tired head...

Sleep comes quickly
He worked hard today
As he tosses and turns
Dreams start to play...

She creeps into his thoughts
Sneaks into his dreams
Crawls under his skin
What scheme does she bring...

Slowly entrancing
Teasing and pleasing
Oh how it sets
His mind reeling...

He worked hard all day
No time to play
But in his dreams
She's there to stay..."when I dream, I dream of you..."
