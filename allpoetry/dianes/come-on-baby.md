Come on baby…
It’s time to wake up,
Oh damn, you look so sweet,
All cuddled up.

How can I resist not kissing you,
Whispering in your ear,
I love you,
You know I do.

Try to pull back the blankets,
To expose you to me,
But in your sleep,
You pull them back quickly.

I know you don’t want to get up,
 I have things to do,
So I gently roll you over,
And go coochie coo.
