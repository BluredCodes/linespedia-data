I look at my life like being on a carousel,

Spinning round and round,

Faster and faster,

Going from the inner circle of horses and animals,

Trying to push away against the centrifugal forces,

Forcing my way towards the outside,

Picking a new steed to ride,

Leaning out into the wind,

R e a c h i n g…

Ssstttrrreeetttccchhhiiinnnggg…

Y e a r n i n g…

Will I ever succeed in grasping my brass ring…
