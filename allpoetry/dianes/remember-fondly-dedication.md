Remember fondly
the nights we spent
talking for hours
all the words we said...

At the time they were meant
especially those three
when I said I love you
and you said You love me...

Remember fondly
all the joyful tears we had
even though now
the tears are sad...

We were brought together
by some twist of fate
Remember fondly
don't let love turn to hate..."you know who you are"
"hate to see anyone in pain"
"remember each other fondly"
