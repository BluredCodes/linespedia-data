title: "How do you say Goodbye"

How do you say goodbye
When all you can do is cry...

She can't even see you
Don't know if she can hear you
Does she feel your touch
Gonna miss her so much...

How do you say goodbye
When all you can do is cry...

Her body lies there
Eyes closed
Breathing shallow
Waiting for God to take her in his care...

How do you say goodbye
When all you can do is cry...

Does she know we are there
Does she know we care
How long will she lie there
Questions of despair...

How do you say goodbye
When all you can do is cry..."Now it's just a waiting game of life..." "sigh"
