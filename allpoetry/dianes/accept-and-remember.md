title: "Accept and Remember"

Accept and Remember,
Our Love was once True,

Accept and Remember,
Love and Laughter we once Knew,

Accept and Remember,
How together we were Strong,

Accept and Remember,
How we use to Love all Night Long,

Accept and Remember,
All the nights we did Fight,

Accept and Remember,
The Tears and the Strife,

Accept and Remember,
Our Love is Over...it's Gone,

Accept and Remember,
It's All Over...it's Time to Move on...
