---
title: "I Choose Me"
writer: "dianes"

I
Choose
ME

For
ONCE
in my life

I will
THINK
of 
ME

Too many
times I thought of
YOU

Too many
times you made me
BLUE

I thought
only of
YOUR HAPPINESS

While I
DWELLED in
MY SADNESS

I
FINALLY
CHOOSE ME

I NEED
TO BE HAPPY TOO
CAN'T YOU SEE...
