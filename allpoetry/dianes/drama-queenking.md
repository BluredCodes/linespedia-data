She/he is a drama queen
or at least so people say
They thrive on the attention
people throw their way

When life gets to be too much
They threaten to do harm
If they say it to enough people
it works like a charm

We all have our troubles
some more serious then others
Not pokin fun at them
We are here to help each other

Friends are here to listen
to offer their support
But don't make fools out of us
Leaving us no recourse

Do you know what it feels like
to sit and wonder if
the friend that you care about
is killing themselves over a tiff

We are helpless to do anything
other than to listen
We don't wish anyone any harm
and our tears do glisten

So please don't even say in jest
that you will off yourself
then go offline and scare your friends
and put us through this test."Sorry ranting, i love my online friends and would do anything to help them. Here I can only listen or read...I care about my friends deeply...don't take my friendship for granted and even tease about suicide.....and if it is on your mind to hurt yourself, how do you think it makes your friends feel to be utterly helpless to help you?"  "I know I sound harsh and unfeeling but I am just the opposite it scares the hell outta me to know someone could be serious and I can't help them." NO RATINGS PLEASE
