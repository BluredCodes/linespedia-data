title: "I Find it Hard to Comment"

I find it hard to comment,
On so many different writes,
Each is unique,
In its own right.

I don’t have the experience,
Or the knowledge to,
Get really technical,
With my point of view.

So when I comment on your write,
I can only say what I feel,
If it makes me think, cry, or smile,
Then you know your write has touched me.

That’s what I love about poetry,
Each writer has different views,
So keep on writing my friends,
And I’ll keep commenting too.
