Am I a writer or a poet?
I have no idea,
I just know I wrote it.

Words may come,
At any given time,
Most of the time they come in rhyme.

If I don’t write them down right then,
I may never feel them again,
Excuse me, do you have a pen?

People ask me about style and technique,
I look blank and get quite meek,
I feel each writer is unique.

Sonnets, Haikus, Free Verse,
Trilogies, Epics, and Odes,
So much ground to traverse.

Think I will just take my time,
Learn a little,
Maybe sip some wine.

Just sit back and enjoy people’s writes,
Enjoy all their words,
Maybe get some insight.I learn so much from all of you,
I don't think you know how much I do,
So I raise my glass and thank all of you.
