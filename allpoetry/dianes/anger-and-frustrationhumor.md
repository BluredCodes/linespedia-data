Anger and Frustration,
Boy it pisses me off,
When I can't do something,
I feel like a blood pressure cuff!

You know how they put it on you,
Pump it till you are grey,
It expands til you think it will blow your arm off,
How does it stay?

I can feel the anger rising,
The frustration is at it's peak,
Damn, I hate when I get like this,
Frustration makes me feel weak!

Ok, I will just sit back,
Take deep breaths as I say,
Come on you #$%^)& ,
Come on I can do this right,
I ain't got all day!!!!!Got a little frustrated HeHeHe!!!!! Couldn't edit something right and couldn't figure it out.....lol
