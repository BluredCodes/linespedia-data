It’s time to tear myself away,
From the computer, and my friends,
Hours pass so quickly,
Messages to send.

I know I spend too much time,
With my friends online,
But most of you know me better,
Than people in real time.

It seems that on the computer,
I’m not as afraid to reveal,
Little bits and pieces of me,
That to others I conceal.

So I will tear myself away,
From this desk again,
But tonight I will be back,
With messages to send.
