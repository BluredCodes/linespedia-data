---
title: "Do You Know Me"
writer: "dianes"
slug: "do-you-know-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/50631-Do-You-Know-Me-by-dianes"
---

See me,
I'm that person,
standing before you.

Listen to me,
I'm that person,
Who is speaking to you.

I speak,
Do you listen,
Or do you just hear.

Take the time,
To find out who I am,
You may even like me.

You say yes,
I know you,
But, do you know ME..."Hmm"
