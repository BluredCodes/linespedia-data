---
title: "Will We"
writer: "dianes"
slug: "will-we"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/49368-Will-We-by-dianes"
---

Will we meet in the light of day,
Or in the dark of night,
Will we watch the sun rise or set,
In each other's eyes...

Will our hearts finally know the truth,
Of which we can't deny,
Or will they find disappointment,
Fantasy denied...

Will you hold me in your arms so tight,
Never let me go,
When you see tears falling down my cheeks,
Will you know its because I found the happiness I seek...

Will our feelings be true,
Become reality,
Do we both see,
What we think we see...

Have we both found,
What we've been looking for,
Will we complete each other,
Forever more...Sigh......will we?
