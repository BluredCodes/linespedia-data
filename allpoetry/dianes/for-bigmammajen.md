---
title: "For Bigmammajen"
writer: "dianes"
slug: "for-bigmammajen"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/44714-For-Bigmammajen-by-dianes"
---

I can feel the anticipation,
Within your very heart,
A trip to see someone,
Distance will no more part.

I wish you all the happiness,
That your heart can find,
Enjoy this adventure,
Bank every memory in your mind.

For these will sustain you,
When it’s time to say goodbye,
Even though you shed tears,
It’s okay to cry.

In a way I envy you,
Scared for you too,
Praying that your search bring you too,
Who or what you need for you.

Remember that your friends do care,
Their words and thoughts go with you there,
Holding you safe deep within our hearts,
Wishing you happiness as you depart.
