You all are just too much,
Can’t stand when you aren’t in touch,
With everything everyone else is doing,
My Lord, you would think I was screwing!

My writes were for you,
Just to have some fun,
To make you laugh,
To make you wonder.

Thank you for making me smile,
Hopefully my writes didn’t beguile,
You brought out the teenager in me,
Smiled so much, I cried you see.This is an explanation to those who read my writes I posted today...to those who busted me lol....(((((hugs)))))
