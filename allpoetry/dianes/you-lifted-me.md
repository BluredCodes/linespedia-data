---
title: "You Lifted Me"
writer: "dianes"
slug: "you-lifted-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

You lifted me,
When I was in dispair,
When I thought no one would care,
You lifted me...

You lifted me,
When darkness was all I could see,
When hope had forsaken me,
You lifted me...

You lifted me,
When my heart was broken,
When tears spilled down my cheeks,
You lifted me...

You lifted me,
When I could barely speak,
You gave me the words I seeked,
You lifted me...

You lifted me,
When I was so heavy,
When troubles were many,
You lifted me...

You lifted me,
When I needed a friend,
You were there till the end,
You lifted me...To all my friends who have lifted me,
Who have always shown their compassion,
Without judging,
Thank you and I hope I'm here to lift you,
When you need me...
