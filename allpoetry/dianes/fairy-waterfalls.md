title: "Fairy Waterfalls"

A lost and lonely fairy,
Flew up on a cliff,
She had so many troubles,
That she couldn't deal with.

So she sat down to think,
And she started to cry,
The first tears came slowly,
From her tiny eyes.

I guess now you know,
Of how waterfalls became,
They came from lost and lonely fairies,
Crying their hearts out in vain.
