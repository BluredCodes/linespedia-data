---
title: "It's too Good to be True"
writer: "dianes"

My friends say, 
“Don’t look now but he’s looking at you.”
All I can think is …
It’s too good to be true.

I’m at work,
He comes to say…
“After work can you come out and play?”
All I can think is…
It’s too good to be true.

So we date almost every day,
He’s so nice and sweet they say.
All I can think is…
It’s too good to be true.

He tells me he thinks I’m smart,
So beautiful,
He doesn’t want to ever part.
All I can think is…
It’s too good to be true.

Three months later we’re engaged,
A life we’ll start,
Never to part.
All I can think is…
It’s too good to be true.

One year later comes the day,
He’s my husband,
I’m his wife.
All I can think is…
It’s too good to be true.

Don’t see your friends,
You’re my wife,
You don’t need them,
I’m your life.
All I can think is…
It’s too good to be true.

Two children later,
Harsh words everyday.
All I want to do is run away.
All I can think is…
It was too good to be true.
