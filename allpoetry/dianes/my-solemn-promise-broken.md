title: "My Solemn Promise Broken"

I broke a solemn promise,
That I made to myself,
I hardened up my heart,
Put it upon a shelf.

Said I would love no more,
For it only brings me pain,
I will just live my life,
With nothing more to gain.

I was so tired of crying,
Hoping my life would change,
I tried everything,
Love, I just couldn’t attain.

One day you walked into my life,
A smile upon your face,
Your gentle thoughts and words,
My pain you soon erased.

Now a special friendship,
Of two hearts and minds,
Hearts know no distance,
Our souls have entwined.

For now I feel so much,
Emotions run out of control,
You are now such a part of me,
Mind, body and soul.

You have broken down my defenses,
Have scaled all my walls,
You have opened up my heart again,
I have heard your love call.

So now you hold my heart,
From a woman who broke her vow,
Precious in your hands,
Questioning what do I do now…
