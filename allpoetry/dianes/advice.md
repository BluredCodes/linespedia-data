Isn't it amazing the advice that we can give,
When it isn't our lives that we have to live.

It comes so easy,
The "Dear Abbey" in me,
Words flow so freely,
I hope that they don't see,
All that is inside me,
The insecurity.

How it is so easy,
To say do this or that,
Words come out so quickly,
Like the drop of a hat.

But when it comes to taking the advice I so freely give,
It's a completely different circumstance,
To live what you give.
