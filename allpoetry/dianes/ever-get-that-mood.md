Do you ever get in one of those moods,
Where all you want is sex…
That raw emotion and feeling,
Just wanting to connect.

That rips your clothes off feeling,
One you just can’t deny…
That give it to me baby,
You can’t wait neither can I.

So body slam me against the wall,
Damn those buttons,
I can feel my skin crawl….
Baby can’t you hear my need just call.

Lavish me with kisses,
Down the nape of my neck…
Emotions taking over,
My hands clinging to your back.

I feel your desire growing,
So hard against me now,
You can hear my soft moans,
My reactions to lusts call.

Our lips touching,
Tongues exploring in our rush,
Hands groping at the clothing,
I need you oh so much.

Now I feel you naked,
Our bodies flesh to flesh,
No more feelings,
Of previous loneliness.

Kisses more demanding,
Bodies are aflame,
Desire we are feeling,
Passion at its game.

Tease me baby,
Please me,
Toy with me all day,
You really know how much I love to play.

Can you feel me peaking,
Can you feel my need,
Baby, baby take me,
No excuses please…sigggggh...just one of those days....hehehe
