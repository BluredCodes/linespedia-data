Went to the lawyer to get a divorce,
When she told me how much it take,
Off the floor they had me to scrape,
That much money would take me years to make!

I went food shopping,
Was going down the aisle,
I stopped in the drug section,
For a little while.

It was there that I saw it,
Staring straight at me,
It was Preparation H,
Lots of it you see!

I read the label,
It said apply liberally,
On your pain in you ass,
It would shrink it you see!

I took all the tubes they had,
Bought as many as I could find,
Because we all know,
Your my biggest pain in my behind!

I waited till you fell asleep,
Then out came the creme,
I lathered you all down with it,
Not a drop did I waste you see!

Who needs a lawyer,
To pay all that money too,
By morning you had shriveled up,
No more pain in the ass are you!
