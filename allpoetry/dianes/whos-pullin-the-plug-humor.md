Ok, who's pullin the plug,
I'm so tired and had enough,
Everytime I have something to say,
The server just blows me away.

It doesn't seem to care,
About who or what or where,
It just goes poof you see,
Boy, that angers me!

You don't know what to think,
Did this person leave for a drink,
But then when you look back,
Everything is black!

So, who's pullin the plug,
I'm gonna hit your ugly mug,
Show yourself at last,
I wanna kick you in the ass!Freakin servers!!!!!!!!
