---
title: "A Rainbow of Friends"
writer: "dianes"
slug: "a-rainbow-of-friends"

There is nothing worse,
Than feeling helpless,
You are feeling down,
You could use a hug,
A shoulder to lean on,
A hand to wipe away the tears,
Someone to help you stand tall,
Show you it will all be okay,
Maybe not today...but someday,

We all need a friend,
Sometimes more than one,
To help us through the darkness,
That descends upon us like a dark storm cloud,
Our friends are that silver lining,
That brightness that comes out from within,
Helping chase the darkness away,
Showing us the light once again,

That there is happiness to be found,
A ray of hope in the middle of a storm,
Friends are our rainbows,
Each a different color ray,
Displaying their friendship...in different hues."let a ray of friendship brighten up your day"
