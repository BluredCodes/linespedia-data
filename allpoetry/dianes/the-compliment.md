A compliment came by my way,
I just did not know what to say,
It does not happen that often to know,
What to say that is apropo.

So I blushed and I thanked him,
Glad he had seen within,
The person that is inside,
The one I try to hide.

It is so hard to let people pass,
So afraid I might shatter like glass,
Can not handle a compliment you see,
Been hurt to may times, that is just me.

I have to learn to accept some praise,
But it was not the way that I was raised,
Never heard kind words before,
Rarely hear them anymore…

So thank you for the compliment,
I hope that you know you put a dent,
In my fortress wall today,
One more brick just fell away.You never know what a kind word or smile can do to make someone's day...Thank you for making mine.
