Oh, how cute,
You named your little lizard,
Better gecko,
Than woody,
Or oscar,
Or penis...

But teasing us ladies,
Is naughty you see,
Especially...
Telling us it's hard bein green!

The most outrageous thing,
You have done...
Is give us the image,
Of your forked tongue!

But the last was the killer,
To tell us ladies this,
That you can grow your tail,
Any length you wish!

So be careful my gecko,
You just never know who,
Will pick you up,
And steal off with you!"just for fun"
*smiles*
