---
title: "Is it?"
writer: "dianes"
slug: "is-it"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/34719-Is-it--by-dianes"
---

Is it you that I long for in the middle of the night,
That I long to hold in my arms so tight,

Is it your eyes I seek when the dawn starts to peek,
To give me strength when I am so weak,

Is it your hand that should be clapsed in mine,
When passion it's in love divine,

Is it your hair that my brush passes through,
When I look in my mirror and see an image of you,

Is it your words that whisper in my ear,
As the wind rushes passed me in the cool night air,

Is it your heart that beats with mine,
As if it's part of love's design,

Is it you that I'm meant to spend my life with,
Maybe someday I'll know the answer to this.
