---
title: "Do you Remember"
writer: "dianes"
slug: "do-you-remember"
source: "AllPoetry"

Do you remember…
When you first saw me,
As I remember seeing you,

Do you remember…
Our first kiss,
So sweet and tender too,

Do you remember…
The first time,
When you vowed your love so true,

Do you remember…
Our wedding day,
When we said I do…

Do you remember…
Having our first child,
Then adding another few,

Do you remember…
How we used to talk,
Love and laugh the whole night through,

Do you remember,
Holding each other through the night,
So grateful you were mine,

How I wish you would remember,
It’s so sad and so true,
I still remember, why don’t you…
