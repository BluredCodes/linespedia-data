I write straight from my heart,

Sometimes I wish it wasn’t true,

For I reveal too much of myself,

To all the world, and you.


I know that this isn’t right,

I can’t help it though, you see,

It’s just the way I write,

Just me being me.


Every reader interprets what they want,

Be it real or fantasy,

But I’m the only one who knows,

How those words were meant to be.
