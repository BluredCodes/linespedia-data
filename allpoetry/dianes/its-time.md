---
title: "It's Time"
writer: "dianes"
slug: "its-time"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

It's time
to break free
from the shackles
that bind my
heart...

To stand
on my
own...

Walk
with my
face into the 
wind...

Even if it
takes
my breath
away...

Better it be
fresh air
I breathe
then
stagnant...

Still waters
run deep
as does the
pain...

No one
left to
blame...

Prisoners
of one 
heart...

Now must
part
Learn to 
brave the
storms
of life
alone...
