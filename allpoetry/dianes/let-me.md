---
title: "Let Me"
writer: "dianes"
slug: "let-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/50620-Let-Me-by-dianes"
---

Let me be your friend,
The one you have spent half your life with,
Me, the one who knows you better than anyone.

Let me remember the happiness we shared,
All our years together weren't all bad,
Try to remember the good times.

Let me continue to be your friend,
Still be part of your life,
Just in a different capacity.

Let me go,
No longer to be your wife,
But the friend that I have always been.
