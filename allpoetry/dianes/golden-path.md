writer: "dianes"

Let me walk down the golden path
But let it be not alone
Let me find someone to share the journey with
Maybe someday we could build a home

Let us share the wonders of this adventure
Weather all the storms
Let us smile in the face of fear
Be there to dry each other's tears

Let us smile in the warmth of the sun
Heating up our desires
Let us hold each other tight in the dark
When whispers of insecurities transpire

Let us skip down this golden path
Creating happy memories
But as we get older we will walk
wanting to hold onto these

For memories are golden
We treasure them so much
Money cannot buy them
But you can grow them
When two hearts touch....
