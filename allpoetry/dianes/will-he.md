---
title: "Will He"
writer: "dianes"
slug: "will-he"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/48250-Will-He-by-dianes"
---

Will he be there to hold me,
In the dark of night,
Or will he slither off,
Before the morning light...

Will he be there to share,
All of my happiness,
Or will he run off,
When dark clouds trespass...

Will he know when to hold me,
And when to leave me alone,
If he's going to be late,
Will he pick up the phone...

Will love last a moment,
Or will it last for life,
Will he always treasure me,
Pleasure me, hold onto me tight...

The begining is easy,
The middle gets hard,
In the end I'm afraid,
A heart gets scarred...

So will he be there,
I guess I don't know,
We will have to wait,
To see if love grows..."tis just a poem"
