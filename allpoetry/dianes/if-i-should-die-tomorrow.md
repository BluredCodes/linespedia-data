If I should die tomorrow,
and never see you again,
my love,
do not despair,
for you shall see me again,

whether it be two buds on a flower,
blossoming together, 

listen for my sighs of love for you,
as the wind blows in the trees,

even the twinkling of the stars,
reminding you of the look in my eyes,
when you held me tight,

or the rays of the sun,
as it warms your skin,
as my touch once had,

when you hear childrens laughter,
let it remind you,
of how young we felt together,

as you see fireworks,
let it remind you,
of our nights,

when the sun rises,
let it remind you,
of the warmth that we shared,

when it sets,
let it remind you,
that you are one day closer,
to being with me in eternity.Written March 12th, 2002 ©  on Mar 11 2002 03:21 PM PST   love
