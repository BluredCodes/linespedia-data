---
title: "It's Over It's Done"
writer: "dianes"
slug: "its-over-its-done"

It's over,
It's done.
I give up,
You won.

Our love use to be worth fighting for,
Now please pack your bags,
Head for the door.

It's over,
It's done,
I can't fight no more,
You won.

I tried to stay,
Hoped our troubles would go away.
You didn't try,
You lied,
Too many nights I've cried.

It's over,
It's done.
Can't cry no more.
You won.

Can't love someone,
Who can't love me.
Don't say you do,
You know it's not true.

It's over,
It's done.
You lost my love.
You won.

It's over....
It's done....
