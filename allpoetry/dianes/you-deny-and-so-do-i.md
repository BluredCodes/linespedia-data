---
title: "You Deny and so Do I"
writer: "dianes"
slug: "you-deny-and-so-do-i"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/49019-You-Deny-and-so-Do-I-by-dianes"
---

You deny,
Just can't face,
It's time to say,
Goodbye...

I deny,
Too scared to face,
The future,
To look it in the,
Eyes...

Deny, deny,
It's easier,
to lie...

To face the truth,
Would only enforce,
Our love has
died...
