title: "Calling from Across the Miles"

Calling from across the miles,
Your voice in my ear,
Words of comfort to my heart,
A smile replaces tears.

I know that you are not psychic,
How could you have known,
But yet you picked just the right time,
To pick up the telephone.

It's like you knew I needed you,
Right then and there,
Your words only solidified,
The fact that I knew you cared.

You calmed my fears,
You dried my tears,
Though you are far,
Your voice brought you near.

Thank you for being there,
When I needed you most..."Isn't it amazing how people close to you know when to call just the right time?"
