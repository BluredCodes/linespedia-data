That smile,
Yes...
The one that goes from ear to ear,
Blushing...
Looking under your eyelashes,
Does he see...

That smirk,
Yes...
The hand goes up to his chin,
Hiding the grin...
Does she see...

The courtship has begun,
As from the begining of time it was done,
A smile, a wink...
A smirk, a grin,
Hiding our hearts, behind the skin...

New love,
No matter what the age,
Makes us feel young...
Innocent and shy,
Sweaty palms and racing hearts...
This is how love starts."inspired by my babies"
