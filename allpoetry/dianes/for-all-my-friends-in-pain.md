---
title: "For All My Friends in Pain"
writer: "dianes"
slug: "for-all-my-friends-in-pain"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/46446-For-All-My-Friends-in-Pain-by-dianes"

I feel your,
PAIN,
As you sit in the dark,
ALONE,
I can feel your heart,
YEARNING,
I can,
RELATE,
I have been in your,
SHOES,
I know that,
FEELING,
Of utter,
DISMAY,
Of,
DISAPPOINTMENT,
Of,
DESPAIR,
You know people,
CARE,
It doesn’t,
HELP,
Even though you,
KNOW,
They mean,
WELL,
For,
ONCE,
You were going to do,
SOMETHING,
For,
YOU,
But unfortunately,
CIRCUMSTANCES,
Had their own way of,
ALTERING,
Your plans,
SWEET WORDS,
Don’t take the,
PAIN,
Away,
But,
HOPEFULLY,
Lets you know,
YOU
are
NOT 
ALONE
and 
YOU 
are 
LOVED...So many of us each with our own trials and tribulations, 
Each knowing that someone else has it worse then us,
But your own pain is just so overwhelming,
Wishing I had a magic wand for each of us,
To make it all go away......
(Hugsssssssss) "Each of you know who you are"
