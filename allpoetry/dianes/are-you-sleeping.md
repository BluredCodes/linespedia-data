title: "Are You Sleeping"

Are you sleeping,
Resting in your bed,
What thoughts I wonder,
Run through your head.

Do you dream of places,
You’ve yet to see,
Or dream of people,
Maybe a fantasy.

I can picture you,
All snug in your bed,
Hair all askew,
Sleeping like the dead.

No noise wakes you up,
As you sleep so deep,
A smile plays on your face,
What secrets do you keep.

Wish I could see you,
Sleeping like this,
Then I would wake you up,
With one sweet little kiss.
