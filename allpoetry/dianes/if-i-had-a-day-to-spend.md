If I had a day all to myself,
That no one else would know,
I’d pick you to share it with,
So our friendship could grow.

We would take walks together,
From the mountains to the shore,
Watch the sun rise and set,
Getting to know each other would be the best.

No clocks or work to bother us,
Just each other to cuddle up,
How timing is my enemy,
When I want your company.

So if I had this day to spend,
I want you to know,
It would be with you,
My friend.Dan,
No matter what does or doesn't happen,
You, my friend, have touched my soul.
