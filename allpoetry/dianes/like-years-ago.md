---
title: "Like Years Ago"

He was out west,
She was east,
Face to face,
Yet to meet.

Letters criss crossed,
Across the miles,
Shortening the distance,
Bringing with them many smiles.

Like the days of long ago,
Before airplanes and telephones,
Friendship was written,
Lovers had grown.

No need for masks,
To hide behind,
As people say,
Love is blind.

Words written,
By loving hearts,
Knowing deep inside,
True love imparts.

Behind the words,
Resides hearts and souls,
In letters sent,
Like years ago..."xxoo"
