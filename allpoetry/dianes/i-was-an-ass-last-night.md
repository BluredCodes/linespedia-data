title: "I Was an Ass Last Night"

I was such an ass last night,
Wasn't looking for a fight,
Just don't know what got into me,
Maybe just afraid you would see,
How much you are starting to mean to me.

You have no reason to feel bad,
I feel so stupid that I made you feel sad,
Maybe it was a small part of jealousy,
I wanted to have you just with me.

I felt so bad,
That you blamed yourself,
You're my friend,
No Doctor or Therapist certficate on your shelf.

Next time I'll learn when I feel this way,
To go to bed,
To stay away,
I was so unfair to make you feel that way....Ever just have a mood descend on you out of the blue.....sorry my friend you didn't deserve for me to bring you down and go blame yourself...forgive me....
