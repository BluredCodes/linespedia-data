Did it make you feel a BIG MAN,
When you RAMMED upon my door,
Did it make you feel so POWERFUL,
When you SLAMMED me to the floor,

Did you think your CUTTING WORDS meant anything,
When you SPAT them in my face,
Or how bout when you BEAT me,
It's only you, YOU do DISGRACE,

You may have the POWER,
PHYSICALLY, that is,
To BREAK my BONES and BODY,
But I have the WILL to LIVE,

It's a SHAME that you can't handle,
All YOUR INSECURITIES,
So you TAKE IT OUT ON WOMEN,
You take it out ON ME,

If you would have taken your POWER,
And TURNED it INSIDE instead of out,
WORKED on our RELATIONSHIP,
Instead of BEATING ME,
Then STORMING OUT,
MAYBE, then a BIG MAN you would be,
But sadly your a PITIFUL ONE,
and NO SYMPATHY will you get from me,

So, did it make you feel like a BIG MAN,
Did you ENJOY your POWER TRIP,
Did you enjoy seeing MY BLOOD as it did drip,

Well MR BIG MAN,
You may be STRONG IN POWER,
But YOU CAN'T BREAK MY WILL,
I'M YOUR'S TO BEAT NO LONGER..."written for a friend, to know that she's not alone, wishing I could give her the power to stand on her own.......sighhh
I feel your pain, let your friends help make you strong.....
