---
title: "Your Words"
writer: "dianes"
slug: "your-words"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Your words touch not only my heart,
But the core of my soul,
So gently penned with such emotion,
Never knowing how deep you words seep,
Into my thoughts and my inner being.

Taking me places that I have never been,
Making me feel like I’ve been there before,
Opening doors to places I’d like to go,
Closing the doors to the places,
That should have been closed long ago.

Write on,
Let me feel your words once again,
Take me to those places,
Open the door to my heart,
For now you hold not only the pen, but the key.
