Oh my, did he just wink,
I thought it was just a blink,
Don’t you just love his style,
Did you see that little smile…

Hey wow, she’s looking at me,
I hope she didn’t see,
That I was looking at her,
She's driving me crazy…

Yes, it starts out with a look,
You hope you haven’t mistook,
A casual glance your way,
What should you do or say…

You stutter and you stammer,
Wished you read that article in Glamour,
Hope you look okay,
He's still looking they say...

He starts to walk your way,
You start to fidget but stay,
Introductions do proceed,
You feel weak in your knees…

Flirting has begun…
Both wonder is this the one,
Time is set for a date,
Now make sure you’re not late...

How your heart does pound,
Your feet can't find the ground,
Maybe he/she is the one,
Flirting is such fun...
