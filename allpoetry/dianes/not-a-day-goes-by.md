title: "Not a Day Goes By"
writer: "dianes"

Not does a day go by without you on my mind,
Nor a night pass by without you in my heart,
Even though distance separates us,
Within my soul you are a part.

I have discovered things about you,
That you don’t realize,
Things that pull me towards you,
Things I can’t deny.

Your simple sharing,
Of your most private thoughts,
Things that you are sorry for,
Things that your are not.

You passion for life is evident,
Upon your written page,
And your words will live on,
With the passing of each age.

I read all your tender words,
About your wants and desires,
How even the simplest things,
Set my heart on fire.

I listen to each word you say,
Not wanting to miss a thing,
Making me want so much,
To share with you everything.

You want the simplest little things,
That would make you so happy,
A walk in the park to hear birds sing,
To my heart a melody you bring.

It’s not treasures or fame you’re looking for,
And it’s not those that I want either,
It’s being with the one you love,
To spend our lives together.

So if I am the one or not,
That you are looking for,
Time will only tell,
You have opened my hearts door."luv yah"
