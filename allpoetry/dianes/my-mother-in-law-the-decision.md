title: "My Mother-in-Law the Decision"

I see you lying there,
so fragile,

Your breathing,
so labored,

At the mercy of machines,
to let you live,

So tiny in stature,
but so bold in life,

No one would ever question you,
you had your own answers,

Now as you lie there,
we are the ones left with the decisions,

How do we know,
which is what you would choose..."she is on life support, we don't know yet which way things will go, but have been asked to make a decision"
