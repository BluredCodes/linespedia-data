title: "Simply Smile"
writer: "dianes"

I always believed that everyone has a purpose in life.
Whether it is a talent or a skill,
One that they should contribute through his or her own will.

Life has so much to offer.
It can be so hard and cruel.
People touch other people’s lives without even knowing it everyday.
It can be through their work, or a simple conversation. 

I believe all it takes is a smile.
If I know I made one person smile,
No matter if they are friend or foe,
Family or strangers,
Then I did my good deed for the day.

How hard is it for you not to smile back when someone smiles at you.
It makes you feel good if only for a moment in time.
It erases those nasty thoughts,
Your fears,
And all the humdrum of the day.

So when you pass someone today give him or her your gift.
Such a simple thing doesn’t cost you anything,
And can mean so much to them.
Smile and the whole world will smile with you,
And you feel better just for doing so.
