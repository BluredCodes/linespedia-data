Souls yearning,
Reaching out,
Searching…

A half,
Looking for its whole,
Seeking…

Looking to find,
One heart,
Soul and mind…

That thinks,
Beats and feels,
Like theirs in kind...

Is our soul real,
Do we hide,
Or do we reveal…

The beauty that’s inside,
Or do we forever hide,
Our hearts, our souls, our minds…
