title: "Just Another Day at Work"
writer: "dianes"

You assume,
Because you don’t know me,

You intimidate,
Because you have power over me,

You talk down to me,
To make me be submissive,

You attack me,
To make me defensive,

You praise me,
Because you have ulterior motives,

You backstab me,
To make yourself look good,

You step on me,
So you can get higher up,

You say you motivate me,
When you hold me back,

You pretend to be my friend and care,
When you're only looking for information,

GET REAL!!!
