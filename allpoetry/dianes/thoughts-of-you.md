---
title: "Thoughts of You"
writer: "dianes"
slug: "thoughts-of-you"

My eyes have never met yours,
But I've looked into your soul,

My arms have never held you,
But my heart you now hold,

My lips have never touched yours,
But each night we kiss goodbye,

My body has never slept with you,
But in my mind I sigh,

My tears your touch has never wiped away,
But your words have brought me smiles,

My feelings of love for you have spread,
But across those many miles,

My lonliness is still here,
But different now you see,

My hopes and dreams now focus on,
Of what someday we might be..."xxoo"
