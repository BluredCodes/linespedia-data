title: "You Bring the Sunshine"
writer: "dianes"

You bring the sunshine
wherever you go
I feel the warmth
of your smile
as mine on my face grows

When you talk
the heat is intense
I never feel 
like a moment wasted is spent

I like to hear
how you speak
what you think
in your eyes I drink

Your rays of compassion
and your words of love
touch my soul
light down from above

I want to feel your heat
as you lie next to me
two bodies radiating
close to ecstacy

And as the sun sets
slowly in the sky
The warmth you give to me
keeps me satisfied...."hmm"
