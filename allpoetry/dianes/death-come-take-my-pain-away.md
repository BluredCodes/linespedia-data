Death...
Come take my pain away,
I can’t handle it anymore,
I’m tired of teetering on the brink,
Of salvations iron door.

I have tried to do all the right things,
All the rules I tried to obey,
But it didn’t seem to matter,
Death...
Come take my pain away.

I’m so tired and so weak,
I can barely even speak,
It doesn’t matter what I say,
Death...
Come take my pain away.

Salvations glory I do seek,
Lord, take me into your flock of sheep,
Oh, Dear Lord hear what I say,
Death...
Come take my pain away.
