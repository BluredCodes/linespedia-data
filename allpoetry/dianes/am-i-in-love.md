---
title: "Am I IN LOVE?"
writer: "dianes"

Am I...
IN LOVE,
Or am I just in love with the idea of being...
IN LOVE?
Who doesn’t want to be...
IN LOVE?

That feeling of being:
Wanted,
Desired,
Needed,
Heart pounding,
Palms sweaty,
Passion aflame,
The response of your body when he calls your name.

You can love lots of people,
But to be...
IN LOVE,
With someone oh how grand.
People can tell you know.
They see your eyes light up when his name is spoken.
They hear the lilt in your voice like your going to burst into song.

Your world revolves round him.
He’s all you can think of, 
Speak of,
Dream of.

Hmm,
Maybe I am...
IN LOVE,
After all,
Forget I asked...
