title: "I Cry a Solitary Tear"

I sit here alone,
Missing you,
I cry a solitary tear,
Wishing you were here.

How many more lonely days and nights,
Shall we spend apart,
Even though our hearts…
Are always together.

My heart has hurt before,
Now it’s my soul that yearns,
Giving me reason for concern,
Is it you, who is meant to be the one…

A solitary tear,
Falls once again, my dear,
My heart telling my soul,
What it already knows…
