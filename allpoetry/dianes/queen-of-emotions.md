I am the "Queen of Emotions",
My heart is worn on my sleeve,
For all the world to see,
But if you look close enough,
You might get to know the real me.
I try to hide my feelings,
But the blood pours down my arm,
My heart continues beating,
No cause for alarm.

I wear the crown upon my head,
Invisible to your eye,
People tell me to control myself,
They don't know how I try.

For I am the "Queen of Emotions",
My heart bares more then you could see,
For every tear that you cry,
