title: "She Sleeps with Him (Adult)"
writer: "dianes"

Slowly she lifts the sheets
Ever so gently
Lifting her body up on to the bed
Not wanting to disturb his slumber
But needing to feel
The warmth of him against her
And his manliness
Her silken legs entwine with his
As her arm drapes over his chest
She nestles close to him
Taking in his scent
Making her yearn for his touch
She traces the lines of his body
With a featherlike touch
He moves and smiles in his sleep
His arms reach out to capture her
Passion has aroused him
Sadly his arms catch only air
For she was just a dream..."or was she?"
