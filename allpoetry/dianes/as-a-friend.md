Wish I could help you,
I know you are in need,
Whether it be a shoulder,
Or a cry that you just need to heed.

Everyone has problems,
Sometimes they overwhelm,
We all get times that we wish,
We could move onto the next realm.

But as a friend,
I say to you,
I’m here to stay,
As I was yesterday, tomorrow and today."hugs to my online son, it be okay"
