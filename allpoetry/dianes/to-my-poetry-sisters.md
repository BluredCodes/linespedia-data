---
title: "To My Poetry Sisters"
writer: "dianes"
slug: "to-my-poetry-sisters"
source: "AllPoetry"

I have some sisters in poetry,
Love them so much,
They are all so dear to me,
Each one knows how to make me smile,
They can chase aways my tears,
From across the miles,
They always know how to read,
In between the lines,
Give me hugs when I need them,
And also some frowns,
How I love each one so dear,
They are each unique,
They calm my fears,
Who knows if they are young or old,
Fat or thin,
Near or close,
All I know is I love each one,
So special is each,
Sisters in poetry everyone."thank you all my poetry sisters, I luv each of you in my own way"
