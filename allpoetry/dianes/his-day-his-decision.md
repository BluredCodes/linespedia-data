---
title: "His Day, His Decision"

It was his day
His birthday
A day to celebrate
A birth

The phone rings
Hospital and Doctors
We're sorry
The news is bad

You need to make a decision
The one you love
Is gone
Just a body left lying there

Voice quivering
Knowing she is gone
He makes the decision
Let her body go

She left that body
Days ago
Never to return to me
My love she took with her

Happy Birthday 
To me
I loved her
Can't you see..."Father-in-law had to make the decision to take his wife off of life support on all days his birthday. My heart breaks for him, even though he isn't an emotional person, you can see the pain in his eyes, ripping apart his soul..."
