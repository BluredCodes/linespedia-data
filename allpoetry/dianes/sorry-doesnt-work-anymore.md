title: "Sorry Doesn't  Work Anymore"

Sorry doesn’t work anymore,
My heart has shut its door,
You’ve said those words too many times,
What else have you got in store…

I’ve given you so many chances,
To prove that things would change,
You think by just saying you’re sorry,
There will be no one to blame…

I was always taught that when you say you’re sorry,
It means you try not to do that again,
But repetition follows you,
I’m sorry never ends…

So don’t bother saying you’re sorry,
It doesn’t affect me anymore,
I know your heart doesn’t mean it,
I’m sorry…I’ve shut my hearts door.
