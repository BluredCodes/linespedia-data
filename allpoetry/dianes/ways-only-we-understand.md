title: "Ways Only We Understand"

I wish I could reach out and touch you,
Not only with my words but my hands,
You know that you hold my heart,
In a way only we can understand.

The peace that I feel inside me,
When we talk together are bliss,
I can’t hide my feelings,
How I wish to share your sweet kiss.

We talk about so many things,
We both have nothing to hide,
There are so many feelings,
Down deep inside.

I long to pull you through the phone,
To sit with you alone,
Hate knowing you are so far way,
The phone is our only home.

When I hear that phone ringing,
It sings straight to my heart,
I only hope that one day,
We will never part.

You hello brings a smile to my face,
I can feel your smile too,
Know that your wish is mine,
My love for you is true.
