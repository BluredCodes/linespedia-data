London is my favorite place On the ice I love to raceNo one messes with the moose Don't you know we'll let him loose On the ice we are the best Never loosing to the rest Knowing that the trophies oursNo ones gonna put us down In the arena we must goGlistening on the pure white snowHolding up our head real high To the goal that puck must flyStand in way and your gonna die'Knights'I think this style is called Cross stick?unless you know otherwise?
