Today reach out,Far and beyond your touchLook for the beautyIn one solitary day;For a moment's beauty,Last a life's span!Smile and rejoiceWalk hand and hand,For tomorrow may comeAnd today's beauty will havePassed you by again,Far and beyond your touch!Eva Marie Ann Dunlap (c) 2001All Rights Reserved
