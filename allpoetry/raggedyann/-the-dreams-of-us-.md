---
title: "' the dreams of us '"
writer: "RaggedyAnn"

I feel tired my love,
I fear God is calling me from above.
I've tried to hold on so long,
But this pain grows stronger every day.
Lord knows I continue to pray.
I don't want to have to leave you my love,
But you know one day I must
And all that will be left for you is

The dreams of us!

Good morning cups of coffee
Shared by you and me.
Watching our squirrel eating
His breakfast too,
Feeding the wild birds that
Flock to our feeders.
Taking the dogs for a walk round the lilacs.
Grocery shopping with me
Pushing the cart for you.
Playing on the Internet together.
Watching a movie and eating popcorn,
Good morning kisses and
Me starting your car,
Making our dreams and plans.
I feel tired my love,
God is calling me from above
Please remember our love.

And the dreams of us!

What it felt like when we had 
Our first kiss
The beat of our hearts making love
For the first time
For I fear my time with you is running short.
This pain it grows stronger
Everyday.
Lord knows I continue to pray.
I just wanted to say,
That time cannot keep us apart.
You and I will be together again
In God's arms
We'll meet again my love.
I promise I'll be there waiting for you.
I won't forget you my love.
I will continue to live through you

And the dreams of us!
