GHOST AND GOBLINSAND TREE'S THAT TALKROUND THE ALLEYA CHOPPING BLOCK.WITCHES BREWOF RAT-TAIL STEW.AND SPIDERS THAT CRAWLALL OVER YOU!A PINCH OF THISAND A PINCH OF THAT.AND OVER THEREA BIG BLACK CAT.PUMPKINS GLOWING YELLOWWITH CANDLE LIGHT.WHILE EERIE THINGSFLY PAST YOU AT NIGHT.BATS, BATS ARE IN YOUR HAIR.THE NIGHTS PITCH BLACKOH, WHAT A SCARE!GHOST AND GOBLINS ARE EVERYWHERE.WHILE TRICK OR TREATERSKNOCK FROM HOUSE TO HOUSE.I'LL STAY IN MINE AS QUIET AS A MOUSE.HIDING UNTIL THE MORNINGSUN DOES SHINE UNTIL THIS HOLLOW EVE IS PAST IT'S BEWITCHING TIME!Eva Marie Ann Dunlap (c) 2001All Rights Reserved
