-












"the statistical mode of reality-adjusted social thinking"
is a computer terminal at which we (all) sit and clickety clack 
input, input, without seeing out...
like those towering anonymous  skyscrapers in  lively,
   but tick tock cities,
those tinted  black windowed buildings that take 
   and give nothing back
but every 9-to-5 Clack spilling out into the grimey streets spitting time cards and puking compromise
choking in smog, disposable restaurants.
is a "useful" "productive" member, team player"
   yea..nay sayer .,.decide us here with your half vote
leave me here on my last note.
















---a life in finances. --a life of an american. --a life i don't want, but must gradually accept. --a life i must be weary of.
