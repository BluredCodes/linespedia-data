title: "the gift of gab"
writer: "giggling nihilist"

.



you.
nosey parker snoop [ ! ]
feeding. on your gobble talk, 
gossipy goop.

meddling  busybody 
seeping rumor by the loyal kibitzer
you scandalous tale-bearing chatterbox [ ! ] 
a blabber mouth gabber
windbag magpie
of idle jabber.

gurgle and coo 
a fictitious murmur
to your wanting crew,
your panting and eager few.

for, in truth you won't get far,
--you've forgotten to tie your shoe.


. . ... . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . 




.this pome is to express 
sort of a Dr. Seuss disdain 
for those prattling rumorists 
trying to put misinformation in a pretty frame.
