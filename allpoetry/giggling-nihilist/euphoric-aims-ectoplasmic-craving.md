"EUPHORIC AIMS, ECTOPLASMIC CRAVING"


his voice, a seizure, more soothing than the falling rain.
those somber lip quiver
and accidental wrinkled eye folded closed 
to screams,
your own screams.
   if thou art to know thy Self, then know thine grave.

euphoric aims 
and the means, ersatz.
 spiraling through viscid black  
feed induced,
ectoplasmic craving.
