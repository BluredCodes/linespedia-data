i go to bed weeping defeated at night.
yeh.  the general consensus. 
cash en yer subordinance for. 
a.
hair.
cut.
// that's about how things go these days //
	my studies are lacking
     had i better get packing?
& what's my options.
   s.t.r.a.i.n.ed.  a.d.o.p.t.i.on.?
here's   my   scheme. 
  nothin   but   dreams.
      a slapstick pie?
         or a withering cry?
t/h/e g/o/l/d/e/n r/u/l/e ?
  or   a    s/e/l/f r/i/g/h/t/e/o/u/s f/o/o/l ?
so what's it going to be then, eh?
   where we goin' with this?
ee. nog. en. go. en.
   leap. frog. en. no. gin.
the old lady watch is tickin' its tune.
i feign to regret. it seems it will be soon.
    ime lame to forget. they think me a loon. 
ime sane to percept. it seems it will be soon.
it seems it will be soon.
it seems it will be soon.





=-

well. now i'm moving. to el pueble de la nuestra reina de porincuncula de LOS ANGELES.
er. sumtin' like dat.
yo.
