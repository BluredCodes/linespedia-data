//second pome erwot in a day. well, actually it was the first//

	it's cold
it's so cold. . . . .
	the sky is smoothed over bread
like buttery purple
	are the clouds as the sun sets

..i.t.s..f.r.i.g.i.d. . .. ..
	my scars show up bright fuchsia like 
& match my dangling gypsy jewels
	then i gaze into my old lady watch,
-- they are keeping time, with the tune of tick - tock, 
	the     old     ladies     in    the    clock
. . .  . . . . .   . . . . ..    . . . .  . . .    . . . .    . . .    .  .







.comment: inspiration for a pome can come from just    
       being abandoned, sittin' on tha corner, eatin' a sanwich on a cold day.
