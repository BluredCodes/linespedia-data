-







the barren cemetery branches
  as the entombed  they shade,
brittle veins 
     no longer enlivened
but with a place in this realm
  as a reminder of the next
             
sprawling across the sky,
   hovering in ominous pattern
before the oranged heavens
   throwing their maddening shadows
all over the softened ground

















-the cemetery near mein humble abode was one of mein favorite places to spend time...
