and so there was a duel, 
   "artists(!),   define your light source"
on the count of three,  (1-2-3)
    draw!

and they were off(!)
    in a frenzy, scratching super realism

o, what a gruesome duel it was!
      --pencils with paper cuts 
bleeding out the dramatic scene...



that is
       to be continued...
   





\yeh.. another half-clack.
but i thought it was too fun
 to keep to meinself.
