"girlish fancy"

her straps snag upon
the jaggedly flexible
stone slabs 
of a cleaving back
and forth
motion.
as she lie lucid,
twinkle-toes sleep.
 
mumbling softly 
as sliding arms encircling waist
curious lips, lend a taste.

she was this one.
she was another.

speak to me in that tender dialect,
as your idiom-specks-echoes dilate,
a lovE en broken glass jars
dripping like bittersweet.


  

}{...mumbling softly...
