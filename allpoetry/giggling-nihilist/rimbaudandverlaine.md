-


the poets of the day 
stabbing silently, maniacally, 
somber at each others' hands
screaming,
 "the only thing unbearable is that nothing is unbearable,"
over and over,
such bitter honesty.


                    
                       that's not true    

      "I haven't set fire to her since Thursday."

"It's pathetic.
Your acts of violence are always curiously disgusting."

      "What do you mean?"

"They're not clean.
 You're always in some sort of a drunken stupor...
and you start apologizing...and groveling."

      "I don't like hurting people."

"Then don't. 
But if you do, then do it coolly; don't insult your victims by feeling sorry for them."
