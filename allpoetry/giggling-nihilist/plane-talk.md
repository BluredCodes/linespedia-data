we sat silent.
      listening to the muffled sounds of  carry-on  thud clap
crack
   non chalant   side glance of curious  clatter smack
   a word(!).... oh, a cough  &   achoo... 
click click seatbelt 

///everything's a floatation device these days

   at least you're not a clickety clack... laptop crack crack floppy 
    --you've got hair, o my nameless frequent flyer!
 thud badumbadum.... 

  "please keep your tray tables snap and seatbelts fast click."

or so the kippety  Cap(t)  frizzy radioed 
    and mein dainty oculus eye speck gazed upon the strange tick tock 
of  "scheduled departure" automatons 
in "ring" "line up" "take a number" mannerism
   
  out of the uniform window of
  "next time you decide to blast through the air in a pressurized tin can, we hope you think of us"
  
    --that was the ol' kippety again 
and so we smelched a bit at that.
    and mein oculus caught yours.

hello. 
            uh   hi.

- - -- the tongue molds sound and we push thought to thought to thought 
      and create conversation- - - --

so, ah, you're a 7th day adventist?  --me?
    oh, i'm an inventist....
but i have sipped of the soup of reconciliation, or some other book of the book
	that i was reading in the ...

"dentist office?"    

              yeh yeh, how did you know? 

"e.s.p.., i'm becoming a dentist, my young inventist"

          that's neat.

--or so i  mused  and  we shared ourselves and ourslefs with
laughs of  shhh... ch..ch...
       and sha... cha... cha..  huh uh huh ... yeh yeh... hee.

silly giggulous altitude i'll never see you again merrimentatious 

 eeeerr  turbulence badumbadum..

"if you look to your left, you'll see ... " vast crack smash carved in the dirt
or maybe just the grand canyon=....-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=----=-=---=-=

  	we are unified in our wonder       ooooh     aaaah    lookie   ....

uh huh.    time wanes invariabletatiously ....

   with  groan of achy ass bones... ears  pop pop   ouch!
	crack elbows  ...pop snap..
  "clickclickclicketyclick"  --or so your knuckles uttered
  the body prepares to become rigid, the mind has become attached.. you are safe, i do not want to leave you
the tin can sprouts legs to put it's feet on the ground... restless shifting muffle badumbadum
     eeeeerk     eeeerk   eeerk   eerk    erk   er....   

  and so it's quiet wait in line,  walk up ramps,  
 rigidbody  pressed against the air of the unknown...
 tired belly..& dizzy gray matter...
stale mouth murmuring goodbyes..  
warm eyes gazing gentle compassion revealed in one last ocular glance
  and we are thrust back into the tick tock abyss 

              "thank you for flying"
