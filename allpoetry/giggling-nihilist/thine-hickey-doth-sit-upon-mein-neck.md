-







thine  hickey doth sit upon mein neck,

mein hickey doth grace thee tambien

(and ache)

sits and aches it does,
sits and fakes it does,

fakes our intimacy

    we    are    not     involved

nor are we close

    you were just there,

just needed a dose.








---hickeys, 1$ a pop.
