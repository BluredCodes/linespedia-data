-















  tired swelling brain,
there are too many words,
  [semantic universe]













-i sat skit fiddle in my confusion and dirty socks. i wrote this little because of the world of made up notions we live in, a semantic universe... everything classified, made out of words.  and this is all we have.. our useful notions and acquired habits.
