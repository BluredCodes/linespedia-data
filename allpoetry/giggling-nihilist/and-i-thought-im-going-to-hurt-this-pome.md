-





and i thought  "i'm going to hurt this pome"

  ime cutting these words in to little creamcakes
ime hashing spleen for my mistakes
   i'll tie words up in rigid shoelace cathedrals
they'll scream bells and  needles 
  as i pick and prodd connotational  distress
as i mick and mod in notational excess
   the story will unwind
\where there's a rhythm, there's a rhyme
     where there's time for tea,
   .....there's.....time.. for you and me..........................................






-i wrote a pome about cutting up the pome to make the pome "heavy dream" .. and this, dears, is it..
  -peas and carrots tell me what you think.
