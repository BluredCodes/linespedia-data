yeh, it was said expectingly
   while not expecting a thing
   and he said, 'fear of dreaming'
   so he used to, but wasn't used to
       he was used too... 

...to clean the crooked clocks on the walls.
   which wouldn't hold up in tomorrow's storm 
   from by the way of the barn in the painting
   the eerie-eyed one came, 
. . . . m u m b le d . . . 
  
   "mmm, yessss, the calm before the storm."

 mother had been painting.
& pretty soon i was laughing
  and eating carambas
pretty soon i was languishing 
 and running the numbers
. . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . 
yes,  yes,  see:::
that lovely red mark on your neck
and that wonderful birth mark
on your side, the left, and back
oh, and of course those cute
freckles and specks,
speckled and freckled
along your spine
where the masseuse with the mask
masqued  in  the masquerade 
is out of time.
. . . . . . . . . . .
rhythmic hands to remove:::
 and slide in to pocketbook gratefuls.
like a housewife and her sponges
bought from the door to door sellsman
    who would die the death of a salesman 
in-between  where the doors always are:::
