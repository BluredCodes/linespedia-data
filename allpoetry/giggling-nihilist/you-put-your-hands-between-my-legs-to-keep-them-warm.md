                       it began so simply. so innocently. 
   you put your hands between my legs to keep them warm
    ..more than a faint sort of curious peering, 
                     i WILL NEVER BE THE SAME AFTER YOU

       fucking like taciturnity,
       like restraint
       echoing on the bare walls.
       vacant eyes.
       and that's the only thing that scares [me.

& i plead ....
    like glass shatter. 
 voice piercing silence.

        it's      over      now ,         but     is it over now?
   we received our resolve 
bathed in the sick sick light of the screen.

and now what do you propose?
after we've  done.
e s c a p ed.
and 
r e t u r n ed.
?
