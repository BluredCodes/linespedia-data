-




today i ate
two
meat influenced
p r o d u c t s 
pork and beans
and 
crab rangoon
--
wait, 
then there's
that jell-o 
with the 
cow hooves.





-accidental jell-o. 
gosh it's difficult to be a vegetarian sometimes.
 and not because i crave meat. 
because because because of the subtly-influenced meat ppproducts that work their way into my diet.
 i must separate myself from the ease of the supermarket.

it's just too sterile.
--i don't buy it.
