/




and the smeared words across the mirror said,
order in no particular,

the giggling nihilist

jason 316-####

Quick Change

me da igual!

Clue

i impersonate chairs

makes you dizzy

oy, what a lovely piece of broccoli...


--that's about it sans the obscenities.
and it's clean, wiped away.
sentimental pictures and comic strips removed.
suction cup stuffed animals packed
along with the inspection ticket from Oahu,
an' some zenish koans, aye, for another day.
i'm cleaning up. and moving away.




/aye, just countin' days.
 mein frens will unnerstand dis.
