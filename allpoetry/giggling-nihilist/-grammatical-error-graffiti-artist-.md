\\\\\\\




 grammatical error graffiti artist

    wall scrawl crude muralist

      scribbling defacement

      street art disfigurement

    *the writing's on the wall*



	-- my credo, and police statement






\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\that's about how it goes these days..
