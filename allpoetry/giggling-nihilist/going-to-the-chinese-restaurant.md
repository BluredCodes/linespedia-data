+




"going to the chinese restaurant"

hallucinating oranges 
wiping away tears 
strange light in strange train stations
green, reflecting exciting-fear,
and happy accidents

driving at dusk 
thinking nothing
eternal waves hit jagged rocks
staring contests with the stars 
who out shine everyone but my mother
the moon, night-mother
kiss me good-night

with candies
with old, torn blankets
stacks of books cast shadows 
in the mind's eye 
where we all live at night
at rest 
with desires 
that are already memories









+wrote many moon ago.
 years.
just found it. 
hiding.
 en mein pillow.
