THIS FAG IS NOT A TOY.

TO AVOID DANGER OF DEGRADATION OF "VALUES":

KEEP THIS FAG AWAY FROM GOD-FEARING POLITE SOCIETY.  

THIS FAG IS NOT A TOY.

DISPOSE OF PROPERLY.
