to The Smoker:
from happy little anarchists 
who write in orange dissent
of a smoking habit
--you'll notice that all the ingredients aren't listed,
we wonder why(?)
. . . . 
you look so cool in your
 in~hale, ex~hale of poison smoke
and 
dirty socks
we are 2nd-hand-smoking-poets,
pleading with you to stop




-. . . . 
ahhhhh!  angst for smokers. 
(of the manufactured-sort, that is)
. . . . 
actually, i wrote this pome randomly and stuck it in PLAD PANTS JASON's pack of cigarettes whence we were at Vino's
. . . .
and i wrote the pome in a orange-colored-pencil, hence "write in orange dissent"
. . . . .
