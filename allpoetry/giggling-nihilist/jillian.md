---
title: "jillian"
writer: "giggling nihilist"

sketch the night we spent together,
framed by streetlights
as we uttered continuously 
from your punched hole insane asylum ceilings
to mein humble dwelling
'neath the stars,
as you beckoned
and we lay aside each other
breathing in the fumes exhaled by the dancing celestials above, 
the seven of  Pleiades wishing us good fortune.
pine for the sacred seven... and pine for my abode
where you will soon be welcome
without guise or concern 
apt to run lithe fingers along my horizons
as we watch the late setting sun 
for oft i have longed to spend 
the waning hours of the day
with you, mein fren--love you jillian!
