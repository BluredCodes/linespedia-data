"Araujo de Oliveire"

 the day we met,
i wrote your name on my knee
in red ink
. . . . . . . . . 
now i'm 'persona non-grada'
not there.
remembering...
your slow blinking disease,
left eye was it?
\ \ \ \ \ \ \ \ \ \
you  b~r~A~s~i~l~i~a~n~s
   and your libidos
you chamelons
   and your mosquitoes.
 . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .
as if what you expected of me had suddenly changed.
at the drop of the last hat.
my personal monologue hairstyle, on display.
for your bodily gains. releases. tranSgressions.
for your libidous fame. sineeses. misdirections.
. . . . . . . . . . . .






;that's about how that affair went..
semi-colon strength!
fuck.
