.




en.. . gulp. . .30 years,
GOODTIMES  replaced by a quirky Newsradio.

will i be sitting and pulled in by infomercials 
trying to sell me back my youth.(?)

will i sit ad-nauseam-nostalgia
  when they show the clip:
Cramer bursting through the door.(?)
in Jon Stewart's pajamas
and 
dirty socks.
Mike Myer's cockamamie camera
     en
             a 
Linda Richmond frock.(?)

. . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .

alas! whence shall i turn that quirk-radio-dial to hear a Radiohead--oldie (?)
and even still. and of more import. do i dread the day?
not quite. methinks. 
 
ah.

the disillusionment of growing (decaying) old in
 american-quick-market-take-a-number-next-generation-please-
atmosphere of time.
. . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .
em...note to self:
...must remember that  one-eight-hundred-number, 
so i can buy
that episode of Who's Line...  





.just musing on the passing of time.
