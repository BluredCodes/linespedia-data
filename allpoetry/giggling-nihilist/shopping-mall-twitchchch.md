My Countrymen’s escalators embarrass me
.          In shiny glassy shopping malls 
 where you catch your reflection everywhere
.         These are the new cathedrals,
.  sheathed in tons of glass 
.       Pass me the capitalist collection basket
.    so I can hand you some of my 
.           well worn wallet plastic
Everything ersatz shimmers, everything shines
.      Sale signs pervade you with nine ninety nines
 “does the buckle match the belt 
.          or the belt match the tie?”
 --these are the conversations of the passersby
.                  ...Don’t stare, honey, don’t look
.       It’s just some madman scribbledverse crook 
.   I twitch, smile for the public eye
.      I watch some uptight business-likes walking by
 One, 
 Drags his left foot
.       But the woman,
 Her, you couldn’t deny 
.      she hid hips, hid thighs
.         in her nun-like   business attire
  -though  She put the  'sin’   in the word,
.           It was easier to lie
 No, she put One Foot  In Front Of The Other
.     One
.            Foot 
.                    In
.                        Front
.                                 Of
.                                      The
.                                             Other
