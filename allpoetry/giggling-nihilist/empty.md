-





empty belly feels right.
 mouth watering for that meat
-eaten cow, am not proud.
 ran before finished cutting
my fingers are tired
my arms are heavy as my eyes are
go to sleep, dreaming of the aurora borealis 
illuminated
skies of wonder
even if you are tired 
you can look up enough to see
you can learn to move with me
you are me

lay down on soft bed
dream of the aurora borealis
