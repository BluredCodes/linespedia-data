a pome bout that darn brazillian

"it hurts when i blink"
lazy eye?    no?
does it bleed?
     . . . tersol . . . 
AH  ...HA...  indeed
   it inflates?
NO  it imflamates ..
     high jinks  AND  mischief 
pattycakes  and  bestmates..
      j a p a n e s e
sushi and green tea
    CAIPIRINHA E GUARANA
DESSA MISTURA NADA NAI DA
  alcohol and caffeine 
THIS WON'T GET US ANYWHERE
     e    E     e   !




!e!

affairs with brazillian boys are bad for your health.
