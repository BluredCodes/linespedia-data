-



standing still,
quieted by the late hour of night,
early hour of morning,
staring still,
looking glass image,
and knowing all the things that could release from you at this moment,
when you lean closer...
make peace with your reflection 
and move slowly towards the door
reaching only your arm out to the doorknob, at first...
and as you do so,
   grasp the knob and pull the rest of your body along with you,
 thank your reflection, 
for the projection,
the ability to address yourself,
undress yourself,
break the mirrors,
    and forget yourself.
