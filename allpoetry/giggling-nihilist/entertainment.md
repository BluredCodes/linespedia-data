"entertainment, styrofoam conversation -
       nevertheless..  what diAboliCal plan has seized you imagination?
like half an eye for life.
 in strange rolling ball structure.. [we] are stuck like toy hands plantain health. 
work work. daylight come me want go home. 
         what you talkin'? 	
    you can't escape the quota.

- - unimaginable b o x machinery. oN dainty check'd table cloth..
sterilized pasteurized homogenized
   and repackaged contaminated.
satiate styrofoam conversation.
  
'you seem well-read.'
we are electronic leash
en rain-X daze.
and though  we en dream escape
m u n d a n e,
      we are nevertheless
tied-strained to black and white tiled ceiling
and ideal institutions.

the table, barren with four skinny chairs.
no smoking.

break-room daze,
e s c a p e.
the conveyor-belt doom.
. . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . . .







-coffee break? 
lonely.
