{




do i even want to remember this night?
surely i don't, but tis my habit to write.

and i thought, sitting in...that movie.
   ' what wonderful fantasy '

o, but twas more interesting, twas more involving to just listen,
 to feel the rhythm of your breathing.
you two.
jason smeeling and smelling like the usual coffee and cigarettes 
jillian in her sweet long hair charm and dainty freckles dusting a       
  cheerful and knowing face.
jason, seeming kind of stiff
jillian, fidgety and happily unable to get comfortable. willingly to make the best of it. 
jason, who (maybe) i have yet to figure out
jillian, who likes to have fun
jason, who, for breakfast, eats jelly w/ his puns
 these two. yes, two of these.




}this ain't no pome. 
i don't rightly know...
what it is...
but this ain't no pome.
you got me?
