life for me ain't been no crystal stair. 
spit.
bricks.
and 
....
song.
....

early morning.
asked to march.

taken away.
taken away.

a rememberance, 
smooth 
cold
and
a 
smarting
crack
against the skin.
.................
AGAINST THE SKIN.
.................


we wake.
begin again.

feeling like 2 FOR THE PRICE OF ONE
and Invention's Mother,
Necessity..
has a gran'baby/....
 alias:
Capitilist.
...........

we rest on the spines of motown.
empowered by song.
and long dangling earings.
with tiny
absurd 
skinny
they were so skinny
bodies.
climbing 
her 
dangling
jingle
jangles.

with every successive throw of limb,
their bones jabbed through skin.
and they went synchronized
tick tock manner.
throw.
whiplash.
and crack.
and 
throw.
whiplash.
and crack.
and 
so
whiplash.
and crack.
.......... 

she.. walked long tall in non-chalance. 
precious.
her skin drank anything you would put on it.
she felt confident in her powers of absorbtion.
while performing the newly acquired acts of contortion.
"the young caucasian is still at large"
wag your dog.
like
liberal distortion.
...................

the day ends.
the night begins.
and
mocks the day 
with her flames.
call us riot,
we are rebellion.
"taking advantage of the situation"
that
would
be 
the
looting
.........
back then those weren't no rubber bullets shooting.
