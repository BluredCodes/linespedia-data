11:29 p.m.

explaining myself.

i grasp the doorknob with a cold fingers' turn.
i stare at the wall  with my head slightly  tilted to the right.
i sing ....desultorily
&  my ears ring.
i look calmly into the reflection of "me" in the mirror.
though i am
                        quite the contrary.





-hmmm.
