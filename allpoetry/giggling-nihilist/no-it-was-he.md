..



no, it was he ...
staring into the neon lights
of his slowly clogging 
a r t e r i e s.
gazing fast
into the murky dungeons wells 
of his fingernail cigarette ash.
 
  



... . 

ahhhhh!  sitting outside another fast food restaurant, 
while mein fren PLAD PANTS JASON orders...
