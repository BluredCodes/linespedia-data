"




"sound business mind"

found sinus crime
spung signals time.
with
a lum limber lyme
on a
crumbling crimey slime.

emily athen crimily craften wrote a silly simile for saffen
then she got a divorce
lipsy lucy sniffed suzie 
and laughed
jimi jimi  gave  earl a hickey
 and passed in the study with Mrs. Plum and her knife
in the back of nifty nancy
who was such a nice housed wife (practical shoed) 
motherly crude

Attention! A certain monk asked To-san, 
	"What is Buddha?"
To-san said, 
	"Here is hemp  --three pounds of it."


~ritz cracker. yo.
