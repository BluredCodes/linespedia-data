"Loren"

* floral lace
in poly
poly lining
has seaming
pleat.
detail.
in poly
elastic.
black.
loren!
split.
sleeves.
in poly,
with allover
cherry blossom
ties   at    back
empire.
waist.
an obsession, 
or just curiosity?
Blind Date,
from shoulder.
to shudder.
f a t e.
fatal.
floral.
in poly
elastic.
let me pull it off of you.
pleat.
detail.
this is manic.
in poly!
in poly!
in poly!


pull off trousers.
manic.
s p a nd ex.
tinted denim blazer.
with diagonal stripes.
in
burgundy.
poly!
rayon!
with
contrast
stretch
l a c e
trim
heringbone
your trousers.


Loren, 
need you.
nothing left.
but dirty laundry
TYING AT THE BACK
