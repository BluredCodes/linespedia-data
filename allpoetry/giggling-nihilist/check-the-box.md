title: check the box
subject: banality of all that unidentifiable contraptioness you buy  


i don't know,
  what does the box say?
and more idiomatic phrases?
read the instructions
  they're all in piglatined hybrid russian slang
i think we need a translator
     i think that is a right right right thought thunken,  spoken.
i don't know,
what does the box say?
nothing... it's just a plane for advertisement 

       "..and other features of  our  piece of  devial contraption that you just can't live without include:

          post- op. banality of cutlery
      
 and significant loss of financial institutional gracefulness with or without exceeding verbose rudeness on automated company      
          dial 1 and   the pound sign or stay on the line for another computerized assistant representative of 
our fine establishedment 
 in a shrill monotony 
that reminds me of  the southern belle hop's distorted speech shreik... 

if you would like to repeat the menu press 146 and the pound sign.... 
 
we appreciate your business....
 we love to tastefully waste you 
and the time you're glued to.    
 
after we are the tasteful wastes of  societalindustry.
a bottle of ...OF.... yes, that will be on any of my scores of plastics. thankyou fakefully. "

and      this      was       just       the         fine         print

yeh yeh that's what the box said, or at least that's what he told me.

the box uttering fine print sharp quick
