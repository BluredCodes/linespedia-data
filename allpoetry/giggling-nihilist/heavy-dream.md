-






heavy  dream

finished cutting

  spoke to you in soft tongue of mouth

empty meat 


lay  down  on  soft   bed  
   dream   of   the   aurora   borealis

move cutting fingers
skies illuminated sleep heavy
tired watering eyes see enough
to learn before wonder ran empty "meat"
but for, if not feels
      my mouth dreams
can the belly look finished
are good arms with that

as i eat my tired 
   cold aurora borealis
