let's have an affair
   --i know... i'm not tall, leggy, blonde
with bubble gum smack smack  between
  perfect teeth anti-freeze smile 
and plastic eye  click click saline solution
turned hollow  to the vaporous skies above-=-=---=-=-=--
 eager  industrialist  eyes below
   nor  HELIUM TALK PINKY WRAP lips 
& matching(!)  fingernail polishoutfit
drinking noxema designer fluids
 in halter top tanning bed heat
neither  transparentglassbottlebliss  in  flimsypuddleconversation 
 while crossing legg'ed nonexistent knees on pantyhose thread formfitted meat,
  shaved meat ..rubb'd razor-skin ersatz -synthetic pretty flesh
 in smiling indecision within a thin pheramone package 
residing in the stain'ed guise of necessity
"i need, i need, ...    i want"
  in the latest outsourced labor and marketed fashion
   choking on grapevine talk of dry white wines

white lies, 

    & bitter artifice, aplombmatic indifference with a stain and
guise that
     "brings out your eyes"
nix. nope. never. by no means. nay. none.
