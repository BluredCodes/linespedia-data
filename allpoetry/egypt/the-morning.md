title: "The Morning"
writer: "Egypt"

She woke that morning, refreshed and calm
Stepping outside, she greeted the dawn
Seregalm sat proud and purred at her sight
Refreshed herself, from the sleep of the long night

She walked to the water's edge and peered at the fish
The swipe of a paw, she caught her morning dish
Her vision self laughed and to her daughter's called out
Early risers they were, already out and about

She heard laughing coming down the long winding trail
She looked and smiled, the vision of beauty entailed
Twins walked with pride, their eyes shown not a care
Flowers of white and vines donned their hair

They rushed to their mother and adorned her with flowers
The three danced together, then came an unexpected shower
They laughed and danced, Seregalm happily ate
Unmindful of their awaiting fate

When at last, they were done, they fell to the ground
They listened to the oasis, and heard of its sound
Soaked to the bone, all four of them rose
Their happiness and peace for the Gods only know

Back to the tent, they all went inside
Happy for once, to really be alive
She kissed her daughters and hugged them once more
Tranquility and peace quieted her corePart IV
