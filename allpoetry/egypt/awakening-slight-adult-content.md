When she woke, she found herself in bed

Closing her eyes, the pain pounded her head

Attempting to sit, she realized she was chained

Moaning again, her voice full of distain



Looking around, she realized she was alone

Pain in her side, she let out a groan

The door swung open and she let out a gasp

Before she could blink, she was in his grasp



The chains disappeared as he pulled her from bed

Her heart began to pound, thinking soon she would be dead

'Not that, Mistress.'  He purred in her ear

'Something I must show you, my beautiful dear.'



He walked her to the balcony, out into the Moon light

She let out a gasp at the wondrous sight

The land was sweeping and beautiful, a sight to behold

A sight so rare, in the company of the bold



'As far as your eye can see is my gift to you.'

'Explore it freely, I ask you to do.'

She felt him move close behind and trapped her at the wall

Amazed at the beauty, his gift, she again felt small



'Your gift is most generous, Dark One.' She said meekly

'But what makes you believe I'll not run?' She asked weakly

He stood closer still and she felt his bodily heat

She realized then, to her horror, how he would to her keep



Deep within her body, a tingle began to grow

Her breathing rapid, her body trembling, this would be his blow

The mark he laid began to pulse and throb

She realized she was at the mercy of this Dark God



She trembled and moaned, the tingle began to build

Crying out as release took hold, this would be his kill

Twice more it happened, each more intense than the last

Each one on top of the other, all happening fast



She leaned her trembling body against his and sighed

'This is how you will never run and hide.'

His whisper sent shivers down her body and she moaned

'I have you.'  He whispered again and she let out a desperate groan.
