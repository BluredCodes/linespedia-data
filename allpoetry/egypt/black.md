He brought her over that fateful day
Hoping his other lady had something to say
Glimpses of the future and auras she could see
He trusted his lady and her special ability

He knew she studied both white and black
Black (Know Thy Enemy) in case she was attacked
It did not bother or worry him much
A force to be reckoned with, he saw she was such

They climbed the stairs slowly and both with a smile
She shivered and became sick - This lady was vile
Glimpses did come - her aura was dark
That instant she decided to use the black art

What did she see?  It sent her in a rage
She saw herself standing beside his grave
Slowly and carefully they lowered him in
Details arose, quite clear, she even saw the men

After they left she went straight into her room
She made the decision, that lady sealed her doom
Carefully she pulled out her books and found the spell
To him, she decided, she would never tell

Fueled by hatred and anger she cast
Afterward and alone, she quietly sat
Knowing this victory would be all too sweet
Watching her fall, would be a tasty treat

The backlash didn't scare her or make her blink
She didn't realize that she was on the brink
Drunk and angry she thought she did it in love
Not stopping to speak to the Gods above.Another series, sorry folks, but this is a story that needs to be told.  And it's too frappin' long to do it any other way, it's layered and complicated.
