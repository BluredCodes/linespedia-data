Great One!  Isis!!  Mother of All
I'm lost and confused - Please hear my call
My Daughter's are in danger, my sister torn apart
I feel their pain and fear and it's killing my heart!

Seeing the moon this morning, I have grown scared.
A ring around it - trouble - do I even dare?
To think of what lies in store
Freezes me down to my core

I stared long and hard, shivering not from the cold
But for the deep-seated terror - my breathing on hold
Ring around the moon - Trouble not far behind
I'm torn, I'm scared - I'm losing my mind!

I ask only one favor and do so with love
Confirmation be sent - on the wings of a dove
Spirit guides hear my prayer, I ask for all time
Send my family love, peace and strength - to help ease my troubled mind

If it is good for the one, take their lives in your hands
If this is destiny - end my worries and speak of your plans
I can't bear to see and feel their pain and fear
For they are truly my daughter's and sister dear.I spoke this prayer this morning during a time of deep meditation.
