A quickening pulse, shallowness of breath
Hands of the Ancient Ones caress my hair and arms
The deep sense of 'knowing'
A gentle whisper -- too faint to hear, but strong to feel
'Come with us'
A life of spirit, detaching -- freedom
Soaring high in blue skies, warmth of the sun -- Descending slowly
Open fields of blowing prairie grass, rolling hills, the air alive with sounds of Mother
A sense of pride, majesty
'Remember'
Lifting, descending, sounds of a waterfall
A woman stands on the banks
A woman in water
A sense of awe & beauty
'Remember'
Lifting....heart quickening further, tears flowing
The sound of a babbling brook.
I am not alone
The ancestors
High Mountains, the Great Tree....The Garden
A sense of Power, Strength and Wisdom
'Destiny....HOME'
Lifting with gentle hands
Airborne
Soaring Higher than the clouds
Icy fingers caress my skin
A Fire deep with my soul....warming me
Floating....A sudden stop
Hanging....Overwhelmed
Tears flow faster
Awe, Love, Power, Strength, Beauty
A Final Whisper
'Beginning'I have been studying Paganism now for 15 years.  This was written directly after a meditation in which the Ancestors took me back to all of my 'enlightening moments' and reminded me just what all of this is about.  I can only say that in order to truly understand it, read each line as a story in itself, then as a whole.  May the Ancestors love you all.
