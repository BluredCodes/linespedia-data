title: "Song of the Goddess"

Mother Isis, Goddess of All
I come to Thee heartbroken, please hear my call
To feel her soft touch, to see her smile
I need her near me, give her one mile.

My heart is empty, my eyes full of tears
Let her come to me and soften my fears
My arms wrapped around her, her words comforting me
I need her near me, I ask this of Thee.

To touch her and hold her, and then our good-byes
To gaze at her beauty, to see love in her eyes
Grant my plea, Oh Great One, no tears shall fall
She is My Mother, My Goddess, My All.

Our world has crumbled, we've no where to turn
To see her one more time, is all that I yearn
To feel the Kiss on my head - I find comfort there
I'll remember the nights, as we sat in her chair.

To touch her and hold her, and then our good-byes
To gaze at her beauty, to see love in her eyes
Grant my plea, Oh Great One, no tears shall fall
She is My Mother....My Goddess....My All.
