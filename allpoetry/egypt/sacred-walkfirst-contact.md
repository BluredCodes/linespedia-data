The sun was warm, the breeze slightly cool
She began the walk, hundreds of yards from the school
Amazed at the peace and quiet there
Deep inside a city that didn't care

She came to a fork in the path and stopped
She looked to the right and her heart flopped
She walked to the left as she heard 'You must'
Head straight ahead, she shivered as her feet pounded the dust

'There's no avoiding, you must come this way.'
A whisper behind her, she heard it say
'Today is the day, to face your past and heal,
I am your first step, do we have a deal?'

She stopped walking and turned around
Looking, left, right, up and down
'Whatever you are, I hear your cry.'
'Let me move on.'  she said with a sigh

'There is much I must do, so much to see.
I ask one request, it's not much, you'll see.
Let me walk in peace for a while, an hour or so
Just understand, I am not saying no.'

She waited for a reply, surrounded by nature's sounds
The warmest breeze blew across her, as she looked to the ground
'Your request is granted, go and make ready.
Listen and learn, then come to me steady.'

She bowed low and softly said 'thank you.'
'My promise I'll keep, I'll come to you soon.'
She turned back around and continued her walk
Not really intending to keep the appointed talk.
