title: "Danger in Shadows II"
writer: "Egypt"
slug: "danger-in-shadows-ii"

Walking back hand in hand
Leaving their footprints in the sand
No words spoken, she looks to the Moon
Realizing the end would be soon

They stop at the city walls and embrace once more
She looks deep into his eyes and is shaken to the core
He furrows his brow when she looks away
He asks quietly, 'Do you wish me to stay?'

She nods only slightly and turns before he could see the tears
Knowing eventually, he'll know of her fears
He kisses her neck and gently lifts her into his arms
She lays her head on his shoulder, giving into his charms

As he takes her to her rooms, she quietly sighs
Doing what she can, so that she doesn't cry
She ages slowly as he walks on
He whispers softly to her of their eternal bond

He undresses her slowly and lowers her in warm waters
He washes her gently and tries to get her to speak of her bothers
She quickly turns around and holds him tight
And begins speaking of flight

He listens intently and his brow furrows deep 
He notices her aging in front of his eyes and begins to weep
She kisses him lightly, loving him, worshipping him
And knew that their love would never dim

Realizing that he could never be told 
And why she began to grow old
Where dark hair once was, now streaked with grey
Her skin once supple and soft, now felt different, to her dismay

He lifted her again and carried her to bed
Began speaking of the first day they met
She smiled and laughed and loved him more
They began making love...with strangers at the door.
