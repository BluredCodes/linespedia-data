The spell was quite simple and easy to do
No tools, no talisman, just words to spew
She cast it in anger and hatred, revenge on her mind
Not even realizing, that she was blind

As she thought of the lady's undoing, at her hand
She smiled and thought of the dark forces at her command
She was in control, but control is just an illusion
But she would see this through to its conclusion

She never asked permission, never minded the Rede
One thought only, was what set this deed
Protect and defend for the one she loved
She couldn't let it happen, so she decided to shove

She couldn't allow his destruction, couldn't allow his death
Suddenly wanting the woman's last breath
Time was on her side, time to wait
To see what would happen, not minding her fate.Told ya I'd rewrite the three, here we go.
