---
title: "Love Ended"
writer: "Egypt"
slug: "love-ended"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/34047-Love-Ended-by-Egypt"
---

Bodies intertwined, love expended
The peace of Love after, only to be crushed
The room suddenly full of those not understanding
Split apart and taken away
Crying for each other, wishing for it to end
Taken to my Beloved Seas as I hear his voice scream
'I love thee, my Goddess!'
Beaten and battered over again
The roll of distant thunder
The hum of the Crystal changing
I look to the skies one last time
Knowing the end is near...

The rush of cool water on my legs as I'm drug
Further and further and I hear his screams again
I look to the city and scream outloud
To see his life end, violence
'The time is now, Mistress' and I hear a laugh
I look in the face of my executioner and smile
'You may kill our bodies, but our love will live on'
Suddenly feeling the waters surround my body, taking me slowly
The sting of water in my lungs as they raise and lower, again and again
I'm frightened and angry then feel him near
The caress I longed for soothed my soul 'Come to me, My Goddess'
I smile one last time as the waters overtake me and breathe in, one last time...This is another one that doesn't need anything from me.  Let it speak for itself.
