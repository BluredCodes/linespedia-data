High in the castle, deep in a midsummer's night
She slept soundly, unaware of her future plight
Her love had turned cold and dark
Before all would end, he would leave his deep mark

He had once been a guardian, father and lover
What he turned to, came from much lower
The changes were subtle until that fateful eve
What she had witnessed, brought her to her knees

It scared her not to think his power had been dark
Given from the Gods to protect, his orders had been stark
Too many lifetimes spent apart sealed her doom
The only one that could touch it, may now send her to her tomb

She suddenly woke from a disturbing dream
What she saw in her room caused her to scream
Body of dark skin and armor, wings of black
Eyes shining silver, his muscles tight - ready for attack

'You're coming with me, there's no denying your fate.'  
She sat on her bed, her eyes filled with hate
With a mighty arm, he picked her from the bed
She screamed as they flew out the window, to the South they began to head

Her mind wandered back to a time of beauty and grace
But Lust and greed had forever etched his face
'Stop your thoughts!'  He grumbled and she let out a gasp
His laugh low and guttural, he released his grasp

Screaming - heart pounding as she fell through the air
Fear, but more so anger, was more than she could bear
She heard his night scream as he caught her mere feet from the ground
In his arms was safety, she once again found

'Know this, Mistress before I am through
Your Power increased, your love for him to me, these things you will do
We are destined to Rule together and Rule we shall
'Fighting this destiny, will not be allowed.'Part I
