A silence falls over the bustling city
Everything turning to glistening white
Amazed at the beauty of even a fire hydrant
She stands at the door holding her cat ~>^^< meow!~

The city comes to a standstill
No noise, no sirens, nothing
But a wonder filled silence
The first Winter Storm finally hits

The magick of the evening
Quiet and warm
Full Moon above the clouds
Would cast a beautiful and eerie glow

Ice pelts the ground, soon snow will start to fall
She realizes she has begun the final stage of life
Once loving summer, she now sees the beauty of winter
From Mother to Crone

No streaks of gray dance in her hair
No lines or wrinkles on her supple skin
Her spirit is what has aged
And she welcomes it with loving arms

Taking the kitty ~>^^< meow!~ away from the door
She goes to the kitchen and fixes warm apple cider
She takes her favorite chair to the open door
And wraps them both in a blanket, and watches the beauty continue to fall.Oh damn, I just showed my age in that one.  Anybody got a sled I can use?  Gotta take my sister's labradors out to play tomorrow!
