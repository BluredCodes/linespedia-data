---
title: "The Guardian"
writer: "Egypt"
slug: "the-guardian"

A shiver down my spine, my body in tune
A whisper sends me back, back in time when we were One
'Atlantean Goddess...'
My soul shivers, my breath only quickens
Sands of Time, Sands of Egypt swirls about me
Intense love and power takes me higher
As he appears before me
Sighing as his mighty arms surround me
To feel love, protection and peace
My heart steadies as I melt against him
My thoughts racing as I feel his hand in my hair
I sigh again as I hear his heart, strong and true
Taken back to a time long lost

Our silence is golden, our love withstanding the test of time
Sands of Time swirl around us
We stand on the shores, the Full Moon above
Warm ocean breezes caress us
He holds me tighter, knowing we cannot stay long
We speak briefly of our love, our life, our deaths
Silent tears roll down my face, but tears of joy
He's found me again, searching over time
My life now complete, my heart now whole
Brought to the present, my heart grows heavy
Wishing to stay with him, but knowing I can't
His hand in my hair one last time, as he fades from me slowly
'Atlantean Goddess....'Anybody that has experienced the remembering of a past life knows how incredibly intense it can be.  But it's even more intense when you find a powerful love and wanting.  This has happened to me, I know I have found my Guardian, HE has found ME, has searched lifetimes looking for me.  Our lives are whole again with each other, and I curse the distance we have between us, physically.  But spiritually, we are One again.
