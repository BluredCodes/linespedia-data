title: "'Effect'"
writer: "Egypt"
slug: "effect"

Her life went on as normal as it could
The spell almost forgotten, but the vision withstood
Asleep in her bed on a late summer's eve
Unaware of the darkness she had actually conceived

She heard a knock on the door and she quickly rose
Looking out the window, her heart suddenly froze
There on her step he stood, shoulders hunched low
Looking as though he received a deathblow

She opened the door and he held her close
They went to her room quiet, on tiptoes
He laid on the bed and she laid next to him
As he began the story, he told how she did him in

Butcher knives, uncontrollable fits he told
She shivered hard as the events began to unfold
The spell worked, but worked too well
Still not realizing that she had fell

She held him close as he drifted to sleep
Tears of her own, were hard to keep
She slipped outside and looked to the rising sun
'So It Shall Be Written, So It Shall Be Done'
