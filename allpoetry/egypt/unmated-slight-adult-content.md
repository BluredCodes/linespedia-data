She spent the next morning exploring his gift
Allowing for a while, her spirits to lift
She watched the animals run and play
She smiled and laughed, silently wishing to stay

She came to a clearing of tall, soft grass
She laid down and enjoyed her warm bask
Her thoughts turned to last night's encounter and sighed
Disgusted with herself, for her inability to hide

She lightly laid her hand on his mark and frowned
Shifted slightly to enjoy the warm ground
She felt the familiar tingle; his dark, sensual kill
The tingle began to build and her mind grew still

Hating her body, but loving it as well, she got to her knees
A whisper in the wind urged her further, 'To yourself, please.'
Her breathing grew rapid, her body out of control
He appeared before her, dark beauty and bold

'Do it Mistress, allow your release.'  Feeling his excitement, dark and low
The tingle to a heat, her hand never moving, waiting to explode
'Love it, hate it, this is raw and pure.'  He dropped to his knees.
'Carnal release, hard and long, this energy to feed.'

'Take it, own it, this is yours - not mine.'
'Do it, do it NOW, without any resign!'
She screamed loud as her sense exploded, giving her release
It lasted forever, then she slowly found peace

Gasping for air, her body shaking and covered in sweat
She looked at the Dark One, their eyes swiftly met
'Strong and deep, carnal pleasures can be.'
'When I take you, out of control you will be.'

With quiet surprise, she began to wonder how soon
The thought alone began to make her swoon.
'Remember my words, I'll take you as I please.'
He vanished from sight and she rose from her knees.
