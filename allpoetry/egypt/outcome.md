The one thing she was not concerned with, hit her hard
The backlash, the nightmares all began to bombard
Her abilities blanketed, she was bound
Spiritually, she made not a sound

She never told him what she had done
She felt she couldn't, this was her demon
One she would carry for the rest of her life
Even if it meant a life full of strife

She thinks back often to the fateful spell
How easy it was, even though she fell
The power she held in her mind, spirit and body
She wielded it, nurtured it, handled it aptly

The power immense, the feeling incredible
She always knew, that her heart could be vengeful
Would you have done the same thing? I ask
The dark energies, you would have to unmask

If given the chance again, I think of that often
I would do it again, if it sent me to my coffin
Drew is special, we've shared many past lives
For him, it's obvious, I would lay down and die.
