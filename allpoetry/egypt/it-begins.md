Coming to her sense, the sounds of the city she heard
Standing on the bridge, she heard not a word
Touching the rail, she smiled and nodded
Back to reality, she slowly began to trod

'My vision was in love and peace, dark one'
'If I come to you now, it will surely come undone'
'I feel hatred, greed and lust coming from you'
'Its my death you wish me to see, I've already been through'

'It's time, M'lady - you must come and see'
'Allow it and own it, for it will set you free'
'There is more to this, more you must feel'
'You've seen it, yes, but you have not healed.'

She started to walk, her breathing came fast
'There's more you want from me, more than my past'
'I do not trust you, dark one, your presence is cold'
'Get Thee behind me, there's nothing to hold.'

Her walk turned fast and then to a run
Then suddenly she stopped, and looked to the sun
No sound could be heard, no birds or wind
Her thoughts suddenly turned, to the voice around the bend

'It is not I that is cold, m'lady know this.'
'The cold you feel, surrounds you in mist.'
'This step must be taken, the time is now.'
'Your promise, remember, you gave me your vow.'

She walked toward the voice, as fear rose in her chest
Around the bend she turned, as she slowly progressed
She stopped suddenly and fell in the mud
What she saw, the taste in her mouth of bloodPart V
