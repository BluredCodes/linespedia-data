title: "Storm's Horse"
writer: "Egypt"

A clap of thunder, a wave of lightning pierces the night sky

Arms outstretched, reaching for what the storm has to offer

Power, passion, strength, and protection, I feel

Of Love lost, remembered and regained



A clap of thunder and the feeling grows strong

He's near me, he's with me, riding the storm's horse

I smile as I accept him, wholly and completely

The Guardian returned



I drop to my knees, I'm dripping wet

Trembling, I lay in rain soaked grass

Eyes closed and smiling, I see his face, his body all mine

'Together again, M'Lord.'





I see his smile, his eyes, two shining stars

The storm rages on, seems to continue to grow

'My Goddess, I have searched and found that which I swore to protect and love'

I shiver and accept



The storm reaches its peak as I feel our time is near

The agony of his leaving, the grief of loss

I feel his hand in my hair, a kiss on my neck

Crying out, I feel him vanishAre all of my writings about this past life I have remembered?  At least 90% will be.  It was incredibly powerful and I felt the need to write it.
