They flew through the night, the Dark Lord and Mistress
She cried silent tears, her heart full of distress
They came to a river and he began to fly low
She closed her eyes as she heard the water's flow

His feet hit the ground softly to her silent surprise
Grace mixed with danger, she realized her fated demise
He stood close to her, his claws sinking deep into her skin
'I'll take this flesh whenever I please.' He said with a grin

She tore from his grasp and slapped him across the face
She began smiling as she started to pace
'This body is mine, only one can take it when he wants'
She tore open her nightdress, her body she began to flaunt

'Dream and wish, Dark One, you'll never have me.'
'Force it and I'll end my life, this I decree.'
He grabbed her and threw them both on the ground
Attempting to scream, she made not a sound

He laid upon her, his hands began to roam
She closed her eyes and began thinking of home
He roared outloud and quickly stood
She crawled away in fear, of his towering godhood

'I'll not have you this way, I want you to come to me'
'Begging and pleading for carnal release'
'I'll rip his memory from you, mind, body and soul'
'Mark my words, you'll have no control.'

He growled and jumped on her, forced her wrists in the mud
She gasped, she screamed, the taste in her mouth of blood
'You'll want me to please you, Mistress, just wait and see'
'You'll crave it, kill for it, deep in your belly'

'This is Destiny, Witch, you can't run and hide'
'No thinking about it, no time to decide'
'No turning back, this is fate, Mistress'
'Accept it now, or face my true darkness'
