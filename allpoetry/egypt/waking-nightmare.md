---
title: "Waking Nightmare"
writer: "Egypt"
slug: "waking-nightmare"
source: "AllPoetry"

Screaming loud
Covered in sweat

Images arise
Going to die

Raping comes
Can't stop it

Voices heard
Hands around throat

Praying starts
No one listens

Forced pain
Closes her eyes

Waits death
Welcomes the peace

Hands clinch
Losing her breath

Everything stops
Quiet room again

Shaking uncontrollably
Body on fire

Wishing, hoping
End is near
