Watching the peace of the storm as it sat overhead,
dropping ice over the landscape
'Ice magick' she whispered 'it's perfect'
She looked to her cat, who meowed as if saying 'Good Girl, go for it.'

With reverence, she gathered her tools.  
On a piece of paper, she wrote out her worries
Read them aloud to her cat who flipped over to expose his belly
'I'll take that as perfect.'

She stepped outside, bundled in robes
One hand carrying her cat, the other a small bowl
As she gathered the ice, she laughed out loud
Silly cat was batting at the ice! >^^<

She dropped to her knees as the cat moved inside her robes
She took the paper out and read it to the Goddess who was unseen, but felt
She sent personal power into the paper, along with her worries
Placing her fingers inside the bowl of ice, she incanted:

'You are a vehicle of transformation'

Stepping inside, she put the cat down and walked into her kitchen
She slowly heated up pure mineral water and gently placed the ice
inside
As she watched the ice melt, she incanted again:

'All the power that you have laid upon me
'All your power I now melt
'By the Powers that Be 3 x 3
'As my will, So Mote It Be!'

When all was done, she sighed and smiled as her cat flopped in the floor
exposing his belly again
She reached out and rubbed him as he purred
Then heard a soft voice 'Well said'.May my spell of Peace comfort all who read it.
