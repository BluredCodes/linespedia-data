title: "Dark Death"
writer: "Egypt"

Standing on the balcony, deep into the stormy night
Her thoughts turned once again of taking flight
Running and hiding, anything but staying there
Getting away from the Dark One and his evil care

The mark began to throb, but the throb was cold
Colder still - her breath it stole
Crying out softly, she moved into the room
She screamed when she saw her evil doom

Standing there amidst others of his kind
The Dark One smiled, sending chills down her spine
'The journey begins tonight, Mistress.' And he sighs
'The first of what you must do, is that you die.'

Drooling and panting, like dogs, they were
Screaming and jumping on her, her death did occur
Tearing flesh and torture over and over again
The Dark One laughed, as they killed her, his henchmen

Her spirit pulled out of her body as she finally died
Looking around the room, she walked over to him and stood at his side
They watched as they devoured her body piece by piece
To her silent horror, as she watched, she found a quiet peace

The Dark One looked to her as the room began to change
She watched silently, and began to feel strange
Lightning pierced the sky and thunder rolled deep
Blinking her eyes, she found herself in the storm's keep

Lightning webbed through her and she felt a surge of power
Her spirit throbbed painfully and began to alter
Screaming with pain, the lightning continued its assault
Her eyes burned silver as her power began to exalt

Dropping, falling through the night sky
Wings of black helped her to fly
Screaming in pain, covered in sweat
She sat up in bed, a dream - relieved - until their eyes met.
