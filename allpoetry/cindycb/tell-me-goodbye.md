---
title: "Tell Me Goodbye"
writer: "CindyCB"
slug: "tell-me-goodbye"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/581-Tell-Me-Goodbye-by-CindyCB"
---

you make me sick
physically ill
I, 
the one who keeps it all inside,
let it all out for you
my words of desperation
and need
needy
wanting
bitch
is that what you're thinking?
I have no clue
I can only assume
that your silence
is a way of saying
leave me the hell alone
oh I fucked up big time on this one
didn't I?
unlocked my soul
and let it fly free
for you
let my walls down
which, as you know
were built so high and strong
your words of wanting
the perfect woman
who would listen
to your tales of woe and distress
I was there
I listened
I am starting to realize
it didn't have to be me
but i was at the
right place
right time
how much more wrong can that be?
it could've been anyone right?
maybe this is too much for you to handle
you probably couldn't stand to look 
into my eyes
afraid they'd still melt your heart?
well they won't
they are cold
they feel no love
or joy
or need
for you
anymore
let's see how much of a man you really are
ouside the sheets
tell me goodbye
I won't cry
