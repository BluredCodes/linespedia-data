freaks and geeksand weed aboundlike furniture left on the curbeveryone slows downto watch this madness soberwould surely make you insanea pill for thisa pill for thata drink at sunriseis it your first or last?doesn't matterthe hours could be minutesthe months could be dayswill you look back on thisin your golden yearsand think oh God what a waste?or will you be forever youngdying in your drug induced fun?
