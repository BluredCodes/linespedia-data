What's the point of whispers in my ear?
By the sea
I bleed for you
I fear
Your whispered words
The truth you say
The lies I find
Do our lives really need to be intertwined?
Eternally you say
I need to let you go
I dont want to let you go
My heart holds the strings
And you seem to have misplaced the scissors
once again
