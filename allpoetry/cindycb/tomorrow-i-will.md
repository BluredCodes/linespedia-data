title: "Tomorrow I Will"
writer: "CindyCB"

Tomorrow I will fly free
with the clouds beneath my feet
slide on rainbows
catch sunrays with every heartbeat

Tomorrow I will love you
with a passion so deep
wrap my arms around your soul
broken hearts are not for keeps

Tomorrow I will work
to make my aura brighter
radiate peace and love
to make my world and yours a bit brighter

Tomorrow I will not live 
in fear about the past
but look to the future
so that my todays can last

Tomorrow I will wake up
and the darkness will be gone
but tonight i need to write
for the darkness is my song
