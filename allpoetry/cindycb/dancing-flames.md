Years of what she could never call livingIn this house that was not a homeEchoes of rage-filled passiongrafitto the yellowed wallsShe has taken all she needsleaving everything behindStriking the match as she walks out the doorTurning backShe whispers goodbyeand sees the flames dancing for joy.
