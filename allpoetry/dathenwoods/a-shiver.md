So swiftly turning the dusk falls.
Mindlessly wondering, and pondering away
The sun vastly pouring into the night.
A chill begins to hum.

Echoing coldly, chanting fiercely through my veins.
A blood stained and tainted wind crawls on my spine.
An Erie silence creeps slowly to my door,

Their drums beat louder now
Crying, raging and pulsing in my mind
I shiver in a trance of fear.Written July 17th, 2001 ©  on Oct 03 2001 07:34 AM PST   angst
