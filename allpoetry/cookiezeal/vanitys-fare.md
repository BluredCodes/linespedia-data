title: "'Vanity's Fare'"
writer: "CookieZeal"

My breath's siphoned ,

and they took it

the words and all

those poesy misers

all consumed

the glimpse of my birth

a peek of growth 

of my history

not censored by

a thing adored

but stripped its bloom

in selfish grasp

for other's view-



And time ticks to

the pit

and pendulum

its rapture

the beginning

to biting end-

Stolen bits

cost naught to share

trusting the invite,

coveted still

encrypted rape

of the heart!(Dearest writers...protect your heart!)*Referencing those who covet the true artist's written word!
