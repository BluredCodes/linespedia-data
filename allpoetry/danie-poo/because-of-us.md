my love is gone because of youmy hope is gone because of youmy strength is gone because of youmy wisdom is gone because of youmy happiness is gone because of youmy laughter is gone because of youbut because of me you seebecause of me you will love againbecause of me you will hope againbecause of me you will have strength againbecause of me you will have wisdombecause of me toy will find happinessbecause of me you will find laughterand in the end we'll be all these things and more
