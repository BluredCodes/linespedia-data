---
title: "Skating"

My heart races 
As I do up my lases
When I skate
I am never late
I steep on the ice
The feeling i have has no price
I skate around 
My blades make the only sound
Sure i fall
But at least no one judges me cause I'm to tall or too small
Its only the ice and me
Sure other people come to see
I skate my best
I put my body to the test
At the end of the day
I always want to stay
Sure there are up's and downs
And I sometimes fall with a pound
I pick myself up off the ice
And I do it again, sometimes twice
I try so hard to do my best
So that I'll be ready for the test
Someday i hope to make it to the olympic  stand
But that's only my plan
The ice makes me a hole
Because it has captured my soul
For i skate for me
And that's the way it will always be
