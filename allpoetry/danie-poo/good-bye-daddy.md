title: "Good-bye daddy"
writer: "danie poo"

When I am about to cry
you always seem to be by my side
when I feel alone you are there beside me
I know u can see me too
but I can not see you
even though u are gone I know you love me too
You let me see the world through your eyes
I never told you any lies
My daddy you were
I wish they had a cure
I know they did all they could to help you
I know you would have made them do the same for me too
you always had a smile on your face it seems
But now I only see it in my dreams
oh daddy I cry for you
I  hope u love me too
good bye
I even had to see you die
someday we’ll me again
my heart will then mend
in your arms I’ll be safe again
my love I will send
