A world of love has no hatea world of hate has little lovebut anger for one self, and others arounda time to die is a time to liveand though we have losta soul has been foundbut does the best have to come out in peoplewhen people do the worst to othersdo people see this hate and confusionor do we have to open there eyes morebut now, we the people have to be heardand i will yell on top of the highest mountainand scream until my loungs bleedWe will be free and in being freewe will love and forgive and trust and hold and seemy life is gone for i chouse to help the victimsthe lives lost are souls that will now singfor us to learn the horrier they saw in there short times livedbut to live in peace and happnesswe will speack together in harmony
