I can see a manI cry as he reaches for my handI try to move awayI don't want to go home I prayI knew the man could see my painhe could see it was driving me insaneyet there was nothing he could do for methrough my eyes he would never be able to seeI try hard to be strong even though everything would go wrongthe guy who was therereally did carehe was my friendmy heart he could always mendhe was always there he always caredhe helped me through hardwhen I was hurt he would send me a cardtelling me it would be alright in the endwe have been friends since we were tenI loved him and I will always love him
