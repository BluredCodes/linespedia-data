title: "eyes"

they make you see
through tears and love
and friendship
seeing your mommy and daddy 
you are sure your going to be ok
what if you couldn't see 
nothing but darkness
what do you do
do you ran
where though
mom? dad?
can anyone hear me
then my eyes open
as I look
I see me
I lay on the bed
my mom crying
so is my dad
 the doctor
looks at me with sad eyes
I'm sorry
I leave you now only to say
I love you
