My tears sing me to sleepas I lay in my bed wishing you were herethough you are gone I feel a piece of me gone with youI close my eyes to see you standing there smiling but when I open them you are gonelike a mountain tumbling on top of melounges bleed screaming your nameand my eyes burn like fireand my heart feels as though a million knives are stabbing mewhy you left I'll never knowI wish I could have gone with youI don't knwo where I standI try to find my place in this lifebut it is too much to handleI remember the words you saida brand new day brings brand new surpriseswell I have had my last brand new surprise Alone I fallalone I prayalone I sayalone I cryalone I singalone I dietogether we meet againtogether we sing together we livenot here on earth but with our soulswe live on together
