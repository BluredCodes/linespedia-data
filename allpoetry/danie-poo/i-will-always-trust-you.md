title: "I will always trust you"

When I fall I hope you catch me
when I’m blind I hope you’ll let me see
when it gets hard for me
my friend I hope you’ll be
I hope you’ll never leave me
when I feel locked up you are my key
I remember when you use to sing to me
we even use to dress up and drink fake tea
best friends forever
when will the road end? Never
You know me so well
my secrets you will never tell
You were never ever mean
you always understood it seemed
you always had a smile
that stretch out a mile
things are going to get better you say
though I feel my body lay
I’m not even sure I can stay 
they said I would last past may
our friendship is what kept me alive
I know you ever since I was five
 you always had the answer 
yet now...I have died of cancer
I will still be there
and I will still care
now I must go
I wish it wasn’t so
