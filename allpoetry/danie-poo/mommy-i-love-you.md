title: "MOMMY I love you"

I look up to you
you probably did the same to your mom too
You are so pretty to me
through your eyes I wish I could see
you always were strong
because you were my mom
there was never a problem you never fixed
even though you were feeling sick
You seem to be always by my side
My lies I could never hide
I told you everything
I always loved the songs you use to sing
Thank you mommy for making me who I am
even though I was never always a prefect lamb
best friends me are
someday I’ll ask for your car
for now all I need is your love and hope
you always helped me to cope
when things went wrong
you were always the perfect mom
Mommy I will always love you
because you help me get through
god times and bad
even when I was happy and sad
yet you were always there when I needed you the most
even though you never always had the best pot roast
you always picked me up when I was down
when you lay me to sleep you never made a sound
I will never forget our memories
you always told me to say please
so will you please keep loving me
you even told me I was pretty
and I always seem to believe you
You always helped me through
today is the day we celebrate all you did for us everyday
even though we only celebrate it once a day in may
mommy I love you
