Bare feet,
shuffling through an old house
in the last lingering hour of night.
Drawn to a narrow window,
facing the anxious eyes of a never ending dawn
with nothing to reach, and nothing to attain.
Dull hands open dusty blinds
and there, in winter grey and grim,
a nameless man hobbles down the road,
in the cold rain of a black and bloodless day.
