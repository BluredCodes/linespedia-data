I tasted my tears
Running down and falling
Past my stubborn heart.
I told myself I wasn't crying,
I told myself I wasn't 
Running away.
Constellations of lies in
My illogical mind
Formed a new sky of sadness,
Raining down acid mental tears.
I slipped into the eyes of a stranger,
I don't know the meaning of love,
I just needed to escape, or so
I told myself, not run away. 
I put my fingers through a 
Candle flame to wake myself up
But there was nothing left to wake, 
I washed away in my own tears.
