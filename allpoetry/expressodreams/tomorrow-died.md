title: "Tomorrow Died"

Daisy chain lovers in a secret garden
Wilt like the flowers when fall comes
Because he turns when the leaves turn
And she cries when the rain runs.

Never look back don’t look ahead
Look up look down side to side
Live for the moment now
You won’t cry ‘cause tomorrow died.

Blood and chocolate are my drink
When it’s cold out there cold in here
Snow on my bed on my lawn
Close your eyes sleep lonely dear.

Crying won’t fix it now
It died he died she died
Past doesnt matter anymore
It’s gone and yesterday lied.
