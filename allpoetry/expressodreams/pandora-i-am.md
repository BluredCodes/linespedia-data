---
title: "Pandora I Am"
writer: "expressodreams"

Pandora opened her box
and let the Sorrows out 
but the Hope that stayed to comfort
brought no relief
because Hope never allows escape.
Catching his scent and wondering
if he saw her lift the lid,
mad with longing, 
chasing the Sorrows
that will not go back in the box
as she sinks deeper
deeper
deeper
into Hope's false promises.
