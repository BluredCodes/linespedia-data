Biting my lips,
but your kiss tastes better than blood.
Salting my wounds,
but the pain you left me overwhelms.
Wind in the trees, sighing...
but all I hear is your voice. 
Dreams inside can't replace
the expression on your face,
the one I never saw.
I'm still holding my breath,
is there any hope left?
It's far too sunny out there,
the peaceful snow melted.
There's nothing left to drown in.
I miss your eyes, 
deeper than any ocean.i know it's dull and sappy, but I had to get that out of my system.
