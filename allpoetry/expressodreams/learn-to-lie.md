---
title: "Learn to Lie"
writer: "expressodreams"

Learn to lie 
because they'll try to cut you down.
Say it's ok,
no matter how much it hurts
or they'll cut you again.
Laugh-when they pour the salt-
laugh yourself to tears.
Kiss them, take them to your bed,
pull the dagger out-
give what you get,
and say you never got.
Keep your friends close
and your enemies within your arms,
make them cry
and say they laughed,
make them bleed
and say you never did.
Make them lie
and make them believe.
Make them fall...
and say you love them all.
