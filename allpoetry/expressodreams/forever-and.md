---
title: "Forever and"
writer: "expressodreams"

Remember when
we said
forever and a second [baby]
forever and a minute [baby]
forever and an hour [baby]
forever and a day [baby]
forever and a week [baby]
forever and a month [baby]
forever and a year [baby]
forever and a decade [baby]
forever and a century [baby]
forever and a millenium [baby]
...forever and eternity [baby]...
but
forever and a moment [baby],
I'm all alone.
