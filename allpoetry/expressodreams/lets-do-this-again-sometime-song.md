Your angel never meant to hurt you,
your angel came around to wipe your tears,
to lift your heart
and chase away your fears.
But his wings are unfolding,
he's cutting the rope you're holding,
and he say's he's coming back 
to do this again.

And he says,
let's get some coffee
let's get some cake
let's take a long walk,
tell me what made you break.

Hey baby let's do this again
some time.

But now it's too clear
that he just meant too well,
he wined you and dined you
and you never could tell
that he didn't want to stay.
He's gonna go away.
He say's he's coming back
to do this again.

And he says,
let's get some tickets
and go see the show,
tell me why you cry at night,
what brought you so low.

Hey baby let's do this again 
some time.

A shoulder to cry on
when the tears come in the dream,
someone to hold you,
but things aren't what they seem.
Let him go, he's gotta fly away.
Let him go, angels were never meant to stay.
He say's he's coming back 
to do this again.

And he says,
Let's do this again some time
Let's do this again some time
Let's do this again...
We'll do this again,
I'm coming back to do this again
some time.think of Alanis Morissette singing that...that's how it's supposed to sound ok?
