title: "If the Dream Never Ends"
writer: "expressodreams"

It's a glass bead chain of words,
don't shatter the moment, please,
I may want to remember this someday.
The trees, too young for a fairy tale,
their spirits are adolescent as I.
Burning moon,no borrowed light tonight,
stars I can't quite see, except in your eyes;
where in the world is our sunset?
I know your story
and come back for more,
serve me your heart on a crystal platter.
Where have you been all my life,
this soap bubble moment is ours alone.
Diamonds in the wasteland 
have found us tonight.
Without a white horse, 
my prince has come.
I'm begging for a happy end,
on my knees in the silver garden of love,
I'll sleep forever if the dream never ends.
Where will I be when Eden falls, 
and your arms no longer hold me close?
Counting glass beads and tears...impulse poem, no editing. what do you think?
