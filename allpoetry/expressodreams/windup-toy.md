Lift you up and drop you down
It doesn't matter.
Stop the clock and leave you there
It doesn't matter.
Ignore you until you're needed
It never mattered.
Because you're a windup toy
And you can't feel a thing.

Endless repetitions of one day
Does it matter?
Suppress the poetry in my soul
Does it matter?
Tear out the silver lining of my cloud
Oh it matters.
Because I'm not a windup toy
And I can feel you're pain.

Melt down your individuality
It should matter.
Forget that you exist
It should matter.
Leave your thoughts to die
It will matter.
Because you don't have to be a windup toy
And you can feel your life.
