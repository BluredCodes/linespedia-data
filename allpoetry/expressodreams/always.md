title: "Always"
writer: "expressodreams"

Dead on this altar
I feel brave again
Like the lone survivor of
An unearthly holocaust:
Unbound screaming detatched.
This body is burning but 
I cannot feel the crumbling flesh.
I am robed in ashes and 
Crowned in flames like starlight,
My life is lost but I remain.
The world is mine but
All I ever wanted was you.

An eternal dance of love and hate
I wish for you, you come and go,
I cry and you are my death.
Begin again do you remember
The things you say each
Turn of the wheel? I do:
Forever love entwined.
Lies that I love I fall every time,
You never bother to help me up.
Tears: ocean, blood, storm, nectar, fire;
Mine fall enternally to light your face but
You cried just once, was it for me?
