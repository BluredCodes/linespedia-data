Fiction is the way you live
Inside of all you have to give
Two secret worlds collide
I believe we could coincide
Fiction story fantasy
Tell me what you want to be
Reality blowing fast away
Dance the hidden sunpath ray
Cosmic moonlight karma love
Underneath the stars above
Lay me down and don't let go
Deep in silky depths below
Now we fly one two three
Keep my heart you have the key
Drowning out the screaming
With whispers and vivid dreaming
Hearts traded time reversed
Deep sea passion pearl immersed
Be my air and I'll be yours
Flowing love on ocean shores
Inside the minds descant beat
Mirror souls melting heat
Locked together purified down
Glitter candle stars a crown
