Autopsies of failed
Experimental romances-
The people want to know
Why?
Dissect my heart and 
Feed it to garrulous 
Media vultures.
Not much to do
It was broken anyway.
Repair it with sugary
Sticky dripping sweet obscenities
But however delicious words are
I don’t care it’s not you.
Ambiguous promises don’t 
Make me want you
I despise you and your chivalry.
I live in stormy mountains
Not sunlit meadows.
