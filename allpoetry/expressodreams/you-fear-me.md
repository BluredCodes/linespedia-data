title: "You Fear Me"

You think I'm not angry?
But deep inside you
Scream to yourself
Desperate to escape my rage.
Pray to the hellbound angels
That carry your tarnished dreams on
Their bloodstained, tattered wings
Beg them to cut me
Down with their soot-black swords
And cast my body dripping blood
Into the deepest abyss away
From true night and
Into false darkness surrounded
By blinding lights.
And I will burn burn burn
Until my screaming soul rises
Once again to 
Break you.
