Cold eyes that could freeze the stars
Bore into me like serpents' fangs.
Words that sear my heart
Drip like venom in my ears.
Do you guess what I feel? 
If you don't, I won't let you know.
I'll hide it within.
In the dark corners of my heart
My anger is concealed.
Frozen rage suppressed by will.
You're not going to listen,
So I won't bother to scream.
