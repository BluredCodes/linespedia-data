title: "Vampire Of My Heart"
writer: "expressodreams"

I've never seen anything like your eyes
(shining in the dark)
witness to eternity,
vampire of my heart...

I'll lend you my red liquid life
if you help me touch the stars
my life is too short without your kiss
vampire of my heart...

come to me in the darkness, 
with the red ribbons coming apart
I'm waiting just for your embrace,
vampire of my heart...
