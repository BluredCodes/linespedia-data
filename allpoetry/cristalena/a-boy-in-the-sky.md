title: "A Boy in the Sky"
writer: "cristalena"

There is a boy I know,Whose face is a sky.His facial expressions,The colors of a sunset.His words shine on me,They're a soft rainbow's light.His kisses are lightning bolts,Covering my face with sweet electricity.His eyes are stars,Sparkling under a seaOf long lashes andEyelid moons.He smiles a sun,Bright and warm and comforting.When the sun rises,He warms the whole sky with light.His sadness is a grey dayOf clouds and storms.The sun doesn't shine. His tears, the raindrops of his heart.But the sun will come again,Shining more brightly.Because pain brings happiness,And the storms bring brightness once more. There is a boy I know Whose face is a sky
