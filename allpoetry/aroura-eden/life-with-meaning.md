Drained am I of life its self
I find myself breathing 
    but I need it not to improve my health
Tears fall frome my eyes
    but they are mearly malfunctioning body fluid leaks in disguise
Full filled are my words 
    but they (to others) form no meaning
In terms of life 
    I'm just dealing

With every sparrow that falls
With every voice that calls
I only take another step towards a brick wall

And this brick is solid 
Too thick to brake
For the mortor is made of lies and heart aches
Poor morals
Bad Judgment
Whored bodies
And my covets

I just feel like a crash dumby
    waiting for my test 
But as time passes me by
    I will just lay here on my back collecting dust on my chest

I find my self wondering when I will be set free
    and why am I here except to be me
There has to be a greater power to crush my spirts
    and to lift them
This is the one I fear 
    and soon hope to find
I can't wait to see him so that he might bind my heart and soul 
    so that they will intwine
And this will fill my life to the fullest 
So that I will be known for being me not just a manikinThis how I feel a lot of the time. 
Can you grade me.  How am I doing?
