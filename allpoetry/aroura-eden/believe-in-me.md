---
title: "Believe in Me"
writer: "Aroura Eden"
slug: "believe-in-me"

Believe me when I say I can 
I can 
and I will
I have done it before 
I can do it again

I can accomplish
I will succeed
I will strive
and will achieve

Broken are your promises
and truths
You were once able
You still are
Yet you claim you don't believe
Believe in yourself
And believe in me too
Together we can conquer the world 
We will succeed
I know We can 

We must Persist
You must have faith
Have faith in you 
Have faith in me
You can strive for the best 
I must be the best 
I got that from you
You can see that, right?

You know I have ambition
I got that from you too

I can't wait for you to come around 
I have to go for my goals now
But I would really love your blessing
Or maybe even your company along the way 
The two of us can have the moonI wrote this to someone I love.  I hope that one day they will believe in me.
