These shadows of mortality 
they cloud our vision of the truth,

We constantly ponder out future
the haze and out death,

Will it be painful
will it be quick
will it be in vain
will it count,

Is the light at the end of the tunel far away
to far to walk
or run
or fly,

Life puts us only a few steps away from the eternal sleep
that is only a cat nap away from judgment,

Why do some people go through life searching 
to cure the human of his faital disease
mortality,

But in reality we anticipate death
subconsciencely expect it,

Each day it drives us on,

We have to catch our mortal dreams before time runs out
before they are out of reach
before we die,

Without death what would life be?

Without death what challenge would come to pass?

Without death is without intrest?

That's the deadly truth.I wrote this when I was about twelve.  I had many theories, this was just one of them.  I just found it in a stack of papers lying in my room, thought I might see what you thought of this little girl.
Roury
