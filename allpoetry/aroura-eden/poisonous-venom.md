The snake bite
It burns
It stings my pride

(Grrrrr, Blast....)
(BLAST!!!)

My cheast heaves
The venom is going straight to my heart
Blast
Why must they all turn against me

****But you know they are doing the right thing****

But why now
Now when I'm so close to being the best
Now another one, wait, no more~THERE ARE TWO~, to take up space

****Your being so selfish****

Only to an exctent
I guess my boundaries are endless
But why must it be now
Cann't it wait a year

****Your a moster!****

Only to an exctent
You know were I'm coming from
You know because you are me
Don't turn your back on what we both have worked so hard for

****Just look at their faces****

No!
NO! I won't

****They need you, they need you more now then ever****

Why does this snake bite me now
It will strike the same time ever two years 
untill I'm out

****It's not a SNAKE, it's compasion and love****

Your asking greed to let this one go?!

****It will just be a year, only a year****

The most important year......  That's all...I spit on this, but my other side uses it to shine.  I'm so selfish!  Blast!  Just make it stop......  I had a predicament I jamed my self into today......  Just venting.  Damn it!!!
Roury
