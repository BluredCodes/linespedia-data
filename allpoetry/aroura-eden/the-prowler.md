title: "The Prowler"

This is what crawls in my dreams,

This tease
This lust,

Smiling appealingly
Touching me tenderly
Climbing me furiously
Devouring me savagely,

Be one with me
Please torture me
But please be kind to me.Dreams are sick minded, but you got'a love'em.
Roury
