---
title: "Use Me to Destroy Me"
writer: "Aroura Eden"
slug: "use-me-to-destroy-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/26937-Use-Me-to-Destroy-Me-by-Aroura-Eden"
---

Build me up with emotion
Construct me with feeling 
Evolve me with desire 
Transform me with lust
Program me to taste you
Expand my mind to dream
Forge me with passion

Demolish me with devotion
Excavate my remains with concern
Bury me in pity
Drown me in tears
Experiment with my full filled heart
Tear me down with love
