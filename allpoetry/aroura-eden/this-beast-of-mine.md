I fear this creature of my past 
She's strong and violent
She's perged of a tender grasp.

There was no reason for why she had become this Beast
You could satisfiy any king with her life's feast
All whom followed and all who passed by always looked upon her with envious eyes
She must not have realized what she had 
But instead she disided to be bad.

She snuck around in the cloack of night 
Disrupting her own soul's sight
As time had passed her nights grew longer 
More times often, they pressed on stronger
Lights in her world began to fade
Fights in her universe became more like an arcade
As life had passed, here existance was filled 
with screams from each of her kills.

Part of her knew that this was wrong
Part of her knew that she was strong
So she turned aside her villianious ways
She stired straight for home and lived the last of her evil days
Once her jorney had end she sang her old song,
not realizing how long she had acctually been gone
Life had changed for every one, but she soon found were she belonged.

I desided to hide her away from all I love
I would save them from the fright of.

There were things that slowly released her 
These are the things that eventually unleashed her.

What your asking from me is the key 
If I give it to you this Beast of mine will be free.
