The pure formula 
It stings your own eyes to think 
The words that come 
The words that go
The confusion in your mind 
Your tongue is not so sweet
But, all the same, you must have leave
You only said it because of the heat in the room 
The rage in the air
You only said it because of the moment
(right?)

The moment is as its self a hell all its own
It causes you not to think 
And it makes you say things you really don’t mean
(I hope that is it, I hope that is all)
But what has been said can not be taken back
I have felt it
I know its fury
This feeling reeks of filth
I know you meant most of it
Those words you would not have spoken in sound mind
(Or were you)
But they came
It scares me to hear those words
And further more it terrifies me to repeat them
I won’t, I won’t repeat them

But just so you know I will not turn my back on this
I will remember what you said
And I will dwell on these words
It will make me sick
But that’s just me
That’s the way I amA certain someone said something to me, which will not be repeated in order to eliminate it from my mind,and I have to let it out some way.  So this is how it has become.  This is my explination for what he said and why he said it.  I hope I was not the cause of his anger.  I'm dwelling again I have to let this go.
Roury
