He came with such a vengeance,his words to destroy and tear apart..A bitterly sad little boy lost,suffering from a broken heart..Love came through,in the form of an unseen face..She spread her invisible wings,and propelled upon him,her Angel grace.His words no longer angry,his heart is beginning to heal,for this once little boy lost,they call the bitter pill...
