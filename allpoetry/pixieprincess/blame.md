title: "Blame"

Beneath these morbid, sorrowful skies,thy soul was banished, disintegrated..Gone without even a sad good-bye,alone in eternity with tearful eye..Remembrance: Happiness in fear..Oh how she loved to hate..Condemned to live beneath black pitch,was her pitiful, horrid fate..No more existence in this vast defecation..Freed to the cage of orb secretion..
