I am forever Babylon's whore,you're the beast in which I ride..I am in each and every one of you,In your heart, I shall abide..Instigate, premeditate our devious evil plan..Justify, don't crucify all the sins of man..The beast- crude and destructive,seven wills to make your mind..Destroying all within his path,racing against all time..Hate is the blood running his veins,flowing black as pitch..Heart as cold as ice,soul a bitter itch..Together, standing firm,spreading havoc in this nation..Chaos with a twist and turn,beyond pure imagination.......
