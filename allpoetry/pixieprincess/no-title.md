writer: "PixiePrincess"

You say you don't want to lose me,but, you're pushing me away..I feel the distance growing,each and every single day..If and when I try to speak,with all the words of my heart..You give such a simple reply,'My darling, we'll never part.'Surely you can understand,I have many insecurities..I fear I can never live up,to what you expect of me..It use to make me feel so wonderful,when you'd say I was perfection..I suppose it's this insatiable terror,of being thrown away and rejected..
