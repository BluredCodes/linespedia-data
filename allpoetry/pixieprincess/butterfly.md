Butterfly, my lovely butterfly,hold your head up high..Spread your wings,wipe the tears from your eyes..Far above the clouds you'll soar,and feel this pain, never more..Float endlessly on the wind,carry you beyond 'the end.'So fragile and delicate you truly are,upon your wings remain the scars..As you, I break free from the cocoon,realizing my life will end much too soon..But for you, beauty will prevail,mine will be eternal hell..My butterfly, take me on this flight..I'll ride upon your wingsinto the night..I shall fall softly to the ground,and break this suffering from which I'm bound..
