title: "Self"
writer: "PixiePrincess"
slug: "self"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

As I gazed upon the forest green,
peering through eyes that go unseen..
Of hatred that lies before me,
the horror of my dreams..
I scampered quite fiercely,
running with intense terror..
Feeling the rage of insanity,
realizing the heart of humanity..
As I gazed once more, 
upon the the forest green..
From these peering eyes that go unseen..
I no longer felt the terror..
Twas' my reflection in the mirror...
