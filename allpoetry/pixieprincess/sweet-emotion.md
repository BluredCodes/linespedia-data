This love for you,
I cannot hide..
It grows and deepens,
with giant stride..
Oh, how I yearn to feel your kiss,
to finally reach heaven's bliss..
To touch you with such gentleness,
and inhale your loving sweet caress..
Through the distance,
each other we've known..
Our love so alive, so pure and strong..
My heart softly whispers,
my Prince, welcome home...
