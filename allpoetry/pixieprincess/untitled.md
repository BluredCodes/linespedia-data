'Tis true, my love,you're the air that I breathe..For thou does surely,have my soul to feed..and yea, this love,forever grows and grows..Bountiful and plentiful,as roses eternal bloom..We shall seek not,the trials of man..But, go on in our own world,with our fairy tale romance..Oh yes, my darling,how I love you so..My heart anew,with passions luminous glow..Take of me in your sweet loving arms,lead me unto the forbidden chamber of your heart..Never release me, never part,for it is here I pray to abide for eternity..
