I miss your laughter,
I miss your tears..
I miss your whisper,
softly in my ear..
I miss your "white" eye,
I miss your pout..
I miss your curls,
I miss our bouts..
I miss your kiss,
your warm embrace..
I miss the beauty,
of my angel's grace..
I miss your sexy butt,
the glorious way you walk..
I miss your humor,
and the arousing way you talk..
I miss eating with you,
or watching t.v.,
the fear you have of honey bees..
I miss your poetic way with words,
the way you say "you turd."
I miss conversing with you,
all night long..
Singing the lyrics,
to a Type O' song..
I miss our moments,
the passion of making love..
Sneaking out just to do it,
beneath the stars above..
I miss your burping,
and yes, even the farts..
I miss everything about you,
especially the way you touch my heart..
