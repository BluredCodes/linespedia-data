title: "Together Again"
writer: "PixiePrincess"

For what does one do,with the calm before the storm?Contemplate perhaps,of these cold, bitter morns?Remember of the golden days of yore,linger in memories of what was once before?How I yearn for those days to come again..To feel your sweet, loving warm embrace..Your touch-so gentle as you caressed my face..My Darling, how I miss you so..How difficult it was to allow you to go..I tried to hold on to your wondrous love..But, there were other plans for you,from our Father above..My heart is speaking with words of our Lord..I'm assured we'll be together again,as we meet on the celestial shore..
