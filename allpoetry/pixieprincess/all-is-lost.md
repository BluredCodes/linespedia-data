Open thine eyes to thy heart..See the pain secreting within my every beat..I question myself:Shall thou be forever blinded?Forsake me not,I beg of thee..For death shall surely arrive at thy door..What has happened to thy freedom fighter?Promises, promises, promises!Promises are only pretty lies left to destroy a meek heart..Can thou see the realities?This world is crumbling down around me..I fear to breathe, for every breath is sheer torture..I pray dear God:Cast away the dark mud from his eyes, the tar from his heart..Allow him to see before all is deminished into oblivion..Before all is lost...
