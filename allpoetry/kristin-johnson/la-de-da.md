title: "La De Da"

We will sail summer soon,
Sing on stones with power potions
        until we fit here
        fit the hues here
        choose a hue here
   
   Bubble mumble crystal sea
   trigger gentle cedar tree

Sing on gems with sparkle tunes
        until we live there
        live and give there
     We will sail summer soon.
