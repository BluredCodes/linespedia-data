---
title: "Rhymes with \"ast\""
writer: "Kristin Johnson"
slug: "rhymes-with-ast"
source: "AllPoetry"

i visit myself in the future,
i visit myself in the past.
when i visit myself in the present,
i acknowledge a different forecast.
i take from myself in the future,
i take from myself in the past,
when i take from myself in the present,
my attempt to absorb doesn't last.
i give to myself in the future,
i give to myself in the past.
when i give to myself in the present,
my alms are most always surpassed.
i long to be back in the future,
i long to be back in the past.
as long as i'm here in the present,
i'm insane and remain this outcast.
