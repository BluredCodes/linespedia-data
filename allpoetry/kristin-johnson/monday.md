---
title: "Monday"
writer: "Kristin Johnson"
slug: "monday"

it's been a most unusual morning,
i can't seem to find my place.
most unusual morning,
i can't seem to find a suitable face
or a 'me' that i'd like to pursue.
i peer into the mirror,
i feel unsure,
but who has the time to complain?
i HOPE i'm not shallow,
but what does that prove?
and so i find someone to judge me.
one of these mornings i'll try to avoid
that cackling glass on the wall.
i'll stride out of my house
and i won't give a damn
what 'me' i've decided to show.
and you won't ask me why i look different that day,
and i will give no explanations.
i'll continue the day, i'll continue to worry,
but NOT about how they might see me.
