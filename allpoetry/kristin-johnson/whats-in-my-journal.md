---
title: "What's in my Journal"
writer: "Kristin Johnson"
slug: "whats-in-my-journal"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/144-Whats-in-my-Journal-by-Kristin-Johnson"
---

Yesterday's significant complaints,
today's meaningless joys.
puzzle pieces that i force together
mistakes that were 
beneficial in the long run
words and phrases that only 
made sense when i wrote them.
thought deposits and withdrawals,
good days and bad days. . . 
undecided days.
ticket stubs and laffy taffy jokes,
clippings from rolling stone.
dream connections,
reviews of anything and everything.
challenges i thought might kill me,
beliefs that i constantly revise,
fickle ideas and
everyday consistencies.
