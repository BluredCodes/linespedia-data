title: "Impression"
writer: "Kristin Johnson"

You have a brilliance,
it's dripping down on me.
It rolls and slides and 
slips around
and leaves a potent scent-
the aroma of an influence.
You have a fervor,
it's seeping into me.
It layers on and covers up and
soon becomes my skin 
and leaves a glowing glimmer-
the reflection of an impact.
You have a talent,
it's hanging over me.
it sways and skips; an
arabesque, 
the dance of difference made.
