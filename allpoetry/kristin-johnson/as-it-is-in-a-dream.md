---
title: "As it is in a Dream"
writer: "Kristin Johnson"
slug: "as-it-is-in-a-dream"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/145-As-it-is-in-a-Dream-by-Kristin-Johnson"

my pillow
is a porthole to
another space, an arena
that has no maximum capacity,
no stubborn ceiling to determine me.
awake, i adopt reserve.
i follow circumstance and the various
conditions that hold me back
and prevent me from exploration and
time.
in sleep there are no obstacles to obey.
i can, i will 
have my way with colors and 
illustrations and words i never thought to use.
holding tight to this bravery,
resisting departure or
eventual realization of my false identity--
my life as it is in a dream.
