---
title: "An Escape"
writer: "Kristin Johnson"

This is my field of vacancy.
Ignoring angst and concern when 
I
need
to collapse,
lose myself in a pattern.
I experience elation--
dive in with naked feet and tangled tresses.
In this realm of artistry,
I feel my surroundings are enamored of me.
A disguise from surveillance
or a promise of seclusion
I will not resent possibility here.
There is no distinction 
between reality and
hope.
This is an escape from my everyday.
