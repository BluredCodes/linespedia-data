---
title: "Same Old"
writer: "Kristin Johnson"
slug: "same-old"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/141-Same-Old-by-Kristin-Johnson"
---

i hum and rustle in my brittle sheets
while hallucinations fester
i would love to shriek or 
rev my sensory engine
as i stumble through monotony
i heed the melodious sound 
of my insides growing accustomed 
to this tablecloth infested dwelling
i hiss and swish at the designs on the 
bone- or was it alabaster?- tinted walls
and overlook the warning signs of depression
i eavesdrop on the muffled clatter of inhabitants
that choose to remain nameless,
faceless,
lifeless,
careless,
while i rustle and hum in my brittle sheets again.
