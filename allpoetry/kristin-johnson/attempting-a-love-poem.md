On an intricate depiction 
of my love i could expand,
though more easily delivered 
from my tongue than from my hand.
In crafting such expressions 
of emotions that arise,
I'm reminded of the light
that you've installed into my eyes.
I can't neglect the majesty 
and grace that are a part
of the simple way you melt me
and you animate my heart.
