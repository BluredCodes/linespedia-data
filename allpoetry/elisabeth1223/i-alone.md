unable to sleep at night - 
I toss and turn
trying to shake these monsters from my mind
unable to face the day - 
I shuffle onward
dragging my feet through this meaningless life
I wear a mask to hide the pain
but beneath it lies the ugly truth
no mask can hide these scars
no doctor can cure these wounds
I alone must struggle onward
I alone must face the day and endure the night
I alone will survive
