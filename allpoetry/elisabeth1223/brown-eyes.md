She gazes into the mirror
soft brown eyes blink back
taking in all of herself

She wonders out loud
"Am I pretty?"
always wishing for something more

If only those big brown eyes could see
past the lashes and the looks
then maybe she would realize

Beauty is defined by more than the eyes.
