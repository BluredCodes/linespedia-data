Sgian Dubh



When you collect whatever I may owe,
Remember: I am heretics
And warlocks, I contain the heat
Of horrid pyres. I write a script
In indecipherable tongues:
Remember.


And when you embark on voyages with me
Remember: I am caravans
And banditry, I raze the walls
Of holy cities. I contain
Marauding troopers in my tongue:
Remember.


And when you embroil yourself within my love, 
Remember: I am pitiless 
And endless, I can contemplate
Barbarities: a rampant love
Is Babylon across the tongue:
Remember.
