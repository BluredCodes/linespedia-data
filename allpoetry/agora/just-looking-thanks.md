Just Looking Thanks


I am looking for the patron saint of concrete
I am looking for the muse of aluminum
I am looking for the next failed Messiah
I am looking for God’s lost checkbook
I am looking for the glue that holds us all together
I am looking for the rivets
The nails
I am looking for the cracks
The crannies
I am looking for the
Secret code on the back
Of the corn flake box
I am looking for the numbers
That summed together offer
More than another number
I am looking for the universal
Anaesthetic
I am looking for the ripened pain
The holiness
Beneath the stitches

I am looking
For a sign
