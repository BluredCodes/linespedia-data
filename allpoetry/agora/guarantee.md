Guarantee



I’ll do my best
but I guarantee nothing.

The galloping wind
grasps the fettered farms
in its unmothering arms.

I’ll do my best
but I guarantee nothing.

She and I complain
about the cold out there
withering the air.

I’ll do my best
but I guarantee nothing.

She and I complain
about the cold in here –
nothing to wear.

I’ll do my best
but I guarantee nothing.

When the raw sun sets
and the obligatory fire is lit
will we be warmed by it?

I do my worst
and I guarantee:

nothing.
