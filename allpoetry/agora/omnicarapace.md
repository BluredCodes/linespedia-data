Omnicarapace


Looking for the mica egg

Looking looking looking looking
For the cutmaking keening voice
Under the molasses rituals

Looking under the cloak of flesh
For the nub of holiness inside the snake

Looking in looking glasses looking looking
For the new erupted smell of birth again

Book-looking looking
For winged wisdom with no strings attached

Looking looking through the microscope
For the pananathema

Looking O God looking for the mummified laughter
Of hardened children

Looking in nine dimensions
For the one dimension we can trust

Looking down throats of Guardians
For gizzards filled with charitable pulp

Lookie lookie here
Looking for the blown off remnants of an omnipolar star

Looking
At the downwound clocks of the Enlightenment

Looking above all below all looking
For crystal spears piercing crystal spheres

Looking looking
In the iron maiden’s hope chest for erotic doilies

Looking looking
For love crazy as abandoned dogs

Looking
As if I had
Eyes.We are a species that hunts for the impossible - the impossible love, the impossible cause, the impossible idea. Perhaps this quirk is one of our greatest virtues and greatest tragedies.
