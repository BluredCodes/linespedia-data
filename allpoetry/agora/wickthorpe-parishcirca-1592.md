Wickthorpe Parish, circa 1592






On a windy night the cross blew off the steeple
Killing Miles and Nigel
Who were buggering each other
In the bushes
By the graves.


Both were buried
Out of holy ground.


God had spoken
Everybody said –
Two naughty men were dead.


The instrument of death
Was never reaffixed.
Instead they rooted it
Deep in the graveyard sod –
A witness
To the justice
Of their unforgiving 
And unloving god.
