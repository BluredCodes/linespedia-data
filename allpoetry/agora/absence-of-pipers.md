The Absence of Pipers Will Seriously Impair The Tune



I ask you to do simple things
yet you do not do them
I ask you to do impossible things
yet you do not do them
I ask you to do simply impossible things
yet you do not do them.

I ask you to restore the shrouds to the corpses in your catacombs
yet you do not do it
I ask you to reinvent and renegotiate your ancestry
yet you do not do it
I ask you to destroy the names of all things I abhor
yet you do not do it
I ask you to donate an organ system to me
yet you do not do it
I ask you to reconcile a paradox, any bloody paradox
yet you do not do it
I ask you to prove I have made you vastly superhuman
yet you do not do it
I ask you to intercede with the Blessed Virgin Helga on my behalf
yet you do not do it
I ask you to make a bone-flute from a perfect feather
yet you do not do it
I ask you to uncover the wells of the celestial drinking beasts
yet you do not do it
I ask you to make the sun and moon a trifle more obedient
yet you do not do it
I ask you to burrow into the molten core of you
yet you do not do it
I ask you to burrow into the murky core of me
yet you do not do it

yet you do not do it
yet you do not do
yet you do not
yet you do
yet you
yet
.
