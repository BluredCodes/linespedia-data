Getting Through the Day



Let there be truce.

You in your armed camp
me in my bastion.

Let there be truce.

Let our tongues talk words
of false civility.

Let us pretend
we have no
history

or

let there be war

something feeding on the soured passion
lying between us like a field
of fetid mines

or

let there be truth:

I warn,
we cannot be
reborn.
