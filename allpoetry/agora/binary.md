Binary




Sometime you were born
And sometime dreamed.
You were incarnate
Or so it seemed.



Sometime you are dead
Sometime alive.
You will never make it
Yet you will survive.
Wreathes and condolences
Do/do not arrive.



You are the eyes of others
You are your own
You are soft as sympathy
Hard as bone.



(My one my sometime love,
check one of the above).
