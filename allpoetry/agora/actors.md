Actors



Fine thin petals fall
From you.

           You carry
Flowers and razors
In your mouth.

           At times
Your open fingers
Cut along my spine
At times they simper
Limpid songs.

          I play 
A mendicant I
Hold your hand

          I hurt
A hundred hurts. You
Kindle me.

          I burn
And fine soft ashes
Fall from you.
             .
             .
             .
             .
