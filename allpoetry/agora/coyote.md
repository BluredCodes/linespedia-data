COYOTE


Coyote in the grass -
a flight of light,
light-footed,
sleek, composed.


Coyote in the grass -
alert, bright-eyed,
watching all that moves -
moves like a plate of air
over the field.


Coyote in the grass -
the quarry is a partner
and the silent dance begins
over the sensate ground.


Coyote in the heart -
you are the endless hunt
the restless hunt -
you capture me
and I am satisfied............................................
