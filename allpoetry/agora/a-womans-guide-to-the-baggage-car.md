A Woman’s Guide to the Baggage Car


Men do this.
It is not right but
men do this.

They craft an angelic You
white impossibly woman-white
discarnate
mother of all mothering
an impossible You.

They craft a demonic You
red impossibly woman-red
a magnet of carnality
unfaithful
impossibly untrue.

In between
unseen,
the You like all of us,
tentative
a bag of flesh
a bag of holiness
looking forever
for a green
safe anchorage

Looking for the lover,
one whose eyes
see only
green.
