She Who Walks With



Weedjeewaugun
     do not walk before me
     do not walk behind me
     do not walk below me
     do not walk above me,
     walk with me
Weedjeewaugun
     do not walk in fear of me
     do not deride me
     do not imitate me
     do not run away from me,
     walk with me
Weedjeewaugun
     do not lie to me
     do not lie for me
     do not strip me
     do not clothe me,
     walk with me
Weedjeewaugun
     be the bird beside me
     be the fish inside me
     be the sky around me,
     walk with me
Weedjeewaugun."Weedjeewaugun" is the Anishnabeg (Chippewa) word for spouse, meaning "she (or he) who walks with me"
