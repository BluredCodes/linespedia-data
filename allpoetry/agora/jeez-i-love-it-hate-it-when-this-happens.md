Jeez I Love it / Hate It When This Happens



The 
lovers
proclaimed
a state of love.
Announcements
were made to their
assembled families.
Legislatures of the soul
Passed heartfelt resolutions
of congratulation. Former lovers
were found and killed: their services
were no longer necessary.

The 
lovers
proclaimed
a state of fear.
Watching each other’s
every moment, every motion,
each suspected counterfeit bills
within their overflowing vaults of love.
Forensic scientists were asked to inspect
the trail, and they found ghosts.

The 
lovers
proclaimed
a state of emergency.
Ambulances rushed to the scene
filled with sympathetic paramedics
skilled in matters of the cracking heart,
Computer programmers began the task
of finding what lethal virus of the passions
could have infected them.

The 
lovers
Proclaimed
A state of hate.
That which once was
charm was now betrayal.
They sent each other pretty
poisoned letters in the daily mail.
Each sent emissaries to the other
to negotiate the terms of war.

We 
proclaim
we are not lovers
any more.
