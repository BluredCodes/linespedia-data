Places





There are places in you
mysterious as grain and air and honey
places hidden from the passing world
places witnessed only by a pilgrim
such as I.


There are places in you
still as a calm pool
then upheaving to the thrust
of a pilgrim
such as I.


There are places in you
full of the eternal taste of woman,
tables set for the dusty darting tongue
of a pilgrim
such as I.


There are places in you
fit for the gift of hardened living flesh
a warm and quivering offering
from a pilgrim
such as I.
