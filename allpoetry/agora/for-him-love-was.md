For Him




For him
love was a crime
committed only once.


He had loved her
like air and water.
She had loved him
for a while.


For him
love was an act of being.
For her
love was strangulation.


She left,
left no address.


He heard later
much later
she had died
of cancer
or something else.


He remembered
everything of her.


Love was a sin
committed only once,
Memory a crime
committed
many times.
