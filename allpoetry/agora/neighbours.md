NEIGHBOURSThere were intense rivalries between the two countries.One cut the head off the other,the other invaded the oneand killed its women.They stopped speakingthe same languagethey stopped speakingthe same civilizationthey stopped speaking.Each raised its childrento see the other’s childrenas expendable fuel.They both lit matchesin the dark.
