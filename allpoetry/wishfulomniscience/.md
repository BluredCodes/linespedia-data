Barren yet strong, you stand
Carved with endless pain, scarred and gnarled
Above, the glorious and the beautiful glare with an
     unrelenting eye
Guarding with an unmoving hand
 
Surrounded by white, you stand
Isolated with your own thoughts, scarred and gnarled
Above, the proud and the weak glance down with a
     careless eye
Watching with an idle hand

Broken and worthless, you stand
Aged by secrets, lost and searching
Above, the shimmering and the velvet look down with a
     disapproving eye
Whispering with a ringed hand

Barren yet strong, you stand
Carved with endless pain, yet hopeful and searching
Above, the glorious and the beautiful glare with a
     weak and ignorant eye
Merely observing
