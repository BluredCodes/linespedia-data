Thine eyes shine in the darkness
As if they posess some knowlage I cannot hope to aquire
The face behind them not yet visible
Why do you mock me with those innocent eyes
What secrets they hold

Perfect form, perfect 
No jangle, not a movement
Why do you mock me with this perfection
What lies to tell

Step into the light
What is it you keep inside
Why do you mock me with your silence
What is hidden

It is I
My face is visible
But perfect, perfect
Why do you mock me with this resembleance
What is missing

Perfection is not in existance
What is it that makes you different
Why do you mock me with thine smile
The one that may mirror my own

Speak to me
Tell me your secret
Why do you mock me with this  transcendence
What lies to tell

Perfection is not in existance
What makes you different
