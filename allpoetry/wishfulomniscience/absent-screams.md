I had a dream one night.
I was sitting right in front of you
you did not see me.
You only saw the shattered window behind me
you saw the pain and heard the absent screams.
The tears you fight so hard to conceal escape from their lonely prisons
they slip slowly down to that carefully pasted smile

and the only sound in the room is the rain leaking into the room through the holes forged by life.
