Mommy dearest, you tried to protect me from this world
But only succeeded in producing a mixed up little girl
Mommy dearest, you tried to save me from myself
But succeeded only in incresaing my stealth
Mommy dearest, you tried to teach me how to listen
But only succeeded in fueling my rebellion
Mommy dearest, you tried to make me in you reliant
But succeeded only in making me more defiant
Mommy dearest, you tried to teach me paitence is a virtue
But only succeeded in fueling my self-torture
Mommy dearest, you tried to instill in me your creed
But succeeded only in making my soul bleed
Mommy dearest, you tried so hard
But you couldn't accept that I'm not who you are
