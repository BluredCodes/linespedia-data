---
title: "The dirty chicken"
writer: "coeus"

You think you bought the white alternative
to all that BBC babble
You thought you saw a great source
of necessary nourishment

You were wrong
You'll be sick
And the medicine
Will only work
on dirty chicken

Stop and think about your nourishment
before they reduce the babble
You are the only real source
of necessary alternative

You were wrong
You'll be sick
And the medicine
Will only work
on dirty chicken
