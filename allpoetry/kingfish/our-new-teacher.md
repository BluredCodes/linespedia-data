Our New Teacher

I joined a new group to see,
If we could get along, the way it should be.
Who speaks first? Who is alone?
Can we fit in a crowd? Not one of our own!

We are all of the same kind,
I guess you could say! We have an injured mind,
To be alone is so easy for me,
Why I thought it was the way it should be!


I know she is trying to break our habit,
Of being that way! I will give a grab at it.
I am not afraid to ask your name!
The thing that worries me! Will you ask the same?

To get down to it, there is nothing I don't know
I hope she will teach me the right way to go!
I am not afraid but I like to be alone
Me and my mind, as I sit at home.
                      by Keith Sherrard
