Stuck

Today I went ice fishing, with a friend of mine!
The weather was so cold, we took a bottle of wine.
The truck got stuck in the snow, way out on the ice
People were all fishing there, no one was very nice.
To all them I was a fool for bringing the truck out there
The thought of being stuck, they didn't even care!

We dug the snow all around, the wheels of that truck
Till the time we knew that the truck wasn't stuck!
We set up our tent and lit the small stove
By then the wind had came up, we were glad we had drove!
The snow it got deeper but we wern't afraid
Sitting there sipping on the wine, with the fish we played.

The day went by and on into the night 
We caught some fish then said home we will go alright!
People came from all around, looking for a ride
We said as we drove on, catch up with your pride.
Just remember if you do see, someone who needs a hand
Stop and help him next time it may be you, then you will understand!Tried to write a story but yet wrote on life
