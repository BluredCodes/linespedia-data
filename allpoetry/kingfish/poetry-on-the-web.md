Poetry On The Web.
To-day I read a poem one wrote for me
The words were thoughts and placed for me to see!
Oh sure they were not of just me my friend
They were of all the poets and the words they send.

The place that I read the poem was for all to read
The computer and the Internet is all the tools you need.
No need to search it is rather plain and easy to do
Ask the computer for poetry and this is where it sends you to!

I will make it simple, write www.allpoetry.com 
Punch enter and nothing can go wrong.
No virus nor bad words poems oh so sweet
You could read forever and no copies you will meet.

The only thing they ask is after you read
Write a few kind words for the poet to read!
Perhaps you will send one there
You see there is lots of poets who really care.
                       by Keith Sherrard
