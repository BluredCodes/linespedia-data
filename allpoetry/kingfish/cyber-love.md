Cyber LoveI wish I could write a love poem, one that is just me,How nice you make my life feel and what you made me see!How softly your fingers must touch those little keys,How tender are those words, make me feel weak at the knees.Oh yes you my cyber lover although we may never meetI will look forward to the letters because they are so sweetI may never know the warmth of your body your so far awayWe will drive each other crazy in a cyber type of a wayI may be good at poetry and may sweep you off your feetThe love this way is better because I think you are so neatMy spelling  if you can read it and it doesn’t take too longI have a letter in your email to read it all night long.                   By Kingfish to a ladyI love , love poems also
