Your Printer

I had a great printer who just loved the ink,
The printer hated me when I made it think.
I used to send it messages that it took time,
To print out the messages for this feeble brain of mine.

I threw the printer across the room one day
Broke it in oh so many ways,
I took the printer to the shop to see,
The repairman fix this printer for me?

The next day I heard the good news,
The printer I had printed now in blue.
The same day I would see,
A colour printer now belonged to me.

The problem was mine I just couldn’t see, 
A colour printer always belonged to me.
I printed in black because I thought ,
I was the one to be reminded, my printer forgot!
        by Keith Sherrard
