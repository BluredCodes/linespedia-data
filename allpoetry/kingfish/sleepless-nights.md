Sleepless Nights 

 The darker the night, the better you feel
 When you are tucked underneath the covers safe from what is real!
 Can you imagine one with problems ones with no control
 From things going on in their mind makes them lonely and old
 
 Life is not so easy for a lot of my friends
 They are on the Internet hoping some email you will send
 That keeps them going something to hold on too
 The letters you read just how many are true

 Will there ever be a tomorrow is there a future for me
 I feel so good telling stories to make you smile can you see?
 I may see your name on the internet waiting just for mine too
 Another sleepless night not knowing just what to do!
                  By Keith Sherrard
