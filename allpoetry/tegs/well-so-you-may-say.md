Well so you may say,
I'm proud to be this,
or I'm proud to be that,
Why can't you be proud to be you?

There are so many people
out there in the world, 
who are just happy to be human
why can't you be one too.

Well I'm proud to be a human,
I'm proud to have morals.
I'm proud to be able to be happy
not about what I am, but who.
