title: "New Years Resolutions"

I promise to reduce my size,
I'm getting way to fat

I promise to stop counting cals
I've had enough of that.

I promise to be more accepting
to stop judging who I don't know

I promise to start a garden
To actually get something to grow

I promise to take better care
of those I love and cherish

I promise to stop wasting money
as my bank account's about to perish.

And last of all I promise now
the most important thing should be,

to stop making all these bloody lists!
And just live life for me!
