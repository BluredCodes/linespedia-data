title: "Nothing to Say"

You don't always have to have something to say
to be worth listening too.
Sometimes the most interesting conversations
only involve a few words.
And the most pointless hypocritical mumblings
can take agonising hours.
Why am I ignored? When the thoughts through my head
are more in depth than you could ever dream about.
