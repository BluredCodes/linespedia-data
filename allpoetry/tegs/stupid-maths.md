title: "Stupid Maths"
writer: "Tegs"

The sides don't match, 
they will not stretch far enough. 
the shapes are all wrong, 
and the colours are off. 

The corners are far too sharp 
and the equations are too difficult. 
I do not understand this problem 
I am faced with. 

How do I put this together? 
there's too many sides for me to comprehend. 
To many different edges of varying lengths 
which matches with which I do not know. 

I've never understood geometry 
I've never understood my life. 
Nothing matches, nothing ever will 
I'm just thinking of dropping it all.
