---
title: "Fuck I can't love you..."
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "fuck-i-cant-love-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/14135--I-cant-love-you...-by-Tegs"
---

What the hell do you do,
when you're in love
with one of your best friends?
And then he admits to you,
(Because you're the only one he trusts)
that he loves your other best friend?
She has no clue about this,
And no feelings like that back for him.
God I wish I was her.

And I wish I wasn't so envious.
