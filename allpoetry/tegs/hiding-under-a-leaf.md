title: "Hiding under a Leaf"

Deep purple memories
reminding of mulberry faces.
Sucked in by Saphire eyes 
into the depths of insecurity.
Pure as black
and clean as dirt
fists clenched and fingers clawing
at the insides of my veins.
Surrounded by fairies
but no wings for me
breaking free is a task
far too hard for one so jaded.
