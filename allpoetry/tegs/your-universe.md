---
title: "Your Universe"
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "your-universe"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

You are the sun.
You shine down on us all
but all I feel when your rays hit me
is deathly cold.

The universe revolves around you
we all have to bath in your glory.
But one day you will swell
and consume us all.

I find your cold light,
is burning me.
You're blinding my eyes 
with your pitiful brilliance.

But you will die,
just like us all, die.
I just wish you would burn up and collapse
into your black-hole sooner.
