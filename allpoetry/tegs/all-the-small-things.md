---
title: "All the Small Things"
writer: "Tegs"

It's the little things I miss the most,
the glint of your eyes as you spin around
the smile on your face when you laugh
the way you casually put your hand in mine
the way you'll call and we'll chat for ages
the way I can look at you and the room disapeers
the way I know that you love me as much as you say.
And that, for once,
I'm sure I believe,
that my answer is true.
