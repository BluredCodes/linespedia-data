---
title: "Life's Good"
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "lifes-good"

Maybe the risks are worth the prize,
Maybe the danger is not really so bad
Maybe your terror is really unfounded
Maybe you don't have enough faith in youself.

Maybe I'm a hypocrite, who should have followed advice
Maybe those people who said 'go for it' were right
Maybe I should have just jumped off that cliff
Maybe I would have found the sweet waters beneath

Maybe the grass really is greener
Maybe the pastures are so much sweeter
Maybe some people take everything for granted,
Maybe I just don't need to be one of them.

Maybe I'm the luckiest person alive
Maybe you have enough strength for us both
Maybe I have to have more faith in myself,
Maybe all I have to do is say thanks.
