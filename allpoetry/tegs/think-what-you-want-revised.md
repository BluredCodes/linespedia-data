Well well well, how many times has the world turned since I talked to you before? Not even once. How pathetic am I? Well that depends on your opinion doesn't it... ! :) I myself don't think I'm pathetic, but that's just me. 
You make up you're own mind. 

It truely is up to the individual, but hey, isn't it what I think that's important. Some may say this isn't even a poem...but in my opinion it is. 
I am the only one that matters! (and I seem to be truely up myself!)

But hey, think what you want to think. This could just be the delusional ravings of a mad, pissed teenager, or the psychiatric brilliance of a pure genius. (But seeing as I don't think I spelled that right I'm not sure either)  Why should you pay attention at all?  But hey...do I really care ?
