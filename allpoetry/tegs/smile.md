title: "Smile"
writer: "Tegs"

Smile Once,
and people can see
you're not as dark
as you make out to be.

Smile Twice,
and those that frowned
may find a reason
to smile back now.

Smile Again
and you will see
the world is not as cruel
as you think it may be.

Keep on smiling,
and those around who felt despair,
and felt left out, may soon see
that for them we could all be there.
