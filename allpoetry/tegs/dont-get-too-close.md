title: "Don't Get Too Close"

Please don't get too close 
don't even try.
I am a pshycopathic maniac
on a suicide route 
to disaster.
Don't try and make friends with me
Because I will burn you
in an inferno of hate,
envy and confusion.
Don't try and touch me
I'll shy away and bite back
so you'll never know what happened
as you sit, 
staring at your mutilated hands.
So now that you know,
why you can't get too close,
I want you to know
one more thing.
It was you that made me this way,
so that I can never trust again.
