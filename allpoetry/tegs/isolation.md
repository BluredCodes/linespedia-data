I stand in the crowd,there's no one around.My head feels heavy,I can't touch the ground.Bars blocking my view,there's much I can't see.Be'n pulled back and forth,where should I now be.I'm not really sure about the category, it's just kind of an expression of my desperation. It was kind of inspired by the painting by Jackson Pollock, Blue Poles, and also the fact that I feel so different and am not sure where I am headed.
