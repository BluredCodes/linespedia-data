Love is...

Trust,  
that you can share your soul with someone
and know they will still respect you afterwards.
Trust that they will always be there to catch you.
But that can not be it all,
because it seems so much easier to say:
I trust you, than...
I...

Friendship,
that is the foundation of your love,
being able to rely on someone,
and tell them anything.
But that can not be it all,
as it is so much easier to say:
You're my best friend, than...
I...

Fear,
that one day you'll wake up, and find out that it was all a dream,
or some cruel joke, played on you for the amusement of others,
and you never really had that love you held so dear.
But that can not be it all,
as I was scared before,
but that was of getting you,
now I'm afraid of losing you.

Need,
to have a certain someone always there,
to speak to, to touch, to share all your fears with.
To know that they need you just as much as you need them,
maybe that is getting closer,
because though it is hard,
I can say, I need you...
Whereas it's just so hard to say...
I...
