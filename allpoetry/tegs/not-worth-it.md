---
title: "Not worth it"
writer: "Tegs"

I get ignored
I get shoved in a hole
just because I am not blonde.

I'm simply not good enough
I'm not worth your attention.
Because my face isn't beatiful.

It took me 6 months 
to get a simple hello
just because I'm not a size 6.

I wonder how many people out there,
are worth your attention at all?
