title: "La Di Da, if you couldn't guess, I'm Happy!"

Trust, is the key to everything
it leads to secrets being revealed
people becoming closer
and sometimes anguish to some concerned.
But all good things come to those 
who trust.
So don't seize up
don't be afraid
take the moment, 
and trust in the universe.
Because it could work out.
It may not, but isn't it worth a try?
I'm telling you now it is.
