title: "Veggie-mites!"

We're happy little veggie-friends
As cool as cool can be.
Be all enjoy our plates of soy
for breakfast lunch and tea.

Our mothers say we're growing paler
every single week!
Because we hate all that dead meat
We have much better things to eat,
We're losing colour from our cheeks!Do Not comment if you are not Australian/Have not heard the vegemite jingle.
