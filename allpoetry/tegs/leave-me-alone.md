---
title: "Leave me alone."
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "leave-me-alone"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Don't they get it?
Don't they realise in just a few years
we'll be on our own, out in the world.

Can't they let us,
make a few mistakes, or at least
give us some of their trust?

Stop treating me as if I don't have a clue
what the fuck I am doing.
Because I do.  I have, and always will.

It's really irritating that you seem to think
I am still 5 years old.
And need my entire day planned out by you for me.

So give me some space.
Stop asking me every 5 minutes what I am doing.
Trust me on my own a little.
Let me take responsibilty.
Let me make my own mistakes and let me learn.

Or you're just going to have to let yourself...
let go.
