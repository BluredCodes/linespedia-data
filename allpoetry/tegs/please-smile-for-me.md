---
title: "Please Smile for me."
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "please-smile-for-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

I wish you could see 
the feelings I have inside.
Because then I wouldn't have to
run the risk of telling you how I feel.
And being rejected.
