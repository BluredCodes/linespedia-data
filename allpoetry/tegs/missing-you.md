writer: "Tegs"

Wasting away without a purpose
I stare at the wall
I know what is missing.

Food has no meaning
Songs just annoying
All I'm really missing is you

It doesn't quite help
I'm so missunderstood
It claws at the walls of my heart

Stretching up to my mind
making everything futile.
Lifes only worth living for you.
