Happy New Year All.
It's a time to start again,
A time where everyones forgiven
and the whole world is one
for those few moments.

But then we all go back to normal,
and we begin blowing each other to bits,
in earnest once again.
The hatred we all find so easy to give into,
comes streaming back out.

How do you feel, facing this new year
which has no more hope than the last.
We're creating a future bleak and full of despair.
So Happy New Year everyone, 
enjoy these few moments, while they last.
