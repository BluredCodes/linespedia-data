---
title: "My world at a glance"
writer: "Tegs"

It shows how lost and alone I must be
when I find myself watching the Flintstones.
Drowning the feeling in cartoon stupidity
Lost to reality, it's too far away.
The real world has left, far beyond my grasp
it's beyond my capability to reach.
But returns to me itself, and for those few days I find
My only few moments of clarity.
