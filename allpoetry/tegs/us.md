A spark of hope to pass between usA peice of hope to share among usA cry of laughter tossed right at usA hand to calm the hate around usOur futures there, the choice is for usOur childrens lives, depending on usOur mother earth, can be saved by usOur friends and neighbours, linking with usI'm still kinda working on this one, any suggestions?
