---
title: "Simple Things"
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "simple-things"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/38550-Simple-Things-by-Tegs"
---

Simple shapes,
create the most intricate patterns.
Simple people,
create the most irritating circumstances.
Simple tastes,
can be the most pleasant
Simple memories
can be the best of all.
Simple dreams,
are more likely to come true.

But who needs simple,
when the way the sun shines on your face
is so full of colours,
and so many possibilities.
