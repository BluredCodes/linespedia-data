It's a little offputting
when everytime you talk
You're laughed at.
It's a little annoying
everytime you try be yourself
You're stepped on.
It feels pretty good
when you're finally accepted.
When you're part of the crowd.
It's feels even worse though
when you find out it was all a joke
they hate you just as much as before.
