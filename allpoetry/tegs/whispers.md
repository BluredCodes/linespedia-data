You hear it.Whispers comments,behind your back.What do they say?What do you do?It starts.Your head is whirling you've stopped eating, they've started talking again.ThinPoleFragileLooneyMessed upWeirdLostCrazyPressureSickDeludedWhyAttentionIgnoreDyingNo hopeAnorexia.
