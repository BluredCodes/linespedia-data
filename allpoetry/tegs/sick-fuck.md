---
title: "Sick Fuck"
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "sick-fuck"
source: "AllPoetry"

Go on, smile you sick fuck.About what you just did to me.The pain you just put me through,and how it will haunt me forever.Your hand prints, all over my body,almost glowing with the torment.
