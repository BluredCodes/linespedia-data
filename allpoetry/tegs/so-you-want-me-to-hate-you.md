So you wanted me to bemore fucking insulting.You wanted me to cut you,didn't think I was trying hard enough.Well here's a slap in the face,you fucking pig faced slut,Your hair so sleek, reminds me notof silk, more like a muddy river.I know why you love waking upbecause you start your day,giving oral sex to a male orangutanWhile fucking yourself at the same time.You dirty piece of distended rectum,I spend my nights dreaming of your death.Of the blood dripping through my fingers.Did this make you feel better? You Pointless Fuck Up.
