AGHHHHHHHHHH!!!!!!!!!!!!!
Sorry, you scared me.

Was it your face that brought that outburst to my lips?
Was it the horrors that you hold inside,
ready to devulge to the world, and burst my fragile bubble?
Was it the fact that I know after finding out your secret,
My world will never be the same again, for good or bad?
Or was it that I already know, and don't want to admit it to myself.

Maybe it's all four of those reasons, and more...
that I don't quite understand myself.
