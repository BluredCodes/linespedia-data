They say I should feel,
stop being so detached,
show how I feel deep inside.
but what if it's to painful
to deal with right now?
And I know it will fix itself.
So what's the problem with detachment,
if it lets you get on with life,
and feel a little normal.
It's better than crying into my hands,
and you constantly asking
what's wrong?
