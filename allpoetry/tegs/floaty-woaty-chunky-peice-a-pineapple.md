No it has wings!
It's flying!
Little wings like a canary, so soft and yellow and sweet.

I'm going to catch it!
And love it forever and ever.  It will live with me in happiness.
I will give it a magical show box to live in,
with holes so that it can breathe.
It will sleep on a silver spatula,
so that it's juice can drip through the holes
and it won't get uncomfortable in it's sleep.
I will knit it a little jumper all in green,
with a little brown wombat on the front,
(So that he won't get warm)

Ahh we will have a grand life together
Forever and ever.
I catch it...
and...

Yum!  I couldn't be bothered keeping it.  Too sweet!
Mwaaa Haaa!I swear I'm not crazy...Please I'm actually quite normal...really!
