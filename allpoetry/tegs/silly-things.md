---
title: "Silly Things"
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "silly-things"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

You fly and twist and turn through the air,
feeling so free and at peace with everything.
Total exilleration, adreniline rush
takes over your body, you're on top of the world.
But what happens when you have to come down?
Do you make the landing, gracefully or only just?
Or do you flunk it all together, and break both your legs, 
ruining your chance of ever flying again...
