---
title: "You're a bloody wordist aren't you!?!"
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "youre-a-bloody-wordist-arent-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/16578-Youre-a-bloody-wordist-arent-you----by-Tegs"

All words have their place 
and should not be discriminated against! 
If you hate swear words, 
well you are a wordist. 
Yes a wordist, 
and I'm sure those words are very hurt, 
that you won't use them just because of what they mean...
it's very harsh, 
and you should all apologise!!!
