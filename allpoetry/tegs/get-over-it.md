---
title: "Get over it."
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "get-over-it"

Stop your bitching you self-centered brat
you don't know how lucky you have it.
You live in a country free and for you
you have a decent income, a roof over your head
food on the table and someone to love you.
Imagine those people, stuck in their slums
with their bellies stuck to their backbones
and no hope for a better life, no way out.
They would give anything to be in your shoes,
even with your broken nail, and pussy pimple.
