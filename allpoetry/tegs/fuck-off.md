---
title: "Fuck Off"
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "fuck-off"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/12961--Off-by-Tegs"
---

You say you are my friend.
You seem to think you care.
But then I see you laughing.
You ask me all those questions
You seem to pay attention.
But I saw that snicker.
You smile and pretend
You give me those hugs.
But I just want to say fuck off.
