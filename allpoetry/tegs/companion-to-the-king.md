title: "Companion to the King."

My travels stretch out thousands of miles.Starting from the most humble beginnings,will only a preasant from an old hobbit.The ring that started the ball rollingthat took the first step of this terrible journey.Nights spent under the stars stretching foreverover the awe-inspiring plains of Rohan.Nights of Terror, never knowing if we will wakeIn the ink stained sky lands of Mordor,With the Dark Lord breathing down our neck.Days of bliss lying in the feilds of Lorien,with the fairie lit flowers twinkeling away.Days of contentment stretched out on Elronds lawns,with Dwarfs, Elves, and Men all as one,all happy to be of the one land, the one people.Evenings of tears, back at Hobbiton,remembering friends lost, and pain along the way.Evenings spent wondering, what lies ahead,as I look to the grey havens, a new adventure,a new world, a dream for me to complete, in my own time.
