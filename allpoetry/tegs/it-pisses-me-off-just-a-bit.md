---
title: "It pisses me off just a bit."
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "it-pisses-me-off-just-a-bit"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/20953-It-pisses-me-off-just-a-bit.-by-Tegs"
---

Why should we all be expected to feel what you feel,
just so that you don't fall further. 
Instead of you putting in some effort for once
and trying to save yourself from this 
self-inflicted torture.  
Instead of relying on us all to save you.
Because one day we won't be there,
and you'll have to pull youself out of this deep old hole,
that you seem to find pleasure in burying yourself in.
