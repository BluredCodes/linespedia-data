---
title: "My Friend"
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "my-friend"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/7970-My-Friend-by-Tegs"
---

My life is just one big circle of change,
Changing scenery, changing schools, 
changing identity, changing friends.
There's very little, that's constant anymore.
Except for you.  You know who you are,
my very best friend.
You said it yourself, it takes a lot,
for two people to stay friends this long,
so far away from each other.
I love having you there for me, 
always supporting me, always understanding me,
even though you're so far away.
I'd kill to see you again, anywhere any time,
You're both a hole in my heart, 
and the feul that keeps it beating.
Like a rockclimber, scaling a cliff,
many pebbles fall beneath her hands,
but there's always that constant rock.
That rock is you.  You keep me from falling,
you keep me safe, you keep me sane.
I have not seen you for over two years,
and yet your smile, your laugh is as fresh 
in my mind, as if we'd just laughed together.
Please stay there for me, I'll be here for you,
We can look after each other, all our life through.
