title: "Why don't you love me?"

Why don't you love me anymore?
Why don't you seem to care?
You used to laugh you used to smile
at my jokes and the clothes I would wear.

Now all you do is shout.
All you want to do is yell.
You don't seem to want to be with me.
Don't want to hear the stories I tell.

I don't think you'd care if I went.
Don't think you'd care if I was gone.
I don't know what I did, or when it happened.
Maybe now I'll make friends with this gun.
