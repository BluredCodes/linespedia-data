You know I write about you
it's how this all began.
Well I just want to say
Thankyou.
Thankyou for having the strength
to see my feelings through,
Thankyou for having more faith
in a possibility than I have
in myself.
Thankyou for giving us a chance,
Thankyou for listening at all.
