---
title: "Which Song?"
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "which-song"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/16577-Which-Song--by-Tegs"
---

I want to sing a song,
but which one should I sing?

I could sing a song of hate...
but I have too much good in my life right now.

I could sing a song of perfection...
but there's a few things I wouldn't mind changed.

I could sing a song of love...
but I'm way too inexperienced.

I could sing about nothing at all...
but what would be the point?

So how about you sing for me...
and I'll just listen, and be content.
