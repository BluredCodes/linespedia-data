---
title: "What an Idiot"
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "what-an-idiot"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Ha ha, you just fell,
how funny did that seem.
I'm being told not to laugh
at your misfortune...
but it's just so funny
seeing you were so full of yourself
before.
I know you don't deserve my pity,
and you won't notice if,
just one person laughs.
