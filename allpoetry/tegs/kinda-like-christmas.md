---
title: "Kinda like Christmas"
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "kinda-like-christmas"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/39160-Kinda-like-Christmas-by-Tegs"
---

Do you ever get that feeling,
that you got when you were a kid?
That christmas was coming,
excitement bubbling up, unstoppable.
It's like the presants are waiting there
all ready to be unwrapped.
You wonder what's inside,
or maybe you already know, 
and just want to get your hands on it all.
Well that's how I feel now.
Christmas is coming, 
and I can't wait to see my presant again!
