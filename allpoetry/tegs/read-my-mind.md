---
title: "Read my mind!"
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "read-my-mind"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/14183-Read-my-mind--by-Tegs"
---

Why can't you see
how much I love you.
How my heart would stop beating
if you weren't here for me.
And yet you don't realise,
and I'm not about to let you know.
It's my little secret
for me to know, and please won't you find out?
