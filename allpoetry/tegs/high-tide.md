---
title: "High Tide"
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "high-tide"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/49221-High-Tide-by-Tegs"
---

Submerged in you, to mix and swirl 
two oceans meet and combine as one 
boundaries melted and ignored. 
There's no definition between, 
where I end and where you begin. 
I feel your pain in your currents, 
and you feel my pleasure in tides. 
When you hold my hand bubbles encircle my heart 
they lift me and carry me, float to the surface. 
Waves carry me further than ever before 
but as long as it's into your ocean 
I know I'm safe.
