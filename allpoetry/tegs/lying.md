title: "Lying"
writer: "Tegs"

You think you're so loved,
but oh my God you're wrong.
Let me put this into perspective...
You're momma said she loved you,
but she was a fat tub of lard 
and she lied.
That guy said he loved you,
but all he wanted to do was sleep with you
and he lied.
That other chick said she loved you,
But did you see the photos all around
The lemon lied.
So you try and love yourself
try and make others adore you,
stop lying to yourself.
