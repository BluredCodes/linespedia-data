---
title: "Aw, you're the sweetest"
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "aw-youre-the-sweetest"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/45669-Aw--youre-the-sweetest-by-Tegs"
---

I used to feel alone,
with no one to talk to.
But now I have you,
and you always listen.

I used to feel hated,
ignored, and abused.
But now I have you,
and you make me feel special.

I used to feel stupid,
that no one understood,
but you laugh at all my jokes
and listen to what I have to say.

I always thought I could never find you
or perhaps you'd never find me.
But here you are.
Please never leave!
