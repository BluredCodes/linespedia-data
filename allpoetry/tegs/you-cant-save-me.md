---
title: "You can't save me."
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "you-cant-save-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/13486-You-cant-save-me.-by-Tegs"
---

Isn't it funny how people always try to save time.
When you can't put it in the bank.
You can't keep it under the mattress,
And you can't earn intrest on it.
People try and save it up, but never seem to realise
that it just keeps slipping through their fingers.
And the time they thought they saved, 
has already passed.
