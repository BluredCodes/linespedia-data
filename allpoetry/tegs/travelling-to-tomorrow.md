title: "Travelling to Tomorrow"
writer: "Tegs"

Often in my dreams
I visit a place
not unlike here.
In which cars fly,
computers talk,
and people are tolerant.

Fish wind through the clouds
and bubbles pop out from pipes.
Squiggles play across my eyes
and I see things as never before.

I see people holding hands,
repecting each other 
for who they really are,
and I'm content with humanity.

Those woken from their apathy
find pleasure in this newfound
love for peace, and friendship.
So we all can be safe, and free.
