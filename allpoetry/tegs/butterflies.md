---
title: "Butterflies"
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "butterflies"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Lightly resting, on my fingertip.Beauty, Freedom, and Love, all there as one.A positive consequence of change,something so right, in a very wrong world.The eptimome of all innocence,a gift of the clouds, to give light to life.Our herald of peace to all the world.Wings now for our hopes, a cure for despair.A giggle transcending hidden pain,our spirits lift and gather as one.A mutual love, of all the world.No matter what race, they're part of us all.
