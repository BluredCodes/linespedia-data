---
title: "Two Faces"
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "two-faces"
source: "AllPoetry"

People always have two faces,
if you're lucky you might always see one side,
hopefully it's the good one.
At least then you get no surprises.
Because when a person suddenly shows you
their other side,
it can be a terrible shock.
Whether you're suddenly seeing the sweet,
after the scary,
or the face you are now presented with,
is rotten and dripping with putrid flesh,
instead of the quiet smile you're used to.
