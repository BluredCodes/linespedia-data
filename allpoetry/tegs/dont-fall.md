Do you feel the sensation?
Boiling rising inside us...
Twisting, turning, twirling

D
O
W
N

Into the depths
Caught up in bubbles
that make my heart glow.

I'll never let go
So hold on tight,
please don't let me

D
R
O
P

Because there's a good chance
I won't be able,
to get back up.
