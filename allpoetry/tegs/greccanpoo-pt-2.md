“Ah Ha!” Cried the cat! “My journey’s complete!
I found a new queen for our realm!”
“Queen?” Cried the girl! “But where is she hiding?”
The cat smirked “But it will be you at the helm”

 Greccanpoo started, “Who do you think I am?
I’m a peasant, and tom-boy at that!
“Oh do not worry my dear Greccanpoo,
you have a good heart” Said the cat.

So the Cat and the Girl away they did fly
To the castle of the land Spekkywack.
The towers were tall and the doors they were wide
And poor Graccanpoo chucked a spack.

“I can’t be your queen, you silly feline,
This place is far too grand for me.
I’d say something stupid far more than once,
And be laughed at before you’d count to 3!”

But just at that moment, a 2 poor little girls
Came around the corner in tears,
So Greccanpoo gave them a big warm hug,
Sat them down and said “whats the problem my dears?”

They told Greccanpoo of a terrible mess
Their brother had fallen down a well.
And kissed a green frog, and turned into a log,
And had started to really smell.

Greccanpoo jumped up, And called for a rope, 
and twelve strong men to help
she then called for a witch to take off the spell
and for some deoderant she did pelt.

The young boy as a log, was heaved out of the well,
The spell it was soon taken off.
The deoderant worked, and he smelled no more
Though left with a strange sounding cough.

The cat looked at the girl who denied she was queen
And said, “Now do you see?”
You are smart and correct, and solve problems like that!
No better queen could have been found by me.”

Greccanpoo smiled, and saw he was right.
“I can do this if I try!”
So crowned the next day she was, and Spekkywak became,
The happiest kingdom no lie.
