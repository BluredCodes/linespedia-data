---
title: "Faceless Dreams"
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "faceless-dreams"
source: "AllPoetry"

Two faceless lovers, blissfully unaware
happy with their unseeing eyes
Marry and plan their life together.
Full of dreams of love and peace,
moonlit nights, warm fires,
Children, each-other, maybe a dog or two.
So many plans, so many dreams
starting with a honeymoon, to New York
On September the Eleventh.
