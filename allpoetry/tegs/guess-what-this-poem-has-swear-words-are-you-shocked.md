Well I'm gonna swear
as much as I fuck'n want!
You bloody fuck'n wordist bastards!
How dare you treat a word 
so fuck'n harshly.
I think I'll fuck'n march in the streets
to protest for their fuck'n rights.
Well don't they fuck'n deserve them?
The worlds a fuck'n cruel place,
and someone moral, and fuck'n kind
has to stand up for the assholes,
why the fuck not me huh?
