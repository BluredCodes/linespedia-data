---
title: "Crazy Dreams"
writer: "Tegs"

Do you ever wonder about dreams?
Whether they are really telling you
what you should do?
Those bubbly feelings and hazy edges
may actually have some truth.
Oh I hope so.  Because then my biggest wish
could be true...
