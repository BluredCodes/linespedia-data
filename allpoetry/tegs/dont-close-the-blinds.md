---
title: "Don't close the blinds."
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "dont-close-the-blinds"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/20986-Dont-close-the-blinds.-by-Tegs"
---

Amber bubbles, slowly flowing
Up.
Floating towards the golden brilliance
And refracting the beautiful colours.

Yellow brings us closer to the spirits
and cements our connection to the otherworld.

Green makes us aware of our surroundings
and how heavily we rely on our earth.

Red gives us the power to do what we dream
to stand up for ourselves and fulfill our destiny.

Blue is the food of dreams.  Creating the possibilites,
fueling our creativity and giving birth to ideas.

All these qualities can be found in that one ray of light
that we close off, with a flick of the wrist,
and the closing of the blinds.
