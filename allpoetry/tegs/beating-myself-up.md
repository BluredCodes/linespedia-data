---
title: "Beating myself up"
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "beating-myself-up"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/40232-Beating-myself-up-by-Tegs"
---

Strange creature crawling just under my skin
prickling and scratching trying to get out.
Trying to cause me as much pain as possible.
Well it doesn't have to work to hard.

This ripping and tearing of my arteries
will continue for 3 weeks more.
Be cured for a while and then keep going,
a continuous cycle, that will hopefully end soon.
