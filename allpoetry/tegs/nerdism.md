---
title: "Nerdism"
writer: "Tegs"

You know why you're pissed off?
You've just realised that
it's the blondes and the cool kids
that have it all figured out.
They are the ones, with the Gardian Angles
who look after them, pull them through life,
no matter how stupid they are,
no matter how many mistakes they make
no matter how screwed up they are.
And no matter how much you try,
it's never enough, you're perpetually damned
to living in a state of nerdism and alone.
