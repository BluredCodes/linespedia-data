LOL.  Laugh out loud...
Why?
This is a shallow way of shutting someone up,
of letting them know that, yes, you are paying attention.
But do you really laugh?
Do you really find it funny?
Are you really throwing your head back,
in gales of joyous laughter?
Do you just smirk, and think 
"That was amusing",
or not really think about it at all...
