---
title: "Don't tell me..."
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "dont-tell-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/13955-Dont-tell-me...-by-Tegs"

Don't tell me he's not cute
Don't tell me that's not so
Don't tell me go away
Don't tell me I don't know
Don't tell me You wouldn't understand
Don't tell me this or that
Don't tell me the rain won't fall
Don't tell me the sun will come out
Don't tell me I'm wrong
Don't tell me I don't matter.
