---
title: "Crazy People"
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "crazy-people"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/13959-Crazy-People-by-Tegs"

Can you see them
lying in the sun.
Their future peeling away?
Just because they didn't want to smell bad.
