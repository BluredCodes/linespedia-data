---
title: "Look here!"
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "look-here"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I think I've realised
the stupidity of continuing.
You were my only goal and dream
you replaced all my hopes
with your beautiful face.
Now I know I'll never have you
because your eyes are fixed
on someone else entirely.
