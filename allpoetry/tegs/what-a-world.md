---
title: "What a World"
writer: "Tegs"

What a world we live in,one that takes pride in selfishness.Takes the deepest love in hateand threatens our own individuality.Whenever you try and fight back,you are just laughed at.destroyed internally, flash flood of sicknessa hand clutching our bleeding hearts.I for one don't want to be here.Who would want to stare into the darkness,when you could be bathed in light.Happy for all eternity.
