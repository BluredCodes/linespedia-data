Ha Ha!
You thought I was gone, 
but guess what...

I've decided it was too much fun
pissing people off.

I've decided I'm saving too much money,
now I'm barely on the net.

I've decided that I miss the sweet comments
along with the bad.

I've decided that you have all gone into
a downward spiral,
without my brilliance.

So watch out...
I'm back!Ha hahahahahahahahahahahahahahahahahaha!
