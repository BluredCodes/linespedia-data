---
title: "Jump off a Cliff."
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "jump-off-a-cliff"

Boo!
Tee Hee, did I make you smile?
Did I make you laugh?
Did I make you cry?

People are all so different
you can never pick the reaction,
that you may get,
by saying one thing,
or doing another.

I thought that I knew you,
I thought you'd be scared,
I thought that you'd hate me,
But I guess I thought wrong.

So maybe we should all take more chances
and leap that hill, or jump off that cliff.
Who know's what's lying beneath,
because yes, it could be a forrest of flames,
but it could be a cool, sweet spring,
and the reward will always be worth the torment.
