---
title: "Unknowing Love"
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "unknowing-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/7387-Unknowing-Love-by-Tegs"
---

A happiness, only found in dreams
finds me and holds my heart
whenever you hold my hand.
My love for you is so strong,
I'm not sure if you even realize
Your destiny and mine,
we're forever intertwined
one for all eternity, if only...
I wish you would find and understand
the elation I feel in your presence
and maybe feel a little back to me.
