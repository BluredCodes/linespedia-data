---
title: "Waiting"
writer: "Tegs"

Two weeks to go seems like forever,
the time stretches on, each minute an eternity.

One weeks to go, and you can count down the days,
what you have been waiting for is suddenly tangible and real.

Five days to go, and the excitement is building,
crossing off the days on the calendar, the highlight of your day.

Four days to go, your imagination is in overdrive,
planning, waiting, hoping, quietly thrilled.

Three days to go, you can't hardly wait any longer,
the seconds ticking by are painful in their endlessness

Two days to go, and you can count the hours,
what you're waiting for is real, it's about to happen...

One day to go, you're about fit to burst,
the waitings almost over, the sweet reward just around the corner,

It's here...! And wasn't it all worth the wait?
