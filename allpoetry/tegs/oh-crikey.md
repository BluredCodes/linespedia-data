Oh Crikey,
Oh God no,
not you.
I'm happy here
wasn't hurting anyone.
But then you came along.
Not only do I have to leave now
I've got to do it on 
international TV.
You bastard Stevie.
I am a victem too you know
the victem of
the crocodile hunter, you prick.
