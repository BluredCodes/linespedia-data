title: "Why did it just get sunny again?"

Is there a reason
for any of what happens
out there in the world.
Or is it all one big weird mess
and everything is random.

I'd like to think I had something to do
with how my life is turning out.
But maybe it's all just destiny,
preordained, with nothing to do with me,
at least then I can blame someone else.

It does puzzle me though.
And why am I so desperate to know 
any of the answers?
When they would most probably,
just puzzle me further.
