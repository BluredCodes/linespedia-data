---
title: "Give up and give in."
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "give-up-and-give-in"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

So she puts in no effort,
just bats those eyelids,
and you worship her.

Whereas I go out of my way
to be friendly and kind,
and you ignore me.

So what should I do?
Starve myself?
Dye my hair?
Paint my face?
Pretend I have not one brain-cell
to call my own?

Would I be good enough then?
Or should I just give up and give in...
