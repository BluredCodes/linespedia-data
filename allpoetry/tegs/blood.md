---
title: "Blood."
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "blood"
source: "AllPoetry"

Ouch.
I just noticed some blood on my finger.
Where'd that come from?

Was it the blood I sucked from my last victem,
the blood that dripped from that insect bite,
or the blood that fell when you said those words.
