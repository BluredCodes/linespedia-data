---
title: "Why can't you just be happy?"
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "why-cant-you-just-be-happy"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/20946-Why-cant-you-just-be-happy--by-Tegs"
---

I am really sick of sadness
and all the horrors that we face.
They bring an individual down
and the disease starts to spread.

It starts in the mind,
personal and painful.
And then spreads to the hands
so that it can be shared.

There are a select few
who are not effected.
Who seem to bounce back
no matter what,

Why can't their joy be as contagious
as the pain we so often see.
Why can't it bring others back up,
instead of the torture bringing them down.
