---
title: "Two sides to life"
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "two-sides-to-life"

I am the Queen 
     I am the Worker 
I Love my life 
     I Hate my life 
I'm waited on hand and foot 
     I work all day, without a break 
Everyone loves me 
     I hate that Queen 
My adoring subjects 
     She's so lazy, uncaring. 
They must love working for me 
     I hate working for her 
I'm so caring, and beautiful to look upon 
     She doesn't care about us, wouldn't care if we died. 
My subjects would lay down their lives for me. 
     She sends us off to our deaths without remorse. 
They adore me, and working for me. 
     I want to revolt. 
I Love my life, I shall live forever. 
     I Hate this life, I want to die.
