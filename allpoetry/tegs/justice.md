title: "Justice"

It isn't fair when you put in no effort
but are rewarded all the same.
It isn't fair that your looks, or reputation
should be worth more, than your dreams.

It isn't fair that someone should be rewarded
for beating a weaker one to the ground
stepping all over his or her face
and then getting up to pats on the back.

No retribution is given to these people
who murder spirits, and crush hopes
All with a flip of thier hair, or raised eyebrow.
And the crushed souls, stay deflated and destroyed.
