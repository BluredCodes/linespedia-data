---
title: "Bush Fire"
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "bush-fire"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/7956-Bush-Fire-by-Tegs"
---

Flames licking my toes,
Creeping up, to my ankles,
The pain is so intense,
My skin  is peeling, blackened
Curling and cooked, flaking away.
The heat, pouring up my frame.
Up to my arms, burning them away,
Sizzling and crackling
As it reaches my hair.
Overtaking my whole body,
Endless agony, all-encompassing,
Silent screams echo from us all,
Caught together inside this fire,
Unable to run, unable to hide.
Charred, lifeless carcasses 
cover this wasteland, nothing left.
And then a seed cracks open,
A new flower, blooming in the carnage.
Feeding off the death, making itself strong,
Carrying on the circle of life,
Forever.
