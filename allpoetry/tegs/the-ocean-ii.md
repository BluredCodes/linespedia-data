Darkness, closing around me
Thick poison. Choking my lungs.
Clouds of destruction, swirling around,
covering us all, halting our breath.
Pinning my fins to the side of my body.
Can't move, can't think, can't fight.
I can't fight the pain, struggling, down.
Down into the depths, no air, suffocation.
My home destroyed, my life over.
Agony, Destruction, Despair.This is a comparison to the last poem, and shows what humans can do to our perfect world.
