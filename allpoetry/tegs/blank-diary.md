There comes to a point where you're life is like a blank diary.
Where you have nothing to build on, and nothing to live up to.
Everything feels fresh and new, and there are no obligations.
You can turn a page as a new person, and build a new future,
or you can cling to what you know is your past, and not change a thing.
There are so many possibilites, and directions you can take
how you write the words, is your choice...
and your future is truely up to you.
