Hi there all you people,out there in the crowds.You don't know what's happening,your head's in the clouds.Our world is dyingHer children in pain,In destroying our future,There's nothing to gain.We're killing each other,through the day and the night.Hardly a day now goes by,without a war or a fight.There's nothing you can do,no way to save us.So keep murdering our spirit,with  your neverending bloodlust.
