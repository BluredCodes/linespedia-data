---
title: "I miss you"
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "i-miss-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/33793-I-miss-you-by-Tegs"
---

Don't you know it's for real...
when it feels as if an apendage has been ripped off
like your blood is slowly oozing from your body
every moment you are away from him...
It feels as if my heart is being smothered
and is gasping for breath that it can only just get
not the easy restfulness that comes when he's here.
Shooting stars fly from his eyes
so deep and clear I can see my own soul
reflected deep within them.
I know this is for real...
as every moment I am away feels like an eternity
and all I want to see around the next corner
Is him.
