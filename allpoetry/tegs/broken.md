---
title: "Broken"
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "broken"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/27176-Broken-by-Tegs"
---

The brush of parting fingers
over a deep, terrible ravine,
is the only comfort she will get
for her love can not be seen.

For they may never be together,
the stars say it can not be.
Thousands of miles apart forever,
they will live eternally. 

The pain burns harshly inside their hearts
fills them deeply with regret and despair.
They contemplate the life they could have
and the death they both could share.
