---

writer: "Tegs"

Locked in a dark room 
with a billion candles 
all around the walls.
But not one match to light them.
Caught in this darkness
one hundred keys on my belt
but none fit the lock
and they just weigh me down.
Given up, lost in thought
staring up without seeing
at the invisble ceiling
through impenitrable darkness.
