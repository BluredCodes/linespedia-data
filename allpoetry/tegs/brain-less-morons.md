title: "Brain less Morons"
writer: "Tegs"

You all line upin the butchering que.To get your brains beaten out, and your blood pour.All in the name ofyour country and pride.It's just a game, but you would kill over it.Clashing colours, mingle and oppose each other,all over the standsfighting their own battles.The two sides off to warpassing that lethal grenade.whoever is left holding it for too longwill soon be destroyed.
