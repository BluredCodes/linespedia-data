---
title: "Stay"
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "stay"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I don’t want to see you
Because then I’ll miss your smile.
I don’t want to hold you
You’ll be gone in a short while.

You only see me at my worst
I only cry when you are here.
It’s not because I hate your presence
It’s because soon you’ll disapeer.

So why does the rain fall
Just long enough to make you thirsty?
Why does the sun shine
Just long enough to get you dry?
Why can’t you stay here
With me for just another moment?
Everytime you leave,
It just makes me want to die.

Why do you always have to go?
Why do you leave me all alone?
When you go it seems like me instead,
Like I’m somehow leaving home.

There’s no comfort for me out there,
No warm hearths along the way.
Can’t you feel the pain we’re both in?
Can’t you please for once just stay?
