title: "Gods Good Graces"

Did you ever notice, how loving God is.
How he seems to care for all the world.
And yet, if it's Allah we're talking of
only the Middle East and Indonesia
know anything about him.
If It's Buddha the fat, then his graces
are only heard of far away, in the far East.
Japan, and Thailand.
If it's the One and Only, Christian Lord,
His messenger was only seen in Israel,
and spread from there.
And what about the Destroyer, Creator and 
The savior of the Indian Faith.  Their Gods placed
their blessings there.
It's funny how, a God could love all the world,
and want us all to meet him in Heaven, and yet
neglect to mention his or her presence, to 
ninety percent of the earth.
It's funny how the word of one person against another
in court is not enough, and yet it was enough 
to prove to the world, that so many religions existed.
That's a lot of faith, that I just don't have.
