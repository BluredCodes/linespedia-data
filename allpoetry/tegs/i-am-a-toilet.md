---
title: "I am a Toilet."
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "i-am-a-toilet"
source: "AllPoetry"

People seem to think I'm a toilet.Each and every day, many different someone's,shove their crap in my face,and force it down my throat.I have no choice but to swallow it,and put up with the bad after taste.From the way I'm treated, I must be a toilet.Each and every day, Many different someone's,piss on me, down my throat and on my face, without a second thought,for how shitty it makes me feel,and the problems these blockages create.I'm bloody sick of being a toilet.These different someones, are going to be suprised,next time they try and piss and crap on me.Because I'll throw it up back in their face,drowing them in their own garbage,And I will smile, and go on with my business.
