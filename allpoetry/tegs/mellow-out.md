---
title: "Mellow OUT!"
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "mellow-out"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/16228-Mellow-OUT--by-Tegs"
---

You took it too seriously
it was only a joke.
Can't you face the truth?
Can't you face up to reality,
your reality that you live in.
But no, You just can't face the fact
that you are a brat.
A brat with a lovely personality,
but you're just too spoiled.
So as soon as someone says it
to your face,
you blow up at them,
even if it was harsh
what I said.
You weren't meant to see,
and how could I tell you.
Admit it.  You would have killed me.
But you can't admit that can you.
Just mellow out, and you will find life,
so much easier.
