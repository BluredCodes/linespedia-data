There's a lack of feeling in this healing
you present with no plain meaning 
for your bone structure is leaning
I think that you're a hypocrite.

But the ovation from this nation
proves that starving may be worth it
although I think it may be jack shit
What I think is meaningless.

It's better drinking than sanely thinking
Why don't you wake up from your dreaming
because you're dying it is seeming
And starting to hallucinate.

I guess my droning needs some honing
The times I need to be with getting
and stop all of this fretting
being a skeleton's ok.
