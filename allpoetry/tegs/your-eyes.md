Now I remember where I saw your eyes before.
On a clear day, with only a few clouds in the sky
the sun is so bright, and the blue so brilliant.
A single cloud can just cover the sun,
so that you can look at it for a minute.
Still see it's brilliance in it's visible outer layers.
The gorgeous gold, shines around the darkness,
with a backdrop of the most intoxicating blue.
It's a good thing the clouds there for now.
because otherwise you would be too much 
for a simple earthbound one such as myself to look apon.
