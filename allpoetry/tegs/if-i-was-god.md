title: "If I Was God..."
writer: "Tegs"

Give it up.
There's no chance of forgiveness.
You have gone to far.
Committed too many sins.
Even I will never forget this.
You murdered a puppy,
stole from the homeless,
raped your five year old sister,
kicked your mother,
worshipped a monkey,
And buggered a duck.
So go on,
pray to whatever Gardian Angel you have
but don't expect too much.
Because they're all on my pay-roll
and you're busted.
 
Heat up that poker
and refresh on your handbook
of all things horrible, because your heading
for my friend down south.
Sometimes I envy him,
especially when Bastards like you
get sent south of the equator.
So prepare for pineapples up the ass
hot pokers up your nose
your ears being pulled 
so you look like a bloody elephant.
You're legs being pulled out of their sockets,
and being force fed your own measly serve of brains
mixed in with your own rancid snot.
I hope your proud of what you've done,
because you're busted buddy.
