title: "Jumper"
writer: "Tegs"

First cut off at the source,
then pulled through a barb wire rack.
laid to rest, then picked up again,
twisted, turned and ripped,
fed through a tiny hole spinning around 
and around, one-hundred miles an hour
pulled thin, and stretched out 
over much too far.
Then grabbed, prodded, poked, 
and tied back around myself many times.
All this to keep someone else warm and dry.
