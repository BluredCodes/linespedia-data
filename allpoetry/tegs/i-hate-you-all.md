I hate you all,yes I hate youI'm not kiddingno I really do hate you.But why you ask?  no fuck'n reason.I'm sure you'll understand.no you won't.You'll try and find some fuck'n hidden meaning or something.Well there's none.I just hate you.Why are you still reading?if someone said this to me,I would have pissed offby now at least.you are very sick and twistedto want to hear this from someone you don't even know.What a fag.Do you undersatnd what I'm saying?Are you laughing by now?I bet you are.  Now I'm laughing at you, I really don't understand it at all.I think I might just go shoot my self now.Or not.But it would make you happy wouldn't it.
