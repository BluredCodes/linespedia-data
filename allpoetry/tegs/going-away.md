---
title: "Going Away"
writer: "Tegs"
slug: "going-away"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/37774-Going-Away-by-Tegs"
---

soar...pain...flight...freedom...trapped...caught...
agony...paralysed...strange...sensation...cold...
seize...short...crunch...spare...cold...drained...
nothing...attention...stifling...leave...no.I'm not sure if anyone really got that, but good luck, and congrats if you did.
