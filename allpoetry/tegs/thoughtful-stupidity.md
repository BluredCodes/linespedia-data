title: "Thoughtful Stupidity"
writer: "Tegs"

They sit and stare,
and dream and wish,
about being a mindless
wonder. They would
give up their future
to be useless. Turn from
a safe full of gold to an
empty silver bag. Thinking
a billion thoughts of
nothing. Willing to give
it all up to be pathetic.
No use but to the equally 
pathetic.
They sit and they
dream, about tarnishing 
their lustre. 
I am they,
I am guilty of dreaming,
I wish I did not shine so.
