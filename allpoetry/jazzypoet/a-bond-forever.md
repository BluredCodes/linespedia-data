title: "A Bond Forever"

Walking along the road,
Under the clear morning sky,
With nothing to fear,
Nothing to hide.

Free as a bird,
Happy as a jay,
With no expectations,
With no attachments.

I was,
Walking along the road,
Under the clear morning sky.

One glance on the side,
One look into the eyes.
One miss of the beat,
One smile so sweet.

And I met you.

Nothing moved,
Nothing mattered.
My life had meaning,
The meaning was life.

It was a bond for life,
A bond forever.
A bond growing stronger with time.
Each moment, each day.

I am yours now and you are mine.
And we are one.
You and I, 
One day under the clear morning sky.
