title: "A 3 Word Specialty"

There comes a time in someone's life,When they want to say those special words,I have been there once before,Yet this is all new to you!I thought about it when I once was there,and how I could make this special for you,I wondered and pondered,and finally came through!I asked you to go out to dinner with me,Then a late night walk on the beach,I looked into your eyes,and said that 3 word special,I love you!As you glanced back at me,Wondering what you would say,You surprised my presence with a romantic kiss,and a new founded I love you back!
