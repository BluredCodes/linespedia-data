When i look into your eyes,i try my best to see,if I'm every thing you dream ofif this love is meant to be.I look deeper finding questions,and a past of loneliness,but i also see a light,trying to fill your emptiness.You want me to understand,that your afraid to fall againYour heart cant take more painand i don't know how its been.But let me tell you something,I've had my share of pain,yet, through it all i learned that there a rainbows after rain.Let me be the answer,to the confusion of your soul.Ill be the hand that guides you.Ill make your spirit whole.Looking in your eyes,i see you need to find,A love to mend your broken heart, So let me give you mine.
