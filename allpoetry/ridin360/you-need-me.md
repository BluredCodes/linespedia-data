title: "YOU NEED ME"
writer: "Ridin360"

Tucked away from life,you're too scared to let go,afraid i'll do something you never got to,happiness is something you'll never know,watching life rule you,the little time you have here goes by,i'm gonna be out ruling my lifewhile you sit in your room and cry,when you sit in your room at night,and try to close your eyes,i'll be the only one you see,i hope you remember all your lies,but you'll wish i'm there when you're old,cause your the one that'll have E.N.S,So guess where i'll be in 5 years,go ahead take a mother fuckin guess,you think i'll be here forever,but you'll need a fuckin telescope to see,you cant go to the bathroom,without yelling upon me.i think a lot of you know what I'm talking about and what i was feeling when i wrote this.
