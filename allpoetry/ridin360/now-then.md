i want to look at you,but too afraid to see something I've seen before,i turn the other way,look forward and shut the door,I admire you from a distance,and hope it wont happen again,i look at you softly,wondering how you've been,we are so close,yet so far away,you always look so bright,like a star you can see in the day,i saw you look at me,then you roled your eyes,i guess you thought i didn't see,i hear your silent cries,i want to get you back,in my arms again,you told me you loved me,why cant things be like they were back then.the star part goes with the part before it, you kinda have to think about it.
