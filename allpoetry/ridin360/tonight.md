squeeze my heart,squeeze it dry,it doesn't matter, my love has gone byit's hurts more every time,maybe this time I'll learn,best bet is not,for your body i yearnwhy the pain,i cant help my lovei hate to ignore you,you are my dovei wish i could tell you,someday i will,why haven't i yet,why haven't i stillYour lips are a rose,but knows not what color to be,they are my idle, the color is meI've liked you for long,i dont have much might,i love you too much,i'll tell you tonighti found this poem in my dresser, it's pretty old and corny but I'd like to know what you all think.
