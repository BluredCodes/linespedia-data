title: "Just to Be Loved"

Would I be remembered. It’s been so very long.
Maybe I shouldn’t even try, afraid that I’ll be wrong.

Raven haired beauty with eyes any man can loose himself in.
A smile that only hints of a sweetness that surely is within.

To be loved is all you need. That’s not much for which to ask.
Never worry, my dearest. Happiness is well within your grasp.

Out there is the one waiting to build his world around you.
Waiting his whole life. Ready to put no other above you.

Wanting to have the truest of loves, like an all-consuming fire.
The embodiment of intoxicating happiness, taking you ever higher.

My truest wish is that your heart forever be without sorrow. 
If your road seems too long, remember there’s a day after tomorrow.

If you ever feel weak and unable to cope,
Please know, I’ll always help fortify your hope.

A friend to you is what I can be. Always know that I care. 
If you ever find yourself lost, just call me. I’ll be there.
This was written for a friend of mine who's desires for one true love I wish to be met as swiftly as possible. I admire her attitude about it and I praise her patience in waiting for it to happen.
