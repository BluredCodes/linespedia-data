---
title: "She Doesn't Even Realize"
writer: "Aaxmym"
slug: "she-doesnt-even-realize"

If he could hold her in his arms.
If she could tell him that she cares.
Under the moonlight, he knows why she can't be there.

(Chorus)
The pain, when only one heart has desire.
He'll survive somehow, someway.
The pain, when only one soul feeds the fire.
His memories remain, always alive.
He'll send her his love,
Knowing she doesn't even realize.

He longs to lose himself in those eyes.
An empty hope of something that can never be.
He knows she doesn't even realize.

If only he were allowed to say what his heart feels.
To tell her that he's with her when she's not there.
His lonely heart, he knows she could heal.

(Chorus)

Dreams of feelings they will never share.
The touch of her hand, the scent of her hair.
He knows he can't ever tell her he cares.

She'll go through her life never knowing.
His only hope...that broken hearts always mend.
Keeping his feelings from ever showing.

The pain, when only one heart has desire.
He'll survive somehow, someway.
The pain, when only one soul feeds the fire.
His memories remain, always alive.
He'll send her his love,
Knowing she doesn't even realize.
These are lyrics I composed while fondly thinking of a female friend of mine who, at the time, I wished could have been far more. Unfortunately, the circumstances at that time would have never permitted it. There's an extremely talented singer/songwriter by the name of Lorne James who I would love to have record this song. Lorne and I are very close friends and he's the only one I feel could do this song justice. Who knows, maybe someday.
