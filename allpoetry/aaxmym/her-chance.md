Some people live their dreams.
Some close their eyes and cry,
Wondering if their destiny has passed them by.

Knowing how a love should be.
Hoping for the courage to make a change.
Finding no reason for things to remain the same.

Not understanding,  
Since things sought are so simple and true,
Why heartaches are many and fulfillments so few.

Weary from praying for happiness that may not come.
Knowing they must act soon 
Or there'll be no other to blame but one.

There's always a second chance
For those strong enough to leave it behind.
To pursue a dream, they know may be difficult to find.

Even though a new life holds no guarantees,
You must take this chance that's been given to you,
And settle only for the best, Debbi.........please.

Your Friend
I wrote this for a friend that was trying to get out of a very abusive relationship, but was unsure if she could do it.
