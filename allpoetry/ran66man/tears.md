Tears

Your eyes are beautiful, are they when you cry
You look at me, I look at you
and I don't know what to say
but I know what I'm feeling
just look in my eyes, you'll think there crying
but you'll see no tears, I hold in my fears
just like the girl with the beautiful eyes

Does your eyes cry no tears
Does your heart feel broken
but there's something that keeps you going
something that keeps the mind knowing
so when you find it
The tears will be pouring
The water keeps the mind flowing
until your eyes are satisfied
until your heart realizes
what's happened to you

Your eyes are beautiful, are they when you cry
You had a hard life, I had a hard life
and I don't know what to feel anymore
but if I knew what you were feeling
Maybe the look in your eyes, maybe your eyes would tear
but I see no tears, you hold onto fears
just like somebody with the beautiful eyes

Your eyes are beautiful, are they when you cry...This was one of my first. As you can tell I like one line the best.
