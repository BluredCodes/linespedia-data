A Flash of Memories

Remember what she's given, Remember what she's taken
Just think of all the friends she's making
because a flash of memories will flood in
and deep down the feelings are within

Remember when she laughed, Remember when she cried
Never forget all the things she tried
because a flash of memories will flood in
then inside the healing can begin

Remember who she's met, Remember who she's known
Start holding all the love she's shown
because a flash of memories will flood in
and living with something you can't hold in

Remember why she's smiling, Remember why she's caring
Listen to all the stories she's sharing
because a flash of memories will flood in
thinking you need someone to reach in

Remember where she's gone, Remember where she's been
because a flash of memories will flood in
Remeber how she was, Remember how she made
a flash of memories
come to your heart
now apart
but never gone
life goes on and on and onThis was written for my Cousin when her Mother died.  It came from her bringing out old pictures of everyone and passing them around so everyone could see how things have changed.
