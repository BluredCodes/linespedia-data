---
title: "The Old Man Wants To Die"
writer: "ran66man"

He tells me stories about way back when
He used to tell me about when he's young
Now he's too weak to even lift his hand
so let him die because he's made his stand

In the will says let him go
when he fails don't keep him alive
Just put me to rest, he pleas
He'll be at his best, he knows
because old man wants to die
just to die on his own, unknown
not to be with the blame, no shame
the old man wants to die

He gave me hope about my upcoming future
He held me close when I was in fear
Now he can't hold my hand, even look at me and know
so let him go, forever to be free
could have been me, so let him be

the old man wants to die
