title: "The corruption"
writer: "skip 7"

Corruption of my mind,leading to self-persecution of my soul.The views of others,the unduly suppressive governing laws,the regulations of state,all have twisted the very skienthat binds my sanity in place.G.O.P. or Democratic bureaucrat.Warmonger or peaceful diplomat.Tree hugger, tree cutter.The faces of our children account for the loss.Planetary wide depredation of nature.Starvation on the African plains.Gluttony in the flats of our rich and famous.Stories plastered on the evening news,glamorizing high society views,all the while ignoring lower class realities...
