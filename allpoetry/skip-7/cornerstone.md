---
title: "Cornerstone"
writer: "skip 7"

source: "AllPoetry"

Everything we have built,
may erode from our despondency.

Everything we deem essential,
will lead to our dependency.

Everything, given enough time,
may tumble down upon us.

Everything, given moral aptitude,
will become stubborn and oblivious.

Everything entrusted unto me,
may lose grip on integrity.

Everything cornered onto you,
will sustain a sensible stability.
