A chain tightened by beck and callanyone sane, would brayif shackled to your ball.Produce a Cat-of-Nine-Tailssanity would run awayunfortunately, I am too frail.One glimpse of your leather gimp suitand a stable mind would say'that is FAR from cute'.With you skirting issuesfree will shall not staytotally ignoring your precious views.I do what normal sanity would not doI live with insaneLove/Hate for you.
