I can neither compromisenor realizemy mentality.I can neither understandnor defendmy mortality.I can neither go beyondnor stand behindmy integrity.To yearnto burnwith wanton desire.I grasp for true spirituality.To denyto liewith an insatiable thirst.Misleading myself into dysfunctional reality.Deep inside the recesses of my mind.Hidden behinda blasphemous sign.In this Godless sanctuaryI cannot define.Underneath the weightof a burdened spine.What I wasno longer resides.As these vestigesof a forgotten moralityremind...Should I use proper punctuation? Or would that impede the flow????
