title: "Hooked on a bad trip"

Pain and hate dependency.Strengthening misanthropy.Enlightening tendency.Deliriously jaded.Living sedated.Experience over-rated.Blinded by rage.Trapped in a cage.Life a cynical stage.Clinically depressed.Feelings suppressed.Total unrest.My God,Such a mess...
