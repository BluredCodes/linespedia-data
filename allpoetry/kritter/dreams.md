Drowning in rose petals,
I gasp for air.
Too much isn't enough,
Yet too much is a dream.
A dream of you by my side,
Honest and good,
Worries and fears piled up in the trash,
Gone forever,
Never to return.
My dreams go up in smoke,
I pray this one won't.
