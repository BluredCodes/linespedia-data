title: "Slither"
writer: "Kritter"

Life is a spiral,
Downwards we go.
Slipping and sliding,
Losing it all.
Conformity is a snake,
Its venom slow.
The fangs sink in and never let go.
Slyly it knows,
Leading us astray.
Whispering thoughts that stick in your head,
Noone is the wiser until your will is dead.
