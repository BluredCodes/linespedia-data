title: "Snow White"
writer: "Kritter"

I think of this glass,
Going up my nose,
And it taunts my every waking moment.
I feel the need,
Feel the greed,
Feel the monkey on my back.
He claws and whispers, "Just a line..."
This evil temptation is driving me nuts,
Just when I thought I was done.
It comes back thrice as bad when you realize
How close you had come.Once you start, it's hard to stop...
