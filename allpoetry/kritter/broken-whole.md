---
title: "Broken Whole"
writer: "Kritter"
slug: "broken-whole"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Looking inside of you I see myself,
Sad and fearful.
Always searching, 
Never finding.
Waiting for the next moment to be with you,
Complete in our Love, 
Only whole with each other.
These feelings tear me, 
Starve me of your warmth.
Yet all at once, 
Realization strikes me:
No one else could come close.
