---
title: "Friend"
writer: "Kritter"
slug: "friend"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Seed in my brain,
Planting its roots.
Thoughts that aren’t mine?
Whose are they then?
A shadowy face,
Grinning and laughing.
His eyes pierce the dark,
And stare into me:
The fear I feel was never here before,
A symptom of him.
