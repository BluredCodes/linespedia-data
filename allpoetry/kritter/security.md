Laughing and Crying,
That's the way it goes.
Tears in my head want to flow out to sea,
Weakness or Strength: Who decides?
Caring words from your mouth explode in my heart,
Opening the gates I thought were locked.
It's all new to me,
Sometimes I'm scared.
But your soft touch is a warm blanket,
Securing my frail thoughts.
