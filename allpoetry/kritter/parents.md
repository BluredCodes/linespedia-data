title: "Parents"

Can't they understand,
It wasn't my fault.
They just stare at me,
And tell me what I did wrong,
When nothing was even done!
Voices are raised,
Punches are thrown,
Can't fight back.
I should hold my tongue,
But for what?
The loss of pride?
I'd die before I lose that.
So on I go,
To try and explain,
But it's like talking to a wall,
A wall of thorns.
