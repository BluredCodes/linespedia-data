title: "Fenced In"
writer: "Kritter"

Clearing one cliff, 
Another in sight.
Never getting out, 
Always trapped inside.
It never ends, 
It doesn’t go away;
Strikes without warning, 
A shot in the dark.
Sometimes I want to run, 
Sometimes I cry.
Sometimes a part of me dies.
