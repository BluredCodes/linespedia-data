title: "Nat"

The glitter in your eyes,
The smell of your hair.
Your touch amazes me, 
Takes my breath away,
How could this be any better?
You’re always there when I need you the most,
Your sweet words, 
Kind, 
Refreshing and true.
I wish things were different,
But for the moment I’m at peace:
‘Cause you’re in my arms.
