---
title: "You"
writer: "Kritter"
slug: "you"
source: "AllPoetry"

You drive me insane,
Yet pull me closer to the ground.
When you're near,
I can't control myself.
Smother you in kisses,
Squeeing you tightly,
You remind me why life isn't so bad
With your words,
Your caress,
Your slightest breath.
The smiles and laughter you give me
Fill me to full,
I almost overflow with pain-
Pain of what you deserve, 
And what I am not giving.
You say it's enough,
Yet there's so much more I want you to know,
But words escape me.
You are a brightness,
Keeping me on the path,
And in your arms.
Have I told you yet today,
That I Love You?*sigh* she's really amazing...
