Expensive cars,
Abercrombie and Fich:
That's what they're about.
Fake on the outside,
Noone knows who they are,
They don't even know themselves.
Parties and drugs,
False expressions,
Outward appearances,
Who do you think you're fooling?
Other people want to be like them,
But not me.
Going through their days in a daze of acting,
Are they happy?Not much originality here, but I really dislike trendy people who put on a giant act.  Fake people make me angry.
