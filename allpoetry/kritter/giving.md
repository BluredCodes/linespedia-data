---
title: "Giving"
writer: "Kritter"
slug: "giving"
source: "AllPoetry"

Time spent together,
Never enough.
It all melts,
Melts in my heart.
Feelings so hot,
Burnt and confused.
Surrendering my soul,
It's all up to you.
