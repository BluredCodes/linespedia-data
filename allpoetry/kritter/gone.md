---
title: "Gone"
writer: "Kritter"
slug: "gone"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

It might not be my fault,
Doesn't really matter.
A picture of us,
On the floor broken in pieces.
Someone else strolled in,
Chance took it all away,
In a heartbeat like the ones
We used to share.
Now in my mind,
I wonder where she is,
All I know is my bed sits empty,
Shared with my lonely thoughts of
What we had.
Like smoke on the wind,
Gone.
