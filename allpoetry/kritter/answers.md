---
title: "Answers?"
writer: "Kritter"
slug: "answers"

Confused and alone,
Not one understands.
A mixture of everything that surrounds and penetrates.
Right and wrong are letters on paper,
Their meanings hidden in the clouds.
The blue sky is filled with visions that scar my eyes.
An answer so dim it might not exist.
It slowly fades,
As the sky turns black.
