Your hand brushed my cheek as you whispered loving words into my ear.
Your lips were soft, hands gentle eyes sincere.
I gave you my heart and my soul and you gave me all you could.
In your arms I was happy, in your love I was content, in your lies I was full of ignorant bliss.
As night must confront the rising sun and be exposed so was your dishonesty.
The truth scorched my heart and you scurried away.
Now here I sit with the memory of you.
I still feel the warmth of your touch.
I still taste your kiss.
In spite of myself I still long for you.
