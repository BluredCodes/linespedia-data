In a dream so real I held your love.I was afraid to hold on tight,I thought it might break.But when I woke up it was gone.At first I was angry.I didn't understand.Then sadness settled in.I long to hold that dream again,to feel your love.In my dream I was complete,because you were there with me.But now you feel so far away.How I wish that dream was alive.Still burning bright within my heart.Maybe it's not to be.But I believe that one day my dream will come true.One day you will be here with me.1999,
