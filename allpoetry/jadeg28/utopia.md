I close my eyes and dream of a world where I am free.
Where my eyes are free to see, the scales fall away so I can take in all the beauty around me.
Where my ears are free to hear, the plugs taken out so I can absorb the depth of sound.
Where my mind is free to think, the restraints undone so I can dwell on the mysteries of life.
Where my feet are free to roam, the chains removed so I can wander this world's many lands.
Where my tongue is free to speak, the grip loosed so I can articulate all my thoughts.
Where my hands are free to create, the shackles unlocked so I can put my perception into form.
Where my mouth is free to sing, the cage opened so I can release my soul to fly.
Where my heart is free to love, the dam burst so I can give you all of me.
