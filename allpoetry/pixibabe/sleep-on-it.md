---
title: "Sleep On It"
writer: "pixibabe"

Decision of two.
Dreams of angels
You and me together,
sleep away the nights
sleep away the days.
Ring the bells,
dream of love.
Between us
Decision of two
thirty three times.
Sugar love
longing sweet seduction,
music is music.
To my ears you sing
July flowers burst open.
sun rise, sun set.
Where has my sun gone?
Sets in the west,
I'm in the east.
Indecisive bitch
choose between us.
