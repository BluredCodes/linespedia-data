title: "Escape"
writer: "pixibabe"

she's looking for an escape
her life nothing but an endless disaster
she's looking for an escape
cause she can't take the pain
can't live the life
after awhile it hurts to smile
because she can't take the pain
and you know there's nothing you can do
when she's looking for an escape
so just leave her ALONE!
