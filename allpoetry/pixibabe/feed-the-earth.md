---
title: "Feed the Earth"
writer: "pixibabe"
slug: "feed-the-earth"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/7931-Feed-the-Earth-by-pixibabe"

Cry like rain,
nurture the earth with your tears.
Winning the war
against the dark creature,
called depression.
Cry, and feed the land.
