---
title: "Expressionless"
writer: "pixibabe"
slug: "expressionless"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Can't even express the pain
the tears I've cried for you,
slowly dripping off my face.
Falling into the water
becoming one with the rest.
driven by the loneliness. 
Won't let you see me cry
but there's nothing here,
for me to hold on to.
