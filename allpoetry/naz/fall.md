I'll pack my bags

come with me

we'll go see everything that i am

we'll be two little kids spinning

         out

of

        this world

stop

        and 

we 

will



        F

        a

        l

        l

 out of my world

like love

spin spin

and everything around you

disapears

and if you're not careful

you will fall



          out

of

           Love



so spin spin

don't stop



cause then you'd fall

and hurt 

       hold my hands

and spin like two little kids

out of this world

and make the world around us dissapear
