as all the lights go off

  and every one sleeps

  as i sit here and shake 



          there is a lonely star that keeps winking at me

          asking for my company



  something inside me died yesterday

  reality bumped into me



          i wonder if that star is okay with bitter company

  

  

           

           even that star is going to fade away *....



  Nothing last for ever





          ~Naz~
