---
title: "spin around"
writer: "Naz"
slug: "spin-around"

---

burn your candles

sing those songs

and spin around

wear your favorite nightgown

dance around



wear your crown

spin around

forget all your troubles

put on your favorite lipstick



you spent too many nights

weeping on that pillow

forget that bed tonight

sleep on your head

upside down

you gotta save your soul



don't put out the candle

let it burn to the end

save the wax

keep it forever

~Naz~
