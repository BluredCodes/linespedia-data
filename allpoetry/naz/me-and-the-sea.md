once apoun a time

on a full moon

there was a little girl 

and there  was a big giant 

never ending sea

the sea mad

the wind pushing 

and screaming

the waves big and splashing 

she 

screaming

shouting

crying

as loud as she could

and there was no one there

no one but the stars to watch 

luna

and the sea

to hear her

the louder she shouted

the higher the waves 

she cried

no one to hear

she couldn't even hear her self

the wind the waves

got louder and louder

she saw the biggest waves 

the most beautiful moon 

so many stars that night

she stood on the rocks

screaming

shouting

crying 

everything she ever wanted to say

the sea shal keep her secrets

everything that night

no one to hear her

no one

but 

the sea~once some one asked me whats the best thing that ever happend to you

and i had to think about it long and hard and i though of the best feeling i ever had ....~
