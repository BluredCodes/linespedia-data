tonight

   as i go off

into my sweet dreams

you'll be off in your liquid world

with your blue hair

and sweet lips

                you  won't remember me tonight

but i'll be dreaming of you

               i'll be a cell in the wall

the wall that's

 w~ r i~ p p~ e l i ng

as i write





~Naz~
