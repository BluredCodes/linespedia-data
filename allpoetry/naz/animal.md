there was many of them but remember That one. remember, that Gypsy girl, the one you couldn't get out of your mind, the one that cast a spell on you, the one that moved like flames with black black hair down to her knees with waves like the sea. the one with eyes that sparkled like the stars the one that you couldn't keep your eyes of off when she danced, the one with the curves the one with the beautiful breasts that squeezed out of her coarsest. the one that cured your son, the one you wanted, the one that was wild, the one with the wet lips you couldn't get out of your mind. the one that you called a witch, the one you burnt
