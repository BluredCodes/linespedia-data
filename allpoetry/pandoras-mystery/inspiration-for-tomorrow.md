Swallow those tears that clung to your lashes
Please smile a smile that comes from this passage

Tomorrow’s a new day the past is behind
It’s time to stop pushing this button rewind

No one can change you although they may try
Don’t make yourself someone who once made you cry

The past is your story  you’ll never forget
But dont live your life with a sense of regret

We all make mistakes we all do the wrong
But you have to cope and try to be strong

If you find yourself lonely your heart growing bitter
Think of tomorrow just dont be a quiter

Don’t take your life you still have to try
Although the pain hurts you’ll always survive

Your heart can be broken and pieced back together
Just know that love fades but friends are forever

And you can cry just not all alone
You need a friend arms to call home

Don’t get upset when your world falls apart
Just think of it as your turn to restart

Your life won’t be perfect, although it comes close
Some days will mean nothing while others the most

Some days you'll be happy others depressed
If you hurry through life you'll pass by the best

'Cause life is too short too fast to rush through
You need to slow down and take care of you

You need to laugh when you make a mistake
And you need to smile a smile not fake

'Cause no matter the difference the changes you do
