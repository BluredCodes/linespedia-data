title: "Trying (for you)"
writer: "Pandoras Mystery"

loving feelings fill my heart
whenever you're so near
the way you hold me close at times
and whisper in my ear

makes me feel that nothings wrong
that everythings alright
and at times i think of you
and dream of you at night

i've never been as happy
like i am when i'm with you
i've cried those tears forever
but now, i know they're through

i remember when i met you
which seems not long ago
i was, but yet a scared girl
that was scared to let you know

to let you know my problems
or the reasons that i cried
but youre the one who helped me
when my best friend sadly died

you taught me how to smile
when the world tore me apart
you taught me how to love again
as you fixed this broken heart

i remember every kiss you gave
from your sweet lips to mine
and when you gaze into my eyes
my heart skips every time

but we all know i'm awful
at writing happy poems
i was sad before you
but they're my favorite poems

so my dear, i'm sorry
i can't write a poem for you
a poem that says my feelings
so i'll just say "i love you"
