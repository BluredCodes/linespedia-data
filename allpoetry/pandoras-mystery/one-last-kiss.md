title: "One Last Kiss"
writer: "Pandoras Mystery"

I wanted to love him forever I wanted to love him for life I wanted to love him this moment But its hard when he used a knife Its hard to love him today Its hard to love him right now Its hard to keep loving him When he took his life somehow I Wanted to have his children I wanted to share our dreams I wanted to grow old with him But now all i do is scream I remember seeing him lying there In a pool full of his blood His wrists were torn to shreds And i hit the floor with a thud I held him in my arms And felt the blood seep through my shirt I knew he was gone forever But my heart it really hurt I wanted to love him forever I wanted to love him for life I wanted to love him this momment for gods sake i was gonna be his wife! Now i sit and wait here Alone in this dark place Its getting harder to sleep at night I can only see his face Maybe i would still have him if i told him these three words i told him all the time but i dont think he ever heard I'm telling him right here before i end this poem I love you and i wish you would have known But since you're already gone I want you to do me this wait for me forever so i can give you one last kiss
