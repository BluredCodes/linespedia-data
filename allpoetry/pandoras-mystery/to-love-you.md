title: "To Love you..."
writer: "Pandoras Mystery"

To love you, i must leave you
To leave, i must let go.
To care, i must forget you
And all the things we know.

To smile, i must not hold you
Not hold you in my heart.
To say your names forbidden
I slowly fall apart.

To keep you, i must lose you
To lose, i must not grasp.
And if you are my "one"
This hug won't be our last.

'Cause today i can't be yours,
And you can not be mine.
But be my friend forever,
And perhaps we'll grow in time.

I'm so scared to lose you
Although i never had.
Be my friend, don't leave me
Dont make my heart be sad.

So be my friend don't leave me....
                       Don't make OUR hearts be sad....
