I am a writer
who fills empty pages
with words of wisdom
with words of hatred

i am a writer
that thinks for my self
that doesn't depend
on everyone else

i am a writer
whose filled with much hope
thats been through a lot
and knows how to cope

i am a writer
that sings my own songs
and knows to admit
the times that i'm wrong

i am a writer
that laughs when i cry
and doesn't give up
without a good try

i am a writer
that puts my self last
i help everyone
no matter the task

but i am a writer
unique like the rest
i know i'm not perfect
nor am i the best

i am a writer
that just likes to write
its all that i am
