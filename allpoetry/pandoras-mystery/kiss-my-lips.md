Repetitive (lol)

Kiss my lips
Hold my hand
Let me know
You are my man

Touch my skin
Feel my hair
Let me know
You’ll always care

Kiss my face
Hold me close
Let me know
You love me most

Touch my tears
Feel my heart
Let me know
We’ll never part

Kiss my eyes
Hold my Face
Let me stay
In your embrace

Touch my soul
Feel it smile
Let me stay
With you awhile

Kiss my nose
Hold me tight
Let me stay
With you tonight

Touch my mind
Feel it tremble
Let me stay
With you forever

Love me dear
As I love you
Say, “I’m yours”
Before they do

Let the world
Know you’re mine
To kiss, to hold, to love
Through time

So that they’ll never
Steal away
The one I need
Day after Day

But my dear
Please tell me true
Do you love me
As I love you?

Do you reach
For me at night?
Does my touch
Make wrongs a right?

Does my kiss
Stay with you
In the day
And all night too?

And do you see me
In your sleep
To dream I’m yours
To always keep?

If you do
My darling know
I’m yours to love
I won’t let go...
