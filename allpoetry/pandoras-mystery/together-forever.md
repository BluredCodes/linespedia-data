---
title: "Together Forever"
writer: "Pandoras Mystery"
slug: "together-forever"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/12048-Together-Forever-by-Pandoras-Mystery"
---

when they say they love you
there should be a simple smile
cause even though you think they care
your heart is in denial!

their touch is soft upon your skin
but your insides crawl with fear
one day they'll be forever close
the next they are not near.

you wonder if they think of you
you wonder if they care
and when they shut you from their life 
you wonder if its fair.

all you do is think of them
and they way they held you hand
'together forever' is what you whisper
but then it drifts like sand.

at night you ist and wait for them
but never will they arrive
'cause to them you are worth nothing
and their love will make you strive.

sadly love will come and go
and again your heart is broken
but always try to remeber
the kind words that once were spoken.

even though they broke your heart
and you fear it'll never heal
please, i beg, give me a chance
to show you how love feels.

So place you hand inside of min
and i'll kiss away those tears
let your heart sleep with mine
and i'll was away those fears!
