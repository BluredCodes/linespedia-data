~Daddy's Girl~DADDY'S GIRL I USED TO BE, BUT I GUESS TODAY I'M NOT, DADDYS GIRL I USED TO BE, BUT I GUESS THAT HE FORGOT. DADDYS' GIRL I USED TO BE, BUT TODAY I MESSED THINGS UP, DADDY'S GIRL I USED TO BE, BUT TODAY HE JUST GAVE UP. DADDY'S GIRL I USED TO BE, BUT I GUESS THAT WASN'T TRUE, DADDYS' GIRL I USED TO BE, BUT I GUESS TODAY THATS THROUGH. DADDY'S GIRL I USED TO BE, BUT TODAY HIS LOVE I LOST, DADDY'S GIRL I USED TO BE, AND MY SINS NOT PAY THE COST. DADDY'S GIRL I USED TO BE, BUT TODAY I MADE HIM SAD, DADDY'S GIRL I USED TO BE, BUT SOME HOW HE JUST GOT MAD. DADDYS'GIRL I USED TO BE, BUT TODAY HE LOST HIS TEMPER, DADDY'S GIRL I USED TO BE, AND THATS WHAT I'LL REMEMBER!
