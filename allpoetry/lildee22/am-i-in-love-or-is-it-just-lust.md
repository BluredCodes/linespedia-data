i think that i am in love?
but how can you tell,
dose it just feel right?
because everything that i do with you feels right,
or is it just lust... a crush?
are you in love when everytime someone says his name,
it brings a smile to your face?
or is it just lust... a crush?
or are you in love when
all that you think about is him?
or is it just lust...a crush?
