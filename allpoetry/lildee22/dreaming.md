I sleep sound in my bed
Unaware of what’s around me,
I dream of things both good and bad
And have no control over what they may be

My dreams I wonder 
Can they predicate my future?
Sometimes I think that maybe they do
And other times I don’t even remember them

In my dreams I wish 
And my wishes come true
I can do almost anything
But I mostly dream of you
