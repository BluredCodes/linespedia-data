it breaks my heart to see you with her
but you seem so happy
and that is all that i want
for you to be happy
to see the smile on your face,
and your problems be erased
and if that meens things going back to the way they were, 
just frineds and 
you being with her....
i will just have to deal
because i think that i am in love with you
