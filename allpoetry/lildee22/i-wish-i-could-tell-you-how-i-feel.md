It hurts me to see you with her,
But I don’t know how to say it,
I wish that you to weren’t “together”
And that it was me in her place.
Some people may say I had my chance,
But I wish that I could take it back.
If I could I would do things different,
I wish that I could tell you this but…..
I know that you are happy and that is all that I want,
I just wish that had a second chance too show you 
How much you mean too me!
