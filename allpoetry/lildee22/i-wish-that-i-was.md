---
title: "I wish That I was..."

I wish that I were five again
When boys had cooties and we knew to stay away
 I wish that I were five again
 So that I could play all day
 I wish that I were five again
 When responsibilities were as easy as combing your hair
 I wish that I were five again
And I had not one care
 I wish that I were five again
 And my only worry was if my friends could play
I wish that I were five again
When tomorrow meant just another day
