title: "I am a Crazy Girl"

I am a crazy girl in love with you
I wonder if you feel the same way that I do
I hear people saying that we shouldn’t be together
I see the way that you look at me
I want us to be together but I don’t want to get hurt
A am a crazy girl in love with you

I pretend not to have feelings for you but sometimes they are too strong to hide
I feel you looking at me but I will never no what you are thinking
I touch you hand in my dreams and wish that it was real
I worry that when you tell me you love me… none of it is true
I am a crazy girl in love with you

I understand that this may not work but I think that I want to try
I say that we are just friends but it is sometimes easier to say then feel
I dream that we are together and happy
I try to see you as just a friend but it is too hard
I hope that we can make this work
I am a crazy girl in love with you
