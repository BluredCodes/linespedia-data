title: "Together in My Dreams"
writer: "lildee22"

We walked down the hall holding hands
no one looked or asked if we were together
It was the perfect day
I had no problems or worries

We hugged and kissed 
We were happy 
We were together all night 
You held me and we looked at the stars

You held me in your arms
 Your heartbeat I listened to
I felt so safe and comfortable 

It was like we were meant to be
And then I woke up and realized that it was just a dream
