---
title: "Shadow and Soul"
writer: "Shadows Dark"
slug: "shadow-and-soul"
source: "AllPoetry"

Shadow and soul,
dancing again.
Soul and shadow,
waiting for the end.

A silent shadow,
slipping through the night,
a patient soul,
awaiting morning's light.

Shadow and soul,
dancing again.
Soul and shadow,
waiting for the end.

One by day,
and one by night.
One banished by the darkness,
the other by the light.

Shadow and soul,
dancing again.
Soul and shadow,
waiting for the end.

Two sides of a coin,
a distorted mirror.
Like this for all time,
and the end draws no nearer.

Shadow and soul,
dancing again.
Soul and shadow,
waiting for the end.

A double-edged blade,
with no hilt to hold.
Paying for eternal life,
not waiting for the price to be told.

Shadow and soul,
dancing again.
Soul and shadow,
waiting for the end.

Bound to the shadow,
the darkness within.
Bound to the soul,
slowly becoming shadow's kin.

Shadow and soul,
dancing again.
Soul and shadow,
waiting for the end.

Perhaps one day,
the two will become one.
Then she can rest,
no longer having to run.

Shadow and soul,
dancing again.
Soul and shadow,
