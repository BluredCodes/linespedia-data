Shadowed kisses bring molten ecstasy - 
Twin points of pleasure
lancing into my flesh:
letting my coppery blood flow...

Willingly submitting myself,
Thinking only to serve her -
begging for the pleasure of her shadowy kisses.

In her pale, white hands,
she holds my life and soul;
But I, I placed them there...for her.

I live only to serve her.
(and when I die -
I shall serve her again, as a lesser copy of herself.)

Until one day,
when the copy overturns the orginal,
then I shall be the one giving the shadow kisses.Yes, this is about a vampire.
