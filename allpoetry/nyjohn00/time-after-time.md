Time After Time

Gazing into your eyes
I see only love 
Feeling the touch of your love
Tenderly caressing me, holding me tight

I melt deep in your arms
As you and I embrace
I would sacrifice anything
To see your face next to mine

Tears of joy stream down my face
From my eyes they flow so freely
For I feel so much love
More, so much more than I have ever felt

Lying in your arms, it is such a dream
There's nothing I want more
Than to be there forever
Like I dreamed of before with each nights passing

There's nobody else
That I want, only you to fill my heart
You are my true loves blessing
Only one I felt so true

Time after time
I love you over again and again
endlessly my love goes on
There's no end at all

'Cause time after time
I desire your tender touch
To overtake my body, embrace me with your love
Time after time, forever is not enough
