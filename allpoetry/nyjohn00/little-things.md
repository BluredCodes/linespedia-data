When you get to know someone
You start to notice the little things
Most, say the little things are what bug
But on the one you love
Oh so loved

When we first were together
All I saw was your beauty
I would look at you and tingle
Feeling the awe of your loving embrace
Oh so wonderful

As time goes on, I see a difference
I see you relax, to let you out
You know I accept and love
So I see your silly, sweet, and joyous self
Oh so precious

I see you eyes twinkle
Is it in laughter or is it to tease
You change your voice
You dance and sing
Oh I love what I see

For you see precious
I love all that's you
I see nothing dumb
You give me happiness
You give me you
