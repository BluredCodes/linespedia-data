title: "Loves Tears"

When ever we are apart
The times that life
Keeps us from even talking
I start to think of all the love
All the love you have given me
Truly more than I have ever felt
More than was ever given
A thought comes so clear
If my life was to end tomorrow
I'd still have the happiness in my heart
Of having felt your love
And as I sit and think these thoughts
My eyes begin to tear
Not with the tears of pain
Nor the tears of sadness
These tears that fall so copiously
Mark all the happiness I feel
Having felt your love
