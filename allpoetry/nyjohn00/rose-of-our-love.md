title: "Rose of our love"

Think of the newly planted rose bush
Think of it as our love
Today it look so beautiful
Trimmed neat with just a few buds
But with the loving care we treat
Tending it with so much care
We hold our love so precious
Giving it all it needs
Tomorrow it will be so much bigger
And the flowers of our love so numerous
