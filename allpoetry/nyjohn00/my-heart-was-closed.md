My heart was closed
Afraid to feel
Watching all around
Yearning

It had been open
Torn and raw
Taken and trampled
Thrown to the wind

It closed itself
So it couldn't feel
Never wanting to let
Those feelings in

Along came one
So unique and pure
Full of love
Full of life

She gave and she gave
Love to all those she knew
Her happiness the joy
She caused to those around her

We met by chance
Both taken with joy
The pure smile
We shared on that day

We met and we talked
Her beauty astounded
The sweet caring
She showed to me

Her beauty flows
The love in her eyes
The music of her voice
Caused my pulse to race

We talked and we talked
A chance kiss did we share
She opened my heart
And holds it in her hand
