Angel With No Wings


As I look at you my love
I know I have found the one
A life with you filled with ecstasy
Your essence all around me
I feel the contentment of you at my side
.
A life filled with joy, bliss, a paradise
An embrace to last forever
Is only possible with you next to me
Hand and hand, thought with thought
US together, makes a perfect one

What would life be without you near
No brown eyes to gaze deeply in
No sweet tender lips, to press against mine
It would be like an ocean, dry as the desert
Life would be empty, without your kiss.

My smile would fade without you there
Life would be a hollow shell
My heart would close, welded shut
I'd be like an angel with no wing, nothing
without you there would be no heavenly place to go
