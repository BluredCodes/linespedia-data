Long ago in the hidden mist of time
In times of legends bold
You are the reason that God made the moon
The stars and sun above
Sun, starlight, and midnight blue
Of every romantic though ever made
They all relate to you
You are the purest angel
The center of my world
The light of day, the romance of the night
The lights shimmers in your hair
You glowed as our paths crossed
A heart touched heart, it felt the magic
An enchantment we couldn't ignore
We walked together, Timid at first,
Then our hearts came to ease
Your eyes told mine as mine told yours
This is God's will, it was destiny,
It was the magic of our love
