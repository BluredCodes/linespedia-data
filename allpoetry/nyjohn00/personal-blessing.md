If I made my own personal blessing for you,,
All it would spell out for you would be
That with ever fiber of my being I wish you
Happiness in all you do,,,
Joy in the life you have to live,
Merriment to follow you like a shadow
Contentment to fill your heart
Peace to fill your soul
Love to hold you secure in its arms
Forever
