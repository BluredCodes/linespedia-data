Why do I yearn
This love that I feel
This strong emotion
There is always a wall
Keeping it still
No freedom in sight
My heart aches to talk
The words that it feels
But this wall is always there still
So much I want to give
This ardor, God it grows
To have and to hold
Embrace it so tight
To kiss it with passion
Hearing our sighs
But this wall is still there
It holds us a part
