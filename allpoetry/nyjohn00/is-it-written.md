Is It Written

It was just another day
People everywhere
I hear a Voice from the clouds
Maybe God has answered my prayers
Am I dreaming with open eyes
Suddenly out of nowhere
She came into my life
Like we known each other
For quite a while
In this Garden of Eden
Time is standing still
What never should be would remain in the dark
Or will it rise to shine its light
And do you really wonder
That we can really be
Is it written in the beginning of time
Are you the one , that the God had made for me
Are you the Goddess of Heaven & Life
Are you the one that is here for me
Are you the one, who is always in my dreams
The one that I have been waiting for
For so long
