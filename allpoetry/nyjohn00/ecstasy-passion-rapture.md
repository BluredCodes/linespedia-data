Ecstasy passion rapture, 
It is ecstasy to hold you tight in my arms
To feel your body laying with me
Your warm skin slides against mine
The way you sigh
As I caress your thigh
Ecstasy

It is passion as we excitedly wrap ourselves
In loves embrace, the fevered caress
Teasing tongue as our lips meet
Moist lips part in murmurs of love
The whispers of love into your ear
Hands seek out delight, pulling you closer
Passion

Rapture, is the feeling that a woman like you
So, sweet, wonderful warm and caring
One with your beauty so rare
Filled with such feelings, ecstasy, passion
I need to be part of your universe
Your love for me and mine for you
Rapture
