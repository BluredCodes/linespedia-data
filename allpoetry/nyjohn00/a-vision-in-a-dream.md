A vision in a dream

I'm lying sleeping in my bed
Drifting into sleeps sweet peace
I lift my silent thoughts to God
Thanking Him for all he does
Asking to see His plan.

Look inside your heart
Comes the response.

Still deep asleep, a peace does settle
my eyes look deep inside
Looking, probing, penetrating the depths
Searching, scanning all I see
I see my heart, holder of all that is to come

I've looked here many times before
I know it well, but now it is clearer
I see lots of doors here. They go on and on
Doors for parts of my life my 

Doors of Joy
Of a beautiful child, my mothers love

Doors of Heartache
Of Lost Love, the emptiness of all my tears

Doors of Sorrow
The loss of loved ones; momma and dad are gone, all alone

Some of doors seem to be shrinking before me
Some are closed and locked there time is long past
I feel a presence around me, I feel you, the essence of you
Turning, I see another door, it glows with hope and love

A large finely crafted door, very ornate and carved
With intricate craftsmanship, bright brass accents 
This door is oh so special, it is different not like all the rest
It is ajar I am drawn to it, I have to see inside,

I step in and feel my love, our love everywhere
The room is larger than the world, space is needed for this love
And everywhere there is you, filling making me whole
There is Joy, Happiness, laughter and love

The room is filled with memories, pictures and dreams
I see the first time I talked to you, your smile was so pure
The feeling that you were more than just another girl
There was a special something about you, something unique

I see the first time I felt strong emotions for you
The first time I felt love for you, and wondered if you felt it too
The first time I said...I love you
And holding my breathe, in wait, you said I love you too

Turning, I see the photographs from my mind
The first time I saw you, you made the world disappear
Touched your cheek, made you laugh, 
Held your hand.

Held you in my arms, embracing all of you
Kissed you, held you, caressed, touched as only true love can
Made Love to you, it was of body and soul
I see the most wonderful days of my life all with you by my side

Turning again, I see a cavernous room; I cannot see the end
A room full of our dreams, hopes, desires
I start walking through it, so much to see
Looking at all of them, don't want to miss one

Everywhere I look, it is you and I, impossible to break apart
There we are together, no one ever between
Getting married, proclaiming our love, for all the world to see
Starting our life together, the way it was meant to be

Learning all there is to know of you
About each other,  so much respect
All my life from before there was you is nothing 
Life only began When I met you.



I start running, so much to see
looking...grasping, wanting to gaze all of us before me

I see us, hand in hand, year after year
Growing old together, a true loves tale
Enjoying each other's company
Never tiring of each other, adoring all that is us

I'm running faster than I have ever run
The dreams keep going on forever
There is no end to this room,  so much of us to come
I stop and look around, thinking of all I've seen

I wake opening my eyes
Smiling to myself, trough my tears of joy
Thank You, God, in your loving heart
For letting me see all the love you have granted me
