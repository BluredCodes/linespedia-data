Journey of minds 
Linked it seems 
Thoughts very alike
A bond is felt 
Dreams expressed
Shared and held
A desire is there
To learn more
The turning of a page
Another thought expressed
The more I see of you
The more I'm enamored 
I see such a gentle heart
Sweet and pure
Lightness, airiness
A peace divine
A yearning to embrace
To feel your warmth inside
Your smile so enchanting 
The thought of it brightens my day
As this life's journey progresses
With each turn of the road
I'm glad I share with you
