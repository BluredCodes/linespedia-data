I have a vision, a dream
Of a woman who from the start
Captures my heart
A sweet beautiful woman
One who walks with me
She is with me in all my thoughts and desires
She will feel all my love
She will hold my heart in her hands
And all that I give her, close to her heart
And love me as I love her
Cherishing each and every moment together
I've found her and I adore her
In my eyes she is the ultimate
When we kiss
All else in the world disappears
And there is only her and
