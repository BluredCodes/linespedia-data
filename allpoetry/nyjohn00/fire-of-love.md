This love I feel
What can I say
It holds my very soul
We greet each other
With a lovers kiss
Moist inviting more
The tips of our tongue
Caress each other
Communing the need we share
When we embrace
We wrap so tight
The feel of you so close
Pressing tight this embrace of ours
Pressing closer still
The clothes we wear 
Seem to vanish in the heat of this embrace
Heat that ignites a fire
A fire of love that rages on
And will last for all times
