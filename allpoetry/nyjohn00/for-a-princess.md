For a Princess
                                                                       
My thoughts are always centered on you 
Picturing your beauty at the first light of day.
Wanting to wish you a good morning 
Hearing your sweet voice echo reply

Your warm heart, has captured my soul
My soul adores, and welcomes its embrace
It seeks you and wants you
You give it such peace

You are so exceptional, no one your match 
your smile, your laugh, the way you say good night 
Sends chills to my spine, it makes me love you
For all I see in you

You are like an angel, my dream come true
A princess, a friend, a lover, a lady I truly adore
I'll love you forever, this Princess of mine
You are my everything , You are my life
