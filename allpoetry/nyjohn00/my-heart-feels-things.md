My heart feels things
So full of love I am
The snows of winter 
Can not quell the fire
It rages out of control
An yet the pain of missing you
Is a knife inside my soul
It twists and turns cutting so
The pain is so much
I try to tell me, in al do time
But my mind doesn't listen
It thinks and thinks
It wonders so
It wants your love so much
It needs your smile your sweet embrace
Or it curls back inside
It feels pain so it wants to 
Build walls so high
Walls to keep the feelings out
To keep me safe inside
I fight these feels time after time
I know your not only mine
But God the pain of wanting you
The love that is inside
Can not be stopped 
Can not be walled at all
So I am open to missing you
For such a long long time
It is part of love, part that can not be denied
And loving you is all you deserve
Now and for all time
