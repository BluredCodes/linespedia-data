What do you do
When you love someone
So much that each and every moment
Is filled with love for them

You met out of chance
A surprise to you both
Both committed to another
But the feelings grows

We meet and we talk
A relationship expands
The spark of love
Takes over the mind

This person you find
Is the most perfect in the world
Beauty that is true
That flows to all around

You tell your feelings
She responds in kind
It is then that you realize
You are hers forever

You talk of this and that
You dream and make plans
The life that you want
Is with her all the time

Then you realize
You have made her problem
Her other in passing
Shows you this is so

So what do you do
You will love her forever
Desperately loving and wanting
But for you or for her

Love that is true
Must be strong for her
To have her and hold her
If in your mind only then

Or are you selfish
Wanting her for you
Do you fight and struggle
To keep her for you

You know that you love her
Her over all
Her happiness is all
The world to youWritten long before I decided
