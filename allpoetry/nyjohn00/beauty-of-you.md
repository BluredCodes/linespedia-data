title: "Beauty of You"

Another night of feelings so deep
Of wanting us together
The tears come like they always do
God my heart longs for you
There is no one in this whole world
Who makes me feel like you do
If I could just find the words
To tell you all I feel
But each time I get this want
The words are eaten by the thoughts
I want to wrap you in my arms
To feel your warmth next to me
To know that with each breath
Your love is eternally mine 
For you to feel in my lovers embrace
That all my thoughts of you
Thoughts that fill my world
With the beauty of you
