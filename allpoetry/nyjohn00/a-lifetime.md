Each time you say you love me,
You really touch my heart.
That's why I can't stop thinking 
Every minute that we are apart.
Every time we see each other,
we play these little teasing games.
You fill my life with laughter
Love and wonder true
That's why I want to share a lifetime,
My lifetime...with you.
