title: "Her Shinning Knight"

Walking thru the mist of time
Toward a castle shrouded in mist
The tops of the ramparts you can barely see
The towers on the corners, mighty and steep
Many have come, Celtic knights one an all
Polish princes, Germans, Slavs
They come to win the hand of the maiden
And she finds none, truly worthy of her love
But to my amaze as I approach this wall
The drawbridge lowers, the gate raises tall
I walk thru this arch leading my steed
 The fog suddenly clears
And I am face to face with
A maiden so pure
She offers me her heart
Telling me of her love
She says she sees, her shining knight
the one, of whom she dreams
She says her heart, has told her this is the one
The one who will love her, protect her from all harm
I mount my war steed, holding reins tight
And lift her in my arms to sit in my arms
Turning about to leave this castle keep
I find the countryside clear of the mist
The flowers are all in bloom
Songbirds in the air
We start our new life
In true loves embrace
