God how she love to tickle me
She knows I can't control
What starts out as an embrace
Leaves me squirming all over the place
She pokes and probes 
Looking for the spot
That will ether make me
Lift right off the floor
Or curled up in a ball
Laughing , laughing, out of control
She laughs and giggles
Oh she loves this game
When she gets this look in her eyes
A glimmer and a gleam
Kind of devilish 
And imp of happiness you see
God how she loves to tickle
She says she loves her hands on me
And even though I jump and squirm
I love her hands on me as well
