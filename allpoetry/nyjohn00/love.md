I know I found my one true love,
Sent from heaven above.

When I look into her eyes I can't help but smile
She makes everything worthwhile.

I tell her my feelings from the heart
So did she from the very start.

I know she loves me, God, so much
More and more as our feelings touch

I could not see her not in my life,
For with her there is no strife

I can't help but know
Love will always just grow and grow.
