Going through me day
Doing all that has to be done
My mind keeps drifting
To thoughts of us

I never knew there could be
Someone such as you
You own me with a smile
All my life is changed

You fill all my days with yearning
Wanting you at my side
Needing just to hear your voice
Feel your hand in mine

I count the hours
Waiting to be together
Finally the time does come
Flying by and back to counting

In all the time that we have had
Only one constant clearly shows
My love and wanting you
Increases, always more
