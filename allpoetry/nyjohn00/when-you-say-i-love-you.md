When you say, "I love you"

When I hear those words
God they mean so much
It's not just the words
It's who is saying them
Those words would be empty
And have no meaning
If they were not from YOU

Your love means so much
I face everyday knowing
Your love holds me tight
I feel it so much
See it everywhere
It is in your smile, all the words you say
It is in the tear I saw today
