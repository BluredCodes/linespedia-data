title: "Had My Parents Known you"

I have been 
Alone for so, so long a time
My momma left for heaven
I was just thirteen at that time
Living here with sister
Moving to my other one
My dad would come and visit
God I wanted him to stay
Now they both are gone
I like to think and imagine
How they would be today
What would they think of you
Would they adopt you as their own
I picture my mom
In her own way
Smiling and telling me son
This woman you love
Is one to cherish
Hold on to her
Be her love
Do it with all of your might
My dad would sit with his pipe
He had a twinkle most times
I see him make you, my love
His favorite of all times
He would laugh and tell you stories
Stories with a twinkle in his eye
And he would tell me truthfully
Son, make her proud
So now I pledge to always be there for you
To love you for all time, care and cherish you greatly
My parents, I know would want me to
They would love you dear, as do I
