title: "A Lifetime With You"

I'll never forget a moment
I'll never forget the thrill
I'll never forget a second
Of the time I've spent with you

Each and every minute
No matter what we do
Shopping, browsing, talking
All that matters is, it's with you

If I could wish for
Anything I could ever want
What I'd wish for is more and more of you
More and more of you, a lifetime spent with you
