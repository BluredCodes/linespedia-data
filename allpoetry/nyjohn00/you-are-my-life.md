---
title: "You are my life"
writer: "nyjohn00"
slug: "you-are-my-life"

At times I get so down
It is when we are apart
I feel like I am suffocating
Needing a breath of your love

When we are at a distance
I get so hungry
I need the food of your love
It feeds and nourishes, makes my heart beat

Being apart leaves me weak
You are the strength of my soul
It is you who bring out the best in me
You are the grace of God above
