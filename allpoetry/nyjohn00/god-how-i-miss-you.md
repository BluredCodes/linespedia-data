God how I miss you
What even just your voice means to me
Honey it sends tingles 
All over me 
I picture you and your smile
At times I think that smile is just for me
I imagine your lips
How they feel on mine
The caress of our tongues
As they meet divine
The feel as we embrace
The warmth that flows
God how I miss you
It's you that makes me smile
