A Part of Each Other 


It was like a dream -
We were together
I felt you breathing
I danced in your eyes
You danced in mine
I stole a kiss

God being with you
Time spent always flies
Just YOU, make my heart warm
I love your laughter
I could listen for hours
I wrapped you in my arms
I hugged you

Our souls are together
They hold each other close
God's Angels danced
The heavens seemed so bright
All above smiles at us
We were together

From the first, meeting for the first time
We saw our selves as one, our hearts joined
In that fleeting minute, the world around us ceased
We became a part of each other, our hearts were one
Souls touching souls, in an embrace of our love
We yearn and want, together as one

It was something fresh and new
Something we had never felt before
Eye to eye we took a part of each
To hold so tight together
A dream was born, a dream of us to come
If this is a dream, then I'll sleep for eternity
