You took your time
Your own valuable time
Time from this 
Time from that
So much to do
You are always doing
Doing for friends
Doing for family
And now doing for me
You took your time 
Your own precious time
To spend with the man you love
You do for one 
You do for all
Never for just you
Sweetheart let me do for you
Let me give you hours of attention
Nights of adoring glances
Give me the time to listen to your day
And then go to sleep in my arms
