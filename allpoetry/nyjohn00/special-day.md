I treat one day 
One day each month
It is just one day
Out of all the rest
It is so special
I treasure it oh so much
And no one knows but us
It started on a day
A little while ago
It was a month in the fall
September was its name
We met by chance, the merest chance 
Destiny it seems is true
And for all you want, you all you might
It was love at that first sight
So now I write you cards 
Expressing all I feel
Telling you how much I adore
Embracing you all the more
So on this 21st day
I'd really love to say
Thank you love 
For giving me
28 months of heaven
