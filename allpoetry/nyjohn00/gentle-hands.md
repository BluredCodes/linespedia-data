title: "Gentle Hands"

My heart feels held by gentle hands
It is surrounded by your love
It feels you breathe your love to it
Sustaining breath of life
It beats for you
Beats in time with your heart
Faster when you are near
My precious love
This heart is home in your embrace
It feels your presence
Even when we're apart
To you my heart reaches
It needs your touch so much
