I woke one day and realized
That my world had changed
It went from drabs of gray
To all the colors ever imagined

I thought my life was over
Nothing would ever change
I'd do my best
Days would come and days would go

I met a person wonderful
She brought my smile immediately
I looked at her, she looked at me
We saw wonder in both our eyes

She gave me back my life
I'm now in love with life
She brought out feelings
I thought were long, long dead

We love each other so, so much
Just her presence makes me smile
I look to make her happy
And hold her in my arms

There is not one day that goes by
I don't thank her for her love
I want her to know
The two of us are one
