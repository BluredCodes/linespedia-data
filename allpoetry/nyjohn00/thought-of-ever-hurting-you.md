The thought of ever hurting you
Slighting you
Making you upset
Saying the careless word
Said in teasing jest
If it were to hurt you
Your tender heart
A heart this heart of yours
I cherish its every beat
It has made my world bright
Heart that has held mine
Nothing in it but love
Oh it cares for me so
God I love you so
For this heart is not in your chest
It is in your feelings 
The thoughts you keep
This heart is all you are
I feel its smile and its pain
I feel it like my own
With out your heart
Mine would be all alone
