Doesn't she see
Doesn't she know
I need her very near
A day without is way too long

I look forward to seeing her
My heart does race
I smile and say its almost time
And then something happens its not to be

She worries about letting me down
But things come up, we can't control
And I don't mind waiting
For you see, she is all to me

So I'll go about my day tomorrow
I'll have her on my mind
I wish she was, mine alone
And we would be together
