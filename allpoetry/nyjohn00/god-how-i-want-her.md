God how I want her
We were meant to be
She is a dream come true
If I would describe
A perfect companion for me
I'd list a list so long
But I'd be describing her

I've only know her for a short time
But feel I've loved her all of my life
In this little time together
We've talked more than some do in a lifetime
Sharing this and sharing that
We talk in words and we talk with out eyes
So much love we share

I wasted so many years
Without her by my side
She makes me whole, makes me happy
Oh I love all she is
If I had but one wish
I'd wish that she would be happy and loved
She would be with me evermore
