Always Missing You

 Seems like only yesterday
When I first looked into your eyes.
I was engulfed by an undying passion
To live within those sunset skies

Your eyes had the warmth of love
Their deep brown color
Embraced my very heart
I felt a feeling I had never felt

The woman I have come to know
The time is never enough
Has since, captured my EVERY emotion
And my heart to a certain end

Before my meeting with "Destiny"
We talked and talked some more
After the first evening we spent together
I always wanted to have you around.

Had so much fun with you, honey, 
God the thoughts we shared
My heart felt so sad  
When it came time for goodbye.

But this meeting couldn't last forever
There was so much left to say
When you said you had to go
You took a piece of my heart.

Going back to my nocturnal wish
I wanted you by my side.
Wished we would last a lifetime,
A friend, I could only dream of more

Since that evening I've come to see
The you inside, a wonder so true
The passion of your heart
The love you have given me

I'll always be careful of your heart so true
I will always give it all the love it needs
My innocent heart will always be longing for you
So in love with and missing you.
