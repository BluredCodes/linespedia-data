Traveling the path
Learning the turns
They twist and turn
So many different ones
Seeing all the paths 
Paths you've already taken
Oh a smile breaks
There you are as a little girl
God your smile so pure
There is still the same twinkling eyes
Eyes that show your love
I see you playing with your friends
Your heart felt so much even then
I see you talking with your dad
Wanting him to yourself
Through your eyes I see his smile
You are his pride and joy
And there us your mom
She has the same glow as you
The twist and turns in you mind
So many paths to explore
I see you now as a teen
I feel your wants and desires
I ask God why was I, not there at that time
I see you at your first dance
Dancing, laughing with friends
These paths and chapters
In your life fill me with so much love
Seeing you then and seeing you now
This epic of your life, I want to see
I want it in great detail
I want to feel it as my own
To know you as I know me
I will be reading these chapters
The chapters of your life
I'll continue this journey
This journey in your mind
I hold each thought you share
Precious and close to my heart
