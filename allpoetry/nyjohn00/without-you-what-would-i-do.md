Without you, what would I do!


Now that I have found you
I would be lost, empty without you.

You brighten my every day with just the thought of you.
You make me laugh , in my heart by just being you.

You hold me to let me know how much you care.
I love holding you in my arms, it where you belong
Because I know we were destined to be.

To spend the night with you, sigh a dream
Able to hold you every minute of it thru our sleep
But this is not all I want, it is not even close
I want forever,,, day after day,,, A lifetime of loving you

My heart stopped when you said that you loved me.
It could not fathom that you would love me so

I just wanted to tell you, so you will know to your Soul
I mean it with all of my heart,
I Love You!,,,, forever , for eternity
