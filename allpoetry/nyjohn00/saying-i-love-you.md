Saying I love You...

I say I Love You,
And I say I Need You
But the words don't tell you all I feel
The total scope of love
It flows so deep, filling all of me
You are in all I do and say

If you could touch my heart you'd feel it beat in the joy
I have when I hear your voice say my name
I look upon your face and the beauty holds me in awe
I see the glow that comes from within, God I love it so.
I'm the lucky one and I'll tell you again, that you should love me so
To have you next to me is all I want for eternity

When we started, this love of ours seemed so special
It was a gift from God, to share his love with us
The joy and love you give to me could only come from above
I knew that for me, forever with you, forever is not long enough, 
So I cry...
This world may end, but our love will always be
