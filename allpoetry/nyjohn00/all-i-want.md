ALL I WANT


There's no sound like the sound of you sleeping...
It's the sound I want to hear next to me at night...
There's no word like the name I call you...
It's the song I sing to make me feel right...
I don't want to change your way of thinking
I don't want to change what you believe
I don't want you to change a single thing

All I Want Is Your hand in mine
Eternity
