Just for you

I try and try to show you
Just what I really feel
I just hope that you see
Just what you mean to me
A love that is stronger
Just made for you and me
A love that is true
Just yours and yours alone
My heart beats wildly
Just beats for you
And when I look into your eyes
Just you and I are there
My heart truly invites you
Just to feel my passion
Come wrap yourself around me
Just you and I forever
