Tonight I sit and feel alone, no one to hold tight
Though deep within my soul I'm whole and complete 
We have not been apart for long, a minute is eternity
But now the hours apart begin, they mount so rapidly
I yearn to have you back in my arms
The thought of your smile brightens my soul,
I cannot begin to thank you, my angel, and my love
Passions kiss, the warmth of your embrace
love I see deep in your eyes, these memories
memories I hold so tight are all I have on this night
I long for these memories while we are apart 
Through the long lonely night, the hours pass so slowly
But when I wake on tomorrow's morn
The thought of you alone brings me the light.
The silent longing I have for you,
Your laughter makes my world go round,
Your teasing, sweet nature is my sun,

You're the one.
I love you completely, forever more
You are all that is important, God I need your smile
You are everything to me, without you I'm nothing
And I can only but imagine, a time for us
When with each evenings repast, I hold you tight
To kiss your lips goodnight and good morning
After a night filled with dreams of you,
Of our time spent together,
You make every one come true.
You are the answer to every prayer,
Every wish made upon a star
To think about a life with out you
Makes my heart ache so
I feel I have loved you forever
God this love does grow 
You are every loving thought, my one forevermore
