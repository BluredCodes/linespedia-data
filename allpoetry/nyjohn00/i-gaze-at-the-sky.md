I gaze at the sky
it reflects life
sky cloudy and bleak
Clouds dark and stormy
Turmoil in these clouds
They boil and they roll
They are life's problems, worries galore
The angry words uttered
Tears that were cried
Problems on the job
See the child cry
The myriad of life
On and on these clouds seem to go
And then a break appears
A lightening it seems
It lightens some more 
Then breaking open, hope ensued 
A beautiful blue sky, radiant in splendor
And the sun shining through
Blinding my eyes
This sun was you
No more the bleak dreary days
But days filled with hope, caring and warmth
Filled with our love
God I need you to stay
