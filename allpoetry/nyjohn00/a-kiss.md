A kiss can ask, "Do you love me?"
A kiss can tell you feelings, "Honey I miss you so." 
A kiss can ignite passion, wanting it never to end
A kiss can make you cry with joy, that she loves you so
A kiss can be a promise, of true loves embrace
And that's the kind of kiss my love,
I'll always give to you!
