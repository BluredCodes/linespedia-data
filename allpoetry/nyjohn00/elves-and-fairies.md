Elves and fairies
Leprechauns and gnomes 
All dance and sing light and airy 
Wee people are all around
They play their games on me
A trick a prank, and magic dust everywhere abound
They laugh in glee as they play with me
They know my mind, this mortal shell
Knowing I'm their friend, they saw my need, before I did 
They saved me from my hell
They were there when we met
And worked their spells and magic fairy dust
It made our hearts beat as one, a complete set
It made them smile, to see my love
To feel the happiness they had done
There is one riding on a dove
With a fairy hovering over there, to guard that all is well
You might think my mind is in another universe
All I try to do is explain this love of mine
Explain it to all in this verse 


They truly are my friends
