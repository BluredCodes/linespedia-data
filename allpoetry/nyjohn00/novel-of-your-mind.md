I'm told that a relationship is like a book
A novel, a story never complete
Each day a new chapter
This journey goes on and on
To know someone 
Feel their thoughts
To travel through their mind
To feel someone and know them well
We laugh and talk awhile
You let me in
To travel the paths
And read the novel of your thoughts
I have to admit
That this epic I'm in
This marathon of your thoughts
Has mesmerized, hypnotized
Addicted me to wanting more
So now with each day
I eagerly await
A new vision through your eyes
