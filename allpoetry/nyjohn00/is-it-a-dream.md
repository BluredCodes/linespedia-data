You said to me this morning
That our love is like a dream
That sometimes you
Wonder if you will wake up
This love and I gone
You're back in your old life
You caught me by total surprise
I ask myself, what are her desires
Does she want this to be real
Does she wish to awake
Surely life was simpler before this love
In ways less heartaches

I wonder, ponder this


Is it all a dream
Will it last forever
Are we just in a long winters sleep
Dreaming of a life that is better

Is it the memory 
Of whispered loves vow
The sigh of its caress
Only a hearts craving

All the times spent
Doing this and that
The hand in hand as we walk
Is it the longing for nearness

The times we've talked
Opening our inner selves
All the sharing of feelings
Only the desire for intimacy

Moments captured in a smile
loves sweet gaze
Eye to eye with only a smile
The want of closeness, unsurpassed

The embrace, as we meld
Holding so close, pulling you tighter 
To remove every trace of separation
The need to become one with you

Passions needs, urgent caresses
Our rapture of motion 
Chasing the pleasure of love
Only the yearning for complete love

Kisses that span all our feelings
The sweet little pecks, the lingering caress 
Tongue caressing tongue in a lovers dance
Desires to impart our loves proclamation

Has all this been, the swirling misty
Eddying on a becalmed sea of sleep
Is it just our minds wanting this love
Enchanted dream brought on with the magic of fairies
Intoxicating elixir of life, imbue me and never part
I never want to wake 
Will I awake and be alone, empty of feelings
Will you wake, and find your life that was

Or is this love real
