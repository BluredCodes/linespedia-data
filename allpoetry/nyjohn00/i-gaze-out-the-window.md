I gaze out the window, thoughts running deep
Reflecting on my life, all that has been
All that will come, wondering, imagining
I see the pure white snow of days gone by
The promise of spring, the sweetness of the air
The heat of summer, the sound of bees
The crispness of fall, rustle of leaves

I think of all the people, I've met
All the places I've seen
The mirth of children the happiness around
I feel the lost of a friend, the emptiness
The loneliness that invades
Possesses my very soul
Is this all that will be

I gaze out the window, thoughts running deep
Now seeing my reflection, with you next to me
I feel your love all around me It holds me, oh so tight, 
Now, I'm not alone, always with you in my heart
My love is with me, never to part
All my life was a shadow. My reality is now
