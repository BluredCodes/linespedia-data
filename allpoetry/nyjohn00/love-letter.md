Its only been a few days since we last met
And yet it seems forever, since in your embrace
They say that absence makes the heart grow stronger
This I never knew to be true
But oh how my heart has learned to long for you

When we are together
I pray to God to make time stand still
So the moments of the evening last forever
Need you near, holding you tight
Feeling your heart next to mine

I want to be your everything
To fill your heart like you fill mine
Becoming a part of all you do
Having our futures blend to one
Becoming one in all
