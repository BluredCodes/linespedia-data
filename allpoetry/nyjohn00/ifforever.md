If...Forever


If the words I speak could make you laugh,
I'd talk to you forever.
If my embrace could make you feel protected,
I'd hold you forever.
If accepting and understanding you could make us nearer,
I'd listen to you forever.
If by holding your hand I could make you tingle inside,
I'd hold your hand forever.
If your eyes are the windows to your soul, and I could see your thoughts,
I'd stare into them forever.
If a simple kiss could make you glow, make you as dreamy as me
I'd be kissing you forever.
If the when I whisper in your ear telling you all I feel, makes you feel beautiful,
I'd whisper those words to you forever.
If the by way I touch you makes you feel wanted, and needed
I'd caress you forever.
If talking to you, and embracing you,
And listening to you, and holding you,
And staring into your eyes, and kissing you,
And whispering to you, and caressing you
Could make you feel all my love, need of you, and desire, 
I'd willingly do all those things forever.
And if someone asks me how much do I love you,
I'd say, "So much, it would last forever."
