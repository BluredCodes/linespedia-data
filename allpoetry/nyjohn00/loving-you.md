I love you,,,
when you ask if I'm sure,,,
I probably feel like you do when I ask that,,
I feel like ,,,,honey If I live to be thousands of years old,,,
and spent everyday with you,,,
I'd still feel like I have not had enough of you.
When I tell you I need you,,,
I'm telling you my heart and soul yearns for you to be next to me,,,,
When I tell you I want you,,,
its because I feel alone and am only complete when your with me
and want to hear your voice,,,see your face,,,,,
When I tell you I love you,,,,I'm telling you all of those things,,,
I'm saying I want, need, desire, adore, put on a pedestal, feel that in
you
I have found a wonder a perfection that makes my heart swell
