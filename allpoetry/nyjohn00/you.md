When I think of you
I remember holding you
the smell of your hair
of your body pressed so close
the feel of your lips on mine
mmmm the look in your eyes

I hear your sigh
feel your arms tighten around me
as we whisper our love
the kiss goes on and on
we are joined
soul to soul

We always walk so close
Hand in hand
The light touch of shoulders
The hand lightly caresses
So much is said
But no words expressed

Your voice is like music
It awakens my soul
As the look of you
Brings my heart to explode
I love you precious
No words can express
