title: "If I Could hold You Now"

If my arms were around you now
My heart would be happy
My soul on fire
My every want secure
My life complete

In my arms is where you belong
Day after day
Year after year
My arms were made for you
They fit only you

There are hugs
There are embraces
There is holding
With you they all are right
With you they all blend to one

My arms are an extension 
Extension of my love
They hold you tight
Safe from all harm
Surround you with love

So sweetheart
Come into my arms
Let my love surround you
Feel all the love I have
Stay with me forever
