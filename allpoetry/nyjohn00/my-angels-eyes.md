My Angels Eyes


I get lost in your eyes of brown
Full of the warmth, full of love
When they look at me on any day
They say you are mine in every way 

Eyes, more rare than a blue diamond
I would trade it for a glance from you
Eyes kissed with heavens worship.
Eyes showing, the deepest passion.

Eyes so beautiful, they speak to me
They tell me your thoughts
Your eyes on a beautiful face
They sparkle like stars in distant space

I'll gaze into those warm embracing eyes
I see a dream of us in these limpet depths
No one ever looked this love at me as you do
I'll hold this love so tight, in an angels embrace
