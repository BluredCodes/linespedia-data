title: "Christmas Since There Is You"

Since you,,,
The decorations sparkle more
The carols have a new meaning
The snow on the ground has a beauty
The laughter of people fills my heart
The magic that is Christmas is so alive

With you,,,
The love abounds
The twinkle in your eye
The warmth and caring
The meaning of the time
The magic you see, is given to me

I thank you my love
For all that you give
For your thoughts and feelings
For all that is you
For giving me your love
The meaning of Christmas is clear
