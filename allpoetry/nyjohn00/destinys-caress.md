Destinies Caress

God I want you 
I need you now
The times that we're apart
I long and yearn for you
You never leave my mind
I miss your gentle touch
The smile that is always there
The way you greet me
When we're alone
Kissing me so deep
Wrapping around
Squeezing tight
Telling me you love me too
This longing and yearning never stops
It only grows and grows
It tells me things
Tells us things
That we should be together
Together not for a day
An hour or for less
But be together for all time
In destinies caress
