Until the Day I die

Suddenly out of nowhere
She came into my life
"Are you the one?"
Are you the one who carved your name into my heart
Are you the one that God sent to me
To give me love
To give me peace and forever 
"Are you the one?"
To live your life with me, grow old and be with me
Who is always in my dreams..
Are you the one
That God had created for me
Are you the one , who is mine eternally
The one who keeps me dreaming
When I am sad & tired
Who gives my life a meaning?
Until the day I die..
Are you the one?
