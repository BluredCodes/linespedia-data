Going about my day
Doing errands and chores 
Want you to know
Your never far

Always on my mind
Wondering how you are
Are you happy
Is your laughter loud

Did you get your errands done
All you wanted done
Are you now relaxing
Or thinking of what needs done

Time when we're together
Always flies so fast
It seems anticipation
Is right next door to goodbye

You are always going
Running here and there
Wish we had more time
Time for us to share
