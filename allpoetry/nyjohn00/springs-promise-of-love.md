As the Crocus blooms in springs first life
Pushing through the frozen soil
To the light of day
I remember how it was before
The life surging forth, before you
The frozen soil is like my cold heart
Unfeeling dead unfruitful to all
Gripped by the frosty hold of winters cold spirit
Unfeeling, there was no love inside
The cold had gripped it so
It had felt the life of spring
The heat of summers passion
But it had been stilled
By words of hate
Feeling dashed and torn
Then you appeared 
You springs promise of life
You the fountain of love
Who made my heart thaw and bloom
Opened the blossom of my heart to the sun
Filled it with warmth and love
And promises of life to come
