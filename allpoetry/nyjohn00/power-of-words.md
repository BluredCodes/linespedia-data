Power of Words

I read a poem, a new one I'm sure
It spoke of words, of words touching so
It said that these words, had such a power
They touched so deeply, right to the soul
These words made worlds appear
Opening doors, doors that never existed before
Closing doors that need to be closed
Touching a heart and making it yearn
Even bringing a tear or two
To write like that would be a dream
To have my words affect someone so
Knowing I had touched someone deeply
Writing about a kiss and making them feel
The kiss, the embrace, the gaze into each other's eyes
To feel the tingles, the heart felt sighs
The feel of contentment with her at my side
A gift from the gods it would surely be
To be able to put these thoughts to paper
And make you feel
