A Wish On Each One

The day has ended
 the feast has past
Blue crab skeletons lay in the sand
The fire burn so bright
 You nestle in my arms the log to our back
The sky is alight, the fire of stars gleaming bright
Leaning back you point at each one
And together we make a wish on each one
