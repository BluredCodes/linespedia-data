title: "Blessed I am"

Friends come and go
I've seen it over the years
But there are a few who are always there
Friends true forever

I'm blessed with a friend
Who is all a friend could be
A Best friend who is so close
Out thoughts are inseparable too

This friend is witty and charming
Caring, protective and all that you could say
We are happiest together, always smiling
It's the best that there could be

Blessed I am for you see
This friend whom I describe
She is my one true love who I describe
So blessed I am, I have it all
