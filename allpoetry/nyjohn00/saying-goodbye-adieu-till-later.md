We said goodbye
Telling all our love
Saying we would talk 
Later Online in the night
We embraced oh so tight
A love made in heaven
And kissed the kiss of love
Moist inviting, passionate 
God it tells so much
Its says I love you forever more
I need you for all time
I want you sweetheart, dearest mine
Till the end of time
Our goodbyes always take so long
Neither wanting it to end
We await the day to come
To never say goodbye again
