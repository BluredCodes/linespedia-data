There's a rhythm about you
It's the center of my of life
It sets my heart in motion
Makes it yearn for you

There's luster in your eyes
Eternal source of peace
I could gaze forever, longing, needing you
Cherished by the one made for you

There is love in your thoughts
I could listen to you forever
All inspire true love and harmony
They give meaning to my life

There's sweet songbirds sound in your voice
It's the soft tender voice of kindness
Bringing contentment and warmth
I need you there all the time

There's a magic in your kiss
It is the pure kiss of love
I feel the depth of your want
Our lips weld in loves embrace, perfect as one

There's sweetness in your smile
It's makes the world disappear 
A light glows blinding me
I see only your loving eyes

There's something about your touch
Teasing tickle to passions embrace
It is very addicting
I need your touch all the time

This list could go on forever
There is so much more to tell
Every time I look at you
I see more of you to love
