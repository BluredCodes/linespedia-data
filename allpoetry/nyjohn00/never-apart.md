Sweetheart, you fill my mind so
On every breath I take there is you
I walk along and see a flower
I think as delicate and beautiful as it is
It is nothing compared to you

God you fill my  life
Together or apart, there is only you
When I hear laughter, it is yours I hear
I feel the wind on my face and
See it blowing your hair

You are my life to such a degree
Its you, I see whereever I go
So when we're apart 
And you wonder if I'm thinking of you
Sweetheart, my love, we're never apart
