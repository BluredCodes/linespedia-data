The feel of you in my arms
As you lay by my side
Thigh to thigh, chest to chest
Lips that don't want to part
Arms holding you tight
Holding with all of my might
Wanting you to know
Just how I adore

God how I love you
I hang on your every thought
I need to tell you 
Need to show you
All that I do feel
Tell and show the only way
Of how you make my heart smile

The intimacy that we share
Covers all of us
Thoughts shared, nothing held
The closeness that we love
You want my happiness
I live for yours
The feel of you in my life
