If someone asked for me to describe you
I'm not sure where I would even begin
Would I first tell them of the way 
My body feels when so close to yours
Or maybe I would start with your looks 
And how proud I am to be seen with you
Something of how peaceful and relaxed 
I feel when I am with you
How I feel with you in my arms the feeling I never
Want to end
Perhaps I might tell them 
How simply sweet you are 
And how you treat me much better than I deserve
Or how I feel God placed you in my life 
And I'm so sure we're meant to be
Maybe they want to know more 
About the things you say to me 
Which always leave me with a smile
Or just maybe they even want to know 
About the sweet tender way you kiss me 
That always leaves my knees weak
Suppose they asked me 
What makes me long to love you so
I would have to tell them 
That it has everything to do with the way you love me                                                                                                                                                                           
So unconditional and never asking for more than I can give
My only prayer now is that one day 
I will be able to love you as well as you have loved me
