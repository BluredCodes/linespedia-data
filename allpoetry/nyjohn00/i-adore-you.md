The winds whisper softly in my ear
I listen closely, a sound I yearn for
Your voice, oh your sweet voice
I look to the sky
The clouds glow in the sun
Their radiance is so like your skin 
The clouds carry your smile
I feel loved, it is all around me
My Beauty ..My Angel
I adore you....
