title: "talking without words"

I have written of this before
But God it is so true
I love being with you
Tonight was just for coffee
Tea, hot chocolate or your desire
And we talked for over and hour
Very few words were used

We talked with our hands held
And the look within our eyes
The words were of love and missing
How much we feel
Words to fill a pamphlet
The words spoken with our eyes
Would fill a lengthy novel

Sometimes the quiet times
Where we sit over a table
Talking a little, sharing a thought
Looking eye to eye
A hand held, smiles enjoyed
These times utter much more than is heard
It is my heart holding yours
