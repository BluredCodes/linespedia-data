Thinking of us
The lighthouse
Like seeing you for the first time
Hearing you,,seeing you move
Like a dream in light of day

In the light of day 
I ask, how can anyone be so wonderful
So sweet, caring, loving
I see purity, a grace
I want to hold close

In the darkness of my loneliness
When all around is dark and misty
The beauty in you shines
Like a beacon calling me to you
To where we can embrace
