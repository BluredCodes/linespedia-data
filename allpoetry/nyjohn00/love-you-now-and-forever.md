title: "Love You Now and Forever"

You are the person with the gift to see into my heart 
Who sees the world in a view just as I
I look into your heart and melt at the visions so beautiful 
I begin to cry, cry at the wonder of what's inside 
Two souls close together 
Two souls sharing the same gift if love 
The smiles we share go deep inside
Two souls mesh as one
Feeling the same feelings
Longing for the same end 
A time for us to dwell
To lay together holding tight
In true loves embrace 
Love you now and forever and a day
