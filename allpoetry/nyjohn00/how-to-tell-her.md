title: "How To Tell Her"

Sitting in a coffee shop
Laptop on the table
Trying to phrase my thoughts
To tell you what I feel

Sometimes it is so hard
The words just never come
And when they do they still don't
Touch what is in my soul

The burning love
Yearning soul is hard to convey
Why just the thought of you
Makes all the words turn gray

So sipping my coffee pondering how to
She has heard all the words I speak
A smile upon her face
Her eyes always hold me in true loves embrace
 
How to let her feel just all I feel
Will it be the bright smile whenever she is near
Or the gentle touch, my hand upon her back
The thrill I have each time that she is near

She has seen how I treat her
Many years the same
Heard my poems as they're written
All my words of lovers praise

Then the thought did hit me
She knows what's in my heart
My love knows how much I love her
She tells me with her smile
