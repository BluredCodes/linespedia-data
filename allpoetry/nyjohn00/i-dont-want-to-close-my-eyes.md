I don't want to close my eyes

There is a song
It comes from the heart
It speaks of not wanting to sleep
Afraid to miss a moment of you
It hit a chord 
It was me
When I hold you as you sleep
I don't want to go to sleep
I don't want to miss
The sound of you breathing
The feel of you lying so close
The sweet fragrance of your hair
I need you around me so  much
Holding each moment so dear
I'll love you forever
