I'm lost in your embrace of love
Your body enfolding me,
Teasing, caressing, wrapping, holding
Secure in loves sweet breath
In a trance of passion
Emotions reach combustion
Igniting, exploring, discovering
Consuming us in an inferno of love

I deeply breathe the scent of you
It is arousing, nourishing, and sweet.
Our bodies have become one
One, our thoughts, our love, our desires
Heaven holding you tight
Your sigh, a songbird's melody
This moment could last forever
Living, loving, loved together
