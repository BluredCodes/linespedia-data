Would that you would take my love
Hold it tight to you
That you would be my shadow
So close to me and we breathe

We shall breathe the air of love
Crystalline and pure
It will fill all our senses
So close to me and we touch

We shall be so close as to always be touching
Hand in hand, shoulder to shoulder
My arms so tight around you
So close to me and we will see

We shall see the world in love
It will be all around
It will flow from and flow from me
So close to me and we will be

We will be together for now and ever more
My heart is yours, your heart is mine
So my love is yours it always will
Would that you would take my love
