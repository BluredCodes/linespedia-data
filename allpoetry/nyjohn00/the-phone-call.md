During the day
You call me
I call you
Just to feel the touch

We talk, tease
Banter, chat
We tell our feelings
Just to feel the touch

Time is short
Time is long
We want the other
Just to feel the touch

All the time trying
To show our love
In just a few minutes
Just to feel the touch

We say goodbye
Thinking, do they know
How I feel
Just to feel the touch
