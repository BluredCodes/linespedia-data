The touch, you and I
Fingertips to fingertips
Ever so gently.
The tingle of excitement
From one to the other,
The feelings running through our veins.

The feelings we feel are absolute desire.
Desire for nothing else but us to be
Looking into our eyes, into our hearts
Of the one who is yours
Whose heart is yours alone.

Never want to be apart, always on our mind
The word goodbye, does not exist
Wanting every moment to last forever
In love, oh so in love, complete
To live this love, day after day
To live this love for eternity
This desire of ours.
