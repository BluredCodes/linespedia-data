I wish you could see all the beauty I see 
The beauty I see in you
I tell you all the time
Just how great you look
How just the sight of you
Does make my heart melt
But you always deny
Saying you don't see
How can you see
I need a special mirror
That shows you what I see
All the beauty in you
It would show you how
Your eyes sparkle and shine
Each and every time, you look me in the eye
It would show you how I love the way you dress
You do for a tee and jeans
What an evening gown does for the rest
It would show you just how
My heart catches on fire
Every time you say the words, "I love you too"
This mirror would also show you things
That only the special have
For honey it would show
All the love you have inside
