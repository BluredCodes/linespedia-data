Thoughts of you
God when we speak
I don't want it ever to end
And when we kiss
Our lips do blend
There is a yearning
That builds up inside
The feeling of wanting
You by my side
We are together for days
And then days apart
My mind wonders
Tries to picture you
I wonder what it would be like
To wake up each day
Feeling you in my arms
Molding yourself to me
As the haze of slumber ebbs from us
Realization breaks in
To say good night
And wrap us up again
All the times in between
The chores in the morning
Helping you or helping me
Just passing with a smile
And then the relaxing time
Cuddled in your arms
Watching movies 
Just laying reading a book
Spending so much time
Together and in love
