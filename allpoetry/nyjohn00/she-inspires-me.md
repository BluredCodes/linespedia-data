She inspires me to write about my love
Its so easy now you see
She is my precious angel, a precious princess
I want the world to see
She took my heart, all it did was beat
Opened it to a world of love and passion abound

I had felt alone for many years
Learning to hold emotion in
It was for others and not for me
The couples, laughter, smiles
At first I use to envy them, the joy, they have
Convinced myself I didn't need all that they had

But now I know, what they felt, all the wasted years
For now when we are together or apart the love is all around
I still thank you love, and all that you have brought
If ever it were to end, another takes your heart
He would have and hold, giving what you need
I would still be grateful love, you who showed me love
