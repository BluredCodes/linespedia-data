Sweetheart your beauty
Takes my breath away
It lights every ember
Passion does awake

A lot of women feel
They need to dress for desire
Stocking, heels, makeup etc.
To add to their appeal

Honey all you need is you
You in your tee and jeans
The natural makeup you wear
Just the look of you makes my blood boil

So you see my love
It isn't fake things at all
It you I need, your lips on mine
Our arms and legs entwined
