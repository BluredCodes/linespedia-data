Have you ever
Found something
Something so wonderful
Pure and sweet

Been able to see it
To picture how it would be
To feel the joy
If only in your mind

Then you ask
Am I just dreaming
Wishing for the joy to be real
Imagining something that can't be

But can it really be
If two people want
To make the dreams real
This life is ours

I have found what I want
Know what I've found
Please want what I want
Being together is what's real
