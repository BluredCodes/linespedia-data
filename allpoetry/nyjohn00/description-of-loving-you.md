Loving you
Feels like a soft soft rain
Falling on my face

Loving you
It's the calm of my
Hearts peace

Loving you
Makes me laugh and smile at the same time
It makes my heart warm, makes me thankful to be alive

Loving you
To feel your hand in mine
A touch, a feeling natural alive

Loving you
To feel your hair slide along the side of my cheek
To feel your heart beating my head upon your chest

Loving you
So many words to describe it
Yet so few

Loving you
I want to take care of you as you take care of me
As we go on

Loving you
I want to hear your troubles and your joys
To feel and be there for you

Loving you
It is an honor to be trusted so with your deepest, darkest Secrets
With your most heart felt dreams and yearnings

Loving you
Sharing little moments with you, looking into one another's hearts
understanding each other's souls

Loving you
Needing you
Wanting you

Loving you
