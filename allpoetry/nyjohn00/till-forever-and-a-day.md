title: "Till forever and a day"

How long will I love you
How long I'll need your smiles
How long I'll be wanting you near
How long I'll be thrilled by your kisses
How long will I want to listen to you
How long what you think will be important to me too
How long I want your hand in mine
How long I want to hold you near
How long I want to hold you as you sleep
How long I'll need your love to be mine
Till forever and a day
