Walking through the night
The moon high in the sky
Its light flutters to the ground
It lights our way so clearly
The moonlight shimmers in your hair
In this light you seem to float
Your magic overwhelms
Your graceful movements hypnotize
Capture my passion in your grasp
You twirl and are in my arms
Pressing so close are we
I feel the heat of your want
You melt into me
Mold to me
It feels like we have become one
Staring into your eyes
Our lips lightly touch
Whispers of our love
The moist touch of your tongue
Our passions soar
And our kiss goes on
Caresses and sighs
Float in the air
I kiss you oh so deeply
Trying in this kiss
To tell you just how much
I worship, and adore you
Want need you to be mine
To have and hold
Kiss and caress
Cuddle and snuggle
And love you for all time
