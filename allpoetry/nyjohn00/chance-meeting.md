The day we met
Was a normal day
I had done you a favor
Just to be nice

There you were
Nervous and waiting
I asked if it was you
You lit up

My smile was natural
Complimenting yours
My eyes saw a wonder
The wonder of you

I thought I'd never see you again
I thought she is so nice
Wish we were friends
To share our thoughts

You sent me a thank you
Asking to be friends
Amazing me deeply
The wonder of you

Now we are
So much more than friends
Caring and sharing
Loving all true

Over all our time
The things we have shared
I am most grateful
For you being you
