Sitting online
Waiting for that magic someone
Looking at this
Looking at that
Searching for you

I wait for you to a peer
Writing you mail
Writing you card
Smiling as the door opens
Sigh its not you

Card after card
Letter after letter
Search engines galore
All over the web I go
Searching for your smiley

About ready to depart
Saying goodnight 
About to click the mouse
A door opens
Its my love!
