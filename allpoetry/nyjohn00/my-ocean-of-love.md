---
title: "My Ocean of Love"
writer: "nyjohn00"

I think about you so much when we are apart
Thoughts of you swim from my soul
Floating past in the sea of life
Swirling around me
On eddies and currents
Seen for a moment and then gone in the haze

From the depths around me,
My mind Imagines images, of you as I love
Of hearing your laughter, the twinkle in your eye
Seeing the love flow like the never ending waves
Its seen in your smile, like the sun on the water
The music of your voice, like rhythm of the sea 

Your voice to me is like the sirens song
It draws me to you like a ship to the shore
The sound alone makes my heart sigh
Your voice alone brings peace to my mind
I live for your laughter
The smile on your lips

When we are apart 
These memories come so strong
It shows me how apart of me you are
Part of my heart, part of my soul
You are in each and every breath I take
You are my ocean of life
