title: "Wishing Words Would Come"

We talk and talk upon the internet
Chatting for  hours unending
Sending smiles and lol's
Telling all we've done 
We send kisses and hugs
Telling how we miss 
And then its time for bed

I lay there in my bed alone
Thinking of your smile
Remembering the times we've shared
Together and online
The walking , holding hands
The gazing into your eyes
The feel of your lips on mine lifts my soul on high

But as I lay there, waiting for sleep to come
I have this hollow feeling, a yearning very deep
Wishing there was something else, something I could say
A word, a phrase, anything that could make you feel my love
The love that longs to hold you
Never letting go, the love that wants you next to me
For all the days to come

These words I long to find, I search for everyday
I hope that they come to me
So you will know my  everything, that the net is find for some
But what I'm really longing for is to feel you at my side
For you to know that words are words
Words that touch and then they go
But my love will touch you always, and never let you go
