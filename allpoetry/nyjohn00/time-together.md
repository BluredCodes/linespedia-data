We've been together brief in time
We've done a lot of things
Places we've been
All are special

We've met for coffee
We've gone on walks
Off to shopping
Time to talk

We've sat and we've shared
We've spoke and listened
All with caring
Showing our love

We've been together, brief in time
We've done a lot of things
Of all that we've done
Being together is the one
