Driving thru life
There are road signs all around
They tell what we're suppose to do
Turn, stop, this way and that
In this trip of life
There are signs along the way
Signs of how we feel 
Of how others feel as well
The word they speak
And we respond
They're actions and ours as well
We meet those we love
And those that just pass by
Since we met
I want you to know
All my signs have been glad
Full of hope and happiness
The happiness that comes from you
