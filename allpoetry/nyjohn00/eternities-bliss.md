Eternities Bliss 

I smile the biggest smile at the thought of you 

How each and every time together is so perfect  

Hand in hand, hand lightly touching your back as we walk

Sometimes, I think it is a dream, being with you is so perfect 

The love you give me so hard to believe, No one ever before

Has ever made me feel as you do

Then you are in my arms, I know it is meant to be

I feel it is mine, given to me by your heart, so pure

Mine and mine alone, you give me your all

My thoughts are pulled to you 

Like a compass pointing north 

Pointing to a love so true, nothing can stop it's course

Billions of people on this Earth  

How lucky can I be

I found you, fell in love, and you fell in love with me too

I found my perfect one, I'll cherish her ever more

It is with her alone, I'll share eternities bliss
