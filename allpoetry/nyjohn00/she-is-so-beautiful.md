She is so beautiful
Spellbinds me, holds me oh so close
I could gaze at her for hours on end
Listening to her words of thoughts
And the only feelings I will have
Is wanting more of her

It is not just how she says it
Its what she says as well
A window to her heart
And such a picture it exposes
Full of wonder, full of caring
And empathy for all

I look at her at these times
As she bares her thoughts to me
And cannot help but think
And wonder at her too
I tell her at these times, 
Sweetheart this is another reason I love you
