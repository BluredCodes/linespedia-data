Needs 

You don't need fancy jewelry

Bangles, earrings chains galore 

To make me love you. 

You don't need a lot of makeup, 

Eye shadow, blush, mascara, lipstick and more

To cause me to pursue, to sigh when I look at you

You don't need clothes from Gucci, Guess or Gloria VanDer

To make me want you more

You do not need to put on airs, Move or say anything
To make my passions rise. 

All you need is to be YOU

a smiling face; to light my heart

Your feeling and your thoughts

And YOUR warm embrace.
