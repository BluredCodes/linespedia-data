Can't Wait To see You Again

Its been two days 
Since we last met
God it seems an eternity
I'm like an addict
Addicted to your love
I need a constant fix
Of your sweet smiles
The feel of your hand in mine
The sound of your voice as you 
Fill me in on your day
The ways that you laugh as I tease and caress
The essence of you in our loves embrace
God I can wait to see you again
