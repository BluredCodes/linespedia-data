You have taken over all that is me,,
In any corner of myself that I look
No matter how deep I go, you are there
In all I think do and want and desire
In all the futures that I see, there is you

I see us on long walks,,
Always hand and hand
I see us talking, the hours fly
Listening intently, to what each has to say
Loving each other, as no other ever has

I imagine a lifetime spent with you
The happiness comes over me
Knowing you and loving you
Gives me serenity, peace of the heart
In one brief smile, I am yours
