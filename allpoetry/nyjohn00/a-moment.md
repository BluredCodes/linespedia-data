I see us together
Walking the beach
Ocean breakers rolling in
The wind blowing your hair
Listening closely to the waves on the beach
Each one saying, she loves you so
The sound of the wind in the dune grass
The way that it rustles in the wind
Remind me of us
Always standing close
Moving with the wind
Feeling the warmth of your skin 
As we embrace, lips seeking lips
Oh for this moment never to end
