Math of  Life

Integers, differentials, quotients,
Sums, differences, products,
Subtotals, totals, powers, degrees
Averages, means, fractions, decimals
The math of life
Writing our relations in number so true
Two plus two equal four
Always true and you can believe
But in life two plus two can equal kinda four
The number don't fit
They curve and they turn
The geometry of life is a shape all its own
Algebra and trigonometry like wise their turn
The one equation that I can make sense of
You plus Me equals One... a love to last 
It was true before and will be true till the end
So forget about the numbers, the twisting shapes
The curve of a graph, decimal in the right place
Think about us, and know it is always true
