They threaten
They talk
They tell us they are going to end it
They say they hurt
They cannot go on
They say they have nothing
They have no hope
They feel alone
They need someone 
They tell of a means
They make us worry 
They don't realize
They cannot imagine
They have no clue
They hurt us so
They all share a common they, not us

Words are cheap, emotions expensive
