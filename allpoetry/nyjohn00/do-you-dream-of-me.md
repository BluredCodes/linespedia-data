Do you dream of me


Do I fill the wants of your desires
If you were to imagine your perfect love,, is it me?
Am I the one you truly want
Will I ever let you down
Disappoint your very heart
Do you dream of us together
How it will really be
Doing our normal day in and day out
All that needs to be done
Would you dream of sitting next to me
Leaning into my arms
Reading or just spending time
If we were together, each and every day
Would you regret your choice
Would you still love me
Do you dream of spending all your time
Sharing your thoughts and ideas
Having someone to listen, someone who really cares
I hope I'm the one
The one of your dreams
I don't have much to offer you
Only my love, my complete love of you
