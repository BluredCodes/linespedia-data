Dancing With You


Look deep in my heart, Do you know what you will find,
Our special place, it only holds us two

The stars and the moon, reflect in your eyes and hair
A place where our dance begins, as lovers trust in love

Our dance starts out slowly, with each tentative step
Touching bodies, dancing closely, in tune with our yearning

You hold me, oh so tender; you lie your head on my shoulder
I feel you sigh in sweet contentment, this is where you belong

Look inside my heart, it's a very special place, where a couple is one
Where lovers softly touch, hearts dance and spirits do embrace....
