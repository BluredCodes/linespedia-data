My World

You would be my world
The heart of my universe
All the rest of my life
Would circle around you
In my solar system
You would be the sun
Your shining rays
Giving light to my world
Your smile is the source
Of my happiness true
The sound of your voice
Is the gentle breeze
Soothing me, easing me
Bringing me to peace
If only you were mine
