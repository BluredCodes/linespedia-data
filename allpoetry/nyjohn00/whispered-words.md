Whispered Words

The words you whisper to me,
The sound of "I love you"
Your voice so soft and sweet
The sound of your voice,
sweet thoughts in my ear.

I breath you so deeply
Your scent I want to melt into my body
like ice on a hot summer day
Your touch sends chills down my body,
These feelings are always there

You are my life; my destiny,
My soul mate and more
Your voice in my ear
Your arms embrace
The words you whisper to me
