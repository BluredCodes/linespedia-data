title: "Love is"

A feeling, a joy
It is happiness, bliss
It is yearning, longing
It is want, desire
It is a need to please

A joy
Knowing they are happy
Wanting their elation
Knowing their needs
Wanting to please

A happiness a bliss
An inner peace
Knowing they are there
A bliss of contentment
With their presence

A yearning, longing
To spend your time
To share your day
Longing for their touch
Needing their kiss

Knowing their needs
Listening, talking
Comforting, caring
Laughing and crying
Day after day

Wanting their wants
Sharing their dreams
Needing their happiness
Because it is your own
Smiling for them

Love is all of this and more
It is not wanting for yourself
It's wanting for her
Its wanting for us
It last forever
