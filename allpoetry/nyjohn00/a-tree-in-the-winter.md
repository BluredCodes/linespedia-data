A tree standing all-alone
In the depths of winter hold
It is so desolate
So lifeless

Cold and frosted over
The only warmth it could feel
Would be to become some ones fire
Barren of life's simple spark

This was my life 
Before you came to me
Barren not really alive
All life held inside

You were like springs call to life
You made me live again
The leaves did bloom
And I grew, in love with you
