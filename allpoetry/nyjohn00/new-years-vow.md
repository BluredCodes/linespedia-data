On New Years Eve, we should be
Sitting by the light of a fire
Wrapped in each others arms
A glass of wine
Lobster and some shrimp
Gazing into each others eyes

It is a time to renew a vow
A promise made to you
It was made the very first time
I said "I love you so"
It was a vow made so easy
For it came right from my heart

So since we can't be together
I'll take this short moment
To tell you my love
If I spent each moment, that is left to me
Spent with you my love
It would not be enough time
To show and tell you 

I love you
