Missing the one 
Whose presence alone
Brings light and cheer 
To a dreary day

Missing the smile
That automatically
Turns your downs to ups
Your lips to a smile

Missing the touch
The slightest touch
A hand on arm
Shoulder to shoulder

Missing the voice
So sweet, warm and caring
Whose sound makes
My heart swell

Missing the kiss
That says with no words
I love you completely
For eternityTo the woman I miss every second we are apart,,who fills my heart with 
smiles
