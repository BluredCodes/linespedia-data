This Kiss,, as we say good night

With this kiss
I will show you my never-ending adoration, my precious angel

With this kiss
I will tell you, I love you, more than life it's self

With this kiss
I will give you all my feelings rolled into my lips caress

This kiss,
I give you for the rest of our lives

This kiss
I give to you
