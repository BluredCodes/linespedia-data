title: "My Flower"

Walking thru the flower garden
Thinking of how you love roses
I walk through the gardens 
With thoughts of you
Looking for the perfect rose
To maybe take its picture
So you could see it too
But then the thought does hit me
As I see all the flowers in bloom
That each and everyone 
Is just short of what I'm looking for
All fall just a little short
A blemish here or there
It could be how the light reflects
The curling of a petal
The way the stem does move
But what am I comparing them too
True their colors so deep
Their fragrance lifts the soul
And all unique as one
Then the answer hits me,
I knew it all along
I'm comparing them to all I see in you
How I love to watch you walk
The wind lightly moves your hair
And when we embrace I inhale so deep
Trying to make your fragrance mine 
To have it part become a part of me   
So we are never apart
The only picture I'll be happy with
Is one of you, my love
