My Heart's Thought


My sweetheart, my angel from above
Adoring angel, who has captured my heart
I would give you anything, do anything just for you
Just wanting to know that you'll always be near.

You see it was not your outer shell 
that captured my heart
My eyes saw and my heart felt the you, in you
I loved you from the start.

My words flow like water in the river
They say all my heart does feel,
I yearn to be with you,
Anywhere, any place...
Everywhere, every time.

It is this thing about you
A wonder and enchantment, wanting oh so much
The only thing I can offer, I pray you always want
Is all the adoring love, my heart has to offer...

Besides the love,
And beyond the romance,
The most perfect moments of all
Are the ones spent anywhere with you just at my side

You took the heart of mine, closed to the world
Opened it to feeling, love yearning and more
It feels all the joys of love, the ache of needing you more
Forever it will be open to you, my angel from above
