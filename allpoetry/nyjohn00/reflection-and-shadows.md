title: "Reflection and Shadows"

The feel of your hand as we walk side by side
Passing by a store, glancing inside
The sun on the window the reflection of us
Shadows and reflections of us everywhere
Seeing our shadows merge on a sunny day
Dancing side by side, never far apart
The shadows dance, touching and blending
Merging as one
The reflections of us are in all that is around
They reflect our love, warmth and caring
The desires that we have are so intertwine
It is like our minds are one in our love
