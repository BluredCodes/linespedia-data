title: "Our Love Never Grows Old"

Our love always feels so fresh
Like the newly bloomed rose of spring
For no matter how long a time life goes on
My love for you will endure
My love has no beginning
I loved you from the moment of my birth
My love has no ending
I'll love you for all eternity
