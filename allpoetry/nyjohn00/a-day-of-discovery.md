A Day Of Discovery


It was shortly after we first met
We had talked and talked
Never a quiet moment with us
A bond had formed 
Oh so strong
Neither of us knew it then

We were so enthralled by the wonder of us
Learning all we could
We'd gone for coffee, sharing feelings
God how wonderful you are
To talk to you is to talk to myself
So in tune were we 

We decided to meet, spend a day
Getting to know each other
A beautiful day in the fall
It was so sunny, actually warm
And the colors of Fall were all around

We went to a lighthouse on the lake
A park was all around
Walking paths of discovery
Feeling this bond grow stronger
I heard your laughter on the wind
Music was in my head

A table near the lake
The sun and the wind very sweet
We talked about everything
And soon were just looking into our eyes
Drawing us nearer and nearer to
True loves first kiss exchanged

On this day of discovery
We found a bond
Two hearts as one
Bound together, happy sharing each other
For on this most beautiful day
True love took its first breatheremembering the day
