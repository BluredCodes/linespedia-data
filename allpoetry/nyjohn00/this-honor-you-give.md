This Honor You Give

So many friends I have made
Many different people
All unique in their own
They all have touched my heart
Their feelings, wants and joys
I wish them all their dreams
I have cried with some
Celebrated with others
Talked for hours on end
The one thing they all have shared
Is the beauty of their hearts
I love them all
God they share so much
So much of their lives
Over all these months
I feel I know them so well
They have become my family
they are the ones I love so dearly
Spread all over the world
I owe them so much
This honor they give
Letting me into their lives
