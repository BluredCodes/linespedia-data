title: "I Can Do Nothing"

I've spent my life waiting to see
If the one I could love was looking for me
Then I found you, so precious and true
We were looking but for what, who knew
Now my life has meaning and reason thanks to you
You my sweetness came in the perfect time
A time of need that I didn't even know
With you in my life, love was born
The season of love for which you came forth
Was the season of the opening of my heart for you
I can do nothing but love you
