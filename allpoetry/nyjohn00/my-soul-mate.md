---
title: "My Soul Mate"

When I hear you laugh it makes a sunny day
Your love's the air of life, I breath it so deep
Your deep brown eyes look into my heart
They see what I feel, and fill me with love
The lips that you bestow a kiss
Live in my mind they belong touching mine
Your feelings and wants, I embrace as mine
Our thoughts combine, they are but one
Feeling what you feel as you do me
We can sit side by side or across a table
Chatting for hours or just gazing eye to eye
We feel contentment, just you and I
All that we need, we have found together
Peace we feel, just from being there
I wake with the want to just say your name
To hold the dream, that has filled my brain
To feel you in my arms like the memories of slumber
Yearning for you to be near
If ever one was made for another
It is you and I, soul mates forever
