It was very early in our relationship
The feelings were growing oh so quick
We chatted, wrote letters galore
Sent a million smiles
To tell our thoughts, hopes, dreams without end
I had written you, God the letter was long
I hoped to show you just how much I cared
You misread,,,, and thought I didn't 
I made you cry that day
In that instant I understood just what I felt
We cleared the air, expressed ourselves 
And together saw, just what our heart had known
As we were saying goodbye
I had this sudden urge
I blurted out just what my heart knew
In this micro instant, time seemed to stop
As I said these words
I held my breath, and prayed I was not wrong
I said with all my feelings
Sweetheart I love you
And as I held my breath that day
I could not believe my ears
For as we were saying goodbye
You said you loved me too
