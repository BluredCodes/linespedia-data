Visions of you

Looking out on the new fallen snow
The sun shine blinds one so
Making it so hard to focus on the scene
All of nature is so hard to see

Looking at you, as we sit and we gaze
Your beauty does blind me, I'm in a daze
All fades to nothing, only you gleam 
Making the world disappear it would seem

Looking at how you capture my mind
I see the answer, it is truly a sign
You are my world, my love
My dove from heaven above
