title: "Do You Feel As I"

Time moves so slowly
The seconds go by one by one
My heart aches, it longs for you
I picture your smile
The sound of your voice
It was so full of love, it wanted me too
I long for your touch, I need it so
You filled my life, where emptiness was
You became my everything
All my dreams and desires
It is for you, that I want the future to hold
When we are apart, oh the longing does grow
I think and I wonder, do you feel as I
