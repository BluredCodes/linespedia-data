It was a day
God it is so clear
The doctors had their suspicions
Symptoms I have had
Test and probing
Then waiting for the word
I called the doctor could not wait
He didn't want to tell 
He wanted me to come to him
To hear the results face to face
I made him tell, could not wait
I can still hear his word so clear
"You have cancer, we must meet"
These words changed my life for good
Never again to take tomorrow for granted
Each breath became so precious
I have known you for so short of time
But you became my life
I saw in you, all my dreams
So much of my life was wasted
These last few months I want to spend
Doing a lifetime of things
All the things a couple would do
A lifetime in a few months
I'd want these memories to hold and keep
To take them with me to heaven
To hold them there and wait for you
My one and only love
