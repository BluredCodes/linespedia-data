title: "The Smiles Of Love"

Why is it so
With all the words I tell her
All the feelings shared
Smiles after smiles
I feel I just can't tell her all
I'm feeling in my heart
It's like the words
Don't measure up at all
They all fall just so short
Of telling her of my love
It's that she has this magic
It is wound around my heart
I want to learn all about her
All I've missed through out the years
The more I see, the more I hear
The more she means to me
But I just can't seem to find 
The words to tell her thus
I'll just have to rely upon
The smiles we share I guess
They tell her and they tell I
All that is happening inside
I'll hug her tight
Kiss her deeply
And share the smile of love
