I wish I may,
I wish I might,
I wish this wish,
for you tonight
For you, I wish happiness abound
For you, I wish you more love than has ever been found
For you, I wish that all your smiles are returned to you hundred times fold
For you, no pain or enemies through your whole life
For you, I wish laughter and joy without end
For you, I wish a strong faith to give you wisdom and strength
For you, I wish for all you will ever need
For you, I wish friendship, more than you can count
For you, I wish the hug of a child, given because of wonder in you
For you, I wish a walk in the forest, at peace with yourself
For you, I wish the morning dew and sunlight, the fresh spring day
For you, I wish the experience of grass beneath your feet
For you, I wish a spring rain
For you, I wish the fragrance of a rose
For you, I wish the songs of a bird singing sweet
For you, I wish a bubble bath by candlelight, to close your eyes and sigh
For you, I wish quiet times with the one you love
For you, I wish the innocence of youth and the wisdom of maturity
For you, I wish you never lose the wonder at this world
For you, I wish a heart at peace
For you, I wish dancing by moonlight
For you, I wish a Mother's love
For you, I wish the laughter of a child
For you, I wish dancing eyes and a ready smile
but most of all
