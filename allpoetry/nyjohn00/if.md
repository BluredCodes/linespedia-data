If, you could feel how I need you
You would know how important you are
If, you could hear your voice as I do
You would know how much I love listening to you
If, my eyes were your eyes
You would see all the beauty I see in you
If, you could hear your thoughts
You would feel how much I respect them
If, you could feel your touch
You would know how it thrills me
If, you could feel your kiss
You would long for the thrill it gives
If, you could know the love you give me
You would understand how happy you've made me
