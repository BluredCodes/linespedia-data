Looking through my eyes

I love how she loves me
She feels so safe and secure
She know my love is always hers
She gives me all of herself

She knows I adore her
Accept all that is her
She always makes my heart sing
Just by being she

She tells me I praise her too much
And all I tell her, she can't see
She says she looks into a mirror
And all she sees is her

I tell her the problem is
She is looking through her own eyes
If only she could see what I see
Looking through my eyes
