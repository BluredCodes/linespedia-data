title: "If We Had Never Met"

If we had never met
I never would have felt true love
You showed me all it is
I never been held in your embrace
Felt your love surround me so
I never would have looked in wonder
At all the beauty I see in you
I never would have felt so alive
So loved, so blessed
I never would have felt the yearning
To hold you by my side
I never would have opened my heart
To all the joys and sorrows of our life
I never would have found true happiness
That only you can give
I never would have felt the love 
I feel so deeply for you
