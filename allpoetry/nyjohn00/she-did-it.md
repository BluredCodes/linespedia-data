She did something today
It took my breath away
It was something she had never done
I had never expected none
It made my heart swell
Made me want to yell
To jump up and down
Telling the whole town
Squeeze her oh so tight
She told my friend in the light
Told him with her voice
And she had a choice
Right out of the blue
She said, "I love John" through and through
