My Forever and Always Love
Tonight I smiled an inner smile
As my mind turned to you
Recollection of all the times of joy
Times of worry, pain and want
Laughter, passion, needs released
Smiles that fill the sky
Only your love will do
My heart feels the call
Near or far, it feels you close
Standing on a lonely beach
Or gazing at a single star
You are close to me

Colors swirled in and out of us
Sound of rain romantic to us 
Our passions had been set free
Nights are crystal clear
The day stood strangely still, no one in sight
We walked together, hand in hand
Your heart and mine found the rhythm 
Worlds of emotion to explore
Each day feels even more right
So much time has passed us now
Yet you and I still feel new
My passion, my heart, my life
I love you......and I always will
