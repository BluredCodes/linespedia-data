They are special days
Time to remember 
Time to reflect
Looking back
Looking forward
Days to cherish and hold

It was a day of freshness
Of seeing someone special
A woman of beauty and grace
One who with her smile
Her grace and poise
Instantly brought butterflies to me

She was one I wanted to see again
My heart wanted 
My mind said why would she ever
I was just a guy, a stranger, nothing more
Who had done a favor
A thank you, good bye, nothing more

To my amaze, we met and talked again
We talked and talked as the days went bye
And oh the feelings grew
I became a part of her and she a part of me
Our hearts became one together
Our love did grow and grow

Now when I look back on that day
The day when we first met
I look at all the days, in all the years' gone bye
And then it really came to me
The feelings and butterflies, are the same 
The day doesn't matter, only her and I
