So many questions as the time goes by
Questions about this and questions about that
I sit and think and ask myself questions
Why did I fall in love with her
Is what I feel love
Is it for, her kisses, hugs and touches did I fall in love for those
Is it for her smile, it lights up the world
It is for her thoughts, her caring ways
Is it for the sound of her voice, music on the air

So what is it about this woman
She has captured my mind
I sit and wonder and ask myself questions
Why did I fall in love with her
Is it her kiss and touch, oh they are divine
Is it  for how her skin feels as I lightly touch
Is it for the way she sighs, as lovers only can
Is it for the way she laughs, oh it lightens my very soul
Is it for the love she holds for all of those around, it glows for all to see

So I ask so many questions, they go round and round
Questions about this and questions about that
I sit and think and ask myself questions
Why did I fall in love with her
Is the answer right before my face
Is it that the questions that I ask, answers already know
Is it true that all I feel amazes me so
Is it that my heart races so, the merest thought makes my breathing quicken
Is it just a fact that her and I belong
