I mark the days
Since we first met
Not a day goes by
When a thought of you
Doesn't warm me to the core

The love and hope
The smiles and thoughts
That you display
Makes everyone else fade away
My heart is yours, captured by your thoughts

The time goes by oh so fast
With each breathe, 
I need you more
In touch with my feelings
God what to do

The magic you send
Goes straight to my heart
It takes possession
It holds my heart so tight
I mark the days
