Every time we are together
I feel an excitement that grows 
A deep contentment
A feeling of joy only you give
knowing our feelings are one

When we are together
We look into each other's eyes
We see the love that 
Goes straight to the core
The yearning grows

When we walk
We're always holding hands
Needing the touch
Feeling the desire
Knowing each other is there

When we kiss
We merge into one
The love that flows
Is so complete and open
Our desires are for one

With you in my arms
I can face any trial
To have you and hold you
I want you to know
Is my dream forever more
