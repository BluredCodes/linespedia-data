Into my arms


Once I was lost, I couldn't find my way

So I flew into the night until my eyes saw day

Destiny called me, I went where I was called

You were my destiny, I surrender my all

You came into my life, without both of us knowing

Our love is special, it keeps growing and growing

I'm not lost anymore, I found someone who cares

I know you care a lot for me, and you're not going anywhere

You're not going anywhere except into my arms.
