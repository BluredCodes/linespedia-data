A Day of Perfect Contentment

Remember a day we stole away
An afternoon to ourselves
We took a drive out to the country
Stopping  at a park
Walking  the trails hand in hand
In the cool shade 
Protected from the summers heat
The leaves, all around
Sunlight danced on the ground
It moved to the sound of our laughter
We got a blanket and laid it on the grass
Laying down together,,,arms wrapped around
We fell asleep holding each other
The love we felt holding tight
Oh the perfect contentment
