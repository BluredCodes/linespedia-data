You tell me you miss me
It makes my heart glow
You tell me you can't wait to see me
And the yearning grows and grows

My happiness soars
With no limits to the sky
Thinking of being with you
My smile goes on for miles and miles

Then something happens
Commitments you see
And my dreams come crashing
The world does darken so

I know I have to be happy
With the time we have its true
But God do I miss you so
My heart cries too, it misses you too you know

You are my light
You make my days so bright
I want your love around me
I want you to know, I love you more and more
