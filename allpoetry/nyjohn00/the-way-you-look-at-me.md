When we're together
No matter how long
The look that you give me
Is so full of love

A yearning I see
Is all over your face
Reflecting my need
To be in your embrace

The curl of your lips
The sparkle of your eye
Makes me gaze in wonder
That this look is for me

Your look so peaceful
When you're here with me
You know you are loved
And are where you're meant to be

So if you look at me
And I look at you
Our eyes will say
What no words can come close
