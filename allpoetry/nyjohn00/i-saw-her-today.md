I saw her today
God she was beautiful
We met for coffee
I was sitting there
Reading a book
I felt her presence without a glance
And her smile lit the room

We spent a few hours just talking and laughing
God how the time flies just being with her
I listened to the events of her day
Her voice is like music
I leaned over closer
Kissing her cheek
And whispered my love into her sweet ear

We browsed all the books
Talking like friends
Talking the talk of only best friends
Enjoying the day
As we walked thru the isles
Her hand never far from mine
A light touch on her back to tell her she's mine

The meeting was ending
A sigh held by us both
The time always vanishes
Hello is just before goodbye
We walked hand and hand
Talking still but our eyes tell what words don't
The radiance of love all around

We stop buy our cars
I hold her in my arms
Telling her she is precious
And I want her to be mine
She looks in my eyes, and whispers her yes for me
Telling me she is, mine forever more
She loves me and wants me to
