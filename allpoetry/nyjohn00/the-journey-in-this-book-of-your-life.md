This journey in this book of your life

A new chapter is coming soon
The last one is almost done
This book you call your mind
This book I just can't put down
It calls me to read some more
This book is so divine
Filled with visions of your heart 
And opening to your mind
I get to see your feelings
Your wants and desires
They all fill my heart with joy supreme
I want to hold you so
I get to feel your tears
They always bring on mine
God I feel you sadness
These chapters give me a chance to see
It gives me a peek at the beauty that is in you
Allows me to pray and give a wish
That for all of you that you reveal
You make my heart swell with joy
I pray you are always happy
Surrounded with love and adored
For all that is you
