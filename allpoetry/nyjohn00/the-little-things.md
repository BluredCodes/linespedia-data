title: "The little things"

I love how you talk
The subjects don't matter
The joy you express
Is the light of the room

I love seeing you
On the floor 
Playing with children or pet
The love just glows

The excitement you show
Over this and that
Is so full of life
The sun shines bright

The letter or card
That you never forget
Is full of your heart
The warmth is all felt

Your giggle and laughter
Is so pure and felt
It's contagious to all
The love that you spread

I thank everyday
The love that you give
For the part that is mine
Is heaven sent
