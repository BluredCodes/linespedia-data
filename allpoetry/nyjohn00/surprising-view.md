Surprising View

Sometimes we meet someone
A surprising view within
The view we see is so breath taking
It captures our every thought
You care so deeply for them
It could be love
It could be friendship
It could be all above
This person you see
Has done nothing special
But you see them so clearly
So warm and loving reaching out
Out for the one they need
For the love and adoration that is their do
Its theirs because of who they are
The person that's inside
Its based on the person you see
Not their looks or attire
So to my special someone
The one who is in my heart
I want to tell her that 
I adore,,,, and love the person she is
