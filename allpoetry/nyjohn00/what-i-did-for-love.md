title: "What I did for love"

Love is mysterious
Like a mist it flows
And comes swirling when you least expected
Alone one day, together the next

I went thru life
Did all that was required
Never giving it a thought
Not feeling the emptiness

I met a wonderful, loving woman
One who's every breath
Is full of love
She sees beauty in all

I did nothing but be myself
Expressing my feelings my awe of her
Every moment together
Seeing more and more to love

She showered me with love
At every turn I feel it
With every moment it grows
Loving me, for me
