You Said You Loved Me

That night after our confessed love
We chatted an bantered, 
We avoided the subject for so long
Suddenly you asked if I had said
What you thought I had said
You were afraid you had heard me wrong
I replied to you as softly as I could
I love you my sweetheart, I do
I see in you all I want
All I could ever dream
I heard you voice in reply
That you love me as well
I told you I felt like I had loved you
From the day of my birth
That it seemed to me
We were meant
Meant to be
Two that became one
