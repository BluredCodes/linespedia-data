---
title: "A Movie"

Sitting with you is such a dream
We make up for lost time
Its like we don't get enough 
We sit so close together 
I feel your warmth on me
Touching foot to shoulder
You wrap yourself around me
Your leg over mine
Stealing kiss after kiss
What was that movie we watched
