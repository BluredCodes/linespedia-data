Our hands were made to touch and caress, holding each other tight
My hands are only soft because of touching you, they feel your silky skin
They long to caress your back in loves tight embrace
Every time we are apart my hands long for you so much

Your skin, for me to slide my fingers down
Caressing your every inch, exploring all your secret places
Your sighs mean the world to me, they trill me so
I crave for your skin next to mine

Our eyes are for sharing gazes
What you see in mine, I have not a clue
But what I see in your eyes, is, love celestial
A love that takes up our universe, for infinity

Your ears for my whispers, words for only you
My ear for your words meant only for me
You to nibble on mine with such passion 
Tickles through my body to my feet

Our lips are for our kiss, God they say so much
They kiss with little butterfly kisses, sweet and full of love
Our lips moist, melt together, parted with teasing tongue
The passion soars, to new heights, wanting them never to end 

For you, my love, I'll give you my all, my soul I give to you
For you to keep, tight to you, as long as you desire
While you hold me till I fall asleep
I'll whisper my words of love
