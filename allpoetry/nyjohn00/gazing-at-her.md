title: "Gazing at her"

When we are together 
Which I wish was everyday
We maybe in an antique store
Bookstore, crystal clothing or more
A romantic little coffee shop 
A table just for two
I find myself gazing at her
Wonder in my eyes
I see her browse
I see her walk
Her beauty and grace abound
We sit in a darken coffee shop
Soft music surrounds
We talk and talk 
The daily happenings, politics and religion
Or just joking , teasing 
We agree on most and debate the rest
Her eyes flash, and eyebrows raise
The look of wanting to strangle me
But then she tilts her head at me
The sparkle in her eyes
Love is always near
She is reaching to be in my arms
She knows just how I feel for her
My love goes deep inside
She and I agree, the love we have
The respect we share is more than surface deep
I look and wonder at how lucky I am its true
I'm blessed to have found my love forever more
