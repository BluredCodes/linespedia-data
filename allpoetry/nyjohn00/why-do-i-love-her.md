Why do I love her
What is it about her
There are many things
Some are big
Some are so small

I never thought these feelings would come
She has captured my heart
Taken over my mind
It is true my day starts
And ends with her

It started with a smile
One so pure
So open and giving
It came from the heart
It was all mine

Her eyes sparkle
With mirth and joy
And touched by a teasing
A devilish glance
That is all of my joy

Her hair dances with the wind
I love how it flows
It has the richness of walnut
And the touch or the suns glow
The smell of perfume

Her lips are so soft
So open and giving
They taste so divine
The give me such love
And they are all mine

Her voice and her thoughts
Are so full of caring
So soft is her voice
And caring her thoughts
She captures you heart, with but a thought

I've answered my question
Describing my love
Painting a picture of one so sweet
It's impossible to think
