---
title: "I Dream"
writer: "nyjohn00"
slug: "i-dream"
source: "AllPoetry"

I dream of a time of us together,
To not have to worry
To not have to hide
To be together, happy with love
It seems more real with each and everyday

I dream of long walks
Watching the wind blow your hair
Seeing your smile at the beautiful things
Listening together to the song of birds
Watching a stream roll by

I dream of going places
Seeing us browse
Our reflection in the glass
Finding a treasure
Taking it home to share the memory

I dream of looking at you
Seeing you each and every day
Listening to the things you did
Hearing your thoughts
Seeing your joy

I dream of holding you
Waking up with you in my arms
Kissing you awake
Seeing your smile
Kissing you good night

I dream of growing old with you
Remembering all of our time together
Living thru day in and day out
Seeing your ups and downs
Loving you

I dream of our phone chats and IM's
The thoughts and feelings we've shared
The laughs and giggles
The beliefs we've debated
The understanding we have

I dream of "US"
