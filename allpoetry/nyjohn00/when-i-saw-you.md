When I Saw You

The first time I saw you, I said you are so beautiful,
Your eyes of brown, like mother earths colors of love.
Your skin with a glow from within
Soft, and pure like a flowers fresh bloom
Your lips they speak, they tell your love
The first time I saw you, God I was in awe
The glare of your beauty stopped me, I trembled inside
I fidgeted and talked, to cover my thought
You thanked me with a voice that soothed my soul
All of a sudden it seems every one has disappeared,
We stood there and time disappeared
All I could see was your glorious smile
It planted a stake, claiming my heart
From that time onward just you and me, 
The way it should be
