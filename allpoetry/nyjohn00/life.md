Life 
Life is a meandering  journey ... 
A path of twists and turns, we never know where it goes
Filled with a numinous array of never-ending 
Events and emotions, joys and sorrows, loves and hates
And on this magical journey ... you'll meet people of all kinds
Some will cause a smile, cry, laugh, to think and love
Tender moments that your heart will never forget  
This I know ... for in my journey, I have met 
So many who have made me proud
Proud to know
Proud to be a friend
Happy that I have met you
YOU!!!!!!!!!!!!!
