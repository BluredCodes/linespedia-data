God not a day goes by
Thoughts of you own my soul
I smile as I remember
All the times together
Not one to forget
All filled with such happiness
All overflowing with our love
I have never talked
With one such as you
Talking and listening for hours
You know my next word as I yours
Thoughts so intertwined as one
The only time the words do stop
Is when we speak with our eyes
And oh the love they send
Longing to be in your arms
Every time I look at you
A yearning grows
To hold you close
Embrace forever 
Lips molded together
And whisper my love for you
