I feel so alone
My heart empty of all
Wondering if life 
Would play a horrifying trick
There are times when I feel
All we want will come true
And others when I think
It is only a dream after all
I think of all our thoughts
The ones we have shared for so long
The love, hopes and longings
And wonder deep inside
If this is only a silly dream
Have I wanted 
Something I'll never have
Has life let me taste
Love, sweet and divine
I wonder if there are
Any words to let you see
Words I have not found
To make you want to be mine
