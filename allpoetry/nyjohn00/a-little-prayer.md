In the name of the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit

God,,, I thank you for giving me my precious angel in my life. You allowed us to
meet. God our love grows stronger. I pray that I am always worthy of
her. that I can always make her happy, that she will know my love and
feel it will never end. I will promise you God to always be there for
her. To love her, to honor her and to always take care of her. Thank you
God for bringing your love to us.

In the name of the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit
Amen
