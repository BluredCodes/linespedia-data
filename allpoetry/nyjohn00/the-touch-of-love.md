The touch of love

Laying down under a starry sky
On the blanket of our love
The moon long gone
The sky lit by only the stars
We snuggle oh so close
Your head on my shoulder 
Embracing like lovers of myth
As we gaze into the night
Not a word is said
As the stars fill our eyes
With the love of ages
And the only sound
Is of our soft breathing
