I've dreamed of an angel
One so sweet and caring
Whose love glows from within

Emotions are a funny thing
We all have a lot
At time they cloud our brains
And rob us of sensible thought

Even though I know you love me
I see it every day
My heart aches
At the thought of you and he

It really doesn't matter if no kiss is
Given or shared
It's just that my love is near
To one that holds her dear

I worry and I fret
In truth lose a little sleep
But please excuse my ramblings
It comes from jealousy

I know I have no right
To feel these feeling true
For I was nowhere in sight
These 25 years ago

I wish I had been with you
For all these years gone past
If I could change one thing
It surely would be that

For now I must long and wait
And I will gladly do
For the day when we're together
As we're meant to be

So please excuse my ramblings
It makes me look a fool
But please understand my heart
Its full of love for you
