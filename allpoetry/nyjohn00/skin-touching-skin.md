Skin touching skin

Wrapped in your embrace
Skin to skin, passions build
A crescendo in a kiss
The heavenly time with
You in wrapped in my arms
Under the covers
Your head on my chest
My lips kissing your hair
Just listening to you breath
Skin touching skin
