title: "I Don't Deserve You"

I look at you and look at me
I see a beauty, a beauty that
Time could never wipe out
Just one smile takes my breath away
A warm and caring love
That the angels are in envy
A delicate sensitiveness 
That must be held and loved
Feelings and thoughts
That must be embraced
The sound of your voice
A sigh, a laugh, is music to me
The mirror tells me
What does she see in me
I'm just average, nothing to adore
All I have to give her is my heart 
Nothing more
