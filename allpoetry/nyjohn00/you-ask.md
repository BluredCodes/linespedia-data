You ask, "Are you sure"?, I stop and think
Have I done something that would make
My precious love wonder.
You have asked this before
My reaction is always the same.
I wonder if I have hurt you,,
Made you wonder in any way by a look, a deed, a thoughtless word.
If I ever do, I pray you tell me right away,
So I can ease your mind and fill it with my love.
Sweetheart, I loved you months ago,
I love you now, and as my love grows daily I know I'll love you tomorrow.
There is nothing you could do to stop my love.
If you ever tell me to stop, get lost, get out of your life,
My love would still be there for you,
You have touched my heart like no one ever has,,soooo
"Am I sure?" the answer is a loud YES!!!!
