title: "Fills My Heart"

Your love fills my heart
It was empty for so long
The tear it shed long ago
Made it wither and wane
It thought it would not feel again
But you made it swell
You filled it with you love
Held it tight to yours
Sweetheart you made it well
It will never feel that emptiness
The hurt and lost of love
It will always be in your hands
Now and forever more
