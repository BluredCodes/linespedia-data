Watching the snow fall
Slowly drifting  past
Like the second hand on the clock
Moving on its relentless path
Today the sun refuses to shine
Shrouded in winters clouds
An icy wind comes blowing in
Chilling me to the bone
I feel the cold of winters death
Reminding me how brief life is
How we only have a little time
A tear slowly trails down my cheek
Falling silently down
It is joined by its brothers
Just like the snow drifting past
It seems to last forever
It grows in intensity
And slackens in the spring
Just as my tears end when ever you are near
So I gaze out at the snow and think
One more day gone forever
One less day of being with you
