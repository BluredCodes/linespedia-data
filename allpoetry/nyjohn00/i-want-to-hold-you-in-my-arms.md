I want to hold you in my arms
To feel our bodies entwine
To feel the warmth of your skin
On every inch of mine
I want to hold you so close
Our breathing becomes as one
To look into your eyes
As our lips combine as one
To see you sigh 
As our tongues caress as one
I want to hold and caress your back
For hours at a time
To take my breathes 
From in your hair
As you relax in my arms
I don't want this for any one moment
I want it for our lives
