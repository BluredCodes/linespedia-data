If you only knew
How much you truly mean
How each and every second
Goes by so slowly
The yearning grows

I try to keep busy
Taking up the time
My mind keeps going back
Seeing you
Remembering all the times

Every time we are apart
My heart feels the ache
Ears that long to hear you
Arms that long to hold you
Know they can't just now

I want to spend all my time with you
In my life was where you're meant to be
Everything about us is so complete together
Our thoughts and feeling match
Without you I'm not whole

Every time we are together
The time just seems to fly
I love every second
But the time when we're apart
Always goes on foreverTo my precious love
