I will kiss you


I will kiss you softly in the silence of the night.
I'll take of your lips the sweet breath of your love 
I'll kiss you illuminated by the shine of your eyes
I will want the possession of your heart

I'll ignite your body with the fire of my kisses
I'll explore each inch with reverence for you
I'll climb your mountains with the desire of passion
I will hold your love so tight to my heart

I will kiss you forever, needing no rest
Whispers of love, caressing your ears
Sighs of longing, your name whispered
In an infinite kiss, of longing for you

I will kiss your eyes, filling them with visions of love 
Visions of lingering embraces, raptures caress
I will gaze into your eyes, seeing your beauty within
Then I will kiss you again
