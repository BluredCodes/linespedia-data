Starving Arms

I'm like a man, lost in the desert
My thirst and hunger can only be quenched,
by your loves grace
It feeds my soul, fills my heart

This thirst and hunger,
can only be requited,
by the eating of the manna of your love 
A food so pure it mystifies, makes me want it more

I'm in a desert,
scratching through the sand,
to reach your oasis,
I hunger for you, need you so, want you every day 

To shade myself, in your trees.
To satisfy my hunger, for you
by feeding on your fruited passion
And quench my thirst, with the nectar of you

With your lips. Mine will form
Tongues will glide on passions breath
My arms will embrace forever more
In the feast of our love
