Its a dream,,,
A dream I want so much
There was a time
A time where I could not imagine
That one could be as incredible
As you are with your charms
You beauty and warmth
Your love
That someone like you could love me
Could actually really love me
A dream to be holding you
Feeling the caress of our bodies
A dream of embracing you
In a kiss that last forever
A dream of knowing you
And seeing deep within your heart
A dream of being together
As we were meant to be
