Life would be so much easier
Without the feelings we carry day by day
They're with us deep inside
They push to the outside all the time
Making life oh so much more complicated

With no feelings
We wouldn't feel the hurt
The pain of lost
They would just be another happening
We'd go on our merry way

But also with out these feelings
We would never feel the joy
Of a Childs happy play
The bird in the meadow
Your loves sweet smile

Feelings we feel are what give us life
They make us appreciate all around
Know all the good from the bad
The rapture of love, passion of desire
The feelings of life
