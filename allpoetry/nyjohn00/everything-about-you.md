Everything About You

You've opened my eyes
I feel so different, feeling things I didn't know
Seeing myself in a different way
I have dreams, that now, I want to come true
What has changed, how is this me
It's everything about you.
The way you make me laugh, the smile I get
When it's your voice on the other end of the line.
It's hard to imagine this happening- especially to me,
True love has always been just a distant dream.
At times I have my doubts, is this a dream
Could this really be?
But then, I think of all the little things you do and say
How your kindness takes my breath away.
Your love flows to all around
This feeling in my heart is so new
You've made me happy; I'd have to say
It's everything about you
