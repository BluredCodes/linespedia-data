Forever Yours

I try and try to show you
How much I really care
I just hope that you realize
The kind of love that we share
A love that is stronger
Than any from before
A love that is true
And yours forever more
My heart beats wildly
When you whisper my name
And when I look into your eyes
My heart goes aflame
My heart truly invites you
To come and feel my rapture
Come wrap yourself around me
It's my heart that you capture
