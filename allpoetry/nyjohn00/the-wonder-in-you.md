title: "The Wonder In You"

I look at you in awe 
And I wonder why you can not see
You are a dream, to me everything
You tell me you look in a mirror
You say, "John Loves me!"
And wonder, "what can he see"
You say you see no beauty
Just an average girl
But what you don't see is the wonder in you
The depths of your feelings
Your caring ways, and not just for people
But for all that he made
I've  seen you tear over another's feelings
I've seen you cry over an animals pain
You show the love that is God's domain
He gave you a beauty
So rare and so fine
It glows and I know it is mine
He allowed me to see
All this beauty that is you
And I thank him each day
For the wonder in you
