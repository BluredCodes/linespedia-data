Sweetheart I really must confess
The greatest fear I have
Is ever losing your love
I sit and try to think
Examine my love
Knowing that I want you 
To have happiness all the time
It should be first, you happiness that is
But then my old selfishness
Comes running to the front
I need you in my life
Friend, companion, confidant and lover
I want you as my wife
To love the years through
Till the end of our time on earth
I cannot even imagine it
It's too painful to contemplate 
The thought of ever, ever losing your love
