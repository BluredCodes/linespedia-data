I dream all the time
Of laying down with you
Of holding you so in my arms
Oh the feel of your lips
So inviting and sweet
They part in need moist and inviting
Of my kiss that lingers on
The passion grows and grows
The need for you goes on
It is not just for the moment of want
It is to hold you as you sleep
To feel you so relaxed
Secure and safe in my arms
Feeling your gentle breathe
As your head lays on my chest
My lips kiss your hair
Whispering your name
With each inhale 
I take your essence
Deep within my soul
Making it part of me
Never to let it go
At last I sleep holding you
Knowing that tomorrow
I'll wakeup with the light
And kiss your shiny hair
Wishing you the brightest day
The love will go on and on
