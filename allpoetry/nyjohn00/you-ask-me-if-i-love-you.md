You ask me if I love you,
and I ask myself how
to begin to share with you
the feelings in my heart.
You inspire in me a love so deep words
cannot describe it,
so powerful that it overwhelms
my every thought.
You ask me if I love you,
and I wish there were a way
you could just see into my soul
and find the depth of passion, tenderness and love
that holds you closer to my heart
than anyone or anything else.
You ask me if I love you, and I look into
the magic of your eyes and promise,
'Yes, I love you..
I always will.'
