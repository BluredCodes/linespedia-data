I ask myself
What do I need
What do I want
Is it me
Do I ask too much

I want a companion
Someone to be with
Relax with her
Enjoy life with her
To be myself

I want to talk and listen
To see the care in her eyes
Share a laugh
Cry with her
To joint together as one

I want to walk holding hands
To share a day
Disclose a life
To sit and be happy
Being by her side

I want to kiss
Feel the mutual desire
To close my eyes
Sense her closeness
And want to shout

I want you, my precious
You are what I want
What I need
For yesterday and today
And all OUR tomorrows
