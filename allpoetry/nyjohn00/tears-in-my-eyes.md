Going to sleep tonight
Tears in my eyes
I have a feeling of helplessness
God the feelings are so strong
I feel I have so much to say to her
My heart feels so full
So many feelings
Running all over the place
I want to tell her all I feel
But what do I feel so strong
I need to find a new word
A word that paints an image
An image of all my love
All held out to her
I offer it completely
It is wrapped just for her
A message for only her ears
But need word to tell her
Just what all this love means
It means that my heart is always
Reaching it needs her so
If means I'm only content
If she is near to my side
It means I have this need
It is centered in the person you are
It adores and cherishes you so
Tears will fill my eyes
Till you are by my side
