We spent the day together again
Talking, laughing, teasing and caring
Kissing, embracing, loving and sharing
Time looking so deep into your eyes
Your eyes misting as you see my adoring gaze
As the time nears to say our goodbyes
We are sitting side by side
Arms around each other
Your head lying on my shoulder
Again I gaze into your eyes
I see a peace and oh such contentment

I love you so
