Looking at you picture
Imagining you here
The warmth of your smile
Sets my heart on fire

Feeling your hand in mine
As we walk along
The sound of your laughter
A songbird could not compete

Thinking of how it would feel
To hold you tight right now
Wrapping around each other
In a lovers embrace
I look at your lips

Curling in a smile
Closing my eyes
I feel them kissing me
As we speak our one desire

Looking at your picture
Imagining you here
The warmth of your smile
Sets my heart on fire
