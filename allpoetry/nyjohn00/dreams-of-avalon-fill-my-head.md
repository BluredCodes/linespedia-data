Dreams of Avalon fill my head
The mist of magical times 
Where Merlin's spells and potions
The might on knights saved the day
King Arthur our liege
A time when the right always prevails
Where Unicorns played in the forest
And the wee people danced around
A time where I could ride on my steed
Armor and sword brilliant
No tarnish to be seen
And streaming from my armor 
Where all could see
a scarf, your colors 
Your heart to mine
I'd ride up to your castle tall
And lift you on to the steed with me
Together we would ride 
Into the mist of our own
And together would be
Forever you and me
