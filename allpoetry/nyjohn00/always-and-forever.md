What does always mean??

To love forever
Endlessly
Without a doubt
For all eternity

What does forever mean??

To love someone
Until your dying day
Love and cherish
Regardless, what others say

What do you mean to me??

My life feels complete
When we are together
I will love you
Always and Forever
