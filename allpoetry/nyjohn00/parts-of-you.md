title: "Parts of you"

Sweetheart being with you
Is like a sweet breeze of spring
No matter what we do
It is always fresh and new

Your laughter is a treasure
That can compare to nothing
For to hear it is wealth
The riches of all

Precious the sound of your voice
Fills my heart with desire
It is always pleasing
So filled with your sharing

Oh my loved one
Your thoughts are all fascinating
I could sit and listen
And never tire

To look at you
Lights a fire in my heart
My want for you
Has no limit, no end

You touch transcends
All the love that you feel
It is the essence of caring
The giving of you

What I'm trying to tell you
Is that the parts of you
That fill my heart with love
Are all so wondrous
