Senses Confused


Sitting in a coffee house
Soft music playing
Working on my laptop
Trying to catch a poem
I close my eyes and think of you
A dreamy state ensues
My mind wanders
Immersed thoughts of you
Suddenly a scent of you
The essence fills the air
Elatedly I look around 
But you are not there
Next the music I hear
Takes on the sound of your voice
Soothing and hypnotizing
Again I look around 
Still you are not there
I see a woman sitting there
The way she tilts her head
The color of her hair, so familiar
A smile moves my lips, calling your name
She turns
Sigh it is someone else
My love is nowhere in sight
Then the thought comes to me
I was just confused
What I am smelling, hearing and seeing
Is love
