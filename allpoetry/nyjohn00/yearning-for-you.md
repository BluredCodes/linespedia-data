title: "Yearning for You"

The feel of you next to me
The essence of you in the air
It surrounds me, entices me
It makes my heart beat so wildly

We sit and embrace so closely
Lips to lips, parting in love
The moist caress, the taste of you
We wrap around each other

God it feels so close
Every inch touching
We embrace and hold
The only sound is a sigh

The sigh tells oh so much
It speaks of our love
The desire we feel
The need to be one

My love for her is so complete
In the light I see my princess alone
I see perfection, of my one true love
We'll be together till times last second
