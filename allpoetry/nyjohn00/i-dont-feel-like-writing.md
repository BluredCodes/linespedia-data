I don't feel like writing
My thoughts all are jumbled today
Doing a lot of introspection
I remember a past, forgotten
When I had no feelings
Where all I said was wrong
I think I feel too much at times
Way to much for a man
Too much emotion floods my way
It clouds my head
Makes me foolish
Acting like a child
Not manly in a way
Taking a deep, deep breath
Its time to be an adult
Don't feel like writing today
