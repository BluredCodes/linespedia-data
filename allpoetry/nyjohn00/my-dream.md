One night I had a dream,
I dreamt that I would find someone
That could truly make me happy
I dreamt that she would be like no other,
To my hopes I might discover
I dreamt that she would be beautiful and warm,
I dreamt that she would not have
Just a few appealing qualities,
She would possess them all far from any norm

I dreamt she would have eyes that could make my body melt,
And that her soft kisses
Could make me feel like I have never felt
I dreamt that her smile could light up my whole day,
That her simple touch could take my breath away!
I dreamt that the feeling of her holding me and me her
Tight could send tears to my eyes...
I dreamt that she was perfect and wanted her to be mine!
I dreamt she would be my first true love

So I hoped and wished and prayed...
And soon the angels heard me and carried her my way
There she stood perfect in my eyes
She was my dream girl!, 
Oh she took my breath away,
Made my heart skip a beat
We share the same life's breath
Now every night before I sleep I thank the angels above
They granted me my wish, they sent me you
