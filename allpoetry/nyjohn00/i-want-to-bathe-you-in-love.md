I Want To Bathe You In Love


I want to pour,
The elixir of my love 
All over your body
Into the depths of your heart
Fill your soul with love

I want to banish all your pains
Wash any worries away
Infuse you with the glow
Knowing you are completely loved
Fill you with the air of a fresh spring day

I want my love to surround you
Protect you, from any hurt
Make you feel secure
Know it never holds you back
Knowing it is always there for you
