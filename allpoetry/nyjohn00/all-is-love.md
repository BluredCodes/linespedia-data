The love she shows
The little things
Caring and true
Sharing smiles
All is love

She loves to give
To make all happy
Give of herself
A little here and there
All is love

Her sweet thoughts
So gentle and pure
Make your heart swell
With pride of knowing her
All is love

The pride she shows
In all she does
Shows the strength
She has deep inside
All is love

All these things are
Big and small
But taken together
Make you hold tight
All is love
