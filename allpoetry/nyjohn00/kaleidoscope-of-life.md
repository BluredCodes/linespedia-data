This book of life
A journey to behold
As I travel the paths
And follow your trail
This journey has become 
A kaleidoscope of colors 
An emotional tour
A dazzle to behold
These colors are calling me to follow
They capture my mind
As my heart yearns for you
The more that I see you
The more you amaze
The depth of your feeling
There is no end to see
So I am eager, I wait to see
This journey once started
Has become a quest
To know you the person
One who means so much to me
The one who I adore
