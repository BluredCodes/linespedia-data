---
title: "Is she thinking Of Me Too"
writer: "nyjohn00"

Duties and obligations keep us apart for days on end
The second seem like hours
So much to do, so much to do
And all my thoughts on her
Is she thinking of me too

Does she sit in wonder
Thinking of our love
Does she miss my smile as I do miss her laugh
Does she feel my lips on hers when she closes her eyes
Is she thinking of me too

I wonder if she misses my voice
Hers is music to my ears
Does she miss my laughter
Does she miss my thoughts
Is she thinking of me too

Is she missing my embrace
The way we sit so close
Does she wonder if I'm missing hers
Does she wonder if I miss her hand in mine
Is she thinking of me too

Duties and obligations keep us apart for days on end
She is always on my mind
So much to do, so much to do
And oh the ache inside
Is she thinking of me too
