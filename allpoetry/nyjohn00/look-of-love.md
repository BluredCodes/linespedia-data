title: "Look Of Love"

There is a look, sweet and pure
It's a gleam in the eye
Gazing so deep
A longing and yearning
A turn of the lip

I've seen it from afar
Seeing two in love
They look so deeply into each other
Seeing the seen, viewing the unviewed
Feeling the depth of feeling

I've always wondered if
I'd ever see this look for me
If this look would come to me
To look at one
And see her looking back at me

Now as if a dream come true
The one of my dreams
Looks at me too
Her love for me true
She looks into me

This dream of mine
This woman so pure
The beauty I see, is there for me
I'm sure that she sees
All my love for her too
