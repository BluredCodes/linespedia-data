Day dreaming 
Oh the tingles on my spine
Closing my eyes
Thinking of you
I imagine laying next to you
Arms around
Legs intertwined
The feel of our breath
Exploding in loves want
As we kiss, oh we kiss
This kiss that never ends
The feverish caresses
The need that wants to explode
It builds in us
To such a peak
This need we have
Is ours to hold
To cherish with our hearts
