Today we spent hours, 
Talking , kidding, teasing, and loving
And I cherished it all you see
For it isn't just any one of them
But all of them together
That makes you  so so precious to me
Makes me want you so
But all that you are you see
That makes me want you so
It is your smile, the sound of your voice
Your touch and feelings too
All that you are
That makes my heart on fire
