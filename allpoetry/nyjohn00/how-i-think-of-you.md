title: "How I think of you"

I first think of you
In the early hour
Partially awake
Partially asleep
A smile touches my lips

My day starts
Wondering how you are
Are you happy
Are you down
Happy, safe, secure

Morning continues
My thoughts linger
Imagining your face
Your eyes twinkling
My heart begins to smile

Afternoon lasts forever
Hour after hour
Wondering how your day went
Is it full of laughter
Do you smile at me

Evening is full of things to do
Running here and there
I know your running too
Always things to do
Are you wondering what and where I am

Bedtime comes at last
I curl up under the covers
Wrapped around my pillow
Wishing it was you
Asking do you think as well
