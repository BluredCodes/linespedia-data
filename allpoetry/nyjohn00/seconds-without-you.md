Oh the seconds
Spent without you
They linger for hours
An eternity it feels
My heart reaches out
Would that my lips 
Be kissing you now
My arms seek to enfold you
Safe at my side
Yours forever
Slave of your love
Needing you with me
Till heaven aboveYearning for you
