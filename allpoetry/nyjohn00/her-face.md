title: "Her Face"
writer: "nyjohn00"

I picture your face
So warm and true emotions a plenty
Love and warmth
A beauty so pure
I could only love

Her eye twinkle
Full of mirth and delight
Expressing her soul
Sending her thoughts
Love abounds

Lips soft and sweet
Turning up in enchantment
Giving and sharing
Receiving and loving
Made for mine

Her ears so delicate
Meant to hear nothing but love
Sensitive to my words
Enjoying the world
Whispering my love in

Her skin so pure
Sweet to the taste
Made to be next to mine
Glows with ecstasy
I love every inch

Hair so soft
Kissing it I must
Dances in the air
Whether up or down
My eyes follow wherever it goes

I could look at this woman
All the days of my life
Feeling a deep glow
Loving her beauty
Knowing she is mine
