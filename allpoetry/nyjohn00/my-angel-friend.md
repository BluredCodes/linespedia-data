My Angel Friend

I have a friend, an Angel you see
Whose single tear does touch my soul
She lives so far away in a land down under
She is my adopted daughter

We became friends right from the start
She shared her life with me
She gave me many a smile
A tear and prayers for her

I've heard of her hopes and wants
Felt her joys and sorrows
This Angel friend of mine
Has a heart that is pure gold

I saw her love a man, love him like no other
Felt it all come together, they would be one
Then in the pain of lost, the agony that they shared
He with no thought for her, went about his life

God how I pray for her
This Angel from far
She has such a glow, beauty from her soul
I want to help her so, this tearing Angel from afar

She has her doubt and her fears
She does not see all she is
Her love, warmth caring heart
Is something to behold

She doesn't know how she touches
Touches me so deep
I see all her beauty profound
I'm proud of this daughter of mine
