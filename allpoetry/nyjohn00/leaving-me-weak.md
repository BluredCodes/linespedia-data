Leaving me weak

Just the thought of you
And you're always on my mind
Just the touch of you
And in my arms is where you're meant to be
Just the sound of you
Makes my heart explode

Have you noticed when we are together
The laughter and smiles never stop
Have you noticed how we always talk
The listening and banter goes on and on
Have you noticed that when we are one
The satisfaction fills the air

In my mind, we are meant to be
Meant to be together for all time
In my mind, no one could be as perfect
You are my everything, more than I could ask
So come with me, stay with me, be with me
Held close by loves sweetheart
