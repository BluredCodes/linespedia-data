Your love, is everything
It took my pain and loneliness
It too my worries and doubts
It lightened my load
So I could soar on butterfly wings

On butterfly wings I now soar
Up in the light
My heart wants to sing
My soul is set free
So I could soar on butterfly wings

Your love has made me whole again
It opened up my heart
Together we combine to make each other one
I hold you love close, never letting go
So WE can soar on butterfly wings
