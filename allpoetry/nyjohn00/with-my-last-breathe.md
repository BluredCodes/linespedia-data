title: "With My Last Breathe"

Filling me with your love
Love that is the air I breathe
It fills every part of me
I'll never get enough of you
You're always on my mind
Wishing we had met 
So very long ago
I'll take, the rest of my life with you
Relish all the moments
Wanting my life to last forever
Forever in your arms
Loving you till the end of my time
And surely even beyond
When I close my eyes in death,
I will whisper your name
with my last breath.
