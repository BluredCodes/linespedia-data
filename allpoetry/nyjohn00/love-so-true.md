God,,, brought us together,,,,
Love comes from him
And what we feel
Is love so true
My heart is yours
My mind filled with you
My souls lays with you
Combines with you
Becomes one with you
One with you it will be
As in nature
Wind, water, earth and fire
Combine to make the world one
