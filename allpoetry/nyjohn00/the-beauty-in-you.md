I stopped bye the other day
I needed to see you
To see your face
I really surprised you
You never thought
I'd be coming
You were busy working
Doing work you really shouldn't
The weather was nasty
And you were soaked to the core
The water was streaming 
Running through your hair
You were soaked 
From head to tail
I stood out with you
Getting as wet as you were
Not noticing the rain
I was too excited
 just seeing you again
You said you
Could not believe I was there
Saying you looked atrocious
All wet as you were
But when you 
Looked into my eyes
All you could see was the love
That was inside
You saw for once
That whenever I gaze at you
I see a beauty
The beauty in you
