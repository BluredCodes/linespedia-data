Sweetheart before you I was a hollow shell
Casting no shadow,,,,,
Light just passed thru my emptiness

Alone, in a dreary world
Feeling as though
There was no one to share

Then out of no where
A woman appeared
And a little shadow appeared

She filled my life
She laughed
She shared herself

She gave me love
She made me smile
My shadow darkened

She made me live
Live for her smile
Exist for her 

Now, I'm a shadow of
My former self
Whole and alive


Thank you my precious
