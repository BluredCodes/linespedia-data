This day so long ago, but yet its not
It feels like yesterday, the memory so clear
I'll cherish the vision, meeting one so dear
She change me and my life 
By being nothing but her

It didn't take her long to make my heart hers
It was planted with a smile 
Feelings blossomed into love so complete
It surrounded and held me
Love was the air I breathed

We managed a moment stolen from the start
A minute and hour, loving what we got
I soon realized just how precious she was
I saw all around her the sweet glow of her love
She opened her heart and gave me myself back

I worried from the start, what she ever saw in me
This vision of mine, was so important to me
I loved how she talked, her views so fresh
I could sit for hours discussing her thoughts
She opened herself, knowing my love

As days turned to months and months turned into years
This vision of mine grew more in my eyes
I loved all about her, nothing to change
Time spent with her flashed bye
All gone in a instant

In all the time pasted, I want her to know
I've learned so much from her
I know now what love is, I'll not be in doubt
I'll not sit in wonder, seeing others together
I'll know I was loved no matter what comes

Sweetheart, my love for you will always be there
It's not dependent on anything you do
Love is there  for you always, so please listen to me
I love you not for your love at all but
I love you instead for all that you are
