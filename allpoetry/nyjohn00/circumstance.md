Circumstance

Being together in a place
A circumstance where we can't show
Can't show the feelings we have
The love that grows and grows
It is almost impossible to stop the longing glance
To still the hand that wants to touch
To hold your hand in mine
To smile at you,
Acknowledge you are there
To talk with you and others too
But not let others see I care
I hope you felt the love I feel
Just being there with you
The words I wanted to speak
Could only be in my mind
That was yesterday
Today we will be apart
I want to say, need to say
I love you with all of my heart
