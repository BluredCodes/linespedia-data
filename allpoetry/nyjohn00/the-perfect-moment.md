A perfect moment is an instance in time
When everything is so perfect 
Time seems to stand still
And you see your whole life in that moment 

We go through our lives
Hoping against hope
Searching for this moment
Praying it will come

I have been blessed 
With my prefect moment
It started when we met
And completed with our love

My perfect moment goes on and on
It is the moment we felt the magic
It is the look in your eyes
The smile on your lips

Sweetheart, you see
My perfect moment 
Was the moment we fell in love
And I'll live there forever
