Reflections reflections all around
We are the reflections of our life
Our vision is surely shaped
Our image is as we're seen
The reflection of what others sees
This all we'll ever be
So on my knee I thank you love
For seeing me in me
You let me see the knight in armor
Always there for you
You let me view the depth of your love
As it reflects out of you
But the most I thank you for
Is the reflection of you and I
