In a little sweet voice
A voice that was full of implore
You stopped my heart
And made my heart do a spin
It did a flip this heart of mine
It filled with a need so strong
Your question made my heart reach out
It brought a tear to my eye
It wrenched my heart 
And showed me again the depth of love I have
This sweet voice in soft implore
Said please never leave me 
Be with me, today and forever more
Never go away but always be there
To love me all my days
And please never go away

My answer was that I'll never leave
I was made to be at your side
The only thing that could make me go
Would be your decision made
If you should ever want me to leave
I would honor your request
But I want you to know 
That if you ever ask
I'll love you till my last breath
And after this breath
I'll be in heaven
I'll be at that heavenly gate
I'll be waiting you see
Watching all that come thru
Because if this is heaven I'm in
I'll have to see you again
