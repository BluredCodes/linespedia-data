title: "Will Never Fade Away"

There is something about your eyes, my love, I can not deny
For, when I look into them
I see my dreams coming alive.

Sweetheart I cannot help loving you
You are all I've ever wanted
For, there is something about you I cannot deny.

My love for you grows stronger with each passing day
This is how I know my love for you...
Will never fade away.
