title: "Are You The One"

I've dreamed of an angel
One so sweet and caring
Whose love glows from within

I've longed and yearned for her
One who will care for me, as much as I do her
So that our loves will surround us covering all

I long to look into her eyes and see the same fire
One who can tell me at a glance, I am her all
Glimpse the same loving, wanting and needing

I've entered a dream world where all this is possible
One who will love me, for me nothing more
She who I adore, for all that is forever more
