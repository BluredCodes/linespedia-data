All Over Again

Every time I see you
The feelings feel so fresh
It starts with your smile
The music of your voice
The feel of your hand in mine
The kiss that lingers so long
The glow in your eyes
As we tell of our love
The embers in my heart
Ignite with true loves fire
Sitting wrapped together
Touching from head to toe
Talking for hours
Always held in passions embrace
What I'm trying to say to you
Is really quite simple and clear
Every time I see you princess
I fall in love all over again
