I want to take a walk with you
We will cover many miles
The sights we will see
Will fill our minds
With memories so pure

We will always be walking hand in hand
Why, before we met
You were already at my side
The heart we share
Was one from birth till now

Along this walk we will see so many things
The people we touch and touch us
Smiles and tears, all to fill our hearts
With love, happiness, fulfillment, and joy
A lifetime of memories 

All these memories to hold
Would fade to insignificant particulars
If I didn't take this walk with you at my side
This walk of life you see has meaning only with you
Without you at my side, the memories would mean nothing
