As our eyes first met
Something touched and ignited a fire
A passion for you and all that you are
It started with a smile 
And consumed with love

Our souls touched on that very first day
I said hi, you said hi
I talked like a nervous schoolboy
My ears heard but, my heart listened deeply
I was amazed at the beauty I saw

I tingled and buzzed
With oh so romantic thoughts
You were constantly on my mind
Thoughts of fantasies of you
Wishing then that you would feel the same for me

I felt as if I had known you forever
A few short moments, felt like an eternity
I took a deep breath and inhaled all the emotions that were in the air
As our conversation ended I felt a tremor run through my body
I felt as though a good thing was happening

So you wonder why I celebrate that day 
You took me and filled me with the breath of love
This fire of love, burn so bright
It embraces and grows, sealed always with a kiss
I knew these kisses meant forever
