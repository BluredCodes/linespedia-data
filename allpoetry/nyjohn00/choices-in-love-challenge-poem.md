Sweetheart I want you to know
My love for you is complete
Real love is given
With nothing expected in return
If I could have but one wish in my life
It would be nothing for me
I would not wish for my health
Nothing to do with wealth
No nothing to do with me
I wish only for your happiness
It is all I really want
As much as I do love you
Need you near my side
If ever I felt I harmed you
Or caused you trouble unbearable
I want you to know
The choice would be easy
Would I stay just for me
Or leave for your sake
Sweetheart I love you so
The only answer as I see
Is to put you first like I should
So for your sake I'd disappear
Remove myself from your world
But want you to know 
Till my last day
