They say the beauty of a woman is not in the clothes she wears
It not the smoothness and softness of her skin
Her shapely body with all its curves
For these may come and go
So the real beauty is found instead
It radiates from inside
It glows and flows to all around
It is captured in her smile
It shines from within her eyes
It's the deep and caring heart
She can envelope you in love
Holding you tight 
she opens your heart with her smile
it glows from within
it makes for such contentment
just being by her side
She does all this just with the beauty of a woman
