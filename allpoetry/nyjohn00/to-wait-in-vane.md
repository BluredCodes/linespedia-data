title: "To Wait in vane"

Listening to some music
The melodies flow
A song begins to play
And my tears begin to flow
This song has a verse
Repeated over And over 
These words do haunt, touch the soul
"I don't want to wait in vain for your love"
The thought of these words
A dagger in my heart
I can face so many things
Face them with a heart that is strong
The very thought of never being
Breaks my strength in two
But then I think of how I'd be
If never we had met
I'd still be that empty man
Afraid to feel emotions
Terrified to show his heart
It's scared and injured face
So long ago it felt so much
Only to be torn and bloodied
It armored itself no one would do it again
Then your sweet words
Touched it oh so deep
All the armor fell to the ground
Gone for ever more
So as my tears trickle down my cheek
My heart answers this song
It added a verse of its own
Better to have felt your love for a second
Than to never have felt it at all
