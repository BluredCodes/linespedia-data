title: "I Am So In Love With You"

Emancipation of my feelings
Enslavement 0f my heart 
To be in love with you
God!  She's all I can think of
On my mind, how is my precious love
All the time, want her near
I just love her so, adore and need her
She is just to good to be true
I am so in love with you
