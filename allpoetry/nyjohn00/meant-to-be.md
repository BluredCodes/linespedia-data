Meant to Be
                                  

Entangled in a love made before birth
This love planned by God
Awareness of a feeling, too strong to explain
A deep contemplation of a dream coming true

A special instant ready to be lived
A look of realization in a second glance
A union of two hearts now one
A completion, the missing half now found

 The sensation as a touch is mutual
 The warmth of a kiss given and taken
 The words I love you meant with every breath
 It is every cell of me yearning for your touch
