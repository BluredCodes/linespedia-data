When you've lost the love you had 
life feels like it is filled with despair,
When you're all alone 
no love or happiness fills the air,
When you see your one love sharing time with another,
Time you use to have
When you find she makes choices...you or the other.
What do you do when love flutters away?
Do you take it and hold it and force it to stay?
Or release it and whisper, "Please come back, one day."?
I feel you grant it,it's freedom
Giving all the love that you have
Let the true love that you feel see the happiness she is feeling
For if you truly love her,  her happiness is all you want

Just a question
