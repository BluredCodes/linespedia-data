Before we met
Life when on as it does
In mist and cloudy days
The shadow I did cast
Was faint and not too clear
A misty spirit who drifted here
And vanished in the fog
Then the sun broke thru
You came into my life
The fog cleared
Then mist vanished
I was caught in your light
The light of your love 
It was everywhere that I turned
And as I look my shadow formed
The image crystal clear
It defined me and strengthened me
And it was because of you
You are the light of my life
So sweet and oh so strong
Shine for me 
Everyday
I want your love forever
