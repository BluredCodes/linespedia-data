title: "feeling alone again"

Real tears flow tonight
The feelings of loneliness 
They cover my soul
My heart tells me something is wrong
It maybe my imagination
Maybe I misunderstand
Or just a feeling I have
In all the time we have shared
I never felt alone
But now the ache that I feel
Is drowning my thoughts
I think I know what you felt
Felt so long ago
On that one night
When you read my thoughts
