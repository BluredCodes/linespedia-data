I adore all the things you do
Really can't get enough
All I have to do, to brighten my day
Is picture my times with you

I picture how we dance
The magic flows and flows
Holding you oh so tight
Swaying to the sound

I picture you next to me
In a silly mood you are
Singing a little song 
Bringing me so much smiles

I picture you on a summer day
Sitting next to me
On a bench overlooking the river
Just content to share the time

I picture you on a windy fall day
Going for a walk with me
Leaves crunching underfoot
The colors make you glow

I picture you on a snowy winter day
In a romantic coffee shop with me
Sitting by the fireplace
Sharing and reading with me

I picture you on a sweet spring day
Light breezes in your hair
Eager to be outside with me
Seeing the wonders of life, rebirth of natures pride

I picture our love
Seeing it grow and grow
Wanting all the years to come
To be better than all that has been
