Emptiness, longing missing
They always go with love
I look toward seeing you 
I know it is not always possible
But then I look for the next best thing
Sharing thoughts and kisses online
At times I feel the same needs 
Are deep inside you too
And when we share of what we share
Is deep in hearts embrace
At other times I need you so
And when we meet to share
I feel you are not in the mood
Or really are elsewhere
And then the feelings of which I speak
Take over my very soul
Emptiness, longing, missing you
Takes over my whole mortal being
Don't quite feel at rest you know
Instead feelings of emptiness
Fill my very soul
The longing there to be holding you
My arms ache so
missing you , all of you
my love for you is so strong
