Geeesh
Your hands are always on me
When ever we are alone
You think it is so funny 
Tickling and probing
God you make me jump
Squirming all the time
The look of excitement you get 
As I try to avoid
Reminds me of a cat
That has it quarry cornered ready to strike
You use your legs and your body
To really hold me down
You want me at your mercy
I think I will let you win
