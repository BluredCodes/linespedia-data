A Colored Rose


The red rose shouts of passion, sultry love
The white rose whispers of love and romance
The red rose is an Eagle, seizing the moment
The white rose is a dove, promising peace

I send you a white rosebud, with crimson tips
It's the rose of our perfect love
Love that is pure of heart but marked with desire
Of loves sweet passion, as in our hearts
