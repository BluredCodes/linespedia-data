title: "Hours of my life"

There are twenty-four hours
Each and every day
They are all the same length
All with 60 minutes
Each and every day

Why is it that time goes so slow
And why does it go so fast
Slow when we're apart
And fast oh so fast
When we're together

The words, us and we
Are the reasons why
Every moment together
Goes so fast
Its gone so soon

Being with you is so much
Of what I want
What I desire
What I long to do
What was meant to be

The two of us
Smile and laugh
Tease and love
For every second of that time
Happy we are together

So if my life is
Defined by a time
A set amount, no more, no less
A moment with you
Is better than years alone
