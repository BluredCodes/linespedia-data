Warriors, Poets, and Bards

Walking over the hillock
On lush green Galway's shore
The mist of old Ireland
Surrounds and mystifies

I am surrounded 
The spirits of warriors
Poets and bards
Surrounds and mystifies

I hear their whispered voices
All telling me this or that
Telling me what the longed for
Surrounds and mystifies

The warriors tell tales, glorious and bold
Of fights for country and honor, to save their love one
Giving all they had, the ultimate price, conquering thru bravery 
Surrounds and mystifies

The poets spin a tail, romantic and with valor or in mirth
He tells of all warriors to fool, lovers an enemies alike
He paints a picture using only words lets you feel all that is told
Surrounds and mystifies

The bard is the traveler, singer o songs, reciting the poems and stories of old
His voice give feeling to all that was wrote, it makes you laugh if makes you cry
His voice can make you sigh in awe. He keeps the stories and legends alive
Surrounds and mystifies

To you my fairest lass, I truly want to be
The warrior that fights for you, putting myself between you and danger
The poet and bard, to write and sing to you, proclaiming all your wonder
Surrounds and mystifies,,, and engulfs you with love,,,
