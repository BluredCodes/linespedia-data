---
title: "Her Embrace"
writer: "nyjohn00"

I tell her my feelings
Confess my fears and shortcomings
On one hand telling her I love her
On the other, saying she deserves more
Thanking God that she is mine

She responds with love
Telling me how she adores
She holds me so tight
Giving me all her love
Thanking God that she is mine
