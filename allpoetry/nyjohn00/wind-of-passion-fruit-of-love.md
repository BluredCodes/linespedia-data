The hot wind of passion
Blows oh so strongly for us
It wraps all around us
As a lovers embrace

All it takes is the thought of US
To make this wind blow
The slightest thought
And the need doses grow

It brings on a fever
That only our kisses can quell
It makes us a delirium 
Only US on our minds

This hot wind brings the promise
Of the ripe fruit of ardor 
As the hot winds of summer
Our love does come true

This sweet passion
The nectar or love
Flows from the blessed
The blessed in love
