The wind blows a frosty chill
I feel a shiver coming
My arms on the windowsill
Gazing at the winter sky
I feel so lonely, this void I need to fill
A vast empty chasm inside
I need you and I always will
My eyes scan the vast gray clouds
No warmth is found there still
I yearn to drink in your warm love
And chase away this frosty chill
