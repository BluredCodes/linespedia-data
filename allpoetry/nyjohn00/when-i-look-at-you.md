Precious, when I look at you
And I really do that a lot
I look into your eyes to see what's going on inside
I look to see; just what it is your feeling
I look to see if that love glows at me

There are a lot of other times
When I look at you
That you are doing other things
You're not aware; I love these times as well
For in these times I see again, all I love so much

I see the warm glow of your skin
The lights dancing in your hair
The way you move, walk and sit
Your expressions when you talk to someone
All make you what you are

You are a dream come true
To look at you is to love, knowing is divine
I need your tender love, your lips touching mine
I want you next to me, your hand in mine
I need your presence, everyday of my life
