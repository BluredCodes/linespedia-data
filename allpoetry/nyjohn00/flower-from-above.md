Flower From Above
                                                                      
There are so many flower
In the world, colors and shapes galore 
It is said Roses, are the flowers of love,
but for me darling, you're my flower
from Heaven above.

A bouquet of Roses, has a fleeting beauty
At first so fresh and lush, for only a day or so
Then the beauty fade and withers
But you my bouquet of love, continue to bloom
Glowing with more beauty as the years go bye

My life was empty, devoid of beauty
Precious you filled me with life, it blossomed and grew
Happiness and contentment, inner peace and bliss
The scent of your flower, melts my heart with the desire for you
I need to breathe you in and never let you go
