Sparkles in your eyes dance
They gleam  like wild fire.
Your hair long and soft
Lips that reach for mine 
Like the petals of a flower
Reaching for the morning dew
Where does this beauty come from
Is it only in my dreams?
No,,,,,,,
She is you,,,,,
You are my dream
