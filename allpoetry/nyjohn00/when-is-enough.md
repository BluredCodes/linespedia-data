title: "When Is Enough"

I've thought about us
The time we've had together
The time we've lost
The time to come
When is enough

If I had met you thirty years ago
Would I still be so enthralled by you
Would I live to see your smiling face
Would I sit enraptured, listening to your day
When is enough

Longing to go on long walks
Talking for hours on end
Wondering at all the love we feel
Missing you the moment we part
When is enough

Remembering each and every moment
Shopping, dinners, movies
Holding your hand
Feeling your lips to mine
When is enough

I think of the time we've lost
Not meeting till of late
I get a sense of lost
Wondering what would have been
When is enough

I know the answers to my questions
They are written in my heart
If I'd met you way back then
And lived on to be one hundred
It still would not be enough
