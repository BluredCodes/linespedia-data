Passions on fire
Love engulfed
The embrace that never ends
Our eyes stare so deeply
Telling all that we feel
Lips pressing together
Moist tongues do dance
Thighs pressing to thighs
Trying to tell, with this one embrace
All the love that is held for one
The love of eternity
The longing that never ends
