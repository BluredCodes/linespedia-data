title: "As I close my Eyes"

As I close my eyes at night
I wish you were at my side
I think of the warmth you give
The touch of your heart to mine

As I close my eyes and darkness comes
I feel surrounded by your love
I picture your arms around me
Snuggled at my side

As I close my eyes waiting for sleep
I yearn for you to come
I feel my soul call your name
My heart opens and reaches for yours

As I close my eyes
I can only think of oh... sweet...
You
