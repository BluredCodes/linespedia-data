I Adore

Giving you my heart
My kisses, all my love
lone, when we're apart
Two as one, a miracle from God above

The sound of your sweet breathing
The caress of your soft lips
The feel of your heart beating
My sweetheart, my princess, she is mine

Honored to call you mine and I as yours
You are the world to me, breath of life so pure
Forever and always, side by side
You are the one that I adore

I love you
