I drowned in a sea of my own self pity,
and lost my heart at the bottom of the ocean,
but please dont give me your's,
because my love died, long ago.

I've never been so lost
but here i am,
deep in your heart
i tried to turn you down
but i couldnt,
not for your sake,
not for my own

Everyone has a weakness
mine is in your tearsPlease feel free to interpret, deconstruct, analyse and most important of all Critisise my work.
but pleease dont give me advice on life :P
