stuck deep in the essance of my thoughts
i lost my head, where did i leave my thoughts
so long ago, so long ago, missplacments seem frequent
who are you, to judge such a small mind,
empty mind
and all these faces seem familier,
yet i cant recall a single one
and damned if i can remember the vowels,
which were used so long ago,
come take my pain away,
but dont touch me.....ages ago,
