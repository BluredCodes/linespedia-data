---
title: "She"
writer: "Jdogg235"
slug: "she"

---

I see myself right in the mirror
But I'm not sure that I am there
She's on my mind, she's in my heart
But I can't see her, it's so hard

She calms me with a simple "hi"
And makes my heart sink with goodbye
Her eyes they seem so innocent
Her body is a compliment

I'm patient while she finds her way
I hope I get the chance to say
How much she really means to me
She's all I ever want to be

She's simple and that's why I love her

She is so sweet and so sincere
I wish that she were standing here
I'm better when she's helping me
I'm better with her company

I've searched myself for every word
But none are good enough for her
She's special in a million ways
Her personality displays

I'm happy that she is my friend
She's always got an ear to lend
Without her I'd feel so alone
She makes me understand and grow

She's simple and that's why I love her
