The world it seems so big to me
Well after all I'm only 3
I cannot write or read so well
But soon I will know how to spell
I'll make you all so proud of me
I know someday that you will see
I'll be the best oh just you wait
You'll see me off on my first date
I'll drive a car and get a job
Why did you just start to sob?
I know it's all so far away
But you must prepare everyday
Cuz soon I'll be leaving you
And there's nothing you can do
But for now I'll walk, not try to run
Keep it simple and be your son
