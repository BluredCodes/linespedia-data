title: "Confused"

the birds they fly they sing for me
cuz they realize that i can be
anything i want to be to do to live to hear to see
but what i really wanna know
is which direction i should go
i cannot follow them to the south
these words are fumbled out my mouth
and i have suffered long enough
but deep inside i know i'm tough
to never stop and just give up
to make mistakes and interrupt
it is my life and it is yours
we make our own beds......we must fight our own wars
but when the road gets bumpy and dark
and the pain swells deep within  your heart
you come to me i demand of you
i could not stand to see you blue
i am here forever more
cuz that is what a friend is for
