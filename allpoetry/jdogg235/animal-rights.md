Deep within the soggy woods
I see the footprints from some hooves
I walk along a muddy trail
I see the body first the tail
But what is different about this scene
I notice this is not a dream
It's lying there and cannot breathe
The hunter got it in the spleen
It's dying slowly so hard to take
But no way in hell that this is fake
The anger swells beneath my heart
That coward thinks that he is hard
It's sport he says but that's a lie
No innocent deer deserves to die
