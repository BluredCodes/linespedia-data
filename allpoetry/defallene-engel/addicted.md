title: "Addicted"
writer: "Defallene Engel"

Laying a kiss upon your palmI'll take away your tearsI'll be your wallWith crumbling bricksBreaking only when you wish itI'll be your shoulderWelcoming your pained criesTelling you it's alrightIt's alright, it's okayI'll be your sent angelThrown away by GodBut I'll protect youWrap you up in golden feathersKissing you gentlyWith a feverish touchIt's okay now, you're safeI'll become your drugFeeding your addictionIt's alright, take another biteNo - stop cryingI'll take the pain awayI'll be your sedativeI'll be thereIt's okayIt's alright...Whispers...
