Ah, another victimDeliciously lickingTickling up the sidesWarming comfortFalse safetyDevil's angelsDancing eroticallyMotioning forthHow gentle it flowsRising higherIn a pulsing rhythmA beating rhymeIn a consuming heatAll empoweringHow it spreadsSmoothly taking overLeaving little footprintsOf delirious impsGiggling as oneDevouring orgasmsAnd leaving nothing...But ash
