---
title: "Midnight Rain"
writer: "Defallene Engel"
slug: "midnight-rain"
source: "AllPoetry"

A steady beatingAgainst the already wet pavementBrushing, slipping off leavesDescending forever, togetherAlmost as one unified bodyFalling against already formed puddlesLittle sparkles of splashesShining with the light of the moonItty bitty dancing angelsGiggling imps, slick and wetDevilish funStolen under the darkened night skyWhispered sinBrushing against and past wet earsDrenched, soaked skinShivering softly with mad delightsOf resurrected pleasuresLong forgotten with failed loveFalling still, heaven's tearsCrying for pain fresh and oldWounds healing with a fresh start
