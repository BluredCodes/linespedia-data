February 20
Is such an evil day
The day that i was born
And when my brother passed away

February 20
It started out just fine
Woke up with happiness
Death never came to mind

February 20
School was going great
Had no knowledge of
My little brother's fate

February 20
They took me out of class
Told me my brother had fainted
With a single quiet gasp

February 20
My little bother died
He was just so young
For six hours straight I cried

February 20
Will never be the same
He was only ten years old
And Stephen was his nameThis really happened to me. I had just turned fourteen. other poems about my brother's death are "No chance for goodbye" by myself and "Stephen" by Phoenix
