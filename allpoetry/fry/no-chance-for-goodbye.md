title: "No chance for goodbye"

No chance for goodbye
It happened so fast
An innocent life
taken by death's grasp

A stop of a heart
A gasp for air
A fall to the ground
Why's life so unfair?

A tender young age
Of ten years old
He lay there so still
On the ground so cold

No birthday for me
No candles to blow
Just sadness and pain
Regret and sorrow

I cannot remove
The images in my head
Of my precious little brother
Lying there, dead

I saw his pale body
And his lips so blue
And I knew right then
That his life was through

A piece of my heart
Gone with the wind
And I know
That I will always love him
