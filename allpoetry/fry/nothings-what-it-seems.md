I went over to your house
Just the other day
Heard you were in an accident
Wanted to know if you were ok

As i was walking down the path
The air was somewhat dense
I rang your doorbell only once
And waited in silence

As i waited on your doorstep
I heard a single cry
It sounded like your mother
So i let myself inside 

I asked her if she was ok
And she rushed into my arms
She looked up at me with tear stained eyes
And her face looked so alarmed

"You mean to tell me you don't know
What happened to my son?
He was hit by a car yesterday
And died just after 1:00
