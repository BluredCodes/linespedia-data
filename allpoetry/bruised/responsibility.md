one more thought
to edit with
highlighter ambitions
claspin hands
around sins and tears
could've killed
with metal monsters
and sleepy eyed sadness
to make me wicked 
bar'd cage home
where i would've
suffer'd rape
of my soul and
viking raid 
lost experiences
due to instability
of blu sky'd
dysfunctionin nuclei
pumpin out
electric bile 
into the 
septic temples
where fuckin
on altars 
is ritual comin of age
processed kodaks
tryin to squash 
you into me 
cause i am cold
and devour your
body heat
into my first layer
knowin it is
already deeper
with rythmic beats
paper lined throughout
my entire being
with book cover
conversations tryin 
to unchain the melody
and crawl into 
the green eyed slave
at my foot with
tainted rags and wipes
not fit 
for my ass
all introverted
and made right
through one 
rehearsed line
