---
title: "who was it"
writer: "bruised"
slug: "who-was-it"

mixin with stigma
cold meat slab
shiverin on the
floor like a 
dyin animal
disturbed by
an angel's embrace
wrapped with love
like a surprise gift
for no reason
eyes heavy and
body weak 
driftin
in and out
of sight
a little dingy in
the torrent oceans
all for you
i do not
wither
like a weed, rose
ready to open
broken thorax
pourin out eyes
dancin with
the light of a sun
onto my fragile
form
all for me
tears kissed and
swords dropped
for a moment
passed by
