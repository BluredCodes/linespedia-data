---
title: "The Muse"
writer: "Linrose"
slug: "the-muse"

She embraces me in sleep ~
Calling to me through images,
Symbolic language often steeped ~
In mystic shadows,
Seeking clarity and outward flow.

She nudges me in waking hours ~
Extending the key of inspiration ~
To magically unlock the code ~
Of those images deep within,
Awaiting the stillness of mind;

Breathing in Spirit reveals to me ~
The story in its entirety.

My lowly mind then seeks to paint ~
This moment with a palette of words,
The hues of which are rather faint ~
When viewed in the Light of my Muse.
