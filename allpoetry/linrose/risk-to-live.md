title: "Risk To Live"
writer: "Linrose"

To chip away at the shell of comfortand walk through realms yet unexplored,Demands the valour of a Trojan will ~Prepared to face imagined foes;To stretch the elastic of hardened minds, Requires faith to leap beyondthe claims of truth which obscure vision ~If lauded past their measured time;To see infinity with finite eyes,Obliges hearts to humbly openand embrace the muses of celestial spheres ~Then speak new truths from wisdom born.
