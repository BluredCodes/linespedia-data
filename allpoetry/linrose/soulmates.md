---
title: "Soulmates"
writer: "Linrose"
slug: "soulmates"

Last night, I felt your touch ~
Sweet kiss of nectar on my lips,
We barely know each other, as such ~
Yet, from love’s cup we’ve taken a few sips;
Your voice, so soothing, is a mere hint of the depth 
of your soul, to which your words transported me;
Your spirit’s fire has burned my breath ~
And silenced my mind, so that I may see 
with my inner eyes, the simple truth of your being ~
And the radiant beauty of your heart;
I felt myself floating, as if I were dreaming ~
To a place deep inside you, that’s set apart 
from the person which the world sees ~
Your secret temple wherein lies your core ~
A place to where you are brought humbly to your knees;
I have felt and seen this part of you before ~
When my own Self has met yours ~
Stripped of the layers of physical illusion,
Those times, when we have met at death’s doors ~
And our souls have loved without confusion.
Most times we forget the pure love we felt as spirits,
But find glimpses when we pass each other in life once more;
Then comes a life when we may be blessed and hear it ~
The echo of our souls’ song, playing dimly in the roar 
of life’s discordant music, in the past, distorting our minds, leading us to fear it ~
The triumphant blare of pure harmony, 
which dares to seize us and cut through to our beings ~
To reawaken our deepest memory
of a love unlike no other, to give our lives meaning;
All this I felt and wonder if the wait ~
Is finally over for my soul’s mate.Written on January 4, 1994
