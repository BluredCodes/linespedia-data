My love is the fountain to quench your thirst;My oasis, within the mountain ~Awaits you to burst ~through locked doors ~Beyond where I lie waitingon love's shores,Watching Time's clock, anticipatingsurrender, sweet surrender.My days turn to nights ~All blending together,I yearn to feel the heightsof our love, strolling through heatheron ancestral land, in a time long ago ~Where our seeds were sown,And with forget-me-nots, left to grow ~Until such time we could hear ourselves moanin surrender, sweet surrender.Now is the time to renew our love ~Butterflies together, in first flight;We soar into the clear skies above,Cocoons of the past left in the night;Wonderment overtakes us aswe linger in perfumed bedsof rose petals, listening to soft jazz, Gazing at each other, like two newlyweds ~As we finally surrender, embrace sweet surrender.
