---
title: "Shadow Love"
writer: "Linrose"
slug: "shadow-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Your voice laps against my ears ~
A gentle tide of tender words;
The rhythm of your fervent heart ~
Joins mine in sweet embrace.

My eyes lock onto the radiant stars ~
To find, glowing within their depths ~
Yours, like phantoms flickering in the night ~
To sweep me up and follow your pace.

In sleep, I feel your feathery touch ~
Soft against my cheek;
I turn towards yours to feel your breath ~
Find only an empty space.
