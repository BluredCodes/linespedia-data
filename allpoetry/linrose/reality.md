title: "Reality"
writer: "Linrose"
slug: "reality"

Moistened grass beneath my feet, 
Scent of roses, a perfume treat,
Chocolate melting in my mouth,
Time freezes, watching geese fly south,
Music swirls throughout my being – 
My heart expands with what I’m hearing;
Awakened senses, so very real,
Yet touch dimensions surreal;
Imagination now takes over – 
I see the stars within my grasp,
I hear the music of the spheres,
Cosmic energy my heart does clasp,
While scent of solar gases nears;
My soul then tastes infinity.
