---
title: "Shattered Love"
writer: "Linrose"
slug: "shattered-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/5611-Shattered-Love-by-Linrose"
---

Taste the salt of my tears
burning their way to my chin;
Watch them fall in a cascade of fear
onto my trembling bosom;
Feel my pulse race beneath your thumbs ~
As you gently stroke my inner wrists;
Hear my thoughts, racked with pain ~
As I listen to your crafted words;
Reach into my aching heart and find
your poison and love as one;
Touch my soul, with your own ~
Recall the depth of feeling
from which we vowed never to run;
Lift me, broken at your feet,
Kiss me tenderly, if you can ~
Pretend, there's still meaning.
