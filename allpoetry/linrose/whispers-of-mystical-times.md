---
title: "Whispers of Mystical Times"
writer: "Linrose"
slug: "whispers-of-mystical-times"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Hush! Be still and listen past your heartbeat ~
To the swelling sound beckoning from an ancient time;
Hear the melancholy stirrings of the Celtic harp so sweet ~ 
And maidens singing verses of love in rhyme;

Feel beneath your feet, the thunderous sound of hooves ~ 
As knights of the Crusades proceeded to war ~
Championing their ladies’ scarves as woos ~ 
Heroes, alive or dead in gore, immortalized in books of lore;

See majestic castles in Surrey and Sussex ~
Glimpse the mystical kingdom of Camelot;
View the sorcerer Merlin preparing a hex ~
Against which no mortal man could have fought;

Smell the putrid breath of a famed Sussex dragon,
Which dwelled in the watery depths of Knucker Hole ~
Its reign of terror ended by a local son,
The Lyminster Knucker dead, never again to take a soul.

Feel the presence of Arthur, Lancelot and Guinevere ~ 
Their triangle of love weaved mythic tales for eternity;
Gallant Lancelot, his desire transformed, removed all fear ~ 
As he valiantly fought for his Lord and Lady.

Remember these things held deep within your soul ~
Let their essence enliven your mind ~
To recall which lifetime's ancient role ~
Your heart desires to once again find.
