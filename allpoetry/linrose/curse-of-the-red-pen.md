Inspired by and dedicated to my friend, Lurie :-)


Verses, tomes, and tests of skill – 
One thing they have in common,
Letters linked in form of words –
Not often used with caution.

That’s when my pen of red is used – 
To fix what has gone wrong,
Just like a hypodermic shot – 
Its sting can be quite strong.

Alas, the hand, like Pavlov’s dog – 
Has learned with every cue,
From eyes that see a gaffe so slight – 
The hand knows what to do.

It takes the dreaded pen of red – 
The slashes rival Zorro’s,
But quickly heals the wounded words –
Not wishing to cause sorrows.

I wish that I could hide this pen –
Whenever I read words,
Beyond the scope of daily work –
All edited out in herds.

The pleasure’s marred now when I read –
For errors jump at me,
Just like a ghost desiring peace – 
They beg to be set free.

So hence this curse has found its way –
Into my very soul,
Language, dressed in Sunday best – 
By pen of red – that is my goal.
