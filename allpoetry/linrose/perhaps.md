---
title: "Perhaps"
writer: "Linrose"
slug: "perhaps"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Perhaps the time for union has come,
Assured are we of destiny’s claim;
Plunge bravely into the depths of scum,
A journey wrought with guilt and shame;
Reach deep within the bowels of Earth,
Your groping hands must never falter ~ 
Give savage fears ample berth ~
As you seek the grail, your sacred altar;
Remember your feelings from long ago,
As memories’ images pass you by,
Medusa’s locks will deftly flow ~
Around you, hoping to pry ~
Your Will, engaged in brutal battle,
Plunging its sword through the decay ~
Of your effigies in death rattle,
Resigned, no longer can delay ~
Your union with the inner Gold;
Should you safely reach the end,
Your task no less than daring and bold,
Release the weapons, time to ascend,
Embrace your soul, your long-lost friend ~
This union you have sought through ages lapsed ~
Perhaps.
