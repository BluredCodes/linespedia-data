title: "Distant Yearning"
writer: "Linrose"

Alone, at the edge of my world
I reach out to you –  
Millions of stars between us;
Moonbeams, catching my glistening tears,
Speed to your distant world – 
To find you waiting, anticipating,
Face worshipping the ebony sky – 
My musky mist to light upon closed eyes;
Heartbeats away, minds touch one another –
Souls calling, a loon's lament;
Serenade me, my love –
With your inner music,
Fling tender kisses as seeds in the wind,
To swell the ground of my fertile being;
Each day's sun brings distance closer – 
To when we finally meet;
Our aching flesh to travel life's road,
To where our souls reside in their loving abode.Written in 1996
