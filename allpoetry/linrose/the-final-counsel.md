Fingers inch across the radio console,Masters at directing soundsfighting to be heard;Staccato voices, racing with airwaves,Cause deft fingers to pause.The shrill ad-pitch over,Other tonal members - Variances of words and music,Are led to the platform of expressionby fingers of fairness.The sounds, having all been heard,Are now shut off.Silence chosen.
