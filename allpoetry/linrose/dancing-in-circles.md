---
title: "Dancing in Circles"
writer: "Linrose"
slug: "dancing-in-circles"

We danced round and round
to the haunting sound
of a long-ago waltz ~
When the music halts ~

Still dancing in circles.

We danced round and round
through the fog of years ~
Forgetting our tears ~
Remembering the sound ~

Still dancing in circles.

We danced round and round
on the hard cold ground ~
Under a sky so grayed ~
The waltz was not played ~

Still dancing in circles.

We dance round and round ~
Though there is no sound ~
Awaiting the light ~
To once more take flight ~

While dancing in circles.
