---
title: "Love Sublime"
writer: "Linrose"

Though an ocean away, I feel your touch ~ Caressing me, like teasing willows;Your love, channeled on pockets of air ~Haunts me on chimes of wind.My longing heart responds to yours ~Cradled in fields of forget-me-nots;Your kisses, butterflies upon my lips ~Fleeting flicks of velvet wings.Rose petals rouse me with your scent ~As I bathe, immersed in your depths;Your fiery eyes gleam in candle flames ~To sear my leadened soul.Soon my love, we'll join as one ~In our magical wood, beneath the sun;Nymphs will sprinkle their enchanted dust ~To seal us, in spellbound rhapsody.
