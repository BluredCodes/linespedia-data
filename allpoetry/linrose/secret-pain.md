---
title: "Secret Pain"
writer: "Linrose"

Tears drop, sharply
onto my reddened knuckles,
Hands clenched against their salty brine;
Liquid shards sear my flesh ~
Pock-marked by the droplets of steam.

Teardrops, capsules of sad tales
to be released, shadowing
the spaces between them;
Dark places, left unexpressed.
