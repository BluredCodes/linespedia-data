F                                     PEACE ~
               L                                Sing the angels,
               A                            A word so easy on the lips,
               G                            Yet forgotten in the heart.
              Atop                                                         
              The                                                           
              Pole                                                         
      That once stood high                     Then one fateful day
      In God's blue sky;                       We saw doomsday
      The glass and steel                      When on tv screens
      That could not feel                      Saw horrid scenes;
      Yet lives moved in                       The towers struck-
      That concrete rim;                       By jets run amuck;
      Thousands of lives,                      The terrorists'ploy-
      As though in hives,                      To brutally destroy
      Worked diligently                        Icons, people, and
      For their families;                      Spirit of the land;
      Symbol of America                        Then the Pentagon,
      It served as a mecca                     Too was hit upon;
      Diverse companies-                       A final blow came
      As monetary sentries                     With some acclaim
      Showed their power                       For the brave men-
      Through the towers;                      Fought evil men-
      The web reached far-                     Crashed the plane
      Linked all to its star                   Away from domain;
      World Trade Center                       U.S. fabric is strong
   Pride of every New Yorker.              Made so by its people's song.


                      The rubble now beneath the feet
                      May lead some to believe defeat;
                    Yet from that pile of rocks and dirt,
                 America's spirit arose, not broken nor hurt.

                    The souls of those departed now fly - 
                 With angels among them in realms of high;
                   Yet their love returns on angels' wings
                   To enter hearts and pour in blessings.As a Canadian, in Montreal, Quebec, I was just as horrified as our good friends to the south by the terrible events of September 11/2001. My condolences to all who have suffered through the loss of a family member or friend.
