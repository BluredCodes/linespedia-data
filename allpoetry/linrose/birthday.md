title: "Birthday"
writer: "Linrose"

Why should you feel so old ~
When your soul has been so bold ~
To weave a dazzling tapestry 
of lives, in essence, so young and carefree;
For what matters the vehicle you choose ~
When the spirit within you moves ~
In successive lifetimes of experiences,
Like a child changing toys to challenge his senses;
So be not concerned with this body, which has earned
every wrinkle and sag in its service to your soul,
For this lifetime is a mere drop in eternity's bowl.
