title: "The Cusp"

Heinous hounds of hell’s domain,
Roam the Earth in search of game;
Terror strikes in random places,
Indifferent to the fearful faces.

Sweeping gestures made by some,
Echo hollow like a drum
for victims of injustice care ~
Not for words but deeds of dare.

Splendid soldiers of the Light, 
Enter night to fight the plight;
Darkness shrouds the Earth awhile,
Silence cracked by tensions’ bile.

Swirls of smoke sear the calm,
Clashes, chaos, surreal realm;
Heaven and hell meet at Ground Zero,
Fury peaks, awaits a hero.

The hounds of hell are kept at bay,
While heaven’s angels save the day;
But clouds still loom above the Earth,
As Evil seeks to forge a berth.

Good and Evil are at crossroads,
Earth at mercy of moon’s nodes;
Cosmic winter falls upon us,
Sword of cusp in Earth's heart thrust.

Fearless fiends with heroes battle,
Earth is shaken by death rattle ~
As Love and Hate are locked in war,
Which will enter victory’s door?Written October 6th, 2001 ©  on Oct 06 2001 04:36 PM PST   abuse
