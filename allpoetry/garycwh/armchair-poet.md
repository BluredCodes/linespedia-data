love, environment and etcetera	anything near or far	provides the longing of freedom	whether in the mind or body	the perfect picture for the words	to the flow of poetry	of the armchair poet	sitting in his fortress	each vivid emotion is tryingly	etched into words	denying justice to the beauty of life	as if the frog in the well	had turned philosopher	and the far bounds of the universe	limited by a stroke of literature	how could the hearts 	that are touched by these words	could in turn be freed 	into the infinite depth of the mind	when the pens are tied to the table	and the poet to the chair	release it and you can feel	the unyielding delicateness	not unlike the rain that shapes the mountains	or the whistling wind that carves the stones	through its delicate prances	and metrical dances	its shapes much more than fantasies	or dreams 	a lot more than it seems....written back a long time ago when i was figuring out why i was into poetry...
