In the deepest caverns of my mind
I’ve always hoped to find
The secret kept by fate
My journey in life ahead
For I’ve always yearned to be a star
Not for its seemingly eternal glow
But for its end when it blows
Into a magnificent supernova
If only for one moment
I too could burst forth
And burn so bright
Lighting the darkest nights
My light shall drown the universe
And send darkness into oblivion
For everyone to see
In admiration of my beauty
For the chance to inspire
With my radiantly courageous fire
One moment is all I’ve yearned
To shine in utmost brilliance
For all to see in astonishment
And forever etched in remembrance.
