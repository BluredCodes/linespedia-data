For every child born into this world
	Somewhere…
	Someplace someone passes away
	Giving up his place in the scheme of things
	One cannot fault nature
	As it can also be beautiful
	To accept that there are seasons in life
	One day when my winter comes to stay
	Somewhere…
	Someplace in the world will bloom into spring.
