---
title: "mannequin"
writer: "garycwh"

my window to the world
	looks upon an interchange
	i stand here for weeks
	basking in the sun
	and the night lights
	this corner of my world
	stand above two stories high
	much nearer to the sky
	
	people passing by
	staring with yearning eyes
	never satisfied
	with what money can buy
	the four ways that stretched
	brings life to my days
	teenagers and school children
	went all the same way
	learning the value of the currency
	is never enough
	for the mind's fancies
	
	a beggar sat on the pavement
	legs raw with wounds
	in front of a posh plaza
	beneath the sun and moon
	staring blankly at passerby
	whose eyes could never meet
	and went on with a sigh
	dazed not by the blazing heat
	or the saddening reality
	but by the freezing coldness
	of human sympathy
	
	i envy what i see
	but even if i could break free
	i wouldn't know if
	the better i'll be.
