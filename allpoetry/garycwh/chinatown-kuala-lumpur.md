Bright lights in the bustling of a modern city
	twilight in the lanes with a colonial history
	tall buildings mark the skyline of the setting sun
	as nostalgia backdrops into a bargain hunt
	in almost a paradoxical ambience
	one can find in here a medley of wares
	testing only your unending patience
	as haggling is a serious affair
	
	with many stalls lined up everywhere
	and the crowd moving at their own pace
	one can almost get lost in there
	sieving through the temptations of the place.
	
	From names that grace the walls of the West
	To brands that say ‘Local is best’
	From adornments laced with glittery
	To food, fruits and not to forget, veggies.
	
	Yet for a brief moment in time
	two lives are touching
	brought together from across the world
	only an acquaintance of the moment
	whether in anger from bargaining
	in frustration from haggling
	in joy from a good buy
	from the stall with the nice guy
	and after leaving this enchanting place
	they’d soon forget each other’s face.
	Leaving only the ware they traded
	As a token of the encounter that faded.
