No, the title is not a technical error
Just a quick take on the site’s nature
Given 5 rows for the new poems list
Must try hard to ensure it is not missed

When adding the poem, the time must be right
To ensure many others are within sight
Once entered, I’ll be busy refreshing
Hoping to see the comments I’m fishing

Within the next 10 minutes I must at least
Be busy adding to the list
Keep afloat within the shutters
Keeping a close eye on the counters

Anyway, I hope this take you do enjoy
Before I continue to make too much noise
This could be an attempt to stay afloat
At least the next 5 minutes it’s still in the note

signing off for the day…. Slurp!
