render me deaf	and take my voice may you	but i'd plead and beg 	for my sight and all that's left	and my heart that's always true	i've found my joy in this world	around the colors of my life	the only thing that my heart	and my eyes	would never betray	the amber dawn upon the eastern skies	and the dusk that skillfully	draw the curtains across the western seas	the grass and mountains so green	leaves my heart and eyes in serene	forever enchanted and drunk	the bright blue skies 	that stretched the four horizons	would never lie	to me	the bright yellow ball of fire	that shines upon all the life	that it gives	the purple, red and white flowers	decorated fields and meadows	all dressed up by nature	to celebrate the joyous love	and life that God has given	everyday i live my life anew	cause i've found all the colors 	for my heart	when i finally found you !... a light poem dedicated to my wife (then girlfriend).
