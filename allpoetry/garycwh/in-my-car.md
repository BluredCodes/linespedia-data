Facing the daily toil of driving to work
In the midst of the traffic of a six-lane highway
I stop to the brake lights of the car in front
Stuck in the midst of all these cars
Stuck in the midst of all these people
Chanced upon by this moment
Shared the frustrations and nonchalance 
The lady on my left with eyes fixated on the car in front of her
…. Hanging tightly to her steering, in depth of thoughts or void
To my right, the driver chatted busily on his mobile phone
…. He’s into himself somewhere else
The man in front adjusting his tie in his rear view mirror
…. Never here but in his hands
…. Briefly touched as our eyes meet
As my left foot tiredly hung to my clutch
Jerking my car as I attempt to inch forward
…. I feel so alone.
