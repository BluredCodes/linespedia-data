a flow of strong emotions
overwhelms me
is this the meaning of art?
the vivid images which shows no bounds
the abstractness which arouses in me
a curious hand that grasp firmly at my heart
drawing every breath with each stroke
whether poetry,music,love or life
an innate instinct stirs me deep
stretching my mind beyond any dimensions
undescribable in mere words
it is the feeling
  of deja vu..
  like i'm passing a deep tunnel
  like the wind that touches the autumn
  like the flowers in the meadow
  like the mountains that caresses the skies
  like dark blue water in a sunset lagoon
  like the eagle that soars the skies
  like the beauty of a dawning sky
the beauty of the common....
