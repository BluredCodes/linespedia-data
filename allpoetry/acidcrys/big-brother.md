Shhh, don't say a word, big brother,just hold my hand.Shhh, don't say this is absurd, big brother just lead the way.I used to always look up to you,you were bigger than me in so many ways.I always tried to be like you,and failed each and all of my days. Shhh, don't say a word, big brother,just hold my hand.Shhh, don't say this is absurd, big brother,just lead the way.You saw the tears falling from my face,I wish they didn't give me away.Please don't leave me alone in this place, there's too many things I can't say. Shhh, don't say a word, big brother,just hold my hand. Shhh, don't say this is absurd, big brother, just lead the way.Yes, I'm oh so scared;I've always been, over the loss of you.Ugh, I don't want my feelings bared.. just know, this love is nothing new.after the other new poem I posted.. I decided to go for a more light hearted one of mine.. the other one was too real.. too honest.. too heavy.. maybe Ill repost it when I can handle it. Anyways, this one is to my brother about him going away to college.
