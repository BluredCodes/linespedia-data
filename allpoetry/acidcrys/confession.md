As night shadowed the world last night

I let my fingers creep into your soft hair

As the lights dimmed into darkness

I let my body breathe against yours

As a yawn escaped from you

I let my wanton lips capture the whispers from your mouth

As the world hushed into a somber silence

I let my fingertips explore every curve and inch of you

As the moon crawled amongst the stars

I let myself taste you



As darkness engulfed me

I let you take me

As my body went limp with sleep

I let my back arch in ecstasy

As my breathing calmed into a steady rate

I let the heat intensify

As I fell completely into that imagination releasing trance

I let my body liquify

As the night rolled on

I let myself be cradled in your arms



I confess,
