title: "Possessed"

I'm in your Grasp;and your Nails are Digging into my Soul,with Cold Dark Blood Trickling Down,Staining my Pure Skin.You're in my Mind;and you're Plaguing my Thoughts,with the Passion of Guilt,Scarring my Innocence.I'm in your Path;and you're Spinning Out of Control,with Darkness Breathing Deep,Swallowing my Life.You're in my Heart;and your Words are Weaving their Way in,with their Daggers Piercing Repeatedly,Killing my...Me.
