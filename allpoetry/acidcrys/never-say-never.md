I love you, Crystal.(today? tomorrow? and forever?)Your eyes are so beautiful.(my tears just make them shine.)I want to Hold you.(close? only to let me go?)I've never felt this way before.((I love you too))I love her, Crystal.(before? now? always?)Her eyes are so beautiful.(her ego just makes them shine.)I am holding her.(in your arms? too tightly?)I've never felt this way before.((but.. I love you..))Why don't you love me, Crystal?(yesterday, today, always forever.)Why do you hide your eyes from me?(your lies make them see all too clearly.)Why Cant I hold you?(stranger's arms leave me feeling cold.)I've never felt this way before.((I still love you..))I love her, Crystal.(until when?)Her eyes are so beautiful.(because yours are so blind.)I am holding her.(I hope she lets go.)I've never felt this way before.(Never say never.)((I never want to feel this way again))I've made this blunt and casual to fit my mood about the matter.. I've gotten to a point where, I'm coming to no longer care that I seem to be everyone's fling. Of course, this is him talking, the parenthesis are my thought
