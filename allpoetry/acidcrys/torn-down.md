Is this what you wanted?For me to fear you;For my everyday to be haunted?Does it make you feel good?To know that Im down on my kneestorn down from where i once boldly stood?Will you ever come to realize,that you cause the sorrowful plaguethat darkens these pair of eyes?Does it make you feel like you're flying?knowing that youve Torn me Down,causing me to wish I was dying?
