Why do I love you(god i cant breathe)Why are you hurting me so much (god i cant stop crying) Why cant you understand (god my head hurts so much) Why cant I stop thinking about you (god i feel so weak)Why do you Hate me (and say you love me?)Why cant we just be honest(why cant you be honest?)Stop giving me a reason to cry(Im telling you, its not me.)Stop draggin me in (i cant take it anymore.)I want to breathe and smile and live again. (Why do your words hurt me so much?) You easy elegant pass-me-overs.Your easy silence. Your easy neverminds and forget its.. why am i letting myself get hurt so easily?this is another oldie.. but I'm not sure when I wrote it.. its kind of really free flow.. another one of those.. one sentence is what I'm saying.. one sentence is what I'm thinking type of sentences.. random painful thought
