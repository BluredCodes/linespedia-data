---
title: "Disappear"
writer: "acidcrys"

Please look down
and walk away.
I don't want to be found
or be asked to stay.

I just want to disappear

Don't leave the fire burning 
or try to make things right.
I want this world to just keep on turning
and to forfeit this never ending fight.

I just want to disappear

Don't tell me that loves there
or there are dreams to endure;
because theres nothing, Im aware,
and That love is surely not the cure.

I just want to disappear

Please dont follow my track,
and my door, please be sure to close.
I don't want to come back
-I know I'm the one you all oppose.

I just want to disappear

Don't say this act is just me trying to be vain,
and try ever hard not to let sadness be.
I'm just tired of being driven insane;
I'm tired of being me.

I just want to disappear.listening to music.. picked a word "Disappear" and wrote a poem on it. Dated August 2000. (wow I cant believe its been a year) one of my more organized poems.
