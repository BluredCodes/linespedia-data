I'm meltingin the pin-needle shadowsof your icy stare.A fever radiates from where your frozen lipsgraze my crinkled forehead.Sweat tiptoes its way down my rippled backbone,glazing the trace of your fingertips.Crimson flames of hairsigh to their death asthey caress your chilly face.The scorching whisperssizzling off my tongue,shatter to pieces at your feet.Inching away,your reach still flushes my cheeks..A slip in my footing,your touch, still a piercing slap.Fallen to hands and kneesyour help, still a cold shoulder.Frostbite lingers in my heart,And Here I am, melting into you.wrote this.. in five minutes..about five minutes ago.
