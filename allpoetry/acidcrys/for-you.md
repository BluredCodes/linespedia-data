Brush your hand across my face.See the Tear, feel its Trace.That Tear is For you;For All The things I wish you knew.Against my lips place your hand;watch as they mark its land.That Kiss is for you,For All the things I wish we could do.Lay your hand against my Chest;feel as it heaves & lays back to rest.That rapid breathing is for you;For this feeling of ecstasy so new.Look into my Eyes.See that in them Hope Lies.That Hope is For you;For all that you wish to come true.This one was forced. A guy asked me to write a poem about him, so i figured hey, Ill put hidden meanings of my feelings for him in it! Never Do that. Damn Clever Guys. The endings abrupt too because it had a really.. personal 'hidden meaning' in it and he figured it out.. so I changed it and Lied. Oi.
