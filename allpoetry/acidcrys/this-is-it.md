Vines climb into my throat,
clutching strands of my hair
intertwining against the 
arch of your whispers
as they claw into my bleeding breath
gushing through my lips
as my skin gasps and 
seeps into my quivering bones,
lunging into your words
sewn through pulses and trembles
slowly stitching my lips,
enveloping them in silence

Intoxicating thorns blossom 
in the hollows of my eyes
whetting the crimson leaves
swimming in my irises,
reaving the shallow depth
licking my savory heart-
held with wavering stability
against my chest swimming
with smiling piranas devouring
the inexistence of my skin
protecting my voluptuous needs.don't understand it? Step outside of the box darling.
