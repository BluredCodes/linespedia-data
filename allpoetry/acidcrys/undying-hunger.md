Sifting the sand of life between my toes,
winding sanity around my finger;
I feed on you hungerily,
savoring the lingering taste.

Each letter of your name
is ingrained within every pore.
The languid sigh of it
dripping from my
beckoning lips.

Each angle of your face
etched on every shiver of my irises.
Your angelic beauty
burning into every corner
of my flourishing dreams.

Each moment with you
laced into the fragments of my mind.
They whisper softly into
my every thought,
plagueing me with unending memories.

Each blood pulsing beat
of my thriving heart;
You engulf me,
with your possessing essence,
drowning me blissfully.

My sand creeps inbetween your toes,
sanity unwinds from my finger;
But my hunger never satisfies,
