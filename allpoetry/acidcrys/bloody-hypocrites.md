title: "Bloody Hypocrites"

I am prisoner to the ground-
held by the cement of your absence;
and you ask,
"will you leave me?"
I can't even see you.

I am raped and undefined-
by the torture of your lust;
and you ask,
"will you touch me?"
I can't even touch myself.

I am bound at the wrists-
by the chains of your ploys;
and you ask,
"will you hurt me?"
I can't even reach you.

I am lashed and scarred-
by the whips of your doubt;
and you ask,
"will you need me?"
I can't even feel..

I am gagged and choking-
on the blood of your silence;
and you plead,
"say you love me?"

..I can't breathe..bloody fucking hypocrites.. why do I love you?
