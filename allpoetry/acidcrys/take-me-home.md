---
title: "Take Me Home"
writer: "acidcrys"
slug: "take-me-home"

Strange faces,
suffocate me.
Can't breath,
in familiarity.

Take Me Home

Grainy fingers,
grasp me.
Can't writhe,
in freedom.

Take Me Home

Rigid backs,
block me.
Can't bask,
in warmth.

Take Me Home

Steel words,
silence me.
Can't speak,
in honesty.

Take Me Home

Fragile bones,
weaken me.
Can't live,
in tranquility.

Take Me Home
(Take Me to a Place that Doesn't Exist)
