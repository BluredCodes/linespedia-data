softly whispering
unconsciously i awakened you
calmly speaking
secretly I beckoned you
serenely yelling
I unknowingly intrigued you
openly crying
I.. lost you
uncontrollably screaming
I tried to understand you
finally silenced
I.. found you

doing nothing
you beckoned me
barely speaking
you entranced me
harshly yelling
you scared me
never crying
you intrigued me
willingly screaming
you enticed me
always silenced
you hurt me

showing you my all
i boosted your ego
requesting the same
i lost you again

why must you confuse mei think this one might be a little confusing?
