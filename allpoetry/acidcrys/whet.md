Sweat trickles 
down the sides of my forehead, 
as my clothes cling 
to my body's curves. 
Sweltering Heat admist winter.

Breeding  inside of me.
Breathing through out me
Pouring rain. 
Rumbling thunder. 
Blinding lightning.

Swiftly crashing into the water
clothes tightening against my body
as they heavy with weight 
Thick and curly lush hair, 
falls moistedly straight.
Tendrils pressed to my cheeks
Skin slick and smooth as silk.

Under the moonlight 
swells of milky white breast, 
the curves of wide hips, 
indents of collar bones  escape.. 
Drips of water gleam
clinging to my eyelashes,
Hanging off the tip of my nose
and the edge of my lips..hm?
