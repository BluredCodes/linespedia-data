I feel your presence..when you're not there
I feel you surrounding me
I feel you caress my cheek
I feel you wisp through and ruffle my hair
I breathe you in slowly and deeply
I feel your coldness through my throat and my nostrils
I feel you come in through my eyes
I feel you flow into my internal tubes
I feel you taking over my lungs

I breathe louder and harder...clearer

I feel you wrap around my beating heart
Causing it to go slower and then faster..
causing it to cry out and ache
I feel you enter my viens
I feel you pulse within their driving of blood
I feel you reach my pulse points causing them to pound
I see you pulsing within my eyeballs

I begin to pace under anticipation

I feel you crawling within my skin
Making it tingle and shiver
I feel you throughout my every limb
I feel you within each muscle

I begin to Run

I feel you within my head
Reading my every thought..becoming my every thought
I feel you massage my brain

I feel you throughout me
I feel you as me
I feel you cleanse my soul
I feel you keep my soul.this one.. i believe is about the adrenaline rush that thinking of a loved one can give you.
