I want a dagger that fits perfectly in my handI want to slide the cold hard blade of it along your warm fleshI want to slowly cut into your fleshI want to lick your fresh wound, to taste your warm bloodI want to hold my hands over the wound tightly,Letting your blood stain my pure white skin..holding onto you until the blood stopsI want to see the tears rise up in your beautiful eyesI want to witness that one salty tear escape from its placeI want to capture that tear in the palm of my handI want it to seep into my skin to become part of meI want to taste every first and last tear from your eyeson your soft, pouty lipsI want to feel youI want to be one with youto be a part of youto understand you..To Taste Youthis may not be exactly erotic.. but its something along those lines.. the 'violence' in it isnt truly violence.. it has a deeper meaning.. I dont actually want to go and slice someone up.. its about wanting to know them as deeply as you can..
