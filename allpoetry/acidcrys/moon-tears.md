Long ago when the night sky held only the moon
A young maiden wandered amongst the ancient ruins.
Her skin was a milky white,
her eyes the shade of the ocean,
and she possessed a velvet soft voice.

She sang of her sorrowful story of long lost love,
causing the moon to leave its place in the sky above.
Before the maiden the moon released a sympathetic tear,
putting it in the maidens hand where it turned into a crystal sphere.

Astounded by the beauty and the way such a thing became of existence,
she realized it was too perfect to keep, returning it with all resistance.
Touched by the maidens selflessness, the moon took its tear,
shattered it into a thousand pieces like a broken mirror,
and then threw all the little moon tears into the night sky;
