You Crawl into my skinand lick my ivory bones..tasting my sinsI should tell youYou enchant my dreamsprowl through my nightmares..possessing my thoughtsI should tell youYou glaze my tearsand deepen my laughter..filtering the pain I hideI should tell youYou Breed within my lungsand you've become my airI should tell you....I need to breathe you,         to survive.I'm not so confident about this one.. I didn't think it was complete enough or whatever.. but my friend assured me it was.. I don't know.. maybe Ill add to it later..
