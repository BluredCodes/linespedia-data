---
title: "I Give Up"
writer: "acidcrys"

Lifeless, my tongue hangs at the back of my throat.
And I gag on the bitter taste of you.

Trembling, my hands clench the brisk air.
and I strangle the scorching feel of you.

Wavering, my eyes drag to the back of my mind
and I blind the intoxicating vision of you.

Running, my feet slice through the grounds
and I get stuck in the mud of your personality.

The foul stench of its lies infumigates my nostrils
The retched material of its false love sneaks inbetween my toes
The determined gurgling slime of its ego climbs up my legs

And I sink my hands into you
staining their bravery with muck
clawing away the suffocating plastered mold
chipping away fingernails
scraping away skin
smearing my blood
as your strengthening mud
doesnt give way, and dries

And I collapse, letting your muddy personality,
your bitter taste,
your schorching feel,
and your intoxicating vision;
seep into my pores.
Claiming my soul.

I give up.ahh I hope this is as good as I think it is..
