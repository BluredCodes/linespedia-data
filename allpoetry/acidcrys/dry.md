Ashen tears collapse
melting into the moist dirt
gathering puddle

salty wind licking
moisture from warm brazen skin
oceans colliding

Darkness captures Earth
sun crashes the horizon
scattered crystal shardswell since ive been dry for ages.. i just thought id throw up some haikus i had to write.
