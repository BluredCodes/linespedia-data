---
title: "I Want You"
writer: "acidcrys"

For once, in my life.. Love me more than I love you. Feel the sighs enter your mouth,moistening your lips and,caressing your tongue every time you see me. Let your eyes drip,with the sorrow of never being understood..never knowing. Caress the air, feeling the essence,of where I recently stood; While watching me leave in the distance. See my eyes and, Feel your interior melt into a puddle of reverance at your feet. Let my voice crawl into your ears, and wrap around your thoughts; like a blanket of warmth on a cold lonely night. Let your heart escape from your chest, and fall into my hands, like mine has done-falling into yours. Let your body tremble at the thought of my body being close to yours.. of my hair softly caressing your skin.. my finger tips tracing the contour of your face.. For Once, I want you to ache.another cheapy. ah. I always feel more than the other person. Such a sin.
