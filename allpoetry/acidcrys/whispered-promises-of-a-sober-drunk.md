Whispered promises of a sober drunk
grazing my hair with his lips...
Trailing a sigh down my back...
Blinding me with surreal tranquility
as he hushes my stuttering lips
with smokey eyes that taste
my soul from a shallow chalice
cringing on the sweet bitterness
of unrecognized feelings...
The flames of  rage licking
every single drop of my soul. 

..Be with me,
you sigh..

So when torn to shreds  
I'm still here to treat you 
like the God that you're not 
and let you crawl back when
I don't satisfy your needs?

So my stomach can ache,
my eyes can shatter, 
and my heart bleed, 
when the silence creeps on 
because shes around?

All you ever want is more. 
All I want to give is more. 
(You don't think I can) 
(You think I'm just shy)
(I think you're just blind) 

I want to fall completely. 
But I dont think you'll be there to catch me.it's kind of in a story format- the first stanza setting the image, telling whats happening.. then his words.. and then my feelings about how he's treating me. I dont know.. I dont know if such a mixed format is good. But hey, its a feeling.
