when you stab that last knife into mepromise me you'll agreeto look into my eyesand watch as my soul slowly dieswatch as the last fire of my love burnsand as the last page of my story turns..don't worry, ill still be smilingwhile all my sorrows keep on pilingdon't worry ill still understandwhile you drown me in your quick sandits short.. but I still like it. another one of those painful.. stop hurting me you bastard type of poems..
