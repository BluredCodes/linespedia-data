Torturers of the Soul;burrowing the deepest Hole,Whispering the unholiest of Screams,tearing apart my life at the seams.The Mistaken emotions of the Heart;revolutionizing trust's painful Depart,Stabbing daggers through the back,in an endless eternal Attack.Whispers from a sullen forgotten past;immersing from the shadows they cast,begging for mercy on their fallen soul,pleading for the love their betrayal stole.im not sure about how to end it... i know it needs more.. but nothing about it feels empty.. im afraid ill.. put something fake into all this real emotion.,.. just so it can 'end'
