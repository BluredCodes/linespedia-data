Shadows creep into my veins,
only undying darkness remains.
Trembles drip from my fingers
as the echoing of fear lingers;
I'm falling.

Shivers licking my skin,
plague the warmth within.
Save me, hold me;
bring me...
deliverance.

His green eyes staring,
my strength is wearing.
His filthy hands yearning,
my melted insides are churning.
I'm falling.

Shivers licking my skin,
plague the warmth within.
Save me, hold me;
bring me... 
deliverance.

In beginning of his feeding,
I'm down on my knees bleeding.
He sinks into my skin so tender,
and finally, I'm forced to surrender.
