Faded ribbons falling from golden curls
Bloody busted knees stain concrete rainbows
Breaking mother's elegant string of pearls
Placing secrets inside only he knows

Lullabies softly fading into night
Cries forgotten bleed from a child's soul
Shivers beneath blankets in complete fright
Footsteps heard that never lack their control

Absent love like thorns on a budding flower
Pricked naive fingers hidden in worn gloves
Breathing toxicants that breed and devour
Leaving the burden that pushes and shoves

But Boogie Man is not under the bed,
and the nightmares aren't only in your head.well.. this is a sonnet (without iambic pentameter) that I Had to write.. and what I want to know - is what you get out of it.. I want to know if the hidden message is as obvious as I think it is. Please tell me.
