Ashes crawl up
my weak ankles.
Clothes dance about
my melting body.
Wind licks the moisture
from my brazen skin.
I am left standing.

Liquid pours into
my calloused soul.
Eyes feed upon
my racing pulse.
Tongues weave through 
my tangled hair
I am left staring.

Ignorance laces between
the grooves of my mind.
Dust breeds within
the walls of my lungs.
Words unspoken graze
upon my tender lips.
I am left sighing.

Grounds collapse swallowing
my hallow stand.
Skies shatter filling
my blind stare.
Sound dies claiming
my unheard sighs.
And I am left forgotten.When I was writing this, and rereading it, I got an image of myself, standing in the middle of a desert.. standing.. and staring at the endless horizons- staring at him. I cant see him, but he's there.  There's a much deeper explanation to all of it.. but I want everyones own view.
