I am depressed today, I am alone;
Not a knock on the door or a ring of a phone.
I give to all the shirt off my back;
It is not the attention yet its friends I lack.
I get so upset,letting myself get used;
My soul achesand my heart is bruised.
Sensitive are my felings and fragile as a flower;
When its petals are wilted all it needs is a shower.
I believe at times I try too hard;
My emotions within are truly scarred.
Then my heart begins to mend;
discovering once more you were not a friend.
As I start each day fresh and new;
I'll continue my journey for a friendship that is true.This poem is dedicated to all the users..LOL but to all my friends you know who I mean..LOL
