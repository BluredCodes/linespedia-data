M is for Michelle which is who you are;

I is for imagination yours will take you far.

C is for your compassion that you give to others

H is for the honor you give your father and mother.

E is for effort which we can plainly see;

L is for the laughter you share with your father and me

L is for the love we would never doubt

E is for the everything you are and we can never go without
