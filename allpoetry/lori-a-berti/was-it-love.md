title: "Was it Love"

She wiped a tear as she put on her glove;
Did she know the meaning of love?
Did she take the vow,for better or worse;
Anticipation for love was it a curse?
She seems happy now or could that be a mask;
Ever was she knowledgable love was such a task?
He empties his pockets and puts out his hand;
Did she know his love carried such a financial demand?
For richer or poorer in sickness and in health..
He said I DO clearly imagining her wealth.
Material items, do as she must.
She realizes now that is was lust...
