Dear President & First lady ,our country is blessed; 
However the recent tragedies are what we detest. 

It was a clear and beautiful day. 
Then within minutes,we felt dismay.
 
Planes crashed and lives were lost; 
Realizing now what terrorism had cost. 

Our hearts broke ,tears filled our loving eyes; 
Stars and stripes displayed, for patriotism has no disguise.
 
You uplifted our spirits by Gods grace, 
We are standing proud by your embrace.
 
More terror occurs throughout our mail; 
Again you remind us WE WILL PREVAIL.

 Troops leaving families to a mid eastern land;
Tears fall like raindrops upon a foreign sand.

We all take different roads;some walk and some may run; 
Knowing each road will eventually lead to one.


You have been here for us during each devistation; 
UNITED TOGETHER makes us a strong ,yet caring nation..I lost my brother on 9-9-01  2 days before the 911 tragedy..I dedicate this poem to him  and everyone who is affected by the current problems ...GOD BLESS
