---
title: "Unworthy"
writer: "Sparkler Bree"
slug: "unworthy"
source: "AllPoetry"

Unworthy to get up each morning, 
Unworthy to sleep each night.
Unworthy to go outdoors
And sit in the sun's warm light.

Unworthy of the air I breathe, 
Unworthy of earth's nourishment.
Unworthy to try and ask you 
For any kind of encouragement.

Unworthy of this friendship,
Unworthy of your trust.
I don't want you to hate me, 
But I know by now you must.

Unworthy of the love you give, 
Unworthy of another human being.
One that loves me so much
But can't see what I am seeing.

Unworthy of being human myself, 
Unworthy of it all.
Trying so hard to make it here,
But only running into walls.

Unworthy of any love
Unworthy even to cry,
Unworthy of this life, 
And yet unworthy to die.
