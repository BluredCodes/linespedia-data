title: "All I Seem to Find"
writer: "Sparkler Bree"

I'm running, but I can't keep up.
I fall, only to get more behind.
I get up, and try to catch the others, 
But failure is all I seem to find.

I'm crying, but no one looks at me.
I try to forget, but it's always in my mind.
I bleed to make the pain stop, 
But hurt is all I seem to find.

I'm screaming, but no one hears me.
I'm trying, but people are not kind.
I just want to live and be happy,
But suffering is all I seem to find.
