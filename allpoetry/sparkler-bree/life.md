Life is pretty funny sometimes.
Just when you think it can't get any worse, 
Something unexpected happens,
To lift a seemingly unending curse.

Then other times, the exact opposite happens.
Everything is normal and going kinda cool,
Outta nowhere you look up, 
And find that you've been made the fool.

For me it seems as if someone somewhere 
Is laughing at me all the time.
It seems I always receive the punishment 
For which there was no crime.

But all this shit is kinda funny,
And for me it has become one BIG pile,
But through it all I look back on it
And I am forced to smile.More irony, less pain in this one.
