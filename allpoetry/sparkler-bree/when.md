title: "When?"
writer: "Sparkler Bree"

So when do you care about me?
When it's convienient to you.
I don't matter really,
I'm just there when you have nothing better to do.

If I sit in a room with you 
And just sorta be quiet for a while,
You'll forget me completey 
You won't go any extra mile.

I need a lot of attention
That's just who I am.
It doesn't make me a bad person,
I'm trying as hard as I can.

I can't deny my feelings
Just because to you they're inconvienient.
I'm sorry that I feel this way
But you need to be more lenient.

I cannot lie to myself
Nor can I lie to you.
I can't see that you care sometimes,
But I still know your love is true.
