title: "Haiku for you"
writer: "Sparkler Bree"

I thought I was safe
No more biting in the ass
I guess I was wrong

The past behind us
Looking on to our future
But the pain comes back

Your words hurt so much
A dagger pierces my heart
Dying slowly now
