All I want is to be left alone.
Get out of this room, 
Go find someplace else to make all that fucking noise.

You people annoy me, 
Your presense is eating away at me.
I just want to be alone.

Peace and quiet, that's all I want.
Why can't you people see that?
Go away and leave me alone.

I leave, and you follow me!
How dare you! No matter where I go, 
You just won't leave me alone.

I realize, no matter where I go, 
Whoever surrounds me, friends or not, 
I am always alone.
