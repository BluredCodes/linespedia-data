---
title: "Here we go again"
writer: "Jinaki"
slug: "here-we-go-again"

See, I only come around you sometimes
 N' when those times come around
 I always find you tryin' to pick at my mind
 Our friendly talks turn into verbal storms
 Soon as I hit you with the thought
 You wanna' get up n' walk out the door
 N' what's that for?
 You rather turn away n' be mad
 If you turn away for too long
 You'll miss what you had
 N' I'm gone
 Ain't no reason for me feeling this way
 For as long
 As I can remember the foul shit that you say
 But, it's cool cause I can get on with or without you
 N' in this case I'd rather move on 
 Cause that's what I'm bout' to do...
 Oh no, don't grab my hand now 
 N' ask me to stay
 I'mma' grab my shit 
 N' walk out 
 N' get on with my day
