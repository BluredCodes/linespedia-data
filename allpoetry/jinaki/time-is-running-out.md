---
title: "Time is running out"
writer: "Jinaki"
slug: "time-is-running-out"

Time is runnin' out 
 So you better put your card in
 Tryin' to do without
 So now, you barely startin'
 No food in ya' belly 
 N' no clothes on ya' back
 Ain't even got no money
 So that is what you lack
 No need to stress now 
 N' start cryin' like a child
 Get up on ya' feet 
 So you can have a way to eat
 Place ya' troubles above 
 N' in return you'll get love
 Which'll give you a shove
 N' make ya' mentals get tough
 If you had things your way
 You'd have never took this route
 But keep ya' head up
 Cause ya' time is runnin' out...
