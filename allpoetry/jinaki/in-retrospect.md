title: "In retrospect"

If I could only go back
  To bein' just that lil' girl
   Wearin' baby doll dresses
    With pigtails n' curls
     Havin' no worries 
      Or concerns at all
       Not even a thought
        Only those about playin' jump rope n' hopscotch
  
 If I could only go back
  To bein' that teenaged jewel
   Only concerned with spendin' money
    And finishin' high school
     Decidin' what I wanna' do n' where I wanna' be
      Where I wann' go n' what I wanna' see
 
 If I could only go back  
  To where everything shifted
   I would relive it all
    And do some things different
     But since I can't go back
      I will enjoy right now
       N' live for each moment
        To make life better somehow
         I will do away with the bad things
          N' make the good ones come back at me
           Only I can live my life
            N' after all...I should be happy
