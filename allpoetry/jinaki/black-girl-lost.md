---
title: "Black Girl Lost"
writer: "Jinaki"
slug: "black-girl-lost"

Sistah, sistah, sisah
 Why must you speak that way?
 "Nigga" this n' "nigga" that
 Girl, where ya' home trainin' at?
 Is it all that bad?
 For you to flaunt it like a fad?
 
 Sistah, sistah, sistah
 You causin' the wrong kind of attention
 N' quiet as kept...
 I just thought I should mention
 You'll soon put yourself in a situation
 That you won't enjoy facin'
 
 Sistah, sistah, sistah
 You just as beautiful as can be
 But, the way you carry ya'self
 Them brothas' just can't see
 I see ya' laughin' now
    But you'll be cryin' later
       Holdin' ya' heart
          Grippin' ya knees
 N'first time for a prayer

 Sistah, sistah, sistah
 You such a sad sight to see
 I done seen too many of my sistahs suffer...
 Believe me
 A "nigga" will only take advantage of your glory
 But a "man" will only purify
 Be the lady he'll see today
 N' maybe the queen he'll take home tomorrow
 I mean...
 Why show ya' ass 
     before showin' some class
          N' actin' way fass?
             Then bein' called trash
 Sistah, 
   my sistah, 
      my beautiful black sistah
 Keep ya' head up
 Eyes open
 N' legz closed
 Your gift is but a token
 N' your heart should be what's chosen
 Like the essence of love potion
 Oh...my sistah, sistah, sistah
 Be yourself
 The Black Queen that you are
