...Space Invasion

 Can I get in ya' space 
     For a moment
 Can I move from 
         place to place
                 Spot to spot
 While I'm zonin'
 Can I speak of spoken' words to a beat
 While I'm flowin'
 Give it to ya' like a toke while at ease
                     That's mind blowin'
 I'll keep it goin'...
        I feel just fine
 Can I sit n' sip some red wine?
 It's too bad ya' couldn't catch me in time
 I raise up n' I make my move quick
 Don't wanna' sit too long
       Gotta' run n' make a switch
 But they say I'm too busy 
        N' I should make my time last
                 Constantly on the move
                         Goin' nowhere fast
 But they don't know how I move!
         Gotta' make my pockets fatter
 Gotta' do what I choose
         Cause the world is gettin' sadder
 Lazzines-I refuse!
         Cause it's money that I'm after
 Know what I gotta' do
         So it doesn't even matter
 N' it's my time to take
 So I better hop up on it
 As I get in ya' space 
      N' burn ya' ears 
           Just for a moment
