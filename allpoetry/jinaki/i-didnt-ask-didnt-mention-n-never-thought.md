I didn't ask for you to come and be my company
 Just so you can enjoy air of silence with me
 I didn't ask for you to enlighten me with...
 your sultry words
 N' I don't recall askin' you to become...
 Comfortable
 Within' my comfort zone
 
 I didn't mention anything about my ideal man
 Nor did I mention anything about recruiting another friend
 I damn sho' never mentioned love
 No sex
 Not even a hug
 I never mentioned time
 Or any special activities of mine
 I never mentiioned a kiss
 A touch of the brow
 Or a touch of your lips
 
 I never thought of anyone like you
 Never even thought of being with someone like you
 Never thought you could like me or even grow to love me
 Never thought of such a treasure that I could share with you
 Forever
 Never thought I'd hear you say
 "I love you"
 In such a way
 That would give me insight...
 Especially today
 So because I didn't ask, 
                 didn't mention 
                    and never thought of such things a man would do...
 You did them all anyway
 And for this,is why I love you
