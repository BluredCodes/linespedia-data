Cash Flow
 
 You know, I get tired of goin' without
            Doin' without...
 Times like this, all I can do is get mad
 Mad because I'm broke-
 No!
 Temporarily outta' cash
 Most times I just sit
 Sit n' try to put myself at ease
        because I know why my wallet
                  n 'pockets are empty
 Yeah, all things are difficult
              before they are easy
 But I can never seem to pay that one person...
 Me
 Treat ya'self 
 Before you beat ya'self
 Is what I say
 Rememberin' that even the "well-to-do"
            n' wealthiest folks in the world
 have debts too
 Nothing is that important
 Except to just...breath
 So see, 
 A job is a job
 Unless it's your career...
 Otherwise
 A job is only your cash flow
 Just until you get there
