title: "Us"
writer: "Jinaki"

We meet on occassions...
 When time permitts
 Enjoying one another's company
 Enticed by our convo' tidbitts
 Exploringly...wanting to continue this on into the mornin'
 Months pass n' pass
 N'I grow to adore you 
 Just as fast
 Never met anyone like you in my past
 But the more time I spend with you
 I wish I had
 So...
 If you don't mind 
 I'd like to shine with you 
 To pursue more of your time
 It's been a while for me
 I don't know bout' you
 But I'm happily intrigued
 So...I'm askin' you to be with me
 I'm not askin' for much
 Just for "me n' you"
 To become "us"
 N' I truly don't know how you feel bout' this
 I just hope you feel how much I appreciate you...
 Every single time we kiss
