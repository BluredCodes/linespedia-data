title: "Angel In Disguise"

Slowly
   Surely
 I am calm and serene
 Next to...pure entity
   Black beach sand to
    Majestic sea shells
 As the wind brushes my body in such detail
 As I walk on scene-
 I see all eyes on me
        Right
      to
 Left
 I'll let the essence take over my attention
    The coolness dance on my skin
       N' the music makes love to my ears
 Smellin' the aroma of smoke
 I quickly adjust my attention
 As I notice a brotha' lookin' my way...
 In his position to wanna' holla'
 I'll let curiosity be all he can swallow
 I continue to sit in my zone
       With my own
         Quite divinely
 I see he's still watchin' me
 As I sip my comacauzy
 I smell somethin' nice
 N' a deep voice says...
 "I knew you were n' angel
    but, where are your wings?"
 So I said...
 "I sprout them only when I'm tickled
     so they're not for you to see."
 
 "Shall I challenge your mind 
    To help you relax
      Or perform a nice foot rub
        Or maybe one to your back?
  Perhaps a candlelight dinner with wine for two
     Then maybe I can tickle
        N' salute you too.
  For the most part Miss lady...
     I wanna' be yours."
 I stand gracefully with a smile 
 As he licks his fulluptuous lips
    "I apologize black man, 
        But I'm already spoken for."
 N' I walked away leavin' my last words
 At his fingertips...
