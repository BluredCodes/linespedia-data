---
title: "Grudge"
writer: "Jinaki"

Walkin' around with a grudge
 Sportin' a full costume of an ATTITUDE
 Being mad
   Seein' mad
      Stayin' mad
 But never glad
 Not even to wish for what you had
 Hmmm, how sad
 To be angry all the time
       Frownin' all the time
             Mopin' all the time
 Snappin' at any n' everyone
 Carryin' evil on ya' shoulders that way a ton
 I certainly don't want none
 The devil won't keep me busy
 Damn, I reckon it's better you...than me
 Don't anything make you smile?
 Make you jump up for joy
 Like a lil' girl or boy
 Or even happy that you've been livin' for a while
 What you so angry for?
       Someone steal your car?
             Beat down your door?
                Or make you get on your hands 
                   n' knees with ya' head to the floor?
 Puh-lease....
 It can't be that bad
 For you to stay constantly mad
 I ain't sorry
 But, I do apologize
 And I don't mean to categorize
 But you need to open your eyes
 There's people alot worse off than you
 Some have no home
      Many have no food
         Alot have no clothes n' shoes too
 But you...
 You have the nerve to be bitter!
 N' the blessings you recieve
 You obviously don't consider
 Cause' if you did
 You wouldn't be that way
 Until you choose to change your ATTITUDE
 That's the way you'll stay
 So if you choose to remain ungrateful
 N' show no love
 Your demeanor will always be hateful
 While you walkin' 'round holdin' that grudge
