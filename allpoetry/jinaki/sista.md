Sista'

 It was one of them days
      A sista' knows 
          Just one of them days
 A sista' goes thru a thing or two
       In all kinds of ways
            It amazes me
                N' keeps me in a daze
 All the troubles of my sistas 
 At times we just go crazy!
 
 Sista'sista'
 Why hold yo' knees
 N' cover yo' eyes
 Try to keep peace
 N' still you cry
 Beg God "Please!"
 For all them times...
 You tried 
 To keep the devil from ya' side
 But yet he gets the betta' taste of you
 N' blows ya' mind

 Sista'sista'
 You try to keep it goin'
 Try to steer away from poisonous fakes
 That got you 
            open
 It's too easy, it's ya' mind that they after
 Wanna' take away n' play with ya' soul
 N' leave ya' battered 
            n' shattered so
 Now ya' caught up in the rapture
 With nowhere to go
        so ya' do it cause ya' have to
 Degradin' ya'self
 Spreadin' ya'self
 Ain't gettin' no help
 Sista' girl, ya' gotta' take betta' care of ya' health
 Raise up girl you too young to be so needy
 These kats gotcha' floatin' for them because they greedy
 "Hey pretty" 
 They say you got what it takes
 Wanna' tell ya' just how easy it is 
 So you can get paid
     The thangs...
           that they dangle in ya' face
                 Maybe shinin' in ya' eyes
                     But it's peircin' ya' grace
 Such a shame
 Ain't no love in da' city
 Them kats cut n' left you alone
 Cryin'
 "Please, don't leave me!!"

 
 Sistah girl
 I understand you tryin' to make it
   Always told how to get what you want
     is if you take it
 Gonna' make it thru
   N' do what you do
     Even if you fake it
       Like let that dude run all up in you
         Cause he ain't gon' say shit
 Girl, ya' takin' away from ya' life
 N' its just begun
 I tried to tell ya' thangs gon' be alright
 But I see it wasn't enough
 If ya' close ya' eyes
   N' imagine things much greater than this
     I can guruantee 
       You'll want much more than this life'll give
 You can start again
                  N' 
                    again
 Till ya' whole life 
                 starts 
                   to unfold
   You can give right back
     But can't take away the things when ya' lost your control
 Sistah girl, 
    You takin' a chance 
      Ain't got a whole lot of time
        Be decisive in what cha' do
          You can always change ya' mind
 Live ya' life while you still can
   Leave that other shit behind
     No need to rush back to that world 
       N' live ya' life in rewind
 Just take a minute n' think
 You can fight it n' win
 Next time he come around you knockin' 
 Don't let his ass in!
