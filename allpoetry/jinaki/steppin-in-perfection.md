title: "Steppin' in Perfection"

She's a thorough bread
 Top notch sista'
 Yeah, she got it goin' on too
 Her eyes don't give in to sour sights
 She's n' indelible,
                Silky,
                     Brown Brown chile'
 She's that woman you never thought you could talk to
     Oh, she'll talk back
           With a conversation a lil' abstract 
                          That'll have you relaxed
 She's that woman
          That seraph with wings 
                that can't be seen
 N' yet she carries them so gracefully
 So, they all wanna' know
         But dare not ask
                    How's she steppin' in perfection
                          When she comes n' goes?...
 Ms. Silky,
         Brown Brown
 She's only seen when she comes around
 When night falls n' her essence spread
 All will wonder how she did it again
 Ms. Brown Brown,
       A sista' that nobody really knows
 She's insidious,
              elated
 Oh, you'll feel her glow
         if you get too close
 Yet, they all wanna' know
             But dare not ask
                   How's she steppin' in perfection
                                when she comes n' goes?...
                       How's she steppin' in perfection
                                   when she comes n' goes?...
                            How's she steppin'
                                    in perfection
                                        when she comes n' goes?...
