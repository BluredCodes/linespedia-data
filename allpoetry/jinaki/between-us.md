---
title: "Between us"
writer: "Jinaki"
slug: "between-us"

He came with no expectations
 No plans, no concerns
 All was too real to convey
 All was but too cool to turnaway
 No adjustments made
 Only conversation at trade
 His company was all I enjoyed.
 Always chillin', layed out
 Shoot! He's my homeboy!
 Nothin' unlikely
 Not even there enough to care
 Till somethin' once said
 Somethin' he once shared
 Around in my dome
 His words came to be
 Words I've never even known
 Erotically...about me
 So...what's a girl to do?
 Another moment spent
 And my level of thinking subdued
 Knowing his life and knowing his past
 I allowed my ears to hear what he wanted to ask
 In such a way...he made me blush
 Flattered to the bone
 I must say, I was flushed
 Suppose I cave 
 N' give him that look
 Will he behave?
 Or will he be shook?
 Gave in a lil' is all I did
 Which led to more
 But that was it
 We're still two cool
 N' don't talk about it much
 It's kept to ourselves
 Because it's just between us
