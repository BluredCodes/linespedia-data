Brotha', brotha', brotha'
 Ain't you tired of standin' out on that corner?
 Hustlin' 9-5
 Choosin' the wrong way to survive
 Yet, duckin' n' dodgin' bullets tryin' to stay alive
 I know you got alot mo' gumption than that
 Neet to get up
        N' get out 
            n' get somethin' betta' than that
 Instead of helpin' our people stay off track
 N' always on that prozaic

 Brotha', brotha', brotha' 
 Do you realize that you poisonin' ya' own mind
 Supplyin' others
 With fantasies that smother
 But at the same time leaves you behind
 I see ya' tryin' to get ya' hands on dirty money
 In a quick fast n' a hurry
 But, listen to this...
 Man keeps ya' down
 Or puts you in da' cage
 Oh, but don't clown
 Cause that's when you'll see his devilish rage
 You stuck in da' game n' 
 Of course you can't see
 Hear 
 Or feel the pain
 That you puttin' before me
 Days be gettin' late
 With you still on a paper chase
 But you mentally tryin' to erase the bad
 To put money in its place
 For what you too scared to face
 
 Brotha', brotha', brotha'
 Why disrespect your sistas in the process
 Tryin' to discuss ]
 But ain't tryin' to hear no fuss
 N' steady tryin' to get some love n' trust
 Black...you ain't nothin' but a hustler
 Hustle ya' way in
 N' hustle ya' way out
 Really, you don't know what livin's about
 So, until the man above pulls ya' card
 Ya' gonna' always have it hard]
 Strugglin'
     Bubblin'
        Steady doin' nothin'
 Brotha', that thunda'll drive you six feet under
 Then you'd have just died
 A hustlin' hustler, hustler
