The hall was lit
with boughs of green
and pinpoint lights
like stars
shone in the semi-dark room
A flute and recorders 
played an English madrigal
Singing the quiet Silent Night
Listening to the children's
storyteller read the story of 
The Three Wise Women-
a touching story of three
women,differing stations 
in life
came to see the New Born King
and they kissed Him
and ministered to  Mary...
We lit one another's candles passing the LIGHT,
Sipping mulled cider afterwards, 
I felt a Peace
I came home and promptly 
fell into a deep sleep
Silent Night...
