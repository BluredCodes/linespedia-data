title: "I'm Glad You're There"

When Ithink of youI tremble like a leafSomething about youdrives me crazyI know I do the samefor youTwo people who lovebut only I can say'I love you'in wordsI've learned to love youfor your actionsI'm really glad you're therefor no-one elsehas dared to love me like you doThe white-pink  roses I send youtouch your heartWe make sweet music through cyberspaceBut I'm glad you're there...
