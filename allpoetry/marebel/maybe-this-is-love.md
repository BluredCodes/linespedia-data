My love for  youhas changedever since New York'sbombingI felt personally violatedso I have to love youfrom another stance'Tis growthfor our former love was a fantasynow I'm on my ownand 'stepping out with my baby'Me!I still love you from afar Passion has run dryNow we have a deeper lovebut not so high...Maybe this is Love, huh?Maybe...
