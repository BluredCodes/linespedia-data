---
title: "The Storm and The Calm"
writer: "marebel"
slug: "the-storm-and-the-calm"
source: "AllPoetry"

Twinkling fairie lights
of Christmas glint though the trees
I am in a surreal December
walking yet not.
I sit with her at 
an opera medley
and enjoy the
sopranos and baritones
singing lovesongs
from La Traviata
and music by Beethoven-
a Serenading Dance of Swans,
young swans in grey and gold
en pointe...
Suddenly she slumps backward
and loses consciousness
swirling world
usher-nurse,doctor in the house
Calmly I did what  I was told
but the moment was lost
Only Hope that she would revive
orange juice poured down her throat
Color back in her face
I fell flat on my face 
the next day
Peace in the midst of a storm...
What next?
