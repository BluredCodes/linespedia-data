The first snowfall
fell in mid-December
a surprise
like white icing piped
onto contrasting , brown trees
big fluffs of white cotton candy
sat on low bushes
Winter birds
fluttered, flickered
from branch to branch
slip-sliding away
some lone red berries
sat quietly at the edges
of a few trees
blood red drops
a reminder
of Christmas holly berries
The First Snow Fall
by the Master Craftswoman...
