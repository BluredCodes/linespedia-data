Heard a  screeching callone I'd never heard beforeA bluejay visited my balcony -so beautiful was shestriking black markingsagainst snowy white onesthen patches of shadow bluepainted here and there,What a palette!She came often andpecked the dried flower pot soillooking for foodAlas there was noneallgone, allgone!She came time and time againand her majestic beauty enthralled meHer tufty crest like a queenin residenceWonders of the Creatorthat make formomentary happiness...There she goes!
