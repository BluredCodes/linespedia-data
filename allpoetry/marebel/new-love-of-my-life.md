The new love of 
my life
is a tiny cinnamon blue
parakeet
Four months with me,but twelve weeks or so
she's just a fledgling
and somethin' special
She is definitely Barnum and Bailey material
as she jumps into hoops and 
does cartwheels in the cage's wall
 lots of delicious treats are hers for the asking
and even though traumatized 
by condo living
she affectionately bites my finger
or kisses it
Still leery of stepping on my finger
she twinkles across her perches
Loves music especially New Age nature sounds 
classical Tchaichovsky
and me staying home to play and talk to her
She does this and much more
Twinkles-
the new love of my Life...
