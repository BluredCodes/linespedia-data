---
title: "I Wonder About Love..."

Love is a full-blown red rose
floating in a sky-blue meditation bowl

Love is a word bandied 
about so much that its lost
its sweet essence

Love is when your pet parakeet
sings you to sleep and awakens you in song

Love is uncertainty
yet knowing you are LOVED

So what is Love?
Ask me another question...

Dedicated to Valentines I knew
and to St. Valentine for his love...
