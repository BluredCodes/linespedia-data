My love for you
is a quiet one
where once it was turbulent
Sometimes quietly intense
Sometimes gentle touches
I never know exactly what you
think
but I know you care
In gentle pillow clouds we could be
We used to be in storms
but now we didn't need storms...
My Love, unique and wanting love
wanting to give love
Squeezable, huggable
My Love and Yours
Together in Spirit...
