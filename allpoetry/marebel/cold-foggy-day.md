title: "Cold, Foggy Day"

Cold, foggy day in our city
the poor huddle in doorways
waiting for the soup kitchens
to open
Faint Christmas carols
heard in the distance
warm, chicken soup
fills the body for a time
but not the soul
Do they wonder about their
mortality 
or do they just lay down and fall
into an eternal sleep?
Wait, the little ,old man dies
with a smile upon his face...
Cold, foggy day in our city
and nameless faces go by...
