---
title: "What is Love?"
writer: "marebel"
slug: "what-is-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Love is hollow

Love is passionate

Love is lust

Love is madness

Love is high

Love is unreal

Love is thoughtful

Love is understanding

Unreal love stinks

Ultimate love

is sublime

peaceful

quiet and gentle

So what is Love?
