---
title: "Love's Lost?"
writer: "marebel"
slug: "loves-lost"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

And now I see
his face gently plastered with make-up
he was once the love of my life
kind, loving, sensual, erotic
Now he lay in a casket
My arms around his wife and children
we sobbed for his earthly self was gone...
sweet smelling red and yellow roses wafted in the air
and mourners quietly gathered in huddles
His spirit soared up high like an eagle
Now he was on top of the world
in a golden forever sunlight
angel-like, he would pray for us all
on our earthly journey
He was happy in eternal rest
youthful man of joy ,playfulness and joy...
Goodbye for now...my friend!
