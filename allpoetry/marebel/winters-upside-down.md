What do you make of it?
Not a speck of snow to be seen

It seems the very skies breasts
have dried up
and have no
"milk " for us to receive...

Dry, brown grass
dead winter trees
muddy puddles
here and there
Winter resorts
have had to close up...

Now some long for the 
fluffy white stuff 
they sing hurray
it's time 
to play in the snow
So hurry up and come on down
we want to ski and ride in the snow...
