Cold rainWet rainyellow-beige leaves in mud puddlesearly to churchlit fire in a chalicelighting red candlessignifying our joys and sorrowspets being blessedguinea-pigs, big barking dogsfluffy cats and a rubber snake!children smilingpeople gigglingat the paradea lonely flute played an autumnal melody...labyrinthine ideologies beautiful Sunday service on a rainy dayin fall...
