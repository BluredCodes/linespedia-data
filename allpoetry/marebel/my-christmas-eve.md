Time passes
time passed so quickly
already 'twas another year
into the 21st century
My mother has lived
75 years
Numbers
What did they mean?
She'd had a bad year
falls, blackouts
lucidity, vacant stares
energy burnout, no appetite
The War at home had shocked her
silence
blankness
a fragile child
we were her mothers
suffering all her life
this had finally told on her
acceptance on our part?
Time was slipping away
Quickly now...
What will happen?
Prayer, a soft touch, helping hands
We'll take it Day by Day,
Yes Day by day...
