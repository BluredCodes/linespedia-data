I Know You by Your Poetry

I know you by your poetry
The soft and silken phrase
It touches every part of me
In most enchanting ways

It lifts from off the written page
And settles in my ear
Abandoning all time and age
In phrases soft and dear

You fill my chalice with life’s wine
I savor every sip
It leaves a taste of lyric chime
To tingle on my lip

For never did such succulence 
Exude from lyric word
Never has a sweeter song been sung
By beauteous bird

Never did such dulcet tunes
Dance on the morning dew
Dear poet, you have touched my heart,
And I’m in love with you
