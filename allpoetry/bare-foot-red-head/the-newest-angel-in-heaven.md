The Newest Angel in Heaven

                               The newest Angel in Heaven
                               Salina is her name 
                               She touched your lives so briefly ,
                               it'll never be the same 

                               God needed a Special Angel
                               No one else could fill her shoes
                               So he opened up the Heavens
                               and called Salina's name 
                               You see , She was So Special
                               that not just anyone else would do .

                               I know it is hard for you
                               loosing someone So Dear 
                               When you feel a need for Her
                               look to the Heavens Up Above 
                               And Remember all the times you had with Her
                               Your Sweet Little Love 
                               And She will Always Be Near 

                               Remember Her as you may
                               because time will come for you
                               to See Her again Another Day 

                               She has gone Up to Heaven
                               Steve is by Her side ,
                               holding Her hands and
                               walking with Her ,
                               In The Promised Land 

                               She will never have to know the pain
                               that Fills this Whole Wide World
                               Because Now She Walks with God ,
                               as She is His Little Golden Girl .

                               A Precious Angel - all the same
                               and Salina is her name . 
                               Time alone will Heal Your Pain
                               and when you see Her again in Heaven
                               You will Understand how much
                               God wanted to take Special Care of
                               Your Precious Little One 

                               There is No Pain or Sorrow
                               in The Promised Land
                               And Your Special Little One 
                               will be there in wait 

                               For when Your time comes
                               You Will See Her ,
                               It's All in His Big Plan 
                               Only then will You see
                               Her Smiling Eyes again 

                               No Pain or Suffering for Her to Endure
                               Nothing like that in Heaven
                               For This I am So Sure 

                               When it comes Your Time 
                               to Walk thru Those Pearly Gates
                               On the other side You'll see
                               Steve & Salina Hand in Hand
                               because for You They had to wait .

                               You see God needed Her so Badly
                               So He called Her Up to Heaven
                               She is His Precious Love ,
                               This Precious Little Lady 

                               There is No Pain in Heaven ,
                               Only Happiness and Joy 
                               Someone Up There to Care for Her
                               and He is Steve , your Brother
                               He needed Someone to Care for
                               and with Him to wait ,
                               Until Your time to Walk Up to
                               Those Beautiful Pearly Gates 

                               The Newest Angel in Heaven
                               Salina is Her name 
                               I Wish there were something I could do
                               to Help to Ease Your Pain 
                               Since there's no way for me to do this ,
                               This is what I Know 

                               She is in a Joyful Place
                               with No Pain or Sorrow 
                               So She will be Waiting on You
                               when Your time is here
                               And again You will get to be
                               With Your Precious Little Dear .

                               There is Nothing I can do
                               to Help to Heal Your Broken Heart ,
                               Except to Remind You that
                               of which She is a Part ..

                               Lots of Love & Sympathy
                               from Me to You .
                               So when Your Sad & Lonely
                               Remember , She's waiting on You ,
                               Never to Feel any Pain 
                               Waiting on You in Heaven
                               So You Can See Her Smile again ...

                               by : Kay Modlin  06/28/2000
                               Bare Foot Red Head
