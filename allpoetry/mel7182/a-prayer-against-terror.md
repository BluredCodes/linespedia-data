title: "A Prayer Against Terror..."

On a day full of fear,We pray for all.For all of those who were lost,We remember and shed a tear.We hope for and seek for guidance.And with all the commotion,there still no sign of subsidence.To our children we try to explain,but how can we tell of someone who had so much hate?And to share how now we all feel so much pain,and why we were all given this fate?How do we teach them now who's right or wrong,that when those people killed, we wanted to kill back?We though must stress to stay strong,and to show love, what this terrorist lacked.
