Some days go by with out a traceand occasionally ones that never endbut there are those special days like when you meet a new friendThe affable gestures you gave mewill somehow always bein countless waysa symbol of what you mean to meWe haven't had much time to spendI'll be sure to praythat somehow we will be alwayseternal friends.
