You are still my dad even though
we've only seen each other to and fro
you are more than a dad indeed
. . .but a friend
especially when in each others need
true that we've spent years apart
but we've known that we're still there
. . .deep in our hearts
there hasn't been a night
when I haven't prayed for your well keeping
you are in every way a part of me,
. . .even when I'm speaking
to me I thought this would be
an easier way to say how I feel about my dad
for my love for you,
. . .will always be true
you've helped me in so many ways
some old some new
for these are only naming a few
. . .I miss you
and I hope to see you soon
we should have long before
because you are my dad
