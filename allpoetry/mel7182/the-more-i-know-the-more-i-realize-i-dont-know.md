---
title: "The More I know, The More I Realize I Don't Know"
writer: "mel7182"

When I feel I'm to the highest height, I can stand tall
Somehow within I still manage to fall
But once something happens again it all seems worth the pain
Then it crumbles again; almost too much to sustain
The feeling I get; nothing can be matched
and how I feel now; I think I was too attached
The feeling I get is deeper than the sea
and exceeds anything I'd ever want to be
It warms me more than the heat of the sun
The last thing I want is it to end; to be done
At times it almost seemed to good to be true
But why then do I feel the way I do?
To spend eternity without being able to feel that wasy again
May not be worth it, not even the extra time to spend
With all of my strength, every ounce of my soul
with every beat of my heart, after that I still don't know
