title: "Life's Forward. . . ."

As human beings brought forth 
in such a divine and heavenly manner, 
we have been blessed with many gifts, 
each man with his or her own purpose.  
A certain purpose for which one yearns for, 
an ache unattainable 
for that one who often searches blindly, 
always asking 'why' or 'why not', 
lacking the faith and passion it takes 
to accomplish such a feat, 
as to actually overcome and know thyself.  
Is it so hard to believe that 
reason is all the reason in itself?  
Each and every element of 
our existence is plotted and planned, 
simplified that all will show reason 
and meaning by means of fated time.  
Love for instance, a virtue, 
a character of such quality 
that is so contagious but yet 
we live in a world where hatred and poverty exists.  
Love is in every essence, 
an inspiration, a conduct of such Majesty, 
it could be ones savior or tis ones doom.
Love can be the brightest light, 
but it is always found in the darkest depths, 
for you have to have dark for light to thrive in.
