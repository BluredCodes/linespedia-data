title: "Only You. . ."

There's only been written few,the topic of matter--gave by you.The fun, laughs, the light,the color, the cheer, and all that is bright.Each and every heeded smile,all the things that make life worthwhile--I am greatful for it all.So much that I hope I never fall.It wouldn't of been there--but you opened your heart and shared.I'm glad it happened that way,and this is just a single verse to help me say,for I'm sure much more could be told,but 'sooner or later' it'll all unfold,that each second--every minute,is that much better, just because you're in it.
