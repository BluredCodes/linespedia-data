---
title: "You Are. . ."
writer: "mel7182"

I fall in love with you again, each day,
you've given me so much, in so many ways.
Our love has no restraints, no limit of time.
You've given me love, I thought I'd never find.

I couldn't describe how truely wonderful you are,
you, the best thing that has happened to me , by far.
Each day we grow stronger, together in love.
This year has been the best, we've been blessed from above.

The greatest thing is we still have a lifetime to share,
and I know through everything, you'll always be there.
I wish I could give you everything, then even more.
Without you my life wouldn't mean as much, just as before.

I just wanted to tell you again how much I love you.
You've tought me love and life, things before I never knew.
I am so proud of who you are and what you do,
you are everything to me and even more, too.
