You are always there for me to cry on,your the one who pushes me along.When I would go the wrong way, or didn't listen and played,you still loved me because you're my mommy.When I was asleep,you didn't make a peep.You were there If I had a nightmare,and comforted me, because your my mommy.I know I did not always tell the truth, or wouldn't sit still in a restaurant booth,and maybe at times I was bad, and you got angry,it mad me sad to know I hurt my mommy.I am very greatful for you,and love you through and through,even though at times it was hard to see,I loved you because your you--my mommy.
