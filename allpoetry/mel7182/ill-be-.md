---
title: "I'll Be--"
writer: "mel7182"
slug: "ill-be-"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/2199-Ill-Be---by-mel7182"
---

I hope in some way,
this will brighten your day.
To know I'll be you friend,
Even at times when your at your end.
I'll be there for you,
as a friend that'll be true.
I'll be behind you to stand,
and try to meet your every demand.
Just so you know,
even if at times it doesn't show,
through time and time again,
I'll do my best to be your friend.
