title: "A Given Chance. . ."

A found reason to love,one handled dearly from above,as how I trust to 'see'.This blessed before me,a love itself I love--deep,one I hope to always keep.It has sequentially proven to be true,something never before I knew.Feelings I get in my heart,the ache when we're apart,remarkable sensations when you are near,a love almost too good to be true...I fear.This love I will treasure like gold,I hope to show in all days, new and old.And when you love me back, it is rich too indeed,just to be there whenever your in need,is much more than any given right,but something I will cherish with all of my might.an opportunity to love you, a given fate,one I thought I was not worthy enough--to forever wait.
