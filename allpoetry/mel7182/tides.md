They come in a patternnot always easy to predicteach one unique in its own waythey have different levels everydayyou never know what the next one will bringlots of aquatic treasures may be seenfor you have to keep your eyes opento be aware not to only the sightsbut to the sensual soundsas one fades after an amount of timeanother one begins in a never ending sequence
