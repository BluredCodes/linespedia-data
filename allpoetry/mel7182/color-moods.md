As the days progress,so do my views--visions.Blue for the soothing days.Yellow for the cheerful times,the ones I am greatful for.white best describes the situations,when I realize what's been given.Red for the times full of furry--green to contradict, forgive and forget.All of the colors--moods are there for a reason.Each completed in its own important balance.Combined together they complete me--that is in describing how I handle life.Two-Tone Poem
