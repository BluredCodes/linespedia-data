In life so much is taken for granted.
In doing so we don't realize how much we miss.
So many missed magical moments--

It's a shame to miss all that beauty.
What if we could all, even for just one moment,
sit and just absorb all of God's given beauty.
Have you ever just looked outside,
just staring at all in sight,
and think to your self
'I can actually see--I AM ALIVE'!!

To be overwhelmed by whatever your looking at.
Wither it is the lush green grass under your feet.
A bluebird crossing over the skies, ever so graciously.
Or even just staring at your hand.

When you sit back and truly enjoy life,
the adrenaline rush is so incredible,
the appreciation for what you have been given is unreal.
I, for one, am eternally greatful for what God has provided for me.
