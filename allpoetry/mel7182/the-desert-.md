In the desert so wide and vast
millions of sandy grains
but the desert is not all simple and not all pure
as told as time has passed

Some large; some small
all different shades
colors vary foot by foot
but they show the scars of all

Many are hidden
with dirt perhaps
not visible to any human eye
for what beauty is within

If looking with a cautious mind
you may be surprised
for many go unnoticed
not knowing what is behind
