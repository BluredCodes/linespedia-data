There is nothing more ironic than love.numerous depths, along with various toils.There is not a more bittersweet ache,nor a stronger wind to soar exceeding from.an almost seemingly luxury with it,on the contrary, an intolerable void with out it.Words do not uphold to its majesty,a song inequivalent to its melody.This explanation so perplexing; to reach; to understand--You only can be sure of it's true meaning--by experiencing it's true sensaton yourself.
