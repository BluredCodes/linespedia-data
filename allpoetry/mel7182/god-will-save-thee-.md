title: "God Will Save Thee. . ."

God will save thee,
no need to fear.
The heavens are watching over
the oceans far and near.

The time will come for us,
to see the magnificent sight.
Far beyond our dreams,
where there is everlasting light.

Embrace His love,
for He will live in your soul.
Trust in our Lord,
and He will make you whole.

Live life as your last,
until you are rewarded eternity.
God listens to every request,
for He is watching over thee.
