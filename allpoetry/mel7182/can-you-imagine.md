---
title: "Can You Imagine?"
writer: "mel7182"
slug: "can-you-imagine"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/6865-Can-You-Imagine--by-mel7182"
---

You imagine,a child's point of view,so innocently pure,concentrated with all life's wonders,visions not diluted by life's worries.Enough love and laughter to fill every canyon.Absent is hate, even as little to fill their tiny hand.They see the light, are blind to the dark that divides.They see the world through an angel's eyes,as how the world is meant to be.Can you imagine if it were that way again?'To see with untainted eyes',to remember those things that as we grew,we unfortunately chose to hide.
