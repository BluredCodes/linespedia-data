---
title: "Would You Still?"

If tomorrow were never to come. . .
Would you still love me today?
If we were to forget all we've done. . . 
Would you stand by me anyway?

If you couldn't remember what you knew. . .
Would you still be able to go on?
If someday it were to come true. . .
Would you still feel like you belong?

If you were to lose all of your strength. . . 
Would you still want to do your best?
If a day had a shorter length. . .
Would you leave everything up to the rest?

If you lacked the faith that you once embraced. . .
Would you give everything up, to have it restored?
If every thought you've ever had were to be erased. . .
Would you want to learn everything you knew before?
