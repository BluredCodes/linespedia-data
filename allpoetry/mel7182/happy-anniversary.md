My love, my partner, whom I will forever cherish,
although someday there Irises will soon perish,
my love that I have for you shall never end,
for these are to symbolize the time we've had to spend.
The bright blue hue to represent the matter just as true.
The bonds, the promises, the devotions with respect to you,
every word, all the smiles,with meanings just as deep,
my love unlike these flowers, will forever keep.
Though there are certain characteristics similar in all.
The sturdy stems that give a foundation to be beautiful and tall.
The frame that can endure the toughest of ailments and foes.
In all each one represents our love and what is shows.
With tender love and care, even over a long time,
each year they will be stronger, and they too will shine,
just as how our love will grow with in every instance.
and even at dry times, with a little assistance,
our love, overall like these flowers will prevail,
for we know what we have is strong and will never fail.
