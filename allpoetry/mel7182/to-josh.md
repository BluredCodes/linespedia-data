I would do anything for you--day or night,even if it was just to be there--hold you tight.I'd give you my life, if that's how it had to be,I hope you understand, you mean everything to me.I'm truly lucky to be with you,also happy and blessed to name a few.I'm overwhelmed whenever I see your beautiful smile,Every minute with you is every minute worth while.I hope I can live up to everything you've given,even all those nights--all the miles you've driven.You've shown me so much--you are my 'light',I'd give up anything--to make you feel just right.My heart is filled with all the love you've shared.To lose that, it would be too much to bear.I feel unbelieveably content, kowing your there.I love you undoubtly--I'll always care.
