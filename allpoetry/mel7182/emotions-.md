Never doubt how I care for you
for you know my feelings will always be true
just as in asking any reason why
trust there are hundreds of stars in the sky

It hurts to think to be away
even though sometimes ask 'why should I stay'
are we even reading on the same page?
Is it to be to let our feelings age?

That last question I feel needs to be asked
for I see, did we even have anything to last?
you ask me, I'm anxious to see
if there was ever a time you cared for me

All this is now is a never ending maze
blinded before in some sort of daze
Don't you even realize what's been given
I can prove you've made my life worth liven'

And it wasn;t all the glamorous things
not at all expecting you to give me wings
could you at least tell me so I know
that all I've said and done was not some show

would you ever, if I needed you to be
be there for me in a time of need?
would you listen and not be afraid?
Would you reapond without being delayed?

Does what I've written make a change?
Would you come to me and help me rearrange?
Questions I have asked, commands I daresay
please know I did it to find out today

My feelings for you will never be sold
as how a good mystery goes untold
I've said this before and shouldn't have to repeat
it should be heard every time I go to speak

Remember all of this that was said
think with your heart and not your head
then you'll know it was the right choice to make
so years from now you don't regret a mistake

I want to save all that I've realized
not as some sort of joke or comprimise
I can learn from all this indeed
just please answer this, my final plead

Were all my worries a waste of time?
Of all the feelings were they only mine?
If so don't look as it was all before
know that I want you and nothing more.
