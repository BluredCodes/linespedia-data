To Forgive. To forgetPlease forgive me,I know it won't be. . .the way it used to.But could we try to see this through?I wish I could rewrite.I dream about it every night.I know what has beenand all that happened then.Even if I tryor a thousand days go byI don't think I could ever forgetevery little thing; every tiny bitIs it possible to prove,to lose all that was true?As I see it lay,If it's in you heart it will forever stay. ©  on Aug 23 2001 04:36 PM PST   0  • 10
