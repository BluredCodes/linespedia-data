To live life without love,
is to live no life at all.
To never feel the rain drop from above.
or not watching the yellow leaves fall.

What would life be--
to ever be able to feel?
A life with out that you see--
would be a life that wasn't real. 

Missing the joy you gave.
forgetting the best in you.
not remembering the things to save.
That is a life I would not want to come true.
