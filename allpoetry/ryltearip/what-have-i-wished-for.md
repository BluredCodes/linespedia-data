'What Have I Wished For?' Down in a hole, with no where to goI've never felt so low, yet I'm undeniably so high I wish I could just fiercefully die, everything you've said was nothing but a lieIt's dark all around, I can't even feel the ground, Ithought I've found something to be so profound  Take a look around...Where is my crown? Every Angel slain My body is just drained, things will never be the sameI open my eyes so bright, yet all I begin to see is thedeceiving night There's simple sounds that shake me all aroundI'm just going to drown in my own fear I felt those tears, nothing around but my own selfAgain I begin to look, and realize that's all it took One second-than I'm here, yet no where near in the clearAll my fears glow so bright, I'm over powered by my might Still no way out, I begin to shootMy pounding heart beat is louder than my cry, God I'm going to fiercefully die Memories flash in front of my face, what a waste and  disgrace    It's my life that I have unforgettably displaced...~Shannon~
