'Crowning of Thee' Poison is what you've chosen, it only left you frozenIt takes you by the dozen You never know until you let go, begin to grow or let it showYou try to say no, but honey it's a desire, desire to go higher, beautification i highly admire I try to acquire, but i beg   Make me pure again make me cleanThrough what I've seen you're not so keen Fuck all them fiends, i live to dream, things are never as they seemDark skies grey clouds, it simply mounds beyond my bounds,until we reach common grounds All i've seen are my dreams fall where dirty demons crawlA dark foggy hall, my vision is blinded with terror, ivebeen through it all, just one time and it leaves a mark They hear my hearts sound straight in the ground, six feet under, oh the sound of the thunderLust in a never ending faze, im simply in a daze I have my own reign    'Its better to reign in hell, than serve in heaven'I'm in my own paradise, for Jesus fucking Christ, id be lying There is no heaven nor a hell    You're left to sour and burn, cower and never learn i long to yearnI keep on thinking but consistently sink, there is no linkbecause i go no where fast.   I'll slay you in a simple grasp than my angel will come       down and pass on the crown
