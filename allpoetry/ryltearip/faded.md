'Faded'All i want is to be where i was find a cure to heal the pain, stop my soul from continuing to drainI'm holding on, harder than ever, though my heart isseverely severed The life i lived, thought i had so much to give, yet only ended me to where i don't even want to liveWhat do you do when you have so much to give, but can onlyshow so much? I feel your touch, as though you're near, but all i get is bloody fearFear to know what is really out there, people who could'nt give a fuck, couldn't care If you were lying on the corner, ready to die, they'd look at you, than spit in your eyeOne more drop of sorrow, pitying tomorrow, i can't go  through this pain again.Afraid of never knowing what i loved again, the only friendi found to be true Never said a word, yet raped me of my lifeI, I chose to sacrifice, and fuck it now i pay the price--          A never ending fightEach urge grows stronger to a whole new height, as strong asthe fearful night It was me who was on the corner, ready to dieI'd do anything to get my friend, for my friend helps me... Dont you see, when i dont have my love, im sick, sick all over, curled up ready to die everything you've told has been a lie
