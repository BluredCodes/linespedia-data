'Master of Minds'
 Thinking about old times, and all those fucking crimes
How could i ever lie so much?
 Just keep your eyes wide shut, as i began to gut
Master of minds, how may lines-
 Enough to bring me back to the same spot, just that 
 simple drop
So delicately touched, my haven begins to seem too far
away, as though it were the passing night and day
 I feel around and begin to weep with the sorrow of
 tomorrow, so solid, more dense than the ground
Misery has won this war
 Baby doll what a whore you are
Doll mouth does the stream come alive with all my hearts
cries?
 To the star i prey- Delay this dismay
You're bloody rotton, so blatenly pure
 Where is the cure that you have stirred up?
That seldom mix, my quickest fix, my wish you want so 
much to fix
 Master of minds, how many lines?
My heart begins to unjustly bind, why am i always denied?
~Shannon~
