'Dying' Only for so long can i live this sad lifeStick a needle in my arm until i hear death I feel my soul screaming, yeah well fuck it, I'm dyingI keep on trying, yet continue to fail, hail to theheavens, bow to the skies, one more push and it'll all bethrough, my time is due, wish you had a clue Passing through my body with no regrets, yet ever lasting remorse, through your whole plan straight off the courseForget the force, i got the mind Thoughts running through me, but the poison is fasterFuck the laughter, i shed my blood throw down the tears,forget them fears, cause only death is near Ain't nothing left, never settle for less, ill burn  your simple caress
