'KEYS'
 I'll look you in the eye, and all i see is a lie
All this silence and rage, its as though my mind were in 
a cage
 I'm so fucking high, how could i deny? The knowledge you 
 fore long will shortly be with held
My star that withdraws and is here to descend, will help
you fake it until you're through
 How will anyone or thing be able to tell when you're true?
The key to unfold and unleash is held within, buried through
my whole life, the one big sin
 Why do i not dream?
    Maybe because nothing is ever as it seems
The shiny beams that you possess in you're eyes is anything
but a disguise
  I dont know where to start- I need to unleash the key to my
      heart...
