'Fixed' Did you fall for that shooting star?It burned straight through your soul, and dragged on yourscars The hole entraps me, can't you see, I'm begging, pleading'The killer in me is the killer in you'The rush i once had is now so through, my time is due Choices i make are mine, the choices i've made throughout my life are mine, and i've done just fineYour heart i've designed  With one simple motion ive created a potion to secure  your devotionNo one could ever know the honest truth as to what i behold, my blood pours out cold Staring up, wishing to be there, forget the cares all i do is dreamMy dreams are what i look up to, cause' im not going far Wishes are what im made of, wanting and fearing what is in storeWhat is the 'Heavens' where my angel stands so beautifuland powerful? Nothing could compare to my despair, but i have hopeBut when looking down the empty valley, where i hear soulsscreaming and running through me, how can i live? I'd like to lie, and say everythings a game, but the  truth is, my life will continue to drain...I took 'The killer in me is the Killer in you' from the  Smashing Pumpkins, i couldn't help it, i thought it was an amazing line, so i put it in quotes thanx
