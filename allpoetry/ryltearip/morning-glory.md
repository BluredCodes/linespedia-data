'Morning Glory' It helps me to think, that we are in sync'You obey and understand, my every last command Than why should it be that I sigh?    -We cry under the same heavenly sky    -We prey and prey, until we wish our misery awayWhat is so beautiful, how could a soul take advantage? We look up and above, wish we could only love    For once, I hope you see what I see-Lift away, yet bring thee' glee The lines and undefining lies, yet beauty has no boundriesBeauty broadens at night, lifts away that intimidatingfright The boldness of the moon, saddest part- I learned  everything all too soonWhen this unusual feeling goes away everyday, all too soon,nothing is left but my unfortunate doom My power and strength will be again soon    In all the heavens and all the Earths, nothing has       ever quite hurt so much        all along you were waiting to crush~Shannon~
