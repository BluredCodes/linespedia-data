'Glamorous Lies' 
 Desirable dreams, are they ever as they seem?
You shine so bright as to another height
 The glow that you have that once was below has now opened
 up, and now has shown
You're on you're own turf and have your own thrown
 The empty thoughts, and missing pieces are so gently sewn
   Together we cry, together we learn
When is it going to be our turn?
 I'll be their through thick and thin, until we show we 
 have it in us to win
A long fighting battle
 Glamorous lies, detailing cries
Too much alike, we're back for another strike
 You know too much, yet fear all too soon
That bewildering shine of the moon that you possess will
lead you out of this unethicle mess
 I have come to trust you, that i must confess
~Shannon~This poem isn't really me, its not really how i write,but I was experimenting with a different style, i don't really like it, but i tried
