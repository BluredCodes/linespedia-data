'Prison'
 I feel you watching over me, but how could that be?
You want my gift, though I'm not quite sure what it is
 I feel different, like no one could possibly know
Maybe since i never show, i don't want to give in, besides
I'm not sure if i even know how
   To let people in, I couldn't allow
If i had to do it all over again, would i change a thing?
 You run through my body like nothing can, you control me,
 I'm not even me, i couldn't possibly be free
I love that sting run through my veins, its the only time
i ever feel sane
 Theres nothing or noone left to blame, its me you see?
How could it be, i bring the pain upon thy self
 what am i worth?
What could i possibly be good for?
 The lust and lure opens a new door,
It can't be me, don't you see
 I won't be let free...
