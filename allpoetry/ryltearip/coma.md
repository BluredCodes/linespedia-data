'COMA'

  Who would have ever thought life would bring such sorrow
  Where i despise you for bringing forth tomorrow-
      I never thought this pain would over power me
      The lives we live are just one big mystery
                   Can't you see?-
  I breathe in the waves of the salty air, as the sand runs through my fingers, like my time is running out-
      Pain clouds my mind, its me im looking to find-
  You have risen so high, why is it the enemy will never die?
      Deny thy father refuse thy name, my thoughts of fear are too far from sane-
  When there becomes nothing left to gain, i denounce my reign-
      Rain pours as i wished-
  The power i have within is held back, for i know so much its as though it were a sin-
      When darkness pours and nothings around, except for your poisoned hearts sound-
  What have you really learned through all of this?-
      To lie, cheat, steal?-
               Well i have discovered how to heal...
   The soul is your treasure, no jewel could compare or measure-
      I couldn't allow mine to continue to severe
 So, ill continue to try, and aimlessly search, for i couldn't allow my jewel to be in the huerst, though it feels as though im forever cursed...
