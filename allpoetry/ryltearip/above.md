'ABOVE'My star that is so far, the dream that i feel but wish i could see- I have to flee in order to be let freeCan't you see?   Release and let go, let yourself beI prey and prey, pretend everything is okay  Where is my heart, do I have a soul?Than how do i get out of this black hole? Will i ever see light againI'm destine through all eternity I surrender my serenity, i wish to start once moreCan't you see  the real whore is yourself Yourself who has snatched the pride i once had when i did confide-But you denied, lied and never were on my side so now, i will not run and hide, i will face my fears, not allow  myself to drown in my own pitiful tearsI take the power and refuse to let thyself cower anymore You should just rot and sourThe sky is so bright, along with the whole endless night I once saw all that glittered in your eyes, but now you can no  hold back your criesSo on this night its everyone who dies, except for i, because i'm so fucking        high...I kind of move around on the topic, but it explains a lot for me personally
