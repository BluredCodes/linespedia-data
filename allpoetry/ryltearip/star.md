'Star' Questions arise, as my heart just diesAll these lies, fakes from the smile straight to the core For god fucking sake, give it a break, it's my life at stake, a give or take situationYour minds are all brand new creations.. Enemy or fake, watch out for your extinction, I'm not always angry, but trust me i am nowI don't really believe i know how, but you better begin tobow, cause' I'm now living a life of hatebreed Another rotten seed, oh yes in deed'No more chances, you've had enough Better do your last dance, simple caress will be no moreWho lied and said it would be easy, let alone fun? Get me the gun, let me run!I thought'd be over, dodge straight for it, but now i start all over, its this sting, let my mind meditate andring A little recovery will bring new dicoveries, uncover the barrier we all live inBring Heaven and Hell in, neither is perfect nor never will be, i dont understand why noone can see    Stop the shadows and masks, and finish this task...You're beauty is beyond belief, but why so much grief?did i bring it upon you? Stick around and see whats beneathIt's not that harsh and not too hard ~Don't ever let down your guard or who you are~ Through those bright gates is your own shining star ~Shannon~
