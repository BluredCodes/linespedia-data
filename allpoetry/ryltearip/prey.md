'Prey...'I prey to the soon, moon and the stars, to a world that seems so far Inevitably i wish i were there, away from the world where i don't have a careI consistently stare into nothing as my mind degenerates.Dissolving more vividly than anything i could know I'm so far down below, there's no words to describe the feelings i have, and how I'm so lowThe feeling of feeling draws in the pain Dig my grave so i can weep, dig so deep for that secret to keep, the possession thats yours and noone elses.I've become undone, now let me run Nothing helps, nothings heredoes anyone honestly even care? I know the truth that is held within, so deviously rotton i'll fake it father for i have sinned, i never win, even  as the day goes down and my body along with my soul go  straight to the ground,i only drown in my own sorrowful tears, dreading those fears, and anything near The light of the sun along with the moon, i hurt and deny all too soon.Who would know, let alone who would care??
