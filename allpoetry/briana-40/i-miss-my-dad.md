---
title: "I Miss My Dad"
writer: "briana 40"
slug: "i-miss-my-dad"
source: "AllPoetry"

I don't understand his reasons,
His excuses for not seeing me.
Just because him and mom are divorced,
Doesn't mean he's separated from me.

Just because I don't like his girlfriend,
Or wife I now should say.
I refuse to go to that house,
There I will not stay.

All it takes is a couple of minutes,
Maybe a drive in his truck.
Just to ask me how I'm doing,
To see why I think school sucks.

He does not call me anymore,
Doesn't even want to try.
To see his own loving daughter,
To understand why she cries.

Does he honestly not care?
Does he really not want to try?
He chose that bitch over me,
I want to see her die!
