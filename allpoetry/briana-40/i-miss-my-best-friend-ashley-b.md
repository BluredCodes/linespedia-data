title: "I Miss My Best Friend Ashley B."

I can't believe you left me,
School and life are just not the same.
Ashley B. I miss you,
I can't conquer this lonely game.

Why can't you just move back?
You could live with me I'm sure.
I can't face another day without you,
Without my best friend I'm torn.

Everything has changed so much,
It hurts so much you see.
I hate going to school without you,
I need you here with me.

Without you here I've lost all hope,
Where's my motivation?
Hate school, hate work, hate my dad,
Miss our conversations.

I know your new school can't be better,
Just come back and you'll see.
If you can't return to this place,
I'll run away to Waverly.

I don't like school knowing you're not here,
Can't you see what this means?
My best friend is in another town,
A lifetime away it seems.

Why can't I see you everyday?
Just to stand there by your side.
God knows I'll always stand up for you,
Even if they're twice my size.

For you I know it's got to be tough,
Everything there's so new.
I'm glad you got away from this town,
But why away from me too?

Each and everyday I get so scared,
So discouraged without you here.
But now I know to relax and wait,
Until I can call you and share my fears.

Even though we feel far away,
We're here through thick and thin.
Nobody on earth can break this bond,
Our everlasting friendship comes from within.
