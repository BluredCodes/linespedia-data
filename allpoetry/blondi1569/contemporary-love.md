I hate it when you say you'll call and you don't
I hate it when you say you'll come and see me and i know you really won't
I hate it when you make me promises but all they do is get broke
I hate it when you just take me as a joke
I hate it when we fight
I hate it when you're right
I hate it when you lie
I hate it when it doesn't seem like you try
I hate it when you act like you don't care
I hate it when you're not there
I hate it when you make me cry
But most of all I hate it when we have to say goodbye!I would just like the truth on what everyone thinks!
