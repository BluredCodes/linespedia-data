The love I hold for you is so strong and so true,
That is what makes me believe I belong with you.
When you look into my eyes,
You captivate my heart and soul,
Giving me no control.
When we are together,
I feel, as we are the only two in existence.
But when we are apart, 
I can't seem to bear the distance.
Why do all of our good-byes have to end with cries?
If love is such a magical thing,
Why does it have all this pain to bring?
With you there,
And me here...
Oh, how I only wish you could be near.
But have no fright,
Because I know I'm right.
This is not the end,
Someday we will finally be together,
Where our hearts can mend and our love can last forever!!!This is a poem about my boyfriend and I who have a long-distance relationship.....i would just like to know what everyone thinks and what i can improve on. i wrote this poem in a hurry and couldn't decide on the ending so please let me know!!
