Where do I start?Where do I begin?There’s so much to tell,So much to explain.How do I reveal all the pain?Why did you have to steal my heart?Why did you have to tear it apart?I thought you loved me,I thought you actually cared.Boy was I a fool to believe all the lies.You aren’t worth anymore of my cries.There are plenty of tears that have already been shed,Those are what gave you the strength and the power,Which just makes you even more of a coward.My love was actually pure and true,But now I don’t even see how,I mean look at you!I hope one day you’ll get your heart broken in two,Finally you will see how I felt when I lost you!this is a poem about one of my x-boyfriends who i thought i really loved at a time but now i realized he doesn't compare to my boyfriend now! please tell me what you think!!!
