A mournful tune floats
On the mist shrouded moor
Enchanting highland air
From lips
Pipe a lingering melody of loss
And love
Fingers touch
Seeking chords of resonance
To drift in the tranquility
Of moonlight
His caress giving life
To the harmony of souls
On the mist shrouded moor
A mournful tune floats
