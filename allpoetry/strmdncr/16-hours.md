Lovers awake to the morning light After a night of pure delight Upon his brow, a tender kiss Begins a morning of enchanted bliss Bodies entwine and move as one Another days passionate time has begun Fingers gliding on velvet skin Gentle kisses, a thrill from within A fire growing deep inside Moving together in a timely stride An hour has passed, maybe more Impassioned lovers not keeping score The clock ticks by, going on noon Their fever is high not diminishing soon Exploring, delightful new things are discovered As two bodies wrestle under the covers Dinner time passes, no ending in sight The sun going down on this day long delight Up in the sky the stars start to glitter While under the covers their bodies still quiver Their fire still rages, out of control An entire day hasn't taken it's toll No stopping now while the fire is hot Another position that hits the spotMidnight comes on all too fast For lovers who haven't run out of gas It's 1 AM and their just about done Sixteen hours of exquisite fun Fast asleep, two sleepy heads Holding each other, safe in their bed Out until noon, who knows what's in store Waking to find they want even moreJust havn' a bit of fun...
