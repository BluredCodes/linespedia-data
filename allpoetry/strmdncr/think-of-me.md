---
title: "~~~Think Of Me~~~"
writer: "StrmDncr"
slug: "think-of-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

Think of me
When autumn leaves fall
And remember when we had it all

Think of me
When ever it rains
And know I want you back again

Think of me
When the roses bloom
And let me take away your gloom

Think of me
When you sleep each night
And hold me close, hold me tight

And Think of me
When the sun rises each morn
And know that I love you even more
