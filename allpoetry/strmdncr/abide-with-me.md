When the light fades into sunset,
and the golden glow warms the twilight air.
Come, abide with me.

When the days burdens make you weary,
and you need to find someone to care.
Come, abide with me.

When your body aches to find fulfillment, 
and loving release is what you seek.
Come, abide with me.

And when your day comes to an end,
and it’s time for you to sleep.
Come,

Abide with meThis was written for someone very special.
