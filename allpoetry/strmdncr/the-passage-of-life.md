I walk a long lonely corridor 
Searching in the fading light 
Too many doors to choose 
So quickly losing sight 

Controlling the chaos inside me 
Keeping it tightly reined 
It slips through my fingers bit by bit 
While another step is gained 

Each door is numbered different 
Still looking all the same 
I try a knob, it's locked 
I fall back one step again 

My eyes search the distance 
It goes on forever in my mind 
I reach for another knob 
My God I'm losing time 

The metal turns in my fingers 
Light breaks through the crack 
Another hallway in time 
Should I go through or go back 

This choice I know is mine to make 
A brave soul I'll have be 
To walk through this new portal 
To find what waits for me 

In life we all have choices 
We all must make our own 
I pray I make my wisely 
Then I'll be happy to be going home
