Gentle lover with tender touch
Nestled in the curve of my breast
Thy lips suckle so softly
Taking nourishment at my request

Thy teasing has delighted this Lady
Now laying by thy side
Awaiting the caress of finger tips
Along ones skin do glide

This Ladies lips wish to please thee
Lips to lips, to nipple to thigh
My moistened tongue exploring
Every touch brings forth a sigh

Falling farther into the rapture 
Of my lover's sweet invasion
Longing for more of my lover's gifts
With each rhythmic penetration

Thrust, retreat, only to thrust once more
Upon this bed of splendor
My lover advances to take even more
Then gives his loves sweet nectar

This Lady begins to respond in kind
Sweet honey gushes from within
Waves of pleasure ravage my soul
Damn me to hell if this be a sin

Two bodies bathed in moonlight
Sleeping, dreaming, contented
Be lovers till the end of time
Not dreaming of love lamented
