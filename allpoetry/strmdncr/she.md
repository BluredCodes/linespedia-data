White canvas billows 
She reaches 
Taking more 
Body arching, flexing 
She moves 
To a rhythm 
Moist caresses 
Lifting her higher 
Faster 
Leaning into her master’s hand 
She begs to fly 
His touch commanding 
She reaches 
White canvas billows 
She soars
