Midnight in the garden of earthly delight
She dances through the tall cool grass
Chestnut hair shimmering in moonlight
Is she fae or a wee bonny lass 

A melody drifts by on the evening breeze
As she moves through the misty glen
You watch as she steps with gentle ease
Dancing round, again and again

Suddenly she is there before you
But all is not what it seems
Though she smiles, her eyes sparkle brightly
Her tears trickle down in streams

No one knows for whom the lady weeps
Nor why she dances all alone
But each night she is there among the trees
Dancing for a love unknown
