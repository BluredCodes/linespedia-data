I can see from the comments so far that I need to include this note. This poem was written for a group of auto techs who work with me. It was part of a bet. I bet them I could make them laugh with a love poem. If they laughed, each man had to buy me lunch. I ate free lunch for nearly a month... I hope you get a chuckle as well...

Words are not enough...

Over the centuries man has tried 
to find the words for what's inside 
The ones that have just the right meaning 
to express his passions deepest feelings 

Saying 'I love You' comes easy to some 
But is it enough for that special one 
I say I love you a thousand times or more 
But how do I know, how can I be sure 

Do you truly know what I'm trying to say 
Do you even feel the same way 
Is there a word that I should invent 
So that you will know just what I meant 

I open my heart and give it to you 
But is there something more I can do 
A word, a phrase, please give me a clue 
Something that says I truly love you 

I could say, you're every breath that I take 
That I think of you every moment I'm awake 
Do you understand that I don't want to hide 
This feeling I have deep down inside 

Not Byron, Keats, or even Shakespeare 
have written the words I want you to hear 
The words I have, I've used before 
The ones that say, I want you and more 

So I'll say it again and hope that you know 
That I want you more, I want you so 
I want you to know you're not just a toy 
What I want to say is 
Ride me Cowboy!
