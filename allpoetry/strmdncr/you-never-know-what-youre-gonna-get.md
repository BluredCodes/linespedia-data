Just a little something to cheer me up.



 

Ah, mood swings 

my favorite time of month 

one minute I'm up in the clouds 

then everything goes south 



You never know what you're getting 

when hormones begin to rage 

I could be happy as a kitten 

or a tiger in a cage 



All the men tuck tail and run 

they don't know what to do 

That sweet lady with loving words 

now acts like she has the flu 



I start the day on a happy note 

singing cheerful tunes 

but by the time night fall comes 

I'm baying at the moon 



You might think this is funny 

But I don't get the joke 

One minute I'll be cheering you on 

the next I'll wish you'd choke 



So all you boys take your manhood 

and stick it you know where 

We girls only get the dirty end 

Damnit, life just isn't fair
