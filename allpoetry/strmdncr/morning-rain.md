Just some early morning fun...


Tap, Tap, Tap
Early morning rain
Cozy bed
Drowsy brain 

Tap, Tap, Tap
Sings a lullaby
Cuddle close
Tired eyes

Tap, Tap, Tap
I wanta stay in bed
Hold you close
Sleepy head

Tap, Tap, Tap
Another hour, two?
Wanta make love
Me n’ You?
