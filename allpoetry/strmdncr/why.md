Why are you just out of reach 
Beyond my touch 
Why does my heart cry for you 
Longing to feel 

Why can’t you see me, waiting 
Praying you’ll come 
Why am I always alone 
With my fears 

Why can’t I find some comfort 
With another 
Why must it always be you 
Whom I love
