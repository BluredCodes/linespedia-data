Have you seen the dead children 
They’re everywhere 
A secret held deep inside 

Don’t tell anyone what you know 
It’s a secret 
Something they must hide 

It was just a game people play 
Nothing more 
But they said you shouldn’t tell 

The game they played wasn’t fun 
Anymore 
It became a child’s silent hell 

The grown ups that should have seen 
Didn’t watch 
They let someone get inside 

Now the innocents suffer everyday 
In everyway 
The grown ups had all lied 

Childhood wasn’t any fun, not when 
They play the game 
Always on guard, trying to hide 

Please Mom and Dad guard them well 
Don’t blink 
They only get one chance 

Let them have their childhood, it’s 
Too short 
Don’t make them dance this dance 


This is dedicated to all the abused children everywhere. 
Guard them well. They need us….
