title: "Did you know"

Did you know that I watch everything 
Every move 
Every gesture 
Every expression 

Did you know that I study you while you sleep 
Your face 
Your arms 
Your hands 

Did you know that I pray 
That I won't have to remember 
That you will always be here 
That it will be forever 

Did you know that I love you 
Everything you do 
Everything you say 
Everything you are 

Did you Know?

P.M.Williams
