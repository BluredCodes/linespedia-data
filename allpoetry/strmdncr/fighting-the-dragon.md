title: "Fighting The Dragon"
writer: "StrmDncr"
slug: "fighting-the-dragon"

It breathes, 
sucking cool clean air 
Leaving the acrid smell of smoke 

Scorched stone and timber mark it's path 
Destruction, waste everywhere 
I follow in its wake 
Moving through the dark interior
 
My sword and pike in hand 
Ready to face the final battle 
My body in tune 
Senses keen and honed 

My armor hot and heavy 
Sweat trickles down my back 
I hear the cries of the innocent 
Urging me deeper into the darkness 

Flames lick at my heels 
While I search every blackened corner 
A child huddles in the darkness 
Scared, crying 

I wrap him in the safety of my armor 
Snug and secure 
He clings to me for dear life 
My strength giving hope 

I defy the dragon to stop me 
Defeat me, just try 
This child well be safe 
Free from the blistering heat
 
Light filters through the gloom 
Guiding my way 
We break through the smoke 
Bursting out into the light of day 

Waiting arms take my burden 
And I return to wage war 

Come to me demon 
Show thy ugly face 
Do battle with this warrior 
I will not be denied 

The dragon's breath assaults me 
Slowing my steps 
I move forward pike at the ready 
Watching for its attack 

Before me the dragon rises 
Rearing its massive head 
He bellows smoke and cinder 
Laying waste to my path 

I wheeled my sword in anger 
Fury deep within my soul 
Slicing, hacking away at the threat 
I spar and drive on with an iron will 

Spiting fire at my challenge 
He parries my attack 
I thrust harder, stronger 
Forcing him back into the gloom
 
Fighting on with thunder in my heart 
I drive my sword home 
Lance in my grip 
I charge the demon piercing him to the core
 
He stagers, spewing heat and smoke 
I twist my weapon digging deeper 
A mighty roar explodes 
Then silence 

One by one rays of light appear 
Illuminating my battlefield 
At my feet the dragon lays vanquished 
Puffs of steam rise from his nostrils 

I lower my weapon, my triumph complete 
My demon destroyed 

Life giving air washes through the ruins 
Removing my helmet I breathe deep 
Adrenaline drains, while pride soars
 
The dragon sent back to hell 
A life saved 
And I will return to fight again 
Another Dragon 
Another day
