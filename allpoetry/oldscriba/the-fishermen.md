we were fishermenwe drove rusted trucksand ancient Cadillacs                                    to the riverour dreams gave us upto destiny andsmall towns                  abandoned us to                  the growing holes in our teeth                  to the dancer in the doorway                  to the wind ---a ghost watching her childrenbringing their catchto the surface
