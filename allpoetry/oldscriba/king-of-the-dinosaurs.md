I am not lost whenit comes to iceI've been here before andI'll always remember the way                            my knife has a saber tooth blade                            my boots are lined with seal fur                            my whale oil lamp gives a steady light                            for music I have a hollow caribou bone                            and the wind                     : the king of the dinosaurs               has none of these things               and without them               he cannot follow me here
