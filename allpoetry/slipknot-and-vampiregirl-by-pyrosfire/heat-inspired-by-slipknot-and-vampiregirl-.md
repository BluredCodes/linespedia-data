JUDGEMENT...
THEN BREATHE
RIGOROUS LAPS OF THIS RATING GAME
BAIT...
FOR THE EYES OF APPEARENCE DEMONS
KEEP THAT CANDLE LITE WHEN YOU LOOK AT ME
LOCK YOUR DOOR
MY SEED HAS BEEN SEEN

SUFFOCATE WHAT HAS SPAWNED ME
TIME WILL CONSUME THEIR DEAD MEAT
RED IN THE FACE
BLACK BEHIND BROWN EYES
TRANCED FROM EYE LASH TO FEET
ENJOY FEBUARY HEAT
WHILE THIS BLOOD LINE CONTINUES TO SEEP
INTO EVERY PUPIL IT SEES

STEEL HUMANS IN HEAT
AGAIN HAS BRUNG ANOTHER ME
INTO OUR WASTING WORLD
NOVEMBER HEAT...
IT WAS PUSHED OUT OF HER
AND WEEPED

2023...
HEAT...
FROM ONE NIGHT OF PASSION
SWEAT AND PLEASURED MEATS...
THE BODY DIDNT SEE WHAT THE FUTURE WOULD BRING
THE KILLER OF DEATH
THE SUICIDE OF LOSS
WHEN HE WAS BORN
PEOPLES FATE WERE SWORN

COURTS AND CONSTANT DEBATE
ON HOW HE HANDLED THEIR FATES
REPITION IN WRITING...
AS YOU'LL SEE THIS TO BE
TEMPERS HEAT...
