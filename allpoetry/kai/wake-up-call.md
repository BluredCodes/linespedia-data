Wake-Up Call

Eyelids fluttering
Open to sensation
Light, Color flooding in
Piercing thought-filled sleep

Drifting sounds
Filtered through window screens
Laughter, small voices
Crack!

Leaving softness spreads
Two unsteady feet
To peer, blinking
To see, excitement building

Last dreariness flees
Clothes fly on
Stumbling out to street
Complement of joyous sounds

Summer suns beating
Summer Quests starting
Seizing the New
Seizing the Day
Finding Life
