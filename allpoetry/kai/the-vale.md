Long have I waitedUnder Shadow, under treeCountless years I have satUN-aging stillTime flies by meEras change before my eyesFar-reaching stillWhy should I let the days fly pastGreat deeds unaccounted for'I shall wait forever'Rued wordsPassing from mind to lipsHeedless stillDestiny's WingsFluttering, alight on branchFreedom stillDestiny's CallCannot be ignoredSo, I seekThe Vale is openedThe World unfoldsWarAges pass Great battles wonGreat deeds doneDestiny embraced stillAt the waning of the Age of WarThe waning of Destiny's TrumpetI seek for quiet placesInnocent stillThen shall I findOnce againThe ValeVale of TearsVale of SorrowsVale of MistokenVale of MisthoughtNowVale of LoveVale of JoyVale of LibertyVale of Strength UnitedFor some at Destiny's EndThe Highest Hills are found-Song of Kai'...for love is as strong as death,Its jealousy unyielding as the grave.It burns like a blazing fire,Like a mighty flame.''...Do not arouse or awaken loveUntil it so desires.'-Song of Solomon
