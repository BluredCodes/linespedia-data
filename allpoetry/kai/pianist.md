---
title: "Pianist"
writer: "Kai"

Fingers sliding gracefully
Over liquid porcelain keys
Thoughts cannot contain
Sounds which words profane
A single fluid movement
Allows a watcher a moment
Of greatest serene reflection
Mind cannot find connection
Between emotion
And wills devotion
