Our love life took
an unexpected turn.
The love you had for me
now fails to burn
Through all the heartache
and all this pain
is one fact
that will always remain
You're my best friend
Forever and ever
And that is one feeling
that our breakup cannot sever
I can tell you anything,
and i don't know what i would do
If i lost my very best friend,
you.
I still love you, 
and that wont end,
but i love you even more
as my best friend.This one is for you Andrea.
