---
title: "Move Along"
writer: "berkels"

You tell me you love him
Is it true?
You have your whole life
right in front of you

Take a step back,
and look at your life
Do you really want
to be his wife

If he brings so much love,
why sing such a sad song?
Thats right, now you know,
you were the one who was wrong.

Now that you know,
you need to move along
to a happier man,
and sing a happier song

But you wont move along,
just as you should
You cant bring yourself to do it,
after you told me you would

You have been with me,
my friend to the end.
But when is this madness
going to end?
