title: "So this is it?"

So this is it?
what its built up to be?
Love is supposed to be great
but this is all i can see.

heartache, pain, emptyness,
sorrow, misery, hurt.
its not what it used to be,
when we etched our names in the dirt

We said together forever,
what a fib
when you look at your child
inside your crib

You hope your child
will never see
a pain as painful
as the pain in me

Love isnt all,
its built up to be
now, the rest of your life,
this is what you'll see

Your child growing up,
Making a friend
Maybe the friend that will
stick with her til the end.

And you hope she wont share
the things you went though
You hope that her love
will be faithful and trueHey this is my latest, i hope you like it!
