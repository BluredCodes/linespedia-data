The sun in the sky
brings us our light
The moon in the sky 
brings us our night

There is a wanting 
inside of each of our hearts
and from each other's kidness
is where it starts

The sun gives to us 
all of its light,
like we should keep each other
in our sight.

The moon is the enemy,
bringing us darkness
making it hard
for us to even start this

We must put each other
in front of ourselves
For through this kindness
is where the feeling dwells

The feeling of being wanted
this is what everyone desires
To be forgiven
and put out old fires

So we must help
everyone we can
To save ourselves
