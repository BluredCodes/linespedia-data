---
title: "Now you come"
writer: "berkels"
slug: "now-you-come"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I longed for you,
long after you left
This empty feeling,
I couldn't fill

But now you come back,
and think i will just take you
Things have changed,
what about that dont you understand

There is someone new
She is incredible
I love her so much,
So much more than i ever loved you

Im sorry if that upsets you
maybe it isnt fair
But neither were you,
when you broke up with me

You cant just come back when you want
thats not how love works
And nothing you say
will change my mind
