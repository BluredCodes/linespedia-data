We were there
lying on my bed
just friends at the moment
hoping to be more

we goofed around
started wrestling
there was a feeling
all throughout the room

was this it?
i didnt know
So i leaned in
as did you

as our lips met
i lost all feeling
a million feelings at once
What a feeling!

i was dead to the world,
and i couldnt have been happier
