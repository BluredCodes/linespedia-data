My tears were waisted,
there's no question.
I called you last night
You werent there
I wish you were
You are a new start
You were a new start
How bout we talk
You dont want that
It was there
now its not
something is up
we arent the same
Your lines, lies
My heart, true
Yours not
Nights meaningless
My time was waisted,
there's no questions
Who cares,
Her name started with a "k"This really has no style, it was just a fun way to write it.
