I Love Love
It leaves me complete
It gets me so high
It keeps me on my feet

I Want Love
From just that one girl
Who can dance, prance,
and even can twril

I Found Love
I find it so fun
She is the brightest part of my life
Just like the sun

I Cant Loose Love
That would leave me so low
And take me to a place
I dont want to go

So many emotions
Love is so deep
It can make you break you
Even make you weep

Yet ill still love her
With all my heart
Just like i have
From the very start
