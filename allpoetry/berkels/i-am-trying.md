title: "I am trying"

Please forgive me
But im new to this
every new corner
brings a new twist

However fun or exciting
this may be
This it the first time it
has happened to me

There have been girls 
but never like you
I love the things you say
i love the things you do

But i am new to this
i must remind you
so please dont expect me 
to know what to do

My words may come up
just a little bit short
Or my moves may be
a little too forward

But if they do
please tell me so
for in the future
i will already know
