It had been two monthor close to itI was in loveHead over healsIts never been like thisYou didn't seem to notice meYou didn't seem to careI tried so hard to hold on to youI still love youAnd maybe your love i will have againI pray for that day, when you might sayI love you ericBut that day might never comeThat brings more tears to my eyesAs i write this stupid poemI dont know what i didTo lose your loveAll i know is i want it backThe tears are flowing freely nowI cant control themBut thats ok, those will go awaybut my love for you never willhey, i really don't care what anybody thinks of this poem.  I wrote it right after my love broke up with me.  So if you like it then fine, and if you don't like it screw you!
