title: "why not me"
writer: "berkels"

I see you over there
across the way
I want to come over
and ask if i may
have this dance with you
no matter how long
for even a few seconds
ill dance to this song
your moves seemed so gentel
so effortless so light
that i would like to see them
all through the night
but when i ask to dance
you say "this one is taken"
and that is one move
i didnt want you making
heartbroken, and sorry
i go back to my place
wishing that your love
i would not have chased
i curl myself up
into a little ball
and wait for another day
i wont like at all.
