MEMORIES



Gentle moans of pleasure,
Kitten whimpers too,
Emerge from deep within the soul,
With memories of you.
Recall of mutual pleasuring,
Bringing flushes to the face.
Mind drifting back in ecstasy,
Heat bursts far into space.

Lazily walk your fingers upon me,
Run your hands along my spine.
Feel my body arch and tremble,
With promises divine.
Fill my mind with wanton longing,
Whisper suggestively in my ear.
Slowly, softly stoke this fire.
Build the embers up, with care.

With hot lips upon my neck, dear one,
Linger just enough to see,
The responses of my body,
Wanting, needing, longing, free.
Let your hands invade the spaces,
Pledged forever, just for you.
Move your lips, kiss my skin,
Triumphant cry resounds from deep within.

There is no point in pretending,
Desire as this knows no reproach.
Not a word about tomorrow,
Right now is what matters most.
Fill this cup up to the very top,
Coil and twist with lusts desire.
Nothing else on earth is relevant,
Than our own eternal fire
