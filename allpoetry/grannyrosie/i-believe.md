I believe

Hearts reach out across our world
Linking minds in a circle of prayer,
Committing friends and strangers’ lives
To our Lords sweet mercy and care.

The thoughts reflect the miracles of faith
As each participant pleads their case
The bowed heads of humility
Beseeching the Lord to give his grace.

Oh Lord we know that when two or three
Are gathered in common prayer
The strength of our committed souls
Will reach our saviour everywhere.

Oh grant us strength to overcome
The non-believers, those who shun
Your words, that they when face with adversity
Plead for the prayer of you and me.

It is easy for some to take God’s words
At random, in contempt
And throw them at His lambs in spite
Without feeling of what is meant.

But never forget the power of He
Who created a world of diversity
He alone sits in judgment on high
Watching the saints and sinners go by.

He alone in majesty created 
The Earth, the sky, the sea,
His is the only plan we see
That allowed for every contingency.

And He is the one to whom all men call
When they are weak, stumble and fall.
