LOVES EMPTINESS
 Something whispering through the trees.
Hark! It is more than just a breeze.
Voice of my love, tis now I hear,
Treasured part of me, I hold so dear.

Beloved, where is it, that you have gone?
Let these tired eyes, once more look upon
Your dearest face, your laughing, teasing eyes.
Hold me again, take me to loves paradise.

How long I have searched, to find you my dear.
Desperate for you, in every passing, lonely year.
How can it be that you should stay away?
Come back dear heart, if only for one day.

The aching, yearning emptiness, deep within my heart,
Grows, engulfs me, every moment we are apart.
Acknowledgement of differences of point of view,
Surely these trivial things would not our love undo?

Write me a letter, call me on the telephone,
I am afraid, weary, can no longer be alone.
Waiting here, keeping those promises I made to you.
I beg of you, what is it, you want me to do?

Does another hold you in her loving arms?
What I would give to once more feel your charms.
Love of my life, you are my very soul, my all.
Be still my heart, watch yet another tear drop fall.
