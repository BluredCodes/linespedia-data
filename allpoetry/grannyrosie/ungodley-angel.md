** 



Narcissistic whore 
With pretence of angelic purity, 
Spiralled as in chastity 
Then, behold, 
He sets his beams on thee. 

So demure, 
With eyes caste down, 
Such 
A small moment in time 
Brazenly then 
You trumpet your beguiling evil charms. 
Is it not enough that you 
Flout your organs 
Of reproduction to the world? 
That yet you offer innocence? 
“Come drink of this juice of life!” 

So angelic are you in 
Your purity. 
White ruffled edges set 
Off by cloaks of green. 
The sap of you reduces all 
That might indulge to some euphoric 
State. 
Nihilistic nightmare. 

Siren, 

Prostitute to every bed in which you lie. 

Yes! 

Overindulgence in your charms can cause 
A mere man to die 
Or 
Go mad returning 
Yet again to thief 
Upon a night 
In awe 
Of 
Your beauty. 
Obsequence in 
Your narcotic delights.
