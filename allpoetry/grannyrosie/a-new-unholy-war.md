A NEW, UNHOLY WAR
 Disbelief.
Denial.
GOD! I dont want to know!
A prank.
Sensationalism gone mad.
Emerges reality.
Television networks show,
Crumbling National Pride,
People still inside,
Domestic flights of angels in innocence.
Nonsense?
Insanity!
Evil epitomised!
Anti CHRIST forces arise.
Invading the heart of Liberty.
Killing wounding, maiming, indisciminately.
'Welcome to the real world'.
Jewish friend, in Israel, says in sorrow.
The world strenght, United States Of America, today,
What target for tomorrow?
Blackmail of reprisal, fear,
So many of the horde appear.
Infiltrating every land.
Now, this monumental grandstand.
Encircling the United States of America with prayer,
People of the world, unite everywhere.
Pray for families,
Pray for friends,
Pray that soon this madness ends.
Maybe the wrath of the free world will rise to decimate,
Those who know none of HIS love, only hate.
A Holy war, it seems, declared.
Rather our loving FATHER,
Than theirs.
