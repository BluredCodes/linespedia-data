ROSE OF FRIENDSHIP
 A rose, dear friend, I give to you,
Token of esteem I have, it’s true.
Dark red rose bud, petals so tight,
Symbolic of affection, shining bright.

Petals so soft, flushed with morning dew,
Gentle and sweet, as my love for you.
Unified, melded, in a common bond defined,
Strong as the admiration for you, in my mind.

Watch then, as these precious petals unfold,
A story of true, lasting friendship told.
Reaching out, revealing beauty deep,
Permeating the air with fragrance sweet..

Each petal is a blessing I pass to you,
May everything you say and do,
Reflect this friendship I give unconditionally.
I pray you will return in kind to me.

When the petals fall, please have no care,
They represent everything that we share.
Love and laughter, sorrow and tears,
In friendship’s comfort, no need for fears.

As one bloom fades, another appears,
A certainty, for countless years.
A rose bud each day, tho we are far apart,
Picked fresh from this garden within my heart.
