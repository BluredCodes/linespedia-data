*Light So Shine*

Azure blue skies above,
Displaying the glory of GOD.
Night heavens declare it so,
Twinkling stars reflect where angels have trod.

Truth, inviting all mankind,
Pleading to be heard.
HIS promise, salvation to all who respond.
GOD’S glorious, unchanging word.

Light of hope, shining on high,
Beckons all to hear HIS call.
Fall down in awe before HIM.
Grand Master of us all.

Bend to HIS will, exult HIM.
Within us all resides that spark.
Ignite your light for JESUS,
Find exit from the dark.

*Let your light so shine*,
 HIS gentle commanding word
Raise HIS name, a mighty monument,
That everywhere, HIS voice is heard.
