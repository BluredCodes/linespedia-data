AS LONG AS IT TAKES

This love, so deep, within my breast,
Will be forever my strength and light,
All encompassing, passionate, pure,
Until it does, your heart secure.

For as long as it takes.

These eyes will search the world for thee,
No other face will ever be,
Supplanted 'fore your image there,
In front of me, so tall and fair.

For as long as it takes.

These lips are yours, and yours alone,
At last, they have found a true ,sweet, home.
Glowing with desire, and smiles for you,
Portraying joy, and songs anew.

For as long as it takes.

This body now, gives you the right,
To be beside it, day or night,
Companionship, closeness, passion's vine,
Take it my heart, for it is thine.

For as long as it takes.

These arms, and hands, will stroke thy hair,
Gently massage stress and care,
Will hold your hand, side to side,
Dear light, sweet love, be my guide.

For as long as it takes
