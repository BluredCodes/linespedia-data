INTERNET LOVER

.

Perfect partner, man of my dreams,
Sitting there, comfortable chair, smiling,
Back slightly bent, concentrating,
Bathed in electronic luminescence, beguiling.

Ultimate playmate, awaits me there
Converging on a world of words,
An ocean away, can do no harm,
Sharing opiates soothing balm,

Blue light playing on unknown features.
Let us share this cyber moon
Pen the words that say you love me,
Night runs in to morning soon.

.Long, supple fingers, play sweet tunes,
Senses reeling, haunting feeling,
Perfect partner found in cyber space,
Draw him in, to my time, and place.

Words of love, and passion’s fire,
Here forever, now beside me,
Play sweet music on my keys,
Let’s backspace into mystery.

Exclamations leave me breathless,
Sit before you in a trance,
Lovingly caress this mother,
Board of our internet romance.

Never leave me for a moment,
Unlimited time is my desire,
Transported to heaven, electronically,
My love and I, burn, in sweet blue fire
