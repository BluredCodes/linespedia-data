Truths

Truths,
Trapped between folds of paper.
That which we choose to ignore,
Not recognise,
Life’s story,
Overlooked realities,
Never lies.

Humans,
Conundrums,
Ensnared,
 In the mesh of existence
Vying for portals
To minds,
And dreams.

Aimless,
Paths travelled,
Deceitfully adhered.
Expectations of fulfilment,
Imprisoned,
Within the fabric,
 Denial.

Revelations,
Inspired,
Release enquiring minds.
Too infrequently,
Barriers fall.
Inhibiting,
Defeating in fear,
Creating yet
Another tear
Of frustration.
