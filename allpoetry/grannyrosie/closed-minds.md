**



Heartache,
Anguish,
Fly through timeless space.
Tears of ignorance,
Forgetfulness
Fall upon face consumed by blankness.
Obscurity.

Brevity of souls chanting songs rejoicing
In pain and misery
Casting
Lots in playgrounds with
Small children where carousels
Take precedence
Over seesaws and swings.

Live ghosts visit
Living rooms of disaster where
The blood
Of angels’ is smeared
On comic books of reality.
Mad magazine takes
Pride of place on the music rest
Of life’s 
Piano forte’.

Jungle beat of drums accompanies
Jazz,
Entangled souls are penetrated by
The thorns of insecurity eliciting
Icicles of plasma
To exude
From minds
Closed.
