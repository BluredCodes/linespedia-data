HE

HE doesn't take tally,
Gently urges me,
Go! Sin no more my daughter,
HE knows I will return,
Wretched, on bended knees,
Beseeching, wailing,
Damning Me.

HE doesnt.
He forgives.

HE knows I undertake each day,
To follow in his gentle way,
HE knows that cries of 'I have sinned',
Mean I am concious of HIM within.

HE loves me.
HE forgives

When doubts and questions wrench my heart,
Anger threatens to tear me apart, from HIM,
HE reaches out HIS hand divine,
Gently takes hold of mine.

HE reaches for me.
HE forgives.

When temptations my soul overcome,
Abandoning HIM for life's games and fun,
He doesn't pay me out,
Or shun.

HE leads me.
HE forgives.

When I am lost, alone and sad,
Believing all around me bad,
HE opens up my eyes to see,
The truth of HIS reality.

HE is my SAVIOUR.
HE forgives.
