THE GREATEST BONDING
 Collecting the tears of the world,
In an ocean of blood, flowing this day,
Container of love, spilling over, with emotion,
Reverberating throughout, evils dark notion.

A festival of joy is about to begin.
The greatest bonding of man, against evil, and sin.
Turning together, with strength in each heart.
Finding GOD'S path, from whence all did start.

Reaching to strangers, regardless this day,
Tearing down the facade blighting our way.
Commitment reborn, compassion does shine.
The message is clear, from our LORD GOD, divine.

Embrace one another, each man, woman, and child,
Prevent adverse reaction, letting Satan run wild.
He rejoices in the dark, the sorrow and pain,
Undermining love in GOD, is his deep, evil aim.

Let the celebration begin, rejoice in our LORD.
Strike evil down, with HIS mighty righteous sword.
Place the sign of the cross on all adversity,
Reject Satan, don't aid in complicity.

Sing songs of salvation,of peace, love of man,
Sing out in clear voices, as loud as we can.
As our world grieves at destruction, for those who depart.
Rejoice! Celebrate this wondrous bonding of heart.
