I WANT

I want to go out bungy jumping
Like all the others do,
I want to ski on snow and water
And walk a mile or two.

I want to leap from aeroplanes
And sail from heights so high,
I want to climb great mountains
And reach out to the sky.

I want to ride on horses,
Motorcycles full of speed,
I want to absail, fly down rapids,
Be first and take the lead.

I want to float on crystal seas,
I want to do it rough,
I want to wander in strange lands,
To never get enough.

I want to do Niagara
In a barrel as one will.
I want to fill my cup right up
And let it over fill.

I want to run and jump and dance,
Alas, it can’t be so
So I will sit and dream of all
The things I’ll never know
