IN THE STILL OF THE NIGHT
 In the still of the night,
Listen!

Soft breathing, of peaceful slumber,
Gentle, rhythmic heart beat.
Night bird calling songs aloft.
Your heart responding, so complete.

In the still of the night,
Wonder!

Warmth enveloping your heart,
Contentment encompassing the mind.
Serenity overspilling, illuminating shadows,
Such wondrous peace, love does find.

In the still of the night,
Reflect!

Steps that led unto this day.
Tortuous, winding life highway.
Lessons learned, fingers burned.
Sadness, sorrow, heart that yearned.

In the still of the night,
Give thanks!

GOD above, such bountiful treasure,
LORD, such gifts beyond all measure,
FATHER, thank you for blessings all,
JESUS, Yes! You heard my call
