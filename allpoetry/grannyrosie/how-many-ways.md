HOW MANY WAYS
 How many ways are there,
To say to you, “I am sorry”?
How can I ever convince you,
You are everything to me?
Where do I go from here?
Without you life is meaningless,
Please answer my calls,
Reassure me that you still care.

Walking a thin fine line,
Months of knowing reality,
Accepting the truth within,
Closing my eyes to the game
Our love the only importance,.
Acknowledgement, it does not matter,
How can it intrude,
As long as you are there?

Ruin my life?
Maybe that is OK.
Ruination may be the way to go.
Reach out your arms,
This is no way to part.
You are my brightest light,
Hold me, say everything is fine,
Otherwise, how can I face the night
