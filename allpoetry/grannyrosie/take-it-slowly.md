TAKE IT SLOWLY
 Please, slow down a bit,
We don’t need to go so fast,
You know I travel slow.
We need to walk together,
On this path we chose to go.
Take in the view, tread gently,
See that mountain over there?
I know together we can climb it,
If we take each step with care.

When the road is rough and bumpy,
Don’t worry, I will hold your hand,
The obstacles are easier for two.
With love as our umbrella,
We can travel throughout this land.
Just guide me, and I will follow you.

If the gradient is steep my love,
Willpower will coax us on.
The pinnacle is closer every day.
Rock falls will never harm us,
Cloaked in the armour of desire.
Follow our hearts, we will not lose the way.

Don’t be tempted by the verdancy
Of the pastures on the slopes,
They indicate an easier way out.
With courage and tenacity,
We will make it to the top,
The success we find together,
Means this love will never stop
