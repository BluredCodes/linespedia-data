FAITH, HOPE, and LOVE
 LOVE, throwing a mystical, ethereal light,
Envelopes this globe, called Earth, tonight.
Outpourings of emotion, from every corner, every heart,
Creating a blessed unity for people, miles apart.

LOVE, A simple word, handed down from above,
“Love thy neighbour, as thy self”, signified by the white dove.
Paul revealed, Love, Faith and Hope, gifts given so free,
LOVE being the greatest, all encompassing, of the three.

HOPE, from ashes of despair, rises on a wing,
Millions, with Angels from above, this chorus sing.
Hope, for the future unity of GOD’S design,
Holding on to Hope, is HIS message, sublime.

HOPE, that no longer will HIS words be banned.
Children of GOD, know this evil deed was planned.
Bring hearts and minds into the fold of GOD,
Hope is a strength that brings it’s own reward.

FAITH, in mankind, in our GOD’S majesty and might,
Bringing the lambs of the universe together in HIS fight.
Faith in HIS mercy, in HIS commitment to us all,
Loss of this attribute has brought about our downfall.

FAITH, HOPE, and LOVE, is the song that tonight we sing,
Reaching across the globe, this message we bring,
Millions join together in this prayer tonight,
JESUS, GIVE US PEACE, FAITH, HOPE and LOVE
WITH YOUR GLORIOUS MIGHT.
