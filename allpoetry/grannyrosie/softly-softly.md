SOFTLY SOFTLY


 Softly, softly, whisper,
Words I long to hear.
Low, and just for me alone,
To this waiting ear.

Gently, gently, touch me,
Sensitive fingers on my face.
Transport me with that gentle touch,
To a heaven filled, special place.

Tenderly, tenderly, caress me,
Hold still this aching heart.
Sooth away this burning longing,
Tearing my soul apart.

Passionately, passionately, kiss me.
Meet these lips, so full of fire.
Meld with them forever,
Feel their burning desire.

Softly, softly, whisper,
Words I long to hear.
Low, and just for me alone,
Draw near dear one, be here
