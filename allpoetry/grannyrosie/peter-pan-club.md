PETER PAN CLUB

Attention!
Annual General Meeting!
Peter pan Club regulations, for review,
Welcome everyone.

Number ONE, 

NEVER GROW UP.

Retain the child,
In purity.

Don’t give in to pressure,
From stuffy “grown ups”,
Else, barred for life you will be.

Number TWO,

NO BOXES

Never confine imagination,
Nor limit curiosity.
Question questions with 

Tenacity,

Unfold the mind,
Outside the perimeters of,

Mediocrity.

DON’T SUCCUMB TO NEGATIVE REASONING

Important item, number THREE

Place no limitation on potential,
Allow reality to live with fantasy.

FOUR.

BE PREPARED TO FLY WITHOUT WINGS

Laugh, let the mind soar with ease.
High above clouds, into deepest canyons,
Through woods, across the seas.

FIVE.

ENCOURAGE BY EXAMPLE.

Let like minds enlist.
Not one potential candidate,
May be overlooked, or missed.

Tho it may sound disrespectful, (AHEM)
Hold a mirror to the world, not the ID.
It is totally, utterly, amazing how reflections,
Illuminate minds well hid.

Regardless of whether lowly, poor,
Academic, or well bred,
Resolve to put body and mind to it,
Will hold you in good stead.

There is never to be a joining fee,
Life time membership is guaranteed.
For those who nurture the child within,
Allow the spirit to be freed.

Well! Tarry not! Its time to fly’.
Lets not conform to meeting etiquette.
There are places to go, treasures to hunt.
We haven’t smiled at one crocodile yet.



Footnote. The song,” Never Smile at a Crocodile”, was used in the pantomime production of Peter Pan, more years ago than a member is allowed to acknowledge having been around. :O)
