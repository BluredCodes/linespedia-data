This Night Not Leave

My love,
Don’t leave me here this night.
Yearnings stir
Within my breast for thy sweet touch.
Alas I know to well
The beast that ravages
My body and
My face,
Has driven thee
Unto the warm embrace of
Another.

A blushing rose,
Magnetic eyes and flowing hair
Imaginations images design
A fairy nymph,
For this
I could not condemn.
Reflections softly cries 
Grief’s lament.

But oh cherished love
Do recall
The binding of hearts,
The majesty
Of ecstasy’s delight
That which verified our existence
Bound in flesh each
To the other.

Oh treasured heart, oh love
Sublime,
Respond to this
Bleeding vessel’s plea
That I
Be thee and thee that half
Of me,
Be mine once more
Before
Life’s curtain is
Finally drawn.

Let me
In slumber feel thy strength
Here in
Our long lost kingdom
Of desire, give
To me this night of angel’s call
One brief glimpse of that
Celestial hall where never satiated 
We did play
And soft
Sweet music permeated each
Day.

Oh let me feel thy heart beat
As tender kisses
I bestow upon
Thy armour of steel.
Let me sigh
With wonder at the tightening
Of your muscles in desire,
Allow me
My sweet love
One night of fire.

Allow my exploration of our love
That lifts us
To heights so far unheard.
Permit
This one last wish of you
I beg
Come forth
Dear love take
My hand
Lead me beyond the gates
Of eternal bliss.
The rekindling of that flame in you
Will be
All that I need
To leave
This mortal frame in peace.

Heart of my heart
Why do you leave me thus?
Is my 
Request beyond the realms
Of thy dear memory.
Revulsion, my decimated body
Strikes at thee,
Reflected in mine eyes be
It death you see.

Look further heart
Beyond the pain
Which scours my very being.
Look deep
Within the vortex of my soul,
Is revealed the beauty,
The love,
The fire that ye
And me created
And both know?

Ah love,
Your step falters at the door,
I see
Realization
In countenance so loved.
Come to my side then lets us
Fly,
Escape this earth together
One more time.

Ah love your stride
Returns with passion’s step
That soft, sweet moan I know
And love so well.
Come to my bosom
Let me hold you
As you weep.

This night we are as we were
Before
My body glistens
In love’s light.
My eyes will lock upon thee
And our combined
Strengths
Will drive
Us over ecstasy’s ravine.
