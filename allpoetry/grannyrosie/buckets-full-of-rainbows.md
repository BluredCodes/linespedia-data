BUCKETS FULL OF RAINBOWS
 Sending you a bucket full of rainbows,
One for every time that you feel blue.
Just lift the lid, gently, cautiously,
One will emerge, especially for you.

Let the colours slide within your mind,
Embrace the beauty boldly flowing free,
Feel the surge of love and life around,
No greater gift could ever be found.

Each thread, every particle of glorious colour,
Is carefully woven with hope, and love,
Spilling all around your special word,
Hugging you softly, fitting you like a glove.

There will even be some left over,
For you to disperse to those you know.
Pass it on, don’t be worried, there are plenty.
Watch it expand, multiply and grow.

See the smiling, happy faces of others,
Sharing this rainbow steeped with love and care,
Should it fade, or lose its brilliance,
There will always be more to share.

Invisible supply of multi-coloured rainbows,
Will be available every day and night,
Transferred from my heart to your own,
To make both our distant worlds shine bright.
