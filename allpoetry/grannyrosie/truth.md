TRUTH
 Living lies within a life,
Brings little self respect.
Weaving webs of fantasy,
Inconsistent with the truth.
When one unable to be true,
To self,
How could it be,
Truth in any shape or form,
Can emerge from thee?

Look carefully within your heart.
Open closed doors within your mind.
Recognition of thyself,
Comes with the truths you find.
Such a simple little word,
Not one to gloss, or hide.
Rules so easy to observe,
Releasing stress, from deep inside..

If memory is good, a little while,
Fabrications may save the day.
Even if, the truth should hurt a bit,
Pursuit of it, the only way.
To live with that, deep down within,
Feel comfort with thy ID,
Take a truth, examine it,
Rather than the lie, you hid.
