MY RAINBOW

I found my rainbow in a dream,
Somwhere, without slumbers call,
It gave to me, my wisdom's page,
Lasting insight from my sage.
It told me never to have fear,
To use my strength,
Fill my cup,
Live,
Rage,
Know just when to turn the page.
To take the pen of love, divine,
Wield it as an act sublime.
Give each ounce of all of me,
In everything I say, and do.
But most of all, to ME be true,
Seek out sunbeams, love anew.
