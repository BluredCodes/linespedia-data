GO JUMP

Not suffering from “Cabbage Syndrome”,
Neither mute, or without a brain.
Not uneducated, lacking intellect,
Tho patience is suffering strain.

A listener, absorbing words,
Reaching, feeling in empathy.
Weaver of words, thinker, doer,
Attributes as found in me.

Better things to do than view the soapies,
Mills and Boone not on my reading list.
Don’t discuss operations, or those next door.
There is nothing much in life that I have missed.

Don’t need to sprout qualifications,
Don’t carry a sign promoting me.
Sure get mad when “”|know it all” ignorance,
Tries to get the best of me.

Mister, take your pathos, and your baggage,
Your academic, superior bloody grin,
Attach all to self, and a block of cement,
Go find a river, and jump in.
