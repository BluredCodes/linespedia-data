**


Constant tears are falling
Torrents of monsoonal rain,
Yet still no way they dissipate
Irreconcilable grief and pain.

Such death there be, deep inside
Decayed joy grow parasitic thread
Invading, destroying memories of beauty
Leaving only bitterness, pain and dread.

Hope has abandoned yet the soul,
Sweet comfort may not be found.
The marsh of desolation decomposing,
Quicksand, rockslides to abound.

Yet tears flow even faster now;
Nay, hear, not in selfish self-pity
Tis yet for those I loved so well whom from
Their giant despair may not break free.

This pain tis for all humanity,
Smites deep within this troubled breast.
Alas, I fear, time doth draw nigh
Satan rejoices in his quest.

Why now doth man turn eyes so blind?
Intelligence? Thought? How can it so be?
So on, the tears cascade, as waterfalls,
Faith, even faith, deserting me.
