SEEKING FULFILLMENT
 LORD, I seek to come unto YOU,
Place my life totally within YOUR care,
Live for YOUR word, and Righteous ways,
Such joy and peace I will find there.

So long on this meandering path,
'On', 'off' relationship with your love,
Procrastination's folly took me,
Far from YOUR light above.

Now I stand naked before YOU,
Beseeching YOU to hear my prayer.
Wash my sins away with the Blood of the Lamb,
With my contrite soul, YOUR presence share.

I will lift my voice to glorify,
YOUR name, forever,and a day.
Walking now YOUR Kingdom path,
The only right, and blessed way.

Guide me FATHER, with YOUR wisdom,
Allow me to overcome evil's plot.
Lift my heart and mind above confusion,
Hold me fast, foresake me not.

Full of imperfection, human frailties,
I ask humbly, take this childlike hand.
Allow me to walk with YOU always,
Let me be fulfilled, at last, I understand. 



This poem is my dedication to CHRIST  JESUS. I was Baptiesed 25/12/01. A wonder, sharing my rebirth with the Birth of my LORD.
