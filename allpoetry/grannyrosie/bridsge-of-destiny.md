BRIDGE OF DESTINY (Mature)
 A beautiful summers morning,
When I first set eyes on you,
Standing there upon a bridge,
Silhouetted by sky's so blue.
Staring into the waters below,
Perhaps admiring the view,
Looking so radiant and beautiful,
I had to get next to you.

Reflecting deep within my soul,
Haunted, from within my heart,
Old crumbling bridge of memories,
My life in tatters, torn apart.
There, a young man standing gazing.
Wish he would go and leave me be.
No time within this shattered life
For on lookers, such as he.

I had to get there beside her.
I drew close by her side.
I felt a trembling body,
Something is wrong I felt inside.
My hand touched hers upon that rail,
She turned and looked into my eyes,
Four brown eyes staring deeply,
My emotions were running high.

A touch upon my trembling hand.
A shiver down my spine,
Looking up, reflecting,
Eyes so brown as mine.
Eyes gentle with compassion,
Somehow, as if this stranger knew,
Dark torment and sorrow,
Deep in my soul, did brew.

A spoken word of kindness
' Hello, I'm looking for a friend,
you look like you do need one,'
I offered her my shoulder, 
Tears swelled up into her eyes.
She accepted my request.
I put my arms around her,
Held her tightly to my chest.

Gentle words of kindness,
Long unknown to such as I.
Overwhelming grief and sorrow,
Tears erupted, within a sigh.
Calming arms embraced me,
Soft soothing whisper in my ear,
No hesitation, or doubts within,
Accepting his comfort, without fear.



I whispered softly in her ear
'Every thing will be all right'
A gentle kiss; upon her scared cheek
I wiped the tears blinding her sight
How could this be, happening to me?
I brushed back her brown hair
I took her hand and lead her away
From the bridge that we did share

Wiping my tears, taking my hand,
He led me from that place.
I could but follow blindly
Such compassion in my strangers face.
Into a quiet courtyard
Under a shady, spreading tree,
He seated me, so courteously,
Spoke quietly, with urgency.

I sat down then beside her,
And touched her olive face veneer.
A forced smile upon her soft lips,
I spoke, 'Come into my arms my dear'.
Cheek to cheek our passion grows,
Our breathing elevates,
This blissful feeling for this girl,
My inner being advocates.

How can it be, these feelings
For a stranger I have just met?
The need to have him closer burns,
This cannot be right, and yet,
Seems I have known him always,
Within his kind eyes I see,
A burning fire that fills my heart,
Ultimate pathway to ecstasy

I gazed into her deep brown eyes,
I then take the initiative,
Slowly moving forward, to her lips,
A fervent kiss is what I give.
To be with this woman so divine,
I felt in my heart was right,
The feeling of reciprocated kiss,
We two lovers did take flight.

Willingly, without a fear,
Taking his hand in mine,
Wings underneath our eager feet,
Transport through unknown time.
A street, and old dark building,
Winding staircase to the top,
Door opening into another world,
To late to let this magic stop.


The room was filled, with a mystique,
Passion, like a flaming heart would shine.
I took her hand and I led her to a bed,
Where soon our love would entwine.
I place my hands upon her cheeks,
Drawing her close to me,
This ravishing beauty that I held,
Would be mine, submissively.

Emotions flared within me,
He led me by the hand,
Wild, hot passion upon my lips,
Returned, with eager demand.
Unleashed fire within me rose,
Arching body met his flesh,
Free of hesitation or reproach,
Two bodies met, enmeshed.

Modesty was not, or virtue,
The last piece of clothing fell,
My Venus, my goddess of love stood naked,
As hot vehemence inside me swell.
We stood in the silence of this moment,
My emotions just won't wait,
Now it's time to show my love,
Your virginity now I take.

Unable to contain desire,
Welling from deep within.
Unclothing each other tenderly
Standing naked, in front of him.
His taut body aglow with need,
His lips full of passion's desire.
Hands seeking, finding pleasure,
Lost within soul consuming fire

Gentle caresses of her skin,
Excites me, with a passionate need.
Her breasts swell up from tender touch,
Soft sighs, and moans express this deed.
Lying her down upon the bed,
Locked in a lovers embrace,
Fervent kisses as hands explore,
What was once, a forbidden place.

Carried away by this madness at play,
Exploring such passion and bliss.
Needing the heights of this heavenly night,
Hunger demanding with each searing kiss.
Fingers exploring, impatient, yet kind,
Muscles hard, taut, burning with fire.
Flowing and swaying in sensual delight,
Giving all of myself, with passionate desire



Surrendering to her hot passion,
She offered herself to me.
My hardness penetrates her gently,
This felt so heavenly.
Two bodies undulating in unison,
Burning passions of sheer delight,
Emotion's running wildly through them,
Lovers orgasmicaly taking flight.

Wild cries of pain, and passion,
Erupted throughout this night,
Yet still my body hungered,
Seeking consummation of delight.
Ethereal splendor possessed me,
Within passionate union sublime.
Would that this passion could last,
Forever, until the end of time

Consumed by overwhelming beauty,
Encapsulated within her love nest
Volcanic lava burning so hot,
Verging on the crest,
As if we were clairvoyant,
We knew this was true love,
Like telepathic lovers,
Was more of what I was thinking of.

His very manliness leading me up,
Within reach of heavens door,
Yet still this body searching,
Seeking satiations outpour.
Ultimate ethereal bliss descending,
Body pulsating in sheer delight,
Voyage of discovery with my stranger,
Final crescendo, into mystic, healing light.

What was it about this woman,
Which had taken me to this height, 
Contained in sensual satisfaction,
As I laid with her this night?
Total exhaustion left us, gasping for breath,
From our chance love making incident.
Lying in silence, still connected as one,
Off to sleep we lovers went.

Gazing at his sleeping form,
His handsome body, glowing in the night,
Arms, still holding me secure,
Passions peaceful shimmering light.
The bridge that should have brought me death
Has given me this stranger dear.
I know deep within my healing soul,
His love will eternally, hold me near

This beautiful woman who lies with me,
With a velvety skin olive complexion,
She has no idea why I was there,
Thinking it was for her own protection.
Sweetheart I was on that bridge,
To jump was my intention,
Something drew me near to you,
Divine heavenly intervention.
