AT PEACE 
 
Storybook eyes,
Mirroring truths,
Absolute beauty,
Love offered,
Shared,
Unconditionally.

Curtains parting,
Veils of mystic shed.
Souls revealed,
Anointed.
Healed,
Once bled.

Hands, aware, gentle.
Caress, soothe, quiet.
Holding tenderly,
Within love.
Eternally,
.Never to part.

Pledging of souls,
Enmeshed intrinsically.
Distance no barrier,
Spirits call,
Converge, rejoice.
Total surrender.

Serenity secured,
Apprehensions discarded.
Life revisited.
Awareness reciprocal,
Two spirits,
Soar on wings.
At peace.
