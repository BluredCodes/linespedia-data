ENDLESS DANCE



Music softly reaches we two,
Isolated, upon a dais,
Adrift from embers of humanity,
Paradise inhabited by only us.

You take my hand, within your grasp,
Such strength, pure tenderness,
Ultimate intimacy begins,
Within this endless dance.

A feather touch, upon my face,
Warm sweet breath there, in my ear,
Arms surround this pliant body,
Arching to be near.

Magical, gentle heat arises,
For we two, there are no surprises,
Passion plays her powerful game,
Our bodies move, one and the same.

Gently rocking, as the motion,
As a ship, on placid ocean,
Following the melody of love,
Through sun drenched clouds, to stars above.
.
Each step in time, choreographed,
By the wonder of desire,
Forever on, forward,
Fanning eternal fire.

Bodies intertwining,
Keeping with the rhyme.
Feel our souls now melding,
Emotions so sublime.

My breasts pressed hard, against your chest,
Orchestrated tempo now increased,
Nearness now that does defy,
Concept of separate entity.

Thus, through life, let us make this dance,
Souls coiled, and tempered true.
My heart, my soul, my all, my love,
This forever dance, I dance with you
