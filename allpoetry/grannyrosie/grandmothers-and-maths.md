Defeatism's dealing a death blow,
To Division,Subtraction and 'that',
My attitude indicates ignorance,
Of square root, and midline format.

Maximise,Minimise..Equations,
Sines,Tangents Logarythums...it true,
Leave me in a state of confusion.
Pray tell me...what should I do?

Ive swatted and sweated and searched for,
The key to unlock my mind,
So I can absorb all the wonders of maths,
That lurk in the depths......
I cant find...a single mathmatical equation,
To this problem that worries me thus,
Sooo its back to the books,
With the hope that the clue,
Rests on the difference between minus and plus
