HEARTS IN UNISON
 Kneeling softly before you,
Feel gentle hands upon my head.
Slowly undoing the ribbon,
Gently tugging that loose thread.

Easing out long tresses,
Fingers combing through with ease.
Little knots lovingly undone,
Fingers by my ears now tease.

Stroking, caressing, loving,
Feeling your breath within my hair,
Smelling sweet fresh cleanliness,
Gently sighing with deep care.

Total bliss, relaxing, enjoying,
Precious, intimate moments of the heart,
Hands envelope my face now,
Eyes lock, small tremors start.

Warm breath tickles around neck and ears,
Delighting, bringing forth blissful smiles.
Whisper my name, touch fingers to my lips,
Igniting sparks, my soul beguiles.

No need for further fulfilment,
All we need is close, right here.
Lips meet in jubilant passion,
Seeking, searching, without fear.

Such tenderness we share each day,
Within this blessed union we have found.
Shhhhh! Listen to the melody I hear,
Two hearts in unison, rapid, longing sound.
