Assumptions,
Dangerous,
Undermining integrity.
How dare “people” just assume,
Answers,
Conclusions,
Deductions.
Clairvoyant, I presume?

Facts,
Succinct,
Explicit,
Honest,
Required before assumptions make.
Basing assessment of others on assumptions,
Unacceptable mistake.

Frame of reference,
Values,
Mores,
Beliefs,
Differ between each human being.
How can one assume they know all,
Without seeing as other eyes have seen?

Categorising,
Dispatching,
Labelling,
Classifying,
Rather an inane process,
As in searching for the “norm”.
Should one assume they have found the label,
Assumptions,
Are not even warm.
