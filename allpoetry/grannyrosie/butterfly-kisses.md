BUTTERFLY KISSES
 Lying snug, fuzzy and warm,
Great big comfy bed,
Know I’m ‘sposed to be asleep,
Dreaming awake, of you, instead.
Don’t have to close these eyes,
To imagine you are here.
Your warmth, hot naked flesh,
Blazing reality my dear.

Caressing fingers touch me,
Body responds with fire.
Face, arms, torso, tingle,
Provoking ultimate desire.
Responding to naked demand,
Holding you close, so tight,
Into the very womb of nature,
Passion’s light burns bright.

Butterfly kisses tickle on my cheek,
Signify beauty, fulfilment that we seek.
Totally cohesive, bond so pure and true.
This is no dream, my reality is you.
Drawing me faster, further down,
Bottomless canyon so deep,
Elation, ecstatic wanton urge,
Within my precious love you steep.

Blissful release from restless demons,
Soft gentle moans, heartrending sigh,
Blessed, ultimate, absolute conclusion,
Sated, within each others arms we lie.
Heights or depths, what does it matter?
Our passion leads us where it will,
Who could deny this mutual need?
Into the future, confident, still.
