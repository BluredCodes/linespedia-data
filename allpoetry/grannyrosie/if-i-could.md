If I Could 
If I could tell you ..in simple words 
What your presence in my life means 
I would speak of exotic places, desert islands, plantations of roses, meandering streams 
If I could hold you long enough 
To fulfill my wants and needs 
It would take the ages that created the universe, eternity, or all the creative seeds of man 
Since time began 
If I could say to you in uninhibited style 
The pleasure I find in your presence, your touch, your smile 
My words would bring a blush to the very earth 
From whence came my words 
If I could fulfill all of my needs, perpetuate all of the deeds that obsess my waking hours 
We two would join in unison ,so long, that existence itself would become a song 
Of pleasure 
Pain 
Relief 
Joy 
Exotica 
Delight 
To you I yield completely.... and your dominant might 
Opens my house of pleasure 
If I could lean upon you when I am weak 
And in your compassion let my hurts steep 
If you were there beside me all life through 
Influencing all I think and do 
If I could take from you and thence be strong 
Then be it far from bad... and far from wrong 
That you could lean, and need ,and hope ,and say 
In any kind of word ...or kind of way 
Your hopes, your dreams your wants, your prime desires 
Just ask 
need 
indicate that which you will 
I will comply 
Because I can
