Little drops of happiness,
Falling with the rain,
Sealing now, this aching heart,
Ready to love again.

Brightly coloured rainbows,
Filling every corner of the day.
Knowledge that you truly care,
Showing the way.

Tinkling, trickling waterfall,
Touching pools so pure and deep.
Invading hidden recesses,
Lulling,  to blissful sleep.

Gentle call of songbird,
Echoing through the mind.
Such peace, tranquillity,
Within your love, I find.

With sweet gentleness, you reach to me,
No demands, no fuss, no expectation there.
In everything you say and do,
Showing how much you care.

So shy, yet bold and loving,
Maybe this time, for me, and you,
Something worth having will materialise.,
Together, we will see this dream come true.
