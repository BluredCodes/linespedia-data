FANTASY'S PLOT 
 
Fantasy devised a plot,
Playing submarines,
Midst sensuous aromatic oils,
Tub for two, within my dreams.
Passion slowly rising,
Bubbles bursting ‘round,
Jets seductively massaging,
Rapid breathing sound.

Hands and bodies clashing,
Washing need away.
Lips meeting, wet and hungry,
Games that lovers play.
Soothing, soft caressing,
Exploring fingers seek,
Within this world of fantasy,
Dreamers spell will never break.

Gentle groans of want and need,
Rise to fulfilments height.
Fantasy, dear faithful friend,
Rescue from bleak, lonely nights.
