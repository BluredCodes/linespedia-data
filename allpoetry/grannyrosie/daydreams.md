DAYDREAMS

A whisper of a pot of gold,
Buried treasure beneath the sea,
A pirate cave, full of spoils,
Waiting there, for me.

Breathless journey on a carpet,
Across far distant shores,
Jewelled city beneath a mountain,
Enchanted island, where time does pause.

Multi coloured heaven,
Where clouds don’t hide the day,
Stairway leading upward,
Where cherubs sing, and play.

A dashing Knight in armour,
Chariots of gleaming gold,
A lake of sparkling emeralds,
Pure love, to have, and hold.

Sunrise on the desert,
Sunset across the sea,
Moon’s regal journey through the night,
A velvet beach, down by the sea.

Ah Daydreams! Such a blessing,
We can choose all things to see,
Or select a black, barren, starless night,
Be doomed eternally.
