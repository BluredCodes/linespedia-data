TRANQUIL MOMENTS
 Come sweet love, here unto me,
Relax, close those weary eyes I see.
Lay your tired head upon my breast.
Release your troubled mind my heart, rest.

Leave behind all strain and sorrow,
Dwell not a moment on tomorrow.
Take this quiet journey to tranquillity.
Shh! Silence! Say not a word to me.

Dream my love, in this precious time,
Cradling you, in these arms of mine.
Set all the worries of the world behind you,
Allow fanciful thoughts to link, as lovers do.

At peace dear one, forget all woe and trouble.
Let no anxiety burst this magic bubble.
Feel warm, loving lips upon your brow,
Grasp serenity offered, here and now.

Tenderness of touch, upon your troubled face.
Haven for both of us, in this wondrous place.
At last, sweetheart, you tremble, breath a sigh,
Bid all unwelcome problems, thoughts, goodbye.
