**



Beginnings from the birth of creation,
Down through the corridors in time
Tis written,
Prophets will come amongst yea
Forecasting,
Proclaiming
Vehement.

Aye true, so many
Harken to admonition
Foretold,
Warning so plain to see,
Those will come in guise
Of knowledge, wisdom;
Antichrist of Satan
They shall be.

So true he walks amongst us,
Prompting, narcissistic prophet of sin,
Denouncing good,
Courage,
No accolades to purity
From him.

Be well aware all goodly souls.
Turn backs on Mephistopheles’ disciple
Stalking
Through all many a places 
Wearing any
Of a million faces,
Casting lots to see
Good fall.

Evil monster
Abandon’s tool
