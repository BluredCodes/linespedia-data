SPEAK TO ME
 Speak to me of love,
I will reply of pain.
For love, is as to one,
Yet another’s gain.

Speak to me of hope,
Lessons to be learned.
Down within the depths,
Soaring like a bird.

Speak yet then, of faith,
Belief to carry on,
Swirling ‘round in eddies,
Bright sword, into beyond.

Then speak of peace, tranquillity.
A noble thought it is, indeed.
Waging wars within the mind,
Blissful silence when in need.

Talk of life, the path we tread,
Journey to the unknown.
Find love, hope, faith, and peace within.
Know truly, you are home.
