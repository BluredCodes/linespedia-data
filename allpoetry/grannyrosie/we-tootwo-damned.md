WE TOO/TWO DAMNED

Young,
Fragile,
Trapped.
A world of depression,
Anxiety,
Fear.
Leaning, forever leaning,
On hollow walls.
Buckling,
Under strain.

Reaching,
Pleading,
Tears.
Exploding vulnerability,
Insecurity,
Despair.
Vertebrae disintegrate,
Collapse of props,
What hope?
Children struggle.

Yelling,
Denying,
Oblivious.
Reality denying recognition.
Self impregnated cesspool,
Socially,
Unaccepted,
Scourge.
Cut, overdose,
Who will notice?
Flip a coin.

Gender,
Age,
Race.
No barrier to cruel master,
Striking unexpectedly.
Forward,
Going back,
Chemical restraints, inhibitors,
Penetrated by persistence.
Hear them,
Protest,
In anger.
