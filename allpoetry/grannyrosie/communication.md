Communication

Silence,
Pleas of desperation ignored,
Haunting scenario,
Alienation revisited.
Such small necessities of life,
Communication.

Listen.
Hear these spoken words,
Absorb meaning , impact..
Deafness of the mind,
Impenetrable barrier,
Prohibiting pathways to,
Communication

Respond.
Without threat, entreating.
Games of cat and mice,
Long since, unnecessary.
Destructive silence echoes,
Disintegrating lines of,
Communication.

Articulate,
Reveal you in true form,
Orate the id that cloisters.
Joy is non-existent,
In backs that turn.
Bonds break in silence.
Communicate.

Scream.
Yell, shriek, cry.
Use right of choice,
Project control as owned.
Unlock doors.
Tear down walls.
Communicate.

Speak.
Throw sorrow in a melting pot.
Imbibe in strength,
So long forgot
Hold hard to reality call.
Else, let the curtain fall, on,
Communication
