**



Surrounding tin fence in elegance.
Tendrils reaching into thin air.
Love heart leaves,
Olive green
Caress
Peep out from joints here
And there.

Fine veined curled trumpets
Quiver in dawn’s first light,
Un-wreathed power dwelling within
Bright blue electric petronels
Shivers in delight,
Acknowledging yet another day
As Sol sends forth his might,
As t’were some signal to the earth.

Butterflies take to
Gossamer wing
With echoes of trumpet,
They who
Jealously sing nodding
Heads as in tune
With life
Revitalised 
All around.
Blue of sky and flower
Combine,
Breeze sighs
Such inspirationally
Optimistic 
Sound.
