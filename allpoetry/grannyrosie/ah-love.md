AH! LOVE!

Desire yearns for a song of love,
Soft, sweet, gentle to the ear.

AH! LOVE!

All defeating,
Soul depleting,
Mind exploding,
Gonad goading.

LOVE.

Heart aches for reassurance of love.
Pulses quicken with resounding beat.

AH! LOVE!

Moonlit places,
Flushing faces,
Pressure growing,
Life seed sowing.

LOVE.

Arms longing for the feel of love.
Reaching out to hold so fast.

AH! LOVE!

Senses roaring,
Closer drawing,
Embraces locking,
Muscles mocking.

LOVE.

Eyes straining for the sight of love,
Searching through each lonely night.

AH! LOVE!

Causing tears of joy and sorrow,
Desperate there is no tomorrow,
Radiating happiness’ glow,
Answer to all ills we know.

LOVE
