Green Tree Frog.

Tongue flickering restlessly,
Black beetle becomes main course,
Fluorescent yellow eyes gaze lazily,
Contemplating dessert.

Gentle spasm, muscle, sinew,
Giant hop, to reach the top,
In a pot plant proudly sitting,
Against mosquito, wits pitting.

Suction pads on window glass,
Awaiting new prey to pass,
Pounce!, long protuberance flicks
Far too accurate, far too quick

Brightest green, a thing of beauty,
Hold so gently, it wont harm,
Hear the croak of reprimand,
Needs to hurry, legs aflurry.

Espied!, A female green tree frog,
Astride moss covered fallen log,
In unison they hop away,
Green Tree Frog music to play?
