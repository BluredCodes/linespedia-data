**


Haunting silence
Is echoing whispered words
Of doubt
And sorrow
Someone
Left behind
Within
The portals of my mind.

Who
Could it be
That caused this cacophony,
Deafening row
Making
No sound yet
Overpowering,
Invading,
Pervasive crippling
Me.

Shivers
Of expectations in
Confrontation
With demon thought.
Unspoken
Words form on lips to spectres
Of a midnight
Dream.


Irrigation
Of tears cascading,
Torrents
Of all the what
Might have beens, rendering
The darkness with
Oppressive weight
Outside
The realms of
Reality.

Let the dirge continue yet a while,
Allow
The cortege to reach
Destination of
Abandoned.
Seep
Within the maw of self pity
Rid
The system of
Bitterness and
Bile.
