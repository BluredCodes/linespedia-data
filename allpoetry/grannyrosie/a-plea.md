Ignorant, pleading innocence,
I come, yet not to beg
I ask allow sanity
To permeate your head.
No offence ever intended,
No conspiring to cut you down,
Situation now intolerable
Forsaking smiles for sorrowful frown.

Relationships so valued, dear
Should not be threatened by trivialities,
Stop, inspect truths as presented,
Response created insurmountable miseries.
We never realise what it is we have
Until it is gone, no-longer waiting there,
Reach inside your mind, re-call her love,
Please let her know how much you care.

Never in a thousand years
Would the lizard or she who is me
Set about in thoughtless destructiveness,
Creating this chaos I see.
So unwell now for so long
Plus sitting in self pity’s pit
Have been unable to let ink flow
Or read others, or comment
Save for a little bit.

No slur was ever my intent
Neither from my dearest son,
Your reaction I cannot comprehend,
Bringing so much good, undone.
The only thing now betrayed,
 Is your stony, unforgiving heart,
Please take another look at this situation,
Reassure her, you will never part.This is a special poem, and the one for whom it is written, will recognise what it is about. I am so saddened by the events that led to this writing.
