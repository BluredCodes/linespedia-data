Love's Daydreams.
 Floating in love’s daydreams,
Refuse to acknowledge now’s reality.
Thoughts of you, creeping in,
All else, oblivious to me.

Automation without you.
Robotic responses to duty’s call.
My love, this heart is with you.
You are my mind, my soul, my all.

Fragments of you invade each thought,
Intrusive, devouring, exploding
Forget to perform simple, mundane tasks,
My love for you is showing.

Recalling all your tender words,
They invade the efforts of my muse.
You walk, sit, and lie with me.
I offer no excuse.

Gazing at the PC screen,
Come alive when you sign in.
Eyes sparkle, breathless, alive,
Cant think for my hearts din’.

I only know I love you,
With every inch of this mind and soul.
Cant wait until you are here once more,
Engulfing me, making me whole.
