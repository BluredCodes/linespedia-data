OCEAN IN MY HEART
 Roar of the surf in the distance,
Knowledge that I have reached my goal,
Aware of the salt in the driving wind,
Uplifting me, making me whole.

Espied, the breakers upon the shore,
Crashing down on rocks in might.
Hurrying feet, carry me down,
Shifting sands hasten the flight.

Arms high to heaven in supplication,
Salt spray misting down on my face.
Hair dancing with joy as each tendril,
Comes alive, responds to this glorious place.

March boldly into the depths of the sea,
Feel the surge of the tide on my thighs.
Inhale the beauty of ocean and sky,
Sink to the depths, with a satisfied sigh.

Emerge, let the heavens caress me.
Salt caked on these eyelids and lips,
Come alive to the life of the ocean,
Rise, buoyancy there, neath my hips.

Plunge again, yet again, in this wonder,
Never enough to satiate this greed.
Affinity with life abounding here,
Fulfils every desire, every need.
