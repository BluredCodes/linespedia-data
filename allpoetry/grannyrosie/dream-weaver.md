DREAM WEAVER
 
AH! Weaver of Dreams, what do you have,
To ease my troubled mind this night?
Where shall you take me, in my slumber?
How will you provide excitement, and delight?

How I long for far off people, places.
Dream Weaver, will you take me there?
Whisk me on a soaring magic carpet,
Create romance, adventure, because I dare?

Guide me through slumbers passion,
Take me deep within the bowels of earth.
Lift me high into the glorious heavens,
Fill my cup with love, laughter, and mirth.

No sadness mind, no wretched tears,
Nothing that will blight each brand new day.
Dream Weaver, build upon my character,
Make dreams of beauty, adventure, play.

Add hope, faith, new awareness, positive outlook,
To the world around me, everywhere.
Enlighten me with words of wisdom,
Empower my heart to share, and care.

I am prepared, Master of Dreams.
Take this hand outstretched to you.
When I awake, the other side of dreaming,
Will you make my dreams come true?
