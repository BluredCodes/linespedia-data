LOVES SONG
 Sing love, such a simple melody,
As you have sung to me before.
Soft and lilting, caressing my heart,
Lifting my spirits to heaven’s door.

Love, weave your song around me,
With harmony, rhythm and flair.
Sweet gentle notes of love echoing,
As a dreamy breeze upon the air.

Take the beat a little faster,
This heart will quicken to the pace.
Lift urgency within the depths,
Pulses will respond, and race.

See this flushed face before you,
Know love, this hunger that you see.
Dedicated to you, always, alone,
Untamed wild beast, at last set free

Increase the tempo, add bass and drums,
Create a sensuous, erotic, steamy, beat.
Vibrations up and down this spine,
Matched with tapping, restless feet.

Ah love!, build to a crescendo now,
Fill all my senses with your fire,
Sway with me across the stage of life,
The music plays, loves sweet desire.
