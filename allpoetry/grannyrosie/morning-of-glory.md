MORNING OF GLORY
 Woke to find you beside me today.
Happiness invades my heart.
Now each day forever,
Nevermore to be apart.

Your arms clasped around me closely,
Head on the pillow, next to mine,
Even breathing, such contentment,
Made my eyes light up, and shine.

You murmured softly in your dreams,
Your hand slid down my thigh.
Warmth and nearness enveloping me,
I blessed your name with a thankful sigh.

You must have sensed me darling,
Your eyes opened slowly, dreamily,
That cheeky face broke into a grin,
Then, ”Hey! What’s this in here with me”?

Stretching, arching your body nearer,
Nibbling at my neck and ear.
Running fingers through my hair,
Making your intentions clear.

Finding out now about Morning Glory,
Why that flower opens with the break of day.
Fulfilled by the knowledge deep within,
Our love will always stay this way.
