GIANT DESPAIR

Friends with faces, there be none,
Battle's victories, never won,
Music of discordant harmonies,
Sol aloft, no rays to bear on thee
Turbulent oceans stayed, there power ceased,
Leaping rainbow patterns, black intensified,
Grey the colour now, of hope and pride.

Chaos splits the air with diamond blade,
Remains there, some faint vestage of life?
From whence this ,monstrous reversal, of all things sanctified,
Within the brest of man, survival denied.

This universe, a thing of lava's breath,
The road to life's own hell, without rest,
Sleep denied,soul withered, festerings weeping,
The Gods in Heaven, dead, or sleeping.
Evil casting lots, with evils hoards,
Flesh dessicated, bleeding not allowed,
Lifes blood drain, becomes a cloud,
Pursuit by mocking skeletal fiend,
Despair, unlock my prison, let it end.
