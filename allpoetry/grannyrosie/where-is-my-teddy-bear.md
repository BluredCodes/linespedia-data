title: "Where is my Teddy Bear"
writer: "Grannyrosie"

Someone stole my teddy bear,
I know I sat him down.
There.
On that chair.
Before I went to town.

He didn’t go all by himself,
He only ever goes out with me.
Who took him?
Was it you?
Did anybody see?

It must have been somebody,
Very strong, and tough.
Coz my Teddy is big,
And heavy.
His hair is thick, 
Sort of rough.

He never goes away from me,
He always greets me with a smile.
He is faithful,
Such a dear fun bear.
Teddy,
Will you be back,
In a little while?

I know you are out there Teddy.
Maybe you are just lost.
Stay where you are,
I will look for you.
Or,
Ask someone for directions,
Someone you can trust.

Please Teddy,
Find the right direction.
Find your way back to me.
I’m lonely,
Without you here,
By my side.
As lonely as a lonely girl can be.
