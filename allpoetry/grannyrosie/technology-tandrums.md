TECHNOLOGY TANDRUMS

I want to throw a brick, into it's lifeless face,
I want to see that mother board, strewn all 'round the place,
I want to take that printer, and shove it up a tree,
And as for that key-board, deary, deary , me.

I hate it, but I love it,why does it bring me pain?
Every time I really need it, the thing is down again,
I just can't live without it,I could punch it's lights right out,
At least if it were human, could don my gloves , give it a clout.

The bane of my existence,that necessary fiend,
The one that I'm addicted to, will kill me in the end.
I know that I'm a junky, but why should it have to be,
Just when I have settled in, the thing drops out on me?

'I am very sorry Madam', the telephone voice did say,
We have technical difficulties, should be right within a day.
Dont they know that I can't wait that long?I need my fix right now,
My system is downloading, refresh don't work nohow.

Maybe another server, would be a good idea,
But from listening to good friends of mine,
I think they all are queer.
All those precious dollars, for a service I can't get,
I need to go online right now, I want my internet
