WICKEDNESS

I'm basking in my wickedness,
It has the best of me,
No one could ever appreciate,
How wicked I can be.
I'm wearing a grin of pure release,
From reins that did hold tight,
The cord that held me broken,
I'm really wicked tonight.

See this silly leering countenance?
See this happy bouncing step?
Reckon I look wicked?
You ain't seen nothin yet.
I'm plotting and I'm planning wickedness,
Even as I speak,
I'm sure my wickedness career,
Is about to reach it's peak.

I might dabble in some voodoo,
Or take up belly dancing,
Come to think about it hard,
Not too old to go romancing.
Whichever way it takes me,
This star is burning bright,
I think I'll follow blindly,
This wickedness, I like
