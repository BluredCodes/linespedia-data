Desire



Life pulsates in the caverns of my soul 
And cries like some wild bird to be released 
Red fires, and mist, and strange unnatural glows 
Reduce my vision to one small dark hole 
Gradually streaks of sometimes smoke 
And minute sparks... then dim faint light 
Appear around that orifice before me there 
And vision is returned 
But sight revitalised 
A reawakening occurs within my breast 
A mad tempestuous storm erupts 
I am divest of all illusions 
Inhibitions 
Prudity
and Shame 
I seek fulfillment... and cry out the name 
Of that which serves me best- or worse 
This passion thing that drives a mind insane 
This bestial instrument of mans great shame 
This act of love... or lust... or greed ...or sin 
This untamed soul... Devouring monster from within 
I am it's servant- my cruel master I await 
And smile with bliss in contemplation of that fate 
Await the divine one daughter of the night 
Await the grandiose majesty and might- 
Of all that makes you female, woman, handservant, peasant, queen, divine being, slave 
All this and more the holy fire will kindle in the pit of my desire 
I know it 
Will not fight it 
Why should I? 
For this act of procreation I was made 
I am divine,of gods,here for the sake of this great fire 
that makes a man a king....a king a liar 
I will take the cup that serves me best 
Then drink my fill,or more- and earn my rest 
And if the price I pay should be too high 
If eternal damnation is the fire of lust...so let it be 
For lust,and love and fire are born in me.
