Such Sweet Evilhello motheryes i can hear youyour words are a molassesclinging to my mindyour nobility unforgottenyour patience undeservedas i lie by this womanher that you call temptressand whorei am reminded violently againyou who are gonestill reign supreme in my lifeas the bottle is passedi think only of youand again as she begs me for moremy thoughts are only of youmy mother and friendmy guide and protectorlove may know no boundsbut i am of a diferent breedmy mind knows the truthbut succeeds at its own endsi need this motherthe naughty things that become my memoriesif i am to be deemed evili know your love will remainso allow me thisthe pleasures of my fleshand allow me to get back to this evilfor it is my eviland it is such sweet evilOur Mother teach us the righteous ways of our youth, but realistically, how many of us hold to them to the letter. huh?
