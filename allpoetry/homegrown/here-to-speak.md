Here To Speakthe womanthe doorthe same old dreamher wordsthe picturesfantasy and mirththe wanderlust of it allfollowyoung sonhold not to old idealsfollow herlearn from herlisten to hershe is your soulshe is here to speakI despise the fact that so many people try to live their lives through their children. I know I am guilty on some level, but I truly want my children, to just be, my children.
