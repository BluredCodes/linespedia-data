nothing is secret, no, nothing taboolife can be shared, and love, expressedlies are transparent, and trivial matterscheating means nothingonly the means to a wholly physical endnever are there questionsnothing breaks the age old bondwe will not out live the companionshipnor will we out last the anguishthat can existbetween two heartsHearts are immortal. Not a part of the body, just a part of the greater scheme of things.
