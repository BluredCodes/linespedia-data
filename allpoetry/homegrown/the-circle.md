The Circlelove begets hateas mother begets childthe circle continueslifes joysand sorrowsthe circle continueslife is its own fulfillmentdo as you pleasedo as you are abledo unto othersthe circle continuesThis one is quite old. I was almost 13 at the time. It blows, I know, but I thought someone might like it just the same.
