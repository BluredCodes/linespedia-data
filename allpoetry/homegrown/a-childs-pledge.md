A Child's PledgeI pledge allegiance to the flagof a country torn to shredsand to the destruction for which it standsone nation, under warirreparable,no liberty,no justice, no hope......amenDoes anyone else feel that this is where we are heading by our allowance of leadership by feudal minded bigots? When will we wake up and put the country before the flag instead of under it.
