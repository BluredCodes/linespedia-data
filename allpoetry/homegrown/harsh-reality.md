love hurts like hellit always has, ever since Edenpain is a necessary part of lovethe truth in love lies in the riskif you are not willingto risk the dull achethe everlasting pain of rejectionthen you do not truly loveit's not wrong, just a factGod loves us, right?well, he is willing to give us a choicehe is willing to risk itcan we do any less and call it love?'The way to love anything is to realize it might be lost.' G. K. Chesterton...Quote contributed by MaureenJust a 3am quicky.
