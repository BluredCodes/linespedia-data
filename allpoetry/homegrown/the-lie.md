The LieI told it onceand told it welli spoke it only to myselfand i believed every wordso decieptful was iso cruel to make believeto call me thattook all my strengthdrained me spirituallyemotionallyphysicallythe day that i saidself, you areunlovableIf you ever feel this way, remember, one CANNOT live without Love. And if you are alive enough to feel this way, then... well, you get the point.
