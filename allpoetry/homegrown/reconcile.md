Reconcilego back to sleep my sonall is wellreturn to the dreamthe bright lit fieldsmorning gloriesdaffodilsbutterflies and poesiesreconcile to the dreamWrote this the day my son, Samuel was born. He wasn't breathing when he entered this world, but he seems to be doing a lot of it now...... while he's dreaming...
