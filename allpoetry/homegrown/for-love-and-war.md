For Love  and Warthe scars of love are dark and boldand carry lies left yet untoldher sorrow in the midnight pondthe pain one feels within a songthe pain will never go awaythrough many loves it's here to stayno recompense or exit herefor all the pain you hold so dear'oh, please let go', your silent screamit comes again in late night dreamsso snuggle with your agonyand let the empty aching be.One of my only 'rhyming' works. Just thought i'd get a little input, thanks.
