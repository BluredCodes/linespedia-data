i love you so much
i want you forever
such a sweet, sweet lie
forever is a myth
love is a game
i, a pawn
forgiveness is unimportant
truth, unnecessary
marry me
why
i want love, and joy
peace and tranquility
and though i know the truth
i have heard the lie
and believing is easierTruth here, look out!!
