Swimming Through Time

memories, like dust, blow through my mind
soaking me like a flood
leaving me gasping
for, the precious air of the now
violent turbulence is my fear as i sleep
the dreams are pleasant
that is my curse
forgetting the good things is my nightmare
a deep breath, i take
plunging into the depths
and swimming through timeThis is a "late-at-night-not-quite-awake-type-thing". Any and all comments will be welcomed
