its monday again5:57pm, any second now...yes...   yes...YES!!! she's here, she's here!my heart leaps for no real reasonI feel no tangible attractionbeauty she does possessa wicked sense of humor does she call her owni remember our first encounter'seventy-five cents, please' nice voiceand now, to have her in my class'princess' her shirt readsbelievableI have a mate, and i am happyis it wrong to see in this maiden such beautyi have no intentions that concern herbut the form that she bring to the roomthe aura of herfragrantly flowing to a seat just before mine'hello'four words so farone hyphenated, five?a friend perhapsfrustrating but livable, as a dreamout of sight she is out of mindbut still a beautiful frustrationThis is a girl that works at the corner market and is also in my anthropology class. No attraction, but I do see in her a natural beauty that is rare in these parts.
