---
title: "Demi-Lonely"
writer: "LlamaX"
slug: "demi-lonely"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/49380-Demi-Lonely-by-LlamaX"
---

I've got handfuls of friends,
with their beautiful charms.
But I've not someone who
I can hold in my arms.
I've got handfuls of friends,
who can cheer when I grieve.
But I've not someone who
through my oneness can cleave.
I've got handfuls of friends,
For their lives I'd give mine.
But I've not someone who
On whose heart I can shine.
I've got handfuls of friends,
My sadness they atone.
But I've not got someone,
As for now, I'm alone.
