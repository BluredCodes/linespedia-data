---
title: "Everyday Messengers"
writer: "danih"

Protecting from danger.
Giving strength and courage.
Helping us to find
Our destination.
Helping us
To Live and Love
Teaching us to laugh
On worst days,
How to smile,
If we cry,
Hugging us
Drying our tears.
When broken down
They are there
Putting us together 
Holding us up.
Where are these protectors?
Don’t you see them?
Seen everyday,
They are people around you
Caring and loving.
You are an Angel,
I am yours.
