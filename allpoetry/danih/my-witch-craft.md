title: "My Witch Craft"

Let me dive,
Let me dive into your eyes.
Let me swim,
Let me swim into your dreams.
Let me float,
Let me float within your heart.
Let me stay.
Anchor me,
Anchor me to your soul.
Love me and never let me go.
Let me love,
Let me leave you.
Let me trick and knowingly deceive you.
Let me cheat.
Let me watch you cry.
Admit defeat.
Let the sorrow never die.
Let me dive,
Let me dive into your eyes.
