---
title: "Best Friend"
writer: "danih"
slug: "best-friend"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/41572-Best-Friend-by-danih"
---

My dearest friend, you are leaving me.
On with your life you will go.
How much I'll miss you, you will never know.
I will always smile, and always laugh whenever you are near.
But when you leave I will go home and face my largest fear.
I'll walk up the stairs, climb into bed, turn to my pillow and cry.
I'll miss you my dearest friend, and these are the reasons why;
I loved you from the moment we met.
Why?  I'll never know.
Maybe I loved your smile.
I might have known you would be my friend.
And all the while,
You've always had a hand to lend.
But now we will part,
You will leave me behind.
It feels like you have handed back my heart.
Do I have a new friend to find?
I don't want a new best friend.
No one can replace you.
I love you, my dearest friend.
Do you know what this is putting me through?
My dearest friend, you are leaving me.
On with your life you will go.
How much I'll miss you, you will never know.
