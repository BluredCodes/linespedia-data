title: "It's What We Don't Say"

You'll never know how much you mean to me,

And how your smile brightens my day,

How your actions make the world so clear
     and helps me to find my way.

How you have taught me to have faith,

And How I have learned to believe.

When I am upset your arms are open and
     your sholders are free.

I can say anything to you and you let me know you 
     care.

We have laughed together, cried together, been mad
     at each other too.

We say, "I love you" without saying it at all.

We show it.

These are the reasons you mean so much to me.\'00,
