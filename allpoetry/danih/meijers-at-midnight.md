After prom,
What do you do?
Go to Meijers at Midnight,
Go to Tech World to have some fun,
You will walk in and later laugh a ton.
When you get to Meijers one in a suit the other in
       a crown,
You can finally begin your night on the town.

Dance in the aisles,
With this you can guarantee smiles.
Draw attention to yourselves and sing,
Make sure you're loud enough to make your own
       ears ring.
To make the experience better tell everyone that others
       like you are coming too.
To top that make that little lie true.

When you get kicked out, don't get mad, don't pout.
You'll have fun no doubt.
Go to the nearest pool hall.
You'll have fun there with no problem at all.

Tech World!
Walk in silent.
Shock them first and hope they don't get violent.
"Hey, WHAT'S UP!" It's all right, it's okay.
They couldn't kick my butt anyway.
If your friend asks you to write anything like this for class,
Just say, "No," it will make you sound like you had been
       smoking bad grass.\'00,
