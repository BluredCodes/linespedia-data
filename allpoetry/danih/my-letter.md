---
title: "My Letter"
writer: "danih"
slug: "my-letter"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I don’t always know what is going to happen,

Or the way I am going to land,

But I know I truly can never go wrong, with your loving and guiding hand.

Things aren’t always perfect,

And I know they never will be,

But I still believe that I am safe with you beside me.

Maybe I am young, impressionable, and naïve,

But I still feel it’s you that I should never leave.

You walk with me through darkness, in happiness, and sorrow too,

Dear God, I know I would never want to walk without you.
