title: "Your Intrusion"
writer: "danih"

I don't want to be alone.
I don't to be without you.
I don't like picking up the phone when all 
     I hear is the dial tone.

I sit alone in my misery,
Secluded and so cold.
You and I are history.
Atleast that's what I've been told.

I lie awake in bed at night,
My only company are my tears.
I remember how you would hold me tight,
And I thought we'd last for years.

Leave me in my misery.
Leave me sad and insecure.
Go live without me.
Leave me while I'm despairing and demure.More ranting!
