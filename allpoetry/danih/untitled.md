Some of the things we do 
we will never understand.
All the hell we put eachother through 
when living life's demand.
Discontent with other's lifestyles,
And our lifestyles are just the same.
Unconcerned with other's feelings.
Unabashed by what we say.
Relentlessly unforgiving,
Misguided in everyway.
We can't change the world 
if we're unwilling to change.
