My friendship is hurt,
Yet I cannot help.
It has been hit a few times,
Now the hurt is beginning to welp.

How do you help a friendship in pain.
One that lies lifeless,
Dead,
Mundane.

Her ears do not listen,
My tongue lashes out.
If she will not listen,
I have to shout.

I love my dear friend,
Yet, our friendships in pain.
Who is to blame?
Is it both of us who have caused a friendship to be lifeless, dead, and mundane.\'00,
