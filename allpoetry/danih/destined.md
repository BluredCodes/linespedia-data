I saw him for the first time,
     and I saw his soul.
I was swimming in a deep blue sea full of his dreams.
I saw his happiness and his sadness.
He delighted me.
I saw his hopes and fears.
He hoped to be truly happy,
     and I wanted it for him.
He was afraid he would be alone,
     but I wouldn't let him.
I saw all of his attributes along with his flaws.
I was delighted to see that he was like me, imperfect.
When he looked into my eyes did he see my soul too?
Did he see and feel what I saw and felt?
Does he know what I know?\'01,
