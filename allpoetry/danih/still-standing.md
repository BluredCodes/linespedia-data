---
title: "Still Standing?"
writer: "danih"
slug: "still-standing"
source: "AllPoetry"

Heart broken, 
Tears cried,
Hope lost,
Dreams shattered.
Broken,
Torn,
Twisted,
Tattered,
Bent,
Beat,
Battered.
Thoughts constant,
Consistent,
Unending,
Scattered.
So confused,
Does anything matter?
Friendships split,
Unravel,
Break,
Tear.
Untimely end?
Yet, through it all you still stand.\'00,
