I lied down and closed my eyes. 
I was surrounded by a warm white light.
I heard my favorite music being played softly behind me.
A breeze swept across my body.
The light became brighter and warmer.
I was hot.
My skin felt as if it were melting into the bed.
I began to sweat.
The light disappear.
I am drenched in sweat.
I am left cold and naked.
I remove myself from the pool I have created.
I put on my clothes.
I hear "next" being called in the other room.
I look at myself in the mirror, and I am red.
I leave the room and all I see are dried up, peeling, red, 
      and dark people.
Damn, the tanning bed is hell.\'01,
