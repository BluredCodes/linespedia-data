Why should I love you?
Why should I put myself through hell?
Why should I believe you?
Why should I treat you so well?

Why should I need you?
Why do you make me cry?
Why do I love you?
Why do I even try?

How is it that you hold me near and everything feels right?
How is it that when you kiss me I never want to say goodnight?
How is it that we have been friends forever, but we never fight?
How is it that you love me, but you are giving up without a fight?

Could you live without me?
Could you not have me near?
Could you not miss me?
Could you not shed a tear?

Do you love me?
Did you love me, dear?
Do you wish that you were here?
Would you kiss away my tears?
