---
title: "Life"
writer: "danih"

source: "AllPoetry"

---

A long hard journey,
Impressed by the mind,
Endured by the heart,
Experience being the hardest of all teachers.
Best traveled with a friend, and you were mine.
You opened your arms to me, even after knowing my story,
I came in,
You loved me and cared for me,
You offered all you could,
I am leaving you a gift for all that you have done.
You have traveled on my journey,
Throughout my life you will be the one who gave me a special freedom,
An invitation to be me.
You have been my friend who has walked beside me.
In my heart you will forever be.
