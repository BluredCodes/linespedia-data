---
title: "Diversity"
writer: "danih"
slug: "diversity"
source: "AllPoetry"

Maybe I am perfect.
Maybe I am not.
I might like to read.
I might like to draw.
Maybe I like to paint.
For all you know, I might like to play ball.
What if I am genius?
What if I am not?
Would you still love me,
Even if I were everything you're not?
Do you even like me?
Do I like you?
Would you even care if I were down and blue?
Are you the one who's perfect?
Are you the one to envy?
Maybe YOU ARE perfect.
Maybe I am NOT.
Would you still love me,
If I were everything you're not?
