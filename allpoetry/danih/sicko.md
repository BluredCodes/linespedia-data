Walk with your head held high.
Smile your coy smile.
And tell your childish lies.

Get the girls and quickly leave them,
Let them love you and readily deceive them.

Open your arms and hold them near.
Walk away, let them drown in their tears.

Squeeze their hearts and let them bleed.
Do what you must to fufill your need.

Feel good about yourself,
And hold your head high.
Let your heart collect dust on the shelf,
And make the girls cry.
