---
title: "Something Like Heart Disease"
writer: "danih"
slug: "something-like-heart-disease"

You dazzle me with brilliance.
You enamer me with your wit.
You make me laugh until I'm breathless.
You baffle me with your bullshit.

You think my jokes are funny,
Although they're often not.
You make me feel so cunning,
I think it's you I've caught.

You like to hear me laugh.
You love to hold me near.
My allure is a craft.
It's my allure that you fear.

I've caught you and I like it.
It's a wonderful, bearable disease.
Welcome to my world.
My love, my heart disease.sick isn't it?
