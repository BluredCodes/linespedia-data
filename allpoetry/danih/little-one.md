Little girl,
Blonde curls,
Blue eyes.
Unafraid of what she would say.
Naïve,
Innocent,
Loving,
Lively,
Little one,
Sweet child.
Change so quickly,
Grow so fast.
Trust…limited, but exists.
Faith and hope…always.
Stands for herself,
As well as for others.
Lives to love,
Loves to live.
Sings and dances for herself 
Wishes to live as a child again.
Wide-eyed innocence.
Happiness for everyone.
No judgment,
No pain,
No reason to envy.
To be naïve again.
Only to love and be loved.
To be a child again.
