i'm exploiting the youth
with these lies

the thoughts expressed in said Poetry Log
do not necessarily represent the thoughts of
Your Humble Narrator

just thought i should take action in warning
you
thatthesepoemsaremoresputteringattheendofalongday

but it's fun
to write like whispering
(and pass hidden secrets on to
           those you love)

i do Not want to take ecstasy with you.
i will Not go to your crappy park bash.
but i still love you sometimes
__
--| the little storage box in
--| the closet.
