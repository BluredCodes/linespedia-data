when we're together we're the two Cutest people in the world (and i see those jealous glances&the snide comments&the green pitiful envy) holding hands in our innocence the sun shines the birds chirp (Yes even in the cold february evenings.)

haha okay so maybe not.
but man, you make me smile like this >>> :D
and all these expletives run through my head to describe and emote
(but how can you describe a kiss like that?) !!!!!!!!! 

and inside i really truly
like you because everytime i see you i
become five (5) years old
dancing/singing/shouting&wrestling
on my bunk bed
(i was a lonely kid.)
