---
title: "would you"
writer: "allmylittlewords"
slug: "would-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

fly
                                     fly
                                 to
                           me
if you could teach 
                                          --would you?

just think!
of the
swirling
whirling
landscapes 
and the
            rushing
                        blue
                               clouds
messing up our hair
as we laugh
and yell
until we
meet
the
ground
catching our breath
and start
again.crazy teenage optimism
