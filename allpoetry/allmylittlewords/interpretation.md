I find it Amusing
that you hold me in such high regard
as to think that the Things I Write 
would in some way have to do with you.

there's more to life than b-n-f
and i'm not talking dufferin and steeles.
if every act of Personal Independence
is seen as a questionable act of slander

then fuck it i'm out. adios.
