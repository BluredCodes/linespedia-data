do you remember that night when we got drunk and put on take that cds and the bon jovi cd got stuck in the player? do you remember born slippy and atomic and babes and babes and she wears the lipstick boy oh boy she's coming for you
boy

?

and then i cried because i always cried when i was drunk and you took it in stride and just assumed the Right Things To Say voice and i felt better, i really did.

you're a house of many rooms, my Friend.
