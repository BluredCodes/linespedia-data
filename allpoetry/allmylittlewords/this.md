this is me making a statement.
this is me making a statement.
this is me making a statement.
this is me making a statement.
this is me making a statement.
this is me making a statement.
this is me making a statement.
this is me making a statement.
this is me making a statement.
this is me making a statement.
this is me making a statement.
this is me making a statement.
this is me making a statement.
this is me making a statement.
this is me making a statement.

14 times. to make sure you heard me right.
I AM MAKING A STATEMENT.

one more for good luck and no one cares what the statement was to begin with.
