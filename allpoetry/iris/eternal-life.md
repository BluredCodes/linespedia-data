---
title: "Eternal Life"
writer: "Iris"
slug: "eternal-life"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/42838-Eternal-Life-by-Iris"
---

I want to glow
From the inside out
Carry warmth within
For all of eternity

I want to be wise
From an endless time
learning
watching

I want to be proud
Strong willed
fair
Unbreakable

I want to be beautiful
Pure
Untouchable
Loved

I want to carry forever in my hand
Years passing as minutes
Life: a never ending story
I want only what I cannot have
