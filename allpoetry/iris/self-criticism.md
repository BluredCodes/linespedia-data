---
title: "Self Criticism"
writer: "Iris"
slug: "self-criticism"
source: "AllPoetry"

Engulfed in your thoughts
Devoured by your fears
Nothing is real
Nothing is true

The happiness forgotten
The hopefulness is lost
Trapped inside your heart
Lost in the hatred

Doubt thrives within
You struggle to break its grasp
So unsure, so scared, so alone
Don't really understand

Forever thinking about the faults
That live within ourselves
How do we stop them from winning
From burying us alive?
