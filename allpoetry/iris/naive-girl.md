---
title: "Naive Girl"
writer: "Iris"
slug: "naive-girl"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/40698-Naive-Girl-by-Iris"
---

She sees only the bright side
Has faith in people
Trusts this place

She doesn't know hate
Or darkness 
Or corruption

She only understands love
And hope
And caring

She lives in all of us
You were once her
And so was I

She lived long before 
Before the world took her
Before she was lost to darkness

Before society struck
Before innocence was lost
Before the truth became apparent

Before you saw the world
Not in colour
But only in black and white

Don't you wish you could go back?
