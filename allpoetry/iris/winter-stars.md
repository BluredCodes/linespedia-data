---
title: "Winter Stars"
writer: "Iris"
slug: "winter-stars"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Cold pierces the night
Washes over me in waves
My breath is thick
My eyes are wide

The universe stands before me
Anxitie lies behind
The world is mine
If only for tonight

I can smell winter
Crisp, clean, pure
Freedom in a breath
Can feel it deep inside

The stars shine bright
Only for me
So clear and strong
What I want to be

But nothing lasts forever
Dusk will meet dawn
The stars shall fade
And so will I
