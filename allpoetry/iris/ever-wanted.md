---
title: "Ever Wanted?"
writer: "Iris"
slug: "ever-wanted"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Ever wanted to detach yourself?
From the world around you?
From the  things you see?
From the things you feel?

So scared
Of my emotions
My dreams
The power they have over me

Ever wanted to let the world go?
Forget they exist?
Forget you exist?
Forget what drives you?

So naive
Don't understand him
Don't understand myself
Don't understand

Ever wanted to forget what hurts?
Forget what makes you cry?
What makes you unstable?
What makes you hurt?

So unsure
Of the present
Of the future
of who I am
