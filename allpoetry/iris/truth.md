---
title: "Truth"
writer: "Iris"
slug: "truth"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/40707-Truth-by-Iris"
---

Don't want to hear it
It's all too much
Can't bear the pain
Can't stand the loss

So scared of the future
The darkness to come
Can feel it within
Crawls through me like a chill

No where to hide
No where to run
No comfort here
Alone with emotion

Want to find warmth
Breathe fresh air
Sit in the sun
Worries traped deep inside

But it follows like a shadow
There's no mercy there
It will find me
It will attack
And it will hurt
