---
title: "The Moment"
writer: "Iris"
slug: "the-moment"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/40704-The-Moment-by-Iris"
---

Consumed within a bubble
Taken by happiness
The world is bright
The stars are glowing

The future is yours
The past to be forgotten
You live for the moment
Never want to let go

This place has no boundaries
No line you can not cross
The possibilities endless
The emotion strong

Your heart soars
Your thoughts fly
You are lost
But you are never forgotten
