---
title: "The Balance"
writer: "Iris"
slug: "the-balance"

---

It's funny how the world keeps spinning
People keep on smiling
When everything we do
Could upset the balance

Life isn't sunshine and daisies
It's who we are and what we do
Everything keeps it going
Yet it's nothing at all

We have to look around ourselves
Take in our surroundings
See the world as it is
Not as we want it to be

Think about our decisions
Who will they affect?
Will they change your life?
Will they change mine?

Never forget the balance.
