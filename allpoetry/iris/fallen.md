---
title: "Fallen"
writer: "Iris"
slug: "fallen"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

The earth is rising
The end is near
Her heart is broken 
Filled with fear

Never the same
Innocence lost
She never realised
The depth of the cost

Freedom she found
From the depths of despair
Color was everything
Darkness was rare

But there are always consequences
When you sell your soul
To let go of the world
To make yourself whole

Deals just don't last
Satan is cruel
Will find your weakness
Will play you for a fool

Reality has struck
So sudden it came
She realises now
That it was never a game

Her eyes have unshut
The colour had faded
The world is not bright
But dank and jaded

No where to go
No where left to run
No light to be found
Nothing remains of the sun

She stands alone
Her decision is made
Turn her back to the world
With the light she shall fade

Now she must go
For Dusk is calling
And Nothing is left
To stop her from falling
