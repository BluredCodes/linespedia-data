---
title: "Lost"
writer: "Iris"
slug: "lost"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/40695-Lost-by-Iris"
---

Her eyes are shut
To the reality that is
Trust in the wrong place
Pain where it does not belong

She doesn't understand
Just how much we care
Doesn't trust  those who love her
Won't let us in

Closes herself in a room
That she understands
No room for us
Those who can't comprehend

She doesn't know she is missed
Or that neglect is in the air
She ignores the pain she causes
Blinded by the new universe

She left us in the dust
For a place so far away
Forget about our world
The place that was once home

But when she finally returns
From her trip among the stars
When the bubble has burst
Will home still exist?
