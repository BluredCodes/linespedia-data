title: "Carelessness"
writer: "Iris"

The sky bleeds black
The earth glows red
My eyes are dry
I see nothing I hear nothing

Silence is the future
Life is the past
Dreams are forever broken
Nightmares reign

The world around us crumbles
Nothing to be done
No sun, no moon, no light
Shadows take their place

Nothing left to live for
Nowhere left to go
All is forgotten
Lost in the collapse of light

Stand alone in the darkness
Take in the stench of loss
For this may be the future
Can you comprehend the cost?
