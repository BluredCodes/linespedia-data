---
title: "Too long, Too late, Too Bad"
writer: "Iris"
slug: "too-long-too-late-too-bad"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/40706-Too-long--Too-late--Too-Bad-by-Iris"
---

An image pressed upon your mind
Everything you wanted
Everything you needed
Lives in one person

Caught in their eyes
And the fragrance you love
Taken by the humour
In love with the mind

Never once thinking
That a flaw may exist
Never losing sight of the person
You want them to be

One day you'll realize
That you were so blind
Naive, careless, stupid
Too long, too late, too bad
