---
title: "Reality Is"
writer: "Iris"
slug: "reality-is"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Your hopes are high
The world is yours
No problems in sight
No chance you could fall

But then the world changes
Before your eyes
Life is unfair
Life is bitter, no longer yours

Happiness is not allowed 
Not without a cost
Take nothing for granted
Anything can be lost

Never assume 
That you can freeze happiness
Stay in a moment
Never let go

Because there is always something
To balance your life
Stop you from losing sight
Of what reality is
