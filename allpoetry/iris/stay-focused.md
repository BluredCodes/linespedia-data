---
title: "Stay Focused"
writer: "Iris"
slug: "stay-focused"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/40783-Stay-Focused-by-Iris"
---

Close the door
Shut out the world
Turn off the pain
Numb inside

Stay focused
On friends 
On love
On hope

Close my eyes
Wipe away the tears
Hold my head back
Let go

Stay focused
On life
On freedom 
On home

Close my heart
From the ache it feels
Won't let it hurt
Shut down

Stay focused
On everything else
Or the world will shake
And my dreams will break
