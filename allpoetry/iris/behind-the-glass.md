---
title: "Behind the Glass"
writer: "Iris"
slug: "behind-the-glass"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/44603-Behind-the-Glass-by-Iris"
---

The sun shines
But beyond my eyes

Birds sing
But I can not hear them

Love warms
But my hands are cold

I am lost in a world
Far, but forever too close

Emotion lost
In the darkness at hand
