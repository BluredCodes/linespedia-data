title: "Made to be Broken"

Arrive Innocent
How long doe it last?
Until dreams are lost.
And Hope Has been Swept Away

Hearts Broken
Spirits dampered
Love lost
And all in between

Perfect in the beginning
Eyes wide open and ready
To take in a beautiful world
How long does it last?

Little girl lost
Little boy frightened
Emotion runs strong
Pain runs stronger

Experience becomes too much
Each lesson penetrates your soul
Can't forget
Don't want to remember

Leave shattered, impure
Eyes closed to the darkness
Know too Much
Broken too long
