---
title: "Out of My Hands"
writer: "Iris"
slug: "out-of-my-hands"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/44604-Out-of-My-Hands-by-Iris"
---

I want to let go
Release the pain
Forget the feeling
Deny the past

I want to be strong
Wipe my eyes
Look away
Fear not

I want to move on
Find more
Live my life
Relax

But my heart wants to stay
Hold still
Shed tears 
And believe
