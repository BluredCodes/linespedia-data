Life is hard
Or so "they" say

It's pain
It's fear
It's struggle
It's loss

It's everything
Everything you don't expect
Don't want it to be
Yet it isn't

It's love
It's joy
It's mindblowing
It's breathtaking

It's everything
Everything you expect
Want it to be
Yet it's not

Life is a riddle
A challenge
A fickle,
Wild place

I don't know who "they" are
But they are right.
