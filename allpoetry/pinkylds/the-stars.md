Tonight the stars are shining bright
Like earth through space they roll
I wonder do some have my name 
Out there upon their scroll?

 For i believe that we did live
before we came to earth 
and we will tread another one,
again in our next birth.

 Keep moving on to brighter stars,
Life's quest, a higher goal,
Through time and space will travel on 
No resting for the soul. 

 Although the body may be old.
the spirit is still young,
the star of hope forever shines 
high in the Heavens flung.
