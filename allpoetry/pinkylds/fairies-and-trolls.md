Fairies beautiful creatures can fly give hope to all.wings that spread out wide.glitter shine around faces andsmile. pixy dust is there magicand love. the troll with a long nose and pale face. will mess withthe fairies heads and scare them awaylooking for them is hard but all you needis children eyes and see innosents. look carefuly in the twin places you could be surprised
