breathing harder and harder
fire buling up, heart pumbing harder
just trying to make it
running toward faster than 
you speed of light
wind of north agaist my back 
kick and run. closer and 
closer kick blocking my score.
all that work for nothing all 
that pushing all 
that running cant breath no longer.....
just try harder.
