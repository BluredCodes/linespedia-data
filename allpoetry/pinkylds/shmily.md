the story of shmily
  There were kids that lived with there grand parents and one day they found something in the dining room mirror saying shmily. well the kids thought that was a guys name 'who's shmily' they asked no one. 'then what does it mean?' grandma said' well tell you someday when the time comes.
well the kids would find that shmily word every where and sometimes the dumbest places like the lipstick on the bathroom mirror would say it its drove them nuts sometimes' whos shmily'they would always ask and they would get the same reply 'well tell you some day'.
  2 years later the Grandma died and on her grave it had SHMILY on it the kids asked once again 'grandpa who or what does shmily mean?' he said 'Kids shmily is a word thats sweet and pure. S-H-M-I-L-Y it means 
See
How
Much
I
Love 
You
and i would tell your grandma this cause i loved her so much that seemed very much what i wanted to say and i just would say shmily and she would know what i was saying and we loved each other for that.
the moral if you love some on tell them but it can be fun if you say shmily or write to them shmily and they dont know what your saying but you do and they'll know someday how you feel.
