This is my country i sing it 
with pride. Whether its desert
sand duned and wide, Mountains 
with pine tree land ice berg.
its here where my home and my 
loved ones are found.
  This is my country and all 
that i do should make it mighty
noble and true i love my country
long may it be a land where my 
people are honest and free.
