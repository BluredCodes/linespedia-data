Have you ever felt like no one in the world cared they see how people destroy it and not how beautiful it is why is that i wonder if just once they stop to look at the sun rise or are they too lazy to get up from there bed i think maybe we've gotten more lazier in the years instead of being great people that we are we complain instead of doing.we get mad and hurt the innocent. i think all it takes is look at your self in the mirror and say 'why am i her, do i have a purpose and what can i do to make it better?'have i done any good in the world today have i help any one in need,have i cheered up the sad and made some one feel glad if no i have fail indeed
