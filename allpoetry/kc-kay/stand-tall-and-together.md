---
title: "Stand Tall and Together"
writer: "KC Kay"
slug: "stand-tall-and-together"
source: "AllPoetry"

Stand tall be proud 
Is what they're all asking for
To hold your head high and straight 
Show them we're something more
Don't stoop down to their level 
For they ask that of us
To cry and pout about our problems 
To get mad and cuss
Must we all fight against eachother 
And cry out in pain
Must this all end in violence 
With God's tears falling like rain
Will the world end in violence 
The way Osama wants it too
Or can we stop this fight together 
Hand in hand me and you
If we hold our heads high 
As God asks us to do
Will we win this fight? 
Will we make it through?
Though no one knows the answers 
Of how this fight will end
I encourage you all colors of the skin 
To join hands to make amends
