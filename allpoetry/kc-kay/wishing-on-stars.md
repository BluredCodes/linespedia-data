---
title: "Wishing On stars"
writer: "KC Kay"

I sat upon the step that night
And gazed up at the stars
I closed my eyes and made a wish
Yet it wasn't about fancy cars
I thought it was a simple wish
A wish that would come true
Yet the more I thought about my crazy wish
I realized, I would never have you
You were Mr. Perfect
And I was far from that
I had the hair I had the eyes
But some considered me fat
I knew I would never hold you
Well, maybe in my dreams at night
But how could I let go?
It was love at first sight
Giving up was so not me
But I felt I had no other choice
I went to bed that night
Sorrow in my voice
When I awoke the next morning
My dream had brought some cheer
I dreamed of you holding me
Calling me names like sweety and dear
I went to school that morning
A smile upon my face
I wore jeans and a sweater
Made of pretty lace
I had many chances
I saw you throughout the whole day
Yet when you walked torward me
I quiclky walked away
The school day was almost over
I had one last chance
I took a deep breath 
The butterflies in my stomach began to dance
I walked toward you casually
I placed a smile upon my face
When your eyes met mine
My heart began to race
After standing there awhile
I quickly blurted out
'Look I really like you'
'Do you wanna go out?'
You looked so surprised
I couldn't help but laugh
You turned red and smiled
And you too began to laugh
We sat upon the steps tonight
And gazed up at the stars
We held eachothers hands tight
And wished upon the stars
You turned to me and smiled
I felt a touch of bliss
It was then you leaned forward 
And gave me my first kiss
I was lost in emotions
And realized wishes do come true
I know now to wish on stars
When I want things like you
