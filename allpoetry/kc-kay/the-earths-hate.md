title: "The Earths Hate"

There's blood stains everywhere On the face of this earthOne mans death Is another mans birthOne mans terror Is another mans dreamAnd one mans disaster Is another mans schemeFor all over this world Is nothing but hateWe must stop it now Before it's to lateFor thousands have diedAnd thousands more willFor in this world The solution is to killWe need to step forward And let God help us throughFor if we don'tIt could kill me and you
