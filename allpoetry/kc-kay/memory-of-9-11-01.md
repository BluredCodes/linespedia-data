My friends were crying 
As I walked in the room
I saw a flash 
I heard the boom
People were jumping
From the building up high
A devil rose through the smoke
As people died
Lives are lost
As the building falls
People waiting for loved ones
Telephone calls
It looked like a movie
Being replayed
A bad dream
Not wanting to fade
But this was reality
Far from untrue
A reality that will change
Me and you
