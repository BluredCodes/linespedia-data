---
title: "My Sleep"
writer: "KC Kay"
slug: "my-sleep"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/2831-My-Sleep-by-KC-Kay"
---

As I close my eyes and lay me headSoftly onto my bedAll my thoughts of loving you slowly pop into my headThe times we share together That happen day to dayAnd then the times that we're aloneWhen you're far awayI begin to think about many thingsAs I lay there deep in sleepThen suddenly I notice all the love in your eyes Reflecting oh so deepI can't believe what I seeFor I've never seen love so strongI look again once moreI'm positive what I see is wrongYou see I really love youBut I know you can't really love meI must be dreaming once againFor you love her not me
