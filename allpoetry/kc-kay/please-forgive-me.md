I met a man a while back 
Who meant the world to me
I never thought I'd fall in love 
With he who is older than me
We've had some great times together 
And shared some great lauhgs too
It's hard to think it could be over now 
Because of something stupid I'd do
Noel I'm really sorry 
For a broken heart I've made
If I could take it back I would 
If only I could make you stay
I was taught awhile back not to lie 
And I wish I wouldn't have at all
But if I hadn't lied to you 
Would we have this friendship at all?
Noel they say age doesn't matter 
If you're truly friends
But is that really the truth 
Can we make amends?
Noel if age doesn't matter
Than can we still love
For talking to you makes my heart flutter
Like the wings white of a dove
Noel I know my sorry
May never ever be enough
But Noel please try to forgive me 
Because living without you would be too toughNoel I want you to know I love you with every bit of my heart and every single bit if this poem is the truth. I love you and hope you can find in your heart a place to forgive me.
