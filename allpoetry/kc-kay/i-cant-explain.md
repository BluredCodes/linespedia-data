---
title: "I Can't Explain"
writer: "KC Kay"
slug: "i-cant-explain"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/37073-I-Cant-Explain-by-KC-Kay"

I can't explain how I feel
When I hear your sweet voice
Or what I feel deep inside
While thinking of your choice
Your choice to want to talk to me
And hold me close at heart
Meet eachother face to face
Maybe make a new start
I can't explain how my heart beats
When my mind drifts to you
Or why my hands shake
When you say "I love you"
I can't explain why I smile
When I say your name
Or why my heart beats faster
When our thoughts are the same
I'll never be able to explain
Just what I feel for you
For all my feelings tangled inside
Are more than just love for you
