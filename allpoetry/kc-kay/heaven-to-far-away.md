---
title: "Heaven To Far Away"
writer: "KC Kay"
slug: "heaven-to-far-away"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/2834-Heaven-To-Far-Away-by-KC-Kay"
---

You are in heaven, 
But I need you here today
Nothing is going right, 
I can't even pray.
My mind is all confused, 
And my heart very hurt,
I feel like a nothing, 
Lower than the dirt.
For you went to heaven, 
And left me here alone.
No number to call, 
Does heaven have a phone?
I need to talk to you, 
I'm hurt and very depressed,
Please dear God, help me to be blessed.
I need to talk to Grandpa, 
Grandma still cries,
I know he's in heaven, 
Cause that's where you go when you die
Grandpa please hear me, 
And come back to your home,
Heaven is to far away, 
We need you here at home.
