---
title: "Can my heart win?"
writer: "KC Kay"
slug: "can-my-heart-win"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/28132-Can-my-heart-win--by-KC-Kay"
---

My hearts been broken so many times 
More hurtful memories than the happy times
More painful tears then any of my friends
More broken hearts with no amends
Will I even find that love who's somewhere far away?
When he finally finds me will he wanna stay?
My hearts sure hopes so for it has felt much pain
But in my mind I vision more tears falling like rain
I want to find my true love who's waiting just like me
The one who will love me the one like he
Yet if I find he who loves me will my heart break once more?
Will it hurt so badly like the love I lost before?
I want to find my love soon for I am about to give in
For everytime I try to love my heart just can't win
