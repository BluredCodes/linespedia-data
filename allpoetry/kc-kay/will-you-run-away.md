---
title: "Will You Run Away?"
writer: "KC Kay"
slug: "will-you-run-away"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/36509-Will-You-Run-Away--by-KC-Kay"
---

How can I tell you what my heart wants me to say?
How can I tell with out you running away?
How can I make it clear what my hearts wants inside
When I'm not sure what you're heart will decide
I feel if I tell you what I truly want from you
You'll turn around and walk away forget about me and you
My heart is ready to explode deep inside of me
For it wants you to be with alone with me
But somehow deep inside I fear you won't feel the same
For if you don't want me I'll feel all the blame
I'm so afraid to tell you what my heart wants to say
For if you don't love me, will you run away?
