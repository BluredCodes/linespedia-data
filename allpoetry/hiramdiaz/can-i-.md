Can I drift into this collaborationof sidereal dust? On the basis ofgetting to know you better, I stuffTime with novacaine, you smile as I do.You understand whyI wrestle; you love,and I continue to askmyself, 'Can I?'Can I fold you,limb by limb, and putyou in my pocketso I can read you in the even hourand smile as you do?Can I pull you out of Heaven?Can I...love you?i dont really like this 'poem' but id like critique.thxhiram
