the globalization of erect structures;the profound towersof born-again-babylonleaning lustilyone        against    the    othermelt into the waterswe drink in, bathe in,and drown in.and we are drunkandwe are hung-over.......waiting for ethereal flamesto devour our molten spires(twisted one around the other,seeping into our waters).
