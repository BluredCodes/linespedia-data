The Preacher's adroit list of adjectives 
circle my head and landing on the softest patch of conscience,
begin to peck at my brain for any crawling, 
limber, limb-less opposite.

I sit and watch as his lips limn my name
with the artistry of Rembrandt.
The ring of his call loudens until...

silence numbs my mind and christ 
approaches me in a three piece suit. 
his right hand fondling a wireless microphone;
his left hand fondled by gold and silver

and diamond studded platnium.:O) Did i change too much?
