Twenty five minutes
until we're replicated -

mixed within a moistened
orifice, pushed back 
out after nine months
of fearful incubation.

She smiles nervously,
praying that ignorance 
will flesh out; 

praying that consequence 
will not drape itself 
with our misdirected flesh.

Twenty five minutes 
till the next episode begins;
till Heated-Passion
steers clear of Consequence.
