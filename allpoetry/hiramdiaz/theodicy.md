They told me that 
somewhere
beyond simplicity

you shake your fists
and cry because 
the living are dead

             and 
  the 
       seeing 
               remain             
 blind   .   .   .

I reply,
here within complexity
masked by the paltry flesh
of decaying truths i cry 

because,

the living die 
and 
omnipotence remains 

paralyzed.vacation sucks.
