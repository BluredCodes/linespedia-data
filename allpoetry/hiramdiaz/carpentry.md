your words dig into my skull.
i feel you chisel me into something
i could never make myself –

as my kindness reduces to sawdust,
and my compassion creates a wooded
flatland beneath your feet,
your curses frolic across my sanity

and glue this masterpiece together.

you revel in the scent of my freshly carved flesh
and run the tips of your fingers across my chest      

     again and again and again and marvel 
        
          in your crowning creation –

something decent, something commendable,
something subservient and languishing with every breath…
