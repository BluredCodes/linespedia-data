Fourteen lines are singing,
the blurred perceptions are ringing;

you crush me for disobedience.

You melt over my crusting mind
scourging my back, staining my time,
punishing me with...fourteen lines.

Forgive me, I beg you, 
forgive my mind's pride,
to think he could love you,
and meet you outside

the Temple of your rigidity,
and, crucify, crucify, crucify me 

on a cross, invisible, completely comprised,
of fourteen, invisible, indivisible, lines.confused
