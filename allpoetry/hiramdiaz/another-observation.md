kerosine sins 
swathe our wounds;

we all use the word
as a touniquet
at times to heal us,

and ramshackle 
the gods to our vices
like rotting corpses 
tied to the backs 
of mourning romans.

plea if you will,
but, i'd rather die 
with integrity -

ink blots on my hands,
for they are much richer

than the blood that will 
be found drowning your own.tell me what went wrong!
ok

bye
:O)
hiram
