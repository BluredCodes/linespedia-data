You open your mouths,
and Handel's baroque 
compositions begin
to envy your pomposity.

Polyphonic addendums
accompany your stately pomp -

I follow behind, I am 
your unhandeled beauty; 
I am your ceaseless praise,
I am that I am; I am human.lost in emotin ....forgive the incoherence....if there is ....

:O)
hiram
