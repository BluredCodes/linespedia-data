title: "Ex Solacium"

(I.)Early morning:rebellious quilts,crunchy green mold spreading across my blue-black eyelids.     (II.)Midday:rebellious train doors,their thick, black, substance smears across my tattered clothing.     (III.)Afternoon:rebellious cars,yellow & identified by a checkered striprunning across their side doorsrun over my just-waking body.     (IV.)Evening:rebellious bodies apathetically runover my rotting frame.     (V.)-repeat-Lots and lots of Angst.
