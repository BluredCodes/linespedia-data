I washed upon the shore
of Existence just yesterday
and listened to the sea sing your name.

Sweeping stones onto sand,
turning land into clodded grains
of eternality, 

you formed life from a cloud 
crawling over the face of the sun,
and screamed through thunder and lightning

while crying billions of tears -
each thinning through 
space, time, and, matter.

I watched the whole ordeal 
and took notes on its nature -
omnipotent, and, immutable,
