I left the door open 
so that you didn't have to knock,
but you never showed up;

I left the water running
so that the wounds wouldn't form scabs
but you inconsiderately shut the water off;

I left you somewhere between
the altar and the pews to pursue truth,
but I think you're following me, daily -

and now all I know is

another 45 min. passing of supressing 
the tears drowning my apathy, to see
the truth everyone, but me, understands -

so I left the preachers, 
to allow you to defend yourself,
but, I think you have no such intentions.Every word is helpful.... i like criticism...


hiram
