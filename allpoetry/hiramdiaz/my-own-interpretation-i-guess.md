She says 'I'll be okay' -and I'll be damned if it be otherwise, but,why...Why the wait?Nine months of swelling and our worldwill explode, and evenDarwinism won't be ableto make sense of such chaos.I watch an outletreluctantly shove ahead and atorso throughitself -like a drunk manregurgitating his pride -and see hernimbly chosen words fallto the earthin somersaults, forced through her lips into a world of various interpretations.How Am I Doin'? How Am I doin'?
