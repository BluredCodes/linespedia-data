it's amazing 
how grace no longer amazes;
it only puzzles

even the wisest of men,
and, women, and, children
(hopefully i didn't exclude anyone).

7 days a week i work
- even on the Sabbath 
(Saturday I sleep, Sunday i wrest 
with fears,

Monday through Friday 
i nestle anxieties in smiles 
smiled to appease 
the wrath of Protocol Forsaken)-

awaiting grace's amazing advent
to amaze humanity, once again.

i wonder 
what the gods 
would think 
of the 

rock-flinging,
sword-wielding,
gun-toting,
bomb-tossing, 

Death loving world 
we love to corrupt 
with all of our

T. V. smiles
- in still life frames,
where we wear

fiery red pupils
(intimating that we are 
the furnace we feared 
for so long,

or feared so long ago).

i wonder 
what the gods would think
and if they ever did at all.?/?/01, God? God? God? gods?
