Sunday morning 
and its all my fault.

It doesn't matter 
what it is, it's still
all my fault.


Sunday evening 
and I wonder 
whose fault it is;

steaming faucet water, 
bleeding wrists
and trembling fingers
 
noiselessly dropping 
a rusted razor
onto the linoleum carpeting.I don't like this very much.....Let me know what you think could be fixed!

  )
