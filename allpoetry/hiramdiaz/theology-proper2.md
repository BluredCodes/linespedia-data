you shouldn't 
cry sweet Jesus -

even though your porcelain 
stitched fingers are torn 
      a   
           p  
                a   
                     r    
                          t
from the blast 
         of our guns' shaft -

no,no,you shouldn't cry,
because,when you don't

an aureole rises 
behind your glistening 
countenance and shines 
more brilliantly than
the sun in all her splendor...

Jesus,
don't cry,

they have better plans for you 
than that wretched old cross
that makes sweat swivel around 
your bloodied crown, and stings 
every open lattismal sore -

a gun,
a knife,
a stick,
a rock,

a nuclear warhead...

all these they have 
prepared for you, 
and, 
you will be avenged.quickie.....god im tired
