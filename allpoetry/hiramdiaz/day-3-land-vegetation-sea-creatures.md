i can't understand your lyrical sophistryskillfully woven onto jagged fragments of mount hebron,like the twisted branches of the sun are sewn overunconsciousmorning eyes.honestly...i never really did.i just smiled, flashed my pasty teeth, and wished i knew what everyone else meant when they said'amen'...Blah Blah Blah
