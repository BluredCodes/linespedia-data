Tonight,     you hold me      so      tight      I          gasp for breath.But instead           of wind,       I swallow you;         you circulatethe course of my   spinal cordas I  cling to yours.There is no end,we hazily gaze intothe blankness of each other's eyes -I wish I could tear my arms off of you,you wish my grip werejust a little tighter...blah....20 million people each day are infected by statistics.
