it rests on the clouded glass table-top
folded - one corner over the other.
mother smiles, her duty is done -
the beast is satiated, the sinner is saved,
redeemed from his own laziness -
she's left him a twenty dollar bill,
he'll look for work next week, he promises.:oP  hiram
