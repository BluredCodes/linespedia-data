wearied...she stumbles,singing garbled theoriesof theodicy to herself -'crazy,crazy,i was crazy for you,but you...you were just a dream'.her arid pupils dart across the subway platformseeking applausefrom an invisible audience,seeking an appraisal for her infallible work.the thundering ovation of blazing iron wheelscleanses her disheveled physiognomy with divine exuberance; as she bowsover the edge of her decrepit dais.
