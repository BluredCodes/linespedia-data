My love 
your eyes are 
             drier than wood
and your voice,
             as coarse as nails -

wrap your arms around me 
and hammer
your words into my body 

as I 
    fixate my existence 
                   upon your eyes.

I will
satiate your hunger pangs
        as I writhe in mid-air,
hanged and saturated with your crimson osculations.*yawn*
