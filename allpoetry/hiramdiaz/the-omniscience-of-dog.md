The fuzzy 3&1/2 inch dog nodded to the engine's drummingon the time-weathered dashboard.I sat in the passenger seat,aghast at the solemnity with which he spurned me.He facedme and blankly grazed on my sanity with each gaze into my fluttering eyes.Her hands clutched the tattered steering wheel.I refrained from sayinga word.
