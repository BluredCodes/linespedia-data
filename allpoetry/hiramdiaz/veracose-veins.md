title: "...veracose veins..."

grandma sat in the plush recliner
as all the piglets gathered 'round
her viloet throne, and taught them.

the respirator made her story sound
even more demanding than the content,
as the children sat, motionless.

the end was approaching and everyone 
was knowledgable on this subject,
even the subjects gathered 'round

her violet throne, decoding her vocal
strains, and trying to trace the map
each plethora of veracose veins created.this really sucks....
