Hammer is pushing something into
your ear so you will rememeber it
but in reality it goes in one ear
and falls out the other

We are the last ones standing
and we have to stay alive
read the writings on the wall
they will show you the right path

The mountain is too high to climb
my skin is running into the ground
I'm to tired to continue
I'll just sleep on this cloud
