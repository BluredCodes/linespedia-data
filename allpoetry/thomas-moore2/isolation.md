This dark room is my little home
it's the only place in which 
I could think all alone.

There are no windows and
there are no doors
for things that I needed in the past
I don't think I need anymore

I sit here and watch
the walls stand tall
just waiting for the day when
they will all suddenly fall

Until that day comes
I'll just sit here and wait
until something will come and
get me out of this place.
