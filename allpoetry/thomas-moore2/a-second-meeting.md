It's easy for you to come here
and face me with such ease
while all this time I've been 
waiting on my knees.

I fell for all your 
tricks, lies, and games
which has left me here
with none of my pride but with shame.

You turned me around and
played games behind my back
and in the end you just stood there
and watched as I crumbled and cracked
