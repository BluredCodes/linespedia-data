Wind blowing on my face
feels like fingers massaging my skin
My feet aching from the long walk
feels like tiny ants eating my toes
You kiss taste like the sweetest candy
while your eyes look like stars

I need someonew to catch me
in case I slip and fall
Just letting you know that
I want u to catch me out of them all
