I want to leave all this behind
I just don't want to face it
keep it all inside a box
and throw it into a river.

Don't give me your advice
I'm just ignoring you
trust me in this problem
I know what I wanna do.

Pick myself from this mess
with a brand new plan
it's my own little scheme
I hope you understand.

I'll go away soon and 
all of it will be gone
by the time you finish thinking of 
everything that is wrong.
