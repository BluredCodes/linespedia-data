I love you Lisa and I know it now
I was so stupid before to push you away
I wish I could take back everything bad I said or did
And start all over again

You may not be think you are the skinniest person
But that doesn't matter
You may not be think yo are the prettiest
But to me you are beautiful
You may think you are stupid
An airhead, yes, but you are one of the smartest people I know

I want to be with you always
I'm sorry for everything
I love you girl
I always have
I was afraid before because I've never felt like this

I though I'd been in love before
But what I feel for you is different
Different from what I've felt for other girls
And now I know
It's you
It's always been you
