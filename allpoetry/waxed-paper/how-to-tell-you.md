title: "How to tell you..."
writer: "Waxed Paper"

I met this girl
Nice as can be
And I found out
She's the one for me

I want to tell her
I don't know how
I'm afraid if I do
She'll hurt me somehow

So listen to this
I'm in love with you
I wish I knew
What to say and do

I want you to knonw
I'm there for you
And I hope you feel
The way I do
