---
title: "You"
writer: "Waxed Paper"
slug: "you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/28042-You-by-Waxed-Paper"
---

You are beautiful
Funny
Intelligent
Fun to be with
And extremely cool

You are my best friend
My better half
The only one who can cheer me up
The only one who believes in me
And who has been there for me always

You complete me
And I need you
I want you

Please, please want me back
