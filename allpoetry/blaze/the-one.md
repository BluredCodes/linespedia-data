---
title: "The One"
writer: "BlaZe"
slug: "the-one"
source: "AllPoetry"

A twinkle in her eye,
One to match her spark for life.
A glistening smile, 
One to match the light she radiates.

A laugher of joy,
To accompany her enthusiastic aura.
A serene voice,
To accompany her peaceful nature.

A mind of love,
To emphasise her caring soul. 
A heart of gold,
To emphasise her purity.

A unique mind.
An extraordinary body.
So rare,
I know she must be The One.Maybe...she is out there somewhere...im looking dont worry!
