I look at you and see a reflection,
You’re a mirror image of society’s expectation,
Conforming just to improve your reputation.

But you don’t understand it the way I do
I’ve been there, just like you.
Being yourself is much more cool, believe me its true!

You think its “cool” being the same,
You think your act is just part of the game.
But that’s just it – YOUR ACTING – you don’t deserve that fame.

Act, pretend, don’t be the real you – conform.
Just remember you can’t change the way you were born
Like all mirrors you are fragile, one mistake and you break and return to the norm.I often debate about what is "cool" and why people conform to friends and society's expectations of them. I guess this is just an example of my beliefs. Nothing to complicated :P
