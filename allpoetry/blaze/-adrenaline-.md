Some people take speed to have a good time,
But I don't need drugs.
I produce my own speed by sprinting.
The Adrenaline gives me a high,
It picks me up, lifting my feet off the ground.
Floating in oblivion.
And for a short instant
I 
Am 
Flying

Who needs alcohol to make you light headed?
Who needs drugs to forget the pain?
All I need is ground to run on
And I’m Flying in a world of bliss
Adrenaline clears my body of pain
Adrenaline clears my mind of thought
And for a short instant
I 
Am
FreeI hope some people can relate to this in some way. I know you dont all sprint like me but many things we do require speed and i think im addicted!
