Life is set
It begins and it ends.
It has countless ups, downs,
Twists and bends.

The older we get,
The wiser we become.
So why do we say:
I wish I were young?

As the journey draws to an end
Revealed is the meaning of it all
Confidence which is always hidden deep in our soul
Will help us to stand up when we fall.

The greatest gift you can have is confidence
This allows you to take as much as life gives you
And with this wonderful power give and learn.
Then the Lord will give you back in return
All that you deserve – Happiness.This poem was written by my little sister - she is 12 years old. I gave her a bit of advice but this is 80% her work...not bad ey?..especially for her first attempt
