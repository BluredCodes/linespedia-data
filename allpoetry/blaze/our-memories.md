Memories are wondrous things.
Whether they are happy or sad,
Weird or wonderful,
Memories create life.
 
Without Memories no one can learn.
Without Memories it is all forgotten.
Without Memories no one exists,
No Memories of them remain. 

With Memories the lesson of life is taught.
With Memories we remember.
With Memories people can live on,
Live forever in the minds of others.

Remember me.
For I will always remember you,
Through those magical Memories we share.
Forever, Towards Eternity…
