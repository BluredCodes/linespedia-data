On the keyboard of life
My finger is forever poised above the ESC key.
I find myself forever in strife,
Never knowing how to act or who to be.
I wake up every morning 
Wanting and trying to be me,
But I seem forever to be in mourning.
I do not understand how they cannot see
The irreversible damage I have been dealt
From my parents who seem so far away.
If only they knew how it felt
To be all alone on this gloomy day

I’m always told to look for opportunity,
“The windows key,” they say in concern,
“May hold the solution to liberty.”
Maybe with desire my dreams will return.
The transparent windows suggest a world of beauty so unmistakable.
I am eager to be on the other side, strive for ambition.
But this glass is unbreakable
It locks me into a life of isolation.

I have been taught many things in my life
But learnt only one message
Life is the only lesson you learn,
And it is only taught when you’re through.
