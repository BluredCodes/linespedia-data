title: "My Knight, My Love"
writer: "Mad Moon"

I lay my body next to yours
then as I close my eyes
The crystals form
the clouds roll in
and spires begin to rise

You rose up from the silver clouds
astride the dragon's wings
you were crossing 
o'er the drawbridge
to the castle of my dreams

Your sword held high and eyes afire
I knew right from the start
that you were sent
to rescue me
from the dungeons of my heart
