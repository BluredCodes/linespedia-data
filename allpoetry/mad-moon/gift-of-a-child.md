~♥~



A miracle 
you came into my life 
Unexpectedly
Without warning
Taking me by pleasant surprise
Your life stirring within me 
Burgeoning
taking shape
becoming a whole in your existence

As you emerged into the world
I gazed into your eyes and saw Love
Absolute
Unconditional
the utmost gift bestowed upon me by God

As I held you in my arms 
I felt complete
Unadulterated
Tranquil
The warm softness of your body next to mine

As you grew 
I looked at you and saw
Reflection
A mirror image
yet an individual entity with its own perspective

It is within this endowment I find 
Harmony
Understanding
and most of all 

Myself



~♥~This poem was an Easter gift to my children a few years back. I'm thankful for them each and every day.
