~♥~



My feelings are so out of reach.
Endless, yet obscure.
Like waves upon an empty beach,
unseen and so unsure.

I dare not let them surface now
for fear they’d overwhelm.
A ship upon a stormy sea,
with no one at the helm.

My Captain has abandoned me.
Emotions set adrift.
I search, and yet I cannot see
just when the fog will lift.

I hope that soon the Captain sees,
this ship, it could go down.
For now, I’ll wait for calming seas,
and pray I do not drown.



~♥~
