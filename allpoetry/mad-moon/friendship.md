title: "Friendship"
writer: "Mad Moon"

You say you are my friend,
then be there all the time.
Don’t be the one who only stays
when things are going just fine.

You say you are my friend,
then look inside my mind.
See far beyond the outer shell,
you might like who you find.

You say you are my friend,
don’t try to make me change.
Accept me just for who I am.
The concept’s not so strange.

You say you are my friend,
you’ll have to earn my trust.
If we’re to feel the ties that bind,
then honesty’s a must.

You say you are my friend,
then let me be yours too.
You need only to believe in me,
and I will stand by you.
