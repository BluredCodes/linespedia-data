I sat down underneath that tree,
caressed by a warm summer breeze.
Closing my eyes, I found myself
gazing out through the window of dreams.

My body (getting lighter now),
gently carried by the breeze;
Ascending toward an azure sky,
rises up above the trees.

A winged horse, my mount appears,
emerging from a cloud.
Soft light reveals the forest below
veiled in a mystical shroud.

Faeries lift their starry wands,
pointing out across the land;
Creating luminous rainbows with
a single wave of their hand.

Playfully dancing in peaceful bliss,
absorbing the wondrous sight.
Soaring through enchanted lands,
enjoying formless flight.

From distant place I strain to see -
Out there, underneath that tree!
A child sits looking back at me,
gazing out through the window of dreams.




~*~A tribute to the childhood dream...lest we not forget those 'nightly flights of fancy.' Faeries is spelled in Celtic form.
