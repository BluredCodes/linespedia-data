My journey towards a sense of "self"
began with the smallest step.
Some unexpected pleasures found
in dreams within the depth.

As a river of emotion flows
and races quickly by,
comes an abstract understanding
of the view from my "mind's eye."

I ride the wake of feelings to
the shores of fantasy,
Reflections of my inner self
exposed for all to see.

So I search the corners of my soul
in hopes that I may find,
the comfort I've felt dwelling there,
in the colors of my mind.Here's to an over-active imagination!!!  :D
