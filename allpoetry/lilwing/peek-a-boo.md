Peek-a-boo

Lost in a world of cyber-space,
looking through each link,
found was a man with no face,
sounding smart, I think.

Found is a place to calmly sit
and wonder at this voice-mask.
To ponder, to enjoy its wit,
in its limitless imaging bask.

Peek-a-boo! Do I really see you?
Are those words of yours so clever,
those ideas transmitting true
blinding me, now and forever.
