Joy


Joy is morning sunshine
spread softly,
efferescently,
within yourself.
Joy is an expression
of all you
hope in
and 
by exhibiting 
joy to others, it is
your faith in humanity.
Joy seeks to spread
from you, 
to emerge to another;
and in its contagion
love is known.

Joy can be obtained
from
another being
but only as as additive
to the
joy
you yourself possess.

Joy is universal,
a celebration
of all living things.
The blossoming of spring,
the buzzing
of summer,
the brightness of fall,
the serenity 
of winter.
A perfect setting 
this world is
for the voice of a 
friend,
the giggle of a
child, 
the touch of the one
you love.

Joy rises to praise
the Unknown
for the fullness
of all you are,
and all
you have been given.

Joy can be dampened,
stilled,
repressed,
never obliterated.
