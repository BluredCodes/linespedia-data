Love Eulogy

She:

My soul is withered love,
     its lost its way!
Oh it did not know dear,
     you would betray!

Tears are flowing down    
     my cheeks, they slide
down deep into my heart
     where they abide.

I am drowning, have
     you got it honey,
that very tiny breath-life
      real piece of me?

Give it back! I'm dying,
     can't you see?
You've got the piece I need,
     come and heal me.

Keep who you are love,
     grow in every way, but
along that trodden road,
     please stop here today.

He:

Dear! Look!  See what I have,
    a treasure to keep,
A treasure for which I 
     know you do weep.

It does not belong to me
     any more, I must
return it now to you,
     with tears encrust.

I wrapped it in our past
     covered it with glory,
For I also cannot bear
     this sad story.

But rather your heart begin to 
     beat now again
And bear open your soul
     so it can mend.

She:

This piece dear that belongs
     to me, you kept
too long, the pain intense
     a grave offense!

I catch my breath, but it
    falters, and dies,
For without your heart,
     my spirit cries.

Oh God how did this come
     to be that you
have stolen so much from me!
     Oh what to do?

All else know the answer,
     there's plenty to spare,
You will find the fragment,
     your heart repair.

Mourners:

Let him keep that rare piece
     of you, and regret
that having met,
      he's in your debt.

For he can never return
    such beauty so pure
but for you there's
     ever so much more...

We miss your lilting zest
     for life, we will
wipe your tears, hold your hand,
     be there until

your smile tells us you're back,
     your eyes glisten,
your lips speak the soft words,
     to which we will listen.

She:

Oh  you ask so much of me
     dear friends!  I know
your faith is the gift you
     have to bestow.

I take it along with the love
     you have shared and
the pain I know  you wished
     I had been spared.

Take my hand, help me up 
     from this grave rise!
In him the well of tears
     will never arise.

You all know how my heart cannot
     come to conceive
what you and my head tells me,
     I must believe.

Damn him, damn him, this ogre!
      What can be worse,
An untrusting cruel lover,
      A bane, a curse!

Mourners:

Here we are,many hands 
     held out to you
take hold, and dont let go,
     they are your cue

that spirit can only die when
     you cannot see
what  you have and what we can
     give to thee.

But you must reach out to us,
     grab a firm hold,
take back your life, your heart,
     vibrant and bold.

She:

My good friends, take a look,
     unsteady and pale
I hold your hands and know
     I will not fail.

Thank you all for your love,
     your trust, your aid.
A path back to my full life,
     you have all laid.

No need to say goodbye 
     to cruel him..
Let my life with returned joy
     hope, faith, and vim..

continue without falter.
    I live to do
a celebration, a journey
    I always knew.

Him:

Don't you see, here I must be?
     Don't you know
that in life, here i stay,
     I cannot go!

I must believe you are in
     that heart-pain grave,
that you are less than me, who
     is so strong and brave.

For I cannot live and know
     your truth and care.
I have lost what few men have
     been allowed to share.

A soul,a heart dedicated,
     loving especially
this strong passionate 
     personality..

Fie on thee, to give me
     something so rare!
I know nothing to which
     you can compare.

If I cry for you, I will not
     ever stop, I will
Run to your heart, your soul,
     I love you still.

Goodbye dream, come nightmare!
     I name it you
I revile, so I can live
     it's all I can do.

She:

Memories I cannot keep,
     I can forgive,
and in doing so, I understand,
     I grow I live.
