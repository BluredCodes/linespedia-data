Love-Living

Her lips saluted
     his essence
and his self evaporated 
     into her heart...
      Love!

His lips curled into 
     her body
while she sustained 
     his oneness....
     Love!

Their lips met
    and melted
Though their minds
    did abstain
     Existence!
