---
title: "Farewell Father"
writer: "lilwing"
slug: "farewell-father"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/24933-Farewell-Father-by-lilwing"
---

I'll never make you 
     cry again!
Your tears scarred 
    my heart.
This burden was mine,
    not yours.
Did you have to go for
    me to see?
Is this your wise
    parting gift?
The sins of the father
    are only
Visted on the children
    when they are 
      blind.
