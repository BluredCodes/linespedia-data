title: "I Can't Get Pissed"
writer: "Bam Bam"

i have this friend,
she is my best friend actually.
we did not meet that long ago,
but as soon as we started talking we cliked.
now we are almost always together.
when we are not together my life is so sad.
nearly everything about our friendship is perfect.
there is one problem with it tho.
it is not her it is me!
this problem is that is just cant get pissed at her.
no matter what she says or does i just cant get mad.
i can get upset, just not mad.
she is the only friend taht i have that i cant get pissed at.
at some times i am glad,
but at other times i wish that i could.
she does some things that i dont want her to do,
and for that i wish that i could get mad.
but i jsut love her to much and always will.
she is my perfect friend!!!
