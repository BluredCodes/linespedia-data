A teardrop of wax
A splinter of rain
Die of love
Live with disease
Nothing can kill it
Nothing can kill you
The blood of others
Coursing through your veins
The thoughts of many
Running through your mind
Nothing is yours
No longer theirs
Take the breath of life
Take another meaningless
Drain the soul
Stop the heart
Lurk in the dark
And come back to me
