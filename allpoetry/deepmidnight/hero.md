writer: "deepmidnight"

A river of blood
Runs hard to the sea
Discovering a flood
Of all that is me

In a motion of darkness
He's put to the test
In an unstable fortress
He dies with the rest

His soul rises up
But then falls back down
To the devil he must give his soul up
But he won't penetrate the ground
