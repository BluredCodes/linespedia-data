---
title: "Midnight's Town"
writer: "deepmidnight"

A warm light passes over
The small town as it sleeps
In the mists of October
The lonely boy weeps

His soul was taken from him
The air has worn thin
And he'll die as the lights dim
New life will begin

Pale smoke rises from chimneys
Painting the dark sky
Claiming new rule over these
The light will soon die

~~with him by my side~~
