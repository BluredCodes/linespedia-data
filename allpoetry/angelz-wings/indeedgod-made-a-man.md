A man is made to make you cry,
but never to wipe your tears.
Indeed, God made a man
to trouble you through the years.

A man is made to make you mad,
but never to bring you joy.
Indeed,God made a man,
no different from a boy.

A man is made to bring you stress,
but never to soothe your mind.
Indeed, God made a man
to waste a woman's time.

A man is made to scream and shout,
but never to talk in sweet voice.
Indeed,God made a man
with hardly any choice.

A man is made to break your heart,
but never to make it heal.
Indeed, God made a man
to remind us  evil is real.

A man is made for "on call" use,
but never to be there for life.
Indeed, God made a man
to bring pain to his wife.

A man is made to plague the world
with deception in his heart.
Indeed, God made a man
to tear us women apart.

A man is made to rid the earth,
of the innocence we bear.
Indeed,God made a man
to make our life unfair.

A man is made with many faults
to build a woman's strength.
Indeed,God made a man,
to test our survival length.This poem is not directed to all men...males please don't get offended!!!
