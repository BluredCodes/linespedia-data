In every school there is at least one girl who is unbeleivably attractive.
Well, I wish I can say that girl was me,but I was more of the average prettiness.
Everytime she passed me by my heart filled with admiration combined with envy.
We were good friends in Junior High...that was when hardly anyone noticed her grand beauty.
But as soon as we came to grad school everything changed.She became stuck up.She would flip her hair in front of me and pretend she didn't know me.
She dated guys by the 10s.
While I was usually stuck with one long relationship a year.
She and I both tried out for Modeling  in our high school show.
Of course she got to walk on stage while other girls and I had to walk off.
She was always better than me no matter what.
When I wanted to play soccer, she had the grades and played.
She was kicking around soccer balls afterschool while I was kicking around textbooks!
So we met up afterwards at the pickup lot.Sometimes we would talk or sometimes we would smile or something just out of common courtesy.
Then one late after noon she actually worked up the courage to say..." you have a NICE body!!!""complete to go with a very beautiful face.""your perfect".
My heart dropped in an instant.I couldnt beleive the most beautiful girl in the entire school actually thought of me as PERFECT!!!
Then I started to notice that all those times she flipped her hair in front of me and pretended not to notice me...it was the other way around!She asked me directly why I had stop talking to her!I was lost for words.Because I spent too much time noticing her blinding attraction outside that I couldn't see that she had a soul!After that I'd walk the halls with my head up high.Still friendly as ever, Just with a little more confidence and sense.
