title: "too busy...."
writer: "angelz wings"

Too busy to see me,
not too busy to shut your eyes.
Too busy to love me,
not too busy to despise.
Too busy to hold me,
not too busy to push away.
Too busy to kiss me,
not to busy to play.
Too busy to talk to me,
not too busy to yell.
Too busy to comfort me,
not too busy to rebel.
Too busy to listen to me,
not too busy to shout.
Too busy to understand me,
not too busy to shut me out.
Too busy to come to me,
not too busy too leave.
Too busy to have faith in me,
not too busy to deceive.
Too busy to give hope to me,
not too busy to destroy.
Too busy to justify me,
not too busy to avoid.
Too busy to spend time with me,
not too busy for friends.
Too busy to last forever,
not too busy to watch it end.
