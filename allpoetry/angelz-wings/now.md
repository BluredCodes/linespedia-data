A second ago I was laughing,
now I'm blinded from tears.
A minute ago I was brave,
now I'm filled with fears.
A n hour ago I loved you,
now I only feel hate.
A morning ago I had a life,
now I belong to fate.
A day ago I held you,
now my arms are empty.
A night ago I longed for you,
now I long to be free.
A week ago I saw truth,
now I see the lies.
A month ago I was stupid,
now I have grown wise.
A year ago I was beautiful,
now I'm an ugly beast.
A decade ago I was certain,
now my thoughts,deceased.
A dream ago you were perfect,
now I dream you,a mess.
A life ago I was happy,
now I'm filled with stress.
