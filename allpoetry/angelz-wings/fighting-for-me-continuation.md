I need the world to know,
just what I'm going through.
I need the world to help me,
so I can smile too!!!
I've been forced to my knees,
By this unknown cause of pain.
I plead to the Lord,
my spirit cries in shame.
Tell me it's okay,
tell me you'll help me fight.
Tell me that in a little while,
everything will be right.
Lie to me,don't tell me the truth,
say there is a better place.
Say people love me,
and wish to see my beautiful face.
Make me beleive,
make me strong.
so I can return,
to where I belong.
Build up my feelings,
strengthen my heart.
Only you are able,
to see me in the dark.
Only then will I fight her,
I'll fight this witch in me.
Then the light will shine brightly.
For all the world to see!
