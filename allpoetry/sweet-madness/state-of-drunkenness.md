Drunk , dizzy and confused yet overwhelmed with joy,
All i see is grey and bursts of bright yellow 
flashing before my closed eyes ,
Blinded by life -- unable to see death ;
My lips wet with living , liquor of wonder on my breath;
The bittersweet clouds my brain with euphoric fog;
But then, by a clash of thunder,
I awake from this drunken stupor of lies 
And squint at the dark light of truth--
