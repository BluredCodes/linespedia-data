8 glasses of water 
Pretty , sweet , blue water;
8 thoughts to comfort me ;
Memories of drunken stupor , euphoric times ;
Memories of orgasmic heights - thoughts of mirthless pleasure
Hopes of comfort from the madness , cessation of this haunting
Nightmares of the demons watching , waiting ;
Daydreams of the fairies guarding , bewitching ;
Despairing thoughts of the hourless eternity trapped with just me
Thoughts of lifeless oblivion , thoughts of peace,
8 thoughts of nothingness ,
8 minutes till i feel the effects of my last glass ,
And 8 minutes till i can "sleep" .
