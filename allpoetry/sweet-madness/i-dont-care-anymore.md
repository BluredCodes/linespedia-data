---
title: "I dont care anymore"
writer: "sweet madness"

As I approach death I do not fear ;
Drained of hope, I have no will to care ;
Tears no longer flood these eyes ,
These lips no longer quiver as this heart cries ;
Locked inside this room,
My mind clouded with doom,
Not caring if I miss 
Your final kiss,
Not caring if this knife 
Will end my life ,
Not caring is my delusion 
As I finally escape this life , this illusion .
