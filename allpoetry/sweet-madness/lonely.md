title: "Lonely"
writer: "sweet madness"

Chilling wind against my skin
Breathing its breath of death on my neck ;
Holding me close, caressing me -
The only thing that caresses me - 
This chilling wind ;
Blowing my hair out of place 
And freezing the tears on my face ;
Caressing me and kissing me gently -
The only thing that kisses me -
This chilling wind;
Freezing my blood , my entity 
And killing the soul within me;
Kissing me gently and killing me soft ,
The only thing that kills me -so kind it is -
This chilling wind .
