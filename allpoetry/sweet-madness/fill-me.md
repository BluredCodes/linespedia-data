---
title: "Fill me"
writer: "sweet madness"
slug: "fill-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/38733-Fill-me-by-sweet-madness"
---

Empty my soul of sinful memories
Wasted nights, hopeless dreams;
Empty my heart of lost innocence,
Hardened love, acid tears;
Drain my veins,let the cold blood gush out ;
Release this mad essence,
And love me .
