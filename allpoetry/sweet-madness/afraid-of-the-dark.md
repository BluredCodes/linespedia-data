---
title: "Afraid of the dark"
writer: "sweet madness"
slug: "afraid-of-the-dark"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/38726-Afraid-of-the-dark-by-sweet-madness"

Every night i can see the fear coming towards me,
Blurring my vision ;
I can feel darkness stinging my skin 
As the light escapes through my window
And shuts me in with the demons 
I can feel the blood oozing from my throat 
As my sealed lips are kissed--
Goodnight
