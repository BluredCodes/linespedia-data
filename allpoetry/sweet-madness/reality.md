Hungering for knowledge ,
Afraid of truth , 
Defying hope with determined misery 
Yet begging for faith to live through this evil fantasy 
Watching suffocated minds 
With sardonic smiles 
Making hell our fetish 
As humanity dies
And brutality reigns begotten by base quantities-
Of self and survival .
Power hungry beggars divided we stand 
Trusting not even the soul within us ;
In faith do we trust ?
Nay, in faith , our souls do rust .
