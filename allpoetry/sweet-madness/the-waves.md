The waves lap my feet playfully 
Leaving a design of white foam around my ankles 
I am enticed and i accept their invitation
I wade knee high through the sea of blue crystal 
And giggle as the green fur of the rocks tickles my feet.
The waves now encircle my waist 
And i lose my balance 
Laughing , i plunge head first 
Into perfection , into happiness 
But, as i break the surface 
The waves encirle my head
no longer playful but intimidating
No longer enticing but menacing 
And they suck me down
Into their pit of darkness
Only to release me for a glimpse of beauty 
And drag me under once more 
Fear has caught my breath and held it ;
My tears are mingled with the ocean of despair 
And i am entertwined with death ,
