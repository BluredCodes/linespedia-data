---
title: "HIGH"
writer: "sweet madness"
slug: "high"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/37039-HIGH-by-sweet-madness"
---

I am blinded by this light 
Demon possessed i have lost my sight
Tremendous beauty is all I see
Visions of rapture surrounding me
Lost in a maze of ecstacy


So high i cannot see
An orgasmic thrill has devoured me
I cannot discern between truth and lies
My body is on a high as my soul dies


Beauty clouded in mystery
My eyes cannot fathom what they see
A damned Nirvana or holy Hell ?
Im lost in a daze as my brain swells .
