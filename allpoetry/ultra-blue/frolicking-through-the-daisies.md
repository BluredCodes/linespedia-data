title: "Frolicking Through the Daisies"

Frolicking through the daisies we go
Laughing and jumping, wide grins upon faces
Rolling in flowers, snickering away
Short light clouds floating lazily overhead
The darkness comes creeping, emptying the day
Rich indigo skies provide just enough light
Us skipping in flowerfields, the home is our way
Frolicking through the daisies shall resume another day
