title: "The Executioner"
writer: "Ultra Blue"

Standing in the gallows a shadowed figure waits
The screeching of a thousand screaming souls emit from the gates
Remorseless the man, his heart is torn black
He pulls on his cowl, brandishing his blood-stained axe
The dark figure stands tall, spirit unwavering
As the condemned man approaches the ominous wood block
The victim is hooded, as to not behold his demise
The hooded man cackles, an odd gleam in his eyes
As the axe raises, the crowd gasped and gaped
The axe was brought down in a single spurt of blood
The Executioner smirks at the thud
