Endless is this dream with fright
I'm in a losing battle but continue to fight
I only see through my subconscious mind
I search my brain and destruction I find
That is the field for my minds eye
Holocaust and war, why must they die
Because of our differences, we must be persecuted
Your holiest and naught are much diluted
Beliefs are far stronger than words
So why can't we think instead of being slurred
Endless is this dream with fright
This War of worlds we'll continue to fightAgain, symbolic
