---
title: "Blink"
writer: "Ultra Blue"
slug: "blink"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/45090-Blink-by-Ultra-Blue"
---

My soul is bleeding, tensions red
As I lay dreaming on my bed
Violent thoughts roll through my head
All in the blink of an eye

My soul is pouring, a blazing white
Is there anything I can do to make this world right?
Purity of thoughts flow through my head
All in the blink of an eye

My soul is streaming, a sharp tint of blue
My dimension set right and true
Fantasy of thoughts scroll through my head
All in the blink of an eye
