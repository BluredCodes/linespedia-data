title: "Army of Disaster"
writer: "Ultra Blue"

Tormented inside, good thoughts escape me
My life is soon ending, my soul full of greed
The Dark One advances to corrupt me evermore
I hold out my hand, accepting my fate
His shadow envelops me as my body grows cold
Screaming in agony yet spewing no sound
My dark soul begins ripping as I slump to the ground
Rending my spirit to the Dark Master
One more essence for the Army of Disaster
