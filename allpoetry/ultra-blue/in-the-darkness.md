Home for the night, tough work today
At my humble abode to watch the big game
Get out the keys and unlock the door
Open the door and can't find the light switch anywhere!
Stumbling around the big living space
Trying to find som...ouch my face!
Must have tripped over the couch
Tipsy to my feet I feel my way around
..OW!  I stubbed my toe
That stupid chair I swear wasn't there..
Feeling my way through the darkness I do
Trying to find my lightswitch, who knew!
Oh hey I think I fo..damn!  I stepped on a screw
I might have a disease so I feel my way to the door
Almost the..and crap I must have fallen over a board
I hit the wall and whadda ya know..the light came onThis sucks..I'm bored
