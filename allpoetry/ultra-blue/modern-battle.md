title: "Modern Battle"
writer: "Ultra Blue"

Swing, chop, slice, stab
I reach for ya throat but I miss tha grab
You evade my blade and dive out of tha way
You catch my chin with a solid blow but stay
I sway left, then right, blast the right hook straight in ya face
Left jab you throw so I roll right and make a jack for tha knife
Roll right over and snatch it up with the quickness
Next thing ya know it's flyin' at ya head with tha slickness
In the blink of an eye your right behind me, knife in your hand
You arc down and try ta cut me, but I fall down and kick ya in your left cap
You go face first in tha dirt, that's alotta blood, it must hurt
Cacklin' like mad I leap to my feet and kick up my knife
Quick as lightnin' I sweep across ya neck and get caught in a spurt of blood
"That gurglin' shit jus' don't sound right man" *blam*Damn I'm bored *cackles*  Just for the record stuff like this just happens, leaving me to merely write about it, not act upon it ;)
