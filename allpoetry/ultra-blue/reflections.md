---
title: "Reflections"
writer: "Ultra Blue"
slug: "reflections"

Reflections of the bad times and worst of decisions
Glare at us all from different directions
The raping and killing of innocent lives
Will come back to haunt you wherever you hide
When the mirror repeats you and shows that you're there
Just think of the world and how it might care
Death and destruction are in our past
Health and repair can come just as fast
The reflections of what has been are drastic and ugly
Yet reflections of what have been are beautiful and enchanting
What is seen in the water is merely a reflection
And ripples in the stillness can put your life in question
What will you do today? Or will you live to see another day?
Should you do it all now, or procrastinate and chance it
Reflections are beasts yet beauties, merely duplications
Of the decisions you make that determine your own reflections
