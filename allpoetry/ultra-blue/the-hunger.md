The hunger for the food is so great
I'm not fat, but big boned, so don't call me fat!
I roll through the fridge, but to no avail
I'm searching for large quantities of food, only to fail
Holy crap I ate it all, maybe I'll go eat that ball
My neighbors back door is wide open
So I roll in and raid the cabinets
But to my surprise they're all empty, my curiosity eats me up
Through the window, what do I behold
A large pig of a woman, only twenty years old!
In her massive ham hands, what does she hold
It appears a small child...now thats just gross
