title: "Crown of the Fallen"
writer: "Ultra Blue"

Never seen within the bright
Always travels in the night
Always creeping around
Never making the softest sound
Might be scary without a fright
Forever seen in such spite
For this creature has not been known
It lives on, its hatred grown
Couldn't have been noticed again
It has never had a friend.
All alone he sits and waits
For his overzealous bait
One day it will come around
Only to promise to wear the crown
The Crown of the Fallen
