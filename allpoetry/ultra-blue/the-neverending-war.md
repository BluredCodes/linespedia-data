title: "The Neverending War"

Once long ago an ancient war raged
Mystical and magical, bloody sword blades
Pawns on the Battle Field fought with all their might
The wizards and warlords planned just alike
Good versus Evil, Dark versus Light
Can never be solved, and neither will plight
Demons and Angels remain just alike
Though they bear different sight
This ancient war, diverse just the same
Continues to this day, and has only one name
The Neverending War, between poorness and fame
Between black and white, there are many colors
Though never the same, see more eye to eye
But still this war rages, bloodstained and broken
Healthy and fixed, but never the same
Will this War see end?  No, not ever
But only some rests in this wicked endeavorThis has symbolic meaning, you just have to think, it's not really that hard to figure out
