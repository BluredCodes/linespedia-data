title: "Trapped in her mind"

The tear fell from the young girls faceHer mind has left her in this open spaceAlone in the field of her mindNothing goes by, nothing but timeTears are rain , memories are treesA dying forest, no one hears her plee'sshe runs but goes no whereno one will help because they dont careAn angel comes to her to speak loveA soft kiss to the cheek and a hugI am the angel, my love is the girlI'd do anything for her, i'd give her the worldShe's been hurt to much, I want to saveI long for her touch, Her mind is her grave
