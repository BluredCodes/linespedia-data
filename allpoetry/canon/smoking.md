title: "Smoking"

What is smokin that gonna bring you
ur lovin that shit but its killin you
put it down and walk away
quit now the longer you can stay
its gonna kill you it'll end ur life
then what will become of ur husband or wife
dont leave ur kids alone to care for themselves
put it up and lock those shelves
save ur life..live longer
put'em away..grow stronger
it will make you cough and hack
it will take ur life which u cant get back
i dont want to see u die from a habit
if i see you light up im gonna nab it
ill throw them in the trash
im willing to risk getting a bash
im saving you cus i care
how many sons will...it's rare
