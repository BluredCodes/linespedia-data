title: "Back to stay"
writer: "Canon"

So many things I want to say,
I changed, not enough words to explain.
A fresh start, a new outlook on this life.
new things I wanna do, I didnt change over night.
How do I explain, the changes I underwent.
The message the lightning sent and person I represent.
A new way to speak and a new way to live,
so many things I wanna take and so little I wanna give.
Just a playa, let me save ya, let yourself train ya, it'll change ya,
thats the only way I can explain to ya. 

Im no longer a poet, ive lost all my respect,
because of who I represent, canon aint canon,
i guess thats what I meant. you can call me canon,
you can call me juice. call a truce take advantage of the fruits, of change.
I cant explain, i refrain from sayin what satin expects me to be sayin, playa im playin, im back as canon and dawg, i promise you im stayin.
