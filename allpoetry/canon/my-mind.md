It's because of you that I write these ryhmes
its what u do that makes me happy inside
u have changed me so much im a different man 
If you let me i'll give you everything i can
I know its hard sometimes,you've been through much
I'll try to be sensitive to your mind and ur touch
People go through a lot in life but you exceed that all
I wanna be there when ur up and when you fall
I think we can work it all out 
Soon you'll see what im about
I'm a nice guy and im sweet too
