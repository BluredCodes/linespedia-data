---
title: "She hurts"
writer: "Canon"

From out of the cold, he stepped inside.
into the warmth, that night she cried.
he didnt care, she pretended she did.
all her fear and tears, ducked and hid.
Her life had been strewn, her heart ached.
she thought she felt the love that he faked.
he is still on her mind, she cant get him out.
she wont tell me everything, i cant figure it out.
please baby, if he is on your mind, just speak.
there is no sense in wondering through life confused and weak.
