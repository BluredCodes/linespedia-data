title: "The girl far away"
writer: "Canon"

I know someone that i can never meet
She's really cool and also sweet
she like's me for who i am
I tip my hat and "thank you mam"
We seem to be perfect for each other but we dont know
we may possibley never meet so we take it slow
i show my sweetness to her, the way i roll
I may never meet her, she might make me whole
she seems perfect but only in words
she's sweeter than the prettiest birds
one day i will see her face
thats when we will pick up the pace
its quite possible she's the one
we dont know eachother..yet she calls me hun
i like talking to her she's the best
i guess this is what puts love and trust to the test
she lives far away, In a place called cold
