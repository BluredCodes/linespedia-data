Zeke is here again, wont this kid ever die
Zeke cant be killed and no one knows why
Well here is the rest of the story continued from the last
He picked up the woman by the throat and threw her in the grass
Walked to her again and and kicked her in the chest
He threw her in a trash compacter and now theres nothing left
He wobbled down the street like a crazy zombie
Thats where i come in...he saw me and jumped on me
started beating my head against the ground till i couldnt take no more
I got up with all my strength and threw zeke into a glass door
He crashed through the glass and tummbled on the floor
He started picking up everything he could in the back of the hardware store
got a shovel and started running towards me like a train with no brakes
I broke a wooden chair and created several clubs and stakes
One stake to the head and a club to the chest
