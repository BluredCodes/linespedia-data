As the young one lies down and cries and frowns 
He realizes where he is as his heart pounds
His arms flail violently and his tears roll
He feels worthless in this place, he feels un whole
No one in his life, he's by his self with no one to hold him
He didnt want to come here, this life sucks but no one told him
He looks around at all the people and thinks of a way out
He's been here 17 years and already this life is played out
~me
