I feel like my life is nothing but a test
Everything i do is a scripted quest
When ever i get close to someone they take them away
I guess the testers wanna see how much i can take
Im goin senial, im going paranoid
I feel my life is empty/void
I find it hard to believe what goes on
My life is a test my mind is gone
People are extras my friends are the actors
How long is this script, How long are the chapters
When it rains, was that scheduled to be
When will i pass this, When will i be free
You may think im talking of good ole fate
Im not, I think i some kind of bait
Its time they tell me...if its true
I know that it is, Im on to you
Im telling you...your answers are due
