Who in this world is my friend
Distrust is just around the bend
who can i tell my secrets to
who can i trust to help me too
when i day dream i think of who's been playin me
i always wonder who's been hatin me
I just cant tell when im gettin played
all those i thought of as friends have strayed
all the days my skies have grayed
my smile turns to  frown
my self esteem is goin down
my eyes start to blur,my tears start to flow
who can i tell,who can i show
if you cant trust anyone isolationism is the answer
not knowing who to trust is like cancer
it spreads to everyone till you become paranoid
you think a plan against you has been ployed
you start to cry when nothing is wrong
you turn to drugs..you reach for the bong
you drink away your problem
you stop lookin for answers...cus no ones got em
Thats when it ends
