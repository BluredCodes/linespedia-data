I had this nightmare once, 
I was so damn scared. 
I woke up though, 
thankful I was spared. 

The nightmare started, 
My dad had just died. 
I sat there in my room, 
My hands over my head I cried. 

The day after his death, 
My mom decided to get with someone else, 
I was so enraged, 
I couldnt control my self. 

Then I found out who it was, 
Richard simmons the non-fat Guy. 
I saw his face, wardrobe 
and video tapes and I started to cry. 

He came into my home, 
yelling one two three, 
screaming one more time, 
stretch them buns for me. 

Each night before I went to bed, 
he made me do crunches, a hundred a night 
I was so scared this one day, 
I saw him in the wrong light. 

He had horns, 
and fangs 6 inches long. 
he was dancing around a pot, 
singing some incantation song. 

He sprouted the devils wings, 
turned and saw me there, 
flew to where I was, 
And took me by the hair. 

He brought me to the woods, 
killed me, raped me, then did an excersise 
I watched him do this a hundred times, 
then I woke to my surprise. 

I was so scared
