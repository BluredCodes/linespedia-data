Our friendship has gone through times of bad and times of good
I couldnt let our friendship fall away...so i did what i could
now we talk somewhat and we have the occasional glance in school
I smile at you, you smile at me....so now everything is cool
you helped me when i was down, I tried to help you too
I think we need to be friends to help eachother with what we go through
Im a good listner and you have good advice
We tell eachother what needs to be said...even if its not nice
true friends we are we can help eachother when times have gone bad
Let me know when you feel down...i want to hel you when your sad
I hope you feel the same way...I think were pretty good friends
I will try to be your friend for as long as possible...i hope it never ends
