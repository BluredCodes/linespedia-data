There is something you people have to learn
Love is something you have to earn
You can't fall in love with everyone you date
Then what would be the difference between love and hate
Love is unconditional..under no circumstances
If you love someone you take all chances
If the relationship ends in a fight
Then you know that they werent right
If they love you they stay forever
leave you they will never
So look for another, cus their out there
