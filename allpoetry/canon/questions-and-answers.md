why is it?..I dont know
Can you see it?...does it show?
did I? I think so
I hope?..Does it flow?
What do you want?..nothing I was just wondering
Is that lightning?...i think its thundering
What?...oh...I understand
whoa...who is this man?
Is he?...I think he is
Wait a second..I dont understand this
I think it means....but im not sure
oh my god...yo is that her?
I think it is....wait no its not
No not her....over in that spot
do you see?...ya i think i do
What?...I wasnt talking to you
oh...my bad i didnt mean to intrude
oh..no problem..its ok...i didnt mean to be rude
can we?... ya we prolly should
Its gettin late...lets bounce back to that hood
that was fun...we should do it again
what if it doesnt work?...what then
