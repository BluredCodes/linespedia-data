It's my fault your so unhappy
It's my fault you feel so crappy
I apoligize for the problems I bring you
All I wanted was for you to be my boo
I need you to talk to me and tell me whats bothering you
I want to fix it because I want to be with you
Your so beautiful to me I cant resist your temptations
If you give me time it will all be better in our relations
Im not in it for your body im in it for your heart
I knew this slowing down would break us apart
Although one night of holding you is better than none
If you stay with me I promise i'll treat you like a woman
I'll give you all I possibly can
Please stay,Let me be your man
