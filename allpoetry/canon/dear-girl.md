Dear girl we need talk about whats between usSo please have a seat and ill tell you whats upThere's another girl on my mindIm wanting her to be mineBut before all that i need to tell youthis thing between us I cant follow throughI feel so bad that it has to be this wayI hope you take it well and will be okayI like this girl too much to pass her upThats why I cant have anything between usIt wouldnt be fair to you If I thought of her while with youSo Im gently telling you that it needs to endBelive me there will be other men
