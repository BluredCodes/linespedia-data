---
title: "No one realizes"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "no-one-realizes"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/18392-No-one-realizes-by-Canon"
---

If I were to die,would it be expected.maybe if I die,my true self will be respected.Will anyone actually care,or will i just be a sad loss.I spent all my life caring,and now that care is lost.No one seems to realize,that im actually a nice guy.So whats the sense of trying,so i wish myself to die.tears can be shed,but picked back up later.no one will come to my grave anyway,so throw me to the incinerator.Spread my ashes in a dumpster,because apprently im a trashy guy.Forget about me never hurting anyone,just dont care and push me to die.
