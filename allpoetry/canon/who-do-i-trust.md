Tell me who to trust and who do I roll wit
Tired of all this no trust bull shit
Need someone to talk to and tell my story
but i know everyone thinks its boring
i have no patience for this shit and gettin real tired
gonna snap lose family friends and maybe get fired
so help me out, thought i dont know how
what do i do, what about you
do you know what i need
how do i set my self free
be boppin hip hoppin writing ryhmes is fading away
no matter how much i write now my skies are still turnin gray
oh forget it ill do it on my own
ill show how much ive grown
who am i playin i aint got shit
split persons in my head havin a fit
canon and juice at war and i cant help
so someone tell me what to do with my self
