I found this girl to me she means so much
My heart starts to pound when i feel her touch
I look into her eyes and my mind starts to whirl
Everything in my life is good now due to this girl
She's so beautiful and incredible in her ways
This love has got my whole mind in a daze
Its definitley a good daze too
All because im falling in love with you
I love holding your hand 
I'll do everything I can 
to make you happy
