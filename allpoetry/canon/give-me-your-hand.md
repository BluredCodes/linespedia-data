Give me your hand Tonights gonna be grandIm gonna light a candleIt's love you cant handleYou've been waitin for this nightYou'll see everything is gonna be alrightWere gonna make love and its gonna be sweetIm gonna caress your body startin with your feetIm gonna kiss you all over...can you feel the heatThis is gonna be love that no one can beatFirst i'll start by takin you out to eatwe wont make it public we'll keep it discreteThen we'll go for a walk in the parkI'll hold your hand till it gets darkThen it's time I carry you back to the houseThen we start and i unbutton your blousewe'll go slow we got all the timeWe'll go all night till you hear the chimeI'll wake up and bring you breakfast in bedI'll sit and watch then kiss your headIm doin this all for your loveIts you I love
