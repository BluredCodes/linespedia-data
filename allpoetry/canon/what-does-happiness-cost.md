What does happiness cost in this ugly life?
What kinda person do I have to be like?
What kinda sacrifices must I make?
What kinda chances must I take?
What way should I live my life and why?
What did I do that I have to cry?
What does it take to mend my heart?
What must be done for me to re-start?
What other pain will I endure?
What other problems will I stur?
Whats the reason nothing good comes my way?
Whats the reason that woman I love wont stay?
What else can I say but happiness is hard to achieve?
What time can you take me away Cus I wanna leave?
