Can I enter your mind through those beatuiful eyes.
Everytime I stare into them, part of my depression dies.
You alone, are defeating my sadness, taking it all away.
It is taking some time, but i hope you believe what I say.
you are creating the life in me, which i have always dreamed.
I always thought my life would be depressive, or at least so it seemed.
I thank you for being with me, you are the only one that takes away the pain
You are my angel, i dont know if you see, you are my umbrella when I stand in the rain.
I love you more than life it self, I really hope you know.
Stay with me for life, you are my everlasting hero
