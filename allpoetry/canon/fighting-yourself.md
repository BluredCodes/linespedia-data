---
title: "Fighting yourself"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "fighting-yourself"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Flaming thoughts shot like shooting stars,
falling from planet of the mind.
coming to your senses in space
racing against time.
Wanting your sanity,
your sanity wanting you.
Insanity arresting sanity
and it all keeps haunting you.
forget all that you were told,
about life and its woes.
It doesnt matter what happens in life,
cus its the life you chose.
Don't remember whats worth remembering
and you should forget what you forgot.
whatever you think you is,
you should think your not.
So wish upon that star of yours
That falls from your mind
your only delaying the inevitable
you will suffer you procrastanator of time.
