We are the ones that fight the wars, we have no choice
we arent fighting for the right reasons, they dont want to hear our voice
We say what we believe but no matter what we say
They send us to die for there beliefs.... anyway
theres no way around it, your picked and your sent
They dont care one bit how your life was spent
we are the young generation considered hoodlums in peace 
yet we are praised when we fight in war, swept off our feet
I believe there will be a war and i think its okay
But speaking in general, why do they treat us this way?
we arent important or smart enough to make the decisions
but you are to old to fight for the way you want to be livin
so i guess it works out in the end...you make our choices
because we are only babies and we have no voices
We are young you are old....go to bed
