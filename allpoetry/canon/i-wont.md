Im always thinking of suicidal things
i dont think about the controversy it brings
i never want to do it it just comes up
im not gonna do it that would be F'ed up
I would be leaving my family and peeps behind
i dont wanna leave i'd be missing the grind
its not always heaven on earth
if it was there would be no pain in birth
i would miss to much in my life
friends,family,girls,maybe a wife
suicide is just a weakness cus u cant handle problems
it would be selfish cus evryones got'em
live life the best you can
dont give up be a man
