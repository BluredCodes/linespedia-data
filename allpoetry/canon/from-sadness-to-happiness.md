Breaking down isnt the hard part 
Id say its the restart
breaking down just hurts 
The pain?..It lurks
The things love brought
things that i thought
I've lost trust for alomost all
Ive placed myself behind a wall



I've never fallen in love
Almost though...i needed a shove
It was a girl i knew not
It was her name i sought
strangley attracted to a stranger
Thats not all..it gets stranger
Ive never seen or talked to her
I know she's there...im sure
She's beautiful and sweet
something no one can beat
It will be love at first sight
I will praise that night
ill wait till the day i find you
I'll love you
First i need to gain some trust
from my friends thats a must
i'll take care of it..i have to
Although im not sure what to do
but im taking down the wall
Its time to start lovin yall
im no longer mad at anyone
no longer will i shun
come here i'll give you a hug
the whole in my hearts filled with a plug
its only temporary it will be filled soon enough
Love...Love...Love...whats with that stuff
