first i was happy then i was sad
i was happy cus of what we had
then it all went away
u didnt want to stay
i told you that you would get bored of me
i tried to stop liking you i cant...im worried
when the numbness hits your body,brain,heart,and soul
you lose the love...your all alone..your no longer whole
its the bad boys you wanted..not the sweetness i had
im not gonna change..just to get you i wont turn bad
its not gonna work and im done so dont come crawlin
when u have no love i know you'll be ballin
i wish i could help myself from fallin
its not easy to turn the other way
seein you hurts...its how i pay
the love no longer flows through my veins
i need a girl one that can sooth my pains
