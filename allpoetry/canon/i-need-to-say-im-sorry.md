Baby girl i need to say im sorry,
for all the times I made you cry.
For all the times I wasnt there,
For those times I told you I wanted to die.
I need to apologize
For everything thats gone wrong.
I wish I had a voice like you
So i could sing you a song.
I want you to know 
that I will always be here
Im sorry for the times you thought i wouldnt.
let me wipe away your tear.

Mama I need to apologize
for everything ive done.
Ill try to make you happier
Ill try to be a better son.
for those times I made you feel unwanted
I just really need to apologize.
You always seemed to forgive me
and It always came as a surprise.
Thank you for everything 
that you have ever given me
I will take care of you till the end
just wait and you will see

Sometimes I need to say im sorry.....
For eve-rything that I do.
So I say im sorry.
I promise I never meant to hurt you.

Sometimes I need to say im sorry.....
For eve-rything that I do.
So I say im sorry.
I promise I never meant to hurt you.

Dad...how's it goin?
I think we need to talk.
This might take a while dad
so do you mind if we take a walk?
So many times I know that I hurt you,
I wish I could take it back.
Do you remember when I was younger?
I will always remember that.
You always looked out for me.
You always made sure I was okay.
Im sorry that we fell apart so long ago.
I want to go back to the old way.

Sometimes I need to say im sorry.....
For eve-rything that I do.
So I say im sorry.
I promise I never meant to hurt you.

Sometimes I need to say im sorry.....
For eve-rything that I do.
So I say im sorry.
I promise I never meant to hurt you.

I never meant to hurt any of you,
but I know I always did.
I hope that you can forgive me
I was a young and stupid kid.
I'll try to change my ways,
Ill try to show you more love.
I need you all to be here with me,
so when one of us feels down we can share a hug.
Sometimes my depression makes me forget
about the problems of all of you.
But I promise ill try harder,
Im really truly sorry...For eve-rything that I do.

Sometimes I need to say im sorry.....
For eve-rything that I do.
So I say im sorry.
I promise I never meant to hurt you.......
