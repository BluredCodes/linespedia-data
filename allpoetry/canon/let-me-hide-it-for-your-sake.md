---
title: "Let me hide it for your sake"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "let-me-hide-it-for-your-sake"

The tears that you see, rolling from my eyes.
Are not the same tears you see, when others cry.
these tears are rare, they only come at a certain time.
it is a point in life where life is pointless, that point is mine.
each tear represents a point in life when ive been hurt, theres many tears.
from my childhood to my teen life, the problems have gathered over years.
my eyes are red from crying, my head aches for each one i hold inside.
I cant change the way i am, im sorry did i upset you?..im sorry i cried.
let me wipe away my tears now...let me hide my pain.
Im sorry im not more exciting, my life it seems, is too mundane.
I will not show my sorrow, i will not bring you down.
i will hide it with all my might, when my heart and mind begin to pound.
forgive me, i did not mean to upset anyone, i will leave you in peace.
besides, it is getting late, its about time i cry myself to sleep.
