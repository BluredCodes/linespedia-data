To her:

You are beautiful in every sense of the word
I love you more than love means, you my girl
I know I told you this all before when we spoke
But everytime i think, talk, see you more thoughts prevoke
I feel love deeper each day that we be together
you say you want it to last...how long is forever
You say your falling, I fell and your on your way
I love you so much that im at a loss of words to say
I will cherish each moment that I have spent with you
I hope when you think of me, you feel this way too
My life has been turned inside out...wait no,turned inside right
My life has been backwards but you fixed it that one beautiful night
you are the greatest, love cant begin to express
There is more love, but only time will show the rest
Sleep on my shoulder, look into my eyes

To everyone:

I want nothing but to hold her, Hug her when she cries
Happy is not what I want her to be
Something infinitley better than happy
As long as she is with me I will treat her the very best
I hope this poem shows my feelings, i need them expressed
Long live the love between me and my boo
I hope one day she will love and cherish me to
