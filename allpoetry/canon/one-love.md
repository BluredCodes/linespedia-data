Baby girl,
I can see your beauty from across the way.
I can see your special in each and every way.
Nothin less than a queen,
you should be treated as such.
treated with the most, beautiful stuff.
lovely as can be, from head to toe,
baby girl I love you and I hope you know.
Your the best thing in my life, baby your my queen
baby your the most beautiful girl that Ive ever seen.
With dark curly hair, and those mystical eyes.
you came into my life, surprise surprsie

My baby girl,
I love you so much.
I can never go on, 
without your touch
please never leave me,
ill never leave you.
im thankful for our love,
baby my boo....

The most incredible in every way,
I can see your beauty in every word you say,
every game you play,
your gettin more beautiful day by day.

im glad that your my girl, and no one else
A beatiful girlfriend, I need you to my self
I need you baby, I hear your good for my health.
be mine for eternity, do you hear the wedding bell

My baby girl,
I love you so much.
I can never go on, 
without your touch
please never leave me,
ill never leave you.
im thankful for our love.
baby my boo....
