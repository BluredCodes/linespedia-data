What does suicide prove
it shows u cant handle the groove
pick ur self up and brush it away
its not that bad..skies just a lil gray
put down the knife,drugs,gun,or rope
talk to me..maybe i can give u some hope
dont leave ur family behind u gotta think of them too
everyone has problems hell i got'em i got a whole slew
sometimes crying can help you out
u'll realize what ur problems r about
let the fellings flow through a ryhme
everything will be better..u jus need time
come here..get away from the ledge
i know a lot of things put you on the edge
it happens to the best of us baby
people will help u'll feel better...well maybe
if its love that does this..dont worry u'll find it again
theres many fish in this barrel many people in this pen
i know what u need..u need some appreciation
god doesnt need u now avoid that confrontation
