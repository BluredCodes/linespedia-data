---
title: "He deserves to die"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "he-deserves-to-die"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/14469-He-deserves-to-die-by-Canon"
---

He is on my mind, all the things he did to you
makes me want to retaliate for things he seemed to do
sometimes i cant sleep, my anger just seems to build
give me the chance to kill this fuck and i will
i want to scream, beat his ass and kill him a hundred times
i dont care how many years i get for my crimes
i want to rip his head off, poke out his eyes
stab him with a fork until he dies
rip off his legs and beat his ass
write bitch on his chest then bury him in the grass
piss on the grass, dig him up and murder him again
its just a vicious cycle that i will never end
the pain he caused should come back a hundred times worse
bring him to his funeral dragging behind a flaming hearse
