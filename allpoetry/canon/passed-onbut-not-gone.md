You left us alone 
how we sposed to get on
we loved u..u left us so sudden
they wont tell me,why or what happened
you were so young 
u left and it stung,u left 2 kids alone
ones in jail the other knows not whats goin on
u changed the way we live life
why r u not in my life
why did he take u so soon
its our lives he ruined
Im sorry i didnt say goodbye
i couldnt believe you had to die
i couldnt go up to ur body
my mind was feeling shotty
u know i care even though i stayed back
dealin with death just isnt my nack
i miss u so much,u were my favorite aunt
thats why i send u this rap
r-kelly once said if i could turn back the hands of time
well i wish i could but i cant so i write u this rhyme
this is how i show my love for you
everyone down here has a way to show there love to you
i'll be up there soon i hope
i wonder on this earth like a mope
ill meet you at the gates of devine
its a beutiful place in my mind
when i die were gonna reunite
i dont know when..anytime..tonight?
i regret not spending more time with you i took it for granted
that night u died "dont leave us"is what i chanted
to all the peeps who lost they loved ones to death
thats what we live with its gods wrath

I love you Tina..we all do
