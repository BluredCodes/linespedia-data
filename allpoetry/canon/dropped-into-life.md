I've been beamed into this life i did not choose
A life full of hate crimes drugs and liquor booze
Have to make decisions that effect my whole eternity
My mind flames up and its always burnin me
Why do we get forced into lives of hate and pain
Why do we live with people that are crazy and insane
I guess thats just the way life goes
Been slipped into life that none of us chose
Good times are rare and bad times flourish fluently
Thats why i write and the words come fluidly
But when time runs out and this hell's doors close
We can ball at this phat party with my niggas and hoes
Block party up in N.Y.C for the end of time
Bored of this place and hate this life of mine
