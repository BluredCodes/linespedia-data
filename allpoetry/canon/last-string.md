---
title: "Last string"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "last-string"
source: "AllPoetry"

My heart has been ripped,
strewn,stomped on and smashed.
Sewn together, sewn together,
taped and re-attatched.

It is on its last string,
it has nothing left to hang on to.
I get hurt all the time,
im punished for what i dont do.

So if you see a heart,
lying dead on the floor.
then i have been hurt for the last time,
my heart couldnt take no more.
