I know this angel..she talks to me
She's says all will be ok...one day i'll see
She told me that the girl i want isnt worth the pain
She said love wont leave it lingers like a stain
I asked if she had a love repellant bleach
She said...think of her as a blood sucking leach
I told her i cant do that
She said think of her as a rat
I cant do that either
A snake that slithers
I said im not that kinda guy
the only other way..i take you to the sky
I asked for further information
She said come with me..its a wonderful sensation
Im kinda confused on what she's askin
She said in love i'd be baskin
How do i get there i started to wonder
then it happened...the lightning and thunder
I was lost in what was goin on
She said no one loves you be gone
She made me feel worse
Out pulled a hearse
She handed me a gun and said to do it
I said im not into that suicidal shit
She started to get mad and changed her attitude
she started to yell started bein rude
then out grew the horns and the pointy tail
the lightning got worse..it started to hail
Your not an angel you want me to be your slave
i wont follow you not down that cave
i see what your doing you think im gonna follow
my hearts broken not hollow
i dont even belive you exist
Thats when he got pissed
Now i understand its not satin
Its love its love im hatin
