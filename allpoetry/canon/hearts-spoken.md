I will love her till the end of timeTake her to fine restraunts for dinner and wineI will be all she will ever need My mind is giving up and my hearts is gonna leadTo find this woman is my dream and I pray for that dayI'll meet a woman that loves me and everything will be okayThis is the testimony of a true love filled heartPray to god we wont ever have to be apart
