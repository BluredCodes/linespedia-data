This is a story of a boy named zeke
used to play ball but his game was weak
he was outcast from the family and lived on the street
all he owned was the clothes on his back and the shoes on his feet
he went to school and was picked on all day
he got biffed in the head and started seein gray
didnt tell no body but it started gettin worse
on his way home he stole an old ladies leather purse
She didn give up the struggle, she kicked him in the junk
He hit her with her own purse and stuffed her in an open trunk
he got to the corner and fell to the curb
yelled a yell that everyone heard
screamin obsenities extremeley absurd
he took a hair pin from the purse that he stole
jabbed it into his skull creating a big hole
bleedin uncrontrollabley all over the side walk
now he's silent no more speaking cus he cant talk
a pin in his head and pedestrians surrounding
the only sound left was his heart that was still pounding
