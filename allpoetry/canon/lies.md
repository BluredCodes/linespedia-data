---
title: "Lies"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "lies"
source: "AllPoetry"

People are always spreading rumors
They start out innocent,but they grow like tumors
people dont realize the pain it brings
till it happens to them then it rings
it doesnt hurt you if your the speaker
but the recipients pride gets weaker and weaker
this would end if people would stop lying
That would end it,end the crying
