---
title: "I need to know"
writer: "Canon"

Ignorance is bliss,thats what i hear
not knowing whats goin on is what i fear
how can being in the dark be bliss
help me i dont understand this
hold my hand,guide me to the light
no ones there..this isnt right
how can i find the light alone
i stay still..still as a stone
yes im scared but dont go tell
id rather be alone..i stay in my shell
i walk away from help,i refuse to work it out
thats why im in the dark..cus i dont know what im about
i know i shouldnt..thats not the route
ok...help me work this out
first turn on the light
im gonna fix this tonight
im gonna make things right
its something i wont fight

im ready...give me answers
