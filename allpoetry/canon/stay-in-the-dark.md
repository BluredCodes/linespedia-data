---
title: "stay in the dark"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "stay-in-the-dark"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/14599-stay-in-the-dark-by-Canon"
---

Creeping in the shadows,
im hiding from the light.
my days are darkened,
i only emerge at night.

The sun is my enemie,
The moon is my friend.
daylight murders me,
when will this pain end.

I dont see an end,
I will never feel free.
my life is doomed from the start,
oh god, have mercy on me.

Endless nights of creeping,
lurking through the shadows.
during the days im sleeping,
at night im hunting in the gallows.
