---
title: "My 'Hero'"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "my-hero"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I want to hold you when your alone
I want to warm you when your cold
I want to be with you for an eternity
I will always love you the same, even when were old
My hands will wipe your tears
My arms will hold you tight
My thoughts of you will flourish
My body is yours, day and night.
The tears I shed will be for you
The prayers I pray arefor your soul
The love that you have given to me
Is the love that makes me whole.
Your arms arm my warmth
Your hands save me from my mind
I am pulled from a deepness by your eyes
Their Beauty is my life line
We are eternity
We are forever entangled in love
I will sing my heart to you soon
The song of our love will forever be sung.

"Hero" of mine you are
"Hero" you will always be
"Hero" I am to you
"Hero" and lovers for eternity.
