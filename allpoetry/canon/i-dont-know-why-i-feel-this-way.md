I dont know....
I dont know why i feel this way, suddenly i feel tired
my thoughts change to paranoia
i think people are out to hurt me
my eyes close but only to hide the tears
my fists clench
my anger builds.....
life seems worthless
no one seems to care
i feel heavy, weighed down
my chest constricts and it is hard to breath
my tears break through my lids
they streak down the side of my face
i begin to believe that no one loves me
everyone wants something from me
I pay for others problems and i cant afford it
this is what its like
I dont know......
