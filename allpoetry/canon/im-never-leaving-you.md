---
title: "I'm never leaving you"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "im-never-leaving-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/10153-Im-never-leaving-you-by-Canon"
---

What can I do to show you that I'm not leaving.That I am with you all the time, even when your dreaming.That your in my mind, I cant stop thinking about you.I never want to think of, how it would be with out you.So look me in the eyes, show me your beautiful smile.I need you to know, I'm only absent from your presence for a little while.don't cry, and if you do, don't try to hide.when you are like that, I want to be by your side.I will love you for eternity, you will always carry my heart.Remember and believe me when I say..... we will never be apart.So dont worry about losing me, only look forward to the next visit.Dont hide when you are hurt, show me, and you know i will kiss it.make it all better, im here for you and only you, im never leaving.So kiss me goodnight, smile when we part, think of me when your dreaming.
