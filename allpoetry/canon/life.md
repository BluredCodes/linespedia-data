Our lives are a river we must row
Many choices determine the path we go
Some people dont choose..but go with the flow
that has consequences that i will never know
Is it right....the life that i chose
I always feel like a dying rose
When im down no one ever knows
I try not to think of my weary woes
so row the river and remember your choices
listen to your heart not the voices
