Can't walk the halls without hearin some kinda beef
This bitch hates this bitch and they draggin in other peeps.
Flippin the bird at the locka, and maybe a push or two.
some peeps bite so much beef that they mouth cant chew.
Racist hicks dissin spicks and bitches dissin niggas wit little dicks.
Why is it all about I hate you and the dissin you spit?
Ya hair is nappy and ya moms is like a bike,
everyone gets a ride on that stupid ass dyke.
Ya girlfriends a trick and she be trippin on everyones dick,
fellas show no respect and call they girl a chick,
this shits sick and it needs to be fixed and kicked to the curb.
when someone disses you just act like you aint heard.
