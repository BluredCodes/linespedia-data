In 1982 in southern honnaluhu a woman named mary sue gave birth to barry lou with a length of a foot 2 and 8 pounds and ounces 2 he was abused by his father at an early age locked in his room like a demon in a cage just buildin up his rage writin about it in his journal page after page and spittin the ryhmes on stage. 

He wasnt popular and couldnt hang with the guys so he started thinkin it would be better if he died and he contimplated suicide had it planned for his birthday at sunrise. it came and he had to decide if suicide should be the way to die before the hammer slammed he changed his mind.

He took the nine that should of took his life brought it to school along with a knife ready to take a life. he knew deep down it wasnt right but he thought it would make things alright if he took a bullies life so with the blade of the knife he sliced toby trice twice watchin his blood spill like melted ice.

He popped off two rounds a girl hit the ground while scared teens ran around more shots began to pound blood spillin and more teens hittin the ground with the sound of the nine as barry watched the blood with the eyes of a hound.

He thirsted for more and bursted out the door and released the rage form the core blastin more and more while he screamed and swore that he wasnt gonna take it no more.

He grew up to be one of the greatest repeated prison escapist and repeated murderer and rapist that carved his name in the faces of his victims while he was burnin down their places and after cases and cases of murder cases and endless police pursuit and chases he finally gave up he knew he was done
he knew because he made up for the love that never was by killin everyone.
