---
title: "Halloween Kidnapping"
writer: "Canon"

The little ghosts and gobblins come creeping to his door.
They want some tasty tooth killing candy, but he has no more.
he looks around for a treat to give to the kids, he can find none.
he sees a little peice of candy but its only enough for one.
he chooses not to play favorites, he asks them to come in out of the cold.
he is a sort of tall man not to fat not to skinny but he's kinda old.
he takes the children to the basement, one by one they go down, but never never return.
the last little boy asks why, the mans laugh begins to churn.
the little boy gets scared, he tries to run, but his barney costume is hefty
the man begins to ponder what to do with the boy, "hmmm i dont know, lets see."
he grabs him by the tail and drags him to the basement, now let the torture begin.
"I am a stranger, dont you know, you are in trouble, you should have never come in."
the children never returned, this is the annual kidnapping that takes place.
everyone knows this man takes the kids, but no one has the guts to look at his face.
so he goes on kidnapping, the parents even know which house he lives in.
they must not want their kids because they dont care if they go in.
all they know about the name is the name on the mailbox, but the letters are kinda weak.
all anyone can make out is that it looks like the name is zeke.
