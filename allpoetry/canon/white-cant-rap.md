title: "White can't Rap"
writer: "Canon"

Im white, Rappin aint right no matter
how tight the rap that I write.
Canon you white, you cant rap,
you aint black, quit with that.
I see the words but the white nerds
see me as imitator, they nigga hater,
lyrical raper.

Why do I get hated on, ugly rated on,
so many times I stated on, my views
black and white, its all the same,
we all play the same game.If what i got
is talent why cant I have it just cus im not black
I cant rap, I shouldnt even listen to that.
My race has nothing to do with my place in this rap space.

I cant wear rocca, polo, tommy, without shit
Im tired of it, im tryin to quit.I aint gangsta
but I aint a' hata, im just wrapped in the culture
picked by the lyrical vulture.

The next time you say white cant rap cus it aint right and you aint black
think of all that i just spat im tired of this crap, this shit, fuck it.
