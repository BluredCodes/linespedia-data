When time stops were will we be
when theres no where else to go what will we see
When the lights go out what will we think
when the grounds crumble will we sink
what to do when heavens gates close
well Its our fault, this is what we chose
Why do we need to go out like that
We did nothing..around we sat
we are lazy and heartless peeps
we live like scums and dirty creeps
why blame anyone else, its our prob
we live life loveless, hearts throb
we dont know what love is we just assume
we care less when its over..we just resume
well im not goin out like that...time to rise
we have to stop thinkin of our demise
we need to accept our fate
we need to stop the hate
we can get through this and maybe more
We can unlock and open that door
what door?...the door of freedom
Wonderful angels wait...i seen em
we must confess...lets rethink our way
