Feel free to write freely for the purpose of expression
Write the way you feel for the release of all your tension
Express what you say with whatever it takes
Get your point across like your lifes at stake
take that pencil, take that pen, write down what you feelin right then
write till the very end
write down your life as you see fit
how you deal with all this worldly shit
exaggerate if you want, but put your shit down
I aint playin, dont just sit around
who cares what people think about the shit you write
if they hate it, fuck it, I know I sleep fine at night
