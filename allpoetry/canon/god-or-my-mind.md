---
title: "God or my mind"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "god-or-my-mind"

I sent you a letter,
but you never sent back.
I asked that you help me,
but you didnt care about that.
you ignored me all my life,
putting me through hard times.
wanting me to hurt myself,
making me commit crimes.
I struggled against you,
tried so hard to save myself.
Hanging on to every moment
But now there are none left.
You saw me cry,
but you never seemed to care.
You always hid in the darkness,
But i always knew you were there.
Your eyes judging me,
well judge me not.
Im better than you make me,
Im nothing of what you thought.
Im stronger than you make me out to be,
I've got strength of the heart,
I must struggle to survive,
but i never quit after I start.
You can do what you like,
Try and make me crazy.
But I will come out on top,
Im no longer a fuckin baby.
Drive that stake of hate through me,
I will only flinch a bit.
I will live on eternally,
until the eternal flame is lit.
