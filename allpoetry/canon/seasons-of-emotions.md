title: "Seasons of emotions"
writer: "Canon"

I search my mind for reasons
Why my life is like the seasons
But latley i seem to be stuck
I seem to have run out of luck
I seem to be stuck in spring
Cus its tears problems bring
I dont think i can take another thing
I've been pushed passed the line
Everyone i know is swine
No one wants what i got, but its the best
Rejection is like a shot to the center of the chest
Stop the hearts, slow the minds
No more love till my sun shines
No more help till im happy with life
No more me till im rid of strife
stop the clock, close the door
My life is empty, my heart is poor
My mind seems to wander
Plans i no longer ponder
i have no more answers for you
what do you want me to do
when i try to help i recieve nothing
At least give me something
please someone give me thanks
So i can rise in the ranks
Find me a girl who knows love
So i can have her and rise above
rise over the emptyness in my emotions
I got love like oceans
im ready to give it away
but im gonna ration
my love will be hard to get
If you dont believe me, make me a bet
i can hold love back
I know my chances are stacked
Just find me girl
Find me a pearl......
