title: "Zeke is dead and alive"
writer: "Canon"

After I clubbed him in the chest he started to groan
I stabbed the stake through his heart and he started to moan
he slowly dropped his head and his knees fell to the ground
he started speaking this weird language and making a weird sound
I slowly walked behind him...slow like in a movie
He pulled a gun and pointed and tried to shoot me
I kicked his arm and the gun fell to the dirt
From behind him i twisted grabbed and pulled his shirt
"Listen to me zeke its time for you to leave"
I wrapped my arms around his neck choking till he couldnt breath
then one hand on his chin and the other on his head
One quick twist, i snapped his neck...now he is dead
but that is not the end because i started to feel his breath
I heard his voice..."you will forever be haunted by my death"
A feeling shot through me and My knees became weak
this isnt the end, because forever more i will be zeke
