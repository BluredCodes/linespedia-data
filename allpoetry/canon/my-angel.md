---
title: "My angel"
writer: "Canon"

Knowing that I love you, and knowing you love me.
Makes me happier than you can imagine, I hope that you can see.
you are my angel, sent from up above.
you being in my life makes me feel unbelieavably in love.
hold my hand, hug me tight, kiss my lips and say goodnight.
your my love, a beautiful sight, being with you makes all the bad alright.
peaceful hearts, happy smile.
kissing you deep, falling all the while.
i know we are young, we will be together forever though.
I love you so much, More than you will ever know.
kiss me again, hold me close to your heart.
mentally my sweet love, we will never be apart.
