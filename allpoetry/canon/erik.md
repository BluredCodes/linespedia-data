Listen up ya'll im bout to spit some rage
Im about to release my anger from its long to locked cage
This one goes out to erik, that little fuckin bitch
im'a slice that nigga'z tire and end him up in a ditch
tie a rope around his neck and take him for a ride
bring him to a dungeon and skin that bitches hide
Burn his intestines, stab at his heart with a stake
The sooner I can kill this bitch is still to fuckin late

You hit a fuckin girl, causin her much grief
Now im fuckin you up when I see you, now we got beef
I aint never liked you, I never fuckin will
Wait for me to slice ya throat and watch the bitch blood spill
187, premeditated, however you want it
gun, knife, rope , whateva nigga you got it
you lucky I aint seen you hit her or else I would'a flipped
dont let me catch you near my girl nigga or ya gonna get cripped
Out of spite i might knife ya ass tonight
kill ya in ya sleep or on the street ya fuckin dyke
I aint fuckin around dawg, im fuckin you up
dont fuck wit me bitch cus you bouts to get upped
