---
title: "End of the fall"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "end-of-the-fall"

I have three words that run through my head
I dont want to go through life with out them being said
But I cant say them now because I might get hurt
They have to do with the heart that lays beneath my shirt
It's hard to have these feelings and not know what to do
It's more of a phrase that begins with "I" and ends with "you"
I hope there's a point in life when I can say this phrase
I know I'll feel this way till the end of my days
Your afraid of commitment, and you dont know if this is right
Well you know I can wait,... night after night
So I hope life is good and things go my way
If things dont work out I wont know what to say
But there is no more falling, I've reached the end
Im sorry if I seem down, I just dont want to get hurt again
