I confessed my love to you, and you to me
I was so happy i started thinking, it couldnt be
Someone loves me, My mind began to race
I love you baby,I could only think of your face
You are my dream girl and my love grows strong
This feeling will never fade, your name is my song
your eyes are my door to your soul, I Love you so much
Your love, my love, im whole, Theres feelings i cant touch
My love is so deep, ive never even seen where it resides
You have found it, the place where my happiness hides
Softly kiss your lips and i tell you that i love you
There are so many things i want to say and want to do
My mind knows and creates images of me loving you forever
I hope you love me for awhile, Im happy we're together
I love you sweetheart
