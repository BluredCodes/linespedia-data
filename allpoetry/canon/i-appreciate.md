I appreciate all the love that I recieve
I wanna show love back like you cant believe
maybe im lazy cus i just dont seem to rate
but i dont want any of yall to think that i hate
I may not write big long reviews but they mean the same
I want you to take my words to heart cus i do the same
if i say its good than i really think that its good
i want you to like what i say becuase you should
i take to heart what you say...when you say that I suck
thats when canon gets involved...screamin what the fuck
But he loves you too just like i do, no less and no more
Check back to him often to see what he has in store
