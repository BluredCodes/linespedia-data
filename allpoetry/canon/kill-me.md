---
title: "Kill Me"
writer: "Canon"

Kill me in my sleep so I dont have to feel the pain
Just take a needle, insert some air and insert into my vein
the instructions to murder me are labled on my back
Many ways to kill me but you can improvise on that
Tie a tie around my neck and hang me high hangin high from the sky
I promise that I no matter how high im strapped I wont cry
No tears will be shed during the murder of me
I dont care how I die as long as I dont have to see
No blood can be spilled, no evidence left to be found
Do what is needed, I wont scream, I wont make a sound
Stealth murder is all that I ask for, and all that I need
I dont care how im murdered, as long as I dont bleed
Clean death, no breath,choke my throat,drown me and whatch me float
Kill me then bury me in the wall, paint it and apply a second coat
If I wake before I die, try to close my eyes so I dont have to see my demise
When im good and dead spray some lysol on me to keep away the flies
7 years later take me from the wall, bury me properly in the bed of a casket
But before all that, cut my head off and place it in a potpoury basket
