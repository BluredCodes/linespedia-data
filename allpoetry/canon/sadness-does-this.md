---
title: "sadness does this"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "sadness-does-this"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/265-sadness-does-this-by-Canon"

I close my lips,I talk no more
im shutting that social door
i have nothing to say anymore
i sit and think what god has in store
when can i talk again when can i realize
maybe when my problem stops and dies
i wont talk but ill look into ur eyes
i try to fix problems but eveyone cries
i hate to see that happen
thats why i started rappin
these problems keep me snappin
who can i put my trust in
i need to let people know
i'll rise,i wont feel so low
im sick to my stomach..does it show?
the lines keep comin..im lettin it flow
its just a temporary fix for my soul
im always hurting,ill never be whole
im goin nowhere im stuck in this role
god doesnt care its my heart he stole
he drops it,he kicks it,he lets it die
when will it be my time to fly
its so slow..thats how it goes by
i dont talk i may seem shy
truth is i just wanna die
good night..good bye
