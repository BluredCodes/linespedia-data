title: "Nightmares"

The nightmares are getting worse
my mind seems to be under a curse
these dreams will lead me to a hearse

they come for me in my sleep
i dont know him he's an unknown peep
he's ugly he's nasty he's such a creep

leave me alone put down that knife
i wont give in to you..u wont take my life
ur only a dream a form of my strife

i wont go down with out a fight
this will all stop on this night
get out of my head..i turn on the light

When im a wake no harm can be done
im tired of this..even in my dreams i run
next time i sleep im goin with a gun

i fall back asleep..i create my weapon
he comes to me..he seems to be steppin
the gun doesnt work..its just not helpin

Nothing can stop him 
dreams forever grim
i sleep with the lights dim

Good Night
leave on the light
thats how i fight
