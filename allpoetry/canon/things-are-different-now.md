good bye to you im glad its done 
i was tired of ur shit i dont need it
the only other thing i could do was run
that would be mean u would have a fit
it wouldnt be to bad i was tired of ur shit
i dont mean i dont like u but i sure as hell like u less
its better this way i dont need that stress
i figured out what the problem was i cant believe u could do
u do like **** if not him then who?
it all started the day u met the rat
whatever i dont care its my turn up to bat
u thought these poems were for u..u were mistaken
they make me feel better what the hell were u thinkin
well i feel better now...dont know bout u
i dont care cus neither do u
i shoulda realized earlier and flew
im back in the game im lookin now
i've opened my eyes i raised my eyebrow
she's pretty she's pretty she is too
so many girls to try whats a brother to do
im gonna take phone numbers now im gonna be more brave
ill find someone that loves me i wont take my love to the grave
i still like u theres no way round that
but u did it to me i cant take that crap
u not talkin was an invitation for me to snap
my skies have cleared,my head is up
ill be ur friend....if u want that is
its just i didnt know what ur problem was
i dont think we can date
why u ask...just because
i dont want to show u hate
but things have changed u didnt wanna give me a chance
when love comes my way again im gonna take a fighting stance
the day is mine my life is complete
i guess to **** i couldnt compete
listen to me blabber on about this..u dont care
well neither do i...even  if it was rare

Good Bye i will always care about you..remember that.
