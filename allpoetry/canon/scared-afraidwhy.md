title: "Scared, Afraid..why?"

Watching the sun go down
My smile turns to frown
As day turns into night
happiness turns to fright
darkness brings fear
eyes start to tear
try not to move, dont make a sound
i hear something but can't turn around
i cant face what it might be
scared about what i might see
teeth gripped tight..i get goosebumps
i hear it again and my heart jumps
tears flowing, hands sweating, goosebumps bumping
and the whole time my heart is jumping
fear is all that stops me from success
it all brings a new form of mind numbing stress
Im out here alone, im in a frozen state
when the sun comes up i'll be great
but till then i'll be afraid
scared of how things will be made
scared of death,problems and pains
all these things run through my veins
here comes the sun, confidence rise
till it gets dark i wont think of my demise
