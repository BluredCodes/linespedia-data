I listen to your problems i give you advice
what i get in return is "thanks josh ur so nice"
its hard having every single answer
its a disease..almost like cancer
I have no problem helping you out
but its me that no one thinks about
things like that make me scream and shout
u say u can help but its something i doubt
the problems i have r unbreakable,they will stay with me 4 ever
u ask when i'll be over it..my answer is never
things like this dont just go away
its me we talkin bout..they gonna stay
it makes me feel good to solve ur grief
but mine lingers for all time..thats my beliefe
I know i am your problem solver
what is mine...maybe a revolver
nah i cant do that its a sign of weakness
problems arent so bad..there a form of stress
i should start a problem solving class
i'll feel better about my sorry ass
tell me your problem
answers?..I got'em
