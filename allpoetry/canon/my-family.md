Im starting to hate my family for what they do
i get pushed around and im always lied to
im the best person in this family...got my shit straight
so you would think im happy, but wait
i have family that does stupid shit
they get the attention...thats it
i wont take it anymore...im gonna remember this
maybe when im gona you'll miss
well it will be to late by then
its their fault for the way its been
i do good in life im respectful and caring
well its love their sharing
but not with me..i know they care but it doesnt show
"josh get over it, you got it made"..no
If i fail in life its cus i couldnt fight anymore
they couldnt just let me be...let me soar
well guess what  FUCK IT!!!!
im tired of this stupid shit
So when im poor sleepin on the sidewalk
dont come near me wantin to talk
