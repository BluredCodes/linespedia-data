---
title: "Making love"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "making-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Speaking erotic fantasies slowly in my ear
Kissing your neck and holding you near
closer than close, my lips to yours
let my tongue open some of your closed doors
sweet aroma of your hair and your skin
I love your life that i strive to be in
Holding you to me and kissing you deep
My fingers in your hair putting you to sleep
We dont have to have any type of sex to make love
With you I am perfectly content with a meaningful hug
