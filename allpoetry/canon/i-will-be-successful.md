---
title: "I will be successful"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "i-will-be-successful"

I need to be succesful when im older
i need to be stronger i need to be bolder
i cant be the guy with beer stains on his shirt
i cant be the guy who's always covered in dirt
i wasnt meant to be greasy and scummy
i wasnt meant to be poor and bummy
i need wealth thats all 
write songs or play ball
its not that im stuck up, i just have dreams
this is what i need that is all it seems
well not all...i need love too
getting these things is what i'll do
i dont see myself on welfare
im not the one who gets the dirty stare
i clean myself i wash my hair
I will not let myself fail
my life is gonna prevail
soon i will have everything in perspective
i'll be polite and respectful
it might take some time but i'll climb that ladder
i wont let life bring me down, my life wont shatter
i'll prove that not all nice guys finish last
no matter how many have in the past
care about people, hold doors, have manners
all that matters
you'll see..all those who dont believe i can
Im gonna grow to be a successful man
