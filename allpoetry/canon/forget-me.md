---
title: "Forget me"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "forget-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/228-Forget-me-by-Canon"
---

My life is over now it hurts so much
i have to live without her touch
im left in this world to live alone
im done with sweetness i have grown
i realize now that sweet doesnt work
it just leaves me hurt looking like a dork
my heart is broke my mind is numb
my life has ended i feel so dumb 
im worthless now i cant go on
i let my heart speak only to get stepped on
**********************************
**********************************
i tried my best to mend the broken
right now i so feel like croakin
why live if love kills you inside
why should i stay on this crappy ride
whats left for me here not a damn thing
i mentioned my feelings they would have  led to a ring
ive never been upset about losing a girl 
now im so sick i think im gonna hurl
nothing can match the pain i feel from it
you've left me alone im in a pit
kick me diss me shoot me kill me its no matter to me
im left in this useless life to never be happy
the ballons have popped the skies have turned black
there is no hope...i can never get her back
my mind was racing now its dead
what was it that ran through my head
i had feelings but never more
i dont care about anyone anymore
i say goodbye to this useless life god take me now im ready
will the pain ever stop or will it stay and hurt on steadily
im not gonna commit suicide 
i might as well finish this crappy ride
to all those who care...the love i showed was very rare
