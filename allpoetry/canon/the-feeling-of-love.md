---
title: "The feeling of love"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "the-feeling-of-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

Slipping through time with no destination in sight
I cant tell whether its dark or light..day or night
my head is spinning and im not sure why
No im not doing "pot" no im not high
im not on drugs i dont know why I feel this way
dont leave me to feel like this alone...please stay
I can feel the odd feeling all through out my body
I think im a little vulnerable...someone might rob me
can you feel it...oh your starting to
I know what it is....I love you
