Do you post poems or do you just read?
Are you invincible or do you bleed?
Do you have feelings about how to handle life?
Have you been hurt, broken, maybe cut by a knife?
Can you organize your feelings, sit down and write?
Does fear get the best of you when you lay down at night?
Does your mind constantly create images of hate and pain?
Do you wish you could drown yourself when you sit in the rain?
Does a pen look like a tool for expression ...and a door for help?
Have you ever wondered what it would be like.. if you killed yourself?
So now tell me, do you post poems or do you read?
Well if you would start to post, maybe you wouldnt bleed
Try at least once, it may take away the pain
Next time write about it when you sit in the rain
Cut into the paper with your pen, not your skin with a knife
Poetry is an extension, and can even release pain in your lifeI know all poets are not depressed but im saying if you are that maybe you should try to write instead of hurting your self or others...it shouldnt hurt to at least try
