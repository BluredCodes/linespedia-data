dedicated to the lives lost on 9\eleven\zero one
Tragic losses this morning has left us stunned
many thousands reported dead, we pray for your families
we dont know exactly who attaked us...maybe from over seas
we send our love to those who lost their lives
from where you are now you should still here our cries
terrorism, politics...together make me sick
we pray, we love, we cherish those who lost it
