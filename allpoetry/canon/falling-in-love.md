---
title: "Falling in love"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "falling-in-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

Your in my dreams when i close my eyes
Everything you say brightens my skies
words cant express the way i feel
but you can be sure that my love is real
your hand in mine makes me fall even more
Your kiss to my lips made my heart soar
i feel like a bird when im with you im high
When I see you my heart starts to fly
I feel a way I have never felt before
Looking into your eyes makes me love you more
I'll give you everything in the world that you desire
You being in my life makes me fly even higher
falling for you more each and every day
With you here my skies will never be gray
