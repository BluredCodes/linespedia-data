---
title: "It's hard to rhyme my love"
writer: "Canon"

I sit here trying and trying to show you my love,
its evident my words are to small to compare.
I cant write you anything that shows you love,
only my actions can show you I care.

I go through verses and verses,
but none mean enough.
Nothing I say can show,
or compliment what its like to be in love.

Im so in love with you, 
when I try to write about you it seems so bland.
So I turn from my computer,
look at you then hold and kiss your hand.

Im inspired by your touch,
the softness of you skin.
Loving every second of our kisses,
loving this relationsship we're in.

Someday we'll be married,
we will become one with a ring.
We're in love for eternity baby,
we are progressing in our love, I love whats Happening
