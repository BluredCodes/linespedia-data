Sally...a little girl from somewhere in kentucky
She seemd to love her little world, seemed as if she was lucky
She would play in the park everyday after class
Her and her friends playing duck duck in the grass
Kick ball in the sun, and tag in the rain
who new her playing here could bring so much pain
across the street parked a blue van with utah plates
A man dressed in black took her and traveled cross the states
He took her far away into lands so unknown
Until deep in the forest they reached the mans home
Poor sally just wanted to play with her friends and lead a normal life
All that ended when this evil man stole her and murdered her that night
Her family has yet to find her, their hearts need some ease
They ask anyone "if you know where sally is then please..."
sadness over came the town somewhere in kentucky
For it is now that they realize their not very lucky
