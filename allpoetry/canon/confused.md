title: "Confused"
writer: "Canon"

Falling out a non exsistent window
Walking into a closed door.
Kicking my self all the way,
Watching the bloody rain poor.

Dripping through my ceiling,
The one thats not there.
Pretending im important,
realizing i dont really care.

searching for a reason,
I know there is none.
I get creative,
and make up some.

I fall to the air,
Fly through the ground.
Im so confused,
my head starts to pound.

I have been born to death,
Im stuck between dimensions.
So I live through my death,
and learn my deadly lessons.
