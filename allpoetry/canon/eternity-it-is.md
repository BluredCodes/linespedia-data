title: "Eternity it is"

I see an angel in you, an angel you are, an angel you will be.We confessed many a times, I love you, and you love me.We promised each other so much, I intend to keep my part.I know you will keep yours, thats what gave you my heart.I realize with such intensity, your love is amazingly true.I will remember like yesterday, The day i confessed to you.When I first took that trip through your eyes, I didn't want to leave.So I stayed in your eyes, you closed them, and went to sleep.That is when you caught me, that is certainly when it began.That is when we entered the road of love, we held eachother and ran.Eternity is our love, Me in your eyes, you also caught in mine.Our souls have touched, we will be together for the rest of our time.We are here for eachother, We are eachothers umbrella in the rain.We are eachothers savior, we always try to take away eachothers pain.So let our lives be full of love, let our love be full of life.One day I will ask you, while on one knee, will you be my wife.
