whoa whoa uh oh I did it again, wrapped my self in a sheet and tied it at the end, rolled my self down the street harrassin little people that I meet definitley not bein descrete then i had to take a leak but i was wrapped in the sheet and I couldnt get free, I started pissin and pissin all over I myself and me.I went to the roof of the hospital crawled like a small turtle wearin a gurtle and restrain around my ankles depressed like kain was shootin hoops out side the Y makin the hoopsters cry cus I was fuckin up the mother fuckas like their fuckin mothers should of and would of if I hadnt beat'em to it, shootin right through it i threw down the ball and said screw it i picked it up and threw it back to the druids who did the dirty deeds in dirty sheets they found on the streets so did i myself and me i was just playin but i pissed on myself and left a stain.
