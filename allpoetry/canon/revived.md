I cry, i feel...overwhelmed,
It is too much.
I stare into her eyes,
waiting for her touch.

She wraps her arms around me,
just a simple hug.
the heaviness receedes,
with the confession of love.

A caring kiss,
shoots deep to my soul.
in her arms,
im brought from the cold.

I fall into her,
I forget all my sorrow.
but it will appear again,
sometime tommorow.

alone in my head,
the void of smile.
Waitng for the touch,
i have to wait a while.

I die, i feel....nothing,
I lay before her, my eyes open so i can see.
she begins to shed tears,
one reaches my soul, her sadness revived me.
