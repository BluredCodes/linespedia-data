The shit that I write comes from my mind
So anything vulgar you read you know its mine
I say what I want cus I dont give a shit
Thats why i sit back with some lit crack and write it
You may think my mind is fucked up and that I might be crazy
well you can write the same way I do but your fuckin lazy
let it out and let it flow, on to the paper
scream and shout and let it show, while i kill my self with a stapler
hang my self with my shoe lace while im holding your hand
bang my head on a shelf, im'a fucked up 17 year old man
On drugs that youve never even heard of and prolly never will
Cus there in my mind i know one day that they will kill
kill me in my sleep while I dream of fuckin up your life
pills in me creep while I scheme writing about fucking your wife
so give me bad reviews and give me bad advice
Remember the ryhme bout kat cobain, it wasnt nice
I can do the same to you and still wont give a fuck
excuse me while I tie my neck to the back of a moving truck
