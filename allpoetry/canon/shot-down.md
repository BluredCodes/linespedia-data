---
title: "Shot down"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "shot-down"
source: "AllPoetry"

I came to confront you, but you shot me down.
I took two to the chest and then fell to the ground.
I came to shoot words, but you was packin heat.
I thought I was runnin this show, but I guess I was beat.
Like a coward in a fight, you shot me in the back.
You thought the only way to work this out was with a gatt.
I didnt even know you, but we had some kinda beef.
Somethin about me dissin ya moms, so you shot me in the street.
With the twitch of a finger my life hung on a strand.
I thought I'd die at an old age, not by your hand.
I guess I should have known that my life would end like this.
I wish I would have known, so that I could have given my goodbye kiss.
