---
title: "I love you mom"
writer: "Canon"

Hi mom, this is your son
We've been through a lot.
I wanted to show you how much I love you,
So I thought I'd give it a shot.
You, a single mother 
raising 3 bad kids on your own.
I loved you more than anything,
I know it wasnt always shown.
You never once gave up on me,
I know how much you care.
I never had to worry,
I knew you'd always be there.
You cared for me 
when no one else did.
I havent been very caring,
and I know im not the best kid.
I'll care for you all my life,
I'll keep you in my heart.
Ive loved you from the begining,
you've loved me from the start.
I'll do everything I can
to show you my love.
Every morning and night,
Ill give you a kiss and a hug.
But I want you to know,
I love you for everything you've done
I'll try to be a better kid,
I love you mom, This is your son.
