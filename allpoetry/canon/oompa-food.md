My favorite food, hmmm let me think, 
Id have to say that its oompa meat. 
The sweet tender oompa, 
the legs the arms the feet. 
Green like peas, food to please. 
Im enveloped by an "all consuming passion" 
to eat oompa legs then pass it on, 
eat the neck, oh so sweet, 
the all consuming passion of oompa meat. 
The hair serves as a side, chomping down on oompa legs with pride. 
Hunt them with deadly smurfs, killing them on their own turf. 
oompa crispy recipe, teasin me, add one cup sugar, 3 cups blood. 
then go get ya self an oompa loompa stud, throw some chicken in the mix, 
stir it with frog legs serving as sticks.you know the best part is oompa dicks, but we save them for the chicks.
