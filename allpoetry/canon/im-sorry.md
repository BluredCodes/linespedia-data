I feel so bad that I had to do what I had to do
I just want you to know I never meant to hurt you
I couldnt keep it to my self cus it would be a lie
Im so so sorry that i made you cry
Im gonna be your friend, ill help you through 
Be your friend, never again will i hurt you
I know my apologies mean nothing now
I want to help just tell me how
I'll be here when you need me, just give me a call
Im extremely sorry I made you fall
Its not your fault
So wipe away the tears and tell me your mind
Im not gonna hurt you this time
Im sorry
