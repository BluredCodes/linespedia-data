My views on god are different then any
I dont understand how he got so many
i dont belive the bible or the word
I grew up to worship him..thats what i heard
well im my own person and make up my mind
I have beliefs like no other kind
I find it hard to believe there is a spirit
if he is so great and kind then why do we fear it
well if there is a god then there is more than one
I cant believe that its just god spirit and son
so what if you dont like my belief
I dont want to hear your god damn grief
oh no i took his name in vein
well im gonna cus i suffer so much pain
more than one or none at all
hey if you talk to him tell him gimme a call
we can chat it up like its our job
maybe ill start to believe and feel hearts throb
i doubt it but its worth a try
If he does exist tell him to let me fly
give me happiness in life and maybe i can see
