---
title: "Fell in love"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "fell-in-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

When I love I love and fall so deeply
Weight of love on my eyes oh so sleepy
Tired of pain but hungry for love
No more looking but wanting the one
Searching is over but not for sure
confused on belief, i believe i love her
No one else can be with me, I am alone
I am a field of rocks, she is my stone
Stone cold love, I have fell deeper and deeper
I only want to be around and to be with her
Have never been this happy and sad at the same time
Opposites and contradiction are confusing my mind
