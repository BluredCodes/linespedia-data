Forget you and your shady ways
I thought to much in past days
I look up now, I see sun rays
I wont dwell on things that have been
Now i write my feelings with this pen
I dont think much anymore, Im not worried
I'll still remember when my eyes were blurried
Im gonna take my time, I wont be hurried
Im on a search, Im on a Quest
to find the one, Better then the rest
But for now im gonna enjoy my life
Im not ready for a comittment, not ready for a wife
