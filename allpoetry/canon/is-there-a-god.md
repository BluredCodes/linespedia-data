How do we know god is real
"he is" I hate to hear that spiel
We belive because of a book
After reading our mind has shook
well i dont buy it, not at all
Why would he watch us fall
he see's us in pain but does nothing
he created us he should do something
I do admit that i belive in him
I need to when times are grim
But i believe everyones god exists
They all sit together in heavens mists
We cant believe in a book that could be fake
We cant hold to him and be afraid our souls he'd take
if there are gods there is more than out there
So many religions so many gods...we can share
It is hard for me to believe in a superior being
If there is than theres more than 1, thats what my mind's seeing
But what have they done for us as of late
School shootings, violence, death and hate
do they get off in seeing us suffer
Like to watch our lives get rougher
Well I apologize to all those who belive
I need to see him...my mind to relieve
At heart im polytheistic
