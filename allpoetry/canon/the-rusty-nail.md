A rusty nail penetrating my brain
Causing unbelievable and unstoppable pain
Falling to the ground and drowning in the rain
Im already used to it so theres no need to complain
Alone and empty i am feeling similar to citizen kane
rose bud are my words but there meaning is plain
Im going the wrong way, ive enterd the opposite lane
Falling forever with the speed of a speeding train
This rusty nail has killed me, I have been slain
Drowning in my puddle of blood, yet my life sustains
What is the purpose of life if there are no gains
