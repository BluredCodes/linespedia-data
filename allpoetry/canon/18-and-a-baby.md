Your havin a baby at eighteen
your happiness never to be seen
what were you thinkin, not using a condom
now you got a pretty big problem
you've got nine months for you to decide
Your gonna blame it on accident that the baby died
your parents have shunned you away
this is how they make you pay
your lover boy left you all alone
before the baby it was love he shown

18 and havin a kid
its something you want to rid
you hate yourself for bein so dumb
your kids gonna grow to be a bum
family left along with the father
your left alone to be a mother

You cant bring yourself to kill this child
you think you can keep it,life wont be so wild
your party days are over, no more drinkin or weed
no more shooting up no more doin the speed
you cant handle life with out your angel dust
thats just something you need....you must
you cant afford to keep this kid and drugs
your gonna be alone, no more hugs
tears start to flow, but no one there to hold
you always said that you would never get old
now you got a kid and life is boring
you wouldnt be here if you wasnt whoring

18 and havin a kid
its something you want to rid
you hate yourself for bein so dumb
your kids gonna grow to be a bum
family left along with the father
your left alone to be a mother

You sleep around and now you got a h.i.v
i know your never gonna be happy
your kids gonna die cus of what you did
you think of yourself, what about your kid
you shared needles and slept with strangers
you never thought of the dangers
you thought you were invincible, no one can touch
but now what can you do......nothing much
you have waisted all your chances on stupid things
what you've done, look at the pain it brings
your gonna die and so is the child
all because you needed to be wild
sex drugs and alcohol ruined your days
your paying for it on so many ways

18 and havin a kid
its something you want to rid
you hate yourself for bein so dumb
your kids gonna grow to be a bum
family left along with the father
your left alone to be a mother
