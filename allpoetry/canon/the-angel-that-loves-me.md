title: "The angel that loves me"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "the-angel-that-loves-me"

For so many years, 
I was wasting away.
Lost with no love, 
pretending all was okay.

Broken, beaten,
my heart of glass shattered.
ripped through my chest,
thrown to the floor, stomped and battered.

I laid helpless, hopeless,
leaving my blood stained tears.
I waited all my life,
All 17 years.

I waited for a miracle,
Like a flash of lightning it came.
changing my whole world for the better,
never to be the same.

An angel was sent to me,
someone that also needed care.
So I vowed to love this girl,
and to always be there.

So now when I see her,
Stare into her eyes.
I can see the mended heart,
and the temple where it lie's.

I caress it gently,
whisper it, my devotion.
Thank god it heard me,
Listning hard through all the commotion.

She has done the same,
caressed the broken heart in me.
I fell in love over and over with her,
Thats how it will always be.

Entangled in the rose's
that feed and nourish my heart.
Her eyes feed the flowers,
I feed hers, We will never be apart.
