title: "An Angel in need"

Can I borrow your lungs, I need to breath the air.
Can you lend me your thoughts, So i can at least seem to care.
Can you let me use your eyes, I need to see the ground.
I need to borrow your ears, I cant seem to hear a sound.
Give me your sense of touch, I cant feel the pain.
Can I have your umbrella, to shield me from the rain.
Will you let me borrow your heart, I need to be in love.
Can you let me have your back, so others can wrap there arms around to hug.
Give me your brain, I need to understand why im empty like a hole.
Let me tell you why im like this, I have died and am nothing but a soul.
An angel that needs your body, in order to come back to earth.
I need everything you have, I need your mom to give me birth.
Here is some advice, never sell your soul to satan, He only pretends to care
He pretends there is a heaven, he even promises to bring you there.
