title: "Sadness devours me"
writer: "Canon"

Sadness devours my mind when I think of the loss
Memories of us fill my mind like steady spreading moss
My stomach churns at the pain that I feel
Its hard to realize that the pain is real
how can something so good turn so bad
I keep running through good parts we had
My life seems to never fully achieve happiness
Feeling this way just delays the realization of crappiness
When something is goin good something comes along to ruin it all
I feel isolated and realize that there is no one to call
no one that can help me becuase no one knows how to care
Just when I think someone does I realize no ones really there
