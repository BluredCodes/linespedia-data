writer: "Canon"

Everyone is no one and we are all something
no one can but i do, do what's done when done is nothing.
for all we are one and one we are all.
take one from us and the rest of us fall.
we can't see what others see but see what they dont.
tell you ill do something, you tell me you wont.
stop and you go i go and you stop
i go to the bottom and you go to the top.
you gallop in the corner, im stuck in a field.
when im just getting hurt, your just getting healed.
pull yourself together, all the while I fall apart.
Im just finishing this, just when your about to start.
You can fly, I can barely crawl
I begin to walk, you start to fall.
speak but no words, your words but no speak.
you can own the sky, Ill stay and own the street.
nonsense, crazy, insane, and of course the all powerfull psycho
i will play with the real cars and trucks , you can play with mattel and tyco.
peace
