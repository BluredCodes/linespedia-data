Through my mind we'll take a tour
We'll go very deep, to the core
You'll see what makes me tick
You'll see what makes me sick
You'll feel the pain and the glory
If you dont like it, im sorry
Bare with me it wont take long
Just sit back and listen to the song

My mind is an endless maze
You wanna see
The things i've done in days
You wanna see
My life has withered in a craze
You wanna see
Why I live my life in a haze
You wanna see
How all this is cus of my ways
You wanna see 
How this shit never pays

Well if your up to it follow me
We'll take the tour, soon you'll see
Bring a camera and take a pic 
You dont like what you see, suck my dick
This is me whether you like it or not
You'll see that my god damn life isnt so hot
There are good parts and there always will be
So if your up to it then follow me

My mind is an endless maze
You wanna see
The things i've done in days
You wanna see
My life has withered in a craze
You wanna see
Why I live my life in a haze
You wanna see
How all this is cus of my ways
You wanna see 
How this shit never pays
