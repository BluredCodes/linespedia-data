Always alone, always sad
always angry, always mad
I need a woman like oceans need water
when will i find..when will i spot her
Days suck so bad with no one to hold
its hard to find the one..i was never told
she's got to be out there
I wanna run fingers through her hair
i want to hold her when she's sad
i want to help her when she's mad
im full of love and no one to spend
hopefully the one is round the bend
its hard not getting loved
always yelled at..always shoved
where is the girl for me
where is she
i need to find her soon
to hold beneath the nights moon
under the stars we kiss
when away we always miss
the girl that will be my wife
