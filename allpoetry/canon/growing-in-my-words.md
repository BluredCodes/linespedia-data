---
title: "Growing in my words"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "growing-in-my-words"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/3296-Growing-in-my-words-by-Canon"

I've grown extremeley, since my first, "what u do 2 me"
To now with "poets death bed", maybe you can see
I feel differently about my life since I started to write
But not so much that im no longer kind or polite
But when I write my words I throw all that out the door
I dig into my soul for what I write, down to the core
Pull words out through my hand, then to the pen, down to the paper
I like to think of my self as the one and only mad rapper, dirty caper
If anyone dis likes the way I write, thats fine cus I dont like you neither
I forget about you, pick up my pen like its my mind bleeder
