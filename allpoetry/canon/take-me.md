My life has been hard the past few days
Im not sure whats playing cus its a haze
im lost in my heart
were falling apart
I found happiness in you when we first met
now all i see is sadness baby girl dont be sad
my lifes problems arent even close to yours 
when you feel down the sky blackens and it pours
i cant stand to see you that way
cheer up sweety pie im here to stay
when were through this i want to hold you near
i want to hold you and wipe away that tear
U told me it will take a while for u to see
I like you a lot.....do you like me?
