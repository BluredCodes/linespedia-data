Your not ready for me and im willing to waitIm falling for you, believe ill never hateI'll do what it takes to hold you in my armsI'll give you all my sweetness and my charmsyou deserve the best due to you being youyou having something good is way past dueYou thought any minute you would run out of tearswhen your ready i'll comfort you and take away the fearsi keep seeing me holding your hand and kissing your lipsI see me playing with your hair and my arms on your hipsIf things dont work out ill try to be your friendbut i want you to know i dont want us to end'Im hanging by a moment here with you'each and everyday im falling more in love with youKnow that i care about you with all my heartIm glad i met you, but now i dont want to be apartBut do what you need to, i'll be hereI can wait short term or even over yearssweet kisses and goodnight sweet girlwhen your ready i'll give you the world
