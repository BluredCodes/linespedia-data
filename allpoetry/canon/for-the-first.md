I look into your eyes
I tell you I love you
We are close
kissing
Breathing
together
eternity is our love
We are one
we sleep in eachothers eyes
our embrace is our love
we become, birds, doves
Our lips meet
We are, together
Life without you,
ceases to exist
I pray to my mind,
that we will always be
at this moment
love is all that matters
we are no longer, seperate

I love you....
