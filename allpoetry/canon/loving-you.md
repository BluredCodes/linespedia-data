---
title: "Loving you"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "loving-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Your eyes that I stare into put me in a trance
Every wish for me to be with you somone always grants
your hand that I hold and your arm that i rub
Everytime I think of you the more i fall in love
This love is endless, My love will always grow
Your falling in love with me, and its starting to show
I can see the happiness in your eyes and the warmth in your touch
I will love you when you cry, I love you so much
I love you when your mad, I love you when your asleep
I will love you when your sad, My love for you is so deep
I dream of the day when you speak those 3 words slowly in my ear
I will hug you, kiss you, dig deep inside you and take away any fear
My love for you will never end, My love will never shrink in its size
You are my love, you are my friend, Loving you is the most wonderful ride
