---
title: "We miss those who passed"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "we-miss-those-who-passed"
source: "AllPoetry"

We all have lost someone that we truly love
All we can do is think how it always was
We miss them, but will reunite in the after death
They will be the one's we think of during our last breath
We pray for you and hope your in a better place
We can't wait till the day we can see your face
so wait for us in the sky of after
wait for me, and remember our laughter
the things we did, the times we had
Remember our memories, the good and bad
together soon enough, were just finishing up life
