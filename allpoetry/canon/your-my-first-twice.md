title: "Your my first, twice"
writer: "Canon"

Loving you in another way,
you being my first, twice.
Your smile and a giggle,
Winking and squinting those eyes.
Trying to be sneaky,
we kissed and cuddled close.
Moving down your body,
and removing you clothes.
Stopping to tell you,
that you are the one and only.
To tell you that I will always be here
baby you'll never be lonely.
I would have loved to make it better,
Im sorry if it was bad.
Many times since the first,
your the best girlfriend i've ever had.
The heat was passionate
the breathing was heavy.
We didnt rush it, we were smart.
I waited till you were ready.
Baby im committed,
I know you are to.
I fell in and made love with an angel.
Baby I truly love you.
You were my first, twice.
And baby im glad that you were.
I dreamed of an angel with goddess like love
And baby you are her.
Im your first and baby your mine.
Im in love with an angel.
Thank you for loving me baby,
Your love is a gift and im so grateful.
