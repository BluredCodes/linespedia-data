title: "Black eyes"
writer: "Canon"

His hand raises and you start to shake
he uses anything at all, today its the rake
you constantly have a black eye
friends ask what happened, you lie
you try to hide your pain behind glasses
you wish for the day he passes
always an excuse for the injury you got
you dream of the day he gets shot
your mom knows not what goes on
you sit crying in the corner till dawn
been this way since you were born
when he dies you know you wont mourn
you'll never miss him, you hate his presence
He says he's teaching you lessons

Its time to speak up little one
He wont stop..he's never done
tell someone..they can help you
others know what to do 
stand up to him and tell him thats enough
your done taking his abusive stuff

If this goes on any longer your gonna crack
you'll lose your sanity...and never get it back
he thinks he's god...punishing you the way he does
he says this is the way it was
back in his day this was the way
you do something bad...you pay
but you did nothing at all

he had an accidental fall
he's dead and questions no one will ask
in his death you bask
you did it but its over now
the only way out that was how
he went to far and your mind snapped
you wanted to kill everytime you got slapped
wipe your tears baby...he's gone from this place
