---
title: "Satan has corrupted me"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "satan-has-corrupted-me"

Satan has corrupted my soul, corrupted my heart.every little problem that arises, is ripping me apart.Every single tear that i shed, is acid on my skin.Every little thing i do wrong, is turned into sin.Taking away my vision, satan is drowning my voice.I am being corrupted by satan, and i wasnt given a choice.I was brought into this world, branded, branded to be corrupted.Everything that seems to be going right,is in one way or another, interuptedI don't want your pity, dont cry for me, dont shed a tear.I have been so corrupted that all the nice things said, i can no longer hearI see all the evil in life, all the problems and the pain.so i sit by my self, crying, hiding my tears in the rain.there is only one angel that comforts me, we are in love.That is the one thanks i have, thanks to all of you up above.
