---
title: "Zeke's Back!!"
writer: "Canon"

This is the second story of zeke...he's not dead
I told you that first story just to put zeke in ya head
He layed there motionless for about five minutes
Then came the ambulance and stretcher and they put him on it
took him away to st. Mary's to remove the pin and revive zeke
After about an hour of surgery out came the pin and blood started to leak
His eyes opened wide and he screamed kill the nomes
the doc's and nurses went and fled to their homes
zeke got up bleeding and all...stumbled to his feet
killed three nurses on his way to the street
looking like a zombie he entered a nice 'n easy
screamin absenities and saying the nomes need me
the clerk grabbed a 12 guage and off with zekes head
Dont be so sure this is the end of the story...he's not dead
