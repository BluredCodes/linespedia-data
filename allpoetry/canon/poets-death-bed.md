---
title: "Poets death bed"
writer: "Canon"

Im hallucinatin, but im not on drugs
Im crusin at 80, but im runnin in my lugs
Im flyin over clouds, but im stuck to the ground
Im hearin explosive shrouds, but theres no other sound
I feel a million needles penetrating my skin, but im in a padded room
I feel his way because of my sin, and im lying in my tomb
The death bed of a poet, but you cant consider me one
Im dying and i know it, just kill me and send me to the sun
The dreams of my life are now nightmares, nightmares are my dreams
Im dying and no one even cares, nothing in my life is as it seems
Awake and im dreamin, asleep and im dead
the devil and I are schemin, welcome to my bed
bed of death and im wide awake
dead is my breath,I died in a lake
Drowned in a lake is how i died
family frowned my life at stake they cried
