---
title: "You Changed Me"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "you-changed-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

The love I feel envelops my mind
Im holding you in the still of time
hold you to me in my thoughts and dreams
with out you life is just life it seems
but with you life turns to love 
I never want to go back to the way it was
I will tell you one day that I love you 
That i want to be with you, kiss and hug you
love creates dreams of blue sky wonders
loving you seems to up my unders
depression to happiness, its all you
When I feel down i just think of what you do
my heart used to be a dead uncared for garden
Now flowers grow wildly trees are alive and branches harden
I love you and loving you has changed everything
I will always love you so there should be no worrying
