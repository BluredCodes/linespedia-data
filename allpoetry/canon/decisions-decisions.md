title: "Decisions decisions"
writer: "Canon"

Torn between two 
dont know who to choose
hardest decision ive ever made
hard cus i dont wanna get played
dont know who to trust dont know who's playin
cant believe what everyones sayin
need to make this decision,and need to make it quick
dont wanna hurt no one but i dont know what to think
have info against both that say it wont work
thinkin about this is makin me berzerk
one might be playin head games the other might not be true
so give me advice,  and tell me what to do
