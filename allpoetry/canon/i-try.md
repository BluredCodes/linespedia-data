Theres things i do to try to save a friendship
cus my friends are things i dont mess with
Im in for fight
things aint goin right
one of my best friends

I try to reason with him
When times are grim
theres only a few friends i would save
he's one of'em, this i swear on my grave

what did i do?
Ill leave that to you
but kick my ass if its gonna fix this
lets end the put downs and the disses
