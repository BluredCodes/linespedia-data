In a building down the street,
to the left of the grocery store.
is a building with broken windows,
and this really weird looking door.

Inside this door is a hallway,
which twists and turns left and right.
Take two lefts and go straight,
it's easy, just follow the light.

In the room with the light, 
is an abandoned room.
old desks, strewn papers,
janitor bucket, mops and brooms.

The walls are bare,
the floor is a mess.
one lonely paper lays,
on one purposley cleaned desk.

Above the desk is a bar,
which extends from wall to wall.
on the bar is a rope,
on the rope is a loop thats kinda small.

Inside the loop is a head,
This place is where I chose to die.
The note on the desk,
is note i left, to say goodbye.
