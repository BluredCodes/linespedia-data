---
title: "I Cried"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "i-cried"

I lay in my bed,
looking out into the sky.
Watching the moon drip,
as I begin to cry.

The stars stream,
pouring down the night.
Distant thoughts in my mind,
My tears shining through the moons light.

streaks of pain,
evident on my cheek.
Hands clenched in pain,
bleeding cus im weak.

My thoughts are strewn,
lost in the nights sky.
Streaking like the stars,
I follow them as I cry.

In time,
the pain will subside,
Forgive me,
Im sorry that I cried.
