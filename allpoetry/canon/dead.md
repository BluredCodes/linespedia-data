title: "Dead"
writer: "Canon"

My eyes closed..Body parts exposed
I think im dreaming, yet im not sleeping
Seems like angels be peepin
what has happened...here comes a flash
my life goes by...then smash
I cant see very well
Is this supposed to be hell
Im starting to float
Im entering a big white boat
All these people have wings
The light is bright and it stings
I cant seem to close my eyes
Is this what happens when someone dies
Im not dead so it cant be
Maybe i am and my soul is free
I feel somekind of warm on my face
Theres redness behind me...it begins to chase
Something grabs my arm
I turn, this one means harm
He has no wings
What pain his grip brings
my head starts to burn
My stomach starts to churn
I start to move in reverse
I see a casket and a hearse
What is this kinda dream
I cant see the disapearing gleam
I see a door close
Is this what i chose
I thought i was headed up
I guess i have no such luck
Why does my life suck
I guess it was because i didnt behave
My eyes open, in a different perspective i see a grave
Oh my god i think its mine
Im dead i...ran out of time
Take my advice and live your life good
All the players get up out the hood
All the sluts stop sleeping around
Or you'll end up 6 foot under ground
All you cheaters stop the cheating
When you get here your gonna get a beating
All those who have done something wrong better make it right
Im dead now turn off the light
Im gone for now good night
Wait for my resurection and reincarnation
I'll be back...just be patient
