---
title: "It starts with tears"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "it-starts-with-tears"

As the lonely tear rolls down my face
another forms and  joins the race
I dont bother to wipe them away
this is the way my mind makes me pay
my lip starts to shake my mind starts to numb
when im down like this i feel like a bum
i cant breath or even move for that matter
the tears start to flow,my teeth start to chatter
my legs buckle my arms hang at my side
i fall to the floor..have i died?
i cant be dead,i still feel the pain
let me go why does my life sustain
my mind is dying so am I
it started cus u made me cry
My heart has been gone 
i give up..i cant live on.....
