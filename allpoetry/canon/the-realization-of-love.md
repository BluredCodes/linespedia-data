---
title: "the realization of love"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "the-realization-of-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/3406-the-realization-of-love-by-Canon"
---

Walking blindly into eternal love
Life's test to see what your made of
Eyes wide open, but can't see a thing
Nothing can compare to the happiness it brings
love is good but hurts more often
Love kills anger, heart begins to soften
growing eternally due to the love we feel
But it takes time to realize the love is real
could be an illusion, could be the one
Breaking of this is a shot by a gun
painful yet recovering all at the same time
lifes contradiction, the biggest one of mine
