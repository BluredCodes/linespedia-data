---
title: "How do I show you"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "how-do-i-show-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/2094-How-do-I-show-you-by-Canon"
---

I can't imagine a place with no you
If you werent here what would I do
I care so much but dont know how to show
I wish I could open my heart and let it flow
you make me so happy words cant express
I want to give you everything, you deserve the best
I look forward to when I can hold your hand
I want to make you feel as happy as I possibly can
Im falling in love with the most beautiful girl
I've finally found a person my heart considers a pearl
