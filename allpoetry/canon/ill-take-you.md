Take my hand ill walk you to the gate 
The party starts at 12 so we cant be late
Im mad vulnerable when im in this state
Im tired of not having and having to wait
In the party we an talk about us
Whatever makes you feel the lust
Your in my dreams and we roll together
Me in my Fubu and you in your leather
We can be close or we can be friends
I just want to be with you when my life ends
I want you to be mine for me to hold
I want to be with you and grow gray and old
Even after death i'll still hold your hand
I'll bring you to the gate, Just let me be your man
