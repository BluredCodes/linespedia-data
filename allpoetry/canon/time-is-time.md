Things can seem like forever but only be 5 minutes
Like being thrown in a crocs tank and having to survive in it
Or holding you breath for like 5 minutes without any air,
waiting for a ride from the bronx when you got a flat and aint got a spare.
waiting outside the ER, waiting to hear if your frined just died.
Waiting in the lobby wondering if your premature baby survived.
5 Minutes can be a long time, but in the long run its just time.
The length it takes to pass is just a fabrication in your mind.
