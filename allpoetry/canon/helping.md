Things go on in your life and I know it's rough
But you know im here to help, you just gotta be tough
A friend is what I'll be, A friend is what you need
Im here to try to clear all this up and to help you breath

These things only happen to good people, and you are good
you dont believe you are, but i wish you would
im here to make you beleive what i say is true
No matter what  happens ill be here to help you
