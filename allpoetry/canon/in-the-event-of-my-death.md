title: "In the event of my death"
writer: "Canon"

In the event of my death, Give a kiss to my mother.
The woman that brought me up, cared like no other.
She's been through hard times, So make it a little easier.
Tell her I love her, Tell my mother things that will ease her.

When you see my dad, Tell him I love him and give him a hug.
He always knew when I was sad, no matter what he always showed me love.
I still love my dad, even though we dont talk much
But i would never be this way if it werent for his touch

Tell my sister breazy, that I miss and love her
Without her life wasnt easy, I want to kiss and hug her
left when I was small, But I love her all the same
We also didnt talk much, Though it is my self that I blame

Tell brandy I love her, Tell her to be strong
I always forgot to hug her, We can always right the wrong
give her a kiss on the cheek, And tell her things are ok
Tell her that Ill miss her, I never wanted to go away

Tell sahara, my niece, when she is old enough to understand,
that I, josh, was a wonderful uncle, and a great man
Give her a beautiful life, make her happy, make her smile
I am going now, but i will be thinking of all of you all the while

Now to my love, the girl I fell in love with
She took hold of my heart, made my happiness lift
Her eyes held me up, I will love you for ever
I never wanted to leave, wished we could always be together

Goodbye.......I love youI dont know why i have just been in a goodbye type of mood
