---
title: "Cherish the lost"
writer: "Canon"

This tragedy brings tears to my eyes
Reading everyones poems..sadness cries
We cherish anyone and everyone that dies
But on that fateful day worst then the rest
Our security has been deminishing...we have none left
Terrorist actions have crippled our minds
these people have committed unspeakable crimes
Murder murder murder...how could you?
how do we cope...how do we get through
Dedicated to those lost on the 11th of september o one
We will remember you forever...we send our love
