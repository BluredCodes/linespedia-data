---
title: "Marry me"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "marry-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/34545-Marry-me-by-Canon"
---

Mount this white horse
and lets ride off to the beach.
Hold me around my chest
and we'll take whats out of our reach.

Take this ring that I hold
and hold it in your hand.
let me place it on your finger,
and forever be yor man.

When this day arrives,
I hope that you'll say yes.
The heart I once hated,
I now believe is blessed.

Live your life with me,
and we can live a lovely life.
Hold my love with yours,
and forever be my wife.

I love you baby, forever and always.
