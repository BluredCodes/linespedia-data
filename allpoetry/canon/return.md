title: "Return"

Im here to tell ya that im back.
that im back to rap, and write like that
I was gone for some time, but im back to rhyme
to write and shine, to criticise, line by line.
Im back in words, and playa im back to stay.
aint no force that could interupt my course, and keep me away.
My game was delayed, gone for some time, but I still played, and I still ryhmed. the son of a lyricist, the prodigy child with a lyrical mind.
the chains couldnt hold me, I broke free and returned, the game tried to stop me, I taught him and he learned. I might of left the game, but the game aint leave me, im back to stay, playa believe me....
