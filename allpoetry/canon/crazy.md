Im bored im going crazy
my day has been hell its so hazy
i find my self writing this rhyme
when i shop i get stares do i look like a crime
im making no sense in what i say 
im lost im happy im sad im all on this day
what do you wanna do im bored lets talk
i dont believe you everything u say is a crock
shhh im sleeping dont bother me
whats wrong u spect me to be happy
u might notice my mind is gone
well i just started im not done
give me your hand we'll go for a swim
that guys a stranger dont talk to him
I hate that movie i dont wanna go
now here is the story ima let it flow
I was born on a base in new york city
dont tell me anything i dont need your pity
i used to be stupid and dressed like a dork
i seriously thought i was brought by a stork
I brought my self up with no help from mom and dad
they fed me and sheltered me but i was always sad
my siblings are rude and i hate them so much
my sisters a slut i think my brothers been touched....in the head
he ruined my life he made me mad ive gone crazy
