---
title: "From then to now."
writer: "Canon"
slug: "from-then-to-now"
source: "AllPoetry"

I wish I had a voice of an angel
so that I could sing my love to you
all I have to give is my heart
I hope that that will do.

I remember when we first met,
I never would have thought that we would be together.
But now i commit myself to you
and I will love you forever.

The first time we kissed
Outside in my car
I never ever thought 
that it would come this far

The first time I told you
that I was fallin in love
you thought it was kinda early
but i knew you were the one.

Sometimes it gets rough,
because you think im gonna leave.
I promised you I wouldnt, 
but sometimes you dont believe.

I wipe your tears often,
But im glad to do it.
You've wiped mine away
and then we got through it.

I waited my entire life
for a relationship like this.
and there isnt a day in it
that I would want to miss.

I would love to be with you
all day every day for every year.
but when I cant be, dont be sad,
because you know im still here.

Read this when your down
to bring you back up.
know that Im with you forver
and that we'll always be in love.
