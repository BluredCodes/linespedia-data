On the 12th day of christmas
I bought my self a rope,
On the 11th day of christmas,
I gave up all my hope.

On the 10th day of christmas,
I tied the rope to a tree.
I watched that rope,
It seemed to be taunting me.

On the 9th day of christmas,
the rain began to drop.
On the 8th day of christmas,
I didnt think it would stop.

On the 7th day of christmas,
The sun began to shine,
On the 6th day of christmas,
I dried that rope of mine.

On the 5th day of christmas,
I made a noose at the end.
On the 4th day of christmas,
I wrote the letter that I would send.

On the 3rd day of christmas,
I sent that letter of suicide.
On the 2nd day of christmas,
I looked at the rope and sighed.

On christmas day,
I slipped into that rope.
Before I fell to death,
I wondered why I lost hope.
