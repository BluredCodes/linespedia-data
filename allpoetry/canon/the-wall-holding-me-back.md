---
title: "The wall holding me back"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "the-wall-holding-me-back"
source: "AllPoetry"

I held my feelings behind a wall like structure
But due to complications i believe it has ruptured
Im easy to hurt, vulnerable more than before
I dont know why but i cant hold it back anymore
little things bother me and I dont know why
Now the wall is gone, its easy to make me cry
But since the wall has left im also more awake
im not gonna put my happiness at stake
so i laugh, and i open up more
Being happy used to be a chore
but now i broke the wall and everything is out
Im no longer fighting my self in this championship bout
