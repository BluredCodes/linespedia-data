---
title: "My goodbyes (in the event of my death)"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "my-goodbyes-in-the-event-of-my-death"

To say my goodbyes, to wipe away the tears.
I want no one to cry, I served my lifes years.
Death is not a time of mourning, It is a time of redemption.
no longer living with pain, from which no one is an exception.
There is nothing wrong with passing away, I say, it was my time.
I have not left, my life still exists, my life will always be mine.
Wipe away your tears, smile once for me, remember me always.
I loved my many years, Maybe soon you'll see, as I walk deaths hallways.
Images of memories of my time with all of you.
I have to say my goodbyes, there is nothing else for me to do.
hold me in your hearts, as a friend, not a foe.
Always remember the happy parts, they will never end, I didnt ask to go.
Assume im in a better place now, Assume my life lives on.
I loved one and only one, My love for you sweetheart will never be gone.
Eternity, remember?...I promised it would never fade.
I say my goodbyes to those that were dear to me, I wish I could have stayed.
Hold my words close to your heart, Never let my memories leave.
As long as hearts beat for me, My life will always breath.
