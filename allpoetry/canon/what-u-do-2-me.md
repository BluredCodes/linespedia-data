title: "What u do 2 me"
writer: "Canon"

What you do to me kills me inside
It isnt you its this emotional rollercoaster ride
Im telling you its hard to live this way
Im hoping you'll realize and say you'll stay
I told you what i think and what i feel
I guess my mind and heart to you dont appeal
I tried my best to show you love you've never had
you ignored me and pushed me away and it makes me sad
I swore to you i would never hurt you 
When i told u this it came from the heart and its true
You told me u cared and ur feelings were pure
Im scared now and what u tell me im just not sure
Just tell me if its over and i will leave
you dont understand what this does you'll never believe.
