I wish it was accepted for men to cry
But I always have to take a breath and sigh
Sometimes I need to shed tears from happiness and pain
I must never cry or only cry in the pouring rain
Imagine a dark alley at night during a thunder storm
Thats my mind in one little area..my feelings in a swarm
The only happiness that enters this dark dismall alley way
Is the lonley kitty thats hungry and has lost its way
Feeding off the kindness of a little box for shelter
But no one will feed the kat, no one will help her
So now i cry in the rain and hide it from scrutiny
This is pain of lightning, i feel that its shooting me
when will the rain go away and the sun shine again
Will it storm in my mind from beggining to end
