---
title: "See what I see"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "see-what-i-see"

Look through my eye's and see what I see
Step into my body and be what I be
Put your Heart in mine and feel what I feel
Step into my life, and see that it's real

I'll do the same for you, if you let me in
Let me clens your soul, take away the sin
Trust me with your love, It will be okay
My silence is me thinking of what to say
But believe me when I say I love you
I do all this for you, one love, my boo

Into eachother, we can be one in the same
Believe me boo, this is no game
Open your loving heart and your mind
Do this for me and i'll show you mine
