Let me tell you the story of me
Let me tell you my life and... you'll see
you'll see why I write ....the way I do
You'll see why I can care less... about you
I was born to a divorced couple and shared
I grew up always thinking everyone cared
My dad loves me more than life it self
but its hard for me to care back cus I have none left
My mom is more the open kinda woman that lets me have my way
I love my mom and dad, about that theres nothing left to say
I hated my moms new husband and my dads new wife
I was around them for a while and they lied to me my entire life
They say they care but I can tell that they dont
They want me to visit them but I know that I wont
I've lost trust for everyone but mom and dad
Everyone else is just there and they make me mad
I cant say that my parents are the only one
Cus I know I love this girl and she is the first one
I fell in love with the perfect girl but Some things are complicated
Sometimes I feel loved by everyone and sometimes i feel hated
I dont trust anyone I know, not even my best friend
I prolly will never trust anyone else again
So maybe now you can see the reason that I write words so anger filled
So I guess you could say it was my mind that...trust....the stranger killed
