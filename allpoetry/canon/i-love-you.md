---
title: "I love You"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "i-love-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/3840-I-love-You-by-Canon"
---

Three words from me to you that mean so much
Three words towards you that are followed by a gentle touch
I kiss each finger on your beautiful hand
I will always love you more than anyone can
You are my dream, My dreams are you
Anything you need, everything i'll do
Holding you in my arms and, you resting on me
When it comes to being there for you, ill be all i can be
Everything that makes you happy is on my list to do
Topping each happy moment with another, because i love you
You make me happy like no one can or ever will
Sometimes being with out you, loving kills
