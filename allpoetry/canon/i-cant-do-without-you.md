---
title: "I can't do without you"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "i-cant-do-without-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

Can you see the blackness, behind the eyes of brown.
Can you hear the silence, behind the noise of every sound.
Can you see the pain, behind each and every smile.
can you heal that pain, at least for a little while.
will you please stay with me, i need you each day.
i promise i will never leave you, i love you in every way.
but behind the happiness you saw when we first met.
is all my pain, sorrow, sadness, each and every regret.
you have healed most of it, but the world keeps beating me.
I will never be beaten down, as long as you stay with me.
