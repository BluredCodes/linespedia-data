I want to be your friend but...
Its hard and i dont know what
I find it hard to want to be friends
I think maybe we need to make amends
Im sorry if i seem cold to you
Its the only thing i know to do
I want to be the best of friends thats all
no more than that or we might fall
Your happy..you found the one who's right
you love them with all your might
as long as your happy thats all that matters
but be careful..dont listen to the chatter
People may not like the way you roll
well its your life...and that shits gettin ole
so love away baby 

Back to the subject at hand
its time me and you make a stand
no more hate, lets talk
lets just take a walk
work all this out
what the problems are about
i want to be your best friend if you let me
but thats all..we are no more to be
