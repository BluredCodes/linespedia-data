---
title: "I quit"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "i-quit"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/234-I-quit-by-Canon"
---

Im done with this world im done with this place
Its time i stop or at least slow down the pace
...I dont care what people think of me now
i feel like in this world im on my way down
...Im done with everything even you god
why dont you take me with a fiery lightning rod
..my heart died yesterday but its cursed to stay on earth
im praying to god that theres no such thing as rebirth
...I dont want to come back to this ugly place
thats why i need to stop or slow down my pace
...I hate everything thats in my life
what can i do but show my strife
...the pain is unbearable in my heart
dont worry bout me im only falling apart.
