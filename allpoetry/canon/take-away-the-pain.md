---
title: "Take away the pain"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "take-away-the-pain"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/2499-Take-away-the-pain-by-Canon"
---

I saw the tears rolling down your pretty face
I wanted to hold you but I know you need your space
You tried to hide the tears, you didnt want me to know
I knew you were crying, so just let it flow
If im the reason that your eyes shed tears
I'll go away and end all your fears
I dont want you to be hurt, But I love you all the same
Maybe if I leave, you will no longer suffer pain
I hope it's not my fault because I dont want to leave
I know that if I do that I wont be able to breath
