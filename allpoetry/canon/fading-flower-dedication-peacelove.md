There is kinda like a clique on this site
I read yours you read mine..everynight
We write our feelings on fadingflower
All the time gaining power
thewritegurl, fred nietz,jfish and watt
all our minds are shot
but we read each other and we share
never mean, always fair
with out this site
id live in fright
no place to post ryhmes
in the worst of times
i dedicate this to those who like my writing
i take your input and my pain is subsiding
thanks for this place to write whats in my mind
about the bad times and the ones that shined
Ive been welcomed in to this poetic world.
