---
title: "Her eyes made me fall"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "her-eyes-made-me-fall"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I glance in your direction and im trapped in your eyes
Its hard for me when you blink, my heart seems to die
Like my life was taken away with a blink of your heart
When you close your eyes, I feel I was ripped apart
Then they open again and I feel deeper feelings for you
Then I see the whole picture and realize i love everything you do
Your perfectness amazes me...I love you the way you are
I want to be with you, I seem to wish upon a star
It must work because now we are together
I told you before, But i'll love you forever
I want you to be happy, I want to give you everything and all
I want you to know I'll be here for you, just give me a call
