Interracial relationships, It dont bother me a bit 
why should I care what people do, I dont give a shit 
If i liked a black girl, or any other race for that matter 
I wouldnt be ashamed to say to my boys that I had her. 
We are all human, just with different features of the skin 
Why be a racist, to me that is a huge sin. 
we are all beautiful, why not dip into the other race 
you wouldnt dump a girl just cus she's got a womanly face 
Maybe if you gots no luck in your race, try another 
white boy might like a black girl, white girl might like a brotha 
So my view on an interacial relationship, 
is that anyone that dislikes it is full of shit
