title: "give me a chance"
writer: "Canon"

Why cant you tell me if its true
thinking this makes me feel blue
are u wanting another man
can he promise u everything like i can
r u waiting to see if u two will happen
im sorry if it seems like im snappin
i cant stand to lose another girl
i want u to be with me..ur my pearl
this has happened to me more than once 
does being sweet and caring make me a dunce
you dont know what this does to my mind
it hurts so much...is ur heart blind
is this how u treat people who care
u dont understand that this love is rare
tell me now it wont hurt so much
i need u in my life,i long for ur touch
do u remember when my heart was beating fast
have u forgoten just because its in the past
i feel that way everytime i see your face
do i have competition,is this a race
i never win these games,i always lose the girl
i always lose the pearl,my mind is in a whirl
please let me love you,one day u'll love me
i just need access to ur heart,i promise u'll be happy
this is the only way i know to try to win you over
it will kill me inside if you tell me its over
