---
title: "To be evil"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "to-be-evil"
source: "AllPoetry"

To be less understanding
to be un-caring
to hate
to stop smiling
to never love
to kill

That is to be evil

To love
to care
to be happy
to save
to smile
to be understanding

That is to be evil

whether you end the sorrow of life or sustain it, you are evil...
