Remember zeke well guess what....he's back again
If you remember...zeke lost his head...that wasnt the end
Zeke picked up his head shook off the dirt and looked at the clerk
The body pointed at him as the eyes winked and he smiled an evil smirk
Zeke stood smiling for several minutes pointing at the helpless man
the man seemed to be thinking of something to do like a plan
Zeke finally spoke but was speaking in toungue
"Niet num jik seito slofor" which translated to that stung
A crash from the back of the toilet paper stack...stood a woman with a child
The little boy winked at zeke and zeke started goin wild
zeke threw his head at the clerk behind the counter
Threw it at his face with such strength and such power
Zekes mouth opened mid flight towards the mans scared little head
The teeth from zekes mouth created a spinnig razor....now the clerks dead
the woman started to run as zeke searched for his lopped off top
Found it..saw the woman running and screamed a scream screaming stop
The woman stopped dropped the child as he ran away
"little woman whats wrong, you dont want to stay"
"no" screamed the woman "im scared of what you might do"
"I wont hurt anyone if i can maybe go out with you"
she turned in horror and apaul...he saw the fear in her eyes
"Thats my little joke" said zeke "but seriously...everyone dies"
"no one will escape my wrath...but before i leave i have something to do"
"let me see...what was it that i had to do...oh yes..now i remember..murder you"
