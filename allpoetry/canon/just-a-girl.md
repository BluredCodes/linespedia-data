title: "Just a girl"
writer: "Canon"

12 years old, she's just a little kid,
ashamed at what she's done,
She's gonna have a kid.
6 months in, she's hiding things that can't be hid.
now she's kicked out, her moms just dis-owned her out,
wont let her in the house, a little dirty girl with a little dirty  mouth,
disgusted at what she is and disgusted at what she's about.
Ashamed her name without a doubt, now she's out...
on the street, with no shoes on her feet, in trouble with the police,
now she's thirteen and she's forgotten about peace.
Now she's stayin in a crack house with about 7 others,
her supposed sisters and brothers.
She's gotta kid now, her little name is marie,
wanderin the streets askin for things for free,
she's only thirteen....she's just a kid,
a kid with a kid, ashamed at what has happened, 
sorry for what she did........
