Zeke and his stories are here in full form
From beginning to end to death from born
Zeke is me and i am zeke
This is my mind...it may make you weak
Zeke is canon...i am juice
one will flourish...you choose
zeke and canon, juice and me
Either way it goes you'll be choosing me

1:This is a story of a boy named zeke 
used to play ball but his game was weak 
he was outcast from the family and lived on the street 
all he owned was the clothes on his back and the shoes on his feet 
he went to school and was picked on all day 
he got biffed in the head and started seein gray 
didnt tell no body but it started gettin worse 
on his way home he stole an old ladies leather purse 
She didn give up the struggle, she kicked him in the junk 
He hit her with her own purse and stuffed her in an open trunk 
he got to the corner and fell to the curb 
yelled a yell that everyone heard 
screamin obsenities extremeley absurd 
he took a hair pin from the purse that he stole 
jabbed it into his skull creating a big hole 
bleedin uncrontrollabley all over the side walk 
now he's silent no more speaking cus he cant talk 
a pin in his head and pedestrians surrounding 
the only sound left was his heart that was still pounding

2:This is the second story of zeke...he's not dead 
I told you that first story just to put zeke in ya head 
He layed there motionless for about five minutes 
Then came the ambulance and stretcher and they put him on it 
took him away to st. Mary's to remove the pin and revive zeke 
After about an hour of surgery out came the pin and blood started to leak 
His eyes opened wide and he screamed kill the nomes 
the doc's and nurses went and fled to their homes 
zeke got up bleeding and all...stumbled to his feet 
killed three nurses on his way to the street 
looking like a zombie he entered a nice 'n easy 
screamin absenities and saying the nomes need me 
the clerk grabbed a 12 guage and off with zekes head 
Dont be so sure this is the end of the story...he's not dead

3:Remember zeke well guess what....he's back again 
If you remember...zeke lost his head...that wasnt the end 
Zeke picked up his head shook off the dirt and looked at the clerk 
The body pointed at him as the eyes winked and he smiled an evil smirk 
Zeke stood smiling for several minutes pointing at the helpless man 
the man seemed to be thinking of something to do like a plan 
Zeke finally spoke but was speaking in toungue 
"Niet num jik seito slofor" which translated to that stung 
A crash from the back of the toilet paper stack...stood a woman with a child 
The little boy winked at zeke and zeke started goin wild 
zeke threw his head at the clerk behind the counter 
Threw it at his face with such strength and such power 
Zekes mouth opened mid flight towards the mans scared little head 
The teeth from zekes mouth created a spinnig razor....now the clerks dead 
the woman started to run as zeke searched for his lopped off top 
Found it..saw the woman running and screamed a scream screaming stop 
The woman stopped dropped the child as he ran away 
"little woman whats wrong, you dont want to stay" 
"no" screamed the woman "im scared of what you might do" 
"I wont hurt anyone if i can maybe go out with you" 
she turned in horror and apaul...he saw the fear in her eyes 
"Thats my little joke" said zeke "but seriously...everyone dies" 
"no one will escape my wrath...but before i leave i have something to do" 
"let me see...what was it that i had to do...oh yes..now i remember..murder you"

4:Zeke is here again, wont this kid ever die 
Zeke cant be killed and no one knows why 
Well here is the rest of the story continued from the last 
He picked up the woman by the throat and threw her in the grass 
Walked to her again and and kicked her in the chest 
He threw her in a trash compacter and now theres nothing left 
He wobbled down the street like a crazy zombie 
Thats where i come in...he saw me and jumped on me 
started beating my head against the ground till i couldnt take no more 
I got up with all my strength and threw zeke into a glass door 
He crashed through the glass and tummbled on the floor 
He started picking up everything he could in the back of the hardware store 
got a shovel and started running towards me like a train with no brakes 
I broke a wooden chair and created several clubs and stakes 
One stake to the head and a club to the chest 
Tune in next time if you wanna hear the rest

5:After I clubbed him in the chest he started to groan 
I stabbed the stake through his heart and he started to moan 
he slowly dropped his head and his knees fell to the ground 
he started speaking this weird language and making a weird sound 
I slowly walked behind him...slow like in a movie 
He pulled a gun and pointed and tried to shoot me 
I kicked his arm and the gun fell to the dirt 
From behind him i twisted grabbed and pulled his shirt 
"Listen to me zeke its time for you to leave" 
I wrapped my arms around his neck choking till he couldnt breath 
then one hand on his chin and the other on his head 
One quick twist, i snapped his neck...now he is dead 
but that is not the end because i started to feel his breath 
I heard his voice..."you will forever be haunted by my death" 
A feeling shot through me and My knees became weak 
this isnt the end, because forever more i will be zeke
