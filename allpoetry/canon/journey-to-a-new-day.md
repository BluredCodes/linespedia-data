---
title: "Journey to a new day"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "journey-to-a-new-day"

Sprout your wings, fly away,
towards the morning of a new day.
Towards the mists of new, the shining light.
sprout your wings, you need to go tonight.
the new day wont wait, its got a schedule to run.
once that day is old, venture towards a new one.
pack your bags, flap your wings, soar to the sky.
follow the morning of new, that is where happiness may lie.
rest at the rainbows, travel under the cover of dark.
for each new night, is a morning at which you must embark.
keep your bags packed, keep your wings strong.
This trip is only a lifetime, its not very long.
When the sun begins to set, you better be on your way.
its time for you to sprout your wings, and fly away.
