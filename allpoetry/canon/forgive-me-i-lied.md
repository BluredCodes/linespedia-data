title: "forgive me, I lied"
writer: "Canon"

Im sorry god for everything ive done
Help me from this life of mine you spun
Im fighting myself, I love and i hate
It hurts inside, Its not great
so forgive me for my mistakes
im ready to ride lifes wakes
watch the sun come up, rain stop
no more will i drop
i fooled people thinkin i was sad
fooled people thinkin i was mad
i do all this for the green
doesnt matter if im mean
i need success cus i cant fail
hell no id rather bail
as long as im makin mad money 
And i got myself a sweet honey
ill be happy, so forgive me for my sin
now lets sit back and drink gin
party on fellas and ladies 
forget the crazies 
time we start laughin and playin round
so turn on some music and turn up the sound
And again please forgive 
for the way i live
