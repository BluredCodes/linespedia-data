---
title: "Thank you mom and dad"
writer: "Canon"
slug: "thank-you-mom-and-dad"
source: "AllPoetry"

your son or daughter has been released into the world
From the depths of love you produce a little boy or girl
precious in your mind, they mean so much to you
the love of a child, deep inside they love you to
as children we dont show our love but you know its there
sometimes your mean to us but we still know you care
Hidden love is what I like to call it and even still do
But im telling you right now, Parents of mine..i love you
I wouldnt be the way i am if you werent the way you was
I developed my self into me because of you and your love
