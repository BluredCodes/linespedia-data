Sometimes I thinkSometimes I knowThere's only oneTo whom I can goDon't have to be strongNo need to bowNo promise asked forNo secrecy vowNot a motherNot a healerNot a loverNot a teacherJust an equalJust a friendThe open mindMy heart to mendWho is this angel?Who is this one?I'll surprise youBefore I am doneHighest confidentO little pure heartA constant of timeWe'll never partStuffed rabbit of mineSoft to the touchFur all a-grayingI loved her so muchEveryone else has teddy-bears. I had to have a little white rabbit; I got her for my seventh Christmas, and haven't been able to sleep without her ever since. Just goes to show you that while I have a dark side, I also have a cute-and-fluffy side.
