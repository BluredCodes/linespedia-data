Mustn't.
Mustn't.
Mustn't.
Four walls.
Ten steps.
This way.
That way.
Darkness.
No smell.
No food.
No water.
I'm lucky.
I'm dying.
Mustn't.
Mustn't.
Mustn't.
Can't think.
I'll lose it.
Go insane.
That way.
Is madnes.
Ten steps.
Featureless.
No ceiling.
No people.
It's quiet.
Mustn't.
Mustn't.
Mustn't.
Exercises.
Illusions.
Eyes close.
Straining.
Striving for.
Strength.
Hair grows.
Time dies.
Wish that.
