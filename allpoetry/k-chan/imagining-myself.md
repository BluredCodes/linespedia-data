---
title: "Imagining Myself"
writer: "K chan"
slug: "imagining-myself"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/17346-Imagining-Myself-by-K-chan"
---

Sometimes I imagine myself
Wind on the water, flowing free
Sometimes I imagine myself
As what I think "me" to be
I see a song about to be sung
I see a poem not yet written
I see a hung and weary head
While starry-eyed and smitten
In the mirror, eyes of blue
Secrets that I've never told
Shields to hide me from you
Petals that I've yet to unfold
Sometimes I've looked within
Tried to determine what is there
Sometimes I've looked within
To locate my heart's flare
I find a thought unspoken
I find a dream not yet made real
I find a tear I haven't shed
A promise that no one would seal
Inside of me, my battered soul
Tempered now, to be strong
I hide it all about me
Where it's been, all along
I never knew what happened
You never would say
You never guessed I would remember
Each and every day
I see myself, I do not lie
I see what is inside
Deep within my falling heart
Where my trembles won't subside
Every action, every step
Made me what I am right now
Every word, every memory
Every tear that I allow
I stumble on a broken chord
To be helped along the way
Music that inspires me
Words that help me to stay
Sometimes I try to guess
What you would have me do
Sometimes I try to guess
What goes on inside of you
I wonder if like me you know
The secrets of your mind
I wonder if you'll search for them
