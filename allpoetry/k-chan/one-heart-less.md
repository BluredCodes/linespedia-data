title: "One Heart Less"
writer: "K chan"

Heart adriftMind set freeLonelinessIn eternityHeart crashingThud, a-thudEmotion-lessIn the floodHeart losingSight of youHopelessnessWith de’ja vuHeart slowingA line drawnExpressionlessI am goneHeart holdingBeing heldFacelessnessPeople meldHeart fluttrin’‘bout to goHappiness Will not knowHeart doneIs not hereLifelessnessDeath will sear
