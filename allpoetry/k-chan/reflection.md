title: "Reflection"

Say to me you know meI’ll say it like a reflectionSay to you I know youPause you for correctionUntil you’ve wronged meWoken in me, grief’s rageI give to you just a maskFor all the world’s a stageSay to me you hate meI’ll say it like a reflectionSay to you I hate youPause you for correctionLace a plague in my mailYet I’ll empty only to fillCurse me loud and strongMutter whatever you willSay to me I’ll fear youI’ll say it like a reflectionSay to you you’ll fear mePause you for correctionOnce I’ve told you rightKindly, I’ll rebuildMourning your ignoranceMy thirst for justice stilledSay to me you’ll end meI’ll say it like a reflectionSay to you I’ll end youPause you for correctionOnce, twice, I’ll beat youYour lesson learned wellWhen your mighty fallI’ll say a brother fellMy first written thoughts on this subject; I must admit some inspiration was given by Del's 'When Daddy's Gone.' Hope you enjoy!
