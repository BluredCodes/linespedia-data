---
title: "A Flower Unsurpassed"

She is a child when she's with him
Innocence and purity returned
Her worries, cares, and gruesome fears
Have elsewhere briefly sojourned
She is a child when she's with him
Soprano laughter trilling her delight
In his presence, quite sure that
They can make everything 'all right'
She is a child when she's with him
Accepting kisses with color in each cheek
Confident and sure beside him, though
Caught before another, her demenor meek
She is a child when she's with him
Her world narrowing to him, to now
Candidly giving her secrets to him
As to those few friends she will allow
She is a child when she's with him
Her hand in his so gently clasped
Theirs is a slowly budding love
In bloom, a flower unsurpassedAnother RP connected poem - for Goddess Steci and Cathan in the first stages of their romance. ^_~
