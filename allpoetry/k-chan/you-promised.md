Sometimes I think that
I should just go.
Just go to hell where
They say I belong.
The cross will pin me
Until I pass on.
Your certainty chokes
You can't tell.
Your ignorance helps
No one but you.

You promised.

You don't even look
When I reach.
Reach for something
That's just you.
Because you promised
You'd listen.
Whenever I was hurting
When I cried.
Whenever I trembled
You'd hold me.

You promised.

When I cried today
I was alone.
I didn't let you see
A single tear.
The last time I came
To tell you.
You forgot I needed
A shoulder.
Forgot I loved you
Like a need.

You promised.

A piercing torment
Trapping me.
Pretending that I
Still believe.
Carry faith for you
Even yet.
When you promised
That one time.
Were you planning on
Breaking me?

You promised.
I never forgot.
