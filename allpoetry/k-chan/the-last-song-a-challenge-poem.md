Enjeru, Enjeru - I was his angel 
And he was my pen's inspiration 
We stepped upon a rising cloud 
I thrilled to our escalation 
With this ascent rose my hopes 
I convinved myself that such 
Things as dreams and magic were 
As real as he I loved so much 
Letters sent back and forth 
With assurances of devotion 
That we hadn't met for real 
Made love no less an emotion 
The computer became my friend 
Because it showed him to me 
Though he was very far away 
I was sure we were meant to be 
But then his letters stopped 
He didn't answer any of mine 
I slowly became more desparate 
Pleading for him line by line 
Until, one day, that letter came 
The last from him I ever read 
I didn't have the strength to cry 
After I saw what it was it said 
"Dear Kaika," was written there 
At the very top of the page 
But someone else called me that 
Two years less than me in age 
"I'm not who you think I am, 
And I'm trying to apologize; 
Please try to forgive me, 
All I've given you is lies. 
That last time someone hurt you, 
I couldn't stand to see you sad. 
I pretended to be someone else; 
Please, big sister, don't be mad! 
I thought it'd only be a while, 
But now it's gone too far; 
Please don't let your heart hurt, 
Don't let this leave a scar. 
Kaika, big sister, I'm so sorry! 
I just hated seeing you cry. 
Don't be angry with me, Kaika, 
I swear never again will I lie!" 
My illusions were shattered 
I no longer could believe 
Shaking softly in the night 
Nothing left but to greive 
But to myself I gently mutter 
That I knew it all along 
That true love does not exist
