title: "First Date"

I’m shiveryAll shakey insideNow that it’s overBiting my nailsStuttering wordsWith chattering teethI’m lonelyThough my laughterWas nervousConversationSlightly awkwardWith long pausesI’m happyIt was worth itBeing with himThe first timeThough I was shyWith him near meOkay. Yes. Um. Weird. But .. it was my first date, and I just got home, and .. here it is. The shivering part comes from the fact that whenever I'm really tired or nervous, I shiver, complete with chattering teeth. Awful short compared to what I usually write, and in an odd format, but I think I like it.
