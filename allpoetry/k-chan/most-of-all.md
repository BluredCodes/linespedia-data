title: "Most of All"
writer: "K chan"

I love to sing
I love to be
I love to dance
Do you know me?
I love to laugh 
I love to write
I love to watch
Sure you're right?
I love to listen
I love to tell
I love to dream
What if I fell?
I love to run
I love to think
I love to walk
Would I sink?
I love to work
I love my blue
Would you come
To my rescue?
I love the night
I love the day
What I don't love
I won't say
Spare me from 
Heart's pain
Run with me
In the rain
By your side
Fingers entwine
Tiptoing across
A jagged line
Though the way
Is tough, untrue
Most of all
