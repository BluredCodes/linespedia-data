title: "Hesitating"

If I suddenly just wasn't there
Is there anything that you would do?
Would you bound down the stair
And call out "Where are you?!"
If I was that one that you couldn't find
And all I left you with was memories
Nothing more to leave behind
But a wind amid the rustling trees
Would the ocean grieve my loss
The waves crashing on solid ground?
Would sorrow and anger bitterly cross
If I was no where to be found?
Would you hear my voice across the way
Imagine my laughter and my face
Would I be in your thoughts all day
If I was gone from your side, my rightful place?
Would this really be for your own good
Or would I only be trying to escape the pain?
Silence pulls down its cape and hood
Is there anything for you I can gain?
I might wonder, if I do leave
What was it that we shared
Grains of memories through the sieve
To which nothing can be compared
A hesitation, and no more
You ask me what is wrong
I tell you it's nothing, look up from the floor
