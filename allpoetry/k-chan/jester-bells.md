A little falter of my footsteps
Tripping along the ledge
A little falter of my footsteps
Am I too close to the edge?
And the world is so bright
You look but I am out've sight
I'm dancing on pointed toes
Hidden right beneath your nose
It wasn't my intention
To escape your attention
But your eyes were crossed, your vision halved
I only giggled whenever you laughed
My blue sneakers, his jester bells
Hidden lies and magic spells
Willow-tree tears and never-tells
I hate the broken farewells
A little falter of my footsteps
Tripping along the ledge
A little falter of my footsteps
Am I too close to the edge?
Masks and masks - I want one for me
Little trinkets flung crazily
A tiny limp, a thousand scars
Bloodied feathers beneath the stars
I hate to say it, your timing wasn't right
You startled me into flight
Sharp teeth and shadows; this is all so new
I'm only here for one thing, and dearest .. that's you.An odd little song for one of my RP characters; she's singing about the fact that she and her big brother have been so much that they're both "faltering on the ledge," or close to losing it. He already snapped and wears jester's clothing, and the last line refers to his comment that he's only here because she is, and another's comment that if he was gone, she would be too. Some of the other stuff is connected with their general situation - she has wings, a small limp, scars; they were fooled by someone who wore a mask and betrayed them, and whenever she cries she goes to hide beneath a weeping willow so no one will find her. Okay, yeah. It's really weird. Even the tune I've set it to is kind've crazy .. But I wanted to put it up, that I did.
