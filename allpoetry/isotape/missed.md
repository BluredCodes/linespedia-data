When it all ends what will I miss?
Time passes me by ever so slowly;
I sit and ponder my question...
Will I miss the fragrent flowers?
Will I miss the sound of children playing?
Will sunsets still be red across oceans?
There are may things I will miss,
I will not miss the pain and sorrow;
Possibly in some small way,
I will be missed.
