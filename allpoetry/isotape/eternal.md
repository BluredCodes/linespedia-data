title: "Eternal"

When we first met a few years ago,It was as if I had always know you.Your ravishing beauty.....Your seductive dark eyes....Your sense of humour.....The love in your heart.....It was as if all these thingsHad been missing from my life;And now in you I found them.The days I spend away from youAre filled with thoughts of you....Wondering where you are,What you are doing;Whether you are allright...Do you sometimes think of me??I think of your seductive lipsAnd the tenderness of your kiss.My heart yearns for more.The time we are together,I never want to end....The bliss of being held by youAnd the wonder in your eyesAlways brings me joy.Was our meeting destined to be??Have we perhaps sharedA past life together??If the answer to this is no....I pray to the Gods thatShould this be our first meeting,It is one of an everlasting relationship,Which will span our manyLives as we travel through time.An existence without youWould not be a life....Simply an existence,With no beauty or love...No purpose to anything....No sunrise or sunset....Simply a dark void in my heart,An emptiness which cannot be filled.You are surrounded by beauty,You are like an angel sent to meTo brighten my darkest moments;A treasure to behold,A priceless jewell upon this earth.......I believe our meetingWas destined to be for all eternity.
