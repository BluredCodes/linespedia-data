Live your life for today,For your yesterdays are goneAnd are simply memoriesOf what was and might of been.Tomorrow will never come;Because a new tomorrow willAlways follow your next day.Enjoy today for what it is,The beginning of your futureAnd another day in your past.Live your life to the fullestAnd enjoy every moment of it;For you never knowWhich tomorrow will be your last.Send your friends a greeting,Simply to wish them well.Show your loved ones you care,Give a stranger a smile.Lend someone a helping hand;Always try to think of othersAnd they will think of you.Remembering that today is the beginningOf the rest of your life.
