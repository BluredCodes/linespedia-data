title: "A Tear"

There are times when I cry;
Tears of happiness or pain.
I feel the warmth upon my face;
Carefully catching a tear drop,
I wistfully stare inside.
There my soul looks back at me.
Next time you see me crying,
Gently catch my tear drop,
You will see into my soul
To find the love and sadness
I have deep within.
