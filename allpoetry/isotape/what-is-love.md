title: "What Is Love"
writer: "isotape"

Love is not being boastfull,
Love is being patient,
Never being concieted,
Selfish or quick to take offence.
Love delights in truth
And doesn't take score
Of any wrong doings.
Love is a special bond.
Three things will last forever;
They are Love,Faith and Hope;
But love is the
Greatest of them all.
