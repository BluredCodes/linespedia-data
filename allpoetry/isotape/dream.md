title: "Dream"

We all have love in our hearts,Some people have more than others.Sadly there is hate as well.A wonderful world it would beIf we only had love for each other,No matter what race or colour.No more sorrow,no more wars.Only joyful times for all;Unfortunately...it is only a dream.
