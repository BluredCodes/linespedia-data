---
title: "Dreaming"
writer: "Fading Soldier"
slug: "dreaming"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/10650-Dreaming-by-Fading-Soldier"
---

A woman, sings, dances, laughs with the angels
like two lines never meet 
she possesses infinite vision of tomorrow
as if she's so near, yet so far
just to touch her with mine eyes, a fulfillment!

Can't she feel the vibrations?
unspoken words of the heart
admiration, that is all the dreamer can do
she's a mirror of womanhood, a model to behold!

A miracle to make you believe
when the moon escape the sky?
when the sun cease to shine?
when all the seas run dry?

Just the thought, again, to love and to be loved
life's color, tone, perspective and wants changed
the desire to be alive, again burning
yesterday twas elusive, like the wild Pismo winds!

Can you blame the dreamer?
by fate... he was tortured, wounded and spurned
cruel, spiteful, unforgiving, savage fate 
all that is left is his dreams, his escape!

If the dreamer is again scorned
denied, worthy not to utter her name
the sorrow, torment, pain and anguish
let them be...his ego long been seized!

Will she grant me a moment?
to caress her in my dreams?
unreal, it is all imagination
it will never happen, only a dream!

She's invincible, an illusion
ultimate desire, to love her
a fool, that's who the dreamer is
it will always be a dream!

Stop...wake up now!...forget
it is a crazy, unreal dream
keep the pure admiration, true love within
let the hope vanish, like the wild, Pismo winds!
