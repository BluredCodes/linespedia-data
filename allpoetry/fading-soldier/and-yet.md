and yet..

the truth is so elusive
a swirling wind untamed
and yet...
time have its own mystery way
it will tell on you

a strong wind will come
blow the sand about
and yet...
all that was written
built and existed there
take flight upon the current
shine behind the legacy
of all that was there

many people walked
in and out of your life
and yet...
only but few
leave footprints
in your heart

;o)
