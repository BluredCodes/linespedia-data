---
title: "Doubts"
writer: "Fading Soldier"
slug: "doubts"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/10653-Doubts-by-Fading-Soldier"
---

in all things there's nothing real
free yourself from the concept of object's reality
if you believe in the reality of objects
then you are bound by the very same concept
concept which is entirely illusive.

if the mind is bound by illusive phenomena
there is no reality to be found
these occurrences are unreal
the belief that reality is within one self
then the true mind is to be sought 
apart from this false illusion.

realizing the essence of the mind
is above the comprehension of the ignorant
it might explain a million ways
but all maybe traced back to one principle.

wrong views are strong cause for corruption
while right ones are stronger to nullify the latter
when both of those views are discarded
then the mind is absolutely pure.

within an impure mind, the pure one is to be seek
once found, and is set right
the doubt disappear
the mind is freed from the evil domain of existence.

wisdom sees things in some ways
without going through the process of reasoning
knowledge is a powerful manifest
it suppresses wild passion, delusion, arrogance and DOUBT!
