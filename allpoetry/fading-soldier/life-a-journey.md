---
title: "Life ~ A Journey"
writer: "Fading Soldier"
slug: "life-a-journey"
source: "AllPoetry"

endure my friend
life is a journey
sometimes eventful and exciting
sometimes the opposite.

nobody is born with a silver spoon on their mouth
creations favors no one, it is naked and innocent
a fair start
nobody is ahead, nobody is behind.

you are the master of your destiny
it is you who can resolve your cause
don't wait for the ripe fruit to fall
reach out, give your best.

ingenuity, resourcefulness, effort and determination
these, together can move mountains
flexibility, willingness to accept life's consequences
preparedness for detours along the way.

there no shortcuts, no easy streets
nobody, yes nobody is granted special privileges
everybody are on the same footing
nobody, yes nobody is chosen.

it is forecasted, accept no deviation
today is today, yesterday is gone
tomorrows will be plenty, they will be all yours
grab them by the handful and more.

don't give up hope 
need to fight for life
for life is a journey ~ my friend 
a journey that we must trod as long as we live.

;o)
