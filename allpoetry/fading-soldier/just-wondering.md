---
title: "Just Wondering"
writer: "Fading Soldier"
slug: "just-wondering"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/9869-Just-Wondering-by-Fading-Soldier"
---

The wealthy and famous.....
I wonder if...
they know the meaning of charity
their heart bleed to the poor and needy
they shed tears when they are sad
they know the real meaning of love!

 How fortunate are they.....
but I wonder if...
they believe in the power of prayer
that it begets miracles
they care about their surroundings
since they only deal with kings and queens!

 I wonder if...
they ever really worked for a living
they ever dirtied their delicate hands doing things
they ever give a d*mn
besides their precious caviar and vintage wines!

 I wonder if...
they care to know
that in some far away lands, wars are raging
they know that the powerless were oppressed
that lives are lost, victims of greed!

 I wonder if...
they have time to recognize the beauty of the rising sun
they have time to smell a blooming rose, its fragrance
they have time to feel the freshness of the evening breeze
invigorating the soul of the weary, a bliss!

 I wonder if...
they fail to heed the call of the spirit
they shorn of all the rhetoric and obfuscation of life
they lead into untold heights of greatness and dignity
to be anathema of history!

 I wonder if...
they know the feeling to be hungry
with just a can of beans for a day
they know how frigid are the winter nights
sleeping without blankets under bridges
and cold benches on public parks!

 Are they to be envied? tell me...
envy with good intentions to enhance life
is a motivation
and envy with bad intentions
corrodes the basic principles of life's ying and yang
it's where greedy deeds are sown
worst than a potent poison!

...just wondering!
