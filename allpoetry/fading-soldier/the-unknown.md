force that push you in every directionno serenity, dangerous and turbulenta robot, brainless, a machinehold my hands, lead me where I can live.a place where there is no ugliness, no bigotrya place where people are peoplea place where love is the passworda place where i can hear silence.a place where i can see the light when i close my eyessee the light of sanity, a force to reckonsanity which projects beautybeauty of the mind and the heart.why tore down a mountain for a piece of gold?human survival?, want?, greed?it is insane, ugly, crazytoo deep to comprehend, a rotten core.hold my hands, lead me where i can finda place where i can hear laughterlaughter from the heart, joy!a stainless conscience, where hearts bleed.a place where i can live for today and tomorrowa place where yesterday was only a hallucinationgone with the bad reminisce of lifea place where i will not be a prisoner of the past.a place where wisdom can flyto soar like an eagle to the spacious skytremendous knowledge untappedraw, unpolished, pure.listen to the murmur of the windthere is a message from the place unknownhappy are those who are already therebut happier are those longing to go there.like a caged bird wanting to flyan empty feeling, constricting the heartlike the hiss of the angry lightningvengeance of the roaring thunder.your emotions let it flowrelease it...don't be afraidclose your eyes, try to see.....beauty, sanity, values, dreams, people,you and me, the unknown!;o)~~***~~
