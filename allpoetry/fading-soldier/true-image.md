---
title: "True Image"
writer: "Fading Soldier"
slug: "true-image"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

do not fix your gaze on what is seen
look in the heart on what is unseen
what is seen is artificial, transitory
what is unseen is true, pure, permanent and lasting.

judge not a person by his outside looks
they are but cloak of evil intentions
value a person of what he is
the hidden traits are reflected through his eyes.

obsession of ignoble ambitions
a monster that will change the whole-being of a man
like a bad weed that is not destroyed
will reproduce abundantly if not in time abated.

the true image of a man
buried deep in the heart
it is a mystery within
to open and to enter, need special keys.....
honesty.....
sincerity.....
trust.....
kindness.....
humility.....
compassionate.....
and most of all, L O V E!
