In the silence that followed...he bent to her ear and whisperedso softly his longing for hershe smiled and said nothing.his heart nearly broke the pain disappearedand he gradually spoke...'are you an angel or a witch''i am both' she replied'and much more i've been calledso many times beforei'm a priestessa Goddess.i'm the stars in the skyi was the virgin birthi am all these things.a nymphand an elf.the salvationthe destroyera child myself.i'm the oceanthat wax and wane with the moon.i am the mountainthat disappears into the clouds.i am the passing breezethat's seldom heard'.yours... ;o)~~***~~be kind to a soldier! just an amateur writer....
