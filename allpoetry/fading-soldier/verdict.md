The fate of the manis in the palms of his handsthe misdeeds, poor judgments, they matteredglorious moments on the pedestal, do not count. The damage was done, do not retracea hole was dug, are you ready?look down before you leap, even it is lateat the bottom , fire of hellfull of hatred seeking for vengeance. How ugly is the circumstanceunfairwrongbiaswho is he, who give the verdict?guiltynot guiltyhe do not have the right. Who is no sinner?whoever claim, he is notlet him throw the first stonedouble faced hypocrite...pretender...liar. A silent but deadly assassinhiding in the darkness with incompetenceno second chance, it is fatalwhat a pity, the fate of the man. Run...run...run...hidebut that is not the nature of the manhe is neither a loser nor a cowardhe knows the value of existence...he goes on. Recourse? step back...thinkconcentrate on what you know and do bestthose dark shadows hanging overhead will disappearafter a long tumultuous night, a new dawn...a new beginning...a new life!;o)~~***~~
