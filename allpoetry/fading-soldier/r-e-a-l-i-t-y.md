---
title: "R e a l i t y"
writer: "Fading Soldier"
slug: "r-e-a-l-i-t-y"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8958-R-e-a-l-i-t-y-by-Fading-Soldier"
---

rarely a human being so gifted can accept reality
it is a hard facts of life to be faced and addressed
it is calling a spade a spade, no otherwise
neither a shield nor protection.....
either truth or consequence.

over period of years, quotas of attitudes were collected
dislikes stemmed from experiences, sometimes transmitted
similarly, likes, built from strong sentiments 
and personal beliefs
attitudes based from emotions, not reality.....bias.

halo effect-judging, based on specific traits
stereotyping-projected mental picture, dangerous as well
premature conclusions, pre-conceived notions, 
false impressions
these are deviations from reality, contrary to norm.

associating human beings with their social affiliations
and cultural origins
development of understanding and appreciation of existence
genuine and sincere interest, 
toleration of faults and peculiarities
discovering rare qualities without certainties....reality.

extenuating circumstance, hearsay and fabricated evidence
coerced witnesses, unknown facts, unfair jury, 
victim of injustice
eventual prisoner for life, chained, hanged
freedom lost, no pity, dying, begging for reality.

;o)
