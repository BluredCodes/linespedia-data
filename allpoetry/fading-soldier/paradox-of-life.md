---
title: "Paradox Of Life"
writer: "Fading Soldier"
slug: "paradox-of-life"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/9866-Paradox-Of-Life-by-Fading-Soldier"

Life is an equation
balance on both sides
not a fraction of anomaly
it must be equal, tuned in on the shame frequency.

 What you give, do not expect reciprocity
the values fluctuate, it is an everyday routine
the virtues will sum it up
to subtract if more, to add if less.

 To persevere when times are tough
patience and hope, the will to overcome
facts of life, constant, a given variable
a missing component to create a masterpiece.

 To fake the way, just to get by
to tap dance, beating around the bush
no guts, scared to face reality
sooner or later, the truth will surface.

 Sanity to keep the human race survive
to project the paradox of the unknown
to force the exposure of customary existence
sign of the zodiac, let it follow its charted course.

 Nonsense, but try to reconsider
up and down, down and up, like a see-saw
peep through the hole where the light is coming
life on the other side has a new meaning.

 Second chance, a new beginning
everybody must be granted, at least once
to prove the worthiness of being alive
a chance for that sunken treasure 
at the bottom of the ocean.

 Abiding by the rules, no exception
the ifs, buts and ands must be discarded
keep on betting while the lucky streak is on
raise the ante to the limit, don't even hesitate.

 What good is to strike the piece of iron when cold
opportunity missed, passed by
knock on wood, for a rare phenomenon
keep the spirit alive, luck may be back again.

 When the desire to struggle glows no more
all the resources to continue the battle, exhausted
the confidence, slowly vanishing
a perfect time to recharge, to regroup
to get back to square one, back to basics
to remind oneself, that life is a mystery, a paradox!
