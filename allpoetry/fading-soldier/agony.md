---
title: "Agony"
writer: "Fading Soldier"
slug: "agony"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8960-Agony-by-Fading-Soldier"
---

buried beneath the vast chasm of life
a speck of sand along a stretch of white sandy shore
a bleak flickering light among the stars of the heavens
an unheard destination at the farthest end of the universe
a broken note on a beautiful sonata
a patch of gray on a green landscape.

the secret of the man, no one will ever know
the dagger that pierced his heart removed
the wound healed, but the scar remains.

over time, the pain subsided 
but the dark images of yesterday keep on re-appearing 
by a thief on the dark, his honor and dignity stolen
drained every ounce of his strength to go on living.

always bottled his emotions to face life reality
tried to forgive and forget
not to dwell on the memories of the past
tried to be vigilant to hold on to the remaining hope
the tribulations of life, tried to cope.

over time, the man tried to overcome
yes...did he tried...
he laughed on the outside 'though sadness tore him inside
when will it last? no one will ever foresee
just him can decide...his secret, the agony
into his grave he will carry.

tried to hide his sorrows with songs
with his writings might release the anger
and might soothe his broken soul
with smiles, he tried to block those sighs
but those whispers still penetrate...tearing his heart.

will there be peace of his well being someday?
only the man can decide and say
will the heartaches, pain, sorrow, suffering, 
and agony disappear?
like a thick black cloud vanished into thin air?
death before dishonor, is it the ultimate answer?
