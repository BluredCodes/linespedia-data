---
title: "Marriage"
writer: "Fading Soldier"
slug: "marriage"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8956-Marriage-by-Fading-Soldier"
---

two people sharing, growing, developing, learning
good qualities kept, nurtured
fresh as the morning sun, unfading admiration
slow to anger, full of compassion.

one is the extension of the other
no 'me first' attitude, no greed
idea of self-fulfillment, a downfall
emphasis is on 'us', together.

like a granite rock, a solid foundation
faith, a covenant without doubt or reservation
trust, a bond earned with time
understanding, accepting misgivings, patience.

sincerety with old-fashioned concerned
no skin deep emotion, fake tears
pure intentions from the deepest core of the heart
expression of desire, displayed in great passion.

marriage is a lifetime possession
it can not be take for granted, even for a moment
it is a sacred union of a man and a woman
guarded with dignity, rich with mutual respect.

God Bless you both!
