what is a perfect upbringing?
growing, coping, and maturing as the earth revolves
not afraid of the dark
head held high, when times get rough.

to be supportive is a consolation and reward
sacrificing, do the best you can, giving everything
encouragement and understanding must be abundant
them alone can own, nobody can share.

any material things are transitory
they come and go like the ocean waves
a rich mind is permanent
chiseled in stone, will last forever.

that special gift, it is there somewhere
it cost nothing but time
time to persevere, to build determination and confidence
give them time to earn 'em.

a good home and good upbringing
how are these related?
they are interwoven, meshed, molded together
a good home begets good upbringing
good upbringing, result...success!

for the sparrow, her young mind, hard to comprehend
it is not a simple a b c
two, sum of one plus one 
she wanted to find life beyond.

satisfy her curiosity, what's out there
test how deep is the water, explore the unknown
to enrich her knowledge, expand her experiences
to learn the facts of life, the truth of living.

to my precious sparrow, all i can do
is to give you my blessings, wherever you desire to go
can only wish you, all the luck in this world with prayers
reminding you always that i'm always here.

spread your wings little bird, do not be afraid
out there is a harsh world, but you can and will make it
be courageous to fly alone
just beware of the unassuming
the sky is very spacious and sometimes turbulent!

(dedicated to my daughter)
