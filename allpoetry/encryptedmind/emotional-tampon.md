Emotional Tamponvirgin flesh upon your lipsso many nights ago i gave my heart to you tie me up in your noose like deceptionsqueeze me heart with your barbed wire liespenetrate my mind with your webs of illusionwhat am i to youbastard wenchgoddess deniedwhy do i care so when i am nothing to you sado-masocistic love at its finestquick sand promisesyou'll never be here with me put on your mask ill weave the moons enchantment just as long as you fool me with your sour lovethis angels flesh and feathers lie in your handsas you seductivly lick my essense clean of them relish in the purity so clean of sinnyoull never know my sweet taste againvicariously feed of my soul what you cravebut can never beproverbial emotional tampontaxidermy and cotton candyadd another trophy to your wallbleed my life away with your hallow faith spiritual rapeeternal nightmare i gave up my wingsfell to earth to be in your armscrucified by my own desires
