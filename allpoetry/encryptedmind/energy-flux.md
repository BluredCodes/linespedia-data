Energy Fluxyou lifted me up just to watch me crash and burn tormented by your sugar coated demon lust who am i to trust?im backed against a walleven angels can fallmemories fade in this facadeof addiction to your quasi-real loveyou broke me apart and put me back together just to shatter my soul againlittle pixie not quite prettyi cant be beautifulicant be your supermodeli cant be your playboy perfect plastic dollby product of lethal lovemy heart stitched an cleanyoull enver understand what i mean when i say i love you broken soul shattered dreamspen me up in your atrium of feardeception so nearwatch my mind crumble in your handsembedded scars convince me im insane to justify your liesi will always remember you weve always been together with the winds that dance up high the rain pouring down in its comforting sadnesssouls united in an eternal energy flux
