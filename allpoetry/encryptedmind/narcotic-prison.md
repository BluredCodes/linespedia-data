Narcotic Prison Nail me up in your coffin of lieskeep me away from the truth and the moon filled skiesshow me things ive never felt nor seen make me your ice queentear down everything i lovegive em nothing to live for but you so that you may imprison me in your icy world of numbness and fantasytie my roots like barbed wire so that i may not strayhold my hand and lie to me tell me my soul will not decayseal me in your novacaine kissput up your addictive walls of illusionto keep me in this icy hellweave your infectious majik you do so wellcaged me in solitudelike an exibit for you to stare at and toy with but to never touchdrowing in your decpetionhypothermia of dissillousionmentpierce me with your morphine embracedrugg me till the pain is gone
