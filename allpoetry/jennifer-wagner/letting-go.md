---
title: "Letting go"
writer: "Jennifer Wagner"
slug: "letting-go"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/6112-Letting-go-by-Jennifer-Wagner"
---

They tell you to go ahead
but then they tell you NO
why do they confuse us like this
which way should we go?

They say its for the best,
to hold us back for now
we don't yet know the limits
or what life will allow

Every time we do wrong,
some how they know
how is it that they figure it out?
what is it that we show?

They want to watch us grow
and show us the ways of life
they are always there for us
through all the pain and strife

Many of us show no respect
to the ones that guide our way
But they keep hanging in there
like something is makeing them stay

Maybe its the love for us 
that they have come to know
or maybe its the fear
that one day they'll let you go.
