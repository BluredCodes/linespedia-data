You know my every secretThough we've been kept apartYou are the one who is there for meYou've been so from the startThe happiness you bring meShows me that you careAnd if I should ever need youI know you'll always be thereThough we are ages apartWe see eye to eyeYou showed me the truthWhen I was living on a lieMy past is but a mysteryto those who cannot seeYou are the only one I trustto see the real meYou gave me a new beginningEndless is my loveYou are the one who guides my waya savior from aboveTo my beloved sister Susyn
