Saddness is all around meMy life is all a messI'm thinking of ending it all.....I can't take anymore stressEach day I grow weakerEach night I cryMy heart is getting darkerAnd there's no point to even tryHolding on is uselessWhat will I gave to gain?When all I do is sufferthese endless years of painMy past is closing in on meMy unforgotten youthWhy did I keep trying to findSome sort of hidden truth?Now I know its pointlessTo keep striving onMy courage is next to nothingAnd my hope is completley goneI don't know what to do To live or to dieThese are the things I ponder,When I stare up at the sky.
