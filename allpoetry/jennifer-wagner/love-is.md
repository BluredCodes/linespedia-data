Love is like an animalthat you can never tamewhen it runs away from you,you've only yourself to blameyou are left alonewith a cold darkness insideyou are left with a broken heartand not an ounce of pridewhy does love treat us so...why cant love be real...love is not a material thingits just the way you feelyou are lost in denialfor fear of letting gowill you ever find another love...only time will showsometimes you are lucky and find the man of your dreamslive happily ever after....only in fairy tales it seemsall youve known is lostand you are put to shame....Love is like an animalthat you can never tame
