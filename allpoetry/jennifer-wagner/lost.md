Have you ever felt like screamingbut knew no one would hearthe words you long to whisperthe essence of your fearIts hard to find releasein a world so cold and bleakits hard to pick yourself back upwhen you feel so lost and weakIts hard to find comfort in things that never comeits hard to feel specialwhen you're told that you are dumbSometimes every problemrises to your headleaves you with no feelingand prayers that you'll be deadWhat are you supposed to dotheres nothing left to tryit seems your only option is to pulln the trigger and dieHow can things get betterwhen theres nothing left to dreamhow can you think positive when no one hears you screamDont tell me that you understandDont say you feel my painDont tell me that its just a phaseYoull never know my shameMaybe things get bettermaybe things turn brightbut when will they get betterwhen will the wrong get rightSo when you choose to live my lifeand have to pay the costyou wont know how to find your waywhen you realize youre lost.
