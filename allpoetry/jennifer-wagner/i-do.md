You try to live a happy life,or make it seem that wayeveryday with a smile on your facemuch to his dismayI know you are tired of cryingI can see it in your eyeswith everything he shouts at youwith all his selfish liesSometimes life and livingseems that of a forgotten youthbut you keep striving ontrying to somehow bury the truthCant you see his madnesswhen you look into his eyescan you see the awful truthwhen he tells his endless liesThings are getting pretty badIf you only knew.....Maybe the mistake was madewhen you said the words 'I do'
