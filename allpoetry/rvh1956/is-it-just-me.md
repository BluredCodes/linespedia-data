---
title: "Is it just me?"

As I ponder the world,
it's wonders, complexity, peoples and events,
my heart is stirred, making my mind seek solutions.

Is it just me?

Hearing the collective cries,
from all the hurt and confusion,
my compassion is awakened.

Is it just me?

Driven by my heart,
my conciousness has found, 
that we are all here together,
yet most are trapped by their own concerns.

Is it just me?

I dream of a day,
when people, using their collective talents, knowledge and abilities,
come together as a community, both local and global,
to make things be as they should already be.

When this happens,
I will no longer ask,
is it just me.........?
