---
title: "easy joy"
writer: "dribble"
slug: "easy-joy"
source: "AllPoetry"

I loved the poetry of your face
the angles and planes
that made up my dreams
far longer than I would admit to

and I loved you arms
circling my timid body
owning my love and my hate
claiming each of my imperfections
just for you

so I loved your call
dialing each number one by one
just to talk to me
blew me completely away
with every casual hello
issued from my smiling mouth

and now I love my freedom
the air tousling my crazy hair
instead of your hand
the wind caressing my face
instead of your mouth
to silence my stilted speech

and I love my memory 
of wind and of passion
with the same jagged skyline
to be my lover
and my muse

to know that I was "the one"
yet still left the party alone
for the chilled air
of my early morning drive
with my music singing
and my heart dancing
in the truth
I have only found
in my own easy joy
