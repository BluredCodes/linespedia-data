the rough of a moment

in the rough of a moment
cut out of the imperfect edges
of my character
in my emptiness
i am looking
searching for that one sunset
they all promised 
would hold the peace
that i crave
and the red smears into orange
and the orange fades into blue
and i’m left cold
in the chill that has leapt off the ocean
and into my heart
i shiver with my mind
and move quietly away
into the darkening light
that holds no tranquility
only the darkening promise
of another sunset
of another chance
to catch
the rough of a moment
