title: "[Quickly now the darkness comes.]"

Quickly now the darkness comes.
Cold harsh fangs invade.
The beating heart subdues and stops.
Now another's made.

Be quiet now, my precious.
The kiss of death is done.
Darkness now your only friend.
Daylight must be shunned.
