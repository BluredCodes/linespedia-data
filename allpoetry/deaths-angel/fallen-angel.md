Once was god's chosen,
now without her wings,
fallen below expectation,
a sad song she sings.

Angel of god's mercy,
filled with sinful desire.
Rejected by her faith,
banshed to the fire.

Hoping to recover
this poor lost soul,
makes a deal with the devil,
unknowing what she's sold.

She sold her life to darkness,
to wreak havok at his will,
a beast of horror she now is,
evil deeds she will fulfill.
