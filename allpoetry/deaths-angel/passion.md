title: "Passion"
writer: "Deaths Angel"

I dreamt I met a lord
in a wood one shaded day.
He said, "your passion is a glutton,
it devours what it may.
As it consumes, it's hunger grows,
for that is passions toll.
I see it burns beneath your breast,
it may soon devour your soul."
Then the lord fell silent 
and held me in his eye.
'Twas if he even ceased to breathe
till i gave him my reply.
"My blood burns red with passion,
my love a gouging knife.
I bleed into a blackend book 
my passion and my life.
For passion is a glutton,
this fact I don't deny.
Either passion will consume me,
or I'll let my blood run dry."
