---
title: "Hollow"
writer: "Deaths Angel"
slug: "hollow"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

An empty heart kills so slow,
lingering love continues to grow
easy to touch but hard to hold, 
reach for warmth but grasp the cold
fading fast from silent screams, 
falling slow to twisted dreams
speak the truth and hold the lie,
fear the day when love will die
tasting the pain so bittersweet,
sleeping alone in empty sheets
when love is lost and times are darkest
a hollow man cries the softest.
