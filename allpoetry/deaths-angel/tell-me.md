---
title: "Tell Me"
writer: "Deaths Angel"
slug: "tell-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Please tell me once again
what it's like to be young
I seem to have forgotten.
Remind me one more time
what innocence is like.
That trait has long since left me.
Show me what it's like
to be free of worry
I can't remember the feeling.
Tell me what life is like 
when it's not filled with fear
I can't recall ever being safe.
Teach me just one last time
what life used to be
when I was still a child.
