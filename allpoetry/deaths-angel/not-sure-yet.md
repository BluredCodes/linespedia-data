title: "(Not sure yet)"
writer: "Deaths Angel"

Prince of darkness
Keeper of the night
How long will it be
Before our souls take flight?
I wish for it now
Though for you I will wait
As long as it takes, love
No matter, the fate
I think I hear you coming
You've come for me at last
I bare my neck to you
There is no need to ask
I feel your teeth pierce me
I feel a quick pain
You stop for the moment
My body you'll not drain
You slice your wrist open
And place it to my lips
My body feels stronger
You've shared with me your "kiss."
