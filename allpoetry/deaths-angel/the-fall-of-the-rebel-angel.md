title: "The Fall of the Rebel Angel"
writer: "Deaths Angel"

The angel is nailed to the wall
by those who tried to prevent his fall
They made him a martyr 
with their sadistic slaughter
They tried to lay him to rest
before he bore the demon's crest
but they should have know 
that through his pain, 
their efforts would be in vain
For his body made the star,
and would bring them eternal war
He looked upon them as he wept
This flock of sheep that he kept
He spoke to them words of choked pain,
A curse made in his own name.
"I give my sins to all of you.
For me these things are now through.
I will fall from the highest depths,
I will gain power from your deaths
By killing me you seal your fate
I give you my sicknesses of green lust and hate."
and so the angel burned,
and as they watched not one head turned
In them his seeds were already sown,
though to them it was not known
and so his legacies of greed lust and hate,
conqured them all and sealed their fate
For those who were infected by his disease,
would enter his Hell, 
begging on their knees.
