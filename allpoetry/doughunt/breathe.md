---
title: "Breathe"
writer: "doughunt"
slug: "breathe"
source: "AllPoetry"

Take a breath
let the fragrance, texture and memory of this moment fill your senses

Grasp it
make this wonder, this glory, the crowning event of your life

Do it again
next hour, next day, throughout the days to come, until day's end

And you will know
happiness in full measure, spilling over to those whose lives you
touch
