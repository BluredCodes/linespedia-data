---
title: "Ride The Wind"
writer: "doughunt"

I awaken to find that nothing much has changed
The winter still brings the cold, can't warm myself at the fire
Seasons have carried away the years and still the story's left unsaid

The scene so quickly fades as I reach out to touch
All things once so bright and new go with the darkness to grow old
Sometimes I feel left behind, yet there's still one place left to go

I've got to ride one more wind 
I've got to beat one more storm
I've got to chase one more star 
I've got the promise spoken in a dream

So I write one more song, sing it into the wind
Pour one more glass of wine until I find a place to hide
It tastes bitter and it's sweet, you can shout it 'til you bleed
