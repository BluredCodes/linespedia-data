enter Winter's clean, pure, whiteness...but cold, so coldblizzard's fury at midnight, dangerous and unforgivingand just before dawn at morning's glowthe earth is blanketed so soft and warmthen first light explodes and dances on the ice crystalsas if alive in the treesand I am alive in the treessinging this Winter's song
