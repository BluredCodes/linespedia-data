title: "Dream Lover"
writer: "doughunt"

Dream, oh keep me captive enslave me in her secret lair tether my aimless wandering that I might find purchase there Just a moment with her countenance perchance, to heal this fevered brow and compose my transmigration that I may return to her as now And in day spring as I awaken my whispered journeys turn to mist her fragrance with me lingers 'twas sun burst when we kissed
