title: "Sing A Slow One"
writer: "doughunt"

Sing a slow one for me
sing it soft, sing it sweet
Take my heart, take my soul, my mind,
lay me down on rolling sea

Paint a landscape for me
paint it green, paint it blue
Give me meadow, give me sparkling stream,
whisper me on gentle breeze

Dance the last song with me
dance it close, dance it warm
Hold me hard, hold me all night long,
rock me where I want to be
