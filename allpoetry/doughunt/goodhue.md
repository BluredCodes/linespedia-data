title: "Goodhue"
writer: "doughunt"

Gazing kindly open eye, 
spy hidden antique stair 
Sparkling path, friendly sky, 
wonder who'd be there 

Forgotten dragon wings his way, 
cliff dwelling shadows begin to sing 
Fiery things behind him lay, 
what offering do you bring? 

Trembling blackness garden cave, 
speaks to open ear 
Glistening staff shows the way 
I feel very near 

Mountain throne, warmth unfold, 
arms of sunlight reach 'round none 
Let us know of things untold 
smiling death, all is one 

Sea of diamond, windcaught sail 
opens into good hue
