---
title: "Blinded"
writer: "doughunt"
slug: "blinded"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/2634-Blinded-by-doughunt"
---

See moments truth listen ancient wind flow ageless stream can't find, blinded, the light Whisper secret storm warn of your fall give time for a place to hide can't find, blinded, the light Grasp moments truth learn ancient wind die, ageless stream can't find, blinded, the light Die, die, ageless stream buried in the sand withdraw your hand can't find, blinded, the light
