---
title: "Autumn"
writer: "doughunt"
slug: "autumn"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Mystical, shifting changes in evening's hue, 
with season, stirs the longing, not fully glimpsed 
but powers the soul like some electric charge
that bears resemblance to that mellow dusk 
that bathes the earth, softly, caressing...

And whisked away, not to gentler, happier climes
but that place, always here inviting me to stay 
just awhile longer, just a moment...longer
The muse's wistful melody entices me to linger
and sample such fare to ease my rabid appetite.

Indulging whim or something more substantial?
At every turn I face myself as if in some
forgotten carny's maze, dark and ill defining
to slip into the morose and crying for release,
the moonlit darkness compelling me go forward.

And ever to return to Summer's hopeful vision
when all is bright, assured and tight within my grasp
the ocean's crashing waves sounding the crescendo
now distant as the undulating tide gently carries
this pyre and it's passenger on the charted course.
