---
title: "Free Falling"
writer: "doughunt"

Sea wind a blowin' 'cross my face, I'm goin' through time to a land that's fair Time waits a moment, silence steals the torrent and I've so little left to careOh, I'm glory bound, gonna' take it to the air and I'm free fallin'Star waits forever, I touch her fever lying with her in the sand My eyes are burnin', my life keeps turnin' the course already planned Oh, I'm glory bound, and I'm weary of this land and I'm free fallin'I won't be burried, I wont be hurried let the others lead the way I know the rain, bring it back again season's gone, no time to stayOh, I'm glory bound, and it's where I'm gonna lay and I'm free fallin'
