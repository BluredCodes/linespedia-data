Shiloh, Shiloh, your home is in the mountain
head into the wind, standing all alone
life is in the breeze on high

Shiloh, Shiloh, your place is in the meadow
running through the grasses, chasing 'way the hours
waiting for tomorrow's find

And the sun's golden peak beckons to you
as you fly to the west, lost in your dream
and the sky opens up, coming to view,
the earth at it's birth, out of your reach

Shiloh, Shiloh, your time is with the forest
lie beneath the pine, starry sky above
and you sigh to see it end
