title: "Lady of the Dawn"

Lady of the dawn lives in magic forest, to rise with the sun, life playing in her eyes A smile graces her lips, she dances in the rainbow mist, song so sweetly o'er the glen, wakens a sullen world Love answers tumult's cry, look toward her lightened face see the peace in her eyes, rest in magic forest Touch your hand in her's, sing her song of praise her home lies with the sun, she's the lady of the dawn
