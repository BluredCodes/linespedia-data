title: "Child of Cheer"

Child of cheer, so far, so near 
oh if I could hear the sounds you hear 
run through meadows golden, 
the touch of dew flashing in the sun 
the taste of life on my tongue 
dream your dreams, beneath the tree 
of peace that could but never will be 
Child of cheer set me free 
...they never will be
