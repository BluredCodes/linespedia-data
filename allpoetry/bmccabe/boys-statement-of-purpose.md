boys.

i waited in the garden that night
for you to kiss me,
and perhaps it was Eve’ the Serpent
who Tempted you away,

and still, i waited
hungered and
prayed
for your Touch

because, I thought,
here is the One
who can only be
Bone of my Bone,
Flesh of my Flesh:

My Adam.

--brian f. mccabe 11/23/99

/ / / / /

statement of purpose

you’ve got that
unpracticed arrogance
lithe grace –violent 
and strong…

	and i can’t help but
step back, fearful
	--who knows what you might
	do…

	to me,
		attracted by that strut
	and smell of sweet sweat
	muscle and youth
	sculpted together so fine…

So, what do i want, you wonder:
	-(and so do i…).
probably, I’d find joyous contentment
to just brush locks away
from those ignorant, blue
boy-eyes.

--brian f. mccabe 11/23/99
