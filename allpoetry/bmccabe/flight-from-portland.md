as i trisect my life, so falls our sun
as if unfurled banner won
and each of my defended vows inquires
“but still… salvation justice truth requires
but more than words?” my mind insists on flight…
acutely asked, no, accosted… Right
demands my all my whole my best not less
and daily i myself unhelped dress
for battle struck by God’s own love, His Son
The Christ, unfurled banner, Striking One.

			-brian f. mccabe 9/8/98
