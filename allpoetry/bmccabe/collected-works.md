-Collected Works—

I have desired to go
where springs not fail…
	       --Heaven-Haven, Gerard Manley Hopkins

His being –I recall its deep sweetness:
wine taken in my mouth, here opening,
forever waiting, powerful caress.

And easily into Him, I confess,
I did fall, ever the long race running.
His being –I recall its deep sweetness

in every glimpse and in His very press:
Strong –nature, song, future all blossoming,
forever waiting, powerful caress.

And each voice lifted heavenward does best
in work and toil: remember Beginning!
His being –I recall its deep sweetness

in lashing wind and world’s most peaceful dress.
Intent, I fall on mountain of living,
forever waiting powerful caress.

Heart’s Bastion keep me safe, one who is less;
Each day then, let me turn, always yearning,
forever waiting powerful caress…
Your being –I recall its deep sweetness.


--brian f. mccabe 12/1/98
