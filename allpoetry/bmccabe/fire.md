Fire
(a poem of A.M.)

i want your fire…
that charged spark
that draws us all to you
like moths

even when your heat
threatens to melt us…
we all cling to
you, our leading
column of flame

we convince ourselves
that, like Sol,
like Ra, like
Apollo
you could never
--would not—
disappear	--	leaving us…

me
(alone)

and so
(sunburned)
i bask
(enraptured)
we rush in
(blinded)
praying

even as we burn.

-brian f. mccabe 4/18/00
