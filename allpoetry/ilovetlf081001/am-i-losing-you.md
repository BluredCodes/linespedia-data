title: "Am I losing you?"

In my mind I think
you are in a distant land.
But here in my heart
Nothing has changed
I'm still holding you
in that warm embrace.
I know I didn't say it,
But I love you.
If that is too late
then so be it.
Isn't better late than never?
Please don't take the devils bait, and
take the easy way out?
For I know with out a doubt
that I love you.
So before I go on much further let me ask
Am I losing you?
