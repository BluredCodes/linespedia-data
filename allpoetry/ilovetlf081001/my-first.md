Never Kissed a Girl In Public
or Even held a Hand
You got me walking arm and arm 
feeling like I am the man
I don't care who all is Watching
I can hear something deep down in




I never knew what was waiting 
there behind the walls 
I built around my heart
Until I let them fall
All I know since I met you 
I can hear something Deep down in 
Telling me that you are the one for me
