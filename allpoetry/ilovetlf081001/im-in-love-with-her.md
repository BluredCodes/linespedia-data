I'm in Love with HerShe's Not in Love with meI'm still holding On To something I can't reachI'm not the one she wantsShe's everything I needI'm In Love with HerShe's not in love with meTime and Time Again I think of Her and then Cupid's Poison Dart Goes right through my HeartI Go Down to My Knees A Willing Victim If you Please
