I need you

Majestic one, his gaze enshrined
dark piercing eyes, his lips defined
to other men, my eyes are blind
I see you

Like silent screams, my deaf ears find
that echo through subconscious mind
your voice subdued, my thoughts entwined
I hear you

Our passions wild with fevers kiss
like crimson rose such fragrance bliss
oh how I long to be like this
I smell you

Our bodies sweat with passions heat
erotic dreams with love so sweet
I cannot wait till our lips meet
I taste you

Your touch against my fevered skin
the heat that swelters deep within
I ache to hold u close again
I feel you

Your sultry words your warm embrace
your taste your smell, your chiseled face
such haunting memories can't replace
I need you
