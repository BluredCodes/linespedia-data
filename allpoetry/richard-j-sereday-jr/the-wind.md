title: "The Wind"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"

My heart is like the wind. 
No one can ever hope to own the wind 
like you would own a piece of jewelry, 
but there are those who are happy 
to let the wind fill their sails, 
helping them to reach their destination. 

There is no telling when, 
or which way the wind will blow. 

This heart of my, 
it's as unpredictable as the wind. 

Even I am unable to tell 
what it will do next. 

I can not control it. 
It controls me. 
That is the way it should be. 

Should this heart of my 
ever find the place 
where it's soul-mate resides, 
that is where it will dwell 
for the rest of its days. 

I look forward to this day 
with great anticipation. 

I am but a passenger on this trip, 
along for the ride, 
but I will reap the benefits 
when it finally reaches its destiny. 

When it arrives, 
it will know, 
and so too will I.
