He said:
In another time, in another place,
I'd have to make you mine.

In another time, in another place,
I'd rock your world sublime.

I'd start at your toes,
and climb my way up,
to nibble on your ears,
with many stops along the way.

The longer it takes,
the better it would be,
no need to rush,

I'd endlessly linger,
til no inch of you be unknown.

She said:
I
have
searched 
the world for my 
one
true
love,

and you,
my
dear,
I've found,

so
in
my
heart 
I'll hold you tight

and in my dreams
I'll
lay
you
down.

In
another
t
i
m
e,


or in another
p
l
a
c
e,

I know that I would be with you,

giving in to your chase.
