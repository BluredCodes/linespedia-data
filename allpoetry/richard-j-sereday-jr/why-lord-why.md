title: "Why Lord, why?"

I walk these streets alone,

I look up to the sky,

as I scream out why Lord, why.

I wonder if I'll ever find you,

or is this my destiny,

this endless search I'm on.

Are you somewhere out there waiting for me?

It's this hope that keeps me hanging on.

So as I walk these streets alone,

callin out your name,

I wonder why Lord, why,

I walk these streets alone.
