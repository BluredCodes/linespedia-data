For a minute or two, 
                                     I wasn't sure 
                                     if what I saw, 
                                    was what I saw. 

                                   So maybe it wasn't 
                                      that, at all, 
                                   Or maybe it was. 

                                 Not a halo, nor wings, 
                                 did I see, but I swear, 
                               I saw heaven, smiling at me. 

                                   So how will I know 
                                   Just who can say. 
                                Who can tell me anyway. 

                                      Time it is, 
                                     that will tell. 
                                    For such a time 
                                    I knew this well. 

                                   I'm learning again, 
                                    as time goes by, 
                                   That life is worth 
                                     another try. 

                                     So try I will, 
                                      try I might, 
                                      And I bet 
                                 I win this lonely fight. 
                                On the wings of an Angel.
