Welcome to my world,
Step into my mind,

Read my heart,
know my soul,
as of you 
I do the same.

With me search for the answers 
to this mystery we call life.

Together we can search,
and what journey it will be.

Holding hand,
skipping stones,

we'll make this world our's
and ours alone.
