I pooped this Christmas,
I shat in the pot.

One turd, it did sink,
and one, it did not.

Mine, it don't stink,
hey, what can I say?

I pooped this Christmas,
it was just another shatty day.Dreading another lonely giftless Christmas for a month or two and now happy to see it go, this just came out of the blue.

I don't know it I should have even posted it, because it's really not me.
