---
title: "Missing her"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"

Why is it that I love her,
no words could ever say,

why is it that I love her
and she's on my mind
both night and day.

Oh, how I miss her so,
no one will ever know.

No one but me,
this is my fear,
is the way that it will be,

because, only God knows 
how I do miss her,
please bring her love to me.

To gaze upon her beauty,
and hold her in my heart,
until that day my arms,
her love does fill.
