title: "Touch me in the morning"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "touch-me-in-the-morning"

Touch me in the morning,
chase my troubles away,

and my fears and my doubts
that you're not here to stay.

be with me always,
as I be with you,

the world looks with envy
upon us, we two.

Dawns early light,
it shine in your hair

with you by my side 
I have not a care.

In my wildest dreams........
