title: "Grieving a loss"

A friend ask, "why so blue?"
                              "It looks like you just 
                               lost your best friend."

                           "I'm grieving a loss," I said, 
                             "an oh so beautiful dream 
                                I once held so close 
                                to my heart has died

                              and I'm weak in the knees
                                and can bearly find 
                               the strength to go on,

                           for I know not which way to go,
                         or how I'd know when I got there." 

                       So I sit in this state of nothingness,
                      looking like I just lost my best friend,

                          for part of my dreams was of you,
                              becoming just that to me,
                                  "my best friend."
