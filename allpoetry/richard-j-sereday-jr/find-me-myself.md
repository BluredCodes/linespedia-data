title: "Find me myself"

Find me myself 
in your arms 
all too soon 
this I pray, my love.

Should heaven smile 
down upon me
once again as before
in my dream that is you.

Breathe fire and life
into my heart,
beating, burning,
racing to meet yours.

Journey with me 
this journey we travel
this journey of heart,
and heart, and love.
