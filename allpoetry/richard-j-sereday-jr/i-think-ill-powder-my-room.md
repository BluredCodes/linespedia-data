I think I’ll powder my room,
cause this stuff feels so good on me.

I think I’ll powder my room,
for all the world to see.

It kind of looks like snow,
I think it looks good that way,

Hope Mommy and Daddy like it,
I don’t think they did the other day.

I think if they see it this way enough,
they’ll start to like the look of this stuff.

It make my furniture feel baby fresh,
and it smells so sweet too.
