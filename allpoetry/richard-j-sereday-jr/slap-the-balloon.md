At a party the other night I started a game of keep
the balloon in the air.  It soon turned to a slapping 
frenzy. And I thought of how it takes two to make it
work or not.
~~~~~~

Slap the balloon,
keep it in the air,

don't let it touch the ground,
don't let it, don't you dare.

For should it touch the earth,
you'll hear a popping sound

and the game, it will then be over,
then the players, they shall frown,

so it is up to you
and it is up to me,

only we as a team
can help the balloon break free.
