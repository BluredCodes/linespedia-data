title: "No one wins"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"

She had but one test, 
                                      she had to take, 
                                      to win the trust, 
                                      to win the love. 

                                          A test 
                                      so easy to pass, 

                                          a test 
                                       so easy to fail. 

                                      To hang in there, 
                                       did she have 
                                        what it took 
                                      to hang in there? 

                                     That was all it was, 
                                      to hang in there, 
                                       and not rush, 
                                     what would come. 

                                     The love and trust, 
                                  coming in there own time, 
                                      given that time. 

                                  But the time wasn't given, 
                                  and the pressure was on, 

                                     and it took it's toll, 
                                    and the house of card, 
                                      fell in all around, 

                                      and no one wins.
