You said that I smiled with my eyes,
and I'm sure they smiled 
even bigger to your words.

Sure, I smiled with my eyes, 
because my eyes,
they were looking at you.

but what you really saw 
was not my eyes smiling
it was my heart shining through.

You have placed a match,
and lit a raging fire

in my heart,
it shines there
just only for you.
