I am like a sheet of discarded paper in an alley, 
                                  whirling and twirling, 
                                     forever being 
                               blown by the winds of time. 

                     I never seem to stay in any one place for very long, 
                                     but someday 
                     I will find a corner that these winds will hold me into. 

                           This will be the place where I belong. 
                            It will feel right and I will know that 
                      it's where the wind was trying to put me all along.
