---
title: "Powder Blue"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "powder-blue"

So there you were
                                     plainly in view, 
                                   it only took a second
                                     and then I knew.

                                I was so totally captured 
                                   by your eyes so blue.
                                 They had a power over me,
                                    like I never knew.

                                 They had a power over me
                                   I just can't explain.
                                  Like the sunshine does
                               when it chases away the rain.

                                 The power had me dreaming
                                   this much I can say,
                                 of words like "forever",
                               and "would you please stay".

                                    So maybe I'm crazy
                                    I don't really know
                                  but your bluest of eyes
                                   just won't let me go.

                               And I wouldn't want them to,
                                    I just have to say,
                                 as they follow me around
                                   throughout every day,

                                    in my minds eye and
                                     deep in my heart.
                                   Got to make you mine,
                                   so where do I start?

                             I don't even have to close my eyes to see 
                                   a vision of you, blue eyes.
