---
title: "I Reach"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "i-reach"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/23838-I-Reach-by-Richard-J-Sereday-Jr"
---

I reach for you,
I draw you in
and hold you
in my arms.

I press my body next to yours
and spin you round the floor.

You reach for me,
and draw me in
and hold me 
in your arms.

I melt for you in dreams of us,
as to my heart, heaven is revealed.

Your lips, they beckon, as mine to you,
our passion knows of this desire.

I lightly stroke your hair,
soft as an angel's wing,

and gaze into your eyes so true,
and see my destiny call to me.

Your hands, they touch my shoulders,
and reach into my soul.

And I know I am right where I want to be,
I saw this from the start.

Then ring-ring-ring the alarm sounds out,
I must wake from this dream,

and daydream all the day away,
until night's dreams again come
and you're once more in my arms.
