---
title: "Two Afraid"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "two-afraid"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8475-Two-Afraid-by-Richard-J-Sereday-Jr"

The paths of two angels meet.  
                                    A love develops.  

                                  The kind of love that 
                               most could only dream of.   

                                So much love, so strong.  

                                 Each offering the other 
                                    all things needed. 

                             A fear comes into the romance.  

                                    A to much to fast, 
                                 to good to be true fear. 

                                  A fear that is born of 
                                 the pains of the past.  

                                The past loves gone wrong 
                         and the pain that come from the losses.  

                        A pain that must be avoided at all cost.  

                                  These fears remind of 
                                   what could go wrong, 

                                  they cause confusion, 
                                 and they get in the way 
                             of a chance for true happiness.  

                           Can these fears ever be overcome ?  

                                 Yes, I say,  with time, 
                                     and compassion, 
                                    and understanding, 
                               and the love of two hearts, 
                               the fears will be overcome.  

                                So much love, so strong.  

                                I look forward to the day 
                           when these angels will no longer be 

                                       "Two Afraid".
