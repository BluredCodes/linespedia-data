---
title: "To have you stay"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"

This is the one, 
                                there is no doubt,

                                 you are the one 
                            I've been dreaming about.

                             And now that your here, 
                                I truly must say,

                         I'll do everything in my power 
                                to have you stay.

                             I'll show you the love 
                               I feel in my heart.

                                together forever, 
                                  never to part,

                                  soul to soul.

                                Lovingly, gently, 
                              tenderly, patiently, 
                           my everything is yours.....

                      But you already knew that, didn't you?
