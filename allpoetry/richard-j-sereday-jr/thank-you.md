To My Love: Thank You 

                                      So wonderful is it, 
                                     to lay my head down 
                                        for the night, 
                                     with you by my side, 

                                    and there were times 
                                        in the night 
                                     that I would wake, 
                                     and look over at you 
                                     sleeping next to me, 
                                    and I just felt so lucky, 
                                  and so where I want to be. 

                                    As I reach out for you 
                               and touch of your lovely dark hair, 
                                and feel of your tender soft flesh, 
                             and taste of your succulent sweet lips, 
                                      I give thanks that 
                                     I have been allowed 
                                        to be a part 
                                        of your life. 

                                    Thanks for being you, 
                                   and for taking the time, 
                                        to see in me, 
                                       the worthiness 
                                       to be someone 
                                     that you could love. 

                                        Such a gift.
