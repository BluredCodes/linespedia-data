title: "I bring to you"

I bring to you these gifts,
                               the gifts of heart and soul,

                                 the essence of my being
                                 layed before your feet.

                                  The most vital parts,
                                   out to you I hold, 

                                 grasp them if you will,
                                seize them in the moment,

                                clutch them to your core,
                               if the inclination dictates.

                                The prize, it will endure,
                               the love, it shall prevail,

                                       you and I, 
                               in the arms of each one the other,

                                          always.
