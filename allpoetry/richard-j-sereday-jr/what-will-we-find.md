What will we find,
between you and I,

will it come easily 
without even a try,

I guess in due time
that, we will see,

Let’s share us this laugher
as we play playfully,

and when the time comes,
that our lips, they do meet,

In my mind I envision,
that the taste will be sweet.

That time is coming,
yes it’s on it’s way,

and when it arrives
