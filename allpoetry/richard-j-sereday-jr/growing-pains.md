---
title: "Growing pains"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "growing-pains"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/16543-Growing-pains-by-Richard-J-Sereday-Jr"
---

We are all brought into this world in the same way. 
                      It is a painful experience for mother and child. 

                     Life is lived, then we are taken from this world. 

                   This too is a painful experience for mother and child. 

                            With birth's pain also comes joy, 
                                The joy of a new life. 

                        A blank sheet of paper in the book of life, 
                         waiting to be filled in one line at a time. 

                                  The first breath, 
                            the air of a strange new world, 
                                never before known. 

                                  Gone forever is 
                              the security that once was, 
                              inside the mother's body. 

                              This tiny little human child, 
                           unable to care for itself in any way. 

                                  Unable to control 
                           even the most basic of functions. 

                                  The first feeding, 
                                  a whole new way 
                                to obtain nourishment. 
                                   The first tooth, 
                            to be followed by many others. 

                            The first steps, the stumbling, 
                            the falls, the bumps and bruises. 

                                  So much to learn. 

                       A seemingly overwhelming task lays ahead. 

                               Learning how to survive 
                                in this world of ours. 

                              Along with all these firsts 
                               comes the first diapers. 
                                 The potty training. 
                                 Feeding ones self, 
                                 dressing, cleaning, 
                                 grooming, all things 
                               that must be mastered. 

                               As time passes we grow 
                           and learn to survive for ourselves. 

                               A living must be forged 
                                  out of this world. 

                           Much sweat and toiling is required 
                            to succeed in this game of life. 

                             Success, a thing measured in 
                                our accomplishments, 
                                 our achievements, 
                               our worldly possessions, 
                             and of course our memories. 

                               Life is lived, and we age. 

                                The time comes when 
                              we start to have problems 
                               doing many of the things 
                         that it took us all these years to learn. 

                         Our bodies become tired, and worn out. 

                              There comes a time when 
                        we can't do many of the simplest of things 
                            that we once took for granted. 

                             Merely getting out of a chair 
                              seems to takes all we have. 

                                 Walking, each step, 
                                a painful experience. 

                               Should we live that long, 
                                  a time will come 
                                    that we need 
                                the same type of care 
                            as we did in our earliest years. 

                            We will have to have our needs 
                                 fulfilled by others. 

                                 As in the beginning, 
                              we will once again require 
                             feeding, washing, grooming, 
                                and yes, even diapers. 

                             Should we be lucky our minds 
                                will still be in tacted, 
                           for after all the work of our lives, 
                               all we will have to show 
                                for it is our memories, 
                              the treasures of our mind, 
                                 and the love shared 
                                with family and friend.
