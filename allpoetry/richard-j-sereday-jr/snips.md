title: "Snips"

Snips and snails and 
                                puppy dog tails are boy, 
                                    of which one am I.

                               Rough and gruff and jagged 
                                    around the edges, 
                              we are, as are our thoughts, 
                                   feelings and words. 

                                  Sugar and spice, and 
                               everything nice, are girls, 
                                  of which one you are.
                                 Delicate and  fragile, 
                             and tender in heart and feelings

                        Course is the grit which is man at core. 
                                  Abrasive to the touch,
                                  stumbling, bumbling, 
                            and bungling his way through life
                                   more times than not.
                                             
                                  Often times abrading 
                           the tender fragile petals of woman.

                                  Theses things I know, 
                          so I try as my words come to my mouth 
                                 to buff and polish them 
                              and knock off the rough edges 
                                    before I release 
                               to your oh so delicate ears 
                                       their fury.

                             Listen with your heart to mime 
                                    and you will hear
                               the meaning beyond my words.
