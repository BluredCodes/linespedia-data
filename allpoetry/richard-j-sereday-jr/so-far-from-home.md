---
title: "So far from home"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "so-far-from-home"
source: "AllPoetry"

My heart, it feels so far from home, 
                            now I know how yours must feel.

                                          for

                                 You came into my life,
                            but I fear that you won't stay,

                             so how can my heart love you,
                             if it believes you'll go away,

                            but it doesn't seem to stop it,
                              It wants to love you anyway,

                                          and 

                         you'll take with you a big old piece,
                             and leave such a gapping hole,

                          how could I set myself up like this,
                              that much I just don't know

                          The pain I've grown to know to well,
                               never seems to let me go.

                          These are the things I fear so much,
                               the uncertainty and doubts

                                So hold me in your arms,
                               and cast these demons out 

                                yes, kiss away my fears,
                               and chase away my doubts,

                        if you can, if you will, if you should.

                           I dream of forever, but sometimes
                        is seems like such an impossible dream.
