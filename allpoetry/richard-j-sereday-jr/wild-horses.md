---
title: "wild horses"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "wild-horses"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I could not love you more,
but still I do,
with each moment that we share,

ever increasing as time is shared
between you and I, my love.

I wish to grow to know of you,
more and more with each passing day,

and you fear that as I learn,
I'll become aware of things 
that make me run away.

You are obviously unaware of how I feel for you,
and how you make me feel when we're together.

How could any man run from feelings such as these?
It would be impossible I say, 

for even wild horses would not have the power
to put my heart away.
