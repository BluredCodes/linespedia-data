title: "Exactly Like You"

In case your wondering
                                      what I did today

                                    I bowed down my head
                                     and I began to pray

                                    I ask of the father 
                                      if there is a way
                                     to help understand 
                                   why you had to go away

                                  I ask if he could help me
                                     start my live anew 
                                 with someone else different
                                      exactly like you

                             exactly like you in all but one way
                                   you see I needed a love
                                     that won't go away

                                   And I ask that for you 
                                    he would do the same
                                  and wash away our tears 
                                    and give no one blame

                                   and accept our losses 
                                      in a graceful way
                                    and find someone new 
                                   that wouldn't run away,

                              from the trials, and tribulation,
                                    that we face everyday

                               our imperfections, and mistakes
                                    that all humans make
                                  like the mistakes we made
                                   that got in love's way 

                                and I ask help to do better 
                                     with somebody new 
                                  and not chase them away 
                                    just like I did you.

                                    because I know that 
                                       I'm good enough
                                    and I do deserve love

                              I still pray that he'll send it 
                                      from heaven about

                            I believe that he heard as I did pray
                              and I believe that he'll answer 
                                     but in his own way

                                  He knows from my heart, 
                                   for these things I ask
                                 And it is not unrealistic 
                               to find a true heart that lasts

                                        and anyway, 
                       I think he has a soft spot in his heart for me
