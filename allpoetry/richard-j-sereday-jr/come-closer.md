title: "Come closer"

Come closer to my heart,
                            my hands to you, I reach.

                             Come closer to my heart,
                            my arms have opened wide.

                                Step into my soul,
                             it's warm inviting glow,
                               beckons from within,

                          as it waits to find you there,
                               next to me, so deep.

                              Know me as your mate,
                                that of our souls.

                      In the time that you feel your ready,
                               all so soon I pray.
