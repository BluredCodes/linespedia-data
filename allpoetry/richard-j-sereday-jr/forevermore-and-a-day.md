To you I came
to help you on your way.
to you, I’ve grown to love.

Not to have you to hold today,
to squeeze you in my arms,
and press to you my soul,

that is how these things, they go
just like you went away,
but in my heart the memories stay.

There is no power could take that away,
so I’ll think of you from day to day,
and only wonder what if.
