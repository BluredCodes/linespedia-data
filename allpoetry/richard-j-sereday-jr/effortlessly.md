From the time we first did meet,
I felt this connection undeniable.

I know you felt it too,
for your eyes,
they told me so.

So where do we go from here?
The answer for me, is plain to see,
into your arms for now and forever.

An everlastingly love will grow,
together forevermore, eternally.

Some will grow apart,
but we shall grow together,
defying all the odds,

and we will make it last
with all the work it take,
but seemingly so effortlessly,
this love that was meant to be.
