title: "Come to me"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"

Come to me from afar,
from that place that seems, 
impossible to escape.

Let me give to you 
my gifts of love undying,
that you have never before 
known to be so deep and true.

A love so unwavering,
until time, it does end,
and then over and again,

eternally yours, 
these flames will burn,
in a time limitless.

As long as breath is in my lungs,
this love will thrive and flourish,
as it matures and grows,

this is my promise to you.
