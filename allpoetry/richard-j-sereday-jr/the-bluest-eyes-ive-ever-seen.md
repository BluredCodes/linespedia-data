They came up to me, 
                            said "hello, come with us.
                                 We have someone 
                              we want you to meet", 
                                 so follow I did,

                        and there she stood, spirit aglow,
                       radiant hair of gold, flowing down,
                          sprinkled with sparkling dust 
                                from heaven above.

                   And before I even realized what I was doing,
                     I took her hand and dropped to one knee,
                          as that hand, I gently kissed.

                       Someone I should meet, fate said so.
                          Someone I should get to know,
                               my heart told me so,
                              should I be so lucky.

                          So there I was, staring into 
                      the biggest bluest eyes I'd ever know.
                      They were bluer that the bluest skies 
                            on the sunniest of days. 
                           Totally captivated by these 
                             heavenly pools of blue,

                              hanging on every word 
                         that came from her angelic voice
                          and we talked, and we shared, 
                       and we learn of each one the other,

                                and I'm not sure, 
                         but I think I caught a glimpse 
                            of her soul a time or two,
                                 as she did mine.

                          Surely I was under her spell 
                      as I stared into those bluest of eyes,
                       praying the night would never end, 
                                 but end it did,

                             and with that end came 
                          a new day and another chance, 

                        another chance to chase my dream. 
                              Dreaming of my angel.
                        I know she's out there some where
                            and could this be my time?
                              could I be so blessed?

                       I just can't get her out of my mind 
                          day and night, night and day 
                               till we meet again,
                              and once more I gaze 
                             into those eyes so blue.
                                      ~~~~~~
                             Thank you, heaven above.
