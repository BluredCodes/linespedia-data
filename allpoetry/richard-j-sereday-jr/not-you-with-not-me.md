---
title: "not you with not me"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "not-you-with-not-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

If you could love me
like I would love you,
it would bring tears of joy to me.

If you would love me 
like I do love you,
what a love this world would see.

But you can't love me 
like I would love you,
if only given that chance.

No, you can't love me
like I'm loving you,
it's an unloved circumstance

So I can only ponder, 
why these dreams
they just can't be.

Yes, all I have to ponder is
this not you with not me
