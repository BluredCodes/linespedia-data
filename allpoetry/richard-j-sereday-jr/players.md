Instantly attracted to the players,
who would have no time 
to get to know you 
and don't want to anyway.

They just want one thing 
and 
it is not the thing that you want.

Deep feelings run deep, 
but lust runs on the surface,
as it comes and goes 
with each passing beauty,
your pretty boys do see.

You're a prize to be won,
not a passion fruit to be picked 
by any pretty boy who 
would reach for you 
only to take a bite,
then toss you aside.

Look before you leap
into those pools of pain
you've grown to know all too well.
