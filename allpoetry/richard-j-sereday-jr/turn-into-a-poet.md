If I don't watch it,
I am going to 
turn into a poet.

I think too much,
I cry too much,

I dream too much,
I cry too much,

I wish too much,
I cry too much,

I pray too much,
I cry too much,

I write way too much,
I cry too much.

Yes, if I don't watch it,
I am going to 
turn into a poet.

Oh yeah, did I mention
I cry too much,
I think????
