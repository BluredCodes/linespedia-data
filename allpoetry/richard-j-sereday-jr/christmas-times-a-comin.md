Christmas time's a-comin,
                                Christmas time's a-comin,
                               my true love she's a-runnin,
                                 she stole my heart away.

                          It'll be a blue Christmas without her,
                           bluer than the world can ever know.

                              And I believed it wasn't over 
                               till it was all talked out,
                              but she believed it was over 
                                   when she walked out,

                                   and I take my blame
                                 for mistakes I've made,
                                     sorry as can be.

                      Self, please help me change the things I must,
                            the love of my life forever gone, 
                               my heart now forever frozen

                          and the best days of our lives together
                           no longer have their chance too be.

                            One more joyless Christmas Season
                                       in my life,
                               too many I have surely seen.

                                 Santa, I wasn't so bad 
                                     that you should 
                                   take my angel away,
                                       was I???????
