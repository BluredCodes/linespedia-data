---
title: "No Angel"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "no-angel"

We know you think that you are 
                              the ArchAngel of the World,

                         but all too many time we have seen you
                           somewhere between heaven and earth

                      and we have seen your feet touch the ground,
                      and we have even seen you stumble and fall,

                           and get up brushing off the dust,
                        and looking around to see if anyone saw.

                       And we look away as not to embarrass you,
                                      but we see.

                          But we know how tuff the tasks are,
                                so we point no fingers,

                        for we have all stumbled as well as you
                        and felt your pointing in our own backs

                                 and we don't like it, 
                               so we don't do it so well

                           So just let me say that we know..
                                   You are no Angel.
