title: "Here and now"

Life is like 
                                   the waters of the river, 

                                          and we, 
                                  ducks between its banks, 

                                   The river forever flows, 
                                        Its waters, 
                                       always coming, 
                                       always going, 

                                    always here and now 
                                       for an instant, 
                                   and then they're gone. 

                                   At best we should enjoy 
                                       that which is, 
                                       here and now. 

                                   Treasure the memories, 
                                   dream of what will come, 
                                 but live the moment we must.
