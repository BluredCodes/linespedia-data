Live up to me my dreams
if you can, if you will,
as I to you, become yours 
time and again.

Be there for me in the night
while I sleep and dream of you,

until the day these dreams come true
and I stand in front of you,

and we touch and I wrap you in my arms,
as you do the same to me.

This embrace that we will know,
like the world has never seen.

Eat your heart out Romeo and Juliet
and try to match us if you can,

as we become the new benchmark
for what the greatest love should be.
