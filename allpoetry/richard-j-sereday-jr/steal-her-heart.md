---
title: "Steal her heart"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "steal-her-heart"

I'm sorry my friend,
for I have wronged you,
I tried to steal her heart away,
I cannot say, I didn't know better,
and lie to you that way.

I believe you were 
close to losing her,
I could see it in her eyes,
but I tried to talk her into
giving you just that one more try.

I told her if it didn't work,
and she had to get away,
I'd hold her in my arms,
and take her pain away.

So I sent her on 
her way back to you,
as slightly I did pray,
that it could all be worked out,
and you two could find a way.

But I must be honest with you,
if you two didn't find that way,
to find the things that you once had,
I'd give them to her in every way.

So, yes, I know that I have wrong you,
and of this I am ashamed,
but she's so sweet and beautiful,
to me , how could you blame.
~~~~~~~~~~
~~~~
better written with my tears than yours,  
at least for you, that is, my friend.
