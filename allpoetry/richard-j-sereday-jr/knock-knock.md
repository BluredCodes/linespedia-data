Knock! Knock!

who's there?

intelligent conversation.

intelligent conversation who?

exactly!

-----

I got a George Foreman Grill for Christmas
and I can tell you one thing.

Don't try to cook Tomato soup with those things!!!Is this funny?

Did anybody LOL???
