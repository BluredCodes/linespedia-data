---
title: "to dream again"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "to-dream-again"

don't be afraid to feel again 
                              if your heart, it tells you so

                              don't be afraid to care again 
                               your heart will let you know

                              don't be afraid to reach again 
                              should your spirit dictates so

                             don't be afraid to trust again, 
                               if you trust, you can let go

                            and don't be afraid to dream again 
                            your dreams just might come to you

                                   don't be afraid of me
                               and I won't be afraid of you

                             We'll fight these fears together,
                                   together us, we two.
