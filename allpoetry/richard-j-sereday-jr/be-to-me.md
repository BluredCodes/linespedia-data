---
title: "Be to me"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "be-to-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/10792-Be-to-me-by-Richard-J-Sereday-Jr"

Be to me mine, 
                                      be to me here,
                                       by my side,

                                      as I love you 
                                     heart and soul,
                                     for all times, 
                                     now and always,

                                      love undying, 
                                      love unending,

                                    ageless timeless, 
                                  eternally everlasting.

                                   Be to me all things,
                                 as to you I am the same.
