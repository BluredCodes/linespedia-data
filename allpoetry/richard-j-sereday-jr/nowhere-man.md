---
title: "Nowhere man"

Nowhere man lost in space,
in this space in my empty heart.

This emptiness I just cannot stand,
for the cure where do I start?

Nowhere with no one, 
but this chill that I feel,
it's numbness 
is making me blue.

If you where my woman
and I was your man,
I would know just what to do.

I'd have you, my woman
and I'd have me a love,
the love I'd be having with you.
