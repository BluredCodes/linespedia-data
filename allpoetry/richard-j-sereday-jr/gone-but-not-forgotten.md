---
title: "Gone, but not forgotten"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "gone-but-not-forgotten"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/21029-Gone--but-not-forgotten-by-Richard-J-Sereday-Jr"
---

She came into my life 
                                 at a time when I was 
                                  lonely and alone.  

                             I had a heart full of pain.  

                                  She healed it with 
                                  her loving touch.  

                                     She showed me 
                                what heaven was like.  

                                  I just knew she was 
                              the answers to my prayers. 

                           I was the happiest man on earth.  

                             She is now gone from my life, 
                                and I only wonder why.  

                                 I miss her so much.   
                                I miss being with her, 
                                    and loving her, 
                               and being loved by her.  

                               I am praying that somehow 
                                   we can find a way 
                             back to each others hearts.  

                           My prayers are usually answered, 
                                  but rarely in time.
