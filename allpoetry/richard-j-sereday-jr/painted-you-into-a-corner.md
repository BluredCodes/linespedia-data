title: "Painted you into a corner"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "painted-you-into-a-corner"

I know I'll never see you again,
and I've caused you lots of pain,
and painted you in a corner,
that's hard from to refrain.

It was never my intention,
to steal your love that day,
but I wanted you so badly,
I just couldn't stay away.

I'll treasure every single moment,
as they live on in my mind,
oh, in another world 
what might have been,
and in another time,

I will always contemplate,
Oh, if you were only mine,
what a love our love would be,
the grandest we could find.

I would like to offer you,
from the bottom of my heart,
the most deep and sincere apology,
so tell me where to start.
