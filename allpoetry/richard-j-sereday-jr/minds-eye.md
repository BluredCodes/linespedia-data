---
title: "Mind's eye"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "minds-eye"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/52434-Minds-eye-by-Richard-J-Sereday-Jr"

I know you see me
through your eyes
in a certain way,

but do you know
the me I am,
as I live day to day?

Don’t feel disappointed
when you find
that I am who I am,

and not who
you would have me be
in your own minds eye.
