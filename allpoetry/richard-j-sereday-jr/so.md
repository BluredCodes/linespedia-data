So, 
you've been watching me 
for years, 
you say.

Been wanting to wrap your arms around me
and hold me tight,

feel my rock hard arms 
around you too.

You say my wide shoulders make you swoon
and upon my chest, you'ld like to tarry.

And you say when I walk 
it makes you giggle,
cause you're watching my butt 
and like the way it does wiggle.

So,
I wonder why, years is what it took,
cause I never knew you gave a second look,
upon me from day to day,
for never one word, did you say.

Yes, it's true, when my dimples you see,
on that big old smile from I to thee,
is that I'm thinking just maybe 
some fun there could be,


So,
All I can tell you is, here I am
and prove that what you say is what you mean,

I'm ready, 
I'm willing,
I'm able,
I'm waiting, 
so come to me.
