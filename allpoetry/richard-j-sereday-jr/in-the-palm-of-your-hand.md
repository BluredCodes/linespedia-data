I wondered if you knew,
                                   you hold my heart
                               in the palm of your hand?

                                 I really think you do,
                              by the gentle way you touch,
                              and hold it close to yours,

                             and the way you make me feel,
                              that I really matter to you,
                          and you apreciate who and what I am.

                               And I do the same too you,
                            for that's how you make me feel,
                           when we talk and touch and share.

                               To find a sharing woman, 
                         in the day and age of a material world
                                 how lucky could I be?

                             A life times worth of future, 
                                one day at a time I see
                                   for you and for me
                                 should we be so lucky

                                         LUCK:
                         (laboring under the correct knowledge)

                         I think we both have luck on our side,
                      and the will to be the best that we can be.

                          So where have you been all my life?
                          I guess that really does not matter,
                            for I know where you now stand, 
                         close by my side, my dream come true.

                                  I feel that we both 
                              have the correct knoweldge,
                                and what a rare thing, 
                            so luck is no doubt on our side.

                                     So happy am I 
                                    that my heart is
                              "In the palm of your hand"Some old joy.
