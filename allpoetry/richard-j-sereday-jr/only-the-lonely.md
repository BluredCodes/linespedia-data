title: "Only the lonely"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"

Only the lonely 
                                  know how I feel,
                                stood where I stand,
                                 feel what I've felt

                                   Only the lonely
                              wept tears such as mine,
                             erupting from deep within,
                          fiercely, forcefully, violently 

                                  Only the lonely 
                                  know how I feel,
                                    empty of hand
                                   empty of heart

                             Loneliness, lonely, alone,
                               Singularly, solitary, 
                                   in my solitude.
