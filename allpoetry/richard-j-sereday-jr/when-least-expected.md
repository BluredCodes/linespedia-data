title: "When Least Expected"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"

I was looking for my angel,
                                  searching high and low,

                              wondering where I'd find her, 
                                and wondering how I'd know,

                                 till one day I did learn
                              that I just had to let it go, 

                                       because.....

                                    she'll come to me 
                                    in times own time,
                                 now this is plain to see.

                                 When I least expect her,
                              that's when she'll come to me.

                               Yes, when I least expect her,
                               that's when the time will be.

                                 And come to me you did ,
                               from out of the sky so blue.

                              And from the very first moment 
                                when I caught sight of you,

                            I knew that this could be the one,
                             my "dream of an angel" come true.

                             Oh, what wondrous feelings I had,
                                that's when I finally knew,

                              It's when you least expect it,
                          that your dreams just might come true.
                                       ~~~~~~~~~~~~~

                          It truly was when I least expected it.
