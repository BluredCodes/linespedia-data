---
title: "Nothing"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "nothing"

You where mine,
                                     but only in my dreams,

                                        and I was yours,
                                     but only in your mind,

                                    as someone to adored you,
                                  and make you feel desirable,

                                       for that is as far 
                                     as you would let it go,

                                      but I kept hanging on
                                  with dreams of what might be,

                                  someday, somewhere, somehow,
                              if I could only wait until tomorrow,

                            though I knew tomorrow would never come,
                             yet still I kept on dreaming of the day

                               and now that I admit it to myself,
                               I can't even feel a sense of loss 

                                    of something I never had,
                                    but in my wildest dreams,

                                  so let go I did, but of what
                                      I'm not really sure.

                                     When you have nothing,
                                    you have nothing to lose,

                            therefore I guess that nothing was lost, 
                             so why is it that I now feel so hollow?
