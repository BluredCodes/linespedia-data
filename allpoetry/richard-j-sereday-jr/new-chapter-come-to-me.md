title: "New Chapter Come to Me"

New Chapter come to me,
                              for this one, it does end.

                              The words no longer come,
                             the pages, they are blank,

                            Their emptiness fills my soul
                             with nothingness profound.

                             An endless trip to nowhere,
                               no beginning and no end

                            and when I finally got there
                               I learned I was alone.

                         The position of my heart makes it 
                          no more or less that what it is,
                             broken, bleeding, weeping.

                               New Chapter come to me,
                              and bring to me the tears
                               to wash away this pain.

                              Old Chapter born of hope,
                               now filled with the pain
                                  of empty promises
                          please set me free to start anew.
