---
title: "A Lifetime"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"

If a lifetime could be lived out in just one night,
then mine was lived with you.

Deep down in my heart you've reached,
where few have ever been.

And I think for you I have done the same,
my heart it tells me so.

Both our worlds so far apart, 
but hearts in perfect tune.

In a different time, in a different place,
how different things would be.

A precious gift I see in you,
I would cherish for all times.

Our one night has left me dreaming,
of oh, so many more,

holding you gently tight 
and never letting you go.

Oh, but to dream.
