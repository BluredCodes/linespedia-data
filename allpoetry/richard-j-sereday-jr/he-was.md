title: "He was"

He was, 
                                                      a leader among leaders, 
                                                      a leader of the lost, 

                                                      Now lost himself. 
                                                      Lost in his pain. 
                                                      Lost in his own losses. 

                                                      Finding himself, 
                                                      somewhere among the lost, 
                                                      that he once led. 

                                                      Lost, but somehow 
                                                      finding himself, 
                                                      once again. 

                                                      Once more to lead.
