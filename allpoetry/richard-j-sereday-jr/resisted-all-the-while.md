title: "Resisted all the while"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "resisted-all-the-while"

I was so attracted to you 
                              from our very first meeting
                        but you seemed to resist all the while,
                         to ever let me get truly close to you.

                             I wanted too so desperately, 
                                but you always kept me 
                                  an arms length away 
                               from deep into your heart.

                             I could see inside your door,
                             I could see behind the walls,
                              to the place never my feet 
                                 would stand, I feared.

                                 Still, there I stood, 
                                 knocking at your door,
                              and I wondered if it would 
                                 ever be opened to me?

                                 Wondering if it would 
                                finally open at a time,
                                 that I was no longer 
                                 standing and knocking?

                             So all I really know is this: 

                           that life is a mysterious journey,
                               and at least for a time, 
                                I traveled this journey 
                          with you and your smile by my side,
                           and for the sharing, I thank you.
