Sometimes I wonder why I write,
always writing or thinking about it.

I guess I think I have something to say.
Something that needs to be heard,

but so few people seem to care,
I wonder if it's even worth the effort.

I'm I just fooling myself
and does anyone really even care,

or am I just wasting ink and paper,
and my time and effort.

I guess when I think about it
this all started as a form of therapy.

And it still does serve that roll for me,
so I guess that is how I should look at it,

I do this just for me,
and anyone else who cares,

and if no one else cares,
who cares?

Me, that's who.
