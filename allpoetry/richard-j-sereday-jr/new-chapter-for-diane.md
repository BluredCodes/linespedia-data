---
title: "new chapter (for Diane)"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "new-chapter-for-diane"

Your new chapter has arrived 
and awaits the words 
that would finish 
the story of you. 

May it be a happy one, 
ever so gentle in it's creation.
