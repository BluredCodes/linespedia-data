title: "One night stand"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"

Our one night together is something I keep remembering and replaying in my mind's eye with much fondness. I wish that there could have been much more.

Such are the ways of the world ,that one could find one not yet ready to be found.

This I now know, and yet my dreams, they do live.
