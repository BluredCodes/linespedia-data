---
title: "Dreaming of You"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "dreaming-of-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

I had a hard time 
going to sleep last night,

I was dreaming 
of dreaming you.

And I had a harder time 
waking up this morning,

because I was 
in that dream with you.

Holding you in my arms, 
we had not a care,

and I just did not 
want to let go,

to meet mornings light
without you by my side.

You see,
I was looking into your eyes,
and there, myself I did see.

Yes, it was like 
looking in a mirror.

I saw pains from our past 
of lost loves untrue,

and dreams of a future
of me holding you,

deep in my heart
as you touch my soul

the truest of love
the world will ever know.

What a dream,
dreaming of you.
