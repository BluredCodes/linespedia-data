---
title: "By now"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "by-now"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/36732-By-now-by-Richard-J-Sereday-Jr"
---

If you do not know by now,
the secrets of my soul,
you may never know.

If you can not see by now,
the treasures of my heart,
you will never hold them.

If you've not felt my loving touch,
sear your tender flesh,
your floods my never flow.

If you have yet to taste
the sweetness of my kiss,
the world will grieve your loss.

The grandest gifts are these
that you will never know.
