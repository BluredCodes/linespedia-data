Will you practice making a porno movie with me.
We'll be the best actors the world will never see.

No lights, camera, just action is what it will be.
As together we make love, with joy and glee.

From dusk till dawn we'll practice this trade.
We'll make the best love that has ever been made.

So won't you practice this movie with me?
I'll meet you in the garden about a quarter till three.
