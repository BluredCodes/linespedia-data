title: "The better offer"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "the-better-offer"

It seems like I am always being outbid 
                         by the better offers of others. 

                        When I offer all I have to offer, 
                        it seems that someone always has 
                       just a little more appealing offer, 
                               rendering my offer, 
                          seemingly short of the mark. 

                          But do these to whom I offer, 
                                really understand 
                          just what it is that I offer? 

            I offer things that most are not even capable of giving, 
                  but I don't try to wrap my offers in a facade 
               of false glamour and glitter as do so many others. 

                  Some of the most beautifully wrapped packages 
                   contain nothing but disappointment within, 

                  while some of the most modestly wrapped gifts 
                       hold some of the greatest treasures 
                           one could ever hope to own. 

         You will not find me trying to sell the value of my offerings, 
                              as a salesmen would, 

            for they are things that only the one wise enough to see 
                      the value of within, is deserving of. 

    My generous offers will be one day received by one who will not be fooled 
                   by the glamour and glitter of other offers. 

               Only she will be deserving of such a precious gift.
