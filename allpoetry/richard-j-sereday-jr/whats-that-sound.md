title: "What's that sound"

What's that sound, 
                                     what could it be,
                                   can you hear it too?

                                   Why that's my heart,
                                    that's what it is.
                                it's beating just for you.

                                    Hey, wait a minute,
                                    maybe it's just me,
                                    dreaming out loud,
                                 louder and louder still.

                                      Dreaming of me,
                                   and dreaming of you,
                                  and dreaming of we two.

                             So now I know, it's just a dream,
                                 and it's not really true,
                           this dream I'm dreaming of the “we”,
                                the we that's “me and you”.


                         I can not say my dreams always come true,
                             though all have thus far but one,
                                  the one of us, "we two"

                         Still I dream and wait ever so patiently.
