My heart leads me on a path,
                                  directly to your door,

                             to the place I've dreamed about
                                  in every dreamy dream.

                              Now wide awake I find myself,
                                right where I want to be,

                               you in my arms, me in yours,
                                    heaven on earth, 

                                  please, never let go, 
                                       nor will I, 
                                   heaven help us both,

                                  as we hammer and nail
                                   and build our love, 
                                 better every day, I pray
