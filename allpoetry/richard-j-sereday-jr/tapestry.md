Bring her to me,
angel of love
and let her be mine.

As we weave our tapestry 
and hold it to our hearts.

As we paint our masterpiece,
with each loving stroke.

As we build our box
to hold our love,
and lock it with key.


Let her see this light 
shining in the distance
blazing so bright,

it is a beacon,
to guide her way
to me and a love undying,

in this battle between you,
angel of love 
and these demons of loneliness.
