---
title: "But I do"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "but-i-do"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8545-But-I-do-by-Richard-J-Sereday-Jr"

I couldn't sleep at all last night,
                                   like so many others,
                             cause all I could do was miss you,

                           and wish and pray that you would 
                          snap out of this trance you must be in.

                          And I wish I did not love you so much,
                              so I would not miss you so much,
                                           but I do and I do.
             
                           And I wish I did not miss you so much,
                            so my heart would not hurt so much….
                                       but I do and it does.
