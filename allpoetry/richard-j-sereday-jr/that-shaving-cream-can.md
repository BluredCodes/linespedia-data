Hey boy, what you doing?

Well Dad, I was feeling my face,
and my beards kind of ruff,

so I thought I’d borrow your razor
and get ride of this scruff.

Now I’m as smooth as a babies bottom,
take your hand and feel.

Wow, good job son,
I guess now you’re a man,

but please save me some cream,
in that shaving cream can.
