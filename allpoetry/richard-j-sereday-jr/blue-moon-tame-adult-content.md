Blue moon over your shoulder I saw,
as you were standing there, over me.

A halo it did produce 
that glistened in your hair.

Gleaming moonlight off your breasts,
as between your legs I was your guest,

as you lowered down on me,
and I filled you so completely.

In this field where we did lay,
as we loved this blue moon night away.
