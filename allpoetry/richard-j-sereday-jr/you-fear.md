---
title: "You fear"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "you-fear"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/32288-You-fear-by-Richard-J-Sereday-Jr"

You fear that I might look into your eyes 
and steal your heart away, 

but this turnabout would only be fair play, 
as you have already to me, these things done 
and I am yours for the taking.

And in my perfect dream,
it was you that I held in my arms, 
as we danced about the floor, 
and whirl you and twirl you, I did.

But our night, it did end,
and only left me wanting more.

So I am praying for the day,
that I might hold you once more,

and I would hold you ever so tightly,
as to never let you go, only to set you free,

free to come back to me,
someday to stay, I pray.
