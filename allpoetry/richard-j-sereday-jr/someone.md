I need someone. 

                                  Someone that I can talk to. 
                                   Someone that will listen. 

                                 Someone that will talk to me. 
                                   Someone I can listen to. 

                               Someone that will understand me, 
                            being aware of what I have been through. 

                                Someone that I can understand, 
                            being aware of what she has been through. 

                              Someone that I can open my heart to. 
                             Someone that will open her heart to me. 

                                    Someone that can see 
                                 the good in me as I will in her. 

                                 Someone that can appreciate 
                                 the good in me as I will in her. 

                                    Someone that will be 
                                my best friend as I will be hers. 

                                Someone that enjoys the things 
                                I enjoy, us doing them together. 

                                     Someone that wants 
                                to get it right this time, as I do. 

                                   Someone that will work 
                                      with me to build 
                                     a good life together. 

                                     I have been praying 
                                        for my angel 
                                     to come into my life. 

                                     I have been praying 
                                   that I will recognize her 
                                      when she arrives. 

                                 Will I find someone like this?
