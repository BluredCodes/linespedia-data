You were an angel sent to me,
to show me my dreams could come true,

but you could stay only for a time so short,
my saddened heart, this knew.

So I know my dream is out there somewhere, 
in someone just like you.

And I only wonder when she'll come to me,
could heaven, of you, please make two?

So, here I am holding on until that day arrives.
