title: "Never had I known"

never had I known such joy
                             as I knew in the meeting of you,
                                it filled my heart and soul

                            you were all I ever wanted and more

                           never had I dreamt such lofty dreams
                               till my dreams of you and I,
                              they filled my days and nights

                            never had I felt such pain as I did
                                    from your departure
                             when I realized you couldn't stay

                         just as all good things must come to pass
                         I know this pain may pass as well someday
