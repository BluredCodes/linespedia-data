---
title: "Crescent Moon"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "crescent-moon"
source: "AllPoetry"

We broke bread by 
                                  crescent moon's light,

                                  crescent moon on high 
                                      up in the sky,
                                 as was the dream of us, 
                                      in our hearts.

                              We walked and talked and held 
                           and shared from the heart that night.

                             And we danced, as you I whirled 
                               and twirled, and spun round, 
                           your feet barely touching the ground,

                             while a crescent smile we wore, 
                                        you and I,
                                  our hearts filled with 
                              the joy and laughter of living,
                                   and the love of life 
                                  in each one the other 
                                         together.

                                  And our night did end, 
                                 but others there will be,
                           and closer and freer we shall become,
                              each one's heart to the other, 
                                          I pray.
