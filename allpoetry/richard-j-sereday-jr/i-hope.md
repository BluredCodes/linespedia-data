---
title: "I Hope"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"

I hope I don't make you feel uncomfortable,
                      by endlessly gazing upon your beauty,

                     eyes fixed for hours, I view your face,
                    my heart, it pounds wildly out of control.

                    Your heavenly smile sets my heart ablaze.
                   In your eyes, I find myself hopelessly lost,

                   and to view your body, surveying all parts,
                  myself, I could not control, even if I tried.

                                 And the touch, 
                                 from me to you,
                                 from you to I, 
                          the meaning of love fulfilled.

                               Thank you, my love.
