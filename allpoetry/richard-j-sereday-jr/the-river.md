title: "The River"

I am the river,
mighty is my flow
over these banks to you.

Reach to me with open arms,
as my current sweeps you away,

to a better place
of happiness and joy,
love and bliss.

Feel my power as 
I churn and whirl about you.

As my vortex sucks you in,
your moisture mingles with mine.

Gently caressing your body,
my flow brushes past your soul.

Every crevasse, every curve,
I grow to know from first to last
as we meld together again and again,

no beginning and no end
always and forever.
