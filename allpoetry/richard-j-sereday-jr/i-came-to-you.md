title: "I came to you"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "i-came-to-you"

I came to you in a time
                                  that I had a need

                              a need to be held by you,
                               welcomed into your arms

                             a need to be touched by you
                                 deeply soul to soul

                             I held out my heart to you,
                            but you would not take a hold

                            and it dropped to the floor,
                                battered and bruised

                                 I picked it up and 
                                 held it to my chest

                              and cradled it in my arms
                              and washed it in my tears

                              I hung my head in sorrow,
                             and turned and walked away

                              and I can only wonder why
                               in me you could not see
                              your needs become fulfill

                          but your world, it spun too fast
                            and I was only a blur to you
                             that you could not even see

                          and though I was there for a time
                            and I was real, I am now gone

                              and when you wonder where
                               and when you wonder why

                         do not feel alone for you'll know,
                                  where ever I am, 
                               I'll be doing the same
