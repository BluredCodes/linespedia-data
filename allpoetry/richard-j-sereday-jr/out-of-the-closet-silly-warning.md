Yes, it's true what you heard.
I did come out of the closet the other day.
Now the whole world knows.

I am a Lesbian trapped in a man's body, 
and I only like women.

And I have all the play toys
we Lesbians like, built right in,

but one warning,
this thing's loaded.

You know what?,
I'm not even trying to fight this thing,
but I'm just going with the flow.

I'm sorry but I just can't help it,
I can't fight the feelings
and I don't even want to,
to tell you the true.

So I guess this is how it will be
from this day forward.
