at your feet I did lay,
                                 and you did not even see,
                                    but looked over me.

                              stripped of all things worldly,
                                     naked did I lay,
                                  my innocence revealed,

                                 coldly, stilly, silently,
                                  until I did slip away,
                                     back to the place
                                    from whence I came.
