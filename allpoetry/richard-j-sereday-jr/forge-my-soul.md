Her letter almost made me cry, almost,

                            but as I hammer and bend and shape 
                         and forge my soul to be what it will be, 

                             I must save all the tears I can,
                           for I need them to quench the flames
                                  that burns in my heart,
                         so that they will not consume me wholly. 

                                  We've all been there, 
                            tasted the bitter sweet realization
                         that pain and loss are the best teacher, 
                                  but only to the student
                                  which strive to learn.

                               More questions than answers,
                            more confusion than conclusion, 

                                   yet we try, don't we?
