All over the road,
from lane to lane,
a tire jumps off the curb.

The brake lights,
and all of the sudden stops,
it must be that invisible cow head?

70 miles an hour then 35,
keep driving like this 
and you won't be alive.

To all other drivers, 
this guy is a danger.

Worse than those Firestones 
on Ford Exploders or Rangers.

This kid I'm behind,
must be a drunk punk.

His front end will end up
in somebodies trunk.

No wait that's my Uncle,
he's on his way,

to take Grandma home,
at the end of the day.

I really think they need one of those little plastic St. Christopher's to ride on the dash.My Uncle is 70ish years old and is blind in one eye and can't see out of the other, and he can barely walk without falling over, but still he drives. He is a danger to himself and everyone else on the road, and I wonder how the story will end.
