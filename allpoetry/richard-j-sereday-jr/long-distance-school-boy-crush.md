title: "Long distance school boy crush"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "long-distance-school-boy-crush"
source: "AllPoetry"

I know you know I like you,
I think you like me too.

But you are there 
and I am here,
so what are we to do.

Can I blow a kiss that far,
and wink a time or two,

see if my arms can 
reach around the world 
and grab a hold of you.

Our hearts have 
made a connection,
this much is plain to see,

but while you are there
and I am here,
how can this love ever be?

To never hold you 
or taste your kiss,

my heart, it knows,
just what is missed.

To not run my fingers 
through your hair,

to not smell your scent,
filling the air.

To not lay beside you in our bed
and show you how I feel.

I reach out for you 
and grab thin air,

but I can just not touch you,
cause you're not here.

So I'll be holding you in my heart, my love.
