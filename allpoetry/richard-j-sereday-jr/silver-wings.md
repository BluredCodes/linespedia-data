---
title: "Silver Wings"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "silver-wings"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/7339-Silver-Wings-by-Richard-J-Sereday-Jr"

Seems like where ever I go, 
                                      and what ever I do, 
                                   there's always something 
                                   to bring me back to you. 

                                  From above I hear a sound. 
                                    A sound unmistakable. 

                                      The same sound 
                                 that once brought me to you, 
                                    only to take me away, 
                                     in a time all to soon. 

                                   I look up and there it is. 
                                   A marvel of modern man. 
                               A massive bird of silver, and fire, 
                                  taking people of this place, 
                                        a world away. 

                                 Upon silver wings they ride, 
                                 skipping from ground to sky, 
                                       and back again, 
                                    like stones on a pond. 

                                Sometimes I curse those wings, 
                                   for taking me to heaven, 
                                      only to bring me 
                                    back to my emptiness. 

                               You were like a dream come true. 
                                    A dream that I knew, 
                             I would have to wake from all to soon. 

                                There I was walking in heaven, 
                           with an angel, only to be sent back to earth, 
                            and the reality that you were but a dream, 
                             a dream come true, for a time so short, 
                                     for a time so sweet. 

                                      For now I dream. 
                                      I dream of the day 
                                    my dream comes true. 
                                    My dream of sharing 
                                        life with you.
