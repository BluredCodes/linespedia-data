---
title: "Neverlasting Love"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "neverlasting-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

This everlasting road 
                                to neverlasting love,
                               I know it all too well

                           I've traveled it all too long,
                            I've traveled it all too far,
                              to heart ache and sorrow

                              I'm dreaming of it's end,
                            and the day that it will be,
                                but a distant memory

                            Everlasting love, come to me,
                          hold me in your loving embrace, 
                           be mine always, leave me never
