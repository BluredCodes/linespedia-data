Spring is in the air,
                                  the daffodils in bloom,
                                 the promise of life anew.

                                Yes, spring is in the air,
                              our heart's, they are in bloom,
                                 the hopes of love anew. 

                                   Walk with me along, 
                                 this flower laden path, 
                               smell of the rose's beauty, 

                                 if you have the strength,
                                  if you have the heart,
                                   if you hold in mind,
                                   these dreams I dream.

                                    They are my dreams,
                                 yet they are the dreams 
                                      of the masses 
                              in the same of times timeless.
