---
title: "The more"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "the-more"
source: "AllPoetry"

the more I know of you, 
the more I dream of you, 

to taste of your lips,
as I hold you in my arms,

endlessly,
for now and evermore.

Dream come true,
I beg of you.
