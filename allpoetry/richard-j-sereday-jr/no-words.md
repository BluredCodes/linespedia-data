---
title: "No words"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "no-words"
source: "AllPoetry"

There are no words that could desribe
the way you make me feel.
No words could do these feelings justice.

It is times like this I wish I was a poet,
then the words would come so true,
flowing from my heart on a path to you.

I wish there was a way 
to let this world know
the way you make me feel.

Shivers up and down my spine,
heart racing round in my chest,
the butterflies wings flutter
like those of a massive bird.

No matter how fast I run, 
I can not catch my breath,
for you leave me breathless.
