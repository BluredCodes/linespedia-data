If this offends anyone, I apologize. When I wrote this, I wasn't sure if I could/should share it. It's just me trying to figure out if man made God in his own image, or if we even truly know the real God. (our creator)  If viewed with an open mind, at least something of it may make sense. 

                                They oppress the human spirit, 
                                   and in the name of God, 
                                    in the name of good, 
                                 in the name of righteousness. 

                                    They who have taken 
                                     the words of men, 
                                    as the words of god. 

                                  A book of paper and pen. 
                                  The paper and pen of man. 

                               The way according to mortal man, 
                               supposedly from divine inspiration, 
                                    but all too many times, 
                                     simply self-serving. 

                                     Man creating God, 
                                      in his own image. 

                                 Man who has this obsession. 
                                  This obsession with rules, 
                                      and controlling. 

                                  Man, always trying to explain 
                                   things that he cannot explain. 
                                     Rarely accurate now, 
                                      and much less then. 

                                    Each group with the way, 
                                        the only way, 
                                     all others being wrong. 

                                        All so sure of 
                                       their set of rules 
                                      being the only way. 

                                  So quick to judge, these men. 
                                   These makers of the rules, 
                                      one of which was, 
                                         not to judge. 

                                      An eye for an eye. 
                                    or, turn the other cheek. 
                                        So which is it? 

                                     If black is black today, 
                                  then, is it not black tomorrow? 

                                        It seems to be 
                                       whatever serves 
                                       the need at hand. 

                                       God has no need 
                                    for the blood of animals, 
                                     as the ancient offered. 

                                      These gifts of blood 
                                     from the very creatures 
                                       that he created. 

                                      This was man's idea. 
                                        Ancient man, 
                                   and an ancient idea it was. 

                                Had man never created this book, 
                              Who would they turn to for the answers? 
                             Would they have to cut out the middle man? 
                                    Would they have to form 
                                   a true personal relationship? 

                            Would they have to talk to GOD themselves? 
                          Would they have to follow their own heart for once? 

                                  No father wishes his children, 
                                 run around so preoccupied with 
                                 worshipping him, that they cannot 
                               enjoy the gift of life that he has given. 
                                          No father 

                                  He did not give us these lives 
                                   to meet some sort of need 
                                    of his to be worshipped. 

                                  No father would ever give up 
                                   on one of his own children. 
                                         No father. 

                              No father would send one of his children 
                             to an everlasting eternity of flame and pain. 
                                         No father. 


                                    He looks at his children, 
                                    with pride, and a sadness. 
                                 The pride come from those who 
                                enjoying this life he has given to us, 
                                    in the fullest, richest ways. 

                                   His sadness comes in seeing 
                                those who would waste their lives, 
                                   too busy following the rules 
                                   man has imposed upon us, 
                                       and in his name. 

                                       God has given us 
                                    these lives of ours to live. 
                                     So whatever you do, 
                                  do not waste this precious gift.
