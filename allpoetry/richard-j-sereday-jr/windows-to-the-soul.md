title: "Windows to the soul"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"

Our eyes are the windows to our souls. 

                        You can tell so much about a person 
                         by gazing deeply into their eyes. 

                            You can see their sincerity, 
                           their honesty, their kindness, 
                       their compassion for their fellow man, 
                          if that's what's in their heart. 

                         You can see the love and the hate, 
                           the happiness and the sorrow, 
                             the pleasure and the pain, 
                         the contentment and the troubles, 
                              the truth and the lies. 

                        The eyes will always give you away. 

                     Some will avoid eye contact at all costs, 
                      as if they are afraid to let anyone see 
                            what is inside their heart. 

                         I invite anyone to search my soul 
                      through these windows of mine, my eyes, 
                    for I have nothing to hide from this world. 

                  Should you choose to take a glance into my soul 
                            through these eyes of mine, 
                          you would see me as I truly am. 

                       So many pieces of this complex puzzle, 
                      which come together to make me who I am, 
                           this person worthy of loving. 

                            Gaze deeply and you will see 
                              the essence of my soul, 
                     as it comes out of these portholes of mine 
                               to meet you half-way. 

                     Yes, the eyes are the windows to the soul, 
                          so remember that just as you can 
                                  see into my soul 
                             through these eyes of mine 
                            and see what is in my heart, 
                               I to can do the same. 

                         The eyes give you away every time.
