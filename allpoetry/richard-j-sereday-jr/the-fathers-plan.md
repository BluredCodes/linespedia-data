---
title: "The Fathers Plan"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "the-fathers-plan"

God worked so hard, planned and plotted,
                           pointed and directed a path for me.

                               A path that lead me to you 
                                on that fate filled night.

                                As he planned and plotted 
                              and lead you on a path to me.

                                      And our paths 
                                        did cross
                                     then and there.

                                  And he filled my eyes 
                                     with a twinkle 
                                    so you would see 
                                   that you were where 
                                  you were meant to be.

                                 And that same twinkle, 
                                   in your eyes he put,
                                    so there would be 
                                  no mistake in my heart

                                 that my endless journey 
                                  had reached it's end,
                                  my angel being found.

                                    And he looks down 
                                 from his throne on high,
                                    down upon us two 
                               with a twinkle in his eyes.

                             The twinkle of a job well done,
                             one of his most precious gifts,
                                 and such a simple plan,
                             that of a man and woman united.

                                    And now the reins 
                                    are in our hands,

                                   And it is up to us, 
                                 to do with this uniting 
                                      what we might.

                                    And we will live, 
                                    and we will love,

                              and we will be to one another,
                                 all things we should be,

                                and we will work together
                                so hard to make the most 
                             of this gift that he has given, 

                             and hand in hand, heart to heart
                         and we will walk together for all time,
                                     be it my choice.
