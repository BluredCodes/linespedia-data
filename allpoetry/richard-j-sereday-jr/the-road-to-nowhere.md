The road to nowhere 
                                   led me to you,
                                   and back again
                                 to where I now am.

                               No stops along the way,
                                 but to wonder why.

                           Nothing to show for the jouney 
                          but wasted mile and wasted time, 
                           and a heart full of emptiness.

                              So what was the purpose 
                                of this little detour
                                    along the way
                               to where I do not know,

                          and the sadest part about it is 
                               that I may never know,

                       though this I will question forevermore.
