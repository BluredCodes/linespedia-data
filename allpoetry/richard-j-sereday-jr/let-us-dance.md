---
title: "Let us dance"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "let-us-dance"

My heart, it beats for you, 
                           this I hope you know.

                             And missing you, 
                           this I've been doing 
                         since last we did meet.  

                         How can I let you know, 
                         how can I make you see, 
                        what your love means to me?

                           Maybe with my words, 
                               or my touch, 
                         or the look in my eyes, 
                               this I hope. 

                            See into my heart, 
                        the treasures that I hold, 
                          waiting there for you, 
                              and you alone.

                               Let us dance 
                            in the moon light, 
                           till dawn does come,

                       and a new day we will share 
                              hand in hand, 
                              heart to heart.
