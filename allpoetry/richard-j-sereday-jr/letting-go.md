title: "Letting Go"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"

So off she goes, once again
                         like she's done so many times before.

                               She said we are through, 
                                don't have what it takes
                             She said it's better this way,

                               So in the blink of an eye,
                                she took her love away.

                             But my heart was all too soft,
                                    even when I knew
                               it was time to walk away,
                                 I just couldn't do it.

                         So I held our love close to my heart,
                             and I tried with all my might 
                              to breath life back into it.

                                And even as I held our 
                               cold dead love in my arms,
                              I just couldn't bring myself
                                     to let it go.

                              But let our love go I must,
                                for I have not a choice.

                                She wins and it is over,
                                    and it is done, 
                                   as it is her will,
                                  and I am letting go
                             for I know not how to hold on.

                             Just one more tragic tragedy,
                               my heart has suffer in my
                            quest for the one that will work
                               with me to make love work.
