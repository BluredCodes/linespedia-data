---
title: "Just begun"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "just-begun"

This story of us has just begun,
the tablet nearly blank,
but for a few words.

Together we will work
to write this story,
if it was meant to be.

Time will tell the tale,
as the words, they do come,
and fill these pages one by one.

And will the ending be
a happy one for you and me,
You deserve it, don’t you?

I know that I do,
that much is truly true,
so now it’s up to you

and me.
