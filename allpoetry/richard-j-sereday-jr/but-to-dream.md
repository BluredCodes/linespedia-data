---
title: "But to dream"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "but-to-dream"

But to dream,
for our piece of this world, 
our share of the happiness,
                                              
and the love we deserve. 

When will it be, 
when will they see,
our value inside?

this we do ask, one and all.
