---
title: "Once I read"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"

I once read that it is in man's nature
                        to love, then destroy that which he loved,
                             only to love it again once lost,

                             and I can only guess that is why 
                             she could not love me once more,

                                  for she was unable to 
                                 destroy my love for her,

                             but one day this love will die, 
                                and she will have suceeded,
                               in her intended distruction.

                                      Then will she 
                                    love me once again,
                                   if only in her heart?

                                  Oh, the nature of man,
                                   and love, and heart,
                                  and the why of it all.

                                      To understand, 
                                       if only......
                                      to understand.
