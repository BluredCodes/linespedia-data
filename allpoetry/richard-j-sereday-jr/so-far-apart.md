---
title: "So Far Apart"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "so-far-apart"

I wish you loved me as much as I love you,

and I wish you missed me as much as I do you,

because if you did, and if you did, we would not be 

So                               Far                             Apart.
