---
title: "Best Love Yet"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "best-love-yet"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/38613-Best-Love-Yet-by-Richard-J-Sereday-Jr"

What if the best love 
we have ever experienced 
is yet to come?

What if the best of days
were but moments away,
though we could not even see?

I'll bring to you 
these gifts in my hand,
won't you please 
take them from me?

Imagine if you will,
all the beauty of the world
wrapped into a ball,
the one that I'd toss your way,

and that's just the beginning,
for I have so much more,
that would entice you 
and then make you stay.

Unity of commitments unbroken,
sincerely steadfast in conviction, 
unshakable in this belief 
of our love forever undying,
of these things I do speak,
can I please provide them to thee?

Frollic with me 
through this game we call life,
as we learn to play it so well,
and in the next million eaons it is,
that time, of our love, it will tell.
