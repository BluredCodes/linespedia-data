Standing in a store 
at the checkout line, 
I just happened to see these boxes 
of chocolate covered cherries.  

It made me think of, 
how much I was like 
these boxes of candy.  

The chocolate, 
so much like 
the sweet part of me. 

The creamy filling, 
so much like the tears 
I have cried over
the loss of your love.  

The cherries, 
so much like my heart, 
floating somewhere 
in all those tears.  

And the box, 
so much like 

the me that can 
just be thrown away 
when you've finished with it. 
~~~~~
Oh, how much it hurts 
to be without you in my life.
