the seed was planted,
                                     but did not grow,

                                    within our hearts,
                                     within our souls

                                 for there was not provide
                                 the things that must be,

                                 the time and the sharing,
                                    between you and me

                                the sunshine, the rainfall,
                                   the weather and wind,

                                 the soil which upon grows
                                 the friendship of friends

                                   the time and sharing,
                                   no start and no end,

                                    to not even start,
                                    is no way to begin

                                     with opened arms
                                    my hand I did reach

                                      outward to you,
                                     over this breach

                                but I've learned over time
                                   this much I do know, 

                                   you can't take hold,
                                    without letting go

                               of your fortress of solitude
                             on which you've grown to depend.
