---
title: "Wide wings"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "wide-wings"

My wide wings,
there power is
just so plain to see,

as I swoop down from above
and grab a hold of thee,

and lift you up beyond the cloud,
heaven bound we'll be.

You've finally found your angel,
the one you've found in me.
