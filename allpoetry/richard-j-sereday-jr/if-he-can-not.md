---
title: "If he can not"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "if-he-can-not"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/23549-If-he-can-not-by-Richard-J-Sereday-Jr"

If he can not love you as he once did,
I can.

If he does not hold you like he should,
I can.

If he nolonger values you as the treasure that you are,
I can.

I can, I will, I do,

steadfast, unshakable, anchored in stone,
till time is no more.

If............
