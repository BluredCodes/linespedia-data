---
title: "Anatomy of a Love"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "anatomy-of-a-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/36705-Anatomy-of-a-Love-by-Richard-J-Sereday-Jr"
---

One night in my loneliest hour you came to me,
and you made me feel once again.

It's hard to explain the ways you made me feel,
but I think you felt those same things too,
for I saw it in your eyes.

Even now, as I think about it, 
the words that could explain elude me,
and they have a hard time coming.

You have all the quaities I'm searching for,
and in them I see you.

You made me feel all the things I once felt,
in a time and place so for away
and I know just what I wanted to do,
to hold you, to kiss you, to make sweet love.

I hope you know I am but a man,
with all my imperfections, 
and could you live with them,
once you came to that realization.

So many in this world 
expecting perfection 
from their mate,

although they know that there is
no such thing as perfection 
in this world.

What if I tried harder than most,
 to be all to you that I could, 
would it be enough,

enough to make you stay, 
enough to make you 
want to be with me always,
now and forever.

Be with me one half
of the two it take to be
together as one,
in this anatomy of love.
