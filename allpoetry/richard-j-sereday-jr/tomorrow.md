Let us walk awhile,
                                                hand in hand, 
                                                  you and I.

                                              Let us talk awhile,
                                                heart to heart.

                                           Lets talk about tomorrow,
                                            plan for what might be,

                                            work to build this love
                                           the best that it can be.

                                           And when our end is near,
                                         we will know without a doubt,

                                        that our's was the kind of love
                                           that people dream about.
