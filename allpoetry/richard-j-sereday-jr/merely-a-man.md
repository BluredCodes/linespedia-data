title: "merely a man"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "merely-a-man"

Since you took your love away,
I can only wonder why.

Have you simply forgotten my,
this all just makes me want to cry.

Have I turned invisible,
for when I pass by a mirror,
it's still me that I see.

I wonder what I've done,
and why you won't acknowledge me.

It's true that I am merely a man,
but I have feelings too.

And what I'm feeling right at this time,
is that I'm missing you.
