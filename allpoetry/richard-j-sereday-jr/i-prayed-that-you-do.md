title: "I prayed that you do"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"

As I laid down for the night,
I wished it was you beside me,
instead of my pillows,

and

I daydreamed that you were,
as we said  our good nights,
and I said to you "I love you"

and 

I said it so true, that there could be 
no doubt in your mind 
that my words were true,

and

as I laid there dreaming of you,
I wondered if you do the same,
and I prayed that you do.
