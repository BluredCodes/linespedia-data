title: "In a Perfect World"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"

Oh, if only this was a perfect world. 
                                  A world without mistakes. 

                                  A world where everything 
                                      always went right. 

                                   A world where we always 
                                 said and did the right things. 

                                   A world where a promise 
                                       was a promise. 

                                     A world where trust 
                                      was trust worthy. 

                                   A world where we could 
                                      count on the ones 
                                      we can count on. 

                               I was one that could be counted on, 
                                   one that was counted on, 
                                   one that didn't always do 
                                 what I was counted on to do, 
                               much like those that I counted on. 

                                How could I have let down those 
                                     who counted on me? 

                                 How could those I counted on 
                                     have let me down? 

                                    How could I have said 
                                  and done the wrong things 
                                to the ones that counted on me 
                                    to do the right things? 

                                     How could the ones 
                                        I counted on 
                                    to do the right things 
                                 say and do the wrong things? 

                                     Why does life take 
                                   such unexpected turns? 

                                   When we can't count on 
                                the ones we can count on most, 
                                    who can we count on? 

                                   Well, just maybe "God" 
                                       and maybe self, 
                                      but so many times 
                                    we let ourselves down. 

                                   If we would take the time 
                                     to look at ourselves, 
                                   and become the person 
                                     we could count on, 
                                    then we could become 
                               the person the ones that count on 
                                      us could count on. 

                               Yes, for this is not a perfect world, 
                             and human beings are far from perfect. 

                                     If we could try to do 
                                     the best we can do, 
                                     that's good enough 
                                  for our "Heavenly Father". 

                                      In a perfect world 
                                    everyone could forgive 
                                        just like him, 
                                    but in a perfect world 
                               there would be nothing to forgive.
