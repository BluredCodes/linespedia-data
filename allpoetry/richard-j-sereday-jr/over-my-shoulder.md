title: "Over My Shoulder"

Over my shoulder, 
                                      there were you,
                                  an angel from heaven, 
                                 my dreams had come true.

                                   I felt your presence 
                                   as plain as could be,
                                    from right behind 
                                     watching over me,

                                     As we did ride, 
                                   the wind in our hair,
                                  with you right behind 
                                     I had not a care.

                                   As we shared the ride
                                   this much I must say,
                                    I felt a connection
                                   in some magical way,

                                   And me, I did wonder,
                                     I pondered away,
                                  did you feel the same,
                                   this much I do pray.
