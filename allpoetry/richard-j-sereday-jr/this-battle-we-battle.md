Battle with me 
                                  this battle I battle,
                                       as for you,
                                      the same I do.

                               This battle with past fears,
                                 the wrongs we've faced,

                                      hurtful words,
                                    that cut so deep, 

                              the painful betraying tongues,
                             the emptiness of abandon hearts.

                                    You are not they,
                                  this I know, nor am I.

                                     Courage we need 
                                    and will we find,
                                    in one the other, 

                                   as we keep our eyes
                                 on our prize, you and I.

                                     Help me help me,
                                    help me help you,
                                     help me help us.

                                     I open my ears,
                                   my heart, my soul, 
                                     to what you say.


                                   Show me the way so 
                                   never shall I stray,

                               from the path that leads us
                                       to our love.
