title: "Mistakes, the lessons learned"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"

Learning, the best that can be 
                              hoped for from our mistakes. 

                                Should we be fortunate, 
                                the cost for our mistake 
                                 would be, not too great. 

                              Should fortune frown upon us, 
                                 oh, what price we pay. 

                           All the treasures of our lives at stake. 
                                How much must we pay. 

                                 Any of our treasures, 
                               things of such great value. 
                                  None go unmissed. 

                                    Our mistakes 
                                  demanding payment. 

                                  How great the cost. 

                                Are we prepared to pay 
                                    such lofty fees? 

                                 No, and yet mistakes 
                                 spew forth unchecked. 

                                     How can we 
                                  stop this madness? 

                                   Paying the price, 
                                  learning the lessons, 
                                  avoiding the repeat 
                                of these costly mistakes. 

                                   Taking the time. 
                                 Looking at our-selves. 

                                   Seeing our-selves 
                                as we are seen by others. 

                                   What do we see? 

                                Room for improvement? 
                                   Areas of our lives 
                                   that require some 
                                   special attention. 

                                   How many live life 
                                  looking only ahead, 
                                looking only to the needs 
                                    of them-selves? 

                                 What about the needs 
                                   of the ones loved. 

                              Can their needs go unfulfilled? 

                              No, these are costly mistakes, 
                                  best gone unmade. 

                               Many times our happiness 
                                    is dependent on 
                                   the happiness of 
                                   the ones we love. 

                                Should they be unhappy, 
                              so will we soon find our-selves 
                                 in this unhappy state. 

                               We will all make mistakes 
                                 as we live these lives 
                                  that we were given, 
                                but we will not all learn. 

                                What a sad thing this is. 

                                    As we live life 
                                   and see the price 
                                   that we must pay 
                                   for our mistakes 
                                    it is hoped that 
                                     we can learn. 

                                 It is hoped that we can 
                                   avoid the repeat 
                                    of the mistakes 
                                  that we have made. 

                                 It is hoped that we can 
                                    look ahead and 
                                  avoid many mistakes 
                                before they are realized. 

                              This is one important lesson 
                                  that I have learned 
                                 at great personal cost.
