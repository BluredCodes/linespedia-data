I would have to climb that
mountain so high, 

to get to you.

I would have to swim that
ocean so blue,

and walk through fire,
hot as can be

so..........

I guess I'll have to go to the hardware store and buy
1.) a climbing rope
2.) some water wings
3.) an asbestos suitSmetimes things just seem so out of reach, what to do?
