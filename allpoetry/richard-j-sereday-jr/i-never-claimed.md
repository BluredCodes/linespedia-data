---
title: "I never claimed"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "i-never-claimed"

I never claimed to be perfect, 
                                            and 
                                   I never thought you were, 

                                  but for a time I did believe, 

                                   that I was perfect for you, 
                                            and, 
                                    you were perfect for me. 

                                     It's not the first time 
                                       that I was wrong, 

                                         and I know 
                                          it won't be 
                                          the last, 

                                        hey, after all, 

                                  I never claimed to be perfect.
