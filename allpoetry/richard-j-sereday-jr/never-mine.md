---
title: "never mine"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"

You were never mine
so why do I miss you so?

I never had a hold of you
so why can't I just let go?

My dreams, they were so strong,
as was my desire you see.

Why does my heart 
want something so bad
that I know will never be?

And I try to tell my heart
it wasn't meant to be,

You don't feel the same as I for you,
that's all too plain to see

so why can't my heart believe it,
it holds on till the end.

Not only did it cost me 
my dreams of you,
but your friendship as my friend.
