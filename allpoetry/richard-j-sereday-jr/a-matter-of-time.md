title: "A Matter of Time"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"

She was the leaving kind. 

                                     She proved this to me 
                                     time and time again, 
                                     till there was no more 
                                         time to give. 

                                    I would have given her 
                                     anything I had to give. 

                                     Anything but my trust, 
                                    for this she had to earn, 
                                    and she was earning it, 
                                      one step at a time. 

                                      One steps forwards 
                                          she took, 
                                        then two back, 
                                       again and again, 
                                        losing ground 
                                         all the while. 

                                     Each time she left me, 
                                       she proved to me 
                                     that she was indeed, 
                                       the leaving kind. 

                                      I do not think that 
                                       she really wanted 
                                      to leave and leave 
                                       and leave again. 

                                    She just couldn't help it. 
                                          after all.... 
                                   she was the leaving kind. 
                                     That's what they do. 

                                        Can a leopard 
                                      change it's spots? 
                                         I think not. 

                                        Yes, she was 
                                       the leaving kind. 

                                      Oh, how I wish this 
                                       undeniable fact 
                                       could be denied. 

                                   Once again she leaves me, 
                                    as I knew she would. 

                                  It was just a matter of time.
