Ocean in my way,
will there come a day,
when your waters 
will part for me,
and I wind up 
where I want to be

and if does come that day,
then I will never stray,
from that path to with you be,
forever, you shall see.
 
I think of you both night and day,
as I contemplate to find some way,

that I could find my way to you,
it is something that I want to do.

And if a love could part this sea,
then you could find your way to me,

and we would both together be,
something for this world to see.

A love truer, there will never be,
from now until eternity.
