title: "tender of heart"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"

I am but a man,
tender of heart,
dreaming of love
everlasting.

And you come to me
and we touch,
as you hold me
and I hold you.

You stir in my heart
emotion not known
for a time so long.

I now must wonder and question
the sincerity of your desire.

The part of me
that has no fear,
has a desire
to hold you near,

as my tender part
that knows of pain
tries to see what is to gain. 

So take the time
to know me well,
and where we go,
that time will tell.
