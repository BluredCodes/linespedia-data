title: "Steal my kiss"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"

Steal my kiss, 
steal my love 
and hold me
in your arms.

Fight this feeling not,
as you never did before,
as kisses I steal from you
and time again.

I'll steal your kiss,
I'll steal your love,
and I'll hold you
forevermore in my arms.

Heavenly lips
press heavenly lips
as passion know 
of it's own fury.

Yearning for the love
that would so wonderfully be
the thing that you and I
are dreaming could soon be,

or is this dream mine alone,
and would you not have for me
a desire in kind as mine 
in just this same way.

'Tis something that I pray .
