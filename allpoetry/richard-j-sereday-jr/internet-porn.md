Nude pictures by the score,
                           internet porn to say for sure,

                         She said she had them taken for me,
                           but not one picture did I see.

                      So shocking, so revealing, it was to me,
                     this porngraphic encounter time and again,
                     and she left so little of her body unseen.

                          And I had to ask myself that day,
                       could a girl I'd love behave this way,

                            It made me want to run away,
                        but my love for her, it made me stay.

                   So which father did these acts please the most,
                                 I will never know, 
                        but she will face them both one day,
                               then at least she will.
