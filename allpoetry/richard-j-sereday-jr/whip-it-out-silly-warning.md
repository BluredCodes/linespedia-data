Whip it out,
poke it in,

faster,
deeper,
harder,
longer,

push it in,
pull it out,
again and again.

Oh, yeah baby,
give it up,

give big daddy
what he wants.

Oh yeah, that's it,
you sure know what I want.

Oh, I've wanted this
so bad for so long.

Oh, here it comes,
look at it, what a wad.

Time to put it back 
in my pants,

til I once again 
whip it out.Thank God for ATMs.
