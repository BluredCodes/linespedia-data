---
title: "Fill my eyes"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "fill-my-eyes"

Tears that fill my eyes,
from there be gone,
so that I might
once again see my way,

on this path of uncertainty
which I travel,
to where I do not know.
