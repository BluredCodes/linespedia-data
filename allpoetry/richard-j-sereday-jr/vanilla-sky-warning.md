Warning: Before you see the movie, 
                Vanilla Sky, be Warned.

If you have ever had dreams of having the perfect love,
only to be lost.

If you have ever had endless nightmares where you had 
and then lost it all.

If your heart has ever been ripped 
into a million tiny little pieces,

then repaired but never quite the same,
disfigured and distorted beyond recognition.

Then you may leave the movie theater
with tear filled eyes, making it hard to drive.

You may feel like your soul 
was ripped from you body,

thrown to the ground,
stomped to a bloody pulp,
kicked around the block,
and left somewhere,
laying in a filthy gutter,

then picked up and thrown back 
into your body, pointed toward the door,
and kicked in the butt
to send you on your way.

Or maybe it was just me??
I don't know.

But don't say I didn't warn you.This movie to me was so haunting and taunting. 
You have never worn my shoes so maybe the effect on you would not be the same.

When I saw his face I saw my heart, my soul, and my life therein.
