it's raining cliches and dogs.

why you, I oughta punch you in the cliche.

I wish I had a cliche for every time I heard that one.

the cliche is in the mail.

when it rains, it cliches.

why don't you take a cliche, it'll last longer.

why don't you kiss my cliche.

is that a cliche in your pants, or are you just glad to see me?

tie a yellow ribbon around the old cliche.

he was flying like a cliche out of hell.

the cliche made me do it.

man, look at the set of cliches on that bimbo.

a cliche for your thoughts.

are you working hard or hardly cliching?

we have nothing to fear but cliche itself.

if you can't take the cliches, get out of the kitchen.

and on and on.....

Just plain silly, having a little fun.
Need I say more???
