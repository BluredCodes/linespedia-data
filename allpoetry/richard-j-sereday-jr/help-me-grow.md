---
title: "Help me grow"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"
slug: "help-me-grow"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/36663-Help-me-grow-by-Richard-J-Sereday-Jr"

You help me and I'll help you,
as together, we do grow,
closer, you and I.

No losers, but only winners
will we be,

not a race, 
but merely on a destination,

to what we might become, 
you and I .

as closer we do grow
and our love does blossom,
bloom and grow.
