Why, just the other day a friends asked me
who my favorite singer is,

and I told her "Me...."

Then she asked what my favorite song is
and I said, of course, "me me me me meeeeee...."

then she asked me what my favorite painting was
and I said, "a mirror with the refection of me..."

Then she said 
"so, you're a little egocentric, aren't you"?

and I said "No, I am a modest person, 
and my modesty is exceeded only by my greatness."

It ain't easy being me.....I was working on the weekend. (slow day) and one of my workmates ask me what my favorite movie of all times was and she got me started.  I then had a goofy attack and this little piece is what happened.

That'll teach her:)
