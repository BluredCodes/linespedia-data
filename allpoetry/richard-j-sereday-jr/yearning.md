Oh, this powerful yearning,
I know it all to well.

Without you here with me,
my life, it has been hell.

To hold you in my arms,
the dreams of us come true.

There is an empty hollow feeling,
that makes my heart so blue.

And if I could only hold you,
I know it would go away.

So if my dreams come true,
with you is where I'll stay. 

Yearning, burning, churning,
endlessly, eternally, everlastingly,
in a time limitless.
