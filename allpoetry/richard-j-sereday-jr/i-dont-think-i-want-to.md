Well, I don't think I want to
                                                 but I will,

                                          and I don't think I can 
                                              although I must

                                         for I know that I need to
                                       cause I haven't got a choice 

                                   but to watch you walkin out of my life

                                            So good bye my love,
                                       you know that you'll be missed

                                 this is one day I prayed would never come

                                         but it has and here we are
                                         we'll go our separate ways

                                and leave our future's dreams so far behind
