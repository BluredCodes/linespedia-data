title: "And then, there was you"

I had finally found my place, 
                                     by your side. 

                            Things were starting to make sense 
                                   in this crazy world. 

                               I had been roaming around 
                                     somehow lost 
                                  until there was you. 

                               I feel like I was but a seed, 
                                  when you found me, 
                                that would one day yield 
                                    a beautiful rose. 

                                 And you picked me up 
                                    and planted me. 

                                 And you were the soil, 
                                    proving me with 
                               all the necessary nutrients 
                                   to blossom forth. 

                               And you were the rain drops 
                                    from up above, 
                                that quenched my thirst. 

                                   And you were the 
                                  life giving sunshine
                                    and it was good. 

                                   And I was growing 
                                  stronger every day. 

                                And my buds had formed 
                            and the day would soon be at hand 
                              that the petals would open and 
                             revile their indescribable beauty. 

                                  We walked together 
                                     hand in hand. 

                                   And I felt so whole 
                                    with you there 
                                      by my side, 
                                      in my life, 
                                    and in my heart. 

                                     ~~~~~~~~~~ 

                                    But in an instant 
                                    you were gone. 

                                 And the thought came 
                                 crashing threw my head 
                                that I would never again 
                                 look over and see you 
                                      by my side. 

                                   And with you went 
                                     the sunshine, 
                                   and the nutrients 
                                    and rain drops 
                              that I so desperately needed. 

                                   And then I woke, 
                                    heart pounding, 
                                     breath racing. 

                                   And in an instant, 
                                     I was aware 
                                    that this was but 
                                   a horrible dream. 

                                        ~~~~~
