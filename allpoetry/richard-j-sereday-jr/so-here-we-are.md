So here we are 
                                    you and I,
                               standing toe to toe,
                                   eye to eye,
                                 heart to heart.

                             Making one another shine
                              brighter than the sun.

                                 You lifting me,
                                 me lifting you.

                                Ducks in a puddle,
                                 happy as a lark.

                              Dancing on cloud nine
                            for all the world to see,

                            Making them all wannabes,
                               wanna be us is what,

                              yeah right, they wish.
                            but fate can only sponsor 
                                one pair so grand,
                                  us, you and I.
