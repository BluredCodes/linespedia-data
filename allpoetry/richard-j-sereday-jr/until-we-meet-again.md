title: "Until we meet again"

So long it had been, 
                                  since last we met. 

                              All the miles between us, 
                             and not a thing had changed. 

                                 They didn't change 
                              the way she looked at me. 

                                 They didn't change 
                                the way she held me. 

                                 They didn't change 
                              the way she made me feel, 

                                It was all coming back. 

                             As we held and as we danced, 
                                   she and I knew 
                                it had been too long. 

                             And then we said good night, 
                            and how many mile might it be, 
                                until me meet again?
