Sometimes in our hurry to try to get what we think 
                we need, we forget to take some very profound aspects 
                           of our endeavor into account : 

                              if you know what I mean. 
                            
So here goes......... Lava
                                   ~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
                                 I needed some water, 
                                    so I dug a well. 

                                   I got carried away, 
                                 and dug straight to hell. 

                                    Water I wanted, 
                                     but lava I got. 

                                  What's up with that? 
                                     ~~~~~~~~~~~~~

                 The complexities of living all to often are overlooked, 
                       and the results of this can be devastating.
