Heaven must have been 
                                  having a very good day,
                               that day that you were born.

                              The angels must have gotten in 
                              a shipment of extra big smiles,
                                      just for you, 
                                  for yours is the best.

                                  And I think they added
                                 a double dose of twinkle
                              stolen straight from the stars
                                    just for your eyes.

                             All things of such great beauty, 
                             what wondrous gifts to the world.

                                  But what I like best is
                                        your side,

                               the place I'd like to be by.
