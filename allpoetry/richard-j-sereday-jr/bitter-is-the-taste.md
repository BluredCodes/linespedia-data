title: "Bitter is the taste"
writer: "Richard J Sereday Jr"

I've been on both ends of this candle, 
both losing ends. 

To have someone who wants one,
but belong to another, 

and I was the wanted one, 
but the one that never got picked. 

Hard, hard medicine to swallow
and bitter is the taste.

I find myself there again.
