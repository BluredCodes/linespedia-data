title: "Evil One"
writer: "DesireeLynne"

Where`s my conscience
In this city of sin
Seems I always lose
But I was born to win 
I`m trying to fight these feelings inside
Nowhere to escape
Nowhere to hide
Scared of this evil I cannot see
Why is he always after me?
No turning back
Looks like a full moon
Over the edge now
Give me the spoon
Steel strikes the vein
It`s time to begin
The rush
The cough
Oh God 
He`s here
My darling
Don`t worry or cry
For now the evil one am I
