Although my life has been 
One long tragedy
I manage to go on
Awaiting my destiny
Everyone has a purpose
Or so I`ve been told
Patiently I seek the answers
Instead of the rich mans gold
I grew up in a nightmare
Then I grew up on my own
I taught myself self preservation
For all the days I`d spend alone
But I will never regret 
One single day of my life
Because with my suffering
I was taught to fight
I can only hope 
My life will not be lived in vain
And that all I`ve learned
Can help to ease someone elses painearly 90`s,
