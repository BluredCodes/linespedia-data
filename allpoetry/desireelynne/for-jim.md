Just once in every lifetime a special someone comes alongWe were so perfect togetherour love was forever and strongThen suddenly you weren`t theremy shattered heart cries for youThe nights will never again be the samenor a love ever again so trueI long to kiss your warm lipsand to hold you close in my armsI ache to hear you whisper I Love Youas we make love beneath the starsBut this pain I feel tells meI`ll never live those moments againWithout remembering the cold and emptylonlinessof losing you in the endJames Mitchell JohnstonOct.28,56-June 20,1996
