Again today I was guided through
Uncertain waters
Just as a warm sense of peace
Enveloped my soul
Then I felt the strength that 
You passed on to me
And fearlessly I did the work
I was put here to do
I will be forever gratefull
To the evil that took you from me
Because unknowingly
It created
A divine guide
To walk with me
Even hate has its weaknesses
When irony shows its face
Because nothing in this world
Could of stopped what was chosen
