title: "Silence Unbroken"

Through my windowpane,the morning sun slips in Delicately and quietly urging,my peaceful sleep to end I`ve often wished your rays were magic,and could tell me all they know I`d ask the many questions,whose answers i`ve longed for so Like how do you forgive someone,you wish you could forget For years i`ve searched for this answer,I`d be forever in your debt Ugly reminders of my pain,haunt my memories How do I erase the hurt,your answer would set me free From where you keep watch,over every living thing Surely you know the answers,yet silence is all you bring
