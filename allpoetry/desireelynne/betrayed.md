---
title: "Betrayed"
writer: "DesireeLynne"

My eyes though they are youngbetray meShowing an age untrue to my yearsLifes triumphs and tragedies erodingA beaten path for the many tearsMy heart though it stillbeats stronglyStill aches with a longing painFor something lost inlifes shuffle,now,vague memories all that remainMy spirit though badly bruisedStill carries me toa newfound dayProtecting me from this emptinessThat threatens to drive me insane
