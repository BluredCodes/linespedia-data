---
title: "Bitch"
writer: "DesireeLynne"

I watch you drain him every month
Not just of his money
But of his pride
You would take his dignity too
If he had any left
What does he see in you?
You are so ugly inside
And that makes you repulsive outside too
He only gets a small check every month
But like clockwork,
here you are
Sitting on his lap
Trying hard to keep your mask on
Knowing he`ll follow you like a puppy
To his demise
Then you`ll get off of his lap
With your evil sneer and words
Words that make him believe
That again
He`s done something to make you leave
I hear him cry some nights
Behind his closed door
I am starting to hate you
For what he`s become
Tonight,as you leave
I watch you drive away
With your bumpersticker
Proclaiming "I am the Bitch"
And I laugh to myself
Soon you`ll get yours
You are not pretty enough to be a Bitch
But I am
