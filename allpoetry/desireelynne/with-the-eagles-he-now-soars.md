You were taken so suddenly from my lifebut you left me many warm memoriesAnd just knowing you are safe in heavenputs my saddened heart at easeYou taught me so many thingsas we walked together through lifeAnd it hurts me deeply to knowthat tomorrowyou won`t be by my sideI know that we shall meet againin a place where there are no tearsAnd I know that you`ll watch over methroughout the rest of my yearsI will think of you each dayand of the happiness you gave meAnd everytime I see an Eagle soarI`ll smile,knowing you`re finally freeFor James Mitchell Johnstonoct.28,1958-june 20,1996
