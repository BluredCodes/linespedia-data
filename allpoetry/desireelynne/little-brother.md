I remember when we were youngstersThough many years have passed since thenNot only were we brother and sisterWe were the best of friendsWe used to get into so much troubleWith all the mischievous things we`d doLike that time I broke all those bottlesAnd I tried to blame it all on youWe had many good timesEven though we grew up poorBecause we had love for each otherI never wanted anything moreNow, the miles separate usAnd we`ve become distant tooBut throughout the years my brotherOne thing remains, and thats my love for youFebuary 201997,
