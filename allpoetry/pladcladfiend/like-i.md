found my heart 
stuck in the wall
throat burns like you

i saw that______
like something
something i never had
thought you knew me[never will]
broken spine
still you whine
kill that want
no more.... why
it was like something i never had

traped in a hole
dont want that
just like i
just like you
no fucking time

let me go
uneeded face
stupid lie
just like i     dont
you and i could have been perfect..why not?
if i was like you


hate in my lungs
tore out your mind
forgive me]
i hate my blind hands
just like i
