(i ask myself)   "who am i?"
but all i hear is silence
a sound that consumes me and takes over my life
SILENCE...i cant stop it, its too late
           its already started
never again will i hear the sweet sound
of you voice caresing my ear as your hands 
work up and down all over my body.....
never will i know the sound of your words 
on the other end of the phone...THIS SILENCE
IS TO LOUD.!!!   i cant hear you .... where are you?????
not now ..not so soon ...we had so many things to do.. i had so many things to say to you... we had to many things left unanswered ...we had to many years left to live to grow old to live life together......come back.......i love you
