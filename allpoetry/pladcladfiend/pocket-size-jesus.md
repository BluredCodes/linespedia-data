I'll taste your inner passion
spit at your disease.
faith forever with her, with jesus in her pocket.
a love so small so rarley seen so rarely wanted
and her heart burns in her own hands.

     her eyes swell and fill with anger
     her faith is forever with her, with jesus in her pocket
your rose will wilt as your soul gets broken just ask for redemption it'll be okay.........The cross has been lost but now becomes right. she's still
got jesus in her pocket she'll sleep tight. i only had to ask once now here is your salvation. HER FAITH IS FOREVER WITH HER SHE'S GOT JESUS IN HER POCKET.
