who are you my friend
hard flesh made for ink
who are you throwing your children at me like fists
WHY DOES YOU BEAUTY GO
it doesn't go 
it changes
a different new beauty
a new beauty of reincarnation

who are you so cold and tall
expanding outward in every direction
sooo many little extintions of yourself
who are you to give me breath .....ONLY TO EXPECT THE SAME IN RETURN
who are you to sway in the beauty of the day
MOCKING ME from my jail cell window
who are you my friend
