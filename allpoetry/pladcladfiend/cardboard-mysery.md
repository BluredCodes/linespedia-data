breath and sound lost in a single echo
   faith sought
[but never found]true insanity true love 

        all so far away.....cardborad music man makes a song for me
his tin cup and no shoes
bitter cold and no home.......why lost why dishoned
toss a coin but not a glance no contact
a loner[outsider]. the everyday stuggles of a man with no hope. no family...shatered dreams 
no way to love....no one to care
the everyday life in a cardborad mysery
