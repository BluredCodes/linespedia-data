OOhh shoe!
Sweet european lady's,shoe
how sinful are you,shoe
theres coffee inside you,shoe
with rain caressing evey inch 
of your sweet synthetic blue leather,shoe
heal, long, slender, shoe
bow, weathered and frayed,shoe
three dollars and years of wear, shoe


OOhh shoe!
your darkness and your love,shoe
with toe so small so ever pointed foreward,shoe
blood inside you shoe
silence, terror, chaos, shoe
label golden old, shoe
time still stands inside you, shoe
poor european lady's shoe
