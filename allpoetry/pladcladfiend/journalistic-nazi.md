rough>brittle pages
word
after
cluttered word.
so many seemingly endless list of ad and---space?
plant your name and face
all for one and a replica of years before..
for our brotherhood and conformity..
we discrace...indiviual? 
conspiriters dressed in lace.
stay to the text and dont FONT yourself
at least say you self proclaimed goddes 
of journalistic neo-nazism
stop censorship but dont be creative 
stay to the line and you will be 
a journalistic nazi too..
