title: "speachless but not loveless"
writer: "pladcladfiend"
slug: "speachless-but-not-loveless"

i'm speachless
but not loveless
this girl
so beautiful
so everything i need
i can't go on to much longer
without her in my life
i feel like im going crazy..

everytime i see her
i melt...
my heart skips a beat
and i have the urge to 
embrace her and never
let go...

she doesn't know it but she glows 
a radiant ora that caresses evry inch
of my body....

she came like an angel to me and i didn't
know how to react..
standing there in a never ending line
and she said...."hi, how ya' doin' "
thats when my heart jumped out of my chest
prewraped itself and presented itself to her
she's had it ever sence...

i've waited a year or more and i still
feel the same..
now she may be leaving soon and i 
just hope i can be with her
even if it's for a little while...
i just want to love her......
