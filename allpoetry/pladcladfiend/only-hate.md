how can you hate with such force all day 
[every day]
this is your life your one and only chance
this is my voice
this is my life
live threw words as if each could be your last
to hate
ANd only hate...what kind of a life is that
what kind of meaning is in that[none]
so live every day every minute every wordi read an artical in a paper about skin heads and i could figure out how people could live like that....
