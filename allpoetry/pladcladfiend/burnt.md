the feeling of nothing
the feeling of something.
thats not there
the emptyness and solitude
all alone left to burn
i feel the evin creepin' into my brain
NO, you will never know my pain
just let me burn...
burnt to the crisp
my rage brings such bliss
only remember this.....the sanity is lost now maddness is the boss
JUST LET ME BURN
