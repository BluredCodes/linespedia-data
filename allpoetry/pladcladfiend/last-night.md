the microphone was nawing on my chin 
i let him do it.
untill i fell asleep.
then when i fell down
and the stoole caught me i said 
"thank you my good man....whatcha up to."
he re-plied"not much.....you?"

then i left to write...."hi mom why are you wearing a blue whale as a neck-tie...?"
"no reason."she said...."it just looks good with my new shoes..."


so i slowly crawl across the bloodstaind battlefield of my bedroom...into the the always forgiving mattress in the corner....
i pull my covers up and look at the homeless man
changing th CD in my stereo....he says"good night" and then sits on the floor......... i sleep
