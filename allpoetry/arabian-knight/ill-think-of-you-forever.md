---
title: "I'll Think Of You Forever"
writer: "Arabian Knight"
slug: "ill-think-of-you-forever"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/33-Ill-Think-Of-You-Forever-by-Arabian-Knight"
---

You are a friend I love indeed

you're never far at time of need

you heal my wounds that slowly bleed

I'll think of you forever



Before we met my eyes were blind

you gave me sight..the perfect kind

your face is always in my mind

I'll think of you forever



You satisfied my urging soul

you gave my life a noble goal

your service is my only role

I'll think of you forever



Your smile has caused my sun to show

and through your laugh my winds will blow

your caring words make roses grow

I'll think of you forever



Without your eyes there is no life

your absence is a poisoned knife

to see you is my highest strife

I'll think of you forever



For you i open all my doors

and fight for you a thousand wars

the heart inside my chest is yours

I'll think of you forever



Your memory revives my heart

your image is my only art

and even if we grow apart

I'll think of you forever
