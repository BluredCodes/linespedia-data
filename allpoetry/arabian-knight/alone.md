Alone I sit and face the sea

waves so vibrant ..birds so free

winds that dance with ecstacy

I sit alone my dear...



I wish I was the flowing wave

to flow to your remote enclave

and be your eyes most humble slave

and guard you from your fear...



I wish I was a bird of grace

to fly throughout the open space

and land beside your lucious face

and wipe your dripping tear...



I wish I was the wind so light

to blow across the gloomy night

and surely we can then unite

and prove our love sincere...



Still alone I sit and pray

will we meet another day

and ask myself is there a way

to move our bodies near...



Only hopes are what remain

and only hopes will keep me sane

and help control my burning pain

until your eyes appear...
