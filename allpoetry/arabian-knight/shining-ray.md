---
title: "Shining Ray"
writer: "Arabian Knight"

Recite this poem all alone

and read it in a grieving tone

its words are echoes of my moan

and tears of raw depression



Through destiny I met a girl

she made my spirit dance and twirl

as if I found a precious pearl

amidst the deepest ocean



Her parents named her "shining ray"

her laugh creates the brightest day

and blows the dark of night away

she lights up all creation



My soul surrounds her like a dome

and in my heart I built her home

and paved my veins for her to roam

to soothe me with her motion



Sometimes she asks me would you cry..

if suddenly I were to die ?

or would you just release a sigh ?

or pay me no attention ?



I focused deep inside her eyes

and wondered why such thoughts arise

then told her that my heart denies..

to think of such a notion



And as we sat upon a dune

and looked towards the glowing moon

reflecting on a calm lagoon

and traded thoughts of passion



She looked at me and shed a tear

and said there's something you should hear

although my love is so sincere

and so is my devotion



With cancer my own life's at stake

and I don't want your heart to break

so I should leave you for your sake

before my sad conclusion



My life was torn with what she said

the moon so white had turned to red

I carried in my pulsing head..

the sorrow of a nation



I cursed the earth.. I cursed the skies

I filled the silent night with cries

and in my tears I drowned my eyes

and felt the worst sensation



In times of pain our souls are weak

they melt and then they slowly leak..

throughout the eye then on the cheek

as drops of pure emotion



I had an urge to thrust my sword

inside my chest to end my world

then hold her as my blood is poured

to die was my intention



But then my faith in God awoke

embracing me inside its cloak

I called upon Him to invoke..

His mercy and compassion



Then told her I will be your friend

until the day my life will end

so let our lonely spirits blend

to form an endless fusion



For everyday your face appears

is worth a life of endless years

so smile and let us wipe our tears

and shun this sad illusion



Lets look towards our future days

and live them in most joyous ways

and rest in God our trust and praise

and cherish our affection
