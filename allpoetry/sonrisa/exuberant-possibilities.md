title: "Exuberant Possibilities"

desperately searching 
for the colours
of exhuberant possibilities
beyond the
monochromatic existence
that holds me captive

wavering in its excellence,
the blinding light of security
clings to me,
leads me farther back
to the comfortable state
of mundane living

invisible force of hope
guides me down the obscure, 
tedious path 
to inner acceptance

hidden in the mist,
promised feelings of clarity,
peace 
and the exaggerated happiness
are apparently well worth
the overwhelming pain
that pours from the heart
