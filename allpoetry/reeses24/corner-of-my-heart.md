---
title: "Corner of My Heart"
writer: "reeses24"

I will keep you in the deepest corner of my heart where you won't become a memory that fades away and falls apart.  I will keep you here inside of me, because what we have just cannot be.  Being with you this way is forbidden.  Your kiss, your touch, the way you make me feel stays hidden.  I can't tell you how much I care because it would tear him apart.  So I keep you here in the deepest corner of my heart.  You will be there inside wherever I go.  You'll be there inside and he will never know.  You'll stay locked away inside of me, a precious love that could never be. As much as I want to I just can't stay.  Fate brings love and carries it away.  I have to go home where he will be there waiting. I have to go home where memories of you have a way of fading.  So I will keep you in the deepest corner of my heart, because the miles that stretch between us have a way of tearing us apart.
~Renae
