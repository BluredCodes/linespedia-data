---
title: "Here Without You"
writer: "reeses24"

I don't know how much longer I can hold on, feelings fade when you are gone.  I don't pretend to care because I really do.  It's just so hard being here without you.  Tell my hands not to miss your touch, tell me how to hold on and not hurt so much.  Tell my eyes not to wash away the memories with sweet, salty tears.  Tell me how to breathe before I drown in my own fears.  I don't know how much longer I can hold on, feelings fade when you are gone. I don't pretend to care because I really do.  It's just so hard being here without you.  Tell my hands to remember your touch, because time is fading fast, and suddenly what we have doesn't mean as much.  Tell my lips to remember the flavor of your kiss.  Tell me how to remember all those things about you I used to miss.  I don't know how much longer I can hold on, it's been so long since you have been gone.  It's not the same anymore.  I still care, I really do... it was just too hard being here without you.
~Renae
