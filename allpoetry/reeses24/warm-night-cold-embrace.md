A warm night
A cold embrace
Salty tears
on my face.

One last hug
One last good-bye
Stars are falling 
from the sky.

Can't look back
I walk away
Swallow down words
too painful to say.

Close the door
things will never be the same
Close my eyes
shut out the pain.

Bitter words
A broken heart
a fragile love
falls apart.

A warm night
A cold embrace
emptiness now
will take your place.

~Renae
