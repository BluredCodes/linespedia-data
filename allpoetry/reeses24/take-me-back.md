Take me back to that picture that sits on my desk.  You held me close in my pretty pink dress.  Take me back to when those flowers were alive and in bloom.  Take me back to those nights spent in your room.  Take me back to the days of poems and letters.  When it was warm and we didn't need sweaters.  Take me back to that summer, it doesn't seem so long ago.  Take me back when you didn't want to let me go.  Take me back to the hugs,the smiles and the kisses.  Take me back to when we used to share our dreams and wishes.  Take me back to our first night together, when I was still naive enough to believe you would love me forever.
~Renae
