And then
the moment came
even the forest was breathless

     An echo would carom forever

The silence
in that instant
exposed the pounding of my heart

     A drum heard so internally

What stillness
through profound pause
where feelings within were unveiled

     My soul grasped for freedom

A shadow
like a willow
slipped smoothly along shores edge

     Love enveloped me with rich compassion

Her wings
surrounded my being
softening the tension of my spirit

     Joy crumbled my fear and pain

And then
the moment came
even the forest was breathless

     Embraced by an Angel I finally cried
