eep eep. 
I'm a freak-freak. 
-Gettin my groove on to some serious song. 
-Shakin' my booty, 
All-night-long. 

In the back streets, 
In summer’s heat, 
I'm sweating, 
-To-the-love-you're-giving. 

I'm breaking in new shoes 
An'an old soul, 
Getting ready to hit the road 
Cause I -- 
Just can’t contain, 
All the delirious love 

--Stains, 
-You're spilling on me. 

I'm flexing muscles, 
Oiling up. 
For the big show, 
Where we... 

--Yeah, that’s what I'm talking about. 

-I'm an Athena-girl 
Love, love, 
and power 

In the back streets, 
In the summer heat, 
Where your love spills, 
As I make my moves, 

And move on, 

Singing of, not the blues, 
Sugar-plum, 
But the raptourious bliss 
Of our last kiss. 

-With that,
