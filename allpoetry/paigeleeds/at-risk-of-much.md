He walked from the room, 
She laid in bed 
Probing questions 
Kept picking at her head 

The emotion was there 
Yes it was 
And passion lived there 
Amongst 

But fear welled up inside 
This siren 
Of what passion becomes- 
Love, devotion 

In loving, 
She left herself open, 
To love's potential hurt 

In her heart, 
Love's burning flame, 
Tickled, soldered 
Protected from pain. 

He walked back in, 
Crawled into bed, 
Slid arms around her 
In which she hid. 

In soft sent of his musk, 
In his warm touch, 
She accepts her love, 
At risk of much.This was a response to a challenge, and had to be under 100 words.
