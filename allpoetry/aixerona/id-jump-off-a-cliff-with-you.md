title: "i'd jump off a cliff with you."
writer: "Aixerona"

all i can do 
is imagine what you’ve gone through. 

but you can’t 
begin to imagine 
how great is my love for you. 


i see you crying (but my hand can’t reach your face) 
i hear you screaming (it bounces off my mind) 
i can feel you cutting. 
but i can only feel 
((too far away to t o u c h)) 



please hush, 
little baby 
please stay 
with me 
i need you here with me 
we need you here with us. 


i would have followed you. 
across the nation 
around the world 
over a bridge 
off a cliff… 


but you’re here and you’re hiding 
you’ve shut me off 
left me shaking for you 
for I can still hear you screaming in my head 
bouncing off my mind. 


(((please come back,
 before i slip 
back
into insanity without you)))
