title: "Forsaken for Distance ((working title))"

you don't know
              how lovely you are
you don't know
              you're forever beautiful
i wish i could
              kiss your forehead,
i wish i could
              promise you'll be fine.
i wish i could
              make you believe
i wish i could
              promise it'll be okay.
sadly, i can't
              hold you close when you shiver
sadly, i can't
              wipe away your tears.
sadly, i can't
              keep you from your razor
sadly, i can't
              save you from your disease...
