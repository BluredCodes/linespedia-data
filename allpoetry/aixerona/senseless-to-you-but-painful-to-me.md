i stole from my own cookie jar thinking 
'ugly porcelain dolls shouldn't...'
ripped open the candy wrapper thinking
'don't, don't, don't, ugly porcelain doll...'
maybe she'll shrink
if i threw her in hot water,
plastic stuffing in her 
ugly porcelain mouth.i feel old and immature.
