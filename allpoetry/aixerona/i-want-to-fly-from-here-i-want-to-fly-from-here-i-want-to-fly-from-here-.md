she was like a bird on mute, her useless wings would [want to] scream :' make me fly away from here!'walls are cages, she's a broken phoenix, pointlessly trapped as she can't fly either way. a cage tossed in endless rain- she's a million miles from her one love, a million sighs and she still won't be there. phoenix cries inaudible, over one love and endless pain. life and death spiraling round her cage. the whole world is running over the cage she still can't get away from- still can't break free from. no hand shall caress her silent feathers, then? no lips to kiss the anguish gone? no voice to sing the babe to sleep? no release from the ropes around her waist... [that's.why.she.can't.fly.]
the cage rolls down a hill, but it's really going in circles.
until her
one love.
unlocks and frees
the broken bird
crying babe
pained mute.
