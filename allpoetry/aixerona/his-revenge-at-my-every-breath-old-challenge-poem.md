a man who should be 
out of my life 
has stepped into my room, 
taken over my bed and sheets 
while i pretend to sleep. 

then out of the blackness of 
2.30am 
he starts to punish me 
and my spine 

with sharp lashes and 
dead hands... 
punished because 
i'm still alive. 

and orders me to 
scream it out 
'THERE'S NO LIGHTING' 
scream in french 
while i stare at his 
heroin eyes. 

bits of old english 
and a french nevermore 
mute shrieks until i topple 
on the rug he once bled on 
(purposefully) 

i choose to roll 
on my cold 3am floor 
shake like mad and wonder 
is he coming to get me (back)
