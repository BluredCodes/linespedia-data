---
title: "who to thank for finding love?"
writer: "Aixerona"

i thank ye gods above
 for finding me a love so sweet
for a reason to open my eyes come dawn,
a reason to breathe throughout the day.

i thank love itself
for finding me, and blessing me with him
for granting me a heart to love
and the honor of his love in return.

but most of all, i thank the one i love
for breathing, living, being himself,
being perfect, perfectly beautiful,
loving me, completing me.
