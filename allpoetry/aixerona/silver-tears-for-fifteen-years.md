---
title: "Silver tears for fifteen years"
writer: "Aixerona"
slug: "silver-tears-for-fifteen-years"
source: "AllPoetry"

The only thing they asked of me
Is that I never forget.
Though I've said, times a thousand
"Do you trust me not? I shan't!"
I will, I shall, I am obliged...
For I am crushed by a thousand visions
Never meant for a man to see
[There must be then
A small difference
Between man and
Girl of fifteen.]

Fifteen summers,
For I've never seen snow.
I'd trade you anything for some,
As the sun isn't my friend
Neither am I his.


Fifteen seasons of cold, harsh rain,
For my land is of sunshine and rain,
And rain is what I like best,
As will a child of the moon,
But there's only one of us left.actually... i don't really like this one.
