---
title: "a million pieces..."
writer: "Aixerona"
slug: "a-million-pieces"
source: "AllPoetry"

i have chosen death to be my love.
and what
?
she's going to smash me into 
a million pieces.
a million, million... painful million...
he's going to tear me up 
and leave me with a million scars
.a million, million... painful million...
blood will drip. 
drip.
drip.

i get to live to see you haunted.
i never die...
