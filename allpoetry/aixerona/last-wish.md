---
title: "last wish"
writer: "Aixerona"
slug: "last-wish"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

another dark trait
more criticism
from my reflection

dire effects
sour words

another emotion
that never 
surfaced.

old wounds
that never heal
heart of hearts
drowned my river of tears.
 
my skies,
pitch black
by day
star deprived
by night

once again
I'm sinking in
this is a 
never ending circle

until one day it 
topples toward the end


a dead, infinite calm
if it is so,
it is my smile
and ever last desire.thanks sweetsadness
