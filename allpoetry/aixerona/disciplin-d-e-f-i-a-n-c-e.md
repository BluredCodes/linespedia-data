They all say no.
Drag me to a bench.
Fools, 
A poet sits on a rock.

                                                You all have rules
                                                A table of laws to pound on me
                                                Ignorance,
                                                My art needs freedom.


                        Take away my pen and paper
                        Tie my arms behind my back.


I can still fly away.

                                                When the world comes to an end,
                                                I shall write it down.i have no reason to be fond of this one...
