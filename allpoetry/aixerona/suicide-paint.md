title: "suicide paint"

he was probably dead, by the time they took him away. i barely noticed. all i saw beside his pale, pained face, was the painting that hung where he once stood. it changed everytime i looked.
it did.
i think it just said 'i want to get away!'. 
then there it goes again.
'goodbye my love. and breath. and heart.'
a brush stroke through the face of a broken heart.
there it goes once last:
wings.
