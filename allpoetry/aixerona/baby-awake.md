hush babyit's lateclose your eyes, child.don't cry...you're not old enough to cry.don't cry until you're in pain.hush baby,momma is so afraid...baby don't you cry.tell me babywhat do you feel?momma and childare very scared.[is there someonewatching?waiting?]it's the morning hours,mamma's still here.baby's still awakewith eyes of ice,child, why do you lookat me like that?
