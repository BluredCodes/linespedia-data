[.......][.......]Extra sugar sweet
[.......][.......]Sweetness
[.......][.......]Sometimes in life,

[.......][.......]Is hard to find.
[.......][.......]

[.......][.......]That’s why the second 
[.......][.......]Grand Prize
[.......][.......]Is that sweetness, plus
[.......][.......]An extra on itself
[.......][.......]

[.......][.......]That explodes,
[.......][.......]Reforms,
[.......][.......]And comes to life.
[.......][.......]

[.......][.......]As girls with bright smiles
[.......][.......]And bits of Venus
[.......][.......]In their eyes.
[.......][.......]Along with rainbow raindrops
[.......][.......]And fairy heritage.
