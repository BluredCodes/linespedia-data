i might have found the most beautiful thing on earth. i think i'm an inch away from bliss. i might have fallen into the most beautiful dream... a cloud of feathers... ever since that night. 
i can't believe myself, but i'm so lost in thinking of you, since that night we said i love you.
i can't be mistaken, we're each other's forever. your early present was the most beautiful thing on this earth. it's safe to say i love you. i love you because i love you. i love you because you let me love you. i love you as i've never loved, i've really never loved. 
everything that could have been has built up into a sumptous pyramid, with the color of passion, a monument of everything i've never felt before. it never crumbles, but it's essence, my love, my love comes rushing down from the top of the heavens and down to you, pulling you closer to me. 
i've never felt this close to someone i can't even touch, but it's quite allright. the wait will keep me breathing, it's strength will keep me alive. i'm staying alive because i love you.
you keep me smiling through the day, black hours go unnoticed, or collapse whenever i think of you...
i will never thank you enough... all my kisses shan't suffice... no limit to our caress, you're mine forever. and i belong to you. it's perfect when i think of you. the world can drift away, more space to us, my love. 
i'll never find a flaw to you, i could never frown with you so close, you've taught me to expect your kiss to make it all flow into place... 
i think you are the missing piece to the perplexed riddle i've grown into, now we're the perfect little enigma, we can befuddle the world, all we need is each other. each other's arms forever. each other's love throughout eons and eons...
