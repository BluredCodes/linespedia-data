---
title: "Love, interrupted."
writer: "Aixerona"
slug: "love-interrupted"
source: "AllPoetry"

To love,
Then to forget,
And then to love again...

To love,
To turn away,
To fall in love once more.

To love,
Then to let go,
To run back to your kiss.

To pause,
To touch your face,
