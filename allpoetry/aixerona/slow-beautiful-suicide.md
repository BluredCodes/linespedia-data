---
title: "Slow, Beautiful Suicide"
writer: "Aixerona"
slug: "slow-beautiful-suicide"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/33924-Slow--Beautiful-Suicide-by-Aixerona"

To depress myself
Drowning in your beauty...

To be your worst mistake...
While we never even spoke.

My lips are on fire,
Waiting for a kiss...

I haven't been allowed to live
On account of your existance.

Sleepless nights and deep cuts
One for every breath I take.

Pretty scars for your pretty face
Pretty daydreams for the love of you.

Pretty in my dreams,
So ugly in your eyes.

Try to shrink for the love of you.
Sink into my new obsession.

But I'm so ugly on the inside...
So rotten on the outside.

To feel unwanted
And take up space.

Dead inside,
Take my blood.

Lifeless, 
Take my soul.

An exploding mirror,
The picture of your face
In bits of shattered glass.
