---
title: "far away love (break in closer)"
writer: "Aixerona"
slug: "far-away-love-break-in-closer"

i expect you one day
through my door like a hurricane.
you'll pull me against you 
and kiss me as if
lightening
was running through your blood
then hold me tighter
than hearts hold life
and be to me
the skies to the moon
as i'll be your
star in the dust
with these lips
        to kiss
a body
        to hold
i'm yours
        to take
for a ride through foreverfor josh ~mwa.
