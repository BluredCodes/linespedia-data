title: "Dead Calm Chaos"

What if we were talking,
But quietly lost words
Until i was left
within myself,
at war with my mind,
Alone
...?
[                                         ]From the dephts
[                                         ]of my lungs,
[                                         ](breathless velvet)
[                                         ]i'd scream.
[                                         ]slow, silent
[                                         ]calm and insane.

what if my skin
could melt the world
do more harm
in sugarcoated misery
while  i stand
and watch,
alone
...?
[                                         ]i'd place my hand
[                                         ]upon your heart
[                                         ]deep in, through
[                                         ]and out of you.
[                                         ]slow, silent,
[                                         ]calm and insane.
