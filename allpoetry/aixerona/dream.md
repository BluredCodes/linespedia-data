sweet whispers
              "aye, my girl, my sweet girl..."
strong arms
              "you'll be fine, you're mine."
his eyes
              "i love you so..."
in mine
              "i love you so much..."


his hands
crept behind
my neck.

                              a swift gesture
                              of his fingers
                              now we're

             face to face
             lips to lips
                                                    lips on lips
                                 we kissed.


my eyes fluttered open

                                                  broken down bed and 
                                                    crumpled sheets.

             [it was all a dream]

he doesn't exist
                               i've never been kissed.
