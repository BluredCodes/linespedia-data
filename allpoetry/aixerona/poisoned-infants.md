title: "poisoned infants"

she told him how she loved him
anywhere, to anyone but him.
he didn't care, 
wasn't moved, didn't move
as a childe was borne 
from her bleeding wrist

the babe said
'i am poison'd,
i will not love.'

she said
'my poison 
is my love.
please exuse me
while i die.'

the infant died
and dissappeared.
his mother twisted
and withered away.

i watched.

she loved.
she turned to death.

i watched.

his indifference
turned him to himself.

out in the cold 
alone with a dead girl
that could have been 
one
with him.-_-.... pfft at me i say.
