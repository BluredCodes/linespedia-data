---
title: "Twisted lullabye"
writer: "Aixerona"
slug: "twisted-lullabye"

The baby broke her lullaby
Gave me an iced glare
I knew she'd never cry
As her eyes told me to beware

I tried to fix the cradlesong
I gave up. It fell apart, bit by bit
She was smirking, singing along
A devil's smile on her face, twisting it

The song shot from her mouth like a flame
I forgot it all- where did I find that baby??
She played my mind like a game
The infant was winning, running after me.

Both of us running as we stayed in place
I, afraid, cursing the song that twisted my night
She, evilly smiling, in love with this chase,
Obviously finding pleasure in my fright.

The infant looked at me with her evil eyes,
Said 'say your prayers, choose your poison.'
Sent a chill down my spine as she laughed at my cries.
I sobbed 'when she smiles at someone else, i feel forlorn...'

My eyes closed, I fell to the ground
I thought I felt myself dying
Opened my eyes to a familiar sound
With my ex-best-friend, and at me, she was laughing.
