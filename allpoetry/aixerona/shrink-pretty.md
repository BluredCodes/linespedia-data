---
title: "Shrink pretty."
writer: "Aixerona"
slug: "shrink-pretty"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/30722-Shrink-pretty.-by-Aixerona"
---

Cleanse             your soul
Wash                me out
Burn                my soul
Blow                our candle

Breathe             my fire
Cry                 pretty blue
Shrink              to pretty
Pretty              nothing.

Belt                your mouth
Tie                 my hands
Tighten             the ropes
Around              my waist.

Absorb              myself
Pinch               our skin
Shrink              to pretty
Pretty              nothing.
