---
title: "thanks for the pain"
writer: "Aixerona"

i thank you for the nights you made me bleed
and for that kiss you would never give
should i cherish every gift-wrapped bruise?
here's your thanks from the girl you broke.

could i fall in love with the misery you cause?
i'm sure you think i love the way you never love me back,
that i fell to your silent promise, one to never catch me.
