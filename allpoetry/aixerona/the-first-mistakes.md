---
title: "the first mistakes."
writer: "Aixerona"

[.......][.......]I’ve got a new idea
[.......][.......]A new teacher
[.......][.......]With one rule:
[.......][.......]Food’s a foe.
[.......][.......]Remember this,
[.......][.......]Then you’ll be fine
[.......][.......]And beautiful.
[.......][.......]
[.......][.......]Gotta love her bony 
[.......][.......]Womanly fingers
[.......][.......]As they walk over my body
[.......][.......]Reshaping me.
[.......][.......]
[.......][.......]She’ll touch my stomach
[.......][.......]It’ll cave into my ribs.
[.......][.......]The daily menu’s nothing,
[.......][.......]The second rule is quiet.
[.......][.......]
[.......][.......]Took so much strength 
[.......][.......]To get in there
[.......][.......]They want to see me out
[.......][.......]Push a plate to my face
[.......][.......]Scream a demand of ‘eat!’
[.......][.......]
[.......][.......]While in the distance
[.......][.......]Pretty girls
[.......][.......]Flaunt narrow frames
[.......][.......]And bones
[.......][.......]
[.......][.......]Here I sit
[.......][.......]At least being
[.......][.......]Prettier 
[.......][.......]On an empty stomach.
