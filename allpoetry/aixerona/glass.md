---
title: "Glass."
writer: "Aixerona"
slug: "glass"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/28879-Glass.-by-Aixerona"
---

all night.
awake.

turning in my sheets.
add a sheet.

thinking of you.
proves how much i
need you.

the whole world is
asleep.
even the moon.

but just now,
not me.
not yet.

i can't...

i can't sleep without your
voice.
i can't sleep without 
a sweet whisper.
of nothing.

i just can't sleep
without you.

me
and peace
are on different sides
of this glass wall

you
and happiness
are on one side

the other side...
me.
