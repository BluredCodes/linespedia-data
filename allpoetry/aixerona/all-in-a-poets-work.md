Locked myself into my room
Threw away the key
Along with anything
Not quill, ink, and parchement.

Sat down with a pen
It ran around some paper.
Not a word came out
Not a word I could write.

The wind heard it fall.
A sharp intake of air, no release.
Until... one word.
Make that four:

            I can breathe again.
A chain of words followed
With sprinkles of sense, hope,
Pain and depth...
