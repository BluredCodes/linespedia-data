---
title: "twisted branches of a year"
writer: "Aixerona"
slug: "twisted-branches-of-a-year"
source: "AllPoetry"

january girl
for a
february bachlorette,
march baby grew up 
to
april suicide. 
may rains on
june mother 
(for
july kisses and
august leaves) til
september tears 
an
october break down.
november sighs a 
forever december.
