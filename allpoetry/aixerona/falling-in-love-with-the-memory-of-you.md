---
title: "falling in love with the memory of you"
writer: "Aixerona"
slug: "falling-in-love-with-the-memory-of-you"

i only realized how beautiful you are
how beautiful you were
i only realized how i loved you
when you were gone.

i didn't know i loved you
i didn't know i loved.
then you were gone.

your eyes were prettier when open.
i loved your mouth when it spoke.
i'd kiss your nose if it were breathing.

love is all i have left to say.
love is all i don't have right now.

you took my emotions
when you took yourself awaybut its all my fault and i hate myself
i cant write anymore either. notice how i suck now.
