---
title: "i am sorry, mon chéri"
writer: "Aixerona"
slug: "i-am-sorry-mon-chri"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/42227-i-am-sorry--mon-chéri-by-Aixerona"
---

so weak from screaming out a lie
((ialreadyate)) 
she yells 
with no exclamation points. 

impossibly narrow frame
((that is so not true)) 
so i say, so she says but
still they point to the bone(s)

the secret (our secret)
is a beast who stole a woman's name
chose her stomach to hole up and
destroy. (it's good for you and me)

w(sh)e usually can't scream it out
((these words are a long, soft shriek)) 
the beast sleeps (she's close to a black out) 
so dangerously (beautifully) fragile

so dangerously (beautifully) fleshless
though flesh is all she sees
i'll wither with her (as i can't help)
i'm only the girl in the mirror.
