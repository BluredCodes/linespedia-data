---
title: "Sharing the Tragedy"
writer: "K.E. Morris"

My love

I miss

Your smile

Eyes smited with pain

My inspiration

Alone in the midst of a devoted audience

Worship

A poet, a painter, an artist's hands

I'd like to hold

Onto this friendship

No longer do I care

If I lose hope

I thought I'd die

Drowning in apathy

Never forgetting

Always regretting

Never appreciating

Of exchanging laughter

Sharing the tragedy

That is our lives.
