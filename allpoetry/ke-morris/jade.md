Life looks bleak
Tastes bitter
These are the same eyes
The same mouth that hardly lets a true smile escape
That as a child held the world with an eager grasp
Impatient for her to whisper in her ear
The mystery behind the doors of 
Endless possiblities
How to abuse the unknown and uncertain hours
How good it feels to lie under the blanket of self-pity
On a bed of lonlines
Under the sun, moon, and stars
Overflowing with an euphoria of hope
And how sipping from the fountain gave her peace
A serenity that outlasts the countless hours of begrudged misery
A new drug
Our martyr from the time we leave mother's womb
To another sanctuary, encasing
What remains of raw purity thrown assunder
To that which will be forgotten
When memories die.
