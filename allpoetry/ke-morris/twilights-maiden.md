---
title: "Twilight's Maiden"
writer: "K.E. Morris"
slug: "twilights-maiden"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/52290-Twilights-Maiden-by-K.E.-Morris"
---

Twilight dancer
Victim of moonshine
Starlight bathes her in full bloom
Stars fall...
Just like us

The Maiden sighs in wonder
As leaves tremble
Cracked and painted with autumn
Journey in the arms of the wind
Rest
On the ground which she treads
In her naivety
Heeds not to nature's lighthouse
A reminder that dawn has faded
Into a prism of color
Dusk is waxing...
Sentencing her to the Summerlands
A comotose patient in sweet surrendering
As she dies
Singing a nameless tune
Loitering in stolen serenity
Another wilted rose.
