title: "An Awkward Silence"

I don't know what to think

My head is an empty space

Clouds of smoke littered with ashes of guilt

Innocence burned and exhaled

An awkward silence

A pause in conversation

Are you who I thought you were?

What do you see in my eyes when you stare and smile

And laugh in our solitude

Remnents of the forest, wild and untamed

In the heart of a civilization patiently waiting to be desroyed once more

We make light of the heavy burdens of our immorality

Mortality

I search you

Knowing there is more

To love's first glance

An enigma shrouded in iron mail of mystery

The more you let go the less I hold onto

I want to love you

Want to show you depths not yet explored and

Undiscovered.

Will you understand through the fog

And hazy vision

As you inhale once again

Or will you turn away?
