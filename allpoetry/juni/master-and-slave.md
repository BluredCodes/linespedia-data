---
title: "Master And Slave"
writer: "Juni"
slug: "master-and-slave"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/44280-Master-And-Slave-by-Juni"

You are very clever...
capturing me...
when I escape...
hypnotize me...
with your eyes...
Tell me to look deep...
I want to hear your voice...
I want to become sleepy...
I want to become tired...
Tell me...
How sleepy I am...
How tired I am...
with your voice...
I am your slave...
I will obey you...
Master...
