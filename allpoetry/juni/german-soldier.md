German soldier...told me to look into his eyes.I looked into his eyes.German soldier...told me how heavy my eyes were getting.My eyes were heavy.German soldier...stroked my face and said 'sleepy,sleepy eyes'.The sleep was building in my eyes.My eyes were sleepy and heavybut I looked into the German soldier's eyes.German soldier...gently kisses my face and neckwhile I am partially hypnotized.German soldier...wants to taste my lips.I cannot say no,though my lips try to.German soldier...told me to sleep.My eyes closed within seconds.German soldier...told me to open my eyes.I opened my eyes and stared blankly.He wanted me to look at his lips,which I did.German soldier...wanted to taste my lips...his lips said 'kiss me'.my lips said 'yes'.German soldier...and I kissed deeply,passionately.German soldier...wanted to bring me out of hypnosis.He wanted me to look into his eyes,which I did,and he told me that I was awakening,becoming refreshed and relaxed.The sleep escapes my eyes-they are clear now.
