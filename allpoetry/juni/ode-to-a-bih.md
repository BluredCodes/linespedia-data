title: "Ode to a Bi**h"
writer: "Juni"
slug: "ode-to-a-bih"
source: "AllPoetry"

Bitch,bitch, 
you deserve a kick in the head. 
Why don't you ever mind your own business? 
Bitch,bitch- 
you deserve that name. 
Who do you think you are? 
Bitch,bitch- 
you should learn to keep your mouth shut. 
You shouldn't be so nosy. 
You're beyond a bitch;you're a bitch face. 
There's nothing more that I hate than a bitch 
who gets in my business. 
Bitch,bitch- 
get some brains. 
You obviously don't have any!
