You can call me Juni(you-knee).
I am twenty-five years old. 
I dreamed that I heard French being spoken in a dream and I said,'please speak slowly'in German.
This was the first time I heard French being spoken in a dream.
I dreamed before that I saw a newspaper written in French and English.
I wrote this by heart.comments welcome
