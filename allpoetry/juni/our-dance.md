---
title: "Our Dance"
writer: "Juni"
slug: "our-dance"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/33476-Our-Dance-by-Juni"
---

I dreamed that we were together, circa the late 1930's or 40's.
I was right across the room, and I ran toward you and jumped in your arms.
Then we floated up and danced. Our feet left the ground.
We danced to swing, jazz or maybe classical music, holding each other.
I definately held on to you, and my feet were swinging in mid-air.
Oh, what a wonderful dream!
