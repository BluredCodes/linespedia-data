---
title: "Don't Give Up"
writer: "amphibianrose"
slug: "dont-give-up"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/49112-Dont-Give-Up-by-amphibianrose"

She cries at night,
continues at dawn.
She's ready to give
it up, become
withdrawn.

Ready to give up her
life as it's been.
Ready to prove to
herself she can't
win.

All for a man who
couldn't wait to love.
Wouldn't wait for
things to improve, when
push came to shove.

Don't despair my wonderful,
lovely friend. 
There is someone out there.
There til the end.

I know you miss him,
I know you're alone.
But, I'm always there.
Face to face, or on the phone.

Don't give up now,
show him you're strong.
Your relationship with
him is over, it was all
wrong.

I know it's not easy,
it will take some time.
Forget him you will never,
good memories are sublime.

I'm sorry I can't say that
you both would be great,
but as they say,good things 
come to those who wait.

Love ya!!! Don't give up!!!!
