Men and women have it
some don't know at all
they should be banned
should be the law

They get in your face
they want to talk for hours
God, please go away
your killing my flowers

There are thing like mouthwash,
toothpaste, and gum
use them please
I'm getting numb

Smelling you breath
Ugh! How foul
Dog breath is bad
even he's starting to howl

On the floor now
I stopped dropped and rolled
You open your mouth
Wow, sure that's not mold

A green fog surrounds
the air that I breathe
When do they get the hint?
When do they leave?

There is now a question
as why my face is scowl
I warned him about his breath
bad as a bowl of bowel.
