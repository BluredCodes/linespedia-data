The mantel is placed
strategically on top
waiting for the fire
to snap,crackle, and pop

You look in the back
and turn on the switch
lights start to spin
this guy must be rich

When people walk in
coats they have brought
they turn towards the fire
wondering why their ass isn't hot

Built in the 60's
bear rugs wouldn't fit
sitting with glasses of wine
My gosh, what shit

Beautifully built
looks way cool
If you believe this is real
then pull up a stool
