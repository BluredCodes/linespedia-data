title: "Do Ya Wanna"
writer: "amphibianrose"

Get in the car,
drive over a cliff
with a little flag
piercing on her midriff.
She's been drinking
all night,doesn't
matter. The car
hits the bottm,
ends with a splatter.

Do ya wanna?
Do ya wanna?

Why can't we stop this
heart wreching saga?
Stop playing with
people's minds and
make them go gaga!
Love is just not
something that comes
with ease, stop holding
a carrot in front of horse.
Stop being a tease.

Do ya wanna?
Do ya wanna?
