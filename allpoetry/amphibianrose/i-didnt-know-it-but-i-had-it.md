title: "I Didn't Know It, But I Had It"

I said to him,
that I was through.
Packed up my things,
out the door I flew.

I never thought that
I was rushing.
How his heart,
I was crushing.

He did everything
to keep me happy.
Treated me with kindness,
I thought was too sappy.

I moved right along,
and found another.
He didn't go anywhere,
only talked to his mother.

The man I have now,
acts like I'm not there.
He sends me no flowers,
not even a bear.

I look back now,
oh how I was dumb.
I saw him with another,
I'm absolutely numb.

Why is it always,
nice guys you don't get,
but you did. I had it
and didn't know it.
