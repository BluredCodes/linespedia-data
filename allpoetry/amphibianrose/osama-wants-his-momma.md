Why is he hiding?
Why isn't he brave?
Why is he cowering
inside of a cave?

Doesn't he have a clue?
Surely he knows.
Without any scissors,
that nasty beard will grow.

If he waits long enough,
he'll have a great disguise.
He can look like Cousin It
covered in freakin' flies.

I know he's wet his little
nightie, a million times
or two.
I wonder what it smells like
there.
Like the mens bathroom I'm sure.
P-Yew

I know everyone is sick and tired
of all this freakin' drama.
But it's getting plain for us to see
Osama want's his Momma!
