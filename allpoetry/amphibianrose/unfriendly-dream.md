My good friend
I cherish you dearly,
in my dream,
I say you clearly.

You went out on a
limb, went a little 
crazy.
The time I spent looking
for you, is just a 
little hazy.

I went searching from 
house to house, going
from party to party.
Found you in a big black
house, you were being 
naughty.

You dissappeared into a
room, where a friend was
staying.
I enter the room to get
you out, something he
was weighing.

When you come out
you sit on the floor,
and snort some good
'ol candy.
They yell to us it's
poisonous stuff, you
grab whatevers handy.

We begin to take you to
a car, to take you to
the ER, you become sick
and cannot move, we
perform CPR.

You sit up, begin to
cry, your head explodes
in a shower.
I run to you, pick up
your head, plant in 
your hair a flower.

The loss is deep, I miss
you so, can't begin to tell
you.
I wake, find out you're
still here, my love
intensly grew.

I think it's my drugs messin' me up!!! LOL
