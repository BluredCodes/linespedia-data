Two rooms, four walls
cat hair, phone calls
ferrets, fish, my mini zoo
we humans live here
just we two

Drug deals, parties
and tremendous bass
don't leave home without it
by that, I mean, MACE

Neighbors that flirt
like to see you nude
"F U!'s" here A-holes there
Gosh! How Rude!

Cars with flats, leaking oil.
Diapers with poo
been left out to spoil!

Yes my box is really cozy you see
GOD I'M TRAPPED
PLEASE HELP ME!!!!!life in an apartment.. :)
