---
title: "A Fairie Death"
writer: "amphibianrose"
slug: "a-fairie-death"

Her beauty steals my heart,
Eyes of onyx, her smile, crimson tipped whiteness.
She sings songs of sirens, luring out the black
aura of my soul. Her beauty, wings of embers, burning
my last breath. As I gasp, my lungs are filled with
the remains of others that have fallen into her spell, my
porcelain skin, turns bitter cold. My eyes, bright with the 
color of hate, turns grey as her silky locks fall on my face using 
her body as my blanket. As I taste her mouth, the coppery sweetness
emcompasses my tongue, pleasing my senses, as my back arches as I reach
for her, I long to taste her, her taste of death. My love for her burns as my body convulses in passionate death. Come to me, my beautiful Fairie of Death.
