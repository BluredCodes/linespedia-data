title: "Pizza on my face"

Hamburger, mushroom
and all the way
there's cheese on my face
what can I say

Tomato sauce and cheese
stuck on the top
I've eaten so much
I think I'll pop

Thin crust, thick crust
large,medium and small
Shit I'll eat it
I'll eat it all

One piece, two pieces
now I'm on the last
pizza on my face
man what a blast

Green pepper yucky
Olives how gross
I've got heartburn
Tums??I've taken a dose

That guy, how cute
he's looking this way
lick it off cutie, 
I'm yours for the day

Ode to the pizza..
