Everyday in many ways
I'm paying Uncle Sam,
and everyday there's those
out there who just don't
give a damn.

Why do I pay for people
today who just don't care
to work.
They sit aroung and drink
their beer, just a daily perk.

Lone Star cards for groceries,
for beer they give their soul.
They keep popping kids out left
and right, use some birth control.

I understand some people can't help
it,they are disabled and hurt.
But those losers out there who
use us all, they're nothing more
than dirt.

The children they brought in this
world, have no clothes or shoes.
When is someone gonna kick their
parents ass and make them pay their
dues.

We buy things with our well earned money
like boats, stereos, and cars. While
they use their government saved money
to go drink at the bars

So what can we do the stop
this crap from getting any
worse. 
We go any living day by
day, reliving this old damn curse.
