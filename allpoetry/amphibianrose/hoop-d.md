You know it's pretty bad,
when you have an XLT Explorer.
The windows work real well, but
it has not fan nor blower.

No A/C to speak of and definitley
no heater.
It's supposed to seat a family plus,
but it's only a two seater.

It overheats real easy,the radiator's
busted. I'm surprised it's holding
together the whole darn thing is rusted.

I'm seen some out there that look like
they've been beat on.
I've also seem some without windows
and they still have the heat on.

Primer, two tone, even different
pieces.Two door standard truck
with family,nephews and neices.

Believe me I know exactly what
it's like to drive around looking
fruity. Oh to drive again my
fav-or-ite 93" Ford Hood-D.


God here we go again. How Stupid I AM!!! Stupid! Stupid! STupid!!
(Hitting head on wall).I told myself never again.
