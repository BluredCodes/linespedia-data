---
title: "Mr. Bossman"
writer: "amphibianrose"
slug: "mr-bossman"

You bust your ass 
day after day and
he just barely knows.
You've worked for him
for 18 yrs. and your
paycheck barely shows.

Bonuses are few and
far between, you
bring in most the 
profit.
The others seem to work
as hard, but they can't
seem to top it

Clients come in 2 by 2, and 
you can't get a break.
Lunch is becoming obsolete
so overtime you must take.

Phone calls coming all the time
you pass some on to another.
When other workers are out
or sick, their asses you
must cover.

Confrontations face to face
as why he can't deliver. 
Your face gets hot, your teeth
clamp down, your hands begin
to quiver.

Your 3,000 dollars away 
from a goal.
The goal is pretty high.
"Do you think you deserve
it?" is what he says, with
that you think you'll cry.

A big company moves into
town, threatens to take
you away.
You wave goodbye, say
"kiss my ass" too bad
I just can't stay.
