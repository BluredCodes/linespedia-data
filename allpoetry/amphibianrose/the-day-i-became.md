---
title: "The Day I Became"
writer: "amphibianrose"
slug: "the-day-i-became"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/46767-The-Day-I-Became-by-amphibianrose"

Hunger between two,
I became
Hurt between two, 
I became
Loss between two,
I became
Passion, existence
hearts on fire
new love
between us two
is
The Day I Became.
