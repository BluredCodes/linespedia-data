---
title: "Mummified"
writer: "amphibianrose"
slug: "mummified"
source: "AllPoetry"

Here I sit after years
of waiting.
A tomb for me,
my mind is creating.

I have felt incased in
these walls.
Never hearing the
desert coyote calls.

I thought you had
treated me with the
best of care.
Wrapped me with truth
that nothing could snare.

My head feels so empty,
the space you cannot fill.
My thoughts have been
removed, my senses 
beginning to reel.

Your chemical kisses
were placed on me
to preserve.
The treatment you placed
on me,not finished, it was more
than deserved.

My love is discolored and
old, crumbling into dust.
For you as my master, I
can no longer trust.

You say the words to
awaken me, to bring
me to your needs.
Well, I have a new
master now, my
heart for you now bleeds.
