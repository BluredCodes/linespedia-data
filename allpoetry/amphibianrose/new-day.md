To feel this way is
feeling brand new
no sorrow
no pain
holding you in my arms 
touching you
feeling no end to the
feelings that have
come back from
the deepest
corners
of my heart
Knowing Life, how I
once was and how
it could be again.
Can't imagine feeling...
feeling how I
feel with you.
