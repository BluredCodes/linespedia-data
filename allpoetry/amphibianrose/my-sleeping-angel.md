---
title: "My Sleeping Angel"
writer: "amphibianrose"
slug: "my-sleeping-angel"
source: "AllPoetry"

His eyes closed
he's so serene
want to hold him in my arms
together we will dream

His quiet breathing
takes me far away
remembering the first moments
of his blessed birthday

I touch his warm skin
as I begin to cry
I feel he's far away from me
I want to know just why

He stirs a little,
what a deep slumber
noone lives forever
knowing God has his number


I lay beside him
Hold his body,
never let him go,
his little hand touches mine
God, I love him so


Together we are on the shore
feet pressed in the sand
His eyes look into mine
He knows we'll always be together
snug into the Lords mighty hand.
