I've danced in your court
too long, my feet have tired,
my bells are not ringing
No one is laughing
My spirit is dead
Your laughter has grown old
You are my disease.
My eyes are open
No more to sleep
Dance my love, danced
feeling how I
feel with you.
for your are your
own fool....
