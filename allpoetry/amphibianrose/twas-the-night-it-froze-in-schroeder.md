As night fell
the mercury did drop
the trailers all frozen
from bottom to top

The buzzards all hung
on the limbs with care
in hopes that a skunk
soon would be there

There on the road
there arose such a creakin'
A '69 Ford truck
lots of oil he was leakin'

Eight little bunnies
shot into the grass
bushy little cotton tail
froze to their ass

We leave faucets dripping
we've had plenty of practice
Nice not to lose any plants
All we have is cactus

The cold will leave
when the sun comes out
OOps left Rover outside
Well, frostbite,no doubt

Now Schroeder is not a
bad little place,
it freezes anywhere, you see
but when that skunk didn't show as planned,
why are they circlin' me??
