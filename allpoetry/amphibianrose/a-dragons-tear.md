title: "A Dragons Tear"

A small whimper in the sky,
A beautiful Princess begins to die,
Abandoned, alone, left to wait.
She looks out the window, beyond the gate.

He listens intently from the sky.
A full moon rises, the hour is nigh.
On his armor the light doth shine.
He's known her, as a child of nine.

Everyone who loved her, they've all died
Daddy's promise for forever, He Lied!!!
All alone inside broken walls.
No servents whispering, in the halls.

He wants to love her, to show her he's near,
She'll never live to see, the dragon shed a tear.
