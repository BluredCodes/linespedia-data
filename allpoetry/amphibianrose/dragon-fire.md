Awaken!! Cold, scaly, Hot
Breath has engulfed me
Burring my heart with 
His fire. Wings stretched
To take flight. Take away
The loneliness. Return to
Your garden of death, Let
It rots, let the stench never
Reach the nose of the
lovers. Bring back nourishment
that I may live to be among
those who grow. Grow in
spirit. Grow to love my inner
and outer self. Let not your
spirit die. The Knight of Loneliness
comes. Bearing the sword of extinction.
My life will go on, as 
Dragon Fire!
