title: "The Frog And The Rose"
writer: "amphibianrose"

As the princess walks
along the Chrystal Lake,
she comes across a rose
bush.A red,red, rose
she would take.

As she bent down,to
smell the fragrant
flower, a splash
was heard. The lake
she began to scower.
A leaf in the water,
maybe a bird?

As her hand went
into the thorny
bush to pick the
ruby red rose.
A sobbing she
heard,cocking
her ear closer,
she froze.

She looked closely
into the space
between newly grown
thorns.
There sits the rainbow
frog, crying as he
mourns.

I have watched you
for years, inside your
ivy covered walls.
Have heard your beautiful
voice inside those long,
lonely halls.

Yet I have never seen,
the way you walk in
splendor and in grace.
Nor have I ever seen
the beauty of your luminicent
face.

I've sat here for years
thinking there was no
other thing so lovely.
Yet here you are,the
one I've watched from
afar, like an angel
hovering above me.

For you I would pick
this flower, blood
from the thorn prick
flows.
To give you one single,
long stem beauty. The 
fragrant petals of this
ruby red rose.

Every so sweetly,
she takes the lovely rose.
Every minute his love
for the Princess grows.

She wonders if a gentle
kiss would make him a
handsome Prince.
She heard too many 
fairty tales for it
to make sense.

She kissed him ever so sweetly,
upon his colorful cheek.
Her eyes closed so tightly,
she was afraid to peek.

Before her eyes a miracle
of loveliness was born.
For he had not been a 
Prince, but a beatiful
white Unicorn.

She climbed upon his
silky back, he licked
her on her hand.
They galloped off deep
into the night, to
their Never Never Land.
