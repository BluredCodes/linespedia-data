There he sits, at
the end of the bar.
Thoughts of his wife,
have gone quite far.

She spots him,from
out on the floor.
She knows if she
tries. Few drink,
they're out the door.

He catches her staring,
the feeling is mutual.
He knows after the drinks
begins the weekend ritual.

The bar become noisy and
hazy with smoke.
It takes no time for
her to be broke.

He pays for the drinks,
puts his hand on her back.
Leads her to the door,
for gentlmenliness,
he's got the knack.

He drives them to her
small, country home.
She takes out her key
it flashed bright chrome.

His wife is home preagnant,
2 months to go.
She's getting quite worried,
her face it will show.

It doesn't really matter,
he still does his thing.
His vows were a lie,
so What Is A Ring???
