title: "The Park Bench"
writer: "amphibianrose"

It's a beautiful day
birds in the park
an elderly man sits
on the bench in the park

He sits all alone
with noone to talk
as couples pass by
on an afternoon walk

Dogs playing frisbee
and boys playing ball
leaves turning colors
it's becoming fall

He sits with his hands
sitting on his lap
no daily coffee
no afternoon nap

A tear slids down his face
as he thinks of his life
it's been a year since
he's buried his wife

He thinks of friends
of the second World War
he thinks of only sadness
has no money, he's poor

A small girl walks by
with mother in hand
sad look on mom's face
no wedding band

Where has the world gone
no longer like then
he takes out his blanket
and thinks where he's been

His life was soon ending
it happened in a pinch
the mans life slipped away
on the lonley old Park Bench.
