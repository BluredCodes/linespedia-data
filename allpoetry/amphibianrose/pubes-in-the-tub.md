After the shower or
after a bath, sometimes
we shave, you do the
math.

We shave our legs,
under arms,and such.
We want to be smooth
and soft to the touch.

We use soap,body wash,
or shave cream.
God all this hair,
you could never dream.

We use girly razors,
or guys cause their cheaper.
Girl razors cut sometimes,
guys cut deeper.

Razor burn hurts, and it
also itches.
God these bikini lines,
they are such bitches.

God I'd better get out
of here before I pull 
back a nub.
Tomorrow is when I'll find
those pubes in my tub.
