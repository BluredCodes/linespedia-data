Years have gone by
since we've seen these faces
who's hair is not short anymore
who has lost their braces

We talked about how plain we were
those things that shouldn't matter
and how those honor student cheerleaders
have somehow got much fatter.

We talked about how alcohol
has claimed some old friends
how dorky we thought we looked
compared to the new trends

Our friends have become less and less
now as we get some older
how our self esteem has improved
have grown much more bolder.

Acne has become smooth skin
age has brought on beauty
whistles are more common now
instead of "Hey there cutie"

Visions have become the same
personalites kind of differ
agility has become much harder
joints a little stiffer

Losing touch was easy then
now we're more grown up
having lunch with my friend,
to her I raise my cup.

Toast to my dear friend Chrystal!!
Many years have gone by.
