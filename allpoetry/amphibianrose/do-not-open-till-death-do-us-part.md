Please do not hit me
in the face or on the head
just because I forgot
to bring a loaf of bread.

Please do not shoot me
because I came home late
or because you came home early
to nothing on your plate.

Please do not kick me
because I'm bearing your child
or because you got drunk with the boys
and came home pretty riled.

Please do not slice me
across my beautiful face
just because I accidentally broke
your grandmother's antique plate.

Please do not hurt me!
please, have you not a heart?
Here's a tag that's labeled
"Don't Open Till Death Do Us Part"
