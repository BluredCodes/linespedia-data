."Here comes Mr.Farmer,
axe in his hand.
Looking for a chicken,
the best in the land.

He picked the biggest
one, out of about a dozen.
Damnit if he didn't look
like Chicken Littles Cousin.

Now the proud 'ol chicken
had a girl,she's the last one
he'd see.
But alas she's glad it wasn't her,
for chicken pie she'd be.

She'd always been his favorite
one,she was always there.
The other hens would cackle
aloud, "They're such a silly pair."

Now mom had told him, be true,
to never be a roost wrecker.
That "Life was
like chicken feed. You don't 
know how good it is til 
you hit it with your pecker."

Now here he was, on the way
to die, his life on earth was done.
The last cackle he heard from
her....Run ,Chicken, Run!!
