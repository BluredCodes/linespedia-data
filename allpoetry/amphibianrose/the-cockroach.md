title: "The Cockroach"

He has no antennea
nor does he have wings
he loves dark places
between womanly things

His legs aren't hairy
his chest is close
but hairy bugs sometimes
are really pretty gross

Roach motel don't stop 'em
Raid doesn't kill 'em dead
He doesn't scurry across the floor
to get under your bed

The phone rings all night long
He doesn't have a clue
That she's had more than enough
her time for him is due

Conversations on the line
make them sigh and moan
last thing that is said
"Cockroach,get off the phone".

Nuclear war won't kill 'em
No sir,
Step on them freakin' twice,
they still begin to stir

God help us all
when they start to infest
Living together?
surely, you jest.
