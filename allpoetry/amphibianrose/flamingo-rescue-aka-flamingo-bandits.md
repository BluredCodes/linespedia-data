There he was
on the ground
not at all
safe and sound

Half in the grass
Half in the drive
worried as hell
he'll never survive

Cars driving around
close to his head
What the heck
Do they think he's dead?

Impatiently waiting
my turn to drive by
no problem at all
he cannot fly

We pulled up fast
I yelled" Open the door"
She picks him up
He hits the floor

Were they watching?
I didn't care
took off fast
Look back? Didn't dare

Now he sits proud
isn't that ironic?
simply cause he's plastic
rescued from Sonic.
