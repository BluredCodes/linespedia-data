title: "Mommy, Look What I Found"

Mommy, look what I found,
I found your smile, 
your laugh, your happiness

I may not see you much
but I know you love me
wherever you are
I know you think about me

I know I don't have 
the best of both worlds,
but there is you
and others who care

Mommy, look what I found
I found your love
and I love you too...
