A,B,C, see
I've done it,
now let me be.

Oh o.k. I haven't
yet,D,E,F.
Stop yellin' at
me already, you're
making me deaf.

G,H,I, ain't I 
done yet, my 
mouth's gotten dry.

J,K,L, I need a
computer to help
me.
Have you thought
of the Dell?

M,N,O, everyone's
going to the movies.
Sure I can't go?

P,Q,R,I know you
keep telling me,
but will I go far?

S,T,U My G.I. Joe
broke, do you still
have that glue?

V,W,X, I heard in
class today, somewhere
far away, women stretch
their necks.

Y,Z, Is that the end
now? My numbers!!??
Aw Gee!
