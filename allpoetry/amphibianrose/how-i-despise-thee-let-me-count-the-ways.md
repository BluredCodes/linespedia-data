From the first day 
you brought me home,
you knew you would trap me,
never let me roam.

In your eyes
I was nothing, full
of many flaws.
Many times I wanted
to hit you ,but
just beat up the walls.

You never wanted me
to have any choices,
grounded all the time.
If I made the wrong noise,
Is blowing your nose a crime?

You point out everything
bad, with my face and hair.
Did someone make you a
God today, when I wasn't there?

I've been on my own now,going
on 14 years.
I've been hurt too much,
the end of two face nears.

You've always worried about
what everyone would think.
What are they going to 
say now? As YOUR shit
begins to stink.

I despise you,I wish
you would go torment another.
I love you and always will,
But I despise you being my mother.
