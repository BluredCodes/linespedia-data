There's a fly on my wall
Oh what he could say!
No food to eat,
except cake from my last birthday.

He sees all that happens, 
to me and my pets.
The cat box is open,
on cat poo he sits!

The buzzing the flying
Oh how I hate this,
A shoe, a paper, 
I swing, I MISS!

There's a fly on my wall,
He thought he would pass.
Of course last thing through his mind
was his ass!too bad soo sad for the fly on my wall!
