Different colors,sizes,
some round, some flatter.
Some know they're getting
fed, the rest scatter.

A weak injured fish
is put to his death.
How I feel he looks
to me for help,as he 
takes his last breath.

Some fish are made to
eat other fishes waste.
Like a dog begging for
scraps, wondering how
they'll taste.

There are some questions
that I need to ask why.
The fish that are colorful,
are they injected with dye?

They are put in aquariums
to watch them play.
The older fish lose their
color, fins begin to fray.

Makes me think of the clear
blue waters of the ocean.
The beauty that lays out
there, while the water is
in motion.

Colors so vibrant, it takes
your breath.
Fisherman in the ocean 
that cause their death.

To have the ocean to see
each morning when you wake.
Slow watchful eyes watching
every move you make.

To live free in that glorious,
vast, "Atlantis".
To swim with the mermaids,    
would a genie grant this??

Tis beauty I seek,
when swimming with the fishes.
If I could stay here forever....
Now I know what my wish is.
