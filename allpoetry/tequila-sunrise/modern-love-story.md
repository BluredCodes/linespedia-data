---
title: "modern love story"
writer: "tequila sunrise"

it happens so quickly
that it leaves you begging for more
you would brighten up her day
now youre just a bore...
why does love end up this way?
and leaves you with nothing to say?

it wears out i guess
after a while
her love turns into fake smiles
those "i love you's" that expressed feelings
turn into a normal everyday word
hurtful and meaningless
as her love wears away
i still stay
deeply in love with her
and i am left again with nothing to say
my tears say the words i couldnt say to you
i cant take another day
as for that so called neverending love
where you felt like you were above
the whole world...
pure love becomes blurred
