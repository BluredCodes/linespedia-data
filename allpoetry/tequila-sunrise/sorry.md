im sorry 
for everything i did to you 
i could never see you through 
but leaving you.... 
was the best thing to do 
you hurt me in so many ways 
put a new meaning to my bad days 
now everything has changed 
were not even friends 

but i fell in love 
correction, 
im obsessed 
i cant stay a minute away from this girl 

and i remember the day 
she took my breath away 
i love her so much 
sometimes it scares me
