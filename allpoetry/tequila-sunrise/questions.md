Here is my new disposition
Youre about to understand 
Our theory is only obvious to the skeptica 
and at the bitter end
Youre staring at a white page 
and the print is plain to see
Now turn the page and read whats written 
Its your history
Do you understand? 
The dream is over 
You havent slept in years
Dont be looking for answers
Youre better following trails
Youre staring at a black screen
Your body starts to tense
When two plus two is five or less 
its time to question common sense
Silence is a secret, a weapon in disguise
Listen to the silence Open up your 
Do you decide what you hear and believe? 
Tell me who makes your mind up for you
Do you believe what you see to be true? 
Can you tell me who owns the truth? 
Who bought the truth? 
What is your disposition? 
Here is my new disposition 
Youre about to understand 
Our theory is only obvious to the skeptical and at the bitter end
