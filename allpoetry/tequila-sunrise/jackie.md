im writing this to you
to a love so true
im writing this to you
raise my glass to another late night
thinking of you
and the things we do
im sitting here
watching the heavens
trade the black for blue
helplessly..
i realize im lost in you
to you
and to a love so truei love you to death sweetie MUAH!
