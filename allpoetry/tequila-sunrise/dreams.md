---
title: "dreams"
writer: "tequila sunrise"
slug: "dreams"
source: "AllPoetry"

i see love in your beautiful eyes tonight
and i knot when im with you everything will go right
and once again
im sitting here writing to you
watching the sun come up
after the best night ive had
nothing could ever make me sad
because just the thought of you by my side
is all i need to be glad

i dreamt of you and me
we woke up together to a beautiful morning
and we could both see
the love we have is incredibly strong
snuggled up in a blanket
what could possibly go wrong

and its like my dreams come true 
everytime i see you
because if i wasnt with you i wouldnt know what to do
youre the love of my life
youre the only special girl in my life
that could make me happy just by thinking of you
