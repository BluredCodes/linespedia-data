And I remember when this used to be fun 
now it holds such a dark place 
All the friends Ive lost 
Is it because of me? 
They said I couldnt do it
But I still swear 
I will Songs of Woo 
So Sad to see them go
Soon Ill Pull the best trick of all 
Soon Ill just disappear 
Color by Number 
Its not just a game 
But its part of me all the same 
Was I too blind to see or 
was it just cold hands and feet 
All the friends Ive lost
Was it because of me?
