so i made you happy from time to time
it wont matter now
im feeding pills with wine
maybe well meet again someday

its just me
im stuck in this
i will never get out, and thats the hardest part
and i long for your kiss
just remember that
i loved you more than anytihng in my life
i still do
but im taken away
is it because i cant see things through?

maybe ill see you on a better day
when things are going my way
because i cant live a minute without you
and from now on
every minute, i will die
ill miss you so much
i still wonder to myself why?
i made the choice i did
before i go, just wanted to say goodbyethis is just a fictional poem about me leaving the love of my life jackie...

which i didnt and dont intend to for a long time =)

its kinda about suicide 
"feeding it pills with wine"
but its fictional =)
