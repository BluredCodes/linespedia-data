---
title: "too late"
writer: "tequila sunrise"
slug: "too-late"
source: "AllPoetry"

i could still smell you as if you were right there
as if i could run my fingers through your hair
and maybe
if you lived your life with enough care
then you could see things clearly
and know that life isnt always fair
because ive found that life is great
couple of months ago when i met
the love of my life
and i could show you
just how good life can get
when you have someone that loves you
and cares for you

you never realized it
i guess you were too young
we both were
and im sorry
i never showed you how good life could get
because at the time i didnt think it could be this goodill miss you wherever you are
