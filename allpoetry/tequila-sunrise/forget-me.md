---
title: "forget me"
writer: "tequila sunrise"

i want you to go
forget me
youve done too much wrong

i want you to stay
remember me
remember me for who i am
remember everything i did for you
just to see a little smile
but it wasnt enough for you

so leave
what are you waiting for?
youve left my heart broken and sore
is that your way of saying thank you?

leave
forget me
but dont forget the sacrifices i made for you
because i feel ive wasted myself
because i will never be the same again
i could have made someone happy
instead of wasting it for your fake smiles
leave and take my memories with you
