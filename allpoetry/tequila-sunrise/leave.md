in constant need of someone new
i look around and find no one but you
its fine really
im always ready for you
sometimes too much
i guess im just in the mood

you break away, i dont let you go
i cant really
you should just say no

from paranoia to pure love
ive tasted every part of you

youre in my mind
its easy to find
what you have to do
it gets me leading to you
