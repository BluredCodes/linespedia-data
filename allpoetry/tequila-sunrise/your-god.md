what the hell is this?
so you dont like me?
youre just another kid
comin up to me saying
"hey man did you know that youre going to hell?"
youre too deep inside the lies that you fell

well fuck you kid 
im not done with you
cause this has corrupted
your whole crew
and you go home
and you pray hard
it wont matter but
you still pray to your god

i dont even know
i dont even care
all i have to say is fuck you
im out of here
you dont even care about me see?
all you care about is your god 
and all your money
since when did come become such a business
do you realize the mess
that you gotten yourself into
but you go home
and you pray hard
just so you can
sleep tight

and no wonder he seems so distant
let me tell you one thing
your god is nonexistentyea i pray.... but not because i want that thing to happend, because ive tried everything and i got notihng else to do but pray... nothing good has ever come out of it....
