title: "better than this"
writer: "tequila sunrise"

Im thinking over it
I think too much
It hinders my spirit
when theres never enough
Shell-game sleight of hand
To wish for something more
Honest husbands, cheating wives
Generous buyers, greedy stores
Ill get through it, Ill get through it
Ill say it ten times over: Ill get through it, Ill get through it
Im better than this

Thats what anchors me... I mean it weighs me down
Cant give thanks to fear, cant say no to crowds
Guess Ill just play dumb..... shouldnt be too hard
Peaceful people, violent guns
Sober drivers, drunken cars

Im better than nothing and nothing is better than this
