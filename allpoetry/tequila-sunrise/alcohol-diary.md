oh, when I think now 
its so unreal 
what did I just imagine 
was I awake 
was I asleep god 
please tell me if it was a dream now 
do you know? 
That Ive been through all this one time before 
I forget cuz, Ive been drinking, 
too much god damn ale 
i cant stand up, 
i keep falling
god I wish I could go back now 
once again are you okay? 
its just a stupid game we play 
still dont matter anyway 
cause we all die anyway
we all fall down 
well thats what they tell me 
when they tell me yes my ego bleeds 
i dont care cause we cant be fair
