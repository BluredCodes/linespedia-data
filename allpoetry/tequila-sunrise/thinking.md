writer: "tequila sunrise"

i sit here and think of you
now, today, everyday
theres nothing i wouldnt do
to hear you say
i love you

3 words
1 meaning
and its the 
greatest feeling

theres nothing that i have to say or do
to let you know that i love you 
cant you see it in my eyes?
its just pure love..
no lies
