title: "drive"
writer: "tequila sunrise"

just drive 
and forget about what we left behind
lets go far and see what we find
i need to get away
so just drive for today
and as im writing this
im in your car, driving far
dont know where were going
why care? because everything is ruined

so drive far away
just for today
the sun is shining on us
as we go the way....
i try to think of things to say
to the love of my life
the whole trip i couldnt get her off of my mind
and the love ive come to find
i think to myself
why am i not with her today
and i miss her more than anything
i wonder if she is okay?
