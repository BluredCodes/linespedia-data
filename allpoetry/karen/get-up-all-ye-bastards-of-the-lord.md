Get up all ye bastards of the Lord
who never knew your Father. Never
felt His arms, nor held were you
against the rumble
of his chest- never
mind the fear,
come sneer the night. Come snatch a 
newer sun. Run on coltish legs. Dance 
a morning dance around the winter
lilacs, 
yes,
the bush
is frozen but 
the buds are deep.
