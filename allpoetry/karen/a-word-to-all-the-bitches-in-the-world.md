Don't
fuck
with me. 

All my life
I've seen your kind
slither in and slither out
and make a mess of things

and never 
break 
a sweat,
a nail, a record without
riding someone's back
or front 
or knee- I'll tell you now:
you are 
the very

choke
of hardship, 

plaque
that clogs the artery, the gasp 
when breath is gone, the croak
ing raven and I'm armed here with the one thing 
you can't move, it is
my mind. It is 
the sharpest one you'll find 
and best of all, it has a laser equipped
accurate-to-the-innuendo

BitchScope.
