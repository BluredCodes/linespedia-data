title: "Hunkering Down"

January
brings cold feet, and hairs that stand up, 
turning skin to suede instead of silk-
I can't get warm, get
liquid-centered;
air around me has a slab and toetag feel.
Dormant as a bulb, I hunch here- January soil
like 
macadamed
street. 
Quiet as the tulips are
beneath a cracked
cold sky
I
wait for robins
