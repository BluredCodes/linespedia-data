---
title: "Hard And Soft"
writer: "Karen"
slug: "hard-and-soft"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/26712-Hard-And-Soft-by-Karen"
---

I am hard
to know.

I have a heart
that smacks
as well as beats
and teeth enough

still, 

to make a mark.

I've lost so many
important things
pinned like butterflies stuck through 
the bellies, wings
and still they slipped away

or asked to be released;
I guess 
Petra
rchan dark is where they'll stay
strumming a form
that's far
from 
real to me. 

I am not
velvet- I am flint. 
While velvet cushions softly,
flint will spark
and some, they need 
more nuzzling
than light.
