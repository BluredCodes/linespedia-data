---
title: "Certainty"
writer: "Karen"

He knew he'd 
squandered everything
and chased a thing
that didn't have a name
though he was certain that
he needed it.

At night he shot up straight in bed
convinced he'd heard a voice
that said, 

'Be cold, old man.
You've passed
the last 
fire."
