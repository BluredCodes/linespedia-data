---
title: "Find your own truth"
writer: "Pyli"
slug: "find-your-own-truth"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/41371-Find-your-own-truth-by-Pyli"
---

Open your heart, feel the love,
let your feelings guide your soul
think about who you are
and who you want to be
let yourself be free.

The other ones lead you exterior
but only you can lead your spirit…
teen wild and free thoughts 
should be the owners of your world.

Naive eyes, a shallow looks
a naughty smile and a sheet little hole
identify me in the world….
but who am I? Who are you?… who knows?
nobody really knows anything about anybody…

Don’t be insecure, don’t be shallow
don’t trust your whole life in just one someone
if that person goes…
you won’t have a history anymore.

Don’t let the people take you 
to somewhere you don’t wanna go
it doesn’t matter if you are afraid,
the fear sure will go away
when you start trust yourself...

Listen to my words:
I know showing my feelings is not my biggest skill
I know I almost never say what I really think
That I hide myself behind a fake reality
But this really comes from the deepest of me…
Think about what are you gonna do about your life
Like your real friend I just hope you take the best decision
But if you fail, just remember that I am here for you
Like I have always been… like I always will be
