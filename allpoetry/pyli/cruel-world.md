---
title: "Cruel World"
writer: "Pyli"
slug: "cruel-world"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/41955-Cruel-World-by-Pyli"
---

Trying to survive
in this cruel world
the sweets words
that used to mean a lot
now are not enough

Power and money
that’s all it counts
again, what a cruel world

I think about it
and I almost cry
we are all against each other
we don’t even think about the love
we are losing it
we don’t even try to get it back
we don’t fight for what we want
but i am only one soul
one lonely soul
in this damn cruel world

					26-10-99
