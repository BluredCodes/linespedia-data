---
title: "I didn't know"
writer: "Pyli"
slug: "i-didnt-know"
source: "AllPoetry"

I drove slowly just waiting for you
i looked back and you were not there anymore
i looked ahead and there you were
you passed me by 
hating me like always
and there i was
loving you like always...

It's a nightmare i said to myself
what it used to be a dream, 
is now my worst nightmare
living in the agony 
that you might fail me


You don't trust me, you practically hate me
i screwed up, i am sorry,
i didn't realize you have such strong convictions
i didn't know a gesture of love
would make you so mad and miserable
i didn't know my romanticness would make you unconfortable
i didn't know a couple of roses and a song 
would take tears of pain out of your eyes 
i didn't know a few chocolates instead of making your day
would ruin mines forever... I am sorry, i didn't know, 
i didn't know asking you to go out with me
would bring me problems with the law
i didn't know i was disturbing you with my presence
i didn't know i was bothering you with my gestures of love
i didn't know you hated to see me
i didn't know you didn't like me at all...


i am sorry, 
i didn't think, 
i just loved... 
i didn't know
