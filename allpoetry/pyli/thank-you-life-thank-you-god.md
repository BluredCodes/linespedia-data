title: "Thank you life, thank you God..."

You take from me
Tears of sadness 
but that's ok
regardless what people say
i won't hide you
you let go my pain...

I need you, 
you don't hurt me
i trust you
you are my confident
you know my secrets 
you accept me
you don't judge me...

i want you, 
every time i look at you
i see the most beatiful smile
i love it, 
as well as your sad eyes, 
your pretty face, 
and your giving heart...

I appreciate your words
they are so reconfortating
they show care, love, 
you are so patient with me
i like you so so much...

I thank god i found you
he heard my prays
and sent you right to me
I am so grateful with life
now i have everything
i have all i have ever asked for...
Thank you life... thank you god

Pyli 22/03/2002
