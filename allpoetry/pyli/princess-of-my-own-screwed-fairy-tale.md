Princess of my very own fairy tale
i think you love, you say you don't 
how can you love someone else
if you don't love yourself...


Queen of my own messed up kingdom
i think you care, you say you don't
the world can tumble all around you
and you wouldn't give a fuck...


Owner of my own inner world 
i think you like me, you say you don't
it's true, you have hurt me too much
you have made me miserable...


Guardian of my own fantasies
i let you into my soul
i let you got in my mind
and made me think crazy thoughs


Leader performer in my dreams
you come and go taking the best of me
i know this is all wrong
but what can you do 
when all you think about is love
the moment i found you
the moment i let you go...
