---
title: "Prove of Love"
writer: "Pyli"
slug: "prove-of-love"

You, my sweet dream 

I keep thinking about you 
I can’t forget your face 
I can’t forget your smile 
Your sweet eyes that make me shake 
Every time I look deep inside them... 

I can’t get you out of my mind 
you are just there , reminding me 
that I still have a void 
that it just can be filled 
with some of your love ...

I can’t talk, I can’t speak 
I have to keep all inside me 
I have to pretend a fake happiness 
when a hard pain in my heart 
is slowly killing me ...

I don’t know why 
but I can’t find you 
every time I think I’m close 
you go more and more away 
I just can’t handle this 
it’s confusing my thoughts 
its driving crazy my mind 
and all I do is think about you...
 
But I don’t care 
day after day, week after week 
I will look for you, and I won’t quit 
because some day, I’m not sure when 
I know I’ll find you, my sweet dream will become true 
and you won’t be just my fantasy anymore 
because you also will be a prove of destiny 
and a prove of love...
