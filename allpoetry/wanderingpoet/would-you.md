---
title: "Would You?"
writer: "wanderingpoet"

What if I told the ape to play nice?
What if I told the bird to fall into a cage?
What if I told her?
I don’t know.
Laughter rings in my mind
As I hold the black rose to my chest.
Crumpled and fragile,
The scent gone from it’s petals.
What if I told the rose to come to life?
What if I told the rose to turn red?
I don’t know.
Impossibilities enter into my mind.
My weary fingers reaching for the keys
As I type simple letters to the page. 
It is only a page.
It is only a program.
What if I told you I missed you?
What if I told you I loved you?
Even now.
Even now?
Would you laugh to?
