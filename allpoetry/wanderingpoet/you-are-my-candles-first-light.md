you are my candle’s first light
in an infinite space and time
born from a friction and our magical heat
slowly to take your place into rhyme

at first, a dim light spreads through the darkness
as you slowly start to give meaning to the flame...
As infinity continues, and hopes strive forward
you brighten within, until you are all I can see...

Finding ways through the winding darkness within my soul
your light grows brighter by the day
illuminating my path, reflecting in my life
so that  you are my candles very first light...
