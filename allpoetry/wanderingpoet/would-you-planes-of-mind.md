---
title: "Would You? (Planes of Mind)"
writer: "wanderingpoet"
slug: "would-you-planes-of-mind"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/47090-Would-You---Planes-of-Mind--by-wanderingpoet"

If I were to burn, 
Would you Ascend? 
If I were to crash, 
Would you rumble? 
if I were to rumble, 
Would you strap? 
if I were to scream 
Would you widen your eyes? 
If I were to release 
Would you breathe deep? 
if I were to strait 
Would you relax? 
If I were to blip off 
Would you rise and stretch? 
if I were to race 
Would you feel my power? 
If I were to stand 
Would you tighten your strap? 
If I were to open 
Would you enter? 
If I were to burn 
Would you fly with me?
