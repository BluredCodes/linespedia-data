title: "'go to sleep!'"
writer: "wanderingpoet"
slug: "go-to-sleep"
source: "AllPoetry"

I’m trying to 
Or maybe not 

For life is full of 
Beauty for which is sought 

Poetry and prose 
Tickle my mind 

As heavy gusts of fan 
And blades, impacted and resigned. 

Kind light and gentle sets 
Weld from passing molecules, 

Appear before exhausted hearts 
For leak, and break to repair the tools 

To stare at a fabric 
Of sleep’s deep desire 

Yet in twinkling continuousness 
Ideas and plots do inspire 

Simplest urges and reckless ideals 
Held waving and gasped and tossed to the fields 

Fearful for the mind’s eye 
Closed in rest, shall it be lost unsealed 

Times and words thrown 
Aside and sundered from peace 

Break forth from dew 
Within it thundered from east 

“Go to sleep” 
Demanded the master and host 

Yet obey I naught can 
Only to raise this toast 

“To life 
To Love 
Shall sweet embrace hold 

I shall sleep 
I shall dream 
After all tales have been told”
