title: "We Say (Untitled)"

Bits and bytes sent over steel nooses 
Yet no response 

Tireless hours Spent in tortures thrones 
Yet time ill wasted 

Waking nights staring at snowy pearl skies 
Yet agonized twists upon rumpled sheets 

And so goes the torture of two stared souls 
Whose’ fought and bled for times within reality 
Who’s playful ambitions turned to lover’s banes 
Who’s realities shuns the other, yet heart cannot release? 

We say we are distant 
Yet, can we know why? 
We say our vibes are evil 
Yet vibes are cured with a sole kiss 
We say we are in love 
Yet where are the words to follow? 
We say we are suffering 
Yet why do we not escape? 
      Escape to our own world 
      To our own reality? 
      Why do we always wait? 
      So say “I love you?” 
You say that I’m distant… 
And to open up is a danger 
Yet to open is to close the distance 
Before we become a stranger…
