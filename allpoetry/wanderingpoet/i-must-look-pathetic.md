title: "I must look Pathetic"

I must look pathetic
My Darkened eyes
My strings, sircles hung below
My shotty, unshaven face
My motted, grimy hair

I must look pathetic
My tired neck collapsing in air
My shoulders weak and shrugging
My even goatee
My Bright blue eyes

I must look pathetic
My slow moving gaze
My Deep, throaty tone

I must look pathetic
They gazed
They stared
She answered and smiled
I smiled and twinkled

I must look pathetic
She smiled and blushed
She lowered her eyes and glanced away
I dropped my pen
She looks up

I must look pathetic
I play with my pen in hand
She plays with her hand in hair
I glance over
She looks away

I must look pathetic
My words of debate
Her tones of argument
After words calmed
She strays from my gaze

I must look pathetic
She looks down
I sigh
I get up
To my room, do I leave to lie.
