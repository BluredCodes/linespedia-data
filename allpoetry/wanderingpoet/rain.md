Sometimes you can't stop the rain. 
Sometimes you can't shield yourself 
from the constant onslaught 
of stinging needles 
as they plummet from darkened skies. 
 
Oh, you can shield your body 
from these pains
Yet never your mind. 
No matter how wide 
the proverbial umbrella is 
in this wicked night, 
the drops still impact your clothes, 
impact your skin with every stinging letter.
With every scorching breath. 
 
Sometimes you can't stop the rain. 
As the innocent droplets 
transform into icy cleavers, 
shattering your delicate heart...
