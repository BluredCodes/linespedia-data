Sometimes the darkness pulls you in. Sometimes the night is all you can see. 
Sometimes when visions and attempts at peace follow your path as in a blind stupor, they fail. 
Fictions Friends and Trusted Strangers reach for wanderers along pitch paths
Yet beyond them they stand
Beyond them they rush along into the piteous void. 
And the one who can stop them
The one who stands between them and oblivion…
Turns their back
And walks their way…
