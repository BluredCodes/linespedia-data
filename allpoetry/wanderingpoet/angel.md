upon a window's shattered night
upon lightened crevices neath door's floored distance
comes darkness's first hope.

upon talon's first scrape
upon wicked sneers and jabs
comes faint brushes silk upon my face...

upon hated heat's first burn
upon salty drops deep travel
phantom hands shielded wounded eyes...

upon plunging edges and razored smiles
upon silent screams and begging whispers
comforting words flow into burned innocence

while bloodied talons and scarred blades hit their mark...
while hatred's words and vile concepts flooded the innocent mind...
Your hand was the one clasped to in the night
Your voice whispered words of hope into that child's ears...
Your fingers brushed away my confused moisture...
You arms wrapped about me, holding me as I cried in the night...
