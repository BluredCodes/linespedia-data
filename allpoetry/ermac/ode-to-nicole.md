I see her looking at me. I try to avoid her Brown-eyed stare.How many times have I seen her?How many times have I helped her?How many times has she helped me?Countless.She is one of the smartest people I know, yet her intelligence is subdued with a sense of beauty and humbleness.How I respect her.How I am grateful to call her my friend.
