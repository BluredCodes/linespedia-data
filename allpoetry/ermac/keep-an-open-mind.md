Keep an open mind
Because you'll never know what you'll see,
What your kids will be, or who you'll marry.
Cause once you're in, there's no chance to flee.
What is to be will be, and there's no such thing as being free,
and hell, maybe some guy will start a shooting spree,
and kill you, your wife, my son and me.
I just have to sit back and count to three, leave me be
as I rock on the edge of insanity.
This might be what you come to find,
so please try yo keep an open mind.
Even if you can't see that you are yourself,
and in being you, you at least resemble me.
Screw all this stupid inequality,
