Don't you hate it when a hot girl asks you something, and you can only studder like a damn fool?Don't you hate it when your stupid little sister starts talking about your buissness at school?Don't you hate it when a pretty girl is trying to have a conversation with you, and you can get up the nerve to look into her bright blue eyes?Don't you hate it when you say something stupid and wish you could take it back but you can't?Dont you hate it when you have one of those perfect moments with someone that you love, and you only hate it because you know it could never happen again?And dont you absolutly hate the fact that you'll probably end up having all of these things happen to you again?
