title: "How to Drown the Water"

Floating limp under the crashing waves of the sea.The sea the represents what?Emotion, Religion, Culture, Economics.The ways of the world.Power-less to do anything,consciencely UN consciencestraining so hard, but UN-able to do a damn thing.I'm sinking faster now,a burst of light strikes my eyes.The water is gone and I stand on land.Suddenly, I can't breath, fighting for air, drowning in the oxygen.Losing my sanity and I dive back in.
