To Break the CycleI will follow you.I'll do whatever you want me to.I'm not a follower, but you're my leaderI'm entwined to you and it's getting deeper.To Stop the Cycle,I will break these chainsI will douse the searing, blazing flamesthe flame that cauterized the batchelor gameand is the origin of my guilty blame.To Reverse the  Cycle,I will break my mold,I will release the grip on my heart that you hold.I will take back my self-controland emerge from my pitty-darkened hole.
