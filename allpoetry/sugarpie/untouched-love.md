---
title: "Untouched Love"
writer: "sugarpie"
slug: "untouched-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/43098-Untouched-Love-by-sugarpie"
---

When I think of our hope,
I know it won't end,
And the endurance won't stop.

Craving your love,
every night and day.
Wanting you more than ever.

Waiting to feel your touch
of you gentle lips,
And just to be with you.

You know how much I want you,
and to feel your loving body
touch mine.

Stay with me forever,
I'll make it all worth your while,
I'll never stop loving you,
No matter what we go through!
