You said it was love,
You said we were meant to be,
You said you would never leave,
You said you would never break
my heart.

But it's all over now,
See it fading slowly,
All the lies down the drain.
The memories of our love
floating high above your head,
But I guess your just stupid
to understand how much they meant.

Can'tou see I Love You,
I would walk a thousand miles,
just to be with you just one
more day.

But I guess that won't happen,
It was just another one of my dreams!
