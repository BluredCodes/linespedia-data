---
title: "Broken Love"
writer: "sugarpie"

You said you loved me,
You said we were meant to be,
You said we would be together
for eternity.

But you lied,
You broke my heart,
You stole the last breath 
from my mouth.


You said another girl was 
what you wanted,
Unsure if that was the right
thing to do,
But you left me without no clue.

Waiting,
Hoping,
Praying for another day to be
with you.

But I guess that won't happen,
Because you loved her more than

you knew.

Just friends you said,
But unsure of that too,
Wishing that someday I 
will be again with you!
