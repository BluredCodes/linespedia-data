---
title: "Forever"
writer: "sugarpie"
slug: "forever"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/41313-Forever-by-sugarpie"
---

To feel your warm, 
and gentle kiss.
Makes my heart, 
feel with bliss.

To feel your
magical touch,
makes my heart
start to rush.

I never met 
someone like this.
If I ever lost you,
I wouldn't know
what I would miss.

Thinking of the 
relationship
we have made,
Hopefully our love
will never fade.
