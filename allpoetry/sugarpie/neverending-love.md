---
title: "Neverending Love"
writer: "sugarpie"
slug: "neverending-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/48770-Neverending-Love-by-sugarpie"
---

One glimpse at you
made my heart melt.
I couldn't help myself
about the way I felt.

You made me cry
becuse I couldn't
see you.
I knew our love was true.

Then I called your house,
asked if you were there?
You got on the phone,
I knew there was 
something wrong.

You said you had fun
in everything we did.
But this was the end,
nomore we,nomore us!?

I didn't want to cry,
so my face was dry.
So confused,so lost.
What could I do?
Now that I had lost you?

You were so precios,so kind,
and you even blew my mind,
about a hundred different times.

But I guess you'll live
after the heartache you
have caused me!

So now I'll go,
Please don't say nomore.
You were my life,
but now I don't have one.
So goodbye,
see you sometime soon!
