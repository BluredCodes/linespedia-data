This one goes out
to all who were on the verge of giving up
And were rescued by love
Saved by someone who really cares

Something everyone should know
A life lesson
Something thats been said before
And will be said again
Is that if you never do anything in your life
You have to find love
If you're a man, you don't have to find a woman
If you're a woman, you don't have to find a man
You can if you want obviously
But just be true to yourself
Love the ones you love
Love doesnt have to be all sexual 
It can be the love for your mother or your dog
Or love for Jesus
But everyone's got it in them
Everyone was born with one purpose
To love and be loved
It's inevitable
If you let it happen
It's contagious
If you do your part to infect
I'm not the first one to say it
This isn't the first time you've heard it
But let this reassure you
That all you'll ever need is love
