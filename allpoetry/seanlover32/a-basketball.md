title: "A Basketball"

Simple and spherical
Orange rubber
Yet I change so much.

I am the perfect complement
To the game dreamed up
By Mr. Naismith years before.

With my rhythmic beat on the blacktop
I can bring you from nothing
To the top of the world.

I am a means of expression
Of Mr. Carter’s spontaneous creativity
And Mr. Jordan’s gravity defying talent.

Who would have thought
That I could bring Mr. Iverson
From the ghetto to his mansion?

Your kids depend on me.
I’ve distracted some from school
Yet I’ve kept some in.

I am influential.
I have diverted some from drugs
But I’ve helped some get theirs.

My heart is the beat on the court.
I have life,
And I can do anything.

How can I, a rubber sphere,
Be more powerful than
A book? A computer? God?

I am inanimate,
But I change the world.
