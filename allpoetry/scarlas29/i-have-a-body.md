title: "i have a body"
writer: "scarlas29"

i have a body-
it does all i ask;
it runs, it bends, it dances for me.
these are the small tasks,
what else could there be?
i have a body-
it does all i need;
my heart beats, my lungs expand, my brain thinks for me.
these are the major tasks,
special for me.
i have a body-
with waterproof skin, blinking lids and chewed nails;
that last one designed by me.
most tasks i do not need to think,
yet appear done are they-
everyday, for me.
thank god, for I'd die-
how creative that man must be.
i have a body-
with emotions designed especially for me;
my heart falls in love, my soul can weep-
all this for me?
how beautiful my machine-
how blessed i should be;
to have curly hair, easy muscle tone-
all gifts to me.
let's think-
how do we repay,
the kindness god gave?
we mock ourselves, belittle ourselves-
just because we care?
well my mind thinks,
words shouldn't be wasted...
i have a body-
and it does what i ask;
my muscles hold the pen, my mind does the imagery-
words are the power, my poetry takes shape.just a thought...comment away!
