as if you might care-
 as if the wind swept,
 intelligence to thee.
 so many days i wander-
 aimlessly.
 i seek the knowledge-
 i seek to find,
 the ones who love me,
 who treat me kind.
 as if you care-
 you whisper lies.
 i seek your help,
 but i run and hide.
 cos never of use-
 are you to me.
 for the wind did not-
 give intelligence to thee.this is about the search for those friends who understand and love you...and the fakeness you find along the way.
it's  a new approach for me, how's it sound?
