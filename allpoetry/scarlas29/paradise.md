see the palms-
 the light and joyous breeze.
 great voices sing to me-
 surrounding me is beauty.
 nature's scent is so clean,
 touched by the hand of god.
 so beautiful this is-
 with all the greenery.
 the sky & the earth meet in great harmony.
 the lapping of gentle waves-
 crash upon the breast of sand.
 singing tropical neighbors-
 a garden of natural splendor.
 the beauty of a dream to me,
 my imagination leaked out before me.
 the gentle swaying of palm fronds-
 create a light musical beat.
 the paradise creates it's own sweet blend,
 oh so beautiful nature can be.
 away are worries,
 far beyond me.what do you think?
