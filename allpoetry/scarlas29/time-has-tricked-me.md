title: "time has tricked me"

how it seems-
time has somehow tricked me.
while here,
i could not see-
the mystery around me.
you evaded me.
w/the shadows-
i now see,
all the beauty-
that could now be.
time has stolen you-
and now, how i need you,
because my life seems tricky,
and confusion fills me.
so alike we think,
and your help, i do need.
but time has tricked me-
and i do wish,
you were here w/me.
for a daddy, i do need-
and i know you would be,
this saddens me.
i think of how it could be,
of how less i mentioned thee-
the little time we got to be.
finally conscious-
of how we could share time,
and how you could have saved me.
but time tricked me-
did not let me see,
the great father you could be.this poem is for my father who passed on (5.29.99) i am now realizing how much i need him to guide me; as we were so much alike and i know he'd help me w/all my confusion. he left @ a time when i had no time to appreciate real things, real family.
