My love I have confessions…
I am not always strong,
I am not always sufficient.
But I trust my love,
Know it has no boundaries,
When It comes to you.
My love…
I am quite often weak,
And your assistance I will seek.
You’re the only one I trust w/my soul evaluation.
I love you so much,
And in our tribulations,
I’ll lean on you.
Do not have fear,
Cos I’ll never leave you behind,
Just have patience w/me.
It’s just the way I am, 
Please pardon me.
With all the wrongs I have,
I’m sure you’re wondering if this is right.
Do not doubt me,
I’ll always be yours,
My heart, my soul…
It consists of you.
My love is endless,
Though I’m crazy,
You can trust me w/your heart.
I’m not foolish w/jewels,
And you heart is a diamond,
My emerald sea.
I can be frivolous,
Quite absurd,
But I love thee.
Never have a doubt about our love,
Cos there is not doubt to see.
My heart is open for you,
Open for you to see…
This is a first for me,
So treat me gently,
And you’ll see:
Our love will blossom,
Into a lovely paradise,
