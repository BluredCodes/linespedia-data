title: "star"
writer: "scarlas29"

---

i watch you from the shadows,
so much better-
you are than me.
my thoughs quite evil,
you sense the feelings-
good for you, cos you're the star;
some thing i will never be.
the limelight surrounds you,
your actions so perfect...
i'm beaten down w/negativity, there i'll remain.
shadows cover my face-
tears hide in my eyes,
a glisten of light-
the glint of HATE.
i run in circles trying to compete,
such a waste of hope-
i must be.
you're the star, something i cannot be-
that occupation's taken; by the best,
a thing i'll never be.
my fists can be clenched-
aggravation envelopes me,
my hard work never meeting goals-
this self pain, you'll never know.
the loser is me, not you dear,
in ways i suppose i love you-
cos you're everything i want to be,
not a fact i can face, easily.
you're amazing, you shine so fiercely;
i want accepted and the star thinks-
i dislike her.
she's the best she can be-
so maybe this isn't for me.
i can bitch,
but the best must come from w/in.
maybe i'm empty-
and she's full of excellence.
she's the star,
she's what i want to be.this is about looking jealousy right in the face...after being provoked.
is it overall alright?
