another bad day-
stacked on the ladder.
the ladder of her life,
a life she does not lead-
for a life she would lead,
her happiness glowed from with in,
some time ago.
the darkness gouged her deep in,
it destroyed her.
so unhappy, no smiles-
for all she claimed,
deep with in her now shattered-
stolen by the SIN.
the sin that corrupted-
that darkened her soul.
never the same will she be-
clear it became on a foggy, cloudy night;
when the demons danced-
their dimples shined of evil.
following and pouncing,
the prey gave in...
she cried to the lord-
take me far from here.
torn were her fantasies-
as her innocence disappeared.
the morning after-
the depression began.
her body was empty-
torn was her gift.
the gift she could of waited to give,
but cruelly it was taken-
no consent given.
and days wear on-
slowly, she gives in.
not holy enough?
she'd question.
where was my lord, he forgot me?
her face clouds over-
unhappiness bursts from within.
lost and confused,
sadness will stay-
someday it'll cost her,
she'll be taken away.
her own lost confusion-
of her self-worth,
the destroyer of her being.first off, this is NOT a TRUE story.
tell me how it is, be critical.
