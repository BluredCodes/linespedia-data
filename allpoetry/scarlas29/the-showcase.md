put me in a glass showcase,hope and pray I'll stay in place.all this prettiness surrounds me-light shines down, penetrating me...expect me to display my imagination,but bad relations destroy me,can't you see my soul is free?that not all is beautiful,for life is handcrafted-no blueprints a necessity?and you will continue to defy the norm,for you are you, and you can like what you see...look upon the mirror, for it gleams-and all reflections, quite distorted.look and go as you feel,Cos what you feel, comes from within-trust you soul, not the showcase;smash the showcase, let it shatter,live your life the way you feel-that's what counts, let love live,don't let them control you, for you are so special.so let it shine thru,in your own mind be perfect-for what true person can disagree?How's it sound?
