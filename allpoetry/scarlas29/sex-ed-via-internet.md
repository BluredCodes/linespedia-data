Cyber dreams can connect me,
But never intellectually.
Sure they know the basics,
But are they really interested In me?
Never so it seems,
All superficial realities and twisted visions-
For your soul to see.
They don’t care about your problems,
Just the pornography you can provide-
With your words creating fictitious visions,
For them to get high…*ahem*
How sad it can be-
This technology provides,
A connection to different worlds.
It always seems to boil down to one thing,
The simple little sex that teeny boppers-
“know” they need.
Puh-leez!
They ask you how you’re doing,
Or maybe where you’ve been,
Ask your favorite color,
or how many guys you’ve been with.
This creates a sickness in mentality,
Of how pathetic Americans, Asians and whatever can be!
You look for mental stimulation,
But always it’s about-
“hey baby…”
when will it end?
As a teenage girl,
The pedophiles are endless-
Need not forget the 10 year old men…*hehe*
How low can it go,
When will it stay behind closed doors?
Where most of this needs to be!
Never wonder where you child learns a word,
Just venture to teen rooms-
Where colorful language is a gift to receive free!
The definitions endless,
For so many words appear…
All about intercourse (not the language kind)-
The internet is wonderful,
New discoveries everyday…
At a loss,
In need of a new position?
Just try a chat room,
Or aka kamasutra.net, it seems.
But that’d be too obvious,
They’d rather you discovered a free education-
And channeled new meanings…or phrases…(lol)
So just enjoy your high speed connection-
And prepare yourself for an education beyond belief!
