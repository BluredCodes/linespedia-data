the feeling of hatred & anguish,
how much you cause me.
how can this be?
i don't know-
how caring feelings can resume,
but hatred grows.
these feelings ignite and i cry.
stirring beneath my eyes-
i know i love you,
so how can i feel this way?
words thrown from my mouth-
hoping to stab your soul.
instant regret, yet sick satisfaction.
i see the pain in your eyes-
and a flicker of sadness.
how could i say that?
yet the fires still burn.
you, half of my soul,
i have hurt me!
i love you & you love me-
but ravaging pains run deep.
our eyes meeting, no words are said...
to where do we go?
picking up pieces, needed apologies said-
ever so hard may they be.
the piece edges cut deep,
our souls bleed together, hands meet...
this encounter must find meaning,
for our relationship grows strong.
but still we know-
those moments must come.
we can release fury,
but feelings must be considered...
and love will overcome...this is an older write, what do you think?
