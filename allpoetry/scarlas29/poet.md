i write what i see,
i write what i believe.
my words,
on a page for you to see.
i've bared my soul-
left my emotions for you to view.
i thought them thru,
then free flowed thru my fingertips-
they came.
my ever flowing memoirs-
concluded on to page,
you may judge, you may praise.
no matter what-
they will stay,
forever as memories of me.
small pieces,
only i could write-
for my experiences spoke free.
they needed said,
i enjoy what i write.
it's an art form,
something delicate-
that few receieve.
it's the gift, the power-
of words & ways to say it.
it is poet,
a beautiful word of thee.how's it sound?
