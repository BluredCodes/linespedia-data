---
title: "illusions called fantasy"
writer: "scarlas29"
slug: "illusions-called-fantasy"
source: "AllPoetry"

twisted curls hide my face-
see the exterior,
and the interior holds wonder-
that many fail to see.
i am me with a vengeance-
a vengeance of truth,
what i always want to be.
i never want to grow-up,
well, stop dreaming of what i CAN BE.
toughened muscles cover me-
to strengthen my dreams,
of the many things i want to be or do.
i want the truth of rebellion-
the rebellion to always live my dreams,
the dreams of my inner illusions,
an illusion i call MY realities.
my reality grows with in me-
fueled by desire,
and the aching to be creative-
deny them all the ability to hold me.
i want to craft a reality-
that will astonish,
make them wish-
they could expand and dream their reality...
where their dreams can EXIST.As usual, how's it sound?
