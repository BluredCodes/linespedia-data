---
title: "my love's truth"
writer: "scarlas29"

why my conscience betrays me,
visions of you steal my breath away.
your soul, penetrates me,
for you are a saviour to me-
but in forms you haunt me,
for good things seem not to last-
for me.
on tippy-toes i beg,
let this be true and last-
FOR ME.
but w/kisses worries fade-
out of view i fear,
but in my heart you remain.
just my insecurities, excuse them-
for i am still me.
just another problem i face,
so never doubt my love's truth-
for always yours, i shall be.just wondering how it overall sounds.
