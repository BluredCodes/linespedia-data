---
title: "fantasy of reality"
writer: "scarlas29"

to me, for keeps.
you sealed it with a kiss.
forever was a promise,
never to be broken.
i grew, i cared-
solely for you.
our world became sweet,
so beautiful-
an image of dream.
only us, no one else-
could approach,
our fantasy of reality.
to you for keeps.
my heart,
you may take-
in your hands to caress.
please savor my soul-
and treat it kindly...
