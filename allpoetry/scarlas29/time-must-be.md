title: "time must be"

time creates it wrinkles-leaving them in your head,to pull out and read again.wonder & amazement-we were once this way?how it all changes-was it better?was it worse?can't tell for sure.time hides it's secret-vast knowledge, it does seem to have.i will never be the same,in many ways-I'm so glad time takes things,so i will never be the same again.though some will cry,and miss many things-that's the way it must be.just the regular critiques hopefully!
