how's it going to be,
where will we be-
in another 2 years;
will it still be the same?
completely the greatest?
a purified thought-
the love you've given me,
it truly released the turmoil of my soul.
i've loved you honestly-
can't deny the truth,
(not that i've wanted to)
and i know i want you forever.
we've made it this far,
(people had doubts about us)
but what do we care?
we care about our journey-
and the life we could someday grasp.
never wander form this unity-
i'm completely in love w/it.
i love you so much-
you're an angel that completed me.
where we'll be in 2 years-
don't matter much now;
(though we've dreamed it all out)
cos we've got the now-
and love is our key.
your thoughts i value-
you see a future, a future you love;
as much as you love me.
another 2 years i cannot wait to see-
don't want to rush it,
(cos i know it'll creep up)
i want to keep you sacred in my heart-
pure in your form,
perfect completion-
to my soul...
in 2 years-
we'll be more in love,
than we could ever be.
(here's to 2 years)the ending is what i was wondering about...does it complete the poem and overall sound right?
