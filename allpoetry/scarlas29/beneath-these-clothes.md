title: "beneath these clothes"

Underneath these clothes,
 I am quite vulnerable-
 But you take the time,
 The time to realize-
 I am me.
 Underneath these clothes,
 I am naked to the bone.
 These clothes conceal me,
 But only you take the time-
 The time to see,
 The real me.
 My nakedness revealed,
 The curves in my shape-
 Displayed before you.
 Long before this-
 We talked and questioned,
 And took the time to see-
 All the real love before us,
 That time would help us see.
 Underneath these clothes,
 You are naked-
 Naked and vulnerable to the bone,
 I look at you in a different light-
 Noting how I love thee.
 Beneath our clothes-
 We consist of skin,
 And our power is gone and we are exposed.
 Exposed to our love, our devotion, our craving-
 For physical love that we’ve dreamed.
 We love each other undeniably-
 Endlessly.
 We talked,
 We caressed.
 Our clothes lay strewn,
 Surrounding us-
 As the layers fell to the ground,
 Like our clothes at our feet.
 Our feelings exposed as we kiss and gaze-
 Loving all we see.
 Because love knows no imperfection,
 And this is fine by us-
 For we see only perfection in our eyes,
 As our bodies reflect back and forth.
 Tis great to find love,
 And be exposed-
 Knowing perfection as we venture on,
 Remembering this night forever.
 The night we lost fear-
 And did what was right for love,
 For beneath our clothes-
 The secrets of that night lay sleeping,
 Where we remember hands tracing skin-
 Eyes basking in glory,
 As true love was revealed-
 Beneath our clothes that night.
