when i awake,
 and i find your gaze upon me-
 studying me, loving me with your sight.
 the feelings that arise within me-
 stir with many passions.
 i look back into your eyes,
 and you see love reflected there.
 the love we share-
 strengthens me in my weakness,
 for i know you're always there...
 knowing this sets my soul ablaze and as our lips meet-
 i melt.
 our halves meet-
 the soul is complete,
 never so sure of anything, except this.
 this kiss shares more feeling-
 than i ever could imagine.
 the most amazing thing we share-
 and we'll share this forever.
 the last words we say;
 before we drift off...i love you,
 and i love you forever.i wrote this awhile ago, just wondering if it sound good.
