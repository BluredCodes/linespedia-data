Yesterday I awoke in life with a smile,
The day seemed to shine a misty gold dusk,
Sparkled and clean,
I walked for a while to breath fresh and pure,
As I moved amonst the trees, they seemed to lunge gracefully to my presence,
The world was weird but I was not,
Truly a different day,
Calm manouvers,
Then I moved faster and ran with the wind,
It caught me and sent my soul flying brisk and smooth through the air,
It was then that I realized how far I had gone, 
It was not just another day,
But rather a day of revelation,
New sight, 
A day of height fantasies and new origins,
It was my past that held me still,
Tight and conformed,
Now I had new reason to live,
Mind eyes returned,
I was born just yesterday,
Twenty one years after my birth,
And tommorow I will be born again,
And every day forth,
For I am a poet,
And this is my life,
The infinite,
I become everyone,
I become everything,
With words that will flow in a passive cool motion in the ever songs of God,
For I am free,
And my freedom will live forever.
