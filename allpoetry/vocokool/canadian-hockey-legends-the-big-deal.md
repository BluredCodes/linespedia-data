Bob and I used to sit on a bench and talk hockey,
Wondering if it existed in heaven,
Former bruisers on line four, we were the cocky,
Bob was Kiler 24 and I was nutty 7,

Then came the deal between old Bob and I,
Let us tell the other if there is hockey in heaven after we die!

So months passed by and the tragic day came when old Bob fell,
Old Killer the fighter was sent to heaven, 
Thank God for not sending the madman straight to hell,
Now all that was left was me, old number 7,

Sitting on the bench thinking of good old times I heard a voice,
Bob the Killer had news from the heavens to rejoice!

"Nutty 7 I have news for you!" 
"There is good and there is bad." Said old Bob ever so blue,
Curiously I said "Well good first and bad last is what I choose!"
Bob said "Very well then ,hockey exists in heaven...... 
And I called you up so my team doesn't lose"I know it's kinda cheap but I love hockey! I AM CANADIAN!!!
