The Sitting Genius:

Yesterday breakfast was made of gold dust and color candy,
  I live in Picasso dreams,
I mold myself into the abstract of my sub-conscious,
  Small town man with cool words,
It is a new world here,
  One, and many ones,
These days are fresh brushed and painted cool,
  Sky music and energy blasts,
In the middle of nowhere,
  A star is born,
And you will never know,


The Hollywood Fame Walkers:

Today I blushed my face and painted my scales,
  I live in spotlight orgasms,
I melt myself in golden statues and crack sugar,
  City man singing repetitive stale songs,
Phony universe,
  Alone and confused,
Dusty dark black days filled with a never ending,
  Burned skin deep and pale faced,
Crammed in the crowd without a breath,
  Born lucky,
And never knowing.


Money or Mind?

Simple choice.
