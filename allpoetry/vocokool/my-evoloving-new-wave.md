She is fire walking....creamed
   (MADE GENIUS)
Poison is her game
  Fame 
 Pill lady and grass strong...moderate
Swing thing and cool beats
    Whipping out words like a lighting flash from hell
Her pen is ink blood and grey toned; Gleaming true 
  Love twist
Gelled and tuned
    Cranking arms...snapping
 Fresh tool
Adoration
