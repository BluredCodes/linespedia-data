I am turning mad searching for you
           Bottomless
Hard faced and fuzz eyed
     Blurred swarm
Rum and water....The easy mix
       Cruel(Heated)
I want to let you go
          Impratical
You are there but I cannot feel
       Damaged
Wait...you can hear me.
            ~Real~

And then the bastard genius spins me again.
