I search and find nothing,
Digging to the bottom,
Where is it?
Shovelling and sweating,
I fear no pain,
I scream for passionate words of eternal serenity!
I am pale and pounded with drugs,
Further downward,
Flying upward,
I am real and in the insane,
It is there, I can feel it.
Fingertips,
Tip of the tongue,
Point well taken.
