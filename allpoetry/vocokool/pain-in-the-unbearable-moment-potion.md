I am enraged in the fury of my write,
  My pen is invisible!
 I scream and wail in the voice of maddness!
Make me real....Un-real! New, and used.
  I am everything and nothing...Floating and insane.
Pale eyelids placed on the barbeque..sizzled
 My blood rolls of the paper.
   Another love poem.
  I love being BEAUTIFUL?
