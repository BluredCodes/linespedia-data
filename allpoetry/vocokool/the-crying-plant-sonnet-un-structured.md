Count me mistreated...
I smoke marijauna
Sirens bounce in my head
      REAL
Bars black and strong
  Am I violent?
 Have I harmed?
   Justice is the devils smile....grinning MADman
  I have walked in dreams
     Jesus passed me a joint....God's plant...beauty
  Society believers erased you during the war on ideals.
    Where?
 Wrong?
 How?
     Momma's crying(tears of the confused)
  He never did wrong? 
 In a repetitive forum the aristos smash fists on "WHY?"
 Politicians Grin.
