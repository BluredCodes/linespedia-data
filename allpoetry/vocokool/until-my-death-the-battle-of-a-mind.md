I love the reflection of myself in the wonder of the mirror,
It is the face to face with the many I have known over the years,
Crazy, insane, beautiful, sexual, masculine,feminine, passive, translucent, patriotic, cool, hard, vain, original, filtered, and eclectic,
To name a few,
Fearless in the path of the beyond I go forward daily,
Passion filled it never fails for the nuthouse to call just to "check up",
And when the game is over will the man in the mirror be there,
Will he have fought till his last breath,
Will he have reached the pinnacle of what is his infinite,
Will it be forever,
I hope.
