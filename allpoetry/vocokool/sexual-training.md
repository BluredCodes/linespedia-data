It is slow motion time.
       Rhythm,
    Cool misted breath on skin bubbles blow gentle on the neck,
Tongue to skin,
   Drenching,
        "Making"
Sucked up,
     Passive smooth thrust tasting,
  Deep in soul,
        The merge of one,
    Sweat,
          Volume,
  Silent stare.
