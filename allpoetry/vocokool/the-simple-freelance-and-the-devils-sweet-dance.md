In an instant my mind becomes a tool of the infinite,
It reveals itself as gentle Mona Lisa with a kick,
I am transformed into a panic-stricken bear with the heart of a cool lamb, 
Perhaps it is the mind that is the beauty of it all,
And I, the blunt one, become that of what I can be,
True to that of which is.


E.J.Karlson 
December 4th, 2001
