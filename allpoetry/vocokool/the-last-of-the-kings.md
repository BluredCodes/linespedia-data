title: "The Last of the Kings"
writer: "VocoKool"

He sat on the throne of the un-merciful
A king forever
As he sipped his golden piss he was struck (HARD) 
White faced and down
Mirror
The people scream "Help us or let us DIE!"
Quietly thinking
Horrid ideal
A laugh....terrified
Decision time

Die
