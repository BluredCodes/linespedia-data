Scraping...Draping and exhausted
Melancholy
Sour ORANGE?
  I am truly tired of this shit...
Paid and RaPeD ( but not known )
    Stressed best hate people; During on time.
Cloudy faced and fire belly
       Clocks tick, tick, tick
   Uniformed black and cold. SO COLD
 Yes Yes Yes Ok Ok Ok Thank You Good-bye....I want to kill you.
Bell finish, beer down,
  HAPPY HOME.
