It is sweat drinking and smooth bay oil that flames the skin,
Rubbing, feeling and squeezing,
Hard, soft, slow, fast and smothering,
Licking gently and pasionately,
Sucking, slurpping and lapping perfectly,
Deep tongue,
And back around through and up and down,
Full, over full,
And into one another,
Staring inside,
Melted skin and eyes pouring to eachother,
Breathing, moaning and gripping,
To the final moment,
Screaming, pounding and driving,
And.......Locked lungs.
Exhale.....slowly and soft.
Fresh Kiss (Wet Lips) 
Small smiles.
