Again... another discovered... screamed the found mind,
  CoNfuSiOn...unconditional
 Famous person, Art and good Sex Candy
Blue paint in green skies and the forever games of "if",
   Crazed dances of my cock dreams became pale faced in the true realm of the aristo's phony song,

     I HATE gold roses. Truly lucky to know.
  Televison pictured pain killed the pages in my book.... 40 years ago
Time to kill motion and become the depth of grip.

  Fucking Fame.
