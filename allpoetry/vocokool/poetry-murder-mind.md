Until I have played my last passion scream
In the heads of my readers
Will I stop this life of crazed, dazed, rock world ways?
Never breaking the offensive slap
Spinning and dancing like a insane jester on speed
Playing the rythmic prowl solid and inflexible
Making the movement
Toil them to the marrow
Grind them through the stone
Ritual suicide fantasies
Crying for forgiveness
And then what?
Do I let them in?
Softly without a word?
Not likely.
