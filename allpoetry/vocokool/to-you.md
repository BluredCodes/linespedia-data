I am a pacified junkie whore,
Yield to the float I come back again and again,
Today is the last day of the words future,
Getting calm,
Move like the silky eloquent breathing sauna of hot fire, and daisy stretched sunshine sprinkle,
Cool smooth rhythmic powers of loves warm kiss in the slow motion action of paradise,
Smeared and slid underneath always asking for more,
I am a purring kitten,
The tender roar,
The confusion is less than I knew,
Clarified and understood I stand like a stuffed monkey in the midst of Town Square.

ã E.J.Karlson
