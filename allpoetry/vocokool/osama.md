Blind man walking into a burning fire,
Crazed, dazed and fucking insane,
Come on people! Join my game.

I swarm to kill and hide,
You hurt me and eye for eye,
My people scream and what do you do?

Come on new times make the better,
For the building has fallin and where have we gone?
A soldier lurks my head and my victory is done.

The next will finish and I will reign,
Hitler and I are walking pure,
This is forever and forever be.

All are punished for being free!This is an Anti-Al-Queda poem directed to fulfill my dream of 
a peace to reign.  Osama can go to hell where he belongs!
