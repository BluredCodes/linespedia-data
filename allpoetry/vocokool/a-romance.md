I sang a song of words to a tune that lives to love
And tomorrow will rain
That day is shining

For I have a flower to feed you
And is this the kiss?
 “Powdered”

It is deep and pale pink faced 
Massive taste buds 
in wine glasses of God

I want to crawl over your skin
I need the sensitive stare
And you can have the world

For it is nothing less that you deserve.
