Politicians are caring...so be it.

And the ride goes on.

They will rip your soul and sell it back to you (priceless)
They will kill your family and smile (hopeless)
They will spit on your grave (emotionless)

The devil shows more mercy

They will slave you and make you pleasure it "forever"
They will backhand you across the face "red hot"
They will sick a prisoner on you "pale-faced"

And you will pay them to do it.
