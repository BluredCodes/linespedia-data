title: "The Melted Writing Song"
writer: "VocoKool"

I am a concerto inside my mind,
  Playing a song like warm rainbow water,
Scraping for depth,
I write the beautiful,

Immaculate chocolate rose,

Again I scream for the passion,
  Coming around often,
I love this world,
  It is too easy.

Laughing.
