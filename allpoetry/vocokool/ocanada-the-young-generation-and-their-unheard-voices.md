I woke to a man screaming "Treason!"
RED and WHITE(My favorite flag)
Royal Canadian Mounted Confusion
As I raised my fist flying in the air I felt patriotic and pure
REPRESENTATION OF A NEW GENERATION
We are not against the enforcement...against the law...rather opposed
And the legal battle grows
A war of words
Sitting here peacefully and in respect I ponder
How to understand...from one to other
We stand on guard for thee and we stand on guard against thee
I love my country
But the translucent ideals fog thicker everday
Tommorow I will write again
Trying for the clear
Trying.
