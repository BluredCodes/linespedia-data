title: "Midnight Mind Screwing"

My eyelids are pulled by heavy sleep weights,
Lead Diamonds...
 I stretch my creaking arms and solid finger to release the pressure of hours of the still,

    Addicted

 My words never seem right anymore...I am lost
      Harder, smarter and smoke brain
 Thick smoke,
     I sip on the red and shoot the hard stuff,
   I drip drabble myself into oblivion.

     I dreamed once that I would never return,
   This is an extremely probable revelation,

 Gone and lost,

 I also dream of fantasy orgies with beautiful women with dark black hair,

I do not know...I will.
