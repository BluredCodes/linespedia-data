It was once that a fresh young man lived on the edge of the sea,
On rocks that beared no soul,
Harsh and jagged,
Like the boy, so alike,
It was here that he learned of pain that existed in happiness,
Itwas brought to him by the man of magic,
The man simply said to the young fragile mind "Let me feel your soul and give me a taste of that sea music!"
With loud tremors,
Huge,
He became the roar of the sea and the movement of his mind,
His options willed themselves unlimited and endless,
In the distance through the deep fog came something surreal, mysetrious and completely unknown,
And through this intervention became that of "Genius",
The man that broke through to the sea.
And heard no voices but his own.
