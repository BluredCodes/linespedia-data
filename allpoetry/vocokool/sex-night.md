Kinky laid land ladies,
      Hips and breast,
     P e a c h e s 
Not a kiss but a eye for play
  Jagermeister and cheap red wine.
 Half/half 
    Nude and naked
 Drunk boys and fun toys...not rough, not smooth
        Pillow cased and white fur sheets - bare
  One night.
     Good night. (exhale)
