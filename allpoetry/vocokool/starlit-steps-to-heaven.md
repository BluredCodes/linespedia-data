title: "Starlit Steps to Heaven"

I flail my souls somber new whisper up the stairs,
Stomp upon slide we feel like mother’s soft gentle breath in the cool tingled mist,
Making the song of god crackling in the beat of voices, we stop to listen to silence,
It is this riot of madness that melts our souls together like golden crowns and massive swords,
Blissful blatant induced trance in never forever lands, 
The tribal continues,
Have you ever flown higher than the universe?

 E.J Karlson
 December 31st, 2001
