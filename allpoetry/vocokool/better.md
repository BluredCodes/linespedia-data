title: "Better"
writer: "VocoKool"

I'm sick and tired of feeling sorry for myself,
  It might be time to move,
On,
Forward to a place,
  For the better,
 To help others,
   To help myself,
It is this choice or the end of my rope,
  I have to start new,
 I must play it cool,
   This is a nowhere approach,
I just want to be better.
