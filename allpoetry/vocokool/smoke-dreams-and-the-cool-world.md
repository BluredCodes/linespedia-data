To all who spit on me, a cool "Fuck you!"
    Smoked all night (Floating)
Heavenly love passion in the smoke dreams
  
      P E A C E F U L  &  L O V I N G 

Go drunk man and kill your next!
             Crazy red faced bastard on earth and in our face!

Roll a joint to cure the pure and make a new face!
Crawl to a land that screams New Fresh and Cool

And make it your own to treasure...forever 
For in this world possesion, power and greed does not exist
It falls to the bottom of your feet like ashes from the candlestick!

Just believe and smoke.I just smoked a huge joint so if my imagery sounds fucked up I suggest you do the same thing! Smoke forever! Freedom!
