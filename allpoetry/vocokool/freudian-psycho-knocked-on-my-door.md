Everyone seems to hate me,

I laugh the world off!

I cannot reach the pinnacle of success,

I laugh the world off!

My lover is not my love,

I laugh the world off!

I am sick and in pain,

I laugh the world off!

My father died,

I laugh the world off!

A nuclear bomb has dropped and killed thousands of my country men,

I laugh the world off!

You stare me in the eye and tell me that I am nothing to you.

I kill you.
