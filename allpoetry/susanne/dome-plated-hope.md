Dome plated hope



in the shiver of night



perched upon the wounded



tree limb of life





Desperate its struggle



amidst chaos and strife



when its foothold falters



and the spirit is smite





Dome Plated hope



in the shiver of night



perched upon the wounded



tree limb of life





Countenance shaken



in this perilous plight



evil's consternation



attempts to blind sight





In the aftermath of terror



the shock of a nation



lifts its prayers to God



in tears and frustration





Ahgast for the "lost"



this a multitude of souls



the cruel and sensless murder



of the flock within the fold





Dome Plated Hope



in the shiver of night



in stillness does abide



while the viper takes delight





It believes the fortress falters



the foundation torn to shreds



that its perpetrated travesty



implants the seed of dread



that soon twill reap and harvest



from its foul and fiendish plan



a cornerstone in heaven



while the infidel lay damned





And this will be the downfall



of evil's ghoulish plot





for what it perceives as weakness



in reality is not...





Dome plated hope



at the dawn's early light



renewed with sense of purpose



of what is wrong and what is right





It arises from the wounded limb



in  flight the silver dove



with blessings from the highest



This the entity of love
