title: "Grounds Pass"

Aimlessly I walk

In the damp and drizzly cold



Along the beaten path

30 minutes of freedom on a grounds pass


Eyes cast down

Autumn colored leaves make crackling sounds


No cadence found in my stumbling steps

Feet full of lead


Walking the walk 
                 of the undead...


Barely the strength to lift my hole infested head

Surrounded it seems by strangely colored cob webs


Body unfamiliar to me

Brain in a purple haze

Lips and mouth dry
                   from the "medicine" high

Into the silent monotony

I look up to see

Father John scury by

Left the church for a wife and home

Now speaks a language in a world all his own


20 years here
              a lifetime to atone... for his sins


As I wither inside

I feel the ache in his bones


Wind getting stronger

thunder clouds ahead


Fear Rolls

Clock on the tower says

it's quarter past the hour


Try to run but can't

Feet full of lead

Sweat on my brow
                 in big trouble now

Gotta make it somehow
                      back...to the cell
