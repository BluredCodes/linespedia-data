My love song

Off key it seems...

Yet I try so hard

to hold on to the melody

that I live and love both day and night


It's the pitch you see

Not quite right...

Ever so slightly off

a quarter or half note

to the left or the right


I fervently fight to find the key

to what once was perfect harmony

In the hands of the composer


Another tune will not do you see

                             not really...
My old love song

Forever steals the spotlight

This tenacity an enigma to me

Why I can't just let it be...


Crescendo calls to me

L-i-s-t-e-n...a-g-a-i-n!


Do the lyrics need to change

Or tempo rearrange

Or is it simply my deaf ear

the culprit here?
