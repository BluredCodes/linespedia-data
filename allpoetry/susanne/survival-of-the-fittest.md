Thrasonical soldier in shades of chartreuse

mimics the fetid cry of the spotted leopard

who clings by a thread to instincts incessant call...


Each hammered breath defys death's thraw

Rivulets of red sink into the earth's unquenchable womb

as sun sparks on burning sands.


Spare me the sound of death's cower collared in retreat

of man's chameleon manifestations of mind...


The infidel moves toward his prey in anticipatory celebration

Survival of the fittest saved for another day.
