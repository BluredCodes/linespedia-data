title: "Imperfect Love"

There within her thoughtful mind

in perfect imperfection lay love.

There, desire allows her mouth

to laugh at its readiness.


Giddy at the thought of touching him again

the fine smooth texture of his skin.

His deep dark eyes such mystery holds

and the entire world seems to unfold

for her, with him.


Oh, imperfect love,

Be not afraid to let her in

Loving you is not a sin


But must she always lose to win

and have to start over with you

from the beginning again...and again...


His soft honey brown skin drinks her in

and she lives once more with the

melding of mind, body, spirit, and soul.

Intricate mass of muscle and mind

A love like no other she ever will find.


Though frailities and flaws he does possess

        Passion Pure remains

An ocean could not contain her love.


While images of the past stay strong

the present the future do belong

To her Imperfect Love
