---
title: "Rape of the Spirit"
writer: "susanne"
slug: "rape-of-the-spirit"
source: "AllPoetry"

Born with the spirit and soul intact

a lifetime of promise ahead

but the evil forces of the underworld

have chosen to make you their bed


Evil incarnate spreads through the world

like a virus whose speed is unchecked

Yet everyday faces conceal many traces

its venemous spew to detect


By using the peole it's going to infect

a susceptible host does create

and once it has firmly implanted its seed

the portal of entry is ready to bleed


As it tortures the spirit of those in its path

You can hear the devil's chortle

and feel Satan's wrath


Through pain and through angish

through fear and through doubt

in anger and hatred

the poison spills out


Diabolic deception

Confusion of mind

Sins of the father

imprinted in time


And with each new invasion

a parasite at large

Evil's infestation

begins to take charge


It pounces on your hopes

and strips you of your pride

Soon a sense of altered self

in prison does abide


Cancer corrupt is now in control

as it feeds upon the goodness

lying deep within your soul


Its mission is to carry

a torn and tattered shell

deeper ever deeper

into the bowels of Hell


Will you become a cushion for Evil's ill bed?


Beware! rest not here

your worn and weary head

Forfeit not your soul

in the land of the undead


Remember that the power

of goodness, love, and light

can save your ravaged spirit

and take you to new heights


Grasp the hands of others

who were delivered from this blight

and understand the horrors

that plague you in the night


For rape of the spirit

to no crime does compare

as it crosses generations

and breeds anew despair
