title: "Flashback..."
writer: "susanne"

Time changes illuminated within a vortex
mind bending, heart pounding, knees shaking mirrored images
twirling breaking, crystalline shards whose pointed tips rip
through my skin - beginning to end.

Ominous rainbow with color so bold it blindeth the eye
and pierces the soul iridescent coal...
blackest of white or whitest of black am I purging forward
or am I falling back?

Thinnest of dense touch it were gone slipped...
through the fingers of images strong. The taste in my pores
as it bites through my tongue hot molten sulfur stench
compared to none.  A penetrating numbness now fills my very
being and I step aside to hear what my third eye is seeing...

Broken reflections from back and beyond are moving fast forward
amazing the speed!  yet in snail like increments learning doth 
proceed...a piece of the puzzle is now taking form who is this child
so battered so torn?

My arms will embrace her...

I'll hold her just right...

Calm and protect her from the phantom of her night.
