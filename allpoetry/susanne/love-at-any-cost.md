---
title: "Love At any cost"
writer: "susanne"
slug: "love-at-any-cost"

Her sense of self is molded

by the look in his eyes

Warning sign of a soul soon lost

Love at any cost

a high price to pay

wouldn't you say

The problem is she sees not

the error of her ways

For it's all she's ever known

Live in chronic sorrow

Give up all her tomorrows

For some crumbs of love today

Desperation speaks

Basic need

She needs to feed

By night if not by day

Wants to feel good

and if she's real good

Maybe he'll let her stay

But like all the others

he takes what he wants

then throws her away

"Dammaged goods" he'll say

Love at any cost

A high price to pay

Whatever the game

she'll learn how to play

and so - dulls the pain within

with a bottle of gin

crack cocaine

men who will pay

The dreams she had of

"being somebody someday"

fade away

Like her health and her youth

Six months later

her "friends" at the bar

now turn in disgust

call her a whore

call her a lush

Not their fault they say

For using her that way

Love at any cost

High price to pay

Mary found dead with a needle in her arm

Alone at the break of day...
