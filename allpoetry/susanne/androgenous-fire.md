---
title: "Androgenous Fire"
writer: "susanne"

I bask in the lure of a brilliant red glow

and the warmth all around bids my spirit free flow

Pleasant vibrations lay under my skin

As I let it all out

and take it all in...

The air crisp and clean as I sit by the fire

alone with the moon and a burning desire

Fantasy fed by the flicker of flame

emerges from two

but then one in the same...

Tis a dance unencumbered by mere mores of man

as it twists

             turns...

                      and takes me by hand


So I go where it leads without reservation

and satin weave dance to the dawn of creation

Serendipitous splendor sensations delight

a true garden of eden is now in my sight

Unspeakable beauty emit from twin suns

chaste in a prism

as two became one...

Immersed in the heat of this hypnotic haze

hermaphroditic proclivity now is ablaze

With feminine desire and the strength of a man

in bold display of passion at the middle I stand

Seduced by the ambiance I'm now a bird in flight

amazed by my perceptions as I soar to greater heights

This sensual serenity holds me in its grasp

and now this pristine progeny

a vessel filled at last...

Androgenous desire harbored deep within

androgenous desire under alabaster skin

Attribute assigned to the her and the him

lose credibility in the evolution spin.
