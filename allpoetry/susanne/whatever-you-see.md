title: "Whatever you see"
writer: "susanne"

whatever you see


you do not see me...



I am a spider web spun to perfection through frenetic
design an impermeable substance encases my mind.



whatever you do


you do it not to me...


the i in me has ceased to be


my skin has turned to alabaster stone
no detection of penetration from the branding iron.



whatever you say


you say it not to me...


i am not in your prescence


i feel my energy break through the striking lightning rod


i am a bright white light

whose iridescent rays splay in all directions

no longer in the confines of earthly projection


No blood not bound by ropes and chains


I am weightless and free

because God's loving hands are holding me



Whatever you see...you do not see me.
