---
title: "[What good is love]"
writer: "susanne"
slug: "what-good-is-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/51798-What-good-is-love-by-susanne"
---

What good is love

when confined to a minute space

where sunlight never sees its face

knowing it could be erased

and the world be not better or worse for it...

What good is love

if it slowly suffocates

from deeply bound and convoluted roots

which have nowhere to go

nowhere to grow

where the very essence of itself

seems to be its foe...

What good is love

if in its hunger pangs it cries

hearing only weary sighs

from the object of its affection

if in its thirst it parched

the very heart that gave it birth...

What good is love

if it holds me at arms length

then reels me in only

when its sense of security is shaken...

What good is love

if it hides in the shadows

fears all the tomorrows

holds its head in sorrow

and simply walks away...


Alas, no not I my friend

for like a tree in the willow

my branches always bend.
