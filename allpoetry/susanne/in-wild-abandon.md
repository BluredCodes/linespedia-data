---
title: "In Wild Abandon"
writer: "susanne"

Senses sharpened in razor thin slices

Sensuous throaty sighs

Vision narrowed in a broadening mind

Ecstasy awaits should you choose to partake...

Drink from the desert sun taste; what you desire


Wet your parched lips with the sea sand


Control is in your hands - your hands...


Speak the words wedged in your throat


Shout them in silence cloaked


Rest assured you will be heard as the echos of a great canyon


Sound streams in desolation

Pluck the roots of isolation


Elusive wisps of wanton cling to the cutting cactus

and capture the heart of you


Speak to me oh hollow winds

Deep dimensions lay within

Your fortress strong...


Touch the space around you

as it slips through your substance

and dances on flickering embers


Kneel to the mana from celestial realms


Flames of fire melt icy crystals

and warm life's blood consumption at the core

Carressing the essence of being

Craving more...ever more...


Only you can open the door


Hear the ethereal music of your mind

While in sweet release your spirit finds its way


Senses sharpened in razor thin slices

Sensuous throaty sighs

Vision narrowed in a broadening mind...


Ecstasy awaits should you choose to partake of the sublime...


Energy engaged in wild abandon.
