title: "Midnight Awakening"

Restless yearnings awaken me only to vanish
before I can see...hear...or feel the need
Yet I know it there; for it stirred within my soul
a soft rumbling of yesteryear.
Ghostly shadows amidst the twilight beckon me
to arise to a solitary dance
yet the melody escapes me.
Soon messages from another sphere
surround and bathe me in their surreal beauty and I,
in a state of inertia retrieve the vibrations
that bade my heart hearken and suspend my spirit.
In symbiotic union I exist
Submerged in warm transparent fluid
that rhythmically ebbs and flows as the tides.
I inhale slowly
deeply
and with every methodical beat of my heart
muscle fibers nerve endings
every inch of my being
aware of passions' hypnotic presence.
Serenity survives

Nothing to fear


Ah....but where do I go from here?

Adrift in a senuous sea of uncertainty
I acquiesce
and sweetly surrender to Apotheosis.
