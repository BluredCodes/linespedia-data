---
title: "Doorway to Bliss"
writer: "Clouded Nights"
slug: "doorway-to-bliss"
source: "AllPoetry"

The moon is a jewel, an ivory gift,
to the stars in the sky, leaving memories to drift.
My eyes explore, the beauty above.
And in return, it covers me with love.
Moonlight so bright, I can't help but stare,
as it guides me along, into the wondrous lair.
A lair I visit, when I just want to get away,
a place only I can go, every night and day.
no one to bother, to pester, or even to hear.
Only you, your thoughts, and perhaps a tear.
Whether it is a tear of love and loss,
or a tear of sorrow and hate,
the dreamer will never forget
how he crossed with fate.
