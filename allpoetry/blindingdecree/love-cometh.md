title: "Love Cometh"

Love is yet something we have to define
one mement there
so full and devine
the next as well as gone
the hope and dear
somewhat like a song
played gently to the ear
and yet so fickle
so plain indeed
for when it is gone
it is love to decieve
and yet when we tire 
of our loveless days
and Eternity is too short
To utter all thy praise.
another shall follow
make you weak in the knees
they somehow heard
all your sorrowful pleas
