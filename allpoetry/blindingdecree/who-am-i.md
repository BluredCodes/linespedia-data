---
title: "Who Am I?"
writer: "BlindingDecree"

I am a paradoxal weave 
I am a query of questions,
Oblivion of answers
I am an innate entity
I am everywhere you turn,
There is no running from meEven when I have gone,
I am not
Even when I am in front of you,
I am nowhere to be found,
Even if you scrutinize too hard
If you have any disregards,
I am right in front of you
I am a genre of emotions
Perhaps too isolated
I am an epiphany of your existence
A manifestation of your soul
A discrepancy of the frail
An exuberant thought of the tough
I am a portrayal of life,
A depiction of the worldA superfluous fragment,
To a known source
I am unfamiliar,
Unknown and alien,
To too many
I am celebrated,
Illustrious and fêted,
To too little
Who Am I?
