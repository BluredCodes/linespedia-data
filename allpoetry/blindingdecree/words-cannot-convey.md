title: "Words Cannot Convey"

Words cannot convey
What is happening to me
Unable to portray
My plights
Habitual discussions
Cannot condone
My choices and repercussions
Nothing to express
What is occurring
No way to run away
Just keeps recurring
No speaking with others
No correlating with friends
About what is going on
Unable to transcend
These diverse decisions
Now I lay here
Unable to communicate
Unable to corroborate
What is happening to me
