As I listen to the people around me
talking about one thing or another, 
the sound of their voices
is like a lullaby. 
But as I drift 
on this wave of words and thoughts,
I realize that somehow, 
I am able to seclude myself.
Even amongst their chattering voices, 
I somehow find a way 
to become oblivious to all that surrounds me,
and finally start to rejuvenate myself. 
I finally start to realize 
that maybe these incidents 
that have brought me down,
might not essentially 
have made me a stronger person,
but only more aware 
of the repugnant world around me.
