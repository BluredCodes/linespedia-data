---
title: "Ambiguity"
writer: "BlindingDecree"

frightning thoughts provoke the mind
leaving nothing but an interest in those provoking questions,
uncertain, beguiled, 
left there from years past
nothin more than that
no feelings left to threaten
no soul left to intrude upon
and yet you are left in the wake of that menacing wave
left you here high and dry
from years gone by
friends and courtships left behind
a niave young girl
left alone to fight the world
left to fight those demons in her soul
ambiguity her only friend
she moves along like the wind
nobody really knows where she will end up
and now her only friend
deception and superfluous emotions,
accompany her on her travels through the ages,
through time
accompany her while she strides along the shadows
leaving but a footprint in the ash of her soul
