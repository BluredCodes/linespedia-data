Just when i thought we had reached the bottom
A sudden feeling of overwhelming emotions
ripping through my soul
I thought you had left me
realized something i didnt see
Thought you were being distant
foreshadowing leaving me
One argument led to another
On tear after the other
Friends telling me its not worth it
friends telling you leave the excess baggage
where in this world would we be
if we had listened to them?
Our Love was Forbade
Yet through the Odds,
I said i loved you
