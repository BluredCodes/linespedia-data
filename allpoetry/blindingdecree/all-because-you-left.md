---
title: "All Because You Left"
writer: "BlindingDecree"

empty thoughts
empty wishes
empty soul
empty bed, 
ever since you left
nothing is the same
trying to find the words
shattered dreams
demolished thoughts
overthrown love
and suppressed feelings
tears sting my eyes
in a bitter sweet irony
that the very day you fess your love
3 months later
you relinquish your words
they hit me like an arrow
straight through my heart, 
and once again
its all over
empty thoughts
empty wishes
empty soul
empty bed, 
all because you leftI miss you, and I will always love you
