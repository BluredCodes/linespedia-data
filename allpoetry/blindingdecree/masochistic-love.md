Hurt me again
I want to feel that heartbreak
do those things you did before
neglect me
i want to feel that pain
express those condescending words
you seem to hold deep inside
make me bleed
make me hurt all over
tears stream down my face
yet hurt me again
I am starting to like the feel of pain
pretend im not here
I like to feel lonliness
then tell me im beautiful
and make that pain dissapear
how i despise your inner workings!
make my heart split in two
creep down beneath my skin
kill me from the inside out
then tell me you love me
go on!
hurt me like i've never been hurt before
make me wait by the phone
knowing you wont call
make me cry everynight
into a sympathetic pillow
then tell me how you will never let me go
Hurt me again
or i will do it myself
Masochistic Love
