Neglected

What happened to those words you used to say?
those sweet nothing, whispered into a dying ear
brung it back to life, so full so free?
What happened to those gentle caresses, left on an arm, a knee, a shoulder
gone cold and nowhere here
away from this place
full of disgrace
dont look into my face
now only pain, sweat and tears
replace the face that knew no fears


Ignored

and now im sitting here,
no calls, no emails
no messages no nothing
work engulfs the vast majority of a soul,
which my love once occupied
feelings died
a set of footsteps
left alone in the dirt
walk alone a voice said
light shown overhead
a man who makes you cry
is worth nothing in time

Rejected

nonetheless
alone she remained
her voice only heard in a whisper
dying promises drift off into the night
now a shaky hand, grasps for what isnt there
a meaningless attempt
to save what is slipping
the silence unbroken
no words here spoken

Forlorn

drift away to those emotions past
loves lost willingly
emotions flaring
overreactions daring
a lonely life, too scary
forlorn and alone
sitting by her phone
a call too distant
a wish made in an instant...


only to have him here by her side, yet at home, he still resides...
