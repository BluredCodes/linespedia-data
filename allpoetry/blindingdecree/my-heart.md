In my heart I know I will survive
and in my heart I tend to thirve
to thrive upon the love they so rarely give
and let the lust of new relashionships live
In my heart I will stay true
and live upon the wish you will too
and forvever I will be here
just incase you have a sudden fear
In my heart I try too keep it together
because I know these times will get better
that one day you'll be all mine
and our live will intertwine
And in my heart I still know
that even though you don't let it show
you still care for me
and one day maybe it will truely be.........
