I love it when your arms are around me
Make me feel safe...

I love it when you kiss me
make me feel loved...

I love it when you kiss my neck
make me feel special

I love it when you whisper in my ear
make me blush

I love it when you tell me im beautiful
make me smile

I love it when you send me notes on my phone
make me giddy

I love it when you tell me it feels so right
make me feel the same way

I love it when you call me
make me never want to hang up

I love it when you look at me
make me look into your eyes

I love it when you tell me you Love me
make me believe you

I love knowing you do Love me
Make me love you too
