title: "Uncertain And Beguiled"

Something in the air 

Something put it there

Now it will never leave

Has left me to grieve

Someone brought this upon me

Someone above the slightest decree

Now here to tarnish

Glaze it over with varnish

And cover her ugly core

Leaving but a poor decor

A shell of a young child

Now uncertain and beguiled
