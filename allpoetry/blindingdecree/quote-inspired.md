"It is not in the stars to hold our destiny but in ourselves." 
-William Shakespeare 

Unwavering labors
Useless, when in pertains to time
Uncertain, when it comes to love
Capricious, when it chooses a friend


To wonder of a fate to distant
In what her future holds
To accept the near future
And what it will unfold
Possible to be too much?
Confused yet not alone
Forgotten perceptions warped
Unhealthy, sickness prone
So what does a future hold
For someone so erratic
practical, and in a rut
and Nothing less than pragmatic?
