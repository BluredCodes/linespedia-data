I pray that cupids arrrow not miss me
for so many times the arrows came close
but so far from thee it would ne'er be
our names together cupid ne'er chose
I only wish for thine own lips to kiss
for that I'm willing to go through the strife
why does love just so very nearly miss
the ones who need it to hold on to life
for when I look in thou's wonderful eyes
eyes that are the true windows to a soul
I can just see the starry moonlit skies
yet it is my love you already stole
my hopes are still not yet all done and through
that somehow cupids arrow will find youI had to make this for english class in ninth grade, it took me forever i hope you like!
