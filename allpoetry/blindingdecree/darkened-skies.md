brightest skies 
nolonger seen
covered by coulds
covering me
nolonger do i see the stars 
how long will this storm be?
coulds rushing by and leaving no trace
leaving my soul
a big disgrace
nolonger do i stare up at the sky
and wonder what life will hold
for nolonger can i see
the blue skies
are now dark and grey
nolonger showing the sun
and its sparkling rays
skies of darkness
clouds of grey
my sould and i
left in desmay
