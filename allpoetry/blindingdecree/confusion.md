Confusion confusion confusion
Something unlike a realization
Unanswered query
Brooding suggestions
Somehow unimagined consequences
Leaving her with unabated fear
Piercing thoughts rung through 
So unwilling,
Yet appealing perceptions
Walking through a yielding pattern
Forever a torment of the soul
