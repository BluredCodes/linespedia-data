When you truly know the meaning of the word love,
You will also know the meaning of the word pain.

-Javan 


I knew the pain
of lossing the one i loved
and i knew what harm could come of it
i knew the day we went out
that some day the love would end
that one of us would be left out
and then when that day came
all those others could say
was forget about him
and move on
but once you know pain from a love
and yet the first one at that
you can't help but think about them
every moment of the day
everyday of the week
i will never forget him
nd the way he made me feel
how my stomach would do flips 
when he would kiss my kneck, 
how we would lay outside under the stars
and talk for hours on end
it took me so long
to give away my love
for the only love you get back
is the love you give away
yet i was scared to give others that comfort
i was scared my heart would be broken
so i kept it shut
in and air tight case
never to be opened my normal means
and then he came along
and changed all that
i will never forget
that he was the one
who forced me to love
he was the one
who loved me
he was the one 
who showed me 
the pain love can bringI wrote this one a long time ago, and it still hurts me to read it.., i always manage to be on the verge of tears, for i still love him so...
