title: "Tears Have No Meaning"

Its only a certain amount of time
When you life isn’t sublime

Before all the tears you shed
Over everything you dread

Starts to not mean a thing
And anxiety it only brings

When you begin not to care
If anyone was there

When you start to realize
You’re no longer a prize

When you yearn for to world to know
That everything you want to show

You thrust deep inside
Demanding to hide

What you once thought
What you once sought

Only brings you despair
And you don’t care

There is a occasion
Where feelings are an invasion

And tears have no meaning
Then life becomes demeaning
