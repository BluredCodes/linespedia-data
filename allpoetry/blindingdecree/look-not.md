look not into the depths of the soul
for there you will find love
look past those eyes
so deep and meaningful
leave them there to wonder
look not at the face that warms you so
leave it be and wander
seems this world is likely to do so too
not to be abound with excess pleasures
and to them you are nothing
a free thinker on your way
to self preservation
never again to look back upon 
those who kept you down
now take those rose colored glasses off
and enter the real world
won dissaster over triumph
free your mind
by forgetting all that binds you
to this shallow meaningless world
leave all those behind who seem to love you
make youself distant yet free
and in that forrest of nomads
thats where you will find me
