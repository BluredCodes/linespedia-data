The necklace
Once hung round my neck
Now lays broken
Into a solitary section
Charm missing
Just like our love
The charm absent weeks ago
He said it would be him
And so I cherished it
Swore I’d wear it eternally
For as long as my love lasted
I used to play with it, 
Fling it from side to side
I used just hold it in my hands
It was just like holding him
I swore I would keep it
Even after he declared
His love was no more
That maybe he'd be back one day
I said as long as i have this 
You know i will still care
Today my memory broke
I tried to save it
Attempted to keep it 
It fell
Into the deep abyss
Charm gone forever
Just like him
He left 
So did the emblem of him
That hung round my neck
Now when I go to hold it
It’s no longer there
Only a constant memento
That neither is he
More symbolic than before
His love is gone
And so is my necklace...
