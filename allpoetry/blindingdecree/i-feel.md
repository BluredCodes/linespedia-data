Overlooked
snubbed
neglected
shoved
spurned
distain
neglect
pain
forgotten
weeping
detached
needing
abandoned
left out
isolated
pout
omitted
helpless
alone
defenseless
ignored
mistreated
scorned
defeated
disregarded
disclosed
dispised
exposed
