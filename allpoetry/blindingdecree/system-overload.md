SYSTEM OVERLOAD!!
Brainwash In Progress...


warning!! you are about to embark on a new beggining,
new yet proseperous
you will work hard
neglect your friends
become the tightly sealed locked black box
that you were before all this
they are all just a distraction
you dont need them!
Step Two!
work work work
never look back
ignore thier pleas for help!
you've given them yours!!
ignore their hopeful phrases, words, gestures
Step Three!
dont even think about going back
who needs them?
you are about to set new goals for yourself
you are about to pass far beyond any of them
they distract you
they annoy you!!!
Step Four!
go ahead
wander on your own!
you like being by yourself dont you?!
do you like haveing no life?
sure you do!
why else would you be listening!?
why else would you act the way you did?
Step Five!
trust me
you will be there in no time....

why do i listen? what is there for me? in a world full of such confusion?! such ignorence! then why was I living there for so Long?! why did i allow the world to brainwash me?
