Lova, Lova 
where'd you go?
Dumped me for another hoe.
You say I got too much flava?
You've been sucking to many life sava's.
Your loss, not mine.
Cause yo colors are starting to shine.
You sorry son of a gun,
you made me fall asleep, like and old man your no fun.
You must have been my biggest sin.
Somebody should throw you
in the state pen.
You worthless
and no good.
Apart is how it should, be.
I must have been high
or was wanting to die.
I must have lost my freakin mind.
Goin crazy is my every day phaze.
And the time we were together,
I'll just forget those days.
