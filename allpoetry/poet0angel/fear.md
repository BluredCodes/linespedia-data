Fear,
it drives me.
i love to hear you scream.
The terror, the fright, the agony,
still I know I'm sane.
Fear,
it stings like a bee,
but approaches like a cotton cloud.
The whispers throughout the night,
the shrieks and the shouts.
The look in your eyes, 
is so deep and piercing.
Nightmares black and dark,
you wonder if you'll ever wake up
from this dream.
Fear,
it burns like hell's fire,
like snakes penetrating you with their venom.
And through all that fear and terror,
it doesn't scare you out of sinnin.This more has to do with a spiritual problem I'm havin.
