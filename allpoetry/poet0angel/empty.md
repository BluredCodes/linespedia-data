A heart is like a desert plain,vast in so many ways.An open road, an endless drivethat never seems to pay.Darkness falls in the heart so early,the shadows never seem to cease.The hypnotizing charm of a person,continues to make my mind bleed.Empty like a dried up lake,a heart can be seen that way.On a stretching ocean lost at sea,for days, days, and days.Sun-kissed faces are not to be seen,toward the end of days of you and me.A heart is and will always be,an empty, vast place to me.
