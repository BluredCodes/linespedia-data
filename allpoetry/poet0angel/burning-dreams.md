title: "Burning Dreams"

Flying flames of a dream I had,A constant burn that never goes away.Hateful people, though some are good.Every evening a lie ends my day.I hold on to my family,because they are always there for me.The sweet little girl is how I'm should be.Enemy's that throw me to the floor,I'll push them right back through the door.Cries and screams that are heard in the night,never ending fear that continues to bite.I follow my dreams but always fall,people convict me as if they were the law.Someday you'll see I'm not what I seem,and we'll all wake up from this horrible dream.
