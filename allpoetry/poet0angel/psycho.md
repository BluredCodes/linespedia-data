Psycho?
Is that how you see me?
If I said I wasn't would you believe me?
Do I go on about how life is not fair,
that I say I dont care?
About what happens tomorrow
or an hour from now.
How can I allow,
these thoughts to run
around in my head.
The thoughts that 
go through my mind 
while I lay in bed.
Why am I this way?
And everyday I keep goin'
just knowin'
I might wake up,
or maybe I wont.
This is a joke,
right?
Trying to be someone 
your not,
then what have I got?
My life breaks down 
on every turn.
When am I gonna learn?
Or maybe someone should call
a psycho hotline
1-800-this life aint mine
and surely it isn't yours
