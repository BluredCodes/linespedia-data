You got what it takes but you waste you're time?
What's wrong with you?
People die black deaths every day
and you say you don't care.
That every day you believe you are true
but lie to yourself.
Somebody mugs you, 
rapes you're childhood away,
and kills you're best friend.
You just kept on walking.
Shot out of cold blood lies you're family,
but you spit on their graves.
What makes you so angry,
what makes you tick?
I don't get whats up with this shit.
When did you follow my path
and become a delinquent of society?
You're heart and blood are black as coal.
So many scars and cuts lie in you're head.
Shot and killed you're soul is.
But mine can be seen that way too.
Quit lying to yourself.
Don't turn out to be the person I've become.
In the end you will be worthless
and no one will care.
