......no
Leave me alone
Let me be
This is all hell to me.
End this life,
it is all a lie.
Fight the anger
or the plan to commit suicide.
I hate you!
Go away!
Stop!
Sorry excuse.
You will be no more.
I want to see you die.
Suffocate you in you're sleep,
a pillow might work.
Just go,
now!
I'll kill you.
Drown you in the water
you bathe in.
God, I'm losing
all of my faith.
I'll shoot!
End you're life,
spit on you're grave,
ambush you're funeral.
My life will be made.
Help me!
I can't take it anymore.
I will hang you 
with you're own bedsheets,
....and in hell I'll let you be.
