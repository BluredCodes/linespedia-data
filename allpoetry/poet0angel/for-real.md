title: "For real"
writer: "poet0angel"

I say what I feel
because it is all real.
When my mind is made up,
yo outta luck.
You can say you wanna die,
break down and cry.
It aint gonna work.
So kick back, relax.
Enjoy the show,
featuring my life.
Betta cova yo kids eyes
and also yo wife's.
You may not like what you see
but it is all me.
This is the way its gonna be.
Watching this show,
you gonna think you know,
who I really am.
My life is outta yo hands,
so dont make any plans.
For the future,
cause I probably dont have one.
I may write about the darkness inside,
but you gotta realize,
I have a good side too.
Just betta hope you dont get on the bad one.
Because you might not see tommorow's sun.
My life aint gonna stop
till I'm dead.
So you'll hear more and more
from me until I get in yo head.
Ya hear me?
That's what I said.
