A darkness in the corner of my mind,
pulls at me at certain times.
It overcomes me like the dark of night,
a rage that has no light.
I fight my anger from time to time,
although I may seem as if I am fine.
That darkness is a terrible thing.
It has no glow, it is filled with shame.
Battles rage throughout my mind, 
fighting anger of every kind. 
Am I going to win this battle
and stay out of the fires of hell?
Or am I going to cave and sell, myself?
I reach for the light, 
I reach high.
I won't give up until I die.
It's time for the darkness to fade 
and the light to fulfill.
My future is dim as I already know,
but I won't give up hope
or reach for the sky.
I'll reach for the stars.This poem is about fighting anger
