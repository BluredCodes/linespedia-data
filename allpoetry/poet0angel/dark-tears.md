Blood Bled Bleed
You just continue to feed
Tears Water Cries
You cannot wait unitl you die
Terror Screams Fear
The life you live you cannot bear
Grey Black Blue
Wht is the matter with you?
Death Graves Sin
As you take another breath,
your life endsThis poem does not flow to well
