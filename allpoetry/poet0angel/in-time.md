---
title: "In Time"

You thought you knew me,
I guess you were wrong.
My heart is not really black,
unless I want to belong.
Rough stormy seas my emotions describe,
like cuts on you're heart and you're pride.
Empty feelings are all you feel.
The anger and rage seem like no big deal.
No one understands me,
and I could care less.
But through all of the pressures in life,
I still try my best.
Looking into the mirror what do I see?
A hopeless face, thats all.
Black, shattered memories of people
who put me down and always made me fall.
Now sharp arrows pierce my mind,
but I know that people will know me.
In time.
