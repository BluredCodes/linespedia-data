You were wrong,
I was right.
You hit me once,
I hit you twice.
Cut you're wrist,
I let you bleed.
You ruined my life,
got what you need.
You ran "round my block,
got hit by a car.
You decided to get drunk
and got laid in a bar.
Slit you're throat,
you dropped dead cold.
This is my life's card game,
so now I'll fold.
