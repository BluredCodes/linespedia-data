Allright ass-hole you've done that much.You think you the big dawg,I can put you on a crutch.Maybe I'm a bitch and maybe I'm not.Somebody will put you in a place to get you shot.You think you're a big man,full of shit you are.Struttin you're ass and layin people in bars.I'll write what I want so don't under estimate me.I'll throw you in front of a trainand in hell you'll be.So jump me and I'll jump you right back.You're shit is good but is starting to lack.Don't fuck with me againthough I know I'm marked.But I'll rip right through you like a shark.Jack-ass
