So maybe I'm young,
but don't say I'm a child.
Don't judge me by my age, race, or style.
What I write is what I feel.
You may think I'm fake,
but I'm all real.
If you don't like my comments,
don't take them personally.
Just disgaurd them 
and let them be.
So I'm sorry if I offended anyone,
those comments are maybe all done.
But don't think I'm an angel just because of my name,
that will start a whole new game.
So I'm only 13 and people see me as a baby.
Give me a break cause I think you're crazy.
My writing isn't perfect,
but don't you diss.
I will unfortuantly get very pissed.
I have respect for people who speak their mind,
but I despise people who continue to lie.
But to the people who see me that way,
don't comment on me because I'll keep writing anyway.
