she was the only one who danced half way
decent was her reply
as she swirled to leave
defiant
 the black straps of her gelled hair whipped about
and struck me in my left eye
blinding 
giving me the excuse to cry
I had long needed
knees weak I trembled as she slammed out the door
dressed in black
a shiny raven
leaving behind my quivering remains
a tiny bit of plaster fell to the oak floor
as I fell to the ground
clutching the invisible footprints
i love you
