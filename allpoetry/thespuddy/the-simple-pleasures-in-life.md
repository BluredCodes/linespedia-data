title: "The Simple Pleasures In Life"

Rosey cheeks 
Bare feet of course 
Cloths hanging on for dear life 
Nothing around her significant 
Her own little ring of books is all that matters 
The simple pleasures in life 
Words being thrown left and right 
Words sounding more like sounds 
Bleac, Mump, Doogle, Napp, Blooop! 
A clump of sweaty hair flipped across her forehead 
Is this book worth me numbing? Naa! On to the next! 
Out of nowhere, the books come alive 
A lively poodle walks by 
DOGGY DOGGY DOGGY! 
The simple pleasures in life 
Out draws her hand 
Her face licked up and down, up and down 
The smile of a child not affraid of anything 
Onlookers amazed by her calmness 
But she is just enjoying what most people miss, 
The simple pleasures in life
