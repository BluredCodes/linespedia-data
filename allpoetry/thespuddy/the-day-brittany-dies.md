title: "The Day Brittany Dies"

years and years passed us by

down the street she laid her head

all the things ive thought to say

now sadly wishing those things were said



if only i could be that way

to say those things that come to mind

instead of putting feelings off

and regreting till the end of time



seeing her everyday

to see her glow and shine

she had that something special

that something i cant describe



i would give anything

to see her face again

that beautiful, stunning smile

that voice that made my heart open



forever will she be in my heart

a sister, a shoulder, a friend

she is never that far away

for our love will never end
