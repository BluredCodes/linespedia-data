---
title: "Chances"
writer: "thespuddy"
slug: "chances"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/40849-Chances-by-thespuddy"
---

not many chances are given in life

to express what you feel deep down

but when those chances are given to me

the words just can't be found



to tell someone the truth

about what eats away inside

that takes a lot of love

but the truth is so easy to hide



since the road that's always taken

is to tell those harmful lies

there's always more to follow

when the tears fall down at night



i never know when they'll hit

always coming from behind

i take those chances given to me

and push them far aside



always missing on what could have been

that moment that can change a life

but maybe my tunnel is short this round

and on that road a ray of light



so when i get my chance again

i'll know which road is right

i'll look again for those thoughtful words

and realize, the words were always in sight
