my tail begins to wag 
my feet scramble for grip 
my ears jump to life 
my tongue begins to drip 

whoever could that be 
knocking at my door 
i can't seem to get up 
somethings wrong with this floor 

around the corner i pass 
my tummy fills with excite 
im at the door now 
i got the guy in sight 

someone get the door 
i cant reach the knob! 
i think i know who it is 
my mouths filling with slob! 

come here summer now! 
oh no dont put me away 
theres kids outside that door 
and i want to play! 

locked away for good 
now back in my room 
what you think im gonna eat them? 
how dare you assume! 

i just want someone to play with 
someone to take me outside 
why is everyone always in here 
its no fun here inside 

but wait here comes someone 
and they're putting on thier shoes! 
were gonna go ouside 
and run all through my pooh! 

gimme my things 
gimme my ball 
come here little toys 
its time for a maul! 

i love to play 
and run around all day 
i love the life i have 
please dont ever take it away 

now im panting and sweating 
i think im getting tired 
maybe we should go inside 
its time to retire 

back to the house 
where its so nice and warm 
people rub my belly 
and cook smelly popcorn 

i go to my spot 
and lay down in bed 
i think of today 
and dream of whats ahead
