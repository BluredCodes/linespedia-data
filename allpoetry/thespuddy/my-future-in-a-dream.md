i have this dream of mine 
its a life i want to live 
i have the woman ive always wanted 
i have the job that makes me smile 
we go through life together 
no matter what it pushes in our face 
we just push back with our love 
our lives are relaxed and complicated 
just as they should be 
traveling around the world 
we arent rich and famous 
we have bills that sometimes cant get paid 
we dont have everything we want 
but we like it just that way 
we grow old together 
having old friends die 
and new friends show us the way 
this may not sound like the perfect life
