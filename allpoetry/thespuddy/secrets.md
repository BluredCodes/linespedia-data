tell me the secrets you hold

allow your guard to fall

let me inside that soul of yours

and let me gaze upon it all



tell me the secrets you hold

i give you my word i wont tell

the things you say to only me

i will take with me to hell



tell me the secrets you hold

tell me about the time you wept

but dont tell me empty lies

cause lies i wont except



tell me the secrets you hold

you have nothing at all to fear

show me the clouds that linger

maybe i can make it all clear



tell me the secrets you hold

being alone is hard to do

but don't fret too much

know that i am here for you



tell me the secrets you hold

tell me what you need

tell me you hate to be alone

and alone you shall not be



tell me the secrets you hold

tell me from the start

tell me anything you want

tell me what's in your heart
