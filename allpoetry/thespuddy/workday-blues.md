behind my desk i rot 
i just sit and stare 
boredem is what i got 
id rather be anywhere 

out the window i look 
i dont want to be inside 
maybe i'll just crawl away 
crawl away and hide 

my co-workers are disterbing 
my boss i wanna shoot 
have piles of work to do 
but i dont give a hoot 

phones ringing all day 
being here is abuse 
maybe i should trying working 
nevmerind, theres no use 

times not on my side 
i got here hours ago 
but now its time to leave 
just to do it again tomorrowi write this while at my first "office" job. i no longer work there. thank god, although it did pay 19.00 an hour...but being so unhappy even while making good money....isnt worth it.
