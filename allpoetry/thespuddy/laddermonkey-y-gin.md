while the rest of the world crawls into bed 
theres another world that plays mg's instead 
while people are sleeping and covered in drool 
we have TD's telling us, ITS TIME FOR THE RULES 
smiley and aus, jena and pooh 
thunder and deb, whats a monkey to do? 
marl shows us pictures of her b and his eyebrows 
anna is on table 4, and is rubbing her eyes out 
goat goes to her closet and gets another tattoo 
i knock on the door, is smarty in there with you? 
while swamp passes out some bux and pooh paints her nails 
angel walks in the door and awakens all the males 
hugs all around, the screen filled with ((())) <--those 
then yahoo starts lagging, and games go real slow 
"lag test" here, "yuckin fahoo" there 
TIMER ON TABLE 6, and monkeys pull out thier hair 
every now and then we see everest over flow 
and the monkeys of late night get a free freak show 
TIME FOR IGGY MONKEYS, AND RD 2 IS UP! 
then a WOOF WOOF is seen, and in comes the pup 
the byes finally play, and round 3 becomes near 
bean shawn finally gets caught at work, and is no longer here 
bries is still confused, trying to change her name 
tone trys to relax, and remembers its only a game 
then a TD from canasta comes in, EVERYBODY DUCK! 
monkeys get hit with spam, and scream, WHAT LUCK!    
ROUND 4 IS UP, WINNERS ARE IN THE BUX 
tables are set to 134??, howdy and gl!

so many monkeys i cant name them all

robferrt and dragon, shaggy and sleepy 
did i miss any monkeys? oh well, guess im a meany  
SEMIS ARE UP, AND GOOD LUCK YOU FOUR! 
monkeys begin going to sleep, and falling to the floor 
my eyes are shutting down, my mind is going numb 
can i get a mg?...chances look pretty glum 
FINALS ARE UP, ITS DOWN TO YOU TWO 
can i watch the game? NO SPUDDY, EVERYONE BUT YOU  
i cant read my clock, the morning monkeys begin to wake

now gin fills up with monkeys i dont know
