our flaws hidden by darkness 
our faces masked by night 
the world's people left hours ago 
along with them, went the light 

im a night runner 
something not too many see 
most people like the mornings 
but hey, thats just not me 

thier alarms go off at dawn 
and out the door they go 
while im still warm in bed 
the sun begins to glow 

crowds of runners amerge 
the streets fill with feet 
they can keep the daybreak 
and i'll just keep my sleep 

ive accepted my night runner status 
and run with the nocturnal ghosts 
i run at night for me 
and a lot happier than most 

im glad to be a night runner 
it gives me such a thrill 
i imagine freddy krueger 
is behind me ready to kill 

i out run the winds of nothing 
and sigh with a breath of relief 
i slow down my pace a bit 
and wipe my sweat on my sleave 

i call the shadows my friend 
i think of night as a gift 
other runners can have the mornings
