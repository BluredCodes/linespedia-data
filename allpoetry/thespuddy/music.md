title: "Music"

---

music is life

i see it for more

than maybe its worth

words with volume

fill my soul

how can one thing

make me feel so good

take pride in every song you hear

laugh about it

let it teach you

let it lead you 

art with sounds

it has no limits or boundries

human expression

never let it die in you

share it, feel it, embrace it

listen with open ears

move, dance, sing

look up look down

there is music

dreams brought to life
