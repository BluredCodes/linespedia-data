the world gets on my case

but i dont let it dig me a hole

because then sooner or later

i just cant take anymore



the world voices its opinions

about people they dont know

they only look on the outside

and never look for something more



everyone needs to take a little time

and give people a chance

they need to put down their coffee

and give a second glance



you might see something great

see someone that you passed by

then maybe you'll realize

everything you assumed, was a lie



so if your hole becomes deep

and you think theres no way out

just tell the world no more

that the time to stop is now



then maybe the world will look past the corners

and see what there is to see

that not everyone enjoys their opinions

but maybe thats just me
