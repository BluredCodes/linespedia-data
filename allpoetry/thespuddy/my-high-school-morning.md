rip away the covers
and there i will be
you better start running
cause your punkass just woke me

another chance to live
starting the day bright and new
another chance to waste
damn, its still not june

preparing for the day
what a horrid task
my face so ugly
they sell it as a mask

eyes wide shut
walls in danger of feet
my body so torn
feels like raw meat

hair smashed and poofed
sheet marks in the skin
oh how lovely we look
i smell like the gym

one glorious twist
pshhhh...the first sound of life
eyes cracking open
and cracks opening wide

now slighty alive
its time for the face
breath in need of miracles
puts me in a daze

next is the tummy
then back to the sofa
breakfast of champions?
try pizza and soda

leave the house
stumble to the car and off we go
swirve here, honk there
wrong lane asshole! 

hold tight to my life
i enter my school
say whats up to my homies
and take a leak in the pool

minute by minute
time slowly passes by
wish time was on crack
then maybe it might fly

is the teacher still talking?
i cant even tell
cause i see the clock on the wall
ring-a-ding its the bell!

fight in the hallway
but i dont care
i check out the hotties
i think, whats under there?!?

now back in the car
and i rush my way back
its time for some grub
its time to relax

the days at an end
and with all of that said
homework for me?
