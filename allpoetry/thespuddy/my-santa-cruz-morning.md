the sky is black

stars roam above

then enter the realm of water

over the hills of nervousness

hands clamped tight

stomach creating waves

suddenly it's over

the eyes set on ocean

everything escapes

fears, anger, stress drown in the tide

the passing world is ignored

for a short time i'm alone

clear thoughts enter through the sand

life is all it can be

sky now blue, a beam of light emerges

the sun begins its charge

starting up the day

the world begins to appear

now with heat on the skin

i embark on the journey back to land
