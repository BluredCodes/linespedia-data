title: "My Usual Morning"

one of my favorite things in life

is to crawl into my bed

to forget the things that worry me

and just rest my little head



the covers nice and warm

my pillow soft and cool

my feet hanging out the end

and my mouth full of drool



my eyes flickering left and right 

with dreams of what's to come 

thoughts of the future bend in and out

my mind wondering where their from



sounds in the night

cant disturb my sleep

for once im out, im out

im in way too deep



the sun rises up

my eyes still stuck closed

then my alarm wakes up

damn it i hate those



my little enemy the snooze

is once again gonna be hit

might be late for work

na, thats a definite



so i rise and shine

to start my day

i look back at my bed
