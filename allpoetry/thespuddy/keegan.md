you ever have a friend 
who you love more than words 
someone who fills you with laughter 
to the point where it hurts 

that someone who cares 
that someone who matters 
who shares all your pain 
and never gives you the ladder 

the friend so close 
your just like thier brother 
as close as close can get 
to you theres no other 

the person youd die for 
that one who knows 
the person that is always there 
through your highs and your lows 

i hope i never lose this friend 
id have too much grief and sorrow 
but no need to worry 
i know they'll be there tomorrow
