title: "These Tear Stained Cheeks"

i look down my chest

past the tear stained cheeks

onto the cold still floor

wondering what i seek



im in my own place

that no one bothers to see

questions piling up

not knowing what the answers will be



maybe its time to give up

i shouldnt have waited so long

my chance has come and gone

maybe all these feelings have been wrong



so i go down to the floor

waiting to be kicked

i should be use to it by now

just make it fast and swift



once again no one is there

to help me when its needed

so i lay there for eternity

waiting to be beaten



if only someone looked down

and even thought to care

if only she knew where i was

knew that i was there
