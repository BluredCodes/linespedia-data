parts of our lives 
we do things in life 
we do what we do and after that 
we cant go back and do them over 
that chance is now gone 
every moment is a once in a lifetime chance 
we may not make the right choice 
but the choice me make can be right if we allow it to be 
years down the road when you look back at that chance 
and wonder what life would have been if you chose another path 
thats all you can do is look back 
but it will never be 
your life is what it is because of that choice you made 
but you wont have to worry 
cause your next chance to change your life
