---
title: "Places"
writer: "PurityMx"
slug: "places"

Gripping me tightly by the troat til i scream 
My blood runs thick
And it hurts to dream
I cut my soul
For others to see
Whats really inside of me 
Nothing at all
Nothing youll see
Nothing inside
Nothing youll be
I cry blood
Runs down my cheek
The warmth of it is consuming me
Into a drak place where nothing is real.
