Feel this pain dripping, 
ive hit the point, 
the point of no return, 
the point where i look back and everythings been burned, 
your cold whisper runs through my ear, 
u feel so far but still so near, 
i got u in my heart we're to close to part, 
the feelings never died, 
i did, 
theres nothing you can do, 
but ill always be there for you, 
dont forget i care for you, 
i love you, 
im watching over you.come on, lifes too short to be pissed off all the time, make something of it i know yer both strong... dont let a good friendship go....
