title: "obscured"
writer: "PurityMx"

why do i bow my head and cry,
whishing once again to die,
how can this be, 
only you and me,
you say im your dream,
your angel,
your savior,
if you care for me so much than why is it,
i always end up gettin' hurt in the end,
all i wanted to do is to love you and hold you,
be there for you and care for you,
you laughed when i fell,
you walked when i cry'd,
borke down my emotions,
and ran them through hell,
cant you see,
what this has done to me,
left alone,
heart broken,
arms empty,
mind clouded,
i can go on,
go on without a word,
and deny my feelings and tell you everythings ok,
ill get through this life one way or another with or without you,
dragging down my emotions,
prying my eyes of tears,
for the rest of these years,
with or without you.
