There for me the night it all fell apart, 
the night ill never forget, 
the night my life changed, 
simple words,
betrayel of my heart,
ill never feel the same again,
you were there for me to comfort me,
and told me to "walk into this world, with your head up high" you felt my pian,told me everything would be ok, 
wiped my tears away,
she left me with nothing to say a broken heart and im fucked to this day,
 thoughts a blur,
lifes dull,
what more can I do except pray for a friend,
to stay by my side,
be there when I cry,
and give me advice,
im here for you to,
always true to the end,
cuz im your best friend,
