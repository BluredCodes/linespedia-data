BREAK!
Away..
BREAK!
you see..
DON’T!
Touch me..
See what u did
 Im just a normal kid
Hearts been broken
Lifes been dying
Angels keep crying
See what you did
To fuck up this kid
BREAK!
Your face…
FUCK!
This place…
Stay the fuck away
I cant be this way
You will all pay
Don’t fuck with me…
