Cry out in pain 
No ones cares
Cry out in pain 
No ones there
This glistening temptress
Shining o bright
Cold hard and sharp as a knife
She sits beckoning my skin
Wanting to see what i hide within
*Let it all out through your blood and tears,
Slide me over your skin to release your fears*
She awaits my pain
To be released in blood
Contaminated blood
Blood thick with pain and anger.
