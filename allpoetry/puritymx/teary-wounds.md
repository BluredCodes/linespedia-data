Can this blade go any deeper into my skin,
screaming within,
no where to run,
but do I want to, 
slicing the pain,
feeling the drain, 
I'll sit here and enjoy this, 
I have nohting to lose, you took it all away,
that’s why Im here today cutting my soul away, 
its all your fault,
stop hiding now,
come out from the dark to see what you’ve done,
nothing to gain, 
nothing to lose SO WHY DON’T YOU RUN!?
