Fading life, 
life without a cause, 
life in hand etched with a knife, 
nothings left but this moon-lit night, 
under the stars you shine so bright,
holding this life in my hand,
your tears drop into the burning red life,
the life you caused with your verbal knife,
this life in my hand,
shining so bright, 
i'll never forget, 
that moon-lit night.I feel like i used "life" too much but if you knew the literal meaning behind it you'd understand, u know...
