' How much do you wanted......... 
My love......... 
So twisted and confused in it’s misery........... 
But so sweet and pour as an angelic dove.......... 

Please ............ 
Don’t flatter me by your pity.......... 
This goes far beyond your common shallowness........... 
You have to be more than smart and witty......... 

Deep emotional reflections................. 
Is the only way you are going to see............... 
If you step into the light, faith in our fate............. 
The image you will see is me............ 

Understand ........... 
I want the best for both of us............. 
I need only the pure........... 
Stripping away your fears and insecurities is a must........... 

You will know if we are to be joined.............. 
We shall be whole, no longer us.......... 
Where love is more than a word........... 
There is a deeper meaning in my soul than lust..........' 

-nytedremer
