'....So what now? You have My attention,' She says.' Open ahhh.....and now swallow me whole, ' He replies.' You are such a dork!I wanted to leave..,But you never wanted me to go.Bibble Babble from a fool she says....Lost cries of anguish and pain he replies.' Why the hell are you so emotional all the time! you are just a fool, I can never figure you out for anything else than that...But I love you....I really do love you,' she says.He replies....' Have you ever caught yourself dreamingand the moment you realize that you are, you wake?'She replies 'yeah, why?' He says Because that is how I feel about you sometimes....she pauses, then thinks to herself...'well, are you dreaming now...'He turns and sigh's....not since I realized....'She replies, ' I never stopped...But I wonder if I ever began....'Nytepoetry
