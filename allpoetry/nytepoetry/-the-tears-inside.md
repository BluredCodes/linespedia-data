' Even though our relationship is over…… 
There is a lot of things I will never forget……… 
The way you made me laugh, cry, smile……. 
I know you still love me, even though you won't admit……… 

I get so mad when I see your face…….. 
But When you call you just make me cry…….. 
I know you don't understand…….. 
Because I am the only one who knows why……… 

Do you know how hard it is to remember you……… 
Tears of frustration I hold in my heart ……….. 
This pain will never go away………….. 
Emotions Drawn upon a page, but more than a piece of art………. 

I never want to let you go……….. 
But holding on hurts me inside……… 
I want our relationship to remain strong…….. 
Even though our love has died………….. 

I hold everything things inside……… 
Even my tears…… 
My pain runs deep……… 
And so do my fears……………….' 

-nytedremer
