' Pain....... 
How do I let it go.............. 
It fills my heart............. 
Upon my soul it shows............ 

But shall I have…….. 

Faith......... 
In That another will come ............ 
Will lead my heart out of this dark place............ 
No longer will I be numb........... 

Or shall I lean to……….. 

Fate........... 
Which Lays not in my hand............ 
I will throw myself into the sea......... 
To be found upon tides, but washed upon the sands......... 

Always having……… 

Hope........ 
That someone will see my soul............... 
The joining on two.......... 
Shall our winters never be cold.............. 

In the end you are left with……… 

Love......... 
Which is more than a dream.......... 
It is the ray of light which shines upon my body.......... 
I envision my love as a beam...........' 

-nytedremer
