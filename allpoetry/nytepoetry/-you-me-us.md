“ You and me remember that night….. 
When us me you strolled the beach side….. 
Not mouth, ears or nose but eyes caught the moonlight….. 
When we let go our emotions, which could no longer hide….. 

So many emotions, where did it start….. 
Gently held grabbed touched your hand….. 
Holding it closely to my heart….. 
Trying to make your minds thoughts understand….. 

A Kiss laid upon my Head….. 
Finding looking remembering no words I said….. 
Emotions were books, but in my eyes you read….. 
I was bleeding, but only my love for you was bled….. 

Salt sand and us on the beach….. 
Blue round, your eyes upon me….. 
Wind stars and the touching of feet….. 
Inside me you, the love we could see….. 

Making love by nights light….. 
Sand dust covers our skin tonight….. 
You were why I found emotions to excite….. 
Boy girl both share in this delight…..”
