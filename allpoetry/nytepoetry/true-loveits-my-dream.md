I hope 
I pray 
For one beautiful day 
For my wife to be 
Shall accept my ring, while on one knee 

I would ask her to love 
With her heart, soul, to be pure as a dove 
I know it will be so 
Because I am her true Romeo 

If She says yes to being mine 
I will set that day in time 
When our love for each other will shine 

I wait for my love 
Black tuxedo in all 
Knowing this is it 
As a tear slowly falls 

She is dressed in all white 
She is a princess to this knight 
So many emotions she does now ignite 

For this day we wed 
So many things shoot through my head 
But I take her hand 
Tears on her cheek says she understands 

We both say our vows 
And then comes I do 
I turn to my bride and whisper 
“ I LOVE YOU” 

I take my bride 
To the castle we shall flee 
It is my love and I 
Just my lovely Juliet and me 

-nytepoetry
