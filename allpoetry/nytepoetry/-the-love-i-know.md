The love I know......' 

' Tears drops like Dew upon the roses….. 
In this morning light you stand before me, wanting to know…… 
But I sigh and tell you that I never knew………. 
What is love without trust, you plead with me………. 
But what is trust built on lies I tell you……… 
Can I be the only lover in your life? 
I want… 
I play… 
I run into you again… 
Feeling what has always been lost… 
Me.. 
You… 
Eternity… 
The sun set's…. 
And you are still here with me……. 
I whisper to you…….. 
I can not tell you love…… 
Only live it…. 
She sigh's… 
Knowing that I never knew love……. 
Only that I know love…' 

-Nytedremer
