Say no......... I have never.......... Please just go away.......... This sin's I plead forever............ Your lips........ Oh do they hurt.......... Passion......... Your wearing that perfume, you are just a flirt........... Kiss you......... But can you feel........ My tongue on the back of your throat....... Things can be more than real............ But I must say no......... I have never wanted this much........ confused........ But oh so confused......... I just wanted to feel your touch.......... Your eyes.......... oh my god....... Your eyes......... Those rays of light.......... But they do shine through me........... Straight through my soul............ These are just little thoughts......... words which can not describe you......... How I feel.......... The images I picture in my mind............... but what is really real.......... My memories........... YOU............' -nytepoetry
