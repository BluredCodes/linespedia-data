writer: "Winter Rain"

Black days 
Leave cold words
Shouting in the wind

Dark times
Leave blind fools 
Spouting idiocy to the night

Dismal hours
Make hearts of fire
Freeze into cold stone

The Long night has fallen
As the sun of enlightenment sets upon a sky of black
No stars will shine through it all
Only a hideous glow
Of dead light.
