---
title: "Air of hatred"
writer: "Winter Rain"
slug: "air-of-hatred"
source: "AllPoetry"

The desolation all around me
Air of hatred
Burns the Spirit's lungs
Ghosts of the past
Make the heart grow cold
Id fills with tar like blood
Words used too often
Made the sword's edge grow dull
I gave all my heart could
Though I guess cold stone is not enough
But I am truly sorry.
