Frigid drops fall from the sky
Only to form ice as it hits the ground
Like Crystal Tears
From eyes of stone
It does not fall for life 
It falls to make the world dark
to make the world sad
to make fake light again rule the day
Here in this rain
I am cold and aloneTo explain the name.
