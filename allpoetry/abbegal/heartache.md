---
title: "Heartache"
writer: "Abbegal"
slug: "heartache"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

As I look out into the darkness
A tear falls from my eye
Is love always this painful?
I wonder
Unrequieted love 
The hardest thing to bear
A sword plunging into my chest
My heart explodes into tiny pieces
No rest, no peace
Will I find tonight
