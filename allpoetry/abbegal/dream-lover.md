---
title: "Dream Lover"
writer: "Abbegal"
slug: "dream-lover"
source: "AllPoetry"

In the distance I see him walking.
I'm barely aware of others talking.
As he gets closer, I see his face.
My heart is aching for his warm embrace.
Eyes as blue as the sea.
I hope that they only see me.
A touch of his hand makes me weak.
He bends down, a soft kiss on the cheek.
I am so nervous, I start to shake.
Then in the next moment, I am awake.
