title: "Faded Looking Glass"

Long ago and far away I can still see your face 
We were the same back then 
We are so different now 
The world seemed like such a different place 
Your face is so youthful 
My face has painfully aged 
We were once one person; we walked as one 
And wondered how the world turned 
Time has passed so swiftly 
And my eyes have turned gray 
Your time stands still, your eyes are bright, 
Go on and play 
Ignorance and innocence are all that I see in you 
I still may be ignorant, but my innocence has faded 
As I toil under books, choices, and consequences 
Heed not my call for help 
Your memory will only wear me down 
Stay in your sandbox and idle the day away 
I'll stay in my metal box, my cage 
You seem so free, but your mind is in shackles 
I look so consumed, but my mind runs rampant 
Who is better off? 
You are just a distant me 
Preserved and pickled, stretched and outgrown 
A persistent memory
