A monument to nature's power
Standing high and proud
Like an iron tower
Its limbs cloaked in an emerald shroud
The mighty tree stands with a watchful eye

A history lies within its trunk so grand
Only viewed by strong of will and sweat of hand
A tale so intricately wound
Wove in rings so tightly bound
The mighty tree stands with a watchful eye

Without eyes, it has seen so many things
A million deer and a splendor of wings
A hundred winters and countless wonders
It has survived when it is cold & when it thunders
The mighty tree stands with a watchful eye

It has given much to the world around it
A page in a book filled with words
A kindling on the fire lit
A home for squirrels and birds
