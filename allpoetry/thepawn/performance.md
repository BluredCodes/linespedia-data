Performing is what we love
Put on a puppet show
Watch the priest pull the flock behind him
Watch the prostitute behead the priest
Marvel at the prole and the pauper
Sucking on the sweet nectar
Sucked into the cement of the city
A little bit of carnage to wet your pallet
Everyone in the spotlight takes a last breath
One last bow and the curtains close
With your ovation, our puppets fall apart
