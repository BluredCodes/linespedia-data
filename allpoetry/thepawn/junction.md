all those exits seemed to be another junction in a circle prepare for departure unincumbered by your fear wake up and change the channel dear god is getting bored of you is real life as bad a reflection of tv as it seems hand job redemption take the train to never never land the story ends when you're happy or when you die sacrificial figure put your best face forward put your best face forward don't worry we've done this before
