title: "Outcomes Undesired"

Deteriorated faces in desolate places
Derived of faulty choices
Empty bodies waiting to be filled
Hopes and dreams long since discarded
have now wilted and dissipated
Formaldehyde has kept us jaded
Sterilizations have left us faded
Toil for the likes of those unseen
The front door has been locked
All the little nuisances have been stocked
Are we looking for a way in
To what we've been promised?
Are we looking for a way out
Of what we've become?
