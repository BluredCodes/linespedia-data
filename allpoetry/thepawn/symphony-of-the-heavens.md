title: "Symphony of the Heavens"

The skies darken
As the black clouds roll in
The rumble heralds the storm
The onslaught is taking form
A gentle drop falls to remind the persistent of heart
The roaring symphony is about to start
As the plains and roads are emptied
The lonely wind surveys the event
Bothering the exposed trees

The heavens are not at ease
Gray sky flickers faintly
Clouds start to churn
Rain falls daintily
Rumbling consumes the battle ground
Peace is shattered; everything shudders

Light dances across the sky
Rain dances on the roof
Our hearts dance with excitement
Crash! boom! the climactic end draws near

The harbingers of the storm retreat
Silence; no acclaim
The sky grows blue from the pale gray
The trees rise again, indignant
Forgetting the sky titans,
The racket of life resumes in full swing... an older poem...
