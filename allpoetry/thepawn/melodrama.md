Even though it seems
Like I'm singing the same old
Thoughtless song
I must say I have changed
Though I still wish I could live
In the past (sometimes)
At least I haven't stayed the same

Kinda' wish you were here beside me
But I wouldn't want time & space to be garbled
For an unappreciated moment of perfection
Stretched out to eternity
Wouldn't want this one-line melodrama
Stretched out to infinity

Kinda' wish the heart would let go and realize
What seemed to be a matching piece
(To life's jigsaw puzzle)
Is now a square peg in a round hole
(That just won't fit)
