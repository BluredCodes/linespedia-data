---
title: "Favor"
writer: "thepawn"
slug: "favor"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Years and years of second-guessing
Countless nights of reminiscing
Why won't you leave me satisfied
With a bit of here-and-now

Oh dear mother-god why won't you
Let me count my blessings
Every day you're taking fruit from me
Without a word of gratitude
Oh why won't you let me
Catch your guilty-pleasure's heart
And taste the spoils of my work
Oh why don't you let me
Sustain all this toil
With a reminder of my passions

Mother-god-lover-future-and-forever
Why don't you kiss this tender body
To bring me temperance
In the face of leering specter
To give me fortitude
I would repay you ten-fold
But I have only one soul

Oh blessed future-lover
Give me more than just a whisper
Give me more than a shade of gray
Just stave off death for one more hour
Just give me everything
Or just go away
Give me what I want or
Step back and fade away
I want to make the rules
I'm sick and tired of this stage
You've placed me on
In your twisted little game

Oh dear mother-god
I would love you greater
If only you could step down from your altar
And show mankind what you've done
Quit blaming us for all our problems
We would do the same for you
Were we not so feeble
We would stop asking stupid favors
Were we not so blind
