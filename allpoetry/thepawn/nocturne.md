Bullets bleeding through
The shattered glass
Of this marble statue's eyes
Pick up the pieces
To fit your peace of mind
Never thought this day would come
The blind shall lead the blind
The shadow is the longest
Just before the sun sets
The first whisp of darkness
Precipitates the night
