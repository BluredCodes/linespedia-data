Ride the course
The straight and narrow
Meager sailors are we now
On the mighty ocean at dusk
Who knows what we'll be
On the break of the morrow

Ride the course
The straight and narrow
Timid sailors are we now
Who are we to deviate
From the unbending line laid before us
Into an ocean vast

Ride the course
The straight and narrow
Intrepid sailors are we now
None have come before us
And none dare to follow

Ride the course
The straight and narrow
Desperate sailors are we now
Let free the dove
And man the crow's nest
Trust the tub in which we float
To stave off fear of the abyss

Ride the course
The straight and narrow
Hopeful sailors are we now
Port lies just beyond the waves
Rum and rowdiness await us
