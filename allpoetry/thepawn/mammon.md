Costly cotton builds the kingdom
Costly cotton kills the king
Costly operation to save his men
Post-mortem assessment all the same
Stuff the bodies with sugar cane
And pay your kinsmen to look the other way
Put some tobacco in the right pipes
And paint pinstripes on the likely suspects
Superstitions drive the little cogs
But we could always use some grease
So up the ante so up the pace
So kick out devoted employees
Throw cheap labor into the fold
Possibility for 10% profit margin
Quarterly analysis looking good
We’ve got the ax we’ve got the scaffold
We’ve even got the black hood
Spill some blood and watch the public stare
Camera in position
Satellite receiver on each and every porch
Turn on the TV and watch the fun begin
Transmission’s looking steady
Ratings are looking good
Satellite puts justice in each and every house
Put down the dishes turn off the lights
Hit record reruns for cousins
Foot for foot screen is bigger than the rest
Kerplunk head hits the deck
Kerplunk sugar in your coffee
Columbia bean offers caffeine
Elixir of our times
Give the kids a Coke
See the man suspended from the rope
Instant replay for human fillet
Instant coverage with Carson Daily as your host
Make a toast for human roast
5 4 3 2 1 ball drops on the new year
5 4 3 2 1 late night bomb drops bringing fear
12 midnight
Time to tuck aborted children into bed
Take two Tylenols make your prayers
Time to go off to work
Off to bury the dead
