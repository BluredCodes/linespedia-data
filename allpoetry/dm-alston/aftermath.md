title: "Aftermath"

Battered buildings sag together.Twisted trees now show their roots.Shards of glass crunch so harshly,Underneath the troopers' boots.Death at last has drifted onwardTo bless some other cursed land.People see the foolish practiceOf building homes upon the sand.Scratches mar the shiny surfaceOf the lives that people knew.Temporal-Ness is ripped asunder,All that's left is what is True.Churches overflow with living,While the cemeteries grow.Shock subsides as insight heightens,So men may learn what angels know.
