---
title: "How About A Drink?"
writer: "D.M. Alston"
slug: "how-about-a-drink"
source: "AllPoetry"

I drink and I drink until plastered,
For drinking is one art I've mastered.
It takes all my dough
To stay this drunk though,
So buy me a drink you cheap bastard!
