Someday ... 
you will hear the echo of my love 
calling you from a far off place 
inside your heart. 

Someday ... 
you will hear a whisper in your ear 
telling you all I wanted 
was your love from the start. 

Wanting you to love me forever,
The way I love you, 
Living in the moment shared,
Thinkng only of today.
 
Loving me completely,
Without reservations, 
Forgetting tomorrow,
Not worrying about yesterday. 

Someday ... 
you'll reach out to me 
calling out my name in despair 
but I won't be there. 

Someday ... 
Long after I've said goodbye 
your tears will fall 
like cloud bursts from the sky. 

Someday ... 
You'll remember how much I love you 
and you'll know that's how 
much you love me too. 

Tag ..... 

How much you love me to, 
Someday ... 
Someday my love, 
Someday my love.Dedicated to my Sandie Angel.  I love her.  But is my love enough?
