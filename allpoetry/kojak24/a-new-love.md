title: "A New Love!"

His words touch my soul 
and my heart, it soars 

THAT'S BECAUSE EACH DAY 
I LOVE YOU MORE AND MORE 

Like the touch of his hand 
I long for more 

EACH DAY WITH YOU IS PARADISE 
FOR YOUR LOVE I'LL PAY ANY PRICE 

Giving life to my passion 
Long since dead 

LIKE A LION IN SUMMER 
HEAR MY ROAR! 

My grieving love 
an existence I had dread 

NO MORE TEARS - NO MORE CRYING 
MY LOVE FOR YOU IS UNDYING 

I feel a healing now 
deep inside 

PLEASE LOVE ME FOREVER 
AND BE MY BRIDE 

A light in my darkness 
I no longer hide 

FOR YOU ARE MY JOY AND PRIDE 
I'LL ALWAYS BE BY YOUR SIDE 

Though I still grieve for my loss 
I begin anew 

OUT WITH THE OLD 
AND IN WITH THE NEW! ... no one can love you like I do ... 

And I know that this love 
is because of you 

AND BECAUSE OF YOU 
MY LOVE WILL BE FOREVER MORE 

Throughout eternity.......... 


All the love I have to give 
is yours and yours alone, 
By your side I want to stay 
and hold you close every day 
to keep you safe from harm 
and free from fear. 
I will always love you dear."You have to know I love this... Pat"
