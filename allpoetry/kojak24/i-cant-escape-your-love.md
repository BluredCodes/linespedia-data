I 

I can't escape your Love - 
It's with me all the time, 
In my heart and on my mind. 

II 

I can't escape you love -
It haunts me night and day, 
In dreams that won't go away. 

III 

I can't escape your love - 
Its magic fills the air, 
Wherever I go you are there 

and I can't escape your love. 

Chorus ..... 

Oh I've tried to forget you - but no matter how hard I try - 
the memories in my heart - always make me cry, 
I feel so lonely - but there's nothing I can do - 
each day I grow crazy - from wanting you 

and I can't escape your love. 

IV 

Yes I can't escape your love 
can't make it go away, 
I'm living in dreams of yesterday 

and I can't escape your love
