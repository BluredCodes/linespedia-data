I'm stuck in the middle of love, 
Don't know which way to turn, 
You're in front and he's behind, 
Guess I've got a lot to learn. 

Tie ..... 

I'm stuck in the middle of love. 

Oh I love you and you love him 
and I love him too, 
You love me and he loves me, 
Oh I don't know what to do. 

Tie ..... 

I'm just stuck in the middle of love. 

Chorus ..... 

Yeah, stuck in the middle and I sure love to twiddle, 
Oh howdy do do do ... make my love come true, 
Both of you are ready and I am ready too, 
Let me play a game of ring dang do with you. 

Oh ring dang do and what is that? 
It's round and fat like a putty cat! 
He's in front and I'm behind 
Oh all you others get in line. 

Tie ..... 

Oh we're stuck in the middle of love! 

Yeah, stuck in the middle 
from the front and behind, 
Oh all this action makes me feel so fine 
that I think Im goin' out of my mind! 

Tie ..... 

Stuck in the middle of love! 

Hot doggies! Yahoo! Tell me what's it's gonna be 
You up front and him behind? Or vice versa(ry) 
For it's wham bam thank you mam, 
oh make it good to me! 

Tag ..... 

Oh I'm stuck in the middle of love! 
Stuck in the middle of love! 
Stuck in the middle of love! 

Yahoo! Here I cuuuuuuuuuuuuum!!!!! 
Ride 'em cowboy! 
High Ho Silver! 
Up up up and away! 
Yahoo! Yahoo! Yahoo! 
Just Stuck in the middle of love!!!!!!!!!!!!! 
Just stuck in the middle of love!!!!!!!!!!!!!
