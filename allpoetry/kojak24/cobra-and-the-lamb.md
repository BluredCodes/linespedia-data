Cobra and a Lamb I am I am ... I don't understand
but I am I am ... Snake in the grass or
succulent feast I am I am! Do you give a dam who
I am I am? I really don't give a (insert here
whatever word your mind thinks I was going to
use) who you believe I am I am... What word did
you use? Whatever it was it was not mine but
yours ... remember that you dirty rat ...
whatever you are you are ... and I am I am ...
you am you am ... we am we am ... they am they
am ... If I stop this mental masturbation will it
lead to castration? Or will I become a
corporation? I'd love to be hermaphrodite. I
think it would be great to (insert whatever word
your mind thinks I was going to use) everyone! I
am I am... What word did you use? Whatever it
was I know what I meant and I think it was heaven
sent. I'll bet your gentle heart thinks I'm
talking about Aphrodite Not slush, slush, slush
and push, push, push ugh, ugh ugh >>>>> what the
un-expressive call "doing it". Do you
understand? I'll bet you do for I was your
Sunday School preacher and now I'm your teacher
and lector and I'll read your future!

"Aye ignoramus can you become famous
hurling rocks at your demons?
Is your little mind always scheming
to promote your own attraction
entering a deep well oiled hole
will give you satisfaction."

You are compelled to chant the above
benediction every day for the rest of your life
to achieve nirvana and move to Alabama.

Yes...I want to be your lector ... injector of
good and evil ... your white protector and your
red master rector.. Hallelujah ... Glory to God
we'll bury you in fertile sod!!!
