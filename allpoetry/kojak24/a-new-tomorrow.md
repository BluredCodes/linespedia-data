A New Tomorrow 

1st verse .....

Will there be a new tomorrow 
or is this just a one night stand? 
Will your arms still be open to me
or is there another man? 

2nd verse .....

If on the street we see each other
will you smile and say hello? 
Will you still see me as a lover
or turn away from me and go? 

Chorus .....

After a night of sharin' all your charms
I think a new tomorrow's on the way,
Another night of you here in my arms
If I can make the choice listen when I say.

3rd verse .....

Yes, there'll be new tomorrow 
if you'll just let me have my way, 
Girl you rocked my world at midnight 
right up through the light of day. 

4th verse.....

Will there be a new tomorrow? 
You know you have the final say, 
So honey please just tell me 
is our new tomorrow on the way? 

Musical Interlude .....

repeat 3rd verse ..... 

Yes, there'll be new tomorrow 
if you'll just let me have my way, 
Girl you rocked my world at midnight 
right up through the light of day. 

repeat 4th verse .....

Will there be a new tomorrow? 
You know you have the final say, 
So honey please just tell me 
is our new tomorrow on the way? 

Tag .....

Is our tomorrow on the way?
Oh yeah  ... is our new tomorrow on the way?
