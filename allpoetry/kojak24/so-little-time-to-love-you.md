So little time to love you 
and hold you in my arms, 
So little time to caress you 
and share your charms, 
To taste your warm surrender 
and feel your passion rise, 
My heart is captivated 
by the rhythm of your thighs. 

So little time to love you 
and be your magic prince, 
To let my lips explore you 
and place you in a trance, 
Until aroused beyond reason 
by the touch of my hand, 
You urgently place me 
deep inside your wonderland. 

So little time to love you 
as I feel myself expand, 
Our thrusts quickened 
by your hips command, 
Ignited by passions flame 
we explode in unison, 
Bursting into a million stars 
in our paradise of love. 

So little time to love you!
So little time to love you!
So little time to love you!
