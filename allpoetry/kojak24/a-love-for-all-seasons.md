I met her in Summer 
and we fell in love, 
Pledging our hearts 
to Heaven above, 
A love for all seasons, 
Let everyone know. 

A love for all seasons, 
Let everyone know. 

Our love grew 
at a rapid pace, 
Everyone admired 
our style and grace, 
A love for all seasons, 
Let everyone know. 

A love for all seasons 
Let everyone know. 

Oh Autumn's upon us, 
It's not the same, 
Colors are fading 
Leaves turning brown, 
A love for all seasons, 
Let evenyone know. 

A love for all seasons, 
Let everyone know. 

Now suddenly it's Winter, 
everything's changed, 
My heart is broken 
and filled with pain, 
A love for all seasons, 
Oh were did it go? 

A love for all seasons, 
Oh where did it go? 

A love for all seasons, 
Oh where did it go? 
Oh where did it go? 
Oh where did it go?
