Long ago you told me what I knew 
that you and I were through, 
And I should stop thinkin' of you 
and find someone new, 

But honey - I can't stop thinkin' of you. 

At night in dreams I call your name 
Then I awake and it's always the same 
I'm all alone - you're gone 
and I don't know how to carry on, 

Cause baby - I can't stop thinkin' of you. 

Chorus .... 

Thinkin' of you and the love we shared 
when you said you were mine and swore you cared, 
And now it still seems like yesterday 
when you turned around and walked away. 

Yes angel, I keep thinkin' of you, 
How you broke my heart in two, 
Making my tear drops start 
each lonely night we've apart, 

And I can't stop thinkin' of you. 

How your love so warm and tender 
and nights of sweet surrender, 
captured my soul, made a fool of me, 
Gave me no choice but to set you free, 

And now I can't stop thinkin' of you. 

Tag ..... 

Can't stop thinkin' of you, 
Can't stop thinkin' of you, 
Can't stop thinkin' of you.
