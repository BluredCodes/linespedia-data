1st verse 

No matter what you say or do ... I keep wanting you, 
You can tell me a million times we're through 
and I keep wanting you. 


2nd verse ..... 

For nothing will change the love I feel 
deep down inside of me, 
I just keep wanting you. 

Chorus ... 

Oh baby your love haunts me night and day and I keep
wanting you ... wanting to hold you in my arms ... 
wanting to caress you tenderly ...  wanting to kiss
your lips ... wanting to make you love me ... the way
I love you ... completely and with all my heart. 

3rd verse ..... 

Oh baby time stands still when you're near ... 
And nothing will ever stop me from wanting you, 
wanting you ... wanting you ... wanting you ... 

4th verse ..... 

Wanting you with an urgent need beyond beyond reason ... 
tearing me apart inside ... 
from wanting you ... wanting you. 

Repeat Chorus ..... 

Oh yes baby your love haunts me night and day and I
keep wanting you ... wanting to hold you in my arms
... wanting to caress you tenderly ... wanting to kiss
your lips ... wanting to make you love me ... the way
I love you ... completely and with all my heart. 

5th verse ..... 

What else can I do? 
Nothing will ever change my love for you 
I just keep wanting you ... wanting you. 


6th verse ..... 

Wanting you so long as I'm alive, 
Wanting to make our love survive, 
Oh please pretty baby say you want me too. 

Tag ..... 

Say you want me too. 

by: William M. Sowman
