At the fountainhead of lust drink the blood
scattered in all directions by hardy inspections.
Lustful suction increases production of the
sweet nectar attached to vectors inside
projectors capturing mirrored reflections on
screens of beauty and virginal selection. While
monks with huge erections chant salutations of
duty to ravish the virginal perfection.

"Yum, yum, yum, we're devils in the mist" they shout, 
"Release all the nymphs ..... let them all turn out,
We'll show them what this is all about.
The hymn of the hymen we're rhymin',
On breasts of virgins we're primin',
to fill the vaginal spaces with slurpin'
tongues of intense satisfaction
preparing them for the main attraction.

Tonight, tonight will be our pleasure,
It's time for us to reap our treasure,
Yes, the hymn of the hymen we're rhymin'.
We've planned this deed with perfect timin'
So puerile voyeur seeking satisfaction
relax and enjoy your own reaction!"
