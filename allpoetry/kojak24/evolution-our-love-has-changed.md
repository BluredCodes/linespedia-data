Evolution ... Our Love Has Changed 

1st verse .....

Our love has changed - it's not the same,
I feel your touch - it seems so strange,
We're somewhere else and we can't explain,
Close together and yet so faraway.

2nd verse .....

The love we had is just a token,
We feel alone and so heart broken,
Our love's no longer a burning flame,
Love has died and only ashes remain.

Chorus ......

Evolution you held the ultimate conclusion,
Changing times destroyed our dreams,
Filled our hearts with confusion,
And turned our love into an illusion,
Evolution ... our love has changed.

3rd verse ..... 

Our journeys end is here today,
The pain inside is here to stay,
But we won't cry or say good-bye,
We'll just turn around and walk away.

Repeat Chorus .....

Evolution you held the ultimate conclusion,
Changing times destroyed our dreams,
Filled our hearts with confusion,
And turned our love into an illusion,
Evolution ... our love has changed.

4th verse .....

Once love was a game we used to play,
Now it's  a dream of yesterday,
Everything that we shared is so faraway,
So faraway - so faraway.
