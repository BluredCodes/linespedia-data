title: "I'm  Breathless Over You"

1st verse ..... 

I'm breathless over you,
I don't know what to do,
You take my breath away,
I'm breathless over you. 

2nd verse ..... 

I'm breathless over you
That's all I can say,
I love you more each day,
I'm breathless over you.

Chorus ..... 

Oh I can't find words to tell you how I feel 
but I hurt so much inside my heart my pain is unreal, 
And though I pretend I'm not lonely when we're apart 
I know before long my tears are gonna start. 

3rd verse ..... 

I'm breathless all the time, 
Wanting to make you mine,
Anxious to share a a kiss, 
Breathless over you. 

Tag ..... 

Breathless over you, 
Breathless over you, 
Breathless over you. 

by: Patricia M. Williams & William M. Sowman
