It's Over - I Give Up - I Lose - He Wins 

I

There's nothing I can say or do
it's time to walk away from you,
You're looking at me but smiling at him
It's over - I give up - I lose - he wins.

II

Oh babe my hurt is deep and wide,
Tears are falling that I just can't hide
You've broken my heart and destroyed my pride
by running to him and takng his side.

Chorus .....

Oh you're the teacher of confusion,
In one session you taught me a lesson,
All my dreams are now illusions
and I've lost your love my greatest possession.

III

School's over now  and you've gained your freedom,
My heart has paid the price for your treason,
So go with him don't worry about me
It's over - I give up - I lose - he wins.
