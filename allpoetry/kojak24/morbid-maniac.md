Ring of fire 
ring of love 
sent from Heaven up above 
Nites of praise 
Nites of terror 
Who's that staring in my mirror? 
Running hearts chasing igloos 
Birds a chirping 
Cows a mooing 
Give them portions of witches brew 
lips all puckered 
vagina open 
feel the semen gently flowing? 
urges uptown in Sante Fe 
who the hell lives in Sante Fe 
many moons Indian maiden 
raped by Captain Pidgeon 
girls are crying 
many dying 
open legs caused the problem 
babies born one a minute 
I will screw the village idiot 
for he will love it 
but cannot tell 
jacking off in the morning 
gives pause for thought 
or is it thought for pause? 
spread 'em wide baby 
my mouth is watering 
and I will give you 
your daily pleasure 
all virgins leave this 
train ... I'm going dancing 
in the rain in Spain 
Are you ready for big fat 
"Freddy"? He's tall and 
handsome and always ready! 
My what a sweet ass you 
have Josephine!!!!!!!!
