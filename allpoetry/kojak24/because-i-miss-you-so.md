I 
 
 Because i miss you so 
 ... the wind, the rain, 
 the tides, the snow, 
 ... all remind me of you. 
 
 II 
 
 Because I miss you so 
 ... there's a place 
 I'd like to go 
 ... to be alone together. 
 
 1st Chorus ..... 
 
 Where I can hold you close to me - listening to the rhythm of our hearts - as romance makes music for a dance - only we can do, 
 Because I miss you so - the moon - the stars - the suns eternal glow 
 make time stand still and everything remind me of you. 
 
 III 
 
 Because I miss you so 
 Miss your sweet caress, 
 Without you by my side 
 there is no happiness. 
 
 IV 
 
 Because I miss you so 
 I see you wherever I go, 
 Can't escape thoughts of you 
 because I miss you so. 
 
 2nd chorus ..... 
 
 Oh yes, I want to live with you - down by the sea - walking hand in hand along the shore - loving you every day more and more, 
 Singing in the rain - dancing in meadows - chasing rainbows - 
 pretending you're here with me all the while - because I miss you so. 
 
 Tag ..... 
 
 Because I miss you so 
 Because I miss you so 
 miss you so 
 miss you so
