Some like it hot ... some like it cold 
some like it ... when they grow old 
I like it ... anyway I can get it 
cause I'm as freaky as I can be 
It's hips, lips, and fingertips 
and every part of me 
AC/DC plus XYZ ... 
Just a geek with red hair 
and purple ears, 
eyes like Satan, 
with a ring in my nose 
a tongue of fire, 
and cloven feet, 
So hungry for your golden thighs 
and wild nights of ecstasy 
searching for your split infinity 
such a freaky geek I be 
Cause baby your my sweet proclivity 
and nothing in the world 
is better for you than me. 
I'm gonna fill your space 
with streams of my love potion 
until you're swimming in love's ocean 
Screaming my name in harmony 
with the sounds of the city rushing in 
Oh Hollywood oh Hollywood ... 
I feel freaky in this city 
I feel freaky in this city 
I feel freaky in this city 
I feel freaky in this city. 
What a place to share our world, 
so geeky and so very much in love.
