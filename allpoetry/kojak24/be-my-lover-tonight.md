Be My Lover Tonight 

1st verse .....

Lover take me down -
to the place I want to be,
Fill my heart with love -
and share your dreams with me, 
Feed my hunger with passion -
and my soul with delight,
Come on baby - be my lover tonight.

2nd verse .....

Oh I can't promise you anything -
but a night of temptations, 
Hold me in your arms - 
and enjoy the sweet sensations, 
We'll dance to the rhythm of love -
and let our hearts take flight, 
Come on baby - be my lover tonight.

Chorus .....

I love you so much can't you see
Share the love inside of me,
Hold me close and set me free, yeh,
Oh come on baby - be my lover tonight. 

3rd verse .....

Oh, be my lover tonight
and let our hearts take flight,
As we dance to the rhythm of love
beneath the stars above,
Share sweet bliss with me, yeh, 
and fulfill all my fantasies,
Come on baby - be my lover tonight.
