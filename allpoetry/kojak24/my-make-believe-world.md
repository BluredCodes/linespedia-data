1st verse ..... 

In my make believe world 
I pretend you're mine, 
For you're far away 
what harm can it do? 
In my make believe world 
everything's fine, 
I love you so 
and it's only part time. 

2nd verse ..... 

In my make believe world 
I belong to you, 
I am your woman 
and will always be true, 
Your love's so deep 
within my heart, 
Every time I think of you 
the teardrops start. 

Tie..... 

I'm your woman and you're my man, 
But only in my world of make believe. 

Bridge..... 

Make believe, make believe, what harm can it do? 
To pretend I love you and will always be true__ 
I knew it was wrong, as soon as it began, 
For my life was committed, to another man. 

3rd verse ..... 

The one who holds me,__close at night, 
The one who knows,__this is his right, 
In my make believe world, it's you___ 
though it never can be true, 
A love he never could conceive, but it's 
Only in my world of make believe. 

Tag..... 

A love he never could conceive, but it's 
Only in my world of make believe.
