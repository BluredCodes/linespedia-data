---
title: "Anyone But Me"
writer: "kojak24"
slug: "anyone-but-me"

There's time - 
for anyone but me
in your hectic life,
While everything I do - 
fills
your
life
with
strife.

But I can tell you now -
While others may 
give you satisfaction,
After the're gone
you'll 
still
be
my
main
attraction.

..... Other loves may come and go
..... a million times or more,
..... But I'll be here for you
..... after they all turn away
..... and walk out the door.

+++++++++++++ Sweet words they'll whisper
to turn your pretty head,
+++++++++++++ While I huddle by myself
in my lonely bed,
+++++++++++++ Arms that hold you now
will forget you very soon,
+++++++++++++ But I'll still be around
morning, night, and noon.

As the hands of time -
move forward into eternity,
Worldly accolades with fade
and whither in your heart,
But my  love for you
will never fall apart.

************************* As you grow old -
************************* when I am gone,
************************* Remember what I say -
************************* Billy loved you - 
************************* really loved you -
************************* forever and a day.

Billy
loved
you -
really
loved
you -
++++++ F O R E V E R  A N D  A  D A Y ! ++++++
