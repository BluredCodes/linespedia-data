I 

Make these fools go away, 
I've had my fill of them, 
For they don't know a thing 
about the mood I'm in. 

II 

They think they're so smart 
and can see inside my heart, 
But they'll never know 
when my tears began, 

Tie ... 

Oh make these fools go away! 

Chorus ..... 

Oh the tears are falling like cloudbursts from the sky, 
Filling my world with sadness since you said goodbye, 
Now it seems that all I do now is sit around and cry, 
Feeling so all alone with a love that will never die. 

Tie ... 

Oh make these fools go away! 

III 

For they'll never understand 
the wonder of being your woman, 
And they don't know how 
to give anyone a helping hand, 

Tie ... 

Oh please -- 

Make these fools go away! 

Tag ... 

Make these fools go away! 
Make these fools go away!
