I 

It's push come to shove, 
Last call for love, 
Should we call it quits, 
Let it end like this, 
Or keep what we've found 
And have one more round? 

It's last call for, 
Last call for love. 

Chorus ..... 

It's sad to feel - the pain inside, 
Because we know - how hard we've tried, 
But angry words - have hurt our pride, 
Now it's last call - last call for love. 

II 

Is it time to admit 
That we've had enough? 
Lighten our load and 
Have one for the road? 
Or take one more chance 
Hold on to romance? 

It's last call, 
Last call for love. 

Repeat Chorus ..... 

It's sad to feel - the pain inside, 
Because we know - how hard we've tried, 
And angry words - have hurt our pride, 
Now it's last call - last call for love. 

Bridge ..... 

Do we hold on - or set ourselves free? 
I think we know - what the answer will be. 
 
Musical Break ..... 

Chorus ..... 

It's sad to feel - the pain inside, 
Because we know - how hard we've tried, 
And angry words - have hurt our pride. 
Now it's last call - last call for love. 

Tag ..... 

It's last call, 
Last call for love.
