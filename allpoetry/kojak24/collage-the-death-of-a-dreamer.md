Charley Manson in my head 
Marilyn Manson in my bed, 
A million virgins bleeding red 
from the thrusting of the dead. 

Clap your hands sing and shout 
Charley's here and Charley's dead, 
Killed by devils in my head 
engulfed by flames in my bed. 

Marilyn licks my corpse with virgin tongue, 
We writhe in ecstasy two as one, 
Split from infinity the universe conjoined with me 
I'm free, free, free - over me death has no victory. 

chorus 

Flamenco dancers ... dance for me 
click your heels throughout eternity, 
Cymbals clash and trumpets sound 
God's music has a beautiful sound! 

So please, please, please ... 
no more crying ... no more woe 
I'm alive and well upon a throne 
so alive and well and not alone. 

Adolph Hitler provides my warmth 
as he burns beneath my feet, 
Jesus rules the world I'm in 
I'm with Him in my eternal home. 

So clap your hands - dance and sing 
throw some dirt up in the air, 
Watch it become birds on high 
flying to our Master in the sky. 

Oh listen and you will hear 
the answers clear ... death has no sting 
victory awaits ... if we listen 
to the call of the Spirit within our heart! 

repeat chorus 

Flamenco dancers ... dance for me 
click your heels throughout eternity, 
Cymbals clash and trumpets sound 
God's music has a beautiful sound!
