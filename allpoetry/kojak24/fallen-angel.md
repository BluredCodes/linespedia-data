I 

Fallen angel ... 
I thought you'd love me, 
Forever and a day, 
Yes - really love me, 
Couldn't see your feet of clay. 

II 

Fallen angel ... 
You promised to be faithful 
and always be mine, 
Then you cheated on me 
time after time. 

Chorus ..... 

So forget the promises you made - or the price I paid, 
Just pick up your broken wings - and walk away, 
Leaving me alone - with dreams of yesterday, 
Just pick up your broken wings - and walk away. 

III 

Fallen angel ... 
Once you flew so high 
way up in the sky, 
Then came crashing down 
to the cold hard ground. 


Bridge ..... 

Oh fallen angel - can't you see what you've done? 
Breaking a heart - that loved you -
thinking it was the only one. 
Oh Fallen angel - can't you see what you've done?


Musical Break ..... 

Chorus ..... 

So forget the promises you made - or the price I've paid, 
Just pick up your broken wings - and walk away, 
Leaving me alone - with dreams of yesterday, 
Just pick up your broken wings - and walk away. 

Tag .....
