Patricia "my hurt is deep and wide with tears I cannot hide. You've broken my heart and destroyed my pride, running .." See: http://www.postpoems.com/cgi-bin/displaypoem.cgi?pid=20022

 
You promised me love forever 
But you made a fool of me, 
Forever ended yesterday 
when I had to set you free. 

You couldn't be true 
so I had to let you go, 
Now I'm just another link 
in your chain of broken hearts. 

Chorus ..... 

A chain of broken hearts whose dreams can't true, 
All linked together by their love for you, 
You cast them aside, left them standing in a row, 
And now here I stand with no where else to go. 

Broken hearts created by you, 
It's love them, leave them 
tell them they're through, 
You've found someone new. 

Yes a chain of broken hearts 
that will never end, 
And a love beyond repair 
that will never mend. 

A chain of broken hearts 
all standing in a row 
I loved you baby 
more than you'll ever know. 

Tag ..... 

Yes, I loved you baby more 
than you'll ever know.
