1st Verse ..... 

Echoes of love - breaking my heart, 
Echoes of love - tearing it apart, 
Echoes of love - haunting my mind, 
Echoes of love - so very unkind. 

2nd verse ..... 

Echoes of love - night and day, 
Echoes of love - since you went away, 
All that's left - are memories, 
And echoes of love - that can never be. 

Chorus ..... 

The echoes of your voice whisper my name 
While I pretend we're were playing love's game, 
But deep down inside I'm lonely and blue, 
Cause echoes of love tell me we're through. 

3rd verse ..... 

Yes, echoes of love all of the time, 
In my dreams you're still mine,
But deep within I know it's not true, 
And my world is empty without you. 

4th verse ..... 

Echoes of love - calling to me, 
Echoes of love - won't set me free, 
Echoes of love - here in my heart, 
Echoes of love - make teardrops start. 

Tag ..... 

Yes, echoes of love 
calling to me, 
Echoes of love 
won't set me free,. 
Echoes of love 
for etermity. 

by: William M. Sowman (for) Patricia M. Williams
