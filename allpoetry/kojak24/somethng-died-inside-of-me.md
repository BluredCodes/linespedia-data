Somethng Died Inside Of Me 
by  kojak24,  2nd December 2001.   


When you walked away ... something died inside of me ... my heart.

 
When I saw you walk away 
Something died inside of me, 
Was it only yesterday? 
Seems like an eternity. 

You didn't say goodbye, 
You didn't see me cry, 
You didn't even know 
Something died inside of me. 

My heart's not beating now, 
I can't feel a thing, 
And you can't see 
Something died inside of me. 

Chorus ..... 

Oh I'm so lonely I don't know what to do 
and I'm trying very hard to get over you, 
I did all I could to make your dreams come true, 
And thought you were happy till you found someone new. 

Oh I hope you're happy now 
with the one you've chosen, 
But when you returned my ring 
Something died inside of me.
