I can love you forever - or forget that I do, 
You will decide - what will be true, 
I promise you love - straight from my heart, 
Only you know - if our love will start. 

Chorus ..... 

It's your choice - what will it be? 
Shall I plan our future - or set you free? 
You make the decision - I'll abide by your rules, 
I can love you forever - or forget that I do. 

II 

Yes - I can love you forever - or forget that I do, 
Do you want my love - or is there someone new? 
Is this the beginning - or is it the end? 
Tell me honey - do I lose - or do I win? 

Repeat Chorus ..... 

It's your choice - what will it be? 
Shall I plan our future - or set you free? 
You make the decision - I'll abide by your rules, 
I can love you forever - or forget that I do. 

Bridge ..... 

Oh baby I love you - more than you know, 
I never want - to let you go, 
I'll do anything - you want me to do
to make - all your dreams come true, 
If you'll say - you love you too. 

Musical Break ..... 

Repeat Chorus ..... 

It's your choice - what will it be? 
Shall I plan our future - or set you free? 
You make the decision - I'll abide by your rules, 
I can love you forever - or forget that I do. 

Tag..... 

Love you forever - or forget that I do.
