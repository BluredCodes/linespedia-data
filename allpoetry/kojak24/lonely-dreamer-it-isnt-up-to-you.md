I 

I know you're sad and want to cry 
for she broke your heart in two 
when she said her last goodbye, 
But there's nothing you can do, 
It isn't up to you - 

Lonely dreamer - it isn't up to you. 

II 

Maybe nothing will ever change 
or take away your pain, 
You'll always be - walking in the rain 
and your dreams won't come true, 
But It isn't up to you - 

Lonely dreamer - it isn't up to you. 

Chorus ..... 

Oh she made her choice and now she's gone 
- and your blue and all alone - wanting her to come back home, 
But there's nothing you can do - Lonely dreamer - it isn't up to you 
- She may never change or want you back - for you and her are through 
- and that's a fact. 

III 

But lonely dreamer - 
Don't run away and hide, 
Be a man - stand your ground - show your pride 
though you feel like dying inside, 
Remember - It isn't up to you - 

Lonely dreamer - it isn't up to you. 

IV 

But maybe you still occupy 
- a secret place inside her heart, 
And maybe she's pretending 
- it doesn't hurt to be apart, 
But it isn't up to you, 

Lonely dreamer - It isn't up to you. 

V 

Maybe one day she'll realize 
- how much she hurt you - when she walked away 
and she'll come back home to stay 
and maybe your dreams will come true - 
But it isn't up to you - 

Lonely dreamer- It isn't up to you - 

Tag ...

it isn't up to you.
