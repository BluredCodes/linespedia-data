A World Apart 

1st verse .....

You think you know what's on my mind 
but our thoughts are a world apart, 
For you can't see how I feel inside
or know the pain inside my heart.

2nd verse .....

Each day I fantasize a love so deep
tossing and turning all night long,
Calling out your name in my sleep
afraid I'll awake and find you gone.

Chorus  ....

Yes I know we're a world apart 
or you wouldn't captivate my soul,
Trying all the while to break my heart
another's love in your control.

3rd verse .....

A world apart - each day a broken heart,
Baby please don't make me blue, 
Or make the lonely teardrops start,
Just make believe your love is true. 

4th verse .....

No matter baby what you do,
Knock me down, destroy me with your lies, 
I'll still be reaching out to you 
to try to find some compromise. 

5th verse .....

For that is all that I can do, 
Hope you find a home within my heart
and change your mind and love me too, 
So we won't be a world apart.

Repeat Chorus  ....
