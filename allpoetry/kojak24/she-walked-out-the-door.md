I 

She walked out the door - didn't say goodbye, 
Didn't turn around - didn't see me cry, 
Said she had to go - couldn't take it anymore, 
Packed her things - walked out the door. 

Chorus .... 

Yes she walked out the door - and away from my heart, 
didn't stick around - to see me fall apart, 
All I could do - was stand and watch her go, 
knowing I loved her - more than she'd ever know. 

II 

She walked out the door - into the night, 
I bawled like a baby  - until it was light, 
No adios - no sad farewells, 
Just made my world - a living hell. 

2nd Chorus ..... 

Yes she walked out the door - and left me hurtin', 
I loved her too much - that's for certain, 
She walked out the door - didn't love me anymore, 
And now my loneliness - has opened hell's door. 

III

Yes she walked out the door - I died inside, 
Wanting to escape - with nowhere to hide. 
She walked out the door - Said our love couldn't be, 
She wanted to be free - And walked out the door on me. 

Tag ... 

She just walked out the door on me.
