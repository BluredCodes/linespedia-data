1st verse ..... 

I will be your everything 
forever and a day,
I will be your compass
to guide you on your way. 
I will be the shoulder
you lean on when you cry, 
I will be your angel
I'll never say goodbye. 

Chorus ..... 

I will be your friend and lover 
there'll never be another, 
When I reach out take my hand 
for you're my woman and I'm your man. 

3rd verse ..... 

Yes, I will be your knight in armor,
I'll pledge my life for you, 
I will be your solace 
and take away your blues. 

4th verse ..... 

I will be your comfort 
and protect you while we sleep, 
Dreaming in a sea of love 
that's oh so very deep. 

Repeat Chorus ..... 

Oh I will be your friend and lover 
there'll never be another, 
When I reach out take my hand 
for you're my woman and I'm your man. 

5th verse ..... 

Yes, I will be the one 
to keep you safe from harm 
holding you close in my loving arms 
and I'll never say goodbye. 

Tag ... 

No, I'll never say goodbye, 
Never say goodbye, 
Never say goodbye. 

by:  William M. Sowman
