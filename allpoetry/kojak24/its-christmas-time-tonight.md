I 

It's Christmas time tonight - let joy and happiness reign, 
Hear the children's laughter - listen to them sing, 
It's Christmas time tonight - gifts the wise men bring, 
All our hearts rejoice - glory to the King. 

Chorus ..... 

Oh yes it's Christmas time tonight - Christmas time tonight, 
Christmas time for all to see - including you and me, 
Christmas time for the lonely - Christmas time for the blue, 
Christmas time because - He'll make our dreams come true. 

II 

It's Christmas time tonight - souls are filled with light, 
A night of eternal love - Angels dance above, 
Praising Him in Heaven - to the stars on high, 
His presence so beautiful - His Kingdom they'll glorify. 

Repeat Chorus ..... 

Oh yes it's Christmas time tonight - Christmas time tonight, 
Christmas time for all to see - including you and me, 
Christmas time for the lonely - Christmas time for the blue, 
Christmas time because - He'll make our dreams come true. 

III 

Yes, it's Christmas time tonight - our Spirits He'll renew, 
For Christ was born this day - to shed His blood for you, 
Be still and listen - you'll hear His beckon call, 
Then you'll know our Savior - loves us all. 

Repeat Chorus ..... 

Oh yes it's Christmas time tonight - Christmas time tonight, 
Christmas time for all to see - including you and me, 
Christmas time for the lonely - Christmas time for the blue, 
Christmas time because - He'll make our dreams come true. 

Tag ... 

Christmas time ... Christmas time ... 
Christmas time tonight ... 
Hallelujah ... praise the Lord ... 
It's Christmas time tonight!
