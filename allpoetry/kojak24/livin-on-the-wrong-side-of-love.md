I 

Once you were mine - love was real,
Then you changed - it lost it's thrill,
Now I know - how lonliness feels,
Livin' - on the wrong side of love.

Chorus .....

Livin' - on the wrong side of love,
Cause you found someone new,
Livin' - on the wrong side of love,
My heart is broken in two,
Livin - on the wrong side of love,
There's nothin' I can do,
Just livin - on the wrong side of love.

II

You said each day - you'd love me more,
Then you turned - and walked out the door,
Leavin' me alone - pacin' the floor,
And livin' - on the wrong side of love.

Repeat Chorus .....

Livin' - on the wrong side of love,
Cause you found someone new,
Livin' - on the wrong side of love,
My heart is broken in two,
Livin - on the wrong side of love,
There's nothin' I can do,
Just livin - on the wrong side of love.

Bridge .....

Oh baby look what - you've done to me,
Promisin' love - that would never be,
Leavin' me - in a world of misery,
Livin' - on the wrong side of love.

Musical Break .....

Repeat Chorus .....

Livin' - on the wrong side of love,
Cause you found someone new,
Livin' - on the wrong side of love,
My heart is broken in two,
Livin - on the wrong side of love,
There's nothin' I can do,
Just livin - on the wrong side of love.

Tag .....

Yes, I'm livin'- on the wrong side of love.
