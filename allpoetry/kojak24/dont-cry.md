Don't cry -- 
Broken hearted fool don't cry, 
Dry your eyes and forget 
you've ever been in love. 

Don't cry -- 
My little dreamer don't cry, 
You should have realized 
he'd break your heart in two. 

BRIDGE 

You were oh so serious 
while he was playing around, 
To you love was so mysterious 
and to him a game for a clown. 

Don't cry -- 
Love for him cannot come true, 
While you're crying for him 
he'll be laughing at you. 

Don't cry -- 
Let me mend your broken heart 
and make your dreams come true 
with all my love for you. 

TAG 

Oh please please please -- 
don't cry. 
Please please -- 
don't cry.
