1st verse .... 

Since you walked out the door ... 
nothing matters anymore, 
Without your love to guide me ... 
nothing matters anymore, 
You burned your bridges behind you, 
left me to smother the flames, 
And nothing matters anymore. 

2nd verse ..... 

Now love's a solitary game ... 
every day seems the same, 
I spend each lonely night ... 
calling out your name, 
All my skies are grey 
since you went away, 
And nothing matters anymore. 

Chorus 

Vows you made to love me were lies to make the teardrops start, 
And the promises you whispered are now empty echoes in my heart, 
You filled my life with illusions then you tore my world apart, 
And nothing matters anymore. 

3rd verse ..... 

I know I should forget you ... 
and find someone new, 
But I'm lost and alone ... 
and there's nothing I can do, 
I'm too broken hearted ... 
to stop loving you, 
And nothing matters anymore.
