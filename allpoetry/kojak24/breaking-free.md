Breaking Free 

1st verse .....

I'm tired of misunderstandings 
and fed up with your lies, 
I'm breakin' free of shackles 
and cuttin' away the ties, 
I'm ready for another romance 
gonna find somebody new, 
Cause baby our love is over 
and I'm breakin' free of you. 

2nd verse .....

No more waitin' up at night 
for a knock upon my door, 
No more sittin' by that phone 
or pacin' the floor, 
The cryin' and the worryin'
the lonely nights are through,
Cause baby our love is over 
and I'm breakin' free of you. 

Bridge .... 

I'm breakin' free to love another 
who will always care for me, 
Someone sweet and gentle 
and nice as he can be, 
Who won't strike out in anger 
or ever try to rule, 
Who'll treat me like a woman 
and never like a fool. 

3rd verse .....

I'm breaking free of your love 
I'm gonna find someone new, 
Who'll love me for a lifetime 
and always be true, 
Someone who will love me 
just the way I am, 
Someone who will please me 
anyway he can. 

Musical interlude .....

4th verse .....

Who won't strike out in anger
or ever try to rule,
Who'll treat me like a woman
and never like a fool,
Yeh, I'm breakin' free of your love
I'm gonna find someone new,
Who will love me for a lifetime 
and always be true.

5th verse .....

Someone who will love me
just the way I am,
Someone who will please me
anyway he can,
The cryin' and my worryin'
my lonely nights are through,
Cause baby our love is over
and I'm breakin' free if you.

Tag .....

I'm breakin' free of you.
