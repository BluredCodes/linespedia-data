I



Baby I can't give you diamonds, 

riches I have none, 

But honey I'll give you 

a heart full of love. 



II 



I'll fill your world with sunshine,

paint rainbows in your sky, 

Each night I'll bring you 

a heart full of love. 



Chorus ..... 



A heart full of love and crazy desire,

Cause baby you set my soul on fire,

A heart full of love and kisses too,

Anything to make your dreams come true.



Honey I'll give to you a heart full of love.



Musical Break ....



III 



Oh a heart full of love

that will never grow old,

Or ever desert you

when nights are cold.



IV



A heart full of love

for the rest of my life,

Oh come on baby, 

be my wife.



Repeat chorus .....



A heart full of love and crazy desire,

Cause baby you set my soul on fire,

A heart full of love and kisses too,

Anything to make your dreams come true. 



Honey I'll give to you a heart full of love.



Musical Break .....



Repeat Chorus .....



A heart full of love and crazy desire,

Cause baby you set my soul on fire,

A heart full of love and kisses too,

Anything to make your dreams come true. 



Honey I'll give to you a heart full of love.



Tag .....



A heart full of love.
