Our love is over now. Patricia is gone.

 
I'm living in a world of broken hearts, 
I'm so lonely and blue, 
A world of broken hearts 
and there's nothing I can do. 

I thought you were my woman 
and would make my dreams come true, 
now even the man in my mirror knows 
what a fool I've been over you. 

Chorus ..... 

Yes, I believed in you, thought you loved me too, 
Then you ran away into the arms of someone new, 
Leaving me with teardrops and memories of you, 
Forgotten and alone in my world of broken hearts 

Oh a world of broken hearts 
Where each day I pretend 
You're here beside me 
and our love will never end. 

Tag ..... 

But deep inside I know it's not true 
and I'm broken hearted 
cause I still love you. 



:::::::::::::::::::: 

Goodbye Patricia and thanks for the ride. 
I hope you are happy with him and your 
love lasts forever. I just wonder why 
did you pick me to make a fool of. There 
were plenty of others you could have selected, 
Many would have been glad to play the fool 
for you. Why me? ... Who loved you with 
all my heart. Goodbye and may you find 
happiness and God bless you and your 
family. "Will Murray"
