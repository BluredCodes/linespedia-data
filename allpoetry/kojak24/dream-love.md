1st verse 

I thought our love was reality 
and you would always belong to me, 
But now I see what a fool I've been 
I fell for someone I couldn't win. 

1st Chorus ..... 

Oh dream love - dream love - a fantasy 
you never really - cared for me, 
You're tied to another - and wear his ring 
but your kisses  - make my heart sing. 

2nd verse 

I should have known from the start 
you were lying to my foolish heart, 
When you said you wanted to be free 
to share your dream of love with me. 

2nd Chorus ..... 

Oh dream love - dream love - can't you see 
our dream of love wasn't meant to be, 
You'll never belong to me 
Oh dream love - dream love - please set me free. 

Tag............
