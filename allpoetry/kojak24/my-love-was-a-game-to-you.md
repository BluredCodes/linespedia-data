I 

You were everything to me 
My dream, my hope, my desire, 
You set my heart on fire, 
But all the time we shared -- 

My love - was a game to you. 

II 

Oh I pledged my soul 
and everyone knew 
I belonged to you 
and would always be true -- 

But my love - was a game to you. 

III 

You destroyed my world 
when you let me go 
telling me that 
you didn't care -- 

And my love - was a game to you. 

Chorus ..... 

You cut me deep, hurt my pride, broke me down inside 
and made me cry a heart full of tears - enough to last for years, 
Oh I couldn't breathe or think about anyone but you 
and I didn't know what to do - I only knew that we we're through. 

And my love was a game to you. 

IV 

Yes, you cast me aside 
and let me go 
hurting me more 
than you'll ever know -- 

Cause my love - was a game to you. 

VI 

I believed in you, 
Thought we wouldn't part, 
But you broke my heart 
and now I know that 

My love - was just a game to you. 

VII 

But I'll never -- 
love anyone but you 
Though I know -- 
My love --was just a game to you.
