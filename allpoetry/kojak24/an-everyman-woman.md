I 

You're an everyman woman, 
Who loves to play around, 
Spreading love all over town, 
First it's Jack or maybe Joe,
You're always ready to go.   


Chorus ..... 

Yeah - an everyman woman - who'll go all the way, 
Even in the back seat - of an old chevrolet, 
Your knees get weak - when a man looks at you, 
For a little lovin' - ain't nothin' you won't do. 

II 

Oh you're an everyman woman, 
Who made a fool out of me, 
Now I'm gonna' set you free, 
I loved you baby more than you knew, 
But now, you and I are through. 

Repeat Chorus ..... 

Yeah - an everyman woman - who'll go all the way, 
Even in the back seat - of an old chevrolet, 
Your knees get weak - when a man looks at you, 
For a little lovin' -  ain't nothin' you won't do. 

Bridge ..... 

Oh baby I've tried to win your love, 
Even praying to God above, 
But no matter what you say or do, 
There's no way you'll ever be true. 

Musical Break .....

Repeat Chorus ..... 

Yeah - an everyman woman - who'll go all the way, 
Even in the back seat - of an old chevrolet, 
Your knees get weak - when a man looks at you, 
For  a little lovin' - ain't nothin' you won't do. 

Tag ..... 

You're an everyman woman 
and I'm just a one woman man, 
An everyman woman 
and I'm just a one woman man.
