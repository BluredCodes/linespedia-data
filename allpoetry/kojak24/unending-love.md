I 

We didn't know love would be 
wild crazy feelings in our hearts, 
An urgent need deep inside 
each lonely night apart. 

II 

Unending love in the twinkling of an eye, 
Feelings so deep within our souls, 
A love so strong it would not be denied, 
Only unending love could make us whole. 

Chorus ... 

Oh we do crazy things to please our lover, 
Acting like fools for all the world to see, 
Nothing's too great for one another, 
This isn't what we thought our love would be. 

III 

How could we know the urgent need inside 
to share our love with each other, 
How could we know until we felt within 
the special thrills of unending love. 

IV 

Oh this love is our exotic treasure, 
Let's take the plunge and enjoy the ride, 
We'll dance till dawn for erotic pleasure 
knowing we will always share unending love.
