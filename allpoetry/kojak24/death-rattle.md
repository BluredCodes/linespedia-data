I'LL ........... I'LL ..........I'LL ........... I'LL
LOVE ........ LOVE ...... LOVE ........ LOVE
YOU ......... YOU ........ YOU ......... YOU
BEYOND .. BEYOND . BEYOND .. FOR
DEATH ..... SEASON .. THE .......... A
.................................... GRAVE ..... MILLON
....................................................... REASONS
                     
Spirit of my heart, please don't run away 
or disappear into the nether world, 
Come sit by my side and be my naked girl, 
Strip bear to the bone and prepare for my invasion. 

I am too tired to use gentle persuasion, 
Lean back in the grave and seek my advances, 
I know your ghostly figure loves to take chances, 
Let me penetrate your flesh of putrid swelling 
with my sword so thick and strong ... 
To plunge inside of you all night long,
While you suck my rod as I sang you a song. 

Let the Shepards bring their flocks 
so my sperm can bless the giver, 
Stay close to me and let us eat their livers, 
Oh breasts of death await my magic touch ... 
my tongue goes deep to seek out its lunch, 
Who are we to stumble????????? 
Sucking your pussy makes me so humble! 

Oh Goddess of love ... let Billy hold you 
among the snakes and swamps of bliss, 
Oh come on girl - give me a midnight kiss, 
Spread your thighs my little Miss. 

Babies we will birth this night ... 
for eons of time and light, 
All is right and the black has passed away, 
You and I have had our judgment day. 
Help I am falling! Stop let go of my rod! Oh God ... 
how sweet it is!!!!!!! 
Billy and his Goddess of Love forever more!!! 
OH 
GOD 
OPEN 
THE FUCKING DOOR AND LET ME OUT OF HERE! 
INSANITY 
IS 
MINE! 
PRAISE THE LORD!!
