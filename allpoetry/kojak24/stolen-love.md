I 

I shared a stolen love with you, 
A love that could never come true, 
And now I'm paying the price 
for living in a fool's paradise. 

Chorus ..... 

Oh stolen love - stolen love - broken dreams - heartaches too, 
So lonely - sad and blue - what can I do - to get over you? 

II 

Yes stolen love in our rendezvous, 
In a world where dreams are new, 
Holding you close in our hideaway 
Not thinking about the price I'd pay. 

Chorus ..... 

Oh stolen love - stolen love - broken dreams - heartaches too, 
So lonely - sad and blue - what can I do - to get over you? 

III 

Yes Stolen love - sweet desire 
kisses that set me on fire - 
Drove me wild - wouldn't let me be, 
Now I live alone - in my fantasy. 

Chorus ..... 

Oh stolen love - stolen love - broken dreams - heartaches too, 
So lonely - sad and blue - what can I do - to get over you? 

IV 

Oh stolen love tearing me apart, 
Living now with my broken heart, 
Knowing you still belong to him 
My stolen love just couldn't win. 

Chorus ..... 

Oh stolen love - stolen love - broken dreams - heartaches too, 
So lonely  - sad and blue - what can I do - to get over you?
