Come here baby - close to me, 
Touch my heart -  set it free. 
Kiss my lips - sing me a song, 
Make love to me - all night long.

I love you so - words can't tell, 
I'm completely - under your spell. 
Without you - life would be hell, 
Your love - really rings my bell.
