Crazy Over You 

verse 1

While I pretend I don't care
that you're with someone new,
I go crazy inside -- when I think of you.

verse 2

And though our love is over
and there's nothing I can do,
I go crazy inside -- when I think of you.

Chorus 1

Yes I go crazy -- when someone speaks your name
Crazy -- my heart is filled with pain
I go crazy -- every lonely moment the same
I'm crazy -- and I know that you're to blame.

Verse 3

Yes I go crazy inside -- when I think of you
Cuz you hurt my pride -- and I feel so blue
Your cheatin' drives me crazy -- you just can't be true
I go crazy inside -- since you said we're through.

Chorus 2

Yes, I go crazy -- nothing will every change
Crazy -- my world is filled with rain
I go crazy -- my dreams all down the drain
I'm crazy - crazy - crazy over you.
