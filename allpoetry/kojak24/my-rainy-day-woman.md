For love to work both parties MUST be completely committed. Only then is love real. Until that commitment is made ... it is only a fantasy. Only one of us made that the eternal vow.

 
Someday ... 
My Rainy Day Woman 
you will hear 
the echo of my love 
calling you 
from a far off place 
inside your heart. 

Someday ... 
My Rainy Day Woman 
I will whisper 
in your ear that 
all I wanted 
was unconditional love 
from the start. 

Asking you to love me   
completely and forever,
beyond reason, without doubt, or hesitation; 
Living fully in the moment shared, 
not thinking of yesterday or tomorrow, 
forgetting the past, forgetting the sorrow. 

Someday ... 
My Rainy Day Woman 
when it's too late, 
You'll reach out to me 
calling out my name in 
darkness of your despair 
but I won't be there. 

Someday ... 
My Rainy Day Woman 
your tears will fall 
like cloud bursts 
from the sky, 
When you remember 
how you made me cry. 

Someday ... 
My Rainy Day Woman 
but too late, 
much too late for you, 
You will know 
that's how much 
you love me too. 

Yes, 
someday, 
someday, 
someday, 
that's how much
you'll love me too 
love me too 
love me too 
love me too. 
the way I love you. 

by: Will Murray (for) Patricia M. Williams
