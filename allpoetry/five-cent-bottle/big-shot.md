---
title: "BIG sHOT"
writer: "Five Cent Bottle"
slug: "big-shot"
source: "AllPoetry"

It was in desperation and loneliness
When they captured me
My heart jumps for the unknown
I mean I rather be safe

And sacrifice all my  liberty

Then be free
