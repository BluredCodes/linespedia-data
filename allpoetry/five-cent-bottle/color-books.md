Tell me about yourself in Technicolor lights 
The dark nights in blue, cyan and pink
The sunny days in orange black and green
What about the semi-phonic sounds of enlightenment?
Do they make the cloudy middays go by faster?
If I were to cut up the echo into hand held pieces
How many would you take?
Escalate into defeat
Until you twirl into the sands that don’t exist
I can run into the feel of the ocean
And see the likes of Japan from so far away
We both know in this small world we are very big



How can you justify your word peace?
You’ve already killed so many with your wholesomeness
So many turn out fine
Until you try to steal their mindsHey. All I am going to say is I am new, soooo hey.
