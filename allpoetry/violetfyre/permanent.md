---
title: "Permanent"
writer: "violetfyre"
slug: "permanent"

The economics professor danced circles
With the overhead projector.
20 minutes of sleep or dream
Or spying on the neighbors.
I was always good at eavesdropping
And reading over shoulders.
Permanent markers decorate best
A desk, a wall, a face.
I am hidden, black, unwashable...
See the scars in beauty's place.

See the economic professor bang fists
Against the chalkboard.
15 minutes of dents or fractures
Or draining blood on the floor.
I have always been a vampire
And a devil with angel eyes.
Permanent vacations are planned best
A new town, a new name, a new face.
I am hidden, black, gone for good...
See the smile in place of tears.
