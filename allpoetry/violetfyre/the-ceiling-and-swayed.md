---
title: "The Ceiling and Swayed"
writer: "violetfyre"
slug: "the-ceiling-and-swayed"
source: "AllPoetry"

I can't feel the love there,
If any is there at all.
All I feel is a warm hand on my knee,
And I convince myself it's the same.

I poked him in the ribs.
He stopped snoring.
A doped-up eye looked at me wearily.
He rolled the other way.

There were chairs on top of tables,
And rainbows on the shelves,
Lamps with plastic shades dangled
From the ceiling and swayed.

It was heaven and hell and everything between.
I convulsed with shivers.
An angel with a devil grin kissed me
Before he left to set the world on fire.

But I couldn't feel any love.
We talked about Christmas,
Dressing me in wrapping paper
And a bow... and nothing else.

A devil with angel eyes
Kissed me.
Left my heart on fire.
But I didn't feel love.
