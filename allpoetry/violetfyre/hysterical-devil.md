title: "Hysterical Devil"

Everything you push to square
Is not everything you've held over my head
That twitch in my eye is you
And everything you've put me through
So stop laughing in your hysterical way
Because I'm ignoring everything begging for my attention
I'm ignoring everything that shot me down before

I loved you Angel, I literally loved you to death
And now you're flying with your silver wings
Aflame in realms of hell
So keep away
This leash was not meant for you to drag me

I can see in her honesty
And see it all in your lies
That you were never worth my pain

You were never worth my pain.

So tonight Jack Daniels and I will sew up
All the holes in my soul and rips in my heart
To make more room for holes in my head
And tears in my eyes.
