title: "Easy"
writer: "violetfyre"
slug: "easy"

He said I fall easily
Out of the sky and into arms
And out of the blue I fall away.

He said I was fooling myself,
Thinking love and love were both the same,
Thinking I was strong enough to resist,
Denying things were just a game.

He said I was easy,
A sure thing for anyone,
Cold and unfeeling,
Incapable of love.

I walked away.
What good is common knowledge?
