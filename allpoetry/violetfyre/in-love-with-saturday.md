---
title: "In Love with Saturday"
writer: "violetfyre"

It was sunny for an instant
Before my eyes could only see my mind
My heart could only think of yesterday
And Saturday was close behind.

And I was in love.

I clutched an angel with devil eyes
I wanted to kiss his arsenic lips
I wanted him to squeeze me until I died
Until he poisoned me with his kiss.

And I was in pain.

Nothing's the same as it was last week
My heart is fickle, my mind is numb
The darkness closes in on me

My mind's confused, my heart is weak
Promises would drip from my lifeless tongue
If I had the ability to speak

And I was a fool.
And I am a fool.

Nothing's sacred in Saturday's starlight.
Not love or pride or happiness
Or life.
