title: "Brandon's Shadow"
writer: "violetfyre"

We were in Brandon Park
In the dark
Listening to leaves skitter
Across the softball field,
Thinking they were footsteps.
Listening to the wind rustling
Through the tree branches,
Thinking they were voices.
Listening to the clock chime ten.

We were in Brandon Park
In the dark
Beneath a lamppost
That shed white light
In a place full of orange.
She would have mused how
Shadows cannot speak,
If she'd been able to talk at all.
She might have stared at me
If she didn't have to follow my gaze
As my head spun around and around
And the swing rocked back and forth.
She might have left me sitting alone
In the dark
In Brandon Park
If she could have.
