title: "Trust"

This is another dizzy moment.I'm losing my balanceDrowning in your voice.Your warm breath on my cheekIs spinning meSpinning meFaster into a world of red roses.This is another dream.I'm losing myself in a false consciousnessLike religion.Your sweet words revolving in my headAre killing meKilling meWhile I sleep on a bed of red roses.We are passionateFire.This love is everything.The numinousIs nothing.This love is nothingI've ever been able to trustBut I trust you'll never lie.And life is burning usBurning usWith a love that will never die.**I've never been good with love.
