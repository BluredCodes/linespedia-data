title: "Here"

I am still right here
Looking out over a shallow town
While down south, Angel makes a drug run
And Beauty will only wear high-heeled boots.

Love's wearing a leather leash
And I am still right here
With kids who can't hold their liquor
Or a straight face
Long enough for the cops to pass.

Love's screaming lyrics to distorted guitars
And I am still right here
Amid basketball games and muddy fields
Where kids used to get fined
For rolling on rainy days.

Angel is howling at the moon
Confused about animal instincts
After ten too many swigs of Jack Daniels.

Beauty's cheeks are lined with tears and mascara
Her lipstick's smudged, her dress is torn
She looks to be the ugliest thing in the world.

And Love is the only one that hasn't lost his mind
He reads St. Vincent-Millay on a velour blanket
Wishing God would quit taking all the credit for his work
He says, "I am still right here."
