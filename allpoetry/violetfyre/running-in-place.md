Where are you?
I'm the same place I've always been.
Flying and lying
With no regard for consequences.
I'm bouncing off the walls as they're closing in.

Where are you?
I'm the same place I'll always be.
Crying and dying
With no shame or pride or love.
I'm screaming at the sky as it falls down on me.

And I tried to open my eyes, open my mind
But I still can't figure out where things go
When they've gone.

I've tried to let go.
I'm shaking off leeches as they suck the life from me.
