title: "Like Beautiful"
writer: "violetfyre"

Beautiful is not a word.
It is a flattery, a cleverly crafted lie.
Such a gathering of letters
To draw smiles and sweet kisses
From pale-faced girls who have yet
To see the backseat of a Camaro.

Beautiful is not a word,
But a trick to draw her in.
A key so easily obtained.
Like "I love you," it's a shortcut
To the promised land--
A woman's heart, a girl's bed.

Beautiful is not a word.
It's a mockery of love.
Like roses, it is wasted
As it strikes my stubborn ears
And shatters, like hearts,
Like the world I've gone numb to.
