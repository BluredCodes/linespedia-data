I awoke this morning to heavy air
With a shadowing feeling of despair

I did not open my eyes at first
afraid I'd be hurt, shocked, or cursed

I felt as though I was moving
I opened my eyes but I saw nothing

When I realized I was in a hearse
I remembered dying of unquenchable thirstIt's kind of a riddle. It has a definite spiritual meaning. It's funny to me in a kind of crude cynical way. ;^) ~JFish~
