Throughout literary history
star-crossed lovers
have left their legacy

Son of Uther Pendragon and Igraine
united the land,
built Camelot,
was betrayed by his wife and best friend
drank from the Holy Grail
killed his son,
rested forever in Avalon.

Corwin and Alissa,
from feuding worlds,
decide to marry
to unite their land
against the Beast
one look into each others eyes
they knew that their love
was the only truth.
savagely torn apart by the Beast,
he and a band of men went to find her
including the old one whose true love 
became the Widow of the Web
but in an instant to save the world,
she gave her life to the girl that 
bears her name
Corwin and Alissa were reunited
and their son ruled the galaxy

Cowboy and me,
from two different races
united in love,
daring the world to come between us
our children,
will heal a nation
and continue
the legacy of star-crossed lovers
