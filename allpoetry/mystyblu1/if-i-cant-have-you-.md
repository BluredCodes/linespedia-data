title: "If I can't have you ...."

If I can't have you in the flesh,
then I will settle for this,
I will immortalize your goodness
in my book.

It is a tale of star crossed lovers,
you and me,
two people who have no business 
being in love,
for I am 10 yrs older and 2 shades
darker than you, my dear.
but I am the creator of my dreams
and in the book, you don't care.
We live a good life, yes we have
conflicts, my family won't
accept you, etc. etc.,
but each night, in the book,
you take me in your arms and love me.

In my mind, I hope that you will
see yourself in the book,
average height, dark curly hair,
moustache, beard, tight bod,
great smile, nice eyes,
and want to meet the writer,
probably won't happen,
but just to help you along,
the main character has your name,
personal pain you have suffered,
triumphs and victories,
otherwise known as your life.

Worst case scenario,
you sue me,
so what,
if i can't have you next to me 
each night in person,
then I will have you each day,
in my book.
