While watching TV one day,
I came across the most
beautiful man I'd ever seen,
looked into his eyes and
straight into his soul.

Strange as it sounds,
with glass, screen, technology,
I knew he was the one for me.

I cast the stones to see if it was really
meant to be,
found his website,
sent many emails,
received few responses,
most very politically correct,
short and to the point,
"the typical male,"
but one response was written
when defenses were down,
sharing innermost
desires, secrets, dreams,
I felt so privileged being
allowed this glimpse into his heart.

Right then I knew
we belonged together,
convincing him was a different story,
slowly through carefully 
constructed emails,
I piqued his interests of me,
getting him to want to know more.

His curiousity aroused,
(secretly wishing it was his loins)
numbers were exchanged,
phone calls were made,
laying on the couch trembling with
sheer delight of hearing his
deep voice,
we truly connected.

Meeting the gentle soul of 
a kind hearted man in the flesh
is breathtaking.
Staring at him over coffee,
I knew I was right,
but then,
I always am,
especially when it comes to
matters of the heart.

Later that day,
watching him on the beach,
tanned and relax,
laying on the sand,
catching the last rays of
a fading sun,
I was reminded,
that sometimes the fates can be kind.

While being held in those 
strong arms,
I wondered if he was ready to accept
what was to be,
put aside his fears,
and just love me,
as i love him,
or will he deny his destiny.

The choice is his,
my heat beats with anticipation,
and my soul will wait,
forever.
