Oh Hardy, my unattainable love,
you would have been so proud,
for once in my sad, sorrowful life,
I refused to back down,
stood up for my sanity,
resisted the voices of my demons.

I didn't beg you to help,
no plea nor whine,
nor tearful overture.

I just said enough,
decided to save my soul,
stared down my oppressors,
and finally found peace.

Oh, you would have been proud.
