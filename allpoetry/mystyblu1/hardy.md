Oh my darlin,how are you?I have watched you for so longand feel that I know you.the real you.When I look at you,I can see a soul,still tortured by memoriesof past hurts.Your physical being encompasses,overwhelms andhaunts me.From the black hairto the smoldering eyes,the infectious smileand the voice that can makeme do anythingYour chiseled body thatgets worked on daily(I need your discipline)strong arms, tight pecs, oh and that other part of youstraining against your shortsto be revealed.I dwell in your beauty.You did things thatwere immoral, unethicaland downright dirty.But, your tears were genuine,guilt working on your heart,eating at your soul.Back to the real world,would we ever meet?hopefully,would you want me?maybe.What the hell,it's  what I'm wishing for.It's time for you to get back to your life,extend your fifteen minutes.but remember,if you need a true friendI am always here.
