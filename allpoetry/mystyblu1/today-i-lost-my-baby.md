title: "Today I Lost My Baby"

Nurse Jackson is very nice
with her supportive words,
kind hugs,
but my tears refuse to subside,
today I lost my baby.

My clock was ticking ever so loudly,
finally I met someone,
who could show me what
forever means,
wanted this child
for him,
for me,
for us.

The pain started
early in the morning,
just a twinge,
then a dull ache,
until I was bent over,
screaming to my love,
I'm losing our baby.

Sirens blaring
rushing me to the hospital,
laying on the stretcher
soaked in blood,
urgently praying and
hoping an angel won't put me on hold.

My voice wasn't heard,
ten minutes later
my baby was gone,
one hour later,
my true love left me,
forever.

Family is supportive
saying it wasn't meant to be,
no one truly understands,
I hear my baby crying,
day and night,
want my baby back,
but she is with the angels,
soon I will join her.
