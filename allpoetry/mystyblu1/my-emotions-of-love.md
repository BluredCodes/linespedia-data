I shiver watching you shower,
tremble while you dress,
shake as you lean forward 
to gently kiss me
good morning.

Chills run up and down my spine,
when you say "Babydoll"
sometimes my tongue gets so tied,
I can't even talk to you,
only babble in some 
distant language.

Butterflies return to my stomach,
when you glance back over your shoulder
wearing a grin,
my knees weaken when you crook
your finger and beckon me closer,
one day my knees will let me
down and I will only be able
to crawl.

Lost weight cause I get so wrapped up in 
watching you eat,
that I forget all about the food
on my plate.

Stood behind you while you typed
on the computer,
some complex software formula,
didn't care what it was about,
just enjoyed watching those 
masculine fingers caress the keys.

Every fiber of my being comes alive
when you enter me,
I feel so good and 
at peace with the world,
you take me to new heights 
every time we make love.

My days are filled with
shivering,
shuddering,
trembling,
leaning on the wall for support,
my love for you is
endless.
