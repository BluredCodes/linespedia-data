title: "Invisible to the one"
writer: "mystyblu1"

Have you ever wondered
why no matter how hard
you try to attract attention
the one still ignores you?

I have sent emails,
no response.
I have mailed letters,
silence.
I have posted on message boards,
no reply.

I must be the only invisible person
with a computer.
I tell myself not to take it personally,
the one is very busy,
but I see those who post above me
and below me get answered.
just keeps missing me.

I don't know what to do,
I want my fifteen minutes too,
who doesn't?
What I want even more,
is acknowldgement that I am alive
on this planet and
my thoughts matter,
count,
mean something
to the one I dream about.
