Can you see inside my soul?
I hope so,
lying here watching you sleep,
peace washes over me,
soaking into every pore,
my eyes close to the sensation,
contentment fills my being.

If possible, seek out my thoughts 
that dwell on you,
Oh, kindred spirit,
snoring softly besides me,
never have I felt so deep
this wonderous thing called love,
I bask in the effect.

Take a closer look inside a once tortured soul,
you who brought me out of the darkness,
rolling over, tossing off the covers,
allowing me one more glimpse of a 
sculptured body,
harden by exercise,
and sensual thoughts of me,
a gentle reminder of how
lucky a woman can be.
