title: "While my life is on hold"

While my life is on hold,
I will think only about you,
with your black curls,
incredible biceps,
pretty eyes,
my new obsession.

While my on again, off again
relationship is once more
off again,
I will dream of the two of us,
entwined like the lovers,
I hope we could be.

While my size is dwindling
to something more acceptable
to society,
I will stare at your picture,
and wish that it would come alive,
then you wink at me
and everything is all right.

While I exist in a holding pattern,
waiting to be the slim, beautiful
woman that,
in a sea of admirers,
your eyes would meet mine,
you'd smile and beckon me closer,
connection made,
it's love at first sight,
well on your part as
I love you already.

Until then it's pictures to look at,
videotapes to watch over and over,
dreams to chase the night away,
wishful thinking and prayers
for a miracle.
