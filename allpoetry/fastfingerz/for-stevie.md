title: "for stevie"

little child so lost in the world, where will you go when all is said and done?  little child, have no fear, for i am with you till the end.  little child, i will not leave, little child, you need not grieve.  little child, i will see you be a man some day. little child, i am your friend.  little child, we'll make amends.  little child, our dreams will come true.  little child, tho you may not believe it, i love you.
