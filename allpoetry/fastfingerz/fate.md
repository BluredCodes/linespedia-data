look outside the trees are crying for the loss of their young  everywhere people gather and wonder what is left out there.  you bitch about your plight, but refuse to fix it.  remember the way things used to be, all the dreams we had.  i went off to fight my war, but came back a different man.  i go insane as i feel the ground beneath my feet, we got people driving fancy cars while children starve and die in the streets.  people afraid to stand and fight for what they believe.  the shots ring out in a cold december night, the screams of pain from the fallen child.
