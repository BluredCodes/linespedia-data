show your guns they scream in the air in the night, give up you think as the knife cuts you deep and the blood flows red and stains your sheets.  stand up and join the armies of the nights, stand up and fight againsdt the tyranny in the world.  is it the fear of the hunt that keeps you at bay, or the fact that you may become prey?  i am your god
