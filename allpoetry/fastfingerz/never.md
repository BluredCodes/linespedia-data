title: "never"

thinking back to a time of innocence.  i see the pain and feel the hate. all i wanted to do was reach out and touch the sky.  i didn't want to know her nor wanted to see her.  i didn't want to love her, all i wanted to do was hate her.  but from the moment i first saw her, i could tell that dreams do come true.  she adored me, but looks at me with pity, she wants me to leave, but won't say go away.  she wants me to die, but won't kill me
