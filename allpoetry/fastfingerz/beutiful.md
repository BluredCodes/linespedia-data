she took everything beutiful in the world for herself and turned ugly.  and now here is she?  living in a rut with her husband wh doesn't even speak to her anymore, and her 3 iligitiamite bastard children.  where am i?  on top of the world, trying to live large, but something in me wonders what would have happened had i not got on that plane
