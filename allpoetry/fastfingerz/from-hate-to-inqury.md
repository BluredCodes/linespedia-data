title: "from hate to inqury"
writer: "fastfingerz"

with all the rage i felt for you, and all the ways i hated too.  i never knew how to talk to you.  so i sat and i wrote.  i was staring at the center of the universe, i was only allowed to catch a glimpse.  then one lonely afternoon, sheer boredom took the place of my discust, and you shone like an angel before my eyes.
