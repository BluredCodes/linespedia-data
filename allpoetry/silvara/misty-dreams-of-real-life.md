---
title: "Misty Dreams of Real Life"
writer: "silvara"

My heart aches as it always does
But why this time?
It’s different; another kind of pain
My loneliness remains as it always has
But now I feel a pain from a far
As if from a near future
Or a distant past

All is strange, and misty
We wait for change, as we see it come
Then feel it pass
So is life, our dreary torment

But there stands a light in our darkness
A light to bring me out
I reach for it, like I reach for stars
I can never touch it, but I feel it’s warmth
So is my dream
To one day grab hold and never let go
One day it will come
And so I wait

If love hurts, loneliness kills
