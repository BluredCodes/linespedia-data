title: "True Love’s Torment"
writer: "silvara"

Life in torment is such pain
As is woven in love’s vain

Trying, testing, our loves vine
Living only but a lie
Hoping, dreading our love’s end
Knowing only to pretend

Loving, hoping, wanting more
You forever I’ll adore
Wanting, wishing for your love
As though promised from above

Knowing only love’s true end
Knowing that I can’t pretend
Loving you for all and all
For no other will I fall.
