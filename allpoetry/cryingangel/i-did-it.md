title: "I Did It"
writer: "cryingangel"

I did it.I caved in and called.I knew I shouldn't.I thought I couldn't.Now I wish I wouldn't.Talking to him was so nice.I asked if he had moved on with life.I saw that he hadn't and I was his number one.I wish I wouldn't but its already done.I did it.I told him I loved him.And he said it too.I somehow knew it was true.I knew I shouldn't.I thought I couldn't.Now I wish I wouldn't.
