History books,
will be filled,
with all this news.
Makes me mad.
People are happy.
This is sad. 
People are dead.
Lives are ruined.
All for what?
All because of?
No one knows.
Well someone must!
God.
He knows.
Didn't stop them.
The Evil has come.
The End has just begun.
The world--is done.
