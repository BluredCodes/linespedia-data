What does happiness cost?
I don’t really know.
I am so very lost.
I really want to go.

I want true happiness.
Not something fake.
Just some eternal bliss
Something to take.

Happiness costs more than some think.
Others know the true tool.
It can all be gone in a wink.
The cost is all of your soul.

But what happens if you can not pay?
Does your life continue for another day?
