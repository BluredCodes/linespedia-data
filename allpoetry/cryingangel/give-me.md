---
title: "Give Me"
writer: "cryingangel"

Give me your hand.Let me guide you to land.I’ll take you out of the sea.If you will be with me.I’ll take you where no one else has before.I will have you asking for so much more.Darling, I will guide you through life.If and only if, I can be your wife.I love you so much. With each and every touch.I’ll take away your pain.And keep away the shame.You are my everything.Only good I will bring.So give me your hand,And let me guide you to land.
