Our first kiss was amazing.
So full of hesitation.
Yet full of love and desire.

It was full of want and passion.
Emotion and feeling.
Nothing I can describe completely.

You held me close
As though you were never...
never letting me go.

You kissed me forever 
as though there would be...
be no other.

You put so much feeling behind it
That I knew I wanted more.
Many more.

The kiss was awesome in
Everyway
and there was definately more to follow.

I remember everything 
about our first kiss.
Even the way you looked into my eyes before.

Our first kiss showed all of the love 
we had for eachother.
I'm just glad we waited so long.
Because it was PERECT!
