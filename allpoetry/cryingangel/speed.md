My stomach it growls.Another day I did not eat.I have lost 10 pounds in 2 days.I feel better about myself.It's sad that I have to be stick thin.To feel good about myself.Thats what the world has come to now.That also when I am not on this.I am bent over the toilet throwing up after I eat. Now the powder is making me shake.And very hyper as hell.I havent slept in 2 nights.All because of this wonderful drug.That is found out on the street.I think it's kinda neat.I am tweeking right now. I often when I get very depressed use 'speed' to keep me up and to lose the extra pounds that might cause my depression. it makes me feel better. just thought i would share. now that i am addicted i caution that speed will have some great results, but can also really mess you up.
