---
title: "Not Going"
writer: "cryingangel"
slug: "not-going"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I’m not going to let you down.Never going to let you frown.Going to take all the pain.And leave only things to gain.I’m going to keep you happy.Make you smile when you feel crappy.I’m always going to put your first.Even if I feel the worst.One hundred percent I believe.You have so much to achieve.There are so many things you can do.Even if it feels like only a few.Baby, I love you more than anything alive.Is that really such a big surprise?
