title: "Don't Want To See"
writer: "cryingangel"

You know two years ago when we first met, 
I thought I was truly your very best.

You took my heart from the first day,
You never gave it back but I had to pay.
You left me there all alone,
All I wanted was to go home.

I love you more than words can say.
You make me happy from day to day.
I love everything about you and your thug appeal.
When you ask me to marry you, you better kneel.

You are my life and you are my love,
I hope we work everything out because,
I don't want to see another girl touching you or kissing you.
And I don't want to see another girl holding you or loving you. 
I don't want to see you with anyone else but me!
