I often wonder what he sees in me, Then he responds with:Your star struck eyes,Your gorgeous hair, Your cute little nose, Your kissable lips,Your lovable heart,Your warm soul, Your great body, Your wonderful personality,Your tender touch, Your loving words,and your brilliant mind.But the reason why he loves me most is because I am ME!!(He loves everything about me!)
