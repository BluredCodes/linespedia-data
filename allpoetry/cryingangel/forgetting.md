title: "Forgetting"
writer: "cryingangel"

I remember again
what happened that day
The day he was killed.
It plays over and over 
in my mind.
In hopes I will not forget.

I could never forget
the love he brought or 
the love he took
when he left.

I can not forget
the pain I have had,
the tears I have cried, 
or the look on his face.

Most of all, I guess
I can not forget
because since the day he left,
my life has been a mess!
