---
title: "Black"
writer: "cryingangel"
slug: "black"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Black is all the colors put together. Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, Blue, Purple, and Brown.
Black is the color of all the things I see.
The sky, the floor, the color of my mind.
Black is the color of the night sky without the stars that burn so bright.
Black is the color of my heart, the way I feel right now.
