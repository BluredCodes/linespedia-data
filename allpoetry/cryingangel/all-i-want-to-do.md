title: "All I Want To Do"
writer: "cryingangel"

To feel you, 
To love you, 
To hold you,
That's all I want to do.
But you tell me you love her. 
Now what am I supposed to do?
I was the one there when you needed it the most.
Now you're just going to leave me and move to the other coast?
Baby, I am the one who loves you the most!
