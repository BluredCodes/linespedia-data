---
title: "You Broke"
writer: "cryingangel"
slug: "you-broke"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/5315-You-Broke-by-cryingangel"
---

You broke my heart when you said goodbye.
You broke my dreams when you told me I should die. 

You broke my mirror when you threw it to the floor.
You broke the memories I had when you walked out the door.

You broke my bracelet when you ripped it off my wrist.
You broke my hand with your fist.

You broke everything I had with a single kiss.
