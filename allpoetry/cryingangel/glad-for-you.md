Loving alone is useless.
That's why I am glad I have you by my side.
To help me through the tough times.
And be happy with me for the good ones. 
I am glad I don't have to do this alone.
I am grateful that you are here with me.
Glad that I have found someone like you.
My love for you will last forever.
I promise that to you.
Now thanks for being there when I need you too.This started out as just a thought. I was grateful for my boyfriend. The I just added some stuff.
