title: "Trying To Let Go"
writer: "cryingangel"

I'm trying to let go, I really am.
I am trying to let go of all the love I had for you.
I am trying to let go of all the pain you caused me.
I am trying to let go of all the memories and all the good times.
I am trying to fill my mind with all the emotions of the bad.
I am trying to let go so I can go on with life.
I am trying to let go that you told me I would be your wife.
I am trying to let go of the future we were supposed to have.
I am trying to let go of all the things you told me.
I am trying to let go of the thought of you.
I am trying to let go so I can move on with my life.
I am trying to let go. But I can't.
I just love you too much.
