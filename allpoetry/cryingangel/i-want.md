---
title: "I Want"
writer: "cryingangel"
slug: "i-want"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/4210-I-Want-by-cryingangel"
---

I want to write poems and live in the woods. No shoulds, no woulds or coulds.

I want to write about my life, every place I've been, every stage, and all the problems.

I want to be successful at whatever I do, from being a write to being a good wife and mother.

I want all the people that have hurt me, to pay for what they've done. from lying to me, to physically or emotionally hurting me.

I could go on for days, because there are a million things I want!
