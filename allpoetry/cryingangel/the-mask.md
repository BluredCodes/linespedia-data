title: "The Mask"
writer: "cryingangel"

I wear a mask over my face, 
It lets me cry without a trace.
It keeps my emotions hidden away.
So no one can see then any day.

Under the mask, I am crying.
I feel just like dying.
Like taking a knife to my beautiful skin.
Because there is no way I can win.

Except with the mask that I can hide behind.
So no one can truly get into my mind.
