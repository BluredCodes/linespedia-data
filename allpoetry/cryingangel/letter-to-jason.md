It's almost your birthday again.4 and a half years since your death.And I still can't believe your gone.I can still see your face.Still feel your gentle touch.I can still hear your voice.Still feel your sweet breath.It's not that I haven't moved on.But you are always going to be there.I talked to your mom the other day.She is doing well. But your brother not so good.I guess he can fit in your clothes now.So he wears them all the time.He still crys every night though.And nothing can ease his tears.He is going to cry no matter what.I know what thats like.I called and talked to him for a while.He still misses you a lot.I can't blame him though.Cause I still wish you were here.You are the reason that I'm still here.I have to keep your family strong.I have to help them move on.
