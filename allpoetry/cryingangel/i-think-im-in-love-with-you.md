title: "I Think I'm In Love With You"

I have known you for less than a week
And I love everything that I know.
I have probably been a total geek.
And I cry every time I go.

Leaving you is really hard for me.
And I don’t like having to do it.
I just wish that you would see,
That I’m so sad I want to have a fit.

I love just hugging and kissing,
But the other stuff was good too.
You are the one that I’m missing
And there are things I have to do.

But I can’t really do them
Because it’s you that’s on my mind
You are my precious gem
And definitely one of a kind.

You are the smile in my life,
And the twinkle in my eye.
I wish that I was your wife
And I wonder why?

Why you are not here now.
I miss you more than I did before.
I want to have a freaking cow.
I want to fly and I want to soar.

We are so perfect for each other.
That words can’t even say
I want to be your child’s mother.
Well, maybe I can some day.

For right now though,
I want to be with you.
I want all to know
That I love you too.
