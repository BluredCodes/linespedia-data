Finally I have found someone I can tell my secrets to.
Someone I can write to when I feel blue.
Someone who always makes me feel good.
Someone who listens, when they should.
She has become my best friend.
And will be there for me until the end.
I can write her when I have cried.
I know she’ll read this and be surprised.
She’s sweet and likes to laugh.
She has become my other half.
I love her like my sis.
There are many times she is missed.
She understands what I’ve been through.
Most of them she has been through too.
She doesn’t judge me for what I’ve done.
She will stop me from grabbing the gun.
Because I can talk to her when I feel its the end.
She’ll be there... cause she’s my best friend.This is for Sweetpea (jennifer) who has been there for me through everything and is the only friend that has stayed. I want you to know that I love you Jennifer and I am so glad to have a friend like you.
