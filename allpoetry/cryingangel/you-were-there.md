---
title: "You Were There"
writer: "cryingangel"

You were there when no one else was, 
I often wondered why, but now I know it's because, 
You love me more than anyone else has ever before,
I want to be with you and no one else anymore.

When we are together, I seem to forget.
All of my worries and the time that we met. 
You are so sweet and I love you more.
Way more than I did ever before.

You stole my heart and a kiss for you,
I want you to know that I miss you too.
I love your kisses on my neck, 
But just remember that I don't want you to spend your entire check.

Atleast with me, you seem so happy,
You make me laugh even when I felt crappy.
There is nothing I wouldn't do for you. 
I would take a bullet if it would save you too.

I don't want to have to live without you.
And I know thats not what you want to do.
Lets make a pact and be with eachother forever.
Meaning that our goodbye would be never.
