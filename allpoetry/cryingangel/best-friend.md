---
title: "Best Friend"
writer: "cryingangel"

He was my best friend in this world.
He was the reason why I smiled and laughed.
He was the love of my life.
But now he's gone.
I have cried and cried since the day he left.
I have smiled and laughed but it's not the same.
He brought me joy and happiness.
This is only a cover-up to my fears and my pain.
I wish my life to see him okay.
He is the love of my life and always will be extremely important to me.
I go to his grave,only to cry,and talk to him again.
This is the only way now that he's gone!
