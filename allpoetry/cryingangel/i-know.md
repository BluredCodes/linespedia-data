---
title: "I Know"
writer: "cryingangel"
slug: "i-know"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/4204-I-Know-by-cryingangel"
---

I know that you love me and that what you told me was the truth.I know it would be hard to leave her after so long.And I know that you want to be with me.So as long as I know that you love me.You can know that I love you too.
