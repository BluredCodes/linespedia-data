You said you never loved me,But you cared about me a lot.I didn’t know what to believe,You go against everything I was taught.I risked it all to be with you,Each and every stepI don’t even think you knew,That every letter I have kept.You were the reason for it allI think you knew it tooI just need to give you a call.But there’s something else I need to do.I need to tell you “I love you”I need to say it out loud.Tell me if you want toYell it in a crowd.
