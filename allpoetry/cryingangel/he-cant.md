---
title: "He Can't"
writer: "cryingangel"
slug: "he-cant"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/5086-He-Cant-by-cryingangel"
---

He can't hurt me.
Not anymore.
I won't let him do it.
Not once more.

He can't degrade me.
Not anymore.
I won't let him do it.
Not once more.

He can't lie to me.
Not anymore.
I won't let him do it.
Not once more.

He can't love me.
Not anymore.
I can't let him do it.
Because he never did before.
