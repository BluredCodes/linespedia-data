When she e-mailed me with her little poem, 
well that was the very last straw. 
She said she still loved you and I was basically in the raw. It's really over babe. 
You did me wrong and I thought you of all people would have been true. 
I'm so through with you and I am glad that I found this out now. 
Before a life of hurt and pain you would put me through. 
I don't want to be with you and I put that on everything I own. I don't want to hear you cry, and I don't want to hear you moan. 
I am sick of all the time going back to you because I felt bad. Well, guess what? It's over and I am through. 
Goodbye... and I am sorry things had to end this way.
