title: "To My Sisters With Love"
writer: "cryingangel"

My sisters you will always be.Through better, thick and thin.Dad can't take that away you see.You are my next to kin.Cause someday dad will be gone.May his soul rest in peace.And we will have to soon move on.We will have eachother atleast.I will love you no matter what.What anyone else says or does.Our bond, no one can even touch.You three have all my love.If ever you need to talk, you can always confide in me.I remember in the stroller we would walk.And sat under the shady tree.No one can take our memories from us.This friendship is definately a plus.
