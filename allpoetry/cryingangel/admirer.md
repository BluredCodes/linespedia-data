---
title: "Admirer"
writer: "cryingangel"

I see you there, watching me.
Through the window that’s all I can see.
You’re always right there, as I pass by.
Some of the days you actually say “Hi”

I don’t know what it is that entrances me.
Maybe it’s the fact that I can see,
Into the window you watch me from.
I might be cute, but I’m not dumb.

I don’t like when you pull away.
It makes the blinds move and fray.
All I do is turn away and smile,
I can tell you have watched me for a while.

I think it’s cute, it you want to know.
Sometimes I don’t want you to go. 
It brings me smiles to know you are there.
I want you to love me, I want you to care.

There are times I leave to go around the block,
And I think to myself, I should go and knock. 
One day I will get the courage to tell you this,
Right now I will stay in my eternal bliss.

I like the idea that this is all a mystery.
One day I hope to finally see you watching me.
I wish for that wonderful day to come,
So we can sit and be happy under the sun.
