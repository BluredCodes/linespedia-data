---
title: "I've Lost"
writer: "cryingangel"
slug: "ive-lost"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/4340-Ive-Lost-by-cryingangel"
---

I’ve lost someone who I loved very much.He was gentle with every last touch.I’ve lost someone who was my best friend.Someone I would be there for until the end.I’ve lost someone that meant so much to me.Someone that beyond my flaws could see.I’ve lost someone I cared,So many memories we shared.I’ve lost someone I could cry toSomeone who would never make me blue.I’ve lost someone who could make me laugh.To think about it—I’ve lost my other half.
