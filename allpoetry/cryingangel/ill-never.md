---
title: "I'll Never"
writer: "cryingangel"
slug: "ill-never"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I’ll never love another
Although I might pretend
Unless it’s like a brother
Because you’re the one until the end

You are the one I love
And the one I choose
But if you fly away with the dove
I will be the one to lose

Because I could never love someone else
Not even in my dreams
Not even for their wealth
Or even to be extremely mean

I’ll never, that’s what I said
Not even if I was dead.
