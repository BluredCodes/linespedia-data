Tomorrow could be the day. The day you'll be mine. Back in my arms, back in my heart.

Tomorrow could be the day. The day you decide to change and give me a call. 

Tomorrow could be the day. The day you realize how good I was to you. 

Tomorrow could be the day. The day you realize she is a bitch and decide to leave. 

Tomorrow could be the day. The day you that you tell her that you love me and pack all your shit. 

Tomorrow could be the day. The day that could change my life forever. 

Tomorrow could be the day for anything, so right now I am going to focus on today.
