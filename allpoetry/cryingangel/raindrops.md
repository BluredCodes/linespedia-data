title: "Raindrops"
writer: "cryingangel"

Raindrops touch your grave,
Then I remember I must be brave.
They are your teardrops from Heaven.
I remember, there were only seven.
When we met, you became my best friend.
You were there until the end.
The car came so fast.
I want to know: Did you see your past?

Raindrops touched the ground.
They touched without a single sound.
You are crying again, I wonder why.
Can you see my tears as I cry?
Do you hear when I talk to our star?
Can you hear me from that far?
You still find ways to touch me.
In my heart you will always be.

Raindrops fill a puddle.
I miss when we used to cuddle.
You used to hug me when I would cry.
I still ask why you had to die.
I remember when we used to play in the rain.
You always made up some kind of game.
I miss the things we used to do.
I know in your heart, you miss me too.

Now the raindrops have come to an end.
You have stopped crying, my friend.
