---
title: "I Found In You"
writer: "cryingangel"
slug: "i-found-in-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

Every girl needs a thug,
I found my thug in you.
Every girl needs a love,
I found my love in you.
Every girl needs a boo,
I found my boo in you.
Every girl needs a heart,
I found my heart in you.
Every girl needs a man,
I found my man in you.
Every girl needs a soul,
I found my soul in you.
Every girl needs a baby,
I found my baby in you.
Every girl needs a world,
I found my world in you.
Every girl needs a hug,
I found my hug in you.
Every girl needs a kiss,
I found my kiss in you.just something i was thinking about.
