Why did everything happen so fast Jason? 
Can you explain to me why?
Because I have no explanation for it. 
You were there Thursday but not Friday, the day I needed you the most. 
It all happened so quick. One minute you were there and the other you were not. 
One minute you were on earth and the next in Heaven. I hope you know that everything happened so fast.
Why did everything happen so fast? I didn't even get to say goodbye to you. No one knows why you had to go so suddenly and so fast.
Can you answer me that one thing? Why so fast?
