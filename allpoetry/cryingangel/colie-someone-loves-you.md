His name is Mike and mine Nicole,He lives in Chicago and I in CaliforniaAnd he wants that special someone back in his lifeAs do I...We have not been a part for long, but He says...'I love you Colie! I always have and I always will... I want the whole world too know it.. You are my 'Angel' and always will be... You are the best and dont forget... I will always be here for you... And I will always have faith in you... I will always back you 100% in whatever you do...Iam just here too love you unconditionally and too watch you grow and be successful... You will always be my baby no-matter what...'You know this!'... 'Uh-huh'.. You get all my love.. Since 8-28-98@5:22pm P.S.T. (when we were reunited),  It is a date I will remember for the rest of my life... You are the best thing that ever walked into my life.. I have no intentions of ever letting you walk out...or walking out of yours. Wet met when you were 3.. And I have loved you ever since then... I will never turn my back on you or hate you.. I love you baby... We have a special bond, please dont let that go.. We have been together for 15 years........ Lets make that 15 into the rest of our lives.. I love you and miss you baby.. This is for you.. I want the whole world too know that I love Nicole A. Weiss... Dont mess with her...She is my 'Baby' and my 'boo'.. Love you 'Angel'...My boyfriend had this in his homepage and I just added some stuff.
