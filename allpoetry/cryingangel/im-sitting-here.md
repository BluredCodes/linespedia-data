title: "I'm Sitting Here"

I'm sitting here
2,000 miles away
looking at a computer screen
talking to you
but there is no voice
just you over there
and me right here
I can't see your smile
or wipe your tears
I can't see your face
Or know if you are 
laughing or crying
but even though
you are so far away
you are very close
to my heart.
