I Am...smart... caring... understanding... loving... sharing... full of life... easy to talk to... trustworthy... honest... lovable... cute... there to help... supportive... compassionate... beautiful... pretty... flexible... sweet... devoting... huggable... outgoing... special... comforting... happy... kind... sympathetic... optimistic... I am ALL of these things and a lot more!
