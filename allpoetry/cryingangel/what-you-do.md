title: "What You Do"
writer: "cryingangel"

Loving you has been so hard,
Everything I say, you disregard.
Talking to you has been a pain,
Sometimes ends with so much shame.
Listening to you has made things worse,
Because I am the one who gets hurt first.
Being near you makes me cry,
Then you just sit there and ask me why.
Explaining to you would take too long,
Maybe I should just be gone.
Being without you is killing me,
It’s only the love I want you to see.
