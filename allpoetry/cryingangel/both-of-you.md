---
title: "Both of You"
writer: "cryingangel"

I try to keep my heart with you.
I have to try real hard.
Because there is someone new.
Someone who has already knocked down my guard.

Someone I was with before.
He has always been in my heart.
Sometimes I think I love you more.
But you both have been there from the start.

The only reason we broke up, 
was because he needed to change his life.
He thought I had given up.
Now he wants me to be his wife.

He has gone back to school,
And got a job that he loves.
If I didn't accept I would be a fool.
Because I am the one he loves.

I know you love me too
he is more than you could ever be
See now what am I supposed to do?
I know this is all up to me.

But I am so very confused.
I love him. I love you.
I feel I am being used.
This is not fair for you to do.

Don't make me choose, 
because I won't be nice.
You might even lose.
Would that suffice??
