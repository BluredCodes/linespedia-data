Another night you didn't come home.Another night I was all alone.Another night you didn't say 'ggood-nightAnother night we did not fight. Another night 'I love you's' were not said.Another night I would rather be dead.Another night I am reminded of the pain.Another night our relationship is a game.Another night you might cheat.Another night I am full of defeat.Another night I cry myself to sleep.Another night that I weep.Another night has been too oftenAnother night the bed would soften.Another night, there has been too many.Another night I wish there weren't any.This is a poem that was written on the spot. tell me what you think!
