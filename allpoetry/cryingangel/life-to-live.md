title: "Life To Live"
writer: "cryingangel"

You didn’t teach me to walk or talk or even how to read. 
You didn’t teach me how to write or fight or even how to feed.
You never saw me ride a bike or swim across the pool.
You didn’t teach me to drive a car, 
Or the difference between right and wrong
You weren’t there when I lost my first tooth or cut my hair.
You weren’t there for my first cut or bruise,
But you gave me a great and wonderful life to live.
