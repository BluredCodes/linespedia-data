---
title: "I Want To Love You Both"
writer: "cryingangel"

We just got off the phone.
We just talked for a while. 
But he is still on my mind.
He has been for a while. 
I can't get him out of my head.
I can't stop thinking about him.
About his kisses. 
About his hugs.
About the way he makes me feel.
But I love you also.
Is this even love?
Sometimes I think it is.
But sometimes I love him too.
You both make me feel secure.
I think I want to be with him.
But I can't let you walk out.
Because we have been through so much.
And what if we turn out to be wrong.
What if we were really meant to be?
I love you both and 
I can't let either of you go.
I want to love you both!
