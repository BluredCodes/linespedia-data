---
title: "I Should Have"
writer: "cryingangel"

I should have told him how I felt.
That every time he was there, my heart would melt.
Now I can’t see clearly, because of all the pain.
People think my love for him was some kind of pain.

But it was not a game, it was very true.
Just thinking of him now makes me blue.
Bluer than the sky with no clouds,
Without him, my heart is torn into shrouds.

I should have told him before it was too late.
I should have—that was my biggest mistake.
