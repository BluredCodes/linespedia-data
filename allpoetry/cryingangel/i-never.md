---
title: "I Never"
writer: "cryingangel"
slug: "i-never"
source: "AllPoetry"

I never want to hear you sayYou love me today or everyday.I never thought you would be Someone I didn’t want to see.I never thought you would cryWhen I told you I wanted to die.I never thought you wouldn’t have cared.After so much we had shared.I never thought I’d remember everythingEven every song you would sing.I never thought speaking from my heartWould tear us further apart.I never thought making you my best friendWould make our relationship end.I never didBecause I was only a kid.
