He's insecure of me.
He thinks I am going to leave.
I just want him to see.
I am not here to deceive.

Leave? Me? Are you crazy?
There must be something wrong.
My whole life would turn hazy!
Do you want my to sing you a song?

Because if you wanted, I would.
Anything for you I would do.
It would ease your mind like it should.
I know you would do anything too.

Because that's what love is all about.
You are supposed to be happy.
Not full of so much doubt.
Even when your day was crappy.

I know I said some things that I shouldn't have said.
But I am sorry for saying them now.
Believe me, they are still running through me head.
I'm only human. Can you forgive me somehow?

Because baby, I am not going anywhere.
It's with you I want to stay.
My life, my world, with you I want to share.
I will be with you forever and a day!My boyfriend was a little insecure cause I had written poems about other guys. This was kind of a reassurance poem since writing is the way I most express things.
