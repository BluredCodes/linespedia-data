“Smile”Smile.For me please.Or when the camera says “Cheese”.Smile.To keep my spirits high.Or when you say “good-bye”.Smile.To keep my happy.Even when you feel crappy.Smile.To make me feel better.Or when you read my letter.Smile.Because you score.Or when I walk in the door.Smile.Your cuter that way!!
