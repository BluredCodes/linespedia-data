title: "Forever Make Our Star Shine"
writer: "cryingangel"

I held your hand until you were pronounced dead.
I cried for days lying in your unchanged bed.
I kissed your picture each night before I went to sleep.
Each night I would cry myself to sleep.
Each night I prayed that I would see you again.
Years later the pain is yet to end.
I wish I could see your adorable face.
And keep you in my loving place.
I am confused about which way to go.
I remember when we sat and watched the snow.
No one can replace you in my heart.
No one would even dare to start.
You are in my dreams every night.
Each night you tell me to "sleep tight"
You are so clear in each of my dreams.
That you are here it almost seems.
I know one day we can again meet.
One day again you can rub my feet.
Things we used to do I miss.
Just send me one gentle kiss.
So I can know that you are okay
And live my life again today.
My tears can form an entire puddle.
Just because of my enormous struggle.
I really miss you a lot.
I would sneak into heaven if I knew I wouldn't be caught.
Just to see you one last time.
And forever make our star shine.
