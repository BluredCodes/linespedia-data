title: "The Best Thing Is The Hardest"

Sometimes the best thing to do
Is often the hardest thing for you.
Walking out of your life was hard.
I know for sure that I am scarred.
My heart still calls your name.
My mind still thinks it is all a game.
You didn't chase after me.
But then say we were meant to be.
I didn't want to have to say goodbye.
But I did and it made you cry.
No one can ever take your place.
You can never be replaced.
Everything will turn out all right.
I'm glad you gave up without a fight.
I don't know if I would have the courage to stay.
Or even to listen to what you had to say.
There is more to life than loving yourself.
You have to learn to love someone else.
Until you have learned to love someone other than you.
I know that I have to be without you.
