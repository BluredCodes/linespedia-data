title: "Mum"
writer: "Banora Boy"

There was a Mum 
who traveled for her son. 
Across the water, 
because she thought she oughta. 

she saw her in-law daughter, 
who was quite glum, 
that's this Mum's son, 
had suffered an affliction. 

Now the son he felt good 
she came acros the water, 
to look after him, and, 
Mum's in-law daughter. 

So Thank you Mum 
Love from your son.
