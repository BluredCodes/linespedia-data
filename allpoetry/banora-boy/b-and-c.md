B and C
Brianna and Conor my little kids
Who I adore so strong 

That being apart
 Just tears my heart

Makes me long and long

My heart filled with joy 
Like a kid with a new toy
As I get to hold them tight

For the first time in nights 
Daddy knows alls all right
Cause this is what really mends a heart

To be with my kids on nights such as this
Brings a smile I can’t hide 
Oh my lovely kids
