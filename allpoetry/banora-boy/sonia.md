Sonia 

There was a girl named Sonia 
Who caught the eye of this guy
Which made him smile inside

He thought to himself 
This girl is mine
She’s gonna be my bride
 To death do us part

This nearly came true
He is now more in love
With girl mentioned above

Now she says if you go 
,or make a no show 
I’m coming too.

Now you Know why I love this girl
And just cannot for any reason leave her.
So my Sonia a kiss I bear
 with love from your hubby 
who will always be there.
