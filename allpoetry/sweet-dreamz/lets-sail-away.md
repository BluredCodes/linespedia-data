Let's Sail Away

Let's sail away from here.
Let us travel the tides of time,
floating on the streams of love,
back to the days of innocense,
when you held my hand so gently.

It was you and I, in solitude,
making mountains, of every moment.
Let us return to days of young,
serenity being savored salvation-
Where happiness was the light in
each of our eyes, one for the other.

Let us sail on away from it all,
all the uneasiness of man's way.
Let us awe the purest of wonders...
the ones that the God of creation
has kept in His golden treasure box 
for so many of us weary, to unlock.

Let us go to where love can burn,
without concern, hands of the clock
as it lasts for the rest of our days.
