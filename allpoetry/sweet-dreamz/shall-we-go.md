Shall We Go?

Rest my weary angel, rest,
as your worried mind drifts away.
while I stroke your lovely hair,
gently, calmly, you shall sway.

And your body will be feeling
lighter and much lighter,
until sweet dews of slumber
quietly take you there;

To the wonderful realm of,
fickel fairies and of fantasy,
where sweet serenity reigns,
anything's a possiblity.

When you arrive, that land,
I will be waiting and watching,
to take you, Love, by the hand,
with my guiding and protecting.

we can skip through the meadows,
happy hearts, play, laughing
endlessly like the times,
when we were little children,

No worry, but to love living,
those purely innocent days,
when eyes of youth would marvel,
beautiful wonders to be amazed.

We can sit by the quiet waters,
wading and splashing in such fun
hold hands while we're running
or flying kites in the sun.

Lie still and rest very easy,
I have blissful dreams to share.
Whatever you heart would plead,
that is why I will be there.
