How It Was

"It is not good that man should be alone".
And so it was... that God, after He gave
the man life with a breath by a kiss,
God made his child fall into a long
peaceful rest of many sweet dreams.
Afterward, God borrowed a bone 
from the man and made him the
perfect companion, one which
complimented this man in 
any way there was to be.
The man was very happy 
at a beautifully grand gift,
to which God gave him,
that was called woman.
They were then brought
together, to remain this
way on through forever.
And this is the story-
How my best friend,
you, have become,
the very best 
part of me.
