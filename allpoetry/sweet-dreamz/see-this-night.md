See This Night

See this night?
See all the stars,
glimmering so bright?
See the moon lay full and low?
Feel the soft subtle breeze blow?
See how hearts beat quiet and calm?
Take a sigh and enjoy the charm.
Something so peaceful,
about the dark sky.
Nothing is more mellow,
just making us high.
See this night?
Let's make it right.
I bid you a good night,
sweet dreams and sleep tight.
