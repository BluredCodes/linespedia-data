When You Dream

When you dream,
could you dream of me?
Dream, the strong knight 
in his armour, sparkly,
who rescues you when
you're sensing danger.
Feel me swooping you up,
carrying you off, away
in a chariot made of gold, 
to live in a big castle 
resting on fluffy clouds,
where cherubims hover,
securing serenity.

When you dream,
would you dream of me?
Dream of how the king,
treats his lovely queen,
loving, living sweetly.
Your long weary nights,
will never feel lonely,
but be full of delights,
I'll think of you only.
In a great fairy-land;
of pleasure and laughter,
from once upon a time
to happily ever after. 

When you dream,
would you dream of me,
a man of your fantasy,
who wants to always be
true, only for you?
For I will do anything,
that you'd ask me to,
be there by your side,
through all of the days
and every single night,
always treating you right,
gentle, faithful and true,
all of these just for you. 

And when you dream,
to be dreaming of me
and you have seen, 
it can not ever be,
just always remember,
I'm out there somewhere.
Find, a kindly gentleman,
seek, him diligently
and if he remains loyal,
love him unconditionally.
Soon it will come to be,
he'll be there for you
and when you dream,
you shall see me.
