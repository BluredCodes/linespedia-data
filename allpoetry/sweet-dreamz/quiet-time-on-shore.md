title: "Quiet Time On Shore"

Be still...
Feel the midst on your face.
Hear that great ocean roar.
Seagulls are squalking in the sky
as I ponder on this bay shore.

That tickles a little bit,
gushy sand squishing between my toes,
but ahhhhh, the sun sure feels good.
It's not too hot and not too cold.

Here it is just so wonderful.
You and I running very carefree,
no worries of today or tomorrow,
just watching life sail by peaceably,
like boats out there, here and gone,
time treats troubles the same way,
for life is just a big blue sky,
after a while, the dark will fade.

Just sitting in this evening sun,
watching all the people at play.
Isn't it really great to be young,
frolicking and laughing all the way.

Let's try one Ok tag! you are it.
Ha Ha ha, you can't catch me.
well shucks you didn't give up.
Oh those days of innocency.

Have more refreshment if you like,
while, sitting, slurping in the sun
and together, we'll just mellow out...
as the sun slowly starts slipping down.
