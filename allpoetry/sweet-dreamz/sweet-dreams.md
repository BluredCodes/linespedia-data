Sweet Dreams 



Sweet dreams I desire for you. 

To dream of sunny skies, 

where the grass lays 

soft and sweet and 

is forever emerald. 



Sweet dreams I wish for you 

to fly, your spirit playing 

in the clouds, dancing on 

the air, like you would 

never have a care 

or be disturbed. 



Sweet dreams I give to you, 

lie in complete serenity, 

to be totally free to do, 

whatever it is that you 

might wish to, for you 

are the ruler of 

your own world. 



Sweet dreams my wonderful friend, 

dream sweet and let it never end 

and if you do awake into reality, 

remember you can be what you want to be- 

In doing that, you just may make 

all of your dreams come true. 



Sweet Dreams, my wish 

and I bequeath this, 

with a kiss. 



Sweet dreams.
