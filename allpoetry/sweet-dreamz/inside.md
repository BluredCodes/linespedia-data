Inside

Find a secluded place,
where no one else knows,
away from all distractions.
Break from the lights, sounds,
voices and any other noises.

Now imagine... yourself on
a great buried treasure hunt,
on an island in the middle
of the ocean, not charted.

There is waiting an important
and fascinating endeavor,
being brought just for you.

Have your implements handy,
look for secret passages
and trap doors or floors.

Use your intuition to discover,
where your leads take you
and leave no stone unturned.

Then, when you search deeply,
open your mind to each and
every, found possibility.

Confide in your soul, things,
you really need to know 
and maybe,
just maybe, you never know...

You could find the essence of
yourself, inside and is this not,
your greatest treasure?
