---
title: "Relax"
writer: "Sweet Dreamz"
slug: "relax"

Take a deep breath,
relax.
Release it very slowly.
Let all of your frustrations go
and those anxieties just float away,
melting into oblivion your tension.
Imagine yourself in a quiet meadow.
Hear all of the birds warbeling?
Feel the midst of the nearby lake,
as the wind blows ever so gently.
You are just under a shady tree
on a mellow summer day, watching
clouds mingle and change into
so many unusual patterns.
The air is fresh and sweet
and it's quite refreshing,
so serene, everything
is perfect bliss, 
pure tranquility,
sweet surrender 
of soft slumber,
takes you away, 
sweet dreams.
