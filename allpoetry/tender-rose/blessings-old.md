Blessings by Ice`Rose

as you lay down on that soft as feathers pillow
dropping your head you let out a small sigh
thinking of all the things you did you smile 
knowing you survived yet another beautiful day
you smile thinking of all your friends,loves,family
how they helped you throughout the years
when you thought no one cared,only prooves love does 
exist though is sometimes hard to believe it can be found
angels watching over you as you close your eyes
your life rests in the hands of our lord God
he's watched you your whole life though it seems 
hes not there..evidence you need none he is the love
your friends shown you..its the true love that exists
close your eyes look into your heart there...
right there you find the warmth and the proof he does exist
through your heart,,but also the hearts of
many..its then you realize when you think the whole world hates you
but really its the people that hate the world that hate you..but
you are not alone relax child..angels are among you
Much closer then you could ever imagine.
they live in the hearts of your friends who love you very much
though they are not there to hug or kiss you they exists
just like angels they are all surrounded inside your 
heart..where they always been start to dream child 
dream out your fears.. surpass them 
with the many blessings you have know that the blessings
are true that they have been inside you all along you just needed to seek them with the tool of life.your heart.if people dont see your heart they do not see the angels nor the friends in the world.. 
release your tensions child why have them..of the past 
..the past is overforget it all concentrate on whats
 most important your heart and contracting it with others
around you so that they may see angels do exist as well
I love you you are my angel you kept in my heart
and now with all things in my heart i still seek these angels
friends = angels on earth
you like my blessings?
I just exposed something trully special...'
my inner spirit..how I feel and what I'm made of.Just a little something I made,to brighten a friends day.
and now for you the poem I'll display,If allowed I may.
