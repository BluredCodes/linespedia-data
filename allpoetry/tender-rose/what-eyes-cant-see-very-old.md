title: "What Eyes Cant See (Very Old)"
writer: " Tender Rose"

tears...shedding..dreadfully.. to the...ground..
a spirit..feels..lonely..and desolate..alone..
you are..there..standing...staring..at..me..
yet..you..are..not..close..by..anywhere.about..
that..night..I..wished..upon..a..star.is..gone..
was..it..all..a..dream..or..did..you..hear..me.
I..felt..the..cold..wind..shivering..as.it..touched.
yet..warm..wings..surrounded..my..life..essence.
I..sensed.. signs of love..there..but..was it..true
Yet..at..night..I still cry..myself to sleep..scaredly..
but..always.. I feel..your presence..again..feeling.love.
are..you..trully..watching..out..for me..day..after..day
am..I..so,..naieve..I..dont..accept what..I..see..afar.
perhaps..I.see..a little.more..then..i give credit for.
can..you..show..me..more..what..can..I..do...to see.
maybe..I..should..use..my.heart..instead..of my eyes..Something that came into mind,started out with it so cold here.
