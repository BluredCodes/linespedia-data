By Ice`Rose 

Sweet kisses as if the best on Earth
Much more then any riches are worth
Better passionate then Future dreams held
More then tender whisperings on my face felt
The hands that lead around behind my arms
Your wings that protect me within all harms
My feet below that always finds their way
To find you wherever they decide to stay
In my heart,you'll find yours there too
That sticks to my mind,just like glue
The over Anxiousness within my every existing vien
Many floodings I shed of tears,from Joys ever gain
A Promise in my heart,I can never ignore.
The Swear to Love you Now and Forever More.I just wrote this out of boredom,might not be too good but eh. its something. *smiles*
