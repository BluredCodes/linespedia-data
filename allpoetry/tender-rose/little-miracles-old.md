Little Miracles by Ice`Rose:

the little eyes,that look to us in curiosity.
their little button noses.
those little lips that say the cutest things. 
the little hands fitting into another,slightly larger.
their ears we see and try to watch what we say.
they give us alot to smile about.
talking bout the Day's events,all Night long.
their minds always opened for Answers to their Never ending questions.
the way they hug so pleasent yet comforting and when they speak those three little words 'I love you'.
in their very little hearts the words echo softly warmth secluding every inch in our bodys.
And seeing their shadow next to our own,a pleasent thought as they grow following our very footsteps.basically what goes through my mind,when I see a child *smiles* 6-15-06 (Now that I have my own child its what I see in my beautiful daughter every day)
