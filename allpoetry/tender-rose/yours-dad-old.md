Your's Dad By Jeannie (Your Daughter  duh! O.o)

Who's hand was it...
I needed to find around when I felt all sad... your's Dad

Who's love was it...
That showed all things weren't really bad... your's Dad

Who's smile was it...
That delighted me when I did a Job well done... your's Dad

Who's ears was it...
That listened when my days I ever felt mad... your's Dad

Who's heart listened...
When I pour my tears for you to hold inside... your's Dad

Who's loving arms...
Held me tight and told me all was to be alright... your's Dad

Who's words was it...
That taught me of people Like God and Jesus Above... your's Dad

Who's hands...
Built our Family hopes and showered bundles of Love... your's Dad

Who's nerves...
were like steel... when I needed help on Life's hill... your's Dad

Who's love...
Was it that Kept love our family so love filled... your's Dad

Who's Face...
Do I look up to for answers, and Know they are True... your's Dad

And 
Last, Who's Dad... would I like you to be?
My Dad  
I Love you! 

To my Daddy I love you very much.  
Love, 
Ice Rose
"Jeannie".

In case those who didn't know Poetic Fanatic is my Real Life dad and he is the greatest.
