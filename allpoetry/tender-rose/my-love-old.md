My True Love: By Ice`Rose

As The Tears Escape My Eyes 
Each Drop A Memory Of Happiness.
But With Each Moment a Pain,
Of Sharp Heart Thrusting Aches Inside.
Yet The Feeling of Warmth Stays.
Just Like The Smile On My Face,
The Weather More Beautiful Then Ever.
The Sun Seems So Much Brighter,
The Grey Clouds Move Astray.
Only Good Thoughts Surround Me,
And The Rest Of The World Doesnt Exist.
My Heart Beating Much More Then Beats,
Like Claps Of Thunder Loud and Strong.
and The Sweet Taste Of Goodness,
Smothers The Thoughts Of Love.
My Heart Sinking Deeper Each Time,
I Think About Him..In My Soul Below.I wrote this when I was younger,when I first fell in love (puppy love) *twinkles in eyes* oh to fall in love *smiles*
