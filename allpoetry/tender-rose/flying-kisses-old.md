~*~*~*~ Flying Kisses ~*~*~*~ 
Catching the Kisses as you blow 
My breathless soul full of glow 
The Kiss that takes away lifes fears
For every kiss,on my body it smears 
The kiss that keeps my soul soaring 
All the time my emotions kept pouring 
Taking my hand holding with finger tips 
Feeling them fly from my hand to my lips 
I feel my knee's begin to crumble below 
More Passion heating,my blood run cold 
And I look to you as you leave with a smile 
Knowing they last more just a little whileThis is dedicated to someone who always gave me online kisses.  You know who you are.Thanks for always being there.
IceRose
