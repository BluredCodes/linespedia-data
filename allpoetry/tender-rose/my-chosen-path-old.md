My Chosen Path By Ice`Rose

Walking along clouds,that lead to my designation
All the things I learned of God my best motivation 
The wings I feel that surround my heart and mind
To listen to many crys,fears,paths of the unkind
Feeling sorry for those who not know one ounce of God.
For they are living day by day not having a true Guide.
I feel pity to those who do not know the happiness I bare.
Those who pay attention to the world without any sign of care.
Tears that fall from many choosing the wrong rhoute.
Having God a blessing, we cant afford to live without.something I wrote thinking of my happiness.
