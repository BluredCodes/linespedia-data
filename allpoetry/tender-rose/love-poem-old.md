As we dance together within in the starry moonlight
facing each other body to body; face to face.
Do not worry milove for we have plenty of time.
For I am drowned by your beauty alone, in all the world.
Time never existing when it comes to love.
Where only your heart is melded together as one 
Intertwining with mine matching my beats, 
nothing but spirits lifting up in utmost estacy.
Our Bodies light but our hearts pounding heavily. 
A glissten of sweat pours down our bodies as we lay.
I wouldn't want anything more then to be with you.
To explore your soul, to challenge your peaks of pleasure.
To tickle your senses along with your body.
Our eyes never closing for I want to see our love in the making.
Lovingly and beautifully; like a just painted picture.
Heaving at the lightest of touches, but it does not stop there.
   I want to be inside your longing body, I wish to stay inside forever 
as I am in your heart.Nothing coming between us cept the 
warmth of our bodies melting together as we lay together
 never to seperate.something I wrote from a dream of my someday Prince; of which I still await.
