*H* Heat taking over my suffering longing body,warming me comfortably.
*E* Everlasting Commitment I promise to you Until the day that I die.
*A* After the world has gotten from bad to worse I will never change.
*R* Racing together providing the world with gifts from our innocence.
*T* The many nights we spend together loving one another in pure love.
*S* Such memories we still express together falling loves as the past.

*T* Too Great of amount of the Love together we bestow upon each other.
*O* Others not even close to our hearts coming between the love we bare.
*G* Gathering the love during the day from others and showering it at night.
*E* Everyone Becoming jealous we stay together so closely to each's hearts.
*T* The many thoughts of you surpassing the rest that fly by ever so quickly.
*H* Happiness fills my veins with each beat that presents itself in my heart.
*E* Ever Magical love much better then any Faery Tale imagined in books.
*R* Roses following our very footsteps as we walk along our garden in earth.

*L* Living with you much more pleasurable then any other person could see.
*I* Isnt it remarkable how much we are in love that we cannot bare to be apart.
*V* Vibrant and alive is how you make me feel when our lips touch with passion.
*E* Ever rejoicing the time I met you that turned my whole world into a smile. 

*F* For all the times you hugged me softly kissed me sweetly held me tightly. 
*O* Overzealousness never wanting to leave you once you have gotten in my sight.
*R* Running the dreams we always loved to think about Every day of our lives.
*E* Eternal our love lasting longer then our own minds could ever comprehend.
*V* Venturing through life through tough obstacles yet one thing heading me on. 
*E* Everything I use to think of as treasures doesnt compare to the way you are.
*R* Reviving all my senses you are the medicine my body yearns and aches for.
