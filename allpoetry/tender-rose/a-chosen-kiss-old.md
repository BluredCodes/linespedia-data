A Kiss By: Ice`Rose
a kiss to last through a thousand sorrows.
the day that heats my face within marrows.
the kiss that warmed my heart with glee.
the sweet little treasure so dear to me.
the taste of a thousand drops of rain..
the trickle of softness I love to gain..
the small yet wonderful after taste..
wanting it all in my mouth non to waste.
loving the sweetness,you freely give.
as if water I could drink forever to live.
the water would be so sweet,so good,so pure.
like nothing I ever drank in my life before.
