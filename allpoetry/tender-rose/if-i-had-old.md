... if i had a drop of rain for each time you helped me stop crying 'I'd need a umbrella'
... if i had a raye of sunshine for each time your smile helped me laugh,'I'd need sunglasses'
.... if i had a flower for each time you showed me over the years how our friendship bloomed 'I'd need alot of acres'
.... if i had a star for everytime you'd help my soul to shine, 'I'd have a whole galaxy'
.... if i had a saying for everytime I saw you i would use every letter of the alphabet to express how I feel'
.... if i had a beat in my heart, for everytime you showed me you love me, i would be healthy my whole life'  (and last but not least 
.... if i had you  
            'I would have 
                   Everything'
