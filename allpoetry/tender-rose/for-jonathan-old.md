Jonathan who help me when I was down
Cared whenever I seemed to have frown
Helping by opening his and my heart...
I knew I loved him from the very start
He helped me see beauty I didnt see
All the world and from inside of me
He's a treasure I dare not want aside
He showed me how to come more alive 
For this he will always remain in me
When I feel loved its because of he
