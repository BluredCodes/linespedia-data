a quick snack by: Ice`Rose             

her ears raised and listening,at any distant sounds near by
her heart pounding trying to find a soothing rhythm
slowly she creeps from behind the darkened night
her eyes focusing on all thats around her body
tail tucking underneath her furred soft stomach
a pawed foot slowly seeping into the path of the noise
narrowing her eyes,ears folding backwards a soft lick
she slowly walks nearer,staying low to the ground
she swiftly strikes pouncing on the swift victim
jaws opening,gnawing at the flesh swiping her claws
she devours it in a instant and then lays down
resting her tired worn body,she licks her lips
another supper,another full stomach..to last
till the next time..just something I wrote,cuz I was bored ^-^*smiles*
