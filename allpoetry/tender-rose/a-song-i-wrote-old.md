(( I didn't know what to call it ))

a song by Ice`Rose:


Look at me,am I all you seem to want or more?
With your love,oh how much will my heart soar?
Does it seem true,to you that I might be
the only one who will find true love with you.

I Think these things and I have a feeling I'm not alone.
as much as I could ever want,but then I will see for sure...
and Soon I begin to think....

Sun and Moon and Stars Above,I think there a sign of True Love.
You're Gonna see,Its not only me,its Love,dont you Know.
Only in the images I see,you staring somewhere here at me.
Though this is only new thoughts,it might be true.

 Look at me,am I all you can dream about lately?
I'm not the only one who thinks of that so.
When you see me,I feel it also,though its new.
I'm finding it hard to believe its so true.

I Think these things and I have a feeling I'm not alone.
as much as I would want,but then I will see
and Soon I begin to think.... 

Sun,Moon and In Even Stars Above,Thats a sign of True Love.
You're Gonna see,Its not only me,its Love too,dont you Know.
Only in the images I see,you here staring there at me.
Though this is new,it might be just be true.

This feeling you seem to give to me,It makes my heart fill up with glee.
I feel uplifted from my seat,as if I could, to go to somewhere new.
Sun and Moon and Stars Above,Thats a sign of True Love.
Lets start life,see our lives together in a new view.This is a song of words,I made up myself..I just heard the melody from a nameless song.. and began writing words to go with the word less song. hope everyone Likes it.
*~Ice Rose~*
