Tears shedding dreadfully to the ground
A spirit feels lonely and despairingly alone
You are there standing, staring here at me;
Yet, you are not close by ...Anywhere 
The night when I wished upon a star is gone;
Was it all but a dream or did you hear me
I felt cold winds shivering as it touched me
Though, warm wings surrounding my life secure
Always sensing signs of love there, But was it true
But at night I still cry myself to sleep lonely and scared..
At times always feeling your very presence again 
Were you trully watching out for me day after day
Am I so naive I dont accept what ....I feel inside
Perhaps I see a little more then I ever gave credit for
Can you show me more..... What can I do, to see
