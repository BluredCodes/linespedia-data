I once knew a little girl 
she was exactly like me
when I looked in the mirror
its her I would only see
she had long flowing hair 
all the way down her back
she had the most lovely eyes 
I always see kept its track
she was always there for me
whether laughs or tears
she held my hand tightly,lovingly 
speaking 'there's nothing to fear'
she would smile when I would
cried when I was nothing but sad
she was a one of a kind
in fact the only friend I had
she was more then anything
what she always appeared to be
she was in my world of everything
I wanted in myself to always see.
she was always the closest  
I have ever had near.
she was my friend 
enemy at times
till the day she disappeared.

Written By IceRoseThis is in dedication to Emily..My Bestest Friend who I thought was a ghost friend of mine...then my mother told me the news Emily was only my Imaginary Friend...it hurt me when she vanished I will never forget who held me tight.. when things would hurt me she would caress me with all her might.
