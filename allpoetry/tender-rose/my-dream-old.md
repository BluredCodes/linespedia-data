It started as a little harmless trip but little did they know the 1O teens sent to vacation were to embark on something very evil in their time,monsters always existing in fiction books doesn't even compare to the danger they were about to embark.laughing and woo'ing having been away from college and with their own lovers.sure this was bliss to them but it wasn't to last very along before this happiness expires.happiness filled in their minds,freedom at last..this was sure bliss the windows open in their cars,the sun shinging brightly,people were talking away while most were kissing their lovers,most were just enjoying the beautiful scenery,day dreaming of the things to do once they got there.their stuff piled on top of the cars inside the trunks,such innocense not a heap of worry on their faces,just joy and relaxation.Yes this was a daydream come true to the innocent college kids.Seona and Jon in the back seat with Devin,the next up was Keneth and Amy and in the front Keith and Autumn. 'Hey this is the life isnt it?' Keneth said in Amy's lap,she was stroking his hair while he was eating grapes out of her hand.Devin just shrugged his shoulders looking at Keneth.'Man you are one lazy bastard you know that?' Keneth laughed in reply 'why yes,I knew that and damn lucky one at that' Amy snickered still feeding him the grapes smiling 'My man deserves nothing but the best' she replied.Seona was looking outside the window missing her home place looking in the skies praying everyone was ok and safe.Jon frowned looking at her he placed his arms around her neck,seeing her jump he blinked his eyes.'Easy baby..Whats wrong..you look like you are not happy why is that?' 'You have me here..am I not enough?'Seona smiled knowing nothing could replace her sweet lovable caring devoted boyfriend.'I just miss mother and father.. and the kids back home..' Jon smiled 'Aww Baby..no need to worry bout them.They lived their life its now your turn' Seona didn't smile afterwards 'I can't help but wor--seona being cut off by her lips being muffled with Jon's he ran his fingers through the sides of her hair and slides his toungue deeply into her mouth probing her insides softly and then slides softly back.Seona smiled 'you're right..you convinced me enough..hell I do deserve this..I've always served others its about time I get my turn to spoil myself' Jon snickered 'That was way to easy,to get you to smile'.
Seona smiled knowing he was right and so she kissed him back hugging him tightly.she began running her fingers through his lovely hair looking in those deep blue sexy eyes of his,she before they heard a 'We're almost there!' from big mouth Autumn.Seona jumped hearing her 'Damn..that girl..cant she shut up for once?'Jon snickered and grabbed Seona hugging her patting her head 'Now now baby..calm down..she's always loud I know..but she is only Human'.Seona growled louder.Jon hearing her growl gulped a bit and patted her head 'I said down girl..you little kitty'.'She's such a wench' Seona growled from her lips.'Yes she is baby' Jon said trying to calm her down he held her tightly in a soft and loving embrase 'But we all can be at a time'.Autumn turned around and yelled 'GET OFF MY ASS SEONA!' Jon sighed and looked at the two sweat drops from the back of his head and held tightly to Seona 'Dont start anything Autumn'.As the teens drove, they felt sudden bumps on the road Seona got scared hiding inside Jon's arms.Keith Autumn's boyfriend stoped the car and held the wheel and turned to Seona and Autumn 'Stop yelling you two..Or I can turn this car right around and drive us back home!!! He glared at the two girls acting like dogs.'Fine.I'll be good' Seona said slumping into Jons arms smiling loving having a very handsome guy for a boyfriend Jon smiled and held her close Autumn stuck out her toungue rudely at Seona who at the time was gazing into the depts of Jon's eyes daydreaming of them playing around once they got there.Keith who nodding turning back around continues driving suddenly he stops seeing a weird animal rolling in front of the road.Seona blinked her eyes her daydream eruptured Autumn growled 'WHAT?! WELL WHAT THE HELL IS IT THIS TIME?!' 'I..saw. something ..maybe a little animal...not sure..I didnt wanna run over it' Keith said wide eyed having never saw a creature so black before.maybe it was a rabbit he thought..he never seen one before. maybe thats all it was.Jon began kissing Seona again smiling she returning the smile.
