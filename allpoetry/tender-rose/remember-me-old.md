---
title: "Remember Me (Old)"
writer: " Tender Rose"

Keep this promise 

In your heart

That I will never 

Leave or depart

I will remain where 

You always need me

Deep in your heart 

If you will let me be

Love lasts longer  
 
Then my days I live

I want you to remember 

All the things I did

Share with others what you

Learned from my soul

Show them things 

To make hearts whole.

In case and shall anything 

Happen sometime to me

Remember me is all I ask 

If you ever let it beJust something I wrote to my pupils; my friends. they are young and pure I wish them luck in adult hood.
