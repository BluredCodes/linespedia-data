Being Away By Ice`Rose

Away with wings,the wings I use to freely wrap myself around.
your heart beat,like claps of thunder the most beautiful sound.
No Tears around here, for you'll always be in my heart.
so when you are away,though it be a while,we never part.
the smile on my face as your memory brings warmth to my mind.
even though you're not here I still feel our hearts intertwine.
though you may be happy,and think I may well be not.
this is never true, for you're worth more then i got.
think nothing of my sadness,think of your heart.
if something happened to you..i know I would fall apart.
if you held my hand if you kissed my lips,if you ran your fingers through my long hair..
But it means nothing to me,nothing at all,if Love isnt in my heart from the start anywhere.
