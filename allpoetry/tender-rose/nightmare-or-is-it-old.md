a Nightmare....or..is.. it? by: Ice`Rose

a Dream within my minds' eye
left here till the day that I die
leaving my body yet I am around
though I am buried deep in ground
thinking I breathe not one ounce of life
I will never have to worry about strife
my body is gone from this hell I once lived
I did my best and gave what I could give
everyone's gone,darkness is to come
but after the dark dream I will be homeSomething I made up just coming onto the site.
