If I could catch your tears By Jeannie

If I could I would catch 
your tears within my hand 
each tear softly shedding 
would tread into palms land 

I would hold them inside 
to see your aching pains 
whether it was as hard
as frozen spattered rains 

I would hold them carefully 
not to drop a single one 
all morning all night 
and till the day was done 

a Great release they be 
and a pain I will keep 
my face it remains a smile 
because of your weep 

For I will take all the tears 
in these two secured holds 
I'll open my empty hands 
show it to you as it unfolds 

nothing left to explore 
as I can show no more 
no sight or light inside 
my heart left, to open wideThis was too someone who made me cry, but also they cried too, so I tried to be the stronger one.

~* Love And Happiness *~ 
Ice Rose
