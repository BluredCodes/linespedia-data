I Dream Of You...
Your Voice Is...A Whisper,
That Breaks The Silent Dark,
Your Smiles Like The Sun...
When It Shines On My Face.
When I'm Troubled...I Think Of You...
And It Gives Me, Such A Great Comfort
With True Security Much Like, The Time
When I Look...At A Delightful Sunrise, 
My Life Is Brightened By It's Beauty,
