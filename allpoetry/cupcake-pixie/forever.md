Forever is forever till eternity do us part
Life is just a passing form of the tangible
Where it does not last
For you and I are like the doves
We’ll soar and dive and fly
Into the highest zenith they say 
Where you and I will stay

Where is the corner stone of luck?
That will bring us back to bay
Where the seas cry out and little rain falls
To polish the glittering day

My oh my, u look beautiful
As pretty as a little dame
Mann your fleece and your coat
For they’ll come useful when you are slain

Pick up your garment and your gear
We’re going a fishing today
To bait the lies and the sighs
For another deceitful lay
