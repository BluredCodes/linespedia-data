title: "Directions to love?"

If love was a misery
Then this must be love
If love was an agony
Then I’m sure I’ve waded deep
I have fallen into a trap
A bottomless pit they say
A fathomless man-hole
I’m falling into the depthless streams
Marriage is not the grave yard
Love must be the cemetery
If they ask you where love leads you
Say the chasm or the limbo
But remember the darkness, the devastation and the desperation
Love is a taboo
Don’t mention it ever
For innocent victims fall
Into the claws of evil doers
This thing they call love
