title: "Somewhere beyond..."

Eyes are blind to the truth
They do not see beyond the tinted glass
It’s such a thin, hollow cascade
But yet they do not see

Eyes are not meant for the unseen
There is another sight
Which is so often neglected
So far deep within

Eyes only see the tangible
The things which are not eternal
But through the window of our heart
We see above the ephemeral

Eyes are not worthy to be offered
Intangible things are always there
But we do not pay heed
Why do we not open the eyes of our heart instead
