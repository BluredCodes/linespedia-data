title: "Cold"
writer: "CuPCake PiXie"

There's the chilly airHere are my frigid handsBelow I see my freezing toesMy slouching body squealsThere's a hunger in meA thirst waiting to be quenchedA soul drenchedA warmth I wait to restThere's an unsettling feeling in meA wavering breeze that rises and fallsKeep it aliveI sought to keep it stillThere's a million holesA thousand ventilatorsThe rushing vapour so quicklySeeks its way inBut it's bright and cheeryI can see the leaves rustlingI can hear the leaves movingI can feel the sun shining
