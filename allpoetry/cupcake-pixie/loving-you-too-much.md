Loving too muchSometimes, it feels as if I could love youFor a lifetime,For an eternity,Or even foreverBut sometimes, dark reality creeps inIt tells me I could lose you In a chanceIn a dayOr even in a momentYou say I love you ‘too much’I get hysterical over the most trival mattersBut how am I suppose to knowThat we could ever love somebody ‘too much’?Time will come to passLife will still move onLove will be a game of triumphOr just remnants of remembered glory
