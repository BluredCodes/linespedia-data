title: "The lies of this world"

There is no such thing as an idealized world
No such thing as a perfect person
No such thing as sheer beauty
No such thing as immense happiness

For I have found the world of suffrage
I have found the most imperfect person
I have found the ugly side to everything
I have found the bottomless pit of sorrow 

It leaves me numb and blank
My heart sinks like a bouncing rocket
My face seeks the mirror
The reflections in my eyes dimmed

This is no hell nor heaven
Nor purgatory for sinners’ sake
This is your illusion 
The matrix of time revealed

My soul has long been transposed
But my body waits to rot
The war and the strife
Swallows and churns us all alive

Why wait for death to come
When we know it is all a farce
It is all a hoax
To make us feel better 
When we realise all these will last
