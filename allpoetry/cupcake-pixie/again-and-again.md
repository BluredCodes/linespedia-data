title: "Again and again"

Again and again it happened
Like a thunderous cyclone that weaves
That gnawn of betrayal and pain
That eats into my tender beating thing

Chains of trust
Chains of love
Who said they can’t be broken?
Easily they give way
Like brittle fragile splits

I ask myself this question
How could it happen again?
But human nature is a whimsical thing
A beautiful creation they say

I feel like screaming out loud
But my faces just flushes red
The blood rising in my veins
Accumulating in my head 

I think I hate him now
There is no eternal forever
I wish I could show him how
That this so-called love could end right now
