A million thoughts swept my mindTunneling through the nerves in my headUnable to think coherentlyIt frustrates me Why is it that I don’t remember?I don’t rememberWhat is this speech, that gibbers along? What is this world?I’m obliged to listenI’m coerced to singMy sorrowful little tuneNothing exists, nothingNothing matters, nothingAs a little dog licks its woundIt sleeps its dreams away.
