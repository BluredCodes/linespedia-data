---
title: "Good or bad?"
writer: "CuPCake PiXie"
slug: "good-or-bad"
source: "AllPoetry"

Beauty surrounds you
Ugliness encapsulates you
Wherever there is goodness
Evil is prowling by

Good deeds encouraged
Bad things inherently spontaneous
So why be good when you are bad
Rise up and let Lucifer soar

Heaven, white and pure
Hell, red and sinful
But is there really is no difference
When your soul gets caught
In the matrix of time

Kill that enemy you hate
Shoot that bird dead
Life is just a meaningless thing
It worths nothing when you are jade
