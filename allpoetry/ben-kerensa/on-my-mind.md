On My Mind

I Have Things On My Mind , I wish angels would fall from far
far above our heads wouldnt that be ironic life should fill your
heart with the things you love and your mind with those you see and believe
but these small things well never stay for your soul is the only real thing
in this wandering universe.


~ Ben Kerensa ~What do you like about my poetry? and give me some views you have on my poetry?
