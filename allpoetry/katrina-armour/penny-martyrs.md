"Let he who is without sin cast the first stone"
So they shot him
Right between the eyes went their cylindrical steel ignorance
And the lions ate "the rib"

The Christians made big bonfires
When they heard the news
Using the cheap wine of vagabonds
And stolen hotel matches
They pressed the flames upon their flesh
Pretending they were cold

And when the daylight came
And the body was gone to nature
We bought wooden penny martyrs
On the corner of Fifth and Main
Unable to comprehend our own madness
As the final wave of euphoria washed over
And we were stupid all over again
