I hear this song that you sang so loud
And I'm reminded that I want you.
I want to lie down on the floor
With you hovering over me.
I no longer want to be in control.
I want you to touch this body,
And take it for your own.
I want you to sing our song
But this time with those hands
It seems I've wanted so long.
People all around us,
But you don't see a single one but me.
I want to be held and I want you to laugh.
I want to poke a hole in my head
And let my thoughts seeps
They're yours now.
Do what you want with them
As long as they keep playing our song.
I wish more than anything 
That you'll never end.
When I'm running my fingers down you 
I don't feel dead.
I don't think what'll happen after the song stops.
I just want to keep hope that it never will,
That neither of us will back down or drop.
Hope.
What a strange sounding word.
I had never believed in hope or love
Before I got in your car.
I get the message your mind clearly sends.
Our song will forever never end.
