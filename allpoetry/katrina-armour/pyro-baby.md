federal arsonthat's what they called itwhen he blew our house awaykapoof!you said it would come back(mommy you lied, you lied)you said that brother was happy(mommy i learned he died, he died)you said i could have it all back(mommy it was inside, inside)but now it's all gonekapoof!i hate you mommyi want to step on your faceyou let him cryyou left him to diei hate you mommyi want to rip out your eyesfor what you make me rip out inside, insidebecause (mommy mommy, you lied, you lied)and i don't call what you did federal arson
