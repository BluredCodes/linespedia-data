I picked you up
You pushed me down
And my heart hit the pavement
But my self-esteem took the brunt of it
And folded like
Oragami
       Oragami thumbtacks
Oragami thumbtacks in my brain
       ThRoB
       tHrOb
And the beat goes on...

410 degrees
In my mind
Blind man steering
Seat-belt burns
Paper
     Paper wings
Paper wings aflame upon the angel
     BuRn
     bUrN
And the beat goes on...

The steel fireworks
That crawled across your face
As you looked upon your fallen prey
And sized it up with seven swans
He crawled upon it and
Sucked
      Sucked through
Sucked through the flesh and into the heart
      DeAd
      lOvE
And the beat falls fast...
