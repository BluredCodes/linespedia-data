title: "Old Man: Local Bar: Twelve O'Clock"

do you stand there for nothing
or are you waiting for love
to come 
your way 
once again

do you stand there wishing
for a break
to take 
your pay
once again

is there anything i can do to help you dear
is there any reason why i caused your ailing

so i don't know what 
so i don't know why
but you just sit there
sit there

and i try to reach

but you're gone

and i resort to free verse again

so do you drink for the priveledge
or are you waiting to be drunk
so you 
can find
your way again

or do you smoke for your intellect
waiting to be smart
so you
can win
another's heart again

and i don't know what
and i don't know how
and you just sit there
sit there

and i try to reach

but you're gone

and i must resort to being real again
