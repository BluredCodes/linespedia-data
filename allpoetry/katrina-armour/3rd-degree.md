Suzy waits
Mommy is beside herself
She stares blankly and debates
The canister sits upon the shelf

Mommy is beside herself
The windows must be closed
The canister sits upon the shelf
No one needs to know...

The windows must be closed
(I will not be consience ridden)
No one needs to know...
(All love must be forbidden)

(I will not be consience ridden)
Wet you down, wet you down
(All love must be forbidden)
Strip your crown, strip your crown

Wet you down, wet you down
On and on the whispers scream
Strip your crown, strip your crown
Sanity is but a dream

On and on the whispers scream
She stares blankly and debates
Sanity is but a dream
Suzy waitsa pantoum
