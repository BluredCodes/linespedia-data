I wander through your mind;
your brain is haunted,
liquified,

but that's okay.

You said you'd never betray me,
you said you'd be there...
you said you'd LISTEN when my wrist got that twitch.

Yet I struggle with these spasms now,
at the breaking point,
and you're still
having
      sex
on
  my
body,
    
though I'm not involved anymore.Tear it apart.
