They walk right out in the line of fireAnd say it's the way it's always beenThey complain of the way they liveAnd say it's the only way they canThey buy handguns and ammo and little sharp knivesAnd drone on and on about the insignificance of violenceThey buy crack and speedAnd say that they can't help themselvesAnd they look at me and sneerBecause they see me as a rich little white girlAnd they say it's not easyIt's not easyBein' a thug
