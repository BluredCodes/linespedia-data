This poem will fall
Title-first into arrays of cliches

I love you
When I'm around you I don't feel like pulling the trigger
I love you
You can have me any time

Falling off

I love you
I can't think of being with anyone else 
I love you
I want you to be here with me forever

Plummeting quickly

I love you
I just hope I make you feel the same way as I do inside
I love you
I just hope you feel the same way

Sunk
