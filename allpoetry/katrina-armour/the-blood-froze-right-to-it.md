title: "The Blood Froze Right to It"

I gave you this
and that,
and the doctors are so much better here.
I love you more than ever,
Now that we know you're just like me.

I'll build you a castle
and a life
for when you come home and see
that the blood froze right to it.
There is nothing left to fear.

Just let them have a look,
let them have a jab,
let them have a peak
at what our life is like.
The most they may give you is three months.

I hope you come home soon;
I'll let you see how the blood 
froze right to it...
Oh, I love you more than ever
Now that we know you're just like me.
