title: "Little Never-Blooms"
writer: "Katrina Armour"

the little Afghan girls
cling to their skin like delicate
lotus flowers waiting to bloom

they wait...
they wait...

a penny's woth of needles...
do we need something to 
pass the time?

(Allah
Allah
Always find yourself
in Allah)

the little moslem girls 
hide inside their mother's
wombs while the preist on the
corner
(the bald-headed 
death parader)
comes to give them 
his Christ 
in a crumbly crust.

(Allah
Allah
Always find me in 
your Grace)

if your tears fall
we will never be able
to reap the 
destruction of 
your
abstinence.

please cling 
to me

Please cling
to me

be the baby at 
Allah's full
breast.
