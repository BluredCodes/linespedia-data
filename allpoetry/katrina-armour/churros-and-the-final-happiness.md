I remember being ten
No problems yet
No breasts
No blood
No boys
Nothing for another year
Loving mom
Loving dad
Going to Disneyland for the first time
Eating churros in front of the Haunted Mansion
Having nothing but hope in my mind
And the sweet taste of cinnamon and sugar
Alive on my tongue
The sweet taste of churros
And the final happiness
Prancing gracefully through my heart
Ah yes
To be ten again
When the smile always won
