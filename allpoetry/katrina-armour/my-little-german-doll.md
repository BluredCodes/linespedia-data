I called her Bianca
and pretended she was starving
because I wanted 
to feed something.

She was dressed in rags,
mouth open in anticipation,
fat legs and
skinny smile.

Bianca want a cracker?
Ja, ja.
Bianca want a smile?
Nein fraulein. Mine is painted on.

I wonder whatever happened
to my little German doll.Someone please correct my German. I really don't think I spelled fraulein right.
