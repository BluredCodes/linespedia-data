title: "Spinning the Gun"
writer: "Katrina Armour"

Sitting in black, cold metal on my fingers
Vodka on my left, a body on my right.
So much blood.
Something is wrong.
I don't know what is going on.
My best friend.
So much blood.
Others cheer me on.
I take another drink.
I still feel the cold metal on my fingers.
Now I feel warmth.
I look at my fingers.
They swirl in blurs of red.
So much blood.
Others cheer me on.
There's just so much blood.
I close my eyes and spin the gun.
