No good ever came from dusting dishes
Or following the path already worn
Trendy shoes will only get you as far as your own front door
Pretty porcelain shadows
Staring from the set
Pretty porcelain windows
Looking out to dirt
Where are the priorities that we tell them we all have?
We have given them to euthanasia
Porcelain euthanasia 
Seeping through the cracks
Where have all our heroes gone?
Why are you so sensitive?
Why are questions so foolish and selfish?
This is our modern porcelain world
It’s why we water our plants

Porcelain seedling,
My porcelain rose,
My porcelain thrift shop gift;
Where do you go when you fall off your shelf?
Or do you go at all…
