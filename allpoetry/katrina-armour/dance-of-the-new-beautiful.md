she pledged to rule the world
the second she got off the table

her clothes were already back on
her tummy already felt smaller

she danced in little circles
she laughed in different tongues

until they were all confused
and she was just another

"but she's not the same girl we had on the table"
they say

"but she's not the same girl that he had on the floor"
they say

and she knows
yes she knows

and today's gonna be a good day
