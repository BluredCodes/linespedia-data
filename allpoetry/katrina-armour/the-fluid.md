title: "The Fluid"

I'm sorry.
I seem to have damaged myself
again.

I know
you want me, but would you accept this void 
instead?

I seem
to have formatted my heart so that it reads like a 
pantoum.

I seem 
to have forgotten what it is to be a
student.

If I
decide to die tonight, would you be the 
fluid

that pumps through my dead veins?
