To do before it gets done:

1. Plant the seeds
2. Grow the garden
3. Freeze the roses
4. Thaw them out
5. Feed them to the children
6. Fix the door
7. Wire it shut
8. Burn it off
9. Heat the house
10. Beat anyone who watched
11. Go home
12. I love you
13. You're fired
14. Call lawyers
15. Feed them to the children
