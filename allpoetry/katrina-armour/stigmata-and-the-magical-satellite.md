title: "Stigmata and the Magical Satellite"
writer: "Katrina Armour"
slug: "stigmata-and-the-magical-satellite"

I'm sorry I fell to my disease.
You know,
the rare form of cancer they call
atheism.

It's okay though,
sir.
I'm getting better though,
sir.
They've locked me up now,
sir.
I'm sure I'll believe soon,
sir.

He who casts the first stone
also rapes me with his crucifix,
until psychoses flash before
my eyes.
Now I have to crawl across my floor.

Of course paralysis zeroes in upon
my life at that point,
and my padded cell becomes more 
like a TV,
devouring my life just like you say
Satan is.

My kindergarten wounds open up
like doors
sometimes.
They wonder, what really was in Pandora's box?
They wonder, how fast will tomorrow fade away?

As they wonder, 
kids everywhere look to the sky.
Little girls ask Jesus for 
pretty things,
thinking of him as some kind of
magical bearded satellite,

as I still dream up my own constellations,
and suffocate them til I cry....me on a bad day
