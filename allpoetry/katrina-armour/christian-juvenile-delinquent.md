My cigarettes go burning
Into God's pocket.
I believe in Him so He'll take my loose ends.
I smoke like a chimney, Jesus.
I swallow more pills than that patient, Christ.
My messiah, I abide by your law.
I'm out of cocaine money.
Can you feel my withdrawals?
I had sex but it was clean.
Mary, I'm still a virgin.
I'm bearing children like you said.
Can't you hear me scream?
This cell won't melt.
Come clean my welt
And ring the bell inside my shell.
I love you who is worth it.
I shreik because you hear it
And I know that you can't save me
So I'm safe.
For now.
Best friend, I'll light the candlabra
As long as you keep me on the tundra.
My saviour, you are my first love
Because you make me look clean
And I make your world look green.
Together we have joint custody of me
Though I have more that you cannot see,
Much, much more that you can never perceive.
I lie.
I drink.
I swear.
I am forgiven.
I cry.
I cheat.
I dare.
I am forgiven.
I die.
I thieve.
I am bare.
I am forgiven.
I stress.
I undress.
I express.
I am forgiven.
I distress.
I caress.
I impress.
I am forgiven.
I am always forgiven.
