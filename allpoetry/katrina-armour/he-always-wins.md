title: "He Always Wins"

I said
I'm like a panic
attack.  I'm sorry I'm
not more like you.

He said
I could eat you raw right
now, Katie.  I could spit
out your bones and lick
the plate, Katie.  Why can't
you be perfect, Katie?
Why won't you be my toy?

I said
Now wouldn't that be 
flirtatious...

He just laughed
and said he was sincere
