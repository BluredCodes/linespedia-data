My mother hates New Year's 
because she says it reminds her
of how she's wasted her life,
and I'm sure my father would eat her alive
if he had the money.

Do you wonder like me
sometimes
why bands first come out raw
and by the next album they've got violins?

Happiness is a vague sarcasm
used to describe humanity;
Humanity is a vague euphenism
used to describe our mistakes.

Mom hates New Year's 
because she says that next year will suck
just like this one.
Maybe next year she'll sing that line
and I could play cello in the background.

Welcome to 2002.Just a little thing I thought of after hearing my mother complain
