'Welcome to my father's house'
The neon letters said to us
As our selfish, pagan car flew by.
My expression at the time remained
100 degrees at the very least
2000 miles per hour

To be or not to be
That is the painful, fateful quandary.
Time will lessen
The cross
Or else crucify you
With it
