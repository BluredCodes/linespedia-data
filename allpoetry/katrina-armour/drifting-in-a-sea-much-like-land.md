This is an ode to the man who kneels constantly in an ocean of his own 
  foliage
His wrapping coming undone
An orange coming out of a peel that only bobs on the surface
This is for that foliage
Existence & suicide & pink flamingos
Everything that was on his front lawn
The crayon drawings on his wall
Drawn by a child in a man's body
If there is ever that day when the sun explodes
If there is ever that day when California sinks much like the clouds in 
  Britain
You will still see him in the hull of his black submarine
Sailing through the foliage
Through his own awareness of time
His orange licking hungrily at the surface
Never to reach land
