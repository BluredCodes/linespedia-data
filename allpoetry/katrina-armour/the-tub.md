The dark side of the tub
Consumes my flesh
While the light side
fights for my sins.
Which is the best?
The dark side shall win
While my toes protrude through
the evil.
But how evil is the other side of a tub?
None the more than the sink
Yet it does not beckon me
As the tub wants me.
Desires me.
Do I want to die?
Yes and no.
No and maybe.
Yes and I will bring my love.
My love.
My love
Wherever I may find it.
No more my love.
You've been replaced
With hate;
My love
My love
Is drowned.Written last year sometime...barely legible
