title: "The Parable of the Butterfly and the Cockroach"

Would it make you sad to see
   A butterfly in a jar
   With no breathing holes
   Or flying space?
What if I stabbed a pin into its abdomen?
   Would you call me cruel?

Now consider a moment the cockroach
A disgusting, gold-backed thing.
Would it make you sad to see
   A cockroach in a jar
   With no breathing holes
   Or living space?
Would you even be able to look at it without vomiting,
As it writhed upon its back?

'Tis the time-old tale of beauty and the beast
The measure of humanity
To tell who's worth the least
