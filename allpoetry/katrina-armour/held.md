As if
     I did not have skin,
As if
     I did not have blood,
As if 
     I did not feel for you,
As if 
     You did not know my heart as well
          as you thought you did.

Dear, 
you should not try to hold
what won't fit into your hands.This is kind of an odd one. I don't really like it but tell me what you think anyway.
