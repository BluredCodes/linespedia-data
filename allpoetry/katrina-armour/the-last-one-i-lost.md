---
title: "The Last One I Lost"
writer: "Katrina Armour"
slug: "the-last-one-i-lost"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/1913-The-Last-One-I-Lost-by-Katrina-Armour"

hello ground control it's me again
yes i'm still here
are you?

i'm cold inside and god has gone
but i'm still here
where are you?

i love you so much
how could you leave me here 
where you have been
so many times before

hello houston do you read me
i lost my air supply again
am i dying?

i'm here inside and you are gone
and winter's come
are you warm?

i love you so much
how could you leave me here
where you have been
so many times before

hello my dear friend
i'm drowning here
are your lungs clear?

i'm here underneath and you are gone
the last one i lost
do you read me?

i love you so much
how could you leave me here
where you have been 
so many times before

i love you so much
how could you just leave me here
how could you just leave me here
how could you just leave me here

how can you just leave me here
in my tear pool
i'm drowning
i'm gone
