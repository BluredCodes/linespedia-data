I washed a fingernailDown the faucet tonightAnd as soon as itDisappeared I thought'Stop!'Of course the plumbing paid no heed,Devouring completely the given piece of meAs I stood with my hands upon the counter,Wondering what on earth there was to do.Alas, there was nothing in my hands that couldBring my remnant backSo I took my pillAnd got undressedAnd fell asleep One part lessKnowing it would grow back anyway.Don't rate this badly if you don't understand it.
