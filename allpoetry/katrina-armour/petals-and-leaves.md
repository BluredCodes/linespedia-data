---
title: "Petals and Leaves"
writer: "Katrina Armour"
slug: "petals-and-leaves"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/7176-Petals-and-Leaves-by-Katrina-Armour"
---

Will you credit me this last goodbye,
This one last pain in your heart?
Can I have the mind you've wasted on her,
This one last moment,
So I can caress it
Then smash it on my knee?

Sweetie, what have we done to our cheeks,
Now tear-streaked and numb?
They used to blush lively
When the other set was in their perception
But now they're looking more and more like
Sagging bags of dry fall leaves,
Withering from their trees
In preparation for the coming winter.

We will freeze together but on different terms;
We will cry together but in different homes;
We will crawl together but to different locations;
We will fall together but break different bones.

Just give me your heart one last time
So I can rip it apart
And piece it back together with the
Tears of my regret.
