Everything After
 the flight
  left a mark
   on my skin.
    Taking in the day
     left me empty,
      as if nothing superficial
       had ever crossed my mind.
     
       Did you disappear
      because I asked you to
     or because it had shaken you
    to see me there
   still loving you,
  still everything I said I wasn't,
 believing I was everything you made me;
Nothing Before
