I left the fever of the blade
For the edema of the pill
And you stayed to roast
Because you thought you weren’t done yet

I tried but I can’t see with your eyes
I tried but I can’t help you
Unless you want to help yourself
So I just have to sit here
I have to sit here until they tell me the news

That you’re burnt on both sides
And unscrapeable
This time. 
I know that scrapeable is not a word, but it fit.
