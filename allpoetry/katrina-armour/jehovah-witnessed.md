While we're on the subject of aneurysms,
we might as well talk about you.

How often does your brain
bleed?
How often do you see the
spots?

Do you expect the best of me too,
or are you a cynic like all the other kids
who think they've been verbally abused?

...and I thought you were brainwashed...

Silly me.
