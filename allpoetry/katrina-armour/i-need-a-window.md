title: "I Need a Window"

Muffled baby cries
Quiet tapping of computer keys
from the business suit next to me.
He sits there alone.
Well, if you don't count the man
Inside of him
or the 18 or so other passengers.

TAP TAP TAP TAP TAP

The woman with the baby looks over at me 
and asks me if I could please shut my kid up.
