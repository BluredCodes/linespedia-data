The ugly pieces float back to me in 
                              waves,
Taunting me,
            Haunting me;
Killing me slowly,
                  But surely.

The teeth of my consience gnaw at my
                               flesh,
Giving my dreams of 
                   Oil and...
Oil and...
          I once drowned in your liquid perfection~
