I looked at you in your full-frontal beauty
Bloodshot eyes and all
But only the monster stared back
The monster that crawls through your veins

Actually we've been talking a lot lately
Your monster and me
It seems you're not around much anymore
Your mother says you've lost reality
She's been crying a lot at night

Doesn't know where you are
She asked me if you had gotten into anything bad
Have you gotten into anything bad?
Your monster doesn't seem to care
Maybe you should stay away from him

He's starting to look kind of hungry

Rumors have been flying around school
They think you killed yourself
One kid asked if you had lost your mind
To tell you the truth, I don't know anymore
I can't tell them you still have your mind

I can't tell them you're still mine inside
I can't tell them I still know you like the back of my hand
I can't tell them I still know you
Rumors have been flying
They think you killed yourself

Did you?
Did you pull the trigger yet?
Because it's dragged you on jagged rocks
Trigger cocked between your monster's teeth
And they interrogate me more and more

What's become of Mr. Ken doll?
I can just shrug and turn my head down
I don't know
Your monster just grins and puts his hand on my shoulder
It's going to be another snow day in Death Valley
