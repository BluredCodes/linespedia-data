They say I'm crazy but what the fuck
Do they know?
I only know when the time is right
That the time is right 
For the stirring of the flesh
On the hook.
The night shade drawn
Black up
Black down
We'll spin each other 'round and 'round.

Your caudil chamber sits licking its lips.
Is there no finer time, sir, than now?
Of course there isn't.
The time is right
Yes the time is right
The guests are set.
The time is right.
The night shade drawn
Black up
Black down
We'll spin each other 'round and 'round.

The memories set the soul afloat
Eating, Dining.
Beating, Dying.
Now elation can finally prevail.
I will have you and you will have me. 
We will dine together.
The night shade drawn
Black up
Black down
We'll spin each other 'round and 'round

Let your cold flesh hold me.
Let your cold flesh be in me.
Let you be home inside there soon.
Oh lovely boy, let me clean your lament.
Let me shine your soes.
Let me ready you for our feast.
The night shade drawn
Black up
Black down
We'll spin each other 'round and 'round

My apron tied behind my back
Kiss my again my lover.
Let you fall into me
As I fall into you
And the seed is no less planted.
Come, let's ready you for dinner, you naughty boy.
The night shade drawn 
Black up
Black down
We'll spin each other 'round and 'round.

Now into your chamber
Inside your fire
I am your abode
You will be home soon.
The baking meat smells so sweet
The baking meat prepared for our feast.
The night shade drawn 
Black up
Black down
We'll spin each other 'round and 'round.

Yes my dear it is time to feast
The last supper, 
The final goodbyes.
I consume of your flesh.
Drink me in. Drink me in.
The night shade drawn 
Black up
Black down
As I lie here laughing on the ground.

I am full
Filled up
Filled up
Full of you
The sweet taste still in my mouth
It's all over. I will hold you forever.
The night shade drawn
Black up
Black down
As I lie here bleeding on the ground.
