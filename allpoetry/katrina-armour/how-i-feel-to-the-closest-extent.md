Scared and totally
Immobile I
Can't Stay in this room forever.  I get so
Frightened of it all,
Disillusioned as to what life should be.  I 
Read and Hear about drugs that do the same things.
Caught in headlights
Trapped in black is what
I Am
Consumed in what the endings could be...
