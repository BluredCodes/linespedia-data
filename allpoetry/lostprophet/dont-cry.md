Your mums fallen ill again,
there's somthings I want you to try,
Whatever goes on in there,
Hunny, please don't cry.

Its my birthday, 7 today,
what a bithday treat,
goin to see mum in hospital
god she looks so weak.
Don't cry

She had a major stroke,
She may not recover,
Please don't lose hope,
She'll always be your mother.
Don't cry

There's somthin wrong, 
she's not the same,
she's taking so long,
...She forgot my name.
Don't cry.

I'm sixteen now, 
still feel the pain,
Life just got worse,
maybe its the same.

Hunny, Daddys got to go away,
the Doctor said I'm gonna die,
Please be strong,
But Hunny, please don't cry.

I started to feel numb within,
no emotions standing by,
I can't describe the pain I'm in,
All because I wouldn't cry.

Well dad, so you know,
this ones for you,
I've broken down 
and can't pull through.

I can't stop the tears now
No matter how hard I try,
All because those four words,
Hunny, Please don't cry.
