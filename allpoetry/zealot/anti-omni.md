anti-grunge
anti-pop
anti-politic
anti-protocol

anti-omni, destroy it all

anti-fight
anti-flight

omni-apathetic leading to PATHETIC

thinking omni-thing and omni-one is inferior
and that's not you, is it, there in the mirror, you're

betterbetterbetter

you are omni-sensual
and bisexual
and your strategic lack of position-
ineffectual.

MIGHTY INTELLECTUAL

we might worship you,
but voids do not exist.
'twould be our own shame if upon the forehead of the 
anti-omni
our lips sought to place a supplicant kiss.This poem is about a pseudo-intellectual I knew.  He thought he knew everything.
