You keep berayting me 
with your words,
Always trying to change me
 always trying,
To make me
yours.
Your nice 
So What!!
You Have a nice house
So What!!
It would be good for me
So What!!
you are the perfect ten
SO What!!
I see inside of you ,
Your nothing but a monster,
Scrapping, Draining my life away.
So What!!
So WHat!! So What!!
I see through your mask of perfection,
You are nothing but a demon,
Trying to rape me,
Of my heart, 
Of my mind
Of my body,
Of my tortured soul,
So What!! So What!! So What!!
So What!! So WHat!! Sooo  Whaaatt!!
your not the perfect ten,
I see you suprise now where do we begin
So What!! So What!! So What!!
your lost now everyone see's you for what you are,
So What!! So WHat!! So What!!
...You know what
