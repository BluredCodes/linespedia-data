A deep drumming within,
darker than death itself,
bursting with uncharted emotoins
the greatest of primal screams.
Then the riviting chords send,
shivers flowing up and down my spine.
thundering heartbeats of soul,
pacing within my heart.
Then you allow a cloud,
soft and familar embrace my soul.
Realizing your sudden Angelic bliss,
despair and unyeilding rage turn into
rivers of light hearted joy.
In the echoing silence I can still hear 
your liberating Mealody's.Thanks for all your blood,sweat and tears, your soul.
