title: "Forever I Bleed"

little child I've
see your blood
and it was the 
same as mine.
I so wish it
could have been different.
I wish I could have known
your heart uncharted.
I wish to know your smile,
and to hear your laughter
echoing beside me.
Daughter would you have looked
like me or your mommy?
Would you have liked purple
or pink?
Would you have been a princess,
or a tom_boy?
I long mournfully to 
hold you in my arms.
I hear you at night daughter,
echoing laughter across
the walls in my heart and head.
haunted by your ghost,
for you I will Forever Bleed.this one is about my unborn daughter that was lost in a car accident
