I stood on the edge of the cliff,
All but lost, and feeling helpless.
As I looked over the endless mountains,
And Vally's i reliezed I had no inspiration.
No more Inspiration to write my soul.
Then out of the vast dark sky,
A radiant light pierced the horizen.
At first I was scared,
But the light warmed my mind, body, and soul.
The light called to me like,
Some sweet siren song.
New hopes flooded my thoughts.
My heart warmed again.
I looked up to look at the sun,
And found to my surprise it was not the sun.
It was a woman who's beauty knows no equal.
Her light was brighter still than the sun.
The mountains bowed before her,
While the green leaves and grass worshiped her.
Her brilliant red hair was as,
The rivers falling down the mountain side.
Her heart was the mighty ocean,
Overflowing in waves of love.
Her smile indeed was the sun shining ,
Loves etearnal light.
her soul so deep it was as if the starry night.
She is everything!
The woman floated to me and I asked her,
to be my girl!
She said yes, and joy rang throuhout the world.
When she said yes, I heard the birds chirping joyously.
When she said yes, I heard the rivers flow.
When she said yes, I knew I had found inspiration again.
She is everything that is joy.
You Are My Everything (Jayme).
