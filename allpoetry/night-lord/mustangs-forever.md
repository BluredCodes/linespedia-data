I am a Mustang true and proud,
Can you hear the roar of the crowd?
Together sharing memories of the good old days,
When we all use to play.
Fond memories no longer black,
Together we had each others back.
Now few know what it means to be a Mustang,
But you and i know with pride,
We go through it together.
We will never forget the old 
Tire swing, The old baskett ball court.
The faces and smiles,
Remember the good times all the while.

Mustangs Foreverthis one ids about my old school and friends remembered.
