title: "Another Drink"

I take another drinkof your death.Your voice amplifiedAll I see are moments.Like lightning,fading only to reappear again.Flashing on and off.Too quick for my mind to follow.Come my harvester of sorrow,I drink you again.Plant my agony.I drink deaths drink.
