Gentle rains have gone away,
For this day there is a very special union.
The main hall red with velvet.
The flowers of many kinds pink and yellow.
Up the isle friends sit waiting anticipating,
The happiest day in the cosmos.
Tiny fire flies circle the room,
as if candles, avoiding dreary gloom.
Large white Statues around the sides,
Still not as beautiful as the love in which we stride.
I stand before the Priestess,
Dressed in in my full armor.
My long red cape matches my red hair.
My black trousers, matching my black knee high boots.
And the best man is at my side,
he is my brother darksyde.
Dressed in full plate mail,
Onyx against his heart so frail.
I turn to look at my beloved friends.
Jeremy dressed in his best flowing Jedi robes.
Steph looking so sweet in her red velvet dress.
With pink flowers on her gown smiling she looks her best.
The other brides maids, All looked grand
Like endless falling sand.
Desdemona smiling fair,
I looked again to darksyde he did not care.
Amethest and Solitare they,
Could not help but too stare.
For the room suddenly got brighter,
As the bells began to ring,
And the birds started to sing.
I take a deep breath nervouse,
For it was time to begin.
Wild thoughts entered my mind confused for a whie.
The butterflies in my stomach, 
causing me to hold back bile.
Harmony sweet and gentle like like your kiss,
Fills me with so much joy and Bliss.
Kelsey dressed in her cute pink gown,
Throws flowers here and there,
And takes a seat in her chair.
Then everyone turned and gasped for air,
Becuase you entered and nothing is as fair.
Your flowing red mane smells,
Sweet like sugar-cane.
Flowing with the wind,
To me you are a god send.
Your dress long and serine.
Silk covered you walk the ilse,
Smiling briliantly all the while.
Your dress flowing as far as the eye can see,
Your love nature flowing like wind in our tree.
You come to stand by my side,
My thundering heart ready to glide.
I cannot speak a word,
Your love is beautiful have you heard.
We turn and the ceramony begins.
I cannot stop smiling by your side,
I cannot even hear priestess until she chides.
I finaly come around,
Still knowing I am bound.
Your viel glistens like a star,
Twinckling so very far.
Time for our vows.
(Jayme my love,
Your love is more than any I've ever known.
The earth could not take it you have shown.
Every single day I do nothing but think of you.
And it gladdens my heart to know your love is true.
Together nothing can stop us Nothing.
Without your sweet smile it can be said,
That without your love I would be dead.
So know my love for you knows no bounds,
With your heart I have no cuase to frown.
Even if I died today,
I know I could feel your love anyway.
Never could anyone love you such as I do,
For my love is deep as is yours too.
Never will I be apart from you.
I Love You Always and Forever.
Priestess pronounces our unoin done.
As i lift you veil,
And we look at one another we,
Can only smile.
We Kiss in sacred bliss.
