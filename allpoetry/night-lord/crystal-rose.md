---
title: "Crystal Rose"
writer: "Night lord"

Sitting here 
in agony
I feel all 
alone.
I need another
shoulder to cry on.
I've finaly found 
someone special
to me.
Another love 
can it be.
I've told her,
of my feelings,
that she warms my heart
I only await your answer.
I wish to 
tell you 
love that I've found.
That together,
the thunder are 
our breathless heartbeats
dancing among raindrops,
splashing among
crystal gardens
of roses.
I just want to take you
and run away.
Lets leave all of this
and loose oursevles in time.
Where there is no reason to hide.
Just come and sail with
me on these crystal waters.
Lets sail to a tropical island
admidst any storm we can conquer,
only together.
I'm joyous that you are here
take my sacrifice,
for tis my heart that i give you.
You've made me believe
that love can exsist again.
You are possibly my everything.
Stand with me
Together we can 
face down any rightous storm.
Come and take my
Scared Crystal Rose.This is dedicated to a woman who has warmed my heart.
