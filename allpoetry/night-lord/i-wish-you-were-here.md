---
title: "I Wish You were here"
writer: "Night lord"
slug: "i-wish-you-were-here"
source: "AllPoetry"

The weather makes my breath tangible, I wish you were here
The children in the park are being silly, I wish you were here
today I got up on time ,I wish you were here
I got my first A today, I wish you were here

This morning I saw a Blue bird, I wish you were here
I awoke in peace, I wish you were here
Last night I dreamed we were on vacation I wish you were
Yesterday i saw the beautiful sunset I wish you were here

This morning it got cold I wish you were here
I forgot the name to our movie I wish you were here
I finaly finished my book I wish you were here
The other day, I heard our song, I wish you were here

Today the wind is blowing harshly, i wish you here
I talked to mom this morning, i wish you were here
And just now I saw an old enemy, I wish you were here
I have a migraine, i wish you were here

I have not talked with you in a while, I wish you were here
Your smile has not touched the heavans lately I wish you were here. I have not felt your grasping love lately, I wish you were here Your laughter has not made everthing allright lately, and still I wish you were hereWhat is wrong with it?
