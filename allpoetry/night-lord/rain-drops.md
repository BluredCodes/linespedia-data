Rain cascades and echo's,across dark concrete for it is late.Sounding like a stream windingthrough the mountain-sides.Slowly I feel my eyes start to relax as I hear the pit, pat ,pit pat, andSplat-of the rain drops.Like a hypnotizing song of restful sleep.Slowly I sink into oblivion.I love the sound of the soft rain,early in the morning.The Rain Drops always put me to sleep.
