I turn around and gasp.
The light blinding calls to me
beckoning me to it.
I leap off my tower and
fly twords it west.
The closer I get I
relize the light looks
somehow hauntingly familar.
Alomost to the light 
my icy skin begins to melt.
My breath no longer tangible
ice falls off like 
beads of sweat.
I finally stop on top 
a knoll not able
to continue the heat greater 
than those of the boughs of hell.
Hypnotized by the light i fall to my knees.
I remenber suddenly days of happiness.
In shock I look up and find that the light is you.
Jayme returned from the shattered crytsal.
For the first time in ten years a smile cracks 
my frosted lips.
My cold heart warms from with in your light.
Yet something is amiss.
For the love is true it is different somehow.
I feel your love through flesh and bone
into my torured soul.
and then you speak to me,
and I silently listen.
You tell me you love me as a friend that 
your heart belongs to another.
Yet your love will always guide me.
You Jayme tell me to love another.
Your red mane blowing in the wind
you make your departure
The Sacred Covent is Broken..
