Your estatic smile
Tells me of your pleasure.
As once again I
Start to devour your Treasure.
Undaunted I taste your every joy
Then you relize this is not some young school-boy.
Swishing left then right
Your pleasure shuddering in delight.

there certainly is no reason to frown.

I smell your sweet smell,
tantalizing my senses now you start to yell.
I moan myself from your delish taste,
your wet thighs moving back and forth with much haste.
Faster and faster we go
your pleasure soaking wet I know.
You reach even greater heights,
as your neibors wake up in fright.
For your screams so loud,
Of my deep probing toungue you are proud.
Thrusting in and out,
You suddenly clamp down and shout.
You sing joyously whimpering.
Now you look to me once more and 
In shock disbeleive.
For the pleasure your about to recieve.
For once again I go down on you and
Devour.
