In My Mind Green eyes look upon green leaves Changing into colors of autumn which no longer can see green leaves That flood with salty wet rivers In My Mind White skin touches white fog, cold and mysterious turning red in the sun which burns like fire Which sheds the old to begin new falling in flakes of itching powdery snow My Mind Red hair flows in the cool breeze shivering calm like a snake winding across the forest floor crimson during the dawning sun like rivers of blood, old and wise In My Mind I see thick morning Myst attack mountains smothering the morning fog, to make mountains seem As if they were islands in the sky While fading into the crimson sun In My Mind Dragons belong with colorful rainbows, even under the moon lit sky While my hands bring forth life to the soft earth My heartbeats thundering in my chest across the sky And this brings further, the life called everlasting love..............Michael Tester.................The flow is their.
