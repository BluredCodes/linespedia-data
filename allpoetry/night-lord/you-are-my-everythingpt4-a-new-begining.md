I can see nor hear anything the world no longer exisit.
We arrive on our new home in sacred bliss.
Here our kindom will prosper,
And a new life begins.
Your palace rises stretched,
To the unearthly sky.
Crystal towers rise above the green ground.
We enter our new home,
You and I.
The long hall filled with memories.
Velvet carpet rolls as far as the eye can see.
We enter our throne room,
Where it is we shall rule.
My smile never fades,
For your love you gave.
We each take our seats you and I,
On the throne with tears in my eye.
Happiness is with us for our,
Love is simply too strong to die.
We look at our guest with joy,
For our friends are with us,
Steph and Jeremy our right hands.
Darksyde my own brother,
And my uncle Tyr.
Desdemona, waving to us,
In our friends we must trust.
Shelly, and Priestess lurking about,
None of them knowing what this is about.
But together with a reasuring smile,
They know us to be thier friends all the while.
Kat and Mtsysaint drinking thier cups,
Of refreshment.
Amerthest, solitary Tear,
We hold you dear.
We watch as Kat, andKym sing away,
Always willing to brighten the day.
Witch, and Jalen wondering why,
That they did not die.
So surrounded by our friends ture,
I stand up and bow to you.
The cosmos is ours Jayme my Love,
For your Heart is the Red Dove.
My brother darksyde snorts in disqust,
For sweet love he mistrust.
In front of the world and all,
Our friends I must bow low again. 
 I bend to one knee,
And and ask a favor of thee.
Jayme my true love,
Will you marry me.
Joyous cheers rang throughout the,
Velvety hall for we are the happiest of them all.this is how much I love my freiends and even more so you Jayme my love...
