title: "I Think You Need To Know-(Jayme)"
writer: "Night lord"

I need to tell you,Jayme my love that I Think You Need To Know.That I need you,Your joyous laughter bringing smiles.I need you words written from the,Depths of your soul.For your words inspire me constantly.That since knowing you,I've never been happier.Since knowing you,I have so much more feeling and depth to my words.You need to know you have changed me in everyway.You have put endless smiles on endless faces.Mine not the least you've made me happy,And light hearted more than ready to smile.You need to know the I think of you everyday.You keep my nightmares at bay.Very much so I Think You Need To KnowThat...(I Love You)..in everyway.
