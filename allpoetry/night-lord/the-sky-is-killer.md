I look over the endless horizen
The red sun ready to sleep
for dusk.
The clouds look like giant 
unyeilding brush strokes
sparayed acroos the sky.
Flowing endlessly over the 
lonely mountain tops.
The mountain breeze fullfills
as I look over the sky
Crimson red sun and brushstroke clouds
I look at its beauty and dream
man
The Sky is Killer.
