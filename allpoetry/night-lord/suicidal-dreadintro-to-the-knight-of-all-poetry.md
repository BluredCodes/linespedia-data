Lost in a web
of a jungle
I stumble
and fall broken.
Only a bright light can save me.
Through transparent
clouds light burst forth
the rain of
suicidal dread.
It cascades off my
Dark  purple cloak
billowing in the wind,
as I stand on the 
wet and muddy earth.
Looking in all directions,
I can only see
and feel suicidal dread.This is simply an intro to another poem I wrote called The Knight of All poetry..
