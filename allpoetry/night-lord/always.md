title: "Always"
writer: "Night lord"

Whenever in despair you were Always their,the world flowed so fast, I did not care.For you were Always their.Feeling so much joyyou made me blush red like a school boy.Restlessly we roamed the endless nights,in shining sun we flew our kites.When the cool wind blew cold, you did not care,because smiling effortlessly, I was Always theirWhen our hands touched, we shuddered,for in the distance lighting thundered.In the rising dawn,we would watch a dancing fawn.When together we did not care,because together, will Always be their
