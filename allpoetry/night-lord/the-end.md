Spring brings forth an end to strifeSpring brings forth green leaves and grassSpring brings forth the coven massSpring brings forth the tears that give lifeBut in The End summer must comeSummer brings forth the shining sunSummer brings forth the boggy nightSummer brings forth the light so brightSummer brings forth so much funBut in The End fall must comeFall brings forth the last of the lightFall brings forth the chilling windFall brings forth listening decaying sinFall brings forth the end of green nasty blightBut in The End winter must comeThe End never ends.........Michael Tester........I think It's different.
