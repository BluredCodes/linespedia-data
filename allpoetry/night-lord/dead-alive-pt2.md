Sitting on the throne of sorrows,
ruling the endless void.
Primal screams only echo etearnal silence.
In a dark blanket of overwhelming despair,
I touch everything, but cold and empty
numbness.
A dark well i once fed on,
I know because 
I was once Dead Alive.I dedicate this one to Slekky the biggest fan of my origanal dead alive poem.  Slekky is one of the greatest souls to know in the world thank you for reading and enjoying your simply Awewsome Slekky..
