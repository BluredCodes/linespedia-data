Cannot see who I am ?
Can you hear my screams riviting.
My soul screams and there is only,
Echoing silence.
Screaming with my hands,
in despair I fall,
To my knees
Begging you please.
Fallen on black days,
Onyx like the starless sky.
I'm torn.
