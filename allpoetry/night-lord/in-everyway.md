---
title: "In Everyway"
writer: "Night lord"

I think of you everyday,
Pondering, Ponderin away.
I cannot help doing things this way,
God thier is so much I whish to say.
My heart thundering like rain in May.
drowning in your soulful love, 
like the crystal water around the bay.
My thoughts wonder to our bed,
In which our heads would lay.
When I listened to your effortless breaths,
When we used to play.
Well your gone now and I truly miss you,
I just thought I would let you know,
That I love you in every single way.
Now I leave you to your peacful slumber,
And I miss you and love you forever, ok.
