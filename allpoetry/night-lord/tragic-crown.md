---
title: "Tragic Crown"
writer: "Night lord"
slug: "tragic-crown"
source: "AllPoetry"

Angels fire
burning desire
on my knees to cry
now do you wish to die
envelope me with
streams of tears,
envelope me with passinate
meaning.
Embrace me with synthetic dreams
I'm tired of seeing through you
with joyful shame
in unconscienceousness
i wear your Tragic Crown.
