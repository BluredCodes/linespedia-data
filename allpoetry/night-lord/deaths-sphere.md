---
title: "Deaths Sphere"
writer: "Night lord"

Can you feel my pain,
no more to gain.
I feel as if a tear,
Split in half no longer dear.
Split I flow around a sphere,
only to come upon another tear.
Endless cycles I cry,
Forever wanting to say goodbye.
One more time around
i flow over Deaths Sphere!!
