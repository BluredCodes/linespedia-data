---
title: "Your Song-(Jayme)"
writer: "Night lord"
slug: "your-song-jayme"
source: "AllPoetry"

Your Song amplifies my heart.Your essence life fullfilling.Your essence is your song.A song with a rhythm that makes,Me get up and slowly loose myself in you.Slowly-I find your tempo,your sensual siren song.The out side world does notExists-Your beat makes me twist and turn,sweat glides over my skin as,I feel your extravagant burn.A smile forms on my lips for,Sweet love is your songs gentle kiss.Swaying this way to that,I feel you are with me even now so very far away.Your song the vibe of souls,Your essence tantalizing.Your soul I love,Because I feel your song.
