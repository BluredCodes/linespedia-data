Bippity,Bippity, Bop,
I went to go jumping Hippity, Hippity, Hop.
I Hippity, Hippity, Hopped,
all the way to the kitchen to get
The Moppity, Moppity, Mop.
I scrubbed the floor, 
and fell Floppity, Floppity, Flop
 decided to go to the garden.
In the garden I Choppity, Choppity, Chopped.
While listening to the radio,
The President is a Crokitty, Crockitty, Crock.
The alarm goes off on my Clockitty, Clockitty Clock.
Time to go say goodbye, to the kids 
that were watching me Hippity Hippity Hop,
and Floppity, Floppity, Flop.
Time to say good bye to the kids,
Who watched me Choppity, Choppity Chop, 
And heard my Clockity, Clockity, Clock.
Suns gone down time for sleep
Bippity, Bippity, Bop
