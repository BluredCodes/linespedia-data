There is a very long hall, lined with mirrorsI walk down this long hall and come to a mirrorI see a man, he is pondering deeplyI see a man, struggling with some great forceI see a man, full of crimson rageI see a man, fighting a great and horrible demon he cannot defeatI walk down the long hall, I come to a mirrorI see a man, he is crying oceansI see a man, he is hopelessly lost in a tangled jungleI see a man, fighting everones sorrowsI walk down the long hall, I come to a mirrorI see a woman, her beauty that of rainbowsI see a woman, she cures pains untouchedI see a woman, defeating her everlasting sorrowI walk down the long hall, I come to a mirrorI see a grea magestic birdI see the great magestic bird try and try, but it cannot flyI see I see the great magestic bird too stubborn to quitI see the great magestic bird try again, this time soaringI walk down the long hall, I come to a mirrorI see a most haunting site, for I see myself..........Michael Tester.........Why can I not fly
