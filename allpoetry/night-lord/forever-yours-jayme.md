Always remeber,
That I've given you my all.
Everything I can
Everything I own,
And everything you need.
Ive given my heart,
I've given my mind body,
And soul.
"You Are my everything".
Without you I cannot breathe,
My life meaningless,
Without you near,
Because I hold you so dear!
My love for you is never gone,
For my love is strong.
So come with me now,
And drown within me,
Because,
"I'm Forever Yours".
