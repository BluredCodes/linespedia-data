---
title: "Nothing to say"
writer: "Night lord"

It can't be this way,
Empty feeling
Bloody ceiling
lost nowhere to go
Tangeld in my web
No where to run
No where to hide,
You might as well
Have died
No longer communicating,
So hard i tried to sever my pride
Yet your boundless love
Took form of the white dove,
You soar, and fly away
Nothing To Say
No longer keeping my demons at bay,
After all we've been through, and still
Nothing To Say
I truly miss you
How can it be true?
I know this is not the real you.
How can it be this way?
That you have nothing to say.
