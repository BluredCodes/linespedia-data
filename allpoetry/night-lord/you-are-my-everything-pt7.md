I watch from my cold tower Dark purple cloak billowing behind,As the dragons all have left taking all my friends with them.Luire arrived late, but ate the roast just fine.With her Lady Ella, spooky that one.I let out a sigh and watch them leave over the horizon.I look around only my friends are allowed here nothing else living,walks my realm of despair.The pain I went through when you left,So great my heart went numb.I think of what Steph said just before she started that sill,Food fight with everyone.Tina in her splendid Armour looked like a queen.Sitting across the table with Trisha and Cinderella,Drinking my finest wine laughed at the food fight.I wanted to but could not.Sharmeka, Steph, and luire got into big time they,had fun.Devoid of feelings I did not even smile.Steph once again berated me for not moving on.How can I I wonder?When you said yes it made me feel so warm inside.I had no longer anything to fear.But your gone now.I walk to the edge of the tower,and look down and around.What destiny am I bound.Then I scream from the bottom of my chilling soul,You Are My Everything always and forever Jayme.I do this every dusk it seems to help,me go on waiting endlessly.As I turn to get out of the wind,A brilliant light shines upon me.Someone has entered our kingdom without leave.The very thought angers me.My eyes flare emerald.Emerald cascading energy flows over me as,I turn around to confront this strangely warm light.I turn and gasp.
