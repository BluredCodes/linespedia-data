In the light it is said we persevereIn the moment it is loneliness severeWith raging tears it seems to meWhat raging thunderstorms brought to meEchos of laughter once filled this hallNow undying pain consumes us allEven when happy I feel empty insideNow what is the event of my down-slideNow with loneliness it becomes darkThe death of summer, I wish for the wings of the larkEvery single day I hang on to every word you sayThen one day we tempted and daredTo get together and love what we have sharedEvery single day I have thought of youNow our undying love is true........Michael Tester.......I think the flow is their and I felt every word what is missing?
