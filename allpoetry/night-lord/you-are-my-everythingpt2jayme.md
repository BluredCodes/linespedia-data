She said yes to me,
I'm speachless.
When I looked into her eyes,
I saw eternity joyfully in bliss.
Our eyes locked, niether daring to,
Move our gazes.
Our hands slowly reached out to,
Touch one another.
And our hands touch.
The earth around us begins to tremble.
The ground heaves like waves of the ocean.
Long exctinct volcanos erupt with massive force.
Mountain ranges begin to crumble.
All the while you and I hear,
And see nothing.We are smileing you and I,
Lost in our world of everlasting love,
We don't notice this world dying around us.
The entire planet erupts into flames.
Nothing left but burning desire,
And eternal love.
As the world burns,
we simply stand there left untouched.
Nothing else exsist but you and I.
Flames as large as mountains cannot,
Harm us for our love burns brighter still.
Then together we leap into,
The clouds above.
Dancing from cloud to cloud.
Weightless we are lighter than air.
Nothing else matters but you and I.
You are my sun, my moon,
And my starry sky.
You I love and nothing else matters.
You are my everything.
Jayme
