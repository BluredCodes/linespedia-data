---
title: "Steph A True Friend"
writer: "Night lord"
slug: "steph-a-true-friend"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/15362-Steph-A-True-Friend-by-Night-lord"

When I was downtroden with no where to go,
Steph you were thier for me.
You were thier when I was so nervouse about Jayme,
And you calmed me down.
Thank you friend.
Thank you Steph for helping others,
 with your gentle and loveing heart.
Your laughter makes us,
 Smile all the while.
Whenever I don't know what to say,
 I can always talk to  you.
Even when you are not,
Here i go to read your soulful,
Heart felt work.
And everytime I do,
I miss your laughing.
You give joy to the hearts of others.
You are a godsend.
Without out you thier would have been less,
Joy here.
And so many smiles would have been lost.
Without you My passion for poetry,
Would have died long ago.
With out you I would be empty.
Steph it was through you I met my love Jayme.
Never will I be able to pay you back.
You are one of a kind and,
From the bottom of my heart 
And from the bottom of my soul
I thank you everyday.Steph you are the best friend anyone could have
