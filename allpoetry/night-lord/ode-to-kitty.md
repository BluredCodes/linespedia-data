Your voice that of an angel,
Your lingering words dangel.
Yuor beauty exotic in nature,
Sweet sensual bliss your feature.
Swooned by your angeleic melody,
Your sweet siren song velvety.
But when I look close, I see your not 
just angelic after all
Why no not at all.
Your halo is supported by your horns,
as I feel it coming deep within.
You scream loudly, and it comes from your soul, 
Electrifying me, tantalizing me.
Your scream fits my soul like a brass key.
I scream in response and jump into the windless air,
letting go of all built up inside, I just don't care.
And you yet swoon me yet again, your voice angelic once more,
You've laid my soul to bear,
Your mealody's raw and rare.
Shivers flow up and down my spine,
Sweet bliss would be to know that you were mine.
