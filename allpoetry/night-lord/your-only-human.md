title: "Your Only Human"
writer: "Night lord"

You destroy the land that is Mother Earth.
Raping her of her beauty,
Cutting her children down
So you can make things easy for your-self.
You rip her skin to create false lives,
And to keep them running the machines.
You kill her children not to eat but for your,
Own pleasure.
Rape her of her soul hear her mournful cry?
Make her life giving breath polluted.
Destroy yourselves it's in your nature,
You don't care,
rape her of her seeds of life.
you can't help it Your Only Human.
