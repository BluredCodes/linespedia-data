True Pain
is far from what most of us believe,
do you know it well, do you really?
Is it a cut on the finger, need a band aid,
Or is it a third degree burn, does it hurt?

True Pain
is it falling down the stairs, breaking bones,
or crushing your finger with a hammer?
Is it getting attacked by a wild dog, teeth ripping,
or falling from a cliff, jaw and teeth shattered?
True pain
Is it stepping on a nail, does it go all the way through,
or sawing your finger off until it hangs by a thread?
Is it a baseball bat shattered, against your skull,
or your flesh being ripped off, without drugs?

True pain is not physical,for I have been through the above.
True pain, is simply not physical,
but emotional, I know for I'm an expert.
True painis when you can't lash out and retaliate.

True pain is when a trusted loved one betrays you,
and when you are devoted to someone, that you can't have.
Is seeing a loved one die in your arms, and you are powerless to save them,
or watching the one you love being blind to the truth.

True pain
Is when you want to touch someone, when they are beyond reach,
or when you lose your soul-mate to the other side.
Is being lost to constant uncertainties,
and it is what hell brings, can you handle it? 
I do!
