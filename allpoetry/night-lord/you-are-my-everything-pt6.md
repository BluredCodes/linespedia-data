The howling wind blows bitter cold,As I walk along the long hall.I remember this hall once decorated with,A never ending red rug.What a beautiful rug it used to be nowtattered and brown decaying.I quickly dismiss the memory,And continue my purposeful stride.I head for my highest tower.I climb the ponderous steps and walk outside,Into a bitter and cold wind.I look over my once prosperous kingdom.The once lush green hillsides now covered in ice,And even the lakes frozen all the way through.A continuous foul wind blows relentlessly.Not a living thing within sight.It wasn't always this way,I remember.In fact ten years ago this very day it happened.My wedding.The most joyous day in my life turned into a nightmare.After our vows looking into your gracious eyes and,Brilliant smile I knew love and my heart was warm.The birds sang and our friends were there.When I lifted your veil I saw,The most beautiful of dreams.Everyone gasp as we bent to kissIn sweet sacred bliss.When our lips touched the world went into chaos.Before my very eyes you turned into Crystal and,And shattered.Total shock and chaos followed.Jeremy had to catch Step for she feinted.Everyone quickly searched for you in the castle.I sank to my knees and tried to piece you back together,only to meet with frustration every time.For months I searched the worlds for you never to find you.The sun was mine only,to be replaced by the cold moon.But I will wait for you no matter how long it takes.I come back to reality.AS I see the first flight of my dragons returning.My ice dragons are first I dispatched them so.I see Step astride Thelsa the queen of ice dragons.Jeremy with her on another great white.I turn to the east and see my brother Darksyde,Proudly astride slayer the great king of the black dragons.right behind him on a platinum dragon my uncle Tyre.I turn to the north and see the flight of golden dragons.Amethyst Angel, Solitary Tear, Slekky, Kym, and Desdemona all in there.Then I turn South and see the flight of blues.Lead by the great King dragon Sky.Astride him Cinderella  rode caring her smile.Then I notice two multicolored dragons not mine.The first lands below me on my bridge,followed by the other.They take form of humans.Priestess and K-chan appear I should have known.Then I see a copper dragon fly over head.Mystysaint's hair flowing in the wind.Then I notice a group of griffins carrying,Nell, SheShe, Kat, and Nell.Whonrock comes riding a stallion galloping closer.From behind me near the door,Shelly takes form from the mist.One more guest I await.Then I see a great flight of Silver Dragons,On the horizon I see blonde hair flowing,An armored woman in the leadriding Queliashma Queen of All dragons,Tis Tina Brannon astride her,followed by Maureen, Butterfly kisses, and Wingless butterfly.All have arrived for the great banquet.I turn to go back within the Castleto greet my friends.A shadow falls over me.I look up and see Thelsa,And a large purple pillow.As the pillow hits I hear,Head shot.My sister Step is truly nuttyat times.I almost crack a smile as I go inside.
