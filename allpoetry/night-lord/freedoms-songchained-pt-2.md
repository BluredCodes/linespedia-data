You've destroyed my
 mind, body, and soul.
Your torture of me is unbearable,
so how can I let you continue.
If I let you continue holding my chains
I will never be free.
My chains flow outward to you,
as my back still bleeds from your vicious lashes.
So with a crooked smile,you lash yet again.
Covered in your lifeless agony.
Still I smile regaurdless of lashes,
The sound of your whip as it thrashes,
and so i take my last breath,
Hoping to come to a quick death.
Then I hear a song, it dances in my psyce,
Telling me to turn and fight Mikey.
Thundering heart beats follow,
I gasp for air, my lungs in pain,
But now I hear It's not all in vain.
A blinding crimson rage boils within,
Now maybe a new life can begin.
With a scream that puts sirens to shame
I relize it wasn't you to blame.
I alone let you tortue me,
Now I will not let it be.
So I scream FREEDOMS' SONG!Thier is yet more to chained yet to come.
