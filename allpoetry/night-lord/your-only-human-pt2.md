title: "Your Only Human (Pt.2)"

Good bye blue sky,sorrows eternal wishes to die.Your life force bleeding redIn the shadows once fed.Sorrow on so many placesEtched on so many faces.From gun to swordcrucify me to your board.You wish to dreamOnly to awaken and scream.Put me up on the front lineswere misery will define.Blow me up with your creations,Destroy us all the generations.Bloody rivers flow like tears,Our lives mean nothing no longer held dear.Shatter us like broken glass,Shine our boots fine like brass.Flowers turn into barbed-wire,pain becomes sensual desire.I hear your tortured screams of agony,I scream for you in come back to me.All I hear are my words echoing back,Your lives will never stay on track.You can't help it Your Only Human.
