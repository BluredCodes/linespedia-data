True Lady It's been a while since we metTrue Lady I remember I was stretched too farTrue Lady hate was ancored beneath my skinTrue lady in my senses I was in Squirming hellTrue Lady walking in hell was everyday for meTrue Lady no sense of who I was being pulled everwhereTrue Lady chaos supreme leeches draining life ebbingTrue Lady helpless hopeless and lost, like a friendTrue Lady many shoulders gone no longer will I lean onTrue Lady my shining light my sanity my guide forever goneTrue Lady darkness reined tears wished to drain no longer caredTrue lady then your foretold wings another shoulder another lightTrue Lady all this time wasteing away tears crammed in sideTrue lady no release until your wings, a bright new hopeTrue Lady soaring like a majestic bird you gave a new anger to go onTrue Lady intertwined with my everlasting destinyTrue Lady addicted to my dance with the reapertrue lady no longer puppet but puppet masterTrue Lady a little piece of sanity at last a little peaceTrue Lady a new way of life my destiny foretoldTrue Lady the reaper cannot stop me nowTrue Lady the reaper cannot touch you nowTrue lady I now dance a new dance with the reaperTrue Lady my guide and shining light you will never know True lady your breathless wings shall never know tormentDeep but does it work.
