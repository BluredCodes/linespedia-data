title: "Stand By Me Christmas Queen"

In Turmoil I'm Bound
Costant strife I'm found
despair fallen into laden ground.
Lost and empty can I be saved?
In your light I long to Bathe.
Lift me up from the ground
in which you have found
Love everlasting forever 
we are bound.
Stand by me My Christmas Queen
and the ground will never
again be bound.
