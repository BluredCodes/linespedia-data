---
title: "Screaming Butterflies"
writer: "Night lord"
slug: "screaming-butterflies"
source: "AllPoetry"

Beat butterfly wings,
beat into purple bliss.
an orange life kiss,
the yellow of noon.
in shades of the blue moon.
upon this green knoll i do sit,
watching the wings beat,
and flutter.
From here I witness,
the screaming Butterflies.
