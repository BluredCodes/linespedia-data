Lost foreverI cannot remember why,Who has stolen thy memoriesof passion and sensual love.Who hath stolen thy boundless attraction.Lost foreverWhat is thy name, forever or and end?For what thoughtless action did thee send,because chaos scatters about.Did thou ever have a doubt.Lost foreverWhat is this all about,Doest thou never shout?Walking in the endless sand,dripping blood from my hand.Lost foreverIn thy love I must depend,for thine heart I must defend.Thy memories begin to fade,For my lonely heart dead, must be laid.
