title: "Peaceful While You're Sleeping"
writer: "Night lord"

I'm watching you now,
each breath enchantingly soft.
I look at you and wonder,
What are you dreaming about?
Are you dreaming of colorful rainbows,
Or Golden Dragons flying in the sky.
I take another sip of coffie, your face,
Is so at ease.
The harshness of the cold world no
longer bothers you.
It's in this i envy you in such bliss.
I want to gently kiss your forehead and snuggle close,
But i won't, not yet.
I can hear the sparrows singing joyfully,
Outside of the window,
You move and sigh, my heart stops.
But you only let out a sigh and continue,
your listful sleep.
I love you in this way,
Because you are Peaceful While You're Sleeping.
