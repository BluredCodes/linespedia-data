In the old days, on a different plane Love Met HateLet me share with you the greatest of tales the greatest of truthsIt began with a tragedy of the greatest of sinsA kind enough man Arragon, saw his landThat was until his dark and terrible master, The Great Evil came from his dark domain and laid siege to the landNow the forces of good, mortal and imortal alike came to this epic battleYet the Great Evil was too strong, despite valiant attempts by the hoerosEvil though weakend , now ruled supreme in the landNot for many cenuries did a force strong enough to defeat the Great Evil come into flourishment, but they came into not in the form of Gods but a snmall group of four heros.  They were all connected but at first did not know howThe wise druid fangil who alsways helped the group with his wisdomThe insane but genius tyrant Sean The ReaverLady Dove Sollomon, men followed her for more than just her beauityShe was a natural born queenHer brother Michael who became the mightest warroir ever in the landMichael also took up another name Sahdow Dancer his name is legend.They were all unique and diverse individualswho eventually were dubbed The Four.They formed a hidden bond that would never seperate them.The Great evil however was not fool hardy and knew of thier comming.The four met Aragon , and defeated him with great pains, nearly dyingAt last the Four met The Great Evil himself, a god he turned out to be.Even with thier combined might they could nopt winYet out of despare came hope, for when Lady Sollomon was hurt in battle,it awakend Michael and he became The Shadow Dancer.  In his new form Michael, with Sean and Fangils help defeated THe Great Evil.The greatest truth ever told, is simply silly Love conquers all.This story is a prelude to a collection of poems,
