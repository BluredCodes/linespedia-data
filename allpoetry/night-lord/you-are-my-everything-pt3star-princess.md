The earth shatters in a thundering,
Roar across the cosmos.
I loose sight of you and all of your glory.
I panick lost again I cannot breath.
I cry out in tortureing despair,
But in the endless space I wonder is anyone there..
My soul grows cold for I am dying,
Drifting into endless space and despair.
Then I remember your gracious words.
You said to me "YES", and my heart warmed.
I look up and gasp,
For I see you for what you are.
You are beyond the earth.
The rings of Saturn your halo,
While The pulsating bright sun:
Is your everlasting heart,, 
That gives breathes life into us all.
The great vastness of space your endless soul,
housing all things dear.
upon your graet flowing red mane,
I see The milky Way,
Your crown of stars.
I feel your love everlasting and overflowing,
And cannot help but to smile.
Then you show me our friends,
Steph and Jeremy who still live.
my love I know now our love is,
 Everlasting because you are everything.
My beloved you rule the stars you are the stars.
You Are My Everything (jayme)
