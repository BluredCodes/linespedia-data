With my tongue going up and down your leg,On the outside i work my way inward.Then I work my way slowly in toward your treasure,until I take in your scent and taste you.When I taste you my tongue is set on fire,and your sweet smell sets me off with untamed desire.I start to devour,such a sweet sour.Twisting and turning eager to please,For now I'm on my knees.Eager to have you moan,I gorge even deeper pleasure to have shown.My hands find your succulent Breast caressing.Softly tantalizing bringing you to whine.Your whimpers show that you are mine.My tongue lashes out in a flurry of blows-to your clitoris - bringing pleasure,as my hands continue to find your measure.My face completely buried I refuse to breath,drinking your joy your taste so good I cannot believe.You scream and shudder legs clamp down,As you squeezeme you now know were I'm bound.To your delightI devour.
