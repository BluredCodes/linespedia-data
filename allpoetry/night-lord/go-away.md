You make me loose face,
Always moving place to place
You get me so angry i just wanna 
Break your face.
You won't leave me alone.
Though your love always shown.
I don't want it never will 
I allow you to have it.
You tried to kill me
with your hate.
But I won't let it be my fate.
You ran me over now go away
don't touch me just....

.Go..(GO Away I'm Ok..Go ..Go Away i'm
Ok..Go Away I'm Ok Goo.)

You tried to stab me in the back
My life may never get on track.
Your smiles evil and wicked
Your driving me crazy I just
Want you to ....

(...Go Go away I'm ok Go ..Go away I'm Ok
Go..Go Awayy..)

Your in my head never 
leaving me to my thoughts.
Can't think straight,
Do I take your bait.
Confusion sets in Just Fucking..

..Fucking Go Away..
(Go..Go Away I'm Ok..Goo..)


You act like it had never been.
So quit confusing me leave me be.
I want you to  just Fucking ...

(GO..Go Away I'm Ok..Go..Go Away i'm 
Ok..Go..Go Away..I'm Ok...Juat ..Go .
..Go Away i'm ok.Goo..)

Shots rang out
And then there was silence..........very loud and heavy song of rage..
