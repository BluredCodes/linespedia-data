Wieghtless against the sea,can you remember me?Floating listly around,to my soul, you were bound.The sea remembers me can it be,The ocean swallows me.Then turmoil follows,As I sink down into the hollows.Down round and round I fall,Knowing not at all,What awaited on the other sidestill further I glideMy world upside downSurely I must have frownedI miss life's gentle kissBecause The Beginning was cerinaly bliss.
