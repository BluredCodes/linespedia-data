---
title: "I Live Inside Of You"
writer: "Night lord"
slug: "i-live-inside-of-you"

I am vengence supreme,
The hate that feeds you.
The crimson rage that breaks,
Up loveable unions.
The destruction that devours
Your spineless soul.
The blood that quenches your thirst,
The worm that makes you sick.
Vomit and eat it again.
I am your starvation.
I am the cold death that awaits you.
The greed, gluttony,lust,wrath,sloth
And pride.
I am your greatest fear
For every one of you know that I am near.
Because I live Inside Of You!!!
