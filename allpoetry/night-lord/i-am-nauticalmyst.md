title: "I am Nauticalmyst"
writer: "Night lord"

Sometimes I'm naughty 
sometimes I'm nice
I'm a never ending story
a dream and
a nightmare.
I can't seem to 
tame my mind
I'm rusted and weathered
I'm barely holding together
yet I'm srong.
I am a dreamer
sometimes my emotions
rule over me
I'm a riot of colors
in a dark well.
I'm a soul
and a body
covered in skin
I'm turmoil
and peace.
I'm deep
I can soothe your
woes yet
it is you who
will soothe mine.
even unto myself i am
a Mystery for
I am Nauticalmyst
