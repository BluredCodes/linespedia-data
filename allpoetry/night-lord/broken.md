title: "Broken"
writer: "Night lord"

Charcoal skinI love never againI thought I founda love like roses.What I wanted,But once againCharcoal burns.Even your roseshad prickly thorns,That bleed my Charcoal skin.I wish to jumpof this cloud in whichyou took me.My soul has died with certainty.I look for you brilliant smileto save mebut you are not here.I was mistaken to havegiving my heart worth breaking.Like crystal shards of glassI'm cut deep forever bleeding.The mountain begins to be submerged,As my blood and tears never stop flowing.I Want to smile at my foolishness,in believing the right one was out there.I want to dieBut all I Can do is Cry,Broken.
