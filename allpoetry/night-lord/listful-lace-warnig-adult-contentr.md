I sit on your bed watching you,
I watch you walking towards,
Me ever so slowly, tantalizing.
Your curves nearly perfect in everyway,
Make me want to devour you everyday.
You come within inches, taunting.
God how I wish to get into your sensual embrace,
Yet you torture me, finally removing your blouse.
you drop it on the floor swaying your hips.
Slowly bringing me to torment,
As I take in every edible inch of you.
You've got me sweating tears.
My pants tightly bulging, wishing to burst free.
Oh God please help me.
I look at your lucious red lips,
As your tounge curls around them.
Hungering for more,
I look once more.
Your hands roam freely over me,
Going slowly over my bulge.
Tortrure, sweet agony, i wish to shout.
But my eyes are watching you no doubt.
I'm caught in your velvety trance.
Sweat begins to stain my eyes,
You finally let it out stretched towards the skies.
After your touch I tremble as do you within.
Now your love could be my death,
Because after hours on end, I can't find my breath.
as we embrace rivers stain my face,
When I feel your velvety Listful Lace.This one I dedicate to a certain someone That will remain a secret...she knows who she is..Michael
