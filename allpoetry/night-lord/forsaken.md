title: "Forsaken"
writer: "Night lord"

Why? did I do something wrong?please tell me my love did I wrong you in anyway?Why is this happening?God I cannot think straight.All emotions forced in never understanding.Were are you?Why did you leave?I cannot stop crying and then getting into dark remorse.I believe I did nothing wrong but please come back.The pain is unbearable.Pleas stitch me skin by skinpiece by piece to  some cross.Then light me on fire and listen to my horrible screams,For as bad as they are,The pain of not feeling your love,and seeing your joyful smile is a thousand times greater.Why? How?Why have you forsaken me?Why have you left me in the dark?please I'm all alone now,Why have you Forsaken me?
