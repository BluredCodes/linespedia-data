You seem ancored beneath the sea
chained down on bended knee.
My friend My sister Steph
I'vwe been watching you
You seem hurt you've been down.
Whenever you are relize I'm 
Here for you always.
You look down
because upon your face is a frown.
My friend I don't like to see you this way
I know its hard but I will help, Ok.
all of your painswith nothing to gain.
Will bring you down my friend
Within your heart you know our
love will never bend for you.
together we can get better all the while
just shut up and smile.
No more frowns my freind
For this I send,
I send this smile over to you.
