---
title: "Crying Shame"
writer: "Buzby"

With your heart so full of love	
Shining bright for all to see
Yet so sad and all alone
It’s a crying shame to me

Why has no one come along
To help share your life with you?
When it’s obvious to me
That your heart is pure and true

You live your life so lonely
Yet you never once complain
How do you stay so happy
Through life’s wet and gloomy rain?

I’d like to give you all your dreams
See the pleasure in your smile
Watch you finally blossom
With such flair and grace and style

You were born to be a wife
And a mother too some day
But the years fly by so fast
Have your chances slipped away?

Oh sweet lady all alone
Will it never come to be?
When I see you by yourself
It’s a crying shame to meWritten about my sister.  Just had her 30th birthday and still single.  If there are any nice English guys willing to change her life, let me know...
