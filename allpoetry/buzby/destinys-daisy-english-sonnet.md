I picked a single daisy from the earth
And gently pulled each petal from the head
To find out just how much your love was worth
With every petal pulled these words I said;

He loves me he loves me not he loves me
He loves me not…and so it went until
The petal that came last would help me see
If true love shared between us was God’s will 

The daisy petals fell down to the floor
I closed my eyes and now began to pray
That there would be another - just one more
Please let our love go on just one more day

Both God and Mother Nature gave their view
And petals spoke of love for me and you
