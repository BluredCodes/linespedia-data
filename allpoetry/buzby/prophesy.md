Prophesy

I shall lie low, and out of sight
In a prophesy of demise
With strong rope tied around my wrists
And a blindfold over my eyes 

I shall lie encased in ice
In a cold store on my own
With silence pounding in my ears
And frost bite in powerless bones

I shall lie in a darkened room
In the silence of slumber’s farce 
Cocooned within eternal dreams
And plagued by regrets of the past

And as I am burning within 
the flames of this, my private hell 
There’ll be demons at my shoulder
To remind me of how I fell
