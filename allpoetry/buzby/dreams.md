Just a penny a thought
How much for dreams ?
And would having them bought
Be all it seems?

Would you buy dreams at all
What do you think?
Can your dreams make you fall
Make your heart sink?

Would a dream of true love
Be priced too high?
Just the death of a dove;
Tears in your eye

And do coloured dreams cost 
Just a bit more?
What price is a life lost
What is it for?

If you could would you keep
Night-time dream free?
To dream or just to sleep;
What will it be?
