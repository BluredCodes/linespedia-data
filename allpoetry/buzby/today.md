title: "Today"
writer: "Buzby"

Today I took stock of my life
realised that I have grieved too long.
Today I wore something other 
than black - It felt good

Today I did not weep for you,
I heard my favourite record
laughed at the Garfied comic strip.
Today - It felt good

Today my mascara stayed there,
when the phone rang; I picked it up.
Today I saw colours other
than black – it felt good.

Today I stopped punishing me,
I decided to live again.
Finally, I let go of you.
Today - It felt good
