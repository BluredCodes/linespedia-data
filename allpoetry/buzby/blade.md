Blade

Beads of red glisten in the 
wake of cold steel that scratches 
and slices through naked flesh. 
Murmurs of pleasure, edged with 
pain, escape the makeshift gag. 
Hands writhe against their tethers
as once more the blade draws blood. 
Soft lips close around the wound
and warm tongue hungrily laps
Losing herself in the thrill,
thriving on erotica,
she finally relaxes 
in his arms and succumbs.
