BAD TASTE TETRACTYS

I
will wear
blue lycra
leggings for you
bear in mind that I do not do this lightly
but
I said
I will wear
the blue lycra
leggings for you, just once, for your birthday"Tetractys is a new form of syllable poetry based on Euclid's discovery that 1+2+3+4=10.  He decided they must be magic numbers!
The poems are triangular shaped, twenty syllables long in five line format (1,2,3,4,&10 syllables)"
