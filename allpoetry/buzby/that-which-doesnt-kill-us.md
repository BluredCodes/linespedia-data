title: "That Which Doesn't Kill Us"

Yesterday my best friend slit her wrists.
She took her own life 
and I never even knew she was in pain.
I saw her every day, and I never knew.
What does that tell you about me? 

She was only twenty-one.
So full of life and energy,
it seemed.  Vivacious.
That was a word for Julie.  Bubbly.
That was another

Depressed. Suicidal.
Unbalanced. Unhappy.
They were words for someone else -
I thought.
Funny how you can be so close to someone
And not know them at all

“That which doesn’t kill us
only makes us stronger”
She was fond of saying that
She said she’d heard it in a Megadeth song
I forget which one.
I forget a lot of the things she said
But I remember that.
