Twisting turmoil takes its toll
Suppressing senses stake your soul
Cerebral cells by chance collide 
Hounds of hell howl and hide
While wizened witches wave their wands
Laughing loonies look too long
Overload of ocular oppression
Dancing devil’s sing depression
Beelzebub’s beasts begin to bay
Dawn descends on dismal day
And  anxious aeons still await
Faltering face of final fate.????????
Sometimes things just tumble out!
