THE FROGGY QUEEN

I believe it was the month of May
When King Of Frogs was heard to say,
“I’ve been so lonely all my life,
The time has come to find a wife.”
He called his footman to his side
And bid him, “Go search far and wide, 
To find your King a froggy bride.
Search every river, every pond
Beneath each green and leafy frond
Turn each stone and lift each log
But find your King a special frog.
A frog with skin of emerald green,
Just perfect for a froggy queen.”
The footman swore he’d do his best
He said that he would have no rest
Until he’d found the perfect wife
To join the King in palace life.

Throughout the day and into night
The footman searched with all his might,
At every pond the rumours grew
A Royal Bride sought? Could it be true?
The frogs poured in from miles around
The footman looked, then turned them down
Not one seemed right within his mind
That special frog he’d yet to find.
The footman hunted night and day
He sent a thousand frogs away,
Then feeling really quite downhearted
Returned to where the search has started
Back to the palace to tell the King
He’d failed to find a bride for him.
But as he passed the palace lake
The footman did a double–take
Sitting right there on a log
The footman saw a pretty  frog
A frog with skin of emerald green
Just perfect for a froggy queen.

The footman rushed to fetch the King
“Oh majesty, great news I bring
Open your window and you will see
A frog as lovely as can be
A frog with skin of emerald green
Just perfect for a froggy queen”
The King looked out, “By jove you're right
She really is a lovely sight
Such beauty cannot get away
I’ll make her queen, right now, today!
Fetch the parson, fetch the ring
Tell the froggy choir to sing
But most of all, please ask my bride
To come and join me at my side”

The footman called out to the frog
Still sitting pretty on her log
“How would you like to come inside
The King Of Frogs requests a bride?”
The frog looked up and gave a croak,
She said “Is this some kind of joke?
Bride to King Of Frogs, be blowed
I’m not a froggy - I’m a toad!”
