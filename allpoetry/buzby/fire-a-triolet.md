The fire glows throughout the night
			hot streaks of orange gold and rose,
			a magical, majestic sight.
			The fire glows throughout the night
			sparks ride up on the wind that blows
			towards the stars now shining bright..
			The fire glows throughout the night
			hot streaks of orange gold and rose."TRIOLET 
A poem or stanza of eight lines in which the first line is repeated as the fourth and seventh lines, and the second line as the eighth, with a rhyme scheme of ABaAabAB."
My first ever attempt at a triolet, hope you enjoy.
