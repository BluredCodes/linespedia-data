(In answer to SweetSadness 86's challenge "write me a haiku or a haiku set about your least favorite subject from school") 


Oh Geography 
What can I say? I find you 
A waste of my time 

As for history 
The past is over and done 
Let sleeping dogs lie 

And mathematics 
Pythagoras' theorem 
What's that all about? 

But top of the list 
Of the subjects that I hate 
Must be cookery 

Measuring, weighing 
Blending, folding and beating 
Please, give me a break 

Just do like I do 
Live on take away pizza 
And weigh twenty stone!In case you were wondering, this is fictional, I don't really live on takeaway pizza and weigh twenty stone!!!!
