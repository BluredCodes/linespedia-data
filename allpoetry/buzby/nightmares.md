---
title: "Nightmares!"
writer: "Buzby"
slug: "nightmares"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/53301-Nightmares--by-Buzby"
---

In dismal dreams of restless sleep
Shadows rule the night
In corridors of wake-less thoughts
Lie beasts about to bite

Within the grasp of slumber
You are helpless, gagged and bound
At the hands of nightmare visions
There’s no mercy to be found

Inside the harsh reality
Of that which we call sleep
Your body thrashes in your bed
While gripped in dreams so deep

Every fear you ever had
Confronts your night time self
Sub-conscious ghouls from deep inside
Creep up with deadly stealth

Sanity slips away from you
As tortured souls cry out
And with syrupy slow motion
You attempt to turn about

But this nightmare you created
From the fears within your brain
Will not be denied the pleasure
Of driving you insane

So each dream step you take
Leads to yet another fear
Your brains screams out to ‘wake up’
The only way out of here

In sweat drenched sheets, no comfort’s found
As morning comes at last
Your daytime fears now deluge in
As the dream becomes the past
