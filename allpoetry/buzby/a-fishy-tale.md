title: "A Fishy Tale"
writer: "Buzby"

Little goldfish spend their lives
Just swimming round and round
They never stop to say hello
They never make a sound

With their golden heads and tails
And their swishy golden fins
All that goldfish seem to do
Is swim and swim and swim

I wonder if a goldfish had a tank
Not round but square
Then instead of swimming round and round
He’d swim from here to there!Just a little light one to counter all the dark stuff!!
