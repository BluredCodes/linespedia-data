title: "A (Very) Silly Sestina"
writer: "Buzby"

My darling are you there
Can you see my lovely hair?
While your head is all bare
Mine is golden fair
So you may  gawp and stare
For I really do not care

No I really could not care
And I know that you are there
I can feel you as you stare
At all my lovely hair,
And I know it isn’t fair
Not when your head is bare

I know your head is bare
And I said I didn’t care
But my locks are soft and fair
I stroke them when you’re there
I love to play with hair
And you know the people stare

At your own bald head they stare
So gleaming white and bare
With not a single hair
You might think you don’t care
But I laugh when you’re not there
Though it’s true it isn’t fair

My locks so light and fair
That all the crowds will stare
Will you cry if you are there
With your head so shiny bare?
You say that you don’t care
That you don’t have any hair

But you wish you had some hair
You say that it’s not fair
I know you really care
That all the people stare
At your head all big and bare
And you wish that I was there

Be bold and bare, at lack of hair
If I am there, then let them stare
It is not fair, but do you care?Ho-Hum, a little bored this afternoon...
