Visions Of  Two 

Give me your heart, on a cushion of red
Crammed full of dreams just of you
Give me a rainbow that sits in a sky
Painted the purest of blue

Take me off on a journey, filled just with you
Where finally we will be free
No questions to answer, no deadlines, no jobs 
Nothing to do, but to be

A simple existence, is all that I crave
With passion, desire and love
To lay upon earth, with you by my side
And a thousand stars shining above

Hold me in arms that encircle me tight
With a touch that is gentle and strong
Stroke me with hands that say without words
While I’m here things will never be wrong

Show me a world, that has space just for us
And a door to keep others away
Show me your love, that burns just for me
And in visions of two we will stay
