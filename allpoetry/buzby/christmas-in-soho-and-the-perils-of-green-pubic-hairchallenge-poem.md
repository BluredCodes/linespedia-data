It was Christmas time in Soho
(and of course not only there)
and by a doorway to a sex-shop
was a sign; ‘Green Pubic Hair.
A one off Christmas offer
for sale inside the store;
Hurry, get yours now
we won’t be getting any more!’

So I ventured in the shop
I was curious to see
what some bright green pubic hair
might look like stuck on me!
(Mine’s always been quite dull,
not the most attractive kind
and I’m always on the lookout
for any other I can find.)

The shop was in a mess
magazines in disarray,
showing ladies bending over
their assholes on display.
A sticky leftover candy cane
lay abandoned on a shelf,
and a woman behind the counter
was dressed like Santa’s elf!

Have you ever seen a bearded lady?
Well it’s not a pretty sight,
and quite scary when you’re all alone
in Soho late at night!
The urge to run away
was almost more than I could bear,
but I forced myself to stay
because I wanted that pubic hair!

I approached the bearded lady
to request my new green hair
and a notice caught my eye,
a sign sat on the counter there;
‘Pulsating Santa beards
(great for giving head)
bring extra pleasure to your lady
every time you go to bed’

‘And a special Christmas offer
from the ‘Land of Sex’ to you
with each pulsating Santa beard
get your free orgasm shampoo.
Wash your hair in a way
that you’ve never felt before
we guarantee that you will be
coming back and back for more.’

Well I only had ten pounds on me
I wasn’t sure it would be enough
to get the Santa beard (with free shampoo)
and a nice new bright green muff?

So I asked the bearded lady
(dressed as Santa’s elf)
‘How much for pubic hair
I can’t see it on the shelf?’
She reached behind the counter
and came out with some tubes
(they looked like they’d hold smarties
not my nice new bright green pubes)

But reaching in with tweezers
she pulled out my new green hair 
said ‘care to try it on dear?
There’s a changing room right there.’
She handed the hair over
with a razor and some glue,
said ‘now will you need some help
or do you know what you must do?’

I looked doubtful at the super glue
but said that I’d be fine,
anxious to get started
with these brand new pubes of mine.
Ha Ha, I thought , it’s easy,
just shave off my pubic hair
then glue the new ones on -
but the glue went everywhere!

I suppose I should have guessed
I suppose I should have seen
I should have took it as a warning
That the lady’s beard was green

I won’t go into details
as I’m quite sure you can guess
what happens when green pubes and glue
get caught up in a mess!
Just say I’m glad that it was dark
So that none could see my plight
there were now two bearded ladies
In Soho on that night!
