title: "Bottle In Front Of Me"

Seems like there’s nothing left for me somehow
And there is no solution I can find
The only thing that’s left to do right now
Is finally to have some piece of mind

So pass another bottle down to me
And let me have just one more healing drink
My brain is still too sober I can see
To find peace, I will need to cease to think

All I want’s a tiny swig of pleasure
Some heavenly elixir for my soul
Put the broken pieces back together
And help my shattered life to now be whole

Don’t preach to me you’ve not stood in my shoes
And I alone must face the path I choose
