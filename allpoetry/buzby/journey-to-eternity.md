---
title: "Journey To Eternity"
writer: "Buzby"
slug: "journey-to-eternity"

On flightless wings
you soar above me.
Freedom from the life 
that has consumed 
and constrained you
for so long my friend.
I send a prayer
for your journey
through winds
and rains of despair,
and know that soon 
you will arrive in lands
where the sun 
will soothe your aches
and lend strength
to your tattered wings

(first published in Harlequin)
