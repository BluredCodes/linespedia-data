FRIDAY NIGHT AT THE COLTS (PART ONE)

It’s Friday night at the Colts again, the same old crowd is here
Smoking lots of cigarettes, and drinking lots of beer
Matt stands at the fruit machine, clad in leathers black
He thinks he’s really sexy, the “leader of the pack”
Dave sits on his favourite stool, spaced out of his brain
A couple of pints of Guinness, then he hits the scotch again
Bonnie’s at the fruit machine, losing all her money
Jon is telling her a joke, he knows she’ll think is funny
Kit is at the juke box, putting on some songs
A couple of beers later, he’ll get the words all wrong
Caroline is serving drinks behind The Three Colt’s bar
Matt really wants to shag her, but he’s not getting very far
Vince comes through the door, a grin upon his face
But he’s not really with us, he’s somewhere up in space
Stan the man is here, he always makes us laugh
Matt really likes Anita, (she doesn’t make him ‘barf’)
In the corner playing Cluedo, Richie Davey, Brad and Liz
They think they’re really clever, ‘cos they always win the quiz
Muz and Tim are with them, they think it’s really funny
Tim’s pissed again on just two pints but I guess it saves on money!
Phil must be here somewhere, we can here him through the crowd
He’s often very drunk (and he’s always very loud!)
Sid’s looking for his Aardvark, or it might be a giraffe
Sidney’s such a nutter, he always makes me laugh
Eleven thirty comes and the night is over then
But tomorrow night you know, we will all be back againJust a little tribute to all my buddies down at my local.
