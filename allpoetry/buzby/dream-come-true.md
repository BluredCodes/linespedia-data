title: "Dream Come True"

If dreams could be reality
If wishes could come true
I would spend each winter evening
Just snuggled up with you

And when winters snow had melted
And spring was on its way
We’d make daisy chains in fields where
The rabbits played all day

And in the heat of summer, when
The sun had stroked the land
We’d go walking by the river
Just strolling hand in hand

When summer turned to autumn and
The leaves began to fall
The wind would whistle round us, and
You’d wrap me in my shawl

If all else I’d ever longed for
Would never come to be
I would never bat an eyelid 
If you were true to me

I’d give up all my fantasies
For just one day with you
I’d surrender my desires
