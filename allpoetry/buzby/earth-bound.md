title: "Earth-bound"
writer: "Buzby"

If all the tears I’ve cried Since the day you passed awayCould pave a path to heaven If our love could light the wayIf each heartbeat was a footstep Each memory a mileIf I could make a highway Out of every precious smileIf I could build a bridge Out of each night spent aloneI would walk the steps to heaven, Take your hand and bring you home
