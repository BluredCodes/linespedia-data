---
title: "Abandoned!"
writer: "Buzby"
slug: "abandoned"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/24192-Abandoned--by-Buzby"
---

Deserted by his family, he is lost and all alone
He roams the city’s dirty streets, no place left to call home
His coat once black and shiny, is now dull and full of knots
How good it felt to be brushed clean, he almost has forgot
A loving word a kindly pat, are things he has no more
When nights are cold and wet he wishes life was like before
Now he raids the dustbins for whatever he can find
Whimpering in the dark, as memories come to mind
Thinking of the people he had thought were lifetime friends
They always seemed so happy, when did all the good times end?
He could not remember a time, he had failed to offer love
Could not imagine what he’d done, to deserve that final shove
A night-time trip out in the car, he thought it would be neat
He thought he’d get a midnight stroll, not a life-time on the street
They opened up the door, and pushed their loving pet dog out
He gazed with puzzled eyes, as they turned the car about
He chased them for a while, as they sped off through the night
They shouldn’t leave him here alone, he knew it wasn’t right
But the car just kept on going, not giving him a chance
No second thoughts, no change of mind, no final backward glance
He waited for his family, to come and take him home
It was weeks before he realised, he was lost and all alone
So now, he is a street dog, full of fleas and thick with dirt
Craving human company, but so scared of getting hurt
He hugs the night-time shadows, the tiny comfort that they give
And he spends his waking hours, just finding food to live.

(First published in 'Furry Fables')
