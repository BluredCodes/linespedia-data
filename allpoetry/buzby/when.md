When the sun and the moon start sharing the sky
When you leave with hello and arrive with goodbye
When it’s dark in the day and the New Year’s in May
That’s when the elephants fly

If I became you and you became me
Can you imagine how strange it would be?
If dogs said meow and cats said bow wow
And what if the fish left the sea?

When the libraries run out of books
When the kitchen has run out of cooks
When the animals talk and the trees start to walk
Well wouldn’t there be some strange looks

If the world gets turned on its head
If tap water starts to run red
If love turns to hate, you’ll know it’s too late
Give it all up, lie down and play deadJust a little piece of nonsence for your amusement!
