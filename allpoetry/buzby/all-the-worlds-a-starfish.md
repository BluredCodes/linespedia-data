title: "All The World's A Starfish"

When all the world revolves dear
And all of the stars shine bright;
And every cloud fills with rain, 
And all of the days end in night

When all the seas are wet dear, 
And all the deserts are sand;
And every monkey’s an ape, 
And every musician, a band;

When all the people grow old, 
And all the days are in gloom;
When every Oak is a tree, 
And every flower’s in bloom;

When all the lovers are dead, 
And all the songs have been sung;
When every heart has been broke, 
And every salt tear has been wrung;

This world will be your starfish, 
Oysters will offer you pearls;
Go wherever you fancy, 
And have you your pick of the girls.
