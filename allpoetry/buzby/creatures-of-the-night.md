---
title: "Creatures Of The Night"
writer: "Buzby"
slug: "creatures-of-the-night"
source: "AllPoetry"

Sometime after midnight, I can hear you summon me
Arising from my bed, I know just where you’ll be
I see you in the shadows, dressed in black from head to toe
Anywhere you try to take me, you know I’ll have to go
Your fangs glint in the moonlight, so gleaming, sharp and white
My time is near, soon I will be a creature of the night
Your hands reach out to pull me near, your touch provokes a basic fear
Buried deep inside my soul, and yet this death has been my goal
For many days, and many nights, my time has come it feels so right
Your undead lips they press on mine, a line of fear runs up my spine
My life is all that you demand, and I must give what you command
Over your fangs I run my tongue, it’s nearly here my time has come
Your mouth moves down, you tilt my head, soon I too will be undead
I gasp in pain as your fangs break skin, holding me tight, your feast begins
Drinking the blood that flows from my vein
As my life starts to fade, so too does my pain
My heartbeat slows down, I sink to my knees
Biting your wrist, it’s my turn to feed
Craving the blood that flows from your wrist
I swallow it down, not a drop can be missed
Life starts to flow back into my veins
As I drink your blood, you cry out my name
Your blood in my veins, you are my sire
We are hated and feared, bloodthirsty vampire
We fly through the night, in search of our prey
Until the first light, until break of day
Then entwined in our coffin, we both must lie
Until night falls again, and once more we can fly
