In the graveyard something stirred 
Through the darkness of the night 
Unearthly sounds are heard 
Like a thousand bats in flight 

Through the darkness of the night 
Sounds that cause your pulse to race
Like a thousand bats in flight
Spirits flee their resting place

Sounds that cause your pulse to race
Un-dead rising from the earth
Spirits flee their resting place
Revelling in rebirth  

Un-dead rising from the earth
Struggling to be free 
Revelling in rebirth
As the ghostly spirits flee

Struggling to be free
Unearthly sounds are heard
As the ghostly spirits flee
