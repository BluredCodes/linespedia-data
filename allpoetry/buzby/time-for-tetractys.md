---
title: "Time for Tetractys"
writer: "Buzby"
slug: "time-for-tetractys"

When 
will there 
be enough 
time to go round?
When can I enjoy the happiness found?
There
never
seems to be
quite enough time
that damn clock is always waiting to chime
