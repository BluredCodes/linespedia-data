title: "Head Full Of Dreams"

When I was a childlanguage fascinated me,even more than crayonsor crepe paper.I wrote rhymesabout kings and queenswith fairies, frogs and dragons.Later, I wrote about heartbreak, unrequited love and tear filled nights.I used words like soul and love, with reckless abandon.I thought I was a poeta sculptor of words,but in realityI was just a teenager -a face full of spots, a head full of dreams and a pen full of clichés.
