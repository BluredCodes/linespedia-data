title: "Heart To Heart"

That which is most passionate
mere words cannot express.
That which is most treasured
cannot be captured in a sentence.
That which is true emotion
will not be made literate by simile,
nor fit neatly into metaphor,
yet can be spoken without language,
understood without speech,
passed from heart to heart
by just one silent look.

(First published in Eclipse)
