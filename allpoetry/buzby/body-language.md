title: "Body Language"
writer: "Buzby"

Softly tanned skin covers			
graceful neck that slopes with ease to	
naked shoulder. Black rose		 
tattooed upon beautifully 			
toned arm, glorious in 				
its feminine strength. Small white scar
traces jagged line from 			
bend of elbow to forearm.  Just 		
visible beneath skin,				
a river of blue leads the way		
to slender wrist, then flows				
out over soft hand, linked by chain	
of gold.  Long, delicate 			
fingers, topped off with perfectly  		
manicured nails, subtly			
display emblem of ownership.		
Such delicate beauty				
so often imagined, yet now
made unobtainable				
by just a single band of gold
