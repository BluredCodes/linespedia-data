---
title: "You"
writer: "Buzby"
slug: "you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/41503-You-by-Buzby"
---

You’re not all hearts and flowers
Nor romantic candlelight
You don’t shower me with gifts
Nor serenade me through the night
You don’t tell me that you love me
A thousand times a day
You don’t smother me with compliments
Nor hang on every word I say
You don’t ask me to reassure you
That I will always care
You don’t tell me if I left you
You’d have pain you couldn’t bear
You never call my name out
While making love to me
You never question how or why
You and I became to be
You never fail to make me love you
Nor to make me feel I’m loved
You never fail to make me thankful
For my gift from up above
You never fail to make me happy
Never fail to make me smile
You never fail to make me glad
That you have joined me on life’s mile
You don’t question all my moves
Nor doubt that I am true
You don’t need me, but you want me
The same way I want you
