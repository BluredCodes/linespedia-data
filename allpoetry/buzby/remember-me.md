---
title: "Remember Me"
writer: "Buzby"
slug: "remember-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/39515-Remember-Me-by-Buzby"
---

Remember me when I am here no more
The gap between us miles that can’t be crossed;
When you feel love to you forever lost
And when your heart is sinking to the floor,
Remember me when riches all seem poor.

Remember me when daylight turns to eve
Take comfort from the good times that we shared;
Neither your heart nor mind should doubt I cared.
Take all the time you need my sweet to grieve
Remember me when love’s what you believe.

Remember me when many years have passed
For though our time apart may seem so long;
The pain will fade while memories grow strong.
The hands of time and fate keep moving fast
Remember me with love until the last.
