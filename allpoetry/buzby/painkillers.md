The label read;
“Take two tablets with water,
repeat at four hour intervals
up to eight tablets a day.
For headache, neuralgia, 
period pains and to relieve
the symptoms of cold and flu”
No mention of the agonising 
twisting pain in my side
that doubles me over
at the very thought of 
what happened to you.
So I amend the label;
“for heartache take 
two tablets with vodka, 
repeat until pain dies”
