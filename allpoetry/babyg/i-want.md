---
title: "I want"
writer: "BabyG"
slug: "i-want"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I want my eyes to dry out....
                                So I cant cry anymore

I want my ears to close....
                                So I cant listen to the lies you've said before

I want my lips to seal... 
                                So i cant bring you back time after time

I want my arms to break.....
                                So i cant reach out to you

I want my lungs to swell...
                                So i cant breathe your name

I want my legs to collapse....
                                So i cant run to you

I want my body to shut down....
                                So I can get over you

I want my feelings to leave....
                                So I dont have too

I want everything to die.....       
                                Before I do.
