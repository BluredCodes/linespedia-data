title: "Fool"
writer: "BabyG"

I know I’m not the firstAnd certainly not the last When you tell me all the thingsThat you know I want to hearI try and explain to friendsBut they want us to endThey know your gamesAnd they want to protect meThey try so hard to make me seeThat you aren’t good enough for meBut I ignore themBecause I know it’s all trueI know I’m a foolI’m drowning in a poolOf all your liesThat make me want to cry.
