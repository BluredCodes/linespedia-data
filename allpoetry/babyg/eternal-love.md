---
title: "Eternal Love"
writer: "BabyG"

source: "AllPoetry"

There was once a girl, who knew not of loveRoots tainted with blood of warLooks of beauty and pureness of a doveWild and free like a bird at the coreUpon he stares with eyes of a cold grayHis heart knew then a romance would beginWith raging desires he couldn’t stay awayHaunting him with visions of pure sinMidnight meetings feeding them with lustIncapable of resisting the cravingAs they endowed themselves with eternal trustLetting go of all they’ve been savingWhen love is as bright and strong as the sunFate lets us know we shall never be undone
