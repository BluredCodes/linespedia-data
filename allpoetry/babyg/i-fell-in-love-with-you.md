title: "I Fell In Love With You"

You pretended to care And acted like you would always be thereNow that the time has comeYou show your true colorsWhen I called out to youYou pretended not to hearAnd whenever I tried to lean on youYou moved farther awayWhy do you say things you don’t mean? Did you purposely want to hurt me?Even if that wasn’t your intentionThat’s what you did. Every time you turned away from me,Every time acted like you didn’t care,And every time you walked out on me,You heard my heart fallWas everything you ever said to me a lie?Or did you truly not care?How could you lead me on to you?Then turn around and leave me hanging there.I realize that I am the one at faultNot youI fell for every thing you ever said or didYou were just saying what I wanted to hearYou never realized that it would hurt You were doing what you thought was rightI was the one who believe youI was the one who egged it on.If it wasn’t for meWe wouldn’t be here todayWe would never of gone through any of thisIt would of stopped before it even started.All I ever wanted was to love youAnd be loved backBut I guess that will never happen becauseI fell in love with you.
