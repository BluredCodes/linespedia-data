Why do I think this way?
Why can't I be happy 
With who I am
And what I have

I think so negatively.
I Think I am ugly and fat
While all my friends
Are pretty and thin 

They all have what I want
They have guys 
That care for them
For who they are inside.

I wonder why
Why not me
Why am i the one 
Doing without

Why am I 
The one crying every night
Wishing on a million stars 
While they sleep tightly

They say I am pretty and thin
While they are fat and ugly
Why do we think this way?
Why do we lie to ourselves?

Why cant we accept 
The way we are
Who we are 
And what we have?
