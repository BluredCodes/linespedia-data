We met over the computerAnd talked until the wee hours of the morning,I gave you my number and we talked againTill the wee hours of the morningWe decided to meetYou said you would come by my houseI walked out and there you wereJust standing there like u were waiting You weren’t what I expectedBut then againI don’t even really knowWhat I was expectingI went for looks at firstI didn’t like yoursBut then we were togetherWe had a lot of fun togetherWe were laughing and having funYou started to grow on meYou started to hold meFrom then you had me hookedWe flirted a while longerAnd made it obvious to each otherThat I liked you And you liked me
