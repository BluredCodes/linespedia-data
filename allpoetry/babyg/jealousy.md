title: "Jealousy"
writer: "BabyG"

We are the best of friends,
Best that there can be.
Cause I love you,
And you love me.

But I can't stop this feeling of jealously
Running through out me.
It is in my bones, it is in my blood.
It is something that fills me up and makes me whole.

I try to ignore it,
But it keeps coming back.
I try to turn away,
But it grabs me again.

The guys I like, all that they see 
Is you instead of me 
I wish the green-eyed monster would set me free.
But I can't hold back this feeling of jealously. 

But the truth is
I always will be jealous of you
There is nothing you can do
To take it from me. 

And this is what you get
When you have pretty friends.
Jealously becomes your one love
And that is how this poem ends.
