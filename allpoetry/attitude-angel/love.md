---
title: "Love!"
writer: "Attitude Angel"
slug: "love"
source: "AllPoetry"

Love is like a plastic flower,
love will never die.
Love is like a pet,
love could never lie.
Love gives reality a little lift,
take care of your love, it's a precious gift.
