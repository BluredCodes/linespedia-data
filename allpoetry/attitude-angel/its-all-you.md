I look in the mirror, It's your face I see.
I close my eyes, It's your voice I hear.
When I ask someone, It's your answer I get.
My feelings are soking, theyr'e drowning, theyr'e wet.
I cannot live, if your'e not here.
I still hear your voice in my ear.
I want to hear it for real, 
and tell you everything I feel.
I just don't know how you will react,
but still you feed my heart,
with lovely crush extract.

I look in the mirror, It's your face I see.
I close my eyes, It's your voice I hear.
You are everything that makes me live.
You make me breath, when I can't get air enough.
You help me when everyone is expecting me to be tough.
But deep inside, I'm breaking down, 
I'm needing you now, to come to me.
Straighten up every little frown,
by telling me you have realiced and started to see.
That you have opened your eyes for what your'e needing.
It's me, your heart feeding..
