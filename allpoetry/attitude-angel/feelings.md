title: "Feelings!"
writer: "Attitude Angel"

Feelings are not forever.But become distant memoriesThat you’ll remember with happinessOr pain forever till your soul leaves your body.What makes you live is not water and food…It’s memories, they keep your thoughts askingPhilosophical questions…Like; why do you have these feelings-Now you’ve got the answer, to keep on living.So to live further into the your kind of world,Ask Philosophical questions about that….
