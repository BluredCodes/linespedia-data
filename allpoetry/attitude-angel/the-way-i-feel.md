I don't know how to tell you,
I don't know what you'll say.
I really really want to,
But however I trie, I just think 'another day'.s
I want you to know what I still feel for you.
What you feel for me, I know, trust me I do.
All of a sudden the paradise was over, 
you told me my sorrows would soon recover.
Have I done any crimes? 
You were the best boyfriend of all times.
What have I done to decirve this?
I happily remember our first kiss.
You were so great, so cind and warm,
You took my world and heart with storm.
Now it's all over, everything is gone.
Except of my feelings, they still live on.
My feelings for you, they just don't die,
Don't you wanna ask me why?
Well, You were my first love, the greates of all!
You were so perfect, so hansom and not to tall.
My feelings just flyed up to the sky,
but left some butterflyes in my tymmy, why?
The answer is that I still love you!
Please find back your feelings for me to.
