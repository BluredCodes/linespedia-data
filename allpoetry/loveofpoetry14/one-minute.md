ONE MINUTE
There's just one minute everyday, 
When you can make things right.
One minute when,
 You can rise above the crowd.
With that one minute,
Pray for guidance,
And put all your faith in God.
There are always 60 seconds in front of you,
And sixty seconds behind you.
So remember, 
You always have one minute,
When you can make things right.I really want to know the honest truth.
