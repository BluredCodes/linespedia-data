Life

Life is like a river.
It has its own course
Its own way
So ride out the rapids and enjoy the journey.

Life is like baseball
You win some you loose some
But the season must go on
So don't get too discouraged.

Life is like a flower
That will bloom and grow
But it must someday die
So don't be afraid of death.

Life is a gift from God
He loves you
And protects you 
So be thankful
Because you are loved.
