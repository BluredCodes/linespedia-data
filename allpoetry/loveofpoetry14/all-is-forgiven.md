All Is Forgiven

I'm sorry Lord  for denying you 
In front of all my friends
I'm sorry for thinking that you had abandoned me 
When I had really abandoned you
I'm sorry for singing your praise just to be singing
When it's meant to get lost in Your love
I'm sorry for committing myself to you 
And then acting as if I had never made it
I'm sorry for not thanking you enough
Because you give me so many things
I'm sorry for everything I've ever done 
That wasn't the Christian thing to do
But I know you still love me 
Because all is forgiven.
