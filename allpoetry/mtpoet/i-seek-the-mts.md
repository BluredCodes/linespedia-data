I seek the mts

I come from mts
where the peaks crest
above creeks,
muddy after rain,
clearing on limestone,
pooling near Lake Cumberland
before rushing over Hay Creek falls.

I seek within the mts
a poetry unwritten
for mts come closer now
bringing with them the sound
of poetry I would shape into my own.

I seek the word
upon the mts
for the word is there silent
& my voice climbs ever up
& across the ridges.

Once on Jack's Knob
there was the feel of an intimate wind
like the gushing of breath against my ear.
It came over the top of Jack's Knob
in darkness
fleeing the echo
of a black and tan hound
that struck hot its scent
but lost it quickly in the waters
that flow thru Upchurch hollow.
