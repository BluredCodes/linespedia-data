To feel poetry

in the eternity
of a moment,

the poet must stand
gin-mellow

before the word
& with a lover's hands

reach out
to find that poem

as firm as hips
that press forward

at a touch--
sensation swelling

until desires
spring forth

like heaven
in a wild flower.
