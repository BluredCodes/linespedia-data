Day in December

begins with snow--
flakes floating thru air--
crisp wind 
driving squalls that

continue until the ground
turns white as the empty page
on my computer screen
before the word appears

first the top of Jack's Knob
then the bottom along Upchurch hollow
wears the blanket
of winter

with cold fingers upon naked skin
the word thrills me
footprints tracking from the trunk
of a large oak will be lost

but not that moment
for I know I am about to be
led
somewhere new.
