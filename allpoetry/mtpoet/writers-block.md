Writer's Block

Joy,
committed to memory
to be recalled--

memory
of you,
the word,

&
a bath
in the morning

after wine
after the art
in our relationship

had passed
& the same sun
continued to rise

oh where
has gone the joy
of dancing naked

upon the page?
