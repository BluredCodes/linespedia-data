Dream of words


I do not remember
to save my life
whether I ran away
or got tossed out
with the word
when we were caught
exchanging dizziness

& for the life of me
I do not know
what they meant
in what they said
but recalling being cast out
enhances the magnitude
of that situation

& I was young enough
to wear hand-me-downs
when we walked 
out of the house
down three flights
of winding
stairs

& I was proud
for in my hand
I clasped the new pen
you planned to write
your poetry with
& the word with me 
was virgin

or was it?

It
has been
so long
now
that even I
have forgotten.
