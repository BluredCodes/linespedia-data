When I go out this door

tonight,
I will wrap tight
my coat against winter's
cold arms
& look up
into a darkness
deeper than a soul
is deep.

If I see stars,
I will count stars
until I can not see
more
& then I will listen
for the word
far off
where silence is kept.

When I hear the word,
I will know it is the word
for I have wrapped my ears
around that aching sound
so many times
that no distance
can numb this longing
inside my heart.
