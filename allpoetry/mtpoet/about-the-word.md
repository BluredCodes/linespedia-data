About The Word

There is a feeling about the word
that conveys a sense of control
like each line repeating
according to a beat--
that beating near the soul
where echoes are not yet art--
are only the thoughts taking form
before the voice is heard

& there is a feel about the word
attached to the memory of what works
like sunsrise at day's beginning
according to how lines begin--
that beginning that lurks
& echoes: poetry is high art
& the one who shapes thoughts into exact form
about the word

becomes the artist.
