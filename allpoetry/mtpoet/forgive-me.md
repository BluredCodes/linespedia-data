Forgive me

not at all
as poet
if I lust
for the word
& the word
is like a woman
naked--
feelings spread 
& wanting
& in that time of
waiting upon the page
knowing her appeal
& knowing that 
one brief glimpse
beyond words
can arouse
a reader
or a poet
to an awareness
that is
a reaching out 
until
all searching ends
at that one
elusive spot
where 
moments
melt
into
passion spent.Found poem suggested by poets Williams and manicmuze...
