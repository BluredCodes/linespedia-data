If

while you are reading
you fall asleep
& my thoughts turn to poetry
& my feelings turn to words
about love
& the poem I write is short
but captures imagination
like a walk in summer
to the top of Jack's Knob,
will the sky be orange,
will dew drop,
will a bluebird sing,
or will the smell
of honeysuckle drift
across the page
until there is left
no space
between us
at all?
