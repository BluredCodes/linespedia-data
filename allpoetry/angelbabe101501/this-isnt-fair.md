She was lying there on the ground,
Praying she would be found,
Knowing she would die.

"Finally," she thought "someones here to help me."
Her Mom came and asked "How could this be?"
"I just saw her,
Who would do this to her?"

She asked her daughter,
"Who did this to you?"
"It was my friend." she said.
"He was drunk."

She was thinking "This isn't fair!"
"How could he dare?!"
"How could he dare drink and drive?!"

She whispered her last good-bye,
As she knew she would die.
She told her mom to tell everyone,
Don't drink and drive,
Look at what it's done.What would you do if this happened to you? Don't drink and Drive you never know what will happen! :(
Hundreds of people die EVERY day cuz of drunk driving! Think about it do you want to be one of the 
statistics?
