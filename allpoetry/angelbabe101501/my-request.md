I have a request I need to make,
For my own lifes sake.
That you'll love me for who I am,
And you'll help me out when I'm in a jam.
Please never give up on me,
Cuz then i know we can't be.
I wouldn't be able to stand it,
Not one little bit.
I love you,
And I think you love me too.
Your my hearts desire. 
Your the one that I admire.
I think there was only one time we were in a fight.
And after a while we both tried to make it right.
I hope it never happens again.
Your my hearts desire.
Your the one who I admire.
