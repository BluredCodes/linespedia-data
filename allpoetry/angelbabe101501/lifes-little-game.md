---
title: "Lifes Little Game"
writer: "angelbabe101501"

Sometimes I just wish it would end,
Rather than wait for my heart to mend.
I want so many things to stay the same,
Instead of playing lifes little game.
Sometime I think it would be easier to end it all,
Cuz when things go wrong i don't know who to call.
Things were easier when i was six,
Because problems were easier to fix.
Because then people didn't move to another state,
And make people sit and wait,
Wondering if things will ever be the same.
I hate lifes little game.
I just need a person to listen to me,
But can I make them see?
I'm afraid of talking to anyone,
When I do I just wanna turn and run.
What if it's not meant to be spoken?
Who cares if my life is broken?
I don't think anyone really cares.
So talk, I don't dare.
My feelings are dumb,
That's just about the sum.
