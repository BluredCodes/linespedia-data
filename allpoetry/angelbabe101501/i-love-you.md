When I'm with you,
I feels like there is nothing I can't do.
You can always see,
When somethings wrong with me.

You'll always listen to me,
You're patient with me,
And you never hide things from me.
I believe that God above,
Sent you for me to love.

*The sky has lost it's color
The sun has turned grey 
At least that's how it feels to me
Whenever your away* 

You're my only one,
And now I don't want to run.
I love you,
I really truly do.
