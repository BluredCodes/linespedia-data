Some people say,
It will be over in a day.
Little do they know,
That guilt will never go.
Abortion. Is this wrong?
They say it won't be long.
Everything will be fine.
No one gave them a sign.
Why can't people say this baby is mine?
And if i do this it won't be fine?
They think it isn't really a baby,
Everyone tells them it's just tissue not a baby.
Why can't people admit,
That they are the ones who did it,
Not the baby.
It's not a maybe.
Adoption is an act of love,
but people don't understand that God above,
didn't give men the idea of abortion,
but gave them the idea adoption.you know if a fetes is JUST cells when's the last time you heard cells have a hearbeat and not be alive? 
And when's the last you heard a tissue have a heartbeat? Baby's deserve to live. they didn't do anything
wrong.
