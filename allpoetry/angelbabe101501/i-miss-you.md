---
title: "I miss you"
writer: "angelbabe101501"

Everything i do,
Reminds me of how much i miss you.
I remember so many things of the past,
They repeat in my mind from the first to the last.
I wish you were here.
After every memory comes a tear.
I need you,
I love you.
No one can replace you,
Cuz you can only do the things you do.
You are the best,
Maybe my faith is just being put to the test.
No matter what I do,
I always find myself missing you.
