---
title: "What Am I to Do?"

I always ask myself what to do,
Now that you've broke my heart in two.
Should i try to fix it?
Or should i just admit,
That it will never truly be?
It's hard being me,
and knowing what to do,
cuz all i can do is close my eyes and think of you.
How could i be so blind?
I just needed to open my mind.
I didn't want to see,
That eventually this would hurt me.
I was lost,
and didn't know what this would cost.
