Why am i only second best?
Why can't I be like the rest?
I don't feel like I'm noticed by anyone.

What have I done?
What am I to do?
I just want to be loved by you,
By anyone.

Why am I always second best?
I just want to be like the rest.
I want to be loved.

I don't understand,
I follow everyones commands.
What more can I do?

I'm so lost.
What will it cost,
Just to be loved?

I know one person who will listen to me,
But why can't anyone else see?
I love him so much
And I think he loves me.
Maybe that's all I need.
