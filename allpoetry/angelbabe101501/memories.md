No matter how much i try,
All I can do is cry,
For everything I long for.

I know it won't change anything.
Sometimes I just cling,
Cling to the past.
But everything is changing and fast.

Memories just don't seem to cut it,
Not even a little bit.
Maybe this is just a faze,
I always feel like I'm in a daze.

I just need someone to talk to,
That's what i really need to do.
But do i dare trust anyone?Stupid...I know.
