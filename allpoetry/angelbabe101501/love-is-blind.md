Everyone says love is blind,
It makes you feel like you've lost your mind.
Well I'm not 100% sure,
I think we're the ones in a blur.
One day we're happy and think everythings true,
the next we're blue.
This stuff is to weird for me,
Only God knows if it's meant to be.
I wish i knew,
What i could do,
To make these feelings go.
I have no clue what will do.
How will i ever know,
What i should do and where i should go?
