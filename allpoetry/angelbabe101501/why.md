You said you wouldn't play me.
You said you wouldn't make me cry.
I didn't know it wouldn't truly be.
Now you pretend you just walked by,
And didn't do this to me.
All i ask is "why?"
And you just say  "it wasn't meant to be."
Why won't you say?
Why won't you really explain?
I sit and wonder all day,
Have i lost it and gone insane?
You say you can't stand to see me cry,
But then I think about it and wonder why. 
Cuz that's all you ever do,
I sit here and think of you and remember all we've done.
And ask have i lost or won?
