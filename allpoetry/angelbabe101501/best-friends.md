You will forever be in my life,
Through sorrow and strife.
Throu anything that comes our way,
day by day.

From the day I met you,
I knew it was true,
That we were meant to be friends.
Through anything that life sends.

When we go our own ways,
I hope we'll keep in touch everyday.
Cuz without you,
I don't know what I would do.

I hope we'll always be best friends,
Through whatever life sends.
