title: "What You Are Like"

I can't stop thinking of you,
I remember all you do.
Your so sweet,
So I'm at your feet.
Your so funny,
So I call you my hunny.
I know you care,
So I just want to share,
That I'll never let you slip away,
Not even for a day.
