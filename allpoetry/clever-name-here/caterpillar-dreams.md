Fixated in a modern dream
Of meaningless metals and lives
Yet the same old feelings haunt me down
Chase me, wound my heart
They are feelings so surreal
Immoral they may seem
They are feelings for you dear
Feelings I cannot escape

Damned I feel
Exhausted in conclusion
Torn and incomplete
Without you, love, I am all wrong
My life will remain bleak.

These caterpillar dreams
They cringe and crawl
They make my mind feel numb and dull
All I do is think of you
And wish my dreams would just come true
As I remain falling
This vortex I fall through
This vortex is you
