The man I love
He's not of gold
Nor silver or brass
He is of simple skin and mind
But has a heart of gold
He has not hair dark as night
Nor eyes of ocean blue
His lips not full nor red
His cheek are pale and blue
But most of all he has a heart of gold
Which mends my timeless soul
The man I love
A simple man
He has the heart of gold
He is not all brain
Nor all strength
He is not perfect
Nor evil
The world will not approve of him
But my heart will
He is the man I love
With the simple heart of gold
He has not money to nor power over the world 
He has not charm to melt hearts
He has not words of art
He speaks the truth
He finds the words
Not with his mind
But with his heart
This posing world will not approve of him
But my heart fell in love with him
With his heart of simple gold
