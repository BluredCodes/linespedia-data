The tick-tick of the grandfather clock never lies

Suffocating the air around us

The conquering sound of loneliness

Penetrating deep holes in one’s mind

Togetherness is only a physical aspect

But our minds, both far apart

Each day passes taking a piece of “love” with it

Taking a part of concealed emptiness

It’s been a while

Wonder how much “love” was swallowed

By the scavenger called time

This silence tells it all

And the grandfather clock never lies
