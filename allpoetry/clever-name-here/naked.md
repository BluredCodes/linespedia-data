My world lost all control
Ravaged, striped of sincerity
Gone with all morals
I die as I see
See my world collapse
A castle of cards balanced on what was nothing
Slowly descending
To eternal darkness 
I find my final place of rest
I find my bed of thorns
I cry
As I see my world die
The cuts of sour pain bleed
