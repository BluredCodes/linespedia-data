I don't know who you are
But I love you.
My life was like a lonely bird
Born without wings
And the wings are you.
You alone have changed my life
You alone have taken my 
Sorrows away.
You alone have made my life 
Worth living in.
I don't know where you came from
But thanks for completing me
Thanks for coming into my life.- - - - - -, Where has my wing gone? =( Away into the darkness...
