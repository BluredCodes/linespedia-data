The darkness casts a shadow
The lights falls far away from home
I'm contradicting my own life
And dreams with the ones you held
I pray to God, if there is one
I pray for your return
My mangled heart can't with stand any longer
I can't stand to be alone
Raindrops fall heavily along with my tears
I stare out the window
Believing you'll be there
I heard it all, I know what they say
They say you're gone
They tell me to move on
I still don't believe a thing they say
Simply because I love you.
You still haven't returned
My belief is gone
But I still wait for your arrival
As the darkness devours my soul.
