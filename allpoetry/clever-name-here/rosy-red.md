Dark and sad is the best way to describe her
Sad and lonely is how she feels
I see tears rolling down her cheeks
Red as blood they are 
Forming thin lines on her cheeks
So dark, and red 
They might leave rosy stains on her
What bothers you so?
Is it the same as mine?
As the tears fall and land on the liquidy ground
They create ripples 
Disfiguring the girls face
Suddenly I realize who I was looking at
It was only my own reflection
On the surface of the cold, dark lake
My own reason
My own tears
My own self
