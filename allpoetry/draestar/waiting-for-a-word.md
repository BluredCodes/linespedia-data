title: "Waiting for a word"

The world stands still
Taking it all in
Unknowingly aware
Unmistakenly afraid
Our actions
Forcing us into silence
Leaving us an absent stare
So many questions yet unasked
No one it seems
Feels it safe to speak
The sanctuary of peace
Crumbles like stone
Of an old fortress wall
In the midst of battle
There lies a silence
Deafening to the mind
Wrentching to the soul
The world now stands here
Waiting for a word
Too frozen to be said
Waiting for a word
to know its not dead.
