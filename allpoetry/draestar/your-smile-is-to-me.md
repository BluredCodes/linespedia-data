---
title: "Your smile is to me"
writer: "Draestar"

Your smile is to me
Like a warm summers breeze
Cooling your warmest eves
Your smile feels to me

Like the waterfalls endless stream
Quenching the Rivers thirst
Your smile feels to me

Like a warm wool blanket
On a cold winters night
Your smile feels to me

Like something lost long ago
That has just been found
Your smile feels to me

Like something so wonderful
An Angels smile
Your smile is to me
