---
title: "A hole inside"
writer: "Draestar"
slug: "a-hole-inside"
source: "AllPoetry"

Where have they gone
Things for which I long
This hole inside they've left
Too big for even for pride
Next to my heart
Just above my stomache
They all used to lie
Keeping me warm
Holding me tight
Once they lived there
Too many to count
Now they are gone
Leavning me alone in the night
Too loosely I'd trown them out
Into a heart to distant to touch
Too often they'd fallen short
Often just before they're destination
Now there's just the hole
Where my feelings once lived
How could this happen
How could I be left so alone
By the feelings I'd once known
