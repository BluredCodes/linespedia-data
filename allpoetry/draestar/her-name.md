What was itSpoken so softly to mebetween stolen kissesWhat did she sayWhispering like the windFlowing through the grassWhat could it beThat could bring a warriorStraight to his kneesBegging and pleadingTo open his soulHow did two wordsConvert a loyal soldierto rise against his sworn commandWhat was...Her name?
