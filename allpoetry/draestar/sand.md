I walked into the waterAnd sank beneath the surfaceThe water poured all around meCradling me in its wavesIt held me tightthen through me awayit pulled me downeverything got darkcolder and colderdowndowndownAt the bottom I restBefore me lay a chestinside a beautiful crownand a heart Nearly drowna note said be careful which you choosefor either way you may loseBut this is the choice all must faceIn this deep dark placeI closed my eyes and reached out a handto this chest burried in the sandWhat shall it beWhats more important to meA face apaears to guideA feeling I can no longer hideIt grows insideIts becoming aliveI begin to float awayBut I don't know which wayupupupI can see the sun shinning throughand its all because of youYou showed me where to goWith a Love only you could knowI touched the crown But through it downWhen all I could seeWas your beautiful face smiling at meI looked down into my handAnd all that remained was sandI had waited too longAnd you had goneBut you have shown me the wayAnd now I must wait for a new day.
