When the waters flowWhere do they go?Do they fall to the deepest depth,Do they rise to the heavens setWhere do they go,I'd like to knowI'd like to sit on the beachThat ends where it meetsJust sit back and watchAs all the waters stopAnd feel the bbreezebr>as from the water it leavesBrushing past my faceMoving on to some other placeOh just to know I'm at the beginningOr even at the endingTo dance upon the waters edgethrough the sand my body dredgedUp or downCan it be foundThis place I'd like to seeLying deep inside meWhen the waters flow,Where, oh where, do they go
