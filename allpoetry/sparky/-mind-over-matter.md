" Mind Over Matter "



My mind,grasping at thoughts
Endless glimpes of happenings
Evolving and deciding,diagnosing
Looking into each word it hears
Time forgotten for it has no meaning

Words unspoken take on many different patterns
To think...is like dreaming
For thoughts are much like dreams
All consciousness seems to merge into
a state of change

Why must my lifebe so complicated,yet wonderful?
What is this special gift that has been given me?
Is lifes continuing saga meant to mystify?
Questions that my mind deals;
Like a poker-game,not knowing the next card

Why should I be concerned?
Life is beautiful,so wonderful
Always mysterious
Live it, enjoy it
Just let it happen
