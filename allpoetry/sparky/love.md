LOVE'S  JOYOUS TEARSLove has entered your life . . .So why are there tears in your eyes?Ahhh . . .but they are drops of joyFor you have not felt alive beforeThese joyful dreams you have had--Brought sweet thoughts of him--Now that your eyes have metThe tears will never be of sadness ...
