Desperation


Crazy thoughts, desperate visions
Right or wrong as they may be
I can’t discern reasons of what my mind does see
For it has gone through hell these many years
Being told what and what not it can accomplish
Now after much illness, the body has endured.

A parting of marriage of many years
No tears are shed, but the awakening dread
Knowing life has dealt a near fatal blow
Why, oh why, is my life filled with sadness
The dreams of younger years still lie as hopes
Dreams unfulfilled, never to be held in the light of day
Deep depression, no pill can take the truth away,
But this man shall endure and make his hopes come true
He will make happiness out of gloom
The sun shall shine again through the dark loneliness
Despair will never show its head again
Never again will the thoughts come to end it all
For he has found a new life with someone who cares

Now the pain of desperation  won’t come around again
For she has shown her true love
A love so pure and unfettered
His path in life lies before him
All the goals he has hidden before shall be realized
An angel has come to earth and made him whole again.This is about me!... would welcome critique and comments, please
