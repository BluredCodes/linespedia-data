Three Little Words

The look you see coming from me
Deep within these eyes so blue
Say the words my heart cannot speak
Meant with passion so very true
Just three little words

Spoken with adoration
This look, finally relieved of all frustration
Can now be spoken outloud
Shouting it to the world’s crowd
Three small simple words

Softly uttered from these tender eyes
I love you
