TWO HEARTS


As the world evolves and turns
The happiness people seek just yearns
To be given and taken with much desire
As two hearts continue on fire.
For Love is an emotion, filling mind and body alike
Be it forsaken or taken to great height
Let it start its heavenly rise
As the sparkle shines in each other’s eyes
Together in love’s ambiguous passions
