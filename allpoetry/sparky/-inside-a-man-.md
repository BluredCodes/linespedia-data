INSIDE A MAN

Look inside yourself and see who you are.
Feel the essence of worth locked there.
Words describing my emotional self are hard to see.
Not being a literary scholar makes
it clouded in a cosmic stare.
To tell of the love and precious
moments I have experienced this year,
Or try to explain away the hurt
and torment of my past.
Thus my mind and heart cry out
as writers try to do.
Expressing the sadness and the joy
with mixed emotions.
What shall become of this mature sensitive being?
How can he evolve and nurture this new found love?
He believes that life has begun for him again.
This world blossoming like an Asian lily.
So white and delicate, yet strong enough
to survive the ills of fall weather.
Though he is in the September of his life,
the young man in love is showing his smiles and actions.
The Inner man, be it old or young,
