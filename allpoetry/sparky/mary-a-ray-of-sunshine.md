MARY ... A RAY OF SUNSHINE

How the sun’s rays adorn you
Leaves me no choice
But to love you with all my heart
And to know though we’re apart
That the longing for each other is without end
Thus we linger
On the brink of romance
Never losing hope
But there still be a chance
To unite our bodies
In words and deeds
And fulfill our empty hearts,
Our wants, our needs
