A WOMAN BOUND IN LOVE

If a woman was but a song,
Would that song be sung forever?
If a man was but a musical instrument,
Would he be played forever?
Lovers, bound together in life's throes,
Shall they evolve throughout eternity?
Our Love shall answer these questions,
For you are the wind and I am the sail
Flowing into life's ever turning circle.

Like the swirling winds of a tropical storm

Our arms encircle each other’s life

Passionate thoughts of fire consuming our minds

As we walk down that aisle filled with yellow roses

To speak those two beautiful words.... I DOA poem written to my now wife... She has been my inspiration for all my work...
