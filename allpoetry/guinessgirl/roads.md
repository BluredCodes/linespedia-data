Roads go ever, ever on             
Over rock and under tree 
By caves where sun has never shone        
Or streams that never find the sea

Over snow by winter sown     
Or the blooming flowers of June 
Over grass and over stone        
And through mountains under the moon

Roads go ever, ever on        
Under clouds and over stars 
Yet feet that wandering have gone             
Turn at last to home afar 

Eyes that fire and sword have seen 
And horrors in the halls of stone       
Look at last on the meadows green 
Before he walks alone 

On he travels,
Taking the low road home
