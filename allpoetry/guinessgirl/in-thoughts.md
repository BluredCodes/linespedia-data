in thoughts thinking
sounding subdued
my thoughts or visions
they turn to you.
you once said that
you were forever;
in flames,
down drains,
or any kind of weather-
and this was the start
of the heart torn apart,
the start,
the start,
of those that lie
then die
as they try
for once they came 
and hence said 
“goodbye”
at the end of the day
on my stomach as i lay
i pray and say
“it’s going my way,
for today”
