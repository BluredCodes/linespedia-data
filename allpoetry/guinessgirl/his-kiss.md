---
title: "His Kiss"
writer: "guinessgirl"

His gentle kiss awakens me to the dawn,
 As I look into his eyes, I become lost,
 They take me away to another plane,
 Somewhere I could stay lost forever.
 
 His touch as soft as the morning dew
 His smile as beautiful as the setting sun
 How untouchable the sunset seems
 At times like these
