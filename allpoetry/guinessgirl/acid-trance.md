The ability in the full capability
of ethereal expectation relies
heavily on the imagination
and the comprehension of access to 
an unknown plain of knowledge
and intrigue.
Logical deviation enlightened on 
a sublime subject of elliptical and 
concave proportions while interpretive
reality is based solely on illusion and
pondered psychedelies and phenomenon
of explained transience.
Proclamation of data inflicted deliberately
with pre-fabricated universal truths of 
exaggerated proportions of statistical components
and displays of moral relationships can 
cause paradoxical ramifications which could 
lead to the destruction of our multi-verse.
