morning comes
though my body still feels tired
slowly i arise, i can hardly step

and the light is shining
brighter than i've ever seen 
i can't help but search for
silhouettes of you

hold you tightly
smile so closely

looking within a thunderstorm
i search for dawn

remember that one time....?
still i long for past times
let me tell you what i miss

i keep missing that

one chance encounter to see you.......

Again
