title: "Lost Gypsy"
writer: "guinessgirl"

Once invincible;
Now, destroyed.
Once incredible;
Now, second-rate.
Once innocent;
Now second-hand.

Life has been little more than a whirlwind
blowing through the years.
Everything that was pure and unadulterated
has been raped by life's' bitter reality.
Living like a gypsy has kept me 
from a mundane existence;
But, no caravan have I,
and gypsies don't survive loneliness.
