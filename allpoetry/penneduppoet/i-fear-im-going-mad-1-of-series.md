The dragon 
I do fear, has changed
instead of lizard; foul 
resembled now 
in horrid form 
the image 
of the owl..
Alas my lord 
I fear I'm mad 
my mind it wonders so 
for in the dark skies 
ore my head 
the stars, they do not glow. 
Instead I see 
this eyes so cold 
they peer into my soul 
They seem to know 
my every thought 
great horror, they bestow 
These demons dark 
they fill the skies 
and watch 
with knowing eyes 
they know that I 
await you sir 
for this, I can't deny. 
I fear that someone's 
placed a curse 
to hault you, 
in your quest 
And I will not 
be given chance 
to hold you 
to my breast. 
I beg you Sir 
to hasten forth 
to mount that steed 
and fly 
to yonder Castle 
were I wait 
watched close 
by evil eyes. 
My mind, my mind!! 
it wonders so..... 
these beasts 
they torture me 
I dare not shut my eyes 
this night, for fear
of what I'll see. 
Take care my Lord
my prayers; my fate
it rests with you
for here I wait.
My heart douth race
with much desire
to see the face
of you, brave sire.This is the first in a series of poems I am writing on a fantasy theme. It is based on the rescue of a fair damsal,etc. and she is held in a castle guarded by a dragon (shapeshifter), etc..this is her thoughts to her lover.....
