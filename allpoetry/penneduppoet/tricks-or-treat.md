title: "Tricks or treat"
writer: "penneduppoet"
slug: "tricks-or-treat"

Dressed to haunt
they walk the street
the children come
on ghostly feet.
They come at night
to trick or treat,
with sacks in hand.
Beneath their sheets,
they stand on doorsteps
seeking sweets.
If denied 
and you mistreat,
the TRICKS will come...
and you'll retreat.
