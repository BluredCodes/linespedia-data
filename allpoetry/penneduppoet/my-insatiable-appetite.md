Whistling kettles, crackling wood stoveskindle cordial thoughtsof homemade cooking, warm and heartystraight out of the pots.Mashed potatoes, homemade gravyturkey, golden brownbuttered carrots, homemade biscuitslots to go around.What aroma!...from that kitchenwhiffling up your noseapple crumble, warm and yummyfilled you to your toes.My appetite for loving familiesnever ceased to growthe love that I felt from that kitchenwill always overflow.Nancy A.
