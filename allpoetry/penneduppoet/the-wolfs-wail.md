title: "The Wolf's Wail"
writer: "penneduppoet"
slug: "the-wolfs-wail"
source: "AllPoetry"

Aweary
wild archaic
wolves
exhausted
fighting to survive,
wail
their languished
battle cry,
across the land.
Only...
to fall upon
the deaf ears
of selfish, arrogant
amoral man.
