I still remember sittingon the front porch swingat Grandma's house,on sunny Sunday afternoons.Being warmed by the sunand lulled by the soothing sounds of songbirds.Just sitting in the solitude.It was a different timewhen life was stillyand Sundays promised me,many more simpler things.Nancy A. Russellmemories from childhood...the word 'stilly' means 'quiet or still'
