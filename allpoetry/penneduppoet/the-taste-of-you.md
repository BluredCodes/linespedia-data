---
title: "The taste of you"
writer: "penneduppoet"

When I think of you
I find myself remembering
the taste of you...
your warm wet mouth
salty carnal kisses
the taste of you...
lingers on my tongue
sets my soul afire
the taste of you...
my one true desire.

Nancy A. Russell
