Thoughts of you (sonnet)


Through haze and filtered twilight, thoughts arise
among the endless layered hues of blue
my world's reality becomes alive, 
and rests upon the memory that is you.
Fond thoughts of love that once were found with ease 
that woke my senses causing lust to rise
are hidden now amongst my mind's disease;
deceit, it peeks through stark cold crystal eyes. 
For your reality was not akin
with what I had envisioned life would be
and I had not a chosen place within
that life,  left lying empty... void of me.
My soul in suffering,  heaves and deeply sighs
and hides amongst that chosen list of lies.
