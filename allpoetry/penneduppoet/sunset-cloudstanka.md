high grey-headed cloudsshroud scarlet spectral colorssecreting beneathglowing with radiance inthe evenings setting sunlightNancy A. RussellThis tanka poem came to me while driving home one night..the sunset was so beautiful and these heavy grey clouds hung in the air with beautiful shades of orange-red amber like colors underneath them. Nature is wondrous!
