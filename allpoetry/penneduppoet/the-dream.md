I've had dreams of you and I,embraced and dancing in the sky.Riding on the tails of clouds,singing, laughing, free and proud.Rejoicing in the love we share,bidding, no one entrance there.How wonderful!...the sheer delightuntil the dawn, and morning's light.Nancy A. Russell
