I feel it throughthe facility of bodily perception,of gut sensation.I can sense it,this impending agony.With such capacity, that it isas if Nemesis herself,were sneering back at methrough the lurid fantastical radiancein the glasswork of my mirror.Time stands still, and once againI am victim to the manipulative distortion.I barely recognize this reflection,that I look upon.It seems to taunt me, to laugh at meI can almost hear it's perculiar chortle.Nancy A. Russell
