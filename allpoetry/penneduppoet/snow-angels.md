Snow Angels

As a child I lay
upon winter's forest floor
forming snow angels.
Innocent and gazing high beyond
the glistening trees, my spirit soared
deep within that velvet sky.
Far beyond reality and 
floating free, I came the closest
I will ever be
to God's ideal...
with this angel,
I had formed
from  childhood purity.

Nancy A. Russell, Dec.21,2001
