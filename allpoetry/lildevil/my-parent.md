---
title: "My Parent"
writer: "lildevil"
slug: "my-parent"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/53814-My-Parent-by-lildevil"
---

all i need is a bottle
i don't need your shit anymore
all i need is a bottle
it gives me the love you didn't store
my bottles make me happy
they give me the laughs you never gave

all i need is a bottle
they give me the feeling of warmth
all i need is a bottle
to fill that empty space
my bottles make me happy
from my memory you're erased

all i need is a bottle
to show me life through another's eyes
all i need is a bottle
to make my heart reach a normal size
my bottles make me happy
..... they were a better parent then you ever were!
