---
title: "I Know"
writer: "yussufhot911"

I know you're not,
the prettiest girl I'll meet, 
and I know I'm not,
the most handsome guy you will find, 
but when we are together,
in each others arms,
that is the sweetest,
feeling I know, 
and the only place,
that it feels right to be...

I know,
we'll have those days,
when everything seems impossible, 
when everything seems wrong,
and I know we'll have moments,  
when we might want to be alone,
or might think we've had enough, 
but I know,
it's just a phase,
and when you hold me,
at the end of the day,
everything will be ok,
I know...
