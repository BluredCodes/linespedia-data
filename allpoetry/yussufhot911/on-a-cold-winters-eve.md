title: "On a cold Winter's Eve"

The day is still,
and undisturbed, but short,
and the night, silent,
and I feel alone,
with only the stars,
the moon and the wind,
and it whispers sweet words,
though sadly I can't hear,
for I am, too far away...

It's another cold day,
and the winds, almost icy,
caress my face,
and calms my soul,
I feel at peace, free,
with not a care in the world,
not a thought about all the things,
that are wrong in my life, 
I'm at ease,
on a cold winters ever...
