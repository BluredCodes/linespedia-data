---
title: "It always hurts when it's Love"
writer: "yussufhot911"

Bitter-sweet are the memories,
that love at times bestows on me,
how could something so beautiful,
break my heart in pieces, yet at the same time,
move mountains I thought were unmovable...?


I've put my trust in what I thought was real,
and what for so long just felt so real,
but fate must have thought otherwise,
for without warning it was over and I could not find my feet...


I've lost count of nights when I held my pillow,
but all I felt were the tears I cried,
I never expected something perfect from love,
but I thought maybe someone for the rest of my life...


It always hurts when things go wrong,
or when hearts adrift can't seem to hold on,
what's worse is the feeling when the walls crash in,
and "what to do", is a thought that eludes you...


I never thought of perfection, when I thought of love,
but I thought to have someone for the rest of my life,
and at times I was close with a walk down the isle,
but there was something that changed and love told me good-bye...
it always hurts when it's love...!!
