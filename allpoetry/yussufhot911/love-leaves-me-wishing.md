---
title: "Love leaves me wishing"
writer: "yussufhot911"
slug: "love-leaves-me-wishing"

I wish I could show you,
how you make me feel inside,
or how you steal my heart each day,
whenever I see your smile...

I wish I could hold you,
for my winter's warm embrace,
and if not forever then for just one night,
so that one night in my life, would be complete...

I wish you could understand,
how you set my emotions wild,
or how I melt inside, whenever I see your eyes,
though my tears remind me that you're just a dream...

I wish you could understand,
how a day without seeing you, just doesn't end the same,
and if I lived a lifetime without finding you,
then all would be lost and my life would be in vain...

Love leaves me wishing, hoping and praying,
wishing I could find someone as special as you,
hoping we could spend our life together,
and praying that I would never wake from this dream,
love leaves me wishing, wishing we were together, forever...
