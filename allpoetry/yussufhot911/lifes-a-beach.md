---
title: "Life's a Beach"
writer: "yussufhot911"
slug: "lifes-a-beach"

There are times,
when life is really like a beach,
when the day just starts out perfect,
and nothing seems out of reach...

There are days,
when you can't stop smiling,
cause the day feels so good,
and you wish you knew the reason,
and you wish you understood...

These days for some, might be so few,
while for others, it's a day they still dream of,
Life can't always be that beach,
but when you're down and about to give up,
remember the stars cause they are not out of reach...

So I hope your day was close to sweet,
but if it was not I'll make this wish,
and maybe tomorrow when you wake up late,
the day will be perfect and you can blame it on fate...Have you had days that just felt so good you couldn't describe them..?
