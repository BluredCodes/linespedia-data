Some may never fall in “Love”, 
and some may never try,
because some days you’ll feel so good,
while others make you cry...

When you fall in “Love” it’s such a sweet feeling,
because  it’s the “Magic” your heart is revealing,
there’s no need for money or pots filled with gold,
because when it’s real, it will never grow old...

Falling in “Love” can sometimes take a lifetime,
or you could be unlucky and it never comes your way,
but if you ever find it, you’ll be floating on cloud nine,
only dreaming of a “Love” that stands the test of time...

So as the time will pass,
and the seasons will change,
I hope all goes well,
and your heart will be tamed...the depth and flow, does it touch the reader and make them think about it..?
