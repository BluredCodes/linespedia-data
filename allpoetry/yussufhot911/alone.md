---
title: "Alone"

Scars still remain,
from days when love was mine,
and days when for the first time,
I could say I felt complete,
but fate was against me or maybe time,
for I was young and my love, younger,
and her parents chose to draw the line...

Since that day, and the tears that followed,
nothing with my heart has been the same,
I thought forever was where we'd be,
but the wind and raindrops whispered to me,
and they told me that our forever was gone...

The pain still remains, locked away in my heart,
and I'm left with only a picture of what could have been,
and though I try to move on, to get stronger and back on my feet again,
I remember what we had together and I'm back where I started, alone...
