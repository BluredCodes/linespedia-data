title: "I wish each day"

I wish each day could be like today,
when I woke up late and saw birds at play,
on a window sill, where they sang all day...

And I wish each day could start as sweet,
when the sun's sweet rays just kissed my face,
and it felt like a dream in another place...

So I wish each day could feel as nice,
as I stepped outside and searched the sky,
and God's glory was all that shone from up high...

Still, I wish each day could be like today,
when mom's sweet pie was the first meal my way,
and her amazing dinner was what ended my day...There are some days which just flow so smoothly you wish they never end, or maybe you can have them again. do you remember those days..?
