You Promised to always be there for me no matter what
No matter what happened between anyone else
You Promised to be my One and True Love
The One who made me believe again in Love,Trust,And Intergity

You Promised me that my past you would never repeat
So I wouldn't have to re-live those memories
You Promised to the Sun in my gray skies to make all
My pain and sadness go away

You Promised to never sail into uncharted seas as long as 
We were commited as One
You Promised to always be true to me even if it hurt you or me
As long as we were One and in love

You Promised and promised and promised me all of these things
And where has it gotten me??
No where but with a stronger heart and mind and spirit
You Promised me but
What Happpened?!?!?!?!??!?.............
