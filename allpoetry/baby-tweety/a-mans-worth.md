A Man's Worth sometimes is greatly under appreciated by the work he has done
What about the Man who takes cares of his kids because the mother has died or left the family??
What about the Man who works three jobs to pay child support for a child that is not even his??
What about the Man who takes times out his busy schedule to please his Woman at home with dinner and all she does is nags??

What about the Man who is faithful to his Woman even though he knows she's cheating on him with his Best Friend??
What about the Man who takes care of everyone he meets and loves even though they may stab him in the back??
What about a Man who lets his Child make mistakes time and time again and never says "I told you so"????

What about the Man who does these things and beyond and we never say thank you or I Love You,Daddy???
What about the Man???

I think that Man deserves a Standing Ovation and he depicts what a real Man is.
This is a A Man's Worth!!!!!!!
