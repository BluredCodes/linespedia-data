title: "I Trusted You"
writer: "Baby Tweety"

You were someone I put my whole heart and soul into
I Trusted You
You were supposed to be my Best Friend who would never hurt me
But I was wrong as I can see now because
I Trusted You

I told you things I have never told anyone before in my life
Made myself confront issues for you to help me mature into this
Woman you wanted me to be-so pure and true
All becasue I Trusted You

I argured with family and friends who told me that you were a backstabbin
Son of a Bitch and I defended you by saying no
And here all along you were doing exactly what they said you would do to me
Making me believe that we had a special kind of relationship that would Never be violated
All because I Trusted You

I left state with the care of my closest friend at hand for you to take Care of-to guide,love,nurture
I certainly didn't expect the turn of events that took place for when I Came back home
Everyone kept saying that you were doing stuff you weren't supposed to
But I said No!!! All because I Trusted You

I must admit that you have taught me some valuable lessons that I in my Life will never ever forget
How I put my energy into a relationship that was a fraud from that Beginning and was never in one way Pure
But I am a better woman now because of this and I'm happy now
None of what's happened to me would have came true
If I hadn't Trusted You
