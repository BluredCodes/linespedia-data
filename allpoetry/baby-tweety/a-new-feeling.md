---
title: "A New Feeling"
writer: "Baby Tweety"
slug: "a-new-feeling"

There is something wonderful growing inside of me
That I never thought would happen 
A New Feeling that can never be replaced by money,gifts,or time.

I marvel every night as I look at you from outside in
And you never seem to amaze me!!
I don't even know who you are yet or what you look like
But please know that I will love you and I know you will love me!

A New Feeling is one mixed with happiness and sadness,excitement,anxiety
love,joyous time in my life but with the feeling of overwhelmness of not being a perfect mother/father for you

Just know that I promise to you and before God that I will do my damedest
To make sure you have everything you need and more
A New Feeling is what I have for you
NOw I just can't wait to see what else is in store.......
