---
title: "I Don't Need You"
writer: "Baby Tweety"
slug: "i-dont-need-you"

I used to think that without you in my life
I couldn't do right
I used to think that during my darkness in days and nights
You would and always be my light

I used to dream of a glorious wedding with flowers,music,birds
families getting together, and our love regconized as ONE
I used to think that of a Love like ours that we could stand the Test of Time-that no matter what race we competed in we won

I used to think that I had to depend on you for my air, blood, sweat, tears, emotional,mental,physical dependents but most of all-My LIFE!!
I remember lying awake at night smiling from ear to ear at the thought of you laying next to me and me being called your Wife.

But, I have come to realize that I can do bad all on my own without your help or desires
I Don't Need You anymore because Baby I have realized that my Love,Lust,Inspiration for you has expired!!!!!!

I DON'T NEED YOU ANYMORE!!!!!!! 
MAYBE THE QUESTION IS THIS: DO YOU NEED ME???
