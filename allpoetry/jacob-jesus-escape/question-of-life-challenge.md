and in an ideal world 
where a question of life 
and time brings questions 
where could what be better 

when short term answers 
loose the loyal hound 
when the baying background 
loses its stupid master 

out on the long haul ahead 
a whip goes to 
a worker showing promise 
out standing in his fielding pain 

where is the mystery 
where is our ideal 
with those born every minute 
with cookie cutter souls 

who can judge 
the special but the special 
who can live 
with their every decision 

and is youth wasted always 
on the youth and the old 
on their last caring leg 
and is any answer right always 

if my answer would be right 
i could answer happily and forever 
if it was this time wrong 
i could presume it didn t matter 

love s not always sacrifice 
love s not always right 
our question will not end until 
our questions meet the light
