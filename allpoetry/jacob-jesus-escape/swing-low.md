/\ 

swing low 

i breathe when i cannot breathe 
i walk where i cannot go 
i laugh as cruel spirits seethe 
i see what i cannot know 

death is a laugh at strife 
and pain is a joy to feel 
the highest points in a life 
are when times come to heal 

\/sickly sweet (challenge submission)
but when it ran by i had to reach out
trip it and laugh ... ha {~D
