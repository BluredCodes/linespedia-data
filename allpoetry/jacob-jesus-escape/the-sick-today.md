title: "the sick today"
writer: "Jacob Jesus Escape"

where to go
nowhere 
to hide
everywhere
wise 
to leave it 
at that 
but 
no you go 
on 
dragging bunny dust across static tubes 
the dirty hairy rabbit blood soaking it all up
from this furred game still kicking like crazy tongues 
never squeezed from the proper end
but flicked like guts
onto shores that flood
the minute we drive away
i give you five stars today
baby
they are the bullets of tomorrow
that you can t leave behind
by complaining about how
that s just not right
as if 
any of us make
as opposed to follow
and make fools
of us 
all pretty now
are we
where do we go
nowhere
do we hide
everywhere
wise 
to leave it
at that
but
no we go
onsorry
i'm sick today
are you
