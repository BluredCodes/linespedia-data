swings like child laughings
oblivious to fine points
touched upon the world

and we waste the words oblivious to spear fine
in spoiled child touched form as touched childlike forms
break like breeding thoughts to bear down upon planet

in the spitting rites of insistent thoughts 
of beasts who coddle these eggs kinking 
genetics of god spilled minister goods for mutant fruit 

pews and childish masters hidden by glass 
of sound proof
and cats craving still times for dog life times

thread for doggish threads of time and doglike fishes
fam lies stringing bead purses of skate key thinking
pavement infinite sinking past rivers

build us a new mountain home upon the sea grass mown 
down like prayer attempting to reach god mass and cast 
a myrrh smoke upon waters pure in mounting old winnowing

death tolls counting down rung upon command
a captain of industry imagination bolstered by those drugs
beyond emasculation and human caring

take me now heaven thrust of godless vision passed
by referendum
prostitution reign over thinking fool fever ever over thought

take spit upon roads
the apple of farmer eye roll all the way down ever planting 
this hell of lost reality till the end of field

plant me now heaven as 
all were planted down here
where the vultures reign

plan me now in hell 
where life can never evolve
in these market stalls shrinking in substance sputtering 

like commercial irradiation 
shutting windows and passing law 
upon green until it grows down and mowers are used in the burning 

mock battles of this greatest game
that of the moment and the using up of time
that change tick tock hearts with plastic baubles mined from this 

shaft of our worlds 
back filled with fire 
air

board us now rockets to carry us off the rock we stand 
before boards painted with caring bright 
in their understanding of how colors lie

and no one believes the raving of lunatics stuffed with broken glass
or notions of want beyond cathode ray mirrors and liquid crystals
but only the night spent before our money comes can hold a dream nowbeware trite raving 
senryu series the same 
as it ever was
