in a 20 gallon tank
waiting for love
to be stripped of starved milt
dying vicariously
with the so called keeper
til death do us part
right
fish settle for flakes
humans run off at the love
channels
like communion and communication
bloat
with living ends
when this cold pet dies
a new task lived
beyond my moving away
to carrying the tragic art of decay
from its putrid aquarium
i dipped a net
and payed the price
of old soup living 
death slipped through my net
and a cold conconction hit me
this creature looking whole had dissolved
into 20 gallons of water
as i tried to lift it away
a wretching job of buckets
carried sloshing stuff of
puke up basement stairs
awaited like the sneer
of a left wife
and reminders
of lifes messy nature
