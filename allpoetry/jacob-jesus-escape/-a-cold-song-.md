dawn blue sings
metal whispers sharp
on sleeping blades
honing winter steel

metal whispers sharp
sleet sprites
honing winter steel
crystal takes to sky

sleet sprites
come to rest dreaming
crystal takes to sky
filling a clear void

come to rest dreaming
like snow
filling a clear void
reflecting ice black

like snow
on sleeping blades
reflecting ice black 
dawn blue sings

-a cold song-pantoum?
senryu series?
who knows...not i
