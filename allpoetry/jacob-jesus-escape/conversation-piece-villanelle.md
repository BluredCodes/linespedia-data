The charm of a bird's sweet chat,
on purple plum branches bowing,
puffed as a cold rain spat

sparrow cheering, warm and fat.
I stand wet not really knowing
the charm of a bird's sweet chat,

sprinkling song, for the brunette at
our bus stop. Her slip, then showing,
puffed as a cold rain spat.

At that breeze I now tip my hat,
for distractions while waiting for going,
the charm of a bird's sweet chat,

for sweet legs as shapely as that
and for that skirt lifting and blowing,
puffed as a cold rain spat.

Snatching it, quick as the cat,
she blushes now, fairly glowing.
The charm of a bird's sweet chat

puffed as a cold rain spat.my first attempt at this form
