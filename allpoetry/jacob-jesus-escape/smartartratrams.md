seed only
unknowing
pretence
pretending
like a poetasked to write
fobizarre
paper banners
of fish christian bliss to sing 
guitar songs ovefrankincense 
murderscalding for breasts

falling to the priestly antithesis

woman solid 
lee torn
of milk
shoving children in
to sound proof glass prisons
of merrimack dinners

as cheese 

see as ever
the milk cough
humangnorance
not gain
again
add how man
y times and
more than 
and how 
i can
say
123UO

mea culpe 
go get it slave
