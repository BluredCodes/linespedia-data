i heard the pain
but the day was good
and i truly believed that 
it just wouldnt growl
like a terribly frightened dog

yet there it was
a bloody treat
in a bitten hand
with a cur in flight
to smoke from a hidden box

well noone knew 
the same old story
so we all failed
in our same old way
and quite helplessly on cue

all threw up hands
and then said what 
with a big question mark
save those with what
remained an untold story

we couldn't even know so
when you leave your friends
and dance with family
a story might end like this
even if your friends are siblings

can dance around your anger
and chastise those that dont
follow a lead not given
in a waltz oblivious
as you with your stepped on toesthrowing this out there without any work...
like anger at your family
