---
title: "out of sight"
writer: "Jacob Jesus Escape"
slug: "out-of-sight"
source: "AllPoetry"

not lacking all vision just
not here
it seems

not wanting to leave the party
just having to save some dough

not wanting to reign dear
not wanting to rain down
but kneeding to get wet
rest and pummeled bout

and when my head is baked i may return with sandwiches for all
