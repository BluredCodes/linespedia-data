fools run the table running 
at the mouth always running 
at the mouth only tabling 
it while they beg their forgiveness
less their keeping words
in their fruitful teeth
while the eyebrows lift 
and scowls form like reefs round hurt
sick of this cruel form
spitting hairs like drunks in bars
calling steps like cats
here steppie steppie
come to my feet like the fool
i cant imagine

being
