\|_


listen to red brick
check dam weight corrals the spring
dead architecture
trees grow over and you bulge
last walls face nerves cracking

pages in our book
mentions of names by structure
bulldozed pictures turn
corners and heads become one
under the cat s track cover

a paper burned down
faces of children all lashed
an old man beat you
one translation now worthless
bitter brick pills for the world
