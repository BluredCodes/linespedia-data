HER FACE IS EVERYWHERE DEATH LIVES

a beautys faraway look draws us in a mysterious way
we follow her life and slowly become
accustomed

her face is gone 
we search others looking for her eyes
the deep hole
she left
she waits

it is the sin to wait
belief awaiting execution
all are waiting at the beginning
all are present in the end
she waits
in mysterious ways94,
