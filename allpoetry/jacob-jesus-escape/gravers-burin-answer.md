the puzzle
oft this trudging challenge
sometimes dancing

never write but often
left 
all 
spinning to die 

for
never right
but left 
there spun 

with graven image
hopes
a false godding
gone a trick or treating

and i got a brown rock
the doctor called
a coprolite
yessss
