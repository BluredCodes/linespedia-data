keep that heart ringing 
with the power to wave away 
 
rock 

with the tide 
sharks fear tearing 
jigged by 
your metal blood lust 

match the sweet rhythm of sea 
and know that none match 
her subtle makeup 

close 

snuggle close to smiling days 
until they crack forgivenesspaper wins...
