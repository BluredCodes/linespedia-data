front
moving
in front
moving in
front moving 

in front moving in 
not there to see it
i m not there

cold front passed 
used
to relish cold 
but i m not
there

dry inside blind dry
cold front
moving in blind
shaking 
insides dry
so i m not there

to see it 
shaking blind so cold
not there so 
dry so
front cold dry so
dry inside

black ice hole 
icy front 
moves in while i m
splitting winter bone eyes dryan empty page blows
