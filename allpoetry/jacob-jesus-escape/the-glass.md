title: "the glass"

rests 
on varnished wood
best
reflections 
on a bar spinning as slowly 
as i can 
spin it
watching 
in its little panes
hot stories 
pressing near
and those treasured legs
twining down 
inside the rim
little rivulets of burning sweetness
that curl 
around my ears
and down my happy lids
to up my smile
i raise you
to the tiniest events
and call for an encore
in hopes of reaching 
another reckless taste
