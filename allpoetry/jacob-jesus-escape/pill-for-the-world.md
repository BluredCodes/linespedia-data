..........()..........

i just made a pill for the world
it s for you my friend for you
it is not very pretty
and surely not too sweet
so you should not have chewed
as it s so bitter to the teeth

i just made a pill for the world
shall you take it or should i
i will in my whiskey
swallow with your rye

there the child i mentioned last
time darkening our doors
down with colliseum dogs 
all picking at the sores

the just make a pill for the world
for you my friend for you
hard and gritty
hardly for the meek
but all of us must swallow
when our kitchens call to eat

your mother and your father
and everything they do
just made a pill for our world 
it s for the child it s you

i just made a pill for the world
it s for you my friend for you
just made a pill for the world
the least that i could
it s the least that i could do
i just made a pill for the world
it s for you my friend for you

...)(...the whole world is two lines
sometimes
and one sick little melody
that s so much fun to sing
it just kills me
