ranks a labor high 
lest asps post scripts to damage 
notions of talent 
that must reign supreme 
in damaged and fragile breasts 
like music popping 
mountain to valley 
for all denominations 
cheapening the bill 
nailed to hollow doors 
like screen windows opening 
to sell pain killers 
as i type this note 
to self a grand eyes holder 
buying my own linesi pulled this
now put it back
as it began here
