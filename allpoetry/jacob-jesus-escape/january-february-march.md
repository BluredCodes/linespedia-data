have you noticed the bird
song changed
the light at the end of the day
have you seen the geese seeking mates
and the willows sucking the light
water greening their whips

and have you survived 
the dark
to find joy
in hating the spring s mad mud

or wilted
have you taken the white hand
to sleep with niggling snow
so that you ll take 
the chance
never to drift pure
as your shining crystal 
to this green earth again

where blood burns
sucks trees down
birds and all
and winter 
returns
year after year
until there is only winter

is there only winter for you now

the blood is fire
to make more
of stars
that take to task
this cold we praise and fear

is there only winter for you now
