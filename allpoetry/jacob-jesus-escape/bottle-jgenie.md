its a gift
to give

it seems you 
invest generous amounts 
of time in pleasing trying lifting 

remember this is me talking...from outside of your world...i may be way off
base
 
i would never feel free to tell 
you anything if i didn't believe:
you could take it
you could derive some tiny benefit from it, 
if only in deciding 
whether it's real or beneficial
i didn't see you as on your way up
i didn't imagine you could so easily fall down

remember...me talking...from outside...world...way off
base

sometimes strangers say strange things
especially when they talk off of top of heads, like this...
and it's hard to read what we really think

and never say curious AROUND ME
all LIVING for curiosity

and perhaps that is why
i've written anything at all
about your giving nature
curious
this suspicious character
to be avoided
we all are

the most difficult thing
finding out 
if what we see is real

as there's that certain flash 
that hypnotizes these innocents
no matter how many puddles of grief
we've been dragged through
all ending up 
muddy

here's to wishing 
to operate more from a position of strength
and not from dark sides
but from change sparkling 
change that falls from our pockets
and looks so beautiful
gracing the ground
reflecting from that fresh 
street rain
so shiny
that we leave it lay
and become lay people
like everyone
but with joy to spare
and freer words

so see what you just gave
what we now have
free words...
