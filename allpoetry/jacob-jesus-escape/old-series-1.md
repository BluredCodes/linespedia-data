i gasp every time
i see those big feet slapping
across the floor boards

those dirty black shoes
with those big toes showing through
man they must be cold

they make you want heat
to see the house burn to ash
to watch the boards fall

toes smashed all around
burnt to the fucking bone and
broke dripping marrow
