capitalists...

can that fat laughter
like little fish from grade school
dead and together
like a puzzle that falls apart when you take it apart

believe the fueled talk
which bounces and sticks 
coming into your eyes light
dark as fucking shit and as likely to save you

buy it all from sam
man of every land
who glows in the rape of song
living off the band
