beaten by care s weight settling 
into the only place to sleep tonight
trying not to turn in bed to these arguments
i fail in my mind
but night s magic comes
again says never again and i must believe
though dice roll the same
we ll make a new game just because we can
and i will return
and sometimes because of this will know that i can
as i rest given to this decision to please
the gods of stasis
assured by my strength
to fly with my sweet children through the roiling air
a river of doubt
drifting by bright shore s passion 
to fertile deltas
