i choose words
to pink the rat s ass 
i could give to you

the plain est love beaucoup
a more plain than fiction
 hat jumps like a frog mouthed

thang of byewitty
shifting out of blue
like an old movie

and that s how they get
you t buy the foolssship
sunk out to see

in triplicate soiled to speak
jane english stilted like cheese
and brought forth plain like allergies

plopped down in poopy tub
with the dawngonebarkinin the background
ricochetaskewingoffthewalldingponds

while weir away yinside
catapoultring our fetturd nastic
kicklint pack ant enjoindring teasevite
