under a foot and over a crab and back and forth again 
plenty of air if i could breathe and water if i could swim 
plenty of sun if i could see and clashing noise to hear 
rock on rock and sand on kelp we all go far and near 
under a shell and over a ray and through a shrimp i move 
back and forth and up and down all falling into grooves 
spit upon this breathing pavement at one highest tide 
i lose my color for a week beach warms and so i m dried 
a rain comes and a rivulet and that high tide again 
and all combine to send me into thrashing seas to spin 
to grind in the excitement of a zillion atoms grand 
one zillion to the zillionth welcomes one more to the sand 
under the moon and over the earth and through an ocean blue 
and left upon a mountain top a puzzle and a clue
