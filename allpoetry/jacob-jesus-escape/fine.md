no time to say
what is left is behind
no time to pay for reason or rhyme
just my old long spiraling staircase to climb
and this same dull promise that i truly am lying
"LIFE is FINE...FINE as WINE...LIFE is fine."a simple greeting to this promise of a busy day
and a few words from Langston Hughes
