what secrets have you
how many sent how many
secrets to marble
rectangle numbers 
protecting kits of foxes
cooped up with chickens saving paper shreds
that spill in case of fires feeding our lit end
while we run like clowns from your fertile brew lifting 
beans to grow counters on counters on growth
like human candy
falling from mother magic 
golden pot boiling 
melting green to wax
poetic in there where the forest turns farm 
hands to the glory that renders buildings
as meaningless as our flesh
the greatest secret
standard of living cancers opportunity in canceling want
careening wildly
like that bull in the market
as concrete as dreams that play every night
remedial broth a la who 
the freak ever cajoles 
that current pact with the new lottery souls
to eat and laugh souls to eat and laugh
at chickens in every pot except tomorrows

we can feed the world has the greatest ring 
of troops queued with empty plates and ready to fight
with the shining vision lite 
miss spelling their way
in that hated face that gripes in its gripping force
and litigious shows that arrest us all after weve won 
our millions with dreams of glory
and buckets of cream to wash away 
our sorrow that we cannot know from 
ignorant bliss fed by public waves 
goose stepping across 
our screens mothers fairy tales screeching 
like chalk boards warning of impending doom
more noise for the breed
pony up children
like old sepia photos
really black and white 
baby 
booming gold on the unredeemable paper of cages
forged in our plastic acceptance of childrens debt 
ever so secret 
so stinking secret because its not on the news every stinking day
this must just plain 
end 
with a big chicken pecking 
at the grit of worldssorry
but did anyone see this episode
