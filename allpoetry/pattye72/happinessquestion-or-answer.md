How do you know what is the right thing to do?How can you be ssurethat your heart is not lying to you?If you follow your heart will it always lead you to the happiness that you deserve?How do you know when to put your happiness in front of everything else?What if making your self happy upsets everybody else.Who is right better yet who has the right to tell you what is the right thing to do?What if you can't find the answers to the questions that you keep asking yourself?Is there any proof that happiness is the answer? What if happiness is the question;Then who has the answer?
