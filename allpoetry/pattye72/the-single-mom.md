The hardest job today is the one people say without pay; the single mom.The most rewarding job today is the one that everyone is afraid to say; the single mom.I didn't choose this life,but I wouldn't change it in any way.The kids are crying,the bills are all over due,I'm feeling sad and lonely and blue.As I look into their eyes I remember what this is all for.Their smiles are my pay,they are worth more than any words could say.Without them there would be no other way,no happiness only sorrow everyday.
