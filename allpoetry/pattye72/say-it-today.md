As the night grows dark and cars start to fade,
Everybody is going home to rest up for another day.
But what if that day never comes?
Are you happy with everything that you have done?
Is there something left unsaid?
Be sure to tell it,
for your words cannot be heard if you are dead.
Yes, you think there will always be another day.
But sooner or later everybody has to go away.
Don't leave the words unsaid for another day
Because the words you meant to say
may never be heard if they are not said today.
