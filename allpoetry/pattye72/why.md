She put a pistol to her head;pulled the trigger BANG she was dead.
She thought that was the only way to end the pain and suffering she felt everyday.
She may have ended her pain and the suffering too;But what are we left here supposed to do?
We didn't even get to say good-bye;all we can do is sit and wonder 'why'.
Didn't she give us any sign?
A sign to let us know that she was ready to end her life today.
I wish that I could have seen the sign so that maybe she could have lived to see another day.
Now I'm at a loss for words to say,this has been a sad sad day.
