title: "Bomb Threat"
writer: "MaryHall"

I am here
You are there
You are away from me
I want something to hope for 
I want something to long for

But leave me here
Just leave me here
I want my face again
I want my life again
you have a life so rich
I want my life again

You have joy 
And you have prosperity
You are away from me
You are away from me
But i see you there

leave me here!
Just leave me here...
