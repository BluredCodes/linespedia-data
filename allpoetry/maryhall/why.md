I woke up to a bad dream last night
I was at the throne of judgment,
with the ones I knew in life 
They stood before me 
I hid behind God
They were judged according 
to what they had done

As they turned away,
They looked and said to me
Why didn't you tell me?
Why didn't you tell me?
