I've had alot of time
to sit and think lately
About where I am today
and where I thought I'd be by now
It's a different place
Entirely
And when we are so young
things look so easy
And we don't have the time to doubt
'Cause we're dreaming
all the time

Look around this room 
and see the ones we love
They've changed alot 
since we saw them first
'Cause we're slaves to time
and it changes us
We all go down this road
and go our seperate ways
Finding other ones
and dreaming different days
to be one 
with the others we find along the way

Oh,cousin it's your turn now
You're changing but don't forget how
you got to this place in life
You've always been precious in his sight
This marrage,a sweet thing
Blessed by God from the beginning
You'll find life has so much more
It's all part of what we're made for

Take the two of them
and turn them into one 
A mystery of God
not to be undone
He hates divorce
Love each other wonderfully 
Don't get torn apart
by this crazy world 
and all its crazy things
Be strong,and keep God in front of you

Our mothers are crying
Our fathers are trying to let go
and let us walk the road of our own lives
And give you your wings to fly away
It's hard to watch all these changes
It reminds us all of our ages 
It shows us the goodness of Christ
It's now your life story to paradise
...to paradisededicated to my cousin,Laura,whose getting married.
