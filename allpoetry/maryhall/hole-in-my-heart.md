title: "Hole in my Heart"

Lying here on a winter night
Staring out my window.
Thinking of the time I spent with you yesterday.

Feeling hollow, I feel the hole grow.
Deeper in my heart.
Wishing there could be a way to tell you how I feel.

Fill this aching emptiness with someone made for me.
Never leaving, never changing, always by my side.
Chase me till I laugh, scare me till I smile, kiss me till I cry.
Keep me from wasting my life away and give me strength to try.

It's crying outside.
I'm raining inside.
Tears roll down my window.
Another smiling face, 
another way to raise the aching in my heart
A war is raging, my body's aging.
I am going to get old.
Help me to lift my head up, find the right one
And stay forever young,
Forever young........
