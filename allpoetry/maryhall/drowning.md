---
title: "Drowning"
writer: "MaryHall"
slug: "drowning"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Last night I was in a place 
where evil had control
A stronghold of darkness 
and the devils own
Death and depression,
filth and sadness
These are the things we hide inside

I can be found once again,
in a realm of my transgressions
Drowning in this lie,
that I have to stay here

Living here,in this world...
I'm drowning here

We stepped out of my car,
me and my mother
and the place outside was dark
and someone screamed
If it's my money,go and spend
If it's my life,then let it end
I would give you all the keys to my heart

We are seeing now a world 
that we have created
Not just in my mind,
but all around,the real world

Someday I will be free
Someday I'll find true love 
Someday I'll live inside
Someday in heaven...
