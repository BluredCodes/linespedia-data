Why you chasing me?
I've never been kissed
I'm running away
Please don't be alarmed
Don't call me gay
I'm looking away
just wait 'till the day
I'm NBK

Keep those lips away
I don't wanna be kissed today
I'm feeling ok
Just don't be disturbed
You'll never get away with this
Oh,just wait 'till the day
I'm NBK

I dont wanna lock lips with you!?
