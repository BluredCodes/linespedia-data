---
title: "Shine Through"
writer: "MaryHall"
slug: "shine-through"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Lots of family came by,
from far away
To see each other now,
after all this time
Not a tear comes to my eye,
that they have gone home
They all just waved goodbye,
and fell by the wayside

Even though we're related
How come I don't see much of you
Even though we're created
The image of God just don't shine through

A friend called yesterday,
when I wasn't home 
He hasn't shone his face,
for almost a year now
He's lost within his pain,
and troubled mind
I don't know what to say,
anyway
 
Even though we're related 
How come I don't see much of you
Even though we're created
The image of God just don't shine through

We're so divided 
That the light we cannot see
It's so unheard of 
The divorce of Gods own family

Shine through
