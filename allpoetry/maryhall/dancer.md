She has a white dress
But there's blackness all around
She's dancing
In sadness
See the mark of shame on her form
but she's dancing 
In a light from above 
we don't belong here...
Every single day here
We oughta know
We don't have our faces here
we oughta know
as long as there's sorrow,
we can see whats wrong clearly
As long as there's pain... 
We know we're far from home
As long as there's pain...
