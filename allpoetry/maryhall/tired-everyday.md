Each day now
It's getting closer
Not much time untill I have to leave
Explain how
I'll notice
If I'm ready to take this
on my own

Getting tired of the choices 
that I have to make
Getting tired of all the faces 
I see anyway
Tired everyday
I'm tired everyday

Let's move on
We've been here long enough
Look at all the girls
I'll slip away
I'm fine now
That I'm dying
And still the faceless children have no names 

Getting tired of the stiffnecks
Getting in the way
Getting tired of the living,
with my heart in the hands of the dying,
Tired everyday
So tired everyday
