This place is dark
This place is cold
I know we don't belong here
'Cause I've got this aching in my soul

When will you come to save us
When will you come to take us home 
When is our work completed
When will you take vengeance for our souls

This world is lost
This world is angry
This is a place of demons
Broken dreams and promises

We watch our children
Slip through our fingers into the world
and begin this hateful cycle
of broken dreams and promises

Torn away with tears in their eyes
Feeling innocence pull away
is such a painful feeling 
and leaves such a deep longing inside

When will you come to save us
When will you come to take us home 
When is our work completed
When will you take vengeance for my soul

We know there is a better world,
when we feel your sunshine...
When we feel your sunshine
