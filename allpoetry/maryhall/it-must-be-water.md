---
title: "It Must Be Water"
writer: "MaryHall"
slug: "it-must-be-water"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/24779-It-Must-Be-Water-by-MaryHall"
---

I have a place on frozen water
I go and live there now and again
The place is dark and the life is harder
but sometimes i don't mind

Will I ever see the light of day again,
or feel the hope?

A foundation built upon the frozen pieces of my heart
The conversations there are with myself but not my self alone
Sometimes I hear the voice of loved ones calling me back from the dead
But i don't feel they're desperation

And I see everytime I'm here alone,
I have no hope

Then I wake up,
and let the light come in
It melts the pieces of my heart,
so I can live again
I see that my heart
is broken when it's frozen,
It must be water to be free
It must be water...
