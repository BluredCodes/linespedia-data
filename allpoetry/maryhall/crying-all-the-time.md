---
title: "Crying All The Time"
writer: "MaryHall"
slug: "crying-all-the-time"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/24730-Crying-All-The-Time-by-MaryHall"
---

The people next door, 
fight all the time
I live in a small place,
and cannot hide
I miss my friends,and my family,
but not for long
There must be something 
I'm doing wrong

'Cause I'm crying all the time

Every five minutes or so,
a jet flies low overhead
It shudders my building,
and the thoughts inside my head
Is there a god so great he can understand,
my human pain?
and if there is,
I wanna know him

'Cause I'm crying all the time
