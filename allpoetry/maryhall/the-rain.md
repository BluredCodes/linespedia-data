Raining out
I drove home in the dark rain
With a dark haze on my brain
Driving in dark rain,hard to see
Someone swerved in front of me
I heard a sound,a gasp
my last scream

And the rain comes down 
And no one is near
No one's around 
And I'm bleeding there 
on the ground 
and the rain
the rain comes down

Raining harder now
I try to breathe but I forget how
No eyes to see what's happened to me
The taste of blood 
The gurgling in my ribcage
What was that they said about God?

And the rain,it fell
I'm too young 
I'm too young to go to hell
And I'm bleeding there on the ground 
And the rain  
the rain comes down

And the rain,it fell
I'm too young 
I'm too young to go to hell
Who is God?
Someone tried to tell me,but oh well
Didn't wanna think about it
No time to think about it
As the rain came down
