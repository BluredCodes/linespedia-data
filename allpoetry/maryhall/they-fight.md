Mom and Dad are fighting
Mom and Dad have fought all my life 
I hate to hear them cussing
I hate the sound of all they're screaming
Say whatever you want,it will not quench your fire
Don't try to understand
Don't try to fix it at all

Little sister's listening
Little sister's watching TV
Pretending not to hear them 
Not seeming bothered at all
It's the only home that she's known 
Just a child but she knows 
If she tried to stop they're war
She'd pay the price for trying

This place of love has turned so cold 
They're sceaming,they're fighting,I hate it
Oh God I can't stand it.

Now Mom is crying
All the kids are hiding away
Dad is always yelling
He says he don't wanna live anymore
I see no love in they're hearts,
but there's nothing I can do
And it will happen again
and I'll just have to sit through
