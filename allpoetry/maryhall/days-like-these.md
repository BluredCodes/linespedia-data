It's days like these 
That make me feel alive
It's days like these 
That remind me of my life

The sun is coming out
The sky is shining blue
The trees are streching out 
just like they're reaching out for you

I remember the time when 
We were driving south west
Filled my car up with high test
so it would run its best
Rolled all the windows down 
for the first warm wind spring
its such a sense of freedom 
that it makes me want to scream

New life is budding now
There's a sweetness in the air 
It feels like we're free now 
Just like we have no cares...

...and it reminds me of my life

The cold is fading out 
like the sadness and the pain
The healings coming down 
pumping love back through my veins
The wind is getting loud 
with the sun that fills the air 
Creation's shouting out
It's how I've known your there...

...and it reminds me of my life
