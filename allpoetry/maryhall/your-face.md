---
title: "Your Face"
writer: "MaryHall"

Are you shining,ever?
When the sun is bright outside
And if you could,would you never,
shine your face on me
Things get hot and things get cold
We work an awful lot these days
Not much has changed since you've been here
They've just gotten worse
Do you ever think of me?
Do you remember me?
Or is this just such a shame to remember
Would you cry for me,
in your splendor?
Would you pray for me,
if you remember?
Is it hard to see?
Is it hard to remember...
Are you shining ever?
