Passions Grip

Stopping time
Yes, every clock

To your chest

Makes me my best

And soon devour
Saying all

God I want you

Just can't take

Flip you over

Slowly now

Of passions grip

In perfect bliss

Tighter and tighter

That go untold

Inner grip begin
