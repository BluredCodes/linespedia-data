My Thoughts Of You

My soul crys out for your's
My fingers cant stop tracing
The line of your jaw and running through your hair
My arms wrap around you no matter  what
As if they have a mind of ther own
My lips long for the moisture only you can satisfiy
My neak.... crave's the bites
That only you know where to place
My back  goes insane with sweet torture with
Every drop of sweat that falls upon it and the feel
of your chest rubbing against it
My Mind lives by the connection we share
My heart continues to beat because of our love
My days pass with a smile on my face because of
thoughts of you and the life and love that we share

                By
           Connie McDonald
