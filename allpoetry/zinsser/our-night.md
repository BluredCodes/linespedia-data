Our Night

In the room 
nothing but a bed
not an uncomfortable moment
not a word need be said

The shower turns on 
and what starts out as play
turns to passion
and time fades away

The candle lit room 
reflects the water in your hair
as you lower me to the bed
our bodies both bare

Slowly your lips seek
the moisture of mine
soft but demanding
never stopping the whole time

 As if for the first time
Our hands explore
every inch of the other
Renewing our love
and devotion for each other

With labored breathing
I beg you inside me, My Lord
To be seprate from you
I can't take anymore

I can smell our bodies 
 all covered with swet 
as you rub it all over us
 Mmmm Im so wet

Sighs and Groans
and mummers of love
connection so complete
as one from above

Over and over
Holding so tight
waves of passion shake us
All through the night

We feed each other
cookies, snack crackers and cheese
Our lips seek each others
Lots of lost time to please

Twice more Our souls merge 
with pure love, not lust
Holding each other close
till dawns early dusk
