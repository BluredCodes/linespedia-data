Loves Haze

So much spoken with out any speech
Laying together between the sheets
Eye to eye and lip to lip
Warm and spent from our recent dip

Moon beam streak across your face
Eyes in the shadows but still a trace
Of the sparkle shining through the dark
Night sky cant even dim your spark

Love for you a never ending river
Running through my viens, legs still a quiver
Don't ever want to brake our gaze
Live forever in loves haze
