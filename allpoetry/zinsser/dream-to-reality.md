Dream to Reality


As I sleep you come to me
The dream seems real when you I see
I know you yet, who are you?
I can smell you and feel your touch
This all confuses me oh so much
For your more real to me than the day
I know I must be going crazed
I feel that you are here to stay
Past lives and preasent days
Or nights I guess is the word I should say
But must I live only in my dreams?
Or will you appear to me in the real
as I feel we need
One moon lit night while driving home
Quite awake and quite alone
I felt your presence in the air
All around me, I could smell your hair
You came inside with every breath
I could feel your cloak brush my neak
Tho my eyes could still not see
I knew you were right here with me
When home in my room I took extra care
Special gown, brush strokes through my hair
And as I turned there you stood
As I knew someday you would
The only love in your eyes
Was all for me, soly mine
I let my gown drop off one sholder
No fear there at all, just love grown bolder
As you neared you warned, and told me not
But I had already given this much thought
With all our pent up loving care
I shared your blood , you shared mine there
Passions pain shared in the night
Guarded by the Moon light
Moon light such the earth has never seen
Each beam almost bowing to it's knee
Knowing this pair would Reign in it's light 
For all eternity
