Its Still You

For you, so you will understand
where my love comes from 
reasons I want you to be my man

My first thought in the morning , you
Wondering what you see behind your eyes so blue
Wanting to be your breath, in and out
Wrapping around your vocal cords as sigh or shout

My mind wants to share every thought
Ears long to listen when you talk
Eyes take in the whole of you
My lips just have one thing they want to do

I love your imagination
Your my fascination
Your intellect is never ending
Never know whats next, whats pending

My body wants you to open all my doors
wrapped up in knots with yours
no space for light between, just with-in
in life and love in a constant begin

You draw me out when I'm mad
You make me laugh like no one ever has
No one could ever hurt me but you
After all these years, Its still you
