Twirling Trance

Come dance with me,
Let me lead tonight
Fear not my trance please
Twirling in tune,
I give glimpse of the real me
In rhythm with the moon
But come daybreak,
Depart from you too soon
Heart ache I cannot take
For yours to give I'll not wait
The odds against are high in stakes
Dance with me the moon awaits

My hand stokes your cheek
as I turn and walk away
Memories best instead of loveless end,
I already regret this day
