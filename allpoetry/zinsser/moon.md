Moon


Let me stretch before you
Not in worship
But in splendor
At the beauty you Bestow
In your ever changing moods
Do you see how
All the lights of heaven
Sparkle in delight
at your majestic presence
Oh Moon,
An ever present comfort 
to my soul


Connie McDonald
