Oceans Edge



Take me to the oceans edge
where salty air 
cleans my head
where thunderouse noise
of tide and wave
calls to me 
when having a bad day
To sit and look
toes playing in the sand
I can feel and see
the real of Gods hand
and believe again
in this to shall pass
for love in life
is what shall last


By
