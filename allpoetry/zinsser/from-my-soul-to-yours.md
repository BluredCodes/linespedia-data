From My Soul To Your's

    My Love,
 My soul cries out for you
 for your very essence;
 But I would never assume
 that you feel the same.

 Nor would I allow
 our union to continue,
 If I felt in my heart 
 or saw in your eyes
 that my being, my intellect,
 left you wanting.

I speak thease words 
to you because our love,
has been for many moons.
In this time we have experienced
all emotions from pure ecstacy,
to pending doom.

In my life and love for you,
I have never ment to hinder
or prevent,
your soul from pooring forth
all that it was ment.

My love should not overburden
or encumber;
but should arouse, invigorate
and enhance.
Making you feel whole and restored.

If you ever choose to stay
out of duty or obligation;
That would hurt so bad
there would be no justification.

So I'm here
vulnerable and exposed;
with everything out in the open
no doors closed.

With down cast eyes
and bated breath
waiting for an answer 
of whats to come next

Will I see your back 
walking away,
never to share another day.

Or will your hand 
reach out for mine
to reaffirm our love
a love that will surpass
all time.
