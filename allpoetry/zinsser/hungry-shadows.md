Hungry Shadows



Driven by the need
Nothing personal, just feed
Blood lust is a must
Continue for centuries
The shadows of the night
Seem to bring unfounded fright
But those who can see
Lock doors at night, will not fight

Black hearted hungry shadows

Connie McDonald
