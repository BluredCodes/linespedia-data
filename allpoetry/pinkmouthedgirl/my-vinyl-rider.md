title: "My Vinyl Rider"

Eyes afraid of the sun
Shy from the truth
Try to create
Order in my head

Full of love
Like an overstuffed cushion
But don't know 
How to control it

He has the order
He knows 
The way of things
He knows his own mind
And often knows mine

Sometimes detects lies
But is sometimes too much
The battering ram.
The cross examiner
Of the cultural misfit
