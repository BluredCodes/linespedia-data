title: "I'm The Ugly One"

Lines, more lines
And the frame drips red
Then she gets me up

Cut and dried
Says a kindly face
Want to put you back to bed

Her body aches 
From the hole inside
Jagged edge 
Of the missing jigsaw piece
She once knew
That the bones stick up
Now you took her place 
I'm the ugly one.

Safe and loud
Calm the frightened one
Keep your feelings down

Paint your face
Build the camouflage
Keep the scavengers at bay.
