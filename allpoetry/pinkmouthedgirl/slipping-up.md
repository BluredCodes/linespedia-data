Whatever she does
She can never defend it
Maybe she loses her mind.
An instant- 
It's gone

Then back again 
And kicking herself.

"Why do I hurt him?"
You're asking the wrong person
You'll never dig out the answer

Give up
Just hope this won't 
Flash before your eyes
When the explosion comes
Hope to forget.
