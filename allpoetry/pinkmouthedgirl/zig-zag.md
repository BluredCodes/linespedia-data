Is it time?
I think I'd better
Find a window
Open my eyes
Pull the stripes out.

It's time
To change the colours
Bang the T.V.
See if that works
Turn the lights down

She's always what the subject says
She hates to say it
But she knows it's true
There's always something in her eye
And she's everything.
