What are you afraid of?She smiled, all animal teeth.Brushing sand from hisArm he squirmed beneathHer icy touch, electricShocks lit up his bonesAnd her adulterousnessFelt superior and alone.Over in an instant As he pulls off into the dark.She closed her wings nowTrailing blood, the sharkOf the back streetsDisguised with that mask of girl.Lips the colour of heartbreak,Features a-whirlThis is my first rhyming piece here so what do you think?
