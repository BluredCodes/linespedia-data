Star
on the top of the tree.
It fell down.
Smashed
to blonde smithereens.
Ring-a-ring-o roses
wrapped around her wrist.

Fairy lights
weren't bright 
enough
        for her.

The little boy 
didn't steal her.
But I still won't put her back 
                              on the tree.Is it obvious what this is about?
What do you think?
