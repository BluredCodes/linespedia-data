Idle stars
Spatter the sheet
Where do they come from.
They're in every 
Fibre of her being
But does she know
That's what she's made of?

Pretty but far away
Bright
And yet dangerous
The yellow gas fills her lungs
And she allows it
Blowing it out 
At her chosen one
