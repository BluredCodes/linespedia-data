I hear your giggles
in the background
as I speak
and I remember.

You were an early bird
and too small 
to believe.
The eyes were too large.

I used to think
you would break.
I'm sure they shared my fears
but you're fine.

He was talking to you,
my clown-daddy.
I heard you reply.
You've come so far.
