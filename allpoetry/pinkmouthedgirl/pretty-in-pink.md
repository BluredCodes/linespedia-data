The plastic fan screams,spinning on a starstruck high.She wore her favorite underwearand hopes it'll get her by.Kenny's girl is dressed in pinkshowing her sickly-smooth skin.She's glad she doesn't need to thinkshe's rejected all but one figure.Marvel at the inflatable girlwho'll appear on her own.No need for a foot-pump,though you'll still feel alone.She's got the dreaded diseasein her plastic heart.She'd rather die pleaseSo leave them where they are.
