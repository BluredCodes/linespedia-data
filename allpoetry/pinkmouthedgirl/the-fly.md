I crawl into spaces
that no human would.
I don't see traps,
always find myself stuck.

Please don't 
take advantage:
My brain is too small.

The blood spills
over my face.
I don't understand
why can't I escape.
I keep hitting my head.What levels of meaning if any do you get from this- be brace and honest!
