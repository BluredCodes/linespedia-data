Brother faithfulfollows me roundsets off alarm bellsand walks blindly through.Brother bloodhoundhas captured my scent.The fear in my eyescalls out to him.Brother roadblockenters my space,he's codedas if he's a product.Brother Stanleywon't fly away.His air sickens me.Does his community care?Brother parasiteburns my backwith his black eyes bombs.I want to go home.Brother Madness.Is it his fault?I'm sure it's not mine but why is he there?
