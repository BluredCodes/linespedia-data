Small
Cold
And White
Covered in grease

She waits to leave
Since her arrival
The white world.
Silver
Blue
Frozen?
But no life.

Ice hairs
Stick up like spines
The stickleback
Music keeps her alive
And yet dulls
Her brain
She wonders
If this is hell.Do you get colour from this?
