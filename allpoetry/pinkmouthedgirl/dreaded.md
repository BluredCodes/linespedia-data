Her disembodied song
Just shows she's trying
To drown us out.

'It's not so bad.'

Well then why won't you just shut up?

I shed some more skin 
Last night
Emerging anew
From my chrysalis
But who painted my wings?
Orange is not my colour.

Broomstick girl
Nurses a split lip.
White unruly hair
And blue button eyes:
My stomach flutters 
With Her feelings.

All I ever do is imagine
In X rated thoughts
What I'd like to do
Hold.
Touch.
Kiss.

'I only wanted to warm you up.'
