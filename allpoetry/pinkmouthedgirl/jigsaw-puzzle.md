Ring of skinwhy do you stare?Should I contractretractand fade?What do you think of last night?'Did I go overboard?'S'pose I was more drunkthan I thought.Blue tongues tell too many lies.Day to DayGoing insaneThinking of her midriff.'But I don't like her like that.'Waking up with injuries:cracked my skeletonon the door.'And it wouldn't even bruise.'
