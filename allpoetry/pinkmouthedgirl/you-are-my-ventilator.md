I'm ready for you
Now.
Perpetually
Waiting

Always going mad
With emptiness.
Hollowed out bark
Though the tree looks fine

She is frozen
Nobody knows she's dead
Until
The lightning strikes 
And she turns to
The dust she is inside.

This dream fades in and out
As the sphere continues
The struggle
For existence
