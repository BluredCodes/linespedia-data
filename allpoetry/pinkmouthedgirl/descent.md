We're back here again

In the land

Oppressed by silence.

He rules.

He

Could never be a she.

He descends.



Wings of bubble wrap

Bear him to us

He tightens his grip

We can hear the hum of him.



Almost a whistle

If we listen too hard

He has a vicious roar

But it cannot be heard



He is a distant cry

But is anyone there to verify

He is the enemy of peace

Because he is too self aware

He is the acknowledgement 

Of self



And of nothingness

He is the downside

He is the prison

He is the end



He is silence

He is the void

He is too many thoughts

He is self analysis

He is the inner army



He turns me against me

He is the barrier between me and sleep

He is the keyring

She clings to

To keep me away

To keep me staring into the void

Trying to find her



He is the blur

He is....
