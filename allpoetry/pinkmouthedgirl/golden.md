Cloudy moments
I seek
your voice.

Dragging you
from innocent
animal
chasing dreams.
Awaken
your twitching legs.

Piteously whining
as you try to be reasonable
to selfishness.

Success?

This is episodic
so anything might happen.

Changing one word
alters entire
plot lines.

Close my mouth.
Breathe the silence.
"Don't be afraid."
