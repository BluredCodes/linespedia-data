She enterslike a scream.He hopedfor a saviourbutshe was on him,a heatrash.But she's not touching.Nevertouching.Achingly aloof.Until tongue unhingesFlapping free in the breeze.She laughsat the truthhe relates.'Liar.'But she loves it.
