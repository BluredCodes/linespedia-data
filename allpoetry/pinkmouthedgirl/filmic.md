Reality bitesdown hardshaking her out of a sleepy state.'What were you doing?''Did you ever thinkabout anything?''How strong do you think you are?'You teetered on the edgeof the whitefor so longbut he never knew.He alwayshung downawayfrom your grasp.Reach out your hand Touch hisand send him life.Instant,but fleeting fast.Soon it's only a whisperin his ear.Or a scent he can't place.Stop blaming yourself.
