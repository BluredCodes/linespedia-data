Girl don't come
she says.
Is she pleading?
He always wants me to come,
he asks
again.

I saw him upside down
with my eyes closed
and my mouth open
like a newborn.

He said it 
was just for fun
he always says that.
Would it be wrong to want me?

"Who do you love?"
Is that a trick?
But he won't stay.

Purple saps his strength
as if I'd cut his hair 
and gone.
