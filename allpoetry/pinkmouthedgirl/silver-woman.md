She shines
In a million eyes
Not tarnished
By tears.
Gifted
To live forever
Shivering 
In twilight.

Her eyes
Are spurs
Humming with every step
Signaling, warning.
A threat?
With no answer
She spins on.

She'll get in your head
Taking over
Repeat, Repeat
She's every form.
