The hum is deafening.
Bass notes
thud through soles
listening 
         to each other
suspiciously.

Workers 
buzz
through intellect channels:
intricate
like the cracks
               in a lived in face.

The honey is
retracted
in their modern selves.

Production
is no longer feasible.
The physical is archaic
now substitutes exist.
                             We all
                                  buzz
                           toward the light

                                sucked in.What do you think of the form?
