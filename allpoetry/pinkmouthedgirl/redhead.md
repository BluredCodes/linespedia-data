Call me out
In chocolate brown
Flush me down the drain
Forget me.

If I'd wanted that
I'd have opted 
For the wash out dye.

The red is
The someone else 
In me

A nice feeling
But like 
A fake ID

One day you'll cease 
To remember
And who will see

The truth
Shining through.
"Work with your own

Tones and highlights"
Simply chameleon change
Hoping for notice.I'd just like to know if you get a sense of colour about this and if you have a clue what I'm babbling on about?!
