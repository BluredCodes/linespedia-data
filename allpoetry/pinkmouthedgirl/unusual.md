I smelt her soap
and her toothpaste
and the inside changed.

Yet the outside struggled
to keep composure.
Did it succeed?
She screamed
didn't she.

She looked in a
fake mirror
and saw
FAT
and ugly.

But I can't relate
the truth.
"She'd hate me."

I've got to get up
I've got to get up

She says
"Don't wake me."

She probably suspects
what's there to suspect.

I'm fucking this up.
