I can see the air in here.I feel itprickle my arms.Has the coldspread to him yet?I hope not.I'd try any thing to keep warm.Perhaps I am cold blooded-reptilianwith my snake eyesand scaly feel.I want to be real again.I've forgottenhow it is.April 1999,
