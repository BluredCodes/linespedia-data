She brushed the dust 
from her face
and carried on.

The same as always?

Too different
to know where to begin.
Reasoning?

Explanations
won't rewind 
the news tape.
Won't breathe life
into lungs again.

She brushed the dust
from her streets,
her buildings,
her survivors.

Remembering her burns.
The lives etched into her walls
Permanently.
