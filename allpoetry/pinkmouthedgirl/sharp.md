Calling out
Sneak to sting.
Venomous,
Selective,
Kindly
Easing you into
Ear shattering
Mind fucking
Breakdown.

Familiar sounds.
Well,
in tune anyway.
She may 
Go down 
A different way...

Probably not
In this lifetime.This is basically a work in progress so feel free to pull it apart!
