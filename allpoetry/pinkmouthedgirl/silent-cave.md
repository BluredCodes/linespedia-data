After the dark squeezeShe lives again.UncontrolledsubstanceflashbacksWill bring her hereAgain.Always interruptingwhen she's happy.Or not.She can shake freebut never swimfar enough.She will neverescapethe throbbing in her head.Just a work in progress. Dissection please xxx
