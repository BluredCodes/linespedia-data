How do you shake off
These guilt inducers.
How to cool a red face.

My face tells me I'm wrong:
"It's not a good idea
Don't use those eyes
I KNOW WHAT YOU'RE THINKING."

Perhaps you'll always need
to restrain yourself.
Retrain yourself and learn 
from the mistakes of before.

Just take the time.

Deal with the masks and disguises
Smile for a scream
And don't curl up and die.

Think of the pips 
And not just the rotten apple.

Forward thinking
I'm glad that I'm not crystal
I'm glad that he's not telescopic.This just came out a few days ago. Does anyone else think like this?
