title: "On My Head"
writer: "pinkmouthedgirl"

I fell.
You caught me
before I hit the ground.
This is no love affair.
You simply want
to sell me on.

I'm not the real thing.
But they all say
I'm the spit.

It's always missing something
special.

Does it matter
as long as I smell nice?
I needn't cost the earth.

I don't
       have a label
                   on my head.Just basically interested in finding out what everyone gets from this.
Any ideas what I am?
