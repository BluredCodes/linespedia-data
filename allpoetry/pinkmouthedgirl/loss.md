Domino rallyshiversalong vertebraetouched by green palms.Aliento me.So wrong.Unreal.I wish.He leavesa stenchoozingtoxic chartreuse.Glowing in every lighteven witheyes clamped.No detergentmakes things right.I can't peel off my skinand exchange for new.If only.New coloursare blinding.Sanctifiedby deathof courage.LossOf unbeaten run.
