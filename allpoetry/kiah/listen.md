title: "Listen"
writer: "Kiah"

Just a little left of center.
A bit right of wrong.
Swirling in the blender.
Spinning right along.

Listen to my insanity.
I can see it in your vanity.
Pristine mutilations of glee.

Just a little left to enter
A bit more of this song.
Circling the empty sweater.
Stringing me right along.

Listen to my insanity.
I can see it in your vanity.
Pristine mutilation of me.

Just love to watch you hurt.
Love to watch me cry.
Just love your war torn shirt.
Love to watch it fly.
