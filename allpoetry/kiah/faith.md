Explanations don’t change things
Actions make things the way they are
Intentions can be strange things
Compared to the way things are

Perverted conversions are here at last
Fractions of a whole are only gathered pieces
The dirty versions that have come to pass
Now they yield this sum of what she is

Explain and justify
The distain in her lusty eyes
Confused and contort the truth
So I believe the words she uses

Dirty girl with pretty eyes
Tainted pearl in sweaty thighs
Stupid cool are her wiles
Lucid fool I am, for all her styles
