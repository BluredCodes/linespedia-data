---
title: "Did you, will you?"
writer: "Kiah"
slug: "did-you-will-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

Did I kill you?Or did you kill me?My dear daddy?Were you, you?Was I me?I'm not sure?Will you tell me?You will tell me.You still kill meThe needle, the night,the mornings red & blue light.Ina godda la vivid memories.The needle, the body I see.Will you tell me?Will you help me?Help me.Help me?Daddy died.Mommy lied.I cried.Did you tell me?Did you kill me?Will you heal me?No shield for me?I steal for me.I squeal for me.
