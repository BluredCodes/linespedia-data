title: "[Feeble and weak]"
writer: "Kiah"

Feeble and weak
Warm and safe
The security you seek
For your escape
People are creeps
Slumped in spit
Malignant sleep
In their own shit

I will house
Care about you
I will hold you
Give control to you

Feeble and weak
Plotted your own destiny
You’ve come to me
To hold you down
and set you free

I will rouse you
To follow you
I will mold you
So I can hollow you

Came to me of your own will
Empty hearted for me to fill
Shame and guilt for me to feel
Came to me of your own will

Feeble and weak
Sick and strong
Constantly seek 
To feel hurt
To be wrong
Play along 

Cynical sadness
Orchestrated madness
Imaginary agony 
For you to be 
Clinical psychosomatic
Self induced negative habit
Reactionary antagonist
You are not you
But you are all of this
