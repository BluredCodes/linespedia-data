Oh you fucking cunt,what did you want?My blood, brains & soul?Are they filling that ugly fucking hole?Is my torment and malice,worth all this evil you practice?Do you feel vindicated?When will you be satiated?When I am emasculated?Then will you be elated?I’ve strung my intestines,Round my intentions.You’ve seared me at every chance.And you question why my hate has enhanced.Now after all this time we’ve danced.You’ve actually got the nerve to ask for the umpth-teen chance.Fuck you CUNT, fuck you.I love our child,it’s too bad I’m stuck with you.Torment & toil will never wear away,this callous hide you’ve helped build everydayRather than screaming something to this effect in my daughters mother's face.  I did this.  It is EXTREMELY misogynistic.  Sorry about that, blame her.  I do.
