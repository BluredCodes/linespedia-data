title: "I'm done"
writer: "Kiah"

Fuck it, I'm done.I can see the sun.Lucked out & I won.I could feel the fun.Sucked into the pun.I should steal her gun.Striving,driving, smiling & lying.Crispy, crusty, rusty & mushy.Broke it & now I'm done.I may want the fun.Stroke misses on the pun.Hope for that simple one.Hope for fat crumpled $1s.Hoped for it & now I'm done.This contains vulgar language.  If that bothers you, please don't read it.
