God is a canker sore
always there
under your skin

Appearing when you don’t want it
making you feel
your borders
tingling
stinging

Your borders
Not God’s
Your borders
It’s not odd

God is a canker sore
Definite persistant presense
The appearant truth
distained by perception

Effect 
        IS
             CausalThink St. Thomas Aquinas

This is my twisted perversion of God proven.  Although, I feel I should note that this is only one side of a distorted equation.
