---
title: "I never"
writer: "Kiah"
slug: "i-never"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8613-I-never-by-Kiah"
---

All the things I never did.
All those places I sat & hid.
Those funny pictures,
awkward mixtures.
I never tread upon their trails.
Only heard once upon of their tales.

All those things I never gazed upon.
Happiest in the simplest parts of the sun.
Those tired moments,
now so poignant.
I never heard an, “I’m so proud.”
From my father ever spoke aloud.

All these things I never had.
All these times I spent so sad.
Torn bloody fixtures.
Blended in with yours.
I never wished for something more.
I never saw you come home once more.
