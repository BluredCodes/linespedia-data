---
title: "Translucence"
writer: "Kiah"

I’m so arrogant
It’s so apparent
That the soul is transparent

I’m so conceded
My love is fleeting
The hole is only deepening

I’m so fucking alive
Into this abyss I dive
Into your kiss I die

When you touch me I feel my heart
You rush me and there is a start
You clutch me and I can’t fall apart

The end is near I fear 
Send the tear from here
Bent by fear it looks clear

Through the prism 
The schism has risen
Abused in the prison
Hiding among the wisdom

I’m so exasperated
In what we’ve perpetuated
The clean glass has been grated
