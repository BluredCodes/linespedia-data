Torn down that which I made
Thrown everything down upon the blade
Taken all the parts away and back
There is no more space for slack

Once I was me and we were all true
Over time I lost myself and forgot who I knew
Often things left unsaid are never through
Our love was strong for who you gave it to

You can’t see me but I’m here
In tinted hues coming through unclear
Yet still you might know when I’m near
You’d swear you can taste what you hear 

Once I was me and we were all new
Over time lost myself and forgot what to do 
Often things left unsaid are never true
Our love has gone with what you knew 

You think I haven’t felt the trespass
In dark night the images pass
Dreary doldrums wafting in mass
Drowning me all too painfully fast

Once I was free and we all moved
Over time I lost patience and grew
Often things left dead are not through
