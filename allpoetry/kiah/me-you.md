---
title: "Me & You"
writer: "Kiah"
slug: "me-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8103-Me---You-by-Kiah"
---

Where have they have all gone?The kidz who helped write this song.Where have they gone to?Whatever happened to me & you?My life gone by & now I’m a man.But these still look like the same old hands.That held you close & held you tight.Through seasons of bliss, once upon a night.It’s a long life I’ve had to liveNow my hands have less to give.Twist of fate and a turn of the clockThere’s nothing left to do but talk.I’ve had this life & what have I done with it.Why do we care about a dream if we’ll never live it.
