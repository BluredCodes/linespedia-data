title: "Saving the flowers"
writer: "Kiah"

Screaming in the shower,
of the hate I devour.
So I can empower,
the beauty in this flower.

Teething on my own hands.
Oh the weight of all these demands.
Push me as far I can’t.
For the hope of a plan.

Appeasing in the hour,
so the clock won’t show her.
A word of what I owe her.
So that we can hate no more.

Rising in the way I strive.
Cleaving the ugly from inside.
All in the name of being alive.
To lead myself to where I abide.

Reaching through the empty expanse.
I am alone like the animals and plants.
Electrifying surges in my own hands.
As I touch what I saw at a glance.

The beauty, the duty, they’ve muted me.
I loved this flower, it once suited me.
You were lovely when you recruited me.
