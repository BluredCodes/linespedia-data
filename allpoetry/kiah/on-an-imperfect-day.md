On an imperfect day
I can see myself
a hazy, muddled reflection
in the windshield
looking back at me

the morning traffic
clipping along
beside me

I can see myself there, in the other car’s passenger door windows

haggard and half-awake
squinting, blinking
cursing to myself

On an imperfect day
I can see myself in my imperfect ways
short tempered
unforgiving

Most days I see through this
although
On certain days 
I see things
in very certain ways
