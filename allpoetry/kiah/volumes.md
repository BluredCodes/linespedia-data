title: "Volumes"
writer: "Kiah"

Play it at volumes low
Then we can forget to know
Innocence now so far gone
Lighted days will carry on

Say it won’t go
I want to know
Where the water goes
Wear from water shows
Say you don’t know
The love we can’t hold

Say it ain’t so
Be my Joe Di’Maggio
Hope you won’t go
Though the day is slow

Say it ain’t right
That we fight
For the hearts tender slice
Our flowing insight
Growing within the blight
Showing in the spite

Turned pages of endless volumes
I hear these years consumed
Tell me it’s not forgotten
Time is sweet, all too often
Time is fleeting and memories soften
