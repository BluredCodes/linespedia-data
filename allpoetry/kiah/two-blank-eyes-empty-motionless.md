title: "Two blank eyes, empty, motionless"
writer: "Kiah"

It was so simple
A wrist and a knife
Fist closed, teeth clenching
Voices filling the air with angst
The slash, the rip, drip, gush
The scream, the panic, fumble, stumble
Face in shock and horror’s descent of the end

Towel, pressure, grab, pull, drag
Ignition, screams, hostility, stupidity
Hurried movement through the streets
Speed, need, bleed, more
Drip, soak, gush, splash

Dandelions in July used to remind of ovaries
The waiting for release, the renewal of birth

It was simple
The wanting to die to be living
The wanting to sin to be forgiven
