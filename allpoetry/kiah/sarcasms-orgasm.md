I’m ripping you off
Your giving me the last toss
Leathered, cracked and worn
This is the creed I’ve sworn

Tearing at what irritates
Seering skin as it bakes
I like this, it’s fun
Just like this, it’s fun

Hours and hours go by
We get closer the mountain side
Death’s last breathe
Freezing wind we’ve met

I like this, it’s fun
I hate you, your dumb
I spite this, for fun
I make you your sun

I love looking for what is not there
I like it when you say you don’t dare
I’m above this, falling into your stare
I like this this burning that I sharetwitch, twitch..."I just feel different.  Not really like I'm high or anything, just different."
