Click-click, click-click, click-ca-lick,
click-click, click,

I hear these noise about
don’t know why
I see these faces come out
I can’t say why

Twitch-twitch, twitch-twitch, twitch
Wish I had a switch
I could turn off all of this
Clicking twitching
noisy nonsense
rambling on again
in ink
with abandon

click          click          click

I hear these noises
I’m not sure why
I made these choices
I know I’ve tried
And I know whyI think the repetion is a bit too overbearign in this.
