---
title: "Just The Same"
writer: "Kiah"
slug: "just-the-same"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/15210-Just-The-Same-by-Kiah"
---

Think I’ll just scream until my lungs bleed
Sink into this just as much as I need
Falling into what I’ve dug for myself
Buried in the pit of my own hell
Drunken, brawling, kicking and falling
Hunkered down through the sprawling
And just the just same 
even though you don’t know my name

You see we not alone in our fight 
You, she & he know what is right
Misters & Mrs. the pieces aren’t fitting
So it’s time to get up and start winning

Think I’m just gonna step off into a sunset
Pink, orange, & red have not come yet
But I know the new day is dawning
So keep your eyes open to what is coming
Because the revolution will not be televised
The revolution is here right before your eyes
Just the same it’s that plain
And still you don’t know my name

I am not alone in my contempt
You and I we are not exempt
Don’t take it the way they give it
It’s my life and I’m gonna live it
