Each day.
Velvet lips,
	crimson smile,
		sultry stare
right at me.
Right
	in 
         the 	
    face.
Of all I was looking away from.

Each day

Subtle T’s
Mixed among the pleasant treesThere is the girl that I work with.  She makes me feel.  She makes me.  Makes me steal glances of enticement.
