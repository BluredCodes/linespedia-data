title: "Frustration"

Frustration,Aggravation.Push myself against this again.Down into my worst sinTired and wasted.Full of the of the life I tasted.Sedation,pacification will not abate me.Listened long enough to the whistle & whine.Habitation,masturbation cannot relieve this mind.Crustaceans builds on my edifice.Flashes in my mind of where I’ve been once.Placation,dissertations can drag upon the very ounce.Frustration, angst and ruin.All of these things are my doing.Hesitation will create more pursuing.Speaking of things that are fluent.Reach for what you think you might.Seek to tell me of what you see at night.Ilumination.Persuasion is your kind knife.
