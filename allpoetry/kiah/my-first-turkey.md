title: "My First Turkey"

I wrestle the birdFrom the freezerThe thirteen pound motherStiff and unyieldingMade my way through the checkoutTo temper the wrinkled delicacySlippery sucker fightingMy culinary waresSubdued with a roasting pan and celeryI am victoriousAgainst the dry tacky meatMoist jubilationIn the rewardHappy Thanksgiving
