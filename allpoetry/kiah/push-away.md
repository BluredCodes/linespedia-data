---
title: "Push Away"
writer: "Kiah"
slug: "push-away"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Blood and wait,flood the gate.Push away,brush away.Rush this way,rust the day.Push away,we must stay.Mud sand paste,grub hands waste.Sub-standard place.Love's hazarded pace.Push away, what I can't say.Ride troubled lanes.Live among the panes.Give the love away.Hide what was once splayed.Push away,lust plays.We must stay.Dusted fray.Just one displayed.Trust is not made.
