I’m teched out
locked in
sucked down
gleaming shout 
hawked skin 
twisted around
double split 
helpless dream
empty shit
huddled seam
gurgled splash
flightless leap
circled ash
righteous sleep

convoluted deluded constituted polluted recruited
absoluted instituted included precluded secluded

I am all of this.
And I’m not me.
I am all this.
And I am free.Disclaimer-->'absoluted' and 'teched' are not words.  I don't care.
