---
title: "Brilliant Lights"
writer: "Kiah"
slug: "brilliant-lights"
source: "AllPoetry"

brilliant lights
shine on the blind
empty thin faces of
nameless blobs
dressed in
ratty and ripped pants

oversized

bottoms

dragging 
                               on 
                                                 the 
                                                                   floor

with the gliterring
party favors
coozing in my backseat
ferile flesh meat
someone's forgotten child
in the dark
with a different name
now

she 
             is 
                           wild 

a resonance in our hollow heads
one, two, three
brain cells buzzing
trickling sweat
pager numbers
sticky eyes

we have no songs to sing
we have nothing but thumping
we can't remember a world before this
wasn't it always like this

we never had standards to meet
there is only this call
of her
beckoning
heat
my beating heart
embrace of the nameless dark

envenolpe and remove all the guilt
lost in the flowers wilt
I care little for rules
my tongue is on fire
she is all mine
she doesn't care who I am
we don't care who we are

we have nothing
this is nothing
but at least it is now
at least it is ours
no
one
else
cares

why do we?

we are just selfish

toorave on......
