Don't talk to me
I'm not you, you see
Don't waste your breath
I'm not living in your debt
I've only lived as long as I can stand
I've only done as much as a man can

Don't feel for me
I never asked you to you see

Don't look to these eyes
I'm not the one to despise
Don't press down on my brow
I'm not yours to anyhow
I've only lived the way I could bear it
This life is a coat & I've had to wear it

Don't feel for me
I never asked you to you see
