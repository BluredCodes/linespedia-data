---
title: "Under your skin"
writer: "Kiah"
slug: "under-your-skin"
source: "AllPoetry"

Another dead starlet
one more fallen harlot
and I wonder where it all got started

Was it when the skies parted
and man was put down & thwarted
by his own hand?

man,

watch for the slight of hand
planned this to go down
like you never fantasized
with all your lies

Eyes that despise
can see the demise
like flies eating pies
the cancer is slow
and nearly 
unseen


But still the whole time
this is where its been
right 

under
your skin.
