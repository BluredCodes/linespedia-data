I like love when it’s newI like when your gone & I’m blueThe first feelings so strongThe bursting in us so longI like you when your pristineI like when I’m in your machineLike when I was youngWords caught on my tongueMight leads the chargeSo heavy and so hardI fight love when it’s closeI might love what I want the mostThe sincerity is not thereThe truth cannot be baredI hide love when it can be seenI try to love to drive the machineWe contrive what we haveWe survive to forget what lastsWe survive, we supplyWe have all died, we are all inside.I like love’s choking stewI like the burning screwThe first screams so trueThe worst dreams so newspastic utterances
