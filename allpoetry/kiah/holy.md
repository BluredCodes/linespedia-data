---
title: "Holy"
writer: "Kiah"
slug: "holy"

Romancing the notion
Caught up in the commotion
Teasing the smell of the potion
While I'm dancing on the ocean

Dreams fade away in the day 
Thoughts in me do stray
Lost in words that play
Seems it should be that way

Holy, holy end coming
Away, away we are running
Totally afraid of hunting
Mostly we stay loving

Holy, holy she’s coming
Away, away she’s running
Totally after & wanting
Mostly she is wanting

Placing flowers on shelves
Pacing hours ‘til the twelve
Eased the slow hell
Freed what was held

Holy, hole I’m falling
Mostly certain I’m crawling
The day is still sprawling
Holy hearts are mauling
