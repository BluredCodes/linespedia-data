---
title: "Glowing Fire"
writer: "Kiah"

Like a glowing fireA cinderI burn and splinterCrooked as the riverSlight as your sliverYou stabbed into my heartStepped off the startRaging, seething, gnarledI’ve only come this farTo stand & endeavorLike a speeding missile cruisesTo fly away
