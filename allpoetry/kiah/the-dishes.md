---
title: "The Dishes"
writer: "Kiah"
slug: "the-dishes"

Kangaroo kisses 
and bedtime wishes
The dog is still outside
and no one did the dishes

Here and there the pieces lie
Once upon 
and our last good-bye

Dangling misses
and my christening
The world is still outside
and the dishes are undone

Here and once upon 
The day was built
There and then gone 
Was all I felt

The dishes are drying 
and the house is sparkling
In the quiets ever darkening

Strangling wishes
and endless eyes
The gleaming dish dry clean
of my demise
