unplug

reach down and stop the feed

put away
the shiny little techno-bobbles
you think you need

get away
from all the electricity

the pulsing 
mind numbing
signal
enscribing
digital death
silent analog
dreaming
streaming 
repetitive
emissions

stop the transmission

Sentience is for knowing the world
not observing it.

C:\>disconnect
