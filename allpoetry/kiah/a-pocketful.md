---
title: "A pocketful"
writer: "Kiah"
slug: "a-pocketful"
source: "AllPoetry"

Head full of dreams 
and a pocketful of guilt.
We’ve weaved these lives like a quilt.
Through our toils and through our time.
We’ve done what we could & found what we could find.
Just like the blanket that as a child you did cling.
Lives have become tattered and the edges are fringed.
So now with a pocket full of dreams
and a head full of guilt.
You must lay in the bed that you've built.
