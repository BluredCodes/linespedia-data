---
title: "I am"
writer: "Kiah"
slug: "i-am"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/12313-I-am-by-Kiah"
---

I am obsessive
Compulsive
Deranged and alive

Inspired by greatness
Derived from misfortune

I am veracious
Ostentatious
Evasive and salacious

Overwhelmed by awe
Instituted by the law

I am instigating
Pervading
Lascivious and waiting

Consumed by apathy
Caught in the catastrophe

Moved by true emotion
Swaying like branches in motionI am self-centered.
