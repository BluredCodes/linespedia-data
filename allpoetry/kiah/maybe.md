Maybe, just maybe.
I’m straight as a board.
Maybe I’m true and through.
Like an arrow flung on course.

But maybe just as much,
I’m as jagged and rocky.
As the earth from which I sprang
Like the bitter and murky waters
That bore me up.Doubtful introspection.
