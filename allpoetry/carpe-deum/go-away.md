Give it a rest buddy
Don't even try
Why can't you get the picture 
And leave me be
I know your only intention
Is to love me and then drop me
Like a rock straight from the fire
I can see it in your eyes
You just can't be serious
To think your lying, scheming
Will work on me...
...AgainI was in an interesting mood when I wrote this. It's for all you people who just can't seem to get an ex-boyfriend/ girlfriend off your back.
