---
title: "Love and Rain"
writer: "Carpe Deum"
slug: "love-and-rain"
source: "AllPoetry"

The heavens are crying 
Pouring down tears in torrents 
I'm running, soaked down to the skin 
And you are waiting there for me 

You are waiting in the doorway 
A blue towel in your hands 
And you hand it to me 
With a smile and a kiss 

A moment later, curled up with you 
Lounging drowsily by the fire 
Just about asleep 
As your hands play with my hair 

The blanket is soft 
Your touch is softer 
And I feel safe here 
Wrapped up in your arms 

It wouldn't matter 
If the whole world was gone 
As long as I still had you 
To have and to hold 

Forever my love, until the end
