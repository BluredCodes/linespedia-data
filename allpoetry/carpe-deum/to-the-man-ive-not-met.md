title: "To The Man I've Not Met"
writer: "Carpe Deum"

Always in my dreams
Getting in my mind
The block I've put before you
Is breached so easily
It makes me wonder
Just how strong I really am
And I'm sure that was the idea
Even though we've not met
I know you
