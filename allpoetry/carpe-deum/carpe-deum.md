Sometimes I would like to throw caution to the windTake you, hold you, make you mineBut you seem as tangible As dust in the windAnd I don't have a chanceCarpe DeumSeize the dayTake to the skyAnd flyThere are days when I want to scream to youGive me a chance, a word, a lookThat pretty girl withThe long blonde hairWon't last longCarpe DeumSeize the dayWhen she's goneI'm thereSeductive darkness, diamond starsMy pleas to you go unnoticedAs they always are Teardrop on the fire loveThat's all they'll ever beUntil you add your heart to mineCarpe DeumI've siezed the dayAnd love is...Perfect
