title: "Yearning for the Emerald Isle"

My heart is on the Isle

With the faeries and the sheep

My home is with the Irish

And the beauty that is Eire



The flowing voice of the people

The power in their songs

The fierce pride in their homeland 

Though their troubles may be long



They're never far from me

Just around the bend

From the silence of the Arans

To the bustle of Dublin



My heart is with the Irish

Though I live so far from home

Give me a moment to be with them again

And I'll never want for more



Their greeting to me will be

Cead Mile Failte

A hundred thousand welcomes

And thank god you're safely here



Sitting around the fire

Telling jokes, stories, news

Beauty in a thousand forms

While my voice raises to the heavens in this solemmn, heartfelt cry...



GOD BLESS THE IRISH
