Jen, you proved that I could get up at six-thirty a.m. and live.
Beth, you were the one who kept me supplied with problems to solve. 
Just remember that Suave is the answer to everything.
Amanda, you kept me grounded in reality
Becca and Cheryl did the opposite
Paul, keep on being your wierd, awesome, hilarious self.
Chris, you're the defense that I thank God I found.
Katie darlin, thanks for being so sweet!
Nick, you are a weasel but I love you anyway.
Matt Brown, thanks for testing my reflexes.
And Ami,you are my better half
Roommates, friends.
Back-to-back, we'll take 'em all!I dedicate this to my friends at LPS. I LOVE YOU GUYS!
