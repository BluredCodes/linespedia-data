>< = Refrain

Standing in the firelight
(You're here; so am I)
It's been a long time
(My friend, why did you leave me alone?)
I couldn't stop thinking about the times we had
(Together, always together)
Times when even a simple handshake
Meant the world to me

>And now we're back to Genesis
The eye before the storm
I thought you were gone for good and now
I can know you once again
Why did you think it was necessary
To let me grow up on my own?
I missed you<

I loved you so much
(Did you have any idea?)
But then you left 
(And I gave you up for dead)
You were searching for the path
(But the path led back to me)
Yes, the path led back to me
And your smile is known again

><

We're sitting in the firelight
(Together, you and I)
And our thoughts are one, the same
(All hurt forgotten, let me heal your wounds)
Everything is right again
(And will be now and always)
No more dwelling on days gone by
Life started once again

><
