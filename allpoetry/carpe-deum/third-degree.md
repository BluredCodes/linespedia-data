* = Refrain

I'm the person that at work you meet
Payin' my bills 
I'm the lady walking down the street
Dressed to kill
Sittin' in my chair
Watching the TV
Livin' my life to the third degree

*Don't you dare
Presume to judge me
You don't know who I am
I'm a little bit of somethin'
A little bit of not 
Livin' my life to the third degree*
  
I'm the beggar at the corner store
Beggin for change
I'm the company CEO, got more
Money than arranged
Give me a chance
Because you see
I'm livin' my life to the third degree

*

Let me live the best that I can
Kill the world with your powerful hands
All I ask for is a niche on this Earth 
And a place that I can call home...

*
