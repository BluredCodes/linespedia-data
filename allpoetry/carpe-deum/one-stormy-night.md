---
title: "One Stormy Night..."
writer: "Carpe Deum"
slug: "one-stormy-night"
source: "AllPoetry"

Picture...
A walled city, guards at every gate
A storm is brewing on the horizon
The rustling leaves shiver with anticipation
Lightning splits the sky

Picture...
A parapet rising from the inky blackness
A palace untouched by wind or rain
A curtain billows on the balcony
A Princess stirs in her sleep

Watch...
A young man vaults the city walls
Appraises the situation, makes a decision
Swings up to the balcony
And pushes the curtain aside

Listen...
The gentle sound of footsteps as he walks across the stone
The sigh of a woman as she hears the random noise
The rustle of blankets as she sits up, startled
The sharp intake of breath to scream

Watch...
He is at her side in a instant
He puts his hand firmly but gently over her mouth
She begins to struggle
He lowers his lips to her ear

Listen...
"I'm not here to hurt you," The man says
"I'm a thief not a rapist,
Let me do what I came for, don't get in my way
I'll be gone before you know it"

Watch...
The princess stops her struggles
Her eyes light up with hope
The thief sees her resistance is gone
And removes his hand from her mouth

Listen...
"Take me with you," Pleads the woman
"Before I'm sold to the highest bidder
Even you would be better
Than a man I've never met"

Listen...
The man laughs, "Why should I little woman?
That's part of your lot in life."
"Yes," She counters, "But I would gladly give up everything,
To have the life you lead"

Listen...
"Alright then," He sees that she's sincere. 
"Pack lightly and come with me
Against my better judgement
I'm taking you away"

Watch...
They stand upon the balcony
Both jump down simultaneously
They set off running across the lawn
And scramble up the wall

Picture...
The two fly over the desert sand
Into the cool,sweet moonlight
The darkness envelopes them in velvet
The golden sand winks at them as they pass

Watch...
For weeks they walk, each depending on the other
Dodging patrols, raiding villages, the princess as efficient as the man
The thief who prided himself on being independent
Has a harder time thinking of life without her 

Picture...
An oasis in the desert
Life giving water flows in streams
Emerald palms wave in the breezes
The man is finally home
 
Listen...
"We'll be safe here, away from the city
And don't worry, I won't turn you in
You are a part of me now," Says the thief
"And so you will always be"
