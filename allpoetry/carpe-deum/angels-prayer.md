---
title: "Angel's Prayer"
writer: "Carpe Deum"
slug: "angels-prayer"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/15165-Angels-Prayer-by-Carpe-Deum"
---

In the world today
You wonder what we're coming to
So many things to deal with
That our grandparents never had
And you wonder
Was the world this shadowed before?
Were the stars this dark before?
Or is it just because of us?
Pity the younger generation
For they will never know what it's like
To dance under the winter moon
To feel cold, glacial water ripple over their hands
Like the Prayer of an Angel
