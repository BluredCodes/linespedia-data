---
title: "Water to Fire"
writer: "Carpe Deum"
slug: "water-to-fire"

Hear the brook ripple
As the eagle soars above
Hear the beat of your heart 
As it signifies your love
Raise your arms
Take a breath
Call down what dwells above
Prince of the Earth and Stars

Andromeda to Pandora
Question to an answer
As water is to fire my love
So you are to me

Let the snow sparkle around you
Let the wind caress your face
Are you earth or are you fire
Deep ocean or deep space?

Andromeda to Pandora
Question to an answer
As water is to fire my love
So you are to me

Can you sing with the stars
Can you dance with the trees
Can you share in the joy of life
Or is it buried deep inside?

Listen to the ancient chants
As they carry you away
Smile and place your hand in mine
At the coming of the day
Crystal soul
Laughing eyes
That's what you are to me
The Prince of the Earth and Stars

Andromeda to Pandora
Question to an answer
As water is to fire my love
So you are to me
