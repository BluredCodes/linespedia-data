---
title: "Dreams"
writer: "Carpe Deum"

Taken again to a place far away
Swung on the breath of the moon
Waiting again for the expulsion of day
Taking a song from a rune

Raise up your hands to the silvery sky
Dance as if no one is there
Let the starlight bedazzle your eye 
And the rose-drops twine in your hair

When the beauty of harmony lifts you away
And the seraphim join in your song
The depth of the darkness can't hold you in sway
As you fly through the heavenly throng

But suddenly feeling the pull of the earth
Before you get to far away
You hold on to that dream for all that it's worth
But there's no stopping the coming of day
