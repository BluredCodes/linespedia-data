---
title: "Forgive Me"
writer: "Carpe Deum"
slug: "forgive-me"

Forgive me for not being perfect
Forgive me for not trying
Forgive the times when you were there
Waiting
And I was not
Forgive me for not telling you sooner
How I really felt
Forgive me for my arrogance
Forgive me for my stubbornness
Forgive me for holding you back 
When the only way you could go was forward
I'm sure you'll be happy now 
You've spread your wings to fly
Just like the Angel I know you are
But please remember what we had
And forgive me my trespasses
Against you
