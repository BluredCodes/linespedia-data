---
title: "Just a moment"
writer: "Carpe Deum"

If I could have a moment darling
Just to be with you
While the world crumbles down around us
Crashes like waves on the rocks
A moment just to hold your hand
To let the teardrop fall
While the snow sparkles down around us 
And the wind sings our song
To hold you close in the crush
Of dancing bodies, twining souls
So right yet so ignored
If I could have that moment love
Life would never be the same
