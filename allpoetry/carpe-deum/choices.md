title: "Choices"

Have you ever made a choice
That you wish you didn't make
From the depths of human reason
You knew that it was wrong
But once the words were out
You couldn't take them back
And never will your life be the same
And the guilt eats away at you
Makes you a different person
From who you used to be
Tears are your constant companions now
Like friends you used to see 
And deep down
Pounding at the guilded cage of your heart 
The bluebird of happiness struggles to fly
And maybe, someday it will
