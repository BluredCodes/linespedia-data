title: "One Step At a Time"
writer: "Carpe Deum"

Hmmm...
Don't you ever wonder
What things could be like
If people would sit down 
Take the time to watch the clouds?

They're always changing
Different
Yet the sky accepts them 
Why can't we?

Hmmm...
Don't you think
That if people would just
Take time to grow a flower
Things would change for better?

How can you think of hurt
Pain
When the petals unfurl
As you watch?

Hmmm...
Sometimes I see
People on the hills
With their fingers intertwined 
And I think to myself... 

...Well that's a step in the right direction...
