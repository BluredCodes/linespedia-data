title: "Dark Angel"
writer: "Carpe Deum"

I saw him standing in the doorway
A mysterious angel with his dark good looks
Hair like night
Face like ivory
Eyes like a vampire's tears
Repulsed, yet not afraid
No, never afraid
This is whom I have been seeking
The one who can tell me who I am
I step to meet him
"Come," He whispers. "Come to me."
But I shake the demon spell he has over me
I hold back, without knowing why
He laughs and the sound is that of a clarion
"You are strong, little one"
"And you will not claim me, angel of the night." I say.
"I am not yours to command."
Finally, finally, I know
I understand
