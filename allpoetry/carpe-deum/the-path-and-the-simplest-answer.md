You spend all your time searching
To find the path that's simpler
But what if the simplest answer 
Is right in front of you

Maybe you don't have to search I've found
It just makes your life complex
Maybe just sitting back and waiting
Is the best thing all around

I prayed to God to find someone
Just exactly like you
But I thought I had to help things along
But really, all I had to do was trust

Because as soon as I did
You were practically hurled at me
I never realized this before
But now I see

That one chance encounter
At a dance long ago
Never really left me
Nor you, or so it seems

Now all I have to do is wait
Wait, wait and pray
And laugh at the others who are digging
Because they're just like me

The person I once was
