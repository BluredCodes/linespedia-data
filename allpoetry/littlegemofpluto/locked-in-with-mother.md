With every tear i cry
My eyes start to become dry
As my heart is filled with pain
I can't help but hear the pooring rain
Beating the roof
Like my parents words beat my soul

In my room for hours 
Not knowing what to do but cry 'why me?' 
And 'let me just be free!'
I can't take all this pain
And because of this torcher I'll never be the same

Thanks alot Jennifer my mom yells
And when she does i just wanna rebel 
The only real woman i could talk to turns away from me
But you know what i see
My mom
A woman who wants to be free
Just like me
