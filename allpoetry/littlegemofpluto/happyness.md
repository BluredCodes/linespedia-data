Finally
A place of peace
A place where my mind is set
A little person inside wonders if it will last
Or is this just a dream 
A dream that will go by too fast
Happyness was the place i wanted to be
And now its true my spirit has been set free
Free in the land of happyness
where critisems roll is put to rest
Happyness is what i wanted 
But was it what i need?
That's what i thought
Nothing but a world of defeat
Where all of us shall never winplease let me know what you think. its somewhat important to me to know how this effects you all. but please dont be too harsh these are my feelings.
