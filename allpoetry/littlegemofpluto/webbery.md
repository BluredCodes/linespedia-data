As i close my eyes
I dream of a place far away
Where unicorns play,fairys fly,and cause mayhem,and my love is waiting for me,for me

I wonder if he'll ever find me
But i'll just have to wait and see, and see
A long wait for someone so amazing is worth it
Even if he isn't perfect

Love,Love,Love
A lil thing called love changes us all 
And yet it always makes me fall 

Deeper,Deeper,Deeper
Into it's tangled web
The web of love 
Which i could never live withoutThis one i like a lot.I tells the truth.
