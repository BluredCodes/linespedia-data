---
title: "You and Me"
writer: "littlegemofpluto"
slug: "you-and-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

You hate me
Fine
But don't tell me
You want to criticize me
Fine
Kill my emotions and feelings
You want to beat me up 
Go ahead
Bruse my body
You want to kill me 
Fine 
But you'll never get my soul 
for my soul is too kind
to be over ruled by someone like you!This was about my middle school and elementary days. I was verbally abused a lot back then.
