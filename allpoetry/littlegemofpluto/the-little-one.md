In the picture that you see
it is me
Young, silly, happy,and bright
playing during the day and asleep at night
i was young and free with no worries nor cares
Exspecialy when we went to the fairs
My hair of brown and my eyes of blue 
Made my mother and father say "i love you"
Now i'm older filled with worries and cares and emosional feeling i cant stand to bare
My heart is filled with hopes and dreams
But my life is not what it seems
My goal in life is to sing
To me singing is everything
My heart is filled with music and song
I hope to perform a life time long 
Yes im full of fears
But i hope one day that my time shall be near
When one day i'll have no fears
But there's one more thing I want to be 
That one thing is meThis was my first poem. I think it's cute. let me know what you think. k?
