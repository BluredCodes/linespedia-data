Dear Grampa,
I love you so dearly, and i always will. But why did you have to leave so soon? There were so many things that i wanted to say. So many times that were swept away, just by that one final day. I wanted to go out for pizza and follow you to the bar. But now it's too late, now the distance between us is just too far. I love you grampa and i always will. I hope that you wait for me in heaven, and till that day, I'll love you still.
                                   Love,
                                 Jennifer
                             Your lil doctorThis was a letter i wrote under a picture i drew to my dead grandpa Jack.He left this world when i was only 5 years old.I never got the chance to spend alot of time with him cause he was always sick.So i wrote this to him. Just to show how much i love him and miss him. 
PS
the "Your lil Doctor" thing is about how i use to love taking one of those fake doctors kits and pretending that i was his doctor. No matter how sick he was he'd always let me and i always told him he'd be fine. But everytime i said that i know now that in his heart he would relize that he didn't have much time left.
