Our life is short 
Though it seems long
Problems Accure
Though we don't know what's wrong
One day everything is just fine
Then the next day you dial the number with two ones and a nine
you don't know if she'll be alright
Afterthis long and unfair fight
And the same day abutse falls upon a friend
A fight between her and her father that will never end
A threat to slap her till she turns blue
A screaming duel between the two
Your friend knows running won't make it end
Yet she knows that all she needs is her best friend
Both in a mix up 
Both in a bind
Life is not sweet
Nor is it divine
Life is a test 
You either flunk or pass
Or you can die trying
