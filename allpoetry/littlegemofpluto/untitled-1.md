A young woman's heart is deeply in love
 and deeply confused all because of someone sent from up above
 She wonders just how soon her love will arrive
For the thought of being alone makes her not want to be alive
 The only thought that keeps every beat
is the one thought of her loves arms rapped around her and telling her words so sweet
 She knows one day
and one day soon
he will return to heaven only for a short while 
then one day return for them to one day have a child.this was about someone i really did love. but i know now that i was decived.
