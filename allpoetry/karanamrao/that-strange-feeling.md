It's that strange 
feeling of intimacy
filtering into my dreams,
that ushered me into
the biege of sunlight,
after the rain-ravaged
                night.
The sweet-nothings 
that you spoke to me
huddled me into
a pahlanx of recollec-
               tions
where we only existed
among the galaxy of stars.
Haven't these striations
 
and the ambient 
          silence of
Spring's dark night,
that brought us face 
             to face
with the quietness
