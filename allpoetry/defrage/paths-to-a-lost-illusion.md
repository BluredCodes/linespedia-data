title: "Paths to a Lost Illusion"
writer: "defrage"
slug: "paths-to-a-lost-illusion"

Soothe me softly with words i cannot hear,
Lift me to the sky's soft embrace once more,
Dilute the words of wisdom that your told,
Drift in and out of ecstasy and soar.

Once more,
I know i cannot remember 
The path to this garden,
So i must forgive the hour, 
And reduce the moment.

Leave me somewhere in the spaces between,
To grasp at that precious piece i lack,
Now time's grandoise facade has become stale,
I'll abandon the pleasure to go crawling back.

Once more,
I know i cannot remember 
The path to this garden,
So i must forgive the hour, 
And reduce the moment.

In silence we find escape from this mad cacophony,
Solitude the price of our dazed reality,
And if i grasp it, it will surely only fall,
A temporary gain before we lose it all.

Once more, 
I cannot remember,
The words i've spoken,
The paths i've taken,
So reduce the moment.
