Dreams forever fade away,
Betrayed by a blacker day,
The sun shines and we're not alright,
Cause mist shrouds our every sight,
And we're swallowed in hate's abyss.

Alone in another's paradise,
Where burning blades tear and slice,
Now we're bleeding in a slow melt,
No tourniquet can hide what we've felt,
We'll dig a hole to our escape.

Down there beyond poison darts,
There's comfort in common hearts,
Sit out the pulse of this raging storm,
Forget any desire to conform,
Reject their world for our own.

Fuck the norm, this is your home.
