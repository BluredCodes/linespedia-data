---
title: "Serene of the Stars"
writer: "defrage"

Bare witness to the beauty of difference,
Surreal moments in the scented air,
As if a dark ghost touched me,
Held me, bound me, freed me.
If i could grasp such serenity,
I could hold my place in this world,
Like cinnamon on the stale taste of life,
A reason to exist amongst the conformity.

And although now gone, like the cool breath of season's past,
Even in this horrific state, one ethereal vision will last.
