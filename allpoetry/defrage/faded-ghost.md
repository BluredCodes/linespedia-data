---
title: "Faded Ghost"
writer: "defrage"

If it haunts me,
Then it taunts me,
I don't know why,
You deplore me.
I fade away,
I fade away,
You don't see me,
The days are pale.

I know that everything is something to someone,
And we're all lost on the paths to fulfillment,
But i'm further gone so i asked for directions,
I'm so naive - you pointed me to my sadness.

I don't want you,
You can't touch me,
I don't need you,
Won't you hold me.
It's called love,
It's called love,
I'm so naive,
Won't you hold me.

It seems i've been falsely drawn down dimly-lit roads,
I've percieved openings in the foggy darkness,
But the ghost of clarity has left me more questions,
And your wicked dance drains all this optimism.

I don't want you,
You can't touch me,
I don't need you,
Won't you hold me.
It's called love,
It's called love,
I'm so naive,
Won't you hold me.It's weird looking back on that even though i only wrote it about two months ago. I guess it captures my state of mind at the time. I think i feel slightly more positive about things at the moment.
