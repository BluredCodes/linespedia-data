---
title: "Demons of Lore"
writer: "defrage"
slug: "demons-of-lore"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/31569-Demons-of-Lore-by-defrage"

This bloodsucking vampire is draining my life,
But who the hell can i blame when i invited it in.
These vicious wolves are tearing me apart,
But who the hell can i blame when i gave them a taste.

I plead insanity in an assuredly sane world,
I scan the horizon for something that won't pass me by.
This splintered mirror reflects a broken spirit,
That poisonous apple a frivolous escape.

This brutal ogre is crushing my compassion,
But who the hell can i blame when i started the fight.
These murdering fairies are blackening my heart,
But who the hell can i blame when i asked for this kiss.

I plead insanity in an assuredly sane world,
I scan the horizon for something that won't pass me by.
This splintered mirror reflects a broken spirit,
That poisonous apple a frivolous escape.

This dark angel is killing me with its touch,
But who the hell can i blame when i took its hand.
These silent voices are driving me insane,
But who the hell can i blame when each is one of me.
