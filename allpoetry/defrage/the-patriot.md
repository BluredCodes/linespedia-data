Just call me a fucking patriot,
Cause the people in my country
Are worth more than yours - 
A racist by any other name.

Just call me a fucking patriot,
Cause i'll sing the national anthem
And believe the lies i'm fed - 
A fool by any other name.

Just call me a fucking patriot,
Cause i'll wear my flag with pride
Even if it's bloodstained from genocide - 
A criminal by any other name.

Just call me a fucking patriot,
Cause i'll follow my government
No matter who they screw - 
A sheep by any other name.

Just call me a fucking patriot,
Cause i'll bomb any nation
In the name of my freedom - 
A terrorist by any other name.Lyrics to a punk song?
