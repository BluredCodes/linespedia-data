title: "Surrender"
writer: "Ladybug"

Jesus in our hearts shall make us weep
For the sin’s we proclaim to be our day
End over the days of our destiny.

Let us come to you in humbling steps
To honor your name in victory
 Let us lead the way to a fruitful day… 

Can our hearts be still when you whisper our name?
O'glory be to God, for you are the only one
That brings to me a sweet sound inside my soul
That longs for a life of victory.

To overcome the burdens of this world
The days are long and the nights too short
For me to give you my all will take oh so much more…

Dear father above, please bring to me
A discovery in your light of a day
To give you honor and blissful joy

Let my voice be a beacon to all that may hear
You are the one that won my heart
In all this land, less any mercy
Let my hands cover someone’s pain in suffering

Let my eyes see others as a glory to you
As we discover your law of love in total understanding
To forgive, and lead, those not quite ready for your glory...

by: Tamara J Baker
