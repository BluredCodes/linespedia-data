Your love reaches to heavens
Your faithfullness streaches to the sky
Your righteousness reaches to the mountains
Your justice glows like an ocean tide...

I will lift my voice to worship you, my king
I will find my strenth in the shadow of your wings
I will lift my voice to worship you, my king
I will find my strength in the shadow of your wings

Your love O'Lord reaches to the heavens
Your faithfullness streaches to the sky
