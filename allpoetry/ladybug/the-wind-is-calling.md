So long ago...
I sat listening to the wind 
call my name
A new direction, new location
where the rain is plentiful

Now I gaze upon this new land
Amazed of the colors upon it,
red, orange, blues and greens
Near a wide open field

The sunset is magnificant
 in hues of gold and blues
the warmth of the sun
shines brightly over the lake

On the back porch
I sit in a swing,
listening to the wind
 calling my name

Earth so plentiful
pecan, oak, pine trees
The cardinal sings
As the bluebird test their perch
 
I love this land
where my heart sings
I hear the wind
calling my name
