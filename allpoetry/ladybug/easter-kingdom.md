My kingdom come
for thy will be done
here on earth
as it is in heaven

I came to you, born in flesh
I left of you as a martyr
You question today
the purpose of Me

I can only answer you
when you listen within
there you find
a sweet reward

A peace of contentment
A joy of merry
A loss for all need
within your lifes history

Come together my friends
for I serve you one and All
In spirit our union is One

As you call for my name
I shall serve your every need
in great prosperity
with an abundance of joy

Please won't you call to me
So I may carry your sorrow
None too large or small
Let me hold You
in the cup of my hand.



It is time we surrender to our calling
the Lord needs us all, 
to protect against the enemy
We need to be together, One is spirit
To rise for the day
when the devil takes the world his reign.Easter is near, and the world is still spinning lost
Hear Jesus name and love within your heart
