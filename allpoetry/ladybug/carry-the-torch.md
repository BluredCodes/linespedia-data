---
title: "Carry The Torch"
writer: "Ladybug"

Carry the torch 
for the victims of the fire
Carry the torch
for the families that lost one
Carry the torch 
for the hero’s in volunteers
Carry the torch
for the freedom that ring’s clear
Carry the torch
for the firefighters and police
who give only mercy
To the many trapped and injured victims
Carry the torch
for the fatherless babies
Who sacrificed a life
in a days work on trade street
Carry a torch for the pilots
in air who fly us here and there
Carry the torch
till there is no more
harm of this cultural war…


by: Tamara Jo Baker
