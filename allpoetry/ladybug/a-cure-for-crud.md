In dedication for Patrick
repo 79



Let me lay a cloth upon your head
to soothe the aching in the brow

Let me smother you in soft velevet blankets
to comfort the cold on your bones

Let me read you bedtime stories
to comfort your mind that aches

Let me stew up some chicken broth
to steam away the hardness in your head

Let me draw you a bath of rosemary and bubbles
to sooth the ache within the joints

Let me comfort you eyes
with a warm compress

All these things I would do for You
to make you feel better soon!

When the crud comes
Let me soothe away all bitter parts.
