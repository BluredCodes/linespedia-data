---
title: "For You"
writer: "Ladybug"
slug: "for-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/47937-For-You-by-Ladybug"
---

To stand for you, I stay
To inspire your every dream

I live for you 
my hope
my heart, my sweet honey

You kiss each whisper in my dreams
Your love fills my every thought

To caress and smolder
the heat that burns you all over

A day without your touch
Is a day without water to my thirst

I hunger for your sweet smell
I hunger for your sweating beat

My love for you will grow
till it fills this universe

For you I will always be
standing by your side
To fullfill every dream.
