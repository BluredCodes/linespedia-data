title: "The Vessel of Light"

Thy kingdom come, thy will be done
Here we lie for our guidance to come
A vessel of love we strive to be
A new message for all to see

Glory is in the break of a day
As we begin a new day in the sun
Carry my heart, my soul to be
A new kingdom in victory

A new song of praise
I give to thee my all
To take it all and wash it away
The union of spirits is here to come

A vessel of light shines a ray of hope
To worship and honor our Father as One
Oh heal my broken heart and mend my ways
To worship only you, Jehovah Jirah, Jesus Christ.
