Sweet kisses of dew,
The smell of roses
And color of hope
My Valentine has come true.

A man of strength,
Yet so kind and tender
As we touch once more
To see our joy unfold.

Hand in hand we stand
To conquer the quest of life.
We change to meet the 
Needs of our family.

To reach inside and find
A tender soul so full,
Of un-awakened experiences
The dream has been fulfilled.this is an old piece I found, written while pregnant and wanting of a new child.
