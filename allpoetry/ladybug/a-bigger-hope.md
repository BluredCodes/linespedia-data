Can you feel my breath
as I walk away?
Filled with disgust
as you lay in bed

Another day wasted
another hour spent
lazily, hazily in misery...

Can you not wake
with enthusiasm and wit
To see a new day rising
In glitter and sparkle

I truly want to know
what weighs you down
full of dreary and scorn

Come to the mountain
and dream
of rosemary and cinnamon

See a new day 
has just begun,
make it a joyfull merry

Carry the love in your heart
spread it wherever you go
learn to let go of all that trouble
that keeps you bound in ruble...I offer you the Terzanelle style by Scindr
Love Anon and Love Refrained
