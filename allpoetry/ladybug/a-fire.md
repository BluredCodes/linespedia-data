---
title: "A Fire"
writer: "Ladybug"

like whispers in the wind
the candle light does glow

a season in time
shall bring
glory for all to see
another miracle


each step is closer 
each fallen star is deeper
watching the light within
grow deeper in ambers color


a walk in the dark
brings light of a day
a new song to be sung
stay here with me


to savor the ecstasy
of the moon arising
spelling a name upon the sky
is the whisper of the wind


the flame is afire
as the fire is blown out
the wick stands tall and strong
after burning for oh so longdid you light a candle today?
