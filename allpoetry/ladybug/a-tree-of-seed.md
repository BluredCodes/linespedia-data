---
title: "A Tree of Seed"
writer: "Ladybug"
slug: "a-tree-of-seed"
source: "AllPoetry"

The tree is tall
as the trunk is round
Like limbs of our soul
they hold their arms out thick
bathing in the warmth of the sun

The trunk of the tree is solid
full of strength
Buried below the ground, oh so low'
they are the roots of our life

We are all a tree full of seed
budding a new life in history
We carry with us the wind and the rain
To hear the wind blow in another storm

A dove of flight
taking us in faith
To withstand the wind of time
allowing our branches to bend for growth

Let our branches bear fruit
tall and strong
Our roots are in the family
From where we all grow 
Let us too, all be a good seed...


 a dedication to Dr.Linda Kraeger
