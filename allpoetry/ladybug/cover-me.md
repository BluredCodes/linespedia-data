writer: "Ladybug"

Cover up my soul
 Cover up my heart

Cover up my tears
 Cover up my fears

Cover up this world
 Everything I know

Cover up this sky
 Cover up this sea

You unfold me 
 With your mercy

You unfold me
 Full of your glory

With everything of this world
 Cover us up
  in your breath of glory 

Free me of my sin
 In all that we do

Let us give it all back
 to youShall we ever be free??
