Beauty is heart felt

  as a butterfly is to nectar



      seeds of love

     in perfect harmony



    In our eye we see beauty

      by the vote to agree



But it is imperial to 

  the one who holds us close



For beauty can only be found

  In it's beholder



      Not in a mirror

    Not in a second glance



Beauty is found in the heart

      when we love

     We are all beautiful

        to one another.



 So you see, with you and me

   When we truly let go

     We find our true love



  Found in the heart of another

     Is the true mirror of ourself...Beauty is found in the eye of the beholder...
