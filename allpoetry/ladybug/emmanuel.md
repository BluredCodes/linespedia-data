Emmanuel

Away in a manger lays a little baby boy
His name is Emmanuel, God is with us
He came to us all, to proclaim a choice
To live among gladness and joy
He asks from us only one request
To live out life in peace
He is well known throughout the world
For he brings warmth where there is cold
He brings cheer where there is sadness
He brings hope where there is despair
He brings gladness where there is sorrow
He can be found in many locations
Tonight his light will shine as the star of Bethlehem
You can find him close by your side
You see he lives within you and me
You will recognize him as Jesus Christ
As you open your heart, he resides there.
To change our will of greed
To a joy full of peace
Won’t you open that door?
To enter the gates of harmony.
And step inside the world of faith 
Or do you choose to live 
On the checks and balances with George Washington...
You see my friend, I chose a long time ago
To live my life in harmony
I was willing to give up those worldly ways.
To leave behind a world of disappointment filled of misery.How does anyone survive this world without his peace?
