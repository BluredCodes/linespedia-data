Shout with Joy
Over all the land

Sing of His glory
In his name

Make His praise full of glory
In His magnificent name- Jesus Christ

All the earth bow down
to sing their praises
they all sing praises under your name

Come,let us rejoice in Him
He rules here forever
Of His power and his glory

AmenHow does man survive on bread alone?
the song in our heart must be sung.
