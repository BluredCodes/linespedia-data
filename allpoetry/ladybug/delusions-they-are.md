---
title: "Delusions they are"
writer: "Ladybug"

you call my name
I melt with each hello
I know only your soul

I don't know your name
or color of hair
if it lays still
or curls between my fingers

I only know your soul
that whispers my name
and tears me away
with the lovely words
that melt my heart

I wish I knew you more
More than the words that play 
in my head and heart
that help me sleep

Awaiting your call
I watch for a new song
I want to know you so well
But I don't even know your name...
