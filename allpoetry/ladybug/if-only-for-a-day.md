If only for a day,
I could give a gift away
that you give to me each day.

Full of hope and promise,
you lift me high
filling my heart of a trusting love.

If only for a day
 I could give this gift away
a gift of love 
to create a world of harmony.

Together, we make a new stand,
to send our love and forgiveness
to each member of our family.

For, we are all God's children
 we need to live together
in a world of peace and harmony. 
If only for a day,
I could give this gift away…
