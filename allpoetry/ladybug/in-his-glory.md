title: "In His Glory"
writer: "Ladybug"

Let heaven and earth 
praise His name
 Of the seas and all that move within them
For God shall save this land
and rebuild the cities of our sin
As people settle in them
 They shall bare children of God's will and reverance
And inherit all the wonders of this land
For those who love His name
 will dwell in His temple of glory.So sad we have to be crushed to be awakened.
