A MEMORY TO US ALL




He came of this earth by fate
He left of this world in destiny
I knew him to have a quiet nature
Until his grandkids came around
Then his heart would melt
As he teased, poked and tickled them

A man of strength, his fight was for peace
A man of simplicity, he loved to tinker with almost anything
A man of comfort, he held us all in his prayers
A godly man who loved to fish
To share with neighbors one and all

A man of vision, he saw a world before him
That gave all mankind a place in the family
He was a Son of God and all were welcome around him

Today he leaves us with his inspiration
To fulfill a plan made long ago…
To give a piece of your heart away
Of each and every day
He knew no stranger, for we were all his brothers
In the kingdom to come, we all live to see.

Although, his name is Howard Ozelle Switzer
I knew him by Uncle Son
People at work nicknamed him as Homer
See he knew his place from the very start
How to welcome all those around him

Amazing how such a simple man can affect so many…
But then again, look how simple Jesus was as a man.have you ever had a relative be your personal hero?
