---
title: "Raining A Storm"
writer: "Ladybug"
slug: "raining-a-storm"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/52428-Raining-A-Storm-by-Ladybug"
---

the rain 
pitters
the lightening
storms
a crash 
of thunder
awakens
my spirit
to the inner
core

music 
to my ears
thunder
to my 
fears

the earth
is quenched
of 
nutrients
needed

moisture
for the leaves
ground
and 
spirit
so thirsty

carry me 
to 
a newness
in awareness
be my guide
for lost
in a storm
I am
