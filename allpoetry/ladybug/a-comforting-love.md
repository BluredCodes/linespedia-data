---
title: "A Comforting Love"
writer: "Ladybug"
slug: "a-comforting-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

May I see you in the morning as a shining star
Let me hear your words of kindness, to carry in my heart.
Let my voice be a beacon of light
To the ears that I may speak unto today.

Let my gift of your love touch someone today.
Let my hands heal a broken heart,
To mend a wound only love can see.
Today let me be that gift for thee

You gave me a heart to be merry
A joyful song to be sung
That only a sinner knows the words of this tune.
I will carry your word of comfort and praise
For my comfort can only be found in you.

As my life breathes anew
Glory to the highest to the Lord up above.
I give you my life, I give you my all.
Please use me as a vessel to tell a sweet story
Of the testament of your sweet glory.

by: Tamara Jo Baker
