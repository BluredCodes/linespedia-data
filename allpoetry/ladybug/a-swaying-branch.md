title: "A Swaying Branch"

the leaf
is but a petal of green
the branch
an arm of arbor

attached together
in unity
to feed only one
the Father

a tree is as good
as its leaves 
in lightning
and wind

we sway
to and fro
only to grow more


We are all a piece of the tree
In a forest of harmony
Unity brings new growth
as division separates the body of One
