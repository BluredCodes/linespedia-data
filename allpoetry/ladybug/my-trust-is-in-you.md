title: "My trust is in You"
writer: "Ladybug"

to you my Lord
I lift my soul
I place my trust
to show me a better place

my hope is You
show me your way
guide me in truth
in all my days

I am One with your love
you are my salvation
rescue me dear One

my broken spirit
my heart cries out
guide me in truth
in all my days
my heart is with You

my hope is in You
show me your way
guide me in truth
in all my daysthis is a song by Third Day
