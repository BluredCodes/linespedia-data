---
title: "Saying goodbye."
writer: "angel of darkness"
slug: "saying-goodbye"
source: "AllPoetry"

You have only gone a few days,
And already I miss you so much.
The thought that I will never see you again,
Kills me inside.
I love you so much,
And now I have lost you,
Forever.
I know that it was all for the best,
And that there was no need to have you suffer,
But it still hurts,
To know that I will never be able cuddle you again.
You made everything seem so much better,
Than they were.
To see you looking up at me,
As if I was someone special,
Meant the world to me.
I love you,
And I knew everyday of your life that you loved me too,
That's why it hurts me so much inside,
To know that you’re not here with me,
And that you will never be again.
So I hope that whereever you are, 
That you'll remember me,
Because I'll remember you,
For as long as I live.I wrote this for Jake, who I loved with all my heart.
