---
title: "Man's Want"
writer: "angel of darkness"
slug: "mans-want"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/18850-Mans-Want-by-angel-of-darkness"
---

You eat,You sleep,You conquer,You destroy.You slay,You Create,You see,You destroy.Man's lust for complete power,Man's hunger for control,Man's want and desire for what he cannot have.The desire to know all,To see all,To remember all.The passion to have people call out your name,And see you as a God.The hatred you have toward's others like yourself.The dream man has that mustn't ever be answered.The thirst power, envy, and desire.The dream of satisfying his every want.
