Dark to dark,
Dust to dust,
We come to day to say goodbye,
To a soul that no longer exist with us.
Though we loved them in life,
We must say goodbye to them in death.
	
So Goodbye,
Let your soul,
Be at rest,
We love you and will till our dying days,
And we can’t wait till the day that we will be altogether once again.
So rest your sweet eyes my love,
For we will meet again,
Someday.
