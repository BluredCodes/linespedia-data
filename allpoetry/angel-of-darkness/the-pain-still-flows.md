---
title: "The pain still flows"
writer: "angel of darkness"
slug: "the-pain-still-flows"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

The anger flows through me like lightning,
The pain I feel is like a deep wound that can never be repaired,
My heart is bleeding like a river.
No one is here to help me anymore,
I start to feel less and less,
And the life that used to flow through me is no more.
I can’t love again,
I won’t let anyone ever love me again,
The way I used to be loved by you.
You were my life,
And now you’re my death.
Please don’t give me your sympathy,
Don’t try to give me your love,
For you’ve hurt me so deeply already,
Just leave me alone,
