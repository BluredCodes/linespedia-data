---
title: "The one you love"
writer: "angel of darkness"
slug: "the-one-you-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/38744-The-one-you-love-by-angel-of-darkness"
---

You watch the one you love give their heart away,
You wish and pray 
That someday they would look at you that way.
But you know in your heart and soul,
That it will never be.
Yet you wonder when it’s going to be your turn 
To see the beauty that love has to give.
You look at them,
And you know that it’s just a fairy tale romance,
You hope they live happily ever after,
Even if you can't.
