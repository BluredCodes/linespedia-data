---
title: "A Wish"
writer: "angel of darkness"
slug: "a-wish"
source: "AllPoetry"

There is a search in my life to find what I can't have.To reach for what I'm not tall enough to have.I search with all my heart to find the truth,When I'm not ready for it.I'm a smart child,Who's lost in the journey of life,Although I appear to be dumb.I fight with all my heart,Even when I can't win.I wish for what I need,I wish to be strong in this world,For I can't handle being alone,And made fun of,By the people I care about.I want to know where and what,I'm doing wit hout failure.To reach forth and be liked by everyone,For I'm no different,I'm like you lost in the never ending battle,To find truth,Justice and happiness.
