---
title: "My Little Hope"
writer: "angel of darkness"

For years I've been running,Running from my feelings,Running from my heart and soul,Running from friends and family,Running from joy and happiness,Running from love,Running from peace,And running from myself.For I had this fear that if they ever caught up,With me that I would be lost like I once was,But lately I have been slowing down.Because my true friends,  Have been telling me that I'm fine just the way I am,And if I would let myself see it that may stop running.And I might become everything I ever wanted to become.No more fairy tales,No more lies,No more fear,No more pain,No more hurting,No more crying,No more loneliness,No more sadness,No more running!For once I hope their right,Because that's all I ever wanted.
