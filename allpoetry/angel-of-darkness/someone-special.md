---
title: "Someone special"
writer: "angel of darkness"

I see you sitting there so calm,
I want to touch you but I'm too afraid to move,
Your eyes sparkle in the light,
I watch your mouth moves when you think,
I dream about you night and day,
I know that you will never want me.
Yet I still have hope that my dreams will come true
And we'll be together always.
I dream that you'll holding me tight around my
waist,
And giving me sweet and gentle kisses.
But I know that they are still just dreams.
I know that I'm all alone,
Without any one to call my own.
I cry,
Because I the truth is that,
That I will be this way for a long time.
So I keep the memories of my dreams close to my heart,
And they will stay there,
For as long as I need them to.
