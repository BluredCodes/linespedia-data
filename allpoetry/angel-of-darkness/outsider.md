---
title: "Outsider"
writer: "angel of darkness"
slug: "outsider"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/38737-Outsider-by-angel-of-darkness"

I see people acting like fools,
I hear people laughing at a pointless joke,
I stand on the outside too afraid to come in.
I walk around but there is no way in,
I sense the calmness in the air,
I smell the bitter sweet of success,
I can't help feel so left out.
