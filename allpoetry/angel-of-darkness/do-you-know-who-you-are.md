---
title: "Do you know who you are?"
writer: "angel of darkness"
slug: "do-you-know-who-you-are"
source: "AllPoetry"

In a world so full of anger and hatred,
Sometimes it’s hard to tell who’s the good guy.
I don’t think that one truly honest person can ever say 
That they can tell who they can trust and who they can’t,
Because in a world and society such as ours,
You really can’t tell now a day’s.
And if you think that you can always trust in yourself,
Because you would always know what’s best.
Well think again, 
You’re wrong.
You can’t trust yourself to do the right thing 
Because you’re human you don’t always do the right thing,
Sometimes you do something’s that are wrong but with good intentions.
Then a person you trusted will do some horrible things, 
To get back at you 
For a reason that you may not ever understand,
So you destroy your friendship the person,
And you start a war that 
Will not end until the bigger man 
Tries to mend the fence and apologize.
Because that’s the right thing to do,
So you may think that you’re a good and trusting person,
But the thing is that you truly don’t know,
Until someone does something to hurt you.I want to fix a friendship I ruined but too afraid to make the first move. And the person knows who they are.
