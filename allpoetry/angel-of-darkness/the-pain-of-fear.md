---
title: "The pain of fear"
writer: "angel of darkness"
slug: "the-pain-of-fear"

You ask yourself a question when you go to sleep.
What pain will I have to face today.
Will I ever awake from this nightmare we call a life?
No, for if we ever awake, we will see pain and destruction we never knew existed.
You sway yourself back and forth trying to try to awake.
You scream, 'Let me wake up! To see the destruction, I will have to see!'
But yet you sleep.

You see the joy some people have.
And you want to destroy it.
You think to yourself death to joy and happiness!
Death to the world!
You hate everything!

It all began by being different.
By being yourself.
People destroying your hopes and dreams,
So you became tough.
You want to die.

You have now became what you hate.
You have now became me.
You have now open the doors to a place you fear.
Welcome to earth!
Welcome to HELL!!
