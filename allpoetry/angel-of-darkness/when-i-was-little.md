---
title: "When I was little"
writer: "angel of darkness"

When I was little I used to wish and pray for the
same thing,
To be normal,
To be happy,
To be liked,
By myself and others.
But after a while I started to face the truth.
That I'm the way I am and nothings ever going to
change that.
I lost all hope,
I felt like I lost everything.
Then you came along and started picking up all those
pieces that I thought that I had lost forever.
You gave me something to question not blame,
You gave me strength that I never knew I had.
You gave me so much,
And all I can say is Thank You.
Some how it doesn't seem to be enough.
