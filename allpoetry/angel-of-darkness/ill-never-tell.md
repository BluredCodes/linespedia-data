---
title: "I'll never tell"
writer: "angel of darkness"
slug: "ill-never-tell"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/39916-Ill-never-tell-by-angel-of-darkness"
---

You see me in your dreams,
Am I really there?
Will you ever meet me?
You know that I'm important,
But you don't know why.
You hear my voice, 
Yet you still don't know me.
I am the faceless partner of your future,
And yet you still don't know me.
I drive you insane,
I fill your mind with doubt and shame.
I am the person you'll love,
And yet you still don't know me.
I am here,
I am there,
I am everywhere.
I am inside of you,
I am apart of who you are,
And yet you still don't know me.
You lust for me,
You desire to feel me, 
You want me,
But you don't know why,
And I'll never tell
