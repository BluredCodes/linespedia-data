---
title: "Just Live!"
writer: "angel of darkness"
slug: "just-live"
source: "AllPoetry"

The people who are full of anger, hatred,
As well as the ones who hold in all their pain and unhappiness,
You know the ones who never let anything go.
Well to you all I'd like to say,
That the pain will pass,
Let your anger go.
Find happiness.
Let go of all your hatred!
Enjoy life!
For you just don’t know 
How much time you have on this planet 
Until it’s too late.
So live your life to the fullest,
Each and everyday
And never give up.
JUST LIVE!
