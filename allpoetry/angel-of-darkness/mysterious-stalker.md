I hear foot steps,
In the deep dark snow,
I sense that there was someone behind me.
Too afraid to turn around,
Too afraid to move.
Then I feel a cold breath on the back of my neck.
A cold hand starts to stroke my face,
And warm lips neck.
I start to shiver 
As his hands and lips move lower and lower.
I try to run,
But  his arms are wrapped around me like wipe.
The like some force of nature,
I start being forced down towards the ground.
There’s no escape,
I struggle but it’s no good,
I scream “HELP! HELP! SOMEBODY SAVE ME!”
But no one could hear me.
Finally I gave up and allowed my mysterious stalker to take control.
Cause no one could help me anymore.This happens to thousands of people every day.
