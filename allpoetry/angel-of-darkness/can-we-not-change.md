---
title: "Can we not change?"
writer: "angel of darkness"
slug: "can-we-not-change"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/36170-Can-we-not-change--by-angel-of-darkness"
---

You are trapped,
In a world,
And society that are ruled,
By people who cannot be trusted.
People who live in on there high horses,
And tell us, 
"Little people" what to do.
We elect people, 
Who will make our lives a living hell,
Why?
Are we stupid or something?
Can we not make our own decisions?
Or run our own lives.
Have we "Little people" lost our minds?
Giving people like George Bush,
A man known for having the highest execution rate in all the states,
The power to run the country.
Have we not learned from history,
That these men, 
Aren’t intelligent enough to say “Let’s have Peace, instead of war.”
Are we doomed to make the same mistakes over and over again?
Can we not change?
Do you want your children to be trapped in the same society you are?
Because I don’t.
