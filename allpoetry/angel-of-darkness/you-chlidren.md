You children,
You self absorbed,
Super star wannabe’s.
Mother fuckers,
Who care only about themselves, 
Have you no compassion for humanity?
Do you not understand 
That whatever you do or don’t do 
Can and will affect others around you?
I can’t stand you’re self righteous attitude,
Or your belief that you’re always right.
I hate you,
I want you and all like you to leave me alone.
So I’m not perfect,
So I’m not always right,
But my imperfections is what make me human,
Maybe same day you should look at yours. 
You insult me behind my back,
And you thought that I didn’t hear did you.
I know my status unlike you,
And I respect all people though in different ways.
You all act like children,
Do whatever you want,
Never thinking of others,
And treating those who are different like dirt.
You do nothing because you think that everyone else should do you work,
Well you know what I'm tired of covering your ass,
For once in your life do you own work,
And leave me the hell alone.
You blame me,
You single me out,
You point and laugh at me,
And you tear me down every chance you get.
I’m tired,
I have my own life,
And my own problems,
I don’t need you adding to them.
I need a break from life,
And especially you!
I want to be left to my own devices.
I want a break.
I want children like you to grow up and leave me alone.
No one ever said that life would be fair or easy,
And it’s about fuckin time you learned that.
So for goodness sake let me be alone,
Treat others fair,
And stop acting like spoiled brats,
And take care of your own shit.
If you can’t do that,
Then stay the hell out of my way.
I’m doing this for my own good,
Because for once in my life I’m tired of taking everyone else’s bullshit,
And I going to start by getting rid of yours.
Compassion for others isn’t a lot to ask,
It’s a simple enough task to do,
So get the hell over yourself,
And teat others with the dignity they deserve.
At least act almost human,
If at all possible.
Grow up and act like an adult,
Because legally you are one,
And frankly it’s about fuckin time,
You all act your age.
In a few years your going to have children,
Just like yourself,
And your going to wonder where you went wrong.
Remember what you were like when you were younger,
And be honest,
You will see that they in fact a younger version of yourself.
I'm not spiteful, angery,
Or trying to be rude,
I'm just speaking my mind and telling you what I see,
And what I see is you throughing you life away,
Acting the way you do.
Your hurting other what are born or unborn,
With your childish acts of stupidity.
Be strong,
Act your age,
And for heavens sake stop being a total jackass to every one you meet,
For if you don't,
I swear that one of these days I will kill you,
This is not a threat,
But a promise I make to you.
Just remember I'll be watching you,
Every move you make,
And every step you take,
I'll be watching you,
So watch you back,
Before I'm on yours.
No threats,
But promises I make to children like you.
