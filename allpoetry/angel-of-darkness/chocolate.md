title: "Chocolate"

Have you ever had chocolate snow?
Or chocolate on your nose?
Have you ever seen chocolate on the floor,
Being licked up by someone 
That you once thought was a bore? 
Have you ever had a chocolate dream?
Or chocolate ice cream?
Have you ever had a chocolate wet dream?
Or thought of pouring chocolate on someone who is mean?
Have you ever thought about chocolate on the floor,
Chocolate on the walls,
Chocolate on your friends,
Chocolate underwear?
Just image that melting on you at school,
Boy would your face be red.
Just image a world of chocolate,
But I guess then everyone would want a world of
CARROTS!
