---
title: "He's in love with you."
writer: "angel of darkness"

I see he's in love with you instead of me,
I have loved him for so long,
Then you come along and take him away from me.
Every time I got the courage to tell him the truth,
People like you come along,
And take him from me,
All I can do now is be a friend,
When he is in need of me.
I love him with all my heart,
And I hope you can take care of him.
I love him,
And I never want to see him hurt,
So take care of him,
Or else I will.
