---
title: "Spare me."
writer: "angel of darkness"
slug: "spare-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

Spare me your expectations,I'm not you,I have a mind and spirit of my own.People tell me that you care about me,Because you care what I do or what happens to me.But I can't believe that, Because every time I turn around your down my throat about something.You use my problems and failures against me.How can you care about me if you'rere always trying to hurt me?So I'm not normal,So I'm not beautiful, popular, strong, or talented,But I try.I don't deserve your verbal abuse.So I'm different,I still have feelings.We're family.You have the same blood flowing through your veins, as do I.In our differences we should find strength,Not ridicule.We should rely on each other as friends,Not as common enemies.I don't deserve your punishment for things I can't control.I deserve your love and respect nothing else.
