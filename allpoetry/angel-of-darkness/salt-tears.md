---
title: "Salt Tears"
writer: "angel of darkness"

A girl sits on a hill,With her head in her hands,While salt tears fall from her eyes.She cries for she knows,She will never be accepted for being herself.She knows the truth about man's journey for achievement,For what he mustn't have.She understands that love isn't real,That true strength is created by people, Who have to fight for everything.She cries,And cries,And still cries the more,Because she knows That no matter how hard she triesTo fight  against the forces that keep her down,No matter how strong she tries to be,That she will still lose.
