title: "Dream"
writer: "angel of darkness"

I had a dream last night that I almost died,
I couldn’t see or hear,
What was going on around me.
The only thing I knew was that I felt a deep wound.
Nothing could stop the pain,
I felt like I wanted to die,
I could feel the pain getting worst,
And I knew that there was nothing 
That could be done to end the pain.
Then I woke up,
In a cold sweat,
And all was well,
No more dream,
No more pain,
It was all over,
Or was it?It's about a dream I once had.
