title: "To the ones I love."

To the people I love I want to say, 
I’m Sorry for everything that I have ever done to hurt you.
I didn’t mean any of the harsh things that I have ever done to cause you pain.
I love you all so much,
I never meant to hurt you,
I only ever want to protect you all from me!
So I wrote this as a way to apologize for everything I have ever done,
Or will do in the future.
I’m very sorry for hurting you,
And I’ll find a way to regain your trust and respect again.
