title: "Big deal"
writer: "angel of darkness"

So you think 
That you are better than me.
Do you really think that,
It's true?
So you say that you’re the top of your class?
You can answer any question a teacher can give you.
And that you fixed the budget for the school board.
SO WHAT!
You know what I can do.
I'm special in my own way.
Can you figure out 
How many licks it takes to get to the center of a tootsie pop?
And I know how long I can chew a piece a gum 
Before it starts to lose it's flavor?
Can you do that? 
WELL I CAN
SO THERE.
I may have done so pretty stupid things in my life,
Like licking that metal pole last winter,
But from what I figure
that's what makes me perfect 
Just the way I am.
SO THERE IS NO WAY IN HELL YOUR BETTER THAN 
ME AT ALL!
You can think what you wish of me,
Because I really don't give a damn any more.
You need to get a life,
And leave mine the hell alone.This is to all those people who think that they are better than me. I would just like to say
WHEN HELL FREEZES OVER!
Thank you.
