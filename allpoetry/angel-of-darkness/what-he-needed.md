---
title: "What he needed."
writer: "angel of darkness"

I am alone,
In the battle for my own soul.
I act like I lost my best friend,
Because I did.
He meant so much to me,
He loved me,
Even when I was being a total ass,
Which was pretty often.
He gave me hope for a better day,
When I believed that none could be found.
I gained strength in the love,
He had and gave to me.
He was all ways there for me, 
When I needed him.
And he helped me stay together when me grandfather died.
He had been here,
For the last fifteen years of my life,
And he gave me hope,
Strength,
Purpose,
Joy,
Happiness,
Understanding,
Protection,
And most importantly love.
He gave and gave,
Every day of his life,
And I loved him all the more for it.
But sadly enough when it came time to let him go,
And give him what you needed most,
Peace,
I had so much trouble doing it,
Because I loved him,
And I didn’t want to let him go.
But I knew it had to be done,
So I will hold the memories I have of you,
Till my dying day.
