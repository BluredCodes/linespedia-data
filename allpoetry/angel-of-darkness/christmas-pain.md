---
title: "Christmas Pain"
writer: "angel of darkness"
slug: "christmas-pain"

I see the snow on the ground outside,
I hear those Christmas carols,
And all I feel is pain and sadness,
All I can think about is of my grandfather and how I
had him around this time last year.
I feel so betrayed,
Because he used to say to me once that he would never leave me!
And then he goes and dies on us.
I feel nothing but anger,
I hate the world,
And my grandfather.
I feel like I'm the angle of death,
Every night,
I pray to God to take me next,
But I'm left here to suffer.
I know that I have a lot of reasons to live,
But I have no one to share my success and failure
with,
No one to love and be loved by,
There's no one in my life,
Anymore.
