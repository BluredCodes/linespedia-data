title: "Life"
writer: "angel of darkness"

I feel the pain, 
That my so-called friends have given me.
I sense the happiness that everyone else has,
And I want to join in,
But I still feel the scars that they have given me.
Their blood drips from my face,
They give me nothing but pain.
I feel nothing but pain, confusion,
And depression.
I guess that’s how life goes.
