I'm in Constance darkness,
                                         I feel nothing,
                                         I see nothing,
                                          I am nothing.
                                   I'm the eternal darkness.
                      You walk around in this cold, dark place and you will
                                      never want to leave.
                        You feel strength that you never knew you had.
                       But at the same time you want to curl up and die.
                        You open your eyes to see pain and destruction,
                         And you will never be able to shut them again.
                               You see the world as one big War.
                            You see My darkness hovering over you,
                                But there is nothing you can do.
                      You let me in once and there's nothing that is ever
                           going to take the eternal darkness away.
