---
title: "Why?"
writer: "angel of darkness"

Tell me why you’re moving away from my heart,
It’s like I’m missing a huge part of myself.
Why can’t I live without your tender touch,
I feel like I need you so much.
Why can’t I move on with my life,
Without you by my side,
Holding me tight all through the night.
The only question I ask is 
Why?
