---
title: "Breathless"
writer: "Theater Vixon"
slug: "breathless"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/5125-Breathless-by-Theater-Vixon"
---

i can feel the way you look at me

no words need to be spoken

the heat

the passion

i can feel you near me

closer than ever before

our bond was never this strong

never this close

the touch of your hand

the feel of your skin

the beat of your heart

it all makes me feel

............

breathless.
