---
title: "At A Distance"
writer: "Theater Vixon"
slug: "at-a-distance"
source: "AllPoetry"

When I look at you,

I know it could never be.

For it is not the same way

that you look at me.

Although I admire you

and you are all I see,

when you look at me,

I know it could never be.

At a distance I love you,

with all of my heart.

And in my mind,

we could never part.

But it is all in my head.

The words never shall be said.

Why can't you see

how much you mean to me.

How much I love you so true.

This feeling I have solely for you,

is a feeling that for me, is new.

And though I will show persistance, 

I can only love you from a distance.
