blood runs thick.



Blood runs cold.



tears flow like rain.



tears flow from my eyes



like rivers from the mountains.



i am alone now,



forever alone,



with no one to love,



or love me



for all eternity.



my love



my one true love



forever gone.



forever untouched.



good bye my love.



forever.........This was written, shortly after Sept. 11, for the families of all the victims.
