title: "lost"
writer: "starchick"
slug: "lost"

help me
i am drowning
within myself
please help
i need it
i know i do
do you care?
do you give a shit?
do you give a rat's ass?
why do i have to 
write
these thoughts?
i should be speaking them
to someone who gives a shit
not drawing chicken scratches
on a piece of candy wrapper
using a borrowed pen
