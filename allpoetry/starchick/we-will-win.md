title: "We Will Win"
writer: "starchick"

Today was a nasty day,
And everyone is trying to say,
That it was not a venial sin,
To let these people in,
To let them in our home,
To let them destroy our home,
But they will not destroy our hearts,
They will not find a heart,
Too weak to defend,
This is not the end.written on September 11, 2001
