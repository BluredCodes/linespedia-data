---
title: "Liar"
writer: "trackchic337"
slug: "liar"

I may look happy
I might appear fine
I may seem cheerful:
But I lie
Please don't beleive me.
On the inside I cry.

When a smile lights my face
A tear drops to the floor of my hollow heart
When I lock my door
When there's no one around to hear
I cry

I may make straight A's
I might have a few talents
I may be looked up to:
But I lie
Please don't beleive me.
On the inside I cry.

When someone tells me I'm gifted
A voice whispers I'm no good
When everyone sees what I do
When no one cares about who I am
I cry

I may be somewhat popular
I might have a guy now and then
I may not be the ugliest
But I lie
Please don't beleive me.
On the inside I cry.

When someone says I'm lucky
I silently wish for anything but this
When my laugh is empty
When I pull the phone plug
I cry

I may put on a smile
I might appear strong
I may seem perfect outside:
But I lie
Please don't beleive me.
On the inside I cry.
