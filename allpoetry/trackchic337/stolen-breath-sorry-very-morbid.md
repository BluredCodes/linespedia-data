title: "Stolen Breath (sorry, very morbid)"

The world distorted and misshapen
The flowers transformed to knives
The sunshine melted to fire
People are evolved to animals
Wolves, foaming mad and ferocious
Grass choked by thorns and
Replaced with nails and blades
The clouds mourn, their tears are blood
Blue skies drink, stained scarlet
The earth gapes to swallow whole
The wind inhales to suck life
Shadows dance to an unknown tune
That of drunkenness and death
Fire is devouring, fearlessly leading
The blood-thirsty dance, it's author
The rustle of cares melts into a wail of despair
Insects infest to eat from within
A blade of denial is thrust into flesh
Swims in its want and desire, is plunged
Into a pool of flesh to meet it's rotted target
Hands are removed from the feeding
Of what is only alive
Knuckles white, ebony bones protruding forth
To contrast themselves with the blood, black as night
Your mouth is thrown open as you realize
You try to scream, but silent, and only vomit answers
The termites swarm and spread to your eyes
And blacken out sooner
What I destined to be ashes
You realize those hands are your own
The knife, fed and satisfied
Slowly breathes in its stolen life
