Socialization mirrors vanity
Conversation hauteur
Interaction deception
Spoken word prevarication

To indulge in ruinous gossip
To feast on others’ misfortune
To drunken oneself in shame
Is to wallow in conversation

To exhibit a jaundiced façade
To camouflage one’s true colors
To display a gaudily ornate masquerade 
Is to engage in superficial dialogue

To display disdain and loftiness
To self-seekingly draw attention
To lift oneself up in prideful acclamations
Is to be participant in social gathering

Confabulation reflects conceit
Discussion disdain 
Communication falsehood
Socialization futilityI believe I was upset with a few friends when I wrote this. I am actually far from unsociable!
