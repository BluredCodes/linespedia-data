title: "Apart"

You are there,I am here.We both should have,a little fear.We know so much,I hope we never lose touch.We want it to last,even though it has been an on-line past.Trying to anew,a relationship between us two.One that can hug and hold,listen and be told.How nice it will be,when it is all real!btk
