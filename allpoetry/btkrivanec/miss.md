I miss you,I only want to able to kiss you.We are far apart,I only want to be close to your heart.We have had our differences through the year,after it all I wish you were near.My love for you is real,Sometimes I don't understand how this is the way I feel.I want to pack my things and goto you,care for you,even if you have a cold or the flu.You and Katelynn,till the end.btk
