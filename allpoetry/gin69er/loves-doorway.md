I thought that if I loved you
That it would be enough
I believed the words you said
But now I call your bluff
For you have uttered 'I love you'
More than once my dear
Well now's the time to prove it
Your failure's what I fear
You whisper that 'I love you'
And then you turn and walk away
Your actions tell me volumes
More than words can say
So if you TRULY love me
Then walk back through that door
You'll be safe in my loving embrace
In my arms for evermore
But if my love for you is unwanted
Please keep the doorway shut
My heart can't take this indecision
I've given my all, should I just give up?
The ball is in your court
It's all up to you
The doorway is just there
But will you my love come through?
