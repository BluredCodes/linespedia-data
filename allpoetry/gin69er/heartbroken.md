What good is it to love someone
When they'll only break your heart
Along with all the promises
They made you in the start
You suddenly find the bed of roses
Has turned to a bed of thorns
That the love that you once treasured
Has turned hateful, full of scorn
Why open your heart to all the pain
For a chance of happiness
When it'll only end in misery
Heartbroken loneliness…
