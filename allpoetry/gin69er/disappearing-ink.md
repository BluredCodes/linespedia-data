I lay here alone on your side of the bed
Your scent still on your pillow
I cry an ocean of tears for what once was
Rivers of pain in your name
My heart bleeds on crimson sheets
Invisible to all but mostly you
Written in disappearing ink
Just like the vows we took a million years ago….
