Shadows dance across my bedroom wall
Reflected in the glow
They flicker across my naked flesh
Ghostly fingertips caress my skin 
Close my eyes, can almost imagine you alongside me
My fists grab only handfuls of satin sheets
While hot, heavy breath stirs the flame of desire
Making the apparition move in time with my yearning
Indistinct bodies enclosed in love’s grasp
As longing reaches exquisite peaks
With one last sigh the fire is extinguished
Once again alone……with my thoughts of you
