title: "The real me"

All these people think they know me
How can they, when I don’t even know myself
I often think the real me is hidden so deep
Cowering in some dark corner, even I’ve forgotten about
A child, a teen, a woman
Wounded and deceived time and time again
Buried under blankets of pain and indifference
Cowering with fear of being discovered
Yet aching for just one real loving touch
One kiss upon my naked face
A face finally free of all it’s masks
