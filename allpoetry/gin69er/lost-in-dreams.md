Lying in a field of wildflowers
Sun against my face…..

I feel you beside me
Your skin on mine
Your breath on my cheek
I feel your hand in my hair
Your sweet kiss on my lips

Lying in a field of wildflowers
Sun against my face….

Lost in dreams
