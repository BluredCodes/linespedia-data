Your love gave me wings
The freedom to love with abandon
We soared to magnificent heights
Higher than I'd have imagined
Hand in hand we touched the heavens
I had stardust in my hair
Turned to smile at my lover
But you were no longer there
My wings turned to ashes
As I plummetted to earth
I gave you my heart
And this is what I deserve???
