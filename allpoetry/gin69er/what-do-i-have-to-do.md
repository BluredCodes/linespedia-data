title: "What Do I Have To Do?"

I’m standing right in front of you
You’re looking straight through me
I’m looking right into your eyes
Into eyes that do not see
I’m standing right beside you
I whisper in your ear
Talking right into your lobe
Into ears that do not hear
I’m standing right behind you
My toes upon your heels
Touching your neck and shoulders
Touching skin that does not feel
I’m here for you all the time
But I might as well be made of glass
You ignore me and my feelings
You’ve ripped my heart in half
What do I have to do?
So you will notice me
So you will hear and you will touch
You will love and you will see………
