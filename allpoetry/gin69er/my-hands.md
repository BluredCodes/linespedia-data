---
title: "My Hands"
writer: "gin69er"

As I sit here wringing my hands
Feeling oh so alone
I look at the rings upon my fingers
And wonder what went wrong
First was a ring of rubies and gold
My 21st birthday gift
Then engagement & wedding rings
My heart they once did lift
A ring of 4 sapphires & diamonds
One stone for each of our children
No 1 mum is what it reads
That was when our troubles began
Then a ring of garnets and hearts
Six upon the band
One for each of our family
Our life was going grand
I look at another ring you brought
Just to say, 'I love you'
Then you go and turn around
And it's the man I never knew
As I look down at my hands
And see these symbols of supposed love
But all I see chains of gold
Surround me like a glove
For I'm married to a stranger
And I don't like this man
They are nothing but painful reminders
But I can't take them from my hands…
