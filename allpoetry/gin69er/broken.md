title: "Broken?"

our love made me feel invincible
you lifted me to magnificent heights
we soared on love's gilded wings
then... you abandoned me
broken
worthless

a shuddering heap among the ruins
I lingered among the rubble
tending my wounded heart
crying myself to sleep
broken
wretched


gradually the dust has cleared
the sun is peeking through the haze
tentatively I stretch my wings
and I slowly realise

I may be bent...but not broken
