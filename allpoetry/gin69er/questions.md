If you never loved me
If you never cared
If my dream was never
One that we both shared
If you never wanted me
If you never truly believed
Why let me imagine
That you would never leave
For now I sit alone
And wonder what went wrong
Why you’re no longer in my arms
Where I thought that you belonged….
