---
title: "I was wrong"
writer: "gin69er"
slug: "i-was-wrong"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/45091-I-was-wrong-by-gin69er"

..you blew into my life on love's warm breeze...
...caressed me with thoughts and dreams...

..I thought that I could live without your words...
...your heart...
..your love...

...I didnt want to....but I thought I could...

...I was wrong...
