Another night finds me here
Laying between you both….
I turn to him
Forever in his eyes
But then your fingers caress my back….
I turn to gaze at you
Just what is in that dark stare?
Is it love or just reflections from my heart?….
As his hands caress me
I lay on my back
And stare at the ceiling….
You both run your hands along my body
My heart knows not which way to turn……..
