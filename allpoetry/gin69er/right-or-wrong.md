This feels so right
So don't tell me it's wrong
There's no use denying
This is where we belong
Our bodies fit together
Like two halves of a whole
Our hearts beat as one
I love you body & soul
Put my finger to your lips
My hand upon your heart
For what we feel, is oh so real
And we should never part…..
