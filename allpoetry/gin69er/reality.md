---
title: "Reality?"
writer: "gin69er"
slug: "reality"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I came across you the other day
And now I wish that I hadn't
For the memories in my heart
Are so much nicer than reality
To think of what we once had
Felt so warm
So alive
Now you are so cold
So indifferent
It's like we never happened
We never laughed
Never loved
Never shared our deepest thoughts
Just like our hearts never beat as one
Memories are so much nicer than reality
That your love is no longer mine
Perhaps it never was.....
