my heart was swollen with love only to splinter in a million pieces upon the floor...seems cruel to me
it lay in fragments like shattered glass, ground under the heel of your indifference
ripped...torn...laying in a pool of blood
remains of what used to be a vessel filled with love
broken, crushed ....no use to anyone anymore
