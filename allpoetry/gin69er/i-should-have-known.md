---
title: "I should have known"
writer: "gin69er"
slug: "i-should-have-known"
source: "AllPoetry"

how could I have been so wrong
to misread all the signs
misinterpret the words 'I love you'
for I should have known
shouldn't have surrendered my heart
my soul
for your to wipe your feet upon
as you walked out of the door
and out of my life
