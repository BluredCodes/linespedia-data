I know in my head
That I should leave you alone
With my heart on my sleeve
I head for the phone
The sound of your voice
Sets me a-flutter
The words that you whisper
Melt me like butter
When I am with you
I can hardly breathe
I want you for all time
It kills me to leave
For you have your life
And I have mine
But I could never leave you
For our souls are entwined…..
