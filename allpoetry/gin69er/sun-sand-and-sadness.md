---
title: "Sun, sand and sadness"
writer: "gin69er"

I sit and gaze across the horizon
As the sun slowly sinks from view
My vision it turns inward
Replaying well-worn memories of you
Meeting you here one summer many years ago
Walks upon the sun-drenched sand
Lost in the ocean of your eyes
Star-crossed lovers hand in hand
Picnics filled with a feast for two
Swimming in the sea as one
Water lapping, my flesh on fire
Warmed by more than just the sun
Nights spent watching the sun as I do now
Setting the sky on fire
Lying together under a star filled sky
Our bodies consumed with desire
Waking upon the same shoreline
In your arms we watch the sun rise
As light slowly fills the horizon
Reflecting the love found in your eyes
The evening you compared stars to diamonds
As you placed a ring upon my hand
Surrounded by those we love
As we exchanged vows upon the sand
For now I stand alone at the edge of the world
Here to do my last duty as your wife
With one last kiss I scatter your ashes to the wind
As the sun sets on the story of our life….
