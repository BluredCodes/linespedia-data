She sits among the statues
Monuments of love
Reminders of our “dear departed”
Called to home above
She weeps among the statues
Her body shudders as she sighs
She cries unto the heavens
Why, Oh Lord, please why
She bleeds among the statues
Her life it seeps away
He comes to greet her, hold her hand
She begs of him to stay
She dies among the statues
A smile upon her face
As they are now together
And tranquillity takes it’s place….
