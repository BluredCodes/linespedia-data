title: "Storms Of Emotion"

Waves crash against the shore
Lightning illuminates the sky
Sand blown against my body
As it shudders with my sigh
Wind whips up the waves
Of an angry black sea
Rain pelts me like rocks
Even the elements are against me
Thunders echoes through the sky
Wind throws me about
My heart in confusion
Storms of emotion inside & out….
