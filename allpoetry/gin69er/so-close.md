---
title: "So close"
writer: "gin69er"

source: "AllPoetry"

---

...my love, you were so close...
...I could feel your breath upon my skin...
...I run towards you, yet you were still out of reach...
...receding faster than I can go...
...my heart is thumping in my chest...
...our fingers almost touch...
...I stop on the brink of the abyss...
...and still you stand there, with that maddening smile...

...so close...

...but yet, so far...
