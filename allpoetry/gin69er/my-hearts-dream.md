title: "My heart's dream"

my heart dared to dream that my soul would be complete...yet I sit surrounded by the shreds of a love, a life...thought of, dreamed of, almost grasped ...yet not to be

a withered, dusty receptacle filled with cobwebs and memories of a life destined not to exist anywhere except in useless dreams

I dreamed of an angel that touched my heart, my soul
I woke alone drenched in memories, 
shadows flitting through my vision
ghostly fingertips leaving their trail upon my flesh...my soul

yet, my heart dared to dream....
