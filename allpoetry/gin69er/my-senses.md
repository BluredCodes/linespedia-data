---
title: "My Senses"
writer: "gin69er"

The taste of you still upon my lips
Your touch upon my skin
Your smell still wafts around my nose
As the sight of you grows dim
Your voice still echoes in my brain
Whispers of “I love you”
My senses are still reeling
Now that you and I are through……
