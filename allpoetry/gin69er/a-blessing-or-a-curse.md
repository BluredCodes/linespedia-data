---
title: "A blessing or a curse"

I am going crazy, I can’t log on you see
Can’t say ‘hello’ to my friends – or read & post poetry
So much for modern technology as I sit & twiddle thumbs
Ten times I have been kicked off today, this is REALLY dumb
Get as far as 61 messages, but can’t see who they’re from
I’m going away next week, I want to tell my ‘cyber-mum’
My server can’t help and Telstra is no better
What’s a girl to do when she can’t log on to read her ‘letters’
My greatest worry, well my greatest fear
The bigwigs at Hotmail will make my mail ‘disappear’
Let’s hope this is temporary, don’t want to wait until next week
Want to submit some poetry, keep my juices flowing, so to speak
By the time you read this, of course the problems fixed
Internet and computers, well my opinions mixed
Sometimes they are a blessing, at other times a curse
But to be without ‘my babies’ I know which one is worse….
