To love and to honour
Until death us do part
That was our promise
Right from the start
What did we know
We were too young
To know that forever
Is much too long
Some promises are made
Just to be broken
They were just words
Some small little token
It’s not even worth it
To give it a try
To stay together
Until one of us die
So let’s cut our losses
Let’s call it a day
I want to leave
And go my own way
