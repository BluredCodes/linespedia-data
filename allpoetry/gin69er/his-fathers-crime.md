title: "His Father's Crime"

As I sat there frozen, to what she had said
Blood pumping loudly throughout my head
When she said what my father had said to her
About his childhood & how much he hurt
That she didn’t believe him, he must have been lying
Took all of my strength, not to start crying
For she liked my grandfather, “he was a great guy”
For she was upset on the day that he died
I thought it was all written over my face
My hurt and my anger and once what took place
For I’ve never told them, they wouldn’t believe me
But when I heard what my dad said, I wanted to flee
All through the years, when she has praised him
I just bit my tongue and tried not to scream
For he was NOT a nice guy, at least not to me
He used and abused my tiny body
Now all this came out after my dad’s “episode”
When his life’s burdens were to heavy a load
He tried to kill himself, he said over money
But now he made these allusions, I start to worry
Was his childhood as horrid as mine?
Was he also a victim of his father’s crime?
