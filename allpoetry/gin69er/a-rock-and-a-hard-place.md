What can you do
When there’s nothing left to say
When my heart has turned to stone 
What is the point, why should I stay
For I know that I’m not happy
The kid’s and he aren’t too
Why do we keep pretending
That this marriage isn’t through
For I can no longer look at him
With tender love in my eyes
But instead dislike and disgust
I can no longer hide
Our “happy” moments
Are few and far between
I’m caught between a rock and a hard place
Between reality and dreams…..
