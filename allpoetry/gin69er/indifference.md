I sit and wonder what went wrong
Never knowing what was truly in your heart
Denying all the feelings my head sent my heart
I truly believed all the words you said
Foolishly, blindly believed
Forever was on my lips but not in your heart
Easily led to think that you loved me
Relying on you to tell me the truth
Enjoying the feeling of your pseudo-love washing over me
Never realising the heartache to come
Crying myself to sleep at night
Endlessly banging my head against your wall of …
          indifference
