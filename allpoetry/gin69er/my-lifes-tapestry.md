---
title: "My life's tapestry"
writer: "gin69er"
slug: "my-lifes-tapestry"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/43166-My-lifes-tapestry-by-gin69er"

events
places
people
each a thread
all interwoven
sewn with memories
of love
of pain
of happiness
existence
tears blur the edges
yet a masterpiece remains
.....the tapestry of my life
