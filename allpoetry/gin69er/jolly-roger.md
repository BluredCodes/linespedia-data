His name is Jolly Roger
He's always lots of fun
But he can never let his guard down
Without his trusty rum
A bottle by his side
It is his greatest friend
A sad shadow of a man
Keep drinking to the end
For we all can see his future
In the bottom of his glass
'But I'm only having fun'
He staggers as he laughs
He thinks he is invincible
As he gets behind the wheel
Someone trys to stop him
With a chuckle his tyres squeal
He takes off at breakneck speed
See his brakelights in the dark
His name was Jolly Roger
And that's the saddest part...
