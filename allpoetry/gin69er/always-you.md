Blood red petals strewn across black satin sheets
Candles flickering in love’s breeze
Incense fills the air with a sweet aroma
While wine chills at my bedside
Shadows flickering around the room
Across my naked body
Our favourite song plays softly in the background
All that’s missing is you……….always you
