My eyes are going fuzzy
I've been on this thing all night
Hours spent at my PC
Can't be good for my eyesight

I will log off in a minute
I really, really will

Just want to check out one more poem
Or maybe two or three
Then hop on back to Hotmail
See if someone'll talk to me

I'll log off in a minute
I really, really will

See what's on the Discussion forum
See if someone's submitted a poem
Check out everyone I know
And even a few 'unknown's'

I'll log off in a minute
I really, really will

My eyes are going fuzzy
I've been on this thing all night
Sent a message from above
As I'm 'thrown' from my favourite site

I've logged off ….for now
