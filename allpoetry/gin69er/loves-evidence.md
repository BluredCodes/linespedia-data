Sunset finds us upon the beach
Walking hand in hand
Laughing as you chase me
Trying to roll me in the sand
Water sprays upon our bodies
As the waves crash upon our skin
You draw a heart upon the sand
With our names both drawn within
Then the tide comes aground again
And wipes the heart from upon the shore
Suddenly I am all alone
Love’s evidence no more….
