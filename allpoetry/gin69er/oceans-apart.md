You are a million miles away
In distance, space and time
But I forever clutch the knowledge
That your love, it was once mine
I take out all the photos
Look deep into your searching eyes
Listen to your deep and sexy voice
As yet once again I cry
You feel so very far away
I feel so alone and shudder
Re-read the phrase "I love you"
Words like a hand upon my shoulder
For love is more than just a touch
We are eternal in my heart
In a special place forever more
Though we are "oceans apart"
