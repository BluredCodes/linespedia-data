I gaze at the splendour of your naked body
Taking in every inch of your glory
Wanting to touch you
But knowing I can’t
Each moment exquisite agony

I explore you with my hands
Fingers of imagination
Wanting to touch you
But knowing I can’t
How can I live with this frustration

You touch yourself and I touch me
Two souls linked for a moment in time
I’d smooth back your hair
Wipe the sweat from your brow
As our eyes lock for all eternity….
