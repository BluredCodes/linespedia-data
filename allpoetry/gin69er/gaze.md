---
title: "Gaze"
writer: "gin69er"
slug: "gaze"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

two star crossed lovers
gaze into each others eyes
they're filled with a love so strong
they can no longer hide
are they just fooling themselves
that this is for real
do they dare to hope
dare to trust what they feel
they've both been so hurt before
and they know this is wrong
as they gaze into each others eyes
this is where they belong
