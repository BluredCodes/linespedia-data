title: "Almost"

source: "AllPoetry"

As I lay in bed dreaming of you
I can almost feel you
Almost smell you
Almost touch you
I feel your breath upon my skin
And my name upon your lips

Almost…..

As I lay on the grass thinking of you
I can almost feel your touch upon the breeze
Almost smell you wafting round my nose
Almost touch you
I feel your breath upon my skin
And your name is upon my lips

Almost…..
