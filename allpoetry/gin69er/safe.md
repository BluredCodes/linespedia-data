Come here, sit beside me
Lay your head upon my chest
Be still your beating heart
You're safe now, you can rest
Wrap my arms around your shoulders
Take you in my safe embrace
Stroke your hair so gently
Wipe the tears upon your face
You can trust me with your heart
For I have done the same
Both it & your love are safe with me
And we should feel no shame….
