---
title: "Shards of Wine"
writer: "Professor Gloom"
slug: "shards-of-wine"
source: "AllPoetry"

A wine glass,
Surprise shattering, it breaks,
Blood red stain,
Demise emotions, it makes.

Break demise,
Rendering backwards, in thought,
Blotting dry,
Scattering a way to naught.

Shards surprise,
Thinking as doing, grabs pain,
New wetting,
Bleeding and crying, add stain.

Giving up,
The memories stay, reminds,
Underneath,
A small remnant square, there finds.

Gloom
