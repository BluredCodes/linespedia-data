title: "Disappearing Shadows"

Where goes the shadows
That runs from approaching day,
I hold my sorrows,
In a cold hearts hide away,
When goes the brightness
That displays my expressions,
I long for darkness
To hides sullen emotion;
How long last the light
That lingers me in the bright
Longing for shadows of night.

GloomIn the classical Choka style of Japanese Poetry
