---
title: "Mistress"
writer: "Beastfro"

I would bow before you 
in public and kiss your feet.  
And I would dance naked in church 
to please you.  
Because you make me smile.
When spectered wisps of melody 
flow from instruments captured by metal 
and magic 
I think of you.  
How easily you samba in my head 
until I am a frothy mess of desire 
begging the sky that you were here 
for me to touch 
and kiss 
and lick 
and smell, 
and surround me.  
So often am I captive of your image 
that I have reached bliss while 
alone in a room without moving, 
or breathing, 
just thinking of you.  
Totally in your control.  
You ain’t here 
and you still own me.  
Thank you mistress.  
Thank you.  mistress.
