I went to sleep thinking of you.

I woke up and found myself in some where I never been before. I look around the sand slowly melting in between my toes. I saw how the warm breeze of the water arose into the sky. I felt lonely how I don't have anybody to share the feeling with. I look around and saw a dark shadow figure sitting on a rock. I look closer and believe it was you. I ran as fast as I could, but the closer I got the more you where moving back. The Pain floated above the surface of the water.I just made this one up ^_^
