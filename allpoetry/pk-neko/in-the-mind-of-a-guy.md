title: "In the mind of a Guy"
writer: "P.k neko"

To a guy a girl's heart is a toy.
They like to play around with it and always have it with them.
They like to show it off to their friends.
When they find a new toy they throw away the old one.
Then play around with the new one.
Until they find a new one.
