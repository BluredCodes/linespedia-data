---
title: "Awakened"
writer: "P.k neko"
slug: "awakened"
source: "AllPoetry"

I thought I'll never open my heart to anyone, but you.
I never wanted to...
I knew you were the one, I was meant to be with.
I'll no longer be blind
I have only felt pain, now all of the memory is gone.
