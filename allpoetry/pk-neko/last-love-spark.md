---
title: "Last Love Spark"
writer: "P.k neko"
slug: "last-love-spark"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/23077-Last-Love-Spark-by-P.k-neko"
---

Is it true that you know me really or are you saying that.
If you really know me, you would know, I like you and care about you.
From the day we meet until the day you left.
I cried night and day.
I was sad every time I heard your name.
I never would take a second back when we were together.
I would do anything to keep that last love spark.
