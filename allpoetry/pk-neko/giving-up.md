---
title: "Giving up"
writer: "P.k neko"
slug: "giving-up"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/23679-Giving-up-by-P.k-neko"

I look back into the past.
Why did it end so fast?
The closer I got,
The more you back up.
I thought it was love,
but it was only a crush.
I'm sad,
but I have to give you up.
