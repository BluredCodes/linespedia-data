title: "Paperdoll Decays"
writer: "ruined"

So broken
All alone
Watch her bleed
Dont' be afraid.
Mouth sore
Kissed by a star
Your all I want
Your all I need
Now is never
and never is now
Nothing but a paperdoll
Can you feel her pain?
Just look into her eyes
They are paralizing
Do you see her eyes bleed?
All swollen and dead
Such a beautiful bruise
Such a infectious scab
She's so pretty
As her body burns
Just another...
Paperdoll Decay
