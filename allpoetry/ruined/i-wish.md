Sometimes I wish
You would just die
You never did nothing for me
But cause pain and grief
Only one way out
Carve upon relief
Your name graced in my arm 
Forever
It never got any better
All I wanted was
To be that SPECIAL GIRL
You just fucked with ne
Like I was your little toy doll
But thats not going to happen again
Because it's over forever
No apologies
No more excuses
Because I don't care
Fuck your opinion 
And everything you stand for
