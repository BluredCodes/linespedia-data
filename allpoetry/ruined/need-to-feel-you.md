title: "Need to feel you"
writer: "ruined"

I need to feel you
Next to me

I need your touch
To help me breath

I want you to hold me
And never let go

You've helped me find
The light back to my soul

You are my weakness
You are my strenght

You are the hope
You are the comfort

You are the dream
That keeps me dreaming

You are the love
That keeps me alive

And love is what I feel for you
Each and ever single day

And it's a love I can't control
Or hide away another day.
