Watch me burn
I'm nothing
A perfect nothing
Star Cunt Star Cunt
Watch me fall
Deep into a dark hole
Star Cunt Star Cunt
Watch me bite
Bite into your beautiful skin
Your warm flesh
So plentiful
I'm the cannibal you come to fear
Star Cunt Star Cunt
Watch me bleed
Razor blade kisses
Is how I die
Star Cunt Star Cunt
Watch me die
Open sores
Infected dreams
Star Cunt Star Cunt
I can't change
Right here forever 
This is where I shall die

Star Cunt Star Cunt
Watch me burn
Star Cunt Star Cunt
Watch me fall
Star Cunt Star Cunt
Watch me bite
Star Cunt Star Cunt
Watch me bleed
Star Cunt Star Cunt
Watch me die
Star Cunt Star Cunt
I can't change
Right here forever
Is where I shall die...
