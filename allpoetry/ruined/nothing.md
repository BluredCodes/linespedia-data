I guess Im nothing
nothing at all to you
It's not alright
Cuz you've killed my soul
I cannot believe you
My life is ablivious
Take a look inside of me
look right thru me
What do you see?
Nothing
Nothing because you broke my soul
I know it's not over
But you've gone to far
Ruin my life
With lies and abuse
I've finally come to realize
That you dont even care
Leave
This is the end
It's all done
I wasn't good enought for you huh?
I'll never be
So i'll just leave
