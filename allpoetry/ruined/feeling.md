Now that I've got your attention
Now that your listening
I have to tell you how I feel
I can't hold it back anymore
I just have to tell you that...
You are everything
You are wonderful
The way you make me feel is
~Amazing~
You have touched my soul
In such a special way
That I just can't explain.
All the time that it takes
to fall in love
I'm glad I've spent it on you
I want you to know I love you
And your always in my mind
Your the center in my life
My shining star in the night sky
What more can I say?
Then I love you more and more
With each waking day.
