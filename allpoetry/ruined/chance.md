She's got a beautiful smile
Hides away the tears that she'd cried

She's been waiting for her chance
To find one true romance

She wants to be herself
But hides the things she fears the most

She wants to make it last
And take just one chance

She needs affection in her life
Instead of feeling only rejection

Your where she wants to be
Wrapped inside your arms is where she feels free
