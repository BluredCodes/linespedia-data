I love cherry cola
It keeps my alive
If you don't like it
Then why don't you just go die?!
Cherry cola is so yummy in my tummy
Get your own or i'll smack your bummy
I love cherry cola
I think you should to
It's so wonderful
Just like you!
Oh cherry cola
Your so great
I love the way you taste
You can never be replaced!
Cherry cola I love you
I think everyone should too
If you don't like it
I'll get josh to shoot you!!
