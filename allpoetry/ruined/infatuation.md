I injected a drug into you known as love
Clutch your fist and feel the blood gust
You have no fucking choice so just enjoy this lust
I don't give a shit, Just lay down. Never leave me
Keep in mind Im not the one who was so naive
Good riddence to the heart ache
Why didn't you just believe me?

FUCK

My heart feels NUMB now that your GONE
I know im not PERFECT
Now I can't hide the GUILT
I had to KILL YOU
and the DARK INSIDE YOU

I hear your cries, can see your fear
Slide the blade along your arm
I could never really free you
I hope your dying and fading away
I need to see you in pain
Under your skin you can't hide
But you'll never see, NEVER FUCKING SEE
How much pain you've caused me.
