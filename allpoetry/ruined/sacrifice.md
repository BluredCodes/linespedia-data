Sacrifice my soul
No more relief
HATE ME
Don't worry
Just WATCH ME BLEED
CRUICIFY THIS LITTLE WHORE
It doesn't matter
I can see the anger
My fiend
EXECUTE this SIN
I'll believe you forever
No regret
BLEED FOR ME
Show me your scars
Carve your savior
In your arm
I will KILL YOU
Wait for you
Till no daylight
Nothing will SAVE YOU.
