I seen death today
not feeling
I seen death today
gripping at your veins
I watched you die
Watched you fall
I wish i could help
But you can only help yourself
I want to heal you
make all the pain dissappear
I want to help you
But there's nothing I can do
I can't even look at you
What am I sappote to do?
Can't stand to see you like this
Living off of machines
Swollen body
Damaged dreams
You need to get better
You can survive
We need you with us
In our lives
We'll love you forever
No matter what
You can survive
