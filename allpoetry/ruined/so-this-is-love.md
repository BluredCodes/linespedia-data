title: "So This Is Love"

So this is love?
All the daydreaming
And long awaken nights
Such happiness you bring into my life
All the fulfillment
Of joy and pure bliss
I'm lucky to have you
Don't ever want to lose you
I want to give you my all
All I need is you
Everyday I look into your eyes
And I realize your all I want
I can't imagine what I'd be without you
Or what I'd do
I don't want to be alone
Cuz it's been along time since I've felt this way
And I don't want to let this feeling go
I've learned the full meaning of being in love
By being in love with you
And I love you for that.
