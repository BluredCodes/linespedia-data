---
title: "Gutter Whore"
writer: "ruined"
slug: "gutter-whore"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Little gutter whore
See her cry
Drowned in dreams
No one will save her now
Afraid of dying
Afraid of being ignored
My little gutter whore
Your so ugly
Scars on your face
From being used 
Hide in the shadows
Away from all the evil
Little gutter whore
Such A lonely girl
Face the world unsure
With perfect scars upon your wrists
You will live a sin
Poor little girl with no savior
You are dead within
Rotting away on the inside
She will never learn
How it feels to be loved
She will never be pretty

Sad little gutter whore...
