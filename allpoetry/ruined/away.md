title: "Away"

You took it all away
All away from me
I guess you never knew
The things I'd do to please you
There's nothing left inside of me
Nothing left to say
You left me here all alone
And you wonder why I cry
I must of been no good for you
Is that why you chose not to stay?
It doesn't matter either way
Because you've lied in so many ways
Now I face the world with pain inside
How could I have gone so wrong?
When things seemed to right
It makes no difference
I might as well say Goodnight.
