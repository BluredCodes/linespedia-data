---
title: "Left Standing"
writer: "SugaAngel"
slug: "left-standing"
source: "AllPoetry"

Sirens of screams and cries are heard
as flames of hate and death rise.
The smoke of pain and heartache 
continue to fog our minds eye.
But through this all there is one 
left standing never to fall.
Love continues to never fail as 
it disintegrates the hate and pain
we have all shared. 
Love is left standing to make us strong
once again, never to fall.

11/30/01
Written about the Terrorist Attacks.
