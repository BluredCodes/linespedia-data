This world is dark and dismal, rarely letting a ray of light 
shine through to feed my aching soul, to allow growth.
Somehow you found a way into my world, through the clouds and through the showers of pain. A silver lining you have become to show me the way into a life of love and hope. 
You are my inspiration, giving me hope and strength to take another step towards the discovery of my journey. A completion you have been working on since the day we met. Our hearts intertwining to make one. Without saying a word you have turned my world into a blossoming garden of renewal and tenderness. The cloudy days and dark skies tremble because of the strength you have given me. 
You are my inspiration to continue my journey.

~Jeff 
Jan. 26, 2001
