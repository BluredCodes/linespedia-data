Friends? What does that mean? Someone that is there for you, 
good or bad. A person that shares special memories
with you. Someone that talks, laughs and spends time 
with you. A person that considers their relationship 
with you as a cherished, full of laughter and good 
times relationship, that will never end. These things are 
included in friends, and much more. Friends 
mean the world to me. They have been there for me
at all times, from good times to bad times.
And they have never given up on me. Friends 
like these are true friends at times friends are 
all i have I am independent, but I can always depend on 
my friends to be there especially when I need 
them. My friends are there for me 100\% at all 
times we have shared moments of laughter, hatred
and sadness. But no matter what we have stayed 
by each others's side, as we always will.
