My heart and soul ache for his touch, his voice, and his presence. 
No other will my heart beat so as it does for him. 
He is my everything, my shining light and my guardian angel.
With each passing moment my heart longs for him, to be without
him is to be lost in the sea of pain. 
Never to leave me, never to disappear from my heart.
He is the one I love, the only one to love me. 
All I need is him. 


~Jeff
