---
title: "Your Love"
writer: "SugaAngel"
slug: "your-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

The love I need, the love that I desire lies deep within the one that holds the key to me. He that holds thee knows there is no greater love than his. His love that was once received, was lost and now rebuilt. That sliver of hope is now blossoming into a whole new love. But now that my hopes have risen, will they one I love, the only true love of mine, will he who holds thee...hold fast to me?
