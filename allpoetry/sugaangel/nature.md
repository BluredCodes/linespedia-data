title: "Nature"
writer: "SugaAngel"

I feel as though I'm drawn to nature. That it is my one
true calling. The creatures of the world mean so much to
me, and they know this. Some say the animals can not
comprehend like us humans do. But I do not believe that.
They have feelings, knowledge and so much more that we
can not understand and these are some reasons why I
want to be a vet. 
I do not wish to save the beings called humans, they
mean very little to me. Our race is so pathetic, we call
ourselves humans; people. We think we know so much that
we are much better than nature and its creatures.
But we aren't, we can learn so much from the creatures
that inhabit this place we call home. Our kind is full of
ego, too much in fact. Instead of being open-minded
about things, we ignore what nature's knowledge has to
offer us. At times I think we should be completely
wiped out to let the creatures roam free to control the 
lands; as they once did before we interfered.
