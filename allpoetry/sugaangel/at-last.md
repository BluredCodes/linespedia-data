---
title: "At Last"
writer: "SugaAngel"
slug: "at-last"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Each evening when I lie and each morning when I rise. You are by my side, a never-ending vigil. The warmth I feel from your arms so lovely wrapped around, has given a constant peace that can not be expressed.
An unchanging love I have experienced. A gift from above I have received. My heart's desires have been fulfilled. And my soul has been completed.
This crusade that we have been enduring is at an end for once and for all. Our hearts have been healing for this moment. As the stars twinkle with excitement, we are able to share an ultimate love at last.
