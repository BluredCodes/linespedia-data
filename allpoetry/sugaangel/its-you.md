---
title: "It's You"
writer: "SugaAngel"
slug: "its-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

When I rise each morning I see the face of the one 
that has shown me how to love again.
It's you who makes my skin tingle with excitement
each time we touch.
I crave the sweet tenderness of the taste of 
your lips upon mine.
It's you whose voice brightens my day with 
each gentle word spoken.

It's you who has opened my heart to the power of love, 
It's you who my heart sings for.
