---
title: "How Do You Know?"
writer: "SugaAngel"
slug: "how-do-you-know"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/5407-How-Do-You-Know--by-SugaAngel"

When love turns your world upside down and your mind is spinning about in a whirlwind. The life you once knew seems foreign as thoughts consist of one instead of many. 
How do you know it to be real? When life appears as a dream of peace and happiness, even though reality may be harsh. and the heart rejoices for all eternity.
How do you know you aren't living in a one way dream world? Is one truly able to discern reality from a dream? When life is so good is it a dream that you refuse to wake from or chase after to live life can be more than a dream. But how do you know how to make it reality?
