title: "Just How Much"
writer: "SugaAngel"

Such feelings that I have for you can not be contained in a sentence or a paragraph. No words can show just how much I do love you. No poem, nore song, and not even a gesture. But as of now, the only way that I can sum my feelings up for you is by saying:
I love you with all of my heart.

For Jeff Osborn
