None compare to the beauty you surpass, 
to the greatness of your authority.
Your throne is my heart and your land
my soul. Forever reigning over this
golden stretch of love.
To spend a day on the beach by your side,
a night covered by the moon in your embrace.
What would I give for the days of Romance?
Our love holds strong as the tides grip
the shore. Never will we leave the other. 
Always there will be the kiss of new days
to bring hope. 
You my dove, I love.
