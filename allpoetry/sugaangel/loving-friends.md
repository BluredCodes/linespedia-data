So we've had our tough sticky spots inour friendship. And we've had those mushy, lovey dovey moments. But those moments are what have strengthened ourrelations. And I wouldn't have it any otherway! To live a life that does not involve you in it, would be a tragic andquite a duller life. The thought of losing you as a friend, companion, andmajor part in my life, brings tears tomy eyes. And thats why I say 'I love you' everyday we talk. BecauseI do, and I wouldn't have it any otherway. God Bless you all!
