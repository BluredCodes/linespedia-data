---
title: "Never Again?"
writer: "SugaAngel"

I hear the soft breaking of my heart 
followed by the chilling sounds of 
shattering glass. And its over, as soon
as my hope had risen. I now know its over 
and never will be again. my heart cries for 
his love,but none is received. And i know 
its done with. Never again, will our paths 
become one, never again will i be held in 
his strong affectionate arms. alone, I walk 
the path of life wondering if all is lost. I
look around seeing the paths I could 
follow, some lead to the star of my 
affection--but his turn away. It's over and 
done, as all hope slowly fades away. And I 
slowly piece my shattered heart back 
together, keeping a sliver of hope alive. 
But will it survive and prosper?
