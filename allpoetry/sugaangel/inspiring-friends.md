title: "Inspiring Friends"

When the world has turned dark with hate and anger, with pain and sorrow My light comes from your strength and endurance. The tender words of comfort I can always count on to receive when the world shatters any dreams of love. My inspiration to continue running the course of life, to take that needed breath to survive comes from your care for me. When I am down and without hope you lift me up to keep me going. Without your care and love I would have surrendered to the hate. You are my friends, who I cherish with all my heart and depend on to lift me up when all hope has been lost. This life I lead of trials and struggles are lightened by your everlasting friendship.
