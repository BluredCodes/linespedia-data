---
title: "My Joy"
writer: "SugaAngel"

Joy sprinkles in drops of glory from the heavens above to be soaked in by my unworthy soul. These droplets from above come in all shapes and sizes, colors and themes. But each brings joy to me in their own way. Each joy drop is delivered to me by angels guiding another soul to me. Some are loved ones, some are lost, and some are new. 
And as each drop adds to the others my joy begins to overflow with happiness and rejoicing as the day grows on. There are main earth angels who daily bring me this joy along with mortal souls toiling this earth on a mission as they deliver good deeds. 
My hope, my faith, my joy is renewed each day by these loving souls that God has set forth to deliver among his creatures, the joy that He supplies.
