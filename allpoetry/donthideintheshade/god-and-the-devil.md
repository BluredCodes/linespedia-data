title: "god and the devil"
writer: "donthideintheshade"

love vs hate
death vs life
hurt vs freedom
atmosphere
is how it may
appear
thourghts must stay
in my brain
life will pay
cant remain
so afraid
lost in hate
cant be saved
destined by fate
you wont 
guarentee
life will set
me free
cant guarentee
death will let
me be
screaming on the floor
bleeding heart
cant take anymore
been ripped apart
body as cold as stone
deserve to be 
left alone
in the end
wont matter
dreams shatter
cant depend
have no knife
cant take 
my life
have no pills
im terrified
of myself
thinking suicide
no longer want to
cry
ask you why
tell you filthy
lies
bother to 
try
just want to
die,
die
let me be 
by hells burning gate
where i must go 
as a victim of self hate
