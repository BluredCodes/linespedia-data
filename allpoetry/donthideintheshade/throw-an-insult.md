title: "throw an insult"

throw an insult at me,
hurl it across an open space,
so when it hits, it has gained speed,
so when it hits, it HURTS,
then you can witness
the pain you cause me
you can watch
as, like a pane of glass, i shatter.

fall to the ground, land in the grass
and scream, let my lungs release it
the insult may as well have been a rock,
you broke me, i cant take anymore
out of the emotions we are capable of feeling
why do you only allow me to fell the hurt and the pain?
i want to feel the others,

describe happiness to me, make me understand.
tell me about the thrill of friends
adn how you can laugh and you can cry
and you wont be judged for doing either.
tell me how it feels to feel love.
to have the trust and the hugs.
you silence me with your stare.

i think you know what you have done,
the pot of pain inside me was building up
and you hit the crack and made it burst
for the first time ever i experiance something i like;
you apologise
i have silenced you with my tears
