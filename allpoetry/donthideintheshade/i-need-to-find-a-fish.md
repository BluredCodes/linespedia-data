title: "i need to find a fish"
writer: "donthideintheshade"

I want to find a fish
One who likes my scales
As I like his
One who will not abuse me
And use me for my gills
One who doesn’t just want to get 
In between my fins
I want one that’s pretty and full of colour
I need one who likes depth
I hate being shallow
I want one that’s the same specis
And doesn’t want to eat me
I don’t want him to find a better fish
I don’t want him to turn out to be a shark
And chose to swallow mw whole
So any respect I have for myself is lost
I want one to make me happy
And be swimming the same way as me
And be willing to swim with me until the end
I need one who gives me butterflies
When I think of him
And when I see him, 
I want to have to open my gills
As wide as they go, to draw in his aroma
I want us to be happy
And I want us to be free
I want to find a fish
