title: "flip a coin"
writer: "donthideintheshade"

flip a coin
to answer the questions
stuck in your mind
to search for the answers
the answers that dont exsist
heads tails; tails heads

flip a coin
to find out what you feel
deep in your body
search for the emotions
the ones you like and the ones you loath
tails heads; heads tails

flip a coin
to cover the haunting noise
created by your silence,
to search for the words
that describe the indescriable feelings
heads tails; tails heads

flip a coin
you wish it was so simple
wish you had a tighter grip on life
sometimes you wish you could find a penny
and the penny would answer and think and search
tails heads; heads tails
