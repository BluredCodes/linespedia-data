---
title: "stop me being"
writer: "donthideintheshade"
slug: "stop-me-being"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/52105-stop-me-being-by-donthideintheshade"
---

stop me hurting on the inside
make the pain be outside
treat me as if im worth nothing
let me bleed on the outside
like my heart bleeds inside
put all my blood in a pot;
save it for someone
who deserves to live
take away my heart
give it to someone whos capable
of giving and recieving love.
cut out my liver
stop it being fucked up
from excessive alcohol 
tear out my lungs
stop them tasting smoke
give them to someone 
who deserves to breathe
rip out my eyes
make them see only good
take away my ears
they no longer need to hear my screams 
cut off my nose
it doesnt like the smell of tears
or the smell of blood
tear off my arms
stop them being maimed
rip away everything until i am no longer me
stop me feeling
stop me being
