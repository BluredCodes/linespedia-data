---
title: "make us go away"
writer: "donthideintheshade"
slug: "make-us-go-away"
source: "AllPoetry"

The world is such a beautiful place
There’s trees and flowers and bird song
Yet we abuse it, it’s a fucking disgrace
We fuck with the gift of life, its so wrong

You are meant to be who we trust, who we admire
You are supposed to run our country, our world, make it safe
But I can no longer even see you through the smoke of this forest fire
We are supposed to feel secure in this planet where we are based

You’ve made us think our whole life is a lie
We don’t want to follow in your footsteps, we know you are doing it wrong
Just fucking give up and let us try
You’ve been fucking it up for so long

We fight, we kill, we maim and we die
We beg, we plead, we wish and we pray
We hate, we steel, we rape and we cry
We pray so hard, make us go away
