title: "fragility"
writer: "donthideintheshade"

if you touched it
it would shatter
if you dropped it
it would smash
if you looked at it
you would break it,
it would feel
alone
insecure
differant
and wrong

its not like other flowers
its doesnt survive by
photosynthesis
it doesnt live on water and sunlight
it trys to live on love
it wants to live FOR love

its silent and its beautiful
no-one understands
how it came to be
it is unique
it stands alone
in the garden of life

it is a crystal rose

it lives with delicate
and precious glass petals
not red or yellow or white
as you'd expect from a rose
but transparent
it owns no colour pigment

it never saw the need
it saw that everything is just
judged on appearance anyway
so instead of being coloured
and instead of being misty
it chose to me clear
it guessed if everyone would judge
it may as well let them see it all
everything
see right through

there is one part of the rose however
which baffles some
there is one part, just left of centre
which is black, deep black
it cant be changed
it is what it is
its there for a reason;
it made the crystal rose what it has become
