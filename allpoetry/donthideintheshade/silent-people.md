---
title: "silent people"
writer: "donthideintheshade"

I am sitting here
And you are sitting there
Maybe the lonely people should join together
There’s silent people everywhere

I am hoping for something that wont come
And waiting to become someone
I haven't yet learned that it doesn’t happen like that
You must learn from everyone

I know that you are lonely
And I know you are in pain
I understand you because I am the same
If you need me, I’ll be here to hug you again

I have found myself a corner
And I know you have too
Both are small, both are dark
You are sitting there waiting for me, I am sitting here waiting for you

I have had too much time to think
And I guess it I don’t make a sound there’s nothing to hear
If I don’t touch the world it wont know I’m here
We could both be happy then, wed have nothing to fear

Close your eyes and go to sleep
Lie, sleep and wait for tomorrow
I think I have found a new, safe route
If you trust me I think you will follow.
