just one more i promise
let me cut my skin
just one more
let me remove the pain within

it all begins with one cut
then the guilt arrives
when you realise what youve done

you have to cut yourself again
just as a punishment for the last one
then you have to punish yourself 
for the shit youve done wrong

its one vicious circle of punishment
it all becomes your fault, you must take the blame
you promise yourself this will be the last cut
even when you know the last cut will really come
on the night you diei dont really like this poem and i want you all to say the same so i can bin it without feeling guilty....
