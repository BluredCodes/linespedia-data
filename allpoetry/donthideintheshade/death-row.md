title: "death row"
writer: "donthideintheshade"

the injection kills you
or maybe the electricity
you took a life
or maybe two
but do you need to die?
is it necessary?
i dont understand it all
i dont pretend to
but i have my opinions.
so they say youre mentally ill
they blame death on illness
maybe its right
maybe its wrong
who am i to judge?
i dont know.
something i do know though
mentally ill people really do not have control
they cant help what they feel
and they cant help what they do
im not on the same scale 
i dont take lives
but i understand you cant help what you do
you know its wrong
but theres nothing you can do
you cant stop yourself
you wish so hard that you could
maybe i should testify
maybe i could save a life
you didnt mean to do it
theyre too blind to see
you cant help what you do
should i try to save a life?
would it be worth my time?
maybe theyd understand
because unless youve been there
you really couldnt
its not their fault
that they are blind.
You took a life
they take yours
then theyve taken a life
surely we should take theirs?
yeh yeh its their job
bloody stupid job though
maybe they are the ill ones?
wanting such a fucked up job
take a needle full of shit
stab it in their veins,
push the plunger,
stap you in the chair
turn the eletricity to 'on'
and you die
you didnt mean to kill
and you didnt need to die
