starve the hungry
scowl at the dirty
laugh at the sick
take from the blind
humiliate the deaf
hurt the dumb
steal from the poor
take all they have 
they'll be okay
they wont notice
hurt them
watch while u hurt them
while you cause them pain

youre so fucking cruel

feed the hungry
clean the dirty
tend to the sick
help the blind
smile at the deaf
play with the dumb
comfort the widows
love the orphans
give to the needy
offer all you can
make them feel wanted
let them feel loved
just love them
watch them as they
thank you,
