you think im enjoying this
you look at me like im stupid
why would i enjoy this?
how could anyone enjoy being 'ill'?
i have a mental health condition
and you think i like it
you think i like having to take pills
just to keep me happy?
just to save my life?
you think i enjoy the pain
and the hate and the hurt
all the shit i feel
you think i enjoy it?
you think i thrive on it
you asked me once
if i was skitsophrenic
how the fuck should i know?
which one of me were you asking anyway?
you know im cronically depressed 
or some other shit
and you look at me
as though i enjoy being sad every fucking day
do you really think i like going to bed
just knowing i have another  shitty day
waiting for me in the next few hours
answer me honestly. do you think i enjoy this?
in case your answer is yes, adn its looking likely
i dont. i fucking hate it,  adn i wish
i wish so fucking hard that it would go away.
Never stare at crazy people.dunno????? u??????
