i have dissapointed you
and for that im so fucking sorry
please dont let anyone else hurt,
never hurt like i do

i must take all i fear
i must take all the pain
its not worth trying to feel alive again
please let me go, i hate it here

leave my room, shut out the light
not even life is guarenteed
please leave me here to bleed
i must leave here tonight
