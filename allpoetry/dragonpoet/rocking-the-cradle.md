title: "Rocking the Cradle"
writer: "Dragonpoet"

Once upon a time I walked in step 
A prefect little soldier 
With not a thought to call my own 
Following every rule, every demand 
With no pride in my step 
Nor love in my heart. 

Once upon a time I followed every rule, 
Never questioning right or wrong 
Believing every word as truths 
Never holding a thought of my own, 
Nor love in my heart. 

Once upon a time I accepted my fate, 
Never questioning right or wrong 
With tormented nights and dreamless days 
A prefect little soldier, silent and obedient 
Never doubting the life I lived. 

Once upon a time I felt a spark 
My soul shivered, hearing an angel’s cry, 
Believing all I heard 
With my head held high, and questions upon my lips. 
Rocking the cradle.
