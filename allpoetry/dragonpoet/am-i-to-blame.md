---
title: "Am I to blame?"
writer: "Dragonpoet"
slug: "am-i-to-blame"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/48078-Am-I-to-blame--by-Dragonpoet"
---

Feeling confused, lost and scared, 
Spinning back the wheel of time, 
Attempting to understand why, 
Why it all had to be this way, 
Was it me, am I to blame? 

A child unwanted, unneeded, 
A child to care for, 
Stealing youth and dreams, 
The child I was, 
Was it me, am I to blame? 

Wanting to change the past, 
To remove the stain I placed, 
Attempting to correct the mistakes, 
To undo what has been, 
And yet, was it me, am I to blame? 

Watching my own children, 
Hearing their laughter, 
Wandering if all is okay, 
Knowing if it isn’t, 
Then I am to blame? 

Glancing at my husband, 
Wondering what regrets he holds, 
If life is all he dreamed of, 
Knowing if it isn’t, 
Then is it me, am I to blame?
