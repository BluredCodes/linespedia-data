---
title: "Fall from Grace"
writer: "Dragonpoet"
slug: "fall-from-grace"
source: "AllPoetry"

Blinded by my own selfish torment, 
Allowed myself to be seduced, 
To fall far from grace, 
To tumble into the dark depths, 
Seeking comfort in the bottom of a bottle, 
Believing it was where I needed to be. 

I knew the spiraling danger I entered, 
But I sought no comfort, no reasons, 
Only the need to flee, to escape, 
To forget, to remember nevermore, 
And thus,now I stand with head bowed, 
Knowing the shame I place upon myself. 

I knew the dangers, the fear, 
And Yet, I still fell, 
Falling far from grace, 
Allowing myself to be my demon, 
Allowing it freedom, 
And now, I stand so very shakily, 
Facing the truth of my stipudity, 
Knowing I can't take it back... 

I fell from grace, 
Bowed to the strength of my demons, 
Accepting their answer for my plight, 
And now, I sit in the darkness, lost in the night, 
Fearing the light of dawn... 

I fell far from grace 
Drowning in the bottom of a bottle...
