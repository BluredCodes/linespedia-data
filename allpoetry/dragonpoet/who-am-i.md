---
title: "Who am I?"
writer: "Dragonpoet"
slug: "who-am-i"
source: "AllPoetry"

I am who I am 
I can't change, 
Not how I look, 
Not how I react, 
Not the fibers of myself. 

I am who I am 
Needing to learn 
To like me 
To love me. 

I am who I am 
A ghost 
A illusion 
A book unwritten 
A fallen block 
A fool. 

I am who I am 
Stronger then I think 
A survivor 
A fighter 
A decent soul. 

I am who I am 
reflection of yesterday 
a contrictation of the past 
a spirit lost 
A soul frozen. 

I am who I am 
But in the end 
Who heck am I?
