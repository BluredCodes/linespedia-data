title: "Saying goodbye"

Everything changes, nothing stays the same 
The seasons change, life changes 
And here we gather not in joy but in great sorrow 
For us, in this twisted world of ours we say goodbye, 
Goodbye to a brother, son, grandson, and friend. 

The scent of fall, the season of sleep is upon us 
With the wonder of color and shades of shadows 
And amongst nature's beauty we gathered 
Laying to rest a wonderful man, 
One who gave more then he took. 

He always had a kind word, 
A spirit of gentleness and kindness 
A man to be remembered for his life 
And not for the ending we can never understand. 

The boy he was, full of adventure and spirit 
We all hold memories of the boy, 
His smile, the sound of his voice, 
And the whispers of wisdom he spoke 
Never to be forgotten. 

He became a man from the sweet boy, 
Always thinking of others, 
And if only he had known how many of us 
Stand here loving him, 
But he lost his way.A piece I wrote saying goodbye to my brother, who committed suicide.
