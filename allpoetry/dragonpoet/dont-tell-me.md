---
title: "Don't tell me"
writer: "Dragonpoet"
slug: "dont-tell-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/48074-Dont-tell-me-by-Dragonpoet"

Don't tell me not to hate me, 
For hatred is what I have, 
Beating in my soul. 

Don't tell me to stop the images, 
I don't know how, 
How to end the sorrow. 

Don't tell me I have value, 
When in the end, I am worthless, 
I hold no heart of gold. 

Don't tell me to listen to my heart, 
For my heart is warped and twisted, 
Black through and through. 

Don't tell me not to hate, 
When hatred is all I have, 
All I feel, all I know. 

Don't tell me to believe in me, 
When there is nothing left to believe in, 
For my heart is black through and through. 

Don't tell me who I am, 
When I was lost long ago, 
For my soul died long before.
