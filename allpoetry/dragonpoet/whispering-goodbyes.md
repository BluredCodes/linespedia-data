---
title: "Whispering Goodbyes"
writer: "Dragonpoet"
slug: "whispering-goodbyes"
source: "AllPoetry"

Upon the ashes of dying embers 
Wrought with ghosts and forgotten tales 
Honoring a soul upon its journey, 
Whispering words of care and undying love, 
Knowing a cycle has ended. 

Alone each soul stands, 
Looking downward and inward, 
Silently whispering their good-byes. 

Upon the moist soil and wind blown hill, 
Gathered amongst the silent angels, 
Whispering goodbye to a soul, 
Forgetting for a time, the ills of life, 
Remembering but an illusion. 

Alone each soul stands, 
Lost in the mist of their sorrow, 
Remembering ill or good, 
Silently whispering their good-byes. 

Upon the shadows of my soul, 
Remembering all that was, and never could of been, 
Whispering goodbye to a soul, goodbye to the illusion, 
Laying to rest the dreams, never to be.
