---
title: "Holding Nothing Dear"
writer: "Dragonpoet"
slug: "holding-nothing-dear"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/48076-Holding-Nothing-Dear-by-Dragonpoet"
---

Listening to the silence, becoming lost, 
Lost amongst the evening whispers, 
Hearing the encouragement of others, 
But believing in nothing, holding nothing dear. 

Allowing the darkness to settle, becoming lost, 
Lost amongst the evening shadows, 
Hearing the beats of my heart, 
But loving nothing, holding nothing dear. 

Letting the silence to enlop my soul, becoming lost, 
Lost amongst the tormenting whispers, 
Hearing their echoing despair, 
But believing in nothing, holding nothing dear. 

Been so long, I listened to the silences, 
Becoming lost within the whispers, 
The tears of my soul, my heart, 
But loving nothing, holding nothing dear. 

Knowing its all up to me, becoming lost, 
Lost amongst anguish inside myself, 
Hearing their cries, screams and shouts, 
But believing in nothing, holding nothing dear. 

Listening to the silence, becoming lost, 
Lost amongst the memories, 
Hearing their tales, knowing the truth, 
But loving nothing, holding nothing dear.
