title: "Whispers"

In this life one walks a fine line, 
Between the wrong and right,
Never knowing what truly is wrong or right,
And as the sun set, its light waning,
And the moon approaches, the stars twinkle,
I find myself wondering what I know.

Sometimes I find myself drowning in the waves,
The waves washing over me like a second skin,
Listening to the whispers of my soul,
Knowing there are no unbeatable odds, 
No locking the doors,no barring the voices.

The rushing wind, a caressing touch,
A simple kiss, a sign of love and caring,
Knowing that here within the waves of sorrow,
I borrow the hope I long to believe in,
As I close my weary eyes.
