title: "Don't Think of me"
writer: "Dragonpoet"

Don't think of me when tears are falling 
And sorrow is all you see 
For each day I walk upon this land 
I feel the threads of the past,
Knowing I can't escape what was.
 
Don't think of me when laughter lives 
And smiles is all you see 
For each day I walk upon this land 
I feel trapped in a shell, 
Knowing I can't escape from me. 

Don't think of me when you need a friend, 
And hope you need 
For each day I walk upon this land 
I am trapped in the shadows, 
Locked in a battle I can't win. 

Don't think of me when a child cries 
And sorrow is all you see 
For I was the child once 
Lost and alone, struggling to be 
Trapped in the shadows, 
Locked in a battle I can't win. 

Don't think of me when answers you desire 
For I hold no answers, 
Only questions.
