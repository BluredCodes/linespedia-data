---
title: "Rotting Corpse"
writer: "Dragonpoet"
slug: "rotting-corpse"
source: "AllPoetry"

Crawling at my face, 
Peeling layer by layer, 
Exposing my soul, my heart, 
Can't you see the filth, 
The torment, the seeds of despair, 
The rotting corpse I am? 

Face to face to me, 
Staring at the face of evil, 
The soldier of despair, 
Can't you see the evil, 
The sins, the seeds of destruction, 
The rotting corpse I am? 

Whispering to me, 
Believing in the mask, 
Hiding my soul, my heart, 
Can't you see the lies, 
The illusions, the seeds of contempt, 
The rotting corpse I am? 

Been living so long, 
Behind the mask, the facade, 
Hiding all I am, trapping myself, 
Can't you see the filth, 
The sorrow, the seeds of despair, 
The rotting corpse I am?
