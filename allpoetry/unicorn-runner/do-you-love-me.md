---
title: "Do you love me?"
writer: "Unicorn Runner"
slug: "do-you-love-me"

I've loved you since I saw you
I can't get you off my mind.
Wishing on the shooting stars
that your eyes will again find mine.

I had you for a while
and then you let me go
The time we had went by so fast
and now life seems to low.

I love you more than anyone
more than anyone ever will
But you look right past me
like you never knew me at all.

I know that you still love me
but not how I love you.
You love me the only way you can

You told me that you  loved me once
but did you lie to me?
If you didn't like u said, 
then why can't it be you and me?

I'll wait for you foever.
I'll be here when u you call.
But baby I ask one thing,
do you love me at all?
