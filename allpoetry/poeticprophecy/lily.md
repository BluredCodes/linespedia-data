---
title: "Lily"
writer: "PoeticProphecy"
slug: "lily"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Lord, I cry for my Lily, the love will forever remain,
pedal by pedal, it was no daisy nor rose.
Exposing the illusion, I felt so much pain,
insane for the seed that never grows.

Lord, oh Lord, does this mean our lives are fake? 
When we realize that we never exist, we fade away?
That moment we think our life is unreal or just a lie, 
we lose our will to live, and thus we die.

Lord, oh Lord, please tell me during this cold hour, 
how could I have wasted my time on a flower?
The realization of what this blossom could convey, 
is the very essence that made it flutter away.

Lord, oh Lord, don't mourn for me now,
I'm turning my back on you.
For my soul, the sorrowful have cried,
come to the funeral, my heart has died...
