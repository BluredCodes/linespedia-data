title: "She's in Love"

My sight, enveloped in a trance, at the fantasy before me,
as the sun radiantly coats the seductive deadly shape I see.
God’s brilliantly grand perfection of a heavenly woman,
gawking are all, at her every solemn fluent movement.
I am able to only gasp at the angelic beauty of my beloved within view.
everything I’ve ever thought, felt, experienced, stood for, was for you.

Riding her path with self-love and sensual dignity,
yearning not anything but to celebrate victory in beauty.
Art defined in a divine monument man cannot deny,   
no doubt, she’s in love with herself, and so am I...
