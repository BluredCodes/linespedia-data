Remembering... 
Gazing at the window while lying in my bed,
the curtains sway silently inside my head.
Wind blows a soft rhythm through my hair,
clearing my thoughts of long despair.
The illumination of reality gleams through to my mind,
for so long, to the light, I had been blind.
Through my eyes, so much I had not been seeing
peering through the screen 'tween dreams and being.
All along, my psyche's judgment was the key,
beyond heart, to view between the lines of reality.
But what is it I see, beyond reality?
Lost entities, long passed,
of an unforgotten past.
Where are you now?
Forgotten...
