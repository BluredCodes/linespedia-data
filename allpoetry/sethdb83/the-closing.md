If I still had you where you belong
It wouldn't be hard to sing this song
But I wrote it down just for you
Hoping someday, you would come to
I know you hear me, my heart is aching
I know you're out there, the claims you're stating
You think I am unable to be the one for you
But there are things i'd still like to do
Things that you don't believe in
I would like to build something
But you'll never see it happen
And there's this burning like there's always been
I've never been so alone, and i've, I've never been so alive

There's a west wind blowing strong
It whistled your name all day long
But still I can't look to see
I try to find my destiny
You know I love you to this i'm true
It doesn't matter what I do
All that matters is one thing, you
So in close I end this song
Just remember who made it this long
I'm the one you know that much
I'll see you someday soon, perhaps even at lunch
