in a material world we shall prevail
giving in to society and propriety
if we do this we shall fail

but if there is one thing that I know for sure
that's that my spirit has flown through this worlds doors
in and out of cars weaving

seeing and believing the deaths of people I have been grieving
but above all else I must keep the earths spirits in tact
if I don't do so no one else will want to that is fact

love, life, liberty, happiness, these are all overcome by one
faith
no one can stop it no one can top it

if we had faith in everything there'd be no violence
there would be no bad cops to hide the evidence
life would'nt be a dream nor would it be a scheme

i'd like to say to all those that pray
your spirits have been sought
even those whose lives have been bought

death may take you away
but I will continue to stay
for my soul is immortal within the earths plain

i've been there done that and experienced the pain
the pains of life and torment with strife
no more yelling and screaming with husbands and wives

us spirits in the material world
we will seek out those who love us whole
even though you cannot see us you will find us bold
