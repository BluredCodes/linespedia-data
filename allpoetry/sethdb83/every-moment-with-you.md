---
title: "Every Moment With You"
writer: "SethDB83"

In my life and the days that I spend
there's only few things on which I depend
mostly is the basic needs we all share
but next to that is something beyond compare

It's every moment with you
including all the things we do
there's things in life we can go without
but you I can't, to that there's no doubt

Certain feelings you give to me are so right
it makes me long for you day and night
how you say your word down to the way you act
everything you do is so in tact

Every little thing we do together
is as wonderful as peace forever
spending time with you bring me so much joy
you never treat me as if I'm some toy

We are always on equal footing, no matter where we stand
in this relationship we have equal say, not just the man
whatever you feel I take into consideration and keep in mind
we need not argue over petty things, it's wasted time

I'm so in love with you and the feelings we have
the way we share our emotions, makes me so glad
so precious is every word you say to me
but you know I love it when you say baby

I love every moment with you that we get to share
I'll never take for granted you being there
seeing you just makes my whole day complete
but the moments we spend are sweeter than any treat

In everything that we share and whatever we do
however we care, or make something new
Just remember baby that it's so delightful
Every Moment With You
