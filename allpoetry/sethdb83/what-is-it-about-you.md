---
title: "What is it about you?"

I could feel something between us, but something this strong?
I always knew I would fall for you-but imagined it wrong...

You don't know what I felt when you confessed your love!
it's like my prayers were answered from god above..

The thing about this is-it isn't normal, I expected what anyone could do
but I never felt this way with someone else, it's so brand-new!

You make everything seem so right and secure I want no other...
you're all that matters to me now, you're my world and my lover

I don't understand this something about you that makes me so sure--
that our love is forever, something I've never felt before...

Can you tell me what it is about you that makes me feel so right?
And why I constantly think of you, and dream of you through the night?

I've been in love, but never to the point of serious commitment,
I can see the rest of my life with you, I'm willing to admit it!

Tell me before I go insane what I see, feel, and love all in one!
I don't know what it is about you, but the love has just begun...
