title: "Promises To Count On"

I hope you believe me when I promise things,
I'm not here just to say it then go back on my word.
I'm true in what I say about sharing wedding rings,
but don't go by what I tell you or what you heard...

Go by what you believe, and the promises you felt;
don't give up on it, stay having your trust.
I'll do the same, and always providing my help.
But believe the promises, that's a must...

The promises I gave you I'll never go back on,
you have to know I'm real in the promises I said.
If were apart, fight to make love last and stay strong,
I'll fight at all times of day, even at night in bed...

If you believe what I told you and promised you before
especially about never leaving or givin up on us,
then you can always rest easily since you'll be sure
that I'll be true to you and I have your trust...

Remember that the things in this poem are what you need
to be sure of, moving on as you would with your life,
don't get hasty and remember to calm your greed
times can be rough for us, even if you are my wife...

I've given you promises you must rely on to love me,
we know trust will make things easy for both you and I
And so as long as there is a god above me;
promises are promises, and mine won't ever be a lie...
