title: "you're everywhere but near"

I wish I could follow you everywhere my love but I cannot
It would be marvelous to see all the world with you but I cannot
The ferverish flux of your fever would've easily been taken care by me
but I was not with you my dear
you travel the world and the seven seas
you've been there been here now where do you be
I wish but I know you aren't here with me
now the tides have turned and pushed you back to my way
stop by and visit at least someday
I know I can't expect a yes if that was a question but at least a maybe
what about our youngest one my dear our little baby
what will become of her if she doesn't see her mommy
her father is there for her constantly
but the mother is out on the sea
or up somewhere amongst the forest trees
or walking upon the desert sand
little princess is growing up fast
I wish you were here to make this last
you could've even been here when little Debby had her cast
but you said you were on an important trip
as if it were something that you couldn't miss
you're everywhere I want to be except here
can't you see that I feel you when you're near
it's sad that we can't be a family
because you're everywhere else but here
