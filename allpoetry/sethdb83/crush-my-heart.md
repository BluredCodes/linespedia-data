when I shadowbox inside my skull
bothered by the senselessness
I wish for the messiah,
Indian woman, Babylon

crush my heart with a hammer
make a purer one
Babylon

don't let the world collapse all around you
cus when the kind consciousness
come unto you
you will know
and it won't stop
