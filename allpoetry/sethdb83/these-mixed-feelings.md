They say a man isn't supposed to cry
maybe I should hide the tears from my eye?

I love you to my fullest, you know that
so you should be here, but you're not, so where're you at?

You claim to love me, but you're not here, what do I do?
If you do love me then you should be here where I need you!

I can't believe these mixed feelings I have inside of me,
then you ask me what's wrong, can't you see!?

When I need you the most, it seems you're never there
actions like this make me wonder if you care.

Come back to me and put me at ease,
calm these mixed emotions, I'm asking you please...
