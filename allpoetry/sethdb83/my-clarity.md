If the world were to be a good place
First it'd have to have good face
along with the rest of the body
we would all be clotty
or would we be dirty and clean
would we be proper or obscene
If the world had someone like me for it's leader
i'm sure it would be a whole lot cleaner
everybody loves living on this earth but not everybody loves me
people look and stare at me in such obscurity
especially those that don't keep this world clean
they look at me and say to themselves how mean
but judge me not because what you don't know
is my clarity on the outside is what shows
that'd be the worst thing you could possibly do
but if poetry were my language we'd all understand
that ignorance and prejudice, and fear walk hand in hand
