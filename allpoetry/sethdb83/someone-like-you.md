If the world were to be a good place
It'd first have to have a good face
along with the rest of it's body
we would all be called clotty
would we all be dirty or all be clean
would we be proper or be obscene
if the world had someone like you for it's leader
i'm sure we'd all be a lot cleaner
everybody loves you but not all love me
we all fall down sometime in obscurity
especially those of us who aren't interested
in keeping this world neat and clean
come now, some of you would call this mean
but judge not if you don't know
that would be the worst thing to do
but if poetry were our language then we could understand
that ignorance and prejudice, and fear walk hand in hand
