title: "The righteous wave"

After hurricanes 

they bowl you 

a mouth full of sand.

You can't stand,

but these are righteous.

So he said. 



"Them waves is righteous."

:tubular,

diagonal,

with crests that spar 

at right angles,

torso to thigh.



"Now them's wicked!"

All toes on board,

hang ten's the call

beneath the sun.



Burning on shore

we watch them fall

where righteous 

and wicked are one.
