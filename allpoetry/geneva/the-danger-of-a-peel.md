---
title: "The Danger of a Peel"
writer: "Geneva"
slug: "the-danger-of-a-peel"

Once I sat legs out flat
on the ground
hands in my pockets.
Just happened like that;
'Twas not cute.

I had one lifted in town
from my unzipped purse
a pick-pocket or some kind.
I'd already eaten the fruit;
good source of potassium. 

Some gorilla left a path of 'em
from hamaca to cabana,
yellow hats along the sand.
Litter! I don't understand it;
like lethal land mines 

them bananas.
