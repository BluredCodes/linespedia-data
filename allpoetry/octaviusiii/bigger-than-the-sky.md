The joy I feel now
Is bigger than the expanse of the sky
Freedom lifts me higher
Further than space
Where everything and nothing meet
Where pain has lost its power

Oh sweet love's divinity
Inspiring happiness beyond compare
Life, far from shunned
Embraced and taken wholesale
