---
title: "Where Have You Gone?"
writer: "OctaviusIII"
slug: "where-have-you-gone"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/50874-Where-Have-You-Gone--by-OctaviusIII"

Life fades from my empty life
Into an empty gray
I long so much to feel you here
I just want to feel some touch

God where have you gone?
Are you leaving me for broken?
Are you leaving me so frozen?
Cause all I want is to be happy again

God, my Jesus Christ
You've felt this loss
While you died and wailed
Moaned in your unforgiving pain
If you survived your cross, maybe
I could live through mine
It's just so hard
When I feel nothing from you

God where have you gone?
Are you leaving me for broken?
Are you leaving me so frozen?
Cause all I want is to be happy again
All I want is to be happy again
Happy again

Happy again...Written for a friend of mine, this was inspired by both what he's going through and some of Jars of Clay's new lyrics.  Feel free to add it to music if you're so inclined, and as usual, any criticisms are welcome.
