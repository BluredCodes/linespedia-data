Remember those days we had fun?
Remember my life was once complete?
I could never see myself there again
I will never be able to see those times again

I never thought I'd die here
I never thought I'd die on my own bed
I never thought I'd kill myself
I never thought I'd come this close to the edge

You won't see me cry
You won't see me die
I just don't care any more

Lights and darkness
Blurred signs of my own want for life slipping away
I think I might not turn back this time
Because all I want to do is just die

Remember those times I asked you for more milk, Mommy?
Remember when I always told you not to sing?
Never forget the things like that
Just forget about me

I hear my friends now
Lauging and telling me their stupid jokes
I loved them back then
We loved back then

I can see my mom looking away
I can see my father crying
It's not your fault
I just made a mistake...
I just made a mistake...

No
I won't make that mistake

I drop my pills,
Kneel on the floor and cry
I forgot what it meant to be broken
I forgot what it meant to try
This time I'll be ok
I know I'll be okInspired by Adam's Song (Blink 182) and a desire to show hope.  The flip side of my first poem on here, the Suicide, but this time hopeful.  Intelligent criticism welcome.
