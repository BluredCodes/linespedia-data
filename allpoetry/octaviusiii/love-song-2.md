Maybe someday I’ll regret this
But that I doubt
No one could take this away
They may think I’m dumb
Boy, are they wrong

When I see your face
I know it’s safe
(Do you see that in me?)
When I see your eyes
I know here is love
(What do you see in mine?)
When I see you
All I can taste is your heart
(Is my taste on your tongue too?)Not sure if this is all that great, once again not autobiographical.  Anything constructive would be a good thing!
