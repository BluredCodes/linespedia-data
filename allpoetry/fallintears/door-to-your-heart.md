If I knock on the door…
And nobody answers
Do I come back later?
Or do I walk on in?

If I stand there long enough
Will you let me inside?
Out of pity…
Out of spite… out of pain?

If I sit on the doorstep
For enough minutes
Days
Weeks
Months
Years
Will you let me in…
Just because I happen to be there?

If I stand there
Until I’m old and gray
Waiting just for you
Then will you let me in?

If I knock on the door…
And nobody answers
Do I come back later?
