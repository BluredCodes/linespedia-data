title: "Trust"
writer: "FallinTears"

To find someone you can trust
Is an obstacle in itself
Someone who listens
And knows you
Someone who is always there for you
Someone you can tell anything to
And someone who can tell everything to you

I thought I found that person
But know I am unsure
The ice is breaking
Slowly it shatters along thin lines
And water seeps out
Frigid

You make me shiver and the tears well up in my eyes
How do I know?
That’s it… I don’t
Are you talking about me… or someone else?
Why can’t you tell me?
To me… that means it’s about me
Is it?
I thought… 

I thought there was at least one person
I could trust completely
But now I am looking back on that
For I do not know
I am here alone with my mind
And it invokes terror inside of me

No tears do I cry… no water falls down my face
My heart it bleeds… and it aches
For the one that I once knew so well
Has changed the wall of trust has fell
