title: "Happy Mother's Day"
writer: "FallinTears"

You were there when I was born 
A soft little pink ball 

You were there when I took my first steps 
Helping me back up when I would fall 

You were there when I went to kindergarten 
I waved to you as I hoped on the bus 

You were there when we moved 
Helping me realize how I value our family 

You were there when I entered middle school 
Helped me not to be scared 

You where there when I just sat there crying 
I didn't even know why I was 

You were there when I need a hug 
And you held me close 

You made me realize how much 
I need you in my life 

And now I am making you realize... 

That I am there for you 
Whenever you need me 

I love you mother...with ALL of my heart
