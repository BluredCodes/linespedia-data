You think you know… but you have no idea
I can’t…
No I can’t
I don’t want to hear your defense
I need a box to toss in your excuses
And the tissues
Of tears I’ve cried
For you
used
You throw them away
Not your fault
Not your fault
Never your fault
I hate to say this but it’s too hard
They’ve always said long distance relationships never work
And now I know why
Shut up 
Tonight I’ll sleep in my clothes
That I put on for you
Tonight I’ll sleep with the runny make up
That I applied for you
Tonight I won’t sleep
Because I’ll be crying for you
All will be forgiven if you just hold me in your arms
But that will never happen
Give me a box for my tissues
For more of them I’ve used
Let me toss them away
Just like you’ve done to me
 Not your fault 
Not your fault
But it still hurts
You parents stab me… but I still bleed
Give me a box for my heart
For more of it’s been broken
And those pieces I put inside
Lock
And throw away the key
Less to break…
The less it hurts?
Until there’s none at all::sigh::
