I pick up the mirror and what do I see
A reflection of pain looking back at me
Thoes ice covered tears sliding along
Soft to myself I sing our song

My heart was warmed but then it cooled
You were different? Yeah you had me fooled
Why am I always the one to be hurt?
Pushed right down face in the dirt

But you can’t never get me down
There’s a smile on my face not a from
One day I’ll turn it around
You’re gunna see the ground

As I stood looking for you
Tears of love not red but blue
Smile on my face of soft desire
You covered it over and smothered my fire

I thought what we had was love
But I know someone likes me above
Flowers and candy disguised your mind
Fake rotten love it what I find

But you can’t never get me down
There’s a smile on my face not a from
One day I’ll turn it around
You’re gunna see the ground
