It says…
Close your eyes and let me take you away.
Let me take you to a place you’ve never been before.
A place you will long for, but not remember when you come back.
Take my hand here in the dark can you feel it?
Let me sing you a song for our journey.
The hard part is soon over.
Yes, our journeys end the place where everything fits together.
Open you eyes… LOOK! Isn’t it amazing?
Now close your eyes and let me take your hand.
It grasps it firmly and you are on your way again.
His road seems oddly familiar a road you’ve thought to have traveled before.
There open your eyes now it says.
As light breaches the security of your closed lids you see you are back where you started.
Seen the chance to be happy…
 Only to have someone take it away.Weird...
