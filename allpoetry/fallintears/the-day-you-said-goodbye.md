It’s raining
From the sky
It sends shivers through my spine
Because it’s raining water
World’s water
From the sky
That once puddle around my feet

It’s raining
From my eyes
And it flows down my cheeks
Because it’s raining tears
My tears
From my eyes
That once gazed lovingly into yours

It’s raining
From my heart
It sends pain throughout my body
Because it’s raining blood
My blood
From my heart
The heart you broke

It’s raining
From my head
And it sends me crashing through the floor
Because its’ raining love
My love
From my head
That I gave all to you

The day you said Goodbye::tear drop::
