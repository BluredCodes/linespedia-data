I hate you you’re out to destroy my life, I know it.
But whatever right?
You don’t know anything. 
You punish me because I have a mind of my own,
Because I’m not brainwashed by your fakeness.
You punish me because I can see through you.
I guess you think that I will give up, and become just like the others,
But I won’t.
So you can stop or keep on going.
Either way I won’t care.
Eventually one of us will crack, and it’s not going to be me.
I just thought I’d let you know… so you aren’t confused anymore
Now you know…
And I hope you’re happyBitches
