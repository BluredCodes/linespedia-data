If I die will you miss me?
No I bet you won’t
You’ll go on living your life and forget about me
Forget about all the times we had
All the fun things we did
You’ll forget I know you will
Because I bet you don’t care
I thought you were different
But no you are the same
I wanted you to be different I wanted you to stand out
I needed you to... you tried?
Is that what you did?
I don’t even know for sure
As I sit here drawing the blade across my leg
I find myself lucky that it’s too dull to cut
Exhausted from an hour of crying
Not wanting to wake anyone up
I cannot retrieve another instrument for my demise
Drawing the blade across my hidden spot
Again and again… all I think about is you
I wonder if you will miss me
I bet you won’t
But I won’t ever know…
I scare myself now because I can’t handle all the pain
I can’t do it alone where have “you” gone?
As I push the blade with all my force into my skin I think
Who is going to say?
She’s gone…
