For one that says they want a picture
So they can see me all the time
You don’t show it when I want to see you
You say you’ll call
Liar
I am waiting
Still here dressed
2 nights so far wasted
8:15 I’m waiting here
Every once and a while a tear falls
Because of you
Because of you
For one that says they miss me so much
You don’t show it
I am here waiting
For you
Why am I waiting for you?
You don’t care
Do you?
Because of you
Because of you
I am alone
Yet again another shatters my trust
That has finally been restored
And yet again
We start the vicious cycle
Of painyou suck
