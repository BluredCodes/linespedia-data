---
title: "Whatever!!"
writer: "FallinTears"
slug: "whatever"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Shut your brain off for a minute 
Not like you need it ALL the time 
Don’t think too hard 
You’ve lost all your cards 
Jokes are meant to be stupid 

Chillax take a pill 
You’re not going anywhere 
Let things roll off you 
I bet you have a personal masseuse 
You lousy son of a goose 
You use her every day don’t you? 

Don’t let people annoy you 
Just tell them to shut up 
Tell them to blow it our their ass 
You’ve got time pour that bud in a glass 
Exhale take off your shoes loosen your tie 

Close your eyes a say whatever 
I might just die tomorrow 
Who cares if people suck? 
What the hell I make good money 
Go out get you a honey 

So that’s all you have to do 
Build up your sense of humor 
Don’t take shit too seriously 
And then mysteriously 
Life will be gravy!
