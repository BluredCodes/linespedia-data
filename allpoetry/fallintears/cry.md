Cry me a river 
Let the salt-water flow in raging streams 
Of hurt
 Pain
Grief
 Individuality
 Difference
 Independence
Stupidity
Vibrancy
Life
Death
Suicide
Hypocrisy
And most of all
Blindness
Cry me a river
And let me drown in its watersHelp...
