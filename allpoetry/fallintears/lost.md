---
title: "Lost"
writer: "FallinTears"
slug: "lost"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

The shadows begin to deepen,
Reminding me of the oncoming night.
I stumbled along the rocks and trees,
A cut here a scratch there.
Thorns and stains adorned my clothing.
My stomach let out a bellow.
When had I last eaten?
My throat was dry and scratchy.
I knew I must rest,
But no, I must persevere.
I couldn’t feel my legs.
Very often I would fall. 
Many animals came across my path,
An owl hooted in a far by tree.
I began to feel dizzy.
M vision clouded over.
I stopped, and waited for the spell to pass.
When it did, I kept walking.
I guessed it was the third day of searching.
But I couldn’t be sure,
I began to think of the most likely.
I was lost, I couldn’t find my way home.
I was cold, tired, hungry, at to the end of my rope.
I walked a little farther,
And then I collapsed to the ground.
I cried away all my energy,
And the last thought I had,
Was someone help me find my way home.
