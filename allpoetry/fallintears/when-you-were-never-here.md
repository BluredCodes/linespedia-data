title: "When You Were Never Here"

I miss you so much
But I realize
You were never here
But you left me
I remember that
But how is that so

The tears are now dry
The tissues thrown away
The pain committed to memory
But there is still a little hole in my heart
Just enough space to fit yours in…
Your love

But that’s okay
Because I never asked you to love me
I never asked you to care
But I asked you to help
And you did… for a while
But how could you stop
When you knew I cried for you…
Just some words
Simple easy words

And as the rain drops on my head
And pierces through my body
I still think of you
And how you made me laugh
And how you made me smile
And how you left…
When you were never here.Just another rambling from my sad sick twisted unloved lil mind ;-)
