I can see it in your eyes
When I call your name… a little sparkle shines in them
Yet you do not smile
But you do not scowl
You say nothing
And I call you again
Your ear twitches this time
And I know you can hear me
Why won’t you answer?
Taking one step towards you… only one
You look at me, and I am lost in the whirlpools of your eyes
And all at once… I am back to reality
And you aren’t looking at me
You're staring off in space… on the other side of the room
How did you get there?
Why won’t you talk to me?
Your name rolls questionably off my lips
Silence
Tears slide from my eyes… down my cheeks
Please?
Just say something God Damnit I cry
You shake your head
And walk away
My tear filled eyes boring into your back

Saturday December 29, 2001

I never saw your face that day
I never saw you smile
I never heard you say a word
You never loved me all the while
All that time you lead me on
Conniving fuck that is you
Never once I thought you the same
Tainted by the things you do
You left me there in the dark
No words fell off your lips
No glances looked my way
No touch with the fingertips
All that time I thought you loved me
You played me a fool
Thoes times I confessed to you
You stayed calm and cool
You never let me on the inside
 I was curious but I let it go
I never thought you were a liar
But look at you now… it shows
I wonder what I would have done
If this went on much longer
I guess I’m glad that I found out now
And because of you I’m stronger
I never saw your face that day
I never saw you smile
I never heard you say a word
You never loved me all the while
