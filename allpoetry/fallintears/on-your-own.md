Why?
Why does everyone have to be the same?
They said… you’d find someone I promise
But no luck have I found
I though I did and I trusted you
You don’t know how much that means to me
Find someone I can trust, I don’t trust easily
For a while you had me fooled
And you told me you had the same feelings for a certain someone else… that I did
And I was happy
A big weight off my shoulders
I was only trying to keep the piece
But then something happens
And my world is turned upside down
I don’t want to care I don’t
So why do I?
You betrayed me
You changed your story
Why are you like the others?
Why can’t you be different?
It hurts it hurts inside
But I don’t want any pity from you mother fuckers
Because I know you don’t care
You’re all the same every last one of you
Fuck off
Because I’m tired of being a good person
All I get is shit
I try and no one sees
I’m just not going to care anymore
You can all figure out life for yourself
Have a nice day darlings
For you are now on your ownPeople suck officially! ^-^
