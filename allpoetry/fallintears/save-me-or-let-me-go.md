title: "Save me... or let me go?"

The wind blows softly like a wave of chiffon across my face
The air smelling like sun kissed fingers upon m eyes
Pushing me back relaxing into my daydreams
I can feel you all around me yet I tend to forgot you’re there
Why don’t you show yourself?
Like a tiny rabbit scuttling into its burrow as you come closer
Whispers escape my ashen lips… don’t be afraid
Lost in the abyss of feelings I am drowning
Farther and father down I go
I still can feel you…
Will you save me?
