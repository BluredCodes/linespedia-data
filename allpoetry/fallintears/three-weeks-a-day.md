title: "Three Weeks & A Day"

I planned it. It was going to be perfect.Just the right amount of people,Everyone happy to be there with who were going to be with. It was just brilliant.But you couldn’t go.3 weeks and a day.Doesn’t seem like much, does it?But to me it’s eternity.Slowly I’m forgetting you.The way you do certain things, how you say things.It’s torture.Now another week is going to pass by without you.I was depressed just sad.You weren’t there it was an odd number of people.I was outcasted and I needed you then?Why does it have to be so hard?Finally I’ve found someone who might be for me…This isn’t fair. It doesn’t make me mad anymoreJust morose.Lethargic I don’t feel like moving.This week will go on of me dreaming of when I will see you again.I guess…I don’t know anymore.We can’t spend enough time together for me to figure out if I like you.It will take long to find out…what if I don’t?I don’t want to hurt you… it just makes me sad.Show me… open my eyes for I can’t do it by myself…I need you, I need your help.Was hoping to see you, but you never came.
