True beauty is not that of body features or a intelligent tongue… true beauty is belongs to your heart… and your heart itself.
If you love everything about yourself… you are truly beautiful.
If you would never change anything… you are’ truly beautiful.
If you can forgive the sins of yourself… and most the sins of others… you are truly beautiful.
If you can see beyond the world’s assumptions and the shallow accusations of god… you are truly beautiful.
If you believe in yourself when no one else will… you are truly beautiful.
If you stand up after you’ve been pushed down… you are truly beautiful.
If you won’t change for anyone you are truly beautiful.
If you don’t use fancy word, pretending you have to try and prove yourself to anyone you are truly beautiful.
If you can smile because everything will be all right… you are truly beautiful.::sniffles::
