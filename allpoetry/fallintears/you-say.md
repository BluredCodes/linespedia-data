title: "You Say"
writer: "FallinTears"

You say you miss me times infinity
You say you’ll always be there
You say you’ll never let me go
As you run you hands through my hair

You say you think I’m sexy
And my hand you hold
You whisper when you kiss me
Wrapped up out of the cold

You say you want to be with me
But two months have gone by
What am I supposed to think?
I’m scared to even try

You say you want a picture of me
So you can see me every day
I have most forgotten your face
And the price I pay

I long for you in the midnight hours
Your arms strong ‘round my waist
On the phone your voice so sweet
Not in a rush nor haste

I must remember our first kiss
A fairy tale one at that
At the carnival, on the ferris wheel
Our next one up to bat

But the kiss I remember most
Was our last one at the door
Two months have gone by
Without any more

You say you’ll try your best to see me
It's not your fault you live far away
I’m jealous of the other people
That see theirs everyday

You say you miss me times infinity
I really wish to believe
But trust is hard for me to find
For many do deceiveI miss you... I want you here with me.
