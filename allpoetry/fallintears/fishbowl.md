Trapped in a box
No holes to breathe
A clear fish bowl
All I do is see

See everything that’s around me
I can never be part of
Pounding on these glass walls
Sending out my warning dove

Get me out get me out
Get me out of this place
I can see most anything
But I can’t see your face

Get me out get me out 
Get me some help
Please let me breath
And get away from myselfCan't get away from myself... I'm always here.
