On any angel hear my call.

Where can you be now?

I know I’m going to fall!

How can I let you know how…

To see my feelings?

Sometimes I hold inside,

What makes me the way I am.

Love, hope, pain, and pride,

I am trying to do all I can…

To help.

The poor human beings,

Some being like me.

To come to private seeings,

Loving forever to be…

Someone’s Guardian Angel
