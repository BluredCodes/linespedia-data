Like the diamonds that shine in your eyes
Speak through me
Let me feel what you feel
Let me understand
Take my hand and show me
Tell me where to go

Like the diamonds that shine in your eyes
Speak through me
Let me hurt when you hurt
Let me feel your pain
Maybe we’ll be the same together
Tell me where to go

Stand up
Sit down
Forwards 
Backwards
All around

Wait a minute
Not it’s okay
Yes 
No
Will you stay?

Like the diamonds that shine in your eyes
Speak through me
Let me know what you are doing
I can’t wait forever
It’s all up to you
Tell me where to go

Like the diamonds that shine in your eyes
Speak through me
He loves me
He loves me not
Will I get the chance…
