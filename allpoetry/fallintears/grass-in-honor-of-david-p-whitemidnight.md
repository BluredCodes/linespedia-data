I bet that you haven’t ever
Heard the story of grass
So sit here for a minute
While I fill up my glass

Its green you know did you?
That’s what color it is
Hold on just one second
I’ve got to take a wiz

Okay I’m back where was I?
Hummm can you remember?
Oh yes it is still green
When the month is September

Sometimes grass is brown
That’s only when it gets rotten
There was something else about it
Oh no! I have forgotten

I already told you right?
Oh I did is that what you say?
Maybe I should save this story
For another day

No I think I remember
What were we talking about again?
Oh that’s it yes
We were talking about my friend

Oh no you say that’s not it?
Oh yes now I remember
How the grass is still green
Even in September 

You’ve got to cut it
When it grows
You’ve got to water it 
When it’s dry
And when there are dandelions
I get this itch in my eye

It’s starting to get on my nerves 
So that’s it about grass
So kids what have you learned?
