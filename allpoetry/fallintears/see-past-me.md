See past me
In my eyes look for something deep
Probe them with your true care
With your love I wish to keep

See past me
Know I’m not all that strong
Understand it’s how people perceive me
Help me move me along

See past me
Know that I can find my way out of it
I know drugs aren’t the answer
But they work; do you really give a shit?

See past me
Worry when I say you shouldn’t
Know when I need you most
Help me when I say I couldn’t

See past me
Know when to say nothing
Talk to me before
I turn back to cutting

See past me
Know why things happen
Smile when I smile
Always keep me laughing

See past me
Know when things are not “ok”
Make me tell you everything
And face the next dayNo one understands... and no one ever will.
