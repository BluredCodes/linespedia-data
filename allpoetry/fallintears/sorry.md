Sorry
Is that is you’re sorry
Are you really? Are you sure?
I bet you’re just saying that.
Stop it.
Stop being sorry.
Your making me go insane.
Why?
Why do *I* have to be this way?
Stop it.
Stop being this way.
Separate me from my soul.
I only want one part.
The happy one.
Stop it.
Stop being a brat.
I can’t get out of this hole.
I have slipped back inside yet again.
Help me.
Please… help me.Whatever... really doesn't matter to me anymore.
