I want to love you
Yes I know I want to
I am afraid
Afraid of love
And afraid
Because I don’t know you
I haven’t yet met you
And I can’t wait for the day

I’m tired of being sad
All alone with myself to cry to
I need you now
And I want to love you
Yes I know I want to
I am afraid
Afraid of love
And afraid… that if I love you
You wont…
Love me

But I can only wait for they day
Love myself
And dream of you
Until I meet you…
Only to find out you are not perfect
I dream you are but I know you won’t be
I’m tired of waiting
It’s not fair…

I’m the last one in line…
The day they ran out
Of loveI'm tired of waiting and being alone... doing all I can and have it no be enough... reaching and being to short... not giving up... but dieing anway.
