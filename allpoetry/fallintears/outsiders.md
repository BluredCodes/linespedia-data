title: "Outsiders"
writer: "FallinTears"

Water streaks the windows
Of my soft and sullen room
A soft staccato melody
Lost beneath its gloom

Dark as the night but it's not
Illusions played in your eyes
Drops of blood are falling
In the rain they do disguise

Of you up in the heavens
Your tears they fall as rain
Drowning out your enemies
Revenge is what you gain

You heart has fallen out your chest
It’s pieces broken and bruised
Seeping through the cracks
Life’s blood you loose

That’s how you got where you are today
That’s why I’m here all alone
Sitting by my window
Waiting for you to come home

Words that other people spoke
Stabbed you through and through
But I’m sitting by my door
Waiting just for you

I’m here day and night
Memories in my head
The though of you not coming home
Is nothing that I dread

The silent tears that I weep
Fall into my breast
Hoping to hear your voice
To cure the aching in my chest

And I will wait for you here
Until you decide to come back
Wanting what was once yours
My love is what you lack

Until you can not care what people say
Until you can handle it all
I’ll be sitting by my phone
Waiting for you to call

So don’t worry about me
I’ll be just okay
Because I am stronger than you
I don’t care what they sayPeople are brainwashed.
