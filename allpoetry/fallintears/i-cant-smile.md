I can’t smile
For there are angry words on my tongue that are fighting to roll of my lips… and land on your ears.
I can’t smile
For if I do the corners of my mouth will slide up my cheeks and push into my eyeballs… making the absent tears slide down my face
I can’t smile
For if I do I would think of how you used to kiss me and hold me in your arms… then how you let me go
I can’t smile
For I know you don’t care for me anymore… and why would anyone be smiling about something like that
I can’t smile
For I hate you with all of my heart… with all… of my body… with all of my soul. You lead me on and deserve not even a smile from me.
I can’t smile
For there are angry words on my tongue that are fighting to roll off my lips… and land on your ears.I can't do it... I just can't.
