I’ve got me this feeling and it just won’t go away
The same feeling I get when I see you everyday
Every time you touch me a chill goes through my spine
Feeling a bit shaky when your hand is clasped in mine

My love my secret love
Where have you been hiding?
What have you been dong?
Are you even trying?
For me my love for me
Can you see beneath my eyes?
Know what’s on the inside
And see behind the lies?

I’ve got me this feeling and it just won’t go away
The same feeling I get when I see you everyday
Every time we talk and I stare in your eyes
The love in my heart is one I must disguiseDamn... this one sucks too.
