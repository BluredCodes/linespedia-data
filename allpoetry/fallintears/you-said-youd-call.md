I’m sitting here
Waiting for you
You said you’d call me
I got ready just in case
You said you were coming to pick me up
Running the brush through my hair I think of you
You never called
I’m still sitting here
Waiting for you
You said you’d call me
It’s like this all the time
It’s like an abusive relationship
And I just don’t want to leave
When it eats you up inside… and you think…
How could you do this to me?
But I want to be with you
The clock became my enemy
Cruel and heart wrenching
Each time the second hand moved
Another stab to my heart
I’m still waiting… waiting for you to call
Or show up at my door
I look in the mirror
And try not to cry
What would happen if you had show up just then?
My make-up would have run
You can’t see me how I normally am
I’m made up for you
Are you corrupting me?
Or are you good medicine for my sorrows?
I’m still waiting
