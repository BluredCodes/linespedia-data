Alone are the thoughts that wander through the void
Between my ears are the memories that I had
Forgotten are the tears that I cried
Late is what you’ve always been 
Never did you say you loved me it was
Always my turn to give you love but you
Didn’t care what I felt you just kept
Using me like I was blind

~The first word of every sentence connects to the last word of the first. For example Alone are the thoughts that wander through the void between
Between my ears are the memories that I had~So it sucks... go easy one me ^-^
