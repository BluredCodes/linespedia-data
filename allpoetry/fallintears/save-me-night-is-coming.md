The shadows elongate
Suggesting the oncoming of night
I shiver from the change of wind
And wrap my arms around myself
I can see your image drifting in and out
A smile cracks upon my face
A soft laugh escaping from my lips
The stars start to twinkle
As I look up into the sky
Reminded of the little rhyme from my childhood
Star light star bright
First star I see tonight
I wish I may I wish I might
Wish upon this star tonight.
I softly close my eyes biting on my lip
I make a wish as I start to walk again
The wind pushes my hair in my face.
Suddenly I feel very alone and venerable
As if someone is watching me
Every step I take, they know where I go,
And where I’m going.
I call out for you
Thinking in the recesses of my mind that you’ve come to find me
The world begins to turn, my head feeling light.
I fall on the ground.
You’re name echoing on my lips
I know you can hear me
I just know you can
So why won’ t you come?
Come help me up?
Can’t you see?
That I need you
