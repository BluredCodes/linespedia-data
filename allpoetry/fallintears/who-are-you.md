title: "Who Are You?"

You are new to me, one that you have caught
You know who I am, but how is it…
I do not know you
From a single messenger I have heard
That you want to meet me
Who are you?
I know your name
Ours together sound like music
Whimsical in my heart
I have seen your face
But that of an old picture
I’ve been told you’re tall
Sweet
Caring
And cute
I’ve been told you want to meet me
Who are you?
Elusive… yet it bothers me
Older
That you are
It sends chills up my spine
I want to see you
Will something happen?
I’m dying to know
They say we’d look cute together
I don’t even know you at all
I would like to though
They say you want to meet me…
Who are you?One of my friends told me there is a guy who want's to meet me. I want to know who he is ;-)
