title: "Half A Heart"

If I had half a heart to give
I would give you my own
For it rots deep inside my chest
Loveless and all alone

It suffocates from lack of air
Shrivels from lack of love
Killing any belief I had
Of angels that fly above

No one to give a helping hand
Not there when I need you most
Wasted in my ugly form
No one to hold you close

If I had half a heart to give
I would give you my own
For it rots deep inside my chest
Loveless and all alone

Four chambers open for you
And you can’t even fill one
One can only try so much
And I feel as if I’m done

Maybe I will bleed to death
From the pain I feel inside
If I close my eyes and make a wish
Will the thought of you subside?

If I had half a heart to give
I would give you my own
For it rots deep inside my chest
Loveless and all alone
