I try so hard… to be happy
And love who I am
But its hard… when no one else
Does


Depression
 

Disappointment


Rejection


Loneliness

But it’s okay because I am strong 
And I can pull through
But I always wonder
For how
Long


It Can tear you apart
And you can keep getting back up
But how many
Times

Worn out

Tired

No hope

In sight
