Today is my birthday
Happy birthday to me
This day couldn't have been worse
If I had planned it
 
I woke up late
And no one was at school
Due to our fantastic 3-day field trip
I got in trouble for sleeping in class
I lost so many things
My instrument mouthpiece to be exact
And I hadn’t even paid for it yet
It was cold
And dreary
And it was Monday
I couldn’t make any decisions
And I was cranky
And depressed

Today is my birthday
Happy birthday to me
This day couldn't have been worse
