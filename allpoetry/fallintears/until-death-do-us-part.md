Soft and silent but you still come my way. 
Inch by inch, step by step, block by block, mile by mile.
Until you are here at my door.
I would have never guessed I'd see you there, with a swift twist of the knob.
But I was wrong...
What could you possibly want?
I try to keep a level head, but I fail.
Like a snake in a snake charmers basket, dancing away.
Slowly falling into your arms.
Yes this is my love.
I wish you to never leave me.
Hold me, until death do us part.Sad mood. Trying to find one to love.
