When I look around
And read
I find a smile on my face
And a cry in my heart
For others that write poems for each other
Telling stories of how they’ve helped
How much they appreciate them
And how the loved them
But I 
                            H a v e n ’t 
                                                                 H e l p e d 
                                                                                                    A n y o n e
No one appreciates me
And no one loves me either
I wish just once
That someone would 
W r i t e 
                A 
                                 P o e m 
                                                      F o r 
                                                                     M e
I try so hard
But I guess it’s not good enough
Little things make me happy
And I guess now… there aren’t enough
T o
          G o
