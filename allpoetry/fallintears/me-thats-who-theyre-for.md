---
title: "Me, That's Who They're For"
writer: "FallinTears"
slug: "me-thats-who-theyre-for"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Pounding inside of my body
Are these feelings that wish to express
Themselves
Pen in hand I write them down
I’m not trying to please you
Make you cry
Make you laugh
Make you mad
Or make you remorseful
Because they’re not for you
Me
That’s who they’re for
If you enjoy them
Put a smile on your face
A tear in your eye
A race in your heart
Then that’s wonderful for you
But if you hate them
Think they ramble
Make you mad
Think they are horrible
With no meaning
No emotion
No expression
Then I don’t care
You will never understand the meaning behind them
The tears the resurrected the words from my mind
The anger that pulled the lines from my throat
The joy that wrote the songs from my heart
Remember
When you write, read, and critique
That my poems are not for you
Me
That’s who they’re forPeople... they crack me up.
