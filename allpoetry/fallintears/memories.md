Memories
They’re all I remember
Can you remember them too?
Like that time we-
It was so much fun,
The times I spent with you

Memories
They’re all I remember
Can you remember them too?
Like when we went-
They were the times of my life,
The times I spent with you

Memories
They’re all I remember
Can you remember them too?
‘Cuz now your gone,
And how I miss…
The times I spent with youRight now I am having problems with my father, again. I hate him so much right now. I wish he were gone, but that makes me think. God why do I have a conscience?
