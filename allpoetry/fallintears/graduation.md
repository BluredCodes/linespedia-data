title: "Graduation"
writer: "FallinTears"

I look back on the day,
When we said our good-byes.
And I will always say,
That I’m glad I cried.
I remember when we looked at each other’s faces,
And cried because the other was crying.
Leaving behind three years of security,
Leaving you, to go on to a place unknown…
I never wanted to leave,
I wanted to be there to the end.
And now I’m glad to say,
I love you like a best friend.
I look back on the day,
When we said our good-byes.
And I will always say.
That I’m glad I cried.
I’m not leaving you forever,
Just to a different place.
It was so, so sad.
To see the tears fall down your face.
You will know how it feels,
When your time comes to an end.
And I hope you’ll be glad to say,
You love me like a best friend.
I look back on the day,
When we said our good-byes.
And I will always say,
That I’m glad I cried.
We shared so many secrets,
Some that none will ever know.
And just the thought of,
You not being there in the morning.
It makes me sad to think,
I will have to wait to share my thoughts.
But I want you to know,
That I value “you “ as a person
So much, no one can explain.
I look back on the day,
When we said our good-byes.
And I will always say.
That I’m glad I cried.
I need a friend so much like you.
I want you to be near me,
I want to feel loved.
And make you feel loved.
Never change, 
Because I will always remember you.
I know I may pretend,
To be one way,
But you know how I am always.
You can see right thought the act.
You can see the real pain inside.
I look back on the day,
When we said our good-byes.
And I will always say.
That I’m glad I cried.
But did you know?
That I am not going to say good-bye,
I will see you again.
I’ll make sure I see you again.
I’m not letting you get past me.
Whether you like it or not,
I’m going to love you like a best friend.
Forever I will never forget.
I look back on the day,
When we said our good-byes.
And I will always say.
That I’m glad I cried.
I will talk to you tomorrow,
Keep me in your thoughts.
And as I go off to high school
Remember me as I was.
I look back on the day,
When we said our good-byes.
And I will always say.
That I’m glad I cried.
I’ll remember you until the end.
I’m so glad you are my best friend.
