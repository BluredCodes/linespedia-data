Rivers of salt
With water mixed inside
Flowing down my face
The pain in my chest I hide

You know there’s something wrong
Read my mind for what it is
The hole in my chest is gaping
For the love that once was his

My voice is weak and pained
He leaves me there in silence
And my trust’s been shattered
Lost in its defiance

Blood mixed with worry
Anger mixed with love
Faces of you haunt
The stars that shine above

Picking up the pieces
Breath comes short and slow
Sorting them to piles
Of memories long ago

I glue the pictures together
Small and shattered pairs
Stamped on and crumpled
By a herd of wild mares

My heart aches for changing
For the times of long ago
Wanting to forget that lonely day
The day you let me go

Finished is the puzzle
One of love and hate
Thus a broken portrait of you
Is made upon my plateI think this is one of my better pieces... what do you think?
