I’m sorry that I'm not
Just the same as you
I’m sorry that I don’t
Have all the things you do
I’m sorry that I’m not
The same as you are
I’m sorry that I’m so
Different from you by far

I’m sorry that I can’t
Answer as quick as can be
I’m sorry that you have to
Make so much fun of me
I’m sorry that you have to
Make a big deal and throw a fit
I’m sorry that I can’t
Deal with all this shit

I’m sorry that I’m not
A perfect girl like you
I’m sorry that I don’t
Like everything you do
I’m sorry that I have to
Pretend to be your friend
I’m sorry that I have to
Be there ‘till the end

I’m sorry that you don’t
Have any problems at all
I’m sorry that you have
Someone to be there when you fall
I’m sorry that you have to
Do everything just right
I’m sorry that you can’t
Stay out that late at night

I’m sorry that I have to
Be the one outcast
I’m sorry that I have to
Always come in last
I’m sorry that I’m not
A perfect girl like you
I’m sorry that I can’t
Do everything you do

I’m sorry that I can’t
Smile and be all bright
I’m sorry that I can’t
Let it go and not fight
I’m sorry that I’m not
Blind to the world like you
I’m sorry that you don’t
Realize what you do

I’m sorry that you are
The little perfect girl
I’m sorry that your life
Is one big chocolate swirl
I’m sorry that not everyone
Can be perfect like you
I’m sorry that you can’t see
What the fuck you do

I’m sorry that you
Don’t know who you are
I’m sorry that you have to
Look at me from afar
I’m sorry that you can’t
Grasp life with your entire soul
I’m sorry that you are
Dug in your little hole

I’m sorry that you can’t
Come out and take a peek
I’m sorry that might
Make your perfection leak
I’m sorry that you won’t 
Ever fully enjoy your life
I’m sorry that you won’t
Suffer from the scythe

I’m sorry that you can’t
Come out into the world
I’m sorry that you have to
Sit and not be hurled
I’m sorry that you have to
Try and convert ones to be like you
I’m sorry that is
All the things you do

So I’m sorry I’m not perfect
And I’m sorry I’m not you
I’m sorry that I have problems
I’m sorry… that’s what I do
So I'm sorry I'm not perfect
I’m sorry you are afraid to be yourself
I'm sorry that you have
