Seeing is BelievingMy eyes are closedNo light shines beneathThey will not openNot even for youTo see is to liveTo live is to seeBut I cannot seeDoes that mean I’m dead?I’m blind to the worldI am a nobodyNo one cares about meOnly you…One personIs that enough to matter?Will that ever be enough?Will that be the key,For the eternally locked door?My eyesI need my eyes!To see youTo see your faceI am hereAlone to wonderIf you’ll ever be The diamonds in the ruffMy eyes are closedNew light shines beneathWill they ever open?Open for you?Rate this, and check out my others!
