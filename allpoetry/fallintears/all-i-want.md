All I want is someone that knows me. Someone to take care of me. Someone who can look in my eyes and know something is wrong, and what it is. Someone that makes a little something tingle in the depths of my body. All I want is someone to not say anything and hold me, making me feel safe at the same time. All I want is someone who cares, as much as a care about them. All I want… is someone who loves me for who I am.Searching it's an endless search...
