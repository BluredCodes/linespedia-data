Star Light Star BrightGazing up into the night, I focused all of my attention the smallest pinpoint in the sky.I blocked out everything but the star, getting lost in the bright, twinkling haze surrounding it.I drifted threw a tunnel of my own time and space, relaxing into he manifestation in my mind. Lying comfortably in a timeless, weightless bed of meditation.I thought about the star, and the star only. Drifting into the dark unused recesses of my mind. I was completely oblivious to the so-called “world” around me.It was then that I felt a light tingling sensation on my skin. I felt, what I could only describe as a hand, reaching out to me.Not a human hand, but a soft foggy hand, only one of the five, belonging to the star. Now I could say I was lost in my reverie.My whole body was at peace. Everything was calm and level. I was lost but not in distress, in comfortable security. I was still, unmoving, but alive.Help me out!
