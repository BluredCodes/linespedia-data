All that I am
And all that I’ll be
Doesn’t mean nothing
If you aren’t here with me

And you can feel young
And I can feel old
No one to find
And no one to hold

And I don’t care
What you have to say
If the sun doesn’t rise
Into a new day

Your heart will be broken
And covered blood
And you will see the stars
That shine from above

And in your eyes
Are tears of pain
Flow down your cheeks
In wonder and vain

And they won’t stop
Till there dry as sand
No one to turn to
Or hold you hand

Can you fell me?
I’m in your heart
It doesn’t matter
That where apart

Cuz I’m here with you
You just have to look
Deep in you body
Within your book

Waves of pleasure
And socks of joy
Sent with a message
From your little boy

We all love you deeply
With our hand to our chest
And my tears fall for you
And slide to my breast

So don’t forget me
Cuz I’m thinking about you
Just remember
What you’ve got to do

And think of me always there
With a smile on my face
Where I should be
Down in my place

And one day
We’ll be together
Somewhere
As light as a feather

So just keep going
Living you life
And don’t dwell on 
Your pain and strife

Cuz I’m here with you
You just have to look
Deep in you body
Within your bookJust came to me... flowed through my veins.
