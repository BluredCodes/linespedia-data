title: "Bleeding Heart"
writer: "FallinTears"

I feel as though someone has ripped my heart out of my chest
Squeezed it, let the blood run down their arm
Let it drip
Drip
Drip
Making a song… a song of my bleeding heart
You stab needles through it
Ever bleeding heart
Sending rivers of blood coursing in streams to the floor
You step it in
Footprints…
Of my blood
They remind me of you
You left
And took my heart with you
All the way home it bled
You pin it on your wall
Your prize
My heart
It drips to make a picture
So you will never forget me
I want to forget you
Drip
Drip
You let it drip
But I can’t
Blood footprints stain my floor
My blood
From my ever bleeding heart
It bleeds for you
It bleeds for me
You smile 
I cry
You laugh
I cry
You yell
I cry
You stab
I bleed
Salt water 
River of blood
YOU its all your fault
That my heart bleeds
For you
For me
No for you
Bleeding…
