I wonder why it is
You can’t ever ask me anything
For yourself
Is it that hard?

Why can’t you swallow your pride?
Get off your high horse?
And stop fucking around?

It’s not fair to me
But that’s right
I won’t tell you you’re wrong

Because then you think
I’ve got something to hide
And I aint got anything

For you at least
Cuz you had your chance
So get over it

I did
So it can’t be that hard for you
Stop think about yourself… for once

Ask me your questions
Don’t lie to yourself
Don’t lie to everyone else

Cuz you don’t know anything
Only believe what you want
And assume the restDamn
