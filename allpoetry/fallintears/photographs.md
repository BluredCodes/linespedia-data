Memories kept for a lifetime
Feelings etched in stone
Expressions never changing
In night or light that shone

Moving but kept still
In color or black and white
Seeming just to capture
The width the depth the height


Atmosphere is paused
Keeping the memories alive
And yet it death they have it
Their memories did surviveMarie, my sweet Marie. I knew you fairly I loved you dear. Marie, my sweet Marie, it's almost been a year...
