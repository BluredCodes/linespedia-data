---
title: "Game"
writer: "FallinTears"
slug: "game"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

There is the pain that radiates
From deep inside my chest
Its wave of depression clogs my throat
And won’t ever let me rest

You think that you understand
But I’m not like you at all
You’re just waiting stabbing me
Hoping in time I’ll fall

That’s what I feel like doing
Giving up and giving in
But I could never live it down
No, I wont let you win

Can’t get out of it
Addicted to depression
I’m almost over it all now
And it starts another session

The worlds leach is what I am
Here let me take your pain
Cycles over and over again
The never ending game

With many winners but the same looser
I can’t handle it at all anymore
I’m tired of always carrying the ball 
And have it taken away when I try to score

So I’m sorry I’m not brainwashed
And I’ve got a bit of luck
So you can shut up and leave me alone
Look’s like your time is upJust go away.
