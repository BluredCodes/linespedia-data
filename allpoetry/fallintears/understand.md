Understand me yes that’s it; it’s what I want you to do.
Don’t think to hard you never know what you might come up with.
Don’t think you know because trust me… you have no idea. 
Why does it have to be so hard?

Understand me yes that’s it; it’s what I want you to do.
I need answers and I need them now.
Problem is I don’t know where to get them… where shall I go?
I am screaming dieing from the inside out.

Understand me yes that’s it; it’s what I want you to do.
Help me and yet save me at the same time.
Understanding me also. Completely and fully.
Is there no one out there, no one out there to help?
No one out there to help a poor lost little girl?Help me out away from myself. Please...
