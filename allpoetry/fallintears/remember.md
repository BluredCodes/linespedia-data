title: "Remember"

I can see you standing there.
The same way I left you.
A distant look in your eyes,
You hands behind your back.
The wind blowing in you hair,
My last kiss upon your lips.
You don’t seem to understand, 
That you hurt me too bad.
You hurt me too bad to be forgiven.
You shattered my trust in you.
Everything that we had together… is lost.
I will never forget your face,
The day I left you standing there.
Because there was something,
Something that just stood out.
It haunts me each and every day.
Every time I meet someone new, 
Something about them reminds me,
Reminds me of you…
That last kiss still burning on my lips.
Now I see how I have hurt you.
And the question is turned around.
Can you ever forgive me?Help me out!
