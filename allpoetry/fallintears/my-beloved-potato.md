title: "My Beloved Potato"

I once had me a potato
And I can of play-doh
And with that I had a tomato
All inside a potato

And when I threw that potato
It smashed with a squirt of tomato
And a big old glob of play-doh
When I threw that potato

Then I cleaned up the potato
And I threw out some tomato
And I recycled that play-doh
That was once inside the potato

That’s the story of my potato
Filled with the can of play-doh
And a big red tomato
My beloved potato
