title: "Oh Won't You Just Be Mine"

There is this space inside my chestA gaping hole so bigI try to clean up the restOf the shattered pieces all around itEach time I put them back togetherAnd wait for you aloneIt’s seems just about foreverWhen you stomp on them againEach time I pick them upI always think about what you’ve donePlacing the pieces in a cupI glue them in a portrait of youYour face makes me shiverWhen I look deep in your eyesMy knees how the quiverOh won’t you just be mineJust toolin around!
