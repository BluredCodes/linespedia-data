Why do I have to
Put on a mask each day?
Chang into a different person
For my soul I wish to save

Why do I have to
Keep what I feel inside?
‘Cuz others don’t want to hear it
And the real me has to hide. 

Why do I have to
Be the fucked up way I am?
I just want to be myself
And hang out with my friends

Why do I have to
Put on a mask each day?
I just shouldn’t do it anymore
For my soul cannot be savedPerils plague me.
