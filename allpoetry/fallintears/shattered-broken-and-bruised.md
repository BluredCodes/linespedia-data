title: "Shattered Broken and Bruised"

There are tears running down my face…
Because I don’t like you
I can’t stand you
And you won’t leave me alone

There is a pain in my heart
Because I know you do it just to annoy me
And to know that
I can’t change you

Can’t get away

From the pain you cause
People… in general
Loveless and all alone
In a world of hate

A name in a faceless crowd
A body in a material world
A heart among a pincushion
A dream amongst defeat

All alone

With myself
Never the right people
Wanting not getting
Waiting not finding

Good things come…
But not for me
I try just to get left behind
Shattered… broken… and bruisedTired of dealing.
