You’re never here when I need youYou’re always here when I want you to go awayYou are the only one that understandsEven when you don’t you make me feel safeSafe wrapped up in your armsNothing can hurt me because someone caresSomeone cares about me and that’s all that mattersNothing can make me madNothing will make you go awayYou’ll hold me there foreverSafe…Can you hear me?You don’t know how much I need youHow much I need you now…
