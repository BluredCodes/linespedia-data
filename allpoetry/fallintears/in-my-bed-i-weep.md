IN MY BED I WEEP It's 12:24 am And I can not sleep The darkness surrounds me In my bed I weep I don't know what to do I am so confused My life is so messed up I am not at all amused As I cuddle my pillow Turning onto my side My tears soak the blanket You can't say I haven't tried All my attempts have failed I am going to sleep I can't stand it anymore IN MY BED I WEEP…Just sad...
