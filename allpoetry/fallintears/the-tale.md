Buried beneath the sands of timeIs a tale so twisted and cruelA tale of death and heartacheAnd of man, the stubborn muleA tale of eyes closed shutA tale of the unforgivingA tale of utmost moralsA tale of man, sinningA tale of beliefs that clashedA tale of cold-blooded warA tale of lonely sorrowOf man, the wild boarBuried beneath the sands of timeThe tale that is old, but newA tale of nothing endingA tale of man, for youA tale of utter selfishnessA tale of blinded prideA tale of shattered dreamsThe tale of man, will not subside.A tale of clouds covering the sunA tale of two wrongs don’t make a rightA tale of broken bonesA tale that will end tonightBuried beneath the sands of timeThe tale shall stay foreverThe tale of eyes closed shutOf man, finally got cleverCourage built the bridgeBut jealously knocked it downRemember that foreverSmile, never frownDream3485@yahoo.com
