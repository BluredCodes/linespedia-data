---
title: "Broken"
writer: "FallinTears"
slug: "broken"
source: "AllPoetry"

I knew that it was all my fault
There was nothing I could do about it
You didn’t share the same feelings as I
And I was sad…don’t doubt it
We thought about things different ways
We just didn’t see eye to eye
And I guess that’s all I’m missing from
That big red heart in the sky
