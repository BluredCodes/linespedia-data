---
title: "A Rose"
writer: "FallinTears"
slug: "a-rose"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

It’s held deep within your heart...
It’s capable of blooming beautifully
Bringing joy to anyone that sees it
Making the world a better place
You can also crush it
It can die
Wither
Wilt
Fade away
If not taken care of
It can be ugly
And worst of all it can break
Thorns may prick you
Held deep within your heart...
A rose::sigh::
