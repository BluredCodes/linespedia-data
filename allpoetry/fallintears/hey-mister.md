Hey mister what are you doing?Hey mister what is that?What is wrong with you?Why are you looking at me like that?Why are you drooling?Why are your eyes so red?Hey mister what are you holding?Hey little girl is that what you said?Hey mister you have a cut.Hey mister PUT THAT AWAY!Why are you trying to scare me?Are you having a bad day?Hey mister is that thing loaded?Really now mister, take a step back.Okay, I’m really scared now.What are you going to do with that?Help me help me someone!!Hey mister is that your cat?Please I don’t know what to do!Hey mister why are you laughing like that?Hey mister your finger’s on the trigger.Why are you pulling it back?What did I ever do to you?Hey mister you’re stepping in a garbage sack.Hey mister there’s a bullet in my stomach.Hey mister I’m on the floor.Hey mister I don’t know what to do.I’ve never been dieing before.Hey mister be careful,You’re stepping in my blood.My world is going black.Where’s my mommy? I need a hug.My eyes are slowly closing.Hey mister wake me up,When my mommy comes to get me.I’m sure she’ll help me up.Anything help me out. Dream3485@yahoo.com
