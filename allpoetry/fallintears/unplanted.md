title: "Unplanted"

Last words spoken in vengeance
You knew what you wanted to say
But it chocked inside your throat
And clogged it… stuck there

Breath taken away
By the words you had to say
You didn’t think of it then
So how does it feel when
Your words are twisted all around
And what you thought you had found
Is shattered and stepped upon
Like the flicker of love that is now gone

You thought you were the only one
That mattered… yes you did
Don’t deny it because now it’s your turn
And it’s all coming back

Breath taken away
By the words you had to say
You didn’t think of it then
So how does it feel when
You cry because you are broken
Lost without the token
Of what you took for granted
Loves seed left… unplanted
