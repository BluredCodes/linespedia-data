Seek and you will find me
I’m waiting just for you
Be careful just don’t break me
No matter what you do

I’m so close can you feel me?
Reach out and take my hand
Don’t be afraid I wont hurt you
Come to my land

You know exactly what you want
So won’t you come to me?
We’ll be so very happy together
Don’t worry about she

Seek and you will find me
I’m waiting just for you
Be careful just don’t break me
No matter what you doFragile Handel With Care
