I Just Need You To Hold MeI just need you to hold me And let me cry on your shoulder I just need you to hold me Keep me from getting colder I just need you to hold me And hug me tight I just need you to hold me All through the night I just need you to hold me Listen don't speak I just need you to hold me Cuz' my knees are gettin weak I just need you to hold me Make me feel loved I just need you to hold me Wrapped up in a hug I just need you to hold me Snuggle me dear I just need you to hold me Whisper so I can hear I just need you to hold me Make me feel safe I just need you to hold me And wipe the tears off my face I just need you to hold me And play with my hair I just need you to hold me Be my big teddy bear I just need you to hold me If you love me let me know I just need you to hold me And never let goPlease read all my poems and help me out!
