title: "Smile Be Happy"

Smile be happy
Don’t worry don’t cry
Smile be happy
Look life straight in the eye
Smile be happy
You wouldn’t want to know
Smile be happy 
Don’t you ever go
Smile be happy
Hold your head up high
Smile be happy
And don’t wonder why
