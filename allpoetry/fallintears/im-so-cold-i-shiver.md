title: "I'm so cold... I shiver"

I’m so cold I shiver
Yet there is no wind
I’m so cold I shiver
But the lights aren’t dim
I’m so cold I shiver
I don’t understand
I’m so cold I shiver
Come and take my hand

Take me away
Away from everything that haunts me
Take me away
So nobody can see
Take me away
From all the shadowed pain
Take me away
Make me warm again

I’m so cold I shiver
But the temperature is high
I’m so cold I shiver
Keeps me wondering why
I’m so cold I shiver
Wipe this look off my face
I’m so cold I shiver
Get me out of this place
