Why do people make other people mad?
Sure there are lots of reasons
But if you saw all the way down… to the inside
It’s all a jealousy battle
Wanting things you can’t have
And hating others because they have it
Not being able to be what you want to be
When other people can
Being forced to do things you don’t want to do
When other people don’t even say a word
Sure we all want things we can’t have…
But I’m not jealous of anyone… really
Because the problem with jealous people
Is that they don’t love themselves
You’ve got to love yourself… before other people can
You have to be first satisfied and happy with what you have
Then go out for more
Jealousy is the root of all evil
When you think about it… it’s the truth
Take wars for example
Jealous for land… jealous for technology… money
All of it could have been avoided
On the contrary I’m not saying you shouldn’t go out and get what you want
Because I believe it you want it… you can have it
You’ve just got to know the right way to go about it
Shut up
And think for a minute
Just think about yourself for a while…
It isn’t possible to be jealous of yourself…
Now is it?
Relax and take everyday for what it’s worth
A step at a time
Breath by breath
Know that you can have what ever you want
Eventually
And be happy for the good fortunes of other peopleStupid little rambling of my minds. Trying to make the world etter for the people around me... and of course... myself ^-^
