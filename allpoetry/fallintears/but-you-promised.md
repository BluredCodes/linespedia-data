You promised you would come see me today
And here I am I waiting
I’ve got on my nice new clothes
My make up’s done
My hair brushed
Waiting for you to call me
You promised you would come see me today
Because you couldn’t yesterday
Or eight weeks before that
And I believed you
It might not be your fault
But you promised
Now I’m just writing to ease the pain
To be stabbed
I need my Cheri’… but she’s not here
She’s out…
Where I’d like to be
You promised you would come see me today
And here I am waiting
My legs are falling asleep
And my eyes burn with fire
The minutes tick away…
And now I don’t know what to do
You promised me 
You liar
