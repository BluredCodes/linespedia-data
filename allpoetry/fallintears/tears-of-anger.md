The tears that spilled down my cheeks
They were hot… with anger
And they burned my face
But still I thought of you

Who am I fooling?
What made me think that you were different?
You’re just like everyone else
And you made me want to change for you

But I won’t and I might say I don’t care
But I do… and you hurt me
Not that you care
You’ve been lying to me…
And I was to blind to see it

Why can’t I make you see?
Why won’t you believe me?
You hurt me more than you will ever know
And I could die tomorrow…
And I bet
That you
Won’t 
CareI hate you... right about... now.
