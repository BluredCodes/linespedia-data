I heard you crying
You wake up with a story to tell
You feel like you're dying
And your life's a living hell

You don't know where you're going
Or how you're going to get there
You don't know why you're towing
The beast inside it's lair

Thoes tears of anguish
And that look in your eyes
Makes the little things perish
And fall down and die

Your mother says she loves you
And she'll cry when your gone
You're with the angels above you
Not alone for long

You miss the little things
That you once took for granted
And that song your little heart sings
When not left empty handed

You're scared and alone with time to think
Of what really means to you
And everytime you take a drink
You don't know what your gunna do

Why can't you see past this?
And not care what people do?
Like that last kiss on you lips
From somone that loves you

What's wrong with your mind?
Why are you so inconsistant?
Your eyes have left beind
That you can't take a hint

No one understands
And no one ever will
No one will take your hand
And help you to chill

It's too hard right now
Too hard to bear
Fallen you wonder how
With a hand in your hair

You can't move or even think
Completly paralyzed
Where's your missing link?

For the record I do not think *I* believe in god or anything of the sort, so it was even stranger to write it.
