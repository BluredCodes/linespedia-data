title: "Tell Me, What Is It?"

I just can’t seem to have
One single good day
Something always fucks it up
No matter what I say
I try my hardest I do
And when I think it’s almost over
But someone just stamped on
My only four-leaf clover

Little things make me angry
Stupid things make me cry
I can’t get out of the hole I’m in
I really don’t know why
People are annoying
I can’t stand them anymore
Especially a certain someone
I just can’t get away from that whore

I want to be alone
Like by myself not really
I don’t know how to explain it
To make you all see clearly
What happens when it’s over?
And I can’t handle all this shit
What am I supposed to do?
Tell me what is it?What's wrong with me, really I'd like to know?
