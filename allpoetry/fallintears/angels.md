If the wind speaks to you On a night the moon is roundIf the grass squeaks at youAs you walk upon the groundIf the stars blink at youAs you look up in the skyIf the mist falls around youYou won’t have to ask whyIf the crickets serenade youAnd you stop to hear their songIf the trees wave to youAs you slowly trod alongIf the water falls on youAnd you shiver in the nightIf the birds call to youThey’ll take away your frightIf the rocks move for youYou shall never fallIf the bunny hops for youYou’ve lost nothing at allIf you can close your eyesAnd see nothing but loveThen you know there’s someoneWatching you from aboveThere is always and If. And If you can see that everything is okay... then you know someone loves you.
