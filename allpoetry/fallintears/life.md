The time is 5:23
The day is the 9th
The month is that of May
The year is that of 2001
The city is Coral Springs
The state is that of Florida
The region is the south
The country is the U.S.A.
The continent is North America
The ocean is the Atlantic
The hemisphere is the north
The place is the World
The entirety is the Universe
The galaxy is the Milky Way
Everything is outer space
The life is that of Jennifer LaganaLittle bit different for me.
