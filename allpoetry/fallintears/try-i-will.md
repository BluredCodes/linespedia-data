For you I’d flip a coin
But it really doesn’t matter
Because I’ve come to find
The coin isn’t mine
The flip lands on tails
That means your mine
But it’s not my coin
It’s someone else’s
I’m just about there
And someone steps in front of me
Just my luck
Don’t you think?
I flip another
But history repeats itself
I was hoping
That maybe I’d get my turn
And I’d finally 
Get my chance
Now I see it coming
The door will stay open…
This time
But I am tired of waiting
I don’t want “you” anymore
Because now 
It’s all your fault
You took too long to open your eyes
And yet they still aren’t
Three just aren’t any
More coins for me
Not one will
Ever belong to me
But that’s okay
It’s getting easier
As the days go on
I think I can
Make myself happy
If I try
And try I willI'm trying to make my life better... trying to handel the hard stuff.
