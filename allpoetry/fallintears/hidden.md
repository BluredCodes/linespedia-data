title: "Hidden"
writer: "FallinTears"

A kiss of fire
The flame burns bright
Of the feelings held
Deep within the folds
Of you’re ever aching
Heart of body
Mind over matter
Soul over eyes
Enchanting words
Spoken in whispers
That echo through
The treeless forest
The waterless pond
The heartless love
A tear from the scar
That formed
From the fire
Of hate and depression
Addicted to the feeling of
Pain searing through
The smile of the other
One of life
Anger and oddity
Outcasted to yourself
Alone in a world of loners
Searching for what isn’t there
A smile of hidden truth
A scream of hidden pain
A tear of hidden love
