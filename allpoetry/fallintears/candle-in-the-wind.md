The candle is burning,Flames lick up to the sky.My life is turning,Where, how and why?The wind pushes it away,It is no longer within my reach.As it swims and swaysI can no longer teach.For I don't know what to sayAnymore I am confusedAnd I hope whatever may,Come to you, you use.anything just help me out
