title: "Heart"

Love is in the air
Suffocating
Me down to the core
Pain in my chest
Tears in my eyes
Unable to breathe
Unable to move

Left all alone
All by myself
To watch
Everyone else
Enjoying
Life and love

Nobody cares
Nobody wants to 
Know me
Or anything 
About me

The day I hate
Is coming up soon
I just can’t wait
For it to be over
So I can go back
To pretending

To 
Be
Happy
 
And pretending that

Everyone
Cares
So
Much

All alone
Loveless on the 
Day of love
Nobody cares

No
Valentine
At 
AllI hate valentine's day!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!
