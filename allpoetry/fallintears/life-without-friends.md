title: "Life without friends"

If you didn’t have friends,
What would you do?
No one to hang out with,
No one to loose.
If you didn’t have friends,
Where would you go?
No one to care about you,
No one to Know…
If you didn’t have friends,
Who would you run to?
No one to catch you,
No one to see you.
If you didn’t have friends,
Would you even cry?
Nothing would ever happen,
You would just die.Friends are GREAT
