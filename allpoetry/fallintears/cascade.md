Deep, dark mysterious,

But gentle do you flow.

Holding many secrets,

But none will hear you go.

Trickle trickle down,

Slowly do you go.

Cold and deep but very clear,

Speaking to those you know.

Many people wonder,

But some people know.

Slowly stepping up and down,

Never shall you go.Feelin the NatUrE!
