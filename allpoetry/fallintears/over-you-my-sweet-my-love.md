title: "Over You My Sweet My Love"

Your eyes they sparkle like diamonds
And in them I can see myself
You hands they are like pin pricks
Running over me in wealth

Your face it is of such beauty
That I can never stop staring
You mind is of such complexity 
That my brain is glaring

Your voice is of sweet whispers
That sent my teeth on edge
Your heart is that of gold
It is my love to you I pledge

Your body is one huge mystery
And I have tried long to discover
If you share the same passion for me
Until then I shall hover

Over you my sweet my love
Please just hold me in your arms
Talk to me in whispers
Soothe me with your charms

Over you my sweet my love
Make the perils vanish into thin air
Close my eyes and wipe my tears
Run your hands through my hair

Over you my sweet my love
I just want to see you all the time
I want you hear beside me
Riddle me with rhymes

Over you my sweet my love
It is long since I have waited
To hear tell if you love me
One thing that cannot be debatedI just don't know anymore.
