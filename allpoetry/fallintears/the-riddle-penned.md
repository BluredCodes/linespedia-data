The sides of my heart have been sown and hemmed
Into this book the riddle penned
My love for you so deep and pure
You’re the one for me of that I’m sure

Smiles of reality and tears pain
Shall never revisit me again
For you have chased them… so far gone
As the day is short and the night is long

But I can see it stuck in your grin
The lines of worry that wrinkle your chin
Deep inside I know you are scared
Yet surprise how our love has faired

You helped me see some people were different
And you provided care just so sufficient
That I thought you were my shining star
Sent to me from a land afar

My eyes are closed just thinking of you
Don’t make me open them for I won’t know what to do
I have it right now exactly what I want
And I have every intention to giggle and flaunt

The sides of my heart have been sown and hemmed
Into this book the riddle penned
