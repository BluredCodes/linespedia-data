Why do I keep putting up with this?
It just causes me pain.
Why can’t I just put and end to it.
That’s it… I’m afraid.
I really don’t want to.
Because I think I’ll regret it.
But it just hurts so bad.
To know exactly what you want.
And just as you are about to grab it,
Someone pulls it away.
They laugh at you,
And it’s hard to get back up.
People just don’t understand.
And it’s getting harder to handle
But it’s like it has always been.
I’ll get over it.
Because what else is there to do.
Sit there, and wait for it to come to me.
Destroy me once again?
No, I will dive headfirst into it.
Hopefully, if I get enough of it over with,
Something good will come out of it.
