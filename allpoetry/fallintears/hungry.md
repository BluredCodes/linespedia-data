I can feel it in the pit of despair
That is my stomach
It tumbles over and over
Pulls at my inner walls and twists
Growling it makes me miserable
Starving
For 
You
For the kiss I still can taste
The one that left you on my lips
For that little smile that makes your cheeks wrinkle
Hungry 
For
You
The way you held me in your arms
And made me feel so secure
Like nothing could ever hurt me
Just because you were there
I miss you
I want you
No
I need you
Starving
For 
You
I need you to feed me
Something that once was mine
I can’t do without you
I need you to survive
You
Are my water and sunlight
Hungry…
