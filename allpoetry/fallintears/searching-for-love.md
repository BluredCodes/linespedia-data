Searching for Love
Where can I find it?
What isle is it in?
Where can I find it?
Where has it been?
Where can I find it?
It won’t come to me.
Where can I find it?
Oh, where can it be?
Where can I find it?
Can I make it personalized?
Where can I find it?
Can it have hazel eyes?
Where can I find it?
Do I have to look high?
Where can I find it?
It’s not here, why?
Where can I find it?
Is it big and strong?
Where can I find it?
Is it always wrong?
Where can I find it?
Oh, where can it be?
Where can I find it?
Oh, love come to me.
