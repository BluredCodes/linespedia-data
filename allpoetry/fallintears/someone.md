I don’t understand
Why?
Why do you have to make everything seem worse then it actually is?
Why?
Can’t you see you are only hurting yourself?
But I’m done 
I tried and for once I thought I could make a difference
I thought I did…
But I guess I didn’t
I still don’t understand
Why?
Why you still have to make everything seem worse then it actually is?
Why?
Can’t you see you are only hurting yourself?
I guess you don’t care
Because you don’t make an effort
Or you can’t
Or something like that
I’m not going to keep on acting like everything’s okay
Because it’s not
But it won’t matter to you because you’re trapped in your own little fantasy world
And obviously…
You don’t want to come outWhatever!
