I worked so hard
And that day you said I made it
I had the spot I desired
It was mine
I held in my hands
Inanimate… in my heart

I was waiting
Oh maybe it will come later
For you to put me in where I belonged
My spot
Mine
You can’t have it back
And my heart was full of pain
As it sunk into my stomach
And made me sick

Then you said it to us…
We wouldn’t be doing any
And you were really sorry
To see someone in my spot
Made me want to vomit
It made me sad
It stirred up my roots of anger

And there was no compensation
The others got some
But they didn’t have their spots taken away
Mess up screwed up
Fucked me all over again
And the cycle continues…::sigh:: Colorguard
