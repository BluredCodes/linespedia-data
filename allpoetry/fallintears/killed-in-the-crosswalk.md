---
title: "~Killed In The Crosswalk~"
writer: "FallinTears"
slug: "killed-in-the-crosswalk"
source: "AllPoetry"

I have seen the light
Now I am strong
I can do anything
My life it moves along

There out before me
Painted in white
Was the short little crosswalk
Of my life

Everything that I ever did
Jumped around on those lines
The bad and treacherous lie beside
There they are left behind

The good were placed at the beginning
The wonderful things happening now in the middle
The things I didn’t know were to come
Standing on the other side… belittled

With my foot I took the first step
Putting myself out in the clear
There for people to deceive me
Hate in their words… I hear

I take a step they stab me
Another earns a new wound
A love thrown in between it
A stare with their eyes… I swooned

Moving on more steps for me
Blood in a trail behind
The end of my road is near
No death… did I find

The all at once in a flash of light
Spun around I saw to my surprise
Was a Mac truck barreling towards me
There I saw it… my demise

Honk honk is all it says
To myself I talk
I was almost there
Killed in the crosswalkJust when you thought it was safe...
