title: "Together"
writer: "FallinTears"

I hurt because you hurt
You hurt because I hurt
We hurt together

I love you because you love me 
You love me because I love you
We love together

I have fun with you, because you have fun with me
You have fun with me, because I have fun with you
We have fun together

I talk to you because you listen to me
You talk to me because I listen to you
We talk and listen together

I love life because you are in it
Do you love life because I am in it?
We WILL love life together

Together because we are two
Together always me and you
Together forever togetherMai Cheri' I love you. You are the best!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!
