---
title: "What's Sanity?"
writer: "FallinTears"

I need to find my sanity
I hopped off somewhere
It’s getting dark, and it’s all alone
I whisper to it beware
I wonder if it will come back to me
I need it! Hurry Fast
I don’t know what I should do
I need to make it last
