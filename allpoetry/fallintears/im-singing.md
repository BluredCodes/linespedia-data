I’m singing to myself
And I’m singing about you
Walking along the grass
Kissed by soft morning dew

I’m singing about your eyes
And the way they shine
Sparkling like diamonds
As they stare into mine

I’m singing about your skin
So smooth and baby soft
Rubbing up against it
My heart so high… aloft

I’m singing about your voice
Speaking the words I want to hear
So deep and full of care
Whispering into my ear

I’m singing about your hands
Your touch ever so gentle
Traveling over my body
It’s feeling sentimental

I’m singing about your love
And it to me you feed
Keeping me safe and secure
Your love is what I need::Sigh:: Dreams
