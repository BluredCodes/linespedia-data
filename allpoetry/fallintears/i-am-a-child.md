---
title: "I Am A Child"
writer: "FallinTears"
slug: "i-am-a-child"
source: "AllPoetry"

I am a child
Born to an average family
I am a child
That has been through too much
I am a child
Of little years
I am a child 
But I understand

I am a child
With a father who doesn’t care
I am a child
With a brother who shows no mercy
I am a child
Who’s mother tries so hard
I am a child
With all too much to bear

I am a child
But I am strong
I am a child 
And life has dealt me my troubles
I am a child
And I have my whole life ahead of me
I am a child
But life forces me to be adult

I am a child
And I just want to be one
I am a child
And sometimes I don’t know anything
I am a child
But I want to be taken seriously
I am a child
And I am mature

I am a child
And sometimes I can’t handle everything
I am a child
And I’m all alone by myself
I am a child
Who strives for normalcy
I am child
Who wants someone to love me

I am a child
And I know what love is
I am a child
And I don’t love anyone
I am a child
But I don’t trust you
I am a child
And I know exactly what I’m doing

I am a child
Brought into this world
I am a child
That has gone through too much… too fast
But I am a child
That has handled it well
A child that says
Come life… bring it on

I am a child
Life you don’t scare me
I am a child
And I know your weakness
I am a child
And behind a mask I hide
I am a child
But I am stronger than youI just feel so helpless... nobody listens to me.
