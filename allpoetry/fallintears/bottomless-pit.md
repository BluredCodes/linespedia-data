I don't know what to do. 
I've fallen in a hole. 
I keep trying to get out. 
Away from the screaming of my soul. 

Every time I reach the top. 
My hand slips and I fall back in. 
Back in the never-ending darkness. 
And the pain I feel within.

I don’t know why I bother.
Because I never get anywhere.
Trying just makes me tired.
And I fall farther in despair.

As I wish upon my star,
Soft tears stream down my face.
Why can’t I just get out?
Get out of this place…

The damp darkness of lonely shadows,
Are taking over my soul.
Someone please come and help me.
Get me out of this hole!Life has eaten me and swallowed me up!!! ::pounds on the insides of it with her fists:: Lemme out!
