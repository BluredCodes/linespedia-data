My HeartThe shadows covered my eyes,I could only see what I wanted to see,My heart was open and bleeding,It is no longer with me.The waves crossed over my body,I crawled on broken knee,I haven’t gotten very far because,My heart is no longer with me.It took so very long,For me to reach, for me to see,I finally made it, but,My heart is no longer with me.My burning candle is dying,And it’s all because of He,My hope is leaving my body,Still… my heart is no longer with me.The shadows of the past echoed,In my eyes, I could not see,But here in death I have it,My heart is finally with me.Anything you wish, help me out.
