Is there light underneath the water?
Is a question I often ask
But no one seems to know the answer
And I put it away to the back of my mind
Until something else makes me remember

The cold dreary days when the rain falls
And I’m all alone by myself
With no one to talk to 
And no one to listen to
Is there light underneath the water?

Those days when I am surrounded
By people that say they care
But they’re off doing what they’re doing
And leaving me to sulk
All alone and jealous… and yet pretend to be happy
Is there light underneath the water?

When you start swimming on a day so bright
And you fell like you can’t go on
You can’t get your arms to move
To help you glide along

Water seeps into your mouth 
You cough and try to swim back
But you feel someone tugging you under
And it’s your breath that lacks

When you can’t keep your head up any longer
And you feel yourself slipping by
Pulled by the weight of the world
No longer can you fly

You’re all alone in the cold
You shiver as you are pulled down
Thinking about the maybes
If you could have turned you life around

Those days that are too good to be true
And you feel like someone cares
With someone to talk to
And someone to listen to
There is light underneath the water

Then the sky comes crashing down
And it falls hard on your head
And you wonder what you could have been thinking
Because you are luckless
An outcast
No, there is no light underneath the water

Is there light underneath the water?
Is a question I often ask
But no one seems to know the answer
And I put it away to the back of my mind
