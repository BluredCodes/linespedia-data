title: "Loving The Invisible Man"

As I sit here watching the calm morning dew
My mind cannot help but to think of you
Those tears that I cried softly that night
Are drying away in the soft morning light

My love for you is pure, like the stars
Wandering from you but never afar
Hold me in your arms and speak to me true
How much your love equals mine that’s for you

You make me smile and help me along
Whispering to me so sweet singsong 
Holding me up and giving me life
Lost in your lust… painful delight

I always can seek and look in your eyes
They soothe my troubles and to pain comes demise
My heart is warm heavy and full of your love
As we walk hand in hand round moon up above

Never alone you echo through my mind
Glad that it was I whose love I did find
Wrapped in your arms so safe and secure
Happy with what I have not wanting more

The sun now shines over the hills
Wake up out of your sleep that’s what my mind wills
Get up and begin a bright lovely new day
Looking for you in each and every way

And when I find the love that I dream
My life will be placid full and serene
My eyes will be shining a smile on my face
I will be no longer without a placeStill... keep on searching... not turning up anything at all.
