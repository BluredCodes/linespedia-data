I think of you, all the time
You’re so sweet, I wish you were mine
I love the way, you make me feel
Safe and loved, to no appeal
You make me smile, you make me laugh
You say things, and leave me aghast
You show me how, to concur my fears
My secrets fall, within you ears
Then I told you, how I felt
And my pedals folded, shivered and welt.
