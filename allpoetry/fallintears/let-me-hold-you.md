I can see the smile in your eyes
And I can feel my tears start to subside
I can see the sun shining through
Come here baby let me… hold you

You feel so safe and warm
And I know you be my shelter from the storm
You will always be there for me
Hold me, come on hold me… baby

I can feel the love in your touch
Let it be just the two of us
Take me come on take me away
Show my eyes a brand new day

I can see the smile in your eyes
And I can feel my tears start to subside
I can see the sun shining through
Come here baby let me… hold you

Speak to me whisper in my ear
Let me know that you can hear
Take me baby take my hand
Bring me laughter help me stand
 
Touch me baby let me know
How far that you want to go
Lead me on to a place unknown
Stay with don’t let me be alone

I can see the smile in your eyes
And I can feel my tears start to subside
I can see the sun shining through
Come here baby let me… hold youYay for songs.
