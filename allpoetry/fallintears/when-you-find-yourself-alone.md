When you find yourself alone,
And the darkness makes you shiver.
In a bare room with white walls,
No lights… blind
S cover the window, only one.
Fan blades spin on low ruffling the air.

When you find yourself alone,
In a bare room with tile floor.
Each step you take echoes to create a chorus.
Chorus of your life…
Sing to me sweet dreamer,
In the corner of your eye.
Nothing
S there

When you find yourself alone,
In a bare room with no pictures on the wall,
Forgotten memories, not lights blind.
S cover the window, only one.
Arms wrapped around yourself.

When you find yourself alone,
In a bare room with white walls, tile floor, no pictures, and blinds covering the only window…
Sing…
Sing to me sweet dreamer,
For you are not alone.::sigh:: Alone is nice... only sometimes.
