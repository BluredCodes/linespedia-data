You’re like a virus that attacks my body
You cling onto my last hopes and dreams
You make me fear you
You eat away at everything I live for
You make me sick
You make me want to throw myself over a bridge
You make me want to give up
But I don’t want to
I still have a little bit left
A little bit to live for
Will you come and attack that to?
I can’t have anything for my own
You take away my little selfish pleasures
I have to keep building up the wall
As you keep knocking it down
Will it ever end?
Or will you succeed?
Will you succeed in destroying my life…
My hopes…
My dreams…
Will you succeed in taking away the only thing that makes me happy?
It’s only a matter of time before we find out…
Find out if you kill me.Help me out!
