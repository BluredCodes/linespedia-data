You have nothing to fear,
But fear itself.
Is it spiders our worms?
Or maybe bad health?
Is it bugs or snakes?
Or big funny clowns?
Or maybe it’s your brother…
Holding you upside down?
Maybe it’s the dark?
Or things that go bump in the night?
They make you fall down,
And scream in fright.
Or maybe it’s something worse?
Something that no one can understand?
Only you can feel it,
With a shake in your hand.
It makes you quiver,
It makes you cry.
It haunts you until,
You curl up and die.
