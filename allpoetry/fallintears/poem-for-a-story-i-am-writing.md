In your eyes I can see it
The love I know is there
Not the “I want to be with you kind”
The love of true care

You’ve been my best friend
For as long as I can remember
Even if you didn’t
Got to school with me in September

But I just want to tell you
That I missed you all those days
That I was learning by myself
Thinking about your ways

The way you say hello to me
The way you smile bright
The way you hold me in your arms
And say everything’s alright

I always hope that you care
About me as much as I you
And sometimes I stop and smile
At all the things you do

You make me so happy
Please don’t ever leave
You’ve been true to me all along
Now please don’t deceive

Right now I know not what your thinking
And ask not I won’t till you tell
These tears that stain my dirty cheeks
In those eyes that never fell

You’ve always been there for me
You’ve never let me down
You always made me happy
And lifted up my frown
