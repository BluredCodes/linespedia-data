---
title: "In My Eyes"
writer: "FallinTears"
slug: "in-my-eyes"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/24993-In-My-Eyes-by-FallinTears"

In my eyes
They shine
They sparkle 
And they gleam
In my eyes 
I see it
Never the way
It seems

In my eyes
They shimmer
They quiver
And they leak
In my eyes
I see it
My life
One that’s so bleak

In my eyes
I see it
Can you see
It too?
The tears 
Of one that
Only
Wants to be with you::sigh::
