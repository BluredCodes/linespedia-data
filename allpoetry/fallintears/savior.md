An imageI can see youA haze within my mindA whisperI can feel youA breath you step behindA shadowYou are thereOnly for now, you move awayMy saviorYou are hereTo help me see another dayAn imageI can see youYou face oh, so brightA whisperI can feel youYour breath runs through the nightA shadowYou are thereYou do not move awayMy saviorYou are hereEach and every dayAn imageI can see youIt will never go awayA whisperI can feel youCome to me you mayA shadow You are thereKeeping me aliveMy saviorYou are hereWithout you, I would not survive(Line used to be, soft mist around the bay. With help from my faithful readers I have changed it.)ANYTHING help me out (Dream3485@yahoo.com)
