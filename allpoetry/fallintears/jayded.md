title: "Jayded"
writer: "FallinTears"

Things are never the way they seem.
You see one thing, and yet it means another.
Everything is so utterly twisted.
But you believe that they are simple.
You’ve loved you’ve laughed,
You’ve hate you’ve cried.
You’ve saw you’ve been,
You’ve cheated and lied.
People try to make you do things, you wouldn’t normally do.
They want you to be just like them,
Because they are afraid to be different,
Afraid to be alone…
You want to just be yourself.
But you are afraid of what other people will think of you.
Who cares what they say?
Because being yourself is what you should be,
And nothing more. 
Don’t change for anybody.
Do what makes you happy.
So what if other people don’t like it?
It’s their problem, and they’ll have to get over it.
And if they can't, Then you don't need them.
Because that just shows, that they don't care.
They don't care enough about you...
To accept you as you are.
