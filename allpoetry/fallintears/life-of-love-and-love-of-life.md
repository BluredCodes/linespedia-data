title: "*Life Of Love And Love Of Life*"

These words that you read are coming from my heart
Straight true and through my chest
Here are the tears of tomorrow
And the regrets of yesterday
They fall off my lips
Like the leaves off a tree
Slowly the rot… fall apart
Brown and disgusting
As they float to the ground
On a breath of air in one blissful second
Until they reach the ground
The cold hard ground of reality
Truth and deceit
Cold-hearted unfairness
Life	                 Life
     Of               Of
        Love     Love
             And
Your eyes hold the memories
And the tears of your sorrows
The blood the bleeds from your broken heart
And the words that roll out of you tongue less mouth
A smile on frigid lips
Breaks the silence of light
Open your eyes and you will hear
Open you ears and you will see
All the things around you are covered
In a blanket of hidden lies
Layer upon layer of snow covered tales
Each only a bit different
Yet each a bit more painful
As you go on down deeper you find
Life	                 Life
     Of               Of
        Love     Love
             And
