Prayers

When I went to bed,
That night my mother said,
“Baby says your prayers.”
So to anyone who cares…

Now I lay me down to sleep,
I pray the lord my soul to keep.
If I should die before I wake,
I pray the lord my soul to take.

That next night my mother said,
“ Say your prayers before you go to bed.”
And then she left the house,
As quietly as any mouse.
 So I said…

Now I lay me down to sleep,
I pray the lord my soul to keep.
If I should die before I wake,
I pray the lord my soul to take.

The next night mom left me alone.
There was nobody home.
To care,
If I said my prayers.
But I did…

Now I lay me down to sleep,
I pray the lord my soul to keep.
If I should die before I wake,
I pray the lord my soul to take.

Mother died last night.
She got in a terrible fight.
But she never said her prayers,
Before she went to her final bed.
So I said them for her…

Now I lay her down to sleep,
I pray the lord her soul to keep.
She did die before she waked,
And her soul the lord did take.~In memory of the late Arnold Coppola, Hank Longo, Nancy Longo, Biase Marie Danny Lagana, and my dear Uncle Frank, Aunt Lucy, and all the others I was too young to remeber~
