Can you hear the words I speak through my lips that roll of my tongue towards your ears?
Or are the pleas and cries that I’ve made silent and lost in the years?

Never say never ever again is that my guiding light?

To live off your love that shines from about bounding through the night.

In pain you come calling for the tears that are falling slowly slide to my breast

Nestled deep within your strong arms. Hold me tight to your chest

Under the fire the eternal flame my tears dry on my face

Entold are the truths and the lies within, lost among your embrace.I like it.
