A single rose you delivered to my door
The same day you broke your promise to come see me
It was beautiful still wet with the morning’s dew
Soft silky petals and sea green stem
It lies in my hands as I run my fingers across it
Gingerly my fingers sweep…
Slowly the slide down the stem 
At the very bottom I feel a tiny prick
Looking at my finger, a perfect circle of blood has formed
Only one thorn… 
You made me bleed
The petals suddenly start to wilt… they slowly die
Withering they fall…
Seeping out of the cracks of the dieing rose… 
My blood
Little rivers of rubies 
Flowing down the ugly thing that lies in my hands
I can hear it laugh
Yes it laughs in my face
Along with the blood that has covered my hands
My blood
The rose you sent to me…
Is laughing in my face
It drops to the floor… and so do I
Covered…
Covered in blood…
My bloodIt feels as though my heart is slowly buring. Dieing it cracks... and falls to the bottom of my chest... down to my stomach... where it sits... and makes me sick.
