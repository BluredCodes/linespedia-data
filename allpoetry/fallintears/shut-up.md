writer: "FallinTears"

Shut up! shut up! shut up!
I don’t want to hear it anymore.
Stop the voices in my head.
Just stop the stupid whores.

Shut up! shut up! shut up
I want to get away from myself.
I don’t want you to feel sorry.
I just want you to help.
