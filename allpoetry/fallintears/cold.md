Cold…
I’ve got goose bumps and I can’t seem to get warm
The frigid air seeps right through me
Into my body
Into my heart
Freezes it there
Still unmoving
But alive
Cold
I quit trying so long ago
Quit trusting
Quit believing
In everyone… and everything
But myself
Cold…
I don’t care what you say
You’re all brainwashed
Wake up and stop being who they want you to be
Complying only gives them more power
Cold 
Tainted by suffering
Aching with blood
Crying with no tissues
Dying without love
I can see it
But you are blind
Filled within the voids 
Placed  into your mindsWhatever!
