F irst time I saw you, I saw a smiling girl
O ver the times we shared, you helped shape my world
R eaching for my goals, you are the one that made me see
E nding with a celebration, you hugging me
V ery often I would cry, and you would hold me tight
E very now and then, we would have a fight
R emember me always, know I am there for you

F or you are my one and only
R emember please, to hold me
I n case I never say
E verything I wish today
N ever forget my thanks, or think I won’t care
D on’t ever think it will, because it will never end
S o you just remember, that we are forever friends
