title: "Listen to What i am not saying"

Love is pain; Love is gone
It perplexes me
You didn't know; Ha! You didn't know Goodbye.
I am dumping you before you could.

Go away (please don't go), Leave me (don't leave me)
Forget about me (don't forget about me)
Be happy (please don't leave)
