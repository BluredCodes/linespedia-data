---
title: "For your Love"
writer: "kaiyan"

I laugh, I cry, I sing, I dance.
Anything to get you in to my trance.
I want you so bad, I can taste it in my mouth.
You are my cool oasis, in the case of a drought.
From the tallest building, I would jump.
From a six-figure mansion, to the dumps.
I would go anywhere for you my dear.
My loyalty, you will never have to fear.
Never wavering from my heart.
Of my life, you will always have the biggest part.
