I'm tired of what life provides.
A shitty existence, then you die.
The promise land you hope for.
Never knowing your final score.
You roam the world, without a clue.
Living in a bubble, if you really knew.
Smoking a cigarette and a course some weed.
Siliencing your thoughts, your needs.
Taking a lil pill, to disapear for a little while.
Exsisting with a wink and a smile.
Taking a hit of sid, always turns you straight.
Tripping untill your ending date.
Shrums make my world a little small.
Making your fell bulletproof and ten feet tall.
But these are all the drugs that I use.
To make it to the next day, to make it through.
With out them, I don't what I would do.
