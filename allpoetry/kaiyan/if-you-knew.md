Do you know?  The things I would do to you?
If you could only see, if you only knew?
The way you walk, the way you dress.
Makes me wonders how you kiss?  Caress?
How your hands would feel against my skin.
Wanting to please you over and over again.
Wanting to lick every freckle on your... back.
Counting every one of them to be exact.
I would take my time with you, I swear.
I want you to groan, shout and pull my hair.
Never again, would you look away.
Entering your mind every single day.
Once you get to talk what I got.
Your body will eternally boil hot.
So hot, all you what to do is scream.
But right now, I will wake up to this dream.
Another day wasted, I passed you up.
Am I stupid, my mind corupt?
Why do I torture myself so?
Next chance I get, you'll know.
