Who knows what life will bring today
Another born, another gay
Children cry, teens die
All we can do is wonder why

What have we done, to earn this fate
Will we ever see the ending date
Will time go on and on and on 
Like that annoying Lamb Chop song

Is all we see, all we get
A couple of joints. a line of meth
Pain and agony in our life
The final feel of the ending knife

No lights, no music, no trumpets blown
Just the T.V. blaring and the ringing phone
Still eyes like a dying doe
Will somebody come for us. who knows?
