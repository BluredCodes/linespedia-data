title: "The Fear of Death"

Death is something that we fear.
But why do we fear him my dear?
Is it the pain of the dark night?
Or the knowledge that we won't see the light?
The anticipation of the coming date.
Always wondering what is your fate.
When will it happen, what time?
Will I have time to conjure more rhymes?
Will it be a bloody death for all to see?
Or a quiet death at home when your ninety-three?
After all this time, thinking of the end.
You have wasted your life, with no more time to spend.
