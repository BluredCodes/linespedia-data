---
title: "For You"
writer: "kaiyan"
slug: "for-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

For your love, I'll cross a river
Deep as time and wide as ever.
To know your touch, I'd tell you why.
Why I need you and how I'll never make you cry.
You mean so much, in all that you have done.
You make the clouds run from the sun.
Rain has never touched my face.
Untill the thought of you leaving comes in to place.
Where would I go?  Without you there.
No one there to grab my hair.
To take me rough and enjoy my being.
Give me everything, I yearn and dream.
The soft whispers, your gentle touch.
Is that why my body needs you so much?
I like to hear you bitch, whine, moan and complain.
To here you hollar out my name.
For you to get frustrated when we don't have the time
To get you off or to relive a rhyme.
To see your face when relief overcomes you
That is what I like to do.
Your release brings me joy.
My body is just your personal toy.
Bend me, break me, I know you'll make me.
Whisper, scream and call your name in my dreams.
I will do anything for your love indeed.
Because you give me, more than I could ever need.
