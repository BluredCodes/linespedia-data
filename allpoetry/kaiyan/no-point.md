You try and try-it's never to change.
The world has slowly turned deranged.
One mistakes, brings you down.
Problems whenever you turn around.
You make up with one, to hate anew.
With evil thoughts to sit and stew.
Never good enough to go home.
In the streets, I am destined to raom.
Why do we even try today.
They never listen to what you ssay.
Your happiness, holds no real means.
Of your home your forced to dream.
Held here without a choice.
Another two months, yeah rejoice.
There is no reason to really try.
Give no satisfaction with tears to cry.
They feed in to your pain.
Claiming that you are unstable and insane.
All these people do is lie.
So, hold the fort and deny.
