I look in to your hungry eyes.
Closing mine to block the lies.
Between me and you, there is no lust.
There is just a hundred bucks.
A business deal that must go on.
Untill I get something I've needed so long.
Money to wish all of my troubles away.
To wake up one morning to a brand new day.
Not filled with dread of going to you.
Even though I need to.
I can't get the money anyway else.
So, it's my body on sell on the shelf.
Fourty more dollars if you will allow.
Shit that no one should do, not even cows.
Refusing the money, I know that I need.
Tomorrow's gas money, food to feed.
So what do I do, with all of this shit.
