title: "The green miracle"

POT IS GOOD, NEVER BAD
SOMETHING THAT I ALWAYS HAD
WHEN PISSED OFF, SMOKE THE WEED
THAT IS WHAT YOU TRULY NEED
WHEN IN DOUBT, SMOKE A BOWL
MIND SPINNING OUT OF CONTROL
WHEN IN PAIN, FIRE IT UP
IS MY THINKING A BIT CORUPT
IT SUCKS THAT YOUR HIGH GOES DOWN
WATCHING THE SMOKE FLOAT AROUND
FORGET IT, MY BUSS WON'T LAST
SO LET'S ROLL, LIGHT, PUFF, THEN PASS
INHALE ALL THE SMOKE DOWN DEEP
FROM YOUR NOSE. THE BLUE GAS CREEPS
YOU START TO FEEL A LIL GIDDY 
STANDING UP A LIL UNSTEADY
IT'S OKAY, IT'S ALRIGHT
I HAVE ENOUGH TO LAST ALL NIGHT
tAKE A COUPLE PUFFS, THEN PASS
LET ME HIT THAT LIME GREEN GRASS
