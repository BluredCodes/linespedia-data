I feel your breath against my neck.
As you creep around the deck.
Any minute the bomb will hit.
And you will die, where you sit.
The world is now, just a big war.
Because a few decided to soar.
This all started when planes crashed.
The twin towers were turned to rubble and trash.
Something that started from over there.
Has finally found it's way over here.
We are only tasting a bit of their fate.
So why did we wonder hwy they cheered with hate.
But understand that I never personally pulled a trigger.
So, I am innocent as far as I can figure.
Those thousands of people in New York ask why.
Those you killed were men, women and kids.
They were not apart of your problems and bids.
So what do we do now, with all of this?
Say goodbye and blow your ass to bits.10-15-01,
