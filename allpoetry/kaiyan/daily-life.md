Another one lives, another one dies.
Another sin is said, another child lies.
The world circles closer to the dark.
The sun's brillance becomes more stark.
The future always hold such gloom.
Filling your heart and head with doom.
Love is a figment of the past.
Now you know, that nothing ever last.
Your dims are broken, forced to use your brights.
Switching all the knobs for realities lights.
No more thoughts of sugar plum fairy tales.
Now you worry about the cops and posting bail.
Watching your back, expecting the worst.
Getting more than you will ever desire for thirst.
Backed against the wall, you'll snap.
Life will end, when freedom collapsed.
But choses are few at present date.
No one understands, so how can they relate.
You're alone in the world, a little girl.
Scared and confused and spinning to whirl.
Time passes to harden your shell.
Ages and faces; no one can tell.
