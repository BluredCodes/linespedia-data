Happiness
As we pass the joint around.
I look at who all surrounds.
No bitches, no enimies, no man.
Just myself, my own little clan.
Everyone else has left my mind.
Untill it wears off, I have such little time.
Enjoying emptiness, it's own bliss.
No arguing, no drama, no one to diss.

Paranoia
I like to feel the smoke.
Hoping that I don't cough and choke.
Feeling the smoke float and curl.
Taking me out of this fucked up world.
To a place of laughter, so serene.
It's too damn quiet, I"m going to scream.
Take me back to the past.
The future, I wish I could surpass.

Dizziness
The ground keeps moving from my feet.
On my knees, I slowly creep.
Back and forth, to the wall.
On my stomach, I know crawl.
Shakily I am getting there.
Hold on...I forgot where.
On my back, I now lay.
Untill it is, another day.
