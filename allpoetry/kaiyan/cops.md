Why does life get so fucked up.
Why does it have to be shitty and corrupt.
Cops stop to find your grass.
They are always there to harrass.
Trying to get some answers to there crimes.
Waiting till you run into hard times.
Follwing you knowing that they can.
Wanting to write tickets-at least ten.
talking bad with your bitch ass badge in tow.
but you are lucky that you really dont know.
I'd choke you with your own fucking stick.
Sliting your throat,cutting off your  dick.
Pop  your ears, gouge your eyes.
Then in time you will relize.
You arent shit without your suit.
Your gun, your cap, Your fucking boots.
So no matter how bad you are, how you can lie.
Guess what bitch all pigs will die.
