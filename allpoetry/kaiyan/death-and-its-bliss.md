This pain I feel, will never end.
Untill I'm dead and my life is spent.
Tryings to find life's meanings, I never will.
Untill my times is up, the clock stands still.

I want my end to come real quick.
To get shot in the back or become real sick.
To never see the light of day.
In the ground I want to stay.

I beg you please-release me.
Or let me go on living, let me be.
Let me live without your lies.
Or let me please just silently die.
