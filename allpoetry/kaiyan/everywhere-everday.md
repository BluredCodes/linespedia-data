title: "Everywhere everday"
writer: "kaiyan"

Everywhere I go,I am reminded of you.
I am so confused I don't know what to do.
I don't want to yell and I don't want to fight.
I just want to hold you all through the night.
Let me leave as the dawn arrives.
Not starting the fight to change our lives.
Back to reality...I really miss my baby.
I can't think of anyone else,I'm going crazy.
I want to go back,but I know I can't now.
Putting up with the shit,I can't allow.
Everyday apart,makes you feel farther away.
Everday your gone,I think of more to say.
I'm sorry, I will always love you.
We can work this out because I know you love me too.
