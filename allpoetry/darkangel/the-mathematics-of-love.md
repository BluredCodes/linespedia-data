title: "The mathematics of love"

Your central frequency beats within me
The calculus of tears is all i see
Your standard deviation
Became part of the equation

Like an exponential function
Your love multiplies with ease
But in your algebraic junction
Your multivariate madness
Can not be appeased

Cryptography
And fractal geometry
To navigate
Your geography
To light my way
In the labyrinth
Of your mental topography

In the realm of number theory
Where one equals zero
I'm just a statistical anomaly
The definiton of anti-hero

Like a summer breeze
You dance upon the vertices
Of discarded, inverted matrices
In the vastness of Cartesian seas
Lies the wreckage
A sinewy debris
