What used to be
Just isn't anymore
Those times have all passed

I don't really remember
What it was like to be your friend
those memories have been trashed

We use to be close
The best of all friends
But we've drifted away

When I see you now
I have no urge to stop and talk
About getting together one day

Conversations have run dry
Smiles are no longer frequent
Your number's out of my mind

You don't know my secrets
You don't know my crushes
And now I've come to find

That theres only one thing
That I really regret
Thats not saying goodbye

It ended without me noticing
Wiothout me giving consent
Without me asking Why?

So Good bye my friend
It was fun, It was nice
I'll miss your smile

Just remember, it had to happen
I didn't want it this way, but
I'll think of you all the while
