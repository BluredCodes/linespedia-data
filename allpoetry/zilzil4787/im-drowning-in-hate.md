You’re pushing me down under
Not letting me come up for air
Always restraining and holding me back
Pushing me into tears

I hated you because 
You hurt me first
You never let me be my own person
And I was drowning

CHORUS
I’m drowning
Drowning in my hate for you
My lungs are suffacating in my hurt and distaste
The grim reaper has his hand firmly around my throat
All because of you

You make me sick
Your urge to cloud my sunny day
That way I’ll never get any sun
I’ll always live in tears

I was screaming
As my hate overtook me
I wanted to kill the world
You made me this way

CHORUS

You took what was mine
And never got punished
never had any regret
never had any consience

now I am paying
paying with my life
for the anguish
caused by you

CHORUS

Its your fault!
God I hated you!  
I hated so much now I’m drowning
My fate is drowning
It should have been you

Although I’m drowning
You will pay for your sins
Althought I’m drowning
you will have the same fate
My hate will not be in vain
The grim reaper will reach you
and you will drown
