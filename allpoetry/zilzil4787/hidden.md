Theres something hidden that I’m trying to fight 
trying to bubble out with all of its might

Its dark and mysterious, something forbidden
I try to get away, to ignore the devil thats hidden

CHORUS
I’m running running running, never running fast enough
im falling falling falling, never holding tight enough

I’m spinning spinning spinning and I’m losing my mind
Why my heart is racing is what I’m trying to find

I’m scared to look and face it
I’m scared to see the truth

I’m scared to look and face it
I’m scared

I’ve tried ignoring but it’s lurking about
It’s tired of being secret, it wants to pour out

I’m frustrated, I can’t seem to win
Pretending its not there is becoming a sin

CHORUS

Although I’m frightened this runnings gotta stop
The fallings gotta end and the spinnings gonna stop

The racings gonna quit cause now I’m ready to look
I’m gonna face my demons, look into every little nook

Chorus
I faced it, and I lived to tell the tale
My fears didnt kill me, so I didnt fail

The running, the pounding, the racing will cease
Im no longer falling, I have finally made peace
