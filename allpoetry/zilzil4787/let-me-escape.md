---
title: "Let me Escape"
writer: "zilzil4787"
slug: "let-me-escape"
source: "AllPoetry"

My heart is bleeding
bleeding from my pain
you can run your fingers through it

My mind is scarred
I’ve seen satan and live to tell
you can see the fires glowing bright

CHORUS
Kill me now
Rescue me from this hell
Kill me now
Life holds no joy
Kill me now
I’m aching from my grief
Kill me now
Close my void

My past is filled 
filled with memories of hate
feel them pulsing through my blood

My voice screams out
tales of anguish and pain
hear them vibrating in your ears

CHORUS

Spilt blood 
tells the story of anger ran amok
feel it seethling through me

my broken heart
proof that something went wrong
run the pieces through a needle

CHORUS

Listen to my screams
screams of fear and anger
Answer to my pleas

Feel my fear
making my heart race
let me escape this place

CHORUS

It’s never going to change
I’ll always be living in this hell
Just kill me now
Let me escape
