Fear
It breaths down my back
tickles my spine
gives me shivers up and down

My heart pounds
the sweat appears
my head jerks at the smallest sound

He's coming to get me
and why, I dont know
This fear is all I knowi was sitting watching the really scary movie Seven, with Brad Pitt, and it was so scary that I couldnt sleep, and to make myself feel better I wrote a poem.
