Glutany
The fat man who can't fit through
how different is he from you?
He hides his problems by eating
Tell me, is he succeeding?

Greed
This man who wants it all
Will do anything to make you fall
Push you down so he can win
He must have everything, is that a sin?

Sloth
She sits around all day
making excuses so she can't slave away
Her energy will finally flee
She just sits and rots, can you see?

Lust
The girl who stole your love
and then pushed him away with a shove
she makes all eyes turn towards her
she'll have everyone, for sure

Pride
Concieted and loud mouthed is this one
His ego weighs a full ton
Wants all the glory but not the pain
For this person I have disdain

Wrath
Anger is all she knows
and revenge is all she will impose
Hate has bended her spine
her creedo is "Revenge is mine"

Envy
This last one is my sin
The dark secret that I harbor within
Everything that they have I want
Only wanting it so I can flaunt
