---
title: "Why can't you See?"
writer: "zilzil4787"
slug: "why-cant-you-see"
source: "AllPoetry"

I see the looks that you give her
And I wish you’d gaze like that at me
Your eyes have been blinded by others
Why can’t you see?

You were the one I wanted the most
But the one I couldn’t have
The best friend and more
But it was her you must have

CHORUS
Why can’t you see
That we were meant to be
you always seem to look 
past what’s in front of you
Why can’t you see
That we were meant to be
You’ve never seemed so close
yet so far away
Why can’t you see me?

Everyone said we’d be perfect for eachother
but you would never hear
Even though I knew everything about you
ever smile and tear

I knew it was impossible
but I wanted it so bad
You never really saw me
and it made me so mad!

CHORUS

You ripped my heart out and crushed it
and you never even knew
I tried to forget and
finally give up on you

So I attempted to move on
put everything in the past
but it hurt too much
this pain just seems to last

CHORUS

(screaming)
I wish these tears 
of frustration would end
I wish my shattered heart
would finally mend
I’ve wished for a lot of things
as you can see
but what I wished for the most
Is that you’d want me!

(softly-no music-slow)
yes one day.....you’ll finally see me
