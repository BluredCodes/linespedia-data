I'm lost 
This emotion is to raw, to new for me 
I'm lost 
How can I make his head turn, and look at me 
I'm lost 
He can never see the truth, see the real person inside 
I'm lost 
He has to see, I can no longer hide 
I'm lost 
No idea what to do, or what to say 
I'm lost 
We use to be friends, we'd laugh, talk and play 
I'm lost 
Now I tremble every time he's near 
I'm lost 
to afraid to say anything, for what he might hear 
I'm lost 
He might see behind the laughter behind my eyes 
I'm lost 
He might see someone he would despise 
I'm lost 
Without him...... 
I'm lost
