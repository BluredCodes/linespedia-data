---
title: "A Little Lost Girl"
writer: "zilzil4787"
slug: "a-little-lost-girl"

She walks alone
stumbles on the path
shivers in the cold
feels the fierce wind’s wrath

a little frightened 
and somewhat scared
a tiny bit sad
that noone has cared

CHORUS

walking alone while the trees whisper and scream
She wants to wake up, and find it’s all a dream
But it’s real, and she’s very alone
All she wants to do is find her way home
a little lost girl, that’s what she is...a little lost girl

She doesn’t know where to turn
and she’s lost her way
she’s fighting real hard
to keep her worries at bay

All she wants to do 
Is sit down and cry
forget her promise to keep hoping
give up and die

CHORUS

Why wind is blowing
whipping through her hair
why hasn’t anyone come?
Dont they care?

Why is she lost
and when will she be found
she wants arms to welcome her
to feel safe and sound

CHORUS

But theres something wrong
her story’s not right
shes not trying to find home
shes trying to leave the fight

this girl is lost in her problems
and now you can see
that the little lost girl
is really me

walking alone while the trees whisper and scream
I want to wake up, and find it’s all a dream
But it’s real, and I’m very alone
All I want to do is find my way home
A little lost girl.....thats me.....
