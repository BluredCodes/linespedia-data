scared
running wherever my legs take me
panting heavily
what am I running from?
Is it fear of living?
Or is it fear of reaction?
Is it fear of trying?
Or is it fear of disappointment?
Is it fear of what won’t happen?
Or is it fear of what will?
When will I stop running?
It’s chasing me, driving me to questions
questions that go unanswered
when will it stop?
and release it’s grip on my mind?
Why? Why is it bothering me?
Questions.  More questions
Too many questions
but the biggest one of all,
Why me?The ending.....
