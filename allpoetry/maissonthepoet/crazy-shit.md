title: "crazy shit"

crazy shit everywhere
on the walls
on the clouds
and the computer screen
wonderin whoes gonna take my place
when the shit comes down
you'll be sittin next to me
are you gonna have your shit wall up?
if I could
I would
take back the things I said
but nothing can undo the things I've done
the wrong I feel
will be there
you don't have to remind me that its there
yeah... it hurts my tummy
but I'm trying to not notice
when I have nothing left 
you pull it out of me
it hurts
to hurt you
it hurts while you stab me with your words
over and over and over
always bringing the tears to my eyes
can't you hear me screaming I'm sorry?
