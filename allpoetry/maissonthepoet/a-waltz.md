a poet once wrote that he could write hte saddest lines
sad they were
but tonight,
I can dance the saddest dance
I dance around the table where thoughts of us eating by candlelight
come back to me
just a memory
I dance around the room with the T.V and the couch.  You laughed,
I cried, we cuddled.
I dance ungracefully on and around the bed we shared for so long.
You were always graceful there.
But now, I dance as if I'm underwater
as if not to get burned
as I watch the flames happily dance around this houseumm.. this is an old one I just found.
