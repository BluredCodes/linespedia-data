hello world
take me as I am
or just plainly take me as you can
beacuse I see you, world
but you haven't seen me
because I walk through lifeless hallways
where plenty of people think meaningless thoughts
perhaps because they haven't learned
the value of a thought

world,
please grasp my arms
and pull me out
