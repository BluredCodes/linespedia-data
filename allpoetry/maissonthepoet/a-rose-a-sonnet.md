i feel like writing a poem
how quaint
you
but you
wrote me a sonnet
a Shakespearian sonnet for me
you called me your lover
and in ending your short letter
you called me love
you bought me a rose
a single rose, that
now brings tears to my eyes
sitting on the top shelf
protected from hands
if there were a way to,
know how much love and caring and compasion
you put into buying that single rose
it would probably burst

so to say
i wrote a poem
and you
wrote me a sonnet
I say
bye love
