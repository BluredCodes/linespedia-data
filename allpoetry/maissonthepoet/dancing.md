meshing
with the ebb and flow of life
me and you
side by side
hand in hand
creating sweet harmonies between us
almost waltzing down that deserted length of beach
smiles and laughter provide the music
constant small talk
all the while we're both going over the same ground in our head
feeling like nothing could ever disrupt our emotions
and no one could ever know our love
so we keep laughing and waltzing our way to the end
we pitch our tent of stars
and stare at the ceiling dreamily
for our breathing pulses the ryhthm for our slow dance now
we wrap our arms around each other
and sit the rest of the night out
