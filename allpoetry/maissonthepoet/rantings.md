down down town...
going down
crawling the streets
flying high on magic elevators
where birds fly through windows
and the windows are always clean

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

I feel like my head is going fall off
and roll across the floor
to play with the spiders
having to stare in the direction it stopped in
hopefully where the scenery changes every so often
by a passerby
looking at feet and dirty shoes
