breathing in through the clouds
and out through the rain
sitting here on my pedestal
singing sad songs again
i can see you clearly
when my tears make everything else blurry
you're my dark angel
carry me in your arms
take me home safely
away from all harm
set me down gently
and bid me farewell
tell me you'll come again
to rescue me from here
rescue me from light 
together we take flight 
together in the rain 
we fly far away from this world 
away from the pain. 
you suffer a lot 
for i am your blackend heart 
with tatterd wings 
that burn in rain 
we will fly away from here 
i am stuck deep inside your soul 
promise me you will never let go 
in the rain you cry 
the sad song we sing is told upon the mountain high 
it is a song about how sad you be 
together we make it better
