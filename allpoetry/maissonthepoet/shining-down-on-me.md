your tears are so beautiful
I know you can feel
when feelings strike deeper things with you
your smiles
ah, your smiles
they make me cry
no, don't smile at me
kindness where it shouldn't be
tenderness when I am wretched
hidden beneath the moss and the weeds
is where I should be
never to see your smiles
