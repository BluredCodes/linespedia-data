title: "A long way down"

its a long way down
the tear travels far
from your chin
to the floor
you hear it hit the cold tiles
even though you are not watching
you know its there
and it's staring at you
realizing it's yours
so you mop it up with your dirty sock
the one thing you're still wearing
that tear seems so heartless
how could it afford to leave your eye
it needed you to keep it alive
it died on the journey
took a piece of you with it
nothing left but a salty trail on your skin
another one rolls down your cheek
wipe it away
wipe it away
lick it off your finger to put it back within
so you can get this way and cry it back out again
