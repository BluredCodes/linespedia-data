title: "kryptonite"

falling asleep
falling deeper
falling faster
screaming louder
and yet
my savior
where are you?
when I need saving
my superman in disguise
I guess I'm your kryptonite
wrapped in a lois clark dress
with a lois clark voice
wait
get naked and dance on the ashes
of my lois clark attire
did you realize those tears leaking from
my kryptonite
my arms wide open
my superman cries paint
