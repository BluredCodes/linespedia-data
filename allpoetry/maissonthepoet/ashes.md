ashes,ashes,ashes,ashes,ashes
ashes,ashes, we all fall down
dancing on the ashes
holding hands
hair flailing
dead smiles flashing
cheery white teeth
dancing on the ashes
sleep is good
when you have nothing
to wake
for
when dreams are more pleasant
