they fight
fight like pigs
pigs feasting
feasting upon their own hatred innards and psychotic confusion
fight like beggars on the street corner
competing for the sympathy and pitty
sympathy and pitty of the unfortunate passers by
going steadily insane
laugh at the most intolerable pain
words of rage
such good weapons to choose
grind and twist at each others spine
nothing but dull vegetivity in the end
