i thought for sure you wanted me
why do you have to be so damn amazing
you take 18 lines of nothing
and turn them into doves
to fly away with my heart
and my emotions
dear companion
companion no more
we have sailed upon each others love
to find to others arms and embrace
upon a soft black cloud
i was surely yours then
this poem is also yours
why should every curve and line 
be dedicated to you
because you gave what you could?
