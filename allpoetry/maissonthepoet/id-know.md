---
title: "I'd know"
writer: "maissonthepoet"
slug: "id-know"
source: "AllPoetry"

i sit in bed and stare at the empty space beside me
i imagine your body lying there
your eyes are attentive and watching
you head propped up on your arm
your other hand brushing a strand of hair back behind your ear before placing it back down on the bed
you're just admiring me as I jot down senseless words
looking at the way i hold my pencil as I write
and erase my mistakes
and discovering my face for the thousandth time,
just as it were the first time
the way my nose is shaped, the way my eyes seem to express my feelings, the curve of my lips
and the way threads of hair are out of place, but
you wouldn't have it any other way
you wouldn't have to utter a single sound
because I'd know
