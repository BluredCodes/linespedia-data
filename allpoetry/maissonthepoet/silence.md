my savior has come to me
teaching how to just be myself again
to let things just just be
reminding me that being happy doesn't have an answer
it just is
self realization is a bitch
but letting go is the killer

spoken feelings aren't the answer
they should just remain feelings
that's why they ARE feelings
and when you understand
words aren't needed
a comfortable silence
a relaxed silence
a FELT silence
