there's a crazy air
flowing through humanity
just when you thought
it was safe to think
tomorrow will be a better day
your breath it stolen
by shock and amazement
but what really gets you
is the fear
so lets camp out on the moon
and watch the earth come up in flames
and maybe one day we'll be free to think again
that tomorrow will be a better day
