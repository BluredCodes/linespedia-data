yo try to touch me and melt the ice
but the ice is frozen stone
layered deep beneath the surface
where no fire has ever shone

you try to heal me
infectious wounds grow near my heart
they've taken over mindless thoughts
and fairies carry reality away from me
bundle it up in bushles
sell it for half a penny pence
wasn't worth much to start

you try to console me
calm my anger and dismay
you keep me on a leash
my thoughts have gone astray

away with my blood
banished from my viens
boiled to death
blood is in the air
