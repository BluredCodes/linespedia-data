title: "broken mirror"
writer: "maissonthepoet"

i look in the mirror
see my eyes
i become frightened
because fear is what I see

but I'm not allowed to be scared
because I'm the strong one
leads you into battle
comes out victorious

fear
because I'm hopeless
my words do not count
only pile up in the back of my mind
thousands and thousands
one at a time

just like the tears
which collect in my eyes
run down my face
which I try to hide

I'm shattered and broken
how could I let myself get this way
I've been battered and beaten
from your path I must stray

you pretend to know me
you know nothing of the truth
you try to disguise your flaws too
but through all the makeup
the real you still shines through
