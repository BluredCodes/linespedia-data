title: "freewriting w/o my mommy"

all alone, crying w/o my mommy to comfortme.  thinking of how I want to hold myself in your arms.  you can take my tears away, like you always have even if you handed them out like candy.  tired, and sore worrying that people like to think I write w/o feeling and every word comes from my heart.  you, my heart have helped me through a lot of things.  your arms are my security blanket, I'm sorry for myself.  you sir, need to come to my rescue and wipe my tears away.  they are here waiting for you.  sir.  w/o my mommy... they are waiting.I'm tired and my emotions are acting silly.  I needed to let some things out. :)
