title: "deeper than deep"

my love for you is deeper than deep
deeper than one thousand seas atop one another
stronger than the currents which carry us together
day after day, one seaking the other
love which is sweeter than sweet
sweeter than the ripest cherry when eaten in delicacy
lovlier than the love song of the most gracious song bird
love which is endless
more endless than the bottomless souls
souls which are filled over and over
filled by passionate kisses, caresses, and grassy fields
my love for you, created by love itself
love which is beyond hte love that everyone knows
crying out with never ending content and satisfaction
burning brighter than all fires
gone limp at your touch
lightheaded at your scent
dead at the thought of your criticism
mourning at the thought of your unarrival
for my love for you is deeper than deep
you keep me afloat, for without you I'd drown
