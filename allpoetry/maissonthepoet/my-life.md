YES
it was worth it
my life only comes once
why wait?
go for what you want... right?
I got what I wanted.
no sick feelings in my head
and how quickly I fall
yes.. I fall quick
if quick is a few months
no one laid me down...
I done the laying
pouring out your thoughts on here
I just can't do it anymore
I'm so uninspired...
tired of words
everything jumbles and never makes sense
kinda like my life
but what did I expect when I have my future living with me
