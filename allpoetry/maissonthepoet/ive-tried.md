---
title: "I've tried"

now is done
my living hell
wait to see 
my eyes they swell
so I..
grip the rain
and I breath
the clouds again

and you know I cried
you know how hard I cried

oh, can you
be my pain
can you be here
time and time again
as my thoughts
begin to scream
i see you
as my shadow fades

now that I 
have seen my fate
i wish to say
the times too late
as my world
begins to sing
look at me
I've grown some wings

look at me
my brand new wings

oh...you know I've tried
you know how hard I've tried
