---
title: "a home"
writer: "maissonthepoet"

you leave me crying to go home
I'm home
what a home
lying to my ears
crying to my own
speach a slurring blur
rattling off excuses no one hears
the tears dry up fast
when I hear my name

crying here alone
wishing for you
where are you when I need you

sympathy for an old man
leaving us dry
my heart pounding in my chest
yet I lend my heart
again and again
because you're still my best friend

crying here alone
wishing for you
where are you when I need you
why is it us you do this to

when will the number of times
I'm there for you
turn into greater numbers
than the times
I've depended on you

I'm not always going to be here
so you can ease your mind
or go drink away your stress
because you've had another bad day

crying alone
sitting at my home
because you've had another bad day
