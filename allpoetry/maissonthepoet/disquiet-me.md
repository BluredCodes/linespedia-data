title: "disquiet me"

there's something disquieting about the way you say my name sometimes
    how can a single word have so much power
    to almost drown in the cycling reminiscence of your voice as I am stuck in the mid-world between consciousness and slumber
    echoing through my body - recollections of your fingertips brushing over me and manipulating various sensations - once again my flesh made to swelter
    out breathing into my ear - transposing your passion to me through your inaudible language when your eyes say as much as your breath may
