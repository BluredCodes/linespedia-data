i'm glad to see the fairies are helping you again
so the revolt is over
but you're still trying to find the answer
as to why something you created could turn against you
here they are
entranced by the mad doctor
carrying the deceased body parts to your lair
their wings flutter
and there pastel green cloaks blow gently behind them like satin caught by a sweet smelling breeze
they know nothing of what lies beneath the surface
the pond beside your laboratory hides many secrets
also known as "# x : failure"
the great brown path leads the way
while it lays very untroden
you greet then at the door as they wipe thier wings on the welcome mat
and yet you still can't see their faces
