I am invisible - no one sees me

I mean nothing to anyone

Do I love my daughter? yes
now she loves another

Do I weep? yes - for another love that could have
been - strong

A life that started right but was wrong

Abuse is like a dying
bit by bit parts of you break away - till you
are no more

I am as I am - invisible
