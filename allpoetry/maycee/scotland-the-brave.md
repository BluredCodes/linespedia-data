---
title: "Scotland the brave"
writer: "maycee"
slug: "scotland-the-brave"
source: "AllPoetry"

'tis the hills I seek - powerful rolling to deep 
waters below

hills glowing shades of green, heather sprouts between bracken
Prickly thistles standing tall - purple heads sway in the wind

'tis the pipes I seek that stir my heart to hope
Kilted dancers reds blues and greens - swirling jigs and reels
Pipes in tune, tapping feet - marching bands - pipes slow in lament
Glorius to the end

'tis Scotland I seek, lochs wide - deep secretive - stormy - silent
changing with the winds
Steeped in history,battles,blood and tears
Strong to stand alone.  Soil of birth
My own Scotland the brave
