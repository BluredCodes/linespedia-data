Soft sounds of water slapping against the rock
The rock i sit on - meditate on - cry on

Only the stars, moon and sea watch over me

My rock is stable and strong, protects me from all evil

Beware rock, that I may leave your peaceful presence
venture into the unknown.  Where danger prevails through loving and giving

And then what will become of me?  

I will grow in mind and spirit - become a shape that fits the jigsaw 
of life

Like you my rock, Whose shape is formed by the many movements 
of waves against your sides
