---
title: "Sleepless Nights"
writer: "maycee"
slug: "sleepless-nights"
source: "AllPoetry"

Deep in thoughts that bare my mind when i try to sleep
Torturous flashing words, scenes - i want to weep

Things i've said and done within my life - that reach
so deep 

To hide the hurt, i pray my brain will rest so 
i can sleep
