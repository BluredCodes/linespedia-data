---
title: "life giving"
writer: "maycee"
slug: "life-giving"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/2968-life-giving-by-maycee"
---

rain, needed rain. falls to drench the earth
to quench the roots and seeds to move to come alive
like oil, life giving to the automobile
protecting, lubricating the engine to life
water, for the thirsty to feed the hungry
to cool the body, flush the system
water necessary to life remain
