title: "the death of reality"

and now it makes sense, i was wrong because
i was wrong, to my eyes, to my face, there
can be so much destruction, like flies on
shit, you only mattered when you were still
important to me, but i forget that i do not
need, all the while i died, but i was not
the tragedy, as long as the sun does not
betray, i will stay to watch days end, maybe
then, goodbye death, i have proved you are
only life, ive only seen reflections of
truth, i have heard, every word, whispered
by every bird, i can smile because it hurts,
i have forgiven everyone but myself, the
thought of forever ends its' existence,
destroy me, i do not belong, destroy me,
because i cannot be wrong.
