i like sunsets,
and cold dark places,
coming out of myself,
when i am afraid and out of water,
standing still is like breathing,
i can do both without trying,
im seeing everything as an echo of itself,
while youll love the things youll never know,
but that is to be left in ancient time,
and the only things left to remember are insignificant,
i only ask for the promise of a smile,
forever is the wait.
