painted walls,
shopping malls,
rusted guitar strings,
beautiful things,

windows feel,
sitting still,
quietly,
warming me,

shadows play,
from far away,
i finger trace,
a strangers face,

i dream awake,
pretend im fake,
blink my eyes,
as sunset dies,

i watch them go,
pretend to know,
they run inside,
and love to hide.

11-20-01
