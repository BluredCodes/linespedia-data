life with sheep,
all asleep,
makes me weep,

watching sheep,
as they sleep,
makes me weep,

counting sheep,
trapped in sleep,
makes me weep,

f*ckin sheep,
deep in sleep,
makes me weep,

f*ckin sheep,
love to sleep,
makes me weep.
