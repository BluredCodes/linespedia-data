purple doctor p,snickers wannabe,poser and she smokes,yellow flower ghost,purple, purple cape,weak sauce, needs mean face,snuggle, blue shirt fiend,schizo, liquor queen,purple, purple skies,small hands, chinese eyes,devil with my soul,drowns in cereal,purple, purple stone,monkey eyebrow bones,princess of the sea,mermaids kiss square feet.
