anything that comes to mind,
keeps me from oncoming traffic,
4.5 billion years,
it's no wonder i'm so restless,
chocolate melts in boredom,
2 miles a day with vitamins,
staples, what a waste,
of an hours pay,
heliocentric theory,
destroys number twos,
djarums give me headaches,
but i feel new,
toast's on peanut butter,
they need mushrooms too,
i can feel it,
when they smile at me,
what's with grounding airplanes?
they were meant to fly,
if i should never land again,
i swear i'll be okay.
