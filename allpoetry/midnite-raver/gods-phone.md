she draws pictures in crayon,
and calls me,
323-514-8472,
“hi, this is god’s phone,
sorry he cant answer your call right now,
but if you leave me a message,
ill make sure that he gets it,
and ill have him give you a call back as soon as he can,”
she is drunk when she calls me,
she once wiped herself in front of me,
she draws pictures in crayon,
and hangs my soul from the ceiling.
