title: "death to time"

i can see sunsets with my eyes closed,
before thought can reach me,
i turn onto wrong streets,
and find my way home,

there are not enough words to feel safe,
but that is why it rains,
i forget what i was counting,
but i saw everything that will come,

souls are ancient fires,
left by our ancient selves,
we are nothing we think are,
we are far more.
