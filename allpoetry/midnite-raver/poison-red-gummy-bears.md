title: "poison red gummy bears"
writer: "midnite raver"

i could see it in your eyes,
all your shit and all your lies,
and i dont know how to feel for one who doesnt feel,

now theres nothing in my way,
and your more like a disease, i suffer when you feed,
i forgave you yesterday,

i could see it in your eyes,
you were fake and i was blind,
and i dont know how to bleed for one who doesnt bleed,

i wasted all my time,
you smile while i die,
and your not so fuckin pretty after all.
