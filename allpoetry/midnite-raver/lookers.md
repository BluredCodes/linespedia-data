i destroy them with crayons,
while destroying all the lines,
how come people in their bmw’s arent smiling?
lets thrive on wine, cow, and conversation,
feeding birds,
drowning from lack of flight,
i smell like a pretty animal,
destroying himself,
swimming in streams,
i figure out the universe,
and the purpose of paper clips.
