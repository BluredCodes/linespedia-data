title: "two notes, four today"
writer: "midnite raver"

i feel so good, all alone,
i save the world, from the cold,
i taste the sun, makes me whole,
i like my shade, with my souls,

drowning with the reasons for a purpose,
who are all these faces on these bottles?
they have left me empty on a friday,
i hope i wake up someday to be something,

i steer my ships, open seas,
dont close your eyes, open please,
i fly like kites, without strings,
as i create, from nothing,

dont be afraid to become nothing,
i have seen a snail crawl to heaven,
i have danced with things meant to destroy me,
yet i am still an ocean crashing for you.

10-16-01
