i stare,
do i really need all these colors?
“we dont believe in nothing lebowski”,
shes beautiful,
i stare,
because i cannot stop looking,
i found that line everyone talks about,
ice cold is best,
i stare,
thoughts cannot see,
demons fall asleep,
tired and afraid,
i stare,
and it makes sense,
suns get tired too,
chasing moons.
