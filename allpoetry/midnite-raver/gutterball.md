gutterball,im bouncing off the walls,heres my song,im nothing after all,im so sad,it really makes me mad,im not here,my shadow has no fear,gutterball,she makes me feel so small,nothings wrong,cause i can break through walls,im so sad,i lost my smile again,take me home,i want to be alone,gutterball,i want to take my time,find my place,and be somebody great,gutterball,im bouncing off the walls,gutterball,im nothing after all,gutterball,she makes me feel so small,gutterball,im breaking through the walls,gutterball.
