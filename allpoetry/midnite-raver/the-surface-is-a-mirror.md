title: "the surface is a mirror"
writer: "midnite raver"

the days take their time with me,
as i uncover secrets,
i stand alone,
and rule the world,

they go, and they come,
but i stay,
im here,
forever,

there are many faces,
there are many sides,
to smiles and eyes,
but i, 
i am safe from these things,

insanity is happiness,
it is rejected and feared,
but not by me,
i go with it,
into today,
into tomorrow,
with breezes and thoughts,
there are many depths,

i smile at my reflection,
it becomes an infinite ripple of my many selves,
i become anonymous,
i become a stranger,
free to rediscover himself,

on warm sunny days,
i love the world,
and all its conversations,
and all its brightness,
all its dimness,
all its traffic,
all its smiles,
all its boredom,
all its nonsense,
all its chaos,
and all its shade,
i cannot stop its burn,
i cannot stop its time,
i am its, it is mine,
creating one another,
together we are safe.......
