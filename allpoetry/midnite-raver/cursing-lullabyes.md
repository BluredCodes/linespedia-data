title: "cursing lullabyes"

tell me about the sky you couldn't love,tell me about the secret part of you,show me how to see with my eyes closed,tell me how it feels to never feel,tell me about the purpose of your heart,tell me about imaginary truth,show me how to drown until i smile,tell me why you always run away,show me how to betray my own self,tell me because i don't want to know,tell me about the voices of your fears,empty bottles hold a lot of tears,tell me why i chase those things that kill,tell me why i make it home alright,ask me how i know i'm more than you,i am all the loneliness of night,i am the destroyer of all lies,i am all the silence of the world,i am all the pain that wallows still,i am all the wounds that never heal,i am the creator of pretend,i am all the worlds that never end,i am all the forces in the wind,i am just a shadow with a pen.11-2-01
