sweet herb of God,
inhale me,
leave me intricate,
infinite,
perpetually insane,
with sad smiles,
i want to imagine i am words wanting to come to life,
i don’t want to hear telephones,
a highway of drones,
i don’t need eyes,
or electric cries,
or bristled wants for fame,
bloody shame,
printed in gold letters allowing me to read itself,
obliterating bus stops,
while people walk through red lights,
going mad,
because theyre sad,
my arms arent long enough to love someone,
so i smile at what i have created.
