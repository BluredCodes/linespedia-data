high,
in the sky,
she flies like nothing,
and uses words like i cannot,
my imaginary floors will never know those eyes,
she decomposes in my mind,
i wish i could too,
i feel like someone she loves,
she scratches me to prove it,
reading books,
i ignore her when she sleeps,
to slay bad dreams,
and cover her,
for all i know she laughs at me,
burns my poetry,
peacefully,
but still,
she’s safer than my own shadow.
