she tames me,
makes me,
with fluids and stares,
my goddess,
my obsession,
my chinese addiction,
if she only knew,
the things i would do,
to make her mine forever,
your taste,
your skin,
your smell,
your love,
i sculpt you with my hands and lips,
i drip you dry,
i am your god and your whore,
and you are my monkey,
my soft shade of brown,
the cutest drunk i know,
the devil with my soul,
with your inviting eyes,
i am in your spell,
on my way to hell,
shes electric,
and im insane,
my shock therapy,
my poison red gummy bear,
my retard,
my yellow love,
my purple stone,
my destroyer.
