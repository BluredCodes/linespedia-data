twenty one,
does it get worse?
does it get better?
im still a child,
im still a boy,
as lost as ive ever been,
with tired eyes,
and endless thought after thought,
endless dream after dream,
will i ever find anything im looking for?
what am i looking for?
i hope i die before i run out of words,
i hope i die before i stop seeing the beauty in the world,
am i growing too fast?
am i growing too slow?
i feel like an old man who knows everything,
but i know that i dont,
have i wasted too much time?
have i polluted my body enough?
am i insane?
am i a poet?
am i anything at all?
what the f*ck happened to that happy little boy?
