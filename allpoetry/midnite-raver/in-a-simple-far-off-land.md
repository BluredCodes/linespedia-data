in a simple far off land,
all ringers are off,
and im so far away from everyone i smile,

in a simple far off land,
i read books and people,
and i smile at a creature smiling back at me,

in a simple far off land,
i am warm and happy,
and i fall in love with strangers on the street,

in a simple far off land,
i hope i never land,
in a world that ill never understand,

in a simple far off land,
i take poems from the sky,
and the secret is there are no reasons why.
