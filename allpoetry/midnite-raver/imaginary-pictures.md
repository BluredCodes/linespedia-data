children keep truth alive,
she wants rings, not love,
just as long as her world can still be flat,
and she can still be afraid of the dark,
im the madman who turns on lights,
the one who doesnt talk,
the one who talks too much,
i destroy, i destruct, i defuse,
i paint with my pen and my words,
you failed to understand,

so she loves, and she hates, and she cries,
looks for magic in someones eyes,
but you fall cause you're high when you're down,
whos the narc with the fake heart and badge?
who cant dream without falling out of bed,
they buy water in plastic containers,
when it falls from the sky for free,
astronomy for covergirls,
keep counting the stars as they fall,
they'll keep you from walking alone.

11-1-01
