imagining i am that which cannot be destroyed,
man cannot handle power,
is it God that they put their trust in or exchange rates?
they are the source of their own demise,
the fluidity of worth,
in a fluid reality,
life from non-life becoming non-life again,
inside the soul, of something that does not know that it is,
"floating upon the surface for the birds,"
exactly where i want to be.
