Clocks tell the time
the time of day
the time to eat
the time to sleep

Clocks tell you you're late
Tell you it's time to go back to bed
Time for that 9:30 appointment
Time for the trick`o'treaters to begin arriving

What clocks don't tell you is
the time is right
the time is wrong 
the time has come & gone
that time is over
it's time to move on1996(?),
