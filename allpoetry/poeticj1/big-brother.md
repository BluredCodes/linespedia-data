Big Brother 
Every since I as a little girl, and the time 
You tried to curl my hair, I miss you dearly. 
I miss your eyes and the times you made me 
    Cry 

Big Brother I wish I was there to say my good- 
    Byes,
'Member the first time you taught me how to 
Ride my bike, I fell so hard it felt like a push from 
     Ike 

You held your hand out to me, and I couldn't say 
Good-bye. When my cat died, you were by my 
Side, oh, Big Brother how I want to cry. 
Your not here to stop my cries, I just hope it's 
            not too late 
            to say 
            Good-bye!
