Love 
It's the sparkle in your eyes, 
The nervousness in your heart, 
The temptation you think you
     Feel and need 

It's the sparks you see, and the way you feel 
The precious time together you spent, 
And pray to God that it will never 
    End 

Some call it infatuation, 
It's the glare in your eyes 
It scares you cause you think it might not last
And the aversion builds up 
As you hope it past 

It's the most unique feeling of them all, 
When you were together you thought he/she was 
The one, but now that's gone, the pain 
In your heart is so heavy, it feels like it 
Weighs a ton!
        Love is what it's called
