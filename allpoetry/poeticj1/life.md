Life 
It's the air we breath 
The blood we pump 
Some live, some die       

It's the time you walk 
Your daughter down the 
Aisle, watch your son 
Grow into a handsom young 
Man 

Some are too busy to know 
What to do with it 
Some are confused because 
They don't understand why 
They have to deal with 
It 

Some will be happy when 
It's over, some don't know 
How to spend it, some worry where it went 
When their time is up 
Or threw, they wish any
And every minute they 
Would have spent
