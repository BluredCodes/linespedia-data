Why Am I in Love? 
Why am I with him if
I don't love him 
Could that be a sin? 

To be with someone 
And don't know why 
Is like a true lie! 

I don't feel him like he feels me 
Or could it be 
I'm with him cause he's free? 

I don't know if I love him,
The consequences are 
Crucial 
But if I'm not with him, my hearts feelings would be 
Brutal 

Then if I don't know why I'm with him 
         "Why Am I in Love?"
