so young at seven 
so far away from seventy 
learning skip rope, hop scotch 
Jesus in Heaven 

Grandma’s teachings 
of:
Life’s Spinning Wheels 

Now you’re twenty-seven 
kids of your own 
mate so successful 
your name forever 
being called upon 

oh my! look how our 
parents have grown,, 

still teaching:
Life’s Spinning Wheel 

Years have flown 
seven turned into seventy-seven 
parents so far in the past 
years gone by 
kids once seven 
now with families 
of their own 

Grandpa, grandma 
now your known 
Passing on teaching’s 

of :
Life’s Spinning Wheels
