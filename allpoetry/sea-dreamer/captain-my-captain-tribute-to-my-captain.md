Captain My Captain 
to another world of 
feng-shui 
you were taken 

given no choice in 
the matter 
leaving those 
you loved 
who love you 
behind 

you were the one 
person who believed 
in me when I didn’t 
in myself 

Your love I cherished 
pride a standing 
ovation to know 
a grand-dee 
with such 
grand-eur 

Here I am a 
different day 
different time 
proud of me I hope 
you would be of 
me 

trying to beat 
the geomancy 

from the teachings you 
gave to me 
of divination 

to be the best 
Captain I could be 

Remembering the day your 
crew became my mates 
the day becoming my crew 

giving me the 
Salute 
from 
Captain to Captain 

passing the knowledge 
of team work 
the strength to sail 
whether it be smooth 
or trying to keep 
afloat 
when gale came 
barging through 
the door 

the words I will always 
recall they still are 
with me 

and when I felt I’ve forgotten 
the days I feel I can’t 
go on 

Remembering the words 
you once 
told me 

when you’re gone 
you won’t be really 
if I listen to the 
winds of the 
sea 


your ghost whispers 
of wisdom 
still whispering strong 
from the feng-shui 
chosen for you 


Assuring me 
there is no other life 
than being a 
ghost sailor whispering 
across the sea 

I know if it were left up 
to you; you’d still be 
here showing off your 
expertise 


telling me to keep my head up 
to step right up 
there is no place 
to drown my thoughts 
no room for pity 
reminding me 
to lay my 
burdens down underneath 
the sea 

Captain, My Captain 
strong you still tell me 
I am, to be the 
best captain I can 
be, 

I miss you 
I always will 
but with your strength of 
encouragement still 
being given to me 
from your ghost whispering 
across the sea 

I salute you still my 
Captain 
from Captain 
I know you’re 
still sailing the seas 
waiting for us 

to set sail 
to listen to the 
ghost whispers still 
passing on 
history 

from ghost past of 
grand duers’ of 
grand-dees
