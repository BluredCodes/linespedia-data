Frozen in Time 

Along the hallway
hangs pictures on walls 
memories of faces of moments  
never to age with time 
captured in frozen states of mind 

walking down the hall
I’d feel warmth of eyes 
seeming to follow me 
as you once did in live concert 
faces filled with love smiling back 
capture in frozen states of mind 

Now your not here 
your memory lingering 
haunting the hallway 
in pictures hanging on walls 
never to age with time 
faces smiling back  
when you had warmth in your eyes 

Capturing our love 
in frozen states of mind
