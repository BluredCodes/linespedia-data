---
title: "Sweet sounds of love"
writer: "Alain"
slug: "sweet-sounds-of-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

Sweet sounds of love
heavens above.
Moist sucking sounds
of love surround.

Surrendered to love
we partake
of its intended
carnal expression.

Kissing
nibbling
sucking
licking
each other 
in sensitive places.

Oh the sweet sounds of paradise!
This is what our consummate love sounds
in the ears of innocent children and cherubs.

Panting
gasping
hissing
groaning
moaning 
imploring

Uncontrolled mouth and tongues
engaged in a fierce duel
of the swords.
Our ferocious pace increases
till we reach the moment of 
release.........
in uncontrollable spurts
and suctioning spasms.
Pulling
tugging
squezzing
milking
phalic skin and flesh
reverberate
sexual liquid
exchanged and mingled together
filling each other
to capacity.

Breathless we rest.
Laying still
we sigh
we suspire
we laugh 
and we smile.

In our afterglow
we look at each other
as if we composed a symphony
fully orchestrated and tuned to the sweet sounds
of love in the making!
