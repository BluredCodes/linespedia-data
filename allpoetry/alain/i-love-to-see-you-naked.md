title: "I love to see you naked"
writer: "Alain"

I love to see you naked stretched out
in bed
like a carnal apple,
Incandescent moon,
tempting siren,
You are my midnight fantasy.

I love to see you uncovered naked
in bed
Delicate,
quiet,
innocently sleeping
displayed to the sun like the earth.

I long to touch your naked warm skin.
freshly baked in the morning.
You feel like a peach
soft
juicy 
warm
moist
tender.

I like to run my hands through your hair,
and your breasts,
hips,
thighs,
calfs,
feet,
caress you lightly with the yolk of my fingers
like drawing you blind, invisible
in my mind and heart.

I want to remember you like this:
Naked underneath my arms,
legs dangling high
Our bodies rocking,
swaying 
in a unison rhythm of love
nibbling and soft biting
Flesh to flesh
meeting pleasure with pleasure
Lips interlocked
making love
Softly,
tenderly,
kissing,
moaning,
pulsing organs.
looking into your soft rounded absent eyes
your curled eyelashes,
your quivering mouth,
your serpentine flickering tongue,
and your blushed cheeks.

Oh coitus magnificat!
Oh ecstasy never forsake me!
Oh desire never leave me!
Halos and orbits around us!
Wandering and shooting stars surrounding!
Bodies intertwined, mingled in eternal solstice.

Let me paint you naked
with soft hues and multicolered love
Let me fill galleries and museums
with your portrait of uncorruptable risen flesh;

You fill me!
Naked as you are,
my gift
and my song.
