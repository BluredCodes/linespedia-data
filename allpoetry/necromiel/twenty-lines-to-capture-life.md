title: "Twenty Lines to Capture Life"
writer: "Necromiel"

The sound of the waterfall and river
washes over me, caressing my ears
the sunshine dances through the water
the moss upon the massive stones
blanketing their hulking forms
which are dwarfed to diminutive
by the immensity of these canyon walls
so vast as to defy the eyes of mortal men
their scale a thing beyond our sight
monumental and the pith of natures awe

insignificant things such as myself
can only be observers of such as this
and bathe awhile in the wonder of it
just to let part of that beauty soak into us
to try to burn it into our inadequate minds
endeavoring to carry such magnificence
striving to retain the miraculous sight
ageless and resplendent and utterly perfect 
from time before memory, from lifetimes yore
mirthfully melancholy I muse, missing you...
