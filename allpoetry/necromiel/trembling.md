---
title: "Trembling"
writer: "Necromiel"

the sky was gold and ivory
as the sun faded from view
we sat on stone steps silently
my head pressed against you

I held your hands and glanced over
and then our lips where all one
the heat of passion burned my soul
I felt so warm I forgot the numb

trembling inside your embrace
I ran my fingers through your hair
nestled my lips to your face
kissing you, more than I could bear 

I long to live inside that moment
I wish I could just freeze time
for without you to breathe is torment
though evanescent you were mine

no...we were each others...
We just held each other

and with my ear to your chest
the beating of your heart was pounding
but somehow, I felt so at rest
as though peace had finally found me
