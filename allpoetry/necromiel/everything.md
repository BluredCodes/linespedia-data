everyday is a miracleor everyday is empty shiteverything is ugly and beautifulit’s only what you make of iteverywhere is the house of godeveryone is a Buddha insideall is perfect, all is flawedeverything is nothing, but aliveJesus, Buddha, Bodhisattvachi and karma, holy dharma samadhi in the diamond sutrapopes and druids, Dali Lamas how can anybody claim they’re rightand condemn the rest as being wrong?who discerns wicked from contrite?can they not see it’s the same song?we’re all just singing alongto the beat of a different drumbut all the words are really one
