Once upon a time,
Is such a sad way to begin.
It stands as a reminder,
That we will never have,
The adventures,
Or even a whispered happily ever after in the end.
We live our own fairy tales,
Locked inside our heads,
They tell us to come out of there,
That we need to live.


Softly sing that lulaby,
I remember it from a place I knew,
"And so they danced away,
In a bubble of azure blue."
