---
title: "Blind"
writer: "silver"
slug: "blind"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Look
At
Me...
I 
Am
All
You 
Want
To
Seem.
Tell me if it's worth it,
The shell that I bear,
If I gave it all to you,
Would you like it too?
No-one else liked it,
They would rather be,
Everything they want,
Not this make-believe.
Sometimes I guess,
Being like another,
Who in the end is fake,
Reminds us of ourselves(to much),
So another place we take,
Hoping always hoping,
For something that is real...
