It just keeps coming back,
No matter what I do.
The endless question in my mind,
Uncertanty haunts my movements,
I hesitate before I speak.
Trembling slightly I run my hand,
Down your face,
Because I cannot tell what is real.
All those diffrent shades of colour,
The reds have begun to fade,
I don't know what is real.
For I live in my memory,
My mind...
Maybe it is just,
A shade of the past,
A memory,
