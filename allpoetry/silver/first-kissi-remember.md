One year ago,
This day,
We were sitting together at Holly's party.
You kissed me,
And I can't help but remember,
How close we seemed then.
We still haven't talked about it,
Instead you teach me guitar.
It's another party, 
And we're alone...
You try to play Tantric for me,
But can't remember the chords.
As we walk back to join the others,
You lean over to kiss me(again),
And I realize that you are taller.
I marvel,
At how much we have grown,
Both apart and together.
One year has passed,
But the kiss itslef is so much like the first,
It's uncanny,
