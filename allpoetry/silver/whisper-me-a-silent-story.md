title: "Whisper me a Silent Story"
writer: "silver"

Remember the scars,
Remember the pain,
Remind yourself,
Of what you lost,
But remember the laughter too.
Don't be ashamed,
To let yor tears fall,
You may yet grow to cherish them.
Remember love...
And tears,
And scars,
And hate...
For they all go hand in hand.
The beauty of peace,
Is also in chaos,
The passion,
The life,
The soul...
Remeber that they are one.
