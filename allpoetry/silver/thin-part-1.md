I look in the mirror,
And see the candle I am burning reflected back at me.
It opens up the planes of my face that I rarely notice.
I look at all the shadow,
I twist and turn to see,
How the candle in the mirror,
Makes everything so diffrent for me.

I look back at the mirror,
A few months have passed,
"It's only 1 more pound,
I can lose it in another day,"
To myself I say.

No more little girl in red,
A gaunt cheecked woman is in her stead.
75 pounds at age 13,
