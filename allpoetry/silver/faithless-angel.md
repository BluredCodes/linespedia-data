I guess it's good,
You didn't kiss me goobye,
Or I might have clung,
And waited.
Now I'm alone,
In you're room,
Reading the note.
"Be back soon."
Soon is to far,
My tears make the ink,
Run like madness,
Ruining your handwriting,
That I knew by heart.
I got a little sick of waiting,
With my shirt collar half undone,
And my lipstick rubbed away,
By the hand that brushed my lips.
It's a good thing you didn't kiss me,
Because a kiss calls for faith,
And it ran out for you.
My cellphon is ringing,
It's my ride,
Jordan as always,
