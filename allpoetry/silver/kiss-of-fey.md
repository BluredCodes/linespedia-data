---
title: "Kiss of Fey"
writer: "silver"
slug: "kiss-of-fey"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/32462-Kiss-of-Fey-by-silver"

The darkness burns with lights,
I no longer breath...
I want to feel your hot tears,
Mingle with my own.
You saw me when I was invisible,
So my cloak I didn't need,
But when I took it off,
It was you I couldn't see.
