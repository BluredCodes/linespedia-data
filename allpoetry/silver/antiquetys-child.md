title: "Antiquetys Child"

The dual blade,
Slices the worlds wrist,
Pulling you into,
And above,
The sphere of life.
Not mutilation,
Just a form of art,
Self expression,
Written in glazed eyes.
Forever forgot to put,
Her trademark sign on this one.
A saddly mortal life,
That drank from the chalice,
But forgot to swallow.
