title: "Jump From The Tower"

I looked upon a flame,
As it flared and died.
Now with all the rest,
Watch with tears in heart and eyes,
As the little star falls;
An angel cast from heaven's sky.
Desperate for one last breath,
And something beyond,
What in life they sought to find.
Beyond every silver lining,
There is a secret,
That no one wants to find.
