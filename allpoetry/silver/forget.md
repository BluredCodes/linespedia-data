title: "Forget"
writer: "silver"
slug: "forget"

I's frightening...
How cold you look,
When you smile.
My mouth is frozen too.
Both to cold to say farewell,
I let my eye's open once,
To convey what I meant to say.
Love is just an instinct...
That I will not believe.
Love is something more,
It just has to be.
