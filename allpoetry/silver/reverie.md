In my memory I see them
In my memory I cry 
My eyes are in the present though
Remaining wearily dry
Stuck within a mortal shell 
I fear I must give myself leave 
I'm out it's over 
Forget what was once so clear
I'm out and it's over 
Lost but unable to shed my tears
You think you know me 
I cant be hurt you say
Always so happy 
I died inside today.
