---
title: "Please Just Be There!"

Tonight you said,
That you were going to die.
Until now,
I never realized all you wanted was to hold a hand.
Maybe I should have listened to istinct,
And gave you a hug,
But you were to far gone for me.
Just please be there,
When I wake up,
Waiting for me at the lockers,
Like you always do,
With that smile,
That always made me,
Smile back.
I swear if you are,
I will hold you,
And this time I will listen,
To what I never heard before.
But clocks never tick backwards,
And all I can do,
Is wait for the dawn.
I Love You.
