Cover it up,
Practice your faces,
Let them forget who is real,
You are the nameless deciever.
Begin to shed your guise,
But then realize that it has already fallen away.
Now you are naked,
Exposed,
And Proud,
In this defiance.

Note:this is for the man in florida who was arrested due to indecint exposure...he was my great uncle(I'm serious).
