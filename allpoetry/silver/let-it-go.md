---
title: "Let it Go"
writer: "silver"
slug: "let-it-go"

Windmill heart, 
spinning round. 
Autumn leaves abound 
but never touch the ground, 
catch just one, 
But they evaded, 
There in the flow, 
I watched them all 
fly by. 
Protecting's not the answer... 
let them go.
