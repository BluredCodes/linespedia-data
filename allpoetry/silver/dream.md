GODSMACK4807: what is beyond deat though 
flameofsilver75: i dont know...i just hope its sleep

I am so weary, my foots fall heavy in the footprints life told me to walk in. I'm hearded like a cow by an invisible dog. I just want to fall down into someones arms but I don't want them to fall too from the weight of all the chains that bind me. 

flameofsilver75: im tired...:-[
FallenWinter: me too....

So let us all be tired together, let us all fall from life's grasp. Lets all be tired together, let us rest in deaths clasp. 

I dont know wat comes after death, I just hope its sleep, dreams filled with lavender clouds.
