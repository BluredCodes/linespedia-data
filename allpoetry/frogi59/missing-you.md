---
title: "Missing You"
writer: "Frogi59"

---

In every mirror
That I see,
Your glowing reflection
Stands beside me.

With every breath
Of the freshest air,
Comes the sweetest smell
Of your beautiful hair.

In the poetic song
Of every bird,
Your softest voice
Is all that I have heard.

Upon every sip
Of the finest wine,
I taste your gentle kiss
And know that you are mine.

But in everything
That I do,
Without your touch
I am missing you.
