title: "Down to Earth"

As I float on a sea of clouds,I step in a hole and plummet to the earth.Above me the stars are laughing,below my land is waiting.Soon I will land on a pillowand my family will treat me.After licking my wounds, I raise my chin, look above,and build my ladder again.
