The beauty for which I live
Is the sparkle of your eye.
To see it once, I would give
My heart, my soul, live and die.

To spend a moment with you
And catch glance of your smile,
I would give all that I do
Even walk the lonly mile.

To share a laugh that will last
Would be as good as a kiss,
But for that I do not pass.
That is heaven with it's bliss.

You're my sun, my stars and more.
You are all that I wish for.
