---
title: "The Salmon Saved the Skunk"
writer: "Frogi59"
slug: "the-salmon-saved-the-skunk"

We draw the first set
Not a fish in the net
Again we lay open
still to come short hoping

Surly in a good day
The run will swim our way
Yet as we draw to a close
we bow to out foes

There are no more fish
Nothing to fill the dish
Still we will try
For this we won't die

Then the skipper Cries out
'There's a jumper' all in one shout
We lay the nets straight
Hoping this fish was all fate

We stand waiting for a hit
No such luck, not even a bit
We get the command 'Bring it in...
Our day will come again'

The first net was empty
The second the same
Then a splash and a tug
maybe our salmon just came

We haul the net in
Pulling with all our might
This fish would not give up
Not without a long fight

Up over the rollers
Down with a loud thunk
We caught our only salmon
The salmon that saved the skunk
