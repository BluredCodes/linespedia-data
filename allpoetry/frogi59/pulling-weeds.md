Thanks dad, You woke me    early, got me out of bed,   and put me straight to work.Thanks dad, You planned out   my day and filled it with   chores, only when I was done,   could I play.Thanks dad, I missed the    trip up to the mountains   with my friends, and I    never got to do anything   fun, until my chores were done.Thanks dad, It seems like    my only pass-time is pulling   weeds, digging holes, doing   chores and never really   getting paid.Thanks dad, for shaking my    hand and telling me,   'good job, you do good   work son.'Thanks dad, for giving me   respect and trust so when   my work was done, I used    my time wizely.Thanks dad, far giving me    character and teaching me   life's lessons like dependability   and reliability.Thanks dad, for being a friend   and putting me to work so   I learned to earn a living.Thanks dad, for everything I    am is from you.  I want   to be like you when I    grow up.Dad, you don't have to pay   me for the work I have   done.  We'll call it even.
