---
title: "Poet's eyes"
writer: "Frogi59"
slug: "poets-eyes"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/10179-Poets-eyes-by-Frogi59"

Your beauty is too deep,
  your skin too soft.
I could never write about you
  I just see you through poet's eyes.

Your heart is too big,
  your smile too wide.
I would never know where to start,
  I just see you through poet's eyes.

Your voice is too kind,
  your smell too sweet.
I could never know just what to say,
  I just see you through poet's eyes.
 
So, if I were a poet,
  I would never write of you,
because words could never do
  justice to you
