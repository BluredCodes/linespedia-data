---
title: "Back to the Wind"
writer: "Frogi59"

It comes to this.
A moment of stillness and sadness.
Does the heart fill with need,
Longing for that which is desire?
Is there pain deep inside,
Only to be described by separation?

Recolection overcomes the emptyness.
What was will be again.
I depart with my back to the wind.
