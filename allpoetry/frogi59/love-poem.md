This is a poem, I wrote for you.
It's about love and joy that I hold true.
It is not Shakespeare, it is not Frost,
But it did come to mind with minimal cost.

It's about early morning, it's about late evening,
it's about extreme beauty in the account of believing.
It may not be romantic, laking the right emotion,
but it comes from the heaven and the stars
and the vast of the ocean.

It is not the future, for this we cannot tell.
It is not the past, those moments did not last.
This is the love of time, that has always been,
made to last forever.
