---
title: "A Delicate Sight"

Come and lie with me tonight.Come and see this delicate sight.It's glow and glamour will enlightenyour spirits and it's magical touch will fulfill your dreams.This sight I speak of is the reddish yellow sunset beneath a beautiful night's sky.
