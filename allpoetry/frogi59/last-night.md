---
title: "Last Night"
writer: "Frogi59"
slug: "last-night"
source: "AllPoetry"

I wrote a poem for you   last night, but I couldn't express your heart.I drew a picture for you    last night, but I couldn't capture your beauty.I sang a song for you   last night, but I couldn't describe your touch.So all I could do was think   about you, and wish you could read my thoughts.
