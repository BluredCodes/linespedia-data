While the fog of fear

  clouds my misguided eyes,

    I stop for a brief moment

      to take in the air

        and I am suddenly

          reminded of my childhood.



Playing games 

  is not far from reality.

    The ground is trembling.

      My right hand falls.

        Death is upon us and I move on.
