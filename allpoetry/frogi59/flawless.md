---
title: "Flawless"
writer: "Frogi59"

A glass that breaks
A hand that shakes
This cannot be so
In the world I know

A history of crime
and the lack of a dime
This cannot be so 
In the world I know

Perfection is my name
Suddenly I have no game
This cannot be so 
In the world I know

Now I understand
No one has a steady hand
So anything can go 
In the World I know
