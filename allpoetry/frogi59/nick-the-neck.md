title: "Nick the Neck"

The sun will still riseBut the water will not fall.Waves will cover the land of heatA drought has swept upon us.Drink the life that is lifeAnd thou shall live.Take the life that is livingAnd thou shall starve.The power that drivesDoes not cross the desert,But the blood of the camelCan last forever.
