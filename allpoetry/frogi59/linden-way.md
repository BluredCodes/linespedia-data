In the Distancethe leaves fallIndian summerblankets a pathReflection of a facein a windowBy my sidewe drive awayThe first flake fallsdecorations on the wallScenic mansionThroughout the streetI want to walk on down Linden WayI want to build a fireand watch it playI want to carve the seasonwith leaves on the groundJoke and laughthrowing pumpkin seeds around
