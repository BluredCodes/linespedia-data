title: "Where the Sea Meets the Sky"

As the sun dips it's feet
  into the ocean, I drift away.
With my head on a cloud
  I dream no longer of the past.
Although the past is not forgotten,
  my new thoughts lead me ahead.
And I drift away with the tide
  to the place where the sea meets the sky.
