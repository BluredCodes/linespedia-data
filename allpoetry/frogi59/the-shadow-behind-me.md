The shadow behind me  guides my life before me.Its not mine  but who I am.My God is my shadow  taking every step as my own.Leading my life before me  but ready to catch my fall.I am guided with every control  but lost in the vast world of life.My destiny is not my own  but a big part of the Lord's plan.Fall 1999,
