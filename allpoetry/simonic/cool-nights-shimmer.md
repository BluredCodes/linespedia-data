title: "Cool Night's Shimmer"

A shimmer
Gently shifting in the cool night's air
As your reflection
Fades upon the water's smooth surface

Shimmering
Shifting to the cool night's breeze
Reflected by the moonlight
Is your image

Dancing amid the depths
Shimmering with every simple gesture
Wisps of your touches play upon the water
Shifting the black waters with light

To beat gently at my feet
Their touch
Soothing, refreshing
Entranced

Your reflection walks on the waters
Taking smooth soft steps
Towards me
Captured by the shimmer

This rapturous anticipation
Holds me, captures me
Wisps of your shimmering reflection
Reach out to touch me

Eyes closed breathing deep, I await
Bliss

Water's licking at my feet
As eye's open
I stare at the moon's reflection
Upon the cold dark waters

I shift within reality
And hold myself tightly
To warm myself from the cool
