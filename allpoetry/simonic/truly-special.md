title: "Truly Special"
writer: "Simonic"

I am here now
To explain to you
How special you truly are
Truly special, to me

You do not know it
Yet, you are it
Special beyond my own thought
Is it because of the love I hold for you?
Or is it something else
Something superficial

No, it is this love
This unconditional love
Truly, I love you
Asking nothing in return
What I give
I give with love

So, why would I love?
Love something that is not special
Do not look at yourself as special
For if you do, you limit yourself
Limit to how special, you think you can be

No, let others give praise to you
Let others see you and say,
“She is a very special person”
Truly, you are very special
Why or how, then you may ask

To tell you, I would have to cry
Cry out everything about you
Truly, I do not love just one thing
But all the things which you are
Hide not the things which you think
Think that make you ugly

Hand them to me, and with every bit
You become more special to me
Yet, I yield with love to your wishes
It is not I, that must tell, but you
And in your own time, you will choose
Choose what you think is right
And patience I will hold



Do not think I will reject you
For anything which you do
I will accept it
For, truly, that is what makes
You special, to others and I

Why then are you so special to me?
I’ll begin my heartfelt cry
You care for those who are not cared for
You care for me
You find joy when joy should not be found
You find joy for me
Your thoughts are gentle
You instill gentle thoughts upon me

Your touch is soft
Your skin is smooth
Your features are perfectly yours
Your voice is the voice I can read
Your eyes are deep with feelings 
Covered by a veil, veil of the world

Oh, you are too special to remain
Remain behind that veil
Slowly, if you wish, remove
The veil which rests upon you
Come into the light, as I may look upon
Look upon, your perfect beauty

Truly you are perfectly special
In that, you are beautiful
Beautiful beyond thought
Do not be fooled by that veil
The veil of the world
Beauty is not in the world
But in you

Do not be ashamed for what is in you
For what you are ashamed of
Is what I find your beauty in
Yet, in this beauty
There is love
Unmarked love

Oh, do not be hurt any longer
Do not wretch inside any longer
Bring your ugliness out
Give it to one who loves
I will turn it to perfect beauty


Yet, I understand the pain
Pain of lifting up the veil
The fear of the pain
The unknown 
The problems within
Do not fear them

Fear not what is old
The past is unchangeable
Unveil so that your future
May be changed
As you understand what comes
From that of the past

Remove this worldly veil
Oh, my special one
Come towards me in perfect beauty
So I may smile with love
Upon the one who is perfectly special

For you, one which I love are
