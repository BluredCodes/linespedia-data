title: "The Feel"

(I thought I just did this
(An hour or two ago
(|Why then-, are you- up again?
(Why don't you [just] leave me alone

(The feel)
(It's all for the feel)
...That is why you're back again

(to feel)

(only to feel)

The night's wrecked when you rise
Unable to feel you--
(unable to please)
You, my dear friend

(You come only because of the feel)

(The feel)
(It's all for the feel)
...Stay down this time

(the feel)

(only the feel)Key:
| = louder/gruffer, if without a closing, just first is loud 
( = softer/smoother, if without a closing, just first is soft 
[] = debating if word should be in there
--- = about how long the word/note is carried 
. = pause
