So he spake
“Give not life, 
Nor give death”
As a cool breeze fell 
Upon that lake

The lake
In a clamourous reply
Drank in those deep dark words
As a life which was not a life
Was given to he as a fake

Within the dark eyes
Of the lake’s own life
A petal fell
Down into it’s depths
As it too did die

“Run no further,
Drink the waters of death,
Only to receive life”
Spake he again
As they fell further

Upon their knees
Weeping and praying
They drank that water they did
And one by one
They became trees

Leaves brown and dull
Bark darkened ash
No nesting birds
No playful music
Only a hollow skull

“Warned you I did!”
He cried
And in unison they did reply
“We did as you had said,
We did as you had bid”

“That you did and now you
Receive that which you had sought
Sink your roots into the ground
And drink the waters for all
Eternity, that is what I now bid”

So they all sank their roots
Into that chalky ground
Unable to move
Under the sun
Under the roots

Death
Only for life
Yet not a life
Given to them
For the path to take
