---
title: "Love to Hate"
writer: "Simonic"
slug: "love-to-hate"

I love to hate you
I live to hate you
You are my hate
You are my life

The life which I hate
Yet, without hate
There would be no love
Without love
What would I hate?

I hate the way you talk
Your sweet tender words
I hate the way you smile
It melts my thoughts
I hate the way you hold me
For sleep soon finds me

This is what I hate
I hate all the ways I love
For love is born of hate
For, without hate
Love would not be

So, I love to hate
With that hate
We may love
The love
That we hate.
