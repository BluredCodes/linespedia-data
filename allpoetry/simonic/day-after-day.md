I don’t know how I let you
Pile up so high
From a small little piece
To now this towering monstrosity

Day after day
I saw you 
Day after day
I stepped on you

I’m not all too certain how
How this came to be
But now you’ve taken
Sleep away from me

Night after night
I cursed at you
Night after night
I gave you more

I wonder now
If this is your revenge
For my inconsiderate actions
All generally aimed at you

Day after day
I kicked you around
Night after night
My smile, turned frown

Now I sit
Waiting on you
To finish up
And get yourself clean

Night after day
You gave me that ache
Day after night
You greeted me once again

You, my friend
Are my towering monster
Not too filthy, but not to clean
Yet, now I can stand and say

You, my friend
