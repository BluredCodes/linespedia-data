---
title: "Is It?"
writer: "Simonic"
slug: "is-it"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/3784-Is-It--by-Simonic"
---

Is it at that point?
When words become lies
Mixed with wretched deceit
Love no longer sought

Is it within death?
That gives one freedom
Or within the knowledge
Of knowing your impending failure

Is it correct to give?
Everything to one person
Only to have it stabbed
Into it’s source

Is it correct to have a heart?
When you know it will soon fade
As it slowly bleeds
Draining your life and sanity

Is it right to die for a girl?
To wither from within
Turning black as the darkest night
Spawning demons of hatred within

Is it to die?
When you lose hope 
In what could have never been
As lies had always flowed

Is it to live?
When you had followed lies
Only to be beguiled
To your grave

Is it?
No, for she was your death
Death that tasted so sweet
Yet, her lure, irresistible

Is it this death
That she has become
The utter ugliness of death
With all it’s ambitions ending the same
