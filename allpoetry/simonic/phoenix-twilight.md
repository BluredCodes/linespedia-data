---
title: "Phoenix Twilight"
writer: "Simonic"
slug: "phoenix-twilight"
source: "AllPoetry"

Standing above this little hole, enticing me to
Look on down and in, as
Ebony walls assail
A poor and helpless, fire’s twilight

Twinkling amid a cold and dark
Sea of restive tranquility
Trying to sway and restore
The flowing indulgences, of this little hole

Twilight pulsing, moments
Pass by below me
Staring on down and in
 Deep into this rotting hole

Rotting darkness conquering, over
This pitiful hole, blinking
Out this twilight, coming
Torrent of darkness filling this little rotten, hole

Standing above this little hole, enticing me to
Look on down and in, as
A prick of fire begins to, twinkle
Amid this flagrant void

Twilight pulsing, moments
Pass by below me
Fire alights within these eyes
Staring on down and deep into this hole

Fire of this Phoenix birth
Spreading wings and soaring up, reaching
up and among the cool night’s, air
Burning away fiery darkness

Standing above this little hole, siren eye’s
Enticing me to, dive into it’s depths, to
Swim amid it’s warm and,
Welcoming ecstasies

Grasping onto this Phoenix, siren eyes
Tempting me
I fall into this hole, letting
Numbing fires lick at me

Melt away, the flames
As weeping darkness, overwhelms
Absolving this fighting Phoenix down into, The
Sea where siren’s lie

Standing below this little hole, crucifying me, as I
Look on up and out, as
Ebony walls assail
Leaving me
