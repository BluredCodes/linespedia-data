Swallowing the chains
Linked from within you

Twisting and turning
Gasping for breath

Choaking softly
Eye's glazing over

Falling into you
Holding on to things

Long since cold
As I bite pure steel

Falling
Holding onto what isn't there

Choaking
Breathing air not there

Swallowing these chains
Broken off from within you

Twisting and turning
Gasping for breath

And I'm reachin out to you
As I fall

Deeper into you
And I'm reachin out
