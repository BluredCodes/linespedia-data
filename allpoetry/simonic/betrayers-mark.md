title: "Betrayer's Mark"
writer: "Simonic"

The mark
Upon your neck
Is the mark of
The betrayed

I tried
So hard
To remain with you
Yet, you
Sought your pleasure

From the
Pleasure’s Will
His will
Has been made

Upon
Your neck
Your sweet soft neck

Now I
Am alone
In gazing at it’s tone
Tones of red
Of your blood
Parts of blue
Of my mood

It’s form
Formed on you
Formed by he
Who left his will

The will
Of the
Pleasure seeking
Oblivious

Now leave me
For his will
And seek till you
See 
That I
Was the one

Was the one
Who was meant to be
To follow your will
Not my will
Yet, now you left that too

Don’t turn back
It’s over
You’ve been marked by he

The will
Of the blind
Hurts those who can
Truly see

Don’t mind me
I’ll leave thee be
I know my
Will, was not to be

Goodbye now
Wish you well now
Go find your
Desire for pleasure
From the will’s
