---
title: "In Thy Sleep"
writer: "Simonic"
slug: "in-thy-sleep"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/3787-In-Thy-Sleep-by-Simonic"
---

Searching through bushes
Bushes of torment
Covered with dust
Long forgotten
The dust that chokes
In thy sleep

In the mind
It chokes
In the eyes
It releases
In the heart
It wretches

Webs fall upon the ground
Upon the dust the web of lies
Strung about this room
Long forgotten
The lies that blind
In thy sleep

In the mind
It blinds
In the eyes
It reflects
In the heart
It mars

Patches upon the ground
Sewn in by thy hand
To hide that beneath
Long forgotten
The patches that hurt
In thy sleep

In the mind
It hurts
In the eyes
It burns
In the heart
It kills


Walls that tower above thy head
Hold pictures of what could be
Never will they be for they are
Long forgotten
The pictures that cry
In thy sleep

In the mind
It cries
In the eyes
It wants
In the heart
It fades

Front of the wall
It shines for all to see
Never will they know
Long forgotten
That behind the wall dies
In thy sleep

In the mind
It dies
In the eyes
It dies
In the heart
It dies

The wall has fallen
Fallen upon the patches
Upon the dust and webs
Long forgotten
Another life lost
In thy sleep
