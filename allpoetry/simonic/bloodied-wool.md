---
title: "Bloodied Wool"
writer: "Simonic"
slug: "bloodied-wool"
source: "AllPoetry"

Wide eyed
Looking for a clue
Hidden within these lies
Handed down
Through masqueraded smiles
Preformed by the masters
Of the hills

Mouths of deceit
Utter truths in their own tongues
Shepherd to the sheep
Who play to their every sound
Indescribable ignorance
Failing to realize 
What stupidity really is

Dismiss their calls
And dismiss their actions
Follow what you see
In the distance
Crawl on knees
Or on your bare belly
Do whatever's needed to stay free

Call on others
With eyes propped open
Walk around with mouths dropped open
See the world for what it really is
Watch those masters wear sheep skins
While they show their shepherd smiles
And gape at the white wooled flocks

Failing to realize
That visions and color
Only stimulate the mind
Unable to hear only listen
Exposing falsehoods
The parasite of lies
Infecting an infected world

Deny them what they want to hear
Fade the showy colors to black and white
Stand against what they tell all
As they send the flock to do their jobs
While the shepherds lean against that tall oak
Rest now with mouth agape
As drooling sheep prance in joyous exultation

A sickness among the sheep
Kills from blind devotion to their shepherds
Coughing up blood and calling for their master
They begin to see the shepherd before them
How they decorate themselves with skins of the flock
Choking on their blood, gasping for air
They finally see as the disease claims them for eternity

Quickly the shepherd tosses the corpse for none to see or questionWhen I started it, this wasn't where I really wanted to take it ;P  But, it works, heh.
