---
title: "Sinah"
writer: "Simonic"
slug: "sinah"
source: "AllPoetry"

Take me now
Roll me over
Bring me back again, dear
Sinah

While the fire cools
Rip these thorns out
Watch me bleed, my dear
Sinah

Take my hand
Spin me around
Once again we’ll fall, dear
Sinah

We’ve dipped
Into a swirl of blood
Choking now, my dear
Sinah

Soaked in blood
You take hold of me
A lifeless dance, dear
Sinah

Desire’s bled
Life’s dripping out
Slowly dying, my dear
Sinah

Hold me close
Let me taste you
Live off your blood, dear
Sinah

Take me, again
Roll me over, again
Bring me back, my dear
Sinah
