Fell into this Spiral,
Darkness as my, dwelling.

Fading dreams giving,
Nothing of their promises.

Laughing darkness, holds me,
Coaxing me further down.

Blood is all around me,
Losing more, but getting none.

I'm falling in this, Spiral,
Holding out, holding on.

Screaming fills this darkness,
As chilled hands, touch my life.

Laughing at me, Insanity,
I'm weeping in this darkness,

Letting, tears of my future,
Reach the brim of this frigid void.

I'm falling in this, Darkness,
Holding out, holding on.

Calling to me, Sanity,
To deaf and blind and hopeless.

Giving comforts, comfortless,
Taking in the given, choking on it's opulence.

Can you save me, Spiral?
Tarry within your dark hole.

I'm falling in this, Spiral,
Holding out, holding on.

Clutch me please, Darkness.
Rip these veins that make me breathe.

I'm dying in this, Spiral,
It's Darkness overwhelming.

I'm dying in this, Darkness.
The twisting Spiral's fading fast.

Can I breathe one last breath?
Before this Darkness drowns me.

Claim me now, Spiral?
