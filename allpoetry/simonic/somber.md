---
title: "Somber"
writer: "Simonic"
slug: "somber"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/23641-Somber-by-Simonic"
---

Calmly standing here
Always holding back
All these things I feel for you
Never letting in

Cursing at my own reflection
Placed within this blackened mirror
Shifting and taking
My own hidden desires

To be,
With you once again and,
To be,
Standing here next to you

Free me of this armor
Arrow pierce it’s rustic gleam
Breaking this reflection
Spat at me through broken mirrors

Can I hold you darling?
Clutch to bleed, broken fragments
Sad reflection’s shattered
Dancing somberly amid these broken pieces, only 

To be,
With you once again and,
To grow,
Inside this broken fantasy and,
To love,
All this pain you put me in and,
To die,
Just so I can hold you one breath longer, so I may

Die! With you in my arms
Don’t leave me here, dear
Staring at this bent and broken reflection
Dissipating with every breath
