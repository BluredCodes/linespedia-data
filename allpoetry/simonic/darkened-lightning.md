Slowly the storm approached 
Darkening fields of flowers 
Beneath it's shifting mass 
Drowning the life 

We didn't see it coming 
From the south it hailed 
Calling on demons within 
To spawn from valued organs 

The light faded from the sky 
As the rolling clouds covered 
Converging us all within them 
My love, please breath 

The lightning cracked 
The machines howled 
That fearful pulsing 
Now a slow drawl 

My love, hold onto that breath 
That last breath 
Till we meet again 
Upon the gates to greater places 

Now, the storm still rages on 
Burning my eyes with tears 
A vow I make to you, my love 
Till we meet again
