Flickering flames, crawled
Madly about exposed flesh
As I writhed, breathing
Hard, as one does far beyond exhaustion

Tilting my head up, like a dying dog
I saw his eyes, full of flame
A demon of my fate, letting
Fire course through my screaming blood

Looking into his blazing coals
A laugh erupted from within his inhuman throat
Spewing forth that vacant merciless laugh
That rent my entire body with agonizing flames, causing

Fiery blood to flow from where sweat once had dripped
Sending my brain spinning madly within it’s case;
My mind seethed, turning violently over itself,
Groaning, as fire singed the edges of my precious blue irises

Time seemed to end, as the horizon
Of eternity rode an obsidian black chariot, amid a blazing crimson sky
As ashen air choked and burned,
Crumbling lungs and heart

Searching through a tortured mind, seeking
For an incantation, long since drained away
Amid this pool of blood, as my own
Life’s essence revolts against it’s master, murdering him

Gasping wildly for taintless air, fire again fills
Charred and blazing lungs, coughing up blood
As the demon’s laughter wove another spell
Leaving my mutilated body cold and numb

The pain fled, and I was freed
Yet, my eyes affixed upon the obsidian chariot
As it melted into a spiral of darkness, crude as death itself
And from the high heights of the crimson sky, it spiraled down

And with deadly grace it rolled about itself, as
The air before it seemed to tremble with fear, approaching my
Numb body, as I starred up stupefied, being
Engulfed and claimed by the spiral of darkness

And as the darkness enshrouded me wholly
Light ceased to be, and so I stared at the,
Nothingness of death, and sighed as I spoke,
