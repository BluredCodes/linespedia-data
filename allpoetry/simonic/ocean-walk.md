---
title: "Ocean Walk"
writer: "Simonic"
slug: "ocean-walk"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I tread slowly about the shallow waters
Glancing once to the depths which I left
The people still blindly swim
Swim within the depths of the water

Some float lifelessly
Drowned by it’s dark pressure
Some laugh jubilantly
Next to others following suit
Some are just floating
Unaware of where they are

I glance at my waters
The solid ground beneath my feet
I try to call to them
To tell them it’s better on the solid beach
None hear

For the waves of the depths crash over them
For the laughs of the mindless fill the air
For the screams of the needy pierce their ears

They are of the deep water
I am of the shallow
I am on solid ground
They are on moving uncertainty
Yet, they know

They know the dangers
They know the consequences
They know their pleasures

Soon, they will know their deaths
Soon, they will become too weak for the waters
And on that day, they will be too weak to swim into the shore
On that day, I will already be there

Cry for help they will
Cry for someone to save them
Cry for strength

All will be denied
For they knew
And they chose

Walk upon the calm beach
Not the submerging depths.
