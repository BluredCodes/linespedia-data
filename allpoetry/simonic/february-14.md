February 14
The year does not matter
For to this day and I
They are all the same

The only company that I receive on this day
Is she, February 14
She serves as an understanding friend
Yet, always likes to remind me of my loneliness

It's hard to understand why she hurts me so
But I know she is telling the truth
The dreadful truth
Which over our anniversaries, I have come to accept

I'll spend this day again
With February 14
And together we'll sleep
