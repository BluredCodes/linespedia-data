O=============================================================O﻿

﻿Kylo mar Minshar


Minshar kio`n ast seno
Palé sen mori Kwar’zah

Minshar! Minshar! Minshar mar Kelah!
Zolek mar kros, pitha se Kros

Sentur Zolek, sen yol’eh ku atha sen seri
Te ses e’mala`mo, whe Krus sien’u

Minshar! Minshar! Minshar mar athé!
Zolek mar kros, mé sien’Krus dia ky

Sa! Sa! Sa, sentur Minshar, maí moya’kalah
Ene sé Koi’on lacid mar ky sagro

Minshar! Minshar! Minshar holo kuno!
Zolek mar kros!

Minshar! Minshar! Minshar shé kuno!
Se, bomo’pusa, eama shé setíah, sí sh’ion’thala

S’woh pusa en yi’el sh’ion eama se Patha’nos

O=============================================================O

﻿Lament of Minshar


Minshar god among men(the earth)
Son and strength of Immortal blood

Minshar! Minshar! Minshar of Kelah(city?)!
Warrior of flames, servant of the Flame(Cilanik)

Great warrior and friend to man and elf
It was a sad day, when Sagrim(Death) met you

Minshar! Minshar! Minshar of men!
Warrior of flames, who met Sagrim(death) at night/in the dark

Oh! Oh!  Oh, great Minshar, why did you have to leave
Upon the(that?) Fateful spear of darkening death

Minshar! Minshar! Minshar our love!
Warrior of flames!

Minshar! Minshar! Minshar my love!
Oh, rest now, amid my breasts, as I weep forevermore

Dwell now in peace forever amid the Ancient Halls
