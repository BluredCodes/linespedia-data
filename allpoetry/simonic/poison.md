---
title: "Poison"
writer: "Simonic"
slug: "poison"
source: "AllPoetry"

Drink the sweet poison
Feel it’s warm touch
As it gently spreads
Throughout your life

The first taste is sweet
Yet, it infects
Throughout the body
It wretches the mind and heart

It’s effects are not physical
But limits thoughts
And obscures emotions
Thoughts of love and denial

The poison does not kill
Yet, the effects are deadly
It instills an addiction
An addiction for one more taste

The taste of it’s sweetness
The false taste of joy
As the pain racks the body
Granting mind to the oblivious

Oblivious of the poison’s nature
The nature of the taste
The nature of the pain
The pain within

This poison 
Is she
Who poisons
Poisons me

She is my poison
That I drink whole
Which I am ill with
Yet, I am denied to die
