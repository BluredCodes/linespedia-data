---
title: "Eye's Desire"
writer: "Simonic"
slug: "eyes-desire"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/3865-Eyes-Desire-by-Simonic"
---

Our eyes seek
What they desire
Yet, nothing fills their craving
The craving of desire

Through lands crossed
Eyes seek pleasing things
Things that please their eyes
Yet, never are they satisfied

Nothing of this earth
Will appease their desire
Nothing of this earth
Will remove that desire

They are the seeking eyes
Which seek what they desire
For what they desire becomes the
Eye’s desire
