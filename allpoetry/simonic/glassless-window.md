---
title: "Glassless Window"
writer: "Simonic"

Sitting, quietly within a window
With sweetening smiles spreading across
Silken lips, as dark hair sways
Within this unseen wind

Letting, these latent eyes stare
Into this glassless window, finding
Your image formed within, gently impressing
True beauty through a calloused mind

Staring, into eyes that know not
What I have come to hold for them
As they look off and beyond
Through this glassless window

Leaving, me to dream of dreams
Of touching tender flesh, caressing
Over a smooth and enticing neck
Feeling this ache within

Dreaming, over what I cannot have
As I stroke this glassless window
Staring into the eyes that know not
As a smile spreads across your lips, again

Smiling, as I watch you through this window
Beholding your beauty with silent ease, wondering
If I could ever step into this window, to
Hold your image within, to feel your warmth

Sighing, as a smile falls upon these lips
Admiring your sweet and tender words, oh
Can I hold you for a moment, to feel
A part of you, within and throughout

Trying, to find my way through all these aches
As I struggle to reach into the window, grasping
To touch and to hold, a warm soul within these cooled
And scarred hands, pulling you closer into me, slowly

Fading, into the night as lights
Slowly dim, taking the beauty within
Away from my silent gaze, and so I rest
