---
title: "Cell of the Oblivious"
writer: "Simonic"
slug: "cell-of-the-oblivious"
source: "AllPoetry"

Life of the dark
Denies the soothing light
Repressing feelings within
To appease the dark desires

Desires that cloud
Cloud the mind
Coating the eyes 
With the dark veil of death

A life falls
Into the vortex of the oblivious
Seeking only desires
Which kill

The dark desires of pleasures
Swirling in all their deadly glory
Among the endless walls
In this cell of the oblivious

The mind denies
That which is true
Living in pleasureful death
Till they too

Join the ranks of the
Oblivious
Within the hole of
Oblivion
