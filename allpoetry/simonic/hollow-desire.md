Can you see through 
What I've laid here before you

Brought down from darkened peaks
Of falling tears and sinking hearts

Lamenting to ignorant ears
Grasping for hollow flesh

To please all these frigid desires
Seeking false warmth in self made lies

Will your touch thaw this frozen heart
And gentle fingers wipe away these tears

Will I find something in and through you
To give me hope in this seemingly hopeless life

As I bite into hollow flesh
Letting Pleasure's warmth feed into me

As nails rack my back
Letting Pleasure's pain sooth my mind

Life's driven me to the edge
Leaving me only the ignorant and hollow

Come, help a poor soul, feed
Upon your hollow pleasure

Life's denied me far too long
I've fallen and accepting, please

Help as I curse my forceful morals, I can't turn from
As I reach out for the hollow flesh

Please, thaw this heart
And wipe these tears

Take this pain away
If not just for a moment, let me live a lie

Come, my hollow Desire, let me feed upon your neck
Drinking your warm Pleasures dripping from marks

Made by passionate bites, seeking more
As you shred my back, seeking nothing

Please, thaw this chilled heart
And wipe away crimson tears

Let me sleep beside you,
