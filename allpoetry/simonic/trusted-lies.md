|-----TRUST-----|

Spun about me in this dark reality
Are the words you fed into my (sleeping mind
Dragging me through, (your) |equivocating truths|
Only to sink through |your created
Reality|

|You told me to believe in you-)
|You told me to do as you said)
|You told me to trust in you-)
(but) 
|NO!| |you had to lie
Trust..(in)..nothing but lies
(trust)|IN YOU|...
(mmm, no...)

Built about you are the walls of your own deception
Waiting and (burning) for your own ﻿deliverance
Unable to see the finger pointed at you
Failing to realize that your own reality is
Cascading...(down into it's truth)

Fallen trust..(lies)..I see you now
|(I see it all so clearly

(now)

|You told me to believe in you-)
|You told me to do as you said)
|You told me to trust in you-)
(but) 
|NO!| |you had to lie
Trust..(in)..nothing but lies
(trust)|IN YOU---|...

(mmm, no...)

|And |YOU| fall at the dawn of reality-|
|Eye's dripping blood|...(but you see)
(you see)

(now...you see---)

(lies)

(no more trust)
|no (more trust)
|no more (trust)
|NO MORE...TRUST----|Ok, well.  This is totally open for comments and suggestions.  This is a song I wrote.  Now, the symbols in it do mean something:
| = louder/gruffer, if without a closing, just first is loud
( = softer/smoother, if without a closing, just first is soft
[] = deciding which sounds better or flows better
--- = about how long the word/note is carried
