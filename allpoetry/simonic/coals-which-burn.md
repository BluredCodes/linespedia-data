I’m sick of you
I no longer want to be with you
You have driven me
To my edge

You have turned
From sweet love
To bitter hate
You hurt far too many

No longer will I shelter
Shelter your problems
I have spit you out
No longer will I aid

You turned
From that which had helped
That which had cared
Now set your feet upon the coals

Coals which burn
Your life will burn
Burn because no help
Will be given to you

One tried
Tried many times
Picked you up off the coals
And carried you in gentle arms

Now you leave
Leave what was good
For what is bad
Burn upon the coals

The hate swells
For why could you have left?
WHY!
No longer does it matter

You have left
Betrayed what was there
For you
And only you

Burn upon the coals
Turn and look
The one which helped
Is not with you any longer

You have left the one
Turned your back 
Now, tread lightly
For you are the only one

You are the only one
That cares
For you left to
Burn furiously upon the coalsSome may say that this wasn't too smooth.  Yes, I know this too.  It is supposed to give the 'feeling' of anger, growling precise details again, and then restating them again.  Making sure, in your head, it all fits.  Now, some people like Kevin may want to know why and such, but that's not why I wrote the poem ;P  I know why, and it's a release for me, but it had something to do w/ the girl I loved, and another guy(no she did not cheat though). That may help in the understanding the 'why' I want them to burn.
