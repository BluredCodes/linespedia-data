O=============================O
"Star light, star bright
First star I see tonight
I wish I may, I wish I might
Have the wish I wish tonight"
O=============================O

Gentle stars, sparkling
Amid a darkened sky
Sprinkling down, this cool
Night's twilight

Dear, can you hear all
My hopes and dreams, as I
Stare up into the eye's
Of the cool Night's sky

Feeling their warm and hopeful glow
Softly caress these dark and hollow eyes
Feeling crystal tears glide over
Smoothened cheeks, stinging bliss

You were the first star, I saw
During the sinking twilight
Dear, please grant me this wish tonight
As we dance amid the glowing Night's sky

Dear, Time is short, we must be quick
Let us fly, far beyond what we know
Plunge deep within the beating hearts, before
The rising twilight

Dear, please hurry
Come now, our time is short
Dance a moment longer, before the coming twilight
Peaks over the shadowy hilltops

Please, if I may, I beg that I might
Have this wish with you, this night
As we dance amid the sparkling Night's sky
While crystal tears, drop frozen below

Dear, Time, has once again prevailed
As the Morning's twilight is welcomed by
Watery eyes opening, as I finish
Collecting the fallen tears

But tonight, dear, as the sinking twilight
Approaches once again, I'll be there
Staring up into your beautiful eyes
Waiting for your hand, to lift me up once again
