Bring Me the cauldron
Burning hot with soiled
Tears, dripping slowly
From plebeian cheeks

Let us step aside, while the
Fiery furnace cools, becoming
Black once again, as soot forms
Upon the edges of it’s dark hate

Seething within the cauldron
Tears boil and shift, calling out
To the cool air to bring them
Hope, till they bubble and fade 

Back down within the boiling
Cauldron, begetting nothing of
Their plight, letting their momentary
Existence fade back within, boiling tears

This is the life, of many as they
Seethe within their own desires and
Hopes, and call out to the one who
Brought them here, trying to save them

From this momentary existence, only
To drown within their own soiled
Kind, tears, shifting and bubbling
