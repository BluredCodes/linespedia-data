---
title: "This Curse"
writer: "Simonic"
slug: "this-curse"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/12745-This-Curse-by-Simonic"
---

I curse myself
For holding these
Accursed feelings
Of wanting you

Unsure of things
That I could do
Failing to grasp
It's reality

I curse you
For dragging me
Away from 
My own reality

False in the lures
Of the light
Draining into a
Darkened vortex

Failing to see
Unable to give
Denied of the world
I rested within

I curse myself
For you
Denying myself
For the curse
