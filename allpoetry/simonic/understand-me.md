---
title: "Understand Me"
writer: "Simonic"
slug: "understand-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/24570-Understand-Me-by-Simonic"
---

And I wish away
This mortality, fleeting
These dispositions that have caused me
So much pain

Understand that it was I, who
Let you know, who
Brought you down this path, too
Painfully close to me, but

Will you understand me,
As mortal blood drips from these veins
Will you understand me,
As I embrace, my dramatized immortality, and
Will you understand me,
When I say, I choose to see my assured
Divinity

The rain, it’s beating hard, not
The life I chose, and
The clouds have grown darker, not
Just above this head

I gave you clues, that
Whispered into your mind, and
All these smiles, to
Help you comprehend, but

Will you understand me,
If I tell you now
Will you understand me,
If this mortality just wasn’t for me
Will you understand me,
If I choose to die, and embrace my assured
Immortality

And as my blood drips from these veins, things
Are getting brighter, and they’re
Getting better, and they’re
Telling me that, now
In my divinity
That I, will
Always
