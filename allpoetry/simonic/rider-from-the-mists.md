---
title: "Rider from the Mists"
writer: "Simonic"
slug: "rider-from-the-mists"

From the mists you strode,
As if gliding upon a gleaming steed.
Breathing in the chilled morning’s air
Exhaling it in a flume of your sweetened
Vitality, gazing down, brandishing a smile
Upon silk wrought lips, soothing this soul.

It’s here where our souls met, and 
It was here where our souls connected.
As I stared into those dark shimmering
Pools, holding the pain and sorrow only 
Known to those who are forced to reach out 
And touch it’s cold surface, freezing the soul.

Those dark eyes called to me, beckoning me
With such a fervent call, I cannot begin
To describe, nor even fathom something
Similar.  For as I held your gaze my mind
Ached with pain.  The pain to know, to
Listen, and to understand, freeing your soul.

Still you gazed at me, uttering not a word.
Oh, how I ached to hold you, to set the
Dark waters at ease, calming the frigid storm
Within. You knew what I felt, you understood as
You lowered your gaze to the grassy floor.  Leaving
Me to stare at glistening hair, desiring a soul.

And upon the grass I saw a crystal
Tear fall, floating down to the floor, as
A feather does in a soft breeze.  Unable
To hold myself a moan escaped my
Lips, as my aching cried within.  Reaching
Out to touch and lift up, a defeated soul.

As my fingertips touched your creamy
Skin, wet with tears, your head moved into
It, as if the soft touch wasn’t enough
Reeling inside, I lifted up your head and
Looked once again into the dark pools,
Gently glistening from, a frightened soul.

Slowly another crystal tear began to
Slide down your creamy skin, and as it
Glided over your skin I gently removed
It with my thumb, continuing to hold you
Within my understanding eyes, admiring
True beauty deep within, a troubled soul.

Now I could feel your desire, for it
Was the same within me.  To hold onto
One who truly understands, and cares.  To
Be held within arms, encircled by their caring
Protection, granting a shoulder to release
All the pains upon.  Finding, a caring soul.

Gently I wrapped my arms about you
Holding you close to me, and with amazing
Force you clutched onto my back, pulling
Me closer and released all your pains and sorrows
Within, upon my caring shoulder.  Releasing even
Things hidden.  Finally finding, an understanding soul.

Slowly the sun rose and fell during the
Embrace.  As the mists left, leaving the
Fresh and soothing air in its wake.  And as
You lifted your head from my shoulder, I
Smiled upon you as I gently wiped
The crystal tears away letting, a soul heal.

And so again we stood, staring at one
Another, souls connected, sharing dreams and
Thoughts without words.  Relishing the
Company of understanding silence from
One who cares.  Smiling upon each other
Knowing that we are, now healed souls.Well...heh.
