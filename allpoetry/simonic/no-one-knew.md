---
title: "No One Knew"
writer: "Simonic"
slug: "no-one-knew"

Across the skies they soared
Spotless record ten years counting
Nothing new
No one knew

A moment stretched
Beyond a moment
A realization stretched
Beyond realizations

A working day
A waking moment
Hearts skipped a beat
Yawning faces horrified

The moment passed
Fires burned
Rescues on the way
Life to return

A second moment
No one knew
No one believed
Ignorance mended

Moment extended
Fires raged
Lives shattered
Mouths agape

Rescue sent
All to be well
Life to return
To normal

Moment gasping
Structure failing
Fires roared
Dust sucked in

Falling through
Floor through floor
A seeker of death
A taker of life

Cries of the loved
Fires screamed
Prayers to gods
Roofs fell

Tears fell upon
Smoldering grounds
Enthralled by sights
Things that could not be

The moment choked
The buildings fell
The lives crushed
Fires laughed

This couldn’t happen
Not here
Nothing new
No one knew
