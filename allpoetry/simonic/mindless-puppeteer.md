{Girls voice}
"What's your favorite color?"
"What's your favorite song?"
"Do you like chocolate?"
......{male cough 2x}.....
"What kind of chocolate?"
{End girls voice}

|Ahhhhhhhh-------------!|


(Questions..are..really meant to discover)
(Drawling out mindless dribble- on|ly- becomes)
(|Pain at [the][your] fingertips [if used "the": of your words]
Questioning to discover all these...
Mindless- answers-

(Are you satistfied yet?
(|Did I say- what you wanted[(me to)]?
(Are you happy yet?
My mindless puppeteer---
{female giggle and saying "Dance[for me]!}

{Change of beat/tempo, louder.  To give those poor guys who like to 
mosh something to mosh to.}

|Ahhhhhhhh-------------!|

|Still playin on [your] puppet strings|

|Dancin to your- |stupid gestures|

|Failing to rid..|myself||of||choking||wo--rds|


{beat/tempo slow down to start}

(|Burning(acid) in my throat)
(Choking to spit)..(-at you)

(spit|


((spit at you))

(Are you satistfied yet?
Did I say- what you wanted[me to]?
(Am I pleasing you yet?
My mindless puppeteer---
{female questioning voice "Baby?"}

|I'm cutting [these]/[your] strings!|
|And I'm spitting at you!|

|Don't waste my time!|
|Mindless puppeteers!|


|Mindless puppeteer-----!|


|Don't waste my time|)

((Are you satisfied?))Key: 
| = louder/gruffer, if without a closing, just first is loud 
( = softer/smoother, if without a closing, just first is soft 
[] = debating if word should be in there 
--- = about how long the word/note is carried 
. = pause 
{} = Other info on the area of the song

This could be placed in society, and yes it does work both ways.  Guys can ask stupid questions...hell often times do, damn flirts givin us bad names ;P  And, I'm sure some of you ladies feel just as annoyed by such questions as I(and other guys) do.  

Again, any comment/suggestions are greatly welcome.  I want to improve the lyrics in any way I can.  Thanks.
