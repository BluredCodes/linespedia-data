---
title: "Thought of You"
writer: "Simonic"

When I met you
I thought of you
Of your smile
And hair

When I spoke with you
I thought of you
Of your gentle words
Mixed with laughter

When I looked at you
I thought of you
Of your simple features
True beauty

When I drove with you
I thought of you
Of how I had lost my chance
To be with you

When I said goodbye to you
I thought of you
Of how we would have been
In love.
