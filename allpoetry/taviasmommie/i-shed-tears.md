title: "I shed tears"

At night aloneI shed tears.Tears that stem fromconfusionheartbreak and painI never knew where they came fromor even if I was heardbut I shed tearsI asked the question'Why Me?' and every thinkable one after but none the lessI shed tearsSometimes I wonder will I ever be able to stop crying and will the tears ever be for a reasonbut aamongthe questions as the days go byI shed tears
