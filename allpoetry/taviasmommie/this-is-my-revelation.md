This is my revelation
my quest for truth
something deep in me that wonders when I look at you
My revelation for truth 
comes stemmed by my pride
my preference 
and even my choice to believe "the lies"
I neglected you,
my bad
I was traced back to blood.
I study the actions
and knowledge you love.
The persistence,
the letters
the unlimited trips for conversion,
I thought I was messed up, 
my mind so confused,
you of all people with so much to lose.
This is my revelation 
my quest to uphold my pride
my preference was not you 
and inside you died.
My revelation is reality
my only choice is to break it down,
the key word was "it" not you,
so don't clown
let's take it element 
by element 
one logic at a time,
there is a way to approach me 
a place 
and a time 
Let's act like women
physically 
we are 
and there really is no need 
to take it that far
Is this a step up in womanhood 
or a step back to being a child 
I really don't want to take it that far,
I don't have time for it now.
So this is my revelation
no time 
to try and understand 
to take all this shit off my hands.
