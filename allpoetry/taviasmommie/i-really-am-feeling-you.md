I really am feeling you right now and I know you are feeling me.I think that you have figured out you are in my deepest fantasy.The one where I am just being me,you know the freak I don't expose.The one where I let you have your way, from my head down to my toes.I really am feeling you right now and I know that you can tell.My body just has a certain response, one that makes me begin to melt.One that I only experience with you and damn I just can't lie,everytime that I get near you my body tends to cry.I guess it may go deeper than sex but if it does then I can't tell.Cause everytime that I come near you my feelings begin to swell.I guess you know I am feeling you, as if you did not know before.I only wanted to let you know, what all the juices were for!
