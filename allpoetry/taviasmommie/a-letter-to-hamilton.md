title: "A letter to Hamilton"

Dear Hamilton,
I express my love for you in my writing
all the seen and unseen feelings that shoot through me.
I express my caring through my voice
all I say and all I hesitate to say.
I express to you my feelings,
all my wants desires and my needs.
I express to you my passions all you know of and you don't know of.
Hamilton,
I guess my letters to you can not begin to go as deep as my love and my depth is greater than any ocean and wider than any sky.
My feelings for you are stronger than any man and are harder than all stone.
I need you to know that my letter may be our last contact 
and maybe the begining of many but however this letter may lead us..
Hamilton,  I love you...
With all my love and my true passion...me!

This was not a poem so to speak but an expression of feelings that I am having today about...well let's just say Hamilton..I don't think he wants his business out there like that!
