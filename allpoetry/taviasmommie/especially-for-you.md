title: "especially for you"
writer: "taviasmommie"

It's raining outside,but it is also raining inside.

Inside of my soul,I am falling apart.

I know that you know because you can see it in my eyes

and everytime I tell you nothing you won't let it ride. 

I think that you know me so you know when I hurt.

Why can't you understand exactly what my heart is worth.

What the cost of your words mean to me.

how much it is valued in my soul.

How heavy it is weighed and why it is not under sold.

I give you my greatest treasure my heart, undoubtedly yours,

I give you my love and watch it unfold.

I watch  you take my heart and my feelings and I see you from a far,

unknowingly you ripe, tear and spread them apart.

I watch you take a peice of my soul the one that I gave to you,

I watch you erase the things you say and do.

But you remember all of my downfalls,

the many mistakes I've made. 

And  I hear you say you love me,

but after watching you it just is not the same
