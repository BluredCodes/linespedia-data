Long ago,
I can't really say when
I found myself in love again.
I kept telling myself to slow down
but yet it happened and I don't know how
I went back and forth
trying to figure if you were the one 
and before I knew it the love had begun
Ever since that moment 
I haven't had any doubt 
that a love like ours 
I can't do without.
I do become afraid
and scared at times
but what my heart can't handle 
is all in my mind
Long ago I found someone true
long ago my heart found you.

This is for my girl Vee and her man James....they really need this right now!
