---
title: "I miss you most when I am near you"
writer: "taviasmommie"
slug: "i-miss-you-most-when-i-am-near-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/13523-I-miss-you-most-when-I-am-near-you-by-taviasmommie"
---

When we are together,just you and I 
when we are touching looking in to eachother's eyes
when we are kissing
when we are gliding on air
when we are touching
when we are both there
when we are dreaming side by side
when we are laughing
when we cry 
when we just sit
when we talk
when we argue
when we make up
when we are one
and when we seperate,
when we are together,
I miss you most.
