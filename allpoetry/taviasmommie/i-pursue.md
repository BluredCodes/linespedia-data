title: "I pursue"

I purse to make you happy
so I challenge and change myself
I purse to be what you want
From the depths within one,
I will never understand
why I conform to your interest
your desires
After all these years of becoming me
I made her retire.
I still have not yet become one with myself 
I am not about to give that away for you
I am going to be me for me and
you be you for me!
