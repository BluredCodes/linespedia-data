She's gone..
you know the girl you met last year...
the one that did not laugh 
or ever shed a tear
Some one 
saw her leave one night 
they did not know where she was going 
or even where she'd went 
all they said was she left that night 
and she left without a fit.
They thought 
that she would always stay
they said 
on them she'd rely
they never knew 
she would get the strength
she needed that July
Someone came 
looking for her today
and they didn't even try to explain
but the more they mumbled and stuttered their words
you knew that they felt shame
I thought 
I saw her downtown
she was smiling so I took a second look
she was smiling and glowing 
looking bright as a star
the sight had me stand and stare
Was this the girl who lived down the street,
the one who once lived down there?
She looked 
as though she was doing well for herself
like she had gone and grew alittle
but as I turned to continue my stroll
she looked and dropped her head
I think 
I startled her so I turned to leave instead
then I caught her glance as I walked by 
and I slowly drew my breathe
she touched my hand 
and dropped a tear and smiled 
and pushed ahead
