Hello 
I briefly start my day, 
to you that is the first thing that  I chose to say. 
Yet you persist in making conversation and it was evident before now that I did not need your salutation. 
Hello 
You continue to make your business known... 
and in the back of my mind I know I should have stayed home 
I am not trying to be mean but I don't feel like hearing this shit 
I mean once we leave here then the conversation is it. 
You won't speak to me any more 
not even make an attempt 
and now you are all in my face like we a joint at the hip 
Like we are best friends and we go way back when... 
when after today I won't see your ass again. 
In the back of my mind I am trying not to be rude... 
I am nodding and "yesing"" like I am listening to you fool! 
And I can't just keep on biting my tongue, 
cause the harder I bare down the more that shit hurts. 
Ok what do I do

 what do I say

 cause I got to get this work done, and I need to be straight.. 
The logic is that I only said hello 

but she initiated a conversation 

that I did not plan to take part of. 
I really don't care how she is doing...I was only speaking to be polite and here I am stuck in this conversation...wishing I could take flight 
I don't want to be rude  but if her other girls were around, 
this conversation would be the last one she'd have found. 
Like I care she has a paper due, hell I am in the same class 
and if the bitch ask me for my notes I might whip her ass.... 
you feel me, 
Naomi, 
Girl, 
NAOMI, do you hear me?! 
YEAH, DAMN SHUT THE FUCK UP!
