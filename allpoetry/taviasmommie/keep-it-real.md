title: "Keep it real"

No matter how hard I try
I can’t understand the idea
Of why half of these girls on this campus 
Aren’t real

They smile in your face 
And talk behind your back
I always thought 
It would not be like that 
I know at this school there are no guys 
So why do females look at me and roll their eyes

I am not one to call you out of your name
But if that’s how you act 
You are one in the same
With a female dog 
And all those other words
I know you know them 
Cause they have all been heard
I can’t understand why you don’t know me 
And yet you judge me 
And then walk around like you don’t like me
I hope you don’t think that you’re all that
Because as far as I can tell you aren’t doing all that well
What’s the cause for you to hate 
I know I’m not all that 
But I’m as real as they come 
And I thank my girls for that 
Stop trying to be fine 
Nobody is looking at your behind 
And the way people here act 
I have to watch out for mine

So when you see me
You don’t have to speak
Hell
I may not speak back 
As far as I am concerned 
I don’t even need you for that
But if you have never spoken to me 
And probably never will 
Keep your smart comments to yourself 
And let me keep it real.
