---
title: "Girl, what can I say??"
writer: "taviasmommie"

Girl what can I say,I never thought you would feel this way..I did not know you had all that strength.To actually go through with what you held with in.Man, fuck what all  them other people say.You had to live your life and do it your way.You had others to think about,and that is why you had to get out..So what if they think that you are wrong,if they were you, could they sing the same song..that you did?Girl what can I say,I am proud of you either way.I express to you in this now,that what ever you do,I am glad you did not stay,the stress was killing you,and you have to much to live for.Girl what can I say,I love you and damn Kem,WHAT ELSE CAN I SAY?
