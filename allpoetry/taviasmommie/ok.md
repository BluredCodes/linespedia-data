OK
you know 
I am laughing at you right,cause you are funny as hell...
You thought 
I was going to forget all of the shit you did to me,but it does not work like that so let me take you back
Back to the days of pain,
the days that I thought 
I'd one day wear your name.
Back to the times, that I thought you were mine.
I really want to take you back,
back to the times 
when we'd shower together,
me not knowing it was for you to know 
if I had been with another,
I really want you to go back with me,
back to the times that you cared for me and my child,
back to the times 
I thought you cared the whole while.
Backe to the times 
when you really did not give a damn.
Back to the times 
when you thought you could ruin my smile.
Guess what mother fucker..I don't need you now!!
OK!
