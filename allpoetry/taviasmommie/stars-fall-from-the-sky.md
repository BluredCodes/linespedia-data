Stars fall from the skyand Lamont landed with us,Stars fall from the skyand our lives were touchedWe watched you pull through a fight that you won,we listened to your heart as you smiled bright as the sun.Stars fall from the sky,and Lamont, you graced us here on earthLamont, you fought to stay here and you shined with all your might,and once your fight was over God let you take flight.Stars fall from the skyand you have found your place again, To look down on us and smile,until we meet with you again.This is Dedicated to Terrance Lamont Joyner who passed away on November 26, 2001!Rest in Peace
