Don’t scream in my ear,
Like I don’t already know your pain
I have screams of my own – muffled and unheard
But you want to cry on my shoulder 
And let me spit word.

My baby daddy in jail 
The man knew he was wrong
But right how he singing the jail bird song.
She don’t know he there,
So what am I suppose to do. 
I been raising her alone but that is another problem to.

Let me scream to you about bills 
My loans, my jobless self. 
I can’t be educated full- time 
And buy damn milk.
I can’t get welfare ‘cuz I am not even going that route
I guess there are things Momma got to do without.

Let me scream in your ear 
About females hating on me.
They walk by and look in my face and won’t even speak.
Females I don’t know and some of them I do 
And because I don’t act like them I just do what I have to do.
Females who don’t have a man but always hating on me.
My man may not look good to you but don’t worry he’s with me
Females who always hating, 
At an all girl school
Don’t you know I’ll hurt your feelings? 
And still act like I’m cool.
I don’t have to kiss your ass 
I am here to make the grades 
And as far as I am concerned ya’ll females can stay detained.

Don’t scream in my ear, cause I don’t want to hear it
As long as I got problems I am not trying to feel it.

Let me scream to you about my baby

She know her ABC’s and all
I am proud of her
She knows it 
And that is reason to scream at ya’ll

Let me tell you ‘bout my man

He dark, tall and fine 
He loves me from the depth of his soul
And makes up my other half of mine.

Let me tell you ‘bout my girls

They are faithful and they are few 
I can count on them not matter what it is 
That we have to do.
We are ready for whatever 
Always got each others back 
And it is not to many girls on this campus
I think I could deal with like that.

Now that is something to scream about!
