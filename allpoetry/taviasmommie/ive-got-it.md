---
title: "I've got it!"
writer: "taviasmommie"
slug: "ive-got-it"

Can't you tell it in my walk..
can't you tell it in my talk..
can't you tell it in my smile..
I make the sun stop for a while.
Can't you hear it in my voice..
I believe you have no choice..
but to only realize..
what you want takes time.
No this is not a joke 
and I have been one known not to play.
I guess you feel me now
and I can't let you have your way.
Can't you tell it in my eyes..
that you have a hell of a surprise.
I guess you never saw it coming,
or heard the drum roll drumming!
So what exactly do I want you to do,
I am not really going to even answer,
all you need to know is
what you searched the whole world for,
has always been right behind the same door.
What you have always thought someone else had,
I had all along and now its in high demand
You knew right from the start that my intentions were to steal your heart
and now that I got what you want,
my intentions are gone
and you are just fucked!
