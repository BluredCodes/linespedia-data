title: "Tomorrow has no hesitation"
writer: "taviasmommie"

Let's not take one another for granted because tomorrow is not promised.
Let's tell each other we love you because that is not a daily habit.
Let's smile at each other in passing because that just might make the day,
let's apologize for the wrong we have done, and even the things that have passed away.

Tomorrow is not promised and it maybe sad but it is true,
and before I let today go by,
you need to know I love you.
Tomorrow is not promised and before I close my eyes,
I want you to know I am sorry,
if I have hurt you at any time.
Tomorrow is not promised and I want to let you know 
that I cherish our every moment spent,
and  I wish there could be more.
Tomorrow is not promised and I only wanted to say,
I love you and you mean the world to me,
and I want to start on a clean slate.

So, Let's tell one another that the day is not promised
and let's give each other kind words
let's thank God for one another 
because we  all were sent from above
I'll allow you some time 
to decide to accept my invitation,
but remember that I love you anyway,
because unlike tomorrow
Forgiveness should be with out hesitation.
