as I walked in the rain,I glanced down at the puddle,
there was a reflection of me and it was like none other
I saw straight through that water.
to the eyes that were full of sorrow.
I looked straight through that water to the face that was so hurt.
I kept going to my destination and saw my reflection in the glass.
This time it did not look any better,it looked tired,
it looked sad.
I tried to pay it no mind and keep going on with my day,
but the more my day seemed to progress the reflections 
never would fade away.
 
A reflection of what was on the inside,but it seemed to just fall out.
A reflection of what I was trying to hide...of what I did not want to shout.
a reflection that I could read with every glance, a reflection full of pain...
A reflection that everytime I looked at 
I wanted to cry again.
