Why can't I have what the hell I want,everybody is telling me that is not true.
But you know and I know you know that what I want is you.
I am tired of those silly games all the strategies have got me shook
As long as you know I love you I guess my heart you took.

Why the fuck can't I get what I need, when what I need is you beside me?Maybe even not beside me 
maybe just you to be with me.
Why can't I get over you because obviously you don't care what I do
so is it that I need you or is it just the really fucked up things you do
that got me wondering what the hell am I suppose to do?
