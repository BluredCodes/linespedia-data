title: "Last night I think I died"

Last Night,
I waited by the phone,
I waited for your call.
I sat beside the window,
I looked beyond the glass
I watched the sun descend
I feel the hours pass

Last Night,
I needed to hear you,
I wanted to see your dark brown eyes,
the ones I love so much

Last Night,
After you came in,
I wish my breath had ceased,
I hoped that all my senses stopped
alone we lay,
side by side
as we have done before
and I just began to dream of you
and wish for
the night I had died before
