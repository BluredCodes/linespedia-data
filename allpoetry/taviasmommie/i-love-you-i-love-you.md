title: "I love you, I love you"

I love you, I love youdon't even pretend you didn't know.I know that you can seeit cause you make my eyes glow.I love you, I love youand I feel that you love mebut instead of you coming out and say it you feel you must be free.I love you, I love youthat is the song I sing,day in,day out wondering will my song bring you to me.I love you, I love youand after 2 years you still can't tellthat every time you touch my soul I hide the joy I feel. I love you, I love youso much and I don't think you can seethat every time I tell you that I don't say it to be funny.I love you, I love youand not to many people understand that it is not because I need you it's because want you for my man.I love you, I love youand I know that it is not lustbecause before I gave my heart to you,you had to win my trustI love you, I love youso this song I sing is for youI love you, I love youand I hope you love me too.
