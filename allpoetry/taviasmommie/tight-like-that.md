---
title: "Tight like that"
writer: "taviasmommie"
slug: "tight-like-that"
source: "AllPoetry"

You know girl, 
we have been through it all,
I remember the times I thought I would fall,
seems every time you were right there 
even the times I thought you didn't care.
Don't ask me why I wondered that you would not be there for me,
you are my sister and you are true to me indeed.
You have been more than my sister,
you were a critical and a friend
I knew I could count on you when my man did me in.
You would fight for and with me
whether I was right or wrong,
you would be right there when I could not stand tall.
You literally had my back at times when I could not hold myself on a wall.
I will never forget you asked me why when I walked 
I watched the ground.
Come on, now you know that no matter what you and I are connected..
so I don't understand why some of these fools be tripping 
when they see that we still kick it...
Yeah, we are not together physically 
but mentally we are tight as hell
and you know Brina, 
I don't care what it is 
I am there. 
I can always know that you have my back 
and Brina I love you cause your my sister 
and we are tight like that.

This one is for my best best best friend and my worst worst enemy...my big baby sister Sabrina!
