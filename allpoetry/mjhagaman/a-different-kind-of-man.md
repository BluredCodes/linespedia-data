title: "A Different Kind Of Man"
writer: "mjhagaman"

Rays of irreverence
Flow before my eyes
Cloudy dreams of yesterday
Conciousness on the rise

Integrity is a virtue
Although frowned upon by you
Criticizing me is easy
Because I'm something new
