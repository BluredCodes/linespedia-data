---
title: "Love Remembered"

Thinking of a river sweet
The cold earth beneath my feet
How many years have we known
All the passion the wind has blown

Im through with you, the moment's passed
My love is old, my soul is cashed
Remember me to your new thing
Happiness I hope he can bring
