Downward, Onward like spite in the wind
Forever undaunted by your intended sin
Carried away, my sweetest of dreams
You must not know how much they mean

Dangerous to you? It's dangerous to me
Too late to speak of what should be
Blades of disgust now come from your eyes
Oh! I hope that you can hear my cries

It's terrible, but I cannot go on this way
Sometimes, babe, we must meet the day2000,
