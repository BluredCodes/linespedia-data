Reluctant gestureswhen I see you are thereI am afraid ofbeing lascivious with youVague impressionsyou leave in my doorwaymy pain is ensconcedour love was ephemeraland attenuatedMy cumbersome desiresrendered presumptuousby your diminutive heartnow my heart grows dima futile sussurationin your ear
