title: "The Reasons For My Condition"

Anastasia
Like wildfire
Is burning up my heart
Has spread through the depths of my soul

Can't She feel the love
That has devoured me

Anastasia
Like a hairpiece
Is keeping me holding on
Has incarcerated my every thought

My picture hangs on the wall
Of her narrow heart

Anastasia
Like a disease
Is thoroughly consuming me
Has incapacitated my love
