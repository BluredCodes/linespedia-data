Anastasia like wildfireIs burning up my heartIf she only cared how I feelShe always hits me like a dartI am bleeding from my fingersI cannot find the ways of stabHer heart is wearing a coatI hope he's only a fadWhat can I offer a girlWho is contentWithout me?
