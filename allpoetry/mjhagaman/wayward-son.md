title: "Wayward Son"
writer: "mjhagaman"

I'm thinking of a distant way
How many time I've thought of you
Grotesque thoughts of my life
I'm now a useless piece of clay

She said that Rimbaud would come for me
By the light of moon in the woods
My fire has ashed, my dreams are dead
Release me from my cage, make me free

On and on, Forever it goes
I need something to live for
I am afraid of not knowing
I wish I would have taken a different road
