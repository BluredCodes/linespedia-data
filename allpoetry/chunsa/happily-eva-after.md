You came into my life
                      with out a reason why,
                        now you left me drowning
                         in my own tears.

                         You said that we would
                           Live happily eva after.
                            What happened?Are you so
                             convinsed of your own being
                              only caring about yourself,
                               Do you understand how i felt
                          
                      When you left me her alone in this
                        so called paradise, or whatever you
                         would call it? Is this what you like to
                          Do to people? Lead them on? Make them fall
                      For you? Not knowing what they are getting into?
                        Nothing you can do will make up for what you did.
                        Thanks for all the lies... In this so called Paradise!!!!
