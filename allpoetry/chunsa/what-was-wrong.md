From the first time I saw you, I knew youwere the one. Something inside told me I was in Love. I thought we felt the same, tel the day you walked away, how could this happen? What did I do wrong? If only I could read your mind, if only i knew what was wrong. I gave you my all and now your gone. Was it something I said? Was it something I did wrong? Now you've moved on. I cant explain how I feel, Heartbroken? Maybe, embarrassed? Yes. you stole my heart and now you are long gone!
