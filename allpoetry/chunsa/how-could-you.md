---
title: "How could you?"
writer: "ChUnSa"

source: "AllPoetry"

How could you make me fall in love, and then take my heart and run?
  After all I did for you I thought you were the one.  You were my shinning
star, I loved you with all my heart. How could you do the things you do I'm
so in love with you.
