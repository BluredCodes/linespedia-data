---
title: "Lies!!!!!"
writer: "ChUnSa"
slug: "lies"
source: "AllPoetry"

You said we
                 would be
                  forever
               and you would
                never make me 
                   cry!

                 You said we
                   belonged
                 together.You 
                 Left me to find
                 you only lied.

              I'm so confused what can i do?
                I'm sick of all the lies 
                  and all the times you
                     made me cry!

                  My minds made up.... I'm 
                       saying good bye!
                   It's time for this to end
                    Because I know our love 
                       we can never mend...         
                 Because I know our love we can never mend!
