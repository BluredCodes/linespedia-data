Where did you go? To Heaven?? Are you withthe Angels all dressed in white? are you some whereun mentioned are you some where out of sight? How couldyou just leave me all alone, I didn't get to say good bye, all i got to do was cry! The last time i seen you,you looked cold and white.. Where ever you are, you will always be in my heart. So take your time and get some rest, and when you wake up in themorning Be your best! watch over me and rest in peace and alwaysremember I'm there for you even if i only can talk to youin my dreams I just wanted you to know... GRANDMA, I love you!
