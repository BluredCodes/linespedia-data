---
title: "why me?"
writer: "ChUnSa"
slug: "why-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

From the first time I saw you I knew you were the one.You made me happy, kept a smile on face. You knew my feelingscould never be replaced.Know that your gone I don't know what to do orsay, tell me how I can make this feeling go away? I thought my feelingsfor you would fade, but have grown stronger everyday! Please tellme what is on your mind, I'll be there, I'll listen to you anytime.If I knew it would make you happy believe I would be there, to make all the bad thing disappear! But know that you are gone, out of sight, I don'tknow what to tell you I guess I have to say goodbye!!!
