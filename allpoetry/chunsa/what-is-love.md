---
title: "what is love?"
writer: "ChUnSa"
slug: "what-is-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

What is love? Is it the feeling you get when he
walks by? Is it a burning sensation that makes you cry?
Love is more the the look on my face, its what you cant see
and it cant be replaced. Its in my heart its in my soul, from
my head down to my toes!
