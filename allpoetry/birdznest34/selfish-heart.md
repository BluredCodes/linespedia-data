---
title: "Selfish Heart"
writer: "birdznest34"
slug: "selfish-heart"

Lost in the blackness of yesterday, 
I'm feeling your love slip away and I don't know what to do,
Asked you why you can't say much, 
Can't help feeling so out of touch,
And all I know is I love you

When you told me, that you wanted to be alone,
Like a King without a throne,
I just felt like I could die.
And I'm sorry, for whatever it is I've done,
All that you can do is run away from me baby,
Must be my selfish heart

Now there's a tear falling from my eye, 
All I want to do is lay down and die
And it isn't meant to be,
It's been too long that we've been apart,
I'm walking around without a heart
And I loved you faithfully,

When I saw you, walking around with that new gal,
Looking in her eyes with that loving glance
I knew that for me there was no chance
And I'm sorry, for whatever it is I've done
All that you can do is run away from me baby
Must be my selfish heart

Oh I thought that we were meant to be,
Forever is what you said to me, 
I loved you so much, I loved you so much
But now she has you by her side, 
I'm left with nothing, not even pride 
But you told me that's tough, tough

I really don't know what to do, 
I can't get over losing you, 
It hurts me so bad, 
It's making me mad
I thought that we were so together,
Never dreamed that you'd find someone better
It's making me sad, can't help feeling so sad

There is no one, who could ever be my goal
Who can wrap themselves around my heart and soul
And take me in their arms
There is no one, who I'd rather be with
Who could make me believe I had a right to live
Must be my selfish heart
