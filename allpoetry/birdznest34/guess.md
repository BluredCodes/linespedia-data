Silkyness caresses yourTightly fitted sheetsSoft to the touchTender pleatsLight and fluffyLike a cloud in the skyUp and down unwindingLike a kite trying to flyWhite ones, brown ones most kinds are goodSometimes quite nastyLike sandpaper on woodRolling, Rolling, pushing onLike feathers in my handsRubbing me gentlyLike waves upon the sandsSliding, swirling, going down, Quickly it is sinking,I'm talking about toilet paper,Now what were you thinking?
