Every time I see you, I just think I am blessedYou mean so much to me, our love will stand all testsCause I love you so much baby, your sweetness and your loveYou're the one I dream of, heaven sent you to me from the skies above Baby we were meant to be, it's the only thing I can seeYou and me together, forever and everBaby we were meant to be, me for you and you for meThere's nothing that can get in our wayYou're my eternal loveI never thought that I would findSomeone as good as youAll the other girls I've hadHave never been so trueYou're the reason that I wake upEvery day and get out of bedI long to see your beautiful smileA portrait of beauty inside my headYou're my world, my life my meaningYou're the only one I seeThat can come into my empty lifeFill it up with so much love, that makes me so happyI've known it all along, but I was just to scared to believeThat someone could make my world aliveAlone I just couldn't surviveBaby we were meant to be, it's the only thing I can seeYou and me together, forever and everBaby we were meant to be, me for you and you for meThere's nothing that can get in our wayYou're my eternal loveYou came down from heaven aboveYou're my eternal love
