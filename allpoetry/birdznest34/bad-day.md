Woke up this morning, sun blasting in my eyesForgot to pull the shade the night before,didn't realizeI feel so weak, Can't get out of this bedMy body is so sore, Feels like something hit me upside the headBut I give a good stretch, And I pull off the sheetsTry and get dressed and trip over my feetI just know, oh no, oh no,It's gonna be, a bad dayWalk in the kitchen, Mom's in a huffIt's 3pm and the house is so full of my stuff'You better clean your room, before you leave', she yellsBut I feel like I just got out of a tomb, feel like hellOpening the fridge, there's some oj leftTake a big swig and go back to my nestFlop out on the bed, massage my headI felt so good last night, now I'm feeling so deadHear mom's footsteps, she's coming upstairs,So I try and get up, and comb my messy, messy hair,ButI just know, oh no, oh no,It's gonna be....... a bad dayAnd when I look in the mirror,I see my bloodshot eyesAll it took was a beer or,A few vodkas to get me wiseLast night at the party,It was so fun I didn't realize,that today oh no, oh no,It's gonna be a ... bad daySomebody pinch me and wake me from this dreamMore like a nightmare, I hate this sceneSplash some water over my face,Come on wake up and get out of this placeThrow my clothes in the closetThrow stuff under the bedYeah it looks pretty clean now'Yeah I think it will fool her till later', I saidCause if I stay here much longerIt's gonna be a bad dayBad bad day
