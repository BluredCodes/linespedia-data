Shining into my windowVery pretty, soft hueBrightly lit,Pituresque glowBlinking,twinklingShimmering starsBut not the ones in the skyChristmas Lights in people's yardsLike candy for the eyeDraped over treesOn window panesIn bushes On the roofA Santa hereA snowman thereA lighted reindeer hoofLike light brite on houses,Sparkling on trees in the nightUp and down every streetIt's fantastic, what a sight
