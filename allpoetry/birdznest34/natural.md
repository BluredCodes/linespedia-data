Perfect bodyPerfect faceSo much make upIt's a disgraceAnd all the young girls think, that's what they should strive forBut most of those models on magazines use the air brush or moreToo much lipstickToo much creamThe stuff is so expensiveI could just screamIt's better to me to have, A Natural body, Natural skin, Natural personality, it's really what's in,It's easy to see, that Natural's the way to beLet's stay NaturalPerfect teethPerfect smile,Get a nose job,Hey Pinocchio, your honker is out a mileBoobs an A cup? Not big enough for sureNeed some DD's, let's hope they don't leak when they ruptureWhy bother pleasing someone elseInstead of yourselfYou already look greatNo need to tempt fateWith a Natural body, Natural skin, Natural personality, it's really what's in,It's so easy to see, that Natural's the way to beLet's stay NaturalAnd if somebody tells youNot to look like your ownDon't listen to themWho wants to be a cloneWith everyone looking alike, you seeThen your true colors won't shine, you won't be freeIndividualityGotta wash my hair,Make it soft and shinySo the boys like itThey'll all want to be with me10.99, for a bottle of shampooWow that's expensive, But if It'll make me look like that model, I'll use it, won't you?That's what some people thinkand they don't realizeIt's just a gimmick they useTo advertiseIt's ok to be yourself, it's really wise,to be..Natural
