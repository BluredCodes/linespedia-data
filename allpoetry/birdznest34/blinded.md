Lit my last cigarette, it's time to quit,I smoke too much and I'm tired of this bit,I'm feeling blue, lost my baby tonight,Think it's about time, to make some changes in my lifeEvery body's stepped on me,It's so easy for them to see,But I was blind, not my own mindI was blinded by loveMy baby she was perfect,To me, it's what I saw,She could do no wrong to me,Not even break the law,I loved my baby so much and she stomped right on my heartShe left me for some other guyHe tore us all apartEvery body's stepped on meIt's easy for them to seeI was just a soft hearted guyShe could have been kind, they say I'll be fineEvery body's stepped on meIt's so easy for them to seeBut I was blind, not my own mindI was blinded by loveAnd now that I think back on thingsI'd do them differentlyI wouldn't be so sweet I guessIt only made things more a messNow when I think of my dear girlThe one who I wanted to share with the worldShe might as well have killed me, when she told meShe was going to leaveEvery body's stepped on meIt's easy for them to seeI was just a soft hearted guyShe could have been kind, they say I'll be fineEvery body's stepped on meIt's so easy for them to seeBut I was blind, not my own mindI was blinded by loveI was blind, not my own mindI was blinded by love
