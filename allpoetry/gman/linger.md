---
title: "Linger"
writer: "Gman"
slug: "linger"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

The light of night appears
Shadows dance beyond me
Lovers parting in tears
Blinded in darkness I see
 
Engulfed by the blackness
Danger is all abound
I collapse non the less
Then crumble into the ground
 
The comfort of being so weak
Is the hell within myself
The answers I never seek
Are internally felt
 
Let me lay here for now
No,don't lift a finger
Because only I know how
To make this pain linger
