title: "Secrets Kept"
writer: "Gman"

My heart still bleedsFor you the unwanted soulThe  one, who is without needsFulfilling every goalI see the blankness in your eyesA hollow space behind every smileI can hear the silence in your criesThat echo with every mileI offer you my handBut take if you dareWe walk this landIn my mind, without a careI will show you this realityWithout any conditionI can show you moralityWith total purificationThere are so many thingsThat linger beyond your wallsI can cut your entangled stringsAnd knock, till every brick fallsBut it is up to you, my friendYou must take the first stepTogether we can make this endTo discover secrets kept
