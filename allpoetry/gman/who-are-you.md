---
title: "Who Are You"
writer: "Gman"
slug: "who-are-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Who are you to look through my guise
Who are you to look past my eyes

What permission did you receive
Who do you think you are
How can you learn to perceive
In another's falling star

Where were you in the beginning
When I was denying myself
Where were you when my sinning
Was kept shallow and unfelt

Am I to broken to be kept
Will you dig deeper
Below where the bones are swept
To the feet of my reaper

So many have left in the past
They could not break my shields
They moved much to fast
Through these empty fields

If you stick around a little longer
You will see the flowers bloom
It will feed your dying hunger
And leave you all but consumed

Just be strong, and keep hold of me
I know I'm not easiest to love
But I hold a heart that longs to be free
To fly on the wings of a white dove
