---
title: "Cold"
writer: "Gman"
slug: "cold"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

If yesterday was now hereWhere would I beWhat could I possibly hearWould it even resemble meSo many unanswered questionsEven more to be askedSociety throws it's confessionsInto promises of the pastI do not waste my timeIn such trivial daysI don't regret my crimesIn morality's waysI live my life for meNot to impress your eyesI refuse to seeIn your world of liesAll I am, All I doIs deep withinFor all I ever knewWas your life of sinI am pure in my heartKind within my soulFrom here,I'd never partTo return, like you, cold!!
