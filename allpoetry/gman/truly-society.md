---
title: "Truly Society"
writer: "Gman"
slug: "truly-society"
source: "AllPoetry"

Another child lost
In the cracks of society
Politician's compare cost
To keep them in poverty

Roaming the streets
On the wings of despair
Life has become cheap
In fashions, none can wear

As pollution evolves
From within itself
The backbone dissolves
From the weight of wealth

The screams of the past
Are heard in the books
The things that were cast
Were only for looks

So in the turmoil
Of a speedy advance
We have forgotten the soil
Where angels once danced

I have question on question
But only time for one
Because it's the end of my session
And I'm all but done

What is in the mind
Of this so called hypocrisy
Will it run out of time
Before it's truly society
