---
title: "I Am Your Friend"
writer: "Gman"

I am death
I am your friend
Give me your breath
And I'll give you the end

No more pain
No more sorrow
No more rain
With no more tomorrow

Come with me
Take my hand
I'll set you free
To roam my land

What's wrong
Don't be mad
After your gone
Yeah, they'll be sad

But don't worry
You'll live on
In every snow flurry
And in every song
