The jagged edge of truthHas now discovered meIn this isolated boothIt has set me freeI've been deafened By structured earsOnly to become numbThrough out the yearsIn a society of my own liesI've walked in the shadowsNo longer bound by my tiesTo the darkened gallowsI am one in myselfNo one rules over meThe rocks that they peltWill never strike theeI'm no longer boundTo fear or realityNowhere will be foundA place of sanctityI have fallen between Your God and my sinI've devoured the fiendAnd his righteous grinA smile will ariseUpon this faceBecause it is the demiseOf your human race
