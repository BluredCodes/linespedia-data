title: "Remember"
writer: "Gman"
slug: "remember"

As the sweats begin
My hands tremble with pain
Another episode I'm in
Cancer is it's name
 
It's in my brain
Runs deep through my heart
Nothing left to gain
And nothing remains to start
 
I take each pill
With a grain of salt
It's no big deal
When my heart halts
 
I lived my life
For the most part
I took a wife
Then she took my heart
 
I raised a few little ones
They make me so proud
But now the time has come
For me to be lifted to the clouds
 
I give you all, my love
For you are all so kind
I will finally catch that dove
As my life rewinds
 
You will have some tears
But they will pass
Just remember my years
So they will last
