---
title: "No Real Cost"
writer: "Gman"

The frown within
The smile could not hide
This heart of sin
Is along for the ride

I turn to myself
Without this glare
As thought does melt
Buyer beware

Am I not well
Am I not fair
Burning in hell
Without a care

My life is grand
Like bare feet on glass
Holding the Devil's hand
Drawn in by every cast

Purity is all but gone
Nothing left is lost
I am just a pawn 
With no real cost
