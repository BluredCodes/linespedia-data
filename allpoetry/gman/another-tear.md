---
title: "Another Tear"
writer: "Gman"
slug: "another-tear"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

In the depths of my mind
I venture to travel
But as I rewind
It all unravels

Lost deep inside
I cannot escape
I have nowhere to hide
From my constant rape

What I once enjoyed
Has become my plaque
Constantly annoyed
By the time I beg

Torment has wasted 
All that I've grown
For I have tasted
What was once postponed

Life will evade me
For yet one more day
My mind won't let me be
Free in that way

It's a cell in here
With pain filled walls
With every lost tear
Another tries to fall
