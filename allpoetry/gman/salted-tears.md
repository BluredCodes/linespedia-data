title: "Salted Tears"

I hide it allIn these wordsMake the callOn blackened verbsIt's all asleepWithin my mindSometimes it creepsTime to timeSafe and secureAll tightly wrappedBehind this doorBones are snappedAll being watchedFrom within hereIt's randomly patchedWith salted tearsSo I remain chainedIn this cellTo become stainedBy this hell
