---
title: "This Feeling"
writer: "Gman"

---

I have a feeling
I just can't shake
My heart is dealing
From dark to day break
 
You have become
What I only need
When the day is done
In you, my heart is free
 
So gentle are your words
In the kindness of your heart
Like a flock of baby birds
That would never part
 
When I dream at night
I dream of you
It only seems right
So that's what I do
 
Thank you for being there
To enter my life
For I know you care
About it at night
