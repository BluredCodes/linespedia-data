---
title: "This Void"
writer: "Gman"
slug: "this-void"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I turn to look, to see you gone
I hold back the tears
I must pretend to be strong

I am a fool in my emptiness
I walk amongst the dead
Not a single word could caress
This void, which is my head

So many years have passed
With not a feeling in here
What trouble I have cast
In the walls of every tear

All my prayers, fall unanswered
All these words, wasted
All my tears, become cancer
All this love, untasted

What is my heart, if not used
What will become of my soul
If what is needed, is refused
