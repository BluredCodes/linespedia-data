---
title: "Still Be Here"
writer: "Gman"
slug: "still-be-here"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/12426-Still-Be-Here-by-Gman"
---

As manic depression kicks inI'll never know, What's my sinI only struggle for my willThe tears dampen the window sillThere is no one here for meSo I am left inside to beWhen it is possible for someone to hearI coward and hide within my fearI hate the feelings I hold withinUnable to repair and nowhere to beginIt's scary to be trapped in one's selfWith no way out and no one's helpBut I have struggled this way for yearsSo hopefully tomorrow I'll still be here
