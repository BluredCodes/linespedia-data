---
title: "Eternal Pain"
writer: "Gman"
slug: "eternal-pain"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

As my life begins to crumbleFrom within my petty mindMy hands forever will trembleTo accept my wasted timeTo a soul I could never feelFrom a heart ever so coldI will never be able to healThe pain that is centuries oldThe bleeding continues to weakenMy tears now refuse to flowThese bones begin creakingAs my breath just lets goIn a final thought I realizeThat only one thing remainsAs this mortal body diesIt releases all, but your pain
