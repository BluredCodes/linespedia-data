title: "I Sent Me Away"

Inside I continue to runI'm running insaneIt's become so much funTo remain so mundaneEvery path I chooseI have dissectedIn each memory I loseAnother is perfectedEach door that I shutAnother never opensEvery strand that is cutI lose what I was hopingJumbled words are tracedFrom my emptinessReality becomes erasedby my simplenessInside I miss youOr I miss meI wish I knewHow to be freeIn all these secretsThere should be somethingWith only minor regretsThat constantly stingI have been lost in hereFor far to longNow the time is nearFor right or wrongI must be triedFor all my sinsI must go and hideWhen it all beginsI live in fear of myselfNot for being a dangerBut for the stones I peltAt myself, in angerI have judged my moralityAs I saw in my own wayIn my complete insanityI sent me away
