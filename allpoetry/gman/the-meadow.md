---
title: "The Meadow"
writer: "Gman"
slug: "the-meadow"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

As I look across
The frost bitten meadow
I see you
The way you used to be
So young and beautiful
This is what
I wish you could see
You're so elegant
The way your hair
Flows in the wind
The way the sun
Sparkles in your eyes
It amazes me
That even the butterfly
Shuttered in your beauty
You are my everything
You are still beautiful
And always will be
Forever in the meadow
To me
