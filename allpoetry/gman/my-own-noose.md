---
title: "My Own Noose"
writer: "Gman"
slug: "my-own-noose"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/9183-My-Own-Noose-by-Gman"
---

Into this bliss I fall
The fire in my mind
Continuing it's call
Upon a soul,lost in time

With each passing day
I become ever more confused
For I have lost my way
In this body, I've abused

A soul cast in torment
For a world of no use
I am my own testament
I am my own noose

My eyes can see
But my mind won't comprehend
Am I really free
Or am I just out to lend

My heart has been led astray
And my soul has taken a dive
I torture myself this way
It's how I know I'm alive
