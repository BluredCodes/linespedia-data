title: "Love No More"
writer: "Gman"

Have I died, to live again
Is here where I began

For these are things
That will never be answered
Because on your firey wings
My love is devoured

If I was to shed a tear
To fall apon you
Would it even draw you near
To the one you once knew

I have tried to be there
For each cry you refused
I left my soul bare
For only you to use

I would die for you 
More then a thousand times
I would cry for you
Till this heart is without ryhme

I have died once before
But without you, I can love no more
