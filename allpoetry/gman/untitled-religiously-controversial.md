Yesterday is now goneOur innocence is destroyedWill tomorrow even comeAs our blood is deployedWhat a messed up web we weaveA love turned to hateIf we could only believeIn a peace without baitOur time here could just be wastedBecause hate has come from religionAnd it likes the blood it tastedPerhaps it's time we give upAnd hand it over to someone elseFor Christ in a cup Offers us no helpDon't mind my ramblingPerhaps I am godlessBut I refuse to turn lemmingInto your false blissI will meet my demiseOn my own timeNever to follow the liesPlanted by small minds
