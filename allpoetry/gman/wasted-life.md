---
title: "Wasted Life"
writer: "Gman"
slug: "wasted-life"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Today I destroy you
Today I destroy myself
If I only knew
It would erode my health

I will now live 
Without my heart
For I only give
To be torn apart

My soul has left me
For another land
I am blind to see
Beyond my hand

This empty shell
Of a worthless man
From his hell
He is now banned

Thrown loose from his chains
Cast out by his sin
Only his name remains
For he could never win

Lost in a sea of flames
Surounded by the black
He plays all of the games
With vultures on his back

He laughs with joy
To cry with pain
For he's only a boy
Who's gone insane

What a wasted little life
What a hurt little man
To have taken that  knife
And put it through that hand
