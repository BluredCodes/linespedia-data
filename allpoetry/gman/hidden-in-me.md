---
title: "Hidden In Me"
writer: "Gman"
slug: "hidden-in-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/10384-Hidden-In-Me-by-Gman"
---

On blinded fearsI travel through lifeWith bloody tearsI deal with tonightAs the sun goes down And the frost sets inI'm totally boundBy nonsense and sinI fall upon my own graceTo make justice in my mindMy heart will raceTo another part of timeWhy have I ignored my soulTo destroy what was pureJust adding another holeTo an unknown cureI torment myselfEach and every nightI do it for my healthTo resist my plightI am truly torn insideA mangled mess I've weavedNowhere else can I hideBut in the recesses of me
