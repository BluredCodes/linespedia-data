---
title: "I'll See You There"
writer: "Gman"
slug: "ill-see-you-there"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/9717-Ill-See-You-There-by-Gman"
---

What love has lost
Time will only find
Whatever the cost
Well,I don't mind
 
I will never forget you
Or ever forgive myself
I should have taken you 
When I was in good health
 
Now my body is dying
But never my soul
Though my heart is crying
It will never grow old
 
I will live in eternity
If not only for you
To put ever so simply
I loved you, and still do
 
I'll see you there
