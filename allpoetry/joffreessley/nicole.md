Skin of dusk and eyes of night
Full of fire, and filled with light
Indian maid of the prairie hills
Sweet flower of the bluestem hills			

From your garden walled without
To a world of sin and doubt
bit by wind where the prairie swells
blooming still where the pheasant dwells		
Laughter deep inside her heart
Ready soon her life to start
Flirting, teasing the prairie boys
Hurting, pleasing the daring boys			

Now beware sweet little girl
Come a day your heart will whirl
When comes desire, dark and tan
