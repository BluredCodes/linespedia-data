Metronome, metronome
sounding so regular
drive the beat, drive the beat
keep it on schedularMore word play.  I've never really enjoyed poems where you have to force a word to sound a way it doesn't want to go, but this sounded so funny I'll gladly claim ownership even if nobody else likes it. 

I wrote it sometime in the last two years.
