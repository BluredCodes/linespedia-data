Your smile came obliquely,
from the corner of your eye,
and your greeting came so meekly
that it caught me by surprise

I thought that love would come
with a thunder and a boom.
Who would suspect a quiet hum
across a crowded room.

The conversation stilled.
There was only you and I.
My heart had stopped and yet was thrilled.
My breath became a sigh.

That was so long ago.
Many careworn days have passed.
The days are short, my step is slow,
yet memories will last.
