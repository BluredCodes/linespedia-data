The errant smile charmed me unawares
her laughter and her words secured the silken snares
and caught so unwittingly
I had not the thought to shake me free

but lingered in the euphoric stupor her look induced
content to my fate, content; a prisoner reduced.
Gladly I offered her my substance and my soul,
glad to satisfy her hunger, in bit, or part, or whole

and left me to dry with the others of my fate
