title: "On Becoming Two"

So I hear you are turning two today
I highly recommend it,
and if I could repeat that age,
with you I'd gladly spend it.

We'd laugh and romp and giggle and play,
and sigh at the moonlight, and rejoice in each ray.
For your are a very special child,
deserving a childhood, sweet and mild.

But I know you want to have me my size
for your only a child of  two
a child who needs the hugs that dad
lavishes freely on you

So laugh and romp and giggle and play
sigh at the moonlight, rejoice at each ray
For you are daddy's special child
deserving a childhood, sweet and mild.Written for the 2d birthday of my eldest, Elizabeth.
