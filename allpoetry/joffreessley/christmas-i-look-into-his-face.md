title: "Christmas - I look into his face"

I look into his face, my vision blurred by love
This little one is surely a gift from God above
Who is he that he has come so small, so young, so meek
Why do the angels praise him, this little one so weak

I am scarce a child, a woman but in name
Yet I have borne a child who's won angelic fame
By prophets I was told, he'll lift our burdens great
He is but a child small, how can he bear such weight?

He's young and needs to grow, I'll lift him to my breast
I'll feed and give him strength, and then I'll give him rest
I know not how to guide this gift, this gift from God above
I know but how to hold him and bless him with my loveLast year's christmas card.
