title: "Sally Laughed"

Sally laughed and the boys ran,
running to be near her.
Sally laughed as Sally can.
No boy held her dearer.

Sally smiled and we all sighed,
content with her attention,
but when she left 'twas me that cried
defying my intention.One of my favorites
