"Why Most Countrys Hate america" (offensive, fair warning!)


(BU SHAKKA ALI!)

The majority of America, who beleives only & does only as a brain washed society instructs~
people fighting blindly against things that a government sets out only to self destruct
And idolizing media scare tactics used for control, most Americans can't even see it's own evil~
home for arrogent, ingrates & infedels who support their bullys of a government in hopes of a world wars sequel
We had been the ones questing for peace, 'till the Americans came oversees to push us around~
there had not been a single sound up to the moment when they bombed my small home and town
And all for what? Because of a greedy Americas desire for cheaper oil~
that they can't see isn't even needed to live, their lives of luxury costing their very souls
So many americans would like to make it a crime to speak against their ways, their protests of (plastic existance)~
the resistance is now! revolution does not die until the primitive ways are back in full persistance
Like the way they destroyed the beautiful paradise of Brazil, then the whole rest of the continent~
the winning streak CAN'T last forever, watch & see the fall of their hypocrisy and democrecy
Now we form back into the tribe, reformation of our people murdered by their divide~
all of my people who died, (God will guide me) to succeed where others have failed but tried
~On one mission alone, to see that Mother Earth survives........

Respectfuly Dead,
~Timmy G.

(ULTU MUXXISHA! LALARTU ALLA KANPA!)
