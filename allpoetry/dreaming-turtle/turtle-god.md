Well here I am again, surely not your lucky day~
the frenetic turtle (unable to live) with all joy moved astray
Longing for you lover, but you think me not in the same way~
even though we be two of a kind, you wish not with me to lay
Well I fefuse to keep this at bay, you're too concerned with love falling right into your lap~
never will you conceive that love must be learned, beleif at first sight is a perilous trap
Rather something you must study it's map, learn it's song lyric by lyric & never turn back
You fantasize from the beggining impression, nothing more than a (secret sexual obssesion)
A trendy condition, you think they're this or that when nothing about them has been mentioned~
like he's the perfect gentleman, though to love you & leave you be of his truest intentions
And you fly off the handle because you beleive your heart to be broken with desolate exposure~
neglecting to realize what it is I offer, genuine affection, limitless passion, both wisdom and pleasure
See, you shouldn't knock something until you've given it a try, I could be your buried treasure~
and once you've had me I gotcha, you would understand the necessity in this struggling endeavor
So this is my ode to you, the one who would oversee the turtle of her dreams~
I hark for you indefinatly, I promise your fixation of him is not what it seems
(Our love could shine bright like high beams), I would pamper you like an (angelic queen)~
come to know my (igneous passions) & learn to love this (boy in green)...

Respectfuly Living-Dead,
~Timmy G.
