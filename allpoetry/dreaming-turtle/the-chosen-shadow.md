Lo! I have chosen to be a shadow and will be hated among theives~
assumptions of tricks up my sleave by the ones so easily deceived
Yet my thoughts will bleed upon those in greed~
offer a hand to those in need, 
~shown the deceased a little haven of peace
And all dead who claim not the honor~
denial of their own states of power
~while in their dreams the living hour toward victory of the supposed "coward"
(Canard this is not), all blocks breath will stop upon the (Joust)~
the lessons of one ghost before the dawn of a political overdose
So slowly (the shadow) moves into verse~
 watching the play of an American made curse
~'tis hated but revered among honest tribes of (which one came first)
Just a quick look inside their lines drawn congruent~
filled with anger over (the shadows) calm refusal of argument
~(twisting and glistening) in the messages which hath been sent...

Respectfuly Dead,
~Timmy G.
