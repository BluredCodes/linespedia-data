(Dedicated to the unknown Siberian Husky of Cold Springs, Stuarts Draft Virginia)


Today was a rough day, but God is still on my side~
still I have to say, when will I find my ride
~Home?

Screw it, I can always walk back~
until I get lost, only learning the drawbacks
Working my way down (a devils road)~
to turn back from the wind blown so cold

Down Through the (Cold Springs)~
nothing to do but sing
Something just made me smile~
because I had a (Friend) for awhile

Although I may never find my way~
at least I know that I had a (Friend) this day

(Spirit God of the earth and sky remember!!)

So whatever became of (Friend)?~
Just an angel sent down unto me?

The way home is where he'd send me~
("Keep Fighting") he said
~"You're allmost free!!!"


Respectfuly Evoked,
~Timmy G.
