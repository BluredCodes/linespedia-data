Fresh meat, I prowl upon the night hungry and bewildered by this towns sum~
so many precious victims to choose from, so I just mingle until I'm sure I have found the one

Miss (Nicknovas) is sitting in the corner with a look that can only mean one thing~
the mood swings and I embrace her veins by the fangs of a demon fiend
Dancing over top of her corpse, orgasmic yet contaminated with regret~
Why is it that (every feeding I get) be filled by so much insatiable spirit?
Always the same trip, my grave reads r.i.p. yet you still (can see me)~
invisibility means nothing when I spill it all into the depths of my poetry
Like the log of my tears these past few years living imortal~
this little bit of time and am already an untamable devil
~Oh well, tomorrow I feed again....

Respectfuly Dead,
~Timmy G.

(Yet the new found memory of giving her children birth doesn't make it any easier)
