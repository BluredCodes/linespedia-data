I charge the president with endangering American lives~
slipped and would slide in confusion of (the day America cryed)
Within minutes the accusations flew, calling for examples  noone had ever knew~
point the finger due west then attempt to conquer all of the rest
(MASKIM XUL BARRA, EDIN NA ZU)
There are no new lands to explore, (live & let live) theories go un-noticed as before~
this is a terror on war, not the other as they will even say once more
How I as citizen so long the next presidential ellections, do away with the ellectorial college~
GIVE US BACK OUR COUNTRY and those rights you deny me because of your political credibility
And it makes no sense, they prophet from every death taken place~
EVERYBODY ARISE! I am calling in to order a re-evaluation of this case....

Respectfuly Dead,
~Timmy G.
