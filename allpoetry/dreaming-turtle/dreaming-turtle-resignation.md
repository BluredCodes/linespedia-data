"These are two different worlds" lady moon sings through the night~
leaves me tripping for words, my love of nocturnity
~but everything is going to be alright
I may already be dead, more accuratly am I an undead imortal (in a half shell) of plight~
most visions really 'rouse me out of trance in fright, but (Lady Moon) is just within' my sight
As I will investigate the world around me by starting inside my very own mind~
(blinks through the air as Turtles leave trails)
~There is only one vision that I wish to find

And that is the lady in pinkish black, ignesant affection of my dream lover in womankind~
translucent predictions of animalistic addictions
~phantast waiting to embrace of her moon sign
And I don't know for sure if it's real, all I perceive is that I haven't made my descision just yet~
maybe I'm not in the right mindset, but my thoughts keep printing off pictures of her face
~like an inkjet
And I am (unable to forget), the undistinguishable charcteristics of her face
losing my mind (in stereo) as I only know her
~once I have become lost in space
Pushing back and forth, I'll try to remember tommorow, but there is only left a trace~
so I remind myself of these visions, scratch them in the sand
~So that I can remember this place

Though eventualy will I lose this race, paranormal conclusions of a (Celtic Bloodtype)~
investigations must be done right, as will I
~On this between the worlds (center seat flight)
And at that time, all congratulations
~We will end it with a Shamanistic style celebration
She become my confederation
~She dance with me down to (shaking at the station)
An ovulation of consideration
~She ponders to herself again "What was the question"?
Our flesh that is meshing
~In nocturnal fashions which are my conimplations

~This is not a Dreaming Turtles resignation...........
