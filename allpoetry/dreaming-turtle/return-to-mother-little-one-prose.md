I can feel these spirits race through my vains, little child, where is your mother? She fell off of a balcany, yet there is no balcany. Oh harken young one. Mother is waiting on the other side, she'll be wearing a blue dress, yellow hair, dark red lips, She will take us all back into her womb. Sing to me your song little one, I will listen. Take off that mask little one, I shan't laugh. Was (Zac Nicknovas) your long forgotten uncle? Did he attempt to open (the gates of Adar) and have you freed? Oh little one, do not cry. I know you where noone else can see you, but I have found you little one. Come with me and take my hand, I cannot stand to see you in such misery. Pull me over unto the other side with you little one, we can find the light together. Don't let go little one, I will help you find your way back to mother. Pull me over to your side of the three worlds little one, so we together may return to her.

Respectfuly Dead,
~Timmy G.
