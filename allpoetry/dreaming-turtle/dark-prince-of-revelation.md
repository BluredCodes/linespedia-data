And thus the trial began, (unspoken words) of the slandered man~
accompanied by his (Friend) and (Gremlin) to testify the plan
Knowing not the secret beneath the sand~
stepping out for the first time in weaks under the sun
~Pale as a reversed tan
When he crept, society slept, yet through the waking hours of day~
he would dream of ways unperceived by the dazed
~Silent in watch of hells igneous blaze
Yet there he stood, knowing the devils well, for he once was them~
until the day he came to forgive his own sins, start another trend....
~Because he has now seen that which must now begin

It was war he got caught up in, for he could not allow the truth to go unsaid~
all pools of red unto self proclaimed christians coveting every neighbor...

He screamed, "Call an end for this nonsense! Let the disbelievers be, for you can not force one to see"~
The faithless children of religous pushers by (fruitless trees), it will be twice the fate of heartless murderers upon thee.....
~Let the dying bleed.....
