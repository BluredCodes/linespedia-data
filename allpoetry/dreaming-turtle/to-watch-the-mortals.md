Let me drown, I burst forth for air~
walking along the paths most are unaware
And so the conformists will (stop & stare)~
I swallow my pride & bow my freshly shaven hair
They had wished me to drop to dead, I live eternal~
post (desert eagle) devil, both am I equaly good and evil
Like the God shown upon my reflection, wait for the proper times to unveil my latest visions~
my decision of wisdom to let each new moment & turn of each corner to teach it's timely lessons
I exist between worlds, know me not but the scrolls of each new fable told~
for every soul that is sold a new beholder can no longer ignore their divine shape or mold
Now tell me about yourself, tell me about those previous lives and all of it's wealth~
just because one is not mortal does not mean they have perfect health
~Tonight I watch, carefuly and silently....

Respectfuly Dead,
~Timmy G.
