title: "'Memoir Of my Baby Sister'"

We were lovers for the world to see, until the adoption was final~
now hiding along this (world of devils) trying to remain leval headed
But all traditional methods have been both severed and disconnected~
the girl I'm now calling my sister soon to become impregnated
And so social services want to make us depart, she holds every muscle and beat to my heart~
should we just run somewhere far off and make a new start? Everyone thinks I'm sick because they took not much time to hark
If we had not took her in~
 the streets would have claimed her sin and then done her in
And as we had been lovers already, I still hold her spirit internaly~
though we were not more than children of that time and sea
~only her ghost shall ever know the true me
They said "Judge not", yet all of them had wanted to play Gods part~
perhaps they just don't know any better, but I didn't choose to walk (alone in this dark)
~So (please don't part), I think I have a flashlight.........

Respectfuly Dead,
~Timmy G.
