title: "'Acid Zombies'"

Come one, come all to our party, we got all your drugs & booze lined up~
women & men come alike from miles around to our cottage here in the mountain shrub
Nobody else for miles, the perfect party where tabs of acid float like clouds of mud~
of a mixture thrown together by the unknown, here comes a peak like you've never known before
The pictures swirl much different now, the point of revelations now but an unspeakable hunger~
the pit of your stomach screaming louder & louder until you can refuse no more to feast upon your prowlers
Silent you sit, liver between your teeth of the person you were supposed to be dating~
watching the rest of your new kind congregating & segregating away from the living
~but of course there is only so much food here, the rest of the world slowly awaits....
