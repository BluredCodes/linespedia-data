title: "'Just To Let You Know'"
writer: "Dreaming Turtle"

I wanna take you on an emotional cruise under the skys of blue
until they just be calling for me & you
I'm real, so what's it gonna take to make you see the way I feel
while your smile is stealing my heart
And I been looking for a new path to start
so please don't part, we could heal the world
in swirls of love and rise above the doves
Straight to heaven, as I dispose all weapons
and unleash these feelings that I got kept in
Together we can bring forgiveness to sinners
it's every thought that you think that I wanna get up into
Can't you see, I been injured of insanity
to gain you as my lady
and I'm going crazy with thoughts of you not calling me
When all I can see is your entrancing smile
and my desire is dancing wild
I wanna raise it up like a newborn child
So I can get up inside your mind
to be one with that light you shine
Girl just take your time
'Cause I want us inscribed unto the neverending cycles of this rhyme
and may it slip side to side for a little ride
That's only for me & you boo
I only pray that you be thinking about this too
So what you wanna do?
I can please you in every way that your last lover forgot
while our thoughts spark a little light into this dark
And dismiss all false crops which knew not
so please Girl just take a little time to hark
I been so alone
with nobody to keep me company but this microphone in the studio
when I wanna know what it's like
To be in that bright aura you glow
so I have to let you know
I wanna get soft and warm
to the (break of dawn), now it's on
And it ain't just about the pantys or the drawers
It's all about me wanting to be yours 
and build family eventualy
and hold eachother through sunny & stormy weather
for all of eternity

Yo, do ya not yet feel me?

~Timmy G.
