title: "'Sold My Soul'"
writer: "Dreaming Turtle"

I sold my soul without consent~
for sex and money, and things unkept
Women and drugs, just got up & went~
as times end nears, and now I've just overslept
And failed, of all things to realize~
my passion in one girls baby blue eyes
But now I'm possessed by a demon in disguise~
trying to break free from this body I despise
So I get on my knees & begin to pray~
but nothing I can say
On any given day~
will make it break it away
And now that my body is all but slain~
away from all sanity I am moving astray
But they would never let my death come so easy~
so I turn to ways of old, looking for something or someone to please me
The paradox in my desire, leaves my stomach fealing "oh so" queezy~
so I'm asking anyone with the guts, someone please just come & KILL ME!!
Before I self destruct~
nothing sane could save us from this runaway truck
Of pure misery, pain & just plain bad luck~
once again, it is alongside of these demons I get stuck.....

~FUCK

Respectfuly Dead,
~Timmy G.
