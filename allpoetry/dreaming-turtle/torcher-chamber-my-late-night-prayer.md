"Torcher Chamber, and My late night prayer"


Stars dance like animated jazz, here's a shot from times to pass~
I thought we'd laugh while pail moons shattered like glass
~Unknowing, it wouldn't go anything like that
Torcher chambers burn backs, all attention to the corner plagued with murder~
inside the minds of just another killer, no thoughts to speak, 'cause he's just another monster
Calls for wars like dinner bells, sells anything that'll triple on the store shelves~
even though they are claiming the lives of our wives hidden beneath the sand
They tell you don't try & figure it out, lest you store a little knowladge inside your head~
lest your third eye thrive on what's been said, this be not another creature of the undead~
~But Another Messenger Painted Red

Respectfuly Dead,
~Timmy G.
