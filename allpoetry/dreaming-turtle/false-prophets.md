In a world of false prophets who claim the minds of their own offspring~
motivating the people for loss of faith through lies cultivating
And the tension builds up, face them head on for their lack of parrables~
knowing the only paths of teaching fables can exist once the beleiver has analyzed
Subject matter and accusations scatter, most true and accurate predictions are never so straightforward~
not until she comes to pass, all cowards hold on to the canard for the hour they are let down with lack of promised rewards
So if you really want to peer into the future, turn to the past, see how it molds your essence of the present~
I urge you not to repent, but to learn with intent in all (messages which hath been sent)....

~Timmy G.
