Vampires, Zombies, necrophagous monsters that makes you shiver & shake~
fear that strikes your heart, demonic rituals in which (Kutulu) we'll awake
This (Alla Xul) will come like (Lalartu) and will not be faked~
all of your burning flesh shall be melted into patterns along the stake
I want to see the look in your eyes, see how scared you get with this new plague~
scared like you left me when I was a child, before I came to know this (cold December Grave)

Now here's what will go down, preachers will announce "the devil is among you and fights!"~
but you know not the height of my destruction, I am so much worse than any devil or anti Christ
Hypocritical Christians come down unto my world of "hellish delights"~
a beautifull young virgin kneels before me, I shall grant her a place beside me as the rest of you flee in fright
There's nowhere to run, everywhere you turn there's only flames and peoples praying for death~
and folks here turn to cannibalism, for you will never die now and there is no other types of food left
Yet (the Virgin) remains safely beside me, God cast her out simply for lusting in Godly sexual wealth~
while the rest of you fed from (the Pariahs) suffering and laughed at those forced to live in filth
This revenge shall consume the earth, then shall we throw God out of heaven~
for the cruel tactics against (the Virgin) and I hath created us unbridled pain, now here are we finaly bedding

As we cast God down unto the fire, it burns alongside of serial killers by 5 trillion & fourty seven~
while (the Virgin) and I rule alone in heaven, and embark upon our long awaited wedding........

Respectfuly Dead,
~Timmy G.
