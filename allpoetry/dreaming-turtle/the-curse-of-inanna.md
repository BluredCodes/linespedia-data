Do I have to be an asshole just to gain a womans affection?~
I attempt perfection of poetic prescriptions to (another lonely nights) (dedication)
Yet they all seem to seek a seperate section as soon as I play the nice guy~
anymore I don't even bother asking why, as one soon learns how quick love can really die
And I cry to ask God, knowing that there will never be a given answer~
so I pray a little bit faster, trance of my past (because I can never forgot her)
Now I carry the curse of Inanna, (Akhkharu) of the night upon her plate plate of copper~
given chase by the dread armys of Enlil, I pop a couple more pills and stand perfectly still
And I await at the (mountains of Mashu), "free me from this!" I scream to father sky (in the air so blue)~
only to be told "fuck you!"
~So I turn around, because theirs nothing more that I can do.....

Respectfuly Dead,
~Timmy G.
