(Aztecks and assets)~
as I reached for her soft, firm breasts
Gentlemen place your bets!~
that I may oneday kill myself
For, in this world I find there is nothing left~
but to exceed in (false wealth) and this (failed suicidal stealth)
At nights I dream of mass bloodshed~
then awake, teardrops in unbearable shades of red
My midnight snack, anothers suffering from which I've fed~
and wicked thoughts of sex all through my head
(instead)
I want you here~
both away far, and yet very near
to lye you down slow upon a satin stain~
so that you may find comfort in both my love and pain
Tasty, yet very plain~
and eventualy you will hate me and then turn away
Leave me, Leave me here alone~
for back into my depression I already roam
Floor act led to lies, deception, my allsteady chrome~
I'm so alone.......

Respectfuly Dead,
~Timmy G.
