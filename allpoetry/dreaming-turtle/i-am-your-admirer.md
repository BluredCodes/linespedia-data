I look at you m'love in adoration of the light you shine~
I could only desire that you would allow these fealings transpired to (become one) and inscribed
I want to bury myself upon your dreams, carress all thoughts among it's seems~
aren't you the least bit curious to (rise above six) and see what's in this thing?
We can be eachothers keepers, all respect to covert emotions and fealings~
a chance to comfort one another from all of a lowly societys teachings 
Together, we can (move mountains) and (make children by building)~
make new trends of a higher form of pen before our life together begins
Now (let us share our wisdom) and plant the seeds for a new generation~
inflation of the infatuation I hold to have your souls education,
~Just me, you, and the legacy we leave behind.........

Respectfuly,
~Timmy G.
