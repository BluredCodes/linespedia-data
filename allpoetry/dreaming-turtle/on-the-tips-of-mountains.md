Something dormant, something alive, I step beneath the hallow trail outside~
You can see me, but you can't kill me
~and I'll give you a little time to run & hide


"Step Off of my sidewalk!" screamed the (Queen Of Darkness)~
before the (Male oppressor) became their successor, and sorcerers undid sorcerettes
~And they wrote it along their book, Confusing every look of the teachings  
~Minus what is left
And now they stare with intimidations, call back, they forgot her balance for segregation~
just another added complication, stand aside me & watch them drown with infatuations
~the fixation of copulation, quite a strand to draw (rebirths penetration)


I am but another mortal, yet my soul glows with a thought in which she would propose~
Deep into the eyes unknown to the beholder, within (the tribes of the wise)
~And now speaks undogmatized, straight to the creator of earthly presence & blue skys

So (where art thou) oh divine creator?~
hiding inside the sacred tenet of Sator?
Watching over the corners 'till the fifth omega?~
or were we still defending the Virginias?

No Man or Woman know the date~
on the tips of mountains we wait......
