The dead sea opened up like a whores vagina for crack cocaine~
consuming the stains of lifes brain, and (only the fit) shall remain
The skys are filled with rage and storms, and only the prohphicies had been forwarned~
in the freezing calm that only comes with death, I make love with (deaths wife) and kiss upon her smooth (silky breasts)
And thus I failed at attempts to rid myself, I exist in (false wealth)~
searching the depths of concious until the world hath no thinking thoughts left
It was emotional theft, (I long to swim the waters) of the desert~
that which we may revive her, the potion awaits to be re-stirred
And so we ate the dirt, seeking and end to my wretched curse~
she (casts shadows) of the (wise mens) lust inside of her guts
~all evoktions about to erupt 

she wasn't even real......

Respectfuly Dead,
~Timmy G.
