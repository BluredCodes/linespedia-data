title: "'Faltered Valentine'"

Only fourteen more days of hell left to go this year, then start to recount her (bloody tears)~
the ring dissapears, and I fall off of track because she hates me sincere
And rightfuly she should, me as a devil hath taken control and corrupted another soul~
yet I had no idea the toll of death that would unfold from my scrolls
The tales of scattered hearts, (Le Goddess Sarah) my prophet to a (new dawns start)~
that I must now (sit alone in the dark), though you would warn me I did not hark
And so the (sparks shoot flames), I dewinged an angel and thus accept the debt I must repay~
repay with my sanity a little more each day, moved away, and thus she decided that she could not stay........

Respectfuly Dead,
~Timmy G.
