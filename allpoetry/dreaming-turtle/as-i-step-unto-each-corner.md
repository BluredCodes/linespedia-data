Wake up, The trees are in bloom, and so we again head east~
let us celebrate then feast, and prepare for the new cycles of soil & seed
A time to start over and relearn faith, (harken and remember) that everything will change~
as nothing shall ever remain exactly the same, and so we move south as fertile grounds take another name
The sun beats down upon our delicate bodies, chaos to balance the peace~
while emotions peak and begin to (unfold from my scrolls), until (the unknown) arives and curiosity hits home
And everything begins to fade away, the earth cools off and we harvest (that which we lost not)~
it's spot that allows us to prepare with wisdom for the coming freezing seasons
And we reflect over each corners vision, look back & make those vital decisions~
each years new resolution, as we await each turn for spirits reinvintation......
Air, Fire, water and earth, centered around that post called spirit~
so happy to understand it and love with it, 
~(And I shall never fear it)..........

Respectfuly dead,
~Timmy G.
