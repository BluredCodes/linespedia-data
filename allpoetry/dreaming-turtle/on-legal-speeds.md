Down the hatch, works almost like over the counter cocaine~
watch you fly so high that only your shadow remains
And thoughts grow insane, keep moving, there's no time to explain~
seems fun now, until your heart beats so fast it explodes, is it really worth all the pain?
(Makes you high, makes you hype), (you're moving way too fast)~
you better slow down, lest you end up just like the others of past
No choice but to fast, starvation, your pulse runs yet you will always come in last~
(feeding the addiction) that causes the mass a quick blast of eternal suffering
And your friends all talk about how much you've changed~
speed in your stomach leaving your breaths deranged
But Maybe one day you'll explode from quickness range~
If you don't slow down now, I'm, afraid you may never see or change
~Why can't you open your eyes?

Respectfuly Dead,
~Timmy G. 

(Makes you high, Makes you hype) taken from the the system of a Down song "Pyscho"

(you're moving too fast) taken from the doors song "Take it as it Comes"

(feeding the addiction) taken from the suicidal Tendencies song "Feeding the addiction"
