Praise be unto Allah, and the angel who spoke named gabriel~
first I believed not until I myself met the prophesized four devils
They carry disguise as men of the scripture, yet they are hopeless sinners~
(breaking every single commandment) through judgemental windows
And thus I pray, let the blind wander off into their darkness~
their fate be that of (awful doom) as the promised day doth progress

And for those who doth know of Allahs word, yet add to for their own advantage~
hath ye not sense? hath ye not seen the truth of that which remains vintage?
Hearken and remeber, change not what Allah taketh not away~
and live by that which you know be true, save for the promised day

Respectfuly,
~Timmy G.
