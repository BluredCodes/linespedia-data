She held me close & cleansed my heart with poison~
said that she would (burn alongside of me)
~It was lies that she had been bestowing
I thought I had a grip on things, yet she was the one controling~
pulling my strings with lustful thoughts in the center of her moaning & groaning
It's been fun knowing you, but now I'm out the door for good~
(I know you're fucking someone else) the way only you could

You might as well have engulfed, bit down, and chewed off my entire wood~
instead you sucked tenderly, then walked out of the door before making me cum like you should
Left me with a ball ache that lasts forever, knowing yourself the only able for it to ease
hateing myself for allowing myself to fall in love with such a fucking tease
You had never come to please, always promising next time for our lust increased~
(broken promises) made in vein, leaving me with empty fantasys in which needs your sin to feed
I can't wait 'till you (show up months later), so I can finaly tell you to go to hell~
though I shall be there too, it will not be alongside of you or the lies that you sell
We won't (burn together), you gave a fuck not that it was from the heavens I fell~
and cast out because of you, now taste my revenge and burn alone in hell.....

Respectfuly dead,
~Timmy G.

(I know you're fucking someone else) is taken from the Type O Negative song "Unsuccesful Coping ......"
