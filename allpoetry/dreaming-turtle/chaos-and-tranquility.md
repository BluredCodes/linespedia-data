Chaos and tranquility, what a lovely pair~
minus ones simplicity, becomes a hovering glare
You'll go blind, unwind~
be forced to beleive lies
As you bow down to these fading skys~
and the ones despised
~become your number one alibi

We stand on neutral grounds~
groove to enter vertical sounds
And just when you thought that noone was around~
there turned up another you left to be found

We as spirit, beings of both love and hate~
us Gods and Devils, living to make one of the two dominate
~Take a minute to contimplate.......

Respectfuly Dead,
~Timmy G.
