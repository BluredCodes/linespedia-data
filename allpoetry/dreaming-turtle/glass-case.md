Time and space, the human race~
being watched through a glass case
~As we wander in time where we're placed
You'll always love and then hate~
trapped in this reef where we're placed
You slowly suck on the scandalous bait~
in addictions of deadly sins without debate
~Stuck on this earth where we're placed
You wake in the mourn, and throw away the boney rot~
or so you thought, then turned back on you
~yes, karma comes back
(You'll rot in heaven, hear an angels voice)~
but you forgot your compulsion of all seven
~Your soul now decomposing that voice

Through a trap door you fall~
loud taunting echoes bounce of the wall
The sound of laughter~
for ever after
A thunder with deafening bass~
slowly cracking the glass case
Your soul lost in a warp where it is placed~
right before it shatters
~Your glass case

Respectfuly Dead,
~Timmy G.
