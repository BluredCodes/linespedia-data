The hackers plans failed~
 (Cursed are they that) I am not a part of their blood trail
Another villain set sail~
curse me for the messages that taketh flight well
~your methods are stail, and I shall see you in hell
Now know about the hidden lives~
 (kitchen knives conspire) an it's about time for you to retire
This is bigger than (the state of Texas), I know you better than the rest~
shall you confess that with times test, I still hold your card underneath of my vest?
Maybe it was her ovaries rupturing, she snapped because I took her beyond realities limits~
that which she could handle not for each minute, ticking for the crimes in which I hold you contempt.......

Respectfuly Dead,
~Timmy G.

(kitchen knives conspire)~ is taken from an Acid Bath song, I forgot the name, sorry.
