Once upon a tweak, I caught a vision of these streets~
where hustlers compete, and familar beats fade away with the weak
And so thumps the bass, another face to vanish, another death grown wiser~
sometimes the miser falls victim to the local crack dealers
~the casualties of illeagal drug marketers
Then sometimes it's the police, or just another brother trying to make a dollar~
quick cash, guard your life overtop of the stash
~and never think twice when in need of a fast dash
Damn, these streets are real as ever they were........

Respectfuly Dead,
~Timmy G.
