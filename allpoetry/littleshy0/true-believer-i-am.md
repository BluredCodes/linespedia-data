True Believer, I am

I’m always told if you believe that it’s always true.
That magic is real and so is the faeries and elves.
Now I will for a chance to walk among their beautiful waterfall and rolling green hills,
To loose myself to their culture and live among them as they so.
Oh that would be a wish come true!
Now wait a moment, look at what I see
Out of the corner of my eye, 
Oh can it be?!
There on my window pane, a flutter of delight matches it’s wings.
Here is a faerie, here before me!
I pinch my seld to see if it’s a dream.
Ouch! That hurts and look it’s still there!
Thank the spirits it’s not a dream, 
but reality.
This isn’t just a dream, to good to be true!
But if you would excuse me now,
