title: "Fae Song - Water"

Rushing, coursing, poerful and deadly,
By the power of my soul, I call you to thee.
Within the turquoise on my wrist
I call you to the bloody deep.
Chilling frozen, almost ice
I am water at it's best and worst!this is in a serise of little short poems I have written for the 5 elements, water, air, fire, earth, light and darkness
