What I amI am a daughter,I might make mistakes and make you madBut that’s what growing up is all about.I am a friend,I’ve listened to your problems and caught you by the handYou know I’m always right there.I am a girl,I’ve been dressed up and I’ve tumbled with the boysMy sex doesn’t change me.I am a lover,I’ve been in your sweet embrace and I long for your touchI’m still waiting.I am perfect,There is no real line for true perfectionWhat can you say?I am a survivor,I have lived through my worst enemy,Myself.I am a true-believer,I know anything is possible, anything is out thereLet know one tell you differently.I am who I am meant to be,I am all I say and possibly more.I’m unique.
