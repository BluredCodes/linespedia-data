I've told you once, I've told you twice.
This is all a dream, nothing is what it seems.
I'm not what I'm suppose to be.
People think be heartless, people think I'm cruel.
In your loving eyes though, you see the real me,
You see what no one else will see.
I'm might be darkness, I part of the night.
Poison runs in my viens, and you see shadows in my eyes.
Though in your dark eyes, you see only the light.
I don't know why, you see this in me
I know deep in my soul your infatuated with thee.
As night turns to day, and back again 
As the forces wake me from this dream,
Than I remember nothing is what it seems. 
I study the Dark Arts, my soul knows no pure.
Doesn't that mean anything to you!
I'm a danger with the element, I can kill you
But yet still everyday, you see the goodness within me.
I maybe darkness, I maybe light, 
As I see you eyes, I'm preapered to fight
No matter what.  I'll always be yours to the very end,
It's still very hard to believe that we started as friends.
We're similar yet different in many ways.  
We're two and the same, and both made of fame.
In time we'll look back, and we will both see.
How our love seem to change me.
