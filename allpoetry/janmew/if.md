If dreams really do come true let me fill my dreams with you and if wishing on a star could make a wish come true, I’d gaze up at the night sky and let every wish be you! If time is all it takes, then I’ll be content to wait, but if every rainbow held a prize and that pot of gold was you, I’d fix upon that coloured arc and follow through the dew! If memories could come alive you’d be always by my side. I’d gaze into your eyes, hear your voice, and see your smiles. I’d only need to close my eyes to erase away the miles. So if dreams really do come true, let me fill my dreams with you. And if time is all it takes I’ll be content to wait.
