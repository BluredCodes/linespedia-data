My Dearest Love, to know 
everything you have said, 
merely means closing my eyes 
and imagining I'm looking into yours. 

The greatest things 
we've ever said to each other 
are the things 
we've never spoken. 

It is in our memory forever; 
maybe not the words, 
but what we see and feel 
when we look into each others eyes.
