title: "Silent Vigil"

A life well lived, 
a gift of love well given.
So many touched,
so many years under the bridge
till the wheels have finished turning
and the circle begins to make it’s final swing.

and they keep a silent vigil
and they bend their weary heads
and they keep a silent vigil
there beside their loved one’s bed

Behind the quiet conversation
and the wiping of the tears;
behind the constant observation
and the worries and the fears
behind the thoughts of all the memories 
that have been made throughout the years
there is a silence overwhelming 
that rings loud within their ears

For a life unfinished, 
and gifts of love yet ungiven.
So much to do
so many things still left unsaid,
but soon the wheels will finish turning
and the circle will make it’s final swing.

and they keep a silent vigil
and they bend their weary heads
and they keep a silent vigil
there beside their loved one’s bedI felt compelled to write this piece when I heard that friends of mine are making the daily trips to the hospital, awaiting the death by cancer of a elderly family member. I have just watched another friend through the same thing. This piece depicts ALL those who have to go through this sort of vigil. It was hastily written and I have made no changes to it, it is just as it gave itself to me.
