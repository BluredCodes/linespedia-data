---
title: "My Gift To You"
writer: "janmew"
slug: "my-gift-to-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/22483-My-Gift-To-You-by-janmew"
---

I've often wondered what gift there could be 
that would show you how much you mean to me, 
so I searched the far reaches of my mind 
but an answer to this I could not find. 

The wealth of the world is measured in gold; 
nothing's more precious than diamonds we're told, 
and paintings by all the artists of yore 
are priced at millions of dollars or more. 

But your friendship is worth much more to me 
than all of those things together would be 
and if a value were put on your love 
it would be a price to be in awe of. 

So much would it cost, that I'd have to say, 
not even the wealthiest man could pay. 
If that be so how could I afford 
any gift to show how much you're adored? 

What I can give may seem meager and small, 
though perhaps it is priceless afterall? 
It may not cost money, but you will see 
I'll give you the same as you've given me. 

For all of the things I value 'bout you 
are right here within me as well, it's true. 
When it comes to friendship, I'll do my part 
and I'll give you all the love in my heart.
