title: "The Most Precious Thing"

You light the spark in my heart. 
You put the stars in my eyes. 
You hold the torch to light my way, 
through the darkest day. 
You show me there are brighter skies 
behind the dark storm clouds; 
you are the sun shining through. 
I have no friend as dear as you. 

You are the branch that shelters me 
when winds blow harsh and cold. 
Your love guides me through the gales, 
your heart never fails. 
Ever thoughtful, ever caring 
always there for me. 
You keep my heart safe and dry, 
you put the sun back in my sky. 

No matter what my hurdles 
or what struggles life may bring; 
when all is said and all is done 
I know I'm not alone. 
Just like a sound so clear and true, 
there's music to my ears. 
Tho' your love makes those bells ring; 
your friendship's the most precious thing.
