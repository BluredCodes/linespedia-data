title: "Between Us"

When I touch your hands 
I touch friendship, as true as true can be 
You’ll cheer for my accomplishments 
but that’s not all you’ll see 
I know that when I need it most 
you’re always there for me 

When I look in your eyes 
I see forever, reflected in your soul 
What we share is timeless 
our love will never grow old. 
I know that with mere words 
how deep you care cannot be told 

When I kiss your lips 
I feel a love that knows no boundary 
for neither time nor distance 
can restrain us, you will see. 
As long as there is breath 
there'll be love 'tween you ane me.
