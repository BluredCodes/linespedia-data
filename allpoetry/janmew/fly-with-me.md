Fly with me Let’s go to Mars or maybe Venus. Feel the love we share between us. Close our eyes and take me there. There is no place that can compare. Fly with me Fly with me Take me to another planet Break me from my grounding habit Fly with me Fly with me Take my hand and lead me with you, and I will follow where you go. Help me to conquer all my fear. Take me where I’ve never been, Dear. Fly with me Fly with me Take me to another planet Break me from my grounding habit Fly with me Fly with me Let me take you up there with me, much farther than the eye can see. I will show you where the beauty lies, as we drift beyond starry skies. Fly with me Fly with me Take me to another planet Break me from my grounding habit Fly with me Fly with me Let’s go to Mars or maybe Venus. Feel the love we share between us. Close our eyes and take me there. There is no place that can compare. Fly with me
