As I was wandering along the shores 
of life’s great open sea, 
I chanced upon your kindred soul 
you became a friend to me. 
We started on a journey 
to where, we did not know, 
content to travel hand in hand 
wherever we did go. 

We shared each other’s laughter 
as the waves lapped at our feet 
and listened to the music 
keeping time with each heartbeat. 
When one of us would stumble 
over rocks or fallen trees 
or feel the harshness of the wind 
replace the lightly blowing breeze 
the other one was always there 
to lend a friendly hand 
and lift each other’s spirits high 
and help each one to stand. 

Somewhere along our journey 
we held each other near 
a bond we felt between us 
that each of us held dear. 
Then on along that sandy shore 
we traveled once again 
knowing only what we felt 
not how, or why, or when 
and silently your soul and mine 
kept walking hand in hand.
