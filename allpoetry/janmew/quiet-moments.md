Take a quiet moment, to close your eyes and see the memories that linger there resting peacefully. Whisper softly with your heart hold me, though we’re far apart. Kiss me Darlin, hold me near hear me say “I Love you dear”. So take a quiet moment and spend some time with me, speaking not a single word, just sharing silently.
