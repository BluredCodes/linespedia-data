When your day’s been long and weary 
close your eyes and let me whisper 
“I Love You” in your ear. 
Just for an instant feel me there 
and let my fingers softly trace 
the tired lines upon your face. 
Let me wrap you in my arms, 
feel the beating of our hearts, 
don’t say a word, 
just let me hold you, 
take a breath, 
and give a sigh, 
and let your worries pass you by.
