In my dreams we walk together 
beneath the leafy boughs. 
We laugh and talk and smile together 
walking hand in hand, 
through autumn's gold 
or summer’s green 
as the seasons unfold. 

In my dreams we find a place 
where calm cool waters flow. 
Just you and I in a peaceful place, 
we hold each other close. 
As waters slowly 
trickle past 
you kiss me tenderly. 

In my dreams I see your face 
and look into your eyes 
locked inside a warm embrace 
where time stands still. 
Silently these dreams I hold 
deep inside my heart 
for in my dreams we never part.
