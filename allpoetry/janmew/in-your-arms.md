There is a place where time stands still; 
dreams come alive and wishes come true; 
where stars sparkle in the light of day, 
and all my troubles fade away. 

A place where things most prized are free, 
and even in a crowded room 
there's no one there but you and me, 
and silence plays sweet melodies. 

That is the place I long to be 
for it is filled with hope and love, 
where I feel warm and safe from harm, 
...within the circle of your arms.
