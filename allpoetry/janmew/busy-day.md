Looking at the clock, 
did someone change the time? 
I've worked enough for one whole day 
but it's only half past nine! 

Thinking through some problem, 
stressing out my brain. 
Fix it, plan it, call them, 
do it yesterday! 

The phone just won't stop ringing 
I'm pulling out my hair 
and then there's all those papers 
piled up everywhere. 

Meetings, deadlines, calls to make, 
what a way to make a dime! 
Someone's waiting there on hold. 
Looks like I'm into overtime!
