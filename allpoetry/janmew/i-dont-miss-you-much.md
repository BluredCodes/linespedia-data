Darlin’ I don’t miss you much, don’t you worry that I do. I don’t spend my days and nights filled with thoughts of you. I don’t spend every minute longing for your touch, and I don’t spend time thinking about you all that much. It’s not every waking hour that you invade my thoughts, only every now than then ....only when I’m breathin’.
