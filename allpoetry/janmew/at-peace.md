There is a wind out in the distance 
rustling softly through the trees 
it gently waves the branches 
and lightly tips the leaves. 
The grasses softly dance 
in time to nature’s tune 
and in the air the scent of rain 
drifts slowly past the moon. 
I lift my face and close my eyes 
to feel it’s gentle kiss 
then it wraps itself around me 
like arms I deeply miss. 
Out across the waters 
a calm begins to grow, 
a peaceful understanding 
of a Love I’ll always know. 
I lift my lips into a smile, 
and wipe away a tear, 
for no matter where your feet may go 
your heart I know is near.
