title: "You Are The One"

I always knew there was one to find; one soul that searched for mine, though never in my wildest dreams did I ever think we would meet. And then... you were just there, and I knew I had found my missing piece. I did not choose you but I knew you were the one. From the moment we first spoke, I never had a choice. You have always been the one, since the day that I was born. It has been a long journey; quite a chore, just to find you, and now I feel inadequate when I try to find the words to tell you what you mean to me But they are only words my Dear; they give only a glimpse at how I feel. The real meaning is in my heart. Mere words can never do justice to how I feel about you in my heart.
