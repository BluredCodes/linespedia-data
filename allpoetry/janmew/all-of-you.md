All Of You 

She said: I Love your smile; 
the way you look at me; 
I could get lost in your blue eyes...forever! 
I even love the way you pout 
when there's things that you're concerned about. 
I love the way your touch 
takes my breath away in an instant. 
But more than that.... 

I love all of you 
Both the good and the bad 
'cause I love all of you. 
The happy and the sad. 

He replied "you're beautiful". 
She wrinkled up her nose, 
smiled and said, "I simply have to disagree." 
He said: you are to me. 
When I look at you, your heart shines through. 
I just don't see, 
all the little faults you think you have 
and that's because..... 

I Love all of you 
Both the good and the bad 
yes, I love all of you. 
the happy and the sad. 

He said: I shouldn't take so long 
to do things I have promised; 
I shouldn't hide behind the laughter and the jokes, 
when there's feelings I should speak. 
She simply smiled, with her love deep in her eyes 
and said, "It's just the way you are. 
One of the many things 
that makes you YOU". 

And I Love all of you 
Both the good and the bad 
yes, I love all of you. 
the happy and the sad. 

She said: I shouldn't burden you; 
cry on your shoulder all the time. 
And you know I always hate to say "goodbye" 
I need to learn to let you go, 
when you have so many other things to do. 
I know I need to act less selfishly. 
He said, "but darlin' that's just you... 
and I Love you." 

I Love all of you 
Both the good and the bad 
yes I love all of you. 
the happy and the sad. 

I Love ... all of you. 
I don't just love you when you smile, 
or when you're dressed in your best clothes. 
I don't just love you when the sun is shining, 
but also when the wind blows bitter cold. 
I don't just love you when your life is good 
I love you even when you're down. 
I don't just love the way you look 
I also love the "you" inside, 
I Love All of 
You.
