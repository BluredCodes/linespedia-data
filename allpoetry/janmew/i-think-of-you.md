title: "I Think Of you"

When the rain falls from the sky I think of you. When the lightening flashes and the thunder claps, and the dark clouds roll by, I feel you near. When a calm falls on the water I think of you. With the cry of the loon, and the rustle of the leaves, and the colours of the sun as it sets you fill my thoughts. When the moon is high above I think of you. When the stars sparkle bright, and the auroras dance, and the night melts into dawn, you’re on my mind. When I’m awake or when I sleep I think of you. With every tick of the clock, with every blink of the eye, with every step that I take, ......I think of you.
