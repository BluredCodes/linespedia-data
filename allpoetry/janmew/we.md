title: "We..."
writer: "janmew"
slug: "we"
source: "AllPoetry"

You 
are a part of me. 
I have known you 
since the sun first rose in the sky. 
You 
have always been with me 
since the first drop of morning dew; 
since the birds first learned how to fly, 
and you 
will always be 
a part of me. 

I 
am a part of you. 
You have known me 
since the bloom of the first flower. 
I 
have always been with you, 
since water first filled the sea; 
since the beginning of the first hour, 
and I 
will always be 
a part of you. 

We 
are one together. 
We have no beginning, 
we have no end. 
We 
are one together 
no matter if we’re near or alone. 
Because you 
are a part of me 
and I 
am a part of you.
