You must have done something to me I have a whole new bounce in my step; I feel like a teenager when I see you. Don't try to deny it, you must be to blame. I walk around all of the time with a silly ol' smile on my face; now everyone wonders what's got into me. It can't be my fault, no it's got to be you. Your eyes of such wonderful blue make my heart skip a couple of beats, That must be it, how you put this spell on me; and I'm sure that I had no chance to resist. That can be the only reason; there's no other way to explain it. You've used your extraordinary magic and look what it's ended up doing to me.
