Beauty is more than a pretty face, 
more than a body clothed in lace. 
You must see the person as a whole, 
for beauty lives within the soul. 
When it shines through, clear and free, 
there is a radiance for all to see, 
that is a beauty so deep and true. 
This is what I learned from you.This is a piece that may eventually become the beginning of a longer poem.
