title: "What I Need and What I Want"

I need to hear, 
I want to hear your voice, 
and listen to your words. 

I need to smile. 
I want to smile the smile your words can bring, 
and see you smile for me. 

I need to see. 
I want to see the look in your eyes 
as you gaze down at me. 

I need to touch. 
I want to reach out to you, 
and touch your very soul. 

I need warmth. 
I want the warmth of your arms 
and your sweet and gentle kiss. 

I need to feel. 
I want to feel the passion 
of the fire you kindle within. 

I need to love and be loved. 
I want to love and be loved by you... 

I am thankful to have what I need 
Perhaps only in my dreams 
may I have everything I want.
