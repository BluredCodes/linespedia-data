You ask me why I Love you, you do not know your qualities. No, it's not just that the sight of you makes me quiver at the knees. And it's not that when you look at me your eyes just make me melt, nor is it that one touch from you, holds more warmth than ever I have felt. It isn't that your sense of humour can always make me laugh, and it isn't that my soul feels like you're it's other half The fact that when I hear your voice, I smile from ear to ear, is quite simply not enough to make me love you, Dear. Though everything I've mentioned is definitely true, they are simply not the reasons that I say 'I Love You' I Love you because you give me more than gifts for me to hold, you give me the faith to be myself, if the truth can e're be told. You give me confidence and courage to try things I never would, and you help me face life's battles that without you I never could When you tell me I am beautiful and I say that's not the case, you say you see an inner beauty, whenever you look at my face; you understand me and accept me for the person that I am, one of whom I can be proud, not just an extension of a man. I Love you because I can be me, whenever I'm with you, the reason that I Love you Darlin is just because you are you.
