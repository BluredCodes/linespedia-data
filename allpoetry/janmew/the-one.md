title: "The One"

Every mountain has its valley; every ocean has its shore; every morning has its midnight, only one and not one more. Each soul out in the universe is searching for its twin; some will drift out there forever, tho' they try they cannot win. The one that is their other half, they will know it instantly. Tho' many search, not all will find, the one that is their destiny. Each puzzle piece has only one that fits it perfectly; no other can be made to match; and you're the one for me.
