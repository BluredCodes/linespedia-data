Sometimes there are grey clouds overheadand  it just doesn't payto get out of bed!But when I think of you,all of that changes,when I think of youmy world rearranges.That's when the flowersare bursting with colour,and the stars in the skyshine so much brighter;the birds in the treessing more merrily too;the day is just BETTER when I think about you!
