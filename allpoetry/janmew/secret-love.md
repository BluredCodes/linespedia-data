title: "Secret Love"
writer: "janmew"

Playfully the breeze whispers in my ear 
the hopes and dreams and wishes of my heart. 
How does it know the thoughts I hold so dear? 
How does it know what n’ere I did impart? 

It seems to laugh and dance around my head, 
then twirl like fingers twisting through my hair 
then with it’s lust for hidden feelings fed 
it flees and flutters softly everywhere. 

Ere darting off to play among the trees, 
through leaves so softly dappled by the sun 
it lays my secrets bare for all to see, 
then on through wispy shadows it does run 

...proclaiming that which no one ever knew, 
the Love deep in my heart I hold for you.
