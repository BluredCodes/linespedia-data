title: "I See Your Face"

I see your face among the clouds 
I hear your voice upon the wind 
your laugh is in 
the ripple of the waves. 
Your smile is in the sunshine, 
your tears are the rain, 
no matter where I turn my face, 
you are there again. 

I close my eyes in thoughtfulness 
and you are in my dreams. 
I feel your presence everywhere, 
your touch is in the breeze. 
You’re dancing in the shadows 
to the music from the leaves, 
but the moment I reach out to you 
your shadow quickly flees. 

You’re walking through my world with me 
holding tight my hand. 
You’re whispering my name 
with the waters on the sand. 
For even though you're far away, 
and “WE” can never be, 
you’ll always be here by my side... 
You’re in my heart you see.
