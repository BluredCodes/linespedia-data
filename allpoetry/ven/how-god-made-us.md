Let us look at His Pride Creation
       There are two types: Male and female
       Only two ways of doing things
       Top down or Bottom up Approach
       Drawing our experience from software
 
       When He made 'MAN' it was Top down
       For 'WOMEN' He preferred Bottom up
       With a pause to look half way through
       Assuming God is a 'HE'
       If God is a 'SHE' then we all know
       It will be in reverse order
 
       HE has no other choice, neither SHE
