title: "I Mumble"

I watch this channel      Where they show the Earth      From space all the time       Sometime I stare      At that beautiful planet      For hours at a time      And Wonder,      We human are stuck here      For all eternity      No because of our inability      But because of our stupidity       God did not intend that      He thought that by giving us      Just two things      Ability to assume      And ability to reason      We human one day      Look for Him in planets      Around other stars       But we human for reason      Unknown, decided to stay      On Earth and fight to exist      Among the bad guys       I still wonder why?Please read and comment
