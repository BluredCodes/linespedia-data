Russia
 
        Still the largest country on Earth
        With Enormous natural resources
        Just 150 million people
        Superior Military infra structure
        World class Mathematicians and Physicists
        Two fields so important in
        Understanding working of nature
        With all that Russia is like Chicken
        Nothing wrong with that country
        Somehow it behaves like chicken
 
        Does Russia has future in this world
        Definitely, I believe so, I it will be so
        For sure one day
        Russia will stand for its rights
        May be in the Baltic States
        May be in central Asian republics
        From then on, No going back
        We will have a 'justified cold war'
        This time it is good for all humans
