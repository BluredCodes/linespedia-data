In His own image, as for we know
       God supposed have created us
       With two qualities to choose
       Ability to Assume and reason
       With two sexes to choose from
       With just two stages: birth and death
 
       Assumption and reasoning all one need
       To know the mind of God who created us
       Bur we human has acquired three attributes:
       Stupidity, Laziness and Selfishness
       Which override God given qualities
 
       Where did we Human go wrong?
       Instead of following the God who
       Created Us in His own image
       We started following the God
       We created in our own image
       Is it not strange?How do you like it?
