title: "I have to say this"
writer: "Ven"

Just remember, only one life to live          Let let it. be a good one, for you, and others          It is OK, that nobody remembers you          But let no one remember you, because          Of something bad you did to them           Learn from a child, how quickly they forget          How eager they are to learn from others          Never let them loose this ability          Because they have to succeed in life           Let us take a Solomon oath that we          Human will never be responsible for          The death of a fellow human, no matter what          We will never knowingly or unknowingly          Hurt somebody and be happy about it           Our ability is God given and we swear          To use it to help others who do not have           We thank God for creating us as Human          HE had a choice that we know very well           We thank God for giving us the ability          To reason and the ability to assume           We will use these abilities for one day          To know his mind with which HE          Created all of us, as human, as for we knowAgain please find time to read and let me know
