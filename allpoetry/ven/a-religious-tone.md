I am inferior to you        My religion is inferior        My God is inferior         Your God is great        Your religion is great        You are a great person        You're great in kindness        So let me live in peace         Your God knew that        My God will create me        In His own religion         The fact remain that        Your God let my God        Put me on this Earth        Which we have to share         There is a message        From your God        To let me live in peace        With you on this Earth        Will you allow?        Will you obey?        Your God, pleasePlease let me know what you do friends think about itThanksVen
