With minimum security it is easy
        To keep an animal in a zoo, as long as
        We provide them with what they require
        We can be sure that they will never
        Try to escape, I mean they won't plan to
 
        The Prisons are like zoo too, for the bad
        Most requirements of the inmates are met
        But they always try to escape; I mean plan to
        Thank to God for giving us the free sprits
 
        If you keep a man under house arrest
        With everything he wants still
        He will plan to escape, you know why
        That is the human sprit, The freedom
        We are all born with it; God gave it to us
 
        Give him an island on his own still
        The human sprit is for freedom, you will agree
 
        But we all human as a species is under
        Eternal house arrest on the place
        We call it the Earth, floating in a place
        We human call it the universe, So much places
        Up there in the sky, to escape to, but
        I do not see anyone has any plan to escape
        I just do not get it at all; do you get it?Comment Please!
