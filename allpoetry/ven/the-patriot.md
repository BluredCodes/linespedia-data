In the immensity of space
          A floating blue dot, planet Earth
          On it a well defined dot, a country
          A leader on this dot would say:
          'You will be called for duty
          'And you will make your country proud'
          A willingness to die for that country
          A heroic term called  'Patriotism'
          Stupidest of all stupid ideas
          Ever conceived in human mind
 
          An idea which will one day
          Make us Human extinct
          For sure, as for I know
