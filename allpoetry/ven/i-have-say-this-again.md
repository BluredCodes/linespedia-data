title: "I have say this again"
writer: "Ven"

I just read a story about          A young soldier in Indian army          Of how he lost his life in the          War between Pakistan and India           This mother lost her only son           I just salute those men in uniform          No matter which country they represent          May God bless their soul and their family?           Whoever we are, Rich or poor          White or black or any other we are all has          Several things in common           We eat and shit like any others          We live once and die once only          This too likes any other human          We all have been born in the same way          The Gods we worship all are in           The sky somewhere we do not know          We all have only two kinds          Of sex to select from, there is nothing          I can think of, that one religious people          Have it, but the other do not have           Can't we just somehow find a way?          To live in peace and harmony
