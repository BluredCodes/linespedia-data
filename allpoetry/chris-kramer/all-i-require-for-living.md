All I require for living is your lovingAll I need for seeing is your eyesAll I desire from life is your presenceTogether we create paradiseWanting us always togetherWanting us to jointly shareWanting to be a reflectionOf love for anytime, anywhereWhen I see all you haveWhen I touch all you areWhen thoughts turn infiniteTime imprints a memoirMoments spent togetherMoments between just twoMoments making magicI see ... I believe ... in you
