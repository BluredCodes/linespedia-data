Although apart, I must impart
A wish in which I ask
And if you say, perhaps okay
Then dreams dreamt surely last

One small request, though not a test
A smart start for lover friends
A way to be, closer to me
As hearts start to journey's end

Sleep bare my true, I swear to you
I'll then render entry to your loins
Though miles away, suffice to say
Our thoughts ought to simply join

My beat so fast, my heart at last
Will fly high adrift in space
For when you show, my thoughts do grow
To be seen from place to place

Dreams are taken, the cosmos shaken
My eyes spy where you lay
As I sigh, you know that I
