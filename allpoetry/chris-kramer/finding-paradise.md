I travel with thoughts that caress up and down
From tips of toes to the top of your crown.
With a touch from heaven or so it seems,
As I awaken your body and live my dreams.
Finding you wanting and loving the touch,
Savoring each moment and wanting so much,
For time to stand still just for us two;
To enjoy each moment of loving you.
Finding paradise is our destination,
When thoughts travel by sensation.
Venturing into this celestial home,
Urgency sensed, becoming known;
Fan the flame, ignite the fire,
