Love without boundary
Timeless and pure
Forged from the same mold
The past a faint blur

Our eyes like windows
Emanating light from the soul
Melded together into one
Two beings becoming whole

Arms wide as infinite
Translucent if need be
Love without boundary
Is how I love thee
