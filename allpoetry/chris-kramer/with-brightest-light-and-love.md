Endings are such plain soliloquy

"Sincerely" a sigh, so not for me;

Let's be heart-strings, let's engage in flight,

By telling others with pure delight-

(Feel the rush escape as you let fly,

watch close the faces of passersby):



"WITH BRIGHTEST LIGHT AND LOVE!"



Is said.

It echoes and echoes

Inside one's head;

And, do you see?



Lover never leaves; Poet begins to write;

An Eye twinkles; Load becomes light;

God-fearing bends knee; Lost feels found;

Dream is now real and Whisper makes sound.

Old becomes young; Busy rests awhile;

Fear has forgotten and Joyless starts to smile;



The Spirit Is felt and all eyes look above,

For life is rekindled ...



With Brightest Light And Love.
