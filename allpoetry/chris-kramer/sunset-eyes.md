Sun flees Sky in order to meet
Laid-back Horizon (who is oh-so-sweet!).
Their moments splashed by candied red,
Effervescent orange lit all aglow;
A flame-aura surrounds Horizon's bodice,
Sun enters amidst a light show ...

Celestial moments worth repeating
At least each day (without deleting).
Such vibrant color is now a-glow,
Reflected right in your sunset eyes.
Warmth to send as deep feeling;
Delight in such ... celestial surprise ...Originally wrote in December, 2001, I have revised this a little - needed something HOT to warm up to today :-) Be sure to watch for that reflection of the sunset in your lover's eyes tonight!!! LoL :-)
