Gypsy caravans enter my spirit
As a warm day spills into view
A dance is being created, as
Thoughts sound because of you

Awaken and feel my presence
Become aware of what is inside
Sense that which has become
This world where two may reside

I open your world to suggestion
Prepared, determined and steady
A gypsy has entered my spirit
Never more than now are we ready

Rhythm wrapped in sweet sensation
Flame fuels the moments spent
As we sway in the throes of passion
Our lives never void of content

We dance the song of the gypsy
No place holds us long in grasp
Caught in the magic of moments
Released to enjoy morning-last
