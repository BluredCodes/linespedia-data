Greatness lies not in pursuing
Nor of gaining of power and wealth
But just in the doing and doing
For fellow man and mankind's health

History is there to remind us
We can make our lives sublime
When we leave we see behind us
Footprints in the sands of time

True measure is in being
In doing as time goes by
There is no good in dreaming
Great deeds unless you try

Take action and sound the way
For others before your song is done
Show those around you every day
Is a memory waiting to be sungSometimes poems are like footprints ... :-)
