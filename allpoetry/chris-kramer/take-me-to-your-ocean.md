title: "Take Me To Your Ocean"

Take me to your ocean
Take me to your tide
Immerse me in water
Wet me till satisfied

Bathe me in your beauty
Bury me in your sand
I'll wander in your castle
I'll defend promised land

Love sends me sailing
Into places little seen
Where men wish half the time
To find when day has been

Take me to your ocean
We'll sail far away
From the shore of survival
To the beginning of new day
