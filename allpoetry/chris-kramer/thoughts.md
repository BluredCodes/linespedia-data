Traveling endlessly down a broken lineThoughts of you penetrating moments of timeOur paths to converge ... passion will soarOne touch and then .... we'll want much moreThoughts so happy, am I feeling my soul?Senses overwhelmed, knowing where to goHave it last forever, thoughts so happy and rareHardly ever does blood boil so ... thinking, do I dare?Its feels so right, so warm to the touchThoughts of you and wanting, wanting so muchTouching your face and soul, sharing a laugh or twoThoughts laid bare and naked ... knowing exactly what to doI wrote this a while back during a relationship that was long distance.  When you have to drive 400 miles to see somebody, oh do the thoughts ever get wild and exciting during the last 100 miles!! :-) chris
