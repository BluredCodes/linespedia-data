Reddish hues heat the landscape around
A whirling dervish crawls ... with a low, dusty sound
Footprints mark, until the winds blow
Sending a message ... no one will know

Desert Wanderer
A nomad without home
A grain in a sand pile
A recluse to roam

Scattered memories, buried under years
Finding stack upon stack .... hope and fears
Desolate land solace, no one seeking position
Never blind-sided ... by power and ambition

Desert Wanderer
A nomad without home
A grain in a sand pile
A recluse to roam

Parched lips, barely able to utter
Spewing structured gibberish ... with slurred stutter
Desert fever rising, relief required soon
Medicinal salve needed ... a cool crescent moon

Desert Wanderer
Searching deep within
Survival, the reward ...
If one were to win
