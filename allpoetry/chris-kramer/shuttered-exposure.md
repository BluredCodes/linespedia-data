Photograph the moment
Capture the scene
Illustrate intention
Vision the dream

Illuminate essence
Render art intense
Imagination revealed
Love the circumstance

Expression captured
Shuttering the still
Developed creation 
Surrendered will 

Behold breathless wonder
Possess paradise
Capture your lover
Without a disguiseAll I want for Christmas, Santa, is a digital camera ..."
