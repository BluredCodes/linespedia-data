Bursting with becoming of age
A bud blooms and is announced
Pretty flower excited at center-stage
Showing pizzazz and a bounce!

Messenger of heart-felt feeling
Join your cousins in a beautiful bouquet
When eyes see you it's revealing
One loves another on this special day

Your aromatic fragrance exudes passion
Your petals touched tell of a gentle way
Eyes will gaze at you in dream fashion
A memory will be conceived today 

You will be the star tonight it's certain
End up at bedside on the night stand
Last one seen when they draw the curtain
First fragrance encountered in dreamland

Where do flowers go, you want to know
When their lifeblood is drawn no more?
Into a lovers heart and a mothers soul ...
Memories that last forevermoreoh, the life of a flower ... is it only to self-perpetuate the species? Yet human beings see something more in a flower than just a seed bearer ... this poem tries to explain what that just might be ... :-) chris
