Calm collected fury.

Sever the jugular clean.

Reach in,take hold,  

Of your sense in being.



Pardon the intrusion,

But cast aside doubt;

To whisper this moment,

Set aside the shout.



Bargain a position 

With subtle strategy;

Dispense justice; 

Hold to dignity.



Drown this possession, 

Sail to the soul;

Listless will be your vessel, 

Watching afterglow.



Lap at the waterline, 

Balance to the sway;

Participant no longer 

In a deadly foray.



When the hourglass turns 

And heaven measures past,

Actions will be noted

And judgement cast.

To be written ...

An incident recorded;
