title: "A Beacon To Guide Me"

Moments born from sensual wonder
Inventing a world for us two
Beginning with relentless pursuit
Of enjoying the essence of you

Holding you tight and touching
Your nubile form everywhere
Each caress brings forth quick breaths
(soft sounds fill the evening air)

Each curve caressed ... a lover's delight
Each place prized ... a treasure chest
Finding the center, the essence
Direction given by special request

Join my hand with yours together
Be a beacon to guide me along
Teach one to touch another
Oh so soft and oh so strong

A beacon to guide me to treasure
A beacon to guide straight and true
Finding out about one another
Knowing how in finding you
