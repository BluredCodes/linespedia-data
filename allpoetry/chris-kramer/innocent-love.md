Innocent Love ... so pure, so chaste
Delighting in life's flavor to taste
No thought of doubt or question to ask
Focused on loving, knowing it lasts

Gentle caress entices cry of delight
Sharing life together this very night
Exposing self; tense observation
Delighted in finding mutual jubilation

Thoughts united; exposing under
Anxious to give, though the wonder ...
How innocent love is, when one tries
To satisfy, leaving self aside

Pure as newborn, suckling on breast
Mother providing, love at its bestThis is a re-write of a poem I wrote back in April/2001.  I love the thoughts it projects.
