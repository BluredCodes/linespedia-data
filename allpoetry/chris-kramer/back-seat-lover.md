Back seat lover takes my hand
Leads me towards the promised land
Buttons to be loosened, clothes to be shed
Large items used as pillows for the head

We used to park at the drive-in show
(But they tore that place down long ago)
We tried the state park just the other night
But got interrupted by a shining flashlight

Sure was frustrating opening up that door
(Cause a minute more, maybe a score ...)
Some young park ranger demanded to know
If I had purchased a pass with my dough

Heck No! I said ... I came to park
The lot was empty and it is dark
I have no picnic basket and will not camp
So do me a favor ... turn off that lamp!

Lucky for us he decided to drive away
No ticket was issued to us that day ...
If you are a backseat lover, listen to what I say
Next time you have the urge ... use your own driveway!
