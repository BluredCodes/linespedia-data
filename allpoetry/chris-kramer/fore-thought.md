---
title: "Fore Thought"
writer: "Chris Kramer"
slug: "fore-thought"

I'll dive to the depths of your ocean
Sail on a breeze to your shore
Reside in an ocean of passion
With love ... strength ... and more

We'll taste the fruits of our orchards
Satisfy our passion appetite
Sweet taste of hand-picked essence
Flavors of fabulous delight

Thoughts made from a cup of wanting
With a dash of sexual energy
Fore thought before fore play
Kneaded for a perfect recipe

I gaze with the clearest intention
And see you without disguise
The one whose translucent spirit
Resides behind my eyes

I'll rise to meet the occasion
Surround ... caress ... stimulate
Thrilled by thoughts of attraction
Love ... after fore thought ... awaits
