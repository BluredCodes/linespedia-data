Cobweb keys, speak to me
To tell me it is time
To move ahead, instead
Of dying inside my mind

With cloth ready, hand steady
I clean off the ivory
Plug in the amp, its time to vamp
Sounds out of every key

No hesitate, I won't wait
Music will send me far away
Into places, familiar spaces
Where one finds harmony

My fingers fly, I fill the sky
For music soothes my soul
With C. King, I now sing
On a rooftop, from a piano

God gave a gift, and I won't drift
Back to dust upon the keys
I'll play the blues, but hear this news
The web is broke ... now I'm freeI used a name of a great artist in this poem --- C. King refers to Carole King who I really enjoy playing --- one of her hits was a tune called "Up On The Roof" - which is one of my favorites --- at any rate, this is my way of dealing with trying to get over being hurt - thank you for dropping in and reading AP friends - you help so much!!!
