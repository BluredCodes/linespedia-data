title: "Mountain Picnic"
writer: "Chris Kramer"

My love and I have waited
Forever it seems to me
Cheese, wine and crackers
Always packed for occasionally

As we travel side a mountain backdrop
Searching for the perfect site
A meadow of wild flowers comes into view
A squeeze of my hand tells me its right

Yellow Snapdragon and Indian Paint Brush
Purple Daisy and Columbine
Our journey for a site ends
Now begins our together time

We sense our connection with nature
We glance with awe at the scene around
Kissing her lips, touching her face
Descending slowly towards the ground

The babbling of the brook below
The song of meadowlark in a tree
Sounds for a picnic for two lovers
Whose beating hearts add to the symphony

As clouds gather overhead above us
We sense change about to occur
Droplets of rain begin to descend
We race for cover ... hoping it won't endure

Under a spruce we find our cover
From the squall as it passes on through
We laugh and cuddle closer than ever
The storm ceased in a minute or two

A mountain picnic can be a challenge
So pack plenty of love when you go
Because nature has always intended
Lovers to enjoy the whole show
