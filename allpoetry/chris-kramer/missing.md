---
title: "Missing"
writer: "Chris Kramer"
slug: "missing"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I gaze into the night seeking recognition and comfort,
an eye relationship with twinkling stars my only solace.
Mind adrift as a lone object in space,
weightless and black ...
black and weightless ...
as I search for definitive face.
One that interprets cognitive thought,
engages in ribaldry and religion,
a tongue hot with flame ...
a flame hot with tongue ...
a passion for presence.
One that re-creates willing dreams,
comforts of security and sexuality.
Partner with love ...
love with partner ...
while I see with twinkled stars
and gaze at mother earth for recognition and comfort.
