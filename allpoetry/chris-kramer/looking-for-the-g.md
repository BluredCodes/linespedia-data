More than anything, I always want to be
A lover that sends ... their lover to ecstasy
Cries of sensation are not enough for me
I just have to know, did I find the G?

Webster's wasn't any help I have to admit
The only written info was smut someone smit
'Strictly reference research' as I search furtively
To find out exactly, just where is the G?

After extensive investigation into written reference
I find dissatisfaction with location indifference
(My best source came from a lesbian named "Honey",
who described the stimulation throughout chapter three)

I've done my homework and now expect to be
The wonder of the world known as "Mister G"
Now all I got to do is figure out how to be
A little bit longer to touch it sensationally

Without the endowment of a Johnny Wad
(Honey writes forget it, too elusive for a man-clod)
But she doesn't know men, so I disagree
Men know nuts and bolts ... technical gadgetry

Off to the novelty store to find a solution
With a little pull, I'll Johnny up my contusion
(If it makes an inch of difference, my lover may indeed,
profusely admit that I found the core of her need)

I spot a cylinder with a pump all attached
Seems you fit it over and then ... voila! What a snap!
In no time at all I'll have a locker room display
And a G spot finder ...(Which is what I really crave)

With laundered cash, eyes down low, I pay for my find
(Then take back roads all the way to cover my tracks behind)
Once safe inside my place, I open it ... now I see!
With a little vacuum, I'll be a big boy indeed!

I feel so excited as I attach this bad boy maker
Forget the instructions ... I'll pump up a near 10 rater
As the vacuum increases I sense an immense change
Oh my goodness! Never, ever ... in my wildest dreams!

It filled the cylinder from bottom to near the top
I should have been satisfied but I just couldn't stop ...
"Seen it all too often" ... I hear faintly in emergency
Brains sucked into you-know-where in quest of the GThis is bawdy ... be forewarned !!!
