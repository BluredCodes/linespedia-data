Child of nature running free
 Painting all the scenery
 Patchwork color tossed around
 Leaves splashed red, gold and brown
 
 Running amok in the aspen glen
 Swirling yellow dripping again
 Ruby-red bled on the oak brush
 Child innocence, nature's blush
 
 How I admire and revere the artistry
 Through lovers eyes and spirituality
 While soaking in Indian summer pools
 Being in and under nature's jewels
 
 Autumn child runs so free
 Paints a perfect symmetry
 Autumn child careless as can be
