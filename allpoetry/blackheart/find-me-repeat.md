---
title: "'Find me; repeat.'"
writer: "blackheart"
slug: "find-me-repeat"
source: "AllPoetry"

Comfortably golden, lining the sun...
Dancing with a blinding inferno.
Turning in,
Licking out,
Touching me inside... burning out.
i'm faded,
i'm jaded,
Dancing with a dying inferno...
Turning in,
Falling out...
i find a hand about you,
Turning away,
Twisting out,
Touching me never... finding out.
i'm wasted,
i'm hated,
Comfortably golden, losing the sun...
Turning away,
Flying out...
i lose my mind about you,
Turning away,
Dying out,
Touching me before... calling out.
Dancing with a fading inferno...
It's light is slowly gone.
In darkness left to sit alone,
Comfortably golden, lining the sun.
