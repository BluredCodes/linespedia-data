---
title: "'My weakness.'"
writer: "blackheart"
slug: "my-weakness"
source: "AllPoetry"

Succumb to the burning anxiety,
And look into my eyes.
Feel the warmth that radiates,
As you dry each tear i cry.

Overwhelmed, you’ll touch my skin,
And breathe so fresh, so sweet...
So when i kiss you lips so soft,
As one we’ll feel the heat.

A rush so unknown, yet unrivaled,
A feeling so new, but unfelt.
Could this be the answer... the quest i do seek...
As i hold you so tight and then melt!

From the heat of my stare, bearing at you,
Hoping and praying that you’re feeling this too,
Again as i look, i reveal the gaze,
The weakness in me...
That is you.
