---
title: "'Perfection.'"
writer: "blackheart"
slug: "perfection"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

When I’m finally with you,
I’ll whisper in your ear,
Tell you, I adore you,
Whether or not it’s what you want to hear.
As I stand beside you,
I’ll tightly hold your hand,
Peer on deep into your eyes,
And hope you understand;

That within them lays perfection,
In my mind.
Within them sits a beauty,
A sparkling adorability,
In them find…
My reflection,
Gazing into,
My perfection.

When I’m sitting with you,
I’ll gently kiss your cheek,
Remind you how I feel,
I will not need to open mouth and speak.
As I lay beside you,
You’ll sleep, I’ll lay a hand,
To comfort you in every way,
And hope you understand;

That with you lies affection,
Please be aware, 
With you lies contentment,
A simple standing sentiment,
Look at me…
My admiration,
Gazing at,
My perfection.
