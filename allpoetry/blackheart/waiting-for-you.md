---
title: "'Waiting for you.'"
writer: "blackheart"
slug: "waiting-for-you"

A twisted elevation in the ecstasy i breathe...
Occasionally.
Broken anchors let me fly,
To find you at the bottom of this growing sea.

i’ll be there in a moment,
i’ll save you from the pains,
i’ll give you my last breath,
Then i’d do it all again!

A sacrificing sentiment that i know you do adhere...
As you reappear.
Today i am the Pegasus,
Carrying you from the places that you fear.

i would never run away,
i won’t hide from any pain,
i will give you my last drop of blood,
And i know you’d do the same!

Returning to the depressing cloudy skies...
It is no surprise.
But you are there my sweetness,
And again a light appears inside these teary eyes.

i’ll be happy every day,
i’ll know it’s me you’ll always save,
i’ll wait for you my love,
Perhaps you’ll do the same.
