title: "'Just to have you.'"

Where is it that you are?
i find i’m lost without you!
There is a road less traveled,
But, i’m happy here.
Do not fear...
You may join this odd parade...
we’ll see this through!

What is it that you are?
i find i’m lost again!
Wandering so lonely,
Yet, i smile on.
i am not gone...
i’ll carry all my baggage...
Well... now and then.

Why is it that you are?
Riding with me, not by my side!
Charging my rendition,
In our melodies of life.
And now i’ve...
Stolen back thewhispers...
You left here once upon a time.

When is that you are?
The angel coming down!
Wings so wide you wrap me down,
And touch me hard.
It fell apart...
But i know you’ve come to fix it...
And turn the pain around.

Where is it that you are?
Are you coming home my love?
This is the road we travel,
And i am happy here.
Do not cry tears...
Walk with me forever now...
Together is enough.For : Molly, wish you with me *s*
