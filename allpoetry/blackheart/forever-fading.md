---
title: "'Forever Fading.'"
writer: "blackheart"
slug: "forever-fading"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Fade out,
See the dark of the screen (it's as black as loud shouts),
The story ends,
It's nothing more,
Than fiction.
Revolving around my head,
Instead...

Instead of walking normal,
Bouncing with desire,
Loving every minute,
Of this very real(deceptive)life!

Fade away,
Every single passing second (feels like yet another day),
Comes and goes,
Sharp to read,
Threshold weaker by the lines,
It hurts my head,
Pretend...

Pretend i'm almost happy,
Feeling full of life,
Basking in the sunshine,
Of this very real(wasteof)time!

Fade in,
The reality is bleak (a forecast disaster begins),
Breaking to the point,
That will surely see my show fall...
Effortlessly.
It makes my head...
Intend...

Intend to shake it off,
See the tunnel, see the light,
Grasp every ounce of breath,
Of this very real(vacant)life!

Fade completely,
Show them black your theatre (curtain draped dark all around me),
Burn the sequel,
Find me a prelude,
Bursting with verity for my honest head,
i am red...

i am red by black and blue,
Merely i ask, a simple cite,
Then perhaps i'll be remembered,
When i leave this(witheredstale)life!
