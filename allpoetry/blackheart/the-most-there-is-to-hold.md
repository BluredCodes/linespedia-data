title: "'The most there is to hold.'"

Superficial stardom, dancing in my eyes,
Love brushes close against me,
Once again...
             it is nothing;

     But the superficial stardom in my eyes.

     Could you love me?
     (i need...)
     Could you touch me?
     (deeper than...)
     Can i kiss you?
     Can i keep you?
                    (my star!)

Superficial sex appeal dancing in my dreams,
Reaching out, i miss the point,
Once again...
             there is nothing;

     But the superficial sex surrounding me.

     And the superficial stardom in my eyes.
