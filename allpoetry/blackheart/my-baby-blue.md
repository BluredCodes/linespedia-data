---
title: "'My Baby Blue.'"
writer: "blackheart"
slug: "my-baby-blue"
source: "AllPoetry"

Baby blue i touch you,
Kiss you on the cheek,
Tell you to your perfect eyes...
i speak;

Remember how our hands would meet each other,
Clasped against the cold, grasping once as lovers.
Remember how the days would seem to last forever,
The way danced the nights away, and always were together.

Yet... something sure has changed.
Perhaps you will return,
Some day.

Baby blue i look at you,
Walk with you again,
Peek into you perfect soul...
i say;

Remember when the skies could cloud above us both,
Leave us in the chance of rain, we loved the rain the most.
Remember when sweet memories would flood within our minds,
We’d talk for hours of the past, even of our future sometimes.

Yet... something sure has changed.
Perhaps you will return,
Some day.

Baby blue i ask of you,
Come back to my side,
Kiss me sweetly with your lips...
i confide;

Remember that i love you.
No one else did that.
You caused so many troubles,
But i always took you back.
Remember that I love you,
When others turned away,
My baby blue with eyes so true...
Will you return some day?
