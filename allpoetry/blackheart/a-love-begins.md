---
title: "'A Love Begins.'"
writer: "blackheart"
slug: "a-love-begins"

Caress me in your momentary wings,
Fold me deep within,
Holding me forever… letting all the love begin…
Heaven.

A perfect image in my mind, slowly racked and abandoned,
Like so many gone before,
Simply and only why, it will not stay,
For sure.

Stalk upon the insanity within my heart,
Tear it apart,
Leave it distant… so that our love may start…
Heaven.

Wonder in my eyes be true, but soon all disappears and leaves,
A simple picture; loveless,
A face, eyes, rosy cheeks, a smile… dead,
I become obsessed.

Reach the stationed remedies inside your head,
Don’t let this one stand bear as thread,
Take care of me… the way that you once said…
Heaven.

But alas, this will not happen now,
I know this true, don’t ask me how.
It’s simple but I cannot see,
The explanation I really need,
Cast aside, I touch my skin,
Feel it dead and withered; thin,
Restored I will not be, so go,
Love me none, but I don’t let me know,
Surreptitious mind you own,
I don’t need that, I’ll stand alone,
Waiting for that one true day,
Someone loves me, come what may.
Sad until that moment wings,
It’s way to me,
A love begins.
