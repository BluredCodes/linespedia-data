title: "'Wondering how it feels.'"

Dreaming nothing dreary,
No rain i see tonight.
No reason to be teary,
But i’m all welled up inside.

Because of all,

Your beauty...

It makes me fly... makes me cry.

It makes me cry...

And now i know, the reason why,

... ... Because ... ...

i’m falling in...

i miss you when you gone.
