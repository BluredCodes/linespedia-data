"dark;less."

i feel better now the sun shines,
casting shadows now behind me...

no more a grey or blackened fog,
clasps my heart to make it stop.
no more i sit alone at night,
with shattered dreams, no end in sight,
no more i pour onto the wounds,
the salt the keeps me in my room,
no more i bite upon my fist,
as evil tries to find my wrist,

no more...

no more...

not now.

i feel better now the sun shines,
twisted mind will not define me...

i feel better now the sun shines,
painful thoughts cannot confine me...

i feel better now the sun shines,
regret be not the whore that binds me...

i feel better now the sun shines,
casting shadows now behind me.
