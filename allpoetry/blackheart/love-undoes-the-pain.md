---
title: "'Love undoes the pain.'"
writer: "blackheart"
slug: "love-undoes-the-pain"

Believe me when i say this,
I have felt your sharpest knife,
No longer can you kill me,
You have already destroyed my life.

I'd tell you where i cry,
But i know you'd lock the door,
So never could i go again,
And relieve my heart so sore.

What can you give me now?
Other than the wettest eyes!
What can you offer me?
Apart from blatant lies!

Look at me....
And say to me,
Why do you stay with her?
Point at me,
And laugh...
Why do i stay with her?!

Because i cannot leave...
She hugs me when she's hurt me.
I cannot run away...
She give's the touch i need.

Love undoes the pain.
She hurts me time and time again,
But still...
Her love undoes the pain.
