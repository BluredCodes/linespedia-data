---
title: "'Silent Shadow.'"
writer: "blackheart"

Morning gaze surrounds me,
A shadow lays behind...
Lazy blackness.
A shallow dark.

Inside this tormented smear,
Lies the dreams of tainted souls,
Nightmares more,
Instead.
Quickly now, don't be fooled,
i must press on,
it's over now.
it's gone.

Vibrant Stellar Flare,
Huge beyond my words.
i'm basked in all your sunlight rare,
Sitting here i start...

To wonder of the reasons,
That you cast this shadow down,
To haunt and hunt and harm me,
When i sleep,
When i wake,
Whenever i am calm.

Suddenly instilled is a fear so aimlessly assembled.
There's no escape, yet try i will...
Still,
Always,
Forever.

i don't know why, i...
i don't know.
As the morning gaze surrounds me,
You catch me,
i die.
