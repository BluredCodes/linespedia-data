title: "'I never knew I loved you.'"
writer: "blackheart"

i don’t think i realized,
Until tonight.
That everything you said to me,
Was true.
You clutched my heart,
Yet no blood fled.
It’s something i...
i did not expect.

i said i love you,
But, then...
i didn’t know what love was,
And, then...

i want to ask you home,
Yet, you never come.
Please my sweet, i beg you,
i do anything my love.


i don’t think i understood,
Until tonight,
What it meant.
To stay with you forever and tonight.
You touched me,
In a way no other can,
And yet, i didn’t notice,
i’m sorry... i’m sorry that i ran.

i was a fool.

No less.
Every night i finally sleep,
i know it’s you i do caress.

Perhaps there’s something missing,
In my life, i do not know.
But, i know what’s gone, i know i miss,
i left before the truth could show.

i miss you now,
i’m sorry.
i know it is not much,
But i mean it now, more than i ever had before...
i only need your touch,
Just once...
Just one,
i love you.
i’m crying now... i mean it.
i love you.This is not a poem i would usually write, but... it's something that just slipped from off of my tongue... happened, without my wanting... and yet, i do want. What can i say?!?
