---
title: "'Heaven or Hell?'"
writer: "blackheart"
slug: "heaven-or-hell"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/40984-Heaven-or-Hell--by-blackheart"

Fornicate, illuminate, pronunciate my one mistake.
Reciprocate the spoken lies, that have slipped my tongue before.
Ever after, ever after; open gate, lead the way.
Ever after, ever after... come... take me there today.
Heavens open, light the path...
A hope stuck in my head.
Hell frozen over?  i do not think so...
Satan plays with me in bed.
Slicing dreams with branded knife,
A mark upon my soul.
Cutting love in two;
My love!
Outside my life...
A gaping hole.

Fascinate and animate.  Laminate my one escape.
Reciprocate the open wound, that bleeds and hurts... so sore.
Ever after, ever after; see faithful minds all play.
Ever after, ever after... please... take me there today.
Heavens gate, stand closed...
i hate the lies he said.
Hell awaits? a big mistake...
Satan... please... make me dead.
Torture my hopes with fire,
Some so old,
Some very new;
My love!
Inside my head...
i give in to you.
