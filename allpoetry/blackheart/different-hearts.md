---
title: "'Different Hearts.'"
writer: "blackheart"

Whispering women, in cloud white and blue,
Brushing the heather with questions now few.
Silent and wild, hearts so defined;

- Torn apart, slashed, and broken in two!

Humouring hags, in a sunset bled sky,
Dusk meets an evening with dark stare in it's eye.
Angry, like child, this heart now turned blind;

- Ground down to dust and left slow, to die!

Wandering women, in a cloud clear, bright, blue,
Touching the dew drops, in this morning so new.
Quiet but praying, hearts soon denied;

- Torn apart, stolen, and left here with you!

Hammering hags, in a flamed sunset sky,
Hell’s freezing over, but the Devil keeps an eye.
Angry but staying, this heart’s met it's time;

- Ground down to dust ... LEFT ALONE, left to die!
