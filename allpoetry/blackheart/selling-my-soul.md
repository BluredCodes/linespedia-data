---
title: "'Selling my soul!'"
writer: "blackheart"
slug: "selling-my-soul"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/36948-Selling-my-soul--by-blackheart"
---

Open your eyes,
See me lifeless and devoid,
Turning in your vulgar aspirations,
To make sure you enjoy,
Sweeping me from nowhere,
And bring me to my feet,
Just to cut me down again,
So you may revel in my new defeat.

Sacrifice i feel,
As ruby tears caress my lips,
Blade among my troubles,
As thorny eyes do kiss,
The vision, in my mind.

The vision of the flowers that surround your sapphire bloom,
Twinkling starlet gracing through the breath in which i croon,
Glazed in overt ecstasy by the luscious sweet surprise,
That beckons as i falter through these perfect blood shot skies.

Leave me to the lion roar,
That echoes in my sleep,
Do not come to save me,
I need not your relief.
Purvey my sacred innocence,
Tatter what is left,
Scrap me to the nearest fool,
As you lift my last fresh breath.

Torn beneath your scarecrow,
A bite of termination nears,
Twist of fate upon me,
As you take my ruby tears...
And the vision, in my mind.

The vision of the worthlessness you desert behind your feathered toes,
Achingly they rest upon the worthless mess i leave, wherever i may go.
Corpulence of anger surrounds the web you’ve weaved...
i sit in silence once again, waiting for a moment of reprieve.

But i know it will not come,
You are my devil loyal,
Forever, in your service,
This heart you spoiled.
Thank you once again,
For branding me inert,
As i cry my only ruby jewel,
i leave to you my only curse.

The vision of my nightmares,
i’ll keep them in your soul,
Once i’m gone, you won’t forget,
That it’s every part of me you sold!

Thank you.
