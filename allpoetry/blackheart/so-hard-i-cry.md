title: "'So hard, i cry.'"

Sunlight whisper through the tears,
As i sit beneath the waves.
i’m,

    d  r  o  w  n  i  n  g.

A water wash of failure,
Dancing on my mind,
Beating hard against my heart,
This is me,
Defined.

Moonlight run across my chest,
Meet me in the darkness.
i’m,
 
    f  a  l  l  i  n  g.

A rush of empty resentment,
Talking me through this,
Deciding i am nothing now,
This is me,
Dismissed.

...

Insatiable madness grasps my ever, needy sanity,
What it means is nothing more than the reason i am, me.
The sunlight through the cloudy day,
The moonlight directing, me; away.

i’m...

D  R  O  W  I  N  I  N  G

...

i’m...

F  A  L  L  I  N  G

...

A definite entrapment,
My soul encased in fear.
Precision in the pain,
My heart survives the tears.
Smiling as i sleep,
At you,
At you,
At you...
The one through all these years,
That made, me see this through...

Sunlight whisper through the tears,
As i sit beneath the waves,
(i know it’s you that now i crave).

Moonlight dance across my chest,
Meet me in the darkness.
(You, my dear, are priceless).

But notice me in madness you do not,
And my sanity's running dry...
And i’m, drowning i’m, falling... i...
Cry... so hard, i cry.
