Section A.

If you were caught inside a tune...
Would it be played by me?

If you were stuck beside some soul,
Should it be me?

If you had to die to live,
Could you?

If you had to love me...
Would you?

If you answered yes to all of these,
Could you meet me please?
If you answered no to all...
Could you go on to Section B.

Section B.

If you were stranded...
Who would it be with?
Why?

If you were asleep...
Who would wake you?
Why?

If i brought a rose to you at night,
Would i receive a kiss?

If i promised to love you right,
Would the answer be this...
Yes :
No  :
please tick.

If you wish to be with me... please call my name...
If you wish for me to wake you, do the same.
If not, then please...
Move onto Section C.

Section C.

Why not i?
What don't you like?
Am i bad?
Do i bite?

i could love you,
Is that not enough?
i would be there,
Would you want my love?

Am i wrong?
Or too right?
Am i sweet?
Why not i?

i will give you pure love,
Does that not persuade you?

i can suffer many pains,
Will that do?

....

Thank you for taking the time,
To fill out this lovers rhyme.
Thank you for making an effort.
Perhaps, love, now i will find.

Thank you for reading my quests,
And giving an answer like i did request.
Thank you for your time.
