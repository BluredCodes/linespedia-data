title: "'Before me comes tomorrow.'"

Yesterday before me, say you still remember,
The exaltation i used to keep,
(Instead for now i weep.)
From January to December,
For the perfect girl... My sweet.

Taste the elevation stirring in my bud,
The simple fortune wrapped upon my tongue...
If only i could speak,
Attempt a little repartee,
Voice my true intentions,
Be...
Me.


Satisfy my foreplay,
As i tingle with the air,
Move my arms through motions,
As if i was still there.

Dancing in the moon beams,
Flowing through your eyes,
Nauseating brilliance,
This life you do defy...

The pleasures i'm without,
A nihilistic revelation,
Denote the ever grace for now,
As i floor my designation;

Yesterday before me, say you still remember,
The words i used to play,
(Instead to sleep i lay).
From January to December,
This is the day i lose my way.

Capture the calenture in my breath,
The sensibility taken for my death...
i wish i had just one last word,
To give to this, a dying world,
To intend a right direction,
For...
Me.

However;

Tomorrow stands before me, a day i do not need.
i’m tired now, good night...

i sleep.bit of a rant... sorry.
