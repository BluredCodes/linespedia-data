Shake me, wake me, see me rise...
Kiss me, love me, light my eyes!
Blue or white,
Red or black,
A rainbow...
By which i attract;

A sea of tears,
Or a pain so dull.
Thudding and pumping,
Upon ... my ... empty ... hull.

But changed that all, you have,
You made me see right through...
Through all the horrid darkness,
That deafens my every view.

i laugh,
i relax,
i’m happy each day...
i love and,
i want you,
As down my head lays.

Bright yellow gleam,
With sparkles of green,
A rainbow of colour,
My eyes now can see...

And all because of you.For : Molly *s*
