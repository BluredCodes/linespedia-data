title: "'Don't fly.'"

An angel sits beside me,
Stars within her eyes...
Wings to always keep me,
Happy in this life,
Careful with my mind.
Turning round i see her face,
Perfect held in time!

A smile i could not forget,
It meets my heart so quick.
Caught by the sensation,
Of each amazing kiss,
Again my heart does trip.
She leaves me feeling special,
Every time i touch those lips!

A moment that forever stands,
Cannot, will not fade.
This perfect little angel,
Will be with me always.
Even if one day,
Her wings find another home,
The memory of my angel... will forever stay!For : Molly... if only i could clip your wings! *s*
