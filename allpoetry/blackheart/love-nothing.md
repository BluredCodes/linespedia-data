Hate yourself,
Before they do!
Wrinkled eyes in summer blue,
Love nothing...

It will only break your heart.
Steel blade, tear me apart.

Hell,
The Devil,
Come be a savior now?
You ask me how...
But,
Questions won’t suffice.

Beauty in the air...fly away hair.

As the winds go cold,
Gush and gust, so hard, so bold.
Love nothing...

It will only break your heart.
Steel blade, tear me apart.

Tip the balance...
A line of fear sings high,
i sigh;
Resign.
This angered, evil, twisted soul,
Be mine.
