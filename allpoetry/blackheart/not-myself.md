title: "'Not Myself.'"

Although it’s been some time,
Since I wrote to you a rhyme,
It doesn’t mean my love is weak, or falling down, with more to seek.

My mind has been a blank,
I’m feeling low, I’ve had enough,
But you alone have saved my soul, I’m holding tight, I won’t let go.

For it’s you and only you I need,
Your the hope I grasp, the air I breathe,
So, that enough, should show my care, with my every word, and every stare.

So I hope you understand,
Why I haven’t been your usual man,
But soon again he’ll live once more, I’m back on track, I know the score.

Because…

I’m getting there it seems my sweet,
I’ve made mistakes I won’t repeat.
I love you more with everyday,
And I wish with you I always laid.
But for now I hope you will accept,
I’m wading through the sea I wept.
So, if you can, love me for the man you met,
Because I love you don’t forget.
