DeLvE into my AnGeR!
Can you see the flesh falling?!
Burning with the rage,
That you bring!

SoLvE my drawing BlAcK!
Save me from myself,
Meet me in the corridors,
Hold me to your chest!


... bring me peace ...
    i'm swallowing
... bring me pains ...
    i'm wallowing.

DrAiN all my BlOoD!
Peer into my souless-ness,
Repair the scars,
Then, bring them back again!

GrAvE is drawn so DiRtY!
i'm covered in the purity.
See me pull the lid down,
That's it... that's me!

... bring me lives ...
    i'm breathing
... bring me hates ...
    i'm leaving!!!

NeVeR will i SpEaK!
You've stolen all my words.
i ask you to remember,
But, what the fuck does that mean?!

DeAtH so slowly NeArS!
Arching high above me,
To fall so fast i feel it...
Take me!!! ... i want to rest, in peace!

... bring me peace ...
    i'm delivering
... bring me death ...
    i'm finishing.

IT IS OVER! ... you are over!

This is over.

It's all...
