title: "'Rainbow in my mind.'"
writer: "blackheart"

Where does this rainbow lead?

What do the colours mean?

Will i find all that glistens is really gold?

Where does this rainbow lead?

Red will bring me anger,
So i think i may walk on,
But sometimes i may need it,
When all my good is gone!

But for now i'll walk on by.

Orange makes me smile,
As i see it's gleaming arc,
i soak myself inside it,
So the smile can make it's mark,

With a grin i'll walk on by.

Yellow brings me riches,
Basks me in a golden sea,
Shimmering with hope and heart,
i witness pure beauty.

Rich with love i walk on by.

Green still carries envy,
Heavy round my face,
i try to just ignore it,
But in time i will embrace!

Looking back, i walk on by.

Blue brings me the white,
A cloud inside my soul,
Keeping safe the fallen heart,
That never will run cold.

With sun aloft, i’ll walk on by.

Indigo will show the star,
To follow far I must.
In time my destination reached?
That star i cannot trust.

So, for now… i’ll walk on by.

Violet brings the fever,
A rage inside my mind,
But quelled in several seconds,
By the thought of gold to find.

To journey on, i’ll walk on by.

Where does this rainbow lead?

What do the colours mean?

i know that what glistens is not always gold?

So, where does this rainbow lead?
