---
title: "'Short and sweet.'"
writer: "blackheart"

Succumb i must,
Desire meets my mind.
Lost in many mazes,
Desperately trying to find...
Those amazing eyes,
So bright,
So wild.
Amazing eyes;
I have to find.

Wander aimless now,
Seeking nothing more.
Alarm bell in my head,
I will not tire, will not bore...
Of those overwhelming eyes,
So pure,
So mild.
Amazing eyes;
I could not bore.
