title: "'Life lost moments.'"
writer: "blackheart"

Insistent chaos fills the air,
As clouds loom ever darker,
All seem quick to bare me down,
And leave me naked with this shower.

Refreshing it may some how be,
Yet the stains won't wash away,
Faces stare from high above,
At the life that has become so grey.

Though sunshine somewhere in my life,
Will always find my eyes,
Remind me not to get too cold,
Or collaborate with my fallen lies.

Caressing clarity finds the sky,
As clouds remove for blue,
And naked still i see the light,
As my life i start to lose.
