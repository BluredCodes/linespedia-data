---
title: "'Blind Dreams.'"
writer: "blackheart"
slug: "blind-dreams"
source: "AllPoetry"

Find an eye inside a head,
Then let it guide the way.
If blind it will not matter,
For clarity of mind i say...
Is guiding light,
In a world without the sun.

But;

A star so renowned it sparkles,
Diamonds in my hands,
A perfect thought in every one,
Dreams you understand.

All come true... they shall, i say,
Perhaps not as, you had foreseen.
But a dream, you see,
Is well,
A dream.

And;

Find an eye inside a mind,
And for sure it leads the way.
The blind still sleep the same,
As clarity of mind i say...
Is guiding light,
In a world without the sun.
