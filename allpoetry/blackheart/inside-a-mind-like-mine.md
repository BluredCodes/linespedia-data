---
title: "'Inside a mind like mine.'"
writer: "blackheart"

Guided through a sequin dream,
I sparkle at the thought,
Of sharing faultless moments,
A sweet rapport;

An undeniable connection,
A relationship imbued...
With harmony and radiance,
An affinity so true.
Empathy, a bond,
Restricted access none...
Simplistic evolution,
A perfection for our love.

Showered with false prophets,
I think of fading dreams,
Of a sparkle in the moment,
A sweet esteem;

A regard left on waiting wings,
An admiration dead...
Wishing i; a virtuoso,
A skillful emptiness in my head.
Losing sight of you,
I lay my arms to rest...
Excruciating isolation,
A perfection for my death.
