---
title: "'Remember me, remind me.'"
writer: "blackheart"
slug: "remember-me-remind-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

Remind me who i am,
By placing me within your hands,
Holding on,
I miss the way you understand,
This heart; inside,
You still reside.

Help me take a stand,
Build me up to be a man,
Looking strong,
I will always be yours to have,
This heart; torn,
You needn't morn.

For in my mind you stick.
A treacle, something thick.
Fastly stuck i look at you,
And think...

Remind me where you are,
I miss your face, miss your heart,
Always perfect,
Yet i am nothing spectacular,
This heart; you do not need,
So still it bleeds.
