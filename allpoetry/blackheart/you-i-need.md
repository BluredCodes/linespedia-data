title: "'You; i need.'"

Surviving one minute,
Without you!
The ache across my empty palm,
Is killing me!

  If i close my eyes i see you,
     But light sees you erased;
        Break this barrier... please...
           It is only you i need!

Delivering my heart a moment,
Without you!
It's like tearing darknes from the sky,
Like breaking me!

   If i close my eyes you're there,
      But tonight i am not asleep;
         Defending tears... my heart bleeds...
            It's only you i need!

Forsaken by all the silent seconds,
Without you!
Tearing flesh from off of bones to know i'm still alive,
You're killing me!

   If i close my eyes i see you,
      But light sees you erased;
         Perhaps you may return to me... please...
            It is only you i need!

                       The pleasure of my angel,
                       It is for you my poor heart bleeds!
                       The wings upon my angel, mean,
                       You may come flying home to me...
                       For, it is only you i need!For : Molly.
