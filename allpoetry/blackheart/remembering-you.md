---
title: "'Remembering You.'"
writer: "blackheart"

Do you remember,
Dancing just for me?
Just your socks for clothing?
A perfect sight to see!
Smiling at me,
We laughed out loud…
Sad to see these days passed by,
The days I was proud.

Do you remember,
Holding hands with me?
Skipping in the autumn leaves?
A happiness no more to be!
Crying with me,
At the beauty that surrounds…
Hurts to know I cry alone these days,
The days I was proud.

Do you remember,
Promises held in kiss so sweet?
Hearts together beating long?
A thought I will forever keep!
Laying by me,
Dreams in tandem found…
I dream on still, without you by my side,
The days I was so proud.

Do you remember?
Perhaps you do.
Do you think that maybe… one day…
You will return, with heart so true?
Do you remember…
Perhaps you might,
Do you remember how we used to fight?
I don’t.
Perhaps my love, this may explain,
Why I still remember.
