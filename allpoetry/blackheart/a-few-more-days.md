---
title: "'A few more days...'"
writer: "blackheart"
slug: "a-few-more-days"

Breathe my little beauty,
Gasp for air,
Take your time...
Just hold on, and i'll be there.

Watch beside the fire,
Flame over flame,
Warm yourself...
Just smile, and wait.

For our time is drawing nearer,
Everyday another step.
Closer to a bond,
We never shall forget,
A feeling unexplored,
Yet, fully understood.
Aching for the seconds,
We'd spend together if we could!
Sweet beyond my wildest dreams,
The kiss and smile will amaze,
So much to come my beauty,
... some day, some day.

Feel the warmth around you,
My arms, my love,
Holding strong...
Just kiss me, hold me, touch.

Breathe my little beauty,
Gasp for air,
Take your time...
Just hold on, and i'll be there.
