---
title: "'Today i realized.'"
writer: "blackheart"

Pause with me a moment,
While we ponder at the day,
It’s perfect little raindrops,
Floating down our way.
Hold it for a moment,
A falling leaf in stasis,
Gently aging in decent,
So this is what beauty is.

Secure a special moment,
Tempt it with the eyes,
So pure and untouched,
Beautifully refined.
Look with me a moment,
Into your own sweet gaze,
Feel my heart ‘come warm,
If only i could taste...

The beauty that you are,
The wonder, oh so true.
The beauty held behind those eyes...
i didn’t notice yesterday,
But today; i love you.

Walk with me a moment,
Peer into the crowds,
See how people just like us,
Can walk together proud.
Exercise a moment,
The chance we could have had,
And just like them we too,
Could wander hand in hand.

Pretend just for a moment,
You loved me through and through,
Look into my heart,
And try not to confuse,
The tiniest of moments,
i keep because i need to,
With what my love we could have had,
If only i could hold...

The beauty that you are,
The wonder, oh so true.
The moments held within my heart...
Made me notice just today,
That, i love you.
