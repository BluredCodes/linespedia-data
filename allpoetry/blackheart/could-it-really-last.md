title: "'Could it really last?'"

Shadows fall around me,
And show my brightest dark.
Opens up my nightfall,
'I'll take you now', i laugh!
You cannot harm me less,
For pain, i promise you, i have,
No, you couldn't break me more,
You may see red, but i am black.

                 But, there is a sunny side you know.
                 I can laugh and i can smile too.
                 I notice all the beauty,
                 As i also notice you... and i wonder;
                 If things could really last!

I'm on the edge of death,
A knife with trigger quick.
Blood on hands i disappear,
As down i lay in pitch
black nightmare they all made,
Kiss my tearing skin,
Perhaps i could still breathe,
Or at least... begin!

                 But, there is a sunny side you know.
                 I have joy and fun inside!
                 I notice you,
                 And suddenly i shine... and i wonder;
                 Could it really last?For : Molly ... everything has a chance i guess! *s*
