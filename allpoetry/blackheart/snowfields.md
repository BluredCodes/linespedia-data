---
title: "'Snowfields.'"
writer: "blackheart"
slug: "snowfields"

Staring at the sadness in your mind,
An aching little sigh... (tears remain)
Calling me to save you, brighten up your darkend skies.

   i am unable!

         Standing in the snowfields,
         Of this autumn bared existence,
         i'll stand and watch you fade,
         Because i have to.

Why can't you keep me with your smile,
Hold me up a little while... (i'm weak)
White is black, but red is all i seem to see within that aching sigh.

   i am unstable!

         Standing in the snowfields,
         Of the winter captured skies,
         i'll die when you can't keep me,
         Because i have to.

Ready to accept the failure i've become,
i know i've come undone... (completely)
Perhaps if you could stronger be, then again we'd share the sun!

   i am unable!

         Standing in the snowfields,
         Of this spring that never shines,
         i'll watch the way you burn for me,
         Because i have to.

Impress the seperation that between us sure is set,
But, please... don't leave me yet... (i'm ready)
Survival will remember me, and my strength will not forget...

   i am able!

         Standing in the snowfields,
         Of this summer soon to thaw,
         i'll save you from this fallen sky,
         Because i want you!

         i'll take the flames, before i die,
         Because i want to!

         Together stood in snowfield,
         We know that we exist.
         This is it,
         i am able.

         i'll save you... because i want you!
