Violet aura kissing,
The beams across the sky,
An effervescent chorus,
Dancing in your eyes.
A supernova talisman,
Within your warming chest,
i’m crippled by aroma,
As, i lay upon your breast.

Sweet sixteen, remember?
The days where time stood still.
Sweet dreams in September,
A madness they instill...
As i remember, my sweet... sixteen.

Burning fever catching,
Our breath as lips caress,
The moment we’re together,
It is nothing less,
Than a sacred situation,
A togetherness; bound.
Lovers for a while,
Don’t make a sound.

Sweet sixteen remember?
The days without a chill.
Sweet thoughts in September,
A roller-coaster thrill...
As i remember, my sweet... sixteen.
