---
title: "'Sorry?'"
writer: "blackheart"
slug: "sorry"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/21956-Sorry--by-blackheart"
---

Resolute I stand in awe,
Of everything you are.
Perfection in your eyes I see,
A bond I find bizarre.
It’s made me turn my head,
Look twice, and again,
I’m swollen by temptation,
To keep you as my friend.

Perhaps this seems intrinsic,
To some extraordinary plan.
But truly is, but nothing more,
Than a chance to feel grand.
To no more feel plain,
And see a beauty rare,
Folding round you perfectly,
‘Tis this I wish to share.

Remember nothing less,
Than the way you have appeared.
That is to say you’ve brought a smile,
And drawn away a tear.
Please don’t feel tempted,
To ever say too much,
I’m simply happy by you,
I think that is enough.
