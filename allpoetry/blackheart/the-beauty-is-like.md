Have you ever really felt a tear?
Your own?
Someone elses?
It's amazing... believe me.

It's like;

A million moments you could never understand,
A second in a minute that lasts forever.
A hand held out you cannot reach...
A dream.  My dream... to be together,
With you.

It's like;

Waiting for a train that comes but passes by,
So fast... in a moment, gone.
Waiting at a junction for the traffic to drive on,
Waiting for the moon to come after sun has shone.

It's like;

Holding your hand... just in my mind.
Holding a heart for a moment, and feeling the truth.
Holding the earth on your aching shoulder,
Holding on.  Trying not to notice... i was used.

Have you ever felt a tear?
Your own?
No? ... it doesn't ( i hate to be cruel )
It doesn't suprise me.
But, let me tell you now...
It's amazing... 

Believe me;

I cry every day.
No matter what i do.
If nothing else because the beauty all around me... makes me see,
That you are all i ever need.
You are perfect... for me.
You are, if nothing more than this... beauty.
I cry because of this,
Have you felt my tears?
Will you cry too?
Wait with me a moment?
Hold my hand.
Love me?
If nothing else because the beauty all around me... makes me see,
I love you.
