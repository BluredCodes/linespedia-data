---
title: "'i guess this is goodbye.'"
writer: "blackheart"
slug: "i-guess-this-is-goodbye"
source: "AllPoetry"

An instant deprivation from your eyes,
As you walk away again.
A painful respiration as you say...

        Goodbye.
        It's time that i should go,
        Don't cry...
        At least don't let it show.
        For this was going nowhere...
        Goodbye.

Missing inspiration as your heart,
Disappears from me.
A silent realization that this just has to be...

        Goodbye.
        i watch you as you walk away,
        Don't try...
        To look back this way,
        You've emptied me completely...
        Goodbye.

A desperation in the leaving hands,
As you fade away.
This is my starvation for our yesterday...

        Goodbye.
        i touch the air you used to breathe,
        Don't lie...
        i knew that you would leave,
        You've left me feeling empty...
        Goodbye.
