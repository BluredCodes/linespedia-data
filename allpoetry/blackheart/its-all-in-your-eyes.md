---
title: "'It's all in your eyes.'"
writer: "blackheart"
slug: "its-all-in-your-eyes"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/23830-Its-all-in-your-eyes.-by-blackheart"
---

Counter my affliction to your eyes,
By turning fast away.
Remove the saturation,
Before you’re stuck another day.
Attempts to leave me fail,
It’s is more than fair to say,
That come tomorrow I’m aware,
You want not be bound to me this way.

Why is it I have done,
Where and how’d I falter?
My icy little princess…
I’m sorry ever after!?
I know however now,
That it matters not one jot,
I’ll love you  ‘til the day I die…
You?
Will not.

Mirror my affection to your eyes,
By looking one last time.
Speak to me with true intention,
Of leaving me far, far behind.
No more I’ll be your soul mate,
I’m an unimportant mind,
Together now we’ll not set sail,
Instead you’ll go and leave me blind.

What is it I did do,
Can I say I’m sorry fast?
My icy little princess…
It’s already in your past?!
Painful indeed!
Constant stays, it will not stop,
I’ll love you still, I know…
You?
Do not.
