I left the light on last night.
Thinking;
If you suddenly walked in,
I'd see you straight away.
If i quickly was awoken,
I needn't fight for day.
My tears would glisten brightly,
As they rolled along my face.
I'd see the leaves fall slowly,
Behind a window and some filthy lace.
What if you were to come,
While sleeping deep i lay?
Could i realise in my sleep,
And quickly try to wake?
I sit now wishing only,
That your eyes could look so close,
Into my heart and see there,
That you're the one it chose.(thanks Sheri... all is corrected, i always do that with your and you're ... don't know why... thank you).
