Peer beyond the window,
What is it that you see?
A river, boats and people,
Brick buildings and a tree?!

But i see something more,
Through that pain of glass so clear,
i see your sweet reflection,
As through that window, i do peer!

i taste the every moment,
That i get a chance to breathe,
You're turning me in circles,
With the star lit eyes that seem to tease.

Touching for a second,
In some unrequited love,
It appears almost impossible,
That forever could be long enough!

So peer again my angel,
And tell me what you see...
Perhaps you may just notice,
Me!For : Molly.  Thank you for everything.  X x x x x.
