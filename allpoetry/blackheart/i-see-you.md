i stare into your eyes...
What do i see?
i can’t see you,
Looking at me,
But,
	More importantly,
	Down so deep,
	Is the spiraling glint,
	So brilliantly sweet.

It turns me over, knocks me down,
i smile at you gently, you turn around...

		"What?!"

i turn away and laugh,
So happy to be here.
Sitting quiet next to you,
Hoping you won’t disappear.
Praying that it’s not a dream,
As i whisper in your ear,
"Thank you for today."
i wonder if you’ll hear.

i stare into your eyes...
What do i see?
i see you...
i’m happy.For : Molly... thank you for putting a smile back on my face! *s*
