Passing moments in the breeze,
A Chance to breathe?

Lost in voices always turning,
Back on me.

Broken silence?
Heart of glass...
Refract the many feelings,
I allow them to pass on by.
Reflect the feelings...
Kept inside;
No more.
Am so unsure,
Repeat the cure...
However unpure.

Reckless with my expectaion,
No more revelation.

Beside myself in anger,
A darkest adulation.

Uttered ardor?
I do think not...
Lost in simple voices,
A void so unrepentant.
Find a hole to fill in me...
Do not be hesitant;
Tentative.
Cannot live,
Visualize my edge...
Am i dead?

Passing moments in the breeze,
A chance to breathe?

Passing moments slipping by,

... slipping by ...

... slipping by ...

... slipping by ...

Good bye.
