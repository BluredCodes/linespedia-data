Black cats dream,
In night so dark,
The moon does howl,
The dogs do bark.

Jewels so bright,
Placed on the sky,
Cats will come,
They’re black; for die.

Stop all scratching,
Cease fire in my veins.
For the river falls free,
But these cat’s won’t tame.

Inside my black cat dreams…
I fall to die.
Inside this reoccurring nightmare…
I fall to die.This was written in 1993, when i was 13.  Not my first poem, but one of the first... just wanted to add it here, think it gives the impression of where my poetry was leading, less light more dark! *s*
