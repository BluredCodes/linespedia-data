title: "'Emptiness Creep.'"
writer: "blackheart"

Encapsulated by your madness,
I hold aloft a steely glare!
At your magnificent eyes… regardless of their insanity.
Astounding that you are,
The wonder that I find.
Hoping senses will be found… and you will notice me.

Step upon my fingers,
See my face say, ‘ow’.
Remember me that way…
At least for now.
Because until you really touch me,
I am all alone.
Until you wrap your arms in full,
I’m on my own.

Rest bite killing me bit by bit,
Glaring at your steely heart!
The way it beats; still as the moment I die for you.
Production of an answer none,
I fret and worry, on and on!
Please will you discard me… as I do not see the warmth in view.

Sit upon your throne,
Beckon me to jester be.
Remember me that way…
The clown you didn’t really need.
But if you touched me… properly,
I would forever keep,
Your heart wrapped up in perfect love,
Instead I feel the emptiness… creep.

Creep.
Crawl.
Aching once again.
Encapsulated by your madness.
I give in.
