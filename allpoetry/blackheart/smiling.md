title: "'Smiling.'"
writer: "blackheart"

Your eyes are so unique,
With almost...
A voice all of their very own,
They speak... and they say,

'You make me smile,
Thank you.'

A glint from deep within,
It makes...
A moment last forever, stand still,
They amaze... and they say,

'i'll always be here,
For you.'

Think about you all the time,
You stick...
A picture flashing through my mind,
They sparkle... and they say,

'You are on my mind,
Thank you.'

However...

Your eyes cannot be with me,
Your heart is far away.
I wish things could be different,
But they can't.
I wish that you could always stay,
But you can't.
I wish i could be there now,
But i can't.

Still...

It doesn't really matter,
An imagination vivid stands here strong...
And anyway,
You make me smile,
Thank you.
