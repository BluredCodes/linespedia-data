---
title: "'Begin an end.'"
writer: "blackheart"

Masked with an obscurity,
Some, Salvador twist in geometry.
Bending me sideways and stretching me out,
i ache from this hostile monstrosity!

The reflection that i see,
When i peer into the darkness,
Marks me with the simple depiction...
That i am nothing more,
Than the darkness that i stand in,
i’m scarred by some sick black infection.

Raped by the shadowy fingers,
Some milestone by which, i don’t pass.
Savoring moments i’m simply alone,
Before once again into darkness i’m cast.

The ardent exposure i feel,
Compels me to rupture my soul,
Regardless of the picture you paint...
Stroke upon dream,
That will not come true,
As one more line marks me replaced.

Dull, i sit in silence,
Staring at the floor,
Finding little pieces,
Of the life i once adored.
Mesmerized i falter,
As the memories disappear...
My time is up...
It’s over,
ill not be happy here.

As i'm...

Turned before the audience so jaded,
i smile outside the hollow dark within.
Whip me one last time so I may fall upon my knees...
This is it, my time is up, it’s over...

My, death, begins.
