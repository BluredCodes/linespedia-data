"Remembering me."

How would you describe,
The solitary omen,
Falling at your feet,
As you say that you don't know them!
It's calling me to something,
That i would never meet,
Sealing all my fate,
I fall at your feet!
You seem to know the future,
As i crumble clear before you!
The ache inside the sunrise,
Is killing me too!

Justice is an arrogance,
That i adhere to now...
Dancing on your whispers,
Feeling alive! ... don't know how!
Leave me alone!
But touch me...
Deeper than i'm able,
Futher still i see!
Bring me to nothing,
I'm complexity at least,
Your nothing but the angel,
With the demon wings; a beast.

Rage before the failing sky,
I fly inside my fear,
Realise i am never scared,
Because i only hear...
The sentance in your voice,
Conviction so bestowed...
I've never realised this before,
My life to you i owe!
I don't know how i got it here,
The day just stays the same.
I'm falling faster everyday,
Who am i to place the blame?!

How would you describe,
The blatant obscurity,
That my life has led to,
I am nothing but me.
But, you are me.
I'm not sure i get it,
Perhaps you will persist,
Bringing me to this,
The final word,
The last curtain...
Fireworks around me,
I bow in crippling pain,

Surface to the angels,
Glide in perferct sweetness,
No more i'll hear your voice,
Or know his emptiness,
I am an aging slip knot,
A fraying simpleton,
Jumping on my grave,
Knowing i'm forgotten!
Glad though i'll be,
Out of reach of all your nails,
Gone will be the scars,
No more bars, no more rails!

Take me to the sky...
Hold me close and fly!
This be the final moment,
So listen, i'll describe;

The dirty turning clean,
The brown becoming green,
The silence is broken,
The silver is golden,
I'm awake!
Leave me be...
I have become a bloom,
I am me!

Once again i get to be...
