title: "'Looking for a friend.'"
writer: "blackheart"

Eyebrights all surround me,
  I sneer at their sweet smelling fragrance...
The anger stirs inside me,
  Cause be none, my rage remaining latent!

        Embraced by some cool arrogance,
        That MY minds hate the most!
        Seeking home that welcomes me,
        My search goes on, i suppose.

Stir the silent thunder, brewing in my head.
Situate your closing doors,
Replace the welcome arms... with vile breath!

        Roll away my barrel,
        My heart and soul inside.
        Perhaps you never noticed,
        And never realized...

   My anger lays here dormant,
   Waiting to be moved...
   In time it will explode,
   Kill you... i'll be free.

As the eyebrights die around me,
i cry upon their weary stems,
All gone now, i'm left alone...
i; desperate, need a friend.




(a real one!)
