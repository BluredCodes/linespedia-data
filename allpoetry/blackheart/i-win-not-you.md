---
title: "'I win; not you.'"
writer: "blackheart"
slug: "i-win-not-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/33504-I-win--not-you.-by-blackheart"

Anchovy pancake, covered in dew,
Left out in the rain again,
Look at you!
Saturated, carbonated, eradicated life,
Corrosive nature wins again,
Look at you.

False flower bucket, painted in blue,
Left out in the snow again,
Look at you!
Angel hair dandruff, they’re after you,
You're made to look a white old fool,
Look at you.

Mistletoe moments, lips pay the dues,
Left to crack in sun again,
Look at you!
Kissed and missed, naughty, naughty, slap your wrist!
Garbled sentence, a judgment drowned,
Don’t frown...
Look at you.

Your featureless fancies, recluse, head in noose,
A girlie boy, nancy... I win...
You lose!
