---
title: "'Disappearing dreams.'"
writer: "blackheart"
slug: "disappearing-dreams"

Whispering starlight through a long forgotten sheet...
Glass.
A window for my dreams...
Gone today;
i think.

i’ll cry a tear or many thus;

	What i’ve been, what’s come before?
	What i could... i do not know.
	Another tear upon this bed...
	i do, but i don’t want to go!

Lingering moonlight through a soon forgotten sheet...
Duvet.
Hiding windows for my dreams...
Gone always;
i know.

i’ll cry another tear and thus;

	What have i become now?
	Why is the world unjust?
	i know you well, and so i ask...
	Could this heart still love?

Wandering sunlight through an unforgotten sheet...
Lightening.
A fork inside my dreams...
Here today;
i sink.

i’ll find a tear to speak and thus;

	This is, i, remember well.
	No more tears as here i lye.
	Instead i’ll find your hand again,
	To, say one last good bye.
