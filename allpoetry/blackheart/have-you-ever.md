title: "'Have you ever...?'"

Have you ever captured silence,
So persistent in the air? ...
Tearing through the dancing words,
You pretend aren't there.

Have you fallen with an autumn,
And drifted with the breeze? ...
A golden dance within your heart,
As away from me you weave.

The silent dance, the golden air,
My only chance to feel you there...
I miss you.

Have you ever stepped into a dream,
That wasn't really yours? ...
Turned it upity, all around,
Created breaks and flaws.

Have you stood upon a hand,
Time beneath your feet? ...
Desperately just trying,
To prevent our guaranteed defeat!

The stolen dream, the hand of time,
My only chance to say good bye...
I miss you.

Have you ever whispered loudly,
To the trees and found a branch? ...
Laid up high and pondered,
On this slowly fading chance.

Have you seen the dying rainbow,
As the rains begin again? ...
Could this ever lead us nowhere,
As i feel the weather tame.

The whispered cry, the rainbow dies,
My only chance for one last try...
I miss you.

Have you ever... touched me?
Have you ever... loved me?
Have you ever...?

I miss you.For : Molly.  i miss you! *s*
