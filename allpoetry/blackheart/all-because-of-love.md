title: "'All because of love.'"

Silently i stumble,
Across the floor,
Knowing i cannot take,
Much more.
Body simply aching,
A pain i can't endure,
Slipping fast,
i try to hide,
Doesn't last...

Soon to scream i know,
My lungs gasp,
Engulfed in visions,
Of my failing heart.
PLEASE!!!!
Make it stop?
Attempts in vain,
... i have lost.

Slowly to the ground,
i lay my head,
Slumber taking hold,
Remembering what you'd said...
"please take care, my sweet...
i love you."
Your hollow eyes now haunt me;
What
did
you
do!?

You've twisted every ounce,
Of my soul!
i simply do not feel,
Well, i feel cold!
Surrounded by my passion,
Raging at my heart,
Lashing at my head,
Tearing me apart!!

It's time i fall asleep,
Perhaps i will forget!
.....................
Sorry, i heard laughing in my head!
i love you, always will,
Look what you have done,
A tattered man you have made,
And all because of love.
