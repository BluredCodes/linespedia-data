Many hours laid awake,
How many sheep, must it take?
Watching trees all skimming by,
Fields, cow and, ha! Sheep, i spy.

A pointless task i find ahead,
With this journey now, thoughts in my head,
Street lights, houses, factories, more...
Does all this seem a frightful bore?

i hope so... it may just help me sleep,
i’ve gone past a thousand of the damn black faced sheep!
The day’s growing old,
i’m tired,
i ache...

Please let me sleep...

For tomorrow’s sake.

It’s tomorrow in which i wish to wake...

Yet still i’m counting sheep!
