title: "'Shouldn't speak.'"
writer: "blackheart"

Resting in a nocturne warmth...
   my head in emotion; mixes!

On a carpet rose i sit in think,
   with nothing on my mind, but potential fixes.

   Yet...

           i have nothing in my able grasp!

   and i am stuck.

i suppose...     it's nothing much.


i won't ask.
