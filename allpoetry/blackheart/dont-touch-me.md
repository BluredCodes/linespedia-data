---
title: "'Don't touch me.'"
writer: "blackheart"
slug: "dont-touch-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/21003-Dont-touch-me.-by-blackheart"

Watching rain drops falling,
Slowly splash...
One, two... a million more.
It saddens me to see,
The hopes each one may hold,
Soon to disappear,
Hopes wasted to be sure.

My feelings.
Like this.
Constant and abhorrent.
Feeling ever empty,
Take my soul.
I don't want it.
Break my heart,
Piece by piece...
It's broken.

Watching people passing,
One by one...
Miserable and forlorn.
Like a mirror,
But, perhaps they all are happy,
Laughing from within,
At me... out, i'm torn.

My feelings.
Are this.
Ever and always on.
Wishing nothing now,
Tear my heart.
It's useless.
Leave me be,
PLEASE!?
I'm broken.
