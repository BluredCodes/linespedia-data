title: "'Simply, disappearing.'"

Painted with blood,
   i'll dry in bitter sun.
Taste the weakness you have made...
   As now i come undone!

Disappearing!
Breaking... (cracking, cracking)
Failing,
Wasting...
Simply, disappearing.

i want to,

Tear the flesh from off your bones,
   Eat the heart, now on it's own.
Crush the souless bitch you have become,
   Because... you are not the one!

The one i thought you could be.
The sweetness in my eyes.
The perfection glowing ever on,
Brightening my life!
Instead you've eaten everything,
Destroyed my hopes and dreams,
i'm slowly disappearing,
I WANT TO SCREAM!!!

Disappearing,
Crying... (trying, trying)
Failing,
Waiting,
Simply, disappearing.

Take the poison arrows,
the bullet wound you made.
Leave me well alone now,
As i'm simply disappearing.
