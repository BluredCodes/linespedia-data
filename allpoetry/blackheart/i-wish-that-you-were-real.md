---
title: "'I wish that you were real.'"
writer: "blackheart"
slug: "i-wish-that-you-were-real"
source: "AllPoetry"

Seditious with your love,
Rampant, yet divine,
Loyal, never do I think,
I have recognized…
The sweetness in a slumber,
A breath so angel like,
Something perfect to behold,
I am sleeping safe tonight.
Tremor as I near this,
Ancient question long,
Manic to depression,
But I, keep pressing on.
Vibrant with a passion,
Sublime with all my eye,
Sacrificing nothing,
As next to you I lye.
Cavort with sacred mountains,
A chasm in my mind,
Gently pushing forwards,
Together we would climb,
Onward to a peak,
Splendid in it’s chorus,
Reaching out and touching,
Anything before us,
Just to get a grip,
Of a moment in the sky,
Remember every shadow,
And every single line,
As the sun returns to sleep,
We’ll hurry on our way,
To reach a true crescendo,
I close my eyes and pray…
That the dream may never end,
And together we will come,
In a perfect gripping moment,
We are done.
