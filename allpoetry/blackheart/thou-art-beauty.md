Thou art beauty in the eyes of time,
i stand alone, but look deep in to thine heart.
Clutching, grasping... dreaming.
i love thee for the beauty that thou art.

Reclaim the spirit.  T’was lost... twisting, turning.
This spirit be thine soul;
Free.  Acclaimed in abundance of truth.
Lov’ed eyes, swimming in announcement,
The obvious is, obviously clear.
Thine eyes now reclaim what was once was stole.

Thou art beauty.
Like the bird.
Raven fly,
Turbulent sky.
Soaring high...
Grace and calm through the eyes of thine heart.

Thou art,
As graceful as the ravens wings.
Thou art,
Beauteous more, than this song you do sing.

Thou art beauty in mine eyes...
i stand with wistful sigh as the birds all flutter by.
Wishing, hoping... dreaming.
For the heart that holds the beauty that thou art.Okay... so it's not Shakespeare... but, it was worth a try, right?! *s*
