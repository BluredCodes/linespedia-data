Intrepid I am not.
I walk with broken glass upon my soles.
I always look behind me.
Just incase.
It’s making me younger by the way it makes me old!
Replace…
My failing heart,
And place a rock instead,
I wouldn’t worry, shake and fear,
Every time I have to leave my homestead.

Shhhh, don’t tell.
Okay.

Intrepid I am not.
Stare into my failing sun… it’s shine is nearly done.
Perhaps they really fucked me.
I need embrace.
But all I find is thorn upon your skin, it hurts to touch!
Look upon my face…
Does it appear a wreck?
If I could just…

Shhhh, don’t tell.
Okay.

Intrepid I am not.
Same old shit I remember… can’t you help me to forget?
Of course you can’t, I’m sorry.
I might break!
So don’t touch me… trust me, your faith in me you would regret!
GIVE ME SOME FUCKING SPACE!
I WANT…

Shhhh, don’t tell.
Okay.

Intrepid I am not,
I have a tongue within my mouth… but I do not have a voice.
Surprises don’t surprise me now.
It is too late, (shit) …Back then I should of screamed… made some noise!
I still taste,
The…

Shhhh, don’t tell.
Okay.

Intrepid I am not.

Shhhh.
