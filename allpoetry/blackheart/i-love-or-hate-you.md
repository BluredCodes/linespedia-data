---
title: "'I love or hate you.'"
writer: "blackheart"
slug: "i-love-or-hate-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/30529-I-love-or-hate-you.-by-blackheart"
---

Replenish my resentment for your face…
Knowing like the mist in tree you’ll clear.
Remind me of your nefarious taste…
I adored… my lips connect with you no more!
Cast aside your judgments of my life…
Stand alone in heather fire, cold.
Invent the morals you could never, ever keep right…
As I grow ever on and old…
That’s fine, I guess… but I love you.
I mean, that’s fine, I guess… but I hate you.

Surprise me with your anecdotes…
Pointless scripts of pasts and yesterday.
Remember how the songs I wrote…
Were all about, all for, always… you.
Temptation growing stronger, longer…
Can only see your hands when I look at mine!
Devoured by your flawless ways to be stronger…
A strength that I will never find…
That’s fine, I guess… but I love you.
I mean, that’s fine, I guess… but I hate you.

Entangle all my heart strings with your pick…
Make me fluster, flutter, ache and wheeze.
Hold me in my mind, it’s completely perfect…
I wish, I wish to touch your skin my sweet.
The clock may see your decision being altered…
When you see that I will beg no more.
Perhaps and only then you’ll see… you’ve faltered…
Yet still my heart is torn…
That’s fine, I guess… but I love you.
I mean, that’s fine, I guess… but I hate you.
