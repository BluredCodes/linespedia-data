---
title: "'Always Longing.'"
writer: "blackheart"

Longing for your pretty smile,To capsulate me, once again,Waiting 'til the end of days,To hold you tight again.Hoping for a moment to find us both,So we'll smile again...Together.I know it's just a dream... but it's a good one.Wish that you were with me,As tonight again i sleep,Repeating many strokes once more,When we are close to sleep.Thinking that you'll maybe,Touch me when we goto sleep...Together.I know it's just another dream... but it's good.Wondering if you'll notice,The way i loved your perfect kiss?Wondering if you'll remember,The warmth in cold December.Wondering if you'll pay attention,To the simple adoration...I keep of you,Hold for you,No matter what you ever do,Whether sky be black or blue,I hold for you,A simple adoration, that keeps my heart beat...Still.Longing for that pretty smile,To come again.Longing for you and only you,To come again.My beauty, with eyes so sweet,Where be you now?No longer can my search go on, i must retreat.For;I know now you don't want me.You have much that you must do.And do it must be done alone.Understood, yet...I miss you.
