---
title: "'Dance in the Dark.'"
writer: "blackheart"
slug: "dance-in-the-dark"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/39174-Dance-in-the-Dark.-by-blackheart"
---

i am ballerina, leg in the air,
Dancing so lightly,
Somewhere, somewhere.
Catching the eye, in the night,
Stepping so soft,
Anywhere, anywhere.

i am singer, song in the wind,
Voicing so gently,
The spirit within.
Touching the hearts, all around,
Taking you tenderly,
To a peace we can rest in.

i am angel, with wing in the cloud,
Holding you delicate,
With the heart you allow.
Jumping from every single pain,
Turning it temperate,
And forcing it out.

i am cupid, bow in the hand,
Firing slowly,
To bring love to the sad.
Nothing can stop all the arrows i bear,
Witness the gentle affections...
Understand.


... Then, all of a sudden ...

i am devil, evil in the air,
Murdering lightly,
Somewhere, somewhere.
Slicing and dicing the souls of the night,
Reaping so soft,
Anywhere, anywhere.

i am ballerina, with glint in the eye,
Dancing in some sugary, sweet lullaby,
Catching the moment you dance in the dark,
Voicing my prance with gentlest remark,
Touching your heart in a sunshiny beam,
Holding the image, to catch all your dreams...
Jumping from every single new soul,
Firing slight, i can’t stop this new role,
Nothing prevents this eye turning black...
Murdering ballet, i dance on your back,
Slicing you inwards and outwards, you die,
i am devil, with glint in the eye.
