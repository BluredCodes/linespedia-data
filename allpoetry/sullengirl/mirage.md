---
title: "mirage"
writer: "sullengirl"
slug: "mirage"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

the road is grim
but with so many memories
that once made it bright
like colors
like a rainbow
pictures that once gave it life..

Sticks and stones I want to throw
at images that once brought me life
But only 'cause I need to touch them,
I need to feel them..

the road is grim
except for what I see
nothing but a mirage
once so alive
like colors 
like a rainbow
memories that once gave me life..
