so you just
want to forget it
wasn't worth
any trouble at all
seems you only
wanted me
for all the good times
that we had
for the happy
not the sad
or bad
When you love
you share the pain
the sunshine
and the rain..
until death
you work it out 
every fight
you see it through
so you just
want to forget it
wasn't worth
any trouble at all
and you don't
want to try
doesn't matter
anymore..
and you don't
want to hurt me
Thanks for that..
