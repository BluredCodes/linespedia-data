title: "all over the sea"
writer: "sullengirl"

so how do you feel
how do you really really feel
the question's getting old
as am I
sometimes I care
but then other times
the fish get tangled in my hair

but you don't tug so hard
and as my pleasure runs deep
my passion goes deeper
the question is bobbing
up and down
so how are you now

Oh that I were a mermaid
when the water turns 
from warm to chill
that I may be still
in such peace & tranquility
that I may love it so

so now you know
how it is that I feel
when the water glows
at the touch of your hand
it's got to be a real distraction
it's got to be something spectacular

but it's glowing all around
when the sun goes down
all over the sea

Oh, that I would see..There is a sea off the coast of Jamaica that glows at the touch of a hand. So at night you see nothing but darkness and then this bright blue light and you know someone is there. So the poem is metaphoric and when I say "it's glowing all around" it is to say "there are other people" or "other fish in the sea." Oh, that I would see..
