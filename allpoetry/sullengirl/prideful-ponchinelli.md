Prideful Ponchinelli..

In the eyes of a ponchinelli
I am less than perfect,
He is less than perfect
In the eyes of God.

In the eyes of a ponchinelli
He is always right
And you are always wrong
No matter how sad your song

Until you cry
He doesn't know the meaning of grim
It's all just gaiety
And we are all still infants
In the eyes of a ponchinelli
