you speak to me
and the trees seem to grow
it's like they know
you're giving them room in the sky

and when you lie..
i only smile
my heart still soars
it goes on for miles

you speak to me
then i feel your touch
like i feel the air
softly filling me up

and when you lie..
i don't really care
i just want to know
if you really really care.
