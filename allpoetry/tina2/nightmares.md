Awoke by screams;
igniting in my dreams.
What is it about the mourning sounds;
that come to me in my sleep?

Tormented cries;
I sit up and rub my eyes.
Who are these faces, I see?
How do they know me?
God I pray, that I, in your arms you keep.

Sullen faces worn thin;
destroyed by sin.
What is it that makes these dreams so real?
Why is it that this I can feel?
Satan, ' GET AWAY FROM ME! '

Peacefulness is brought;
a smile is caught.
Happiness shines through, while I sleep.
Peacefully in God's arms; his word, he did keep.
