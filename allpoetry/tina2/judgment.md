JudgmentIn the winds of the flurry there came about a sound.A trumpet was playing we were home bound.The light from Heaven was released through the sky.Jesus has returned to take home you and I.Oh what a glorious day this will be.The Almighty God, we will finally see.As we lay our worldly crowns at his feet.Life we have now surpassed, the devil we did defeat.Times of sorrow no longer will we bare.Heavens purity we will now share.Upon the streets paved for all who will hear.No more burdens shall we carry, no longer will we have to fear.The path to Heaven reverberates through our land,as the mourning of a tragic loss begins to join hands.No time shall we pass or live another a day without the Lord.The train is moving quickly make sure you are on board.Spoken in the book of life, he said there would come a day.That he would take a look at all his children heading in the wrong way.He would lift His protective hand and a sacrifice would be made.Lifted up to Heaven to be with him would be those who were saved.Our world is full of everything he is not for.Open up your life to Jesus, don't leave him knocking on the door.Repent of your sins, He will forgive you.Open up others eyes, to a life born anew.The day of the coming of Jesus, one will never know.But the world is showing all the signs that it's time to go.Those who listen to his word and obey them with all their might.Will not be left behind to face the horrendous sights.In the end years, man will face much pain and grief.Forced to take the mark by the most practiced thief.He knows all about trickery and despair.For no one on this Earth does this wicked one care.He knows he is destined to eternal damnation.For it was written in the Bible before our creation.He wants lots of company in hell so he will try to veer you in.Tempting and teasing, tormenting you with sin.He tries to veer you in every which way he can.Destroying the morality of every man.Children are killing each other parents are killing their own.Divorces are common in the average home.Witchcraft and evil are televised every day.Condoms are handed out in our schools, yet these same children are not allowed to pray.Alcohol has consumed so many lives today.Drugs have killed many, leaving families in dismay.Churches judge each other listen as you walk down the street.God said do not judge another by the food he does eat.What this is; is a parable to one’s faith or belief.If you say one is wrong that will only bring you grief.There is one God, who is waiting for the day of man to come.Where he will send down his once sacrificed Son.There will not be a Baptist door that waits for you in Heaven.There will not be a Buddha door, the one that you knocked on while you were living.There will not be a Catholic door to walk through after mass.There will not be a Jehovah witness door for only those with class.There will not be a Presbyterian door to open with your hand.There will not be a Muslim door that stands open so grand.There will not be a Jewish door with a lock and key.There will only be one way in for all who come to see.It will be the entrance to the glorious Heaven above.Where God is patiently waiting to show us all his love.Do not judge another by the food on his plate.For if you were fed the same thing, you would feel you had a clean slate.People all over the world live as they are taught.Religion is a belief, not a manly based thought.So steer clear of judgement, for we will all be accountable for it in the end.Watch carefully over your actions and ask to be forgiven of sin.Help a stranger out or even invite someone to church with you.In the end Jesus will come with open arms for you.
