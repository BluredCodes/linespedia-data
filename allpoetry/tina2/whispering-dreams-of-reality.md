What is ones dream, consisting of?
Lots of fame or lots of love?
Dreams are of things imaginary that we long for.
But in what direction do we strive more?
Reality in such, is a dream we can catch.
Setting the truth aside from the rest.
How is it then dreams do partake.
At the feet of the ones who put everything at stake?
Abiding by what lies inside their heart.
Ambition and drive from the very start.
Desire to achieve what one feels to be.
Something possible for all to see.
A dream at last lying in the midst.
Years of ones heart and soul did persist.
One can make a dream come true.
One such as I or you.
Our heart is set to achieve certain things.
Regardless whether it be reality or dreams.
If the course you choose is embedded in your soul.
Then no matter the recourse you will not let go.
Stand strong, stand proud your dream will become true.
Reality can't escape us, nor take your dreams from you.
Walk with God in your journey to dream.
With God there is no one impossible thing.
So fight with your heart, mind, body and soul.
Then your dreams will become reality and you wont have to let go.Written October 16th, 2001 ©  on Oct 16 2001 07:05 AM PST, Tina Leonard   spiritual
