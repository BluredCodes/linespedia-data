What is it about a rainbow,that leaves a smile on your face?Or washes away burdens, from a deep down place?What is it about the rain,that comes before this sight?That takes away the blue of the sky,to return this light?What is it about the pot of gold,that waits at the end for all?A wondrous thought,yet such an obstacle.How do we find it,Before the road disappears?I have thought about this journey,for years.Once conclusion I have stumbled upon,may seem far fetched.But in that sky, I see the joy,of Heaven so seldomly etched.The beauty of what will,be to come.That is for those,who know the Holy one.God gives us the light,so that we can see.And sais, ' You are welcometo walk with me. 'Follow the light,that leads to the gold.Years and years,it has forever been told.Walk the path,that shines the way.To our home in Heaven,where we will forever stay.
