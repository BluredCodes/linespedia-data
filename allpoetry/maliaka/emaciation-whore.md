blank reflection
broken smile
don't be afraid to run that extra mile
(nothing to smile about)
blind association
try to get rid of the doubt
a glance in the mirror
can't even see
fake and whithered
confusing trapped with free
it's easy to ignore
"everything is fine"
don't want to be a bore
to that crowded room
what are you even faking this smile for?
it's all gonna get fucked up anyway
you'll just end up here
still an emaciation whoreHi everyone! This is my first poem submitted here. I chose this one because, for one, it is nice and short, and because it's quite imperfect. I wrote it when I could care less about the meter or rhyme scheme. I felt strongly the emotion, and so I never had the nerve to alter it to something more "perfect"....Rate this and be honest!
