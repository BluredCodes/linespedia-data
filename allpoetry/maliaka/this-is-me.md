title: "This is me"

this is me 
sitting in the dark
letting the blackness cover my being
this is me
mistaking bleeding for seeing
this is me
sorry melodramatic excuse of a girl
this is me
still overwhelmed by every corner of the world
this is me-mistaking fleeing for being
if this is me,
tell me who the hell I am
this is me
always running for cover
peering over every shoulder
in constant search of a new fad
this is me
with a face that doesn't laugh like a ghost
with a smile that isn't forced
If I'm happy then why do I look so sad?
this is me
with a constant struggle to fight back tears
I only scream when I hope no one hears
this is me, who am I to you?
Drown out the pain with blood
starve away the shame like a drug
cheating a new tomorrow
with every vice that rids the sorrow
this is me
