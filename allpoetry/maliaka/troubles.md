Trouble let me be
Oh please, please let me free
Oh trouble let me be
The only one being punished here
Is me
Oh trouble, let me breathe
It feels so constricting and tight
Suffocating
Oh, let me be alright
Trouble, oh what is wrong?
You've been coming to me
Nights like these for so long
Oh, nothign bad happened
So, trouble
Why are you doing this to me?
Oh trouble, please leave
Oh trouble, please set me free
'Cause nothing feels wrong
And I've been hurt by you
For so long
Oh trouble, Please
Oh trouble, maybe the only one I'm in trouble with is me
Oh trouble, set me free
Let me breatheI can't decide if this is a poem or a bluesy song...what'cha think?
