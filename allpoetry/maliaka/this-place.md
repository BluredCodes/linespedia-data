In this place\
sitting in limbo
wanting darkness to invite me in
wishing my need was to have light
I don't know what to do
What can I say?
I just want to be taken away
Some where
but I'll never know
the place I need to be in
This Place
is scary. but in a way
It's for the best
I'm not numb
But I'm not too alive to see
that what I want is to go back
back into the world I was in
When I used everything 
to make me more numb
but I can't figure this out now
In a good way
I just want to disappearWhen I wrote this it was just thoughts...not an actual poem...but I guess it's kinda poetic anyway, lol:)
