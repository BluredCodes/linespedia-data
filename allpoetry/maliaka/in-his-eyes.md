The night is still
with only sounds of trees
and soft melodies
to warm me
trees echo with whispers of yesterday
the make me remember
all the things he's told me 
Beautiful and full
and rich with color
without those memories
the air would lose it's breath
and die
crossing my arms now,
hand on chin
the truth comes to me
so suddenly
he keeps me alive
though I don't need his touch
to calm me
his energy fills me more than anything
the night is darker
it's embers fill the room
much like the embers
of his eyesHi everyone!
