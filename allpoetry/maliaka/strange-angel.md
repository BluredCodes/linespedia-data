words don't touch you
as boldly as I'd like
you're wisdom is lacking my heart
I haven't tried to impress you
but my thoughts can't be used by you
~~leaves tangled in your hair
your love deserves what's fair
from me I can't begin to start
you gift to you is barely art
your opera days and acoustic ways
they tear my heart apart

what can I do
what do you need from me
you need nothing
the perfection in your eyes
too fulfilled to see
that I can't do this anymore
my Jeckel and Hyde ideals start wars
the space you give is too far apart
to tear me apart
but it's what you do
took away the black covered blue
made my imagination take flight
in my reward for you I find
nothing I try to do
to match your heart is rightWhen I first wrote this I liked it because it was a different style than I was used to writing in. But now read it over and I'm actually ambarast to re-type it here, lol:) Believe me, this is not my best! :)
