---
title: "Pixels"
writer: "Netsubo"

Red and Blue...
Swirl across my line
Mixing with the hot Whites and Oranges..
Flirting with the Greens and Yellows.

Has this time come already?
Judgment is to be given
In such a short time...
With such horrid odds...

A pair of eyes stare back
But only a few pixels.
So trite...now...
Beauty was once scarce.

The world is being molded
Smaller and smaller
While our cities grow...
Higher and higher

Soon.. with a quick touch of a button
The world will be in your hands
And the love of one-hundred lost lives
“Found”

You say we can play God
And that it’s all for a better purpose
Maybe your dead ears will hear...
A laugh aimed at you from hell

Deep down you’re scared
Wee see it.. That twitch..
You’ll fall soon
But that won’t stop the fight

THIS HAS JUST BEGUN
