Tremble on my own
Shivering deep down in the Ice
It all covers me..
In such a glassy suit
Shining clear armor
So cold and numb and gone
You cant even see my face
It’s faded into the shadows..
Forever trembling!
You’ll never find my heart
‘Cause you cant see what’s not there!
Oh sweet ice..
Keep this covering
Diluted and so cold
Keep it sweet as blood..
As sweet as the deep red fire
Burning through my veins
But filled with such desire..
Everything is falling out
It’s all flying in..
You’ll never see my glare..
From deep within the cave
I’m deep inside your heart
But you’ll never know my pain!
Run and fly and run
And run and cry and hide
No matter how far you go..
I’ll be waiting..
For one day you’ll ender that cave
Deep within my mind
And I’ll show you the hate and pain..
Why im trembling..
