---
title: "Moonlight In My Heart"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "moonlight-in-my-heart"
source: "AllPoetry"

Running through the ocean
Letting the waves cover me
Saturate me in earths blood
Bringing me closer to my beginning
Glancing back to the material world..
One last look at it..
I jump into the ocean
Letting the smooth current take me..
On a course never traveled before..
Such vivid and pure energy..
Like sunlight flowing through my veins
Moonlight illuminating the dark crevices in my heart
Showing me how to fix this..
Giving everything except experience..
Jumping from the earths bloodstream
The past glance WAS the last..
Through these eyes
My vision..
So much clearer
Changed from distortion
Everything is visible now..
A smile crosses my dripping lips
My body covered in moisture..
Covered in energy..
Scanning the beach
And the surrounding woodlands
I stare menacingly at the sun..
And soften my brow
My hearts dark spots hold the moonlight from before..
Never again will they lose their luminance
I jump off into the trees
And start anew..
