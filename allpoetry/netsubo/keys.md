---
title: "Keys"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "keys"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/46320-Keys-by-Netsubo"
---

Sit down again..
Stare at the page
Didn’t this used to be a jumble..
Of characters never seen before?

The black and white..
These were once ivory
Wait..
Too long ago..

Turn the dial and vision is given
A quick glance at the paper..
And a tense in my fingers
Resting on the black and white voices of heaven

Slow and melodic
Pulsing.. along with my veins
The voice of an angel is heard
But the healing power wanes as the moon

Ups and downs..
Nocturnal demons interrupt my waltz with heaven
Such a wonderful gift, and I dont know how to repay..
To speak in such a language..  without words..

A quick trill and the melody ends
Flying into a scale..
Emotion pours through my hands and arms
So many feelings placed upon these keys..

What would I have done..
All those years..
Without a face to turn to
And only this seat, keys and pedals..

Such a harmony emanates
And I feel as if I’m lifted up to the sky
Too bad this song couldn’t last longer..
Too bad I fell so quickly, at the last tone..

Another tear crosses a love filled face
And the pain of rejection is apparent in her eyes..
If only I could help.
But I’m her son, and can only give her a shoulder..
To lean upon

A few kind words
And a quick hug show I wont repeat..
The actions others have imposed upon her
A bit of comfort is felt, I hope

A glance back at the keys..
They must rest for the night
Along with the guardian angels of this house
A blue mist covers our bodies in protection
Yet I still feel the pain..
Of another’s sorrow..
