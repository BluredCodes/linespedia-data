---
title: "Forget The Sun"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "forget-the-sun"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/7998-Forget-The-Sun-by-Netsubo"
---

So confused..
As to..  where im going
Paths in every direction
All going towards the horizon

So uncertain..
This is all dark
Will the path I take lead me
To the sunrise?

On my face
A look of pain
You wonder why..
When i see your face.. Gone..

Stomach dropping
Feeling empty once more..
Tears on the verge of escaping
But I hold them in..

Ill cry them for you another day
A day when I’m strong enough
A day when I can accept myself..
A day when.. I can live..

Hope deep within my heart
And I cant to take it out..
Without killing myself
So I’ll leave it there..

When am I gonna need it?
No clue..
Spinning
I’ll fall soon.. 

Stop!
Regaining the bearings..
The sun is out now
Can I make it?

Smirking..  I turn..
The rays of heat spraying across my face
Haven’t felt this in so long..
I can make it..  I can reach the horizon..

A quick tense...  legs rock solid
Dash! Away from the crossroads
Im running towards the sun once more
This time I wont stop..

Before was a dream..
Why else would I be limited?
I know I’ll never touch the fiery sphere..
But does that matter?

I’ll get as close as possible..
I’ll spread my wings and fly off into space
Cant keep the stones on my ankles anymore..
So I cut through them

Faster and faster..
Gasping..  I’m at the rainforest..
Little does humanity know..
I’ll find what im looking for in here

Forget about the sun..  It’s impossible..
Earth is beautiful..  I see the eyes off in the distance
Dash! I’m here
Where to go now?  Anywhere..
