---
title: "Petty Bullshit I Call Life"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "petty-bullshit-i-call-life"
source: "AllPoetry"

My mind descends into this nothingness
This black abyss surrounds me
Covering me in its dark cold
Sharp steel penetrates my open wounds
I look in your eyes and ask you
Why?  why do you do this to me?
All i want is some comfort
And all i get is pain and suffering!!!
I pull my rage out of my chest
Fill my emptiness with anger and hate
Pure FIRE for you
Only for you..
I tie you down, just like you did to me
I rip your skin off, just like you did to me
I spit flem in your face, just like you did to me
I pound you over and over again, just like you did to me!!
Revenge is not the word for what I feel
Because of you, my world collapsed
Because of you, my mind fell apart
Because of you, i lost my heart
Because of you, i feel as if im dead
Now, i start anew
Right after i torture you!!!
Right after i tear through your skin with my teeth
I will become stronger, smarter, bigger
Better than you’ll ever be
So fuck you, and fuck your superficial bullshit
You live off of that
Thats why im killing it!
Your life depends on bullshit
Because your fake, you arent real
You left me alone in the dark
You left me to die and scream in agony
You left me alone
You left me, ill never leave you
Ill always be your shadow
Following you through life
Reminding you of what you did to me
Making your life a hell
Making you wish you could die
But ill keep you alive
Ill never give you what you want
Ill fuck you over every way possible
You’ll never have happiness
You’ll never have what you took from me
You’ll never have my heart again
