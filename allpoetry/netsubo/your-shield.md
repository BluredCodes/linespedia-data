title: "Your Shield"
writer: "Netsubo"

High pitched ringing off in the distance..
Yet sounding so close..  So clear..
Penetrating.. What is this?
Humanities manifestation? Foreshadowing?

So unsure of my footing..
Is the ground steady?
Will it fall once more..
Out from under my feet..

Do I care anymore?
I can climb up through the dirt..
Using the roots and stones for footing
No more do I need your..  Protection
