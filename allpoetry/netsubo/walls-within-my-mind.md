---
title: "Walls Within My Mind"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "walls-within-my-mind"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/4106-Walls-Within-My-Mind-by-Netsubo"

The sweet taste..
It consumes my taste buds
Tantalizing my needs
My need for death
My need for anger
My needs...
Quenching..  this blood
It runs all over my body
Covering me in its filth
Numbing my pain
Holding in this small warmth..
Close to my core
My soul..
Such hatred..
Such anger..
Such denial..
Such self despising feelings...
All consuming
Turning my mind from reality
Facing it towards fantasy
Facing towards beauty
Facing towards happiness
Facing towards solitude
Facing towards fallicies!
Facing towards fake pleasure
Facing towards a deep black abyss
Changing my soul.. I rip through this wall
Even though it doesnt exist in reality
My mind is so warped..
It only exists in my psyche
I rip through another wall within my mind
Another..  another..
Until i reach my soul
I open my palms..
Delicately..  i caress it
Moving it to my heart
My core emanates bright beams of light
Bright beams of warmth...
Changing my soul back
To how it once was
To what it once was
Before my life turned into a fallicy
Before my life was a lie
Before my existence was fake
