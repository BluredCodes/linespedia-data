It was you who kept me from going insane
It was your love that kept me alive
I never knew...
I never knew I was asking too much of you
I never knew that I expected too much of you
I never understood that I wasn't right for you
I never understood that I didnt matter that much
Not until now...
I never knew that wanting to see your eyes was too much
Never knew that wanting to hear your soft voice in my ear was too much
Never knew that I wanted too much
