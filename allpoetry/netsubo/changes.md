Rip me a new..HeartIt was an empty crevice..AnywayContinue the painIm starting to..Like this..  Want this..TortureThis hate and rage..These tears and sweatThey stain and taint..My heart and soulAll you see in meAll you expect of meIs a lieYou will fall..  Just like me..
