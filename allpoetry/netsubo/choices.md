Wandering..
Flowing through the sky
Flying beneath the stars
Beneath the bright shining moon..

For this night..
Just this one night
Everything goes away
And my mind is clear..

God.. Thank you so much for this..
Chance..  to live for once..
I ask of one thing..  One thing
Someone..  to share this star-lit night

For this once..
My heart can be pure
Please..  let this happen
Give me back that feeling in my heart

I miss it..
That warmth..
Covering my body
Fighting off my loneliness..

It is here no more
I'm left alone..
I fend for myself
To fight on my own..

Now..  Im getting used..
To this.. being alone
So go ahead..  send no-one in my direction
I'll kill you along with all the others

You abandoned me..
Left me to rot out at sea
You left me to drown in the depths..
But you were wrong
