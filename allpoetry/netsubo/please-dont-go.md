---
title: "Please Don't Go"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "please-dont-go"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/15230-Please-Dont-Go-by-Netsubo"
---

I’ll miss you with all my heart
If you leave me..
In such a short amount of time
You gave me reason to live
And I can’t stand the fact
That I might lose you..
Cause You gave me life
And You gave me hope
Long gone..
Without you
Tears have flowed from my eyes
Because its been to hard
But you take them away..
With the comforting words you give me
I would give a piece of my soul..
To keep you by my side
Dear friend..
Can’t believe
How this has happened to suddenly
And I’ll think of how you smiled
And helped brighten my day
Can’t believe
That someone brought me off the floor
Can’t believe
You were the reason I threw the knife away
Can’t believe..
I didnt take it all away
Can’t believe..
That you might be gone..
