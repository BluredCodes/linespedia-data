---
title: "Forever"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "forever"
source: "AllPoetry"

A quick run through the forest
And a skeleton from the past holds within..
Marvels, through which you may see
Into the history, of our souls

A few falls..
Expected, however
Not too painful in the least
Quite comical.. as my companions humbly exclaimed

Too bad..
Those times are gone
And I can’t even remember how..
We left that state, of euphoria

We were expected back..
But a “little” late..
Couldnt you stay there..
Forever?

A child is held within the eyes..
Of a rigid pane of glass
And motherly love..
Within the eyes of a child

Little do I know what is in me
A mirror is not a looking glass
For mercury pupils will let me know..
What is held inside

Too bad..
I miss those times..
When a run along that line was so sweet
And took the pain away

But now i can simply see..
All too clear
I lied...
To myself, along with everyone else.

Couldn’t you stay there..
Forever?
I would’ve loved to
But can I backtrack..  I hope..

Couldn’t you stay there..
Forever?
Hurry..  The forest calls
That concrete giant is but a monster

We need not heed it’s call
Attention it seeks..
And dies off of ignorance
Please..  run with me..

My friends
Come to the forest again
I miss the ferns and trees..
Letting the sunlight come down in patches..
Across the faces of newborn children

We would’ve stayed there..
But forever?
We would’ve stayed there..
If we knew this would come..I wish i knew what was going on lately..  maybe its just me, maybe its everyone else, maybe its everyone.  not much seems right though..  i'm sorry for how i've been to you all..  I love you with all my heart, never forget that..  youre the reason im here right now
