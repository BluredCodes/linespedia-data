---
title: "Looking at the Stars"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "looking-at-the-stars"
source: "AllPoetry"

Just here..
Wondering where im gonna go
Is it gonna start again?
Or will i open up to a new page?

I just know it..
If i open my arms..
Will the knives be thrust deep once more?
So..  unsure..  of my bearings

Looking up to the stars
Which have helped me countless times...
Their message is clouded..
And unclear..

This seems so strange
To have..  something like this
Someone.. being truthful
Its so..  comforting

Yet..  I feel uneasy
Since I’ve felt this way before
Since..  this has hurt me before
I look down to the ground
Beckoning mother earth to yield..
All the motion
To open a door quickly for me
So I can dash into the space..  Away..

This place right now
Getting better
I like this..
So much tension..  calming..

At first, i wanted that there
It kept me safe
Kept me looking behind my back..
But for now..  ill take a break..

For now..  i think
I think ill be able to live
Without paranoia or hate in my heart
Ill live..  with hope..
