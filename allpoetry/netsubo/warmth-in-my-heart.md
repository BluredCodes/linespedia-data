---
title: "Warmth In My heart"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "warmth-in-my-heart"

Running through this field
I search for my life-line
Yet I find nothing
Although I am exhausted
I cannot stop searching...
Searching for my reason...
My reason for living...
My life depends on this warmth
Sometimes in my travels
I catch a small sliver of it
Of the warmth...I feel a bit in my heart
We all search for this
Our place in life
The unending field yields unending possibilities
Search long enough
And you will find it
That overall feeling of happiness...
Solitude...Peace...Love...
Whatever is your deepest desire
Search...Yearn...Need...Love...
And you will feel this
And the pain of life will end
