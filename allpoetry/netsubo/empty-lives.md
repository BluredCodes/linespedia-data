---
title: "Empty Lives"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "empty-lives"
source: "AllPoetry"

Heart..
Such a powerful thing
Yet some live without it..
How can this be?

Soul..
So amazing and intriguing..
Yet with some it is empty..
I can't comprehend..

Happiness..
Without reason
Depression..
Without reason

Everything with no reason
Yet its all this “master plan”
Go ahead..  think that..
Don't try and figure yourself out..  be lazy

Live without a heart..
Live without a soul..
Have happiness and depression..
For no reason..

Don't care about your life..
Dont be thankful
While you're here
Close your eyes

Run away from the truth
Run away from me..
See how much I care
Youre selling your soul in the end..
