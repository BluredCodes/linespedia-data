title: "Between the Lines"

A mistake from the beginning
I try and understand your words..
But the sound is blurred by the screams..
Of my own mind

All I see is red...
And the pain creeps..
Into once unscathed areas
Of my soul..

Trying to define
The difference between love and hate
In the book of our lives..
I stare at the sentences..

I live in between the lines
And look up and down
Trying to find a difference
But only seeing one feeling for me...
