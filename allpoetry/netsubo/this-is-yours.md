Run away!Close your eyesYou cant see the lightYou cant see my lightYou know you want it..Come on..  come after me..I’m waiting for youIn the middle of the highwayYou know you want it..Im vigil and staringAwaiting for your returnSo i I can keep my promiseTruth..  hah..  you dont know itLove..  you’ll never understand itHate..  you think you know..  you’ll never feel itRage..  you dont wanna find out..  i’ll show youFire in my eyesHatred in my heartDenial in my soulAll pointed towards youMy arms outstretchedReaching for the unreachableClosing in on the goalBut ill never get thereYou walk over me..And say that you feel painThe shards of glass and concretePenetrating my skin..  yet i dont feel..That pain is in the past..Old..  and known all too wellYou’ll know one dayThat this highway..  is your deathbed
