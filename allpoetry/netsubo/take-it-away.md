---
title: "Take It Away"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "take-it-away"
source: "AllPoetry"

So pointless
No need to go on...
So worthless
Not like I could if I tried.
So tired
Can’t even get off the floor.
So rip my skin apart
And pour my life across the floor
See the smile that crosses my face
And I’ll laugh,
As I fall to my knees...
And finally let go
Of the pressure
