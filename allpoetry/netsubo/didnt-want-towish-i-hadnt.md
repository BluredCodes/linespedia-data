I miss writing of love.
Those sweet feelings,
That saturated my every word,
Kept that mode going.

A nocturne,
Slipped through my lips
Every night.
Those words were made...
Thinking of you.

And those feelings stayed in my mind,
Resided in my heart.
Tomorow..
I can remember...364 days ago.

Should've known,
With those black words
On the white surface...
Should've seen the future.

But my arms still held you close,
And these memories will be forever.
I've gotta learn...
That this is my place...for now.

A pair of brown eyes whispered to me,
Yet I didn't heed their call.
Your scent held me
Like the covers of a warm bed,
On a cold winter morning.

Didn't want to let go...
Didn't wanna walk away...
But I had to.
Had to walk away.
I'm sorry it turned out this way...

Wish I held on
And showed you it was right

Wish I hadn't..
