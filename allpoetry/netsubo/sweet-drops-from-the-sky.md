title: "Sweet Drops From The Sky"

Tear drops of Luna fall from the sky..Directly into my outstretched palmsLooking at the pearly marvels filling the night sky..My eyes open, my arms open, my mind opens..All for the truthMy reason for being on this planet..Why im aliveIts pages are now free for me to look overI search through this book..For the one i cherish mostTo see if she is within the paper and ink..She is not in the bookIn my heart..  That is where i find her
