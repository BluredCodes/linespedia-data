---
title: "That Feeling"
writer: "Netsubo"

Come closer..
I dare you..
That white line is there..
Only for reference.

I would love to see..
Metal molded with flesh
By the hot burning..
Of the gasoline that fuels you

See the light of my eyes recede
Enter the ditch..
Feel the dirty water enter my veins..
And join your heaven sent spears of machinery

The sirens will follow
Pray to God for me..
Ask him to make their flight slow..
I don’t know if there’s much more to live for

A little hope was felt today
But now..
Its all..  distorted
Something isn’t right..

Won’t you come closer?
Cross that white line if you wish..
Or leave me to feel the wake of wind..
From your back..

That hope is something never felt before
Tainted and wrong
Why is the future so blind..
I’ll have to wait..
I’ll have to wait again
For that feeling
And try to keep it..
