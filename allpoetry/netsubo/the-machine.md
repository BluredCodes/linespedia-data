---
title: "The Machine"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "the-machine"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Wine flows through..
My bloodstream numbing..
The day to day..
Pain and travesty..

Tripping down the road of life..
Once more..  Bringing me closer
To my demise..
I smile..

My smile recedes
I no longer wear this mask..
No longer cover myself in...
Lies...

It weighs me down..
Now..  such freedom..
I rid myself of that hate
Here comes some more..

This time..  its not just..
For me
Its for anyone worth hating..
Anyone in my way

My sword unsheathed
My arms cutting a deep line..
Through all of you
And all your brethren..

Kindred..  what a wonderful word
You think youre like me..  Leave me..
Leave me be and let me live..
Without you dragging me behind

If you wont leave..
Ill make you..  leave..
This world
Cover me in a thick blanket

Saturate me in you..
Liquefy all your feelings
Sell them in a plastic bottle..
Sell your pathetic soul

Whoring yourself on shelves in stores
Giving your heart to those you dont know
You dont know shit..
So sell yourself

Ill get the last bite..
Maybe scraps of your tormented being
It wont matter..
Youll never escape

You can never escape
From this machine..  youre not strong enough..
Youll never rid yourself of this machine called
Society
