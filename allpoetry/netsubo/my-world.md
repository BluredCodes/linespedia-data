---
title: "My World"
writer: "Netsubo"

Shiny silver skies
Bleak, yet colored paths
Make up my world

A white sun
Shining through
The dark forest

And a red moon rises,
On the opposite horizon
Giving the clouds a morbid visage

Black water flows...
Through the rivers,
That feed my brethren

Here I belong
My eyes no longer green
My skin no longer bright

Silver eyes..
Silver hair..
Silver skin..

Angular,
Yet curved at the edge
Oh so perfect..

Contradicting my rights
And proving my wrongs..
With use of my claws..

I stare at the sky now
Filled with shooting stars
And streaks of energy..

Who says I can't live here?
In my mind..
Who says..

This earth is right for me?
Society has dragged me down...
But what does it matter?

I wont be here much longer anyways..
