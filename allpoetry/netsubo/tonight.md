Tonight I saw you
But you were blind to me..
Tonight I held you
But it was like I was air

And I fell to the ground
Imitating something comical..
On the outside
But the inside..  Hurt

Tonight I felt rejected
Sine your arms where somewhere..
Else
Since I got my hopes up

And I contemplated
Confessing my emotions
But I held back..
Sine there was no point..

Since..
They wouldve been
Thrown back
In my face

And I saw your spirit
Filled by others
And mine..  came to stale-mate

Since I couldn’t lift your heart
And wasn’t about to bring you down
Since I let my reflexes take over
And I stood to the side..  inanimate

But your golden gaze..
Was somewhere else
And your sweet voice..
In the ear of another

You dont know how I feel..
And how it kills..
To not have you by my side
So run..  or come closer..

So run..
And abandon me
Or come closer
And lift me off the ground
