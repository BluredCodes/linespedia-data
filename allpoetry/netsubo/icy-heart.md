title: "Icy Heart"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "icy-heart"

These pieces fall together..
Ever so slowly
Coming into sight..
Nothing
No more pieces are falling now
Nothing new coming downward..
Down to me
Screaming..
Crying..
Kicking..
Punching..
Looking for another puzzle to make
Finding nothing... its all in the past
Everything behind me
Nothing in front of me
Emptiness beside me
What more can I say than,
I'm cold...
