I stare at the sky...
Ever changing
Never the same...
Just like my days

No matter how I feel
Nothing is the same
No matter how numb I become...
My soul will stand alongside me

We are the chosen...
Taken out of the factory
Chosen to feel pain.
We benefit all...even the blind.

A wonderful bond
Forced by a need
Crashing through barriers
And opening up paths

We’re the unappreciated warriors...
Of the human race
Hope runs rampant through our hearts
But the ability to see it...is rare...feels like something is missing here..
