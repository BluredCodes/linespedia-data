---
title: "Remember"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "remember"

The novelty wears off again..
An angel cursed with horns descended
And the sheep flocked to the marvel..
Only to grow sick of the visage

The tale had been hear all over
And the world was in such anticipation to see
At least most..
Some knew..  folly was apparent

A quick dash towards an unknown forest
Ahh..  again, we’ve reached this place..
A black cloud swirls to the sky,
At an exclamation of my anger.

My words are lost
And maybe I should wait..
But this is too exciting
Plus, I might get caught..

A fence..
Nothing when left open
So I travel somewhere familiar
Or so I had thought..

A blank stare crosses my face
And another shock of that..
My words are lost again
Forget it..

Hope was lost,
And my strength..
Crumbled under the pressure
Forget it..

Remember me when I’m gone
And the stones have filled my eyes
I love you all..
Please..  remember the good times we had..im not going to hurt myself.. dont worry.. i just.. i dunno where to go anymore.
