I heard a whisper on the wind
And it told me...
Something that brought it all back...
And more.

These icicles
Digging deeper and deeper.
It's too bad...
Those glances and smiles turned out...
To be nothing.  Nothing at all.

I would've thrown you a secret
Through the mob..
Right to those blind eyes of yours...
For nothing at all.

Too bad...
It's so damn easy to read people,
Sometimes.
'Cause it felt like something...

Maybe I'm being wishful,
A stupid dreamer.
Wishing for something out of his league.
But there's nothing else to hold onto...

Just that whisper on the wind.
Was it too much to ask...
For these words in me ear...
