---
title: "Again"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "again"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

A bright covering...
Surrounding the lunacy
That covers my body in light.
As I lay under the stars...

A soft wind caresses the grass
And brushes the groundcover across my arms.
How can I feel..
Like this again?

Just a few kind words to me..
And, It happens once more
A returning feeling..
But, I Can’t tell if its right

To believe you’d be here.
Cuz I don’t know if you would
And its too hard to force those
Words out

You made me happy
And I can’t believe
It’s my fault that it all fell
Apart

And I can’t believe..
I would give it all away
To hold you close..
Once more...
