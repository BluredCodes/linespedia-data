A black spiral
Flying through all of us..
You cant see it
You turn away..  AWAY!

Leave me alone to fight
Leave me behind to fend for myself
Leave me behind to end myself..
Why such fear?  Cant take the pain?

The black spiral works through your heart
You dont even see it..
Cant you see its killing us all??
Humanity is working closer to the edge...

Its the force behind your empty eyes
Putting that fake light in them
Lying for you..  Lies..
Your compassion is nothing but hate

You open your arms for me
Just so your counterparts can stab me
Just cause i opened my eyes..
Just..  cause i can see your disease

Is it getting to you yet?
Can you still see out your eyes?
Wait..  you never could in the first place
Patience is running low..

Pouring my blood everywhere just..
Isn’t doing it anymore
Can I handle you anymore?
Can I deal with all your lies?

Not too sure
Walking on the edge..
Contemplating If you would care
I think not..

You matter anymore?
You dont care..
You dont care..
Dont make me do it!!

Reverse the glares
Ill turn you on yourself
Opening my arms..  You come to me..
Knife in my hand, fire in my eyes, I hold you..

Tense my muscles..  Release!
Pushing you away for a split second
Oh I’ve waited for this taste..
My blood is boring..  yours is sweet and unscathed..

Deep in your chest i pound
You cry..  Like me...
You bleed..  Like me
Never thought you were like me?

NEVER!  I put the light in my eyes
Try and take it away
Ill rebuild myself once more..
Dont think I can?  Wont be the first time..

Oh this pool on the ground feels so good..
Its covering my hands and my body
Bathing..  In you..
Ill find another some day, you wont be the last..

Clean the filth from my lips..
Lick your blood from my lips..
Ill taste it soon enough..
Now my love is complete

My love for you is here..
Your eyes are still those empty..
Fissures I used to love so much
Now..  I obsess over them..  Keep them dead

Keep it all in my head..
Crying and running through the rain
What have I done?
