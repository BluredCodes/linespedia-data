---
title: "My Kind"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "my-kind"
source: "AllPoetry"

My black brethren flies through the night
Cutting invisible lines in the air with their claws
Their eyes glow and fly like shooting stars
I belong with them..
But not yet
For i need to live..
And accomplish my purpose..
Once i do so
I can join my kindred..
Then..  I will flap my wings
And fly off into the stars
