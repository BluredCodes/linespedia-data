---
title: "I Miss You"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "i-miss-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/22934-I-Miss-You-by-Netsubo"
---

I’ll move on from this place
With tears in my eyes...
It still hasn’t struck me
That I can’t call you...
It still hasn’t struck me
That I can’t hear your voice
I still miss you
After all this time...
I still miss having you...
By my side
I wish you would kiss me
And take away the pain
Like you once did...
But it’s so hard missing the past
And thinking of the present
Since so much has changed
Since then...
I’m so worthless
And of no consequence
I miss you..
So much..
I wish I could hold you once more
And feel you fall asleep in my arms
I wish I could stare into your eyes
But it would break my heart..
Knowing they’re not on me...
