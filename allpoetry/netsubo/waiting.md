---
title: "Waiting"
writer: "Netsubo"

A pillar of fire rises in my heart..
Those words of hatred again..
Pointed at me
From the same vantage point...

You think youre above me..
But I can bring you down here
I can fly up above you if i want..
Care to see?

Ill drag you down through hell..
And back up into your “heaven”
You take His name in vain..
Yet aim the fiery arrows at your seed

I feel the flames raising in my arms again..
And as the adrenaline fills my heart
I stare in the mirror..
With a single menacing pillar in your direction

I’d love to rip your mask off..
And stare into your soul
You’re gonna know me forever...
And I’ll always be linked to you..

But that doesn’t mean you’ll know who i am..
You act like you fucking know me
And that I like having your words..
Forced into my mind

Patience is a glass sphere falling..
I’m still wondering if you’ll catch it..
Your arm is none other than the one that threw it..
So high

Was your intention to make it invisible?
Did you wanna get rid of it for awhile?
Or forever?
Now..  or forever?

An impact..
Is something you rarely had
And all the screams..
Were nothing other than your own bitching..

I was never good enough..
Doubt I ever will be..
But its not like any of this matters
Since you wont know me either..

You closed yourself to me..
And expected me to open to book for you to read..
But there was never anything to take from me..
So You gave it away, then took it all back

Now im left here..
Covered in the dust and grime.
Watching to see if you forgot about that glass sphere..
That was thrown so long ago

You could never hold true..
To your soul
So i’ll hold true to mine
And stare at the sphere..
Waiting for you..
