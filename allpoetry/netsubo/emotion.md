---
title: "Emotion"
writer: "Netsubo"

I’ll stand by your side
Though it may bring me pain..
I’ll undertake the sharp,
Unforgiving emotion...

I know it flows through your veins
As your soul does..
No matter what you say..
No matter what you think..

I know, I can
I will..  If i must
The knife will enter my chest
As I jump between you..

And your attacker

So take this pain in stride
Since I will take as much as I can
Pain...so convenient
Something so close

Doesn’t it make you wonder..
Thats why we feel it?
The only food within reach...
Of our crippled bodies

And we can’t escape
Without incinerating what is left..
And all of those who look,
Directly in our eyes..

But dont even see..
The emotion in our hearts
I’ll hold you close
And protect you from the cold

Icicles will form..
And numbness will overcome me..
But I’ve felt that before..
And I’ll take this...

All for you..
