Quite a strange word
Wouldn’t you say?
But damn..
It’s got a great meaning

Talk about doing this on the weekends
And people will wonder what story apartment you’ve got
Wanna throw something out a window?
Now you’ve got a damn strange word to talk about it.

Found out about it from a magazine.
One of those new punk bands..
Came from Britain.
I wonder if that craze is gonna re-light.

Wouldn’t you love..
To just throw every goddamn thing out the window..
That ever got to you?

Hmm..
Well, there’s only three steps...
To this new anger management program.

One.
Find a window.

Two.
Open it.  

If locked.
Unlock.

Three.
Toss that shit out!

Defenstration..
A damn cool word
A damn cool action.

Follow those steps.
Feel a little satisfaction.

But regret...
I can’t guarantee it wont be a side effect..
