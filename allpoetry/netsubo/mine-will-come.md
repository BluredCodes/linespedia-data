title: "Mine Will Come"

Spill the water across the floor
I’m thirsty, ever so thirsty..
Make me lick it off the floor where you walk
Make me crawl before you..  Vengeance..

The thought is corrupting me lately
Such inner feelings.. So deep and dark
Why is my vision wavering and clouded?
Is it from the thirst?

Staring at you from my deathbed
You laugh and sneer at me..
The yellow candlelight illuminating your face
Come on.. Go farther in the light..

I’m drawing a picture in my mind
Remembering your face, your laugh, your smile
If i die right now..  you wouldn’t care..
Ill meet you in the afterlife..  and achieve..

Vengeance
