---
title: "Time"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "time"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

An isolate figure walks,
Down an empty road...
Cloaked in a thick sheet..
Of elemental energy

A blue aura swirls around,
In his mind
Emanating the cold...
Expressing the numb...

Snow drifts in and out of his way
Covering up the path he has made
Lost in time
Are the memories we hold

Lost in the depths of the earth
Are the imprints we make on each other
And we try so hard
To tattoo the earth with our own scars...

And we paint our pain onto the surface
To try and send a mixed message to the skies
When there is no point..
Since we’re all erased in time nevertheless..

Try and make an imprint on society
When we created it
Try and make a crease in time
When its our own fallacy

Think about that..
Time..
We can break it
Since we made it

But we would break ourselves..
Can you handle the pain?
I doubt that..
So lose yourself in time...

I’ll be waiting on the other end
Of the spectrum..
