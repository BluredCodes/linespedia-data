---
title: "Fester and Bleed"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "fester-and-bleed"

At one point
This language seemed to familiar
All these faces..
Friendly

Your words hit the glass window
Separating what might be
All I can see is a pair of moving lips..
We should be able to converse, but something is wrong

A few words..
Just the same
Relating me to my hate
So who says thats wrong..  becoming my own hatred..

The fault is not mine
You seared that image onto my skin..
Burned me and scorched that innocence
Fester and bleed..  I’ll laugh and cry all in the same

My tattoo
Of a lost happiness
I’ll brandish it..
With your name on the signature line

A “cocky” look I’m giving you?
Maybe it’s cause you just woke up
Since its a look of anger
No..  not hate..  You’re too worthless for that

Heh, now you’re joking again
And expecting my smile to be so apparent
Just like the piece of shit that you project
As your being

You failed..
But take it out on me
You say blame it on you
But bitch when I place it on your shoulders

Nothing else will be considered
Fester and bleed.. I’ll laugh and cry all in the same...
Nightmare
But understand one thing

Although it has just begun..
And your words are rarely backed with actions
I had admired you..
For such a time

A so called caring individual
I say a selfish bastard
It’s only when its convenient..
But my mouth is stapled shut, with your fiery steel

Feel the canister enter the chamber..
The trigger tense and the tension rise
Gunpowder would smell so sweet right now
Wouldn’t you agree?

You taught me how to pull that trigger
How to draw a bead
Sight in my prey..
Feel nothing for the blood on the wall

I’ll keep this inside
And let it out when you’re gone
Can’t open these emotions...
They’re too frightful for me to look at

I can’t believe you blackened what was pure
Made me fester, bleed, cry, and laugh
God.. This pain feels so good..
I’ll let you in on this treat someday..
