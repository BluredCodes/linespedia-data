title: "Rain Drops...Our Souls"

I watch the sky turn grey
And feel the moisture overcome me...
As the air thickens, so does the plot.
And the pain reaches a shadow in my heart

Blinding the insecurities
And holding my tongue
I watch the raindrops fall...
Ever so slowly

I stare out the window
And slow time with my mind
The raindrops...taking longer an longer...
To crash into the earth

The silence of the spirit
Becomes my hatred
And it shines light into the blind eyes...
Of my own cursed shadows

So the vision is reunited with understanding
And a calm breeze cools my sweat
To speak my mind...
I must fly high...above the stars

But my wings are withheld
By patience
As the raindrops...
Fall ever so slowly...
To their knees.
