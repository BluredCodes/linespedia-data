Trapped inside..
Once more
This hurt..
I’m used to it

Just take everything..
Away
Give it all to someone
Who cares about your pain

You think something’s killing you?
I’d love to meet it,
Shake its hand
I’d love to trade places

I’d love to thrust the cold steel
Right through the nothingness you call..
A heart
Right through, your empty eyes

I’d love to see the blood spray across my face
See your helpless body fall
It’ll be less painful
It’ll be quicker..  than my death..
