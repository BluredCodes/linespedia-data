---
title: "Mother Nature...Save Me"
writer: "Netsubo"

I stared out the window..
At the falling droplets of rain
Took in this picture of heaven..
Some say the rain is evil..  can I agree?

A feeling fills my heart..
With the moisture so apparent
I feel as if in the ocean..
Again

Hot sand filling the space between my toes
And a warm ray sprayed across my face..
A quick dash through a wave..
And I dance with the water once more

Father and farther..
Swimming, until I dont even know where I am
Whistles off in the distance..
Beckon me to return..  to “safety”

How can this so-called safety be labeled that?
When it’s the demon that holds a knife against my wrist..
I wish to fly with the eagles on wings of purity
And to swim deep into the ocean..  As far as our core

I wish to take a new form
Forget my past and present..
Ignorance would be such a wonderful utopia
But Atlantis is far gone..

How I would love to live underwater
Travel in any direction..
How I would love to feel the hot sun and warm wind across my face..
How I would love...
To be in your arms

Mother nature...
Hold me close
Humanity cannot see..
Into my soul, as you can

Your sky blue eyes hold the answers..
To secrets that plague me
But your eyes were only seen once..
And I could not study the colors held within

Even more is held within the ocean..
You placed those treasures for others to find
But where should I look?
When the question of what I seek is quite apparent..

Your sapphire eyes..
Could make my puzzle full
Unfortunately I lost some of the pieces..
Did you happen to find them on your travels?

Mother nature..
Humanity is too far lost to save..
So make me a new form..
Save me from hell..  once more
