title: "A Leper's Gift"

The night falls into view
And the leper walks down that path
Full of the struggling
You hide within your heart

A deep soliloquy of voices
Surrounds what was never there
And a blank space of time
Is felt in my heart

Is that really the truth?
Fall deep into darkness
And pierce the eye of a Cerebros
With the tongue you brandish

It all goes too fast..
Deep and dark voices
Surround what should not be
A soliloquy..  Of hatred

My requiem has already been written
And ridden by the tramping of humanity
But my vision still remains..
Forget my words, you will not

Come down again..
Laugh with me under a palm
Come to that starlit path
That was treasured by none else..

Take it all away again
Won’t you?
Leprosy swirls around
And we all fall apart..  In our minds

It’s so strange
That constant insecurity
Join the soliloquy..
Or be shot down by the arrows of time

This doesn’t concern me..
Anymore
I’ll place the leeches on your neck
And drain the evil blood from your body

Take away what was never there
Make it even emptier..
You said you could do that
Show me, make my void larger

Make something of nothing..
And forget whatever we said
Our words matter, not at all
For the wind calls...
Asking for a red filled palm

Cup your hand
Outstretch your arm
I’ll spit my hatred..
And make you feel what’s inside

Your blonde hair..
Once flowed in the wind
But now, so different..
Rip it out..

Try and piece back that heart of mine
I’d love to see you try
You can’t fix what is not broken..
You never had an effect

A leper may lose his limbs
But an understanding is shown
All falls apart..
Nonetheless

The devil once called
And we conversed for awhile
I contemplated reaching into the dark
And feeling what lay ahead..

But your deceit reminded me
Forget all hope..
Nothing was left of that tattered book
And the pages were all ripped out

Try and comprehend..
What was never there
Fill my void
With another black hole..

Reach into the dark soul
Of a traveling leper
He loses all of his limbs..
