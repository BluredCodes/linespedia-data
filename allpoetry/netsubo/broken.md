---
title: "Broken"
writer: "Netsubo"

The dark captivates me..I see black figures,Dancing off in the distanceThey see me..  not at allThe figure sitting off to the sideThats me..  Hiding in the shadowsUnseen by the worldThey walk into me and all over meSitting right next to me..They don't even know im thereMinds clouded, eyes glazed overThey forget I existPromises broken..But I forget it allSit there and stare at the fireWith those right next to meThey forget im there..Hah..  so much for me..Holding each otherForgetting im thereRemembrance is an unknown word for themHappiness..  long pastHah..  so much for meI guess..  they forgot
