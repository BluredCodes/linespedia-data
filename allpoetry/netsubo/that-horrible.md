---
title: "That Horrible"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "that-horrible"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/45546-That-Horrible-by-Netsubo"
---

Another crowd..
Another day..
I hadn’t seen much different yet
Except that set of eyes..

Dark and intriguing
However full of light
It seems like God had placed stars in those pupils..
And in a passing glance, something was shared

A cloud forms my mind..
And I try and figure if any hope is held
A guess is given..
The same as the others..  none..

Never was held in a moment of purity
For thoughts of another were always apparent..
In the others stare
Am I really that horrible?

Not perfect.
Nothing close to it..
Still cant understand why,
Nothing has happened..  if it should..

And those words have been spoken before
With all the attention I could muster upon them
But why so long a wait..
For something said to have come soon?

I see hate in that mirror staring back at me
And it seems like that cannot be myself..
How did I become this horrid..
How did my soul seep through my skin..

A black orchid rises through the ice
The last whisper of winter..
I miss the cold..  somewhat
Since at least then, it kept my skin numb

The sun heats my body up..
And gives me more temptation than before
I can feel this pain coming once more
Please.. help me survive..

Fly my way..
Your downy feathered wings envelope me
With a cool blast of air
I’m back to normal..  I guess..

The blue sky prophesizes that more is on its way
Sweet angel..  stay with me..
Dont fly away once more
Am I that horrible?
 
If so..
Then leave me to my devices..
For I’ll forget all the hope that was ever held dear
If so..
Forget I’m here..

If its that horrible
And my skin turns green with fear
And these once green eyes become decayed..
Leave..

Find something worthy of your embrace
I’ll hide beneath the mountains
Never to show my face..
And to forget this ever happened..
