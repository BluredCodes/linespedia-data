title: "A Soldiers Lost Hope"

Olive drab rubber..
And an insect-like mask
Hide the face of a guilty conscience
For through the mist
The eyes of a child were not seen
The howling of the wind
Mimics that of a banshee..
Crying to the moon.

Why is it so ravaged?

A wretched wasteland..
Filled with nothing but barren hatred
Scalded by the hot sun..
And torn apart by a raven..
With the hunger for a snake

Filled to the core
A sign is raised..
And with it,
A fist.

Raised towards the sun
Asking for an eternal embrace
With humanity
To take away all the pain and suffering
From this haven gone wrong

Didn’t you listen?
We could’ve kept this world,
Intact

But at your coarse hands
And that vise grip you placed upon it..
The earth crumbled from between your fingers

Didn’t you listen?
To the rubber coated soldiers
Controlled by your actions..
They didn’t mean to kill all of our children

Why didn’t you open your ears..
To the hatred that boiled in your veins?
A desert is our only land now
Feed off of the decaying bodies
Feel the disease reach into your heart

You can’t cure what we never knew of..
All those antibiotics
That chemical waste you threw down your throat
Keep it up..
Burn a hole through your stomach..

You’re all the reason we had to leave
That safe haven
That place of sanctuary..

A pair of blue eyes once stared at me in despair
And I took her body away from anything surreal..
For a quick moment
Just to forget,
The pain you’ve inflicted

Drums deep under the crust
Radiation fills your pupils
Discoloring your skin..

Speak your name
Oh..
The toxins youve fed yourself,
Have eaten through your tongue

I’ll throw you into that sea..
That was once pure and blue
Along with all the olive drab donned soldiers

Feel their pain..
You can’t control yourself..
Didn’t you listen?
To the words your body was telling you

Take it all inside..
For humanity
What little hope we had
Was crushed by mechanized nightmares
Haunting our slumber..
Take whatever you had left..
Swallow it all down..
Forget me..
We can save ourselves..
