title: "A Cloaked Mystery"
writer: "Netsubo"

A snowy terrain paints a picture..
Well known to my kind..
Flash in..  Flash out...
As quick as the first rays of a sunrise

Our blades fly like scythes..
Cutting through thick grassy plains
Very little escapes
From the keen eye of a mercenary

Luck may be on your side..
But good fortune is nothing against skill
Come..  attack..  honor will evade you forever
You abandoned it all when you ran

A cloaked mystery follows..
Can’t you hear the whisper on the wind?
A full moon has risen..
Be wary of evil tonight..  my friend
