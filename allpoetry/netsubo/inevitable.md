---
title: "Inevitable"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "inevitable"

---

As I fall from surface to surface
I lose a small bit of myself every time
But its towards nature..
Back to my beginnings

Looking at the ground
And how it all comes closer..
How the end of this journey draws near
I feel weak

I've been giving all my reserve away
And now I'm running out..
And no the cold sets in..
Who can make me strong enough?

No-one but me

I'll look deep inside this clear casing
To try and understand how to make something
Of this lack of will
Can I borrow from my mother once more? No..

No-one but me

With every fall..
I wonder if the ground will hurt more
And try and picture how I will be
After the cycle starts over again

Will I collide...
And fly into thousands of pieces of my own self?
Will I merge...
And not be able to tell who I am?

Taking a break in the crevice
Of this upturned leaf..
Caught in a thicket..
I stare at all the passing droplets.

Can't run from my destiny..
Can't speed it up either..
I'll see how to create
The iron sheild needed

Before I meet the beginning
Of this cycle once more
For there's no reason
To try and escape the inevitable

Is there?
