---
title: "Steel"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "steel"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Concrete and Gravel..
Pouring into my face
Cutting into my skin
Scaring my scarred..

Red..  Pouring..
Mixing with the mechanical waste
Becoming one with humanity
We call us..  Humanity...
What we create is our soul
Since we put our hearts into it..
Killing ourselves blindly
Does anyone even care?
Do you even care..
That you're killing me
From miles away..
You don't even know
You're ripping me apart
So many years ago
Come on..  come back to me
Find out what ill do to you..
Find me..  and find your demise
Grinning with a knife in my teeth
Blood pouring out my mouth..
Its you doing this to me..
From the inside
Youre cutting my heart away
Bit by bit
Someday ill get you out..
Of my head..
You dont wanna go?
Ill show you who’s problem that is..
You’re the slut
You’re the whore
You’re the real threat
To small children..
Stealing innocence?
Hah..  Ill kill you for that..
Wanna watch from far away?
You dont hafta look into my eyes
I’ll look into yours
As i drive the blade deep in your chest
I wanna see the pain coming from you
Come on..  spurt your blood upon me
Youve got no innocence..
What else have you got?
You call that a life?
No..  youre numb and dead already
I’m just speeding up your clock
Come on..  you know you wanna feel..
Everything im feeling right now
But you..  just cant..
So..  Ill make you feel something else
Ill make you feel..  STEEL!
