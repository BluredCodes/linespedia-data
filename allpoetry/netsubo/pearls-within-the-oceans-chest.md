title: "Pearls Within the Oceans Chest"
writer: "Netsubo"

I hold an oyster in my hand
My eyes squint as I study its features
I look into a small crack, and a sparkle catches my eye
I see a small pearl, glistening like something I haven't seen in awhile
I remember now, her eyes..
How the sight of them had brought such happiness in my life
It was something I had never known before
I think of her now..  how I'd love to her see her
How I'd love to hold her body next to mine
Now..this yearning returns
I feel it tugging at my heart
More, much more than before
Please..let things be as they used to be
Let it all go back in the past
I promise you, with all my heart I do
I will change how i act, how i think
Anything to start again
Anything to be the only!
Im begging your forgiveness
Im sorry for putting this all on you
If I can lift any weight, tell me how and I will
Anything to make it better for you
Anything to be with you..
