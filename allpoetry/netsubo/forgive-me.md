---
title: "Forgive Me"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "forgive-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/31938-Forgive-Me-by-Netsubo"
---

As I look to the path...
That my life has taken me,
I see my footprints...
Drying up ever so slowly

My actions have placed something upon this earth
Which would not have been...
However, they will never live..
Forever

So I try and continue
Without looking into my past
And at the footprints that are covered up...
By time

A teary eyed glace gives the vision...
That my hopes and dreams are gone
Yet a small feeling of warmth remains...
For an unknown reason

I’ll always look to you
I’ll always look to the sky...
I’ll always ask of your advice
Since you’ve always loved me...

I’ve strayed from your glaze
But the light you emanate never left...
The night I held a sliver of cold steel against my wrist..
You.. and one of your angels stood by me

You gave me life once..
And this wonderful gift i thought was only once..
Was renewed by your love
And the forgiveness I felt...

God.. I beg of you..
Forgive me for the temptation in my heart
Forgive me for the hate I have created...
Forgive me... for I cannot stand on my own..
Anymore
