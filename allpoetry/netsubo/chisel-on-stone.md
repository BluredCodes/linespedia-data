---
title: "Chisel On Stone"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "chisel-on-stone"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/4495-Chisel-On-Stone-by-Netsubo"
---

All these toils
For what?
To bring life to something
Intended for death?
Such futile actions..
In the past..
Possibly now
Definitely in the future
Trying to live in a lie..
It’s so fake..  nothing real
No foundation
No footing
We’re left to plummet through smoke and lies
Left to rot and die
We only take up space..
Trying to make something..
Something imperfect..
Perfect
Wasting our lives
Throwing gold out the window
Putting our own shit on the dinner table
Everything is backwards..
Nothing right..
It never was
We’re too close now
No turning back from our fate
The fate carved in stone..
We stand beside that stone
Holding the chisel and hammer
Lying to ourselves
And blaming those above us
Go ahead..
Live in your lie painless
When you die shards of the truth will force into your mind
I’ll live painfully
Not in a lie..  Not trapped in my own fallacy
I’m away from you
Don't come near me unless you understand..
If you know your life is a lie..
And you want to live purely..
Without emptiness in your heart
Like I once was..
