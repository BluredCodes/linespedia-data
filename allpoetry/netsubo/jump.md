---
title: "Jump"
writer: "Netsubo"

Nerves dangling..No feeling anymoreSo numb..  So..HeartlessI cant.. control myselfNot able to..  thinkWhy am I doing this?To myself..  once more..Reaching into the depths with one armMaking up my inner selfPainting the mask i wear for the weak with the otherYou’ll never see my true colors..One thing i can keep from yourGroping hands..  Is my identityYou saw my face..  or what you thought it wasYou felt my skin..  what you thought was meIm so glad..  I didnt give that to youIll hold this in‘Till the time is rightTo show to the one..  I can call mineYou thought you wanted meBut you didnt know meYou thought I loved youBut you didnt know meCome on..  you know you wanna ask..You wanna ask who I am..Its for you to guess..  You’ve got all the answersDont you?  You left me guessingTrying to figure outWhether or not i should stayHell..  you pushed me towards the edge..I stand here..  blankly..  awaiting your demise..What will i do?  Nothing..Stand here..  with a smirk on my faceShowing you..  that you’re in the dark for once..I won over you..  finally..  ive got my vengeance..What are you gonna do?Stand there and cry like I did?Or jump off into the nothingness..I so many times contemplated...Its up to youCome on..  The pressures the other way nowYou know you want it..You know you need it..JUMP!
