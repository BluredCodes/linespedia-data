title: "'Let Go of Regret'"

A dream was had
And those icy spheres returned.
I wish I could’ve stopped
But your lips touched mine all in the same.

Something was different..
Besides this being a dream
Something was out of place.
I think I might know...

Your words called the shots,
As always.
But my actions controlled your words..
I remember now..

It was you..
Begging for that kiss.
Even though you didn’t even feel it..
Tell me..  how is it when the tables are turned?

Even this house was different
The walls..
Carpet, ceiling..
Everything out of place.

It would’ve been nice if it was real
Cause before I woke up,
That feeling returned
And it was like I was a year younger.

Damn..
The phone off to the side was a good interruption
All the time I knew it was a dream
But took those sweet kisses as something to hold onto

Gotta forget about all the bad
“Let go of regret”
That was once said..
They made it sound pretty damn easy.

I guess,
I’ve gotta try something else
Another path..
Cause waiting doesn’t do anything.

And even though my legs are afraid of moving
I’ve got to someday.
All my words are in such a jumble..
A rambling idiot, the speaker of those words.

Wishing I could get off my feet
Fly into whatever will come to me
Nothing seems right..
Its all some bittersweet nightmare.

A year ago, such happiness filled my heart,
All for a waste of time.
I’d love to take you back again...
