---
title: "Fallen"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "fallen"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/12551-Fallen-by-Netsubo"
---

One of the fallen
Turning into all I hate
I’d rather die
Than go back
I’d rather stay here in torture
Than go away

One of the fallen
Can’t stop..
All this shit in my head
Can’t stop..
All the fucking lies!
Can’t stop!

One of the fallen
A freight liner
On it’s way to demise
Filled with hate and..
I’m unworthy of forgiveness
Tied in the middle

One of the fallen
Trapped in it’s path
One of the fallen
Crawling away from the pain
But the cloud can follow..
So good

I can stay away
From that fallacy for awhile
And keep the curtain drawn
Who gives a shit anyway?
No matter how far I run..
My demise gets faster and faster..

All I can do is be..
One of the fallen
All of my charity is wearing thin
Exclusive for my loved..
Falled comrades
Exclusive..  filled with love..

Dont turn your back please
Dont close your eyes
And don’t stop holding out your arms
We all fell down this trap in humanity
And we fell together..
Dark and falling.. light up and down

Cavernous and black as a raven..
The moon and sun are with us
Never let go of the light in your eyes
For we are the fallen
And need everything possible
For when it all catches up..
