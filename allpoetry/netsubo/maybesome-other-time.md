A quick smile
Turn of a head..
Once again its all blocked
And I couldn’t smile

Those butterflies came back
It’s like I’m in the same place..
Back in the mall
Yelling down to a pretty face

Heh.. I can actually feel..
Has a star fallen again?
Hasn’t happened in a long time
Even..  with the one lost chance

Couldn’t this moment have kept awhile?
Or maybe a few words spoken?
I may be silent..
But a thousand words in me

I’ll keep them quiet
Your heart belongs to another..
Maybe our paths cross at another place
Maybe we’ll meet some other time

A stare for a familiar stare is blocked...
I knew something had been up
The moon isn’t out tonight
Should’ve known..

Maybe some other time..
Those few words can be spoken
And these eyes can meet once more
But for now..  It must be quiet..

There’s a balance now
Don’t take it away..
Wait awhile..
Wait
