---
title: "Bring Me"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "bring-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/33480-Bring-Me-by-Netsubo"
---

A demons tears are never shed..
They hide within their minds
Are released as blood..
From their wounds

Aren’t all demons..
Just fallen angels?
Aren’t they corrupted..
By their own minds and hearts?

God dealt us a bad hand of cards..
Yet we must work with what we’re given
Is it for strength?
We dont know the answer..

So we look deep within our souls
To find that precious little jewel of truth
But little do we know..
That what we seek is much more than a possession

Hold out your hands..
I can give you humanity
Open up your heart..
And I can fill you with something more

Am I to know what my gift is?
Only time will tell..
Sweet love...
I know nothing of your face..

Yet I still long to stare into your eyes..
Jade? Sapphire? Tigers eye?
The question holds a heavy weight upon my shoulders
And I still wonder what color I may see within..

Pillars of granite hold everything inside
While the blue light keeps everything within reach..
Can I live much longer without your touch?
Even though I have not felt your embrace..

Hold me close once more..
Forget all the pain
I’ll make your words into scriptures
Your actions into dances...

My shrouded angel..
I’ll reach to you from the depths of hell
We’ll meet on this planet..
Souls destined from birth..

Will you dance with me?
Fly across the night sky..
For all the beauty within..
Can warm my soul

Take me to heaven..
And I will fight alongside you
Against my past..
Take me to heaven..

Hell cannot bring me down once more
With you by my side.. 
Will you bring me to heaven?
Shrouded angel..
I beg of you..
Bring me to heaven..
