I’d love to see this..Happen.. Again..I want to see my life...Go to hell..Throw me in that cesspool..That I’ve jumped in and out of beforeDrown me in everyone’s..Lies..I want to leave this world..If you wont leave meI would've given everything for you..Would've..I try and push you backInto the black hole in my mind..I cant..You stay here up front..  In my thoughtsYou’re the scar on my faceIt splits me in half..One side, hatredThe other, denialI love you..I hate you..I LOVE YOU..I HATE YOU..Back and forth in this..Hellish worldIt’s too much for meLeave me to myselfLeave me to people..Who actually careMy only family..Just leave me ALONE!Run away!Chase your shadowIn this room with no doorsPointless and hateful, I give you my painDEAL WITH IT!
