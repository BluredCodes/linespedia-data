title: "Massacre in the Hallway"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "massacre-in-the-hallway"
source: "AllPoetry"

You stare at me..
As if there’s nothing but hatred,
And anger within my heart

I must say..
You’re not far from being right

I guess it was too much..
Asking for a pair of angels wings..
And a little bit of love in my heart

Just a little too much..
