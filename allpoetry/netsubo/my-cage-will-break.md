title: "My Cage Will Break"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "my-cage-will-break"
source: "AllPoetry"

Hate me..Love me..Show me..Blind me..Kiss me..Kick me..Help me..Abandon me!Leave me in my cageScreaming for helpTrying to get outTurn your back on meYou’re the same..Just like all the othersOne day..  I’ll break..Through my bars of steelChasing you..Down this roadTrees pass me on my sideI’m getting closer..This blood, it’s my own.I can taste it..  feel itI can taste the beautiful..Red..This reminds me of your lipsAnd how I loved them so much..God.. I still want you..Kill me, so I can miss you no more..
