---
title: "Ashes"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "ashes"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

These ashes form once more..
From past midnight fires
Fires that fought off the cold...
Now this ice forms in my heart once more..
All my hope is dying
All my sorrow.. returning
Searching for more wood
To make a fire
To hold close to my heart
To fend off the icy numb within
Reaching in the dark...
Something is found..
Placing it close to me
Pulling fire from my mind...
Warming this kindling..
Another person comes near..
Holding a torch
She looks in my eyes
Full of care and sympathy in her heart
The fire starts again
I bring it close to me
The closer to the fire I come
The closer I come to this form
She brings me close to her..
This fire rises in my heart
Killing off any traces of cold
Thoughts of love fill my soul
They fill this hole in my heart
Finally..  one to join me in my travels
This fire now burns eternal
There is no need for external happiness
Internally... the warmth grows...
Caressing this..  Hoping once more..
My hope lives again
My happiness lives once more
A small smile comes forth
My eyes soften
I look at her once more
Noticing..  the beauty
The inner shine in her eyes
A glistening smile
Making my heart grow and fill..
Not a word is spoken..
And we put out the fire..
Not needing it anymore..
This internal fire burns
I depend on fuel no more
This love is unending
And it feeds my newfound warmth
