---
title: "A Silver Lining"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "a-silver-lining"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/51078-A-Silver-Lining-by-Netsubo"
---

The daily look at the sky..
Passing clouds,
A few straying rays of sunlight.
But something was different..

It was like God cut out a piece of a rainbow
And placed that slice of beauty on a cloud..
Floating along,
My vision followed...
Until I was too far away

This helped me remember
When such a close soul let me in on,
A really sweet secret.
She said she asked for a sign..
And a few rainbows were given to her eyes only

A mountain was reflected in a lake of mercury..
Which in it floated a dream
Only the elven can feel..
Or is that what the fairytales have told us?

I guess my ears are pointed..
And fair skin is quite apparent
A beautiful appearance
Along with grace must be here..
To hold this dream

But when I stare into that lake
All I see is an ugly human being..
Nothing more than another dreaming fool
Staring at the ocean instead of jumping in

Give me something..
To grasp on to
Spring comes invariably
And the happiness surrounds me

A little precipitation would be nice..
Since it’s got to be something in the water
And I’d love a drink from a silver lined cloud
‘Cause some of this is too hard to see...
In that mercury lake.

I’ll stand here
Keep my disease quiet..
Is that elven soul within me trying to awaken..
Or are my dreams becoming my reality again?
I’d love to see..  if this mercury is truthful..

But without anything else
You can’t look into your own eyes
I see a dark hole in my spirit..
And see the damage with my name branded..
Across the middle

All I see..
Is a dreaming fool
Wishing to live a different life..
With only one chance.

I felt the blood in my veins last night
It scared me so..
That everything is so fragile
And today..
My bloodied hands chipped away at the earth.

All for the “benefit”
Of the human race.
We dont see..
All we have to do is reach for a cloud..

If a silver string enters our fingers follow
If you come out empty handed
Reach for another..
There is more than once chance..

It gets so hard..
Seeing some get theirs on the first try
When the cloud dissapears
Before my hands can get close..
Even though my eyes see something more than a silver thread..

Maybe patience is what needed..
It was never asked for before
And my satchel is empty
Maybe that forest holds what I seek..

A bit of patience
I’ll hold onto it..
Like the very blood within my body
When found

Never let go..
For there is more than one cloud for every person..
Maybe a trite dream
But who says..
You still can’t ride on simply a smile?

I’ll walk through those ferns..
To the soft groundcover
And search for some patience..
So I can wait,
Look around a little..

Maybe wait..
For that whole rainbow to grow
Finish..
What was begun
So long ago..
