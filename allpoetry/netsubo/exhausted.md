Walking in this circle again
Just to confuse myself
And pull a curtain over my face

Looking up to the stars
As if they will whisper to me..
The answers

Screaming at the mechanized humanity..
Flying by me..
Just to let out some rage..

Breaking the empty souls..
On the side of our demise..
Trying to fill myself

So ironic..
Filled with Emptiness
So..  Me..

Can everything be right?
No..  I don’t hope for that...
Anymore

Can my mind work...
Right?
No.

So I sit here in my cage
Since the keys are too far up
And I’m too tired to jump for awhile.
