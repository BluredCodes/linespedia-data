A quick walk down a familiar road
Seemed so peculiar today.
As a wave of intoxicated air was sensed,
A horrid image came to mind

I saw a demon today..
With leathery black wings.
His skin was discolored,
And something dripped from his mouth.

A hand reached down...
And tore out the heart of a helpless wretch
The fiery light illuminated those empty eyes,
But for once...fear wasn't felt...

The long strongs of hair seemed so sparse,
Yet I knew something was wrong.
Standing there in amazement...
Realization came...
