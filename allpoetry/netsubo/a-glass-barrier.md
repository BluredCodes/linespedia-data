---
title: "A Glass barrier"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "a-glass-barrier"

I lay on my side..
Staring through this wall of glass
Towards the one I adore
The one who takes up a small space in my heart

Her eyes.. radiating sunlight
Her hair.. curved like the oceans waves
Her skin.. golden like a thousand coins
But she lays on the other side..

This glass separates us
For now..
I ask..
Where is my hammer?

It’s time will come soon enough
So i can break through the glass..
And feel wanted..  needed..
So i can feel..  worthwhile
