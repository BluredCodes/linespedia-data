---
title: "My Lost Chance"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "my-lost-chance"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/42873-My-Lost-Chance-by-Netsubo"
---

As I passed a tattered soul..
I quietly revered it
And thought..
How not much is different

I see my desire in the reflection..
Not the true sky
And realize...
All those dreams are fake

A chance..
Was given
Yet was thrown away..
In my stupor

Those blue eyes are still so clear
And then..
A shock of uneasiness
And my actions falter once more..

Why..
Every time I see the light at the end of the tunnel
Do I blind myself..
To the truth?

That chance was so..
Wonderful
For once,
I may have done something right

But it was only me..
Who held my tongue
No-one to blame
But myself..

Can’t believe..
I let another,
Slip through the cracks
Can’t believe

Can’t believe,
I felt a little more hope..
And dashed it away
In a moment

I’d love to go back..
And add a few simple words
But.. too far away now
