---
title: "Bottom"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "bottom"

A cliff..
From afar
Such a wonder

From so close..
Destiny is in your pupils..
Can you see the possibility?

Quick!
Grasp into something!
Gravity has slowed for a moment..

We must not lose..
What we held so close to our hearts
And can feel leaving every second

There used to be a fire..
Within my heart
That burned oh so high

A light was felt for miles..
Across villages
And across this cliff

But the fear placed in my heart
I will not deny..
A force to be reckoned with, nothing else

That fire is hard to see now..
Am I just imagining it is there?
Hoping for some kind of hope...

I surely believe there may be something
Left that can save us
Dont you believe?

Nothing is in front of me..
And I must fall much lower to find something..
Strong enough to hold

This moment of flight is a blessing
And cannot be asked upon for much longer..
I wonder..  If I can latch on

I wonder..
If my loved will latch on..
Please answer me yes..

So little to hold on..
We can live..
Can’t we?

Reach out with an open palm
Reach that branch..
Awaiting your grasp

Dear God tell me I’ll live..
This fear..  is so apparent..
My eyes twitch with the buckets of rain
And so much has been lost..
Must I hit the bottom..
Before I can reach the top again?
