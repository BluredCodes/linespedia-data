---
title: "Fallen Angel"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "fallen-angel"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

The black heart of a fallen angel..
Resides within my chest
Filling me with the tainted blood
So commonly found in hellfire..

Tattered wings..
Bloodied and torn..
Escaping through my spine
Enveloping my body in a thick coat...

Bright eyes..
With an underlying light
So reminiscent of demon skin..
Which can scorch the eyes of the innocent

A wall of mercury..
Placed upon my wall
Is something more than a liquid mirror..
For I can see into my own soul

The black smoke fills my veins..
And the red light shines through my skin
Helpless..  are my actions...
Empty..  are my emotions

Yet the words to describe..
Are rarely lost to the black ocean
Where my dreams float
Just out of my reach..

To a depth I reach
Yet the shining light so pure..
Lays so close to my reach
So close to my heart...

If only love would fly my way
And these torn wings would heal...
If only I would feel that warmth again
My skin could be shown..

I pray to fly with the angels once more
But my bloodied wings hold me to the ground...
I pray to feel the wind across my face
And the sun shining brightly once more..

How much more can be lost
Without losing all hope?
And how many more pains will I feel..
Before there is no feeling left?

I know I will fly again..
And my hate will fade
But the question is when..
Which will plague me
Until the answers have fallen from the sky..

Speed quickly to my heart..
Sweet love from the sky
I beg of you to find me..
For I know not where to look..
