---
title: "'Finally'"
writer: "Netsubo"

It’s been so long
Since I’ve felt your skin.
On that stormy night...
Our lips brushed together
And the small words we shared
Mixed with the screams..
Of a siren.
The word..
“Finally”
Slid through your lips.
And I was given the thought...
That something had started.
But I can’t live,
That far in the past
No...
Something is so wrong..
I’d love to ignore it
But how can you ignore...
A knife going into your heart?
