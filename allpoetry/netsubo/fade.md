---
title: "Fade"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "fade"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/35255-Fade-by-Netsubo"
---

I try to walk away..
From myself..

Can’t believe my face..
Has gone away

There is nothing left..
But how can i save?
Anything..
When there was nothing to start..
It all falls away..

You can run away
From me..
In the end..
I will understand..

If you cant..
Handle the pain..

Look me in the face
Once again..
Before I fade..

Let me see your eyes
Let me see your mind..
How can i save..
What was never there?

How can i hold on..
To myself?

When I’m fading all away..
Fading away..

I’ll hand you my heart
My soul

In the end..
We all..  fade away

Cant you understand?
There's more to life
Than just yourself..

I would give it all
To let you see..
Inside of me

But you dont even care..
So what’s the point..
I’ll wash away..

Wash away..
Wash away..
In the end..
There was nothing left
Nothing to start..
