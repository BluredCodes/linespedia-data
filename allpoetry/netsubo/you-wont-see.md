---
title: "You Won't See"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "you-wont-see"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/26224-You-Wont-See-by-Netsubo"
---

I tear apart...
Then put together
Running around...
Leaving my tears

Behind
Behind the wall of hate I feel
Behind the barrel aimed at me
I want to pull.. and fall so endlessly

It’s gotten too hard
To laugh it away...
You’ve gotten too far
Yet I continue to pray

Your return is my smile
For I miss you with all my heart
And the pain drags on behind
But I know I can’t go on...

So this wave of tension will drown me
And I’ll go deep into the hate
Aimed at myself...
Aimed at everything

Can’t you see?
That there are ways
Can’t you see?
I’m falling down

And I feel so pathetic
Since I can’t even save myself
So I’ll lie here on the ground...
And drown in my tears

It’s amazing
What little I ask
Of you...
It’s amazing...

The treatment I receive
Makes me feel in a way
That brings back the thirst for blood...
That brings a blade to my skin.

I would give it all
But that would do nothing
So I’ll walk over the coals...
You set upon my path

I’ll take this pain for you
Since I still hold hope
I’ll take this pain for you
And won’t let you know...
