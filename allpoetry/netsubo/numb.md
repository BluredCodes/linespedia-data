---
title: "Numb"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "numb"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Jettison me off into space
Covered in an icy shield
All your empathy runs through me
But who says my feelings are real?

Flying under and over
Going back, then away
Returning to the ocean
Diving deep

Coming to the surface
Spread wings..
Throwing my tears at the sun
I rid you of my pain

And go numb
Since you wouldn’t care
Anyway..
So I go numb..  for you..

I watch the clock tick away
Then throw it out the window
Build you a castle
Then burn it to the ground

I give you my heart
And smash it in your empty hands
Nobody cares
So I go numb..

There were no tears left anyway..
