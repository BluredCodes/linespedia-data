---
title: "Unborn"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "unborn"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/44896-Unborn-by-Netsubo"
---

A few words are spoken
And the dark womb of night has broken
But only in one place..
Only in one color..

The babies eyes are scorched upon the red light..
Infesting its home before its time
As the red recedes..
More join, yet some remain..  hidden

I have not yet placed my log upon the fire
But sit heavily on it..
Filled with anger
Towards the first light

My eyes have yet to be seen
For if they were..
The darkness would return
And swallow all hope..

Hope must not be lost..

You say something has changed..
But a few green words
Spouted forth from the mothers mouth..
Does nothing... To me

The babe is held deep within the womb..
Crying for the light to leave..
Yet only more feeling is introduced
Along with pain..  all too early

The fake reflection in the mirror..
Is so repetitive..  so familiar..
I’m sick of seeing this face
I’m sick of seeing this body..

You all make decisions which make me cringe..
In this house..
An unborn’s umbilical cord holds the babes neck in rapture..
Yet your unblinking eyes still see nothing wrong

I can’t believe you can’t see..
Build something with the tools you’ve been given
Chipping away at humanity..
Makes it all even worse..

The path were on is bad enough
Dont place your own coals under my feet..
I’ll spit your emotions back down your throat
In a fake embrace of care..

Take all your shit and leave
Get out of this house
Go and cry to your fucking mother..
My light will never be seen
My log never placed on your fire..
Staple my mouth shut with my anger for you
Fuck you..
Sit here with an iron fist
But who says it will be used
Stumble upon me..
I’ll take away that hymen of night you so humbly trust
Throw my iron fist away in one last fit of rage
Forget yourself..
You never made a difference..  Along with your empty cries
You hurt our last chance..  and scorched its eyes
A blind cause for life..
Is what you have given..
Me.my brother..  i wish he wouldve stayed away..
