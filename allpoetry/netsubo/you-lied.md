---
title: "You Lied!"
writer: "Netsubo"

Go aheadRip me..Slash me..Tear my heart apartIts nothing differentSame old story..Different individualHurt me..Torture me..Leave me in darknessTake away my light..My visionDont kill meKeep the pain goingMore and moreEvery second..More and moreEvery thoughtMore and moreEvery moment..More and moreSpill my blood across the worldTaint my soulMy mind caves in..YOU LIED!!Never againI swear on my heart..Never again will i be lied toWithout opening upSo many lies beforeSo much pushing..Pushing them away..  forgettingNo MoreThats behind me now..Never again will i be lied toAnd have my words pushed down my throatNever again will i be lied toAnd still forgive..Never againWill i be deceivedWithout actions madeMy eyes never lieMy tongue never liesMy heart never liesMy soul NEVER LIESYoure nothingNothing except..Lifeless...Alone...Miserable..At least im aliveI am alone..But at least i liveWith all my heartWith all my soulI hate you..Youre never leaving that listFilled with those consumed with deceptionYOU LIED!!
