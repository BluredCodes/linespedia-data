title: "Smoke In My Own Night Sky"

Burning embers...
A smoke trail twisting off into the night sky
The sweet smell of jasmine fills my heart
A slow stare across the star lit sky...
Stars..  flying in the night
Dancing.. putting me into a trance..
Pulling my mind away from past pains
Replacing the sharp steel with warm wool
Replacing the shards of glass with clear vision
Staring at the smoke again..
Wishing I lived in it
Constantly traveling upward
Towards the stars...
Towards the moon
Towards what I love
The freedom of open space
No pain.. No hurt..  No sorrow..
Just pure freedom
I would look straight into the sun
Asking for one thing..
