---
title: "The Angels Have Gone In For The Night"
writer: "Netsubo"
slug: "the-angels-have-gone-in-for-the-night"

Two shadows..
Coming from the same place
Yet only one makes a difference
While the other blends in.

I take a deep breath..
Yet I still feel empty
Can so much be lost..
And something still be left behind?

The angels have gone in for the night..
And a teary eyed glaze is so distorted..
I wonder if this is real
And if those passing souls hear my screams..

One answered my call
Although it had been long ago..
And we exchanged a few words..
A kind courtesy, for a fallen angel..

But the tears in my eyes were hidden
By the blinks of pain..
As the light drew forth,
To me

I yelled out a few more cries of pain..
And decided to follow the angels
Goodnight..  for now..
Sweet slumber will help me escape..
This teary eyed glaze, for awhile..
