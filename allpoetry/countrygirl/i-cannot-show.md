---
title: "I Cannot Show"
writer: "countrygirl"
slug: "i-cannot-show"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/47005-I-Cannot-Show-by-countrygirl"
---

i cannot show my feelings.
i musn't show my feelings.
that's what i was always told.
so now after all these years---
i have hidden all my feelings.
i cannot show;i musn't show.
now i don't even know
what my feelings are.
