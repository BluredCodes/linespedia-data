title: "who am i now?"

who am i now?
now that i'm alone?
i'm sitting by the phone
waiting for it to ring
but i haven't heard a thing
since december
who am i now?
now that no one can remember me?

i used to think i mattered to someone
i used to think someone would care.
but now that i'm all alone,
it's like no one is even aware of me.

how did all this happen?
how did i let this all be?
who am i now?
now that there's only me.i don't really feel like this now; but i have in different times of my life. i've learned how to reach out whenever that alone feeling starts!
