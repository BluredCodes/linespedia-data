title: "Today Might Just Be"

Today might just be the day I have waited for, prayed for, hoped for.
I want so much for this day to be free of wonderings.
How will I do this?
How will I be able to do that?
Who can help me with this?
Who will help me with all the other things?
All those thoughts intruding into the moments of my life.
I want to enjoy the little moments.
I don't want to concentrate on the how to's.
I want to lavish myself with the yes I can's.
I just want to enjoy again!
