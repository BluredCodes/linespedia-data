Domesticated violence,
Thrives upon our hate.
Twisted in our heads it's,
Not to celebrate.

Once your love is gone,
Your hate is free to roam.
Unwary of authority,
Hate consumes its own.

Gropeing at our bodies,
Pulling at our hair.
Hate destroys our innerselves,
There's murder in the air...

Hateful dayz bring hateful nights,
And hateful nights bring dreams.
Dreams of torment, blood and death,
Reality.............. In dreams.
