I have a friend, He is a Crab.
His name is Jim, And he is sad.

I have a friend, He is a Cat.
His name is Bob, And here he sat.

I had a friend, He was a Goat.
I took a knife, And slit his throat.

I have a friend, He is a Frog.
The hungry cunt, He ate my dog.

I have a friend, He is a Horse.
He killed a man, With no remorse.

I had a friend, He was a Fish.
