title: "Love Is Repitition"
writer: "KozMic BluEs"

How many times have i told you?
How many times have you lied?
How many times have you done this to me?
How many times have i tried?
I wanted to love and to hold you.
I wanted to hold you and smile.
I wanted to have you to love me right back.
I wanted to be in your eyes.
How could you do this to me?
How could you do this to us?
How could you bring this on yourself?
How could in you, I trust?
You brought him home to our house.
You brought him home to our bed.
You brought him home to the place where we live.
You brought him and now he is dead.
I'm sorry I got so angry.
I'm sorry I drew the gun.
I'm sorry I pulled the trigger.
I did what had to be done.
