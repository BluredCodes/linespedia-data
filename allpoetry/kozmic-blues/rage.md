Fuck off
I hate you
Go die
Burn in hell
Die
Die
Die
Fucking bastards
I hate you all
You've pained me for far too long now and it stops here
Fuck you, I hope you suffer a slow and agonising death.
And if it doesn't come soon, I'll make it.Yesterday was not a good day in the life of Hayden.
That would be me, idiot. (Not you Nell, or you other lovely people who leave me wonderful notes, but all you others....)
