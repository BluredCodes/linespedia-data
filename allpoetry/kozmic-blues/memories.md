I
Just
Can't
Take
It
Any
More
STOP KILLING ME!
STOP RAPEING ME!
STOP HURTING ME!
Just let me be.

Where you walk, I fall.
Where I fall, you fly.
Where you live, I die.
Where I die, You die.
Because I'm gonna take you with me.
You've caused me so much hurt and anguish,
And now it's your turn for pain.
It's your turn for torment.
The everlonging sound of grinding flesh as it's ripped from your legs.
As your ankles are shattered, your fingers snapped, your knee-caps...

Disintegrated.

I hate you and I want you to die.

You have no idea of the harm you have done.
But soon, you won't just feel it.
You will die from it.
Slowly.
So you can feel every last bit of pain you have caused me.
So very, very slowly.
You will die.
