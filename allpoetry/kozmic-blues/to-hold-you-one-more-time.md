I watched you slip away
I felt the blood run down my arms
As i held you
And watched you slip away
Towards the evershining light.
I looked into your eyes and watched you.
I watched you slip away.
You stared into the cold of night.
Your breath floating away
On icey whisps of wind.
As i held you in my arms
And watched you slip away.
