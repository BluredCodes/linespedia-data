My body's buzzing, My mind is numb.
Goodness gracious, Oh what fun.
These drugs I'm on, They are most great.
They ease the pain, My mind's sedate.
They help me live, They help me breath.
They make me think, The world's at peace.
Vibrant colours, Neon lights.
Sounds that echoe, Through the night.
In the fog, I see a beast.
I see it's headz, All three of these.
Towards me now, The beast doth come.
Towards myself, It does run.
It's breathing flame, It's eyes glow red.
I shit my pants, I fart instead.
It spins me out, It spins me in.
It smoke my mind, And breathes it in.
I drift away, I drift to sea.
I have more drugs, Come drift with me...
