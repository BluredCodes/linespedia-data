Psycho Psycho Chipmunk
Kills and hunts with style/funk
Shot a little Squirrel punk
Square between the eyes.

Little Little Squirrel Pal
Ran away and got help
Psycho Psycho Chipmunk
Shot him in the back.

Daddy Daddy Squirrel Man
Come and see what's happenin'
Psycho Psycho Chipmunk
Shot your little son.

Mommy Mommy Squirrel Whore
Get on the phone and call the law.
Daddy Daddy Squirrel Man's
Got a score to set.

Psycho Psycho Chipmunk
Run away from Daddy Punk
Coz Daddy Punk is pissed now
And wants to break your neck.

Hide behind the chestnut tree
Daddy's blind with rage you see
But Daddy spies you near the tree
Quickly, shoot him dead.

Psycho Psycho Chipmunk
I'm sorry but you're outta luck
The cops are here to bust your arse
But kill the ones you can.

High up in the treetops, right here in the park 
Are sounds of gunfire blasting loud
As Psycho Pyscho Chipmunk,
Slaughters one more crowd.
