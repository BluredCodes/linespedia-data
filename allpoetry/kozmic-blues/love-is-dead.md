---
title: "Love Is Dead"
writer: "KozMic BluEs"
slug: "love-is-dead"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/47448-Love-Is-Dead-by-KozMic-BluEs"
---

Love me, love me.
Hate Me
Die

You break my heart girl.
Why Girl
Why

Cut me, cut me.
Watch me
Bleed

This is how you.
Hurt me
Please

Tears in vain now.
Cry I
Cry

Love is lost so.
Die I
Die

I want you, need you
Hate you
Dread

Hurt and loathing
Heart is
Dead
