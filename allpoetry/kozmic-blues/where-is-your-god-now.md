title: "Where Is Your God Now"

Ring around the rosie, Your life is nice and cozy,
Fuck you, Fuck you, I hope you drown.
Your cozy little cottage, And a white picket fence.
You think you own it all, But it's all just false pretense.
All the land and stock, Your life of innocence.
All the trees and plants, It's all the governments.
Don't be fucking stupid, I fucking hate you all.
You think you're all just so damn smug, You can't see hate at all.
Well listen Jack, now I got news. You live in a world of greed.
You're just too fucking blind to see. Little Freddy's fucked on speed.
Yeah, that's right man, Your precious little boy.
He hooked up with the wrong damn crowd, And now his drug's his ploy.
He's bunking school and getting high, His life is just a mess.
He turned up stoned to class today, And flunked his IQ test.
I'm sorry Jack and Jill, Your boy's fucked up and fled.
A deal he made went so, so wrong, And now he's turned up dead.
You made your bed, now sleep, You prayed for Freddy, true.
You closed your eyes and prayed for good, Your God has failed you.
He's gone now, gone for good, To heaven you believe.
But Freddy was a bad, bad boy, He's gone down far beneath.
You thought your God would save your son, You thought he would save him.
But God fucked up, he fucked up big, Yet, STILL you pray to him.
I shake my head and walk away,
I cannot stand for this.
I'm sick of seeing people pray,
YOUR GOD IS FULL OF SHIT!!!
