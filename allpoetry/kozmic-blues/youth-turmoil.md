title: "Youth Turmoil"
writer: "KozMic BluEs"

PAIN shoots through my body.
BLOOD streams from the wounds.
LIFE is quickly leaving.
DEATH slowly consumes.

This world I leave behind me,
This one I do despise.
With love no longer in me,
My hate is sure to rise.

I cut and feel the pain,
Longing for my death.
And as I see the blood flow,
I want just one more breath.

The anger festers in my head,
Tormenting me with rage.
Your life is just a crock of shit,
And things get worse with age.

Everyday I read it,
Everyday I see.
Our youth is lived in anguish,
And everyone's like me.

Noone here to hold me,
Noone by my side.
I am all alone now,
And from myself I hide.
