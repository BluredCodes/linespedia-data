title: "Watching The World From A Small Dark Room"

I'm blind to this world
Blind to great mother earth.
I want to see your face
I want to see the mirth
I can't view the beauty
I cannot see the hate.
I can't predict the future
I just lie here in wait.
Knowing what's around me
Unable to just see
Only thoughts inside my head.
Only blind by me.
I could open up my eyes.
I could see the facts
I could watch the war and death
But why would I do that?
Push the button
Power on.
See the armies
March along.
Turn the TV off now,
It's isn't worth my time.
There are more important things
Than watching people die.
I'm blind to this world
Blind to all that is.
I cannot see the hate and death.
And I am glad for this.
