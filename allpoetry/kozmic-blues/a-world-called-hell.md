---
title: "A World Called Hell"
writer: "KozMic BluEs"
slug: "a-world-called-hell"

I've seen better days,
All my life in a haze.
Running through this solid maze,
This is no fad, this is a faze.
Watch you now, you run like sheep,
Waking from your restful sleep.
You dodge the bombs,
You shoot your guns.
You kill the people,
The ones who run.
What is this now?
Why can't you see?
That they are you,
And you are me.
You look at life,
Through tunnel vision.
You set your goals,
You stock provisions.
What's the point of living life,
With no risk of pain, no chance of strife.
How can u live to the fullest potential,
When what u do is far too gentle.
Get out there, and live your life.
Go get married, get a wife.
Have some children.
Teach them well.
And let them live
In this World called Hell.
