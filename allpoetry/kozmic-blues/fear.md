Be afraid.
I am.
Afraid of myself.
Afraid because of the sick and twisted dreams that plague my mind each night as I sleep. 
Dreams that not only manipulate my thoughts, but change my way of thinking entirely.
I dream of holding an axe. 
I am at a school and I see all the people that I hate in the world, crowded onto the field. 
I walk towards them and swing the glistening death-bringer. 
Decapitating and disemboweling each one. 
Watching their entrails fall from their bodies with merciless joy. Now I see myself.
My head splits in two and my brain falls out.
I have lost my mind. My head reseals itself, and the onslaught begins all over again. 
I am afraid, that if this power is harnessed and unleashed, of what I could do.
The entire world would speak but a single word.

Fear
