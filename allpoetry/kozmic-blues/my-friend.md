I did wish upon a star.
I wished you were dead.
I wished it so hard.

I wished for a dagger.
Plunged straight through your heart.
I wished for it turned.
To rip you apart.

I wanted you dead.
Your fear I could smell.
Your life was worth nothing.
I sent you to hell.

But then it did hit me.
What had you done?
And then I remembered.
You gave her a son.

You fucked her so hard.
You fucked her right through.
You gave her a child.
Inside her it grew.

We were friends, you and I.
I thought we were mates.
But you had to go fuck her.
You just couldn't wait.

Inside me I knew.
I always had known.
I want to forget.
YOU LEAVE US ALONE!

KEEP AWAY FROM ME NOW!
STAY AWAY FROM MY WIFE!
HOW DO YOU SLEEP?!
YOU'VE RUINED MY LIFE!Desiree Is Beautiful
