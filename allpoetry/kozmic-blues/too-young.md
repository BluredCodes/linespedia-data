---
title: "Too Young"
writer: "KozMic BluEs"

Shiver and quiver
And blur at some speed.
Reality's gone
I know what you need.
You're tired and beaten
You tremble and shake.
I want just to help you
Your life's a mistake.
A wandering mistress
Asleep in a ditch.
I found her so cold
Too long since her fix.
It's hooked to her arm
The needle of death.
There's nought can be done
She breathes her last breath.
Slowly she dies
This junkie so young.
A tear down my cheek
Now dead she's become.
