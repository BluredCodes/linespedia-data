title: "The Bond That Ties"
writer: "KozMic BluEs"

I love you so, Please break free
I want your love, so constantly
Stand up straight, You're down, not out.
I can't stay here, with you without.
I'm here for you, Please reach to me
I want to help, to set you free.
From dark times here. I want them gone.
Our friendship bonds, Our love so strong.
Heal my heart, mend to yours.
Our love is strong, for now contour.
But fill the void, and kill the pain.
Turn your life, around again.
I know you can, I'll see you through.
For you love me, and you know...
I love you.
