There was an eerie chill in the air that night.
That night when we walked along the dark, lonely road.
I didn't see him appear, not until he rubbed against my leg.
And then the hammer, the hammer in my hand.
I swung it low.
Swift,
Deadly.
Bloody,
Brutal.
I killed my poor cat, and the blood was there to show.
I don't know why I did it. I don't why he died.
I don't know why he died by me. I know that now I cry.
The line between reality and fantasy draws thin.
I wake up and pat my beloved friend.
Asleep at the foot of my bed.
How could I kill him?
Even in a dream.
So again I lay down, again I drift off...
Again I walk along a dark and lonely road, an eerie chill in the air...This was a dream I had, I called my cat (Jordy) with a hammer.... Yes, it was quite odd....
