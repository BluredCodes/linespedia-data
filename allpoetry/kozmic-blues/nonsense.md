title: "Nonsense"
writer: "KozMic BluEs"

Open your mind,
Let in the light,
Release the darkness,
Into the night.

Black on black,
White on white,
Children playing,
Through the night.

Body rots,
And mind decays,
Black on black,
It's all a haze.

Children screaming,
Children dying,
White on white,
Angels flying.

A man is murdered,
A man is dead,
A man is sitting,
Awake in bed.

The dawn is now,
Of new tomorrow,
A sickled weapon,
He doth borrow,

Unto thee i vow,
This curse of hate,
Thou shalt stand,
At Satan's gate.

Cut the last flower,
Chop the last tree,
But I, my friend,
Am infinitly....
