Jim Mcleod was a quaint old man,
And Jim did own a gun.
He drew it from his bag of tricks,
And blew away his son.

Janet Rose did love the church,
But Janet had a knife.
She plucked it from her kitchen sink,
And took her very own life.

Timmy Jones was just a boy,
But Timmy owned a dart.
And when his parents went away,
He stabbed it through his heart.

Karen Hose was but a girl,
But all her friends were dead.
She took a meat hook from the fridge,
And plunged it through her head.

Now Timmy had a lovely dog,
He called his pet dog, Fox.
He fell from high atop a cliff,
And splattered on the rocks.

Karen had a great white horse,
Her horses name was Coast.
He tried to jump a wooden fence,
But was impaled upon a post.

Gregory Chapell, the butcher's son,
It's lucky I remembered.
He took a cleaver from the bench,
His father, he dismembered.

Now Jason Leir had bought a gun,
He shot it through his head.
The bullet travelled round his skull,
And killed his brother, Ned.Well I find it humourous....
