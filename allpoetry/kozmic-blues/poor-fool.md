---
title: "Poor Fool"
writer: "KozMic BluEs"
slug: "poor-fool"
source: "AllPoetry"

A lost hope
A hated life
I wanted you
I want my knife
To slice it out
To cut the pain
It shall ne'er
Hurt me again
Burgeoning its way through me
Ripping at my soul that's weak
Tearing at my flesh that's strong
Breaking through them both complete
So sad and down
No life, just hate
So dead and drowned
In a cold, dark lake
A lake of hated, vile stench
A lake of death to innocence
A lake that kills but one true kind.
A lake that's here, deep in my mind.
Pity me for I'm alone
Spit on me
I don't belong
Hate me
Rape me
Kill Me
Destroy me as I have destroyed myself
I fear you not
But I long for your hands around my neck
To choke me, to help me die, to remove my sins, to purify my evil.
Laugh at me.
I deserve it all.
