Let yourself go,
And come with me now.
I know you're in pain,
And it pains me to see.
Let's go together.
Just you beautiful,
And me.
I can't stand to see you crying,
I can't bear to see you hurt.
I want you to be happy,
To be happy just with me.
I love you.
Never forget.
I love you.
Come with me now,
And don't ever look back.
Come with me,
Stay with me.
We'll be together, forever.
Forever.
I love you my darling.
Take my hand,
It's not so bad.
We'll be together,
Always.
Take my hand and walk with me.
We'll just keep walking,
Straight into the vast blue ocean.
And we'll be together.
Always.
Don't cry my love, just remember,
I will always love you.
Now,
