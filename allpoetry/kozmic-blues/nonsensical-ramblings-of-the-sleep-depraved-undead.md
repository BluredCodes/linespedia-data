Insanity insanity,
It is my abnormality.
Knawing on my mind,
And feasting on my brain,
Insanity deludes my thoughts,
And back it comes again.
Watching in despair,
Az prickles prick my hair,
Insanity insanity,
Is anybody there?
Emersing in my brain,
Embedded in my mind,
Insanity insanity,
Is there another kind?Don't ask...
