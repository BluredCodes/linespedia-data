---
title: "What The World Wants"
writer: "KozMic BluEs"
slug: "what-the-world-wants"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/49333-What-The-World-Wants-by-KozMic-BluEs"
---

The world sees only what the world wants through the worlds eyes. 
Never caring. 
The world is a cold place, full of hatred and misery.
That cannot change.
Your own world falls apart randomly before your very eyes. 
Denying the fact of your unworthy existence.
The rain washes away the blood that pours from the open wound of an innocent, gored violently to death with a knife. 
But the world turns it's head and blatantly dismisses this.
The world sees only what the world wants through the worlds eyes.
