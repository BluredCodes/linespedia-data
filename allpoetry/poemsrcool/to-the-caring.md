Whatz more important to you
having lotz of friends who don't really 
give a shit bout what you do
or having just a few friends that care
and would give anything to see you happy?

I have a few friends who care
so much bout me i find a little too much
but they are what keep me
going and help me try to forget
bout the bad things that happen to
me, they give me a new prospective 
to the word "caring"!!

I love you all so very much and this
poem is for all the people out there
who care enough to be there!!
