You help me
your there for me
you tell me the truth even though i don't 
want to hear it..
friends are supose to be there for each other..
they...help you
guide you 
talk to you 
let you cry on there shoulder
and you...
you are my friend and you let me do all these things and more....
i'm so grateful for that
with out you i would never
be able to move on with my life
i would be in a deep dark whole 
but i'm not and thats all cause of you
you helped me out you guided me back to the right track
thanks alot for being there....dedicated to heather ( best bud )
