---
title: "Would you"
writer: "poemsRcool"

source: "AllPoetry"

would you cry 
over me if
i wasn't there 
if i never exsisted
would you cry if
all of a sudden i 
decided to leave
and never come back
would you cry over me 
if i would cry over you?
