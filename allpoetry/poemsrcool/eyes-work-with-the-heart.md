i was lookin at you earlier on today
i was watchin you just standin there
waiting not talkin with your friends
just starin off in to space
i was wonderin what you were thinking
what was goin on in your head....
 
no matter what you've done
to me in the past what you've said
that doesn't change the fact that 
i can't get you outta my head
that i can't seem to love anyone else more than
i love you..

it might sound weird a 15 year old gurl who
is sayin the word "love"
but its true i do love you 
so much with all my heart
no matter how much you've cause me pain
i've still found a place in my heart to forgive you
i guess when you see somethin you truely desire
your eyes work with your heart....
nothin matters more than the feelin you get
when you see the person you truely "love"aww no matter how much i try i truely can't for get you i will never be over you!
