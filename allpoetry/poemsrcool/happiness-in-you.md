Happiness in you...
makes me feel not so happy
as i watch you fall for someone new
i can tell your in "love"
some way or another
your always talkin about her
and thinkin of her
you seem so happy why can't i
just be happy for you
why do i have to feel so left
behind....and jelous
this isn't me
why do i have to be like this i want to feel
happy for you but i don't

i put on a happy face and try and help 
you with her but it doesn't change the fact that
sooner or later your gonna go out with someone else
and i just wanted you for my self
for ever and ever...
but your movin on now
and i have no control over it.....dedicated to shawn.....
