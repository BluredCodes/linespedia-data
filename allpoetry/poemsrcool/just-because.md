Just because....
you love me doesn't mean i have to love you back
just because you hate me and treat me badly
doesn't mean i have to do it to you
this world is made up of people who believe in revenge
and I think that if someone who has hurted you doesn't mean you have to do something back to them
something much more hurtful...that could hurt that person forever...
just because you have said that you don't feel the same way 
i feel for you doesn't mean i have to stop loving you
doesn't mean i have to put my feelings aside 
becuase you don't feel the same way
i shouldn't have to bottle up my emotions this is a free
world and everyone is allowed to show there feelings for someone how
ever they want
just because love means everything to me and means nothing 
to you doesn't mean i have never show my love for you.... because i do and you mean everything to me and some day i will show you 
just how much you mean to me!!!do you feel the same way???
