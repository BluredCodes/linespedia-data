people whispering
pointing, laughing
calling names 
being quiet as i pass and then laughing as i walk 
away..
do you know why they are doing this,.... all 
because of one thing...
people always believe what they hear even 
though you tell them its not true
doesn't it hurt doesn't it make you feel
mad and hurt all at the same time.......
the people that should be mad and hurting are 
the people that do it to you who cause 
you all this pain and discomfort.
