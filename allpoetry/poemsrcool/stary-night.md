Have you ever looked up at the
stars and wondered what else is out ther?
could there be anything else other than
us here?

the stars are so beautiful you
often wonder what makes them so
beautiful what makes them shine,
bright what makes them what they 
are why are stars so mysterious
is it because they hold a hole
other world on the other side
or is it because thats the
jobs if the stars to keep 
us wondering?!?!
