am i lonely?
i feel as if i am;
no one seems to understand
what i am feeling at that time
I try to talk to some people
but they don't have that answer
I wanna hear
all they do is critize
and tell me not to feel this way 
but why is what i'm feeling  not real
why am i different?
is this why i'm lonely?
