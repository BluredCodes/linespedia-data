you've changed me forever
one night you took advantage of me
you touched me in places i didn't like....
you held me close to you so i couldn't move away
I told you to let me go or I'll scream
you did the right thing by letting
me go but that doesn't make 
a difference...
you've changed me,
how i feel towards people touching me
and most important how i feel about myself
you've changed me forever.
