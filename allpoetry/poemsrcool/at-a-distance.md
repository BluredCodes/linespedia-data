I watch you from a distance
watching you talk and laugh with all your friends
wanting you with me 
wanting to hold you
have you all to my self 
but being me i'm too shy to go up to you
and say "hey" i only go up to you 
when your alone and thats not to often
is that cause you don't like to be lonely thats 
why your always with your friends and i never have a chance to 
spend time with you and when i do ppl always have to come up
and stand there too....when i try to make plans with you
you just put them off thats why i watch you 
from a distance.....
its much more pleasent.!for all those gurls out there who watch there guys instead of going up to them and talkin to them
