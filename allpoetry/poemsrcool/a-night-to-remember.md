as you pulled me into 
your room i thought we
were gonna hang out, but
you wanted to do more than
"juts hang out"

as you started to un do my
shirt i started to feel ashamed
that i'm takin this, letting 
you do this to me hurt me
emotionally and physically

by why i ask? why
did i let you do this to me
was it because i was afraid 
to say stop or was it because
i was afraid that you would tell
other people maybe that was
it i dunno,

till this day i still dunno why
i took this from you why i didn't 
just fight back and tell you
to get the fuck off me!! 
why??*sigh* its not exactly what happened but close enough
