your palms are sweaty
your heart is beating really fast
you don't know what to say
everything you do or say evolves around that person
the feeling of being in love 
its a feeling of a life time 
some people experience it more than others
but when you do it feels really good
some people get addicted to love
were they think they are in love when the really aren't
but you must know when your really in love
its a feeling you can't shake
something that makes you feel the happiest all the time
when you feel it you know its love
love is something every body experiences
but every one is loved and thats not a feeling 
thats BIG its the feeling of being in love thats what really
feels good...to be in love
why when we fall in love were more happy then 
any other normal day is it because 
its the best feelin were you feel as if nothing
can put you done or is something 
no one can explain its just a natural function we
