the heat from the fire creates warmth inside of my heart
as i watch the flames i dream of a perfect life 
without stress and broken hearts
i think of everyone around me 
my friends and my family
these people are the most important things 
to make a life perfect even though at times it may cause
stress and broken hearts in the end its all worth it
as the warmth leaves my heart as the fire dies all that feeling
i have just experienced has drifted away i am my same old self
again it was all a dream a "day dream."why do we dream if its all fake it just keeps us from wanting more?
