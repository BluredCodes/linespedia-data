You can't help who you love
no matter how much you try to just forget about the person
or just totally ignore them 
they always find a way to your heart
its just something about them that 
your attracted to
what is it? is it there smile
there laugh they way they make you laugh when you thought
no one could
the way you just look at them and everything else 
doesnt matter only that person
no one is perfect untill you fall in love with them
when your in love you dont notice the imperfections that person has
only the good things and good side side of that person 
that one person could be the best thing that ever happened to you
but sometimes there not 
and you just dont want to stop liking/loving them
but 
You can't help who you love*sigh*
