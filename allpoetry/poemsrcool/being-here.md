being here on your wedding day
is something i will always remember
its not the fact that it was your wedding day 
and you too are now one its the whole
fact that you now are married and that
you too have created a life of your own 
and i wish you too the best of wishes..

being here on your wedding day 
is special but being at YOUR wedding
something i've wanted for such a long time its
not just the fact that i get to dress up in 
a pretty dress and get to stay up late and
party all night its the fact that i'm here
at YOUR wedding the fact that you are finally married 
feels me up with joy that i can not explain

being here with you on your wedding day
is something that i will always remember and something
that is very special to me!My uncle has not yet married but he has been with his girlfriend now for a very long time for like ever all the years i can remember hes been with her and i will read this to him at his wedding day when ever that will be!!!
