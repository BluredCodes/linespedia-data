do you know 
how hard it is to let go
of something
you love so very much
and worked so hard for. its something
people shouldn't have to experience but sometimes
its for the best but
at that time its the worst thing ever
but thats just how things happen
maybe theres better things out there
I hope theres better out there for me.....??
