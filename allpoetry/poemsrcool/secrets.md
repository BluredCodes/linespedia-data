Once you tell someone
something about your self that is very
deep and personal...
that tells the person that you 
trust the them and you can count on them
Now if that person feels as if they have to 
tell someone else this little secret about you then that 
is someone you shouldn't trust someone
you must be careful of what you say to them
because at any time they can turn there back on you
Secrets are powerful and you must chose carefully
who you tell them too, becuase if you just tell them to someone
who doesn't care bout you or anything
it will hurt you and cause you pain you can't imagine.its happened before to me so becareful
