---
title: "5 minutes of fame"
writer: "poemsRcool"
slug: "5-minutes-of-fame"
source: "AllPoetry"

you change from year to year
never staying the same person
you've changed for the better though 
in your looks your style and your personality
your a way better person i really like you the way you are
but why is it so hard for me to accept 
that you could be better than i am
is it because i use to have it all
the looks the grades the friends 
but you had nothing and yet now you have
more than i do and i feel so left behind
like i have nothing no more as if i have no reason
to go to school to do my homework to even get up in the morning
i loved the feeling of knowing i was the smartest of them all
i was the prettyest 
but now you've changed for the better and i love you the 
way you are but why did you have to take away all i ever 
loved all i ever wanted
i guess every ones 5 minutes of fame run out sooner
or later....
