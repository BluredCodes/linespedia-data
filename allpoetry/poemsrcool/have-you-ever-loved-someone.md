title: "have you ever loved someone"

have you ever loved someone for
what they are and what they mean to you
have you ever loved someone for how they make you feel
not by what they feel
have you ever loved someone and they didn't
love you back
have you ever loved someone who you didn't want to love
but your heart knew the love was right for you so
slowly you started to fall for them
have you ever loved someone and 
never told them and they moved on to be with someone
else because you failed to tell them what you really felt?
I have....
