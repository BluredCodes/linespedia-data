Don't you wish you had someone
all to your self
not having to share them and just be with them
all the time without anyone commenting on you
dont you just wish you could kiss him and find out what 
itz like to have that feeling of your first kiss
dont you wish you could try something with them
something you wouldnt want to with anyone else
you see the best in that person but yet 
why doesnt any body else see what you see in him?
