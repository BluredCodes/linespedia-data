My eyes are all dry i can not cry another tear 
no matter how much i try i can't cry another tear
no matter how much i want to i can't
why is that?
in a time i find would be ok to cry i can't because if
i could i would feel so much better cause
its my release cause i must cry to feel
better so as long as i don't cry i will be this way.....
