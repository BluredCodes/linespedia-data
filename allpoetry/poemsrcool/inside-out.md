Don't let my image fool you
I may look happy
I may look cheerful
and i may act as if i have no care
in the world but i do i care so much
i find to much...
I may be happy and smile all the time 
help people out and never be sad nor angry
I'm always happy but if you would sit down with me
one night one time and just talk to me
you would find out i'm not ok i'm hurting
more than anyone could image i'm goin through
so much but the worse thing is i have to hide it just so that 
i don't show you who i really am cause maybe just maybe
you would find out who i am and i'm not to sure
i want that right now!!!i'm like this with my friends at school but i'm a completly different person on the net....
