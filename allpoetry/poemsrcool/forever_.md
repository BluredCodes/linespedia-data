Forever am i yours i will never find another
person who cares as much for me as you do
i love you and cherish you for doin all you've done for me
you fight off the guyz that hurt me
and you tell me to hold on longer to the good guyz
when i come on the internet to talk to you about a fight 
i just had with my parents you don't seem to
want to change the subject you just comfort me
tell me it'll be alright tell me how much you hate
to hear about the fights but tell me you feel
my pain and that makes me feel really good
like you can feel my pain and have pain the same time
as me and be happy when i'm happy...
you asked me all the time weither i'm happy cause 
i just went through a tough time and just you askin me if i'm 
alright makes me feel as if i'm cared about 
and i can't tell you how much i care for you cause
its impossible for me to do so.....

but what u can say is thanxs and i wouldn't 
be able to be were i am today if it wasn't for you.....dedicated to best bud....
