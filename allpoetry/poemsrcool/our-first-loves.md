your heart pounds really fast
when that special someone comes into 
contact with you
you stumble on your words 
not knowing what to say 
just the mention of his name
gives you butterflies in your stomach
making you want to melt inside
just the thought of him makes your 
face light up
and you start to glow

first loves are great 
if only they didn't cause so much pain.
