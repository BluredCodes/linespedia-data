i was yelled at 
for doing nothin
for not doing the right thing
for doing something 
someone didn't agree on
why was i made fun of infront
of so many people i loved and cared for 
afterwards i saw myself talking to a certin
someone that which i was close to
while him telling me
its ok dont listen to what he says
i was crying my eyes out and yelling 
at that other person cause i was so very 
upset...
why would he say all that doesn't he love me?
and my certin someone i was talking to and confide in 
came over gave me a hug and wouldn't let go of me 
just letting me cry in his arms......doesn't this sound so very real...it was one of my dreams i have had lately and afterwards i woke up and i felt as if it had happened....huh...confusing?!?!?!
