he helps me through everything
through things my friends can't
he talks to me and has all
the patience inthe world for me
he explains things to me and
helps me figure out things that 
i couldn't do my self.

he says do well in school
and becareful or what you 
do because i care and
when he syas that i feel 
like theres nothing in the world that
could hurt me because hes 
with me 24 hours a day

helping me, guiding me
and protecting me from
the most harmful things
and i can not express my 
delight i have when he 
tells me how much  he cares

and all he wants to see is me
happy but if one day i'm 
hurt or feeling not my self
all i gotta do is pick up the phone
and hes there for me through it
all no matter what, because he 
cares i will be the best i can be......

I love you
dedicated to the worldest greatest uncle....
'walter"
