I could never imagine
you with someone else
seeing you with 
her each day hurts
to much to even start
to explain it!

I'm just gonna have to 
live with it because
i never thought you
could love anyone else
other than me but i guess you can
I could never imagine 
you with anyone else 
other than me why did
you have to move on so fast
I thought you were mine forever
but you thought otherwise...*sigh*
