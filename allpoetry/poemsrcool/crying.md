I cry each night 
all because of you 
you hurt me, whatever you do 
will never change the things
you've done and said
those mean and hurtful words
will live on inside me forever
as i cry, i feel alone and unloved
like no one cares cause i am alone
in this mean and cruel world and if anyone 
is as cruel as you I'll have many more sad nights,  
why do guys i give my heart to always rip it out like this?.....
