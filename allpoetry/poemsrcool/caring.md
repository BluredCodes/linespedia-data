Why doesn't he care?
theres only one thing in my life that i need
is for ppl to care
even if they don't care at all they could at least pretend
when ppl show me that they don't care bout anything or just show like
absolutly no feelins at all it makes me feel as if i'm
not good enough as if what i'm goin is silly or foolious 
why is what i do not important to him can't he see that 
it means everything to me??i wrote this afterwards i told someone close to me what i do i write poems.....
