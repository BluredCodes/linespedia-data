feel free and good about yourself
for it does not last long
take advantage of every good thing
every nice person every free ride
be good to someone sometime
pass on your good kindness
smile its a attractive thing
once you smile the next person after that will smile also
and that one after that....it just keeps on going
and going

when times are ruff and not going so good look to
the left for there will be your best friend
in the whole world someone you can always count on to
be there for you in the weirdest times

when times are good and your ready to laugh and feel
good about your self again
call your friends up and just chill 
somewere either at the mall or even watching a movie one 
late saturday night...
when you feel free and good about your self
everyone else can see it in you and
that is something that doesnt happen to often
so take advantage of it my friends!!!!
