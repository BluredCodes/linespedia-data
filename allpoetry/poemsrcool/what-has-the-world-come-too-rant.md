These days you dont go into the store
and buy candy for a penny
you cant walk to school and be free 
no you have to watch your back
we arent safe anymore
just when you think you are you get 2 dumbass
guyz who drive 2 planes into a building and die
trying to prove a point 
that makes me sick why dont we do something
instead of sitting on our asses watching T.V and getting FAT
get out and do something be nice to someone
maybe you'll pass on the good thing and someone will do it for the next
then we might prevent the next world disater...

Bombing in 95 
then the world trade centers in 2001
what the hell no one will ever be safe again
we must always watch our backs
not once must we let our guards down cause once we do then 
the problem starts
we have the terroisim, bombings, school shotings, the fuckers on the
internet lookin for young gurls to pray on
why cant people go on there own busness and leave
everyone alone
and just not cause anymore trouble 
cause i wouldnt want to see what the world looks like and what its come to
in the next 10 years....i'm actually afraid to see what it looks
like in the next 10 years it'll be in pretty bad shape
unless someone takes action and stops or at least trys and help 
to stop all this hurting of each other
what is the point in hurtiin other people
were do people see the joy in it
cause i see no joy i only see pain!!!
dont you?
