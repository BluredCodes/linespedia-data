Dieing scares me 
i dunno what it is really that scares me 
it could be the whole feeling of leaving this world 
and never coming back or it could be
that when i leave no one will remember me for who i was
they will just forget me and i don't want to be forgotten 
i want to be 
~*"Remembered"*~
