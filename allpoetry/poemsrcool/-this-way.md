go this way
you must go this way
its the better more likly chosen path
but what if i don't want to go that way
what will you do?
i want to go my way 
my own chosen path.
who cares if its not what others want
aren't we supose to lead and not follow others
havn't you been taught to do what makes you happy?
i have and if i wanna go this way instead
of going the way most people go doesn't that make me 
independent! i must follow my heart 
and all i want you to understand
is that you can either suport me
or just leave me cause i'm going 
this way>>>>
