At christams time everyone is happy not sad 
ppl gather around each other to wish them good health
and happyness in the new year but there is this one younge little girl who
sits in the corner doesn't participate in any activites she feels along 
she has no family, she is a orphan she lives in a group home
wishing that this year will be the year when she gets to go home and spend time with ppl that love and care for her as if she was one of there own....as families come and go lookin at each and every little child they always pass her and say nope she doesn't look queite good enough why she thinks to her self when doesn't ppl except me for who i am? is it my looks my smile what? 

The following year just around christmas time this little girl has given up all hope now..she doesn't believe in miracles she thinks no one wants her she says to her self "i will always be a orphan no one will accept me" and as a tear falls down her sweet face a young woman wipes it away and asks" poor child whats the matter...." the little girl says nothin and walks away...wait yells the young woman come with me, i have watched you over the years the way you smile the way you look at things the way you love life i want you to be my child....the little girl runs up to the young woman and gives her a hug and says you are "my miracle"anything is possible you just have to believe in it...!^*~
