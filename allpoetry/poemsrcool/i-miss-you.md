I miss you
i miss everyting about you
your smile your laugh
i wish everything would be the same as last
year i keep playing back those times
when you and i first met 
the first time i told you liked you
the first time i told you i loved you
i miss you so much 
whatever last year was,
will never happen again....*sigh*
