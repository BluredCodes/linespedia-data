Shawn, you are not only my life
you are also my friend 
i love you to death
everything about you makes me want
to be the best i can be
you have so much power over me
you smile i smile
i sometimes just take time outta my busy
life just to think of you
just to think ofyour sweet smile cute laugh and your 
way to make everything sound so perfect
you are the most important person to me right now
you are my first thought in the morning
and my last each night..

the only that isnt so perfect is that we are not together
oh how in a heart beat i would make your day just say yes to you
and go out with you but you seem not to want
to whatz taking you so long why wont you ask me
are you trying to see weither i am right for you or not
let me help you I AM perfect for you just give me once chance.....

i'd treat you better than better can be treated
