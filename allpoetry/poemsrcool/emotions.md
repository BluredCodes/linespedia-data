Breaking up a 
really good friendship gone bad
because of a problem 
causes a tear

recieving a email from a lover 
or best friend someone who you 
care for alot
causes a smile and great joy

seeing a friend for the very first time 
in months causes a tear
but this tear is different
then the ones we experience in a break up
this is a tear of joy

these are our 
emotions confusing yet good
at the same time.........
