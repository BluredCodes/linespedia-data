I have finally come to a point
were i can say
" I'm over you"
its something that has been very hard
for me to do in the past...
you have pushed me along and you have givin
me hints that you aren't to fond of me 
i was to blind to see them but now they 
are clear to me...
you don't like me you never did
you may be a friend but i wanted more
i wanted to have you all to my self
i wanted you to love me the way i loved you 
but you were somehow to afraid to let
me so this is why i'm sayin "i'm over you" 
I will never think of you the way i did 
Its over!.....dedicated to shawn
