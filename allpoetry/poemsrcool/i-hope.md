One day i want to wake up and have some lying there
beside me someone who loves me someone who says good morning to me
some one who asks how i'm doin when i don't look to happy
some one who will bring me breakfest in bed when i'm stuck 
in bed with the flu
I hope one day this will all come true..
some day with that some one special
