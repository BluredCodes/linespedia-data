Do you really see what i see?
i see things that you dont see you
only see the good things about me
you dont see my pain nor my broken heart
you believe in me you sometimes even believe
in me more than i believe in myself is that a good thing?
what cant i always see what others see in me?*sigh* i need extra pushs some days
