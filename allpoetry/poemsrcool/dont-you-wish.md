---
title: "Dont you wish?"
writer: "poemsRcool"

Don't you wish that at times
you really feel like crying your eyes out
you can't cry one tear
whatz up with that?
no matter how much you try to just let it all out
you can't
you just cant its like if you were
holding it all in and doing a really good job of it.....
but then when you dont wanna cry and be brave
you are the biggest baby there....
Don't you just wish you would cry when you want to
just to relive all the pain you've
held in for so long
just to make your self feel so much better maybe
you might even feel like your self again
Don't you wish??.........
