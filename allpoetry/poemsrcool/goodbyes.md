you've ignored it all night
you've never wanted it to come so fast
you never see them very often
even if they say oh you'll see me much more
you never seem to see them half as much as you'd like
why?
there headed to the door standed there makin
small talk puttin there jackets and shoes on
you know its comin up and sneakin up on you
like a knife in your back
you never wanted to see them go
the time pasted way to fast
now its time you feel the depression and sadnesss comin on
you are now holdin back all the tears and sadness you dont want to look
bad in front of them now you go in for the last kiss goodbye
for awhile to come now......if your lucky you get a good bye hug
they walk out the door
your thoughts are running wild
thinking
when why where?
Why is it so hard to say "Goodbye"*sigh* *snif snif*
