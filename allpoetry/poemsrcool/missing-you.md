I'm sitting here 
wishing you were
here with me
but why aren't you
is it because you have no time
for me or is it
you don't love me 
but thats impossible 
you love me and 
you can't be here cause the distance kills 
so its just me
i miss you terribly and i 
can't do anything about it
the only thing i can do is try
to deal with it 
it'l be hard but i'll try real hard 
just for you.lonely times are hard.....when you want some one to be with you
