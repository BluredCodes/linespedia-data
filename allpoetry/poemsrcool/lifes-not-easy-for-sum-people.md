lifes not easy for him
no matter how much he trys to get the gurlz 
he likes so very much it never
seems to work either they
dont like him back or his friends 
go out with them..so very sad
just why cant he find someone who will
like him back and were his friends dont fall for
i only wish for that just so i could see
him happy just so that he could have that feeling
everyone gets when knowing you have some one beside
you forever someone that will
accept you for who you are
either he gets the gurlz he doesnt like and they like him
or he likes them but he dont like them back...

i've had passed experiences with him
i to did like him in a way i couldnt shake
and i think that feeling will ever go away but
what i do know is that we will be friends no matter what 
i got his back i watch out for him if he finds a gurl 
or not he will always have me 
so either way he wins he always got someone 
to watch over him he will never be alone weither it'll
be me or some other gurldedicated to my lil buddy shawn...luv him so very much
