in the arms of my angel 
nothing could go wrong i was 
protected by the joy and happiness this angel 
carried around with her...
as i was held by her sweet soft arms that night i was
drifted away and taken into a world that only 
angels live so free and soft and love all around
nothin could go wrong there was always so many people around to
protect you...
why isnt there this many people in our dailys lives
that care and watch for us as these angels do
but i do believe that every one has a angel 
and my angel is with me everyday protecting me
and guiding me on my journey to growing up 
in the arms of my angel i'm loved and cared for...
something i cant give more thanxs for just to get me through
each day is the greatest gift ever....*sigh* it just came to me but its kinda confuzing i find....
