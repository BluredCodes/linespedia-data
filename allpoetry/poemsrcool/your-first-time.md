you open up with your life
you sound unsure of this one time
you said you were ready but now your not to sure
hes more experienced then you are
with his calm sweet voice he tells you 
its ok i'll go in gentally i wont do anything you dont want
alright you say and open up oh i can't quite have enough room open
open wider...
yah thats it nice and wide alright 
i'm gonna start to go in real slowly now
you can feel him intering inside of you something you havnt let anyone
do to you, you feel the tears comin on 
but you hold it all back dont want to look like
a baby in front of him now
in and out in and out he slowly caress' your skin its
ok were almost through
he finally takes it out you feel a sigh of relief
coming on
finally its over that wasnt so bad after all my first
time wasnt to bad i would do it again ...

now that wasnt to hard now was it i told you a 
cavity filling wasnt so bad.....
