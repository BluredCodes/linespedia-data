I hope one day after all this soul searching 
i find the better part of me
i've made losts of mistakes
but with ever mistake i've made i've learned from it
whatz right and whatz wrong


people come and go but friends are the ones
that stay with you through thick and thin
i've learned that once you've done something
you changed it you can only change the future
sometimes in life you do things
to fit in just so that your peers will 
see you as one of them but most teens
dont listen to there feelins they just go with
what everyone else is doing 
there tryin to be someone there not

I hope one day after all this thinkin 
and soul searchin i will find the better part of me
someone that doesnt judge people someone that does things
for her self and not what people want her to do
but if i've learned this much seems like i'm 
almost there to finding the 
better part of me*!*
