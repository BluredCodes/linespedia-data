We use to talk all the time
send notes back and forth in class
talk to each other as much as we could on the internet
now all it is
is Hey how r u?
thats it 
it doesnt go very far what happen
did something happen to us did something happen
i wasnt awhere of 
did you replace me with someone more exciting and
full of energy someone were you can experience that "new"
feeling with?? 
what happened i thought we would of at least stayed friends
but its seems as if you've moved on 
but you've moved on with out me and i thought
you would of taken me with you...
why didn't you?*sigh*
