if only i knew
i wouldn't of
gottin so involved 
in the whole mess
now i know what
to exspect so i'm 
keeping guard
if only you were 
nicer if only you
could of loved me the way
i love you
things would be different things 
would be good, I'd love you and you'd 
love me back
if only that day could exsist.!
