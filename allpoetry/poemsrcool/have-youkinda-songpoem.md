Have you ever loved somebody
so much that you couldn't sleep at night
Have you ever loved somebody so much 
that you couldn't think straight
you thought about this person 
so much you just couldn't think of
anyone or anything else
Have you ever loved somebody
so much that no matter how much that
person hurt you 
you would always go back to them
forgive what they did to you
and just pretend that nothin happened
Have you ever loved somebody so much
that you couldn't imagine your self 
falling for someone else other than that person
Have you ever..
