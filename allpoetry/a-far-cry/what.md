title: "What?"
writer: "A Far Cry"
slug: "what"

Stop staring
Whats the point?
Its out of reach
What was i reaching for?

Stop talking you'll make it worse
How can it be worse
it can be worse
What was I saying?

Stop
They will stare and laugh
who cares?
You do
Do I
Stop it
What was I doing?
