I am a robot,
an empty human shell.

Whats wrong corey
wheres your emotions
reach deep and pull them out corey,
your on the verge of waking up
becoming human
unbury what been lost corey.

but I want to sleep,
Ive been here so long he just wants to stay
my human side isnt mature
only the robot can bear it
humans are so cruel
so mean,
I doent want to be human again
if it means i have to become all that
humanity makes me sick
leave me be, corey is dead
i am an imaginary living bodymy real name is corey for those of you who dont know
