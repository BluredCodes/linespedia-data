---
title: "roses and tears"
writer: "A Far Cry"
slug: "roses-and-tears"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/22838-roses-and-tears-by-A-Far-Cry"
---

Its been so long,
so long since ive heard your voice,
your laugh.
Its been so long since ive allowed myself, 
to enjoy it without you.
Its been so long since i was myself,
since you made me who i was.
Its been so long since you left me,
with this rose and these tears.
