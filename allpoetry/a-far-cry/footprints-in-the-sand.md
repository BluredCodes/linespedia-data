title: "footprints in the sand"
writer: "A Far Cry"

AM I here?
am i now?
What is this thing
this thing i call life?
How can I prove that I live
when I want die.
How can i know i exist 
when all I leave
are fading footprints in the sand.
Am I alive
I wish i were dead
Do I exist,
please tell me,
please dont let, 
these footprints be earsed.
