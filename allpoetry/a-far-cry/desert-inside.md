---
title: "desert inside"
writer: "A Far Cry"
slug: "desert-inside"

I think I spoiled things,
I promise i didnt mean to.
I thought I had something,
but now its buried,
beneath the sands,
of this endless desert.

Tell me I can dig it up,
before the malestrom,
washes it all away.

I swear I can feel...
Deep inside myself,
I feel, I can feel...
You
