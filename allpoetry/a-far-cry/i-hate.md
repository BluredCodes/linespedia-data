---
title: "I hate"
writer: "A Far Cry"

How long has it been,
since you decided to leave?
You said you thought,
that you were better off.
But let me ask you something,
Did you find what you were looking for?

I hope you didnt.
I hope you lay, 
awake,
Dwelling in whatever hell your in.
I dont care.
I hate you now.
I hate, 
I hate I hate.


ITs been so long since you left me alone,
in this desert,
underneath this colorless sky.
