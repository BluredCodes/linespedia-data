If this comes out 
right it'll be a follow up to my
"Hands Thoughts" post


MY hands,
stained with guilt and memories.
bruised and broken.
fumbling, 
franticly,
through the broken pieces.
trying to put them back together, 
not knowing what they looked like to begin with
The tears fall like feathers
onto the backs of my bleeding hands
