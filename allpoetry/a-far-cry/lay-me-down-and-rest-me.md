---
title: "Lay me down and rest me"
writer: "A Far Cry"
slug: "lay-me-down-and-rest-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

Im tired,
everything is getting so hard.
I just cant seem to keep up.
I can feel it slipping ,
through my limp fingers,
falling,
breaking.
Things always fall apart dont they?
Maybe when I wake up tomorrow it will be better,
or maybe I need to stay in bed and rest.
That sounds so nice.
Ill lay me down and rest me,
and i wont get up.
