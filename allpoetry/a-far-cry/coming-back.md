Who the hell do you think you are?
Come Walking back into my life
and act like you can play with the feelings I once had.
"Still bouncing on bananas?"
How original,
Fuck you.
YOu critise me for who i am,
yet what about you,
you dumb fuck.
you and your 13 year old girlfriend.]
How can you presume that there isnt someone better for me.
Why do you think I would dwell.
Im not like you,
I dont care
because i am me, 
not you.
