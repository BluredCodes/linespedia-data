Hi mom,

Yeah i knows its been a while

whats that?

No ma i don't have a place yet but its warm at night

yeah i got a job

Where? over at the K-art ma

no i don't get paid much, 
but the doc says the scars on my wrist will heal up soon

don't worry ma ill have my chance
whats that?

I'm on a pay phone ma.

yeah i know its raining mom

I just wanted to call to see how you're doing.

thats good.

I'm sorry, i gotta go now ma.
I'm sorry, i love you.
click
beep beep beep beep
