title: "think"
writer: "A Far Cry"

Just when i thought
that i had a chance.
Oh wait.
theres my problem,
I thought.
I thought that i could come close enough,
to touch the black wings of an angel,
but your still so out of reach.
No matter how hard i dream,
if i can even dream anymore,
giving up sounds so nice
but i still think
and thats too easy.
