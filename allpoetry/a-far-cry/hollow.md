---
title: "hollow"
writer: "A Far Cry"
slug: "hollow"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

just shut up,
leave me alone. 
It's all in your head.
You're making it up.

HELP ME!!

stay away.
You dont understand,
no one understands.
SHUT UP! SHUT UP!
I dont need help.
I can make it.
I just need to get a foot on the ground,
but there is no ground. 
falling falling. 
Im empty, empty empty.
