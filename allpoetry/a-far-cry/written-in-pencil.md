What do I do?
please tell me.
Tell me what im feeling today just like before.
Tell me what i think when I get,
that feeling in my stomach.
Tell me what to do now
can't I turn back?

What now?
Why so Quiet,
You were always there before,
why not now,

Oh I see
its all too clear ,
its my fault isnt it?
because im diffrent?
but why avoid the subject,
go ahead and say it.
where are your kind words now?
where,huh? WHERE?
Or were they written in pencil,
so you could whipe them away
Go ahead and say it,
Im a fag, and
erase your kind words like everyone else
