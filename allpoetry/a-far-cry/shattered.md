Note: Im new and this a sper of the moment kinda thing.


What is it now,
what have I become?
I find myself,
crawling,
picking up the pieces.

I dont know what it is.
Is it broken,
Somethings broken.
Is it dead,
Somethings dead.

Why cant i leave myself be?
Leave me be.
Somethings dead.Just help
