---
title: "ashes"
writer: "A Far Cry"
slug: "ashes"
source: "AllPoetry"

Let it burn,
It means nothing,
I dont care.
Let it burn,
What does it matter?
Nothing lasts.
Let it burn,
I never cared anyway,
Let it burn,
to the ground.
Ashes, ashes, ashes
dry, black. 
Ashes, 
blown by the wind.
Nothing left but the ashes,
the ashes of my mind
