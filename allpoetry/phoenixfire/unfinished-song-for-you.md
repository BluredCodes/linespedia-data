---
title: "Unfinished Song for You"
writer: "Phoenixfire"
slug: "unfinished-song-for-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/4709-Unfinished-Song-for-You-by-Phoenixfire"
---

When I look to the sky
I see pieces of my broken dreams - 
scattered in the stars.

Now you're gone and they have died
Insignificant reminders of a life gone by

When I look to the sky I see my memories
I see you and me together forever

And I know things can't be the way they use to be - 
But I'm still enveloped in these 
blinding dreams that can not be...
