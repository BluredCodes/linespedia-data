I am the Phoenix.

Man has shot me down and stolen my feathers,
Yet I have still flown.

Man has peirced my skin and spilled my blood,
But my heart still beats on.

The rain fell, soaking my body, 
But I did not fall.

The sun dried my skin
the earth wore at my body
But still my spirit soars on...

I am Phoenix
Yet I have smouldered in my own fire
felt the flames burn at my flesh.

I was my own demise
Turned to dust in my mighty flame

Yet my fire still burns
I will rise above, 
I WILL overcome
I will not be forgotten
