title: "Just Breathe"

There is something about the sound of the crashing waves 
that can be so soothing

about the tangy air
that fills your soul with hope

The constant change,
that is so similar to the canvas of our lives

perhaps that is why I'm drawn

to the stars
the waves
the sand between my toes...

the water laps at my feet
like fingers of tiny children

reminding me what its like
to be young and free
to let go and live...

reminding me to breathe

and for once, 
if only for an instant
I am Free.
