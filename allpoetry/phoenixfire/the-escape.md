It left me one dayIm not sure where it wentIm not sure what it wasHope?Faith?Love?Life?Happiness?Magic?it left with a breathwith a movementwith a sighgone...Fovever?life will never be the samebut life is often like that - never the same -always changing...parts may be lost, stoelen, forgetten...But its still life...its still worth living..Isn't it?
