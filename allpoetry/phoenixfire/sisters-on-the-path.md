title: "Sisters on the Path"
writer: "Phoenixfire"
slug: "sisters-on-the-path"
source: "AllPoetry"

Come with me my sisters,
and sit.

Let us speak of the time gone by
Let our spirits soar together
and unite as one -
as they once were...

we were all born 
from the same flame,
the same light,
out of the same darkness...

The earth is reclaiming her children...

The path is before us...

The choice must be made..

It starts with one...
