---
title: "I Feel Alone Tonight"
writer: "Phoenixfire"

I feel  alone tonight,
As I sit here watching the water crash at my feet.

I feel alone tonight, 
As my soul is slowly pulled out into the abyss.

I feel alone tonight
As a watch my soul drown with out me.

It slowly sinks down,
Succumbing to the crashing waves
Leaving me empty

I scream- But the sound is lost in the thunder

I yell- But the waves drown me out

I cry - But the rain washes my tears away.

I feel alone tonight.

In a world where I speak, but no one listens,
Where I Scream, but no on hears.

In a world where I love, but no one understands
Where I give, until I am empty.

How long will it go on?

How long can I last?

How long until I fade away?

I feel alone tonight
As I sit here watching the waves crash at my feet,
Wishing I could be those waves,
Wishing I could hide in their depths.
Wishing i could be...

I feel alone tonight.
