Witches flying on their brooms,A werewolf howls at the moon,Bodies raise from ancient tombs     ~Walking stiffly    ~Spreading DoomThe Night is DarkThe Air is ChillThe Devil's childrenStalk their killDeath conducts a symphonyof screaming wailing agonyBald Mountains terrors have come trueCould this be the end for me and you?Corny poem I wrote last Halloween
