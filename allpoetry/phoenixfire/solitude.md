title: "Solitude"

I sit alone
stranded in my own fantasies

I walk alone
tripping on my own dreams

I float alone
sinking in my fears

I choke and my emotions
and am smothered by my tears

breathing is no longer possible

How can you see your way
if there is no light?

How can you move on
If there is nothing to move to?

They say fear is our enemy, and love our savior...

But to me there is no love, 
and perhaps there never was.

I will never be saved...

and so each day, 
i sink a little bit further into myself
walk a little farther into the darkness
fall into the void of me...

I will never be saved from me

I can never be saved
