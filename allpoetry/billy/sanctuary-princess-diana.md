SANCTUARYthe world never understoodexactly what they put me throughthey did all they could to bring me downi hated having them aroundall i wanted was to live my life in peaceto them, i was beauty.....and the beastwas this the price ive had to payis this my only sanctuary?now their words cant reach my earsi'll never hear what they think of metheir eyes cant see me here,where i remaini'll never have to hide againno cameras flashing in my faceup here no one invades my spaceim finally out the goldfish bowlnow im in sanctuary alonenow im in sanctuaryand no one can hurt me nowall i wanted was to be freenow i am in sanctuary....that poem is about what diana might be thinking now.as she had an unhappy time with the press while she was alive.....
