title: "Dreams can come true"

Think of all the things you've longed for
And all the places you've longed to see
Think of all the money you could wish for
And all the people you'd like to be
If you don't want pure fantasy
If it's the real thing that you'd love
Remember dreams can come true
If you wish hard enough.

Don't give up,on your hopes,and dreams
If that's what you believe
They'll say,you're wishing your life away
But why should'nt it happen to me
So with a little bit of luck
And help from up above
Your dreams can come true
If you wish hard enough.
