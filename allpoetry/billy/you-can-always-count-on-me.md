title: "You can always count on me"

If ever you need a shoulder to cry on
If ever you need someone to rely on
You can always count on me
Just call me when you're feeling down
One word from you,and i'll be around
You can always count on me

Don't hesitate,You don't have to wait
Just call me,and i'll be your mate
You can always count on me
Remember,You only have to say
Any hour,Or minute of the day
You can always count on me
No need to feel alone again
I promise that i'll be your friend
You can always count on me..
