How did our everlasting love,run out on us?
When we had so much to give each other
After all these years,we spent together
We thought we couldn't love another
It seems so long since we said our last goodbyes
Nothing left but memories from the past
I always wonder,what went wrong back then
I thought our love was meant to last.

I want to wake up next to you again
In the morning's early light
I want you to be with me,here right now
so our love can re-ignite
