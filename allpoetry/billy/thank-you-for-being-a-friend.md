title: "Thank you for being a friend"
writer: "billy"

Thank you for being there
When i needed a friend
Thank you for being one
On whom i could depend
You lifted my spirits
When i was feeling down
You make my life seem worthwhile
Whenever you're around

We all  have  ups,and downs
There's times when we feel low
I need your friendship most
For the care you do show
So thanks for being there
When i reached for a hand
It's good you're on my side
Someone who understands..this poem and others ive written like this were written with some wishful thinking,as ive gone through my life[ 42 now] and never made any real friends,such as i write about...why is that? where do i go wrong?
