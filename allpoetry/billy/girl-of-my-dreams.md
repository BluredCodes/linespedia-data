---
title: "Girl of my dreams"
writer: "billy"
slug: "girl-of-my-dreams"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/7425-Girl-of-my-dreams-by-billy"
---

when i close my eyes,i slip away
into a fantasy world of my own
but my dream girl is right here with me
i am not alone
is this a dream,or is this for real
cant explain the way i feel
theres visions of you,running through my head
as i lie here sleeping in my bed

you only appear when i close my eyes
thats when i look at you,and visualise
when the lights go down,and its time for bed
your vision comes into my head
im afraid to wake up
and find my life so empty,when youre gone
so i'll keep on dreaming,youre with me
for its only in my dreams that you belong

girl of my dreams,girl of my dreams
can i stay with you?.
