title: "The man in the mask"
writer: "billy"

He rides into the wild west town
Guns hanging menacingly by his side
Eyes shifting back and forth,behind his mask
As along the dusty street he rides

He's a man with a mission
And no one stands in his way
He's feared throughout the wild west
Who is on his hit list today?

He stops outside the saloon
And secures his tired horse outside
Conversations stop,as he walks through the doors
as up to the bar he strides.

There's a poker game in the corner
And the stakes are running high
Our man drinks up,and walks back out
To face the dark night sky
As he gets outside,he's startled by a shout
Of "reach for the sky",and a shot rings out
He turns and shoots,and from a roof falls down
A cowardly man from the one horse town.

He rides into the distance
With another notch on his belt
While his victim lies still on the ground
In the cowards blood he had spilt

No one knows the name of the mystery man
But very few will stop to ask 
Why he came into their town
And why he hides behind the mask...
