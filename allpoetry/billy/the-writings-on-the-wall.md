title: "The writing's on the wall"
writer: "billy"
slug: "the-writings-on-the-wall"

You thought you had it all
Thought you'd avoid the fall
But nothing lasts forever
They all looked up to you
Success is all you knew
You went hand in hand together

They put you on a pedestal
Like a god up in the sky
So high above the rest
You never saw the warning signs

The writing's on the wall
For you this time
You're coming to the end of the line
I knew the day would come
When your reign as number one
Would come to an end.

How will it affect you
When you're down to number two
And end up as yesterdays' man.
The writing's on the wall....
