---
title: "The time is now"
writer: "billy"
slug: "the-time-is-now"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Seize the day,and take a chance
Never say never
don't discard your plans
You only live once
So live for today
Enjoy yourself
And do it your way.

Don't think twice
Do what you want to do
Out with the old,
And in with the new
The past is where you used to be
The time is now,live for today.

Get on with your life
You only live once
Seize the day
See what you can become
Be a new person
And show them how,
It's a brand new day,
The time is now
