---
title: "A losing game"
writer: "billy"

I've tried to put you out my mind
And set my heart on someone else
I've tried to forget about the past
And leave what we had on the shelf
They said go and find somebody new
But some things never change
If i can't be with you
Then i'm playing a losing game

I've tried to look around
For others who could impress
But my mind,keeps coming back to you
For in my heart,you are the best
No point trying to re-write the story
Because the ending stays the same
If i can't be with you
Then i'm playing a losing game

Up there you always stood alone
And i looked up to you
You were like a  hero to me
You were the best i ever knew
I know which road i'm taking
Now it's clear,and it is plain
If i can't be with you
Then i'm just playing a losing game....
