---
title: "My love"
writer: "billy"

I Want to hold you in my arms
I Want your body next to mine
I Want to hear your voice say
That you'll be with me for all time
I Want to give you all my love
Because i've saved it all for you
It's locked away,inside my heart
That cupids' arrow shot straight through

You are the sun up in my sky
You are the apple of my eye
You are a pleasure just to know
You're the pot of gold,at my rainbow

Never change,and be forever my shining star
Because i love you,just the way you are...
