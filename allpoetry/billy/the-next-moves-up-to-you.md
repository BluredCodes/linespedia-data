title: "The next moves up to you"

I thought we had it all
I thought our love would never die
Then you decide it's time to go
But you never gave the reason why
Don't keep me hanging on
For it'll hurt me even more
Looks like the game might soon be over
But i never knew the score

There will be no second chances
When you walk out the door
There's no turning back this time
Because i could'nt trust you any more

I've done the best i can
I've done all that i can do
I've dealt my final hand
And layed them down in front of you
I thought we had some trust
But i found out things,i never knew
Looks like the party could be over
And the next moves up to you....
