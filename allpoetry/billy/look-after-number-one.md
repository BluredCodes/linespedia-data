It's time to look after number one
The rest of your life has just begun
Don't worry about what people think,
Or what people do
This time,the only one to think about is you.

Throw away the ties,
that have been holding you back so long
It's time to put yourself first
Because that's where you belong
Think of no one but yourself
Never mind everybody else
Your new life has just begun
Look out for number one.

You always were so generous,and kind
You left your own needs behind
You always came second,or third
In the race of life
But now it's time for you to decide
If you want to have some fun
Look after number one...
