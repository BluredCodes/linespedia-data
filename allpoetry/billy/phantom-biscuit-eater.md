There's no biscuits in the tin
The phantom biscuit eaters' been
No one owns up,to the trail of crumbs
The mystery remains of who succumbed

One minute it's full,the next it's lying bare
There's possible culprits everywhere
But no one will say" yes it was me"
Who ate all the "digestives" and "rich tea"

This leads us all to believe
The phantom biscuit eater lives
He waits for a chance to make his raid
Of being caught, he isn't afraid
He will munch his way through all thats there
Then disappear into thin air

Next time around,i'll lay a trap
And catch the phantom in the act
Then maybe i'll get a biscuit to myself
From the biscuit tin,upon the shelf..IN MY POEM I MENTION "DIGESTIVES" AND "RICH TEA"
i come from scotland,where these 2 types of biscuits are popular...i hope the line in the poem,never got lost with anyone,who is not familiar with those biscuits...
