---
title: "My mind keeps coming back to you"
writer: "billy"

In my dreams,your face appears
And when i'm sad,you wipe my tears,
And when i'm somewhere far from home,
It feels like i am not alone,
Although i try to tell myself we're through,
My mind keeps coming back to you.

Everywhere i go you're there
It seems like you are everywhere,
I can't believe how this makes me feel,
It feels like this is so unreal.
But no matter how hard i try,
I just can't get you out my mind.

Maybe fate has dealt it's final card
And we're meant to be as one
We've both been down our seperate roads,
But now we're back where it all begun.
