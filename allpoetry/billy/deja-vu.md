title: "Deja vu"

We're walking hand in hand
On a stretch of golden sands
As moonlight shines on,we two
It's like a dream that's coming true
I get the strongest feeling
This is happening once again
Is this second time around?
Is this deja vu we've found?

Deja vu,have i seen you here before
Deja vu,now lets do it all once more
Do you ever get the feeling
That you've done this in the past
Deja vu,now i want to make it last.

It's all coming back(to me now
It all seems familiar somehow
I think i've seen your face
In another time,and another place
Deja vu have i seen you here before
Deja vu,let's do it all once more..
