title: "The meaning of christmas"

What does christmas mean in far off places
To different religions,and different races
Do they celebrate the same as we do
Do their christmas wishes all come true
Christmas trees glisten,with tinsel,and lights
And brighten up the dark winter nights
Holly,and the ivy,blend in with the mistletoe
While the kids are having fun,playing in the snow

Unwrapped christmas paper,strewn across the floor
While seasonal decorations,hang upon the door
Rows of christmas cards,display a winter scene
While classic hollywood films,dominate the TV screen

All these things,are part and parcel
Of this festive day
All around the world,people celebrate
In their own special way.

But celebrations differ,round the world that's true
Not everyone is as fortunate,as me and you.
As we sit down to a five course meal
That leaves us all feeling as stuffed
As the turkey that we just carved
Let us spare a crumb,if not a thought
For the millions who have starved.
