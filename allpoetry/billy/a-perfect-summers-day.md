title: "A perfect summers' day"
writer: "billy"

There's a clear blue sky
The sun is shining,We're all alone
Just you and me,after all this time
This feels like home
I Can't believe you're here with me
My life was dull,until you appeared
This is a perfect summers day
It was spoiled by this cloud
hanging over me
Now it's brighter since you came on the scene
I woke up sad,and feeling low
I had no special place to go
Now you're here
I don't want this day to end
If you left,
it would never be the same again.

What more could i really want
Is this called heaven,or not
The sun shining,the sea,and the sand
You and me walking hand in hand

It turned into a perfect summers' day
The sun is out,it's here to stay
My sky has turned to blue,from grey
It's just a perfect summers day....
