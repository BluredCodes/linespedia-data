---
title: "DAD"
writer: "billy"
slug: "dad"
source: "AllPoetry"

Dad,are you now watching over me,
Dad,do you like everything you see
Can you see your grandchildren,you never knew,
Standing here with me.

Do you know how much i miss you
Do you know how much i cared
Thanks for being a brilliant dad
And for all the good times that we shared.
I miss you more than words can say
I wish you were still here today
It feels like you're always by our side
In the present and in our past
You'll always be my our loving dad,right up to the last

I will never forget you
For you're my dad,and that's a fact
If i could have one wish in my life
It would be for me to have you back
Dad you know i've always loved you
And i miss you more,and more each day
But then you always knew that anyway
