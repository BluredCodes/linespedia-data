Eyes sparkling,like the stars,in the sky at night
So rarely have i seen such a beautiful sight
The hardest of hearts,would be melted by the smile
Of that there can be no denial.
There's beauty,and character,in the face
That like a constellation shines
And brings nothing but admiration
From this heart of mine
Her glowing prescence lights up the darkest of days
And inspires all these words,that i have to say.
