always be aware of what's aheadand never believe a word thats saidby madmen who rule in far off placesplaying mind games with their many facesso we must be on guard,and on our toesin this crazy world where anything goesthe dictators with their fingers on the pulseplay with the lives of everyone elsethreatening this peaceful world in which we livethey're willing to take but not to giveso lots of vigilance must be shownfor we must never trust the unknown
