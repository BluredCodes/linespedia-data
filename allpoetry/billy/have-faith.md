title: "Have faith"

Too many people passed their judgment
Though they never had a clue
About what it takes to make it to the top
they never had any faith in you

When they said you were too young
I told them they were wrong
i said time was always on your side
you'd soon be up where you belong

i never had any doubt 
about the talent you possessed
and i knew all along
that you were better than the rest

you had the determination and the talent to succeed
on that i could depend
and i always knew
you'd prove them wrong in the end..
