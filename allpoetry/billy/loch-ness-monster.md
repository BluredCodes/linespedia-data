LOCH NESS MONSTER

was it just a ripple,upon the waters' so grey
or a log that you saw on a cold murky day
do you believe something exists in loch ness
or should the stories be finally laid to rest
lurking deep down in the black eerie waters
is there  more than just fish ,seals and otters
will we ever know if it really exists?
was it nessie,who was seen through the low morning mist?

rising then falling through the water it goes
as onlookers stare out waiting for nessie to show
what if the people who sited the monster are right
and they really saw nessie appear one dark night
but the doubters still laugh ,and fail to believe
that the loch ness monster,lives down in the deep
so the searching goes on,with the mystery intact
is it all make believe,or is really a fact?
