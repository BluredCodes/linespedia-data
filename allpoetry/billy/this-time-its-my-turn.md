title: "This time it's my turn"

Too many times in the past
Things haven't gone the way i'd hoped
I never thought i'd see it through
I wondered how i'd cope
But there's now a light at the end of my tunnel
Where it once was only dark
Now it's my turn for a new life
And it's time for me to start.

I'm trying to put behind me
All the sadness and the tears
I'm trying to forget what i went through
In those so unhappy years
For this time it's my turn
And a new beginnings'on the way
The rest of this new life of mine
Begins for me today.
