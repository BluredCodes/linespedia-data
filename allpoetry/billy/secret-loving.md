---
title: "Secret loving"
writer: "billy"
slug: "secret-loving"

Seems forever since our eyes first met
We both love each other,Yet
They're all against us from the start
Trying to drive a wedge between our hearts

They warned us,it would end in tears
Saying our love wouldn't last the years
But they don't know our love is strong
Now were set to prove them all wrong

                                  
                                        

Between us we exchange our secret signs
No one else can understand
Using secret code names on the phone
Pretending were going out alone

Like strangers,we pass each other
But only with a glance
Trying to forever keep a secret
Of our passionate romance

Were playing this game of secret loving
Because thats the way its got to be
We've got to have it all or nothing
Because i need her here with me..
