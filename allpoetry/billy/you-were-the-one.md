---
title: "You were the one"
writer: "billy"
slug: "you-were-the-one"

You never realise what you've got
Until it's finally gone
I should've told you how i felt
But looks like i got it wrong
Didn't know what you meant to me at the time
Now you're gone,i wish you were mine.

But it's too late now,my chance has gone
And now i'm left here all alone
Would things have turned out different
Had i said my feelings from the heart
Or were we destined,to be forever kept apart.

Looking back i should've known
You were the only one for me
But i let you slip through my fingers
Far too easily.
I had you in the palm of my hand
But i was confused,and didn't understand
Now it's over,and it's done
I wish i knew you were the one.
