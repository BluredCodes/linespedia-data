---
title: "True love"
writer: "billy"

source: "AllPoetry"

---

I would do anything for you
Just to show you that i care
You're the one who's on my mind
When true love is in the air


I want to be right here with you
I want us to stay together
I want to show what you mean to me
And that our love will last forever


For yours is the picture
Upon my mantlepiece above
And you are the only one
With whom i wish to share this love



Yours is the name that is etched
So proudly on my heart
And mine is the heart that breaks
Everytime we are apart
