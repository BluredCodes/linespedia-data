If there's a god in heaven
Where was he,the night she died
We all believed,we'd lost a friend
And we hung our heads and cried

No more,will we see her
Or hear the words she'll say
We all looked up to her
In our hearts she'll always stay

Our memories,we'll treasure
With fondness,and with love
Her caring,sharing,nature
Will be continuing up above

Though she's gone,she'll always stay
Forever on our minds
She left many people some hope
In the legacy of love,she left behind..
