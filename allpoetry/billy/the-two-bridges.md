title: "The two bridges"

I live in a place
Where the waters' embrace
The banks of the river i love
The glistening waters run swiftly along
While the two bridges stand towering above

It's a view i've enjoyed
Since i was a young boy
And after all these years,it's not lost its appeal
The two bridges span,the dark sky at night
And their lights give them a heavenly feel.

The rail bridge is over 100 years old
And still looks good,after all of that time
While the road bridge runs alongside
It's beauty it does not hide
And they both form a fantastic skyline.the two bridges in question,span the river forth in a place called south queensferry,where i live,which is 10 miles outside of edinburgh,the capital city of scotland,great britain..i hope i set the scene  ok...you must visit if coming to edinburgh
