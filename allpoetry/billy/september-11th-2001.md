Tuesday the 11th of september
the day that changed the world forever
our lives will never be the same again
as terrorism had its evil way
as the scenes unfolded on TV.
we couldn't believe what we were seeing
thousands of innocent people lost their lives
for reasons you cannot define
together we cried our tears
and together we face our fears
but they'll never destroy our spirit
or take away our pride
and they'll never take away our freedom
if we all stand side by side
so tall and proud
with our heads held high
we are strong
we are determined
and we are unified....
