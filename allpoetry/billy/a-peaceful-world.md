A PEACEFUL WORLD

lets show a little love and understanding
to those who are our foes
start by extending a hand of friendship
and we'll see how far it goes
you can then ask yourself whether
we can all live in peace together
lets try and put an end to all this war
what are we really fighting for?
what kind of world is it turning into?
is war the only solution for me and you?
what does the future hold for us here?
will it be one of calm,or one of fear?
why cant the superpowers up above
stop this hate and learn to love
then we can all join together
and make this world a whole lot better.
