title: "I'll never fall under your spell"

I Won't look into your eyes
I Won't be hypnotised
That's why i always shy away
When i'm with you face to face
You'll never get me as well
I'll never fall under your spell.

Everytime i turn around
Everytime i hear a sound
Your always in my face
Invading all my space
But you'll never get me as well
I'll never fall under your spell

I Know the signals that you send
Usually signify the end
And when you cast a glance
You send them in a trance
But you'll never get me as well
I'll never fall under your spell.
