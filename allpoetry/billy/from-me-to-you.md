---
title: "From me to you"
writer: "billy"
slug: "from-me-to-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

Do you really like the words i say?
Written especially for you
They're completely from the heart
And they are honest and they're true
Some people utter them freely,and easily
Some find them difficult to say
But i like to put mine into verse
In my own special way.
Spoken words sound nice to the ear
But in a moment they are through
I prefer a permanent record of my feelings
And they come with love, from me to you
