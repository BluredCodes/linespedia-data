title: "The last time"

When she walked out the door
I knew it was the last time
I remember the words she said
I can't get them out my head
It cuts me like a knife
To know she's no longer mine
It's the last time.

There was nothing i could say
To make her change her mind
Though i was begging her to stay
She turned and walked away.
And left me standing there
Knowing i had seen her face
For the last time.

Why did it end that way
After all those years we spent
Was there nothing i could say
Did she have no regrets
No words of hope came from her lips
Not even one last parting kiss
Looks like it's the last time..
