---
title: "Words"
writer: "billy"
slug: "words"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

there are words for every occasion
whether they be good or bad
there are words that make you happy
and words that make you sad

there's words of praise,for a job well done
and words of criticism for getting it wrong
there's words of comfort when faced with grief
and words of thanks for something received
there's words of excitement when having fun
and words of elation,when something is won
there's words of love showing feelings from the heart
and words of hate that tear you apart
there's words of encouragement when needed most
and words of arrogance when faced with boast

and there's times when words dont come easy
and times theyre better left unsaid
and next time you think of saying words of anger
.....why not try a quiet word instead...
