title: "Say it with flowers"

I never meant to hurt you
I regret every word i said
I was scared i'd end up all alone
Can't you see i was afraid.
Sorry isn't nearly enough
To make up for all the pain
Please give me one more chance
So we can be together again

Harsh and hurting words were said
But they weren't really meant
We've too many memories to throw away
Too many good times that we spent
How can i help to mend this bond
And to replace the hurt you felt
I thought i'd say it with flowers
I hoped that they would help

There's flowers for every occasion
They can say more than words can do
So please accept this bouquet,and my love
To say i'm sorry for hurting you.
