---
title: "Bravest of hearts"
writer: "billy"
slug: "bravest-of-hearts"

The bravest of hearts,can never be broken,
Once the words of war have all been spoken,
They can take our pride away from us,
If they dare to try,
But to take away our freedom
They must be prepared to die.

Standing up,and fighting
For the countries we all love
We've already gave our hearts
Now we're prepared to give our blood
With the stars and stripes,and union jacks
Flowing around our veins
We'll ensure these nations of ours remain forever free
Over which no terrorist shall reignhands across the world,fighting terrorism
let the world unite,and remain free.
