---
title: "There ain't no in between"
writer: "billy"
slug: "there-aint-no-in-between"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/15620-There-aint-no-in-between-by-billy"
---

Is it love,
or is it hate
Will you go,
or will you wait
Was it planned
Or just a twist of fate
There aint no in between

You're either up,
or you are down
Will you go
Or will you stick around
Will you smile
Or will it be a frown
There aint no in between.

Is it yours
Or is it mine
Did you do it all
Or did you run out of time
Are you falling apart
Or is everything just fine
There aint no in between

You can't be stuck in the middle
You can't be torn between
You can't sit on the fence
It's either you or me.
There aint no in between
