What new years resolutions
Will you decide to start on new years day
What bad habits,
Will you decide,to change,or throw away
Will you try to be more tidy
Or plan to go on a diet
Are you going to get yourself fit
Will you even try it.

Do you plan to give up smoking
Or will less drink pass your lips
Do you plan to give up eating fries
Have you really had your chips
Only time will tell
If these changes do come true
Do you have the resolution
To see it all the way through.
