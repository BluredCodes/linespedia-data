---
title: "Take a look at me now"
writer: "billy"
slug: "take-a-look-at-me-now"

You said i'd have to change
Or you'd walk right out the door
I never thought i'd ever see
You here with me no more
So i took a look at myself
Through the eyes of someone else
And decided to make the change
And leave the old me on the shelf

We could't go on no more
Living our lives so differently
I never thought i'd get the chance
To let you see the change in me
I was far too blind to see
What was driving you away
So won't you give me one more chance
And please tell me that you'll stay.

Why don't you take a look at me now
You won't believe the change you'll see
I'm not the man you used to know
I'm not the way i used to be
It's like the difference between black and white
When you see me in a different light.
Come and take a look at me now
Come and take a look  at me now...
