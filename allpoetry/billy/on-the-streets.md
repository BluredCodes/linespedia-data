Please can you help meCan anyone make my dayDo you know what i go throughAnd could you live life this wayIs there the slightest hint of kindnessIn your warm and tender heartPlease help me to surviveBefore my world falls apartIf you were my eyesYou would see the state I'm inAnd if you were my earsYou would hear the sufferingIf you were my handsYou would feel the pain i feelPut yourself in my shoesAnd you will see this is for real.So next time don't pass us byRemember it could be you one dayWho puts up with all the sufferingThat forever comes our way..
