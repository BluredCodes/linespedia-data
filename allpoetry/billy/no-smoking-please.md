I am a passive smoker
It really is a choker
Surround me with a no smoking zone
And let me breathe fresh air alone
Everyone to their own tastes,i suppose
But smoking gets right up my nose

Everyone has their pleasures,and habits
That they enjoy from time to time
But smokers should keep it to themselves
Because smoking isn't one of mine...I must make the comment that i dont have anything against smokers in general,and each to their own i say.
everyone is entitled to do as they please,but i do not like the fact that i have to breathe it in at close quarters sometimes.it was written tongue-in-cheek so ...no offence smokers...i realise it must be a hard habit to break..
