A woman's work is never done
That's what they always say
So i made a deal with my wife
To do her job for a day

Well i must admit,i never realised
Just how much work they do
I was completely knackered
Long before my day was through

Washing dishes,making beds
Making sure the kids are fed
Hoover,and dust,ironing clothes
How they cope,i'll never know

Preparing the meals,is never ending
But that's not it by half
Got to keep the kids amused
Then take them for a bath
Then take them to bed,read a story,tuck them in
By the end of the day,i feel done in.

So next time a woman says" i'm just a housewife"
Appreciate her what she has done
For they are cleaner,cook,maid,dishwasher
Mother,and wife,all rolled into one..
