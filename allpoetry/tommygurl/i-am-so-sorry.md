I'm So Sorry


I'm sorry for everything,
     that isn't right.
I always think about you,
     every night.

I don't know what I can say,
     to help you through.
But I do know that I want,
     the best for you.

I always wanted to be,
     the perfect friend.
Now things are different but still,
     my heart I lend.

I just wish you could know now,
     the way I feel.
Why does my heart always take,
     so long to heal.

I won't let anything change,
     us, you and me.
I'll be the perfect friend yet,
     just wait, you'll see.
