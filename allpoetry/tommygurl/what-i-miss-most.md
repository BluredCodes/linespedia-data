title: "What I Miss Most"

I know the day will come,
The day my dreams come true.
All that I want right now,
Is to wake up to see you.

I just wish I knew why,
Why you left and crushed my heart.
I'd love to hear from you,
But we're now two worlds apart.

I just don't understand,
Please explain to me again.
Since when is it better,
To watch me from heaven?

I wish I could see you,
Even for just one glance.
I wish I could feel you,
If only I got the chance.

The tears won't stop falling,
I miss everything you do.
But what I miss the most,
Is how much I loved you.
