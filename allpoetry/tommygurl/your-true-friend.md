Even when the truth hurts, your true friend never lies...She knows when somethins up, she sees it in your eyes...A true friend always listens, and responds with all her heart...She tries her very best, to keep from growing apart... Sometimes you hate your friend, so much you wonder why...Why you ever became friends, but then you start to cry...You remember all the years, your friendship stayed alive...You cant forget your true friend, 'cause she's all u have inside...
