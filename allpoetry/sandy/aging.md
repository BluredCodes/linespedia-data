title: "Aging"

Winter chill within a heart
        Anger thrust through every part


        Despair, frustration, anger, rage 
        Where has youth gone?
        When did I age?

        A time when any task was taken
        No feat was ever left forsaken.

        
        The ease in body, spirit, mind
        too late to ever again find?

        The rage is aimed at one and all
        Inside its fires grow way too tall.

        Peace must be found;
        contentment met

        It's not the time to sit in regret.
        A smile is harder
        inside as out

        Where does this anger find release?
        When do you gain an inner peace?
