Reason is it's meant to be
          Can't change the course; it's history
          Oh darkened sky, frightened air
          Man pays his dues for lack of care.

          A stranger sits at heavens' door
          Steeped in pain down to the core.
          Look back around, see endless scenes
          Lack of heed to others' needs.

          Remorse is felt, first time for he
          The strength is such.. his pain is free.
          Gentle hands, bright golden glow
          Reach out to heal a troubled soul.

          Heed well man, grasp this source
          Save thyself a frightful course.
          Show yourself, your joyful heart
          Not after death.. no, from the start.
