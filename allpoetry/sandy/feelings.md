The night is long
when fears are strong.
When thoughts are jumbled
and words are wrong.
Oh Happy
Oh Sad
Oh Joy
Oh Glad
Oh Envy
Oh Pity
Oh Laughter
Oh Fear
Oh Sorrow
Oh Pain
These are just some of the feelings in store
for us in this life
