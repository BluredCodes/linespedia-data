Willow tree..... gently sways
on soft warm breeze of summer days.

Tranquil moments are spent so rare,
under limbs bowed down
to shield us there.

Summer heat can't find you now
Cool long leaves seem to vow.

Rest and dream.. play and smile
I'll be here for quite awhile.
