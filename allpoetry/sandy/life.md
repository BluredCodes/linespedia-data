It's not the dying that scares me so.
It's missing out on all life holds.

I want to live,
to laugh, to cry, to grow old and tired before I die.
After I've lived through many a night, 
of fun and laughter or toil and fright.

When my ears grow dim
When my eyes lose sight.
When my body is weary,
Let my soul take flight.

I want to feel all of life.
Even the troubles, toils, and strife.
It's not so bad to give in and go
when people say....
She lived a full life
