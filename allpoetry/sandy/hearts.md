---
title: "Hearts"
writer: "sandy"
slug: "hearts"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

My heart touches those so dear
Their heart touches those so near

Each knows the next--be it kin or friend
The circle is made--there is no end

No one knows them all alone
No one knows them on their own

Only God knows every part
Only God knows every hear
