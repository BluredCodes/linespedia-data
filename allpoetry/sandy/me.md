Me

                                                                         I wonder if I'll ever be
         the person all the world does see?

         To some I'm zany full of fun 
         some think I'm often as the sun
         bright and cheery, up all day
         always laughing happy and gay

         Yet others see me dark and bleak
         never a smile do I seem to seek.

         A serious person ...steady and solid
         dependable, worthy, stalwart, and valid
         righteous strong witty outspoken
         yet shy and timid. with heart easily broken

         A childish girl, young lady, woman
         daughter, aunt, mother, cousin
         wife, friend, lover, grandma

         Of course I am the one they see
         because they all see me differently.
