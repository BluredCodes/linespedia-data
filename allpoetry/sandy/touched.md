Touched




     It shines so bright
     as a beacon in the night

     Awesome power does it yield
     the kind to break through any shield

     It softly conveys a feeling of warmth
     Yet boldly can change to one of great mirth

     There's nary a heart
     not touched by this part

     The sight of which lingers 
     in one's mind for awhile

     Is that of a loved ones'
     endearing warm smile.
