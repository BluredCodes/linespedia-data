Sunny days in safe unknown
       Wintry days with grief not shown

       A touch of hands
       A smile of faces

       Nary a show of griefs sad traces
       Enjoying of this time called life

       Little things should be foremost
       Because when it's been lived, loved, lost
  
       That's when we start to know the cost.

       To never see a loved one's face
       To never even know their place.

       Will all the senses I use here?
       To know of life from gay to tear

       Be what I need to cross the line
       and recognize my loved ones' shine?
