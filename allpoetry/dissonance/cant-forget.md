---
title: "cant forget"
writer: "Dissonance"
slug: "cant-forget"
source: "AllPoetry"

i remember the days 
when it was all ok
"we were invincible"
"nothing could hurt us then"
you went home and you played and you laughed
i went home and i cried and i screamed
the bruises , the tears
the screams and the fears
what i wouldnt give to forgot those years
"yea we were invincible then"
"....nothing could hurt us then"
