---
title: "unfamiliar happiness"
writer: "Dissonance"
slug: "unfamiliar-happiness"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/45545-unfamiliar-happiness-by-Dissonance"

and your skin touches mine
and a familiar shiver hits my spine
ill never get used to this
 no ill never get used to this

this warmth i feel
this happiness

no ill never get tired of your caress
so this is love..
and ive never felt any better
no i cant think of anything better
