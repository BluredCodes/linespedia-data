i almost broke it off today
i almost walked away today
i almost said im sorry for the words you didnt say
this odd depression swept over me
making me want to flee
i gazed at you this morning
yout ook my pain away
somethings still wrong 
something tells me we need ot talk
somethign tells me
tells me
tell me 

( its all better now , love you )
