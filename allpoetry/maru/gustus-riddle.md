title: "Gustus'  Riddle"
writer: "maru"

I wished the winter holidays,

But not for being alone,

And those cold days, 

I stayed again.



The wind came,

From between the stars,

For me to go,

But I just stayed,

Still waiting for you.



When you shown up,

I was green but dry,

I had no water,

At all on my arms.



The cold winter was leaving,

And the nice spring should come,

But I shall never know,

If it will be nice.



I sat next to a flag,

With white and blue,

And I had a nice view,

Of boys and a park.



And that was my life,

As simply as that,

But one day all got black,

And no memory I have,

From that moment till now.



If you know who I am,

I would wish to go back,

To that same simple life,

That I used to have.



Dedicated to the students of 1 year (2001)
