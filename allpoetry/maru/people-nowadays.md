The people you now see,
it is not like the ones you saw,
now the troubles invade them,
and you have to be carefull.

If it's society what corrupts men,
then hell to the society, 
and live your own life.

If it's power what corrupts men,
then I'll give you money,
but let me live!

If foreign people invade your country,
be carefull they might be spying.

If politics corrupts society,
then kill politics,
and make your own society.

If the lies corrupts people,
then hire more lawyers,
but good ones instead.

If there were two World Wars,
why don't you put away your militia,
and learn how to improve yourself!

People nowadays is crazy,
because they have no other choise,
so try to make some good,
and leave this back in time,
so the next generation,
will cope in this world!
