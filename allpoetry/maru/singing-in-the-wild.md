---
title: "Singing in the Wild"
writer: "maru"

Walking... The Earth has betrayed them,
They're lost in the Wild World,
Goblins took them... down to the depths
Of the caverns they'd tuneled
After The War.

Now nobody can help them,
Prisioners from the elves... only magic can
save them, and some luck either.

Roaming... through all the castle,
Spying the Good People without being catched,
Magic indeed will help them here... now
A new challenge begins.

Wishing... he was at home now,
Looking, for all his friends and
Stealing food from the Good People
or not... at all

Scaping... a night of joy and,
wishing the old wizard... was there,
Bilbo could help again and,
The dwarves are pleased... oh yeah.

The dragon, is waiting at the end,
So ya must be carefull... once more,
Your gold is now closer than,
It had ever been... wake up!
Better thank your dear friend Bilbo... again.
