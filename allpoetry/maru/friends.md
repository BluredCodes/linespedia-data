---
title: "Friends!"
writer: "maru"
slug: "friends"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/13119-Friends--by-maru"
---

A real friend,
will be there for you,
and it will always be welcomed.

A friend like Chandler,
might be very fun,
but take into account,
that he won't let you alone!

A friend like Monica,
might be very 'heavy',
but to have such a tidy friend,
could help you during life!

A friend like Joey,
might be very silly,
but friends like him,
won't be forgotten!

A friend like Rachel,
could be very dangerous,
don't lend her things, 
for her to loose,
and be carefull to her gossips,
and you can tell her 'Honey', 
she will absolutely love it!

A friend like Ross,
could be very interesting,
but do not talk him much about science,
or you won't take him off from you!

A friend like Phoebe,
would be very nice,
but be carefull with her songs,
they have a deep meaning!

If you have friends like these,
do never let them go,
trust me, you'd better keep them,
they are more than a treasure,
and by far really funny!
          
Dedicated to Emma and all the Friend's Fans!
              M.Hotes
