title: "School Figures"
writer: "maru"

Sometimes you may feel,
that masters at school,
are always trying to beat you,
but you have to be sure,
of whom are you talking about.

There might be some masters,
which won't like you,
but they are not all the same,
and you'd better try them to like you.

If you have words,
for describing a master,
it will be a good idea,
to teach me some,
while not all of them,
are that bad,
and not all of them,
are so good.

The masters are differents,
but you shouldn't hear the gossips,
'cause some gossips might be true,
and some others are just fake.

If masters don't like you,
never try to buy them up,
just try to make your best,
and never lie to them.

Masters are like boss',
they might like you,
or they won't,
but you'll have, 
to live with them,
there is no other choice!

Oh Masters! Oh Masters!
they are not all the same.
     
For students or teachers.
