Summer holidays, summer holidays! those magical words that echo on my mind,all the time like if they were divine,hhuumm... aren't they?I guess the hot days make that feeling,so alive so continue and so nice,the holidays in a seaside resort,and there are so many here,and all so nice like Miramar,either Pinamar or Valeria del Mar.Probably you don't know those places,You should visit them on January,although they're also nice in February,If you have the opportunity,do never miss the chance of knowing those places.But be careful with the sun, You may probably prefer another place,in the South were the climate is not that hot,and the landscapes are great.Then you should visitPuerto Madryn and make some Whale watching,Bariloche and enjoy the warm sun with some wind,You can choose from all the Argentina,it has this 'touch' made by nature,Which can be enjoyed as many ways you like.From all the places of the world,I would recommend the Argentina, to enjoy the climate the animals,and its beautiful landscapes,while the magic words echo on your mind,SUMMER HOLIDAYS SUMMER HOLIDAYS!
