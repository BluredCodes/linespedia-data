A friend is the one,
Who will be at your side,
No matter what happens,
He will be at your side.

A friend is the one,
Who will give you a hand,
Be with you all the time,
and will never let you down.

A friend is the one,
Which you'll always trust,
He will be honest too,
And the word 'lie',
Will never know.

A friend is the one,
Whom will never distrust you,
He won't let you alone,
Unless that's what you need.

A friend is the one,
Who won't wait for your call,
But he will be there,
When you really need him.

A friend is the one,
Which will understand,
If you wanna be alone,
And he won't be offended.

A friend is a treasure,
Which you'll always keep.
And you'd better remember,
That he will still be there,
When you most need him.
       M. HotesMake your own comments, I'll be pleased
