In the old days,When the elves sang,And the dwarves ran,And the Earth was a beauty,Which never ever more was seen,The Middle-Earth was great.The wizards were just seen,When making fire-lights,Or either rings of smoke,But they never fought,And ogres were never seen.The hobbitts never appeared,Much more than ussual,They were just busy,With their flowered gardens,And they never went on adventures,Which would late them dinner.Lothlorien was the liveliest place,With all its magic,As Elrond said,The elves and Galadriel,where happy in the woods.Saruman The White,Was an old good man,And he was the head,From all good wizards.This was the perfect world,Before The Eye and The One,had ever appeared.But afterwards,The Eye appeared,And the rings were forged,Peace and quiet ended, And now the Darkness spread,'round all the Middle-Earth.       M.HotesI know it is not pretty good, but I wanted to put it on here!
