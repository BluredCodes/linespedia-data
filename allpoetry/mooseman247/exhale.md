fighting the urge
in a quiet room
to hold your hand
and smell your hair

fighting the words
and what they might do
I'll kiss your hand
and hold your stare

-this is not the time
-this is not the place
hold my breath and fingers run

fighting this urge
by the shady bar
to kiss your lips
and meet you there

fighting these words
they go too far
the window drips
with foggy air

-this is not the time
-this is not the place
hold my breath and fingers run

this is not the time
this is not the place
-my first
-my last
-my only

fighting this urge
in a quiet car
on a lonely road
with company

fighting the words
in a shopping cart
can you hold the phone
while holding me


-this is not the place
-this is not the time
but I suppose it's good enough...
