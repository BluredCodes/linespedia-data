---
title: "The Long Ride Home"
writer: "Mooseman247"
slug: "the-long-ride-home"

even though my shower left me cold
and your number washed away
you should know
I'll call you anyway

you may have led me on
but still I chased

you may have promised me
that I could stay

but still I feel the need
I need to say
I'm sorry

even though I knelt and kissed your feet
at the moment it felt right
you should be
with someone else tonight

you may have held my face
and warmed my skin

you may have promised me
it's not a sin

but I can't shake the thought
of you with him
I'm sorry

Scream about it like it was the end
at the moment I felt wrong
never knew
an hour quite so long

you may have changed my life
but I won't bend

you may have promised me
that I could mend

I'm not the kind of guy
that would pretend
I'm sorry...
