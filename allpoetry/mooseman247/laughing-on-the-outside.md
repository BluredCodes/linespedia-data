title: "Laughing on the Outside"

Welcome to the tortured mind
of the imperfect
perfectionist

and oh, please do enjoy your stay
it's colorless
and comfortless

self-depreciation in a sea of flame and fist
self gratification for the things that I have missed
grief anticipation, read the numbers on my list
but it's not so bad as this

(chorus
Some days I'm easy to please
when everything has gone right

Sometimes it's hard to breathe
when you're laughing on the outside
 chourus)

Stare into my perfect eyes
a vision of
confessonals

They look so ugly from inside
perception is
corruptable

Question my creation, and a God that's made me weak
No deliberation, just a sense that I'm a freak
just a flavor of the week

(chourus)Well, can you hear it as a song?
