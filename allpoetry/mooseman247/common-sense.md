thinking back
about what you said
common sense and all that

now I know
I never got the chance
to know what you knew already

never had
sleepovers like you did
laughing and sharing thoughts

every time
I got used to my home
I had to leave it once more

so you see
I could not have possibly
been in this situation before

you believe
we are all the same inside
I've come to prove you wrong, again

common sense
nothing more than common lives
to which I do not belong

so I dream
like no one else can dream
you can keep your history
