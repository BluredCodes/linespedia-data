---
title: "In the Wild"
writer: "Mooseman247"

When did you stop
learning from mistakes
when did everything I say
stop mattering

when did you become so
well-to-do
when did everything I want
start haunting you

from the start
I felt sorry
you have lived
through agony

the worst part
I was helpless
had to leave
you comfortless

I wish I could take it all away
and make you smile
but it's not in my power
and it's not my style
to leave you lost
in the wild

when did you get
that obscene tatoo
since when did the sight of blood
stop scaring you

where did you learn
nasty things to say
where do people like us go
to sleep again

from the start
I was lonely
I had longed
for somebody

the worst part
I was breathless
had to stop
your recklessness

I wish I could take it all away
and make you smile
but it's not in my power
and it's not my style
to leave you lost
in the wildI think this one is done too...
