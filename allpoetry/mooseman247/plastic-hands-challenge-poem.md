I don't expect you 
to understand 
you hide behind your plastic hands 
laughing 
pillow walls fade black 
like memory... 
can't hurt me 

and this is not a window 
so high and far away 
and I can not go... 
on, those lonely nights 
you think, that I imagine lights 
a dancing blight, when minds collide 
and hide... behind 

laughing 
and pillow walls fade black 
like memory, can't hurt me 
all I see is agony 
and tragedy 
imagine me 
if I can be 
the enemy 
of pleasentries 
and harmonies 
then reason me 
and season me 
to cook me up 
a bitter cup 
of medicine 

laughing.....laughing....laughing...
