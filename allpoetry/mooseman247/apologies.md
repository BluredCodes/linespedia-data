*I knowyou've been here for so long... so longso sadto watch you leaveI knowI can see your mind is achingso badyou can't believeevery body's sorryI knowI can see your body shakingso sadto watch you grieveI knowI can see your heart is breakingso badyou can't believeevery body's sorryI knowI can see your empty pocketsso sadto watch you freezeI knowif I had something to give youtoo badyou can't believeevery body's sorrysorry's not enoughsorry won't abate thissorry isn't lovesorry cannot cure mesorry cannot helpsorry leaves me emptysorry must be feltevery body's sorrywords of hollow weightwords of little meaningwords I've grown to hate*I'm trying out some Zoloft.
