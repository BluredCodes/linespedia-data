Time honors nothing
traditions fade with me
I cannot find release
in you

Walk with a question
and leave with open ends
and no one comprehends
the truth

I am the seer
I do not see my worth
the glories of my birth
are few

Time is a nameless
shameless
helpless
killer

Time honors nothing
it ravages and rends
we all seem to depend
on you

Live for the future
and laugh at what I know
and watch everyone grow
confused

I am the statue
of stone and hollow steel
of flesh and bone I feel
amused

Time is a blameless
shapeless
hopeless
killerThis is the dark side of what a little time can do for you. Time away from the things you love, time wasted, or spent making money, time that could have been used to realize your dreams. These are the days that haunt you.
