title: "Subject to Change"

Life
comes crashing down
and up
around me

lost
just when I've found
a place
to envy

words
by candle light
a storm
and fury

worse
than parasites
of rhyme
and worry

cope
with inner dread
a dream
of treason

rope
and hanging dead
without
a reasonFor the life of me, I can't figure out why I wrote this one.
