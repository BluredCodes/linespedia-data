Close your eyes and I will give youall that you can seeClose your eyes and I will give youthe emptiness in meClose your eyes and I will kiss youit's where I wanna beClose your eyes and I will give youall that you can seeIf I look backwards through my mindwill you see through me tooand the blankest features of my eyesI will turn them out to youClose your eyes and I will give youall that you can seeClose your eyes and I will give youthe darkness that's in meIf I could take back what I've doneand left certain things unsaidIf I could change the words of what I singleave a hole inside my headClose your eyes and I will give youall that you can seeClose your eyes and I will give youthe evil that's in meWhat do you see?
