why is it so cold
inside my hell?

sinking in the fold
I might as well

lie inside my cell
yes I'm okay
locked up like a dead insect
why are they afraid

I'm only staring through you
I'm only breaking free
of rules and chains

I only recognize the truth

haunted as the time
I need to fill

sentanced for my crime
I might as well

lie inside my cell
yes I'm okay
pinned up like a dead insect
why are they afraid

I'm only staring through you
I'm only laughing at
the ugliness

I only recognize the truth

driven by the voices
in my head

frozen to the shackles
of my bed

shiver in my cell
yes I'm okay
locked down like a freak show beast
why are they afraid

I'm only staring through you
I'm only chattering
with friends and teeth

I only recognize the truthEven psych centers need central heating...
