You tell me that I'm different
you tell me I'm alone
well, while you're at it
tell me something I don't know

you tell me that I'm jaded
you tell me that I'm cold
well while you're at it,
tell me something I DON'T know

it always seems
you're standing on my dreams
holding the book open

if I could change
the horror that I see
