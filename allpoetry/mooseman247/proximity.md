-In a crowd
in the midst of chaos, there's a single driving force
He's alone
and he's pushing past inatimate control
There's no touch
no feeling but the pain that is his own

But when we're close
I can feel it changing, somehow I'm not alone
And when we touch
I can feel you breathing, and never let go...

-In a crowd
in the midst of strangers, there's a single entity
He's alone
and he's activated by proximity
There's no touch
there's nothing he can feel but misery

But when we're close
I can feel it changing, somehow I'm not alone
And when we touch
I can feel you breathing, and never let go...

-In a crowd
in the midst of thunder, there's a little boy asleep
He's alone
and unless he's found, that little boy will dream
There's no touch
no he cannot feel, but I'm sure you hear him scream

But when we're close
I can feel it changing, somehow I'm not alone
And when we touch
I can feel you breathing, and never let go...
