I'm feeling dizzied up
If I can keep it up
I'm trying to stay up
and keep the earth from falling

I think I've had enough
If I can keep it up
I feel like giving up
and running from my calling

I am a razor cut
I am an idiot
A sorry celibate
If I can keep it up

Please don't pity me
I won't accept your empathy
Please don't riddle me
I didn't expect
I didn't expect to bleed

I'm feeling like ATLAS
If I can keep it up
The world is heavy but
I am the only pillar

My back is breaking up
If I can keep it up
I'll save the virtuous
but I must save the killers

I am a razor cut
I am an idiot
A sorry celibate
If I can keep it up

Please don't pity me
I won't accept your empathy
Please don't riddle me
I didn't expect
I didn't expect to bleed
expect to bleed
expect to bleed
