for every note he sings
there has been a drop
blood, sweat, tears, and... other fluids

and as the chourus rings
you realize
oh... my... God... he fills a bucket

the rigors of the road
leading up to this
ego, grit, and talent were built

not an overnight hit
months streatch into years
drums, drummers have been replaced

survival at its best
for the sake of music
dreams, aspirations for success

he's in a local band
for the sake of music
support him, and shout its name

shout a little louder
drink a little more
maybe you can get up on stage
