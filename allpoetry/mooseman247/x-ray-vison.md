I can feel it
the abscence of the butterflies
finally I can realize
my dreams

this is serious
accepting my meager prize
I'm holding my head up high
it seems


-but this is my way
of standing out in front
you're all in your underwear
and I'm laughing
but you don't know why

and this is my way
of standing up in front
you're all wearing bunny shoes
and I'm laughing
and I can't say why


I have a job now
lamenting the end of life
it pays for the style of mine
(ka-ching)

this is serious
announcing when someone dies
I'll wear black up to my eyes
I'll sing


-but this is my way
of standing out in front
you're all in your underwear
and I'm laughing
but I won't say why

and this is my way
of standing up in front
you're all wearing crazy hats
and I'm laughing
and you don't know why


think that I love you
I want to spend the rest of life
I want to spend every night
with you

this is serious
asking her to be my wife
right after the bar tonight
I do


-but this is my way
of standing up in front
you're all in you're underwear
and I'm laughing
but you dont mind

and this is my way
of saying I love you
elusive in your bedroom chair
and I'm smiling
and I know why...
