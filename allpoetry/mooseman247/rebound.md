Love is a powerful thing
like any weapon that is used to kill

how can I believe in anything
the rules are changing
but like hell I will

love is a reason to sing
like any victory of bloody skill

how can I believe in anything
the music's fading
but like hell I will

phonelines drenched in a tenious uproar
sidewalk screams at the happy couple eyesore
everything reminds me of the time I spent in Bayshore

when everything's a f*king love song
and everybody else seems happier than you

whatever happens just keep holding on
