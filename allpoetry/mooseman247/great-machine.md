I've got a need for automation
I've got a need to integrate

I've got a love for replication
I've got a lovely metal face

I've got electric stimulation
I've got extensive enterprise

I've got a drive to fill the nation
I've got place to fill my drives

I know you're pressed to follow
But soon you'll be like me
I know it's hard to swallow
But soon you will believe
In my great machine
In my great machine
In my great machine

I am a human emulation
I've seen a planet come and go

I am a doplegang relation
I am the fear you do not know

I've got a sense of deprivation
I've got a sense of foolish pride

I've got a drive to fill the nation
I've got a place to fill my drives

I know you're pressed to follow
But soon you'll be like me
I know it's hard to swallow
But soon you will believe
In my great machine
In my great machine
