Sleep now child,the monster is hereby the light of dayrest your eyesthere's nothing to fearso don't run awayLet yourselffade into subconsciousLet the daemonsflutter through your brainLet yourselffall into the alleywayLet the nightmarebeginI'll watch you sleeping...
