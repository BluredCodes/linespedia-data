Super villans never phased him
one burrito rendered him ill
sweat ran down his spider-forehead
get home before spider-pants fill!

Peter Parker Packed with peppers
shouldn't have had that mexican meal
as he ran to get some water
spider-belly rumbled and reeled

pestered with persistant heartburn
spidey felt his stomach displeased
ran to get the pepto-bismal
his spider-gut was gurgling

"close one..." sighed the superhero
as he finished sloppy gulping
ran his fingers to his tummy
spider-intestines... settling.
