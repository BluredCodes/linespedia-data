I'm tired of hearing about loverswindow shoppingfor things I can't affordI'm sick of being the wallflowerhands are shakingmy eyes stuck to the floorI'm tired of questions without answersfriends are laughingat me forevermoreI'm sick of changing to please mastersquoth the dyinghe whispers nevermorenevermoreI'm gonna takea beatinglet it comeI'm tired of being so damn uselesssomething's brokenthat cannot be restoredI'm sick of making these excusestime for actionI will not be ignoredI'm tired of hearing the same voicescrucifixionthat hangs above the doorI'm sick of making the wrong choicesquoth the ravenhe's crying nevermorenevermoreI'm gonna takea beatingbeating...Please excuse the blatant plagiarism, I was trying to make an allusion to Edgar Allen Poe's 'The Raven'.
