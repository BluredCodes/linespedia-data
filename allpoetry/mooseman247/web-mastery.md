what you see is what you get
WYSIWYG for the few
engineering in 3D
is the least of what I do

the content is the hardest thing
but layout is a breeze
except when you are blind and tired
and the basement starts to freeze

then it's one click at a time
and caffiene is your friend
but good is never good enough
you never reach the end

it's always; wait, this needs to change
but that, changes this too
so then, you have to FTP
twice more, before you're through

almost forgot the frequent updates
that change the whole damn site
and setting up the POP email
could take the whole damn night

O' woe is me, the webmaster
but hey, it could be worse
I could be Steve, he has it bad
cause he does all the work!You're the man, Steve. Couldn't have done it without you.
