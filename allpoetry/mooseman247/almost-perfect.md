---
title: "Almost Perfect"
writer: "Mooseman247"
slug: "almost-perfect"
source: "AllPoetry"

You make no sense
you make me mad
you make me stupid and crazy

You take offense
you take my hand
you take my will and you break me 

you make me bleed
you make me bow
you take my blood and you save me 

you set me free
you set me down
and then you set your teeth in me 

-almost perfect
-is almost good enough
-it's not enough... 

You dig your hole
you dig your grave
you dig to reach something deeper 

you find control
you find a way
you find an innocent sleeper 

you sell him short
you sell his name
you sell him lies in a suitcase 

the last resort
the last you blame
at last you look at your own face 

-almost perfect
-is almost good enough
-it's not enough
