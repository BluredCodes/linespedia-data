---
title: "Aimless Earth"
writer: "Mooseman247"
slug: "aimless-earth"
source: "AllPoetry"

soul floats away
on uranium wings
as the amber sky
flickers
and dies

look down below
at what confidence brings
at the boiling
oceans'
demise

thrown to the wind
like a fluttering seed
like a body
that's caught
in a blast

floating in space
like a sputtering steed
disembodied
a ship
with no mastcreepy piece I've been working on.
