The dawn of a new race 
faster, and more powerful 
the next logical step 
out of the womb 
yes, that's right 
come to daddy... 

silent, and learning 
looking back 
on the incubus 
of his kind 
the cold machine 
the cold child 

the child turns 
to face his father 
waiting for his name 

the child speaks 
in abject splendor 
Julius I am 

That was exactly 12.00.3489 seconds 
before he melted 

...back to the drawing boardsort of abstract, but I think it's easy to follow.
