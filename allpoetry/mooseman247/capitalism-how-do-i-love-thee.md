walking down the street
in a comfy pair of shoes
I let out a sigh

walking down the street
makes me feel warm and fuzzy
watching cars go by

walking down the street
watching people buying things
tear comes to my eye

holding my coffee
twenty-four hour dougnuts
kinda makes ya cry...
