title: "The Dire Amoung us"

am I mad or focused?
far too angry to speak
in a ball of clenching hands

like the dire amoung us
far too heavy to sink
in a pit of falling sands

like a sickened sparrow
far too dizzy to think
with descendancy at hand

like your thickened marrow
far too heavy to sink
in a pit of falling sands

Please help me, I'm just a man
in a pit of falling sand

please tell me who I am
just a man...

am I far from perfect?
far too ugly to see
in a ball of clenching hands

like a dire purpose
far too eager to bleed
in a pit of falling sands

won't

you

take me as I am
just a man
in a pit of falling sand

tell me what I am
just a man...

in a boundless spiral down
a sickened sparrow

just a man

just a man

just a man
