---
title: "Kingdom for a song"
writer: "Mooseman247"
slug: "kingdom-for-a-song"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/15333-Kingdom-for-a-song-by-Mooseman247"
---

let it in your eyes
let the beast inside
filling up your mind
reaching new
levels of depravity

let the devil cry
let the beast inside
filling up your mind
crying out
in newfound insanity

foot inside the door
leaves you wanting more
tasting what's in store
selling out
your soul for a melody

leaves you on the floor
tasting what's in store
leaves you wanting more
handing down
your soul to the enemy

the deal's not what it seems
grand illusion dreams
fattening your seams
swallowing
your trace of humanity

if you hear the call
do not take the fall
say that will be all
I don't need
your pricey commodities

soul to the enemy
sold for a melody
your kingdom for a song
