sitting on the cold
first thing in the morning
the door is locked
the sleep is fading

slippers on his feet
first thing in the morning
his pants are down
and he is waiting

reading breaking news
first thing in the morning
of men and sheep
and human cloning

sudden heart attack
struck him without warning
he couldn't wipe
while he was dying

that's how they found him
later that same evening
ass in the air
and house flies flying

Ugh. What a way to go...Not my best work, but maybe you'll get a laugh out of it, if you're as sick as I am.
