---
title: "Nightmares?"
writer: "Mooseman247"

prophetic dreams of ugliness
or was it all okay?
perhaps I need to look inside
or maybe look away

fumble spit and screaming fool
a spectacle of sound
blind and bloated broken boy
a prison all around

where does meaning enter in
the space between my teeth?
what the hell happened to me?
what bleeding lies beneath?

a symbol of my selfishness
or was I just afraid?
perhaps I need to change my mind
if that's all I can change

pale and beauty atrophy
a cage within my ribs
grave and graceful gallantry
is where my deamon lives

where does meaning enter in
the space between my teeth
what the hell happened to me
what bleeding lies beneath...
my tortured sleepVanity... Is hard to fight.
