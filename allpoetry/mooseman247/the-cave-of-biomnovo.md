I didn't like the sound
but I hated that smell

the hissing mechanics of it all
the thought that it might explode
the thought of being devoured
alive

still, we ventured on
through the maw of hell

nothing

nothing but the sound
the smell
the walls of flesh

we never looked back,
I don't know why
or how
he followed

he seemed to know the way
in our confusion
panic
disorder
we follow orders

one by one
up in arms
single file
all the while

fire
run
cover
fire

no effect

the mission
the men
all gone
nothing left

nothing

but the sound
the smell
the walls
of fleshWierd inpiration, eh?
