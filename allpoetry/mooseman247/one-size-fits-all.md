Woke up feeling kinda funny
legs too long and
arms too short

hit myself from every angle
movement was a
last resort

the pain started getting to me
anger was my
first reply

everything was falling through me
cant adjust I
fear I'll die

one size fits all
that was a lie
when your size changes
things go awry

What was worse was I was worthless
right before an
interview

slammed my nose into the doorway
as I walked
into the room

couldn't find a good posistion
as he told me
he would call

Left him with a withered handshake
then I walked
into a wall

one size fits all
that was a lie
when your size changes
things go awry

as I felt I was no longer
tripping over
every song

I began to feel strange again
legs too short
and arms too long

acclimation wasn't with me
as I stumbled
off the stage

Left the audience with nothing
throwing fruit in
fits of rage

one size fits all
that was a lie
when your size changes
things go awry

I hope I will be so perfect
when my hormones
take a rest

right now things are not so lovely
looking ugly
I confess

like some distorted backstreet boy
six foot two inch
monster twig

how the hell can I find sneakers
when my feet are
so damn big

one size fits all
that was a lie
when your size changes
things go awryWrote this in 45 minutes. This song is finished.
