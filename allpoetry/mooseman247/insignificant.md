Lying on my back
I'm unafraid, and helpless

watching clouds take shape
they're whispering, amorphous

the canvas of the sky
is stretching out, it's endless

the world is far too big
for one like me, to harness

a passenger of fate
a foolish tag-along
afraid to lie in wait
I'm lost in transit

became a broken wheel
turn to an empty promise
beyond superfluous
I am unwanted
