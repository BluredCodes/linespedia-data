Frantic, in the darkI'll save my useless wordsthe ones you'll never seeWorthless piece of artsmile for my faceless friendsthe ones I'll never meetSearching, for a souland cherishing the waythat we can share our needsFeeling less alonefrustration melts awayyour notions are your deedsYou said you laughed out loudat my nefarious emoticons,and if you ever find your asslet me know if you can put it on againnot finished. This one goes out to all of you people that really interact across the globe. Finding friends and soul-mates, without ever physically meeting. These are the relationships I cherish. Thank you all for being part of this community.Mooseman.
