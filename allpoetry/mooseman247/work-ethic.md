9 to 5
from 10 to 6
then 6 to 12
and after midnight

40 hours
of misery
at $6.50
it's just not right

work is crap
an Illiad
a myriad
of high priced food-junk

dangerous
embarassing
and harrowing
as suicide monks

burning with a purpose
wanting to go home
and get a real job
