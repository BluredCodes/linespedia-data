too much all at once
stop it
i can't fight back

have to choose my battles
storm away
scenarios flash through my brain

need to break something
teeth grind
pillows go back to shape

need to leave a reminder
eyes roam
need to destroy beauty

find a spot
perfect
expensive and important

dig my fist into the wall
feels better
pull out dust and mortar

vindicate my pain
revenge
on the inanimate

f*ked this up again
knuckles bleed
why do I even bother

start to relax, now
anger fades
form follows conformity
