---
title: "Leave ( fallen bones )"
writer: "Mooseman247"
slug: "leave-fallen-bones-"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/50436-Leave---fallen-bones---by-Mooseman247"
---

you're screaming like a banshee
you're wailing at the sun
why don't you come down here
we could just drive
and be happy you're alive

it's not like sleep
you're wailing at the sun
why are we waiting here
we could just drive
and be happy

resounding drone of overtones
an echo of the fallen bones
so far
so long ago

overtime and over-zealous
you're wailing at the sun
you shouldn't be here
we could just leave
and be happy you're alive

It's not like sleep
you're wailing at the sun
why are we still here
we could just drive
and be happy

resounding drone of overtones
an echo of the fallen bones
so far
so long ago
so long ago
so long agosleepy thoughts
