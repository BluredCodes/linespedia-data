run around
wield the axe and laugh
at what I am
and what you have become

nothing hurts
everything is fine
if you just watch
I'll dedicate
my anguish to your fun

stay with me
I promise to be bad
if you persist
my horror is your wealth

I'll be your fool
a wrecking ball
a comical
neandertal

until I find
a better kind
you people are
the purpose of my fall
