---
title: "My First Love Song"
writer: "Mooseman247"
slug: "my-first-love-song"
source: "AllPoetry"

knowing you
not just in my eyes
not just in my hands
but in my mind
and in my heart

finding you
learning who you are
learning how I feel
like something new
and beautiful

reaching you
on my tippy toes
on my whisper's edge
what lies beneath
glitter on gold

losing you
only to  be found
only to come back
where I belong
where dreams are told

yes,
I'll miss you too.see you soon...
