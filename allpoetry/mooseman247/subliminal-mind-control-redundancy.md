you're feeling sleepy
your eyelids grow heavier
you are now relaxed

I'm going to speak
you will be obedient
microsoft is love

mother and father
your alpha and omega
mac sucks donkey balls

you love microsoft
because not loving bill gates
will cause you great pain

I snap my fingers
you will not remember this
go and buy XP

*SNAP*If you play the windows startup sound backwards, it says "YOU SERVE THE ALLMIGHTY GATES! BOW BEFORE ME! AAAARRRGHHHH! POTATO."
Maybe I misunderstood.
