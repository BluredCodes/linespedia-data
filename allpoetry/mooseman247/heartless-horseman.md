still think about her sometimes
when I smile, and then I cry
and wonder why

still see her laughing eyes
so sure of what she felt
I wonder why

still feel the warmth sometimes
sends shivers down my back
and I try to hide

I want to love again
I want to laugh again
not sure I will
not sure I can
I want my heart back
give me my heart back
so I can love again

every time I go to that store
I feel she's right behind me
reaching for my hand

every time that I lock the door
I feel she's right behind it
I dont understand

every promise on the floor
I feel she's right beside it
reaching for...

I want to love agan
I want to laugh again
not sure I will
not sure I can
I want my heart back
give me my heart back
so I can love again

every time I close my eyes
I reminisce on happy days
and see their end

every time I search the sky
I see a falling star and think
I'm falling in

but every time the morning dies
another night for someone else
can now begin

I want to love again
I want to laugh again
not sure I will
not sure I can
I want my heart back
give me my heart back
so I can love againCountry groove here.
