Dagger toes, a scratching post
a mother and a child

stillborn fangs and baby's breath
a patient running wild

like a spider's offered flesh
a cradle and a curse

wrapped in hybrid devil mesh
a patient growing worse

a mother and a child

started with a little kick
a mother and a child

threw his feet and thrashed his neck
a patient running wild

growing dead and living yet
a cradle and a curse

blood red sun and baby's breath
a patient getting worse

a mother and a child

feeding from the inside out
a mother and a child

syphoning the sinner's mouth
a patient running wild

burden of the animal
a cradle and a curse

the joy of artificial soul
