title: "Half Empty (Half Full)"
writer: "Mooseman247"

not losing a son
but gaining a relation

not losing an hour
but spending time

not judging yourself
just making observations

not killing yourself
just drunk and high

not dying
not dying yet
not dying
not living yet

not curing the pain
but killing the sensation

not curing the doomed
but streatching time

not loving yourself
just self gratification

not killing yourself
just drunk and high

not dying
not dying yet
not dying
not living yet

not eating your words
but service of contrition

not saving the world
but taking lives

not thrilling yourself
just making conversation

not killing yourself
just drunk and high

not dying
not dying yet
not dying
not living yetChanged the last verse, like it better this way
