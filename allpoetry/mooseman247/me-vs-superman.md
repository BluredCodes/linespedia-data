What are the odds
that you coul sabotage
another perfect plan

how did you know
just how the wind would blow
a psychic weather man

I was as mean
as my own rain machine
I piloted that day

I was a god
the governer of sod
pulse of the weather vane

he was the one
that ruined all my fun
I tore his cape and ran

I was not done
although he thought he'd won
me versus superman

threw him a storm
a brilliant force was born
he waved it all away

took some attacks
but lightning struck him back
we raged on anyway

long story short
I needed to abort
to fight another day

we'll meet again
on that you can depend
Lex Luthor gets his way!From the perspective of Lex Luthor in some big robotic rain machine, fighting superman. Hope you like it!
Trying something new here. Telling a story is difficult in poem format, even more so, in lyrical format.
