---
title: "Unhallowed Tonic"
writer: "Mooseman247"
slug: "unhallowed-tonic"
source: "AllPoetry"

I don't want your
robotic answers
unified, and final

I just wanted
some contemplation
shoulders, not solutions

you can't solve this
with synthetic thoughts
it's a moral question

you think you can
rationalize me
I spit on your logic

I will not take
the easy way out
your unhallowed tonic
