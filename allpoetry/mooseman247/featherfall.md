---
title: "FeatherFall"
writer: "Mooseman247"
slug: "featherfall"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

you stole my words
you stand between
reality and dizzy dreams

just like a bird
your suffocating wings
surrounding me


that's why it hurts
I lack the means
to shake away this effigy

just like a bird
a swarm that never sings
is drowning me


magnetize a little blood
escape the flood of feather claws
because
of all the walls and sudden falls
distracted by the horror of it all
I call to all the shadows in the hall
a message in the squall

just like a bird
your suffocating wings surrounding me
I cannot shake away this effigy
it's drowning me
drowning
..............mesickness and self pity, 
magnetized and empty, 
save me 
please
