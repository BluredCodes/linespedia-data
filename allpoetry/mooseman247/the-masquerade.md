Born of a sire
I'm a childe
I'm a slave

Damned to be dead
and alive
in my grave

the masquerade...

(chourus)

The Masquerade
and the slow Jyhad
the end of days
the wrath of God

(/chourus)

Lust for the blood
of the kin
and for power

Damned to be dead
for the sins
of my father 

the masquerade...

(chourus)

(bridge)

Wake and thirst consumes me
Wake the beast consumes me

(/bridge)

Sleep in the day
for the sear
of the sun

Damned to be dead
and to fear
everyone

the masquerade...

(chourus)

Know that the end
in your hands
is approaching

Woe to the men
who are pawns
of the ending

the masquerade...

(1/2 chourus)
