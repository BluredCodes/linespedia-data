I know why you shiver
how can you be sure
how can you still love me
what are boyfriends for

what if I was perfect
what if we were wrong
how can you be sorry
how can I be strong

I'll be around
if you ever change your mind
I'll be in pieces
while you allocate your time
I'll be in shadow
still grieving the goodbye

I know why you shiver
you say your heart is cold
but I can feel your fingers
as they finally unfold

so what if we are sorry
so what if we still feel
it doesn't seem to matter
when he's behind the wheel

I'll be around
if you ever change your mind
I'll be in pieces
while you allocate your time
I'll be in shadow
still grieving the goodbye

I can still remember
how can I forget
how can you still love me
why should I regret

I hope you are happy
I can still forgive
I don't want to hate you
I just want to live

I'll be around
if you ever change your mind
I'll be in pieces
while you allocate your time
I'll be in shadow
still grieving the goodbye
