Hey Mister, "Would you like a Hot Dog ?"

outer coating,
waxy touch,
inner putrid,
nutrition's flaw.

dog-meal leftover,
trash-can filth,
acid-rain runoff,
nuclear glow.

stale bun,
runny ketchup,
tasteless onions,
bacteria present.

"Sure, I'll have one"This was just a late-night rant!
