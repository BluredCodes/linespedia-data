---
title: "Curling"
writer: "Spiderman"

Strength and love
They are what you need
To play the sport
That passion heads
To stand up straight
To make the call
That can win the game
Or crash and fall.

Slip and slide up down the ice
Make those shots so very nice
With the hits and all the draws
We can win, define all laws.

For A vs. B, which should win.
B should not A is champ
But laws are flawed and so am I
So B will win overtime.
For they have passion and they have heart
And that is all they need to start.
