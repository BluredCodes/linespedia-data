title: "Drink, Drive and Die"

Each day that goes buy is one less to be had
To fill up with joy for it’s now in the past
There is no turning back, no travelling through time
To change what you did, hope it was fine.

For you will not get a chance to do it again
There’s no chance for you go back to change what you said
For the clocks of time go forward, there’s no stopping at all
For if they could stop how could you tell time at all

And you made a mistake, a rather large one at that
One you did not understand until after the fact
While your looking straight up, ‘cause you’re down on your back
Wondering why you got in the car when you were staggering like that.

Then you slowly open your eyes a word you try to say
That you so are sorry for what you had done on that day
But there is no sound that comes out, not even a whisper
Then you here the sounds of your younger crying sister.

She’s asking, “why isn't brother going to come home?
Why is his body laying in the middle of the road?
Why is there blood oozing from his head?
Oh mommy please don’t tell me that he is dead.”

Then you finally understand the mistake that you made
The one, that’s now cost you, it’s your life that you’ve paid
What were you thinking drinking and driving, insane!
You put your own life in danger but for the last day.

For it’s too late to go back, it’s too late to change
To late to say you’re sorry you won’t do it again.
What a horrible thing that could happen at this tender young age
Phone the high school and tell them that yesterday was the last day.

Now your falling asleep, everything’s getting dark
Why didn’t you clue in at the very start?
That by drinking and driving it’s your life that you’ve paid
And your life will end on this dark ugly day.
