Where shall I go
What shall I do.
Should I help you
Or should I not.
Risk my job
To make you glad.
Lose a game
So you're not mad.

Oh where shall I go
What shall I do.
Help not do I need
To get past this problem.
But to get to the problem
Now that is a different story.
You want me to quit
I want to win.
It's the only thing I love to do,
But then again, I love you to...

Oh where shall I go
What shall I do.
I have no clue
I don't know anymore.
Your happiness means so much to me
Yet you wish for my to give up my goals.
A chance to go the Olympics I see
But you want me to stay home with you.
Never had I made it this far
Never again shall I have the chance.
Yet you wish for me to stay behind
Just so you will be happy.

Where shall I go
What shall I do.
Stay here with you
Or take off for school.
Broaden my learning
Or hinder myself for life.
Seems like an easy question
Yet when I see your face brighten up.
I always wonder how to keep it as such
And not bring a frown down upon you,
With a decision that I make.

Where shall I go,
I don't have a clue.
What shall I do,
I need help with that too.
Make you happy or save my job?
Keep your smile or go get the gold?
Have happy thoughts of you,
Or engage my mind in school?
Oh where shall I go,
What shall I do
I don't know but this I do,
I
Need 
HELPI personally tend to place the happiness of others above that of my own.  If somebody is happy because of something that I have done then that makes me glad.
