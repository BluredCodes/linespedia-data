title: "What is that?"

What is that?
That beats in my chest,
Faster and harder then ever before
Upon the time in which you enter the room.

What is that?
That brings laughter to my lips,
Portrays a redness upon my face
Creates feelings I've never felt before?

What is that?
That makes me smile
Turns me happy from sad
Glad from mad?

What is that?
Well that is love
A felling I can not understand
But greatly enjoy having.
For it is of you and you alone
And now I understand that my love is out of control.

What is that?
