---
title: "Birthday"
writer: "Spiderman"

Once a day and once a year
There comes a day when you shed tears
And on this day you came to Earth
For it ‘tis the day of your birth.
And for on this day for a hundred years
People will shout and call in cheers
For they all love you and will cry
That they're glad that you’re ageing
One year at a time

Now here is a gift,
But not one that you hold.
For a birthday surprise
Is fun to behold.

For the best gift you get,
Will not cost a dime
It will open your heart
Maybe even makes you cry.

It matters not where you are
Even if you are afar,
For today is one of the greatest days of all
It is your birthday, and that’s all.To all who have a birtday today, tommarow and in the future.
