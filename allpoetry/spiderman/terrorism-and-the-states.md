title: "Terrorism and the States"
writer: "Spiderman"

They fight,
They strike,
Without a single thought of death.

They threaten,
Cause fear,
With one goal amongst their minds.

Terrorists are what they seem,
Close but not quite are they,
For they are the Americans,
Terrorists in a way.

Bin Laden attacks the states,
No proof do they have of this,
But they act like terrorists and strike back
With a swift iron fist.

They threaten Afghanistan,
To give up old Binny or else,
They'll bomb the country to get what they want.

Just like a terrorist.Unfortunatnly I know I will recieve slack for this but this is they way that the americans are acting to the Sept. 11 attack.  They did not negotiate with the Taliban but only 'threatened' saying give us him or else.  Just like a terrorist.
