A day of chocolate
A day of filth
How can somebody enjoy such a day

Prance around with their little candy dish
The kids with bows and valentines
But never really wanted one have I.

Now run out and get your lover a rose
Don’t forget the chocolates in the store down the road
Oh and you defiantly can't forget to get a card
With "I love you" written on it.

A little conceded I may sound
It is true, so what oh well.
For the way I think, the way I feel.
Why should you show your loved one you love them,
One day a year.

If you’re going to buy them a rose, get it in spring.
If you’re going to send them chocolate, buy it in the fall.
If you going to give them a card, well don't send them one at all.

Take them a letter, written by your hand.
Telling them how you feel when you’re apart
How it makes you so sad.
But whatever you do, don't wait until Valentines Day.
For this can be done on any day.
But you should do it today,
Just so they understand how much you feel for them.I don't see why I should have to wait until valentines day to tell people how i feel.
