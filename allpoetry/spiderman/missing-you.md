---
title: "Missing you"
writer: "Spiderman"
slug: "missing-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/34998-Missing-you-by-Spiderman"
---

It is so hard to write to you
When the words I can barely say.
For the way that you make me feel
Is with me every day.
I can be sad, depressed, angry, and mad
Each so much the same.
For when I finally talk to you,
These feelings are washed away.
They are replaced with hope and love and joy
And feelings I didn't know I had.
I wouldn't trade them for anything in this world
For if I did I'd go mad.
Being apart from you is so hard
For every day feels like a year.
But when I finally get to talk to you
It is a blessing to my ears.
No voice in this world can sound so sweet
Nor can one represent such beauty.
Once you stop you lovely speak
It does seem quite a pity.
For I could listen to your voice all day
Whether you are talking, or even singing.
For miss you now and every day
I do wish that I were there.
To make sure you had a fun filled day
And that pain harvested not your tears.
To see you go through any pain
Would be to bring hurt upon my soul.
Live not, could I, anymore
Without you by my side.
You’re the only person I need in my life
To let me carry on.
With you there, then I need not despair
Of the path my life shall go on.
Being with you is number one
And allow nothing in the way shall I.
Not work, nor baseball, nor curling too
Shall keep me from being with you.
I would willingly give up anything
If it meant that I could be with you.
Even if I shall die
My last request would be.
To see that beautiful face you have
So I could take the image,
To heaven,
With me.
