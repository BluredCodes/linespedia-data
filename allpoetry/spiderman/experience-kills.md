There are some in the world

That think guns and cars kill people.

Then there are those in our world

Who believe that it is people who kill people.

Where now those who live in our reality

Know that it is inexperience that kills people.

For the inexperience to properly load a weapon

The inexperience to swerve our of a skid

The inexperience to drink to your safe limit.

There like getting keys to a bomb

In which everyone in the world has a key.

A key to a car in which can kill in a second.

A key to a gun which kills many in instants.

A key to get drunk which can kill one or kill many.

The second you life is in danger

When you think that you’re going to die,

Believe all that tell you, for it's true.

Your entire life DOES flash before your eyes.Recently I have just got into an car accident where my car skided out and I went into the ditch.  A few more feet and me, and my car would have wound up in a cannale no less then a hundred yards away.  It is an experience I wish not for myself nor anybody in this world to have to live through.  Unfortunatly all I could do is wish for it happens everyday.
