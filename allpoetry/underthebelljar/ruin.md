It's every single time
That it happens again
Once again
And it's my own fault
And my own stupidity
And I try to learn 
From my mistakes
But, wait there I go again

And
Again...
Again...

I build my house
With cards
And here comes the wolf
To blow them down
Only it's not him
It's me

My hands shake
The cards sway
Fall to the ground
And it's always 
A losing hand

These events grow 
Tiresome
I punish myself 
Once again
Through sleepless nights

I always get so far
So close to OK
Then I blow it
Push it all away

Because I just can't stay
When I have a phobia
Of order and calm
When every day feels the same
I crave chaos
I can't live this way
