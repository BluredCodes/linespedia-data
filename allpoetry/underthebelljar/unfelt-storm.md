I'm starving, I'm weak I'm tired, but I still have so far to goI'm going nowhereBut, I'm going there so slowI'll be content when I finally reach my destinationWhen, I can lie and restOh, To Sleep!To feel the warmth of a blanket on my skinAs I lie in the cool night airDreaming of a calm peaceTo lie in the eye of the stormTo see everything spinning around meViolent, twisting, spinningRoaring, ragingAnd to not feel it's affectsTo not see the aftermathWhat a gift!
