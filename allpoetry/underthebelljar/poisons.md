I wander through this life
What am I supposed to do with no direction?
Endure, until I'm through?
Enjoy this moment while I'm still here?
Maybe, that's what I'm thinking now
As I'm consuming this poison
Now, maybe I can get through
Accomplish what I need
Moment of solitude
Maybe I can get through
The high
To satisfy
This hunger that life can't subdue
Looking for help now
What to do?
I just might come unglued!
Would you like me better then?
Would I excite you?
Nourished, yet lonely
I need you!
The panic rising
Taking over now
I'm numb, and quickly losing touch
Help me now!
Come on, I don't need much!
Give me all you have!
Here I go
I'm diving into the deep waters
Don't know what they have in store
I'll find out soon enough
Do you care?
I'm a good person
Do you know that?
Does it matter?
Would you follow me if I left?Let me know what you think, thanks!
