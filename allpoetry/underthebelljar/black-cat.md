As I choose my new path
Heading straight ahead and looking back	
There's always the haunting black cat	

Twisting, turning, switching roads	
Trying to lose it	
But, failing completely
It follows like it knows exactly where I'm going
It follows like it's inside my head...
And clawing

Confusion sets in
The roads seem to be spinning
What to do?
What to choose?
Better do it quick
Cause here comes the dark feline
Quick decision
I continue
Oops, wrong decision
I'm lost again
