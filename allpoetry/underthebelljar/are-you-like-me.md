---
title: "Are you like me?"
writer: "underthebelljar"
slug: "are-you-like-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/7921-Are-you-like-me--by-underthebelljar"
---

Have you ever waited on a call never receivedAre you like me?Have you ever stored away a thought to be pondered laterAre you like me?Have you ever winced upon a haunting memoryAre you like me?Have you ever seen the hurt in the eyes of a silent strangerAre you like me?Have you ever regretted an unspoken thoughtAre you like me?Have you ever forgotten the details of an important dreamAre you like me?Have you ever longed for something impossible to have?Are you like me?Have you ever felt disconnected from everyone you meetAre you like me?Have you ever wondered if you're different or all the sameAre you like me?
