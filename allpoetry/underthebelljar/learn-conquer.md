The latency roared 
Through cacophony
Mauling obstacles 
Breaking up straight lines

Lessons with punishment 
The beaten realize
Finding resolution 
For defiant attack

Life possesses a possibility 
Of bane
Unless maneuvered correctly
Skills acquired 
By many fleeting defeats

Molding time is a must
Otherwise the journey
Evaporates into the fabric 
Of sky and air

Leaving things the same
Isn't worth the hurt
