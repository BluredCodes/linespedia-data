Trapped in the shellCovered, tarped in worn skinShaking to breakNaked and analyzedFalling down like glassWith the rhythm in the distanceMaking the monsters danceTo an unsteady beatThe buried emotion creepsSeeping through the cracksThe forgotten openingsOnce thought tightly sealedRising, dominating the surrounding sceneThe weaker sensations fallingFeigning death with acquired skillIt towers and takes the reigns
