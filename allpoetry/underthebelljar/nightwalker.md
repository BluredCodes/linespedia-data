---
title: "Nightwalker"
writer: "underthebelljar"
slug: "nightwalker"

Walking through halls
Towards an unheard calling
Legs scissoring slicing
Hours to seconds
The night seems to never fall
Combatting the loneliness
A stubborn opponent
I'm always falling short
It seems
Of the rest in the state
Of dreams
In this house, I'm wandering
In my head, I'm pondering
Exhaustion atrophies
Building complexities out of the basic
Getting stuck in the mirrors
Searching for images in the shadows
Nothing ever seems any clearer
Wishing it all away
I turn on the light
And pretend it's day
Until the sun creeps to the horizon
To consume the moon and the toll it takes
For once again it will soon be rising
