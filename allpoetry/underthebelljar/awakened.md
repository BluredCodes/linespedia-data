Awakened
Rise to the day
Swallow the Prozac
Help along the way
Eat salads
No red meat
Blend in homogeneous mixture
Make it feel okay
Or we will drain you out

Draining...

Continue forward
No, we can't hire you
This is Revelation
You haven't taken the mark
Garden your nutrition
Pray your soul to keep

Pray...

Don't climb up here
There's no room for you
You don't belong
We're from a long line
Of conformity
Begun by rebellion
Lay down and forget

Forget...

Calm down now
Just stay put
Don't step out of line
We'll have to amputate your foot
Not my fault
Just the way it is
Learn the rules before you play

Play...

Breed
Someone to take your place
Innocence is an illusion
Make them fit
Kill your children 
Through birth

Birth...


Red panic
Don't fret
We'll kill you off soon
Somebody's got to do it
Go ahead
Try to runaway

Run...

Caught you in the corner
Didn't honestly think you'd get away
Cut you open
Dismember and send you to the lab
For research and display

I guess the joke's on me
