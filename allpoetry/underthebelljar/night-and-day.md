The moon glowing
Bright as a feline's eyes
Which reflect the fallen star
Supine, but radiating
Black magic
Provoking the helots'
Blood to mad vortex

The serfs run helterskelter
Under the unconscienable tyrant
He grins in understanding
The joy sometimes found in insanity

He reigns unopposed
As his slaves wander
In this new found
Light-ridden land

Then, the revolution
The almighty Sun
Rises from his grave
To blanch this unfortunate spawn
Of his light
He giveth and taketh away

He strips the earth of darkness
Illuminating with grace
The great flood...
Of light 
But dims memories 
Of the night before day

As the creatures below
Under heavy-handed rule
Slow to a steady pace
Returning to their lives of servitudeMetaphorical...
