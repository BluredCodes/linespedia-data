Here it comes once again
The effect of too many nights alone
I start to look within
So redundant the feeling
Of disappointment
Tangled up in all my shortcomings
Oh, the wasted time!
Oh, the lack of accomplishments!
Oh, the addictions!
Oh, the self-centerdness!
Oh, the lack of ambition!
Oh, the lack of direction!
Look at me!
I'm doing nothing!
I need to start building
A life
But I don't have the plan
Oh, well lookey here...
A knife
No, snap out of it
I'm snapping for sure
