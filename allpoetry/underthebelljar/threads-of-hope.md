title: "Threads of Hope"

Threads falling from the skyTwistingThickening cablesReaching and grabbingWrapping around my fingersDesperately pullingTrying to riseLooking downJust for a brief secondThe ground shakesI quickly look aboveAs the the cord falls limp And I focus on the distant sunFlamingThe surrounding blue-white heatMelting the cloudsDripping down on my faceSliding down my cheek
