A strange season
Winter and warm
Put out the logs
Lit prematurely
Awake sweaty and 
Too weak to throw
Blankets to the floor

Take a walk
Temperature rising
Inside a turtleneck
Watching men in shorts
Wash their cars
With reindeers and Santa
Decorating their lawn

This day last year
It was snowing here
Did the world stop spinning
While I was in my room?
Seasons went still
Maybe Winter overslept
And it'll be here in SpringIt's about 80 degrees outside in December...something's wrong with this picture, it's usually 40 here.
