What I wouldn't give To spin verses with pithTo protrude like a knife InsideThe mind's Manifestation of a soulBe possessed With a fecundity To leave ones mind laden With wonderings of whyA bundle of wisdom to leave Some day before I dieTo inspire a search, a seeking, then a find
