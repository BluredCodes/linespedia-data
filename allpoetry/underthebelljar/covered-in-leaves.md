title: "Covered in Leaves"
writer: "underthebelljar"

In this dismal one room house
The windows are covered with leaves
Where everyone left
Now, I can't see out

Parasites slide up and down
The walls and onto and into me
Taking the little I have left
I'd fight back
But, I'm running low on fuel

I sleep on the bare floor
With my dog
My best friend
He'd leave, but 
No one else would take him
Just like me

I pace at night
Don't want to lose 
The time of darkness
To inhale 
Slow, woeful, melancholy energy

The contrast 
Sunlight depresses me
So, then I sleep

Backwards, the way I've always been

And, no one wants to visit me here
Because once you're here
It's hard to break out
There are no doors
And the windows are already
Covered in leaves...
