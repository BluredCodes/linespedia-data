It's been an eternity since I've seen your face
I wonder how you've changed
Would I recognize you if I met you in this place?

In a letter you asked if I think of you
And, I reply "everyday..."
An aching truth

When I'm in bed and feeling lonely
It's conversations with you I play
I spent last night screaming

Where are you?
Why all the mystery?
Hiding under that shroud
Yesterday, I thought I saw you in a crowd

You're always with me
But, why are you so far away?
Where you trying to escape me?
Or maybe just yourself?

All these questions 
No answers to fill the void
You left
