Empty days
Empty nights
Seems like yesterday 
We were discussing our plans
For flight...

I didn't know then
I don't know now

We were standing in a circle
Roads stemming outwards 
Trying to decide where to go

Everybody left me
I was feeling the vertigo

I sprawled out on the ground
Staring up...
Making pictures in the clouds

Stranded and alone
Stunned...
I still can't believe they left me 

Yesterdays ago
And Today I'm still trying 
To find
Tomorrow in these...
Empty days and
Empty nights
