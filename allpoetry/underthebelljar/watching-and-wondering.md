title: "Watching and Wondering"

Watching the people parade
In a great motley mass
Strange faces slipping
In and out of my focus
My contemplative gaze
Pondering as they pass

What shapes their lives
Their days
I can't help, but wonder
Do they know the way?
If so, did they stumble
Across the hidden path	
By way of misguided foot
Or did they have to dig
Claw deeper into the soot

So, I watch per chance
To discover
The way without strife
The endless road
That leads
Down to the meaning of life
