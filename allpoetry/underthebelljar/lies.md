The moon has come full circle above
Stirring the blood in everyone
As the plastic half circle spreads across my face
To indicate I'm listening and understand
Halfway, I rummage through past scenes
Separated inside myself
Feeling things having nothing
To do with things outside
Adding up the clues
To see my way through
Reaching the truth strangled
Inside a cocoon of dishonesty
Second-guessing, trying to peel layers back
To grasp the immature worm
