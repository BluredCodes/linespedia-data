---
title: "Tragedy?"
writer: "underthebelljar"

They are
Sad and fat
They feel 
Weak and helpless
Against fate 
They worry 
Yet do nothing

They think 
Getting high... 
Is a tragedy

They lie awake
At night
Anxious 
About tomorrow
Replaying 
Stupid things
They said

They think 
Getting high... 
Is a tragedy

They have
Everything
But feel
Nothing
Unsatiable
Appetite for
Success

They think
Getting high...
Is a tragedy

They drink 
Sour wine 
To relax 
But, end up 
Sobbing
In their napkin

They think 
Getting high...
Is a tragedy
