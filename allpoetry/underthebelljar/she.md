She strolls along the deserted streets
Waiting for some forgotten stranger to meet

She dresses all in black
And speaks silently

She looks, but never quite finds
All that she needs

She treads the untested waters
In the cold, blue sea

She fumbles with the secrets
Held deep inside, unseen

She makes contact
With the vast, wide eternity

She stoops in the hidden caves
Down low, cowardly

She blooms dead roses
Then stores them away in me
