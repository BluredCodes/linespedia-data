title: "[I held on so tight]"

I held on so tight
My hands went numb
And, I couldn't feel 
You slipping away

Now I gaze over the plains
The sky, the only thing
Obstructing my view
Of things more distant

No life to be seen
I wrap my arms 
Around myself
Refusing to let go
