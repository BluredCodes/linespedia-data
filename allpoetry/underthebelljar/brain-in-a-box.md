---
title: "Brain in a Box"
writer: "underthebelljar"
slug: "brain-in-a-box"
source: "AllPoetry"

Ignorance creates serenity
As it breeds inside the skull
Delusion of Utopia follows
Ambition drains
As potential is robbed
Knowledge starved mind
Withers to insufficiency
Locked in a box
