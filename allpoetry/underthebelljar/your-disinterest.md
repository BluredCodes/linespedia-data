The way you act
I guess I don't matter
As your lies tickle my ears
It's something I've come to expect
Something I uncomfortably accept
My problems just aren't as glamorous
As you make your's out to be
Your's all action
Mine slow reality
I'm not angry
I'm just sad
They say anger is sadness without passion
Yeah, that's me
The one you halfway see
You know I'm just quiet
So you don't even ask
If anything's wrong
It'll probably just passNot good, I know.  I just want to save it here anyway.
