---
title: "Did you?"

Did you feel it
As it slipped through your fingers 
Once again...

Did you choke up the words
Or swallow them
To let them settle within...

Did you go up the road
Stumbling, and wandering
Trying to find your way...

Did you seize it
Another wasted moment
That died on the same day...

Or did you leave it
To boil and rot
In your mind while in bed where you lay?
