---
title: "I Can't Feel You"
writer: "underthebelljar"
slug: "i-cant-feel-you"

You hold me close...
But, I am ignorant of your warmth
I know only that
I don't want human contact today...
It burns my skin

You whisper,
"I love you, do you love me too?"
Only to be disappointed by the reply,
"Not today, not yet.  But soon maybe...
Soon..."

I know it'll be never
But, I don't want to hurt you...
So, I'll give you your daily dose
Of hope

And soon I unwrap 
Your arms from around me
And announce...
"I just want to be alone today."
And I wonder...
Will it always be this way?

I wish I could explain to you
If I could just say,
"It's not you, it's me.
I just can't feel...
I'm sorry, If I could
You'd be perfect for me...
But, I can't so just go away.
I'm sorry, maybe I will feel someday."
