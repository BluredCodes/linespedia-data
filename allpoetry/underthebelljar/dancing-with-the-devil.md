title: "Dancing With the Devil"

As I peer through the shattered window
Inside the black house I once lived 
The walls eaten and the floors beat in
I can see you dancing with my old friend

He whirls you around as you laugh insanely
The twists and turns look so familiar
As the fog spins round in all its fury
And as he turns I see him smile
All the horror, madness, and excitement
In his grin

With great skill he plants his foot
And lifts you up into the dusty air
Then slams you into the ashes and soot
Before he drags you to his lair

The old place has a bright new flair
And, at first, you are happy there
Since your bed has been laid out with great care
No more of this scene can I bear
And as I turn to leave, you will see
The knife still bloody in my back
For Satan has danced with meThis is a new version made with suggestions from vrsmulator who helped me polish it up a bit.
