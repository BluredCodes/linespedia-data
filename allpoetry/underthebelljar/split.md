Life split into two realities

The ground bursts with energy
In form of green blooms
Flowers with hues
Too many to name

Children run madly
Intoxicated with youth
Discoveries to be found
Everyday inside old truths
Refreshed when uncovered

Sun glows cosmic red
As it retreats turning
The sky navy to set the scene
For the moon and stars
Light years away yet close

    Then, the world spins

        The soil is dry with 
        Brown sticks embedded
        Flowers once juicy now brittle
        Monotoned with death

        Children lost before play
        Died while ripe on the vine
        Never had a chance
        To decide whether life
        Is curse or gift

        Grey clouds cover sky
        Until dusk when it fades
        Descending even more into black
        Staring at the stars 
        Making you feel small and helpless

    These are just two planes

    Of the same reality

    If sensitive, you'll feel both

    Chaos trying to decide which is true

    Both is why the most tortured souls

    Hide inside a beautiful mind

    It stings, it's so magnificent

    It stings, it's so gruesome

    Maybe one day both with strike

    Together to pull forth peace

    Into the split level perception

    Become one and clear

    To find an intersection 

    Of extreme planes

    May bring order to 

    Anarchy of judgements

    Will let me rest

    Maybe one day

    Not yet
