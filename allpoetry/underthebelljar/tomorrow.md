Tomorrow                                                                                                                                              
Everyday, I live for Tomorrow
But, everyday is today

And, I tell myself
That everything I'm learning right now, will be put to good use one day
And, there's so much I will do Tomorrow
I will change the world, and everyone will be glad I was born, but me
And, as I lie in waiting, I dream of life
The life I will have one day when I'm done with this fitful sleep

But, I'm losing my meaning in all this misery
Misery without a moral
And, I can't wake up from this nightmare
I pinch, I cut, I bleed
But, I will not wake

And one of these days, I'll show you all!
Well, maybe just myself because I know no one else is following this
But, I'll show me 
I'll make something of myself
Tomorrow, when I escape from this pain
I will see, my suffering was not in vainLet me know what you think and what can be improved...thanks
