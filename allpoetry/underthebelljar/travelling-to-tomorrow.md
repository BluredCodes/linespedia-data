title: "Travelling to Tomorrow"
writer: "underthebelljar"

Exhausted by the everyday battles
I still have far to go...

Drowning in the distance
I may be going nowhere
But, I'm staggering there so slow...

I'm waiting to take control
Choking on contradictions...

I'm quickly losing touch with reality
Not strong enough to hold 
A mind that floats on fiction...

Looking back, not watching my step
I don't notice the obstacles spawn...

Eyes wandering back to the future
Crossing over, growing near to the hot coals
I must walk barefoot upon
