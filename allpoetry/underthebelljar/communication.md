I try to explain
But, the words that float from my mouth
Evaporate before reaching your ears

I want to tell you
But, I know you'd take it the wrong way
For how do I describe something which exists only in my mind?

So, I don't even try 
And, you think it strange
I cry for no reasonA little short...let me know what you think.  Thanks!
