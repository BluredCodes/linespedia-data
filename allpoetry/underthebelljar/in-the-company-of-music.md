title: "In the Company of Music"

I adore this noiseThe instruments blending togetherInto a fine-tuned melodyDripping with inspirationIt flows into my earsThen continues all the way throughTo stir the emotions once settledThis music is fine companyHolding my heart tightSitting here aloneIt simulates a presenceIn the otherwise silent nightI turn the volume upJust loud enough to drownOut the lingering thoughtsSo that my focus is spent Completely on something more distantWrithing with passionIt reverberates throughoutThe empty corridorsSaturating the airWith relatable notesUntil I flip the switchThe sound drainsAnd I'm all alone again
