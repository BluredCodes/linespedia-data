I'm having trouble...
Walking around on this 

S H A K I N G

Ground...

I thought I could handle...
This much freedom
I can do what I want
When I want...
Great!
I will have so much fun
Wasting this time

Well, I'm having no fun
Even though time's being
Wasted...
The days are slipping...
Through my fingers
I don't know what to do...
With the slipping days

'Carpe Diem'
Easier said than done...
