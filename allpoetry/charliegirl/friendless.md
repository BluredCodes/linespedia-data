---
title: "Friendless"
writer: "CharlieGirl"
slug: "friendless"

I wish I had a friend
someone who is there for me,
and even with this smile
its my pain that they can see.

I wish I had a person
whom I could call a friend,
and even on their toughest days
I know I can depend...

On someone to hold my head up high
and wipe away my tears.
Make me laugh or even smile
and chase away my fears.

I wish I had someone 
with whom I could trust.
A friendship that will never die
or fade away to dust.

I wish I had a friendship 
that would never come undone.
I wish I had a friend 
or maybe just someone.

I wish I had a friend
so that when everyday is done,
I wouldn't have to come home 
and realize I have no one.
