---
title: "Alone"
writer: "CharlieGirl"
slug: "alone"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/4754-Alone-by-CharlieGirl"
---

She sits naive in darkness, holding out her hand.
Waiting for her Prince Charming or for someone who understands.

But her hand lies cold and empty the same goes for her heart
for every guy that left her first tore it apart.

She sits naive in darkness swearing never to make the same mistake.
For every guy she learned to trust she later discovered was fake.

She sits naive in darkness craving love or someone's touch.
Wanting every guy she ever loved because she misses them so much.

But nothing lasts forever even love must come to an end,
and she will fall for everything until her heart can truly mend.

But until then......
She sits naive in darkness, holding out her hand....
Waiting for her Prince Charming or for someone who understands
