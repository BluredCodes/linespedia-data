---
title: "I Alone"
writer: "CharlieGirl"
slug: "i-alone"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I alone found no one but me.
I alone see these tears I see.
I alone opened my soul to you.
I alone was pierced through and through.
I alone many nights have cried.
I alone have more than once died.
I alone drink the blood from my lips.
I alone have released my grips.
I alone fear the possibilities.
I alone have seen what I see.
I alone have cut to the vein.
I alone live this life of pain.
I alone told you my secret.
I alone thought you could keep it.
I alone gave you my heart.
You alone, tore it apart.
