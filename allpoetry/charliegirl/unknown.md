Lying in bed,Thoughts in my head:Fears that I dread,Words that I've said.Turns into tears,too much for these years.Is it my fears that hit the wrong ears?Things you've saidRumors you've spread.It's going to my head,Everything is mis-read.Is it our fate to purely hate?Or is it too lateto change the stateof life we're leador so it's said,'Our friendship's not deadit's just been mislead'.
