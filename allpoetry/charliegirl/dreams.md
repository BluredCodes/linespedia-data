So many nightsI cry myself to sleep,wishing I could hold you tight,or one promise you could keep.But then I wake to findmyself stuck in reality.Wishing the love I give to you for once would return to me.It seems as though when I get too close you always drift away.You hold my heart in your handwhile my love is disarrayed.You know the power that you havebut know not what you do.I can only wish and hope thatsomeday you can love me too.
