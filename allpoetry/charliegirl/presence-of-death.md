title: "Presence Of Death"

She cries innocent tears
for all forgotten years.
She misses him and he
has forgotten her.
She cuts deeper everyday
wishing her life didn't go on this way.
She cries out for him and
he has forgotten her.
She doesn't smile as much as she used to.
What right does she have after all she's been through.
She wants him back and 
he has forgotten her.
She cuts one more time, 
deeper than ever before.
All her pain is gone...she 
craves for him no more.
She has forgotten him.
And they have forgotten her.
