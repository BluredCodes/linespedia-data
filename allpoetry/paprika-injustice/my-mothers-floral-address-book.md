i spent the sky, orange and tepid and truewith a hand alone i stung my childhood.a wall i corner to the best of my heart.with every crelune and magenta starship, i shattered,wry glances from men wearing red stopwatches keep me from sharingmy only living caresswith you.just know my poems are.....offbeat? yah thats it
