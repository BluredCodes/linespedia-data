she catagorized her smiles, to a tune written by her tears.
a charismatic glow seeped from her hands,

there was so much love in that summer, at 7:00,
what happened to her nostagia enducing words?

promoting herself, by the electrical box,
the little boys on their aluminum childhood.

i can't belive in all her splendor, 
with one good night glance,
hazed my eyes for tomorrow.not much to say....its my emotions....this one was a hard on e to write for some reason.
