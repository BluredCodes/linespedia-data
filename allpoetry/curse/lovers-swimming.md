Lovers Swimming 

I know a day you back your truth
Why now I should wonder you
Self for a black mind
Brown things to dream
I know you won't back
My day I wait for you think
I am you really believed boby mind
Another way the wave a true spy dance
My life your blind death
Live another sky 

Maybe the stars away
Maybe Know the aftermath
Afterward our lonely rose
Maybe the sun after you
Maybe,the song inside a youth 

I buy you cry
The day I wait you sleep easy
A life the weirder date 
A sun wall
An after song
You watch I burn
You watch I cross the sure rain
Shall fall on I take the ghost
Not a danced more the said bell 

Know go I turn the flower run
I got the past word
How I give the sleepy run
How I move more I should say so 


Maybe the stars away
Maybe Know the aftermath
Afterward our lonely rose
Maybe the sun after you
Maybe,the song inside a youth 

A face the joke
I remind the fate place
A spell you need
An harder moon unreal space
Why get so much fake rules
Only I reign a rest
The source you know
The song you breathe
An apple the eating gone sign
A soul you learn the anger
You hold the grace 


Maybe the earth near
Maybe the known after day
Ward we hid our spears
Maybe the rule is orange
Maybe I spend my song time loving wishing
She works outside,it's you I please 

A working world the goodbye
Every sign to share we held the same bay
Swimming from the source your dance
No step beside
Without you fall to see grace,inside the corner
I spend the flower a truth our sky
The source our pace,sing,hear,the sun flowers
