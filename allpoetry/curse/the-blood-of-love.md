twenty years ago tonight
my darling
made up these kisses in our minds
babies we are
always the realm of time
made up this cry
lonely in our stars, we were
into the heart
the blood of love 

twenty souls ago this life
from the born heart
imagine
our song and what is to smile
have ever made up the kiss
the chant of our mind
was it a hand close ours ?
which one was yours in mine ?
how much times the song ?

babies we are
always the realm of time
into the heart 
the blood of love

Near dream, near art
from the strong else life

from the magic which is about
strange looks of souls
maybe you in mine
maybe i you cry

babies we are
always the realm of time
into the heart 
the blood of love

only twenty steps ago the top
my sweet dream

ninety-nine pieces the one
we cry from the dawn
we born the old chant of soul
that's only in our minds

babies we are
