You sit across the tableand tell me of your latest loverGreenYou talk about the futureand cry about the pastBad timingI watch you sip your coffee and nibble on the pie I love youIt's all in the past, no sympathy, please.  This one has confused people in the past...so, How to read this poem:   Two lines of observation, one line of thought.  Rinse and repeat.
