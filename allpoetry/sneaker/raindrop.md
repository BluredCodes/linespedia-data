dripping, falling
gravity calling
nearer my earth to thee

branch to stem 
then fall again
all the way down the tree

down from the crown
past leaves now brown
still struggling to be free

the ground below
is where I must go
fulfilling  my destiny'Well, procrastinating makes one do strange things like this challenge. This is what I came up with in about two minutes.
