title: "Leave a message (limericks)"

A new season,
now it is Fall.
But the weeds are ever so tall.
So outside I'm working
on tall grasses choking
and that's why I'm missing your call
------------------------------------

I can hear the telephone ringing
though in the shower, I'm singing.
But if you should shout 
what the call is about,
I might come to the phone water clinging.
----------------------------------
I hear the telephone beeping,
but in my bed I'm sleeping.
So just leave your name
and a lively refrain
and I'll call you again when I'm creeping.
----------------------------------
OK, relax these are just for a lark, 
something to leave on my answering machine.
Normal messages are boring.Nov 26, added the third.
