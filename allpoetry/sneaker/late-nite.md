Sometimes I find myself alone with Dave on the screen.I watch as New York preensand fawns before the King.  Brief view into another worldwhere diamonds aren't foreverand shooting stars slow down to talk.What secrets revealed this night?What escape from daily pain and drudgerycan I find in their lives?What am I doing?Off evil cube!  Destroyer of minds!Tool of Corporate America!I must respond to Sandman's songwith sagas of my own.I can muse,and dance 'neath lids closed tightWait! It's time for the Top 10.This one's been sitting around for a while, unpolished, but I decided to put it up when I read Auroura's 'Call to Arms.'  It's sort of an opposite take, but with a similar meaning.
