Witchcraft ivory 
Sorcerer's orbs 
Speak the words 
I am transfixed 
Utter the barest mumbles 
and I am yours

I hear your command- 
and draw near, nearer still 
Foreheads merge 
as noses touch 
Slowly the world vanishes 
Only you and I remain, 
and we are one
But in a flash
the spell is broken 
Magic too intense
