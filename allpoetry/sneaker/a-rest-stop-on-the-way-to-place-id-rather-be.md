Let me get your bags, please
No problem at all
All part of the service
Always at your call

How is your dinner
Have a nice day
How was the weather
Did you enjoy your stay

Leaving the B and B
Come back and again soon
I say to the couple
and remember the June
when you were hereI need to polish up the ending.  I'm not happy with it as it is.  I think I loose the journey aspect I was shooting for
