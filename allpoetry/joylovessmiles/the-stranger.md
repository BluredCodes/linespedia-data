He is a stranger even to himselfhe thinks he know who he is but he don't and that makes him a strangerHe is a stranger to others even the people that know himhe is still a stranger because he never lets anyone into find out who he really is A stranger to us a stranger to himselfand as long as he doesn't face himselfthen he will always be a stranger because he will never truely know himselfand for all time he will be know as the stranger
