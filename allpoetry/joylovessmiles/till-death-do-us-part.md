It started with the words "I Love You"
and you held my heart completely
I thought that you love was honest and true
you gave me a ring and we said " I Do"
then you changed and started to hit me 
I loved you completely and would never leave you
everyone tried to help me but I loved you completly
you said that you loved me and that you were sorry
and that it would never happen again
but each time it got worse and you were sorry again
and now you lose your temper a whole lot more
and I just tell my friends I walked into the door
I can't please you no matter how hard I try
and I will forever you love until I die
but I also hate you because you took my life away
and you keep my always in fear
i just keep loving you day by day
but all it takes with you is just one beer
my vows were to death do us part so i will and stay
because I will never give up on my marriage
because god will always heal me and take my bruises away
or I will ride in that last and eternal carriage
because I will love you til death do I part.

                                        Joy Lewis
