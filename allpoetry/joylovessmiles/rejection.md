Hurt in which could not be explainedPain in which cant be controlledSadness in which takes over your lifeTears in which never stop flowingLoneliness in which should never be feltSelf doubt because your actionsSelf worth is questioned because of youIgnored because of the feelings I haveLeft behind like a old toyLeft alone because you no longer need me Used because I have served my purposeAbused mentally by your uneffected heartTormented by the love I will never recieve from youAnger because you have rejected my loveThese are the true feelings of REJECTION.
