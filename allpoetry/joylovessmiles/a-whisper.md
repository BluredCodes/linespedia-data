A WHISPER HERE, A WHISPER THERE

TO SPEAK ALOUD THEY WOULD NOT DARE

TO SAY TO MUCH, TO SAY TO LITTLE

WOULD JUST ONLY PUT YOU IN THE MIDDLE 

SO A WHISPER IS SPOKEN TO SAY IT ALL

BUT IT ONLY BRINGS THEIR DOWNFALL
