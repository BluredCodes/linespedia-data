all i asked was for you to love me 

all i wanted was your love 

and to be able to trust you

you said that i could trust you

you said that you would never hurt me 

or lie to me but you did 

you looked me in the eyes and lied to me

what did i ever do to make you lie to me 

to disrespect me to hurt me 

all i ever wanted was to make you happy

and all i got was hurt and betrayed 

i gave you one rule in which not to break

all you had to do was not lie to me 

and you couldnt even keep that one simple rule

so now you have lost me 

if i cant trust you then there is no us

and dont blame me blame yourself

it was you who lied and betrayed me
