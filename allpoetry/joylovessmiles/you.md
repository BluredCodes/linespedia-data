THE LOOK YOU GIVE ME TAKES MY BREATHE AWAYAND MAKES ME LOOK FORWARD TO IT DAY BY DAYTHE SMILE YOU GIVE ME FILL ME WITH PEACE AND JOYIT'S THAT INNOCENT BEAUTIFUL SMILE OF A LITTLE BOYTHE WAY YOU HUG ME MAKES ME FILL NEEDED TO KNOW THAT OUR FEELINGS HAVE SUCCEEDED THE WAY YOU CARESS MY HAIR WITH PURE PLEASURE MAKES ME FEEL LIKE I'M SOMETHING YOU WILL TREASURE THE WAY YOUR MOOD CHANGES WHEN YOU HEAR MY VOICE MAKES ME FEEL SPECIAL AND MAKES MY HEART REJOICEAS LONG AS YOUR LOVING FACE I'LL SEE THAN THERE WILL FOREVER BE A YOU AND ME
