lying is the one thing that i do not tolerate
there is no excuse or explanation
there is no reason or choice
either you tell the truth or 
dont talk to me at all
lying is the one thing that breaks my trust
and without trust we have nothing
no friendship or relationship or anything
so you know my rule so please dont break it 
you will have to take the conquences
in which woud be that we were no longer 
friends in any way or fashion
I AM TRUSTING YOU PLEASE DONT BREAK MY TRUST!


                                   JOY LEWIS
