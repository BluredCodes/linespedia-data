---
title: "? Why Not Me ?"
writer: "joylovessmiles"

Why not me?

Why am I chosen last?

Why do I always get overlooked?

Why do I feel alone?

Why do I feel used?

Why do I feel abused?

Why am I the strong one?

Why not me when you fall in love?

Why not me when you need a friend?

Why not me?
