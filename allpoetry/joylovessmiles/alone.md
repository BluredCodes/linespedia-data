I AM ALONE WHEN YOUR NOT AROUNDMY HEART ACHES FOR YOU YOUR TOUCH, YOUR VOICE, YOUR KISSI CAN'T STOP TALKING ABOUT YOUI CAN'T STOP THINKING ABOUT YOUI CAN'T STOP WANTING YOUI FEEL ALONE EVEN IN A CROWED ROOMI FEEL ALONE WHEN I WITH SOMEONE ELSEI FEEL ALONE WHEN YOUR NOT THERESO PLEASE DON'T LEAVE ME ALONE
