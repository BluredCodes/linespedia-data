To My Child In Need,

I came to this earth to feel what you are feeling to experience the hurt and the shame and the disappointment. I too have been hurt, abused, wrongly accused, betrayed talked about, prejudged, judged for what I look like, hated because of my heritage, not given a chance because of where I came from. I have also felt love and lost it, I have felt alone and forsaken, unloved and unhappy I too was put through trails and tribulations to build my charater and my faith. Yes I can heal and raise from the dead, open blinded eyes, but Ifirst had to fight my own demons and temptations too. I too had things in which I had to overcome in which made me stronger and who I am today. I knew who I was and what I could do, but no one else did so they judged me on what they could see and not for who I really was. That all changed when they found out what I could do and who I was. But first I had to have confidence in myself and what I could do and not care what everyone else thought  of me. Now I have thousands of people who truley loved me becuase of who I am and what  I can do.  But thisonly happened after I showed everyone what I could do and who I truley am. Yes I am your Heavenly Father but I too had to go through all this and I can tell you from experince that everything shall pass, and the storm doesnt stay long and that you are never alone because i am always with you and i will  never forsake you becuase i love you and you are my child and i know what you can do. So have faith in yourself and do what you have been born to do and all the trails that you have now will soon seem small, becuase you know that it is my will and when it is my will i will  always be with you, and i will be behind you all the way and help you through what ever you go through so chin up and be strong for you are my child for I will forever be with you and look after you and keep you safe.


		                                                                                                                                                                Your Heavenly Father,
                                                                                                              Jesus Christ
