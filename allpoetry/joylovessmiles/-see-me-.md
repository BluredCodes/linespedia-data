Do you not see me?

Am I not here?

Am I that easily ignored?

Am I that easily overlooked?

Am I that easily looked through?

Do I not catch your attention at all?

Well I guess I am, that is too bad
you've missed your chance
with the only person who truely loves you
and you never even noticed me. ©  on Jul 02 2001 11:58 AM PST   0  • 1
