YOU SEE ME BUT YOU ALSO SEE THEMYOU HEAR ME BUT YOU ALSO HEAR THEMYOU FEEL FOR ME BUT YOU ALSO FEEL FOR THEMYOU DON'T TRUST ME BECAUSE YOU CANT TRUST THEMYOU KEEP ME AT ARMS LENGTH AS WELL AS THEMYOUR SCARED OF ME BECAUSE YOUR SCARED OF THEMYOU DOUBT ME BECAUSE YOU DOUBT THEMWHY CANT YOU JUST SEE ME AND NOT SEE THE OTHERS
