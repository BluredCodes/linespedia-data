I LOVED YOU WAY BEFORE I KNEW YOU
I LOVED YOU AND PULLED YOUR HEART TO ME
YOU SAW ME AND YOU TURNED ME AWAY
YOU KNEW ME AND HAD NOTHING TO SAY
I CALLED YOU AND YOU TURNED YOUR HEAD
YOU ACTED AS IF I WAS DEAD
SO I LOVED YOU ALL THE SAME 
EVEN WHEN YOU GAVE ME ALL THE BLAME 
YOU CURSED AT ME AND ASKED ME WHY
ALL I SAID IS WHY I HAD TO DIE
I DIED FOR YOU TO SAVE YOUR LIFE
AND YOU TURNED AND CUT ME LIKE A KNIFE
I LOVED YOU WHEN YOU HATED ME 
I LOVED YOU AND WANTED YOU TO SEE
THAT IT WAS ME WHO LOVED YOU
IT WAS ME WHO CARED FOR YOU, IT WAS ME 
SO I LOVED YOU WHEN THEY BEAT ME 
SO I LOVED YO WHEN TH SPIT ON ME 
I LOVED YOU WHEN THEY NAILED ME TO THE TREE
I LOVED YOU AND I HOPED THAT YOUD LOVE ME 
I ROSE AGAIN TO SET YOU FREE
I GAVE ALL OF ME 
I GAVE YOU THE SPIRIT OF WHO I AM 
I GAVE YOU THE CHOICE AGAIN AND AGAIN
IT IS YOUR CHOICE TO LIVE OR DIE
ALL I CAN SAY IS THAT I TRIED 
I TRIED TO MAKE YOU HOLY AND PURE 
BUT ALL THIS TIME YOU WASNT SURE 
SO WHEN I CAME YOU WERE LOST 
SO NOW YOU MUST PAY THE ETERNAL COST
YOU COULD HAVE LIVED IN GLORY WITH ME 
BUT NOW IN HELL YOU WILL FOREVER BE
I TRIED SO HARD TO MAKE YOU SEE MY LOVE
BUT NOW YOU SEE IT ONLY WHEN YOU LOOK ABOVE
YOU SEE YOUR FRIENDS AND FAMILY
WHO TRIED TO GET YOU TO LOVE ME 
SO NOW YOUR IN HELL AND FOREVER YOU WILL SAY
WHEN ALL IT WOULD HAVE TAKEN WAS A SECOND OF THE DAY
TO CHANGE YOUR LIVE AND MAKE IT PURE
BUT ALL YOU CAN SAY IS THAT YOU WASNT SURE
