once i was free as a bird

was not captured by anyone

but one day someone captured my heart

at first i didnt feel captured in any way

so i gave up my freedom freely

it started off that i was full of love and joy

but then things stated to change

he started getting violent and possesive of me

then he started hitting and beating me

so one day i got up the nerve to leave 

but he said if you live me i will kill you

and to tell the truth i think he would
