I WISH ON A FALLING STAR

UP ABOVE THE WORLD SO FAR

UP ABOVE WHERE I CAN SEE 

ONLY THE LIGHT THAT SHINES TO ME

IF ONLY IT WOULD BE A NIGHT TO REMEMBER

LIKE THAT COLD SNOWY FIFTH OF DECEMBER

IT WAS A TIME OF LAUGHTER,LOVE,AND JOY

AT THE MARRIAGE OF A GIRL AND A BOY

THEIR LOVES SURPASSED ANY OTHER

TOGTHER FOREVER THERE WOULD BE NO OTHER

FOREVER AND ALWAYS THESE TO SAID

THE VOWS SAYS TO WE ARE DEAD

NOW IN THERE BED OF FLOWERS THEY WILL SAY

OUR LOVED SURPASSED EVEN OUR DAY

FOREVER IN ETERNITY IT WILL BE

FOREVER TOGETHER YOU AND ME
