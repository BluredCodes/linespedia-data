IT STARTED LIKE A NIGHT DIFFERENT THAN ANY OTHER
WE WERE FAR APART AND NOT NEAR EACH OTHER 

THE THOUGHT OF YOU MADE ME SMILE WITH JOY
THE PURE EMOTIONS OF A LITTLE GIRL AND LITTLE BOY

THEN I HEARD YOUR VOICE AND MY HEART SKIPPED A BEAT
AS WE ARRANGED WERE WE WOULD MEET 

I WAITED FOR YOU WITH A PATIENT HEART 
KNOWING THAT NOT MUCH LONGER WE WOULD BE APART 

THE I SAW YOUR FACE AND BEAUTIFUL SMILE 
WHICH OUT SHINED ANY OTHER BY A COUNTRY MILE

YOU TOUCHED ME AND LOST MY BREATHE AND ATTENTION
AS YOU WHISPERD I HAVE MISSED YOU I FORGOT TO MENTION

AS YOUR HOT BREATHE TOUCHED MY EAR
YOUR SWEET WORDS AS YOU BROUGHT ME NEAR

HELLO BABY WAS ALL I NEEDED TO HEAR
TO MAKE MY HEART LOSE ALL FEAR

YOUR SWEET KISS AND LOVING TOUCH
JUST KEEPS DRAWING ME CLOSER EVER SO MUCH

SO IF YOU DOUBT MY FEELINGS ARE TRUE
JUST THINK ABOUT ALL I HAVE SAID TO YOU
