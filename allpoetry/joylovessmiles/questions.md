my heart is heavy

with pain and sorrow

with questions 

of why?

how could you?

what did i do?

why are you treating me this way?

why are you running away?

why cant you just trust me?

why cant you tell me your heart?

all these questions i have for you 

but all you say is there is nothing to talk about 

and to drop the subject but i cant just drop it
