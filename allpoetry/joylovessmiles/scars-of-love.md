to have a heart that is perfect is fine 

but to have a heart that has lived and learned 

is something speacil you have felt love 

even thought it was taken away you still felt it 

and the scars all have names 

in which there are also good memories 

and the scars made you the person your are today 

and for that you can thank the scars
