---
title: "Hurt"
writer: "joylovessmiles"

you hurt my heart

you made me feel used 

you say you love me 

but im not sure 

you say you respect me 

but im not sure of that either 

you make me doubt you 

and i hate to doubt 

but it is like your talking two ways 

one to me and then who you really are 

and that hurts that you dont trust me
