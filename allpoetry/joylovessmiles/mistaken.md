hi i am mistaken
im never asked a thing 
but always accused of something
i am looked at with hate,but nobody asks why
im always overlooked never chosen
im the one who everybody knows
but no one really knows 
im the one who everybody makes fun of
but no one asks why i am the way i am
they have their own opinon of me
but no one truely knows who i am 
i am mistaken for who i am 
for what i do, believe, say
and even for how i live my life 
but the only reason i am mistaken 
is because no one truely wants to know 
who i am or what i can do 
because then i would be taken in
and i would no longer be mistaken
for all this i am me but always mistaken

Joy Marilyn Lewis
