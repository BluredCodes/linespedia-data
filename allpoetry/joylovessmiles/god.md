GOD YOU ARE A GREAT FRIEND

YOU ARE SOMEONE WHOM I CAN DEPEND

WHEN I AM FEELING DOWN AND BLUE

ALL I HAVE TO DO IS TALK TO YOU

YOU MAKE MY STORMS TURN INTO RAIN

YOU EVEN KNOW HOW TAKE AWAY MY PAIN

YOU ALWAYS LISTEN AND NEVER JUDGE

I HAVE NEVER HEARD OF YOU HOLDING A GRUDGE

WHEN I THINK IM ABOUT TO GO INSANE

YOU JUST TELL IT TO ME PLAIN

YOU DONT BEAT AROUND THE BUSH 

AND SOMETIMES I WISH YOU WOULD HUSH

BECAUSE YOU MAKE ME SEE THAT I WAS WRONG

TO LET MY WORRYS STAY TO LONG

YOU TELL ME TO LET YOU HAVE THEM FOR GOOD

AND THAT I WORRY MORE THAN I SHOULD 

BUT I CAN HELP I TAKE EVERYTHING TO HEART

EVEN IF IT TEARS ME APART

TO KNW THERE IS NOTHING I CAN DO

THAT IS WHY I ALWAYS COME TO YOU

BECAUSE  WHEN I TALK TO YOU I FEEL BETTER AND SANE

SO I AM GOING TO TELL IT TO YOU PLAIN

WELL AS SANE AS YOU CAN GET IN MY FAMILY TREE

THANKS ALOT FOR LISTENING TO ME
