You lie, you try
you're always high
 you broke the promise that was there
and now you're acting like you care?!
drop the bullshit, take off your mask
you're a nice guy, you finish last!
the beat of the heart hurt every second
stomach aches are killing me
punching walls is my only hobby
it burns, my blood is all I see!
ahh fuck why? am i a slut?
all i wanna du isfuck?
why don't u just think about it
and just keep your big mouth shut.
little prude boy makes me sick
with his horny big-ass dick
you are slefish can't u see
lost your verginity to me...
now i'm laughing crying too
just how loyal i was to u (hint hint)
spilling my guts on this paper
only trying not to cry
thinking of all the things we've been though
thinking about you and I.
why would I ever lie?
I don't wanna say goodbye
because noone knows the meaning
 of how lies ruin your life!
It makes my brin and heart rott
and eventually I'll die!!
