she swallowed it, she swallowed it
why'd you tell, now she's jelous
she would be though anyways.
anyone would be in this situation
You were the one to tell me you fell for me
I'm not sure that you understand yourself.
Ahh If i wouldn't write this my head would explode.
Just either tell her or this is over, if it will
end between you two there will be shit
on both of our asses! Two reputations go bye bye.
and I don't want to be presured into anything
I don't know if I'm sure, if I want to do this get
into a relationship. Your roomate made me think after
the things he didi to me.
"do not trust any male relationship-wise"
- but I can't help it I love you - I think
My face is getting hot, tears are coming out,
she hates me though she doesn't even know most of 
the details of the past.The music makes my stomach turn
@ this point. I love you I want you to be mine and I'll
be yours but the house you live in makes it worse!They all hate me there. CAN'T BE TOGETHER! finnnaly i feel like that whore that doesn't want to cause any harm, just like the one that scarred my soul with "the roomate". HURT
