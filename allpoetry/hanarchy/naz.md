NOT
A
ZEBRA
!
!
What has stripes and short black hair?
Who lives in a dream and doesn't care?
Who laughs loud and makes US laugh?
And she seeems like she's on crack...
She's a chodine-popping girl
Lot's of it can make you hurl
She's is there most of the time
on her shoulder I can cry
I dunno, I'd rather not
better let her smoke some pot
drink some vodka and some beers
'till foam comes out of her ears!
Then she'll go nuts, kick your ass
Yeah you guessed it SHE IS NAZ!
IF YOU GUESSED ZEBRA YOU ARE WRONG 
I WILL PUT YOU IN A THONG!
