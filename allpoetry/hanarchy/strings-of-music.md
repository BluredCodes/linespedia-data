Noone ever notices how they try
try every day and every minute
every hour and every second
express emotions though our systems
we hear it with our uncountious minds
 the media that covers it up
with stupid pointelss commercials
we don't seem to care but yet love the music
some of us didn't grow up with it but I did!
Try to understand the history of classics
one piece can change our understanding 
of anything or anyone.
 I whish I would be a composer to 
write a piece about myself.
When people would hear it they'd cry and 
laugh, mostly run out of the closed interior 
It is the personality of classic music.
Don't laugh cause if you laugh
you can compare your IQ with George W Bush!
Vivaldi, mozart, bach Beethoven
Shubert, Tchaikovsky, Grig and Liszt
You just don't understand untill you hear it!
