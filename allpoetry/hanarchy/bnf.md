The smell is rising in the air
I can't believe that I am here 
again this dirty coffee shop
"hey look there's Willie and the cops!"
good old neighbourhood of mine
The plaza makes me sick sometimes.
It's old, experienced and wise
you go to stafford you'll get high
the smell of weed is everywhere
times i spent here just make me care.
Not that I want to, I hate this place
brings back memories of hate 
and trust, love, honesty and faith.
Love is here once in a while
but mostly we just get on file
use each other, smile and nod
some love satan some love god.
It's soo funny it's all good
we smoke weed and we eat food,
go to school and theN skip class
"Ms.Kennedy! KISS MY ASS!"
Sittin' smoking underage
like rats locked up in a cage
Triple-triple, diet coke
sit and talke then have a smoke.
God! it smells like shit in here
"ahh fuck it let's get some beer!"
