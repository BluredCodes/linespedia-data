I fucking hate you
why do you come back after two long years.
I have almost forgotten you!
How could you, I wish i could kill you!
It is such fear it makes me angry, anger is 
not the feeling for me.
I go insane, don't enjoy anything!
WHY DID U HAVE TO COME AND STAY!
You're not welcome here,DOOR CLOSED!
"WE'RE CLOSED SORRY" or is it the other way around..
Shit I have to care for u like a little child.
Arrive home early, i whish people would understnad.
Right now you're sitting on me cracking 
and bending my back, it hurts it pains.
People think they went though this while they lie!
DON'T FUCKING LIE TO ME! PLEASE! YOU DIDN'T!
I don't want to be educated, you should stay dumb.
If you grow you'll get worse, overpower me.. but don't!
I will do anything for you just stop
whatever it is youre doing....!
Do it to people that are not afraid
of things like you.
I wish i would be one of them!
DO YOU WANT TO TRADE BRAINS?...ANYONE?

P.S: sorry i ditched you guys!
BY:HANARCHYIt's something i'm going though right now.
