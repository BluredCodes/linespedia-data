Stupid-head,  
blue-veins but my blood ain't royal
paranoid-brain,  
don't they say that royal blood is blue?
open-mind,  
scandals all the time becasue of their color
scarless-skull, 
the scars hide on the inside of the skull.
free-soul
I don't believe in them, threrefore 
not-open minded.
well proportioned-body (to others)
not good enough for me.
red-hair
love the color, hate the texture.
Brown-eyes
he loves to look in
they dry out and make me cry.
CONCLUSION-NOT CLEAR!
YOU CAN NEVER BE PERFECT
EVEN IF YOU SUCCEED YOU WILL NEVER NOTICE... HA-HAPfffffmurahha
