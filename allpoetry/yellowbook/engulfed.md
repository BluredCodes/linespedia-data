I see you with your head in your hands
sitting outside wiping tears from your eyes.
We had so much fun together 
we had so much time but no time for goodbyes.

Like watching a movie,
as you recall the good times and bad
You stare out in silence,
I don't think I've seen you so sad.

Hey theres my mom and dad,
my sisters and brother
I see your grandma & grandpa
your father and mother.

Why are you sitting alone?
I've only seen you cry twice.
Oh I wish I could comfort you
to at least feel you; would be nice.

Oh I'm sorry sweetheart,
I didn't ask for this
but what happened was out of my control
ignorance is bliss.

Everyone looks so lost
so tear stricken and heartbroken.
But remember like I remember
the words you once spoken.

You said "Julie I love you
and not even death can obliviate us.
I will hold your hand even in heaven
or in hell whatever we must".

"You are my puzzle piece,
my jelly bean,and my safe sleep.
No matter what happens
In my heart you I will keep".

So now I made it to this place,
unfortunatley you have to wait to come.
But I am in your heart always,
and will send my love no matter where I'm from.This one was thrown together pretty quick so it may be some what off track.
