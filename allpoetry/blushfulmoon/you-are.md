You are moonlight on the water,On a dark summer nightYou are the beauty of the sunriseWhen the world first See's lightYou are laughter in my ear,When tears come to my eyes,You are a welcome signWhee other's have said goodbye,You always have a kind word,When there are none to be hadYou are my laughter,When i am so very sadYou are like a cool breeze On a hot summer's day,You are my road map when i have lost my wayYou are a  candle in the darkAlways burning brightYou are the personWho leads me into the lightYou are inspiration and hopeWhen i can no longer seeYou are of courseAll these things to me(For my husband Danny,as of 18yrs of marriage this February)
