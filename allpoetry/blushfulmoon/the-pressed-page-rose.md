Nestled deep inside my Bible,
I foud a deep red rose
I tried to think of the emory
As i held that wilted rose now cold
I had stored it away so careful
Inside that hallowed book
And it had all been forgotten,
As for something else i had searched to look,
But at last now I remember,
It was the day I gave you my heart
Our love, never it last
But with my rose
