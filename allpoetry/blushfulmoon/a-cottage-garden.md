My garden each year it' such a pleasure
The flower's all picked by me
Moss rose ,hibiscus ,and foxglove
It beckon's ,me each day to tend it
The flower's are my heart's treasure

With careful attention
I give each flower
Hollyhock's, pansy's and zinna's
Sweet nuturing

A warm night
The jasmine bloom's
A home grown delight
Scent's haunt me
With such sweet delight

From seed to bloom
I love them all
My Cottage Garden of pleasure
With you i will share it until frost
And never grow tired of the fare
