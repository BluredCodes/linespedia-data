title: "The Country Store"

Remember
Those country stores
With wooden floors
Iceboxes
That sat outside
Bolts of calico
Cologne and porelian dolls
Adorned the store
with wooden floors

Licorice,
and jellybeans
in huge glass-filled jars
Flour and country eggs
Sat upon the bar

Speckled-blue water basins
with a dipper
Quenched the thirst
For the neighbors who walked
To the country store

No, now not many
of those
country stores
with wooden floors
Are to be found

Instead we have
Flourescent lights
Automated machines
Frozen foods we find
Galore

Coke machines
With talking voices
No bolts of cloth
To be found

No not nary
A country store
With wooden floors
Are to be found!
