title: "Celebrate Spring"

The many colored daffodils
Open their blooms and stretch toward the sun,
Winter ice breaks
And sleepy creeks and rivers again start to run,

On a clear day you can see the bumblebee's buzz,
Spiderwebs everywhere soon will be spun
Robins fly and chrip,
"It's Spring"
"It's Spring"

The old brown fields turn from brown
To a wonderful emerald green
Flowers bloom bright reds, violet, blues,
And every color in between

Filling every field, yard and garden,
With endless scenes so serene
Bluebirds perched on picket fences
Chirping,
"It's spring"
"Its spring"

The sleeping earth comes alive
You can't resist the magical pull
Throw off the warm blankets on the bed
Store away those sweaters of wool

Breathe in the spring air,
Until you are overfilled with joy
Then join in with the robins and bluebirds
and sing,
"Its Spring'
"Now it's Spring"
