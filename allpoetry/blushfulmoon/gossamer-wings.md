Gossamer wings
Reflect myraids of color
Abounding, the spectrum of light
Translucent,luminous,
I emerge from a silky cocoon
Into a world filled with rainbow colors

I am life
A wandering luminous soul
I sparkle i shine,
I am now one,
As i fly off
Into a world full of color
With my wings,
