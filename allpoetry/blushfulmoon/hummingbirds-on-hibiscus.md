Hummingbirds flitting on pink hibiscus,
Hovering from one pink petal to another
A shimmering spirit,
Untouched by the breeze
So delicate
Your glistening, shimmering, silken green wings,
A messenger of beauty
I am always in awe,
By your tiny presence
