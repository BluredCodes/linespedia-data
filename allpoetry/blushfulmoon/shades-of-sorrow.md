Ah! Shades of Sorrow
Come with me share your pain
We'll put  our thoughts in a magic box
Never again to be the same

Ah shades of sadness
I can't show my face anymore
Life is passing before my eyes
Loneliness creeps at my door

Ah shades of sorrow
Let's not give up on love,
I will soon again be happy
With an open mind and heart
Ah shades of sorrow
May you soon depart
