Walking down a crowded street one day
I thought i saw an old familiar face
Familiar yet,
I can't remember where
Seems like year's ago

He see's your face now
That same questionally stare.
What now?
Speak or look away

What a relief,
He is turning the corner
and leaving
Just a passer-by

Good you thought!
Nobody  you knew
But looking up again
You see he is walking 
Straight toward you
And with a feeling of numbness
You wonder what to do next

( While in my mother's house cleaning the upstair's i ran across this book
with poems i kept in high school...just thought i would share one)

Susan
