So elusive is this place i seek
While i wonder about my loved ones,
Does your heart  ever hurt?
Making you ache, needing  love the most

Yesterday we had such a storm
And the wind was very strong
Thunder and lightning
Lit up the sky
As nature played her song

Suddenly it grew so quiet
The sun came out
And i walked outside
As i looked up 
A lovely sight was before my eye's
The colors were arched
I couldn't reach it
No matter how hard i tried

The rain of many colors
Didn't last very long
But what a sight i did behold
Touched with sunburst and magic
And blessed with nature's gold
