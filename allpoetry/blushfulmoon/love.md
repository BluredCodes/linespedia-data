Love the word is so precious
Cherished treasures deep in the heart,
Feelings too long for descriptions,
Yet a force some fail to impart

Love is the beauty in sharing a sunset
As if seen through a child's trusting eyes,
Without you nothing would i be
You alone were meant for me

Its understanding when words are unspoken,
It's praise when one spirit's are low,
It's a hug that so desperately needed,
This Love,
We hope never to outgrow
