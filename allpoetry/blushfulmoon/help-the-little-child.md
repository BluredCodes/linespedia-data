Mommies mommie help me!The precious child criedCan't you hear my silent screams, can't you even tryI can't hear you i can't see you!Once again the mother criedI feel nothing in my heartA mother's love deniedMommie mommie help me! The precious child criedCan't you see he's hurting me, the precious child criedI can't hear you i cant see you!Once again the mother criedI feel nothing in my heartA mother's love deniedI sure must be a very bad girl,deserving all this harmBecause if i were very good i would be in mother's armsI can't hear you i can't see you!Once again the mother criedI feel nothing in my heartA mother's love deniedSo now the child is all grown upBut a precious child still insideThe mother is not the mommieEvenif she triedThe adult and the child went hand in handThe day her mother diedBut the child would harldy look at her momThe child never cried
