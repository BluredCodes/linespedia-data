Come to me, my love
Let us whisper sweet nothinngs at noon
About our clandestine meeting at noon
Underneath a fair moon

As fireflies light up our night
And the secret moonbeams  shine down
I will meet you by our secret pond
Wearing a sheer white gown

Lets dance among the dandelions
While faries frolic and play
We might even here a sweet song they  sing
Underneath the williows sway

But kiss me once before you go
To seal opur agreement so sweet
Tonight our passion will ever bloom so sweet
Under a full moon when we we meet
