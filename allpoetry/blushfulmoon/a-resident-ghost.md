title: "A Resident Ghost"
writer: "Blushfulmoon"

This tiny town,
Has become a part of me
I live here,
Yet it lives with me
This small town of ghosts and gossip
Is my home,
I am a legend
And people tell my stories
Of gossip and whispers,
I can't break their silence
Nor can they see my tears,
For i am a elusive ghost
Merely a shadow here
