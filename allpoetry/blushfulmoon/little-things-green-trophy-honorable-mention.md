Little stones makes big mountains,
Little steps can cover a mile
Little acts of kindness,
Gives the whole world a smile

Little words can soothe big troubles
Little hugs can dry huge tears,
Little candles can light the darkness,
Little memories can last for years

Little dreams can lead to greatness,
Little victories to success
It's the little things in life,
