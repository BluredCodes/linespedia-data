Here i am God talking to you, seeking your advice,
Can't you help me see what's going on in my life?
Some days i am so confused and i hurt really bad,
Somedays i am not happy at all
In other words i am sad

Why do  i feels so angry, no i am not feeling low
I'm not jealous or mean -hearted but my thoughts i just can't let go,
oh for just a few days what i wouldn't give for joy,
But on the days i hope for joy i am so annoyed,
I know things could be much worse
But i am not alone in this nightmare of a curse,
So won't you help me God?
I need your understanding and advice
Can't you help me please?
Enjoy htis world called life
