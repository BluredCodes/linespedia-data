---
title: "I'm Not Perfect"
writer: "Blushfulmoon"

I may not be perfect,
But you love me and my many imperfections
Yet you see me beautiful
You love my pale skin
But i have a colorful soul,
You love me
Like i am a porcelian doll
That may break any minute,
You always hold me
As you give me a kiss every day,
And sometimes you just look at me
With amazement and wonder
Yes, you love me,
And yet you understand
What you have vowed to love
Is not quite perfect!
