City Church By The Road




The city church beside the road
Has stood there many years
You've see many come and  go
And dried  so many tears,

From the podium above the height
You have preached sermons day and night
The church bell rings loud and clear
For those who come and hear

You tell your congreation
The future belongs to thee;
And to thine own self be true,
Come closer to God
You tell those who have sinned
Repent and start anew

Oh city church beside the road
So many pass you by
Each Sunday as God call's them closer
To that heavenly home 
In the sky,

So when next you pass
That city church by the road
For inside the doors
Are the answers
More precious than any gold

(Blushfulmoon)
aka Blush
