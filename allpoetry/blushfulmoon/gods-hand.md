title: "God's Hand"

If it rain's or snow's tomorrow 
Well then let it be
And even if the angel's sing i will be oh free
If it's warm or if it's cold
Or i might leave today
Then i may stay where i am
And live to be so old
He is strong and i am weak
He shelter's me from harm
And even when anger speak's
He is my rock,my life, my soul
My heart forever is his to hold
If the sun should never shine again
No  more heartaches nor will i bleed
Yes with this miracle, my master can succeed
I am still but a child in his eye's
But in his kingdom of love i shall arise
It is my master's plan
All my life to him i give
Resting in the palm of his hand's
