A tiny piece of Connemara marble
Is held in my hand
I am surprised
To see this heavenly marble
With greenish hues 
Coming from wetness reflecting
The big heavenly sky
Where the cliffs are cathing
Rays of sunlight
Somewhere in Ireland calle Galway
Where these people have chosen to live
I understand more now of their small pleasures
And i am pleased as i look around 
With the innocence eye's of a small child
