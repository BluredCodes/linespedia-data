Sunnydale Manor quite the nursing home
Squeaky floor closed no earthly sounds
Not a window to let in the sun Sunnyvale will take care of you
A door opens up revealing stark white walls
Yes Sunnvale is just like a home

Two meatal hospitals beds sligtly askew
As they sit side by side
Plastic padding to keep their bodies in place
As their old bones toss and turn
Keeping them happy in  Sunnvale's home

Chairs on wheels that recline and bend
No private bath baths that have no doors
Closets in bedrooms with stark white doors

Chalboakboard with picture of family from years ago
Do they ever look at them or even know?
Is this what we are looking forward to?
Dining areas where meals are fed
Crafts here to  who can remember in their right head.....
A piano yellowed that played too much
Large screen t.v. Can they hear what they say?
Its now quite time from one to three
Patient's taking a nap to be worry free
Head fuzzy from Thorazine glad its not me
Are you looking forward to Sunnydale
I got to tell you no not me
