Now the pear,wild plums and cherry trees
Have dropped have dropped white bloosom outside and over the wrought iron fence,
With hues of green and gold
Summer sun dapples
Green- colored stones with muted tones
Tall pine trees sawying and boughing in the wind
So intoxicating you can smell the aroma
Pears, wild plums and cherreies
Soon will be  in  old dishes of  glazed fruit
Pear, plums and plums meant to be canned
Lie unfinshied drying on the screen
Abandoned
Here the dead have all become
Like sping and summer casting off a hand me down dress
