As summer and autumn come to a still
Chill winds blow
As I look to the sky
Dark clouds deliver our first snow

Snowflakes fall
The earth's awashed in white
Snow-covered fields,
Shine in the pale moon-light

Winter is here with an icy breeze
Landscapes changing
Around snow-speckled trees

Creeks now stilled
Where peaceful waters flowed
Glazed over with an ice
Emanating a diamond glow

Ice droplets cling
In winter's frigid hand
As we too await
The arrival of springI have read and commented on
