In the dark consuming night,
As dawns beauty is hidden from sight
Two lovers in secret  are on  the prowl,
Nothing stirring but stray dogs and owls
Two silent lovers,secretly intertwine
Two souls forgotten
Lost in time,
Holding on for a few stolen minutes
Taking their hearts to broken limits,
A forbidden love not meant to be
Hidden, from all eyes to see,
So in the darkened night they meet
Embracing before the light,
Silent lovers, secret lovers
