title: "Reminisce The Dead One"

As I walk around in the cemetery
And try to portray the dead souls
I think of these people
Whose life had yet to unfold~

I pay respects to those I lost
And think of the family 
That once was~

As I looked at the tombstones of the dead
I think of the coffin
Forever their bed~

All these dead people
Take away my breath
Yet still they are here with us
And it's called death~
