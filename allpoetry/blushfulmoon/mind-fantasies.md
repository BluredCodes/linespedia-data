Some think life is a fantasy,
Yet very hard to find
But i explore my inner thoughts,
So calm in my mind
And far off in the horizon i see
Deep thoughts inspiring me,
I created something deep in my heart
Love unconditionally,
Until life and death we do part,
The sky is filled with clouds so billowy
A satisfying feeling so true,
Hours will pass until the stars will arrive
I lay content with my own sweet sighs,
Quite an experince
Of the ultimate kind
Thanks for allowing me this journey
With thought os everything,
As I take you on a jourey
A walk through my mind
