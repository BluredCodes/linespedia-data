title: "Eternity in Love"
writer: "Blushfulmoon"

I long to feel your touch,
Loving you so very much
Hearing your laughter and seeing your smile
Makes life so much worthwhile,
Feeling your body next to mine
More intoxicating than any wine,
Our two bodies now are one
Two hearts beating togother,
Two loving people togother forever
