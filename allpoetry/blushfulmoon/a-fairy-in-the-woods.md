Have you ever seen a fairy

Flitting through the woods

On her wings on her wings of gossamer colors

Sparkling with magic

In the dark nestled forest

Shes so rarely ever seen by you or I

Tho if you listen very careful

Hes song is heard by small tinking bells

Just for us to charm and please

And welcome us 

In her sunny dales of color

Whe she passes you so swiftly by~
