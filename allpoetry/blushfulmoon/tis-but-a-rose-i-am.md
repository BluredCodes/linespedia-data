From a root i grew as a rose
Soaking up the rain and sun
At night as i dozed under the watchful eye's of moonflowers
Come morning
The dew dripped off my petals
Falling in creeping thyme
I bloomed blossomed,and opened myself up to you
As my blooms fell off, my dried petals linger
With intoxicating smells on you clothes and linen's
From the center of my bud itself i give you oil,
That softens the skin and delightfully
Perfumes your bath
For i am a rose
Fragrant with love
