title: "In My Old House"
writer: "Blushfulmoon"

Petals from peonies fall,
Gently drifting to the floor below
In my old house

The grandfather clock ticks louldy
In the eerie silence
Sometimes i hear ghost-like voices calling me
In my old house

The spectacles lay on the table folded
Patiently awaiting their owner
For no one will ever come claim them
In my  old house

Oh to loved ones who has gone on
Will you see me as i rearrange the memories?
In my old house
