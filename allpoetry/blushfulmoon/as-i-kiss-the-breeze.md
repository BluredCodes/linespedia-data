title: "As I Kiss The Breeze"

A gentle breeze is blowing,
in the night wind
I hear the windchimes tinkle,
And tree frogs are chirping,
I wish my love could feel it
But he lies sleeping,
So i put my fingers to my lips,
And kiss the sweet air,
Knowing he will recieve it,
As we are now one,
It was that way from the start,
A kiss,
In the night,
For you to catch,
Upon awakening
With a kiss from the breeze
