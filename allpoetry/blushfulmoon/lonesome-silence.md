Dark depression
	Floats like a cloud over me
	Yet I feel so alone
	Crying tears
	And not even knowing the reason
	Why!
	The earth comes alive
	With trees and flowers of spring
	But my mind and thought's
	Are like those
	On a dark dreary day
	All these tears
	Of uncertainty and grief
	I feel like I am drowning
	In a raging river
	I try to smile
	But the mental anguish
	Grows in confusion
	Shadows surround me
	Like desperate people
	And once again
	I'm hurled back into darkness
	June 1994,
	Written January 25th, 2002
