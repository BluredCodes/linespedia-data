Have you ever wondered just how the river flowed?
Or where it ends on it's way
How the sun and moon trade places
Each and ever day
How do the evening star's remember?
Their places every night
When darkness acts like acurtin
As we see this wondrous sight
How does the wind remain unseen?
Yet we feel the breeze
Everday are works of nature
This we have known
Not often thought of
Yet daily we are shown
