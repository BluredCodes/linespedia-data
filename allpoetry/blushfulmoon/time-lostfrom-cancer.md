I knew somewhere  along the way,
 I would get this dreaded diesease
 Breast cancer
 For it's heredity
 But when the time came
 I asked God,
 Why me?

 The chemotherapy makes me feel sick again
 I am so confused, alone,
 Painful thoughts tear my soul apart
 My body feels like a corspe,
 Yet I am not dead,
 
 Weak and silently i wait,
 My tear's fall like rain
 From this dreadful disease inside me,
 Locked in like a prisoner in his cell
 I cry and my heart breaks,
 For the fragile life i once knew,
 I asked why why why?
 As I put my head down and silently criedWritten January 5th, 2002 ©  on Jan 05 2002 08:52 AM PST, Susan Vessels   personal  • angst
