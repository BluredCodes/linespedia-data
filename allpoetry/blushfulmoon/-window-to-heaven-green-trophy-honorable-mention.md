I wish there were 
A Window to Heaven
So that I might glimpse and see
Just what is waitng on the other side
For a person like me~

Is the sky etched in stained glass?
Like the windows of a church
Will God be there waiting for us
Or will we have to search~

Do angels really float along?
And are the streets paved with gold?
After all for years and years
That's the story we've been told~

Will loved ones that gone on before us
Recognize us once we are there?
And will our friends and family miss us
Or will they really care~

Does it rain or does it snow?
Or is it radiant everday
Do we laugh and recall memories
In the youth of our yesterday~

Will we  look like we did on earth?
Or are we forever young
So many questions left unanswered
That's a mystery to everyone~

I wish there were
A window to Heaven
So I might glimpse and see
Just what is waiting
On the other side
For a person like me~March 1994 This one has been published......In a Christian Magazine.... I have always been told this is what heaven looks like with Streets Paved of Gold we're not hurting anymore and are forever young
