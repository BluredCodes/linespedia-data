title: "Life's Simple Jewel's"

I am a very simple personLet me tell you why,I see beauty in simple thingsWhen other people pass it byTo me the trees are emeraldsAgainst an azure skyAnd the sparkling Dew upon the grass;Are like diamonds to my eyesThe sunshine is my GoldOn a summer day so brightAnd my Silver is the moonThat shines on a dark nightThe tender touch of a child handJust knowing that they careI may not have much moneyBut my riches are beyond compareYou see there is beauty all around usJust take time to look and seeUse your imaginationAnd you could be rich like me
