Upon a hill i saw a view
I wished this land belonged to me,
Surrounde by the many trees
And in the distance 
A bubbling stream
As the sun shined down on the water
It sparkled and it gleamed
A field full of white daisys
In the meadow beyond
I saw little ducks swimming on the pond

In the trees the squirrels play
Running and jumping
Up and down
Birds of every color
Flying all around

As the evening fades the red sun set
In the shadow of the moons silhoutte,
And when the shadows melt in the night
I can recall the memory
Of this breathtaking sight
