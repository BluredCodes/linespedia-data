I am a poet
Words are my muse
My pen is an angel wings
No thoughts or words confused

My imagination is full of wondrous thingss
Mystery, romance, magic
and super-frivolous flings
My pen and I
How swift and fast it glides

Sometimes late at night around the midnight hour
Even then the pen
Still has me in it's power

Poems, oh how some fill me with cheer
Other times full of sorrow
Looking back over the past
Dreaming thoughts of tomorrow

I am a poet
Words are my muse
Intended to last
As I express my thoughts
For now and times gone pastWritten January 5th, 2002
Molly Susan Vessels aka Blushfulmoon
