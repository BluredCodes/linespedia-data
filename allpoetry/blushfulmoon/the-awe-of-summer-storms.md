Brilllant lightning flashes to the ground,
As far off thunder rumbles,
And threathen's the peaceful summer's eve,

Torrents of rain fall to the earth
Deadly lightning zigzags to the ground
Day turns into darkness,

A blue ozone of noise,
Unleashes blessings
That mother nature has cried,

Afterwards, the sweet smell
Of the summer storm,
Calm once more,
As we wait for the darkness
