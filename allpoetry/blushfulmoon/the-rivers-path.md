Walking down a path i see a river gently flowing,
A beautiful spring day and the sun is brightly glowing,
Birds everywhere singing their song
I see small schools of fish
Quickly swim along,in the distance i see a deer
Quickly get a drink
For fish, birds, and deer
The river is a mighty link
A fragrance of cedar trees lingering in the air
Wildflowers and mushrooms are popping up everywhere
I was feeling slightly stressed and came here to unwind
When i leave i will forever carry the peaceful river in my mind
