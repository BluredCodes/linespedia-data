The wolf stalks the forest,
Looking to stop and rest
Hungry and alone;
The wolf
She is now alone,
The full moon rise's high in the sky
A blue moon bright with light
We can hear her wailing sounds
As it resonate's through the night,
Ghostly she looks with eye's of amber
Hunting and looking
Trying to remember
Where she belong's
As she howl's her lonesome song
A wolf
Searching and lonesome
On her own
