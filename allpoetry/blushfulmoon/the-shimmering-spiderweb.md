Oh how diligently the spider has spun it's web,
In the corner of a post
To catch,
An empheral dewdrop,
diaphanous, glistening light
it shines, it shimmer's
completely caught unaware,
In the beauty of all it's splendor

A tiny black spider,
With a dot of red on it's back,
Weaves furiously, back and forth
Unaware that someone is watching,
As he criss-crosses his web
Within thi drop of dew,

Raindrop hues reflect off each other,
As the web of silk dances
Decorating the world with beauty
The beauty of nature
The beauty of life,
Made possible by the intricate web of the spider
