We have thought's that soar above the sky
	That even surpass the clouds up high
	A magical joy we want to hold
	River's of tranquility,
	And wondrous peace untold,
	A brilliant light that will shine
	From the depth's of our soul,
	The circle complete
	That makes life a whole
	Hope and inspiration are two
	Ingredients we want to blend
	Memories past,future''s cast
	Time and again
	Paradise found in warmth and smile's
	Feeling blessed and loved
	Is what makes our life worthwhile
