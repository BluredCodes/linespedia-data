---
title: "The Dagger & The Rose"
writer: "Blushfulmoon"
slug: "the-dagger-the-rose"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/30880-The-Dagger---The-Rose-by-Blushfulmoon"

You are the dagger,And i am the rose,We are inert winedThat's how the story goes,we are interlocked you seeFor the dagger and the rose;Represent you and meThe dagger is very strong and sharp,And will strike a deadly blowTo anyone who harms meYour beautiful lovely rose,The rose may look soft and fragileBut looks are so misleadingAnyone who harms my daggerMay find themselves cut and bleeding,And so our love will lastThats how the story goesTogether forever, you and meThe dagger and the rose
