All these years we've been togother
And God all that we've been through,
It should bring us closer
Instead of tearing us into

We no longer talk
Our interest they have changed
We have all but grown apart,
Nothing is quite the same

Your too busy for me
And i am too busy for you
We are both just too busy
Doing things we want to do

You have that meeting honey
I must be on my way
Honest it really has been
Just a busy day

This is like a nightmare
Or is it just a dream?
What happened to us?
Nothing is what it seems

And when it's too late,
We will realize
We were already leading
Quite the seperate lives
