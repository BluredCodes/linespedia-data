I once had a rag doll
So tattered as could be,
But that little ragdoll
Meant everything to me,
She wasn't as pretty as other dolls in the store
But every time i looked at her
I wanted her that much more,
She didn't wear a fancy dress
Covered in layers of lace
But i knew in my heart
Shed had found a special place,
She knows she is loved,
It's very plain to see
For she's a special rag doll
