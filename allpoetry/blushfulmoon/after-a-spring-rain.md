Alone,
I sit in the quiet woods,
After a spring rain
And suddenly the sun's rays 
Break through

In silent stillness,
I watch a glistening raindrop
Roll off a shiny leaf
And hit the wet leaves
Still untouched,
By last winter,

Jack-in-the-pulpits,
Rise majestically,
To life,
And fern's fronds bounce back to life,

I smell the fresh earth,
After a spring rain,
And i wonder why,
It can't be like this all the time
