Happily I blow bubbles
	With the children on the street
	They are amazed,
	That I can blow one huge bubble
	With my lips, pursed just so
	Up into the air they float,
	High, higher, higher,
	Multicolored at first,
	Then changing to shades
	Of purple, green and blue
	The higher up they float
	The more opaque they become
	Floating upward
	Toward the branches of the trees
	They seem to kiss the air
	And then

	They burst!
	Written Feb 7th 2002
