PhAnToMs LuRk ArOuNd ThE dEeP dArK dUnGeOnS, 

wOnDeRiNg WhAt tO oVeRcOmE.

DaRk cOrNeRs Of mAsQuErAdEd viLLaInS,
   
cOmE tOgEtHeR tO hIdE fRoM tHe WiNd.

/YoU aRe iN tHe ReaLm Of ThE uNkNoWn SiNiStErS,

oF lOsT fAiTh AnD BrUtaL mUdErIsTiC CaLLs.

WiTh OwLs On ThEiR pUrCh

TiS wHeN yOu KnOw,

YoU wiLL NEvEr mAkE iT oUt.
