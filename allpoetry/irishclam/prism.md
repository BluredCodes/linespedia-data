title: "Prism"
writer: "Irishclam"

My thoughts are coming together
like a dozen crimson roses;
beautiful in stature,
pure as love.

Lightning strikes the timber
outside the window.
The window of our lives,
for the rebirth of our hearts.

My heart skips beats,
.....breath races,
as I suddnly realize,
I found the one for me.

Music is my rainbow,
And you are my pot of gold.
Now there is no more thinking,
....../I do love you.
