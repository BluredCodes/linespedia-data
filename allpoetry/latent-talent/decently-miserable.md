---
title: "Decently Miserable"
writer: "Latent Talent"
slug: "decently-miserable"

Take the fall... Off the edge
Give it up... Let them win
Fall into fear
Feel the hands.. that shoved you there

Look in the eyes.. Of those who lied
Let it out... What you hide
Push the ground.. On which you lie
Pick yourself up.. Keep walking by

Slam your head... Into the glass
Count how fast.. Your hours pass
Push your hand... Through the wall
Grab the one... That made you fall 

What you think... Is what you know
How far you walk... Is where you'll go
What you trust... Is what's inside
Everything you know.. Is mostly lies
What you feel... Is wrong in life

Spit in the face of blame.. to crush your shame
Take pride inside your name

Grip the throat of the voice that lies
Tighten until your hands turn white
Don't let go until the voice is gone
Don't let go until its not strong

Take the torment sent to you.. Take the blame for what you didn't do
Make up for what isn't lost... Let them think your body is soft.. 
Tear the rope from around your neck... Give them what they can't accept
