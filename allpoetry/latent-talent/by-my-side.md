title: "By My Side"
writer: "Latent Talent"

I lied
I cried
I denied
I despised
By My Side Was You

I cheated
I deleted 
I decieted
By My Side Was You

I hurt
I cursed
I burst
By My Side Was You

I bled 
I shed 
I fled
By My Side Was You

I was lied to, cried to
Denied and despised
Cheated, deleted, decieted
Hurt, cursed, and burst
Bled, shed, and fled... 

By YouGetting what you give... can suck...
