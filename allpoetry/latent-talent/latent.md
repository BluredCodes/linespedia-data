---
title: "Latent"
writer: "Latent Talent"
slug: "latent"
source: "AllPoetry"

Things you've done
Are not who you are
I never knew 
You'd reach this heart

You were held down
Hold me get up
I know you're scared
I'll go slow

The feel of you
The feel of me
Close my eyes to see your face
Close your eyes to taste my kiss
Gone away this emptiness
Feel it now
Your endlessness
Feel me now
My gentleness
It lies along the lines of the lips

Answer me
I question this
Does it bring it all to end?
Me, I lie
Honest lies
Behold my truth
My truth is you

Feel my pain
My pain and sorrow
I'll take yours
And it I'll swallow

In my hands I feel your fingers
On their tips I feel their reasons
In your skin I taste your sweetness

In my arms I feel your perfection 
Inside them is your protection

Understand my words and movements
Taking away my tears and bruises
I give you my eyes and fingers
View your face in all its beauty
And feel your skin in all it's splender 

The wish of time to stop
Makes the hours faster
The wish of you as real
Makes my fear disaster
The thought of you in mind 
Makes the stars shine brighter
And the thought of you as mine
Makes the angels seem less higher
