---
title: "Time & Space"
writer: "Latent Talent"

The stars and skies in lover's eyes...
In the black of night... and cold of ice....
The water blue in raging sea... 
Is crashing waves of jealousy... 
While the crying clouds are falling down.. 
Raining their tears onto the smiling ground.. 
Drifting scents of happiness...
Are blown by the strongest winds of enchantments.... 
As the light of day by the shining sun... 
Brings the sound of laughter... making sad ears burn... 
Tears of pain are cried by the moon.. as time goes on.. 
And goes on to soon... 
Fairies dance... on leaves of gold... 
In worlds where young.. is same as old... 
Angels fly with wings... only children see...
And sing songs only tiny voices can sing...
The open arms of broken hearts... always seem so feather soft... 
When the thoughts of thinking minds alike.. 
Dream the mystery of a dreamer's delight... 
The untouched hands of dying trees... bring the flowers to their knees... 
Under lies lives this world of peace.. 
Look in my eyes... and see through me... 
Hold my hand and feel this place.. 
It all lies in time and space... 
Unlock the door ... walk through with me.. 
I'll show this world of complexity...
Where the souls are freed... 
We can swim the waves of jealousy..
And dance with fairies on golden leaves.....
Trust this world... trust in me.. 
The stars that light the blackness of my skies... 
Lie within the blueness of your eyes.... 
And the crying moon shines it's light...
Across this kissed by you face of mine....
As here we stand... on the blade of life... 
Staring through the gates to the otherside.. 
The fairies wave.. while the sea crashes by... 
The sun and moon at the same time shine.. 
While the dreamers.. are dreaming their delight.. 
The old are young.. and fly with angel's wings... 
The trees are touched.. bringing flowers to their feet... 
A world of peace... without the lies.. 
The key is in two lover's eyes... 
Join with mine... and come inside...
