---
title: "Content of A Tear"
writer: "Latent Talent"
slug: "content-of-a-tear"
source: "AllPoetry"

All the emotion a person feels
Seem to barely fit inside them during anger
All the thoughts a mind thinks
Seem to barely fit inside a mind during confusion
All the pain a body feels 
Seems to barely fit inside it during sadness

As tightly squeezed as emotion, confusion, and sadness may be
Inside such large containers
They amazingly all come together
They form together
The fall together
They are wiped away together

Everything fits in a tear
