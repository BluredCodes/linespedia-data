---
title: "To Let You Go"
writer: "wwjdmommy"

I shared years of my life with you
I became a part of you
I lost myself in you
But now I must part from you
Now that it's time to let you go
Time to set you free
To tear apart the you from me
Confusion intertwines with pain
It makes me want to run to you again
For I know nothing but you and me
And I fear the only me
I know you have caused me pain
Have hurt me again and again
I feel the fool to feel this way
I should relish this separation day
My inner strength to you I'll show
And in the end I'll let you go
To be strong enough to stay away
To face this world alone this day
Strong enough to let you go
To place you in GOD's hands.....
and let you go
