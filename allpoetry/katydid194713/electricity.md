Our electricity was out this morning.
Have you ever thought of how we depend
on this miracle of power that we 
thought would never end??

I sympathize with the people;
our friends on the West coast.
They are learning to live with less,
when society shows us the most.

More, more, and more the markets
of the world say to buy.
Get more of these appliances 
and those appliances to apply.

With the energy crunch making
every wattage of energy felt,
and the price of gas is making
everyone tighten their belt.

We don't realize what a wonderful
world we live in until
we get to the point of having
to live without or pay that BILL!!
