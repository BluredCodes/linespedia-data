One thing about camping.
You can always find a friend.
Campers like to sit and visit
and we don't ever offend.

We're usually always a happy bunch.
We're ready to have fun.
People ready to help each other
to get any job done.

Whether it is backing in
or hooking up a rig.
Campers like to meet new people
and friendships are really big.

We always stay in touch.
We may see other once in a year.
We make friends for a lifetime, tho,
and hold these friends very dear.
