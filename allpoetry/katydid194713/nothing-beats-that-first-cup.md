I find I can't really get going well,the very first thing in the morning.Take it from my husband; you needto stay away or give me warning.The first thing I need to start the day,is the FIRST cup of coffee from the pot.Nothing tastes quite as good or flavorful,and it is the FIRST cup that hits the spot.Mmmmm!! It has to be one of the mostdelicious tastes I have found.First thing, to start the day,the coffee beans all freshly ground.That aroma is not like anything elsein the world, to my perception.It has a smell all of its' own,and there is no exception.I just GOTTA' have my very first cup,to start my day out right.Depending on what kind of day I have,I might also have a cup at night!!
