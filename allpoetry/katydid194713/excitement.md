title: "Excitement"

This week is when my grandchildren
are supposed to come here.
They don't live that far away,
but mother won't let them be near.

I call to try to talk to them,
but mother hangs up on me.
I miss out on their school awards,
cause their mom won't see.

She would rather see them punished
by not having us around.
Than giving up her hatred and 
letting forgiveness be found.

But, if things go right without
her putting up some fight.
The children should come to their dads'
this week on Thursday night.

They get to stay with him
through the summer, the courts say.
And I get to take care of them while 
their dad works through the day.

Oh, what fun I have planned for us.
I want to go so many places.
We will have fun and play all day
and I will see their happy faces.

I have plans for them to be
able to play just like a kid.
No worries or problems to be found,
all the bad things will be "hid".

I hope this will be their special 
summer; to be in their memory.
This excitement isn't only for
them, it is also for me.
