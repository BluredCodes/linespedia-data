Is there a dark side to us all?
Do we really have a manner in which,
we can change our personalities, 
and, therefore, become a bitch?

I know of many people who can
do this very thing; well.
One minute they are nice, and 
the next minute they're from hell.

What enters their psyche?
To change their state of mind?
Was it something I said?
Or just their need to find?

To find a way to release
that ever present dark side?
Is it in all of us?
But where does it hide?

I hope I don't have it in me.
I guess no one can tell.
Unless the dark side takes over,
and you find yourself in hell.
