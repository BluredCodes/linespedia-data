---
title: "Learning Experience, I Hope"
writer: "katydid194713"

What makes a person always try
to cause hate and discord?
A person who has always made
making his parents fight; his reward.

A person who can't stand the fact
that his parents have stayed together,
even after all the years of his
causing problems and stormy weather.

He has done everything in his power,
everything under the sun,
to make life miserable for them.
But, they are still together, as "one".

He would never acknowledge
how they've pulled him out of a mess.
He's caused his little family pain
by marrying a person who wasn't the best.

They've always been there, 
johnny on the spot.
To take over and help,
whatever problems he's got.

But he never acknowledges
any of the love and care.
He thinks it's all owed to him.
They're supposed to always "be there".

He's rude to his mother,
downright hateful, you could say.
He tells his father stories
different from it's actual way.

And yet, they go on loving him,
he is their darling son.
The product of their love,
when all is said and done.

They will go on protecting,
but now they are learning,
after jeopardizing their retirement future,
they can't satisfy all his yearning.

He's got to live his own life.
He's got to learn to cope.
Life wasn't easy for them either.
But, they never played their parents for dopes.

Let him make his mistakes and learn,
probably from many falls.
But, learn he must, from now on
because they can no longer be at his beckoned call.
