There is another topicI am quite passionate about.I have definite feelings;of this I am quite devout.I am an active supporter,of our 2nd Amendment Right.I back the NRA, Second Amendment Sisters,and anyone else who supports our fight.I am so terribly sorry,for parents of the students slain,at Columbine school and others, but, to me, it's so very plain.It was NOT the guns that killed.It was the poor heartless child.There was some kind of problem,or they wouldn't have gone wild.We need to be more involved.Our children need more love.They need to have more guidance,from us and our Lord, God above.So, protect my Constitutional rights,and protect our children, too.Believe and trust in our Lord, and these things, I think, we can do!
