I have an older sister,
her name is Helen Mae.
Today is pretty special.
It's her 73rd birthday!!

You may not think this is special.
In fact, probably quite plain.
But, I am the youngest of eleven kids,
and every year is an important gain.

I am considered the "baby",
since, this year, I will be 54!
I think we need to realize,
I am NOT a "baby" anymore.

Anyway, I have one more sister.
She will be eighty this year.
She is the oldest of the family.
She needed to be like mother; "the dear"!

If you have figured this out by now,
I think that is really great.
Yes, we had a LOT of brothers.
In fact, there were eight.

We have lost only two of us,
which is a miracle all of its' own.
With the age everyone's getting to be,
I am thankful for God's loan.

He has given each of us a loan,
to live another year.
That is a reason for us all,
to give this a big, "Birthday Cheer"!!

So, Happy Birthday, Helen Mae.
We wish you many more.
Yea, in another year,
we hope to wish you seventy-four!
