A word that strikes fearin every person's heart.Whether you take the doctoror the patient's part.So many kinds of cancerare taking people's lives.It makes no distinction'tween husbands and wives.It took part of my family.Namely, my dear sweet mother.And then another kind ofcancer took my brother.I saw the pain and torturethat my family endured.Through all the supposed'treatment', they were never cured.I hope with early detectionand getting closer to a cure,People can live a betterquality life; longer, for sure.I wish Mom and Brothercould have had a better life.One free from the pain andfrom the surgeon's knife.They are now in a better place.Heaven is the place to be.Our Lord is caring for them.While they wait for me.
