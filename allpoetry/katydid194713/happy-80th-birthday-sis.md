I have an older sister. 
Her name is Lois Irene.
11-09-01 is very special.
To me it is almost a dream.

It's a day of joy,
and a day of fun.
Her 80th birthday in
the year 2 thousand one.

She is our oldest sister.
The one every one counts on.
She has all the answers
about family and times gone.

We thank God for Lois.
Her good health and more.
She is wished many things.
That's what birthdays are for.

It's hard to believe she's 80.
So vibrant and lively is she.
And she is still quite lovely.
Age doesn't change that, you see.

So, HAPPY BIRTHDAY, Lois.
You are wished the best.
We all want to see you for
eighty-one and all the rest.
