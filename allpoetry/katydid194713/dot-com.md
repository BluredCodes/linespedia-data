Have you heard of this new entity?Her name is www Dot Com.She also has a friend,and that one is CD-Rom.I know there are many peoplewho are involved with this term.There is a love affair with them.But, everyone tends to learn.This computer age has overtakenalmost everyone's lives.There's also a DVD, thatinvolves husbands' and wives'.Whether you are male or female,she is so helpful and fun.You can't help but love Dot Com.You'll swear she's the ONLY one!
