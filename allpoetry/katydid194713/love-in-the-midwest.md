It's a boring kind of placecalled the Great Plains or Midwest.There's not much to do here.Life here is kind of a test.Can you endure the winters' cold?Can you endure the summer humidity and heat?Do you ever think about leaving?So you can find a place that's really neat?You don't have any place to compare.Other poets talk of the romantic shore,beautiful seascapes, and sandy beaches.All we have is soy beans, corn, and more.The only thing you find romanticwhen you are quite young,is the person whom you are with.Then the praises of romance were sung.You could take a walk in the rainand laugh when you got all wet.Or, the hay loft was a popularplace, you can surely bet.I guess you don't have to havethe romantic surroundings to love.All you gotta do is be withyour sweetheart and God above.There you have the story.No matter where you are,if you fall in love with the right personit's always like a shooting star.!!
