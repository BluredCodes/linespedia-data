Wow!, I went to a reunion last weekand it was the first I had gone to.I have been out of school 35 years,and thought, 'something different to do'.For some reason, everyone looked older.What an eventful surprise!!It seems as though everyone aged,right before my very eyes.I don't think of myself beingany older than what I feel.And after seeing some of thoseold farts, that's a good deal.We all used to have a lot of funand now we have the right,to just sit around and rememberwhat we 'used' to do at night!!It was an enlightening night,yes, it was very strange.I will have to go again sometime,to see if any more of them changed.
