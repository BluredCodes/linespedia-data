title: "My Husband, My Love"

Many years ago, I found my love.
"They" said, "It wouldn't last".
I wonder what "they" would say now,
because 36 wonderful years have past.

Well, I shouldn't say all wonderful.
There have been "ups" and "downs".
But, that is what love is all about,
the smiles along with the frowns.

I will love him until my dying day.
I made that vow over 36 years ago.
On our first date, I said to myself,
"This is my guy", but didn't let him know.

Eventually,  6 months later,
we were still passionately in love.
We couldn't wait any longer,
so we asked God above.

Shouldn't "we" be together?
Until death do us part?
We took those vows seriously,
straight from the heart.

I still feel giddy when he hugs me,
each morning, every morning;
I get a kiss and a hug and I
feel that old passion warming.

I hope it will always be this way,
I have heard that it can be.
I love him so much it hurts sometimes,
So, I hope "US" can always be "WE".
