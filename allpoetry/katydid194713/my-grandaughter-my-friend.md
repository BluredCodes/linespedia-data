She is only eleven years old.But, she has had so much heartache.I only hope it won't hurt her future, divorce is so hard for her to take.She is my wonderful little friend.When she comes to Gramma's house,She doesn't have to worry about things,She can be a lion or a mouse.She can pretend to be a movie star;She can just stay in p.j.'s all day.She can be herself and have fun,Like a child ought to be able to play.We play together and she likes to help,with housework and things Gramma can't do.She wants to be with Gramma and Grampa;I wish she could live here, too.
