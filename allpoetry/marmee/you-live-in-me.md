---
title: "You Live In Me"
writer: "Marmee"
slug: "you-live-in-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/4961-You-Live-In-Me-by-Marmee"
---

You're now a part of me and

you reside deep within in my heart.

You have planted your seed of love deep

within my soul and I love you so.

 ::~

Your name flows through 

very vein in my body, making

me long for you to be here by my side...

 ::~

You're the air that I breath for it's

your love that warms me through and through

when I feel the chill of missing you.

 ::~

As I sit thinking of you

I vision you handsome face before me,

and your huge eyes looking deep into my mine.

No words need to be spoken between us, for

we're always in tune with each others feelings.

Treating each other with such tender loving care,

respecting each others feelings to the fullest. 

 ::~

You and I together on the same page..

together hand in hand.. .. 

together by each others side.. 

two hearts that beat as one...

We've found our soul mate in one an other 

We've searched for so long and thank god our

search has now ended.. 

for now we have each other .

 ::~

My love you fill my heart

with such love, for it's you

that makes my heart sing

out with such joy and peacefulness.

There is no other in this world,

that can make me

feel the way that you do.

::~

Our love is never

ending and oh so true... 

for now you live in my heart 

just as I live in yours..
