---
title: "The Games People Play"
writer: "Marmee"
slug: "the-games-people-play"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/4989-The-Games-People-Play-by-Marmee"
---

There seems to be a bit of confusion

these days with the games people play.

They say they care,

they say they worry about you

and say they are a friend!!

I wish people would stop playing head games.

If you come knocking at my window and

say you want my friendship .. be prepared there

will be a few rules to be followed .

You Must Be Honest

And..

You Must Be Trustworthy

And..

if you can not be either then I suggest

you just be on your way, just keep a movin on !!!

For there comes a time when we all get tired of

all the little games that people play with our

hearts and with our heads .. do you not agree?
