---
title: "It Felt So Natural"
writer: "Marmee"
slug: "it-felt-so-natural"
source: "AllPoetry"

You  came into my life and

moved my soul to dance~

You awakened me with

 the passing whisper

 of your soft gentle voice and

I felt the beating of your heart 

with the palm of my hand~

You have made the sky

more beautiful to gaze upon

and made me wish upon a star~

Making my heart want to soar 

because it felt 

so right.. 

so natural ..and

so beautiful.. now 

I shell never, be the same~
