title: "**~ Come Soar With Me ~**"
writer: "Marmee"
slug: "-come-soar-with-me-"

Free yourself from all the Pain and hurt that now surrounds you Unleash the chains around your heart Unleash yourself Set yourself free Fly oh so high my handsome bird ~~**Fly high like a graceful bird in the sky Fly free towards me Fly to the one that loves You more then you’will ever know Spread your wings and Come soar with me ~ ~**
