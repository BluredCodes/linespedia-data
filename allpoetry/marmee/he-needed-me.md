He called out my name into the wind ona gray and rainy day ..I heard his voice calling me from a far ..I heard his voice calling me~~'~The bond between us is a specialone as if it weremade of steel Strong to last forever~~'~My Loveneeded the warmth of myvoice, to bring a smile to his face.He needed my cheerful wordsto make his day shinyand bright.. to take awayhis gray rainy day ~~'~He needed Me ..andI needed him..We needed each other~
