Two lovers danced

within a room with no walls.

The moonlight in the horizon

casted a perfect romantic glow .

~`~

The sky was filled 

with millions of twinkling stars

that shimmered and glistened like

precious gems of the night over their heads

A perfect setting for two lovers in love

~`~

He held her tight in his arms and saw

the reflection of the stars in her eyes.

With their souls filled of 

joy and their hearts full of love, the

lovers were lost in the beauty of the night.

Indeed such a romantic site to behold.
