title: "Will You Be The One ?"
writer: "Marmee"

There may be days that I'll come to you feelingsad and my spirits may be down and perhapsthing might not be as you hoped they would be.When I cry will you stand by meand hold me in your arms andtell me everything is going to be ok?Will you give me the needed support and lovethat I may require from you .. when tears roll down my face?Will you keep me focused in theright direction when I feel solost and alone.. always guiding me along the way.My love will you always be the one standing by me ..always loving me ?
