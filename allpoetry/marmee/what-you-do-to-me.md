---
title: "What You Do To Me"
writer: "Marmee"
slug: "what-you-do-to-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

Oh, what you do to me ...

You make the fire deep inside me

burn hot and get me steaming ..

only wanting you to extinguish it~

 ~*~

Oh, what you do to me ...

You make the palms of my hands sweat .

My heart begins to beat faster 

as you stand before me

with those huge gorgeous eyes~ 

 ~*~

Oh, what you do to me ...

I melt when you sit there and just 

stare at me and suddenly I see 

that sexy little smile softly 

come across your tantalizing 

lips and my heart begins to race ...

 ~*~

Oh, what you do to me ...

You can make me soar 

to our cloud in the sky, or 

make me climb to the

top of our mountain high~

 ~*~

With you by my side and

us hand in hand 

there's nothing that we

can not accomplish 

just you and I~

 ~*~

Oh, what you do to me ...

You make all my dreams come true

and for this I thank you, for

now you're the keeper of my heart~

 ~*~

I've have handed you the key, and so

for today and all for all my tomorrows

You're the keeper of my heart ..
