title: "Magical Carpet Ride"
writer: "Marmee"

My friend, look into my eyes,

and tell me what you see.

Do you see deep within my eyes that

you're the one always meant for me. 

Take my hand my friend and

I'll follow you anywhere,

don't you see I care?

~o~

Time spent with you, is like a magic carpet ride.

Hold me tight and never let me go

as we explore the wonders of days gone by 

and our fantasies of tomorrow
