title: "You"
writer: "L.t."
slug: "you"

Tears I cry cause, I can't deny I might be falling in love with you.In fact I think I'm obsessed with you.I close my eyes and I see you.Honestly I would never lie or deny you.I'll always be true to you. I honestly truly like you. I would never wanna make you cry neitherwould I want to make you sad or mad.When you cry I cry.
