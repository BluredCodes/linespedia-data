You've given me everything I've ever wanted,
You loved me, you cared for me,
Though I can't see it in your eyes,
I can feel it in your words,
But how do I know if I truly love you,
I've written it before,
and I've said it but I did'nt know for sure,
At times I feel it,
At times I don't,
They say it's how you feel but,
How do I know I'm feeling the right thing,
