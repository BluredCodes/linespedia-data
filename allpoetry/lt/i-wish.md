---
title: "I wish"
writer: "L.t."
slug: "i-wish"
source: "AllPoetry"

I wish you the love that I open my heart  for.I wish you were the guy I always dreamed of.I wish you were the light that brightens my day.I wish you were the voice that always made me smile.I wish you were the moon that always makes me cry.I wish you were the the stars that bring a twinkle to my eye.I wish you were the music that I sing to.I wish you were the love of my life.I wish you were the one I cared so much about.My wishes have come true you are everything I wished and hoped for.You are the love I open my heart for.You are the guy I always dreamed of.You are the light that brightens my day.You are the voice that always made me smile.You are the moon that always makes me cry.You are the stars that bring a twinkle to my eye.You are the music that I sing to.You are the love of my life.Sorry for my extreme use of the words 'I wish you were' and 'You are'.
