He was sounding the beeps of his nature,
And of the parts of his nature that were deeper than be,
Going back intothe womb of time.
He was mastered by the sheer surging of life,
The tidal wave of being... 
It was a glow and rampant expressing itself in movement,
Flying exultantly under the stars...I found this poem in an old library book I checked out.
I wanted to see if anyone could understand what the author 
was saying in this poem.And what you think about it.
