Lookin' in our eyes
Is like lookin' into a mirror
No longer wearing our hearts on our sleeves
Fallin' deep into our hearts touching the greatness of our souls
We are no longer in control

Lettin' our hearts collide
Our souls fly
Our feelings bring out into view what was hidden
Deep in the core of our flyin' soulsI wrote this poem while watching a movie.
I got caught up in the feelings of the movie and wrote this.
Let me know what you think, also it's not really finished yet.
