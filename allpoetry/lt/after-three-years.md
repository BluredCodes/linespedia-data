---
title: "After Three Years"
writer: "L.t."

I open my eyes and realize that after three years I still cry tears just thinking about you.
After three years my mind still runs wild, my heart continues to race, my lips begin to 
Tremble and my knees always get weak because of you.
You're irresistible to me. My heart melts for you.
I'm crushing, missing, and continuously loving you.
You're very very hard to forget and extremely easy to miss.
It's amazing how I could remember three years of watching you, liking you, and dreaming of you
And still remain with the same deep everlasting feelings for you.Please give me a strict comment on this poem.
I'm thinking about sending this to person it was written about
for Christmas.
