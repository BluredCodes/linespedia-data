---
title: "Would I be wrong"
writer: "L.t."
slug: "would-i-be-wrong"
source: "AllPoetry"

If I told you every other second I think of you would I be wrong?Would I be wrong if I told you my heart sinks deeper than the earth each time I see your gorgeous face.If I told you that I care about you would I be wrong?Would I be wrong if I told you how I feel?Well if I am wrong, sorry because I just did.
