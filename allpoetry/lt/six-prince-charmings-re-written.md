Six Prince Charming’s 

I take a pen in my hand and I will write.
Write about my six prince charming’s.
 The first has dimples, like a newborn baby.
Eyes like the moon, 
And a voice like the whistling wind.
That catches my heart and tosses it,
Into the blue heavens above.
The second gives me care and loves me
Through the keys always giving me 
Precious advice that touches my heart
And soothes my soul,
Like the wind caressing the trees.
The third brings my 
Hurt, anger, and pain 
To understanding words.
The fourth is the one that I love 
Care for, adore and appreciate.
The fifth is sophisticated and sweet,
Mature and neat.
The sixth is the man that I call father,
At times I love him at times I don’t,
There’s nothing more to say he’s nothing more than an average father.
These, these people that I write about are the people I cherish the most.This poem was re-written today within 5 minutes.
