---
title: "I'm In Love"
writer: "L.t."
slug: "im-in-love"

Can you feel it?
Do you hear it?
Can you see it?
I can.
I feel my heart jumpin for joy.
I hear my heart screamin out for more.
I see my eyes light up and my face glow.
It's love that I feel, that I hear, and that I see.
It's brighten up my life and it's changing me.
For the better, for the worst.
