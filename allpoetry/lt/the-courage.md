I have the courage to tell you, your wish is now my command.I have the courage to tell you, I've found a man who sees my real beauty.I have the courage to tell you, I've seen the greater days of loving you.Now I've seen greater days of a man who knows how to love me back.I have the courage to tell you, I don't need you anymore.I have the courage to tell you, I don't want you anymore.I have the courage that you didn't have to tell me you didn't want to be with me.I have the courage to tell you, I was a fool to give you a second chance.I have the courage to tell you, I'm becoming a strong women who does'nt want to play childish games anymore.I have the courage to tell you, Your a free man. Without this young woman annoying you.I now have the strongest courage to let you go.
