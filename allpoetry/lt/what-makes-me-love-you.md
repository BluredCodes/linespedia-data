Is it your eyes?
Or
Is it your lips?
Maybe your slim sexy hips.
That makes me love you.

Is it your broad shoulders?
Underneath that shirt?
Stacks and cross lines
That makes me flirt
And
Makes me love you?

Is it your long legs and baggy pants or 
The myth of your big feet?
When I see you walking down the street.
That makes me love you?

Is it your hard core personality or
Your ghetto mentality?
Forever luring me.
That makes me love you?

It's none of these beautiful things.
It's your untouchable soft vibe.
It's the chills.
It's the tingles that I get when I see your beautiful face.This poem is about the one I love.
Charles
