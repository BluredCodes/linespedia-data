I take a pen into my hand
And I shall write,
Write about my six prince Charming's.
The first had dimples like a newborn baby.
Eyes like the moon and a voice like the whistling of the wind,
That caches my heart and tosses it into the sky blue clouds.
The second gives me care and advice that touches my heart,
And soothes my soul, like the wind blowing through the trees.
The third brings my hurt, anger, and pain to understanding words.
The fourth has ignorance that brings strength and full power to my poems.
The fifth brings adventure, hope and thought to my heart.
The sixth brings reality and laughter to my heart and joy to my soul,
With big dimples.
These are the people I cherish the most.This poem will soon be re-written.
This poem is about (written in order of poem)
Allen Hicks, James Few, Daniel Allen Hester, Charles (the one I now love), Daniel and David.
