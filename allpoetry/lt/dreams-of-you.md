title: "Dreams Of You"

Dreams of you and I in a field of 
sunflowers and daisy's, dandelions and roses.
My heart filled with hopes and dreams of you being there everyday.
For me to see your cute smiling face, with your sexy brown eyes,
and your delicious dimples.
That make my heart burst into billions of pieces.
Me telling the world about you and hoping your doing the same.
Going to school everyday watching you.
Our friends say that were going together but we say were not.
When truly in my heart I felt as if I were going with you for months.
