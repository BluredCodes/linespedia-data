You're all I think of night and day.
The question of being in love with you hangs over my head.
These feelings must be real.
These feelings just won't  go away.
These feelings can't be erased.
I'd love to tell you how much I do love you, but I'm not sure you'll care.
I'd love to be around you but I'm not sure you want to be around me.


Dedicated to Charles a.k.a. ChuckieThis poem was written for Charles.A guy whom I am now in love with.
