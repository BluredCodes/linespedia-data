title: "Sweet Smell Of Flowers"
writer: "L.t."

I get lost in the sweet smell of flowers.
The scent of love.
The scent of joy.
The scent of happiness.
The scent of prayer.
Oh how I love the sweet smell of flowers.
The smell just touches your heart,
Your soul it's so wonderful.
