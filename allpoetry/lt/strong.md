I don't want to run from what I feel.
So I'm facing my fears, tearing down my walls that I built becauce of people like you.
I'm waiting for you to come near, but all you do is ignore me.
I'm sure you no longer will when you see what your missing.
I refuse to give up, I will succeed just you wait and see.
You might think this is phase and it will pass. 
Well your wrong this is a battle I choose to fight, and shall get what I want and feel that I deserve.
No more tears, no more frustration, no more pain, and no more holding back you're getting the real thing.
When your ready come and get what a real woman has to offer.This version of this poem was extremely edited from the original poem I wrote.
