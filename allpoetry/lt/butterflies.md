title: "Butterflies"

The sight of you is creepy.I can hardly breathe.My knees are seconds from being weak.My heart begins to beat faster and faster.Then it speaks to me honey you've got butterflies.My lips begin to tremble.My stomach begins to turn.My blood pressure rises.My body gets numb, I swear I'm having a heart attack.My conscience then tells me baby you've only got butterflies.I can't stop thinking about you.I can't stop talking about you.I can't stop writing about you.I'm afraid to tell you how I truly feel because, I'm honestyly not sure if these feelings are true.I can tell you I'm nearly past these beautiful butterflies.
