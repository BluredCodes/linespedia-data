Did I push too much,
Did I tell it all,
What went wrong,
Please tell me what went wrong,

I told the world about you,
I wore a symbol of my love for you as a bracelet,
reading "I Love Charles" on it,
Did I scare you by wanting a picture

Did I say something wrong,
or is it my looks or my shape,
What went wrong,
Just please tell me what the hell when wrong,

I wrote you letters,
I wrote you poems,
I received nothing from you,
What did I do wrong,
Whatever I did I'm sorry because,
"I love You"
