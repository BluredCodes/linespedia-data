---
title: "I Love You"
writer: "L.t."

If you follow my eyes,
They’re always looking at you,
If you read my lips,
They’re always speaking of you,
Walking passed you,
Sitting near you,

Tears, tears,
Down my face cuz` it’s hard to say,
I love you, to your face,

I want to,
I want to,
I want to tell you to your face,
I love you,
Looking in your eyes seeing the real you inside,

Don’t read my lips this time,
Look deep into my eyes when I say,
I love you,
It’s the honest truth,
I do, I do love you,This poem was written for Charles.
The one that I love.
