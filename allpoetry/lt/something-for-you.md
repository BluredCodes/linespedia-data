---
title: "Something For You"
writer: "L.t."

Rippin' and Runnin' from you.Duckin' and Divin' because I'm scared.Runnin' and Hiddin' from those tingles that I get when I see your gorgeously amazing face.Lookin' and Peekin' in search of your precious face.I would and could say I hated you for receiving those letters that I wrote from my heart.You didn't say a word, you didn't even give me hint whether you read them or not.How could you look so unbelievably cute and seem so horribly rude?I wrote those letters hoping you would respond with some care, but you didn't respond at all.Then you had the nerve to have this girl come up and question me.What's wrong, your weren't man enough to come up and ask me yourself?It's cool you gotta forgive to forget.Besides I don't want to end this just yet.
