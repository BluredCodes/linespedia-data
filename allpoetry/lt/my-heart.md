My heart is weak but it holds so much.
Sometimes more than I can handle.
Sometimes I find my eyes over flowing with tears.
My flesh turns red as I lay in bed.
So many thoughtsand memories run through my mind.
Causin more and more tears to come pouring down like rain.
So many headaches, so much frustration, too much pain, tons of sorrow, and
extreme amouts of anger.
It's so strange one simple thought can trigger all these memories of me and you.
