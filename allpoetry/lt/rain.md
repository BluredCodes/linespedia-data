title: "Rain"

I can't fight it, it's too hard.I'm depressed and your the reason why.Every morning, day, and night is another emotional tragedy.Just thinking of you brings tears to my eyes.It's a shame how I could fall in love with you in just two minutes.But your not even man enough to tell me your not interested.Nevermind sorry.
