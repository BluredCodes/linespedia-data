---
title: "Complete"
writer: "L.t."
slug: "complete"
source: "AllPoetry"

You are the apple of my eye.
The rhythm of the beat of my heart.
The thought of my mind by morning, day, and night.
The secret to my dreams.
The ink to my pen.
Deliciously sweet.
You makes me feel complete.
