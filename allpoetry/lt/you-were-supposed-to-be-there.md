You were supposed to be here to defend me.
You were supposed to be here to protect me.
But you weren’t.

You were supposed to be here to love me.
You were supposed to be here so I can watch you on your wedding day.
But you weren’t.

You were supposed to be here on my sixteenth birthday.
You were supposed to be here to watch me get married.
But you weren’t.

You were suppose to be here when I needed you the most.
But that damn disease hurt you so bad.
Jesus had to take you early.This poem was written for and dedicated to my big sister Adriane.
(R.I.P. Dree- Dree)
