I look apon your eyes so dark and scared,as if it would lose something,so precious and rare.There is no need to think that it will go,because its here for you to love and to hold.The first time I seen you, I knew you were the one. The one I wanted to be withthe one I wanted to hold, to treasure, to love.I knew you were the onethat I would do anything for.And when I heard a song on the radiothat reminded me of youI thought to myselfit cant be true.I can't be falling in love,with someone so sincere,to think that i will be hurt, in the future so near.
