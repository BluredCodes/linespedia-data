The Littleton School Massacre


Everyday was like an ordinary day
Of the Littleton School.
Laughing in the cafeteria
Gossiping within the walls
This safe school did remember it all.
That was true until one day,
Where death was called upon
In an unusual way.
With shooting guns 
In the halls
Screams of terror
The prayer to all.
Two students who wanted 
All to pay
For all the pain,
They have gained.
Bullets on the ground
Bombs set to play
But it did end in pain.
Last two shots
It was the end
However, the killers were gone
With their lives taken in.
This ended in pain 
Of family and friends,
Many injured 
But were saved at the end.
Except 15 people
Who have passed away
Embrace these memories 
Please DO NOT forget this day.

**To all those who have lost someone in such a horrible occurrence**
