---
title: "Wont Last Forever"
writer: "twina"
slug: "wont-last-forever"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/9533-Wont-Last-Forever-by-twina"
---

I lye awake thinking of you,
I can't sleep , there is nothing I can do.
You are in my mind, help me god so true,
I don't sleep at night...
Just day dream of you.
Those sweet lasting memories
That god made come true.
Can't forget the first kiss 
On the midnight road of blue.
My love for you is more stronger then ever...
I can’t help thinking that,
I want this to go forever.
But I know when it comes to the end,
You will have to go away.
And that’s what hurts most,
Not having you here every day.
But I will enjoy every moment with you,
Keep each memory , old and new.
I will keep all the notes that we have written,
Nor throw them away or keep them hidden.
But every moment that we have been through,
Has been a wish, a dream come true.
But forever this wont last,
I know this for a fact,
But please do remember,
I do cherish you forever.
