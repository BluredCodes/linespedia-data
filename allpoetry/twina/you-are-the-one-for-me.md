---
title: "You are the one for me"
writer: "twina"
slug: "you-are-the-one-for-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/12624-You-are-the-one-for-me-by-twina"
---

Days go by,
And months pass away.
People are free to
Come and to go,
But then there is you, 
Who I forever hold.
You are always on my mind,
Your the one I cherish,
With my love so kind.
The one I talk about,
From day to day. 
The one that,
I am so proud of,
That I just have to brag.
You are the one, 
That I think of in the day,
And dream of in the night.
The one I will say to,
That everything will be all right.
And now that we are settled,
In a relationship built for two.
We can trust and love each other,
For what’s to be the future,
For always, we will be together.
