---
title: "The Days of Fall"

As the leaves,Began to fall,Cold came about.Days got shorter,Everything got dull.Fall was here. Ghosts and goblins,Horrors of Halloween,Infect the area,Jumping with screams.Kids with pumpkins,Lacking the respect,Making some trouble,Not reasonable acts. Over the mountains,Peaked through trees,Quivering snow thoughts,Requested by thee.Some time flys,Then one day,Under the sky,Very cold winds,Where flakes went,X-citely to play.Yet to remember,Zealously October days
