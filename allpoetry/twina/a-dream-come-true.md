---
title: "A Dream Come True"
writer: "twina"
slug: "a-dream-come-true"

Star light, Star Bright,
The first star I see tonight.
I wish I may, I wish my might,
To find a guy of such, 
A beautiful sight.

I had a dream,
Just the other night.
Where the love,
Of my life,
Was standing before me,

He smiled, so did I,
I sigh with relief,
Yes....
I have found my guy.

Remembering my dream,
About the other night..
I sigh of the thought,
And wish it would come true.
Some day, some place,
not far from now.
I hope , I dream,
Please come true.

I step out in the garden,
I glance to see..
Oh such a sight..
It was him, 
From my dreams.

He had a rose in one hand,
and a soft kiss for me.
A smile of comfort,
OH can this be?

I wake up,
It was just a dream.
I sigh, 
Wishing..
For it to be reality.
