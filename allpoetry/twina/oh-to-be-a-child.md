---
title: "Oh, to be a Child"
writer: "twina"
slug: "oh-to-be-a-child"

Oh to be a child,
How wonderful It could be..
No worries of life
And how hard it’s going to be.

Oh to be a child,
That can play as if they
Are as rough as a porcupine
But when hurt,
Acts like they are as soft as silk

Oh to be a child,
Who enjoys life as it comes
And have no worries of the future.
Who have dreams of there jobs
But education is no danger.

Oh to be a child,
Where simplest things please them
Like a pot and a pan
This can lead
To hours and hour and hours of fun….

Oh to be a child,
Is the greatest thing to be
Has no stress…
No pressure
No complications in life

Be careful, very careful
Do not let the children
Fade away..
They are Gods Angels
Loving, caring..
Of course has trust
That has not been taken away.

Oh to be a child,
Oh to be a child,
How wonderful it would be
To be a child.
