---
title: "Blind Love"
writer: "twina"
slug: "blind-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/12340-Blind-Love-by-twina"
---

You think I don't care,
But you are always on my mind,
where ever I go, what ever I do,
I can't stop thinking of you.
even if its been a day,
that I haven't seen you ,
I will miss you even more,
by the second, by the minute, by the hour.
Don't say I don't care,
Because I really do.
If only you knew,
how much you meant to me,
but the way you act,
you must not see.
