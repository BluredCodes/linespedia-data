---
title: "Memories of You"
writer: "twina"
slug: "memories-of-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/9392-Memories-of-You-by-twina"

The time that has gone byFrom being with you,Has been such a great experienceFull of memories that can't be true.Thoughts of airplanesFlying through the summer sky,Lying in the grassAs the days go by.The fragrance of flowersThat smells sweet to me,As we walk through the forestHolding hands, just you and me.Cuddling on the couchWatching a good ol movieOr out for coffeeWas always fine by me.And now that 4 months has gone byWhich may not seem like very longBut I am very happy to be with youAnd I hope it goes on
