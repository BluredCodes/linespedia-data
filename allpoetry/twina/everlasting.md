---
title: "Everlasting"
writer: "twina"
slug: "everlasting"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

What do I do without you,
Going along, day to day.
It feels like its been forever...
Since the last time we were together.
Together was right,
It sure felt great, 
Knowing we loved eachother.
But that love we had,
I thought it would last.
But I was so wrong,
To say it was over.
Im trying to move on,
trying to get strong.
But its just not working,
Without you here holding me..
Holding me so close and tightly.
Without you saying,
Saying those words,
Of love.  
Ive made some mistakes,
I know it was wrong.
I just wanna forget it,
I don't wanna think about the past,
Don't even wanna hear it.
Its just pain,
Bottled all up,
Waiting for the moment.
The moment that u find out,
That I have hurt you in so many ways.
But what can I do,
Its all over now
Me and you will no longer,
have each other. 
But time is patience.
And patience is love.
Love is ever lasting.
