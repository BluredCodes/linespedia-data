title: "The Evilness of Nature"
writer: "twina"

I stand upon a porch
That is cold with fear.
I see the rain falling
Like an open tear.

I see the lightning
I hear the crack of thunder
When will this stop
I really do wonder.

I am the spider
Who crawled up the spout
Who prayed for sun
To shine all and about.

As time goes by
Everyone knows…
That the sun will come out
And the river will no longer flow.
