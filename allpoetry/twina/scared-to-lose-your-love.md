How can love be so miserablewhen I have someoneso sexy and irresistible. Your smile makesme glowwith happiness inside.But if we ever broke upI wouldn't know what to dowith a broken heartthat would be in two.As long as you are happywhich I know you will bethen we will be together for eternity.
