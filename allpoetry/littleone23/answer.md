Food is not the answer
You cannot eat your problems away
They just grow
As do you

Pain is all that you know
Why do you cause this?
Do you know?

It all comes from inside
Throw it up
The pain will go with the food
Don't worry
It only burns for a while

Stop thinking that way
It's just more pain
Can you handle it?

You think you can
But the taste
The sour taste it leaves
After you're heaved your life away

Don't over eat
But don't under eat
Either way you'll gain
Problems mounting with each breath

Death
Of course,
That's it!
The answer

No,
It can't be
But the insomnia swells
Your heart yells
for an end
"Please! Please! make it stop!"

You poor little girl
You're just a child growing
But it's never showing
The pain and strain life wears on you

The gears in your mind
Turning and churning
Stopping to rust
Just to be revived

Don't ask
Don't tell

What they don't know
Can't hurt them
But, it can hurt you
So, stand and scream
Don't bear this burden any longer
At the top of your lungs ask:
"WHAT'S THE ANSWER?!"

Wait for a reply,
I doubt you will hear one
Waiting, for the answer
You'll die1-7-01,
