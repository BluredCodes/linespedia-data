The feelings 
and vibes I felt from you
all seem to have been a lie
You're still with her
I'm still just a poor snowball
melting
waiting to be put in a freezer
to be found by your cool hands
And be cradled
And loved
but I'm just a snowball
waiting and melting my feelings away
the next time I see those cool grey eyes of yours
I'll become solid
And the feelings will be back in full
For now I'm waiting and melting
till the next beautiful time for your cool grey eyes to meet my honey brown 
   eyes
Maybe oneday
your lips will find mine
And I'll be in a freezer with you
