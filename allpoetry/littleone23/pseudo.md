Crying inside
Purging thoughts
Psuedoeuphoria
Make me feel so good
Take this shit from me
Throw out all the fucking pain
Spit it in their face
Clever, Clever, Clever
Little girl
Kills herself every night
Doesn't let her soul show
Hides it down inside of her pseudolife
Feeling cheap 
And even dirt
Scrubs her soul right away
Forgetting who I am
Never knowing who told her what to do
Silly, Silly, Silly
Little girl
With her pseudolife
