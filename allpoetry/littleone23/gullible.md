I always wanted to be loved
Only truths 
No lies
I've been liked
and lusted after
It's hard to believe 
I've ever been loved
I'm so gullible
and seriously niave
That I'll believe
Any lie you tell me
Your eyes seem so true
and it makes me really want you
But I know I can't have you
and I sure as hell can't tell you
How I feel
When I knew
I like you
