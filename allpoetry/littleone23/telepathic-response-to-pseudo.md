You tell me to get rid of the pseudo
To make it all real
But how can it be real
If that's not how I feel?
The pain inside
The pain outside
I don't know what's right
I want to feel the way I should
But the only other thing I can be is numb
Ongoing headache
Since the day I found out
I know what can alleviate it
But that will only hurt me more
Because then I'll have to deal12-15-01,
