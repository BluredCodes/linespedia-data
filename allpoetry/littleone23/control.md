I can't control the way I feel
It's tearing at my insides
I can't tell you cause I don't even know
I want to be your friend
I don't want to love you
But I do
I can't control the way I feel
I wish I could
These feelings hurt so bad
I can't tell anyone how I feel
They'd kill me
I can't control the way I feel
Trust me I want to
If I could I wouldn't love you
I'd stop thinking about us and why we went wrong
I can't control the way I feel12-7-00,
