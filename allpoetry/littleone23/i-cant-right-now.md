"I can't right now."
What dost thou mean
You find that I like you
And your reply is
"I can't right now."?!
Your words
And actions
Spin through my mind
That look on your face
When you sat back with her
And looked into my eyes
What did you mean?
Was that pity?
Remorse?
Mutual feelings?
I cannot see
Through this cloud of shame that has been placed between us
All too strange
Confusion streams through my veins
Fluttering trips in my heart
Shall I know at lunch?
Or will I wait forever?
All I know is
"I can't right now."
