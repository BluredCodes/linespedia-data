It felt so wonderful to kiss you
To be in your arms
I can still feel you lips on mine
Our tounges pressing together
Sparks flew 
I think oceans even crashed
But that one night
Was the only magic we had
You took it all away
I don't know
Exactly when I lost you
But is was before you said 'We're through'
Saddness had now eclipsed my being
I am only a shadow of painThis is for daniel, my most recent ex
