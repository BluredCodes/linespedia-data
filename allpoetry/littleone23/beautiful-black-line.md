What's this?
A plain, black line on my paper
My paper now filling with words
As they all flow from my head
This line
so simple,
May I ask thee
"How can you be?"
I don't understand it
I really don't mind
I don't see why you're there
Ya see?
Oh, wonderous
Plain, black line
You fill my pen with joy
With each passing line
Each line that I write
About you, plain black line
You're so simple and complex
You fill my head with near rhyme
Closer and closer you draw my mind
Beautiful, black lineI wrote this in Biology when there was the smell of preservation fluids (I don't know if they still use fromaldahyde still or not) all around me and I picked up a piece of paper out of my binder and it had a black line on it. I'm not quite sure which one inspired me, but I wrote about the line.
