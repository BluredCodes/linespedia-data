I don't know what to say
I don't know what to do
Because I really like you
I can't help it
And you know I do
Crazy thing is
I really don't know what to do
Or what to say
But what is there to say that we haven't already said?
There's nothing at all for me to say
It's all been left up to you
There are only a few things you can say
You can say you want me
You can say you don't
What to do.......What to do
Who knew that I'd like you?
You and your flourescent orangeness
Did you know orange was my favorite colour even before I met you?
