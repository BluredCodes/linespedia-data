Deep is this hell
Stuck in a shell
Strange and cold
Pain aches through my being
Modified trust
To all who ask to care
Everything spins around in circles in my head
Tornado in my brain
The aftermath
Distructed disbelief
Things were once so simple
Long ago
It didn't matter
But now
Modified trust is all that can be offered
All have been spurned
All cannot trust with true hearts
Strange that not all can see
What has come to be
Strange that we fear
What we all know is here1-2-02,
