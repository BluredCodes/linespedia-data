When I think of the way it really is
Tears fill my eyes
And I begin to weep
When I face reality
I run to the comfort of a pill
It takes me away to a better place
Where everything is alright
But when I start to come down
The pain comes flying forward
Dragging me down
Down to hell
This place I call home
Straight and sober
Pain and sorrow
Killing me
Soul fading away
Losing more every day
I feel so dirty and ashamed
I go home and scrub my morals every night
Wanting to be clean
Wanting to be free
But only one can be
Clean and sad 
Or dirty and glad
What's so wrong with wanting to be happy?12-06-01,
