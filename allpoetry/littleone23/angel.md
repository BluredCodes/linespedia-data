title: "Angel"

Life bestows upon my breast
A fear of all that lies
All that pains
And stands behind
While the fearful take their blows
Undoubtably
Unknown to all
The pain inside my hand
None knows the true nature of any
Above
Below
Forever
Never give up
Never let go
It will prevail
You, I, shall win
