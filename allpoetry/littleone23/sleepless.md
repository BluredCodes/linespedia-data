Tired, tired
Screaming mad
Ranting
Raving
Back and forth
Smiling, smiling
crazy in my head
Sleep beckons unto me
but my reply must stay
"No"
I cannot give in to my retreat
I will sleep some later
Maybe the maddness shall subside
But very unlikely to this one
My maddness is rising
Dreams fill my head
My eyes tire and strain to close
The need and want for sleep tingles through my veins
I must go for I am weak with fatigue
