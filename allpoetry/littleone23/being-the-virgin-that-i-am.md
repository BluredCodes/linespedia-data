I feel so stupid
Being the virgin that I am
Sometimes I feel hopeless
Like I'll always be the last
Last to know
Last to tell
Last to experience
Being the virgin that I am
So many around me
If only life could contian stamps
I feel partially ashamed
Almost useless
Chastity forcefield partially in my mind
Strange to say
I almost want it that way
Being the virgin that I am
Cheerful
Yet rather insecure
Everyone around me has found one to embrace
To give the gift
Never cherished so
Being pure and clean makes me itch and feel dirty all over
Damn this being the virgin that I am
Crazy
Horny
Virgin
Waiting for love
To be set free
Wanting to find it soon
Soon to go mad
Am I undesirable?
I feel so
Being the virgin that I am
