Oh Dearest Lust
it tis a sin to want THEE
But it TIS a greater Sin 
to DENY my Heart and my LibidoI was looking at the grey blue sky yesterday while i was in the car and it reminded me of the one I am currently lusting after (He has amazing grey blue eyes)
