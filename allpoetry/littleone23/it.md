I can't describe this feeling of utter confusion
So, I'll just talk about it

You,
You have no idea how you twist me up
You could never know the sheer joy in spotting your orangeness
You could never see the truth in my eyes
Because you won't look
You don't know how much I hate you
The way you flirt your lure at me and the second I bite you reel away to 
  other fish
Is that #1 or #2 I wonder?
You have no clue as to just how beautiful your eyes are
You have no way of seeing the truth in my eyes
You never look
You will never know any of this
