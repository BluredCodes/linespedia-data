Insanity! Insanity!
I wish to scream
But my vocal chords have betrayed me
They speak not when I want them to
And say the wrong things
My heart wants to die
This aching is breaking my soul
I feel like dying
But all I can manage is crying
The knife in the drawer
The pills on my desk
The rope in the garage
The poisions under my sink
The guns in the cabinet
The car in the drive
All call out to me
"Here! Here! We can help you!"
The heart in my head
The brain in my chest
All this is backwards
Just like the rest
I want to fix it
My life is such a chore
The pain and burning
The saddness and crying
The joy and laughter
Have all but escaped
Numbness
Pain
Happiness
I want none
None but a dagger dragging my heart12-11-01,
