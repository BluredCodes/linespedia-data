So low and insecure
Backwards rhyming 
Chiming in my head
Torture! Torture!
I call to thee
But thou doest not answer to my plea
I ask for love
I ask for joy
But all you throw at me is unjust cruelty
Insanity 
I once said
to the chiming in my head
Loathing and pure hatred I did fear
For there is none to keep me near
Hold me as I weep
I ask of thee once more
And yet again, no reply
Seeming hopelss
I'm now sure
Sure that all this backwards rhyming chiming in my head is to keep me safe
Safe from death
Safe from life
Safe from all emotion
Pity, I do not ask of thee
I ask the one last time,
Wilt thou love me?
