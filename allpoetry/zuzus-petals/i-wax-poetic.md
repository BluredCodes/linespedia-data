I wax poeticIn times I dig beneath the snowAnd kiss The frozen earth;To caress the frostThat turns to dewThen back to frost.I wax poeticIn times I run my hands Just aboveThe stillest watersAnd feel It quiver in my wake;To see it trembleAnd settle Smooth and dark.I wax poeticIn times I shed tearsFor a museAnd strain To hear The softest voicesAmong chaos;To graspThe slightest Hint of hopeIn visions That taunt my soul.I wax poeticIn times I trap myselfIn a struggle To destroy And create;To foldBack brittle paperAnd start Once more at the beginningA new pageEmpty and inviting.“…I wax poetic.”um there isn't much to say about this one.  I wrote about having these poetic thoughts that I cannot convey in words (if that makes sense).   -zuzu
