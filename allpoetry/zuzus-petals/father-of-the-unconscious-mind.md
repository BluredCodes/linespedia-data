Father of the unconscious mind
Take my hands
And walk with me down under.
Reach inside my sleeping head;
Make logic of my world.
Be there when I wake
To comfort me;
To take the blame
When reality just sounds
Too random.
Then, we can 
Walk down under yet again
And beneath my trembling lids
You can shut the doors
That once stood wide open.
