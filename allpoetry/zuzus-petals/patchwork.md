The crevices in your heart
Are dried at the edges
And each gaping hole
Is held together
By thin threads.

I’ve tried for so long
To follow the map
Said to lead to 
The softer parts of you,
But, I have yet to find them.

Perhaps my own heart
Is too much 
Of a patchwork of seams
And mismatched fabric
To understand
Your threadbare soul. 

I’ve tried to deceive you,
To convince your tattered spirit  
That I am whole,
That you are the only one 
With stitches.

But, you too
Have found a map,
And now,
I am just as exposed;
Just as vulnerable
To the elements.hi...please read me-zuzu
