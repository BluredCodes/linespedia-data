You build me up then pull me piece by piece apart again.
I’m a jumble, a mess, a child’s play thing.
Keep me in Rubbermaid; take me out on a whim.
I’m a weakened structure. 
You take joy in leveling me with your sticky hands,
In putting together my mismatched plastic body.
How big does it make you feel? —You’re in control now.
I can be anything you want me to be.
Build anything out of my elements;
A rocket that will always be grounded,
A tower although weak, higher than any other,
A racecar with square-wheels that will never go. 
Throw me to the floor, take it out on me.
Discard me, spread me around, and leave me incomplete.
See my brightness, the colors I wear for you.
Touch me, my ‘perfection’, and wish for something more.um--yeah--I'm in a bad mood... not the finest I admit-zuzu
