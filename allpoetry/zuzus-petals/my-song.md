I wrote my song with phrases I heard from you.
Borrowed from bits and pieces of your many melodies.
I found them hidden away, deep inside boxes cloaked in cloth,
Scribbled on scraps of paper.
I can almost etch upon my own mind those same notes
And beat upon my own wrists those same rhythms.
I absentmindedly replay those same tunes over and over 
With streams steadily flowing from the tips of my fingers.
My song breathes the breath of your life
And waits for your touch to be inspired;
