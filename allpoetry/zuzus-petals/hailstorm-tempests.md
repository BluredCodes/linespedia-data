I am 
Inches away from submersion;
Seconds from tasting
Splintering hailstorm tempests
Which I know will
Tear me to bits.
I crave to be put back together again
Like every other time
When ice tore flesh
And I was left broken 
Wishing to be whole
Once more
Walking down the same sunken path 
Towards a blizzard that warms my soul.not much to say
