Don’t tell him his soul isn’t cryingThat his heart doesn’t acheHe sends himself on a guilt tripRecalling each last mistakeChorus: Like a lost child to fireOr a moth to a flameFind the path out of exile Looking for someone to blameTime to shed all the dignityReturn home from graceLike the noise after silenceOr the infinity of spaceHis life, like a two toned photoEach print like the one beforeRipped and torn at the edgesMistreated and ignoredChorus: Like a lost child to fireOr a moth to a flameFind the path out of exile Looking for someone to blameTime to shed all the dignityReturn home from graceLike the noise after silenceOr the infinity of spaceHe counts every reflectionBetween him and his coreHe dares to dig deeperAs if searching for moreChorus: Like a lost child to fireOr a moth to a flameFind the path out of exile Looking for someone to blameTime to shed all the dignityReturn home from graceLike the noise after silenceOr the infinity of space* Like a lost child to fire  Or the noise after silence  Find the path out of exile   And return home from graceI was in class one day and I came up with the chorus and worked from there... I think that I need one more verse...It might sound funny just to read it out like a poem (quite funny in fact) but I've been fooling around with a few tunes and it sounds pretty good put to music. So what do you think?-ZUZU
