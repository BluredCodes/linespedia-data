Sometimes I wish
That if I had a cross,
You’d burn it
And hold it high.
The light would echo
Off the trees at night,
Leaving me
To discard the ashes
Of a relic 
I’ve never really held
(Nor cared to).

Sometimes I wish
That I could see you
With eyes untainted,
To prove to myself
That if I could love this “emptiness”,
That you could find
Even more for me to hate.

Sometimes I wish, 
That I could return to myself
And cry once more,
So I know 
That I cry not for what I see,
But for who
And what remains no longer.This in NO way is anti-religious...but anti-hate...it says that sometimes I wish that I could see the world "with eyes untainted" so that I know why I believe what I do -ZUZU
