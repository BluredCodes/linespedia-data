It’s time to take 
Your rose-flavored medication;
Lift your rag doll head
And show me once again
That your eyes 
Are part coal and part diamonds.
The words you don’t speak
Mean the world to me,
I only need to hear them once
To know I am the weaker one.
But, if you say 
I live my life in the past tense
Then how come I know,
That you’ll be drunk forever?My favorite line in the poem is '...your eyes Are part coal and part diamonds.' and I didn't know what to name the poem...so I kind of just took my favorite part and used it as the title hehe.
