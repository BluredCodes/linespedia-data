I’ve never seen a muse
With destruction on her mind,
But, as smooth as the summer’s coolest night, 
Still steamy, but softly calm;
I break from the extreme.
Now, my throat is dry with silence,
Parched for a breath
Only I can summon rarely.
The humidity once filled me
With temporary satisfaction,
But now, the thought of being breathless
Nearly knocks the wind out of me.like I said, I've never met inspiration with the thought of my destruction, but so many times creation = destruction (my other poem KARMA is about that too).
