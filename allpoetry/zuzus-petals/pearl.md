As tiny opaque pearls
Blush and break
And blow away gently:

Blow away,
Brush aside
Your wandering glint
Of cool opalescent light.

Entranced, the spheres
Roll and bounce
And beat down lightly:

Beat down,
Fall to the ground
And find yourself
Alone, where you are left to rest.

Beg me,
Yell and Scream
And call to me frantically:

Call to me,
Beckon my touch
And help me
Piece you back together.

Breathe me in,
Inhale and smell 
And sense my weakness:

Sense me,
Taste my sweetness,
And once again melt
Like sugar with my touch.

Blush and break
Beat down lightly
And beg me once more
