All I am is a symbol, an icon of human existence.I am here to be everyone summarized.What I suggest is so weak, but I really wouldn’t know.I’m not the one to ask.I am just an icon and in brief yourself and everyone else.My only purpose is to be vague.I am not one but a race compressed into a single being.I am small, a representation of culture.All I am is a symbol, an icon of human existence.I am everyone but myself.But then again, I’m not the one to ask.
