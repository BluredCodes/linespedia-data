Soft intricate patterns
fall like ivy 
In the afternoon.
The delicate shadows
Greet my hands
As they anxiously
Creep up the windowpane;
An attempt 
To count each raindrop
As it reaches it's end.
I myself like liquid
Pouring gently 
From one thought to another,
Flying from sky to earth
In one blur of midday light
And with a clap of thunder
I stream back to actuallity
Only to find I have lost count...This is one of my first poems and it always seems so soothing to me
