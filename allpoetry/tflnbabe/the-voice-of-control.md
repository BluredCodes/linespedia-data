---
title: "the voice of control"
writer: "Tflnbabe"
slug: "the-voice-of-control"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

my voice seems to have a mind of it's own
words cross my lips before i can think about them
before i can stop them
encouraging your ravaging of my body 
all the while my mind is screaming no
as i feel like a prisoner in my own body
unable to control my reactions
unable to control my words
unable to control anything
especially you
