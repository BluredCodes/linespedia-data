he used to mark me 
pressing bruising kisses to my pale neck
as if making sure that no other man could look at me without realizing i had been claimed

he used to blow rings of smoke into my eyes
the smoke seeping into my skin and nostrils
making me crave what i knew i shouldn't want

he took my virginity
roughly banging his bony hips into my tender thighs
as if he went hard enough and fast enough i'd enjoy it


all that's left is nicotene and emotional scars
but when i look in the mirror the physical is still there
and though i don't want it
i miss the possession
i'm still marked
