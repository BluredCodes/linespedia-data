title: "glimpses"

i'm only allowed brief glimpses of happiness
never a full drink from it's cup
glimpses of the possiblity of love 
glimpses of life without disease
glimpses of what it's like not to be lonely
glimpses what it's like not to worry all day
glimpses of what it's like to have emotions
they flee away leaving me again the unfeeling object
grasping in the air for some hope of another glimpse of life
