my heart turned itself off today
maybe it shut down long ago
but the wall was noticed today
as striving to feel even of a sliver of what i used to
i let him touch me with rough fingers
straining to find some emotion so that the pain would be worth it
i let him drool on me under the pretense of a kiss
thinking i felt a small twitch of something 
i let him do with me what he will
maybe if he pounds deep and hard enough
something will break the wall
