title: "the smell of you"

the smell of you that lingers on my body is mixed with the smell of sex
both making me want to vomit
maybe if i scrub hard enough the smell will disappear
and i'll feel like less of a whore
even if i'm fooling myself
anything to hide the truth from those around me
even if i can't hide from myself
as i walk down the hall
i know they can smell it from a mile away
judging me though they've all felt the same before
to make themselves feel better about what they've done and the mistakes they've madeInspired by 'Wash you off in the Shower' by NGurl911. work in progress
