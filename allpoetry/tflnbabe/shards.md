the pieces of my life are falling into place as they fall apart
the lost loves...the once thought loves...the never loved at alls.
each shiny shard of glass glinting to attract my reflection 
that i would mull over the tiny flaws in each and the mistakes that broke everything
in the first place
embedding themselves into my hands
into my skin
into my mind
permanent reminders of the past that was, that could have been, that will never be.first draft written in a few minutes
