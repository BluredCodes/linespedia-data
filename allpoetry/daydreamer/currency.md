With this wide world of bluried motion
Comes within, a satin devotion.
One step up equals no promotion,
But two steps back is one demotion.

----------------------------------

Screaming with a fluid voice...
This will be your final choice... 
Take a long and looking glance...
For this will be your final chance...

----------------------------------

Worlds of victims, slaves and more,
But we shall settle out the score,
And what is left; what is it for?
The beastly eyes in days of yore...When I wrote it, I felt some connection to everything that's been going on. Whaddaya think?
