If I could dreama Wild Dream,I'd split a heart,But mend the seam. If I could growfrom Head to Toe,a Beauty that wouldNever slow....If she would seeIt had to beThat she would endUp next to me...If I could fly,Or live or die,For her it's doneWith a sparkle in my eye.If the snowWould often slow,So I could figureWhat I know.If I could twirl,Or swish or swril,It'd all be done,Just for the girl.I think this poem is Kind of Juvenile....
