title: "I'm Confused!"

I'm so confused,
I feel that I've bruised,
I don't know what to do next.

To choose her or not,
To Decide on the Spot,
I really feel like I've been hexed.

All the possibilities,
Mixed with all tranquilities,
The pain that I feel won't decrease.

Me with no pride,
But with her by my side,
My anguish will finally cease.

Eyes of a butterfly,
And her with a howling cry,
I'm not sure to die or to live.

I want her with me, 
And I want it to be,
FOr me and for her to give.

With waves of emotion,
Entrappment, Devotion,
I leave and for you two I miss.

I might say good-bye,
Or break down and cry,
SO I leave you with one fatal kiss....Nothin...
