I am…

Lonely beyond my wildest nightmare…
Tired past the faintest breeze…
Paying the price for paying too less…
Too low, to be near the trees…

I am…

Scared of what I will become…
Scared of what I won’t…
Scared of if it's "Do or Die"...
Scared of if I don’t… 

I am…

Falling back on no hind legs…
With no one there to catch…  
Starting fires with one spark…
But stranded, with no match…

I am…

Closing hearts behind ice doors,
Fighting with the dawn…
“I am” is close, “I am” is past…
I am… I am… I’m gone…Yeah...
