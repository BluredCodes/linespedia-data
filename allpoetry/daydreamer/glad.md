She's feeling bad today.
And I'm glad.
She's finally feeling pain!
She's finally feeling what she put ME through!
I'm glad she's feeling all of this.
Her selfishness is coming back at her.
She's suffering, and I'm glad.
It's like her dream.
I wouldn't touch her.
Reality.
I'm glad.
And the other one.
I'm glad she cuts.
I'm glad she sometimes has pain.
I'm glad that both of them feel physical and emotional pain.
I'm glad.
They need to feel what I feel. 
They need to experience MY pain!
Her with her "anguish" and selfishness...
And the other with her "boredum"...
They need pain.
Because maybe then they can be glad.
Like me.
I'm glad.Despite the Soft Sounding title, this is actually a VERY angry poem. Does "Angst" mean Angry?
