Deep in sleep, I heard him say...
"You're to die - this ho8ur, this day!"
And through a tear, I heard my cry:
"You cannot take me! I cannot die!"

I did not commit a crime!
I can't be killed without reason, or rhyme!
Give me just a litle time,
to blow the air into a chime!

"No, you're gone," he sol'mly said,
And in a minute I was dead.
I left my body, not looking back,
And through my viens, the blood ran black.

As my self gets left behind,
Nothing left but soul n' mind,
And maybe someday I can find
the light that always glowed and shined.

I see a bright light high above...
But I'm thrown back, with forcefull shove.
And then I wonder, feeling shod,
"Was that Death, or was it God?"Just somethin... I wrote this.. um.. beginning of school. So.. yeah.
