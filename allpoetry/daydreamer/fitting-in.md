Fitting in
is like a sin.
It's over before
it can begin.

Get picked last
is in the past;
But you can't get
there very fast.

Breathe in air
of the fair
ones with the
Sparkles in their hair.

Ace to Spade,
You've got it made,
Pulling down
the Darkened Shade.

Do not shout
or Cry or Pout;
but Fitting In
