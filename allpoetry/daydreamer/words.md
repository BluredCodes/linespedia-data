Tiny writing as I see.
Who will come next after me?
Inspirating inspiration,
Waiting for an invitation.
I try to be all that I can,
Less than a God, but more than a man.
no one told me what I was to inherit.
They just handed it over, and told me to bare it.
How can they give me such an altered place?
They lied to me! Right to my face!
What started as happy all turned to rage.
I think that it's time now, that I turn the page.....Not very good - just felt like sharing.
