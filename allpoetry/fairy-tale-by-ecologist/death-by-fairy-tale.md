---
title: "Death by Fairy tale"
writer: "Fairy tale by ecologist"
slug: "death-by-fairy-tale"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/10444-Death-by-Fairy-tale-by-ecologist"
---

Night fell      came down crashing,      upon my head      leaving me crowned      with bloody ribbons      crucified, crowned with thorns      Christlike, for the sake of Henny Penny      under       ebon shores      While Angel tears      drowned      my pet chicken,       Humpty Dumpty,      and       Goldilocks and those Damned Bears.      No more fairy tales or fables      they hurt too much.
