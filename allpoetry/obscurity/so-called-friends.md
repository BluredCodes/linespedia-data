WHAT THE FUCKS WITH YOU
YOU ONLY CALL ME WHEN IN NEED
OUR FRIENDSHIP AINT WHAT IT USED TO BE
TRUST SEEMS LIKE A MEANINGLESS STUPID WORD
YOU JUST DON’T SEE THE MORAL OF IT ALL

YOU USED ME ONCE
I DIDN’T CARE
YOU USED ME TWICE
AND I STARTED TO GET SCARED
I THINK ITS ENOUGH
BUT IT KEEPS COMING ON AND ON
THIS IS THE END
I THOUGHT YOU WERE MY FRIEND

WHY LOOK AWAY
I SAW YOU ON THE STREET YOU TURNED YOUR HEAD
JUST LIKE A CURSE YOU JUST DON’T QUIT
LOOKS LIKE THE END OF WHAT USED TO BE
I’LL SAY IT ONCE DON’T FUCK WITH ME

YOU USED ME ONCE
I DIDN’T CARE
YOU USED ME TWICE
AND I STARTED TO GET SCARED
I THINK ITS ENOUGH
BUT IT KEEPS COMING ON AND ON
THIS IS THE END
I THOUGHT YOU WERE MY FRIEND
YOU USED ME ONCE, I DID NOT CARE
THE TRUST I GAVE, IT’S JUST NOT FAIR
YOU’VE MADE ME BLEED, I CANNOT HEAL
THIS WOUND SO DEEP, YOU CAN NOT FEEL
THE MOON SO FULL, I CAN’T HOLD BACK
GET READY TO PAY, THIS ONE WAY TRACK
THIS TRACK IS NEAR, THIS TRACK YOU’LL FEAR
THE END IS HERE, SO DON’T FUCK WITH ME

DON’T FUCK WITH ME
