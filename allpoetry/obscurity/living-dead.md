---
title: "Living dead"
writer: "obscurity"

COME FOLLOW ME WHILE I GO HIDE AWAY
MAKE SURE TO STAY CLOSE BEHIND
MY HEART IS BEATING OUTTA TIME
YET I DON’ WANT TO LEAVE THIS PLACE
STANDING ON THE STAIRS DARK COLD MORNING 
NO SLEEP FOR YOU FEAR THE END
NO REST FOR NO ONE AS YOU SAY
“THEY’LL TAKE ME WITH ‘EM”

DREAM OF DEAD 
WAKE UP DEAD
TO A WORLD THAT DOESN’T EXIST  HERE (GOD WAKE ME)
IMAGE YOU FEAR 
IMAGE IS CLEAR
(TAKE MY HAND)

WERE OFF AGAIN TO FACE EM AGAIN
TUBE LIGHT FREIGHT TRAIN NOT THIS DREAM AGAIN
A CHASE I’M ALONE SWALLOW ME WHOLE
COME WITH ME LETS GO HOME
GET UP STOP THIS SHIT YOUR DEAD, GONE AND YOU HAUNT ME
GOD HELP ME 
SAVE ME
KILL ME

DREAM OF DEAD 
WAKE UP DEAD
TO A WORLD THAT DOESN’T EXIST  HERE (GOD WAKE ME)
IMAGE YOU FEAR 
IMAGE IS CLEAR
(TAKE MY HAND)

WILL YOU SURVIVE THIS FUCKING NIGHT
OR BE A VICTIM LIKE OTHERS
SHE APPEARS IN MY SIGHT AND READS ME MY RIGHTS
NOW I’M A SLAVE TO THE UNDEAD
SHE SMILES BACK AT ME WITH HER BLOODY TEETH
IM SO COLD AND WEAK HEAVY EYES THAT COULD SLEEPS
IS THIS A DREAM OR BUT A WARNING
IF I SEE YOU THERE NO MORNING GLORY
