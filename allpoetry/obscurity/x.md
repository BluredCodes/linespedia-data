YOU’RE THE ONE WHO SENT ME HERE
YOU’RE THE ONE I WISH WOULD DISAPPEAR 
SORRY FUCK I DIDN’T FAIL 
THROUGH MY HANDS THESE RUSTED NAILS

CAN YOU FEEL THIS PAIN I FEEL INSIDE
NO MORE FAKE TEARS YOU’RE EYES WILL CRY
SO I CLOTHE ME IN MY OWN DISGUISE
JUST TO LOOK BEYOND YOU’RE GOD DAMN LIES

IT’S THE PRICE THAT’S PAID IN THE END
WHAT WAS ONCE A BEST FRIEND
WHEN I LOOK AT YOU’RE EYES
I CAN NOW REALIZE

CAN YOU FEEL THIS PAIN I FEEL INSIDE
NO MORE FAKE TEARS YOU’RE EYES WILL CRY
SO I CLOTHE ME IN MY OWN DISGUISE
JUST TO LOOK BEYOND YOU’RE GOD DAMN LIES

BUT IT’S ALWAYS MY FAULT
AND I’M ALWAYS AT FAULT
CUZ IT’S ALWAYS MY FAULT
