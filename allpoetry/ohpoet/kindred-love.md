---
title: "Kindred love"
writer: "Ohpoet"

A kindred love... two souls beneath the sun!-so if you ever spread your wings and fly,my love for you and the tears that I'd crywould never end! How could I leave behindthe peace now in my heart?- a special kindof love! I can't quit what we have begun...forget your touch... your smell... that we are one...become a quitter and lose this fight? Fie!How can I find if I make myself blinda kindred love... two souls beneath the sun!I promised like some god,'My love won't die!'My hand held out but now you wonder why!-my touch forgotten like mid-summers wind?A familiar smell lingers in your mind...smell of love... love forever in season!-oh, the smell that now defies all reason!Did you think my promise was just a lie?Are you so blind?- I tried too hard to finda kindred love... two souls beneath the sun!
