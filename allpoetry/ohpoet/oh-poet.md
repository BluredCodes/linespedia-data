I am a mighty warlock!-Oh, Poet, write of me!-And make my name immortal...Tell all I make you see!    'I see a simple question...    Sorcerer or fool?!-    The magik I create     Would be the death of you!'He looked at me bewildered...Strange power your words weave!-Oh poet write not of meCan't live what you cconceive
