title: "When my heart is broken"

When my heart is broken and I find rest
in the cold grave, give me at least a tear...
then come and whisper into my dear ear
words of sweet love... before I turn to dust!
Remember I never betrayed your trust...
remember how I face my loss... no fear!-
when my heart is broken.

You took all my love and cast it so far...
disturbing the peace in my heart! Why must
you be so cruel?(Of love can you now boast?)
Will you regret it when you I can't hear?-
when my heart is broken.
