I should love this day this I know,
For my FATHER tells me so.  He has 
made it pretty as can be.  He has 
made it especially for me.

To do in it important things To love,
To hope, and to share my pain.

For each day is a blessed gift even 
when its bad I still have to sift.
Sift through the tears, and sift through
the pain makes life easier and eterinty
to gain.

I love my FATHER I gave him my soul,and
my life I gave as a toll.  For in HEAVEN 
you will see a smile to you from blessed
ol' me.

Do only this on this blessed day say 
"I love you" in more than one way.
There's to much hate as you can see,
lets just end it with you and me.
