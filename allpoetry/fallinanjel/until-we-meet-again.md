Until we meet againYou came into our livesso tenderly, so loving, so shortOur precious little babyOur precious little boy.O though we never held youyou still know we care,andeven when we whisper you hear our faintest prayer.We will always love you and you'll be in our hearts.Our precious little babyOur one and only son.You have been chosen to be by God'sside there is lots to do up theremaybe even slide.until we get to Heaven keep this inmind you are not just God's angel you are also mine.
