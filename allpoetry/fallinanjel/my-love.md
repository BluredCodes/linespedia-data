How could this be the love of my lifelying next to me in bed along at my side.How could this be my friend, and my loverto have and to hold from this day forward.How could this be this cherrished soul,so sweet and tender the kisses he holds.To love and to show with each waking daythe love I have grows more every day.I love you my dear, my love, my life andI thank GOD for making me your wife.
