My Friend I ask today
How would you like to play?
No more work or school this day
Only laughter, and smiles remain.

To be free in body spirit and mind
not always worrying about the time.
Not to worry about things that have past
only worrying about our daily task.

To play,
To laugh,
To care,
and to remeber to always share.

"Have a blast!"
Is what I say nver worry bout yesterday.

Yesterday has come and gone
Tomorrow is a day that hasn't shown.
All its glory on the way
lets "JUST SKIP" and PLAY today.
