To my daughter
I send you a brother
to play, to love like no other.


to watch and keep him safe
until I get there some day.


Keep him close and watch him grow
for your his sister and you should know.
He'll get into trouble just wait and see,
but he's a spitting image of your Daddy and me.


I love you so my baby girl
until we meet again
when I leave this world.


Watch your brother and you will see,
No one loves you more than your 
Daddy and me.
