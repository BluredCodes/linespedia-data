Words as fake crystals, 
rain from your mouth. 
The fear for silence 
drives you to paranoia, 
you need to escape. 
Double-mouthed beasts 
in your nightmares singing 
the song of silence. 
To be stuck in past 
erases your shadow, 
chokes your soul.
