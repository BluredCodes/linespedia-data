I am the glue that holds us all together
I am theirs to use at their disposal
Without me they would be just personalities 
Just voices in the wind

The first one represents coolness and social interactions
She lives a very sheltered life
She's perfect in everything she does
Everyone loves her
Everyone

The second one is the brains of the operation
She is very smart 
Although she is smart she is very timid and shy
People scare her
She doesn't like the way they act
She doesn't like their touch

The third one is always angry
Always yelling and screaming
Always pushing and kicking
All the pain and frustration

The fourth one is always sad
Crying and rocking back and forth looking out a open window
Watching the children play
Wondering why she can't be just like them
Happy and interacting with one another

All of them put together make up me
Without me they would all just be personalities
Just voices lost in the wind
Without meaning or hope
They would just be nothing
They would be just how I feel sometimes
PowerlessThis one is more serious than my last one.
But I'm not all entirely sure that it makes sense.
But I guess as long as it does to me its fine.
