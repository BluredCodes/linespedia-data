Sometimes my world is sad and sometimes it is glade.
Sometimes it is fuzzy sometimes it is shaved
Sometimes it is bumby sometimes it is paved
Sometimes it is naked sometimes it is clothed
Sometimes it is open sometimes it is closed
Sometimes it is blue sometimes it is red
Especially when Im smoking from the head 

Sometimes my sky is orange with green elephant shaped clouds
Sometimes The cows are smurfed blue with gold strips
Sometimes I see young children poking these cows with sticks
Wondering what kind of milk comes out of them
When the cows get fed up they spread their wings and take flight
Soaring in the sky like a bird free of worry

Sometimes I see them fly over my head
They swoop down and pick me up
I see many faces as I look down upon my world
Upon the purple colored grass and it is the color of life
Some of these faces are orange some of them red
I see the small skinny children prance around like melting snowmen

I see the rich and the poor 
They don't look any different then eachother
One day I hope everyone can get along
And dance in my streets of candy and cheeseI know this poem sounds a little weird but everyone sees the world differently and this is how I see the world sometimes.
