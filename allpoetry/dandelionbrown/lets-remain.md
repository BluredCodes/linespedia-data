title: "Let's Remain"

Hold me to your heart
I can feel the beat
Fast and rhythmic behind my head
It calms the wildfire in my soul…
It takes me away 
And I wish to reach below
But my conscience pulls me back
And I am content to feel you beside me
Though I desire as all
I could desire forever…
And never be as happy as I am now…
In your arms, in your heart
Just you and me for eternity
Like this, like this 
And I find the cure for my nightmares…
Hidden beneath your skin…
Let me crawl beneath your soul…
Let me reside here until tomorrow 
Beside the one I love
Beneath the pain of the conscious of the world
Within the hands of time
And let tonight last 
In happiness, in love
Let’s remain…
