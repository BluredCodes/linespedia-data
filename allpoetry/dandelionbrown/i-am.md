I am… Combat boots and floral perfumeGundam Wing meets Sailor MoonMiniskirts and baggy jeansPsycho chick, or so it seemsComputer addict since days of yoreKiss me, baby, I want more!Touch, taste, sight, and soundHead in clouds, feet on groundNo ones angel, coloured wingsLover of all artistic thingsCalm river running deepOnly stopping just to sleepNothing more, nothing lessNot the damsel in distressUnderestimated in every wayAnyone want to play?
