Can you just hold me tight?I’ll wipe the sweat from your browBreathe your scent deep insideWhere I can hold it foreverLocked into the depths of my soulForever and EverMaybe you could touch my lips?Whisper into my earsWords that lift my heartI’ll watch your smile lightEven the blackest nightForever and EverPlease just spend the night?I promise we’ll be okayJust hold me until oblivionWe’ll let the rest just melt awayLet me sleep safe in your armsForever and Ever
