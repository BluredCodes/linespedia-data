title: "I am not alone"

I am not alone,
They can feel my pain,
Through and through,
They sense who I am,

Deep beneath my skin,
Below my armored shell,
In the core of my reality,
Their cage won’t be my hell,

Life will find its way,
And so, too, will I find,
The moments of my freedom,
With feelings I need not hide,

So let this be my prayer,
A light to guide my soul,
Past the hatred in their eyes,
And the damnation of this hole.
