---
title: "A Million Thoughts"
writer: "dandelionbrown"

A million thoughts flash through my head.
With one sigh, I see you smile my way.
I can’t start to express how I feel,
For fear of pushing you away.

I’m afraid that loss of expression,
Will lead to chaos in the end.
But I can’t shake this sinking feeling,
When you leave me no place to begin.

You make me laugh, I must admit,
Though you often make me cry.
Tears not from the words you say,
But from their absence deep inside.

You contradict the words you say.
Your expressions say it all.
I’ve never seen you as happy as,
When we’re laughing in the hall.

So take this confusion you’ve given me,
And bring an answer to my ears.
Give me reason to confide in you.
Help me clear away my fears.
