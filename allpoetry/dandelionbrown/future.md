The days flow softly by
And I am at my knees
As the time passes me along
I am at mercy to destiny’s plan
But I dream that if I close my eyes
Tomorrow’s light won’t shine
Until I am that satisfied
That your presence is everywhere

I can see the vivid colors of the morning
As I taste the salt of your lips
But, as come the rainy days,
I am left to be on my own
My wishes extend to merge as one
Between our locking gaze
And I only dream to please your heart
As your heart brings pleasure to mine.
