---
title: "My One"
writer: "dandelionbrown"

The rhythmic beating of your heart,
The even pattern of your breath,  
The warmth of your touch on my skin,
The idea of you being alive and real,

The way you look at me through your sweet eyes,
The way you light up at my sight,
The way my heart beats when you’re near,  
The fire that ignites my soul to breathe,

The reason my whole world revolves,
The music echoes throughout my thoughts, 
The aroma taunts my heart and soul,
The illusions breaking like a passing storm,

The will of me resides in you,   
The loving anger toward all you do,   
The loss of sanity when you smile my way,
My one true love for all my days.
