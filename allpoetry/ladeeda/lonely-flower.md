Lonely flower
Bloom for me
Show me the vibrance
I know you are hiding

Your petals are falling
See-through lies
Charred and burned memories
Retract and close
They don't want to 
Know your secrets
Trap them with guilt
They'll flip it around
And make it your fault
You ever said a word
Trust can't be guaranteed
But you knew that before
You gave it all away
Too late for regrets
Lonely flower 
You made the decision
