you don't know me
so don't claim to
you can't read me
i'm not a fucking book

i'm not lucky 
because i have what you want
i never asked for it
so don't resent me

i can't take living
for your fucked up expectations
one small slit 
and it could all be overa really, really old poem. i was crazy then.
