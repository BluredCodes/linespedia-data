cold trembling fingers
don't care anymore
dreaming of a way out
and can't figure out why

the dark is now fading
but i'm not used to the light
the light filled with promise
and hope that can't last

i close my eyes
try to remember
a time i had faith
but the memories are gone

was i always this empty?
did God forget me again?
or does he want me to be ashamed
for everything i tried to control?
did he take it away to see how i'd cope?
i didn't need anyone
is he proving me wrong?

i need something to grip
and i see it right there
but my cold trembling fingers 
can't reach anymorei was just thinking about a time long ago and trying to figure things out. i'm not sure if this worked, it turned out differently than i expected.
