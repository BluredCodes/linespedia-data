---
title: "blurred vision"
writer: "ladeeda"
slug: "blurred-vision"

cold tile floors
digusting weaknesses and lonliness
series of stories
tangled in knots
maybe i'm wrong again
but that doesn't make things okay

we've failed to see 
the truth that's left
because we're angry
and walking while our vision's blurred
by salty tears

i can't give up
i'm losing myself 
everytime i let you
convince me it's my fault
it was always my fault
and i'm not good enough
but this time you made me bleed 
despite what you say

my refusals 
your illusions
it's all the same
and no one's really sorry

for the record
you weren't the one who broke me
