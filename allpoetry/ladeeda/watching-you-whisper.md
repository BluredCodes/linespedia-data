---
title: "Watching you whisper"
writer: "ladeeda"

Watching you whisper
Afraid to be heard
Cause when you are 
They'll find you
You won't let it happen
Not this time

Watching you whisper
Cringing at your voice
The silence is burning
But you'll die
Before you break it

Watching you whisper
With tears in your eyes
You want to scream
But you've forgotten how
You're crawling and falling 
Don't make a sound

Watching you whisper
Sliding on ice
Turn the lights off
No one can tell

Watching you whisper
Blood on your arms
Hide in the shadows
Don't let them see
Your tears turn to glass 
And shatter
When they hit the ground

Watching you whisper
As you try to find
What you lost in yesterday
But it's not coming back

Watching you whisper
Afraid to be heard
Cause when you are
They'll hurt you
You won't let it happen
This time
