---
title: "violent love"
writer: "ladeeda"
slug: "violent-love"

tears frozen inside
memories hidden under ice
add another layer
it won't shock me anymore
accustomed to violent love
rewind and repeat
flowing through cycles
of destruction
did you gain insight this time?
my observations are invalid
juvenille emotions and opinions
drowning in pools of frustration
imprison salvation
unaware of the damage
blinded by denial
you're afraid and insecure 
but can i please have an explanation?
