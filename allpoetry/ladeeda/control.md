Interrogation
Of my inner composure
Sporadic answers
One word at a time
Some concept of pacing
Understanding, completion
Pass around the control
In an untraceable pattern
Push it back
Push it back
Are you sure that's the enemy?
You're just ashamed of your fears
You don't need some security
Because you're in charge  
With your toothy grin
Trained in persuasion
I saw you
Hiding in the alley
Cowering from the lights
Afraid to expose 
What lies beneath your skin
