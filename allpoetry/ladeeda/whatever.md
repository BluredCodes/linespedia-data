sugar coated corn stalks
cotton candy dreams
blood covered lightening bolts
raining irrelevency
enough bullshit already
you know everything
i'm always cold
i've goosebumps on my skin
don't hand me you sweater
are you oblivious?
unaware?
don't touch me again
mirrors and deception
can you make me feel special?
still trying so hard
have you forgotten already?an old juicebox. i changed it slightly.
