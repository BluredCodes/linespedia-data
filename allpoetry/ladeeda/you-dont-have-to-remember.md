title: "you don't have to remember"
writer: "ladeeda"

i'm not sorry 
that i refuse to go back there
and i'm angry
that you speak so obliviously
maybe i don't want to talk about it
maybe it's easier for you 
you think it's been so hard for you
but you don't have to remember
the crash from the other room
the mumbled words
yellow-blue skin
eyes that you know won't open again
you didn't have to search for a pulse
while you're alone and hyperventilating
and you didn't have to wait endlessly
suffocating in a moment
that won't ever go away
