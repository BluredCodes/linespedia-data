Cut and paste stars
Coated in glitter glue
Stuck to your finger painted sky
What if reality caved in?
And everything was two dimensional
No myserious depths
Lurking behind every door
Lies written on your face 
You'll never come clean
But wouldn't you like 
To get to the finish line first?
Break him
Before he breaks you
Don't crawl away
There's still some innocence left
If you'd like you can take it
It's collecting dust in the basement
And I'm not going down there
Bodies and broken glass
I'm imagining everything?
Thanks for the diagnosis
