---
title: "Forgotten By Then"
writer: "ladeeda"
slug: "forgotten-by-then"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/35363-Forgotten-By-Then-by-ladeeda"
---

Fatal seclusion
Deathly silences
Carousel of thoughts
Packed away in the attic
Mirrors that see
Behind glazed over eyes
Intensity
Don't let it fade
Into remnants
Of incomplete stories
Scars that grow deeper
As the days pass 
Questioning, replaying every instsant
Reassure me,
I wasn't naive
Just a fix
Feel complete
Throw me away
I don't want to think
About the next time
I'll be forgotten by then
