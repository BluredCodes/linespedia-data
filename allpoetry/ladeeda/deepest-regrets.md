Falsely informed
Sadly misguided
Travelling in circles
Yearning for meaning
But you'll never be special
Innocence died
And left you here bleeding
Ran away and followed the tracks back
But you didn't end up where you started 
Seems your soul has relocated
Deepest regrets
Shock hasn't set in
Waited for years
Why don't you stop pretending?this is one from grade eight.
