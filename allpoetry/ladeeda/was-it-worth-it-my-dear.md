---
title: "was it worth it my dear?"
writer: "ladeeda"
slug: "was-it-worth-it-my-dear"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/40953-was-it-worth-it-my-dear--by-ladeeda"
---

rip me at my seams
do you know i don't need you?
lust for innocence
that has been destroyed
blind and reassured
you'd do anything for a moment
to define your meaning
so above their madness
endless searches
you know they won't find it
but neither will you
shards of pride 
you can't steal
because i'm still alive
amongst the chaos
i still manage to smile
was it worth it my dear?
disappointed because you wrongly perceived
everything about me
so who's to blame?
your walls collapsed
you hands are cold
drowned in shallow desire
bet you never figured
i would get there before you
