flames flickering
don't stop the fire
i'm not finished yet
what were you saying?
eternity's a challenge
can you make it that long?
numbing appendages 
i'll give it a try
sadistic attempts
to earn your approval
leave me alone
poisonous noise
betrayal in boxes
convenient deception
i don't need it anymore
tell me a lie
i'll see if you're bluffing
perfection killed me
but i'm still living
cut me into pieces
and magnify my flaws
contrary to your assumptions
i'm not that naive
and i can handle it fine
grin and bare it sweetheart
it's not changing anytime
memories can't be painted over
with desperation and denial
blurred past existances 
confirm my predictions
yearning for the answer
but i haven't found the question
can someone please tell me
where the hell i'm going?
decaptitated street signs
