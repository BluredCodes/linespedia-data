---
title: "love me"
writer: "ladeeda"
slug: "love-me"

Melted promises
Rearranged 
Into a mutated version
Of the truth
Your eyes burn my flesh
Tiring of empty desire
So mold me
However you please
But love me,
This timeabout something in the distant past
