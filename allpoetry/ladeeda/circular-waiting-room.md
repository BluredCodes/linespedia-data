Black coils
Linking, enclosing
This circular waiting room.
Life's aquarium
Of impossibility
Stretching beyond.
Empty and pale,
May I paint while I'm here?
Just a little purple flower
Right near the edge.
I'll give you my secrets,
But I'm the only one here.
