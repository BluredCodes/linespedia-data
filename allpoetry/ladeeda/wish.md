burn left over anger
grinning with denial
product of your secrets
and circumstances
that stay glued to my skin
drop a penny in the well
and wish for a sense of something
~*~
destroy the shadowed memories
that pollute your insides
crucify the fear
and dream dandelion dreams
~*~
repaint your creation
sew the clouds back together
don't let the rain through
the flowers melt in the water
