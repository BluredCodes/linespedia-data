title: "unwanted time"
writer: "ladeeda"

not afraid
not now
we can burn the blank pages
and all the ones you didn't like
pretty colours
matching smiles
is everything like this? 
dreary sundays
went away
let's replace it
and let's erase unwanted time
too short for anything
