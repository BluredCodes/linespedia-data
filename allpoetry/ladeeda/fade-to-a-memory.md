---
title: "Fade to a memory"
writer: "ladeeda"
slug: "fade-to-a-memory"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/35396-Fade-to-a-memory-by-ladeeda"

Fraudulent whore
Dictate my mind
Tell me why I'm searching
For what I'll never find

Make me complete
Then tear me apart
Twisted emotions
I was right from the start

Don't let the tear dry
You'll fade to a memory
Soon that memory will die
Buried in fake apologies
