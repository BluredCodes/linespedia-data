Feel the connection?
Eternity's a joke
Grudge against lust
Let's get it over with

Jealous because I'm afraid
Just a passing faze
Simple truths
Are complex
Don't try to disect them
Weave me in with the lies

Your hands are still warm
The sky is transparent
Don't be so condescending
Isn't innocence fun?
Yes, but darkness is better
Rain contradictions
My ocean is empty
And I'm drowning in yours
But you haven't noticed
Insincere words
Magic embraces 
Just tell me,
