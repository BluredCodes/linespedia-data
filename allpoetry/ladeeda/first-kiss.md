All the words 
I want to say
Don't come anymore
The sky has changed 
To a pastel pink
And charcoal grey
As we stand 
In the bitter cold
In silence
Beautiful silence
I kiss you
Your lips warm and moist
I want the world to stop
Freeze this magical moment
For eternity
But the world doesn't stop
And I feel tears in my eyes
As you walk away smiling
I am vulnerable
The future's unclear 
But I am not afraid
AnymoreA poem about my first kiss with a guy that I really love. I am such a loser, I make a big thing out of one little kiss. Oh well.
