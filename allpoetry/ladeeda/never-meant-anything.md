---
title: "Never meant anything"

Time obsessed
Paranoid
Faith's for the fearful
Sink your teeth in
What's the big fucking deal?
Lust is a game
Are you bored of it yet?
I never said you could touch me
Don't tell me to trust you
Delicate darling
Try to destroy me
Prove I was right,
It never meant anything.
