---
title: "Shiver"

shiver
i demand you
you wanted something extraordinary
shiver
i command you
you wanted intensity
here it is 
can you feel it now?
shiver
i don't want to be the only one
stuck in the cold
how does the warmth feel?
shiver 
cause you're the one that brought me here
unaware of your power
did you know it would kill me?
shiver
it's all your fault
i'd like to see you frozen
you made me believe 
but i was right all along
shiver
don't smile
you might melt me again
i want to feel solid
so you can't break me this time
