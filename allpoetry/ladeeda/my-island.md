---
title: "My Island"
writer: "ladeeda"
slug: "my-island"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Battles and hurricanes
I have arrived
But I don't understand

I see the demons right behind me
Waiting for me
To take a step back
So they can ravish my mind

They carry the knowledge of my fears
I'm wrapped in soothing denial
Wishing for a new beginning
Longing for the view of an empty sea
Without mistakes and evil eyes

Late nights and restlessness 
Nothing's been accomplished
But I suppose I'm not alone
