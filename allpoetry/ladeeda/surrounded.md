surrounded
filling with grey anger
betrayal
no it cannot be

stay under the water
tornadoes blow past
taking away the clouds
that sheltered you 

i was never a part of anything
half way connected
forgotten and disincluded
tell the stories of my faults

i don't want to remember
the time i was weak enough to cry
tear the pages from the book 
rewrite it carefully this time
