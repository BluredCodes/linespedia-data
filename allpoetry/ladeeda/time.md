Forever is a fantasy
Created to make us feel secure
Don't worry honey 
Time will heal
Yeah, but time will kill
It killed you

Rewind in my mind
Smash that fucking clock
Time forgot to pass then
Didn't it?
Trying to keep me
In that endless nightmarecrappy again
