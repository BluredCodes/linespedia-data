Little girl
In the corner
Innocent and pure
Baby blue eyes
Pink rosy cheeks
Heart eternally untainted
Good intentions
Please stop crying my dear
Don't tell me
Things I don't want to hear
Pulling at my seams
Shameful denial
Quiet my dear
It can be our secret

Little girl
In the corner
Why aren't you breathing?
I didn't mean to kill you
But you knew everything
Why are you disappearing?
I want to see you smile
Don't leave me alone
With the one who did this
I'm sorry
Come back now
I'm too imcomplete
Dance for me darling
Careless and gracefully
Forget everything
Let's be together again

Little girl
In the corner
Brave sweet daughter
Taught me everything
What will I do now?
All that's left
Is what I didn't to face
