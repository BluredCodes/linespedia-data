---
title: "i won't break"
writer: "ladeeda"
slug: "i-wont-break"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/52287-i-wont-break-by-ladeeda"

majestic and full of pride
burn your devious grins

efforts and strength
there will be no collapse
i won't allow you to succeed
