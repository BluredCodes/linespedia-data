title: "classical pain"

black out the thoughts  
i don't want to think
breathe in the tears 
i'm not supposed to cry
and i never did
til you lied about everything
you said i was broken and weak
well there's more secrets than you could imagine
when i remember it gets hard to breathe
i'll never be needed just quickly forgotten
burned out smiles and classical pain
i can't say anything right
but they're not listening anyway
pass by and leave a piece of yourself
til there's no room left for who i used to be
i'll keep on pretending
reading your script
and creating emotion
that matches the rest
but it's dark and they can't see
the tide is creeping closer
maybe it'll wash away
i only had a moment
that didn't want to end 
and i made all the mistakes
ruined everything
the sky kept moving by
frozen to the ground
i can't leave 
or it will happen again
