oh the good times,
the good times.
wild stories 
and adventures 
in Warrior Land
you've been there
through it all
personal pan pizzas
texas tang doritos
flintstones and aloros
smoke and gift shop
yoyos and pogs
and when you beat up that little kid 
and his mother came to our door
or when that older kid stole your sword
and i got it back for you
collecting caterpillars
you mocked the way i danced
camping in the back yard
and i fell off that roof
antique shops
you bought me Dickens
soccer out back
lost bets
i never paid you back
poker and crazy eights
remember that snapping turtle 
we saw on the biking trail
and i rode across the bridges
even though i was afraid
breaking into the Bell building
illegal trespassing at 9
and that white cat on our street
jenny and molly
oh, what carts!
frisbees and goosebumps
precious piggies and puppies
magic cards, i never knew how to play
hurricanes and bob's milk
icense and teabags
aliston potato fest
dragon wagon
starship 2000, you threw up
and i got angry cause i wanted to win a fish
but we had to sit at the picnic table till you felt better
spreading chain letters across America
born trouble maker
crazy carpets and toboggans
pioneers and church
you wouldn't shake the minister's hand
and you called him a jerk
kicking doanld duck
when you went past the thing in the museum
and told the security guard i told you to do it
wing-ding carnival
trading in old stuffed snakes
so i could get a tweety bird
ice skating outside iga
you ran away 
when you smashed that window
buying smokes at bob's milk
with a letter from grandpa
apper cider from back's
you thought some guy was a mannequin
and grabbed his leg
mario time macine
i learned more from that game 
than any history class
diddy kong tournaments
i restarted whenever i was losing
sending megan to rent pyscho
i told on you
when you peaked at your Christmas gift
i ran away
when you called 911 by accident
and they sent the police over
cat of the day
and adam got beat up by a midget 
your inflatable chair
and i sat in your room 
and played banjo kazooie
eating rice from the pot
lying to Megan
about evil wizards in the lamp shade
wagon wheel milkshakes
our paper route that payed next to nothing
calling much on demand
and psychic neworks
you set off the fire alarm
at the old age home
zeller's midnight madness
you broke a lamp and never said anything
picking other people's flower
and giving them to mom
you almost drowned at white water rafting
remember day camp?
you won the pizza eating award
and ripped up you red measle form
cause you didn't want a needle
that guy at the beach
who was gonna beat up Megs
and you told him off
when you bought beer from the general store
and they told you it was pop
setting the carpet on fire at Easter dinner
or when you got caught 
for using counterfeit money at pretzel twister
staying uo to watch duckman and seinfeld
with ice packs on our heads
so many memories
*tear*
strange, adventurous little children
now crack jokes
while listening to finger eleven
and playing harvest moon
edgefest, directing traffic
holding some guy's beer bottle
and i got lost
when we got shoved into the mosh pit
you understand me more than i admit
and you're easy to talk to
complaining and lounging
they're all insane 
but someday we'll move to B.C.
and all will be goodabout Ryan...yeah, we've had some pretty weird times...
