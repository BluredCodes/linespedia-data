---
title: "i don't need"
writer: "ladeeda"

i guess i was right
i'll remember for next time
lock it tighter next time
don't break down next time
i can swim on my own
i've done it before
i don't need 
i don't need anyone
i don't need
i don't need you
disconnected
silence without tears
i know how to be strong
so that's what i'll do
i don't need
i don't need to cry
it's not worth it
i wasn't worth it
cold skin covered in stone
won't open up
won't break down
i won't melt away
everything else just melts away
breathing cannot burn
if i don't stop for a breath
run till the sky spins away
and there's nothing left
to remind me
of everything i used to believe
i don't need
i don't need anyone
