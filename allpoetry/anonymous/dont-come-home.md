Everday its get colder, being by myself.
"Borneback ceaselessly into the past."
I fall behind everytime trying to get ahead.
Your voice full of apathy and your eyes filled with tears what am I lead to believe.
Every time you cry, I die inside.
"If you ever change your mind I'll still be here,"
the enclosed card never lies.
Die and wither away one dozen times,
the only mistake was coming home.
Its colder in the car,
but you can keep me warm one last time,
before we go our seperate ways.
My life has been a lie,
I was wrong about you, Was I ever right?

Unfortunate circumstances keep us apart,
or at least thats what I like to think.
Around the world and back I waited for my chance to fail.
Watch the door it opens slower than you think. 
"If you ever change your mind I'll still be here," 
waiting to bring you near, the enclosed card never lies.
