title: "dancing"

return to humanity
	& rediscover old selves
	u&i&we written like letters
	("we" are only one)
	magnificient pain
	break it! break it, break it,
	it's cold but hearts are
	    (sometimes) warm
	lean on me & we'll be friends.
	(this dance is for u&i - old&new - hope&praise)
	tango with pointytoe
	spit on the bushes
	revel in moon, and
	discover age.
