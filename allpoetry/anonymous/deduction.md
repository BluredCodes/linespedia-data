the man wants to see the way
but the poisonous haze clouds his vision
he waves bye bye to sanity 
and commits adulterous acts 
against reason
seeing his soul’s deepest desire 
awakens him
to the fact that immortality exists
in the eyes of the beholder
he undresses ambitions
and starts to realize
‘corrupting youth,
that’s what america’s all about’
