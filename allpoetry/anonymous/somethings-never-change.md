title: "Somethings Never Change"

I'm still here and your still there, nowhere near to me. Im still alone and you still don't care, nowhere near to me. Somethings never change and change is hard to find if you cant look in the right places. Sometimes I can hear you when I breath, whispering in my ear letting me know that your ok. Im tired of trying to make you see the way that we are meant to be. Im tired of you and tired of me. Tired of trying to change the impossible. Its not worth the worthless struggle for a prize thats not as good as it seems. Im tired of trying but somethings never change and thats just me.
