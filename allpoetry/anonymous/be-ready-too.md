why can't you fucking be ready too?
now's the time to begin again
get your fucking ass together,
i'm fucking sick of waiting.

Son-of-a-bitch, I can't sleep for dreaming
you, the devil glowing in my mind
what I'd sacrifice to understand myself
is put at the altar of your feet.

dreaming, past the door we walk together
dreaming, mouth to mouth we finally kiss!
dreaming, I can't live without bliss

You've got-to realize what you miss.
