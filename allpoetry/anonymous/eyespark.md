My hand rests at my side, curled in lonliness
my heart shrinks at the feeling
of eyespark.

My hand asks softly, to take in its gentle caress
your heart, and grow
to truelove.

My hand needs.  Needs you there - it is lost,
and our hearts, diverging from our souls,
seek out.

It's so easy just to walk by - but what of that glance,
that feeling I know you felt too -

our lives need but brush once,
for our hands to meet, grasp,
and become a romantic plural.

lonliness shrinks at eyespark

Let our hearts guide,
let our lives follow.
