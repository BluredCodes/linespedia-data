Snow begins to fall and still by myself. Forgotten lost, heart-crossed and girls are my only problems. Lay beside me, tell your dreams and I’ll shatter them all. Sometimes I think that she was the one but sometimes is never enough, never enough to be with anyone, so this is goodbye. Easily amused, just as easily confused, the sky’s still up when you’re upside down. I’m falling in love (with no one in particular). Take this note for your eyes only and mark the box that breaks my heart, there’s only one option. You can’t have me when I’m busy somewhere else but no matter where I am or who I’m with you’re still the one I want.
