Fear

I hear it calling my name,
I shiver to mere thought of it's presense,
it whispers calling my name
continuously bringing this pain,

I feel lost, I fell weak
thinking only about what I seek,
I begin to weep 
try to be discreet,
not wanting to realize
that i'm facing defeat,
composure complete,
I feel dirty, not neat
and then the whispers
come back in a fleet

calling my name, I hear it,
I quiver as I begin to get near it,
one life to live
so why do I fear it?
