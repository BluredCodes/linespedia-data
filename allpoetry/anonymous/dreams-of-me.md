So how have you been?
 Are things still the way that they used to be?
 Can you still close your eyes and just forget about me? 
It's not as easy as it looks to sit back and do nothing,
 to see you hurt when you only deserve the best....from me. 
Try to see that when I sleep, 
I can only see your face and hear your voice calling me. 
If I've changed its for the worse,
 its all for nothing, if its not for you. 
Step away from the light, and hide from your shadow, 
because it might not be what you expected
