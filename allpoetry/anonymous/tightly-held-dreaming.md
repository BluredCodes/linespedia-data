title: "Tightly Held Dreaming"

the rising of the sun
beholds a spear of light
that pierces through a barred window

the anxious boy looks up
to see through the sleep
many long shadows

the new day brings the same
like every day previous

the life which he grew up
was always with hope

what he doesn't know can't hurt, i suppose
just if he keeps his eyes closed,
tightly held dreaming

the setting of the sun,
another day gone by
piercing through his dreams, revealing the darkness

there's a pounding on his brain
that leaves him warm inside,
reminds him where his heart is
