watching, watching
everyone's watching
eyes on me, can't escape
hiding with myself by my side
what did i do wrong, nothing to confide

my eyes stained to survive
all consciousness drained, desperate to stay alive
won't somebody help me get out of this mess
he was a madman, now we can rest

what is this, why are you all after me, i did it for you
innocent conscience for all to see
and there's nothing more to do

he destroyed lives
and tried making you machines
and now i hold the key to unlock your dreams

no more insanity....
