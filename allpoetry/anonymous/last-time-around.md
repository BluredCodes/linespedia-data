Better than I've been in months,
is better than the way I've felt.
Im finally happy but not happy to be happy.
It's easier to be lonely but it's better to be complete...with you.
Things won't be the same when I leave
but I want hold on to what is left if its still there.
Trust in me and I'll trust in you
but broken hearts are always blue and so am I.

You'll always have a friend in me,
a friend to call and a friend to see.
You'll always have a friend in me.
A familiar face with a strange voice,
that has nothing left to fight for.
Nothing left for me.
