feminine is
beauty, hair, grace
it is that smile upon your face
it is the love of life
it is the sense of things gone right.

I know you now
and you me, too
yet we're both so young
a relationship
choice: grow seperately or apart?
time with you is heaven!
   (clouded, angelic)
& your wings that enfold me: such rapture!

just hold me
how can we relax?
secceding to inertia
we have our entire lives -
no rush

though, our bodies tell us otherwise.
