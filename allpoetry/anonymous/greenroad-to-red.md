I was angry at you -
fire-emotion-drips.
slips away at
yourarrival, replaced ofcourse
by feelings, more subtle
I can never predict
ourtimetogether - you're assertive:
I like that & follow
this greenroad leads to red,
&from red to whiteyes, the spacing is intentional.  I was influenced by e.e. cummings in my early poetic days (ok I guess I still am, but I do the word-combining thing less).  I think wordcombining give us a additional level of creativitiy over standard language, and makes ya think about how you really want it to sound, not how you're forced to have it sound.
