the lead phone
wish it was aluminum (maybe soon)
I know she likes me - this shouldn't b ehard.
I'm so innocent!
still, just where I started
the kid myself is asserting: thinking
I really still am that unsure
the horrible 'blah!'
oh, just call her you fool
(feel (comfortable))
phones aren't made for kissing
so good night!
