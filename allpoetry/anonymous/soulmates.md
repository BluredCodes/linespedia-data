---
title: "Soulmates"
writer: "Anonymous"
slug: "soulmates"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

You challenge my ideals and thoughts,
and show me it is o.k. to be different.
You make me think it is o.k. to love,
and help me to appreciate life a little more.
For this you will always be in my mind,
my heart, my soul.
My soulmate.
