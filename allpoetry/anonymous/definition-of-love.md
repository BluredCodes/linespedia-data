I know not the definition of love
  any more than you, but
I do know that I like you

I like what you stand for, what you value
  and what you will become (whoever, however,
    for you are intelligent + respecting and shall grow well)
  and that I I like being with you,
    feeling your touch on mine ( &mine on yours ) - our fingers entwine!
  and thus, by sheer mathematical prowess I deduce:
