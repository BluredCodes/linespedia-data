title: "Decay Of Your Time"

the decay of your time
moving along the open road
life passing you by and you catch a final glimpse
in the corner of your eye
constant fear, living the nightmare
of desperation and decay
moving along with time
it's passing you by
you can't win the race
you can't stay alive
it's almost won
what do you do
faster, faster
like the wind
keep on driving
you've got to
but you can't beat time
you can't stay alive
the track is worn
you're falling apart, but keep on driving
you're dying, but keep on fighting, don't give in
faster, faster
you've got to win
