my moon,
pull me tenderly to your breast
I feel your pulling deep inside,
and you smile at me.

that quirky friendly smile
you are my friend -
I respect you, yet you mock me!

what can I do?
that you will notice me?
my life is a flash
so unspecial to heavenly eyes

yet; I love and am loved!
I create and am created!
what more can a human be?
perhaps I am special, or are we all?

:Your heart is big enough for two,
:Your eyes encompass my race.

help us lead ourselves to glory, O mystery!
