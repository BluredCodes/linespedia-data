title: "Way out"
writer: "Anonymous"

---

falling down a steep hill,
the world is still
could this be real?
this hill's now a mountain,
this world, it's dead,
a waterless fountain,
this hill never ends
it gets steeper and steeper,
as I reach up
I fall deeper and deeper,
finally, i'm there,
does anyone care?
