****
I LOVE YOU
****

As i look at photos of you
I wonder why
You'd want to be with me
And not someone else

You say I'm not ugly
But I know I am
You say I'm a babe 
But i know that I'm not
You say you love me more than I love you
But I know thats not true cause I love you the most
And that I would never lie

My only wish would to be with you
Right to the end
And that we'll always love each other 
Untill the day we die

My love for you will last forever
Even you dump me or maybe the other way round
Which i hope will never happen
But I'll still love you at least as a friend
