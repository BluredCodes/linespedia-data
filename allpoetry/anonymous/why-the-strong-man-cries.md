title: "Why the Strong Man Cries"

The strong man smiles, masking a face filled with utter humility
Because now his life isn't a place he wants to be
He has been robbed, but not of possesions, but of a friend
A friend who had too quickly met his end
A friend who made him laugh, and made him cry
A favorite of him, and a favorite of I
A good man, who gave all he had
As a brother, as a son, and as a dad
But in the strong man's strong heart he is alive in love
Flying around in it, on the wings of a dove
The epitome of grace, he never dies
Yes I know why the strong man cries

In Loving Memory of Owen Hart
