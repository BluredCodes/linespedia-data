A promise..

A promise is a red red rose
A hope held tight within it’s folds
A winding river throughout our souls
A tide pounding upon the shore
An anchor on the ocean floor
A promise is a red red rose
A tingling from our head to toes
A hook which snags our very nose
A note tacked to the bathroom door
A pang which makes the lion roar
A promise is a red red rose
With petals as soft as morning light 
Which cling together in the night
Cemented by words once spoken
Which bleed dark red blood when broken
Upon a stem of sharpened thorns

Christopher Cole
