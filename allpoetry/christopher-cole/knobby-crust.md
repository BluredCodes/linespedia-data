Knobby Crust

Where goes this being called man
Who o'er the firmament does roam
Feeble creature of skin and bone
Who early on worked with mud and stone
Has since raised monuments to the sky
From whence may come his ultimate demise

Knobby crust shrouds the brain
Hidden space that causes such pain
Is it there that the spirit does reign
Or only a stage where beauty is feigned

Is there some plan, which makes him strive
Or like the insect to the light must dive 
Blindly driven yet not to survive
Fanatic obsession to know more than he can 
Overwhelmed by truth smitten by his own hand

Like the river constantly changing yet the same
Man the contradiction still remains
Good and Evil Pleasure and Pain
It's up to him not to refrain
From making sense of Chaos and changing the Game

1986
Christopher Cole
