Middle Aged White Boy Rap

Sarah Tonin was a friend of mine
She made me happy all the time
Whenever she came out to play
We’d sing and dance and feel okay

Clickity click clickity clack
I need to get my Sarah back
Clickity clack clickity clicks
I need to feel my Sarah fix

Then she just ups and went away
We laughed no more the sky went gray
My heart was broke my soul went black
Clickity click clickity clack

I sought this pro whose name was Zack
He went and got my Sarah back
Now that she is home to stay
We sing and dance and play all day

Clickity click clickity clack
He went and got my Sarah back
Clickity click clickity clicks
Sarah T is back in the mix

There’s no need to be sad and blue
When Sarah can be kind to you
But if she leaves here’s what to do
Seek this pro his name is Zack
I know he’ll get your Sarah back

Clickity click clickity clack
He went and got my Sarah back
Clickity click clickity clack
Clickity clack, clack, clack!

Christopher Cole
