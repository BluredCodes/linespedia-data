As a young man I often lied
kept emotions deep down inside
afraid to feel and to confide
I grew up cold and cynical
a tree without light, without love  
a soft hand in an iron glove
a rock, an island without a dove
a poor excuse for a boy, the pinnacle
Yet kindness came my way
and music did brighten my day
and thawed the cold with rays
of hope and laughter and the whimsical
a real person began to emerge
and new feelings began to surge
and today was the very first time I cried
as someone said George Harrison had just died

Christopher Cole
