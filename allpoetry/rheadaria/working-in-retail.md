Hard tolive in the lightwhere we see our reflectiondistorted by the mirror of lies.The wings of realitythrust out the feathers of dreamsonto the sea as we soarcloser the sun.Trying to hang on tothe tail of a cursive-tinto the next letter withoutbreaking the fluencyin some kind of attemptat perfection.
