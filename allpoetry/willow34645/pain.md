here i sit, in front of my computer 
alone
the rain beats the window 
yet it doesn't phase me 
i put up a tough front during the day, 
but when i go home, 
it's your taunting that drives me to cry
you are the root of my pain
when i close my eyes, it's your face that haunts me
torturing me
pointing and laughing
not seeing me as the person i really am
i would never let you see how your words affect me
i would never dream of it
that would just bring on more
i try to push away the feelings inside
but they just overwhelm me
i open my eyes
the pain is gone
. . . for now.
