how could you lie to me?
you had said you cared.
but when i went to lean on you,
i fell cuz you weren't there.

you led me on,
you made me think that you were there for me.
you made me feel good about myself,
i guess that was just make-believe.

so now you're gone, and i'm alone,
i hope you're happy now.
i hope that you can't sleep at night,
knowing how you've let me down.
