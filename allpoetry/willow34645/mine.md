you're all i want, you're all i need,
to get me through the day.
but the problem above all else,
is that you don't feel the same.

i tried and tried, and tried some more,
to get you out of my head.
but when i did, i'd remember
everything you'd said.

you said i was beautiful, you liked my hair,
you liked to tickle me.
but when the night would come,
my tears are all that i could see.

it was all a lie, you didn't care,
that night was just a "good time".
i was a fool to think that i'd reached you,
and now, you'll never be mine.
