The world we live in
Isn't the nicest place;
I always tell preps
To "get out of my face."

"Don't fucking touch me,
Or I'll rip you apart;
Reach inside your chest
And just tear our your heart."

Cause I really "don't
Give a damn what you say,"
And I'd like it if
you would just "go away."

I'm "not really nice" 
To the people I meet;
I'm "kinda scary"
When I walk down the street.

I'm shy and quiet,
When I speak, listen up;
It's not real easy, 
Always bein this "tough."

I put up this front
That you cannot see through;
It seems as if I'm
Just a "badass" to you.

But I'm sick and I'm tired
I'm worn out and I'm done;
Tomorrow I'll rise,
And I'll have the same "fun".

'Cause inside my world,
The pain never ends;
It barely just fades
When I'm with my friends.

So when you read this,
Don't feel bad for me;
But I'm slowly dieing,
From a pain you can't see.
