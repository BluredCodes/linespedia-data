I think I love him,
But I'm not too sure;
I only think about him,
Just the way we were.

Laying there upon the couch,
Content with our company;
Arms holding one another,
Our love out for all to see.

When I'm with him, I am myself,
I can be no other way;
His presence makes me happy,
His smile lights up my day.

He would never break my heart, right?
He loves me just too much;
He'd never dream of leaving me,
'Cause he would miss my touch.

He wrote me a poem, he got to me,
He touched my heart like no other;
I want to give him my heart so bad,
But I'm afraid to even bother.

What if he breaks it?
Just smashes it in two?
Who am I to turn to then?
Just what am I to do?

I'm not afraid to be in love,
That's not the point at all;
I'm more afraid of the sudden stop,
Than thinking of the fall.
