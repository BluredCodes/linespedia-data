there are so many things i never said,
that i really wanted to.
but i figured if i said them,
they wouldn't mean the same to you.

i thought about you day and night,
you never left my mind.
and when i went tthrough-out the day,
you were all i seemed to find.

i loved your hair, i loved your smile,
i mostly loved your eyes.
i don't know why i still love you,
even through those lies.

but you've found another, and maybe i should too,
why is it so hard?
how come when i find someone,
i can't let down my guard?

am i afraid of love? am i afraid to fall?
am i afraid of something new?
no, i think that i'm scared i'll find someone,
and i'm afraid it won't be you.
