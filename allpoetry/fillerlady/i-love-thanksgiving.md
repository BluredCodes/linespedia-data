title: "I Love Thanksgiving"

I truly love Thanksgiving,it's my favorite holiday.It's time to give thanksfor things going my way.I love it when the table's setwith lots of things to eat.Of course, there's a turkey,and candied yams so sweet.I love the cranberry sauce,it really tastes so fine.But I'm so very glad 'cause,my family's having quite a time!
