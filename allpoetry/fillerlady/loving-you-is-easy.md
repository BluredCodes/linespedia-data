title: "Loving You Is Easy"
writer: "Fillerlady"

Loving you is easy;
Losing you is too hard to bear;
We had such a true love,
remember how we cared?

Your heart was my heart;
Our love was combined.
When did all this change?
When did we become untwined?

How I long for your lips,
but they have gone astray.
I just have sorrow
each and every day.

Loving you is easy.
Please stay by my side.
I need you oh, so much,
My tears I cannot hide.

Every night was magic;
The stars did twinkle so.
Now I see they're darkened,
Because you did go.
