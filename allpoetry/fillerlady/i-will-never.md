I will never leave you,   I promise I will stay.I'll give you happiness -   Each and ev'ry day.I will bring you rainbows   and bright moonbeams too.Because my love is deep -   Know I do love you.Please don't ever worry,   I'm here forever.Then we can remain   always in love together.And the world will be so bright,   nothing can go wrong.I will never leave you -   Our love is a perfect song!
