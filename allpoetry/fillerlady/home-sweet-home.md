Was it the "Home Sweet home" plaque
that was tacked upon the wall?
No, it wasn't that plaque
which made our home the best of all.

It was all the cherished love,
and the special laughter,
that would linger in my mind
then, and forever after.

It was because of my sweet mom
and the way she showed she cared,
that our home was filled with song,
from the happiness we shared.

The "Home Sweet Home" plaque
was just a symbol of mom's love,
making pungent mem'ries
that we would always think of.
