writer: "Fillerlady"

Sisters are very special
because they'll lend a hand.
They're one of the few people,
who always understands.

Oh, sure sisters do disagree,
but it doesn't last long.
A great sister like you,
always make me belong.

Thank you for being there,
when I needed a friend.
You're the best sister,
no matter where or when!
