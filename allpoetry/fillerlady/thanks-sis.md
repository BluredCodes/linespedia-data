You're the one who I looked up to;
You taught me everything.
You showed me a caterpillar
and a robin's pretty wings.

I watched you comb your hair,
getting ready for a date.
You said my time would come -
I would just have to wait.

You made my life exciting,
easing me into the world.
What I learned from you is
how to be proud I'm a girl.

That's why I want to tell you
you mean so much to me.
Though there are many sisters,
I'm glad we're family!
