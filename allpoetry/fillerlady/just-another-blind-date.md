It was just another blind date,
I didn't want to go.
My friend said you were special,
I still thought I'd say no.

Yet she wouldn't give up,
she said I should take a chance.
Who knows, with the right man,
I might find some romance.

I went out with you that night,
and much to my surprise,
you were very special
with beautiful blue eyes.

Now, I found true love,
We've never been apart.
Mom was my best friend - 
I thank her from my heart!
