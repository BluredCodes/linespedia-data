---
title: "I'm Thankful"

I have so much to be thankful for,
something I feel completely through.
I'm thankful for my husband's love,
and seeing our dreams come true.

I'm grateful for my mom and sis,
for their love I hold so dear,
for their support and loyalty,
with every passing year.

I'm thankful for every day,
that I can see the sun.
And touch a leaf, or feel the earth,
with God's blessings on everyone.

As you can see material things
are not important to me.
I just need the special love
from friends and my family!
