title: "My Needs"

I see the depth of your concern,
 although you try to disguise it.
The love in my heart for you
 knows you, and pulls away your mask.

I feel the warmth of your desire
 to make life easier for me. 
And I need you to know that each gesture 
  no matter how small..
Is a pillow for my aching body.
 and a lift for my sinking spirit.

I cannot tell you what I need,
 although you ask with resolve.
For I myself am unsure.
Because in this game, 
The rules are continually changing,
 making improvisation a necessity.

All I can say to you
  is what is within my heart.
To just be next to me--
Holding my hand
 and looking into my eyes.

Don’t try so hard to understand.
For there are times 
 that knowledge doesn’t matter,
 and will never change things.

I do not need a guide on a journey 
 where there is no map to follow.
But, I do need a companion
 to be along side of me--
Enjoying the good moments when they are here,
And sharing my pain 
 when the bad times come along.

That is all that is important.
That is all I need.
Just to have you.
That is all.
