title: "I Am Here For You"

I was a child when I first saw you
The sparkle in your eyes 
and your bright giggle 
drifted into my world,innocently
And bonded with my young life's desire
To find the ideal best friend

We soon became inseparable
Never leaving the other, 
In thought or in heart,
My secrets became yours
As yours did mine
Our days were filled with delight 
And our friendship established its foundation

When my childhood tragedies took their toll
You were there, by my side
Taking my hand and looking at me 
And you would say to me, gently
"Your eyes are closed, but I know your heart sees me" 

Hearing those words beckoned my eyes to open 
To look into the loving light of your friendship.
Where there was no room 
For storm clouds to gather

I look at you now and know
That if you had not been there for me Through all of my hard times
My eyes would still be closed, 
And my heart may never have opened

I look at you now and I need to know 
Where the storm is coming from 
that shadows your brow 
And wipes the sparkle from your eyes
Making me watch you slowly disappear
Into the thick fog surrounding it

I look at you now, and I need you to know That my eyes are wide open 
Along with my heart
Please allow me to help chase away the clouds
So that I can see my best friend again. -
