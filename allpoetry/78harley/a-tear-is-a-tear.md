title: "A Tear is a Tear"

A tear is a tear
I look into your eyes and see not a glimpse
  Of the river of emotion banked behind 
    Your floodgate 
Mine flow so freely
Attacking your solidity with violent hope
Of finding a crack in the wall
It took years for you to build

A tear is a tear
Can you not feel my moisture
  Extending a lubricant to your parched heart?
Can you not fall upon my gift
  Without fear of reprisal or pain?
Can you not allow me inside to help heal you?
Can you not try to forget?

A tear is a tear
You witness my emotions disrobing
And say naught, as I stand naked in front of you
but you cover me with your battle worn blanket
  You hold me in distant but smothering arms
     You let me feel the wetness of my tears
 as you remain detatched from a fear 
   that they may splash upon your heart

A tear is a tear
In the pool of water I have shed
I will look for your reflection
  Bending over to grasp my being with your lips
I will look at your want with mine
I will look deeply into your eyes
I will find the tears at the bottom of your dry well
And then I will guide you to their peace
