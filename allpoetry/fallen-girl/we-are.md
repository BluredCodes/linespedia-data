---
title: "We Are"
writer: "Fallen Girl"
slug: "we-are"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/23226-We-Are-by-Fallen-Girl"
---

we are the youth of america
a generation lost.
searching for the missing pieces
of the dreams that have been smashed
and shattered on the ground.

we are the children of disregard,
thoughtless words were whispered
behing closed doors.
invoking shame and depression
causing imeasurable loss.

we are the future,
struggling, striving, pushing ahead
finding our feet and embracing failure
no matter what the cost.

we are the youth of america,
recovering all that had been misplaced.
proving all disbelievers wrong
that we are no longer lost
but in all reality we've found our
wings...
