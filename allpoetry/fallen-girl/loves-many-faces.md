love is fleeting,
love is frightening,
love is confusing
and chaotic.

love is timeless,
love is agless,
love is ever-changing
and precious.

love is hard,
love is painful,
love is simple 
yet so complex.

love is undefinable and unmeasurable,
it comes and goes like the wind.
sometimes you never really know
what love is, until its vanished.
love is...
