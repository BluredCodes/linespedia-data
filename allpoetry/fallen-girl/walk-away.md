---
title: "Walk Away"
writer: "Fallen Girl"

I wake as the grey blue
light filters in through
my broken reality.
what is real and what
is not I can no longer
tell


When you left you shattered
my sense of reason
my sense of life and love.
I can no longer tell
night from day


When were you going to
come back and complete
me, when were you
going to release me
from your strangling
grip


I slip into the pain
and wish only for the
sun to break into the
shell that now binds 
me in its hollowness


When were you going to 
let me live again, when
were you going to let me
breathe again, let me go
let me be free


Stop swallowing me up
don't protect me anymore
let me be hurt, let me
feel love, let me reach
my arms out into the never ending
horizon


Unlock your leash 
let me become lost
let me fall
let go of my hand
and walk away.
please, never look
back...
