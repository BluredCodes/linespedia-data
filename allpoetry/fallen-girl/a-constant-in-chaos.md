title: "A Constant In Chaos"
writer: "Fallen Girl"

There is always a constant
in this never ending chaos
that we call life
hidden amoung the ruins
lost amoungst the junk
secrets shimmer like diamonds
thoughts shine like the sun
whispers blow through the
maze like a spring breeze
as youth passes by on the
lips of rosebuds
minutes tick by like
fading memories
as the light dims from
the darkening sky
as the clouds drift away
to stars and the
sun is replaced
by the silver moon
I become lost
intangled in the very
esscence of nothingness
the very being of emptiness
I merge with the stars 
and I become part of the
whole.
