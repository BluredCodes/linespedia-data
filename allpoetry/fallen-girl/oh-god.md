Oh God Why?
The little girl asks
Why do you not love 
me, Where have you
gone

Oh God Why?
Have you forsaken me
I'm in so much pain
can you not see my tears
Please God

Oh God Why?
Will you not save me
Will you not come
I'm Trapped and I need 
you

Oh God Why?
The little boy cries
My pain is great
I'm so small and
afraid

Oh God Why?
What have I done
What must I do
To make you love me
again

Oh God Why?
I scream I have
always been there
where were you, I need
you

Oh God Why?
Will you come
Will you save me
For you are to late
For I'am gone...
