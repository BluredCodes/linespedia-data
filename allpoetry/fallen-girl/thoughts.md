I met someone last night, thatswept me off my feet.Someone who made all my fears and pain fadeinto the background.Someone that for just a momentmade you disappear.He was beautiful, a smile thatcaptured and filled yourevery thought.He was funny and charming,and he could steal your breath with one look.Last night I was beautiful, and I was special.I was happy.I had almost forgotten what itfelt like to smilebut last night I wasreminded.lacey*
