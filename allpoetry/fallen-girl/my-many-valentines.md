---
title: "My Many Valentines"
writer: "Fallen Girl"

You were my first valentine
sweet, smart, young,
confusing and wonderful
you were my first.

You were my precious valentine
loving, supportive, caring,
never leaving my side
my precious valentine.

You were my pretty valentine
quite, simple, striking,
always dreaming
my pretty valentine.

You were my wild valentine
funny,charasmatic,adventurous,
full of new ideas
my wild valentine.

You were my bittersweet valentine
crazy, fun, beautiful,
i still miss you
my bittersweet valentine.


I've loved all my valentines
no matter how big or small
young or old
they left a lasting impression on 
my life, my heart, my soul.


lacey*
