catharsis
so fragile are you
pristine seraph kneeling in shallow pool
tears from Heaven lovingly scar you
yet you catch them on your tounge
tasting them like one thousand years before.
how quite you are
on this Beltane eve
when the labrithine depths
are bathed in the deepest hues of
fallen angel sorrow.
Release
solitude, a cold, dark fear
hidden in forgotten sanctuary
you, serene
decaying wings folded in bitter conceit
praying, one final Sabbath
how beautiful the night can be
you, draw your chalice close to
perfectly formed lips
the sun also rises only to see
you in perpetual slumber
most merciful sun
holding you in blind embrace
you, sweet seraph
this garden, you are home...
