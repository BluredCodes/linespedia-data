Fare thee well my fallen one
my circle of light 
has come undone
I taste sorrowed tears
from angel eyes
I bleed your pain
through my demise
fare thee well
my fallen girl
flags of isolation
now unfurl
I hold up high
your memory well
as I for you will
transverse this hell
through bloodstained soil
upon sacred earth
I will come back for you
sathriel of angelic birth.
