---
title: "Love's Story"
writer: "Fallen Girl"
slug: "loves-story"
source: "AllPoetry"

tears fall from my eyes
as i read your simple
yet striking words.
all i am left with
is silence, heart breaking
silence.
i will always love you,
i am  forever unable to
forget you.
in my heart you will
stay, the memories
of your face, your smile
your eyes are etched
eternally in my mind.
i will always need you,
my best friend you will
always be.
but it is plain to see
we will never 
forgive and forget 
the hurt runs
too deep.
the past is unchangable
and it is clear
we will never be free.

lacey*to him, i am truly sorry...

lacey-a soul free-
