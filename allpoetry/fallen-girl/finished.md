---
title: "Finished"
writer: "Fallen Girl"
slug: "finished"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

What do you want from me?
what is it that you need?
If you hate me so much
why can't you just let me
be?

You wish I would die,
well if that is the truth
I'll turn around and be
dead to you. 

hey, already
your prayers are coming true.


I can't spend my whole life,
making up for my mistakes
I won't spend my whole life,
trying to erase.

Fuck this
I can't do this anymore,
I've said I'm sorry
a thousand times before.
I quit, I'm done, finished.
you have survived me, you
said it yourself,
It didn't have to be this way,
My best friend you will always be,
but from now on you are nothing more
than memories.

lace*i wrote this about someone very close to me.(he knows who he is) you think that your words wont hurt me, you are the one person who i thought would never make me cry. well-you have.
