I walk slowly tonight
surronded by secrets,
pursued by questions,
haunted by the memories
of our past.

I speak carefully tonight
treading ever so lightly,
holding back the pain,
hiding the sorrow that
fills my voice.


I sleep restlessly tonight
dreaming of our broken future,
covered in guilty sweat,
plauged by the thoughts of
what will never be.

I wake broken tomorrow
wishing for change,
hoping for peace,
asking for a different ending
to our sad story.
