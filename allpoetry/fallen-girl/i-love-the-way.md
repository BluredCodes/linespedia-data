---
title: "I Love The Way"
writer: "Fallen Girl"

I love the way you love me
I whispered into the fragile
emptiness of the night.
As you walked away
from me under the
broken street lights
that sent down jagged
streams of yellow light
that seemed to illuminate
your face and make you
shine like an angel,
The wind tickled my
bare shoulders and played
with my hair, causing
me to shiver and turn cold.
Lost in the stars
and lost in the never ending 
silence of the night
I searched for you,
I searched for the meaning
of the hollowness in my heart.
I love the way you love me
is what I heard just above
the deafening roar
of the shattering moon,
as I turned and walked ever
so quietly into eternity.
