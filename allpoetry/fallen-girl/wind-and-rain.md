---
title: "Wind and Rain"
writer: "Fallen Girl"

The wind and rain
keep me locked 
in the hollow of
my soul
my pain swells and
engulfs me as
I wipe the tears
from my cheeks
strained I love you's
spill from my 
lifeless lips
flooding the morning
wiping away you
intertwining with the
shadows of my heart
masking my true emotions
my unreveling
face hiding all
that I have left
all that I hold dear
The wind and rain
drown out the
hum of humanity as
I become lost
in the fog, as
I travel away
from myself and 
my hurt, taking
only my tears 
with me.
