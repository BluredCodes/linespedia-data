title: "Beauty"
writer: "Fallen Girl"

Beauty is only skin deep
and you are ugly
and you are spreading your
filth, and disease
you are pulling at me
to sink into your depths
But I am beautiful
and I plan to stay
that way
Even if I have to rip
your icy hands from my heart
and spit in your face
But I am still Beautiful
and you will always be ugly.
