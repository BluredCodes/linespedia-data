title: "Empty"
writer: "Fallen Girl"

now you have gone, and I must long for you.ran away so far, since you left I cannot feelyour absence or recall your presence.although, I am sure you were here.seeking security and strength in others,to replace trust in you.independent I may seem, as I roam,never wanting to go home.never less, dependent upon fate, never wanting to return.causing chaos in the midst of calm,I turn closing eyes,becoming blind, deaf, numb, apathetic towards othersand life.one day becomes two then three and so on it goesone day becomes a week like dominoes.faded and distorted memories of what has happened.only one thing I knowone day I must go.not happy, but content, awaiting change,never wanting to wake from my sleep.all alone I cry, for no reasonthat I can recall, other than I feel empty,not missing you at all...
