why are you capable of leaving me
breathless
after all these years i still
don't know

your words will always hold me
captive
i am forever under their spell
a prisoner

I'm sorry I forsake you
I'm sorry that I left you
I'm sorry that I made myself feel better
by making you feel never good
enough

But i know you will shine much brighter that i ever could.
maybe thats why i was scared of you, and i know you were scared of me too.

but i miss you...

lacey*
