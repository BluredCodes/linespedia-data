---
title: "Time"
writer: "Fallen Girl"
slug: "time"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

you asked for time
how come you never
finished the sentence
you need time without
me

what do you want from me
you want me to wait
sit by the phone
cry myself to sleep
dream of you

do you still find me
beautiful, yesterday
i was your world
but today i dont even
exsist

i just need to be alone
you said, well than
leave me be, go off by
yourself. i dont need
you anymore...just
GO

walk away from me
it isn't forever you said
but you haven't reached for
my hand since you
left me in the cold

you said i needed you 
to be me, take care of
me, please
i said i need some time
and i finished my sentence
AWAY FROM YOU!
