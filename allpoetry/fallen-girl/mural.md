---
title: "Mural"
writer: "Fallen Girl"

touch this dying ember
how silent you look
underneath this decaying
earth

I found something I was not looking for
it had appeared suddenly on the floor
it was small and had collected dust
a memory, a piece of rust
I saw it first from my sleep
a part of her, for her I weep
and for me, this thought I keep
tears of pain I seep
I found nothing I was searching for
nothing was something and nothing more
a memory, a blood drop shed
part of you inside my head
pictured in grey, this dying hue
hardly a mural left, left from you
so, I paint this collage
as I hide behind this darkening fassad
my pallet of colors, in chaos swirl
it is finished, this mural of this fallen girl.
