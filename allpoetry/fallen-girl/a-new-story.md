---
title: "A New Story"
writer: "Fallen Girl"

I know your scared, because
I tremble at at the mere thought
of having found someone
who can take my breathe away
with one look.
Someone I'm comfortable with,
Someone who I could actually
be myself with.
All my old fears are returning,
my heart is preparing for the
worst,
But despite everything 
I still would risk it all
for the chance to try.
It has been an eternity since
I've felt this way,
But with one kiss you've captured 
my heart,
and left me spinning.
I want to run,
I want to hide,
Because I don't know if I can be hurt
again,
Finally I know how this story will end,
But I have to try for my 
happy ending...
