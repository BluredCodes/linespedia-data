I have an 
Alpine 3 disc changer 
In the deck 
Two amps and 
Two 12’s in 
The trunk 
And all my neighbors 
Know when I’m 
Within a block of 
My house 
Because I don’t 
Listen to music 
I experience it 
My car stereo 
Rattles every piece 
Of plastic in my car 
And today I drove 
Home with the 
Radio on mute 
So I could be 
Alone with my 
Thoughts 
Just me and 
My thoughts and 
Wind blowing in 
The windows then 
Out again 
I was waiting for 
That one brilliant 
Thought 
And I didn’t want 
The radio to 
Drown it out 
I realized I think 
Way too much 
About video games 
And sports 
And my children 
And women 
And every mundane thing 
In this world 
My restless mind 
Won’t let that one 
Brilliant thought 
Into the congested 
Thought Queue 
Maybe I have no 
Brilliant thoughts 
Maybe dolphins have them 
And we are too busy 
Making food chains and 
Putting ourselves on top 
To open up our minds to their 
Telepathic broadcast of 
All the universe’s secrets 
Or maybe they sit around 
Making fun of us 
Dumb simple creatures 
And that’s why they 
Always laugh when they see us 
Eh-Eh-Eh-Eh-Eh indeed 
I was alone 
With my thoughts and 
I realized that if I 
Said everything I thought 
Out loud 
Even I wouldn’t 
Hang out with my 
Crazy ass 
I drove and listened 
To the wind blow 
In and out the windows 
And for a moment there 
It was quiet 
My thoughts stopped 
I just listened to 
The air moving around me 
And at that moment
I had the most 
Brilliant thought 
I’ve ever had 
I am at my most 
Brilliant 
When I stop thinking
