---
title: "Have you been spotted?"
writer: "white chef"
slug: "have-you-been-spotted"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/41000-Have-you-been-spotted--by-white-chef"

Have you been picked out
Have you been seen through the crowds
Only your face remains hidden
your actions act as your looks
They the memory that remains
They the attraction that draws
out a sketch of hopes and dreams
and in those who admire.
Only ask yourself.
Have you spotted?
