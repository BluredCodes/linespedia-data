---
title: "Blind Fisherman"
writer: "white chef"
slug: "blind-fisherman"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

He casts out
in darkness.
He hears the hook
hit the waters surface,
he waits.
Time passes by his eyes 
but not does not register 
in these hollow sockets
until he tastes his first bite
and feels the pressure
up his stick.
The smell of success wafts up his nostrils
but this was to be shortlived as he lost sight of his target
