---
title: "Valentines"
writer: "white chef"
slug: "valentines"
source: "AllPoetry"

Love lives
revived for one day
in the hearts of all.
It fills those who lack 
the courage to come forward
and declare their love.
It brings romance 
to a boiling point
where some are bound
to get burned
but for most
the heat 
will thaw their cold hearts.
