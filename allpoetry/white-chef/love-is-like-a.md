Love is like a seesaw
Sometimes a high, sometimes a low
Love is like a lottery
A lucky few win every time
While the others keep trying and losing
Love is like a star
Sometimes you feel over the moon 
and then you plummet back to Earth
Love is like a light bulb
Spending an eternity being normal
Then for one second being brighter than ever before
Before your light goes out
