title: "Among my souvenirs"

After my holiday
I brought back a lot of objects.
One of them was my broken heart
After I had to leave my newfound love
Behind me.
Not enough room.
I also have one picture,
Which I cling to each night
As each moment with her
Falls into oblivion,
And so she is gone.
I guess what they say is true--
Holiday souvenirs always fall apart.
