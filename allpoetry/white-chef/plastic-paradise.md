---
title: "Plastic Paradise"
writer: "white chef"

All is yours
Yours is all
everyone seeks
a view of perfection
a glimpse of affection
Control of the controllers.
You are important
to those you control
and to yourself.
Yet life continues
and sometime
an end will come
to your 
plastic paradise.
