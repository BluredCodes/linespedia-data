---
title: "A tale"
writer: "white chef"
slug: "a-tale"
source: "AllPoetry"

A memory
played out in the memories
of those that wish to believe.
A story
which is never ending
yet always ends.
For those that create
create for those that
will carry on
what will not be forgotten.
