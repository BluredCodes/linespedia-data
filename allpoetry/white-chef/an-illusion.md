---
title: "An illusion"
writer: "white chef"
slug: "an-illusion"
source: "AllPoetry"

Pierces your mind,
you see only what you want to see.
To be
secure
to find
life beyond 
life.
More than time
More than passion
but the future
that exists just beyond 
your reach 
everytime you come close.
