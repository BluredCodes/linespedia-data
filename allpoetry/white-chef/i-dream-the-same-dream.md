---
title: "I dream the same dream"
writer: "white chef"
slug: "i-dream-the-same-dream"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/38581-I-dream-the-same-dream-by-white-chef"
---

I see the unseen 
I hear the unheard
I know things unknown
I have feelings unfelt
I love but I am not loved
I care but no-one cares
I help but I am helpless
I search but I never find
I follow but I am found alone
I believe in belief
I hope in hope
I share but they are not aware
I want but I am unwanted
I desire but I am undesired

But I still dream the same dream
