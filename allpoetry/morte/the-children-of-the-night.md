title: "The Children Of The Night"

The Children Of The Night 
With their big smile 
They loved life 
Running and jumping 
For their life 
As they cried on my shoulder 
Their hearts died 
My hands all bloody 
For I am the Ripper 
One by one I took their life 
With no pity 
I killed these creatures of the Night 

The Children Of The Night 
As they cried, they died 
Demons live within these children 
Blood came down from my eyes 
For I am the Ripper 
One by one they died 
So damn happy, I just can’t deny 
For I am insane… 

The Children Of The Night 
No longer were alive 
The Ripper took their life 
Now the demons are within me 
The longer I tried 
The longer I felt 
The pain within our hearts 
I took my life 
Just for The Children Of The Night…
