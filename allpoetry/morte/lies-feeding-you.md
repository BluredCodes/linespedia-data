---
title: "Lies Feeding You"
writer: "morte"

A life full of love 
Yet so much emptiness 
Never seem to get enough 
Never seem to get that much 
Always wanting something more 
Didn’t you know, what you have is enough 
Breathing lies like oxygen 
Dying slowly as you choke on them 

Lies is all you know 
Lies is all you’ll ever be 
Lies following every step you go 
As a hunter hunts its prey 
As a liar you will pay 
And for all that you’ve done 
You’ll get the prize of loneliness 

So cold, so cruel, so empty 
Lies feeding your soul 
Lies feeding your brain 
Lies slowly taking over pain 
Lies destroying all that is sacred 
All that is true, all that belongs to you 
Playing with lies, it gives you pleasure 
As a man play with his prostitutes 
Lies will destroy you 
Choking lies that’s how you’ll die…
