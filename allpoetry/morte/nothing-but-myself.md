title: "Nothing But Myself"

Sadness is all I felt 
Loneliness is all I had 
Defenseless is all I was 

Like the moonlight I wanted to shine 
Like a butterfly I wanted to live 
Like the angel I wanted to be 

I walked around like I was free 
Seeking for the angel I wanted to be 
I was lost with the wind 
And the angel came to me 
Took a minute to realize 
That this angel was nothing more but myself 
I got lost in my own reflection 
As I seek for it’s own perfection…
