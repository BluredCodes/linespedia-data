Possessed by a demon 
Strong, evil demon 
He wanted my life 
For I was his bride 

Selfish, horrifying creature 
My life he wanted 
My life he couldn’t have 
A strong desire we truly had 
A desire that lead us to temptation 
A temptation that lead us to destruction 
A destruction that lead us to chaos 
A chaos that lead him to his own death 

I looked at his eyes 
He was crying 
For he was dying 
Felt a terrible pain inside of me 
Felt like I could no longer hold on 
His life passing through my eyes 
And I did nothing but stare 
Fighting feelings within me 
Slowly begging for his life 
Slowly dying for mine 

Possessed by this demons 
A demon that I truly loved 
Died in me, I felt him in me 
For I was possessed 
I gave up life 
Just to give him another chance…
