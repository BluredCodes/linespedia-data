title: "Prisoner Within"
writer: "morte"

Being able to see what you see 
To touch what you touch 
To taste what you taste 
To believe in nothing but yourself 

Being able to see the world through his eyes 
What I’ve always dream off 
All that I want you have it within you 
Still I feel special for I am IN you 
I see you reflected on me 

Too late but I noticed 
I’m your prisoner within 
That one thing you won’t ever let out 
To embarrassing to let out of this cage 
Seems beautiful, so well done 
Yet I’m feeling so alone 
Can’t get out, I’m trapped 
So hopeless, so useless 

Holding onto what I have 
Being so transparent that’s how I am 
I see through you for I am blind 
Not able to see who I am 
Not able to see what I want to see 
Not able to be what I want to be 
Just your prisoner within.
