---
title: "Destroyed By Myself"
writer: "morte"
slug: "destroyed-by-myself"
source: "AllPoetry"

One day I’ll wake and my head will shake
From all the madness that remains
And poison will run through my veins
I will drown in the river made by the rain
And my life forever with chains
I’ll live with pain and my blood will simply drain

Thinking about yesterday
Can’t believe the life I lead
Wondering about it day by day
Can’t believe the lies I spread
Knowing myself very well, I have to pay

My life someone will take
And there will be many years to waste
My soul forever will ache
And my blood I will definitely taste
My feelings I will fake
And forever I’ll remain a mistake
