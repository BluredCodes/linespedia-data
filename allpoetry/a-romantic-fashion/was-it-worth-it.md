I truly did love that thought of thinking that someone could love me
Hold me
Care about me 
want me
Or was it all useless because it lead no where?
He broke my heart,but gave me happiness before
Maybe it would have been better to have never loved or hoped
Maybe it is better to never love 
As to avoid getting hurt
One day when my heart is healed i might look back and think,
Was it worth it?How was this written? I never write poems even this long, so I want to know how I did
