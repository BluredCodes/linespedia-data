---
title: "Him"
writer: "A Romantic Fashion"
slug: "him"
source: "AllPoetry"

I felt his hair
The texture so smooth
I felt his kiss
A romantic bliss
I felt his eyes looking at me
It was so innocent, so true
I felt his hand in mine
There was a deep connection
I felt like I was in love
It felt so good
