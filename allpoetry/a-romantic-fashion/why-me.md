I want to die
I want to die
Please don't make me cry
I'm of 13 years and sick of shedding tears
I wish to die everyday yet people simply think I'm a-OK
I told THEM how I feal,that I just didn't know how to deal
I said,'I need some help and guidence too,especially when im feeling blue.'
Instead they said,'Your so dumb,go to an insanalsilum!'
I forgot to tell you who I call THEM
There the two people who I see everyday
The ones I call mom and dad
The ones who make me so sadhey there im 15 now and i found this a so here it is
