Insanity weaving in
A sound mind merging out
What is it that we battle within,
That gives us ultimate doubt?

What are we fighting?
What steals away our peace?
This thing we find so exciting,
This thing so that is so hard to release.

Is then an end?
A way for the simple minded to comprehend?
