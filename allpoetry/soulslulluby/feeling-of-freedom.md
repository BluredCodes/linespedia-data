Running away from everything
That crosses my pathway
I’m like an unstable swing
With ropes that are near fray

Won’t some one lend a hand?
Won’t some look in my direction?
I’m hanging here by a strand
And I don’t think I can stand any more rejection.

Please, someone look at me.
All I want is to feel free.
