Death surrounds them
Taking away their breath 
They shall be condemn 
Drowning in clouds produced by the meth

Stabbing their arms
Leaving behind their tracks
Throwing themselves in the way of harms
Persuading them it’s only to relax

Lies of denial 
A self trial
