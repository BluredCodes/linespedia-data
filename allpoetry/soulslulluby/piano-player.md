At the piano I watch you 
The way your fingers stroked the keys
How smoothly your fingers flew 
Playing them with expertise 

Your mistress you called her
And I could see the feeling in your eyes
I was lost in a blur 
As I envied to be mistress that you prize

Your perfect hands touching me
As I became your mistress instead of she
