Heads rolling on the ground
Hearts being stomped on
There isn’t a single sound
The people are all withdrawn.

The TV’s are blaring
The children are crying
But no one can stop staring
Thinking of those dying.

The world, it seems is coming to an end
There is no more need to pretend.
