With life spinning around,
Images blurred,
And hardly more than murmured sound
It’s hard to realize what has occurred.

We find ourselves in a state
Where we can finally believe
That because of this hate
It has brought our US together to grieve.

To all of those who lost family or a friend
Our thoughts are with you to no end.
