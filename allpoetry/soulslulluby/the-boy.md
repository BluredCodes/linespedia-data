When I stare into those brown eyes
I get tangled in magic… 
Listening to your sweet voice
I just have to sigh.

Butterflies invade my stomach 
With your simply soft touch…
When you smile at me,
I can’t help giggling.


I look forward towards seeing you…
I daydream about our kiss…
I jot down poetry… 
I scribble your name…


I carry on like a school girl,
But I really can’t help it…
You make me feel special;
Among other things.
