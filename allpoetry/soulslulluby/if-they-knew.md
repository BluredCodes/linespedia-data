Gracefully gliding 
Beauty dances on her toes
She has come out of hiding
To dance to a bit she has composed.

It has been a while since she’s been seen 
Shy obviously so
But ready to make her debut at age eighteen 
Without a mistake in her elegant flow.

What a stunning girl Beauty has grown into,
Only if they all knew…
