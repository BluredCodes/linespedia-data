title: "To the odds"

A whisper in the wind
It came then was gone
Like the life of a man who sinned
It was forgotten at the first sign of dawn

No remembrance of the spoken expressions
Faded reverberations in the background
They were worldly possession
That beautiful sound

Lost to the gods
And unbelievable odds
