title: "Musical love"

Piano player with your perfect hands
Wisk me away with your beautiful notes
Leave me to dance within your harmonic plans 
And quiver to those classical quotes

Your musical love
Takes my breath away
Sends my soul to a place above
As your fingers gently play

Indulged in your passion
Feeling musical compassion
