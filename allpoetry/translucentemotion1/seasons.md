When seasons intertwine,
a chill runs down my spine

The once green leaves-
are soon turned brown with ease.

The rapidly flowing rivers
Are then turned to icy slivers.

My heart is active and content
only to have it end,without the slightest hint.

As the coldness comes,late at night
I curl up in my blanket tight.

But I keep in mind 
Summer will be back in due time.
