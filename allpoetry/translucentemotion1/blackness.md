It is a darkness that entraps your mind.
It's a still calmness daring you to be fearless-
To not be afraid of your minds conceptions.
Yet as your mind wanders it is like the darkness of hells powers
are released upon your soul, planting itself like a bad seed
in the very depths of your soul.
Determined to turn your mind against you and cause you pain and regret.
The saddest thought is it is caused by your own minds disobedience.
