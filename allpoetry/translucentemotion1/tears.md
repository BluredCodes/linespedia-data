Salty tears run down my face.
Are they started by rejection, Critism, or my own lack
of self-worth?
They run down my face in steady streams like chilling secrets
revealing themselves to the world.
Determined to be recognized.
They move slowly,warm and steady like blood.
As they absorb into my skin I grow stronger and stronger on 
the outside.
But weaker and weaker on the inside.
