Everyone sees my shell and assume im depressed.
They see my anger and frustration and call me mean.
They see my cheerful side and think i'm high.
What side would you rather see, cause all three make up me.
So if you don't agree-well you don't know me.
Im not depressed, I'm not happy, Im not mad-Im just me.
