It is the thought that defys all boundaries
of the human mind.
Racking our brains as to when out time
will come.
It is the very thought of "who" will steal
our souls-the burning embers of hell or
the so called clouds of eternity.
The real question is when-
We all struggle to find the answer.
Will we wait out our time and live our lives
to the fullest?
Or will we get it over with, and cut our lives
short in agony of our tourtured and oppressed souls.
What happens if our minds can't continue will they
turn demented?
Will the thought of death change them into monsters of
themselves, emprisoning them into the darkest chambers of the mind?
They will then strike- harming whoever "tries" to help, for they don't
know what exactly they are doing it is just instinct to 
push others away when you yourself cant handle the thoughts 
racing through your OWN mind.
