If Hope could wash a face in Fear

And brighten to our thirst,

If Truth were but an artless light

And darkness was her church,

If Love was shameful to sore need

And Hate was to be nursed;

Then, vain would be that treasured dram

Abel's gift posed first,

As Taste would be that only damned

Flaunted by the Cursed.
