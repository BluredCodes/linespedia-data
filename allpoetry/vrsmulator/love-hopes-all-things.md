L ove is the best of God for Man
  where Justice, Faith and Mercy stand;
O f faith was sent from far above,
  for Need was mingled with His blood;
V isiting children with one call -
  An offer of mercy to us all
E ntirely without fee.

& 

H ope is the answer to our goal,
  and a sure anchor for the soul;
O f Love unfeigned oft' sheds red tears,
  by Faith aflame casts out all fears;
P ublishing Peace 'midst raging seas,
  promise of Life which will not cease -
E ternities without end.
