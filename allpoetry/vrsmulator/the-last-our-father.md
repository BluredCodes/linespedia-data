Our Father of - the turning wheels -

Years within - and days without,

Spread your glory - through the four winds -

Light'nig fast - and with a shout.



Your Kingdom come - with Will redressed -

In the valley - of death's test;

Canaan can - rebuild again -

And saints - with vigor rest.



Give us this day - the Early Rain -

The Latter - then the Harvest;

Perform all your words - in one day -

No longer planting patience.



And -lead us not - by the viper's nest -

Nor near - the spider's web:

'Stead, hold our chin - and kiss our brow -

When the Beast - his ax - does wet.



For - your kingdom is - our reward -

Your Glory - is our hope,

Your power is - eternal life -

Eternity - our scope.
