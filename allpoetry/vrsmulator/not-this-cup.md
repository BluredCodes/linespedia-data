---
title: "Not This Cup"
writer: "vrsmulator"

A rosary of wines worry on our lips
An abacus of lies tallied into chits
To us before we kneel, pray & genuflect;
Kissing Truth so soft only our reflect-
ed charms persuade
us to be
real.

!  !
