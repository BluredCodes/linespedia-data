(Rev. 13:10 & 14:12)



Sweet as honey to my tongue,

And as pure, are these words

Bitter to my belly's keep

And its lodging hire;



"Here is the patience of the saints" -

Once the taste of grace descends,

Life is fleeting in our wounds

While Judgemnet is everlasting;

And,"Here is the patience of the saints" -

From glory first to glory last,

Conscience is beating at our breast

Instead of Judgement's blasting;



So, standing first to testify

Against my heals, I will thirst

After Love's amber dawn

With a lasting fire.
