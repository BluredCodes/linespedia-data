(Luke 14:28)



Teach me the value of my cross,

Teach me the virtue of my thorn -

Lord, oft' I thank you for your own,

Yet praise departs when I am torn.



Instruct my heart now tear by tear,

Reminding me of each your own;

How, drop by drop, your love did yearn,

And how by blood your peace was sown.



Lord, I've counted costs like bargains,

Charging desires against your pain;

Never chasing, never daring

Greater works for the Kingdom's gain.



Enlarge the boarders of my heart

And send the Word to fallow ground;

Breaking up the doubt filled places,

Crushing to dust all idols found;



Grant me Burden's faithful stuards -

Peace in the midst of ev'ry storm;

Teach me the value of my cross,

Teach me the virtue of my thorn.
