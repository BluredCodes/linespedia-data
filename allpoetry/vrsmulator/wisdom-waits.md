My ear is only half attunedTo merry things and cheerful boons,And sets itself as if by SpriteOf variable appetiteTo magnify some hope to chanceAll else which comes by circumstance.Though anguish, anger - all that's blue,Might over-hunger for some proof;Hope quells some fear with beliefMixed with something of relief;And, reverence pends most my doubts;Upholding faith, casts lies out,Until the facts renew againBoth gratitude and Amen.
