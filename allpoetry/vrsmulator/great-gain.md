Oh Lord, my life, crown my heart
And hold me in your eye;
Oh Lord,My God, create a new start
And my longing satify
Death to pride and vanity,
All hell-black greed of gains;
So, hope replace calamity
Setting this heart to praise!

Oh Lord, my God, light my eyes -
So fill me with your life;
Lord, my death is of grace,
As sure your love does make
This life to sing all your fame,
This heart to share your pains;
And all my days found in peace
With justice, faith and mercy.

So Lord, my death is not strife
As once my pride proclaimed:
'Stead, sweet Christ, it is small price
You turn into great gain.
