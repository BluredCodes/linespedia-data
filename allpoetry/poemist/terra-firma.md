Air: filthy, foreign particles of waste
destroying our lungs

The beaches: perdition
sea gulls slain by spills
From metal giants off the shore

What have we done?

Asphalt trails that mar the landscape
with garbage-laden shoulders
What a mess we made!
Return the land to the Indians

They knew how to use it

Let's move somewhere else...
Perhaps to another planet
But what if someone's already there
Living peacefully?

Run them off!
We've done it before.
