title: "Every Time I Fall"

I thought I had found the right oneTo open this heart and not want to runBut I guess you’re mostly like the others.You took my heart and said goodbyeAnd I can’t understand just whyYou took so much and left me here with nothing.Every time I fall, I tryTo come up with the how and whyCan’t believe it happened againI guess it’s finally sinking in.Every time I fall, I sayI’ll get back up another dayIt always happensEvery time I fall.If you should ever want to see meAnd try to be like we used to beI’d take you back without hesitation.But until then, I’ll just say goodbyeAnd give this heart yet another tryTo find someone like you to love me.Every time I fall, I tryTo come up with the how and whyWould love to find true love againPerhaps this time I’ll get to win.Every time I fall, I sayIt hurts, but then I’ll be okayIt always happensEvery time I fall.
