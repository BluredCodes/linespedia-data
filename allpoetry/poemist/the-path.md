I summoned Heartache to walk with me
O’er rocky paths and amber trails;
And once in league to his desire,
Would feel no more the wintry gales.

And Sadness followed me as well
Along dark roads and scattered leaves;
I shook the hand held loft to me,
Unaware of the trap she weaves.

And once in the company of Fear
I rested only to wake in lies;
Abandoned hope of walking on,
But shared my heart with vacant cries.

And Love would only glance my way,
No interest in what I had to say.
Would only whisper in tones of gest
The motives that live in all the rest.
