Am I awake?I pray this is not a dream.I pinch my self.I punch my self.Its not a dream.Thank god.We Danced.We laughedWe went to drop you off at home.I kissed you.2 months later.You told me you ddon't wantto see me.A tear.I don'twant to argue with you.I just want you to be happy.If I did argue........There is no one sided relationship.You had a heart of ice.All I do is ligh in bed.And think of you.God I miss you.Now all I can pray for...........Is a dream.
