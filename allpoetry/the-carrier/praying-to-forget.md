I knew a girl.
A long time ago.
She cared about me.
I cared about her.
We had gone out for about 3 months.

But.....
In a 5 minute phone call it was all over.

Ever sense that day ive tried to forget.
Just like she did.
She used to be the first thing I thought of..
And the last.

All i want is that feeling when you know someone out there cares about you
for you.......
