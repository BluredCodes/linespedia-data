Im alawys alone......
 Its so quite.........
  I need some real friends....
Friends who don't take advantege of what you have.
Friend who actual try to help with your problems.
Intead of making new ones.
My so called friends only make me feel worse.

And thats why I can only trust
Me.............Your comments are of great value.
