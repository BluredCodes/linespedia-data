Please help.
Please God.
I dont Know.
I dont Trust.
I dont trust any one.
I dont Want to be hurt.
I dont care.
I just dont care any more.
You will Forget about me.
In your thoughts.
And In your heart.
When I die.
When I die no one will care.
No loved one.
I cry.
This........
This is how it feels.
When Angels Deserve to Die.............
