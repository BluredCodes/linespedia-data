I need to breath.
People tell me I need to forget.

I cant.
Thats like forgeting to exhale.

I need her back.
Saturday was the best night of my life.

To see you smile.
Your face.

To hear your laugh again.
That made the waiting all worth while.

I thought I was over you.
But im not.

Nathan made me belive in love at first sight.
I think he was right.

Now Im asking you.
Please let me breath.Your comments are greatly appreacheated
