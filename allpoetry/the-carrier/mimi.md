Last night I had a dream about you.
In this Dream I sat down right be side you.
And it looked like every one was having fun.
The kind of felling I waited so long.

Don't stop.
Come a little closer.
As we jam the rythm gets stronger.
Theres nothing wrong with just a little fun.

We were dancing all night long.

The time is right to put my arms around you.
Your feeling right you wrap your arms around me too.

But suddenly I feel the shinning sun.

Befor I knew it this dream was all gone...........
