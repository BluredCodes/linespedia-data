What is it you want from me? 
You have your man 
And I've already taken my stand 
You've showed not signs of response 
So I walk away 
And you say stay 

I've never let you down 
But you've always made me frown 
While your around me 
I'll never be free 

How was it that I went from 1 to 3? 
Was it because I wanted to be free? 
Did you need a crutch? 
You still do 

Whenever you've flown astray 
I've helped you find your way 
Back to that place, that quiet tree 
Up and Up where I wanted to be 
There you keep the others, 
Who are just like me 

Will I ever be free?A year or so ago,
