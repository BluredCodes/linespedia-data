Senses deprived
I was feeling so alive
What happened?
I became blind to your needs
Your signals and calls 
I couldn't heed
As if I didn't know where I was going
Or what I was saying
Oblivious
To the game that you were playing
Oh how I could've been yours
And you could've been mine
Together we could've been
Passing the time
But it is I who was blind
And now it is eating me from the inside
Eventually I will swim to your safe shores
With the incoming tideI wrote this after finding out one of my best friends was/is madly in love with me.  I have yet to do something.
