---
title: "Riven"
writer: "RiVeN"

[...Torn apart and split...]


[...You could say my life is full of shit...]


[...But I prefer to think of it differently...]


[...Looked down upon by my peers...]


[...My eyes are tired from shedding these tears...]


[...I can change my future but I cannot change my past...]


[...A past I wish to leave behind and forget...]


[...Give me a new chance...]


[...Surrounded by ignorance...]


[...I pay no attention to their arrogance...]


[...A venerable member of this race I am not...]


[...As I sit on my front porch looking out...]


[...And watching the world before my very eyes rot...]


[...There is no longer anything I can do I have given up the fight...]


[...It has come to its final height...]


[...I am not the one who is Riven...]


[...I am but a member of a world that is Riven...]


~~July 20, 2001~~An Oldie
