I have spent my whole life
Looking for something I thought
Would never be found
I have spent minutes in eternity
And I can mimic the sound

One that does ring true
I would take out my heart
And give it to you
In a moment that would seem so right
Now I wonder if that moment is tonight

Looking deep inside of you
Your eyes are of a different color
They speak of a different hue
Looking at a face with soft skin
As an old love dies
A new love will begin
