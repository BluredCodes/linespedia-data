title: "Echoes"
writer: "RiVeN"

Twitching in my ears
Hearing voices in my mind
Bound to loose sense of space
Bound to loose sense of time

Echoes of our love
Beat in my heart
Echoes of our love
That is what's tearing me apart

Thoughts now turn to dust
And my eyes close
Your hair is the color of the sun at dusk
And the echoes of our love
Wilt like a rose
