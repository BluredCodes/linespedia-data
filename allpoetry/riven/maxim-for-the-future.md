Sell your soul
Tumble down the mainstream's rabbit hole
Manifest your thoughts and dreams
And welcome to the machine

Everyday you pour your heart into your work
For some fat cat on the hill
If you wish to make a dollar, do not shirk
Given the bare essentials of what you need
In you the government has planted the future's seed

Don't dare ask what it's all about
Rise like a Phoenix and shout
What you see is (not) what you perceive
Having been told what to believe

"Workin Hard-- The People Lead The Way!"
The maxim for the muture
This is what they say
Slowly drudging
Emotionless
Like a machine
Life isn't as good as it seems

Flying cars and colonies on Mars
All just delusions from the past
Mere peasants workin hard for minimum wage
Rich & wealth is the unreachable rage

Depleting the sources we only have one home
Dreams are gone citizens are grown
[War constantly raging and blowling each other to bits
Have we reduced ourselves to such pieces of shit?]

Friend, this life isn't what it seems
Manifest your thoughts and dreams
And welcome to the machineEssential listening: Queensryche's "Operation: Mindcrime"
