---
title: "Glass Lies"
writer: "RiVeN"
slug: "glass-lies"

You were once the object of my desire
The one thing I wished to ever obtain
And now that I had you
You are driving me insane

It seemed like we would end every day with an argument
Never choosing the same side
Do you find it odd
That with each other
We went on this emotional ride

I hear your echoes throughout the night
It seemed as if you never put out the fire
It seems for a while you still were
The object of my desire
Hearing your voice ring through my ears
The memories tear at my heart 
Damn my eyes with sadness are beginning to sear

Summoning up spirits I believed to be dead and gone
I can see my mistakes
I can see where it is I went wrong
But our lies to each other were so clear
It was almost as if we were looking through glass
A glass that would eventually break
A heart that is cold
An impostor
A fake

Is that what I once was?
And that I hopefully never will again be
"Never reliving my past"
It shall become my new creed
One that I will adhere to and abide by
But I will constantly have you on my mind
But never again by my side
Until the day that I dieOnce again I did this off the top of my head so I'm not too sure about it
