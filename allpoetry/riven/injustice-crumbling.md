Justice
Why have you failed me my dear?
I thought you protected those who were right
Those who may live in fear

Society has taught you otherwise
It has taught you a different set of rules
And now society is using you
As one of its tools

Breaking free of the mold from which you live in
Is something you cannot do
Or is it something you merely wish not to do?
Do you fear that they might not accept you
That they might throw stones at your ideas

Manipulative
Putting the wrong people in the wrong place
You have given yourself a bad name
And a bad face

Injustice is what you have become
Taking over your mind and your soul as a whole
Crumbling down do you never see the light?
The light I once tried to shed on your dreary world
You found another way out
And now your cries cannot be heard
For it already too late
