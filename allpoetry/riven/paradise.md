Irony; the difference between what we expect
And what comes true
If a dream is only in my mind
Then why is it I have found myself in a new place and time
If it is only two dimensional
Then why am I feeling
Things I have never felt before
Why has the window shut
And opportunity has cracked the door

Is it ironic that the dream world in my mind
Is the same vision you held at one time
Is this world in my head dark or bright
Is it alright if I give up the fight?

I am [dreaming] in a world
One of no questions or rules
The gods of my mind are battling a bloody duel
No temple or shrine
This world does exsist [in my mind]

It is locked and you have the key
Open your eyes so that you may see
That you still share this dream world
Why and try to deny a vision so true
One in eternity spent with you

Paradise is not just a word but a state of mind
One you must achieve
One you must be able to perceive
Wipe your eyes free of tears
And spend with me the rest of our years
