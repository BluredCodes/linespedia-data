An arrow
Shot from a distance

Sent to my very heart

My Mind

At sixes at sevens

Over the thought of you

My thoughts

Running circles through my head

Waking me from my sleep

The very thought of loosing you

Saddens me as I begin to weep

Thought to heart

My heart flows from my pen

My eyes close

And the lights dim

Winter rains fill the nightsky

I wonder how and when we might

Be able to see eye to eye

Running dry and out of ink

Should I loose hope?

And let my feelings sink?

I will try and persevere

Loosing you would be my only fear

The arrow

Piercing my very heart

Pulling me two seperate ways

Pulling me apart

The first step

Is being able to communicate 

The hours pass and the skies grow dark

It is getting late

Writing my thoughts by candlelight

Struggling with my emotions

Putting up a neverending fight

Pushing my chair in

I am finally done

Looking out to a new dawn

And a rising sun
