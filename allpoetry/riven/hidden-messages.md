John woke up and poured himself a cup
Cold stale brew
The ideal way to wake up
If there is a monkey wrench in the stew

A quick stop at the library
Need to find todays top story
And revel in the rats almighty glory
Newspaper; biggest source of the news
Telling the stories
Of how we are raped and abused

Still sippin at his coffee as he flips through
Page after page marking in red
"Ideal America is Dead"
He mutters in a low voice to the young librarian
"And how do you know of this?" She asked
"Because every American takes a chance
We are walking on glass"

It seemed as if he was marking only certain letters in red
"Hidden messages can be found.  If you look hard.
Not close.  Because if you look too close.  You'll wind up dead"
The young librarian rolled her eyes and went back to work

She was a hollywood star who fell from the scene
The irony of the situation was she never became famous
As she was promised 
Just like she was promised the green

The next day she went to her job
And picked up todays story
Headline on the frontpage:
"Conspiracy Theorist Killed In A Battle that was Gory"
And there was John's picture

He looked too close.
