What once alive
Now been slayed
What once was breathing yesterday
Choked today

...Disillusion...

Was it a love or a game
One whose purpose was to drive me insane
It was all an illusion 
I was just caught up in the midst of confusion

...Disillusion...

This game that you call life
You see no wrong you only see right
But at the end of my tunnel you saw
You saw....

WHAT DID YOU SEE
These games you are toying with me
Tapping into my head

...Wake up...

Ahhhhh nightmare I've grown to love
Soring high my mind flies above
Anger is the least of my worries
As time and the past scurry

This illusion?
Disillusionhad to get it out
