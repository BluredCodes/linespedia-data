It's been a while

Since a situation of this kind

Has come up in the family

Yes

It has been a long time

The votes have been counted and tallied

In favor of the family

Against John

They consider punishing him

For doing very little possibly no wrong

"He is hindering the growth of the family

He brings nothing to the table money wise"

The grandfather points out

"This is not a democracy

But a tyranny run by a system of old guys"

Paul shouts trying to defend his blood

He should have known

Uncovering the past was an unwise choice

And now the ground will forever become John's eternal home

The bright lights of Hollywood and Beverly Hills dim

As a new breed takes shape

And new tragedies begin

Not even the mirrors see the true citizens

Maybe that is why they are vanity mirrors

They reflect what has been done in the past

And what is in store in the future near


The End?
