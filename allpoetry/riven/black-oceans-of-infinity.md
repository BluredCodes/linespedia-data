title: "Black Oceans of Infinity"

So many thoughts
As deep as the sea
Drowing in a black ocean of infinity
Whispering in my ears
Its scent is what led me here
Emptying my head of its needs
My mind a voyeur of the world
A good view
I can see the planting of the seeds
A new order to life
One with no wrong decisions
Only right
Painting a vivid picture in my head
Waking up in the middle of the night
Do the same thoughts plague you in bed
A lifesaver come to me in the sea
A raft
One made of clear words
As if my mind has been graphed
The only crewmate I wish to find
One on the edge of revelation
One that I can say is more divine
But until then I travel alone
Searching for the right spot
Searching for a new home
Do not fear kin
My journey is about to begin
You must stay here and not loose hope
In you there is good and right
I am searching
For the same thoughts that plague you at night
I am searching for the same
Thoughts that plague you at night
In you there is goodwill
Life is a battle going uphill
I intend to find a way down
