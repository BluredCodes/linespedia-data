The population mimics the name
The delinquents are the players in the game
Exiled from society
"Uncapable of sobriety"

Out of the ashes
And up from the ground
Welcome to Nowhere Town
Home to procrastinators galore
But we are capable of so much more

Sayin we live our live Catch 22
But what does it matter?
It's all the same to you
An age old cliche
We are not what you perceive
We are not mainstream paper machet

A population of six million
A metropolis thriving
Into our future we are diving
I intend to crack the mold

Population: 5,999,999damn stereotypes
