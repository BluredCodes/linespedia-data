"The human mind
Can only possess certain powers
At a certain time
What one may perceive at night
May be different by day
When the mind is asleep
Your subconscious is at play"
The doctor began

His entire life was devoted
To the study of time and dreams
And the life he lived was one
Or so it seems


"The mind can only do so much
When the body is at rest
But when the two are combined
It is like an ongoing contest
There is a battle going on inside"
He opened the door to his car
"Hop in, I need to take you on a ride"

The world outside my window
Seemed like an abyssmal blur
And in his mind something began to stir
"On the topic of nightmares
Nobody thinks about the day
They only count nocturnal
When your subconscious is at play"

I had no idea where he was driving
But something evil was thriving
He locked the doors and continued
"People are constantly wanting more
Their dreams they do not care to analyze
Soon we could be one with the celestial skies"

We were at the waterfront
And he drove onto the dock
I looked into his eyes
And saw that it was a devil in disguise
"Nightmares-can drive a man insane
The true nightmare is reality
And I want to wake up"

He crashed the car into the water
And he and I both woke up from the nightmareI was originally going to title this "The Nightmare That is Reality" but I didn't
