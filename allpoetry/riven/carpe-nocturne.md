Flash
One second and it's fone
Is it just me
Or does the dark of night
Seem unbearingly long

Wake up
From this nightmare
Staring into pitch black
The unreality of the situation
Is something I cannot bear

Cold sweat
Trickeling down my face
Staring into the nocturnal space
I know it gets bad
When the dreams effects my everyday life
Maybe a change of scenery
Can do the trick
Because these dreams are making me
Mentally Sick

Am I becoming one with the night?
As the sun goes down
In my eyes it is a beautiful sight
