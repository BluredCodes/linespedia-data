title: "Old Dog, New Tricks (Sudden Silence)"

Born into a world of sound
Raised outside of isolation
Happiness- his only consolation
Stumbled upon an underground world
Sudden Silence
No more sound

Exposed to a new realm and light
Changed his outlook on life
A world of sex, drugs, and lies
And run by unmentioned men
A rookie given a gun to take home
Drink away his sins
And harden his bones

Six months now fitting in
Corruption at some time has to begin
And what better time than now?
A promotion possibly in the works
The clock is counting down and starting to slow
Effects of the job are beginning to show

"Boy don't get cocky
These delusions cookin in your head
Could have you wind up dead"

--pay no attention to them
jealousy can turn a man insane
workin hard is all part of the game
go for the cold I can see it in your eyes--

Success like a diamond was hard to find
But it would come in a matter of time
Word got to the top and it made them sick
They wanted to show what happens
To old dogs who learn new tricks
Shots rang out in the middle of the night
Sudden Silence 
No more soundOne thing we should've all learned from Icarus; you fly to high you get burned.

One note: The passage in the hyphens (--) is supposed to be the characters conscience
