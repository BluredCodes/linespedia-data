---
title: "The Beach"
writer: "RiVeN"

Sun hanging high overhead
Thoughts and dreams
Pop in and out of my head
Spectacles shade my view of the world
Spectacles shape my view of the world

An ocean big and blue
My heart is aching for you
Paying no attention
To the words being said
My hunger for love and enlightenment has been fed

A short burst of joy
Can last a lifetime
In a world of sad and gloom
In a world full of adults
I am a boy

I wish to view
You with my own eyes
If I have not been paying attention I apologize
I left my mind at the beach
While dreaming of you
Of the stars and the skies

When I look through 
The looking glass
The only thing I see is you
This place may only exsist in my head
I'm sorry if I have not been paying attention
The only things I've been listening to
Is my heart beat
And the words you have said
I left my heart at the beach
But it is within your reachThe spectacles in this poem have two meanings: Sunglasses, and amazing things I have seen
