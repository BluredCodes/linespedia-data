Ring
Pick up the phone
Bring the fear and the madness
Home
Paying for your sins
This time you cannot lie
Wind muttering thoughts
About how you might die

Speaking in foriegn tongues
Speaking dumbly in voices
On the path of life
You have various choices

But when the skies turn black
At twelve
Plunged into darkness
Into Madness we delve
Open your arms wide
Find that place in which you confide

A single crow flies
And signals your doom
At the opposite end of high noon
Ride alone
Extra parties only jeopardize
The violence you shall soon condone
Leave behind your kids and your wives
It is better to loose one life
Then all of those helpless lives

Become one with the ground
Soul lost
Dimentia found
Do you speak in voices of more
The subject of old folklore
Open your arms wide
And welcome the dark one's rules
