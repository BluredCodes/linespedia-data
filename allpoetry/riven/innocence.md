When was that time
That seems so long ago
When I had no worries
And when monsters were my only foe

Is my innocent lost
And never to be found
Or merely just tucked away for the time being
The world through a different pair of eyes I was seeing
A vivid imagination
And adventures were abound

When was that time
When I didn't know lust or need
My mother's soothing voice
I would quickly heed
The time I was afraid of the other sex
When dealing with them I could not keep a straight face
Tell me, what happened to that place

When was that time?
