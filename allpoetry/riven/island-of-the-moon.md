Come with me love
Grab my hand
Let us escape the world
And watch it become a grain of sand
Run away to Saturn or the moon
Does it matter of we take forever
Or if we get there soon
Escape our problems
And leave it all behind
Living in eternity
Flowing in time
The moon will be our island
Sanctuary Home Haven
Baby your kiss is what I'm cravin
Just you and I alone
Together in our celestial home
You are a heavenly body
One with the sky
I see you
And my heart will no longer lie
Run away with me 
Race to the moon
And let love roam freeJust a quick little something I came up with the other day when I was bored in class
