A living medical mystery
Stumped them all
A troubled future on this man would befall
Living his life as a mystery and a lie
A gift dropped from the sky
Living his life behind a mask
Keeping everything a secret
Was a great task
Artificial Intelligence
Born in a lab
Knowing he isn't real
Is driving him mad
Memories put on a microchip
No such thing as childhood
Created to do no wrong
Created to only do good
Now he wants to live
Thinking it's all a bad mistake
And he wants to forgive
The authorities won't let him move on
They are now realizing what they did wrong
He should've led a sheltered life
One inside
At least then he would not resist
He would not fight
Born into slavery
Unborn in paradise

"Shouldn't they be free
We modeled them after ourselves
To deny our brethren
Would be like being locked in a confining cell"
The fight may never end
Until then we are trapped
In a world we made our own
A technological hell
