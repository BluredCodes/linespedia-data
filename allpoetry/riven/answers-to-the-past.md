Looking
Waiting
Silently praying
For the day that'll come
My future shall come and that is what I fear
The ghosts of my past
And the present and sanity
Two things I hold to me dear
All of this nonsense happening in the world
What is the point of all of this calamity?
Can our answers be linked to the past

Watch it
Grasp it
Let go of the things you cannot hold onto
Some things you cannot cherish forever
Some you can
Love indeed is one of those dangerous endeavors

All good things must come to an end
Unfortunately my advice does to
You may heed if you want
Pay no attention to me you may
But do not go the wrong path
Do not go my wayFollowing a person.  Idolizing them.  The last paragraph?
