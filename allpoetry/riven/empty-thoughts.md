The soft winter rain
Taps on my window
And beckons for me to come outside
But I do not play that game
I'll stay inside
You may taunt my ears
But I am retracing my steps
And going through the years

People may say they were a waste
But I fear for my future
It is tangible
Something I can see and taste

Grey covers a sky
That at one time was blue
My fond memories I bid you adieu
I stayed on one path
For too long
And now the oppotunity is gone

I could write you a sonnet or a verse
My feelings for you I kept inside
And felt like I was going to burst
Rafting down a river of truth
Emptying into a lake of lies
Grey covers a sky
That at one time was blue
I bid you my love adieu
