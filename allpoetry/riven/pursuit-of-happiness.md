What you may believe
Maybe different from what the government may perceive
What you may say is right
They'll say it's wrong
An uphill battle
Lasting horrendously long

Happiness is only defined by the law
If happy to you is being drunk off your ass
Then you my friend are walking on glass

Life, liberty, and the pursuit of something fake
The insanity of the situation is too much for me to take
"Happiness" is a mere joke
And the people do not get the punchline
And someunfortunately end up doing timeA short one.  But gets the message across nonetheless
