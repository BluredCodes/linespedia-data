It felt like judgement day in Hell
Dr. Keith Jones was a doctor in the field
And was giving his findings 
To a court of his peers
He had been working on for years

They didn't see the point
In extra terristrials
Or Telekinetic powers
It was a rough crowd in the joint

"Dr. Jones we just don't see any credible evidence of what you say"
Dr. Hardy began
"I mean there is just no way
If little green men have psychic powers
Then make my watch appear 
In the commissioner's hand"
Silence.  Nothing happened.  So he sat down.

They argued for hours
Bickering back and forth
Tension saturated the court
Points were made translucently clear
The consequences if he lost were dear
Job at risk and some said his life
But he felt it was right

At the end the court did not rule in his favor
Keith Jones felt as if he had committed a crime
Dr. Hardy looked at his watch
To check the time

...But his watch was in the commissioner's hands...The truth is out there.  Please keep in mind I typed this up while listening to the song Echoes by Pink Floyd
