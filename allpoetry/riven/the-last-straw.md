Patrick came home a day early from his business trip

Parked his car and opened the door

Listened very closely and heard a woman

Inside the house screaming for more

He ran up the stairs into his room

In his bed with his girlfriend he saw his very own kin


This is where the madness begins


The brother ran out of the room and quickly left

Patrick standing in the treshold

He orders his now ex girlfriend out of his place

And he never again wants to see her face

He gets his keys and drives to the local 7-11

A place right now on earth to him 

That seems like heaven

He walks in and asks for a pack of his favorite cigs

The storekeeper looks around and digs

Nothing of the sort fresh out

Skies turn black and his mind begins to shout

He drives back home and decides to go to bed

He finds a bottle of whiskey on the table

He downs half of it and now crawls into bed

Wakes up the next day with a pounding in his head

And a ringing phone

"Hello?"He says in his most human voice

"Patrick gets your ass down here! You're an hour late!" His boss screams



The demise begins


He flies out of bed and down the freeway

Only to realize this is the beginning 

Of a very bad day

He parks in not bothering to notice the sign

He brushes himself off and attempts to look benign

He checks in for what would be the last time

As he finds out he has been replaced

By someone with a good reputation and a clean face

He shuffles back out the door

And sees that his car has become no more

Towed away and now crushed into a cube

His sanity

Slowly draining down the tube

It is getting late he goes back home and sees a note 

The front door tells him

"Pack your stuff--you're being evicted"


He opens the door and sees somebody has already done it

All that's left is a table with a phone and his refrigerator

He finds that half empty bottle of Whiskey

And polishes it off

Goes into his closet and pulls down a box

Goes back to his table and sits

Underneath tissue paper lays a gun

He picks up the phone

Cocks the gun

It seems his sanity is no longer ruled



The last straw has been pulled
