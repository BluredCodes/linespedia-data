The winds from the north
Came and brought with them plague
Of the mind and the soul
Seven deadly sins consume the town as a whole

The fire in the bar turned a ghastly shade of black
As darkness rode in 
It had no intention of turning black
Bright to dark day to night
Victory not in sight
The town's only hope:
A necromancer exiled
Forced out fore he was tried and trialed
"Days of the new are gone
Banish all hope fore it seems wrong"
The preachers sermon was one of grave
They abandoned faith
And thought mankind could not be saved

As the necromancer Nos received word
That the town needed his help
He found the very idea absurd
But he had a love back home
That he wished to save
He did not want to be alone
He took up a sword and vowed to fight
Somehow in a dismal world
He had found the light

To find the source of darkness
He had to travel far and wide
His spiritual journey wasn't just inside
And then in the Valley of Styx
Light disappeared and fire scorched the sky
A man dressed all in black said,
"To save mankind, you must die
To save your soul, you must be destroyed
But to save the future is impossible"

Nos pondered what the cloaked figured said
Drew his sword
Struck himself
And he was dead
The frown of the hooded figure was feigned
Because after that, darkness reignedFeigned: faked (pretty much)
