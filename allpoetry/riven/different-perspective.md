How could I a child
Of young age
Understand the world
To a greater extent
And a greater guage
A mediocre society
A media kingdom
This is the reality come to reign
And in such times of trouble
How do I stay sane
I took up a sword
And a verse
I would no longer shout
At the world
The one I would previously curse
And in such times if madness
And insanity
Not know what may be
Around the next bend
How can one truly understand?
How can one truly comprehend?
Being fed corporate feed
Writing was the call
I would soon heed
A mere novice
Oblivious in the sea of experts
One nobody would notice
My journey in life is not to satisfy
Or with the rules and regulations
Not comply
I viewed the world through
A different perspective
A different lens
My status varied with event
Everyday on it depends
Until I am "sleeping under the rain"
I will not stop the flow
Nor will I stop my musical show
I do not want a grave
To be honored as if I were brave
I am no hero
No mortal God
Just leave me to myself
And in time I will put books on the shelf
