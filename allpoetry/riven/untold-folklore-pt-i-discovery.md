A family secret kept hidden for years

The elders have known it

Sixty winters this very year

Young John

Sixteen and kickin in his prime

Will soon find the answers 

To this unspeakable crime

Asking his aunt

Questions that she must surely know

Lies being told at the front

As his suspicion and paranoia begin to grow

"Why must you ask such foolish things?

Can you not see our family is a group of peaceful beings?" 

John not satisfied with the answers

Not finding what he wants

He turns to the youngest uncle

Paul also known as the black sheep

John discovers his family's past

As Paul begins to tell

Young lover with an old lust

Audrey is brought into the scene

As Gary's girlfriend, Anna, decides to split

The family does not approve of Audrey's ways

So the members devise a plan

To get Anna back and to have Audrey pay

A shot rings through in the night

All is calm now as she is buried in moonlight

Was she still beating as she was lowered into the ground?

The desert has secrets to tell

And bodies to be found
