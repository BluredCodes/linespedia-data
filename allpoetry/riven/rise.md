Awoken from a century's slumber
The toils of our past
Along with it's demons
Have awoken
And now they're here to have a blast

Seemed so long ago they dragged me down
I became a victim of my own devils
And they kicked me onto the wrong side of town
And beat me to a pulp

Physically beat mentally dead
How can a generation that is so alive
Be so dead
One that is full of life and potential
Yet everything it does seems consequential

In time it will rise
And the toils and demons from the past
Will return to the roots
And we will look to the skies

A hot fiery wind blowing across my feet
Taunting me with words of defeat
Yet in time I shall rise
And bring society's demiseI'm back
