---
title: "Gild The Lily"
writer: "RiVeN"

In a sense
You are beautiful in your own world

These materialistic things

You do not need

Makeup is not a miracle

Plastic surgery

Is a call you need not heed

Beauty is only skin deep?

So why is it I find your beauty

When you are awake

And when you are asleep

This feeling floating through my head

Your lips the darkest shade of red

My heart

Aching to be one with yours

My hands long to hold yours

One day soon

Our love will come into view

Eyes the brightest shade of green

I guess our future together

Has been forseen

In my deepest thoughts

And surrealistic dreams

You need not gild the lily

Your beauty is natural

And your love

Is mine
