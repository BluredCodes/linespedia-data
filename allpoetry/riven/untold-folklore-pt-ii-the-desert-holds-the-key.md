"Tell me land--

On your driest field of sand

Do bodies lay buried?

Is one of your plants Audrey?

Tell me uncle Paul--

Why do I feel compelled

For revenge against this deed

Scruples of remorse

Are the things right now I don't need"

"Child do not seek revenge

What has been done has been

The past does not need to repeat

Another member of the family

Does not need to suffer the same feat"

Research done on the web

Beginning to find answers on his own

Considering he no longer knows

The people living in his home

This charade that they call their family

Is run by a democracy of older men

That decide who stays

And who must leave

The torch has been passed down to this very day

Now under their interrorgating scope

John's unspeakable way

"Why does he want information about this girl?"

They soon pass a vote

Two years go by

And they have reached a conclusion

Another problem must die
