---
title: "Playing God"
writer: "RiVeN"

---

I am deep in thought
And I have this urge
I cannot overcome
It cannot be fought
And so I succomb

I create you in my image
Right down to the last detail
I give you a name
I give you a face
And I put you in a strange place

A world I created on my own
A world that you will call home
I planned out your life
From beginning to end
I know who is your enemy
And who is your friend
You are my puppet
And I am pulling the strings

I am a writer
