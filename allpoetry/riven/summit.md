Climbing a crest
Descending into the peak
I inhale cold stale air
And grasp my chest

On hallowed ground
Flying amidst Winter's eve
I see my goal tower over me
And I find it hard to breathe

In silence and speaking seclusion
Is the distance just an illusion

Living once in the vast starry sky
Having wild dreams is my alibi
Or am I staring at a greater risk at hand

Looking down from the summit
The valley below is dangerous and damned
From the top there is no other way
But while I am up here I shall enjoy my stay
