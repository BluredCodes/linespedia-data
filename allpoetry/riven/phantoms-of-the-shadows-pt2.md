I took the light and walked back into the room upstairs with the noose and opened the
door.  I held the light up and still saw nothing.  I went across the hall to the adjacent
room.  The door was locked so I kicked the door down.  There was a hole in the right
wall and a cage.  Inside the cage was a letter that read, “One of the creatures has
manifested and come out of the shadows.  I was able to put it in a cage, but I fear it will
outgrow the cage soon.  It has psychic powers and has been able to control my mind at
times.  I also fear its thirst for flesh and blood has grown.”  I looked through the hole in
the wall and saw nothing in the next room.

I crawled through the wall and the room was exactly like the previous; it was dark, small,
and had little in it.  The only thing in there was a bed.  The beds sheets were soaked in
blood.  I pulled up the sheets and looked under the bed.  There lay John.  His face eaten
away and his stomach’s entrails exposed.  My stomach began to turn.  I turned around and
held the light up to the corner and the light revealed in the shadows the beast described in
the letter.  It was a large circular body with six legs and the size of a dog.  Its breathingwas loud and came in short breaths.  It had no eyes;  Only a mouth with razor sharp
talons.  Though it seemed to be staring right at me.  I creeped back out through the hole
and closed my eyes.  I opened them and luckily the creature had not followed me.  I exited
the room and went back to the one across the hall.  The noose still hanging from the
ceiling.  I closed the door as I heard the one across the hall open.  I stepped onto the stool
and then became a phantom of the shadows.The End.  Kind of disturbing.  I tried to write it in the style of Thomas Ligotti.  I wrote it during two periods one day in school.  I personally like it.  Best if read at night....in the dark
