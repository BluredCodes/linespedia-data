Dear Mother,
I'm sorry I haven't been there

I apologize for my actions

It seems as if I don't care

My actions

I cannot take back

But the responsibility

I will hold

My thoughts have manifested

And this is what I've done


Everytime a crisis comes my way


I turn and run


Though sometimes I may up the arms


I never give a seconds thought


To the toll and the harm

They are right when they say

Actions speak louder than words

Clearly my actions have been heard

I cannot fix the things

From the past 


And now

As I see you begin to die

I am questioning my actions

And asking "Why?"

Living the consequences

I am beginning to pay

I'm afraid to go to sleep

For I fear tomorrow will not be another day
