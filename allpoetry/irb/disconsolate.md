Repetition speaks volumes
Of my insanity as of late
Happiness is for the weak.
Happiness can wait.

Common sense visits often
'Though it sits in perfect silence
My stupidity leads to flaws.
My stupidity leads to violence.

Desperation limits drastically
What patience holds so dear
Loneliness causes heartache.
Loneliness lives in fear.

An apparition of a second chance
Beckons me to try
Broken heart asks how.
Broken heart asks why.

Isolation commits suicide
In response to something new
Solitude will return.
Solitude knows love is never true.Uhm. Who knows?,
