---
title: "Where's my heart?"
writer: "Irb"
slug: "wheres-my-heart"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/27556-Wheres-my-heart--by-Irb"
---

Where
is
my
heart?


I think 
I left 
it
somewhere 
far.
It really
sucks
when you're

alone

and you
don't
like
who
you
are.
I promise
to
myself
and
whomever
gives
a
damn
I'll 
find
some way

to
fix
things 
so I can
like who
I am.I know it stinks, but I just wrote it today, and there wasn't much thought in it... I just like how the last stanza thing forms a tear drop. *giggles*
