---
title: "Half a dozen after midnight"
writer: "Irb"
slug: "half-a-dozen-after-midnight"
source: "AllPoetry"

I'm on my way to enlightenment
looks like I'm here alone
because I ain't seen no one else
along this scary road

the man down at the crossroads
said enlightenment's got its cost
but I threw the finger at 'im
I think that's why I'm lost

I guess it's been a long time
I'm feeling rather beat
I could get there a whole lot faster
if I quit tripping o'er my own feet

you know for all this walking
it feels I haven't moved an inch
I might as stay just where I am
'cause ignorance is bliss
