I was standing there waiting…
For someone like you to appear
No one did,
Because I am nothing.
And no one did
Because I am doomed
To be alone forever…
To never find anyone like you again.

And I'm sitting here thinking…
That it's okay to be let down.
It's okay,
Because I deserve it.
And it's okay
That I deserve it,
Because that's the way things are…
Because that's the way things will always be.
