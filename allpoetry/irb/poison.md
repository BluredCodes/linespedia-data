fall asleep to the lull of white noise
(the moon is so bright)
awaken to the smell of death
take a shower, but still feel impure
i need a cure
where is the cure
i need a cure

there's poison in my medicine cabinet
(out of sight)
of its origins i'm unsure
it's labeled with pretty letters reading "l i f e"
maybe someday it will be my cure
maybe someday life will be my cure
maybe someday it will be my cure
