Isn't she pretty now? 
Hunched in a corner with her arms hanging down...
Look at her smile?
No, look at her frown...
She said it was all your fault.

A straight-faced lie.
She makes up another when you go to ask her why...
Look at her smile?
No, look at her sigh...
You said you loved her still.

A heartfelt apology.
Wishing things were the way they used to be...
Look at her smile?
Yes, with absolute glee.
She's bending you to her whim.
