Perched
on half a minute
on MY Thursday's day off
and counting
red and pink hearts
which loom so proudly
so nauseatingly
in the air.
BLINK.
I vomit false poetry
I leave a note on
the door
to your heart-
your soul...
blackened from my arson
of millenia past
rises-
that FUCKING Pheonix-
and I am left
in ashes and 
could-
have-
beens-
BLINK.
Forever was an instant
Forever was a lie
dark wolf
in white sheep's clothing-
I vomit eternally

I digress.
