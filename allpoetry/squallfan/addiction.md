Today I went shopping
Just like I'd been doing
But today was different
Because today- I went shoeing.

I was only looking casually
(I can stop anytime!)
I perused a rack of sandals
I mean, that's not a crime.

I saw a purple pair
With straps and an open toe
I just had to have them-
So I shelled out the dough.

But- wait a minute-
I just can't stop there
I'll need polish and lipstick
To add a little flair

As you can see by now
I was going for broke
Literally, not figuratively.
(I won't cry so I joke.)

Those shoes, in all
Cost me more than I'd wanted
But I can overlook that now
'Cause me feet will be flaunted.I bought the cutest shoes today! ;D And some matching lipstick
and nail polish! ;D And I have the cutest top to wear them
with on Monday! ;D
