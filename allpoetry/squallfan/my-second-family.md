I have a second family
I know that sounds unusual...
None are related to me
Yet most know my deepest secrets.

We share our hopes and dreams
Relationships and progress
Sometimes we get on each other's nerves...
But we know we'll still care in the end.

I only see them once or twice a day
In the morning and afternoon
We laugh and cry and stare in silence
Always a different play to act out.

Have you guessed who they are yet?
That's right.
They're the other students on my school bus.
My best friends.I like ellipses...
