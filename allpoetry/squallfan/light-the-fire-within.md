2
1

And you're off down the mountain
The clock ticks away at an unrealistic speed
The same as your heartbeat right about now
The first place competitor stands smugly at the line

.01 of a second
How did you slow up at that gate?
Was it the screaming of the crowds
That threw you off pace?

Or maybe you're standing in the middle of the ice
The judges sit warily to the side
Your coach hoping, praying
Knowing you can pull it off

The soft music sweeps through your being
You dance like a tiger
Here it comes- the triple lutz
The crowning point of the program

Even though it's snowing you're warm
Readying yourself to jump in that bobsled
The three others standing behind you
Their lives are in your hands

You get a good push off
Already leading from the first run
Just one more turn
And that elusive medal will be yours

Just one more turn
Your legs are growing weaker
There's only one more skater in front of you
The time to pass is now

The bell lap has come
The crowd's cheering is becoming stronger
You push yourself beyond your limits
Because you feel you must

Those who watched the glorious exposition
Displayed by these special people
Were touched in their soul in a way
That cannot easily be explained.

We know the effort put forth by those athletes
Envying, admiring, loving, feeling them.
We were lucky enough to witness history
And we'll never be the same.I was so sad when the Winter Olympics ended! *sniffle*
