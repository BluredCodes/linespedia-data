---
title: "Lost In The Night"
writer: "thespin"
slug: "lost-in-the-night"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/17726-Lost-In-The-Night-by-thespin"
---

Wondering from room to room.Lost.In and out.Up and down.Roaming.Lost.In another endless night.Silence.The ticking of the clock.Ticktocking my Life away.Lost.Waiting for the dawn.A ray of light.A new day.A new beginning.Lost.I wonder from room to room.The pendulum swinging away.LOST IN THE NIGHT.
