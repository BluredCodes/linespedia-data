---
title: "Home at last"
writer: "thespin"

Home.
Home at last.
Safe.
Safe in my savior's arms.
Safe in His palms.
I'm home.
Never more to roam.
I've weathered all the storms.
Christ and I.
I can lift my voice to the sky.
I can proclaim Him on high.
Christ didn't die.
I'm home.
Home at last.
Safe in my Father's arms.
Safe in His palms.
No more to roam.
I'm home.
The dark clouds are clearing.
The skies are bright.
For I'm now walking in His guiding light.
I'm home.
Home at last.
Safe in my Saviour's arms.
Safe in His palms.
I'm home.
Never more to roam.
The waters are calm.
There's no cause for alarm.
Everything is clear and blue.
I've started anew.
I'm home at last. 
Safe in my Father's arms.
Safe in his palms.
I'm home.
Never more to roam.
I'm home.
