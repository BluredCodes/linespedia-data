---
title: "Stay"
writer: "thespin"
slug: "stay"

Stay.
Don't go away.
Stay.
Stay and see.
Me.
I am you.
We are one.
Don't run.
Life for us has just begun.
Stay and learn.
Try to show some concern.
Please, don't look back.
Life is an attack.
So, halt.
Look. I will not turn to salt.
You can't escape.
You won't win this race.
I will keep pace.
Stay. Don't hide.
Together we will keep astride.
Stay.
