---
title: "I Float"
writer: "thespin"
slug: "i-float"
source: "AllPoetry"

I float.An iceberg in a sea of flames.Aimlessly floating.Always UN-penetrable-never dependable.Forever revolving-never dissolving.I float.I sometimes get the merge-to melt-to shout-to the flames-RENEW ME!Underneath-just beneath-can't they see?I FLOAT!
