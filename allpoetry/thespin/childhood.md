Childhood is forever.
To smile. To laugh. To play.
Children light our way.
This day.

Childhood is forever.
It is here to stay.
Everything is spent.
There is nothing to relent.

Childhood is forever?
Never.
We wish.
We pass our hours in dreams.
And it certainly seems.
It would last.
Never.
It is past.One of my older poems. Set out to say something. I think I got lost. Let me know.
