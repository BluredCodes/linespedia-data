---
title: "Walk in the world for me"
writer: "thespin"
slug: "walk-in-the-world-for-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

Walk in the world for me.So I can see all there is to see.Look at the changing sky.Watch the birds on high.Take a swim in the sea.Climb a giant tree.There is so much for me to see.Smell a blooming flower.Walk hand in hand in an April shower.Watch the earth smile.Laugh, and become a child.Hold some strangers hand.Run barefooted through the sand.Remember me through all the years.Remember me not with wasted tears.I may be gone.But through you I am reborn.So, walk in the world for me.So once again I can see all there is to see.
