Cone in hand God whirls it.
Around and around.
Hot air.
Circling, Rotating, Pulling, Forming,
Whisks of cotton candy.
Pieces and strands.
Treads hanging, stretching,creating,
Angel hair.
Animal crackers, Pillsbury doughboys, Tar babies,
Floating, spinning, revolving.
Silently shifting.
Covering us all in imagination formation.Say what you will. Just be honest about it.
