title: "What went wrong?"

I've said to myself,"I'll close the door."
She never existed before."
But things are never that simple.
Nothing seems to change.
Nothing is new.
It's only a constant repeat of you.
Your memory is burnt into the corners of my brain.
And like some scheduled train, it stops, refuels, and moves on.
It has been a long hard time.
The world moves fast.
but your memory lasts.
A faint distance chime.
A hundred different faces have come and gone.
They leave me empty, always asking, "What went wrong?"
I wish I could again feel.
Nothing is real.
Everything is cold. A heartless ordeal.
I long for you in the night.
I want to feel you beneath me, pulsating with delight.
Oh, why can't you see?
I need you to need me.
But that could never be.
You are forever gone.
And I'm left alone asking, "What went wrong?"
