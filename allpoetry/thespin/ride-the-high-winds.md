---
title: "Ride The High Winds"
writer: "thespin"
slug: "ride-the-high-winds"
source: "AllPoetry"

Ride the high winds.
Swoop and glide.
Push against the winds.
Find an opening and glide
Ride the currents.
No need to push.
Be at peace and float.
Play among the clouds.
Glide.
Join your friends in the sun.
Ride the high winds.
