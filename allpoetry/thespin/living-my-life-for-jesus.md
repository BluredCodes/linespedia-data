title: "Living My Life For Jesus"

With a smile on my face.
I erase,
all my troubles away.
From day to day.
I walk in His way.
Living my life for Jesus.

With a song in my heart.
I get a clean start.
My old self dies.
No more hate, no more lies.
Living my life for Jesus.

All the loneliness is gone.
With Christ I am reborn.
No more sorrows.
No more strife.
Joy and Happiness fill my life.
Living my life for Jesus.
