Question and questions.It is time to re-evaluate our intentions.What do we have to fear?That 'they' will come to near?Is it so hard to give rather than take?Why can't we learn to share the cake?Is it that we are all so unwilling to hear?Or do we value our 'whiteness' so dear?That we are refusing to hear?We have all shared the darkness,more or less.Is it all so useless?Why can't we step in to the light?Why do we always take flight?We all need to cross over the line.and maybe we will find,that this land has always been a globe of multi-colored hues.and we have nothing to loose.BUT EACH OTHER.
