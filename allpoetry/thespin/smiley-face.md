I am a smiley face man
doing what I can.
To put a smile on everyone's face.
To help the human race.
To erase
All suffering and pain.

I am a smiley face man
doing what I can.
To bring perfection to the world
Before they are hauled
into the abyss.

I am a smiley face man
doing what I can.
To bring everyone my own special brand
of God.
So that they will know just who is their Lord.

I am a smiley face man
doing what I can.
To lessen the enormity.
To increase the conformity.

I am round and have no ears.
Two dots for eyes that don't shed tears.
No nose so I can smell.
And a smile that comes straight from Hell.
I am a smiley face man
doing what I can.I wrote this piece for fun and also I know a few people that really are smiley face men.
