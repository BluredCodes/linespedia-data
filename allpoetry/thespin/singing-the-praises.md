title: "Singing The Praises"

Sing praises to God.
Sing praises unto your King.
Lift up your voice and sing.
Sing. Sing.
For He is the Lord of all the earth.
For He is the Lord of all the earth.
Sing praises to His birth.
Sing praises to His birth.
He is the Lord of all.
The Lord of all.
On Him do we call.
On Him do we call.
Sing praises with love and understanding.
Sing praises with love and understanding.
The Lord is on His throne.
The Lord is on His throne.
We are not our own.
We are not our own.
Sing praises to his throne.
The Lord is greatly exalted.
The Lord is greatly exalted.
He is our shield.
He is our shield.
With His blood we are healed.
With His blood we are healed.
Singing praises to our Lord.
