title: "Look At Me"
writer: "thespin"

A merry-go-round of whispers and lies.
A thousands hello's and a million good-byes.
Tissues and muscles turning to stone.
Faces...faces...all alone.
A line.
A mark.
Some light, some dark.
They have all left their mark.
Beacons.
Shinning.
Blue, brown, and green.
What have they witnessed?
What have they seen?
Open caverns echoing-spouting.
Spawning.
Whispering.
Shouting.
Smile. Laugh. Shed a tear.
Can't you see?
I am here.
I am here.
