title: "Another Morning"

Dawn plays hide and seek through half-drawn blinds.
Leaving the velveteen night behind.
Lights scintillate, signal.
Lighthouses in a small world.
Echoes of crying children. A howling dog.
Life steers behind closed doors.
Dinosaurs spring from the mist.
Awakening Atlantis.
Emerging from their layers they attack.
Welcoming the yawning armies back.
Their rumbling resounds towards the sky.
Appearing and disapearing.
Moles burrowing their cargo through the ground.
Their mouths devour.
Closes.
Releases.
Discharging their food.
