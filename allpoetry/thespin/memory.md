title: "Memory"

Memories linger like the scent of yesterdays flowers.
They return in the dark quiet hours.
You die not so quick a death.
You have substance- breath.
In sleep I say, "I'll hide you forever more."
But alas,
It's another door.
You resound through the attic of my mind.
I try to run forward.
But you always behind.
I want to forget.
but yet,
You are there.
Always there.
Oh, that I could cease to care.
Yet tomorrow is another day.
Hopefully, your memory will fade away.
