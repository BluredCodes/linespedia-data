Limitless wastelands of Mt.Olympus.A dream. Formulated. Calculated.Solitary. Eternal.A cornucopia world.Gods of Conception. Deception. United.Zeus, the ruler.Socrates, the philosopher.Prometheus, the thief.They all add to the cauldron.Bits and pieces.Fire and Light.Adding. Subtracting. Creating.Children of the Gods.Monsters.Embryo's of destruction.Mushrooming above its Fathers.Billowing. Enlarging. Spreading. Illuminating. Blinding.Destroying Mt. Olympus.
