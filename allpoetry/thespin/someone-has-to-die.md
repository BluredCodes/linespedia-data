---
title: "Someone Has To Die"
writer: "thespin"
slug: "someone-has-to-die"

Kill-Pill.
Bang-Hang.
Fry-Die.
Gas-Blast.

An eye for eye.
Someone has to die.
Vengeance is mine.
Let my hate shine.

Kill-Pill.
Bang-Hang.
Fry-Die.
Gas-Blast.

Invitations for the few.
Invitations to view.
All gather around.
The son-of-bitch is glory bound.

Hang,Pill,Gas,or Fry.
We're watching the son-of-a-bitch die.
