We meet before the world.
The earth was a void.
Out of its vapors you came.
Engulfing me.

We meet before the world.
No stars shining.
No lights on high.
Nothing.

Only the blackness.
Only you.
Filling the world.
Creating the light.
We meet there.
There in the stillness.
There in the nothingness.
And you became my world.
