Framed.
My world.
Reflected in moonlight.
Vague shadows.
Images.
Moving.
Spreading.
Creatures.
Hiding.
Lurking.
Seeking.
Substance.
Shelter.
Warmth.
Fathoms.
Stirring.
Revolving.
Framed.
Creeping into the early morning light.
