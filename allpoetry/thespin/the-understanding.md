Beauty.
Real and unreal.
Dream-like.
Touching.
Feeling.
Knowing.
Mysticaly loving.

We touched each other.
I felt you.
Swallowed you.
Our eyes meet and parted.
Reality!

Faded dreams.
An understanding.
Removed.
Unanswered.
We loved.
But how?Confused. Again, had an idea. I don't think it came to light. Any comments on the small work welcomed.
