---
title: "Winters Song"
writer: "thespin"
slug: "winters-song"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/11974-Winters-Song-by-thespin"
---

Silent.Peaceful.Earth.Waiting.Blue gray.The world hibernates.Listens.Watches.White specks.Join.Swirl.Dance.The winds.Bellows.Plays.Folds.Sweeping up its lovers.Virgins.Twisting.Cascading.To the ballet of Winter.
