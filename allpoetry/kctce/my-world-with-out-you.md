---
title: "My World With Out You"
writer: "kctce"
slug: "my-world-with-out-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/11150-My-World-With-Out-You-by-kctce"

Once again
I've fallen into you
You lead me to a world that only I knew
A world where I didn't have to pretend
you never expected me to.
And into your world and into your heart
I tried to find my way
And without you now, time and distance apart
I'd rather be in your world
then to have to live another second without you in mine.
I dream one day I find you
and once again
you welcome me into you.
And I will fall
into depths of rage and pain and love I still do not know
And your tired heavy hands you will place on mine
and hold me in your eyes until I close my own
and once again
find sleep
I dream that I may never open them again,
to my world without you
