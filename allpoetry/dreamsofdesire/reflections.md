There is a mirror in my room
That I often walk by
Sometimes I stand in front of
it; admiring myself

Offtimes I wonder: is that really
my reflection in there?
Or am I the copycat staring back?

I look at my reflection

I laugh; he laughs
I smile; he smiles
I frown; he frowns
I touch; he reaches forth

Only a cold sensation seperates
our two worlds from my touch
and I realize...

As I examine, he has the entire
universe watching behind his back.
