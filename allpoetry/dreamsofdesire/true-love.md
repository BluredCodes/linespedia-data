I used to think there
was such a thing as
true love, where you
could fall in love eternal
with the guy of your dreams

Now, I'm not so sure
anymore. Was it just a
childish dream to think so?
or a fantasy to be filled?

Lonliness can mar perception
like an intoxicating drink
bitter and cold, it can make you
take up what was cast down before
and make it seem a revered escape
from reality.

There is no shining knight
in armor - I know and accept that
Yet, I look around myself
and see those who give their hearts
and recieve others in return
and wonder - is it something to do with me?

Maybe.

I sit and wonder if there is a true love...

I sit and wonder if there is one for me.
