title: "The Golden Sunset"
writer: "Dreamsofdesire"
slug: "the-golden-sunset"

I stand here, silence all 
Around me, in an empty house
Looking out the window
Wishing you were here

In the view of my window
I see many things.
Ducks migrating to the south
Gracefully gliding with their wings

Clouds drifting by slowly,
Without a need nor
Any care in the world.
Watching down on us

Softly golden, they are
Touched with the radiance
Of the golden sunset…
Never have I seen such a perfect sight

The golden rays touch
The emerald leaves of the trees. 
Stirred by the breeze, it seems 
They sigh with such pleasure.

The golden sunset goes from
golden to red; a mixture of colours
all over the horizon is spread
I sigh at it's perfection.

Standing here, watching this sunset
I sit and sigh, wishing
With all my heart, that 
You should have been here with me.
