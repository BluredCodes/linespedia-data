THE WAR HEXAD
PART ONE: THE WAR OF HEAVEN TRILOGY
MORNING STAR
Morning star, oh Morning star
caller of the sun, bringer of the day
Pearl of the skies, how bright you are
But even beauty may fall astray
DRAGON'S BIRTH
father look at the skies:
the stars are dividing!
Turn away! Son! From the lies
from he who wants to be King
DRAGON'S FALL
oh Mother of our nation
I saw it tumbling down!
Screaming in frustration
In anger at the Crown!

PART TWO: THE WAR OF EARTH TRILOGY

DRAGON'S VENGEANCE
oh look out for him, Mother!
from the heavens he's fallen
claws ready to tear and smother
for our blood he is roarin'!
MALE CHILD
I saw angel wings carrying Mother away
I saw the dragon's claws ripping futilely
I saw the raging river swallowed, held at bay
I saw the beast-dragon turn on me!
WAR AND STRUGGLE
the dragon fought with flame and claw
but Father thy hands saved thy children
now the dragon's fraudulent frippery thaw
their resolve, much to Your chagrin

* Revelations 12:1-14:5.
