WHITE STONE TRILOGY: Part 2


'have you not heard
of the fair white stone,
with its written word
by one soul known,
and the Lord alone?'*
-* Emily Hickey

Somewhere out in a desert
 near the edge
             
               of the world
lives a strange old man in an adobe
 of earth and wood
with hair and beard gray as a sunbleached ironwood

on the top of a mountain ridge
            at a stone of pure white
 with bowed head he
                 kneels
 for he's the Chosen; reads 
                          One Word


The old man cries but with every tear 
               a miracle
is born
as on the stone
               there appears a single 
               Word 
               alone
a Word never to be stained, dimmed or worn
for on this world 
it is to be known to one soul aloneTo know the Maker's true name is to know all the secrets of the universe ... according to old legend his name would appear on a white stone to but one that is worthy. In a time long ago it was custom to give a white stone to some as an accolade - for to one of them might the Maker's name appear
