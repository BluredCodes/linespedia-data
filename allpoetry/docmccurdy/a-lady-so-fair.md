---
title: "A lady so fair"
writer: "DocMccurdy"
slug: "a-lady-so-fair"

I LOOK AT THE SETTING SUN,
AND WONDER,
AT THE STILLNESS AND WARMTH FILLING THE AIR,
WHO CAN I LIKEN TO WITH
THIS FEELING,
BUT THE BEAUTIFUL,PETITE; THE LADY SO FAIR.


SHE HAS THE MONA LISA SMILE,
SO PRETTY,
HER EYES ARE ASHEN. HER PREFERENCE IS GREEN.
SHE STRUTS LIKE A PEACOCK,
SO ELEGANT,
THE LADY SO FAIR,NONE LESS, A FAIRY QUEEN.


SHE REMINDS ME OF AN ANGEL ALIVE,
ONLY BETTER,
FOR THIS IS THE FACE OF ONE; THAT SAYS IT ALL,
SHE LIVES,TALKS,PERHAPS SHE FLIES'
GRACEFULLY,
CARRYING HER SELF PROUDLY,ALWAYS WALKING TALL.


I WONDER WHO WILL BE SO LUCKY,
SO FORTUNATE,
 TO BE ABLE TO FULFILL HER WANTS AND DESIRES,
SHE HAS TO BE TREATED SOFTLY,
SO TENDERLY,
AND MUCH MORE THAN WATER TO QUENCH HER FIRES.


I CAN ONLY DREAM OF ENTERING
HER THOUGHTS,
FOR SHE LIVES IN A WORLD SHE WOULD SELDOM SHARE,
HER CHOICE WOULD BE EMPYREAL
SO HEAVENLY,
'CAUSE SHE IS SO SPECIAL,SO UNIQUE,A LADY SO FAIR.
