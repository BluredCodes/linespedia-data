---
title: "I ask myself?"

I look at myself,
then dream
Who am I?
I ask myself?
I am indeed not a prophet
nor a  messenger of God,
nor am I looking
for a retinue of followers
to  lead them
to their salvation.
I am not a preacher
nor a spokesman
for God
or the Devil.
Then what am I?
Who am I?
Why am I `me'
and not `you';
For am I a God
or merely a simple life
on earth--
I ask myself?
