title: "A magical moment"

The thoughts are alive in my mind,
Dancing to the rhythm of my heartbeat,
Burning with desire, and ecstasy,
Reminiscing that magical moment.

That magical moment,
When we were last together,
Walking arm in arm,
My hands brushing your bosom,
And the heart missing a beat,
Wicked thoughts a flashing,
To hold you dearly,
Crushing my lips against yours.
