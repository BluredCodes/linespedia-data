title: "From where shall i bring?"

From where Shall I bring
The love,
the way you want it.
The Smile,
whenever you need it,
The hugs,
anytime  you feel like.
The kisses,
always soft and sensual.

From where shall I bring,
The diamond ring,
for your finger.
The necklace,
you want to flaunt so.
The beautiful mansions,
for you to live in.

From where shall I bring,
The dreams,
you long, to awaken to.
The needs,
you are so used to.
The ambitions,
that drive you on.
The pleasures,
you feel you are obliged to.

For  I am not perfect,
Yet I am there always besides you.
