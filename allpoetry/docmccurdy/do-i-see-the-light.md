---
title: "Do I see the light"
writer: "DocMccurdy"
slug: "do-i-see-the-light"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/14037-Do-I-see-the-light-by-DocMccurdy"
---

I see the tall trees swaying,
I see the clouds so white,
I see the mantis praying
But, do I see the light.

I see the streams flowing,
I see the fish in the sea,
I watch glow worms glowing,
And birds nest, up a tree.

I see the beauty in my heart,
I see all that is humanity,
I see the world as work of art,
Yet, is this all a reality?

Then I find myself face to face,
With a very familiar sight,
Injustice both to mankind and race,
Tell me, do I see the light?


For I must now meet this mirth,
And see things in actuality,
Yet, seeing good giving evil birth,
I laugh at this profanity.

At first it is hard to believe,
What true picture I can paint,
I know I should never conceive,
My character I can forever taint.

I know I can stand to lose,
My freedom and my vanity,
And forfeit all, should I choose,
To draw a line with sanity.

Although my life took on the day,
I was borne out of maternity,
When I think of death I pray,
My belief to stay till eternity.

Now what I learn is startling,
All is not what it may seem,
For only death is a sure thing,
The rest is merely just a dream.

So, I see myself getting old,
I see my life passing by,
No matter what is said or told,
One day I shall say goodbye.

I shall not wallow in self pity,
I shall not hurt with false pride,
I shall look up to reality,
I shall not behind stone walls hide.

I shall live not just exist,
I shall not fall without a fight,
I have one life, I must subsist,
For, this is how I see the light.
