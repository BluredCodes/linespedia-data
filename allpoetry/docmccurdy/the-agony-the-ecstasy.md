title: "The Agony, The ecstasy"

Her sparkling blue eyes,like a vast ocean                       
is inviting me to its utmost depth
To a new destiny...but...without any experience
Then I am just a poor sailor...drifting aimlessly.

Now I am sinking...I am drowning deep...
into the whirlpool of those enchanting eyes..
So aid me..redeem me from this ordeal...
But, alas, for surely, now there is no retreat.
