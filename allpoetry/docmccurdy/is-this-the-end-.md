---
title: "Is this the End ?"
writer: "DocMccurdy"
slug: "is-this-the-end-"

Cloudy skies, 
hazy sunset;
breathing falters.
Dark shadows forever pre-dominant.
Light receding- 
barely the glow;
The end of the tunnel.
Body trembles, 
the ground shakes.
Then . . . is this the end?

Nightmare over, 
the dreamer awakens,
hopes take a tumble.
The rat race; 
Over and done with.
Reality strikes; 
Dream-worlds shatter,
Will Power no more.
The future aimless, 
life meaningless,
Then . . .this is the end.
