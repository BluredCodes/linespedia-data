title: "The fun no more"

When I think of life
I wonder
What is next?
Unscrupulously
A part of me.
Faded memories
Of fun times
And the carefree
Now dying inside,
Like a cancer,
Eating me alive,
Hurting me.
So, why do I have to?
Continue living,
To face the despair
And just go on,
Existing, drifting,
Day after day,
The fun is no more,
Nor the carefree,
Just a burden
A baggage
Called life.
