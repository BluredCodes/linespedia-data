title: "LIFE"
writer: "DocMccurdy"

Awaken;  Early in the morning,
A walk in the woods,
Drawing deeply, the air so fresh,
the sense of  exaltation, of fortitude.
I could do with that.

Immersed; In deep golden foam,
A feeling of solace,
warmth flowing, eyes unblinking,
The top of the world, the pinnacle.
I could do with that.

Satisfied; all the world riches,
strewn at my feet,
Desires fulfilled, wants exceeded,
Every wish a command, an order.
I could do with that.

Yet, I couldn't do with that.
How could one have everything.
What could he do with it.
In true sense  to live, not just exist
That! I could do with that.
