The eyes of a woman  So loving  so caringThe warmth of her lipsSo sensual so sharing. Her flowing black hairHer ever inviting stare, When the softness of her touch isSo pleasing so exciting. When she swaggers alongFlashing words in a song,					Then the message is clear, itsSo burning so inviting.           												                                             The pleasures of her passionsThe modes and the fashions,Her demands  so modest, they'reSo daring so defying.																							    But the wrath of a woman  So aversive so crushing,  and the extent of her hatred So taming so finishing.									And her wants and desiresare both honey and fire						For the needs of a woman areSo filling  so satisfying.                                               The eyes of a womanSo gentle so baring,The warmth of her lips                     			So alluring so daring.
