Hours upon hours spent in laughter and joy
Movies, and hikes, and late night drives

Friendship, the only goal we made
Expectant always of the great times to come.

Once it leaves, is there any way of retrieving it—
Or is it lost in the twisting labyrinth of time?

An untimely vulnerability resulting in soft rejection
Where does this leave us?
