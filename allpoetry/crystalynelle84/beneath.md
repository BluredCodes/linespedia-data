To pose and smile is easy,
Carelessly shelving true feeling.
Placid, stoic, emotionless
What do you feel?
Anger?  Hatred?  Loathing?
Pulsing through your veins –
Captive within your mind –
Never will show –
Until out of sight costumes come off,
And an explosion of sobs erupts.
Slowly, once pressure has returned to safe levels,
Stage makeup is cautiously replaced
And you pose and smile.
