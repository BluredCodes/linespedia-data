'Streets Of Crime'

The cries of pain in the city streets,
angers those who walk the beat.

They're the ones who see the grime,
in the streets of painful crime.

They fight for justice all their days,
to put the violent men away.

For the many in which they save,
we pray for you the ones so brave.
