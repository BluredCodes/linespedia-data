''NEPHEWS''

Boys they have such funny little souls,
playing all day and digging big holes.

While jumping and running and falling down,
they're always smiling never wearing a frown.

Pockets are full of something so bold,
maybe a snake a bug or  a toad.

I am so blessed to have known you my dears,
so never feel lonely as long as I'm here.
