‘Doom and Despair’ In the darkest of times I am alone, surrounded by fear my soul is in stone. Awakened by dread, everyday of my life, an unhappy woman, a terrible wife. Sadness and doom, envelops my soul, I'll never feel joy, I'll never be whole. In the depths of despair, I hope for no other, be close to my heart, my wonderful mother. I pray for the day, to end all of my fear, no more sorrow or pain, my Lord to be near.BY DIANNA NUTT
