Remember eyes
no names or faces
only eyes.
Believe eyes.
Words won’t
reveal
profundity, passion
only eyes,
rarely the mouth
never the rest
only eyes.

Pools like 
liquid mirrors -
some say
you can see the soul -
most won’t look
for eye to eye
steeled in space
empathetic
love or hate,
at its least
 erotica,
only eyes.
