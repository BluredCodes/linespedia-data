Lately,
most intensely now,
the furniture, this room,
all that makes somehow queer
this immediate environment,
fluctuates between
very far, very near,
sensitive and sincere.
 
Constantly,
like salt in the eye
can seem so –
And this is why!
We now know why!
This schizophrenia 
grows in us, panics
until you need to know
but never can be sure,
if this is love or not
or some demented ruse.

“So please”, 
he now says quietly.
“The children are sure
to hear, besides we
will do it your way
of that you needn’t fear”,
as he drops 
back on the sofa
now placid and demure.
