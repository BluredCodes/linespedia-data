Quest

Stranded, taking again
nearly as many years,
I retraced my life –
connecting every silver hair
that had fallen from my head –
creating yet a new maze
through the forest of my youth.

Seizing Spring,
renewed and reeling –
the Machiavellian quest abscinds –
the birds of then 
have strewn the way
and coil gray threads
through nests of lust.
