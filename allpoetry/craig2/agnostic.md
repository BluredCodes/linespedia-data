With all the wrath of the Mencken wit
I criticized the critics,
mocked their lives as words.
With all the pride of adolescence,
verbiage of a life’s work
I sought the renaissance, 
fought the holocaust
armed with the lie of silver and gold.

A self appointed, enlightened speaker,
prodigal priest of Xanadu,
recognizing the righteous burden
to annihilate approval,
subtly selling subtlety’s memories -
time chasing progress 
to a conceptualized end.
