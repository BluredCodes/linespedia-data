There really are no limits
depending on how you feel
to what one can do for another
to keep on an even keel.

Always arriving, departing -
destination is never a place
but some new way of copulating -
making love to your face.

It’s a begging, aching void,
a need to be deep inside -
any paradise worth its name
may be flawed but never denied.

Piercing, penetrating moments
cut the middle like a start -
first time and forever wetting
the dried hymen of your heart.
