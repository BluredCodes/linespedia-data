Drenched from games 
on an unguarded beach 
we removed our jeans 
to a blanket between the dunes. 
Pillow of sweet sex smell, 
soft moving mounds 
of warm moist sand, 
ink black waves 
tongue our naked island - 
the moon's silver path 
beckons night be 
spent, caressed.
