Your touch -
distant now,
but permanent
like Merlot
birth mark.

Your fingerprints
tattoo soul-soiled,
gauze-thin
slips of memories,
wind tossed, 
seeking solitude -
begging be identified,
remembered as 
satin fishnet leaves
of lyric carnal romps.
