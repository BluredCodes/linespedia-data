Peter quoting Old Testament Joel
about speaking in tongues,
“God will pour His spirit
upon all flesh, and
your sons and daughters
shall prophecy and see visions
and your old men dream dreams.
The mountains shall drip sweet wine,
the hills shall flow with milk
and all the stream beds 
shall flow with water
and a fountain shall come 
forth from the House of the Lord.”

I ponder 
the poignant moisture
of this spirit often, 
when pouring wine 
or making love,
but always 
when the mist,
the tongue, the words
ascend the 
trite and mundane.
