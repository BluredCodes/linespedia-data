title: "Styrofoam Domini"

Upper middle metamorphous,
dangling mortal pride
on gated rural purgatory -
fool’s escape from
roach and worm.

Remote retreat,
aluminum-sided suburb -
desperate  hellos cross
bridges of rodent crested sea.

Aloof, comfortable,
button-down in exile,
waving back remorse
at melodrama’s homeless -
never daring pay surrealistic tolls.

Dynamically lazy yet proud
in Kierkegaard concrete cashmere –
dirty, guarded martinis
at charity’s open-guilt bar.
