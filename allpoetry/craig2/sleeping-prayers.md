When two or more
       are gathered to
              bleach shattered praise,
                     cleanse violent need,
                         the majesty of mob
                              absorbs the incognito sin.

Desert treks in narcissism,
       desecration and celebration,
              frantic nights of flagellation,
                     in the garden masturbation -

       in comatose, disciples
              dreaming of pornographic 
                    congregations -
                      tongues of fire screaming,
                           ranting in orgied release
                               of some future Aquarian Age.
