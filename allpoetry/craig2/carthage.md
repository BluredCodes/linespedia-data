It thrived, alive
scattering the dream 
within a dream
to surfaces frontiers
have never known –
here it encountered Rome.

Fought for generations,
with heroes born of myth,
the taste of suicidal dying,
the sound of empires
based on war, for such 
was the legacy of Rome.

Burnt and enslaved,
salt sown through its 
awareness, shuddering
at its own existence, 
drowned in the blood 
of the sword of Rome.

Unable to recall 
the history of the race
that posits one with Gods,
slave conformity was tolerated –
this was Roman 
Rome had won.
