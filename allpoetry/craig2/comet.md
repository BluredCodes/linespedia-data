As thing meets nothing
only where there is life,
a spark of primal  force
meets smoky essence kindling
toward some stellar Capricorn event.

In thrusts of gilded splashes
or softened strokes
through misty haze,
messages in blinding smear
each a wave of blinding heat,
each to crest, break then blend,
each to swell and soar again.

This mass of nothing plane
tides in ocean-like response
to the thingnesses
of tangible wherewithalls,
becomes the only border,
seers the final spectacle.
