I have one toothbrush
     too many;
          not even counting
               the one for travel.
          and so they rotate.
I can’t account for it
     either; one 
          I know I killed
               the pharmacist for
          the other, well,
perhaps you left it,
     but your 
          teeth are whiter.
When they come for me
     you can 
          have all three,
               until then feel free
          to use which ever
     one you choose.
