Tryst

Among strangers 
she would steal a stare,
speak of Egypt and China
in no uncertain terms,
of social mores,
decadence, moralities
old and new.

Alone as the sex
matured between us,
her eyes caressed like lips,
she rose, explaining that  
a bit of honey dew would 
prolong the moment,
sweeten the scent, 
wet the dust to be
mingled with mine forever.this has been published - 'Naked Poetry' - Jan. issue
