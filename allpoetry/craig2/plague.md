A cure they shouted
to no one there,
dying numbers
stopped count -
dynamically vexed
yet proud, 
diseased,
mortality is 
common ground.

Stumbling homeless,
searching hopeless
magic atonement
with sour-shriveled skin-
knowing the roach,
forgiven by worm,
untold futures
march in.
