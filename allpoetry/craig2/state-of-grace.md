I’m down right now
to skin and bones
like Picasso’s ‘Forlorn’.
Adrenaline is all
I eat. My dreams
are all cartoons.
I was on top
but bottomed out,
it really is quite funny,
if word gets out
I’m broke and down
people will send money.
If you think me
quite naive depending
on such graces,
the good news is
on top or not,
I get laid both places.
