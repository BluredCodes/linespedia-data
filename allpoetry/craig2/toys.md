Orson,
        of the trinket wares,
        peddler of aerosol flesh
                stands glassy-eyed, naked
                        in front of Saks.

      Dolls,
        mistresses in the window
        tease with pouting lipsticked
                lips, and ease and prod
                         near the anus.

      Penance,
        gimmick, awareness, trade?
        Empathy hides behind
                the ice in his eyes
                        as his wife fiddles
                        for a price tag.
