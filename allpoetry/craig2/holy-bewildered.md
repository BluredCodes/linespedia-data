But what of lust, evil, pain –
God’s despair or Satan’s fame?

Do you see love
in the whore’s response,
beauty in a murderer’s eye?
Is it tender youth 
or lack of faith
that blind you to our Christ?
Is life not war 
with a wicked spouse,
a chance to fight for the Good?
Did the Lord not call
for the walls to fall,
the Jerichoan’s blood?

While you proclaim,
“God’s not on high”
but right here in your palm,
the wandering arm 
of the rich man’s plague 
smiles as it sings your psalm.This does not reflect any point of view of mine which I would not try to do in a piece like this, anyway. Mostly just wordplay.
