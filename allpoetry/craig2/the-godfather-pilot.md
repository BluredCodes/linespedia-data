There is a Jesus Revolutionary Party
and the only five year plan they need
is to make every day a Golgotha
every night a Gethsemane.
The modus operandi being
that each calendar day of the year
be assigned to a new party member
as his institution of fear.
Then a personal trek to sainthood
“Christification”, if you will,
to culminate in a planned persecution
propaganda ready for the kill.

Then half a decade later
there are enough men in the field
that the project becomes perpetual
as you add the next cog in the wheel.
Imagine histories of the future
discussing the ultimate renaissance of man
crucifixion will be daily bread 
there will be peace throughout the land.
The plan is now three months old
progressing to our Number One Christ,
who roamed the streets in prayer and fast
for forty days and nights.

His untimely murder the other night 
by an amateur down by the docks
will throw the plan back at least three months
and have party members in shock.
But right now its only I who know
and I’ll keep it to myself awhile
for tomorrow night there’s Number Two,
and the Party leader’s Judas style-
calls for hammer and run at seven,
plane to New York at eight-
the insurance check in a month or so 
should keep party finance straight.
