Polo and Marcyannounce their engagement,Edwardo demonstrates his backhand,Bishop Sheen glares smilingly over his mint iced tea,and as the olive turns in my stomachI spill martinion my new flannel pants,Molly becomes concerned.Closing her Chippendale eyesshe wraps her Andalusian tonguearound the stainand I remember whyit was I had agreed to come.Published in 'Spinnings' - quarterly publication
