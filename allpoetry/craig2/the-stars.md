Cash 
was the only syllable
that remained remotely
understood. And food,
food like rodent’s food -
and slime. It was 
within this time
that death as death
was nearly overlooked.

Concepts
like loves and lovers
vanished in the dark
of night. These heroines
absorbing cosmic trauma, 
tiny quartzite 
pools of sight.
Cherubins,
the last of last
endangered karma and delight.
