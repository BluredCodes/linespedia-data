Alone in karmic
sea of wandering waves,
prozac-nurtured tides
and then by chance
on shore.

We all come to it.
Or do we ?
That docking when all
thoughts change to
extraneous, honest, calm.

That life changing port
so simple, yet
so out of control,
somehow the only
balanced place to land.
