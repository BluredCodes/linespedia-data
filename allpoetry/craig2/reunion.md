One friend tells me
his cardiologist sees problems
with his left ventricle.
Another friend tells me
he is two weeks a new father
and I should stay in closer touch.
One friend says
that sobriety would add
a new dimension to my life -
another asks me 
when we can 
get together for drinks.

The talk show host
asks would you give up
your best friend
for a million dollars.
My best friend wonders
whether my stares
are the result of empathy,
love or tranquilizers.

Come across this room
with your long brown hair,
I have taken a drug
that makes me want to talk,
ask me poignant things
and be my friend.
