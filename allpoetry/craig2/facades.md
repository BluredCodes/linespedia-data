I am attracted to,
you repelled
by the same 
unique characteristic -

like a dangling magnet
with changing fields
the soul-searching need
for reasons to have
and to have not -

as opposed somehow
to ancient miseries,
more contemporary guilt.

This independent understanding,
constant metaphors of love,
never knowing one from another,
never separating twins,

afraid of unmatched tomorrows -
'only death opens destiny's doors'.

I see it
as amazing syzygy,
you as cruel elite.'The syzygies are the paired opposites, where the One is never separated from the Other, its antithesis'.   - Jung
