You pushed me 
back on the brick
lost in new neighborhoods
seduced by new neighbor

No words 
just spiritual
pout-puffed red 
blush
from tongue-tickled
throat

Your small breasts 
perked against my chin
your back arched 
climax -

we came quietly
deliriously
together -
God seemed
close as cloud 
on your cheek
