We all come to the words,
…or do we,
full on the fingertips,
song from the veins,
limitless, flawless, calm.

More the bar-spoke moment
…out of control,
eye to liquid eye,
simple social beer-toke,
extraneous, honest, calm.

Needing a balanced, sober
place to stand,
fleshing patron’s visions,
attracting open souls
remaining ready, sane…calm.
