On a couch made of clouds
like a mist in a dream 
it will come to you
and you will hasten -
in shoes made of dusk
running you headlong
into the dark,
trampling unseen,
out of control
toward an intuition.

Stars crisp against
the black sky
will melt the brown
low clouds like butter
so the wolf moon
can point you 
to my ashes.
