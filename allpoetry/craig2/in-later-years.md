In later years
senility, indifference,
one loses meditation -
the first lapse 
of self knowledge
stirs the death wish,
dawns the tragedy
of climactic now.

A conch 
on a coral beach
the novice prays
to be removed to 
priesthood and laymen
listening intently 
for the sea -
had it remained 
unto itself
the mantra would 
do more than echo,
dream of having
been eternal home.
