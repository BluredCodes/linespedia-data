title: "Sizar of the ‘Post’"

Dubbed to be a student of
                 peculiar and incredible potency
                 but of meager and limited means
                 your sizarship begins with being
                 pecuniliarly renumerated from
                 the ‘Post’s’ unlimited currency.

                 It is explained that those 
                 of your particular station
                 “Are getting ‘commons’ free also”
                 but will be required to register 
                 in diary form for the ‘Post’
                 a log of such and your initiation.

                 ‘Commons’ is not interpreted
                 so you wait, as you await the rite,
                 being no longer homeless
                 you become easily adjusted
                 to the darkness of the ‘Post’,
                 the record becomes easier to write.

                 Your ‘Post’ induction is announced
                 to be the spectacle of the evening.
                 You are undressed, shackled, rubbed
                 with herbal oils and Cantharidin…
                 the gathering crowd and sjambok
                 leave your breasts and body gleaning.

                 You become the pool of common joy,
                 reduced to the most primal need 
                 which is now spiritual in essence,
                 seemingly emerges from a common womb.
                 You are promoted to voracious,
                 sent to transcend and bleed.
