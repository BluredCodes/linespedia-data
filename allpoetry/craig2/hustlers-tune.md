I thought my leaving
wouldn’t interfere,
it seemed appropriate freight.
I wasn’t able to
hold you tight enough,
the train was running late.
Glance not at the sweat
of my cheating hand
or question destiny’s slate,
for I swallow the dew
of your soul’s concern,
realizing you can’t wait.
Today I’ve written
a rhyme for you,
the effort seems too late.
