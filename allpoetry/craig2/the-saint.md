I want to walk
where Christ walked
barefoot and naked –
dogs will gather quickly,
they will bite my ankles,
lead the way,
scenting the adrenaline
eating my insides,
purpling my skin.

Tattooed harlots will
bring granite nails,
rub my cheeks
with jewelry of thorns -
I will bleed 
upon their breasts, 
Heaven will take note
