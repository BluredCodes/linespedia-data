I’m tired of being me.
I want to think
like a woman,
piss like a dog –
or perhaps
something else –
that won’t answer
the phone,
open the mail,
masturbate -
something that
will be ignored,
not trusted, mistrusted
or depended upon,
never tired or caring,
never loved.
