Where are those
when death has come
that understand its calling,
like the dead certain
empty chamber
that fires anyway
when teased.

The masochist
in search of
final destructive anger,
becomes affixed
by the ability
to read tea leaves
in fingerprints of wonder -
know that violence too
is a spirit, a muse
an acquired taste 
an unsupported
addiction.
