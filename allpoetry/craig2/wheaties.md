Tiger Woods
Tony the Tiger
Mickey Mantle
Mary Lou Reton -
three dollars a box
two hundred flakes.

Cereal for fiber
milk for the cat -
Breakfast of champions
lunch for losers
dinner for the destitute -

soggy in the sink
the rest of the day.
