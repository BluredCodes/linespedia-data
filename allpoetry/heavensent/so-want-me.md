---
title: "So Want Me"
writer: "HeAveNsEnT"
slug: "so-want-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/18592-So-Want-Me-by-HeAveNsEnT"
---

Temptation, sensation
Resist, persist
I want to, but I can't
I can't bring myself down
Down to your level, not again
I'm too good for that
And now I see
No longer will I be your toy
So want me
That's as far as it goes
