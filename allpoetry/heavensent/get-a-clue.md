---
title: "Get a Clue!"
writer: "HeAveNsEnT"
slug: "get-a-clue"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/18209-Get-a-Clue--by-HeAveNsEnT"

Look at you
Now look at me
You think you have a chance to see?
As if! Please! Forget it man!
I think you need to change your plan
Don't kid yourself:
Get real
I'm the chick with all the appeal
And you are?
A player? "Da Man"?
You talk to me as if you can
You don't fool me
You'll never see what I can do
Just move on and get a clue!
