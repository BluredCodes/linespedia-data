---
title: "Better Off Dead"
writer: "HeAveNsEnT"
slug: "better-off-dead"
source: "AllPoetry"

Can't do this, can't do that
Walked all over like a door mat
Alone with the thoughts in my head
Better than this, better of dead

Can't go here, can't go there
Sliced into pieces just like a pear
Alone in the sheets of my bed
Better than this, better off dead

Get me out, take me away
Here- no longer can I stay
Gone to far, been mislead
Better than this, better than dead
