---
title: "Don't Play Me"
writer: "HeAveNsEnT"
slug: "dont-play-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/22011-Dont-Play-Me-by-HeAveNsEnT"

You don't return my calls
Don't call up at my place
Why don't you get some balls
And say it to my face?
If you don't want to talk 
To me anymore
I think it's time I walk 
Right out that little door
Don't play me with this shit
I don't get down like that
Just spit it out, say it
Lay it to me flat
I don't want to hate you
Or dislike you at all
But if you play me like a fool
Then you're the one at fault
You send me these mixed signals boy
I don't know what to do
Should I stick around and be your toy
Or move on like you seemed to?
What's it gonna be
Forever or not at all?
I don't have time to be
Waiting for your call
