Hello...
Are you there?
Weren't you sent to protect me?
To help me along my way
Where the fuck are you?
Are you even there?
I would really like to know
Where are you when things go so wrong
That I feel like giving up
And letting go to all of the things that tell me
It's not even worth it
Where the fuck are you?
Tell me please!
Where are you when I need someone
Who can actually do something for me?
Because it seems that no matter how much I beg and pray,
Nothing ever gets through to you
...some guardian angel
