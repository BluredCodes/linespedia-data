---
title: "Anything For You"
writer: "HeAveNsEnT"
slug: "anything-for-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/18210-Anything-For-You-by-HeAveNsEnT"
---

I love you with all of me
My body, soul, and heart
No matter how happy I may be,
When you're so sad, it tears me apart
You say you need me, not in that way
Just for now, as your best friend
Well that also means that you need to say 
That you will be there till the end
So hard to keep these feelings undercover
But it's just so plain and mere
I need you as my friend and my lover
Don't forget about what's still here
So I'm asking you now
Please answer me true
Do you think you could ever somehow
Love me as much as I love you?
Let's forget about the past
It holds all pain and sorrow
We need to stop putting ourselves last
And look upon tomorrow
Every time I see your face
I look into your eyes
It makes my heart skip in its pace
A small piece of me dies
At night I go to bed
I cry myself to sleep
Thoughts that race throughout my head
Tell me I'm in too deep
I can't live like this, it's killing me
But I know what I have to do
I love you so much that I would be
Anything for you
