---
title: "Who Cares?"
writer: "HeAveNsEnT"

Slap in the face
Stab in the back
Play with my heart and step on it when you're done
When you're done using me, throw me away
Don't worry about how I feel
I have no emotions anyway
I am empty, my senses are dulled
Get my hopes up only to let me down in the end
It's ok. I can't feel it.
Remember?
I try to recall who I used to be...
Blankness.
It's over.
