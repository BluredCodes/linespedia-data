---
title: "F*ck You!"
writer: "HeAveNsEnT"
slug: "fck-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/664-F-ck-You--by-HeAveNsEnT"

All night and all day
I've been feeling this way
Doing nothing more than being lazy
I think I'm going completely crazy
I'm wishing for anything
Even a mood swing
Feeling like I'm canned
While my life is being planned
By people who are not me
That's not how it should be
I can handle my own life
Without you to add to my strife
Don't get mad when I raise my tone
Just shut the fuck up and leave me alone
I don't want your bullshit
Right now I can't deal with it
Don't take a step over that line
Or things will become much less than fine
You're trying to tell me what to do
Well excuse me but, who the fuck are you?
Exactly who do you think you are
To press on my buttons and push me this far?
Oh, I guess you think you're pretty damn smart
But you've got something wrong there sweetheart
I am not stupid, I know what's going on
You're playing my life like I am your pawn
From your dream you need to wake
And try to stop being so fucking fake
Don't tell me what to do 
By the way, I've got two words-FUCK YOU!
