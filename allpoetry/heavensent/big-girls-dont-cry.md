They say big girls don't cry,
But what do they know anyway?
They say don't let love pass you by,
But what about the games you play?
They say what doesn't kill you makes you strong,
But how strong can one person be?
They say live life full, live life long,
But sometimes my future is hard to see
They say go with the flow, 
And if you love something, let it go
I guess I'll open the door and let you flee
Let your wings spread and fly,
Hope you might come back to me
Because I can't let love pass me by
I won't hold you down and keep you here,
No matter what, I have to face this fear
So off you go, into the world,the sea
I hope you know you mean the world to me
And if you don't, I'm telling you now,
We'll be together some day, some way, some how
And when that day comes, I will finally be complete
But for now, as incomplete as I may be,
I will wait for you because you are-
