I can make you feel ecstasy times three
Show you the way it's supposed to be
If this could only be the way it goes
Only thing is you don't wanna be with me

I can keep you up all night
So good you don't wanna turn out the light
If this could only be the way it is
Only problem is you don't think me right

I can bring you to the point of no return
Heat it up baby make the fire burn
But the thing is you won't let me show you
I could make it so right, my touch you would yern

If you would just give me a chance
I could teach you how to dance
Make you reach insanity
It's insane that you don't want me
