title: "Mind War"
writer: "HeAveNsEnT"

In the evening, when the stars are bright
Within myself there is a fight
My emotions war, battle, and scuffle
While my mind is caught in a shuffle
They scream, yell, curse, and bicker
As my cloud of thoughts grows thicker
My mind is racing, I can't even think
Can't bring myself to even blink
Which side will win? Which side will lose?
Or will I again be forced to choose?
To choose between the bad and good
To choose the good side I know I should
It's just that sometimes, the emotions won't let me
They cloud my mind so much that I can't see
So what am I supposed to do?
Because to myself, I can't be true
