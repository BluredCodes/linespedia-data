title: "Turn It On..."

Tension, angst, desperation
Turn it on...
I let myself go
Go to a place that I can only see
Filled with my deepest dreams and desires
It takes control 
And releases me from all of my fears and pains
My heart swells up like a balloon
I think it may burst
From all the beauty that overcomes me
Like a warm bath, I sink in 
And let the waves consume me
They heal my body, inside and out
And make me one with myself again
It's my love, my passion...
