I just don't give a fuck
It's all coming to an end
You've been pushing your luck
There's no more luck to spend

Everything is bullshit
I don't believe it anymore
The tip of the fuse has been lit
I'm throwing your shit out the door

This whole place is about to blow
Time to hope 
Pray for luck
No more playing the dope
Cuz I just don't give a fuck

I don't give a fuck about you
Cuz you don't give a fuck about me
So what are you going to do
When I am no where to be seen?

No more playing nice
No more playing games
Now take your chances roll the dice
See if I care when you're up in flames.
