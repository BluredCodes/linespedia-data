---
title: "Keep Me Warm"
writer: "HeAveNsEnT"
slug: "keep-me-warm"

What I like to do is watch you sleep
Late at night when my thoughts are deep
My eyes search your face-they wander and wander
The love I feel, you can't even ponder
In your arms I feel protection
Behind your closed eyes there is perfection
Chills up my spine whenever you touch
If you could only know I care so much
Every time we're together, I get this sensation
I know I could never resist your temptation
You open your eyes and look into mine
It's too hard to take, this crosses the line
I just don't understand how you live each day
Being so wonderful in any possible way
Your face moves to mine, our lips come together
How could this night get any better?
It can't possibly
Because close to you I wish I could always be
But I know this night will have to end
With other things my time I will spend
For now I sleep under your arm
With all of your body to keep me warm
Close your eyes, go back to sleep
The memory of this night I will always keep
