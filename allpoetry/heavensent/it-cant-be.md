---
title: "It Can't Be!"
writer: "HeAveNsEnT"
slug: "it-cant-be"

I can't believe you're leaving 
It simply can't be true
I can't believe you're leaving
What am I gonna do without you?

I can't believe all these times are over
They'll all be in the past
I can't believe all these times are over
But didn't we have a blast?

I can't believe you won't be there
When you're the one I need
I can't believe you won't be there
Will you stay if I beg and pleed?

I can't believe everything we've done
Will have to be kept in my memory
I can't believe everything we've done
And do you know how much you've done for me?

You've been by my side through thick and thin
No matter if it was a lose or win
And when the rest of the world was walking out
You were the one that was walking in

If ever I needed a quick fix
Or to talk about how guys are such dicks
All I did was pick up the phone
And dial your number to get my kicks

I know it's really not the case
That it's all coming to an end
I can always remember your smiling face
The face of my very best friend

I know it's really not the case
That this is our last endeavor
There will still be more fun times to face
And you will be in my heart forever
