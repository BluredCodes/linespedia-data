---
title: "I Hate Myself For Loving You"
writer: "HeAveNsEnT"

I love you
But I hate you
Time and time again
You shit on me
And I've come to a conclusion
I'm stupid
I'm stupid for letting this happen
I'm so stupid for doing this to myself
Because I love you with all my fucking heart
But you don't care!
You know what?....have her!
Just fucking have her!
Because I can't do it anymore
It's so fucking hard
To live
To live with you and to live without you
This isn't how it should be
I'm 15 fucking years old
I should be having fun and going out
Not laying in my bed crying myself to sleep
Praying to God to help me or let me die
Oh yes I've been there
Been there and back and where has it taken me?
Back to the same goddamn place over and over
That's not love
So do I really love you?
Or hate you?
No one has EVER made me feel like this
So why do you?
Just kill me...PLEASE!
Because living is too much for me
I am weak
Weak because of you
I hate myself for loving you
