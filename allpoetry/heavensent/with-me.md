---
title: "With Me"
writer: "HeAveNsEnT"
slug: "with-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I thought you were the one for me
As I was for you
But now the truth I clearly see
I don't know what to do
She's got you wrapped around her finger
To you, she can do no wrong
Whatever she does, you still linger
Because you think your love is strong
When we were together it was all good
Until I found out the game you were playing
You were keeping it all under the hood
The things you were doing and saying
You didn't think about my heart
Just broke it right in half
But why, with me, would you start
Only to end it all with a laugh
She's the one that plays with your mind
She's the only one you see
But I'll be damned if you ever find
Somewhere better than it is with me.
