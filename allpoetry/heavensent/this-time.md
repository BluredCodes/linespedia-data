---
title: "This Time"

This time I can't say that I don't care
About this bond we seem to share
I need to know you feel it too
Is this love really true?
Before I met you my life was a bore
Everything I did, seemed I'd done it before
And every day in my sorrow
I prayed there would be no tomorrow
But this time I can't say that I don't care
About my life that's so unfair
I finally got what's long overdue
A love so perfect, only you
You brightened up my life for me
The light was out, I now can see
You make me want to be alive
And pick myself up from that dive
And every night I fall asleep 
Knowing that you're mine to keep
Don't ever leave, promise me please
I need you, all my pains you ease
If we ever were to part
You know that it would break my heart
This time I can say that loving you
Is all I really want to do
