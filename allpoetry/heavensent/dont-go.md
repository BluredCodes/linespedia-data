---
title: "Don't Go"
writer: "HeAveNsEnT"
slug: "dont-go"
source: "AllPoetry"

What's been going on between you and me?
Is there a problem that I can't see?
Please tell me what's wrong before it's too late
I know we can change this relationship's fate
I love you so much that it hurts me to wonder
What would happen if this all went under
We can get through it, this is something I know
You just need to let how you really feel show
If I lost you, I don't know what I'd do
Just how could I live, live without you?
Was it something I did, something I said?
Don't you know that I'd rather be dead
Than ever hurt you, or make you feel bad
It would pain me too to see you so sad
You know that it was meant to be
For you to meet and be with me
So why do you doubt what we have here?
Losing your love is my biggest fear
How are you so blind not to see
That how you act is killing me?
I need your touch, I need your kiss
I know that I can't live like this
Just look in my eyes and see if you feel
Anything that tells you it's still real
Because every day I get up only to
Live my life to be with you
Please baby, don't take that away
I'm begging you, don't go, stay
If you go, you're taking part of my life
I know I can't live with that kind of strife
The strife of knowing that I can't say
That you are mine, forever, always
Let's talk about it and work it out
We can make it happen, I have no doubt
But don't just let it slip away
Because I love you more with every day
