title: "Left Out"
writer: "HeAveNsEnT"

A picture's worth a thousand words
A memory's worth a million
A story's worth a thousand laughs
To have been there's worth a billion
The time's that are spent are worth so much
But what are you talking about?
The pictures and stories will have to do
Because I must have been left out
