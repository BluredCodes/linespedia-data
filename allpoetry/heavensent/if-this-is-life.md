---
title: "If This Is Life"
writer: "HeAveNsEnT"
slug: "if-this-is-life"
source: "AllPoetry"

Wearing out my socks, pacing
Heart racing
All of this from facing
The fact that it's done
It's done and gone and it's not coming back
And I'm starting to wonder if I even want it
Suddenly break into tears
Having to face my fears
Through all of my years
I've never felt pain like this
And I'm starting to wonder if this is life
My hands and legs are shaking
My body aches from taking
The gash you've given to my heart from faking
Your promise to me that's now been broken
And I'm starting to wonder if you ever meant it
I can't breathe, my chest is weak
Hard to bring my voice to speak
I feel so small and meek
Because I don't want this anymore
And I'm starting to wonder if I need it
Is this life, or hell?
Or are they the same place?
Help me.
