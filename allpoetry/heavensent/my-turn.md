---
title: "My Turn"
writer: "HeAveNsEnT"

I have to be strong, I tell myself now
I have to find how to get past it somehow
I live and learn and learn it well
I'll hold on to what I've got like hell
I have to be wise, I know how to be
I have to know even the things I don't see
I found myself, and within I found power
Power and respect that can make them all cower
I have to be tough, there's no other way
I have to block out all the things that they say
No one should matter to me, but me
Just being myself is all I can be
I'm glad I know now, what took me so long
I have to grow wise before growing strong
It may be hard and hurt it may
But sometimes I've just gotta turn away
Moving on and letting go
Holding back feelings I can't bear to show
What feels right is mostly not real
People change with the way that they feel
Experience is my teacher, I know him well
He helps me remember the times that I fell
I use them to guide me through new situations
Without having hopes and high expectations
And when I start to lose my way
He tells me tomorrow's another day
Another day to live and learn 
Let myself live...it's my turn
