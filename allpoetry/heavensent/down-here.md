---
title: "Down Here"
writer: "HeAveNsEnT"
slug: "down-here"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/675-Down-Here-by-HeAveNsEnT"
---

Here I lay, face down on the floor
Thinking of everything that's been, that will be
I just can't take this anymore
Help me out of this place that I've put me

I'm trapped inside of myself, deep down
So dark down here, I'm scared, terrified
The water is rising so high I could drown
I could drown in this sea of tears that I've cried

Here I lay, back flat on the ground
Dreaming of everything that's been, that should be
The thoughts in my head are spinning around
Lend me your hand and set me free

I'm locked up within my own mind
So cold down here, I'm shivering, shaking
The fog is clouding so thick I can't find
I can't find the way out I should be taking

Here I lay, spread out on the wood
Wishing for everything I want, that I need
I would help myself if I only could
Just lift me back up and do one good deed

I'm shackled and chained down inside my head
So lonely down here, I'm abandoned, alone
The walls are closing in on where I've been led
Down here I shall die, my fate is now shown
