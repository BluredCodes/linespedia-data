LOVE

The theme of love
Has been a constant thread
Running throughout time.
And a building block to civilization.
WITH IT,
People are able to work together.
WITHOUT IT,
People have torn each other to pieces.
WITH LOVE,
Even though poor or suffering
People have found hope and happiness.
WITHOUT IT,
Little else has meaning.
WITH LOVE,
People thrive and grow.
WITHOUT IT,
In one way or another~
They die.
Love's absence brings about death,
While love's presence 
Creates life.
It gives energy and strength
it brings unity and peace.
To the extent that there is love~
There is also human persons.
Love provides the meaning
for why we are all here.
Love is the most powerful 
And creative force around.
Nuclear Technology
Can harness the physical energies of the earth
BUT 
Only love can touch the human spirit.
Weapons kill the body,
But
Only the refusal to love
Can destroy the soul.
