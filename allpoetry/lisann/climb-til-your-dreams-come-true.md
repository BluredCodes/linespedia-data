Climb Til Your Dreams Come True

Often your tasks will be many
And the more you think you can do~
Often your roads will be rugged,
And your hills insurmountable too.
But always remember
The hills ahead
Are never as steep as they seem.
And faith in your heart starts upwards,
And climb until you reach your dreams.
For nothing in life with worth
Is ever too hard to achieve.
If you have the courage to try it
And the faith to believe.
For faith is a force that is greater
Than  knowledge,
Or power,
Or skill.
And many defeats turn to triumph
If you trust in God's wisdom and will.
For faith is a mover of mountains
There is nothing that God cannot do
So start out today
With faith in your heart
And climb until your dreams come true.
