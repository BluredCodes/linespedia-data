THE AWAKENING HEART

Awakening~
Is not a matter of weeks,
Or years,
Or lifetimes.
It is a loving and patient unfolding.
A journey INTO love.
We only have to let go 
Of what blocks our hearts 
To see the love and light
That shines within
Each and every one of us.
Oftentimes.....
We allow our fears and frustrations
Rule our lives
And we miss out on all the love and compassion
That is being offered to us by others.
The most difficult task
Any of us can endure
Is to believe in ourselves.
To quiet our spirit and listen.
Listen to the answers that lie within
Our hearts and souls.
We have all come upon a moment in our lives
When~
In deep pain and suffering
We question our faith.
We pick at the scabs and scars
In our hearts
Searching for answers.
Answers, we fear
We may never find.
BUT.....
If we truly listen
We will know
That fear is a place
That has not yet discovered love.
FEAR SEES LIMITS
WHILE LOVE SEES POSSIBILITIES.
Sometimes....
We allow our fears to paralyze us
We clutch our past
So tightly to our chest
That it leaves our arms
Too full to embrace the present.
We all have lessons  to learn
In our lives
But~
We  only have one purpose---
LOVE.
Without love in our hearts,
Not just for others,
But for ourselves as well,
We are lost.
Love is the seed 
That gave birth to my spiritual awakening.
It is the only truth I have.
It is the most precious gift I have to offer
And I offer it to you
UNCONDITIONALLY
All I ask in return,
Is for you to open your hearts to receive it.
And live in the light and the love
That is within each and every one of you.
