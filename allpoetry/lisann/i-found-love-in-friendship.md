I Found Love in Friendship

Just when I thought 
Time had set me free,
Memories from my past 
Kept haunting me.
I did everything I could 
To ease the pain,
But those memories were too strong,
And I'd relive them once again.
I am so tired of
Listening to the sound of my tears,
Tired of allowing my life
To be controlled
By my fears.
In my life,
When everything had gone wrong,
I needed a friend
And that's when you came along.
You gave me a gift
When you opened your heart
I knew you were special
Right from the start.
You entered my heart
And destroyed my wall,
You've shown me more patience
Than I can ever recall.
I wanted so much
To run from you,
To protect my heart
So you couldn't hurt me too.
BUT~
As time went on
You opened my eyes
And you set my heart free,
You are more than a friend
YOU ARE A SISTER TO ME.
With you in my life
I learned how to love
You are my angel
Sent from God up above.
I spent my life 
Living in fear
Running from love
Year after year.
I finally stopped running,
Stopped being scared.
I learned how to love,
I learned how to care.
I still get confused at times
And I hope this will change
Your friendship has helped me
To release my heart from this pain.
You gave me hope
Deep down inside
You've shown me
I no longer need to run and hide.
You have broken my walls
Released me from my past
I see my future
With love at last.
You gave me it all
But you will never know
Just how much 
You have helped me to grow.
