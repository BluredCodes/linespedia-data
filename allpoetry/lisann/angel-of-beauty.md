Angel  Of  Beauty

A beaten, tortured child
Whose pain is very deep
A child with a battered heart
Is tormented while she sleeps.
Tose who care~
Cannot be found,
To offer the love she needs
Her body and soul so badly abused
She lies in wait~
While her heart bleeds.
She cannot seek out comfort
From any human being
When those that are supposed to love her 
Are the ones to make her scream.
She struggles to survive in life,
Has no one to show her the way,
Can any one person make a difference?
YES~
I found that woman today.
Her heart is filled with beauty
Her soul,
With pure, sweet love.
She's a woman of strong nature
An "angel" from above.
She's given so much to this child
More than she'll ever know.
The love she has to ffer
Has helped this child to grow.
This "child" is no longer a child,
But a woman fully grown.
Her heart no longer fearful,
From the love this angel has shown.
This battered beaten child,
Has learned to share her light
The  angel of great beauty,
Gave this child new sight.
You see..........
It only takes one person
To readily impart
That goodness really does exist,
It begins inside your heart.
I have one of these "angels"
One of my very own.
She gave me all the love I needed,
When she made her heart my home.
I will always be very grateful
To this angel from above,
She has given me the gift of friendship
I have given her my love.
May our souls be always united
And never grow apart,
This angel of Great Beauty
Lives forever in my heart.
