IN  THE  LIGHT

I see the land before me
So soft and moist with dew.
The river and most beautiful trees,
Have come into my view.
Beyond the path I walk now---
A woman does appear,
This vision of an angel
To take away my fears.

I take the path that leads me
Walk in this place of light,
I sit here and I ponder
While the candle burns so bright.

My heart it is so heavy
My life is hard you see---
So much in life to deal with
It takes its toll on me.
I try with all that's in me
To always do what's right
But each day is a struggle
But I try with all my might.

So much of life around me
That troubles my heart so
It's hard for you to imagine
This sadness that I know.
How could you possibly help me
What is it you can do?
To ease my troubled spirit
And open my heart for you.

With purity of heart and soul
Within this sacred place
I heard your "special message"
It filled me with such grace.
I only needed one person
TO show me a gentle love
The spirit of an angel,
Sent from up above.

Joy that falls upon you
When you reached out to touch my hand
Reaching out with comfort
You had a "special plan."

The greatest gift you've given
That I could really use
Wisdom of my freedom
That only I could choose.

I cannot return this "gift" now
For I found a friend in you,
Your heart that shared a message,
The heart inside me---grew.

I gathered all my sorrows
And left my heart with her
She gave me all the courage
To open my hearts door.

Each step I take with wisdom
A smile I find with peace
Love that I've discovered now
Let happiness increase.

For those times that I felt burdened
Each tear that I have cried
Was caught within your heart of love
The sadness in me has died.
For you have brought this to me
This joyous,
Wonderous start,
For you have done God's blessing
When you reached and touched my heart.
