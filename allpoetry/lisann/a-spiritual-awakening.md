A SPIRITUAL  AWAKENING

Can I touch with love
All that I have closed my heart to,
And share honestly with you
The true gift of myself?

YES!

I can meet the world 
With an open heart
And receive all the blessings 
Of this abundant universe
WIth gentle and sensitive hands.
I can look at the world
Through the eyes of compassion
And of LOVE.
Each of us has a spirit in us
Sometimes hidden,
Sometimes more available.
But this light,
This generosity,
And this peace
Are our greatest gifts to the earth.
We share in the beauty of life
And in the ocean of tears.
The sorrow of life
Is part of each of our hearts
And part of what connects us with one another.
We speak and touch one another
As a reflection of our deepest understanding
We discover that our breath
Our body,
And our human limitations
Are a part of the divine,
Spirituality~
Is based simply on the capacity
TO let go.
And to love.
To open the heart to all that is.
The most special gift
YOu could ever give or receive
Is the gift of oneself.
Every person,
Every situation we encounter
May hold some teaching.
Some guidance,
And may become 
The doorway to our awakening.
Accepting one anothers gift of self
As a treasure
Can bring us
Out of the darkness
And into the light,
Out of  our loneliness
And into love,
Out of our partial living
And into the fullness of life.
There are times in our life
When we unexpectedly experience a moment
Of mutual love and care
So sweet and so powerful
That it takes our breath away.
I have experienced this moment
Along my spiritual path
I have come to realize
How beautiful, 
And special, and unique
Each person is
And how God's grace
Flows into this world
Through the channel of our love
My journey,
Has been a slow and gradual process
But I am now ready to accept 
All the joy and prosperity
That life has to offer me,
Emotional scars will always remain
But I see now......
That they are the badges of a 
SURVIVOR
They remind me of my pain,
Yet~
They also remind me
That I have lived through it.
Through forgiveness,
I release the pain
And accept the change.
I see the world with a joyful heart.
Which enables me to 
Love myself,
Love others,
And to love God.
I now see life as a gift to be opened 
Rather than a problem to be solved.
