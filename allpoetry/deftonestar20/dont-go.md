---
title: "Don't go"
writer: "deftonestar20"
slug: "dont-go"
source: "AllPoetry"

I try and act like I don't care.
But I sit here worring if someone will take you away.
You say no one will, ever.
Well hopefully, in my opinion.

You say your attention is on me.
But the moement the attention shifts to someone else,
I start worring again.
Is someone going to take you away from me?

You only say things you mean,
You never lie to me.
You care about me,
But I still worry that someone is going to take you away from me.

I sit here waiting for the phone to ring,
Thinking is that girl on the phone going to take you away from me.
Then I begin to sit here and think, do you worry?
Thinking someone is going to take me away from you?
