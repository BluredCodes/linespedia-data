Stitching of soft beauty,
Starved cravings within.

Gentle touches of a body,
Feel it close to your skin.

Out of hands of effort,
Feeling it deep within.

Wondering if it's stirring,
Stirring from within.

Harden stares of movement,
Feeling it move in.

Closer I feel to you,
When you move it in.

Craving you with each touch,
Tasting you within.

Stunning movements of beauty,
Feeling I'll give in.
