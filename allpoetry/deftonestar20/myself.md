What do I do? Where do I go? Who should I be? Who do I love? I love you You, only you, but you is not here. Where do I go with you here? No where. My life is held on pause, every time you run back into my life. I am now unpausing-pause my life from you, and going on. What do I do? Move on. Where do I go? Away, away from you. Who should I be? A strong willed person, who doesn't need you to live. Who do I love? Myself.
