Inspired by Mystysaint

I walk into fate unknown
the rythm of footsteps upon concrete and water
give calm to racing thoughts of uncertainty.
My world reduced to a bleak sky, an over coat, a hat.
The sinking day wears on
in perpetual rain.

Regrets pit my soul 
Anticipation of dissaproval makes rationale irrellevent.

I open the door
the house is empty
she is gone.

Just as I'd suspected, 
shattered hope.

I sit by the fire
by the window 
with the burning
