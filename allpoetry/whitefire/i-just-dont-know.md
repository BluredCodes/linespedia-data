I care about him so much,sometimes I think he feels the same way,sometimes I am doubtful,I think that I need to get over my own insecuritiesI want so bad to believe that you love me, as much as I love youI will never be able to describe your kiss to anyoneI am the only one who knows the meaning of itI like it that way.I hope that I will be with you foreverOr as long as nature permits~*~WhiteFire~*~(kind of 1 of those things made in a moments thought & stuff, a quick written thing & into really a long thought poem, but still, i feel like sharing it with u all)
