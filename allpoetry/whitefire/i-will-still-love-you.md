---
title: "I will still love you"
writer: "WhiteFire"
slug: "i-will-still-love-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/9382-I-will-still-love-you-by-WhiteFire"

Till ocean currents stop their flow
Till gentle breezes cease to blow
Until the sky's no longer blue
I will still love you
Till there is no more morning dawn
Till all the desert sands are gone
Till rainbows lose their radiant hue
I will still love you
Till birds forget how to fly
Till stars quit glowing in night's sky
Till there's no hint of early morning dew
I will still love you
Till Earth's rotation stops it's turn
Until the sun no longer burns
Till dying...all things fade from view
I will still love you
~*~WhiteFire~*~
