I wish that I could say
Exactly how I feel
But sometimes my world is such a blur
It doesn’t seen to be real
I know that if my heart could have
What it wants deep down inside
You’d be next to me
All the time
If only you had not been taken
So far away from me
Maybe then, and only then, 
Would I be truly happy.as always comments are welcomed, positive or negative :)
