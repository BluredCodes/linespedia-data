I am the wind as I dancein your hair. A breeze gentlytickling, touching u there.The cool air at night, giving lifeto your breath. The soft humminginside, giving rise to your chest.Just like your shadow, I'm alwayson guard. Protecting you always,from all that do harm.The sky, it is me, watching youfrom above. Surrounding youalways, in layers of love.criticism? compliments? anything u wanna say 2 me about my work please do not hesitate to post or email/im me....i won't be offended....cuz knowing how everyone reacts 2 my work & what they think of it helps me become a better writer...thank you,WhiteFire
