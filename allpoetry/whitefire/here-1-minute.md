Here one minute 
the next you are gone.
A love not good
A love not bad,
A love that is broken
Before it had a chance.
So what now? another girl has your heart,
Alas there is one who wants it too.
She cries and moans
About how things are not fair.
Alas you don't comment 
thinking you are in love with this girl
As I lay here and cry myself to sleep
I wonder if I dug a hole to deep.
To tell you how I feel is not possible
It is to hard to tell what is real
