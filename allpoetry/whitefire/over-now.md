So... our "perfect" fairy tale is over
Still I can't escape the ghost of you
Your presence is with me everywhere
And in everything I do


I hear you whispering softly
Everywhere your sun does shine
And your face is unforgettable
It's tattooed in my mindcomments are welcomed & appreciated.....
WhiteFire
