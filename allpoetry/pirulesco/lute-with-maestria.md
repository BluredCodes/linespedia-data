---
title: "Lute with maestria"
writer: "pirulesco"
slug: "lute-with-maestria"
source: "AllPoetry"

Caressing fingers
flowing through the tensed chords
in painful focus
transmit fleeting melodies
as unforgettable gems

************

This was written at the occasion of a lute concert
given by Hopkinson Smith here in Ghent-Belgium
