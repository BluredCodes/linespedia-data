title: "Mystery"
writer: "kay a"

Such a mystery to me 
you are
with your words
of freedom
love speaks to you
I envision 
myself with you
wrapped in your light 
that shines
to the heavens
your heart so pure
so fragile
ready for me 
with scared eyes
but open arms 
shivering from the 
cold before
the sun came out to play

Such a mystery to me
you are
your star lays in my
lap 
cuddling it
closely to my
beating chest afraid
of the moon shining
too brightly
scaring you away
from me

Such a mystery to me
you are
destined I am 
to find you
to feel you
your heart open
my eyes closed
then opened wide to embrace 
all that you give me
