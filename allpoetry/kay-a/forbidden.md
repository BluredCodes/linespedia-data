---
title: "Forbidden"
writer: "kay a"
slug: "forbidden"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Forbidden
is our love
it is much too 
complicated
to feel the way we
do
we want so much 
to be
together
in each other's arms
and yet
it hurts not to 
be
our hearts
intertwine
the way our bodies
yearn to 
but 
we can't
our love is forbidden
so our 
desires cannot be
fullfilled...
