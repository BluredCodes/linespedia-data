---
title: "Your touch"
writer: "kay a"
slug: "your-touch"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

mesmorizing
i'm in another world
soft
enticing
your fingertips
i'm biting my bottom
lip 
waiting for more
slowly
you explore each 
space
each place
on my body 
i'm going crazy
i'm getting wet in your
arms
i'm weak by the 
simple touch 
of what you do to me
limp
and still waiting
for you
to take me 
i moan 
as you moan for me 
to touch you the same
but you have
perfected the art of how
to touch me
i can only lay here
tingling
excited
waiting 
for you 
to touch me some more
