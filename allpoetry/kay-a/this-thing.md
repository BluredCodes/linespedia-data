---
title: "this thing"
writer: "kay a"
slug: "this-thing"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

there are so many questions 
left unanswered 
in this thing called life
in a blink of an eye 
everything is taken away from you
even though it took 
forever to get
love
affection
acceptance
things that are taken for granted
are stripped away
from someone wh cherishes it
more than anything
this thing called
life
is worth nothing 
if in life you have 
nothing...
