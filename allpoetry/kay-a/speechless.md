---
title: "speechless"
writer: "kay a"
slug: "speechless"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

.....I don't know what to say 
you really shocked me
I can't believe that you
out of all 
people
can say that to me
I have been wrong about you
you are just like the rest
.....My mouth hangs to the floor
as my head does in shame
I've started to fall in love
with pain
once again
It always happens but for some 
reason I thought
you were different
you have hurt me
deeply
so deeply that it cuts
like a knife
into my soul
I'm a dissapointment
was all I've ever heard
a failure
I thought that I was an angel
in your eyes
.....You feel the same as 
they do
you hate me for being me
and I am in a puddle 
of hurt 
drowning once more
over you
......I can't say no more
