title: "idiosyncracies"

your words jump up at me
making me feel their meaning
they have their own life
their own soul
they run around the page
whispering sweet nothings
making me afraid of what to
write next
i tremble as i read the 
melodic eccentricities
in each electronic message 
sending me to a place
i'd never thought i'd be
words have soft voices when 
they are sweet talking
blinking eyes at me
changing colors at times with
corresponding backgrounds
to follow the theme
and they make me feel so good
your words
your heart
from miles away
