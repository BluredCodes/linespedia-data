Never in life
can I smile
never in life 
can I laugh
never in life 
can I live a 
sngle happy moment
everything is taken 
from me
and stripped of its
meaning
because of people 
full of hatred 
and
betryal
not knowing who to trust
not knowing who to
turn to
only knowing how to 
suffer alone  

Never in life
can I see
never in life 
can I hear
that maybe one day
life can be different
to how it always is
becase no matter 
what
the world is full of
pain
lies
sorrow
and nothing else
and will never anything but
because
no one cares about nothing else
but themselves
and envy and jealousy
rules their brains
not knowing how to 
live
not knowing how to
love
not knowing anything but how to
suffer alone
