hot and bothered 
in the darkness
my skirt hiked up 
and your warm hands on my thighs
making me want to scream
yoour zipper is down 
wanting to penetrate my insides
wanting to tear it apart
with sweet
pleasures 
of the heart
we try not to make noise
but it is difficult
in these seats
they squeak
we laugh which makes us
want each other more
your tongue
hungrily searches my throat
for love
and 
you squeeze me tight
with satisfaction
i scratch your back and
with one thrust 
you enter me with force
while the movie plays 
for the fifth time...
