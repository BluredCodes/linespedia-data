---
title: "The music"
writer: "kay a"
slug: "the-music"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

How sweet
the sounds of happiness
though I will never
hear it
the pitter patter
of heartbeats 
in love
that I will never feel
it's the cold hard truth
that I must face
pain will 
forever surround me
and
I have no choice
but to deal 
with it...
