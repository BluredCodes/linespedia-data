---
title: "Searching"
writer: "kay a"

She looks upon the heavens
for answers
but they seem to be higher
than that
she can't seem to find
a resolution for her
heart and her soul
dreams are not her reality
yet her downfall
for she isn't
positive that she will finally
be happy the way she 
knows she should 
her possibilities are so
far away but
she still prays
that one day she will 
soar above the clouds
with her one true
love but
she falls down into a hole
of negativity once
she sees an eclipse
taking over
she has been faithful
she has been true but her
life is broken in two
never to be put back together
to be unfixed forever
is not how she planned it
secretly still dreaming 
of being
wisked away to boundaries 
unknown but
she has to wait 
even though 
she may not have enough
time...
