DREAMING...

my heart opens wide
my legs do the same
waiting for you to 
come inside
and take me to a 
faraway land of make-believe
where we can pretend
we are alone
hot and bothered
ready to embark on an
adventure that's all
our own
taking my skin
and bonding it with yours
wrapping around you
becoming one
as we brn inside each other's
fire
exploring each crevice at the
same time
slipping 
sliding around our naked
wet bodies
out of control 
entering a world beyond
pretend
where it is suddenly reality
and we both explode...

then i wake up
in a puddle of my desire
for you
