It's amazing 
the things you think 
of when 
you are at your worst 

jumping out of the 
window 
seems like the best solution 
to all of life's 
problems...just reposting this poem, because i mispelled 'jumping' by mistake and i seemed to have confused everyone...sorry for the inconvenience
