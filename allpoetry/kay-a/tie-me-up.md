Tie me up
do away with me
love me from head to toe
without any interruptions
from me
lick my lips
let your tongue
do the walking
my hard nipples stand at 
attention
as you suck away 
the firmness
my belly trembles with
excitement
anticipation
my navel ring jingles
our favorite song
making me high
go further down to the place
you know you
want to be
make my clit swell up to the
size it should be
so that I can take you in
whole
give it to me
slow 
then hard
until we both can't take it 
anymore
Tie me up 
baby
and do away 
with
me...
