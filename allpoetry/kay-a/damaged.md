---
title: "Damaged"
writer: "kay a"
slug: "damaged"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/5478-Damaged-by-kay-a"
---

Over and over 
again
making mistakes
comes natural to you
hurting yourself
hurting me
it doesn't make 
sense
to do this to yourself
over and over 
again
it can't feel good 
to cry
to be in pain
you tear yourself 
in half
doing damage
to your soul
to my heart
just stop
I tell you
over and over 
again
but nothing registers
you don't want to
you want to carry
on this way 
forever
but I can't take 
much more 
so you have to 
wallow in your own 
self-pity
for the mistakes
life threatening mistakes
that you make
over and over 
again...
