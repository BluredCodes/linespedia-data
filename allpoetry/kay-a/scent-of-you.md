---
title: "Scent of you"
writer: "kay a"
slug: "scent-of-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

I can still smell you 
on my sheets
your scent drives me crazy
as I am 
alone at night
longing for your touch 
that sweet caress
that I miss
the way you kissed me
left me 
yearning for more
your body
now more defined than ever
conjured feelings 
for you 
that I just can't control
my body shakes at
the very thought of you 
squeezing me
your sweat in my hair
moaning softly in my ear
the distance between us
are much more than
miles
we are oceans apart
and I want you here 
with me
doing the things that
you do to me
I dream of you coming to 
me with that
strut in your step
and taking me to ecstacy 
without even a thought of
coming back
to reality
all of this and more
from the scent of 
you
