I like the way you mess with me
in the morningtime
rubbing yourself 
against me 
hoping I wake up soon
Kissing my neck 
Touching me softly
gently
barely 
just enough for me to moan
tossing and turning
with a smile on my face

I like the way you mess with me
in the morningtime
your finger massaging my
legs
thighs
that forbidden area
unknowingly wet
and waiting for more
your breath
hot and on my neck
changing my dreams into passion
feeling your hardness
on me
wanting to so much to 
be inside
but I'm not awake yet
so you go only so far

I like the way you mess with me
in the morningtime
maybe I'll surprise you once
and 
open my eyes....
