title: "'The Truth Within My Friend'"

Last night was very hard for me to make it through,I was afraid our friendship was coming to an  end,but I wouldn't let that happen, I need you as a friend.I will forget about the past,It's all gone, so let it restIt seems like you're my sister,I tell you all the facts,I trust you more than anyone,No matter if or thatSome people think we're close,but others disagree, Although i could tell you truthfully,That we were meant to beI don't know but it just seems we've come a long, long way,It all went by so quickly,but in the end so swiftly I know we may move on and find another friend,but I will not forget you, I know our friendship won't endI will be there when you are down,I will miss you when you are not around,I will trust you always, I hope you can trust me,You will always be thought of everyday,As the greatest friend to me
