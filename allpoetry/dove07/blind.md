BlindSitting by the window, I feel the sunshine rays,Caving into me like the heart warm of the day,Casting shadows on my eyelids, no longer are theydry lids.Walking through the hallways, people rush by me,They won't take a moment to even let me breath.Getting on the school bus, I always miss the steps,You'd think I learned to get around but well you know the rest.
