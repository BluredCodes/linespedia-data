Winter Blizzard    The white snow falls so silently,    As it touches the waiting ground.    It keeps coming so we can see that is covers all the blossom bees        Sitting in front of the window,     The wind just nicks the pain,    I watch as it suddenly starts to rain.    The leaves turn to ice,    They are so thin,    Just one touch and they start to dim.    The grass is covered very deep,    Inside the snow where it will sleep.    As the sun comes up,     The snow is melt,    The Winter Blizzard turns to felt.
