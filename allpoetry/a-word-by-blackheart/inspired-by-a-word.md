title: "'Inspired by a word.'"
writer: "a word. by blackheart"

Inspirational austerity... eating at my words,
i scribble endless apathy, without a cause at all,
It’s all without a meaning,
Implication or devise.
But yet again i sit and compose...
A waste of time.

And yet, here i am... again.
Inspired by inspiration...
Such a pretty game, dancing with my muse...
Inspired by inspiration.

Perpetual indecency in my every other utter,
Some way of being dirty to you and every other,
It’s all somehow structured,
Strong with standing pride.
To you i send my greatest lines...
A waste of time.

So here i am... again.
Inspired by inspiration...
A reverie of upland wonder showed me how to be...
Inspired by inspiration.

And...

Perhaps i’ll leave it here,
Just words enough to make it clear,
That i will write on always,
Even if it’s nowhere near;
Meaningful or perfect,
Implied or structured thus...
Because i am inspired,
It’s writing that i love.To : Highland Dreams ... for inspiring me with a word *s*
