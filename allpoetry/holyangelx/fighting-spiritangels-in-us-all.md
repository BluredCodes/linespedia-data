One day you see deathDeath of family death of a friendIt hurts so bad and will never endYet time has come to end with themYour mind circles and becomes blurYour heart akes and becomes absurdWhy cant you stop it stop the hurtIt causes you to cry and and you keep asking whyThe pain of hate and fear lye withinYet still you keep asking whenThen one day you wake and prayYou walk outside to see the dayYou see children running around in playBut you hear a scream and people turn the other wayNext your fire burnsYour blood boilsYour heart beatsAnd your mind whirlsYou scream for strengthAnd your soul swellsThe next thing you knowYour raising some hellBefore the days outYou hold the enemy in you handsYou gaze in thier eyes As they gaze in yoursThen they says bitch I never thought I would see the dayThe day when I would have to pay
