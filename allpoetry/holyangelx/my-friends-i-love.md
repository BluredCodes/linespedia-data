title: "My friends I love"

All my friends I do loveThe wicked the sorrowfulAnd the ones that need hugsThanks for all the timesWe have been with each otherLife continues and we will always see each otherLife is smooth and life is sweetBut the friends I have are the ones I wish to keepI love my friends cause they weepI love my friends even when they sleepMy friends are great and irreplaceableThere priceless and thats what the meaning of life isI was told that I was popularAnd I didnt know why butThe reason was every body knew my stuffThe way I see it is every one knows my styleIts crazy I would think But the friends I have are the friends I keep
