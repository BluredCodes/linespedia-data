title: "The World Color's I see"
writer: "HolyAngelX"
slug: "the-world-colors-i-see"
source: "AllPoetry"

Blue the color of the sky peacefully and high.  Green the color of leaves swaying in the trees.  Red the color of blood so hurtful and full of sorrow.  Then black the color of shadows that I hide in the night.  Yellow the warm sun that feels great on your face.  Purple the beutifull color of twilight.  The darkness is bleak and fear settles in black thats when we wait for the day of the white peace to play back.
