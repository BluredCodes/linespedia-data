I have a love in my life it feels great knowing she will soon be my wife.  Yet my life and purpose will not end till the world is at peace again.  It feels great knowing I'm a soldier in an incredible entities army and I fight for peace in my life.  I'm a kind warrior with a heart fill with hate and anger.  But its only there for me to stay and fight longer and more for other days.  I have friends who need to be protected so thats why I'm here for there sake and peace too.  I have a very fragile blonde acting friend whomes protection from me will never end.  I have a sweet and sorrowfull girl that needs my hugs to keep her warm.  Theres a friend I know who just needs to see that life is full of people new everyday not just the one she wants to win.  I have a friend who weeps and mopes hes the one I watch secound to the most I worry cause I fear he may be the one to die pesamesticaly before the three.  Last but not least the one I watch the most my soon to be wife and the one who will always be in my life.  She bears my child he will soon be born then there will be another soul for me to watch over.  This is my life my purpose my path and why you ask I watch these people what do they do for you and I still say dont worry they dont have to do anything just be my friend and watch me too they all watch over me as i watch over them and it feels great knowing that people care for me and will to the end.
