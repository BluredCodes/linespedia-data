What kinda world would this be if I weren't hereHow would things change if I never existedI could never grasp the answerOr ever grasp the matterI wont know cause I am alive But this question stirs my mind like a buzzing hiveWould my friends be different in ones senseOr would my friends be the sameWould Chris be a syko suicideor would have all ready diedWould Heather be more wickedOr would she just always be tickedWould Becka play all her lovesOr would she stick with oneWould Pam be the same Or would she be just a painThen the thought of how Erin would beWould she never have a free will Would she never know the feelingThe feeling of love The pation of heartfelt feelingsOr would she just not existSo I ask what would this world be likeIf I were to never exist.
