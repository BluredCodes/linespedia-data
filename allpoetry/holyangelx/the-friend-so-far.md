The friend thats so keen.My friend thats so neat.She's the best friend I met.And was always the best.At first she seemed to be distant.And I wanted to be her friend too.I don't know why but one day.She came up to me and almost made me cry.You could say tis a miracle.But that day she wanted a hug.She said hi and I was surprised.I didn't know why but she was kind.It felt great knowing she was kewl.Yet now I know how life can be cruel.Because even though she turned out for the best.Life still put us to the test.When our seeing eachother kind of seaced to exist.She moved far away but still we can all se her in the sky.She smiled so greatly yet she came to visit that one summer so greatly.But still she had to go and she is still my friend my very own.So lifes little test didnt prove anything wrong becuase we always have eachother and always for long.
