The night was quiet and I was silent.  There was no light and there was no traffic.  The wind in my face and the moon lit my rage.  I am the Angel of Death and my life is evils last dyeing breath.  I fly the nights as my wings glow with pearl lights.  My anger is in my fist and my heart is frozen with hate.  I fight this war to keep my fate.  I'm the angel of the light but there's always a shadow in my life.  I may be the demon of the night but when you see my rageing fire dont be afraid im here for all those who live my way hate my evil and want to live life with peace and right.  Im here for those who love those who are loved im here for the hearts that are burning with life and have no end.  But the ones that hear my name and fear my game are the ones you will see vanish only in the night so look out because once my wings glow so bright with the moons light you will disapear from site.  This is my life as and Angel of Death and this is always my fight.
