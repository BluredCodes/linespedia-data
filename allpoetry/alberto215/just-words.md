JUST WORDS

THOSE OCCASIONAL INADVERTENT WORDS THAT OPEN THE GATE
OF MEMORIES REPRESSED;
THAT REMIND YOU OF YOUR CYNICISM, DISTRUST AND DESPAIR
THOSE GENTLE WORDS, THAT COME WITHOUT WARNING
SPOKEN SOFTLY WITHOUT MALICE
NOT INTENDED FOR THEIR EFFECT
SPAWNED FROM CARING YET DRIPPING WITH NEGLECT

WHY ARE THEY SPOKEN?
YOU FIGHT THEM OFF
ALLOWING THEM TO PASS

THROUGH
