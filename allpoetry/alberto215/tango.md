I cannot do justice to Tango in verse
To show it in film would even be worse
You must take my hand and let yourself go
Trust yourself to my arms and let the beat flow
A glimpse of its power you've been able to hear
In the music created by masters we cheer
But understand this, you will never know dance
Until Tango entraps you with its magical trance
