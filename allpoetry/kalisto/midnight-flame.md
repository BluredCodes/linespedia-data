---
title: "Midnight Flame"
writer: "Kalisto"
slug: "midnight-flame"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/3687-Midnight-Flame-by-Kalisto"
---

Lost soul,
Lost night;
burning so strong,
burning so bright.
I awaken at
the midnight hour-
what controls my mind,
the unquestioned power?

Soul-less visions
I attain in sight.
Rid me of them,
they give me fright!
The Master comes,
I feel the desire
to burn my love
of midnight fire.

Ah, yes the footsteps
they come so near.
Inside I acquire
an ungodly fear.
It is the Master,
my flame of sin.
I must make haste
not to allow him
in!

I know my hunger,
runs deep to the core.
The Master of night,
his charms I adore!
Passions so strange,
sweet enchantments I find,
yearn to obsess,
control my mind.

The hour has come.
The Master is near.
I feel his breath
upon my ear.
I cannot break away
from my endless cries.
For the words he speaks
are dark, sweet lies.

Evil intentions,
the deepest of hell.
Can I escape 
his unmerciful spell?
He now appears
to share his treasure
in return
for virtueless pleasure.

His heart is cold,
yet his eyes are aflame.
I realize my desire
may never
          ever
               change
For I cannot break the spell
                       
                        of the Midnight Flame!
