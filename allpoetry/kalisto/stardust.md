---
title: "Stardust"
writer: "Kalisto"
slug: "stardust"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Magical madness, momentous night.Sweeping, swerving; we shall take flight.To yon we go, to places that hidein pulsating purity of passions nigh.Stardust passions are wrapped in lust;your arms embrace me, a gentle thrust.Whispers are wisping so soft sighs.Bondage of flesh, in love we cry.Magical courtship enchants my willwith dancing, daring, flirtatious chills.From break of dawn, all through the night;luscious lips upon breast-beating height.Candlelight burns, gleaming and tallas shadows dance across the wall.And for a moment we stop and gazeinto a bright-eyed lover's daze.Watch and wait, watch and wishas truths unfold from veiled mists.As stardust falls, I go asunder;you flit in rage, pant with hunger.As you imbed your soul into my chest,I find a rare token, a magic crest.In heart's delight, in soul it seemsto glisten, to shout, in stardust dreams!So as we leave this secret haven--I take with me your image, graven.We shall run again and run we willto the wistful shadows that haunt us still.
