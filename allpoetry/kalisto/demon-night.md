Innocent soulsare treading the city.Malicious eyes lie in wait.With nostrils flared;he smells the blood of many.This blood he needsfor his achromic life.Footsteps are comingever nearer--destiny unfoldsher wicked wings:A scream rips open the night!But goes unheardwithin the sound-proof wallsof this godforsaken city.The demoniac murderer has wetted his tongue.In blasphemous contempt,he exenterates his victim.Counting the piecesin enumeration.The blood flowslike candescence gore.Blood of the innocent,blood of the deceivedd r  i   p    s from the lips      of a lowly, bastard demon.The feeding is over.His belly full of abomination.The insidious demon walks quietly away...His mind begins racing,burning and churningas the wind brings hima messageof his treacherous ways.Thou shalt not kill.Thou shalt not kill.THOU SHALT NOT KILL!Meaningless words.No heed is taken.There is no atonementfor his sins.His figure becomes evanescentin the demon night...
