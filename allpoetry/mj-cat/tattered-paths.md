---
title: "Tattered Paths"
writer: "M.J. Cat"

Is ‘t truly wrong to grasp 
The happiness that lies within another’s coarse palm
Undeniable love
I cannot
Resist

Your heart belongs to another
And yet remains within mine own 
Shall I set thee free
By means of repressing my love

By my heart
I feel as if I will split in two 
My soul burns to love
And yearns 
To be delivered from all the 
Flames of hell

‘Tis not my nature
To steal from another’s nest
Shall I let my love astray
Or shall I be the 
Snipe of Lucifer 
And deceive 
Deny
And destroy,
Only 
To create another of our own 

O’ my ailed soul,
Eaten away by the love,
The same love
That suspends 
My pounding heart
In a sweet embrace2001,
