Mirror mirror
In my soul
Thy foul form
That lingers within the depths of my heart
Grasps me by the hand 
And guides me astray
Into a world 
Torn between Truth 
And Hope

The black river shimmers beneath the clear blue sky
The powerful rays of Apollo
Forever fighting 
To break in the still colors of the deep bottomless lake

My limp hand swims amongst the solid color of the night
The warmth from the rays warms my back
Only to be possessed by the icy waves that flow 
From my fingertips 
To the very depths of my heart and soul
Coating my spirits 
With its true form

A cold feeling of dreaded realization 
Settles upon my beaded forehead 
As I fall
Headfirst 
Into the bottomless waters that we all fear
As I fall 
Headfirst
Into the reflection among the ripples 
That we all fear the most

The plague upon mankind
The plague
Mankind2001,
