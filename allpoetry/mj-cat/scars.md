Each momentum of my grievances 
Like shapes etched in marble 
Cross my heart
Because it will never die 
But sleeps
As a simple reminder
Of my unmended heart

Each tells a story 
And each paints 
A lesson
That I learned 
By the pain 
In my
Body and soul2001, Dedicated to Ty Thompson
