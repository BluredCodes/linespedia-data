title: "Verchiel"

He sits quiet and still on the rock
His dirty feet in the clear blue lake
His rough scarred hands running through the bright green blades of grass
A sigh leaves his parched lips
His tangled hair and rugged clothes dance gently with the warm breeze
The soft sunlight pours over his bloodstained and dirt-smudged skin
Forever Pale 
And drained of life

He stares deep into the pool beneath him
He sees a girl and her mother cuddling by a fire
Smiling as they watch the snow dance 
Through the frosted window of their modest but cozy home
At the same time
He sees the tears of a man
Who remians on his knees
Looking up at the bright rays of sun
Cursing God's name
As his son lies in the dirt beneath him

He sees a bird laying on the slippery rocks
Drops of rain 
Washing away the blood
From the bird's broken wings
As it draws its last cold breath
He sees a newborn baby 
Cradled in the arms of a mother
A tearstained smile painted on her face
Beads of perspiration still scattered over her forehead
As she rests in the arms of her loving husband

The boy tears his tired eyes away from the picture of the woman and her baby

Every moment 
Someone takes their first breath 
While every moment
Someone's breath is taken away
Every moment
Someone loves
While every moment
Someone cries
Every moment
Someone smiles
While every moment
Someone bleeds

The boy returns his gaze back to the water beneath him
He wipes away his tears and smiles
As he dives into the blue oasis
Leaving a single white feather where he stood
All the water's elements 
Engulfing him
In warmth
And wrapping him with its cold touch
The angel closes his eyes
As he holds everything around him
In a sweet embrace
