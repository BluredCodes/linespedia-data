---
title: "Naked and Sacred"
writer: "M.J. Cat"
slug: "naked-and-sacred"

Dost thou speaks her fair when yet her fair is none but a clever façade 
For the fair in which thou swallows with thine eye 
Lays within the mind 
Not that of the heart
For being of thy heart, 
Thy soul
Engraved within thy deep blue gems 
Wouldst feel the malice
That creases the fair that thou speaks of so fine
In round perfect tones 

That true fair
That thou hast seen within thy trance 
Awaits thy mortal touch 
Behind the thorn bush 
Beneath the Laurel 
Where the Earth and the Sun wouldst never meet
Naked in its true form 
The way nature intended it to be found
Naked and sacred 
Simple and fair
Glistening in a pool of tears 
Naked and sacred2001,
