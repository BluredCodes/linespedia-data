title: "River Styx"
writer: "M.J. Cat"

I walked along the dried yellow grass
Blood dripped from my wrists
I am dead
I come to two trees entwined as one
Casting a dark gray shadow on my lifeless body
Then a shadow flinches from behind the lowest branch
A young girl with brown curious eyes smiles at me
I wonder if she knows I’m dead
“Hi” she greets me cheerfully
I stare at her blankly with my sunken eyes
“He sent me,” she tells me “He sent me to get you” 
“Who” I say
She giggles and steps towards me
She gives me her hand
“The father” her eyes fade into a light gray “the one who created our kind”
I silently slip her my limp cold hands
“Close your eyes.” her voice fades
I close my eyes and feel everything around me melt away
I awaken to discover my new and unfamiliar surroundings
A black thick river surrounds me as leafless trees stand around the dead blades of grass
“Welcome child.” a deep thick voice sounds all around me
“You are between worlds my son. I give you now one choice.”
I stand still and listen
“You have already felt the hunger and tasted the wine to end that hunger. 
You have already begun on the path to our world.
A cold kiss from my hand and you can walk onward.
Or you can turn back towards the light and see what your life has added up to.
However, entering our world and becoming one of us means that you must feed upon souls
The souls in which you loved, hated and cared for”
I gazed down at my bloodstained hands and closed my eyes
“I see what I have become. I cannot deny what I am. I will walk the dark path which is my fate.”
“Very well my child. A kiss upon my hand and we shall be one.”
I look up to see him
Upon a stone cold throne
His misty gray eyes seem to penetrate me as I approach him
I place my dead lips upon Death’s hand
A sudden sharp pain shoots through my entire body, knocking the air out of me
Blood spurts out of my open mouth
I scream, but no sound comes out
My eyes sink back into my head
My skin burns with an acidic sizzle
I convulse and fall to the ground
I lay motionless for a while
Numb but oddly refreshed
My eyes open as if for the first time
I look around as I feel a grin form on my new face
I see everything as I have never seen them 
I see the sick truth that I was too blinded to see before
 I see my purpose now 
And I am born again2001,
