amidst the toxic ashes
beneath the blazing grains 
the faceless roam the endless shores
into the crimson sea of flames

my angel lays in Chaos’ arms
her eyes burning into mine
she watches my features distort in agony
and my screams echo throughout time

my sanity long gone
my love for her gone as well
but the intoxicating sickness remains
until the day I drag her down with me 
into the burning flames of hell2000,
