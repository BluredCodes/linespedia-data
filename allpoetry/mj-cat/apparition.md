---
title: "Apparition"
writer: "M.J. Cat"
slug: "apparition"
source: "AllPoetry"

The scent of the autumn wind
Of the day
The taste of you
On my lips
Was lost

The bitter taste of cigarettes 
That spread through my mouth 
When your wicked
Sweet lips
Tainted mine
Remains stained
Upon my drugged senses
Drowning in the poisonous venom
You left in my icy veins

The blood keeps running
From my deep gashes 
All along my tired body
Yet the vile syrup
Never leaves my veins

Gashes upon gashes
Blood upon blood

As I sleep 
Whenever the moon shines
Upon your cold stone bed
Amongst the roots
Beneath their feet
Covering the stone
And filling the engraved letters
Of your name
With the never ending river of a crimson red
Trickling down my pale cheeks 
From my tired tearstained sockets 
Through closed eyelids
Never to open again
Never again
Never
Again
