title: "Upon the Shore of Wonder"
writer: "M.J. Cat"

Upon the shores
where my tattered soul sleeps, 
a sparrow spotted red
perches upon the trees to rest
cocking its head 
from left
to right

its eyes cried out revenge
its soul screamed release
and the waves sighed for it
as it consumed the pain
that had eaten away
at the sparrow's tired heart

from river to bay
from meadow to mountain
the lost souls look to find me
upon the shores
where my tattered soul sleeps
amongst the forever sinking sun
upon the shores where once I loved
and once I smiled
now washed away 
by the clear blue tears of those that come
to wash away the spots of red
left behind
by the weapons of war
upon the shores of the great sea
of Oblivion2001, If only it were that easy to wash away the pain.
