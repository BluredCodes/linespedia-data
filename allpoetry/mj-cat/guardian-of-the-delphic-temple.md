title: "Guardian of the Delphic Temple"
writer: "M.J. Cat"

The tears 
That are composite of my blood
My essence questioned
My heart – 
Naked

Glance at me but once
For never again
Does the gift of melody 
Grace my being 

The weeds that now grow
Shredded over the marble 
Of the temple
Once emanating life and true beauty 
Now hidden beneath
The rusted Faith
Seem almost alive
As it sways along
The cold thin breeze

Like the day you found me
Swaying to and fro
My neck warmed by the coarse vine
As I swayed 
Under the rusted emblem of Truth 
At the top of the forgotten temple
As I swayed 
Above the unused walkway
Of the great temple
Of Phoebus
Of the great temple
Of Truth 
Perishing in the Wind
As Death
Sighs no more
His reasons gone
As only a trace of his presence remains
With the single black rose
Left on the walkway
Beneath my cold calloused feet
Enwrapped in its own beauty 
Beneath my rusted being…

Goodbye
