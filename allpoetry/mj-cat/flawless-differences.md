title: "Flawless Differences"

Crystalized beneath my cape
of the eternal night sky
The bluest rose
Not a drop of blood
on your 
Frozen petals

Blushes of crimson
Their ruby red pupils
Glimmer with hate
Confusion
Envy
Without a drop of blood
on your frozen petalsI just found this poem in my old notebook. I had forgotten about it completely...
