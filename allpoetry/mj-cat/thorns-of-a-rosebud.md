title: "Thorns of a Rosebud"

The beauty of the pale snow
Comes only with the piercing wind
As does the crashing waves of salt
When poured into the heart so near
To live is to bleed
To have is to fear its loss
And to dream is to wake into the arms of Reality
Yet a still-born hope
Floats near its pains
For our wandering love
Marches aside the unthinkable peril
With the fruitless victory
Handed into its open pupils
Misguided by the ones so close
Misinformed by the whispering winds
Take heed to listen
To one 
Other than yourself
For a life without true love
Is no life at all
