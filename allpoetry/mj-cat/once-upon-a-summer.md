Walking along the bumpy road of life
I came across a soft melody sung by the gentle summer wind
It blew through my hair and urged me off the long dirt path
A tiny sparrow flew gracefully down and landed on my shoulder
It told me I would find you

What I was looking for, I didn’t even know
But I knew that something that I had been waiting for my entire life 
Was just around the corner

I wandered to where the breeze had carried me
I was standing in front of a shimmering clear lake
A diversity of colorful flowers surrounded the vast lake
But my eyes fell upon something much more beautiful than any flower that ever grew on this earth

As I stood in midst of the grass
You looked up at me
Our eyes met
Every beat of my heart echoed throughout my entire soul
Your kind brown eyes looked into mine with warmth and compassion
You smiled
I felt myself smile back
Never had I seen such warmth in one’s eyes 
My pounding heart overflowed with a sense of relief and happiness 

You took my hand 
And I took yours
And we began again 
On the long dirt path
Over bumps and curves
But this time together as one
