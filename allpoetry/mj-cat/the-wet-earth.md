---
title: "The Wet Earth"
writer: "M.J. Cat"
slug: "the-wet-earth"
source: "AllPoetry"

I dreamt of the trees
Standing where the waters flow
Over her grave
And under the falling leaves

Sometimes she stirs
But mostly she just sleeps
Once in a while
She’ll call out my name

The dirt on my feet never wash off
I don’t even know how I got it on there
It’s cold 
And wet

I woke from the dream
Where the waters flow
Over her body
And under the swaying shadows

Sometimes she leaves quietly 
Back into her blanket of dirt
Once in a while
She’ll scream out to me again 
And again
And again

And once in a while
She’ll take me back with her
Into our earth-filled bed
Beneath the flowing waters
And the whispering shadows
