When the chirping sparrows fade into the cricket’s melody
And the heaven’s lights do dim
The leaves sing out their greenness
To call upon the wind

Where alas the Morning Glory sleeps
Its beauty limp and low
‘Tis when the lovers seek the liberty
The freedom they wish to be bestowed

When ‘ere the shameful sun will rise
And the clouds part revealing light
The forbidden lovers be not seen
Till the nightingale sings by and by2000,
