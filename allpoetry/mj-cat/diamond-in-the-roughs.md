The stone was priceless
And now amongst
The pebbles collected
It shines like no other
My being gleams with pride

The stone was priceless
Still amongst
The pebbles collected
Still shining 
I ponder its presence 
Amongst the pebbles collected

The stone seemed priceless
Resting amongst 
The colorful pebbles collected
Glinting consistently
Catching my eye
As I ponder its worth
And its place

Amongst the bright
Colorful pebbles collected
Amongst my ambiguous
Being collected 

The stone 
That seemed 
So pricelesshave you ever had something where you overestimated it's worth and thought it so priceless...but now ur not sure....
