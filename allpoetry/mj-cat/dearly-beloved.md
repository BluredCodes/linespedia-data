title: "Dearly Beloved"

I can’t sleep
I can’t dream
My heart lies still
And yet burns
My feet splash into the deep blue waters of confusion
My hope remains
Perched somewhere high in the clouds 
It has yet to come crashing
Into the wet earth

I promised myself
Long ago
That I wouldn’t cry
That I would keep my head up
And that I would never fall

I keep looking outside the window
Watching
Waiting
For you to come
For you to tell me that you love me

I want to cry
But I swallow my unshed tears
Because 
I believe
And I know
That it is only for the best

Each tic
Each step of the hand
Echoes throughout my heart

I’ve grown to set my heart by this clock
And the funny thing is,
I think it's broken2001,
