---
title: "When the blood runs dry"
writer: "M.J. Cat"

Butterflies
In my dream
They don’t stop screaming

Paint me red
A deep dark red
And crucify me

The river runs dry
When the butterflies come
They won’t stop screaming 

She never told me
Why the blood from my wrists
Never runs dry

Drip drip
She never told me
Why the butterflies 
Never 
Stop
Screaming

When the blood runs dry
Maybe they’ll 
All 
Stop
Screaming
