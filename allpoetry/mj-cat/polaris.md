---
title: "Polaris"
writer: "M.J. Cat"
slug: "polaris"

The brightest in the heavens
Friend and savior of the adventurous
Alongside the Ursa Major
Where around it evolves
All thing hidden
And all things tender
All things unspoken
And all things true
All the love inside
Unrippled and untainted
Behind my mirrored tears
Mingling with the starry pool
Where Orion sleeps
As he grasps his club
Even in his slumber
