title: "Shattered Rays"

The blue shimmer within your pupils 
Stands as nothing but a purple illusion 
Embedded within my foolish hope
That suspends my footprints 
Within my own tearstained path

If the light blue sparks
Be none other than cruel pretense, 
Then let the strong winds
Take scathing revenge
Upon your dark black soul
For I
Am too tattered and worn, 
Abused by neglect
And torn apart
Limb by limb 
By your cruel and calloused hands  
That rang my love 
For all it was worth2001,
