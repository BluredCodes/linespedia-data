title: "Hades Calling"

The only force that surpasses them all
The only element that defies all else
An eminent poison
In my dreams

The lake shimmers
Invitingly
But to ripple
Would be to taint
The untouchable oasis
In my dreams

The venomous fang glistens
Behind those sweet soft lips
                               For one kiss 
                                                One touch
                                                             Lies at the end of my tearstained path

Your poison runs sweet
Through these pounding veins
And chokes my heart
As it still beats within your 
Warm dark paw

The grains begin to pour
As the sweet plague takes it toll

Had I only torn my gaze
From those piercing blue gems
That reflects my bare soul
That I thought I cloaked so well

Had I only kept my wits 
Had I only prayed that night
To save my heart 
From the luring shadows
That whispered my name so sweetly
The deep husky whisper
Dripping with a tempting sweetness 
Dripping with danger 
Echoing in my heart 
Whenever the winds guide it through 
My pounding stained heartenjoy
