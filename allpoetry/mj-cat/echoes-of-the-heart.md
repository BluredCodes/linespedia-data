title: "Echoes of the Heart"
writer: "M.J. Cat"

Among the shards of Phoebus' crystal temple
High above our most vivid imagination
He shines within the aura of light
That He warmed our hearts with 
Then 
And 
Now
And although His mortal heart ceased in place
Upon this tragic day
His soul remains etched in our hearts
Forever echoing throughout the halls of the utopian palaces
As He 
Shines down
Whenever Phoebus' chariot
Illuminates the air
As He 
Sleeps
Amongst the speckled blanket
Alongside Orion
Within Diana's shadows
That She cast with each arrow
Of each falling star

With every tear that drops
Alongside the pear-shaped stars that fall 
We will remember You
With every smile seen on every child's face
Alongside the smile that always touched 
Your lips so gently 
We will remember You
And as the world turns 
Day by day
Not a second goes by
When Your presence doesn't 
Grace the hearts
That were grasped so gently
And yet impacted so heavily 
By Your joyous birth
And by Your sudden death
By Your kindness
And Your charisma

Never will we forget You
Nor will we forget what You did
For those who You loved
And those
Who loved You
Still love You
And always will love You

Rest In Peace


In memory of Zach Jacobs
We will never forget youThis was dedicated to our dear friend Zach.
