title: "Ocean Breeze"

Elements of the wind
Though they pass
Will never fade

Peaceful bonds of friendship
Lie awake embedded within the twinkling dust
Of the moonlit night

The ember that sparks
Remains asleep beneath my soul
Forever glowing
With each moment 
That I cry out your name

No matter how long I wait
The deep footprints you leave in the cold sand
Will never cross mine
But runs alongside another set of footprints aligned with yours
Facing a new direction
Towards another horizon

The smooth grains run through my tearstained fingers
Filling the footprints you left behind
Soon to be washed away
Under the same speckled blanket above us 
As you slip through my tearstained fingers 
Filling my footprints 
As I slowly walk away
