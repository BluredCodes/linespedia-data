---
title: "Fields of Barley"
writer: "M.J. Cat"
slug: "fields-of-barley"

Testament of my emotions
Loves of their lives
Bittersweet waters of my own confusion
Make me a field of barley
To lay in 
To sleep in
To die in 
Pull this decayed tooth out
From my dry mouth
Will you miss my footprints of red
In the deep white snow?
I think I’ll sit with the doves
I think I’ll change the course of my time
Will you change yours?
The wind blows through my skin
I am transparent again
Can I give you my
Testament of emotions?
My eyes are numb too
Remember 
And I’ll be
Breathing 
Once again
