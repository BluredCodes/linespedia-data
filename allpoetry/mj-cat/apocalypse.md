The end of the cave
Before the two men
Seemed to grow before the two mortals

The mouth of one
Beyond the clouds
Beyond the sand

If I could only reach the last strip 
That touches the golden pot
And Iris would fly by my side

Could the gateway 
Fit in the surface
Of my glistening paw

The men walk on
To the violet lake
Where the blue swan swim 

The aquamarine shimmer
You saw in your deepest trance
Will soon be here

The cave between your reality
And our mirrored world
Crumbling to our feet

Where nothing is tangible
Yet none stands as an illusion
And we 
Are finally oneit's not that easy...
