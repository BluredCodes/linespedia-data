Two sunflowers from the same damp soil
Entwined by the roots
Growing side by side
Under one sun
And enduring the warmth of one ray of light
Lingeringly following the sun’s daily cycle
And feeding upon the same showers under the silver clouds
Existing to support each other
Just like you and me
Friends until the end
Until we see each other fully in bloom2000,
