“Look at the path stretched out before you
The choice is yours…
What are you waiting for?” 

“A sign” 

“You should know in your heart”

“My heart is deceived 
My eyes lie to me
They see the rainbow in the sky
But look, there isn’t any, is there?”

“Your feelings should lead you there.”

“My feelings have gone astray
My ears lie to me
They hear the soft music drifting through the air 
But in truth it could just be the wind.”

“Look deep into his eyes 
They won’t lie”

“He hides the truth within them
As does his heart within him
As does my soul from him.”2001,
