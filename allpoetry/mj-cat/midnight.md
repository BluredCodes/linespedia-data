title: "Midnight"

To love, to be, within my soul
To weave, to paint the mirage inside my heart
If I seek Cupid’s bow
Would the amorous speech be untainted? 

Aim now 
For Apollo’s great chariot
Stay hidden beneath Earth’s midnight cloak

Let the prince’s kiss never come
Let the aurora never shine
Suspend the solar movements 
For the cock cries too soon 
And the owl sleeps too long

The missing half of your 
Pounding heart
Would be the reason 
For Cupid’s tears 
For he weeps for the 
Tragic lovers
Whom he shot one 
Too many2001,
