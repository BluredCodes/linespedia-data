title: "Sunken Conviction"

Each wink of the bright stars
That speckles the blackness
Brings me closer 
To your rhythmic heartbeat

For I 
Am the fallen huntress of Artemis
Whose celibacy was tainted 
By a small sunshine 
That whispered a new meaning
Into my heart

You were the deer 
Who had escaped the arrows 
Of the huntress 
In Diana’s forest

You were the one
Who led me on the path
And lured me away from the great Mount Cynthus 

I turned my back
 On my Goddess 
And let my bow fall
Along with my convictions 
Deep into the rivers of Peneus 
Under the great sighing Laurel

How is it that
These consuming sensations
Unknown to my virgin soul
Can causes one
To betray their morals and beliefs 
Simply to feel
The warmth
Of another’s hands 
And to be able to feel the heartbeats 
Together as one2001,
