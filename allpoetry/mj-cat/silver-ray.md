The frozen stars in the heavens
Fall from the midnight sky
One by one
The silver moonlight pours out its sweet pain
As I shed another tear

Dreaming of you
Knowing how far away those dreams lie
Embedded in the pearly sands of the deep blue sea 
Unseen and forgotten

For you
Every flower blooms
Every single day the sun shines brightly upon the vast lands
And every bird sings its harmonious melody
Carried along by the gentle wind

Impossible wishes
Foolish desires
Torturous nights 
Drowning in an ocean of tears
Each tear 
Each star
Falling from the same frozen night sky
Each light of hope
Shattering into shards of cold fragments of ice2000,
