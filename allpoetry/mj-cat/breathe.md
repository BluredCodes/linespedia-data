As the blood pounds through these veins
The urges grow increasingly 

Breathe 
And I close my eyes
Dream
And I am the form
Of my past, present, and future
But only in this trance
Do we meet again

The sin done well
By the soul
Now embodied
To cleanse itself to the sin
That put itself in this world
Of shadows
That flicker on the cave wallsthe last paragraph was in relation to Plato's theory of forms
