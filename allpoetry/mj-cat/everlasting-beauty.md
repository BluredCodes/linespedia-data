A single frozen flower
Suspended in the cold
Still a young bud
Wrapped in loneliness
Motionless and still
All life around it
Coming to a halt

From inside the cold confusion
A bright warm stream of sunlight
Showing the way
Melting away the ice
And slowly spreading across the sky
Touching everything with life and love

The clouds disappear 
To reveal the true colors of the world
Spraying the sweet taste of true beauty

A soft gentle breeze 
Guiding the new seeds of life
To create a new world
