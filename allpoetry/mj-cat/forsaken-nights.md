---
title: "Forsaken Nights"
writer: "M.J. Cat"
slug: "forsaken-nights"
source: "AllPoetry"

The damned ones, eternally plagued with sorrow and pain
Walk in the shadows of their own sins 
Dragging their cursed lives beneath them

The weary sun retires behind the hills to the west
And the skies darken into a deep shady color
Caine slowly raises his dark clouded eyes
As he wearily roams on into the unbearable solitude which was once his birthplace

A single crimson tear trickles from the corner of his eye
And slowly reaches his parched lips to leave a scarlet trail on his pale cheek
The bitter- sweetness of his own wine spreads throughout his mouth 
And quenches his hate for the ones above

Ahi hay Lilitu
The words cling to his brain
As leeches for blood
Truth reveals itself from every black corner of the earth
Each seed within its earthly womb
Another to see what vile creatures they become

Caine of Nod 
Walks on again
Never to turn back to the ashes of D'hainu
And lives on
Damned for an eternity2000,
