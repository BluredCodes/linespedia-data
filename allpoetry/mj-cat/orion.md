He climbs eastward
Forever blinded
Stepping on the forgotten words
Of the prophet
Embedded within the dust
Amongst the darkness of the cave*
Wishing that he had taken 
The road that shone 
Under Polaris’ bright blue rays of light*Plato's Allegory of the Cave
