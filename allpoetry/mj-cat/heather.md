title: "Heather"
writer: "M.J. Cat"

The tiny purple belled flowers
The little violet buds in my garden

The one ray of sunshine 
That shines on us all
Divine
Pure 
True

Like Eros 
You strike the hearts of many
Like Iris
You blend together the colors of the world
And hand them out 
To all those around you

And yet the story was not complete
You looked past it 
Because you refused 
To look into the eyes 
Of any
Again

And yet
And yet,  you didn’t realize until now
That God made you wait
For a reason

Because He was preparing you
You
The one who deserves it more than anyone
For the one 
That you were always looking for
In the back of your tired heart

It was His way of telling you
That it was time 
For you to be touched 
By that same sunshine 
That you touched the world with
Since the day you were born



This poem is dedicated to Heather from the bottom of my heart. 
You’re always taking care of me and everyone else. Maybe he’s 
The one that’s meant to take care of you. 
Whatever happens is for the best. 
You deserve the best. 
We love you.Dedicated to Heather - the greatest person ever.
