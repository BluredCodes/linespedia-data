---
title: "An Apple from the Tree of Knowledge"
writer: "M.J. Cat"
slug: "an-apple-from-the-tree-of-knowledge"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/11947-An-Apple-from-the-Tree-of-Knowledge-by-M.J.-Cat"

Betrayal
That burns in my heart and soul
Guilt 
That leaves me suffering forever
Destruction
Of the happiness we once possessed
Pain 
Which I reflect upon myself with my own wrong doings
Secrecy
Fearing consequences
Agony
That stalks me in my dreams1999, This is a rather old one that I wrote when I first started writing.
