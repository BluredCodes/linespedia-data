---
title: "Broken Mask"
writer: "M.J. Cat"
slug: "broken-mask"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/15506-Broken-Mask-by-M.J.-Cat"
---

Let me brush off the red on these petals
It wasn’t meant to be

Don’t make me look into your eyes
Please
You’ll see what truth lies within 
What I’ve so cleverly weaved 
With my amorous wishes
Into a simple pattern of a solid yellow

Don’t stand so close to me
Please
My shameful hand
Aches to find yours
Don’t speak so close to me
Please
I can feel your breath on my shoulders

Night falls
With the bittersweet pear shaped tears
As I hold the naked truth
Closer and closer to my pounding chest

Close your eyes
Please
Just shut them tight
My uncloaked soul
Lies before you only once

My fingertip dips into the cold sand
Keep them closed
Only once
Will I show you
What my eyes have been saying
Every time I look at you 

I love you
A strong wave washes upon shore
You open your eyes
The salty blanket crawls back
You see the wet sand
Even under the moonlit sky
Not a single trace of my feelings can be seen
You look at the wet sand 
Smooth and still
Even under the moonlit sky
The single tear
That falls with the shooting star
Seems invisible 
Just like the words that remain etched carefully into my heart
