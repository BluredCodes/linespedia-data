I’ve forgotten 
The date
Or the time a minute
From now
Only other people
Who dance in
Swings left and right
Cracking the egg
Shells that decorate
The ballroom floor
In place of rose petals
Are remembering
For memories sake
To crush a rising
Spirit at weakest point
I’ve forgotten
The face
Or the shadow of thought’s
Only when my heart it breaks
I do feel low
And wish to know
Why forgetting soothes my soul.2002,
