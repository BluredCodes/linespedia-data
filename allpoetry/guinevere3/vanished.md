What a lovely ending 
To a
Tragic speech… 
Could someone ever 
Notice 
When I'm 
Out of reach...
Wishing once a 
Memory 
A philter
Upon his heart…
Constantly consuming me
Dreams that
Never start...
Love, that kind of
Meaning 
Losing evanescent...
Ever to behold your
Blissful
Treasure my heart's
Lament...2001,
