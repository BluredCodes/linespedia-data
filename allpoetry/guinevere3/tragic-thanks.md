Crisp sunshine outlines 
This day filled 
With visitors and 
Laced with change…

Who is the bearer of 
All these leaves?
Contrasting with yesteryear
Somehow still okay...

Engraving and carvings 
Everyone remains present, 
I must have been pardoned,
This fine day so enjoyed...

Salubrious amazement
Tragically lost,
In this ceremony of
Century's engulfing new leaves...

A victorious day, 
A new one indeed,
Comfort with nothing 
Brought unto me…

Most joy to all
A crisp morrow I bless,
For tears they do cry 
As I shall someday rest...

Crisp sunshine outlines
This day filled
With visitors,
We will meet again one day...2000,
