Where there are no winners
Only 
Lots of losers,
Good
Bye…
Only someone to hold 
Me close at dusk and
Never let me go, or
Maybe someone to 
Miss me
Each breathing second that
We part,
Good
Bye…
Longing to lose our
Fingertips as we walk
Hand in hand where
Ten become five
Good
Bye…
The rain is pounding
Down 
My slipper was given
The pant size unknown
Good
Bye…
Let he who is true
See that here
I am
I wait, 
Good 
Bye…2000,
