Enclosing the dark flower casing with 
Amber crystal and enduring liquid 
Power to be and power to keep 
Quiet your speech 
Hush now your heart 
No thinking of wonder 
I saved in this life 
Writing now true our love 
Story and of our path 
How I will love 
You see, we will grow now 
How you will embrace 
Me in silent bliss stares 
And how our lifetime 
Shall bring about us such sweet 
Pure life smiles and children for miles 
Believing in the magic 
Within these soft pages 
Writing life into all sad your smiles 
Oh yes I will be 
Here and wait all the while 
As you grow and I too 
One day enjoying our life 
Peacefully united the time 
To grow, age not parted
