A suggestion that marmite 
Might intensify your life 
A suggestion that marmite 
Is more like peanut butter than strife 

Upon suggesting we'll escape 
The lovely escape of green grass 
You'll tip your hat and 
I will bow to your feet at last 

Renderings of poetry, we will 
Paint and polish our lifetime 
With tea time after eight 
Escaping again to the light of a lime 

My dear gentleman you are my escape 
No thinking of time 
We rewind and hit pause 
TO live in elegance and rhyme 

My garden will grow lovely 
And we will endure 
Rhubarb engravings, let's give it a go 
Enchanted evenings all to allure 

Upon suggesting we will escape 
The lovely escape of green grass 
You'll tip your hat and 
I will bow to your feet at last 

Renderings of oceans mast 
The rain drops fall and kiss 
Your tidy nose and beard 
And I  your lady, will so miss 

Your face in my garden 
I've grown just for you 
Roses and Lily's 
Rustle your voice on that pew
