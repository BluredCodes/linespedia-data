"You are such a sweet girl..." 
She said with a deceptive smile 
As she pranced up the staircase merrily 
Awaiting discussions of my worthwhile... 
"We only want the best for you..." 
That is why they lock me away 
From all that they know and love 
All that they left in this ash tray... 
"You are not strong enough..." 
To do anything or be half of what 
They are not or more than they invent 
Dismissing my name in stories 
Of their journeys to seem content... 
Constant illness, of stress or of pain 
Created by the lonliness that 
My mother and father create for fair gain 
Instead of embracing my colorful hat... 
I'm not like my sister, 
She's perfect in form 
And she never colored outside 
Of the lines in the storm... 
The storm I created 
At least they said that 
The caos I created 
At least they knew that... 
But why did they missuse the gift 
Of my birth, leaving a fog 
Of maddness starvation away 
Tearful in silence drinking egg nog... 
Weeping alone, and they wonder why 
I live with insecurity I live with no faith 
Weeping alone, and they wonder why, 
Cause perfection seems simple to those not enslaved
