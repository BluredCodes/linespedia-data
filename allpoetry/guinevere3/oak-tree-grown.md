I'm not okay
Just thought you should know
Talkative secrets
That mascarade my woe
I feel so shaky
Don't know where I went
The day that my branches
Split my extent
I hide in the dark 
To sleep in the light
Confusing the copius
Prize for to egnite
Reality dreaming
I'm not who I am
Sleeping a memory
Forgot the exam
I'm not so okay
But pretend and pretend
To be what I thought
Was who I ammend
I feel so uneasy
Don't know where I've been
The day that my branches
Split my loose end
