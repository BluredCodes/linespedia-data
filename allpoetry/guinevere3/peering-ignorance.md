---
title: "Peering Ignorance"
writer: "Guinevere3"
slug: "peering-ignorance"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/49140-Peering-Ignorance-by-Guinevere3"

Can you see through 
The walls of my heart or 
The walls of my mind 
To know of all secrets Ive 
Hidden once so well 

Can you see behind 
The mess in my room or 
The mess in my dress 
To know of all beauty Ive 
Hidden once away 

Will you see above 
The treasure of found pieces or 
The treasure of beaten dreams 
To know of all brilliance Ive 
Dreamed once so well 

Will you see beside 
The presence in longing or 
The presence in endeavors 
To know of all grace Ive 
Dreamed once away
