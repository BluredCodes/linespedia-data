Trying to see, willing once more
To grasp the balance of night and 
Of day, embrace the existence
Or not of self-loathing in self-love.
Their faces wear other truths and
In their voices I hear past times,
I wonder, is it there…
What is to come of me when only
My words ring on paper but some
Slight giggle loses all the grace
I once did have…  As I child I live
Knowing the feelings there lay…2001,
