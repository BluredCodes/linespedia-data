Wake in slight 
Of fright and Gleam 
Empowered response
Just in your seams...
Mirror!
In my year
All sights to see 
Remember those are
Still unseen...
Mirror!
I bellow a hush
My slighted soul sings
To echo your praise
Tortured gold rings...
Mirror!
Listen...Such surprise,
Unrelenting pearls of
Behavior bead inside
Still palms above...
Mirror!2001,
