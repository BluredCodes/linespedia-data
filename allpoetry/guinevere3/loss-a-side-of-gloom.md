---
title: "Loss... A side of Gloom"
writer: "Guinevere3"

Frightened by the
Light of love
Joyous sounds from
Hell above

Singing praise to
The one I know
Should be falling
For a doe

Startled at the
Sounds I smell
Remembering songs
You knew so well

Crawling on 
Your dirty floor
Dreams of dancers
Winning wars

Frightened by the 
Love of Light
Hellish sounds in
Joyful delight

Praisful songs
I knew the one
Who fell silently
But now is done

Smells so startled
He never noticed
How I loved him
Strangers remembered

Dancing in a
Smaller room
Wishing to be
A side of gloom
