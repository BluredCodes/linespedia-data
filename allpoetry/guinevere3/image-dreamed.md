Do you remember when we first met?
You had I’m sure doubts of several
Lives I misunderstood myself and
Maybe I felt and spoke different truths
Unintentionally, never wishing to harm
The rare friendship I knew was there
For all years I have known and all I’ve
Forgotten… Maybe I confused what I 
Never knew in form for youth’s sake,
Not so long ago… But today as I remember
Your face I see and hope you do as well ,
What friendship is and has for all…
For now misunderstanding
Leaves me Thank you my dearest 
Friend of all…
I just woke up
And it is much
More beautiful
Than what I dreamed.2002,
