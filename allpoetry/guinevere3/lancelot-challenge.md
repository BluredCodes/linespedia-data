Close your tainted black eyes and feel my pinkest kiss 
Shaking down your slumbering leg 
and breathe dusk's light in... 

Imagine once, my mystical black sleeveless dress 
Falling soft petals against 
My strands of pearl skin... 
and shutter victoriously... 

Remember the way my blood stained hands feel 
running through your midnight velvet hair, 
Holding on tight to the 
Silver of shiver in the radiant moon above us... 
And lose control in bursts of blue... 

Awaken to my brilliant name upon your 
changing essence... 
For dreaming yellow roses is what 
Now I keep for you... 

Lusting what I knew in your blue awkward pick-up truck, 
as my black playful mask fell into 
Your absent arms again...
