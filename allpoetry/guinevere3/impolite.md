A whisper in a tunnel
In a mountain far from home
Or a whisper to some honeysuckle
Through heat waves on the phone...
A whisper never thunderous
Is often never heard,
But he felt silent..
Repercussions revolting
Polite electric rage.
Can you blame me? 
An Orphan all alone...
Newborn Eyesight stolen away… 
but why?
I’ve never known reality,
Just Exaggerated despair...
Politely then, I disappear
Loudly, then I fall.
Still polite, I am alone.
With less pain, for now.
Not standing politely 
Alone I Whisper, 
Those of importance
Are here…2000,
