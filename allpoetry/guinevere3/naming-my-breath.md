title: "Naming my breath"

Forgo the steps that
Babies first take and take
Flight again, take it away 
Far from the road I’ve
Traveled in birth…
Far from the dreams paved
With stardust.
There’s no need for
Theft, please take
It, it’s yours…
Do take up this wind
Gust and fly in the
Night, glide over 
Clouds and give
Breath a name…
So leave those
First steps for others
To gain…
Accept flight
Your gift wrapped
Neatly and great…
Your beauty in
Flight is now just
For you.
