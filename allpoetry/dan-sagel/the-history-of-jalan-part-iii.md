First he will defile
Your bowls of cereal
With the most depraved
Of perverted acts

Undulating the convulsing
Grinding and drooling
His fingers working
The devil's evil work

Next he will shave
All his body hair
And with bloodsoaked passion
Weave it into an afghan

His dreams are of you
Naked and drenched
In strawberry jelly
And covered in Crustaceans
