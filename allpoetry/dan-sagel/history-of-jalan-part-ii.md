Unholy baby
Eating the souls of all men
Must smother it quickly

Too soon it will grow
Murder us all bloodily
Why does God hate us?

Jalan the jolly
Jalan the foul destroyer
Jalan the kitty

By a thousand names
He will be known unto us
The hydra, the tool
