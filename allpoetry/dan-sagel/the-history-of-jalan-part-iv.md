Oh jesus,
She's got it to my head!
She's going to put it in my mouth
Oh god oh god
Whore.
She's gone crazy this time,
Someone please call the police
Yeah, it's no wonder dad hates you
I would too if my wife were as ugly
Are you even allowed to play with the dog
No!  Not in there!
I'll kill you one day!
I hate grape popcicles, mom!i don't know where the hell i'm going with this...
