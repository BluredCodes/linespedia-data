Okay guys, i know i haven't been as prolific as Jalan, and i've had a couple choice, although occationally regretable, words for a select few, i am also on my way out.  I will miss you all, and i will miss this forum, but i am off to work on a new tower of babel which will be less like a structure to reach Heaven, but more like a delux duplex.

please any of you reach me at dawnofmars@yahoo.com (and maybe soon at potentpoetic.com!!!)

you are all artists.  use your powers for good, and make the world better in the way in which your artistry deems fit.

good luck and may the force be with you!

Rob Bexley (aka dan sagel)
