So worn so torn sobriety
Never dwells in these eyes that cannot see
Does it burn you like it burns me
Charred to the heart just let it be 

Together forever for now
Does it mean so much?

Unclean unseen unwanted here
Threatening the preservation of their common fear
Does it burn you like it burns tears
Fiery rain melting the years

We’re all forever for now 
Does it mean so much?

Too late to wait tomorrow comes
To it’s breath we will succumb
Does it burn you like it burns some
Bound so tightly we come undone

We’ll live forever for now
Does it mean so much?
Does it burn you like it burns me
