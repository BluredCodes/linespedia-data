look at all that I have done i always cost too much anyway my other half, my worthless half has taken all your paper love awaynow listen to the void overflowing with the things that you’ve destroyed now smile at all the pretty walls you never liked locked doors if i recalland as you cry your selfish tears i will be drowning in my pity for youi’ll feed my other half this time the deprivation’s turned my bodybluenow listen to the void purple swollen with the passion you’ve destroyed and while your scraping at the walls  you’ll learn to like locked doors after all numbing, fear has got you numbing, through and through numbinglook at all that i have doneThis poem is actually a song so forgive me if it doesn't flow well on paper (or computer screen in this case), you have to hear it...
