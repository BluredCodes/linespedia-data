Drown the meaning from my soul
torn apart until I’m whole
what would it be like if the world could feel
if the world could feel
Beauty ripped and glory torn
the consequence of hatred born
what would it be like if your god was real
if your god was real

Raped the truth out of the sky
now not even it can cry
what would it be like if this cure could heal
if this cure could heal
Child of mine lost deep inside
shattered hope and shameful pride
what would it be like if she was revealed
