---
title: "Walk beside me tonight"
writer: "burning ember85"
slug: "walk-beside-me-tonight"

I know it’s late and you’re probably sleeping
But then again I guess you’re always sleeping
I just need you for a little while, a few moments will do
So if you can, please walk beside me tonight

Our star’s out, it’s so bright
And you probably have an even better view than me
It’s great how it’s always there to comfort me
But God knows I’d much rather have the real you

Because the stars can’t talk the way you used to
Laugh and love the way we used to 
It seems like I’ve been staring up at them forever now
There’s so much pity in their fiery eyes

I’ll let you go now, it’s time I get back anyway
It’s too cold to be out here alone
I’ll pray for you again tonight, like last night and the night before
And I’ll see you again someday soon, I know it’ll be soon

I know it’s too late and I wish I were sleeping
But then again I wish I was always sleeping
Like you, with you, for more than a little while
So if you can, please walk beside me tonight
