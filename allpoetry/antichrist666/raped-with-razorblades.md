Excitement pulses through me
As I pull my luscious haul

I intend to maul

Let it drip right down my chin

Raped with razors

Frail limp bodies
To boil and cook

Sodomized with a corkscrew
Intestines twist around
It pleasures me immensely
As the body hits the ground

Put my dick into the hole
Where your head once used to be
The flesh surrounds my hardened prick
As I cum inside the stump

Lapping up old rotted cum

Raped with razors
