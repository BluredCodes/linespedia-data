title: "Checkmate"

In the courtroom of bigotry the shadows sigh 
Walls heave as elevated judges mock the black
Of their robes and indentured jurors fail to cringe. 
Light is there delivered sentence on their hearts. 
Heavy on the soul of the stygian condemned.

Shackled by defeat even as metal
The false felon accepts mortality
Welcomes the release of imminent death
From the searing glare of accusation.

But he is begrudged even that, his own death
Placed in its stead a play of intervention
That tightens the noose of respite around his throat
Naught but a pawn, he awaits their finale
Dark against light they determine his fate.
