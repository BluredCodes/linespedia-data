---
title: "Together Again"
writer: "mcl"
slug: "together-again"

---

Come sit with me, love,
By my side on the bed
Take my hand the way you 
Used to when we walked.

Don’t be sad that I’m leaving
For my body is tortured,
My mind is weary, and
My heart only beats for our family.

Time will take me away, I know,
From the girls, they are so young
But I will watch them from the silence
And hope I have left enough of myself with you.

Tell them I love them when they ask of me
And how I used to comb their hair,
And sing off-key lullabies,
And tickle toes to get a laugh.

Birthdays, holidays, weddings
I’ll be there for them through you
Love them and hold them and know I’ll be waiting
‘Til we’re all together again.
