red satin lover
rent
spent
within
hoarded wealth
gentled tilth
did you discover
actual sin

or does love turn filth
quite
white
for eyes
that want more
candy store
she’s bad for your health
say your goodbyes

come out the back door
see 
me 
waiting
take my hand
understand
I’ve forgiven your 
trespass mating.
