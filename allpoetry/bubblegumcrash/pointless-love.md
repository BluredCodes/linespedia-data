---
title: "pointless love"
writer: "bubblegumcrash"

I want to break down and cry 
With you in my arms
There’s a warmth I need around me
I feel so far away
I’m empty in my dreams
Your picture stares me in the face
Discarded on the floor 
Along with my tears
Show me where I can sell my soul for a dollar
And I’ll write it down
They can put it on the shelf 
And I’ll belong to someone else
They can have this metal box aswell
And own all of my dreams
Maybe they can make them come true
Tomorrow’s not another day for meoct 2001,
