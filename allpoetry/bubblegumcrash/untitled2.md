I have a line,
Just the one to start,
I jump on a train to see where it will take me,
 Who knows.

My prison is closing in,
The walls are getting taller,
The sun is just a dot,
On the wall in the shade.

When all I’ve got for company,
Is high speed trains and cocaine,
All that’s left to do is,
Follow the yellow line to the end of the track.nov 2001,
