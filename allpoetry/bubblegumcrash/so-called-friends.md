Those times when we were young
You were never there
We grew apart as children
Went our separate ways
We smiled politely if we passed in the street
No need for anything more

Then we were older
You needed me again
I was there for you as always
We were friends once more

Now the tables have turned
I could do with a friend
You've left me yet again
When I needed you most

For three weeks I've needed you
And you know full well why
But you've chosen to ignore me
You've found your better life

That's fine with me
I don't blame you at all
Who needs to return the favour
When you don't need me anymore

So I'll just keep it all indside
Let it eat my soul
But don't expect me to be there
When it's you that's made me diewriten about my supposed best friend who hasnt contacted me since new year. I'm annoyed because my grandpa has just passed away and she knew he was ill. I've had no support from her at all! I'm not going to be used anymore and that's final!! :)
