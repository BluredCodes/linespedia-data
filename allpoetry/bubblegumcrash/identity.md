Identity.

I am the writing on the wall,
I am the whisper in the night,
I am the voice in your head,
That you try to fight.

I am the changing of the seasons,
I am the ticking of the clock,
I am the father of time,
Time you try to stop.

I am the clouds up above,
I am the wind in the trees,
I am the storm on the horizon,
That will knock you to your knees.

I am the bird on the wing,
I am the lions on the plain,
I am the dog in the kitchen,
The one that’s completely insane.

I am the ink in the pen, 
I am the lead in the pencil,
I am the blade of the dagger,
That’s poised to kill.

I am the dreams in your sleep,
I am the dreams of your life,
I am the dreams that don’t come true,
That cause trouble and strife.

I know who I am,
Do you know too?
