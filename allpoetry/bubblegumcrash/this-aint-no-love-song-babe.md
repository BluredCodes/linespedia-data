I’m being taken on a joy ride around the nicest parts of hell
You’ve made me feel the way I do
Playing games and fucking up my head.
I’m just like a toy to you
You kiss me then make me bleed
You’ve ripped up my insides
Now all I’ve got is skin and bones
Just a shell of what I used to be,
So fuck you cunt I hope u bleed!

I’ve got two sides too you know
The one you’ve got and the one you ain’t seen
So remember if you make me weaker
I only get stronger
You ain’t gonna like who I’m gonna be
You’ll wish you’d never laid eyes on me
I’ll take you to hell 
And I know how to drive
So fasten up fucker boy 
We’re going for a ride!

I used to worship you,
I used to be your only one,
You used to be my saviour
But, oh dear look what you’ve done

You’ve turned me into someone I don’t want to be
You’ve made me look at this life
Maybe I should thank you.
Nah FUCK THAT pass me that knife!!

I’ll slit your throat like the fucker you are
You’re gonna bleed so,
BLEED BLEED BLEED!!
 I ain’t gonna kill you 
So don’t fear me lover
I’m just gonna hurt you
Like you hurt me MOTHERFUCKER!!

I wanna rip out your insides
And shove them down your neck
But I’ll just kiss you babe
Wonder what you’ll get next!2001, :s
