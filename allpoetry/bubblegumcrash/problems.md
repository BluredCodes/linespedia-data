These problems you give me
Are too hard for me to cope with
I try and try and try
But no answer comes to mind

I want to help you both
But you make it so hard
You answer with another problem
One that’s harder than before

My problems are getting lost along the way
Being pushed deeper within myself
Eating away from the inside
I’m crashing in on myself

Having to cope with a thousand others too
You seem to think I can handle one more
A drop in  the ocean of my abilities
But my ocean is full

I love you 
But I can’t do this anymore
I need someone to be there for me 
But I don’t think it’s you.
