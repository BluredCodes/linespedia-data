vineyards dead sitting in the breeze
cool as a butterfly
window starring into my eyes
vineyards dead cross legged high it seems
the grapes are none
tomatoes hiding from my teeth
flowerbeds blooming colorful dreams

'la vista ka madecon de tiajuana el matedor
un etto la vigneto pace nonno mia'

beauty in silence  around silent beauty
breezy soft aroma  wisp of wind 
eyes of the world with strong visions
vineyards dead imagine the bliss
euphoric sunrise Venus blows a kiss
under the garden
