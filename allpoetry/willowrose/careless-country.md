---
title: "careless country"
writer: "WillowRose"
slug: "careless-country"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/35416-careless-country-by-WillowRose"
---

we wonder where we went wrong
the cycle of life destroyed by the strong
the wise men are out of touch
careing to little and thinking to much
sucking oil straight from the core
cutting up trees like a rapist whore
poisoning the water that we need to drink
landfills and garbage dumps peeuu they stink
big business bitches takeing over the town
as family owned gems start to drown
the freedom we love is fadeing away
as politicians sing god bless the USA
