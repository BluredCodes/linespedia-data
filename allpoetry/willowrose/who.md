---
title: "who?"
writer: "WillowRose"
slug: "who"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/40611-who--by-WillowRose"
---

the seventies
stripped pants
wigs for all occasions
platform shoes
and heres me
shy but true
the eighties
mini skirts
big hair with spray
and heres me
lonely at play
the nineties
plaid shirts
white girls with dreads
and heres me
outrunning the feds
i have time on my hands
long hair cut short
thin body toned with muscles
walking barefoot 
traveling with the wind
i have lines on my hands
philosophy is found
silence is wonderful
dancing naked
painting the canvas of life
i have two hands
pressed together 
universal energy flows
extending the the body
to reach a place of wellness
i have fingers on my hands
poking into the mysteries of the unknown
dreaming of creating 
speaking without words
being free is why I live
my carriage is my feet
taking me wherever 
moving sideways to go forward
clawing my way
as moon guides me
