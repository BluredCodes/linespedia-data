---
title: "to hell with money"
writer: "WillowRose"
slug: "to-hell-with-money"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/33121-to-hell-with-money-by-WillowRose"
---

money sucks the life out of you
it takes and never gives back
what one has he never needs
but what one has had is always wanted back
more --more -- more --more of it
that evil that money
killing--stealing--rapeing --jail
money is now needed to make one's bail
drugs -booze --dead
money is now needed for one's life long bed
to hell with money i'll never know
you can't wish you had it till you had it you know
and money like the faces of dead presidents
will only get you one place--buried six feet below
