title: "found"
writer: "WillowRose"
slug: "found"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

take a mundane thought
from a simple lost soul
during an otherwise ordinary day
pen it to paper
purify those thoughts
by simplifying the verse
let the sun shine upon it
and let it be energised
with words of vibrant beauty
take it in hand
walk with it 
across the nation
spread charity
set it free
and the moon rises
above a cypress tree
the wind whispers
the words of everlasting bliss
upon those simple souls
