theres a soul in the river of my dreamstheres a rhythm in the rapids so it seemstheres a tell tale heart and you know you got to partwhen you come to the levee of the streamtheres a whistle in the wind thru the treestheres a vision in the waves of the seastheres an apparition that leads to indecisionand take hold of the voices that it freestheres knowledge in the mind of a mantheres danger in the horns of a ramtheres ecstasy and love and death from aboveand peace to be found if you cantheres life in the womb of a ladytheres imagination in creation just maybetheres the moon and the sun god with a guntrust in dog if your crazy
