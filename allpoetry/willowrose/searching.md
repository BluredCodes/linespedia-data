i took a long look 
to my amazement i did see
a freshly potted flower
i wonder what it could be
a rose to be loved 
a daisy to set free
how i wonder-how i feel
oh i wish i had sex appeal
to whom i know
i know not where you are
with this freshly potted flower i'll find you
no matter how far
i catch a gust of wind
i hold it's tail so tight
i search where mother nature takes me
i shall find my love tonight
as my potted flower blooms
from the sun's bright rays
a raindrop falls from the sky
giving hope for one more day
