starring off into the mystic..it's bombastic
clairvoyent dreams of surrealism
a backdrop of hazy lights clouding the horizon
feetcarrying bodies wherever they go
looking up in wonder monolithic towers
looking down in sadness spare change
it's madness
run away if you can
paradise is only a word
shopping cart traffic
conversations of lunacydal gibberish
disturbing fabric
pythogenic fantasies whisper introductions of plutocracy

where the hell are we going
demoralizied by stygian declerations
have you slapped a politician today
have you kissed a dictator
it's 4:20 do you know where your children are
?????????????????????????????????????????????
all the world is a gallow and our limp naked bodies hang
where can we find the elysian fields
to sing our songs of freedom
