---
title: "dark angel"
writer: "WillowRose"
slug: "dark-angel"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/30649-dark-angel-by-WillowRose"
---

strong dark outer shell
misunderstood soul straight from hell

a delinquint bitch in her own mind
truly honest and very kind

miscievious smile
big brown eyes

a truly beautiful combination
so hard to find

sorrow and heartbache
an unhealthy trend
traces of anger i do hope to mend
creativity and wisdom
she has it all
talent and beauty
i couldn't help but fall
shy sweet and kind
but afraid to show
her true qualities
i came to know
peace and love
will help her grow
with me i only hope so
