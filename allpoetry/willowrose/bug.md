I saw liquid dripping dreams 
as trails
of the new millennia lay in squander
the path to destruction intensifies 
every so often
today it reaches climax
an orgasm of evil spews out 
over the world
from that milky way
mother of invention should have had that abortion
killing then what cannot be killed now
roaming the streets
walking through a zoo of zombies
exiled from freedom
destroyed by laziness
surfing on their ass
glued to the morphine like tv
(is this why we call it programing)
in a time where 
eastern philosophies are growing
we are still weakened
mind  body  and  spirit
can run no faster than
the virus that deteriates them

squashed
like
 a
bugwhat?
