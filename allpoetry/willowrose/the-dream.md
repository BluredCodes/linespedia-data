---
title: "the dream"
writer: "WillowRose"
slug: "the-dream"
source: "AllPoetry"

i'm standing here alone
until i start to spin
i'm standing here with wolves at my feet
and eagles circling my head
liberty above inertia below
i'll start my journy i'll go slow
as i spin some more i begin to float
upwards i go leaving the beasts to fade
i am out of sight spinning
until i stop 
high above floating in nothingness
i feel there is a battle raging on
flashes of light bouncing around me
sounds of thunderous booms
it all goes black
everything slows everything quiets
it is now silent
i spin and spiral down
i reach the river
i am in front of the horizonnow i stand still
as the sun rises in front of me
the sun spins away
the moon appears
and spins away
venus is now 
it spins away
mars i now see
and spins away
mercury takes place
and spins and gone
saturn 
spins
jupiter appears
spins and fades
it draws dark i spin
now standing in the light
i see an angel appear
i follow the angel
it is bright
i'm now within the light
as i follow i enter a dreamy world of aspirations
i continue to follow and enter
an area of destruction
passing through now into
love and it's beauty around me
then i emerge into progression
and as i follow i feel power
and then i enter the wilderness
i spin again with the angel as my guide
i am an infant
i spin more and am a child 
i spin into a youth
then to man
i continue spinning now i am mature
and spinning some more i am now old aged
i' am now here nor there
i begin to cry tears fall
i spin downward
and am lying down
this rain of mine falls
my eyes open
i see a rainbow
planets
and stars
i spiral through the rainbow universe
i try to speak   i can't
i start to crawl towards the rainbow universe
the rainbow universe expands
i continue towards it but no use
i can't reach it
fading away
i feel squeezing sensation
i'm being constricted
a snake has coiled around me
my body restricted
i see hope and faith and love in the distance still fading
into me is squeezed fear and dought and despair
into my soul thoughts
am i changing or dyeing
am i transforming or being destroyed
my eyes close and then finnally open
i see a caterpiller
crysalisis
a butterfly now flies away
i breath a deep breathe
i am free 
mind body soul
i am free
i spin away
i am now at two paths
a wide road clear and easy
a narrow road difficult to move through
a crossroads in my journey
i am floating here
what am i waiting for 
take a road and follow it
but i can't move
around me only blackness
a struggle emerges
the wind blows 
a fire begins to blaze
tidal waves rush in
and trees timber
it is all madd
the elements are in battle within me
i feel the power
wisdom
intelligence
mercy
of all things real and unreal
severity
beauty
victory
eternity
where are thee
the foundation
the kingdom
i shall find my way
the battle fades
as i rise above 
i now KNOW which way
i DARE myself
i WILL follow a path
but only in SILENCE
as i float though mirrors
of mine own image
all different faces spinning
and morphing
reason
truth
justice
will survive
i am now naked
heading toward the path
i feel strong
as i head toward the narrow difficult road
i am feeling scared
i am feeling nervous
the unknown surrounds me
but i continue
there are many obstacles
it grows dark
but i feel the rainbow universe is this way
at the end of the dark path i see a glimmer
as i travel down the path
it becomes rough 
but love is in my soul
there is a lightforce
and i hide myself in the bosom of the eternal mother
i follow
the guiding star of love
i relax and breathe easily
the truth is upon me
grace comforts my resting soul and obliterates all evil until there is nothing but perfection
nirvana
paradise
heaven
or hell
