---
title: "why sleep"
writer: "WillowRose"
slug: "why-sleep"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/33117-why-sleep-by-WillowRose"

theese days i only go to sleep to dream...my dreams are supernatural horrors, sweet lulabies, extraordinary adventures, hallucinations of a reality i couldn't find.
when i can dream no more only then shall i awake.
