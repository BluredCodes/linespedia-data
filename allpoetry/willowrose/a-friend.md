a friend stopped by last nighthe is always on the runin search of enlightenment...........he enters...........my mind with excitementi feel his presence...overcoming mecannot deny what i feelmy friend is strong...he is a Creatormadness he brings .....sooner or lateri say leave my houseyour nothing but an altered stateif hit again -better beware the fatefeelings i get...when under his spellpsycho analytical nightmares...straight from hellmy friend scares meputting thoughts in my headgoodness is gone--my friend................soon to be deadoh he don't go without a fighttrying to deceivetakes on new meaning..actions become strangeri follow him---he leads mei see trails---he has me followthere are manyrainbows.........behind the mountainscolorful....if anyi take his hand...scared to go highertogether we peak....with forbidden desirehe smiles at me...full of laughtermy soul now blackerheart beating fasteri stare deep...eyes of disastermy friend...decieves me no longer..nothing else to hidelong trip home...pain makes me cryfinally asleep...new day to comemy friend(like a shadow) foreshadowing events to comei will not be followed--cannot relive the pasthave to set him free for he's destined to last
