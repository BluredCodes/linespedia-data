i knew a lovely girl who was round
then one day wellness was found
big brown eyes and a seductivly sexy grin
sweet soft voice my heart is hers to win
a chance meeting a touching of the fingers
eyes starring deep, captivated, desire lingers
a walk in the hills together under the sun
dancing, talking and laughing  what fun
pretty vibrant colours flowers in bloom
day turns to night we kiss under the full moon
lovely years as our hearts grow fonder
we builld our log house way over yonder
time stops ticking rain pitter patters
crazy in love and madder than the mad hatter
we walk through our meadow and row down the river
my hand slids up her thigh as she begins to quiver
we hold each other close and slid under the covers
we are as one with each other the perfect lovers
