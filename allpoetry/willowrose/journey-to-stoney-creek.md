title: "journey to stoney creek"

jeremy's pearl - the blue grass
cloudy smoke - the circles high
our intelect and visions of grandeur
pass thought of otherwise dorment nature
into conceptual conscienceness
as waters soothe
awarness for humer is ever rising
our smiles begin the healing 
of our mind - body - soul
to degrees unforeseen in theese troubling times
but not in this state
back again into the unreal reality
of peaceful innocense
and naive wonderment
we fly
we soar
above all as the stae of I begins
a stoned bliss journey
to recovery
we are now in fulfilled perfection
looking into the rippling waters of stoney creek
