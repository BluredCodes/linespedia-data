perplexed by the understanding that nothing is real
for the most unreal of matter is something even more real
undiscovered are the missing links
for which all matter generates from
worried that nothing will fail
brings hope for those without gain
dizzied by the utterance of mundane societies
will forever go untamed
darkness has much lite
light has much energy
the extreme nature of chromatic organisms
defy the deliberate ability to habituate
the tolerance of programmatic thoughts
