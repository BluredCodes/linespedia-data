title: "rainbow 1999"
writer: "WillowRose"

all across the nation
rainbow gathers for celebration
mother earth is creation
peace and love and liberation
all across the nation
rainbow love for all population
peacefull harmony and elation
share in kindness end starvation
believe in me my words are your salvation

they shall set you free
the birds flying high like me
my words with the winds are free
