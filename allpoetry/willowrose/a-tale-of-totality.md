seemingless endless
among my uppermost high
dazzled by the friendly
faces .. smilin' .. faces..
the road nowhere leads
thoughtless and scared
quietness is still tranquility
a candles flame burns all night
a dream * burning the mind
a rainbow .. a tropical .. a rastafarian .. Say
"I and I will put the fire to our tea"

spiraling higher
a kite in the wind
a deep breath of 'om'
chi .. blissful .. chi..
stillness awakens the soul
blind and simple
the mourning is still emptiness
a stars glimmer lights the sky
a dragon * phantasizing pasts
a meteor .. a galaxy .. a skywalker .. Say
"space the not so final frontier"

halucinating taboo
soaring with an eagle
trip the light air fantasy
free .. spiritually .. free..
nakedness in the lotus
flowers and eroticies
for spirituality is still orgasmicly
a smile warming the heart
a butterfly * renewing life
a lover .. a friend .. a world .. Say
"explore freely  be gentle and kind"
