hello zumbi smoking his pipe
upon the red rocks is the break of sunlight
the birds of prey are taking flight
i can't see a thing have i lost all sight
i know the stories that others have told
mine is true and generations old

'ole zumbi the chief who others forgot
so i begin with him while the coals are still hot

he was a tall green man with sun dried skin
He had a Buffalo a tepee a squaw so thin
he was naked as a bear and danced all around
he walked like a chicken and barked like a hound
'ole zumbi you crazy witchdocter man
mix me up one as fast as you can
zumbi got cactus juice and a peyote button 
mixed with yage but all for nothing'
before i new it he had drunk it all
he did a little dance rain started to fall
the wind was blown and the sky turned grey
listen carefully to what zumbi had to say

meditate on the essence of all things
suppress suffering eliminate anger
don't confuse with illusion
for the mind itself makes all possible
do not learn from a disillusioned mind
do not drink from the water that is poisoned
do not eat the meat of an animal in extinction
when you reach the top of the mountain keep climbing
compassion is the heart living beautifully
surround yourself with zen lunatics and hepcats
sip tea for it brings joy.gladness.serenity.madness.ecstacy
be equally empty. equally to be loved. equally a coming Buddha

ole zumbi - wise ole man
being for years
the simply nothing man
