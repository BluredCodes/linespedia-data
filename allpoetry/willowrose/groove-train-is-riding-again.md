the groove train has pulled into whistles
woooo!!!woooooo!!!!
just two months after the last strange dance
and fear that we would not ride that train again
the dancing will continue
the music will continue
thankyou
i'm just happy...and hear is what i want to say to all of you
everyone
have peace on your mind
love in your hearts
and freedom in your soul
i want to see smiles on your faces
dizzyness in your eyes
undulations controlling your bodies
groove with us to the music
feel the rhythm throu your feet 
and stomp 
spin
frolick
grab your friends and do the dos e do
put flowers in your hair 
paint your shoes blue
and bring with you kindness
have a few drinks
smoke 'em if you got'em
and if you don't let's toke about it
the really fun times
the strangers that you you meet
shakedown out in the street
scarlet and her begonias
sugar dipped magnolias
barefoot dancers with bells
a friend of mine 
a friends of yours
the heart and gold band
the greatest story ever told
