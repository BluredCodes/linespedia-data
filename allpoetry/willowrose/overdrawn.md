---
title: "overdrawn"
writer: "WillowRose"

the spilling love of the hub lawn-
overdrawn
the quite breeze the beautious trees the leaves-
overdrawn
the girls of colour of the islands of the mainland
walkin talkin not to me i'm-
overdrawn
the gazebo holdin me up
the music mellow keepin me high hello
a happy moment a smile not for me not for long-
i'm overdrawn
