My time is now to look back on those whom I am not whole without
The city finally spoke to me. the golden gate is no longer a bridge.  I am not a prisoner in paradise.  The streets have many shadows and even more shopping cartz.  People roll by me as I stroll down the Embarcadero.  Up down up up up down all around water is to be found. A bay an ocean another bay.  All over here's grover or there's elmo biracial lovers or illegitamite brothers..grouch..you decide it's all garbage anyway..grouch
Goodbye I'm low hello I'm high..even higher in coit tower..wheres my power.  Lets walk the city streets.. walk up peter macharini steps.  watch the red light hustlers play, the hip poets say, and the no names in north beach stay..this way...
I ride with strangers all of whom are afraid to sit by the flame for fear of getting burned..fire full of desire.. Turn on hate or haight love it.  I'm off, I stroll thru the dead streets.. no longer alive. but clinging to a lost life..the summer of love is long gone...i hear voices.....head to the park..weed//buds//fluff// words on the street..I stroll by the Janis tree sit on hippie hill...puff puf untill....out from the haze the fog clearly now i see the blount the sutro tower my power that generates the burningto keep me high..sutro is high fog and haze cloud my vision thoughts and time to envision ...
wheres BART that old steel fart bringing me underground..city to city...playing his harp..or violin..some spare change will get you a grin..kaching in the tin..another spin...I had a Korean lover her name muni..i ride her good ..to her house on Saturn...or is it neptune always spaced out... zoomin' shroomin' we  mount diablo high in the east hills..fires from hell burn in the dryest of dayz.
a gate and seven bridges....a skyline a pyramid  a rock sand sun fun..waves sonar tidal waves like a sunburn...i go undergroundtracks and voicesfrom nowhere..somewhere..i will go ..where? tell meapproaching one car J(i'll smoke it)followed by two cars K...in 2 minutes..i car m.. in 5 minutes..1 car l in six minutes...damn where is that J.. i need a ride
coming out of darkness the the light...smile wink touch speak
speak my name her name we together be insane..I'm game we game who's game...
the sunset the sunrise...we ate we spoke we joked we toked....drank coffee it's brewed we screwed..she got off got soft is this a loft.
a toothbrush some eggs  hottleggs.used for walking..out the door my mind is soar.  like nothing ever ..i scored..
richmond-seacliff--marina--excelsior.Italien chinese mexican foods for poetic moods..or erotic grooves...leave me
drive to Ny--germany..costa rica--swedish massage i hear voicesback again my trip is over did it even begin..
the sun is shining but i can bearly see.
trhu the haze crazy dayz.. hammer a nail in the peninsula..it's frail..shaking..rattling...no more lock downs no escaping..here foreveraway from it all......goodnight sleep and dream forever never ever..forever whatever
