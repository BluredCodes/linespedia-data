title: "Hunger"
writer: "Lisanne"

When I am old and am forced to limits
Concerning matters of food and love and
Passions better suited for sweet young maidens,
Will you think me foolish then to want
Chocolates instead of toast
Pleasure in place of sleep
The song of your hand against my flesh
Echoing into the quiet stillness of night.

Will you paint my lips with the
Blood of ripe strawberries
Drink of my honey as if it were wine?
When our lives are trimmed in shadows
Will you still nourish me with your flesh
Feed my soul with the fire in your eyes
Take me down so that I may once again fly?

Quick!  Before it's too late.
Must I cook us bread and sausages?
Or might we dine on the banquet of our bodies.1999,
