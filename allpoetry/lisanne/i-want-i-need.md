---
title: "I Want / I Need"
writer: "Lisanne"
slug: "i-want-i-need"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/16314-I-Want---I-Need-by-Lisanne"
---

I want to awaken at dawn
to find myself wrapped like a gift
within your arms
and to tremble
as you untie the ribbons 
to my soul.

I need to look into your eyes
and see the passion ablaze within
and hear you call out my name
as if it were born 
against your tongue.
