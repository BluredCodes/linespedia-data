---
title: "Love Letter"
writer: "Lisanne"
slug: "love-letter"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

The page is blank and begs for words. Shall I
Write what I feel?  Or would you want to read
Romantic lines? I could give those a try
And be your soft and proper girl, indeed.
Perhaps you'd like to hear the words I'd choose
As my fingers curl 'round this pen's firm shaft
And touch its tip to warm moist lips and muse
Upon the lines I'd like to script.  At last
I'd press the point to paper like a cure
And ink would flow like honey from my thighs.
Would it shock you then? Would you find allure
In learning of the words between my sighs?
I tremble now as I begin to write
And stroke my darkest fantasies to flight.
