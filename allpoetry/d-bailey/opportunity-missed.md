Some how I ended up next to you,
A few minutes late to the classroom,
It took a while for me to realise,
You were what I've been looking for all my life,
 
Opportunity missed,Your chance has gone,
You should have said something,
What could have gone wrong?

Beautiful,funny and you've got a brain,
I knew my life would never be the same,
You'd talk to me like you actually cared,
And laughed at my jokes that no-one else had ever heard. 

Opportunity missed,Your chance has gone,
You should have said something,
What could have gone wrong.Title say's it all.
