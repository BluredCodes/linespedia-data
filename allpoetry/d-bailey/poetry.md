Wank,wank,wank,
Why me?
Wank,wank,wank,
Self pity,
Wank,wank,wank,
Why is the world fucked up?
Wank,wank,wank,
I'am different,
Wank,wank,wank,
Why do they stare?
Wank,wank,wank,
All alone in my pain.
Wank,wank,wank,
I'm off now to cry,
or maybe,
Wank,wank,wank.
