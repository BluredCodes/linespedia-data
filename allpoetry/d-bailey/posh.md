A symbol for all that's gone wrong,
Hollow, corporate, all creativity gone,
Famous, adored, A national icon,
Your face is the product,
Who needs a good song?

Part of a group that defined a generation,
ZigAZig ahh; what an inspiration,

Pefectly packaged in five different flavours,
Please God will somebody save us,
You broke away and now you're out of control,
"My New CD Is On Sale In Your Nearest Mall",

Part of a group that defined a generation,
ZigAZig ahh; what an inspiration,


I'll sell my story to the highest bidder,
Hello, Ok; My battle against getting thinner,
Things, though, are not going quite as you planned,
"You'll Find My New CD bargain binned At Poundland."Posh Spice the perfect example of what celebrity has become.
