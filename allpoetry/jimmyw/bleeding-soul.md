My soul is just one
For others I am jealous
My life is my own
To share it I am selfish

My pride steps around me
To open her door
She has what I want
I have lost this before

Her giving takes persistence
This threshold is cold
There are other besides me
Who are closer I am told

To most she is a flower
To me a stem with thorns
I am cut with her silence
Eleven years we are born

To share it I am foolish
So for this I must be mean
Her presence makes my life
My bleeding gone unseen
