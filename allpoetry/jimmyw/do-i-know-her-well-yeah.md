title: "Do i know her? well, yeah!"

IF THERE ARE COOKIES
SHE WILL EAT THEM
IF SHE IS SLEEPING SHE WILL HIT HIM
IF YOU MAKE HER LAUGH
SHE WILL HEAR YOU
IF YOU GIVE HER PEACE
SHE WILL LISTEN TO YOU
IF YOU LOOK HER IN THE EYES
THEY WILL BE THE LAST THING YOU SEE
YOU WILL WANT NOBODY ELSE
'CAUSE YOUU KNOW THAT YOU ARE HAPPY
DO NOT MAKE HER MAD
WITH HER IT IS A CURSE
AND YOU CAN FORGET 
FOR BETTER OR FOR WORSE
HER VOICE GIVES YOU CHILLS
THAT REACHES YOUR HEART
UNLESS YOU SCREW UP
THEN IT WILL TEAR YOU APART
YOU KNOW THAT SHE IS SMART
BUT SHE WILL TELL YOU SHE IS NOT
ALL YOU DO IS WATCH HER
AND SHE WILL TEACH YOU A LOT
YOU TELL HER THAT YOU LOVE HER
AND SHE CUTS YOU WITH A KNIFE
SHE SAYS THAT YOU DON'T KNOW HER
BUT SHE'S WRONG
FOR THE FIRST TIME IN HER LIFE!
