It was a Sunday evening, Kentucky I was in
To pick up Sam's family, when her cousing walked in
Her name was Rebecca, with a smile on her face
To show off her beauty, style, natural grace

We wen to the movies, Blair Witch I've seen twice
She sat right beside me, the smell of her nice
We all shared some popcorn, cup filled with coke
I look at life funny, but this was no joke

my heart seemed excited, my voice with a quiver
If I held her close, not doubt I would shiver
My thoughts were my own, cause today we just met
My instincts won't fail me, at least notjust yet

Moonshine was his name, seven shots I surrenter
The girls would take none, for them it's the blender
If the ice was thick, then this was the breaker
So who was the giver, and who was the taker

The camera was out, this night we will remember
I must see her again, will she be here in September
This girl has done something; I can feel it in me
Big smile on my face, her heart I set free
                       JimmyWilsonJr
