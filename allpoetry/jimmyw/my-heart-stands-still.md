The moon stands still, the trees rush by
My breath is deep, my heart is sighs
Looking at my thoughts, the picture is you 
Your beauty, your laughter, your smile too
My thoughts all of you, I will not deny
It's you that I want, it's with you I am high

You are so far away, I find this not fair
So I write these words, might feel like I'm there
Toghether we'd walk with nothing but time
May I take your hand, and put it with mine
We'd stay up all night, watch the stars rise
Our souls wide open, no boundaries, no lies

Don't let me scare you, my heart it writes true
So I hold it responsible, for the things I will do
We talk with each other, your voice soft, so sweet
The day that I met you, my heart skipped a beat
My words I speak with, will never be for spite
So I leave you a question, love at first sight?
