Decisions, decisions the ones I have made
Some make you happy, others make you grave

Mixed emotions up in the air 
Making the wrong move I do not dare

You can't see the pain I hide it so well
It's so deep you'll never be able to tell

i cover up the scars imbedded in my skin
I'm playing the game I'll never win

Am I dissaponting, do I put you to shame
How can I only be the one to balme

I fall deeper and deeper into a hole
I myself have dug 

These lies you wrap around me
