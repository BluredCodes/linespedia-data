title: "Letting you"

I ley you into my life, 
Letting you see
Who I am
And what I could be 

You may not understand,
But you'll know 
Why I am the way I am 
By the feelings I'll show

you can't change me
Nor would you try 
Even when I stare
You never ask why

You liked me
Just as I came 
Accepted me 
From my lips to my nose and face

You excited my expectations 
By far
I liked you you for everything your not
And everything you are1-7-02,
