SO BEAUTIFUL, SO SAD, SO ANGRY, SO MAD 

Her only companion   
Her reflection 
Her infection

So beautiful, so sad, so angry, so mad

Her only space 
A four walled place 
Her case

So beautiful, so sad, so angry, so mad

Her only outlook
Her life shook
Her life took

So beautiful, so sad, so angry, so mad

Her only sin 
Was their win
She held down her chin

So beautiful, so sad, so angry, so mad

Her only cry
Her soft sigh
Her final good bye
