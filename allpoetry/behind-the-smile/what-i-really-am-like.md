Sometimes I won't shave my legs
Or i won't brush my hair 
I like to wear my pajamas all day long
I eat frosting from the can
And drink from the carton
On sundays I sleep till noon then watch the movie chanel all day
I wait till the last minute for just about everything
I'm really bad at calling people back
Taking a shower is my favorite part of the day 
When I'm tired I get pale and cranky
I can't keep a strait face, NO matter what
Some days I like to cry all day long
Other days I can't stop laughing
I crack my nuckels all the time 
I can't watch people make out, It's gross and just weird
I have many alter egos
I play couch comando
Long car rides make me puke up everything I've every eatin
I have to pluck my eye browns every single day 
Wearing bras suck some days I won't wear one
