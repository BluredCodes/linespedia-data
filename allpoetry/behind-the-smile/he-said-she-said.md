title: "He said, She said"
writer: "Behind the Smile"
slug: "he-said-she-said"
source: "AllPoetry"

Hi he said
Bye she said 
Why he said
Die she said
Yes he said 
No she said
Stay he said
Go she said
Please he said
Stop she said
I will he said
Your not she said
I love you he said
I hate you she said 
You won't he said
Don't she said
Escaped he was
Raped she was1-7-02,
