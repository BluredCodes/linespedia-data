'A daze'A daze, a dream, sleepslumber mind, summer heatman to no where with staggering feetFirst a daze, then comes the dreamThen the sleep, no yesterdays to rememberno tommorows for seenjust him and his jim beamhe thinks of the future and what it may bringnow it is again springa daze, a dream, sleephe dreamed of tomorrow yesterday, but now he has forgotten,so he must repeat the method, the method of the forgotten.
