'Ocean eyes'

Oh what can i do?
what can i say,
your eyes of blue
chases sadness away

leaving me standing
gazing up to the sky
you look with your down cast eyes,
blue green and shy, hair like the clouds

you float over my sadness, with a loving frown
the light permeates, from a smile,
a touch of an angel lifts my soul
this you do, very much so

my sadness becomes gladness
from your presents it's known
my soul leeps with joy!
when you come home

come back to me, touch again
forever i love you
ocean eyes,eternity
