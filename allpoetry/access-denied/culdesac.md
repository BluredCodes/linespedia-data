'Culdesac'Whoa!! unto those who do not study, histories past is quite bloody'the names of times are now gone, different ones appear, but the show goes onYes a show you are part of, bow to your masters, put the chains on your feet, a sword above you're head makes this complete.You have given over the sword to a new misery, a new law makes you a criminal, because you have given power to the beast. A beast to which must be fed, your blood!! your head!!Look at the French revolution, study what happened and it's conclusionsfirst you was like anyone else, i didn't know you said to your self.Are you still chassing the tail? around and around we go, a culdesac to where you should know, are you able to peer through the vail? set long ago at the dawn, the sun rises from east to west, lower to the sky on the horizon it sets, a son is risen this day, a new dawn you have heard, can you unminse the words? who is the son and what is the name? misery is his game. A new old name it will be, history past paving the way, can it happen in this day? oh yes it shall come, it has many times with the setting sun, over the two mountains in between, it puts forth a new old gleem, rising again between the peaks as it warms you're cheeks, you first feel its warmth, then you shall burn.........
