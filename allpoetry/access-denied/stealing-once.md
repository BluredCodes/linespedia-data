'Stealing once'It is said that love is blind,to whom that love comes.Giving over ones heart, and reaching for from the startfor that ones love not depart, is hardest on the mind.When it has risen, and the feeling is forwarded, the trust grows and growspraying that this one never reaches, reaches the death throws.I have giving you all i have which to give!love me in turn, this i only ask of you, for tomorrows dreams not burn.Burn away no please not! feel what i have gave to you,its my heart that beats, and my soul repeats for your love in return.You have stolen my heart from the start, that which comes once to thee,Once stolen it is gone, never to be the same, harder and rougher this will make it, the beat is lowered i can't fake it.This soul, this mind has this dream, of tomorrows love for you can be seenthat no other can give me what you have shown, so please leave me not alone'Alone i am without you, my heart has concurred!all other thoughts are just a blur, i put you first before my thoughts, i will cast lots with my other thoughts leaving them for idle, i have giving you the power to bridle.You have stolen my heart, to which i will not prosecute.
