'A shadow'

It is enough just with a passing shadow.
Your presence, your life next to mine in this time on earth.

So little time to do so much, to love and create love for the tomorrow.
The passing years as we age, the children brought about to love and cherish.

memories are forever, and your shadow was there.
Just as you move in my corners eye, I think of those times I seen you not.

I watched you walk away from me, many times, I've seen you from this angle.
Your hair when it was messy, and when it was up right, your side as you read.
The light from which you read from, it shinned the poses of your thoughts.

Your shadow as it brushed me by, I was not at attention, as I looked it was to late to capture the moment.

I've seen your shadow, and that alone comforted me so....
