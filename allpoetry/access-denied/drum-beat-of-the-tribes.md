'Drum beat of the tribes'

Gather around this midnight fire
The mob masses, blood is desired!
Anger and chanting, waving their banners
here you will find no manners.

Hysteria is here once again, you may be without a friend!
singled out 1-2-3, one by one, neighbor against neighbor
this is your chance to rattle your saber.

There are many not just a few, once is was against the Jew!
this time it may be you!

Now you call yourself a patriot, just a few years ago it was a sin
today everyone is in!
Parroting is the theme of the day, everyone seems happy they say;
to be like everyone else, join the crowd! plenty in the house.

When did you discover? when did you bleed? where did you plant the seed?

Do you remember what it was about, the  fore fathers brought it out,
loud and clear easy to read, this was the task against tyranny!!

Jefferson, madison, Adams just a few, they knew what was in the brew
Franklin I think said it best, when he answered a woman for the rest!

A republic madam,( if you can keep it!!!)
