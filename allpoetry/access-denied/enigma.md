'Enigma'

I am an enigma, i can fit in any situation.
I can lower my intelligence to fit the crowd.
I can observe, and I can remain silent.
I can suggest a thought, then sit back and watch the action.

Look at the dumb founded frown, or the glazed eyes.
I can take criticism, then mold that to find your weakness.
Then I tell you about you, I can destroy your fantasy land,
Shatter your glass world.

Sometimes things broken are better when fixed.
Stronger, alert, caring, more loving.
First you hate me, because I seem critical.
Then you love me, because I show you yourself.

Then you realize you hated yourself,
and i took your hatred, then showed it to you.
Then you see better, like putting on new glasses.
The whole world is new, and you learned new things.

Then you asked me, how did you do it?
I answered, I stopped hating myself.....
