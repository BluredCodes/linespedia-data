Things to be

As I am working, I think about what can be
looking back on my past being brought down to my knees
I have learned many things in the walk of my life
and sorrows that can be

till this ends and a knee is bent to a wife yet to be
I am ready for that day, and my past is history
where is this angel that the stars intended for me
a new start, a new day is born, knowing she will never scorn

she will be my love, yes that is true
everything to us both will be new
love without pain, joy splended
the greatest gift God intented

for a man such as me, to love the ever more
I have a gift for her waiting, not a score
come to me my angel, yes I am still here
your words did not fall on a deaf ear

come quick! I am about to wake
my day dream almost over
run run please!! don't walk through the clover
in my arms rush, they are open

I will welcome you with my gift I have to offer
be there for me please......
