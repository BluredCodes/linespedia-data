'Taking away'Taking away my existence, i have an appointed time.You take away my tomorrows and leave me tired.You hurt me, you scare me.You take my breath slowly and leave me gasping.You take away my youth, you take my speed from under my feet.You give me no options, you hold me ransom.You Slowly creep upon me as i sleep, when i awake you are more prevalent.You lie to me, you cheat me.You are slow to forgive, you mend me to just lend me another pain.You can be horrible at times.Yet you can be gracious.You are perplexing, but i have no choice but to eccept you.You are age
