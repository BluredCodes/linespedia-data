' The enemy 'The enemy is lurking, the enemy is nighhis face on a poster, he's broadcast highhigh from an office,the antenna beamssoaring his image across the tv screenhe comes from a desert, he comes from an islehe comes from a mountain thats most highdown he swoops like an eagle, to devour us all!but his grip is like that of the 'lions paw'he tears into your mind, ripping your thoughtsfear, anger, surprise, you may bejust finding out, the enemy is you and meso look in the mirror, there he is!so tell your children to beware, the enemy is everywhere!in the buildings, behind desks, on the play ground with the restpoint a finger here, point it there,lots of fingers to spareso next time you stand before yourselfpoint a finger, yes, it may be you!!! boooooooooooo!!!
