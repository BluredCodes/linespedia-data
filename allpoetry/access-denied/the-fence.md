'The fence'

Oh there you are, sitting on that fence again.
Which way will you decide? Do you remember the last time you were there?
You jumped off, oh you know which side you did.

I'm watching this time, i will see the direction.  you can't go back, you cannot go forward.

It is hard to walk the fence huh?
Did you work for a circus? I didn't think so!
So it would be hard to remain on the wire.

Are you wishy washy? Confused?
I bet you are..
Jump!!! Jump!!!I will watch where your feet are placed. Is it solid ground?
You cannot remain there forever, you must decide, sooner or later. if you don't, someone will make the decision for you.

Then you will know it wasn't the right one.
You think about it! While I wait and watch.
