'Give me the belt'Oh how much i have desired, this you much admire!you walk into the room, witchy witchy you need a broomyour face smirked, your grin of grimace, no holds barredno limitsyou wear the tool belt this day, what is attached you won't say!Your fuzzy hand cuffs around my wristyour riding crop with a twist!black and leather brush to the skini feel it as you move inI peek around to catch a glimpseblack shiney latex with a sheen, a little slit in betweena teasing site i must say, what is in the tool belt today?no questions just response, i will only ask once!!!!A stretchy squeaky sound is heard, she say's don't utter a word!The sounds of jingles from the belt, santa clause would surely melt!!!Honey suckle is in the air, as she brushes my skin with her hairrubbing my back with her rack, something is in my crack!!!!my desire quickly turns to fear!! as i rise up like a steerwhat the hell are you doing dear?Give me the damn belt!!!!!Pooh is so bad hehehe
