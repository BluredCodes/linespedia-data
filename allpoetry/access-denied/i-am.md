'I am'


I am mysterious
Who could i be?
I bring many to their knees,
Your weakness is my power, that to which blooms me like a flower.

A flower has many colors, i change like the seasons.
You give me the reasons for your despair, you have yet to discover.

Each day that passes, You!! yeah you with the rose colored glasses,
Your next on my list! I won't miss.

I work as you sleep, you can't keep!!
'NOTHING' from me. I hold the keys for your direction, your past, your 
future.

Can you fight me? Can you win?
Do you have what it takes to turn a side your fears?
Sleep well my child, a horror is upon you!
Its not a nightmare yet.

I am older than you, i am the first light!
My shinning over takes my greed, i am wise, i am a comfort for the waiting.
I wait for you, the centuries have past, i have been the good son and the bad to which it serves me.

I am your friend, but it cost you much, what is it thou needest most?
I will deliver it unto you.
Whatever you want i shall give, i promise nothing after that which you take from me.

You decide what it is, money? power? control?
I hold the key, enter my temple.
There are no windows to look out from, there are no windows to peek in.
It is our secret, our work.

You will find many just as you, you can conspire against a few, rise above all the rest like the phoenix to her nest.

But!! Remember there is a price, can you pay for it?
Even the phoenix was consumed by me..........

'I am he'
