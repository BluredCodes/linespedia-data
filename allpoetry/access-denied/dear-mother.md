'Dear mother'

Dear mother, i have seen your pain
I know the changes you have felt,
the deep scares within you.
The men that have abused you, ravaged your soul and spirit.
Still you move on.

I know your beauty, and i know your rage!
Sometimes they are violent with fury, but i love you still.
You are much older than me i know,
even the short time of my presents here tells me so much about you.

You never sleep,the pain is deep.
Your cries are loud, but yet you are tender hearted.
Oh how many times have you turned over?
Been covered with many tears.
You opened up and replenished your soul, gave birth to many.

You started a new life, you can brush off the dust and begain a new.
No one can take away from you, what you haven't gave first.
No one can over power you! You are mighty, and men are small.

Dear mother (Earth)
