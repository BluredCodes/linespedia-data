'Suddenly'Your eyes blurry, twinkling light.The candle shimmers all the nightOur lips press once again! Over and over as in sinIf it were; then it is heaven.I consider the thoughts, my mind as a flowing river.Flashes of memories, the light casts it's shadowsa dark side then light, just as a moon lit night.A noise, a groan, fragrance all around as we dance our dance to the beautiful sound.My arms around you pulling you neareryour eyes reflect me as in the mirror, a flame is seen in your eyes, the passion glow as i touch you so!This i capture as the candle flickers, a moment in time like a picture,what an album this would make 'For God sake!!Weary, tired, half minded, the passion still there, i don't need reminded!Our love ever so strong, this is where we both belong' in each other's armsas we snuggle, minds connected, the river flows wellnever a drought, never a diversion.Suddenly i awake from my slumbering sleepblurry vision, i rub my eyes'Yes she is still next to me, was it just a dream?Only the residue left of the flickering light remains.
