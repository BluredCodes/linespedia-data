'Step in line'

Step in line, I'll wait I say.
Pushing me into line whipping me into shape.
Save what I have for me, go ahead do it!
I deserve more than you.

You step in line! You do it!
Its yours, not mine. I have nothing!
I don't care its your duty.
Why? Because I say so.

I'm the father you're the son. Why do I have to die?
Because I say so. I'm the father.
What difference does that make? Because I don't have to, you are here.
So get going and do your duty....
