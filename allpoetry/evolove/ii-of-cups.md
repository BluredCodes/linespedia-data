covering with words     that skim to fall
         hang basket hang
just a little longer    while 
                 wiping my own tears away


your grip is failing 
barely contemplating 
on coming back ~to you~

       ~to her~ 

to the moment thats scaling
downwards~ 


COME BACK TO 
this 2 of cups

to the one }that i say{ 

I love ~over ~

          again

repeat


convincing           is only  half right
when these lonley              actions  
                          are keeping sight


    when will you stay 
                   all night 
even when crumbling 
to the 
                        fight


       you leave grieve
conceive and achieve

    believe

as always


be here and wait for a promise 
of understanding
                                       when 

~i have to go now~


the cause that we choose to wait for
~entering ~
impact breaks skulls from the door


                  just a little longer
give to me ... more you swore 
adore and explore your whore

just ignore her obscure

~but nothing of you~

I cherish without knowing 
if///what \\\
im holding is true

see from everything that
   this flesh of body holds

wait patiently for lotus coals



              ~im falling and 
              this grip is lost~



                           taste this exhaust 
                           ~from her muted frost
