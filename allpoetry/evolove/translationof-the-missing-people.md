::












dedication~beliefs only in maximum capacity to withstand thresholds of self fueling manslaughtering pain

flirtation~involuntary manipulation to achieve ultimate skin bearing hunger arousal even if self satisfied

castration~removal from delicate torture that makes geometry miss spheres ability to line fertilize me on her plane

mutation~to alter or receive an affect of artistic change to ones outer structure that gazes their way into chromosomal alienation

lactation~milk ~them like beef searching for "hay" dont push me over to die on my feast from a forest fire in the park full of the missing people

translation~more then you really can handle
