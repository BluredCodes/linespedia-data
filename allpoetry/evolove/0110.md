~~~






Ever hear a sound so inspiring ...

You fell to the ground in shame for having heard ...

For being the only one who could ever understand
the severe meaning ...

Is it a wonder why geometry takes shape 
patterns always changing ... 

And the only one who could dare see it 
is the one who discovered why ... 






~~~xxxthe most beautiful peace in the worldxxx
