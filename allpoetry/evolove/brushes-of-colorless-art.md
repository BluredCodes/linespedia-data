brushes of colorless art



|desaeced| 



steps leading outward


vortex to create dizziness


world of outrage over her shoulders


cover me


cover me


bring back this person left in a bottle 


smoke rolled in paper to be coated 
of a numbing gel

covering your soars{

}sores covering her face 


  { dont decide }
until you reach bottom

that you made a mistake 


___listen to her scrape___

__her voice onto you__

_but you haven't got a clue _


never to change never to change rearrange 

distortion


you will bleed clear


\\no longer //


will you be of color


artist chooses 
to die inside of her strokes


{stealing the beauty 

brushing the shame}


rub this masterpiece ///
///out of the pane/niap


your vanity has stolen her~ grace~
your lack of grace has stolen her sanity

she has lost you

leave you
misplace
