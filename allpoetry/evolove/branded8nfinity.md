~Branded~8nfinity


If the wind takes me further out ...
will you reach for me?


Im     DR i fti n g


 Si f   t  i n  g   through this hole in the sky

the way you look       has me smiling shy

  ~Branded	~

with your violent scent 


buried under sacred cement

never let out

never let go


If i give you my life 

 will you leave me control?


a way in further 
filling your hole
to become your scars


a place you have never been kissed

inside the reservoir of your dismissed


Throw your Holiest of waters against me now 
for I have sinned

How could I lie in between two of the brightest stars
without letting one ~ in ~

a little deeper then the gods have allowed 

Bow~ before the crying blackness of infinity
and pray~ that the emptiness that still remains 
will forgive me~ 

               888
