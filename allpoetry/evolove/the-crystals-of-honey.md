Waiting is gone ~the distance is here
clenching the minds       comforting fear

                wrapped you up in wooden cloth ~ cry honey
Gratify
bees wax sealed his lips shut in the sweetest addiction
                                                  he has ever killed for
                              revere the dropping of her sincere

from the back he pulled out to let go of her hand
already hidden in the trees shaped for land

Into the river she cant swim

             Off the bridge she cant fly

                        Onto the bottom where she doesn't have faith 
to look to the top

We shall sink

and all the while she hums cavalier in the fishes ear
flatfish puffs and takes off his fin
forming an oval and starting to spin

Hurricane tides stop the rivers flow
and she gets lost into a tornado

Honey oil crystal thiefs~ bringing back~ her disbelief's
found and left on the pillow bed~ another murder overfed

Demonesque Angelic notes
guiding free the river boats

 

                              sail free over me
