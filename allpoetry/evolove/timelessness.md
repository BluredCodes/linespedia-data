Timelessness      

In your eyes,
I feel the pain of your tears, and the depth of your passion
spiraling down my breath ...

With your lies,
they burn within my mind, taking the hand of my thoughts
to dance with my death...

In your smile,
I dream of the eternal bond, that we once shared 
in that yesterday's tomorrow

With your touch,
I leave the truth and the influence behind, that has brought the 
demise in my sorrow...

I will not pretend,
that I knew how to love, before your strength unleashed me
from the wild...

In the end,
all will see that eternity, has no choice but to let us walk 
beside as her child...
