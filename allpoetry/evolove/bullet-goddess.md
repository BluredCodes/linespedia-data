bullet goddess

He filled her ass and felt through rancid love for her exit of mouth to open an escape

she held his scrotum close to her heart as she died a century of lives upon each second

she became his machine and he became her morphology through the feel of her breasts

Ever holding his shape ...

they loaded their babies armed gas masked in a 4 shooter revolver to pierce the world

one by one in a row of sibling united at embryonic gazes again in steel

spring counted backwards forward standing still for the powder

blow me with your squeeze

dont pull me into her rage

the twin goddesses saw the stretched face of the alien queen gazing in humble admiration

and they just froze into a painting stare that stalled her missing tongue for their next kiss
