~

~devious nature }beauty capture

naked in pale{ quivering skin

"~you are my kind~"

your face makes 

me weak

mind of a drifter} {in my remembrance

nocturnal ~ silent

like a ghost

invisible {in} spikes

scare them

fisting your madness

~elegance~  

midnight massacre

organ calls whimper

}mystery clinging{

holding the note that makes ~~~horns cry~

grow

red like your curiosity

~cum\/bustion
~I want you so hard } you break {
