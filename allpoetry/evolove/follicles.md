~


Morning came and she felt so peaceful ... feeling all has come to be as should ... quickness turned to sand .... As always...

as always ~     the women in skin tones started to sink ~


the memories taunt her laughing .... and haunt her passing ~ child

with images of her ... spread eagle on the bed ... sucked ... cleaned

mortified and dreaming of the doctors face before the countdown of 

15



                 10

pretend it doesn't hurt you that his brother had to come~ stole the car to run

from his slower side ... 
away from the speed that broke into our room and offered to rape me

hospital room

another bed to lay on

please put your hands inside me ... more then that hose outside of your pants ~ that gurgles

doctor~  will you kiss me after

can i have a flashback for the medication

She was mislead into believing ~ that she didn't exist ... how could she dream of her baby in a world she couldn't even see them in ....


mother isn't even here my love~

she will not be here until i kill her myself

afterfall of the mutilation

Your condemn for all eternity ...

the sight of children break your face ... you hate them ... you are a Demon
Slut of the ones who wish to know ~


your body~ the way it craves pain

feed me ~ to drain ~ 

peaceful girl in the closet ... come out to meet your maker ~

Destroy the world with your riddles and make them blind ... 
your art will pollute minds~ as it does ~ your own ...

bleed them like an animal ~ your the butcher of this meat

inside of your skin


~
