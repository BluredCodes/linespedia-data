__


Expression of pain] 

kneel into a deep song
sackcloth and ashes

mourning, with straps across her back 
weeds, that blow along our face
willow, under this branch ready to fall
cypress, rendering
personal affections

collection under
deep mourning; 

fondling 
amorous glances

succubus and succuba


__she fell to her knees and opened her slice 

*eat me*

oXXXo
Ashes ~ kiss the sky
4Ashley Bright
