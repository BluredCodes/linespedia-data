What unhatches from this creature of gold?
       Be bold...
         
Unfold...
              
The cold,
       blue surface...


I pull the skin up over my eyes,
and walk through the surface of my disguise...

I sing the memories like a melody ... and with every thought,
      
    
       ...IT consumes me....


...And THEY consume me with every thought...



When I walk through the surface of my disguise,
I will pull the skin up over my eyes...

Deny...
         
       ...The lies...

When I touch the cold blue surface...
...and I start to unfold...

I will be bold...

because the night will hold
this creature of gold
