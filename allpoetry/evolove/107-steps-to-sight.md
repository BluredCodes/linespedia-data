Ever since she was a little girl ~ her eyes failed in seeing all that was there
nothing looked as it should always in a fog but things of sight filled her mind steadily
more then enough she had to be strong and continue as all that could be 
... would be

give birth to me~  dreams and songs sang constantly as she wanted to be on the other side
holding something that meant more then herself ... something worth sacrificing all she ever 
knew and even her life would not be 
to much to ask

more then anything~ she wanted him to have eyes ... the kind that she never was able to keep
her boy~ her murder~ herself... i will kill and die for my only one~

nothing will ever be the same again~ my lonely child will still have a friend~ 
i just wont be the one to see him~

i wont tell~ ever ~ with secrets tying around my neck~ i have nothing to lose but the fact 
that im alive/// is to have held \\\so small just once.... to be able to look down at the only
thing that i could bare my life to ... ~~~ i work bleed and dream for only you~ but there
is never time to sleep~

must continue to dance~ 

strap me to the board ~i cant walk now ... even the rhythm that got me here is feeling strange
and she holds me to my strength~  bring me glass ... bring me the right to my breath seconds
before you crack my neck... i dont have eyes so let me see ... run into the glass that 
falls from my
hands

you will see in the dark
