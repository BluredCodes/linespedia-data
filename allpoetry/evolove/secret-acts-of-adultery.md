~1201~



It was that second ... the one where you looked in my direction / we locked \ for more then just affection 

A connection of bodily fluids exchanged in an instant ~ stiffening the wetness getting clouded by judgment 

As I walked around ~ I felt you enter as I continued to surround

I am not able to have you ~ as you are not able to need me 

/set the passion on fire and rub in the debris

Take you to my entrance and lead you in 

further leading down the basement inside my skin

Hammer your body to the wall
\ flatten your decadence before we fall

My canvas to keep 
as I brush your streak 
Against my thoughts 
locked tight ...

Disposed among these ...

secret
 acts 
of
 adultery

steal            your              sight  ...
