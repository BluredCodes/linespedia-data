? anticipation



pinned just beneath the sky
wind tunnel closing in 
at the speed of thought

succumb vile through extension
being blown just above ground
? ever hear a sound so inspiring

faces just rip apart the peace
pieces always flying toward 
asphyxia's sodomizing wool

crash into the wreck
? you fell to the ground in shame for having heard
a childlike cry faintly dismembered

in sleep you look to be dreaming
of times I never had
your skull is facing the wall

? for being the only one who could ever understand
the severe meaning
presision is the prismatic blade

? is it a wonder why geometry takes shape 
patterns always changing ... 
plane faltered at its crashing angles

trespass beyond will against domination
exhausted with bending morphology
bullwhip the beast in flesh tone attire

Mouth quivered spherically
? And the only one who could dare see it 
is the one who discovered why ... 

virtue stands no humility
