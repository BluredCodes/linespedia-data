fuck her


fuck her like you want to 


eat at her shell and empty this girl


fill her with promises of faith


promises of tomorrow


a different path 


a narrow line


as she stares


it makes her blind


what can be so bright


what can rape this night


pounding


i love my only one


infinite


how many faces will appear before me


eternally


you are the wretched souls


that thieves innocence


at the speed of silence


caged within this room


await your doom


black turns orange


cake filled with juices


drink me*princess of her own death*  
~loving a Hungarian King~
