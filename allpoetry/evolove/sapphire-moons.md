~~


Sapphire emotions and blood red moons ... the maroon of passions carries us below the lagoon ... 


Floating through the waters of your sweet embrace ... the excitement of my flesh peers through the lace ... 


Carried away by this timeless delay ... 
Never to stray ... I am yours today ...
Forgetting who I am and where I belong ... 
This feeling cant be so wrong ... 
I've never felt so strong ...


With blood red emotions and the sapphire moons ... enter into my cocoon, I want to taste you soon ... 


~~

I'm drowning in the river of your luscious grace ... let me die in the place ... of your beloved embrace ...
