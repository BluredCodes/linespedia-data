Portrait of a Last Dance  ~oXXXo~


I want to become the image of your dreams,
I want to mesh into your moist tongue when you speak of me...

Im awaiting to explode among the midnight sky of my fate,
Im awaiting to dissect the portrait melting my mind that will set me free...

Discovering the madness of simple seduction through a devilish smile,
Discovering the ultimate climax through manipulation of your denial...
stay a while...

Linger in my mind like the stained memory of your last kiss,
Linger between my flesh and absorb this heat that melts your frozen heart...

Dwell with me of the secrets we shred amongst the sheets of satin,
Dwell with me the ultimate price we paid so that the earth could never sever us apart...
now just colors of art...
