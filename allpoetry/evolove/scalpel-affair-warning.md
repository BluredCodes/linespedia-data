your the father to the children that bare themselves
                      for your needs

selfish disaster dressed in an old mans face

                       for you they bleed

feed them a plate of scum
each and everyone of them 

must                      commit

no one leaves without spreading shit filled 
cheeks                                            apart

swords inside only one
decapitating there only way to excrete 


bodily fluids
              harm us none

gold on my face
i want to teach the ways to feel nothing

drink from your over filled 
                                        bladder of my own urine
                       you drink from me

you eat for me

                                 all in a row undressed
virgin to all but my own phallic
                        incest

scalp you like a semen butcher learning to cut hair

                                               Scalpel affaiR


you know i cant kill you
your the one i like to fuck

                             so much
             your life exist in my sperm

you will never think of me the same
