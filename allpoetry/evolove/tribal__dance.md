~~~~~ ~  ~~~~ ~  ~ ~~ ~ ~  ~ ~ ~  ~ ~ ~~ ~ ~ ~ ~ ~ ~ ~ ~ ~  ~ ~ ~ ~ ~



for your love 
in the distance with silhouettes holding the staff of Slavic flames pleading a chance

just one glance 
of your Tribal Dance

you wear the flesh of exotic animals dying from a broken heart never to be of your 
kind
not able to keep your mind

bleed 

me

knead 

me 

breed 

me

mislead 

me

The rings of Saturn watch in awe ... As you Steal the Stars ... and their eyes fall

Fire kisses in water to hold wet skin ... fold within ... Mandolin

King of faith in a sea of Horse ... rising remorse ... transcendent force

Royal of radiant countenance ... romantic dominance ... misleading all in trance

entrance bechance this Tribal Dance
