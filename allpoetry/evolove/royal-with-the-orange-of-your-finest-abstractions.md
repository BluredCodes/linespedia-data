__



whispers ... 

like raindrops against the softened painting of your soul

motionless ...

suspended drifting lightly over the world 

cut me with the knives of your protrusions

powdered glass of finest grain

i want to burn in this dead of winter

reflecting our long night of rain 

eskimos unleash their volcano dance 

i run my fingers through the rope of yr hair

hear mother earth skim the crystal of seduction

trembling like a castaway finding port in a gale 

for the layer that will run to be done 

oh yr like these sun kissed sands

finish every last morsel of my crumbs

that slip between my fingers

brittle inside you with clenching teeth

bring me to water

to brand your claw over her silky meat

kiss the salt from my lips

toxic of my companion

yr jungles will nourish me 

through your eyes i will drown

in yr waterfall i will bathe
