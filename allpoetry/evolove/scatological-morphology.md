Scatological Morphology                              ygolohproM lacigolotacS



         Excrement crusts over an escape pourus in containment fillers

malodorous drifts deeper in nasal passages leading inwards focus along cells 

not in confinement as animals seek revenge ... punishment is what they crave

            as the fuel for wisdom ~open possible edible fetishes ...

Cannibal as in eating ones own taste ... different in consistency

      but still one claims the same ... One ~excites vulnerability in attack 



        Derivation lubricating holes for selfish satisfaction ...

           Drench skeleton quarters fit puzzled in pieces 

             Seized engine for slicing 

               Hand carved in personal skill

                 Be still quiet remorse 

                   Heard through strips of drilling panic

                     Regressed shock electricity 

                       VOLTAGE 

                          engraved
