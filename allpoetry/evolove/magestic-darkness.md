And all you want is a simple touch
to soothe away the madness ...
And all you need is an honest smile
to wash away your sadness ...


Insecurities pulling without remorse 
eating at the very defense ...
Scraping through your undiluted smile
etched surrounded in condensed ...
  

Eloquence 



Flowing with a kind of desire
most have not known ...
The scolding passion of 
majestic darkness
is roaming your heart ...
 

Alone 



I will be there for you if only you let me 
I want to be there for you to ease your pain ...


If only just a little 



I'll be there to share your eyes and help you to see 
when they are drowning in the rain ...


Of your crashing riddle 






... for you ...
