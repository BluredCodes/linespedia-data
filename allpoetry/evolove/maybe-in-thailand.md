*   ___   *




                            maybe in ~Thailand 

                     if I was there to dance for you

                            absorb your stares 

                         and bring your through

                                   }{
                             my lustful dance 

                            inside this cage

                            I could lick your

                        stiffening engulfing rage

                                   }{
                            to be the slut 
 
                           to bring you home

                                   }{
                         there is no where else

                           I escape to roam

                                   }{
                        If I could be that moment

                         in the undressing body

                         to escape this life for

                                   }{
                          a second } reality

                           would the Heavens 

                                   }{
                          }}} } } fall into me

                          entering so UnGodly

                                   }{
                                as I am

                               Stripped 

                                and tied 

                               for SeXXX
