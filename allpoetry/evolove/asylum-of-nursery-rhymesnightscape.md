Nightscape:



terror form

external remorse ~ follow this                vacant lot

                                        homicidal intercourse

phallic symbols 

                             over

vagina scabs                                      ~ eat his meat ~

       _____steadyhardsteadyhardsteadyhardsteady______


inside this                      ?scar~a~fice?

your life


                  pinned in this vice~      order of pain

wheels flutter
               fester faster

until no more 

                                   scrape metal flying in castrating screeching

rips into the pavement of the milk filled cell

bleach me for purification

                                                   eyes are sunken deep

they make me weep


                                      obsession seep

Asylum of nursery rhymes

                                                 \Sleep/

                  and you die                                             lovE
