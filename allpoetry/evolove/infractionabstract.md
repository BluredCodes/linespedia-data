Infraction
Your hot cum all over me *spill words across my chest *no rest *until your drained of this spill *fill me up once more *take me under the floor *and break away *you wont stray *I define you *you refine me *feline across*adobe *bridges *leading to *my lips *deadly *on *impact
Abstract
