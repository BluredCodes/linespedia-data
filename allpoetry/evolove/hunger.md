|

recycled flesh of the memory tainted 
with the words of your painted
exsistence fainted
at my feet...

I'm crying for the sin
that I'm longing to begin
sinister grin
hands across my skin
flushed in a spin
i'm dying for you...

with every breath I want you to
undue
the things you thought you knew
pursue 
and consume these thoughts
of me...

|
