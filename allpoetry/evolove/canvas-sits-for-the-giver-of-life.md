canvas sits for the giver of life::


the giver of life   
Flair of woman with horns of hair

Canvas is empty
and so alone

she appears every time birds howl
and the giver makes the canvas
fill

Red and against the wall he waits 
for the brushes she makes
against his face

White with traction he sits with her 
water and bleeds with gentle fur

the giver runs across the street and
we watch him wait so discreet

statue like in the rain
with no movement even in Pain

no breath without her consent
forgotten into this Accident

mirage of her sits in the corner 
waiting with him for someone to mourn her

canvas abandoned empty twitches 
soon to be met with 
loving stitches

sew him up doll of life
one with the horns 
cut of blackend knife

::he smiled 
when i said
you make 
art together::for the couple i watch 

when no one else will 

see them::
