{Sibylline}



corner stone with 3 sides says 

~take me~ 

to the walk of life

i love the way you throw your rocks to the sky

a string of auburn tendrils drips from the leaves

cold blowing

always below -0 to need the warmth

the pictures you mark in the sand

leaves me curious to the riddles of your myth

over looking the waves crashing upon the shore

just one to hold on to

they say she is many

but in fact she is only one lining to the ultimate

she deceives others of things they shouldnt see

so that the ones who can

come to her without hesitation



{Ominous}
