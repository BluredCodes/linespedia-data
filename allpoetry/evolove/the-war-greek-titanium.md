(nm-ss)Nemesis

Vengeance. 

my love



sweet decay


another acid outpouring

burn bitch~


eat your skin off of the lips that suck 


bones 


contaminate through enamel

herpes itch

penis tick

Themis\The"mis\

protect the one from hell

im going


coming out of the walls


like asbestos

(s-bsts, z-)hurt me

from my ceiling that waits for the

 sun 
            dont  shine 


i dont even know if im alive 


~but~ for~
~sure~


your already dead
