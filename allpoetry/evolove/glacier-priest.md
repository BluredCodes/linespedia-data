les dechirures d\'angels peignent le ciel....

sad like glacier tears~ i hear such sad songs in your fight to whisper me 
love notes ~


we both dream... of the time when i take your body ... when i steal your mind from slumber ... ... how many times do you let me enter....
 and how many nights do i let you cum inside me... take me out of this place just for an eternity stop like a heart attack beat.... 
you feel so good...

your obsession with demon //the demons in your mind ~ has me walking the string outside of the window sill

over looking the burning city ~ i watch the geese pull boats to safety...
they learn to fly when they soar the sky ~ the castle sits vain and the glorious evening continues uninterrupted ... 
they will all melt soon 


you knew i would come... that i cum for you ~ inspiration of the undying
your voice// crying ~ to me ... worship this lonely girl ... watch her body of a woman \\
