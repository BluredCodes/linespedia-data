(skzm, sz-)

dignity tastes like the salts from your sweaty palms

dig them in sweet child... o come to me

constancy

grounding out our heresy ... bring it

with the shelter in the company of Christ 

rejected

resurrected with the holy waters of his mighty thrust

insert words of wet

bleed for me tender sanguinary

meat of faith

schism baptize exorcism

masochism of religion comforts breeds of  these

worshipped sins

discipline through our hopeless worth

(skzm, sz-)written for Hecate vs Lustmord -Heresy Ressurected
