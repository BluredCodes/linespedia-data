Caught in the rising storm sinking hours that tilt along precision
blades to give up hyped sands creating stained glass windows
                                             over their well carved eyes

Her lips held wrapped around the emerald idleness while
Bio Chemicals filled her lungs and stuck life into a stance
that liquefied her skull filleting a slide that would support no spine

Under time over the hook engaged to a neck cradling a fading face 
peeled then chipped away like concrete walls over looking the joining 
                   of the only thing on his mind... inseparable... Admiration

   steel plates withdraw the textures of flesh tones and split the layers
extracting themselves ]so very slowly[  and never has anything ever 
undressed with whispers before chanting patience of creation and 
                                                                                Removing time

Van Della Mia drew geometric names over the circle of her heart /fractures
             and launched it to the crossing of entrancing death spells
 
  Dying mistrusted that she wouldn't distance herself from the embedding
friction of passion molding into the rusted Armour becoming her own shield
