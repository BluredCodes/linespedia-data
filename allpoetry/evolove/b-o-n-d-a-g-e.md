::






clasp the addiction smoky filled curves with noize to kill 

sacrificing your senses of guilt never to know as we 

exploit the bodies in this dark feeling along moving against two 

wild scents meet and my hips get felt up by the gripping palms 

of my own flesh just as your meat presses into the softness 

of my sweat and we remain lost paralyzed in the moment

suffocating by the fragrance of our destroying lust lost in passion









no rules 
no boundaries






::
