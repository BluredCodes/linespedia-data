*


The evening glared at them like a 
watercolor painting with yellow moons 
mingling in the werewolf blackened clouds ...

Emerald hues kissed the sky 
and the purple made pink seem 
somber generously offering to blend ...

Warrior sat their humble and silent 
on a rock that lifted him to inhale her chest ...

He was able to see this heartbeat through Famishes
 slightly tinted gown that offered flesh as a dream ...

The gates stood tall before them 
and the ravens landed to greet this walking ...

Warrior spread demonic wings in a sermonic love cry 
and held there like a statue for her to admire ...

Famish held her breath and focused on hooded visions 
lightly whispering, "is he the one" ...

Ebony hair covering along trailing her back 
and eyes match this with her morsel that beats backwards 
in time ...

He held his lonely arms together and cupped her soul 
so Famish would see how close that she could be held ...

With a tear in her eye ... she said good-bye ... 
and proceeded through the bars that closed Warrior behind ...


~their love is forbidden~
