And God was born ...
he fell from the ground to the sky ... 
the loneliness of perfection engulfed his cries ...


The brightness of the nights threw back to him his own reflection 


... for he was still alone ... 

and this "perfection" ...
burned for affection ...

And so Angels were born ... 
and the heavens rose from south to north ...
escorting only the strongest wings forth ...


The brightness of the heavens soon reeked with strength of war 


... and the heavens he tore ...

Lucifer achieved what 
God had never conceived

and this "perfection" ...
was swallowed by infection ...

... for he wished he was still alone ...

And so I was born ...
lifted from my upside down existence ...
and Father showed my eyes from a distance ...


The blackness from the heavens that appeared to be from Johovah's failed creation 


... and it fed into my heart much temptation ...

To extinct the "mutation" ...
of this creation ...


ABOMINATION
