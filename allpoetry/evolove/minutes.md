.



tornado brewing madness empire into a song of lost ones leading the way

then metal grinding to horses slaughtered telling of the wars and chaos that they have fought.... 

skill~saw cuts metal to bone to lives among outer dreams

c   o  n    t   i      n   u      e

destination
destiny

sawing the blades~

electronic lab hooks electric pulses over catatonic mumbles

skipping stones
into a memory nightmare...

end.

pleasure
________
