title: "Tangerine and the Headless Boar"

Bats number the sky in thousands swelling dusk into 30 hours 

Mortician lines his bodies with salts of his ancestors
lonely tree of family burns to the ground when the bark absorbs acid rain

Clouds form to make manifestations of the molten suicide they watch 

They change for us to keep the leaches of the swamp from eating themselves
they fold in a way unthinkable just to eat away when nothing else is left

Put your finger to your mouth and chew to have more
never satisfied

Boar of a missing head charges into a cave formed into rock
not yet opened ~ but he knows his head is inside and splatters
his body to the minerals

meant to be

the Tangerine snake eats pork one foot at a time
drags the body to the lake where arms wont find him

The burned forest takes the head she stole and crowns herself 

she wraps Tangerine against a ripped abdomen and
the snake absorbs her acid Reign

reptile blends to zebra and the Stripes of tiger on her back 
feasts on the leaches that fold to eat themselves
