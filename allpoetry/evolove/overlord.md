spit to make wet faster faster

legsover yourshoulders

long hauls and then it cums

more then i could describe to you

even this metal spike guiding past unholy wars

our earth to be only what we want of it

monsters sail under our seas

sent up to pull them back below 

an echo lifts 

all in fear of this wrath

want me instead 

and the first animal is dead

pumping in consent and lustful agony 

protect me from this shielded passion

voice me over the edge
