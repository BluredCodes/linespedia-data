~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~`

mother 

~

im sorry for being so repulsive to your affections~

i just said what i thought would mean a lot more then lust~

maybe i wasnt clear ...

that i love you~


but its too late now~

its always too late



Friend

~

we fight everyday~

we cant understand the rancid in the sweetness that covers

our calls

to reach each other~


we claim to be the ones who will see all

but still are blind to our own needs



Love
~

here me say that i didnt mean this~

for loving so much ~ when all i could do is hate

hate for everything that is in my reach

kill this feeling of love~

you save me but cant hold on to everything

with a thousand miles of distance 

set a drift~ to absorb the swelling dusk

i will become

torture~ to gush for you 

but i will try~

to do it slowly~
