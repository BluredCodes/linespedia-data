Caged in a Splintered Cell




I close my eyes as they rapidly search for the light through the skin they are kept under ... my heart pounds as the engines control the metal that runs my mind over and over again ... endorphins ... drive me to brake the glass into the machine that will save my cell... 


I see my family running ... running away from me ... into my arms and then away ... Unwell ... 



Fires sweep inside the building my sister lays her head down ... raping her ... fires sweep ... leaving my sister and taking my friend ... again and again ... the reoccurrence is sucking out my life... 


whats left ... sabotage ... climbing fences that only get further and future away ... the day breaks 


and pours shadows of pitiful hope within feeble attempts ... splinters cover me like clothing ... coats of nature slowly demolishing cages ... cage of madness ... slightly bent and unable to speak ... my mother comes over just and they come through us ... disappear like they were never hear ... hear wailing fear.... calling us over ... offering her hand 


into this escape plan ... become this fan and spin above your self ... then beneath ... where you 


are constant ... constantly in cycles of self withering ... never to get away...
