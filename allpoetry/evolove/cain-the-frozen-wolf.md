ambers : mortality distant

only a moon can call

truly deafening~ in all scapes

scraping stone

lay fetal in all positions

poor : crying 

smooth spying
~night and day~



                play inside the trees

         hallow breeze      } lifted

leather from your prisoners

for you
jagged walking

pierced
with traction

                  all 

on the 

                  floor 

of this 

                  room

coyote milk ~ silk petals

leaves in an animation
cycle

24~per sec
                   ond


Cain called  out

but was heard frozen
