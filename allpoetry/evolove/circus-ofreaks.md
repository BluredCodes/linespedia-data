ladies and gents:


welcome to the freaks of nature ~

                here we have ... a man with two heads


supposedly he couldnt help this affect and cause from the womb

but we will bash his horrible worthless face in~


APPLAUSE~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~





THANK YOU~



next over here ... we have a child who likes to suck dick~ 

notice her lips form the circular oval of her daddys cock~


she seems to love it with the tears in her eyes... 

they no longer even fall~


ROAR OF THE LIONS~~~~~~

forming AN OVALIC 

APPLAUSE~~~~





~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~THANK YOU


thats not it~




look at them~~~~~    the ugly misused unproportion gluttonists

cellulite rots their meat

eat another refridgerator for your insecurities... They 

cant have feelings under all that fat~~~


they must not ever come out again....

cage the animals~~~~ 

we are the judges or misfortune


the freaks of nature that must be put to rest~


Devil Woman~~~~~~


comes out with her slither~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~``


a SNAKE OF AN ENTRANCE~~~

 the crowd begs to punish her~~~~~~~~~~

rape the Devil Woman~~~~                     send her back to hell

    she will steal our children in the night



burn her at the stakes~~~~~ make no mistakes


she must die for us~~~~~




APPLAUSE~~~


                         we are perfection~


~we are the ones that delivered the baby with two heads
~the ones that snuck into her room and forced her to love you ... too much
~the ones that want to self mutilate until we cant be seen anymore
~and the ones that wish to kill: all that can destroy us and ultimately give us life...
