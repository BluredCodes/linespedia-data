electro
     mag netic wave

conflict mis behave

porno pulses
             open slave


zero to none

your the only one

     when we become


motion~make me your ocean

swim deeper
your my keeper

of unbetraying meat

burn me liquid of fire
spit for me~ even higher

reach the star *~that you are

bright
            in a dark universe of 

-light

black hole 
                     burning as coal
