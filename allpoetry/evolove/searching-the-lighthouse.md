slam the skins down 
drink from lives renown

coming up coming up
ceramic lava fills the cup

hiccup before losing beads
your neck from shackles 
finally freed

listening to them hit|~|hard thumping
pounding deeper|~|to get humping

spoil the rotten
soon forgotten
unforgettable

thats what you wish

whitefish with no fins
gills to breathe
            no way to swim

follow and search the:
lighthouse
madhouse

whorehouse
slaughterhouse

meet them here
for the marriage 
of your spouse

suicide bride in 
blade to cover

trapped in this glass box
waiting for her lover
