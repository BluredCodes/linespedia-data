before:500B.C.E

The Underworld.(Hades)

mortals kneel before the cold heart
somber justice and no one will escape 

land of the beloved sanguine 
eats the heart of the grim goddess

Athene's shield carried at its center the head of Medusa
make us love you with the gift of the olive tree

we will honor your grace with a city for your taking
keep Odysseus and serve as his guide and protector

mortal man of long suffering travel
meet your wife and child through the horse that will slay all

and help serve the one who watches over
Assume the shape of men... women... and children

you will be joined

Her love and protection will lift the long suffering hero 
and he will be reunited with fateZeus saw that Metis was pregnant and he was afraid 
that the child...
if allowed to live... would be his doom 

So he swallowed Metis to prevent the birth...
It didn’t work... Athene/a\rose fully armed and armored
from the head of Zeus but Metis was never seen again
