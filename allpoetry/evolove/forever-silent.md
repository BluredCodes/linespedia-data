}silent{


I lost my confusion  
in the mortal delusion

that I was to have you tonight
under this desolate light

to mesh with you again 
with no penetrating end

of the abandoned sky
the missing rain to cry

even beyond truth ecapes lies
and ignorance, wisdom denies

I want to dance to your song
and escape all thats wrong

about loving you still...
... even after I'm gone ...

{forever silent}
