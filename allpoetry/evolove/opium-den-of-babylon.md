~~


shameless vice
  draining the clouds
     one tear at a time


~~~~


tear through lighting 
     so close from missing\
your beauty

remain ugly as ever~


call her name of Greek cities
  wars taught 


      appear caught


fought     for more life


decreased        held the cruel hands


fade into        deteriorate


abandoned 
     bass


heartlessrace

place your rings

each one over my neck


crowned by a king ruling regret


take out your gun
   run it against          alovedone


and blow the back of her face into the chamber


barrel of powder

running to light


looseness of morals
wicked slut will
eat your very 
last line


bleed from purity

Opium Den of Babylon


hand rendered viciously

demoniacal


~~~
"the trail of the serpent is over them all"
