Necropsy 

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Into the dark I masterbate~ ... 
yearning for more meat to mutilate~ ...

Come lay your body upon me ... and offer your love to casterate~ ...

Desire to watch your open wound fall ... upon the blazing ebony to dialate~ ...

~oXXXo

Just you and I alone in this room ... nothing else matters tonight~ ...

Deadly edge pierces my flesh ... to make these sins take flight~ ...

Deminish into this bloody night~ ...
trapped your eyes within my sight~ ...
