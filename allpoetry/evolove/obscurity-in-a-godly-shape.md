Never a chance to breathe beneath the tiles sealed below ... protect us 
destroy us ... contraction as if your mother delivered you through holes fed upon 
screeching calls of the father who died in the hands of his creation 
Creator ... 


Erection in his hand timid he falls above the cries .. pleas for more than one moment left 
servant to the blistered minion blowing leaves dead brittle in the distant landing 


Obscurity in a Godly shape 


Like sirens attempting to blast through hearing penetrating with vibrating drills 
repeating in formation as it blinds vision ... sound in motion ... devastating commotion ... 
flesh flips inside out to reveal inner meanings beyond reality ... sublime in focus 


Slow ....... 


Arrange ......... 


Tilted to the point of falling ....... 



Another servant to your conquering hate ... creature of sustenance fails to create ... 
pyramid crashing into the mounds of radiation ... Obscurity 
in

                        Godly 
                                     shape
