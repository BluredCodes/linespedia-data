~

He was supposed to ruin the world 
with the punishment of his fathers wrists

four walls 

compressed

corners with no room for air

keep him contained

another breath- from his fractal sewer 
spitting{
volcanic urine soaked feces 
and{
he would of been flushed

the movement of bowels drown in this spill 
of him{

father used to watch as he lost his innocence
to table scraps

"finish your meal or Ill insert it into your _empty hole_
how much more could you take for me
you will bring back what I cant take for myself"

You will be a Hungarian King

make something of your tilted corpse while the clock remains
to decapitate virgins

fill their "_empty holes_"

~}teach your father to love you on the outside filling inwards
towards the manifestation of quiet alleys into 
homicidal hotels

trash-can to bathe in 
wheels to push yourself into remorse for no conception

scour perfection yelling internal blisters to break
when you accept to leak

if you could reminisce another capsule to soak your sickness
~powder 
would still spill to liquid to explode inside your heart

~~broken soul ... but he is loved eternally~
