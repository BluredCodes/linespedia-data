~~ ~~~~ ~ ~   ~  ~  ~ ~  ~  ~~~~



grass burnt over an edge with curves that meditate


cypress will drink of earth ~ and earth will drink of rainfall

leaves ~ never let go to the hands they are made to believe

In ~ with the stems turning to quilts warming the blossoms cooling

in pollination flight ~ enduring escapades and the soil plants more life

More Life then African Violets~  call the almighty Oaks possessing the miracle

of thought ~ chant under the season spell of nocturnal spirits ~ bewitching falls

boulders climb to jump into ~ puddles of never ending rivers of wicked water streams
