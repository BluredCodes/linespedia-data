~
~
~
you cannot strengthen the lines 
   with fear

 ~thumped
break away~ 
 ~restriction

conviction~ becomes the matter of addiction through confliction

(-dkshn)


~devotion; inclination

when nothing is the same 

~your visions haunt in pain

hold on to the very last thing you remember

~compulsive chronic need
for more then

all the time that isnt now

wishing later was the past
hoping that the future will descend

back into~

the moments where we felt no doubts

only torture for you~

can you hold on to the only thing that seems to matter

//once it dies inside of your flesh

\\once it embeds itself into your arms of spikes

can you keep it safe always~ never letting her 
rot on the inside while seeming alive 
detouring death from your eyes

im so tired...~ sleep and you die
they infest me like waters 
of the shallow lake

nightmares loving my dreams~
my truth awakens me siik
and freezing ... and sweating 
thick

smelling the place~
hearing them scream for me to come
back to them~

for me to come back so true

split in two
half my life i gave to you

holding on to his skin screaming for my abandoned//\\faith to listen
stay with me for eternity

while i run to him~
cover me with your hands to my face
and steal my breath~

~
~
