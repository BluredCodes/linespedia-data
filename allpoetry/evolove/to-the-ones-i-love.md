title: "to the ones i love"

screw your corpse into my bones like a wrap around rope carrying you through my death as you appear before me for which i have become 

for which i extend

to the ones i love


i seem to drown only in the shallowest of waters as they somehow mislead me to believe that its an ocean needing to taste me deeper }savoring all my misfortunes hostile currents

for which i extend 

to the ones i love

running to this recoil of nightmares that so ever wants me back }wants me to remember our sex and the filthy things that we used to do \break my back for pleasures bleeding aroma of nothing more then wanting to be

just wanting to be

with the ones i love* to the ones i love *  ~BloodlosS~
