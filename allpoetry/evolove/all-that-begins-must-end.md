All 
    that 
         begins 
                 must 
                        end















Fatally Exposed ...  observing the girl in the woman
delicate fragrance ... from tears of yesterday

father ... your strength will forever show me the truth of ...
pain in pleasure, fears in lost hope, no tolerance for broken hearts ...

Remembering that forgotten day when you ... hammered mine apart ...

mother ... your distance will forever show the me torment of ...
being ugly, screaming silently, words that kill ...

The unanswered grief for your affection ... is what I'm longing for still ...

sister ... your lonliness has forever showed me the delicate softening of...
brutal blood, misunderstanding, forceful sadness ...

You are the only sanity I have left ... through all of my madness 









~no more tears of things i cannot change~00000000, *somewhere in the past maybe still dragging along side of me oXXXo
