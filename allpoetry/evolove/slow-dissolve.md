Slow Dissolve


Slow Dissolve 


betrayed 
once 
again 
inertia 
from 
this 
reoccurring 
dream 
jagged 
blade 
of 
friendship 
rips 
the 
exterior 
of 
this 
thinly 
exposed 
layer 

confiding in you ... was all I had left in this isolation 

but when I froze for a moment in a twist of shame 

my 
heart 
forgot 
for 
an 
eternity 
how 
to 
beat 
drowning 
itself 
in 
the 
sweet 
decay 
through 
it's 
own 
reign 



of tender blood 

... Twisted words ... 
... lost friendships .......................... 
... Trying to stay together while drifting beyond the plain..... 

so 
steadily 
losing 
the 
passion 
of 
being 
a 
family 

rewind 
fast 
forward 
the 
memories 

... Showing like a catastrophe ... 

I 
cant 
ever 
forget 
this 
second 
in 
distant 
space 

... For Im afraid if the sands of time have me dance by this grave ... 

He 
will 
wrap 
me 
in 
his 
arms 
again ... 

Slow distance ... 

is 

all 

but 

Slow ...
