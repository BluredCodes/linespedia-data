room filled with the breath from heat rising multiplying inside closed doors
roar of the bodies over each other with no end only a beginning 
disciplining panting yells for God to hear you whimper
Oh Yes Oh Yes Oh Yes
Bless me with this Holiest of 
Holes filled with Worship
Me until Blasphemy

is it all over ~my cake
opaque glaze ~drips deeper
keeper of ~exposed secret~s
secret~e ~ for me ~pleasure doll
