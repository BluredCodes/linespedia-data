~~~~regurgitate~~~~~


red dots breaking away at the green pattern 

                            circles around my mind in constant format


  trailing the cycle of confusion
    
                        Delusions of lust with inverted emotions


      Commotion that makes me scream from sickness

                                         I am your witness

        that you are no longer with us


    I am your voice that will enter you through

               the beginning and rise with the end


                  Mend me in the open wound and 

               scab me into your pours


         Whore to the world with a 

            virgin entrance 


       and the assault that fills my 

                      mouth 

              Will somehow save me...
