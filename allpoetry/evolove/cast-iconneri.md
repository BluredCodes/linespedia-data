i connor i





rectangle of blackness 

                                        strips   a head to sit as the window to this missing~


chin slims at this angle hidden from                        the cast iron palm



one finger      extends over face     centerline        while the other two       search skull


thumb shape of an arrow 
                               becomes  tool of carving details

                               run into backbone    so vertebras become your silver


put on safety pin    glasses and close the end

                               your head made of spikes                stuck to the wall inside of a frame


with  screwdriver eyes      body of circuit board                           your flesh gets peeled



lick her with Dick shaped heads                ~have her sucking hoses for life



                          he was part of her hips              stuck fetal in ovaries




he wears goggles made of flesh and she is the straps //that  become these parts\\



buckle around prongs~ hooked inside of pins ...           

all that    he is                           she          offeres him
