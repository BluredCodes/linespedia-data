~



convulsion of your tired meat

fortified ... nourishable 

beckon your quest for sustenance

hunger for a mouth no more

as you wrap your pretty face

around the dying animal

stabbed in the heart for you

come on and eat your way through

another emotion limitless in your 

capture~


~enrapture

escape nocturnal 

as you soak up the sun

drink from the night

lose all that you know

escape on the run


remind us of this
~                ~
Spasm

        Revolution



~
