---
title: "If I Fall For You?"
writer: "EveJustWantedToKnow"
slug: "if-i-fall-for-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

i'm sitting on this fence,
swaying back and forth

you stare up at me
eyes hopeful
waiting for me to fall

but will you catch me?
i wonder

i turn to face you
your arms stretched wide,
calling my name

the wind combs my hair
as i debate your offer

but will you catch me?
i ask as i slip

will you catch me if i fall for you?
