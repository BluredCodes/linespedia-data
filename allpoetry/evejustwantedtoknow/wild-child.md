as her aunts had 
always seemed to say
"well she is a bit of a wild child"
with a disapproving frown
whenever trouble came her way

sun times 
she climbed trees,
ran through the wet grass
and danced with the wind

moon times 
she wished on stars,
touched the heavens
and dreamt her dreams

dawn times
in the rosy glow of first light 
she'd stretch up to the sky 
reaching for all of a brand new day 
filled with rainbows and fantasies

these days
that little girl 
is too busy with
math homework and soccer games 
but her spirit still
dances with the wind
