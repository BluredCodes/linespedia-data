---
title: "Growing Up"
writer: "EveJustWantedToKnow"

I never said
that I'm an adult,
only that
I'm not a baby.

I never said
that I'm a mature person,
only that I'm trying,
but I can't if you hold me back.

I'm not sure
if I can stand on my own,
but I'll never know
if you don't let go.

If you love me,
love me for who I am;
give me sometime alone
to find out who that is.

Let me soar.
Set me free.
Allow me to live.
I only want to be me.this one is to my parents.
