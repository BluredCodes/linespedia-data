---
title: "Please Don't Tell Me"
writer: "EveJustWantedToKnow"
slug: "please-dont-tell-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/36976-Please-Dont-Tell-Me-by-EveJustWantedToKnow"
---

don't tell me to get up
i can't move

don't tell me that everything will be ok
how the hell do you know?

don't tell me you understand
trust me you don't

don't tell me to take it one day at a time
do you know how long a day can be?

don't tell me to try
i died trying
