---
title: "Close My Eyes"
writer: "EveJustWantedToKnow"
slug: "close-my-eyes"
source: "AllPoetry"

looking inside,
searching

finding nothingness,
emptiness,
voids filled
with fear and pain

I shut my eyes
but still I see
the wounds
that won't heal,
the scars
that will not fade

looking inside,
I turn away
and close my eyes...
