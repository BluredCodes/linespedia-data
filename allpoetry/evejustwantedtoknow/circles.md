driven from this place
I search again for who I am
dashing down a road to nowhere
I smash against another dead end
breathlessly I turn
and run in endless circles
bringing me back to this wall
where I know I don't belong
sharp pains in my ribs
from going so long without rest
I stumble onto the cold cement
blood drenches my hair
there I lay waiting for my release
