---
title: "Rainy Days"
writer: "Schitzo"

On a rainy day in May,
I found the one for me.
On a rainy day in December,
You start to slip away.

I wish I could do something about it,
But you have made up your mind.
It is the day which will be lived in infamy,
Not just for Pearl Harbor......But for my loss.

I still beleve you are to much to lose,
But I think I have lost you.
I still beleve I love you,
You still have so much of my heart.

You question if you love me,
I can see why,
Who am I to love? 
I am just fifteen.

I wish I could take it back,
When I say "you're my life",
I know you do not want to hear it,
I wish I could take it back.

I look back,
We were so HAPPY!
We knew we loved each other,
And I still love you.

These tears falling from my eyes,
Show the pain in my heart.
I can not stop thinking about you,
I love you so much.

You will always have a special place in my heart.
I will always have love in my heart, for you.
I really hope you will come back to me,
I know you will realize what I mean to you.

I know I will see you again,
But I thought I would never say "Goodbye".
I wish you would let me show,
My love for you.

I just wish I could hold you one more time,
I know I love you.
We were ment for each other.
Our love was stronger than everything in the world.
            
                Goodbye my baby. I love you.
                                            Schitzo
