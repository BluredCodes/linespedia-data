No speaking,
   or thinking
just quiet
   all to my-self

Though still running,
   diving,
into my mind.

I can't think about,
   Well, anything,
I just don't feel like it.

Sky!  
   Wooo.....   The sky
how blue
   funky cloudy.

Chills,
   From the wind,
run down my spine,
   To no-whereI wrote this at lunch while i was talking to nothing and thinking about nothing.
