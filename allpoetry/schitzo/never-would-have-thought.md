title: "Never would have thought"
writer: "Schitzo"

denocement,
confusion,
sweet soft smells,
of your beautiful body.

As we lay,
here on this comfertable couch.
You let me hold you
like no other.

No regrets,
we both are overwelmed with joy for what happned.

I have always thought anything was possible,
with you,
but I never believed 
it would happen.
