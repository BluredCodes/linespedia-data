---
title: "When I Feel This Way"
writer: "Schitzo"
slug: "when-i-feel-this-way"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/23374-When-I-Feel-This-Way-by-Schitzo"
---

When I feel this way,
I can just hit the floor.
I do not know what I am feeling,
Because I am so confused.
I wish I knew what my heart wanted,
But it is for the best that I don’t.

When I feel this way,
I do not know what I want.
I know I want my love back,
But am sure that you are my love?
You both care for me,
But which one more?

When I see you around,
I know that you love me.
When I see you alone,
I need to comfort you.  
When I feel this way,
I know that I love you.

Everything gets to me,
It just piles up inside me.
Everyone that tries to reassure me,
They just end up hurting me even more.
If I could find some closure,
 My mind will relax.

When I feel this way,
No one knows that I am dejected.
I don’t really know what is wrong with me,
Except for I don’t have you anymore.
If you just knew how much I care for you,
You would look at me in that way you used to.

When you look at me in that way,
I am on top of the world.
I know you know that,
That you made me happy.
I wish that you would see that,
I actually care what happens to you.

Your heart is what comforts me,
When I am down.
Your love is what keeps me warm,
In the cold wintry weather.
I just wish you would hold me again,
To keep me warm so I am not so cold.

I know you see the pain,
I am in.
You still see the love in my eyes,
And it still keeps you warm.
I still look you in the eyes,
I know you see my love.

I know you miss the way:
I looked at you,
In that special way.
I know you miss the way:
I held you in my arms,
With pure love in my heart.
