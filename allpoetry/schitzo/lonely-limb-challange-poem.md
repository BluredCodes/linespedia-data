Lonely limb, 
why won't you just fall off, 
and save yourself the embarassment of hanging on by a little piece of hope of living. 
Why did the lightning strike such a beautiful limb, 
so alive and well. 
Just fall off, 
and just live on the ground 
with all us other broken limbs.
