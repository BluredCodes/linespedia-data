---
title: "Siting Here"
writer: "Schitzo"
slug: "siting-here"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/22571-Siting-Here-by-Schitzo"
---

I sit here alone,
thinking of you.
I don't know whether to love or hurt.
My heart has pain,
I don't understand my mind.
I will love you until the day I die.

The love of the world has been deprived,
taken away for now.
I wish you would see,
the pain in my eyes,
the cold blueness in my eyes,
that have shattered for the last time.

There is no pain like losing the one you love.
There is so much pain in my heart, from losing you.
I know I might be pathetic,
but I never wanted to lose you.
I wish you will realize,
how much I love you.
