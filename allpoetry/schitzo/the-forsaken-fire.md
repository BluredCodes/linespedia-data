title: "The Forsaken Fire"

It goes up the scale,
And then back down.
Somebody please get me out of this hell.

What are you going to do?
Wait until
You actually have a clue?

I see you at every daybreak,
And you couldn’t possibly appear any more contented-
Even if it is just fake.

I need you to tell me what you desire,
And I really hope it is I
The forsaken fire.I wrote this in a style that i made up.
