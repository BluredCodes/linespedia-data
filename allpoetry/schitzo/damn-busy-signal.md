---
title: "Damn busy signal"
writer: "Schitzo"
slug: "damn-busy-signal"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/45575-Damn-busy-signal-by-Schitzo"

beep beep, and beeping again,
it's driving me nuts.
just get off the damn phone.
i need your help
to get me through this tough time
that you don't know that i am having.
