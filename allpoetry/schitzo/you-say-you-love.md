You say that you love me,
than you go off and leave me for someone you don't know.
Why do you not care for me anymore?
Because I shure as hell will always care for you.

I have loved you this long,
And I will never give up on you.
I love you,
Love is way more better that lust.
thats all you feel for him is lust,
............RIGHT.
You haven't even known him for a week,
and you choose him over me.

Obvesly me alown isn't good enough for you,
because you also want to be with other people,
and you know I can't live with that,
And you know why,
and you are the only person that understands that reason.

I just want to speek to you,
and tell you "I love you",
and you actually listen,
so you can hear how my heart really feels,
Because I really love you.
