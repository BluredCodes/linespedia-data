---
title: "Because,... Why?"
writer: "Schitzo"
slug: "because-why"

---

I can't protect you,
because you won't let me.
I can't hold you,
Why won't you let me?

I know you feel bad,
about what happend.
You keep it all jumbled inside,
because you don't want to show your pain.

Do you even have pain?
because I really do.
Do you even have eyes?
Because I wish you would look at me with them.

I know you,
used to love me.
But what can change that in a month?
I am still wondering that.

Because, has reasons,
Because, is not an answer,
Because, is what I feel,
Because, I love you.
