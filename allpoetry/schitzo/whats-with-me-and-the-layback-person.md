---
title: "What's with me and the layback person?"
writer: "Schitzo"
slug: "whats-with-me-and-the-layback-person"

url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/44286-Whats-with-me-and-the-layback-person--by-Schitzo"
---

I don't know
what all i do to deserve this.
I am always the lay back person,
and it is starting to get to me.

Why me I am not the greatest looker,
why me?
I don't know why i feel like this,
obviously i am never good enough.

Do i always fuck things up for myself,
or is it just who i choose?
All i can say is-
Why me?
