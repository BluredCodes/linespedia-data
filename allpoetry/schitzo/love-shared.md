---
title: "Love Shared"
writer: "Schitzo"
slug: "love-shared"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/20072-Love-Shared-by-Schitzo"
---

With this tear rolling down my face,
I think about our future together.
We have so much love,
in our small hearts.
We have made it through the thick and thin,
just to find us here, in love.
It is the most beautiful thing,
to be in love with you.

And now we are waiting for a day to pass,
so we can hold each other, in our arms.
Love shared with warmth,
while in the cold wintry weather.

I see you across the street,
waiting for me.
I try to scream for you,
but I am so far away.

I walk to you.
There is a tear frozen to your cheek.
I ask 'What's Wrong?'
you don't answer.

Your brother walks to you,
gives you a kiss,
and he says 'I'm so sorry I know you loved him.'

She always said the unexpected can happen,
and I still love her.
She would be here in my arms, with love shared.
If I was ALIVE!
