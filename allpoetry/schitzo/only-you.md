as I see your face in 
the desolate candle light, I suddenly 
realize how much you mean to me.

You look at me in a very special
way that can never change, or be deprived 
in any way.

Live is something I have 
for you and only you, Love is 
only shared in my heart by you.

If I ever lost you 
I don't know what I would do,
I know I would be faithful to you, only you.

You are my love, In this life 
and the next, I truly believe you 
are my soul-mate, and my lover.

Every breath I take, It
makes me love you even more,
and lightens my life even further.

As green grass grows slowly
in the parries my love grows 
stronger for you.

My love will always be growing
for you, you are just so perfect
