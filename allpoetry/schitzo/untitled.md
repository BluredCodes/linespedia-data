Your words cut through me like blades
but i try not to think about the bad times

I still think about what I could have done differently
on a nightly basis

I know i still love you 
and it is just a mask i hide behind saying that i don't care

You are still my Rose
I am still your Schitzo

You are still my baby
that will always have a hold on me
