Hey, It's me,
The lost love of your heart.
I never thought I would say this,
but I don't think you truely love me, anymore.

I just always wanted to be thre for support,
but hell, that just drove you away.

How come, once everything starts looking up,
something I instigate brings me down?

In your mind,
it is all for the better.
In my mind,
everything is going to hell.

Why when I think you love me,
I am just a friend?
Why when I met you,
you truely loved me?

Everything I say to you,
you never hear, you just hear jieikeoen
You hear nothing.
Why is that?

You know my love will always be there,
anyknow yours won't.
You know I will always have a place in my heart, for you
and it will always be opend,
..................Just incase!
