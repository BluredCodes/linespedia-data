With rain drops hitting the ground,
It was going to be a long night.
Lightning striking trees around,
All children have fright.

With casualties on the rise, 
The tornado drops from the sky.
All the sinners confess their lies,
And soon start to die.

The tornado starts across the land.
You can see the fear in her eye.
When you look to the sky, you see the hand,
That just may make your child cry.

When you see your child cry,
It may be all over,
And, You want to die.
Then its all over!
