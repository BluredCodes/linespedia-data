---
title: "Happiness won't exist"
writer: "Schitzo"
slug: "happiness-wont-exist"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/38493-Happiness-wont-exist-by-Schitzo"
---

Thoughts of losing you run through my mind,
as I sit here thinking about how miserable life can be.

Life may go on,
but happiness doesn't exist without loving you.
I can think about the happy times,
but they make me miss you even more.

Why is death so prominent?
Why is it a part of every-day life?

I haven't lost you,
but one of these days you will be taken away from me.
You will always be there for me,
You will be my gardian angel.

You say "Everything happends for a reason"
I say "Happiness doesn't exist without you"

You never know,
in the end, everything might turn out for the best.
Thank god upstairs,
That I still have you today.

You are my love of my soul,
And happiness doesn't exist without your beautiful face.
