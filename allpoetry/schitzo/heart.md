---
title: "Heart"
writer: "Schitzo"
slug: "heart"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/19370-Heart-by-Schitzo"
---

You do not know, how much love is going through my heart,
Ever since the first kiss, I have not even thought to part.
My heart aches, when you are in pain.
Nothing can stop that fear that came.

On rainy days, all I want to do is shield you. 
I can protect you from anything, even what goes through my heart.
All my protection comes from your heart.
You would not let anything happen to me, even when were apart.
But we are never really apart,
Because we are always in each others heart.
