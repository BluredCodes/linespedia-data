title: "My haver"

My haver,
You are always there for me,
You care like no other.

My companion,
You are my friend,
And I care what happens to you.

My friend,
I am closer to you,
Than I have been to anyone in my life.

Age is just a number,
Why does a number matter?
It doesn’t in my mind.

Feelings are mutual,
You feel the same about me,
You couldn’t possibly care more than you do.

My friend, haver, companion,
I am there for you,
You can tell me anything.

 I am your friend, your companion, your lover,
You can trust me,
Because I will always care.A haver is a hebrew word that means: friend or companion.
