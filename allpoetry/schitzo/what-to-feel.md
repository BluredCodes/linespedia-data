title: "What to Feel"

What to feel,
I don’t know.
Does anyone know what I want?
No, only I know,
I want you.

You make me on top of the world,
When you hold me in your arms.
Do you know why?
Yes, because I love you with all my heart,
And then some.

The words "I don’t know",
Hang over me like a endless void.
Why don’t you know?
I know you love me,
And I love you.

So what do I need to feel?
I know,
I need to trust you,
Until the end of time.
And feel love for you until I die.
