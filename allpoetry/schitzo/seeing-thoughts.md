I'm ok when I don't see you,
because you are not there.
I go throgh hell when I see you,
because I can't hold you.

You act like there isn't a void between us,
but you won't let me get close.
Everytime I do,
you just move away.

I hope you realize
the pain I feel.
I hope you know
I love you.

I see you
in my dreams.
I hold you 
in my thoughts.

I just want you to know,
That i will always love you.this was one of the first poems i ever wrote and mabye one person knows who it is about, and i'll never tell.
