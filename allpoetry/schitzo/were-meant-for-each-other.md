I know I'm in love,
-nothing can stop that.
Kismet is what we are,
-meant for each other.
I'm sorry,
-for what I can be.
Our future in the inevitable,
-we're meant for each other.

It's funny how:
-I was always looking for someone perfect, before you
/And you we're right under my nose.
-I was being stupid, ignorant,
/you we're being strong, faithful.
-I was weak, ill-minded,

WHAT WAS WRONG WITH ME?
I should have realized you were for me earlier,
-But I have you now, in my arms
You're everything I've ever wanted, needed.
Your love couldn't be stronger.
I love you with all my heart,
-I couldn't possibly think about losing you.
We're meant for each other.

We are invincible, and immortal,
-when we're together.
We are strong , and impatient,
-when we're apart.
Our concerns fore each other are great,
-we don't want to lose each other.

You're all my interests, my thoughts.
You're all my love, my life.
