---
title: "Talking to the Wind"
writer: "Schitzo"
slug: "talking-to-the-wind"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/42897-Talking-to-the-Wind-by-Schitzo"
---

Why can’t you just blow me away,
Into her arms?
She seems so far away,
But she is just sitting over there.

Feelings of kismet,
Run through my mind.
All this was set up,
Just to help me realize that she really is the one for me. 

I have known her for years,
But I am just now, realizing what we have.
