---
title: "Demented Mind"
writer: "Schitzo"
slug: "demented-mind"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/19369-Demented-Mind-by-Schitzo"

It's slowly creeping death everywhere.
A mocking bird sings to the silence.
A beautiful face walks in,
it's my love.
You my love, 
don't belong here,
Its not good enough for you. 
Please save yourself, 
before i corrupt you.
