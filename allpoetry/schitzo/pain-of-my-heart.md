---
title: "Pain of my heart"
writer: "Schitzo"

The pain of my heart,is like the pain of my mind.I love you, with all my heart,but I can't hold you.The crazy pain,is your mind.The great pain,is my heart.You walk off like I don't exist,like I am invisible.I watch your every step.You don't even acknowledge my step.I don't exist,not even in your state of mind.I know I love you,that is all that matters.I hope you still love me,I really do.I love the way you look at me,I just wish you would.I know you still love me,but I still have the PAIN OF MY HEART.Schitzo
