You're my sunshine when it rains,
You're my moonlight at night.
You help me through my hard times, 
I try to help you. 
At times I don't think I'm good enough for you,
But you always love me and that's all I ask.
Your love is strong,
you never doubt,
thank you, your all I've ever needed.
I always love you,
No matter what happends.
I never doubt your love,
I hope I'm good enough for you. 
I never doubt my love for you.
You're perfect'
I'm glad you love me.
We're perfect,
I know your glad I love you.
We can make it through anything.
Our love is stronger than everything in the world.
We're perfect for each other.How is it overall, Its my first to summit.
