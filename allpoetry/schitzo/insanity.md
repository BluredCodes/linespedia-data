---
title: "Insanity"
writer: "Schitzo"
slug: "insanity"

Why does everything stack up and melt down
all over my heart.
Things like this don't bother me,
but silence is never a virtue.
Hell is what I put my-self through.

Eversince you said the word,
       "NEVER"
My heart was filled with love-
only for you.

I might be easy to hold my heart-
on my sleeve,
but honestly I try not to.
I just try to find someone,
who cares as much about me as I do about them.

Noone understands that when,
I say "I love"
It's something I truely feel,

I sit here on your couch
trying to gather all my thoughts,
but I don't even know what I am thinking.

I may be insane,
but insanity is what makes me, ...me
No one understands me!
Me Insane?,......Well that is me being my-self!
