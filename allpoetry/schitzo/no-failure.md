---
title: "No Failure"
writer: "Schitzo"
slug: "no-failure"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/36855-No-Failure-by-Schitzo"
---

If a book is a window,
what am I, a fucking piece of transparent paper?
But, no body truely understands me.
You see how I am, but don't understand.
You can sit there and look in my eyes,
and see straight through my soul.

But Helping is who I am,
And caring is what I am about.
If you can be honest with yourself,
Then I have done what I came to do.

Surviving is a game,
that one day everyone will lose.
But If you lose today,
Than I have failed you.


...........And that cannot happen
