---
title: "Good Things, ...Bad Things"
writer: "Schitzo"
slug: "good-things-bad-things"
source: "AllPoetry"

Love is such a good thing,
That is why it hurts so much when you lose it.
It changes from a good thing to a bad thing,
Because it seems to be useless,
But I will never give up.
