---
title: "Stepping Out"
writer: "Kenneth M."
slug: "stepping-out"
source: "AllPoetry"

Much afraid of the sound, the eyes draw closer and in them the years
The years have swallowed it whole, the eyes are now the only beauty that's left
A pot of cold coffee sits dormant as it has for several hours
Mindless chatters coming from the television fills the silences that she fears

And in those eyes is the pain of yesterday and the doubt of tomorrow
Affixed to the glow of a computer screen, an electronic world seems much more appetizing than that which created it
And the anger sets in, but nothing can be done but to light up another cigarette
So she walks outside and takes a drag or two, she loves this time of year

The cold strikes her gently on her soft pale skin
It all seems refreshing to her, the near autumn breezes
The orange colored sky stretching over the world
And all over the world she knows that there must be others like her
Perhaps even looking at the same twilight stars, dreaming these dreams

She steps out this evening feeling more alive and awake then she has felt all year
She steps out into a world with those sounds only she can hear
