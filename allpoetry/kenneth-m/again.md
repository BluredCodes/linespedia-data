---
title: "Again"
writer: "Kenneth M."
slug: "again"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Hear the whispers in the garden of my lifeI shed my inhibitions and thoughts, their not good enough tonightI want to see their faces in the pools of dream timeTheir voices I can hear, I hear them, only now they screamLet my tears fall away on quaking ground that I walkSo I can see through the starry mist and be one with my lovedThen like awaking in a cold sweat, from a dream that I swore I once knewYou walk into my most pulsating chamber and breath life into my mouthYou are the double sword, the one that I despairBut then again you are my reason, the beautiful candle in this ugly lightAnd you were the one who called to me, in twilight's eyesI could see your shadow hanging above my bed, yet you told me it was all rightLet the show end now, we'll write the final act of our own deceitLet me be free, to be innocent and give me back the limb that was cut from meHeal me and let me be no longer a cripple, a hideous maimed corpseGive me back what I have been searching for all along, what has been the source of my griefGive me back, the chance to dream, so my mind can runLike a wild and untamed beast free to roam the landscape of the universe again with you
