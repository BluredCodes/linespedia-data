Tears From The Sky
(For Ariel Osborne 
May She RIP)

Would you know me if you saw me again?
Would you show me the path of life?
I must be strong
I must go on
Mother Nature took your life
Father Time still ticks on mine

Time will break you down
Time can crush your heart
Have you asking why
Asking Why Oh Why
Lord only knows Why
There are Tears From The SkyI wrote this for my friend Ariel who was killed in 1999 in a car wreck
