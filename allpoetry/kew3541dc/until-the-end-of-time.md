Until The End Of Time 
(For America)

Perhaps we failed to realize
Only love can conquer hate
Life is a privledge
Never take it for granted
Love each other
Until the end of time

Sorrows for the dead
Stories untold
Love ones lost
Hatred unfold
Its clear it will always be the same
Until the end of time

Who can I trust in this cold world?
I need You to guide me
Until the end of time
Remember me
A civilian
One who took a stand
Remember me
Until the end of timeThis is for America and my American people everywhere
