Tears From The Sky

Verse 1
Would you know me?
If you saw me again
Would your smile be the same?
If I saw you again
I must be strong
I have to go on

Chorus
Cause I know
I cant be where you are
And I know why 
Tears flow from the sky

Verse 2
Would you show me the path of life?
If you saw me again
Would your life be the same?
If we saw each other again
I must learn 
Life goes on

*Chorus repeat*

Verse 3

Time will break you down
Time will take its time
Time can crush your heart
Have you asking why
Asking Why O Why
There are Tears in the Sky
