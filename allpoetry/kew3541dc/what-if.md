400 years of injusticeYet we still going strongSo many died for the causeToo many hurt by the effectWhat if this happened to you?And you lived in fear and cried constant tearsWhat would you do?What would you say?What if the tables were turned?What if it was you that got burned?What would you say?What would you do?What if we spreaded lies about you and you knew they weren't true?What would you do?What if?What if?How could you do us this way?How can you live with yourselves?We wont take itWe will fight youWe don't have to take no more
