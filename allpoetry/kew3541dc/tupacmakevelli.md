Tupac/Makevelli

He looks at you with a gleam in his eye,
Mostly known as a thug,
But a gentleman in my eyes
His juicy lips open to say the words "thug life"
His voice is deep and sexy
He is the love of my life
More noble and brave than knights of Camelot
This beautiful black male knows the true meaning of survival
He has been through alot
His many faces have been shared with us all
