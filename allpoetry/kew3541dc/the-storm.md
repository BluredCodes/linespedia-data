Have you ever felt lightening hit your heart?
Like it was tearing it apart
Indicating it has no intention to stop
I wish that I could wear the storm
Your life is soon to be torn
A silent storm is taking your life
You are patient taking it all in strife
This storm is more deadly than poison
And you the victim its chosen
