One chance I ask
To see the real you
Is that too much of a task?
I only wanted one chance I do

I only wanted one chance to see you laughing
Is that too much to ask?
Who knew you were only acting
That you only wore a mask

I only wanted to be some kind of friend
I would never ask for more
I would treasure that till the end
For my heart has closed that door
