Young Girl/Heartache

Please young girl dont cry
What he did was wrong 
I cannot deny
You are strong
Dont let him break you down
I am sorry he made you frown
You are beautiful regardless
Dont let him take your pride
I will be by your side
I have been through this
See I know you will survive
So please young girl
I cannot bare to see you cry
