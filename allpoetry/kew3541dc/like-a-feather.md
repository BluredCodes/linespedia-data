Like A Feather

I always knew this day would come
I would see you once more
Thinking I would let you back in the door
You would ask me if I still care
If I would be there
Like a feather floating in the air
I knew you would come back here
Wanting a second chance
Wanting me to take you back
But you have had all the chance
I am going to give you
