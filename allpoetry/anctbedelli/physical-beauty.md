PHYSICAL BEAUTY:When I look into the bloom of a flowera despondency is reflected back,as if it is awareof the briefness of timealloted to beautiful things.This depth of soulcan only be seen from a distance,because,the role played by beautyblinds the worshipful viewer,who is drawn to its brillianceas a moth to a flame.Within this blooma young virgin stands,naked,with beauteous splendoreager to burst forth,to steal the breath of the ordinary,in that fleeting flash of glorythat is physical beauty.        By Anctbedelli
