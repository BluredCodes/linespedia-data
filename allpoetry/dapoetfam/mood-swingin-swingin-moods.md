title: "Mood Swingin, Swingin Moods"

What is it that makes me swing
Closer
     Then away
I'm swingin in moods to you 
Swingin in moods away from you
     I know I love you 
It's just that I'm 
Mood Swingin
     Am I trying to find the right spot
Close 
     Or away
     Away or 
Close
I love you 
     Love you but I'm mad at you
So am I really mood swingin, swingin moods
     I still love you 
Close
     Or away
I'll always love you
Sometimes I think I love you more
Cuz I realize how much you put up with
And I realize how much you love me 
And I love you 
     Thank you for loving me 
And I'll work on my Mood Swingin
     Swingin Moods
Cuz I love you and I wanna be 
Close to you, Lovin You
