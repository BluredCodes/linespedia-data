---
title: "I Get Stronger"
writer: "DaPoetFam"
slug: "i-get-stronger"
source: "AllPoetry"

I hunger for your negative energy
   feed me with your hate
Make me stronger with your pessimism
   That only makes me great
You see your aversion for me 
   only tests the limits of my 
   womanhood
Every feeling of your dislike
   Makes me stronger
Every time you cut your eyes
   I get stronger
Every feeling of detest
   Makes me stronger
You walking with your nose in the sky...
   I get stronger
Did you understand that 
your stupidity makes me better
you don't phase me for the worst
like i said 
   I get better
Ha Ha Ha Ha Ha 
   you make me laugh
   your sorry sass
   your'e full of trash
continually you make me laugh
continue to feed me 
and make me more wise
I like for you to hate me cause 
I can still rise
