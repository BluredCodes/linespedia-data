Friends

Sometimes friends can be hard to find
Not knowing who will be true and kind
It is often difficult to keep them
But, sometimes your lucky and find a gem
With the world moving at such a fast pace
Most people act  like their running a race
When you find a friend, let them know
Try your best to allow the friendship to grow
A good friend will laugh and cry with you
Attempting to please and always stay true
Some friends will stay, and some will go
True friends will always let you know

5-4-01
