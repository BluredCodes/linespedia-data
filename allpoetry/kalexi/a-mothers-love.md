A Mothers Love


My love for you is like the sun
Shining brightly for you to have fun
I love to see you smile
It really makes me feel worth while
My love for you grows everyday
Stronger in every single way
You will always hold a special place
In my heart
That has been there from the very start
I feel so proud to be your mother
Only you can make me feel like no other
