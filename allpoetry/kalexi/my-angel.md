My Angel 


I have searched high and low 
Yet, still I dont know which way to go 
I have looked left and right 
Never willing to give up the fight 
Everyday I feel your presence 
In the purest form of essence 
I feel you in rays of sunshine 
Knowing that your truly mine 
Seeing you in drops of new rain 
Trying to wash away my daily pain 
Praying with all my heart 
That you and I will never part.
