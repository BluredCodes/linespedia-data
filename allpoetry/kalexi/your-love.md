Your Love

I cannot explain the way you make me feel
Always making me smile
Feeling like I could run a mile
It is obvious for everyone to see
What your love has done for me
Always feeling brand new
I never knew until I met you
My favorite pleasure is your touch
making me want you ever so much
Your love reaches up to the sky
Allowing my wings to fly
Your love is so special to me
I hope you will see
How blessed I truly feel
All of my dreams seem so real
For your love I know
I only beginning to grow
