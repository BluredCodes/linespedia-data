Lucky


Some people dont know how lucky they are,
Able to reach their shining star.
Some are born with it,
Others search for luck all the time,
Never hearing any bells chime.
Lucky ones never give up the fight,
All of their days are always bright.
Hopefully I have what it takes,
To find some luck before I wake.


3-29-01
