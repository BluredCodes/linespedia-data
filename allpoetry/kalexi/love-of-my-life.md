Love of my life


We became friends first, and when I first met you,
I knew there was something special about you.
You gave me a feeling of contentment, that I never knew.
Allowing all of my impossible dreams to come true.

When you hold me, I come to life, glowing so bright,
Taking me on a never-ending flight.
In your eyes I can see our happiness growing stronger each day,
Our future is so promising, nothing will stand in our way.

I feel so lucky to have you in my life,
So proud and honored to be your wife.
I just thought you would like to know
That you are truly the love of my life.
