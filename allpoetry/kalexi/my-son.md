My  Son


Precious eyes of blue
So fresh and true
Constantly wanting to play
Each and every day
You always make my day
In your own special way
Your growing so fast
But I want this time to last
You have a very special soul
That makes me feel truly whole
Time is quickly ticking away
Making me love you more everyday
I hope you truly see
How much your love means to me
Your the best thing thats happened to me
Without you where would I be

6-21-98
