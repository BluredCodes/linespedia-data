Hidden Beauty


When you look in a mirror
What do you see?
Someone who is satisfied with life,
And confident with their ability
To handle everyday strife.

Some only see the imperfections,
That others dont know exist.
Some see the lines on their face,
hoping others wont notice as
They attempt to hide their disgrace.

People say beauty is in the eye of the beholder,
This is a saying I truly believe.
Getting to  know someone from the inside,
Is the greatest reasons
To see the real beauty they cannot hide.

8-22-01
