When I Found You


There was no more feeling blue,
Everything seems like brand new.
You worked your way into my heart,
Right from the very start.

When I found you, I became whole,
You were the missing piece, awakening my soul.
I hope you know how happy you make me feel,
Sometimes it seems so unreal.

Now I know my life is complete,
Only you can make my heart skip a beat.
I'm excited to be sharing my life with you,
Cherishing the day we said I do.
