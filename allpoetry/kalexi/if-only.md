If only

I wish I could take a hike,
Or even ride a bike.
If only I could do these things,
It would make my heart sing.
Since I have this disease,
I no longer walk with ease.
If only I could play chase with my son,
We would have so much fun.
Please dont feel sorry for me,
It could be worse, dont you see?
