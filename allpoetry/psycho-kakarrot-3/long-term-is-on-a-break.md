Looking for a female to hang with, not likely.

A relationship thats not just about sex, not happening.

Girls that erouse me mentally, haven't found one yet.

Someone to get close with, nowhere in sight.

Well I was hoping it wouldn't come to this, but I have no choice.

No more of this sentimental rubbish, love and cherishment.

Sex and lust is what it's about now, who cares about feelings...

Wait thats not me. What am I doing, I can't do this.
