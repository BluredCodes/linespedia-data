---
title: "The Choosen One"
writer: "Psycho Kakarrot 3"
slug: "the-choosen-one"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

As I walk through nothingness, I see and hear a being.
A troubled and tormented soul, who needs some special healing.
Am I the one with the power, we'll have to wait and see.
It'll take all of my innerstrength, to set this spirit free.
As I take their hand and begin the process, I witness their experience.
Death and destruction within a lifetime, now they seek deliverance.
But what has caused so much pain, that they must hide alone.
I guess I'll have to find out, and they're in their safe zone.
As I search within myself, I find my inner chi.
It's strong, powerful and energetic, as if it were Bruce Lee.
I understand it will take my all, the mind, body and soul.
But what I do will help this spirit, and that’s the ultimate goal.Are you the choosen one?
