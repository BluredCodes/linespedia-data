---
title: "Natural Beauty"
writer: "Psycho Kakarrot 3"
slug: "natural-beauty"

Take off all the make-up,
Lose every piece of jewelry,
Never paint your nails,
Don't think about perms,
Or using fake hair,
'Cause to me it's all about...
The Natural Beauty.

Try not to over-exercise yourself,
Surgery shouldn't enter your mind,
Forget about those minor blemishes,
Don't worry about your height or weight,
Your voice doesn't matter to me,
'Cause to me it's all about...
The Natural Beauty.Do you agree with me, Ladies?
