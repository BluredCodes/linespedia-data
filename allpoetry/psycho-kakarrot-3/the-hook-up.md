Oh, excuse me miss, didn't mean to scare you.
In all honesty, it's the last thing I would do.
But if by anychance it's okay, I'd like to sit and talk.
Get to know you better, maybe hold hands and walk.
Please don't run away, there's no need for that.
I'm the same as you, except you’re more phat.
It wouldn't hurt to converse, meeting eye to eye.
Talk to long hours, then kiss each other goodbye.
I hope we can be close, maybe more than friends.
Holding each other tightly, until the very end.
Thats all I have to tell, but I leave you this one tip.
I see you as a beautiful woman, not just a pear of lips.Wut do u think? Be honest.
