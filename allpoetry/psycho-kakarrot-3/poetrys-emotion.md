Oh, man. It's been a long time since you last wrote a poem.
What's going on, where've you been all this time?
Please, don't tell me your still on this "I'm a bad-ass" routine.
Man, come off it!  When are you going to understand?
Believe it or not, your body is made of Flesh and Blood.
Okay, you question where you come from, and you’re not sure your human,
But human or not, you still have a heart, and it is full of emotion.
This isn't healthy, you shouldn't be doing this to yourself.
Don't you know it's not safe to hold everything back!
How long has it been since the last time you were pissed, 7 or 8 years?
All of that could come out when you really get mad, what then, Huh?
You've never cried, you've never even teared, what are you, and Android?
When are you going to learn to open up, drop your restraints and BREATH.
It's okay to be strong, but it's also not a sin to cleanse your heart and soul.
It's in your power to do it, no one is stopping you and you know it.
So do yourself a favor, set your emotions free.
Everything, let it go, just let it all go.Same as always
