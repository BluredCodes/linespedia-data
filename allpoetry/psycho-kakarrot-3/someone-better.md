What the hell do you mean "I deserve someone better".
Come to think of it, ranks as a Dear John letter.
What your doing now is what others have done before.
Shit, quit downing yourself, you are not a whore.
Jesus, why do some girls do this, you find the perfect guy.
Then you say "He doesn't deserve a slut, I have to say goodbye."
All men aren't perfect, you should know this from the start.
But if I'm that special, you should keep that in your heart.
Oh, okay, I know why you won't stay.
You figure I'll be successful in the future, and then you'll be in the way.
Someday I'll be an athlete, artist, or actor,
and you believe what’s stopping me is you the "Negative Factor".
But what do you say after the talk, you ask the question like everyone else.
"Please don't forget about me", but your really forgetting about yourself.
Come the fuck on girl, of course I'm gonna forget about you.
If you knew I was gonna blow up, you would've stuck to me like glue.
But you know what, that’s okay, because I deserve someone better.
Someone who doesn't blast their faults, and likes to stay together.Wut do u think?
