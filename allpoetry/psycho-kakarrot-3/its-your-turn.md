---
title: "It's Your Turn"
writer: "Psycho Kakarrot 3"
slug: "its-your-turn"

I've wasted my time, I've wasted my breath,
I've wasted my energy, now it's your turn.

I'm tired of looking, I'm tired of walking,
I'm tired of talking, now it's your turn.

No longer will I allow myself to always have to approach.
My time has come, I need a break.  You all must now confront.

No more intros, no more questions, no more compliments, they all stop now.
It's the new millenium, old fashioned is gone. There's nothing wrong with asking.

I'm done being the initiator, so from now on...
It's your turn.How many of u have reached this level?
