---
title: "It's the End"
writer: "Psycho Kakarrot 3"
slug: "its-the-end"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/21098-Its-the-End-by-Psycho-Kakarrot-3"

How dare you kill this relationship stood for, 
That type of action is rotten than the core.
For once in a lifetime things have finally worked out well,
You continue to fuck it up, and my brain continues to swell.
So many things are racing through my mind,
and now I know where to go.
You think I'm going to marry you, the answer is Umm, NO!
What a careless attitude, how could you let this happen,
If you truly cared about me, you would have taken affirmative action.
I have nothing further to say to you, this relationship is at its final halt.
But before we part, I just want you to remember,
YOU DID THIS TO US, YOU DID IT, IT's YOUR FAULT!This ever happen to you?
