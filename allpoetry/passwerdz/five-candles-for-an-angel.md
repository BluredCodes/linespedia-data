5 candles for an Angel
----------------------

five 
such an age 
big dreams 
high hopes 
time to grow and change 

five 
can it be 
almost here 
so close and still 
so far away from me 

five 
I was ready 
to see you fly 
to see you happy 
but...not to die 

five 
a big guy now 
I guess i'll never know. 
what five wouldve looked like 
what things you would have known 

5 
the snow is falling 
a blanket on the ground 
traces of you fading 
memories abound 

5 
a magic number 
not a baby now a boy 
forever 3, forever gone 
5...you'll never be. 

5 
lines of poetry 
five candles burning still 
16 months to understand 
I doubt I ever will.I raised a little boy for almost 4 years, he was my foster child and my nephew. He was murdered 14 days after leaving our house, by his new 'family". No one can ever say he wasnt "mine"...I think I am the only one who remembers...
