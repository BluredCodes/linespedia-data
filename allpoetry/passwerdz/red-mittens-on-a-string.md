Alex
If you came back now
would you see the new tile in the kitchen
the new furniture
your bed thats missing?
I have put away your little clothes
red mittens on a string (tears came at those)

Would you see that we have changed some things
 but mostly we're the same
 would you recognize us right away
 do you hear me call your name? 

this is supposed to be a holiday
 friends and family thanks to give
 but for me it is a reminder 
of the one who doesnt live 

of you, who never got to see the new couch or the floor
 I wish that we could stay the same
 and have you back once more 

Jesse had a birthday hes five now you're still three
 I guess you will a toddler stay an angel you will be 

I want to say Im sorry to hold you one more time
 I cannot change the things that are done
 I cant go back to when you were mine 

But I wanted you to know that even though
 things will probably change
 that I will always have a spot in my heart
 that bears your name.december 00, 3 months after the death of my son, I cant explain it any better than that...
