Best Friendlike rain falling downgently tracing pathways on the windows of my heartyou have touched me and left your gentle markon my soul.when thunder came you stayedfierce windsdidn't chase you awayseparately, we were fragilelike willows bending in the furytill you reached out and wrapped your arms aroundmy soulyour spirit became a part of meand your courage gave me strengthsoul sisters standing strongno winds powerful enough to rip us apartno fury as sturdy as our loveWe take turns being each others rockyou were mineI will be yourswhen I tell you always, you answer foreverand I KNOWthat you are the onewho is my mirror my soulmy spiritmy best friend.    ~Michellesummer 2001, I wrote this for my best friend...and was promptly laughed at for being 'queer'....interpretations please?
