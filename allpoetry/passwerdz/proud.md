Born again
into my country
red white and blue
with pride
Born again
into the faith
that God 
stands by my side
Born again
in my belief
that we are strong
that we will fight
Born again
into the world
of honor and duty
a sense of right
Born again in vivid color
standing tall
standing true
I ask the world
to not forget
the red white and the blue.
The twin towers
they have crumbled
the dust settles
all around
we watch the planes fly overhead
and pray they reach the ground
united not just for today
patriotism abounds.
I am born again into my world
American, and proud.
