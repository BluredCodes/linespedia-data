Touched down in New York City
she knows that I still care
she wants to see me after work
I'll just tell her where

I'm here early, in my car
half a tank of gas
butterflies deep inside
waiting for time to pass

Up the steps after all these years
feelings coming through
hold my breath as I ring the bell
a smile that's overdue

she took my hand and looked at me
her heart was her guide
told me all about her world
invited me inside

so much to say two bottles of wine
like a girl I'd hardly known
so much closer than before
we both had really grown

won't you fly away with me
won't you give it a try
can't stand another long plane ride
without you by my side
