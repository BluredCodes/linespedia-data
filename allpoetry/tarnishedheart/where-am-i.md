Standing

    Alone in some kind of 
sleeping
 circus
where I pretend to be someone else

 And I wonder
around
          Stepping on voices
 whispering sounds
that made my skin crawl
   And I look up
 to see the face of my past
 
the face that brought me here
 i 
  n 
   t
    o into
this nightmare of being awake
 and I realize

 I am in my HeAd
my screwed up reality
 that i CANNOT ACCEPT!!!
                     and I cannot get out
I am hear alone with these
 happy tears 
and patronizing thoughts
     and I will run
but
only 
into 
my dreams
where more nightmares lay.
