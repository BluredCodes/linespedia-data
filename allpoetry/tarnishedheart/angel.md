It's like a dreamsomething wonderful that takes your breath awayto be with youis like being witha beautiful angeland i never want to wake upYou have opened my eyes to the worldtaken me places i neverexpected to goand in my thousand sleepless nightsit was never you i thought i would loveSo from here on outi'm making a promise to you and all the worldi'm not going to mess this upI'm not going to loose the angelwho gave me my wingsand taught me how to fly.please feel free to let me know what you think
