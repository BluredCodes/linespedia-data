okay this has needed to be said for a long time, I am kind of upset a little because it seems like my poems are not getting read, and I know alot of other people here feel the same way.. I feel like I have to beg to get them read and then there are some people that read them and do not leave a comment. 
 At least say something, I always request critical comments so I mean you could even tell me how much the poem stunk, just leave something. I just feel like this is supposed to be an interactive site and there is not much interacting going on. And I think it is sad when people have to use crazy title to get peoples attention for them to read their poems
 Anyway, thanks for taking the time.
