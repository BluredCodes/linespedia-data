I found your wings today 
I saw them laying barren and soiled 
in a corner of my mind 
I had once forgotten. 

I followed the trail of feathers 
and I saw a lost soul 
placed helpless on the ground, 
you were there, but your back was turned. 

I cried out to you, 
I told you to look at what you've forgotten, 
told you to look into his eyes, 
their your eyes too. 

I then realized why I found your wings, 
they had fallen off 
because of your thoughtlessness, 
proving you were no ones angel. 

I tried to talk to you 
but as the words came out of my mouth 
I saw you getting smaller 
and smaller 
until you were as helpless as your son, 
and as you began to cry 
I could only look on in disgust 
with the fulfillment of knowing 
that it was time for you 
to truly grow up.
