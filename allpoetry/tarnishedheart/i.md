I am in misery when i am alone. I am in heaven when i hear my son laugh. I am in control of my thoughts. I am in hell when i cry. I am a mother, a child, a sister. I long for a sense of belonging. I need to feel wanted. I want to feel needed. I feel alone sometimes. I sometime feel a sense of loneliness. I feel i have been forgotten. I am what you are not.
