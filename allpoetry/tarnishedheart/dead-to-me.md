---
title: "Dead To Me"
writer: "tarnishedheart"

You made me
question my sanity
you made me believe
I was so much
less than you
and I could never
be more than a simple
person
I believed you
when you said
that you loved me
and would be there
for me and my son
your son
I believed you
when you said
you loved me
I told you
that you were my angel
and it was fate
and all of that
now clouds my memory
because I am trying
to look through a viel
of pure hate
for you
and everything
you ever stood for
I believed you
all the time
and that is why
you are dead to me!This may seem cruel and I do not want anyone to think of me as a bad person, but the guy I am talking about here is my sons father who has not seen him since the day he was born. I have a very good reason for feeling this way, he beat his 2 ex-wives and 2 out of three kids that he has, and I will never let him do that to my son, he is an evil man.
