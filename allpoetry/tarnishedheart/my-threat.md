title: "My Threat"

You can still go about your everyday life as if nothings changed, as if there is not a little boy just waiting to ask where his daddy is.
 You still smile, joke, laugh, but I guarentee, if it takes until the day I die, I will make sure you never have a reason to smile again.
 You have stolen a part of my sons heart that he can never get back, and that is his right to know who his dad is.
 But that's okay, just remember, each time you feel joy, each time you feel the urge to smile, I will be right behind you, ready to tap on your shoulder, so you can turn around and look into my eyes and feel the fear dwell up inside you.
 It's my wish that you never feel joy again because you don't deserve it!
                This is my threat.
