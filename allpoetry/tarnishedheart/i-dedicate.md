I dedicate my heart to you,as you mature, as you grow, know that I will always be there for you, I will be the love you need when you scrape your knee or when you first learn to crawl and you stumble, I will be there to help you up.
 I dedicate my soul to you, as your growing inside me and you hear my voice, always know that is the voice that will calm every bad dream and chase away every monster under your bed.
 I dedicate my life to you, even before you know what words mean, I want you to know every time you see my mouth move, every time you see me smile, know that it is for you, everything I do from here on out will be to make you into the man I know you can be!
