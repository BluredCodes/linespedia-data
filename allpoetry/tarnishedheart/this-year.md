This year I will love you, not because of what you can do for me,but for what we can do for eachother.
This year I will remember to call my freinds back when they ask me to.
This year I will start teaching my son to say please and thank you.
This year I will be to work everyday on time.
This year I will respact my parents more and remember to appreciate how much they have done for me.
This year I will love myself more and realize that is the only way to get you to love me completly.
This year I save more money.
This year I will try harder to make myself happy.
This year I will enjoy life.
