title: "I Can't Make You"
writer: "tarnishedheart"

I can't make you understand
all this pain I have inside
there aren't enough words
to describe the toment

To be thrown into a world
never before seen
and to be there alone
is indescribely painful

to have tears as you best freind
and night your only escape
to reach adulthood
before it's time

you will never understand
the hurt of knowing
what you want will never be
the finallity of that

knowing I will never see
my angel truly fly
and that his sun
will never shine bright enough

I can't make you...
