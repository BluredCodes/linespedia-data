---
title: "Picasso's Moon"
writer: "tarnishedheart"
slug: "picassos-moon"

We will find as we dance
under his moon so bright
that everything once of color
will soon turn black and white.

 Under this same moon
will will march for our rights
we will dance for our freedom
we will continue to fight.

 A world once so full of wars
will finally find peace
and souls so full of hate
will finally find release.
 
 Picasso's moon will shine
into every soldiers heart
and soon they will not know
the feeling of being apart.

 With this new freedom
we will find a new peace
we will learn to love again
together in harmony.
