title: "The Fire That Melts My Heart"

You know that you hold the love that could make me whole, you've had it from the begining, and I allow it.
You hold the rainbows in my sky and the light in my darkness.
You have the fire that melts my heart.
You hold the butterflies in my stomach when we're close and the tears that I cry when we are not.
You hold the beautiful images inside my head.
You hold the stars and the moons in my world.
You hold the fire that melts my heart.
You hold the gleam in my eyes, you hold my heart in your hand.
You hold the decisions I make and the responsibility for them, you hold my hand just when I need it.
You have the fire that melts my heart.
