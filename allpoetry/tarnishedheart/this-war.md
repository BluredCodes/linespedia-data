We never said goodbye like we should have
We didn't spend the time together like we could have
Why didn't we laugh more
because you're gone now
gone fighting this war.

A million miles away
yet under the same moon
and all that time spent is gone
gone way to soon.
tell me, please tell me you still remember
because I still dream of you.

I just want to hear your voice one more time
to feel your arms around me one last time
why didn't we smile more
because your my hero
a hero fighting this war.
