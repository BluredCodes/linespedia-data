Why is it that we all see things in our everyday lives that don't make much sence, things that are out of place, but it seems like we close our eyes to it.
 We all know about the whole presidential election was supposed to be rigged because of what happened in Florida, but yet a couple months went by and you heard nothing else about it.
 And what about the whole "did we land on the moon theory". I mean if you honestly think about it the whole concept is questionable, but you never here anyone talking about it.
 What about the C.I.A.? What are they hiding from us? We all hear about thses secret government agencies, but what are they keeping secret? Is there really a cure for aids and do they really have aliens hidden?
 And I have to wonder about why they haven't caught bin laden yet, I mean we have the highest technologies and yet we still are searching for this man who has been seen and even caught on camera dozens of times.
 And lets not forget what was seen in the smoke of the world trade towers, it was on 20/20 and there are many websites on it, yes folks, it was the devil himself in that smoke, and it was investigated and they are not doctored pictures.
 So why is that with all the stuff that we see everyday no one has yet to say anything about it, are we all just scared, are we being brainwashed, or do we all just close our eyes?okay ppl, I went with a different approach here, let me know what you think though.
