title: "Mom"
writer: "tarnishedheart"

In the begining you kept me under your wings, sheltered me from the harms of the world, told me if I believe I can do anything.
 You kept secrets from me as I tried to keep them from you, but in the end both of us would find out the truth.
 Throughout these times, through the good and the bad, you never once let go of me, you showed me a loving mother, no matter what, I will always have.
 Now it's came time for you to lift your wings, but not completly, I'm never to far away from you and in your heart is where i'll always be.
 It's my turn now to face the world on my own, but I want you to know, no matter what, wherever you are will always be home sweet home.
