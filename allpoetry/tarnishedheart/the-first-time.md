I can still remember the first time
I laid my eyes upon you
I can remember the way that my soul felt
a feeling so brand new.

I rember the first time we talked
inside my car for hours
we explored or deepest thoughts
to my words you did not coward.

I remember our first night
when you held me so close
that's where the magic began
a magic unknowing to most.

I remember our first argument
and the feeling of pain i felt
then I remember our first make up
and how tight to my heart you held.

I remember the first time I heard I love you
come out of your mouth so sweet
and I could not answer you right away
because that moment I could not beat.

And I look foward to remembering
all of the things to come
like hearing the words "i do"
or seeing a new daughter or son.
 
I just want you to remember 
if nothing else, one thing
remember you are my soul mate
and the love only I can bring.
