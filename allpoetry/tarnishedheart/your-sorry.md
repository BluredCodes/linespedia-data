Tell me your sorry.Tell me you realize how stupid you've been while you were so absorbed in your make up fairy-tell world. Tell me how bad you feel for causing me to almost go over the edge of my sanity time and time again. Tell me you realize now how bad your son will need you and how much you regret denying him.Then it will be my turn. My turn to laugh in your face and turn my nose up at your tears. My turn to lie to you and build you up only to let you fall. My turn to watch you beg for just one chance.My turn to let you think you'll get one. So go ahead,say your sorry.
