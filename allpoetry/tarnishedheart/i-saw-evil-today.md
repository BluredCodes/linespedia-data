---
title: "I Saw Evil Today"
writer: "tarnishedheart"

I saw that redness that enveloped you
going down the street you were there
right in front of me
I saw the evil that you carry so well
and yes, I did try to hide
but as we turned you switched your lane
and I slowed, trying not to pass you
but I did
and I swear the moment that I 
looked into your eyes
I felt a hate hell wouldn't even know
and I felt like screaming
"LOOK AT YOU LIVING YOUR LIFE
WHILE YOUR SON IS AT HOME
YOUR SON THAT WILL NEVER EVER 
SEE YOUR EVIL FACE!
THAT SWEET BOY WHO DID NOTHING TO
LIVE HIS LIFE WITHOUT A FATHER
I WISH THAT YOU COULD LOOK INTO HIS EYES
AND FEEL THE LOVE INSIDE OF YOU
LIKE i FEEL EVERYDAY
BUT YOU WILL NEVER FEEL THAT WAY"
But instead I looked away.
