Clear your mind for a momenttell meare you capable of feelingloverespecthonestyhonestly?look at this little boya beautiful creationfrom Godis your mind still cleared?now thinkhow can a manwhat youclaim to benot feel anythingfor this beautiful boy?you liedyou said you'd be therethe only thingi haveis tearstears don't buy clothes,diaperstoys.So what is itcan you give me an honest answeror will i be forced to hear your lies again?Is it still clear?there are children involvedand you are neglecting themyou are thier fatherare you proud of that?Just clear your mindand think of all thatyou've destroyedand right nowif your happyenjoy itjust enjoy itno threats no promisesjust truthno happinessno smilesjust tears.still clear?remember all the pain you caused your 'freinds'remember?i hope sohope you took notestime for you to play that rolejust truthjust tears.
