Where is your heart?
Does it still soer through the midnight sky
does it float with the carelessness once so obvious?
Is it still locked to the love so perfect
or does the purple still have a hold on it?
If I was to knock now would you let that door be open
or will I be left standing out in the cold?
I have floated through maroon colored clouds
for so long
just to have a constant aching soothed.
And for awhile,
I almost forgot
but it's all back now.
So take me under your wing once again
so we can fly once again
because you know
without even hearing it come out of my mouth
you know 
you always will.
