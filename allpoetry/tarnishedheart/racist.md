Crosses burning
children learning
to hate others
of differnt color

Why do you hide
i thought you had pride
you wear your mask
to hide the facts

You have a sickness
ask for forgivness
we are Gods children
all made from him.i wrote this simply because i come from a very mixed family. i am white myself, my son is bi-racial, my dad is jewish, my-step dad is black and my brothers and sisters are all mixed, i think that it is sick the way some people think
