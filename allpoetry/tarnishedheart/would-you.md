---
title: "Would You"
writer: "tarnishedheart"
slug: "would-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

If the sun stopped shining who would you run to
would it be me?
If you could pick one person to spend the rest of your life with
who would it be?
If you could truly love only one person
would you love me?
If the world was ending who would you protect
would you choose me?
If you could stop loving
would you stop loving me?
