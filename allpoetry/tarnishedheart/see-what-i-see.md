title: "See What I See"
writer: "tarnishedheart"

How can an agel make me cry, why can't he see the way I feel? He should see I'm falling into an endless oblivion
for him.
 How can an angel not see forever, what it means to me? The warmth is my reason to keep going
for him.
 How can an angel not grant my wish, to live forever flying beside him?
The sky will be our limit, an endless eternity
together.
 So angel of mine, you've always wiped my tears. And as long as we continue
to fly.
 Remember i'm always beside you. I will continnue this journey if it takes an eternity to put us together, I'll be there on that day
finally.
