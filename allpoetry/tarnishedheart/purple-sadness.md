~Written to the beat of purple rain~

 I never meant for us to be together
never meant to cause you all this pain
 I only wanted to be rid of all this madness
only wanted to get out of all this purple sadness
 
 Purple sadness 
purple sadness
 Purple sadness
purple sadness
~only wanted to get out of all this purple sadness~

 I never wanted to be some partime freind
I only wanted to be your girl.
 Why should I have to impress you
A shame we've ended up like this.
 
 Purple sadness
purple sadness
 Purple sadness
purple sadness
~only wanted to get out of all this purple sadness~

 Oh baby I know I know we've grown closer
I know every time I touch you
 You feel it too
You say you want forever
 But you can't decide
I think you need to come
 let me take you
Out of this purple madness

 Purple madness
purple madness
 Purple madness 
purple madness
~only wanted to get out of all this purple madness~Anyone who loves Prince as much as I do will appreciate this poem!!!!
