Every day that I look at you I'm reminded of my future, because I know now that's what you are. You taught me to listen to the beutiful music of love if only for chosen seconds at a time, but I've always listened. And in time those precious seconds will turn into days full of magical symphonic memories, and we will share the joy together, making music other lost loves can dance to. The sounds of violins that come out of your voice when I need to hear it the most is beutiful, proving you created, if only in my world, the music of love.
