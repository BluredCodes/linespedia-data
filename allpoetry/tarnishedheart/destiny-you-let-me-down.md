---
title: "Destiny, you let me down"
writer: "tarnishedheart"
slug: "destiny-you-let-me-down"
source: "AllPoetry"

Destiny, to love you was a journey through a road I never thought I'd travel, you made me a better person and helped me survive. That's why I guess right now I could cry, I could feel my lifes over, but all I can say is destiny, you let me down.
 Destiny, I've questioned you for so long, I've tried to figure you out again and again, but all I saw was what words cannot say, and it hurt, so that's why I say, destiny, you let me down.
 Destiny, you always came first, and not once were you not on my mind, I gave up everything for you, even myself. I climbed that mountain and when I reached the top, I realized there was no one there to help me down, and all I kept hearing was destiny, you let me down.
 Destiny, you can say what you will, but I've always known the truth, and even if I have nothing to prove it with, you know too! I can no longer go on like this and hopefully the life your living will someday catch with you, until then, I know, destiny, you let me down.#3
