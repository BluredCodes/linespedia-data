You are my angel
 Without you my heart would no longer have the will to beat, without your smile, I'd no longer have the will to be.
 With your wings you will fly to heights otherwise unknown to man, and I will be there to guide you along the way.
 As you grow your beuty will surround you making an aura of gold that will carry you to greatness, a greatness I know you can achieve.
 And when you cry my soul will too, I wish I could steal your pain for myself, but you must endure it to grow into the man your father wasn't.
 Don't be scared my baby, no monsters under your bed will harm you, for they are afraid of the light, and you carry the brightness.
 Achieve what you can achieve, cry when you must, smile when you feel your heart shining, and love all eternally- Mommy loves you!
