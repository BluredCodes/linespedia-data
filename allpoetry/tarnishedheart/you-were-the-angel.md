---
title: "You Were The Angel"
writer: "tarnishedheart"

All the time wasted on fighting, if I would have known you would not be here right now, if I would have known that I would miss you this much, it never would have happened, you were the angel.
You left and from there my world fell apart, I have a beautiful baby boy, but I can't look into his eyes and see you because he is not yours, I told someone else that I loved him endlessly, when all the time you were the angel.
Now I am trying to fill the whole in my heart by using someone who could never be you, someone who I will try to mold to be the person I remember you as, but all the time, you will be the angel.
People come and go in your life but I will remember you for the rest of mine because no matter what you will always be my first love, and you were always the angel.
Now you are gone.:(
