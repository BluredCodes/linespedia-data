title: "I Need A Release"

It shouldn't be like this
I should be happy now
but my world is falling apart
how much more can I give
before you take all of me
how much more can my heart take
before you break it completly

I need a release...

I cannot keep pretending
I am completly satisfied
I cannot keep wearing
this artificial smile
just to make you think
I am emotionally healed
from others before you

I need a realease...

I wish I could cover
my eyes for one day
so that I couldn't see
the emptiness in yours
and I wish I could cover
my ears when you come around
so I couldn't hear all the love you don't have

I need a release...

I can no longer sleep
on a tear soaked pillow
and I can no longer dream
of unavoidable breakups
your hands don't touch me as soft
and your words don't sooth me
as they have in the past

I need a release...
