---
title: "It's Raining Again"
writer: "tarnishedheart"

How can it be raining again
drops so full of the past
dripping into the future
drying on wilted leaves
made of sworn secrets.
Puddles made of tears
and I walk through them
and i melt again
into who I used to be.
I let you mold me
and suddenly all those voices
return in my head
and I am out in that garden
lying on dead flowers
because the one before you killed them.
And I look up at gray clouds
silently wondering
how can it be raining again
drops so full of the past
dripping into the future
drying on wilted leaves
made of sworn secrets.
