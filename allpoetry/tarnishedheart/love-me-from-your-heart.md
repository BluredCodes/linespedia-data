---
title: "Love Me From Your Heart"
writer: "tarnishedheart"

source: "AllPoetry"

Do not love me from your mind
because then you will be forced to think
Do not love me from your eyes
then you will be forced to see

Do not love me from your mouth
then you will be forced to talk
Do not love me from your soul
because then you will be forced to fall

Do not love me from your past
then you will be pushed into the future
do not love me for your well being
because then it will make you ill

love me from your heart...
