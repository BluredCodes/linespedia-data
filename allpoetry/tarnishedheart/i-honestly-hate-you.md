---
title: "I Honestly Hate You"
writer: "tarnishedheart"
slug: "i-honestly-hate-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/33681-I-Honestly-Hate-You-by-tarnishedheart"
---

I found myself thinking about you again.
And every time I do it makes me sick
because I seem to think about the good times
and I hate you for that.

I always focus on the front you put on
the times you told me you adored me
the times I believed you
and I hate you for that.

All the time wasted on you
when I could have been with a real man
And I told you that you were my angel
I hate you for that.

My family loved you
never seeing past your mask
worst of all they trusted you
I hate you for that.

I forgot who my freinds were
focusing on you
I lost so much time for you
and I hate you for that.

Most of all
my son will never know who is his dad is
you threatened our lives
I hate you for that.

So I will raise him
to be the man you are not
and I will be his daddy
hate me for that!
