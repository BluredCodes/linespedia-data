Maybe I was underwater
it is harder to hear under there
because the whole time you
were making promises
I obviously wasn't listening
We were swimming within
a school of fish that fought
to be next to you
but I wasn't listening.

Maybe were puppits
set up, designed by, you
guessed it, yourself.
Making us dance and move
all by your hands
a shame we broke the strings.

Maybe I was blind
although I could see what I wanted
but only because you let me.

Maybe they were right when
they said all you ever did was
lie,not maybe definatly
I have living proof.
