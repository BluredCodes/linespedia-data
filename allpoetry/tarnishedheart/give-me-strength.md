title: "Give Me Strength"

When my clouds are falling
I turn upward to the sky 
and scream as loud as I can
"GIVE ME STRENTH"
When I can't feel my words anymore
the way that I used to
before I said them
and afterward I lay in a puddle
of guilty thoughts
and I see the ripples spell the words
"give me strength"
I trace my fingers
over the soul that I placed
carefully beside my bed
and I see visions
of times when I was satisfied
with the trials this world has given me
then it fades to black and all at once
I hear a high pitched yawning voice
that came with the words
"give me strength"
So I kneel beside my bed
and I pray to a God I do not know of
and I ask for some guidance
because latley I have been walking alone
but I hear nothing
not even a sign
or whisper
and suddenly I am so weak...i don't know anymore..
