You sit there
with the hatred in your heart
while others still love you.

You sit there
you rich and greedy man
while the poor ask you for money
and you laugh in their face.

you sit there
old man
with diamonds on your fingers
while others pawn
their belongings for a meal.

You sit there
drinking your imported wines
and smoking your expensive cigars
while other drink out of sewers.
 
But there is something
greedy man,
that you will never have
a pure heart
made not of stone like yours
and that's worth more money
your many stocks and bonds 
will ever make.
