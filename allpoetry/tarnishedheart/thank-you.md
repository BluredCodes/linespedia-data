This is not a poem, but i just wanted to say how thankful that i am for having found this website, before i came here i had no where to vent, i have been through so much in my life and i feel poetry is the perfect way to let it all out. I have met the nicest people here and you are all so very wonderful!!! i want to give a special thank you to 3 people. first, to Karen for being so open. Sweetie, you are a very strong person and i adore you for having the courage to go through what you have been through! Second my thanks goes out to mystaint, you have always commented on poems and everything you have said about me is so sweet! I love your work and your style, you are a great person. And lastly I want to say thank you to sheshe. I'll tell you what, when I saw that poem to me i about cried! no one has ever taken my stuff so seriously and i am so thankful to you, you have inspired me in ways i have never known.  There are so many more of you i want to thank, this is such a great community with wonderful people and i am so glad to be a part of it!!!! I love you all :) :)
