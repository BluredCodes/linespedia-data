Beautiful beautiful bird, fly away, fly into the unknown sky. Pretty bird, please don't look back, we all know it's came that time. Magnificent man who taught me how to love, don't turn back now, beautiful beautiful man, don't try to say goodbye, I won't know how. Caring soul, I will shed sorrowful tears, beautiful loving man, don't forget me, that's my only fear. Beautiful beautiful bird, it's time for me to let you go now, beautiful beautiful bird, I know you'll find your way back somehow.
