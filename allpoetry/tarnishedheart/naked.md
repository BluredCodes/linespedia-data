Steal the laughter from my cloudy mind
steal the love in this heart of mine
take away the tears from my confused eyes
take away this mouth that has caused all the lies.

It doesn't matter...
I come to you with no armor
I come defenseless to stand before you.

Take away my soul that has made me fall
take away my heart which to you it calls
steal the light that shines inside
steal the secrets in which I can't hide

It doesn't matter...
I come without guilt
I come to break down the walls you built.

Steal my hands from my useless shell
steal my feet, I know this road so well
take away this jealous mind that has caused me to hate
take way my feelings before it's to late.

It doesn't matter...
I come to you with no artificial feelings
I come to you forever warm under your wings.Tina Brannon's poem I Dreamt
