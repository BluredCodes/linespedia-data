I've learned that no matter how good of a freind you think you have, the better you get to know them, the less of a freind they become.
 
 I've learned that it takes a life changing experiance to bring out the real in someone.

 I've learned that no matter how well you think you may hide behind your "mask" eventually the real you will come out.

 I've learned that the easier it is to fall in love with someone the easier it is to fall out of it.

 I've learned that sometimes to get your point across you must go past words and let your actions take over.

 I've elarned that though the truth may hurt, lies do more damage in the long run.
 
 I've learned that when someones life may seem boring they tend to make up lies to make it more interesting, forgetting where they truly came from.

 I've learned you can bring up a person with all the respect in the world, but as they get older, one person can change all they've been taught.

 I've learned you can meet someone with all teh brains and beauty in the world and fall in love with them, but if the feeling isn't mutual, what kind of relationship can you truly have?

 I've learned that the minute you shy away from the people you call freinds, is the minute your soul begins to die.I think everyone should write an "I've learned" poem
