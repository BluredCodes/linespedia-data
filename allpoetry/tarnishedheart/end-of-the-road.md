title: "End of the Road"
writer: "tarnishedheart"

You could travel a thousand miles, searching for your dreams, you could give up on the world, just don't give up on me. Because at the end of the road that you felt you've traveled alone, and at the end when you feel you have nowhere else to go, turn around and there you'll see me standing, with my open arms and a love that's never-ending. You could search coast to coast for whatever would make you happy, but at the end of your journey you'll turn around and there I'll be. Because at the end of the road I'll be your constant, and if that's alll I have to give, then that's it. And at the end when you feel you have no one to turn to, just turn to and I'll be there to see you through. Because when your at teh end of the road and your standing at the drop off, I'll be that extra few miles you need to make it across. And when you feel you don't want to go on, I'll be at the other side of that cliff, stick with me and you won't go wrong.
