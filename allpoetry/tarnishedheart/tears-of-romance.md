---
title: "Tears Of Romance"
writer: "tarnishedheart"
slug: "tears-of-romance"
source: "AllPoetry"

I lay beside you
in a room full of stars
with memories of the past
memories of our hearts.

And I can't help but wondering
where we would be today
if our selfeshness had not
gotton in loves way.

I hear fate screaming
give this love a chance
as my eyes well up
full of tears of romance.

You hold me so soft
yet strong and I feel safe
tell me you hear my soul aching
for you to feel the same.

To feel that wholeness again
to go back in time
to really open your eyes
to feel our souls intertwine.

And I hear fate screaming
give this love a chance
as my eyes well up
full of tears of romance.

And when this is over
we will float on silver clouds
we will be that high forever
never coming down.

The stars can be our home
as we dance outside of gravity
we will sleep floating together
forever just you and me.

until then fate will scream
give this love a chance
as my eyes well up
full of tears of romance.
