title: "I take it all back"
writer: "tarnishedheart"

I want it backI want back all of my words that made you feel like more of a man than you really were.I want all of my smiles.I take back all of the times i cried over you.I take back all of the lies i told because of you.I want back all of the friendships my love for you took.I take back all of the times i told you i cherished you,I take back all of my praise.I want back all of those times i could have been laughing.I take back all of the tears.I take back all of the times i felt sorry for you.I take back believing you when you said you'd be there.I take back trusting you.I take it all backAll those times i thought you were my angel and told you so, I take it back.I want back your innocense, so I can shatter it to pieces like you have your sons.
