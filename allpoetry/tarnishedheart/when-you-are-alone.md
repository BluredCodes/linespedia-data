title: "When You Are Alone"
writer: "tarnishedheart"

When I am gone you will respect me
when I remember where my priorities are
and my sanity finds it's way home
you will miss me

When you are emotionally poor
physically drained
and mentally disturbed
you will come looking for my comfort.

You will not love me until I am gone
you will not appreciate my worth
until it does not
support you anymore

and the first night you reach over
to find my warmth not there
and you lay awake staring at the ceiling
you will regret your misguided love.

So when the first tear falls
out of pure regret
and you realize the loss you have brought on
do not call me

And as the second tear falls
out of lonliness
and you realize you are by yourself
do not turn to me.
