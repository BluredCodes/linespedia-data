I've learned not to listen to the words but the silence in between, sometimes containing every hope and dream, sometimes containing your deepest fears, whatever the situation, I've learned from you. I want to thank you for being my light in the darkness and it seems now, more than ever, your halo shines, proving you truly are my angel.
