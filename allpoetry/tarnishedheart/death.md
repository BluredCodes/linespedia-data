So now you want to know your purpose
you want to see your final destination
 you want to see the faces of the shadows
you want to wonder no more

You want to see death.

 So now you want to see the angels
maybe demons even more
 you want to confront Mr. fear
ask him why he hides
you want to see death.
 So now your face to face with the dark angel
why don't you speak
 you want to take it all back
you want to erase the words
you can't.
