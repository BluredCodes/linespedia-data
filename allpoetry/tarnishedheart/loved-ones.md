Like a beautiful song that replays in your head all the good things in life somehow come to an end. We always focus on the good times we shared and it brings nothing but tears, sometimes forgetting we always had a friend through the years. We sometimes think of our loved ones as angels and when their gone we feel we have no where to turn but we have to remember that times heals wounds and only time will help us learn. The world is full of questions yet we still ask why, we wonder why God took them away, we feel it wasn't their time. We won't sleep for fear of our dreams, we won't stay awake because we're afraid something might trigger a memory. We're afraid to forget yet so quick to cry, we tell everyone we're okay, but we know that's a lie. We must remember when it's time to take that trip the good life we've led and no matter what there will still be tears shed. So in our times of grief we must look up at the stars and when we see one twinkle we'll know where our loved ones are.
