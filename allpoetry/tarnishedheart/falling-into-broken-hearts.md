Catch me
I keep falling
down this tunnel
lines with 
outsretched hands
and every time
I try to grab one
they pull away
is this depresion
or am I suicidal
and these are the images
an almost dead person sees?

I need you
to tell me it will 
be okay
I need to hear your voice
I need words
that will fully express
the extent of my
longing
I need tears
that will cleanse 
the inside
I need an 
explanation
of why I can't make
anyone
anyone...
understand my grief.

I keep falling
into thse broken hearts
I used the first one
on you.
