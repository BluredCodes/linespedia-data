In loves eyes
we dance united
flirting with the birds
against a purple sky

I loves eyes
we are never alone
and laughter becomes
a nesessity for us

In loves eyes
our world is turned around
by fates hands
and destinys soul

In loves eyes
we will be satisfied
we will understand our hearts
and the moon will guide us

I loves eyes...
