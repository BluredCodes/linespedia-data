---
title: "Where Is My Hero?"
writer: "tarnishedheart"
slug: "where-is-my-hero"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/33669-Where-Is-My-Hero--by-tarnishedheart"

Where is my angel
that I can fly next to
that I can feel free with
that I can love endlessly?

Where is my mentor
that will teach me life
that will guide me
that will listen?

Where is my soul mate
that will listen to my heart
that will talk to me
that will connect with me?

Where is my best freind
that I can cry with
that will be unconditional
that will love me.

Where is my hero....
