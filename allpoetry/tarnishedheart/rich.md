You in your 3 piece suit
thinking your better than everybody
while they sit on the street with a 
little tin can and beg for whatever
change they can get
you know who they are
but yet you go home every night
to your perfect life
and sit in your big house
and drink your imported wines
they wonder where they are
going to sleep at night
if the rain will sweep away
thier home
if the roaches aren't to bad
in the old abandoned crackhouse
they decide to sleep in
you worry about someone stepping
on your manicured lawn
never once thinking
the extra 5 dollars it took
took make it that way
would make a world of difference to them.
