Just when I think it's over
just when I think that I'm done
when I am ready to give up
loose control
You make me love you again.

When I think that I have cried my last tear
and sang my last love song
the moment that I am ready to fall out of love
and into desperation
I fall in love again.

Because I keep falling in love with you
and falling out again
I keep crying at night
and sleeping on a tear soaked pillow
I keep loving you.

When I think that I have loved so much
that my pain won't allow anymore
when my hands shake to the point 
that I can't hold your hand
You take mine.

when my eyes are so filled with pain
and my head hurts from trying to 
stay calm all the time
and I feel on the edge
You bring me back to saftey.

Because I keep falling in love with you
and falling out again
I keep crying at night
and sleeping on a tear soaked pillow
I keep loving you.

Tell me what the pains for
I need to know right now
don't tell me you can't comfort me
I refuse to accept that
I refuse to be in pain anymore

Because I keep falling in love with you
and falling right back out again
and I keep crying at night
sleeping on that same tear soaked pillow
I keep on loving you.Okay this is my first real attempt at writing a song, please be honest and let me know what you think.
