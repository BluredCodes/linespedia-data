I I was to tell you 
I never stopped loving you
would you break down
would you promise to love me?
  
If I was to tell you
I adore your bravery
would you lay down your swords
lay down your armor?
 
If I was to tell you
you have an invite
to soar through my clouds
would you fly with me?

If I was to tell you
I've remained silent through smiles
would you frown
would you scream into darkness?

tell me.
