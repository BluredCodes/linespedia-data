---
title: "The miracle That is You"
writer: "tarnishedheart"
slug: "the-miracle-that-is-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I'll be there when the rain stops falling, when the young and old go down to a few, I'll be there when the birds stop calling, I'll never give up on the miracle that is you.
 I'll be there when people have forgotton how to love, when no one has anyone to turn to, I'll be there when we take that journey above, I'll never give up on the miracle that is you.
 I'll be there when you've given up on yourself, when it seems like everyone in your life was untrue, I'll be there when it's my turn to help, I'll never give up on the miracle that is you.
