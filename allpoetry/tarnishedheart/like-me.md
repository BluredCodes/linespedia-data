title: "Like Me"
writer: "tarnishedheart"

Have you ever laid awake at night wondering why you can't get me out of your head?(feel like me). Have you ever cried yourself to sleep because you wonder if I am okay?(feel like me) Have you ever looked into my eyes and seen a future between us?(feel like me) Have you ever fallen in love with me to the point you want me around at all times?(feel like me) Have you ever woke up in the morning and wished that I was laying next to you?(feel like me)pleasefeel like me.
