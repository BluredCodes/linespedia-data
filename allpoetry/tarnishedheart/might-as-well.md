title: "Might As Well"
writer: "tarnishedheart"

You might as well have told me, spared me the heartache, the tears, the questions. You might as well have said you didn't want a relationship. You might as well have just took my heart from the begining and spared me the heartbreak now.

 I might as well have said here, take my heart, take my pride and everything I've ever stood for and step on it and toss it around. Leave me lost, cunfused, and hurt once again because I'm stupid enough to let you do it.

 They might as well have said look,no matter what I tell you about him you are not going to listen so make plans to spend the rest of your life with this guy and keep listening to the lies because in the long run you are the one that's going to fall flat on your face, again

It never stops!
