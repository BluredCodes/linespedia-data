Please don't talk like that
you told me you how you thought 
about killing yourself 
over and over again
it struck me hard
because you are my best freind

You don't realize
I can't see you die
call me selfish
but I'm not done
loving you yet!

you said
what kind of life
is getting fed through a tube
not being able to sleep
next to your husband 
because you have to
stay close 
to the bathroom

you said
what kind of life
is not being able to hold 
your grandkids
and not being able
to stay awake'
longer than two hours because
of the morphine.
I had to agree

But i need you right now
i need to know you are okay
need to hear you laugh
to smile
to talk
to be there when i need you to be

so you can't leave this world
not just yet
i love you to much
to much to watch you die
we all do!i wrote this to my best freind, she is like my mother in law and right now she is really sick, and she was only given a little while longer to live.
