I can't love another man because it wouldn't feel right, and when i look into everyone elses eyes i see nothing but emptiness but when i look into yours I see eternity and if that's how long it takes for us to be together, I'll be there the day after forever.
 We've created something beautiful and we can't turn back now, we've shared our secrets and made sweet love and if it takes a lifetime for you to see, I'll be there the day after forever.
 I get a rush knowing no one knows, the things we do are perfect, the freindship is so strong. The ehat in my heart will burn on even after I'm gone and if it takes until the end of the world for you to realize, I'll be there the day after forever.
