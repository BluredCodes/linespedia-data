---
title: "This Heart Is To Broken"
writer: "tarnishedheart"
slug: "this-heart-is-to-broken"

These hands are to damp
to shakey, to cold
to many have passed through them
for them to be safe to hold.

These eyes are to weary
to see the love in your eyes
darkened by the past
the pain in others lies.

these legs are to fragile
to walk that road again
to afraid that I will fall
or not know where to begin.

These arms are to tired
to try to hold someone 
tired of them being pulled away
tired of falling in love.

This heart is to broken
to let another in
there is no room left
and no where to begin.
