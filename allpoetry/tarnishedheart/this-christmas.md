This christmas there will be no laughter coming from the family who lost their own on that horrible September day.
 There will be so many questions from a little boy who can't understand why the angels his mom alaways talked of weren't protecting his daddy that day.
 There will be a million and one moments of silence to remember the men and women who died, a million and one tears of realization that they were our own.
 Lonely families at home praying that thier loved ones are okay while they are out fighting this senceless war.
 This Christmas a woman will deliver a baby of a firefighter who died on that horrible day, she will be alone.
 This time the carolers will sing a different song, one of hope, love, and one full of tears.
 This Christmas...
