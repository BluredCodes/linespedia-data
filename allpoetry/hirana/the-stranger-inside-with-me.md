---
title: "The Stranger Inside With Me"
writer: "Hirana"
slug: "the-stranger-inside-with-me"

As I sit inside and ponder thoughts
thoughts of today
thoughts of yesterday
thoughts of the day after
tomorrow

ramblings in my mind
running on fast forward
rewind
stop
pause
play me again sam

over and over in my mind
I stare at the silhoutte in this moonlight
this shining glow from above
being strong
being brave
sitting here
pondering
waiting

staring deeply
at the crescent
the half and half
the one object I am visible to see in this darkness
The moon on the inside of this outhouse door
staring down at me

I can't go now
Damn you moon!
