Hot Damn!

Six shots

As I bend over backwards for you

I smell your fear

I submit to be your master

Your parallel parking skills

Need some brushing up

I wore leather tonight

Your leather

To find sin against skin
and skin against sin
again and again

I bend over backwards to pleasure you

You respond with

A Kama Sutra silhouette

More beautiful than any ancient painting

Your pain soothes me

Your soothing pains me

Back and forth

Up and down

Inside out

You read my insides like a subway map

Fuck Freud

And cum to me
