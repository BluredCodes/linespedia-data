They said
we were
inseparable
bounded by faith
and I was led to believe
that that was true
but when the storm came
they turned their backs on me
leaving me
alone to die

[Refrain]
Betrayal
Betrayal
Left behind
Out of mind
Betrayal
Betrayal
Deserted now
Uncertain how

Then You came along
Claiming to be
Lord of lords
King of kings
Lord God Almighty
Then You
promised to be my friend
Redeeming, receiving
my sin-stained soul
Fulfilling, renewing
my broken heart
lifting from

[Refrain]

You came to save my life
How could I be so blind?

I said
we were
inseparable
bounded by grace
and I tried to lead You
to believe that that was true
but when the cross came
I turned my back on You
leaving you
alone to die

[Refrain]

Betrayal
Betrayal
Judas defied
Crucified
Betrayal
Betrayal
Peter denied
Disciples blind

Please forgive me LordHere's another oldie from my early days of songwriting.  I wrote this a few years ago, before I had really developed any of my musical skills.  It's basically looking back on those fair-weather friends who split when the storms come, and realizing that at some point or another we have been God's fair-weather friend, and far worse.  Thus this one carries a two-pronged message.
