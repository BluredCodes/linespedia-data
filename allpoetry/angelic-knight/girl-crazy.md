Here I was minding my own business
Then uh-oh, here she came along
I think I’ve been struck with a case of madness
So it’s time for a little love song

[Pre-Chorus]
Uh-oh, I’m going really crazy
My heart’s thumpin’ messages my brain can’t understand
Oh no, my senses gettin’ hazy
This hyper kinda feeling, it’s really outta hand

[Chorus]
I’m girl crazy
Girl crazy for you
I’m girl crazy
And you’re my only sanity

The guys around me say she’s a real hotty
But lemme tell ya, she’s something way much more
So what – I’ve gotten really mushy
That’s part of being crazy, so just go ahead and snore
It’s something in that smile, it knocks me off my feet
Little hearts round and round in my head
Because she’s so darn sweet
But does she like me, really like me, or will it be a no?
Oh my gosh, look at this, I’m trapped in 90210!!

[Pre-Chorus]
[Chorus 2x]

(slower)
There’s magic in the twinkle of her eye
And something a bit hypnotic in that lovely smile
And her voice, it makes my world slow down
It’s in her embrace I could simply drown

(fast again)
Oh great, look at this, I’m loosing my feeble mind
Writing lyrics here and there 
About this girl I’ve always wanted to find
I never foresaw this day heading straight for me
And all the time I’m writing this
I don’t even know if she likes me!

But it’s just that her touch is mystifying
And that look she gives me is so flat stupefying
Maybe it’s love, maybe it’s not, but it’s something really neat
Cuz when I hold her close, man it’s just oh so very sweet

[Pre-Chorus]
[Chorus]

One way or another
I’ll learn something great from this
This heart will soar or may be broken
But for now I’ll just await that kissPerhaps the result of listening to too much MxPx, I was hot in pursuit and in the mood for punk rock when I cranked this out.  It's by no means a serious piece and is a diversion from my usual style of writing, but it was fun to play with.  It's a reminder that in playing the game of dating, you should never take things TOO seriously!
