Love of mine
I am truly thine
Destiny will find us
With our hearts entwined
And when our souls are as one
Our song will have only begun
The angels will sing it,
Of our love never undone

By your side, my love, I’ll stand
As the tides of destiny ebb and flow
And even as this world collapses
I’ll face fate to the end holding your heart in mine

Thus I long for the day I find you
(Those other boys are just playing games)
When your voice will whisper through the night
(Scattering the clouds of faceless names)
Until that day I patiently await you
(I won’t fail you no matter the odds)
For the day when our eyes shall first meet
(I don’t care how long this journey prolongs)

Men claim to know the meaning of romance
Of heart-stricken love and chosen partners
Yet they break their dreams in shameless pursuit
Swimming in lust and hiding from security
But my love, I wait, I wait for you silently
Forsaking forbidden dances and aimless romantics
For the thunder cries out and the lightning makes clear
