Give your statement to the public
So they’ll feel the sting of your deception
These days are dying now
And it seems your grave lies with them
Humanity is a pitiful existence
Self-indulgent in the standard of decay
So go ahead and give your speech
While the casket’s open, don’t delay

These are the politics of confusion
The safe standard for killing the soul
Bury your guilt under its warm soil
But in the end the politician will be impeached
And fire will burn a new policy

When your address to the states is over
Life will then cease to be
The blind will open their eyes to the fire
And the contingency will follow you to hell
So share your vision with the people
Because they won’t live to see reality
Sweeten your tongue with political correctness
So the world can grow numb to mortalityI wrote this after an experience I encountered with some nasty rumors and threats that were going around, while no one was taking the time to piece the whole picture together.  This poem is not directed to an individual, but rather to those who choose to stray from the truth and lead people with them.  It's a hard, straight telling of the fate of those who choose live a public lie.
