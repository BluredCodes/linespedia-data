---
title: "Chameleon"
writer: "Angelic Knight"

Crawling through walls of limitless self-deception
Your desire is to wire my brain to misconception
Slithering into hearts, seducing timid minds
Hysteria is regular but sweetened by your lies
Changing your colors to fit the picture of the moment
You’re killing while thrilling in subtle mental torment
I despise your ways -- your fake smile makes me sick
Keep it up, little lizard, and your soul He will evict

[REFRAIN]
Chameleon
You’re the master of blind conformity
Chameleon
The bitter taste of hateful animosity
Chameleon
You’re here to set up my final fatal folly
Chameleon
Breaker of dreams, societal anomaly

But there’s something more in life than YOU

Give me one more kiss, so my lips can rot with poison
Your so-called love ain’t from above, it’s spawned from evil notion
Seduce me with your smile, bludgeon me with praise
Turn my back to a whack, asleep in your web of haze
I regurgitate your affection, before you stop my heart from beating
I know who you are, what you are, my blood spilt from your deceiving

Your breaking your breaking your breaking my heart
Twist the knife, bring the strife, kill my sense of compassion
Your breaking your breaking your breaking my heart
Support me while I stand, kick my while I’m down

[REFRAIN]
