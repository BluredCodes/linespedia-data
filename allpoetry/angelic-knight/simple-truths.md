title: "Simple Truths"
writer: "Angelic Knight"

There upon eternity’s edge
I see You there, softly calling
"Come home
Child of Mine, come home"
And here amidst the chilling night
I cry back for the release of my heart

I try then to reach out
But my hand won’t stretch
The depths behind me pull back viciously
And I fall into my self-abyss
Release is so close, yet I am so far
I am the slave and the master rolled into one

This abyss of mine is the pit of guilt
The snare of pride and of sinful youth
Its night is deep and its deception true
Lie veiled as truth and all too real
Forgiveness is close but I’ve damned myself

It is there in the heart of the night
That I see Your eye in the midst of the storm
Subtle yet true, I can’t deny You any longer
Hypocrisy dies in the grave of confession
As a ray of light pierces the clouds

Funny, my night wasn’t deep as it felt
Not to You anyway, it was merely an inch
Your hand reaches down and in a mighty flash
Hell runs for cover from invading love divine
A few nails later my life is reborn

It’s not over yet though
Back into the night I shall charge forth
I left those behind who shared my same cry
If only they knew, if only they could see
That night in its darkest is no thicker than a shadow.This was written for my Creative Writing class at UALR.  It serves as reflection on the dark shadows I've faced in my own life, and how they have served to push me to stand beside those who face those same shadows today.  The point: there is no point of no return as long as you're willing to fight your way back up.
