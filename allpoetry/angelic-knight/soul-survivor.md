[Chorus]
I wanna go out just like Elijah
Chariots of fire
Kissin’ this lonesome world goodbye
Chariots of fire
Welcomed by the angels up on high
Chariots of fire
Never gonna give up till I die
Chariots of fire

If I stand alone, I stand
My sword shant waver
Nor my hunger cease
My chariot awaits
For my ascension to release
Take me when you’re ready, Lord
For now though let my love increase

[Chorus]

If I fight alone, I’ll fight
As a soul-survivor
Yet all beside me fall
Your glory beckons
And I can’t forsake Your call
Let demons come and temptation thrive
Yet You will reign as my all in all

[Chorus]

Come to me, Abba

[Chorus]This is the only song that I have complete music written to.  It's a two-vocalist rock piece where our lead vocalist handles the singing while I chip in the "chariots of fire" lines while doing my lead guitar duties.  It's our first complete song, so I hope things will only improve from here.
