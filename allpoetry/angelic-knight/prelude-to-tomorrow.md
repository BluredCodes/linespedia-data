---
title: "Prelude to Tomorrow"
writer: "Angelic Knight"

Here in this solitude of night
I cry out silently from my bed sheets and pray
Searching through my untamed emotions
I try to make sense of the intricate disarray

It’s here in the silent calm of night
That I find you waiting quietly for my thoughts
I search through the maze of haphazard feelings
Mindful of how this reality decays and rots

I’m alone tonight
These thoughts they won’t stop screaming

(REFRAIN)
Break into my mind Adonai
Invade my very thoughts and make me see
Come down into my heart, Lord on High
Snap these bitter chains to set me free
Because you and only you
Can break me

I’m falling on my knees tonight
If only your majesty will come into this bedroom
To take my thoughts away, far away
For if I die tonight it will no doubt be too soon

So I ask, fill me with your light
Chase away the shadows and burn away my fears
Awaken me from this earthly illusion
So I may stand up and wipe away these tragic tears

We’re alone tonight
This heart it can’t stop pouring

REFRAIN

So here I stand tonight
I might be shaken but I’m still on your firm rock
I’ll look onward to tomorrow
I may close up but alas you break the lock

Thus I have hope tonight
Now I have a purpose and a full pardon
And so I rise up from this floor
This heart may break but will never hardenThis song is the result of many bedside prayers growing up, and is just a simple pouring out of the heart.
