Each year they come 
They come and go away
With so many different colors
Of happiness and of Gay

I had wanted to hold on 
With the first the second or few of them 

As one of them had the innocence
of a naive child playing at the bay 

one had this love 
which made me blush away

Yet another had this cleverness
could make me smile always

yes these were the seasons 
which showed me my love in so many beautiful way

I wanted them to stay on 
for now and forever

But arent these are the seasons
Each year they come 
they come and go away

taking away a part of myself 
leaving behind an ounce of pain
with so many different colors
but of Happiness or of Gay ?Do give any comments
