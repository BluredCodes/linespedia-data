there is living on a certain street
a certain grouch that you may meet
his humour never is discreet
with insults he doesnt miss a beat

one day he found his routine waning
and decided that instead of just complaining
he's try to catch himself a bear
and tie it to a rocking chair

he figured first he'd need some bait
and realised what would be quite great
would be a penguin he'd have to get
from where its never temperate

and so he travelled to alaska
and spotted the fellow he was after
and set himself up in a little tent
with a trap that was quite elaborate

while his bait walked across the icy floor
oscar didnt even *see* the giant paw
end his arctic fun with so much force

he can no longer act so smug
when hes better suited as a rug

speaking figuratively, of course
