(tribute to having attractive female friends)

when i have nowhere to hang my towel
i think of my friends
and a place appears
how lucky i am

when i have nowhere to place my donut
i think of my friends
and a place appears
how lucky i am
and noone will steal
my lunch

when i have misplaced my flagpole
i think of my friends
and im at full mast again
how lucky i am
(patriotic too)
