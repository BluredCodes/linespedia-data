---
title: "...loves company"
writer: "vague hit"
slug: "loves-company"
source: "AllPoetry"

as she walked she didnt notice 
the trail she left behind her
shining brightly coating the street 
from pain she held inside her

and those who came near
would be coated and stained
by her exhalation of emotion
her unconscious sharing of her pain

they all try to help her
to live in the calm she holds in sleep
in the end they are defeated
because her pain has roots too deep
