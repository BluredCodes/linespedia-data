I burrowed into the earth one night
felt warmth scrape across my skin
i knew if i dug deep enough
id see my life begin

my air was not a problem there
although the space was tight
it filtered through from above-ground
and was scented with the night

as i got deeper in my quest of sight
i noticed warmth around me
i rested for a second
and let my bearings fall around me

for a second i felt truly whole
for a minute my soul had mended
for an hour i gazed at my form redone
time stopped and i ascended
