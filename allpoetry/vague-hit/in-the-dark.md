in the dark
i dont have my teddy
i cant see my teddy
i can only feel my teddy
for all i know my teddy
is really a monster

Or Evil Teddy!

Woe!

black fills my room 
like a punctured vein
woe fills my room
like a flooding bathtub
an evil one!

oh woe is my teddy
he has turned
to the dark side
the force is not strong
with this one

my teddy's
now called freddy
and its never ready
to heal my hurting head-y
my poor evil teddy

and i feel all alone
i feel oh so forlorn
forlorn!
forlorn forlorn!!
id rather be in a park
than all alone
in the dark
without my teddy

!!!
