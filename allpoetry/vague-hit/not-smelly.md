Natasha is my friend
my talky friendly friend
my very very special friend
i like my friend
she is my friend

and for my friend
i will bend
and i will never comprehend
why forever she cannot be
my special friend
she is my friend

and even if i start a trend
that involves ducks and virgin hens
id never forget that in the end
my wierdness all comes from my friend
ahh my friend
she is my friend

and she no longer needs to shave her legs
