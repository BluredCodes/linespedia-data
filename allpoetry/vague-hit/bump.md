title: "bump"
writer: "vague hit"
slug: "bump"

is this where its coming from 
angry ungrateful unconscious 
turned round again 
too many places where we become hollow 
hungry to reach a quick end 

and me with no knowledge 
to find out whats been right next to me 
ive finally found where im coming from 
ive finally found the end 

my minds been traced in the snow 
i watched it melt away 

too late to hold 
whats been steadily shifting away 
why bother when 
i dont need whats come back again 

hang on to your complexes 
unravel and lie in the threads 
maybe we're all found unconscious 
maybe its time to play dead 

hungry for what i can see 
but dont want to heal 
saved when i touch whats come round again 
saved when i no longer feel
