---
title: "enclosure"
writer: "vague hit"
slug: "enclosure"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8768-enclosure-by-vague-hit"
---

hold me tight in your container
so i can see these walls i love so much in detail
run my hands over my enclosure
love every strand of being that holds me here

seal me tight in your container
that i may never see the light
guided only by what i remember
perfection in my memory ive kept of you
