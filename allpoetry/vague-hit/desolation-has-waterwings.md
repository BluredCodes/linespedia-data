A duck
has wings!
but ive only ever seen it float

why cant it fly
fly away?'
doesnt it want to?
stupid duck.

stupid fuc*ing duck
no, im not stopping now
does it just want bread
stupid fuc*ing dolebludging duck

it took all my bread
now im starving 
and homeless
and it just quacks
stupid fuc*ing dolebludging housewrecking duck

my wife left me
for that duck
did i forget to mention
that?

yeah
i think
i did
oh

desolation!
concentration!
claymation!
