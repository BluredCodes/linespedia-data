I guess all I have to say is that 
Growing up is hard today
Hating parents for force feeding you lies
Breaking bottles for not getting  gigs
Drinking like there's no end to help dissolve the pain of 
The closed hearted world in which we live
Being punished for doing things your own way
Beaten down by society
It’s not your fault they’ve given up
Just tell um all to fuck off
Don’t even waste your time trying to open their minds through the closed eyes
Too weak to keep fighting back and walk away w/ another bruise
That’s ok, we’re all bleeding w/ you
You just gotta keep going, keep going till u die
Pushing on and on through the pain
Gonna fry your brain trying to forget the past, its ok
Everyone’s trying to escape where they come from
It’s not your fault they’ve given up
Just tell um all to fuck off
Don’t even waste your time trying to open their minds through the closed eyes
Too weak to keep fighting back and walk away w/ another bruise
One day we’ll make it
It’s not your fault they’ve given up
Just tell um all to fuck off
For now just be brave
We’re all the living dead
