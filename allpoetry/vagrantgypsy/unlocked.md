So now I’m free 
They say this is the way to be
It feels so liberating being in this place where
The wind blows and chimes through the leaves
People finally know, something I’ve been hiding all my life
Locked away in a part of myself I never knew existed
They say we learn something from everyone we open our minds to
I’m glad I met you
Cuz now I might never be the same 
And a confession I must make,
Not all change is bad, it may even be for the better
Something I may never admit to again
 And yes it’s true some will turn away
It doesn’t matter
My mind will probably  be a mystery to them and
I’m probably better off but
For you the door will be unlocked
Just like my eyes
