title: "this class sux"
writer: "VagrantGypsy"

I sit here and watch the world go by and people changeDon't know why I'm sitting here with this bad taste in my mouth thinking of my demiseJust know that I wanna leave this place and go somewhere far awayYou tell I'm not smart enough or strong enoughWell isn't that your jobTo beat your students down and compare them to one anotherMaking the prejudice that already exists more potentI refuse to be judged against the kid sitting next to me just because you think hes betterSo I'll get up and walk out of your concentration camp you call a classroomYou have no right to tell me what I'll never be just because I didn't want to deal with you and cut your classSo I'll look you straight in the face and say 'I'm goin home' even though I don't know where home isI have to look for a place that loves me as I am instead of my GPA
