---
title: "Loosing a whole year"
writer: "VagrantGypsy"
slug: "loosing-a-whole-year"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I can feel my heart beat as my eyes bleed
And I lay here, so confused.
Don’t know what to do, 
Don’t know what to say to make everything ok again,
Even though it never was.
I just got pushed back a whole year.
These familiar faces are new .
I can’t believe I lost a whole year and no one has changed or cares.
I wonder if all these people are worth it,
If all my feelings and emotions are being wasted.
Yes, it’s true that I want to die, but I’m too afraid;
I lost the courage along the way.
I can’t believe I got shoved back a whole year, even though I’m not the same.
But when it’s all over,
You have to ask yourself
Was it worth it?
Because it better be.
