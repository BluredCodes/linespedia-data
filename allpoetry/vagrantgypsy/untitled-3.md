It brings me down to think about all the times we'll never have
My address book is filled with names I don't even know
It just pisses me off to feel the whole world beating me down
And never let up even when I’m on my knees crying
There's so many songs that offer hope, but
Hope can only take you so far before you realize there's nothing left
Spending my days in the light smiling, only to go back to my dark room where sorrow consumes me
I just don’t know what to do;
When you hate yourself more than the others around you,
And there's nothing left but a dark room to come home to
