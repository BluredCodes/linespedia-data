Swaying at a standstill where nothing is right
I have no faith 
Will I die tonight?
When I'm bored!!
Being this way makes me hate you so
I'll slam my door extra hard to try and make you disappear
You think you know mother fucker
Well you ain't got a clue!
Wish I could have my way with you
Trap you in a room 
Show you who I really am!
Watch your eyes bleed when I scream into you
All the lies I live you'll never admit to
I'll slit your throat and let you see 
What you gave birth to
And how fucked up I can really be!
I'm Bored!
Threw out all my god damned pills last week
Can you tell?
Well I'm still fine
I'm me again 
Back in my old worn skin
Makes me think into the past of memories that
Rip through scarred flesh
I wanna leave this shit hole but the got me caged in 
Chained by my arms
I can't escape so
I sit down and think of how  
Fucked up this life is
I want it to end!
Too much goes on inside my head when 
I'm bored!
