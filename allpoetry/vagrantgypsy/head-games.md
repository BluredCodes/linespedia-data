We're standing here speechless and theres nothing left to say
I try to tell you how I feel but right now I'm numb
I know it's not fair,the power I have over you,
But I can't change the past or conrol my future
I don't know why you take up so much of my sleep
Maybe because I'm pathetic and melt when you tell me you love me
I know it isn't true and this illusion will soon fade away
As did your past affairs
But for me the memory will stay because thats how I am, 
Even though I'm the one pushing you away
I know I'm wrong to play these games with people's minds 
But I can not control it even when you are beat and I prevail,
I still hang my head in shame
Maybe you deserve this, maybe we both do
We're two dirty people covered in the filth of our own actions
I'm just wondering why you take up so much of my thoughts
It's probably because I'm a loser whose addiction is head games
