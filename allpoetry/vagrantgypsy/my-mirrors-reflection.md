I won't be seenI can't be heardThere's so much more out there than this,So I've readI wanna get outOut of this townAway from these peopleI wanna experience something more than theseIll lit rooms and aged yellow wallsStillStuckIn this placeSame minds, same scenery as any otherI am told andA lonely poet who can't seem to get 'it' togetherWondering what 'it' isFeeling the emptiness grow each day andThe all too familiar sting accompanied by each breathKnowing what will make her whole is not within reachDrownded streams of ambition and hopeCascading down rosey applesAs acid filled rivers prism the light from A mirrors reflection
