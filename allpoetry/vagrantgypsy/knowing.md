All is lost, and songs can no longer save you
Sometimes hiding is a death wish
There is nothing left
Everyone is gone or leaving and no one cares to say so long;
You’ll just be deserted alone thinking back to times with those you loved 
And how time can change people and force desicions that effect everyone else
I guess drugs were a good way out, but you didn’t have to leave me
I was just a little girl knowing more pain than someone twice her age
And now I must pay for your selfishness and all those who choose to follow you out of my life
Well, that's what living is like, and I’ve learned, 
That people are punished for things that aren’t their fault
But that's ok, I’ll manage alone with out any help from you, because
Now I don’t want it
And everyday you don’t call, write, or think of me; I grow a little more
A little more bitter
But hell that's what ‘adults’ are, 
Because they’ve seen too much and think it’s childish to believe
In anything
To them fairies and elves only exist in the ranting of some insane author
And the only angels are second graders in Christmas pageants
But I’m still that little girl
Waiting in the corner by the door wearing your silver rings
Knowing...
