You’re such a fucking bitch and 
I don't care who u gave birth to you're still a fucking bitch 
Don’t know why I got to live with you 
Is there something else u can say besides ‘no’ cause if u can 
Then I don't have to sneak out to rid my mind of you
Didn't mean to be such a let down 
If you knew I'm sure u would have thought twice b4 u got laid that night and waited for tomorrow but 
How can I be sorry? 
Don’t care where I was conceived but its just so easy to believe that Destiny wasn’t paying attention when it let dad sex you up
And I'm not sorry for being alive just wish people wouldn't beat me down so hard 
But I don't care and I don't apologize for being here and making all of you hate me 
There’s nothing I can do I've been put here to destroy u 
So fuck off you stupid bitch 
I'm about to self-destruct...
