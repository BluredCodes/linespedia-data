I’m so sorry, I should’ve tried to save you from yourself,
Instead of secretly hoping for your survival.
I shared in much of your pain,
See my mother left too,
If only you knew then maybe things would be different and  
You might not be sitting alone in a cold cell.
I offered my hand, but you didn’t think you were in any need
No one can save you from yourself, yes I know
But I should’ve tried to help instead of secretly praying you’d be ok.
That one way track was easy to predict
And we all saw you drowning, but we’re trying to keep ourselves afloat.
Everyone's so bitter at the world for making them who they are
No one even thinks to help those suffocating in there own wake.
So selfish, so afraid
We all end up dieing anyway.
I should’ve tried.
Now there's bigger waves.
