Somehow I figured it out by now
Took me long enough
All the hurt all the pain
Embedded into me
So now my eyes are open
Took me long enough
To see your true colors were always that bland
Always there those colors were 
As colors often are
I lost myself inside you somewhere
Somewhere deep in that cold,dark hole
And that part you stole from me 
I may never steal back
Too afraid to touch you
Too afraid to try
To afraid to find myself in those colors that you hide
So go on,take everything away
Turn your back and
Leave your trail of ashes
Use that paint brush to cover up and hide
I lost myself inside you somewhere
Somewhere deep in that cold,dark hole
And that part you stole from me 
I know now I will never get back
So take all that you've gotten from me
I hope it was enough and
I will go on sweeping up the
Trail of ashes that you've left behind
