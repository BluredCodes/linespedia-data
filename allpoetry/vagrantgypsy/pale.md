Today was just a blur, a smudge in time
I wonder if my whole life has been a day dream so far and I still haven’t woken up
“You need to sit down, you just got really white...
I’ll get you some water, how many did you take?” 
Your temple is eroding and your face is very old
You keep repeating past mistakes and can’t admit to new ones
“You’re really starting to shake, are you sure you’re gonna be alright this time?...
Don’t lay down...
Can you hear me?”
Stay awake to dream of a different life than that of which you’ve created
“You can’t fall asleep”
