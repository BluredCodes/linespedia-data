As I rested my head on your shoulder and fell asleep,
I thought about how safe you make me feel and 
How comforting it is to have you to lean on.
The sweat from the concert was still fresh on my face, and 
You didn't mind me getting your shirt wet.
This is what I've always wanted.
The cars on the freeway remind me of the beaming neon lights and
Their exhaust of the cigarette smoke.
Right here,I am happy.
Sitting in the back seat of a car driving home after inhaling colored smoke and moshing to our favorite anthems.
Falling asleep with you and a pizza box on my lap wishing we could drive like this forever because right now I am happy 
And so are you.
