title: "Someone please find me"

Being in this place 
Looking at these people
Makes me sick
I'm not like them
Where do souls of the tormented belong?
We belong here,among the others
Our gifts appear as burdens as we walk the crowded streets,
Symbolic of our confused minds
But our hearts will be found by fate before we are swallowed by
The sea of high heels clicking around us
I only wonder where 'we' are
For I see myself a lone soul
Someone please find me...
