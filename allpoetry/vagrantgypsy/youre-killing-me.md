title: "you're killing me"
writer: "VagrantGypsy"

My jail cells on fire
(The one you’ve locked me in)
And Standing right in front of me
You’ve thrown away the key
But you tell me your so sorry it fell into the flames and your afraid
Your shift is over; you must leave
As my skin melts away onto the cement floor
It’s ok just close your eyes and go get high
Erase the memories of mascara running down my face
The mascara I never wear
Forget what it sounds like to hear me cry for your help, 
The help thats not even there
Just one more time,
You’re standing right in front of me
You’ve thrown away the key
You tell me your so sorry 
Your shift is over; you must leave
Inhale the smoke and image you didn’t walk away with the key
