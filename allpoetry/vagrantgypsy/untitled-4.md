Write a suicide note or do my math homework
The choices teenagers have to make these days
We’re all worthless
We’re all confused and frustrated
But most of all, I think we’re sad, no, angry
Actually, I don’t know
I don’t know how anyone feels
I'm not even sure what I’m feeling
Just a sense of hopelessness
There are a few people who can make me smile,
But they don’t care about me
Life makes me cry
I’m sick and tired, just like everyone else
But everyone is stronger
No one else is on their knees
I’m not alone, even though I feel it
And I’m so lonely when I think about it,
My heart aches, I can feel it as my eyes burn
I don’t know what else to say because
I’m crying now, and it’s hard to write through tears
