Can’t define destinyEspecially mineSo what is this?Take a deep breath and open my eyesBeen kind of long since I looked outsideIs the sun always out at this time?Sitting on a strange street corner Twittling the grassFeeling as though I knew where I was withThe same sun setting everywhere else I stand but Only hereBeing able to smell the amethyst, apricot hue andSip the last remaining drops of mango Listening to what the stars have always promised while I sit in a crowded car going to a place I don’t know the name of withPeople who drink gulps of life with as much difficulty as I
