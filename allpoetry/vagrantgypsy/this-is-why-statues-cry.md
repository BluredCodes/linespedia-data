Hating where I come from
Loathing where I am
To engrossed in selfishness to know where I'm going or what I'm doing
Having no patience for myself or anyone else
Losing control,when I never even had it
Here I go again on this carousel ride of a life time
Too afraid to look back and refusing to open my eyes
Wish I could explain it to someone and make them see me when I look this way
I feel so stale
Brittle and rough
Breaking apart and crumbling at the smallest amount of pressure
So this is me
No wonder they all hate what they see
Sometimes I wonder what that is but I guess it doesn't matter
I can feel the little girl dieing
Silently shes slipping away
And it's her silence that scare me the most
I always thought she'd go down screaming clawing her way to the top
But there she goes
Fading away into the blackness
Motionless hands clasped in fists stuck to her sides
Goodbye goodbye dear child goodbye
I never thought I'd let her die with out a fight
But all I can do is remain motionless and watch her suffocate
This is why statues cry
