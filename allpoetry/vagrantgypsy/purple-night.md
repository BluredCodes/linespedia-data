title: "Purple Night"

Blood on the moon and I’m with out my tigers eye
Black ink of the clouds covers pale faces and blurred minds
Trying to justify the wrong in everything that feels right
We’ll talk about this with a long sigh somewhere in the future
If we make it that far
Remembering that we didn’t notice how cold it was
Sitting on the curb
Making false promises we could never keep
In a place we had long since forgotten until I found my tigers eye
