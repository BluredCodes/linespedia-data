Lay all the blame on me
Everything you refuse to look at is shoved inside of me
No sleep
Too tired
Closed eyes, sealed mind
Blurry vision so I hit the floor 
And crawl along the ground
Dirtying my hands in black soil
It strips me down to bad decicions which push me in the wrong direction
So now I'm now I'm naked searching in the wrong placeI can't remember writing this one or anything about it for that matter..it was just one of those days.
