I went to the planetarium today and
I saw how many galaxies there are
And I decided that we’re not alone, nor are our lives worth anything
I realized that if I die today, tomorrow, or right now, it wouldn’t mean fuck to anyone or anything 
Except maybe to some people who knew me, but in ‘the end’ they’d die too
Everything we know isn’t anything and
Everything we are means nothing
I just thought you all should hear what I felt today
And feel as worthless as a piece of fly shit in the ocean
