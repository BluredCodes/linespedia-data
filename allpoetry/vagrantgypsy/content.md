I’m so glad all of you fucks think I’m betterNo, I really amHappy to see your vapid little being is peacefulSo when you smile at me I'll smile back just so I don’t hear some fake attempt of concern Do you know what I think about when I see you smiling?I think of all the ways to make u miserableI want everyone to pain like me, forever Because a day wouldn’t be long enough and I know you would forget about it an hour laterI’m not asking for helpI don’t want any from youI just want u to frown when you walk by me or turn away just don’t smile cause it makes me sick to know that You are content w/ yourself
