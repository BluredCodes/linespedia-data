I sit here motionless staring into the nothingness of life
Focusing on the swelling pain of my arms and legs instead of the 
Yelling taking place outside my bedroom door and inside my head
A cheesy info merchial blares in the next room and 
Blood starts to drip from my frustration
I hold my arm afraid that I've gone too far this time and can't come back
There's no more t.v. screaming through the wall from the other room
Only the bantering voices that fade as 
Footsteps become distant down the steps
Now I'm left alone in the high pitched silence interrupted by the occasional raised voice from the basement
I hold my arm against my chest and wonder how there can be so much rage inside of me from
A world full of noisy info mercials and fading footsteps
