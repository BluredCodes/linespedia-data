title: "I just don't see..."
writer: "VagrantGypsy"

So confused
Fell right back to where I used to be
I’m starting to believe this is where I belong
Since no matter how hard I try, I can’t break free

Angry
That this is how it ends
With memories I wish I didn’t have
And broken promises

So sad
Don’t even know how I feel
One moment I’m alive, the next
A black out and I wake up
Lying on the asphalt bleeding from the mouth

It’ so hard to love
When no one loves you back
And they don’t care
My friends are gone
They never even knew me
I just don’t see a reason for living
