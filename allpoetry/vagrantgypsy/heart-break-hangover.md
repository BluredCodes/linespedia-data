title: "Heart Break Hangover"

Today my heart fell apart again, 
Just like it does every time we are forced to say goodbye
I hope you miss me while you’re away because 
I long for you so much

Our hearts will never melt in that romantic way because of all the time we spent in 
Diapers together fighting over who gets the last Jell-O cup, 
That's ok
I just hate it when we are both made to go our separate ways
Wish there were someway we could be close again instead of 7states apart
I guess that's the price you pay for someone else's happiness
But hopefully I’ll see you next year,
And even though are lips didn’t meet at midnight, are arms did, and 
We fell to the ground in a stupor of bliss

I love you so much,and even if we never tie the knot 
That's ok, because 
We played tug-o-war as kids
Please just feel the same because
Life has already broken my heart
