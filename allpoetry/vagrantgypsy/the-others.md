Look at meLook me in these crazed eyesSee through to my drowning soul andWired mindAre you scared yet?GoodI want you to see the tears in my faceYou cut me open now Watch it bleedSoon your nights will haunt you too withYour eyes a mirror of my ownShadowing flashbacks in your headMy nightmares will invade your mind andEat your dreamsThereafter you shall lye cold and DeadBut you can't look inside meFor then you too can share my secret world andI have made the Land of Sleepless NightsAll my ownSo I close my eyes and shun away as I'm walking down the streetSleep well
