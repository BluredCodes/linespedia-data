Today I hate everythingMy alarm clock is so fuckin loud butI don't have the strength to get out of bedSo I lye here and think of how I can't sleep and how pissed my mom is gonna be when she sees me laying hereBut I don't care becauseToday I hate everythingAnd school looks worse than the gates of Hell so I'll get to the doors and turn around Today I hate everyone Since I know people are so stupid and act so cruelI'm being punished for individuality butIt's such a beautiful thing to see so many people jusdge me into more creativityToday I hate everythingi know it isn't good at all, but it gets the message out...
