I am a piece of ice
So cold and hard
Covered by the filth of society
The specks of darkness you find in me are not my own
They have been bore into me by others
Only a few at first but
Soon I am covered with the pollution of hate
My glowing,transparent,innocence is no longer visible to anyone
Not even myself
I am too far gone to even redeem my cleanliness
The mocking sludge you have covered me in has smoldered my
Obliviousness
The sun no longer graces me with its unfamiliar sense of 
Warmth,tranquility,and comfort
There is no hope nor love or glow
