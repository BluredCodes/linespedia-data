I'm so like you it makes me sick
The more i deny myself the closer I get to reality
I know you see yourself in me but it's not true!
I'll never be like you!
EVER
I'm getting out of here and flying just above the ground between the trees
Even if I never soar at least I'll be free
Not like you!
Thats what I refuse to be
So I push farther from the truth and lift myself up in a wind of lies
I'm getting out of here
Don't know  how I'll make it alone 
But it's better than sitting in a crowed place all by yourself
Realizing you don't know whats behind the familiar faces in your life
I'm alone soon to be gone to a place I don't know of yet
Knowing only a single soul,
Myself
