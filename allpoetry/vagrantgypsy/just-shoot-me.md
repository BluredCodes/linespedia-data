title: "Just shoot me"

Please, just shoot me; end it all now
Make sure you aim is true and penetrates deep into my mind,
Kill the brain not the heart ‘cuz once I read that loves never dies
Ha! What a lie
The only thing certain is death,
The end,
The apocalypse; the beginning

I hear songs about crowded rooms and lost souls
But somewhere, somehow
I stopped listening
I see it all but see nothing
I’m lost but I’m home
I have no voice but there's a lonely echo
I’m alive and cold
So please, someone give me something I can believe in
Not just words with no letters
I wouldn't take them anyway,
Even though I’m offered nothing
