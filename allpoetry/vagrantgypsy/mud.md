It seems almost too easy to close my eyes in daylightPretending everything is alrightTaunting questions that come with no answersLetting hope drip awayClawing to the nothingness of lifeWhy is it that i can't find peace at nightThe silence and infinite emptiness burn with such a rageIt sends my hope trickling down Soon to be a dried up puddle ofCracked clay
