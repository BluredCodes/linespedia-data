My soul is a leperinfecting those in my wakeresigning myself to the ground devastating the once tranquil flowers beneath my rupturing feettoo weak to pull strengthfading from inside outmasking my decrepitudeashamed of my powerlessnesswoeful of the woman once insidesurrendering my putrefying soulI spat my requiem
