Doubt meDoubt what I speak is trueDoubt my capability to fall through with what I can only conclude you wantSlay meSlay that piece of me that despairs Slay the woman who seizes my desires and disappoints youlet me honorably trudge in my corner, gilded in midnight Hoping not to be regardedHidingHiding from the dense pain that wafts through the air especially here, here on the west side
