Do you listenMy AdonisDo you hear the words I speak?Sweet SurrenderMy pretenderWhat have you to critique?Can you see me CasanovaHow is the view from above?Get an eyefulThen be sighfulFor it simply was not enoughKeep an ear outHave your eyes peeledOne day I may appearThen your brillianceShall cast my shadow And again I shall retract in fear
