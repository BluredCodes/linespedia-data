title: "fetus"

The warmth of the ceaseless darkness calms my raw headI soak I fadeBlinded by the vacancy of a flameI am EmbracedI feel ClothedSensing what can not be touchedI am honoredI feel ecstasyPeaceable midnight, so earsplitting with silenceI coilI twist Breathing in dense, leaden blacknessI am Disguised I feel CensoredDrenched in soiled solitudeI am NourishedI feel SheathedUnwept tears float, kept unweptI am CourageI feel ControlI can no longer bleed, for I can not see the wound
