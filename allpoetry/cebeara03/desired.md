The smell of your skin,
Leaves something to be desired-
For so long I have pushed it away-
That nagging want-
From my mind.

Would it be shameful for me to say,
That I can longer hold back?
That I desire your sweet-smelling skin,
Your wonderful touch,
That I desire you?

I will wait patiently for you,
And only you my love...
To desire me back.
When you give the word,
I will give you something
I have never given before.
I will give you me-
Until you, and I...
Can take no more.I do know where this came from however. Naughty me...*chuckels*
