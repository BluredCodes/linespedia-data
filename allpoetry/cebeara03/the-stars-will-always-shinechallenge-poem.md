Love is knowing 
That their smile 
Is the only thing 
That makes you feel whole. 

Love is knowing 
That their voice 
Is all you want to hear 
Until the ends of your days. 

Love is knowing 
That even though 
The road is tough 
They will always light the way. 

Love is knowing 
That no matter 
How far away they are 
You feel them 
As if they were right next to you 
All along. 

Love is knowing 
That no matter 
How dark the sky may be 
With them... 
The stars will always shine.
