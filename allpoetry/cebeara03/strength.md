title: "Strength"
writer: "Cebeara03"

The world around you
Cold and harsh
Seems like they dreams you have
Are too far away for you to grasp



You have always strived
To do your best
To make your life worth living
While this cruel world
Has tried to push you down




Struggles thrown your way
Sometimes hit you hard
But what you have to do
Is start again, and never give up
What you need is the strength of thousands
To keep you on your feet.
