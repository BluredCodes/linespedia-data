I'm sorry for the sudden outburst of screaming
After a while, I didn't even understand
What I was actually saying.
I'm just so exashasted...
There are far to many things to finish
In one extremly short day.

I'm sorry if I seem distant at times
I'm just trying to stay ahead of things
Trying to keep all that needs to be done in check.
Trust me, it's nothing personal towards you.

Sorry if I forget to say things sometimes
The things you truely need to hear.
Just know that I feel them in my heart
But my mind is just too cluttered right now
To rememeber to tell you.

Sooner or later I will be able to rest,
Just drop the load of stress off my shoulders
And breathe for once in 5 months.
But in the meantime I just need you to bare with me.
Can you do that?
