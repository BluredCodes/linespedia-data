Why should I conform to you?
You don't matter to me anymore
I never mattered to you.
So you think you would be glad
That we are over.

You tell me what I believe in
Is nothing.
Isn't it funny that you used to believe
In the same things.
Forget you...
I don't need you now
Thanks for nothing
You pitiful fool.

You gave up everything we had
In mere seconds
And you were never sorry
Too bad I wasn't either.
You are a mistake
I never should have made.

You never cared
And showed it
By single-handedly ruining everything.
But what you don't realize is that
I single-handedly ruined you reputation.
Isn't it funny...
That the same person who told you not to mess with fire
Is the same one that had you burned?
