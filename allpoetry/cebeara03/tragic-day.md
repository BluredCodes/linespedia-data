I looked at him
And saw nothing but a facade
That mask he put on everyday
To make it all seem right
It was one great lie
Everyone believed





When I walked on campus that day
I never would have thought
I would have been met with such tragic news
"he's dead" was all I heard
The rest just faded away





They handed me a small envolope
With my name neatly written on the front
I noticed the ink was smeared from what seemed to be...tears
And my heart sank





I stood in silence
I could hear nothing but my own thoughts
And could see nothing but my clouded memories
I tried ever so hard not to shake
As I opened the envolope and pulled out the letter
Which read:


Dearest love of my life,
Please forgive me
But this had to be done.
I never meant to leave you,
But the rest of the angry world.
Don't shed tears from those beautiful eyes
Because I am not there,
For now I am free.
This wasn't your fault,
So never think that.
You were the only thing
That made life worth living,
And I am Sorry I ever messed up what we had.
Just know that I love you,
And I always will.
LOVE ALWAYS,
Your Angel





The letter dropped from my hands
And fell to the floor
As my fallen tears were added to those shed by him
It pained my heart to know
That I was the only thing he thought of
In the end.
