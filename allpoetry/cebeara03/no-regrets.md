---
title: "No Regrets."
writer: "Cebeara03"

I'm sorry I left you,
I'm sorry I didn't stay-
I just needed something,
Something, you couldn't give.

It's hard to get used to-
Seeing us together,
I know.
But you really shouldn't get angry...
It's not anyone's fault.

You should just move on,
Leave me and those tattered memories behind.
I am sorry it had to come to this,
But my heart holds no regrets.
