title: "Let Her Know"
writer: "Cebeara03"

Deep passion contained in her heart
And tragic fires held within her eyes
Has she seen life?
Has she possesed it?

Why must it be her
That must go through that pain?
Of dying to young
Before her life has truly begun.

She doesn't feel sorrow
All she knows is her geniune smile
And her joyous laugh.
It seems all wrong...
She cannot die like this.

I know I cannot stop fate
It does what it pleases
But untill it takes her
Away from this gorgeous world
I will let her know
That someone
Will be crying when she leaves.This is about a girl I had the chance to visit at a hospital. She will only live untill she's 15. Right now, she's 8. Can you imagine? No prom, no wedding, no life?
