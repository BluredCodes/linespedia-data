---
title: "Have You Ever"
writer: "Cebeara03"
slug: "have-you-ever"
source: "AllPoetry"

Have you ever
Looked at someone
And seen the pain in their eyes
Have you ever
Had to turn your back
And tell them goodbye

Have you ever
Seen the tears
And wish you could wipe them away
Have you ever
Had a conversation with someone who thinks
They can't last the day

Have you ever
Seen someone fall from glory
Right before your eyes
Have you ever
Had to sit down
And explain to them
Why they were told lies

Have you ever
Stood before someone
Holding a knife
Have you ever
Had to speak words of comfort
To help end their strife

Have you ever
Looked around
And seen the whole world pass you by
All because you were too afraid
To step up and try
