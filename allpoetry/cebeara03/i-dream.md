title: "I Dream"

I dream of a world
Contained in anger
Continuing tears 
Shed into an ocean of lies
Hurt looks forever escaping 
Once innocent eyes 
Smiles that were once resident
Faded with age
For time hath all marks upon this place
Running ever so slowly 
Despite knowledge of a quickened pace
Upon first sight
This world shows pure beauty
But underneath it
anguish lies.
