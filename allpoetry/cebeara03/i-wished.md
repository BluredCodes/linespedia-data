You walked into my life
Or was it that I walked into yours?
All I remember is liking you
From the very instant you spoke
I was drawn to your every word

I wished I could hold you
On nights likes this one
But I knew that friendship
Was something that stood in the way
Now that I look back
I never regret barging into your life

That day...
When you asked me
To ask her
If she would be yours
Was something I would never forget
But I knew that being your friend
Your best friend
That I would never let you down

When she broke your heart
I thought nothing but regret
Because she may have done it
But I felt like it was my fault
How could I have done that do you?

From that day on
I wished upon every star in the sky
That I might be able to show you
Just what true love was like
