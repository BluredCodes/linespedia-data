The casted shadows across your face
With lingering longing in your smile
I see that the past has caught up with you
Please just let it pass you by.

The distance in your eyes
Tells me you'd rather be somewhere else,
Anywhere, but right here right now.
All you need to do is forget the painful memories
And live the future that's right before your eyes.

You are strong, I know this
But somehow you let the past eat you up
Until there is none of you left.
You can't let it take hold of you.
Just forget the tragic past that made you cry
And look towards what the future holds.I dunno where I got this one from, it just sorda wrote itself.
