A dream
My mind did play
Oh so real one beautiful night
Of you and your dear sweet love.
So real did it seem
That I could've sworn
I felt your touch linger 
Sweetly upon my skin
As you took me into your loving arms.
So real that your breathing
Seemed so near and sounded so lightly
In my ears.
So real that your beautiful whispers
did dwell in my mind,
Like a prolonging memory.
So real, that when I called your name,
You responded and smiled such a smile
Glorious enough to make the sun shine from it's beauty.
So real it was that I did not dream it at all.
For your sweet touch I do feel
Your breathing does lightly pass through my ears
Your beautiful whispers I hear
And your glowing smile I do see.
Why must I sleep then? 
When no dream can be better than the real thing.
