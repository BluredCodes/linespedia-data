She looks at him
As if she has something of importance to say
But she turns around
And keeps her emotions at bay





Their past has been rocky
And their future looks worse
She wonders if meeting each other
Was some kind of curse





He smiles at her
Wonders why she looks so sad
Then wonders if living with him
Could be that bad





He loved her from the start
That much is true
But what happens when those feelings fade
And you don't know what to do?





She looks at him one last time
And walks away
Because she feels that the pain
Is just too much to take today
