title: "Don't Blink"
writer: "Cebeara03"

All the world moving by so fast
In a whirlwind of emotion
With quickened smiles
And hurried hellos
Don't blink-
Or it might pass you by.

Honking horns
Busy streets
And crowded coffee shops
The world full of people
Who never take time to realize what they have.
Don't blink-
Or it might pass you by.

You were always too busy
Rushing to do this and that
While negleting what you need
You blinked-
And life passed you by.
