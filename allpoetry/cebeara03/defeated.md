You think you hurt me,
That much may be be true.
But let me let you in on a little secret...
I hurt you even more.

You think you ruined my life,
And I laughed as I watched you fall.
It's not my fault you made the wrong choice,
So why should I feel bad for you.
You caused this for yourself.

You think you pulled me away from love,
And now I have someone I love more than anything else,
I smile at him with more conviction than I did you,
And I Say "I love you" more.

You think you made me tragically sad,
And you did for a while.
Then I grew up and realized I hated you,
Everything about you.

You think you own me,
And I laugh as you walk away
