My best friend 
Gave it to me 
One day out of thre clear blue sky. 
She held out her hand 
And bathed in love, 
There it was... 
The thing I would find myself 
Living for. 

My connection to world 
Connection to life 
The reason I breathe 
All encrested in this tiny 
Piece of jewelery I hold so dear. 

Unsubstantial to others 
But everything to me 
It holds my life.... 
My heart... 
My soul. 

My cross... 
The one thing I could not live without.
