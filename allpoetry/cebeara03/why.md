Why must he go through that pain?
The yelling and screaming;
Will It never end?
It's tearing him up inside,
Can't you see that?
What you do and what you say
Is leaving him suspended in pain
Why must he go through that?
No matter what, it's not going away
And I can't help him.
Why?this is about someone very dear to my heart. I wish I could help him. I wish I really could do something to make this go away.
