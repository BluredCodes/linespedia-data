title: "One Of Them"

The look in their eyes
Cold and gray
Propel you into deeper infinity
Passed reckless waters that were once peaceful streams
Do you realize it at last?

They pull you in one direction
Conform you to the next
No longer are you indepedent and youthful
But old and loosing faith fast


They tell you you are never right
That you've always done it wrong
No matter how hard you try
They push you down and keep you there
Their pleasure springing from your pain


Years have passed
Your faith finally gone
Deteriated in the midst of your sweat
But mostly from your tears
Nothing great of what you have become
You are now one of them.
