Beautiful as the sky is dark
She has the grace of a thousands angels



Her eyes glow brighter than the most gorgeous star
And her smile paints the most beautiful picture




Is true beauty just what you see?
Or something more to be told?




Think carefully before you answer
Because you might just be caught in beauty's fateful web.
