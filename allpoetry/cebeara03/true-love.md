---
title: "True Love"

He sits entranced
As the hallowed shadow of a nearby flame
Lights the deepest corners of his eyes
And catches their innner beauty

He notcies her beautiful features
As she lay beside him
Sleeping soundlessly in the dim light
With a ravishing smile
Placed lightly upon her lips

At this moment
He knows no one in the world
Has had the chance
To see what his eyes are gazing upon
For he has never known anything to be more gorgeous

He lays down beside her
and holds her tight in his arms
As his mind retraces all the memories they have made
One day after another

He can't help but think of himself
As the luckiest man alive
Because he has the most wonderful girl in his arms
And to know that she loves him
Is nothing like he ever dreamed

Then he lays his head upon his pillow
He stares up at the ceiling and whispers
"so this is what true love is..."
