For years it went on
Relentless and constant
Not a word did I speak
In pure and utter fear

I wish I could take back the days
I should have fought harder
I wish I could have my childhood back
One without tears
I was forced to grow up far too fast

My body a sanctuary
Of that he did invade
Now because of him
People's touch I evade

Now that I'm older
And have much more of a voice
Why is it that I am still silent
Forever having this secret that I keep.
