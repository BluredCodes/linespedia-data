That time has arose
Where all is lost
Those who choose to take a path
Find themselves down the wrong one
Lost in their pitiful cries.

The travel constant
In the wrong direction
Calling out to no one
Hearing only their own voice echo back.

'What have I done to deserve this?'
They cry aloud in the lonely dark
Through their screams 
They have yet to see
It was their own bad judgement
That brought them to this place.

They chose to let temptation
Obscure their vision
And lead them to the damned
It is their own fault
That all they had to live for
Saw  right through them 
deep down to their ways.

They only walk alone now
Wallowing in their grief
Wishing that their reason to live
Could just be there
Just for one more sinful night.This is sorda a puzzle to all those out there who read this. Try to figure out the true meaning behind it.
