I'm not stupid,
And I hate you for implying I am.
Who are you to judge someone,
You don't even know?

Fuck you,
And all that you pretend to be.
You should take some time to notice,
That I just might be more than I'm percieved.

There are better ways to say things,
Than the way you fling hateful words at me.
Fuck you and the horse you rode in on,
I hope you cry when I prove you wrong.

I come to school everyday,
Not to be called stupid, but to learn.
So who are you to chastise me?
A teacher with an inch of sense wouldn't have done what you did.

Just because you are there to teach me,
Doesn't mean you actually taught me anything.
No wait...yes you did.
You taught me what an asshole is,
And what I shouldn't be.
Trust me...
I'm not stupid, and I'll prove it to you
By undermining your stupid ways.fucking teacher who thinks I'm stupid just because I can't write essays very well. Fuck him.
