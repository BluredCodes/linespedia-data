The stars whisper in their galant sky
words that, only those who know they whisper, can hear
Do they foretell all future that lies ahead?
Or are there secrets of which they withold?
I did not deal in their secret words until now
But if I had, 
The stars would have beautifully spelt your name
With all the glory that they posses
For they knew my own hidden thoughts and whispered secrets...
Of you.
