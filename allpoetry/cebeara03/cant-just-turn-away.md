He can't just turn around
And walk away from her...
For the pain is too much to bear.
"I thought you said you'd never leave me,"
He whispered to her one day,
"And now you are with him."

"Give me one more chance
Let me begin this again,
Please don't just shut me out
You're the one that keeps me alive."

"You want me to accept you and him together,
But how can I possibly do that?
He's my best friend..
And you are my true love.."

"I promise to open up next time,
Even if there will be a next time...
I promise to love you more than I did then,
More than ever before."

She still stays with the other,
While tearing him apart.
He is no longer like he was,
So tragically sad,
So bitterly alone.

She hurt him so badly,
Although it was not her intent.
And every time he looks at her,
He always sees an angel
Everything about her that he loves,
And his soul cries,
because he can't just turn around,
