Through my eyes
Painstaking steps are lost
Taken over and replaced
By a smile so beautiful
It would put the stars to shame.

How did we reach this place
Without looking at the signs?
Somehow I mistakenly
Looked over my shoulder
When I should have kept walking the other way.

I grew too accostumed to that place
And that's why my heart
Wouldn't allow me to let go
I have put myself to shame
Too many a times
To see it happen again.

Forgive me, but I can't take it anymore
You must have thought it was right
But I knew it was wrong
I now say goodbye
To leave to another place
But this time...
I'll be sure to take heed of the signs.
