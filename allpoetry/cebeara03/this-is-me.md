---
title: "This Is Me."
writer: "Cebeara03"

I know I'm not beautiful,
With my baggy clothing,
My short whispy hair,
And seemingly plain face.
This is me.

I'm not exactly popular,
I don't talk to the "in" crowd.
But I do have my own little circle of friends,
And I think that's more important.
This is me.

I know I'm not the best,
I always fall short of the goal.
But in my heart I come out on top,
Because I tried.
This is me.

I know I'm not always what people expect of me,
I can't be the picture-perfect girl.
But what I can be is true to myself and others.
I don't care if you don't like who I am.
Take or leave it...
This is me.
