You look at me 
With those cold hard eyes
And expect me to believe
The words that come from your mouth.

If you only knew
That I could see right through you
Right down to the empty space
Where your heart should be
The place where you supposidly
Held your love for me.

I knew it was a lie
From the very beginning
But you just continued
With the deceat
Little did you know...
It was I who was tricking you.

"I pity you, for turning away from me."
Ha, what a joke.
We didn't have anything,
So you can take that pity
And use it for yourself
For it is I
Who pities you.
