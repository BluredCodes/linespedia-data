Are you scared by the
Deep anger in my voice?
The neverending
Hostility in my eyes?
It's ok. So am I.

I'm sorry I had to leave you,
But didn't I come back?
Can't I redeem myself
And make it all go away?
You are angry with me.
It's ok. So am I.

Hurt escapes from my fingers
Whenever we touch.
I never knew how to be normal,
I never knew I could be nice.
You think I'm horrible.
It's ok. So do I.

I can't make you forgive me
That's impossible.
But if only I could take away
Everything about me you don't like.
I don't fit in.
It's Ok. Neither do you.another random inspiration.
