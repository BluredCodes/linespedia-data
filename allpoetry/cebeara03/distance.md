You say that I am beautiful
You say that you care
But how can that be true
When you are never there?

One minute your eyes show longing
The next they show fear
Or perhaps what I saw was love
and remnants of a tear

You stay with me one night
Then slowly push away
But I just watch you walk off
Because I am lost on what to say

You smile at me
But behind it are there lies?
I can never tell what you are thinking
Please say that look doesn't mean goodbye...
