Isn't it strange
How one word from tainted lips
Can shatter a fragile soul
When tears fall from your eyes
It is I who will always know

You wake up every night
Seeing my face
To realize it was only a dream
Just fantasy
Mixed with cruel reality

Thoughts cloud your mind
Of ways it could have been
"I love you..."
You whisper
But you know it is something
I will never hear

You wish for me to be in your arms once again
Just to hear my voice
For one more second
All you know is that you feel
So alone...
