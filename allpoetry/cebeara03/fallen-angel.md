Her bright infinite eyes
Contained in a worldly smile
She glows in lasting beauty
An angel from above.

But now life has befallen her
Taken her down the wrong roads
Took those bright eyes
And drained tears from their depths.

He has noticed her pain
His beautiful angel in distress
And tries to wipe those tears
That slowly caress her shining cheeks.

Life threw her another tragic mistake
Followed by an aching heart
The angel is slowly falling from Heaven
And he is running behind her
Trying to catch her before she falls.
