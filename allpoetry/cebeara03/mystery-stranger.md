title: "Mystery Stranger"
writer: "Cebeara03"

Who are you?
Queit stranger who looms in the dark
Not wanting to be seen by wandering eyes
So why is it that I see you?
Is that your will?
To elude other's eyes
But let mine see what they will?
Aprroaching from your hidden corners
You look reluctantly towards me
As if what might come
You are afraid to see
Why is it you are drawn to me stranger?
With all those in the room
You wish to come to me
From out of that gloom
Sitting next to me
Your mystery is no longer there
I know who you are
For you are no stranger at all
But the sadness and regret
That's hidden in my heart
Oh, how I wish
You would have stayed amongst the dark.
