Please don't look at me like that,
I know I hurt you-
But shouldn't our friendship
Reach above those hateful eyes?

I know you care abut her-
The thing is..so do I.
Fate was bringing us together,
While pulling you apart.

Please don't be angry
Because we are in love.
Please understand, 
I didn't ask this to happen-
But I can't just let it pass me by.

You understand, don't you?
Please don't be angry with me.
