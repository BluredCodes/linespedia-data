title: "Just Stop."

Can't you see what this is doing to us?
Just stop this insanity now.
I'm tired of being pulled in between,
I love you all, but I'm not choising sides.

I just can't handle both your problems and mine,
And frankly I think that I need no place in this.
This is between you guys-not me.
I will give advice, but that's as far as I go.

Just stop all the badmouthing,
Just stop all the "I just don't understand"s
And fix this your own way without others help.
You need to help yourself.

I've said it before and I'll say it again.
I love you all, I do.
You are some of my very close friends.
So why do you put each other through this?
You need to mend this the best you can,
And you need to do it now.
