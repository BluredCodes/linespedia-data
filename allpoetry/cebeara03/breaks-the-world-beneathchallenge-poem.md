She sits quietly 
With her rasberry lips 
Glowing from the melancholy sun 
Pain surrounds her. 
As I stare into her gorgeous eyes 
I see that she holds 
Onto nothing but despair. 
It seems that rusting hate 
Lives where her heart lies. 


The gray tint 
Of this lonely girl's finger 
Shines adoringly 
As she turns the pages 
Of that cheap paper book. 

Perhaps the feelings 
Inside the memory-bound book, 
The naked revaltaions 
Of her long lost love, 
Are just too much to bear. 

She whispers softly 
"These hypnotic lullabies 
You sing me dear, 
Hold not truth. 
Why must you torture me more? 
It's hurts enough without your touch.." 

I feel 
She has this worry 
That forever pangs her heart 
She knows not why 
She feels this way 
Or why her heart breaks so 
Somewhere there are answers 
But will she ever know? 

Questions swarm idly in her mind 
Fear rips at her heart 
Then a single tear falls from her cheek 
And into a thousand pieces... 
Breaks the world beneath.
