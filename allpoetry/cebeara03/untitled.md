It seems the entire world
Was aware of my love for you, except for me.
Or did I know it and just tried to deny it?
For fear of hurt from the look of rejection
In your wonderful eyes
Yes, fear kept that love hidden
Deep in the trenches of my heart
Ignorance fooled me and made me believe
That I was actually kepping it a secret.
All the while, the world was light-heartidly
Waiting for me to confess
Which makes me wonder...
Did you know, my love, beforehand
That I loved only you?
