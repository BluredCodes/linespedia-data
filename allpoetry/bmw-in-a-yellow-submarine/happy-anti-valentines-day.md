Walking through the halls of school 
Most people in red and happy and in the lovie sprit
But me dressed in all black 
Valentines???? what a stupid holiday i think to myself! 
Everyone is so obsessed with one day 
That you show your love to you bf/gf(if u have one) 
When you should be doing that everyday of the year not just Feb 14th!!!!
Why is every one obsessed about this one day of the year?????
I sit in a class room of people in red and me in all black
What goes through my head......
Why does everyone make a big obsession about a holiday a card company made up just to sell more cards durning the year?


well thats all i have 4 now more later

HAPPY ANTI-VALENTINES DAY!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!
