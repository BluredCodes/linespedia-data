It's a simple thought so wonderful and grand
Embraced by all through out our great land.
We take one day to give thanks and share
Our love, hope, dreams and prayer.
We come together united by love 
For neighbors, friends, family and God above.

It's a journey of the heart and mind
Wishing peace to all mankind.
This day is heaven on earth
It's about our freedom to be, not our self worth.

Can you imagine an idea so bold? 
Where one day we can behold
That the whole world stops to pause
And give thanks for such a noble cause.
