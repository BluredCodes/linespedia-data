Depression......... My Friend comes and goes just like all my friends
Depression......... Scarry just like how i am an when i am depressed
Depression......... Conscumeing just like alcohol my resort
Depression......... Addicting just like drugs
Depression......... Different just like how i act when depressed
Depression......... Destructing just like i am when i am depressed destroying things for pleasure
Depression......... Hating just how i feel when i am depressed loathing everything
Depression......... Hurting just like what i want to do to myself
