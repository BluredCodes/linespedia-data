this world is screwed up
we have humans using eachother as bombs
we live in a world of war and hate
when were older what are we gonna teach our kids?
that it's not ok to fight when there is fighting all around us
and there is a war all because one stupid fucker decided to do a stupid thing
resulting in like 4,000 dead and who knows how many injured 
all because of one act of stupidness
what will that say to our kids?
now we ask our parents where they were when president regan was shot
now when we are older our kids will be asking us where we were and what we were doing when the planes struck
also another thing is if there is a god and he's supposted to be so great then why did all this shit happen?
we're suposted to believe he's so great and he can do anything.... well then why did't he stop the planes from flying?
in these types of cases where something like this happens it makes u think twice like is there accually a god?
what will that teach our kids to believe in?
what i wanna know is how are we suposted to raise our kids in this world of hate?
how are they gonna know whats right and wrong when we are telling them one thing and they are seeing another?

what are we suposted to have them learn?????
