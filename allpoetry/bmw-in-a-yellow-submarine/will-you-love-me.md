I wrote you a letter
Inside i asked you to love me
As i sit here waiting and wondering what you are going to say
The thoughts go through my head like i was stupid to send it
And why would anyone want to like me or even love me?
I'm to ugly and stupid for anyone to like me
Why should i even bother when i know you will never love me how i love you
I don't deserve your love yet i ask you for it
I don't deserve anyones love
I don't deserve anything that i have
I don't deserve the people who care about me 
Why should any one care about me???
Espechally you why did i ever think for a minute that i would ever have a chance with you?
I was stupid to think you would ever like me!!! 
Why??????
Why did i ever send it????
It was a mistake!!!
I shouldn't have sent it
I know what the anser will be
The answer will be.......

NO!!!!
