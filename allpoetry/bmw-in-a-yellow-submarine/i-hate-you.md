I hate you
I like nothing about you
All you do is use me
You don't really like me
Don't say you do and lie to me
Don't lie and say your my friend just do you can use me!
Don't think you'll be able to get away with it because you won't 
Your just greedy and only think of yourself 
Well learn this noone likes it
And I don't like you your just a selfish Bitch and a looser
So get out of my life
All you living in is a life of lies 
I hate you you selfish peice of shit
Get out of my life

I HATE YOU
