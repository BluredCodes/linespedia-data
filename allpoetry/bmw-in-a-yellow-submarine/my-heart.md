You riped out my heart and you steped on it
Not even caring if you hurt me
The only person you care about is youself
I had told you about my bf before you and how he hurt me
I asked you not to do that but you did
You took what you thought was the easyst way out and did it that way
Not even thinking about my feelings
Not even caring 
Making up lies to aviod talking to me making you friends do it
But then agian that is you!!!
Not caring about other people just youself
Why did i fever fall for you????
I knew in the end i was just going 2 get hurt but i still did it!!!
I fell for you and i fell real hard!!!
Everyone told me i'll get over you but i don't think so 
Eventually i will but not now!!!
I still care about you but i know you don't care about me
But why did you hve to take the most shallowest way out of our relshionship????
All i want to ask you is why did you do it?????

Bunny, thats all i want to know.
