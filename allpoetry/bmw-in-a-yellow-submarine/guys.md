Guys one minute they say they love you
Then their ex-girlfriend walks through the door
And you get dumped
Well all i have to say is that guys suck
And i am sick of getting played because thats all i ever get
Arn't i worthy of more then that?
Guys think it's ok to play girls 
Well boys you know what it's not ok
We HATE it
It's about one of the worst feelings in the world
And then getting dumped ontop of that is even worse!!!! 
Don't mess with our emotions because you never know when we will strike back
Guys...........


Thats about all i have to say right now except for all you guys who are the really nice sweet guys all i have to say to you is THANKS!!!!!!!!
