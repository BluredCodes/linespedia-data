I'm outside
And its about to rain
As odd as it sounds
As wierd as it sounds
This is how I feel
Like the weather
Its been cloudy all afternoon
The sun peeks through every now and then
But I know its going to rain
And oddly enough
I want it to rain
The rain keeps me inside
Away from the sun
which is good because
If I see the sunshine
I'll want it to stay sunny
But its inevitably
Eventually its gunna rain
It feels like its always raining
And no matter how hard I try
I cant make the sun come out

The thuds of thunder are my screems
Loud at first
And then they die down
Becasue no matter how loud the thunder is
Its the rain people focus on
It still thunders
Only every now and then
That lets you know the rain will fall soon
People aren't scared of it anymore
They used to be
Then they got used to how loud it is
How much it hurts

I try to ignore it now
When it isnt thundering
I can close my eyes
And pretend it wont rain soon
But the wind still blows
And the clouds look darker
So I have to go inside now
Oddly enough
I'd rather stay out here
