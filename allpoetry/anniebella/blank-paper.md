A weeping artist
Sat at her table
Brush in one hand
Colors in the other

She stared at
The blank paper
Wanting to say so mush
But not feeling she was able

In some strange way
That large piece of 
Expensive blank paper
Said more than she ever could
With her brushes and Color.
