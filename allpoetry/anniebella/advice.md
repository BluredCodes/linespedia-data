Love father.  Impress Mother.
One foot in front of the other.
Step lightly-not too fast.
Look...There it goes...Your life just passed.When i wrote this poem, I wasnt sure what it was s'pose to mean.  But I kept it anyway.  Then I let one of my best friends read it, and she knew.  She said that this poem is me in a nutshell.  And then i knew.  I looked back over it, and thats exactly what it is.  Now if I can just figure out that ending...
