Silk Pajamas
An A
An old person smile
Sleep
Last bell
Sunlight on my shoulders
A little brother's "I love you."
A B
Snow days
A handshake
A baby's coo
Laughter
Friends
Grandma's cooking
A good joke
Mom's cooking
An OK joke
Flowers
A passed-down quilt
Being hyper
An old upbeat song
No homework
Alone time
A baby smile
Dressing
Hello from a friend
Dressing down
Drawing
Open windows
Forgiveness
My room
My style
Stuffed animals
Giving a gift
A good book
The movies
Poetry
A soft pillow
A sunset
Roses
A picture of friends
A starry night
Butterflies
Seashells
Kittens
Birthday cards
Puppies
Love letters
Snow globes
Silk pajamasWe all need to pay more attention to the little things.
