I woke up this morning
And wished
That for one day
People would see me-
Not my hair
Not my complexion
Not my clothes-
Just see me.

I woke up this morning
And wished
That just today
People would hear me-
Not my nervous stutter
Not my quiet voice
Not my shy tone-
Just hear me

I woke up this morning
And wishes
That wished came true.
