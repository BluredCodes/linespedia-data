---
title: "Wear A Smile For Me"
writer: "anniebella"

I dont expect you to understand right now,
But I selfishly expect you to cope somehow.

And if you hate me for going away,
That's okay.  I'll love you anyway.

My purpose for leaving is not to hurt you.
Please understand its just something I have to do.

You have taught me more than anyone ever could,
And if I could stay and be happy I would.

You've shared with me good times and brought me through bad.
The time we spent togather was the best I ever had.

Know that this bond we share will never die.
We will be friends forever, you and I.

I'll still be here when you need me-
Whenever that time may be.

And if you could do just one thing while I go,
Wear a smile and let your happiness for me show.

Because leaving you wont be easy for me,
But were I go is were I have to be.
