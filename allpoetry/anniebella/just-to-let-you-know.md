Just to let you know
I turned seventeen this year
And ironicaly
It was the SAME day
I turned sixteen last year

Just to let you know
I got lots of preasents
Nice preasents and cards
Yours must have gotten lost 
In the mail

Just to let you know
I think I missed your call
While I was being
Taken out to celebrate
My seventeenth birthday

Just to let you know
I still stick up for you
When mom talks about
How irrisponsible 
You are

Just to let you know
If you did forget
(Even though I know you didnt)
Its not too late
To call
