title: "You are my Sunshine"

When I am sad,
You stop my tears,
make me glad,
And bring a song to my ears.

You are my sunshine, my only sunshine.

When I am wrong,
You tell me so.
You teach me lessons lifelong,
And everywhere with me they go.

You are my sunshine, my only sunshine.

When I am confused,
You push me to achieve.
Your timeless knowledge used-
You make me believe.

You are my sunshine, my only sunshine.

When I am lonely,
You are there for me.
Our time spent together is lovely.
You are the best company.

You are my sunshine, my only sunshine.

You are a friend, a shoulder, a place to fall-
Unlike any other.
But, most of all,
You are the greatest mother!I wrote this poem for my mother on her 41st B-day.  It doesn't seem like my usual style, but she loved it.
