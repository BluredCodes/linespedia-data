---
title: "Are You There???"
writer: "anniebella"
slug: "are-you-there"
source: "AllPoetry"

I need You.
Wont You tell me
It will all be okay,
And tell me 
I will find my way?

All I need is
You to tell me
What to do.
I will completely
Trust in You.

If You could just
Let me know
It will be alright,
My nightmare may ceace to grow
And i might sleep tonight.
