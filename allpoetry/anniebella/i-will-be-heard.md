---
title: "I Will Be Heard"
writer: "anniebella"

Listen to Me!
I will be heard!
I wont be shoved
Into a corner
And told to "Hush!"
I will be heard!
You cant give me
A little toy 
To Distract me
From my thoughts.
I will be heard!
I might not be
As old as you,
As pretty as her,
As smart as him,
But My thoughts 
Still count, and
I will be heard!
You wont shut me up
With your rude remarks
And awkward glances.
Why?...Because,
I will be heard!
If I'm gunna listen 
To your comments,
You will listen to mine!
I will be heard!
Don't think I'm gullable enough
To fall for your
Tricks to quiten me. 
I will be heard!
And, do realize
That the sooner 
you listen to me,
The sooner I'll shut-up.
