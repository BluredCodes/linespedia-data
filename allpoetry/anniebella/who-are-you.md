---
title: "Who Are You?"
writer: "anniebella"

I am impatient
But I am still waiting on you

I am confused
But I know that you are still there

I am unhappy
But joy overwhelms me when I think of you

I am not the smartest
But I know what must be known

I am...
             What ever you want me to be.You can look at this as a love poem, but when I wrote it, I was thinking about it more as a spiritual poem.
