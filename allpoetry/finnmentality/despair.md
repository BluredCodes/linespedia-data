the quiet eats
inside my skull,
fingers tapping gently,
no noise, no noise,
help me,
no escape,
endless turmoil,
and the silence
is endless
and the screams
echo silently
telling everyone
telling no-one,
and my mind
gets eaten up
by the quiet,
slowly, slowly,
tapping gently
onwards, no stop,
my skull, onwards,
no stop, despair,
ever onwards, despair,
no stop.20.11.1995,
