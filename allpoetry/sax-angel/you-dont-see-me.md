title: "You Don't See Me"
writer: "sax angel"

You claim you're my friend.
You think that's enough.
You want to be pals.
You believe that we are.
But, you don't see me.

Please don't ask to be introduced to my friends.
You don't know how that hurts.
Why don't you look at me the way you do them?
Just don't be surprised when I disappear.
Because, you don't see me.

You never see the pain that you cause.
Can't you see the hurt in my eyes?
Able to see with the eyes of the heart?
Won't you see I'm a woman too?
No, you don't see me.

Will you help me feel special?
Won't you take me in your arms?
Please look at me.
Won't you love me?
Look! You don't see me.Musings of the "invisible" woman.
