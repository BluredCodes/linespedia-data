---
title: "Broken"
writer: "sax angel"
slug: "broken"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/46112-Broken-by-sax-angel"
---

Broken promises
Broken glass
Broken dreams that didn't last

Bruised and battered
Tired and worn
lacking strength to go alone

Helpless, hopeless
in despair
lacking even strength to care

Withering, fading
day by day
Silently, she slips away.
