From the first moment that I saw you, 
that is when I knew.
I had this happy feeling.
All my dreams would now come true.

You looked at me and smiled
with a smile so bright and sweet.
It happened in that instant.
My heart did skip a beat.

Now that we're together,
I feel that I've come home.
With you here beside me,
there's no more need to roam.

We have come together beautifully
to form this family.
I pray that for this lifetime
we'll live on happily.

And if life's roads should ever
lead us far apart, 
please hasten back to me,
for you will always have my heart.Written for a friend to give to his girlfriend this Valentine's Day.
