---
title: "All About You"
writer: "sax angel"

You said that you loved me,
but you never knew
the things that I felt.
It was all about you.

You promised you'd hold me
and always be true.
That was a lie.
It was all about you.

Did you appreciate me?
Did you give me my due?
I made all the sacrifices.
It was all about you.

I needed your support
to help me make it through.
You provided nothing.
It was all about you.

I tried to tell you
each time that I was blue.
You never listened.
It was all about you.

I supported you endlessly.
That was nothing new.
You took that for granted.
It was all about you.

I finally had enough
said that we were through.
The cheating was the final straw.
It was all about you.
