title: "Remembrances"

As you lie there tonight, remember
the sound of my name on your lips,
the taste of my kisses,
the feel of you body pressed against mine.

Do you ever stop and remember
the promises that we made,
the look of love in my eyes,
how right it felt to walk hand in hand in the pouring rain?

I will always remember
the smell of your cologne,
the feel of your kisses on my skin,
all the funny things that you did.

Will you remember?
