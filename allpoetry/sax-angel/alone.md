I saw a man today
while waiting in the Chick-Fil-A.
He reminded me of you.
He dressed and stood just like you do.

My heart dropped.
For a moment I thought it stopped.
Why does the mere thought of you
still break my heart in two?

I was sure our love would last.
To you, now it's just the past.
I wanted you to care.
You swore you'd always be there.

Don't you see what this does to me
to just be some nice memory?
No, because you are there
and you no longer care.

So, now I cry
Alone.
