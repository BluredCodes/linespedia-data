Slimy
Smelly
Pestering warts

Pointed
Darting 
Capturing tongue

Horn(y)ed
Hungry
Waiting for prey

Cold
Clammy
Unprincely beasts

Ugly
Hideous
Ribbit, croakFor some friends who like to collect frogs.  He he
