I got it on a Monday, and my heart stood still--
Boo-Hoo Ron Ron Ron
Boo-Hoo Ron Ron.
Arriving straight from Hell, it was my telephone bill--
Boo-Hoo Ron Ron Ron
Boo-Hoo Ron Ron.
Yes, my heart stood still
When I saw my telephone bill;
Now, I've got to mortgage my home--
Boo-Hoo Ron Ron Ron
Boo-Hoo Ron Ron!

I knew, then, that I'd stayed too long on the line--
Boo-Hoo Ron Ron Ron
Boo-Hoo Ron Ron.
Now, my bank account and I don't feel so fine--
Boo-Hoo Ron Ron Ron
Boo-Hoo Ron Ron.
Yes, I'd stayed too long on the line;
Now, I do not feel so fine;
And I've gotta mortgage my home--
Boo-Hoo Ron Ron Ron
Boo-Hoo Ron Ron!

No more calls from me until
I have paid this nasty bill
And the mortgage on my home--
Boo-Hoo Ron Ron Ron
Boo-Hoo Ron Ron;
Boo-Hoo Ron Ron Ron
Boo-Hoo Ron Ron!I wrote this in July of 1996, inspired by the eye-popping total of my most-recent phone bill!
