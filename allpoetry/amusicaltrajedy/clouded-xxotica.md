I know what I feel must be love,
But the words I say do not match a dove,
And if your beauty isn't a sin,
Its as if there were a heaven blooming on the horizon again,
And if space and time stopped leaving me alone,
I could think of a million and one things for you to be shown,
Because you are my angel,
My heart and soul,
A cute and sexy girl I will always want to love and know,
And when I awake from this crazy dream,
I awake to find you sleeping soft and soundly next to meThis was written after this girl that I dated for awhile and we started sleeping together...
