Broken bones,
Broken heart,
My flesh wounds are deepening,
But the doctors don't know where to start,
I didn't see it coming,
But I never said I could,
Nothing can sew back together my flesh and blood,
This time I know i'm as good as dead,
But i'll die knowing the sooner the better inside my head
