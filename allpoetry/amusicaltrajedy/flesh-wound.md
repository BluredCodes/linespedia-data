In my heart and in my mind,
I'll always love you knowing we are two of a kind,
But if what I feel isn't for real,
Then how will I know what you're doing to me isn't just a show,
But even then if it's so I want you to know,
That I really don't blame you if you want to go,
Because i'd soon rather be dead then be mislead,
And as I already know, 
I will always feel worthless without a direction to go,
But promise me you will never forget our love for eachother though1998,
