All of the words I say,
Mean nothing to you,
You say i'm fucked up,
But what can I do,
You stand to return the favor,
Back to me from you,
And in return,
You fuck ever other guy,
Right in front of me too,
But still remain confused,
So what do you do,
You lie, cheat, and steal,
Ending things between me and you2000,
