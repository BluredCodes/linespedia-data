title: "Ice Elemental"

Ice in the frozen state,Is without lust,Deep, and darkened,But with a passion for mystery,Which seems almost lost,But as you breathe in,You breathe out,But your breath never makes it out of your mouth,It freezes in mid air,Cracking, With the sound of crumbling icicles,In the cold arctic,As they hit the ground turning to dust
