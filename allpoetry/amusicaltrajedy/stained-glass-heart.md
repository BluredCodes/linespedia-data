title: "Stained-Glass Heart"
writer: "aMusicalTrajedy"

My heart no longer belongs to you,You've shattered it so many times,That I've taken it away from you,But you've longed to have me,And still say you love me,But I feel used,Because by you alone,That I've been abused,And it only breaks my heart,To know of this pain,But I feel you've cursed me,So no matter where I go,I won't let you touch me again
