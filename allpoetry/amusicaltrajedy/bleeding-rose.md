title: "Bleeding Rose"
writer: "aMusicalTrajedy"

Wipe away those tears, From those teary eyes, You've got nothing to fear, But fear itself, Drawing from your emotions inside, And if it seems this isn't for real, Just look above and beyond your afterthoughts, No not suicide, But believe in yourself, Because you are as beautiful as a rose, Bleeding on the outside
