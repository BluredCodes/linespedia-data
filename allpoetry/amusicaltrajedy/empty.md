I just want to die,I've figured it out,But I don't know why,Maybe i'll jump through a window to see if I can fly,And I don't really care if anyone knows I want to die,Sometimes I think i'll be better off then alive,I don't care if I am missed,Because i'm all alone,So whether it be falling, hit by a car, or eaten by dogs mauling,I just want to die1999,
