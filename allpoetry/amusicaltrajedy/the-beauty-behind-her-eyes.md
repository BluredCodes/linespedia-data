I stare into her dark, devilish eyes and i'm lost,Its as if i'm staring at my reflection though i'm not,I only see myself through a long darkening frost,And only then its something that I might see,But then I stare through them seeing her eyes shiny gloss,Of her beauty, its outta sight,And those daring ways, shes got me right where she wants me,I find it a turn on for several minutes, hours, and even days,I awake and snap out of it,And shes standing beside me saying, ' hun, are you ok? 'And I reply with a simple, ' yes! I love you! take me away! '2000, This was written about another girl I dated for awhile...
