My heart is safely locked up for you,
Broken bones and shattered ribs will simply not due,
But it'll never be broken down,
Or maybe torn apart,
No matter how many times someone wants us to give up,
Because you'll always be my start,
So when you turn that key,
It ignites my heart,
Lighting that eternal flame,
Because I love you together forever or apartthis was written about a girl who I dated for awhile just recently and then lost touch with because i don't where she is anymore or what happened...
