My heart is filled with love for you,
But what I fear I have to say,
Is i'm afraid of losing you,
To ciggaretttes, another guy, or even worse,
I feel its my own fault,
And that I have been infected by this curse,
I cuss, I screw, and I swear all day,
But nothing helps,
It just reminds me more of you from that first day,
Your long flowing hair,
That kiss of a rose,
And those beautiful eyes,
I would just hate to lose you,
It would cause me to die,
And if I had the chance i'd do it again,
I would even take a slegehammer to my playstation,
If it meant it was you I could win,
But I really do hope you'll want to stay in my heart,
And stay my favorite girl till death do us part1996, Somehow I foresaw this event. This actually happened 4 years later after I had dated this girl for 2 years...
