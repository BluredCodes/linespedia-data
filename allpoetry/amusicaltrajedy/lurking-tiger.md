As my hand caresses her face,
With a touch to each cheek,
She smiles from ear to ear,
With a sense of small pride,
She then gains some confidence,
That must learn to control,
So she can abide,
And in her world, 
There isn't a place, 
That her mind's eye could hide,
As i'd just find it with another kiss,
Before I shed a tear of love from eye to eye,
And then with a flick wrist,
They'd gentley be wrapped at her waist
Followed by sublte movements of my tongue,
She'd then be ready and waiting,
