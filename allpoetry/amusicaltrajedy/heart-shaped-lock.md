title: "Heart Shaped Lock"
writer: "aMusicalTrajedy"

With a kiss,
And you're stuck in my heart,
Safely locked away,
But stored in my heart,
So when you remember your love for me,
I know its not to far apart,
And when you're ready,
You come back to me,
To unlock my heart,
So I turn the key,
But you still turn my heart2001,
