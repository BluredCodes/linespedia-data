Why must I always chase you,
Is there something I'm missing,
Or am I just not seeing it from your point of view,
It's like i'm chasing myself,
But in turn it's not you,
I just only look back but I don't see you,
Instead i'm chasing myself for a girl I once knew1999, This was written after the same girl I had dated for two years and we started having relationship problems when we were realizing how different we really were from each other...
