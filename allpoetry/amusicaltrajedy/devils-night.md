A spark,
Followed by a sudden flash of light,
Darkness covers all,
As it presses on,
Smothering all stading within day,
Night by night,

All I see when I look up now,
Is black by day,
Nothing but eternal night,
I often take the time to look around,
But see nothing more,
As it creeps closer and closer,
I prepare for a fright,

Tis my own death at this wish,
But i'll still put up one hell of a fight,
Look out though someone screams,
As they dodge, weaving in and out to save me from tonight,
And so I live once more in fear,
Dreading that I know I can't last much long each night,

Given in once more,
To the darkened powers of chaos, destruction, and fright,
We all fear this devouring evil,
As it haunts us each year around halloween,
