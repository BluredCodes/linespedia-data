If love is such a wonderful thing,Why does it bring so much pain,At the cost of hurt,It just kinda sneaks up on you,Smacks you around a bit,With a good kick in the butt,And when it rubs your face in it more,As you get thrown in the mud,Now you feel washed up,Covered in nothing but soot and dirt,But if all this anger and pain is so true,I've got one last question,Why is it forever love we persue,So it can last longer in our heart,Or is there more to it,Then that of petty greivences,With all this pain foretold,Why must we continue spending time on love and hate,To flirt
