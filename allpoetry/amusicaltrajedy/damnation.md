Out of all the things I do for you,
You say I'm damned to hell,
Condemning me to a fiery death,
As you throw my body into the abyss below,
You then scream for me of what you hate,
You even tell me,
I'm driving you to suicidal tendencies,
As it turns your mind inside out,
Its your hate thats driving you crazy
