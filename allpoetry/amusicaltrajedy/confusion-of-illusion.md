Dreams are the source of my pain,
I feel as if i'm just playing another damn video game,
A world within its own self,
The graphics change,
The surroundings suck,
I hate this dumb game,
But I can't get enough,
Its just another prison for my own mind,
A head game to set me up for a fall,
And dive plummeting to my death,
Hiding from all,
This is my confusing thats killing me now,
But when I awake from my own dream,
I know it's just an illusion somehowthey say Life is just a dream on the way to death...
