My thoughts of me and you,
Are no longer doubts of one but two,
Actually not even that,
Its more like a few,
Only for me to say I love you,
Means nothingness,
When i'm tearing my heart out too,
So don't play these games with me,
Because the truth is,
I'm doing it for you,
And thats why no matter what you say or do,
I'll always come carrying back a shattered point of view,
Because I want to be with you,
But alls I ask of is for you to be careful,
Or it might cost you me too1997,
