And my heart fades,
As my time unwinds,
I sit here thinking to myself nothing,
While I get left behind,
But I can't sleep,
I can't concentrate,
And my lifestyle is what I contemplate,
Because girl I love you,
Our bonds are so great,
But what I don't know,
Is how much more I can take,
Am I outta my mind,
Or am I just a lost cause,
Whose lost for time2001,
