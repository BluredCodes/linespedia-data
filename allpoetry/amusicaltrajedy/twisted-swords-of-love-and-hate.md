Twisted swords of love and hate,
There is no other force at work now except fate,
A knife shatters,
But I hear no cry,
Except a song of birds with no lullaby,
And for this I know i've spilled my blood,
But I don't know how much more I could,
A river turns red as I lie there still,
But time stops at my will,
And as I lie there half a wake,
I think of a distant time and my mistake,
A portal opens and the door is shown,
But instead I am left alone,
To wonder this earth for countless years,
But my time does not fade,
It just dissappears1998, Severe deppression...
