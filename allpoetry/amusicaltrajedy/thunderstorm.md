Lighting flashes,
And the thunder strikes,
I still hear nothing,
No sound,
Not even through the blackened night skies,
Just that of a broken heart,
My living pain,
The skies grow cold quickly,
With each nights drop of rain,
And as my body dies,
I fade away,
My soul travels back to the land of the living,
Awaiting to unfold a better day1995,
