title: "Break-Down"

Inside myself I feel nothing but anger with hurt,
Simply because our relationship has been charred to a crisp,
And the bridge between us is condemned to be burnt,
But the slightest whisp of wind could break it,
And no matter how hard we try,
I don't think we'll make it,
Because this rusty old thing is reduced to dust,
As we jump up and down on it,
We're only losing our trust1999,
