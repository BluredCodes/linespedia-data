title: "Eternal Flame"

With a spark,
And my heart ignites,
My hate goes up in smoke,
While my flames of passion rise,
Standing lit forever, 
Remaining lively in front of you alike,

And as you leave me with a kiss,
You can say my anger is long gone,
When the clouds come out,
It is the sound of my sadness,
As it pours down rain,

But even if you could turn off the gas to my burning heart,
Your passion and love for me,
Will always set me ablaze,
Rekindling my ever-lasting eternal flame
