title: "Vacancy Heart"
writer: "aMusicalTrajedy"

Pain is my sorrow,
Long forgotten within,
Screaming to get out,
But locked inside,
Tied up,
Chain mounted to a wall,
That's held by two single pins,

You can feel my heart pounding,
As my soul screams with deprivation,
From my depths again,
When you find nothing inside my head,
But a vacant lot,
Where corruption and greed lurks,
Jumping at my frozen heart,
Eating away at my bleeding insides within,

From within,
Now this urge wants out,
As you remain my last hope,
Even though through morse regrets,
I know I've done you wrong,
And so I can never forgive myself,
Now that I've have had this taste of your doubt
