Darkness sheds no light,Sparing no good,Unleashing it's dreaded evil,Reaching out with it's deadly grasp,Taking foothold upon the land,And so as the story unfolds,Light doesn't steps in,But won't stand aside,As it wields it's sword and shield in hand,This hapless battle wages on,Decimating all in its path,Dispite the innocent cries and screams,Now this all seems wast within evils band,But as good fights evil...No lives can be spared...Justice for the living...Darkness for the dwelling...Corruption feeding within...And innocense lost...No matter how much good vanquishes evil,Evil remains frozen in time,Dormant, Waiting for a lost soul,To be cursed for it's own selfish costI was playing Dark Angel for Playstation 2 when the idea for this poem came to me...I had been killing and killing, trying to vanquish evil, but still it kept at it!
