As my words change,
My mind fades,
And my thoughts one by one are rearranged,
But as we separate,
You're control over my heart fails,
Going out of range,
Things between me and you then never remain sane,
But even then though,
I say I love you now too,
You tell me thats what I get,
Sending me to hell,
Because you don't believe my love for you is true,
And thats why we tend to argue,
I then fall apart,
As i'm being sucked inside out by my blackhole heart2000,
