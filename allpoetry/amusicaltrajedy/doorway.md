My heart,
And my mind,
I've shown you my door,
Its open,
But never closes,
And when you step through,
I can feel your loving touch,
As I feel the touch of your shoe,
Its the very essance of my want true love,
That being you,
So heres some love,
And a hug and a kiss for us too...
