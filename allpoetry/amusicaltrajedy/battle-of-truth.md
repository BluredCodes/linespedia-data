---
title: "Battle of Truth"
writer: "aMusicalTrajedy"
slug: "battle-of-truth"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/23777-Battle-of-Truth-by-aMusicalTrajedy"
---

I've got this pain,
But it hurts inside,
It's the feeling,
Of emotions gone wrong,
And I hear the same old warcry,
As it plays its song,
But I can't make sense of it,
As I traverse the endless battlefields,
I've been shot, stabbed, and wounded,
As my blood comes pouring out along,
I wish everyone could care for me,
Before I say so long
