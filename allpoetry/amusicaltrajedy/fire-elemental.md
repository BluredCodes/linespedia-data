---
title: "Fire Elemental"
writer: "aMusicalTrajedy"

Fire is the flame of love,Burning passion,And a hearts content,For desire,But one can think not to ask of the other,Though they can't be touched,Until they're in the other ones heart,And each others minds,With a burning flame of passionIgnited by desire,Heart and soul connected,Burning flames intertwined,A what was once lost love,Condemned to a new find,As these two eternal flames reunite,Remember one thing,Passion is grand,But love is divine
