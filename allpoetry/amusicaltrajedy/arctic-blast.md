Darkened Days,
When Fire was lost,
We were swept away, 
In the depths of the cold arctic,
By a chilling frost,

Our eyes melted,
With our line of sight in the cold,
And as each single tear we shed froze,
It shattered upon the ground spreading in rows,

And for that we share our piece of mind,
Today was chilling,
But for tomorrows adventure, 
I hope we'll be fine,
So that we won't be stripped of our warmth, 
As to what heat we've managed to keep hidden,
Holding it within our iced over bodies,
Keeping it from the cold
