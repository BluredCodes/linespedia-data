Ashes to ashes,
Dust to dust,
I'm searching for something in a girl,
But it isn't lust,
I sit and think,
And then start to wonder,
But then maybe again,
I'm still alone by myself so I hear no thunder,
But there is something in a girl I need,
And no of course it wouldnt be greed,
Or perhaps it's something I may seem,
But in this dream I had, 
Something told me that it wasn't just lust,
Because in my heart I know it's gotta be a definate yes and a must1998, Written when I was feeling lonely...still do occasionally
