title: "Dis-guarded Angel"

My minds made up,I don't care how,I wish I could smell you,And feel your touch,But I just want to give up,As it's painful now,And I don't care what's gone wrong,In the past,Because I'll love you forever long,Even when it hurts,And I'd like to just end it somehow,But that would just make things worse,Then if I can't have you,I'd like to think I'll try,Until you've understood,What you mean to me,Since you won't let me have you now
