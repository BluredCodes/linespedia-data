Skys are blinded,
When clouds spread apart,
As the rain falls down,
Earth moves and sets part,
But when it separates,
The boundries of time dismay,
And the Faultlines of a planets destruction,
Seals its cracks and begins to fade away,
And ss time splits,
It separates in two,
As life is created by the heavens 
With an astounding view,
And with it comes unearthed dimesions,
And new galaxies,
The stars are born,
Creating the sun and a new moon, 
When the planets align as they may,
Then in the bright sky lit heavens,
The raining falls down,
As it crys with joy for this day
