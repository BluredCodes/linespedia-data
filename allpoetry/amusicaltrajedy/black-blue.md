title: "Black & Blue"

Black and blue is the color of my heart,
After you've beaten me up severely from the start,
But the pain is so unbearable,
That nothing can fix it,
And so my livelyness is crumbling as I fall apart,
My thoughts and drams are shattered,
As we immerse them into one,
But you don't care,
Because you're pulling the trigger,
As the bullet comes out the barrel,
Through the heart and into my head from the gun1999,
