I love you,
You're my butterfly,
You're sent from above,
When I get hit by your hammer,
So I now see nothing but stars of love,
And when your looks become stunning beauty,
You give me this sensationable feeling,
As I fly high to be with you,
Like i'm trippin, 
I'm floating through the clouds in the sky with you,
But I think i'll need a bigger net to catch you,
Cause if I don't,
I won't feel alive,
But I know i've just gotta have you,
And that at least I tried,
Because i'll then know that I missed out,
As you end up long gone waving bye,
So come back to me my sweetheart,
Let me catch you my butterflythis was written about this girl named Diedre that i've known for about 3 years now. I always call her my butterfly because she has touched my heart in ways no one else could come close to...and to me...that is something real special! 8o)
