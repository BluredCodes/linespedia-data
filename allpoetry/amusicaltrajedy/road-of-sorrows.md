My thoughts of you,
Are being erased slowly,
As we break in two,
And of all things,
I know i'm losing you,
But you continue to remind me,
Of how you want me to be a more romantic guy for you,
And that you want someone who doesnt even think,
Hes just gotta worship you,
But what you don't realize,
Is that i'm ripping my heart out,
And throwing it on the floor for you,
But it's killing me,
So I must go now,
Saying my goodbyes,
I bid you adue1999,
