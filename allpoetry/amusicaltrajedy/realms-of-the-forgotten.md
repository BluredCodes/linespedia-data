title: "Realms of the Forgotten"

These walls,As they're torn down,My heart's set ablaze,From the fires of hell,To the depths within,A prison for my mind,You ask is a definite no,Unleashing unto myself,My own dragon,A coerced version unto thyself,Equaling massive destruction,And as my heart settles unto the gates above,For no one into my heart,Do I share my mind with desecrated love,But with myself for now,Until someone can save me from myself,With equal understanding powers,That do not defy heaven, hell, And earth itself above
