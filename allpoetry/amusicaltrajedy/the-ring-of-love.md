title: "the Ring of Love"

My flower,My rose,A symbol of beauty, and everlasting love,Send to me by the gods themselves,From the stars I see above,Because space-time is a beautiful thing,Even if each enchanted adventure,Is a depicted plot,To prick the king with a poisoned flowers thorn of hate,To steal his ring,Leaving his knight to fulfill his quest,And capture the rose of life and love,To hand over to his new bride,This old magical ring,
