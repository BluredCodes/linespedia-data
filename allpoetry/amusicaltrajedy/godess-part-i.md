My godess,
An angel within,
Her heart,
My soul,
The two combined,doubled,tripled,and folded again,
But riddle me this if I don't know,
Is she beautiful,
Is she fit for a queen,
Yes,
It's inseparable,
The fire in her eyes,
A beauty from above,
Now I know this must be love,
But wait,
I feel different now,
Maybe lesser of a being,
But stay tooned,
For I am the to be continued...,
