My heart and soul are intertwined,
A passion for love that's undefined,
With a prison of undying love for my mind,
My heart and soul just can't compare,
And sometimes I wonder if you even care,
I even wonder whats more important for you to do,
I've had dreams and nightmares even,
Where I need weapons to protect myself from you,
But I am gunned down,
And instead I die,
I then find myself swimming in a lake of red love,
But then again maybe i'm really drowning, 
As a view myself floating looking back down from above,
All because I believe in fake love1999, A girl I dated broke my heart...
