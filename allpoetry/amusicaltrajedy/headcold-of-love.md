title: "HeadCold of Love"
writer: "aMusicalTrajedy"

You fancy these words I say,
As I cough them out 2 by 2,
But your really in awe and mystery,
As you're emotions show the same way,
As I sneeze them achoo,
Speaking with Love, hope, and anger,
Are just but one in a few,
And as I start to feel sick,
You begin to take notice,
When I wander about,
And sing my song of praise,
As I share my love for you,
