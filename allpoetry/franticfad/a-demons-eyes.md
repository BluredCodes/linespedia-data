title: "A Demon's Eyes"
writer: "FranticFad"

A harsh word, a sullen look
Like a hammer to my heart
Frozen shards of brilliant loss
A summer’s hope lies torn,
Forever lost
Withering before my cold feet

Gleaming demon eyes
Flashing ruby anger
Only they stared back at me
From the back of your fleeing car
Never you, never, but never you

My sorrowed fingers claw
The frozen ground, unyielding
My burning eyes turn upward
To heaven, un-answering

The demon’s breath roils
A poison cloud of soft disdain
Purring evilly at the corner

The eyes are gone
Night’s silence returns

My love has fled
