title: "Whispers"

Soft, quiet voice, like the breath of a fawn
An arrow of bright hope, she strides ever on
She speaks of small things, in her eyes a great wonder

Kittens and puppies, strings and small things
Whispers of magical places and dreams

Bright eyes ever searching, she welcomes it all
Her small heart a great giant, if not very tall
Each day a new journey with eyes lit like fire

Kittens and puppies, strings and small things
Whispers of magical places and dreams

Ever questing, voracious, hungry young mind
She gathers her life and leaves nothing behind
No object, no process too mundane to amaze

Kittens and puppies, strings and small things
Whispers of magical places and dreams

Magical Mary, I love you forever
