---
title: "As you sleep"
writer: "FranticFad"

The sun broke in scattered, pale shafts
Casting wan, shy light across your sleeping face
I drew a breath and looked upon you
Lost, as always, in the glory of my need for you

I feared the spell might break
A slight noise or sudden draft that might awaken you
A soft moan, a slight sigh
Wakefulness comes upon you, teased by the soft morning light

Your heavy lashes rise across your enchanting hazel eyes
Your gaze falls upon me, and drops
Taking with it my heart, as I see the contempt again
Illusions scatter like frightened spirits as you turn away

My chest pulls tight, clasping my sore heart
I draw a slow, deep breath, unwilling to display the edge of pain
How can you no longer even care that I hurt
How can you no longer even hurt that I care

Did you love me, I wonder
Those long ago days, in our lost, lamented youth
The smiles, the tender words, were they plumbed from your heart
Or were they instead, only pleasant gestures of the fire of youth

A day of pain, of fear that I will tread athwart your anger
A day of shame where I speak of caring that happens no more
A day lost to time, where we speak not at all, and share even less
I long for the night, when you will sleep again

And I watch, as you breath slowly in the gloom of our giant, cold bed
Laying a soft hand upon your shoulder, careful not to awaken you
I let myself sleep and I lie as I go
Loving you
Believing you might still know how
Might still wish
Might, if only just now
Love me too
