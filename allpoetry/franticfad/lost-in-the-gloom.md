---
title: "Lost in the Gloom"
writer: "FranticFad"
slug: "lost-in-the-gloom"
source: "AllPoetry"

Shattered into crystal light
A piercing howl splits the night
It speaks of pain, of hunger and need
It crys for comfort and solice, a plead
Trembling voice, a call of great fright
Her driving need a thing of strange might

Trembling hands grasp the cold rail
Her dark eyes glitter as she suppresses a wail
Gazing off into the pale twilight gloom
Afraid to re-enter that cold, empty room
Her wan, sallow features tell a sad tale
The home that she loved is now but a jail

The pulse pounds in her ears, so fast and so hot
All of her life is now only one thought
How could she put so much into that fool
He never quite loved her, she was only his tool
No pretty gifts could hide the deep rot
Promisses and lies for a love that was not

He as never really here, her broken heart cries
Never my love or reason she lies
Dressed in smooth satins, fragrance and lace
Her features are drawn, tangle hair hides her face
Broken trust and harsh words have shattered the ties
The abused bit of her heart stammers, and dies
