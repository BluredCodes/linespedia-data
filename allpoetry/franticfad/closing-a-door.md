Thrashing and twisting, fighting for life
Longing for morning, another day in the strife
Craving, denying, reaching out for the light
Burning and livid, driven mad by the night

My strength bleeds away as I sink to the floor
Lost and confused I stared hard at the door
My eyes wide in rage, tightened against tears
Her harsh, angry voice had awakened all of my fearsIt's a memory from my seperation... (blech) .. and I am trying to learn how to capture my feelings in rhyme. I have a fair sense of rythym, but am a little awkward with this.. :P
