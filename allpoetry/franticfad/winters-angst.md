title: "Winter's Angst"

Called from above
Pulled hard aside
Reason and faith
They've all but now died.

Who wrote this harsh play?
Who demanded the day?
I toil and I strive,
in slim hope that I may?

Love lives alone in a tower in my heart,
never another to do her own part.
