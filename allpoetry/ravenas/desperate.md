-:WORK IN PROGRESS:-

Lying twisted, torn
A piece of here and there 
Is everywhere, is nowhere- to me
Burning, hollow, singed
All of the things I had
And what I have is nothing now.
…I don’t know what to say
My hollow heart bleeds for you…this is like... a fragment of an emotion that partly emerged before being stifled and stuffed back in those recesses i hide those undesireable emotions in...
