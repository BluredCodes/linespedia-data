---
title: "The Little Me"
writer: "Ravenas"
slug: "the-little-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/28999-The-Little-Me-by-Ravenas"
---

When I look at you
i hate you.
You are so strong
you've grown so cold to love,
I hate you because
when i look at you, 
i see failure,
You're no good.
All you do is no enough
and you know that.
You know the pain,
and you know the abuse.

I hate you because to save yourself
you forgot the little me,
you forgot how to heal my wounds.

when i look at youi know you will never leave,
i know you'll live another day,
i know that you'll wake up again,
And i'll have to see your ugly face,
and you're the only one who see's the real me.
You're the only one i can talk to,
because you can't argue back,
and you can't say no

I hate you because,
on every peice of your shattered self,
Is a dirty layer of suffocating residue,
left behind by those who have carried you
and dropped you like yesterdays garbage,
That's what i believe you are.
