Sex
             Deception
Pleasure
                Pain

Your love has stolen something precious from me
In return i'm going to steal your life from you
 Spurt by Spurt
I'm going to break you down
 Moan by Moan
My lips are the sugar coating 
 on poison pills
Each dose of which flows black in your veins,
 Killing your heart
 Numbing your mind
Feel the heat of my sin as you caress my skin
Embracing your sweet death

"I Love you, I'll Kill you."-EnigmaThis one kept me up last night, wouldn't let me rest till i had released the feeling in some form. Words are like my tears.
