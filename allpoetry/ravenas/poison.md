---
title: "Poison"
writer: "Ravenas"
slug: "poison"

I’m floating in inky darkness
Unable to endure
The weight of the world bears down on me
And I sink
Sink
The darkness swallows me. 

I have hit the bottom now.
I cannot get any lower
Can’t get any closer to death.
The darkness seeps into me
Poisons me
Permeates my body’s functions
My heart pumps this darkness,
It courses through my veins.
My brain thinks darkness,
It controls my every action.
I can no longer feel,
There is only an insidious inhuman sensation. 
It brings a sense of relief
I no longer have to suffer
I no longer have to bear the curse
Of being me, the stigma of this soul I house. 
I just have to be
One with the darkness.
