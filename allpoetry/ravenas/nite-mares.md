---
title: "Nite Mares"
writer: "Ravenas"
slug: "nite-mares"

Coal black
Creatures of the night
Running, gleaming

Bring the dream
Tossing, turning
Recipient of the dream
Waking, screaming
In a cold sweat

Wide eyes.
Searching, feeling
Trying to find, trying to hold,
There is nothing there…

Untameable, wild
Perpetual, immaterial
Servants of evil
Terrors of mortals
Till the sun comes and banishes them until dusk comes again
Night mares
