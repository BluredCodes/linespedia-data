You reached out for me
But I was afraid your embrace would break me
~I am so fragile~
So I flew away
And now I am dying
You are not there to save me.
I just want you to understand,
Why love doesn’t always return
~Don’t let your butterflies fly away~Again, another of my poems which is uncharacteristic of my overall style, and unpolished because of that fact.
