---
title: "End of the Road"
writer: "Ravenas"
slug: "end-of-the-road"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/29933-End-of-the-Road-by-Ravenas"
---

A silent spirit hovers over the carnage
13 miles down the highway
observing what lay beneath
seven lives
Carelessly thrown on the pavement
Seven Dreams 
Mangled, in crumpled heaps
Seven people 
who will be missed dearly.
A silent spirit watches the shapes below
in a desperate attempt to save
what was sacrificed to a drink
The spirit sees it's best friend bleeding
Their life together has come to a close
and the spirit knows
18 years was not enough time
