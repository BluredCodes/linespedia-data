---
title: "The Last Dance"
writer: "Ravenas"

Now you've gone, we've danced our last dance
No more of this blissful romance.
The song fades slowly in the mind.
The loving memories of all the times you've been kind.

But there you went, being a fool,
Knowing that I could never stand without you
You took your chances, you played your games,
You destroyed everything you had, who's to blame.

See how much you've sacrificed for a few minutes of fun,
Now take a step back and look a yourself hun,
You're broken, you're bruised,
A slave to all those drugs you use.

So now it's all over, you said your goodbyes,
And you don't know how I've cried.
You played russian roulet behind the wheel and lost
Not knowing how much a few drinks would cost.

So now you've gone, we've danced our last dance
No more of this blissfull romance.
The song fades slowly in the mund,
And I'll live forever knowing you're no longer mine.
