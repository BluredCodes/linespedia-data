title: "Saviour (non-religious)"

Lying helpless
Broken, mute
Feeling so small, fragile.

Touch me, make me new.
Give me back what was taken from me
By the merciless winds of change.

Or you could leave me here,
To suffer ~torment, pain, abject misery~
Subject to the maliciousness of the monsters
That hide behind inconspicuous camouflage…
And I will surely die.

~ My life depends on the whims of strangers~
