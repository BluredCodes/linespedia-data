One vs. World

The war rages on
Some battles lost
Some battles won

I am a soldier on the loosing side
I have ammo but no gun
I have no trench, just the burning dead to protect my hide

Time and time again I push myself forward
Into the thickness of the fray
Time and time again I am shot down
Horribly maimed I lay.

After much suffering I do die 
But my mind remembers my last wounds pain 
and am reborn… smaller, drained, fragile, 
Dispite this I drag myself back again

I fight for my motherland!
Beyond the opposition lies my peaceful home
I must defeat these intruders
That expelled me from my rightful place!
So I can one again live with myself in my paradise

Unfortunately,
I stand alone, one soldier
My fellow soldiers dead
Or fled
Or assimilated to the other side
There are no new recruits to aid me in my endeavour
