Angel
As you make your way to the paradise from which you came,
Let not the merciless winds of change break your wings.
Adversity does test us all (some more than others)
But what does not kill us makes us stronger
So we can soar higher when the sun turns his face our way.

Angel
Live with fire in your heart and questions in your mind
Seek to unravel the great mysteries of life
But let not the brutal truths bite you too deep
Wounds which do not heal will fester and poison
Perfect good needs absolute evil to be true.

Angel 
Be wary of the corrupted spirits that hide behind prefabricated masks.
Do not follow, nobody leads in this rat race.
Be true to yourself, kind and wise.
And pass the knowledge you gain unto others
I know I could have used this advice when I fell from my heaven.
