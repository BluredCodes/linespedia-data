title: "Silence Never Sleeps"

There's no lies,
yet no more truth.
All i see is blank decisions.
You will turn your back
until the day is gone.

Never holding
on to anyone's beliefs,
I am lost and lonely
stranded in this sea
with..

no one, nothing.
Not a word could open eyes.
I'm just unwanted.
I just wanted
to feel,
to breathe.
But feelings die

All my nerves are cut.
I see the light.
Slowly i will talk
until you know the visions
in my
collapsed little world
left without time.

I'm running with zero room.
I feel such a great depression.
You will always act
like yesterday was lost.
and I
never shouted
out any of my beliefs.
I am lost and lonely
anyone can see when there's

no one, nothing.
Not a word could open eyes.
I'm just unwanted.
I just wanted
to feel,
to breathe.
But feelings die

All my nerves are cut.
I see the light.
Slowly i will talk
until you know the visions
in my
collapsed little world
left without time.

All who push the silent walls
never went away.
Now i can hear their madness scream
as it came so silently.
We used to be so innocent.
All so innocent until we felt this world's...

Crash -
Downs -
Screams -

Silence never sleeps.

All my nerves are cut.
I see the light.
Slowly i will talk
until you know the visions
in my
collapsed little world
left without time.
