title: "Yet Another"
writer: "eihcir x"

I hear you loud and clear.
You made me scream
Deep inside i need relief.
I've let you down so many times
You see the sorrow in my eyes
I beg forgiveness.
So please let go
Let me lay here in the cold
I've tried to believe you
You lie to me
It's nothing new.
I feel this pain.
I only needed you inside
to know the pain i felt tonight
You deny the thought of change
There is no change.
It's all the same.
Another day of memory.
Sad to say
