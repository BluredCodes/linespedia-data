Modeled for breaking,
and suffering.
Insides frustrating
and draining me.
Paused still and aching
emotions ill.
Anticipating the end
of all this complication

bleeding
tear down
shoot through
the head

fiending
turn round
and watch
it shed

bottle and break
hold still
relax

take it
you give
for taking back.

Swallowed in sadness
lays innocense,
I shape my mood with
my tortured skin.
I've breathed through madness
I've given in
to what was killing me
and i'll
take the knife
Watch. It slits me.


bleeding
tear down
drip through.
Accept

fiending
turn round
and meet
the edge.

bottle and break
hold still
collapse!

waken
the blood
time soon
will pass!!!!!!

Wake the blood......

Soon the time will pass
