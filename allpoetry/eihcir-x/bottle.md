Sometimes i feel like a bottle
filled to the top.
With intense pressure building
i can only wait till either the
glass shatters or the top explodes off.........
But either way conclusions erupt.
The chaos that fills this bottle is
nothing more than a question
A question that eats away at what little
hope i have inside.
This question grows.......
and grows........
Without focus
all words and questions are forgotten.
And this explosive little question is
.... when will nothing matter?
