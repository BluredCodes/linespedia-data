Covered in darkness
leaving us speachless.
We pray for light.
Running from shadows
when help feels so reachless/
when so many are crying out

Waiting for answers
to all of the questions
that put is in tears.
The tv is on now
and feelings won't lessin
while channeled inside our fears.

We fell in this weakness.
Lost our way but we will find it in time.
With our hopes we are dreaming
to erase pain filled minds
to erase pain filled minds that hide.

Countless the faces
down in such horror.
No where to find life.
Broken and nameless,
crossing the water....
the smoke will never die.

Pictured on paper 
are strangers and families
giving each moment.
Keeping awake. Hurt.
Why were they buried?
Where is the closing end?

We fell in this weakness.
Lost our way but we will find it in time.
With our hopes we are dreaming
to erase pain filled minds
to erase pain filled minds that hide.

Changing eyes...
Changing thoughts...
for a better world to walk upon

Changing you.....
Changing me......
Changing all through memories.
