i slip away
to a thought you couldnt imagine
too ashamed to say
too horrible. way too tragic
these words
might break
this is
my maze
these words might take
a century
these words might make
eternity
when i wake up
it burdens me

LIES!!
FEAR!!!
HATE!!!!
CRIES!!
TORMENT!!!
PAIN!!!!
This is impossible
EVERYTHING DIES!!!

I will slit the throats
of every mother fucker
like you just woke up
and you choked on your own mother fucking life line
this place
this world
is mine!!!!

It's mine!!!unknown,
