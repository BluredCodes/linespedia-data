You tried to fight addictions.
Broken, the way you're livin'.
Don't bother with decisions.
Escape from your whole vision.
Don't try to turn this around.
It only brings the volume down.
You're scarred and it's too late now.
That voice inside is coming out.

All i want is
you to be better
but you humor me.
Don't give up
when all you need
is open eyes for you to see.

You try for comprehension
but ignorance is your extention
to step away, to make confessions
when will you learn its all in lesson
changing face to ugliness
pushing through the emptiness
taking chances, better yet
no more glances, rest your head

All i want is
you to be better
but you humor me.
Don't give up
when all you need
is open eyes for you to see.
