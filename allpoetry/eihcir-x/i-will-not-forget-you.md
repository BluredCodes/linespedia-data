title: "I Will Not Forget You"
writer: "eihcir x"

I will not forget you
You will always be with me
I will not forget you
You are there in dreams

Through the brand new doors
i don't know what comes tomorrow
but i know what's true, and
i don't need to wake in sorrow

I will not forget you
All your words remain in sleep
I will not forget you
You are clear to me

Through the brand new doors
i don't know what comes tomorrow
but i know what's true, and
i don't need to wake in sorrow

I will not forget you...
