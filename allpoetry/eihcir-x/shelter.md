---
title: "Shelter"
writer: "eihcir x"

In this place that you call home,
I spend everyday alone.
I try and put myself to sleep
to escape the pain
when i can't face reality.

And when i fall
to my knees
i'm forced to crawl
and it's killing me
and it's just not right
to feel this way
and it's just not right to feel

You never let me speak my mind
and i just want to turn back time
to show you why i'm so depressed
It doesn't really matter
in such a sheltered loneliness

And when i fall
to my knees
i'm forced to crawl
and it's killing me
and it's just not right
to feel this way
and it's just not right to feel
when i feel this way

I'm the only one
that you've left behind
and i come undone
as i start to cry
for what i've lost
i can see myself
and what you've done to me

I remember long ago
when life was perfect.
Now i'm cold
and maybe someday
you will see
all the choices that you've made
keep crashing down on me

And when i fall
to my knees
i'm forced to crawl
and it's killing me
and it's just not right
to feel this way
and it's just not right to feel
when i feel this wayunknown,
