title: "So Sad (far from home)"

Have i been so blinded
that i couldn't see between the lines?
With a voice so quiet
I sit and stare. I've never been heard.
So sad, but you don't really care.
It's way too late to head back home.
You turned your back and now i'm gone.

How can you deny me
of knowing that there's something better
than this frustration?
i'm paying for everything i know
So sad, and you don't really care.
It's way too late to head back home.
You turned your back and now i'm gone.

So sad, and you don't really care.
It's way too late to head back home.
You turned your back and now i'm gone.
