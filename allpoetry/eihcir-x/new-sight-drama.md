today i woke to find myself covered in every lie
A part of me has gone away. now i'm stuck to the sky
And as i day dream you all hate me for what is inside
for you i will die

tell me
who i am
will i find myself?
help me
understand
because i need your help
drown me in my fear
and bring me back again
can we still be friends
can we still be friends

lately i feel like i am an empty. fading into dust
i cannot breathe, and now i'm empty from my lack of trust
i'm so consumed by all the hatred and from my disgust.
This is what i love


tell me
who i am
will i find myself?
help me
understand
because i need your help
drown me in my fear
and bring me back again
can we still be friends
can we still be friends

Please cut me out
Back down to size
I'm lost inside.
Inside this mind
if i am dead
tell me what is life?
ill try to hide

so.......


tell me
who i am
will i find myself?
help me
understand
because i need your help
drown me in my fear
and bring me back again
can we still be friends
can we still be friends
