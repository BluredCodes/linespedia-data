---
title: "The Games You Play"
writer: "eihcir x"
slug: "the-games-you-play"

I gave you my best
and gained only lies
You lifted me up
and turned as i cried
All feelings were stretched.
You opened my mind,
then dropped me back down
to see your disguise

And these are the games that you play

From happiness
to loneliness
to emptiness
to nothing left

it's hard to vent
emotionless
....
and sadly now i understand

I showed you my space.
A world that you killed.
You filled me with love
but just to stand still
and promise me trust
while draining my life.
You shined like the sun
that blinded my sight.

And these are the games that you play
