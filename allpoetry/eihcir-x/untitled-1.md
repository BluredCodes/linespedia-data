i stare into your heart
and see my life inside the walls

i take myself in
a new direction
and then break as i fall

What can i say?
What can i do?
How can it be
I never knew

im standing behind you
away from everyone

So Don't look back
when there is nothing
I never said
that i was something
I need to feel
some way, somehow
give in to me
and then take it out!!

Now, im shaded in grey
without a doubt
im just a number
but i can't count
on myself anymore
so i shut you down
and shut the doors
that was the pain that i wore

i look into your soul
and put myself inside a hole

i get a little bit further
this is my life
I have no control

What can i say?
What can i do?
How can it be
I never knew

that i remind you
of the pain in everyone

So Don't take facts
right out of nothing
I never fought you
you're so disgusting
you need to heal
some way, somehow
so give in to me
and then take it out!!

Now, suffocated each day
without a doubt
im just a number
but i can't count
on your mind now anymore
so i shut you down
and then shut the mother fucking doors

tell me right now
well are you blind?
tell me right now
before i cut inside you
and then rip beneathe the skin
and bleed out your innocense.....
AND BLEED OUT MY INNOCENSE!!


What can i say?
What can i do?
How can it be
I never knew
Anything at all
but i still try
to put the thoughts behind my eyes
before im layed out to dieunknown,
