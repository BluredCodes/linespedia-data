---
title: "Closed Eyes"
writer: "eihcir x"
slug: "closed-eyes"
source: "AllPoetry"

so much
anger
strangers
look at me
they talk
they think
like i am just a freak
i was
like you
such a simple mind
with your closed eyes

i was
heartless
just like you
and i felt
emptiness
so confused
i was so depressed
and abused
but i can hold it in
just like you

my screams
take me
to that special place
silence
is good
but its not the same
all i could do
was hold myself in shame
i was your game

i was
heartless
just like you
and i felt
emptiness
so confused
i was so depressed
and abused
but i can hold it in
just like you

my scars
my tears
never felt so great
reminded
that
i just give and then take
i need you to feel
not to be afraid
please stay away

i was
heartless
just like you
and i felt
emptiness
so confused
i was so depressed
and abused
but i can hold it in
just like you
