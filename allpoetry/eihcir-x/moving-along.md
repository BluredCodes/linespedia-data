With everyday,
I move ahead
to find an answer for the hours
I have spent just thinking.
I try to find a light.
What holds me back just shatters.
And every second just keeps passing
and passing.

I'm moving along

I know there's problems up ahead.
They come in different forms
through hours that I spend in wonder.
I know i'll make it through
and someday i'll discover
the way that every second matters.
It matters.

I'm moving along

When everything is going
a little bit downhill,
remember this is just
a temporary problem to face.

I'm moving along
