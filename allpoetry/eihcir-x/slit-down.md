Look around,
I lay on the floor.
Shaking,
I can't take this anymore.
Inside - on my own.
Inside - no one knows

You've raped me.
You've made me
someone else.
You break me.
Look around.
Restrain me.
You're so proud.
I'm aching

You laugh in my face.
You love
watching as i'm struggling.
Gasping - i need air.
Everything is clear.

You've raped me.
You've made me
someone else.
You break me.
Look around.
Restrain me.
You're so proud.
I'm aching

You don't understand
the way
you just take the innocense
away, out of me.
Away. For your needs.

You've raped me.
You've made me
someone else.
You break me.
Look around.
Restrain me.
You're so proud.
I'm aching

I'm aching!!!!!
