---
title: "Cold Cell Factory (to mom)"
writer: "eihcir x"
slug: "cold-cell-factory-to-mom"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/4626-Cold-Cell-Factory--to-mom--by-eihcir-x"
---

condemned
inside
without a light
wasting away
as i wonder why
you could do this
to your only child
and runaway

I (i am not your son)
Lies ( i am not your son)
Die!!! ( i am not your son)
WHY!!!!!!!!?????

you
throw away
an innocent face
you filled that space
and turned it to hate
i am so cold
and so afraid
you runaway

I (i am not your son)
Lies ( i am not your son)
Die!!! ( i am not your son)
WHY!!!!!!!!?????

I am not as i seem
I am only so weak
I ask how can you be
so fucking hurtful towards me
and you turned just like that
and you will never come back
everything you had
is everything i lack
innocent and so sad
nothings left but the facts
and thats that
and thats that
and thats that

I (i am not your son)
Lies ( i am not your son)
Die!!! ( i am not your son)
WHY!!!!!!!!?????
