title: "Where is the sun?"
writer: "eihcir x"

Everyday i wake up to see the sun
but the sun wasn't here today.
I ask ... Was it on a break?
Did it stay out late?
Is it caught somewhere up in outer space?
And if i step outside will everything change
or will it just seem far away
and turn into something worse?
My hopes are all cursed.
I hope the sun comes to me.
I know that for some people this is just a dream
and they can just wait for rain.
Is it in selfishness to just want the sun
to make the rain go away.
