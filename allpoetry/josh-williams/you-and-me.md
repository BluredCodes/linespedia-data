---
title: "You and Me"
writer: "Josh Williams"
slug: "you-and-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/46-You-and-Me-by-Josh-Williams"
---

Can't remember why we fell apart from something made by destiny
Forever crying from my eyes, tears from you and tears from me
Are you thinking of me when he's not around
Or have you run me into the ground
Do you ever find yourself smiling and laughing at my memory
Do you ever find yourself thinking of you and of me
Is there a me in your you anymore
Or have you already closed the door
Because I know I want it more than life
And I hope you'll be my wife
Take you home to my bed for all eternity
Then there's you where there's a me
When the ages have made us into stars so bright
And our love draws us to it's finest night
I'll know I'll want you back more than ever before
Just when I'm knocking, will you answer the door
Now I'm hearing so loudly the call of eternity
Cause I know there's never a you where there's a me
