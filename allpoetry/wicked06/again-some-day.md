title: "~~~**Again Some Day**~~~"
writer: "wicked06"

Since we were kids, 
I've been wanting to tell you this. 
I love you as a friend, 
And I love you as a lover. 
I wanted to tell you last summer, 
Of my feelings for you. 
If I cant be with you, 
I wont know what to do. 
I want you by me side for the rest, 
Of my life. 

I knew we were meant to be, 
And I hope that you can see 
How much you mean to me. 
I knew this could ruin our friendship, 
But I thought that you should know. 
The feelings I have for you, 
Will all the suddenly show. 

You have always been my best friend 
And will be till the end. 
We've been through everything together 
And that's what made it better. 
You knew me more than anyone. 

When we were kids, 
We fantasized how life really is. 
And how life would be 
With just you and me. 

You have always made me laugh 
In times when I was blue. 
Now there's no one here to help me through this, 
And I just want you to know, 
You'll always be missed. 
Only the memories can get me through this. 

I stood by your side 
until the day u died, 
that's the promise that we made 
and ill see u again someday.
