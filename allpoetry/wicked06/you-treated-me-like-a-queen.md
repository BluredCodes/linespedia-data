title: "You Treated Me Like  A Queen"

I remember the first time we met. 
I looked into your eyes, 
And that reminded me of the pain I have inside. 
I thought that pain was hidden, 
And would never creep inside. 
The last time I felt this way, 
My heart was broken in a very bad way. 

Some part of my heart said to me, 
That you were the one and only person for me. 
I know you wouldn't treat me bad, 
And not even make me mad. 
Cause your that type of person, 
Who would treat me like a queen. 

The day you died, 
Weeks I sat and cried. 
I knew I wouldn't find someone just as caring as you. 
When I was blue, 
I know you knew that way to cure my sadness. 

Now that your gone, 
I feel so all alone. 
Now when I'm blue, 
The only thing I can do is just sit and think 
of how much I miss you. 
I stood by your casket, 
Feeling all alone. 
I thought of the day we both fell in love, 
You were like an angel from above. 
I will never love someone, 
As much as I loved you. 

Now I have noone to comfort me, 
In this time of need.
