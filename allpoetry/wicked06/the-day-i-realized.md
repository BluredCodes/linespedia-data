title: "~~*The Day I Realized*~~"

Everytime you walked in the room, 
My heart skipped a beat. 
Then I got to know you, 
And you were such a creep. 
How could you be that way? 
If you would have changed, 
I might have stayed with you. 
But all you did was play with me and those other girls. 

You didn't know how much I liked you, 
And you didn't realize 
What was in my eyes. 
How could you miss that love I had buried deep inside? 
I know I shouldn't cry 
Cause you weren't even worth my time. 

Everytime I looked in your deep, deep brown eyes, 
I thought that I could trust you, 
But that was just a lie. 

Now it's my time to shine, 
Cause all I did was hide. 
I thought you were my guide.
