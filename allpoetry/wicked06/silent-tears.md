title: "~~*Silent Tears*~~"

Silent tears 
Fall from my cheek, 
And in my room, 
I sit and weap. 

I long for a love 
that can never be missed. 
And my heart is yearning, 
For that one special kiss. 

I cannot stand 
The way I feel 
Cause I know my heart 
Will never heal. 

I will always remember, 
The way you held me in your arms, 
Cause with you, 
I felt no harm. 

I wish you wouldn't have died, 
Because I know how hard you tried. 
You fought your battle, 
And even though you lost. 
In my heart you'll stay forever, 
And your memory will never be lost.
