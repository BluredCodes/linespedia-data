title: "Phoenix Firebird"

Where is Phoenix firebird?
Have you called her name?
No, but I have searched throughout
Fire Flicker Flame

Have you asked the others
Where Phoenix has been today?
They were the ones who sent me to
Fire Flicker Flame

Maybe she is sleeping
Dreaming through the day
You know how time passes by in
Fire Flicker Flame

Yes, that may be so
But I worry all the same
It's not like her to go outside of
Fire Flicker Flame

Listen to the others cry
"We are all to blame!
We have chased her outside the realm of
Fire Flicker Flame!"

Ah, dear me don't despair
Go about your play
I see our Phoenix coming now from
Fire Flicker Flame

There you are, sweet firebird
But where've you been all day
Everyone has searched throughout
Fire Flicker Flame

Then everyone has failed to see
The lesson in my game
That all of you should go beyond
Fire Flicker Flame.

                 The End.
