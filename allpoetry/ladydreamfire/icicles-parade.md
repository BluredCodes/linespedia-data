---
title: "Icicle's parade"
writer: "ladydreamfire"
slug: "icicles-parade"

See the crystal gates
Displayed on sight for all
Now see my frozen love 
Encased within it's walls

Notice the marching icicles
Defending human heart
Sharp as pain, cold as ice
Keeping us apart

Your light is not invited
Until I know for sure
If that light is lying
Or if it's simply pure

Deep within the icy grip
And chilling through my soul
Your caution is thrown into the wind
And I am thrown to cold.

My earthly life is fading
Melting past the ranks
The icicles jumped, quite startled
Into my pool and drank

Glistening frosted houses
Built upon the sea
In softest whispers tell me
That I'd be a fool to flee

My heart is slowly melting
Wishing to be free
But the icicles are alert now
My escape won't be made easy

I know the fight is futile
I haven't got it left in me
To fight my allied icicles
And leave my cold security

Even if you'd succeeded
In melting the ice away
I'd still be nothing more
Than a shadow in the lake.

           The End
