The words wont come to me
Am I afraid to see them
Afraid of what I might see in myself

But why wasn't I afraid to give them to you
To show you the deepest part of me
So fully
So completely

There was no fear, only love
In the things I showed your heart
But now it seems my love
Is what is pulling us apart

How was I to know you'd lie
Tell me you didn't love me too
When you looked at me with honest eyes
And commitment in your words

You're afraid...
to love..
to be loved..

I couldn't see that you weren't ready
And now it'll haunt me furthermore
For I only wanted to love you
But you've shut and locked that door...

Please let me in..
