It's all the same
These couples in the hall
they think they'll last forever
they don't have anything at all

I know I seem too forward
But I'm losing solid ground
How dare you leave me stranded
With simple nothings all around

It's all the same to me
The way you make me feel
Blinding hatred runs within me
As my heart breaks and reels

Why can't you be the one for me
Why do you choose to leave
I love you can't you see it now
The tears are soaking through your sleeve

So tomorrow is goodbye
and that's all that we have left
I'll forget you and move on
but my heart aches of this theft

I better find an angel
someone to hold me dear
because right now I don't think I'll make it
Without your heart right here...
