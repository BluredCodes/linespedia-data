Quiet
Like my head is under water
Cold
Like I've been frozen in the ice
Still
Are you here with me?
Fear
Runs in my blood

I feel as though I'm dreaming
Losing myself in your touch
You hold me dangerously close
But you tell me you dont want my love

Confusion
The train is gonna hit me
Frustration
Why cant we just be
Depression
I need your love inside
Agression
I'll get you 
You'll be mine
