I hate being weak
I don't understand your actions
I want to break your face
Beat you at your little game

But I love you too much
I've fallen in and you've trapped me
In this chaotic laberynth
AND I'M SCREAMING FOR AIR

You've got me on a chain
Make me think I can trust you
And then you bite me again
Why do I always come back?

It's bull and you know it
What you're putting me through
You've been hurt in the past baby
but what did I ever do but love you?

So who's weak now?Oh man.
