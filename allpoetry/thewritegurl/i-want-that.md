You knew the words to sleeping beautyYou're favorite was Mary WeatherYou love the color greenTell me, could you ever love me?I want thatI want that so muchI want youI want your touchWe haven't known each other longbe we know so much alreadyI've seen you as yourselfI'm waiting and I'm readyI'm ready for anythingI'm ready for youI want you to seeThere's not a lot I wouldn't doI want thatI want that so muchI want youI want your touchSo singand smileand stay for a whilebe yourselfmake linolium angelsI like you that wayI like you from all anglesI want that
