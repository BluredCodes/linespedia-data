---
title: "Honey."
writer: "thewritegurl"
slug: "honey"
source: "AllPoetry"

Long painted nails
A sweet little skirt
Small dainty feet
Covered in your dirt

But I loved you anyway

And they say I'm confusing
But they can't see my eyes
For if they looked deeper
They'd see all your lies

I can't remember feeling
So loved and complete
Like that night we had our first kiss
And then walked along the street

Until now when I've been given
The chance to start anew
And it's such a great advantage
But will I always think of you?

I used to think that way
Wanted to retreat to the past
But now that I look deeper
There was no way for us to last

And that's not what we wanted
We both needed something more
And I'm convinced that both of us
Have great futures left in store

We were much to young
For the problems that we had
And it's feels so good
To no longer feel so bad
