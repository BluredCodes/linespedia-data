Afraid of my own feelings
I have fled from my pens
My journals
My life

I didn't know what it was
That made me so Crazy
so alone

But I remember now
What it is that sets me back
You've been gone a year this Sunday
I can harldy remember your face

Because when I dream...
I see you lying there
Lifeless, unsmiling
Alone

I didn't want to remember you that way
But it's not a choice that I can make
Eventually I'll have to move on
But right now it seems so recent

Oh how I wish things could be different
That you could have been here to graduate
Your locker is so lonely
Without you and the guys around it

I'd cry, but my eyes have dried up
With love I'll keep you in my mind
But on Monday, it'll be the same again
Missing you, because in death we had to part...
