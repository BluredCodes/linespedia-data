Bitter cold of the snow
Kisses my cheeks and makes them pink
I see you standing there
I'm too engaged to think

I stand there for what seems like ages
Then you walk up to me
I feel my knees start shaking
And your eyes are all I see

My fingers have all frozen
But I yearn to touch your lips
I invite you with my eyes
And you hold me at my hips

We know we can't stay together
No one would understand
I am still a child
And you're almost a man

But tell the world to hush my heart
Or inside of me it will break
Can I let you go in reality
Or will I choose to make a mistake

I need you16 and 21...
