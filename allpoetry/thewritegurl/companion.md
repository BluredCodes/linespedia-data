There is so much behind those deep brown eyesRegrets, promises, torturing liesBut something inside them caught hold of my heartSomething that's breaking you, tearing apartI'm here and willing to share secret thingsI'm ready to show you, I know what that meansBut is the connection that I feel for youSomething you know inside to be true?God, don't tell me the road's one wayI'm yearning and waiting for what you have to sayIt's new and exciting, reckless and suchThese feelings inside me confuse me so muchYou're so much like me, like a mirror reflectionI'll show you myself, not just a projectionIf you feel it too, in your arms hold me tightI can see and can feel it, OUR future in sightWrote this after the most intimate of all conversations in my life.
