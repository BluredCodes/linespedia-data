Sexy black panties
Anticipation
My body quivers, crying out for your touch
Beautiful sensation

Pull me against you
Kiss my lips
Stroke my back
Let your promises swim in my head

Lie me down
Let me feel the weight of you on me
tease, torture
Take me, Tame me

Satisfy your urges
Live out your fantasies
Strip away the clothing
Keeping you from me

You don't have to make it gentle
Don't hold back, Be aggressive if need be
Do all the things you dream of
It will only pleasure me

Let your breath run faster
Speak in husky tones
Let all your energy plunge deeply
Don't suppress your moans

Give yourself completely
Take advantage of this time
Blissfully receive me
 Heart, Body, And Mind
