The sun warms my faceand there's wind in my hairrussling the leaves outsideThis cool summer airThe sky's so blueNow I am freeIt's all different nowThat I believeYou made the sunYou made the starsYou made my friendsYou made my heartI want to thankThe one aboveFor making meWith so much love
