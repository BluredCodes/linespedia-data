Her heartbeat quickens
as she closes her eyes
Nothing can stop this
her thoughts never die
He doesn’t know
and probably never will
How his laugh fills her heart
and his words give her chills
A deeper connection
she longs to feel
like everything’s perfect
and everything’s real
Her mind has no answers
she needs not to care
She dreams of his side
but her soul’s unaware
