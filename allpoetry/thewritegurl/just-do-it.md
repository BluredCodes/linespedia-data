I said I would today
Yesterday...even last week...
something in your eyes shocks me
and makes my knees grow weak

I want to be with you so much
but I'll be going back
it makes me hope and wish
I'd just refuse to pack

Yet I know that it'd be wrong
to stay here and give it a try
but how can I lie about my feelings
just to go home and cry

I'll try again today
but I wont get very far
am I afraid of rejection
or just a broken heart

I tried to find the words
told the group I had it together
but at this rate 
I'll be "trying to" forever

"Suck it up" they tell me
But he's more than that to me
And now I bet they're laughing
Cuz I'm pathetic as can be

So TODAY will be the day
and we'll just see how it goes
things could turn out my way
I guess no one really knows...
