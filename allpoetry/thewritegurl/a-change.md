Never beforeHave I felt so weepy as I spokeHave I felt so neededAnd so LovedAnd never before youDid I let myself smile When it rained outsideBut now things are betterGod is in my life againAnd I've lost all passion to RebelYou sat with meYou said helloYou have time for me stillAnd you don't know what that doesAnd I'll singI'll sing so loudYou'll hear me miles awayBecause my heart can feel the musicThe music in our livesAnd even when we cant see itThe sun is thereshining on usWarming our heartsJust like HimOur God.
