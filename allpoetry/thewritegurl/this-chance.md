---
title: "This Chance"
writer: "thewritegurl"

Meet me in the darkness
and I’ll turn on your lights
Kiss me in the moonlight
and I’ll brighten all your nights
tell me that you love me 
and I’m forever yours
take me in your life tonight
and end this storming war
show me your deepest fears
your heart I wont betray
Let me take your falling tears
and show you I’m here to stay
Sometimes I am so scared
to feel what I do inside
I want to scream and cry
but instead I run to hide
I lost my sense of control
I gave it all to so true
making me feel so helpless
but I always trusted you
A heart beating alone
it’s a sound so sad to hear
so why does mine continue
it’s so cold without you here
Smile once for me
from a real genuine place
and tell me if you feel the passion
or just an empty space
You made me feel so infinite
you made me hope and feel
so why did I say goodbye
to someone so surreal
Never underestimate 
the feelings of my love
For nothing that I say to you
will ever be enough
I want to be back at your side
but was I ever there
Did you know my love existed
did you even care
I know it’s been forever
since I held your hand in mine
but nothing that I say or do
will ever turn back time
so take me in your soul tonight
and give me one more glance
I know I don’t deserve it much
but please may I have this chance
a chance to show you everything
the things I never did 
the feelings that I hid from you
and the words frozen on my lips
just this once I ask 
for an understanding touch
for you are the special one
that I love so very much
