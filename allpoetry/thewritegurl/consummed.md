She cries
Soft tears that slide unnoticed down her cheek
She can't decide if she is strong
Or if she's utterly weak
To love him deeply
And know he cares not
To believe in magic
And not give in to logical thought
She walks alone in solitude
To give herself the time
Time to heal and space to breath
And air to clean her mind
Alone she wonders despreatly
How long her heart can wait
Before it bleeds internally
And gives up all debate
But flowers bloom inside her mind
Each night as this woman dreams
And nothing takes away her hope
For she is strong it seems
Yet can you see the torture
Of the chamber deep inside
Even if you ask her
She'll find a way to lie
Some people are too selfless
And are slowly consummed
By the pain of every stranger
And the fears in every room
