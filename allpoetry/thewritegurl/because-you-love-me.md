The rain used to pour down so hard
It broke my heart in two
But now I know you love me
So I can count on you

To be my umbrella
To hold me up when I can't stand
To kiss me when I need you
And to always be my man


The wind blows hard
And my feet don't feel solid ground
But I know that you love me
And you won't let me fall down

Because you love me
You love me
And you'll never let me go
Because you love me
You love me
