It hurts more to cry
Than to put on this smiling face
That belongs to someone else
And just pretend it's all okay

But what can I do
I'm sick of this feeling
There's so much anger in me
It's there everyday

And I have to smile
Because you say 
"My pain is worse"
And that makes me okay

But I can pretend
If that's what you want
There are things you don't know
Things I refuse to show

I want to break out
To yell, scream, and fight
Fight for my freedom
My happiness, My life

Please don't hold me backI don't know what came over me.
