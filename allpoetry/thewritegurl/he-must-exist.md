He must existI've met him in my dreamsHe holds me in the moonlightWaves crashing next to meWe sit there in each other's armsTime moving nice and slowI love him with each part of meTell me could he know?He must existOr else why am I here?He's the only one I wantThe only one I'll bring nearI caught a glimpse of him last nightI think he lives close byHe almost cried as he sang his thanksTo the God that supplied our livesI know it's too soon to tellBut right now I just want to knowCould this be my destinyCould he be the one to let me glow?I know he must existFor my heart is full of gleeAnd I'll sing right by his sideUntill he comes to meThis all just came out, so I'm not sure how it sounds...it's honest though. :)
