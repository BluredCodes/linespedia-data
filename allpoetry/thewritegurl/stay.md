These tears are like daggers
Bitting at my heart
Leaving me helpless
Leaving me scared
I don't know what it is about you
That's made me feel everything new
But I need you too see me
The way that I've fallen for you
You've been hurt
Those girls were different
I promise not to steal your individualism
But I can't promise not to give you my heart
For you are everything I've dreamed of
Everything I need
These feelings of desperation
Are taking all of me
We have some deep connection
I feel in my inner being
You walk the other direction
But you taunt me with your words
Don't drag me through false commitment
Don't lie to make me happy
Be honest and be open
I wont shut you out
We've got so much in common
From our feelings to our lives
Don't throw away an oppurtunity
For happiness
For truth
I need you
And I can't walk away
Please stay..Do you feel it too? can you feel me?
