The tension in the airAs we sit so close to each otherTemptation pulls us into oblivionWe all want someoneBut only for tonightNo strings, no attachmentWe all have someone we loveBut tonight we just want to give in to raging hormonesYet we don'tThough we joke about playboy We want someone in the room to take us awayKiss us, and then be doneNo stringsOnly tonight...
