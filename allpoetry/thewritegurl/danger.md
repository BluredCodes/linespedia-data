I’m only here so long
I’m bound to make mistakes
Bound to throw away the gifts I’ve been given
In an attempt to understand them
Thought I was strong
Perhaps that’s why I’m so weak
Never felt so confused before

Shut the door
It’s cold in here
My eyes are shut
But I can see quite clear
I’ve gone deaf 
But I can hear your intentions
Screaming, Screaming

When we’re together
I’m alone
When I’m alone
I feel, so happy
Does that make you sad?
Does that make you wonder?

Well, life is such a rollercoaster
Up and down don’t fall through
It’s a constant ride
You were to short to enter
But you snuck by
But you snuck by
And now your in danger
Of subjection
Of tactility
