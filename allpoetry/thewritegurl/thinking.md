I was thinking while I sang
In the shower just last night
How do we get into these messes
Who decides what's wrong or right?

It took me quite a while
To decide what I should do
While world peace does sound pleasing
It's impossible to do

We fight about importance
We want a pleasing end
We pretend to care so much
But give only to win

And there's really not a whole lot
Of things to rid the hate
We can get more to love
But it's hard with such a rate

And just like I've always said
You can't fight fire with fire
But then when I get stressed
I sometimes lose desire

We tend to get distracted
From what is really truth
And how we even know of perfection
It's as absurd as the fountian of youth

Perfection can't exist
At least not in this time
For as long as we are here
There will be pain and crimeSometimes I really don't have too  much hope...
