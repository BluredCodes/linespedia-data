He lies in perfection
He lives inside my soul
The day that we meet
I let everything go

I only know he exists
by my faith and my dreams
He’s beautiful inside and out
As impossible as it seems

How long can I wait
Before I look into his eyes
Before he holds me close
And life just seems to simplify

I'll wait forever if it means
I'll have the one in my dreams
