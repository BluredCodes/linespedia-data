Your wisdom fills my mind with power,Your love is true and never sours.Your music though I've never heard,Is filled with passion, strength, and courage.Everything you set to do,Is done with grace and perfection too.No one in this whole world can get as far,As the man I know you are.You’ve silenced my mind and turned up my heart,You’re the one that gave me my start.A brother figure with a state of mind,That showed me how to leave my troubles behind.I want you to know all you have done,Has made me see I am someone.This poem was given to someone very special :)
