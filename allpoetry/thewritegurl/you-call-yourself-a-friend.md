title: "You call yourself a friend"
writer: "thewritegurl"

You and I haven't been friends long
but now I know I made a mistake
I shouldn't have let you in
Or let you beat me in your games

How can you call yourself a friend
then slap me in the face
stab me in the back
and try to take my place

How did I let this happen
I never thought I'd feel so down
Like you harshly chewed me up
then spit me out on the ground

I wish I was just angry
I wish this would just go away
But deep inside my heart
I know these tears are here to stay

So prove me wrong if you dare
but please don't prove me right
I love having you as my friend
and for this friendship I will fight
