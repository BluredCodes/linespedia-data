A lone tear
slips down blood stained cheeks
She’s done it this time
She’s killed him
He lies there cold
Her hands covered in his blood
His eyes look up in fear
It’s a final expression
She feels a wash of relief
Her hands whipe the tear
As she realizes her flaw
She wakes up suddenly
Broken into a cold sweat
Her enemy still lives
