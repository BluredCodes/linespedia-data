title: "To you"
writer: "thewritegurl"

It's amazing
how you tilt your head just so when you smile
It's ground breaking
how you just walk by and I start to shake
It's confusing
how your friends see when you don't
It's heart filling
how when you sing you give your all
But nothing compares to the emotion
That I get with just one thought
And though it doesn't seem to be that much
You're amazing
You're ground breaking
You confuse me
You fill my heart
And nothing compares
To you.just some thoughts.
