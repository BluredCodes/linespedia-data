So overwhelmedI feel numbStaring into spaceFeeling so aloneAnd everyone is hurtingThere is nothing I can doCuz when I try to help outI hurt them with the truthAnd it comes back to haunt meThis love I have for allAnd I feel so rejectedAs I take this plunging fallA fall into the oceanOf black hatred and painAnd there's no hope coming toward meI need to breathe again
