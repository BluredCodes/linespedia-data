---
title: "You'll always know.."
writer: "thewritegurl"

You might not feel it yet..
you might not see it
but you know in your heart 
that it's true.

She looks at you 
she sees more than they do
she wants you to see her
see her beyond what they see
will you take her with you
will you give her that chance
or is it too  much to risk
too much to ask...
