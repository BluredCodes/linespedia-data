The faces 
Faces 
Not one the same 

But have you seen the face of Death? 
Hiding in the shadows 
Where all light is consumed? 

Have you seen the face of Death? 
Who takes orders from no one 
And feels no remorse? 

Have you seen the face of Death? 
That lingers long enough 
To pass upon the innocence of life? 

For some have. 

And.. 
Have you seen the face of life? 
Brilliantly shrouded in light 
Of every flower on a summers day? 

Have you seen the face of life? 
On the innocent faces of singing children 
Underneath the layers of lies? 

Have you seen the face of life? 
That men all feel not as a privilege but a right 
And that we take so for granted? 

For some have. 

These faces, these faces 
Be careful not to stare 
Their eyes hold the mysteries 
And you're too weak to care... 

These Faces 
Faces
