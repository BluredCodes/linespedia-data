title: "I didn't ask for you"
writer: "thewritegurl"

source: "AllPoetry"

I didn't ask for youTo come into my lifeAnd be so nice to meBut it's good you areI didn't ask for youTo lead me so close to GodTo show me things withinAnd you didn't even tryI didn't ask for youTo say Hello to me that dayTo make me feel welcomeBut if you hadn't I wouldn't know youI didn't ask for youBut someone knew I was in need.
