Here we go again
Another hole in the wall
Another sleepless night
I don't want to take this fall

Daddy why do you get so angry?
Why are you never satisfied?
I try my best to serve you
But when you get home I hide

Though you have never touched me
With your hands your feet your knees
Your words still penetrate me
And leave me begging please

I want it to all be over
All the screaming and the pain
Don't tell Mommy it's her fault
When it's you that made the stain

I know I should be careful
Because Mommy reads this very page
But I can't love you like Mommy
When you're filled with so much rage

Please don't fight around us
Little brother gets confused
And we cry for you together
Because your words they cause abuseMom, if you read this, remember it's just a poem.
