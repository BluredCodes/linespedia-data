Something about this summerSeems to fit so perfectlyLast summer we had no time at allWe felt so cheatedMy FriendRemember the waterfront?The endless games of around the world?It was fun wasn't it?It was perfectionMy FriendYou knew I liked your brotherYou helped me outYou gave me strengthMy FriendShe tried to take him from meI criedYou were my shoulderMy FriendCrystal, You and I are the best of themThe perfect examplesOf divine perfectionThe best of Friends.
