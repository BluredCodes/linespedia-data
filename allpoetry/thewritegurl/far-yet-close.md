---
title: "Far yet close"
writer: "thewritegurl"
slug: "far-yet-close"
source: "AllPoetry"

We've never met in person
but I think you've seen my heart
I'd do anything to know you've had
the same feelings from the start

It's hard to say I love you
but not with you and me
and it makes it all so special
and it makes it all so free

So maybe in the future
we wont be so far in miles
but right now I know you're here with me
despite all our denials

so just one last thing I'll say
I love you more each day
and when at last you know this
it won't all be left astray..
