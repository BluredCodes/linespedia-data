title: "Tell me what to do"
writer: "thewritegurl"

source: "AllPoetry"

Every night since we broke upI've prayed you'd talk to meand there must be a godCuz that soon came to bebut now I don't know what to doI'm loving you againBut I can't tell what you think about meI'll talk to you, just don't know whenI've been by your house a million times or morejust today I had the courage to knock at your front doorIt's probably good you were not home cuz I'd just freeze and stareWhat can I do without you, this just doesn't seem fairTell me what to sayand show me what to doCuz sitting here doing nothing isn't the way to get to you
