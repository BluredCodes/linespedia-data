Why do things happen 
to me in this way
was I born to suffer..
was I born to fall astray?
What do I do 
when I lose my place to run
when even my heart falls through
and there's no uplifting sun?
Have my tears turned into clouds
following me everywhere I go
droping their rain in my eyes
burying me deep in the flow?
Will I ever find him
the one that wont make me cry
or is it so impossible
that alone and bitter I will die?
