You came in so quickly
I hardly noticed you were there
I wasn't sure you were a friend
I never meant to stare

You seemed to be interested
but you told me I was wrong
You made me open up
then droped me like a rock

I want to kick you out
throw you on the ground and scream
but how do I forget your smile
It's so much harder than it seems

Just forget I ever loved you
and you'll be off the hook...
