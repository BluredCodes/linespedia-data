title: "For Crystal"
writer: "thewritegurl"

You're like an older sister
without the nasty fights
you always make me smile
when you go shopping late at night

There isn't another person
that I will miss as much as you
when we leave to go to college
and the things we choose to do

Crystal you are special
and if he can't see it too
then there is someone else out there
made especially for you

But I just want to say I love you
as a sister and a friend
and I'll remember you
far beyond the very end
