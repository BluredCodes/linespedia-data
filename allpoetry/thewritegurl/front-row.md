The silent classroom was perfect
As my pen slid out of my hand
You looked at me with intention
To take me away from this land

And it was our little secret
To meet only in our minds
And you kept the eye contact
Until we ran out of time

And something about you caught me
In a more unnerving way
And I didn't like how it felt
When I blinked or looked away

It's almost as though you had taken
Something away from within
Something that kept me grounded
That I can't get back again

And we don't know each other
And your girlfriend wont like me
So tomorrow I'll sit in the back row
And wear sunglasses untill 3.weird I know.
