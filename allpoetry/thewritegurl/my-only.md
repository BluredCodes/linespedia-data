Something in your eyes
Those eyes that I can't turn away from
Has made me happy
And at the same time made me cry
There is something in your smile
That holds me in
And feels so warm
Oh, the feeling when I make you laugh
Your words are intense
You don't know what you do
It's so hard for me
Not to fall in love with you
I love you Now
I'll love you then
And I can wait, and until when?
I'll wait forever
You'll be the one I trust, my love, my only.goodness...wow...yeah, I've never felt like this before, I guess in my sit. this isn't good... :*(
