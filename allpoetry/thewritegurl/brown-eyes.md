You've come into my school an exchange student
You'll leave a teacher
I am curious and drawn
To those deep brown eyes

So full of things not hidden behind cement walls
Or ugly orange locker doors
Simply loving and educated
In things I've never even imagined

War and hate are not so hidden to you
Not sugar coated or padded
Like the walls of the rooms
Where we go out of our minds

Tell me about that place
That place of greater understanding
Because I want to touch the face
Of the one who knows more pain than I
