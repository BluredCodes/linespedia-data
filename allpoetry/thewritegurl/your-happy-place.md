Though I was slipping down,down
You caught me, before I hit the ground
You have things you're working through
But I just cant forget about you
You said, "I'm in my happy place."
And I looked up and saw your face
A smile not unlike my own
Pain and doubt all overthrown
You've helped me get to here and now
Time is all I can believe in
Hope that I'll feel you're arms again
But next time more than to save my face
And if it's not, I'll know this much
I now have my own "happy place"under construction
