title: "One of happiness"

Not a cloud in the sky
Not a tear in my eye
Or maybe just one
One of happiness

Not a soul on it's edge
Not a man on a ledge
Or maybe just one
One of happiness

Not a reason to shout
Not a feeling of doubt
Or maybe just one
One of happiness

Only Happiness.
