You kiss me softlyAnd brush the hair back from my faceI smile on the insideAs you take my breath awayWe remain silentBut our eyes speak for our heartsWe lie togetherAs the light consumes the darkWe find perfectionIn our subtle embraceTime moves slowly for usWe feel no need to raceEmotions erupt madlyAnd true love finds inner growthBut hearts shatterWhen they awake to findThey were dreaming hopelesslyAnd were tricked by their unconscious mindDreams...
