How can you do this to her?She loves you so deeplySo honestlySo trueAnd this is how you repay her?Pick up someone new?Well, we saw herThis girl you say you loveBut she doesn't compare to herI can see it, why can't you?You're digging a hole nowThat's too deep to climb outBut she's holding a rope for youBegging for a chanceTo be lovedDo you know what it's like?TO love someone so deeplyAnd have then look awayIs it because you're afraid?That her love is so honestThat her arms carry warmth?I'm confusedI can only imagine what she's going throughGod man, She love youAnd if you were honest you'd see the truthYou love her tooFor you Crystal StClair. I love ya like a sister.
