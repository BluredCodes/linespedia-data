I took some time
and thought it out
I know what I need
I will not pout

I have all the time
that one could need
to find someone
to plant my seed

My fire will
continue to grow
here inside
my heart's it's home

And when I'm ready
And I know what I need
He'll come to me
and plant his seed

And together we will watch it grow
healthy and happy
in it's new home
Our Soulsthis is just the second half of my thoughts..once I'd had some time...
