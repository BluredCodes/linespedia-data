You make things easy
You try so hard
I just wanted to thank you
For all that you are

Please continue
To be exactly the same
And don't worry
Poker is only a game ;)

Sometimes I wonder
Just how I deserve
A friend thats unconditional
and can handle my curves

I know it's hard
For you to keep up
With my many "changes"
But that's just my luck

I'm still trying
To find who I am
And I guess I was blessed
With the gift of your hand

Thank you for being
My friend
