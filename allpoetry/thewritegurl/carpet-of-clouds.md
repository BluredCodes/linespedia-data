I knew you once
Before you went home
You and that smile
You were never alone

And now you walk
On a carpet of clouds
hand in hand with jesus
Love all around

I knew you once
You were happy and carefree
I wasn't quite as cool as you
But you had time for me

And now you walk
on a carpet of clouds
hand in hand with jesus
Love all around

When I meet you again
up there in the sky
I know we'll be friends
You and I

So walk on
In pure innosence
and save me a place
in your heart of repence



I miss you Brandon.
