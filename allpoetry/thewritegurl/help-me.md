Help me, I'm fallingFrom this place where I have found happinessShe told me todayShe did it again and I'm scaredI don't want to lose herCan't afford anymore lossI've just gotten back on the horseBut I feel like I'm gonna get tossedAnd those eyes of judgment scare meAnd they make me squirm in my seatAnd I almost want to forfeitDrowning in my own defeatSo I told her she could comeTuesday nights are the bestBut I don't think she believesThat my feelings come from the chestHelp me show her everythingLet her realize she is lovedCuz though you are so far awayHere in me I'll show her your loveAnd she knows that you existAnd I think you're in her heartBut she's falling out of sync with youAnd it's tearing things apart
