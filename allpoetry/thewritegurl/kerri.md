You and him are perfect
you're so cute I want to scream
and I feel so freaking jealous
I don't want you to think I'm mean

You are just so honest
so real and so unique
It makes me feel needed
when I have a place to speak

You and I are perfect
in this friendship that we have
and I'll never trade it
I'd rather get shot or stabbed

So when you think about him
and you think about me too
I hope we are the people
that are always there for you

A boyfriend that he is
A girl friend that I am
and with the power that you have...
I hope you'll always stick around.
