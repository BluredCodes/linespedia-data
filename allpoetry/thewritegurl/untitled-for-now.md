title: "untitled *for now*"
writer: "thewritegurl"

It's always been so easy
For me to hold on
To some shred of hope
But today it's been hard

I'm waiting for the phone to ring
For it to be you
But it's not
Not ever

And I cry
But it can't change a thing
And I yell
But I'm just making noise

Why?
Why can you hurt me without even trying?
Why?
Why is it always the same.
Desperate. No, scarred.
