Had a hard dayagainI walked awayagainFrom the painagainIt's all the sameagainand again and againOver and overwe repeat our mistakessooner or laterour heart bends or breaksbut what can I do when you are not hereI just become one more person in fearHad a hard dayagainI walked away again againagain...againPick  me up, tonightI need you hereI want to be All rightIn your armsagain...it's always againagainDon't let tomorrowbe todayAgain.
