tick tick tick tick
the ticking
the tocking
of my watch
it's not stopping
i try
to escape
but it seems
this is my fate

endless, eternal
agonizing time
why you brutalize me so?
never thought i would be
controlled by you
is that possible...
glowing red or Roman numerals
brilliant but consuming
the difference between day and night
yes...
but so much more
i fear i will have to 
wait quite a while
for that last breath
to be set free..
