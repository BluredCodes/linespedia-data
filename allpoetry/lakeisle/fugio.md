title: "Fugio"

i need to talk to someone about this
but i can't
there's no one i can talk to
i feel so alone and i know i've messed up again
but i can't stop
how can i tell her that i miss talking to her?
that i miss being friends?
wishing that this whole issue could be set aside
gracing some dusty corner of my mind
never to be brought up again
but it can't
torn between two loyalties is tearing me apart
i love and hate both
i'm not used to this sadness in my life
and mind-blowing reality
so i call upon my good friend time
to help heal as he always does
and hope for a quick recovery....
