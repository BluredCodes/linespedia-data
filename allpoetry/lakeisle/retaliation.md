The rain falls outside
while the phone rings
Agitated, I wait for
someone to answer.
But it's for me for once.
She wails about her boyfriend
cheating on her and I feel
like killing him.  She is already
planning to seek revenge
on him.  Ahh, revenge is sweet!
I will enjoy this devious
plan.  Morals are thrown
out the window.  This
is downright
WAR!a long time ago...i don\'t quite remember when..,
