---
title: "Puzzle Boxed"
writer: "LakeIsle"
slug: "puzzle-boxed"
source: "AllPoetry"

fuddled i am over you
and your mind
brilliant mind
that won't let me in mind
how i long to read your thoughts
and see how you think
like the deranged scientist i am..
i want to dissect your brain

for i am puzzle boxed over you.
