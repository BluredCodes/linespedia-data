they make coming home a whole new experience
my eyes light up in their presence
melting my heart with love
overwhelming love
kisses and caresses they give
playing with needle-like teeth
soft brown eyes so full of warmth
noses not yet turned black
so fuzzy yet so soft
so small are their ears
it seems like only yesterday...
they were small enough for the palm.
why must i give them up so soon?
6 weeks is not long enough.
knew from the beginning to not get attached
but alas!
i am attached.
just hope they know i will always love them


my sweet baby puppies.
