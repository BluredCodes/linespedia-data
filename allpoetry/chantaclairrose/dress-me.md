Dress MeDress me in strawberriesPaint me in creamName me by experienceColor me a dream. Call me a thousandnames plus a fewCall to me ferventlybut call me to you. Soft does a memoryplay on todaynames go onthough muses awayTell me you need meand my soft lullabyCooing your soul beyondthe earth and the sky. Pliant perfectionin flesh's union sweetlacuna of spiritfilled with your heat.Clothe me in spirit thenbath me in ruesremember me dailyjust name me your Muse. ---© 2k M Rose
