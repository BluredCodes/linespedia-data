title: "Who Am I, Who Are You??"

Who Am I, 
Where Am I going?
Who wants me,
and who doesn't?

You say,
I am a doormat,
and I need to change.

You say,
I need to stand up..
for myself.

I am me,
I am a loving,
feeling, independent, woman.

I have changed,
but don't like the change.

I stand up for myself,
and you don't like it.

I asked do you love me,
do you want me...
and you don't answer.

So now I ask,
who are you??
