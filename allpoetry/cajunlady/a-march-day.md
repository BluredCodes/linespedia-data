Its a cold windy March Day
It blows my soul away
The dark gray clouds slowly float by
On this cold windy day
I want to stay in bed snuggling against my lover
But there is know one there
So your up and about to face this day alone
As you go about your day
You look at the dark sky
Hoping and praying for
That warm gentle touch of sunshine upon your face
But instead you get the cold winds of March
Blowing against you
As you pray for the warmth of a Spring day
You look around you, and you can see the signs 
That tell you Spring is not far behind
As you pull your coat about you
And go on your way
Thinking of the beautiful warm Spring days ahead
Long walks in the woods
Beautiful flowers in bloom
And Hope that you will find your love again.
