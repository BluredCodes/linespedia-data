title: "My Hero, My Love"

Your My Hero, Your My Love
You keep me safe in Your arms at night.

Your My Hero, Your My Love
You are My Light, In the darkest night.

Your My Hero, Your My Love
Your My Life, My Hope and My Happiness.

Your My Hero, Your My Love
Your My Everything.

My Hero, My Love is gone
He was my everything.

My Hero, My Love
He left me here to Face the world alone.

Tell me My Hero, My Love.
How shall I go on without You?
