title: "I Want To Be Freee"
writer: "cajunlady"

Setting here looking out my window, 
Watching the squirels playing,
From tree to tree, limb to limb,
The birds flying across the clear blue sky,
The morning sunshine warming the earth,
Everything running free, 
Enjoying the beaurtiful morn,
Everything is free,
But here I set behind my window,
Wanting to be free,
I want to be free, 
I want to fly like the eagle,
I want to howl like the wolf,
I want to be free,
I want to be me,
Why can't I be free?
