title: "Reflections of A Broken Heart"

This reflects the way my heart feels,
Dark, cloudy, cold and rainy,
If you could see inside my heart,
This is what you would see,
My heart is breaking,
I want to scream my pain to the world,
For the pain you inflicted, 
Upon this heart,
To the world,
I look like I am fine,
But I am dieing inside,
And crying out my pain,
How could you do this to me,
In reflecting,
I have never done anything,
To please you,
And this may be for the best,
But how do I,
Live with the pain of a broken heart,
How do I move on...
So many reflections,
For this broken heart of mine,More therapy guys...just bare with me.....
