title: "Tell Me You Love Me"
writer: "cajunlady"

I wonder do you love me,
Do you want me,
When I tell you, I want you,
I need you, I love you,
I give you my love, I give you my all,
And all you give to me is your silence,
You tell me that,
I should know you love me,
But how do I know, 
When the words never fall from your lips anymore, 
Are you afraid,
If you say I love you, 
I want you, I need you to,
That I will hurt you,
Is the hurt you felt in the past,
So bad that it can't be over come?
Come and let me love you,
And let me heal the wounds
the past has left behind,
Let me tell you,
I love you,
And you tell me,
You love me to.
