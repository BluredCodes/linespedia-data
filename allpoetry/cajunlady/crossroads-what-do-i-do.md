title: "Crossroads (What Do I Do?)"

I am at a crossroad in life,
The questions is, what do I do?
Do I stay here in the middle,
Of the road in continual limbo,
Or do I go back to where I was?
Or do I move forward into..
The unknown future,
Where I have no idea what to expect,
Which road do I take?
Each road will take me places,
I'm not sure I want to be.
I can't stand here
In the middle of the crossroads forever.
What do I do?
Which road do I take?
No one can make this choose for me.
I am at a crossroad...
What do I do?
