---
title: "Let's Make Love"
writer: "cajunlady"

Lay me down my love,
And hold me tightly,
Against your body tonight
Kiss my lips,
Kiss me deeply, passionately,
Oh so hotly,
Caress my body,
As I caress your's,
Let me do the things, 
I love to do,
Let me take your breath away,
Let me send you to the heavens,
and let me bring you back again,
Oh my love the things,
I Love to do to you,
Let me make love,
To you althrough the night,
And in the dawns early light
I will hold you tightly against my breast, 
And tell you how much,
I love you,
Let me make love to you, My Love.
