Under the Sky of Midnight Blue

As I lay here on this bed
of emerald green....
looking at the sky  
of midnight blue...
and wondering
where your are tonight my love....

As I smell the rose 
you gave to me....
and look up at the stars
in the sky and wonder
where you are tonight my love....

As I lay here on my bed
of emerald green...
under the sky of midnight blue....
wondering my love
do you miss me in your arms 
tonight, as
I miss you in my arms tonight..
and wonder where you are tonight my love
