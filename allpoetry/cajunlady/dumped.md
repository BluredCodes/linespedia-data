You dumped me out of the blue,
Because I talked to another, 
that was suppose to be a friend,
You said "I played you for a fool,"
You said "You had been hurt before,
and couldn't go through it again,"
I told you the truth,
and you wouldn't believe me,
I told you, 
I love you,
and always would,
You had no reply,
You talk to others, 
that you say are friends,
And I don't dump you,
I will not beg you to believe me,
But remember this,
I love you,
And always will,
You'll Always,
Be my love,
Its not fun being dumped.I know this isn't good...but just bare with me....its a therapy for working out the pain of just being dumped this week...But will always except all the help I can get....
