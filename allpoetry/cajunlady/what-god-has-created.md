I love to feel the sunshine,
Shining down up on me,
On a sunny day,
I love to walk,
I walk through the woods,  
Communting with God and Nature,
I listen to the birds sing,
And watch the squirrels play,
And is awed at the miracles of nature,
That God has created,
A walk in the sunshine and woods ,
Lighten my heart and soul,
And I realize that,
God can work miracles in this,
World of pain and strife,
And he will get me through the pain,
That dwells in my heart and soul.
