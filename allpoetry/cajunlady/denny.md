Den, Den He's my man, 
He's the best friend in the land,
He's the best friend, 
A woman could have,
He's always there when you need to talk,
Or reaching out a helping hand,
He always has a kind word when your hurting,
And he always has big wide shoulders to cry on,
He always has a smile that seems to out shine the sun,
Denny's loving embrace, and caring heart,
Reaches thousands of miles,
Or through space,
To bring comfort to a broken heart,
He always makes you feel better,
When your feeling down,
And brings a smile to your face.
Thats my  best friend...Denny!!!
