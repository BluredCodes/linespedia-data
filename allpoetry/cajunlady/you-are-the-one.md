---
title: "You Are the One"
writer: "cajunlady"

I set here with my eyes closed, 
Clearing my mind of althoughts,
except of you,
I imagine myself in a warm,
Beautiful green meadow,
With the sunshine shining down on me,
And I hear the wildflowers lapping up,
The morning dew,
I open my eyes and..
Your standing there in the meadow,
With your hands reaching out to me,
I stand there with my hands,
Reaching out to you,
But I am afraid to take that,
Step to your warm embrace,
You take a step toward me,
And my fears and doubts fade away,
I walk into your waiting embrace,
And I feel the warmth of your loving embrace,
You tell me, you love me,
And I am forgiven, for all that I have done,
And come live with you forever,
To live as one,
For
You are the one.
