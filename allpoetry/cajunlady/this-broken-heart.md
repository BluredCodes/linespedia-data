Here we set with our broken hearts
You say you love me still
And I say I don't love you anymore,
I love another
I set you free to fly away,
to find your own happiness
You say I am your happiness
and you don't want to go
What will I do?
I don't want to hurt you anymore.
But I can't stay I have to go.
