My Welsh lover,
Takes me back in time,
To when the Celts and the Druids lived,
Back to the time our love began,
When our love was new,
I love my Welsh lover,
His body is like a warrior of old,
Lean, strong, and
Oh so hard,
He takes and spins the magic of love around us,
And transports us across the sands of time,
Oh my Welsh lover,
You stand the test of time.
