---
title: "Trading Places"
writer: "AmberDawn"
slug: "trading-places"

Here you lie
as death slowly consumes your body
the pain evident on your face
but you welcome it
Embrace it
Invite it to take you
and I weep
wishing I could take your place
wanting to breath life into your blood
hoping to see just one glimpse
of the vibrant woman you once were
praying that we can trade places
so you can once again shine your wondorous light
on all those who cross your path
But as I look at your pale face
the light is no longer evident
as you grimace in pain
I curse the fate
and I weep
