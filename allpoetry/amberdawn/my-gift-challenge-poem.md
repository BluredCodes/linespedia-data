Anticipating 
Hoping 
Praying to God for a miracle 

We had been trying for years 
to no avail 
but hope had returned 

When I learned my prayers had been answered 
the tears flowed like a waterfall 
I would become a mother 
my hope turned to complete bliss 
in a matter of seconds 

Your aunts and uncles 
your grandparents 
and mostly your father 
cried tears of joy 
as emotions withheld were finally released 

and I gladly welcomed you into my world 
and thanked God 
for a gift beyond comparison 


My Gift, My Salvation, My Child
