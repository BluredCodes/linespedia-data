SUNSHINE SOUL
giving giving giving
all of me is offered
as my heart explodes fireworks 
of compassion and love
and I strive to see the good
behind every evil eye
behind every wicked smile
Until this soul is kicked and beaten
and trampled down
leaving bruises so massive 
that it casts a shadow upon the light beams

My smiles become strained
My laughter becomes feigned

Now this soul is dark with fury
a part of me I kept locked inside
but you found the key
and you abused your power
until you unleashed this "other" me
this monster only you can see

So look into my blue eyes
see this fire of hatred blazing fiercely
and call me MIDNIGHT SOULHmmm, it's very hard trying to write about two sides of yourself, but I tried!
