Embers take form
and shape themselves into heated daggars
raging fiercely upon windy embraces
illuminating a blackened sky as angry flames leap
and threaten to raze mellifluous skin
Smoke clouds bellow
reaching long fingers towards the Heavens
as the furious blazes claim their newest triumphInstructions: write a poem about fire, no longer than eight lines. good luck!
