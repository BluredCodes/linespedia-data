These Words
striving to be heard
fighting to escape the mind
strangling all rational thought
I choke on these words
spilling forth with such force
that my pen may not keep up

These thoughts
pounding in my mind
playing scenes from past and present
rushing to be formed into words
I dwell on these thoughts
infinitely racing through my mind
until one thought overlaps another

These emotions
flowing freely from within
feeding the restless soul
struggling to be formed into thought
I taste these emotions
revealing my innermost secrets and desires
as each emotion gives birth to new thoughts

I succomb to these elements
surrendering myself to their power
allowing my pen to take control
permitting complete authority to my muse

Until at last
