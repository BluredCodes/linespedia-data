title: "Running"
writer: "AmberDawn"

Frightening figures
Materilizing in my mind
Laughing
Taunting
Sneering

I can't escape the voices
Can't stop the torture
I'm screaming inside
Begging the demons for release

Hiding my torture behind these fake smiles
And false happiness
Yearning to be liberated
From the evil
That has imprisoned me
For so long

I realize that when I sleep tonight
My dreams will carry on this cruelty
And when I awake in the morning
I am one day further from sanity
One day closer to death
