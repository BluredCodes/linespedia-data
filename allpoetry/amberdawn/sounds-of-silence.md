Deafening
Sounds of Silence
Thoughts of Torture
Ringing through my ears
Unwanted Memories
Raging like a thunderstorm
In the depths of my mind
You took my innocence
You stole my dignity
You corrupted my childhood
And now you spend life in a cell
But it's not enough
IT'S NOT ENOUGH!
