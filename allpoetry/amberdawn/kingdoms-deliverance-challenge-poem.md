Seventy Five years of unrest 
sent me on my quest 
to find the perfect Queen 
one with youth, strengh 
and an unwavering spirit 

My search was long and tedious 
but my four legs kept moving 
wit desperate determination 
and then.... 
on one glorious Spring day 
I found her 


I rejoiced! 
My tired feline legs carried me to her 
where I cautiously approached her 
she was perfect 
a young tomboy, with a spirit of gold! 

I told her my wishes 
and she enthusiastically obliged 
proclaiming this as her big opportunity 
her chance to prove that she was worth something 
but more importantly 
to show the world 
that underneath that tough exterior 
and that playful boyish attitude 
was a sweet little girl 
a true woman to be birthed 

Again I rejoiced! 
I thanked the Gods for deliverance 
I celebrated my great fortune 
to find this young lady 
so perfect for the throne 

I will never forget Coronation Day 
The day my Kingdom was saved!This is what I posted to k-chan's challenge about a talking cat approaching a tomboy to be the Queen of a Kingdom after seventy five years with no rule!
