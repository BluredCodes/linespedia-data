title: "Roses have Thorns"

As she packed her bags
salty tears stained her face
she wished she could stuff
her pain and sorrow in that suit case

The night before she was raped
but she couldn't tell her man
and now he sat in the corner
cradling his head in his hands

He wondered how he hurt her
or if it was something he said
she always made him feel so alive
but now inside he felt dead

She knew she couldn't tell him
that now she was filthy and impure
but without him by her side
the future seemed bleak and obscure

He couldn't hide his sorrow
or slow his breaking heart
he never saw this coming
and now he was falling apart

As she paused at the door
she looked so tattered and worn
he said "You'll always be my rose"
She said "Roses have thorns"I know this isn't very good, but it just came out so I decided to share it.
