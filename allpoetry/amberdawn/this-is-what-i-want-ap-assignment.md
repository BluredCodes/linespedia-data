I want warm days
and starry evenings
playing volleyball in the sand
I want success and happiness
forever for my kids
I want true romance
quickening heartbeats
and butterflies in my stomache
for a man that loves me too
I want to look in the mirror
and know who I am
and have something to offer the world
I want enough money 
to feed the hungry
and shelter the homeless
I want Red
       White
       Black
       and Yellow
To live together in PEACE!
