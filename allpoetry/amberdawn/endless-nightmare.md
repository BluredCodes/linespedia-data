I plummet
deeper and deeper
into this black hole of hatred
nothing to grab onto
nothing to soften my fall
I hear the voices
speaking to me
getting louder and louder
until they ring through my ears
like the screams of tortured souls
dwelling in the depths of the earth 
they grab me and fondle me 
pulling me deeper into their Hell
sneering and laughing
with voices low and evil
I beg and plead
and fear overcomes me
as I realize
this is where I shall abide
FOREVER!This is a poem about a reocurring nightmare I have and it always end in me realizing I am in Hell for eternity.   Scary stuff!!!!
