Poised on her knees,
with intentions of pleasing.
Eyes from above
exchange glances of lust,
gripping my flacidness,
in tightly sealed clamp.
Motions of earth revolution in mist,
squeezing mylife in your soft,supple fist.
Pulsating, now against boundaries of flesh,
heightening pace promotes stiff swolleness.
Throbbing and thick,
with a glistening tip,
optics alligned with a parting of lips,
a slow taste at first,
a sample of sense,
a survey of staff from base to its tip.


With moistening lips,
and poised like a fish,
I entered your mouth.
warmly, wet silk,
you took me inside far,
coating my trunk with baths of saliva.
Your lips were pressed firmly to base,
feeling your nose poke under my waist.


WITH SUDDEN SHUDDER
I SQUEEZED AT YOUR SCALP,
RELEASING BREATHS OF ENERGY OUT,
MY BOTTOM LIP BLED,
AS I BIT IN SEQUENCE,
WITH YOUR TEETH AT MY HEAD,
SCRAPING,SOFT GNAWING,
RIPPING YOUR HAIR UNCONTROLABLY GROANING
SPLASHING OF SOUNDS OMITED FROM SUCK,
FIRE LIKE ROUNDS OF YOUR TIGHT FACIAL FUCK.
AT THIS MOMENT BOTH LIPS ARE ALIKE, 
STIMULATED CLIT,
OR THRUSTING AT THROAT,
UVULA, SPIT, SURROUNDING MY BOAT.
MY HIPS THRUST YOUR FACE,
IN RHTHYMNESS PACE,
YOUR NAILS IN MY CHEEKS,
DIGGING CLOSER TO SPACE,
WITH ROCKETING FORCE I HOLD STILL YOUR HEAD,
YOUR FINGERS SLIDE CLOSER AND PENETRATE RED,
UUUUUUUH!!
OOOOOOOH!!
A BODILY TWITCH,
ON THE EDGE OF EXPLOSION,
YOU LOSE HALF OF YOUR FIST,
YOUR MOUTH UNRELENTING,
WITH PUCKERING GRIP,
YOU TUG ON THE FLESH,
NEVER SEEN BETWEEN HIPS,


IN FULL STIMULATION,
I THROB IN YOUR MOUTH,
BREATHING IN SWEAT,
AND SPEAKING IN SEX,
MY BODY CONVULSES,
RELEASING THE RUSH,
COATING YOUR THROAT,
IN THICKNESS OF LUST...
