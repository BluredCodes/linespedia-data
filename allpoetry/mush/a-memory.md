---
title: "A Memory"
writer: "mush"

A life so short,
Not even enough.
Doesn't seem possible,
He was so tough.

Ten yearsold,
A little boy.
Who brought the world,
So much joy.

Dearly loved,
Deeply treasured.
His love for life, 
Can't be measured.

A happy child,
One cool kid.
There was soo much,
He said and did.

His great life,
Sadly missed.
February 20th,
He was dismissed.

Up to heaven, 
He has gone.
Though his spirit,
Still lives on.

In our hearts, 
He will stay.
Forever and ever,
Day after day.

I deticate to my dear fiend Erin
