I am Alcohol,
I live without pitty.
I dress myself in expensive suits, 
As well as rags of poverty.
I am welcomed into many homes.
Even though i am a criminal,
I am not an outlaw,
but your politicians support me.
I sit in Government,
In the classrooms of your schools.
I relax in lounges and Hotels,
Drive your cars and sit with you at parties.
Many of you think i am harmless
While others think they are my masters.
None of you are my masters.
For i am the master of your destiny,
if you make me too close of a friend.
I am a thief a murderer,
I break homes,
Slap children,Abuse wifes,
And celebrate destruction.
It is my intent to destroy 
Ether slowly or quikly,
And you can't stop, 
For you worship me.
I rob you for groceries, 
Take milk from your babies,
Drive your cars into the innocent,
Decease your bodies an fog your minds.
I am your excuse for violence,
But you only slap me on the wrist,
And tell me to behave.
How i loath you,
Your weakness,
Your compassion, 
Your greed,
And you lust.
I live to take advatage of you,
To lie to you to use you for my destruction.
