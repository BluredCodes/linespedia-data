Kill the Children
With Words and Thoughts

Make them feel how we feel
Until they become us

hit back
hit harder
sharper knife
shoot first


Make them feel how you feel
Don't trust anyone

Strength is Power
be strong
stand up for yourself

we know better
because you know better
only weakness will
Kill the children
