title: "In the court of love!"

Between you and me 
No secrets are there as I see!
We differ, of course , now and then,
But our love is our mediator!

Our love gives us strength
For ironing out differences,
In the court of love 
Our judgement is final!Differences are common between couples, but they extinguish in the thin air of understanding.
