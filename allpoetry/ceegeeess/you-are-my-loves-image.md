I love you ,my beloved, for ever and ever!
Might clouds carry no water,
Dried off, the oceans might be!
Eclipse might, the sun and moon ,each other,
In war and hatred to be supreme
For people on earth to adore!
My love to you shall not extinct nor extinguish!
An orator if I am,shall narrate
My love, in episodes, in thousands!
Weary shall I never be of your love,
Ever green shall it be like a dove's!
When in doubt, verify from the mirror all lovers love!
Glow in your face, musings on your lips,
Your inquisitive and seductive eyes,
Curly hair that blossom,dropping on your bossom!
Your moonlit teeth like pearls,in a smile!
You are my love's image!Thrilling is love, no doubt;But unselfish , sacrificial and reciprocal more!Love mesmerises, the claity of mind makes the mesmerism permanent!
