A decrepit father am I,
Tired and retired in the public eye!
I did read the world a lot,
But still, a child, when I behold,
Every wisdom pooled in you, my child!
My wealth, my youth and my beliefs
Have not gone waste, but well spent,in truth,
As they are in you, as treasure incontovertible!
Though, one day, I am to honour 
The call of the Allmighty,
Proud shall I be ,
With my head high, to obey!
My last breath I shall treat 
As god given honourable wreath!
My youth I spent to make you big,
I live every minute of the evening of my life,
Enjoying the sight of your 
Progress and prosperity in life!
I shall die, only to live in you!Children are the assets left by the elders who die!
