All lovers of the world unite!

Love keeps us together,
Makes us live each for the other,
Unites people far and near!

In love, life is a mission!
Angels and evangelists of love
Are lovers world over!

Love is a poetic thought
Above self and full of sacrifice,
Perfection, beauty and valour!

Love is meditation
Of two souls living together
To bring Heaven to earth!

Give a clarion call
"All lovers of the world unite!"
To bury hatred and terror!Eradicate hatred with love!
