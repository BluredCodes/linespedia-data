Love at first sight

Bewitchingly beautiful,charmingly bright,
Her smile pearly white,confining herself
Inside glass chamberof a high class
 Computer house in city,
Beauties of nature and miracles of Web 
She conveys and communicates with grace,
Ever smiling, never harsh,Either by word or by face!
Her looks are not swords,
But sure, are arrows of flowers
That hit in the kernal of the heart!
I stepped into her chamber,the heaven on earth because 
Of her angelic presence!
Alas! chambers of my heart palpitated at once!
I was staring at her eyes glaring,
I wished her, I thought,
But the words evaporated in a whisper!
Invitation by her smiles,Offer to be seated by gesture,
Our eyes looking at each other,
I took the seat in her front;but floating in thoughts!
She was mute and in silence cute!
Understood later that I loved her!
Thrilled and happy ever after
Recollecting my love at first sight!
