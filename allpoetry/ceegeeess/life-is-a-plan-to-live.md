Life is a plan to live!

Desires are like morning shadows,
Fulfilment is like shadow at noon,
Fears are like evening shadows! 
Lust is like a candle that burns out,
Love is like a stream
That becomes pure and serene
With passage of time and understanding!
Mistake is misuse of our intelligence and kowledge
When corrected keeps us growing!
Perfection is an illusion;
As a goal, it improves our vision!
Good and bad are relative,
Nothing is bad when there is nothing good,
Nothing is good when there is nothing bad!
Life is an analysis of 
Plan, execution and evaluation,
And repeat the same again and again!
Necessarily everything connected 
With life is the same!"Life depends upon the liver!". It is absolutely true!
That is why we have to plan our life so that we can live it truely!
