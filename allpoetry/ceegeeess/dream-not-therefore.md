---
title: "Dream not, therefore!"
writer: "ceegeeess"

When we close our eyes in drowsiness,
Dreams colorful encompass our mind
and infuse hopes of different kind!
Or deter our faith in mind!

When sleep is distant,
when mind taken over by wakefulness
By thoughts scattered haunt,
Day dreams dance and daunt!

Dream beautiful, to get,
Is everybody's wish and desire!
But, when dream is disturbed,
Depression sieges our heart and mind!

Love and marriage, lovers dream about
Make them disturbed day in and day out!
 Despite disturbance, they enjoy serene!
Dreams keep them engaged ,however!

Robber ,dreads his captivity in dreams!
Students, in dreams, fear the failure!
Like-wise, people dream, in fear or hope
Respective unpleasant or colourful hallucinations!

Dreams are various and many,
As each man differs from the other!
Dreams,our fickle thoughts in sleep,
Dream not therefore!
