---
title: "make the night bright!"
writer: "ceegeeess"
slug: "make-the-night-bright"

Sky is dark in the night,
But many a star make it bright!
Dark night turns bright as sun rises!
Every night gives way to morning bright!

In life, every failure, pernicious and fatal,
Gives us experience rich, to advance vital!
Life is beyond blaspheme, though
Obstacles and mishaps it carries with!

Defeat is fictious, which the timid console in!
Stepping stone it is, for success concealed.
Every rose is fragrant and fine
If beware of thorn and confine!

Water-falls from mountains high
Fall into valley deep,
Everything beneath they sweep.
But engulf surroundings exhilarating
Make our lives live and scintillating!

Spoil not the present spilling 
Sorrows of yester years;
Welcome the coming years to make them dear!
The bliss we know , if happiness, 
Only when we taste sorrow's bitterness.
Face the cyclone with grit and grin
To embrace and enhance the calm and serene!

Day and night intertwined twins of nature
Like sorrow and mirth of life!
Search for light to make the night bright!
