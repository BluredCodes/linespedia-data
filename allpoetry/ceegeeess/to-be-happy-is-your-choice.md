title: "To be happy is your choice!"

Though you wait for decades
Happiness never dawns on you ever,
Unless you work out for it!
Be grateful for whatever you have!
Try not to quench your thirst
By distant and extinct waters of a desert,
But by acute spring under the feet!
Happiness is sister of contentment,
Lack of it is brother to desires more!
Wake up not the snakes of desire,
Make up your will to kindle latent fire!
Accumulation of ambitions is painful,
And achieving a bit at a time
Makes you happy all the time!
Red hot fire of anger,
Quick flow of tears of sorrow,
Rain of flowers of smile happy
Are not guided by the reason external,
But the results of 
Our mental make up , internal!
If you know what you miss to be happy,
Attempt and achieve it,
Or fill the gap with something new,
Or burry it fathoms deep 
Outside the yard of memory;
Choice is yours, but happiness certain!Happiness is the target and goal of everything we do!
