Life is a delightWhen it is safe and right!Even when it is notWe must tie it to a knotOf sweet relationshipIn kind and in mind,Not minding the failuresAnd many a disappointments!Only then , in the end,Our miseries end,And the life settles well!Live a life well,It sounds sweet as well!concept of life is very deep like an ocean and I feel like sharing it with other poets in depth!'life depends on the liver'!
