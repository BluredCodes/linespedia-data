title: "The ladder of memories!"

Your house was warm
When I just came in, out of a storm!
Your smile, a blank cheque, 
To credit my account with,
When in troubles and tribulations,
My mind waver and perturb!
Your voice sweet
And your song soothing;
Your looks make me dream!
Angel you are, messenger of love,
And why, you are incarnation of love!
Memories real and your presence unreal
For the entire world;
But for me you are always alive,
Alive and lively in my mind!
My feeling of love for you
Is fresh and fragrant real 
for ever and ever!
Heaven is so near
As I use the ladder of memories!"Memories tone up the mind and freshen the feeling,when they 
are sweet"
