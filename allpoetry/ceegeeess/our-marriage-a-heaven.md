title: "Our marriage, a Heaven!"

Touching each other, no ocasion missing,
Finding ample time for kissing
And pledging our love,
Doing things sweet,one to the other
Indulging in criticism, anger,dissatisfaction never,
In love so deep ever,
Like flowers that fade never!
'Made for each other'feeling,our minds sweep
Doing everything , the feeling to keep!
Never regretted ever after 
Our relationship for long shall hike!
Rose high together in other walks of life
Smooth and nice without a strife!
Marriages are made in Heaven, they say
We made our marriage a Heaven, in gay!Marriage is what we make of it!
