Oh! wandering cloud,Moving ever from place to place;You always make sounds hoarseAnd dance in strings of light!The rain that you promise to bringmake people forget about scorching sun! When you fulfill the promisepeople dance and sing in the rain!you act as an exchange for loversAnd deliver messages of love and romance;The notes of mutual concern, Pain in separation,The sweet musings about reunion!Traveling over sea and the rivers,touching the mountain peaks high,While trees welcome you with dance,You look like an ambassador of rain!Every drop of water in the seaRound the world near and farUnite in the sky and move in your form,Taking heat from the sun And the wind giving the momentum!Entire globe you travel,Dissolving while you travel,Parting with the water and rainSo that people world over gain!
