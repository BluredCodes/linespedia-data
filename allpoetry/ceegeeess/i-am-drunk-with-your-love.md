---
title: "I am drunk with your love!"
writer: "ceegeeess"

Your eyes charming
Singing songs of love,
Your lips cute
Eager to exchange love
With my lips 
Intoxicated with love, 
Your looks romantic,
Your speech silent
Sending electric shocks of
Passion and mad desire
Through my veins,
Your smile exhilarating 
Exciting my heart and mind,
Your song unsung
Musing my thoughts 
Overpowering my restraint,
Everything you own,
Shall, no longer, be yours!
You are mine
For your beauty, charm and love, a mine!
I live and survive
For you and your love, a beehive!
I am drunk with your love!
