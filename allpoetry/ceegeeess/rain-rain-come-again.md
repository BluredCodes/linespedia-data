title: "rain! rain! come again!"
writer: "ceegeeess"
slug: "rain-rain-come-again"

Raining it is!
Raining to clean us all
Of dust of disunity!
In the rain we drain
It is raining,
Raining to clean us all 
Of the dust of disunity!
In the rain
We drain our prejudices!
Let it rain
To make the land green,
The nature serene and 
our thoughts gay and clean!
Cool down in the rain
To get rid of
heat of anger in the brain!
Drenched in the rain,
I like to sing
with my wings
Of imagination,
And voice of a cuckoo
For my joy to regain!
In the rain
Lovers together cling,
songs of love they sing!
refreshing rain, come once again!

clouds disolve,
clear the sky
So that the starry night
Is more beautiful and bright!
