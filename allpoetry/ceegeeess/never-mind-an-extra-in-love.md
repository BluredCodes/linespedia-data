---
title: "Never mind an extra in love!"
writer: "ceegeeess"
slug: "never-mind-an-extra-in-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8944-Never-mind-an-extra-in-love--by-ceegeeess"

An extra spoon of sugar 
In a cup of tea,
An extra pound of weight
On an overweight man,
An extra year of age of
An extremely beautiful dame,
An extra man in a lift
Which is already overloaded,
An extra word in praise
But used in a context wrong,
An extra marital affair
Continuing for long unfair,
Perhaps lead to extra risk,
Damage and loss
irreparable and irrecoverable indeed!
But an extra parting kiss to lover
In bidding farewel
After making love fullwell,
An extra whisper in her ears
Communicating best of your love to her,
An extra visit to lover, 
An extra greeting before meeting
And while saying Adieu
Stand in good stead,
Flower and bloom your love
With every passing day
In the kingdom of love!In love extra rewards you extra!
