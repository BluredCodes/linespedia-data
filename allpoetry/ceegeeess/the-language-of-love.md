title: "The language of love!"

Staring eyes,glaring looks,
Dry lips and hot breath fry!
Desire to convey love, 
Fail to express and lips compress;
Heart beat high,thoughts multiply!
Hopes about engagement,
Fears crumble down the hopes!
Fight between positive and negative
Taking rest in the neutral!
Smile for a while,
Frozen in thoughts not clear!
Fly in the air in joy, 
Perspire profusely and cannot enjoy!
Dialing phone but
Dilemma stops to answer;
Pretending sleep but
Being fully awake!
Kowledge of secrecy pervading
To keep the affair to self !
Looking in the mirror and 
Adjusting hair, apply lipstick 
And powder fair!
Hungry for love and
Distaste for food!
This is all and there is more
That is the language of love
That only LOVERS can understand anyhow!All lovers are requested to recapitulate their experiences of firs love!
