title: "Golden truths!"

During the days of friendship
Everything looks nice and cute!
When the ship of friendship broken,
Many a mistakes found, bearing no ground!

Lotus flower,when out of water,
Rays of it's lover, the sun, make it dry and die!
In alien place friends become our foes!

Pledge not the gold,
Don't show your back in war,
With a fool never make friendship,
Never take provisions on credit,
Follow these four sermons for your good!

Don't stay at a place
Where kinsmen not found,
Doubts haunt your mind,
Where people hostile dwell!

Fool he is, who likes not to
Leave the woman hating him,
And the employer, who,terminated his services,
The friend who turned enemy!
