The young couple newly wed,Inside the bed room,On the bed decorated,Under the blanketIn each other's arms,two pairs of lipseagerly touchingIn close proximity,Increased heart beatBodies emitting heat,with consoling warmth,staring at each otherIn the darkBut blind with emotionSpeaking out words sweet,But nothing audible,except whispers!The embrace tighteningSpace contractingAs both of themWant to unite as one!The embrace that was tighteningWas loosened all of a sudden!The heart of the husbandCame to a sudden halt!The wife sceamed in fearLost her conscience!God was cruel to themDeath snatched away the husbandHell on earth befalling On the wife for no falt of her!The sound of the fanDeclared as closed The game of the God,Darkness dancing wildSilence encompassing the roomAs if surprised to know The tragedy enacted byGod, the saviour of All the mankind!The shepherd killing the sheep!
