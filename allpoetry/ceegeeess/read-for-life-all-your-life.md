"No time to read", often we say! 
To watch the T.V. many hours ,we keep,
Match our leisure with our sleep!
Programming hours together,
Performing nothing out of the plan!
Know thyself, know the people thine,
Know the world, know it's ways of life!
Every book you read
Is surely , for better life,a road!
Reward infinite for the time spent,
The pleasure on knowing things,
Got on reading a book!
A good book is good friend;
Read one and take a lead,
To gain the habit to read!
Carry a book with you everywhere,
You shall not be alone any where!
Read for life, all your life!When no friend around, our book is our friend!
