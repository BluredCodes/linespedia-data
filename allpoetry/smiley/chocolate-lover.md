Chocolate Lover,you are so fine and so sweet.
I wish you were my chocolate treat.
Chocolate Lover,you are so smooth and so fine.
You make me want to taste your wine.
Oh your eyes are precious to me,
they have set me free.
Chocolate Lover, I want you all to me,
so leave your wife and be with me.
Oh Chocolate Lover don't listen to me,
for I know that you can't leave her for me.
With this Smile I'll say goodbye.
Maybe one day we will give it a try.

Yvonne Griffin
