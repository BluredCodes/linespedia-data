Have a nickel, have a dime, maybe a quarter
and I won't waste your time.

I didn't ask to be here today.
Don't you know Society Banished me away.

I was CEO of my own company!
Don't you know Society Banished me away,
because I was tired and grey(so they say).

Wash your windows for you today.
Don't you see that I'm homeless.
You have to Pay!

I didn't ask to be here today.
Don't you know Society Banished me away.

My home is made of four Card Board walls
and for my roof a Plastic Bag that covers
it all.
I'm well educated with a college degree.
If I could just stop these voices talking 
to me.
I would still be part of Society,
but instead I'm begging you for a
piece of Bread!

I didn't ask to be here today.
Don't you know Society Banished me away!

Now that I told you my story today.
I hope and pray that you see me in a 
different way.

I didn't ask to be here today,
SOCIETY BANISHED ME AWAY !
