FREEDOM is like the buterfly in the sky.
Watching the sunset with your own eyes.
Feeling the JOY from your heart and not you mind.

FREEDOM is when you know that your life is not a lie.
When your smile is genuine.

FREEDOM is giving with a cheerful heart!

WOULDN’T YOU LIKE TO BE FREE???

YVONNE GRIFFIN
11-18-99
