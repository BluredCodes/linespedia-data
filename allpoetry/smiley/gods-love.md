God's love took me away for when all was said and done. 

God's love healed my soul and washed my tears away. 

JUST REMEMBER GOD'S LOVE NEVER GOES AWAY, WE ARE THE 
ONES THAT RUN AWAY. 

God's love shines in us everyday, for we need to look inside and pray. 

If it wasn't for God's love, I wouldn't be alive today. 

FOR HIS LOVE IS SO GREAT AND STRONG, GOD TOOK MY 
PAIN AWAY. 


Yvonne Griffin 
12/31/01
