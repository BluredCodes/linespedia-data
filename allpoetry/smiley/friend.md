I would have given you the world,
If you had given me a chance.

          But......

I now realize that no matter what I 
do or say...
It won't make you look my way..

No matter how nice I can be to you,
It won't make you see me in that way.

Now I understand that we are just strangers
passing in the hall.
You say that you are my friend,

         But.......

Your actions speak louder than you words.

One day people will stop looking at color,
shape or size.
On that day you will become that friend I 
visualized.

Yvonne Griffin
