What is freedom??? Is it in another person?? Or is it in our children???
No, freedom is in GOD.. Freedom is in our faith. We have the right to be free.
All we need to do is look inside ourselves to see the freedom..But when you look make sure you see GOD. We are his temple and he can only set us FREE.
Free from our worries, free from our STRESS, free from our pain, and free from our iniquities. Just remember that it is only GOD that can redeem us and set us free for life.

Yvonne Griffin
