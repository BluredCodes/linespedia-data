As we lay here Cheek TO Cheek;
sharing that one thing that is neat.

As we look above at the stars so deep;
I enjoy that we are Cheek to Cheek.

There is nothing more than I adore is
being here with you laying Cheek to Cheek.

As the sun rises and I am still alone;
I realized it was all a dream.

I hope that one day my dream will come true,
and that we will be laying here very soon !!

Yvonne Griffin
1/03/02This is dedicated to an old friend with love and smiles.
