DISAPPOINTMENT IS WHEN YOU POUR YOUR HEART OUT TO SOMEONE YOU CARE ABOUT; AND THEY DON’T RESPOND TO YOUR AFFECTIONS AT ALL.

DISAPPOINTMENT IS WHEN YOU TRY YOUR BEST ON A TEST; AND YOU STILL FAIL IT.

DISAPPOINTMENT IS WHEN YOU TELL YOUR CHILD TO DO SOMETHING OVER AND OVER AGAIN; AND IT STILL DOESN’T GET DONE.

PEOPLE NEED TO HAVE DISAPPOINTMENTS IN THEIR LIVES IN ORDER TO SEE THE RAINBOW AT THE END OF THEIR TUNNEL. 

DISAPPOINTMENTS ARE OUR NEGATIVES IN LIFE; AND A SMILE, SIMPLE LAUGH FROM OUR FRIENDS ARE OUR POSITIVES IN LIFE. 

JUST REMEMBER WHEN SOMEONE OR SOMETHING DISAPPOINTS YOU, THERE IS A RAINBOW AT THE END OF YOUR TUNNEL.

YVONNE GRIFFIN
