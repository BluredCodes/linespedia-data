There's a man I know, that so beautiful;

He doesn't even know.

There's a man I know, that's so beautiful;

Even through his anger it still shows.

There's a man I know, that's so beautiful;

If he were to look in my eyes, he would see

What I see.

Oh beautiful man that I know; stop, stand

Still and look deep into your heart, so you

Could see the beauty that you control.

Oh beautiful man that I know; if you only

Knew what true beauty that you hold.

Oh beautiful man that I know
