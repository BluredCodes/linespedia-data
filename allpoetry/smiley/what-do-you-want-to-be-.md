I want to Soar like an eagle.

I want to Roar like the lion.

I want to be Sleek like a tiger.

I want to Roam like the pather.

I want to walk on the Moon like man.

Most of all I want to be Free like the butterfly

you see.

"What Do You Want To Be?"


Yvonne Griffin
