title: "What is love ?"

Love is a fresh flower picked from the
garden.Saying I love you over and
over again.

Love is looking deep into each others
eyes, a gentle kiss, and a simple embrace.

Love is enjoying the sunset and long
summer drives. Waking up in each others
arms.

Love is knowing when to say "I AM SORRY".
Love will not allow you to go to bed angry.
Is it all about the material things????
Let it be known that "MONEY" can't buy love.

Love is you
Love is me
Love is GOD.....

YVONNE GRIFFIN
