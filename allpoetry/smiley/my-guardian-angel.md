Life is so precious to me,
that is why I don't understand
why you had to leave me.

If you were alive today,
you would be 8 turning 9 years old.
For I believe that you were suppose
to be my Boy to be. God thought
different instead.

I thank God for at least allowing me 
to carry you the the 12 weeks that I did.
My heart broke into many pieces that day
when I heard your heart beat fade away.

There is not a day that passes me by,
that I  don't wonder what life would
have been like, if you were alive.
Would you be as handsome as your
sisters are pretty.
Would I have given birth to my 
other 2, if I have given birth
to you ?

All these years I grieved so hard
and so long, I now realize that it
was wrong.

God showed me in my heart, that 
you have been my Guardian Angel 
from the start.

My tears of sorrow have turned
into tears of glee, for I know
that you will always be with me.

Thank you for choosing me.

Yvonne Griffin
