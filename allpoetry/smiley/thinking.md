I WANT TO STOP THINKING ABOUT THE SAME PERSON AND THE SAME THING. I WANT TO REMEMBER THE SUNSET, BUT ALL I DO IS THINK ABOUT BATHING HIM AND RUBBING HIM DOWN.
I WANT TO REMEMBER WHY I’M AT WORK, BUT ALL I DO IS WONDER HOW HE LOOKS WITH NO CLOTHES ON . I WANT MY LIFE BACK TO WHERE I CAN DO WHATEVER I WANT TO DO AND NOT FEEL BAD OR GUILTY.
I WANT MY LIFE BACK. I’M LOOKING FOR THAT FREEDOM OUT THERE AND IT'S NOT SUPPOSE TO BE IN A MAN. I WANT TO BE ABLE TO WAKE UP TO THE SUNRISE AND SAY HOW BEAUTIFUL IT IS AND NOT WONDER IF HE IS UP TOO.  I KNOW ONE DAY I WILL GET THAT FREEDOM AND I WON’T BE THINKING OF YOU.........

YVONNE GRIFFIN
12/09/99This one is about a Manager that use to work at a store that I shop at.
