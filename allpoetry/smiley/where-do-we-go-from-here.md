When I first saw you and you smiled, I said there goes a gentleman.
When we spoke to each other for the first time, I knew that you were a catch. I wonder if you had someone. You didn’t show it at all. I, like a stupid fool went for you. You didn’t say yes BUT you didn’t say no. Instead of telling me that you had someone, I had to find out from someone else.
 
WHERE DO WE GO FROM HERE???

Why didn’t you tell me??? I would have left you alone and kept it to a simple flirtation. But now I can’t even look you straight in the eye. What happened to us being friendly ?? 
why did you let  it go to far?? 
Now we are beyond the point of being comfortable in each others presence. What a shame that is... I am just a simple girl in this world looking for some company and I thought you were it. I now realize that you are really a boy in a man’s body(no match for this girl)......

I know where we go from here....

It is time for me to say thank you for the fun and games that you let me play. You came into my life for a reason and now it is time for it to end for that same reason. THANK YOU AND GOOD-BYE..

Yvonne griffin
