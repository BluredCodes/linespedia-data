When you feel that your burdens are weighing you down.

ALWAYS SAY A PRAYER

When you laugh to keep from crying.

ALWAYS SAY A PRAYER

When you feel like dying.

ALWAYS SAY A PRAYER

When you have no one else to turn to.

ALWAYS SAY A PRAYER

When your boss doesn't understands you.

ALWAYS SAY A PRAYER

When your pain takes control of your soul.

ALWAYS SAY A PRAYER

PRAYER will set you free.
PRAYER will make you believe.

So, the next time you think that your
life is in DESPAIR

ALWAYS SAY A PRAYER
