AS I LOOK UP INTO THE SKY WITH BRIGHT WONDERING EYES, WHAT DO I SEE TO MY SURPRISE?

ANGEL WINGS IN THE SKY !!!!

FOR I KNOW THAT THESE WINGS WERE SENT TO ME, FOR NO ONE ELSE WAS AROUND TO SEE.
I ASKED AROUND AND NO ONE SAW THEM. 
PEOPLE MARVELED AT THE BEAUTIFUL SUN SET THAT NIGHT, BUT ALL I COULD SEE WERE MY ANGEL WINGS IN THE SKY.THIS HAPPENED ON FRIDAY 12/21/01 AT 5pm
