---
title: "If only i had another chance"
writer: "GM Devotion"
slug: "if-only-i-had-another-chance"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/52023-If-only-i-had-another-chance-by-GM-Devotion"

I feel as if I was a fool
Not asking you when I had the chance
Now I’ll never know
Cause I’ll never see you again

You left a big hole in my life
One that can never be filled
I only wish I knew
Is it love, is it real

If I could change it I would go back in time
To the days of my innocence and prime
As of now I’ll never know
I only wish your heart was as mine
