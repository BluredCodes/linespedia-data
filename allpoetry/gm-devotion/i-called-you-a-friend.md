title: "I called you a friend"

I called you a friend
You called me a friend
I called you my best friend
You called me your best friend

I made the mistake 
Of having you as my friend
You said it was my fault
For thinking we were friends

You disrespected me
In so many ways I don’t understand
Am I not human
Am I not with out these human hands

You took advantage 
Of both my soul and mind
You said do this and do that
And I thought you were right

It’s hard to lose a friend
That I’ve known through out the years
Especially when we were never really friends
It especially hurts right here

I thought you were different
But you are just like the rest
You take advantage of the weak
And consider yourself a friend
