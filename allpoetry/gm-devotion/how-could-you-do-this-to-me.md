How could you do this to me
I thought you were my friend
I realized I was mistaken
Well I guess this is the end

Over the past years
You’ve meant so much to me
We’ve laughed together, we’ve cried together
Look what has become of me

Why did you change so much
Is it the desire for new friends
You took advantage of me as a friend
Now you’ve got a broken heart to mend

I know that you don’t care
But I do cause I’m a friend
Perhaps we just weren’t friends
Is that why I strike this pen

I write this out of love and hate
Love because you were my friend
Hate because how you made it end
Is this the end, or are we still friends
