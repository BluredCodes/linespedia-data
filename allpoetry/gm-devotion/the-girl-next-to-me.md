You’re that girl that sits next to me
Too shy to talk, too shy to glance
Always quiet, so aloof
Your beautiful eyes so full of wonder

You turned and smiled at me
That smile that is ever so tender
Your giggles set my heart on fire
Your love is the inspiration of my desires

Everyday I glance at you
Not knowing what to say 
I wish I could find the words to put it
Knowing the only remedy is your affection
