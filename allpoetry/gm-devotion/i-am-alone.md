---
title: "I am alone"
writer: "GM Devotion"

I am lonely with out a friend
I thought it was suppose to be
Friends, friends until the end
Now I know who are my friends

I know who is my true friend
With that I have no friends
I am alone in the dark
Tossed away in the deep dark corner

My mind fights it
My soul feels it
Pain strikes my heart
Just at the thought of it

Where are my friends now
To be there for me
How can I call anyone a friend
When my best friend was never a friend
