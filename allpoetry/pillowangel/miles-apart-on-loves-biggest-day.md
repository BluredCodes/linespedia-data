title: "Miles Apart on Love's Biggest Day"
writer: "pillowangel"

Could you be further away, 
as close as the back of my mind, 
miles between of saltwater sky, 
And all I want to do, 
is find myself in you- 

Some days it seems 
we're stuck between 
all the things 
of real and dreams, 
waking lives, far away, 
dreaming of together days 

Could you be further away, 
as close as the back of my mind, 
miles between of saltwater sky, 
And all I want to do, 
is find myself in you- 

Ive lost myself 
in trying to find 
a way to bring you 
in my life- 
and now I wait, shadows long 
upon my face, was I wrong-

Could you be further away, 
as close as the back of my mind, 
miles between of saltwater sky, 
And all I want to do, 
is find myself in you- 
and all I want to do, is find myself in you.
