---
title: "In My Place"
writer: "Wings"
slug: "in-my-place"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/10380-In-My-Place-by-Wings"
---

I wanted to reach out to you. 
I wanted to dry your tears,
Hold you until the pain went away. 
But it was not my place.

I saw you look for me, the smile you managed, 
Just to say thank you for being here.
I moved through the line and ached
For the moment I could put my arms around you.
Did anyone notice you held me to long, too tight?
Could they see the many times
I had comforted you when she hurt you. 
Cradled you when she would not?

I tried to draw the pain from you 
And hold it so you could carry on.
 I felt your heart. 
I felt the love you could not express, 
Could not say but in a whisper through your tears.
Did they know? Could they see?  
The purest love between two friends 
Unadulterated untainted by crossing the line? 

They buried your father and I took my place
Back into the shadows of your life. 
You knew where to find me, when your heart hurt again.
