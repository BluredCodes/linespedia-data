The Little Girl’s Prayer A little girl sits in a rocking chair,In a pink night gown with her Teddy bear,Wondering if any body out thereWould take the time or even care.Because her Daddy is gone And her momma’s not homeThe baby sitter is on the telephone.So the little girl sits in a rocking chair In a pink nightgown with her Teddy bear.Wondering who will hear her say her prayers?Her Momma has a dreamHer Daddy has a scheme.But the little girl don’t know what it all means.She sits alone in her rocking chair In a pink nightgown with her teddy bear.She ask the stars and she ask the moon,Tell me will my daddy come back soon? But the moon don’t even say hello,The stars twinkle back “Darling we don’t know.”So A little girl sit ’s in a rocking chair In a pink nightgown with her teddy bear Wondering if anybody out there Would take the time or even cares.And who will hear the little girl say her prayers?Oh who will hear the little girl say her prayers?
