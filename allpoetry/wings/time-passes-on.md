Time Passes On
Walking,
wishing, 
wondering...
Time passes on
Crying,
cringing,
cursing...
Time passes on
Hoping,
hearing,
hurting...
Time passes on
Pleading,
praying,
pacing....
Time passes on
Sighing,
seeing,
sleeping ...
He's homeSince I am better known for lyrics any comments will be welcomed.Wings
