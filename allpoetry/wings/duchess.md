Duchess
I wanted to say thank you for your loyalty
I hope some how you know  just how much you meant to me.
The time we shared together the times we’d run and play
You were there when I was born I thought you’d always stay. 
The years past by and you were part of our family. 
You were somehow changing but I didn’t want to see,
That my first pet was getting old and soon her time would come
When you’d lay down in peaceful sleep but in Heaven you would run. 
So my dear beloved pet now the end be told .
While  I was growing up.... You were growing old . 
I love you , love DanaI am ask often to write memorials for those who have lost loved ones. This was for my granddaughter when her 14 year old pet died.
