I took myself out shopping, 
I really had no means 
I used a credit card 
Simple; so it seems.

My credit  card was welcome
I nearly every store .
Before I paid for this trip , 
I went out and bought some more. 

This was just too easy 
The card controlled my will .
All my heart desired ; 
Then I got the bill .

I am in a prison now .
though bars you will not see .
I found my own self guilty 
The warden here is me . 

I do regret what I have done 
You would think I could have stopped it 
Before I put myself in the 
Prison of empty pocket.This rhyme is about 10 years old . I am still serving time Wings
