title: "Satan Laughed"
writer: "Wings"

A young girl cried , not yet a wife ,
found herself bearing life. 

Satan whispered in her ear," 
This is not for you , you are so young dear. 
" What of all the things you'll miss 
parties good times what a list . 
Just call the doc , " it'll be okay . 
He'll fix you up take this thing away ."

The young girl thought ," why for one mistake, 
my life spoiled , let this THING them take" .
Inside her womb a baby cried and wondered 
why before he died .

Then satan laughed and danced around , 
" Oh the perfect thing I have found . 
The seed of life and they don't know 
I am one step closer to their soul. 
I can make a mother kill her child ".
Satan laugh and Hell went wild . 

While at Heavens gates the angels 
cried for one more baby that had died . 
God spoke up and said ," you've won again. 
But victory is MINE over all ole friend . 
I still hold the power to lock you away . 
Forever and always and then for a day . 
The children who have died by their mothers 
hand will rule with me in the end I WIN ." 

Satan laughed ," That may be so .  
But see what I've done before I go ?
I can make a mother kill her child " . 
Satan laughed and Hell went wild .
