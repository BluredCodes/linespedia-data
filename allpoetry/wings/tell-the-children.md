title: "Tell the Children"
writer: "Wings"

Hear me little children 
Be quick and be alert.
Not all grown up’s are friendly 
Children they will hurt.

Never talk to strangers.
Don’t get in their car.
Don’t even tell your name 
If you don’t know who they are.

If you should get lost 
While in a public place,
Look for a policeman
Not just a friendly face.

Always play where others play,
Don’t hide or be alone.
Know your parents by their name 
Learn how to use a phone.

Should someone try to grab you 
Kick and bite and scream.
Some adults aren’t very nice 
Some are even mean.

Don’t touch or hug or kiss,
If it does not feel right.
Even if you love them, 
Tell,  put up a fight.

If it is Mom or Dad,
Grandpa or Uncle Joe
That make you feel uncomfortable 
Tell someone else you know.

Tell a teacher, tell a friend,
Tell until someone hears you.
Don’t be afraid to tell the truth 
If anybody hurts you.
