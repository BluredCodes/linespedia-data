---
title: "A Memory of Love"
writer: "Wings"
slug: "a-memory-of-love"

Our love was wild full of youth and foolishness,
testing, trying, learning.
Time filled our minds with useful things,
home, work the children
We drifted apart and could not
find our way back.
We were lost, lost from each other
and lost in our own lives. 
We moved on. 

Though our paths crossed from time 
to time our hearts never connected again.
We grew up.... then we grew old.
I have long since released the pain
even replaced it with more. 
I must challenge myself to stir a memory.

But sometimes I hear a song play 
Or see a tree in an open field
with a tire swing.
I close my eyes and we are
together again young and in love.
