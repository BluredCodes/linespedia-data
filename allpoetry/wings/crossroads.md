Cross Roads
I stand at the cross roads of never or forever.
I really do not know which way to go. 
Nevers' road is dark , unfamilar with no promise. 
Forever is a rocky winding road .
Neither road is easy , both hold hidden dangers . 
One is right the other is so wrong . 
There is no turning back once the road is chosen , 
No matter how rough or how long .
So many broken promises on the road we have just traveled . 
The wounds are deep the scars are never gone .
Forevers road is together through all kinds of weather . 
Nevers road is traveled alone .
