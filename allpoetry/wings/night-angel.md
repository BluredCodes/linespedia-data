---
title: "Night Angel"
writer: "Wings"
slug: "night-angel"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/13138-Night-Angel-by-Wings"
---

Take my hand 
Walk with me to a place of fantasy 
Close the doors and shut out the 
World for the time we have together. 

Tomorrow will bring chaos and trails 
But tonight let me brush the worries from 
your brow.
Let me stroke your hair and kiss your fingers.
Don’t talk; let the power of my love 
sooth your troubles.

I will be your angel watching over 
you while you sleep.
I will keep away the demons 
of the night and let only 
Peaceful dreams come to you.
Tomorrow when you wake I will be gone. 
But you will know I was there for
I will leave my love by the bedside.
