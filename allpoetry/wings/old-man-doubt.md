---
title: "Old Man Doubt"
writer: "Wings"

Old man doubt ,Why do you show your face
When my hopes are high ? 

Why do you beat down my door 
Every time I try ?

How do you know when I am weak , 
And bring failure to my mind ?

Why do you fight encouragement 
You tell me nothing kind ?

Your voice is loud though only I can hear ,
So baffled here I sit .

The battles over , the victory yours.
You win again I quit !
