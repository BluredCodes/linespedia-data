I see  you every day 
At your best and at your worst.
Some days I can’t stand you.
Other days I adore you. 
But some times I look at you and my heart 
is over whelmed with my love for you. 

I am breathless and  a rush of emotion fills me.
I know the touch but it feels new and different.
It is gentle , yet it is a hungry touch. 
I am swept away in your embrace 
Lost  in a world that belongs to just you and me.

We are one I begin where you end.
This  could be puppy love  or infatuation 
So what if is ... It has lasted for 15 years.
But you can still give me first date jitters.
