---
title: "Garden of Thorns"
writer: "Wings"
slug: "garden-of-thorns"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/42440-Garden-of-Thorns-by-Wings"
---

In this world of grief and pain ,

Pleasures far and few .

In a place of friendly foe ,

I can't tell who is who . 

I plant a garden of those whose path 

I cross along the way .

Who to trust and who to watch 

I weed it every day .

As seeds for weeds and flowers grow 

Only time will tell ,

Who to leave and who to love ,

Who will bloom and who will fail .

Sometimes my garden of friends looks bleak 

In anguish I do morn . 

But then I see a rose has grown 

In my garden of thorns .
