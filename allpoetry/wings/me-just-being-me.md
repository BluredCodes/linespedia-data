Comments and Suggestions
A hint here and there
How to match your clothes
How to wear you hair.

What will others think? 
See what others see
What, I ask, is wrong 
With me just being me? 

I tried to be the good guy
I tasted being bad
I laughed my darn fool head off
At jokes that were just sad.

Pleasing one offend the other
I have forgotten who I am 
Who are you what do you want?
I am just a sham. 

Still I tried to play my part
Time and time again.
I really like my room here 
In this Looney bin.
