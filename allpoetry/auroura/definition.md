ME
	noun: 1. daughter
              2. friend
              3. poet
              4. activist
              5. bulimic

	adjective: 1. intangible
                                 (adj; incapable of being felt with touch)
                   2. not corporeal
                                 (adj; of the physical rather than the spirit world)

	an amorphous collection of
	thoughts
	and feelings
	and opinions

	without physicality

	lacking the sense of
	touch
	(vb; 1. to come into contact with
      	     2. to have an effect on
      	     3. to fondle in a sexual manner)

	synonyms: see nouns
	antonyms: loverI'm just really unsure of this one in general.  Any feedback
greatly appreciated.
