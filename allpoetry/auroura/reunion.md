heyit's been a while(sweet Christ you!)how are things?I'm good thanks(shattered by the sight of you)I thought you were in Toronto(why aren't you in Toronto?)my summer?it was alright(I spent it remembering your smileand the way you tucked my hand to your heart when we danced otherwise it was great)what about you?(do you smile when you kiss?)that's good(what if I kissed you right now?)yeahI'm going to the party tonight(does it matter?)are you?greatI'll see you there(deep breath)save me a dance
