I'm the girl in the 
corner of the gym
watching a 
certain someone
dance with
someone else

I'm the girl you
turn a way from
everytime a 
long legged ten
strides by

you come to me for 
free consultation 
and intellectual stimulation

discarding my
body 
for the 
fine mind inside

you'll psychoanalyze
you'll kindly criticize
you'll praise me to the skies

you'll debate the nature of the universe

but you won't
touch
why no one wants to
touch me

somebody
someday
is all you sayThis is very unfinished, but I've got the world's biggest case of writer's block.  Any suggestions on where I can take it?
