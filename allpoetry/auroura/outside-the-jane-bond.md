12:00 am
Friday night
            spins
around us
bright and loud and dizzy

we spin from the
dance floor
to the table 
            and back 
under a green disco ball

the party is just beginning

1:00 am
another hour
another bar

we're saturated with
                    music and liquor
so we fade into the corner
to talk 

sorry
I didn't catch that

we lean together
                         
what were you saying?

2:00 am
your head lolls
and your eyes flutter shut
                          time to go

in the doorway outside you 
strike a match 

you bring it to the
cigarette in your lips
 
and you look at me
                  right at me
through the flickering light

then you smile 

and everything 
stops
