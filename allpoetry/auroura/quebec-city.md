MEMO

TO: LAW ENFORCEMENT OFFICIALS
FROM: THE PRIME MINISTER'S OFFICE

RE: SUMMIT SECURITY PROTOCOLS

1. avoid physical confrontation
   if possible
   (but remember
   you've got
   tear gas
   and rubber bullets
   behind you)

2. grab the ones with
   megaphones and
   influence
   first

   then 
   take anyone who
   looks disrespectful
   (ie. anyone with
   dreadlocks or body piercings)

3. militants
   will have to be
   coerced
   into obedience

4. speak freely with all
   media representatives
   who approach you
   they know what to do

5. your duty
   is to preserve peace
   and the status quo
   at all costs

good luck
