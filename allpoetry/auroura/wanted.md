wanted

single male

 funny
self-aware 
and curious

should see the humour in
American politics
 
and know the true meaning of 
"anarchy"
(doesn't have to believe in it)

cannot confuse feminism with communism
 
heated debate required

must enjoy holding hands as much as having sex

and remember 
why he loved his last girlfriend

messy hair and slim hips prefered

rightwingers need not apply
