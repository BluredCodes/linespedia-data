A MESSAGE FROM YOUR LOCAL REVOLUTIONARY COUNCIL:

fellow citizens!
                             
our brains are 
shrinking 
to the size of
cathode ray tubes!

we are being 
commodified
lobotomized
and depoliticized
into Model T citizens
for the global village!

resist!

we deserve a world
uninterrupted
by commercials

we deserve a society
unmeasured
by the Nasdaq

and it's up to
us
to create it!

so pierce yourselves!
shred your Levis!
storm the House of Commons during question period
and refuse to read mainstream media! 
take to the streets with guitars and bongos and molotov cocktails!

put someone up against the wall because 
the revolution is
here!Just a little bit of fun I had...blame the Sex Pistols! ;)
