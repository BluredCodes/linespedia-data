dear sir

I'm not a whore
so stop 
trying to make me turn tricks

I don't want to 
snag my prince in five easy steps
(no one worthwhile is that easy)

I don't want to 
enhance my eyes with your
new mascara
either

this inquiring mind would rather know
why 
she's getting seventy cents to the man's dollar
why
modeling agencies recruit anorexics
why
you aren't asking the same questions

I know 
I know

your job is to
sell me
society in a pretty package

don't bother

last time I bought it 
I developed bulimia

so keep your pop psychology
and your prepubescent waifs

I'm warped enough to see 
through 
the looking glass
					
					Alicedoes this come off as too bitter?
