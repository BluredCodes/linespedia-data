it's been 
two years
since I saw you play

you're still 
singing the same songs
(including the one  
for me)

I'm sitting on the
same stool
with the same friends

I even get the same
melty feeling
in my gut
when you say
this next one is for a very bright young lady
and smile
in my direction

suddenly
I'm not just a 
lonely girl

I'm a poet with a past

(much more
dignified and melancholy)

I bet Mary Magdalane 
felt like this 
when she realized that 
Jesus was the son of god

after your second set
I run home to scribble until 
3 am
because that's what 
lonely poets
do

and because after two years and
several major traumas
you can still make me
