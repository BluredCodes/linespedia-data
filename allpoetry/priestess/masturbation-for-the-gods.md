...MaStUrBaTiOn For The GoDs...


';';';';';';';';';';';';';';';';';';';';';';';';';';';';';';';';';';';






    Deep red Cauldron holds his essence…
   Releasing; yet ceasing in denial of remnants…
  Begging for a cast of a Cosmic spell…
Waiting for a reaping from a Karmic hell…

Shattering lies smother sweet Virgin eyes…
 Molesting WaRLoCk saturates & MaStUrBaTeS for his GoDs…
   Where is he… 
     Or is it she…

     He cums to seek his refuge in a Sacred bowl…
     He said that soon he’s save her Soul…

     A Myriad of sounds ringing in his ears…
   Justify his tendencies…
 Despite their pleas…
He licks the juices from their fears…

     Found there wandering from figments to flesh…
     Resinated in my mind; vividly fresh…

Tearing our Petals again & again to satisfy…
  Finding our tears sweet as Honey when we cry…
   From what dusty trail of Starry skies…
    Shaddowed those Prisms in his eyes…






S.S.Snapp
2/23 & 27/02
