Black Rainbow...



She is so beautiful… 
mysteriously dark… 
there are many shades to her Aura of blackness…
Sensual… 
erotic liquid night… 
brings her forth… 
casting prisms of wisdom into my eyes…
I love her spirit… 
kindred to the Dragon… 
that painted this vision of mystical madness…
Incredible sadness… 
when man sees nothing… 
& there is everything there to soothe despair…
I try and count all the hues of black 
& still I am counting… 
forever mounting the Rainbow…
When she does shine with Goddess eyes… 
brown as earth… 
and deep as space… 
I race…
To the stars that cast her down for us to see… 
her vibrations set me free… 
for eternity…



S.S.Snapp
