Desperately Trying...

....................................................





My leaning turns... 		
     ...reach broken spirals...
	...Of peering twists... 		
   ...into profound depths;

Desperately trying...
          Until reaching upwards,
		
                    cuts me deep.

My spilling blood... 		
     ...staining canvas...
	...Of lifetimes... 			
   ...painting lies of illusion;

Desperately trying...
	  Until filling scenes,
			
                    in my sleep.

My sinful thoughts... 		
     ...peaked wanton minds...
	...Of symbiotic peers... 		
   ...needing answers;
			
Desperately trying...
	  Until faith was found,
		
                    where we weep.





S.S.Snapp
~ 3-6-01 ~

Author's Note:
It seems that in our lives no matter how hard we try we fall even harder... sometimes wanting to give up because we are so dizzy from failure. Sleep seems peaceful until the nightmares begin... but when we find that our dreams speak to us of what & who we are & are to become then we can begin to set ourselves free. The most important thing in life is to have Faith... either in a higher power or yourself... understand that they are the same... we have God/Goddess inside of us... elements of the Universe... trying is succeeding... if you believe.
