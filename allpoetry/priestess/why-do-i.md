Why Do I…?






Why do I feel like…
   There are a few of them… 
      Many to me…
         I need them to…
      They make me feel better…
   My friends…


~ Talents screaming and crying in vain.
~ Tormenting souls disillusioned by pain.


Why do I feel like…
   They have to come with me…
      It’s our trip…
         Many things we’ll see…
            Self discovery…
      I think that they…
   Do they feel it like…
I do…


~ Talents inside us; they’re crying in vain.
~ Non – expression of Genius is Literal pain.


Why do I feel like…
   There are a few of you…
      Many to me…
         I need you to come…
      You make me feel better…
   My friends…


~ Stop the insanity; we’re dying in vain.
~ Through Art we create and lessen the pain.


Why do I feel like…
   I need you to come with me…
      An art filled fantasy…
         Scripted tapestry…
      Sweet soliloquy…
   We are alive…
      My friends…
         You and I…
