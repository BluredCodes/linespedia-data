A Thinker...


.......................................................... 





My thoughts not yet conjured into words...

These thoughts which are like the winds of time;

brought by the lioness to the wizard;

to this wise man for he is kind.

Thoughts drift off water drops from my wings;

into his realm...

so full...

Pondering awhile on life,

sweet...

yet cruel...

Now is just an impressionistic residue of time.

I shall own the world for atleast one day, 

and it shall be mine...

for an eternity...




S.S.Snapp
