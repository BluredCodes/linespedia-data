Written by Fear...




This is for the Ladies of Afghanistan……



I read the words 
written by Fear.

I cried out loud.

Ignorance beat the Goddess 
without shedding a tear.

They hide; not proud.

Jealousy veiled her flesh, 
but not her Soul.

They denied their Shroud.

How can Yang love Yin 
with a heart made of coal.

No pride allowed.

By exposing their Cage 
was like a call for Death.

She can’t confide.

The game isn’t done until Woman 
has exhaled her last breath.

Suicide.




S.S.Snapp
11/6/00




Author's Note:
Oddly enough I wrote this last year & it is the topic of discussion now... a letter was sent out last year asking Americans for help for these ladies... they were being beat to death for trying to get to their only hospital that would see women for whatever ailed them. What can we do but speak of their pain & let others know we will not stand for this here... We can't do much about it over there if they are not willing to fight, but we can let them know that we feel for them. I know from being a woman that has been beaten much in the past that sometimes you can feel utterly helpless... especially when it's your lover, mother or father beating you. Well........ Justice will prevail... I have to believe that.
