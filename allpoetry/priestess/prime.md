Prime...



My whore wanting more... 
torture is but a slave to passion... 
darkened thoughts of wanton ways... 
my mental slave submits his heart with pen... 
and then I drink in, the sweetened tones of fantasy... 
tapestry of truths & dreams of silent screams of destiny... 
know me with my words & script... 
drink from the pool that cools the sword... 
that shines the last light of the torn sun... 
I am but one.




S.S.Snapp
