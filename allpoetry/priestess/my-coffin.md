My Coffin...



****************************************************************



Cold frail fingers returning me from my coffin;
     Through the webbed page of a winter season.
   I desire those eyes of eternity looking at me,
        Seeking absolute sub-conscious reason.


                I am coming out;
          Breaking free from confinement.
                I will be myself.


   Who will be Desire found searching inwards?
        With self-development; I see lifetimes within.
      Only those who stay awhile can answer.
           It will be you and I peering through our sin.


                      A wooden window
                For which I can see my light.
                      To us we are right. 



S.S.Snapp
~12/20/00~
