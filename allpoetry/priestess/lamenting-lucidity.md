Lamenting Lucidity...




Lamenting Lucidity ~

I am lost in thought
Why does it come in waves.
I’ve no time for brain rot;
A screaming soul it saves.

Lamenting Lucidity ~

I am lost in time.
Lost within myself I try.
Without words I’m a mime.
Silently in my head I cry.

Lamenting Lucidity ~

I am lost in space.
An ancient black abyss.
With the lips upon my face;
I seal my fate with a kiss.





S.S.Snapp
