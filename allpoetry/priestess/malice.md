Malice...


***


Premeditated aggressor finding time for rage.
Looking at my Soul through this fleshy cage.
Where is it that I may hide from you?
Is there anything you would not do?
Seeing life through a chaotic peace.
Do you know the Demons you release?
Aged and bitter is the child inside.
Probably wondering from what you hide.
How can you live this mournful game?
I can’t deny that we are the same.
Fevered torment makes them worry.
You’re willing to destroy them in a hurry.
Ancient battles awakened here.
You live your life based on fear.
You can’t escape from your death.
You live your life as if you’re SETH.
Why do you live in each and all?
Some will wait for you to call.
Before it happened; I did dread.
When I was gone they found him dead.


S.S.Snapp
3-15-01
