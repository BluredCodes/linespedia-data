Forbidden Fetish...
Rhyming Syllabet
Created By: Selene S. Snapp
......................................................................



Passionate Pleasures...    of the flesh elude 
my senses to a dream of submission.

Forbidden Fetish...    seeks to justify
my hidden desires for meat.

Totally Trusted...    is the subject not to 
hesitate without absolute permission.

Perfectly Pulsing...    are the veins
that pump full of heat.

Gracefully Groping...    a salted cage
with teeth; an animalistic rendition.

Assured Appetite...    from lovers
makes this oh so sweet.



~ S.S.Snapp ~
~ 1/27/01 ~

Author's Note:
* Rhyming Syllabet * ~  The first two words in every other line or rhyme scheme start with the same letter. The first 2 words that have the same letters must total the same amount of syllables as the other pairs of words with the same starting letter.
