Dawn Of Justice...
 



********


Dimly lit is the seeker; 
   a chaotic silence is the sound. 

*8*

A sense of peace is the search; 
   yet none has been found. 

*8*

Questions becoming endless; 
   reasons I'm not to know. 

*8*

Blissful feelings surrender me; 
   soon they're sure to go. 

*8*

How many must we covet 
   until we're finally free? 

*8*

When will our enlightened Dawn 
   of Justice let us be? 

*8*




S.S.Snapp
