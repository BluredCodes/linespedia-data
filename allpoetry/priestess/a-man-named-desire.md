A Man Named Desire...




For Richard Dunn
My Lightening God…



Without Desire 
I am not alive.
I have found in my Lover 
something delicious;
the flavor of freedom; 
a sense of sensuality.
My Lightening God; 
my mighty Warrior;
come dance with me, 
and let us fly.
Together we will live 
this life complete.
We cannot die.
All will see our destiny; 
they can't deny.
Entwined as one; 
we'll soar the skies.
I'll hold your hammer 
as you hold my pen.
If a name 
I were to give to you,
it would be Desire;
for without Desire... 
I am not alive.




S.S.Snapp
