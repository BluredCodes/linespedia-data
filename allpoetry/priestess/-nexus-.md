Nexus...
Alphouette - Created By: Selene Snapp

....................................................




Vivacious victims…
 Relishing radiance…
  Perilously plunging…
   Chaotic cadence…
    Seemingly simple…
     Mastered madness…
      Truly tyrannical…
       Sweetened sadness…
        Bastard brothers…
         Fighting freedom…
         Losing lucidity…
        Kindred kingdom…
       Harboring heathens…
      Fiery fist…
     Riveted refuge…
    Idiots insist…
   Plane pirate…
  Fathered fear…
 Clarion call…
Nexus near…




S.S.Snapp
9/14/01

* Alphouette * ~ This is a form that uses the same consonant or vowel for each word in one line. This may be rhyming or not. There is no limit to how many words or syllables used only that the same letter is used at the beginning of each word.
