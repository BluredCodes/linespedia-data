Weaver... 

....................................................................



The instant Sadness cocked back reality;
I found you looking in the eyes of Tragedy.
The Blue was lost...
and your life it cost.
She will never know by knowing you...
how great a man you were to be.
Your Soul, too soon for us,
from that cage was set free.
There are many more than you knew...
who cared...
for the wild man that did what he was dared.
For with us there was something 
intense you shared. 



S.S.Snapp
~ 10-2000 ~
