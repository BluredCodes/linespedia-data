Selene...Celestial sphere spiraling deeper into an erotic liquid night…Silence casts beams of serenity forever across Sapphire pools of slumber…With depths found perpetual mirrored in glassy eyes…An unseen dance swept desire higher than the stars…Serene senses triggered prisms… Lovelier than silver crystals gathering souls…She waits amongst the Divine and Crystalline…Exquisite moon-drenched Goddess bringing life by illumination…I am consumed by passions of knowing you in me…To set me free from confinement in this cage…I want to know…Written by:Selene Snapp9/18/01Authors Note:We are all drawn by something or someone real or not… My father had 8 months to name me & after much research of first & middle names he chose Selene Song… since I was a child I have been drawn to the Moon & the powers it hold… One of my favorite Goddesses in history has been Selene… I have a picture I made for her… silver, blue, with women & circled opalescent symbols… I had to share this…I hope you enjoyed the ramblings from my mind...
