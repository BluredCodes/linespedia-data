Confusing Reality...



When walking barefoot in the morning dew;
then back again;
I remember Love and being lost in you.
Locked in eyes so blue.
Lingering Desire still winning aged innocence.
Confusing reality rings.
I do not want to leave this place.
I see your face.
I had found in you... 
a song from ancient times;
soulful rhythmic rhymes.
Floating away in the notes of our music;
then back again.
My eyes are open;
Yours still shut.
Your Golden locks in a dance from sleep.
I inhale your scent;
to last all day long.
You are my song.
I shall see you soon, 
but sooner it should be.
We will meet under the Moon.
It’s our Destiny...



S.S.Snapp
