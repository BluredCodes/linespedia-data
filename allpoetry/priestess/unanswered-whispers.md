…Unanswered Whispers…



o888o888o888o888o888o888o888o888o888o888o888o888o888o888o888o888o888o

What fleeting epiphany reveals clashing desires reborn…
   Whose ancient whisper within muted wake strains visions…
      Why are those agonizing remnants dying silenced & sanctified…

I tried to be…
   …the whisper in my dreams…
      suffocating those horrid screams…

…but I am desire…
   perpetuating surreality in a cage made of flesh…


S.S.Snapp
2/20/02
