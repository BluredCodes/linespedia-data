…Bitter Ignorance…

;;;;;;;''''';;;;;;;''''';;;;;;;''''';;;;;;;''''';;;;;;;''''';;;;;;;'''''




Deformed echoes reflect chaos 
      & shadows from worlds known but forgotten…

Diverting forever found blinding; 
                 once ripe; now rotten…

Savage realms called mirrors of Souls 
      who’s images were bitterly crying…

Spiraling madness warped their 
                 ignorant minds from trying…




S.S.Snapp
2/22/02
