My Devil’s...





Victims found moaning for me...
........Their lips trained...
................Silky...
................Curvaceous...

Why do you cry...

My devil’s siren unnatural spirits...
........Their tears stained...
................Woman...
................Delicious...

Don’t ask me why...

Who will be the next to see...
........Buttocks mamed...
................Lover...
................Suspicious...

Welts never lie...





S.S.Snapp
8/3/01
