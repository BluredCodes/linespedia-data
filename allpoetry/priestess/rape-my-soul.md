Rape My Soul...



The clutch from unprotected hands 
leave slashes upon morals caged.
I have not refused them broken fingers, 
and desire into myself without flesh.
***
Chase me away with your lies of Lucidity?
***
I think not the thoughts of failure 
as your system tries to rape my soul.
You’re buying time as your dreams meld
with the scurrying rats of your race.



S.S.Snapp
