...Set Aflame...

It happened to me... 
Hell opened it's gates and fed me fire from it's lips... 
I quiver like a babe alone burning in fear...
Of would be lies & could be truths...
Own me in this pit of shame...
Amongst the fists I know so well...

I feel kept...
In a furnace...
Warmed by anger...
My refuge in this nightmare...
When will we speak again...

Living near insanity...
Neighbor of my mind...
...“Can I borrow a cup of blood...?”...
...“Will you feed the beast...?”...
...“Don’t you have a snack at least...?”

What life is there for a shredded carcass set aflame...
Will I have my day to rise from this grave...
I played his game...
Breathing future from the sky...
Don’t ask me why I sleep...
Nor why I need this pain to reap...

I smell the stench of horror...
Brewing torment in my soul...
Leave me be...
I must set me free...

I need to justify why I live...
And why to you my flesh I give...
When you have sought comfort in the cold...
Let me know...
I may meet you there...



S.S.Snapp
1/9/02
