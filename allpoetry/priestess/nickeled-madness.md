Nickeled Madness...



Debatable questions of rights and laws,
It is humans that are consumed by flaws.
Shiny beasts will blow brains on walls.
Will having one near sound clarion calls?
Shoot to kill was taught to us before;
As the years go by we’re learning more.
Humans are the ones who kill and hate.
Our in-animate guns cannot relate.
They feel nothing except our calloused hand.
Beautiful nickeled bodies had never planned
On killing our youth and elderly too.
I won’t be a slave because of a few.



S.S.Snapp
3-23-01




Author's Note:
I am against gun control… I believe in crime control… Everything can be used as a weapon (everything!)… Guns, knives, bats, swords, bow’s, knanchakus, poisons, axes, hatchets, rope, wire, fire, our bodies, chemicals… etc. to name a few of the obvious choices… and some  hidden weapons are pens, doors, keys, hammers, screwdrivers, cords, pillows, chairs, any kind of glass, cups, plates, shoes, shirts, socks, etc…anything that extends your reach. You get the idea I’m sure. If someone wanted to they could kill with any of these instruments & objects; any of these. So are we supposed to ban all of these because they could kill? They are just as effective as a gun. The weapon is not where we should be looking, but rather the individual. Each of us has a secret or two… if one of them happens to be a violent tendency then how is banning guns going to take care of that when anything in reach will do. As far as our children are concerned… the parents should be aware of their child’s problems, instead of blaming an object on the death of many. We are to blame not our creations (they do not create themselves).Thank you for listening to the ramblings of my mind...
