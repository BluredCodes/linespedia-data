Tribal Tunes
Alphouette
Created By: Selene Snapp

...........................................................................




Tribal Tunes	
        &
    Romanic Runes
Awakened Ancient Astral Abyss;
     Eradicating Egyptic Escape Erotically.
             Bewitching Barbarians
                       &
           Bequeathing Beastly Bliss.


Chilling Chimes
        &
    Rhythmic Rhymes 
Vigorously Vexing Viking Victors;
     Simply Satisfying Scathing Sins,
	     With Women Who Worry
                       &
                 Wanton Whores.




~ S.S.Snapp
~ 12/2/00

* Alphouette * ~ This is a form that uses the same consonant or vowel for each word in one line. This may be rhyming or not. There is no limit to how many words or syllables used only that the same letter is used at the beginning of each word.
