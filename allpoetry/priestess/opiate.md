Opiate...


666888666888666888666888666888666888666888666888666888666888666




She’s so smooth…

Demanding my attention 
   with her curves like smoke.

Sultry sin of my inhabitation…

Deep penetration of my Soul.

She changes me…

        …Initiating Narcosis…

I welcome my host of misology.

Sweet dreams perpetuating 
   while my essence released.

Increasing fluidic desires… 
   and salient tendencies.

Once chaste now wanton 
   freed by a prick…

Making me sick…

Crawling for more 
   like a chemical whore…

Scraping for lies…

        …Narcoleptic Insomniac…

Am I dreaming of Charon 
   or sailing towards the brink of Lucidity.

Hades beckons me while submerged in her 
   Euphoric Ejaculating Eden…

Cover me in kisses of hysteria 
   as I drown in this sea of Solemnity.



S.S.Snapp
1/13/02
