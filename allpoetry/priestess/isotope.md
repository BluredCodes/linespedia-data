Isotope...




......*......*......*......*......*......*......*......*......*......*......


Uncovered remains in a life of Dimentia;
Eloquent passion unveiled in timeless Beings…

Isotopic behavior burning images of Yesterday;
Deny destiny begging for wrenched Sufferings…

Fevered torment trying the Unforgiven;
Clenched tight the innocence once Existed…

Having been beaten down, alas we have Risen;
Integrity blinded by contempt once Resisted.

Author's Notes:
This is one of my favorite pieces because my sister & I wrote this together... I see we can still finish each others sentences... This is about some of our feelings about growing up in our environments with each other & the pain we shared through the verbal & physical beatings. We are stronger now for it so I am not sorry about our up-bringing... but we are still healing from those scars... this piece helped us a lot.
