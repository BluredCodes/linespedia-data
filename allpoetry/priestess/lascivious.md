...Lascivious...




...*...*...*...*...*...*...*...*...*...*...*...*...*...*...*...*...*...*...


Visuals enhanced day by day.
Gentle brushes along my skin.
Flesh flushing... 
boiling mercury...
surely this is no sin?


Arrousing aromas fill me.
Senses drawn to Water Beast;
A Sign that I am drowning...
can my Soul...
this lust release?


Passion called my kisses.
It beckoned me to satiate.
Epidermal pleasures...
inhaling deeply...
what is my fate?



S.S.Snapp
12/2/01

Author's Note:
I think I will have to explain parts of this piece; some of you may get it, but the rest of you won't.

I see this person almost every day. We brush against each other... laying a gentle, or not so gentle ;-) hand apon one another. We are instantly aroused & our passion boils by being near, hearing, or catching a glimpse of one another. This person is a water sign... as is most of my past relationships... I am fire. 

I am drawn to the Water Signs. My Water Beast loves to breathe in my fragrances from head to toe... revels in it... as do I with the alluring essence that envelops me. I believe kissing is one of the more passionate acts one can do, if done right... I will usually hold out until my heart is theirs... but I could not resist... I fell deeply & hard in the curves & silkiness of lips... I am torn between right & wrong... 

Anyways... this is a basic overview of thoughts that stirred this piece. I hope you can relate to this in some way... I know some of you didn't need this, but some of you may have.
