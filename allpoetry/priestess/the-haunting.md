The Haunting...




What haunts my mind…
With dreams unkind…

Taunting me…

I dreamt of war & burning embers… 
A ship aflame & I remember…

What did I see…

Bodies lying behind charred shrubs…
No buildings standing I was wearing scrubs…

Tyranny…

Went through fire to grab their needs…
On our pain the tyrant feeds…

Breaking free…

Aside the land of smoke-filled death…
There was the sea, which stole my breath…

A mystery…

We sailed away on another ship…
There were a few of us on this trip…

Where is she…

Three days later another dream…
But in reality I did scream…

Insanity…

Voices woke me with quaking sounds…
What was left were 2 massive mounds…

Reality…

They are free…




S.S.Snapp
9/19/01



Authors Note:
I had this dream on Saturday 9/8/01... spoke to my father & boyfriend in the morning about it & we tried to find the meaning in it... on Tuesday 9/11/01; 3 days later it became clear what the dream was... the only thing is... "she" is my sister & mother... they weren't there, but my father was... "she" lives by the ocean... there was a ship on fire... we can’t quite figure out if it is from a plague (biochemical warfare) or by a bomb or such... there is plenty more to this dream but I’d would rather leave this at that... I have a feeling what is coming next... it is far from over... my dreams now that come are haunting me...I hope you enjoyed the ramblings of my mind...
