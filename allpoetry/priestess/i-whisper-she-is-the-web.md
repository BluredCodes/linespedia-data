...I whisper  -  She Is The Web...



(The lines, "I whisper. She is the web." is to be whispered when read; the rest is read aloud.)






CALL ME!!!
Don’t change it.

***

I whisper.
She is the web.

***

This is the one.
With all the colors in the web.
The web.

***

I whisper.
She is the web.

***

You are weaved in the web.
I am weaved in your web.
Colors are laced.
Bodies defaced.

***

I whisper.
She is the web.

***

Her past has been traced.
Goddess wrath you will face.
This is the one.
She’s where colors deepen;
The web.

***

I whisper.
She is the web.

***



S.S.Snapp
~ 11/14/00 ~



Author's Note:
@thena $napp ~ My sister e-mailed me one of her Graphic drawings, and after looking at it for awhile in Awe; this is what I wrote back to her which started as an e-mail but ended up as a poem. She sent me a picture of a woman wrapped in her own webs which were filled with life & death; colors were spinning & spiraling in the background... she is a Genius... she entrances me with her visions... I do not expect you to understand the meaning nor the style of this poem, but I thought I might give an explanation.
