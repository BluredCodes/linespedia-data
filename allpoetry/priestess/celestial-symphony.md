CELESTIAL SYMPHONY...




I see you every night; you’re beautiful….


Your Celestial face revealing a tear. 
The frown and wrinkle near your eye.
Why….?
You ask as you look at us; 
scampering like the Rats we resemble. 
You see no rest; no rest in our Destruction.
Yet….
You still shine, so bright and wise…. and mine. 
Mine to see; my Throne in sleep.
I fly….
I fly in sleep to you and touch the sky. 
When I awake I’ll share your tear.

*888*

I see you every night; you’re beautiful….

*888*

When I look into your face I hear Music; 
music of Innocence. 
It makes me want to dance naked, 
and sing songs to you; my Luna….
You….
You know the Power over us you have. 
We submit to you. 
You’re beauty cannot be compared….
Maybe….
To a Celestial Symphony….!


*888*




~ Friday the 13th in October
~ Selene Song Snapp
