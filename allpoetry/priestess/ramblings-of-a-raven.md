Ramblings Of A Raven...

.........................................................



In a life before I knew my mother;
Under a Full Moon, Raven was her Lover.
Watching me grow into a Warrior of Dreams;
My Raven-like father is how it seems.
In that life, I fought since birth for what I knew.
Life came to me in sleep; oh how I grew.
Native was the land that fed me.
Running with the wild cats; I felt so free.
Staring into the eye of my bird;
The Ramblings of lives is what I heard.
Cursed by stones as black as coal.
They could not take my Soul.




S.S.Snapp
~ 12/21/00 ~

Author's Notes:
I believe this to be one of my Past Lives...
There is so much that goes along with this poem, but that will be in my book & screenplay... Sometimes we just feel strongly about things... having reoccurring dreams... going through hypnosis to bring things out to surface... trying to find a meaning... going to pshycics... This all started when my mom was pregnant with me... she had dreams of 2 Ravens & then dreams of 2 Butterflies... I was hit in the womb & my Korean grandmother wasn't too happy about my mom being pregnant by a big white man... not in those days... she didn't tell me about this until my book was done & she read what I wrote. It was very nice to be connected with her like that... fulfilling.... & sharing all these things I never knew before...

Thanks...I hope you enjoyed the ramblings of my mind...
