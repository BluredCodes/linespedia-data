Watcher...

;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;;




Piercing eyes... 
toward tender lies... 
of illusion and flesh. 
Through window pane... 
in stabbing rain... 
I’m waiting for 2 to mesh... 
Voyeur beast... 
within released... 
I remember those times 
when I was teased... 
with drawn blinds...
watching an eye-filled feast.
Shadows dancing...
silhouettes enhancing... 
our perverted minds. 
A sudden surprise...
would be demise...
of my fantasy released.




S.S>Snapp
~ 12/27/00 ~
