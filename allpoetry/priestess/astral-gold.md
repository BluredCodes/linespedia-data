Astral Gold...




The weaver of my shadows glowing
Raping light; the dark is flowing.
Where Astral Gold shows dreams awaiting
Spiritually and mentally penetrating.
Morrow’s laugh wants void from chilling
Chambers filling;
Blood is spilling.   

When I awake will I be leery?
Will my life still seem so dreary?
Astral Gold shows me tomorrow.
I must stop choking on my sorrow.
I feel alive deep in my sleep
Sanity I keep;
Where I weep.   

Friends will look with widened eyes
Thinking I may be speaking lies
When I awake I speak my dreams.
Morrow’s ramblings is how it seems.
Silent Mind would kill; will mortify
I would  die;
I would cry.   

Finding peace with Astral Gold.
In my dreams; my words are bold.
Flying with the Goddess Moon.
I close my eyes; I’ll see her soon.
Deep inside; ebony will flow.
I will show;
What I know.   



S.S.Snapp
