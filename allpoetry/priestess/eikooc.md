…EikooC… 






…My Knave… 

I asked thee only to watch my sweet… 
But in your spare time you ate my treat… 
See those swordsmen dueling there… 
I’ll bid them to thrash your bottom bare… 

…So Brave… 

You dare to suckle my chocolate kiss… 
Not a crumb nor morsel did you miss… 
Fevered cheeks gave your lies away… 
Savor the last meal you’ll have today… 

…My Slave… 





S.S.Snapp
