Safety Word...


*** *** *** *** *** *** *** *** ***



Your bound by me to do my will.
 I slap your face to give me thrill.
Inside your eyes I see a chill.
 When I say; lie very still.


Do not make a single peep.
 Or you in chains here I will keep.
On a pillow you will sleep.
 Do not flinch when you hear me creep.


I will keep you nicely bound.
 Through our sessions I have found;
I like having you around.
 Especially when I command; you sound.


Do not cry when wax gets hot,
 Or I will pour it; the whole lot.
You’re the best slave I have taught,
 And my service you had bought.


From your lips which quake disturbed;
 I had heard a single word.
For Safety’s sake you whispered BIRD.
 I’ll see you next time on the third.




S.S.Snapp
 7/27/01
