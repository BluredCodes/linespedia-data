Eradicating Minds...



***


Eradicating minds...
Simplifying life long dreams.
Trying to unwind...
This is only how it seems.
Fear was left behind...
Where we felt the silent screams.
This life I’m trying to find...
Against the grain of Rat Machines.


***



S.S.Snapp
2-18-01



My friends, boyfriend & I were all standing around in the desert after a day of riding dirt bikes... our philisophical conversations made me realize how different we were from most. A strange bunch of fleshy beasts.

***I hope you enjoyed the ramblings from my mind...
