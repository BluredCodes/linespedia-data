What Will Come...



Where confusion descends with the reminiscent;
Mere vagabonds’ despair deals darkness, 
found tranquil,
And the innocent will suddenly lie.
Memories become illusion to the flesh we see.
Having felt a dream becomes absurd to some.
~ What will come? ~
If I cannot be driven still; 
to fulfill my destiny.
Because my mind sees not through these caged eyes;
Through the Beasts lies of man-made truth.
What was here before us; 
before our mass?
We are not the smartest ones.
~ What will come? ~
If we cannot follow Astral Gold of stories told; 
Of ancient colors of worlds unknown …
Where is our growth? In my mind I took an oath
To find myself & destiny.
How can I fly if I know not 
What compels me to do my deeds;
Who will explain all my needs? 
If without this …
~ What will come? ~


S.S.Snapp
