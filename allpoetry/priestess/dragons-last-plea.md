Dragons Last Plea...




A ripple in time;
Time made by physical form.
I will walk through it.

I look in the past.
There lies a splendid Dragon
With a dirt blanket.

The Dragon sees me,
And says, “You are me awake.
I will see through you.”

“Keep me covered please;
from the cold winds and mankind.
They are not ready.”

I am not ready;
To let you lay under ground.
Will you hear my sound?

I will speak with you;
With my script and with my voice.
We will ease the choice.



S.S.Snapp
