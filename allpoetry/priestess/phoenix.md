Phoenix...

.........................................................................



I am blushing & flush with flattery... 
bestill my heart that feels the whisper from your mind. 
Come to me & swim in my words... 
dive in my thoughts of Universal understanding. 
Sensual wonders vibrating their ways through these pages; 
titillating Souls with comments of stimulating genders... 
You move me with your candor & impressed by your honesty am I. 
Dive into our world of scripted fantasy... 
my whore... 
wanting more... 
I am your Mistressex of passioned dreams. 
Listen to the silent screams...




S.S.Snapp
