...A Clockwork Orgy...



..........................................................



Sullen whores before we meet...
......		Trust me...

I have an endearing way to greet...
......		Lusty...

Your lips have never been so sweet...
......		Must we...

I want your bush trimmed nice & neat...
......		Musty...



Watch the movies on the screen...
......		So crude...

I will never get real mean...
......		Just rude...

That’s the sweetest ass I’ve seen...
......		So prude...

We will build your self-esteem...
......		Lie nude...
