OPENING MY EYE...




~~~ 3 ~~~


What speaks to me in my voice?
“ Rejoice! “
Why….
I try to be who I am, but why….
I cry….
I feel too strong the pain I see,
And hated you for being me….
I died….
So many times before you came….
I lied.
I dreamed of things that came to be;
The things I dreamed were scaring me.

“ Don’t hide! “

What do I have to tell myself that cannot wait?
“ Rejoice! “
I sighed….
My voice then said “ Start being me; I’m buried….”
“ Inside. “
“ Don’t worry what they say or do 
their sorrow’s not for you…. “
“ Confide….”
“ With script you’ll say what their lips could not. “
“ Have pride…. “
“ Within myself; behind my lids; you will see…. “
“ My Eye. “

“ Don’t hide! “





S.S.Snapp
