title: "Words Unsaid"
writer: "colleen"

Regret weighs on the heart of the you girlLike a load of steel onThe back of a worker.A list of emotions unsaidFill her worldLike white clouds and sunlight the skyAnd air fills your lungs.She has a script of wordsShe would've saidAnd things she would've done differentlyShe cries herself to sleep at nightImagining what she could've had.She cries herself to sleep at nightFinally sharing her emotions,A few years too late.
