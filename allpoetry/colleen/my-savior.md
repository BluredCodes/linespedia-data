title: "My Savior"

I stood there naked in front of the crowd
Tears streaming down my face
That came from no where
No cause,
They just started and never ended
As the people sat there
Whispering comments in a hushed tone
So I wouldn't know that 
They were talking about me
As they sat there staring with disapproving eyes.
I look for something to cover myself up with
But there is nothing around me;
No one to turn to for comfort.

Then from the back of the crowd
Appears the most comforting face
I have ever seen.
His deep brown eyes are full of love,
Familiarity and comfort,
Not shame and disapproval as everyone else is.
He runs up to my aid
Wrapping his warm arms around me
Covering my naked body
So that the crowd can no longer see me.
With all my flaws, all my nakedness.
He walks me out of the room,
Away from the crowd and the embarrassement
My head on his shoulder,
My wet tears staining his shirt,
He wipes them off my cheek
Kisses my forehead
And whispers softly,
'Everything will be alright.'
And I believe him.
