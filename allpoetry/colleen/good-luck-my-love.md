---
title: "Good Luck My Love"
writer: "colleen"
slug: "good-luck-my-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

I would gladly stand in the dimmest shadowsIf it means being able to watchYou shine brighterThan you ever could have imagined or dreamed.I'll gladly watch from afarIn a clouded haze of lonlieness and A love that will never dieAs you dance amidstYour praising admirersCharming them all with your Brilliant wordsAnd radiant smile.I'll stand outside the partyWith a smile on my faceKnowing that you have made it,All your dreams have come true,Which is all I could haveEver wanted.Good luck My love.
