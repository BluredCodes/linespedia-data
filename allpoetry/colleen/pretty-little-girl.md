The little girl dances in front of a mirror
With a wreath of flowers on her head
And her mommy's best old slip on her body.
She has bright red lipstick on her mouth 
And teeth
And blue eyshadow from her lashes into
Her eyebrows.
Hew cheeks are a bright pink,
And as her mommy enters her room, 
She smiles and asks,
'Don't I look pretty?'
