The pure white snow outside
Makes me feel like I'm flying.
Through the night sky I soar 
Unable to feel the cold 
As the white, untouched land spreads out beneath me
And I stretch my arms out to my side
As I fly over your house
And feel as though we 
Are falling in love all over again
And I can't get down, 
No matter how hard I might try.
It's just like being in your arms again,
Just like Heaven.
