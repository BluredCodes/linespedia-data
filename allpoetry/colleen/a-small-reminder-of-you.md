title: "A Small Reminder of You"

It doesn't seem fair
That you can't be here with me
That I'm forced
To walk into the ball alone
But the air smells like you
And it brings a small smile
To my perfectly painted face.
You're here in my heart my love
And that's all I could ask for
On this day
When the sun is shining brighter
Than the summers
When your smile was all it took
To light up the sky
And your hand fit so nicely
On the small of my back, 
Leading me through
All the balls and parties
With a love that took my breath away.
