---
title: "The End of Summer"
writer: "colleen"
slug: "the-end-of-summer"
source: "AllPoetry"

I think it's so sad
How the sun
Will never shine for us
Quite like it did 
So many years ago.
It wil never shine wonderful hues
Of pink, orange, and yellow
For hours and hours,
Long past the time 
It was supposed to be in bed,
Tucked away,
Asleep in its bed
Resting its rays
Until the next day.

The sun
Will never again
Fall gracefully on your face
Touching with love
Those soft lips 
That felt like home.

Never again will
We own the summer,
Feeling free to run around
In the midst of our youth,
In love with the summer
And each other.
