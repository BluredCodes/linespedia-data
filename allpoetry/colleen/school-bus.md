The bus left us behind today
Again,
If you wake up
Just half a minute too late
You've missed it all.
All the happy boys and girls
Bundled together on the bus
And we're still left standing.
You'd think we'd be best friends by now
We're always the last ones.
The last ones at the bus stop
The last one's to the party
The last one's to realize 
The lonlieness of holding onto your heart 
Too tightly.
You'd think by now
We would'e taken the time
To learn each other's secrets,
To know more 
Than that you oversleep too much
And I take too long getting ready.
But you're still hung up 
On the time I laughed at you
For saying you were in love
With me
