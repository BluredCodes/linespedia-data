title: "Sleepless Nights"

I search for answers To questions I don't fully understandAnd it keeps me up at nightScanning the channelsHoping my answers will come to me From the late night GodsBut it gets me no where.The clock ticks by slowlyUntil the next thing I know Light's seeping through the blindsAnd my eyes begin to give way to sleep,Giving up on these tough questions of lifeUntil the alarm blaresUnwelcoming musicStarting another day of more unanswered questions
