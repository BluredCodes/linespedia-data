---
title: "Feeling Poetic"
writer: "colleen"

I feel poetic right now,
Doesn't that figure?
The world is ending and I'm feeling poetic.
The ideas are stirring in my head,
Ignited by my fears of what is to come,
Whether or not this night will ever end.
Chaos is brewing all around,
The sound of sirens fill the air
And the land is dark,
Leaving open all our fears.
But until my last candle burns
I will contunie to feel poetic,
This pen will never leave my hand 
As the world turns and the chaos ensues
So does my pen and my writing.
