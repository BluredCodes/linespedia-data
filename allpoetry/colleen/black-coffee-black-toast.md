Black Toast, Black Coffee

She eats toast
With coffee
Black, 
Every morning
While skimming through the paper
Trying to relate to world events.
It’s not that she likes the
Overly burnt toast
Or the bitter coffee
That once made her grimace 
On the first morning he made them for her.
It’s not really a matter of liking
Or disliking though,
It’s just something she does
Like brushing her teeth everyday
It’s just something that needs to be done
So disease doesn’t spread
And things don’t become uncomfortable again.For a friend:)
