What if I was your Princess
And you were my Prince?
We could have stayed forever
In that immaculate castle we loved.
Never being forced to leave by anything.
Not wind, or rain,
Not the snow that fell
Like soft tears from Heaven
The day after I left
And the wall wouldn't have crumbled
In a moment of sorrow
The day we said 'good-bye.'
