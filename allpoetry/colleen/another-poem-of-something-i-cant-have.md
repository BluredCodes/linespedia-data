Paradise visited me
In the form of a dream once
It felt so real,
It's hard to believe it was
Just a dream
But you were there
The man of my dreams
And love of my life, 
I wonder if I'll ever see you again?

We loved each other endlessly
On long summer days.
By the pool
In your car
Locked safely in the staff closet
(They'll never know).
I woke up smiling every morning
Until August died
And we said 'good-bye'
Swearing we'd never look back.

And last night my dreams
Brought me back to a land of paradise
Where you hold me in your arms forever and ever.Yeah, I know, I like to write about my dreams and I know it's sad, but it's all I got...
