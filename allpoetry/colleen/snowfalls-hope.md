title: "Snowfall's Hope"

Crystalline shimmers
Fall aimlessly into the night sky
And there's a warmth in the air
I've never felt before
This late in the year.
The trees shake with fright
At the wind's overwhelming presence
But I smile to myself
That this time things might be different.
