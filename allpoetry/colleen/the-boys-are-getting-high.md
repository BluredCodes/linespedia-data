The guys smoking weed
By the pond
in the deep darkness of night
were gone as quickly
as you left my arms 
in late August all those many years ago.
But the uplifting smell
Remained long after thier absense.
And I was sad
because I meant to say,
'Hi'
Before I passed them by,
I always thought the short one
With the shy eyes
was cute.
But then again
I always did have a thing
For short boys who smoke too much weed.
