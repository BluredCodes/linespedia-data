---
title: "Secret Love"
writer: "colleen"
slug: "secret-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

It's late. 
I crawl out of my bed
And up to the school 
Where you are waiting
I walk past all of the sleeping houses
To meet you, 
My love.
The rest of the world is oblivious,
They don't need to know.
We kiss each other hello,
I feel your tongue inside of my mouth
And your hands roaming my body;
They are cold on my bare skin
As they move underneath my wool sweater, 
Searching for warmth.
This is so right.
Who cares about the cold,
We have each other
And our seceret love,
Our meeting underneath the stars
That will keep us warm throughout the night
And all of the cold winter nights that will follow
For as you kiss my body
I am reminded that nothing matters more
Than having you here with me tonight.
