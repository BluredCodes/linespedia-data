My name is Colleen
I don't like to eat green beans
I swear I'm not mean
Even though my feet may not 
Smell Clean.

It's because I wear sandals everywhere
Even when I do my hair
Or when I ride my mare.
Yes I know, we're such a great pair!
I hope he doesn't get eaten by a bear
Even though I didn't see him 
The last time I was there
And it's not fair 
Because I think I loved him
Even though it seemed 
Like I didn't care.I was in a goofy mood, the effects of too many exams...
