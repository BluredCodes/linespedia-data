Here we are, Here's the bridge.Are you ready?Are you going to jump,And dive head first into the crashing waves,Head first into what may lie below the surface,Into the unknown?Not knowing what the out come maybe,Not knowing  after it all,After falling more than150 feetYou'll ever surface againOr if you'll be able to touch the bottomAnd bring me up a shellOr if you'll end up lost on the bottom of the sea,Never to touch the world you love So much ever again.So, are you ready?
