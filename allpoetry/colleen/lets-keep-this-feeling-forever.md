title: "Let's Keep This Feeling Forever"

There is nothing quite like the feeling
Of kissing the one you love
For the first time,
Feeling their soft lips easily meet yours
Meshing together in beauty.
Under a blanket of stars
With the summer air on your face
And your hands in his hair 
As his hands slowly explore every part of your body
And you can feel it all the way down to your toes
While you say a silent prayer that this feeling
Will never end.
