This land is haunted with memories of another time 
Old friends and lovers 
Who wandered along these paths together. 
If these trees could talk 
They would tell tales over a hundred years old 
Of first loves and first kisses, 
Laughter and tears. 
When he first said, 
'I love you,' 
And she first cried out 
In pleasure with his magic touch. 
These trees could tell us unimaginable secerets 
Filled deep within this forest.

There's the large tree 
That was her shade
Comforting her from the awful news
While she sat on that rock
And cried for hours
Even though the telegram said he felt no pain.

Outside 
The fighting continues
Harsh faces of reality greet us all each day
But within this home of trees 
And stones unturned
Lies an untouched land of mystery
And stories left to be told.it's just a lil bit extra, still not quite done...let me know how it's coming:)
