New Love


I think its great that you have a new love
I'm very happy for you.
That you have found somebody who
Doesn't mind when you come in smelling like smoke
Or when you show up at 9
When you said you'd be there at 8.
I think it's great 
That she's a size 2
And her hair is always perfectly straight
And you couldn't find a blemish on her face
And she never breaks a sweat
Even when the temperature breaks 100.
If you tried.
And I'm glad that she knows when to laugh
and when to shut up;
I guess I always had trouble with that.

I have a new love too.
His name is Dave,
He's older than both of us.
He sings me beautiful songs through my radio 
And keeps me company on rainy days.
He sends me presents from the road
And treats me like a queen.
But when I go to sleep at night
My bed is empty
As his voice comes through my radio
And sings me to sleep
As I drift into a dreamland
Where I can be with the one I love
And I am no longer afraid of love
And the power it has over us all.
