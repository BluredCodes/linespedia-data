Winter's song
Calms a lonley soul
As I stand here 
Before this place
I've spent my life.
21 years spent
Growing and learning,
Laughing and crying.
Is there really going to be a day
When this isn't home?

The crispness in the air
And the first few stars
Glimmering around
As the sun gently sets
And the temperature slowly drops
Remind me of times
When college was a dream
And my 'true love'
