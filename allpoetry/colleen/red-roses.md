The red roses are dying.
I didn't even notice.
I didn't notice the red fade
To a gross shade of pink
As I happily cleaned up the dying petal,
Oblivious to the brown on
The edge of each petal.
I didn't see the brown of 
The rose water
Or notice the awful stench
That filled the room.
It wasn't until one petal remained
That I took noticeOf the dying flowers
And I cried over my last reminder of you.
