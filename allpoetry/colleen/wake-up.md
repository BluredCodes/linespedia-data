Springtime’s approaching, 
Don’t you think it’s time you woke up?

It’s already March,
Can’t you feel the
Sunlight shining on your sleeping body,
Calling you to wake up?
You’ve slept too long
The alarm clock has been
Going off for weeks now,
I really think it’s time you wake up.

Summer’s shouting in the distance,
Can’t you hear her,
“I’m on my way
and I expect you to be awake!”
You can’t let her down.

You’re body’s well- rested,
The bruises have healed.
So your leg’s broken in a few places 
And your fancy car
With the over- priced rims is no more-
You don’t have to shy away from 
Your beautiful world.

So don’t you think it’s time you wake up?This is for a friend of mine who's been in a coma for over 3 weeks after an accident.
