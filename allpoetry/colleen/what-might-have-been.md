---
title: "What Might Have Been"
writer: "colleen"

Sometimes I wonder if I would be happier
Just being your wife.
Staying at home with the kids,
A hot meal waiting for you
After a long day at work
You'd greet me with a smile
And flowers every evening.
And each morning I would
Be able to thank God
For allowing me to be lucky enough
To be able to spend my life
With my soul mate.
But I guess I may never know
Since I left before I got to say,
'I love you.'
