---
title: "Memories"
writer: "colleen"
slug: "memories"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I hold in my memory 
A magical, special place 
Where two lovers are forever happy together
They do not let the 
Evil tempters of time and distance
Stand in the way of their love.

It is eternally summer,
The sun shines forever
In this paradise
And at night a gentle breeze blows into the bedroom
Of the lovers
And they can hear the waves crash 
To the shore in the distance
As their bodies become one.
Nothing on Earth is like this place.

Sadness and heartache
Are not welcome here,
In this paradise of love,
They try, unsuccessfully
To sneak in every so often
But are never allowed in
The love here is too strong.

In my memory
He lives and breathes for her,
And she does for him.
Savoring every moment together.
In my memory
Their love has no end.
But it is just a memory,
A memory of a distant place far away
A place I dreamt up
On a lonley night;
A dream I hold onto
In hope that it will one day be my own.
