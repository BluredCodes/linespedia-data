Time passes us by so quickly
and we don't even realize it 
Until it is too late
To say how much fun you had
Just being you
Or how beautiful he looked
When the sun shined on him in the middle of 
A hot summer day,
His eyes glistening with love.
Or how happy he made you feel with all of the little things
That he did
Just trying to get you to smile;
Or to simply be able to say 
'I love you.'
