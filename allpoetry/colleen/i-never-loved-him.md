---
title: "I Never Loved Him"
writer: "colleen"

I never loved him.I know you may have thought I didOnce, a long time ago,But I never did.I never loved him.I realize it may seem I didBy the things I write,The mushy phrasesI'd never utter aloud,But I was in love with loveBecause I never loved him.Maybe it was the Magic of the summer airThat made it seem like my eyes shone With love only for himAnd that put that silly grin on my face.But don't be mistaken,I never loved him.Maybe all my talk of such beautiful eyesWould make you think otherwise,But I can guarantee, I never loved him.His eyes really are the most beautiful brownI have ever seen. It may seem like a defense,But you can take my word,I never loved him.
