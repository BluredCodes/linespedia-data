Hiding from our boss
In the closet,
In the stall.
Long looks of desire across a crowded room
When it felt as though non one else was around.

Long hours of overtime together.
Butterflies in my stomach
When he stands in the doorway, 
Ready to discuss 'work.'

Not wanting to get caught.
Praying the door is locked
As my bra falls to the floor
And my body lets out a moan in pleasure.

The look of love in your eyes
As we go back into reality
Where no one else knows about our
Private, daily meetings.
The smile on my face as I return
To my daily duties counting the minutes until
My next break.
