---
title: "Lost Dreams"
writer: "colleen"
slug: "lost-dreams"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/9992-Lost-Dreams-by-colleen"
---

What happens to our lost childhood dreams?
Do they get tossed away
With the rest of our childish toys and ways?
Or are they collecting dust somewhere,
Locked tight in a must, old attic
Only to be taken out
In times of nostalgia
To bring a tear to our eyes
As we remember lost loves
And things we forgot to say?
Will we ever recapture 
That blind ambition that led us 
Through those 18 or 20 years,
The ambition and hope that couldn't be stopped
By anyone or anything?
Will we ever reclaim that hope,
To believe that such dreams were possible,
That he and I would really get married,
That I'd write the all- American novel one day?

What happened to the wish 
The young girl used to make
On her star every night?
Has she given it up for a satisfactory life
With a man who is anything but
Her Prince Charming?
Did her dreams fall
When her star fell,
Into the great, dark night
Never to be seen again?

So what has happened to our lost dreams,
Did they get tossed somewhere at sea,
Or are they still deep inside
Each of us
Waiting to be rediscovered again
And to finally be realized.
