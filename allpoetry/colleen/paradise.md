I had a dream of paradise last night
We lounged on the beach all day.
We were all dressed liked hippies,
Even you.
You gave up your thuggish ways
For a tie-dye shirt
And some birkenstocks
And some sugary kisses
From a long- lost- love.
Money wasn't an issue
And I knew I'd never have to worry about missing you
Because in my bed you'll lie every night
As our bodies move together
With the steady beat of the ocean.
With each crash to the shore
I feel you in me more and more.
And your body is my blanket,
And your kisses heal my pain
As we lie together in paradise.This really was a dream i had last night.
