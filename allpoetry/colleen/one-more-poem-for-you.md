I miss the feeling of being 'your girl.'
The only one who could tame
The wild butterfly
Without her fighting through the net
For freedom.
You held the net with such grace
And gently pulled her majestic wings apart,
It was painless
Until that day you let my wings go.

I miss that feeling
Of you always being there
And my wings being free to spread
Far across the sun-lit sky
Without the fear they'd
Be torn apart
By viscous, careless hands.
