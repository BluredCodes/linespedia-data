title: "Summer Girl"

I was blessed enoughTo have been born a Summer Baby.August gave birth to meIn a time of uncontrollable warmthUnder the full moon's gazeAnd they smiled with gleeAs I danced into the world Promising to alwaysStay true to my natureAs a Summer Girl.Never letting my raysShine in one place for too long,Sharing its light with all,But always coming back just in timeTo see the flowers bloom And the butterflies flyAs the rest of the world sets thier Clocks an hour aheadAnd lost loves are reunitedIn a magical time called Summer.
