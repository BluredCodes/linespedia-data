The fireflies light the skyFlying in a maze,Teasing the childrenTrying to capture themTo make a new pet.And the children run aroundWith a look of absolute glee and uncontrollable happiness,Like we did when we first fell in love.And I smile at their innocenceWhile laughing at ours.
