It hurts painfully,
     Why must I be perfect?
          Just like my brother?
     I scratched my wrists,
'til they bled,
     Missing a vein,
          Not by much, not at all.
     I'm a brainy person, 
Have a lot of smarts,
     I'm an artist, a poet
          A writer as well,
     I even have a boyfriend,
Don't I just have it all?
     No. I have people,
          Expecting way too much,
     Like all A's forever,
And the best at all the sports,
     I just want to sit to cry,
          I'm just the quiet one,
     I do the best I can,
What the hell more do you want?
