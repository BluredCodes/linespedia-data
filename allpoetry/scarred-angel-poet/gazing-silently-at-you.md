title: "Gazing Silently At You"

My heart's confused,I don't know what to do.My heart's telling me,That I'm in love with you.I look around,Seeing couples kissing.Then I begin to cry,When it's you I'm missing.You pull me to you,Into a heartfelt hug.Knowing this won't work,Gives my heart a tug.I want to tell,Just how I feel,But when I try,My lips become sealed.When I give youthis, if I do,I wonder what reaction,I'll get from you.
