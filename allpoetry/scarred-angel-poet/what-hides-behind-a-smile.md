Many things hide in my eyes,
Reflecting brightly what I feel,
Showing emotion in greater size,
Love, hate, when I want a meal.

My eyes express many things, 
They burn with much emotion,
And whether or not my heart sings,
Or if I'm delighted by a pun.

Somehow things hide inside,
Even through traitor eyes,
Now it's if I've died,
No one notices, not even guys.

Even though I laugh and play,
It's as if inside's down a dial,
My depression's here to stay,
Isn't it amazing, what hides behind a smile?...
