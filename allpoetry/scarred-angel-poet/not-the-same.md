(Dedicated to David Griffin)
I call you everyday,
Just to hear you speak,
Just to hear you say,
Those words I love to hear.

It's never going to be the same,
As talking to you in person,
And hearing "I love you" by my name,
Or having you hold me close.

I walked three miles with you,
And I was just content holding,
Holding hands, oh what can I do,
When it's you I'm dying to see?

I yearn to have you hold me,
Close, but gently, still,
And I want you to see,
How much I really love you.

I call you everyday,
And we talk, as always,
And I'll love you all day,
But it's not the same as seeing you.
