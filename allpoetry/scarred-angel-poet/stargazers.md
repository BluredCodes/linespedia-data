I look into the midnight blue,
Then I look down at you,
I draw closer to you lips, 
But I saw something I could not miss,
A shooting star!!!

I make a silent wish with all my heart,
That we will never,ever part. 
I turn my head back to your lips,
As we share a deep, passionate kiss,
......Just two stargazers, always and forever.
