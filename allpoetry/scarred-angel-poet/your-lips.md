---
title: "Your Lips"
writer: "Scarred Angel Poet"
slug: "your-lips"
source: "AllPoetry"

In your dreams,
There's magical thoughts,
All things are real,
Unless you dream they're not.

So if this is a dream,
Don't wake me.
Be nice, because I'm glad,
I have you, you see?

I love to talk to you,
And just hear you laugh.
We make a perfect couple,
You do the math.

Sometimes I'm a poet,
A singer, or songwriter,
But just talking to you,
Makes my day always much brighter.

My heart sings for you.
And I have to ask you something...
What's the best thing you can do with your lips?
The answer's not kissing, but...talking.
