Mom and Dad I'm sorry,
I'm sorry I made you mad enough to hate me,
I'm sorry for any pain I caused,
I'm sorry for everytime I broke your laws,
I'm sorry you're ashamed of me,
I'm sorry for everytime I tried to make you see,
I'm sorry I wanted you to love me,
I'm sorry I hoped you had a heart,
I'm sorry I tried to play a son's part,
I'm sorry I ever entered your lives,
I'm sorry for getting my blood on your knives,
I'm sorry I'm not what you want me to be,
Mom and Dad I'm so sorry,
That you never felt I deserved an apology...
