"Strange Dreams"   11 - 21 - 01

this world is strange
these lands unknown
these creatures alien
these lives untold

where am I?
who are they?
how did I get here?
I had been led astray...

The world goes black...

I awake to cold
a single light
I then feel the poke
the men in White!!

What are they doing?!
I am alive, you see!!
Oh no, I can't move!!
They will surely devour me!!

The world goes white...

I awake and scream
my mom rushes in
it was only a dream
and hugs my silver skin...i was half awake and just finished watching a marathon on the Sci-Fi channel -- no more explanation needed, eh?
