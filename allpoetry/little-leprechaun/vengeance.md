'Vengeance'  9 - 9 - 01the pain I feelwhen it looks at methe hate I feelwhen I look at youthe acceptance I wantwhen they look at methe love I so needwhen I think about uthe long nights I crywhen I think about itthe dark rage I feelwhen I plan itthe vengeance feelwhen I commit to itthe anger I feelwhen I pull back on itthe relief I feelwhen its over & done withvengeance exactedpain releasedI got them all backI got them all beat.....Columbine.... do you think this is what they were feeling??
