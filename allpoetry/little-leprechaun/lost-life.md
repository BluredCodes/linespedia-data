"Lost Life" 1 - 21 - 01

do u see the sorrow between these eyes??
caused by the hurt, the blade of lies
can you smell the death in the air??
lingering doom, forever there
can you feel the heat of his soul??
the rage is great but his body cold
live his life and do the math
the end result is this blood bath
was there ever hope for his heart??
now, all I know, are the shredded parts
of a once bright, vibrant boy
i will not lie, I wanted to be that boy
free from her, the controlling bitch
free from pain, its searing itch
now it's over, his goal has been reached
He escaped from hell, he jumped into the breach...

I miss you, manlost a friend a few hours ago and this is the result of my tears.... enjoy, I know I never will     no offense intended...
