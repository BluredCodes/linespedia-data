"The Test"  5 - 15 - 01

Battered and Bashed
tossed and turned
Wishing an end
to this hopeless world

Bruised and Broken
scarred and stoned
I so hate these feelings
I so hate this world

Sick and Tired
lonely and depressed
I so wish an end
to this gut-wrenching mess

Betrayed and  Beaten
used and abused
I so hate these people
I so hate this world

so short and sweet
sour and cold
this life has been
a hurricane of hate

An end to this life
and end to this mess
I flunked this life
I have failed The Test
