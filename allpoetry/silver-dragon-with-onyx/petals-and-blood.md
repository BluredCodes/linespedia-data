---
title: "Petals and Blood"
writer: "Silver Dragon with Onyx"
slug: "petals-and-blood"

Sweet, velvet rose of red
I stare at thee, 'til thee is dead
And when thou art withered and dry
Diamond tears flow from my eyes
I watched thee grow from a bud
As thee died, petals fell with blood
Even though thou art crumbling and small
Thou withholds your velvety feeling through all
My sweet, innocent rose of red
I stared at thee, 'til thee was dead
