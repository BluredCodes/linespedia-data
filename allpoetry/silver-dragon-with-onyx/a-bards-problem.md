title: "A Bard's Problem"
writer: "Silver Dragon with Onyx"
slug: "a-bards-problem"

I sing of death and pain and such
And wonder where the beauty went
I wrote such pictures and happiness
Or at least I wrote in mystery
I don't know where these depressing words come from
I want to write of joy again
And soon I will
When I have reason
But for now...
I have reason to write in pain
When I'm inspired 
I will write more lightheartedly
