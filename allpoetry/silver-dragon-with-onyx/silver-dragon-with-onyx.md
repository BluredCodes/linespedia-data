A blackened stone
Shining smooth and sleak
Magick lives within
It's hardly for the weak

Power to those
Who gain control
Which few have done
With the stone like deep black coal

But I have succeeded
Where most have failed
The stone is part of me now
Although the truth remains veiled

Strength and hope radiate
I am from the ancient times
Wisdom adds to the ability of my stone
And I dance to the music of lyre, flute, and chimes

Under the stars and moon I sleep
By a flickering fire
As it soothes into soft, glowing embers
I shine like rain drops as I lay in the shire

I wake and stretch my metallic grey wings
Creating a cool breeze, that blows the leaves into the crystal sea
I am the Silver Dragon with Onyx
By the sea and under the the ancient willow treePoem behind the name
