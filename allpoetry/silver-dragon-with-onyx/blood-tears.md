title: "Blood Tears"
writer: "Silver Dragon with Onyx"

So many things said by peers
Passing through my sensitive ears
Across my heart it all smears
I try to make it disappear
All is following and never clears
I wish only to eternally hear
The gentle music of the spheres
Yet at me, reality snears
Reminding me, hurling spears
My dreaming they annoyingly interfere
A quote from a song, I'd like to share
"Games without Frontiers,
War without Tears..."
I find that beautiful and try to adhere
My point,I'll say, as my end draws near
From fears
Over many years
Flow my crimson-red, blood tearsEnjoy, it took me a long time to get all those rhyming words :)
