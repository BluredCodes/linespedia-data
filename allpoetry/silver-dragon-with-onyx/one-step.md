---
title: "One Step"
writer: "Silver Dragon with Onyx"

I try to step
And nothing's there
My heart begins to fill with fear
I'm lost in my world
I thought I knew it all
But suddenly I start to fall
Dropping past everything
Mysterious and known
Hidden and shown
I hit the ground
Hard and painful
I have never been disdainful
And yet when I see a sword appear
It is so beautiful, I take it quickly
Suddenly, I don't feel so sickly
Before my heart hurt deep to the core
And when I let go of my blade
My anguish returns and happiness fades
I love MY world, but reality hurts
I'm falling through a hopeless hole
It all seems so completely out of controlReality sucks, my world is all I need
