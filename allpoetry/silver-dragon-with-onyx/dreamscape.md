title: "Dreamscape"

Flying through clouds
Icy blue skies
Like eyes watching
Caring and strong
Warm air
Refreshing breezes
Night comes
A cresent moon
Glowing like a candle
Stars sparkle
Like gemstones
Sprinkled with glitter
Sprinkled with star dust

As the scenery changes, I become excited...
Flying like my dragon self, floating as I dance to the most beautiful music...like a Siren's song, and oh so much more I can do whenever I feel.

A storm starts
Thunder, lightning, rain
It may scare some
But it is beautiful
So calming
One of the many musics of nature
Puddles sparkle in the new light
The fiery orb that is the sun
Makes the dew glisten
The waterfall is fresh and full
Overflowing down the smooth, water rounded rocks
The trees, green and lush
Now filled with colorerful birds
Chirping their song....
More music
I practice fighting with you
Kick, punch, flip
Some don't understand, but it's, 
It's so fun and exilerating
I lay on the grass
Near the lake, once a puddle
Listening to you sing
Your voice like honey-sweet ambrosia

And then I wake....
The dreamscape gone for now
Closed out as day breaks
Yet I still hear your voice
Reassuring me that I'll return to you,
And my extraordinary world... OUR extraordinary world
