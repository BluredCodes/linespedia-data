I am a dragon
Compassionate and warm
Yet strong too
I tried to love before
But it only made me weak
I swore I would learn
And not let it happen again
Not to say that I don't care
Because I do, I care for others
But LoVe?
Me?
And yet...
When you appear
Shivers run up my spine
A tingling feeling all over
I can sense you even when I don't see you
You make everything right
And as much as I have to be strong...
I must admit
Even though you've appeared less in my little cave lately
I
LoVe
YoU
Just because I'm a dragon
And just because you're, well...YOU
Doesn't mean we can't Love

I wish I could see you more
