Drip

With a single drip of warm, soothing blood
Blood drawn in anger
All time stops
This was foretold by the wise three
I thought things would be better this way
But I could not resist
Sometimes change must occur
For the realization of how good things were before
To appear in the mind
I stand in the this...Nexus
Waiting for the return of what is known
Nothing is perfect
But this way, I do not lose
What I have worked so hard for
Another drop

Drip

And I am back from my inspiring and painful journey
It was not all bad
I learned to live with past
And I think,
That's what the wise three wanted
The entire time...
