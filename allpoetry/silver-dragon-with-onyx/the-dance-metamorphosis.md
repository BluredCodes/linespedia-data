I dance by the soft candle light
Each move flows just right
All grace is shown as move each limb
I float and glide as each flame dims
But the light in me endures
And each step I take assures
I take form of a mortal girl
Wearing a silken garment as I move and swirl
Dragon claws turn to fingers and hands
And toes that that touch the golden sands
I reach up and touch the stars through mist
The moon arises and by its blue light I'm kissed
My bronze, brown hair sways with each maneuver made
Mortal eyes of brown are turned from Dragon's of jade
While I dance I feel at peace
Whether in the forest or on the cliffs of Greece
Blood red silk flys with me
As I dance like the waves of the crystal green sea
I love to dance in candle light
When I do, I have no fright"Peace is worth fighting for!"
