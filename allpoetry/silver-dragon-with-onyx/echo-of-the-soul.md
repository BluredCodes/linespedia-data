In a dark and hollow cavern,
I feel lonely, and my spirit guide is missing
I call out "Hello!"
And to my surprise, a response come back,
"Hello!"
But I hear sadness in its voice...
"What's wrong with you?" I ask
"You" is all I hear
"Me?" I reply shocked
"I've only hurt ME" "What have I done to you?"
And so once again, the faint replies come
"Hurt me, hurt me... what have I done to you?"
"I'm sorry, I didn't mean to... As far as I know you've done nothing to me, so I shouldn't do anything to you."
"I shouldn't do anything to you." I hear again
Curious, I ask, "Were you planning to?"
The voice calls back, "Planning to"
I feel bad, to cause pain to the undeserving
"Truce" I call in guilt
"Truce" I hear with relief
And then I realize something...
No one else is here
I was talking to my soul
Talking to my spirit guide the whole time
