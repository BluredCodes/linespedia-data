Before ya read this...here's the info...i wrote this about a year ago at the spur of the moment at around 4 in the morning..my chat friend was feeling down and out because a few guys friends that she new all told her they loved her at the same time lol...and she liked them as friends..so she wasn't too happy...i just came up with this corny lil' poem to cheer her up and also let her know how i feel :-) i hope ya enjoy....-------------I love your room, and all your friends    I hope our friendship, never ends I wrote this poem, not long ago to show i care and i hope it shows.... Your smart and caring, sweet as can be thats why I like your friendship with me When you give me, the 'club of power' I give out of friendship, a beautiful flower And although you are beautiful,gifted and sweet.. All my kisses are for your cheek! If ya ever get sad or want to pout.. Just tell me their name and i'll kick their ass out!....lol If your ever upset or just having a bad day... Please give warning so i can get the hell outta your way! lol Just thought you'd need to hear this stuff... that your an awesome person..both friendly and tough. So in the future if there's anything wrong..... I'll try to make it better, if it takes all day long!
