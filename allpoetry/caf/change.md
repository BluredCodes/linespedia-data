It's almost time,another chapter of minestarting to closebut i feel fine.At first it was freakywhat's gonna happen?I've had this freedomnow it's gone again.But with good reasoni know i shouldcause tomorrow will be better therethan if i stay.. i know it wouldSo change i've gotta deal withand sometime settle downUntill i settle though,keep a smile not a frown.I take some memoriesmore than a few,of each place i goand everything i doMoving on though, i've thought back awhileAbout my life,my surroundings,and it brings me a smileMaybe alot of people won't feel finished by the end of the poem.. i don't think i do.. so when i get the rest of the story i'll add it. hope you like what's there.
