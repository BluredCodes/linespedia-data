It's late...or early I should say,Rain is pounding the windows,Thunder is rattling the floorLightning is blinding to even closed eyesso I guess I'll stay here.Tomorrow is a busy day,Gotta do a whole lot of nothing.Don't know why I even get up mornings,at least the morning i was able to sleep.Guess i'll just write a poemwell here goes...I know i know...but it's my first one...I'm not saying they'll get better but at least i have an excuse this time and I'm using it lol.
