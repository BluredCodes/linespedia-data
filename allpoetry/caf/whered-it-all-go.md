Yesterday I was sitting in class,
counting the minutes down.
saying to myself...
Pretty soon the bell's going to ring,
we'll be let out at the sound.

I went out to the school yard
at the end of the day,
With school-friends to the swings,
we stayed there awhile to have fun and play.

After sometime our parents would be there,
to pick us up and bring us home.

It was nice..supper was cooked, 
we ate and watched t.v
I had no worries about money, 
payments and bills where not for me.

I just thought about the next day
when I'd go back to school and have some fun
and after school we'd play again,
untill the evening comes.

Today I'm thinking about credit cards, 
rent and soon a morgage.
Gotta pay bills and go shopping,
I'm looking at the list up on the fridge.

Yesterday there was none of this
fun is all i had,
Today it's just responcibility,
but i guess it isn't that bad.
