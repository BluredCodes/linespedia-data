title: "The Guys"

Late nights at the coffee shop,
i spent with all the guys.
talking about what movie to watch
before we go home and close our eyes.

They know that when that happens
and they're off to sleep for the night
I'll be there, watching over them
where it's dark i'll be their light

When they wake up sunday,
in the morning i'll be there
Maybe not at my best,
with tired eyes and messy hair

They know I'll be there for breakfast,
and i'll help them make their lunch
Supper time any time,
i'll be there when they're in a crunch.

No matter what they say to me
or a moment with some hardship
i'll be there when they need me most
it goes way beyond just friendship.

I won't be there as much now,
not like before
That's a different chapter now
but i'll never close the door

I hope they know even though i've said
many times to them what i think of,
Those guys are everything to me,
And i hope they feel my love.I wrote this about the guys i work with... they were a pretty important bunch of men in my life... i was there to show them how to do things and teach them, but they have taught me more than i thought i could ever learn.
