The sun is shining,with a cool in the air.Everything is falling,as i watch from my chair.The day is just starting,with the cars back on road.Nobody is in a rush,just speedy work-mode.Today is different,as i watch the cars pass.Everyone's a little sloweras Autumn blows in fast.Soon I'll build a snow man,and have fun December eve'sBut for right now i think I'll stop,and watch the falling leaves.
