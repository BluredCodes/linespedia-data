title: "While You Were Sleeping"

While you were sleeping, 
I thought about you
Looked with closed eyes,
and saw right through

You sure don't admit, 
to everyone you know
How weak you are,
you wouldn't let that show.

They see you smiling,
never catch you frown
But While you where you sleeping
you let your shield down

Then and there i saw it all,
not what you play
but what's behind the wall

You shouldn't keep it up,
cause it's only holding you in
although you think it's helping
It will never let you win

But still you haven't found that out
and when you do it's far to late,
so long at this, all you have,
is a mind that's full of hate
