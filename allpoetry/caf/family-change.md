I seen ya in the hospitolYou seem alrightYou stayed there a couple daysAnd I visited on night.I have seen so manyPeople die and fall illBut when i saw you therei felt a pain that could killHow can you be therewhen you're so strongyou've been there for everythingmy whole life longI have taken people to hospitolsI have helped a few when sickBut seeing you, not so strongI could feel something clickI wasn't ready to admit that you may dieSo when i didn't come to visitit wasn't you it was I....Who couldn't see ya lying thereYou're my best friendAll the things we did years agodoes the fun really end?I'd come over and make breakfastThen we'd play a board game.Now everything differenti want it all the sameYou're in a home now,and you say that everything's swellBut i know you don't smile like beforebut for my sake you will not tell.You've always put me before youanything bad i had to sayYou'd listen to everything,then find a way to make my day.I just want to saythat as strong as you areIf you ever need anythingI'll never be too far.This is about my Grandmother, she is in a nursing home now and i don't think she likes it.. she's very independant.. but the doctors orders... but no matter how she feels .. she never complains or anything because she doesn't want to bring anyone down.She's so important to me.I'm sure everyone has that ' favorite family member ' .. well she's mine :o)
