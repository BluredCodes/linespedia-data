Jewel, she's a great poetshe's confident,has a good voice,she's awesome and she knows it.Peanut butterOK it's a little sticky,getting stuck on the roof of the mouthand when it's drop it's just ikky.Chat roomsAround since the PC's birth,Everyone occasionally visits themYou talk to all kinds of people, all over the earth.Pizza and a movie,nothing beats that,Well it's pretty good,i think i still love chat.Why make a list,of things loved by me?Cause if you don't like them,Deal with it or leave me be.
