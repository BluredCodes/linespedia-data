My eyes are closing,
but it's morning
I should be still in bed.
I've kept awake
many hours 
just typing the thoughts in my head.


Now I've gone brain dead,
no more thought, not a sound
At this point even coffee won't help
that with experience i have found.

So no more poetry,
for me to see,
at least not for today
Cause my eyes are closing, I'm about to sleep,
and I've nothing more to say.



Except good night/ and good morning to others :) or good afternoon..
Bye for now :o)
