Where am I going, where will we be?Share our destination, especially with me!I've said I'd go west, and you know I'll go eastAnywhere ya want..just tell me at least.You've said we're going here, and to plan according to thatI've made several plans, but still here i satIn the same old place, that we've been for whileWe have started to plan, but haven't went the mileSo when you figure out, just when this traveling endsI'll still be right beside you, why ? because we'll still be friends.
