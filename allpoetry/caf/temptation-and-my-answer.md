---
title: "Temptation and My Answer"
writer: "caf"
slug: "temptation-and-my-answer"
source: "AllPoetry"

You and your words
always so nice

you and your money 
buy innocents like pet mice

you and your confidence
that you'll get me too

I'm special I'm me
and your just you

I can be sly, 
clever like a fox

I've never let the shield down
just try to enter my box

It's all light and your dark can't go through
I'm smarter at this, I'm more clever than you

You'll offer all the power that one man could desire,
if he takes it you have him, he'll end up in your fire

I'm not going to burn although many others have fell,
you don't want some of me,
cause you'd loose and i'd rule hell
