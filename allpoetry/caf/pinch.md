title: "Pinch"

Toss and turning through the night,
Others fear yes i feel fright.
At times it feels never-ending,
Like you've finished the application,
but the decision is still pending.

When will the two, be open again
I've all ready suffered 8 
will it go up to 10?

It seems like it's gone, 
past there and still here,
Barely escaping the sadness
but demolishing the fear.

All you need to know,
when you're falling from the sky,
Worried, that the end is near
if you land instead of fly

Finally a pinch comes,
and it shows you it's all fake
you jump out of the nightmare
relax,

you're now awake.
