Take the knife,                   run it slow,                   down the arm                    let it go                   it won't stop                   till it's dry                 he blood has left,                    now you die                  Better place?                  so you think,                you're not welcome,                 you start to sink                             Down to the bottem,            that's where you will stay                 Forever in hades,                  that is the way             Now you've hit the bottem,             and you feel a pain inside.       that's the devil and he's got your soul,             you've got no where to hide.There is no real title... i would love any opinions of what i should call it... it doesn't really matter to me if it ever gets a title... anyways i hope ya liked.
