Feeling..gotta have itOHH looky! here it isOH no it's goneGotta have iteveryone doesdon't they?Songs...that' feelingPoems .. all feelingPictures, paintings, Photos..Ahh all the feeling Everyone has it right?I have feelinga strong ongoing feelingalways the sameCONFUSIONWhy???Where's my feeling??Do I have feeling?Have I lost it?will i find it all at onceI am sure not gonna loose any sleep over itinfact i feel finei'm not worriednot scarednot mad just not happy eitherwhy?Well i'm not sureI don't really care where the feeling wentor when it comes back or if it comes back untill it doesif it doesi guess i'll just sit around feeling confused, why'd it ever leave?
