Each morning I wake up to you,Your different each timebut always what i need each dayYour scent i know, your taste i craveI'll have you i want youtill I'm gone to the graveYou are perfect to mei wouldn't trade ya for all the tea in china,With my tongue, my insides,all of me loves yaI'm horrible with out youand i can't complete a dayMy work and my thoughtsall mixed up they will stayUntill the time i'm holding youagain in my handI might as well be sleepinga day with out i cannot stand.And so i give you the ode you deserve,for you and from meCause i, like others cannot live with outCoffeeThis was just a brainfart lol ... i am sitting here at like 11PM and drinking a great cup of coffee.
