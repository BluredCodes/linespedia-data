Rock and sway,
to and fro
Why the movement?
I don't know

SHUT UP, DAMN YOU
I don't mean that
I'm a nice guy
although i'd kill your dog
and skin your cat

It's not really me,
who has urges to make pain
I'm not the one who's unhappy,
not the madness you should blame

That fire place is nice,
i think that looks good
maybe it would be better 
if it was made out of wood
haha

Your head is nice and round
I enjoy your stylin' hair
but maybe i should cut it off
to fill the space of that empty chair

Like i said I'm pretty nice,
i have no quomes with you
My brain is different, i am unique
that's why i think what i doThis is a weird poem.. i know, it's about a man i worked with that really was a gentlemen, but had some weird thoughts and at one time i was asking him if maybe i should get another person to sit in the 4'th chair of our card table. so that we'd have someone else to play crib with. And he said that maybe my head would look nice there.. and asked if he could put it there ... but just for the card game.
