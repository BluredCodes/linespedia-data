---
title: "Killing An Innocent"
writer: "Danie"
slug: "killing-an-innocent"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/6668-Killing-An-Innocent-by-Danie"
---

you must have no heart,
you must be evil,
how could you do that?
how could they let you?

you knew he was coming, 
you knew he was there,
so you killed him
and you don't even care.

so now his chance at life is gone,
but you don't care, because you're still around.

so now that he's gone
you move on with your life,
trying to forget him...

and that big mistake last night
