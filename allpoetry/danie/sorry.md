---
title: "Sorry"
writer: "Danie"
slug: "sorry"

does this mean I'm insane now?
does this mean I'm sick?
does this mean you don't love me?
does this mean you don't care?

I don't want to hurt you
I'm sorry if I do,
but life is so cruel
and I don't know what to do.

so I'm sorry dad
for hurting you,
I'm sorry for making you lose your job.
I didn't know what I was doing,
I didn't know where I was.

sorry mum
for breaking your heart,
sorry for all the pain.
I was scared,
I didn't mean to do it.

sorry sister
for breaking your bones.
sorry for causing you pain,
please forgive me.

I must be insane,
I must be sick.
so sorry
but I didn't know what I was doing,
I didn't know where I was.
