---
title: "Misunderstood"
writer: "Danie"

source: "AllPoetry"

---

you sit alone and dwell in you own sadness.
you hide your hideous face from the world.
you never smile,
you never laugh,
to the world you are misunderstood.
in your eyes
the world is a cruel and unpleasant place.
you wish for a life filled with happiness
and good memories.
but the only memories you've ever had
with happiness are your dreams
that haven't come true.
to the world you are misunderstood.
