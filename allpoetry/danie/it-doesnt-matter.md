---
title: "It Doesn't Matter"
writer: "Danie"
slug: "it-doesnt-matter"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/6705-It-Doesnt-Matter-by-Danie"

it doesn't matter what you look like,you'll always be pretty to me. it doesn't matter what you say,you'll always be knowledgeable to me. it doesn't matter what you do,you'll always be clever to me.it doesn't matter if you lose,you'll always be a winner to me.
