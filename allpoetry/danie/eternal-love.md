---
title: "Eternal Love"
writer: "Danie"
slug: "eternal-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/7020-Eternal-Love-by-Danie"
---

I blow the candles out
and look up at the night sky.
I know you're up there somewhere...
somewhere. 

I wish you were here with me,
maybe then I wouldn't feel so alone.
I cry sometimes when I think of you...
you were too young to die,
you were too special to die.

it takes people years to work out 
why they were put on this earth.
but I knew as soon as I looked in your eyes
I knew I was put on this earth to love you.

and even though I can't see your face
and can't  hear your voice,
I know you can see my face,
I know you can hear my voice.

so look at the sadness on my face,
and know that I miss you. 
hear my voice say "I love you"
and I will forever,
until we meet again.
