---
title: "Christopher"
writer: "Danie"
slug: "christopher"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I hate you with a passion
you're as evil as it gets;
you're so selfish,
you're so vain.

you have stolen my pride,
you have stolen my hope;
and I hate you with a passion.

why do you walk over me
like I'm an old door mat?
why must you be so powerful?
you know I'm weak.

you make you cry
you treat me like another one 
of your yes men.
but I hate you with a passion...
I hope you choke!
