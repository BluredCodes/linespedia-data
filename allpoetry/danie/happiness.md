happiness is the only way out,
i can feel it becoming closer,
i used to cry myself to sleep,
but then you told me not to worry,
you told me you still cared,
you told me that you'll never forget what we shared.
so now the happiness is creeping in
its touching my soul so deep.
even though it hurt for you to leave,
i'm happy to watch you move on.
i'll never forget you and i know you'll never forget me too25. 2 02,
