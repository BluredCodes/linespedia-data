title: "Not Just Nobody"
writer: "Danie"

i may be small and unseen,
i may not exsist to you.
but you cant pretend i'm not here
you cant send me away
because i'm here now and i want to stay.
i'm not just nobody
i'm not a stranger
i'm you, you created me, you put me here,
i just want to be loved
i just want to be wanted
because i'm not just nobody..
i'm you22.2.02,
