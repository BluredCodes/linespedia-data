---
title: "Ocean"
writer: "Danie"
slug: "ocean"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

the ocean takes pleasure in stealing away
the fragile sand castles and swallowing them whole.
it grabs an object with it's many hands
and hides them in it's depths. 
the ocean may seem calm to the eye
but underneath it is a dangerous force,
which is hungry to kill. 
so as you step into that ocean be mindful
of your actions because it may drag you
away from the shore and to another place.
