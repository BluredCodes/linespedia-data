---
title: "Goth"
writer: "Danie"
slug: "goth"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

goth...

silly woman,
all dressed in black.
so serious and ready to attack.

she holds the knife
up high
and aims it for my back.
soon I feel the pain
and the hurting that I caused.

so I'm sorry for not understanding,
I'm sorry judging you,
but please now I beg you!
look at me and then judge,
and don't stab me in the back.
