take my hand and lets dance,
lets look into each others eyes and feel the connection
lets lose the others that dance around us,
dont break away.

let me drown in your sweet sent
let me taste in from your lips.
you touch my soul from the outside in.
but dont break away.

keep moving slowly from side to side,
watch the bright lights flicker around us,
feel my hand so cold in you warmth.


and so the clock strikes midnight,
and the light become dull
i feel you slip from my soft grip and disapear into the crowd,
i try to run after you for a good night kiss,
but i'm afraid when i get there it'll be a goodbye kiss.

dont forget about me,
i wont forget about you.i'd appreciate contructive comments
