title: "What I Want"

To leave the past behind.
To take the final step.
To walk away forever.
To not look back.
To forgive myself.
To REALLY move on.
To try again.
To feel good inside.
To learn from the bad things.
To remember the good things.
To believe that I deserve it.Just a thought.
