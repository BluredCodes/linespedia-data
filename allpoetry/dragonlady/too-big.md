---
title: "Too Big"
writer: "Dragonlady"

Too big to hold onto.
Too proud to stay and fight.
He didn’t know what to believe in.
He didn’t know if it was right.

Small enough to slip away.
Weak enough to cry.
I didn’t know what to do.
I didn’t want to say goodbye.

Cold enough to forget it.
Tired enough to end.
Never being able to settle,
For having just a friend.
