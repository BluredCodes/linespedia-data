title: "Rewind"
writer: "Dragonlady"

I’m just not sure
Where to start
To explain the way
That you flow through my heart

You talked about her
With such love in your voice
I know you’d pick her
If given the choice

The tears sting my eyes
The emptiness grows
But still in my heart
The feeling just flows

What would I do
How could I replace
The brush of your eyelashes
Across my face

Thinking back
Us lying there
Running my fingers
Through your sweet smelling hair

When you talked about her
It tangled my mind
Tonight I just wish 
That I could push rewind
