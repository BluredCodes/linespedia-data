---
title: "All Alone"
writer: "Dragonlady"
slug: "all-alone"
source: "AllPoetry"

The silence wraps me,
Like a warm blanket.
All alone,
With room to collect my thoughts.

The distance chills me,
Like a cold, howling wind.
All alone,
With nothing to hold onto.

The solitude comforts me,
Like a kind, loving friend.
All alone,
With room to breathe.

The separation breaks me,
Like a fragile, shattering glass.
All alone,
With no one to kiss goodnight
