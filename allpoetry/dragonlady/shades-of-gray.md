---
title: "Shades Of Gray"
writer: "Dragonlady"

Writhed in pain
Twisted in fear
Hoping and praying
The end is near

Overwhelmed with thoughts
Of blackness and night
All that I need 
Seems out of my sight

Wanting to give more 
Needing to receive
Obsessed with all
That I cannot retrieve

It would be simple
If it wasn’t so though
It’s all that I need
But it’s just not enough

Needing it to leave
Wanting it to stay
Watching everything
Fade into shades of gray

Sitting and watching
You’re turning cold
This is too much
For one person to hold
