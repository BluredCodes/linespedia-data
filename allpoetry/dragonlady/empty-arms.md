---
title: "Empty Arms"
writer: "Dragonlady"

Empty arms
Hurt so bad
 They make you wonder
About what you've never had

 Empty arms
Crush your insides
 They make you uncover
All the things you try to hide

 The world seems so big
And you feel so small
 But you can't run away
Because you just learned to crawl

 And you want to cry
Because there's nowhere to turn
 And the more they say
The less you learn

 And the more they do
The less they say
 And you watch until
They've gone away

 Empty arms
Are all they leave
 And you use to try so hard
But now you can't believe

 Because empty arms hurt
Love hurts to
 But it always seems like
They only hurt you
