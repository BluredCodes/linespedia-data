Take these shoes...
Wear them down.
Like you did my love,
My soul,
And my patience.

Take them and use them.
Like you did me,
My money,
And my mind.

Take them and run.
Like you did from me,
Yourself,
And anything that might mean being depended on.

Take them and laugh at the color.
Like you laughed at everything that is a part of me,
At everything that ever meant anything to anyone,
And at the last tear I will ever cry for you!
