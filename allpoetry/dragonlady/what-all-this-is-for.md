title: "What All This Is For"
writer: "Dragonlady"

The worst part is the feelings,
The secret stuck inside.
Think how much pain there would be,
If she found out you lied.

Squirming with curiosity,
Wondering who you are.
Wondering how you feel about me,
And if we’ve gone too far.

The emptiness that I feel,
When I think of losing you.
The guiltiness that I feel,
When I think of what we’re putting her through.

I sit here and wonder,
What all this is for.
And I cry when you hand me,
A double-edged sword.
