---
title: "Now I Know"
writer: "Dragonlady"

I’d never want to take back
The time I had with you
I never will forget about
All the things that we went through

We had a lot of good times
We had a lot of bad
When I think of all that happened
It often makes me sad

I’ve learned not to let be smothered
The things that make me, me
I’ve learned you did not leave me
What you did was set me free

It seemed like when you left
There was a hole within my heart
I’ve learned that the best thing to happen
Was for us to grow apart

I’ve learned a lot of things
I remember who I am
I swear I won’t walk down
That lonesome road again

Now I know that tomorrow will come
I know that the sun will rise
Now I know that I will never again
Be your consolation prize
