---
title: "Everybody Be Aware"
writer: "Dragonlady"
slug: "everybody-be-aware"

Everybody be cautious,
Everybody be aware.
There’s a mean cold-hearted person,
Roaming the streets out there.

  He cares about himself,
That’s about it.
He’ll hurt you really bad,
He’ll give you lot’s of shit.
He’ll step all over you,
Because he doesn’t care how you feel.
He’s always really selfish.
He never lives up to his end of the deal.

  You’ll start to get really angry.
You’ll feel like you’ve been had.
But he really isn’t stupid,
And he knows you’re getting mad.

  So he’ll start making up excuses,
And he’ll stop by once or twice.
He’ll catch you by surprise,
When he actually is nice.
He’ll hold you really close and say,
“I’m glad that you are mine.”
And you’ll be really happy,
You’ll think everything’s just fine.

  Then he does the same thing again,
And he makes up more excuses.
And you realize you’re not someone he loves.
You’re just something he seduces.

  So everybody be careful!
Because he’s back out on the make.
And he’s trying to find out,
How many more hearts he can break.
