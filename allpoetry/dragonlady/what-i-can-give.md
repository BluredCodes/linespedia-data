---
title: "What I Can Give"
writer: "Dragonlady"
slug: "what-i-can-give"

I can listen to you
I can share all control
I can look past your eyes
Straight into your soul
I can deal with the hard times
And see them through
I can listen 
And believe in you
I can hold your hand
Help you be strong
I can not criticize
If you've done something wrong
I can give you space
With your thoughts and fears
If ever you need me
I'll welcome you here
I can take care of you
Make you smile
Carry you down 
That last long hard mile
I can give many things
And what I have learned
Is that all I can give
I need to be returned
