---
title: "Something To Hold"

The feelings don’t change,
Just the circumstances.
Why do I keep getting myself hurt?
Why do I keep taking chances?

Maybe,
I should just be alone.
Not let anyone,
Invade my safety zone.

Keep my feelings in,
Make myself smile.
Learn to take care of myself,
For awhile.

Learn to be happy.
Grow wise and strong.
Figure out exactly,
Where I belong.

I just hope,
When I grow old.
When I come home from work,
I’ll have something to hold.
