These are my words
Before I even reach you
~I am knocked down
You have no idea what I think
~You think you do
Never take the time to ask

Is it just sex
 Or is it my company too
Is there something about me you like
~Other then what's between my
        Belly Button
            And
          My Knees

Somehow I feel  
         Steered
        Down 
       The 
       W
        R
         O   P
        N     A
       G       T
                H

Do I want more then 
      You
Or do you want less then
       Me

When we talk 
 Do you listen
     Hear
When i'm out of sight
 Am I out of mind

Do my words ever make you think
 Just of something else
I don't want
I don't need 
       More
~Maybe just a little
Maybe a real thought
         Now
         And
        Again

The last thing I need
 Is to be confined
And I don't need you to assume
       What I feel
     Ask
I'll let you know
 I've shown you my body
Do you really want to see me
   Do you want to see my soul
Do you know that I have one

I think we can learn from 
 each other
If you fall into the arms
 Of another
When you leave these arms
~That's o.k.
I may be so inclined
 Myself

I am still the same
 Person I was
Before you nailed me
  But to you
       I
        Feel
           Less
