---
title: "Have You Ever?"

Have you ever seen forever?
Have you ever run your fingers through its hair,
Or heard it whisper softly in your ear?

Have you ever held eternity in the palm of your hand?
Have you ever looked deep in its eyes,
Or felt it touch your soul?

I have seen forever.
I have held eternity.
And as long as it may seem,
It could never be long enough.
