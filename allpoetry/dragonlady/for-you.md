---
title: "For You"
writer: "Dragonlady"
slug: "for-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

For you…
I would walk the world for you.
I would swim the ocean and the sea,
If I knew that it would keep you~
                     With me.

For you…
I would sell my soul for you.
I would steal the stars from the night,
If I knew that it would make~
                   Everything right.

For you…
I would leave everything for you.
To my friends and family,
I would lie.
If it came right down to it,
I’d die…
           Anything
                    For 
                        You.
