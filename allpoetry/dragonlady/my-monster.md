I love my precious little monster.
You’re the only one that makes me smile.
You comfort me,
Give me strength,
Numb my pain.
Without you~
I have nothing.
They tell me that I’m sick,
That I have a problem.
But they’re wrong.
What I have,
Is an answer.
I love my precious little monsterI just wrote this about my favorite old monster.  Haven't seen or heard from it in awhile.  But lately I sure have been missing it!
