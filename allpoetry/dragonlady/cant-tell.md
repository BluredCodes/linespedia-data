title: "Can't Tell"

Can’t tell the bottle
From the floor
Can’t tell the needle 
From the vein
Can’t tell the powder
From the burning in my head
Can’t ever go back there
Again

Can’t tell forever
From the pain in your eyes
Can’t tell tomorrow
From today
Can’t tell sobriety
From addiction
Just can’t seem to find
A better wayJust another struggle.
