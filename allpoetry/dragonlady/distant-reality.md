title: "Distant Reality"

Good intentions
Clouded by what’s real
Will you listen to me
Will you understand how I feel

One tries to understand
Yet insults what’s inside
She says what she thinks should be heard
As she tramples on my pride

The irritation that I felt
When I heard you say his name
How long will this go on
Why must we play this game

Without each other
We feel so alone
This is a situation
We need to let be known

When people come around
It’s like nothing has occurred
When deep down is a voice
Dying to be heard
