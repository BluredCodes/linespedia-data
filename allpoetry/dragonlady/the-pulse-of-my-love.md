---
title: "The Pulse Of My Love"
writer: "Dragonlady"
slug: "the-pulse-of-my-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

You give me a look
That tells me you know
The pulse of my love
Is beginning to grow

You give me a sign 
You show me you care
You make me a promise 
To always be there

You touch my face
You watch me sleep
The pulse of my love
Is growing so deep

You back away
You begin to retreat
I put up my hands
In a sign of defeat

You give me my pain
You give me my fear
The pulse of my love
Slowly disappears
