---
title: "Angel Wings"
writer: "Dragonlady"

---

When you were here
Things weren’t as tough
But as soon as you left
Things got so rough

The days went by so easy
They almost went to fast
Now things are so much harder
And the good times are all past

You made me feel so different
You made me feel so proud
And ever since you left
I’ve been getting so lost in the crowd

You made me want to live
You made me want to fly
You always really cared for me
Unlike any other guy

How much sadness
Your memory brings
But I can still feel you protect me
With your angel wings
