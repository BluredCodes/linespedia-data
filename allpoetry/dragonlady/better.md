I’m so tired.
Sooooo much pain.
No strength.
He said,
“Things will be much worse,
For awhile”
You’ll need someone,
To help you;
Move, stand, walk,
Really anything.
But eventually, 
I will be better.
A
 H
   H
     H
      H…
            BETTER
Better then now.
Eventually he says,
I will be “ better then ever”.
I have in my lifetime been:
Good, okay, I have been better.
At some point,
I must have been,
Better then ever.
I just didn’t know it.
This time …
             I will.
~ Looking forward to better.
