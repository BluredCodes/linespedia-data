I walk through her gardens,
When the sun will not shine.
Her words always cheer me,
She’s a good friend of mine.

Her cyber arms hug me,
When I have a bad day.
She comforts and encourages me.
She makes my sadness go away.

She offers me truth,
When I am in denial.
Her words show us a better way.
And she ALWAYS makes me smile.

I will always be grateful,
For what she’s done for me.
She’ll forever hold a place in my heart.
I love her dearly….
                             Lurie.I wanted to write something for awhile.  The words just found me tonight.  I'm glad.
