---
title: "Will The Sun Ever Rise?"
writer: "Dragonlady"

Will the sun ever rise?
Will the nighttime break?
Will I grow an understanding,
Of why my heart aches?

Can I live until tomorrow?
Can I see the break of day?
Can I find the words to tell him,
What I have to say?

Does wisdom ever come around?
Does the cycle ever end?
Does he have an explanation,
For deceiving me, a friend?
