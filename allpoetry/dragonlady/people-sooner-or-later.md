---
title: "People ~ Sooner or Later"
writer: "Dragonlady"
slug: "people-sooner-or-later"
source: "AllPoetry"

Don’t get close.
Don’t open your heart.
Don’t let them in.
Keep them far apart.

They'll hurt you,
They'll mock you,
They'll make you cry.

They'll back stab,
They'll use you,
And they will tell you lies.

They’ll make you love them, 
Never want to let them go.
Sometimes they can be soooo nice,
And it will be really hard  to say no.

But please listen to me,
And please believe.
Sooner or later,
Everyone leaves.Sorry about this one.
