Two dragons,
Come together in the night.
He walked into the room,
Surrounded by his amazing glow.
Walked right up to me.
He held me,
Like he had just rescued me from a storm.
He continued to hold me,
With his stare.
A protector,
Always looking out for me.
He watched me all night.
 I could feel his gaze,
Sending warm chills up my spine.
He made me smile, 
Laugh,
So much that it hurt.
~I never wanted it to end~
Then it did.
He left.
Although I felt he didn’t want to.
Two dragons,
Go their separate ways.
Him to another.
Me to my empty lair.
But I’m still smiling.A little birthday experience.  But I don't feel sad.  I feel really happy!
