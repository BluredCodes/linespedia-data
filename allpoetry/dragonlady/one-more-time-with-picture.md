Sing to me once more
Please sing me
Your beautiful song

Let me walk with you
Once more
Please let me sing along

I never really knew you
But I knew you
Oh so well

Your words were always there for me
Picked me up
Each time I fell

I will forever ~steal your face~
Believe me
I’m gonna ~shine~

You are the ~blind man that took my hand~
You helped me
~Make it down the line~

I’ll always be a part
Of your
Family

Please walk and sing with me
One more time
