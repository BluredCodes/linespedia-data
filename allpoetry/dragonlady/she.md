title: "She"
writer: "Dragonlady"
slug: "she"
source: "AllPoetry"

She smiles
She cries
She laughs
She yells
She dances
She fights
She feels content
She feels alone
She feels helpful
She feels useless
She feels beautiful
She feels transparent
She feels encouraged
She feels lost
She feels smart
She feels idiotic
She feels hopeful
She remembers
She feels whole
She misses him
