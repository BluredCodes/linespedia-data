---
title: "I'm Sorry"
writer: "Dragonlady"

I’m sorry
If I said something’s
You didn’t want 
To hear

I’m sorry
If I told you
Of the things 
I hope and fear

I’m sorry
If I had to
Make you think 
That I’m not there

I’m sorry
If you think
That I was
Treating you unfair

I’m sorry
That I didn’t have
The option
To choose

I’m sorry 
That in the end
All we both could do 
Was Lose
