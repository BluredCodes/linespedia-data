It’s a crazy little itch
Somewhere
Deep inside

A crazy little itch
That says
Forget about your pride

A crazy little itch
It only wants things
Like before

A crazy little itch
It only wants 
A little more

LEAVE ME ALONE YOU CRAZY BITCH!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!I realize that it doesn't go together that well.  But it is exactly what I wanted to say.
