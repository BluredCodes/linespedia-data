Today I got the “handicap” plates.
A stickman on a wheel.
And it suddenly became a reality,
When before it seemed somewhat surreal.

I don’t want to be labeled handicapped.
You may wonder why.
Because I can walk, and someday I’ll run…
Or at least I’m going to try.

So go ahead and call me handicapped.
I don’t really give a damn.
I just hope you know, my physical situation,
Does not define the person that I am.Sticks and stones...
