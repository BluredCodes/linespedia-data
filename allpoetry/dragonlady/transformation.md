In the moonlight
I see it glistening
Shadows fall all around
It’s cold
It’s damp
Can it grow stronger
Can it become ones self
Transformation
What could it be
I want to hold it
Become part of it
Take its’ hand
And gleam
Hover
Laugh out loud
Become one
With the magicToday is a good day!
