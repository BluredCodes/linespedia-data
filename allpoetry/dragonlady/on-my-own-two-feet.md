Today I started 
Walking again.
Not alone,
Not without help,
But walking.
Not quickly,
Not swiftly,
Or even gracefully.
But walking,
None the less.
I stood up,
On my own two feet,
And I moved forward.
YES!!!!!!
I’m walking…
             Again!!!!!!I actually did just take my first steps today.  The first steps I've taken in a long time.  I'm pretty happy, and proud.  But I think later, this will mean more then just that.
