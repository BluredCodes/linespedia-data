Help
I need to get out
Of this world
So full of pain
I feel so insignificant
Born in the dark
And just left there
Not knowing the whole truth
Left to wonder
Help me
What should I do
Do you know
Have you ever known
Help me understand
What is going on
You were born to live
But instead
You destroy
What we need to survive
While you kill yourself
You kill me
Help me understand it
Why must you do this
Do you not understand
What you’re doing
Please look to the future
What do you think
Will you please stop now
