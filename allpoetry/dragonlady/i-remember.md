---
title: "I Remember"
writer: "Dragonlady"
slug: "i-remember"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

When I remember,
 A tear stings my eye.

Memories come,
      Flowing,
 So fast.

You were a part of me.
     When you left,
 I died.

I see a vision,
      Of you.
 I remember.
