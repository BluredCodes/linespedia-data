They took off the cast,
And I got to see,
The ugliness and nastiness,
That has become me.

I use to have these legs,
That would turn most heads around.
I’m sure now if you saw them,
Your eyes would wander to the ground.

I cannot run, I cannot jump,
I cannot climb a tree.
I cannot do so many things,
This isn’t how I want to be.

But I can stand, and I can walk,
And I can move ahead.
I can do a couple of things,
So that’s what I’ll do instead.

I can’t put on pretty shoes,
Over soft silk stalkings.
So I’ll put on my shinny new brace,
And I’ll just keep on walkingSelf explanitory.
