Hopeless,
Like a puppy in the freezing,
Pouring rain.
Feeling rejected,
Forgotten about,
And unloved.

Longing,
For a glimmer of hope.
No anticipation,
Just dark,
Cold,
Empty needing.

Pacing back and forth in my mind.
Needing something so much,
That I feel stale.
I feel,
     No hope.
              I feel,
                   Too much.
