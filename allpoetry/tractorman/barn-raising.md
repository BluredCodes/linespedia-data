The neighbors came
From miles around
To help raise a barn
From the ground
They brought their hammers
And nails of steel
They brought their families
To prepare a big meal
We started at dawn
It would take all day
We used a tractor
To clear the way
Trusses were made
To support the roof
We all weren’t carpenters
There were some goofs
A wall went up
We stopped for a break
Our muscles were sore
And began to ache
The crew worked hard
And we finally had
All four walls up
Not too bad
The roof was done
Amidst much pain
We did it right
To keep out the rain
The siding went on
And with it the paint
It was great to work with
All these saints
The work all done
It was quite a deal
We all finally sat down
To a hearty meal
With the sun setting
And folks going their way
I thanked them all
For their help that day
