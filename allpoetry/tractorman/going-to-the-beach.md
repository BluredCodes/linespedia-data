title: "Going To The Beach"

I like the beach
With its sand so fine
Squishing my toes
Through it all the time
Riding the high waves
Into the shore
Body surf till I get cold
And can’t anymore
Then lay on a towel
And get a bronzed tan
If you talk about the beach
I’ll become your fan
Enjoying the ocean
Where all can have fun
On it sandy shores
One can for miles run
When it’s cold
It’s kind of a bummer
So I wait expectedly
For next Summer
