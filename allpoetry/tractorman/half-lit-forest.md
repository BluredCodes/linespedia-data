In a half-lit world
Of soft light filtering thru
In a thick pine-scented forest
I make my way to you
So calm and peaceful
With nary a sound
Still air fills my lungs
I know you are around
Stepping softly
I approach from behind
And gently embrace you
Happy that you’re mine
We talk and we laugh
And fritter away the day
Sharing our deepest thoughts
Before I quietly slip away
We talk of tomorrow
And so it shall be
When again into this half-lit forest
To be alone with thee
