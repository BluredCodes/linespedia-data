Most people thoughtTheir chances were slimFour superbowl tripsAnd not a winHeavy underdogsAgainst mighty GreenbayFor the Broncos to winSome began to prayThe first series of playsA touchdown for the PackThen the Broncos startedTheir awesome comebackNot looking behindAnd not digging a holeThe Broncos ran steadfastlyOnly toward the goalThey tied the gameAnd then went aheadWearing Greenbay downWho seemed to slow like leadRunning and passingThey couldn’t be stoppedThe line was awesomeSound of the blocking was pop pop popWhen time ran outAnd all was said and doneDenver 31 Greenbay 24The Broncos had won
