I wish to use a little plot of land
To grow all my favorite crops I can
Carrots, cucumbers, corn and potatos
Lettuce, peas, beans and tomatos
On this little square plot of mine
The soil is rough but will do fine
Prepare it with the tractor and tiller
Spread the right chemicals and compost filler
Turn the ground over and remove the weeds
Rake it out smooth and plant all the seeds
With tender care through the Summer and all
By God’s grace I’ll be reaping this FallJust a ditty...
