After school in Winter
With the air so cold
We pack our gear
To reap the ocean’s gold
And brag to each other
Who would get the most
In harvesting scallops
A little way up the coast
Putting on our diving gear
And slip into the sea
Free to swim about
Made us feel free
Gathering from the biggest
Down to the least
Take them home to mom
Who would cook us a feast
