The day is over no longer to wait
Out the door and through the gate
Crank up the car and off I go
To the warmest place this man knows
Out on the road a casual drive
The sun is shining it’s great to be alive
Around a curve and straight ahead
A few miles and a left turn is where I’m lead
Birds and dogs and an antelope or two
Dotted along the roadway in a makeshift zoo
Stopping for a school bus along the way
Parents meeting them just to be safe
Checking the mail at the top of my hill
All is well there are no bills
Opening the gate at the bottom of the drive
Dogs greet me excitedly as I arrive
Up to the house they run beside
And out on the porch my lovely bride
A smile and a kiss to greet me in love
Hugs for the kids and thanks to God above
