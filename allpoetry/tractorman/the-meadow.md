In a colorful glen
Of shrubs and trees
I lay quietly in the grass
To wait and see
Into a clearing
Two deer step into
Framed by the woodlands
It’s a spectacular view
Just in front of them
Three Grouse fly by
It’s a visual feast
Just for the eyes
I visit this place
Every once in a while
It lifts the spirits
And brings a smile
