My wife just lovesThis hulking beastA gas-guzzling monsterBoasting three rows of seatsMore than enough roomWith seating for eightShe steers down the drivewayAnd opens our gateOut on the roadShe fears little or noneLook out motoristsHere comes the big red onePlenty of powerAnd a big V-8Stepping on the gasNo way she’s gonna be lateYes, this vehicle isQuite an attractionBut arriving safelyBrings mighty satisfaction
