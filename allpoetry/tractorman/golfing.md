On hole number one
I’m confident and sure
Birdies and pars
My thoughts stay pure
A 250 yard drive
But into light rough
Not a big problem
I swing mighty tough
Sending a 4 iron shot
That finds a sand trap
I don’t get frustrated
And will bounce back
A pitching wedge loft
Beyond the pin
A three putt to get
My Top Flight #2 in
But none of this
Really bothers me
Because there’s still 17 holes left
You see
