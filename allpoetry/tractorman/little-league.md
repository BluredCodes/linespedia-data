Saturday’s were
Always fun
We’d suit up
Grabbing hats and gloves
A ride by dad
To the ball game
My uniform bore the Cubs
And Orioles and Yankees’ names
Whether at first base
Or in center field
My goal also as a pitcher
No runs would I yield
I struck out and walked
And even got base hits
Hitting three home runs
Gave one pitcher the fits
After the game
And at the end of the day
It was then time to go swimming
And continue to play
I sometimes miss
Those days of the past
I’ll tuck away those memories
As long as they last
