title: "Quiet Road Home"
writer: "TractorMan"

Calm overcast day
As I drive past a farm
Contemplating the season of winter
My head supported by my arm
Down a winding country road
Covered with well packed snow
Slowly taking in the scene
As around a lazy corner I go
Looking out the window of my car
At cows gathered in the field
Eating next to an old mesh fence
Feasting on their noon day meal
The farmhouse on the corner
Nestled in some covered pines
And hanging from them sparkling
Icicles frozen to power lines
Slowly by the large white barn
Standing near a grain-filled silo
Along with a well-kept stable
For all their work horses to go
Past them all and down the way
Through valleys and over some hills
I take my time on this country road
Just relishing in the thrills
This road I travel almost every day
Savoring the moments alone
Enjoying nature’s elegant beauty
On this peaceful and quiet road home
