The sun has set
On this old country farm
I’m out riding my tractor
Hauling hay to the barn
Snow lightly packed
This cold winter’s night
Puttering across the yard
Guided by the full moon’s light
Our cat scampers by
She’s out chasing the mice
Darting into the barn
Where the hunting is nice
A few more trailer loads
And the work will be done
Then go inside our warm house
With my family to have fun
No I don’t find
This farm work a bore
It’s all part and parcel
Of my evening chores
