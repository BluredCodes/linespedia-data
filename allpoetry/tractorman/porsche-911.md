It cost a pretty penny but it’s all mineBlack on black and looking real fineSleek and machined ready to runBlowing cars off the road is just too funGrips the corners oh so sweetDriving winding roads is quite the treatAs my foot pushes the gas pedal downBack tires go spinning round and roundTearing up the highway my speed is fastPull over everyone I’m gonna passWith a car like this in high gear modeIt is really true I’m the king of the road
