Into the Bernese Oberland
I really loved to go
High in the Swiss Alps
Blanketed by snow
Little town of Grindelwald
Spread out over the hills
Surrounded by steep mountains
Providing numerous thrills
The views are spectacular
Breathtaking scenery replete
With lakes, wildlife and plants
And vistas hard to beat
Hiking in the Summer
Climbing all year round
When it came to winter
The skiers could there be found
Cog rail trains climbed
From the town down below
Up to the chalets
And the surrounding ski slopes
Being very ingenious
The Swiss built a rail
Right through the massive Eiger
And stopped at a glacier trail
The Jungfrau hotel they built
At the top of those mountains above
Right into the heart of solid rock
Truly a labor of profit and love
After a day of playing real hard
Getting hungry and having sore feet
Sitting down to a hot cooked meal
Then off for a good night’s sleepNot really meant to flow... just wanted to record this for my own memory...
