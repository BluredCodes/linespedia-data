Through sun-filtered trees the leaves I seeHow beautiful they are to meThe days are warmAs wind stirs aboutThe colors of Fall resplendent beOn Oak, Maple and Aspen treesBut still more beautiful than all of theseWhen at last in glory Your face I’ll see
