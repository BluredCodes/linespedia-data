title: "Jake's"
writer: "TractorMan"

Behind our houseAnd across the roadTwo ponds ringed by treesIs where I used to goSwing on a ropeHanging from a treeAfter schoolIt was the place to beSkated on the lower pondWhen it was a sheet of iceSwam in the upper pondWhen the weather turned niceKids would party thereOn Friday nightsThe police would chase us outTurning on red and blue lightsThe place is a park nowOpen during the dayMy kids can now go toWhere I used to play
