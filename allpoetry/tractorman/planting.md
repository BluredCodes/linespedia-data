Springtime is right around the corner
Out here on the semi-arid plains
Looking forward my trees to plant
While expecting late April rains
Holes I’ll dig deep and wide
Sometimes working into the night 
Making room for all my trees
And space them all just right
Off to the nursery I drive
To purchase all my trees
Maples, oaks and aspens
I want the colorful leaves
Joyfully taking them home
And planting them with care
A natural wildlife habitat
My friends said I had there
You know they’re right
I said one Summer day
A forest on the prairie
Not a barren yard of dirt and clayJust trying to capture part of my life in words...
