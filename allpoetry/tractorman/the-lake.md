The kids are ready
Smiles abound everywhere
A hot day in July
Electricity is in the air
The car is packed
And we’re finally ready
Out of the driveway
We glide slow and steady
Suitcases and inner tubes
On top of the car rack
Our eyes are ahead
With no looking back
Out on the road
The 302 we take
A couple of hours later
Pull into the lake
The kids jump out
And scream as they run
Leaping off the dock they go
To begin their fun
In the water all of them swim
Jumping and diving
They yell ‘Dad, Come on in’
While the dog looks for shade
To rest his tired bones
My wife and I are grateful
For our peaceful Summer home
To leave the bustling city
With its noise and traffic lights
To retreat to the county
Has us feeling just right
I’m looking forward
To some quiet and rest
Lakeside with my family
Is life at its best
