I like to read
All kinds of stuff
From heavy thoughts
To lighthearted fluff
From gothic romance
And folklore fare
To science fiction
And space battles out there
History and nature
My interest tweaks
Revolutions and WWII
And sometimes a mystery
When the weather’s rainy
I find a cozy nook
Sit down with a snack
And read a good bookJust some musings... nothing serious here....
