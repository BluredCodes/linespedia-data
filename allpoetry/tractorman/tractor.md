The weather is just right
The field is ready
I doff my cap and overalls
With a whine and a sputter and a little bit of smoke
The old Ford comes to life with a coughing jolt
My oldest son smiles
Into third gear and the clutch is engaged
Out on the acreage mowing the sage
Round and round we go over the field
The yard’s looking great before the first snow
A rabbit is flushed and off he goes
Chased to the fence by canine foes
Oh this is the life I tell my son
A man and his tractor and a job well done
