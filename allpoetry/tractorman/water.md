Water lives
In three discreet forms
Solid, gas and liquid
Which one is the norm
Just to ponder water
I think it’s really neat
How in a liquid we drink it
Then crunch ice with our teeth
And if you heat it nice and hot
To make some great tasting tea
It will start to slowly bubble
Turning to hot gaseous steam
We wash in it we play in it
And it tastes just right
We’re made mostly of it
It’s critical to our life
So the next time you think of it
As you open up the tap
Look at water differently
As it pours into your glass
