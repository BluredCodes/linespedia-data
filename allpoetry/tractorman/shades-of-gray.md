I want my life to count
For everything that’s good
Not just wishing my life away
Upon the maybes and the shoulds
Trials and heartaches
Are as sure as rain
I’ll accept the challenge
And embrace my pain
My soul splashed in colors
To the fullest I choose to live each day
Not with darkened clouds hanging low
In pallid empty shades of gray
