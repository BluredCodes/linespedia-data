---
title: "Out On The Waves"
writer: "TractorMan"

I was out there
That fateful day
Calm water suddenly
Turned into giant waves
Other people were screaming
We all wanted just to survive
Would I ever make it back
To my family alive
It seemed never ending
As we were tossed around
What were we doing here
I cried out for dry ground
Surges swelled ‘round me
And while on a particular crest
I think I spotted land
And began swimming my best
Just when I thought
That I could not go on
My feet and hands began
To hit the bottom
I pulled myself up
Grateful to be alive
When I hear a voice
From a kid of five
Don’t be afraid mister
Of this water park’s wave machine ride
