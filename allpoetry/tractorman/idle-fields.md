The fields are quiet now
Chunky sod overturned in rows
Stalks of corn stripped and broken
Half covered in patches of snow
Tractors sitting by their barns
Lonely, quiet, not a sound
Patiently waiting for winter to pass
With the thawing of now frozen ground
The old farm sits peacefully
Taking a well deserved rest
Some folks round these parts
Say that winter’s season is the best
When all the farming chores
Are at last finished and done
And idle fields stand bearing witness
Of a hard battle fought and won
