I stood next to it 
With great anticipation
Little black and gold 482
Was stopped at the station 
Passengers were boarding
All with a smile
The old iron horse was classy
Our trip would be in style
With a conductor’s whistle
And lots of commotion
The train crept forward
Slowly in motion
Wheels were spinning
Before they got a grip
Waving to the locals
We began our trip
High in the Rockies
Feeling the autumn breeze
Leaves turning to gold
On whitened Aspen trees
Through rocky gorges
And up pine-treed hills
Taking lots of pictures
Soaking in the thrills
Pulling into the station
Right around noon
We eat a hearty western lunch
And none too soon
Back on the train
For our trip back down
I can hardly wait
For the next time around
