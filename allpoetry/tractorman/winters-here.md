---
title: "Winter's Here"
writer: "TractorMan"
slug: "winters-here"
source: "AllPoetry"

The cold wind blows
Over the grassy plain
The snow pelts everything
Like freeze-dried grain
The clouds are low
To barren, frozen ground
Passing by as if racing
To places and parts unknown
Cozy by the fire we snuggle
In our warm prairie home
Listening to the lonely winds
Passing by with howls and moans
