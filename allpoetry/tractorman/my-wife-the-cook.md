It’s time for dinner
I wonder what she will make
Chicken over rice
With some fresh bread baked
Or will we get pasta
With hearty chunks of meat
Top with parmasian cheese
That’s hard to beat
Maybe cheeseburgers
And some hot french fries
Break out the hot dog relish
It’s a feast for the eyes
Cordon bleu is something
She does so well
Chicken, cheese and ham
What a tempting smell
She’s the best cook
That’s easy to say
I love her more
Each and every dayJust an ode to my wife who keeps the family very well fed...
