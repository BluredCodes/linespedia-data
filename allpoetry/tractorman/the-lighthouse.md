Near the rocky coast
An old lighthouse stands
Painted red and white
To lend helping hands
For all who travel
On the open sea
Crafted by hands long ago
To help guide you and me
Whether on a gray and foggy day
Or in a dark and stormy gale
Her candlelight shines bright
For all those who set sail
Has steered them safely
Into a protected bay
From a frothing ocean
Of angry and sullen gray
To lead them home
Where the old sailors dock
That old lighthouse stands
For all like a steady rock
