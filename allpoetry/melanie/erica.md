one day i came from far away
we hated it in one room we stay
we had a friend that kept us apart
i hated her as much as the dark
the next year came 
as it went on we became sane
though we went through alot
still to the end
we remane best friends
then that time came 
i had to go far away
and still to this day
she remanes in my heart
like we aren't even apart
i love this girl like she is my twin
and i always will until the end.

dedicated to the bestest friend in the world
~~ erica deanne englert ~~
