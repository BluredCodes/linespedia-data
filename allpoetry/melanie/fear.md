common sense away it rinses
forcing one to great extent
fear we cannot pay the rent
ringing inside's one ear
death we feel is drawing near
convinced our own child will runaway

but fear is what we took
hope is there for us to find
fear always in our mind
it plays with own imagination
easily with grace and cordination
fear is something you must fight
as it creeps around you in the night
with the thought to remane
