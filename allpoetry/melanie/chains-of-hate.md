the chains of death
wrapped around me
suffocating all the life
the squeezing pain
the unbearable feelings
each swallow and breathe i take
farther away from life
closer and closer to death
pressured by emotions
trying to do something i can't 
therefore shunned away
all in all i have nothing
no one caring for my dying soul
with the last breath
