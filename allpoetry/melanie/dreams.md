title: "DrEaMs"

i dreamt one day 
oh the things i saw
peace on earth
goodwill to men
it was only a dream
the hearts of men
so cruel and loveless
in this dream it was not so 
a man falls down 
his brothers help him up
tha playful feet of children sing
poverty none 
sickness gone
laughing, singing all around
every face a large bright smile
in this place 
i wish to stay
this happy, loving land of many
i would have stayed
the rest of my days
now it is time to go astray
the dream is over
back to the world of hate
