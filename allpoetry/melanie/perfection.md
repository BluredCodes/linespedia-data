a moment of perfection
a time of perfect peace
then a flash of reality

a broken dream

a second of perfect love
an hour of no hate
then i fall into life

a lost vision

a day of no sorrows
a minute of no fears
then a glance of the outside world

a world full of hatred


dedicated to my friend Courtney Alexander!!
