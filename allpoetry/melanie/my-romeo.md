---
title: "my romeo"
writer: "melanie"

love came so suddenly
that it almost past me by
i've dreamed about it all my life
so it made me start to cry

i thought this day would never come
i have began to see the light
you wrap your arms around me
on that wonderful spring night

as i think about our love for each other
through out the night and day
the thought of you leaving me
made me begin to pray

as our love will grow older
i know we wont pull apart
we will hold on forever
i know you wont ever break my heart

when i think about that great day
the very first time i saw you
i feel a tear run down my face
at the thought soon we could be through

our love is so wonderful
i'm hoping it will last
my heart is filled with happiness
when i think about our past
