what is love?
does anyone really know?
it makes you feel like you are flying
in heaven up above
flying through the sky like an angelic dove
it feels like a sweet kiss from an angel
and like a heavenly song
like the sweetest taste of a chocalate pie
you might say this person is the apple of your eye
it brings out the happiness
when you are feeling blue
sometimes it makes you say "hey i love you"
some people say it is like a best friend
still it leaves people wondering in the end
what is love??
