---
title: "eternity with you"
writer: "melanie"
slug: "eternity-with-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/47683-eternity-with-you-by-melanie"
---

i look into your eyes 
and all i can see
is that burning desire 
you have for me

no words need to be spoken
no feelings need to be shared
everyone can feel it
it is in the air

our love is undying
it will never go away
we will be together
when we are old and gray

i am writing this poem 
to say to you
that everything we have been through
i will always and forever love you
