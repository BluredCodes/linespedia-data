our love is like a candle
it shines as bright as day
each time i feel you near
our love shines one more ray

everyday i keep the candle lit
each day i try my best
and whenever i look at you
i hope our love will stay at rest

our love is undying
it will never go away
we will always be togther
no matter what happens each day

i look into your eyes
and all i can see
is that longing desire 
that you have for me

i think you were sent
from heaven above
because it is just like i said
i think i am in love
