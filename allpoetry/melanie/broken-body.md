i was cut at the heart
sliced and diced
my feeling of peace was gone
depression replaced
i lost myself
it broke my body
the stars shone brightly
the clouds all gone
i thought maybe someone cared
as i sit here in wonder
sitting with my broken soul
among the ground i see
my tears fall from my face
so i took the life of someone dear
and that dearest person was mehelp me on any poem please.  hope you enjoy my writing.
