---
title: "So Beautiful"
writer: "sadpoet"
slug: "so-beautiful"

everything about you is perfect, there's nothing i would change
i get this feeling inside of me, it is often very strange
you make my heart  beat so fast, when i see you, i blush
you get my blood 'a  pumpin, the sight of you's a rush
you have the cutest look to you, a touch that makes me melt
i've been in love forever, such loving feelings i have felt
what to do with these feelings, when i know yours are not the same
telling me you love me, is just this endless game
never knowing whether to believe you, or if its just a lie
the thought of you, not in my life, makes me want to die
waking up knowing i'll see you soon is always worth my while
i'd walk a million miles through fire, just to see your smile
loving you would be the only thing i could ever want 
i'd do anything in the world for u, just to put it blunt
if there's something you ever need, just please come to me
i'll be there on the double, whenever i need to be
seeing you unhappy, would just break my heart
i'll solve your problems now, before they can ever start
i'll take all of your problems, and add them all to mine
knowing you'd be problem free runs chills up and down my spine
no one creature i've ever seen is as beautiful as you are
you're the apple of my eye, you are my shooting star
lets stay friends forever, let us never part
cuz if that ever happened, you'd just re-break my heart
