---
title: "Good time to die"
writer: "sadpoet"
slug: "good-time-to-die"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/1571-Good-time-to-die-by-sadpoet"
---

these tears are dripping down my face
as my heart begins to pound and race
the thoughts of death flow through my mind
looking for answers i've yet to find
i have these terrible feelings inside of me
these feelings that u will not see
i cry out for help but no one hears
i've been crying out, for too many years
i'm giving up hope, slowly but surely
i want to live but i'll probably die early
i'm trying to get rid of my pain in a hurry
when i'm cutting myself the pain gets blurry
i like this sudden relief
this sudden numbness away from grief
to think if i would cut too deep
i'd be in this restless sleep
no more worries or time to cry
what better time, than now to dieAnother splurge in my poems. I write about my pain, what else can I say?
