---
title: "My Perfect Friend"
writer: "sadpoet"

from the moment i first met u, i knew u'd be the one
the one i'd cut it loose with, with you i'd have my fun
i could be myself around you, i wouldn't have to change
you would enjoy the way i was acting, even if it was strange
i can come to you with problems, i know you're always there
seems when i feel so alone, i always know you care
you make me feel so special when you are around
a lost and fallen angel, which i am glad that i found
such a special person and nothing less than kind
such a beautiful friendship, i've tried so hard to find
you fill me with these happy thoughts, constant self esteem
you're such a perfect friend, found mostly in a dream
god's blessed me with your friendship, please never let me go
i don't want you out of my life, until i let you know
you are what keeps me standing, keeping me from death
you are what fills my lungs, with my every breath
why did god pick me, to have such a perfect friend
i'll tell you this right now, i never want this to end
thanks for filling up my life, i love you my sunshine
so many people want your love, but i tell them its all MINE
