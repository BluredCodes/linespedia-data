title: "I'm Losing You"
writer: "sadpoet"

i have this leery emptiness growing inside of methe spot inside of me where u used to bei never thought u'd be slipping awaybut i see u less day by dayu say u'll come see me, but then u don'ti want u to stay here, but i know u won'twhy can't things be the way they were beforeu wanna see me less, i wanna see u moreits like that lump in your throat that won't go awayi don't wanna lose you, before i get to sayi love you so much and u mean the world to mei wish things could go back to how they used to bei don't want u gone before i say goodbyeeven though it'll hurt, its better for me to cryi need to let out my feelings, let u know them allhow u ditching me, makes me feel so smallmakes me feel unworthy, less than all the restwanted u to know, i thought u were the bestu've made such an impact, made me feel so wholei wanted to be friends forever, that was my only goalwanted to help you with things u thought were wrongwanted to have a great friendship and it to last longi want you to know this isn't ure fault, its prolly all minebut most of all, i want u to know i love u my sunshine
