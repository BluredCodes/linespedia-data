i have these feelings every dayits like my life is slipping awayi don't want to die but i know i willand i'll have no more dreams to fullfillmy pain and sorrow will be taken awayi am longing now, for that daythat i can be freeand won't have to seemy family madand my friends so sadthey won't have to worry about me anymorecuz i'll be lying dying on the floorbleeding to deathgasping for my own breathhoping to liveand for someone to giveme a new way outbut no one's around so this leaves me in doubti didn't want to gobecause i wanted everyone to knowthat they were all loved by mei wanted them all to seehow much their love meantand how i cherished all our time spenti wanted to share with them every last wordi wanted them to know i loved them and that to be heardearlier on, i slit my wristsso as time goes on, less pain existsmy pain is fading as my breath slowsi just hope now that everyone knowshow much of an impact they made on meand how much i wanted to bewith them till the endand i wanna thank them all, for being my friendAnother goodbye for when I go. Everything is here, for you all to know.
