---
title: "A Little Girl"
writer: "sadpoet"
slug: "a-little-girl"

there was this little girl, she was so small
she wasn't hated by anyone, she was loved by all
she tried to be nice and make everyone happy
but no matter what she did, her life was always crappy
she was always scared of dying, of her parents dying too
she couldn't seem to relax, she didn't know what to do
she was so small, her world should have been worryless
but instead she had a young life full of stress
she had a drunk for a dad, he always made her cry
a mom who couldn't defend her, but she didn't know why
she had a brother and sister who went through kinda the same
but she didn't want them in trouble, so she'd take the blame
she always tried to be good, so dad wouldn't get mad
but no matter what she did, in his eyes she was always bad
the hits she took weren't always that severe
it was the rage that came from daddy's drinking beer
all she wanted was to get some love returned
but it seems every time she tried, she only got burned
she was always different, so she was left on her own
she would have died to be loved, but she was always alone
as time went by, her life seemed to get better
she made some friends, they were glad to have met her
her dad quit drinking, but his temper stayed there
he tried to be better, tried more to care
she still felt left out, even though she fit in
she was always still sad so she could never win
her life was always a mess, something was always still wrong
but she tried to be happy, and tried to stay strong
as she thought about the past, she found a disturbing memory
she knew this was wrong, and this should never be
she remembered her cousin messing with her when she was little
knowing she was small, weak and brittle
he was not forceful physically, only with things he said
remembering this shit, made her want to be dead
he started it first, when she was six or seven
then started again when she was about eleven
not knowing what he had her doing wasn't right
she would do things for him without putting up a fight
he knew what was being done, when she had no clue
she didn't know it was wrong, she just did what he told her to
she messed up with guys a lot after he got her started
she did stuff with guys, and was left broken hearted
her parents found out that she had sex before
and they sat her down and questioned her more
she told them how her cousin messed around with her too
she told them she just did what he asked her to do
they were very mad about what he had done
so they told his parents and ruined his fun
he denied it at first to try to make her look bad
and took for granted all the feelings she had
so after this passed things kinda went well for awhile
she had everything out, she wasn't living in denial
she still hurt and couldn't move on with her life
she couldn't cope with her feelings so she cut herself with a knife
she started this when she was about twelve years old
it helped her out when she felt alone and cold
no one knew about it for quite awhile
she would walk around, put on a fake smile
she'd wear long sleeves to hide the scars and cuts
she wanted to tell someone but she didn't have the guts
eventually her excuses for the cuts didn't work anymore
so she told her parents and what she did it for
they tried to be there for her, but sometimes they just got mad
they couldn't understand why she was always just sad
they didn't understand that no one made her that way
that she had realized it was her fault from the very first day
she lost a lot of friends when they found out what she did
some long term friendships from when she was a kid
they didn't understand why she would hurt herself and how it felt good
she didn't mind, cuz she didn't expect that they would
she had a big heart and loved everyone the same
she didn't take anyone's feelings for a game
she knew how it felt for people to feel alone and sad
so she gave them all the good advice that she had
she tried to help her friends whenever she could
she did the things that most others should
she loved everyone quickly, and when they left she was sad
so she was back to square one, her life was again bad
junior high came around, she didn't think she'd fit in
she never wanted seventh grade to begin
when she first went there, she was still all alone
she was once again, out there on her own
she met some friends and they stayed for awhile
and when they were there, they helped make her smile
junior high passed, she had more and more friends now
she was so astonished, she couldn't imagine how
in ninth grade is when most of her trouble came
she messed up things constantly, she was living in shame
when ninth grade passed, she had nothing left to hide
she wouldn't have to worry about the times she lied
she made many new friends in tenth grade, she still has them today
if it weren't for everyone, she would have taken her life away
her friends helped her most throughout all the trouble she had
they made her happy, so she was not always sad
they made her life as good as it could be
well i told you a story about a little girl, and that little girl was me
