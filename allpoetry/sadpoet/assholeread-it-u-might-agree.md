a sick person is one who would trick a child
into doing something so sick and wild
starting at a young age, working your way
breaking her down each and every day
starting off small, then increasing to big
this is the perfect plan of a monstrous pig
start her when she's young so she won't know its wrong
put her hands where you think they belong
you think you're cool cuz you're getting some
but she will realize someday that you are just scum
she will realize how you took advantage of her young soul
and you'll say she's a liar and she's out of control
you'll deny the sick things you used to make her do
you'll put the blame on her and take it off you
you've ruined her life and you don't even care
cuz unlike her, you can pretend she was never there
you can block it out and move on with your sick game
leaving little girls, to live a life full of shame
is this what you're proud of, is this how you'll live
taking whatever a little girl will give
you are a sick fuck and you deserve only to die
to make up for every fucking little girl u made cry
who knows how many there were, that were before me
how many more, two or maybe three
your death should be painful and slow, but as long as it takes
to print boldly in red all your fuckin mistakes
i hope when you're dying you realize and regret what you did wrong
and i hope your pain and suffering lasts very long
you've left my life with shame and even more regret
you made me feel like i was your fucking pet
i hope you fucking die and die in such pain
or maybe before you die, you'll be a cripple with a cane
whatever happens in your life, i hope its really bad
so you can feel some of the pain i've had
you shouldn't have had me do things that you had me do
cuz those are the reasons that i fucking HATE YOU!
