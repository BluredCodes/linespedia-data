---
title: "not finished"
writer: "sadpoet"
slug: "not-finished"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

someone tell me whats wrong with me
i can't be the person im wanted to be
im not the kind of person anyone would want
im not good enough for someone to flaunt
i try to be nice and give them the best
but it seems to me, i just become a pest
i can't be their friend because they don't want me around
they used to be that one best friend i found
but now im not good enough to satisfy their need
so now its time for me to sit back and bleed
why should i bother living anymore 
without this friend what do i have to live for
she pushes me away when i want her to be there
i know i treat her well, i know this isn't fair
