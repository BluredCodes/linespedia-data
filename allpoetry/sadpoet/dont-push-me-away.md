please don't push me out of your life let me stay here with youlet me give you advice when you don't know what to dolet me be your shoulder to cry on when no one else is therewhen you think you're all alone, please remember i carei'll never leave your side, it'll have to be u leaving metrust me, thats not the way i want it to bei want to be here, when u need someone to be your friendif that is what u want too, i'll be here until the endyou won't have to worry about who'll be there for youcuz if u need someone, i'll be stuck to u like gluei'll try to lessen the hurt u feel, turn ure frown into a smilei know that this friendship of ours, is definitely worth my whilei wake up daily because you make my life seem goodu make me happy, the way a friend shouldits usually u who comes when i've been let downbut lately, its been the other way aroundis there something i've done wronghave i been your friend for way too longdid i hurt you or make you saddid i turn your happy mood baddo i make u feel guilty about things u dowhen u need someone to love do i say fuck youif im doing something wrong, just tell me and i'll quiti'm feeling so empty and confused, im getting sick of itare you trying to be distant, trying to get apartcuz if this is what you're doing, its gonna break my heartmy friends mean everything to me, nothing more or lessthinking of losing them, is what brings me stressplease tell me you aren't distancing yourself like u said u might dobut if thats what you're doing, just know that i love you
