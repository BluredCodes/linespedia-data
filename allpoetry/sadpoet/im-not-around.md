soon you'll see im not around
and know inside that i've not found
the strength to live through the days
and i couldn't change my suicide ways
this feeling inside must cause you pain
but later on you'll only gain
the relief
from all the grief
i've given you
you know its true
but im gone now
i don't know how
i had the guts to end it all
i tried for so long to stall
but i got weaker as the days past
now im free from pain at last
i took a gun to my head
i pulled the trigger, now i'm deadAnother deep brainstorm has brought another sad poem.
