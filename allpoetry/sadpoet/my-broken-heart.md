it was those times in your armsi was free from all my harmsi thought u loved me and felt the samewhen i found out different i felt pure shameu crushed my heart and let me downu turned my smile into a frownwhen i was with u i felt so goodbut u fucked me over like i knew u wouldhurt my feelings and broke my heartripped my precious feelings aparttook the love i thought we hadturned my happy feelings sadmade me cry and took away my smilei thought u'd love me for awhileu stomped my feelings like they didn't matterbroke my heart and watched it shatteru tried to use me just to get somei shoulda known this, why was i so dumbi shoulda expected it from the startso i coulda prevented my broken heartwho hasn't broken this heart of mine?
