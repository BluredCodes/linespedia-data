i fell in love and hit the floormy heart is broken i can't stand much morebeing near u makes me happywithout you around everything seems crappymy days are long and my nights are tooall the while, im thinking of youyou've got that smile to put out a firethe thought of you fills my every desireconfessing my love in so many waysthoughts fill my mind thinking of all the dayswe sat around and i was held in your armsbreathing your scent, thinking of all your charmsi can't stand the thought of u ever bein gonei know in my heart, i'll never move onno one on earth could replace this feelingthrough these words i've been revealinghow your existence makes mine worthwhilei wake up each day just to see your smilealways in my thoughts, constantly on my mindyou're this irreplaceable love that i'd never findi've fallen in love and i know this is trueif you didn't know yet, this person is youi cant go on without you herei just wanted to say, i love you dearjust another poem...
