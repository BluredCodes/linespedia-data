Licking gently, Furling orange fingers 
Blistering everything 
Underneath it's writhing sea of colour 
Panic in it's wake 
Finding the line, Between love and hate 
Would you freeze or burn? 
If you had the choice 
Would you let the flames engulf you?a while ago,
