You asked me to write for you,
"Make me feel it"
You asked me to sing for you,
"Let me hear it"
You asked me to live for you
"Make me believe it"
So I cut out my soul,
Snapped all the sinnews
And posted it to you,
With the words and notes
Written in blood,
Sealed with tears,
Smeared with spirit,
