title: "So you'r leaving"
writer: "Cally"

You say you'r leaving
Though you've already left.
I felt it in my soul,
Deftly you crept away;
You protest you have to go,
I laugh:
"You've been gone for years"
My face forces smiles
To delay the tears.
And what's sad,
I watched you go
And I forgot to say
Good bye or take care,
Or to remind you 
That I'll always be there.
You say you'r leaving
Things to see and do,
"Catching up and
Little things."
In you'r leaving 
I find relief;
I grant my face
