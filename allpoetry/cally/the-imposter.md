Your icy fingers slither up my spine,
Move through my mind like eels through midnight water
You wisper gently to my concience, tickling reason
Sugar sweet kisses, acid rain on my skin.

Your echo bounces around my brain
Smoothing silken sins into my scalp, burning images
Behind shimmering eyes that see little else,
You call to me through smoke and laughter

I hear your voice, fragments of Hollywood reality,
Glamour and glitter as you shimmer my way,
Holding fingers like tendrills around my sanity,
You ignite the all encompasing fire, you are an imposter,
