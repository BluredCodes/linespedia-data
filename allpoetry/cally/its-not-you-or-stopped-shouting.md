I am not angry with you
I am angry with life
Like a child fighting to grow
Struggling against
the confines of it's own body
I to my mind
My stage, my life,
I struggle,nothing is
Quick enough
When your rareing to go

But do not think
I am angry with you
I am angry with 
All that I have to do
All that won't come
Quick enough
All that feels
A bit too tough

In the midst of the struggle,
Which is really a joy,
I fight to prove
My self, my life
My dreams and hopes
All of which you are

Woven through my soul
You bear my frustrtaions
Without a murmer
YOu allow the outburst
You do not shy away
I am not angry with you
But you get the blame

You are my solace
My heart, my soul;
My boy
You hold me up
You are my centre
My peace, my home
My love
Thank you.Every felt like you taking everything out on the people you really shouldn't be !! Hope this one helps with the sence of the other two ('Shouting' and 'Still shouting')
