I am tiny 
I bring life with friends 
Death alone 
And I sit up here and wait. 

Waiting for the right time 
To burst and shine 
In my transient abode, 
I whistle to and fro. 

The moment comes, 
My life begins 
In that ever downwards spiral 
I am released. 

Pulled out vertically, 
Still together 
I sail, buffeted by sheets 
Of steel and peuter, 

I sway into the feathery breeze 
And hover gentley 
Just to tease, 
My friends and I 
Wait, bye and bye. 

Until the crashing 
Thundering, whipping 
Rain, beating down 
Through vast expanses 
Open seas of cloud and air, 

We reach the soild, 
We are there, 
And with a feathers kiss 
Or with titans rage 
I shatter into fragments: 
Make pools for birds to bathe.a while ago,
