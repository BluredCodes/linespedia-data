Your ribs are talking to my feet,
They rumble away
Talking in tongues, 
Messages that can not be voiced
As I lie next to you
With you, on you
Rumblings that make me feel warm,
Your hands talk to my skin
Finding fireworks where there was no flame,
Your voice awakens symphonies,
Your arms speak to my soul,
As our bodies entwine,
The longing and the painful hope
Fades, like the absence
As your heart talks to mine.Hmmmmmmmmmmmm
