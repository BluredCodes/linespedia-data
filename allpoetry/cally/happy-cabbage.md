I am the happy cabbage,
Or carrot, turnip, sweade?
I have no desire to go out at night
No desire or need
I sit here with my telly
And my music on
And read books, take baths
Eat chocolate
And still the world goes on

I am the happy cabbage,
Or brocile or pea,
I take no time to shout about
How happy I'm being me,
Friends call up to say hello
And we chat for hours,
And when there's nothing left to do, 
I'll sit and look at flowers

I am the happy cabbage
Or artichoke or plum,
But I really must go out
Some more and have a bit more fun.Just in case, a happy cabbage is not meant to be offensive and just means that I don't move alot (I am in fact quite lazy)
