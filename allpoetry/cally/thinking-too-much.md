---
title: "Thinking too much"
writer: "Cally"

Sitting here,
Thinking
Sitting here,
Aching
Wondering 
what's left to do

Sitting here 
Thinking
Sitting here
Aching
Wondering
What's wrong with you

When I know
It's nothing
Turning into
Everything
Wondering
What I'm
to do

To tired to argue
To frightened
To fight
And I don't have
The legs
To run

Sitting here
Thinking
Sitting here
Aching
Knowing 
How far you'd go
Just to come.not the best expression of this but never mind :)
