Lying awakeWatching youAsleep? or not,Silent either wayWaking up 2,3,4, timesJust to touch youAnd to know I have.Sleep steals thatSleep steals; timeI want not to waste sleepingTime chased away by the sunOr not,Just chased away by timeAs you sleep on.
