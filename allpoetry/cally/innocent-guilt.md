Why do I feel this guilty?
When there was nothing I could do,
He was a substitue
When I wanted to be with you,

There were no kisses
Certainly no sex
Although he tried and tried
And he gave it his best

Silliness took over
But there is not enough,
beer in the world
To make me forget

Maybe I feel this guilty
Because I think I led him on,
Maybe I feel this guilty
Because I know that I was wrong.

There is no-one who compares to you
But lies can always be told
And the guilt of doing nothing
Will soon be getting old

I can not count how much I love you
Or how you make me feel
And every time I'm tested 
Just makes my love more real

And so I ask for forgivness
For alwayts saying no
And putting myself in situations
That allow the guilt to grow.Went out got drunk, got chatted up, feel bad 'cos my fella wasn't there,nothing happened but I still feel guilty - go figure! (and before all you psychologists get going there was no desire for anything to happen either)
