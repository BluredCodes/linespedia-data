---
title: "Deeply Dissatisfied  (or 'Shouting')"
writer: "Cally"
slug: "deeply-dissatisfied-or-shouting"
source: "AllPoetry"

Wondering about you
Disregarding me
Feeling small
Insignificant and silly.
Overreaction has been my forte,

I need to know
If you needed help
Who would you call,
Collegues to catch you,
Would I watch you fall?

Nothingness niggles,
Feeling small,
Downsized, Disregarded
Deeply disatisfied

Do you think
That I can't do it?
Just not want to ask?
Making me feel small
Insiginificant and silly

I mend, I hold, I cheer
But don't come too close
Always; sheild fear
To delicate to ask
To powerfull to tell

Downsized, disregarded
Esteem shot to hell.bad mood that day :)
