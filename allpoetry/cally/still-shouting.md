Not asked,
Not considered,
Fleeting through the mind?
No, not for a second
Not you: just your kind.

The dawning of illusion
The germ of an idea
Never asked or considered
Never drawn near

This is not for sharing
With the likes of me
Your dream, your hero worship
Your Epiphany.

Not cut, never cast,
No role to play in this
Never thought of in your lime and fire
No mouth, no voice, no kiss

Off; you run ideas headlong
Screaming passion raw
Never thinking, never pausing
