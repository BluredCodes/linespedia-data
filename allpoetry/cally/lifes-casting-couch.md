title: "Life's casting couch"

Drifting towards my fate,
Unknown as of yet,
But that's the best part.
I open my arms
To the unknowing
Unseeing, uncertainty,
Thriving in darkness.
I'm not asking for answers,
The questions; Not there.
There was no audition,
There was no cast,
I did not ask for the role,
But I will play the part,
Dreamily drifting
