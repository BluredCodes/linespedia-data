---
title: "The death of my muse"
writer: "Cally"
slug: "the-death-of-my-muse"
source: "AllPoetry"

I sit here, 
Head in hands,
Lamenting your loss,
Though you never left,
Once you gave me words,
I'd never imagined,
Numbness replaces
Control overwhelms
Calmness descends
seeping into my, pours
over me like
Liquid nitrogen
Chilling my soul

No kiss will breath life
No touch
Warms your skin
No words
Awaken your eyes
My soul is out of reach
Your smile inspires,
