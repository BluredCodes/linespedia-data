title: "Voiceless"

I spoke to you,I spoke with you,I spoke at you,You listenedYou laughed,You took my voice,To mold, to mendTo createTo killYou took my voiceAnd left me shattered.My language rapedLeft bleeding, brokenI did not rageThe words weren't there.An empty shellLeft lifeless but livingHumiliated.The ultimate TriumphIn your theftThe shining jewelIn your armorCrushing the spiritExulting in your gloryYou took my voiceAnd I don't want it backThe enery is lostThe passion dousedThe lightPut outYou took my voiceAnd I can't rageThe words, all died.
