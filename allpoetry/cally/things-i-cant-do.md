---
title: "Things I can't do"
writer: "Cally"
slug: "things-i-cant-do"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/44466-Things-I-cant-do-by-Cally"
---

I can't spell,
What the hell.
My grammars not great,
Comprehensions late.
Poetry unpublished,
Novel not read.
Talent is terminal
Flare is dead,
But my lines still rhyme,
So it must be fine.
And I can still get
Work in on time,
I might not be
the next big thing
Can't write, can't dance
Not to mention sing.
So I'll keep penning
In my darkened room,
And think of life
With a litle less doom.
Happy thought are more my style
So what does it matter
If it makes someone smile.
