---
title: "Bound Independence"
writer: "Cally"
slug: "bound-independence"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I can't touch myself
Without touching you,
I Taste my lips
I taste yours
My skin is your skin
My soul is yours
There is no
Dis-association
I can't touch myself
Without feeling you
And I lie awake
Breathing you in
Breathing me out
I don't lose myself
In you
I am still my
Identity
but I can't touch
myself anymore
Unnerving, unraveling
Within my soul
My skin
I feel you
And I can't touch
myself anymore;
My life is my own
I own my soul
But I can't touch
Myself
Without feeling you
I am one
We are two
My soul is mine
though it's with you
In this bound
And glorious independence
I can't touch myself
