---
title: "Spare Life?"
writer: "Cally"
slug: "spare-life"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

You ask me what I'm doing,
And when I reply,
I'm living my spare life,
You can't help but ask me why.
And What's that and
What d'ya mean;

I'm living the life that's 
in between,
Not my ideal,
Not my dream,
Just the life that's
in between.

You wonder, what's wrong,
Can't see that nothing is,

There's things I want
In this life,
And something has to give.
So I'm living my spare life,
The one I keep in reserve,
And I'm waiting, working,
Wanting
