title: "Losing You (Challenge poem)"

I can't think of losing you, 
My mind shuts down 
My soul turns blue 
I can't go through all that again 
The hurt and agonising pain 

So I build walls 
Tell myself it doesn't matter 
Close down my heart 
And soul hereafter 

I can't face another loss, 
So I'll cut out all 
Potential gain 
And never fall in love again. 

I'll close down my mind 
Wrap up my heart 
Stifle my soul 
Tear myself apart. 

I can't think of losing you 
So I'll lose myself before I do.
