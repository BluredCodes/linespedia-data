---
title: "I used to dream of poetry"
writer: "Cally"

Floating here,
On the edge of reality,
Slipping through walls
that border insanity
Wondering about 
the transience,
That makes up life
And desire,
Wondering about 
the drive,
the passion
the fire
Floating on dreams
That once were of poetry,
Changing focus
Edges blur, in
a new clarity
Worries ebb
And slither away
I lift my heart
To the new day.
