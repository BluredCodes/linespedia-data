---
title: "How he felt"
writer: "Cally"

I'm sorry that
You find me threatening,
You don't see me
Quivering.
I'm sorry that 
You think I'm too much;
Much hides inside
This solid frame.
I'm sorry that
You must be near me,
And that I can't shrink inside.
I'm sorry that
You almost fear me,
And that Hate that
I won't hide.
