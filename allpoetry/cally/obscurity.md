---
title: "Obscurity"
writer: "Cally"
slug: "obscurity"

She stood silently alone,
That night by the ocean.
Moonlight turned tears,
Into diamonds
As she peeled off her clothes,
Icey fingers through her soul.

The water rushed around
her sorrow,
She held her breath,
and thought
'No more tommorrow'

Floating on a blue glass fabric,
Black, blue velvet
Made her shroud,
As the current pulled her down
Where she could hurt
No more, there was no pain
To endure.

Through watery eyes
She viewed the stars,
Jealous in their isolation
sent back cold stares of condemnation,
Peppered diamonds in the sky,
She sank silently through
She let herself die.
