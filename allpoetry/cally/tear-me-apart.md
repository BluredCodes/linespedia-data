---
title: "Tear me apart"
writer: "Cally"
slug: "tear-me-apart"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/33592-Tear-me-apart-by-Cally"
---

Untangle my soul,
Don't. Leave me alone.

Run your fingers through my heart,
Uncomplicate my life,
Deconstruct my longing,
Wrap your soul around my scars,

Don't. Leave me alone.
I ask all this and more
Don't leave me alone.
I ask too much,

Reconstruct my being
Within your hearts home,
Don't leave me;
Alone
