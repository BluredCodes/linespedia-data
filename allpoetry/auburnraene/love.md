I always wanted to taste the sweet taste of love.
I always wanted to know how it must feel to fly like a dove.
Flying carelessly through the winds of life,
How it would feel to be someones wife
Oh the wonders I knew it would hold!
I wanted to be the princess 
So then my fairytale could be told.
Then my sweet love I met you
And all that was ever dreamt of came true.
You have shown me what the power of dreams can hold.This one almost has a childish feeling to it but I really love what it means to me. What can I do to make a little more powerful? Any suggestions would be appreciated. :-)
