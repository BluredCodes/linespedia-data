Burning tears streamed down my face that day
And I remember all you could do was look away.
I poured out my heart and soul
I was shy but yet I strayed if only for a moment 
to plead with you to stay
I needed you so desperatly so
you reminded me that you had to go
Sometimes, even today, I go back to that dark place
and remember the expression that engulfed your dying face
I can not put into words how it felt when I recieved the call
mind numbing pain my heart seconds from the fall
I have tried to move on and I suppose that someday I shall
