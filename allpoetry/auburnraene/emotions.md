I am supposed to be so happy but sometimes all I feel is pain
I have so very much to be thankful for but sometimes the rain just seems to drown me
I feel so angry with no apparent reason why
When I try to find the problem the rain gets deeper
A world of emotions so strong I get dizzy 
I don't know how to deal with all of this
It would feel so very good just be able to sit down and cry
Please don't get me wrong
I love my life dearly
