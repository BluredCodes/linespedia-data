title: "Summer Nights are Gone"

Summer nights are gone
Replaced by Falls beautiful dawn
Crystal blue skys with puffy clouds
The wonderful sound of the crickets song
What a wonderful world that we live
That something as beautiful as summer nights
Are replaced with the gorgeous awakening
Of Falls enticing lights
Yellow and orange leaves to tweak the soul of the unexpecting
When the beautiful Summer nights are gone
Take a look at the wonderful Fall Dawn that God has given us
And the world seems better than what we make it
All of the wonders nature holds shouldn't be taken for granted
When problems grip seems so bold
Go outside and look around
For worries seem to melt at Falls wonderful sound
