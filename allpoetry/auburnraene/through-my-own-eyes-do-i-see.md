---
title: "Through my own eyes do I see"
writer: "AuburnRaene"

I see you as you need to be seen not as how you want to be
Those are the words that on a daily basis I need said to me
I take so many things for granted
Hate to say it but my sight has been very slanted
I beg for words of encourgement and praise
But when I receive it, it all seems just a haze
I always think that I am seen as "not good enough"
But the only one who's words are so so rough....
Are my own
