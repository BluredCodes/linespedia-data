---
title: "What did you mean?"
writer: "AuburnRaene"

Emotions run so deep they hurt sometimes
Am I supposed to laugh, Am I supposed to cry
What did you mean by that sigh?
As I looked at you tonight everything felt so right
What did you mean by quit being so uptight?
I try so hard sometimes to be full of joy
It comes off as being a ploy
What did you mean when you said that I looked beautiful and a little coy?
I want so badly to quit being so paranoid
What did you mean when you said that you were slightly annoyed?
I want to take things as they are but are they really what you say?
