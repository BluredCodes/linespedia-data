Sometimes you will find beauty in the ugliest places
So look past the faces and into their souls 
And you are bound to find something worth more than gold.I know that this one is short and to the point but that was on purpose. I was awake late one night watching TV and I just got so SICK of how beauty is portrayed in todays world. So, a few lines ran through my head and I didn't actually put them on paper till a couple of years later. I hope it something that you guys will enjoy reading :-)
