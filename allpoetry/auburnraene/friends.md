Friends come and go
As the years pass you by look back in time
And the true face of friendly love will show
All the times you laughed and 
All the times you cried
Remember the ones shoulder that was supplied
Every time love was lost
Every friend that passed
Remember the ones who guided you through
Never forget the angels that God has given 
To help guide you through life's rough path
