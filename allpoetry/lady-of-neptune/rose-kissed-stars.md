`
A hearts violin plays whimsical tunes of love

The ~romantics dance with their dreams of desire

as rose kissed stars alight the heavens above


 
Innocence adorned on the wings of a dove

Soaring gracefully over passion's frantic empire

cooing songs that dismiss the gray clouds with a shove


 
Tears of the angel’s pool into your eyes alcove

A prelude of loving rains before the birth of suns fire 

Hearts radiate in the morning glow of love's treasure trove
