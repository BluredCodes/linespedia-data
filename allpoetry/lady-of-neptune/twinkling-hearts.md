Twinkling hearts bloom stars

Falling moonlight paints the sky

Spring diamonds shining

~*~

Day glows Amethyst

Silhouettes in passions dance

flaming spirits rise

~*~

Sun shards cutting night

Blue eyes casting days glory

Heavens testify
