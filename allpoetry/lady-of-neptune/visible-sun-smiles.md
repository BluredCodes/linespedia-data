title: "Visible Sun Smiles"
writer: "lady of neptune"

Kiss Me
beneath the visible sun
anticipating tastes
beholden
to your smile

Parting Lips
light shines anew
while hearts sing
hypnotized
with passions symphony

Slow Dance
waltzing to dreams
until morning comes
Breathless 
from all her sighs

Kiss Me
beneath the visible sun
Senses Euphoric
transformed
by your smile
