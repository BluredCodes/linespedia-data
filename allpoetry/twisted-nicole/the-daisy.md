title: "The Daisy"
writer: "Twisted Nicole"

I consulted a daisy today…
He loves me? He loves me not?
…He did not say.
That’s one thing the daisies should be taught,
To help out damsels in distress
Figure out what’s going on,
Because to this day, I cannot rest
Wondering if that daisy was wrong.
Because I want to stay this way
Just happy, like right now
But there’s not much left to say
And I love you, but I don’t know how.
What’s the difference between daisies and weeds?
I can see the beauty in the two…
I hope you really need me
Because I really need you.
Mr. Daisy, tell me what’s going on
Please help me on my way
And I hope that you’re not wrong again, 
Or I’ll consult another daisy today.
