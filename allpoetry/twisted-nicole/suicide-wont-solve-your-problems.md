It's a lonely world when you just have shadows to talk to.
It's hard to live when no one loves you for you.
But we keep on going,
All we can do is keep growimg.

There's too many people who just don't care
There's too many souls who are unaware.
All you can do about it, is be a man,
Becuase I may be weak, but I understand.

So tell me what is the point for us?
Why keep on fighting when there is no justice?
There's so many problems...
And  just so you'll know, death won't solve them.

No one to talk to.
No one who loves you for you.
Why keep going?
How do we stop ourselves from growing?
Nobody cares,
So many unaware.
And we all have our problems,
But suicide will never solve them.
So we shouldn't give up,
Becuase the world for everyone is this fucked up.
