Hunny don't look at me with those eyes,Because they'll cover up all your lies.They make me fall deep into your soul,I think this is love and I'm out of control.What would you be without me?It's like water without a sea.And if I could stay forever like this,The only thing I'd want is your stare and your kiss.You've pulled me in, I'm in so deep,To the point where I think about you in my sleep.Ooh, you know I've got it bad,Because you're just the best I've ever had.Thank you for always being thereTo wipe away all my tears.And thank you for telling me It'll all be alright After me and my parents got into that fight.I'm sorry if I ever hurt you,That's the last thing I'd ever want to do.Even though we've had some rough timesYou know that you'll always be mine.And still your eyes just tell it all,Baby, they really make fall...Over and over againI hope this time we both can win.I love those eyes and that smile,It wouldn't be enough for just a while.You could hold me forever and I wouldn't mind,Just you and me standing still in time.So please just stare into my eyes,Even if they'll cover up your lies,Because no one will love you like I do,And i really do think It's too good to be true.A poem for David :o) I love you!!!!
