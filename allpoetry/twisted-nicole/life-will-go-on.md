---
title: "Life Will Go On"
writer: "Twisted Nicole"
slug: "life-will-go-on"

Lost in the hallways of this stress
Getting myself away from this mess
Lonely on the highway of hate and sorrow
Wondering if I'll get there sometime tommorow
Waking up in a moment of gloom
As I lay in a daze inside of my room
The dreams may come, nightmares go away
This pain is surrounding and it wants to stay.
Looking around, the love is gone
As i whisper to myself, life will go on.
