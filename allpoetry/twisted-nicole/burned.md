You burned my flesh
With your words
Of fire.
Like a dragon,
You breathe on me
And cuase scars.
If I was
such a distraction,
What does that say
About you?
David
You burned me.
Scorched my flesh
Took my oxygen
Left smoke 
In my eyes
Everything left
Is a pile of ash
Grey and fragile
Breaking at the touch,
Just like me,
How you left it all.
I always believed 
When I got burned 
You'd be the one
To pour on cold water
Not lava 
To make it worse.
