---
title: "You Didn't See"
writer: "Twisted Nicole"
slug: "you-didnt-see"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/36067-You-Didnt-See-by-Twisted-Nicole"
---

Sorry I was never the perfect one 
For you
But we had something since it's begun
And I'm sorry it's through.
I tried the best I could
To make, you happy
I loved you like no one else would
But it's not something you'd see
But why is it over?
Can you tell me why?
Now that we're older,
Did the time just pass you by,
Without me,
Am I the one you miss?
Didn't you see...
that I can still taste your kiss
And it's something I appriciate,
The time we had together
But now you cannot relate
To my words, lost like a letter.
Didn't you see?
That we had something going?
But i guess it wasn't me,
You thought my opinions were always annoying,
And I still don't know why
We just paused like that
And why did you lie?
Then don't even stop to chat
But what is it I didn't do
To make you happy?
I thought that you knew 
All that we could be,
All I did was love you
Nothing really went wrong
Are you thinking of me too
When you hear our song?
Baby, without me...
Are your days a little lonely?
So much that we could be
But maybe you just didn't see.
Did you ever notice that the spark has left my eyes?
And are you aware 
It burned out when I started to cry?
But it's not like you care,
You don't care anymore
Not about anything to do with me,
But my heart you had torn
But I guess you didn't see.
