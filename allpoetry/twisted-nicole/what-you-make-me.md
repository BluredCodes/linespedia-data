---
title: "What You Make Me"
writer: "Twisted Nicole"
slug: "what-you-make-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/16204-What-You-Make-Me-by-Twisted-Nicole"
---

You Make me everything I don't want to be
But you just don't see what's inside of me.
I say i hate you, and i don't know why
Then once I love you, you make me cry.
Do you enjoy messing with my head?
Maybe I should forget you and get out of here instead.
Sometimes I know you care
Then it all comes back and i don't feel there.
Why don't you shut up now?
You tell me to, but i don't know how
What am i supposed to say?
I'll never compare to her anyways.
You know I don't want to act so mean,
It's just the things unsaid and the things unseen.
And what am i supposed to do?
Just sit around and wait for you?
No thanks, thats just not me
Becuase you make me everything i don't want to be.
