---
title: "Wash You Off In The Shower"
writer: "Twisted Nicole"
slug: "wash-you-off-in-the-shower"
source: "AllPoetry"

I step into the shower
To wash off the dirty things you've left me with.
I can't tell
If that's a salty tear
Rolling down my face
Or just
One of the times you said
You loved me.
You've left pieces of your heart
Between my teeth,
Your lies
Underneath my nails,
Aggervation 
Tangled in my hair,
Hand prints
Left on my waist.
My wounds bleed,
The color of orange
Diluted blood
Fills the tub.
They've healed before
Then you stuck in your knife again,
Then poured the salt one last time.
It stings,
Every part of me.
I scrub
Sarcasticness 
Off of my shoulders,
The kisses
Off of my lips.
A layer of skin
Off my back
From the memories 
We've shared.
I turn off the water
And watch you
Spin helplessly 
Down the drain
In your own filth
And I laugh
As you complain,
I get out of the shower
And dry off again.
