---
title: "The Evil Ones"
writer: "Twisted Nicole"
slug: "the-evil-ones"
source: "AllPoetry"

Don't you even look at me
The people like you are so fake
Don't even pretend to be,
Your friendships are all mistakes.
The root of evil begins with you
It contaminates a healthy mind
I can't even look at you,
Hell's flames will make me blind.
Don't you even smile at me,
I see Satan in your teeth
So just let me be who i have to be
Give me some room to breathe.
I'll always be better than you,
It's something I've always felt 
You can't pretend and hide the truth,
So go back to your home in hell. 
Just pick up your ghetto ass, 
And please get out of my way
Don't look at me when you pass
I don't like the look of evil's face.
I'm not like you at all
Don't give those evil vibes that you send 
The fake rubs off when you fall
And the real you shows up in the end.
