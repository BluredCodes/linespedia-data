I don't know what to do today
Should I cry or be happy?
Becase something strange came this way
And hit my family.

A baby's being born
His name is Andrew
But in this same morn, 
An old man's life was through.
So as you can imagine.. 
Our family is quiet confused,
We're stuck in our emotions, 
And don't know what to do.

He was like my grandfather
And I was shocked to hear,
That this once beautiful morning
Would result to tears...
The family was at the hospital
Then I got the call
And it was my job to tell
The bad news to all...

While some were so excited
About the new addition
Others didn't have words to express
Their lost emotion.

But maybe it's a sign from God
Telling us to keep our heads high,
Because even though we lost a man,
Our family got a new little guy...

Our hearts may be torn
From the surprising lost,
But for the sake of more babies,
Sadly, that's the cost.Today my Aunt is going through labor, 2 hours later I got a phone call saying Mr. Johnson (who is my cousin's grandfather) passed away. No one could get a hold of anybody at the hospital because cell phones weren't allowed. This is just a quick write I did, I pretty much had to stay strong and talk to somebody there and tell them the news.
