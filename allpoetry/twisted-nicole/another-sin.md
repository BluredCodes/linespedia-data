---
title: "Another Sin"
writer: "Twisted Nicole"
slug: "another-sin"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/16152-Another-Sin-by-Twisted-Nicole"
---

The door slams in front of my face
I'm left alone, without a trace
Just what's left in my memory
The things you said and did for me.
I place my hands on the door and feel the cold
...All the secrets i kept, and never told
Yet you betray me and leave me alone instead
It's just a game you play to mess with my head.
I promised myself, I'll never do that again
To be with you is just another sin.
Why didn't I turn and walk away?
There was always that something i had to say.
I lay my forhead on your icy door
I remember everything and I'm chilled to the core.
The door swings open, welcoming me to come in
To be with you is just another sin
And it's all happening once again.
