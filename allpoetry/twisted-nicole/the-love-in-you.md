---
title: "The Love In You"
writer: "Twisted Nicole"
slug: "the-love-in-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/15859-The-Love-In-You-by-Twisted-Nicole"
---

The love she found, was the love in you
She never showed it, she thought you knew
She had a chance to say what she wanted to say
But time passed and she ignored that day
She never realized you had the feeling too
You should've told her, you thought she knew
Time went by and you both grew up
Never telling the other, just what was up
The days passed by and the feelings were there
You waited so long, she no longer cared
She should've said what she wanted back then
But you never realized the chance wouldn't come again.
You tell her now what you felt too,
Now the love she found was the love in you.
