"Shut the fuck up" you said...
It's playing in my head
You say you do care?
Show me you're aware,
Of all the things you did
And treat me like a kid,
...Oh,am I prude?
Well I think that you're rude...
You act like I never gave you anything
My feelings mean nothing...
It's pretty rough now
But you don't know how.
I can't get my feelings straight
Do you remember our first date?
At the movies...
I thought you had cooties...
It was so funny though
When in between the show
We would make out
And then giggle loud.
But do you remember those days at all?
Do you ever wonder if I'll call?
Well try to think a little harder for me
Maybe someday you'll see
That I'm letting you go
And I want you to know..
That even though I spilled my heart out, so you could see
It doesn't make me someone whose weak,
But I fell for you,
And I loved you too,
And now I'm falling hard,
And my heart you've scard...
But It's really okay,
I'll forgive you someday.This is just a quick write I did, :o)
