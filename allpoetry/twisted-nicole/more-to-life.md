title: "More To Life"

There's a mother
Works all damn day
Just to come home
And cook dinner for
Her unapreciative husband.
He doesn't care
Or realize that 
She's there.
She takes care of the
Baby
While he sits
On his ass and watches
TV.
And all she wants to do is cry
But she can't 
There is no time
And the next day
She has to wake up
And do it all over
Again.

There's a father 
Married to a woman 
Who thinks the world revolves
Around her blonde head.
And the worst thing
That could happen
Is her nail appointment
Gets cancelled
Or the color on
The box of dye
Isn't the same
As the color of her hair. 
He's a workaholic
He forgets about
His own daughter.
Too busy working
He doesn't realize
The wife may be
Cheating.

There is a woman
Who feels that
She's got it so bad.
Everything is so horrible
Except for 
Her grandchildren
Unless 
They screw up too.
She had her kids in her teens
She's generous
But has had a rough life.
She seems unhappy
I don't know why
But when I think about it
It makes me cry
Since there's nothing I can do.

There's me
Watching the people 
In my life
Sort out thier own hells
Make mistakes...
And all I want to do
Is remind them that there's more to life
Than living
They just don't realize 
The other one has it
equally as bad.
