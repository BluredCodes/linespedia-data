---
title: "To Be Wrong"
writer: "Twisted Nicole"

I miss you with these tears I crySlower and slower the time goes byMy heart aches with dying painIf I don’t talk to you soon I’ll go in saneWhat we’ve got now, its good but seems like not enoughNot having you in my life would be toughOur wishes and dreams may come trueBut all I want now is to be with youThis house seems so empty without you hereIt would be so great if you were even nearWords cant express my love for youIf you looked into my eyes it would shine throughI think about you all day and nightIf loving you this much is wrong, I don’t want to be right
