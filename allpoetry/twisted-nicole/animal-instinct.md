title: "Animal Instinct"
writer: "Twisted Nicole"

Push me with your words
Throw anger at me in heaps
But still I fly like a bird
And like a rabbit I leap.

Do you hate my pride?
Or do you just hate me?
Do you like the horse I ride?
Am I what you want to be?

I’ll ignore your snide remarks 
And let you say what you want
But I’ll turn into that shark
You wish you didn’t confront.

I’ll never run like that fox
From your words of hate
I’ll stand still like a rock,
Maybe your heart can relate?

I’m that bee buzzing in your ear
That will never go away
Until you listen up and hear,
Just what I have to say.

Because I’ll turn into the animal
That you never thought I was,
Here you thought I was just a girl
Without any cause.

But keep talking, 
Just say what you really think
You’ll become the prey I’m stalking,
I won’t give you the chance to blink.

So push me with your words
And say what you really think
You’ll get a taste of both worlds
And my animal instincts.
