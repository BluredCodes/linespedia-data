---
title: "Your Voodoo"
writer: "Twisted Nicole"

You play your voodoo on me again
I try to love you but I just can't win.
I'm under your spell...
Lately my life has been hell,
It's not like you give a damn about me anymore,
But everyday I just seem to want you more.
And I can't have you...
Because I know we're through,
But you keep sticking in the pins
When you play your voodoo again...
It hurts so bad...
Like your love I've never had...
With or without you,
The needles still go through,
Deep into my skin,
When you do your voodoo again.
Controlling my every move,
What is it you're trying to prove?
Hurting me so bad,
Because your love was all I had.
And it's gone now...
I can't make you love me, I don't know how.
Play your voodoo
Is that the best you can do?
Hurt me more and more...
And I don't know what you're doing it for...
Tell me what's going on, that pin was a close call
You aimed for my heart, I'm the abused voodoo doll
You play with me, and my head 
I never thought, you'd want me dead,
I'm just the one you mess with and hang on the wall,
I'm just the one you make fall
All of the time, in and out of love with you
I thought that it was all true.
But you do it all over again,
Playing your voodoo on me is a sin
But you don't give a damn anymore,
You know what the devil's got in-store...
You play your voodoo
But I still love you.
