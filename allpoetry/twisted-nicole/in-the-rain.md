title: "In The Rain"

In the rain
You see yourself
As a blur
Of the better man
You could be...

But don't doubt
Your own strength...
Didn't you find it
In me?

You gave up
The opportunity,
And blame your problems
On your father...
He want's what's best for you
Just like I wish you would see...

That I can help you through
It all
When the storm comes
And the rain blurs
Everything ahead.

I helped you see
The better man
Inside yourself, 
But instead...

You gave it all up
For nothing worth more
And even though I 
Want to help you
It just rains
More
