title: "Face"
writer: "Twisted Nicole"

I’m screaming for help from a mouth with no voice
We want to escape, yet have no choice
This world seems so bitter to my eyes which are blind
People around all hate it but they don’t seem to mind
My ears hurt from their screaming that I don’t  at all hear
The senses are good but they don’t smell my fear
I look around, naïve, thinking people are starting to care
But  then I soon realize I’m another face 
That’s not there.
