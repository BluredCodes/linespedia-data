---
title: "Charm"
writer: "Twisted Nicole"
slug: "charm"
source: "AllPoetry"

You think your charm can lure me
Maybe I don't want to stay
The love is there, it's plain to see
I just can't find the words to say

If you let go now, I'll be gone forever
You know I hate to fight
When I said now, you should've said never
Your charm can lure me, now i know you're right.
