---
title: "In and Out of Life"
writer: "Twisted Nicole"
slug: "in-and-out-of-life"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/40496-In-and-Out-of-Life-by-Twisted-Nicole"
---

Come on get up

Out of this hole you've dug for yourself

Don't tell me to shut up...
I'm the one who's cared out of everyone else.

Give me a break...

Tell me the truth,

For heaven's sake,
Will I ever know the real you?
You're in and out of life
In and out of life,
It's taking so much time
To figure you out.

I want to love you...
But that we'll never do...
I don't know how to..
When you're in and out of life.

So tell me what's wrong,
Let's just talk,
Do you remember our song?
What about that walk...

Just daydreamin' about the future together,
Then it all eroded with the weather.
Thinking about you all of the time...
Wishing that
You were still mine...

You've got me in and out of life,
In and out of life.

You pick me up
Then throw me down
Treat me rough
And make me frown...
Then you open my heart,
Let me be free
Let me out
And just be me.
 
I can't chase you
I can't let you go
I cannot win
But I want you to know

You've got me in and out of life.
In and out of life
Just like you're in and out of life
And in and out
Of mine
