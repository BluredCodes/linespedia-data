title: "How It Seems"

The smile is gone from my face,The sparkle's left my eye,A salty tear rolls down my cheek,As I begin to cry. I could go sit in the dark,Far away from sight,I wish that you could hear me now,So I could hold you tight. But you don't even know I'm here,I doubt you know I'm real,But you never stop to think,How other people feel. So now I'll hide inside my head,So I can sit and dream,And realize a funny thing:Nothing is how it seems.
