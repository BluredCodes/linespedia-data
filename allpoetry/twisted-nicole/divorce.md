I get myself wet in a puddle of pain
Wipe away my tears as i cry in shame
"It's not your fualt," they all say
"But why?" I scream, then look away
What I knew once as my father
Is more like a friend, whos call is sometimes a bother
I love them both with all my heart
But my family's broken, torn apart.
No one understnds the way i feel
Wishing it's a dream when i know it's real.
