They were invited to a party, Kate and Dan
They went to the party, hand in hand
The lights so bright, the music so loud
The went inside and fought the crowd.
Kate chatted with friends, she giggled and joked
Dan went with his buddies, and sneaked out to smoke
When Dan and Kate started to dance,
Dan felt light headed and in a trance
Kate noticed how weird Dan was being,
But Dan, he still had no sense of feeling.
They kept on dancing, the beat in their heads
Dan kept getting drowsy, wanting to go to bed.
One of Dan's buddies who had also gotten high,
Ran into the room with a gun in the sky
The group soon noticed, people started to scream
To Dan, the vision was still just a dream
Kate pulled Dan onto the floor to take cover,
A fire was shot,
The party was over.
