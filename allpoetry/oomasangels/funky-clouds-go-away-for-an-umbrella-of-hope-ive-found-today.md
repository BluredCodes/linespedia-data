Funky clouds above my head,
Funky clouds making me wish I were dead.

Funky clouds following me,
Over land and over sea.

Funky clouds why must you come,
Funky clouds why do you hide the sun.

Funky clouds full of dark and gloom,
Funky clouds outside my room.

Funky clouds shading me from the light,
Funky clouds your always night.

Pouring rain upon my soul so sad,
Making me feel lowly and bad.

OH  FUNKY CLOUDS LOOK WHAT I HAVE FOUND TODAY.
IT’S AN UMBRELLA OF LOVE AND HOPE SO GO AWAY!
