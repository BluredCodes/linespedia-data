I shall not sleep without you love.
For sleep just will not come.
For without the sound of your sweet breath.
My days of sleep are done.

I shall not sleep with out you love.
Not one minute shall I reap.
For without your loving arms about me,
I shall never sleep.

I shall not sleep without you love.
As weary as my eyes shall be.
Not one wink will I get,
My dreams shall not be free.

I shall not sleep without you love,
I cry all night through,
For trying to sleep without you love,
Is something I cannot do.

I shall not sleep without you love,
Not even in eternal rest,
Until beside you I lay once more,
And then truly I will be blessed.
