Angel sent to me.
By Stephanie Linderman


Today an angel spread her wings and soared down from the sky,
And  delivered unto me a precious gift, I could not believe my eyes.

Long brown hair and soft, soft skin and the sweetest little nose,
A perfect smile a perfect form ten perfect fingers and little toes.

God has smiled down upon me today from heaven up above,
And given to me this precious gift this daughter for me to love.

As I look down into her perfect face into her sparkling eyes of brown,
I pledged right then to God above that I would never let him down.

For trusting me with this precious gift, for allowing me to be her mother,
For giving to me a child like she, a child like no other.

I could see right then and there that this child was special and rare,
And to be with out this precious child was something I could not bear.

So I raised her with lots of love and did the best I could,
Maybe not my very best but she always understood.

And that’s another thing that makes her so precious to me…. When…..
She always loved me no matter what I could have or should have been.

Now the time has come for my precious baby girl,
To spread her own angel wings and fly into the world.

To test her wings with out me to soar high into the skies,
To feel her own wind beneath her wings, to cut the childhood ties.

And this is another precious gift to watch your daughter grow and soar,
Into a strong young woman who makes you proud, a woman whom you adore.

So I still thank God everyday for this beautiful gift of life from above,
For sending me, here on earth, one of his own angels to love.

And I also thank my beautiful daughter for all she has been to me,
