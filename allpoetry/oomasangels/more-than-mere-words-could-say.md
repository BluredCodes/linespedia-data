My darling  how long has it been since last I kissed your lips,  
and felt your warm and tender skin beneath my finger tips.

One day one hour one minute all would be to long to bare,
For just one second with out you close to me would cause my heart to tear.

How long has it been since last I said I love you more than life, 
And that someday I would be more than proud to be your loving wife.

That being with out you would be such a sin life would cease to be,
For just one second with out you my love I could never bare to see.

For with out you I am only half , it takes you to make me whole,
For you  my dear are my life my heart my breath my soul.

So I tell you now I touch you now with these words that seem not enough to say,
That I love you more than I thought I could even just yesterday .

And tomorrow will bring more love to my heart, for you give me more each day,
