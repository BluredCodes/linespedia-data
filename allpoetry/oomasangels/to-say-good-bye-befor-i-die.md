A few last lines to say goodbye, 
one last tear and one last sigh.

Before I go just to say ,
I love you all and wish I could stay.

I will  miss you but the pain shall be gone,
And  I'm sorry I’ve left you to carry on.

The pain I know you will carry with you,
But we must face it my time here is through.

I could not go on not one more day,
Hurting and fearing and crying this way.

My life I could not find meaning for,
To get up in the morning has become such a chore.

And calling my life living would be a greater sin,
Than taking my own life could have ever been.

Becoming more of a burden each day to you,
Is something  I am no longer willing to put you through.

And enduring this pain I can no longer take,
For it would be a bigger mistake,

To stay here on earth when there is a better place to go,
Where sadness  and pain I shall never have to know.

So I bid you all a fond fair well ,
And pray that for this I don’t go to HELL!
