I sit here thinking what I can do,
To help you with what you’re going through,
I care for you my dear good friend,
I want to put your pain to end,

You are one that we all greatly cherish,
Just know, if we hold on, the friendship will never perish,
‘Cos true friends never stop holding on,
The real friends are the ones always holding strong,

Jess, I just wanted to let you know,
That I’ll always be here, let it show,
That in my heart you have a place,
I’ll never abandon you, without a trace,

So let the sun come shining in,
Let your pain fly out from within,
Let it go, let it disappear,
Try to let go of your fear,

Let the hurt leave your soul,
And take a turn, take control,
Because all life isn’t so bad, I know it’s true,
