Dear Abbykins,
Hey chicka, I wrote you a poem, just wanted to let you know how much I love you! :) You've been a great friend and helped me through quite alot, I've done some things that haven't been nice in the past, and I'm sorry for those times, I was a bit of a bitch, wasn't I? *hangs head* But I love you and I know that cos you're a good friend you have forgiven me! 
Love always, Mellykins! xoxo



I’m glad to have a friend like you,
I hope you’re glad to have me too,
We’ve made our friendship true and bold,
So it’s worth much more than gold,

When I’m blue you make me smile,
It’s stretches over half a mile!
We’ve spent together joyous times,
Like a star, our friendship shines!

I’ve done things, that were mean and wrong,
And it made you sad, but not for long,
‘Cos in your heart, you forgave me,
And ‘cos of this, I love you chicky!

Deep deep down in my heart,
I know that we will never part,
For if you’re sad I’ll make you cheerful,
Even if you’re getting tearful!

And when it comes down to it all,
I’m proud for this, I’m proud to call,
You my friend- my bestest friend,
You and me will never end!

And listen all, to this poem,
Best friends are there, always luv ‘em,
So if you want your anger freed,
A friend in need is a friend indeed!!Hey everyone, :)
