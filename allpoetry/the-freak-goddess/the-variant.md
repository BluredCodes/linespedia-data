---
title: "The Variant"
writer: "the freak goddess"
slug: "the-variant"

You are killing me
with your roundabout way of answering.
Your devious ability to make everything 
sound like a maybe.
You are killing me
because you are a variant,
not yet a stable aspect in my life.
You are killing me 
because I cannot keep you..
and though I may visit,
I may not stay.
