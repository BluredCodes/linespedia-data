---
title: "Silent Goodbyes"
writer: "the freak goddess"
slug: "silent-goodbyes"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/4491-Silent-Goodbyes-by-the-freak-goddess"
---

No sound escapes.
No sobs to hear.
Sadness expressed in rolling tears.
No sound escapes.
No words to hear.
Part of me taken away..
Fear.
No sound escapes.
No wails. No cries.
Only the roars
of silent goodbyes...
