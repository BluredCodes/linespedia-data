My God watches over me.
I falter like a broken clock.
Tomorrow's seconds tick and trickle away.
Can I ask you to wear feathers and fly away from me?
This healing hand guides me awry,
I stumble like a tiny child.
Yesterday's years fall and falter away.
Can I ask you to wear flowers and bloom alone, away from me?
This idiocy spurs me on.
I revel, Yes, I guess I like the pain.
Today's sorrows...
Today, Today, Today..
Can I ask you to wear ribbons and twirl and twist away from me?
