---
title: "Butterfly Tattoo"
writer: "the freak goddess"
slug: "butterfly-tattoo"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/4913-Butterfly-Tattoo-by-the-freak-goddess"

Slowly you landed
inch by inch
making your way
back into my life, my heart.
I drifted away
in soft memories
of scratching pains
that will never 
let me forget you.
