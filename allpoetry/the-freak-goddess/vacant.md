---
title: "Vacant"
writer: "the freak goddess"
slug: "vacant"

I walk the halls
and answer questions
in quiet voices.
I comment 
and laugh in seriousness
contemplating trivial matters.
I'm moved to another dimension
while you laugh
through my vacant smile
and cry through my vacant eyes.
And I, being far removed
glare through the silence
of your thoughts
to decipher the hidden secrets
locked within your soul.
