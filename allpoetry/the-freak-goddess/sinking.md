---
title: "Sinking"
writer: "the freak goddess"
slug: "sinking"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/4496-Sinking-by-the-freak-goddess"
---

You were always so close by
I never had reason to miss you.
And now, I hear your voice only on crackling phone lines
when you call to give me "news".
Nothing looks the same now.
The pavement has a sticky gooey property to it
that causes me to sink 
deeper with each step
into our memories.
The sky has a vastness to it now.
It reaches across the distance
between us to form a cloudshape of you.
A thundercloud that vibrates with torrential rain.
This city is much too large.
The distance it puts between us too great.
And I think of you often,
when my mind roams
and I forget to live 
in the present.
