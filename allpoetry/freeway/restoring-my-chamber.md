---
title: "Restoring My Chamber"
writer: "Freeway"
slug: "restoring-my-chamber"
source: "AllPoetry"

My chamber alive
within my head

Mentally tarrying
too close to dread

Putting pen to paper
reveals my tale

Woe rises above
the place it dwelled

My chamber no longer
has a hole

Others align on paper
joining my soul

Lifting personal clouds
I shall grasp

Expunging all anger
once being cast

Above depression
hanging to survive

Ah...replenishing love's memories
I return alive
