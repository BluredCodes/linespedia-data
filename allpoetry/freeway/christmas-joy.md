COME LET US ADORE HIM 
'twas how the choir began 

Building into crescendoes 
with joyful noise across the land 

Though we hear of nations' wars 
and then more wars as told in rumors 

After standing to praise our God 
we shared Christmas cookies and thankful humor 

Ah the choir did sing with glee 
sending warmth high on snow-capped summits 

Up and over the pieces of music 
melodies danced gaily without limits 

Our congregation eased into peace 
while hearing each word of SILENT NIGHT 

As we began searching beyond the sadness 
of September 11th and its fright 

We listened to the melody. . . 
O CHRISTMAS TREE, O CHRISTMAS TREE 

And then we heard, O HOLY NIGHT 
with faithful words, FALL ON YOUR KNEES 

Oh how our church's choir did capture 
Christmas's meaning during the hour 

Though terror did strike our nation 
we were reminded of Christ's great power 

WONDERFUL COUNSELLOR we did hear 
throughout the Hallelujah chorus 

Thrilling us with God's magnificent love... 
on anyone else we could not focus 

Exiting church doors with newfound faith 
we met outside, hugged, and then we hurled 

Lighting candles in wintry weather 
singing with peace and joy...Joy To The World.
