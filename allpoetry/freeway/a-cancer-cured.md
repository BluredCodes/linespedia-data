title: "A Cancer Cured"
writer: "Freeway"

From birth to grave
I see God gave

My closest friend
life without end

Sweet-voiced through life
though terrified

A faithful friend
who never lied

Her eyes of love
gingerly allayed

Peace on earth
she oft displayed

Staying alive
without cast of anger

Life's end...a smile
lifted from cancer

My friend's hand dropped
then God caressed

'Twas God's way 
of healing both her breasts
