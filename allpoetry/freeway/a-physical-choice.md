title: "A Physical Choice"

Alas! 'Tis here 
Alas! Celebrate 

Tarry not 
reform from wait 

Retrieve the tune 
of heat once given 

Brimming into
heavy breathing 

Unite with allspice 
from each berry 

Cast out pain 
labored to carry 

Acuminate and create 
one red point 

Fevered sensations 
combine one joint 

Hot and spicy 
tender too 

No sweeter furnace 
relished by two 

Couple enmeshed 
without force 

Clinging to 
s physical choice
