title: "If I Cry"
writer: "Freeway"

If I cry 
will you delete my tears 
will you boot up your P.C. 
reminding me in e-mail not to fear? 

If I cry 
will you restore my descending faith 
by creating a greeting card 
on your computer while I wait? 

If I cry 
will you be my cyber buddy 
and then crash my pain and heartache 
by forwarding e-mail funnies? 

If I cry 
will you grant me indication 
that God's angels fly above 
by sending a link of inspiration? 

If I cry 
will you visit my chat room 
and elevate my spirit 
as though you're feeding me with a spoon? 

If I cry 
from pain that's real and not a hoax 
will you mark on your calendar 
reminders to forward me jokes? 

If I cry 
will you take a break from using excel 
deciding to rise to the occasion 
and send me a spiritual tale? 

If I cry 
will you store my e-mail address 
using it to encourage me 
to meet life's challenges at my best? 

If YOU cry 
not knowing why or having a clue 
drop me a note so I can e-mail 
jokes, angels and love to YOU.
