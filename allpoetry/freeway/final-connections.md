---
title: "Final Connections"
writer: "Freeway"

Who would have ever thought 
cellphones would mean so much 
while delivering communication...
final words with a loving touch? 

Who would have ever thought 
love calls would grant peace of mind 
to those who soon would perish 
after terrorists forced them in line? 

Who would have ever thought 
cellphones would offer last words 
from America's courageous family... 
those slashed, overthrown, and then hurled? 

Who would have ever thought 
cellphones in air would unite with land 
bringing us closer to our loved ones
before death knocked and grabbed each hand? 

Who would have ever thought 
we'd one day savor this small device 
that sent to us hugs and love
prior to our Commander in Chief's advice?
