We've heard the recent tale,
women are from Venus, men from Mars.
Women tend to get emotional, 
so men grow closer to their cars.
Won't you join me in changing a bit?
Our logic must be just around the bend.
Maybe men will work on emotions.
Wouldn't that bring on a grin?
A start with hard hats will do,
we'll get tough and start construction.
Since we've decided to work on logic,
we'll need to forget cosmetic suctions.
We'll go to the site of hardcore logic
with those hats shielding our tender hearts.
You can plan on calling me Johnny, 
and I'll simply refer to you as Bart.
What do you think now that we tried it?
We heard them hammer and cuss together.
As for me, I'm discarding my hard hat. 
My head is craving a hat with soft feathers.
