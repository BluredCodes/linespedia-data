title: "A Two-Part Day"

How joy surpasseth gloom
'Tis night that never looms
'Tis morn with a new breath
Never lurking threat

Sweet wonder doesn't stray
during night though at bay
'Tis morn filled with light
guiding without fright

Morn and eventide do lark
never to dissembark
each survives discord
letting go of sword

Awake and rise with morn
beginnings have no thorns
Sleep well throughout the night
brimming soul's harmony into flight.
