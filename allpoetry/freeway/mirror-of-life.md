---
title: "MIRROR OF LIFE"
writer: "Freeway"
slug: "mirror-of-life"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Though her life 
is ebbing painfully away,
she'll not allow joint pain
to ruin her every day.

At the moment of relief 
she attends to love for friends
holding stress at bay
rarely welcoming it again.

Now beyond Biblical years
of three score and ten,
she gives thanks to God--at thirty
he guided her from gin.
