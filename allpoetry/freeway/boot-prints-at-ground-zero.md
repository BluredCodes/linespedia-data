As many Americans are aware, Canada sent boots to help our canines work through the ruins at Ground Zero...a gift that will never be forgotten. Thank you, Canada. 

---- 

Canada, have we said thanks 
for the doggie boots you entered 
into America for our canines 
to search loss at the World Trade Center? 

Canada, have we told you 
how much your country means to us 
making it possible for our dogs
to work Ground Zero with less fuss? 

Each one barked with relief
when you sent their little boots 
for difficult days that follow
in clearing the horrific loot. 

Canada, have we reported 
our dogs claim you a best friend 
as you keep their four feet softer 
while working on Twin Towers' land? 

Canada, have we mentioned 
that America loves you very much, 
along with each canine in our country
oh how they praise your loving touch
