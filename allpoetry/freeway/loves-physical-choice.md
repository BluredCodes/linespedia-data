title: "Love's Physical Choice"

Alas! 'Tis here
to celebrate

Tarry not
reform from wait

Entice the tune
of heat once given

Transforming
into heavy breathing

Unite with allspice
from each berry

Cast out pain
labored to carry

Acuminate and create
one red point

Love's tapestries
combine one joint

Hot and spicy
tender too

No sweeter furnace
relished by two

Couple enmeshed
without force

Clinging to
love's physical choice
