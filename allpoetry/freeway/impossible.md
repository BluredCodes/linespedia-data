title: "Impossible"
writer: "Freeway"

She was overjoyed 
to find herself
no longer presupposed as a phoney 

She stripped away 
all false fronts 
that filled her former life with baloney 

She grew tired 
of mirrored images 
all of others with none of her own 

What a wonder 
when she discerned 
to reveal nonfiction down to the bone 

She made a decision 
to be herself 
without pretending to be another 

Each new day 
shined with glee
transforming deception to truth...incredible

Today she rests 
sharing with others...
being someone else is IMPOSSIBLE.
