At the ballroom dance, 
we drank punch, and danced the waltz.
I slipped away to powder my nose 
and I saw it all
I saw you meet and greet another woman
I saw you kiss her hand and bow your head,
I saw you, I saw her, I saw it all
you thought I left to powder my nose
 but I saw it all
I saw it all from behind the punch bowl.
