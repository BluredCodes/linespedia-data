You,like paralell lineswe will never meet, I know who you are, do you know who I am?I lost my self in a hopeless dream only to be dreamt and never to be felt.this stranger I love is caught up somewhere elsewith someone else this stranger could be no strangerthis stranger could be someone I know know very well but not as well as I would like.I'm pretty sure this makes no sense
