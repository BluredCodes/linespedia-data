Bees those asses are everywhere
I run from them and I run,
 into more of their kind.
i run from the garbage man
 and into his garbage truck. 
I run from the lunch lady into 
and into the cafeteria.
Why I run form these things 
I dont know 
maybe cuz im Paranoid PAranoid
