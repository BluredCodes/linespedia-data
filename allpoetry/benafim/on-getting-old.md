Getting Old



I know more dead people now
than live ones, they visit me in
dreams and never get older.
Like my five year old son who,
clutching a Donald Duck plastic
toy, asked me if he was going 
up to Jesus when he died. 


Desperately, holding on to 
a fading hope, I said: “Yes my boy
but not just yet”…While he was 
serene I tried my best not to cry.
“If you’re in heaven, dear boy, 
can’t you tell me now?” He calmly 
smiles and vanishes.
