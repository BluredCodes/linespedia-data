The Ex.


Nearly sixty she is, but still
beautiful and ambitious, newly
been promoted to private secretary
at the finance director’s office.


Invited her for lunch, we went to 
a place where she didn’t risk 
meeting colleagues after all she
had her position to think of.


The lunch was, for her, a success,
she spoke endlessly about herself,
her work and plans for the future 
of which I was no longer a part.


I listened, as usual, nodding my head,
from time to time, while dreamily 
thinking how nice it would be to have 
her back in my bed again.


Escorted her a part of the way to 
her office, we kissed briefly and I 
watched her disappear out of my life, 
still beautiful and ambitious.
