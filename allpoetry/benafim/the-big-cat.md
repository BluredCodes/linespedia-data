The Big Cat 


On the wall, in a hunting lodge,
hangs a moth eaten tiger pelt.
The once fierce animal, shot by
a red faced colonel… and now?
A dismally, forgotten trophy.

There are no tigers in Bengali 
except for a mad one in a zoo
wears a helmet to stop it 
committing suicide by banging 
its head against iron bars.

Tigers are not social animals, but 
each one knows where the others 
are and that’s good for a tiger to 
know There are no tigers in Bengali 
the last one hangs on a wall.
