Haiku (clichés)I pity shy bluebells written about so oftennow a sad cliché-----------------------------Dandelions toohave become gloomy victimsof lyrical abuse.------------------------------Water lilies pink,in the gardens little pond are blushingly trite.-------------------------------Cheerless orchids try,unsuccessfully to hide… when a poet’s passing by.-------------------------------
