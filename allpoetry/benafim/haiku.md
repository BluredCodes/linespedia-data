Haiku


A visit by spring.
Got the timing rather wrong.
It’s snowing now.


Red rose on a wall.
Spray painted by a hooligan. 
Beautiful sight.


Seven nights ago
a full moon dripped silver
on birch trees.


After heavy rain,
the beck laughs heartily.
Rainbow trout spawn.
