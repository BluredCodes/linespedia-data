Jealousy When I loved her days were brightI adored her voice and sunny smile.Then the madness of her jealousy cast and evil shadow; hectoring, her voice and false her laughter Slowly the light of love dimmed and my heart was filled with anger and contempt, I fled into my own silence shut the doorbehind me and ignored her pleas.Back up to my mountain I went where nature whisper words oflove when nights are long and I miss her voice and mirth butnot the lunacy of her jealousy.
