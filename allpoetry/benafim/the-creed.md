The Creed



On a dump
we found a
painting of
Jesus.
Wanted to put
him in a gilded
frame,
but we didn't have 
any money.
Nailed him to
the wall instead
and since
the unknown 
artist had
made him
slightly cross
eyed
Jesus could see
us no matter
where we sat.
