A Few ThoughtsWe only take a comedianserious when we laugh.Dust is us …Atomized.So think beforeYou mopBone rattling stupiditydestroying an infrastructure,in Afghanistan, that doesn’t exist.A superpower at war against one man.The blowback will be awfulwiping Israel off the map.
