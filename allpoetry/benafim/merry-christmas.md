Merry Christmas



On this holly night I miss
the children I never had.
Between me and the silencer
nothing to remember.
Never shall a son fondly say:
“As my dad always said…”
Childless men walk in parks
looking as children’s play, 
a cape of loneliness they wear 
across shoulders that never 
carried a happy child as their 
own fathers once carried them.
Childless man, be damned!
