The Cherry Tree


Sonia, when walking her dog,
fell down a narrow burrow, 
slowly slid till she reached
its base, covered in dark soil.

Her little dog barked for her to 
come back up thinking it was 
a new game, when she didn’t and 
darkness hovered above the plain
her pet walked home unable to 
tell anyone what had befallen. 

Out of Sonia’s heart a shoot grew,
seeing light up there it quickly 
rose and when it reached surface 
Sonia was a pretty cherry tree 
that bloomed all year. Alas, like 
the dog she was unable to tell 
anyone
