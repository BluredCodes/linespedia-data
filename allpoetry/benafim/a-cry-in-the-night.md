The Cry In The night 



Awoke, the child in the broom 
cupboard cried…neglected for 
years now it could not longer be
ignored…Unnamed, an abortion
left behind in Japan, blood on
a white sheet. Heartbroken its
mother had expected a healthy
love child…and the ship sailed
without her. There, there my 
mysterious child cry no more, 
I know that you miss your mother,
so do I. The ship sailed, as ships
must, and never returned. I have 
an image of you in my heart and 
the sun rises over Yokohama every
day just for you my unlived child.
