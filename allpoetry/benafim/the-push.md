The Push


Hadn’t seen her for awhile,
she didn’t return my calls.
Then she rang and said, meet
me at the new hotel at five.
I was glad to hear her voice
so melodious and refined.
she wasn’t alone an elegant
man, tanned and in tailor 
made suit, sat by her side.
Charmingly they executed 
me and meekly I protested
when the man insisted on 
paying the bill….Dazed 
watched them leave, a gent
he is opened the door for her.
