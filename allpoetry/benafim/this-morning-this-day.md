This Day...This Morning



   Morning broke shattered night
into fragments of shadows.
I opened shutters wide and 
life cascaded in, filled rooms
with gladness and left no
corners dark….This is your
new day, the first do not be
gloomy even if it rains during
the day” The wheel of seasons 
hummed, leaving deep furrows 
of time on my brow.
