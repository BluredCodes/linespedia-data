An Algarvian Moment




The olive grove tickled a low 
hanging sky till it cried and 
rendered enough for a lone sun
ray to light up a glade that looked 
like a newly shampooed carpet. 
Walked across it only so I could 
be the undisputed first and to see 
my own footsteps. My witnesses 
were a flock of sheep that reeked 
of damp wool and an elderly dog 
that would rather have stayed at
home snoozing in the barn. 
The day breathed calmly as time 
commenced
