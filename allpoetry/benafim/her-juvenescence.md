Her Juvenescence. 


Spring nipples
And sweet
Belly button
Pert nose of
Youth.
Sun and sand
Golden dawn
Shimmers in her
Eyes.
Melodious she
Snores,
The sound of 
The well 
Contented .
She is May,
Winter in my veins.
Days ain't getting
Longer.
