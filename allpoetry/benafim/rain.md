Haiku (Rain)


Large snowflakes 
gently fall on wet asphalt
winter meets spring 


Heavy clouds part
delayed rain on bushes 
transient pearls.


Dog in doorway 
watches rain drip from roof 
Is it counting them?


The postman arrives 
wet hair and a damp bag 
the water bill is due
