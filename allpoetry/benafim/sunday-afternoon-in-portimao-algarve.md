Sunday in Portimao. 
(Algarve)


An empty can
of lager,
in a sudden gust
of wind,
fell off a wall
and rolled 
along the pavement.
Came to rest 
in the gutter
along side 
a red lighter
thrown away
when no longer
useful.
From under 
a car, a cat 
watched me 
watching 
the can.
The wistful
Sunday afternoon
was full of 
silence.
