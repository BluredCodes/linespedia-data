Grandmother’s Funeral.


You died at 
an inopportune 
time, streets of 
coloured light and
artificial snow
on bright 
shop windows.
Hastily interred,
Christmas next
day, barely 
time 
decorate 
the plastic tree.
One thing
though, real
snow fell
on your coffin.
