When the winner Loses 



Here is a blanket and a parcel
of food I’m so very sorry for 
the damaged my bombs have 
caused and I grief for the loss
of your children who sleep 
forever under building debris 
How kind the beast is before it
enslaves and steals your wealth.

Globalisation, is implement 
The strong uses to exploit our 
world it creates hate, poverty and 
fear. This powerful force that 
tramples on other cultures and tries
to ram down our throats the junk
philosophy of the victorious,
calling it freedom of choice.

The beast’s war cry is democracy,
its right to exploit the lesser.
leaving the defeated with a cyst 
of hate that will grow, explode
and poison our planet for 
a thousand year…and the dying 
beast will ask: Why did you hate 
me so much?
