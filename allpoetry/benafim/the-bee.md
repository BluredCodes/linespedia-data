The Bee (how the East was won)


The honey be gives us sweetness
it has got a poisonous sting in its 
tail that hurts but can also be
used as medicine.


City dwellers think that honey is
made at a bon-bon factory and if
they see a bee they flee or try to
swat it with a newspaper.


The bee is a great pollinator helps
farmers grow an abundance of food 
which we eat, when it’s fancily
wrapped in super markets freezers 


Yet we try to kill it with pesticide
and only spare those we have 
colonized to ensure that we have
an all year sweet supply of nectar
