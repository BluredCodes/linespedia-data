---
title: "Erase time"
writer: "krybabie"
slug: "erase-time"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/22269-Erase-time-by-krybabie"
---

everything reminds me of you
from people laughing to my dreams at night.
a vision of you makes my life so bright

but that's all over now
no more hurt no more lies
tears will go away 
and not come out until another day

i was so stupid to put my heart on the line
i wanted so much for you to be mine
now i'll i want is to turn back time
start all over anew 
so you wouldn't know how i felt for you

erase the memories that we've shared
like our first kiss and the night we danced
i should have known this wouldn't turn out in any romance

he's playing games people would say
i was told that almost everyday
i thought you were true,
but i guess i just didn't now the real you.
