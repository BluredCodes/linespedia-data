title: "i want you to be with me"

if only you knew how i longed for you to be with me
for us to be couple
for us to be as you were with her, no wait more.
i sometimes wonder if that will ever be, 
me wanting you, you wanting me
or will we be just friends?
is that where this story will end?
i relize there might be things holding you down, 
keeping you away, things making you frown
but please see past them and move on to me, 
i'm still waiting for you to show what emotion if any you hold for me.
please make your move and make it soon 
brighten up my life and take away the gloom!
