---
title: "Don't Play"
writer: "krybabie"
slug: "dont-play"
source: "AllPoetry"

Tell me why you act the way you do?
Why is there two sides of you?
One side spits your game so tight
The other makes me wanna cry late at night.
When we are together, you act so real 
But what you show me isn't how you really feel.
Stop playing games 
Be true to me, 
If it's not my heart you want, 
Then please just let me be.







this poem is for a friend.
