I LOVE EVERY THING YOU DO
I LOVE EVERYTHING YOU SAY
I LOVE HOW YOU SMILE IN THAT SPECIAL LITTLE WAY
I LOVE TO HEAR YOUR VOICE
I LOVE TO FEEL YOUR TOUCH
I JUST WANT YOU TO KNOW I LOVE YOU VERY MUCH!02-14-01,
