(made this up just now)
Day by Day BY Ice`Rose

within each passing moment, 
my heart beats far true.
you are the sun that lightens,
always seein bad days through.
you are the full inspiration, 
that keeps me to hold a glow.
a heart intertwining mine,
as it will always show.
you gave me a reason, 
to smile the entire day.
your love for me lasting, 
longer then anything may.
your joy I am for living, 
just to love me day by day.
I promise to do the exact,
thing in the same way.just a little one I thought up! hope you like it!
