My father is a drug addict and beats me and my brother...
My little sister is the queen my father beats my mother,
I've tried suicide a million times as much as I can say,
I hate my life more and more every single day,
I'm trying to explain why I want to leave,
I know I have some problems mostly with me,
I smoke a couple packs of cigs,
My father hangs around with pigs,
I live my life in awful pain,
That's slowly driving me insane,
I cannot hide my feelings of why I wish to die,
When I'm sitting on my bed all I do is cry,
Some think that I"m so perfect for my brusies are excused,
Yesterday I feel on a bag of shoes,
My friends jsut shrug and walk away they can't see behind my tears,
I'm slowly loosing my mind that's inflicted with fears.I may always act so cherry I'm not the story hasn't ended.....
