That day we broke up,
The threw it in your face,
I ran away from love,
Keeping with my pace,

That night I sat up in my room,
Wondering why you lied,
I sat there without talking,
Alone I sat and cried,

At schol I was silent,
It was a merical,
I couldn't eat that day,
My hurt was completly full,

You know the little story,
About the house of glass,
Here I sit in studyhall,
HOping time to pass,

The next day I tear up everything,
That you gave to me,
And I cry to myself,
Knowing you don't see,

I bet your out with you-know-how,
Dancing at the Shack,
Knowing that you like her,
When it's all such tact.

No girl will ever love you,
Like I know I did,
Please just listen to these words,
My heart no londer lives,

Please just tell me,
why you lied,
Because I know,
I've cried and cried.

Please tell me,
Please right now,
Why you had to lie to me,
Please Why and how.
