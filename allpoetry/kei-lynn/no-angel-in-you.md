---
title: "No angel in you"
writer: "Kei lynn"
slug: "no-angel-in-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

If a angel has wings,
Can she fly back to heaven,
If an Angel spreads such loss,
Why cannot she go to hell?
If the angel's like you,
Fallin' from the sky,
Why must you hide behind yourself,
Or am I not to ask why,
Are the Angel's fair,
Do they tell the truth,
If you think of me as a child,
How come there's no angel in you?
