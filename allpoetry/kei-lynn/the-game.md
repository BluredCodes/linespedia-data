I have the ball,
I driblle downt he court,
We only have 2 minuets,
It's not my kinda sort,

Of thing to make a shot,
in this old game,
I shoot the ball,
and hold now shame,

I made the shot!
I'm finally free,
And noone made the shot,
But me!

The stands stood up,
And cheered so loud,
I saw my mother,
Standing proud,

Now maybe I'm not,
The perfect girl,
I know I live in my own,
World,

But At that moment,
IN my mind,
I finally knew,
I wall doing just fine,

I had a smile on my face,
and a tear in my eye,
I finally felt proud,
and I didn't want to die,

I was happy at last,
I was so darn glad,
That finally,
I made my self,
Feel alittle glad.My shot! It was a 3 pointer!
