I know he shall return,
I whisper to myself,
I know he will come Back again,
In perfectly great health,

Momma says to stop dreaming,
And meet her in town,
So I feel I must live on,
My prince isn't come now...

I sigh walking threw the town,
As I see his shinning face,
He lowers himself from his horse,
And I am in his embrace....

My friends cannot stop staring,
At my knight so true,
I felt like sticking out my touge,
I told you it wasn't veiw...

He carried me away that day,
And Here I live inside,
A pefect house,
With lovely kids,
I am his humble wife...
