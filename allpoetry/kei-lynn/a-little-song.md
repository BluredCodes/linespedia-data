title: "A little song"

Once more you yell at me,
For something I did wrong,
In my head I'm singing,
A painful deadly song:

Crying in my own/
lifeless lulliby/
Dieing to myself/
Here I have to die/

Sheltered from the rain/
You poor upon me/
Everything you know/
Just why can't you see/

I'm diffrent/
From what you think/
Not sorry/
For what I bring.
