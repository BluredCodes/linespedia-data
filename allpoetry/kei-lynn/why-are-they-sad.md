title: "Why are they sad?"
writer: "Kei lynn"

why does everyone,
else have to cry?
Is what my life,
stands for a lie?

Am I just stupid,
To hurt and pain,
just this year,
I've felt so invain....

I hear of some people,
Who kill themselfs,
Why do they do it?
When there's so much wealth,

Why do they hurt Themselfs,
Just like others,
hoping to find a life,
with eachother,

Can't you wake up?
And see it's wodnerful,
Or is it me who's,
Strickly unpure...
