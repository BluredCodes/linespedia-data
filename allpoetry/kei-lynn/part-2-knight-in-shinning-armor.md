My father says I'm dreaming,
My wildest woman dreams,
My mother says it's romantic,
And father's being mean,

But I know I saw my prince,
Ride up on his mighty stead,
I know I heard those words,
Will you marry me,

They tell me I'm a child,
That I'm foolishly blind,
But I know he's riding out there,
Know he's all mine,

I'm writting this outside,
Looking at the stars,
Knowing that he's out there,
Know the love is ours,

I know he's out there somewhere,
Where no one else can see,
I know that in his head,
He know he loves me,

I'm putting down my pen now,
It's time to go to sleep,
Please dear lord,
Bring him back to me.
