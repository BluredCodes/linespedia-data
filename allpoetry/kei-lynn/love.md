The sun it sets around us,
We're in eachothers arms,
The angels press upon us,
And shelter us with charms,

In a word without cover,
And shelter fromt he storm,
It's good to be here,
In your love it's warm,

You're kisses,
are so sugar sweet,
You're whispered words,
Are all I need,

YOu're soft sweet touch,
Across my hip,
As your tounge,
Hit's my lip,

You push me,
up against the wall,
You shelter,
me I will not fall,

You're kiss me sweet,
And passionate,
With my heart,
The candles lit,

This love is sweet,
and will not last,
It is a love,
that's in our past.
