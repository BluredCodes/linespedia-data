Maybe it's you I love,
Or maybe it's your heart,
Maybe it's my imagination,
That has made us take a part,

Maybe I just love you,
Because you're mine forever,
I will be with you always,
And I shall leave you never.

I remember the night we ment,
You were happy and upset,
Your girfriend wasn't there,
quite yet,

SHe wanted to make you jealous,
So you kissed me instead,
I got these stupid thoughts,
Way up in my head,

They next day you apoligized,
But oh may I ask for what,
You slowly pasted me by again,
As you slaped my butt,

That I guess is how it began,
And how we came to be,
And I will love you always,
Because you will love me.
