This is just a poem my aunt and I made together a long time ago. It was a homework assignment in elementary school. we had to make a short poem. I just remembered it and thought it was cute.

Wild bright 
Such a delightful sight 
To see the flowers bloom
If I might 
See tonight
A flower as pretty as the moon.
