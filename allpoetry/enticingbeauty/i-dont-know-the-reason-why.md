title: "I Don't Know The Reason Why"

You cant see me
You cant hear me
You cant even feel the love I have for you
So why do I keep on loving you?
Is it your hair?
Is it your eyes?
Is it your lips?
Please tell me why
Is it the way you laugh?
Is it the way you cry?
Is it the way you make me feel inside?
I dont quite know the reason why the love I have for you still lasts inside
All I know is I love you
Maybe you dont know I exist but I know you do.
