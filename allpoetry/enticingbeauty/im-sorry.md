---
title: "I'm Sorry"
writer: "EnticingBeauty"
slug: "im-sorry"

I thought that I was over you
I thought that we were through
But for some reason
No matter what season
I always seem to think of you
You said "I love you"
And I know it was true
But that was then 
And this is now
Could it be that we're just pals?
But it cant be, I want more
I am sick of all those guys who are just trying to score
Our break up was mostly my fault
I admit, I made our relationship come to a hault
Now as I sit here in thought
I've realized, we never fought
You were always good to me
And were as truthful as can be
You were so fine
And you were all mine
You were so sweet 
You couldnt be beat
You were the best
Not like all the rest
And then one day someone tried
To make it seem as if you lied
I asked you about it
You said it wasnt true
But I didnt believe you
I made a mistake
And now my heart aches
I'm sorry, I didnt mean to make such a big deal
Out of something that wasnt even real.
