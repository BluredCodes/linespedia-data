Perfect is it
Perfect is the key
Perfect is what they want you to be
Perfect isnt at all what you are
All you want to do is play your guitar
Your friends all love you so why should 
you care
About some stupid people who just care 
about what you wear
Being perfect isnt what its all about 
Being you is what makes you count.
