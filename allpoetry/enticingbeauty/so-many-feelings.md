So many feelings
Rushing to my head
So many feelings
I seem to never want to get out of bed
So many feelings
That make me want to cry
So many feelings
I keep locked inside
It seems to me
That no one understands 
Why the beating of my heart and the drumming of my hands
Never stops or slows
Which just goes to show
I have way too many feelings
Rushing to my head.
