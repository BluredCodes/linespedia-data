title: "Please Tell Me!"
writer: "EnticingBeauty"

What have I done?
What did I do?
All I've ever wanted is to be with you
To be there when you laugh
To be there when you cry
Do you have any idea how you make me feel inside
I have a feeling I did something wrong
Because suddenly our conversations don't seem that long
I have always loved you
And I know you love me too
All I wish to do is be near you
Please tell me what is the matter
Because all I'm getting is needless chatter
I didn't mean to hurt you if that's what I did
Would you stop treating me like some little kid!
I am beginning to feel you don't want me here
Or for that matter nowhere near
I want to be with you cant you see
It isn't fair the way you are treating me
Please clue me in
On what I have done
I want to fix it
And have it back the way it was
Can you please tell me what I have done?
Fine I give up you won!
