---
title: "Just Go"
writer: "EnticingBeauty"
slug: "just-go"
source: "AllPoetry"

I'm scared of you
How you act how you seem
Just leave me alone and let me be
Please go away 
Your hurting me inside
I think I no longer like this ride
I beg you, I beg you
Please leave me here
Alone and quiet 
Just the way I like it
I'm scared of you 
I really am
Can't you see I don't give a damn?
It's over don't you know?
Now please, please, just go.
