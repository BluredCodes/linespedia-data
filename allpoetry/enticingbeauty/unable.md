---
title: "Unable"
writer: "EnticingBeauty"

They are unable to be together.
Like two same sided magnets that can't stick together. 
Too many problems in the way.
Too many people in the way.
It is all so complicated.
All they want is a simple thing 
But someone else already gave her the ring. 
She wants to be with him but how can she be?
Because one just turned one and the others almost three.
It's not really them more like their maker.
He got in the way of the other trying to take her.
She is grateful to him for what they made
But she now wants him to fade.
Would she be able to get out?
And even if she could, would he leave his girl and be faithful like he should?
Because you see he has his own little one too.
And he doesn't know what to do.
Should they leave their relationships and try to be together.
Or should they just go behind backs whenever
She wonders all this as she sits here today 
Thinking, wishing, hoping that they'd someday find a way.
