Suck me in...
to your vaccuum of zero space.

Blackened hearts never start
on the coldest of days.

Raise my hands, lower esteem
shattered dreams that flash between your pictures.

I once knew a girl
who grew into another enemy

Stop complaining...
you've gained by now...
these confessions...
have drained me out.

Been through hell
for a chance to taste your taste.
My given all
bounces down this old hall.

Am I preaching to myself?
No companion...

Except an echo off this aging floor.New song I just wrote for the band.
