Surrender metal fantasia
Lost among the minds of losing
Craziest to the sheet metal worker
Attracted to his pale blue light

Fight the madness
Bottle-fed Jack Daniels
Teething and tossing
Car-struck at crossing

Vapors inhaled
Trees impaled
Smiling to an underneath

Liquid life...

Location...

A lunch box.

We all know what you're drinking.
