Withered upon dried
Stepping on my heart has become daily
as it sinks to my feet

Crying for the sake of pity
Shivering in bed
Lusting for one sensation
in an empty shell of a relationship

Attached to bark
Clinging on your motionless emotion
I squeeze the light from the firefly
and allow my finger to glow like E.T.

I only wish to extend my neck as such...

To peer over this pressure, above the crowd

and follow your shifting, seductive, pelvic movements...

large enough for child birth
yet, small enough to fade away...

into, I'll never know.
