(Verse1)
Push it down, way down low
Simple stares to your blinding glow
have left me helpless
a mind of mindless thoughts
we ought to know

Let the rain drown my soul
fill my lungs with pain that showed
can't keep my hand from shaking
the Earth from quaking
a love from making me whole

you know, you know...

(Chorus)
What you take away
has left me so afraid
a change I could'nt be

let me go, let me go...

(Verse 2)

Whats this?
So pissed, among all your shit
try to plead a case
can't stand that face
lingerie and lace
again I fall for it

thats low, so low...

(Chorus)

(Breakdown)

Cover lies with trust
Drape this love with lust
Closed fists don't touch
They strike blows
You better go...

Just go..
