Traces of rough skin down smooth necks
beating within def
hearing your silence
watching you silently

miles of childish laughs
kissing a dream
passion appeared against pale hands
fluttering in speed
trying to spell my love as fast as possible

Whats in a state?

This state of mind is all we ever needed.
Laws passed by simple bliss
Constitutions laying down perfection
Finding a true peace...

amongst your grace...

observing eyes of wide pupils.

Allowing all of us a hope of finding true love...

Allowing all of us to smile a little wider.For my great friend Brian(Dark-Solstice) and his infatuation Jen(Jenneddin).  Best to both of you.

Carl IV
