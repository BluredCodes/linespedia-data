title: "Fade like a Sun: Screening for now on."

Light me on fire like a full moon
Call me with dial tones
Stick me with new states.

Throw away beauty in a receptacle
of careful oxygen.

Watch me in sleep, closed eyes to available friends.
Believable daylight fading into a window reflection

Graduation has allowed me to cry in death
Ring your voice like my phone

Tears fall like running faucets
I'd drown in your soul If it never left you
Scared to swim
Living for presence, to die in your absence.

During sunrise my heart would beat...

During sunset it skipped.

Deflating like a nail punched tire.
Jaw-dropped, I'll never answer a call the same.
