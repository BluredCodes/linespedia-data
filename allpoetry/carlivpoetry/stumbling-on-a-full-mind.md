Untie a brain
knot of intelligence
we're sewing a future
crushing deviant behavior

small belt lashes
trained to drink the blood of youth
passing the future on the right hand side
shoulders length from oak and lust

fatal circles on wet asphalt
four sets of yellow lines
3.0 and rising
laugh in this faded hollow

A haze of light
dizzy to your ways
sliding down the side of the hill

Jack counts losses.

Taking Jill away in crashes.
