Fever boiling to stir
in a brace of God hangs the cylinder of below
Spelling blocks have failed
Now I'm three seconds away from alien

Outerspace has brought me tranquility
Stars sing like Macy Gray
Minus the animal stuck in her throat
Plus the figurines lodged in my eyes

My fingers have become partially oblivious
only touching sky-filled thighs
theft of sexual feelings
vitamins equal D, all of the above
now my scores have failed

Stuck between a foot and a knee
as I grin for a shin
sin wears thin
In a holy dream

Heavens have cried for me
Jesus has died for me
and my cares barely exist...

due to this ridiculous myth?
or am I mistaken?

Creeping behind a shallow wall of athiesim...
