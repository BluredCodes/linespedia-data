Descending.

Dropping in on high-rise thoughts
Killing off your mindless lock
Changeless pockets fled the flock
Counting the sand that made the rock

Faithful prayers able to fuck
Apostles words way less in the luck
Clovers' lacking the limbs of push
Chemicals kill our burning bush

Teeth that chatter
backwards ladder
I'm on the floor...

Stories above your orgasm.
