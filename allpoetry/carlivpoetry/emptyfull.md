Reaping upon dirty sin
falling from the sky
colors dry in eyes
Shakespeare is blind.

Busy hands in unobtainable territory
rising above
drowning below
Lost in Romeo.

Never round me, never trap me.

Wonder in my universe...

Have the pleasure of my pain...

Trapped on a map with no compass.

Deaf to a scream, locking myself to hope.

You pray on the beast I'm vegetarian to
Pick the meat from her bone

and leave my pain to the chest...........
leave me hollow.
