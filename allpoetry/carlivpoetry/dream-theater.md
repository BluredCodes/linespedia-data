(Requested by Brian(Dark Solstice), please take the time to read his work.)

I dreamt a dream
floating on end
Suddenly falling
for you my friend

Can you taste the flower?
drag across your nose
excruciating delight
soft thorns of this rose

Four pedals bound together
by the beating of our hearts
but when you passed away
our rose fell apart

So now I only see you
when my eyelids fold down
like small movie screens
without surround sound

and for everytime I feel your presence
the taste of salt grows sweeter
and always we shall have our love
in my dream theater.
