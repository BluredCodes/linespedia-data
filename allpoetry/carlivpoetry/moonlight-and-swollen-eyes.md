title: "Moonlight and Swollen Eyes"

Across the water
Reflections cracked preciously on liquid
Bloodshot and overflowing with saline
Full moons on your half-split smile

Along this beach
Sparkling full harsh on sand
Crystal clear and blow dried by delicate ocean breezes
Quarter moon against two glistening lips

On top a sand dune
torn to pieces by the wind whipped grass
pulled closed to a squint
Half-moon pierces through your fingertips

Fifty years should pass on
and the fires down these white sands will extinguish
tides will rise to wash away the childs architecture
thousands of memories will become here

just like my recollections of you...

Forever etched in my mind, my heart, and in your swollen eyes.
