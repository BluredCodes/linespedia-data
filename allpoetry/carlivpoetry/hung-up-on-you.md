Set you a fire
in flames of desire
pushing the worlds buttons

In shock to pull
an abandoned fool
from glitz to glory in nothing

Wastes a taste of triple sec
mispelled is three-way sex

Dropping dimes in quarter ponds
engaged in a blouse unblessed

Nylon rope and lack of hope
has left me not around

Dangling from a popcorn ceiling
chairs length to the ground

With your beauty I find myself all choked up.I've gotta rhyme sometimes, lol, enjoy :)

Carl IV
