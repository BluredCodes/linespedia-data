Good guessing
Drop clues on an upheld heart
detection to the smell of passion
dropping drips of holy saliva upon your breast

Like a dog in heat I pant
to the temptations of sex or temperature?
a hole in my sock, a hole in your head
thick fingers slip inside my chest

disbelief in these split ribs
forever doubting your intensity
the true rank of bliss
the false standing of love

wishing to fall...
she aches for none of us.

Leaving this tall man leaning...

Against a short poll.
