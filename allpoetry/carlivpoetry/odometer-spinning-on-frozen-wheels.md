Hair line fractures spider across like veins 
grasping to one last spoken word of love 
looking inside 
as my heart of glass shatters 

Walking across me she takes her time 
making sure to twist an ankle, stretching my delicate skin 
ensuring me with subtle kisses 
lashing out erotic circumstance with sloppy tounges 

In my eyes brilliance. 
As a circle of friends squares off with pity. 

I indulge in my sacrifice, giving away maturity to faintly 
taste what we once believed in 

A love that was mechanically sound. 

Now left broken down and motionless on the side of an empty road.
