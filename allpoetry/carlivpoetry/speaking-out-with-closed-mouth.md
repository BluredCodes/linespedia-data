Vibration of female vocals
rattling like the numb of conscience
one country, rich in a minds poverty

Do you feel the compression?
Do you lack in comparison?

We call ourselves literate...
yet, cant read a poker face from a gifted soul

Radical movement spinning like a bicycle wheel with no chain.

Bare feet in a circle of spokes.

A naked Earth...

     Finding shame in our exposure...
Loosing rotation, gaining thousands of years in backtrack.
