One-hundred watts blared by glass
shadows among finger patterns across an underdeveloped
facing pain, turning fear
glance into the blind, open and close

Shutter the shatter of a teenage bladder
sexual acts too lewd or just too deep, causing infection
summer docks, seagulls, birds and outdoor fucking
fresh cut lawns, fertilized with semen

poolside and disguisted
swollen and bleeding
dripping and swallowing

in dire need of a towel...

one to clean up and dry.

In fantasy she smiles...

Picturing the one that wiped away her sins.
