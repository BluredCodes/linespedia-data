title: "A Girl"
writer: "CarlIVPoetry"

Three men from virginity
scorned for then on
textures of skin
transforming like her beaten soul
Falling hard for the wrong colored eyes

Six men towards insanity
recollection is still found but hidden
praying that one day you'll forget
a quarter hour of love

Seventeen men of blurry faces
that glass wall you neglected to shatter in a younger state
crumbled upon the floor, lodged in your feet

All of the life you inhaled away, bloody noses
and all the life you gave away, thigh bruises

One man across the bar never touched you...

he remembered your name.

He cherished your heart.
