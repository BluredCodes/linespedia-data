(Verse)
You go out once again
and as the night comes to end
you're feeling so alone
and decide to take one home

Drunk and disorderly
of course you'd pick me
take advantage of my mind
just for your good time

Now I wake up unclothed
wondering where did you go
why the hell would you leave?
oh no, no no no

(pre-chorus)
What a waste of my count
What a blow to every value I hold

(Chorus)
I'm growing old
the blood runs cold
and women get easy

Dont want you to stay
Dont want you to leave
I need real love
Now who could believe
That I'm just some player
I'll never be a star
cause one-nighter songs
dont get you far

(Pre Chorus)

(Chorus2)
I'm growing old
the blood runs cold
these women are sleazy

Dont want you to stay
I'd like you to leave
In need of some love
thats the truth just believe
never gonna be a playa
and pray to be a star
with luck this dumb song
will take me far.
