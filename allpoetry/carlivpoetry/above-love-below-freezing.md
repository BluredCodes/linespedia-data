title: "Above love, Below freezing."

Series of wisdom scorched by stardom
burn my soul and inhale the ash
trouble washed away like filth on workers hands

Sharp intricate bristles
to walk upon over spoken breath
tripping on each tongue movement

Suck me sideways
ears drawn to a close
I awake in erectile activity

Placing my feelings on ice
sliding across shallow thoughts
dull blades have brought me to fail.

another day of frozen lakes...

another love of frozen hearts...

another kiss I dared to miss under thirty-two degrees.
