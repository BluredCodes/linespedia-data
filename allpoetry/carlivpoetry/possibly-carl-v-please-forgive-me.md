Flashing neon
Closer to all the vain I could kiss
Losing the endless battle.

Morning, tear-eyed to his smile
Milk from her breast, milk in his cereal
Still to never touch your face
My imagination has created your life.

What have I done?

Termination to the soul that shed from mine.
Gods' given talents that I allowed to lose the show.

I pray you smile in Heaven.
Dreaming you grow old...

Hoping you understand my mistakes, even though I cannot.

Collecting the tears I shed...

Maybe to see your reflection, God, I'm so sorry.
