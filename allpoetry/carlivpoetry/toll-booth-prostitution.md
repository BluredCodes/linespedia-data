Sucked in, sly, shy, outspoken
Closed out mind with pay toll tokens
Cash in dreams so easypass
15 Miles per hour too fast

Red to green my dues are paid
Skip the yellow and flash the slave
That keeps inside her Highway home
Collecting coins all misthrown

Wide awake...

I slapped the whore.
