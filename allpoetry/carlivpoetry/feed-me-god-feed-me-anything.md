Like a shock through a finger
tasting the tingle with a hint of alkaline
swinging on empty monkey bars
in time a childs innocence whispers goodbye

a life of lazy
drifting along stratus
misunderstood guidelines to a horizon
waving to a lord with closed eyes
catching winks, dropping lies, empty prayers

steal the bread from my mouth
devoured by the yeast
like a million choking of thirst
Catholics grow suicidal

heats arrival delayed to inclimate hunger
small scars across bloating stomachs

these never matters are absence to a mind...

lacking integrity...

another operational human below four feet screams hello to Heaven.
