title: "Divorce to a Science."

A matter of mind in a question of density
Gallons of love in a two liter bottle
drowning an alloy in selenium
wedding rings scale away in a dry winters' day

Seperated from my soul and sugar coated blood loss
Closed eyes never shielding a mid days sun
hands in a V in an attempt to prove my life...

to a holy lacking a preferred membership

In ticking we dock the tock of a clock on shores to stop...

then seconds to go we know the blows of a family to split...

leaving these grieving children believing we never caused our losses...

now alone we've thrown away the known structure of mother and father.
