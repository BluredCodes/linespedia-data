More entranced in meantime
Simple stares to a complex galaxy
jaw-dropped in shirtless fetish
kiss me in hellish romance

to lick a fingertip
then to touch upon an emotionless face
drawing a sudden response of rosed, rush cheeks
lifting off in passion, crashing in question

You are my final embrace...

Pearl mouthed and flat chested...

I drink from your stream lined breasts, choking on unbelievable.

Free formed and natural to my own 4 limbs.

Dismember my originality...
                          and stitch me with the commonwealth.
