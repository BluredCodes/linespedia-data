A legacy too absurd to adoreShort stares at freedom have rendered me stale and stringylonging for the taste of female flesh I close my eyes and chew upon myselfWishing to hold my desire in contemptto own the materials to keep my hands above the waistchastity bid farewell too may lovers agoan ego forever wounded by todays never-mindsMy addiction far succeeds any drugpulsing screams to enter youthrashing you down madly falling outside, rushing to place back inFantasy was once reality.With closed fist I make love to you again.
