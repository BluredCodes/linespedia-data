Shivering under a wet blanket of pity
In need of truth inside an overwhelming false
skidding on an empty road
traveling side to side, fearing a ride of glide

Wonder in a blender, mixing the fix
alcohol on a rise, quarter-full stomach of sick
Bitchs' legs in drag
Forty-year old breasts in sag

Text messages and ringing for sex
Each manhoods subtle size to the next
quivering thoughts lonely to being
just one whores' money touches greedy in need

making mistakes, taking the clues

turning heads...

fact of the matter, I've done wrong...

So wrong.
