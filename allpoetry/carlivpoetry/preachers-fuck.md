Retreat into a solice of solitude
Never ending laugh
Slippery hands on a tacky underneath

You've lost your glow
draping the dark cloth over your glow worm
sleeping with trouble
awake in perfection

Scratching a Cayman Islands half-moon against a shallow
grown beard.

Panty hose pulled to a tight nose.

We rob your purity during oblivious...

Give to my sexual needs....

and rape you with a Christian mind.

(Praise me below the belt.)
(Never to know me above.)
