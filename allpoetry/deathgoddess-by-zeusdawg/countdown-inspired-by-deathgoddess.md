My addiction is returning to me
Soon I will do all the things that I like
I gave it up for Lent, this deed
My body certainly put up a fight
I loved pushing all the right buttons
Doing this deed I gave up for lent
My fingers were moving so quickly
Soon my hands were crooked and bent
But I'm good at this task set before me
Nine days to go I'll jump back in my shame
On Easter I'll insert my cartridge
And keep playing my video games!!!I got the idea for this from deathgoddess's uncanny ability to describe normal everyday routines in a rated R+ manner.
