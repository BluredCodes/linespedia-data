You like her
Young
Perky
Naive
turn your crank
no need to wait
I was her last week
Fix it up
Do not stare
she is not wearing underwear
Lick your lips
run your fingers through her hair
Dirty thoughts
trash hard
she will cum anywhere
I am done
I have finished my share
Lick my lips
run my fingers through your hair
Dirty thoughts
trash hard
I feel like hell
Beware
I am done
I have finished her share
