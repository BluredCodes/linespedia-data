I saw my shadow for the first time.
I sat composed.
Selfless
I confess
Rise from the hole that covers the sadness.
Questionable virtues.
I'm not the only one.
Swiftly I stand.
The shadow plummits.
I should not have shown disbelief.
Involvement
Life
Mystery
The wine Ive drank turns my body opague.
It's only a memory.
Not a boulder with chains at the bottem of the sea.
Not who I am.
Melodramatic as always.
Forget
Forgive
