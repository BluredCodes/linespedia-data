Warning some might be bothered by the content.Really not a poem or well written.  Just me thinking out loud.

I thought I'd have this body forever.
I'd wear a short skirt , low cut top and my point became valuable.
Well to men anyway.
I look at it now as tits of steel.
Nipples who invite without words.
I was so mistaken to fixate on my blessing in disguise.
Time happens, and the illusion that beauty is who we are, becomes well unimportant.
You realise that you are a person.
A human being with thoughts and words even opinions.
I know its hard to believe when you are young but growing older is a blessing.
Men hear me now, respect and value me now.
I am capable. 
I dont spread my legs to get a point across.
I'm no authority but the point was never heard anyway.
