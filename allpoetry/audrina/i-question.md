---
title: "[I question]"
writer: "audrina"
slug: "i-question"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I question
bring forth
ponder
all the above
continuously
my actions


I negotiate
hide
procrastinate
all the above
continuously
my feelings


I judge
opinionate
isolate
all the above
continuously
my neighbor


I deny 
I anger
reflect
continuously
my love
