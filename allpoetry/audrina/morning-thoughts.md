I have become transfixed on escape, forever. 
Slowly a disguise masks my heart. 
Porcelin face, blameful eyes......disgrace. 
Not my intent to struggle, but sweet pain drives my appetite. 
A morning flower leads my dreams, a rising sun suprises my eyes, and tragedy shines. 
Achievment admist the struggle.
Paradise leading the desires. 
I lay weeping...listening. 
Each tear is sadness....each emotion is a lesson...the daffodil of my dreams prays for confidence. 
The petals scream.....hear me dont compare me!!!
