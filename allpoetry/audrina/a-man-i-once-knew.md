A man walks alone.
In the beginning fearful. 
Each road travelled forgetting the sadness.
Endurance led by insecurity.  
Threatened by society he tried to control the world.
Thosands mocked.
Confused by his own power he removes his cloak of vulnerbility.
The man experiences laughter, sadness and love.
The pursuit of spirit begins.
Subtle harmony.
Joy grasps the man and showers him in humanity.
It was then thousands followed.
