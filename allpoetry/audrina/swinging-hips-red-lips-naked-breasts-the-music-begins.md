Swinging hips,

red lips,

naked breasts,

the music begins, 

body sways,

thighs quiver,

womanhood exposed tauntingly,

the mirror weeps. 

each curve,

young, 

firm, 

racing thoughts,

insecurities,

even alone,

the dance continues, 

fingers roam,

lapsing,

brighter days,

a duo of emotions, 

exhausted,

the music fades
