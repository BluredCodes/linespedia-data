title: "thoughts I dont say"
writer: "audrina"

Painted nails, luscious lips, fur draping the hips.
Women of class.
Personalities so sheen.
Hungry thoughts becoming obscene.
I have studied and contemplated each gentle move.
Caressed his thigh, even stroked his bone.
Silence reins.
I felt and complained.
Now I am bewildered trying to stay sane.
The ongoing struggle lay still.
The heavens have left me unfullfilled.
I am content.
I am restles in flow.
I am just not sure I am raring to go.
