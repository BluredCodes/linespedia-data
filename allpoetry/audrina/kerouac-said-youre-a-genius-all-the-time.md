Kerouac said " You're a Genius all the time"
I look at the image before me, at a loss for words.
Aware of my surroundings, constant reminder that I am a genius. I just dont appy myself. Thats okay, maybe I enjoy lacking in self discipline.  Possibly the decision to lax (according to todays fast paced culture) is genius in itself.
Kerouac said "Remove literary, grammatical and syntactical inhibition" Well this for me is not challenging.
Kerouac said " Write what you want bottemless from the bottem of the mind"
Losing inhibitions, judging others for their dysfuctional thoughts, never.  I agree.  We are all fearful of our inadaquacy.  Look at who you are in print. Answer your own questions.
Kerouac said " Submissive to everything, open, listening"
To learn of others and look at their lives for what it is, valuing their experiences with an open ear. You will learn and become more aware of your own destination.
Kerouac said "Write in recollection and amazement for yourself"  If you don't who will?
Kerouac said " Be in love with yr life"
Kerouac said " Write for the world to read and see yr exact pictures of it"  Need he say more.
