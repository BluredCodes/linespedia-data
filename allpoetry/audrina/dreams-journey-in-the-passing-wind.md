Dreams journey in the passing wind.
Background flows,
a wish,
just one.
The moist lips touch,
the response a conscience whisper.
Pure words,
unspoken,
protecting the guilty.
Joyous expressions contained.
A fingertip brushes,
the electricity heightens.
The simplicity calms.
Three spoken words.
Forever love.97,
