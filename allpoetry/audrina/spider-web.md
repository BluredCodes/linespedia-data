A spider weaves a web of perfection.
Each pattern percise,
Intertwining with great effort.
The work he must endure,
The wind blowing  exhasperating this tiny creature.
He thrives off the challenge.
The morning dew glistens as he lay resting.
The web finnally complete.
The meal awaits.
