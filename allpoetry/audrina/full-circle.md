---
title: "Full Circle"
writer: "audrina"
slug: "full-circle"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

The haze covers our eyes.
Pain in the minds of the children.
Never to see the reality.
Candy coated smiles hurting the unknown.
Years pass by in seconds,
as we all lose time.
Forgetting prescious memories.
To see the dragon is to live.
Beware of the fire.
Running to escape the inevitable.
Irony strikes again.
Death is the beginning,
not the end.
Open your eyes.
Listen to the wind blowing secrets through towns of hatred,
resentment never ending.
An arrow is pierced through the deers heart,
never did he run.
THINK,
IS IT YOUR TIME?
Learn to embrace the dragons breath.
Befriend the unknown,
and you will truly remember!!!!
