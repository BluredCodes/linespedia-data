I sit lusting a stranger.

He brings me moments of rest, 

suprise

creativity.  

My body hungers the mystery.

Imagination wanders.

Masculine build rehearsing the ritual of desire.

You climb my spine, 

the chill allures me.

I find myself making love to your disapearing eyes.

You are an abstract painting yet the connection explains.

I thrive studying the lines,

 sexuality leaps and I embrace the page.
