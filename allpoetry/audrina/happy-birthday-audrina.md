It is your first birthday.
You have learned so much.
Physical milestones, emotional bliss.
The joy is turning on the radio and watching you dance.
Carefree.
Your little feet shuffle to the beat.
Rocking in our favourite chair.
Your heartbeat slow and calm.
You have grown.
I cherish you.
I long to whisper, "Hold on to your innocence."
Instead we dance with the fairies.
We sing in praise of nature.
We laugh and fall to the ground.
Little one you are precious and today is your day.
I love you.
Happy Birthday
