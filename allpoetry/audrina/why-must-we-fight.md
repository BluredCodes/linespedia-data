---
title: "why must we fight"
writer: "audrina"
slug: "why-must-we-fight"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/43191-why-must-we-fight-by-audrina"
---

I salute your wingtips, objectively.
My disapointment follows.
I appreciate your challenges.
The problem lies with my preconceived expectations.
My ideal of you takes over.
We lose each other and the fond memories we've shared.
As the cycle of words begin I see no tenderness in your sheer glare.
Understand my frustration.
Have patience.
I am grateful.
I salute your wingtips, objectively.
