Make believe, my dreams pressured within, tears held in a glass jar painted
red! Feelings hidden, desires escape in the night. Beneath the stars I lay
praying. The fairy tale unobtainable. You smile, I wink! I dance provocatively
alone! You see my hips sway within souless eyes, or are you hiding? No
longer shall I wait, learning lessons of pain. Understanding love, or lack of.
You know the one, fate taps your shoulder whispering bliss. My body feels
numb...I wait to awake. SEARCHING SOULMATES!!!98,
