To my lordly love:

Fate has brought me your burning eyes.  The depth of your gaze inspires my wearied spirit.  I am blessed with tenderness, your everlasting loyalty.  Tragically we must endure many obstacles, but our determination will end in two lonely souls uniting as one.  My first tear of love will bless your flesh in days to come.  I feel alive!!!!!!  Not even the stars could deny our bond.  I am yours as the clouds to the skies, or seaweed to a tide.  Although our direction is unknown, the wind will carry our love for eternity.99,
