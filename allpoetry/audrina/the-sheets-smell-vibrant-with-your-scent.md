The sheets smell vibrant with your scent. 
I lay naked  beneath you. 
Drifting in the garden of your aroma.
You touch my flesh and my heart takes flight. 
Your  eyes burn within my thighs, your soul has released my
 inner child.
The radio plays softly in the background with sentiments of love, your hands caress my face, each finger shares your inner soul. 
These moments release my fear ,and I fall......99,
