On the rocks!
A saddened face.
Cold glares.
Longing stares. 
The beginning ,a ritual.
Inadaquacy a haunting reality.
Quickly the glass empties, replacing the last. 
A smile invades.
Personalities complain. 
Conflict of the mindful soul. 
Lose the thoughts, boost the top! 
An ego appears. 
Self esteem, positivty. 
Refill the resource please. 
Have another, bring on the lovers. 
Its all a blur, the day appears, frightened disaproving stares.2000,
