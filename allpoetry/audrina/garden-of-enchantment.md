Tonight I lay beneath the stars, listening to the wind in the trees, imagining peace and harmony within my soul.  
Soft footsteps invade my retreat in the woods and I lay silent .  The footsteps are making me uneasy for I am susceptible to the spells of evil.  Quickly they fade and I am relieved.  My attention shifts to the sounds of the soul.  My heart beats with great intensity as my emotions manifest themselves.  My delicate enclosed heart leaps within this passive surrounding and for a brief moment my garden of enchantment is revealed.99,
