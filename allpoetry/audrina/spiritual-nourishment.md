Gaining more understanding.
Spiritual nourishment.
Sustain the magic of nature.
Without resources our souls wither.
Streams and vibrant moss are our nutrients.
Moutains build gratitude.
Negate your own negativity.
Live in knowledge.
Live to be nurturing.
Discover your gift.
The power of love being honour and respect.
The hunger of the spirit should not be ignored.
Devotion to spiritual growth.
