I have experienced a clarity within myself as my world offers knowledge long awaited.
I love and rejoice to this new found happiness.
Calmness surrounds my new beginning.
Flowers spread their petals for all to see,
awaiting expansion and change.
Years of confused thought drifted me down many roads of sadness.
Eyes closed all along to the helping hands on my way.
I thank the positive,
for it's their smiles that brighten rooms of gloom.
Their touch that could cure the sadest child,
and their soothing voices that connect friendships for lifetimes.
Dreams....we all have them are fullfilled with action and prayer.
Prayer to our soul...asking for the drive and the stamina to suceed.
IF ONLY.........
If only you tell the secret loud and clear,
I AM GOOD ENOUGH!!!!!!
then at that moment...who I am.....who you are....creates mysterious potential.


Whether it be.......
DARING OR SCARED
GODDESS OR CUTE
GREEDY OR THANKFUL
Fly with your new found freedom,
singing words of joy.
Others will flock to you,
they will surround you with positivity...forgiveness......and humanity,
for you are unique,
as you have been all along.96,
