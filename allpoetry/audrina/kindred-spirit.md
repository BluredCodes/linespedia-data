Disenchanting reality spinning a web of shadows, hovering.
Light unobtainable. 
Smiles from afar. 
Soft breeze cover me, release my thoughts to the world. 
Circle my followers with my discontentment so they too cling to hopes and desires. 
The world lacks magic. 
Naive dreams have become prayers of the night. 
Musical delight.
Vibrations and visions prey on everyday life. 
Secret lover, whisper words I long to hear. 
Mean your embrace, feel my tears. 
Soulmate. 
Kindred spirit, desire my heart as much as my touch, feel my fear.??????,
