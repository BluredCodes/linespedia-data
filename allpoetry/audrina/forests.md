Explaining this effortless culture drowns the spirits of childhood.
Sacred forests are clear-cut ,
dragons are seen as absurd.
Society is at a loss of soul,
disaproving of  paradisical beliefs.
The flesh is an illusion!!!
Pride ravages the imagination.
Each day heros are forgotten as cloudy productivity inhabits the naive.
Dance in the deepest woods and the fairies will adore your dedication.
Hold a butterfly gently in your hands and all daily limitations will be forgotten.
Extravagant forests are a necesity to live mysteriously.
Participate in their sacredness and you will be rewarded.99,
