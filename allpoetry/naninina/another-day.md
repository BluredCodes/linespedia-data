---
title: "Another Day"
writer: "naninina"
slug: "another-day"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/39734-Another-Day-by-naninina"
---

we were
hungering
for laughter
and
sensation.

our worlds 
hadn't 
yet
met.

but another day soon began.

your passionate
lips
and
mine
pressing
against each other's.

your warm 
hands
and
mine
exploring
our
heated bodies.

your wondering
mind
and
mine,
flying 
high 
in the sky.

but another day soon began.

i changed my mind fast.

your
calls, 
unreturned.

your
affection, 
ignored.

your
love, 
rejected.

and another day soon began.

you never
fully
recovered.

i looked up.
i saw the blue sky.

guilty
sometimes
i felt.

another day soon began.

one
kiss 
you  wouldn't
give 
me. 

another day soon began.

i
deserved coldness.
