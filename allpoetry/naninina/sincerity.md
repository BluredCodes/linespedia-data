Every morning at work
Everybody greets: 

* How are you today? * 

Run into an old friend
She says:

* Oh my gosh, how have you been? *

And two seconds later:

* Nice seeing you again, 
I gotta run *

On Fridays they say:

* Have a nice weekend * 

On Mondays:

* How was your weekend? * 

And at the end of the year:

* Happy New Year! 
(How was your New Year's eve?) *

* Happy Holidays! 
(How was your break?) * 

...

* Happy Valentine's day! 
(Did you do anything romantic?) *

* Happy Turkey! 
(How was your Thanksgiving?) *

* Happy Halloween! 
(Did you wear a costume?) *


Give 
me 
a 
break

Do you really
MEAN
it
when you
SAY
it?

(And if your answer is 
YES,
I would say
* Have a good day! * )Can anyone think of a better title?
