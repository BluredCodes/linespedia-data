---
title: "I am"
writer: "naninina"

I am for getting along

I am for forgetting and forgiving

I am for letting people go first 

I am for letting the elders sit down when the bus is full

I am for shaking people's hands with my two hands

I am for smiling to strangers

I am for bowing to the talented and great people

I am for serving the needy ones

I am for pleasing the unsatisfied others

I am for giving not receiving

I am for sharing my plate

I am for peace

I am for love**
I am for thanking you who are reading this
**
