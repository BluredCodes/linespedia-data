he doesn't like flowers
"they die", he says
i'm thinking,
"as long as your love doesn't"

san francisco is a romantic city
we wander around chinatown, 
looking for a dim sum place
we sit in the dark and watch a band 
we have a drink and fall in love all 
over again 

the way he put my hand 
inside his sweater's pocket, 
only to find a jewel box, with 
a pretty cat-eye blue bracelet 
inside it 

oh it's almost midnight now
and he hasn't called 

i haven't seen him for days
wonder why i haven't missed himThis one is a bit mellow. But you know how I feel, don't you?
