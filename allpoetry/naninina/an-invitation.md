Words exist so truth can be spoken and written
You are a beautiful thing
People lie, they lie all the time
I tell lies, but not to you

A new world, I have created for you
Come, won't you see me now?
Why, no need to be be frightened 

Darkness exists to hide wicked deeds 
Darkness exists to cover unforgivable sin
In the new world, only daylight 
In the new world, we're naked 

The new place exists to make you feel better
Forever shining sun, growing flowers, fresh smelling trees
The new world is uncorruptedTo all the good people. You know who you are.
The new world *does* exist. It is reserved only for you. Smile.
