Come 
out 
of 
that
steamy 
bathroom


This 
delicate
skin
is 
begging
oh 
begging
and
begging
to
be 
touched
by
your
fine
skin


This
heart
between
my 
breasts
is 
pounding
oh 
pounding
and 
waiting
for 
sensation


These 
lips,
ah these 
lips
are
longing, 
oh 
longing
and
craving
for
a 
kiss,
an
endless
kiss


Don't
...
make
me 
wait
forever


Let 
me 
dance, 
let
me 
dance
before
your 
eyes
and
show 
you
how
sexy
I 
can
