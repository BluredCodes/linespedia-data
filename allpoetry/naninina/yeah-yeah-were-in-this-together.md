Yeah I'm feelin' all right today
Yeah Yeah I feel like smiling a lot this very minute
Look over there,
look here, human beings all over the place
Let me come to you, greet you with this smile
Must this life be so difficult?
Would you allow me to get you out of this darkness, 
this place gets so dark you forget the sun exists
Like water in the ocean, 
we are together, be it a calm or stormy weather
Why should we care where we're standing
There's always someone who's luckier than you, than me
There's always someone who's not as fortunate as we are
Why should we care what we're seeing
We're in this together
Yeah yeah I'm feelin' all right tonight 
Would it make you feel better if I keep you company 
Yeah I'm going to pick up fresh leaves that fall from a tree 
And smell them, smell them, smell them
I smell humans, oh yes I smell you 
Where are you going
Like this ground that we're standing on
We are oneThis one just sort of came out. 

I'd also like to dedicate this piece to my new friend, 'Dragonlady'. *smiles*
