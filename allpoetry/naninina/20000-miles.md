Eighteen years,
of growing up 
in my motherland
Four years,
of being separated 
from it

I am about 20,000 miles 
a w a y 
from home
but 
I need to be...

t h e r e 

No one 
Nothing
Can wipe 
this tear off unless I am 

h o m e

***

Pick me up, I am drowning
I need to feel whole againI'm not sure if 20,000 miles is the accurate distance from here to home. 

I'm just really homesick now.
