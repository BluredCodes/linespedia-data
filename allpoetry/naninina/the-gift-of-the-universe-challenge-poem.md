1
 
(Morning) 


The sight 

is full 

of 

scattering 
sandals 
and colorful towels 

Sand, 
water, 
and humans 
unite

Through tinted glasses, 
the women 
and men 
witness 
and enjoy 
the gift of the universe 

Through the eyes of 
innocence, 
the lovely children 
run helter-skelter 
and scream 
uncontrollably, 
but 
happily, 
at such 
an open space 


2

(Right Before Sunset)

Two
humans left 

A man 
and
a woman

Touching 
skins
Pressing 
lips
Feeling 
love

Walking 
side by side 
Holding 
hands 
Watching 
sunset

Whispering
Laughing
(Almost unheard) 


3

(Night) 

No single being
Just nature

Quiet
just the sound of the flowing water
just the sound of the surrounding 
animals and trees 
just the sound of the wind 

Dark 
just the light of the moon 

Peaceful
Just
Nature
and 
the Ocean

The Ocean 
is the gift of the Universe.Submission to Challenge held by webdreamer
