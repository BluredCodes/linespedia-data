Today she finally comes to town.
I wonder what 
I did yesterday to deserve her visit.
I pull my hair back, 
and feel her on my backbone.
Blackbirds are approaching me 
shyly but fearlessly, 
They know I'm in a friendly mood. 


My thoughts are dancing 
with the unhasty waving leaves.
On days like today 
I know heaven exists
and God is smiling at me. 
The cold air and the wind 
had created a storm in this heart. 
Today she has renewed my heart, 
it is now again bright and open.
I sit on the grass, 
my eyes look far on the horizon, 
full of hope.The weather is very nice today in San Francisco. :)
