Would I become who I have become
if you and I weren't meant to be?
That is a question I'll never know the answer

Would I be the person that I am
if I didn't come out of your womb? 
This is a question you and I will never find out

Would I be the friend, the lover and the daughter 
that I am now
if I weren't blessed?

The questions don't matter anymore
I am grateful 
for who I amSome have abused and treated me wrong. But I have forgiven them.
