title: "Never Thought I Could Miss Something So Much"

People speak 
but I don't speak what they speak anymore

In my homeland, 
people eat
but I don't eat what they eat anymore

The sun starts rising, there 
when  
the sun has gone down, here 

They go to bed
           at the time...
                when I get up 

Home is very far

far

away
