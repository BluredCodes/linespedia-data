title: "when i stare at nothing"
writer: "naninina"

your face that used to 
steal my sight 
is here when i 
stare at nothing 
i wish i were
where you are but
where are you?
whose hands do 
your hands hold?

i saw tear 
on your face 
then i touched
your head gently
it's that 
sensitivity i loved
about you 
strong in the outside,
soft and complicated 
in the inside
mysterious and
humorous 
at the same time

days have gone by
the world's gone crazy
i am not  
who i was 
anymore 
but 
here i am
my heavy mind 
sailing along 
these thoughts
of you











and i still 
don't know
why i never
tasted your 
lipsFor Olala

Just someone I really fell in love with for the first time in high school.
