Oh how I believed that 
the pretty flower
in my sunken garden would never die
 

How I nourished this 
delicate plant 
and watched it grow 


I relied on such
a precious thing that bloomed
in every season


I cried so miserably 
when this lovely flower of mine
looked pale and withered 


Oh how much I was so very
fearful to lose this very blossom, 
so I always tried to mend her


My oh my, how I entrusted 
my heart, my body, and my soul
to keep her alive


How much, oh how much
faith I had in her, oh it was
just too much 


I feel, oh I feel
so guilty now, my little flower
has fallen apart 


How it weighed me down to
the deepest melancholy, 
when I realized 
my love for you had vanishedI used to think that my love for this person would never die. 

Originally titled: "Vanishing Love"
