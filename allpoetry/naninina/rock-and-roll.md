The night hadn't yet arrived
We couldn't wait till it's dark
I can't remember any romance
Candles were not lit
Soft tunes were not played
It wasn't planned 
that you and me would become
one

Under the blanket 
we commited sexual acts
You and me, 
with uncontrollable desires 
Gentle tounges hungering for naked skins
Eyes closed, minds shut
Only groaning and moaning

Under the blanket
we stopped breathing
and merged as one flesh
Hearts beating faster
and faster
We rock 
and we roll 
We were playing it rough
For the blaze of lust had driven us to 
a new world,
a world 
full of 
man's obliviousness

It really wasn't planned 
that I would get
preg*nant

***

Now the night hasn't yet come
But I don't think
I'd like to go on"...A world full of man's obliviousness..."
Note that the word "man" refers to both men AND women.
