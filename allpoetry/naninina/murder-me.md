Murder Me
3/7/02

++

Come here baby

Undress me 
s l o w l y 

Massage my back
s e n s u a l l y 

Squeeze these breasts 
g e n t l y 

Lick and eat me
d e l i c i o u s l y 

Put it inside 
p e a c e f u l l y 

++

Lock me up  Throw me down to the ground  Tear these clothes without patience 
Cover my mouth with poison  Violate my human rights  
Force me to perform what you command me  Slit my throat 
Step on my broken heart  Step on it and destroy it into pieces 
Slap my face  Slap it hard and make some bruises  
Threaten me with a knife  Threaten me with a gun  
Keep these tears running Rip my only dignity  
Hear this scream without care  Keep this blood running
Put it inside--a n g r i l y , v i o l e n t l y , v i c i o u s ly 
Don't quit till you exhale your last breath of lust
Don't feel sorry.This was really hard to write. 

In Memory of TEENA BRANDON
