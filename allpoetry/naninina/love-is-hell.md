---
title: "Love is Hell"
writer: "naninina"
slug: "love-is-hell"

Try as I might, 
to get it right

Tell me
if you're ever satisfied

Leave, come back
Love me, hate me

Dress me up
with your favorite
lingerie

Say sorry 
again and again

Choke me
with your lies

Kill me
with your mystery

Why 
this lonely
Why
this lonely

Run from me
when I'm talking to you
Ban me
when I'm not needed

Sleep soundly 
when I'm crying
Do it
when I forbid it

Try as you might,
to resist 
when I'm in bed, 
naked

Say it
then
contradict it

Fill me up
with 
disgust 

Thank you
and 
Good bye

Regret it
When it's too late
