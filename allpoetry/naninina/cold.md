---
title: "Cold"
writer: "naninina"
slug: "cold"
source: "AllPoetry"

I 
no
longer
feel
the 
urge,
the 
need
to 
kiss
and
touch
you

I 
no
longer
yearn
for 
your 
sweet
voice
before
I
go 
to 
bed

I
don't
know
what 
happened

Something,
someone
has
stolen
my affection to...
my belief in...
my dependence on...
YOU

The wind has swept away the air 
of romance
of lust
of love
that
I 
was
