---
title: "Between You and Me"
writer: "naninina"
slug: "between-you-and-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/46699-Between-You-and-Me-by-naninina"

I need to talk to you
I'm only human
Feet and hands just like yours
I've written you these songs
I've written poetry
I'd like to share with you
I thought I could get over this
The more I hear your voice
The more miserable I become
Nobody understands but you
This is between you and me
Just you and me
If there are others I'll be sinking again
It's four o'clock in the morning
It's noon
I am running out of time
I won't shoot myself
I want to hear you breathing so bad
I'm not asking for your signature or
your signed guitar, I'm not thinking about
your money and possessions
Only honest and positive intentions
I know I am impatient
I haven't been waiting for ten years
I live like there is no tomorrow
Let me taste a bit of a good life,
a bit of your fame, 
let me taste a bit of you
Grant this request
Give me a drop of your water
to quench my prolonged thirst
Enlighten this soul
Fill up this heart with affection
I'm only showing you that
I am alive
