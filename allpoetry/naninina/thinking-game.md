I'm thinking are you an evil
or a life safer? 


You recognize this game,
this thinking game. 

***

Allpoetry has helped you grow 
as a writer. Can you wait 'till
you post a new poem? Can
you stop thinking about the 
constructive (or destructive)
comments you'll get? 
Can you help not paying
attention to how many stars
you earn?

What is it, tell me
Is it some nasty imagination
that you've got, to satisfy your 
penis? 
Is it some wrong plan
that you've designed for your boyfriend,
like using his money to spoil your 
fantastic little body? 

You never stop thinking,
even when you're sleeping.
Do you fancy about 
getting married under an old tree
in a huge yard on one beautiful day?

What is it, tell me 
Are you thinking about corrupting
the third world countries? 
GAP and NIKE owners,
are you all thinking at all? 
Now reveal your guilt 
Tell me, how much
do you compensate these 
primitive people's hard work?
$2 dollars/hour?  

Before you put something in
your mouth, don't you want to 
know what you're going to be
swallowing? 
Tell me
Why do you believe in God?

President Bush,
Tell me what you're thinking
Which part of Afghanistan is
to be bombed next? 
Are you going to start
another war?

This thinking game
is screwing up and playing with
my mind,
your mind,
their minds.

***

Don't mind this gibberish
But can you help not thinking about 
what I've said?
