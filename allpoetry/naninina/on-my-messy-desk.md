Sun-Ripened-Raspberry lotion

An empty pink mug with a silver spoon in it 

A free magazine: "Student World Traveler"  

A black desk clock that never stops ticking

Two English dictionaries

A poetry book, full of empty pages

A Japanese soup bowl contains sweets and tea bags

Two pairs of scissors

A bottle of Hydroxyzine Tablets

Candles: the yellow, squared one is from a special person

A wireless phone that doesn't ring very often

A CD: Delerium's "Poem"

A round white pin that says in red "Sex with Strangers"

A liquid lip color: Sheerly Cocoa 

Two empty bottles of mineral water

Unread Books: "Girl, Interrupted" & "The Saving Graces"

Pens, highlighters, and other stationeries

~

Love particles
sprinkling on the surface 
of everythingI think each article on my desk tells you something about me. On a bigger note, each of these things might reveal something about the life of a girl. 
If anyone would like to give this one a close reading, I'd be glad to see/read it. One day I will reveal my own reading of this poem. 

Thanks for reading it. Very much appreciated.
