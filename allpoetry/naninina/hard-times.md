---
title: "Hard Times"
writer: "naninina"
slug: "hard-times"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/36159-Hard-Times-by-naninina"
---

Look 
at 
you
 
Look at 
who you are now
 
You were once
a miserable,
angry,
(and failed,
you thought) 
creature

Digging 
thousands of
trash cans,
only
to find others' 
left-over foods
(Hunger never stops) 

On the street
looking pale, 
looking weak,
begging
for pity
(Countless cruel people
are out there) 

And sleeping 
not peacefully
in cartons
and papers
(continuously
weeping) 

...sigh...

What hard times
you were facing. 
What an unfair way for
humans to live

Injustice 
had been done 
to you
my child,
but 
nothing 
could fix 
the past

Look
at 
you 
these days,
equipped with well-made 
and new garments 
and shiny shoes
 
Look at you,
looking fresh
looking bright 
and certainly clean 

Look at you,
now living in 
a decent house,
cozy and warm.
Protected 
from the hard rain 
the unbearable heat,
and the heavy wind

Look at you,
looking so serious
and working so hard

Don't 
count 
your money
(too much)
Don't 
think of
your possession 
(too much)
(Cherish is the right word)

Don't chase
the ones
who ripped you off
until you die
 
Don't hate
the ones
who were mean
to you

Smile
Like you used to
on the street,
when friendly strangers
offered kindness

No need to bear
your grudge 
(forever)
No need to be angry
(forever)

Let 
go
...

Let go...

Let go
of the past

(No need to be miserable
all your life)
