Little teeth
Little hands full of cookies' crumbs
Little skirt 
Little shy face giggling

Hanging around
Your parents' little convenient store
Watching people pass by

Remember 
The stars are beautiful
Your Barbie dolls are cute
Ice cream is cold but tasty
You are innocent 
and you will always be
Never blame yourself 
for anything 

Here you go,
I made you 
a little bracelet
Don't lose it
But if you do,
I'll make you 
another one

Now let's go back outside
and playI don't have a little sister. But I always wanted one. :)
