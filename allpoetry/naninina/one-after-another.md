---
title: "One after Another"
writer: "naninina"

Lovers break up easy, she sees 
Love after love, one after another ...
First loves were gone short or long time ago 
Just yesterday they met their 8th lover 
Lovers are not sad, she thinks 
Nor are they afraid to love anyone else 
Kiss after kiss, one after another... 
She's worried, not that she's alone 
Not that she just lost her loved one 
But lovers are not sad 
and are walking strong 
After falling, one after another... 
One love and one kiss, she has 
and once she falls, falls in love 
But when one is no longer the only one 
and first is not the first anymore 
When one is after another one, 
Has love lost its virginity?
