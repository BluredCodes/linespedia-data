title: "Music"

There is reality in front of my eyes
The moment I press the 'play' button,
My mind frees itself from the brain and flies away
Drawing entirely new pictures in my head

I dream when I'm sleeping, I dream when I'm awake
When the tune dances, my thoughts dance along
No one can interrupt me, not even the strangers in the bus
No one can disturb me, I'm not here

I am a rock star, I've toured around the world
I have a cancer and I am dying 
I am a president and I like to be worshipped
I am blind but I am a true believer 

I am overweight and desperate 
I am a prisoner and I don't have any regrets
I am God, witnessing the sin of man
I am a dirty homeless, seeking for justice


I am a little girl who dances with the best songsIt is amazing what a simple melody can do. When I listen to certain songs sometimes I can feel like a completely different person. When I listen to certain songs sometimes I am happy, sometimes I am sad.
