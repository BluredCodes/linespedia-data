Poems are like letters,
only without the envelopes,
stamps
and specific 
addresses

Who finds it, gets the privilege
to read
or
to ditch it

***

Brothers and Sisters,
I write about you all the time
I write to you all the time

***

So come to me, come
Tell me 
Please tell me from the start

Talk oh talk to me
Spill your guts
Pour it out, pour it all out on me

Do anything that will make me 
see you
know you
like you
hate you
want to touch you

Do anything 
for the love of the world
and 
don't stop, 
you never cease to amaze me 

***

Know that my affection to you is 
always present
If you don't see it
or can't sense it,
don't turn away,
I promise you'll find it again

Know that I miss you all the time
Like breathing, I can't stop missing you
Like breathing, I can't stop writing to you

***

I will give you water

I will give you
flowers, the stars in the sky
and the mountains, 
and all the things that make you
blush
smile
stretch
sigh
weep

Even if you sob, 
it will be a good cry
So peaceful and
relieving 
you will want more

***

I promise I will never leave my pen and paper
I promise I will never quit you

Like breathing, I can't stop writing poetryI see my relationship with my reader as an intimate one. And 
this one is for each of you, my reader. 

I'd also like to thank two people that have helped and inspired me a lot lately: Lurie and Sheer Poetry. 
Thus this poem is dedicated to them. 

Again thank you everyone, from the bottom of my heart, for reading my work.

Happy Valentine's Day
