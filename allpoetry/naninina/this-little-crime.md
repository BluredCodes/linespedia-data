Cmon,
let's go outside
Let's play, let's scream, let's do anything
that we'd like to do
Who cares if we look unsexy together
Who cares if we are weird and boring 
and kinda geeky
Who cares if we have different colors of skin
People can go home if they want to
People can choose to ignore us
What is wrong with loving you, loving me
What is wrong with touching, kissing,
and running our tounges wild
We are all only humans
Let's get outta here, ride a bus, 
stop at the park
Who cares if we are both fat or skinny
Who cares if we have the same sexes
Let them watch if they want to
Let them laugh at us 
Ah who cares if we are careless
Stop I don't want to hear anymore
Who cares what people say
Let's go home, let's eat mangoes
and get naked
Nothing is wrong with
freeing ourselves from the hot pure lust
that has been driving us CRAZY
