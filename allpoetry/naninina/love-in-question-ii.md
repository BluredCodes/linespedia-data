Been avoiding your phone calls
Finally you reached me
I've always admired your compassionate soul
I'm not sure if things are still the same

Laying down lazily on your bed,
The two of us did amuse each other
You strung your guitar and
sung a lullaby with your sweet voice

San Francisco is a lovely city
That clear night, under the stars
we wandered around downtown,
and walked through the art galleries

My head would fall into your shoulder,
Your brown sweater smelled like vanilla

We moved on and we meet again now
You said, "from time to time you passed through my head"
You are asking for one kiss, 
I'm not sure if I'd like to grant your wish

I like the way you worship me
We both seem to enjoy this whirlwind romance
If only one kiss is what you ask, 
This crime will never endThis is not written to the same person that I've written 'Love In Question (I)'to.
