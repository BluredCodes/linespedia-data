Challenge Description: write about your favorite time of day...why...? do you spend it with someone special, or is it just because you dont have anything to worry about at that time...make it special, make me actually see the sun rising(example) or the sun set. or something to that extent...thank you...good luck (60 points)



Napping is to repair my soul
Yoga is stretching and relaxing
In the afternoon,
the sun hasn't yet gone
But the most heat has passed 

***

Rush for a cup of coffee
Rush for work and school
and things that need to be done
in the morning

In the evening other duties are in my list:
The bills have to be paid
The stomach wants to be fed

Exhausted, is how I feel
as I approach the end of the day
The bed is waiting when midnight comes
I need to recharge the battery 

But,
The afternoon is 
when the flowers grow most beautifully
The birds fly most free
The beach is calling, inviting me

Around two, three, four PM is 
the perfect time to be outside with nature
The precise moment to be reading and writing poetry
And dreaming and practicing yoga 
and all the good acts that can be done on a fine day

Whether alone or with company,
such a wonderful part of the day shouldn't be missed
