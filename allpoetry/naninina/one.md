One thing that we all do is,
thinking
Hours a day we spend, trying to solve these Earthy problems
Hours we spend cluttering up our minds

So many thoughts in our lives
So many dreams we hope to come true

Those thoughts, those dreams
Where do they lead us?
I do not know for certain
But it's probably best to compromise
our thoughts and dreams

We smile, we feel, we talk. Don't we?
We all do the same things

So many live on Earth
But there is only one Earth
We are stuck with our thoughts and dreams
But we'll certainly be okay, if
We are one
