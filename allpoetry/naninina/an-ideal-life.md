Wearing sandals 
Eating mangoes 

Naked  

Walking through a friendly village 
Smiling

Tanned skin 

Dressing up 
Dressing down 

Playful mode 

Reading a book 
Writing a poem 

People, together 

Dancing

A big yard with an old tree 
Few cars on the street 

Swimming

Exotic drinks 

Listening to music 
Eating outside 

Volleyball at the beach 

Innocent laughter 
A wallet without any credit cards in it 

Learning 
Experiencing 

Time follows, 
not haunts you 

Deserts at night 
Unfamiliar languages

Taking pictures 
Painting an abstract 

The sun 
The moon 

Wearing a skirt 

A cool breeze 

Letters 
and diaries 

No worries 

Peace 

* What do you think? * 

Oh 
one more thing, 

a soul to hold hands with
