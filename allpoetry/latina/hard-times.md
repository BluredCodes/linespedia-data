Soon I will leave my homeThe one that I grew up inJust because my parents are getting divorced!Now I have to make a choiceWho will I live withMom or DadMy brothers are leaving meOne to collegeThe other to the Air ForceNo siblings to help me outIts like I'll be an only childMy parents have me on lock downThey say I am out too muchFor only being 14The only 2 good friendsKier & AnnaThey help me get away from everythingI Love You Guys!!!!!         (Anna,Kiersten)
