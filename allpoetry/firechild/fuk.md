title: "fuk"
writer: "FireChild"

With tear stained eyes i watch you drown
in anothers self pity

With a heart bleeding i watch you die
stabbed by a decietful dagger

With a soul screaming i hear the lies
falling from the mouth of the liar

please, i love you
i know its not the kind you were looking for
but you know that its all i can give

With dry eyes i watch you drown
in anothers arms

With a heart numb i watch you die
stabbed by a blood dried dagger

With a soul whispering i hear the lies
falling from the mouth of the liar

please, i love you
don't
