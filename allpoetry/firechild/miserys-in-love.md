---
title: "Misery's in love"
writer: "FireChild"
slug: "miserys-in-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/28152-Miserys-in-love-by-FireChild"
---

tortured souls 
drawn togather
paying their toals
in the house of forever

huddled close
they shiver as one
awaiting there dose
of justice undone

their only condolence
is eachother
their only assurance 
from one another

one thing can be said
for this sight to see
shes not alone in bed
misery loves company
