---
title: "Seeing?"
writer: "FireChild"
slug: "seeing"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/27254-Seeing--by-FireChild"
---

lonelyness
emptyness

something comes to take my hand
its contact the only comfort

it brings me closer
closer......

the death kiss of insanity
the thrill of the imbrace

the fire of passion
the passion of madness

caught up in the moment
all reason void

the lonliness is gone
the emtyness departed
 
a scream escapes my lips
the only sound i can make

deep within a light
it crys out in pain

the fire of passion is singing my being
i can feel madness burning my soul

realisation takes over
instinct guides me

i fallow the twisted paths of confusion
i fallow the small light that guides me

i hear the voice of sanity
the voice of reason

take my hand
take my heart

the light breakes and i see myself
writhing, struggling to see

wanting to know
not wanting to find out

trying to find it
not knowing what to look for

not wanting to find it
knowing only the darkness
and seeing only the light
