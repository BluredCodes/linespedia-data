people rushing
all of them thinking
about something

that can not be known
because if you knew
the knowledge would destroy you...

for an eternity you think about everyone else
when after a while you realize
that you no longer have thaughts of your own...

youve become obsessed with what everyone else knows
and what everyone else knows
becomes what you know...

and at last lifes kicked in
your thaughts are no longer of your own mind
they are a product of everything except for yourselfallright, i wrote this quite some time ago.  I think i might have been in eighth grade or so. ummm i didnt change it, although im quite tempted to "fix it"allright, well i hope you enjoy, and mabey even understand....
