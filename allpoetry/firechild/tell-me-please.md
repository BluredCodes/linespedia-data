Tell me something….
Does it hurt?
Can you feel it when life hits you in the face?
Like a hammer hits a rusted nail.

Answer me….
Do you cry?
When you can no longer stand that the answers you wanted aren’t what you needed.
Like a flu shot

Why are you starring?
You didn’t know I knew?
Couldn’t you see that every step you took I was at your heels?
Like a puppy without a home.

Don’t cry….
Why are you crying?
It’s not your fault that you couldn’t protect me from it, from life, from knowing.
Like a mother with her child



I have to go now. 
Do you mind?
I have to start early if I want to get a head start.
Like the bird with the worm, or something……This is the first time that I've let someone besides a good friend read something of mine, be nice, please?
