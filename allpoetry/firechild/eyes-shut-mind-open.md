title: "Eyes shut mind open"

I see my life in a dream
I wake up and start to scream

Nothing short of a miracle
I'm still alive and cynical

There's no escape from lifes nightmare
Wake up from it and I'm still there

No ones around to hear my scream
So I fall back into the same dream

The monster inside, chasing my with its eyes
The monster I am, chasing me again

I take a breath and feel the cold air
I open my eyes, and look into the mirror

Th reflection that stares back at me is my own
Far off in the distance I hear a moan

No ones there to help me over
So I return to my fitful slumber

I see my life in a dream
I wake up and start to scream

Nothing short of a miracle
I'm still alive and cynical

My eyes open and I see the beast
My eyes open and I realize it's meI think i wrote this for a friend, but it might be me, i dunno.
