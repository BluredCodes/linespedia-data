---
title: "Nothing"
writer: "FireChild"
slug: "nothing"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/36885-Nothing-by-FireChild"

My first thought
purge it
my last hope
almost gone

my dignity....
what dignity
What am i with out that?
with out it
im nothing

My first hope
god please
my last thought
god no

I'm so sorry.
