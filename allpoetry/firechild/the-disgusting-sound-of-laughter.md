title: "The Disgusting Sound of Laughter"

It crawls up from my stomach
Clawing it's way to my throat

Touching the back of my tounge
The bitter taste makes me flinch

It falls from my lips
Forcing it's way out to the world

Spewing itself from my depths
Poisoning innocent ears

my laughter......
my cynical laughter
