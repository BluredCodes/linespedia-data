---
title: "No Borders"
writer: "muse amused"

There are no borders
In this war.
The terror now is
The same as before.
The terrorists have struck
The whole world-wide.
But this time it was
On the USA side.

The shock and the grief
Is not unique to the States
More than half of the world
Suffered the same kind of fate.
Now is the time
We must unite
To ferret out evil,
The ultimate fight.

We must make the doubter
Clearly understand
This is not just U.S.
But everyone's land
Who must take up
The banner of Freedom's right
Bringing to end
The terrorist blight.

This is not a jihad
As many may say,
The true Muslim will tell you
That is just not their way.
They believe in a God
Who demands a good life,
Not evil destruction
And death with a knife.

Christian and Muslim
Jew and the Sikhs
Harmoniously together
Until these past weeks.
The code of America
Guaranteed them these rights.
We cannot allow evil
To cause Americans to fight.

The Christian American
marries the American Jew,
Some disagree but in
American it's not new.
We have freedom to worship
Any day or way that we choose,
Yet internal attacks
Are making the news.

We are creating borders
And drawing wrong lines
And letting the terrorist
Make up our minds.
Wake up, you people,
Who live in this land!
Don't strike down your brother,
Give him your hand
To encircle  the globe,
Brown hand in white
Close ranks on this terror
Show them our might!
