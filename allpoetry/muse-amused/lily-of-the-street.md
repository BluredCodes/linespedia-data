Lily of the StreetWhite dress; slightly soiledHair lifted by a sea breeze.She stoops to re-tie the ribbonOn the wrinkled shopping bag.Where is she going?Does she have a place to go?I wonder about this LilyWho wanders in the streets.She is a storyI'm sure.
