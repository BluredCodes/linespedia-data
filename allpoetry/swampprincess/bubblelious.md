BubbleliousThe shadows danced in the flickering candlelightMaking the huge claw foot tub  shimmer in the nightShe slipped off her clothes and stepped into the bubblesLaid back and relaxed she tried to forget her troublesShe has a date tonight, but she needs this bubble bath so badThinking this will fix the rough day she has have had?Soon she is soaked in bubbles up to her hairFor the world out side she has no caresA manly smell does prevail, before she knew how to reactIt’s obvious she’s a victim of a bathroom attackA masculine hand on her shoulder comes to restAnd slides over her shoulder and down between her breastsA voice whispers in her ear, relax darling there nothing to fearI’m just early for our date and see I am needed hereWith that his hand surveys her stomach and then her moundThen plays a sweet tune when her pleasure garden is foundHer emotions are screaming and her body tinglingAs his magical fingers have her soul singingJust when she knows she can stand no moreHe joins her in the bubbles, with his tongue he exploresWild splashing, screams making bubbles galore                                         The couple comes to fulfillment and so much moreBubbles can wash way your troubles yes its true But they are lots of more fun when shared by two!I wrote this for all the lovers who enjoy a bath made for two.
