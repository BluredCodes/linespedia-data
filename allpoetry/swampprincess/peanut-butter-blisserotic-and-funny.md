I am here to tell you Peanut Butter rules
It has the power to heal and chase away the blues
Loved the world over by people and animals too               
Whether it’s the crunchy or creamy smooth

It tastes great on bread and veggies too
No matter how you eat its nutritious for you
I spread mine on my...well I won’t say where
But it sure feels good when it’s licked from there

  
A spat between lovers can be healed and everything made right
With a few spoonfuls of peanut butter placed just right
Spread it on bananas and apples too
But the best way to eat it is right from the spoon

Cover an ear of corn with it and hang it in a tree
And watch the birds come and devour it with glee
And then there’s a yummy peanut butter shake
It’s a sure-fire way to ease a stinging heartache

As a hot oil treatment it is divine
Brings dull dry hair right back to a great new shine
Spread it on those heads that are completely bald
And hair will be growing in no time at all

Now if milk and cookies are your pleasure
My peanut butter cookies you will treasure
Incredibly delicious from start to finish
Making all your day’s troubles seem to diminish

Now you’re laughing and shaking your head
Thinking this is a lot of hogwash you have just read
Georgia is the peanut capital of the world it is true
So would a born and bred Georgia girl lie to you?

Mom put a little on my pacifier when I was a baby
It smoothed my teething gums and kept me happy
She put it on my toast, and even on my grits
Is it no wonder I am passionate like this?

But do not take my testimonial words on this
Try any brand and but the best one is “JIFF”
Spread it on absolutely "anything" and eat away
