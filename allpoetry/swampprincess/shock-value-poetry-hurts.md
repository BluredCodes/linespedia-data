Lonkert, and Antmonkey
You have all tried to shock the world
With your sick drgrading poetry 
Little children were your subject

How you hurt them with glee
This my friends is not poetry
It's a true sign of your immaturity
I am not alone there's many who think like me.

Your all a talented bunch of poets
Come on guys and clean up your act
Its upsetting to us and thats a fact
Lets get passed this and move on

If you must write smut then find another site
This ones for people who love to write
Clean and cute and even sexy
Poems to tinkle every ones fancy

Not to hurt innocent children!I felt like I needed to do this.I asked that if you agree with me please say so.We need to heal and get this sites integrity back
