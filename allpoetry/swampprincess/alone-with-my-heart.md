title: "Alone With My Heart"

Alone I am
More than you know
For you are my one true Love
And I still Love you so

I see your sweet smile
Over the distant miles
or a laugh that
brings me Home

All I have ever wanted
No longer coming my way
The shadows grow darker
They lengthen every day

I know my chance
Was lost long ago
for my one true Love
I still Love you so

 
I am alone with my heart
At least I know My Love
That you are still there
Across the many miles

My love,as if,a whisper
In your ear,in the dead night
It's me sweet darling 
Kissing your lips Good night!!
