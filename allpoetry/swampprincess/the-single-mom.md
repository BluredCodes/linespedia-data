The Single Mom
 
The long day is over I am so tired
The boss was cross I could have been fired
I like all days I do my best
For a single Mom there is no rest  
 
 
I was up all night, the temp was so high
My son was sick.He has me alone
I have no Insurance,doctors aren't free
With daylight came a blessing
My prayers were answered, the temp was gone
 
My baby better, off to work I went 
Tried as if my day was done spent
But knowing I had no choice you see
For as a single mom, my baby counts on me
 
For all things I have to provide,
The Judge said pay support for this child
But his Dad so for has managed to hide
He thinks he's won, for no money is paid
                                                              But it his sad loss,you see
When his little arms  encircle me
I am blessed with hugs and kisses for me alone
When like now I come home..
