My Precious Savior                                    
                                                         
My last boyfriend beat me
What made him do this you ask? 
Because I said “NO” to the creep
So he left me on the floor in a bloody heap


My wounds healed but not my heart
Nightmares plagued me from the start
In the dead of night I would awake
A monsters hands around my neck


Up in the night and could not return to bed
Not with that monster still in my head
I got on the computer and found a new friend
An intelligent, honest, caring man among men

He kept me company and chased away the fright
with caring and laughter till daylight
Just by being there to listen  he set me free
This angel called “Precious” was a savior to me

He never asked me for a single thing
Always there when I came around
when I  was frighten  in the middle of the night
Always with strong arms to hold me tight

In  time I found I could sleep without fear
Precious had chased away the monster and my tears
He saved my life and taught me to trust again
By being there and being my dear friend

This poem is dedicated to 'Precious'
the  friend who put me back together again
and never once thought of himself. Thank you !!
