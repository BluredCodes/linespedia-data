Did I Hear An Angel?
I heard him in a whisper
I wondered is this real
His words are like an Angels
Knowing all my worries and how I feel
The Heavens seemed to open up
My heart for me that day
It seemed that he could understand
Every word I had say
I said,”Now do I hear an Angel
Whispering to me?
Do I hear an Angel
Setting my heart free?
His words come straight from heaven
Healing all my mental pain
Did I hear an Angel
Here in my souls pouring rain
His words reached deep inside of me
Like he had known me for many years
He seemed to care that we could share
Our heartaches and some tears
Right from the start he stole my heart
He made me laugh till I thought I would die
Showing me just how special angels are
And that I no longer needed to cry.I wrote this about a special friend who I met in the chat room
and who helped me through a living hell and reminds my special angel today.
