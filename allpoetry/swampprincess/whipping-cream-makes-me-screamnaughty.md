Whipping Cream Makes Me ScreamWe go grocery shopping you and meIn love and happy as two people can beAs we walk the aisles up and downStealing little smooches when no ones aroundWith loving looks and sweet caressesWe look for food we can put places  Thinking of where to put this strawberry or fruitCould lead to a delectable yummy body suitTo be eaten by ones lover In times of passionCan lead to wonderful and delicious reactions.Now the best of all is whipping creamLicked off the it leads to magnificent screamsI wonder should one of us stay homeAnd the other one come shopping aloneTogether we never think of meat and veggies to eatJust filling the cart with all these sexual treats!Written by Swampprincess for her man..Hey. So you know where my mind was today!
