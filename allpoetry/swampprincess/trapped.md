Waiting in line in the big store
Surrounded by candy and much more
When all of a sudden, out of the blue
A flagrant bomb exploded .what could I do?

Quickly the source I had to find
A head of me or from behind.
Could be been anyone in this line
All I see are innocent faces, staring back at me

Trying to find someone to blame
For this evil smell. That could start a flame!
Oh God help me please.  
Get away from the rascal who cut the cheese!Come on and tell me you never had this happen?
