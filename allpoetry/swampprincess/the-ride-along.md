The Ride Along..............                                        
I  live in New Zealand and deliver  beautiful live plants everywhere
Getting out in the fresh air traveling from here to there
Now I am not allowed to take riders in my truck with me
Only my trusty old sweet dog gentle Jenny
We travel all over from one end of this island to the other
There no place my big white plant truck does cover
Stopping and selling plants and fresh potted flowers
Driving the roads in my big truck for hours and hours
Now I mentioned to my sweet mom" I am off to Wanganiu"   
She exclaimed “your granny needs a visit so I will go with you"
Now I know it’s been a while since mom visited her hometown
Bringing a ride along is forbidden, but how could I turn Mom down?
So off we went Mom , Jenny and breaking the rules me
 Mom chatting  for miles and filling me in on past family history  
We  stopped for fried fish and had a picnic on a beach   
Then finally around 4 pm her hometown we did reach 
After a  heartfelt quick visit with granny we get out a again
Running late now and time to make up I drove like the wind
Mom screamed oh my, We have to go back I left my purse!
Knowing it was 3o miles back under my breath out came a curse
Back we went and fetched mom’s precious purse
And off again now the time thing much worse
Stopping for tea in Dannevirke where mom informed me with glee      
The second home built here was by your grandpa great x three
We got back to Palmerson I dropped mom off very late
My boss was was steaming when I pulled through the gates
I explained Mom was with me and we have some bad luck
He yelled quite loudly “No Ride Alongs In my truck”!
I grabbed my bike and got out of there fast
Was half way home before I remembered Jenny at last 
Still in the truck behind locked gates of my job yet
I climbed the high fence, and rescued my faithful pet
Now who shows up to catch me in the act?
But our local coppers on patrol ,yes its a fact
I tried to explain" Sirs I work here and forgot my dog"
They did believe me saying we will let you go  soon
But  just watch out for a bad guy, who peeps by the moon !
I rode home with Jen running along side
Thinking so what Mr. boss man if I took my Mom on todays long ride?
And It had its rough spots that is very clear 
But I will  treasure this special Mom and Son day for many a year !I wrote this from a very funny but true email my Friend Jon In Palmerson NZ.sent me..I hope you like it.some times life is far better than fiction.
Swampprincess Gin
