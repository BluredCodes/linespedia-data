Did you ever pass a field of wild flowers swaying in the wind and not smile?
Or did you ever get a bouquet of flowers from a child?
Every bloom was special because it came from the heart
There are no ugly flowers; it’s been that way from the start
 
God created flowers to bring smiles to our faces 
And to make us glad we are a part of the human races
There is evidence everywhere this is true 
We send flowers to cheer friends when they are blue

Weddings are embellished to add beauty and cheer
Flowers bring smiles to all we hold dear
Men bring flowers to impress they dates
And home to their wives when they’re going to be late

In the 60’s the flower children rebelled
Their flower covered buses, cars and clothes regaled
Why it flowers they shoved up the rifles of police
When they were marching and screaming for peace

Bouquets we send to friends who are sick
Its colorful flowers bring cheer and make them get well quick
There is wonderful magic in all flowers you see
If they come from the tiniest weed or the biggest tree

A well planted garden, manicured to the last detail
Or an incredible display of wild flowers along a trail
For God put them here for all mankind to share
As a reminder of his love and how much he cares

If a man wants to tell a woman he loves her dear
Bouquets of red roses at her door may appear
For there is no greater power  to win affections 
As the sentiment of flowers its God’s creationsI have never seen an ugly flower not one. have you?
