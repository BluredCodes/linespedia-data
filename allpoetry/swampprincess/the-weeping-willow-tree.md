The Weeping Willow Tree
Come sit down beside me, my love
Beneath the weeping willow tree
Nestle close and forget everything
Yesterday was but a moment in time        
 A  full life,somehow unfulfilled
You are with me now,my love
Listen closely and hear my heart
Time itself has stopped now
You and I are alone together
Our world is the one we make
Your smile touches me warmly
You are the only true love for me
Looking up with innocent expression
Kissing lips as clouds drift by
This is my dream of tomorrow
For I see this ever clearer now
It has always been just a matter of time
Weeks and months that lay just ahead
The Weeping willow knows not when
Only we shall fulfill such a dreamThis poem was published this year.
