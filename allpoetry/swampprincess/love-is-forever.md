Once it is present, 
Love

Love is beautiful.
Once it is present,
It will be forever.
Love is pain.
Sometimes love hurts, 
Fear not, for it will heal.
Love is power.
When love is with you, 
You can accomplish any dream.
Love is wealth.
When you are one within love,
You can never be richer.
Love is eternal.
You will be forever.
