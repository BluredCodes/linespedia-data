I come here almost every day
To sit from a distance and watch you play
Your golden hair shining in the sun
An angels face you have my son

I was so young when you were born
Not able to take care of you myself
They said" Put him up for adoption
really girl its your only option"

After 20 long hours of labor pains
A miracle happen and out you came
Then they took you away before I could see
What a wonderful gift God had given me

I went to the nursery and peeked at you
My precious little boy bundled in blue
A birthmark gave the secret away its true
Mine on the same hand told me it was you

I stood for hours there by the glass
Would this horrible feeling ever pass?
But I was only 15 and what did I know?
You needed two parents to help you grow

Now 5 long years have come and gone
Walking in the park one day,I chanced to see
A boy with a birthmark just like me
My heart almost stopped could it be?

You looked the right age and had my smile
Can you be my sweet lost child?
With every day that passed I almost see
That no one else could this possibly be

Now I come every day for a maybe chance to see
A little boy who means the world to me
Given away at birth,but always loved so much
Watching,Waiting  but never to touch, 

Would I ever tell you who I am? Be so bold?
No, your someones elses adorable angel to hold
All I get are these sacred moments of view
That I will cherish forever, because I love youI wrote this for a wonderful friend of mine who gave her son up at birth but never found him..
