title: "Looking to Distant Lands"
writer: "SwampPrincess"

Alas,if not for time and space 
I would touch the placid waters
Of distant lands of the never seen
Oceans bathed in blue moonlight
 
Mountains unscathed by man   
To wish upon the furthest star
through a shining nights sky
To think you off miles a far                    
 
Only they realize how small we are                        
Then I behold heavens starry sea
And know there is no distance so far                         
Nor so vast that will prevent me
 
From wondering if you are there
With your eyes turned to the same
Heaven drawn seas of distant light
Winds above the trees swirl and sing in the night
 
My soul hears and calls out beyond this place 
And beckons the universe to right this wrong
To bring souls apart in time and space
Together to live in harmony so very long
 
Aboard this world so lost in an ocean
Long forgot as heaven's shore brings
The endless chain of new and old
Both worlds and life of stories left untold
 
Tomorrow will come and go on these
Distant worlds as will newborn dawns 
Of morning and daybreak upon the
Stars hidden yet felt this very night
