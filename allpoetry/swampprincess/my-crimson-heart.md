---
title: "My Crimson Heart"
writer: "SwampPrincess"

When I start thinking and the words begin to flow 
>It is then that I feel you beside me and my heart beats slow 
>I hear you whisper tenderly in my ear sweet reminders 
>Quietly given as you glide closer to show me so 
> 
>You know by now darling and I am sure you always will 
>I can't love you any less and cannot love you any more 
>The fate's put you far away from me , I found you all the same 
>Upon you sweet face I dream and dream the nights away 
> 
>Until that fateful day I hold you so very close 
>You'll hear my beating heart and know your place 
>Beside me ,not in front or back , our spirits will embrace 
>Yes, I can't love you any less or any more 
> 
>I guess they call it emotion , what I feel for you 
>And sometime , someday ,we'll share our dreams too 
>Kiss me each and every night as you draw back your sheets 
>In my visions I'll paint your face with beams of moonlight 
> 
>I will live my days with the sweet eternal thought 
>At least in all the world I have truly loved another 
>Felt your soul and presence upon my crimson heart 
>Yes, I wouldn't love you any less and couldn't love 
>you any more. 
>I wrote this for a very special man.
