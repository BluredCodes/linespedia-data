How to Find A Special ManIf you’re looking for a special manA guy who listens and understandsOne of the special intelligent fewWho bothers to ask what is special to you?Don’t be impressed by a handsome faceOr a guy who can win the big raceA great physique and good looks are niceBut if it’s all they have, you’ll pay the priceGo beyond the cover and look inside the bookHow about that quiet guy, who might be shyGo talk to him, he’s worth a second lookHe may stimulate the senses and make your heart flyOr that rugged guy who works with his handsBuilding things, or planting the landHe may warm your heart and have you smittenWhen he reads you romantic poetry he’s written Never dismiss a guy for what he’s wearingOr how he talks or earns his livingThere are  very special guys to be foundSo get out there and look around!I found my special man you seeAnd it came as a complete surprise to meConceited buff macho guys,are a thing of the pastExchanged for romance, honesty and a love that will lastwritten for 'Precious' my special guy.
