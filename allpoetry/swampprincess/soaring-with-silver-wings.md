I was alone ,cooking for one
Not looking forward to the years to come
Each day a copy of the last
A solo trip that never moved fast

My friends said"come on to the dance"
It's for Seniors like us
Who want a new chance
They persuaded me, without much fuss
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                The music was very good                     
IT took me no time, to get in the mood
Asked to dance.My old feet seem to fly 
It made me feel young again,and very spry

A chance meeting at the punch bowl
Our hands touched and magic unfolds
Two Silver haired souls,soar and meet
Two hearts entwined never to retreat

Now love and romance are back in my life
I am proud to say I am a new wife!
To everyone looking for a chance
Silver hair can lead to romance!I would love to let people know there always another chance.
