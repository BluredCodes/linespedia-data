Spooning

He lifts the covers and says come to me
And pats the place he wants me to be
I slide right in and snuggle close

We lay like spoons my back to his chest
His strong arm around me 
and his hand cupping my breast

The length of our bodies fit like a glove
Like two spoons stacked together
Entwined and in love

His manly aroma envelopes my senses
My neck tingles as he gently places butterfly kisses
I feel lucky and complete, as we two spooners drift off to sleepI think this is the best way to sleep when your in love.
