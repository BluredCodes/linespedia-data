I'm on my knees
For whatever you please
My song, you have sung
as I used my tongue.

As I nibbled your sweet thighs
I hear your lovely sighs
Your moans are great
My sweet mouth you appreciate


My throat welcomes your sweet Nectar
As I swallow hard, your in shock
Your body shakes and starts to rock
With a smile, I enjoy the specter


Your screams of  'OH GOD HONEY!  '
Warms my heart,and makes me horny
I 'm here on my knees you see
Because I know you'll do the same for me!
