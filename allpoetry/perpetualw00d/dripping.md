title: "Dripping"

your lips drip hate,
like a faucet that wont completely shut off.
annoying little drips 
that ping against the metal of the sink.
resounding over and over again,
like a gong.
You are nothing.
PING
You are worthless.
PING
You cant do anything right.
PING.
PING 
PING
