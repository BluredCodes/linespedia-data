---
title: "Murder Me"
writer: "Perpetualw00d"
slug: "murder-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/21902-Murder-Me-by-Perpetualw00d"
---

Murder my heart with your cruelty
Leave me bleeding in the street.
Batter me with your coldness
Leave trembling from your chill.
Slaughter my soul with your bitterness
Leave me broken in the gutter.
Bruise me with your indifference
Leave me aching in the night.
Murder me, 
batter me, 
slaughter me, 
bruise me
With your cruel, 
uncaring 
heart.
