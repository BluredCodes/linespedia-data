Who the hell do you think you are?
My mother?

When she tells me to fuck off,
she says it with love.

When you say it,
its dripping poison
that paralyzes my heart
like a nasty bite from a python.

You think you can control me
change me to be what you want
by screaming filth at me?

Do you realize that your words 
do nothing more
than make me want to do exactly 
what it is that you dont want me to?
