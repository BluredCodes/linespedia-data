---
title: "Crashing Through"
writer: "Perpetualw00d"
slug: "crashing-through"

Crashing through the forest on my ride
Looking for a place to hide.
No one to bother me
And no one to see
The pain I hide when the tears fall free.

Crashing through the forest on my ride
And suddenly I spied
Someone I'd like to bother me
Who I might just allow to see
The pain I show when the tears fall free.
