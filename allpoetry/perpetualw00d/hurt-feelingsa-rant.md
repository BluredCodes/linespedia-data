She shit on me
Called me a fool
A player
A liar.
And now I write
Angry
Pissed off
Ready to HURT someone.
But I use a pen..
I write it down, 
Get it out
So I don't  HURT anyone.. especially her.
And now
I'm WRONG for writing it out.
NO her name is not on this
NO none of you do NOT know her
But I can't write about her
I will HURT her fucking feelings.
What the FUCK is up with that?
Did she NOT think it hurt my feelings
When she slept around on me?
Did she NOT think it hurt my feelings 
When she told me I was trash??
DId she NOT think it hurt my feelings
When she told me I deserved to get AIDS and die lonely????
What the FUCK?!
I think I DESERVE TO be angry
To write it the fuck out.
Or I will go and throttle her.
I will make her life HELL.
SO instead I write.
A pen and paper.
Don't hurt
as much as MY hands around her throat would.
Thank you for caring enough
to be hurt by my words.
But STAY THE FUCK AWAY if you don't like what I have to SAY!
My feelings are FUCKING hurt too!
