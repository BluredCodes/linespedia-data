---
title: "You lied"
writer: "Perpetualw00d"
slug: "you-lied"
source: "AllPoetry"

Last night you said you loved me
You lied.
You put your arms around me and
you lied.

Last night when you whispered in my ear
I died.
You held me close and fed me shit.
I died.

Last night when you fell asleep
I cried.
I watched you in your only honest moment and
I cried.

Last night you said you loved me
You lied.
