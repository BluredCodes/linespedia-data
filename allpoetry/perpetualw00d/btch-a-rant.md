Go ahead bitch
And bite me with your cruel
Condescendence. 
You can’t hurt me anymore
I am walking away.
What a bitch you are
You think we’re all so stupid
That we don’t see right through your
Less than well planned game.
Alone.. You will be.
Crying… late at night.
Go ahead bitch
Try it again.
The next time you 
Try to bite me with your
Words.
I will bite back 
With the fact
That I
Don’t
Care.
I am through 
With your 
Bullshit.
And payback’s a 
BITCH.
