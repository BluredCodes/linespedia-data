I asked the old man a question
He did not have an answer.
He just lay staring at the cieling,
Fighting the war of cancer.

I sat by his side,
Praying that he would be ok.
crying on his old hand,
Begging him to stay.

Staring deeply into his lifeless eyes,
holding his hand tight.
I wish I would have known,
That he was going to die that night.

I didnt leave the room,
until they had taken him away.
I still prayed to a god that didnt exist,
That he would be ok.
