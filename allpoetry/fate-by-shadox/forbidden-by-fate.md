title: "Forbidden by fate"
writer: "fate by Shadox"

The light comes over me,
Still dominating my heart.
I said I love you, 
But you turned away.
When I smiled, you turned, 
Laughing,
Ripping out my heart,
Crushing my emotions with your heel,
Your foot on my soul.
What is this I'm feeling?
Is it love? Am I in love?
I don't know. I think so.

You're the best thing that's happened to me,
But you don't know i exist.
Yet, I don't want to lose you.
For you, I would risk my heart
Tempting it to be broken, 
You're the best thing thats ever happened to me,
But you don't know I exist.
When will i get into your heart?
