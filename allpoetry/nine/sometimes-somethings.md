---
title: "sOmEtImEs SoMeThInGs"
writer: "nine"

Sometimes, somethings in life
are just so hard to predict
so hard to understand.
                             
Sometimes, somethings in life
are just so easy to think of
so easy to misunderstand.

Sometimes, somethings in life
are just so hard to get
so hard to find.

Sometimes, somethings in life 
are just so easy to find
so easy to lose.

Sometimes, somethings like that
are just like your love for me -
So hard to predict, so hard to understand
So easy to think of, so easy to misunderstand
So hard to get, so hard to find
So easy to find, so easy to lose.

Sometimes, something like love
is just so contradicting...
