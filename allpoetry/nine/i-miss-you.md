---
title: "I mIsS yOu"
writer: "nine"
slug: "i-miss-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

Didn't know that you'll be gone,
gone just like that.
Didn't even get to say goodbye,
not even for the very last time.

How terrible you must have felt,
don't worry, I felt that way too.
That journey must have been long -
the journey of no return.

How did things turn out this way,
I guess we'll never know.
Don't be sad my girl,
you'll always be in my heart.

Come to me when you feel lonely,
Mr Sandman would be pleased to help.
There in my dreams we'll meet,
together with feelings from Reality.

But promise not to cry when it's time to go,
for that will break my broken heart so.
When shall we meet again,
I'm sure it will be soon,
