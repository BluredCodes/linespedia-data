---
title: "wHiTe LiEs"
writer: "nine"
slug: "white-lies"
source: "AllPoetry"

I see the darkness,
I don't see the light.
My eyes are covered,
by terrible white lies.
I tried to see Truth,
but it wouldn't meet me.
I tried to kill Lie,
but it just wouldn't die.
