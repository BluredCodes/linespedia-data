title: "Painting you"

Painting youPhotographs in my mindYour flesh --- my paletteFill the silenceOf my nightYour breath --- hangsIn the airMist --- on the moorsOf your bodyI watch you sleepDreaming, feelingIn the darkI touch your thoughtsShamanistically --- give you releaseInto the darkness of usWrapping ourselves In each otherA gift of time
