Why am I here

I dream
 
        + 

           drift

amongst the currents

          UNSEEN

          UNFELT

becoming
        something
                 once

DETESTABLE

a ghost

tall white-faced
        
                WEIGUK

a being translocated

       in time and space

Einsteinian error

        BLOOD

on the face of the

                   MOON

the puppetmaster's

    TOYWEIGUK --- the word means foreigner in Korean
