Salteensshe always ate themcovered in cheesein that subtle sexual waywhenever she wanted eroticaher tongue licking off each single grain of saltI remember those actions each time I eat crackers each little crumba single reminder of ecstasy and delightI remember crackers on the sheetsafterwardsin the afterglownow I see them on the tablesitting there,and am remindedin my sorrow.
