I watch the surge, fall...of you -- tidalrhythms, pull -- pushfeelings...emotionson the windof our beingCaress the breath..flowing -- an ocean...ofyou and I, --I lose myselfdrowning in you...a sightless swimmercircling the echo -- of a sirenwho haunts his soulLooking in to me ... I seeyou, part of memerging, synthesis, -- one beingwithout identitya grown orphan, or orphan grownwho knows, caresthe defined us --lacks borders ... we only have -- eternaltidal rhythms
