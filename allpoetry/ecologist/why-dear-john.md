title: "Why  Dear John"
writer: "ecologist"

why... the question
simply sits
on my tongue 
some foreign taste
terrible destructive
all I can ask
it sits there
bitter --- harsh
i can not bear it 
no more
You did not even 
leave me dignity
just sent it 
electronically 
some simple words
left, destroying
all that i had
now I sit
here -- feeling 
the pain, the silence
empty -- consuming me
some desolate dragon
last of his race
wanting so much
to destroy -- left with
nothing... just this 
empty lonely feeling
and the simple
question
why...
