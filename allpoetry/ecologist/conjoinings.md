tHE Color 
     of her 

      HEART

paints crimson hues

 amongst 
        the 

   AMBERS 
          + 
            GREENS

lining the boulevard
   where

    BUTTERFLIES

play + dream

             of 

                  lOVERS

lying in the long grass
       beneath

          SPANS

 of sky 
         without end, 
 a roof
        for their

HOUSE Of BEING

where 
    
      kisses 

             + 
 
                caresses

silently, sensuously 
                       effortlessly

          MERGE

on into the other
    bleeding, blending
                       coalescing

with breath

     BECOMING

all   
       +
             nothing

while the color of
      her 
          heart

    BLOSSOMS

her flower

          your flower

a choice


     CHOOSE WELL
