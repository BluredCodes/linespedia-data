liquid Smoke 
     hung, mistlike

in the spaces

     BEtweEN
              those

reminisces 
        + 
       cravings

      of

a butterfly
         sipping

     NECTAR

hanging sweetly
   from,
    
     MOIST
       LIPS

wanting to be 

    TASTED

 by hungry 
    touches

desiring 
       eyes
