title: "watching"

Butterflies on bed sheets/ skyline...eclipsed/ by cerulean night/ and voracious cicadas/ barking... after 17 years/ of silent hunger//Breathe...in the echoes/ of lightning torn trees/ screaming to the hollow dark/ ...and the whine/ of rubber on asphalt//scars...furrow the surface/ of my face/ lines...charting the movement/ of life/ through faceless, nameless/ children.../proving themselves.
