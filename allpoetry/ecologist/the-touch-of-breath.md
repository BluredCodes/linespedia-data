The Touch of BreathSlip into meas breathon a winters night... I feel youwrap about me -- touchingall there is to feel ethereal, surrealbeyond sensationI feel you enthrone...yourself in meregality --inherent... to the beautyof youbecomes the essenceof my beingI reach outyearningly... for beautywithin your touch --within you, I feelthe touch -- your touchevery minute, secondfreezing timeyou wrap yourselfabout me... in the quietof morningPenetrating --- the darknessof my -- shattered worldremaking all...in timetouch me for eternityas you touch me now...--like your breath...touches me
