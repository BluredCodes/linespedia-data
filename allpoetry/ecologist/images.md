title: "images"

Chaotic, random, jumbled
beings hanging
by a thread -- of dignity
leaves fluttering, falling
disjointed echoes
of humanity...
carnations in a field -- of roses

twisting, turning, tumbling
further into you
observations, reflections
mirrored in the waft
of feathers
falling from mars
or venus -- meteors

Breath, dropping, sliding
cementitiously into the pool
icicles -- words to pierce
my breast, moonbeam kinves
paring, baring
the drift of time
to Janus's scrutiny.
