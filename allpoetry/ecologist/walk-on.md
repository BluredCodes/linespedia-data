---
title: "Walk on"
writer: "ecologist"

Feel the silence
embrace you... in the darkness
- waiting for your touch
2:00 am walks
along vaulted streets -- cicadas
drilling into my sanity
finding you, wrapped in me
nightmare insanity
conformity caught... in
the blackness of ... an
empty soul, wishing
for freedom -- to rip
it apart
fill it with riotous
longing, desire
touched -- by the painting of
wheels, roads, color
filtering through passion
lying bereft ... upon the dead
night -- nothing remains
silence
and taxicabs at 3:00 am
watching the hookers
go home, with the johns
the police caught
watching
I am insane, driven into
the arms of delerium
by wanton desire ... for
an unattainable woman, unseen
...does she exist, beyond 
my mind -- I wander 
through dingy night, touching
nothing...hearing all
night sounds
become my reality
a feather on a tepid 
black pond
drifting in and out
waiting for something
to catch me, hold me
in this darkened night
of my soul
nothing does
so I walk on...
