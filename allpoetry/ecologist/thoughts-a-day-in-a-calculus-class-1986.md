Boring, unintentional
assassination 
character bled out
by arthritic words
scrambled thoughts
give rise
to chocolate lips
purple skies
ghosts -- of yellow 
beeswax, haunting 
the hallowed halls
of businessdom
cataracts -- of listless
bubblegum
popping to the bounce
of teenage girls
walking to the sound 
of Lauper
and Jackson
Michael -- his name
Cyndi -- hers
facsimile
reality 
or just a chaotic night
mare -- horses without 
riders going nowhere
should they
who cares -- do you
thoughtless ideas
lacking guile -- or guidance
wandering
into the black night
my soul
searching 
for the home
of the pineapple tree
what is home
Orions belt --holds
the question or is it 
the answer
to the riddle
of whom I should be
who I am 
what I am 
who cares
not you 
to you I am just
words 
scattered 
across this page
hiding---- behind
grey electrons
and purple 
mushrooms


Math -- the numbers keep
coming -- or is that cumming
70% effective --- 90% effective
double the run 
twice the fun
English the language 
or the game
what is the difference
semantics
perhaps
listening -- the banter
flies
fecal matter
from under the outhouse
sweet smell to a 
gardener -- 
Chemistry -- or insanity
not sure, induce me
my mind
into something more "realistic"
curious -- is reality 
part of chemicality
thinking -- Rust is life
or life is rust
that is it -- life is rust 
eating things 
perpetually -- some dynastic
dragon...consuming 
everything
including the tin
man's tears
just boring
and stable 
acceptable yet contemptible
nice to think 
about
boring and stable
acceptable -- contemptibleOriginal ideas from 1986 -- added to and finished in 2002 after a rather interesting late-nite conversation
