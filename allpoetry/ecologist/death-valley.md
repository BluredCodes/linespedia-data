Dreams  fall

     Pounded

 into oblivion by

    GRAVITIES
         WEIGHT
 
      a feather's tears
   lying in life's

     GUTTER

Leaves --- turning to amber
     to rust

        FALLING

into the echo
           of me

   dying, 

           dying, 
  
                 dying,

in the wasteland

          WASTELAND

of anhilation

         where even ghosts

 fade ....
          with time.
