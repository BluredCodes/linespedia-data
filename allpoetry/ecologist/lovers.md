Lovers

Whispers in the morning
caress the softness....of you
stillness, calling
the breath...of a thousand hearts
touch....light echoes
race across the depths
of us....calling out
to the night skies
our worlds merge
become one....deep purple
pulling us...together
into each other
blood, sinew, flesh, 
life, essence, breath

hands reach out...touch
feeling tentatively.....ax skin
into the being.....held 
in the quiet...in the dream
reality or fantasy
no difference......no delineation
we are one
caught in the vortex of this....our love
our essence floods us
inundates us.....leaves us
taut in each other.....nerves

fill me, fill you.....who are we
echoes of each other
blown on the breeze
in time......with Gaia's breath
recreating ourselves
in the still night
holding me, holding you
kisses.....become brushstrokes
lines to color
i am you, you are I
we are us....in the shimmering night

I reach into you....into us
find the center of our essence
held in corporeal form 
our bodies......become one 
undefined...by our forms 
I am you, you are I 
breath....in one breath 
merged...together....unified 
one being, one essence 
two entities....one love 
in our unity, universe
