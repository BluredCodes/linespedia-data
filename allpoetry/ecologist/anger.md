anger... supperates--- seeps from open wounds screams at empty heartsin bellowing heat...decries its vituperativeness---to unseeing eyesfalls from a screaming -- earthmy body shuddersflies aparta planet...in its death throwsturning to a skeletonwhose fingers reach outto pull...blinded eyes from broken socketsin righteous vindication
