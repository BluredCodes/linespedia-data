the last morning -- your scenthung in the airroses in Decemberwhite nightshanging.... one last breathof youfloats into my soula final drum-beathungerlying last nighton sheetsof silk.. or was it Satinwho knows, who caresyou leftempty tears … staining a blue pillow casethe skywhimsical, thoughtlessplaying, upon nights beating heart.
