I watch... nightdecaybecome --- mute grayparabolic thoughts...rippedawayclouds, after the stormlinear dreams -- turn intocircular reasonstypically Cartesianin their fallacyI am haunted, huntedby ambiguity, bywordswithout definitionprostitutional sex...meaningless, advancing...thesouls corruptionwalls close indankness, darknesspermeate, suffuse themselvesinto me... altering allI seea hall of mirrors, --bendingtwisting, - all aboutinto illusion...perhaps a nightmare, enhancedby fear
