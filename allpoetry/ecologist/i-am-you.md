---
title: "I am you"
writer: "ecologist"
slug: "i-am-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/14196-I-am-you-by-ecologist"
---

Water, I am water
around you, in you
caught...by your 
dream...your echo
drifting in and out
floating through
the center of you

Scent, I am scent
the sweet soft perfume
haunting you...in 
time, space ... you
desires antiphon
building from tremolo...
to crescendo in you

Light, I am light
touching you 
caressing you, a flickering
announcement...breath 
writing on your face
in silence, in...
light, in you

Hunger, I am hunger
written...across you
in pangs, ardor...
for more of something
waiting...to find
it, searching the world
for you

I am you, in you
without you, all of 
you, echoes of you
haunting you...breathing
you, feeling you
touching you, wanting
you, I am you
