Candles...an empty cake burnt down...to nubsmute piles of paraffinmark -- dead agewasted for idiocy, whilst carbon filled the skytore ozone holes  we watchedirreverently --- glee at empty flamesdeed to a destitute worldheld in...unfeeling fingersdreams collapsing...aboutleaving gilded graves, --- polished headstonesreading RIP Gaia...a shattered hulking -- twisted planetscorned by...its caretakersthe wax...on the table toplittle silent globsstilled --- eternallyand stars bleed... dusty tearsthe requiem of the planets.
