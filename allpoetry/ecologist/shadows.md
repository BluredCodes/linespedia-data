Shadows 
Drape… the silence
Of you
Words, without place 
Fall from your tongue
Cluttering…
Your hungry heart
Waiting
For silence
To cloak your
Mind… freeing
Emotions held locked
With your errant voice

Embrace… the shadows
Free your voice
To the silence
Of…your starving
Heart
Dying…for love
To love
Without words
