Room to room …I walk --- through Silent echoes of usReflections ---Hanging on the walls Time passes ---Into the nightOf my soul --- I wanderAimlessly --- pondering theMirrored hauntings – of Forgotten considerationsLeft wanderingThrough my mind – some Wayfaring sailor…looking for homeA port he has lostFor too many trips ---At sea...homeless --once again
