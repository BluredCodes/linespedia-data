Erythroxylum coca...whitecandy - sears thru meice leading to hellmelting into - purple shadedojinga, coffee on icediamonds...water turninginto black flamesconsuming, devouringtapioca colored dreamsregurgitating neon... nightmares, of myfingers...melting into talons...ripping ax the chalkboardof your fleshin the tango...of time.couple of things -- Erythroxylum coca is the name for the Cocaine plant and Ojinga is the Korean name for dried Squid.
