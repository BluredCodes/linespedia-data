---
title: "Loving you"
writer: "ecologist"
slug: "loving-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

that night, the dance
echoes drifting 
across the night 
sky --- filling 
my dreams, thoughts
ideas -- reality
holding you
in dawns light
watching gold
fill the sky
fill your hair
memories
surging, pulsing
through my blood
body, you and I 
becoming one
slowly -- inexorably
inevitably
merging into each 
other -- paint on a 
canvas of many colors
blending

feeling you
melt into me
I loose myself
not caring -- nor
wanting to
I feel the touch 
of you -- become
the fingers floating
over skin 
velvet, silken
smooth
nothing can
make me leave
nothin will
I am here
still 
now -- part of you
through
the eternity 
of this fragile
world
holding you
even today
