Tainted love

falls 
      d
        o 
          w
            n 
                - spent-

 in the moment
   
      MONEY

for the scene painted
   in violet hues

 - against - 
      the velvet curtain

  of 

         TIME

torn down from 
      its pulpit of
  
         HONOR

fed to the Christians
      - or lions -

an altruism, or

       ANATHEMA

of pangyric cavortations
      around the

THOUGHT TEMPLE

    where ideas
      birthed - cremated

are thrown to the 
                  three-headed dog

       CEREBUS

stalking all

     who come to the 

                RIVER

to partake of life
     slaking their

        THIRSTS

    on dead, dying 

          LOVE
