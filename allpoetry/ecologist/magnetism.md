We swim into each otherpulled...magnetically, throughtime and spacelife's murky watersclear, a cloudless nightstarry, brightmy fingers...patternyour skinwith lines -- of silentadmirationfeel... my touchcaress the velvet of you...fingertipsof brilliance -- to pullskeins...out of your skinmy glittering emeraldlamp.. in ebon darkI delve into you...searching for -- finding the passion of uscolored throughoutyour soul...my torchfeel me..pulsein you, frenzied,driven... a maddenedsoul, bereftshreft of guidancesearching, reachingfor a polestarfor home
