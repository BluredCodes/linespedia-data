---
title: "My Abyss"
writer: "ecologist"

Look into these eyes
feel... death seep
slowly into .. the 
butterfly valiantly
vainly -- beating its
wings, a heart pulse
fading, falling deeper
into this abyss... a 
lightless soul
searching, reaching
grasping ...for 
redemption always
beyond... the reach
of broken - skeletal
fingers
