Night falls  -- engulfs medark ... becomes ebon blackjet -- falls upon me eyes begin to diescreaming...to the nothingnesssurrounding me --driving me deeperinto the nightcoalescing about mefeeling itpalpable -- clothsmooth silkencloying about mestealing mea thief, with softskeletal hands my heart poundsdrum-beats swallowed -- by the darknight ... draining mewith silent screams haunted by nightmareby dervishes --
