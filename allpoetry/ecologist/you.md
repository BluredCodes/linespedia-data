title: "You"
writer: "ecologist"
slug: "you"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

drifting 
in --- out
feeling... the 
slow ussurations
of you
watching the 
rise --- fall
your breast
in soft light
wishing
to absorb 
this moment
some surreal 
Dali painting
hanging 
in my mind
while
the wind blows
outside
turning
all into a 
Frost word-painting
watching
your breast
rise -- fall
in slow ussurations
and the 
painting
drifts
in and out
tidal
