Days...words...thoughts
merging silently
effortlessly -- into
one another...heartbeats
resounding, blurring
tears without origin
endlessly falling down
ax the pages 
of a life without
form, shape...
hands reach out
grasping the silence, 
the moment of truth
finding... the rusted
edge of a damoclean
sword...cutting 
into flesh, into 
screams -- the sound 
merging
in my head
