---
title: "Love's Death"
writer: "ecologist"

My tears hang

lying in words

dying -- on a page

waiting .. haunted 

by the feeling

unfinished

of something 

left -- undone

hiding in plain site

feeling crystals

shattering -- within

my quiet soul

wanting to speak

feeling the words

crumble

upon my lips

unsaid 

pictures

you walking out...

the door

seeing

feeling

the void --- open 

beneath my feet

you leaving

never to return

watching

my sun --- explode

into

millions of flaming

fragments

of pain
