Winter hangs in 

    the HOLLOW

of  my heart

    NEBULOUS

echoing the tears

               of the 

       MIST

Haunted by the 

             FALL

of the Moon


Let the 
        DREAM

   CONSUME

your soul

          Ephemerality

the world

        DREAMS

of tomorrow,

lies caught in 

            TRANSCENDANT

shadows + reverberations

held captive
    by 
 
        the

    WIND
