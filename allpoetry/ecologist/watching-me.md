Watching me

       Swirl

spiral down the 
    drain in reverberations

  REVERBERATIONS 
        +
       REFLECTIONS

of you --- haunting me
in

   echoes

        echoes
 
              echoes

of times, places
   faces

         seen 
in shadows, nightmares

yesterday, today

     TOMORROW

the sounds perceptive
   in the shadows

gentle, soft, sensual

    breathing, the swish
  and swirl
        life .... tumbling

from      eyes


         EYES

observing, stealing
      preserving

the memory
     in Kaleidoscopic
   multi-colored


      nERVE Pulses

showering the cortex
     with ---- 
          halucinogenic

        EXPLOSIONS

of fireworks in 
      faceted gems
          ----- your EYES ----

eyes ..... watching 
   me


      SWIRL
