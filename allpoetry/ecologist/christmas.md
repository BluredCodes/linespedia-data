Christmas
Icicles hanging --- silently
Marking time … alone 
Nothing else –silence 
Permeates my bones
Breaking, falling tears
Into the empty black
Of night --- feel the heart
Beat, fail – k-tump, k-tump
k-tump silence black empty 
silence--- behind dead
voided eyes --- life without
hope, no happiness
tumbling further
down into the night
