fire -- she is fire
life, blazing 
a humor, without 
restraint -- exploding 
fireworks on life's 
page
I reach out -- try 
to touch the fire
feel heat search
my soul --- a wanderer
guided home 
by her light -- 
her brilliance
marks my soul
incandescently
with euphoria
joy -- a smile
hidden behind 
dark brown eyes
chestnut hair
she is fire
she is life
blazing brilliant
across nights sky
