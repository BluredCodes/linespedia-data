I remember the smellpart decay, part lifehanging --- perceptibly in the airan invisible curtainshrouding this placeforbidding, forewarningnightmares -- and ecstasyMy dreams piled up ..blankets, lying heapeduseless -- in the cornerthe irritating hum --- of fluorescent lampsa thousand annoying contemporary insectsDead charnaloffal -- of prostitutes,my dreams, where were you? what were you doingwhen my dreams were consumed -- by incandescent flaresof failed realityI walk --- across your form... a ghostunseen and unhearddying more...with each passing step...into anonymityone step moreI'm gone...
