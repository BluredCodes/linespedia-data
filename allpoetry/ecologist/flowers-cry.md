title: "flowers cry"
writer: "ecologist"

Memories, chained to falling tears
cascade 
down into the darkness
my night
nothing remains .... mossy walls
falling tears
die
upon the dark earth
like pain
knowing no release

touch my tears
feel them
taste the salt
feel the pain ... etched
into silence
of this soul

flowers
cry ... no one sees
the soft tears
fall...into the empty ground
withered on the vine
grapes shrivel
and I fade ... into
the darkness of your
unfinished heart.
