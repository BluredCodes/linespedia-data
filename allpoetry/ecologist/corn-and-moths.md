---
title: "corn and moths"
writer: "ecologist"
slug: "corn-and-moths"
source: "AllPoetry"

ears wide --- full
to catch gossip...all about
silken hair traps ...
her wings
restrains flight
seduces with aromas --- beyond desire
they talk, speak
little signals of...lust, 
passion --- fly between 
leading to consummation
an affair months -- 
in the making
seconds in the act
the moth and the corn
seduction without premise
just promulgation, ---
and procreation.
