Voiceless ----- Intercourse
intertwining
drifting into each other
water
life
echoes...giving way
to silence
rising into us
blood...life....water....
memories
transcendental
visions....hang upon my tongue
taste ....blinds my sight
leaving me mute
blind
haunted....by memories
by dreams.
