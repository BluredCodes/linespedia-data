I watch 
      the smoke

gather ----- hang

in the air

looming
 
       a 

          THUNDERCLOUD

WAITING

         WANTING

to br    e     ak

           upon my head

                    + 

                HEART

cataclysmically echhhhhhhhhoooooooooiiiiiiiiinnnnnnnnggggggggg

ZEUS 

      overthrowing 

                  his father

chaotic  strife 

                 of ages

lying upon 

       the pages, of

          TIME
