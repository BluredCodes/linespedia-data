Whisper into the wind
Dancing on the tip
Of your tongue
Soft, gentle in the night
Let it touch you
A lovers caress

Let me reach into you
Fill you with the sweetness
Of honey
Touching...the darkness --
Of you, with dawns first 
Kiss

Hold this touch, a 
...flower of soft,
subtle shadows
us caught in each other
together before the wind
and its whispers
