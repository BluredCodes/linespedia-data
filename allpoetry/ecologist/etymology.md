the birth 
of words
Ow, Wow, OOps
hidden
in 
the birth
of you
so many. so few
all, or nothing
lining
the sidewalks
the pews
of space
indecent, decent
or descent
I watch
stars, orbs
fall
spiders traps
sprung
in brilliant
explosive
sunlight
where
crystals shine
glittering
in darkest night
I am alone
lonely
watching night
consume, devour
