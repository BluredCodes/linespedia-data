riding the edge -- i plummet into you falling beyondthe edge of me into the dark nightof your hairthe softness of youmimic'd in lustrous beautynight, unfolds itselfin my fingers, run between my lipsyou cascade about mein silken stringscatch yourselfon mesome unseen knotsholding you to me in satin ecstasydevoured by timefalling into the centerof youlike night.
