Foucault's Pendulum rotating in --- the earth sun circled round man not...the center of the universe no need for empty tears Iconoclasm the only remainder Godlessness the only dictum we threw them all down trashed the essence...supernatural scorned those who believed without reason Deus ex MachinaChaos in civilization end begets the way existentialism and greed driving nature to...the extinctions brink  who cares not man he watches and... makes token gestures some manner of...remaking his destruction penitence and absolution in one action fallacy disjointed idealism lies at the end...of an empty road.
