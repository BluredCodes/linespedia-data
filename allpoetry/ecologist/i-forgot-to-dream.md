title: "I forgot to dream"

Today I forgot to dream
My world collapsed into the 
Dark night, ... of my hardened soul
I fell to the ground
Smelled dead flowers at your
Cold feet
Where the perfume lingers
Like tombstones amongst...blades
Of grass.  

I woke into the light
Cold, --- felt it embrace
Me 
In the silence you left behind
On the morning 
You left
I held the dark 
In my dead fingers
Felt it fall between 
Dead bones.
