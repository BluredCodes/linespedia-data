feel it dripfalling about youcloying youin its depthsit turns about clothing youin fine, spun linenssensual, commandingyou reach outtouch itreverentlya motherless childholding it closeyour sustenancein the darkfeeding yousilence brushes youfilling your soulwater in an urnheld in beingwhite silencecloses about youunbroken, uncrackedceaselessit rises intoyour embracedeaths last visionjust silence
