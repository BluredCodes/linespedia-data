---
title: "Black Holes"
writer: "ecologist"

Myst hangs
surreal --- drifting
across the tide
into your eyes
breath from heaven
holding life
in fragile hands
broken fingers
wandering
through glittering
embers --- of
night
caught on the 
precipice of time
tick-tock, tick-tock
black holes without
form --- holes
without eyes
to watch 
light be devoured
in silence
night -- without day
I am a black hole
