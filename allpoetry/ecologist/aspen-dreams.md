title: "Aspen  dreams"

I watch
       the
    
     ASPEN

ageing in silver

   MEMORIES

 in lines -- drawn 

against a shadow background

   LEAVING

TRACES of you 
               to linger

in the silence

            imbued  

within my soul --- SHADES

of you, 
 
        of me, 

              of us

     HANGING

in the space 

         between the shadows

of the ASPEN

CIRLCING 

the grove in which

we stood, 

        ALOOF

      from all about us

just in the aspen

of life, of dreams

                  of time

watching us  
               in the 

       SHADOWS
