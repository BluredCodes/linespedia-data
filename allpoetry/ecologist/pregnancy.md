Pregnancy
Hangs --- in the air
Immutable, an echo
Of life…death 
Aborted reality
Lost – tractless 
In the destiny 
Of silence
Palpable entity … without
Voice
I am, you are, it is
Pregnancy
Without thought
Without form
Hangs
On voiceless lips
While time 
Ticks on – in finite
Limit.
