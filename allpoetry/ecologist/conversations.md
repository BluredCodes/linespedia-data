RAPID

tick-tock, chitter-chatter
   nasal sibilants 
        with 

gu  tt  er  al  

     PLOSIVES

whipping through  --- the air
    in frenzied   ((Dances))

      r i i g t e e h r
       o l n   h   t e    (roiling the ether)

with 

      TURBULENCE

A  sCREAMING wake
    at 750 miles per hour

   your tears falling 
     - hitting - the 

          PAVEMENT

  gouging, scarring

     IMPACTS

         LEAVING

             C           S
               R       R
                 A   E
                   T

OF ambivalence
  
           + frustration

eternal reminders
     of

          INFIDELITY

+ arguments

left hanging, 
     unfin  ish   ed

as the screen door

       SLAMMED

   its echoed finality

  into my mind

a rifle bullet

           unhindered 

       =

            unhampered

life
     hanging ---- upon

a barely

        perceptible
           thread
