Water... dripsdown into her soulechoing sadness...painbutterfly with torn, shreddedwings, once necessary...become impediments to life ---life, short + fragilepassing, an instantPassenger pigeon...extincther last, cyanotic breathfalling ax crumbled lipsa gasp, grasping outwardstowards the shadowsof youth...stayed,frozen in time...
