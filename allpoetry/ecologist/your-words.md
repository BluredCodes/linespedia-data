---
title: "[Your words]"
writer: "ecologist"

Your words
cut -- left me
bleeding 
crying in the snow
wanting to die

my heart
broke -- leaving me
emotionless
dead in this cold world
wanting to know why

my world
crashed -- holding me
relentless
upon a pyre of flames
my heart and soul

my sanity
vanished -- freeing me
perpetually
to the insanity 
of a crushed soul

my love 
burned -- consuming me
eternally
in the flames
of past memories

My mind 
collapsed -- destroying me
repetitively
with every passing
day and night

now nothing remains
of me --- I am gone
tied eternally to 
an ever consuming
pyre of destroyed
love
