And so the twilight 

              CAME

Breathing fire into the night as Gatsby looked out at the ocean and watched.  
 
     
            THE SUNRISE

I stood there dreaming of the life and the beauty inherent in the world around me.  Looking at the silence hearing it wrap about me 


          HOLDING THE WORLD

MY WORLD

While my breath coalesces into you

              The being of

			You
Touching me in 

			With
    
                           Fingers of feathers

Drawing out my life --- bringing 

 				You and I 
     Sustenance 
                        
 In the glimpse

Of each other

          I am a dreamer, without strategy

                 Inable of enacting seduction 

                          I cannot reach 

The fire of your soul

				----Why should I----

Dreaming of you 

				Consumes me

In the half-light ---- I want to possess you

       Take life --- within you 

			A complimentary appendage

I hear you 
                 --- inside my mind 

wrapping around my synapses 
becoming me – entwined 

                              in my thoughts 
                FILLING ME

with

eSSential 
           NECESSARY

thoughts

I am what I am -- I said

you need a plan, 
                A STRATEGY
                           A GAME
                                   --- she said

Truth before games said I -- a tenet to my existence

rules I apply to myself and not to others

                       CANNOT --- be wrong

because I can only rule myself

BUT STILL I DREAM

                    TO FIND

someone for me 

                  TO FILL

THE   hollowness of my soul

when I watch 

                   the SUNRISE
