Let me taste of thy lips one brief kisssavor it through this eternal nightas a sweet dream never-endingtouching alabaster skinbe-jeweled with pearls saltylet me savor your essence softly caressedby moonlightamidst the breath of starshold me twixtyour breathless eyesupon a silent star-strung beachas the moon fadeswith the kiss of dawn.
