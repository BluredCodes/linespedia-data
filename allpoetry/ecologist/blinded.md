Madmen...with little toolsdrill, into my skullpour molten lead across my brain -- searing...this achewith a laser -- sealing, my vitriolic fateinto -- an empty, dark soulwithout a heart...I see with blind eyesher petulance...naivewhimsical and carelessit derides, scorns meplays...upon heart stringsa musicianwith a troubled...soul
