games played...to 
   demean, - artifice
echoed from facile - 
   - intactile --
surfaces, ghosts

of a reality longed 
   for ---
in the twilight

    TWILIGHT

a deer caught --- in 
  the headlights

      EYEBALLS

of a phantom car, train

dreaming of the next
   longed for
meal       
     to satisfy this

           PRETERNATURAL
hunger

hunger - foaming 
    on rabid lips

RABID LIPS

kissing, licking

my translucent skin

devouring my innocence
   with vapid glances
brushing my membranes

eroticism 
    in the context
lies hissing
             hidden by the verbifuge
hanging

PUNGENTLY

in the water
            circling 
incessantly --- down
    the drain


looking for, longing for .....
