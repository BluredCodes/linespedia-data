---
title: "Taste Me"
writer: "ecologist"
slug: "taste-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/10395-Taste-Me-by-ecologist"
---

I taste you      in my mind      on my lips      hunger,       rampant desire      floods my soul      I feel you       beside me      inside me      your love      liquid and facile      surrounds, en-folds me      takes me to your breast      I want you      steal you      giving to you       my self, essence      my being      in one moment
