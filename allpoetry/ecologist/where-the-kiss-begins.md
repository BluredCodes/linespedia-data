I want to taste your breasts
feel them burning
beneath my lips
in my mouth... 
to caress them with
my tongue...I want to--
feel you beside me
in the softness
of the morning

I want to wake
up -- finding you there
in the quiet
waiting silently -- sleeping
in the embracing...darkness
I want to lean over
kiss you softly
hear the air
escape -- gently 
from your mouth...

I want to touch you
intimately -- to release
the encaged fire
from within -- let it free
to consume us
in delirious rampaging
hunger...as we lie
in each other
waiting -- for dawn

let me touch you 
with hungry lips
hear the air with 
ardent ears...
feel my lips -- caress
you, everywhere
all parts of you
as this kiss --
