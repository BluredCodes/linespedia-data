I think about you
drive myself crazy
wish I had enough 
alcohol to forget
then I walk
wandering from 
edge to edge
thinking of the fall
wanting to question
the answer
desiring retribution
then
wanting to forget
nothing settles the 
mind
haunted, driven
derisive
fading to
melancholy
tears
wanting to get
drunk 
wanting to hide
finding nothing
to balm
the hurt
knowing nothing
will
just time
re-reading the letter
kicking myself
once again
emotions
flying pellmell
through
my chaotic mind
nothing 
just lost
knowing that 
the 10000 miles
did not helpventing destructive and self-destructive emotions
