The shattered glassfell...from bleeding handstinged, with empty dreamsthat screamedinto her shattered worldapocalyptic memoriesflayed...her nervesleft me...destitute of desirea lifeless mass, before you standsso much carbon...worthlesslystacked, molecule upon moleculein the blank emptinessof meshe walked...in my shadow for yearslike pure crystalI shatteredshe left me...brokenand bloodiedon a plain whitefloor
