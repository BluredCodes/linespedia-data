---
title: "Leprosy"
writer: "ecologist"
slug: "leprosy"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

feel it... creep
through you... some 
latent death
consuming you
inch by inch
nerve by nerve
whole, digestion without
consummation -- swallowing 
you piecemeal, in totality
devouring 
all of you --
feel your soul
burn, anger -- frustration
screams from every pore
fire etching --- your
being
some guided sword
to cut the edge...
from your soul
give you rebirth --
trust it not
leprosy -- consumes
all of you
leaves you empty
without hope
just practice...
