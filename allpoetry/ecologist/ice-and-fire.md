My frozen world
Ice as fire burns
carnubian, crimsom scars
ax the silent
black waters 
my night... claiming
all, leaving naught
in its wake 
of death, destruction
leaves drip down
tears of the trees
calling out
to an unanswering 
world... haunted by
icons, images
of lifeless gods
waiting to devour 
day...watching Icarus
fall into the hungry
sea --- while ice 
becomes fire
in glittering 
silent death
