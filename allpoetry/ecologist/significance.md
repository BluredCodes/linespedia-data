The drift
 
        --- clash

melee  round + 
          about

wisps of haze
     in a
  TWO
    DIMENSIONAL

  WORLD

clang through my

  mind

a derailed  train

  PLUNGING

into the dark 
          of you

the tide of people

lifts  ----  swirls
  +             +
eddies ----  currents

pull at the corners

  of 

     my 
    
         mind

drowning me
   with

INSIGNIFICANT

thoughts + ideas

running

     HELTER
        SKELTER

pandemonially 
     through this


shattered world
