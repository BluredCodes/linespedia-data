A star-kissed glance
touched her face
while the moon smiled upon her silver eyes
I tried to fly into the dark night
where none could touch me
but silence
and I hid under the mushroom
until Alice ate it
exposing me to unflattering light
unkempt and destroyed
a silent gnome
with a broken heart.
