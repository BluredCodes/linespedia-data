---
title: "Black death"
writer: "ecologist"
slug: "black-death"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

exposed --- lying
cold ... on the pavement
shadows play ax
my empty flesh
watching roaches ---crawl
into places -
indescribable 
my heart freezing
into the night
of your ---
dark soul

I died there
waiting...for night 
to end, no rescue,
resolution... 
the night faded
into day --- i left
walked away 
never looking back
dead --- dying
to all around
me

reborn--- I stood
upon the sand
black as ebon night
imprinted 
with nothing --- a shade
to all about ...
watching your world --- crumble
end... in a tidal wave
crashing down --
about your ears
in black night
