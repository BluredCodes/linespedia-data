Naked lovebellows... it cadence in purple heraldsand carnubian symphoniesembrace... my shadowcaramel tears flowerin your translucent visionand blood pounds through...the woman you areI remember...liquid honeyplaying axdelicate marbled beauty...as chocolate kisses latheredmy wild prisoner lipsand unmarred fleshtasted...the half-hiddenmoon of passions...lapsed memoriesI felt those kissesreach through...timeand space, a lifelineunerringly guided...into the darkened nightof my possessed...soul.
