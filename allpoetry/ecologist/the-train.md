motion
pushing, pulling 
back and forth
gently rocking us 
into each other
feeling the movement
pull me toward you
no hesitation, no 
break --- the soft 
smell of you
permeates
drifts in, around us
feeling the movement
pull me toward you
skin...soft, supple
warps about me
enclosing me, enveloping
us, -- feeling the train
moving beneath 
us
making love
