title: "Affections"
writer: "ecologist"
slug: "affections"

her fingers

    linger, on his lapel

            fold

    WANTING

to be his world, his 
       form

    his life

falling down, 

   one 
       step
            at 
               a 
                 time

to the beat of a
       melody (unheard)

untouched
   
          VIRGIN

her dream 
           or
                his dream

the scent of it
      wafts
            once again

in his 
        HEAD

touching, caressing
 
         the pulses of 

         HER

caught amidst the

             CHAOS

    of her
           caresses

lies the sensuality of 
          her

       TOUCH

in memories
            + 

               affections

of brushes, glances

           +
        
        EMBRACES
