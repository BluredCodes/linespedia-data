---
title: "Acidic love"
writer: "ecologist"
slug: "acidic-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

acid touches my mind 
lifts me high --- 
into the stratosphere
above contentiousness...beyond 
paltry earth
no chains to hold me 
I escape my binds
sail free
into your dreams
untouched
consumed by -- your hunger

elephants with pink ears
fly up -- into my nose
while dragons eat my feet
in grass...only an inch high

you are the dragon
you are the acid
you corrode my being -- essence
leave reality voided...
passionless life
while I float...down 
the gutters of life
drifting -- with the flotsam
about me
lying...in a chemical deluge
marking civilizations progress.
