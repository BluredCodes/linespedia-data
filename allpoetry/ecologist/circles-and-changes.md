Poetry lies ...
      on the floor

Decorated

in blood, black + inky

  slain, by dead


      IMAGINATIONS

and corrupt souls

Who will stand at the graveside
    --- in memoriam ---

of fallen words 
               detritus now

food ---- 
          for worms

I AM A WORM

         eating decay

           + dirt

a smorgasboard of
   tasteless delicacies

   devoured by the 

  gram, kilogram, tonne

of inconsequential 

    KALORIES

are energy bound
    as  ---- atomic

ATOMIC

particles, flitting, 
      slipping around
in the universe  -- creating


       POETRY

of action, form
coalescing into bodies

      BODIES

lying in unison -- producing
   poetry of being

       BEING

giving life to poetry

    once again
