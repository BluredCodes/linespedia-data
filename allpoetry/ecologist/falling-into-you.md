title: "Falling into you"

And I fall ---
Quickly, quietly, into you
A breath, a wave
Surreal
Touching you ---
As you bathe, in light

Tears falling down
Washing me
Loves soft rain
Filling me with calm
In nights ---
Star-filled embrace

Snow falls
Drifting into me
You --- filling this
Empty haunted house
Kisses hang ---
Upon these lips

Breath, in the silence
Sings --- in voiceless
Whispers, some ancient
Rhythm --- of life
Echoed in my veins
By thoughts of you
