crimson shards litter the floormemories of -- a heartunbroken, flash through my mindbleed my emotionsrob my soulwho cares --- no onethe emptiness drowns meleaves me dead, lifeless watching blank starspass before dead orbswhile the world turns effectless without reason, or purpose I curse my infectivity rail at furtive eyes … behind 300 dollar sunglassesyou turned --- the knife in my breasttortured my being with your ineptitudes and I screamed whyyour crimson kiss bled my tearsemptied them onto dead soil watch me crywatch me become -- the dearth of sadness upon the ---silent eyes that watch my soul fade…into ambiguity like the blood on the sanddrops washed into …the sea, unnoticed by any.
