Purple walls, Hangul etched in firerancid lucidity... the nightmarefolds -- into itself,echoes in midnight bluefilter through --- the heatinto cat yellow eyesBlood red pulses...pound intomy brain - haunted drivingreverberations of crimsonfading to purple - blue - greenwalls melting, movingin and outussurations ---of insanitywritten while under the influence of extreme heat -- 104 degrees F or 93 degrees C (whichever you understand) and nearly dead
