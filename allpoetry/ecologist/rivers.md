---
title: "Rivers"
writer: "ecologist"
slug: "rivers"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/10298-Rivers-by-ecologist"
---

rivers      tracks       across the desert      of her skin      etched by salt      lines      leaving echoes      of a pain       flooded soul      echoes       racing through      an unquiet soul      haunted      rivers rise      overflow banks      tracing lines      across this haunted plain      touch me       she cries      to an unhearing world      unnoticed      time flows      numbing pain      scars remain      on etched souls      rivers of tears
