title: "The Emerald Chalice"

"She had blue lips,
....Blue lips!"

They were cracked, peeling
a foreign sight
with her frozen
fingers......tightly curled,
wrapped around
the green chalice,
some priceless treasure,
.... drops of some
heady yellow liquid
remain

He bends over
......her unmoving form,
latex fingers ........ reaching out
he grasps
her emerald chalice
removes it from solid
fingers,
........... her last possession

He turns to his partner
"it was cold last night"
then sips his
steaming coffee
and burns his tongue.
