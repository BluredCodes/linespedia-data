Isolated ....birds
     in a rainstorm

HUDDLED

in the memory

      MEMORY

of a fallen 
       Leaf
           of sunny yesterdays

washed ... down the
soul, into night's 

        sodden

     THUMP

goes through the silence
  vanishing amidst

SEQUOIAN sentinels

Sentinals

standing guard
       over nymphoid
enchanters

waiting --- for cymbaline

   ECHOES

to fade, fail in the 

---vertiginous black ---

     CONSUMING 
her being, her light

     burning 
 brilliant, a nova

cigarette glow


dEAD - in space

     ISOLATION
