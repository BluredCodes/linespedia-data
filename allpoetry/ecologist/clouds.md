the clouds...lie on the horizonwhen the sky beamsbright red at nightdreams...founderon blood red shoressunset steals my soul...leaves me fragmentedempty, in the stygian worldof sand kisses, and empty stars, -- I look out...see the darklonging for lightto touch my cold soul...warm me...in its salty breathhope lies within...my faltering grasp
