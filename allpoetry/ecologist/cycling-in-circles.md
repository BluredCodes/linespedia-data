(Note this poem is originally written in a converging vortex)

Cycling in circles

Inside the vortex of my mind ... you will find the echoes of your dreams ---capitulate to these musings and find the circle of --- insanity as it chases you through the night of your soul.  Breathe the perfume of the night and feel it coalesce about your heart --- while the chains of inanity bind you forever to your broken dreams.  let them haunt your soul -- as you cry out to your beliefs, -- that do not answer your calls.  I am the sword hanging by a thread -- which can sunder your life from its reality.  watch your life be pulled down -- further into the ambience , of this vortex, deeper than night itself.  Wait for the night to end -- and find that it eats itself --- and pulls us all down...
