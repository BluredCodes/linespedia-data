Repetition
mirrors upon mirrors
falling into the night
incessant 
retelling
of thoughts, 
dreams, nightmares
quests -- without 
completion
just endless
repetetive droning
prayers, 
drifting before
snapping prayer 
ribbons --- high
above the 
Himalayas, or
next to 
the Mekong
while --- water
flows
repetitively
on
