Remember

I remember silence … hanging
Like dewdrops from petals
Time drifting endlessly
Along… silken water
Life, ending & beginning
A surreal montage
Of echoed actions

I remember people
Traversing life in wading
Boots… plodding inveterately
Into the ages, a Herculean
Effort – for each step

I remember the peace… it 
Came with the dawn + dusk
Reaching out… to guide you
Through the mazes, created
By the insanity of our world

I remember days where 
Night never happened…the world
Spinning, or not spinning
The light forever filtering
Down…into my soul
Bouncing around the silence
Of my day

I remember things
As I hear the sounds
Reach out, up … to charboil
My being, some barbecued meal
To fill…a gnat as it eats 
Through the world 
Of my soul

Mostly, I remember the silence.
