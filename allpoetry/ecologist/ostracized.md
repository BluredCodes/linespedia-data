---
title: "Ostracized"
writer: "ecologist"
slug: "ostracized"

I fall -- further
into the darkness
crumbling, tumbling
into nothing
tears slip -- roll 
down my cheek
pain, unshown
hides behind this
mask --- waiting for
the silence
to explode --- leaving
nothing, plain emptiness
to hold
a cracked...shattered
mind
ostracized by all
including memories
and nightmares

I look out --- through
dead eyes
into a bright...world
unforgiving, incompassionate
cold -- silver white 
light
breaches my soul
lasing through my mind
a guided missle
tearing --- apart 
anything there is 
of me
friends, enemies
all the same
I can't change 
them, it is all
the same
nothing changes

I go --- leaving 
nothing behind
a shadow --- quickly 
fading in light
dying -- my heart
failing... beating
less with each tic-toc
a metronome 
with no one... to
wind it anymore.
Ostracized and ignored.
