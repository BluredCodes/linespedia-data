title: "Turning inwards"

I sit here...
turning inwards
losing my self with
each beat...pulse
of music, my heart
the same...I feel 
my skull insubstantial
surreal...fingers
turn to water..pass
through me
I sink into...
through the table
pooling on the floor
quicksilver...liquid
gold, passing
between your fingers
disappearing with
each beat, pulse
of music...my 
heart
