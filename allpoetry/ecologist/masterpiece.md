title: "Masterpiece"
writer: "ecologist"

Water...seeps 
over me, in dreams 
of you ---
drifting...in and out
of sleep
memories,-- flood my soul
pictures cascading
into visions of you
becoming me...
in a moment
one touch...kiss
skin on skin
water beading, bending
into us...lyrical
leaving me...
wrapped in the silence
of you...

reach into me
find yourself
indelibly brushed, painted
across me
a Renoir landscape

I miss the green -- of you
floating, into .. my silence
your touch held me...
captive, in this 
your masterpiece
painted...by 
fingertips, hair
souls caressing souls
hungry to be touched, held
waiting... for human 
response
beyond the black ---
empty night
to the light
with water
seeping o'er me
in dreams...of
you

your masterpiece
