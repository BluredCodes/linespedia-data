Her Smooth, sweet honey...shimmers +floats --on her skinembracing my translucence ... tosilence my haunted nightwith her breathher embrace...fills mewith silvering echoesfrom a fairy tale songheard...only by a childas daylight kisses -- hersoft eyelids, ending my hidden , nightly reverieI drift -- into her soulfeeling,... my way throughthe tangled web of herdelving deeper ... into myselffinding my soul ---in her.
