---
title: "You Killed ME"
writer: "ecologist"

you cannot see
lines -- in my soul
trails, left 
by tears -- silent
reminders of 
a haunting echo
ringing its -- my -- death
nell... 
a silent bell
in the black 
rain of my soul
tolling out 
meting out its 
pained punishment
upon this dead
haunted soul
at midnight

my breath fails
flails at my body
grasping out 
to straws -- not finding
anything -- grass
blades cut my hands
bleeding salty tears
into the dying 
ground
pulling my life
from open cuts
I kiss with dead
lips ... dry
bled out lips
flavoured 
cherry blossoms
gone now

A heart beats
its final -- pulse
crushed in, by pain
not broken
just destroyed
subtly -- s gentle
soft twist
of a feathered
blade slashing
across its last 
final pulse
torn, tattered
I lay it out
on the dried dead
ground
no grave, no marker
just a dark
black spot
fading quietly
to brown -- dead flowers
