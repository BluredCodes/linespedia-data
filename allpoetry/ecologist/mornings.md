Whisper into the wind
Dancing on the tip
Of your tongue
Soft, gentle in the night
Let it touch you
Caress you as a lover

Let me reach into you
Fill you with the sweetness
Of honey
Touch … the darkness
Of you with dawns first 
Kiss
