the walls 

      REPEAT

   the hollows of 

      her BODY

a memory falling
 
     TUMBLING

into the void, left

            HUNGERING

for the touch, the 

      BREATH

of you, playing 
 
             TENTATIVELY

    over my skin 

the beat -- of the 

     MARIMBA

singing to your soul

the soft muted 
                REDS

becoming 
   
     AUBURN  hair

under the moonlight

with me.
