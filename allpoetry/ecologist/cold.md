title: "Cold"

I feel it       seeping into me      vibrations      rhythmically resounding      patterns of darkness      bound in the light      white cold      a blizzard       of worthlessness      screaming through       purging this night      black icicles      falling       tearing into       the whiteness       of me      finger-like knives      baring my bones      to the scouring      of the numb       wind      cut me      i do not bleed      ribbons of flesh      hang upon gleaming      frozen white bones      feel the cold      seep into you      an undesired friend      let it touch you      in the darkness      bare yourself to it      feel it scour you       clean, before the       watchers, in diamond       brilliance      can you trust it      honesty, unsearched for      is, hard, brutal      brilliant, gleaming      steel, cold
