---
title: "A thousand tears"
writer: "ecologist"
slug: "a-thousand-tears"
source: "AllPoetry"

a thousand tears      a thousand tears...      times river      flows over       your flushed skin      tremulously      like the breath       of gaia      bleeding life      into a dying earth      frailty      caught upon       the sword of man      dying ingnominiously      in front       of static eyed      cathode blinded children      sterilized to all about --       caught in the machine      keeping them alive      the iron lung      of civilization      feeding recirculated blood      through dialysis tubes      into the veins       of visionless      lusks      and a thousand tears      fall
