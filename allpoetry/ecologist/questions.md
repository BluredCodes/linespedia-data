title: "questions"

Tired, taunted
haunted---by echoes
reverberations
of self
within, without
everything and nothing
perfection --- found
in imperfect
thoughts, dreams
of fake, false
reality
I am, I was
difference
of perception
or reality
past, present, future
what is essential
all, nothing
questions
of what
or why
