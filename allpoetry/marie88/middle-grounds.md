sometimes those grounds are where the battle lies.
sides as differnt as day and night.
only a thin line divides the reasons to which we bear arms.

so hard to find, at some times, 
i'm probaly miles from it by now.
sometimes you don't know for sure if you really want 
to see the other side. 
it's like washing a window that hasn't been washed in 20 years
will you like what you see in the inside? 

after from  jumping from one extreme to the next,
that was ever so much fun.
now i'm flat on my butt, eyes tightly shut.
i refuse to look, for fear of what my middle grounds look like.

what do i have to loose?
what do i have to gain?
someone please tell me.
