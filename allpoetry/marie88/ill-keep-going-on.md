---
title: "I'll keep going on"
writer: "marie88"
slug: "ill-keep-going-on"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/45323-Ill-keep-going-on-by-marie88"
---

I can't seem to make myself turn around,
to look at what I left behind.
But look ahead at my new life without you.

And sometimes it's hard, so hard,
cause I wish that you could hold me one last time.

Praying to God I'll get through
without you.
But deep down
I know I could never go back.
I drove to far,
I tried to hard to make it work.
And I loved you long enough to finally to better.

And this time I am gone for good.
I am not turning around just to drive 
this same route soon again.
And just for the fun of it, 
if I'm not over you by Georgia then I'll head 
to Alabama and I'll go all the way to Santa Fe
to get over you.
