you wonder why i have a hard time telling you things
it is when you say mean things that cut deep,
it was like throwing salt into an open wound.
you hurt my feelings but that's all right, 
because you gotta learn to except me for who i am.
not who you wish me to be.
now that's unattractive, you not being okay with who i am.

i so wanted to tell you that's it is attractive to run your mouth 
like the energizer bunny, but that would have been hurt talking.
your mouth is only a part of you a part i chose to except with the rest of you.  why cant you do the same?
i am i that ugly?
somebody tell me how one can say such things? my own flesh and blood.
why, i should thank you, for your rude un-nice comments.

well you know what you can do with comments of me?
shove them where the sun don't shine and keep them there!!!
truely yours,
     unattractive marie88
