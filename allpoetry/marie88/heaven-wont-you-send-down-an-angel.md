Heaven won't you send
down an angel from the blue
cause I am in need of an angel from above.
Cause he struck without
a warning now 
I am drowning in my shame and sorrow.
It happened so fast, during
the whole time I prayed 
he'd go straight to hell.

Cause what he did
I cannot change, how
he walked away with a 
smirk upon his face.

My cloud of misery keeps
gettin' smaller day by day.
My angel tapped me on my shoulder
to let me know where she was.

I cannot change the
mistakes I made along the way.
Or how it took it me awhile 
for me to live in the present and not the past.
And I will never forget what happened.

Thanks to my angel
and the guidance I got from her.
For the strenght to go on, that I 
never knew I had before, thanks.

So heaven won't you 
send down an angel from
the blue...........
