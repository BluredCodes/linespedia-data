let me be
let me be wild as thee wind.
let me be free to roam.
let these chains that bind me be gone.
let be free of this....this unexplainable pain,
pain that won't let me be.
let me be of this fear that'll you'll leave and forget me.
let me free of the fact we're growing up and you must move on.
let me be free of the fact i can't hear your stories anymore.
let me be free of the fact that your moving on.
let me be free to except that my sister is leaving me too soon,
for my liking.
let me be free of the guilt of the nasty things i have ever said, i 
can't take it back, but god help me.
let me be free.
