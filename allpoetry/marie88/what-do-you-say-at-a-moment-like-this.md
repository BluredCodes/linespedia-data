What do you say at a moment like this?
     Do you tell it like it is or bite your tongue?
     I know where you've been,
     and it wasn't with me.
     It was the middle of the night when I reached out,
     only to find emptiness where you should have been. 
         
          Four hours later, cup of coffee, legs beneath me, I sit and wait.
     Ooohhh, how I waited for this moment all to long. 
     You came in the front door, your hair ruffled to a perfection
     only your lover could achieve.
         
         You stuttered and a stammered, but you couldn't think of an excuse 
     that hadn't been used.
         
         I got up just as the tears started to roll, all this time and for
     what?  
         It took you a moment before you noticed your suitcases.
     I know you'll be fine with your little fling, I'll be sure to send
     you the papers.
          Cause I cannot love you after this.....after.......
