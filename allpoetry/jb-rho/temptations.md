it's so tempting 

i know i shouldn't 

but it's hard not too 

look at her 

she's not helping at all 

i need to keep control 

need to remember my promise 

i can't 

what the hell is she doing? 

this is really tough 

don't know if i can hold myself back 

but i have to 

can't even watch her 

maybe just one lick...NO! 

no one will know 

no one will find out 

but i'll know 

i'll have to live with the guilt 

wish it didn't look so good 

wish my imagination would stop 

making me see, feel, and taste 

what it would be like 

ok...she's finally finished 

i don't think i could've watched her 

much longer without giving in 



that ice cream cone would've 
ruined my diet!For those of us with the dreaded New Year's resolutions.
