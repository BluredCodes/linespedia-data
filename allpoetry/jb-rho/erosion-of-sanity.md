title: "Erosion of Sanity"

Obsidian hue lingers

Time awaits all life

Chaotic script relegates

Single mind to feast

On obvious oxymoron

Ignorant, inane reaction

Thrust into turbulence

Reticent soul seeks 

Pure, undisturbed sanctity

Sanguine stature longs

For release among

Eclectic verity

Ubiquitous soul

Encounters loss of 

Reason, relativity hidden

Though logic rules

Sanctimonious minion

Bear down restlessly

Tirelessly erode

Banks of sanity

As the waves of harsh

Reality pounds away
