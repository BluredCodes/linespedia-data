Grabbing desperately at straws,

thoughts come in a trickle of 

shadows that melt if held too tight.

Fiery pain blossoms

then congeals into a 

speckled cluster of 

steaming syrupy liquid that

surreptitiously seeps into

half-healed, open wounds.

Inadvertent cracks form

along surface of stone heart

weakening Soul's resolve.

Humiliation gnaws relentlessly

with dull fangs at

possibility of torturous,

merciful end to pain.

Intermittent drops of 

comfort, wisdom, happiness

pelt window into mind

leisurely tormenting

captive spirit.I just don't know about this one...
