---
title: "A Look in the Mirror"
writer: "JB rho"
slug: "a-look-in-the-mirror"
source: "AllPoetry"

The biggest frauds I see

are not as big a fraud as me.

And even though I try

I always become a different guy.

Regardless of the day

I very rarely stay

the way I was before.

Isn't this an awful bore?

I never even know

which one of me will show.
