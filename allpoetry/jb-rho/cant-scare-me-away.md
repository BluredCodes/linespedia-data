---
title: "Can't Scare Me Away"
writer: "JB rho"
slug: "cant-scare-me-away"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/29197-Cant-Scare-Me-Away-by-JB-rho"
---

reach out to me
let me in
don't think about
the past 
just look within

i can see 
your hidden light
it shines so
wonderfully warm
and bright

let it out
share its gleam
never hold it in
let me hear you 
scream

will always be here
you can’t scare me away
i’ve chosen to be
your friend
so i’m here to staydedicated to all those who need a friend to talk to...and those who offer to listen without judgement
