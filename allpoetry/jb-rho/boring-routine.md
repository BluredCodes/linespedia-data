There is no point to this

whole boring routine.

Go to work to make money...

spend money...then go back

to work.

There's no challenge anymore.

I strive to do what?

Nothing!

Make more money...buy more stuff.

I don't even know why.

Nice car, nice house,

nice life...HA!

Who even cares anymore?

"The future is bright"

That's what some people say anyway.

Prove it!
