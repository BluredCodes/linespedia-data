---
title: "What I seek."
writer: "JB rho"

I seek the truth.
Why does it evade?
run and hide truth
for when I find you
I'll use you.

I seek love.
Can it be real?
keep coming in and out
of my life
for when I catch you love
I'll hold on tight.

I seek happiness.
Is it too much to ask?
continue teasing me
happiness
for when I finally have you
I'll not give you up.

I seek true love and happiness.
Is this really out there?
If it is and I stumble
upon it,
I'll never give it up.
