Brilliant azure sky gleams anew

as sun rises fervently casting

rays of light throughout 

Spring’s excited dream

leisurely awakens rose 

blossoms eager for the day.

Robins playfully hop from

tree to tree as they sing

harmoniously about the new

Day’s hypnotic beginning.
