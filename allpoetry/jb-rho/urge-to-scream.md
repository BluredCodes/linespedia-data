---
title: "Urge to Scream"
writer: "JB rho"
slug: "urge-to-scream"
source: "AllPoetry"

sitting alone 

time stops not for me

people move on 

excluding those they do not see


pounding head

tough decisions invade the mind

at times wishing I was dead

others can be so unkind


urge to scream

futile though it may be

euphoric dream

peaceful world I want to see


I'm sitting alone
with a pounding head
and an urge to scream.
