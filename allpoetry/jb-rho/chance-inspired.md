Ravenous hunger takes control of senses
yet determined by strength of will
ensues raging lust for comfort 
for attention, for unbridled affection.

A smile, a hug, a few kisses
wondrous world awakens 
emotional void slowly fills 
still reserved, still incredibly unnerved.

Passion brings down wall of protection
chaotic flights of confusion
wreak havoc within the soul 
a glance, take a frightening chance.

Earthly rewards revealed in truth
challenges met with determination
love emerges unscathed, assured
release desperation, accept inspiration.
