title: "Waiting for news"

Frightened here I sit and wait

for any news

of her fate.

She holds a section of my heart.

If she were to leave

it would tear apart.

Can’t bear the uselessness I feel.

Please I pray

for her to heal.

Don’t understand, it’s not fair!

I keep her in my heart

I hope she knows I care.Get well soon Jen...
