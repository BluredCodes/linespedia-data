Internal conflicts escalate 
Into all out war within
Sending spies to investigate
True nature
Leaving base unprotected
From attacking force
Of will

Recycled youth reaches
For past years
Fading into memory’s
Black hole
Into time’s oblivion
Never to reincarnate
In this world

Power clinches onto
Life with full
Strength, with entire
Weight of Soul
As I stand, 
Absorb, resist,
The temptation
To Quit.
