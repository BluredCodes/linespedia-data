title: "as I await your return"
writer: "JB rho"

your scent still lingers on the pillow

bringing memories of desire

lost in daydream of your curves

in visions of our bodies joined 

ecstasy, harmony, euphoric

waves cascade through us

as we release all stresses

giving in to passion 

without a look back at where we’ve been

without a thought of where we go

just being one, being together

losing ourselves in one another

freely, completely

i wrap my arms around the pillow

breath in deeply, longingly

as I await your return
