I wrote this funny little ditty in honor of bigmammajen


I saw jen’s ass today 
Just clicked on a link 
And there it lay 
On white porcelain 
Come what may. 
Buns of Steel? 
I’d have to say 
Not afraid to ask for more 
I may 
Ask jen to send 
More pics today.
