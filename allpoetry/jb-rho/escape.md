Again I'm off.

Another trip to

escape the

reality of life

even for just a few

days.

I reach for life 

tentatively...

for I fear the outcome

may not be what I

desire.

So I run

at every opportunity

to avoid stumbling 

headlong

into life's

treacherous cavern

where light,

engulfed by darkness,

seeks rescue...

and loneliness reigns.
