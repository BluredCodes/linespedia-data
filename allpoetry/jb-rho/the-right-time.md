---
title: "The Right Time"
writer: "JB rho"

Long lost love

unattainable yet 

just within reach

Clearly sings with

a glorious voice

urging and pleading

to “Receive Me!”

Take me away

from this loneliness

Oh how I long for 

your caress

how may I express

this need?

For your acceptance

For your acknowledgement

at least

give me a sign

make me believe 

this is the right

time for us.
