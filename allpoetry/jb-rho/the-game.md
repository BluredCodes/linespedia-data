The Game 

I don't understand the game. 
I've been playing it my whole life 
but i still don't get it. 
As an outsider looking in, 
i would be confused. 
We piss each other off 
at every opportunity 
and call it love. 
I must be missing something. 
When women meet nice guys, 
they say they are like a "brother". 
When they meet an ass, 
they "fall for him". 
And when asked what kind of 
man they want... 
Women always say they want a 
guy that will treat them right 
and take care of them. 
Men are no different. 
A beautiful woman can be a 
bitch twenty-four seven... 
and we'd treat her like a queen. 
We meet a nice woman, who may 
not be as good looking, and 
treat them like shit. 
Does anyone get it? 

This is a rhetorical question. Please don't answer. 

RHO
