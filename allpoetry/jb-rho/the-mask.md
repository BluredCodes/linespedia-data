---
title: "The Mask"
writer: "JB rho"
slug: "the-mask"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/28320-The-Mask-by-JB-rho"
---

the screen and keyboard
my mask
i hide behind
for a beast am i
repulsive
disgusting
socially outcast 
and alone.

yet behind this mask
i can release
what i have inside
and some accept
me for me.
i confidently 
speak my mind
and my opinion freely
flows.

as i strike the keys
i become poet or bard
artist, sometimes
hero
no thought of 
hiding in the shadows.

from the screen 
she gazes at me 
through those wonderful, dark,
piercing eyes.
she sees not my
ugliness.

behind this mask
beauty speaks to beast 
and listens to my 
words.
