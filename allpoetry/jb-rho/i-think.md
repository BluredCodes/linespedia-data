i think?

A rash of affection
infiltrates my skin
bringing laughter 
and joy
i think
how am i to know
it's been so long
ago since 
this has happened.
Frightened of the
meaning
anticipating with
nervous zeal
what could start.
It's time to 
brace the heart
for an unexplainably
bumpy ride.
I think...again
I think...it's been
so long ago.


ready for the ire
which walks with 
desire,
maybe so.
I think...
it's been so long.
passion precedes
lies, deceit,
mayhem.
chaos rules as
love unleashes
new, uncontrollable
emotion.
captivated by 
bliss
suspended on a
cloud,
all intelligence
is lost.
heart takes over 
mind stealthily
before defenses
can be forged
lost is the battle
though victory and
defeat may be one
given time, the
light may shine 
brighter than the 
sun.
I think...again
I think...it's been
so long ago.
