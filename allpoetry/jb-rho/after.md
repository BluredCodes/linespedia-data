title: "After"

You lay there like an

Angel sleeping

Without care or humility

Naked

Unashamed

Comfortable

I will not 

Cannot take my eyes off 

Your glistening skin

I trace your curves

Lightly with 

Trembling fingers

Trying not to

Wake you

Hoping I do

So we can

Enjoy each other

Again.
