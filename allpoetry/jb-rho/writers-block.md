---
title: "Writer's Block"
writer: "JB rho"
slug: "writers-block"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

i've lost all inspiration. 
there's nothing left to 
write about. 
everything in the world 
has been discovered or 
uncovered. 
there are millions of 
manuscripts written 
about everything 
anyone 
can comprehend. 
what is left for one to 
consider? 
there have been far greater 
writers before me. 
there will be greater 
writers after. 
everyone has something to 
say about everthing. 
it's all futile. 
why would anyone read 
anything i wrote? 
my opinion is just that, 
MY opinion. 
who cares? 
i'm nobody.
