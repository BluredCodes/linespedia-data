Lumber through the day

not knowing what’s

coming 

even though life’s telling you

that everything’s ok 

you know that 

you don’t feel that way

something’s missing 

moment by moment 

but what it is 

seems so hard to grasp

Then she looked into my eyes.  

Yes, maybe in a different

way completely 

from how I interpreted

it, but 

the memory of her gaze 

haunts me constantly  

Stunned by both

her beauty and grace.  

Frozen in her

stare as she 

looked up at me and smiled.

All I wanted to do was make her happy.
