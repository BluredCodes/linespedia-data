title: "Periphery of Flame"
writer: "JB rho"

Pale light of a
wintery desert
ignites a
periphery of 
flame around
a weary soul.

Surrounded,
the traveling Soul
grows cold
with regret 
and anguish.

Looking only
for a comfortable
place to rest,
he wanders endlessly.
