---
title: "Teach Me"
writer: "JB rho"
slug: "teach-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

Tell me what you want

Let me know what 

makes you feel

what makes your 

skin tingle

What heats up your

insides

Treat me like an

Ignorant man

Command me

use my Hands

my Face

my Lips and Tongue

to move you

to give you pleasure

This is my wish

to please you

I want to learn

what you want

from me

my Body

and my Soul

Teach me.
