Radiant moonbeams race through
Dusk’s dull oranges and browns 
eager to touch Earth’s accepting skin
to cool her down

Midnight blue engulfs the senses
as evening rolls into plain view
Moon gently pushing down the sun
“It’s my turn,” she says

Stars furtively, flirtatiously wink
allowing gazer a look into heaven
to ponder what the Sun may bring
when she returns.
