Haunting gaze jumps at me from the screen
I look away to prevent being hypnotized
Her wondrous eyes lock onto my soul 
Leaving not a route, a passage for escape.

Stare me down relentless, unyielding
Every flaw revealed, uncovered, realized
She unearths my heart flailing stubbornly
Not attuned to the music of her will.

Ravage my mind with attentive, inviting look
Starring in my daydreams, she leaves me breathless
Unable to dodge her powerful penetrating glance
Only hope, to let go, to succumb.

Alas, all is lost...hedonistic view prevails
Her intricacies relegate me to ponder worlds
The pleasure I receive from her, 
Addicted am I to her image.hmmm...i don't know about this one.
