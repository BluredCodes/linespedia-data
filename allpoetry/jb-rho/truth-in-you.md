title: "Truth in You"

Respect for self rests

Upon respect from others

Keeping all insecure

For no one wants

To find a cure

Responsible for me

Only I can see

The truth to all

This dribble

True respect earned

Through deeds and

Action deflated by

Obsession to gain it

Through manipulation

False pretense infiltrates

Trust in others for 

No one is ever true

Even to themselves

Always hiding from 

Each other

Never seeing the

Truth in you.there are good days at work...and then there was this one.
