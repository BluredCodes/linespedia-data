The aroma of her hair

The curve of her lips

The sound of her laughter is…

Infuriating ,

Frustrating,

Exhilarating.

How can anyone be so beautiful?

It’s just not fair.

The sound of her voice is 

Intoxicating.

I get lost in the gaze of

Her deep, dark eyes.

I long to see her smile at me…

To acknowledge that I’m 

Alive.
