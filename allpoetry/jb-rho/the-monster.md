Whether it's money or power, 
It’s still the same old green monster.
The one under the bed 
Or in the closet.
The one you thought daddy killed
A long time ago.

As long as there is inequality,
As long as there are social classes,
If we can’t get passed our own 
Petty differences, 
The monster lives.Ok...now I can get off my soapbox.
