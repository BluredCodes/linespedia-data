title: "Let Time Pass"

Love’s sweet odor wafts
softly across their path
White doves circle above
songs linger slowly down
Crystal blue waters
glisten calmly
Stone thrown by children
at play produce only ripples

Oblivious to beauty which
surrounds them
Continual gaze acts as 
blinders 
Interested only in lover’s eyes 
all else fade into background 
Locked in each others’ soul
trapped by colliding hearts
Neither wills accept less
than full attention

Sun touches glassy horizon
leaves most in awe
Lovers hold each other
no thought of release
Comfort encompasses
both content to sit 
To remain in the other’s 
embrace
To let time slowly pass
them by.
