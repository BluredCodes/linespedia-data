The greatest game
at least to me
involves a blocking scheme 
and passing tree.
Helmets, shoulder pads, coaches, and trainers
the players aren't just entertainers.
49 men all with one goal
to get to the "Big Show",
the "Super Bowl".
16 weeks of toil and trouble
banging heads
then back to the huddle.
The passing game and
running back.
Linebacker stunts,
good hit!
what a sack!
The fans go nuts.
They scream, they shout.
They do the wave then jump about.
A Bomb is tossed 
then intercepted.
The Head Coach screams
cause he's dejected.
There can be only one team
at the end of the year.
Holding the precious trophy 
and smiling from ear to ear.
What a game!
What a sport!
The fans all agree
Damn the season is way too short!Ok...I'm a dork...but I love this game.
