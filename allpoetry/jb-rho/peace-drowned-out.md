title: "Peace Drowned Out"

Listen to the serenity
Do you hear?
Quiet solitude and peace surround
Not a sound
can be distinguished 
In the din of silence.

This is an amusing dream that
Makes the waking world
A disappointment.
Chaos deafens 
And it’s peace that is drowned out.
Even if you shout from
The highest points.
