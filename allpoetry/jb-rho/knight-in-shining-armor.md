I want to be someone’s Knight
in Shining Armor
I want to be the one 
she wishes for
The one she brags about 
to her friends
I long to hold someone 
in my arms
For her to tell me that
I make her feel safe and warm
And that I’m the one 
SHE longs for TOO.

I want to be someone’s Knight
in Shining Armor
I want to provide her with a
Castle and call her Queen
To bring her a dragon’s head
To save her from the 
“Bad Guy”
I want her to call MY name
when she needs help
For ME to be the first one
she thinks about when she
Daydreams 

I want to be someone’s Knight
in Shining Armor
To come riding out of the 
fog during her darkest hour
and save the day
To shield her from the world’s
ills and transport her
safely to paradise
I want to be the Knight that she
longs for
The one the little girl in her still
believes will appear someday

I wait patiently for her
to discover me
To look deep inside this/my
ordinary exterior and see
that I am HER
