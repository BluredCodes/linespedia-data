---
title: "RUSH"
writer: "JB rho"
slug: "rush"

---

lost in the world around me 

people rush in and out of my life 

sometimes not stopping 

even for a breath 

always looking for something 

following whatever leads them 

around in circles it seems 

every time they stop or pause

the world passes them by 

so they hurry off into the sunset 

leaving me behind forever wondering

why?
