title: "Battle within"
writer: "JB rho"

Burdened by life
I cower
Frightened of Chaos
as it bands with 
Time
to erode this soul
Violence engulfs
this mind
this heart is bound 
by Hate’s chains
unable to escape 
the Wall of 
Agony
Alone…Hope
is unable to attempt
rescue
for the minions of
Chaos and Time 
present powerful foes.
Chaos rules with a 
fiery whip
Time with merciless
greed.Just trying something new...
