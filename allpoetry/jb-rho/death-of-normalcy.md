longing for my muse
constantly sending but
receiving not
time flows 
still undisrupted
seeking comfort yet
unfound
struggling to be 
unobstructed
relevance of past 
downcast and yet
unyielding
everything accomplished
all but forgotten
faces constantly 
deceiving
sly smiles and 
hollow praises
give raise to 
insignificant phrases
deathly afraid
flashes of truth
may detonate
unstable chemical
changes
seek not the warmth
of ordinary and safe
reach out 
walk with danger 
and unknown
hazardous companions
they may be
yet lead not
to the death of
