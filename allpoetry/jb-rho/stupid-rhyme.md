To someone I just met. 
I'll not soon forget 
your eyes or smile. 
Not for a while at least. 
You've unleashed a 
sleeping beast. 
You captivated me that day. 
In your own quiet way, 
you made me lonely. 
If not for only 
another chance to see 
your lovely face and hear 
your enchanting voice. 
I wish I had a choice 
in the matter. 
But now my brain is 
scattered, 
thinking about you all 
the time, 
hell, 
I even made up this 
stupid rhyme. 
I can't seem to get you 
out of my head. 
I toss and turn in bed. 
Enough said. 

I just hope we meet again. 

Rho
