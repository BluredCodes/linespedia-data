Constant indecision riddles 

Mind and soul

Creating layer of scar tissue

That surrounds the heart

Impenetrable to regular

Emotional euphoria

Only allowing pain to

Break the threshold

For pain allows mind

And soul to feel 

Something 

Anything

To pretend life

Exists in this

Form

In this body
