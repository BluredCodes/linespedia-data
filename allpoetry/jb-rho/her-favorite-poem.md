Singular voice instills
such splendor in
Life
capturing essence of 
warmth and comfort
with every word.
Tone and Pitch
incite feelings of 
harmony
Euphoria lingers
for a time
as wondrous 
prose escape her 
lips.

Sincerity and Acceptance
flow through her
like the soft, steady,
current of a summer
brook.
Wanting only to listen, 
I patiently await
the next 
Utterance
the final verse
of her favorite
Poem
that she 
has chosen
to Share
with me.
