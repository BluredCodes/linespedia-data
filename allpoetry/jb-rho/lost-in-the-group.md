---
title: "Lost in the Group"
writer: "JB rho"
slug: "lost-in-the-group"

Stuck in groupthink forever

believe what everyone

does 

Not able to create own

thoughts, beliefs based on

others’

Lost if alone

Questions of independence

unthinkable, frightening

keeping all in focus 

not willing to stray

To “Think out of the Box”

Creativity alien

concept 

too ridiculous 

Always ready to jump

onto new bandwagon

No consideration

of own safety

or happiness

Just content

with what works

for all
