title: "Earth's Equation"
writer: "JB rho"

Kaleidoscope visions ignite 
sequence of light bursts
through crystal prism
allowing multitude of colors
to cascade across
Horizon’s accepting canvas
perpetuating brush strokes 
of an artist

~~~**~~~

Hues strike out 
natural brightness encapsulates
energy released into
atmosphere disrupting attempts
of overcast sky 
to take control 
leaving colorful world
to reign 

~~~**~~~

Crimson, azure, auburn,
mix incessantly capturing
Life’s mysterious rainbow
sends Sun and Heavens 
future coefficient to
enable Earth’s equation
a relevant, undisputable,
Outcome.
