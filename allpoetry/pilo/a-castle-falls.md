Spinning in circles
on this terror-go-round
moving so fast
it dulls sight and sound

I hear your pitiful sobbing
I'm sorry... I feel so weak
I want to reach out and hold you
But I can't raise my arm or speak

Staring eyes now see memories
gratefully I picture your face
as an angel reaching to hold me
a movement of slow motion grace

An army lays siege to my body
I do struggle with this foe
must keep this home defended
Please Help Me... Don't let me go

I'm locked inside this castle
that once stood proud and tall
but with this relentless onslaught
Is it inevitable.. I should fall?

so tired.. so fragile and old
