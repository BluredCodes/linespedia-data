I'm Silent, I'm Deadly, I'm in your home right now.
I think I may just kill you, let me tell you how..

When the temperature rises, I multiply at speed..
Thousands then Millions all with a special need.
So Silent, So Deadly, So Transparent to the eye,
a need to get inside you, a wish to make you Die.

Meddling with nature wasn't very smart,
now this body's failing, no good grabbing for your heart.
Did you really think you could Contain Me? Train Me?
To only work for you! Nothing now can stop Us,
You'll see you what We can do...

Take Us to your Wise-Man, We've locked horns before,
But this time We are different, We are So.. So much more!
With protective deadly armour, we're staying where We are,
Try your drugs!Try your Screams! we're the toughest bugs so far!

So Silent! So Tough! So Deadly! You're right to make a fuss
This body's so much weaker, and it's all because of Us
We'll leave when We are ready, There's nothing you can do,
remember you are very silly people, Now I put an end to you!
