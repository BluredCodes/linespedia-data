I Felt Like A Raving Pervert
When BIG JOHNNY Came Out To Play..
Co's Deep Inside My Trousers
He Usually Likes  To Stay..

OK! So I've Been Drinking
But That's Really No Excuse..
For BIG JOHNNY To Be Hanging Out
Like The Neck Of A Strangled Goose.

The W.P.C was Sympathetic
As She Listened To My Pleas...
Then Suddenly I Realize
She's Dropping To Her Knees..

HOLD IT..! IT'S OBVIOUS..! 
I've Been Dreaming All This Time..
Any CHOPPER That BIG
Couldn't Possibly Be Mine.

My Willie's Nicknamed TINY
And Before You Ask Me Why..
It's The Only One I Know
You Can't See With The Naked Eye..

SO.. All This Talk Of FLASHING
Is Really Out Of Line...

WHO THE HELL GOES FLASHING
WITH A WILLIE THE SIZE OF MINE!!!
