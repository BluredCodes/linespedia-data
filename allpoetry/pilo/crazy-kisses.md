Crazy kissin’ for a lark,
Just crazy kissin’ in the park,
Just crazy kissin’ crazy.

Sh-lur-py love kisses,
I wish she were my misses,
Here comes another one, shlurp plop! 
“Try that little smacker on for size.”
You roll and blink your eyes, 
You let me touch your inner thighs.
Then tugging at my hair,
You French kiss me back.
I need to catch a breath,
Before another heart attack.
Tongue delves in, 
Flicking all about again,
Going backward north and south,
Caressin’ all around my mouth,
Those crazy, crazy kisses.

Chills running down my spine,
When you whisper you are mine,
As long as I can keep it up this time.
I’ve never felt so proud,
I love to tell the world out loud.
I love these crazy kisses
Love these crazy kisses in the park,
But wait with antici-pation,
For-after–dark!
  “muea-wa,”
“A little peck upon your perfect nose,”
It’s hard to find a guy, 
That looks so good in ladies clothes,
Crazy kissin’
Crazy kissin’ in the park,
Crazy kissin’ after dark,
Just crazy kissin’ crazy.
