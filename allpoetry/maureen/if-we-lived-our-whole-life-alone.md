If we lived our whole life alone

What kind of life would it be?



No family, no friend,

No one on whom we could depend



No dating, no mating,

No loving, no hating



No marriage, no passion,

No children, no fashion



It would be like living 

In The Twilight Zone,

If we lived our whole life alone.
