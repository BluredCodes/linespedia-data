Words, words, so many words,

So many voices needing to be heard!



Some make sense,

Some seem absurd!



Don't know where to draw the line,

The voice that I should hear

Is mine!



It tells me not to take

What others say 

So seriously.



People talking about dying

One day,

Then rallying mysteriously!



It all gets to be too much,

It's taking its toll



I think it's time to stop

Getting so involved



And tend to my own soul!I just needed to vent!
