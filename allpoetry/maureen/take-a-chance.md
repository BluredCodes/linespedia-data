Take a chance!
Life can be a ball!
Don't just get along
When you can have it all!

Take a chance!
You've got nothing to lose,
Except loneliness
And a mess of blues.

Take a chance!
Come on, get with it!
You just might have
The winning ticket!

Take a chance!
The next person that you meet
May be the one
Who will make your life complete!

If you want to find romance,

TAKE A CHANCE!
