title: "Can I...."
writer: "Maureen"

Can I be strong
when things go wrong
day after day?

Can I be wise
when I open my eyes
and see sorrow and pain?

Can I be glad
when I'm feeling sad
and lonely?

I can say this only....

I will try.  I will try!

It's very difficult to be an optimist when you are faced with calamities and sickness every day!
