---
title: "The Power of Love"
writer: "Maureen"
slug: "the-power-of-love"

---

Bask in the sunshine,
Don't stand in the rain.
When you lose focus,
There's nothing to gain.

When your mind is riddled
With fear and doubt
And you're worried about
How things will turn out...

Remember the times that
You felt the same way.
When nothing bad happened,
Fears faded away.

Believe in yourself,
Know that you are blessed.
With love as your guiding light,
Fear can be laid to rest.

When love is in your heart,
You can't go wrong.
It is fear that makes us weak,
It is love that makes us strong.
