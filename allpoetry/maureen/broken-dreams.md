title: "Broken Dreams"
writer: "Maureen"

Where did my hopes and dreams go
That day that I saw you?
I thought we'd sit down
And have a nice talk...
We did, and now we're through.

What was it about that girl,
The one who took you from me...
Was she lovely?
Tell me, were you lonely?

Don't you know that I'm crying?
Crying over broken dreams!
Don't you know that my nights
Had been filled with careful
Plans and schemes?

Plans that concerned our future,
Yours and mine alone...
And maybe a little curly head,
One we could call our own.

I may not know why you left me
When all is said and done,
But I'll always know, my darling,
For me, you're the only one.
