---
title: "The Me I Used To Be"
writer: "Maureen"
slug: "the-me-i-used-to-be"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/4611-The-Me-I-Used-To-Be-by-Maureen"
---

The passing years have taken with them
parts of me that I miss.
I'm not the person I once was,
others will attest to this.

I don't run to see the sun set,
I don't listen to music all day.
Music groups come and go..
I don't know their names
or the songs that they play.

I don't ride my bike,
I don't go for a walk,
I don't call friends 
on the phone just to talk.

I don't play games with children,
I don't play cards with friends,
I don't go to an amusement park
and wish for a day that never ends.

I don't make silly puns
to laugh at the groans they cause.
I no longer wait for Christmas
and gifts from Santa Claus.

I wonder what's become of me?
I'm still here, that's plain to see

but..

Every time I discover
I've closed another door,
I see the me I used to be
doesn't exist anymore.
