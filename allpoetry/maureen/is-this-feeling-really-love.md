---
title: "Is This Feeling Really Love?"
writer: "Maureen"
slug: "is-this-feeling-really-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

Chorus:
Tell me, why do I thrill
When you hold my hand?
My heart stands still,
I can't understand...
Is this feeling really love?

Verse:
Can't sleep, thinking about you
And of our dreams.
Will they come true?
I've just got to know...
Is this feeling really love?

Bridge:
So many, many people
Have felt the same as we,
Thinking they were meant for each other,
Only to find that their love was blind...
What if the same should happen to you and me?
Oh..Oh..Oh

Verse:
Tell me, why do I thrill
When you hold me tight?
Think of your kiss all through the night...
Is this feeling really love?

-Repeat Bridge-

Repeat Chorus:
Tell me, why do I thrill
When you hold my hand?
My heart stands still,
I can't understand...
Is this feeling really love?
-fading-
Is this feeling really love?
-intense-
Is This Feeling Really Love?
(last line slower and drawn out)
I believe this song would be classified as a "Ballad" (Easy Listening).
