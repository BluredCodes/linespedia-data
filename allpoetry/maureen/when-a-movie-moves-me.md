When a movie moves me....
I don't want it to end

For when the movie's over,
I must face my world again

It's like waking from a fantasy
that seems more real than life

Then going back to a world  
with naught but stress and strife.

               

Ah, but, when a movie moves me....

I share the heady feeling
of the ones who fall in love

I scoff and get impatient
when the shy guy needs a shove

I pretend that I live among
the fairest of the fair

Their lives look so much better
than the one that I must bear.

I feel the music as it plays,
enhancing every scene:

(Loud) angry tones; (Soft) woman moans;
romantic love song in between.

I'm transported to another world
I gladly pay the rent

For when a movie moves me....
