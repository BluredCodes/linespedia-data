Verse 1:
Does he care for me at all?
Was I just a fool to fall?
Should I wait here for his call,
or should I forget him?

Verse 2:
Can I go on in this way?
Will he want me if I stay,
or should I just go away
and try to forget him?

Bridge:
Is there any hope for me
or just a life of misery?
Am I too blind to see
that I should forget him?

Verse 3:
I guess I'll never know,
for I love him so,
I can't let him go...
I'll never forget him!

-Repeat Bridge and Verse 3-I wrote this song when I was a teenager.

It is a ballad "Easy Listening".

   Maureen*
