Who? Me?



I'm doing just fine,

Life is divine!



Thank you very much!



Haven't got a care,

Life's been more than fair!



Keep in touch!



What more can I say?

Everything's okay!



We'll do lunch!



Don't need any help at all

If I do, I'll call



I'm having a great day!



Why would I lie?  

Love you! Buh-Bye!Sometimes you have to read between the lines.



(A picture's worth a thousand words!)
