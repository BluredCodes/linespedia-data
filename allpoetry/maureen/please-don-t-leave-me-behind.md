Dear husband of mine,

You're my very best friend,

Life with you was so fine,

I never wanted it to end!



I felt secure until

The day you almost died.

It was such a bitter pill…

So many times I've cried!



Though you're still here with me,

You have a lot of pain!

You're not the man you used to be,

It's driving me insane!



All you want to do is sleep,

It's more than I can bear!

A sad vigil I must keep,

Watching you lying there.



It's very hard to concentrate,

These thoughts won't leave my mind:

Don't leave me alone...don't let that be my fate...

Please don't leave me behind!My husband was seriously injured in a car accident in June.

Since then, some days we have hope that he will recover,

other days we don't.
