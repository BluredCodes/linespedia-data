Another Christmas has passed, 
The wrapping paper stashed 
In boxes to be hauled away, 
But sweet memories will stay. 

I love when my family gets together, 
Sometimes it seems like it takes forever! 
We see each other once a year, 
And yesterday, that day was here! 

We all talked at once, had a lot to say, 
Expressing love, each in our own way! 
Shared laughter, hugs and good cheer 
Make me so happy this time of year! 

Surrounded by people I love, 
I prayed to God up above... 
"When it's time for me to pass away, 
I would like to leave on Christmas day!"I am blessed because I am able to enjoy visiting my family at Christmas without any stress about cooking, etc.  Since I don't get out much, seeing everyone on Christmas day is a real delight!
