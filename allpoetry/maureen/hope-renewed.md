The purple dusk mellows the spirit...
Makes one feel quite finite.
What had been a beautiful, endless day
Now fades to a quiet, endless night.







Each morning is a rebirth,
Fresh, bright and new.
Hope is once more in the heart,
Sprinkled like the morning dew.
