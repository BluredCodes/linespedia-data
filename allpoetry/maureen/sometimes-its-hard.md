title: "Sometimes It's Hard"

I depend upon my muse
to give me inspiration,
but when I want to amuse,
it takes some perspiration.

My poems may not be deep
but I am far from shallow.
Writing poetry serves to keep
my mind from lying fallow.
      
Hopefully, I'll write a poem
that makes your day today.
I pray someday I'll write a poem
that takes your breath away!

Rhyming words is my style,
at times, it's really tough,
but if I make one person smile,
that's reward enough. 

I'll keep on writing poetry,
although sometimes it's hard,
for when I die, I'd love to be
remembered as a bard.
Some days the words just flow,
some days it's just "no go".
Someday I hope to find
an enema for the mind!
