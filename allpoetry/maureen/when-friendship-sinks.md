---
title: "When Friendship Sinks!"
writer: "Maureen"
slug: "when-friendship-sinks"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Some friendships last,
some friendships don't,
some folks forgive,
some people won't.

When there's been a misunderstanding,
and a friend feels betrayed,
that's when a little understanding
goes a long, long way.

A house, once warm and inviting,
now fills you with despair.
The welcome sign has been taken down;
you're no longer wanted there.

What took a long time to build
can be destroyed in a day.
A heart infused with love
can get defused that way.

There's nothing you can say
and nothing you can do
to save a sinking friendship
when your friend won't talk to you.

Join me in a glass of beer,
let's have a round of drinks.
I'll drink to 'love' but not 'old friends'
'cause sometimes friendship stinks!
The worse thing that a friend can do is to suddenly stop talking to you.  When you don't even know what you did wrong, how can you make things right again?
