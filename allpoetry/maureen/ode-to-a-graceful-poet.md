His name is David,
but to you and me,
he's the sweet brother
in AP's family.

He claims he's unattractive...
but when I see his face,
I see a graceful poet
with amazing style and grace.
 
I wonder how this man
can see so much beauty
where others only see 
squalor and misery.

His captivating words
show us his great soul;
the contents exposed, 
a wonder to behold!

I pray that someday
his brilliant poetry
will be carved in stone
for all the world to see!


 They say, "Love is blind!"
I say, "Love sees what others fail to see."
