title: "No Greater Love! (Humor)"

I love your perfumed hair,
I love the clothes you wear,
Can't help it if I stare,
Your beauty is beyond compare!

No matter what you say or do
My love for you will always be true.
I want to spend my whole life through
Making beautiful memories with you!

You're the reason I'm living,
You keep on giving and giving!
And when I've felt like sinning,
You've always been so forgiving!

Each day, your face is the first face I see,
You've always been great company!
We make each other so very happy,
That face in the mirror and me!Written with tongue-in-cheek!
