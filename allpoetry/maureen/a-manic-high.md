---
title: "A Manic High"

When you look at things you do
as absolute perfection,
and you cannot conceive of
any form of rejection

When you envision someone
that you've never even seen,
And you're sure that person must be
the best! superb! supreme!

When there's never a doubt
about what you believe,
and it seems there is nothing
that you can't achieve

When there is nothing on earth
that you won't try...
You're well on your way
to a manic high!
A manic high is very similar to the feeling we experience when we fall in love!
