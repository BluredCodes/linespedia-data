title: "A Heart Made of Stone Can Never Break"
writer: "Maureen"

Not taking any chances 
With this fragile heart today, 
I still feel the pain 
Of what happened yesterday. 

He said, "You know I love you, 
But I have to go away. 
I need to spend some time alone, 
Please don't ask me to stay." 

I knew this day was coming, 
I could tell he didn't care, 
He wasn't listening to a word I said, 
It was as if I wasn't there. 

I tried to be the person 
That he wanted me to be, 
But it really was impossible 
To be anyone but me. 

(Every day we make a choice...
Should we stay or should we go?
Some days we feel we have to leave
If we intend to grow.)

(Time heals all wounds, they say.
"Please, time, heal mine", I prayed.)

Yesterday he made his choice, 
Today I am alone. 
I should be feeling....something?? 
NO!...My heart has turned to stone.
