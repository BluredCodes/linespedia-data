---
title: "No more war!"
writer: "Maureen"

Some people make you want to live,
Some make you want to die,
Some people love to hate
And I don't understand why.

There's so much hatred in the world today,
It upsets me more than I can say.
Unless hateful people start changing their ways,
None of us will live to see brighter days.

We're all in this together,
What affects one, affects us all,
Unless we learn to love each other,
Our civilization is going to fall.

There was terror in America today,
The terrorists had a field day.
Will this be the beginning of another world war?
Will the United States even the score?

I Pray for the people who died today,
I Pray that the hatred and killing will end,
I Pray that there will be no more war,
I Pray that what happened today will never happen again.
