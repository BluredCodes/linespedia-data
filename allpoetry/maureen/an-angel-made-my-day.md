She's pretty as a picture,
	although she's only ten,
	the first day that I saw her,
	I knew I'd found a friend.
	She comes from a poor family,
	I met her at their farm.
	When she smiled and spoke to me,
	I was captured by her charm.
	I knew from the beginning
	that she had won my heart,
	a very special little girl
	who touched me from the start.
	She came to me with flowers
	held behind her back,
	wanting to surprise me,
	catching me off track.
	I smiled from ear to ear
	as she handed me her gift,
	better than plastic surgery,
	(sure gave my face a lift!)
	That night, I took a picture
	of her bright bouquet...
	I will always remember whenan angel 'made my day'!
	Maureen Halasovski
