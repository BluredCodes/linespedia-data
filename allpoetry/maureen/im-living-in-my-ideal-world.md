I wake up each day with a smile on my face,
I reach for my lover's soft embrace,
My home's safe and warm, a very special place...
I'm living in my ideal world.

I've never lost a loved one, not one pet has died,
I never feel afraid, I've never even cried,
I've never hurt a soul, I couldn't if I tried...
I'm living in my ideal world.

I look in my mirror and see a pleasing sight,
I've always lived in peace, I've never had a fight,
My dreams are so much fun, I have one every night...
I'm living in my ideal world.

I never feel lonely, alone or in a crowd, 
I have faith in my beliefs, I'll say it out loud,
I've met my goals in life, I'm feeling very proud...
I'm living in my ideal world.

I'm the life of the party at any get-together,
I never feel discomfort in any kind of weather,
I love my life so much, I want to live forever...
I'm living in my ideal world.

2.  My personal Utopia
