~

While conversing with a friend
about this poetry site,
I talked about the stars we get
for the poems we write.

I said, "I make a list of those
who comment on my poetry,
so that I can do for them
what they did for me."

Then he made a comment
that didn't seem quite fair,
He said, "Sounds to me like 
you're playing solitaire."

Ever since I heard those words,
I haven't felt the same,
wondering if what I'm doing
amounts to no more than a game.

Sitting here making comments,
(Are they being read?)
Starting to believe
the words he said...

playing solitaire.

~
