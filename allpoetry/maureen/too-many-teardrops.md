title: "Too Many Teardrops"

Too many teardrops
Keep falling from my eyes,
Too many heartaches
Caused by all your lies

Too many times that
You proved you were untrue,
I made a big mistake
When I fell in love with you.

~

I thought I'd find another guy
Easier to tame,
But when I try with someone else,
I just don't feel the same.

~

Too many nights that I waited for your call,
Too many days I didn't see you at all,
Too many words spoken that were cruel,
I made a big mistake..you're history, you fool!
