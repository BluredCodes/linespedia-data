---
title: "Hopelessly In Love!"
writer: "Maureen"
slug: "hopelessly-in-love"

When emotions are in motion 
and your thoughts begin to stray, 
and you no longer see things 
in the same old dreary way 

When your heart begins to flutter 
like a butterfly in flight, 
and you shiver with excitement 
and you giggle with delight 

When you feel like doing somersaults 
just like a circus clown, 
and you greet the world wearing a smile 
instead of a weary frown 

When your day, once dark and gloomy, 
now seems glorious and bright, 
and you find you're wide-awake 
in the middle of the night 

When you arise with a song in your heart, 
giving thanks to God above, 
there is no doubt about it, 
you're hopelessly in love!I wrote this for the young at heart and for those who have never been in love.
