title: "~MISTY~"

I discovered Misty at a clinic for cats,

I thought she was so pretty!

A woman standing beside me said,

"Look at her, what a pity!"



Misty was very confused,

But was as friendly as could be.

When I sat and patted my lap,

She jumped right up on me.



Even though I knew she had problems,

I took her with me that day.

She knew she had finally found a home,

I knew she was with me to stay.



She was so sweet and gentle

And patient as could be,

She would sit by her food bowl and wait,

Without making a sound, for me.



I adopted a little kitten

That harassed her constantly.

Misty took it all in stride

(Didn't have a mean bone in her body).



I recently took her to the vet,

He told me she had a tumor,

She began losing a lot of weight,

And slowly got weaker and weaker.



When it appeared she was in pain,

And she let me know with a softly uttered mew,

I realized I had no choice,

I knew what I had to do.



I took her to the vet today,

And tried hard not to weep

As I picked her up and held her

And had Misty put to sleep.
