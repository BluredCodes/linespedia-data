title: "Believe In Me"

When everything goes wrong,
When you've sung your last song,
When all your other friends have walked away,
When you feel you can't make it one more day...

Believe in me.

I'll be strong,
I'll help you along,
I'll be your guide.
If you need me, I'll be right by your side...

Believe in me.
