I loved to dance the polka,
Twirling round and round,
When my mother spun me really fast,
My feet would leave the ground.

Then, when a special man said,
"May I have this dance?"
Listening to a lovely song,
I danced and found romance.

We square danced with our friends,
So much fun!  It was a riot!
I loved to yell "Yee-Haw!"
(And I used to be so quiet!)

Things are different now,
I'm sorry to say.
Our friends no longer call,
My Mom has passed away.

I have time to sit and think
And wonder what life is for.
I thought I knew the answer once.. 
But now, I don't dance anymore.
