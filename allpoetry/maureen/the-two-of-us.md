---
title: "The Two of Us"
writer: "Maureen"
slug: "the-two-of-us"

We don't go anywhere
so we never have to share
each other with the world.

Sure, we feel lonely
living this way,
but at least we can say
we have each other.

We're addicted to each other,
that's the plain and simple truth.
We're never really comfortable
unless we're under the same roof.

We want to die making love,
take our last breath together,
joined in death as we were in life,
forever and ever.

If death takes only one
and cruelly tears us apart;
the other will soon follow..
Cause of Death: a broken heart.
