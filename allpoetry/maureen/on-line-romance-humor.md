Her on-line romance was going great,
They were having lots of fun!
They had so much in common,
She felt her life had just begun.

He was sexy and smart
And sensitive, too.
Right from the start,
She knew this love was true.

He was so different
From the other guys,
He talked about HER,
Much to her surprise!

Then one day they decided to meet,
Her head was in a whirl,
That's when she discovered her ideal man
Was a beautiful, voluptuous girl!

She didn't know how to act at first,
She was in love with her, don't you see?
She said to herself, it could have been worse,
She sure looks good to me!

So what happened to their on-line romance?
To tell you is only fair;
They were so much in love, they went and got hitched,
In fact, they're a perfect pair!
