---
title: "I Dream"
writer: "Maureen"
slug: "i-dream"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/3344-I-Dream-by-Maureen"
---

I dream 
about the past
and how good things used to be

I dream
about the fun-filled days
and nights of ecstasy

I dream
about the time I told you,
you were my best friend

I dream
about the love we shared
before its tragic end

I dream
about the accident
that took your love from me

I wake up to reality

and that is when

I scream
