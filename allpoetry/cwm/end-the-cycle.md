A light, so bright,
shines through at night.
It is there,
were everything began.

But then,
the light dies,
and darkness,
sets it's shadow again.

Incomplete,
this self-less defeat,
causing the cycle,
to forever repeat.

Find a safe place,
to forever, fight the case,
of injustice and unfairness.

End the cycle,
end the cycle.

Make the change,
end the rage,
just turn,
this life to another page!

End the cycle,
