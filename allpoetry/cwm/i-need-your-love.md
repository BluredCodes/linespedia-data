your love,
i aim to please,
i want it,i need i,i love it.
Even though,you seem
to never notice,
or seem to care,
I will pay for your love,
for any amount of fare!
I will do anything,
for your love,
i would cross bridges,
wash your dishes,
answer any amount,
of your greatest wishes!

Your love,
is for me to depend,
your love,
i can fully comprehend.
Its rich,it heals me,
from any love itch.

Please don't ignore my craves,
or else i will be overcomed,
with rage,
No,no,
don't throw away tis page!
Your love i need,
let it grow in me,
like a grass seed,
your love,i wish,
for you to forever feed.Romantic poem.
