title: "Lets Not Fight , My Love"
writer: "Cwm"

Is this,
to much to be asked?
Is this,
to you,a hard task?
All i want,
is for us,
not to fight,
please for one night.
How are we going to live,
when all we have,
is resentment to give?

Are we just known,
for getting into fights,
on the phone?
Our love is hurting,
but i feel it turning,
but it can't
if we fight.
So,take that anger,
out of my sight!
Because on the next night,
I vow,
that we won't fight!Bad love verse.
