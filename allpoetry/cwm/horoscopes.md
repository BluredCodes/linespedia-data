Whats in store for me?
Will there be love,
for me to see?
Opposites attract,
and those signs re-act,
all because of the horoscopes,
truly,truly,wonderful facts!
There so true,
they give hope,for you,
to be able to start anew!
There i am,scramblind,fumbling,
through,they weekly t.v guide,
who cares who wrote this,
if its the truth or a lie!
As long as hope, remains to live,
my money, and my attention,
to astrologers,i will give!

Aries,taurus,gemini,caner,leo,
virgo,libra,scorpio,sagittarius,
capicorn,aquarius,pisces,
are all of the horoscopes names,
be as you can you,
there are very diffrent,and not the same.
Till proven false,and a shame,
i will continue to read,with sheer curiousness,
and learn to get the edge,
in the astrology game!Im a true believer now! Wecome home,future!
