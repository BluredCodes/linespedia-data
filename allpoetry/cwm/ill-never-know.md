I'll never know,
how you feel.
I'll never know,
how much you care.

I'll never know,
how much you think of me.
I'll never know,
how much your love me.

I'll never know,
im the only one.
I'll never know,
you plans for me.

I'll never know,
and it makes me,
cry out in sorrow!!!
I'll never know, even for tomorrow.

Why can't I see,
that you're really in love with me?
Is it because, we are not to be?
Or is this is our path, of destiny?

I'll never know,
i'll never know,
i'll never know,
even for tomorrow.

~CWM
