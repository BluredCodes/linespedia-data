I have nothing to prove,
im simply a man,
trying to live the very best,
nothing to prove,
im just trying to live life,
with full of zest!

Some confusion, and altercations,
have followed my way,
but with prayer, and time,
I will cast them away today!

Don't mistake me from,
the lives of other sinners,
treat me as though,
im new, a new beginner!

A mistake was made,
a mistake in which, i've laid,
a mistake in which i've sadly paid.
But enough,with this long, drawn out mayhem!
I show you respect, so give me some!!

Nothing to prove,
I still remain,
your resentment, may will drive you insane!
But I refuse to give, the answer,you long for.
For I have nothing to prove,
now, and FOREVERMORE!!!

~CWMA very personal poem, to Tommy, to my nephews mother, and to anyone who thinks diffrently of me. ~CWM
