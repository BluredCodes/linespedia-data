I know that i can change,
but it seems,
to get weary with my age.
I feel the pain,when it begins,
i won't feel it no more,
when I shall END!

Please,will you give me,
peace of mind,
if you did,itll be oh,
so kind,
if you can't don't waste your time,
as i know ill change,
and continue with my life,
of crime!

But Wait! I know that i can change,
i know i can, when i feel this rage,
forget it,cause i know its just a stage.
Theres the gun,its on the bed,
should i get it?
so ill be fed?
No need to answer,
cause my pain is cancer!

I know,that i can change,
i know i can ,when i feel this rage,
forget it cause its just a stage,
lifes never turn,of the same page.

Somehow, i know that can CHANGE!It could be a song. Something very personal
