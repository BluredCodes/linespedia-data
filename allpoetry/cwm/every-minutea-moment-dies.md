Every minute,a moment dies.
And that moment,wll die forever.
Every day is a new day,
and that day, will die forever.
Every kiss,is unlike,
that kiss before,
and one kiss,will fade,
fade into nothing more,
but a vague memory.

Our moments fade,
fade into a pool of memeories,
where they will swim,
into a sandy shore,
but only to be swept away,
by the memory-taker currents.

Our life,is a reflection,
of memories.
Memories of lifes pure joy,
and of lifes agonizing,regretful pain.
These too,shall die,
and gather along,
with other memories,
where it'll paint a picture,
of our life time,
our happiness,our pain.
Then it too,
shall fade,decomposed,
into fallen ashes,
because every minute,a moment dies.
