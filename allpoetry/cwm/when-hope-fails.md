When hope fails,
and when it sails,
to a distant shore,
there wll be no hope,
no hope no more.
Hope is what,
we live for,
hope continues,
to knock on our door,
it pleads,it begs,it weeps,
forits wish,to no be ignored.
Hope will last,till the end,
of our days,
then another seed will start to begin,
so your final hope will be able to stay.
