Drifting again,
to the same shore,
life of fright, begs for more.

sleep-less nights,
continue to awake,
the more, unease, I take.

Twitching,turning,
with my head in my palms,
growing grace, begs for open arms.

Wondering what to do,
the emotions bring a grey hue,
as more and more, the hearts tears to two.

The hate, I should not,
I never can talk things out,
all we do is ever shout!

Never-Ending cycle, remains,
less and less for us to gain,
ease the pain, joy strike again.

Cycle remains, to forever grow,
what remains, I don't know,
tomorrow waits, I know so.Part two of "Cycle" Series. I think this is better than first.
