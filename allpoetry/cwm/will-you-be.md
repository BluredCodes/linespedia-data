Will you be...

Will you be...
Well, how can I ask ?
I mean, will you say no ?
Will you laugh at my face ?

Will you be...
well, im afraid to ask,
your smile is to bright,
your laugh to charming.

Will you be...
I can't wait any longer,
im dying for you,
I need you on this day.

Will you be...
What? You know, what I want?
Yes! Thats it! What???
Will you be my Baby-sitter ???

Huh ???
Oh nevermind.

~ CWMTo bad, he asked the wrong person.
she's taken.
