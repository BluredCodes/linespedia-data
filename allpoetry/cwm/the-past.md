The past is gone,
the past is dead.
Its just a burning lingering,
memory, of regret, and sorrow.

But the past,
somehow opens the doors,
for tomorrow.
Learn from mistakes,
know what to partake,
for your own sake!

Last year,the past.
Your first kiss,the past.
That special birthday, the past.
That painful brake-up, the past.

Soon,the memories,
will gather together,
dividing from good to bad,
from east to west.
Then they'll meet,
greet,and retreat,
with a past,full of bleak.
