title: "Stop That Snoring! (Humor)"

I tried to sleep,
but I heard,
this loud repeat!

His mouth opened wide,
I saw his decayed teeth,
from his inside.

I put pillows, over my ears,
but this annoying sound,
I continued to hear!

Finally, I got up,
and covered his mouth,
with a cup.

I layed back down,
and quickly pushed him,
off the bed, onto the ground!

I laughed "ha no more."
But it got louder,
this was now a repetitious bore!

At 2:00 a.m, I finally shouted ,
"STOP WITH THAT IRRATATING SNORE!"

~CWMchildhood memories, from when me and my brother, had to sleep in the same room. As you can see, I couldn't sleep! ~CWM
