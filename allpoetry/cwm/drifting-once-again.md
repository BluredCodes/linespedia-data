Drifting once again,
when will life end,
come, and take me away.

Lonely grunting in pain,
day in and day out, I go insane,
Friends, greet me, with nothing to say.

Hopeless, blackness, invades,
drifting to the past, I can't save,
crying again, for tomorrow and today.

Fly way, to a diffrent land,
drift off to a diffrent plan,
where hopes and dreams lay.

Soaring above this imaginary line,
hope my path is hopeful but it's blind,
the winds blows, as I fall to a lonely stray.

~CWMDrifting with a journey full of bleak, that I wonder, that journey I shall seek! ~CWM
