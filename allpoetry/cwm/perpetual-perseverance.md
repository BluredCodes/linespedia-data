Persevere these obstacles,
persevere from your numereous fears,
perpetual perseverance, with-stands,
any arduous task.
Perseverance shines the suns hope,
it lights the glow of candles,on hopeless lives.
Persistence is a must,
holding on, till the last thread fails,
keep clutching,grasping, till the last breath.
Hold these truths,and rebuke those lies!
Cast away that fear,
fight away those tears.
For with perpetual perseverance,
any distorted storm,any distorted wind,
any distorted path, which seems to be obscure,
the path is open, surrounded by teeming hopes.
