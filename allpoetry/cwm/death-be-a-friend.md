Death be a friend,
give me,
a shoulder to lend.

Although i've died,
in sin,
you've been my friend,
to the very end.

You are my refuge,
to take me,
out of my pain,
you will give me peace,
when i'll go insane.

Death be kind,
ease my worried mind,
when the end comes,
of the end of time.

You've been here,
ever since i've begin,
death please, be a friend.A sentimental to look at death.
