Feelings and pains,
of angst and dread,
leads me nothing to gain,
the countless worries,
and dreadful pleagues,
all remain the same,
continuously threating me,
to be driven insane!
My muse has died,
that had caused me to have cried,
more turmoil,was given,
into me,in the inside. 

Romance failing,
our feelings sailing,
i see us fading,
and tears casading.

Rents over-due,
what else,is new,
my life full of black and blue.

Its torture!
Its torture!
Its mental torture!Lifes problems,well some of many :)
