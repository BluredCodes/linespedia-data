---
title: "Healing Us"
writer: "Cwm"

I'm trying to convince you,my love,
To me,you're as beautiful,
as Gods creation,of the white dove.
Healing us maybe impossible,
but even impossible dreams,
can be redeemed.
My hope,my crave for you,
as a friend,will never do.
I'm obsess,and depressed,
but baby,I long,
for your body to caress!
Healing us,is in our grasp,
so lets not that be passed.
We hold the outcome,of our destiny,
a destiny,I won't let fail,
or drown,on a sail,
to a distant trail.Don't give up,on a broken relationship,it can be healed by love.
