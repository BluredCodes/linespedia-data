We faded away,
once Our love was so strong,
what ever happen to us?
Why did our love have to die?

Flashbacks and memories,
flashbacks and memories,
of our once love,
is too much, too much agony.

I begged to God,for us to save,
for our sentinments,and attitudes,
to be well behaved.

I remember the first time,
that we kiss,
now my mind is bleeding,
for that thought,and memory,
to be dismissed.

Distractions,camed,
distractions pulled me away from you,
distractions is the blame,
for causing,causing,
our hearts to be filled with pain.

We faded away,
we faded away,
because,those feelings,
feelings just was'nt there.

After all of those sacrifices,
and pain that i've endured,
I was wishing and praying,
that you would certainly mature,
but no,your heart,
would have none of that,
so I just sat here,
and ignored those feelings,
not kowing,we were,
the ones,that made,
our love go fade.

We faded away,
un-saved love,for another day.

-CWMDon't you hate, when yourin love,then it just fades?
