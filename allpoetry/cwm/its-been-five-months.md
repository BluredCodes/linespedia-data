Its been five months,
and I can't believe,
im still here,
its been five months,
and last friday brought forth,
my greatest fear.

Five months ago,
I did'nt know,
that i'll love you so,
and I did'nt think loving you,
would give me so much sorrow.

Five months ago,
I just thought I would,
be your friend,
I did'nt know or have,
any intentions,
for romance to begin,
or you to give me your body,
for me to gracefully lend.

After five months,
I can't believe,
all of the travesty,
surrounding me.
The pain,has been to much,
to bear,and wasteed of time,
of precious care.

After five months,
I hope for a new begining,
and our friendsip,to never end.
But my hopes,are incomplete,
without your hope as well.

Its been five months,
our future unknown,
will my feelings for you,
be disowned?

Its been five months,
my trust,has been crushed,
my heart,has been torn apart.
Will you be able to repair it?
Can you change?
Can you love me?
Will you love me?
Can I trust you?
CAN I TRUST YOU ???

-CWM  09-05-01 - 02-05-02Written in perspective of my relationship. Love is cursed.
