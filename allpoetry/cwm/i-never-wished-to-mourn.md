I never wished to mourn,
for you,or anyone else.
I never wished to shed tears,
but I did,I surcomed to the dread.

I never wished to dream,
or to be depressed,to be alone,
alone in my comforting closket.
It seems,all that is left,
is my consulating diary,
a friend,who seemly adores my touch.

Despite vast gories,
and countless sucess,
some wishes remain ignored.
Its as if they've been looked upon,
the shot,with cruel intention.
where ever the wish cather is,
please circumvent its direction!
