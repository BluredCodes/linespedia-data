Although, it seems,
that hope is gone,
and life seems worth-less,
hope is here, waiting for you.

Remember,the good times,
is waiting next door.
Peace and closure is waiting for you,
to explore, and to receive.

Although, the darkness,
the pain, tragedy, sadness,
seems to trap the soul,
so is peace and joy within.

Although, those clouds,
will rain,
hope is not lost,
hope is everlasting.

Although, friends leave you,
theres always someone there,
that someone is yourself!

Your greatest love!

-CWM
