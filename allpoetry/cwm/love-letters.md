Love letters,
are messages of love,
from your heart.

Love letters,
keep us close,
when were apart.

Love letters,
keep us tight,
when your not here at night.

For however long,
i search for you,
my letters bring sentiments so true.

I'll be so sad,
with out in my arms,
or within my sweaty palms.

I wonder how you look,
will our love survive?

For now,i rely on love letters,
to send messages,
for the better.
