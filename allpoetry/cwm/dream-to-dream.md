Dream to dream,
wishes to wishes,
longing for happiness,
longing for peace.

Destroyed by the wrath,
destroyed by lifes fate.

My mind keeps dreaming,
my heart keeps wishing,
for hope, for peace.
Dream to dream, wishes to wishs.

Shackled by lack of faith,
neglected from failure.

Darkness then light,
life then death,
joy then pain,
happiness then sadness,
dreaming to reality.

Dream To DreamKeep dreaming!!! ~CWM
