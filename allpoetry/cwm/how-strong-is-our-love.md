How strong is our love?
How many mountains,
are we willing to climb?
How many tornadoes,
we will survive?

The earth is shattered,
of choice's and fate,
of hardship and tragedy.
But theres love,
love holds our hearts together.

Will we survive, family confusion?
Will survive these awful illusions?
Despite our love,
having Romeo and Juliet,
like pleagues,
our true test of love,
will come within our selves.

Can we survive our arguements?
Can we survive our resentment?
If we can,
I have yet, been able,
to answer such.
But hopefully, love,
true love, holds no limits.

~CWMMany questions, for unanswered questions, that make the path,
bleak ad unsure. ~CWM
