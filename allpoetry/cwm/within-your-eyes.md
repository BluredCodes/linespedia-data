title: "Within Your Eyes"
writer: "Cwm"

Theres deception,
crawling for me.       Verse 1
A trickery, I want,
to let flee.

But then,
I see the joys of love,
heavily bond,
from peace from above.     Verse 2

Confusing me again.
Is your soul,
for peace or sin?           Verse 3
When shall we begin or end?

Within your eyes,
I feel the pain,
within your eyes,            Chorus
I feel two-faced again.
Which side shall I choose?
You I don't want to lose,
cause within your eyes,
the surprise, leads to a disguise.

To our demise,
to our demise,            Ending chorus
to our demise,
from your disguise.Another song, to end song, repeat chorus again, then, leave off with, "To our demise."
