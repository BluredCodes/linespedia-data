One by one
each blood stained letter,
is counted.
Each letter,
revealing the victims pain.
Detectives stood,
and read,
the horrendous tragedies.
The torture,
that they could no longer bear,
the torture within there sadden hearts.
Each message,
was left with one,
trampled tear,
which was the signification,
of there gloomy suicide!
The detectives,
soon realized the messages,
but there realization,
was to late.
If only they knew,
three suicide victims,
layed coldly,next door.A crime scene.
