Sitting,grunting in pain,
our boring lives are driving,
us insane!
We laugh,and talk and watch time die,
but we know,
we really want to cry.
Ask  question,
an ill say "yes"
we should try living,
our lives the very best.
We can't do that watching time elapse,
we should keep living,
till we collapse.
hearing laughther,
of the school children ,up above
makes me want to starngle them with a glove!
the fun,the joy,the love,
they've shared,
i will begin to live like that,
I DECLARED!

The tasteless crumbs around my mouth,
describes my life,to live in the south.
Living here,till im old and gray,
i better stop watching time elaspe,
and live another day.  
- CWMTime is precious,use it!
