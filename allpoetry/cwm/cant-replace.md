---
title: "Can't Replace"
writer: "Cwm"

Can't replace,
that life you've lived,
the life that you've wasted.
Why death?
Why death from your own hands?
Who promoted such an act?

Can't replace,
you laying on the floor,
even if I tired,
to ignore that sight,
more and more.

Can't replace,
the times of joy,
when you were a happy boy.
Can't replace the love for you,
and love I wished,
you had for you too.

Can't replace,
your demise in hell,
where you'll burn and rot,
for the life,
of lives that have been wasted.

Even that, you can't replace!
