I too,beg forgivenss,
for those i've hurt,
i too,beg forgiveness,
from johnny and burt.

I've seen the tears,
and my greatest fear,
is when i pledge forgiveness,
that you won't be near.

I must release,
this gulit of mine,
the way i've hurted you,
could be considered a crime.

I would jump over mountains,
attempt to drown in water fountains,
if my forgiveness,your heart resist it,
then my pain,would be persistent
