The want for greed,
for their selfish need,
continues to grow,
and to proceed.

This selfish impluse,
leads to a toss,
of integeity,
of theft from lost.

Selfish Desire,
for more money to acquire,
which fuels the fire,
of a heavy liar.

What will lead of greed?
The selfish need to receive.
Is our morals up for stake?
Or just a numerous reason for hate?A look into the minds of those in the society.
