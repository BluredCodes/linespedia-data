Oh God,oh God,
I thought my love was gone.
Oh God,oh God,
i thought i did,my lover wrong.
Please Lord,please Lord,
keep us always,
and forever,
keep us always together.

My life would be nothing,
with us apart,
my body would just suffer,
along with my aching heart.
Oh God,oh God,
i'll pray to you always,
i'll worship you in all,
of my days,
if you give me,
my love,back again.

-CWMA prayer,and a heartfelt plead!
