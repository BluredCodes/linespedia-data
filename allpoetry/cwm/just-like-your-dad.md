"Loser!"
"Dead-beat!"
I cringe at those named repeats.
"It's not my fault!"
I screamed,
all along my innocence,
I have to redeem.
My foul speech,
that my mother did not teach,
sucks on my with bitter anger,
like a haunting leech.

The low values,
those failed commitments,
just makes me cower,
in my own existence.
Respect?
Respect what is found,
respect should abound,
my respect has been tossed to the ground!

Once again,
my frustrations begin,
all along I can't mend,
this unmoveable curse,
that im suppose to have,
because my father was so perverse.

~CWMThe opposite of Dreamweaver's poem, "Just Like His Dad"
