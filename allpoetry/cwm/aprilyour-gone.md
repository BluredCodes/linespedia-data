April,you've lefted me,
lifes uncontroled fate,
has destroyed us,once again.
I could'nt believe those words.
"Im leaving,im moving,we're separated."
Why? Damn it!
Fate cannot destroy my dreams,
it can't destroy my friendships.
Yet,reality,it can,and it does.
Fate has once again,ended painfully,
dreams shattered,hopes blown away.
Why? Why?
April,I like you,I need you,Your my friend.
You cannot go! Please!
Im alone,now.
My consulating adviser is gone.
My search of hope,has faltered.
Goodbye,and good luck.
Blessed be,for I enjoyed,
your divine spirit of kindness.
And hopefully,hopefully,
i'll find a replace,in yours.
