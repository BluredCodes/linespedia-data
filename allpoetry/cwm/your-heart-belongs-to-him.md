Despite,the love i've shown,
and the sacrifices,
which i've taken,
your heart belongs to him.
Your heart belongs,
to your first love,
the first person,whose tongue you tangled,
with the first person,
you really cared for.
You used me,
because at the time,
you needed me.
Despite your past romantic failure,
your heart still belongs to him.
Theres a deep thought,
just telling you,
to try and get him again.
Our love is gone,it was never real,
it was a sham,a mixture of anger and immaturity.

Remember that first kiss?
That moment that you won't dis-miss,
it was form him,your first love.
I've cried,lied and died inside,
for a heart,that was untrue to me.
I knew what,
I was getting into,
but I played the fool.
Next time,i'll be wiser,
next time, I won't,
get involed,with a love-struck person.
Next time,i'll be with someone,
much better,in a better situation,
who is really in love with me.
That next time,I wished was you.

That next time,
may never happen.

-CWMStill,writing about this girl,i fell in love with.
