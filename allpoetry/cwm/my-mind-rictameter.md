My mind,
opening, closing,
words flowing from my pen
wonderful, sentiments, expressed

Beautiful sights, images of the night,
turn back on the light, shed delight
holding thoughts, with my might,
never waste time,
my mind.Whew, it was kinda tough, limited language to express a complete thought. Not as easy as you think people, good challenge glad others have tried it. ~CWM
