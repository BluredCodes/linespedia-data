Fifty-three stories,
above the ground,
I cringe at the thought,
of me falling, in pure horror,
to the bottom of hopeless death.
Heart racing, at an estimate,
of 180 beats a minute...
Sweat coverig my eyes,
drenched all over my body.
Nausea consumes my body,
I want to vomit,
I just want to end this tormenting,
fall to end!!!

At last,
the empire-state tour,
is over!
What seemed an eternity,
was really only two mintutes.
I brushed it off,
took a deep breath,
and prepared, for the next,
tour,another fifty-three floored,
building, I wanted to conquer.

~CWMWho's not afraid of heights?
