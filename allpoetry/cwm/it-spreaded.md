It spreaded to,whatever it touched,
it just ignited it,into a torch.
The fire grasped my hand,
then traveled up my arms.
My body,begin,to fall into,
a big pile af ashe's.
The only pigmented filament that remained,
was my hair,but that too,
was being evaporated by the flames.Written as a challenge poem.The challenge of writing about a fire.
