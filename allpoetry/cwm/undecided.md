Undecided, mind not made up,
clueless, to what to do.
What to see?
What to do?
Im all undecided,
confused, my mind is blank.

I guess im tired,
no, maybe just bored,
what am I to do?

My friends are there,
waiting to converse with me,
but I don't feel like talking,
or maybe, I do.....

Im all undecided,
a clueless mind,
waits and waits.

Who will lead me,
through the path,
of the open gate ???

~CWMEver feel like this ???
