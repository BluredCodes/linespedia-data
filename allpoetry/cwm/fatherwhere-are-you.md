Father,Why are'nt you here,
with me and mommy?
Father why are'nt you here,
for little tommies birthday party?
Father,why are'nt you here,
to protect me,
when I have fears?

There were'nt any fatherly talks,
no taking me,
to the beach or park.
Father,I needed you,
and I needed you,
and I still do,
but your chances,
are running few.

Answer my questions,
to give me a clue,
I want to know,father,
father where are you?

-CWMWritten for my best friend,who grew up,asking this question.
