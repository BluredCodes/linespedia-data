The thin line,
is a great white line,
its as small as lint.

Once the joys of love,
surrounded me, that I glady greet,
then comes the agony of hate,
then I at once, re-treat.

The chants of love,
the screams of hate,
confuse me,
in every which way.

Love me? Yes.
Hate me? Yes.
Which is it???
A two-faced monster, has knocked on my door.

How will I live,
with the insults?
How will I live,
with knowing, how thin the line,
begs me for love,
but for even more despair and violence?

~CWMIts a love / hate thing.
