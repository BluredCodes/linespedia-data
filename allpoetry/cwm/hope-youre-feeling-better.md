Hope you're feeling better.
Hope you get everything together.
I hope many days lie ahead, with fair weather,
for you, and hope your day, flys like fallen feathers!

I see, and feel the pain,
I know you feel you have nothing to gain,
but give hope a chance, and helpless please refrain,
knowing peace one day, will be here again!

A lover's lost,
hope you're feling better.
A death of a loved one,
hope you're feeling better.

These words may not help,
because of the hurt, you've felt,
my condolence is so sincere,
may you walk with hope and not fear!

~CWMTo myself, Nell, and anyone who's suffering like me,i'm feeling better, and I hope you're feeling better. ~CWM
