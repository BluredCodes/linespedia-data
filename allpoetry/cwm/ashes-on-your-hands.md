---
title: "Ashes On Your Hands"
writer: "Cwm"
slug: "ashes-on-your-hands"
source: "AllPoetry"

Lingering memories,
burning desire,
ravenging, twisting,
your stomach,
to a knot.

Old, passing,
unforgiven, forgotten,
pictures,
torn edge to edge,
stained,
with years of tears.

Walking,
grabbing the neglected clothes,
touching,
those precious belongings,
once,
worn with such honor.

A fire starts,
destroying all that is left,
one picture remains,
a picture of her,
but that too,
caught on fire,
which burned your hands,
and all that remained,
were,
ashes on your hands.Not sure if this is understandable. I tried to creae a picture, and a scene. ~CWM
