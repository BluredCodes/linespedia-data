(CWM talking on the phone)

I know,im not them,
those women,from your past.
Our relationship is totally diffrent,
maybe for better, or for worse.
The future unknown,
but we owned our destiny.
Who cares if they're better,
or funnier, or more interesting,
than me!

They're gone,they've forgotten you,
they no longer call you,
so sad, but the truth hurts.
Have they called you?
Do they think of you?
Maybe so,could be no.
Im here,a true friend is here,
give me the chance,
they receieved!
Im here,my line is open,
my ears are'nt clugged.
My mouth is ready,
to input thoughs, of friendship.

I know im not them,
and I won't try to be,
I love you,
that you fail,
to remain to see.
I know im not them,
but who knows,
I could be better.
You know in some ways,
I AM!!!

- CWMWrote this for a friend,who lives in the past,and neglects anyone including me,his true friend, get clse to him.
