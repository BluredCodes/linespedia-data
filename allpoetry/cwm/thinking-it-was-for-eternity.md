Trusting, thinking,
wanting to believe,
thinking it was for eternity.

The hugs, the kisses,
love so dear, so strong.
"I Love You"
thinking it was for eternity.

The gifts, the joy,
the cries, the passion,
all has vanished,
all of it, just a memory.
Thinking it was for eternity.

Nothing stays forever,
everything has a birth,
then everthing dies.
Everything grows,
then then growth stops,
drifting to the past,
thinking it was for eternity.Trusting, wanting, but a sad reality. ~CWM
