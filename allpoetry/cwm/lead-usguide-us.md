Lead us,
guide us,
show us your wisdom.
Give us words of courage,
and peace within our distressed hearts.


Sort out the truth,
solve the question,
be our fighting knight,our crusader,
of grand honor.

Many disputes we must face,
but you,
need to take charge,
if you don't
then no one will.

So who will lead us,
guide us to our path to zion?
Will it be you? Or you?
Unanswered questions,
makes our path bleak.
