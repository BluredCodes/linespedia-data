---
title: "The Loner"
writer: "Cwm"

The sun shines,
and the sun inevitably sets.
Life begins,then life ends,
happy and sad times,
come and go,
but the loner,
will never share these moments,
with no one but him self.
The loner,
walks along the sandy shore,
he sees happiness,
and joy revealing on,
as he walks by.
Its winter,
and it shall snow,
causing him,
to be packed in,
for days without anyone.
The house is silent,
there is'nt even a sound,
of a pen that has dropped.
Having to force,himself,
he drives to the store,
to be lonely some more.
But wait!
Theres that pretty girl,
that he saw up state!
He raise's his eyebrows,
to invoke an attraction,
she looks as well,
but its not him,
that she will soon dwell.
He walks toward her,
with hope in his eyes.
But once again,
it was a lover's disguise!
"Take one look,
then walk away,
thats allright with me."
So,the loner,
goes back into,
his one room apartment,
where his black pen awaits,
he grabs his journal,
and writes about his day,
"Im lonely,im lonely,
please will someone come to me,
please don't let the loner,just be,
lonely s everyone sees."
He finally realized,the meaning of his name,
and he realized,of the meaning,
of this game.
Now,the loner sleeps,annother day,
another without someone home,
to be able to stay.
If he's lonely,
well at least be lonely,
when your happy and gay.We're all lonely at times.
