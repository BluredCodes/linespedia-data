Don't tempt me,
for my heart is broken,
my soul burns,
and I can't blame this on you,
because you didn't do this crime.
I try to speak, but i'm shanking,
i'm hurt, and I have fear,
but I know you'll be right here,
listening with an open ear.
Forget everything I should've said,
cause i'm really lonely in my head.
Please erase that frown,
i'm sorry, i'm alone for now.

I feel the pain, in your heart,
and it tears me apart.
I don't know, how long,
you'll wait just for me,
for i'm nothing can't you see?
I try to speak to you again,
but my words get in my way,
I only know that one day,
you'll be happy with what I say.
Forget everything I should've said,
cause i'm always lonely in my head.
Please erase that frown,
i'm really sorry, but i'm alone for now.

I love you more, than anything,
at least you know I do,
but the joy, you long for,
I can't give to you.
Beacause i'm hurt, i'm torn,
I cry all of the time,
I hate to be this way,
but you look at me, with a smile,
and you know what, I really want to say.
Forget everything I should've said,
because i'm really lonely in my head.
Please erase that frown,
i'm sorry, i'm alone for now,

Alone for now, alone for now.Not letting go the past, can hender future relationships.
~CWM
