---
title: "Tasted, What I Could Never Have"

Oh, the sweet taste,
revels in my unholy existence,
the honey sweet nectar,
still torments my whole confused, pain trapped world.
Those lips, that were made for mine lips,
now touches someone else's lips,
your body, that I use to hold,
is now being touched with someone else.
The agony, drives me insane!
The pain, the lost, is here again!

Tried to taste, tried to hold, tried to kiss,
what I could never have.
Made love, to someone belonging not to me,
now i'm forced to see,
the laughter, the smiles, happiness,
glowing on someone else, that was mine.
Now the tears flow,
gently down my cheek,
as i'm forced to hear,
the love-making every night,
from the next room!
With a pillow over my head,
I lay in complete tormeted-despair,
because what he has now,
I tasted, what I could never have.Boy, is this tormenting! i'm forced to see this, arrghhhh!
