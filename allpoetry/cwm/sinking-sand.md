The toes,
sink into the sand,
the sand,
now being moist,
with a sweat gland,
of the grasping foot.
Soon crabs,and other crustaceans,
walk and join,
the once lonely foot.
Each,try to escapae,
the scorching sand.
The crustaceans,
all,flow gently,
into the top.
Suddenly,someone,
poured water onto,
th sand surface,
which inevitably,
caused sinking sand,
which inevitably,
caused the foot,and crustaceans,
to suffocate!I tried to show the image of suffocation.
