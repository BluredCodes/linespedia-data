Lonely lover, taken from me,
forever, sealed to the ground,
six feet into the ground.

Deaths cold grasp,
came from the night,
and secretly, took your breath away.

Laying thinking of our dreams,
laying, dreaming of how wonderful the night is,
laying, as your breathing stopped, and your heart suffered.

Why did I wake, last night,
and witness, a sight of fright?
Wake up, and rise again, live again!

Flowers surround your coffin,
maggots,worms eat your flesh,
as I sit in a puddle of tears, as I remember you.

~CWM~Something new. ~CWM
