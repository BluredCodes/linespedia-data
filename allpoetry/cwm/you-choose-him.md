---
title: "You Choose Him"
writer: "Cwm"

You choose him,
you love him more.
That person from your past.
It failed once,it'll fail again.
Love you won't get from him,
will come from me.
Yet,you choose him.
Theres no stopping you,
no changing your mind,
your feelings are clear.
You love him more than me,
all because of phone conversations!
How weak of you,
yet,you choose him.
Days months,years,i'll still love you,
yet,you choose him.

Dear,my phone line is open,
and whenever,you want to say,
"Sorry,I should've choose you,I love you,
your the true one for me."
I'll be here and i'll listen.
But remember,when he calls again,
you'll leave me,for him.
I'll still remember you,
send my blessings towards you,
Something he won't ever do.
Yet,you choose him.More sad love.
