A distant shore,
is neglected once more,
from the ill of gore,
of decisions that were so poor.

Once filled with life,
without any strife,
it's now consumed with grief,
thats been plunged under-neath.

Under-neath this ground,
dreams were once dreamed,
filled with love, all around,
now face's are filled with frowns!

Death bell rings,
a murder, a sin,
that needs to be redeem,
to bring joy again.

The shore awaits,
open that mysterious gate
cast away that hate,
or a doomed durmise, shall be for us to make!

~CWMBack to us, our faulty society ~CWM
