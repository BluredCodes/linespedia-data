Make me believe,
that those words I hear,
are true sentinments,
from your heart.

Make me believe,
that you love,
more and more,
and for us not to part.

Make me believe,
that im in love with you,
that your on my mind,
with everything I do.

Make me believe,
there is no other one,
that I belong to you,
so that I can stop being so blue.

Darling, I have yet,
begin to realize, your love for me,
I guess thats how it must be,
for your love, all at once, I remain to see.Some people, don't know how to express there  selves.
Or show love.
