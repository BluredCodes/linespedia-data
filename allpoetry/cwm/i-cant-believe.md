I can't believe,
I can't believe,
I can't believe,
i've become my greatest misery.
I can't believe,
I can't believe,
I can't believe,
I was the monster,that I could'nt see,
it lived there,deep inside of me,
I just can't believe.

I can't believe,
I can't believe,
I can't believe,
my life is full of tragedy.
I can't believe,
I can't believe,
I let your prescence,
be a painful memory,
I just can't believe.

I can't believe,
I can't believe,
all of the respect for me,
had to flee,
I just can't believe.

Misery,misery,misery,
I can't believe,I can't believe,
I just can't believe.

Tragedy,tragedy,tragedy,
I can't believe,I can't believe.

Memories,memories,memories,
I can' believe,I can't believe.

Respect for me,
respect for me,
respect for me,
I can't believe,
I just can't believe.Extremely dark.A personal perspective.
