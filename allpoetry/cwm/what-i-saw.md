I saw,

Children laughing,
adults working together,
lover's holding hands,
smiles on every face.

I also saw,
violence erupt,
segregation,
little girls getting raped!

These dramatic diffrence's
hold the world, in a halt,
from reaching its destiny,
a destiny on Heaven or Hell!

Can't explain,
what I saw,
its all to confusing,
why can't life be stanza, number one?

What minds are in,
stanza number two?
Sun light, shall shine again,
along with acid rain, destroying its end.

~CWMI can't believe what i've seen.
