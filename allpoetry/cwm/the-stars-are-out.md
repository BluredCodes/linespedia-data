The stars are out,
the stars are out,
the stars are very bright,
as we sit in awe, of the light.

The stars are out,
the stars are out,
the stars brights a new light,
as we sit in awe at night.

Those startling stars,
fill my every existence,
as you fall into my arms.

We peeked, at the hightest star,
and I dreamed we saw the milky-way,
this night of stars,
I hope will forever stay.

The moon eclispe,
is such a celestial bliss,
i'll cherish this,
for this night,shall be surely missed.

The stars are out,
the stars are out,
they'll be here here,through the night.
OH! What a beautiful sight!
