Just a few months ago,
my world turned to sorrow,
I never thought, you wouldn't be here,
without me for today or tomorrow.

I wonder if your laughing,
I wonder do you cry,
do you dream about me ,
and in those dreams, do I die?

I thought my world surrounded you,
I thought I surrounded you too,
those words were so untrue,
but I was never given a clue.

I wonder what your thinking,
is your heart still sinking?
I wonder where you are,
come back and take away this scar!

All of your love deceieved me,
that I now only see.
A heart of truth  did'nt recieve,
I still can't really believe.

Wondering,
Why you left,
Wondering,
where you went,
wondering,
where you stayed,
why did you ever runaway ???

Wondering, wondering,wondering, everyday.

~CWMJust wondering.
