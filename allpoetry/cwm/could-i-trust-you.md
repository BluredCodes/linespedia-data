Could I trust you?
Even after you,cheated on me,
You lied,you went behind my back,
and engaged in a sexual act,
of plain stupdity,and ignorance,of unfaithfulness.

I did'nt deserve this,
I did'nt deserve to call you curse names,
and forever blemish your name....
I did'nt deserve to have to cry,
for your lack of caring feelings.

Love,I beg myself,
to take you back,
but I called you love,
yet there was no loving for me,
last friday.


How can I trust you after this?
You've scared me,forever stained me,
im broken,im broken,im only half,
of the passing shell of my once self.

Time is a healer,
and don't do it again,
for next time,if it'll come,
your loving,man shall begin,our end.

- CWM
