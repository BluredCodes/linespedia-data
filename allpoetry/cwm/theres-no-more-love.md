title: "Theres No More Love"

Theres no more,
theres no more love,
theres no more,
no more love that I have for you.
No more love, that you have for me too.
Once our love, filled both of our hearts,
now they're memories,
of our long tales, of broken hearts.

Theres no more,
theres no more love,
theres no more,
no more love, that I can give,
it hurts me, I regret, but I must continue to live.
No more promises, no more dreams,
no more hopes, as one of us leaves.
The passion isn't there, no more desire,
no more excitement, there isn't anymore fire.

Theres no more,
theres no more love,
theres no more,
no more of us holding each other at night,
where we use to hold each other so tight.
No more, will I dream of you,
it's too bad, but too true,
you no longer dream, of me too.

Theres no more,
theres no more love,
theres no more,
theres no more love.

~CWMSomeone talking to his mate, realizing that the end is here.
Kinda sad, but he knows it's too true. ~CWM
