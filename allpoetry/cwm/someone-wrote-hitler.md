There it was,
in simple plain english.
The words spelled,
HITLER!
Some careless,heartless bastard,
wrote this sicking despicable name!
One by one,
the school childen,
gathered in the classroom.
Stunned,shocked,terrified.
Comotion,filled the warm air.
Education had stopped!
Silence and profound hurt,
was in everyones eyes.
Dozens of minorities,
were angered.
No one was more angery,
than the jewish teacher.
He walked in he class room,
and stopped,then anger,
and sadness filled his eyes.
His family,the family,
he never knew,
all becamed a flashback,
into his gazing,glaring eyes.
How can someone,
we wondered,celebrate,
such an evil,perverse,human being?
Who still believes in global genocide,
of the 1930's,1940's ?

Why now?
Why now?
Growing questions filled,
every mind.
Who wrote it?
Who wrote it?
The answer,unanswered.
Tears,and hearts were broken.
Memories of slaugther,
and unprovoked murder were present.
Cruel intentions,insidsious messages,
were the focus.
The pain,and sadness,
this person caused,
is onerous,how will Mr. Berger,
forgive and forget?
After class,a student,
walked by Mr. Bergers desk.
Mr. Berger sat cold still,
his face in his sweaty palms,
his eyes red,of uncontroled hurt,and anger.

The boy put his arm around,
Mr. Berger and consuled him,
Mr. Berger hug the boy,
and said "Thanks."
Still deep in everyones mind,
was this question,
who wrote it?
who wrote it?
Untill the question,
gets answered,
it'll still remain,
as someone wrote Hitler.
Someone wrote Hitler.
