Trust me, trust me.
Im your friend to the end.
I would never leave you,
like your other friends do.

Trust me, trust me,
don't be surprised,
my love and loyalty,
is not a demon in disguise.

Trust me, trust me,
my heart is true.
Although at times, I may act cruel,
it has nothing, to deal with you.

Trust me, trust me,
let us be a happy family.
Although we live in tragedy,
trust and love, could bring unity.

- CWMTo a friend. He needs to trust me.
