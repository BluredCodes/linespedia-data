Your smile,your laughther,
in turn,brings out that side in me.
Your words,your understanding,
is so encouraging to my heart.
our principles,and thoughts,
are so like and unlike,
but we never argue or fight.
When im down,
your always there to console,
to reason,to my troubled soul.
Your sense of humor,
enlightens every core of my body,
it brings forth heaven again,
and sends satan, casted away!

Your caring soul,
i envy in contentment,
always answering my foolish request.
Some human souls are special from above,
bringing forth,peace love,and respect.
Blessed be! For i appreciate,
your divine spirit of kindness.

To April,from Curtis -CWMTo a very good friend,i never have a smile on my face,but then i see,and talk to her,she gets me going.
