I remember, the taste of that kiss,
I remember, the journey of living with fruitful bliss.
I remember, us traveling up the halls,
and us catching one another whenever we would fall.

I remember that lovely trip to jamaica,
you looked lovely that night, senorita.
Holding hands, laying in the sand,
wonderful,beautiful trips, so many plans!

I remember sitting, eating ice cream together,
on a sunny day, a day, of such warm weather.
I remember the plans, of getting married,
dreams, wishes, hopes, plans, now burried.

(Sighs) Yup, all joy is now gone,
your lost hurts so profound.
I remember, if you, or anyone else must know,
I remember as we use to dance near the mistletoes.

Oh yes! I remember darling,
I remember...

~CWMAh, the memories, enjoy while the present, because the past is so haunting. ~CWM
