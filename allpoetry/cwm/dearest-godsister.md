Dearest Godsister,
sister that I hardly know,
wasn't there,
for your first words,
wasn't there,
for your first steps,
wasn't there,
for your first day of school.

Dearest Godsister,
when you laugh,
I laugh,
when you cry,
I cry,
when you feel pain,
I feel pain as well.

Dearest Godsister,
your name is so sweet,
your body is shaped so heavenly,
whoever wins your precious soul,
shall cherish, your remarkable beauty,
that only the suns shadow,
will light up souls with a morning shine.

~CWMLovely, for Andrea.
