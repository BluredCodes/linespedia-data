---
title: "Couldn't Sleep"
writer: "Cwm"

Couldn't sleep,
thinking about,
what lies ahead,
couldn't sleep,
thinking about,
the horrific past.

Couldn't sleep,
knowing you wasn't
going to be sleeping,
here with me again.

Half-awake, half-sleep,
I reached, with my righthand,
grasping for you,
I grasped,
your lovely sheets,
you laid in so many nights.

Couldn't sleep,
knowing you will,
never be here again,
couldn't sleep,
knowing our bodies,
will not touch,
just for one more night.

Tumbling,
twisting,
turning,
I still couldn't sleep.

~CWMCouldn't sleep, the realization, was too much.
