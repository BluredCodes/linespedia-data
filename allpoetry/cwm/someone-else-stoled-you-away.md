Someone else, stoled you away,
I looked, but I didn'tsee you today.
I searched, I searched for my pride,
I searched for my bride,
I didn't find you, as I sat and cried.

Someone else, won your heart,
someone else, tore us apart.
I searched for my pride,
I searched for my bride,
I didn't find you, as I sat and cried.

Someone else, you loved more,
someone else, you now adore.
I searched for my pride,
I searched for my bride,
I didn't find you, as I sat and cried.

I looked, but I didn't see you today,
someone else stoled you away,
I didn't find you, as I sat in cried.
I continued to search for my bride,
I must, I must,I must for my weakened pride.

~CWMWrote these last night, just flowed, thoughts just came to mind. ~CWM
