---
title: "Shattered Tears"
writer: "Cwm"
slug: "shattered-tears"

Tears,
            dripping like rain,
            off a house,
            once again is shattered.
            Drip by drip,
            the tears and rain,
            slide off.
            They meet and greet,
            to nothing more but sadden images.
            Each tear,represents a tear,
            of a broken heart.
            All that is left,
            is a vague memory.
            Memories,of shattered hopes,
            dreams crush,
            like a sledge-hammer,
            destroying a used car.
            The tears,
            all gather one by one,
            coming together,
            and multiply,
            of several shattered tears,
            which was moist,
            from a distressed soul.
