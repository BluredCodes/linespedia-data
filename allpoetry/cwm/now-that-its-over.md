Now that its over,
the burden,its gone.
The burden of loving you,
and you treting me,
like a dog with rabies.
When you wrote "Its over."
i felt my life stopped,
i felt my body go,
into a spasm of shock,
i was speechless.
I never cared,
or loved like this,
now that this relationship,
flys away into the bleakness of mist.
Oh GOD! How i wish,our fate,
i could prevent,
why did your passion,
ever relent?
The days,and months,
that i truly regret,
never though,you,
i'll ever forget.
You were first,
true love,
but we did'nt have a blessing,
from above.
Maybe we'll see,
each other again,
and perhaps maybe,
start a new begin.
For now,my love,
today is our romantic end,
i hope somehow,
we can still be friends,
but i hope and pray,
for me love to lend,
and that your return,
you love to send.
Now that is over,
single again yea me!
I wish you the best,
that i hope to see.

Now,that its over,
goodbye,goodbye,
let me begin my process,
ill start the process now,
as i begin to cry.Kinda down right now,i broke up with my g/f today,and its not easy.Ill get pass this too,but i only hope she'll realize her mistake,and take me back.If you want to rate it go ahead,but it dose'nt matter to me,i just need hugs,and advice - CWM
