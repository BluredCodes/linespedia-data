title: "Our Father"

Our father,remains silent,as if ignoring us,hes probably laughing,at our senseless pity.He laughs at our failures,and see how we destroy his creations.He gets angry,at our sins,then he uncovers his almighty wrath!But why? Hes perfect,yet this isn't a perfect world.Theres after life,yet,theres no sign of it.He cares. But where is thee?Does he care? No!I at once can't believe!But the truth hurts,We pray,but theres no answer,we shout,and scream to the heavens!Yet silence,is our response.Silence will continue,its reality.We strive on faith ,faith along shall survive us,until we realize the day,faith falters,and then hope shall falter,and burn,because of lies,and deceit,and hideous,false illusions!The curse will remain,from OUR FATHER!Many may disagree,with me,but I'm ready for the heat.(smiles)
