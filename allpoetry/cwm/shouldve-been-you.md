title: "Should've Been You"

"What was I thinking?"
"How could I be so foolish?"
It was last year,
when I thought,
my mind was clear,
I let someone go, so dear!

Now that you're gone,
I realize,
I was the one for our demise.

Now,I see you,
with someone else,
someone much better than I,

Now I have someone else,
the jealously,envy, consumes my soul.
I can't believe love can be so cruel,
but I do believe, it should've been you.

Regret,remorse,sorrow,
all pack such a painful blow,
I love you, that I know.

Should've been you,
should've been you,
you're gone forever without me,
it's so sad but so true.

Should've been you,
should've been you,
should've been me,
me and you.

~CWMShould've been you, you and me. ~CWM
