Across the sea,
                   my lover waits,
                   across the sea,
                   I hope to spread,
                   my love bait,
                   across the sea,
                   i hope my love, 
                   my lover will take.
                 
                   Just because,
                   were seperated,
                   by this lake,
                   that dose'nt mean,
                   our love is fake.
                   When the currents,
                   soon become one controled state,
                   I will save you,
                   even though,it'll be late.
                   Lets hope and pray,
                   for heavens sake,
                   that this sea,
                   won't cause our love,
