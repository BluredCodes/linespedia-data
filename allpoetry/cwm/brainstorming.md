"Clutter,clutter,clutter,
  nothing but pure clutter."
  "Lets see,should i go,
   heavy or soft today?"
   "Come on!" "i've been sitting here,
    for hours,
    yet i have'nt got clue."
    "Has my head,
     suddenly become brainless?"
     "Don't i know,walls can talk?"
      "Hey,hey thats my ticket,
       thats my prize!"
       "I've got it!"
