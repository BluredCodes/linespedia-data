Innocent,
just doing his job,
picked as a target,
kidnapped,tortured,
afraid for his life,
and afraid for his dreams,
with his life and unborn baby.
Held captive,
for a month,
left in hot,dirty,
filthy conditions,
he begged pleaded,
for his life,
but once again,
fellow mankind would,
have no compassion.
"Im a jew!" He screamed.
"My mother was a jew!"
The last words for innocence.
A video tape,
recored the grue-some act of murder.
One knife,into a throat,
cause innocence to die!
Why murder?
Why did they kill him?
Evil corupts man,
for evil is within us all.
Some by prayer and faith,
cope with it.
While others, thrive in sheer EVIL!!!

~CWMMore like a rant, just had to write about something that has made me so sad, and angry.
