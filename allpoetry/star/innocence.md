She stands alone in the rain
She's so numb now
God cant you feel her pain?
She's been spit on and kicked around
but yet she says nothing...
never makes a sound
She just stands alone in the rain
Watch the mascara drip down her face
She feels unwelcomed in any place
God I will take her in
I will hold her high
I will kiss her lips never make her cry
If only you coud see
How innocecnt she can really be
Why must you push her down
Face down in the mud
Why must her cut her palms
I'll do what no one else would
but yet she'll stay in the rain
crying in pain
It's you who's insane
Dont take her life for granted
Dont let her drown
in the (sic)ness that's overwhelming
The earth doesnt make a sound
I remember how the sun
used to light up her ski
A golden shine
With an innocent heart
I wish that was mine
 and her hair let astray
With her cute little part
Im wide open
She walks into my arms
I wont take her for granted
I would put her to no harm ........I wrote this for my best friend Bobbie:)
