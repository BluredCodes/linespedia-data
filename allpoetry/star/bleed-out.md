Breathe In, Bleed Out 
My throat is dry when I stain it
your lips are tight when I 
cover them with mine 
your lips are dry when I tie it up and
cover them with mine 
knots and everything tears me apart
caustic insecurities
surface above 
every time I need
everything I want
aesthetic disguises
everyone's right up in front
push me against the wall
but still you can't make me fall
separate your mouth
scrape away
prosthetic misery
Reach And Touch I've seen it turn from red to grey:::::::::::
