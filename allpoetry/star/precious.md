I had to fall to lose it all
Pushed as far as I can go
I've put my trust in you
Can you take it all away?
You knocked me out
You bit my lip 
You held me down and kept me sober
It's hard to find someone to love you for you
But I found you
And you made me feel worth something
Made me feel new
You know I want to pick you up
But they don't want you up
Watch me with your eyes
I want to be just like you
Always kiss me goodbye
You're the first star
You're the one to see it all
THE ANGEL
I push you up against the wall
Consider your position
Framed to be consumed
bathing in your presence
New life break away
Along the edge of the glass is where you will stay
You bleed out of control
It's proof to know you're alive
All looking for a new place to drive
You push me up against the door
Slide your hand down
Still I want a little more
If you sting me I wont mind
Tonight I feel like more
You make me feel surprised
Love me;dig in deeper
It matches your eyes
The rain keeps pouring
And we get wetter
Cause you're mine
I taste you much better
I knew this could be right from the start
I watched a change in you
A new cavity in my heart
We gave our eyes 
And we gave eachother
