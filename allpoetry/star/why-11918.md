Feel my pain
Know my pain 
You are not the only one who lives this life of anguish
{no response...}

Feel the warmth of friendships
Not the coldness of a stranger
I dont hate you I never could
I just want you to feel me
If only you understood
{React...}

Dont you care?
Have you no feeling?
{Take my hand...}

Dont do this dont ignore my pain
I dont understand you
You're driving me insane
{Dont just stand there...}

Let my tears melt your heart
Just understand I would never tear you 4 apart
{ K -D- J -S}

I can remember the old times 
You all went places
Had your fun
All had smiling faces

Im sorry If I messed things up
Im sorry I even got involved
But I cant erase the past 
I cannot go back in time

I could hate you
I could tell my boyfriend things too
But im not like you
I want to befriend you
But you wont let me through

So Ill leave you to yourself
but remember I still care
Ill still be there
You can hate me but I wont hate you

I must release the past and claim the future
I hope you 3 realize I would never hurt her

Scared as I may be
Uncertain at times
I wont turn in anger

I will stay 
I will not move
I will try to talk
In hopes that we can improve

I realized that I had long been dead
But I could choose instead 
To reawake
And be alive for {THEM}...    K---- J--- D---I hope you can understand this.......
