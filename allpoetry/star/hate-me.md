title: "[Hate me]"
writer: "...Star..."

Hate me
Kill me
Rape me
Fuck me
I dont care just let it out on me
Stab me
Cut me
Bleed me
Love me
Fuck it all I dont want to see
Hate me 
Drown me
Steal my heart away
Fuck this I dont want to stay........
