---
title: "Cry Me A River"
writer: "...Star..."
slug: "cry-me-a-river"
source: "AllPoetry"

I hate when you feel empty
I hate when you feel sad
I hate when you feel any pain
I just want the love we have
I'm dying inside
because you feel this way
I'm crying inside
cause your bad feelings you wont say
to me or anyone
just let all out on me
let me be your happyness
I'll take the pain away
just tell me how you feel
and I will make the night into day
Cry onto my shoulder
bleed into my hands
ease your pain onto me
and I will bury it under the sands
I will always be here for you
so dont keep these feelings away
I was just like you
I didnt know what to say
I used to doubt love
and anything it had to bring
i doubted being happy
I had a broken wing
paranoia is like a drug
its seeps inside and lives
but dont worry about me
I'm the one that will always give
Give you all my love and all my time
give you every peace of me
till you know your just mine
no one in the world will make me feel this happy
no one in the world will kiss me like you do
no one that I know
can make my soul anew
Let me be the your hands that you can cry into
let me be the arms that will always hold you
let me be the one to take away your pain
cause I hate the way you feel
its driving me insane............This was a poem I wrote for my good friend a while back.....This poem was for Drew
