I close my eyes and wait to dream
I close my eyes and hope for the unseen
I close my eyes and wait for you
I close my eyes theres nothing else to do
I lie in bed thinking just a couple more hours
A couple more hours till I get to slip away
Slip away into the darkness run away from the day
To dream is to escape 
To escape is what I need 
To slip away and dream
To follow and have someone to lead
A sweet surrender
A great escape
A beautiful story
My mind left to break
So now I close my eyes and wait and wait and wait
Now I close my eyes for the darkness to consume me
Now I close my eyes and wait for my dreams to take me ........*........A poem about dreams :)
