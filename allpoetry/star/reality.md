Lost Inside
Cannot Hide
Break through the Hallow
Cannot Swallow
Tear it Down
You're the Clown
Deep Inside
Let it Slide
Fell from Grace
Lost this place
Struck Again
Can't Tell When
Fuck It All
Break the Wall
Smear the Mud
Taste the blood
Cannot Pass
Break the Glass
Set On Fire
Pushed up Higher
Cut my Skin
Let You In
Drown and fall
Lost it All
Twist the knife
Lose my Life
Stuck in my Head
Left me for Dead
No Excuse
Cut the Noose
Be Abused
Let it Go
Seal it Slow
Taste the Cold........A song I made for my band called ~*Crave*~
