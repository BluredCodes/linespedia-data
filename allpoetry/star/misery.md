writer: "...Star..."

Your kiss is till burning on my lips
This is getting old
My bodys getting cold
Nothing seems to feel this place
I cant let go
etched upon my misery
The soft touch of your fingertips
These ropes they bind and choke
My life seemed like a joke
I rise above the pain
I'll never be the same........
