Take my hand
says the girl with the hungry eyes
She pulls her victim to his suprise
She kissed him deeply
The victim followed her as if he was in some kind of trance
She looks across the dance floor and see HIM.
He nods his head in agreement.
She smiles and takes her victim away from the dance floor.
She leads him outside and kisses his neck ever so gently.
How beautiful she looks in the moonlight, skin alight tan, long brown hair with blonde bangs and those eyes so deep, so secret,..so inviting.
She walks her victim down a dark alley.
The ground is damp and eereie, but yet she says nothing and leads her victim on.
She pushes him against the wall.
The victim looks into her eyes as she smiles....Her fangs exposed.
The victim screams in fear and tries to run, but is held back by HIM.
His eyes how the could pierce your skin, his hair spiked as if it were flames kissing the night hair.He pulls the victim back and sinks his teeth into the victims neck.
The victim lets out a cry of pain.
The girl with the hungry eyes just waits patiently.
HE stops and looks into her eyes then pushes the victim to her.
She smels the copper blood and sinks her teeth in.
"Listen to his heartbeat."
She drinks and feels so full of life, full of so much energy.
She can hear the victims heart be slow and finally stop.
The victims body goes limp.
HE smiles and kisses her as some blood escapes from her crimson lips and slides down her chin."Not bad for your first time," He says " Ready for more?"
