---
title: "::Clinging::"
writer: "...Star..."
slug: "clinging"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/53541-Clinging-by-...Star..."
---

I've shed so many tears 
Dreamed so many dark dreams 
I feel so bleak, numb, desolate... 
I tremble if people come near 
Please don't reject me if I try to push you away 
Although you probably will  

I wish for compassion 
Some understanding 
People don't realize 
I'm still here.. Somewhere in this black pit of despair 


A weight on my chest  
The tears keep coming 
I feel confused
Im not like the rest

I've failed at all the simple things
I've fallen and tried to get back up
brushed the dust off
but I still feel caught up

I don't know what to do...
I don't know how to fight this...
I don't know where I belong...
I don't know if I even deserve this...

I thought of dying 
To throw myself away
Im swept up in confusion
I don't know if I can stay

Bitter laughter hides the pain 
Of weakness and self pity
The world is full of smiling faces
none of which are meant for me

Through My tears of pain I smiled
Told you I was ok
My smile lit the room 
Said it didnt hurt...
But it did

The tears are falling they will never stop
I will hold on tight
To the love in my heart
It will keep me safe
Through the many long nights of grief and wept tears

Come in pain
Im tired of trying to be strong
because I cant always be strong 
I cant always hold on
I cant do this on my own

I will close my eyes 
and let the world pass me by............I dont know where I belong Im so confused .I dont know who to turn to........
