Here I stand along the wall
waiting for someone
to keep me up high help me stand tall
Everything goes by so fast
making my head spin
I stand alone again waiting and hating the past
what good is the future when I have nothing that will last.
Im not afraid of being here
Im not afraid of spilling my tears
Im not afraid of being alone
Im not afraid to bleed
Im not afraid to see
What good is being loved when there is no one who does love you
What good is being needed when there is no one to need you
What good is following when there is no one to lead you
But yet here I am alone again along the wall
Waiting for something to happen
Something...Anything at all!
Ill wait on this wall till the end of time
Ill bleed on this wall till I am found
Ill cry on this wall till I have someone to call mine........A poem I made with my friend Bobbie:)We kick ass so booyah!
