Press your face against the glass
Remember me
Remember your past
You smile to make it feel alright
You laugh bitterly to put up a fight
You walk down the street alone
The earth is cold
What do you call home?
{Chain yourself to me 
stitch up your hope to feel
So precious having that girl
You will never be the same}
{Chain yourself to me 
Stitch up your hope to feel
So precious having that girl
You will never be the same}
Loving you is strange
and adored by me throughout
Blessing you with every kiss
Your so patient 
waiting in your world
Lets see what new disease you fetch
Someday soon you'll find that someone 
Waiting for the chance to beat you 
So precious you know this hate of mine exploded 
They talk about you 
Then turn your confidence against you now 
Blow it back at them
{Chain yourself to me 
stitch up your hope to feel
So precious having that girl
You will never be the same
Chain yourself to me 
Stitch up your hope to feel
So precious having that girl
You will never be the same}

So precious having that girl.......A song I wrote for my band....Dedicated to Kels
