Picking through the parts exposed
I am the push that maks you move
I break from the pressures of life
I bleed into your hands
Whatever may come moves slowly to the back of your mind
This mode is incredible
It's out of this world
I love you in 0-circles-0
I taste you in 0-circles-0
Our hands entwined through circles
Got a silver cloud high above us
Got champagne bubbles surging through me
Running through my veins
Fall from these loaded wings
To let it in
But I don't have to hear the words
'Cause I already feel your thoughts
Fill you full of reason
Force it down to bring you back
Pleasingly falling apart
Pleasingly oblivious
Empty Cavity
I want you in 0-circles-0
I see you through the 0-circles-0
I need you in these 0-cirlces-0
Empty Jars Of Blood
When Flesh Is The Enemy
Sedate the innocent
It's the violence in the silence
Don't move 'cause I love you
Don't move 'cause I'll cut you
Nothing I feel myself in
Nothing I burn myself in
I see all your love in here
Bleed in through your smile
I don't need control
A life they'll never know
So enter my mind
You move me in 0-circles-0
You push me in 0-circles-0
It follows in 0-circles-0
Loved right, Lived wrong
I'd tear my heart
The world surrounding us goes on
Come into my side
It's in your hands
I'm still slipping down your well
You're watching 
You're wanting
You're hunting
