Everytime I start to believe
something's raped and taken from me
feeling like I have no relief
how many times must I walk through this disease
somebody get me out of here
tearing at the walls sick and tired
it falls upon us all
everday its the same thing
and it blows across my mind
I didnt mind its not my time
I've been steeped on 
and spit at
but never thought to question why
I wanna push it right over the line
gotta cut away
I cant stay
and I always seem outnumbered
I know it will never end
As I walk down this road
I know what lies around the bend
It's not the life I wanted
need to heal all my addictions
I let it happen over and over again
I need to take this valediction
I will dedicate
and sacrifice
in all the choices that I make
How much further do I have to go
When I feel too close to break
happiness is something that I lack
emptiness is lonliness
intoxicating with madness
I'm not coming back
the (sic)ness keeps on seething
and I am thrown about
the trick is to keep breathing
even if I still feel doubt
sick and tired  I stand alone
a world that doesnt exist
forever I may roam
I'm looking for someone
blood stained route
I'm tied up for nothing
blood stained brow
fear is not the end of this
I cant stand any of this now
I'm not going to hurt anymore
Im not goung to be the whore
Its my turn
my turn now
Its my turn to stand tall 
Its my turn not to fall
but I cant seem to recall when you came along
Can you explain just what you are
starving for you
you no longer feel far
Im chasing after you
set me on fire
hold me tight
and fly me higher
nothing is real but this feeling
nothing is real but your face thatI'm seeing
pulled me right out of my misery
place me into your reality
my world is crashing inside out
you're everything I need
let the clouds shade us
in the electrical skies
smell you taste you and see those eyes
my love for you cant you see
I've got the answer right in front of me
I want to look at the things you do
I'll take the breath you breathe 
make our dreams come true
is it any wonder I found love?
through you
you take all of me
you're in my head
and this about this moment
as I lie awake in bed
red clouds shade us
cannot forget this
I'll always rememer this love, this trust
you are something so new to me
it feels so exciting yet scary
I never thought a person I could love could be right in front of me
but I took a chance and dove in blindly
took a chance for you to find me
my world wont last without you here
I wont last without you near
no more fear
you were always there and I never even knew
you've always been there to help me
and it feels so good to finally say.....
I love you
