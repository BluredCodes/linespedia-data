I can remember when we first met
You were with the one I loved
Happiness from me is what you'll get
but then you turned around and stabbed him in the back
now happiness is what you lack
I want to push you right over the line
cut you right back down to size
tear at your back
rip out your spine
leave you lying there for dead
lie awake hearing voices in your head
and all the people that you hurt all came down on you
I hope you fuck up again
leave you with nothing to do 
I want you to learn
I want you to pay
I want someone to break you down every fucking day
I want to bring you down
cause you're not good enough for him
Choke you as you're losing all your air
never make a sound
I never thought I could feel this much hate
I wont bother with you...
Leave you to your fateThis poem is about a girl at my school who is so stuck up and just foul to everyone* pardon the bad words teehee*
