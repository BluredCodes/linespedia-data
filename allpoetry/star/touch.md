I sit alone in this dark room
hearing the buzz of a broken light overhead
The floor is cold I can barely see the moon
I sit there breathing 
feeling my chest heave in and out
Sense someone; wanting to shout
Alone in the middle of a dark damp room 
Alone and cold nothing to assume
I feel a hand slide across my back
I smell your cologne and taste it too
chills escape and i am consumed
Just imagine
Touch
It can mean so much
Touch
Feel the skin in a electrical faze
Soft and warm
lost in this maze
Touch
Is so much like a natural drug
Touch
A moment... a... kiss... a hug
Just feeling the warmth from a another being
It amazes me I want what Im seeing
Dont leave just yet
I want to feel you again 
Dont go now
Touch...A poem about touch nothing sexual though just a good feeling when it comes to feeling skin on skin :)
