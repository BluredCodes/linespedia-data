---
title: "She"
writer: "...Star..."
slug: "she"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/44759-She-by-...Star..."
---

She is the sunkissed light that falls upon your skin
She is the shadow that follows you to wherever you've been
She is that strand of hair that falls upon your lip
She is the hand that grabs you by the hip
She is the nails that dig into your back
She is the happiness that you lack
She is the knife that cuts into your skin
She is the arms that will always let you in
She is the one that watches you sleep
She is the one that you should keep
