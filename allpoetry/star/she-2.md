She's the only time spent right
She's the air you breathe
She's the one to make you feel right
She will stay with you till the end
She's your lover
She's your friend
She's not the kind of girl who likes to tell the world about all she feels inside
Always the one to track her down
She doesnt want to fight against the tide
She wont take it like the other girls
and let the pain go too far
She knows the human heart and how to read the stars
and to your heart her love she feeds
She's all you'll ever need
She doesnt play games
She'll burn up the pain
wont you let her show you how it feels
wont you let her take you far away
Dont you want to stop living in this wheel
She needs you to hold onto
She wants to see you shine
She will be the one to bleed for you
