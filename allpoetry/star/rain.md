I look outside my window
Watch the rain slide down the glass
Remembering you 
Remembering my past
Im missing you
I want you here
I need you forever
The rain doesnt look the same without you 
My eyes aren't as bright as they were when I was near you
Come back to me
Dream back to me
I dont want to lose you
I want to be able to see
How alone the earth looks through my eyes
I remember the visions you shared with me
I remember our kisses goodbye
I know your smell 
I know your voice
I know your touch; your every choice
Im not upset
I am finally able to see again
but it cant last unless you're here
I told you I loved you
Did I make it clear?
I want to show you my love again
I want to see you as I am...There was a time the pieces fit but I watched them all fall away........
