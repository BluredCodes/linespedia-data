Today I woke up
and you were gone
I looked around
wondering what I did wrong
I put my clothes back on
washed my face and brushed my hair
Wondering if that night you cared
I slip on my shoes
and grab my purse
Telling myself I have nothing to lose
knowing in life there are things far worse
I put on my make-up 
and look in the mirror
Dazed and confused
What I do fear
I open my door apartment # 109
I walk down the hall
telling myself everyhting's fine
Out on the street
The earth beneath my feet
The world just passes by
I take a deep breath and let out a sigh
I walk down the busy street
not bothering to look around me
I stop by a Starbucks and get some coffee
White Chocolate Mocha
We always used to drink that
Im wasting my time
I look at the table where I knew how you sat
Where did you go?
Why did you leave?
Did you get hurt
or was it just me?
I finish my drink
and set back for home
I unlock the door
and answer the phone
I wonder who it could be
"Happy Birthday Babes!"
says your voice..
"Did you miss me?"I never did mind about the little things...:)
