I tried to tell you over and over again it was better when we were just friends
You say you dont know why you recieved this rejection
but its better than feeling this cold impulse injection
I thought I loved you I really did
but I dont know now cause all you are to me is dead
You set all of this pain on me
but please just listen to me and you will see
that i do care for you, but only as friends
I just wisj that all your drama will end
are you just going to wait for my last breath and set me to my final test
there's nothig else that I can do
because you torment me and you think I torment you ........
