Here with you I feel safe as can be
The way you move and the way you see
feels so intoxicating to me
The night is our canvas
Together we create a masterpiece
The stars that shine above us
is a moment that we keep
Forever intwined in this passionate dance
Locked in your eyes; held in your arms
Knowing we stand a chance........This was poem both ...Flame... and I have written
