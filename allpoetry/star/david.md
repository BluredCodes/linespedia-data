David sits along the stairs
and David wonders if anyone cares
David feels alone and without any hope
David is a loner tied up with rope
He is entangled and consumed by thick clouds of hate
He feels like he is the one to be raped
David Cant take this anymore
David doesnt want to feel like the whore
David knows its time to escape
David knows that he can now be the one to hate
He hates all the people who shove him down
He hates all the assholes so dont make a sound
Hes not gonna bend over backwards anymore
Hes not gonna leave his arms open 
or hold open a door
David is going to leave
But he wonders to himself
If I go through with this
Who is gonna miss me? ........*........This poem is a story about A guy named David whos life sucked ass.
