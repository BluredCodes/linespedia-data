In this state of mine,
You're what I need, nothing close to what I want.
{I breath you in}
I'm in love with your confusion, on your knees,
When you're breathing through your elbows,
Down on me.
{I wanna live}
Can't you see?
There's a feeling that's come over me
Close my eyes
You're the only one that leaves me completely breathless
No need to wonder why
Sometimes a gift like this you can't deny
{I wanna love}
There's a will
There's a way
Sometimes words just can't explain
Cause when there's you, I feel whole
And there's no better feeling in the world
But without you I'm alone
{Why are we afraid to be in love?}
To be loved
I can't explain it
I know it's tough to be loved
Turn out the lights now
To see is to believe
I just want you near me
I just want you here with me
And I'd give up everything only for you
It's the least that I could do
{Take me in}
leave your body alone
somebody's gotta change
you're the reason i've been waiting for so long
{Breathe in deep}
I've seen attention - see through me,
I'm everyone -	I need You
Call on me 
in me you're better than I wannabe.
You can keep breathing,
I only fall when you aren't near me.......{8}........A poem I wrote for ...flame... I love you sooooo very very much:)
