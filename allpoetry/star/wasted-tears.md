---
title: "Wasted Tears"
writer: "...Star..."
slug: "wasted-tears"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/48520-Wasted-Tears-by-...Star..."
---

You watch us
and hate us
Not wanting us to last
I know it hurts
But its now in the past
Just let it fucking go
cause you cant break us apart
Just fucking let go
You no longer rule my heart
Talk after talk after talk
Im so tired of talking
Cut in deeper; bleed
No more of this, im walking
You want me to talk
You want me to see
but no wait I shouldnt talk to you
because you think Kelly would influence me
You say you love me but I dont fucking believe you
If you loved me you wouldnt say that I'm weak
Dig into my heart let it leak
No more being nice
No more letting you walk on me
Roll the dice
Push you to your knees
I've fucking had it with you and your eyes
I've fucking had it with you blaming me for lies
I know what I've done and I have felt guilt
But no more being upset for me
No more saying Im sorry pleading on my knees
I dont deserve to be treated this way
You dont deserve to be able to stay
Just fucking let go
Cause I dont need you
Just fucking think straight
There are no more fucked up things you can do.
Kelly and I are going to stay together
Whether you think breaking up with him will help
Because Kelly and I love eachother
I've cried for you
Wasted my tears
I hate you now
I hate all our years
You can tell all the people
about all the sex
But nothing you say will ever connect
Tell all you want
Let them decide
I was so true to you
I stood by your side
But you were the one to tear it apart
I dont care now if you have a hole in your heart
I may have been your first in everything we did
But I cant stand to see you now 
You're only deadI really did waste my tears for you........
