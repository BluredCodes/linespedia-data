---
title: "~Run Red~"
writer: "...Star..."
slug: "run-red"

So much confusion 
Twisted illusions
Its fine
Im ok
Dont let my pain get in your way
Just keep on walking and don't look back
I wanted you to stay
I needed you to hold me
but I understand you needed THEM too
I've had so many bad dreams
Please make them go away
Please lay next to me today and stay
I don't know who else to turn to
I don't know where else to run to
Why is it that the good ones always get hurt?
Or am I really not as good as I think I am?
Am I really just a bitch?
Do I really misunderstand things?
I don't get my life
I don't see where I've done wrong
Ive tried my hardest to keep the walls from crumbling
Im using all my energy to make you all feel better
I want to smile again
I want to laugh again
I want that love we share
But you're so far away now 
Im so lost in this haze
Lost all alone
Stuck in a daze
I cant erase the past
But I can try to make the future greater
Just give me a chance
To make things better
I try to improve things
I try to stay Strong 
But really Im not that strong
I have a good way of hiding my pain
hiding my tears
I cried yesterday when you were holding me
Lucky me you didnt even notice
or did you?
Did you see my tears and just let it pass by? 
I dont understand this
I need to run away instead
Crying in my hands
Till the tears ~Run Red~
