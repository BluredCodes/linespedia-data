title: "The Cold"
writer: "...Star..."

I never thought I'd love the cold as much as I do
I never thought I'd let the cold air caress my skin alone with you
I never thought I'd fall in love with someone so close to me
I never thought your love for me could really truly be
I never thought I'd be lifted so high
Alone in the park seeing the star shoot across the sky
Our days alone I'll always remember
Our days together stay in my heart foreverI wrote this when I heard my boyfriend tell me once I never thought I'd love the cold as much as I do..:)
