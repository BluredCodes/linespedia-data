Waiting waiting here for you ...
I'm so foolish to believe that you'd come...
It's cold here I feel that I dont belong
I need you still, I need you here to hold me,to look itno your eyes and see your soul within
It starts to rain,I feel I should leave, but I cant because something tells me I'm not the same
I see a shadow along the streets could it be you? 
I hope I think

Its so dark I cant see
I see the dark shadow approaching me
Whats this another shadow has been waiting too
I know its you now I can see your face, but no hte other shadow awaits
It has been following you 
I see a shimmer of light as if the shadow had a knife
Before I could save you the knife cuts through
The shadow lurks away but I stay

I see you fall to the ground with hands upon your heart
Blood pours beneath you and I dont know what to do
I scream for help...The streets are quiet just as before
I lay your head upon my lap as blood soaks through my dress
I cry so hard begging you not to leave me
You just smile and place your hand on my face ever so gently
You tell me everyhting will be alright but I know my life will be no more except night
I wish I was dying instead of you
I wish I was the one lying on the cold wet floor
I cant be with you anymore
I see you die before my eyes and kiss your lips as my last goodbye........I had  dream abou this so I decided to put it in words
