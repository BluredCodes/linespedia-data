Tell them she wont be back 
cause she needed a change
An escape from the pressures of life
She has been falling down
And crying blood
They've pushed her down
Pushed her face down in the mud
But she got back up and walked on
Smiled and gave them all the finger
She knows she can change 
She knows she's not what they call her
When she comes back she wont be the same
She turned the bend
and walked on
laughed at their stupidity
They'll regret what they've done to her
They watch and stare as she walks by
But she doesnt notice them anymore
She just lets out a deep sigh
and walks on by
She'll be back
and they'll be fucked
She'll be freed
and they will drown 
in sorow and depression
She will look on over the city
and she will give them no pity
They deserve this 
So all she has to do is sit back and relax....
