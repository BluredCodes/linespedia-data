So difficult to stop pretending 
what's this to you anyway ?
You're fearless in motion
Nothing common suits you 
Mistreat another piece of you 
Feel this emotion
I want you to be free
Leaving your new found cool 
I want you to see
What's behind your painted face 
Revenge you breed
{Dont let your secrets get out 
hating yourself
Just let the pain drip
drip away
slide down your face
smear it on the wall
they cant hurt you at all}
Push yourself to the line
Dig the knife in deeper 
break their spines
Dont let their voices get to you
Screaming in your head
Just throw it all back at them
They're already dead
The darkness envelopes you
You just gotta cut away
Break through this sickness
You are stronger than them 
You can win
{Dont let your secrets get out 
hating yourself
Just let the pain drip
drip away
slide down your face
smear it on the wall
they cant hurt you at all}
 Drip just let it fucking drip away
Wash it all down
Smear it all away
Drip 
Drip away ...Cause Ill stay.....Dont let stupid people get to ya they're a waste of your time...
