Its not your fault that you're always wrong
I can ease all your pain
Make the bad feelings gone
It feels so unreal
To know how you feel
When I gaze into your eyes
I can lose myself forever
And telling me you loved me was the greatest surprise ever
Thoughts freeflow
and it starts with one thing
all I know is time with you is a valuable thing
Open up your mind
and you will see in time
all the love I have to give
and all I want is for you to stay mine
When I feel your touch
It brings me to my knees
My love for you is liek a disease
I want to sleep where you do
I want to feel you near me too
I want to feel you oh so close,
that your breath keeps me warm
and your lips you let expose
The taste of heaven you've brought to me
and how I feel I want you to see
There's nothing else that I can say or I can do
but all I can give is my love to you........
