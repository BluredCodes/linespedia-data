In the O all we do is fuck with that dro
that shit that have you and 
your hoes coming out of those fucking clothes
that good shit
yeah I see you itching your nose
twiching your hips 
licking your lips
damn near about to fold
cause that dro got you froze
I told you this green ain't for boys
cause we about to make some noise  
get them lungs poised
and sit back and kick it   
until we get muthafucking evicted
some people say i might be sadistic
cause i been fucking with this dro 
up in the O living a life of sin
not given a damn weather or not 
i get judged by them twelve men
cause when i leave the court
i'm still going to have a blunt in my hand
got to, for all those fucking fans
so fuck society and its plans
you want to judge me
for the shit i do in the O
nothing crucial but fucking with this dro
cause when i got stress
i got to fill my chest
cause by far i wasn't blessed
so when i have a cardiac arrest 
in your town 
don't weep nor lose sleep
just bring me another pound
cause i love weed like i love my mounds
cause you know the golden rule in the O 
we blaze until we can't smoke no mo!
and yeah we blaze a few phillys 
for those hoes
cause you no hoes smoke too
just like me and you
gotta show love for those fucking good drugs
and if shit get hectic
will just turn to the next page
and start fucking with that Haze!
you know
cause thats how we do it in the O!
you know!
pufffff puffff puffff
blowwwwwwwwwww!
damn this shit got my nose burning!
damn! some icky icky ickyyyyyyy!just keeping it real for those in the O
you know
role one for me your pale cursed!
