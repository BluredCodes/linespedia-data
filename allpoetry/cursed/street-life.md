Stepping out the pen-back into society 
it's 2002 and I'm taking no shorts see 
went in as a pee-wee 
and came out as an L.S.G. 
Down wit my nigga pimp Daddy Squeaky 
who caught a dope case 
when he got raided by the fucking L.P.D. 
kept his mouth shut 
went out like a real G$ 
didn't snitch on none of his homies 
that's why my nigga from the Westside get his props 
but can't kick it out west 
cause the west is too hot 
so we're headed to the southside chilling in the vill 
got to be strapped with A.K.$,Tec$ & Nine Mills 
cause fucking with these nigga's on the hill will kill 
but little do they know I ain't the next meal 
every where I go I am always strapped with my steal! 
cause me and my nigga's wont die 
you throw bullets and we devour them 
than turn around and fuck you like LiL Kim! 
look at this shit got blood on my rim 
know bitch made nigga I gotta kill him! 
why ask why as I look up the sky 
damn the sun is about to set 
and the rush is going down 
so me and my fools is about to clown 
putting it down pound for pound 
while still sporting my vest 
and my nigga Pimp Daddy squeaky 
about to put his game to the test 
and my nigga LiL'C still playing 
these hoes and me 
I am still putting down for these hoes 
playing these hoes like a game dominoes 
teaching these hoes a lesson 
and if these bitches get me to stressin 
I hit them with the Smith $ Wesson 
and that,s  just how it goes and 
like that nigga Snopp Dogg said 
we don't love you hoes and 
thats how the game is played 
in this Street Life 
so act like you know!Just a lil taste of ten inches in you face!
