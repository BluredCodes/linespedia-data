What can I say
of my little orange chair?
Passing day by day
silently sitting over there!
Oh, sweet little chair
of orange descent,
please don't be fooled
by my mexican accent
My love is greater for thee,
Oh chair orange in hue,
than any other little chair
of lemon, grass, or blue.
I wish you had hair
that I could run my fingers through!
Oh little orange chair,
how sweetly I love you!
