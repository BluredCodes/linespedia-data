Nothing could even compare to the joy
If I could work at Initech with you
I'm the stapler,you be the copy boy
On channel 39, let's watch Kung-Fu
My David Herman, if you only knew
I want your remainders in my account
Way more than Mark from Blink182
At Chotchkies, I would give you a discount
And of course I don't like Michael Bolton
That no talent Grammy winning loser
For you my love is lava, molten!
For you're my copy machine abuser
I'd do a million T.P.S reports
If only I could get into your shorts
