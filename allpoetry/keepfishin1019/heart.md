---
title: "*Heart*"
writer: "KeepFishin1019"
slug: "heart"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

If there were only words good enough
I'd come out and say them to you
But being with you makes it so rough
To figure out what's right to do

You're everything I could wish for
Someone exactly like me
Now I find I don't wish anymore
Because the stars fade, when you I see

I may not be the best at showing
When you're around the tounge gets tied
But hear you me, my heart is glowing
And in this paper, I'll confide

Thinking of you brings tears to my eye
Just something you make me do
One last thing, I could never lie
I'd be lost without you
