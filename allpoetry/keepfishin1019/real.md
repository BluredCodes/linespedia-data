---
title: "Real"
writer: "KeepFishin1019"
slug: "real"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I can't even believe
What you're doing to me
It all seems so real
But more like a dream

I hope I never wake up
To you not being there
Cause I'd swear it was real
More like a nightmare

So I'll dream and I'll dream
About your hold on me
Cause I want it to be real
As real as we can be
