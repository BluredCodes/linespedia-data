title: "Geography Song"
writer: "LeBirk"

I held your place
in line in front of
the Coffee Jag place
on half price night
and they were gonna
play those jumpy riffs
just for you
on the snare drums
and you said that
you were just going out
for cigarettes.

Just as that was sinking in
I got a postcard from you
with palm trees
and big gaudy red stamps
and a weird alphabet.

You always do that to me
but at least you always
come back and take
your place in line.
