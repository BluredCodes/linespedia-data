I loved the birds
but they flew too high
for my reach
and I loved my dog
but she had already been taken
(from behind)
also my cat was loved by me
but she disdained my neediness
and lack of independence
so she could exist alone
which left my white mare
an Arabian I named Calliope
and when I asked my mum
if I could marry my mare
she said that at least
the foals would not
be born as bastards.I deliberately left out the punctuation, save for the full stop at the end.
I thought to use line widths and breaks to serve as regulators of rhythm and stress. 
Should I have punctuated?  I'm trempted to go back and do that, yet at times, lack 
of punctuation lends a clean, pristine look to a poem. What do you think?
