and when she's down upon her kneesand she begs you and she pleadsfor you to take her in your heart,to give her one more fresh start,for you to take her by the hand,Do you ever understand?of do you look her in the eyewhile your look tells her good-byeand turn away for your own sake,          can't bear to see her heart break.and when she falls onto the groundwhen there's no one else aroundand the tears like bitter rainflow down her cheek againcould you take her in your armsand protect her from all harm... ...or would you look her in the eyewhile your look tells her good-byeand turn away for your own sake,          can't bear to see her heart break.-Jada Marie Andrews ©  on Aug 26 2001 10:27 AM PST   0  • 1
