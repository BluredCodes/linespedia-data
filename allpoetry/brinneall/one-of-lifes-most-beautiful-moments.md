Have you ever stood outsidein the pouring rainat that one instant when you've never felt more freeand miserable at the same timewhen you want to laughand cry    in equal amountsas you feel the droplets wash away your heartbeatinto the puddles beneath your feet. And you could keep walking and go into the warm safety of your house, but insteadyou wait,and feel the pounding sheets of rain roll off your face, down your arms, soaking into your clothes.      Never felt a rain like this before.It washes away so much more thanwhat's on the surface.Standing there,  looking up to watch    heaven's teardrops fall...As another one of lifes most beautiful moments   passes,      just passes you by...-Jada Marie Andrews ©  on Aug 26 2001 09:48 AM PST   0  • 12
