Come sit with me  in the shade of the trees    and I'll sing you the song      of the roses,Come, lie with me   in the sycamore leaves    and I'll sing you the secrets      of oceans.I'll sing you a song  for as long as you'll stay    and I'll sing you to sleep      in the moon's light,And I'll sing with a voice  sweet as summer's soft breeze    and I'll sing you the songs       of forever.I'll sing to you      now and forever.-Jada Marie Andrews ©  on Aug 26 2001 10:20 AM PST   0  • 8
