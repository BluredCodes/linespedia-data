Falling without knowledge
to the ground
Very pessimitic and depressing
we are found.
Splattering on pavement and grass,
Not making much sound
Only on rooftops
Do we ever pound.
Exsisting in the neverending circle
Do we fall back to the sky
Evolution is unessessary.
We are elevated so high
in the heat of summer
We refresh and fly.
So run free and dance
Dance with us before you die.
Quench your thirst
Quench your body, dance in the rain
Quench your thirst, and down the drain.
We are the beautiful,
We are the creators of green.
We go hand in hand with grey skys,
So it may seem.
Spiraling down, as the pipers play,
Upon the wind we lean.
Sliding down windows, sitting on leaves
Leaving behind a chrystaline gleam.
