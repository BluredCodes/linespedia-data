Walking down the street
I walk down past your house, anyway.
All the birds are singing hello to you,
And all the dogs are barking, but they dont have a clue.
You've come home,to yesterday again,
You want it like before you left,
You want to have a friend.
The world has changed you through the days,
I never really liked you much, anyway.
One sunny rainy day,
I look right past your crude face, anyway.
You run to catch up,
You slip and fall.
I'm no longer here to catch you,
Maybe you'll realize that too.
