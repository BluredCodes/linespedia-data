Life, Just one big game.
Life, All you cause is pain.
Life, As crazy as ever.
Life, Like bad weather.
Life, Just roll the dice.
Life, Dont let it pass by.
Life, 1 token per play.
Life, Take it day by day.

Life, Just one big emotion.
Life, Could be as big as the ocean.
Life, Can take you away.
Life, Can make you decay.
Life, It makes you think.
Life, Gone in a wink.
Life, So long yet so short.
Life, Just a popular sport.
