I can feel the cells hitting the walls of my veins.
Moving from side to side, the crashing continues,
until I take my last breath.
As I take my last breath,
I feel the last crash.
My body isnt used to this.
Im going through changes.
Changes that make me new to this world,
Changed that I've never known before,
Changes that perhaps mean something more.
As my soul escapes through the gaps of my mind,
As I leave my crash and burn body behind,
I leave this mask I've walked in some quite some time.
I feel a warm sensation travel through my being,
As I rise toward the clear blue sky and into space,

Everything is silent.

As I drift upon heavy oxygen,
My soul shatters into its own sphere.
My eyes open to the bright light,
I feel the wind, I feel alive.
I'm going through changes,
