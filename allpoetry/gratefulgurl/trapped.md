Trapped inside my body,
I feel a need to be freed.
Trying to break away,
an essential need.

Trying to break free of my shell,
my arms and legs curled.
Breaking through my shell
Ready to embrace the world.

Running free of my thoughts,
I have no worries.
Now being able to breathe,
My goal has been achieved.
