Moon dance to the sun,
Embrace the spirits on the run.
Lost anyway, far away,
From anywhere but here.

Midnight sky captures them,
Lonely alone composed again.
Gone away, long away,
From here but anywhere.

Painting colors across the sky,
Playing face across they fly.
Shimmer ayway, gold away,
From everywhere but here.

Fill your body spirits full,
Giving them hosts to pull.
Lost no more, for no more,
Everywhere and here.
