Awaken my soul,
For it has it has been asleep.
For hours on end,
And into the deep.

Out of the cage, 
The bird does fly.
Afloat on wing,
Into the steaming cool sky.

A breath of fresh air,
That fills my body... overflowing.
Feeling of good,
As if I am glowing.

Time being non-existant,
Allowing to lose myself.
Into thoughts and dreams,
Putting them along a dissfigured shelf.

Falling asleep,
And into a rutt.
Feeling of alone,
Like eveyone's door is shut.
