---
title: "By and By"
writer: "GratefulGurl"
slug: "by-and-by"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/50766-By-and-By-by-GratefulGurl"

You think you know me,
Like the back of your hand.
You think you can tell,
Whats been on my mind.
Well you have no idea,
suprise suprise.

Im more than just a soul,
Walking around in a horrid mask.
Im more than just that crazy girl,
Wearing my wide leg pants.
If you'd just stop a second,
and look at who i am.
You'd see that im more than willing,
To be one of the damned.

You think you know,
What goes on in my head.
Just a bunch of haze,
and nothing much said.
Well I think more than you would imagine,
I think a whole lot.
If you dont believe me,
Take your best shot.
Ask me questions,
Get inside my head.
If you dont soon,
We all may be dead.
You could have had the chance,
But you let sleeping dogs lye.
Now days and nights,
Just go by and by.
