---
title: "Simple"
writer: "AmandaLee"
slug: "simple"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

It's so beautiful
To see a sunrise
It's so peaceful
To feel a butterfly

It's helping an old man
To push his cart
It's giving a friend some camomile tea
While she pours out her heart

It's the simple things in life
That make you feel good
It's all that you do for others
It's appreciating what you should

When you look past a day gone wrong
And you feel so bad inside
You look at all the good you did
And at least you know you tried

It's so pretty 
To see a voilet and orange sunset
And hear child laughing in the rain
It's those moments you can't forget
And no moment is one in the same

So when you're feeling sad
And your tears begin to trickle
Just remember to open your mind and think
Of all those things that make life simple.
