---
title: "Him"
writer: "AmandaLee"
slug: "him"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/39542-Him-by-AmandaLee"
---

I look for him just to catch his eye
And when I do I look away
Could I, should I...would I?
Would I talk to him today?

I see him in the other room
And he looks so good to me
I love the way his neck slopes
And I love his white, perfect teeth

He smiles as bright as the sun
And his hair is as rich as gold
Do I have a crush on him?
Is it love?  I just don't know

Would I...could we...what if?
What if he doesn't like me?
I hide those thoughts within myself
Always longing them to be free!

Do you think he even knows my name?
How would he know what I feel?
And could I talk to him casually?
Does he notice the glances I steal?

I don't want to keep guessing!
I'm sick of screaming at myself!
I have a plan and I think it'll work
If I can just carry it out!

I brushed back my hair
And I walked over as if on a whim
I put my hand behind his head
And....I kissed Him.
