---
title: "I Don't Want to Hurt You"
writer: "AmandaLee"

I don't want to hurt you
I just feel so confused
About myself, and my life
I don't want you to feel used

Have a panic attack
Almost every day, 
I'm unsure of myself and my life
But I notice when you're away

I long for a medium
A way to deal with it all
Life is full of buildings
And i'm struggling up the wall

I don't want to hurt you
I care for you so much
I love you, and I'm scared
But I long for your touch

I'm just confused 
About myself and my life too
I love you and I care
But I don't want to hurt you
