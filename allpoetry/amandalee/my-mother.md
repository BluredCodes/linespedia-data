---
title: "My Mother"
writer: "AmandaLee"

Sometimes I remember 
A distant memory
Of me climbing into your lap as a child
And it seems like centuries
Although I never understood
Your ways or how you thought
I always looked up to you
And it was comfort that I sought
As I grow into a woman
I seek another woman's advice
And only you as my mother
Could teach me, as your vice
I know we can be as different
As night and day itself
But I am still learning
And finding things within myself
You will always be a part of me 
A part of me like no other
That voice that's always with me
You will always be my mother
