Everyone wants to see a smile on my face.
What is that suppose to replace?

Never try to guess what I'm feeling by the look on my face.
You will get the wrong imformation,
Your thoughts will be in the wrong place.

You don't know what is in my heart.
Never try to guess, that would
Not be very smart.

Just because I'm not smiling like eveyone else,
Does not mean I'm feeling sorry for myself.

You just be sure you have not crossed me the wrong way.
Then you can be assured , I'm having a great day.
