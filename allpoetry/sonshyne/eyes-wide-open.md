title: "Eyes Wide Open"
writer: "Sonshyne"

With eyes wide open I can see abroad,
At the start  of every new day, I thank the Lord.

I imagie being first as a bird.
I can recall words of wisdom, from my mom I;ve heard.

My body feels asleep and numb,
As if I'm waiting on the clock to alarm.

Feels like I'm floating in the air,
For nothing in this world, have I a care.
