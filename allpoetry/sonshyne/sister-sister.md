title: "Sister Sister"

No matter what I do,
My sisters will always be there.
Though they might not understand,
They are willing to do all they can.

My sisters are smart, and sweet.
They are also cute, and always neat.

From up close, or far away,
I can always trust in what they say.

We do not see each other everyday,
But I know that, they care for me.
I know by what they say.
And the things they do, lets me see.
