---
title: "Color Of My Skin"
writer: "Sonshyne"
slug: "color-of-my-skin"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/43790-Color-Of-My-Skin-by-Sonshyne"

You can not tell by the color of my skin
What I am within.

There is no way you can know,
Unless you are willing to go,
Go beneath what you see, 
And get to know the real me.

If you can get pass my outer shell,
You will be surprised,
I have a great story to tell.

No one can be judged by the color of thier skin.
If you want to know the real story,
Take the time, to look within.
