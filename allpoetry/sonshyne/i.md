I stride with pride and 
serve with grace,
the look of confidence
shows on my face.

I don't boast or brag,
but I sure don't walk around 
in rags.

My life is simple but sweet,
I don't consider proples expectations
they think I should meet.

My happiness rest in me 
and not someone else,
I can find tranqulity 
inside myself.
