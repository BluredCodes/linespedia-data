I can still hear your words
Spoken softly in my ear.
All the things you taught me, I remember.
Even though your not here.

I can remember you doing things alone.
You once told me to do all I can,
All I can do without depending on any man.
I can recall those words so clear.
As a child , I did not understand. But now
It's clear to me. I get the picture.
Even though your not here.

I still enjoy the fellowship that we have.
I still feel your love.
The love that flows from heart to heart
And breast to breast. I feel you very near.
Even though your not here.
