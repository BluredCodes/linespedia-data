It's a shame to be a racist.
How can you look people 
In thier faces?

You laugh and smile in thier face,
As if you are thier friend.
Shame on you, you know thats
Not the case.

Why waste the time?
You don't have to make the effort,
You don't owe anyone a dime.

If you have a problem with anyones race,
Just simply, stay out of thier face.
