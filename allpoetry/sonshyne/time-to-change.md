If it hurts you to see someone happy
With what they do ,
Maybe they are not the problem,
The problem is you.

Open your mind,
Try to be loving and kind.

It won't hurt to change your style.
Try if for just a little while.

You might like it better than being a prude.
You will be happier, maybe you won't be so rude.

It does'nt take a lot to be kind.
Think of how you want to be treated,
Keep that in mind.

You may even find a true friend
In the end.
