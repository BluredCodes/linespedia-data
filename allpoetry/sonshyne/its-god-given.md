At the time we feel, we could care the least,
God will give strength and peace.

He will not abandon us, he constantly there.
Through gladness, mourning,peace, and despair.

Ifwer abide in him he's always near.
He does not want us to have the spirit of fear.

He will carry us in our weakest hour.
For  he gives unto  us his strongf power.

The ability fo be peculiar, distanct as a dove.
He had empowered us, with his spirit of love.

He's always soft, loving and kind.
We've been blessed with a sound mind.
