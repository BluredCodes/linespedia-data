title: "Unspoken Words From Daddy"

I dreamed we said good bye last night.
At the time you were leaving this  world,
I 
Saw it, you smiled at me.

You had the look of relief on your face.
As if you wanted to say, I'm glad to be
Out of that place.
I 
Awoke in a frantic sweat.
It's so amazing, how in the end we met.

I have heard people speak of,
Communication with someones
Spirit when they were dying,
But until it happened to me,
I 
Thought they were lying.
