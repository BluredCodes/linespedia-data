When I feel the wind blowing,
It does something to my soul.
The sound of it opens my eyes.
Suddenly, I see everything that surounds me.

The silence is loud.
The chirping of the birds,
Turns the pages of my mind back to shen I was a child.
I see myself running nda playing, without a care in the world.

I enjoy the sound of running water. It sends me into a deep 
Meditation.
I began to realize how eminent Gods power is,
How mighty  his works of nature are.
I regonize how marvelous, how phenomenal his love for me must be.

If ever I forget,
I just stand still and listen to his words of wispers in the wind.
I look at the leaves falling from the tree,
As he blows them across the concrete and I hear
The ruffuling sounds they make.
Oh! There is always a reminder,
From the reflection in the lake.
