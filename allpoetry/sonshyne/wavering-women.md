title: "Wavering Women"
writer: "Sonshyne"

As pretty as a cats eye,
As sweet as a piece of pie.
Capable of change, like the direction of the wind.
As tranquil as a fish and the tank it,s in.

As sure as a bird that heads south when  it's cold.
As confident as Maya and just as bold.

As secretative as the C.I.A. 
A women had a trend of doing things her own way.

She can adjust to change.
Just be careful of what you try to rearrange.
Somethings just has to be done a certain way,
No matter what some people say.Do you think I should use the double spacing as I do, or should I let all my sentences go right under each other with no double spacing.
