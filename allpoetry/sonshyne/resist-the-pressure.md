---
title: "Resist The Pressure"
writer: "Sonshyne"

If you can say no to drugs,
You can say no to kisses and hugs.
This leads to other things,
No I don't mean a wedding ring.

Peer preasure can get strong,
Especially when they're trying to
Get you to do wrong.

Everything is in your hand,
Say no, take a stand.

Let no one persuade you,
Surely if it's not what you want to do.

Never be a follower of the crowd.
Be a leader, make your parents proud.
