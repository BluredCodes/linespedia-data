title: "One Person, Two Faces"

How many faces do you have today?
Not bold enough to say what you really want to say?

People may down talk you, and call you certain names.
Never worry about it, you just remain the same.

Never let what people say, affect what you want to do.
Remember who your doing it for, not him, or her, but for you.

I enjoy knowing that I have people's attention.
Quite often, in thier conversations, my name must be mentioned.

When you set out to ridicule others,
You isolate your sisters and brothers.

No one wants the stinch of a rat.
I decided long ago, I would never wear that hat.
