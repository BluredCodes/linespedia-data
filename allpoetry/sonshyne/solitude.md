As I gaze into the sky, my mind is quiet,
But there is noise all around me.
As I see the cars moving fast on the beltway,
I can hear everyones conversation.

Seems like I'm on the outside,
Looking in. Noone sees me here,
Noone feels my presence.j
I have the power to speak and they listen,
Whatever I say they just obey.

When you sit and watch and don't say a word,
You learn alot. You hear things you may have
never heard before.

To be seen and not heard is what I choose to do,
Try it sometimes, let it work for you.
