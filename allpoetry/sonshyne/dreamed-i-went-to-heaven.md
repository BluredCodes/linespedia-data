I dreamed I went to Heaven last night.
Everything was sparkling, the sky was so bright.
I was only there for open house,
Everyone was as quiet as a mouse.

If only you could have been there,
Then you would have felt the love in the air.

The streets were made of sparkling gold.
Therer was an inscription that read,
Bless The Lord Oh My Soul.

They welcolmed you as you entered the gate,
No matter what time you arrived, you were never late.

Oh the gates!
They were made of pearls,
In all shaped and swirls.

There was a sweet smelling frangrance in the air.
You saw smiled on peoples faces everywhere.

Suddenly, I was awakened from my sleep,
Forever this memory I will always keep.
