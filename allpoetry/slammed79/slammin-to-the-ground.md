It all starts high above the ground
Jet black as night wheels roll 'round
Chromey spoked rims with the low pros on
Badass show truck from now on

8 inches up now 7 inches down
The frame's an inch above the ground
Body sittin low with the air ride on
its a badass truck from now on

Big block motor done to the max
600 horse pumpin out the shaft
Engine runs rough but she goes fast
You see it comin but ya cant look back

Paint job wild as a wolverine
Body lines shaved and squeaky clean
Roof and floor chopped down three or four
Your ass is almost through the floor

Its a badass truck running down the street
Nobodys cars or eyes dare meet
This wild machine so fast and lean
