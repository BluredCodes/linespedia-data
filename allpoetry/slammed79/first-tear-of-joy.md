title: "First tear of joy"

With a tear upon my cheek
A smile upon my face
I read what you have sent me
Your hearts words give me grace
A constant reminder of who i am
A silent finder of the soul within
My tongue does no justice
To the words inside my heart
My life means so little
Without you to give it start
From the tips of my fingers
To the toes upon my feet
You are the dearest thing to me
The first and only thing for me
I love you for now and forever
