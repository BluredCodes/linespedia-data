All about my girl, Brittany Nichole

She makes me laugh when i'm depressed
Yet I cannot see why
Her name brings a smile to my long face
Eyes light up my dreary sky
The sound of her voice guides my day
Her gentle touch melts fear away
Shes my girl for now and to come
Yet i cannot see why :)
She loves me for who I am
I love her just the same


If you want to ask her anything - her name is 'Brittany'
