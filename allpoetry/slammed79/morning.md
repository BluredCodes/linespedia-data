I wake up in the morn
I Think of only one
I eat my breakfast and toast
I think of only you
I have my shower and shave
You are the one for me
I go to work and say
I love you, Brittany
You are my life
You are my soul
Hearing your voice makes me lose control
You are the only girl i think of
The first one that i dream of
The one i want to spend
With held forever in my arms
