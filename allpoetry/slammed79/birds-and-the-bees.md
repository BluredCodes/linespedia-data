title: "Birds and the bees"

Put beside an apple tree
Just the birds the bees 
Just you and me
I see you there smile on your face
I want to kiss you, grim looks to erase
To hold you forever
Forever and some more
To keep you by my side
Till the end of time does come
My heart swells at the thought
My soul bursts at the touch
Sitting under the apple tree 
The birds and bees gone
now just you and me
I hold you in my arms quite snug
Till the break of nite
through till the spread of dawn
My life devoted to you
my heart forever in your grasp
take care of it forever
Held with your gentle clasp
my love for you grows day by day
my heart doth follow suit
I love you now
I will love you tomorrow
And i will love you till the end of time
