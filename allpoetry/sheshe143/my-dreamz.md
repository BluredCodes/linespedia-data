sometimes my dreams scare methey haunt me while I'm weakdark images is what I seealone in terrorwhere can I possibly be?I am lostin my own mindin this haunting dreamof in a far placethat can never be seenrunning foreverin constant fearlooking for a way outout of this petrifying sceneI am lost in my dreamhelp me get out-----please------
