title: "~Waters of Tranquility~"
writer: "sheshe143"
slug: "waters-of-tranquility"

i wish that i was at the
ocean right now, laying upon
the shore, with you by my side.
we hold one another, while
gazing into each other's eyes.
with the stars shining down
in the midnight sky above,
passionately kissing you
as we keep each other warm.
gentle touches of comfort 
we feel, as the wind starts 
to tingle our skin
we hold each other tighter,
we feel more secure.
absolute ecstasy,
the highest of all highs.
such power in tenderness,
slowly bringing us to
great heigths.
like a painting,
showing signs of perfection,
just like the way you are.
something that has a meaning,
something that tells so much.
we watch the stars shine down,
each one with a story to tell,
each one is for every time
you made me smile 
and so much more
the stars are shining for you,
they are here to guide you,
and when all feels lost
and you feel as if you have
lost all hope, just
look to the sky and always
remember that i will be here
forever, just like the sky
being with you brings such
happiness to my life,
happiness is laying upon
the ocean, holding you
as the moon glares over us,
reflecting the waters
of tranquility......dedicated to ~ eRiC MiLLaRd~
