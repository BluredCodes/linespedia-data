where do we go when we die
do we go to heaven 
or is that just a lie
how do you know this 
is where we will be
how can you be so sure
if you cannot see
everyone has their own
thoughts of where we go when we die
maybe we don't even exist
right now...maybe we are already dead
and we just come back to life
do we really even die
or do we just go away
you will never know for sure
until the day comes for
you to see
maybe it's a place where 
we are going to be free
all I got to say is
that I know that death,
one day will overcome me
