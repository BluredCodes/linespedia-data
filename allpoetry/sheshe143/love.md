People say love is so special, 
myself, I don't really understand it. 
Love should not consist of anger, grief, and jealousy. 
Love should be shared with someone without hate, without any doubts of failure. To love someone does not 
mean to betray them. Love shall never cause pain and tears. Love is something so special, 
one shall not use, should never let you down. 
The main thing in love is trust.
 Everything revolves around trust. 
If you don't trust the person, you don't have nothing. 
Love is not to be disregarded, 
not to be teased. 
To love someone is to risk your heart from breaking.
 Most emotions are held back from love. 
Sometimes one does not want to express their true self.
 Maybe scared of heart ache.
 To expose your feelings is to 
risk exposing your true self.
..but only one who risks is free.
