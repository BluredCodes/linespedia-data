A single day without youis a day without sunshineyou light up my lifeyou always seem to make everything alrightyou care about me like no one elseyou make me feel appreciatedlike something specialdifferent from the restyou are there to guide meto help me through lifeyour eyes give me the strength to liveand to go each day without no worriesyou are speciala true work of artliving in complete happinessis the kind of life you bringforever in your arms is where I'll beBy sheshe143
