as the silence of thoughts 
begin to fade
the shadow of darkness
begins to show its light
after one has seen the light
you'll never turn back
for the light brings 
eternal beauty,
eternal life
follow the shadows through 
the light
hold the vision within
your mind
you'll see the true
color of darkness
