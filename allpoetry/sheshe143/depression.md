depression causes fearsdepression causes tears nothing hurts more than emotional painthis is somethingthat will never go awayit can never be healeddepression tears you up insidenot ever giving you a place to hideand all you can dois just live with itand try to bare itdepression causes confusiondepression causes hateyou begin to hate yourselfand all the pain thatis kept insidethe confusion hurtsthe depression hurtsI hurt
