title: "Cloud of Anger"

it is the cloud that
holds me in it's grasp
still showing me the
emptiness and providing
me with fear
this cloud of anger
controls how i think
and takes over
what i feel
it tells me to never forget
constantly reminding
me of the tears
i try and try
to overcome the pain
but all it's doing
is driving me insane
please take away my pain....
please let me end this game.....
