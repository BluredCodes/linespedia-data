i know we can do this
because i know we have the strength
i know that we will
make it through this
it may seem as though
it is going to be hard to do
but i know we will 
stand by each other with 
the strong bond and love we share
this love thats so true...
we will guide one another
through our times of greif
we will help one another
with our problems without
ever leaving out a piece
i know we can and will do this
for i can feel it in my
soul as well as my heart
i always knew you were
ment for me...i knew
this from the very start..
i will always have faith in you...dedicated to *Eric Millard*
