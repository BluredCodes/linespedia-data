it's all the little thingsthat hurt....they put youin fear of not knowingwhat to think. i ask myselfcan this possibly be true?all these things are storedin my mind till today...these are my doubts;the little things i fear.why does it have to beso hard just to be in love?why do i cry?why does it hurt so bad sometimes?i guess i just want allof the answers......i can't stand the feelingof not knowing.......
