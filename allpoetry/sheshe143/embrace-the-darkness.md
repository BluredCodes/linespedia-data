---
title: "Embrace the Darkness"

embrace the darkness
for the darkness will bring light
it will brighten up your days
and let all your fears pass by
the beauty within the darkness
will tell you the truth
you never have to hide
from the pain you feel
deep inside
hiding makes it worse
you have to face the pain
to see if you have the strength
the power to overcome the pain
and find your way out
even though sometimes
it never goes away
but with the light of the darkness
you don't have to be afraid
you are stronger than the pain
it tries to take over your body
but it is all in your mind
fighting it will make it worse
you need to let the pain
fade away it's self
you can never just make it go away
so why even try
all I know is that I am the beauty 
of the darkness
I am the light that leads you through 
I will make all the pain go away
just close your eyes
and set your mind free
with the pain
that is now gone
I can be me
