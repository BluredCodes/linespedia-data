title: "'SaD bUt TrUe'"

oh yes! 
it's sad but true
that i have this 
pain in my mind
whatever will i do
it's a release 
i must find
desperately, 
i am seeking 
to find the way
as i said once before
"it's sad but true"
but i still hope to
find it one day
