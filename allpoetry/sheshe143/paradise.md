*

a fantasy thought 
of when happiness appears
when all good is remembered 
and your guilt will be cleared
a time that brings words that
conversations couldn't hold
having someone to care for
who's heart is never cold
if you someday have a chance
to visit this place
'paradise' i call it
no love you will chase
compared to a heaven 
but there is a way to sin
not to let an innocent frown
overcome a grin
remembering promises 
you would sometimes say
ecstasy is now a feeling
that will never go away
you never gain a friend,
that way you won't have to choose
setting goals are done by him
worrying what to lose
once you arrive,
 you won't want to leave
destiny and happiness are all
you must achieve
reason is, because you're sad
you cannot find this place
my soul is in serenity,
i'm lost without a trace

*
