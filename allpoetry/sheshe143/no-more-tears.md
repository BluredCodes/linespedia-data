---
title: "::No More Tears::"

there are no more tears left inside...
these are the feelings...I must not hide
I have cried my eyes out
while I've been in constant doubt
but now I am done crying
I am done being depressed
I am going to live my life
as if nothing ever happened
I am now happy...
and this will show
I have tucked away
the painful emotions....
not never will they be exposed
I am ready to go through life
forget the past...
and hide the strife
I can be a confident person
without any fears
just be myself
and forget the tears
and why even try to cry...
when there are...
no more tears....
