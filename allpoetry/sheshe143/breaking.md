---
title: "Breaking..."
writer: "sheshe143"

---

i feel as though
i am breaking
i am losing a 
part of myself
my soul is 
slowly fading
in which this
i can feel
i see the pain
that rests in 
your eyes
i cannot bare
this pain
in which i cry
you can't do this
i need you
to be here
we need each other
please don't do this
i don't want
you to die...
