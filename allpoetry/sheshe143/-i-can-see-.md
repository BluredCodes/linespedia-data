i can see
  deep inside your mind
devouring your thoughts
        i can see
 all of your fears 
   sensing every tear
        i can see
 so much compassion
   love so real
        i can see
     your every need
   for this i can also feel
        i can see
   you are a part of me
        i can see
    you really do care
