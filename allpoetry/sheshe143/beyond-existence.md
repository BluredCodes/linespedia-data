what is beyond this world...beyond everything that existsbeyond the futureand beyond our only wishwill we ever see whatz out there,far beyond timewill we ever know how to get thereall these things we'll have to bearfor our own sakenot nothing so rarewatching the stars late at nightalwayz thinking something's not quite rightwill we ever know whatz really thereDo we even really care?one day we'll all see thingsthat shall not be
