all I see is darknesswhere am I?am I dreaming or is this reality?I cannot tell the differenceI am floating upon the darknesscan you see me?I can see youI watch as you drift off into the darknessas reality followsdo you feel that?it's so coldI think we just entered another placeanother place in timewe rise above the horizon,above the darkness,and above it allI can see itit's the vision of beautywe don't have to give up nowwe are on our wayon this journey of lifewe will need to have strengthto make it all the waycuz I know we will make it somedaywe will follow the lightit will lead us throughto the place where a dream really does come trueyou don't have nothing to fearI am by your side foreverholding youBy sheshe143
