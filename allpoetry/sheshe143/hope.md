Where would we be without hope?Hope for the future.Hope for everything we have.Where could we possibly go without it?What would we do without it?With all the pain that life brings,We would always need to have hope.Hope for ourselves.Hope for each day when you wake up,knowing that life does go on.To look towards the future.With hope your future can be beautiful.Without hope where would we be?By sheshe143
