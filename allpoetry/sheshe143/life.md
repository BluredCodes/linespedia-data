Life is something
we can not control
you never know 
what will happen next
and where you will go
we are always hoping
for the best
in this world today
but why are there 
so many rules
we must obey
we all ourselves
why does it have to be this way
why can't we be free
sometimes we feel trapped
like the pressure
is holding us down
you can control your own life
if you try really hard
the only thing
we can't control
is how and when 
we are going to die
as we all know life is
filled with confusion
but we all must go on.....
