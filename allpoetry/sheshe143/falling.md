Falling
into this world that's unknown
trying to follow your dreams
to make it all worthwhile
forever trying to make everything alright
but you now see you have lost sight
holding onto the past
hoping for the day to come
when you'll have a firm grasp
of your dreams that are yet to come
all that we are is what we think
and remember to never let go
never lose hope
and believe in yourself
never fear yourself
never let the hate get in your way
for all you hopes, dreams, and wishes
will come true someday
always wishing for the perfect life
hoping you will live your dream
but you can only find 
the true meaning of life
if you know how to find the truth
the truth of others
and especially your trueself
what is the meaning of the truth?
maybe it is just what we say it is
but at the end
we will see...................
we will see it all
every little thing forgotten
every little thing remembered
we will all make it someday
just never lose the hope
that we all seem to need the most
never forget how to be yourself
cuz it will soon all be over
you don't have to be afraid
