I want to drift off
way up high
to where there are no fears
to my place in the sky
I will never have to shed a tear
for there is absolute 
beauty found there
I will be filled with happiness
nothing to be afraid of
will ever come near
this is a place where all
the pain and sorrow disappears
where you will never feel alone
having nothing to fear
flying high through the sky
feeling free 
away from time
loving every breath
every taste of the air
flying in a neverending fantasy
feeling nothing 
but absolute ecstasy
