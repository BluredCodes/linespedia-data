---
title: "Because of You"

i constantly ask myself
"Why am i still here"
why wasn't this soul
taken from me
so much pain i endured
i'm amazed i'm still alive
death has teased me
it only gave me a glimpse
but not enough to see
i want to see whats
beyond reality
death has refused me
why didn't it take me?
why am i still here?
i think its love
that saved me
it gave me the strength
i needed to hold on
the power kept me
in it's arms
reminding me that 
everything is going 
to be alright
and every time 
i opened my eyes, 
even if it was just 
for a split second,
i called out your name
and began to cry
because of you is
the reason why
