---
title: "We will be One"
writer: "sheshe143"

look into my eyes
and tell me what you see
do you see the pain that
you implanted in me?
i am hurting inside
there has been many
times i have cried 
this pain i can't take
but i do know that you
really do care
why do you say these things?
it's tearing me up inside
i don't ever want
to lose you...
i beg you please
never go away
you have so much
to live for
you may not see it
but in your heart
you know it is there
please don't be afraid
we will make it through this
we will be one...
