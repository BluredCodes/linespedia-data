as I open my eyes, trying to catch my breathI see you standing therewith a beautiful smile on your faceas I gaze into your eyes, I realized that I made you smileI begin to smile as wellthere's something about you that I can't ever explainyour eyes give me strengththey keep me alivethey give me hope and make me strongthey give off a feeling of security and warmthas I gaze into your eyesI now see where forever lies
