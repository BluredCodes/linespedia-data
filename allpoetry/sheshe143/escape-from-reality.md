i am going to leave this worldthis place in time, we call realityi feel as though i need an escapei'll only be gone for a little whilei need to experience the feelingof being free, free from the pain,free from the suffering life bringsi'm afraid that once i get therei'll never want to turn backdo you believe i will find peace?maybe i will find freedom ori will find truth...the reason for beingand why we live for deathi will see why we have toendure so much paini will tell every little detailonce i find my way,but that's only if i don't decide to stayi wrote this piece before my accident....i did not find peace. i found pain.
