anytime you need me
i will be right by your side
you will never have to worry
about the hurt that you may hide
all the thoughts i think about
and all the words i say
will never make life easier
or make the pain go away
but one thing to remember
is i'll always be around
whether it is physically
or buried underground
nothing can ever change the
friendship that we have got
not a single soul will choose
the pain that we have brought
the hurt we feel and tears we cry
will one day disappear
but just remember, one day at
a time, i will always be hereby:jezzika howard:
