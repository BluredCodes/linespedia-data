---
title: "another place in time"
writer: "sheshe143"
slug: "another-place-in-time"

in another place 
and another time
my mind roams free
with nothing to fear
I sit alone 
gazing upon the beauty
of what we call reality
while overlooking upon
the beauty of 
what we call light
this must be the 
light of reality
without it where 
would we be?
would we even exist?
how do we know that
we are living in reality?
what is beyond reality?
what is beyond the light?
would we be able to breathe
in what we call air?
would we suffer in dire pain
in the place 
beyond reality, beyond time?
the reality of us
feeds on the light
in another place
and another time
I sit alone
and wonder why we always
try to run away from the light
we try to run away from
this place, so called reality
we try to hide from ourselves
but there is nowhere to run
or nowhere to hide
we are living in reality
we have things to fear
why can't we just be free???
