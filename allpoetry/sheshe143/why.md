why does love have to hurt so bad?
'we ask ourselves'
why is it that love always
seems to cause pain?
maybe love is pain
they don't mean the same thing
but they both can hurt
how can there be so much love,
yet enough pain to pull you back?
does love even exist
or is it just what we say it is?
there are so many painful memories...
deep emotions that fill my thoughts...
making me feel trapped.
but i don't understand that
if you are in love with someone,
why does there have to be pain involved?
why is it that jealousy takes its role in love?
why can't love just be what it means?
why can't we just be in love?
