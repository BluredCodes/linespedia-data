---
title: "((The Truth))"
writer: "sheshe143"

the truth always seems to hurt
nothing can ever change the past
even if you tried
some things are hard to live with
but this is more serious than anything before
the truth can sometimes make you feel empty
like nothing at all
the loneliness of the truth can make you cry
even make you want to die
sometimes I feel I just want to go away
away from this world and away from the truth
I hurt so bad inside
it has taken over a part in my mind
this part is full of sadness
full of anger and pain
just thinking about the truth 
makes me break down and cry
you know, I really don't understand why
why I cry?
does it really even hurt?
