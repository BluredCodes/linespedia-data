while I gaze out the window
watching the clouds sway by
I often wonder if just one day 
I can fly
drifting upon the clouds 
in the ever so clear sky
not a fear in the world
will ever pass me by
floating into the future
to a place so sweet
a place with every cure
while I'm feeling so weak
I often wonder 
if I could just fly away
above the clouds is where I'll stay
away from the world
in my place of happiness
this is my only wish 
as I gaze upon the clouds
from out the window
