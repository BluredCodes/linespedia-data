i awoke from this sleep
and just realized what
has happened to me
many say i have been
blessed, but i don't
think i am any different
from the rest
i survived the crash,
so many things happened 
so fast....i don't remember
the terror, yet i feel it inside
sometimes i ask myself why
am i still alive?
why was this pain brought 
to me? i guess this is
something i will never see
there are so many questions
that will always be left unanswered
i believe that at the moment
i may have died
but ignored the light
and found my way
and deep in my mind
while in another state
of consciousness, i prayed
i prayed for another
chance to live this life
i must now look to the future
and somehow hide the strife
i am so lucky to be here today
and i have now realized
life is precious
and here is where i will stay((i wrote this piece after i got out of the hospital, after my accident on Sept.1st.))
