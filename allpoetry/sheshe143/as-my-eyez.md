title: "as my eyez........."

as my eyes begin to gaze
I see the movement
the movement of time
very slow, yet so extreme
I close my eyes
nothing but darkness
I gaze into the darkness
I can see my light
my eternal flame
I can feel the heat
as it burns through my soul 
I am falling motionessly
enjoying the fear that's found there
traveling far in my mind
I have now lost reality
it seems so far
I follow the bright lights
in my place of eternal peace
I get to the center of it all
and now I begin to see 
I can now see my true self
my every fear
nothing is really as it seems
