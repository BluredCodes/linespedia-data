I am so scared....why iz the fear still there?sometimes it seems as though itcan control my thoughtsthe pain I once received...and the memory of it isin the back of my mindI can try to forget it..which seems impossiblebut I know it will never go awaynothing ever goes away...I cry no tearsas I cry on the insideI often cry myself to sleepbut I have now realizedthat crying does not help...it doesn't make anything betterjust tell what do I have to do to make itall go away...........Copyright © sheshe143
