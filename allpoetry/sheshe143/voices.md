voices set in your mindstill calling your namethis is what I cannot substainhearing the whispersin the silence of thoughttrying to tell you that everything is lostseems like the thoughtsare never going to go awaythey are teasing your mindmaking you afraidtearing up your thoughtsand taking overnow it's time for the confusionthe voice tells it's liesletting you hear all the criesthe voices are getting louderyou don't know what to doyou close your eyes tightto keep from going insaneit is quiet no sounds are nearnow I know that nowthere is nothing to fearBy sheshe143
