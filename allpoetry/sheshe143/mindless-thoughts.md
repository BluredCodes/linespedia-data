---
title: "((Mindless Thoughts))"
writer: "sheshe143"
slug: "mindless-thoughts"
source: "AllPoetry"

.
.
.
.
.
.
.
.
.
Mindless thoughts are in my head
maybe soon I will be dead
my body is lying
while I'm dying
my body is aching
my body is breaking
I feel my soul coming out
by then I begin to shout
I wake up in a place that is white
everything is out of sight
I started to scream
then I see a beam
people start to call my name
I see my body while people are in shame
I go back in my body
I begin to see everybody
I am alive
I'd never thought I would survive
.
.
.
.
.
.
.
.
.
