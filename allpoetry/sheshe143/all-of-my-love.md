---
title: "*All Of My Love*"
writer: "sheshe143"
slug: "all-of-my-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

why does it have to be so 
hard sometimes...
to the point where
all you want to do 
is just give up...
deep down, i know 
i can't give up
because i need this...
i need these eyes that
look into me,
i need this kiss that
soothes my soul,
i need this love
that we share,
to have and to hold...
most of all,
i need you; my light...
the one who keeps me
going each day in this
world thats unknown
i need what we have
to live my life...
all of my love is for you,
every inch, ever so true...
