my heart beats faster
when i look into your eyes
you give me this intense feeling,
something i can't exactly describe
you make me feel special
in so many ways
you let off these 
rays of beauty
leaving me in
a trancing daze
your eyes are my happiness
your eyes are my strength
my heart beats faster
as you slowly 
squint your eye
and *wink*dedicated 2 :eric millard:
