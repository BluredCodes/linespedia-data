---
title: "::i believe::"
writer: "sheshe143"
slug: "i-believe"

i believe that the second
our soul leaves our body
we face all the little things
we may have forgotten
we face all the scars we
have left in one's mind
as the darkness draws closer
we see a pair of eyes
eyes that you have
never looked into before
these are your own eyes...
scary to know that you
don't recognize them....
they are crying...
pouring out streams of tears
tears from the fear of the pain 
you have caused another
for each time you have hurt...
while looking into these eyes
you begin to see...you begin
to see the shame... 
you ask yourself why..why did
i have to hurt the people i love?
now you see the terror...
now you see what its all about...
just remember that everything
comes back to you in the end...
