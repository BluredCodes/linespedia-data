---
title: "my shining star"
writer: "sheshe143"

source: "AllPoetry"

flying high above the clouds
letting every thought fill the sky
leading me to my shining star
which I know can't be too far
reaching out to taste the wonder
rising above my every fear
grasping on to my shining star
I will now never have to be afraid
for my every fear has been taken away
this is my star that brings 
magic through thought
drifting far beyond time
with me and my shining star combine
sailing above the world so high
with complete happiness 
running through my mind
