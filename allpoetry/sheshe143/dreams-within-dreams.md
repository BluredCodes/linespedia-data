as I wake up knowing
where I would be
with dreams within dreams
you never know how
it's going to be
I wake up in the wrong place
knowing that's not where I wuz
I don't know what to think
and where I could be
all that I know is that I am free
everything seems that if 
it could not be
dreams within dreams
has taken over me
I wake up again
I know this is where I'm suppose to be
I go back to sleep
cuz I know where to go to be free.
