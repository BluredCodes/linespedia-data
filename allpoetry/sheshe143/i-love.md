i love when you tell me
that you love me
and i'm all that you
could ever ask for
i love that you are
such an amazing
and caring person
you are everything
i need and so much more
i promise to always
be the one who will
make you feel safe and secure
this love is filled with
such magical beauty
a love so pure.....((smewm))
