so much paini feel insideso many tearsi can never hidei miss you......i need you......i know this feelingwon't last for longcuz soon my lovewe will we hand in handthe way it hasalways been....and the way itwill always bejust wait and seewe will make it throughi swear my lovefor you is ever so trueclose your eyes baby,dream a little dreami'll be therewaiting for youwaiting to takeyou in my arms...so much lovewe will sharea dream come truesuch compassionbeyond comparededicated 2:Eric:
