~*~*~*~*~*~*~

i watch you as you sleep

you look so peaceful,

so warm,

so perfect

i wrap my arms around you

you let out a little *sigh*

then begin to smile

i lay my head upon your chest

i feel so safe

there's no one in this world

that could ever take your place

~*~*~*~*~*~*~dedicated 2 ~eric~
