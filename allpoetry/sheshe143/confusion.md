lost within your own world
looking for a way out
the confusion is there
staring right back
running forever
getting lost with confusion
feeding throughout your soul
searching for the right path
that will chase the confusion away
but first you must learn to obey
watch for the darkness
and stay away from the light
you must know all of this
so you can always get it right
confusion is a game
a game to test your strength
the power of your mind
your mind will lead you through
beware of the confusion
it's a very hard game to play
it teases your mind
letting bad thoughts enter,
wrapping you around its finger
and never letting go
it takes your ability to think for yourself
it makes you feel empty
once the confusion has fully entered
it takes you into its maze
testing you to see if you can find a way out
confusion is a very hard thought
confusion always seems to make you feel lost
