---
title: "Your Loving Touch"
writer: "sheshe143"

feeling you is needing you
needing your loving touch
wanting to be in you arms
and never letting go
I need you
just like I need to breathe
without you,
what will happen to me?
I look deep into your eyes
knowing that I am lost in them
lost in your love
never looking for a way out
I want to be your future
that is no doubt in my mind
I need you more than myself
I need you to live
I need you just for the sake
that you are a part of me
and without you
what would I be?
you are the source of my life
the only being I need
