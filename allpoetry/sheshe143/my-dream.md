title: ":::My Dream:::"

I wish I could fly away
to a place where it's an
everlasting dream
a dream that brings no
worries, no anger, and no pain
a place where I am free
free from the world 
and free of time
a place to go and abandon
all the fears life brings
this place is so perfect,
so pure
a place of great beauty 
that is unexplainable
the stillness in time
will bring music
in the silence of thought
