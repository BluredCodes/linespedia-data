if you were to ever leave this world,i would follow you..i would follow you to the othersidei want to live this life with you..i wouldn't ever want you to go awayi want for us both to live in happiness...without any fearsi see your pain...i can see it in your tears..i can also see it when i look into your eyesi want to take your pain away so badso you won't feel any hurt or endure anymore paini want to be the cure for everylittle fear you havei want to be the one who will take away the shameit hurts me so bad to see an ounce of pain in your beautiful eyesall i can do is breakdown and cryi am so sorry that you are hurting insidei want you to know that i willbe the one to guide you through......i want you to know i will always be here for you.........
