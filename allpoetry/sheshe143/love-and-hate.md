---
title: "Love and Hate.........."
writer: "sheshe143"
slug: "love-and-hate"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/1350-Love-and-Hate..........-by-sheshe143"
---

longing for the touch of love
hoping for the hate to linger on
forever holding on
by the strings that are left
trying to make the hate go away
which is the only thing
that is holding me back
for the love of hate
is unbearable
loving the beauty
hating the fear
hoping for the day to come
for it all to disappear
the fear of love
has to be full of hate
but can this be true
if you think you have
found your soulmate
forever trying to let go
but still holding on to
the hate of the fear
deep inside
just hoping for the love
to overcome the fear
hating the fear
loving the tear
the tear from hate
the tear from all the fear
love brings nothing but
confusion
but most of all 
it's the fear from the hate
that always seems
to be holding me back
