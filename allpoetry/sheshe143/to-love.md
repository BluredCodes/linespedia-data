To love someone is such a beautiful thing. 
Actually knowing that someone cares for you so much, that they would do anything for you. 
Love is the key to happiness. 
Love is a necessity. 
I say that you should always have someone to love and be loved back.
 It makes you feel so good inside.
 To know that someone could be thinking of you the exact moment you are thinking of them. 
The person in my life is a big part of me. Without him, where would I be? Every thought is of him. 
I could never get the thought of him out of my mind.
 I have never felt such love in all of my life. 
True love between two people is infinite and immortal. 
I believe in love and I believe, actually, I know that true love only comes once in a lifetime and I have found my true love, my future, my baby, my soulmate. 
Every day I think about the future and how beautiful it is going to be.

By sheshe143
