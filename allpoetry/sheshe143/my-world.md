in my mind
i feel as if there
is another world
outside of our world
another place beyond,
what we call reality
that eyes had never
before seen.......
sometimes it seems 
as though i find myself
searching for the way
i fantasize of this world
or could it be a dream
that only exists in my mind?
or do you believe there 
is a way that it can be true?
a place where you can be free
deep down inside i feel there
has to be more to existence...
more to the creation of time
i feel that there is more to it all
everything hidden from our vision
and everything hidden from our mind
this world may only be my dream
or my thoughts of what i see
but someday i will find this place
if only i can remember 
where i put the key.......
