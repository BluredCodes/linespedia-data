the sun lets off signs of comfortto guide you through the dayfor the beauty of the sunwill forever staythe brightness of the sundoes not stay for to longit's true beauty is in the nightforever singing it's peaceful songstare into the light even though for a momentyou can't seethe beauty of the sunwill forever beby~sheshe143~
