i want you to touch my body
gently slide your fingers 
upon my skin
i want to feel the power
in your touch
and the warmth of your lips
softly and passionately 
teasing me
asking me to beg for more
i am in complete happiness
it is you who i adorededicated to :eric millard:
my baybee!!!
