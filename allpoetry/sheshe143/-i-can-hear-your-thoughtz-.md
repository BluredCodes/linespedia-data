I can hear your thoughts
and taste your fears
I can see the emptiness 
in every tear
I can feel this because
you are a part of me
you are the part that 
controls my feelings
when I look deep 
into your eyes
I can see your future 
and where it lies
between you and me
we have a closeness
that never dissipates
and your voice
lingers in my mind
I feel that you are 
the one being I need
to go on
please don't be afraid
please don't ever shed a tear
because I am always
going to be right here beside you
to comfort you
and to make sure you keep
a distance from the darkness
I want to be the one who
knows what you are thinking
I want to be the one
who will feel your pain
I want to always be 
with you --without any fears
I just want to be
in love with you
