---
title: "::Fear::"

open your eyes
I can now see your fear
the fear you
brought upon yourself
it's the only thing you have to hide
hoping it will never show its true self
you are afraid of the effects of the fear
the little secret you hold so dear
you are afraid
I can see it in your eyes
this secret must be tearing you up inside
living with the fear
tucked deep inside
where will you go?
there is no where to hide
face the fear
show your true self
let me see your strength
tell me what you think
don't tell no more lies
I am truly sick of all the cries
the fear that lives inside will never go away
this is where it will forever stay
