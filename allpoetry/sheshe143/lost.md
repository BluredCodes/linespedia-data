---
title: "LOST"
writer: "sheshe143"
slug: "lost"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

have you ever felt as if you were lost?
lost so far from reality,
without any way of getting back...
or is it that you aren't even 
looking for the way?
do you enjoy being in your own world?
does it make you feel more secure?
you may be lost,
 but you are still in reality
and while you are here,
there will never be anywhere to hide
we face our fears everyday
in this world of pain
i think we are just trying to hide
could it be that we are trying
to hide from ourselves
it must be the pain that
is telling us that we are lost
but maybe we are just stuck...
in this place we call reality
or perhaps we are stuck in our own mind
but do you know if the door
is even locked or did
you happen to misplace the key?
we always seem to be searching
for something...
do you even  know what you are searching for?
is it the truth that we need
that will open up our minds
and set us free...
do you really know if you are stuck
inside or is it just the little
things we try to hide?
