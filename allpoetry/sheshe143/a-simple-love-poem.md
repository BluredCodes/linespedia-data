I long for the warmth 
in you touch
for you are the 
one I love
I care for you
more than I do myself
I have so many
unspoken emotions...
I want you to see them all
day after day....
more in love with you
I will fall....
I promise I will always 
be right here with you
and I never thought
I'd find a love so true...
you are my love...
you are my light...
you are the one
who's there beside me..
in my dreams....
night after night...

::dedicated 2::my baby ~Eric Millard~
