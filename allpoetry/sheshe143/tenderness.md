---
title: "Tenderness"
writer: "sheshe143"

tenderness is love
love is emotion
i feel the warmth
caress my soul
such sweetness
i taste
as i tenderly
kiss your lip
i gaze into
the light
for it is
the light
in your eyes
leaving me
totally mesmorized
