Why must we fight?
Why do we play these childish games?
i am sick of all the tears..
i want to be with you,
without being in fear..
i know we both want this love to last...
there is one thing i need to do
and that is to forget about the past
i am going to do this...
this is what i will achieve..
i look to the future and see us 
hand in hand.....
i know this is the way 
it should always be
our love is so strong...
and we must not let the pain overcome
we will be the future, 
just wait and see
we just have to forget about
all these painful memories..
i swear i will never lie to you,
i swear i will always have faith
you are my destiny......
the one being i truly need...
we must not let these little
fights get in our way...
we will make it baby...someday...
