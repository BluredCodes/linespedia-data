title: "::PAIN::"
writer: "sheshe143"

pain is the sickness
that confuses the soul
it overtakes your mind
makes you feel like
nothing at all
feeling the pain is hard to bare
why does it have to hurt?
why does it have to be this way?
the sickness of pain
digs a hole in your mind
forever grasping on
never letting go
making the confusion of the pain
take over your soul
while you're crying
sad with despair
all the pain brings is laughter
making you think that it really does care
pain is something that never
seems to go away
forever in your mind
is where it will stay
