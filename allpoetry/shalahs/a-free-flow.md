title: "a free flow..."

Is it overwhelming?
drowning u in iniquity
that den from which no one can flee
see, truthfully,
the only salvation
the only redempion 
is Thee
and time will tell
the lies that fell from your lips
can be washed away
if you leave them where they lay
in the past
those last moments you've known
can only be shown in the rear view
so to be born anew
you
must be that change you wish to see,
break those chains that seem to be
strangling
dangling your ravaged body from that tree
of shameful ant-I-individuality
its not dependence its repentance thats key
so show me you care
are aware of these circumstances
these lopsided chances
hypocritical stances
of strangers who dont know u...
cant see you...
could never ever be u...
even if they tried to
cuz no one can safely hide in the clothing of another,
only security is in the arms of a mother...
whether she be the bosom of love,
the peace of calm doves
or on the wing of your dream flyin above
so love, find your way
and day to day live it
know it
be it
to the fullest	
until your belly aches from that marrow,
and your heart soars like the sparrow…
