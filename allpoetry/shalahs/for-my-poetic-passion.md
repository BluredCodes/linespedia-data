---
title: "for my poetic passion:"
writer: "shalahs"

you are the mental poison i have chosen
as the blood rush flows and
rose cheeks show that 
no
I have not the power to fight
the might of warriors 
whose poetic potion has seeped
yes
reached within to a depth 
some would call: sin
but i name it according to its origin
my form of gin
a tonic of tones expressed
never repressed or hushed
just divinely touched
thus, i call it...
perfection
