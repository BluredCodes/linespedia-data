perhaps ill be left to carry the torch aloneexpress lightly toned verses to the shadowsthe memories of one once intimately bound but in time found to be as far as darkness can travel by nightI might whisper my secrets into the ear of your eyesAs you read and hear thoughtsfeelings that have you caught in the traces of happinessI understand that time and space may not be forgivingThus immediate living and loving cloud the need for a distant voiceSo as its your choice I also control one similarAnd I chose to maintain the familiar exchangeIn it I find inspiration to change, evolution through painAnd the simple joy felt in reminiscence of your wordsCrafted deliberately, passionately shining brilliantlyIgniting the waxen flow from our candle of lyricality
