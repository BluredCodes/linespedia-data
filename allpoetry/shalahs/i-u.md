title: "i u"
writer: "shalahs"

To start, i send to u a soul's tear
one not of fear neither even of joy
simply the overflow of emotions tightly kept
expressed in the vacuum of my mind's heart
So as the essence of pleasure begins to leak
I find it necessary to speak its lyrics
To the spirit from which it became
The shedding of shame was the opening act 
To which reaction brought satisfaction
until now when our soul-ar attraction brings
gravitational pull 
on the string of my soul’s stream
