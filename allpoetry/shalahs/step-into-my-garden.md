title: "step into my garden"

Earthen events transformed delicatelyProcessed to purity within the void of wisdomLeft to ponder If all life is suffering, and jealousy like the graveCan a spirit behave with self in mindFind dignity in the footsteps evenly imprintedAnd the dissemination of sense to thoseWho might only half deserve her mercyIs it sin to give love rationallySilently lacking mercy for passionShunned loneliness replaced by actionsDrastically dramatized by puppets of knowWhen you get it, and truly understandThe possibilities entangle you like vinesAnd the hedonistic wines of pleasure Can never fix your thirstFirst times prove to lead the lifelineThe fateful walk towards an end not anticipatedMaybe she can shake it and resist confidentlyPerhaps she'll see the truth behind the need for external bliss The kiss of deathEarthen envy of life's true gift
