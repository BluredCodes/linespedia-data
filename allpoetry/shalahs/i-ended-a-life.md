---
title: "i ended a life"
writer: "shalahs"
slug: "i-ended-a-life"
source: "AllPoetry"

i ended a life for moments of pleasure irresponsibly
i entered an act without taking the world that came with it
and for the rest of my life i will mourn a life, i took

when i look thru rational-eyes,
the thought of ever repeating such actions
seems undeniably selfish, and i can say,
sinful

but in the moment i sometimes fail to see these things
