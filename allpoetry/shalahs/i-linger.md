---
title: "i linger"
writer: "shalahs"
slug: "i-linger"
source: "AllPoetry"

Somewhere in that zone between
What seems and my dreams
Scenes of you flicker past my lids
These like the eyes of a kid
A child, a novice to these dealings
Stealing lessons of maturity
Thru attempts to taint purity
But to no avail
My veil maintained by spirit guides
Though stained it still hides
Shields that which lies protected beneath
My silken sheath woven by divine hands
But one day you will stand before me 
As though you understand what I need
Raise my cloak and immediately
Create a nest gently
Within your chest house my soul
While you control outward defense
Inside I will commence to nourish your spirit
With the warmth of love
Then you will feel it
