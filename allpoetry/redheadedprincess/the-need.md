Been alone a long time,what is wrong with me?Is everyone so blind?I have so much to offer,a lot of love to give,no one wants to share it with me,how am I to live?Rejected,dejected,unwanted andJaded,My dreams and hopes have all quickly faded.If someone would reach out their hand to me,love me deep in their soul,my life would be so complete,we would both be whole!~~*RHP*~~
