Once you loved me,once you lied,I trusted in you,my heart has died.I miss you so,you don't care,not having you is...too much to bear.I forgave you then,still you left,in my soul,our life I kept.No one else will take your place,Come home to me,or tomorrow I cannot face.~~*RHP*~~
