I looked for you forever,around every corner,in every coffee shop.I thought I found you once or twice,you never stayed long...and each time you broke my heart.I caught a glimpse of you,but only for a second,you became disinterested in meand so easily found somewhere else to abide.I stopped looking,hoping that you would soon find me,alas, I am still without you!You allusive love!~~*RHP*~~
