I would die
just to see you smile
I would die
So I'd never have to see you cry

I used to think I was so important
I used to think the world was mine
Then one day I had a revolation
Then one day I had a explanation
I never really cared, if I lived or died
I just knew I couldn't do either alone

That's why you mean so much to me
That's why I would die
Just to see you smile
And I would die
So I'd never have to see you cry

I used to want my own happiness
I used to like playing around
These days, it's your happiness
That gives me mine
If it makes things better in your life
It's ok if I have to die

I would die
Just to see you smile
I would die
So I'd never have to see you cry

I'm not fanscinated by my life
And it's ok if I die
But it has to be your finger behind the trigger
Becuase there's dying in love
And there's pain in love
But I hope they're never vain in love

Baby I would die
Oh I would die
If it could somehow make you smile
(You don't that much)
