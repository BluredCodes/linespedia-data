---
title: "My Halo"
writer: "Eagle"
slug: "my-halo"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/53843-My-Halo-by-Eagle"
---

I didn't put myself in this awful place alone
You had just as much to do with it as I
Stop pushing me away and handing me these black wings
Then punishing me for all my sins
And ripping away my halo
Taking away my last piece of holy jewelry
The one thing keeping me from full damnination
You've made me halo-less
You've given me black wings
You've tainted my view
My halo shined and took away a small part of the pain
You just had to have it too
And now you're sitting up there, planning my demise
I can't even stand on my own
My halo always held me up
And you can't even need him
Why have you done this to me God?
