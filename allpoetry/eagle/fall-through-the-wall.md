Fall through the Wall.
Down an Out.
In and Around.
I had my soul trampled by muddy boots.
And I had my mouth gagged by birds with bloody feathers.
Fall out the Wall.
It's not that strong.
Up and Through.
I've been pondering these questions.
I've been busy wearing my disguse and hiding my face.
Fall off the Wall.
It can't hold you up.
Fresh and New.
Old and stale.
Traces of my blood are everywhere,
Luck can't be real or I was delt an awful hand.
