Look at us now
Examples of what will happen
If you dare to question the hand the steals your food
Then hands it back mangled and diminished

Look at us now
We're the individials lost for society's well-being
We were the ones forced into the ground
Then shoot for trying to get up and walk

Take a look at us now
We're Bonnie and Clyde
We broke the laws made by nobody wiser than us
We lived but sooner we died

Look at us now
We're deemed heartless
We only killed so we could survive
But that's not how they told our story

Look at us now
Every cop in America is on our trail
We've been chased while people were starving
But stopping our ways gets prioridy

Take a look at us now
We're Bonnie and Clyde
We wanted to suceed in a broken time
And now we've been broken too

Look at us now
We were so much in love
But that story never made headlines
Nobody really cared they just wanted to drown us

Look at us now
We're examples of bad gone worse
Stay away from the likes of us
Warn your children of the devils we are

Take a look at us now
We're Bonnie and Clyde
The law fought us and so we fought the law
And that turned us to fugitives

Look at us now
We were killed so society didn't have to suffer
Since it was in such great shape that year
The world really doesn't need us

Look at us now
Open up your sunday paper
There we are on the front page headline
All this stemmed from a forbidden love

Take a look at us now
We're Bonnie and Clyde
While tax money went towards hunting us down
Babies died of simple colds

I guess you could say
We really were Romeo and Juliet in a getaway car
