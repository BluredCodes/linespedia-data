You don't deserve a happy Chirstmas
I hope nobody sends you greeting Cards
And I hope you miss all the good sales
And I hope you sit alone until you start to regret
No I won't walk past you and smile
Just becuase it's the holidays
That doesn't forgive all the things you've done
And it doesn't change
That you don't deserve a happy Chirstmas

You don't deserve a happy New Year
I hope nobody comes to your party
And I hope you fall asleep at nine
And I hope you sit alone until you start to cry
No I won't send you any kind words
You never had any for me
The season can't forgive the things you've done
And it doesn't change
That you don't deserve a happy New Year

I hope nobody buys you presents
I hope nobody fills your stocking
I hope nobody tells you white lies
I hope nobody makes you smile
I hope you have an awful Christmas

           Without love,
                  The one you hurt that hasn't healed
