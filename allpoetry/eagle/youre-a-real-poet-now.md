Load your semi-automatic
Grab your weapons
And prepare your insults
Shout at each other
Then maybe shoot
Hate each other
Before you ever meet
Write your poems
And blast them loud
Knock those who've worked hard
In a matter of seconds
Use the same old formula
All you have to do
Is blast names and pretty verbs
You have to get a dictonary out to understand
Just make sure it's all bad
Even when you're feeling good
You're a real poet now
Yeah Next thing you know
Somebody will use you to become a real poet tooHrmmmm... Not saying depressing, dark, swearing or violent poems are bad things... But when you use them as a way of making your poem "rock" rather than of being a personal message you just have to write down, it becomes another on the lists of fads people run into. Then it's bad. :)
