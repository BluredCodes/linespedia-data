title: "For Daniel"
writer: "Eagle"

Sunflowers and birds with cherry wings
All of the worlds beautiful things
The things you can't see
Becuase all that's in your face is a gun

Freedom is something you now have to prey for
Taken in by the mindless whores
None of them care
About you, your child, or your wife

I wish your story weren't stuck on this page
Such a beatiful bird trapped in a cage
Caught in the net by the butterfly collector
As you're proudly displayed within his showcase

I'll still hate them but I hope they treat you kind
And if one day they make you blind
I'll scrape out my eyes and give them to you
And you can blossom like a Sunday flower

With you gone I'm in a craze
You need to see to make it out of this maze
They're not playing fair they're changing the turns
You keep wandering but there aren't any doors

They've got you down weeping on your knees
And I'm on the sideline just begging please
I love you like you were my brother
I'd take your place though we've never met

Blossom like a Sunday Flower
Never give up your mind power
Even at the hands of your collector
Take my hand and I'll lead you out

They clipped your wings and now you're bound
Sneak out slowly and don't make a sound
Through the chains you struggle free
You've been so strong to be so patient

Someday there'll be a hand to lead you out
If it's not mine I promise you that I died trying
The world can't let another Sunday Flower wilt
You will blossom in innocent hands when God sets you freeFor Daniel Pearl... Everybody say a prayer for him. I don't care who you prey to - God, Jesus, Allah, Buddha, the Goddess - Just prey to help bring him home!
