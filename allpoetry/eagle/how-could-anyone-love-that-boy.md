A girl with her blonde hair tied back
In her cheerleading uniform and her spirit makeup
Walked past us in the hall and gave me a stare
She asked with her eyes why I had just kissed you
And later she stopped me after science class
And she said

"How could anyone love that boy
Is there something he does good I should know about
Can he give my boyfriends lessons maybe
You must be good at acting to make out with him"

I walked away but didn't answer her
It hurt to much because in her eyes she was serious
But she's never even heard you speak a word
I'm not really sure how she got my number
But that night she called me
And she said

"I just found out you were serious
I laughed a little while until I thought about it
You poor girl we can out on Friday
I'll find you a guy that you really deserve"

I hung up on her - Not sure if she got the point
Maybe it'd be fine if she was the only one
But Mr. Bruton asked me to stay after class
Just a week or so later
Apparently I had overdue work
And he said

"Sorry Amber but we need to talk
I know I'm your teacher and I'm conserned
I'm worried about you as I well should be
That boy of yours in just bad news"

Too bad you're not even in Mr. B's classes
I didn't even know he knew your name
Certianly you can't be bad news
When you brighten my day like a flower in the rain
And if that wasn't enouph - Mom and Dad joined the club
They said

"How could anyone love that boy
Baby we're just worried you're our little girl
This boy spikes his hair and has a tatoo
He keeps you out long past curfew"

I thought at first that they were joking
Since they've always understood when I'm confused
I ran into that cheerleader again soon after
She was working her job up at HMV
And she said

"How could anyone love that boy"

She scanned the CD I bought for you
And I said

"How can anyone love you?"This based partically on stuff that really happened, but not all of it. It's really free style and may not fllow well... but I wanted to say what I had to say without limiting myself.
