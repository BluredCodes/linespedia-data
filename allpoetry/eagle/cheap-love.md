---
title: "Cheap Love"
writer: "Eagle"
slug: "cheap-love"

---

Pull a petal
Then another
He loves me
He loves me not

Wish I was cheap love
Paid for by the hour
You're a sinner
And you're a liar

Back away
Hide in a closet
And find on my wall
"A + J forever"

Seduced by you
Brought down on my knees
Shattered by you
Until I felt like cheap love

Turn around
Just try to get away
Once I really
Believed you loved me

Wound on your finger
I am your puppet
Rip at my strings
Until I fall apart
