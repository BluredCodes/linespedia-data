Siren wants your soul
Siren wants your life
Siren wants you to hear her song

Siren wants you to die
And if you hear the Siren call
There's no escape from her voice

Siren wants your body
Siren wants your last breathe
Siren wants you to hear her voice

Siren wants you to cry
And if you can make our her haunting words
It's foolish to even try to save yourself

Fear the Siren
Run away
Get away
Fear the Siren
She wants to kill you
But she wants you to hear her sing
And she wants to love you
Use you and Abuse you
She wants to kill you
Fear the Siren
Run away
Just get awayThese kind of only work as lyrics... It's sort of supposed to be like it actually *is* the Siren's song. :)
