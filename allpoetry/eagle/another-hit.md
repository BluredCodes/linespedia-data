It was fun for a while
Now it's just confusing
Becuase I'm straight and you're laced in the morning
It was great feeling high now you're aiming for normalcy

You never show up 5th period
Your withdrawl already started
You need another hit
You can't even make it a day

We started it to have a good time
But I quit when I woke up shaking at 3 am
It was all the more thrill for you
Just wire your brain and your troubles hid in the corner

You make the entire table twitch at lunch
You can't help but bite your nails
You need another hit
You can't even sit still

I wasn't conserned at first
It's you life to live and I won't interfer
But I can't just watch
As it becomes you life to die

You need another hit
It's making me sick
I wish I was the addict sometimes
This is harder on me than it is on you

It's funny how it consumes you more and more
Until there's nothing left but your harshened words
And you wake up in the morning next to someone you don't know
Even then you just take another hit

It doesn't feel good anymore
You don't want another hit
The weird this is
You need it like you need the air
