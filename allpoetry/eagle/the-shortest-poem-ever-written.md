I sat down with a notebook and a pen
Hoping to describe you in just a few words
Assuming I could sum you up in a nice little poem
Barely even one line long
But that's when I remembered
A picture's worth a thousand words
And you're a moving picture of perfection
I figured it out, at thirty frames a second
I'd have to write you eighty six million four hunderd thousand words
Just to come up with a day of what you mean to me
I couldn't write you the shortest poem
And I couldn't write you the longest
So I just you'd like to know you're worth more words than I can count
