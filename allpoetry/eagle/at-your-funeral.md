---
title: "At your funeral"
writer: "Eagle"
slug: "at-your-funeral"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Some people are so so afraid
of living life in the fast lane
It's such a wast to think
I'm never allowed to live the good life

I'm too young now to be in love
Or so I'm told day by day
And yet, these are the best years of my life
But I'm too young to do things my way

I might be young, not wise with age like you
But at your funeral I lay a rose upon your grave
I'll be telling the young what I told you today
I'll sill be walking this world and you'll wish you'd been dumb too

I'm too young now to be in love
I'm curious, then, what this feeling is
If these are the best years of my life
Then how come my heart is in so much pain

Don't you ever wonder
If at your funeral people will cry
Will they say your time was too short
Or will they be relieved your're finally gone

When everyone gathers
At your funeral
Will anyone be able to honestly say
"Well at least, You had a good life"
