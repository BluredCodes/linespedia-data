God made you as an angel that could only last so long
I hope you enjoy heaven or your Kingly incarnation
It's hard to take it all in
So everyone reacts by spitting it all out

There are people in this world
That see beautiful things
And look to make them ugly
They like to nail head upon thier walls

That's why when they saw your colourful wings
They just couldn't let you alone to be happy
And so they killed the butterfly in their view
The only think to thank God for is they couldn't get you as a catipillar

They took pride in carrying it out
Clipping your wings before ripping them off
They whispered to each other and thought of ways to be
Better and crueler hunters

The only positive side that I can find
Is that the world can't hurt you anymore
And the world can never make you cry
Now the world can never rip apart your wings

You were such a beautiful bird in an ugly cage
Now you can't even be beautiful
Becuase they wouldn't even let you be at all
They're freedom fighters since they fought against your freedom

This sunday no flowers will bloom
And those living with wilt away and die
Without a seed there can't be a stem
And without a stem you can't blossom like a Sunday Flower

I promised you I'd help you out
So many people who wanted to save you
But maybe you found your own way out
And maybe it's better where you now reside

It's been a while since you've had freedom
And you'll never see your child or your wife again
But now your spirit is free and now you can fly
