---
title: "The Hypocrite's anthem"
writer: "Eagle"
slug: "the-hypocrites-anthem"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/47102-The-Hypocrites-anthem-by-Eagle"

Be more like us.
Just never be yourself,
You're the worst person to be.
Join us now,
Break through sentimentality.

Our lives were like yours.
but now it's all under control,
We found peace and harmony in conformity.
