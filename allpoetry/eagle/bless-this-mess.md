---
title: "Bless this mess"
writer: "Eagle"

Dear God Bless this mess that I am
Bless these dirty feet that walk on tainted ground
Bless this young body that was never a virgin

Dear God Bless this mess that I am
Bless these scarred hands that will never heal
Bless the child within that just wants to run free

Dear God Bless this mess that I have become
Save the souls that got lost along the way
Bless the things I believe in even when I'm alone

Dear God Bless this mess that I am
Bless this forehead that's still marked from last Ash Wednesday
Bless this poet who's lost her edge

Dear God Bless this mess that I am
Bless these knees that I've fallen upon
Bless this revolutionary who's been enslaved

Dear God Bless this mess that I have become
Save the children who were sacrifised to make this world
Bless the things I stand for even when I stand alone
