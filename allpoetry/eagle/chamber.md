I like the colour of your eyes
No one else's are quite like yours
Your words are captivating
And bring the whole world to their knees

Now take me to your Chamber
And lock me up in chains
Yeah take me to your Chamber
Throw away the key
Then stay here forever with me

I love you
I love you
You're so inverted
I love you
I love you
You're so religious

When I wake up in the morning
And you're still next to me
I'll just be really glad
I didn't chain you down so you've have to stay

Now take me to your chamber
And  tie me to the wall
Baby take me to your chamber
Throw away the key
Then stay here forever with me

You bring the whole world to their knees
You bring the whole world to their knees
You bring the whole world to their knees
You bring the whole world to their knees
Baby take me to your chamber
Invite me to your chamber
And throw away the keyThese are lyrics so the lines that gets repeated almost the same over and over *are* intended that way. It sounds odd as a poem, but remember - these are lyrics! ^.^
