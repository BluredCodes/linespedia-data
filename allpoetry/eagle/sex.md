Beautiful hair and gorgerous eyes
Smooth skin and a perfect body
There you are in the corner
Confusing sex with love
Whispiring "I love you"
At all you one night stands
Born a bastard child
It seems your goal to make another
Sex-driven and without real love
Commiting victimless crimes
Against your own humanity
All through the night
Bitter and outraged
You reach out for sex
Because it's so much easier
To find these days than love
