---
title: "Strawberry"
writer: "Eagle"
slug: "strawberry"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/46763-Strawberry-by-Eagle"
---

Strawberry,
Laced up in your own sins.

Laced in gold,
from head to toe.
Laced in lies,
between you and I.
Laced in black,
so much like you.

Strawberry,
laced up both of us.

Laced in crimson,
like I can't tell.
Laced in lies,
between you and I.
Laced in yellow,
but you can't block the sun.

Strawberry,
laced up in what commitment means.

You told me sugar-coated words,
with a strawberry on top,
but what you wouldn't say,
is you said the same words twice,
once for me,
and once to her.

Strawberry,
laced up in what I mean to you.

Laced in nothing,
I'm alone.
Laced in lies,
between you and I.
Laced in Gold,
from head to toe.

Strawberry,
Laced up in your own sins.

Doesn't it get cold,
living life that way?

Can you believe,
in this day and age,
people still need people,
to get along?

Strawberry,
laced up in what commitment means.

Strawberry,
laced up in what I mean to you.
