Mommy doesn't cry anymore
On your birthday and on New Years
She tells me that she wanted you to go away
And that she's happy now

Thank you Daddy
For going away
Thank you Daddy
There is nothing left to say

Mommy never explained sex to me when I was young
I never really understood
If Daddy never existed, then neither would I
So thank God I can thank my Dad

Thank you Daddy
Even though I never saw your face
Thank you Daddy
I sure do love this place

Now that I'm a grown up girl
And it's time for me to find
A daddy for my own little girl
You're an inspiration for who not to choose

Thank you Daddy
For being the perfect anti-role model
Thank you Daddy
My little girl will see Daddy beyond her cradle

I know everyone's time comes eventually
And mine should be pretty soon
I'm sure you're dead by now
But I have to say just once more

Thank you Daddy
Even though I never saw your face
Thank you Daddy
I sure did love this place

Heaven is such a beautiful place Daddy
I haven't seen you here
I know you must have done some wicked things
But I'm so glad that you made me

Thank you Daddy
For giving me the one thing I asked you to give
Thank you Daddy
For giving me that chance to liveThis is for both my friends in this situation - You know who you are! And anyone else going through this, I wish you luck in everything you do! :)
