My paramount goal in this endeaver
Is just to bring you down close to forever

You don't even notice when down come the walls
Playing games while your empire falls

And in your black heart all I see is anger
And still I fall in love with that danger

Call all your princes into place
And close the last page to this case

You love it when things are easy
Even if in the process you make others queasy

You didn't even know when somebody loved you
And now you wish somebody would come brand new

Now, Cold and Broken
Somebody's going to assassinate you
