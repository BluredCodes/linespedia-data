You're the only one
Who saw my flaw                          and didn't correct it
     Instead                   You taught yourself to love it
                         You made beauty from
         repulsive things

                          You made love from
          hated flaw

I used to want to be abused          by anyone
             I think because I             was never           loved
     Now I've                     had love
                   Nov I've lost                 a love that withstood
                                      repulsive things

                            I love your flaw
      I love your scars
               And you                    love mine
                            I don't fear             this anymore
  I've got an angel                  with black wings
                        to watch my shakey back

                I am consumed in               selfish violence
                                   I wanted to die               You
        made me want                             to live
                          Consume me
