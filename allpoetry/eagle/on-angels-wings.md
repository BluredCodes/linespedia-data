title: "On Angels wings"
writer: "Eagle"

Do you think that you could fly on Angels wings?
Do you think you could soar above the trees?
Or would you fall and come crying to me,
Like a child would you give up at the sight of a little blood?

If someone ripped off your left wing,
Would you teach yourself to fly again?
And learn to live with your disability,
Or would you cry to me to protect you?

If I took your hand and picked you up.
Would you drag me down with you?
Or would you let go and fall ,
If you're going to crash do I have to colide with you?

Are you willing to be strong?
Are you willing to survive even if it means you have to die?
I died slowly last night,
And when I need saving you're even further gone than me.

You might think you need me,
But can you fly without a guidelight?
Before you go diving into the ground,
Remeber that at least you had a moment to be alive.

I'm stuck on the inside,
Wanting to go to play outside,
Where all the angels walk and never fly,
And even though they died they all survived.
