---
title: "Why I want to be Abused"
writer: "Eagle"
slug: "why-i-want-to-be-abused"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/33724-Why-I-want-to-be-Abused-by-Eagle"
---

I want you cut my fleash,
Rip me apart from head to toe,
I want you to hurt me until I start to cry,
Then leave me in a ditch to die.

I want so bad to be abused,
I long so much just to be used.

I want to trip,
I want to fall.
You always take me to the top,
Just push me off the ledge.

Stop playing games,
Stop wasting time.
Just pull me under or shove me down,
Just hurry now and make your move.

You're done to everyone you know,
When do you plan to do it to me?
I ask the questions but I know the answer.
If I beg you'll push me down.

I want so bad to be abused,
I long so much just to be used.

I wish you'd knock me down,
And I could see who'd help me up,
So I could know where to go,
When I don't want to be abused.
