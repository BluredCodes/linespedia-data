Why are you trying to be a false hero
I loved you when you were a zero
I felt my back against the wall
No one would hear me scream in this hall
There's only so much that one can do
When confroted with someone like you
As must as you thought I was a liar
I'm gonna set your mind on fire

I don't blame you for using force
Looks like we're failing this course
I'm not about to trip on my own wire
Look at us we're idiots for hire
We can't even find a decent place to sleep
This world doesn't like us becuase we're not sheep
And I know you'd love me if only you tried
I might deny it but both my hands are tied

You pushed my back against the wall
Nobody would hear me call
If I blamed you would you blame me
I'm at fault if only you'd be
You can rip my heart out
But I will always continue to shout
As much as you thought I was a liar
I'm gonna set your mind on fire
