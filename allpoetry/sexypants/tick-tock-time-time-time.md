The days 
cant decide
whether or not
they want to move
QUiCKlY...................
or
........................SloWly

Somedays,
the time flys by
as we talk for hours
about what we will do
where we will go
what we will see
and how it will feel
to hold each other
for the first time.....

Other days,
the time takes its time
and the hours 
feel like eons,
and I will the clock
to move a little faster
yet it continues
its monotonous
tick tock tick tock....
exploding 
in my ear
leaving 
cryptic messages
in the ash

Either way,
its getting closer
and soon,
I will be there
with you
willing time 
to slow down
for I know
it will go by 
all to 
QuIcKlY...............I love you :)
