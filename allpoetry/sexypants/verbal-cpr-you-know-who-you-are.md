Your lips breathe life into me
verbal cpr.

Continuous 
Parade of
Relaxation.

Does no one else see
does no one else hear?

If I could sit at your feet
for just one day.
One hour.
I would have you sing to me.
And watch you.

For the dream I hear in your voice.
I long to see in your eyes.
Because I know it is there.

Even you
do not know
what you have
but I do
I hear it
I feel it
And I love it

Verbal CPR rescued me.
And you were the one
who kept me from dying.

Breathe.
Sing.
Live.thank you. maaaaah.
