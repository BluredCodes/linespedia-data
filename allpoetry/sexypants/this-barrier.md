---
title: "This barrier"
writer: "sexypants"
slug: "this-barrier"
source: "AllPoetry"

I sit here 
looking at you
on the other side of the world
through this glass window

I cant touch you physically
but seeing you
makes me warm inside

I cant taste your lips
feel your skin against mine
hold your hand,
fingers entwined
warm and soft....

All I have
is a cold barrier
lying between us,
there you are
oceans away
yet inches...

I cant wait
to feel your skin
taste your lips
hold your hand
see the  magic 
in your eyes
when you sing
when you play

I cant wait
to look you in the eye
without this barrier
and tell you
