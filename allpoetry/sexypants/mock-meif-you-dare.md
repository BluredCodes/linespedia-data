title: "Mock me.....if you dare"

They laugh when I tell them of love,
through words
and songs
and smiles.

Mocking me,
telling me Im a fool 
to believe in you
and the love you bestow upon me.

Must I have you physically here
in order for it to be "REAL"?
What IS love anyway?

Is it when two lovers share 
their bodies in a moment of passion?
Or is it when two lovers share,
their souls in a moment of comfort?

Is love defined by tender kisses 
upon saddened cheeks?
Or is love defined by loving words,
upon heavy burdened hearts?

Mock me if they will,
for they know not what I do of you.
If you were standing before me
naked,
I might "SEE" you
in your beauty and glory.
But I would not see your true depth,
or your soul laid bare before me,
in words for me to absorb.

No,
I see you.
And distance keeps our bodies seperated,
like a cruel fence with jagged edge,
that neither of us can climb over.
But I see you through the holes.
I hear your voice from the other side.
And Mock me if they will.
But I love you.I love you monkey. :) I see you. And thank you for seeing me.
