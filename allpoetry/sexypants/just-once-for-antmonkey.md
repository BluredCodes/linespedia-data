---
title: "Just once (for Antmonkey)"
writer: "sexypants"
slug: "just-once-for-antmonkey"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/29726-Just-once--for-Antmonkey--by-sexypants"

My tears they fall so freely,
wishing I could look you in the eye

Just once.
Just one time 
before I go.
Afraid, I wont ever get to.

Im trying to be brave.
but I crumble
under the weight of the world.

Just once,
Just one time....
I wanted to see you smile at me.

Im trying not to cry
but I cant stop the flood,
the never ending parade of tears.

Just once,
Just one time,
I wanted to kiss your lips.

Im trying to stand tall
but the ache in my chest 
pulls me back down.

Just once,
Just one more time
I want to tell you I love you

If I dont get to say goodbye.
Know,
that just once,
Just one time
you lived in my heart.
For once is all it takes.

I love you Ant. 
Dont ever forget it.
