The ants go marching one by one 
hurrah hurrah
the ants go marching one by one 
hurrah  hurrah,
the ants go marching one by one,
there are arguments to be won,
and they all go falling down
to the ground 
boom boom boom boom...

The ants go marching two by two 
hurrah hurrah
the ants go marching two by two
hurrah hurrah
the ants go marching two by two,
He sang to me what do I do?
I go swooning all around
boom boom boom boom...

The ants go marching three by three
hurrah hurrah
the ants go marching three by three
hurrah hurrah
the ants go marching three by three,
Im laughing so hard I have to pee,
and I roll around the ground
boom boom boom boom 

The ants go marching four by four
hurrah hurrah
the ants go marching four by four
hurrah hurrah
the ants go marching four by four,
He keeps me smiling, I want more,
laughter is the sound,
boom boom boom boom

The ants go marching five by five
hurrah hurrah
the ants go marching five by five,
hurrah hurrah
the ants go marching five by five
He makes me feel like Im alive
Isnt that just profound?
boom boom boom boom

The ants go marching six by six
hurrrah hurrah
the ants go marching six by six
hurrah hurrah
the ants go marching six by six,
He promises me magic tongue tricks ;)
steals my breath cant make a sound
boom boom boom boom

The ants go marching seven by seven
hurrah hurrah
the ants go marching seven by seven
hurrah hurrah
the ants go marching seven by seven
once in his arms  I will be in  heaven
happy tears fall to the ground
boom boom boom boom

The ants go marching eight by eight
hurrah hurrah
the ants go marching eight by eight 
hurrah hurrah,
the ants go marching eight by eight,
To look in his eyes, I just cant wait,
lost in his eyes I will drown
boom boom boom boom

The ants go marching nine by nine
hurrah hurrah
the ants go marching nine by nine
hurrah hurrah
the ants go marching nine by nine,
I take my heart and give him mine,
my love it does abound
boom boom boom boom

The ants go marching ten by ten
hurrah hurrah
the ants to marching ten by ten
hurrah hurrah
the ants go marching ten by ten
He brings love unto my heart again,
Friendship and love is what Ive found
boom
boom
boom 
boom
