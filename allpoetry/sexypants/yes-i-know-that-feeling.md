title: "Yes I know that FEELING"

I know what you mean.
I know it.
When you tell me,
"Do you ever get that feeling,
that when you are walking down a road
you've walked down a thousand times,
and you think.....
I wonder what Jen would think of this"

Yes.
I know it all too well.
I get that feeling,
when I lie down in bed at night
curled up next to my pillow
place my face upon it,
thinking....
I wonder what it would be like
if it were really your chest
I was laying on
instead of a cold lifeless pillow.

Yes,
I do know it.
When I see man on the corner
playing guitar for quarters,
not really needing the money per say,
he just wants someone to listen to him sing.
I think of Anthony,
and how I would give anything
to sit at his feet and watch him play
and finally look in his eyes
while he sings for me.

Yes,
It is with me everyday,
when I drive by the Music store,
on the corner of Bakerview and Northwest,
and I see the droves of Metalica shirts
pouring from the building
upon young hopeful musicians.
I think,
I wonder if Anthony would like that store.

Yes.
I know that feeling.
When I go to sleep at night
I know the sun is rising where you are.
The thoughts are there.
I wonder what Anthony looks like
when he wakes up in the morning.
I wish I could wake up
and roll over to see his smile.

But it is so.
That when I am falling asleep
thinking of you waking,
you are waking,
thinking of me falling asleep.

Yes,
I do know that feeling.
Its called....longing.Yes, I know that feeling Ant, I love you.
