silence falls upon my being
as halfway around the world,
you turn off your light 
shut your eyes
and fall into a sweet slumber.

when you stop singing to me,
turn off your mic
and go to bed,
I sit in silence.
I dont want anything 
to remove the last voice
my soul heard.

If I had to choose
the way I wanted to die.
I would choose
to have you lying next to me
holding me close
as you sang to me.
Your soft voice,
tenderly lulling me 
to my final rest.

Once again
comforted by you.I love you
