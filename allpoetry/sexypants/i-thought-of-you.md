I was driving home tonight,
and a song came over the air.
Normally I wouldnt pay attention
just go driving along,
singing away to whatever they play next.
The Dj's monotone voice as significant
as the sound of the tires upon the pavement
as I sped down the highway.

The first few notes had barely reached 
my inner ear
and my finger shot out to the volume button.
A smile was instintanious as I recognized the song.
One youd sang to me.
you consume my mind lately.
you were right there with me.
the long ride home.
wasnt so lonely.

Trees passed me by,
construction cones screaming orange at me.
cars flew by as they raced home
to avoid missing dinner 
and facing an angry wife.
Lights became streams of light
instead of dots.
Nothing mattered,
but spending time with you in my thoughts.

I think of you often.
And wonder.....
do you think of me too?you know. :)
