title: "I cant help but wonder"

As the days draw nearer
to the moment our eyes first meet,
the knots get bigger,
more complex
tighter,
more strangling.

What if after we exchange glances
shy, nervous smiles
as your eyes begin to take in 
all of me....
What if I watch the smile fade...

I cant help but wonder
will I be what you hoped?

Looking through the closet
of all my options,
nothing seems right
for the first encounter.

I try on every dress,
they all reveal
what I try to hide....

~~ME~~

I sit on my bed,
holding a purple dress
of velvet in one hand,
a red sweater in the other...
I cant decide
for I guess it doesnt matter
what I am wearing
for its what Im not wearing
that worries me....

My mask.
I cant hide from you.
You will see me
no matter what I wear.

I cant help but wonder...Dont mind me...IM just nervous. I love you Ant.
