---
title: "Nothing more"
writer: "Chad"

source: "AllPoetry"

---

I am Atlas
and I carry the weight of your world on my shoulders
I am Neptune
holding back the scarred sea cliffs of your doubt
I am Samson
I will shake the pillars of your fear
I am Theseus
leading you from the minotaur
I am Hercules
removing the burdens of your labours
I am Moses
showing you the way to the promised land

I am all of these and none

I am a man
nothing more, nothing less
