---
title: "Night driving"
writer: "Chad"
slug: "night-driving"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

white lines
guidence signs
empty roads
traffic cones
and lost souls
Passing me by

...Your forty minute shelf life,
ticks down in the half light,
as the CD skipped
through songs by Buffalo Tom...


ghosts in transit
worlds within worlds
solitary and defined
by these white lines
these guidence signs

...These mornings are too cold
But I guess the shaking proves I’m still alive...

                ... But I was barely awake...
