title: "It ends in Red"

You said the scars were plain to see
(But I don't think we'll ever see them all)
Stitches hold together your guilty secret
Which fractures your smile
Twisted by desperation
And your life drip, drips away
Into a black nothing

And the nothing consumes your fools gold smile
Boiling your blood until you must set it free
Until it cries with sweet liberty...

... For you to fall

The bathtub stained red...

Rope friction on skin...

... If suicide is painless, why do we use knives?
