Ripped to the teats on caffeine and Nietzsche
I ride the train to you again
My head awash with doubts and worries
My head awash with love and hope

Saint David is a cold and brutal station
A match box awaits my heavy burden
I sit among the lost and loveless
I sit among the blessed and loved

Distance never kept us apart from our embrace
Of sex and love of love and hope
And distance didn't keep us together
And distance will never rip us apart

I sit among the lost and loveless
My head awash with love and hopeOK, so I'm a one trick pony at the moment!! But at least I'm getting it out of my system :)
