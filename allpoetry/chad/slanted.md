Listen to the melody... 

                   (but not all of the song)

Back to a time we pretended we were friends.
Tolerating each others nervous ticks,
but catalogued, and thrown back, like jagged bricks.

I sink slowly before I remember...

                    (The melody changes now)

Goodbye my friend, hate to see you go,
You gave me this life, and it fitted better.(A work in progress me thinks) About someone I haven't thought about for a long, long time.
