---
title: "Failed Actor"
writer: "Chad"
slug: "failed-actor"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8278-Failed-Actor-by-Chad"
---

Who am I kidding?
I'm not cut out for this
Just put me at the front
Let me perform, let me show off

Who am I to help?
What do I know?
Not wise beyond my years
Not experienced enough to lighten your fears

I feel like a child, wrapped up in a frame
Growing grey, going bald
I've got tattoos you know
I'm not responsible, not quite mature

Who am I kidding?
Who am I to help?
