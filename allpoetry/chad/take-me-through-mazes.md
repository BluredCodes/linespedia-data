---
title: "[Take me through mazes,]"
writer: "Chad"

Take me through mazes,
living through the fall.
I'm losing to the inertia of it all.

to you...

from you...

Losing to it all.

And you've never felt so lonely,
never been here before.
Images in your head,
as you were held to the floor.

And I'll find my way to you
when I'm losing to it all
