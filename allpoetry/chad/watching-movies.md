title: "Watching Movies"

In glances, were spoken
words of sweet regret
With movements too subtle
you've broken everything

So I'm climbing, it's evening
These stairs are all I know
And I'm hanging on nothing
and words you never spoke

And we talk in small words
Burned to the wall
of these lies, these lies

If words are not broken
The chances never gained
Like watching old movies
When the love scene never fades

Like watching old movies
fading to blackThis is about lots of different stuff really, kind of an amalgamation of a lot of memories =)
