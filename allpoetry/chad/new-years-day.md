---
title: "New years day"
writer: "Chad"

The aftershock of all our broken dreams,
lay naked on the table; remains of last nights beer.
And we made it to the exit nothing spoken:
no more words to say.

Push back the doors
and let the sun
come fill our empty heads
with the day.

Cold morning, new year.
Just like the one we knew before.
Cold morning new year.
With memories and things to come
We steal into the morning sun...
... and shine

You held your hand so close to me,
because in words you were not able
to explain away tears.
As we stumbled to the pavement edge,
The world dropped away

Lift up your eyes
and let the sun
come fill our empty heads
with the day

And it's never coming back again.
And it's never gonna be the same.
And I'm never coming back again.
