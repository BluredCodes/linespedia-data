Oh... 

Hello...

You... you just put me off my stride,
I was just telling this amazing joke
but you threw me when you arrived.

Now where was I? oh yes that's right
You see there was this, this, oh it's no use
i can't concentrate with you in sight

Look, just go away for now
So I can collect my thoughts
What do you mean, "You can't, you don't know how"?

It's simple really...
go to the bottom and give me a rating
Look, please leave I'm all embarrased
and you reading me is grating

Come back in a while and I'll tell you then
.
.
.
.
.
.
.
No, no, no, too soon try again.Boy, was I bored
