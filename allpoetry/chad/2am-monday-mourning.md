---
title: "2am Monday mourning"
writer: "Chad"
slug: "2am-monday-mourning"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/14573-2am-Monday-mourning-by-Chad"

It is all silence now,
the doors are closed
the lights are put to sleep.

But the red flickering lights
of time silently pass by

Outside the wind tells a tale
but to deaf ears, sleepy crowds,
an audience of dreams
too involved in their own lies
to listen to the wind cry.

Inside, the walls fall silent
no sound for them to turn around,
no light for them to trap
in corners where secrets lie,
where spiders hang their lives

And everything stops
for those who choose it to
we wait in dreams,
the moon and stars slowly
wind the world back up

But the red flickering lights
Of time silently pass by
