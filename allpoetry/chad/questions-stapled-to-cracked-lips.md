Questions stapled to cracked lips,
come in from the cold, where the memory lingers.
And serve a round of glances, 'till it gets old.
In whispers, to reasons... the letters say nothing,
Stand over shoulders and watch as the words spread to pages.short.... random.... um, yeah there you go, make of it what you will =)
