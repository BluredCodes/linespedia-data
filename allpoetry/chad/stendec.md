Lost to the jetstream
you're just high enough to feel.
Fifty miles from where you would be
Inertia's taking hold.

five forty five, the time clocks in.
Still on the wrong side
of reasons why.

Selling the mystery,
but you don't even know
of vague messages,
growing cold in the snow.

Melting to the surface,
with the ice keeping hold.
Unaware of anything,
bet you thought you were going home.

five forty five,
still on the wrong side
of reasons why.

Selling the mystery,
but you can't even know
of vague meassages,
