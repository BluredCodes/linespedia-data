Sounds from the battlefield,
Out gunned,
wounded and weakening with every breath.
The final volley 
of violent questions and complaints:

"Don't write in blue
the fluoresent flickering obscures the view.
Can't you make your head transparent?"

(what do you think?)

I feel the bullets thud into my chest;
my last round is a dry, mutilated marker.
At times like this I question the phrase:
"The pen is mightier than the sword"
