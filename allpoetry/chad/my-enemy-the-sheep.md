---
title: "My enemy the sheep."
writer: "Chad"

They just will not be counted.
Huddled together, a cloudy mass,
the future's jumpers hiding out in the open.
Can sheep laugh?

(They have good memories, so the experts say)

My enemies the sheep.I am going slowly mad... but it's ok I'm rolling with the punches.
