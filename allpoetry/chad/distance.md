Let me wrench the earth
Shorten the tracks
Quicken the weeks
Turn a day into an hour
And make this hour last as long
as this cigerette

Let me cut short the day
Let me squash the nights
Let me make this island
A road... A room.
And this room my world

Let me turn distance into nothing
So that nothing stands in my way
Let me run, let me fly
So i can cross the miles in a stride
Let me hold you, let me love you

For three hundred miles.
