title: "OK"
writer: "Chad"

White washed and brokenA blank page staring openA knife to the sideRusted with timeSo OK I criedA bitter moment in a carLimping from a new scarA smile painted overThe loss of a loverWell OK I criedWhispers like antsRun blindly through friendsBut I kept my mouth shutAnd never said a fucking wordBut OK I cried
