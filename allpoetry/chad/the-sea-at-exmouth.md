This is not the sea
Where is its roar?
Why doesn't it show its claws?
Claiming back the land
With a rattlesnake hiss

Its' jagged, glassy teeth
(Which some mock by riding)
Don't show themselves here
Instead it sleeps, tired, old
As if it has been beaten by time
Forced to sit, being not what it is
But being removed, being chained
By a calming wind...

... But I can see its' want
Look hard enough and you can see its teeth...

This is not the sea
This is a blue field of turgid calm
A mockery of what it wants to be
A tidal push, pushed to despair
Scared by boats, but not scaring the Earth
It sits and waits for the world to turn its back
So it may devour the beaches once moreDon't get me wrong, I love the sea =)
