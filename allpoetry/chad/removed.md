They carry the wait of the world on their shoulders
As if the world put it there on purpose
Their ambitious songs struggling to the first chorus
Others here will stay
but all will return
To finish their song
to scream out their final verse
To light the flame and watch it burn
And when the world sees fit to apologies for its burden
They will stand up straight and join our choir
And so the liberty of innocence is brokenIt's about teenagers that I know
