---
title: "Console with silence"
writer: "Chad"
slug: "console-with-silence"

Fingers hover over lips.
Breaking through the gaps in the silence.
Eyes take a fall, and recover,
and through the silence you say more.

(I just don't say anything anymore)

Run circles around your time,
that slots you in between others.

It's getting old now,
and you're just beginning,
Selling secrets,
a prize worth winning.
