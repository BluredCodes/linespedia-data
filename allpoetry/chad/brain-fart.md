It never makes a smell
Which is good i guess
It just sneeks out silently
Without making a mess

You could be doing fine
Until it decides to let rip
Then it all goes pair shaped
And you feel like a drip

The problem is you wont notice
Until it's too late
It just sneaks up on you
Quickly sealing your fate

Before you know, it's all gone wrong
And everything falls apart
And you sit alone in pieces
Cursing that damn Brain FartYep... I am an idiot =)
