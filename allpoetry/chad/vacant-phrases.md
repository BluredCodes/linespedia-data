title: "Vacant Phrases"

Talk in small words.
Vacant phrases in small rooms,
did I hurt you?
Well I don't know you
and you're still talking,
still fucking talking.

Blacked out names
but I remember faces.
As I wait for you to inhale
nails claw at red palms.
But, you're still talking,
still fucking talking.Pointless conversations with people you don't know/don't like  :)
