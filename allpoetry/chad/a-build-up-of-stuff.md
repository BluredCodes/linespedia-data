Have we not been tested enough?

Three hundred miles is not enough,
the world sees fit to add to that
two seperate lives does not surfice
we  must stretch that till it is transparant
but we have seen all, and exsited all
laid ourselves naked over a distance
and discovered all that would be
we have through time and place explored
every inch and every word which would lay us bare
for I know you, and you like no other understand me
so pretend is just a game, just a mockery
and yet the world seems fit to play
but a chance... but if only...
...we grow closer with every step
yet the very fabric seems to want to play with our
wovern cloth of spiritual understanding
to pull and tease... to undo.
Unravell what has been made
or at least pull and un-make what has taken a life time,
no, not so long, but long enough,
un-create a life, unstitch a blanket
which had begun to warm our souls...
not only warm, but to blend; to mix two lives, 
which alone screamed empty pleasure
scraping through a wall of history and pain
emerging from the fire a pheonix
a new born baby... accepting all new emotions
but temporing the flames with wisdom and guile
Because we found each otherA kind of early morning, lots of beer kinda thing... Pour of emotions with no real control...... It happens.
