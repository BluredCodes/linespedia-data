She held me up.

I was falling and she caught me,
I sobbed into her arms
she held me close and whispered,

"You're my baby"

"You are mine"

I could not talk, words were not enough.
Her arms were like wings around my body
and her body became the only place I could sense.

I had let these feelings stretch my bones, pull at my heart
leave me sleepless and frail
She kissed me and whispered,

"You are my baby"

"You are mine"

Man, I held her so tight that night
and nothing could ever hurt me again
