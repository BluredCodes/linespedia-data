---
title: "Closer"
writer: "Chad"
slug: "closer"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/4406-Closer-by-Chad"
---

She draws closer
And is still a hundred miles away
We speak in words without sound
we speak in messages without words

She draws closer
And I am a hundred miles away
We hold each other tightly
So as not to forget
The feeling of our skin sweet with sweat

She draws closer
Yet we are still too far away
