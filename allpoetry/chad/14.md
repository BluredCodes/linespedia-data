Fourteen days through March,
wash away the rain with chances
making things better.
And I do know the faces
but the names aren't familiar.
Tick away the edge, or the pressure
to reasons or weather.
Spiral to the false floor
which covers up secrets.
(Can I cheat a little more?)
A fix, before fixed,
making things better.

Sometimes the rain soaks through.
And sometimes it's colder too.
Shaking without you,
the rain soaks through.Yet more vague lyrics!!! =)
