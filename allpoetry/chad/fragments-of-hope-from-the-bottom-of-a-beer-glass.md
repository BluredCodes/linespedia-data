It's a little bit of everything
sitting on your back and forcing you to kneel

And every now and then
I feel the storm clouds coming back
and I open up my arms

I don't know what's in this medicine
but if it tastes so bad
it can't do you harm

It's never easy just to say the words
when in your head, they make no sense at all

And these shoulders are just wide enough
to catch you if you fallSong lyrics for some very special people, who I worry about far too much at times =)
