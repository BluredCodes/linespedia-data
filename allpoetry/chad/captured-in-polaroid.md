Captured your smile,
under arm for a while, 
watch as you fade from grey.

This silver box could
steal a moment,
held in infinity...
                
Hold the moment...
                         
...Holding the moments

Make it worthwile...
This is your only chance,
No second take.
No practice run.

Captured in Polaroid and placed on display,
So make it count... make it mean the world,
A corner of your soul, chipped away with the flash.
So make it count...

...Hold the moment.um.... yeah, been having fun with a polaroid today =)
