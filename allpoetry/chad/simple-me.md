I haven't changedI'm still hereI haven't moved onThat was last yearI haven't changed my hairOr grown a beardI haven't pierced anything...Anything weirdI'm still me, that's all I amI hope it's enoughFor you to turn toThrough the smooth, through the roughendings a bit weak
