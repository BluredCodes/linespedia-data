---
title: "Bed knows best"
writer: "Chad"

Don't leave yet
It's early, and outside it's wet
Cuddle some more
And let the rain lap at our shore
Kiss some more
Now I ask you, does that seem such a chore?
Let's make love all day
You see, things will be far more interesting if you stay
Breakfast can wait
There's no point arguing, you see it's already become late
So come back to bed
And go tomorrow instead
