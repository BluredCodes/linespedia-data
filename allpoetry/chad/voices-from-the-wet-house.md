Life in a plastic bag,
you never had it so good.
I'll spit these words out,              (Can I cheat?)        
'cuz they're all I've got,
beyond the shaking.
          
                            
There's a life here...
 behind the eyes,
gasping for air,                         (Can I cheat a little more?) 
between hits from the bag
But I know, there's no way out, 
but I know


Out of the Wet House
Into the Drinking School                (Need a fix)    
Cash my giro
and share my past with a spirit
           

Can't eat... Why do I need to eat?
Celebrate enough and I forget


                                                  (Before I'm fixed)
               

        Tattoo reminds me...
                  ... I can do anything,
                         But for the shaking

              When I remember what it is I need to do
              I think it will be welcome.
