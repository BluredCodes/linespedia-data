title: "Catching the Sleeper"

Catch the sleeper...
And listen as the night fades into an echo.
I don't feel a thing, but maybe I should,
as these faces fade away.

I think I may have peaked too soon
and there's nowhere left to stay.
But that could be the feelings of right now,
as the moment washes me away.

Catch the sleeper
Never thought I'd travel this far
In so few years.
And these moments are too short lived,
But seem to last a while,
as they boil down to salt.
