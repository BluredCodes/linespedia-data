title: "Last Me"
writer: "Chad"

The rain wakes me up to say
That it's just another day
And nothings changed

And these hours are irrelevant
And these days become an element
To be chained

She lay naked under colored sheets
Man, she smiled at me
And when she smiles at me...

...These hours are irrelevant
And these days become an element 
To be tamed

She lit up another cigerette
And blew smoke rings across my chest
It's all I need to keep this thing alive
All these moments need to last a while
Need to last me 300 miles.Yes this has been on before as part of "The rain..." But it was never meant be there, so I put it here instead!!!
