(Can you skim read?)

Picking out.... 
                 random phrases.... 
                               to make meaning?

This is the first....
                   
                        ... A Highland policeman.... 

....Since a Clockwork Orange.....

                                                ....The film grows..... 
.....lost footage.

(It would seem I can't)*shrugs*
