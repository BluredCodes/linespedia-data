Refrain
In fields of tobbacco sweatshops and brothels
Let us gather now and sing this song
We are ruled by child abusers
Blood-splattered sneakers emblazoned with coporate swastikas
Dont be fooled we are the losers
It is our due to die in debt
But the actions of a few leave much to regret
Fortunes pooled the more is our accusers
As time and space will not erase
The terrible things now taking place
When the summer comes every seed will be numbered
