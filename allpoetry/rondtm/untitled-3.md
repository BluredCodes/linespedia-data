We've been made mad driven to babble on
Twisting and contorting the words beyond recognition
Pitting one against the other
And what works we have built up
Tiny pieces of salt one atop the other
Hardened by experience ever reaching
Noble intentions inspire grand constructions indeed
While some human elements erode what we have wrought from within
These pillars of stone bear no malice
It is the clay beneath that undermines their foundation
And sets them to topple
Upon my noggin
Birds nestle down and flock together
Like bees work hives and swarm as one
To shelter their little ones
The secret that everyone knows
Is the shame no one will admit to
A crime unconfessed
