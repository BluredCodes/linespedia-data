Ignorance is the mother of all wars
It is a smart-bomb that tracks me to my lair
And ignites a fire for all to see

Neither here nor there, stealth is a mirage
When your looking for answers
They're here, in the fire
When ugly becomes beautiful
Where children have no place
As all hope is lost
Forever, hear their cries
