title: "Another Cut"
writer: "rondtm"

Lets face the world together
Back to back
Dueling with our opposition
For a few inches of miserable ground
Lets have two lives
Two swords working like one blending
As we turn around
One shielded from this dubious entanglement
Where it all commes together
And leaves awarm glow
One open to the dicing and slicing
Uncertainties mixed with perilous flux
Away we go
