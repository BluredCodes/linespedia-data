am i so still like this inanimate? 
lips i move to speak make but kisses, 
to hold your hand i would need your hand 
but also your permission, 
and i am scared. 

i am scared of feeling lost without 
seeing home in your eyes, 
i am scared of being a phone call, 
a movie, a restaurant 
when i am more.  

more than these walking sticks 
under breathing bags and digit palms, 
more than these blues.  
me and proposals
is carbon under pressure;  

let me know i can breathe without rejection.
