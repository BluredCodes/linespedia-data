i saw you naked in the morning
through the glass of my bedroom window,
and if i could put angels on paper and leave them there;
i would save you in my sketchbook,
brushing your hair out,
accidentally nude in my on purpose view.

and you… 
will never read this poem to realize
once upon a time, i spied
with my little eyes and memorized
your graceful breasts and fragile lips, 
hungry for someone else to kiss them.
