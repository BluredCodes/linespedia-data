---
title: "Every Now And Then"
writer: "ShadowDancer"

Every now and then,
I close my eyes and think.
I'm taken to a place so very far away.
Some place I once found love,
and sometimes my heart sinks.

To think of all the things
we never got to do.
To think of all the things
I really wanted to.

Every now and then,
tears come to my eyes.
I think of all the things you said,
that now just seem like lies.

Like all the times you said you love me,
and when you said you missed me too.
Every now and then I wonder,
how could I have been such a fool.

Every now and then 
I think of you,
and it makes me happy too.
Just to have had the time, 
I got to spend with you.

But only every now and then,
is when I think of you.
