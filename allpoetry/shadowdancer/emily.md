Where did you go?
Won't you tell me where you've been?
It's been a long time 
Since I last saw you smile.
I've been looking for you for so long,
Won't you tell me why you were hiding?
Tell me why you've been lying,
Why you've been trying
to make people see a you,
that really isn't you?
Emily, can't you see you're beautiful?
Emily, don't you know how great you are?
Emily, when will you realize, you're everything to me?I wrote this one a while back.. But I still like it. I don't know anyone named Emily, but it is about someone I know.
