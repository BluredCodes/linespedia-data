Sometimes I look for you.
Sometimes I can't find you.
Most of the time you're 
nowhere around.

Sometimes you say you're sorry.
Sometimes you don't say anything.
Most of the time
you just tell me to kiss your...

Sometimes you run when things get hard.
Sometimes you don't do anything.
Either way, It's gonna be,
Either way, someday, I'm gonna say,
"You Head south, baby catch you later."

Someday your sky is gonna fall.
Someday I've got to find someone
who wants to be around.

Someday your ground
is going to shake,
You're going to fall,
Down, down, down.

You're gonna reach for me,
and I'm not gonna be 
Around, 'round, 'round.

Sometimes you run when things get too hard.
Sometimes you don't do anything.
Either way, it's gonna be.
Either way, someday, I'm gonna say
"You head south, babe, won't catch you later."
