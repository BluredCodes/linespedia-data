---
title: "Pride"
writer: "ShadowDancer"

Taking a look deep inside,
I can't help who I am,
So why should I hide?

I should stand tall,
I should stand with Pride.
Just let my faith be my guide.

You can love me or hate me,
you can want me to die,
but I can't live life with it built on a lie.

Know that I'm real,
I'm human just like you.
All I ask is respect,
and I'll give it back to you.

So please take a look,
please look deep inside.
see who I am and don't ask me to hide,
and know from now on I'm living with pride.
