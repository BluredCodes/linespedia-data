---
title: "Love You Enough"
writer: "ShadowDancer"

At First,
It was funny.
It was kind of like a joke.
But Now,
I can see,
You're really through with me.
And it hurts.
Yes it hurts.
My tears come,
When I'm least expecting.
When I'm laughing,
When I'm dancing,
When I'm having a good time.
That's when it hits me,
Your memory.
I wish I had no feelings.
It's like a shot,
Through my heart,
now that we're apart.
I can't see you.
I can't feel you.
But when I get the chance,
It just kills me.
I wish that I were dead.
But I know,
That someday,
I will smile again.
I will laugh.
I will dance.
I will love again.
Just so you know,
I still Love You,
And I always will.
But now I know,
I don't need you to survive.
I do miss you.
Maybe someday,
We'll meet again.
But until then,
I will love you enough to let you go.
