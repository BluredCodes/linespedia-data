Late one night,
I caught a dream.
It's carried me away.
On moonbeams and stars tails
I will fly away.

Floating upward in 
The silver stream,
Reaching for my dream,
The stars will guide me,
And comets will carry me away.

Flying in the velvet sky,
I will shine so bright,
I will know there's nothing,
That I can't do that night.

Late one night,
I caught a dream,
It's carried me away. 
On moon beams and stars tails,
I will fly away.I wrote this poem back in 1998... but I still kinda like it :)
