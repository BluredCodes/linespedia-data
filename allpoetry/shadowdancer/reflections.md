title: "Reflections"
writer: "ShadowDancer"

You hate me, I don't know why.
What is it that I have done?
The color of my eyes, my hair, my skin?
The way I act, the way I talk, the way I walk?
The way I laugh, the way I cry, the way I live?
What is it that makes you wish that I would die?

You look at me, the feeling comes from deep within,
Ripping and tearing your heart apart,
scraping at your brain like a razor,
the hate you feel towards me, 
why do you hate what I have become?

Looking in a mirror, you try to turn away.
But you can not go, for something makes you stay.
Looking in the mirror, the only thing you see,
Is reflections of you, reflecting me.
