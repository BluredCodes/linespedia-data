---
title: "Sometimes I lie"
writer: "ShadowDancer"
slug: "sometimes-i-lie"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/12753-Sometimes-I-lie-by-ShadowDancer"
---

No matter what you say or do,
I can't keep from loving you.
I can't help it that
you're in my heart and in my head.

But sometimes I lie,
and say I'm over you,
that there is nothing you could ever do 
to make me come running back to you.

But deep in my heart I know,
to say my name 
is all that you would need to do.
I still want you in my life.
I want your arms to hold me tight,
keep me safe all through the night.

But sometimes I lie,
and say I'm over you,
that here is nothing you could ever do
to make me come running back to you.

Sometimes I lie,
when I can't take it anymore.
Can't face the hurt,
or stop my tears.

Sometimes I lie,
when someone asks me how I am.
Can't let them see 
the pain I'm in.
I tell them there is nothing
you could ever do,
to make me come running back to you.

but deep inside I know,
to say my name,
or touch my face,
is all that you would need to do,
and I'd come running back to you.
but sometimes I lie.

Sometimes I lie...
