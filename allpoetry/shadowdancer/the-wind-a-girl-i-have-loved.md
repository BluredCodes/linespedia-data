title: "The Wind (A Girl I Have Loved)"

There is a girl I have loved.
The thought of being with her,
warms my heart like a fire
on a cold winters day.

There is a girl I have loved.
Though she lives many miles away
she carries my heart 
in her back pocket.

There is a girl I have loved.
She sets me free.
And like the wind,
she carries my heart away.

There is a girl I have loved.
Sometimes she leaves me,
but like the wind,
she always comes back to dance with me.
