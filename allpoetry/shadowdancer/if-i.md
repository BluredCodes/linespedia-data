---
title: "If I..."
writer: "ShadowDancer"
slug: "if-i"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/35623-If-I...-by-ShadowDancer"
---

If I took your hand in mine,
would you pull it away?
Or would you let it stay?

If I looked into your eyes,
would you see what's inside?
Oh, would you turn and try to hide?

If I whispered in your ear,
that I want to hold you near,
would it cause your heart to fear?

If I tried to kiss your lips,
would you want to kiss me back?
Or would you end it with a smack?

If I said I love you,
would you say it back?
Would you take it to your heart?
Promise me that we'll never part?

If I took your hand in mine,
would you pull it away?
Or would you let it stay?

If I did...
...Would you?
