---
title: "Field Of Thorns"
writer: "ShadowDancer"

Would you come to me,
through a field of thorns,
Keep on coming,
even if your skin were torn?

Walk a thousand miles,
through the roughest land,
to pull the thorns, 
from my dying hand?

Would you swim across
a shark infested ocean,
Do all you could,
to be beside me in the mornin'?

If my heart were broken,
would you mend it dear,
in the midnight hour,
if my heart were broken?

If I were weeping,
would you dry my tears,
do all you could,
to calm my fears?

If there were another girl,
would you go to her,
forget our love,
or would you stand by me?

If I should lose my faith in you,
would you take my hand,
hold it 'til I know I'm wrong,
if I lose my faith in you?

Would you come to me,
through a field fo thorns,
keep on coming,
even if your skin were torn...
