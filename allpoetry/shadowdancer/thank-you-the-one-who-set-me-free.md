I often think of her.
Silently I thank her.
For a while it hurt,
and I thought I hated her.
But looking back,
When I met her,
She truely set me free.
It was then, That I accepted
who I was meant to be.
Now I'm free to live my life,
to find the girl who's right for me.
I loved her then,
With my whole heart, body, and soul.
So how could she do what she did to me?
I've since forgiven her,
For the pain she caused inside of me,
and Everyday, Silently I say,
"Thank You", to the one who set me free.
