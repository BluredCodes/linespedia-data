---
title: "Blue"
writer: "ShadowDancer"
slug: "blue"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/12982-Blue-by-ShadowDancer"
---

You've shown me
the color of the ocean.
You've shown me
the color of the sky.
You've made me feel
the color of the tears,
that keep falling from my eye's.
I can't stop myself from crying,
I can't keep from feeling blue,
When I know,
I only want to be with you.
