---
title: "Colors of my life"
writer: "ShadowDancer"

Yellow- The color of sunshine streaming down When I was Young.

Orange- The color of Fiery thoughts of you that kept me warm at night.

Red- The color of my burning heart so full of undying love.

Silver- The color of the lining of ever cloud you chased away.

Purple- The color of true royalty, which is how I felt when I was with you.

Green- The color of my skin, I'm sure, evertime I thought of you with her.

Blue- The color of my tears when I knew that I'd lost you.

Black- The color of the emptiness that had leaked into my chest.

White- the color of my happiness, now that I'm over you.
