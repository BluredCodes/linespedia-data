---
title: "If You Forget..."
writer: "ShadowDancer"
slug: "if-you-forget"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/12980-If-You-Forget...-by-ShadowDancer"
---

If you should always remember, how I feel for you,
I will hold you in my arms again, And kiss your lips so sweet.
Give to you the warmth of love, And the comfort of our dreams.
I will forever keep you safe, And dry your every tear.
Sadness never more will reign, Within your heart my dear.
And I will tell you every night, That I always want you near.
But, If one day, our happiness should subside.
And from my side you leave.
And if all our memories,You should forget,
Therefore, forgetting me,
If you forget the love we share,And turn to someone new,
My love for you will never die, But shall no longer grow.
I shall often think of you, And hold you in my heart.
The memories of loving you, I never will let go.
If one day you should forget, That you are everything to me.
Don't look back, don't think of me, I'll be forever gone.
