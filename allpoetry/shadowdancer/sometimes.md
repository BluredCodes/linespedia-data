Sometimes I feel lost
and I don't know where to go.
Who can I turn to,
When I can't turn to you?

I'm feeling so old and tired.
My body feels weak. 
My eyes are heavy and 
I only want to sleep.

Sometimes I hate everyone.
Sometimes People make me sick,
tell me what can I do,
When I only want you?

Sometimes I feel so ugly.
Most of the time I know you are.
You tore my heart out 
and left me a scar.

Sometimes I feel lost 
and I don't know where to go.
Thats when I turn to myself;
For I've brought me safe this far.
