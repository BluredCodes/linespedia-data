---
title: "In Darkness"
writer: "ShadowDancer"
slug: "in-darkness"

I'm covered in darkness
As I watch you
Laughing and smiling.
My heart feels warm 
And a smile lights my face
When I think that you are mine.
I hold out my hand,
Your hair barely brushes my fingertips.
We move closer together.
You're teasing me.
I reach out to hold you,
I can't get close enough.
You put your arms around me, 
And for a moment, I am happy.
I try to talk, but the words won't come.
"I love you, I adore you, I'm so glad
That you are mine."
I start to speak,
Just as I feel my eyes flutter,
I open them.
Warm tears fill my eyes and
Sorrow floods my heart as I look around.
Empty darkness is all that fills my sight.
For you are gone.
                 (I miss you)
