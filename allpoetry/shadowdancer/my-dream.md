---
title: "My Dream"
writer: "ShadowDancer"
slug: "my-dream"
source: "AllPoetry"

I have spent many hours dreaming of you,
enough to consume 4 life-times; I'm sure.
I envision you walking with me hand-in-hand
in the middle of the night as the silver moon
dances on the waves, that rush eagerly
to meet us at the shore.

I see us frolicking in a field on a warm
summers day.
Oh, and there you are;
kissing me in the rain.

And when I close my eyes,
I can almost feel you holding me close,
as we lay by the fire on a cold
winter's night.

Maybe it will always be nothing more than a dream;
perhaps it is a dream that we both share?
