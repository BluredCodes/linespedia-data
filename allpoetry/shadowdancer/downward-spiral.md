writer: "ShadowDancer"

I'm falling
I'm fading
My heart it is breaking.
I'm living in limbo
always unknowing.
Sometimes I'm floating 
don't know where I'm going.
Squeezing my heart,
then I trip on a thought,
I'm falling 
I'm fading,
My heart it is breaking,
I'm taking a trip...
I'm on a downward spiral again.
