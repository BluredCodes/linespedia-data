title: "For my friend"

I'm so scared of being hurt by you
That I tell myself what I feel is not true.
I've been hurt by guys before
So instead of talking, I show myself the door.
I try not to make myself fall
Because I have built my hopes so tall.
All day I think of excuses for this
And in my dreams I fantasize we kiss.
I miss you and you're not even gone.
Every day I want to wake up at dawn
If it means seeing you more 
Because it's only you I adore.
I won't admit I, but I'll wait forever
So that I'll be happy with us together.
