In my mind I see youLooking deeply at meIn my thoughts I hear youTelling me what's rightIn my soul I feel you Wanting to me wholeMy heart is flipping outIn my dreams I saw youWith me holding handsWith my eyes I saw youWhen you walked byWith my mouth I chokeMy heart is in my throatWith my words I touched youAnd I warmed your heartWith my hand I waved to youGoodbye to my soul mateWith my heart I love youCan I do this again?My heart is in my handsMy whole body feels youAs you hold me nowWith my lips I kiss youKnowing I probably shouldn'tIn my arms I hold youAnd you kiss my neckPlease don't hurt memy heart is in your hands.
