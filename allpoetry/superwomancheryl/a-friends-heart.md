title: "A Friend's Heart"

I see how you act around him.
I see how you try to be friends.
We both know how you feel.
We both know you deny it's real.
How can what is inside be fake?
Your future is only yours to stake.
Is love really so overrated?
Confusing how it's always contemplated.
Yes this love will probably pass.
Don't let him have your heart of glass.
I see you suffer and I feel your pain.
The first to witness your emotions drain.
You deserve to be in love and happy.
You deserve so much not guys who're crappy.
I see how he makes your heart jut.
But is what he gives really the worth?
I see the pain you can't help take,
They are your decisions to make.
If you can't I'll understand.
