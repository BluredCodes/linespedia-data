I'm nervous for you, call it sympathy pains,
I feel butterflies too, I don't want to again,
I wonder what he's thinking, I wonder what he'll say,
I wish today was tomorrow, And that today is not today,
Because then it would all be done,
Then we could move on with some fun,

I'm nervous for you, as I walk you to class,
My stomach is going to give really soon and fast,
Is today even the day that he's gonna talk?
I see how scared you are, and how slow you walk,
I can't imagine how you feel if I am going beserk,
Why couldn't he just say it right then, that jerk,
We can't take this pressure! We can't take this strain,
The only solution is never to live and love again,

Would it be better that way? No love, no pain?
No, I suppose not, and it's gonna happen again,
So I gess the best I can do for you is just to be a friend,
Be there for the good and bad, be there til the end.
So I guess I want you to know you not alone,
Especially in your time of need I will be there.I love you Kelli.
