I see your smooth face and clear pure eyes
Revealing eyes I have figured you out
when I look into them I see gorgeous skys
I know I love you with no doubt

Because of your intent sweet stare
I feel it all over my face and soul
Your voice and stare keeps me there
You make me feel intensely whole

I know you are considerate and great
Your reputation isn't all that bad
Meeting you has got to be my fate
When I am around you, I'm not sad.

So instead of telling, inside it stays
Some other time when I feel more secure
I'll tell you in some other ways
Everything except you feels obsecure

I dream of you all day and night
So I think my love is pure
Making me feel so safe and right
For my lonliness you're the cure.

I wish for you to hold me tight
With you I don't feel any alarm
I feel like I'm a high flying kite
And I know you'll keep me safe from harm.
