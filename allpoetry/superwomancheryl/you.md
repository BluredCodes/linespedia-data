I see you hurt
Inside I hurt
I see you cry
I'm crying too
I see your pain
I can't imagine
Why the people you love
hurt you so bad

I hear your cries
In the middle of night
I hear you suffer
I try to make it right
I hear your heart break
Into pieces never whole.

I touch your wounds
With gentle hands
I touch your soul
I can't help you
I touch your heart
Please help yourself

I smell your fear
I fear for you
I smell your blood
On his clothes
I smell a rat in here
You deserve better

I feel your pain.
And I love you
I'll support you
through anything
