I look at you from far away,I watch your moves every day.I want to follow you home.I want to talk to you alone.I'm worried your very vain.Thinking of you my heart painsFor me is Eric a better choice?Well at least he has heard my voice.I think I'm playing silly gamesI'm trapped, surrounded by chains.I think your mean and a big assholeBut since I won't talk to you, I'll never know.I just like to stare and see what you'll do.I am confused, do I really like you?
