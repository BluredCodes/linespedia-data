A friend is someone who will be there,
Not somebody who leaves when it gets rough,
It is easy to be a friend when all is well,
it is when the going gets tough that is a test,
I had a friend once who was moving to Cali.
Another friend is now mad at her for not talking,
That is how I feel, My friend left me
Alone in the middle of a discussion.
I don't want to fight, she said
Well if talking is fighting to her, I dont know what to do.
I am a friend who needs to talk
FRIENDS HAVE TO TALK TO EACH OTHER
work things out, 
The reason I wanted to talk was so I WOULDN'T be mad
Now I am more mad at the fact she left 
than what we were talking about.
As for what we were talking about,
I am mad because one doesn't try to justify 
that what she did was okay by falling back on your imperfections
changing the subject to glorify yourself
I ask you now, is that a friend?
Did I do my part to atleast tell you I was upset?
Did I not listen to you gripe about me
When what I had started was for you to listen
Do I not listen to you complain about everything?
When do I get listened to?
Has it come down to me putting a poem online
For you to listen to everything I have to say?
Now I ask you is that a good friend?
I understand that nobody is perfect,
I am included in that, however you have to try
I try, I feel like that is all I ever do
And this is how I get treated...
It figuresIn addition to all I have said here, I just want one person to know that I was going to blow off my friend so that I could spend time with YOU. AFTER you said you were bringing a friend, I decided to bring one too, except mine came much later.
