title: "Two Worlds, One Choice"

"Two worlds, one choice"
Says a soft, whispering voice.
What can that one choice be?
It is, of course, Christianity!

Or is there another possibility?
A different choice that's just for me.
What does this "New Age" stuff have in store?
It looks as if it leads to more.

More of what, I do not know.
Is this really the way I want to go?
Is New Age a possibility?
Should I let it take control of me?

"Two worlds, one choice"
Says that same soft, whispering voice,
But continues on this time instead.
"Oh, what is that I hear inside your head?"

"Are those rebellious words of doubt?
Tell me, please, what is your doubt about?
Have I not loved thee?
Dost thou not love me?

I gave my Son that tragic day,
Just so I could bring you out of harm's way.
Hear me now, faithful and true.
No matter what you do, I will still love you!"

I just stood there speechless, my eyes filled with tears.
Had I forsaken what I learned over the years?
I had accepted Jesus so long ago,
But now it felt like it was all just a show.

Two worlds, one choice. Did I make the right one?
No, but it's not too late to be undone.
With tears in my eyes, I started to pray,
"Lord Jesus, please forgive me today.

I do not deserve any of your love
Except by grace that comes from above.
I pray for your strength to help me live right
That everything I do brings glory in your sight.

Oh please, God, forgive me, for I have done wrong.
Help me to live for you and be strong!"
Two worlds, one choice.
Just listen to God's voice!
