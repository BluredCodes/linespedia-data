I don't remember the exact day we met,
But I remember the occasion well.
That was the very day that
Into love's sweet arms I fell.

I can still clearly see
That blessed, wondrous day.
I fell in love at first glance
What more can I say?

A lot has happened since then -
Some things good, some things bad.
There were many times
When one or both of us were sad.

But now you hate me,
So there's not much I can do
Besides accept that fact
And wish you luck in all you do!
