title: "The Wrath of God"

When Moses went to Pharoah
With his anointed rod,
He tried to warn the pharoah
Of the wrath of God.

When up on Calvary's hill
Jesus hung there, and with a nod
He died for us to save us
From the wrath of God.

When we disobey Him,
And tread where we should not have trod,
His mercy saves us
From the wrath of God.

Even when we just act like Christians,
Though it may be just a fraud.
His mercy still will save us
From the wrath of God.

When we finally do believe,
And it's no longer a facade
We will fully experience
The gracious love of God.

At that very joyous time
The angels will applaud
And all together we will praise
The one, and only, true God!
