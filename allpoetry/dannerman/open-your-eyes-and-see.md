The title of the preacher's sermon
Was "Open your eyes and see."
But when he first said that,
I didn't know what it meant to me.

I listened anyway, clueless to the meaning
Of a sermon obviously so well prepared
About a man who gave his life
So that all of us were spared.

It started to make sense -
What the preacher was saying.
At the end of the church service,
I couldn't help but start praying.

I prayed for forgiveness
To a man named Jesus Christ,
And that day I'm proud to say
I asked Jesus into my life.

My life is changed now,
But it's for the better.
'Twas all because
Of that simple prayer.

Though sometimes I doubt my faith
When times begin to get tough
But then I remember Jesus
Who had it even more rough.

The hardships never end
Because of Satan's opposing force
But I know that in the end
We will overcome them - of course!

When I first got saved
And heard the altar call
I decided that then was the time
I opened my eyes and saw!
