---
title: "Betrayal"
writer: "dannerman"
slug: "betrayal"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/50324-Betrayal-by-dannerman"
---

I'm tired; It's late.
I can't sleep tonight.
My mind is filled
So I start to write.

The words seem to flow
Without any thought.
All of my emotions
In the stanzas are caught.

This is how I cope -
This is my escape.
There is nothing to fear,
And nothing to hate.

I write of my feelings -
That much is true,
But they're *never* a sign
Of what I'd actually do.

So why risk a friendship?
Why break that trust?
Why for the betrayal?
You should tell me - you must!

What were you thinking
When you called them to tell?
Did you not realize it would
Make the past day complete hell?

They kept me awake
'Cause they thought I would die.
By my own hand. But know this:
I'd *never* even try.

It's all a big waste -
None of it is true,
But "reslife" now thinks differently
Thanks only to you.

Integrity is important,
Not easy to mend.
You've not only lost it -
You've lost a good friend.
