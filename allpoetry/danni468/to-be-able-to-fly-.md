I often sit and wonder how my life would be
If my actions were different, would I still be free
You never really realize the pathways that you take
And just how much simple chances put your happiness at stake

If you could change just one thing, what good would it do
Would your life be happier, would you, well, be you
A lesson learnt always seems to come with so much pain
Yet clouds dont always bring the pouring rain

Love, what sort of game is it that we play
And howcome we as people, cant seem to stay away
And I believe through everything, love is well, quite sad
It tears you up inside, and leave a taste so bad

Love seems to cure even the faintest of hearts
Yet so many heartaches come and people fall apart
If love is for the birds, flying way up high
And if many people fly like birds, why, oh why cant I
