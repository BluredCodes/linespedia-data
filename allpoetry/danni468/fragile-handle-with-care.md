Gone into the stage of "Handle with care"
You no longer see, you blankly just stare
Loneliness creeps up and lets in the cold breeze
You crawl into the shadows with your "unknown disease"

Gone are the days of laughter and fun
And thoughts wonder around like a loaded gun
Nothing around can calm your tidal wave
You realize you will take your pain to your grave

Wounds seem to heal slow, yet leave a lasting scar
As you leave your hopes and dreams of lost love so far
No one can tell you different or try change your mind
The heartache lasts forever, it is never left behind

One word one motion, one simple little sight
Brings all emotion right back into flight
A love lost does not always fade and die
And for that very reason we usually sit and cry

No clouds in the sky, yet its raining inside
The sun is shining, but behind the shadows we hide
And when the time comes we force that practised smile
And go around with the state of being fragile.
