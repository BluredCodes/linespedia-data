Leave me alone!!
Im so sick of your game
Yes, Sheylin is also your child
But your excuses to see ME are so lame

Stop calling me, 
Telling me you will always love me
And when I call you and "she" is with you
You pull me down and tease

You left me for "her"
So dont come crying
That you've lost everything
I did my fair share of fighting

When you come see your daughter
Dont try and get me into bed ok
Because never again, 
Will I give you your way!!Sorry!! Very angry - he always does this (My baby's father) and last night I had enough "she" and "her" is the girl he left me for when I was pregnant!! Whenever they fight, he makes an excuse to see me and try get me into bed... NOOO!!!! Never again!!!
