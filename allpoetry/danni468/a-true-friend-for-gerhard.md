I know we have only just met
And you are Quintins best friend
But when I'm around you, 
On your loyalty I can depend

I have told you things
I usually tend to keep locked in
I hide my deepest thoughts and fears
But in you, I found trust within

You understand my pain
Because you can relate
You have been through it too
And seeing you hurt, is one thing I hate

Friends till the end
Is how I want us to be
even when Quintin is gone
Please done forget about me...Gerhard is Quintins best friend and we have clicked so well... I treasure his friendship so much but Im afraid I will lose him once Quintin has left... Gerhard you are an angel!!!
