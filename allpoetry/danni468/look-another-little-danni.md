You've made me laugh
You've made me cry
You've helped me live
You've helped me die
You found my smile 
Then took it away
Ripped out my heart
When you decided not to stay
This heart inside so full of love
Just wanting, waiting for its gift from aboveI think I wrote this for my father when he left... This is cool.. Looking at all my little girl heartache poems... I was so naive!!
