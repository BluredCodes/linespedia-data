Hey baby, Im coming to see you tonight
I miss you so might, cant wait to hold you tight
Sure thing angel, I can hardly wait
Im so happy, in an exstatic state

Hey baby, should I hire us a movie
Heard about this new one, sounds kinda groovy
Ok angel, sounds like a cool plan
You know, your one amazing man

Hey baby, I think Im getting the flu
Think Im gonna stay home, I'll make it up to you
I understand angel, go get some sleep
My excitement drowns, inside I weep

... No answer when I try you on your cell?
You're not home either, yr hiding something I can tell
I send you a message asking why
You get your friend to reply... hmph! Tough guy!

Baby, please forgive me, I didnt mean to lie
They begged me to stay, I didnt mean to make you cry
No my angel, not this time
I've had enough of this lying crime:o(... Im sure you can imagine for those of you who were here yesterday & saw how excited I was!! :'(
