YOU SAID THAT WE HAVE ANGELS THAT KNOW EXACTLY WHAT WE ARE THINKING. MY ANGEL IS PRETTY SHY & KEEPS MY THOUGHTS TO HERSELF, SO I GUESS ITS UP TO ME NOW TO TELL YOU HOW I FEEL

When I realized it was you at the club
My heart started to race and my palms began to sweat
This was the moment I had been waiting for
I hoped it wouldnt be something I'd later regret

Your eyes met mine and your smile, 
Well, it cut me deep - deeper than I ever thought it could
You walked away with no emotion 
and like a pathetic fool, I just stood

You came to dance with me 
Something you have done many times before
But why was it different this time
Was it the hidden pregnancy scars I wore

Eventually you kissed me and didnt let go
and I felt this was to good to be for real
My poor, splintered heart
It has just begun to heal

Quintin, my angel, its only been four days
but Ive already fallen hard
Please dont hurt me
This love has caught me off guard

I know its difficult for you as well
But Im not asking you to be a father to Sheylin
She already has one of those...
I just need my love story to begin!Sorry - My lame attempt of a poem - cant seem to get rid of this bloody writers block!!!
