Hickory Dickory dock, siem sala biem
Who knows whether we'll loose or win
Today, tomorrow, yesterday, last year
Life is full of confusion and all known fear. 

Once upon a time, when the beanstalk started to grow
Little Red Riding hood the big bad wolf did blow
Thunder, Lightening, Sunshine in the North Pole
Little Hack Horner ate out of Miss Muffets bowl

The cat barked at the postman and bit the dog
Granny kissed Grampa and turned into a frog
The birds were swimming in the shallow sea
And all because you are not here with me...
