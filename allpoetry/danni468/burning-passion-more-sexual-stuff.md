I want your knees to tremble at my touch
And when you do touch my body
When you touch my naked skin
I want you to have the feeling of a natural trip
I want us to watch our bodies drip
In the silent dead of the night
No one around, no one to hear my cries
I want us to kiss deeply

I want our feet to levitate
The ecstacy I will not abbreviate
Open my eyes to you not there, 
My body sweat, now this is rare
