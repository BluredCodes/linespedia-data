As each day passes me by
For not one more will I carry this lie
The truth is that I love you
And through this may bug you
If I carry it around one more day
Cursing you trying to make you pay
To make you crazy with jealousy
It's not working I can see
So, now that you know
It's time for me to go
And even though you will never be mine...

I hope you have a great Valentine!!HA HA... just found it!! My first poem ever that I wrote in Std 6 to one of the most popular guys in my school!! I even remember writing it....
