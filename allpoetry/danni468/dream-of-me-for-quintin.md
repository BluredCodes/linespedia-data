DREAM OF ME AS I DREAM OF YOU, 
COZ A DREAM FROM EACH MAKES BOTH DREAMS COME TRUE....

Baby, even though you have not left
Im watching you slowly walk away
Why does it always happen to me
The ones I grow to love, never ever stay

This thunderstorm of a life
When will it ever end
I fell so hard for you
just as my broken heart began to mend

Whatever it takes my angel, 
I will be dreaming of you every night
If thats the only way I can have you in my life
I'll make sure I do it right

I thought you were the one
What am I going to do without you to hold
I can imagine when you are gone
Every lonely night will be so cold

Two years is a long time 
But what will I have to loose
If I decide to wait for you
What if thats what I choose

My wish is to dream of us
Till infinity and beyond
Every time I close my eyes
Until we meet again....Umm... 2 months until he leaves and I dont know what to do - every time we see eachother, we cant let go... The line on the top was on a piece of paper he left at my place last night... I love him so much?! What am I going to do without him?! Sorry all.. having a down day!!
