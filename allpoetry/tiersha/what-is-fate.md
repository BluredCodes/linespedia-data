---
title: "What is fate?"

Why does existence include life. Why does a void imply death? Is there truth within a reason? Can we avoid what is fate? How can we breathe without a breath? Are we always ghosts after death? Will the world die while we live on? Why do birds sing a paradox of song? Where are our saviours that promised us a life of happiness, love and health? Have they forsaken the killing people who lust and long and want for wealth? It seems that we, in our strength might fall in pain to floors of dust. Our dreams, the ones we want, the gilded silver fallen to rust.1986,
