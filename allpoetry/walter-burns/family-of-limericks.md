for each member of my family****


There once was a lady, Elisa
Who racked charges on her new Visa
Her vision askew
As debts only grew
Like liens on our tower a' Pisa 
-----------------------------------

There once was a boy named Matthew
Who almost choked on a cashew
The cashew was small
But that isn’t the all
Better run, its now comin at you!
-----------------------------------

Oh, there once was this girl named Melanie
Or at least that’s what you been tellin’ me
You say she stinks bad
Well I think that I’m glad
Cuz that means you haven’t been smellin’ me
------------------------------------

There once was a boy named Chandler
Whose rhyme was only “man handler”
So we thought he’d be mean
But it remained to be seen
Instead he grew up: Adam Sandler
------------------------------------

There once was a guy name of Walter
Who married his wife at the altar
All mentioned above
He dearly does love
Oh there once was a guy name of Walter
