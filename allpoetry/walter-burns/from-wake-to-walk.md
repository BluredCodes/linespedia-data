title: "from wake to walk"
writer: "Walter Burns"

I won’t 
take 
for 
granted
this 
transplanted 
enchanted 
world

From wake 
to walk 
and back 
again
each night 
I dream 
your name
All my life 
empty faces
shameless places
spoke of you
said they’d
tell me 
what to do

How could I believe? 
my eyes were closed
now 
they lay
open wide
to watch
the sky
and they 
never tire

You are Haley’s Comet
missed you the first time
but not again
Come back to me…
