Billy beat the piñata with a stick
Little suzy liked to watch him spring
His underdeveloped muscles cage
And then inflict a candied reign

Billy brought that stick into the bed
And beat blind folded suzy every night
His overdeveloped muscle in a strain
But they both enjoyed the candy that spilled out

Billy used that stick to edge along
Suzy died with marks upon her back
Life has made his muscles weak and sag
And now he offers candy to a child
