I keep these feelings bottled up
I’m not allowed to speak
And if I were to let them loose
You’d think that I were weak

Stuffed, bottled up and vacuum sealed
Where no one hears me scream
To let the feelings drip till dried
Seems just a distant dream

So then I twist and hear the fizz
Depression starts to sink
I drain the thoughts and read the cap
“You've won another drink”
