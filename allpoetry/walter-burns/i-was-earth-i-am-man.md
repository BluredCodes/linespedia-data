A horizon handshake in a warm bundle of birth
My love for you was born before there was an earth
And then became the earth, I watched myself depart
While placed in God’s own hands, revolve a love at heart  
Then the doe and dove, set free, to roam upon my land
I searched for soul, in hope that love, someday would understand
Then God was feeling lonely, he breathed life into some clay
I was pulled down to earth, from it, and born on hopeful day
God always sees so fit, in His hand I accept my fate
He took my rib, and for me, bestowed a loving mate
When I was Earth, I revolved so simply around the sun
Now I’m Man and I follow You until my life is done
On the seventh day, sabbath day, God stopped and rested
