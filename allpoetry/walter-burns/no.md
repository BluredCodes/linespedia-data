No smoking
No eating
No drinking

No dreaming
No yearning
No thinking

No cussing
No swearing
No fighting

No knowledge
No reading
No writing

No pets
No kids
No fun

No simile
No metaphor
No pun

No running 
No kicking
No cheating

No kidding
No tricking
No treating

No shoes
No shirt
No service

No rules
So makes
