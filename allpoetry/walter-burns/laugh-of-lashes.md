title: "Laugh of Lashes"
writer: "Walter Burns"

You’re a laugh of lashes
A sudden splash
Of smashes
Running through grass 
of dashes
Like a kiss 
on the fifty yard line 

I’m a frown 
you turned down
cuz your smile
is so adept
and your lashes
are like passes
I’d gladly intercept

We have our 
touch down dance
Not lacking in romance
As the end zone’s
The beginning
And both of us are winning
With your lashes
I advance
