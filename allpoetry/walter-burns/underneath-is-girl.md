---
title: "Underneath is Girl"
writer: "Walter Burns"
slug: "underneath-is-girl"
source: "AllPoetry"

Tailor-made,
fancy, frayed-
her bluejeans 
curve her thighs.
Took a walk
to the lake
I couldn’t 
trust my eyes.
Underneath 
that denim
Do you know 
what’s in em?
The purtiest 
girl alive!
