I’ll give you 
the whole length of the playing field 
if you give me the same
And we will go about 
erecting our walls, 
oblivious to each other
I whistling Dixie 
you hard at work
So it really doesn’t matter 
until one of us nears our goal
And the sad thing 
how will we ever know 
we’re too busy building walls
And who won? 
For the lever retrieves 
and the foundation falls
