title: "There are no Words"
writer: "Walter Burns"

There are no words 
In space,
Or that’s where they are kept?
Because words 
We can’t erase
Like days
We only wept

There is no love
In heart,
Or that's where it’s all found?
Because words 
May tear apart
From space
The lack of sound

There is no faith 
In trust,
Or that’s where it is known?
Because words
May be a must
But space
Is overgrown

There isn't hope
In us,
When thought to be so much?
Because words
Might help discuss
Like space
An icy touch
