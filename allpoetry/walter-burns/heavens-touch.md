---
title: "Heaven's Touch"
writer: "Walter Burns"
slug: "heavens-touch"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/826-Heavens-Touch-by-Walter-Burns"

From on high above she came
Born a silver streak of flame
She tore a trail through my soul
Burns of passion I can't control

Her beauty described in mere words
A sweeter song than morning birds
Who ever hope and ever try
To please her as she passes by

When she dips and when she bends 
Her graceful body never ends
And when I look into her eyes
I see a fire that never dies

Watching her dance toward the ground
The greatest entrance makes no sound
And then forever my life has worth
I've seen where heaven touched the earth
