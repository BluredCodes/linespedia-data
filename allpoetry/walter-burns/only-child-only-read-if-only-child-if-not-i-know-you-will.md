...Anyway You Brat)




I’m a messed up individual
I don’t have a place to begin
You see I don’t have siblings
No nutty folk to call kin

I’m an only child
Of an only child
Of an only child
My temper is wild!

You see it’s a shame
When your dolls have names
You’re a twelve-year old boy and you still gottem
So estatic they’re signed all on their bottoms

Monopoly and twister
With no brother or sister
Who spins the board? 
Whose hand’s on blue?
Who gets to be banker?
Who gets to be shoe!
Who really is winning?
When it’s me against you!
I guess I’ll be spinning
What else can I do!

My baseball and glove
Ball drops from above
No umpire will doubt
He’s safe! He’s out!
Really quite eerie
I won the World Series
What else to make up?
Slapshot from the hole
Now the Stanley cup!
Then the Superbowl!

But then there is Christmas
When I run to the tree
There isn’t a mix up
All the gifts are for me!

Every race I’ve entered
I’m the winner-to-be
I’m so self-centered
When it’s me against me!

I never drove a kid sister up to the mall
Not knowing her any, I haven’t missed her at all
My big brother never took me to show and tell
Matter of fact I think I’ve turned out quite well

See here is the problem it’s been mounting for years
My wife has a sister and several cousins as peers
And I must be cordial when they come over to stay
When they get on my nerves I can’t hide and go play

I bring out the monopoly and I bring out the twister
My wife rolls her eyes and the laughs from her sister
An only child makes social disaster somehow
So I phone my mother, “Make me a sibling right now!”Another one that is a lil messed up so give it a break :)
