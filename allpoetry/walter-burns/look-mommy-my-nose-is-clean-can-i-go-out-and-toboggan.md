Watch out for the Barbed-Wire Timmy!!!


Snow comes round these parts
Each four-year falls an inch 
I never made use of truck
To pull my car with wench

We could host Olympics
Four years we’d light the fire
Ski down the rolling hills
Stearing clear of barbed wire 

Sloshing through cruddy, icky, 
Muddy, tar tasting snow
Don’t eat it if it's yellow
I really had to go

Angels don’t take showers
I don’t think that’s e’er been said
But I just can’t believe 
Snow is dandruff off their head

“I’m dreaming of … White Christmas
…every Christmas card I write”
My greetings to friends up north 
Their chatter is my delight

Then, sigh, no snow days e’er
When it snows we’re unprepared
Ever see a suth’ner run
From light flurries, we are scared

Slushies, flurries, blizzards
Are desserts in my town
And if they ever come to be
In Mexico I will be found!
