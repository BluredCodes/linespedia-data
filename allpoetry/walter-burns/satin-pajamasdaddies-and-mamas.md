Satin Pajamas…Daddies and Mamas

In satin pajamas
Daddy’s and Mama’s,
“We’re not goin out tonight”
Daddy likes something
To make him feel pretty 
Something to make him feel right
That would be Mama
And satin pajamas

Kiddies go play in 
Middle of streets
Or just as bad at
Granny with sweets

Daddy and Mama
Full of delight
Say, “we’re not goin” 
“goin out tonight!”

So bottled bubbly
Candles, no kids
But tired and doubly 
They’ve heavy eye lids

So Mama and Daddy
Sometimes don’t fear
Sweet bedtime, sweet silence
Sweet avenues here
