---
title: "Thank the Artist"
writer: "Walter Burns"
slug: "thank-the-artist"
source: "AllPoetry"

The trees...sigh your name
My ears... hear the same
The space of which your beauty travels
In my eyes, it still unravels 
The kind of poem that I admire
Is one I read and never tire
I always ease my eyes toward you
And thank the artist for what they drew
