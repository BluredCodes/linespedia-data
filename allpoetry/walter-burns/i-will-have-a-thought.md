title: "i will have a Thought"

i will have a Thought
                   and soon as its forgotten
                   i will have Another

                   "this is how my day would go
                   this is how that i would know"

                   don't ever let acquaintance
                   sit down at your feet
                   tell them eye to eye
                   there's no need

                   dehumanize

                   the observance of your bliss

                   i keep a hefty tablet
                   for my own appraisal
                   for these musing thoughts
                   less than selfcontained

                   as if its 
                   music i'll forget

                   until its on my tongue 

                   again                                                     dancing for its worth
