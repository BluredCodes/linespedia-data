title: "A Scary Nose Cannot Be Ignored"
writer: "Walter Burns"

My little girl, she’s only three
Last night she came and cried to me
"Daddy, Daddy, outside my room
A floating nose to meet my doom"

"A floating nose? You’re being silly"
"A floating nose, Daddy really!"
"Wife of mine go see that nose"
But wife of mine gently dozed

I was naked and she was three
The covers grasped so tight to me
I’ve never been a homophobe
So I grabbed a frilly robe

Down the dark and chilly hall
Each nightlight we would stop and stall
At her door she looked to me
I hoped my frown she didn’t see

For all the silence we had held
Her puffy cheeks, they swiftly swelled
She cried to me. How? Heaven knows
Crepe myrtle tree could sprout a nose

"Deary, Deary, your eyes of suede
Have spied a blossom Jesus made
That’s not a nose of monster scary
That's not a Badman big and hairy

So Deary, Deary, you close your eyes
Let hungry monsters sit and cry"
"Daddy Daddy what’s a blossom
A gift from Jesus and you got some"
