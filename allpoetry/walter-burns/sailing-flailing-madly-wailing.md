title: "Sailing, Flailing, Madly Wailing"

I see you as you dream
Thoughts in clouds
With broken seams
I sow them up
and in our sky 
Pop them once 
and watch them fly
I jump in one 
with me inside
Now the future’s 
open wide
Sailing, flailing,
madly wailing
The tether to your mind is lost
To the sea, Oblivion
Let not our love 
be tossed
