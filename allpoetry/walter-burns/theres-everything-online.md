there's everything online 
                         from a           
down
                     to 
                                       z 
            if it isn't online 
then I guess it can
                    not be

you can go off to college 
I took a small course 
                                                             you can find a young          bride 
and then cause a bad divorce 

you can learn to cook dinner 
you can learn how to sow

you can chat all night long                                     with nobody that you know


but one thing to be sai
d                     bout getting online
if you haven’t got your own, best be getting off mine!
