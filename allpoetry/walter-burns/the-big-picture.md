Come closer
I'm about to die
The lights are about to dim
...On the Big Picture

Here come the credits
Before the movie hits
Just to let you know
The ending will be quick

A scratch on the screen
So the film must be flipped
The Big Picture doesn't mean
My life must have a script

Fade into the streets
Into your homes
And where you meet

But remember this...
It was the scratch
on the screen 
all along.
