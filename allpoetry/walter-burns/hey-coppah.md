Hey Coppah 
Makin' a diff'rence 
Why' d you let me speed passed 
To 85? 

Hey Coppah 
Policin' my life 
Stop me unexpected
Running the light 

Hey Coppah 
Left you behind 
Watch me go down here 
Exit and die
