I love her,
Her name is hard 
To say for me
The bouncy ball
Will not fall
When I want to say it…

Tiffany,
I love you…

The bouncy ball
Is on its way
With words 
I didn’t want to say,
I need you,
Be with me-

But you’re a drawing...

Such artistry
As you are
In color
And in my heart…
Bright red lips
Make me want
To kiss you…

I love you…
Tiffany…

The bouncy ball 
Is on its way
I need you
You’re so perfect-
You’re the girl 
I buy potions from
In a video game
Am I dumb? 

I love you…
Tiffany…
I need you.

I save my gold
From treasure chests
Just to come
And see your best

I love you…

Tiffany… 

The bouncy ball
Says your name
In perfumed shawl
I’m not the same
Since I met you-

I love you.

Tiffany,
With golden hair
The bouncy ball 
Hangs in mid air-
It loves to say
I love you,
I need you,
Tiffany.

I will go to bed
With thoughts of you,
And dream of you 
And wake of you,

I need you. 

The bouncy ball 
Must say this
Then I’ll leave you
With a kiss-
Am I wrong

To love you?

I need you,

Tiffany-

Good night 
My love
Mix a potion 
From above
And bring me
To your world
I love you

But,
I can’t have you.
