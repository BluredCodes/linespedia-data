title: "Here Me Sing"

the magic of a song
from beginning
to its end
you can form a lover
from once was
just a friend

I've been in love forever
waiting in the wings
while other poets wanting
of songs they can sing

this time I jumped in first
heartfirst, unafraid
I let you know at once how long
my decision has been made

I cannot recall before 
there was you inside my song
I don't think I could even sing
and if so I did it wrong

I gained my voice inside for you
the desire, eternal sprung
and in the wings I fell to earth
your beauty was always sung

it's grand how a song
can join hearts that were two 
the music is now fading 
never was dissuading 
hear me sing how I love you
