I will live and I will die
In the blink of an eye
Corrective glasses may help
But again I'd prolly just yelp
For they pinch my nose I need air

So for the sum of it all
I live and die for I'm small
Without my lenses for aid
I might just blink and betrayed
Adjusting to darkness down there

So if I'll live and not die
By just not blinking my eye
Moses arms high on the mount
As his foes die I lose count
Said he "It's impolite if you stare!"Moses was at the top of a mountain and as long as he had his arms in the air his army would be winning. I don't want to explain it so see if you get it.
