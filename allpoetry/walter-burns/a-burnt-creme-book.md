The claimed dessert
           A burnt crème book

Regard the delicate, 
silken, sinful, rich
blending velvet with a crisp,
simple, sugared
Discovery.

Transformed elegance
            A medley of aromatic  
flavors 
            on basic theme
Beyond!

Explore!
            The wider world.

Generous contribution
            A unique recipe

The temptation,
to stake a claim
      of your own.
