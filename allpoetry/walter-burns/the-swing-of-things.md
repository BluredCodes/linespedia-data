title: "The Swing of Things"
writer: "Walter Burns"

I need this... 
The swing of things
Always occupado
on the playground’s 
dull staccato 
the recess bell
just won’t yell
no kinder words
were spoken
to end this hell
of reveries 
never to be broken
I need a ride
On its seat
The faster 
Speeds away
The further 
I go 
the lesser I know
the central point 
of beginning
of end
is hazy
is crazy
I guess I’m lazy to say
I need to sit on 
the swing of things
And ride
