Say, please do you get it
I am hungry right now
You gave more to the chickens
The pigs and the cow

Gimme some feed back
Quit saying nice stuff
Just strap on the bag
Being hungry is rough

You gave me some wheat
Barley and grain
Then you took it away
And I’m goin’ insane

Gimme some feed back
Sugar cube or some oat
Don’t tell me I’m shiny
And have a nice coat

Gimme some feedback
Don’t gimme no carrot
I’m a horse and a poet
Your praise I can bare it

We’ll be best of friends 
We'll go to the track
But you’re being a hog
Gimme some feed back
