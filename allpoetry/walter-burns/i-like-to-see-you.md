---
title: "I like to see you"
writer: "Walter Burns"
slug: "i-like-to-see-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/51528-I-like-to-see-you-by-Walter-Burns"

I like to see you out of water
and suffering from your state
Breathing poison -- what I call air
and wriggling at your fate

I like to see you bark and bitch
as your head hangs out the car
I want to hit the powered switch
and cut off that puny roar

I like to see your eyes bulge out
not knowing what is wrong
I like to change the station once
I hear your fighting song

I like to sit and imagine this:
Scarlet marks upon your scream
I like it when you wave your hands
in front of my staring dream
