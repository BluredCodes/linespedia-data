---
title: "Baby's First Snow"
writer: "Walter Burns"
slug: "babys-first-snow"

Cast by the hands of the Maker above
Kissed by the lips that gave us all love
Breathed by the fire that gave us all life
Filled in the belly of beautiful wife

Held safe in the hands of the Cradler’s arm
Taken at times and rescued from harm
Held in the air, a hand that's unsure 
Taken, unseen, felt that its pure

Cast by the hands of the Maker above
Kissed by the lips that gave us all love
Breathed by the crystals that gave us all sight
Filled in the sky with blinding of light

Watched by his parents, safe in the yard
Baby's first snow, walking is hard
He’s innocent, beautiful, lovely and young
Kissed is the gift on his giggly tongue
