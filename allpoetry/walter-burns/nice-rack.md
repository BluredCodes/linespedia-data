title: "Nice Rack"
writer: "Walter Burns"

The biggest cantaloupes
The perkiest watermelons
Grapefruits round and ripe
Look at how their swellin’

Oranges...coconuts...
Everything’s set up right
Heck I even dig the feeling
Of those pesky skeeter bites

Drive your car and where you park it
You’ll find these things at the farmer’s market
