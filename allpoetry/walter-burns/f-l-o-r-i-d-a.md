Scandal in the papers
Missing ballot boxes
All the votes were in
But where the heck were they

The whole world was watching
Even in Australia
Down to just one state 
Holding two men in sway

Rainy night in Georgia
The nurse is surely spooked
One man, the loser 
Is yelling out the door

What's it mean? What's it mean?
Such a strange word to scream
He is President
Though he lost years before

He died tonight, in sleep
The snow globe shatters loud
Strange, it never snows
Irony of his fate

His things were sold or burned
In Xanadu, his home
The tote board on fire
With one unshaded state

Florida...
Florida...
Florida...I know it is dated but yuh follow the Citizen Kane motif?
