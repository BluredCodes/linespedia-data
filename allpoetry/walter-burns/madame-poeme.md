Madame Poeme 
I so fashioned you
I drew you line for line
And no matter how I followed through
You were meant to be divine

Madame Poeme
I so fashioned you
From everything I knew
I stroked you with the knell of love
To ring the song of you

Madame Poeme
You're in my heart
We live in symmetry
And if I ever needed hope
I would ask you read to me

Madame Poeme 
I can hear you now
As the day you gave me life
You walk with me eternally
As my poem and my wife

Madame Poeme
I do love you so
You make my heart resound 
You’re spirit lives so fluidly
This once I write you down

Madame Poeme
Sleep well my love
You’re how I breathe at night
Never thinking how to live
But loving these words I write
