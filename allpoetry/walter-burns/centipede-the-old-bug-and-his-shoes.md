Moccasins, loafers 
Slippers and ropers 
Flip-flops, high tops 
Slickers get you 
Puddle hops 

Mukluks and chukkas 
Sandals and thongs 
Pick the perfect sneaker 
And you can't go wrong 

Heels and steels 
Cleats and taps 
House shoes are good 
When you're 
Done with naps 

Shoes for bowling 
Skates for rolling 
Soccer shoes for when you 
Go out goaling 

Swim shoes, stilts 
And even waders 
Running shoes are good for
Them see-you-laters 

Wing tips and oxfords 
Shoes when you ballet 
Working those pumps 
As you pay the valet 

Granny boots lace up 
Hiking boots race up 
A mountain full of 
Those blue suede shoes 

Golf shoes for courses 
Riding boots for horses 
Space boots for the race 
We're not going to lose 

When they're getting snooty 
Babies wear their booties 
Wearing stilettos get you 
A bad case of cooties 

Mules wearing horseshoes? 
Hush puppies being quiet? 
If you wear combat boots 
You'll likely cause a riot. 

Shoes that are dyed 
Snowshoes really wide 
Rolling runners have a wheel 
You can pop up and hide 

Platforms for feeding hogs 
When dancing wear your clogs 
Walking shoes are good when
Being walked by your dogs 

Duck shoes on a boat 
Boat shoes on a duck 
Buster Browns and Mary Janes 
Can cost alotta bucks 

These fifty pairs of shoes 
Wear them as you choose 
But I'm a centipede 
Fifty pairs of shoes I need! 

I wear them all at once 
From Velcro to shoe laced
Snaps, straps and zippers
And wild ones kids embrace! 

Now I change a diaper 
Forty-nine are for my wife 
Soon our baby grows up 
And shoes'll be her life 

What's that I hear? 

The pitter-patter 
     pitter-patter 
       pitter-patter 
     pitter-patter 
pitter-patter 
pitter-patter pitter-patter pitter-patter 
pitter-patter pitter-patter pitter-patter 
pitter-patter pitter-patter pitter-patter 
pitter-patter pitter-patter pitter-patter 
pitter-patter pitter-patter pitter-patter 
pitter-patter pitter-patter pitter-patter 
pitter-patter pitter-patter pitter-patter 
pitter-patter pitter-patter pitter-patter 
pitter-patter pitter-patter pitter-patter 
pitter-patter pitter-patter pitter-patter 
pitter-patter pitter-patter pitter-patter 
pitter-patter pitter-patter pitter-patter 
pitter-patter pitter-patter pitter-patter 
pitter-patter pitter-patter pitter-patter 
pitter-patter pitter-patter pitter-patter 

of one hundred feet? 

IT'S TIME TO SHOP FOR SHOES! 

It makes a centipede's life complete.
