---
title: "Hand Grenade"
writer: "Walter Burns"

I kicked a rock today
And cut my giant toe
I stopped to watch the flowers
But they didn’t even grow
I saw a friend I used to like
But she said she didn’t know
Anyone quite like me:
“A circus side freakshow” 

I told my mom and dad 
I was good and they were glad
But then I spilled some soda
And quickly they were mad

What could I just do
Is there even such a thing?
That promised it would work out right
And end my losing string

I sit and often staring
The teacher thinks I’m daring
To get her heart a racing
If she asked I would be sharing

That the world is so unjust
And I’m filled with wanderlust
I think she popped a vessel
And I think she even cussed

I drew my Uncle Dave
With gun and hand grenade
Teacher kicked me out of school
And I got a failing grade

The assignment I only did
And I’m just a little kid
Take away my innocence 
And slap the word “Forbid”

I kicked a rock today
And cut my giant toe
I stopped to watch the flowers
But they didn’t even grow
I saw a friend I used to like
And I hit her just for show
Is there anyone quite like me
And to whom my vengeance owe?
