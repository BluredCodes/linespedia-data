The way the moon plays off your eyes
I would say is paradise
Stars that shine for heaven’s show
Can only match our afterglow
We are swans so rare and fine
We form a heart with yours and mine    
But I digress, its you, whose blessed
You, the world and I, your guest

Cupid’s bow and Saint Valentine
Would like to know that you are mine
The angels fight for good and vice
Would drop their swords in sacrifice
And reconcile their age-old fight
To give the land to Canaanites
But most of all, our God would weep
If all your love was mine to keep

So if the world should end my dear
I would not mind, with you right here
For I can see within your eyes
Lands of fruit and paradise
And most of all we cannot fail
Our love could be the Holy Grail
Drink with me to love divine
Tell me dear that you’ll be mine
