title: "Daydreams seem to lend"

Daydreams seem to lend
To nightmares never end
They could both be my friend
If they would never end
But you’re calling my name
The same way that I came
Into this world!

Cursed and fondled on your tongue
Something said for dying young
Something said for never living
Something said unforgiving

You’re messing with my head
All my real friends are dead
Or at least in my mind 
So am I 
Let me die
