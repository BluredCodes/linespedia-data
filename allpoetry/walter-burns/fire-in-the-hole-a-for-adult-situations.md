The war is on another shore
Explosion is in our kiss
You are the land I will explore
To look for a spot I've missed

Your neck it dips and meets the sun
Your warmth and flesh I envy
Let the moisture sink to depths
As deep as you will send me

Fingertips are probing missiles
Soft and lethal, quite extreme
I know a spot where I could touch you
And wake you from your bad girl dream

Blue jeans fall to bedroom floor
And panties add to the pile
I can’t camouflage my growth
So I salute with perk and smile

Now a prisoner of our war
Caught by grip and your juice
You thought you’d won, laid down my gun
As I fill you, we cry for truce

Rep'rations made from the war
Clean sheets cover up our sin
You eye me as I grip the edge
And we’re off to war again
