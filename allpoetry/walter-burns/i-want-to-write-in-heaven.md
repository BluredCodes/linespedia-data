title: "I Want to Write in Heaven"
writer: "Walter Burns"

Not one can be omniscient
Not one except our Lord
To assume I'd ever be,
Is folly I won’t afford

I want to write in Heaven
For I’ll never know it all
I want to scribe the beauty
Of Man redeemed from Fall

I will not write of longing
I will write and never tire
I won't write of sin and then
I won't write of lost desire

In my heart are living two
Christ and belov'ed wife
Praise and love I will commune
And pen eternal life

Boundless Heaven will not end
And my heart will ever beat
Reams of writing will ensue
When one day, His eyes, I meet
