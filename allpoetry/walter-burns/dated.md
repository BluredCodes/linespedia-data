Today is

January 29, 2002

2002
backwards
and forwards 
it’s the same

Here's the proof!

2002: Backwards 
2002: sdrawroF 

just like S-S
the days of the week
Sat in the Sun
Got hot

Just like the months of the year
J-D
With JASON* in the story

12 A.M. is in the middle of the night!

Sorry I’m cofused

itsallthesame




*Jason from the Friday the 13th movies wherein the plots are all the same (and JASON the initials for the months of July-November)
