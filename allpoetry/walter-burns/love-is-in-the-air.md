title: "Love is in the Air"

Lost, alone, afraid, aware 
Looking for a scare
Each and every hackled hair
Love is in the air

Duck… duck… Goose!
Tied 'round 
A neck
A noose
Love is in the air

Ring around the rosy
Pocket full of nosy
Friends who like to stare
Love is in the air

The game of Life:
Monopoly
You own my heart
You’re all to me
Waterworks
I do not want
But now... 
There ours to share
(sniff) Love is in the air
