We've been here a short while
But in the infinite scheme
We've done it all, my friend

We've been down the road
The uphill battle
Faced the fear of unknown worlds
And swerved before the cattle

There were times when I said
The road is getting slight
Upon the fork that next we see
I think I'll take a right

And when I miss my exit
You smile and we keep going
Nothing can e'er be kept away
