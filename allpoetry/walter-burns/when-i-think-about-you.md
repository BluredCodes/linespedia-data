When I Think About YouI think of sunny daysRunning free through the parkAnd passionate displaysLying 'lone in the darkOf a candlelit nightA sudden gust of windWhen you say it's alrightIt doesn't have to endThe only light remainsCut deep into your eyesNeither one of us complainsAs other lovers cry
