R.L. Valen Stines

In nightmare room, dark dungeon, or the creepy crypt  
The blood from chewed on beauties is so daint'ly sipped  
Rose stem glass raised in the air, its  R.L. Valen Stines
Choc'late covered eyeballs and black lotus smelling fine 

Milady, I think of her as romance fills my veins 
Made the reservations, we'll be eating human brains 
Look into her deadlight eyes and stroke her serpent hair 
Then a stroll inside the sew'rs, we'll gaze at garbage there 

On this day, I shall propose, we bond immortal souls
At least until a wooden stake makes a goary hole  
I've loved her since the time we shared at the city dump 
But most of all I love her for the times I get Goosebumps
