---
title: "I'm a Cowboy!"
writer: "Walter Burns"
slug: "im-a-cowboy"

I’m a cowboy, did you hear, I’m a cowboy
I live near the range yet I cook with an oven
I’m a cowboy lookin for some cowgirl lovin
I’m a cowboy I live in good ole Fort Worth
I’m a cowboy at least by the rite of my birth
I’m a cowboy cuz I’m down on my luck
I’m a cowboy shh I don’t own a truck
I’m a cowboy I listen to "Oldies 98"
I’m a cowboy "Country" sure makes me irate
I’m a cowboy folks I wear a ball cap
I’m a cowboy ya’ll just shut yer yap
I’m a cowboy I hate five card Monty
I’m a cowboy pass the Pace Picante
I’m a cowboy I eat varmits for my dinner
I’m a cowboy I put their heads on my veneer
I’m a cowboy I know that didn’t quite rhyme
I’m a cowboy? Geesh! I get that all the timeI'm not a cowboy!!!
