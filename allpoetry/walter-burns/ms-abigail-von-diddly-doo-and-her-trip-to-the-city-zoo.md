Ms. Abigail von Diddly Doo and her trip to the city Zoo

Ms. Abigail von Diddly Doo
Watching the monkeys at the zoo
Found a keeper by name of Lou
Bravely, she told the man 
what she saw those monkeys do
Then she blushed and went on asking
"Can I get a cage like that for two?"
