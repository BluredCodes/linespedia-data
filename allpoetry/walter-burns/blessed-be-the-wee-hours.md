title: "Blessed be the Wee Hours"

Blessed be the wee hours
For they shall inherit these:
The poet and the love he has
To share all that he sees

Blessed be the wee hours
For they shall grow in worth
When every poets’ solitude
Is shared across the earth

Blessed be the wee hours, 
A poet at his best; 
But blessed be the morning, 
For a poet then can rest

Blessed be the poets
Who can't sleep when others do
For they shall get their naps
While their words are read by you
