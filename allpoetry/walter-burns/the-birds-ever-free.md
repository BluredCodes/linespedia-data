---
title: "the birds ever free"
writer: "Walter Burns"
slug: "the-birds-ever-free"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/40754-the-birds-ever-free-by-Walter-Burns"

The birds knew they'd never see
As you flew away from me
A love like ours tonight
Then you took upon your flight

The birds up in the sky
Never knew they were to die
Like the love we had inside
A dove sleeps on the tide
