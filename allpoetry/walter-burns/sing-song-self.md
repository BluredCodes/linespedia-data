---
title: "sing song self"
writer: "Walter Burns"

am going to spiral this poem
because it’s a reflection of my life
and with each enjambment
and trochaic meter
you will understand
the shaky pattern
the instability
the walking-
working-
living-
dying
of my 
day-to-day
die-to-die
sing song self
do you feel ashamed
paying five cents for a peep?
