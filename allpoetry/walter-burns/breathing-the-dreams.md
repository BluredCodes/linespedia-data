---
title: "Breathing the Dreams"
writer: "Walter Burns"
slug: "breathing-the-dreams"

The luminous foil
It covers the sky
I feel that we’re here
Just so we can die

You’re taking me out
You put me back in
Thought you would kill me
You’d kill me again

I’m breathing the steam
Of a thousand dead dreams
I’m breathing the dreams
I’m breathing the dreams

I wasn’t that pleasant
When first you arrived
So what do you think 
That might have survived

Now that I’m lifeless
You stir me around
Pick up the pieces 
That fell on the ground

From out of the cold 
And into the heat
You’re taking my heart
And then you repeat

Our love has a taste
Of bitter rebirth
Why do you save me
What is my worth

I’m breathing the steam
Of a thousand dead dreams
I’m breathing the dreams
I’m breathing the dreams

Now I can see through
The luminous seams
To see that we’re breathing
