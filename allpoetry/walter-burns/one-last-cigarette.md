title: "One Last Cigarette"
writer: "Walter Burns"

Crickets chirping 
Lizards lurking
On my house of brick,
How'd you feel 
‘bout creepy crawlies
Whose eyes they hafta lick?

Horny toads?
Well so am I!
Hiding on the porch,
Sucking in 
My final breath,
Extinguishing
The torch.

My head is swimming
I sneak to bed
I better gargle 
Or she'll know,
A couple sprays of
Air freshener
And I’m sure as 
Good to go!
