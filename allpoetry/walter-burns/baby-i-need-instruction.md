title: "Baby, I Need Instruction"

The room is vast 
Its speeding passed 
But why its fast 
I cannot ask 

I cannot ask 
The reason why 
I cannot last 
Within your eyes 

The page is long 
I'm writing songs 
But still 
There's something wrong 
Beyond 
Everything that's right 

The last note 
It will not play 
I've tried it every way 
Each note a half 
Each rest a whole 
Like cadence of my soul 
A child who jams the keys 
In random Rock n' Roll 
There's no escape 
In your eyes 
Yet still 
I yearn 
to learn 
their rhythm 

Baby, I need instruction
