---
title: "Keepsakes"
writer: "Walter Burns"

I see you 

In the sting 
Of swift emersion

As if something, 
stunning 
stroked the darkness

A light glows
my eyes 
blink back levied tears

You are not real. 

You seem
as if divine.

I’d rush 
to meet you 

But you’d simply fade 
Into the canvas

The stars, 
keepsakes 
of my loss

I ache so

I lived 
through hunger’s path

Lay me down
I’ll feed you  
grapes of 
our past love
