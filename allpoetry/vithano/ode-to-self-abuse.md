How grand it is to masturbate!
O what a joy it is!
I’d like to thank Whoever gave
A gift as good as this!

What joy could come more quickly,
Convenient as a switch?
What high could nigh so cheaply,
Without a single hitch?

No need for dinner and dances,
No need for pantomime; 
Leave suggestive glances
To those who have the time.

All you need are fingers
And a little time alone;
But if you feel somewhat guilty,
There’s still time to atone!

What joy it is to be onanistic,
To be best friends with yourself;
A party autoerotic
Is good for both health and pelf!
