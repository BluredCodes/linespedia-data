---
title: "Like dreams, unfulfilled"
writer: "vithano"
slug: "like-dreams-unfulfilled"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/14886-Like-dreams--unfulfilled-by-vithano"

Like dreams, unfulfilled, we float 
Our phantoms to the soil,
Resigned. We shall never rise again,
for, having once laid these hopes to rest,
the damp earth receives our breath
like seeds that one day sprout 
flowers of dead dismay. Nor 
shall the sun see our spite again,
for our souls are sunken 
beneath the surface of society, 
though not yet forgotten, 
very much dead. 

Six feet beneath your busy feet
Lies the flesh of my discontent,
That flowers feed on;
Those deeds left undone, and the
Million words unspoken
Toss and turn in the grave ---

Those dreams of the dead, though left unspoken,
Are debts unpaid, and pale bones broken.
