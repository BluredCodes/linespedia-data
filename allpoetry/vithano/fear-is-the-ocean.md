---
title: "fear is the ocean"
writer: "vithano"
slug: "fear-is-the-ocean"
source: "AllPoetry"

fear is the ocean
fathoms deep, which drops
to depths undiscovered still,
waiting to swallow
light and living things.

putrid vessels struggle there.
bloated, misshapen
devils with ancient clans
from forgotten times
when lizards ruled above.

eden had no sea, 
so while adam’s shame first grew,
mermaids in their secret pools
Swam nakedly still.

cities fall and mountains grow
smooth with time and tide;
the sea stays
moving but unmoved.

fear is the ocean 
where forgotten things
lie swimming still,
naked devils with ancient claims,
moving yet unmoved.

fear is the ocean 
fathoms deep,
yet unfathomed still.
