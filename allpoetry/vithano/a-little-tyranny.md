title: "a little tyranny"

I have a little tyranny
Whom I love to delight;
It bears a smack of villainy
And huddles in the night.

A guilty pleasure is a funny thing. 
It is the pleasantest thing of all,
And though it looks like a little thing,
It is really ten feet tall.

A guilty pleasure is a funny thing. 
It is the pleasantest thing of all,
And though it makes us feel like kings,
It also makes kings fall.
