Mouth full with Mama’s breasts,
I happy lie in Mama’s arms,
Meekly minding Mama’s milk
So she doesn’t spill.

Tickled tit makes Mama laugh
When tongue on teat taste-touches
So Mama tilts her eyes at I
Telling me that tickles.

Stomach full with Mama’s breasts
I quit the feed, let go and yawn
Away from meal Mama:
Sleep, slumber-slurp.I have always found the line between the erotic and the innocent mind-bogglingly thin.
