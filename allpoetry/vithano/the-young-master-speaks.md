title: "the young master speaks"
writer: "vithano"

When, as a child,
damp and moist
in the toilet’s gloom,
my fingers fumbling
for release,

I shivered.

What can lusting children do,
fumbling in the darkness,
But fear 
Discovery ?

the parents
would not approve.
