---
title: "They Won't Agree"
writer: "natb17"
slug: "they-wont-agree"

I watched the sun fall down the day you went away from me
Another one gone
What have I done wrong?
I just wanted love and they won't agree
Nobody's fair and I knew it all
So why do I fall in too deep?
Feelings untrue and I always knew
I just wanted love and they won't agree.
Opened the curtains and made up my bed
Well, that's what they said they'd always do...
How could they leave me on a thread?
I'll never do the things they do..
Photos betray
How could you play with the arm of cupid's wing?
Another one gone
What have I done wrong?
I just wanted love and they won't agree
I need to walk a regular mile
Put up a bridge between sun and moon
This clock is ticking under my skin
Will I ever.....?
Damn! I'll never love again...
Here in this fog I'm seeing clear now
Isn't it weird how I was so blind
Reflections affecting what I believed in
I'll find the cleansing throughout time...
And so it stands,standing it all
And they ain't got a tab on me...
This is my song
Done nothin' wrong
be as it may
They won't agree.
