As the swelting heat rose and the spotlight blinded me
a sense of surreal dreams possessed me
It is moments such as these that a single quality of life resides
There I was living in the presence of every stranger
They gazed upon me as my foreign face connected into their world
All of their worlds.
And I stood there, relieved by their applauses and cheers.
My existance was welcomed by their smiles and laughter
Gratitiude was followed by tears...
The quantity of roses grew and I proceeded to hold them closer to my heart as well as my dreams
Out of the multitude to me, 
in this bliss of glory and grace,
The curtains were then drawn.
My co-conspirators had flown
and I stood again...
an average joe with a story.
