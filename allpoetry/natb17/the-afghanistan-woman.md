title: "The Afghanistan Woman"

Resuscitate those progeny of hatred and greet these children of fear
Hide your identity with dark sheets and silent mouths
Huddle yourself in shadowed corners and nibble salvaged bread crumbs in the way the rats do..
Live dumbfounded and used.
Scatter yourselves to shelter at the slightest boom
Afterall, this is hell and anything goes..
I see you now:
dying,
You carry blisters and scabs
lumps and diseases
You have entered the realm of significance
and I'm on my way...
