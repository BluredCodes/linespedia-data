title: "Eye of the Beholder"
writer: "natb17"

Trapped in a spiral of my own infamous
blunders I gaze further into my distorted
reflection and all that I've become.

I see a girl:

so blindly driven by a world of cosmetics, 
nail polish and boys.. 

so naive, in the way that a single smile, in
my presence, justifies my perfection..

so curious as my expression finds a rebirth over and over again..

and yet, captured, in the bliss of beauty so 
profound..

I'm winding through your mind with my 
introspective words and these eyes,

so piercing, deliver unto you a sense of 
reverence...

I have intrigued you now and you are mine.
