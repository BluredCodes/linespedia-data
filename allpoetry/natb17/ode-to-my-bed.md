Here I lay
wrapped in these sheets of colored cotton
embraced by the thickness of warm blankets
dependent upon the springs of my mattress to console my tired body
secured for rest as a pillow of silk gently cradles my restless imagination
So here I lay,
dreaming sweet dreams as my body heat circulates
Into a deeper trance I fall..
Then I awake
the sun is upon me
My nocturnal grave is crushed.
