What can I say
When it's all been done?
Like my moonlit dreams
Remain crushed by the sun
And so goes the words
Set in your way
A dead bed of roses
And I'm so ashamed...
My coast is clear
When you're out of mind
I'm rocked in the cradle
Of my own kind
And you say I'm weak
When I'm feeling strong
So strongly in love
Just leave me alone....
Here once again someones slowing me down
I just wanna make it home.
My love wasn't one for the heart to unhand
Can you understand...
when I wish you Sweet Repose.
So treat me unstable
Like the clouds above my head
You tell me it's true
Then you leave me...
There's no record playing and there's writing on the wall
There's nothing here but these memories..
Memories of regret....
Here once again someones slowing me down
I just wanna make it home.
My love wasn't one for the heart to unhand
Can you understand?
Now and again you'll be calling my name
I'll be walking alone..
What words put me through you were never a man!
Can you understand
When I wish you..
Cause I don't need you...
And I wish you sweet repose.
