Standing by the moon in a place unknown to all
We're ascending into obscure thoughts and descending back to earth again
You were only waiting for a rebirth to recapture 
Watching as people and events consume us
Waiting for the things yet to construct us
Until we find oursleves left...
To stand starring into the water.
