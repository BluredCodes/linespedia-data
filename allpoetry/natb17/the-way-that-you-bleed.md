title: "The Way That You Bleed"

Inhaling, exhaling
cause and effect
beaten and broken
together again.
Laughing and crying
weeping inside
shutting your mouth
conceiving a lie..
No one to hear you
No one to see
No one to feel you 
The way that you bleed.
Pushing and pulling
slave to the world
given and taken
disposable being
You tear and you scratch
You rant and you rave
whispers uproar
in ways that you ache
No one to hear you 
No one to see
No one to feel you
The way that you bleed
Moving and moaning
passing unpleased
a voice calls your name
your mutters are hushed
silence forbidden
now face to face
stories unfolding
scraps of disgrace
closer to closure
laying it out
sobbing and sighing
and I've found you out
Now I can hear you
now I have seen
I won't forget you
