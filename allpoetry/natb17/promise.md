You come to me now offering warm smiles and careful hands
Oblivious to the fact that they say we're too young
At this point all is our keepsake
Resting preciously untouchable in the multitude of their sticks and stones
...and I love you.
While hours consume our minutes and days consume the hours,
weeks consume our days and months consume our weeks
You remain as an everlasting soulmate,
consuming my life and my dreams...
So here you are for a second time
Offering your promises to my worshipping face
...and I love you too...
