So this is life...
Like you,
I want it all
the others feel the same
Your face doesn't exist to my world
Your death means nothing.
I could care less about your destinations
even though we share the same highway to survive
Your just another face in the crowd
alien to my character
You're everywhere!
The long lines
the car in front of me going 20 under who'll never move aside
You're there again,
with enough groceries at checkout to solve the world's starvation
Why can't you move!!
You people annoy me and you only slow me down
But then again...
what's wrong with solving the world's starvation?
Maybe you're in my selfishness
Maybe I'm to blame
Life was made for killing time
Maybe I can wait.
