Something about the way he bit
The way he pierced my vain...
He gave unto me a brand new fit
I wouldn't be the same....
No one knows of what I am
Nor why I'm so intrigued
Possesed in beauty everlasting
Death shall bow to me
Now I sit in his darkened layer
lit by candlelight..
playing predator, catching prey
every single night.
He gives me words of wisdom
He is the map of hell
He gives me cunning ways to live
Until I've learned them well.
No other man will be this way
trapped inside the fire
should commom men approach in time
