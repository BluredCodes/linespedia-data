Bound to him he draws me in
His skylit eyes possess me
His hands secure me at slightest grip
The thought of his simple grins deliver the sweet nostalgia of solace
It is here that I dwell in everlasting contentment
And then he speaks:
Tender words caress my heart
He now holds me in the palm of his hand
Both the power to break me or build me
My heart surrenders as blessings are counted
I'm left to fear the day I shall long for him alone...
