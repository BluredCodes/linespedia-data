title: "Valentine's Day Gift"

On this Valentine day
What gift should I give to you?
On this Valentine day
What gift can convey my feelings for you?
I searched the whole world for a gift but found nothing
That could convey my feelings towards you.
I searched through the wild oceans and the blue skies,
For a special thing for you, 
But found none which could convey the feelings of my love to you.

Now, on this Valentine’s day the only thing that I can give you,
Is my heart, soul and life.
I don’t know whether it is worthy of being given to you,
I don’t know whether it shall be accepted by you,
I don’t know whether it shall be cherished by you,
Maybe it will be rejected by you,
Maybe it will not gain much attention from you,
Maybe it will be of no use to you,
But these are the only things that I can give to you,
On this Valentine’s day.
My heart, soul and life are the only things
That I am capable of giving to you,
That I wish to give to you,
That I want to stay with you,
That I want to remain forever with you and only you.

Happy Valentine’s day!
Please forever with me stay.
Keep our hearts together till the doomsday,
And be always merry and gay.
On this exotic and special day,
Let me wish you a special Valentine’s day!
