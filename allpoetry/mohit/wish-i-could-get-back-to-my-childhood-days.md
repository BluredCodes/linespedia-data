Wish I Could Get Back To My Childhood Days,
They were so full of merry and gay,
It was so carefree and cheerful,
So lovely and so bright, humming the nursery rhymes.

Wish I Could Get Back To My Childhood Days,
Where Santa Clauz visited the Christmas nights,
Where gifts and affection awaited me on each occassion,
Where the Kindergarden world was so serene.

Wish I Could Get Back To My Childhood Days,
Days which were so positive, prosperous and bright as the sun,
Where fairy tales twinkled in the starry night,
Where the birds sang hymns to the soul.

Wish I Could Get Back To My Childhood Days,
Where I could receive the same amount of love and affection,
Where I could feel the same amount of merriness,
Where I could hear the hymns and the fairy tales,
Where I could feel the serenity in the world around me,
Where I could see the angels around my world protecting me.

Wish I Could Get Back To My Childhood Days,
Or rather go back to my days of innocence and holiness,
Go back to my days of serenity, happiness and dream,
Wish I Could Get Back To My Childhood Days!
