Walking across the sea-shore under the purplish sky
In a serene environment under the twinkling stars
Listening to the sounds of waters splashing in the sea
I sat down looking up at the sky feeling the tranquility,
I sat down and looked up at the sky and felt peace within myself,
I sat down unaware of the miracle that was gonna take place. 

I was enjoying the mysticism when I saw a bright light in the sky,
A light that was fast approaching my forlorn world,
A light that landed next to me and dazzled my eyes forcing them to close for a while,
And even in that state I could see the brightness.
I opened my eyes and looked at a beautiful fairy,
Yes, mysterious because such beauty exists only in the fantasies of a poet,
Or in the inanimate world of a painter, or in the descriptions of a passionate lover.
 
Her hairs were braided, and looked like a bunch of flowers,
Her eyes had something in them, something shiny; a sparkle maybe,
Her smile was hypnotizing and captivating, they had the power of magic,
With her was an object that looked pretty because of the hands that held her,
She was dressed in all white and she looked like an angel.

She opened her eyes and I got to look at the two brightest stars in the cosmos,
She smiled and lit up the surroundings and brought a cheer among the birds,
She raised her head up and I got to look at the face of a beautiful girl,
She then spoke and the waters stood still listening to what she said,
Her words sounded like music and the music ran all over my body,
Purifying each and every cell, purifying my heart and soul.

She spoke words of encouragement, words of confidence,
Words of enthusiasm, words of happiness,
She spoke words of belief and spirituality,
She spoke words of friendship and love.

She brightened up my whole world,
She provided me with that zest to carry on, 
She gave me that confidence that never allowed me to loose faith,
She passed on those magical words to me that:-
“Love in this world is like the sweetest rose in a garden of weeds,
it’s the sweetest melody, the most wonderful journey,
its something where a person’s happiness is connected with the happiness of his or her loved,
its an inexplicably wonderful feeling, a slight touch of it can change worlds,
Love is magical.”

And saying so she entered into my world, into my heart,
She became the music dwelling smoothly in my heart,
And I was blessed with the birth of love in my heart,
My whole world looked a better place, a serene place,
My whole world got a new meaning, it became a wonderful journey,
And I know love dwells in my heart always encouraging me to move ahead.
And I know love has spread all over my body,
Each and every part of me is filled with that magical potion called love.
