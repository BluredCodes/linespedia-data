---
title: "Angelic Beauty"

No one made me feel the way you did,
No one attracted me the way you did,
No one’s looks mesmerized me the way your looks did,
No one visited my dreams the number of times you did,
No one distracted my concentration the way you did,
No one made my heart beat so fast as you did,
I don’t know what you have done to me?
Have you cast a spell on me? 
Or is it your pure innocent looks that I am obsessed with?
You seem to be out of this world, an angel
Yes, that’s what you are, an angel who has overpowered me,
An angel, who seems sweeter than the word sweet,
An angel, whom I want to meet,
An angel, whose eyes seems to be a deep ocean,
Inviting me to dive into it forever,
An angel, who is the description of beauty itself,
Beauty, which has overpowered men, including myself.
An angel, so mystic and wonderful,
So enchanting and beautiful,
That’s what you are.
I have no words to describe the qualities in you,
It, but seems, beauty is the word which best describes you.
