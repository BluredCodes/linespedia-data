I had a dream tonight, where I saw her clothed in a red dress,
I had a dream tonight, were I held her hands in mine,
I had a dream tonight, where I saw her in my arms,
Where the moon encouraged me to kiss her and make her mine,
Where she looked prettier than ever when the moon’s light fell on her softly and gently,
Where the harsh sun-light was far far away.

Soft wind blew over her hairs, under the purplish sky,
We were sitting under the stars, feeling the love around us,
The fairy tale continued and her eyes met mine and she felt shy,
The time was right for me and I gathered courage, I thought of saying her how much I loved,
And I could see the sea-waves rising high encouraging me to make her mine,
And I could see the smile in the face of the moon,
And I could see the happiness amongst the stars,
And I could hear the soft music in the air.

I looked at her once again and watched that innocence,
That serenity in her eyes, that happiness in her smile,
I was glad that she was by my side, 
I was glad that she sung me another fairy-tale,
And yes, I made my move tonight,
I went and held her hands in mine, 
I looked deep into her eyes and said how much I loved,
She smiled and I said how much I had been captivated.

She accepted my love and became the music running smoothly in my heart,
She accepted my love and brightened  up my world,
She accepted my love and I felt heaven on earth,
She came into my arms, into my world like an angel and gave me a new life.

And I saw the heavenly waters rolling down onto the surface of the earth,
And I saw goddess moon, surrounded and not covered by clouds,
And I heard the birds singing in the air,
And I heard the angels sing me a fairy-tale, a tale of love,
Yes, I saw and heard all this,
And I had to pinch myself hard to realise that this wasn’t a dream that I was dreaming,
Yes, this wasn’t a dream, she was actually there by my side dressed in a red wedding dress,
And I saw my heart placed next to hers, dancing to the tunes of love.
DREAMS COME TRUE!
