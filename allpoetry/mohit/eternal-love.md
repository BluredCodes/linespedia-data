---
title: "Eternal Love"
writer: "mohit"
slug: "eternal-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/33796-Eternal-Love-by-mohit"
---

Sailing in a ship, moving coast to coast,Under the twinkling stars, under the shining moonlight,Feeling the freshness in the air of a starry night,I sat down eyes closed, sailing in a world of dreams.In a huge ship, I saw a girl,Hairs braided, twinkling eyes, charismatic smile,A creature that made my heart skip a beat,For one moment my world consisted of just me and her,,But the moment was broken when I heard a voice inside me say,That she was a star out of my reach.She was a princess and I an ordinary lanky man,She was a symbol of etiquettes and beauty living in the realms of luxuries,And I was a symbol of a rowdy teen living in the realms of an empty space.That was the difference but the difference couldn't prevent the birth of love, an eternal love.The next time when I saw her, she was opposing life,Looked as if all her desires and feelings were left unanswered.She intended to commit suicide,She had tears in her eyes, sadness in the depths of her heart,with intentions to jump over-board.I somehow managed to prevent her from doing so,And I saved her or rather my life,I prevented her or rather the death of my soul,And once again my heart skipped a beat,When I heard her say that she was engaged to a young prince.We became frens and I saw much of her,I saw the beauty of her heart, the beauty of her conscience,And love conquered all the rest of the things in the world,And when I realized that she was engaged against her wishes,My heart skipped a beat,When I heard her say that she had fallen in love with me.All these things brought me and her in a state of bliss,But the young prince when he heard of this,Sent out his men to arrest me, that he did,But he couldn't prevent the princess,She came to me and freed me of all the barriers,And we ran to and fro fighting and defeating the enemies.In the meantime, the ship was struck by a cursed ice-berg,And the water gushed into it, piercing it into two-halves,And as the ship sank, I witnessed the worst scene,Nothing could have been worse than what I had seen,I saw hundreds of people drowning into the sea,I heard hundreds of people screaming and pleading for life,I saw little, cute and innocent children coming face to face with death,I witnessed the cruelty of Lady Fate.To save my princess, I put her up on a raft,I lay on the freezing waters, I lay on the cursed waters of death,And I made her promise me that she wouldn't give up,That she would live a life of merriment, That she would always remember me,And then I kissed her good-bye and sank into the arms of death.And at that point I opened my eyes,And my heart skipped a beat,When I saw the same young girl I dreamed about,She was in my arms, She was close to my heart,Our love was deeper than the heart of the ocean,And the love between us went on and on forever and ever,And nothing could come in between us, nothing except an eternal love.
