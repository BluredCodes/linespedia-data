---
title: "Love You Till The End Of My Life"
writer: "mohit"
slug: "love-you-till-the-end-of-my-life"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/14217-Love-You-Till-The-End-Of-My-Life-by-mohit"

The stars shining high above in the sky,
The music going on near by,
The soft wind blowing,
The meeting of our heart and eyes,
And the love all around us
Makes me feel like staying with you
Till the end of my life.

Me and you sitting all alone,
And the moonlight falling on your face,
And you speaking words sounding like music,
And your smile doing wonders to me,
And the soft breeze blowing through your hairs,
Makes me feel like looking at you
Till the end of my life.

Me and you trying to be close to each other,
And the magic in your eyes,
And my feelings of love for you,
And the union of our souls
Makes me feel like being united with you
Till the end of my life

Me and you enjoying the mysticism
And the bond between us,
And the stars twinkling,
And the moon shining,
And the breeze blowing,
And the music and the night,
And the splashing of waters in the sea,
Makes me feel like keeping you happy,
Till the end of my life.

Me and you sitting under the stars,
All alone, my heart singing to you,
And your heart playing the soft music,
And both of us lost in deep thoughts of love,
Makes me feel like being in love with you
Till the end of my life.

It really makes me feel like being in love with you
Till the end of my life.
You are the very essence of my life,
My heart and my soul, and I know
I will be in love with you all my life,
Whether this or the next or the next life.
Never, never go away from my life,
And I will be in love with you
Till the end of my life.
