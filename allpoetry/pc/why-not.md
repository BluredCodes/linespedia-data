Why not

Why can’t we live in peace
Why must we live in pain
Why can’t we live in harmony
Why must we live in hate
Why can’t we live in love
Why must we live in death
Why can’t we live in kindness
Why must we live in greediness
But most of all we live
In fear of each-other
And each-other live in fear of
DEATH..

Pamela Massey
