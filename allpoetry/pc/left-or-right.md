Left or right

I’m standing at the crossroads
To heaven or hell go left go right
Which way will I go
Left is heaven but go get there
I have to overcome all my suffering
Fight all the memories which haunt me
Forgive all my ghosts and forget all my pain but its not going to be easy.
Right is hell to go there all I have to do is let them win
 keep all the memories and all my pain.

Pamela Massey
