A Little Girl

A Little Girl curled up in a ball 
With a single tear drop in her eye 
So fragile, So pale 
If only someone could see her sweet heart 
Crying out to be held 
Wishing for someone to wipe away her tears 
Inside she is screaming, shouting 
Wishing someone could hear
But no-one is listening to her tears

Pamela Massey
