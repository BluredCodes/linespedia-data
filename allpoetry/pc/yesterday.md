Yesterday

Yesterday I saw your smile but
Today your smile was away.
Yesterday I heard your laughter but
Today your laughter was away.
Yesterday I saw the sparkle in your eyes but
Today that sparkle was away.
Yesterday I had everything but
Today I have nothing…….

Pamela Massey
