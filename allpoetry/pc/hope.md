HOPE

Looking into your eyes
Seeing the life sparkling through
Feeling the warmth of your heart, calling out for my love.
Your lips trembling for my kiss.
Your desires to be loved and cherished.
To have your dreams come true.
Your dreams of love and happiness.
Being held by the one you love.
Feeling their heart beating next to yours,
Nothing can take this feeling away and only one can cherish them.
Pamela Massey
