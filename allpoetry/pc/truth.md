Truth

My light covered by darkness
My hopes in the shadow of hate
My dream’s lost in the anger
My love trapped by the truth
My peace smothered by pain
The life which my heart dreams for, hopes for, is not
The love I’ve always cried out for is unanswered

Pamela Massey
