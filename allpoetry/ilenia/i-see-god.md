title: "I See God"

When a Cardinal sits upon a tree
and sings prettier than a symphony
I see God
When a Rose bursts forth in all its glory
and a Peony tells a similar story
I see God
When a mountain peak is capped in snow
with the sun hovering over it all aglow
I see God
When the oceans waves curl up with foam
in the place that Sharks and Whales call home
I see God
When a Baby suckles at it's Mother's breast
and tiny eggs hatch in a Hummingbirds  nest
I see God
When all Creation is doing what it must
and we do the best we can, without fuss
God sees us.
