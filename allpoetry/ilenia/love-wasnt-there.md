title: "Love wasn't There"
writer: "ilenia"

I took a trip to distant places, love wasn't there
It seems to be absent no matter where

I go, even in some friendly places
with many familiar faces

It makes one wonder where it disappears
When you're not in it up to your ears

What makes love so elusive
It really is most exclusive

We seek for this favor to gain
Yet, who among us can explain

What love is, not to be confused with lust
Or when in marriage you put your trust

I'm talking about an earthquake in the heart
That will never be the same if you ever part

Like, when together you make lyric and melody
The kind you think will last an eternity

That's what being in love is all about
So why can't it last, what makes it run out?
