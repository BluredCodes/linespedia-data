A faint reply to the refrain 
Was spinning round in my brain 
So bittersweet the melody 
It made your presence real to me 

Where are you in the continuum of infinity 
Close enough for our destined affinity 
I feel you now inside my pores 
Your essence reaches to my core 

The lightening touch of you 
Sporadic but deep and true 
Am I losing my sanity 
Or is this a case of vanity 

To think such divine grace 
Would be mine to embrace 

To go back now to the mundane 
Would be worse than being sane 
So I'll stay in this realm of wonder 
Where you can reach me with your thunder.
