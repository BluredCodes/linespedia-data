title: "An Occasional Whim"

Explosion of meteoric activity
Coursing through my brain
On the right side where creativity
Rivals the thoughts of the sane
That form multiple questions
Pertaining to inner selections
That eliminate outer accoutrements
Which denies free souls contentment

Where art thou O Melchizedek 
Art thou not still of Salem
Whose name denotes what I seek
Peace in the inner parts not mayhem
Yet, some outer pleasures not meek
But exciting enough to satisfy
The bold of heart, but not to sin
Only to live an occasional whim.
