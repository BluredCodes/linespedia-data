the notes insinuate themselves  
serpentine snake movement
slithering into my brain,
the beat taking hold of my
senses like a torrential rain

blotting out all otherness in
its completeness of sound
meaning to excite at the
same time to confound

drums  stampeding horses 
vibrating your nerves to 
excrutiating exultation
matching the base forces 
that scream for the
ultimate sensation

like a whirling dervish
achieving a spiritual high
creating a wondrous wish
that unite both earth and sky

or a ballads soft melody 
soothing nerve endings
romancing your body
caressing and blending
teasing and sending
messages to every 
fibre of your being.

nothing can hypnotize
surprise and energize
or make you fantasize
like music!
