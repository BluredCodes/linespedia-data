Blade of Love

I see you in the Moon at night
and in the Suns morning light
at times I see you in the stars
and other cosmic nebulars
In seeing you so very much
I'm fervid to feel your touch
and though it hasn't happened 
yet, I'm so glad that we have met
 I have you in the falling rain
that comes to kiss away my pain
and the flakes of pure white snow
that tell me you know that I know
on the shore through oceans breeze
to me you whisper, words that seize
swept on your wave of desire, I'm set 
afire, and with the ebb and flow of the 
tidal tow, you thrust your cosmic blade 
of light, into my void and I take flight 
for I must be where e'er you are
even if on some distant star.
