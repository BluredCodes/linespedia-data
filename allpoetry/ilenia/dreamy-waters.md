hush, don't speak, don't 
break the spell, softly,
softly, let the swell of
your waves flow
over me 

immerse me in your 
womb of satiny wetness
billow and flow pressing
 me close as you caress
let your rivulets run rampant
across my riverbed

meander your  mellow brook
across my creek into the  crook
of my  riparian tributary
sluice as you drain into 
my watercourse

mellow me as you cascade
down into my waterway
let your rapids ripple playfully
across my current
a----hh,  Poseidon come lie in my lagoon.
(;-;)
