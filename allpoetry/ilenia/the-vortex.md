title: "The Vortex"

The dreamer looked into the vortex
he was caught in the headiness of it's spin
For a moment he thought he would be pulled in
The sensation was not unlike having sex

until he saw a skull and crossbones float by him grinning...

Things were disjointed, he wanted the woman 
that had called him across the great barrier 
he wasn't sure she was human

He had never experienced anything like it, a moment of
supreme beauty as she called out to him in  a voice
more mellifluous than any sound he had ever heard

His eyes seemed to have left their sockets when he first saw her
she was unlike anything he had ever seen, but he could not see
her clear enough to distinguish any features except her lips and eyes

It was more like a feeling of knowing what was behind the black 
covering yet he could see the huge black eyes that mesmerized 
him and large ripe red lips turned up in a smile that thrilled his
body at the thought of them touching him

Where had she gone this rare creature of mysterious beauty
Suddenly he saw coming towards him what looked like a black hole... 

As it got nearer he saw it was her, she wore no clothing for
she was covered with her long hair which fell in endless waves of 
what looked like black satin. It took his breath away, and before
he could speak she had him enveloped in an embrace that made 
him shiver with anticipation

He looked into eyes black as coal that said things which gave him
a rush, as he opened his mouth to speak she filled it with
her essence...

He was so overcome he wanted to swallow her whole, but she grasped 
him tighter and he couldn't move...she made him so ecstatic he could
not control himself...

She opened her mouth and he was drawn in, and as he began spinning downward 
he realized he was in the vortex...alone...
