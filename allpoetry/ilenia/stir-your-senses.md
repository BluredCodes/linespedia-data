Stir Your Senses

Can't you feel Me
in the wind
and smell Me 
in the rain
and know to love Me 
is to gain
a friend in need
forevermore
who when you call
won't shut the door
who sees in you
the person He adores

Don't you see Me in the sun
and on the mountain high
and know that I'm the one
who is with you when you die
to this temporary life on earth
who will raise you in the sky
to the home of your new birth

Can't you hear Me rustle in the trees
as I gently caress your soul
like a warm summer breeze
shaping you into a whole

Can't you taste Me in the bread
that gives you nourishment
and My saving words you've read
a time that is well spent

Let My spirit stir your senses
respond to this My ploy
all your sins are now past tenses
celebrate in My celestial joy
