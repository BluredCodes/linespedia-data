Last night was fantastic...
It was electric
It was orgiastic
It was energetic
It was prismatic
It was prophetic

It was not terrestrial
It was most celestial

Like a mythical land of means
Where one can satisfy their dreams

Gliding on glistening glaciers of glory
Observing  the  story of Orion
Feeling sometimes like a lamb
Sometimes like a lion

Traveling through the realm of pleasure
Reaping moments of bliss to treasure

Being seduced by supernal sights
Reveling in the land of delights

Eyes made clear so I can see
Visions of what it next will be

Beings unlike what mortals know
Portraying for me a cosmic show
Making me part of seraphic flow
While under the same divine glow

O Please, never end this night so glorious
In which I'm feeling most victorious!

 Eileen T. Waldron © 2001
