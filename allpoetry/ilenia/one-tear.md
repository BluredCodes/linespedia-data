The park smelled like spring
On the bench i sat staring 
down
I watched a cherry blossom 
skitter in the breeze across 
the ground
hearing a spanish guitar in
the distance
I felt each string of haunting play
somewhere in my being
the hurt in me so intense
I gasped
I knew one day he would
leave
I felt a wet drop on my hand
rain
no a tear only one
there won't be 
anymore
The breeze arose
picked up the blossom
it's movement fusing with 
the pulse of the music and
the quivering of my heart
all coalescing 
in one cluster
spinning towards 
oblivion
It isn't like I didn't know
the music would end
the blossom would disappear
the tear would dry up
and all would be gone
forever gone
like all moments that don't last
hold fast
hurting heart
hold fast.
