---
title: "The Best of Love"
writer: "ilenia"
slug: "the-best-of-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

Having reached the summit of love
how then can one settle for less.
If you start with pure gold then
it's hard to continue with brass.
Why does passion have to fade
I don't believe it should, if one
becomes jaded, something is
misunderstood. If there is love
then it should thrive, unless one
stops contributing, for it takes
some work to keep it alive, and
both have to be willing. Why do 
we invest years in each other yet
cheat ourselves out of the zest
being willing to settle for much
less, when there is nothing but
our own lethargy to keep us 
from having the best. If I am
the recipient of the pearl of love
I will respond with the diamond of love.
