O Sainted hallow depths of night
In which you draw near in my sight

You hover over me transuding
While I am eagerly exuding
We dissolve into each other
amalgamating into the beat of one heart


Re -emerging in new form
My fruit is sweet and succulent
While yours is ripe and opulent
Our juices flow like vintage wine
Benificiently blessed by the divine

There is no degree of separation
We function in complete combination
Oh what Joy what elation
Our bodies entwined in new creation

We are in heavenly harmony
With you forever inside of me.
