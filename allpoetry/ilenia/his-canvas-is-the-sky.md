title: "His Canvas Is The Sky"

His canvas is the sky
On which He paints His
Hello and good-bye
At daybreak His Sun of 
Golden hues, says
"Good morning" then His 
Skies turn to lapis lazuli
So we can enjoy and play
Among His colors of day
When daylight fades 
The Sun recedes, He
paints violet hues
Turning to indigo
As He says "good-night"
The darkness descends
Upon all He attends
As our thoughts take flight
God points us towards
The black-hole of sleep
Til again we awake to keep
Our rendevous with Genesis
