---
title: "Lena's Escape"
writer: "ilenia"

source: "AllPoetry"

Lena curled up into herself. She became 
a circle with no beginning and no ending.
This was her escape, how she lost herself. 
This is how she survived all the cruel abuse 
and wicked things, that seemed to be her lot 
in life.Then she would sing her song.

There is a place
where I can go
it's warm like the sun
and white as snow
this is a place inside 
of me, only God 
can touch
Where he can tell me 
He loves me
very much
The world outside
is rife with harm
My world inside
is safe and warm
I can take the pain
abuse and trials
When I know 
inside of me 
God smiles.
