If I could find the words to say
How rhythm makes me feel this way

I'd have the world at my feet
For music and I blend so sweet

But no poetic rhyme or psalmody
Could capture this unique sinuosity

That produces movements of rotations
causing the most incredible sensations

As I mate with musical fascination  
in this dance fueled by syncopation

We move together in occillating oneness
Achieving a perfect degree of doneness.
