Surrounded by midnight
I was arrested by your radiance
Like a star you impaled me with your light
You shone with flashing brilliance

You awakened in me a symphony
I could feel your drumbeat
Like a Cello you stroked me
A sonata that when complete

Took me to the seventh kingdom
Where the Luminairies are aligned
With all the Cosmos  in unison
and in me your light was enshrined.
