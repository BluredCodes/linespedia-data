title: "Mutant Symphony"
writer: "ilenia"

A nocturnal affair cello collaborating
with dark deepness
violin in  higher illumination
both seeking oneness
stars cooperating in  profusion
casting loved  lost in illusion
upon the moons dark side
until the suns soft rays can redeem it
mystical moments of madness ensue
as the two seek each other in wrong times 
and places one in the day one in the night
all that ensues many meteoric faces
violin decides to seek the night
cello decides to seek the day
both in their madcap flight
lose each other along the way
passing through Orions gate
the star crossed lovers meet their fate
as coming from the outer ring
a mutant symphony without strings.
