---
title: "Lost in your Depths"
writer: "ilenia"
slug: "lost-in-your-depths"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

My love is only a reflection of what
I see in your eyes, that have me so
mesmerized. 

I am lost in the deepest 
depths of your eyes that became a
transitory tunnel into your soul which
has swallowed me whole. 

As I travel through the inner you, I delight 
in our oneness, alight in the glow of the fire
that ignites my desire.

 I blaze a trail through your emotions and sail 
on the sea of your devotions. I submerge into 
your night, and emerge out of your light

This wondrous intermingling of our
substance supplies all that is necessary
for me as I thrive in your passion and
survive because of your infinite love.
