title: "Utopian weaves"
writer: "ilenia"

You have woven yourself into the fabric of 
my life,and your threads compliment and 
enhance mine in such a way that I don't know
how my pattern ever did without them.  You have
blended your brilliant hues into my color scheme 
in some places complementary and in others 
vividly contrasting with, and even creating a new 
spectrum. You have fashioned a new design, 
entwining my silken threads with your woolen ones
and my satin threads with your cotton ones 
arranging them in a new and fascinationg format.
You have created shadows by aligning my  lights 
with your darks and vice versa, in new striations.
Your visionary concepts have invented a new
utopia in which we can enclose ourselves in the
cocoon of contentment having secured ourselves
from any loose threads.
