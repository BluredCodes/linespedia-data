title: "One More Bill"

The mood is cool, the meaning misting
     Wrapped in notes of scattered blues and muscles aching satisfaction
The mood is calm, though undercurrents shifting
     A heart of steadiness that subtly threatens sudden still, inaction
The mood is shaken, the man returned
     Yesterday’s ethics obsolete yet binding, haunting
The mood is silent, shift in music spurned
     A restless relaxation – something hiding, taunting
The mood is fog, and fraught with pain
     A man who seems to crumble yet I dare not feel for?
               He will only pull me down more(?)
     Late hour thoughts – no gain.Do not recall the exact circumstances that prompted this one - but 'Bill' is a person.
