Caffeine Hover

Caffeine catch me cause to hover
Inches just above the covers
Static tire and flat emotion
Naught to focus my devotion
Broken lines, unfinished verses
Hint of Muse who soon disperses
Wasted surf of fiscal waters
Spend thrift shoals of sexy daughters
Satisfaction intermittent
Dreaming dreams so inconsistent
Till I can’t remember when
Restless overtook and then

Blur into an overcast
Half the ‘morrow drifted past
Scattered garments ‘round the floor
Glass and flatware still ignored
Dog-eared tomes and texts unfinished
Motivation slack diminished
Ache and stiff and pound the head
Enticements empty back to bed
Lest I sink back in malaise
Just to overcome the daze
Crack another White and Scarlet
Satiate the Caffeine Harlot

Tracey K. Scott
July 4, 2001
