Monday mornin’ magic missin’,
All your achin’ moanin’ pissin’
Bitchin’ ‘bout the way life
Left you dullin’ on a knife
Lucky live without the strife,
And overstress and over guess
I guess that goes where great expected
Not in the scene, not self affected
Long hand pages
Gonna show you what your age is
Cause I ramble, and I gamble
That the words that I assemble
Form a sensible preamble
Some remark that you resemble
And I’m stuck again, put down the pen
I take my own advice and then
Know what to do, know what my role is
Stand by and listen if she bares her soul, this
Perspective is revealing, 
Of the hardships we’re concealing
Doesn’t matter, isn’t major, 
Minor league and little danger
That the tan lines will show
Might be Giants, you don’t know
Sans the supermarket special
It can really leave me low
Stretch the muscles ‘fore they tighten
Stare and blink and squint and brighten
Cause I’m waterless and drownin’
‘Nother round of drinks we’re downin’
Down in City Place, a friendly face
A screening and its dark embrace
She didn’t see it coming
Cause she wasn’t really looking
And she knew it wasn’t likely
Gotta block and take it lightly
And the odds she figured never
Gave a second lease on ever
She’s a part time muse
Fuckin’ doctors won’t abuse
Years of learnin’, corporate earnin’
Gave her something she could use
Through the early mornin’ haze,
False emotional malaise
That she knew it and she did it
Still I’m standin’ here amazed
But the movie site’s distractin’
My computer’s hyperactin’
So we jump to the afternoon
Late arrivin’ all too soon.A stream of conciousness prompted by a long phone conversation with a friend
