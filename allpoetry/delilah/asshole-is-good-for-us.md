This one that is called asshole,
needs no instructions nor commands.
It comes whenever it needs be.
Just part of human nature.

This one that is called asshole,
a person who has placed himself(?),
before our heart felt writes,
to laugh and lame our beauty.

He clearly has a point of this deed,
a right with the freedom to say
what is least on his very mind.
Do take whenever comes from him.

Do not take it personally,
an asshole is good for us,
that aides that our stools 
may not remain with our systems.
