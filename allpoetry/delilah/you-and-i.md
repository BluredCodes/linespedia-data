---
title: "~You And I~"
writer: "Delilah"
slug: "you-and-i"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/45237--You-And-I--by-Delilah"
---

you

are my

fortress,

for i am least

nothing, a wreck.

yet i stalk no fear

for through me,

then mighty 

is your 

fame. 



i am 

the land,

and you are

the deep sea that

ever surrounds these 

shores, for without 

the faith, to the

bottom will 

i slowly 

erode.


i am

but an

instrument,

and you are

the sole music

from the heavenly realms 

that ever strikes out

the olds of olds,

sweetly sways

from on

high.
