---
title: "Tree of Love"
writer: "Delilah"
slug: "tree-of-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

My love grows and blooms
momentously upon this tree.
Growing out the many 
precious moments of branches,
streaming out from within,
from stems of tender touches.
Once my small branches of love,
reached out to receive the same.
On branches hang my loving moments,
all precious, and adorned.
Branches of many stem out,
all stemmed from 
the love of all loves, 
my Lord, Jesus.
My tree of love,
I sit and savour 
this time, to recall
the many precious 
memories that grow
continuously upon 
this tree with lights
with musical a tuning,
to bestow, and relenquish
the momemts of love,
upon this tree,
where my love 
continues to grow.
