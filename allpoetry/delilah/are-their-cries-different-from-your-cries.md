---
title: "Are Their Cries Different From Your Cries"
writer: "Delilah"

So much can be said.
So much can be poured,
words black on white,
hearts white on black,
but no heart is any 
different from another.

So much can be painted
and so much be bled
onto the cloth as to
where the heart lays moaning.
Should one have to beg
to plea for a chance 
to be heard, a heart's cry.

Give some heart a chance
they have just as much to say,
and why do they have to cry
for a chance to be held gently,
to be held in your acceptance,
and why are their cries 
different from your cries?
