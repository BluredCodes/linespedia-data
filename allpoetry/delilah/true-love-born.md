---
title: "True Love Born"
writer: "Delilah"

From the star's twinkle touch,
to the moon's quarter halo,
shining forth a morning glory,
onward on to memories
to keep in the heart
forever, the love 
born many centuries ago.
Came from heaven's gates,
to the stable, cattle's refuge,
sheeps' watched by the shephards,
who first heard the news of the king 
soon to be born tonight, in a manger.
Angels sing on high the realm of king
born unto us this day,
on Christmas day.
For true love is born unto us 
this day on Christmas day.
A boy of small stature,
came a stumbling along,
to witness the babe 
laying in a manger.
Knowing the baby 
was indeed important,
seeing the Kings of east,
their rich frankicense and mirth, 
they presented to the baby,
kneeling and praying 
to the holy baby.
What can he give to 
this baby, when he had nothing
to give at all.
He looked down at his drum
his mama gave to him to play,
then, thought the boy to himself,
'i shall play my drum for him',
he strolled in the stable,
and beat his sticks 
to the skin of his drum.
'Come they told me,' started singing
to the beat of his drum.
A poor boy nothing to give 
to the king born this day,
on Christmas day,
now gave his best, 
as the baby king Jesus
smiled at his attempt.
A love born this day,
on Christmas day.
