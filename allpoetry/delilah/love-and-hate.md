title: "Love and Hate"
writer: "Delilah"

I do not feel sorry for those that are different from the Caucasians.I do not even pity their miserable lives caused by differentiation.I do not feel remorse for those beaten and spat on.I am not moved by their good intentions of life.I do not feel bad at all for them.I am prejudice.I do not feel sorry for those that take me, a poison to their lips.I do not even pity them as they stagger away, out of their minds.I do not even feel for those beaten and killed, because of me.I am not moved by the broken homes and the families affected.I keep on hurting and killing.I am alcoholism.I do not feel sorry for the many that have grown hungrier each day.I do not care if even one dies from hunger or love.I watch for the forlorn and the hopeless to take.I do not care for any one at all.I go everywhere.I am death.I do not feel for those falling from their high empires.I crawl into the veins of those that are young.I show the great wealth, causing greed.I cause them to see only wealth.I do not pity them.I am greed.I do not feel pity for souls that die without God's so-called grace.I sent the dark fallen angels to kill and to haunt and to inflict.I roar like the lion but we cannot touch God's very own.I do not care for those that suffer in agony.I come to kill and running out of time.I am Satan.TAKE HEART DO NOT LOSE HOPEGOD IS AT HANDI feel greatly for those that are treated unfairly for being unique.I feel for those living in misery, beaten and spat upon.I feel for those that are taunted daily.I consider their goodness for ME.I feel with them.I AM GOD.I feel considerable campassion for those that are trapped in alcoholism.I care about them as they stagger around out of their minds.I care deeply for those beaten and murdered by alcohol.I feel deeply for the broken homes and the families.I can take away the desire to drink alcohol.I AM YOUR GOD.I feel for the hungry, try not to understand my reason of inaction for them.I feel for those that lost their loved ones dying from famine and love.I have grace and mercy for everyone who comes to me.I love everyone so very dearly.I am everywhere, look for me.I AM YOUR ALMIGHTY GOD.I feel for those that put material goods first in their lives.I do not intervene nor meddle, they too have a choice.I will call for the ones I choose for my mission.I will only deliver them if they should call.I care and love them too.I AM YOUR GOD.I have made it my desire for everyone to come to know my love and grace.I will forgive those that ask for forgiveness of their sins.I have sent my Only Begotten Son Jesus for all.For I do love and feel for you all very much.Come to me all of you sinners, come soon.I Am YOUR ALMIGHTLY GOD...LOVE YOUR HEAVENLY FATHER
