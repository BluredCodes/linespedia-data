title: "Dream Lover"
writer: "Delilah"

Came to me by my bedwith your kill-me eyes.My gazes at your perfect bodyfearing that you might vanish.My heart pounded at your presence,with butterflies in my stomach.You only come to me by nightwith your charming features,ones that I could die for.For a deep passionate love.Loving hearts delight for now All too good to be real.thought you came to offer me you,as you held out your arms.I, reaching out just the same.My heart beat sky rocketed to the moon,and back, as I etched toward you.Yet, I was stunned to find someone in your arms.My heart was in pieces,your charms you came withto have a shot at my heart.to hold another in your arms,instead of measly ol' me.Though she was beautiful.It came to me that same night,this is my dream and I will dream the way I want.I grabbed her from you, and shoved her away,and told you 'I yours, baby.'Hold me, love me,just for tonight.
