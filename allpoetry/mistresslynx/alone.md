---
title: "Alone"

I walk this world alone,

So many times betrayed.

I'm caught up in a whirlpool,

Living every day afraid.

What's going on around me?

Could it be wrong, or right?

Am I weird just because

I don't stay out all night?

What is so different,

That everyone seems to see?

Is it just because I choose,

To just let me, be me?

Things just don't seem fair,

But there's nothing much I can do.

I guess I'll just stand tall

And bravely endure it through.

Life continues on,

Forever, day to day,

But I'll dance to my own drum,

And walk in my own way.
