The Confusion...Where is here? And why?What have I done?The Loss...I am alone, separated,Lost and forsaken.The Memories...Once free from restraint,Running, proud and strong.The Love...I was wanted, neededI had a family.The Regret...Things I cannot do now,Things I wanted.The Anger...Why must it be me?Why is this good-bye?The Betrayal...Didn't you truely love me?Why was I abandoned?The Fear...I know what is comingThe chute, the man...The End...If you have the stomach for it, please feel free to visit the Internet site that was part of my inspiration for this poem. http://www.equus.org/  Be forewarned, it's not pretty...
