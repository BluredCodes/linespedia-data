---
title: "Your Gentle Caress"
writer: "DreamingAngel"
slug: "your-gentle-caress"
source: "AllPoetry"

I see you loving me as I look into your eyes

The feel of your warm touch kindles a flame deep inside

Your fingers tracing around my face
Brings a chill down my spine

And when you kiss the tears from off my face
All sadness fades away

Your gentle lips caress mine
and my doubts and fears are erased for all time

My life is consumed by your love
and I thank you every day

I miss you when you are gone
And I hold you in my heart

My stomach drops to the floor
When you flash that handsome smile

Every litte thing you do
Brings a smile upon my face

I know without you I'd be nothing
Because you fill my life with joy

Ive never felt this way before
and I want you for all time
