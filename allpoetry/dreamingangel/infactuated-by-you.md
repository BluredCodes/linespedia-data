Infactuated By You

I don’t know whats happening
I don’t know what to do
But I know one thing for sure
I was infatuated by you

But now, it’s not so clear
How you feel about me
My heart was closed for good
But I was sure you had the key

One minute,
For me you seemed to care
Then the next minute
You act as if I’m not even there

I wish you could let me know
What your feeling inside
You don’t have to be afraid
Your feelings, you don’t have to hide

I know I’ve been confused lately
To you, I don’t know what to say
Sometimes, I don’t feel anything
But other times, I don’t want to take my heart away

Your eyes seem to say
When you look at me
That you feel the same
That together, we need to be

Your feelings are locked up inside
You don’t say what you feel
You may be confused too
You don’t know if what you feel is real

This pain in my heart
To describe it, I can’t even begin
I even cry for you
But my other feelings, I will keep within
