---
title: "My Feelings For You"
writer: "DreamingAngel"
slug: "my-feelings-for-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

Every hour, Every second of everyday
I love you so much, 
But dont know the words to say

I lay in bed thinking of you,
Thinking tomarrow I'll cry for you again

I try so hard not to lay awake and cry
But I know your no longer by my side

I just would like to hold you close
So I can feel your heart with mine

My friends tell me it's just a crush
But how can it be when you are my dreams when I'm asleep
And the memories I will always keep

I wish I could say I hate you
For the times you made me weep

But I remember your loving touch
And when our hands would interwind
And my hatred then becomes weak

These feelings I feel of you and me
Are coming from my heart
I hope one day you'll see
That you and me were meant to be
