title: "The boy is You"
writer: "DreamingAngel"

When I first met him
My heart was going to pop
My eyes were frozen upon his handsome face
My life was changed
But...
Love isn't that easy
Joy became sadness, smiles became tears
I can't tell him I love him
His girlfriends right there

The hardest part is waiting
But I can wait forever
Because...
The boy is you
