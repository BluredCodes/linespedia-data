---
title: "Alone"
writer: "DreamingAngel"

Late at night
When I should be asleep
Into my heart, he quietly creeps

I close my eyes
and reminisce 
Of the times he held me tight
Wishing that one day
He might come back in sight

I turn on the radio 
To ease my pain
But all I can hear
Is him calling my name

The loving arms that used to be
Are now just a memory
Inside of me

I look out the window
Into the bright stary sky
But all I can see
Is the twinkle in his eye

A tear runs down my cheek
And I suddenly become to feel weak

My pillow smells
Of his sweet cologne
And now I finally realize
That this time...
Im alone.
