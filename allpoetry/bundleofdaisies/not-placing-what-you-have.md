A day in the life of your neighbor
    can you describe it all in a single word
upon your lips, my hands tremble.
Speaking of a higher power,
    a power so strong,
a country it may lift.

A day in the life of your mother
sweet and dainty she goes on
with her day, which has no end.
    When will she break through
will she break through?

     A day in the life of your husband,
not knowing where you will end up.
Choose a day when you will decide
        what needs to be done.

A day in the life of your daughter
    Whispers soft songs in her ears
as she lays sweetly upon your chest as
her heart beats
       and beats

A day in the life of a stranger
   Who will you be today
but a bum and nobody to another
   stranger walking by
will you say hello to someone?
will you stop for anyone?

A day in the life of yourself
      not placing what you have
unempathetic to the world
your surroundings. Call out
    save what is precious
