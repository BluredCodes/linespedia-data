One, two,
I feel blue,
Cough-cough, oh-no the flu,
Whoever knew?
That's my que,
Not you too!
It's time to review.

Three, four,
Second World War,
Responsible for,
the basketball score,
Daddy's big snore,
the building supply store,
Time for some more!

Five six,
How many licks?
how he depicts,
grandma's famous brownie mix,
your sons bag of tricks,
your familys conflicts,
how do you get your kicks?Haha, is it cute? I just wanted to let people understand that rhyming can be overdone! Can we all say Dr. Suess?!
