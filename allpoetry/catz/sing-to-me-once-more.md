title: "sing to me once more"

sing to me once more

you used to sing me love songs
as we lay awake each night
our bed still warm and wrinkled
from our ecstasy
holding each other close

silly little love songs
you’d compose as you sang
words only I would ever hear
the music echoed through 
the hallways of our hearts

i’d listen to every word
each note a beat of our hearts
and fall asleep in your arms
knowing you’d be there 
when i’d awaken

but now my sleep is broken
by your soundless song
for your heart beats no more
except for another
and still i listen

i need to hear your song
sung for my ears alone
the words so soft and sweet
wake me from this dream and 
sing to me once more
