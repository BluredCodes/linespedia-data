sometimes…

sometimes…when everything is quiet
and there seems not a soul around
and I’m missing you with all my heart
there comes into my head
little whispers of what was
almost nothing, just barely audible sounds
like a light rain softly falling
gently speaking as it touches the ground
‘I’m here’ it seems to say
as if it’s only me to whom it speaks

sometimes... when I’m all alone
with nothing much to do
I try to think of something
anything …but you
you broke my heart
before we said goodbye
yet  you linger in my mind
even though you’re gone
you’re still here with me
you just wont leave

sometimes...  in the darkness
I listen for your voice
wait patiently for your touch
and if I lie still long enough
not moving except to breathe
you come to me
quietly whispering my name
your lips gently pressing mine
then I stop breathing
for fear you’ll go away

sometimes... I wonder
if I’m still real
always listening, waiting
breathing, not breathing,
hoping, almost giving up
you’re gone from me forever
I know that, but
I can’t help but believe
you’ll creep softly back to me

sometimes...
