you and me

i heard your call and hesitated...
should i answer? no... wait...yes
i almost didn't submit to your beckoning
then i knew i had to
i knew, it was you and me
strangers, reaching across the abyss
reaching for each other
you and me, our souls reuniting
our hearts finding each other.

we touched, we spoke, we lingered
you and me, together at last
together again, together for the first time
together for an eternity, for always
so long we've waited, not knowing
only existing, waiting, drifting
you and me, so close, so distant

we may have passed in the night
many times, not realizing our nearness
only knowing there must be something more
not just you, not just me
in our meager lonely existence
there had to be more
then a touch across the nothingness
brought us together
you and me

by Dee Garner
July 1999This was written for a most special person in my life ...it's how we found each other, how our love began, how our lives became forever entwined.
