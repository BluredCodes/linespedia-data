OOPS! ANOTHER YEAR

By Dee Garner
November 20, 2000

When I was young and full of life
And time went by so slow 
I thought life would never end
That forever I would go

Then at twenty-nine I thought
Thirty is way too old
So I'll just remain here for now
If I may be so bold

Then another decade slipped by
The time is going faster
How can that be I thought
Am I no longer my own master

There's still twenty-four hours in a day
It still takes three hundred sixty-five
To make another year but hey..wait
I'm way over the hill and still alive

I don't feel that much older
Look at the calendar, it can't be right
My birthday's here already?
Oops! Another year took flight
