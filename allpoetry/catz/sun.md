SUN

The morning sun rises slowly, then
Glaringly dares the start of a new day
It’s heat already building, waves of heat
Caressing the trees, stabbing with each ray

This dastardly sun, two faced, vicious
Bringing welcome warmth, then burning
Scorching all in its path
Leaving pleading souls, thirsty, yearning

Ahh! A breeze, momentary relief from Hell
Rustling the leaves ever so slightly
Cooling my brow, giving me hope
Touching my soul ever so lightly

The day wears on, will it ever end
Surely the evening is near
When the sun will move westward
Lower itself and disappear

My hate for it grows, detested
And just when it seems never to cease
It dips, barely peeking above the horizon
A portrait of beauty, blues, pinks, peace
