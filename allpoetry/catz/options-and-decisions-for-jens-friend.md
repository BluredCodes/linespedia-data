options and decisions

each day we're faced
with options and decisions
which way to go, what to do?
some are tough ones, some
maybe not even important
decisions which we think
may not matter much at all

and some are the hardest 
we'll ever have to make
like choosing between 
losing a breast or dying
dying or living without
this special part of us
that has become so important

important?? really!? well
how important is it 
for our children to grow up
with a mother, how important
that our husband has a companion
how important is a breast?
does it have arms to hold them

does it have a heart of it's own 
to share lifes burdens
will it care if it isn't there
to share in your children's happiness
can it possibly replace 
the whole you for 
your man to come home to

is your child going to miss 
ALL of you, just so YOU
wont have to miss a breast
are  your parents going to mourn 
the loss of a daughter
so you wont have to feel
the loss of a breast

you have an option here
and a decision to make 
you need to do it soon
before it becomes too late
your family hopes you'll opt
for life, for them
they don't want to bury you

they don't want to mourn 
the loss of a whole human being
because you don't want to mourn 
the loss of a breast
so please rethink this matter
look within your heart
there's more to life than perfection

there's yet another option
a decision you could make
breast implants are all the rage
you could have the size and shape
you want or nothing
a flat chest that's alive
is better than a mother buried

we don't want to write upon your marker

here lies a mother, a wife 
a daughter and a woman 
with two breasts because
she chose them over all elseBreast cancer is indeed a formidable experience.  But hopefully the decision of life will be her option... she must make that decision soon.
