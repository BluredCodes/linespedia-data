beginnings 

a seed is planted 
no nurturing 
growing 
bent by the wind 
suffering 
beyond all hope 
of normality 

torture untold 
wasting away 
knawing pain 
rotting 
knowing not 
of love 

all beauty 
marred by hate 
leaving scars 
ugly with 
indifference 
shamed into 
hiding 
from the light 

years 
of hopelessness 
seeking 
until 
at last 
hope fulfilled 
suffering 
ended 


as the shiney blade 
marks 

.....the 
beginning
