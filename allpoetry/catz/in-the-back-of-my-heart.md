title: "in the back of my heart"
writer: "catz"

in the back of my heart

tomorrow i’ll wed another
who’s love i try to share
he’s gentle and he loves me
i know he’ll always care

but somewhere deep inside me
in the shadows of my heart
you shall linger on forever
where I know we’ll never part

what is this tie that binds us
why can’t we be set free
i just can’t seem to let you go
in the back of my heart you’ll always be

we dreamed the dreams of lovers
we laughed, and cried the tears
knowing it could never be
except in dreams beyond our fears

our love, beyond all hope now
for another holds you tight
tomorrow i try to cut the tie
but i’ll treasure you tonight

and someday in another life
our dreams wont fall apart
so i’ll hold you close forever
safe in the back of my heart
