daydreams

clouds drifting overhead
relaxing ‘neath the trees
mind drifting to another place
another time another day

sitting here leaning against a log
watching a lizard
hearing, not listening
tuning out the world

the song of a bird 
drifts into my thoughts
enhancing my reverie
my daydreams

i come here often just to feel
to shut out the rest of the world
and forget everything else
‘cept whatever comes into my head

letting me drift from hither and yon
to know only what I want to know
like hearing the croak of a frog
from the pond just over there

thoughts of you 
slip into my mind
thoughts so precious
holding hands, whispering

our lips barely touching
then exploding into passionate kiss
ahh, the daydreams I dream
here ‘neath the trees

with clouds drifting overhead
the trees softly rustling their leaves
as the breeze shifts and wakes me
from my daydreams


Dee Garner
