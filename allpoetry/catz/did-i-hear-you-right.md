Did I Hear you Right?

‘Bye, I love you’ .....you caught me off guard
And then you were gone
Only silence on the other end
Did I hear you right?

My heart stopped ...did you really say that?
How could any other words sound like ‘I love you’
How could I not have replied quickly enough
‘I love you, too’, my heart spoke to a silent phone

I can hardly wait to see you now
To hear those words in person
That is if you really said them
Did I hear you right?

By Dee Garner, September 13, 2000
