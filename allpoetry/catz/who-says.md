Who Says

So.....what if the body creaks and groans
Who's to say it's not a new song
That the body sings, maybe a little off key
Who says it's not, who says it's wrong

What if we walk kinda stiff and slow
Who's to say it's not a new dance
Better than a Tango or the Bunny Hop
Who says it's not, we'll take a chance

And if we forget what we were about to say
The words are somewhere within
Our mind just decided to wait awhile
Who says it can't rest and begin again

For we've lived long enough to deserve a break
To travel the world if that's what we choose
Or sit by the river, teach the worms how to swim
Who says we can't, if we listen we lose

By Dee Garner
(C)December 25, 2000This was written for my friend Wanda, who at the time was feeling really down because of her health and is often in a lot of pain.  We were talking about our age (she's my age) and all the little dings in her health and how it seemed to effect her on a daily basis.  She's a fighter, though and tries to live life to the fullest....
