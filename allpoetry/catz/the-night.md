The Night

Between the nightfall and the light of day
When all the earth is still
As I lay beneath the covers
Crying, against my will

The night is long, the tears flow
And I feel the cold empty space
Where once we held each other
I can still feel your embrace

Throughout the night until the dawn
With all the world asleep
Your love once filled my dreams
Now I lie awake and weep

Is this my fate, to be alone
Forever wanting you
Or is there another love for me
A love that will be true

To fill this void within my nights
And bring a new tomorrow
I pray each hour for one to love
To end these nights of sorrow
