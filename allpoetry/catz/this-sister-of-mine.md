This Sister Of Mine

(Originally titled 'Darlene')

I often wonder how she works
what makes her tick 
so smoothly, on the surface anyway
She always seems to do just right,
this sister of mine
A creative genius, one might say

If  God needed a helper 
He'd call on her
and ask,  'What should I do now?'
She'd reply, 'Why this of course'
'Just watch me'
'Do it this way, I'll show you how'

And when I need a confidante
a friend to see me through
to cheer me up, to hear me out
I call on you, sister of mine
You often give me hell
but you’re my friend without a doubt

Dee Garner
