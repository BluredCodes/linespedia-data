Lost

As I look into your eyes 
I see that vacant stare of death
Hiding the spirit within
A man still alive but dead

From too many needles, too many  tokes
Too many pills and nights alone
With nothing but darkness 
And terrible visions filling your head

You have such a kind heart
But nothing to give to those who love you
Not to your children, not to your mother
Except for your promises, broken

We love you in spite of yourself
But hope is fading, you seem so lost
We tried but failed and now 
Perhaps you’ll live when God has spoken

By Dee Garner, 
December 21, 2000
