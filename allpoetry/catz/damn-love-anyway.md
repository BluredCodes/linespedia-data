Damn Love Anyway 

It’s supposed to be so wonderful 
So full of joy and hope 
Or so everyone says 
But not me. Nope 

What about the heartaches 
The lonely nights, the pain 
Forgotten dreams, the lies and flowing tears 
Drowning me, driving me insane 

Do you ever sit alone and weep 
Hastily wiping the tears away 
Maybe, but I doubt it, my dear 
So…Damn love, anyway 

By Dee Garner
