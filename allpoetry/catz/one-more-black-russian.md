one more black russian

I walk through the door, 
home at last ....
tired, so tired
my head aches, my feet hurt
....sitting right there
just a few steps away 
another five minutes
oblivious to the pain
I can call it a day 
it’s so far to the bed
I bet my feet only carry me 
to the little wooden cabinet
on the other side of the room
the tall clear bottle 
it’s contents nearly gone
the dark one beside it
can’t see how much is left
no matter.. it only takes one 
....well, maybe two tonight
pain, it wont last long
ahhh…your warm caress 
surrounding me
closing in on my body
soothing away the aches
i'm almost there
your task nearly done
damn, not quite, don't stop
just one more black russian 
.... no more ....

Dee Garner
November 3, 2001Best pain killer I know of!
