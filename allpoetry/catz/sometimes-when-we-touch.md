sometimes when we touch

we pass in the hallway
between your office and mine
and as your arm brushes against me
i tingle with longing

i want it to be more than a touch
i want to be engulfed
within your embrace, to feel
the warmth of your skin against mine

just co-workers, that’s all we are
yet in my mind you’re more
you’re my hero, my lover,
my confidant, my everything

your eyes meet mine across the room
i quickly glance away
on with work, no time to spare
but something seems different 

or is it my imagination
can you see the way I feel
can you be feeling this way too
sometimes when we touchThis isn't about me .... but a friend of mine is going through this, so just thought I'd write about it
