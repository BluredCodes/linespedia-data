You’re Gone

You’re gone and all the flowers have died
The birds no longer sing their songs
The crickets are silent
The wind no longer rustles the leaves
What used to be the sun has become an ember
The moon has left forever
My world is dark and sad

Yet I cling to the hope of a brighter tomorrow
When once again the flowers bloom
And the songs of birds fill the air
As the trees whisper their secrets
The wind carries their words for all to hear
When once again the sun lights the sky
Because you’ve come back to me

Dee Garner
October 26, 2000
