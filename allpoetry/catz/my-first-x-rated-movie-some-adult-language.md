My First X Rated Movie

when I look around me
at all the porno flicks
with naked boobs and buttocks
and big hard horny dicks

I can’t help but think back
to the first X rated movie I saw
oh so many years ago 
‘twas unclothed bosoms in the raw

it was at the local drive-in
that me and my boyfriend went
thinking we would get a thrill
for the money we had spent

well it took forever for us to see
what we’d been waiting for
but sort of a big letdown
we’d really expected more

just two young lovers
who ended up on the beach
and as they lay upon the sand
‘neath her blouse he reached

as she lay there all expectant
waiting for his advance
he pulled down her peasant blouse
ahh, we thought, at last, romance

her young breasts exposed
was quite a thrill that night
as her soft skin shimmered
in the pale glow of moonlight

oh, wow, we thought as we watched
this amorous dirty movie show
now we’ll get to see some stuff
about which we didn’t know

we waited motionless and breathless
and held hands real tight
as he kissed her lovely silken neck
oh my gosh, we gasped, what a night

well, that was the end of that
the movie faded to the morn
they didn’t even have sex, we said
so much for early porn

I can’t recall the name of it
nor who starred in the show
or why it was even rated X
have no idea, just don’t know

for today as we watch on our tv
the daytime talk shows and soaps
we see more flesh and intimacy
hear filthy language, dirty jokes

the x rated movies have become porn
can be seen by young and old
‘cuz movie makers don’t seem to care
they just go for the gold

so now I wish that I had seen
the worth of that movie long ago
instead of being faced today
with sleaze and lust …I’d rather go

leaving something to the imagination
I wish once more I could see
the innocence of love on screen
