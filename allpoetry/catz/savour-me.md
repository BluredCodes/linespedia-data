title: "savour me......"
writer: "catz"

you watch me as I walk across the room
your eyes following my every move
i glance your way, see that little twinkle in your eyes
i know that look, that smile
savour me…….

hold me in your eyes, long for me
caress me with your smile,
i feel your touch from across the room
gentle, pulling me, tugging at my heart
savour me…..

let me hear your song, the one sung just for me
coax me with your gentle words
spoken silently, for my heart only
share for just a moment, let me feel your love
savour me……

come for me, lead the way
to our own private world
stroke me, touch me, love me slowly
sensuously, as I rise to you
savour me……

......then devour me completely
