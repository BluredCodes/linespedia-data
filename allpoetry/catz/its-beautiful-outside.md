it's beautiful outside

sitting here 
all alone
only these walls around me
open window
warmth streaming in
chills me to the bone
can't feel
can't see
nothing but coldness
in this place i'm in
this shell i've built
surrounds me

wake up 
says a voice from within
get out of this prison
this place
you've locked yourself into
it's beautiful outside
there's a world
there's love waitng
things to do
places to go
open your closed heart
stand in the sunlight
 
i try to quiet the voice
better to weep here
no one to see
no one to know
my sorrow 
my broken heart
shall remain a secret
forever more
perhaps i'll go now
pretend all is well
out of this cold place
