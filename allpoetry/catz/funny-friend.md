Funny Friend

I have this funny friend
Who fills my mind with fuzz
I don't really know quite why
But maybe it's because
She laughs at my predicaments
And even laughs at her own
We have our serious moments of course
And we know we're not alone
We laugh and cry with each other
We seldom miss a chance 
To exchange our latest escapades
Our misdeeds and romance
I hope she never disappears 
As net friends sometimes do
For I'd never find such a friend
As I have found in you

By Dee Garner 
for my friend Lee
October 31, 2000Lee is a special net friend, we have been in touch with each other for about 5 years now, she's a kick and we have some wonderful times on chat.  I hope we have a chance to meet in person someday soon.
