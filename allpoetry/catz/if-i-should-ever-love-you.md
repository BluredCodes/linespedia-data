title: "If I Should Ever Love You"

If I should ever love you
And please don’t think I do
But if I did, would you love me
And be forever true

Would you hold me close and tell me
That you’ll always be my love
And swear it by the angels
And all the stars above.

When we dance to the song of life
If all our dreams come true
Would we always share our happiness
Our sorrows and troubles, too

Would I cherish each and every moment
The way that I do now
The time with you so sweet
I sometimes wonder how

In all the world you’re most dear
The one I feel so close
The one who makes my life worthwhile
The one who loves me most

So if I should ever love you
And really know for sure
That it’s you who loves me
With a love that’s true and pure

Could we go on forever
Through life’s ups and downs
Treasuring each and every moment
The smiles and the frowns

Would I always want to see the look
Of happiness on your face
When we see each other
And share a warm embrace

Would your touch always excite me
And make me want you more
A kiss, a look, a moment alone
With the one that I adore

I must not really love you
For if I did, you know
I’d never ask these questions
I’d know which way to go

The days go by, the nights are long
Whenever you’re not around
But that doesn’t mean I love you
Too many fears abound

But maybe you could love me
Maybe hold me dear
The way that I could love you
And always want you near

Let’s just pretend that we’re in love
And see how it makes us feel
Just try it on for size
It’s really no big deal 

We’ll share our lives just for awhile
And see what happens next
Well, maybe we could do that
It wont be too complex

We’ll act as though we love each other
We’ll do all the common things
We wont know for sure of course
But we can see what our ‘love’ brings

And if someday we should  fall apart
As lovers often do
Would you say let’s try once more
After all that we’d been through

For if I should ever love you
And please don’t think I do
Do you think that you could love me
As much as I love you

By Dee Garner
