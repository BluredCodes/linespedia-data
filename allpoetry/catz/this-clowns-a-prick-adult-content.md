The biggest prick in town 
I thought, was a guy that I had met 
But now that I've seen all of you 
Hey... at last, him I can forget 

I thought I'd seen it all you see
You each think you have the biggest dick 
And they are quite large...But 
That still makes you ONE BIG PRICK!

Or could this be in jest perchance?  
Am I being plain unjust?
To judge you by appearance alone?
