your touch

your touch... 
sensuous
slowly
gently 
caressing every inch of me 
finding hidden places
a mind of its own

your touch....
gently demanding my response
softly skimming my body 
pausing
persuading
setting me on fire
burning
yearning

your touch.... 
your lips so warm and wonderful
feeding on me 
as if I'm made of the sweetest honey
your sustenance, your very life

your touch.... 
begging
coaxing
knowing 
one more moment 
and the world will disappear
leaving only 
...your touch
