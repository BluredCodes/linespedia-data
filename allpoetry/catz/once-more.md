once more

written with love for Jen, a lady whom we all love and pray for

once more i sit here writing
just some words upon a page
not making much sense yet
until my mind, my heart and soul engage

don't know yet if it's laughter
or sadness i'll create
i only know i must do it now
for tomorrow may be too late

once more i hear you singing
your voice so soft and sweet
promising me the world and more
when someday finally we shall meet

i hold that thought close and dear 
for i know there's a tomorrow
just hold my hand across the miles
so your strength i may borrow

once more i'll lean upon you
i know you really care
just give me one more chance 
to grasp whatever's there

my sons, my hopes, my dreams
all layed bare at your feet
i need you more than ever before
your love, your caring, oh so sweet

now i must ask ....

once more to see the light of day 
to see my children as they play 

once more to feel the rain 
upon my brow, washing away the pain 

once more is all I ask 
...NO !!
make that a million times, I wont go 

once more is never enough 
I must be strong, I must be tough 

once more you'll see me at my best 
'cuz after 'once more'... 
is all the rest

dee garner
january 8, 2002
just something for Jen ....I wish it could be more
