another bottle

time for another bottle, i see
the last one’s emptied and gone
and here I am without
never seems to last long enough

the last time this happened
i didn’t want to leave the house
too many prying eyes
seemed to watch my every move

when finally I dared to go
the shelves were filled with choices
too many for this old mind
hard to comprehend their effect

would that one make me feel better
make someone care more
or would I just sit around and wonder
why I even bothered

which one shall I choose
that dark one looks sorta dreary
this one has a fancy box
ahhh, there’s the one I want

this should make my dreams come true
one bottle does it all
i’ll feel good, conquer the world
no one can bring me down

take it home, pour, mix, shake a little
add some water, get a towel
ahhh, there ....perfection
soon a new woman i'll be

~  ~

no more gray hair for me
once again 
......i'm blonde
