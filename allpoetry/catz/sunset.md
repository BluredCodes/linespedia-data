Sunset

As the evening sun slowly sinks below the horizon
And another all too short day comes to an end
I sit and wonder as I watch
If there will be another tomorrow

The blazing sun meets the black outline of the far hills
It’s blinding light preceding the beauty to come
As the sky changes to pink and blue
Such beauty amid the sorrow

All the days of my life seem to blend into one
With only memories to fill my thoughts
I ask myself why, what for
I know the answer of course

So much love, so much happiness, so much joy 
It makes the sadness that slips into my heart
Just a little easier, less important
Almost no regrets, almost no remorse

I pray for just one more day on earth
Just one more moment with the ones I love
Before my days are done
But I can hear the far off voices call

I must use this night to relive my dreams
To say farewell to all I’ve known
If I can just do all this tonight
Is the morning so important after all

Dee Garner
