Special Friends


Across the miles and vastness of space
at a time when I needed a friend
And didn't even realize it, you knew
You told me stories, you made me smile
and finally made me realize
That I had a friend in you

When first we met after all that time
you treated me like a princess
You were my special friend
You helped me believe in myself
when I never thought I could
My ally to the end

I think you feel the same as me
this friendship and special love
I really hope you do
To bring each other laughter and joy
and maybe share some tears
As only true friends do

For my dear friend Roland Garner
By Dee Garner 
October 31, 2000Roland and I first met through email when he was trying to find family members.  That was back in early 1996.  We determined that we weren't related (it would have been on my husband's side) but we became good friends through many emails after that first one.  He's told me many stories about him and his life, some of them very humorous.  He lives in Maryland a few miles from where my son and his family live and when I went to visit them, we made a point of meeting in person.  We spent quite a lot of time together and I also met his wife who was in a nursing home.  They're both wonderful people but Roland has been a true and delightful friend.  

Update:  Roland passed away in November 2005 and I miss him a lot.  I spoke to him on the phone just a couple of days before he died... he was his usual rambunctous self, wanted me to break him out of the nursing home which his daughter had placed him
