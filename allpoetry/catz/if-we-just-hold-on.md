If We Just Hold On

We’ve put our dreams aside
All our plans on hold
Some say those dreams have died
They say our love’s grown cold

But if we just hold on
Our dreams will all come true
It has to be that way
You love me and I love you

Don’t let us lose our dreams
Don’t let them die away
If we just hold on
We’ll have it all someday

By Dee Garner,
