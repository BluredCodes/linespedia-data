crystal tears

tears fall from my eyes
crystalizing as they touch my cheek
i’ve become so cold, so unfeeling
too heartbroken, too numb to speak

i tried so hard to stay warm inside
to go on with my life once more
but you said goodbye, just like that
just left, walked out the door

now here I sit, so alone
convincing myself I don’t care
but my eyes keep crying crystal tears
shattering noiselessly as I stare

where do they keep coming from
surely I must be dry inside 
yet these tears keep falling, falling 
and here am I, no place to hide

someday when I can cry no more
and the tears lie as dust at my feet
when the coldness turns to warmth
perhaps again…I’ll find love so sweet

but for now it’s too much to ask
i’ve nothing warm to share
only tears to cry, crystal tears
shed from this heart so cold and bare
