Once In A Lifetime

Once in a lifetime
You find someone
Who captures your heart
Who brings life to your soul

Once in a Lifetime
You find someone
Who stands beside you
Who walks with you

Once in a lifetime 
You find someone
Who loves you for what you are
Not just what you could be

Once in a lifetime
You find someone
As I have found you
As you have found me

Dee Garner
December 1995
