wintersong…my tribute to granpappy 

neath the bluegray sky 
lies the poetry of life 

the song of a bird 
slow meandering of a caterpillar 
the croak of a frog 
a bent twig hanging, lifeless 

mornings’ foggy mist 
becomes a reverie 
of times gone by 
or a promise 
for the new day 
of things to come 
which lie in hiding 
waiting for the sun 

memories become 
works of art 
words, subtle or screaming 
days and nights run together 
forming what was 
what can’t be changed 
no matter how 
terrible or wonderful 

a childs tears of pain 
begging for the comfort 
which she has never known 
becoming the product 
of that abuse 
and all it’s consequences 
little could she know 
she was poetry in the making 

a flower, a mountain 
an old hound dog 
dust, rain, wind and snow 
the woodpile, aching back 
the aroma of dinner cooking 
or loves’ sweet perfume 

a young boy 
growing up in Oklahoma 
ice cream and love 
heartaches and soul searching 
night sticks and alfalfa 
milk and cookies 
spitin' and whittlin’ 
tarzan on the screen 
ma and pa 

drugs and lovers 
good times and bad 
all part of the poetry of life 
each day flowing, one to the other 
each day a world in itself 
rivers of the mind 
flowing endlessly 
everything’s a story 
life is but an unending poem 

he lives life to the fullest 
been through it all 
setting life to poetry 
poetry to life 
each stanza a portrait 
in my gallery 

forever in my heart
