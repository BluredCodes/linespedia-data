---
title: "loneliness"
writer: "catz"
slug: "loneliness"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

i sit in the darkness, silence fills the air
i speak your name, there’s no response
only silence, everywhere
i long for your warmth, your touch
there’s only coldness around me
the loneliness, it’s just too much

you’re gone, oh God, what can i do
i need you to hold me close
to hear you say ‘i love you’
why did you leave me, where have you gone
i need you, i want you
please come back, i can’t go on

i love you, i love you, surely you know
what is life without you
forever lonely, filled with sorrow
lonely days, lonelier nights, must I go on
longing, wishing, waiting, hoping
loneliness .....you’re gone ...
