Oh, To Be a Badger Assassin!
	I know not yet of what I speak
	but then I’m not a poetic geek
	perhaps someday they’ll let me learn
	for this awesome  knowledge I do yearn
	and if ever I’m allowed to know
	a’ bandersnatchin’ I will go

	
	though I’m not quite sure for what they stand
	I feel it’s a good thing for all the land
	Badger Assassins can’t be bad
	they’re righteous folks, for which I’m glad
	because this much fun must be pure
	however, soon I shall be sure

	
	that is of course if they allow me in
	to bandersnatch, I will begin
	I’ll uphold this mighty group, I will
	down in the valley and over the hill
	and everywhere there’s poetic sin
	I’ll urge them to stop and start over again

	
	if the mighty badgers should cast me out
	I’d cry and beg and plead and pout
	for one more chance to prove my worth
	to bandersnatch on this good earth
	so please, please consider me,
	an honored Badger Assassin to be
	Dee Garner
	November 5, 2001
