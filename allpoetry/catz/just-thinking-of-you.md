---
title: "just thinking of you"
writer: "catz"

just thinking of you

remembering.....
warm embraces
promises made
dreams to come true
wishes to fulfill
hopes never ending
sitting here alone
just thinking of you

Dee Garner
October 31, 2000I wrote this to a very special person in my life.
