When all the world seems against us
And everything seems to go awry
We ask ourselves, “What’s going on
What’s happening with me…why?”

Just when we think things are going good
And we’re finally getting it all together
Along comes someone or something
Raining on our progress, crappy weather

The gloom sets in, the storm clouds gather
Can’t find cover, there seems nowhere to run
But then something reminds us
Behind each cloud shines the sun

Of course we know it’s not over yet
We still have a long way to go
But a glimmer of hope shines through
It has to get better, somehow we know

When it’s finally all over and done
When everything starts to fall into place
Then we know that little glimmer of hope
That shines, is coming from God’s grace

He takes care of us, He promised he would
He’ll never leave us alone, watches from above
All he asks is our undying faith
And a place in our hearts for love

So whenever all you have seems lost
And you’re feeling alone and blue
Just say a prayer within your heart
“Please, God, help me see this through'

“Help me see that glimmer of hope
Show me the way, I’ll try once more
I’ll do my best to keep the faith
I know you wont shut the door”

For a glimmer of hope is all we need
To remind us of his love
He’ll show us the way, guide us through life
As he watches over us from above

By Dee Garner
For my son Stephen
