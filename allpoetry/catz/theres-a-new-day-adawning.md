there's a new day a'dawning

morning sun quietly,
slowly, spreads it's rays
across my pillow as if to say
wake up, sleepy head
you've better things to do
than dream your life away

there's a new day a'dawning

ignore it, says my sleepy eyes
but sunlight creeps closer
warms my face, nudging 
grumbling I turn away
don't want to get up
but give in, i must, grudging

the clock is ticking

there's a new day a'dawning


Dee Garner
