---
title: "I try to love again"
writer: "swf27texas"

I can still feel the music
But I cannot dance
I still dream
But I cannot sleep
I go on living
But I cannot move
I still smile
And I still laugh
But I cry
Sometimes I think I can finally move
But then I hear the music
And still I cannot dance
I think you are gone sometimes
But there you are
With eyes of doves
I try to run
But I go on walking
I try to hide
But there you are
Gone
"this too shall pass"
But still I cannot move
I try to love again
But no one compares
You are so potent
"time will heal all wounds"
But still...
Do you know
Sometimes I cry for you
No one can hold me
I search in every crowd
I go out every night
I drive for miles to find
There is no escape.
