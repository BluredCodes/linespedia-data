Where is my knight in shining armor
That is suppose to sweep me off my feet?
When will I feel the secure emotion ,
Making my life complete?
When will I feel his warm tender touch,
That lets me know he needs me that much?
How long do I have to wait
To find my destiny?
Doesn't he know, 
he is suppose to  be out here rescuing me?
How long must the search last 
To find that one in a billion 
True romance?
When do I get my chance?
All I want to know 
When you come trotting down my trail is,
Knight in shining armor,
Will you be my fairytale?I have to note here that this is a poem from when I was 14! Still I am asking these questions at 27!
