title: "He is all that you could never be"

He is all that you could never fulfill.
All that you could never be.
He is a brightness inside of me 
That you could never see.

He is safeness in my world of fear.
He is direction that you could never give.
He gives purpose to all that I do
In a life that you could never live.

Answers that I have eluded , he simplifies.
That is what he does for me.
All that you could never be.
He  replaces your haunted memory.

You force shackles onto my bruised ankles.
He holds tomorrow like it were yesterday.
He is provoked by my enchanting grace. 
You consume my passion as if it were play.

He answers my cries with a warm sweet touch.
I cry for a man I can never replace.
Aspirations of a life that is contested.
Hopes for a quench I will never taste.

He is all that I pretend replaces you. 
All that I need to convince my devoted heart.
All that I pray for to take  the place 
Of unending miles that set us apart.
