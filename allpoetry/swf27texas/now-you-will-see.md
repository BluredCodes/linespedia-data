---
title: "Now you will see"
writer: "swf27texas"

My sincerest,
So sorry.
My baby, you deserve much more.
I will do my best to make it up to you.
But that will never do, 
I will never be through.
I am trying to feel more guilt
I am trying to feel more pain.
I am trying to pay my debts.
I don't want to live the same.
Trying not to find MY  way.
I want my heart to know you again.
To feel the tears welling up and not hold back.
But it won't change the way things haven't been.
To feel remorse for all of my transgressions.
Promises that blew away
You never asked anymore.
All those disappointments, you could never say.
No faith, but I was to blame.
All of your worth
You were disregarded.
Like an unwanted birth.
It was all about me 
But now you will see
The facets of our possibilities.
Just you and me.
You will see my great intentions
My chivalry for you and alive.
I want to feel your laughter contagious again.
Your merciful pardon I will strive.
So jelous they will all be.
Me living  vivaciously.
All for you and me.
You will see.
