'The best things in life
 never seem to last'
Before you know it
Ecstasy has already past.
'If you miss beat 
You could loose your rhythm'
And the rest of  your life 
Could forever remain dim
'Chance of a life time'
Could only last a moment.
Before you know it
Life is without fulfillment.
'Glory and Fame'
Could flash before your eyes.
You could leave everything the same
But your life still cries.
It cries for a chance 
To be set ablaze.
Power of a reflex 
Is where it all lays.
Sometimes no time to think
Interruption of the way it is suppose to go.
A single thought ending the possibilities
Of eternal adrenaline flow.
You never know what that opportunity holds
Your reaction may seem crazed and deranged 
But is well worth the risk
Your whole life could be changed.
