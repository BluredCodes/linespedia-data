title: "It would all be so easy"

I have let you down so many times
So many times I have lied.
Stabbing vicious at your barely beating heart.
Stripped away your pride.
But I still haven't heard good bye.
Why don't you break my tie?
Turned on you ...late late late ...at night.
So many times I have done
So, if you only knew ....how  wrong.
Why do you put up with me so long?
Torn up so bad
But you still hold my hand.
They tell you......let her go.
How do you answer with no?
You can't be this blind.
Hurting something tragic
But you don't seem to mind.
I am  not trying to cause you insomnia
Sometimes it is just the way it turns out.
Baby,     Give me up.
You are not deserving  of my wretched pain.
There will never be my love to gain.
I wouldn't blame you if you walked far far far ...away.
I don't plan to stay.
You are better off ...Can't you SEE!
It would all be so easy.
