I wish I had longer than forever
To repay you for everything you have done.
You picked me up after I was defeated
And I owe you for all the victories I have won.
You were there to listen,
When nobody else could hear.
You walked me through the hard times 
And helped me to conquer my biggest fear.
You were the one I could confide in
Of all of my hopes of my dreams coming true.
You were the one to make me smile 
When the whole world had made me blue.
You always helped me to understand 
What I was too blind to see.
You believed in all my crazy dreams,
And you always had confidence in me.
When I was lost or scared,
Your arms were always wide open.
You taught me that the world was mine 
And I could make my dreams happen.
When I finally conquer my world
And I hold in my hands my dreams come true,
I'll give you my moon and all of my stars
Because I couldn't have done it without you.
