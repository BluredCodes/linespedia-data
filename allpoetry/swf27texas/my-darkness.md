Do you understand my darkness?
Do you even see my tears?
If I could take you to my darkness,
You would understand my fears.
Looking into this flame 
Feeling one hundred mile away
I can feel the fire
Getting hotter every day.
The flames rush up into my soul
When I feel your body near.
They burn a hole full of darkness and fill me up with fear.
I try to keep the flame real near
In case I need some light.
Then I can cover the flame with my soul,
When you see too much I can make it night.
Lower the flame It's getting so bright that you can see.
Bring me my darkness you won't see through me.
