title: "No Plan To Stay"
writer: "swf27texas"

I know this game,
I know the way you play. 
I have played it many times before
 and I know you don't plan to stay.
You think you have this game won.
But you don't know who you are playing.
You had better make your move quick
Because I know exactly what you are not saying.
I can see right through you
Your blind and hidden eyes
I can feel your pain
Pain of late night cries.
I know your darkness
I can taste your tears
I know this place 
I know these fears
You think you are so clever
...that I won't know what to do
What you don't know is that I am just like you.
If you were so good at this game
You would of seen through my eyes
Quivered with my fears
And recognized my late night cries.
I am not your fool
Game isn't through
Keep your guard up
I play better than you.
