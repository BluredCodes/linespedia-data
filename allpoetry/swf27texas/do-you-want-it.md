You are the one who can make your dreams come true. Whatever it is, it is all up to you.So many dreams out there that you could make into reality . It could be the way you want it to be.You do not have a chance if you give up and never go the distance. The key to success is achieved by persistence. You can't sit back and dream of some day having more! Stand up and bring your world to the door! Dreamers believe in all their dreams and wait patiently for them to come true...Achievers believe in themselves and make their dreams come true!If you live life in your dreams, In life, you should live out your dreams! Quit wishing on those stars! They haven't made your dreams come true! If you want it...bad enough...GO AHEAD! The world is out there waiting ...for YOU!
