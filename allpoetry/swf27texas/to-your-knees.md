title: "To your knees"

I know there has to be something...Something I did to make you fall.That brought you down to your kneesAnd made you crawl.What could I have doneTo make you stick around?To keep coming backAfter I knocked you to the ground.Now, after all this time,we have been so far apart.You are back in my life but....With a stronger heart.I thought you would of collapsed by now.I have tried everything I can restlesslyTo make you fall again into me But you are not as easy.Strange the way you have changedKisses don't seem to mean as much.Your reaction isn't as weakWhen you feel my touch.When I look in your eyes  of sweet purity of dovesYour glance is sidetracked as far away...I no longer feel I know you in all of your waysNothing comes to mind to say.My body doesn't know your as it is used toI can't read your sick and twisty feeling.You can't find a door to openMaybe there isn't anything.What will I reach forTo find your blocked barriered doorI promise baby you will fall into meJust like you did before.
