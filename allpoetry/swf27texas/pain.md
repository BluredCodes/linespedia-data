How did you find me? You've been gone so long.
Darker than the shadows that sing my silent song.
Beauty is the face that will show you no fear.
Terror is the soul who will always keep you near.
Wherever is the day that you will be done and gone?
Further than the sky that brings your dusk to dawn.
Death to my soul when you can not be found.
Tears that haunt my heart out loud whenever you're around.
The deliverance of strength is followed by your presence
But will only be delivered by the tolerance to your weakness.
You are treatment to a wound that will never have a cure.
But sadness fear and anger to a child that once was pure.
Tears are the sound in the silence of my soul.
But your eyes are the ones who lead your voice through the hole.
Sadness for a child who never had a choice.
But strength to the ones who listen to her voice.
Bring me your pain, oh child of tolerence.
Let me be your strength while you find our happiness.
