Without your smile, the world is without the glory of light,Without your voice there is no sound to soothe the troubled soul,Without your gaze the world is an instantaneous blinkWithout your humor there is no echo of laughter,Without your touch there is no pleasure to be experienced;Without your shadow there is no duplicate of youWithout your embrace there is no sanctuaryWithout your happiness this is grief stricken worldWithout you I have no reason for existance;Without you, what would I do?
