---
title: "[Angels cry too]"
writer: "Halocination"

Angels cry too,Their tears blister the sky,And rip apart rain cloudsIt hurts to see them cryTheir sobs are beautifulLike an orchestra on ecstasyThey render souls to their pleasSinging the legends of unsung heroesThey wail into the nightWhen you feel pain,Your angel cries for you toStanding over your shoulderWashing your wounds with her tearsDrowning your pain with murmurs of loveMy angel screams for me right nowRipping her hair out in handfulsTearing her clothesHolding torn hair and garment to heavenWhy, so much pain for one to bearLet me take him homeI can't protect him from thisYou tell me to protect him,And then he has to endure these thingsWhat should I do, just let him hurtI feel a just a fraction of his painAnd it is too much to bearWhy have you forsaken me?Let me take him now......Sorry to those that I hurt...
