title: "Army of Me"

Align my spine to sprout wings
Let me live up to my name, 
Not just a halo above the head of a seraph
But the risen warrior summoned for battle
Along side Gabriel I want to stand
My sword in hand
Girded with my armor, and shield
And understanding what I have to face
Come and do battle with me
There is an army of me waiting inside
You think my eye brows arch for no reason
My eyes squint because of the battle that churns within
Time is of no relevance, time comes and goes
Only what happens today, only what happens now
That is what matters,
So either come in fighting, or bow down.
Because not all angels bear mercy on their wings
Not all angels follow their heart
Not all of them give in to love
And not all will bend for emotions
Some stand tall against the wind
Waves split around them
Duty and honor come before love

Do angels have guardian angels
I hope this one does, because I try but I'm not perfect
I fall in love with faces that pass by in pedestrian traffic
I cry for babies that don't get their way
I die everytime women are abused
And arm myself everytime races are misused
This Seraph will strike back,
This is working for a position in the army of heaven
I want to be on the frontlines of that final battle
So all the pain that has been inflicted on all my people
Will generate through my mace when I strike the skulls
Of the brothers Mephisto, Beelzebub, and Lucifer.
I know your names and I'm not afraid
Yeah I sin to, but if I go to hell, 
You better watch your back, I'm either going to take over
Or make you regret ever looking in my direction

I'm that Seraph that will not die
When I leave this world
I'm just beginning my journey
That's why I'm reading
The Basic Instructions Before Leaving Earth (BIBLE)
I'm coming for you...
