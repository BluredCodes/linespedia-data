---
title: "Forever..."
writer: "Halocination"

---

We all live on the tree of life
Just leaves blown around
By all these violent winds

Sometimes it's calm,
Sometimes it's rough,
And you never know when a friend
Will wilt and fade
Or get blown away by a gale

Maybe we'll soar together someday
Even if the currents change
And we are separated
There's always that chance
We may fly together again

And who knows what the future holds
Maybe we'll end up by the sea
On a rock
You and I to be washed away
To a quiet place

Where we can be together forever...
