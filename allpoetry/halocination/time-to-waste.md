---
title: "Time to Waste"
writer: "Halocination"

Last night I stood outside your window,
And hoped to see your face.
The rain fell and soaked the earth,
As I walked away from your place;
I promised myself, that never again,
Will I endure such disgrace.
But tomorrow right about this same time
I'll have a couple hours to waste...
