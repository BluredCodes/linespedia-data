---
title: "Wings"
writer: "Halocination"
slug: "wings"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/4503-Wings-by-Halocination"
---

My eyes slowly shut, and my mind drifts away
To that place where you and all of my dreams stay
Not a single thought to things undone, thinking transcended
The dancing of fireflies in the cool dusk of days ended
The rustle of fairies skimming the lakes surface
The soothing breeze, how familiar everything tastes
As I sit in my swing, I'm diluted by dreams
Sauntering the air on butterfly wings
Floating on notes of beautiful sirens that sing
Every branch of every tree becomes a woodwind flute
Lending harmony, eyes closed still enroute
Stars blink at me, and the moon begins to grin
Staring at the sky, where am I going? Where have I been?
Laying on dew kissed blades of green,
My wings tightly folded, my hands reaching places unseen
The music begins to subside,
And fairies, sirens, fireflies, stars, moon, even sky 
They all find a place to hide.
My wings waft away like they never had been
My eyes, slowly open.
