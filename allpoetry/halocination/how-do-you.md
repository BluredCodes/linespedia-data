title: "How Do You?"
writer: "Halocination"

How do you?
Face the one you love 
Hold a conversation with yourself?
Answer all your own questions?
Give yourself different opinions?

How do you face the one you love?
When she turns away from you
Says she's not good enough for you
But means you're not the one for her
When you can't hold her at night 
But she can hold you when she needs to

How do you face her?
How can you meet her eyes?
When she doesn't trust you
She trusts strangers on the street
Because they are like her,
She and they are both strangers to me

How can I face you
You've already judged me
Why don't you just let me go
Let me leave, if I'm so dirty
So disgusting, decrepid and worthless
All I wanted was to make you happy,
But my best, my love...
None of it is good enough for you
I don't want another,
I just want to be left alone
Without your face to haunt meI hope she reads this and cries her eyes out...
