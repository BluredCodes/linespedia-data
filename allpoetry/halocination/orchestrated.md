My life was orchestrated, 
and the symphony bored me
So I strangled the conductor, 
and burnt the sheet music
Now I carry a big stick,
instead of that little twig
I wave it around and conjure tones,
and notes that long lay docile
Drums, I love drums...
Strum the strings of the violins,
until they cry
Tug the strings of the cellos, 
until your finger nails fall off
My symphony will shatter cymbals
Horns pierce the listeners ear into bleeding

My life was orchestrated like preprogrammed
radio broadcasting
What exactly does preprogrammed mean?
Think about it, and break all the records
Break the rules that bind and the ropes that 
restrain
Preprogrammed like, 'You can't go there,' 'You can't do that'
'You can do this,' 'We don't allow that here.'
'Go back to where you came from,' 'What are you doing here?'
I'm here because this is where I belong,
This is my song that will be sung
Welcome to my uproar
It used to be an Opera, but I stabbed the fat lady
With her horned helmet
I hung the tenors from the curtains ropes

Dare to orchestrate my life and tell me what to be
I will be
I will become
I will be coming
I will be coming out
Just wait, wait and listen...Symphony in F U
