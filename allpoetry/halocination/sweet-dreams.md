title: "Sweet Dreams..."

I want to fall in love with your lips,
Their curves and every thought that you whisper
Drowning in your stare your eyes are oceans
There is a storm raging in there
The same storm that has consumed my heart
I want to kiss you but I fear I will melt
I will be consumed by the desire that burns
Fueled by the storm that rages in your eyes
And the warmth I feel from your touch
Can I fall in love with your gaze
Clue less and wishful, wondering in innocence
Looking on in admiration at nothing in particular
And lost long enough for me
To permanently place the image in my mind
I want to fall in love with your smell
Like happiness and angels laughing
Like a baby's smile and a sleeping child's sigh
I want to be cloaked in your scent
So after I leave you tonight I can close my eyes
And picture you sitting staring and smell you
As if we were still together,
I want to fall in love with your touch
So that miles cannot separate us
Distance cannot come between us
I want to have this familiarity embedded in my soul
Touch me under my skin,
Kiss me and hold my heart and soul in your hands
Let me feel what it is like to be free
To be loved by someone that is happy 
Just holding me
Someone who is happy just knowing that I love them too
I want to fall in love with you and all that you are
Fall and never stop falling
Drown and never stop sinking
Fly and never stop rising
Be free to see and be all those things
That I do when you are with me
When I am in your arms and nothing can come between
That is all I need
This time, this place, this presence...
That is all I want, 
I want to fall in love with you...On a positive note...  =)
