---
title: "This Rose"
writer: "Halocination"
slug: "this-rose"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I give you this rose today
To symbolize our journey under way
The long and sometimes crooked stem
Is the path we hopefully travel to its end
The leaves are those things that help us along
Like God, friends; whatever makes us strong

The thorns are trials we must overcome
So later we can be happy of where we're from
I know that there are less leaves than thorns
But I promise to stay through sunshine and storm
I will be with you at every bend,
I will help you decide at every branch or stem

At the end of the road is a beautiful flower
That will only bloom at the right hour
It will announce our ultimate arrival
That you and I made it to a love that can survive all.
