---
title: "Rain"
writer: "Halocination"
slug: "rain"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

On rainy days I become one with the storm
My tempest grows and tornadoes somber flights into mystical places
My rains water the hillsides and wash blades of grass
Lightening strikes my gaze at naked thoughts,
At peripheral emotions that have been released without constraint
Uncontrolled to rampage the unwary hearts of lovers lay bare
Lovers with open minds and naked hearts begging for love’s descent
Begging to endure the purgatory that is love’s embrace
I thunder replies at the naked thoughts that come to light
And they shudder like little children and hide under their beds
They cannot bear to look at the eyes of the storm
They don’t see its smiling face, and stern brow,
The black hair that shades the clouds
Clouds that carry millions of drops of emotions to wash away all…
To drench those walking the streets fretting in lonely passage
Soaking the heads of unsheltered and unprepared travelers.
The rain and I are one,
When I cry I bring tears to the eye of the storm
And the sheets of torrential rain are my coat that flies widely, wildly free
In the wake of my flight through never before navigated sky
As I storm my way high above the parched and yearning valleys
The infertile lands become watered, and dust is washed away
New hope, new life and new love
The storm and I, we do not need the sun to light our way
We have bolts of electric thoughts connecting between our axons
And our dendrites branch out to unfathomable extremes
Maybe I’m twisted, maybe dementia has set in
But this storm will rage on and shower on earth dwellers
Until I find a way to rain on Jupiter and Mars, Neptune, Venus and Uranus
Until I find a way to pour my rains on the sun, and extinguish the blinding rays
That hide the truth, because the best place to hide something is in the open
The best lie is one mixed with truth
But the truth always comes out no matter where you hide it
In the depths of the ocean I will evaporate the waters and fill my clouds 
I will find the truth under waves of lies and doubt
That’s why I like the rain because I believe it washes away the old
And all things are new, the hope of life is realized all over again
So today, tomorrow forever, let it rain,
