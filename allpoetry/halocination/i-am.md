---
title: "I am"
writer: "Halocination"
slug: "i-am"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I am abandoned hope,
Found and spat upon,
I am countless dollars
Wasted on insignificant obsessions.
I am that blank faced stranger you pass by
I am not a poet.
Just a depressed paint covered moron
Staring at a canvas of layers of wasted colors
And an abused brush that couldn't create art
If it was dipped in my own blood
I am shattered dreams
No not shattered, shitted dreams
Wasted years on trying make happy
Those that are not
And fix lives that are shot
If you wait for me you wait in vain
I will never come
Because that is who I am
