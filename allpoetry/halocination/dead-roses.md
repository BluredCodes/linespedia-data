---
title: "Dead Roses"
writer: "Halocination"
slug: "dead-roses"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I don't know why you still keep all those roses
I gave them to you so long ago
You don't act like they mean anything
At least I can't tell anymore
All there is each day is another reason
Why I should go away
I've come to realize what you think of me
I'm just any other guy
No longer the special one
That brought you roses and smiles
I'm not that handsome guy anymore
And my roses aren't as pretty as the ones before
But what hurts the most
Is that you keep my love in an old shoebox
In the corner of the closet
With all those dead roses,
And never let me see it anymore
