For Maryam R

God was just showing off when he made you,
He thought to himself and these thoughts grew.
What is more beautiful than a thousand seas?
What moves lighter than a summer breeze?
What type of voice can speak celestial tunes?

How many strands of hair does it take,
To create the appearance of an ocean's wake?
What shade do you color the eyes of a soul
Whose appearance renders you speechless, and swallows you whole?
How beautiful are eyes that can drive you to insanity, 
That you can get lost in far away from humanity?

How do you shade the body of an angel
and placeher on earth among humans to dwell?
What shape are the lips that say
Without speaking the greatness of One who took time from his day
And decided to think of how to make and what to do,
To show that he could create the most beautiful of things,
That he could create you...
