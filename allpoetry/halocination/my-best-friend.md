How I love you
PISS OFF, Get out my face, 
And you just stand there, 
Taking everything I have to say, 
Always listening,
My best friend...
I can kick you and hurt my foot
Stab you and you still stand in front of me
Shoot you and only dust falls away
When I hold you you're so cold
It soothes me so
You're rough to the texture
Always strong and sturdy giving me support
I can scream my lungs out and you wont leave
You won't cry,
You always listen
My best friend
You'll never tell I know you won't
Everything is safe with you
Even when I had to go sooo bad that I went on you
It's convenient you only stay down the street from my house
In this abandoned parking lot
You don't have a single thing to call your own
Except me, you know I will always return
To kick you when I get mad
Punch you until my knuckles bleed
Leaving pieces of flesh hanging from your face
My best friend
I'm glad you don't tell me anything
Because I really don't care what you would have to say
I just want you to listen
Just stand there to the weather everyday
And listen
My best friend, the Brick Wall...>:)
