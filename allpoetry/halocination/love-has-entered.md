title: "Love Has Entered"
writer: "Halocination"

Attractive lips say such beautiful things
They soothe the soul, with a single kiss
Softly wish away the troubles of the day 
They speak everything into celestial bliss

Soft hands comfort me like hot-springs
They support, and wipe away every tear
Holding me entirely so time is stationary
Life becomes to short when I hold you near

Beautiful eyes transport me far, far away
Taking me helplessly to places I long to be
Eyes that seem to only see the good always
Beautiful eyes that find the good even in me
