---
title: "You..."
writer: "Halocination"
slug: "you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/4904-You...-by-Halocination"
---

My thoughts of you are wandering streams,
They start high in the mountains and descend to earth
You bring love to all you meet
You are the essence of nature and serenity
What can be compared to you?
You elude me like moments lost in a lovers embrace
Like kisses blown to the wind,
Strewn to be never heard from again
Caught by strangers that stray into my path...
I only know you through dreams and glimpses,
You are the face in the crowd that catches my eyes...
The gaze that meets mine at every serene moment.
You are the face of the comforting stranger
Whose smile tells me everything is ok...
Who are you?
I fell in love with you like one would a sunset,
You are too beautiful to be hidden from all that would care to notice.
And I love you too much to try to hold on to you forever,
I cannot begin to imagine or imagine when you began.
I just know that you exist in my heart, my head, even my hands...
