Today I read your letter
It took me quite some time
I thought what we had was better
And now it's on my mind
It started off:  "Hello my Dear"
And ended "Always yours"
I'm asking you now through my tears
Please tell me why, because...

I can't erase the memories
I fall into your eyes
So angel, won't you tell me please
I'm praying to the skies.

Today I read your letter
It was stained with your tears
I wish I had treated you better
Instead of wasting all those years
Why didn't I hold you tighter?
Why did I ever let you go?
My apologies to the writer,
But my love I have to show.
