My covers are the walls of a tower
Protecting me from the oncoming storm
My sheets are the arms of the one I love
Keeping me safe from harm
Closing my eyes buys the ticket
For the train that's dreamland bound
And my thoughts drift away on pillows of blue
Freeing me from the binds of realityI wrote this in seventh grade. Just felt like postimg something.
