I await you, ever faithful
Silent here right by the phone
I find it a bit more painful
Each second I can't feel your touch
Each moment I can't hear your voice

It hurts so badly to not be held
I haven't felt you near in days
I'm overwhelmed with screams inside
I'd cry for a moment or two of embrace
Yet I'll only await your call..

You are out, and having fun
Vigilant, I wait for you
Quietly I call your name
Knowing no response will come
Wanting to think you'll hear my cries..

Love someone, and let them go
Be happy my love, be free
I know you'll leave me soon enough
But if you need me, here I am
