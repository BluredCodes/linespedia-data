title: "Untitled (Without Pattern)"

I sneak away while others stare
They see, yet they see nothing
I cling to this world
Cling? I am chained
I'm chained to this world
But when I close my eyes I drift to yours

Here I am embraced by tranquility
I spin in euphoric circles
Peacefully deceived
Torrents of pain subdued
Pretending, my guilty pleasure
No one knows the calm of it

Unreal is my world of freedom
I am aware, yet careless
An escape from the bitter cold
A place of warmth to call my own
Where everything is as I like it
Joyfully lost within myself

They try to wake me to their world
Painful realization before long
My eyes are glassy, my stare is blank
They beckon with harsh words
Quietly, 'I do not care..'
As long as I'm with you and the melody calls

Ah, my sweet visionary
My tired vessel fails me so
As I fall into your arms of illusion
Into your loving embrace of solace
I care not for this world I leave behind..
Come, my love, and carry me home..I wrote this in math class, oddly enough.. I don't even think it's poetry.. It just.. popped into my head so I wrote it down.
