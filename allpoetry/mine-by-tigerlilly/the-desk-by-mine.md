loving
you comes easy
though
we're breaking
all the rules
the moments 
that we steal
are saved for lovers
and for fools

your name
brings sweet music
to my ears
each time it's said
i often hear
her speak of you
when she's
making plans
with ted

and
i eavesdrop
when she's
calling you
saying
"sorry, running late,
important papers,
deadlines..."

tonight bob's
her dinner
date


the office
closes
half-past five
i'm on my way
to meeting you
and i know
these
stolen moments

just get easier
