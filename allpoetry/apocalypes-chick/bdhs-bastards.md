there is this girl,
who i consider a friend,

there is this girl,
who i want to remain close

there is this girl,
who is ridiculed for her hair

there is this girl,
who is laughed at for her peers

there is this girl,
who had a girl friend

there is this girl,
who is the coolest individual

there is this girl,
who goes to this site

there is this girl,
i just want to say u know who you are

there is this girl,
who i will stick up for whenever

there is this girl,
who is as normal as you and me

there is this girl,
you should get to know

there is this girl,
who you possibly teased

there is this girl,
one i would like to prase

there is this girl,
smart, funny, ORIGINAL

there is this girl,
who doesnt care what she looks like

there is this girl,
all u BDHS bastards should admire

there is this girl,
a friend, sister, individual.....
