tall, dark, handsome man.....

he is the one i desire,
the one i need

tall, dark, handsome man.....

he lives in my head,
but is no-where to be found

tall, dark, handsome man.....

why cant you be with me now?
i know you are not real

tall, dark, handsome man.....

i long for you to hold me,
for you to kiss me right

tall, dark, handsome man.....

i think i have found you
yes,

tall, dark, handsome man.....

i have found the one i love,
you are here, finally

tall, dark, handsome man.....

there is someone for everyone,
