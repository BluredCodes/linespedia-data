title: "A day by the sea"
writer: "the sea by pl rage"

I sit by the sea watching the sunrise,
A colorful display of a new beginning
It looks like a fire cleansing the world

The sea whispers of hidden secrets,
Luring the unwary to its cold embrace
I sometimes wonder what lies on the other side

I think of all the choices I’ve made
All I’ve missed because I was too shy,
All the times I’ve been used for being too kind

The sun climbs higher, touching my face
A warmth gently spreading inside me,
Drying my unshed tears

I hope I’ll find another source of heat,
Something that will burn longer than a day
When the night comes I’ll be cold again

The wind flows around me,
Bringing the scents of life and decay
A reminder of the limitations of our existence

I try to think of what the future will bring
My dreams fading under the glare of the sun
Nothing comes to mind, a void inside

The day passes slowly on the desolate dunes,
Still too quickly it’s over for me
Nothing to fill the empty times

I sit by the sea watching the sunset,
A majestic finish to the glory of day
It seems the night will claim me again
