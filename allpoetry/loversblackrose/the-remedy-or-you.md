---
title: "The remedy or You?"
writer: "LoversBlackRose"

What is a remedy?
What does it do?
Can it help me?
Or will it somehow kill me?
No matter what can you help me?
Can you save me from hell?
Or just pull me in more.
Whatever it does i wish it would happen fast cause i need some help.
The smallest thing can pull you in...
Or push you right back out.
Pain pushes me in.
You pull me right back out.
Deny me the one last thing i need.
Deny me love and bring w/ you only hate and pain.
I can't fight it anymore.
Deny me pain and hate and leave me alone.
All alone w/ nothing nor no one.
Just bite marks and my mind full of you.
Death waits for me.
But i stay alive to wait for you.
Are you my remedy or are you my death?
The scars in my arms.
The pain in my head.
Tha hate you placed my my heart.
I wish you'd take back.
Your soul belongs w/ mine.
Together in pain and love.
We waited,I found.
You waited,you lost.
Even in death i would wait for your love.
But in life i've already found it.
Soulmates we are,That you can not deny me.
