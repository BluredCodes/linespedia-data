---
title: "I hate you"
writer: "alyssa"
slug: "i-hate-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/413-I-hate-you-by-alyssa"
---

You screwed with my mind 
I left my trusted world behind 
You tore me apart 
I should have kept my heart 
You were cruel and kind 
It really fucked with my mind 
You didn't care, like you said 
I wanted to be dead 
You messed up my life 
Because of you 
I used that knife 
You said goodbye 
I sit and cry, ready to die 
You meant nothing to me 
I felt sorry for he 
I loved him the whole time 
I was his, and he was mine 
You tore us apart to have me 
You tore me apart then let go of me 
I never so much as liked you 
You didn't care what you would do to me 
You didn't care about me 
You didn't care
