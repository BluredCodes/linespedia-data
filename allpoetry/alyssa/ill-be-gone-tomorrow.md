---
title: "I'll be gone tomorrow"
writer: "alyssa"
slug: "ill-be-gone-tomorrow"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/412-Ill-be-gone-tomorrow-by-alyssa"
---

You found out today that I'm no longer here 
That I have faced lifes biggest fear 
You may be feeling the pain and sorrow 
That you're not going to see me tomorrow 
But you're not going to see me 
Cause I'm not going to be 
Here ever again as I once was... 
I wanted to be 
right beside you, you and me 
But I couldn't handle the pain 
I cracked, I went insane 
Too many problems for one girl to take 
dying was an easy decision to make 
No more pain, no more fears 
just long lasting internal tears 

I'm no longer here 
and I'm not coming back 

I'm not here today 
I won't be here tomorrow
