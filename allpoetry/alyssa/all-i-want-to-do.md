title: "All I want to do"

All I want to do is Cry 
All I need to do is Die 
A fake smile is all I show 
A fake smile is all I know 
Because I am hurting deep down inside 
It's getting hard to keep the pain there, it's hard to hide 
My pain is building up everyday 
My pain has worsened in everyway 
And I can't stay 
I can't take another day 
So I say goodbye 
And so I die 
But Death is a painless 
long lasting rest 
And every day and every night 
through my life, an awful fight 
All I want is to die 
And All I need, is to cry
