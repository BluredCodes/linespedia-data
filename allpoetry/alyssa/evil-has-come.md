---
title: "Evil has come"
writer: "alyssa"
slug: "evil-has-come"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/411-Evil-has-come-by-alyssa"

Evil has come to steal me away 
I just can't stay 
I just can't deal 
my pains too real 
too hard to live 
too hard to stay 
too easy to simply walk away 
needing to go 
wanting to stay 
why does my life have to be this way?
