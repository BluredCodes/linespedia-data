Long ago, and far away 
I fell in love, a big mistake
He took my heart, and stomped it down
He made me cry, he made me frown
My mommy always told me 
He was just a heart break
I didn't listen, and now im lost in heartache
He spoke the words i wanted to hear
And on cold nights he held me near
But, the day came when a baby started to grow
He denied me and our daughter
Just left me with the woe
My daughter always asks 
"Where, oh where could he be?"
I don't know what to tell her
Without her hating me
Her little eyes are lost
When i say i have no clue
He left me with this heart ache
And nothing left to do
She has her daddy's big brown eyes
And his little attitude
If only he could see her now
My only point would be proved
