I miss you with a passionThat cannot be explainedEvery time you come to mindI feel this horrible painI wish I could have let you knowExactly what you meanEven if you’re eyes were openYou never would have seenYou saw your feelings And you saw my faults We fought like childrenCouldn’t be like adultsSo where do I go from here?This misery is destroying meEven if your eyes are openYou will never ever see
