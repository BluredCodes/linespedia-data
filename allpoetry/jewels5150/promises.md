Promise me
you will love me
no matter what

Promise me
you will be there
when I need you

Promise me
you will miss me
when I'm not around

Promise me
one day if I'm gone
you would still smile at me
and say that I'm special
as you used to tell me

Promise me
you would still 
say the three little words
you used to say to me

Promise me
you would come see me
if I lay down to sleep

Promise me
you would touch me
kiss me, miss me
for the last time

Promise me 
you would not hate me
and love me forever

Promise me
you would touch the grave
feel the cold ground
stare at the stone
that bears my name
lay down my favorite flowers
on the ground

Promise me
you would not hate me
for what I did

Promise me
you would give me
the last smile 
feel the warmness
and love from me

Promise me
you will know
my love will never die
fill you up and let you live
in the world, that awful place,
without me.
