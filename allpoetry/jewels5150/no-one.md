Nobody knows how much I hurt,
Nobody knows how I am.
Everybody thinks that I am problem-free 
What they don't know is that I have pain in me
I have more pain than you think.
Every time I smile I hurt,
Every time I laugh I cry,
But that is all on the inside
I look around and nobody is there
Tears roll down my face,
Something here is out of place..
