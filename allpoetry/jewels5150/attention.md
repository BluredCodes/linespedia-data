I'm screaming so loud,
Yet no one can hear me.
 I'm smiling so sound,
Yet no one can see me.
 I'm loosing my mind,
Yet no one can help me.
 Still standing time,
Yet no one can keep it going.
 Life as I know it flashes before my eyes,
Yet no one sees the pain going through my mind.
 I look in the mirror, as I take my final breath,  
Yet no one can stop me!
