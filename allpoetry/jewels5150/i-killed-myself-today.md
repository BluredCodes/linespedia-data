I killed myself today
Put a gun to my temple 
And pulled the trigger
It didn't hurt at all 
It was so quick
The blood flowing down the side
of my face looks fake
My eyes are open
My gaze is fixed on the flowers
Swaying in the window box
I have slight smile on my face
Like I'm or think I'm going to be
There's a single tear on my face
a last regret
I didn't know where I was going
But I knew where I'd been
I couldn't go back there anymore
I killed myself today......
