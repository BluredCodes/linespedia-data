I once knew a girl who had eyes like glass spheres.
You could see all of the courage, kindness, love, and confidence she had in her.

I once knew a girl who had a heart of gold. 
You could feel all of the warmth, beauty, generousity, and joy she had in her. 

I once knew a girl who had a shining personality.
When you were around her, all you wanted to do was laugh.

I once knew a girl who had a smile of sunshine.
When she smiled, the room lit up.

I look in the mirror, and I now see a different girl.

Her eyes are full of pain.

Her heart is dull and lifeless.

Her smile radiates sadness.

I really miss the girl I used to be.
