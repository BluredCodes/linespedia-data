---
title: "why me"
writer: "Jewels5150"
slug: "why-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

my tender heart is covered in hate
the hate you have implanted in my soul
you showed me my ultimate fears
and uncovered your own sweet demise
i hate all the things you have put me through
i blame all my hate and sorrow on you
the day will come when you'll get your return
on the events you've caused 
maybe then you'll learn
you can't trash every single person who cares
when you asked for a hand 
i was always there
i gave you my heart, my all, my love
you took it for granted it wasn't enough
the world was mine before you came 
you stole that too put me to shame
i will leave you with a question
one simple inquiry
of all of the people
why did it have to be me
