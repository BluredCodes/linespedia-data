---
title: "My Soul"
writer: "Jewels5150"

A dead soul lives inside me,
  A dead soul cries to me,
A dead soul pleads for help,
  I try to help this dead soul
But, it won't listen.
  This dead soul has died 
And now I will have to go with it
  For it was part of me
And I was...
  part of it
