I hate this feeling it happens everyday. Depression overcomes me in every single way. There is no way to stop it, it controls me on its own. There is no way to prevent it when I'm stuck in this horrible zone. I wish I could fly away into a beautiful land. There would be no problems, no worries, and someone to hold my hand. Within this hell I live in, I have no one on which I  can depend. My life is a very sad one for it is a life without any friends. My tears they flow as an ocean would. I try to stop them, i wish I could. My life has become one big giant blur. I feel like I watch it pass by,I can't control what occurs. Maybe one day I shall find my place. My place in this life, finally get dealt my ace. My expression is blank, my tears are never ending. 
For I forever cry inside, my destiny slowly descending.
