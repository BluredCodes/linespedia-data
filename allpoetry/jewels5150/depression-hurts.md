Depression has come back again
And death is lurking somewhere near
I have lost my way once more
And they are feeding on my fear

My heart cries out in anguish
My agony has grown too strong
I cannot bear this pain anymore
I have felt it for so long

I have so much hurt and anger
And too many emotions to feel
I am no longer in the driver's seat
I have relinquished the wheel

I feel I can go on no longer
The pain is just too great
Depression and death are upon me
They don’t have long to wait

One after the other
I start to swallow each pill
Depression and death have won
I have lost my will

No will to survive
No will to live
No will to go on
Nothing left to give

I close my eyes
And with my last breath
I say good-bye
And take my leave… with death
