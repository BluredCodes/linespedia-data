Around, all around, the angels gather.
My dread grows as the dagger of your words falls against my naked soul.
It severs me, and darkly my
blood drips
to the cold, uncaring tombstones.
In a strange and terrible glee I call your name
while Death's shadow takes my hand.
Now alone, my cascade of tears falls upon bloody eyes.

This is your love
