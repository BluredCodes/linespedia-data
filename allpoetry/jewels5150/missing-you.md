title: "Missing You"

This world was so much brighter
When you were standing beside me
Your hand always there to hold
Knowing you'd never deny me

Your touch was very warm
Your hugs were very needed
I haven't forgotten anything
And this loss WILL be defeated

Whenever I needed a friend
Or just a sweet embrace
Whenever I was in need for a lift
I would turn to see your smiling face

Can someone please tell me
Just how to let you go
To cherish and love your memory
Without letting this pain grow

The way you laughed
The way you cried
The way you cared
The way you justified

I promise I'll never forget you
Nor turn your memory away
I promise I'll always love you
Every hour, second, minute, and day

It's all because of you
My world is sad and blue
You went away
Now my life is just a rainy day
