title: "Empty"
writer: "Sunnyone"

I thought that you filled up my world
You took away the space inside of me
then filled me up so I could be
a vessel of a love so free...

Within my shining love lit eyes
You stood as tall as any Prince could do
I didn’t know until today
that I would not be all to you...

And taken wholly by surprise
I watched as all my dreams came crashing down
I am alone, nowhere to go
just holding on, and spinning round...

Where once you filled my heart with hope
now lies despair in all it’s deepest pain
my glass of love is powder dry
and only emptiness remains...


for my friend, who is no longer
I miss you
