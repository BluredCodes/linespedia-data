Well…long distance love is inspiring…
It leaves me with urges untold…
I’m sitting here hot and perspiring…
Though weather up here is damned cold!

Your voice makes me happy and horny…
It tickles me down where it should…
My nipples get hard with the wanting…
My womanly parts all feel good…

My body is patiently waiting…
My parts are all wet to the touch…
There’s nobody here, but I’m ready…
The strain’s just a little too much!

I open the drawer where I hid it…
The jelly is lying there, too…
In all of it’s magnificent Pinkness…
It’s one goal in life is to screw…

Eight inches of flexible rubber…
with various speeds for my taste…
When plunged deep within, I discover…
Not one single inch goes to waste…

When I think of you, I go crazy…
I turn up the speed by a notch…
My Pink Thing goes so fast, it’s hazy…
There’s steam rising out of my crotch!

In one final moment of glory…
In shudders of sexual delight…
My body explodes, and it cums to a close…
And my Pink Thing is done for the night…
