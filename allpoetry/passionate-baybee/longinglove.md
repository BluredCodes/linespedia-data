I keep searching, 
while my heart continues yearning.
When will i find a love so pure, 
that is impossible to cure?
When will the man of my dreams come along 
to sweep me off my feet, 
so no longer will i have to weep?
I can't handle feeling so incomplete, 
another's heart i long to keep.
No one can see all the burning desires i truly seek.
So much love i have inside, 
each day that goes by makes it harder to deny.
There is someone i wish to be mine, 
and have wished this for quite some time. 
Being with him would be so sublime, 
the feeling i could never define, 
but would be totally devine.
Oh if only he were mine.
Never before have i tried so much, 
to feel a man's touch. 
Maybe one day i can put an end to this pain 
by letting him know i feel this way.
