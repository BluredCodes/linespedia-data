title: "'~*I've Fallen In Love*~'"

Just when i thought i would never find love again, 
is when you came along and changed my point of view. 
At first it was simply innocent lust, 
but as time went by i got to know a lot about you. 
That was when i started to fall, 
and love reopened the door to my heart once more. 
The feeling feels so great, 
and i love how it makes my heart race. 
Ohhh the butterflies i get when my tummy starts doing flips. 
Every time i see your face or look into your beautiful blue eyes, 
my heart melts all over the place. 
I know inside that you care for me too. 
I don't know exactly how much, 
but i know you do because certain things you say 
makes it appear that way. 
It's the simpliest things that you do or say, 
that have got me wanting you in every way,
more and more every day. 
I've fallen so in love with you, 
and thats how i want it to always stay.
