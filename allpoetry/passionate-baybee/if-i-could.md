---
title: "'If I Could'"
writer: "PaSsIoNaTe BaYbEe"

If only i could turn back the hands of time and make you mine all over again, i would fix what ever it was i did wrong to keep you from leaving me. If only you knew how much i still love you, and long for you. If only i could tell you how much i wish you and i were still together, with our hearts entwined and our souls together as one. If only you could feel all the heartache and pain you have caused me. If only you could see the way you've tore my heart apart, maybe then you would understand how a broken heart feels.
