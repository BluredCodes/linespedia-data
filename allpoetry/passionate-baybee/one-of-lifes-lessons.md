Listen to your heart 
and everything it has to say. 
You could be surprised 
at some of the secrets it has held inside. 
The more you open it, 
the more you learn, 
for your heart holds the answer 
to every question you yearn. 
Allow your heart to guide you through, 
and you might find 
a better understanding of YOU. 
Now listen to your heart, 
and tell me, 
what do you hear? 

The heart knows your every fear, 
it can see your every tear. 
It feels all your pain, 
and helps you to regain. 
Always follow your heart, 
i was once told and forever treat it like gold. 
You can only hear when you listen, 
so take this as 
one of lifes lessons!
