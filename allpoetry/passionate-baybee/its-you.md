---
title: "'It's You!!'"
writer: "PaSsIoNaTe BaYbEe"
slug: "its-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/1702-Its-You---by-PaSsIoNaTe-BaYbEe"
---

It's you... who is constantly on my mind. Day or night it doesn't matter the time or place. It's you... That i have this undoubtably huge crush on and the burning desire to be with. It's you i want. It's you i need. It's you... That gets my heart racing at a high speed. If only you could see how great you and me together would be... It's you i talk about 24/7. It's you i see every night in my dreams. I just wish you knew....IT'S YOU!
