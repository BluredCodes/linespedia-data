---
title: "~*Where Was Joe?*~"
writer: "PaSsIoNaTe BaYbEe"
slug: "where-was-joe"
source: "AllPoetry"

I know what I'm about to write 
Will probably make you mad
And I'm really sorry
But the things I see are sad.

I know you guys have been
Best friends forever
But have you ever thought
That maybe it's past due time
For your fucked up friendship to end?

When has Joe been there
When you've needed him to be?
Was Joe there all the times
You were down 
And felt defeated by misery?

Mike please tell me...

Where is Joe
When you're far away?
When does he ever wait for your call
Or drop a line to say
"Hey man how was your day?"

Does he ever care
About how you're doing
And if you're okay?
Does not having you around
Affect him or his life
In any way?

Does he worry constantly
Day after day?
Why is Joe only wanting you around 
When its convient okay?
Would Joe do anything for you
That I haven't done?

I know the old line
'Bros before hoes'
But ask yourself just one time
Is Joe ever there
Like a 'REAL' friend should be
And if you died tomorrow 
How much would he care?
Sure he might show up 
For the wake and funeral
But would he visit your grave
And mourn with sorrow?

Mike take the time
To stop and think
Has Joe been there for you
At all 
In any of the ways
That I have
And does Joe love you
The way that I do?
