I use to get so lost and confused i wouldn't know what to do. I felt so alone and then i found you. You showed me something i never knew... you showed me the insides of me, without you i would still have no clue. You brought out so much in me, you got out of me the person i one day hope to be. You taught me that i could love again and to my own heart be true. I know only time can heal a broken heart, and i guess mine has ran through because now i know im in love with you. My heart is whole once again and the feelings all feel brand new. Deep down inside im so happy i could cry!
