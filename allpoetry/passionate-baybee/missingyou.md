title: "~*MiSsInG*YoU*~"

I miss the way you used to look at me. 

Your beautiful green eyes burning into me. 

I miss the way you used to jokingly make fun of me. 

I miss our talks, 

and the way you'd always wipe the tears from my eyes when i would cry. 

I miss you all together, 

more and more with each passing day. 

There's just something about you, 

and i can't even find the words to say.... 

just how perfect you are 

in each and every way. 

I wrote this for my friend *CONNOR* I met him in treatment, he is very dear to me!!!
