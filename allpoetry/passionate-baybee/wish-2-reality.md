---
title: "\"Wish 2 Reality\""
writer: "PaSsIoNaTe BaYbEe"
slug: "wish-2-reality"
source: "AllPoetry"

As i sit here and look up at the star filled sky... 
a million thoughts of you flow through my mind. 
I make a wish on the brightest one, 
and pray to god that he will grant me 
this wish of you to be with me. 
Although i know inside 
that you're to good for a girl like me, 
i still hope that maybe one day 
you'll look at me the same way i do you. 
If only you knew what it is you do to me. 
At the site of you i feel my heart race, 
and i forget to breathe, and when i know you're near 
my knees get weak and its hard for me to even speak. 
I want so badly to be able to tell you all that it is i feel for you, 
but im to afraid of what you may say 
so instead im writing this poem, 
praying non stop that this wish i make everynight 
will one day become reality so i can have my chance 
to hold you close to me and feel the warmth of your body next to mine
