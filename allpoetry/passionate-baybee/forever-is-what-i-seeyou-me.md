Forever is what I see

I see forever in you and me
Beacause in my heart I know
You're all I'll ever need

In your eyes I can see
My very own destiny

Destined to be

Forever, you and me

I love you,
With all that I'am
And all I will ever be

Babe, I'm so glad 
To have you
As a part of my life
And you could never know
How happy it makes me 
To know, that you one day
Want me to be your wife

So forever is, 
What I will always see
Because I will always
Believe in you and me.
