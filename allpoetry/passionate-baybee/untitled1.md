I loved the way you touched me 
late at night lying next to me. 
You held me close which gave me the strength 
to open the door to my heart that at one point felt so sore, 
and almost impossible to ignore. 
Just when i thought i had lost love forever 
along came another. 
The one who made me feel good 
The one who made me feel great 
The one who brought me joy 
but in the end made me feel used and played with like a toy. 
You left me all alone after you promised you'd always stay. 
I still don't know what it was i did 
to make you leave me like you did on that day. 
I hoped and i prayed 
that you would come back to me one day, 
and it hurts me so bad to say that, 
that never happened in any way. 
Now i realize that theirs nothing left for me to do, 
but let you go and stop denying to myself 
that me and you are definetly through.
