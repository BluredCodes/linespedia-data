---
title: "~*Please Give My Heart Back*~"
writer: "PaSsIoNaTe BaYbEe"

Lies, lies, lies 
Is all I'm told
I'm tired of being left
Out in the cold

Don't know what to do
Don't wanna have to let go
But don't want to live like this
My whole life long
Always questioning and wondering
What I'm doing wrong

So afraid of being alone
And not having anyone to hold

I'm to damn young 
To keep having to be put through all this

Why do I have to be to in love to let go?

Take anything you want
Keep all the love 
I so willingly gave

Just please give my heart back
And stop making me suffer 
With all this heartache and pain

I can't keep doing this
Baby I love you sooo much
But I'm going totally insane

So keep my innosence 
And the purity I gave to you
All I'm asking is PLEASE
Give my heart back
And let me be free
