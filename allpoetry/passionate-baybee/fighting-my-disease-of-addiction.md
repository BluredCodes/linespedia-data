My mind is swrilling, I feel like I'm twrilling.I'm fighting temptation, But no long know if that is truly what I want.I want to give in...I want to give up!I want to say fuck it...so I can go get high and drunk.I'm torn between myself.I want to be clean!At the same time I want to go out partying,God knows I'm only 17!Where do I go?What do I do?Meetings aren't helping, I feel so alone.Lord please help meHelp me fight my drug addiction and the demon of my disease.Help me please!!!
