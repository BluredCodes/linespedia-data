Just as before
I gave and I gave
Only to have you
Walk out on me
And slam shut my hearts door
Once more.
~*~*~*~*~
I was 15 then
A weak little girl
I'm older
More mature and wise 
This time
I'm stronger then I was before
With more power and will to survive
More able to get on with my life.
~*~*~*~*~
I told you last-night
I'm not gonna run
Nor will I hide
I want and need you
And I'm more then ready
To give it all I've got
And put up a fight.
~*~*~*~*~
Three years ago
I just stood around
And let you escape
Without a sound
This time I'm not giving up
You better believe
I'm gonna stand my ground.
~*~*~*~*~
If your not sure 
You can be with me right now
Then your friendship
Would be nice
At least 
That you know I deserve.
~*~*~*~*~
I'm not asking for much here
I'm only asking...
Mike please in some way
Stay a part of my life!This is for the love of my life Michael.
