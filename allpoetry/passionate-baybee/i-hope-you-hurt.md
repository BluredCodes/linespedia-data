title: "'I Hope You Hurt'"
writer: "PaSsIoNaTe BaYbEe"

I can't believe i allowed myself to trust you like i did.

I know i wasn't the first 
and positive i won't be the last girl you use,
but some day someone is going to do to you
what you've done to me.

I hope you one day fall in love so deep,
you don't know what to do
an i pray she'll tear your heart right in two.

Then maybe you'd be able to understand and see
how i felt when you did it to me.

I hope you hurt so bad,
you learn people are not toy's
nor are hearts.

Maybe then you'll think twice 
when it comes to hurting someone again
