I love you
Baby you know how much i do.
You claim to love me too
So why are you forever hurting me like you do?
~*~*~*~*~
Always lying
And Caniving.
Why can't you just be real
What the fuck is your deal?
~*~*~*~*~
I can't continue going on like this.
Can't keep playing these games
Day after day.
~*~*~*~*~
When you look at me can you ever see
The pain that lies deep within my eyes?
When you touch me can you ever feel
All the hurt I try so hard to keep hidden inside?
~*~*~*~*~
I'm no longer blind
Or to nieve to see
That no matter how many times you promise
Things will not be different.
They will never change
~*~*~*~
