---
title: "~*~*The Pain You Made Me Feel*~*~"
writer: "PaSsIoNaTe BaYbEe"
slug: "the-pain-you-made-me-feel"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/1694-----The-Pain-You-Made-Me-Feel-----by-PaSsIoNaTe-BaYbEe"

I feel as though my heart is being ripped from my chest 
and thrown on the floor. 
All my hopes and dreams are being shattered 
and thrown out the door. 
My life was complete but not anymore, 
my heart was once whole but now it is torn, 
it has been ripped right in half 
and stabbed with a thorn. 
I'm in so much pain, and no matter what 
the pain will always remain. 
Hurting and crying, 
screaming and sore. 
You are the love of my life and my hearts deepest desire. 
I feel as though im trapped behind a locked door. 
I cry an i cry, 
but iam ignored. 
You were my heart and soul, 
i willingly trusted you with my heart, 
not thinking you would ever break it. 
I never knew what love could do until i found you, 
and i never knew what pain could do until i met you! 
I hope and pray my heart never has to come this way again.
