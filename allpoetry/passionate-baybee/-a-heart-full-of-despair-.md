' A Heart Full Of Despair '


A heart full of despair
Seemingly impossible to bare.
Forever feeling alone,
Like an empty ghostly soul.

Not knowing what to do,
Nor where to go.

Just knowing you feel utterly alone.
Always questioning,
If what you are about to do is right.
Worried and full of fright,
Awaiting the next lonely night
When you lay your head down to sleep
Anticipating the morning light.

Hoping the dawn of a new day
Might bring just an ounce 
Of actual delight!
