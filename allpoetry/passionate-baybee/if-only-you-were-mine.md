Every now and then i wonder what it would be like if i could be with you. I wonder what it would be like to feel your body against mine, with you holding me tight in your arms. If i could touch you... i surely would. If i could touch your lips with mine... i'd do it in no time. If only you were mine... I'd hold you close for a lifetime, if ever i had the chance. I swear all it takes is a glance and it's like i'm stuck in a trance. I just love your eyes and the way you smile. I'm tellin ya it's more than enough to drive me wild. You make it so hard for me to keep my sanity. Looking at you makes my tummy tingle, cause i know inside that somehow, some way, some day... i've got to make you mine.
