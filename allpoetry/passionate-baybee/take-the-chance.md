---
title: "*~*~*Take The Chance*~*~*"
writer: "PaSsIoNaTe BaYbEe"

Seize the moment for all it's worth, waste no time in telling that special someone just how you feel deep inside about them, for you never know when even the best of things will find an end. People come and go so be sure you let them know just how you feel. Tell them today just in case that tomorrow never comes. So many times we hide the things we know we want...need, and should say, and all to often we regret not doing so. So take that chance and open up no matter how stupid you feel, don't be shy say what it is you have in your heart... and on your mind
