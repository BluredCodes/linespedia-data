My love for you comes from deep within.
It's pure and true,
I don't even know where to begin.
I wish i could just come right out and say it,
But if i did...
Would you okay it?

I'm not usually this much of a coward,
But when it comes to you...
I lose my will, and all the power.
So tell me how to share with you...
These feelings from deep within?

Tell me,
Is there any chance that you feel the same?
Or would i be left wallowing in misery and pain?
Please tell me,
What if...
I came forth and confessed to you...
My love from deep within?This poem was chosen for publication in a book due out this summer...also it was picked to be put on a CD.
