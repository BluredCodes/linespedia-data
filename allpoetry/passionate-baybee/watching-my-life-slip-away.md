title: "*~*Watching My Life Slip Away*~*"

My life is only a breath away 
and without your love i have no reason to stay. 

Alone in my pain and so scared of fear, 
i scream out for you wishing somehow you'd hear. 

I see me still fighting deeply for life, 
but then you leave me alone 
and it pierces my heart like a knife. 

I feel my world fading slowly in front of my eyes 
as i listen to my deep and painful cries. 

Alone in my blood as i stare up above 
and wish only that you would've saved me 
with your warm, gentle love.
