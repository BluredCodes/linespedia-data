---
title: "*~*In My Dreams*~*"
writer: "PaSsIoNaTe BaYbEe"

In my dreams 
i can feel you, you see.
In my dreams 
you become my reality.
The longing inside for you to be mine, 
is gone away at this point in time.
Because in my dreams you're here with me, 
you're not just a fantasy.
In my dreams i can feel your love.
Up and down my body with your hands you rub.
I feel i could fly like a dove,
soaring the skies so high up above.
What you mean to me, 
obviously you can't see.
But here in my dreams you can see, 
that the two of us were meant to be.
