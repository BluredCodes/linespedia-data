---
title: "~*~*I Promise*~*~"
writer: "PaSsIoNaTe BaYbEe"
slug: "i-promise"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/1700-----I-Promise-----by-PaSsIoNaTe-BaYbEe"

You've touched a place so deep within my heart, 
that no one else has ever reached or touched before. 
The depth of my love for you 
i promise is so real and sincere. 
I never thought for a moment 
that love could feel this great, 
but now that i know i'm going to do everything 
that i can to make sure it never escapes. 
Since day one i already knew 
that i was hopelessly in love with you. 
If only you could feel exactly what it is i feel 
when you touch me or hold me near. 
There's no one in this world 
that i could care for more than i do you, 
and thats a promise i can garantee you 
is deffinatly true, 
because i can honestly say 
that i really, truly do....love you!
