A precious smile 
And a tender kiss.

When you're far away
These are a few of the things
I really miss.

Moments of fun-filled laughter
Sweet talks of 
Living happily ever after.

Oh how I hate
When it comes time
For you to leave me
All over again.

I can't wait for the day
When your going away
Finally ends.
