She’s not at all

Bashful, my sweet little girl

     Sometimes she’s 

Rowdy and loud 

     As I watch her, she

Enchants me with her

Ability to mother her ‘babies’ with such

Natural ease

     She brushes their hair, and

Nurtures them all then lovingly

Admires each one as they sleep

     Completely unaware 

     of how lucky she is

     that her babies never grow up.
