Wordsmith, woo me with your wares.
Waft words of woe that make me weep.
Then croon away my childish cares
With lullabyes that make me sleep.

Wordsmith, weave me wondrous tales.
Send seas of stories without cease
Of storm-tossed ships in whipping gales,
Epic sagas of war and peace.

Hypnotize my soul and senses.
Realize my dreams and fears.
Rationalize my mind's defenses.
Tantalize my waiting ears.

Wordsmith, vow ne're to depart.
Sing sonnets sweet, of life and death.
Caress this hapless, humble heart
Within your constant rhythmic breath.May 2001,
