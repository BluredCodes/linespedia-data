---
title: "Forever"
writer: "Jackie"
slug: "forever"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/5288-Forever-by-Jackie"
---

Take a chance, catch my eye,Tell me you're my kinda guy,Walk this way, say hello,Prove me wrong from all I know,Say you're the man, the one I need,The one without I'm not complete,Take this hand, hold it tight,Never let it go tonight,Touch my heart, make me smile,Tell me this will be worth while,Say the words I wanna hear,Whisper gently in my ear,Only love could feel this way,It's suddenly so very clear,Call me up, Ask me out,Tell me all that you're about,Send a card, with a rose,Tell me I'm the one you chose,Keep me warm, keep me yours,Tell me I'm worth fighting for,Take one knee, Look in my eyes,Make me almost start to cry,With this ring, I thee wed,All these words to me you pled, With me you want to spend your life,Lovingly as man and wife,Steal my heart like long ago,Say you'll never let me go,Tell me I'm your only one,Forever in the days to come.
