---
title: "Blue Eyes"
writer: "bartman"
slug: "blue-eyes"

---

I see him
vividly and vaguely
at the same time

In plaid patterned pants,
In baseball caps.
He has risen like Jesus

And died like Spring
each in tiny parts.
Sounds surround ears, then fall away,

Eyes hold eyes for that one second,
create a memory
The combination of a physical look

And an electric jolt inside.
Your roses bloom, then wilt,
Look down at themselves, self conscious 

As I am now,
Under your stare,
Aware

Of a certain amount 
Of beauty lost,
Yet love endures

Like distant thoughts
Of eyes as blue
As the summer is long.
