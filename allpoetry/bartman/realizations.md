Oh meWith my Silly deceptionAnd my Happy masochism.The perfectSpecimenWith a bright gold toothAnd a diamond stud,You pushand pullTo gain controlInvisiblyAround me,Without real effort.And i retreat NowWith disdainCan't hardly stomachYour breath,You swine.Sleep easyNowIf you can,Knowing what you've done.
