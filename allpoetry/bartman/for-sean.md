title: "For Sean"

Sometimes we get close-
too close for me to breathe
But i can breathe you in
if only i believe-

That a jealous sun
In her lovely sky
Could refuse one day to go down-

That nothing ever
Begins or ends
And can't be lost or found-

That a million stars
From a midnight sky
Could break at any moment-

That you and I
When we're standing here
Could end a whole world's torment.
