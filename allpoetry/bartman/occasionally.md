Occasionally I look on you
Without my solemn reserve
In a place where time stands still
And I forget to breathe
But dwell upon a beauty
And live inside a paradise
Delusions disappear
And clarity presides
Inside this other realm
Of silent words and light,
I escape reality
And experience perfection.
