---
title: "I still think on you"
writer: "bartman"

I can remember a time
Was long ago indeed.
When strength lived in this body,
Before the dawn of darkness
And before the presence of sadness,
Beauty was predominant.
Where once there lived such greatness,
Youth has been laid to rest.
But I still think on you
As softly as I ever did.
Can still feel your breath upon me
Painful as it may be.
And here inside this room,
Sorrow lays beside me.
During sun's rise and set
I remember you most clearly.
