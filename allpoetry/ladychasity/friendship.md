This friendship is hard,
And sometimes rough.
But you never leave my side,
When the going gets tough.
I hope this friendship 
Lasts forever.
And you give up,
On me, never.
I want to thank you,
From the bottom of my heart.
For being there for me,
Right from the very start.
This poem doesn't say,
Exactly how I feel.
But I feel like our friendship,
Will always be real.
