---
title: "Love Sucks Sometimes"
writer: "LadyChasity"
slug: "love-sucks-sometimes"

You told me you would love me and never let me go.
But now I realize that it wasn't so.
I guess you were lying and really didn't love me.
But I still feel like we are truly meant to me.
I know you don't feel the same way,
But this gets harder and harder every day.
Now I need to move on with my life.
I want you to know you have caused so much strife.
If you change your mind and want me back.
I will take you but there is something our life will lack.
I don't know if i could trust everything you say,
Because all you did was take my heart and play.
If you want it you can have it back,
But don't make even one little crack.
For now i will sit and think of how it used to be,
When you would always say you love me.
I need to tell you I found someone new.
But she will never amount to you.
