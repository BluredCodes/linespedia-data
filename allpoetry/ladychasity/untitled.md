I love you and you know it's true.I just want to be with you.Every night and every day,I want to be with you in every way.I hope you realize my love it true,And I hope you can say you love me too.My days are filled with loneliness and fear,I would feel much better if you were here.I want to show you how much you mean to me.Always, with you, I want to be.
