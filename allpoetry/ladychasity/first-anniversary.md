Today is a day I will never forget.
It's been a year since we first met.
I even remember that first phone call.
You didn't even know why you did it at all.
My friends thought you were crazy,
For wasting your time on me.
But now I think they know,
We are truly meant to be.
Now I want to thank you from the bottom of my heart.
For being there for me, right from the very start.
I just can't wait till the day we meet.
Every time I think of you, my heart skips a beat.
My love for you is strong, and grows stronger everyday.
And now I have one thing to say,
Happy First Aniversary.
