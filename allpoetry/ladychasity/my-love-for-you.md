---
title: "My Love for You"
writer: "LadyChasity"
slug: "my-love-for-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I can't hide my love for you,
Although I often tried.
I guess I love you a little too much,
I always want you by my side.
I wish you'd know,
Just how I feel.
I feel like my love,
For you is real.
I want to hold you close,
And never let you go.
Because baby,
I love you so.
I want to be,
With you till the end.
And all eternity,
We shall spend...
Happy times, sad time,
Heartaches, and cries.
I hope my love for you,
Never dies!
