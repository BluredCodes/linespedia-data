---
title: "conventional end"
writer: "lost child"
slug: "conventional-end"

invisible tender glance
a vague strange senseless sensation
of unskinned feeling
perhaps something dissappeared
before painfully sick 
nerve endings saw
the haze-filled sinking
as this familiar farewell ripped 
into kiss-less goodbyes
