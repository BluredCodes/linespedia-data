title: "woolgather's accrue"
writer: "lost child"

a star-filled apparition,
she's found dreams of Gathering influence.
moon-drenched,
her senses are swept.

in the depths she flies
a Perpetual angled dance of serene silence

and falls.i frequently dream of this woman, but there is no saving her...
