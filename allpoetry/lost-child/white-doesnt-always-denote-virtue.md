title: "white doesn’t always denote virtue."
writer: "lost child"

you lift the oranges and greens
of your drunken muddled spectrum
try them on for size
do your best to find a hold
(that fits your state of mind)
the light shines differently here you say?

these balls are not the sort that bounce
break toes they do, when you lose your grip
(and heads and souls)
leave your cavernous prints and coloured dust 
and i’m content just to see your teeth
