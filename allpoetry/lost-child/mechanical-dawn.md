he sits beneath a blue-white glare
reading "help for the hurting"
and a fat book of plays.

he swallows too bitterly
(a sentiment well suited
 for a sunrise so bland)
and stirs in his sugar, asleep;

while i lap up his S's and T's.

i can taste when i swallow today
how corroded this Peace has become.this will probably shift and grow, but whatever.
