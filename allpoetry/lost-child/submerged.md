Let me drift in sunwarmed waters 
the colour of your eyes

submerged

anchor me there in your arms
i'll surface 
for air and light kisses
and savour the taste of your smilei had the strangest sensation one day while i was nestled in arms...  i felt as though i was suspended... floating in warm water.  i started this poem in my mind then, but i never put it to paper until today.
