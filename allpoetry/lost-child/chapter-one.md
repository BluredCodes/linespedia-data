i wedged myself 
against a sliver of sky
and tried my best to kiss the rain
but the rain 
would have none of my advances

i feigned innocence and 
ran naked in the woods
chasing my first kiss

i held fireflies in my hand
held them as if they were stars
until he bid me let them go

and he followed them
into the darkthis one is dedicated to Adam Cidade, whose name i have never been able to spell correctly.  it's a lot of personal reflections on my 'first love', nothing more.
