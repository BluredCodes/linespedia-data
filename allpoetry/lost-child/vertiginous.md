there are too many 
colours. 
the carpet holds the universe
celestial bodies pressed brightly 
to be trampled beneath 
sticky feet. 
and blue neon lights 
no they're not neon truely
i know
but they're blue and bright 
and twisting 
around the ceiling 
as though they're about to 
coil 
and 
attack.i've been having problems with colours and patterns for the last week... they make me feel dizzy and as though i'm on hallucinogens.
