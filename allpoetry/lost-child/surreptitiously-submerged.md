title: "surreptitiously submerged"

he softly drowned
dancing in secrets' circle
amid dark white smiles

seeking but a breath of
voiceless nighti gave up on that dance, the rhythm didn't suit me... but there are many more casualties than can ever be counted...  my baby brother being one of them
