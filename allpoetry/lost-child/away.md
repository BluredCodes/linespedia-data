the taxi comes to take us 
(away).
and there is a valve on the back 
of my head 
no my temple 
the left one 
and it's a valve 
like on a great blue balloon. 
someone's blowing. 
smooth and steady, the breath is 
almost sweet. 
i suspect my head will fill 
the entire cab 
in but a moment. 
but i can feel the seam now
where the air 
escapes 
and leaves my skull feeling 
stretched 
and 
weighted. 
i stare at the grey of the ceiling 
and it feels much further 
(away) 
than it is, 
with much 
more 
depth.the last of "progressive insanity".
i broke them up because they grated on me.
