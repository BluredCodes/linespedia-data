title: "absolution (behind glass)"
writer: "lost child"

you'll break Peace
and the china that 
we keep
(admire on shelves 
but never touch)
with your loud fire
you rule today
but never touch 
the stars 
in the back of my
head
or my inner breathall he can do is stare as i take flight.
