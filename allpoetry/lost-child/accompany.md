I’m damaged
I’m damaged
she wears me like a crown
accesorize her iron chains
that aid her while she drowns

I’m peeling
I’m peeling
and we’re sipping on red wine
from tiny crystal amulets 
she’s slinking down my spine

it's hazy
it's hazy
her methods are unsound
these dreams have caught the gear train yet
our minds are twisting ‘roundthis is how to hold your tongue while shovelling the snow.  my mind is caught in umpty-bumps, it's frustrating as hell.
