title: "possession."
writer: "lost child"

I would have your lips
if not your eyes,

your hands
if not your head.

guard your thought, you may
if i may have your deed.

i would have now.
yes, i would have now.

and we sit time upon her alter,
and we worship her with knives,

pray with naked bodies
and shackled minds.

but i'll have today.
i could breathe for today.

and i'll have what i'll have,
if you would just call it mine.
