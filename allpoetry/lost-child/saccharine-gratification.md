---
title: "saccharine gratification"
writer: "lost child"
slug: "saccharine-gratification"

from shadows she passes glances
over ill illuminated caramel faces
and sucking,
sinking 
skin.
she clutches her concrete rock lollipop 
as her tongue goes rolling off
of the painted bittersweet smiles.
