MIND BODY CONNECTED

Heart hanging on like a
pocket full of loose change
aimed for a cherished liquor bottle
destined for a dusty shelf
saved for that rainy day
mind like binary fusion
absorbing all heard and seen
confusion is the theme
nothing seems real
humankind at the bazaar
no rhyme, no reason
what is our reality?
life waits for no one
heart crying out loud
mind guilt by association

MJPease

THE EARTH THE SON and THE MOON
