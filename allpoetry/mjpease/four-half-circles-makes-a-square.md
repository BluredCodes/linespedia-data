FOUR HALF CIRCLES MAKES A SQUARE

Sun changed poles today
Earth is way over due
Moons position more distant
Son changed poles today

North up Memorial Drive
Winding high to White Face
Impervious to Wind and Rain
Basking in Shades of Mind

Adagio for strings, Rhapsody in Blue
Gazing at the Two Beautiful Flowers
A little Morrison and Hendix too
Balancing Destiny

The Foot of West Ferry
Pondering what the Dinosaurs Did Wrong
Did I tell you about the Constellations
The Son changed poles today

MJPease

THE EARTH THE SON and THE MOON
