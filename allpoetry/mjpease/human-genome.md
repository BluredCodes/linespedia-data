HUMAN GENOME

Wandering down this genetic trail
eventually becoming so frail
fountain of youth the goal of the day
but who will be able to pay

A promise of life ever after
amazingly with out fear of disease
designer drugs insuring vigor and looks
a frozen egg and a pocket full of genes

No need for that elaborate family desired
necessity nothing more than a warm womb
a mere imitation of life, lets hope they see
or maybe the modern day Noahs Ark, or our

Salvation preparing to get on its way?

MJPease
