BALANCING DESTINY

Need to scream
remove my clothes 
shed my skin
where do I begin

Cold the river flowed 
as you jumped in
you took control
ending your life

I hear you calling
from the other side
maybe the dark side
maybe the right side

You passed on 
ending your search
you passed the torch
that makes me search

Spreading your ashes
you flew in the breeze
recklessly you landed
on your fathers grave

Winding on down
this genetic trail
unable to avoid 
becoming so frail

Wizards and Demons
man of extremes
chemical imbalance
or so it seems

Talk to me now 
as you have in the past
the need to know 
will this day last

MJPease

THE EARTH THE SON and THE MOON
