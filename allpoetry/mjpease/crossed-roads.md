CROSSED ROADS

A tangled traffic past
stalled in the itersection of life
tormented by the yellow flashing light
as tangled traffic passed

A freight train of life, joy and fears
with out caution, loud and powerful
throwing caution to the wind
unwilling to get out of the way

Lost for words, left in a state of confusion
my lifes work, lingers in slow decay
as I reached for the blue light
memories extinguished by electricity

Someone tampered with my destiny
standing in the intersection of life
comforted by the yellow flashing light
watching all the tangled traffic pass.

MJPease

THE EARTH THE SON and THE MOON
