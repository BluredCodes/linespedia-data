Moon turned to the Sun
Moon smiled and then backed away
Earth nodded in total agreement
Sun warmed the Earth with great expectations

Earth's heart began to warm
Polar caps began to melt
Rivers rose to meet the Sea
Sea gave birth to intelligent life

Balance of nature was pure
Dinosaur began to roam the Earth
Heaven smiled for a while
Moon turned into the Sun

Fire rained down on Earth
Earth cried and succumbed to fever
Volcanoes erupted simultaneously
Ash rose into space blocking the Sun

Intelligent life sought refuge in the Sea
Earth's fever began to break into a chill
Earth plunged into a deep freeze
Mountains of expanding ice split the crust

Moon turned to the Sun
Moon smiled and then backed away
Earth nodded in total agreement
Sun warmed the Earth with great expectations

MJPease
