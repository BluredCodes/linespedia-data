BALANCING DESTINY / part two

With no urn for you ashes
no sarcophagus made of stone
your ashes laid naked in despair
exposed to the frigid night air

I returned this past March
as we laid Gram to her final rest
eyes searching for the past, remnants
of your ashes, left four years before

Why did you decide it was time to depart?
the loudest guitars can't calm my heart
visited the foot of West Ferry on this solemn day
the place the slaves escaped to freedom they say

Suffering from destiny's indelible stamp, pondering the freedom
you searched for that day
why is it that so many brilliant minds
wander from this Earth before their time?

Standing on the edge of the mighty Niagara
the powerful spell you must of been in
mysteries of the river as it doodles in my head
reaches into the mind and attempts to pull me in

You come across as one with such a cold heart
I know this isn't true so I'm left here to ponder
you had to know what you were doing that day
the mighty Niagara leaves no room to play

MJPease
