---
title: "The light within"
writer: "Channa"
slug: "the-light-within"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/14186-The-light-within-by-Channa"
---

This early dawn
still in my night gown
I look out of my window
heavy clouds hang low 
gray fog and dreary skies
greet my sleepy eyes
yet the Son shine
brightly in my soul
I feel no emptiness
no fog can evanesce
the light that is within
