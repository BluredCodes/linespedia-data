---
title: "loving  you."
writer: "Channa"
slug: "loving-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/10892-loving--you.-by-Channa"
---

I want to love you with a perfect love
a pure sweet love coming from above
loving you with all my being
holding nothing from you,
this love to you, I bring
loving you unselfishly 
giving of myself
totally, faithfully
with my body my soul
whole heartily
loving you with no restraint
more love for you, I want to gain
I want to love you during the day
helping you each step along the way
loving you thru the night
your arms around me  holding me tight
I want to love you with a passion
filled with desires, no logic or reason
Loving you with a perfect love
eros, agape, all encompassing love!
