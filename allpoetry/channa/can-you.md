---
title: "Can you..."
writer: "Channa"

Can you...
Taste the salt from the tears I cry
As you just said your last goodbye?
Can you...
See the hurt in my chestnut brown eyes
as on me, you chose to walk on by?
Can you...
Feel the ripple of this wretched pain
that you inflicted in my heart again?
Can you...
Hear that agony in my confused mind
screaming for a love that I cannot find?
Can you...
Smell the scent of utter desperation
like sweet flowers cut to destruction
Can  you...
Sense that pure innocent trust
shattered at your feet like diamond dust
Can you...
Imagine the hope laying at my feet
as cold as the icy  rainy crystal sleet
can you...
Hold on to your righteous religion
when you've played so well with my emotions?
Can you?
