title: "Thoughts of love"

Come with me to the fields so greenI will show you things unseenlet us sit under the willow treehold me in your arms gentlyI will lead you to the crystal streamwhere poets wrote and dream their dreamstogether we will be freetaste of my soft lips, kiss mewe will run in the meadowup way above the world belowunderneath the night canopyyou will make sweet love to meunder the stars up in the skywe will never have to say goodbyeI will lay my head on your shoulderour two hearts will be one forever.
