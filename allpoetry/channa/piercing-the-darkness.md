title: "Piercing the darkness."
writer: "Channa"

I can feel his evil gray eminence
from not so far a distance
now I am able to fully see
the grotesque form of my enemy
crouching like a gargoyle in the corner
his eyes color of red hot amber
he is motionless, studying me
his prey I am sure to be
he has come to claim my soul
years of sins has taken their toll
a single prayers I cannot utter
still...I must fight and not surrender.
The flapping of his leathering wings
send a frozen chill to my inner being
but I know that my mind
to him is not yet bind
An unspeakable terror has seize me
I cannot give in  to the enemy
with a last effort in my part
to the King, I now give my heart

Another presence is drawing nigh
with a radiant light from on high
swords appear out of nowhere
thundering clashes fills the air
a bright angel and a dark one spar
the bright one calls on the Morning Star
to bring healing in His wing
for me, a mere human being.
My mortal foe is defeated
thick gray smoke has dissipated
the angel wings bellow over me
envelopping me, forming a healing canopy
His words in my soul are etched
I'm no longer a sinner, a wretch
all my past has been forgiven
when to the King, my heart was given.Idea for this piece, was taken from the book "This present darkness", writer Frank Perretti.
