---
title: "New born"
writer: "Channa"
slug: "new-born"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/7495-New-born-by-Channa"
---

Sweet little one, when you look at me
with your big brown eyes, what do you see?
you're a miracle of life, perfect mystery
in your still wet, shivering body
Brand new, pure, so innocent
you're an unwarranted gift, a present
do you see the look of pride and enchantment
as I look at you in this very instant
your tiny head rests upon my breast
from it you drink nature's very best
can you sense the overwhelming love
bestowed to me from G-d above
Do you feel the same kind of wonder
as your tiny hand grasps my finger
do you see the look of contentment
drinkink with my eyes this very moment
Welcome to this world of discovery
My little wonder, my brand new baby
