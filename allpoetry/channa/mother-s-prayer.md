---
title: "Mother' s prayer"
writer: "Channa"

source: "AllPoetry"

How can I teach my child not to break my heart
Not saying unkind words that would tear me apart
How can I teach my child by wisdom and reason
without criticizing not wanting confrontation
how can I teach my child all about compassion
when she seems so bound toward self destruction
how can I raise my child to learn from her mistakes
balancing all things, learning to give, not to take
How can I give my child a sense of morality
without blame, no shame, just trust and honesty
How can I make her see that life is sacred
G-d's given, not to be abused, but to be guarded
how can I presuade my child to live to her fullest
for the glory of G-d and for His faithfulness
how can I protect my child from a cruel society
Thru prayers and petitions to G-d Almighty
these are questions I ask myself often
The answers I do find, when I look toward Heaven.
