As I look around me, I'm in awe anew
The glory, O my G-d, belong's to only You
rainbows of soft delicate hue
summer skies of azure blue
butterfly wings transparent and light
strong enough to carry their flight
delicate flowers, blossoming in spring
emanating scent, fit for a king
the vastness of the ocean so grand
could only come by the work of Your hand
the warmth of the sun, moonlight at night
You've created, so we could see Your Might
stars in a cloudness night
countless in number, shinning so bright
rain in the summer, snow in the winter
declaring Your Glory forever
the majestic crown of creation belongs
to us, men and women, alone
You are a Father to those who seek Your face
and they can find You any place
No, I can't believe in evolution,
heaven proclaim's Your creation
there is so much evidence
of Your loving providence
"All" was good in Your perfect way
whe are the one who sinned, 
the one who went astray
I have looked in my geology tree
and for a father, didn't not see a monkey
"Charlie" maybe you thought too much
you wanted recognition and such
now our children are left with your theory
from one cell evolved all these species?
Fairy tale for adult,... tragic fantasy!it has a little bit of humor at the end, when I tell "Charlie"
(Charles Darwin)that my geology tree doesn't contain a "monkey"  :)
