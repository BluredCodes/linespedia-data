title: "Thoughts of the air.  (Somewhat humorous)"
writer: "Channa"

By the window, there I remain
my thoughts went out of it again
to join others in the air
in the spirit of the atmosphere
my thoughts conspire against me
they ran away and are set free
to compose their sweet poetry
under the heavenly canopy
thoughts of the air are gathered
by angel wings, they're so many
then later they return to me.
So next time I sit by the window sill
I will be quiet, I will sit still.
