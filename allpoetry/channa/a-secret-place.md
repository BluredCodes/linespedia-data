When your heart is breakingand the tears won't stop fallingTake my hand I will lead youwhere the sky is always blueleave your sea of tears behindthrow your trouble to the windcross the threshold of you mindlook within, there you will finda secret place that only youcan expand and make anewyou will learn to fly up highyour spirit will touch the skyyou will find seneritymoonlight will light up your wayfor the beauty of this placewill dry up your tear stained facea new love is there waitingif only you are willing to step into its realmit's soft glow will be a balmthat will heal your wounded souland guide you out of the shadowfrom the lies and cruelty of our cold society
