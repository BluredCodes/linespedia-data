---
title: "Broken pieces"
writer: "Channa"
slug: "broken-pieces"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8914-Broken-pieces-by-Channa"
---

In an epidemic proportion
all across our nation
our children are the price
we're willing to sacrifice
when mothers and fathers 
who will not stay together
our children pay for what we doing
Our children are the broken pieces 
when our home falls apart, 
yes children are the broken pieces
Who's going to mend their tender hearts?
our future generation
knows only hurt and sorrow
our children are neglected
too often rejected
our lack of compassion
for our most treasure possession
Our children are the broken pieces
when our home falls apart
yes children are the broken pieces
who's going to mend their tender hearts?
The death of morality
for the god of society
working for better condition
our children are in confusion
looking for advancement
we know no commitment
our children are the broken pieces 
when our home falls apart
yes, children are the broken pieces
who's going to mend their tender hearts?
we need moral values
it's up to me and you
our children are the voice
we have to make the choice
if we listen to their plea
their cry, their tragedy
our children are the broken pieces
we will mend their tender hearts.
