And it kept on falling..tiny snow flakes dancinghugging the ground softlyenshrouding the stark cityAnd it kept on falling..on my shoulders landingfrom the light of the city streetto the sidewalk underneath my feetAnd it kept on falling..beautiful, enchantingoverlaying the dark groundserene scene without a soundAnd it kept on falling..never ceasing not abatinglooking upwards to the skiesan eerie glow captured my eyesAnd it kept on falling..beneath the pure shroudingthe starkness of the old oak treewithout its leaves, looks ghastlyAnd it kept on falling..all thru the night to the morningcoating the window sillmade me shiver, gave me chill!
