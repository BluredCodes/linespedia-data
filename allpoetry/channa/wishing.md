---
title: "Wishing..."
writer: "Channa"
slug: "wishing"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Would I, like a spider trap you in my domain
Webbing treads of silk instead of chain?
Could I hold you in my soft embrace
with my finger tips, your body I would trace
could I take possession of your soul
wanting to bask in it's sweet hold
could I make you prisoner of my love
carrying you to the farthest cloud above?

but...I don't want to tie you down
I only wish for your sweet love now
time is spent, time is ebbing
a web of love, I am spinning
I want you free to roam and play
I love you much more this way
but...if I could, Would I?...Would you?

Could we make love in the morning dew
making one flesh instead of two?
could we ride in butterfly wings
astonished at their design?
could we dance under the midnight skies
just with the truth, stomping all lies?

We could bathe in the moonlight
you and I, in this magical night
if I could catch a wishing star
I would keep it in a glass jar
to dream of you when you're not here
wishing only that you were near.
