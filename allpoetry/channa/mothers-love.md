---
title: "Mother's love"
writer: "Channa"
slug: "mothers-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

The love of a mother
soft as a whisper
Steadfast and steady
it does not waiver
she believes in her child
even when he is wrong
She'll give her life for him
her love is so strong,
She is warm security
for the wee new born one
a trusting confidant
when he turns twenty-one,
The love of a mother
will last forever
she will always be there
her love cannot falter
she is a beacon bright
on a dark stormy night
her strong convictions
will lead him to the light
The love of a mother
is patiently waiting
for her child to come home
never criticizing
she is always hopeful
and not ready to end
the circle she started
when that new life began
The love of a mother
as strange as it may be
will give her child courage
faith and self esteem
then, with his new found wings
her child will start to soar
the love of a mother
will be his open door.
