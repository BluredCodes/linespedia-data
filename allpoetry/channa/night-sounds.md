title: "Night sounds"
writer: "Channa"

The night spread herself
like thick black velvet
giving the eye a hint
of soft hues so faint
the night hollow light
magnify the shapes
and the sounds of the night
trees, their limbs reaching
dogs barking, howlings,
leaves on the ground rustling
each step you take, you fasten
in your mind you listen
sweat pours out from your pores
as you turn to see, even more
not a living soul is out tonight
the street has lost it's only light
the storm that passed thru
has knocked down a power plant or two...
