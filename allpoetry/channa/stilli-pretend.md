title: "Still...I pretend..."

I pretend...
you're laying next to me
your loving arms holding me
slowly I turn my head to see
There's no one else, but me..Still..
I pretend...
I press myself so close to you
to make one flesh instead of two
I look hoping to find you near
my lonely heartbeat is all I hear..Still..
I pretend...
your lips softly brush against mine
sending waves of desires sublime
my hand longs to run thru your hair
I find nothing but despair..Still..
I pretend...
your expert hands so lovingly
trace every curves of my body 
I then open my eyes to find
You were only in my mind.
Still..
I pretend...
