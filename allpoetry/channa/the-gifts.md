---
title: "The Gifts"
writer: "Channa"
slug: "the-gifts"

---

There's a power within me
To choose my path, my destiny
Options are mine to take
and I must learn from my mistakes

There's a light within my soul
to guide my mind, to keep whole
this little glow must be so bright
to keep me from stumbling in the night

There's so much love within my heart
I must give it all, not in part
I have to give it all away
to those I see everyday

These gifts are mine to share
with people I meet everywhere
Given to me by G-d above
Yet the greatest of these is..LOVE.
