title: "If I was a queen..."
writer: "Channa"

If I was a queen from a distant land
would I be wearing your wedding band?
or a maiden so fair from the northern Shore
would your heart to me, be an open door?
If a grand castle was my domain
would you kiss away my pain?
and if at night I roamed the marble hall
would your hold me so I wouldn't fall?
If I was a princess so fair
with wild flowers in my hair
would you shower me with love
and take me to your sea shore cove?
would you kiss away my tears
and like magic, chase away my fears?
would you hold me tender and tight
in your arms all thru the night
would you make sweet love to me
and cover me with your body?
But...I am just an ordinary girl
and insults at me, your hurl
take a good look into my eyes
see the wounded heart that cries
I have no crown of jewel upon my head
I wear the crown of womanhood instead
you've torn away a part of me
Yet my soul, forever is free!
