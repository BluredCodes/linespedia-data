---
title: "My sphere"
writer: "Channa"

Dreams often fills my mindreally often intertwinethe world of my imaginationwith the stark realizationthat I could not take youalthough you are now part of meand you share my destinyyou could never comprehendnor could you understandthat my imaginationis filled with emotionsfragile as I may beI possess strength you cannot seewhen I am lost in my dreamsNo one's evil, no one's meanI don't feel anymore painlove there has no restrainthe beauty that fills my eyesis held deep within blue skiesmisty aura helds me boundall is quiet, there is no soundonly for the song of the larkof the squirrel on a tree barkI always smile and I'm happyin my dreams, in my imaginaryCould you ever come with meto my world of fantasy?I don't want to seem selfishand at times I think I wishBut I'm not sure I want you thereFor this is my place, this is my sphere.
