After I've cried a million tearsAfter I've settled all my fearsAfter I've healed this heartfelt painWill I be able to love again?After I put you out of my mindTossing our memories to the windAfter I finally let go of youWill I be able to love anew?After all the sleepless nightsAfter the silence and the frightAfter I calm the storm in my soulwill I conquer your soft shadow?After I truly rationalizeafter my heart realizethat we were never meant to beWill I love again uncondionally?
