---
title: "My humble prayer"
writer: "Channa"

As you would a child, you took me by the hand
Gave me your wisdom, so I could understand
You never walked in front or behind me
You were always right here beside me
You are my friend, faithful and true
Father teach me to love only You
Let my ear recognize the sound of Your voice
Striving, wanting to make the right choice
Be with me for the rest of my life
Don't let me say things that would cause useless strife
Let love be the mantle that I wear
Forgiveness the witness as Your heir
Let others see in me the Light of Your Glory
Keep me in your shadow, striving for purity
I ask of you for the strength to carry
my daily simple tasks without any worry
And when I fall, please pick me up again
Wipe my tears away, remove my heartfelt pain
Heal my broken heart with Your gentle touch
Teach me to be grateful for little as for much
Create in me a heart willing to accept
Those of whom society has deemed rejects
I want to love them, with a heart like yours,
Father I offer you this simple humble prayer.
