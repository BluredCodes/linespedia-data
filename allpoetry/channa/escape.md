---
title: "Escape"
writer: "Channa"
slug: "escape"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/10889-Escape-by-Channa"
---

Where would I go if I could fly
if I could soar way up high
if instead of arms, I had wings
Would I care for finer things?

Where would I go if I could swim
and of you, will I still dream
would the cold water cover me
in the deep green of the sea?

To this earth my legs are bound
and true love I have not found
I want to soar, swim and play
I don't like to feel this way

As I sit and look outside
from the sun I want to hide
from the brightness of it's light
even the sun can't make it right

We are the crown of your creation
You gave us these emotions
then, tell me why there's much pain
for Your creations, please, explain!

Make me an eagle or a dolphin instead
Take this heavy crown from my head
I don't want to feel this pain
I can't stand this hurt again!
