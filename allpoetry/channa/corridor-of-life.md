title: "Corridor of life"

The corridor of life extend beyond our sightstraight and narrow with a flickering lightyet within the wall in this long and narrow waycountless doors can be seen, gates of opportunitywhatever door you open, bewarethe entrance you opt for, can leave you in despairFrom an eagle point of view the corridor is seeneach door is a web that traps a human beingthe corridor of life doesn't look appealingit looks quite boring and it goes on unyieldingbut for those who enters in those alluring doorsplease be careful, you'll stay spiritually poorfor in the corridor of life, the road is often longkeep your steps steady, don't look back, just go onhave your eyes fixed on Him and soon you will findthe efforts were worth it, it purified your mindyou are not alone in this long, yet short journeyI am here to help you, hold my hand on your wayand when our lives end, I know that on that daywe'll be together my friend, thru out eternity.
