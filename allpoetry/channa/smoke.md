It pollutes the lungs and the airand forms an ugly yellow film everywhereit also been said it causes strokesI don't really care, just give me my smokecan't light up in restaurantsnot even 20 feet from public tenantmentsyou add more taxes on it, I'm going brokebut...I will not give up my smokeI will smoke in the streetI will light up after I eatin my home in privacyI will puff away, can't you seeafter great sex, it's the bestMr Clinton, I inhale, do I pass the test? PS: This was written one year ago, I still smoke, but I have cut down somewhat :)
