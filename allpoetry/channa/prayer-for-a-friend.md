---
title: "Prayer for a friend"
writer: "Channa"
slug: "prayer-for-a-friend"

Father I come before your Throne
Humbly yet boldly I stand alone
Father I'm here to ask for a request
My soul at present is at rest
My L-rd I need from you a favor
not for myself, but for another
This precious friend you've given me
will be with me throughout eternity
Greet his mornings with your sweet light
dissipating the darkness of the night
Shower him with blessings from above
Cloak him Father with your mantle of love
wrap him in your strong loving arms
keep the evil one from doing him harm
grant him your strength and your wisdom
to bring the last soul to your kingdom
sprinkle your grace along his way
smoothing the path of his journey
stay with him in the path that he takes
give him mercy for your name sake
be his feet when he is weary
comfort him when he is lonely
let your words be his spear
and the armor that he wears
Give him your heart, your compassion
for your justice, your mission
Encourage him during the day
His doubt and hurt, please take away
Send your angels to guard his life
allowing no one to cause him strife
Craddle him gently and take his fears
with your soft touch, wipe out his tears
I thank you Father for this precious friend
May our friendship grow, now until the end,
Amen.
