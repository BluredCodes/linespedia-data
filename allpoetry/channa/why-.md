title: "Why ?"
writer: "Channa"

Why do I keep on loving
When  know I will be hurting?
I thought I'd conquered these emotions
after many years of no passion
You came into my life 
when I wasn't looking
your loving ways, so enticing!
my mind knew you weren't free
yet my heart couldn't see
I now feel cold and lonely
my heart is crushed and empty
I thought my children had filled the gap
yet thru my heart, you found the map
you wedged yourself so completely
and I let you become a part of me
without a word, you went away
leaving my world a shade of gray
colors no longer sooth my eyes
they fade away as I sense your goodbye
your silence is louder than words
it has the power to crush my world
once more I clench my fist at G-d above
Why can't you send my one true love?
