title: "Love still burning  (Somewhat sensual)"

Propped on an elbow
He drinks in her face
Her hair spread on the pillow
her cheeks flushed from his night embrace
softly he trace her lips with his finger
with her eyes still closed, his name she whispers
Love within him stirs once more
slowly she awakes, deep into his eyes she bores
she sees his passion aflame, she knows her powers
She is his companion, his wife, his lover
her face she offers, her lips does he take
her body still yearns for his sweet intake
she welcome his love, fully, completely
and gives of herself unashamed, totally
Their days will start like everyday before
with a lot of loving behind that bedroom door
The world around then can slowly fall apart
as long as they can share their soul, their heart
Her beauty throughout the years has increased
his life she has enriched, his bed she has graced
the man she vowed to love is laying besides her
she presses herself against him, deliberately, slowly he takes her.
