title: "' Big Girls Don't Cry ?'"

It has been said
Big girls don't cry
and where am I to hide
when the tears silently fall
from all the cruel good-byes
Should I cry inside
smiling all the while
hiding the hurt I feel inside?
I am still a child
somewhat tame, somewhat wild
I can't always hide the pain
I've tried in vain
I don't really care
who sees the scars I bear
I have to be me, as you can see
and can't be molded by society
My eyes reflect my very soul
Kept prisoner in the shadow
SO..if you see me looking up the sky
Don't say a word, don't pity me
Quietly wrap your arms around me
I want to be loved
Not only from above
Don't believe that lie
Because...Big girls DO cry.
