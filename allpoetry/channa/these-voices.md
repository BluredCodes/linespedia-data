---
title: "'These voices'"
writer: "Channa"
slug: "these-voices"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Sometimes I can hear them loud and clear
other times, they're just a muffle in my ear
but regardless of what they say
these voices are here to stay
They remind me of the innocence
of a childhood faint fragrance
the memory of a laughter
tears shed from bruised knees
at times they're a faint blur
still very close to me.
I can hear the clear giggle
of a small girl and her long curls
under a pale straw bonnet
that she wore until sunset
fishing for toads and frogs
for companion, a little dog
caterpillars weaving round a leaf
waiting to die to be set free
lady bugs running up and down.
In a surprise she twirls around
to see a salimander frown
the beauty, the sound of wonder
recorded in these voices forever
enclosed deep in my mind
it is where I often find
the solace I seek today
when my world is in a array
I can hear these small voices
I then make a personal choice
Staring at a star across the lake
this is the decision I make
I will keep them for posterity sake.
