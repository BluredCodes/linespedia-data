---
title: "She is..."
writer: "Channa"

Only fourteen, spirited and carefree
one late evening, she confided in me
"My dad's just remarried, you see
doesn't seem to have time for little old me
my mom is working and obviously too busy"
she asked candidly
"would you be my mommy"?
with her strong spirit, and a heart of gold
she won me over, I didn't have to be told
that with six children of my very own
could I take the time for that little soul
and be there for her, as I do my own?
she had wisdom, way beyond her years
a young teenager with a lot of tears
how could I say no to this little bird
fallen from the nest
she needed some rest
still a fledgling
I took her under my wing
she befriend my daughter
became her sister
was loyal to her
she's always been there.
As she grew older and she moved away
We kept in touch, for her I often prayed
Still her strong spirit has always prevailled
she gained confidence and remained unspoiled
I take great pleasure being around her
So...as I look back....
I have no regrets
I am so glad I've "adopted" her.
