title: "nightly friend"
writer: "Channa"

Welcome again friend of mine
never tardy always on time
you clothe yourself in mystery
and bring peace to the weary

Spreading your mantle of darkness
Bringing to the earth a stillness
dreamers awaits your presence
visions of lost sweet innocence

Fairies dance beneath your sky
mothers sing sweet lullaby
while cherubs sleep peacefully
I will keep you company.

Under your thick canopy
I long forever to be.
