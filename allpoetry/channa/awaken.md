---
title: "Awaken"
writer: "Channa"
slug: "awaken"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/7820-Awaken-by-Channa"
---

As I lay awake in the stark of the night
sleep will not ease my awaking feelings
I am Like a child, expectation running high
my mind has taken me to forbidden dreams
childhood is gone, memories are fading
through my mind eyes I see places of delight
paradise  of beauty, there love is abiding
this world has disappeared, in its place came the night
dreams of you and I by the ocean tide
your hand brush against mine stirring the waves inside
I stare at your eyes, color of the night skies
I can't look at them, too afraid of good-byes
strong urges repressed, resurfaced once more
dorming for so long, they're now knocking at my door
warmth engulfd me, throwing caution to the wind
my flesh cries out, Oh, the power of the mind
ever so subtle never ashamed to restore
unspoken words of love, whispered so long before
time for love, giving of oneself completely
in dreams only, my bed is cold and lonely
Dawn has broken, giving me a moment of respite
Soft rays of sun bathe my pillow with golden lights
I run to the pure river's cleansing stream
I need to wash away this invading dream
Yet I know, tonight I will be in the shadows
and visions of you will encompass my soul.
