The night has a way to mock one's mind
Where reality and fantasy often entwine
In my sleepless nights you are so near
Your sweet whispers I can hear
I can vision you soft caress
a gentle cry my lips express
I long to embrace you
but you're not mine
you slip thru my fingers
like the sands of time
you are the shadow of my desire
and stirs within me the fire
my mind detect your manly scent
torturing me, forgetting the present
oh! the sweet nights of sensual dreams
the make believe, the silent screams
out of my grasp you are my love
You've flown away like a dove
in twilight dreams you do exist
my confused soul, you do habit
night after night, the time is ours
dream after dream I become yours
Your sweet embrace evoke feelings
Buried for so long, now resurfacing
Dreams not tangible, just like vapor
disappearing thru an unseen door
you then return as an uninvited ghost
at nights only, I am your host.
