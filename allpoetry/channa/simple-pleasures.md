---
title: "Simple pleasures"
writer: "Channa"
slug: "simple-pleasures"
source: "AllPoetry"

Running bare foot in the rain on hot summer days
With my hair drenched falling down to my waist
The smell of lilac in the month of may
these are the things that I like best

To cuddle under a blanket on a stormy night
With nothing for light, but a candle bright
Laughing at the dance of the firefly
these are the things that makes me high

Looking at the moon in the firmament
spotting a shooting star, just for a moment
breathing the fresh midnight air
these are the things for which I care

Don't give me jewels, expensive treasures
I'd rather have simple pleasures
Give me the night dreams and the love
that can only come from above.
