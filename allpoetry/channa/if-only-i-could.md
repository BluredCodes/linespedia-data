---
title: "If only I could..."
writer: "Channa"
slug: "if-only-i-could"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/10288-If-only-I-could...-by-Channa"
---

If I could change your yesterday
for a happier tomorrow
if I could beg you to stay
and take away your sorrow...
If I could fetch a pot of gold
at the end of the rainbow
and keep you from getting old...

If I could wipe away your tears
grant you perfect happiness
if I could have you near
for complete blissfulness
if I could take away your pain
the broken heart, the loneliness...

I would do it in a moment!
without a second thought
I would, like an angel descent
to give you hope, without a doubt!
but I too, hurt as you do
For you see, I am human too
But if I could, you bet I would!!

Yet I know, not so far away
There is a certain Someone
He will forgive your yesterday
He'll help you to carry on
He'll give you a better tomorrow
Give you the grace to live today
and wipe away your bitter sorrows...

Don't let self pity keep you on the shelf,
go on living, give of yourself,
forget the past, get out of your shell
by loving others, you'll do your part
let the Son shine brightly in your heart
the love you sought so hard to find
you will see, is there in your mind.

But if I could you know that I would
Make your nights as bright as a summer day
stop the rain from drenching your way
move the mountains if only I could
catch a star, give you a rainbow
I would love you and your tomorrows
If only I could...
