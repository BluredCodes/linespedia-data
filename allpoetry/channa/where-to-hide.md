---
title: "Where to hide?"
writer: "Channa"

Where do you go
where there's no place to hide
when the hurt
is so deep inside
what do you do
when no one's around you
when the tears are falling
down your face cascading?

I've seen you silently cry
said you wanted to die
where would you go
tell me, if you know.
Left up your eyes
to the heavenly skies
seek His mercy
drink in the beauty

He will grant you
a love of life anew
He'll refresh you
like the sweet morning dew
He is forever near
let Him dry up your tears
and there....In the shadow of His wings
I promise you...You will find true healing.
