There's an ocean of tears in this heart of minetears of joy and sorrow often enter-wineit's a secret place only known to mesadness and loneliness always hold the keyno one besides me knows the secret placejust a glimpse of it when looking at my facethis ocean of tears is seen thru my eyesA shade a bit lighter from your many lieshow could someone as small as meshed so many tears to create a seaevery crystal drop contain a storytale of grief broken hope useless tragedyeach night I return to my secret shoreI stare at my ocean thru an unseen dooras I shed one more tear for the loveless nightI've no one to kis me or to hold me tightas this tear drop falls I feel the rippleFrom this ocean right down to my soul.I need help with my English, as in forming the right sentences in the right context.
