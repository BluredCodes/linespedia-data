This was written 2 years ago, when I wasn't 'bulging' as my estranged husband asked me to remarry him, for the third time...LOL...:)Stubborn? who me? Nawwww, only a little too independent, I had to learn to make countless decisions, be the bread winner as well, to support six little mouth, So many times I had resentment toward their father. Yet I still loved him!*******************I kiss the moon night skyand mothers sing lullabyI dance with tiny wee sprywhen I look into your eyesI ride the rainbow soft pastel huesWhisper words like 'I love you'I fear no words of goodbyeswhen I look into your eyesDancing in the warn night rainpretending to ride a toy trainleaving behind the world's lieswhen I look into your eyesDawn sheds her palest lightshe brings along new frightI want to dream of deep blue skywhen I look into your eyesA snow flake kiss my cheekthis morning looks so bleakOn cold satin sheets I lieAnd cannot look into your eyes.
