---
title: "Subtle masquerade"
writer: "Channa"

I wear the mask quite well
I seem happy can you tell?
my face lights up with a smile
But I am cringing all the while.

I am laughing like a child
Not too tamed, not too wild
but looking deep into my eyes
You'll see the tears I cry

I don myself a subtle guise
to the untrained eye I look wise
yet behind the quiet air
I live a life of despair

The choice I opted for
Sent me to an unseen door
I'm trapped by my own demise
tears too often brim my eyes

Yet no one should ever detect
the lack of love and it's effect
I've resigned myself to this road
and for my heart I write this ode.
