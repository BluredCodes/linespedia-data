---
title: "Who am I?"
writer: "Channa"
slug: "who-am-i"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/14723-Who-am-I--by-Channa"

Who am I
that You should care for me
one small crying soul in this galaxy
Who am I
that You should shower me with love
plus a gentle rain comimg from above
Who am I
that You should give me a land
and led my people by Your mighty hand
Who am I
that You should die for me
forgiving all my wrong and setting me free
Who am I
that You've chosen me for the heavenly
to remain with You throughout eternity
L-rd, ...... Who am I?
