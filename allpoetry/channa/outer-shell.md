title: "Outer Shell"
writer: "Channa"

The outer shell that houses my soul
is quite pretty I've been told
I care for it each day
polishing it nicely I must say
everyone notice the outer me
where my spirit is not quite free
but if you look into my eyes
the tears cannot be denied
The outer shell do not portrait
the hurt, the pain and the hate
I have carried all these years
when you left, you brought in new fears
the outer shell can wear a smile
while the inner keeps a secret file
of all the wrongs that you have done
my life without you has gone on
I have to care for the inner me
I need to get down on my knees
I want to ask for forgiveness
and take away the bitterness.
