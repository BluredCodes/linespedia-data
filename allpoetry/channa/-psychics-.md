The 'psychic line' called me againThey asked for Jamie, Judy or JaneBut..if you were a psychic, as you claimyou should have called me by my nameyou ought to know the date of my birthwhen naked I came to grace this earthhe color of lipstick that I wearnot to mention the color of my hairall that to you, should be no mysteryif in fact you are who you claim to beaway with you, charlatansyour intentions are fraudulentI will not give you the time of the dayFor, all you want is my money!
