---
title: "Angel of light?"
writer: "Channa"

I am an angel of light
master of the night
I can show you mysteries
tell you many stories
you've listen to my tunes
mournful sounds from the sand dunes
you've swayed to the sound
and to me, you are bound.
I am the enchanter
no need of a decanter
I am always present
at your every moment
so...take my hand so slightly
I'll lead you from the frays
you won't have to worry
I'll throw the 'book' away
but if you want religion
you can have the option
what you choose doesn't matter
for I am their master

With a stare and a glare
I replied to that thing
Boy! I know who you are
from you, I want nothing
I have heard all your lies
saw some of my friend die
from listening to you
and not seeing things thru
I'll not follow your light
my darkness is still bright
when I walk with my king
I have everything
you have not swayed me
I much rather be free
under the perfect law
than the bondage that I saw.
You are that slithery serpent
who can never repent
of your wicked evil deeds
and death follows your lead.
Satan, go find yourself another soul
as a bequest to your shadow!
