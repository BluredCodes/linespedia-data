Loneliness is wedged deep in my heart
Emptiness came along playing her part
Memory didn't want to be left out
so...she sprinkled few regrets about

Frustration is nagging and insisting
to take part in this staging
hopelessness wants a role in the prop
and blindly causes the curtains to drop

Passion is taking center stage
forming around me a burning edge
my flesh dressed for the rehearsal
is determined to enslave my soul

They play with my emotions quite well
as evidence of the tears you can tell
they miss not a single mark
of a play left for me in the dark

The director of this insane play
is none other than my mind I dare say
and by the time the final curtain's fall
they all will have answered death's call.This was written last year when I was in deep depression, I am much better now :)
