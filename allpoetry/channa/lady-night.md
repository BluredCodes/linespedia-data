---
title: "Lady night."
writer: "Channa"
slug: "lady-night"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/7239-Lady-night.-by-Channa"
---

Outside the howling of the wind
Sounds like a banshee in my mind
inside the house, silence is loud
it is screaming in my ears
I have no clues of what this is all about
all I sense is a deep fear
Lady night makes her appearance
she shrouds herself in mystery
Sending me in a dream like trance
she brought along her memories
Each crack is an explosion
I crouch in a fetal position
I feel your strong arms around me
Pulling me closer to your body
I long to feel you, yet I pull away
My tears once again drop freely
for all there's left is regret
from a broken vow I cannot forget
Lady night you're insensitive
and each passing night more intrusive.
