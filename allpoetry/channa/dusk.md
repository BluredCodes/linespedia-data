When the earth meet the sky
as the sun takes his bow
and the curtain slowly falls
at the end of the day
a splendor greet my eyes
shadowing the rainbow.
Veils of red, pink and blue
soft orange, light purple
seems to spread on the earth
radiant rays of light
filtering thru sheer clouds
announcing the gentle night
though I am well aware
of the scientific air
my hear still take a leap
at this sight of delight!
