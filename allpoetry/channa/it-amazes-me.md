---
title: "It Amazes me!"
writer: "Channa"
slug: "it-amazes-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

It amazes me
the startling beauty
of a single rose
the scent it disclose
the soft falling snow
covering the earth below
it astounds my mind
the soft whisper of the wind
the ocean's mighty roar
crushing on sandy shores
the quiet country side
meadows open wide
there may be some flaws
yet I am in awe!
The peaceful scenery,
mountain promontories
the valleys down below
and the quiet river flow.
I cannot understand
the plain beauty of the sand
or the complex human cell.
For the beauty in which I dwell
was created with such care
I say a simple prayer
a prayer of thankfulness
for His love and goodness
though I can't comprehend
I'm in the palm of His hand
He is my Creator
And my loving Father.
