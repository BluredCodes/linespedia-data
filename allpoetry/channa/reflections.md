title: "'Reflections'"
writer: "Channa"

Everyday I pass her by
her eyes color of summer sky
sparkled away from many tears
tears she cried all these years

The somber face that I see
somehow rather startled me
ripped away from her native land
faithfully wore his wedding band

For all his children that she gave birth
are elsewhere seeking roles on earth
left alone with her memory
she feels cold, she is lonely

What I see is a lost soul
feeling useless growing old
as I left her company
sudden fears grappled me

My own tears form a knot
and yet I can see
That I am not
The same as she.
