---
title: "Do you know Me?"
writer: "Channa"
slug: "do-you-know-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/7243-Do-you-know-Me--by-Channa"

You close your prayers in my Name
Do you even know who I AM?
I only desired compassion
no ritual, no religion
I came looking for mercy
I found children poor and hungry
you're so busy with your committees
blinded you are and cannot see
although I am in your midst
I don't edge you, I don't insist
you are certain of what I want
then, why are you so complacent
these little ones who go without
will have no hope, there is no doubt
unless you practice what you preach
give them the love that you teach
feed my sheep, clothe the door
I will then open wide the door
Until you learn to be more like me
to walk in my light and be ready
to care for those who are helpless
relieving them of their distress
Give them the hope of Salvation
Share my heart, my compassion
humbly and meekly do these things
with no pulling at their purse's string
be a faithful servant after me
I came to serve you, to set you free
I ask of you the very same
That, you can do in my Name.
