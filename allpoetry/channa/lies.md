I've often wondered
Where do you keep your lies
are they buried
deep into your mind
or simply hidden
underneath your cheating heart?
do you find them up and ready
to tell of a new story
or are you trying to hide
the real man that is inside
can you still be you 
and lie as you do?
are these lies etched 
rooted into your soul
can they ever be erased
as you would, a bad mistake
for you see sweetheart,
your lies break my heart
your lips can spread poison
or be spicy as cinnamon
but your eyes cannot lie
so... go ahead darling
and say your good byes.Sorry gang, My family and I have been quite sick, I am still under the weather but I feel better :)
