title: "Cyber screen love"
writer: "Channa"

You came into my life out of nowherewith a screen name you just appearedas your fingers typed in the wordsyour sweet spirit, I could observewe shared our beliefs, our passionFor G-d, justice and compassionCyberspace has become my friendwhere I could hide to escape the painI still feel the scar in this heart of minewhen on me, you walked out for the last timethey say 'time does heal all wounds'It has not been by leaps or bounds!Tonight, I am again drawn to that screenyour name appears, it can be seenyou are not free to love me, this I knowto another, you've pledged your undying vowI feel in love with a name on a screenstrange, because you, I have never seenYou were the one, I wrongly thoughtcould clear my mind of the fog of doubtalas! as always, I have lostI stand alone, an empty host!This piece was written 2 years ago, when (not proud to admit it) I feel in love with a name on a screen! he was married, therefore taboo for me, I myself am married now, so all things worked out just fine :)
