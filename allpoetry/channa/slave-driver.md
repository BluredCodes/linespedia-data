title: "'Slave driver!'"

I can't wait to grow up and be free
No school, no chores, I've got to be me
Momma, are we your slave?
and now you've asked us to behave!
you think you are the bureau of labor?
we have to clean our room and change the cat's liter!
As I listen to their grumble and complaints
I can't help in disbelief but only shake my head
"My little ones, if you only knew
as your mother, what I had to do!
Wake up at 6 AM and make you breakfast
so you could learn, knowledge has to last
off to school you go, I do some cleaning
morning dishes, laundry and some vacumming
then off to work, we have bills to pay
now, to deal with the boss, each and everday
investigating for our small town lawyers
come home at lunch, put clothes in the dryer
the afternoon is very much the same
at five I get you from afterschool program
make dinner, clean the stove, the counter
wash more dishes, sit an hour on the computer
Sit with you and watch telivision
get your bath ready, to relax your tension
off to bed you go, a prayer we will say
Asking for G-d to bless us, each and everyday"
So cherubs of mine, I would glady take
the baton from your hand, a trade I would make
see my little ones, don't try to grow up too fast
live carefreely but don't live for the past,
Learn to be responsible members of society
Don't grumble and please, do your shores TODAY!
