My eyes again caress the screenas I long to caress youYou rage a battle insideand I am fighting one tooI've tried with all my mightnot to give in to your plightevery night you've beckoning me to talk with you, but you're not freeYou have conjured up in my mindall sorts of desire I've hoped to findmy flesh burns with a passionfreshly awaken, new emotionNeither of us can truly conveyour words are shadowy grayyet, I can sense that you toofeel the same for me, as I do for you.
