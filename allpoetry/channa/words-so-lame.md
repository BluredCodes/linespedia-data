---
title: "Words, so lame!"
writer: "Channa"
slug: "words-so-lame"
source: "AllPoetry"

With a pen I cannot describefor I am not a writer nor a scribeAlthough I tempt it every dayno words can fully relayno rhyme, sonnet or prosecan ever come remotely closeto define the emotionof a lost love or a passionthe word that I do findwhen looking deep into my mindare so pale in comparisonand lack the art of expression!
