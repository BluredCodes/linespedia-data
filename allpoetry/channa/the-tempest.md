---
title: "the tempest"
writer: "Channa"
slug: "the-tempest"

This battle rages within my soul
the war has been won, I've been told
then...Why do I feel so lost and worn
Fears all around me, I am torn

As a ship in high sea tossed about
I am in tempest, I have doubts
You came into my life as a surprise
Where I could go for a reprise

Confusion fills my tortured mind
I am like a leaf, thrown into the wind
The truth in You, I must regain
still, my undying love You retain 

The color of my world has waned
to palest shades now, untainted
Longing for the peace I once knew
I seek now, Your pure morning dew

Help me to follow only You
Please renew my faith anew
weld in my heart, the love that I need
to love others and to walk in Your lead
