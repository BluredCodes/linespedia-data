title: "Why?"
writer: "Sweeetsuz"
slug: "why"
source: "AllPoetry"

Bejeweled grand dames and 
city fathers decided to arrange
homes for mad and indigent
gather all the disables~~
the halt, pay someone to keep
them out of the public eyes
four or five to a room
terrible food and no heat or cool
maltreatment and disgusted 
neighbors.
They then wondered why she 
did not report she was raped?
