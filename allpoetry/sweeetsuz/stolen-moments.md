---
title: "Stolen Moments"
writer: "Sweeetsuz"
slug: "stolen-moments"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/852-Stolen-Moments-by-Sweeetsuz"
---

Stolen moments like fading dreams,
Pass too quickly to feel.
Giving the sense of impermanence,
And missing the taste of reality.
Hoping for another chance,
Reaching for normality.
Sensing a fading memory,
Stealing from parts of life.
Guessing what is left,
Going for more incessantly.

Knowing that these will never be,
More than what they are.
Stolen moments from stealing lives.
Loving moments from missing lies.
Seeking for something more to be,
Than stolen moments and memories.
