Rape! Cried the whore crawling beaten and bleeding
Probably some “John” didn’t pay her so she tells
She beat herself to get some poor man into harm.

Rape! Moaned the addict showing torn and broken
Drug deal went sour said the detective 
She is covering so her pusher will not kill her.

Rape! Called the teen, my “father” every night, sobbing.
Something everyone must deal with screams mom,
You “slut”, you led him on, enticed him since forever.

Rape! Sobbed the woman, no I was robbed and hit
Ahmad said, she was raped and she must die
She has brought us dishonor. Not rape she told the lie.

Rape! A woman single and on a date, a drugged state
Some position you allowed yourself, to be lured into
Her friends and coworker always knew she was loose

Rape! Whispered the 15-year old street boy, nobody wanted
Except the gang who held him down and hurt him
Don’t tell, mustn’t ever tell, I am not gay, a homo!!

Rape! Rape said the murdered housewife, as the monster
Also murdered her little son. Play down the sex stuff !
We do not want that, here in our town, murder only, no sex!


Rape! All people, all cultures, all ages, all victims, any survivors?
Never believe, never accept, accuse them and blame them
Allowing them to be victims, yet again then again.
