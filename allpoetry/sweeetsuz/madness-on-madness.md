---
title: "Madness on Madness"
writer: "Sweeetsuz"
slug: "madness-on-madness"
source: "AllPoetry"

Hey down dilly, down dilly, dilly.
That she’s so silly, down silly.
Let’s take her freedom away.
Who cares what she can say?
Willy, Nilly.
Down dilly, silly, silly.

Oh down dilly, down dilly, dilly.
She’s mad and so silly, down silly.
Let’s put her under lock and key,
From here on, that’s where she’ll be.
Willy, Nilly.
Down silly, dilly, dilly.

Go down dilly, down dilly, dilly.
Go hope, so silly, down silly.
Never let her do any suicide,
You can always say she only tried.
Willy, Nilly.
Down dilly, silly, silly.

Way down dilly, down dilly, dilly.
Seems so silly, down silly.
She’s mad as a hatter,
Don’t let her get better
Willy, Nilly
Down silly, dilly, dilly.
