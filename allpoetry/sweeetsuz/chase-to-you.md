---
title: "Chase to You"
writer: "Sweeetsuz"
slug: "chase-to-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

My thoughts chase to you
With everything that I do
And they are so bright with
Hope and promise, so full of
Dreams and lifetimes. 
When I bathe, as I draw the 
Soap across my skin, clean 
smooth, and scents of softness,
my mind sees us in the early
morning embrace. Fresh wakened
and sultry from the night, with 
time for dalliance.
When my skin is goose bumpy 
With the cold, I feel your 
Hands, strong, sure and warm,
Stroking me, quieting, the shivers
Succumbing to my kisses with the 
Heat of passion warming, arousing,
Loving.
Each time I raise my lips to a straw,
I feel your lips, kisses connecting,
Covering, caressing, conscientiously 
Coveting, cherishing my returned
Passions.
Breathing slowly I muse about how 
I drink in the very all of you, wanting 
To know your scent, and wear it always
Making it part of me. I want to taste 
Your skin, your lips, to really know you 
As intimately as I know myself. 
My thoughts chase to you and all
Of the ways I want to love, and things
To share and give and have with you!
