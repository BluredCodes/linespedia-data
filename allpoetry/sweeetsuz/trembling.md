---
title: "Trembling"
writer: "Sweeetsuz"

I saw you today, but not near to touch.
You knew I was there.
Ah to be close, have you hold me,
To hold you!
To think of that makes me feel,
The trembling at my very essence.

To look in your eyes. How I long
To touch your face, hear your voice.
I long to feel your lips on mine.
When I close my eyes, I feel 
Your arms press all of me close.
Here comes that trembling again.

When I feel you close to me my heart,
Sings and my senses reel with
Longing and passions afire I
Reach out to you with all
That is wonderful and giving in me
When I want you like this
Here comes that trembling again.
