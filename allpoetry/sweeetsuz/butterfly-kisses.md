Butterfly kisses from here and to there
Marking the spots where I wish to fare
Mapping the places I wish not to miss
When I arrive with my butterfly kiss
Knowing that each place which I mark
Will become a place for me to park
Each little passionate butterfly kiss
Bringing to you such pleasure and bliss
Putting your fingers on places where
You wish my sweet kisses were there
Tears on your cheeks for each that misses
