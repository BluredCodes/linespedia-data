title: "Compulsions Patterns"

Trying to release compulsions
Streaming into my visions
Tracing living patterns
Sensing where the call
Comes trickling down
My spine as I see
The ghosts of reality
Stomping through the
Streams of timely matters
Shaping thru visions wisdoms
As the natural needing
Flows out in tracing
Patterns of my compulsions.
