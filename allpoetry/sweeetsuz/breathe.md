Holding my breath, I barely move
Sensing that to do so might
Cause you to feel unsure
As you explore the possibility
Of pursuing our sexuality, to where
We do more than only touch
Feeling this too much
I breathe……..
Touching you, touching me,
Where I want you to be
And……………….
I breathe.
Tasting you, tasting me
Where I want you to be
And…………….
I breathe.
Wanting you, wanting me,
As I need you to be
And………….
I breathe.
The fragrance of our lusts
As we progress further into
Our seeking sensuality
And………….
I breathe.
