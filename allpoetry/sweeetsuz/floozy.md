---
title: "Floozy"
writer: "Sweeetsuz"
slug: "floozy"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

There once was a girl named Suzy
Who became a supreme type of floozy
Then one day on the net
Who do you think she met
But a man who left Suzy, woozy
Suzy then went all out 
She would have left no doubt 
How the floozy felt while woozy
But the woozy man said whoa,
I am unready just now to go
To such dizzying heights just yet
I love you a lot,
But I have just got
Free from a long rebound
In my experience I have found
It is best to be friends
I do not mean to offend
This Suzy the floozy 
My favorite woozy friend
So they phoned and they did call
Their relationship grew quite tall
Then one day the man did not call at all
Suzy the floozy again got woozy
As only a Suzy can floozy 
Her days stretched out so long
As time went along with no song
And Suzy began to feel bereft
As if there were no sunshine left
Her friend did not call
Her heart felt sort of small
She was a floozy left bereft.
Her computer said oh no,
No more does Suzy glow
So to her woozy man I must write.
I must beg him this very night
To bring back her light
To give Suzy the floozy a call
