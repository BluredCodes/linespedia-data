Rainbows laughter softly echoes beneath
The silvery lover’s moon, while softly, silkily 
I seduce your willing trusting thoughts.
Contemplatively you decide upon whether
I am playfully playing sensual games or
Sensually playing sexual games with your
Body, thoughts and heart, trying to impart
That we are sexually sensual playing parts
Both of our sensually sexual playing hearts.
Playfully, I am sensually, a sexual plaything
Sexually playing sensual playful sexual games.
Sensually, sexual, we playfully play, sensual things
Playfully sexually trying all the most sensual games
Silky rainbow’s chuckles softly under a playful moon
Playfully silky silvery, moon playing a sensual tune
Seductively playful we play under the sensual moon.
