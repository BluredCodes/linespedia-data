---
title: "Deja Blue"
writer: "Sweeetsuz"
slug: "deja-blue"

I wait for the pain to ensue,
I have been here before.
I feel this sense of deja blue

This feeling is always so taboo
Let me wake from this I implore
I wait for the pain to ensue,

The dream is about to come true
Like opening a painful sore
I feel this sense of deja blue

Like a foolish child without a clue
Straight ahead and then go more
I wait for the pain to ensue,

I knew inside I had to subdue
This thing tearing at my core
I feel this sense of deja blue

So now I flee and you pursue
I feel this eerie déjà vu, “No more”
I wait for the pain to ensue,
God, I feel this sense of deja blue.
