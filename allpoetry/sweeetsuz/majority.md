I am a member of the majority
Being White
I would not want to be the minority
I might lose some opportunities
People would do to me
The things and ways they do
To you
Being Black
I would find that I lack
Certain things I need back
Like dignity and equality
But that is not for a minority
People would do to me
The things and ways they do
To you
Being Brown
If I suddenly found me Brown
It might turn my whole life around
I would find that what I’d lose
 A whole lot more than I’d choose
People would do to me
The things and ways they do
To you
Being Yellow
I might see an unhappy fellow
Whose life would not ever mellow
If I woke a were suddenly Asian
Instead of being as I am Caucasian
People would do to me
The things and ways they do
To you
Then Revelation
Finding a new level of education
Brooking no chance at mediation
Being female I am not the majority
I have already been a minority 
People already do to me
Things and ways they do 
To you
Being woman.
