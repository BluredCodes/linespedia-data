What’s that tap tapping at my heart
I play at not wanting any part
But the noise will not go away
Who is this who wants to play
There seem to be these love taps
They are coming here with a patty pat.
Who is it that sends these to me, who
Well what do you know it’s you!
Love taps tapping at my heart
Tap, tapping out the start,
I see the taps beginning to ring
Tapping out the sounds of love sings
What’s that tap tapping at my heart
I play at not wanting any part
Who is it that sends these to me, who
