Waiting with anticipation
Hoping always for more
Knowing that there may
Never be anything less
Than what we have now
Breathlessly wanting to 
Feel that there is one
For each and that one
May be there for me
As the moment nears 
The anticipation grows
Leaving the tense pulsing
Playing with my heartbeat
As time becomes a part
Of the wait of the feeling
Knowing that this anticipation
This sense of needing someone
