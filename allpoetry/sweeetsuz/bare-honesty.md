I stand here before your loving gaze
Naked in my honesty 
The years have softened curves 
Widened parts often thought 
Better slimmer
My body soft from child bearing
Scars of life showing here and there
Love shows in my eyes too
You are naked in your truth also
Life has made us who and how we are
