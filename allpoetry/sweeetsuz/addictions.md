I wonder if my addictions 
Cause all of my afflictions 
Or if it is the other way around
The answer is one I never found
It might be that my afflictions
Cause all of my addictions.
The question remains indeed
What sort of doctor do I need
Should I try one of psychology
Or the need be for one of biology 
I maybe only require pharmacology
I must find the best answer for me
I wonder what that may be.
Whether actually my addictions
Are the real causes of my afflictions
Or if it is the other way around
The answer is one I never found
I know that it might be my afflictions
Which have always caused my addictions
I am always so confused.
