---
title: "I Would Give You Wings"
writer: "Sweeetsuz"
slug: "i-would-give-you-wings"

I would give you wings to fly
To soar above the world
To set you free from mundane things
And give you clouds to furl
I would build each pinion 
And paste it on with love 
Straight from my soul.
Color them with dye from blossoms
And gilt them so they shone
I would give you freedom 
To soar above all cares
To keep you safe from fears and worry
Painting on your cheeks the smiles
And on your lips the laughter
Each tiny feather of your wings
Would be kissed with gold
And you would float above the harried world
From my love I would create 
Another angel sweet and free
I would give you wings to fly
Tho it may mean letting go
You cannot keep the things you love
They can only stay by choice
Love freely given will grow 
Set free will soar and sing
So my love I start to make you
Yet another wing.
