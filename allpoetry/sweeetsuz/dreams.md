---
title: "Dreams"
writer: "Sweeetsuz"

Slowly slipping into ecstasy
You do that to me.
Driving my senses to the brink.
That's the place I cannot think.
Going with me,
Over the top.
Play our game.
Where to stop?
Always
sensually
giving
pleasures.
Roiling
emotions
feelings
measured.
Awakening
innermost
sexuality
easily.
Foundering in a sensual sea,
Knowing what you do to me.
I am saddened by echoing screams.
Awakened to find you're only my dreams.
