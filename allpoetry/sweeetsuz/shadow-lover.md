Shadow lover, come to me 
As you do each velvet night
My hungry, willing body opens
To your intangible embrace
though your arms are smoke
they ignite fires with each caress
when you take me to your 
breast I find my arousal
complete. Your loins thrust
at mine and I become as 
an opening flower ready 
to take you inside and
cherish each movement 
as a tribute to our needs.
Intimately each time we
Embrace I find your touches
Enflame my senses 
As your lips and stroking
Hands are barely felt, yet
Touch me so poignantly, so
Surely arousing my passions
Once more.. opening my eyes
I find you gone, yet I know
When I return to the shadows
