Taxi dancer, my company for hire
For the price, I am yours to admire
Pretending at love, feigning my passion
I am yours for a time, after a fashion
Someday I hope for a love of my own
Sadness is the only life I have known
When time comes the sun goes down
I wait for you to come to town
You pay your money and we dance
I want you for mine there’s no chance
That you can care I pretend in my heart
At dawn you return where I have no part.
