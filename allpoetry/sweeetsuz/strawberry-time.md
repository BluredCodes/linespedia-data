title: "Strawberry Time"

Looking out of my window near
I saw covering powdery white snow
Upon my breast I dropped a tear
Watching the snowy blossoms blow
For what I beheld upon the ground
Was not winter’s white at all
But springing blooms all around
It is springtime not the fall
My hearts pounding within my breast
Just ere the time of the ruby cherry
Truly my luck would leave me blest
Being the time for the sweet strawberry
So come my love and lie with me
I shall touch your willing lips with mine
Making love to you neath yonder tree
Feed you sweet berry’s from my vine*
