---
title: "Alone Together Alone"
writer: "Sweeetsuz"
slug: "alone-together-alone"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/1066-Alone-Together-Alone-by-Sweeetsuz"
---

Unhappy people, unhappy lives,
Going along, shifting ties.
Seeking, forgetting, seeking hope
Staying alone, to frustrate and mope.

Financially, emotionally, morally wrong,
You mustn’t quit this hopeless song
Mired in misery, still stuck right here.
Too much to lose, loneliness and fear.

Intimacy’s a joke, cause love’s gone away
Sadness and grief are here to stay.
Reasons are myriad, yet fear is the glue
Both are in misery, the outlook is blue

No love for yourself, nor for each other
To talk or go back seems too much a bother
Turning to each, is turning to stone.
Pity is, you’re all alone, together, alone.
