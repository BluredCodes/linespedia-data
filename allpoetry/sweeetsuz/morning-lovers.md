Morning lovers replete with the night
Snuggling close and feeling just right
Reaching over to know how near
Sated and glowing waking and dear
Knowing that the other will also soon wake
Delights and gifts are there to take
Sensing the feelings knowing the need
Tendrils of wanting sowing the seed
Reaching out feeling the heat from a mate
Knowing and wanting to stay a bed late
Reaching and wanting rumpling the covers
