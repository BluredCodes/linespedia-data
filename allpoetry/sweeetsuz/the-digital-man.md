title: "The Digital Man"
writer: "Sweeetsuz"
slug: "the-digital-man"
source: "AllPoetry"

The digital man, in his digital house
Ticking away at the streams of time,
Clock, dickery, doc, and his digital mouse,
Scurrying along some unseen line.
His, is a clock, which has no hands,
While his calendar has no days,
Digitally he tightens it’s bands,
Since numbers change their own ways.
That digital clock stands in an empty room
Which rolls, but gathers no dust.
Ticks off his seconds in the gathering gloom 
For his digitally sanctimonious lust
A digital man in his digital house
Ticking away at the streams of time
Racing the clock face, with his digital mouse
Dealing out futures, thoughts so sublime.
