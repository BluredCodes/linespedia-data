title: "CYBER LOVE"

There’s a new strange “magic” in the air!
It’s called the “Net”. It’s everywhere!
Start up the modem. Dial the “Net”
See if there's an e-mail yet!

Go to a room! Start a chat!
I wonder! Is this where he’s at?
Hello! is anyone out there?
Is there someone, for me to care?

Is he real, or just cyber space?
How can I know? I can’t see her face
Check my e-mail! Send ICQ again!
Will she be on? Oh where oh when?

How do I know, if he’s really there?
Is she really there? Does she care?
Is she married and just playing?
How can I believe, a word he’s saying?

Is this picture really true?
No word yet! God I’m blue!
What does he look like? How does he feel?
How can I know she’s really real?

My heart goes out over the “Net”!
Will she write? No e-mail yet!
When can I meet him? Who will he be?
What’s in her heart? Is she for me?

This cyber love, I cant’ decide!
Should I quit? No! Let it ride!
There’s a new strange “magic” in the air!
It’s cyber love! It’s everywhere!
