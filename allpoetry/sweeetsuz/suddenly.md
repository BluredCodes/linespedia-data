Lifetimes of seeking, yearning,
Unfulfilled needs, confusion streams.
Suddenly, again he was here, burning,
With questions and longing, dreams.

Like a curtain opened, thrown wide,
A glimpse at a well loved soul.
I've been here before, the long ride.
His knows me too; the whole.

Aeons of loving, true to our heart.
Years of searching, each the other.
Separate lifetimes of being apart.
Knowing that he was somewhere.

He touched my life and I knew.
My heart answered knowing his.
Suddenly clear and so true,
That we had always known, this.

Lifetimes of seeking, yearning,
Unfulfilled needs, confusion streams.
Suddenly again he was here burning,
