---
title: "I Am Wolf"
writer: "Sweeetsuz"
slug: "i-am-wolf"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/1086-I-Am-Wolf-by-Sweeetsuz"
---

I am wolf,
See me hunt.
The puma prowls,
Seeking prey.
I am eagle screaming,
Eyes so keen, talons so cruel.
The father sun,
Warms the springing earth.
I am fawn daintily,
Taking tentative steps.
The comic possum,
Quickly digging roots.
I am raccoon, masked,
Nature’s sly bandit king.
Tiny insects scurrying through,
Preying or becoming prey.
I am the breeze clearing,
Fleecy clouds scattering warm rains.
Majestic horse, racing
With the wind, winging, strongly.
Singing feathered bird, sowing,
Pollinating beautifying the sky.
I am a “human” a person 
Of the “People” keeper,
Caretaker; child of the Great Spirit.
I am all and I am man
