title: "Dark Lover"
writer: "Sweeetsuz"

Merrily comes the day break
Skipping over mountains
Kissing trees with gold

Merrily comes the smile
Shyly cross your lips
Kissing promises laughter

Gently come morning breezes
Crossing darkened valleys
Kissing earth’s life

Gently come mirrored eyes
Fluttering sleep flushed cheeks
Kissing lovers promises

Hopefully comes night shadows
Yearning upward seeking
Kissing dawn’s gold glistening

Hopefully comes dawn’s warmth
Alas, alas my love you go
Kissing you gone away

Wanting comes to reddened day
Wearily hiding, glow purpling
Kissing velvet dark, calmly

Wanting comes the rising need
Floating in a sea of passion
Kissing, touching intimately.

Merrily comes the smile
Shyly cross your lips
