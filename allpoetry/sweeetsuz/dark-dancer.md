---
title: "Dark Dancer"
writer: "Sweeetsuz"

Darkling dancer
Dancing darkly
Twisting as it seems
Strangely whirling
Darkling dancer
Dancing darkly 
Through my dreams.
Wildly twirling.
Darkling dancer 
Laughing darkly
Through my life
Tinkling gaily
Darkling dancer
Laughing darkly
My future’s rife 
Running daily
Darkling dancer
Loving darkly
Eerily prancing
Soaring motions
Darkling dancer
Darkly dancing
