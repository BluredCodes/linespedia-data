title: "Digital Man 2"
writer: "Sweeetsuz"

There stands the man with no face,
Who loves no peace where he stays
While the woman he loves, only plays
Seeing this, he flees from this place,
That’s while his life, it races apace.
There stands a calendar with no days
Which in so many, many digital ways 
Does separate her from his space

Watching the time slipping through the sands
They are feeling their impending doom
Giving them both totally unhallowed lusts
The huge, shadowy, clock has, no hands
Stands by itself in that lonely room 
With it’s numbers obscured by the dust.
Gathered by the clock in the shadowy gloom
