Memories float, of longer days,
Carefree and warm, with simpler ways.
Childhood dreams of long summer nights,
Hot hazy days and firefly lights.
Ice cream and sno-cones dripping off chins,
Sunburns and freckles and scrapes on our shins.
Fishing with bobbers in warm muddy pools,
Diving and splashing, trying to keep cool.
Lying back on the lawn, counting the stars,
Dreaming of ships and our first trip to Mars.
