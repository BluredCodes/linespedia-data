---
title: "Rainbows and Rubies"
writer: "Sweeetsuz"
slug: "rainbows-and-rubies"
source: "AllPoetry"

Pretty little thoughts chasing through me
Skipping where I wish your lips to be
Ruby red kiss marks on my willing skin
Turning my thoughts to rainbows within.
Your hands they tease and release me 
Also petting where I wish them to be,
Stoking fires of rainbows from far within
And ruby red kiss marks on my willing skin
Emeralds, diamonds, pearls of sensuality
Kissing the places I want you to see
Torrid fires of rainbows under my skin
Ruby red kiss marks building within.
