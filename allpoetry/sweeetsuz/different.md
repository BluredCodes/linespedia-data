title: "Different"

He threw at me, that I was not thin enough
She said my teeth were not straight
They offered that my legs were bent
There was a hump upon my back
Soon it became apparent that I was too tall
Then my skin was not pale enough 
The final blow came when someone said, stupid 
Low life uneducated dummy, trailer trash.
Everything they told me was correct
Right from their slick ad campaigns where 
There are no souls, only cellophane slicks
I noticed I could not discern which of them
Had slung the arrows of my hurt
~~They were each and everyone exactly the same~~
