title: "Wanting More"
writer: "Sweeetsuz"

I think of how it feels touching you
Having you touching me and I find
Myself always wanting more, always
Needing so much more than time allows
When my mind goes to how it seems 
We always find our eyes meeting across
A crowded room and a glance says more
Than words would ever mean, when I look
Into your beautiful eyes seeing deeply into
The place where you love me, I love you.
The touch of your hand in the most intimate
Places, still makes me tremble with desire.
Thinking of how you sigh and the little 
Panting noises you make when you want
Me to reach further inside you, when my 
Tongue and lips tease you beyond delights.
Wanting more, I can even now feel the tips
Of your nipples on my chest as you tremble
With desires as I tremble at the wonders
Of you and the wonders you show me 
Even now we still find such sensual 
Pleasures in each other even now
I cannot forget always wanting more
Of you, of us of our love and desires,
Even now I cannot forget always wanting more.
