Petals of flowers, essence of musk
Stirred together in a cauldron of dusk.
Two drops of some sweet personality,
Soaked through with steamy sensuality
Perfumes of a hot and erotic lust
Blended into a body of wanting must.
Add huge amounts of wondrous needs,
Many more kernels of sexual seeds,
Drop in large does of magic phernomes,
Some panting gasps and excited moans,
Fold in some beads of hunger and sex
Basic ingredients of this little hex,
The only thing missing now it is true
Larger and longer times spent with you.
Pieces of stars and a taste of sensual musk
Folded and stirred in my cauldron at dusk.
