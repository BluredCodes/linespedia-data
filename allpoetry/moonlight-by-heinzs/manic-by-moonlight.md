He howls by the full moon's light -
his mood, somber - unfathomable.
Even his visage seems to darken.
My son stalks out into the night
on an unrecounted quest.
The next day he sleeps
in trancelike stupor until noon.
This pattern repeats itself
for days, then inexplicably
his world brightens and he returns,
as if from a distant land.
Cheerfully he greets the day
and all is well... until next time.
Maybe there are werewolves...

10/31/2001
