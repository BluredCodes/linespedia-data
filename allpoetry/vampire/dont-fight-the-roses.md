title: "Don't fight the Roses"
writer: "Vampire"

Don't fight the roses the roses fight back
tearing your heart open into the sack
chaining you down with the poisonous roots
it grapples your neck and pulls you in two
until you suffocate with its words and actions!
before you start to panic and suffer spasms!
you slowly,turn, and look away
in failure...

its a wonderful, wonderful life
beaten,
wonderful,wonderful life
weakened,
wonderful,wonderful life
i die..

black rose petals fall on my grave,
nothing's right as i slowly fade!
demising, im dieing, fuck dieing insane!
all for nothing
INSCRIBED ON RAIN!
IT WON'T GO AWAY!
No.....
INSTEAD ITS MARKED BY MY PAIN...
wonderful life,
fearing...
wonderful life...
