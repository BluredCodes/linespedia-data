writer: "Vampire"

theres a sad truth about christmas
that darkens the fright
the street lamps light up
all through the night

--

A christmas party starts off with a bang
the relatives are strangers
i want them to die,
I hate all my uncles, dirty they lie
Christmas is unlit
the tradition will die.

Im stuck thirsty poor
all i got was some toys
the meaningless pile of toys
oh i hate those pointy elves..

I envy friends, who smile and play,
their playstation 2,
and brag all day,
Christmas is cheap and all it gives me
is a bucket of jealousy..

I don't like the smiles
thats full of deceit
all christmas is now
is money to beat

when you got no money
you don't want to get
the fake thats uncunny
for the poor who're in debt
the stories of families joining together
is all just a myth.

My dad and mother
argue all year about eachother
it gives me fear
this isn't right at the christmas time
that to me is just some lies

I feel like im gonna cry...
