Some seeds are born from iniuqity.
Breeding hatred
And Chaos through its roots
causing lies and felony.

Some seeds will die naturally,
never born with its leave
but is raped by nature
from its poor fertility

Some seeds will rule greatly
trampling over the weak
who have no purpose of living
they fall prey to the deadly

Seeds of the bad will rise
and grow into the trees
and the good and weak will die
no time for the wise
the strong will prevail
no time for cries
the freaks fail
you live or die
you don't live twice
you died,
bye bye
