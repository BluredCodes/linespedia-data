click,
click,
click.
the computer commands every instruction..
click,
he mumbles, as he decides the path he makes..
copy and paste?
leave don't take?
or sell as a fake?
he cleanse his thick black framed specs..
he rubs his sweaty hands on his pencil neck.
his eyes glares with those large freckles,
on his nose is plenty of speckles.
he steals...
he adds his name on,
he grins with his braces shining on the screen.
the nerd has once done it again,
plagued the poet, what a fake.
he sits there smiling, waiting for a reply two
three eager readers wrote...
the first and second were glad as they reckoned
the poem was great, as they spoke
he finshed the reply within a second
he sat down smiling, as he drank some coke
he replied in two words, the words were thankyou
the last but not least, was a vibrant red note
he opend the letter, the first word were
'YOU!!!!!!!!'
'YOU TOOK THE CREDIT, YOU STOLE MY POEM I WROTE!!'
'YOU!!!!!!!'
 THE PLAUGEIRIST SCUM!, IM GONNA CUT YOU IN TWO!!'
'THERES NOTHING YOU CAN SAY! NOW IM GONNA SUE!!
