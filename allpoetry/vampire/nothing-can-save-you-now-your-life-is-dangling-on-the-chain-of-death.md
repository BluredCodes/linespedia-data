Nothing will save you now shithole,
I mock your gasps for mercy..
FOOL!
don't ask me mercy,
i plunged the sword down your ill heart..
im glad you will die, for the better,
For my joy, and my pleasure..
You deserve to die, pathetic plea,
nothing can save you, from the likes of me..
You throw yourself down, beg on your knees,
you tug my leg and kiss my feet..
I kick your skull back and you bleed..
blood out of your nose, haha hehe..
Fool, i pity you, im proud of your defeat,
you are pulled of your thrones, on chains..
chains of death, still you grovel at me.
I punch your gut and blood spits,
then i stump on your skull, you cannot see.
you can't talk now, i can't hear your pleas.
This day im proud when i took you down.
the years of oppresion
brought to this sound.
the sound of defeat.
the sound of glory.
you make no  sound..
i laugh.
beaten to the ground.
