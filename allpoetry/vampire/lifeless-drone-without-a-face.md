Im a lifeless drone doomed to infinity
living the the curse of immortality
inside this glass container connected to electrical wires
i hear the sound of computer beeps against the marble tile

the wavy patterns up and down my empty socket follows
all that remains is my head, i cannot show my sorrow
why was i put in this place, why im the one
demoralized and forever living on

"please"
I say in subconscience mind
alone,
Ive grown truly blind
nothing
can comprehense this place
i feel incomplete..
a drone with no face..
