---
title: "The Face Of God"
writer: "Vampire"

When i turned
I could distinquish the look of his dreaded face
torn and scarred with no flesh
yet not bleeding..

When i blinked
bones of white sheets flashed before me
with a paradox of anger
yet not breathing..

When i closed my eyes
for the very first time i saw his face and he was a beast
a dammed soul who lies and cheats
yet not decieving..

This god ain't no luv'in people freak
instead he crushed the guts and bones of people
with their pale face exploding into fear
yet not feeling..

he would burn people flesh of skin
wreck havoc and shattered the very souls who were promised
a promise which  led to their infernal tears
of misery..

he gave no mercy, he just grinned with his shark teeth blood red
with evil eyes in his face how people were so blind
and continued to laugh at the faces of those who loved him
stripping them of  dignity..

god is the beast who betrayed the people and shattered there souls
in which he just enjoyed causing pain
yet hes receiving eternal gratitude.
from those brainwashed to follow the cult of iniquity of lies and deceit

yet... that god is so weak..
