in a park filled with kids playing on their boards and blades
a sunny day reflects the grass and blaze
the hotdog stand opened up and many kids were hungry
for hotdog they gave out their money

the orders were of hotdog and mayonaise
but still noone wanted the bread
the orders were of hotdog and ketchup
but still nooone wanted the bread
the orders were hot dogs and onions
but nobody liked the bread

noone wants the bread alone, it just comes to be put in hotdogs
still it  grows old and left alone
it is spent by hot dog stands for money they don't own
and they don't make a profit alone

the hot dog can't live without a kennel
and the home is in the bread
the bread that makes the food
still hot and not dead

hotdog will not be the same wihtout bread
it will be just a weener with mayonaise
how dull will that be 
the food won't be eaten it remains dead
if it doesn't come with the bread

**this was made whil i was eating a hotdog**
