A nation under a God..
We are patriots, which is religion...
beleinging to the saviour..
jesus christ..
oh please, under the fifty stars..
not one state has freedom...
still we're oppressed, the nation
has cut our throats off...
i swallow in the air to say
the pledge, that is false...
A nation under a God...
that i choose not to worship..
a nation thats lied to us..
about a thing that is for freedom...
being patriotic is the onlyt hing...
if your not patriotric..
then you're against a God..
that you choose not to worship...
I hate the pledge stuck in my throat...
I shout it out...everyday...
all these tlaks of freedom makes me sick..
a nation founded by pilgrims...
i guess that the indians were slaughtered..
because they never heard of Messiah..
we feast on their blood and turkey,
on a day,,, where we should be thankful..
but im not...
We celebrate, the true fathers..
we raped their fathers..
then we call ourselves fathers..
when we can't even keep to a trust..
a trust that is blake...
God didn't find this world,
but man did...
the men were blind fools..
and there liars...
we are not great america...
I hate the flag,,
I hate the pledge!
i hate what i stands for!
i hate the words thats fed to me..
A NATION UNDER GOD!
the pledge is just bull to me..
can't you see...
that america is not as great
s its said to be...
like cows to the slaughter..
praying to a cult leader..
because they beleive in fear
of the antichrist.
im no antichrist
i am man
we're all man
and man is no slave
slaves to a god that doesn't exist
schools get to dam religious
america is not as great as its to be...
following blind faith over events..
i resent..
to pray blind...
God, rules brittania...
britiania rules the waves....
God save our glorious Queen..
for kingdom tides...
