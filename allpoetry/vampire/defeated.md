Defeated, stripped of my nobility
my honor
and my dignity

Defeated by my own failure
from stupidity
as with my fallen anger

stripped of my family
my friends
and my rule

And my freedom is withheld
and my people
my wonderful people

must suffer from my defeat
If i were to be alive
i would say my last words

'I die with all things raped and taken,
but you cannot take the love
that his hidden in my heart'

I fought with the people in mind
before my selfish life
as im torn to death on the battlefield line

Farewell my good friends
may it be the story of our bloodshed wars
goodbye...
