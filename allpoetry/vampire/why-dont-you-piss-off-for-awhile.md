I am not gonna suck up to your mistakes
I am not going take the blame
I don't care for the actions you take
You're acting real insane

I don't care about your life
I don't want none of it
Your so stupid crying over a knife
and you're just sad shit

Fake cries and a whole lot
 your overeacting is sick
stupid boy wearing slipknot
saying i want to die, stupid gothic

Trendy follower who's showing your fists
crying over the wish of being dead
oh please your just sad having tattoos and cut wrists
those £10 chains are crap on your head

I turned away from being the blind
when you follow the trend and be sad
uniqueness is lost, because of the line
of people praying for Korn like a fad

Stupid people just be normal
get some sense in your sick heads
you just ruined the point of 'goth'
bloody manson's wearing spikes of lead

I laugh at your stupidness trying to be stupid
pack your toys and stop hating yourself
You cry over nothing, the fact that your stupid
just plain metal bashing stupid

You care all for pot and pot for all
weed is your need and being weird isn't feared
When you begin to fall
looking back at your stupidness
stupid stupid boy..

you labelled yourself with a pricing gun
the price of being a fool
