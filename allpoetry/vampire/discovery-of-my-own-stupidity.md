I looked left, brushing my shoulder on my cheek
I hesitated what is wrong
i look down, a pool of blood was at my feet
The trail of blood  was long

Lost in total mind and confusion
I wondered what must be going on
Could this be a sick illusion
Used with Thor's hammer & tongs

Im too stupified to know why my mind ceased
but yet i know not the cause
My mind has been hexed by a voodoo priest
Playing tricks with Murphy laws

My transgression of thought hits hard on my head
As i still ponder my ultimate question
My brain was too late, when i was surely dead
within the short uncounted seconds
