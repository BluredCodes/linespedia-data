I felt extremely pissed walking down the corridors
thinking to myself the next thing to write
I envy the poets that make me feel torn
As i spend countless hours all night

as i spend 5 minutes writing a poem of no emotion
i make up the lines that rhyme
i just read it after no devotion
i feel i commited i crime

I cannot write at all
it blows i got the blues
never cared much for poetry
thought of it as taboo

my poetry never comes out my true feelings
i cannot capture the image for the reader
So i will say
i got the writers blues that im not beleiving

I fade off
as the reject
no poet can make
the full effect

because in the end we all feel blue
