I am no Satan , I am no christ
im no iron maiden, i just have a life.
im not the cold anti-christ
im not the angel that gives advice
im not the devil  with wings or horns
im not the follower with wings torn
i am no soldier
i am no coward
i am no stronger
i am no wiser.
I am not the mad heretic
and im not the bible basher
I am no braver
i am no younger
i am no slayer
i am no beast
i am no saint
i am no genious
i am nothing devious
I am me
I am an independent thinker..
I choose my own path to lead.
if you don;t like me,, so be it.
freedom of speech my friend.
Come as you're
no need to pretend
if you choose to pretend
then i am not your friend.

For we maybe different,
but we have so much in common
perhaps we put our flaws behind.
and stay to your trust..
cheating yourself
is beating yourself..
just stay you,
be true to us
we'll like you anyway
as long as you stay true
so just be you...
stay true..
