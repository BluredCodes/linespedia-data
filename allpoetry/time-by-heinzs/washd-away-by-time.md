---
title: "Wash'd Away by Time"
writer: "Time by heinzs"
slug: "washd-away-by-time"

The Lovers’ kiss and first embrace,
The discover’d Joy upon each face...
--Wash’d away by Time.

Baby’s smile and premier word,
Leading to the lies absurd...
--Wash’d away by Time.

Grief and pain—the scars remain,
The tears that sting the soul insane...
--Wash’d away by Time.

Where is the son, the shining One?
Once upon a life, undone...
--Wash’d away by Time.

And all the Fear and all the Hope,
Burning up in fog of dope...
--Wash’d away by Time.

Far and near, all we hold dear,
By-and-by will disappear...
--Wash’d away by Time.


Feb. 2001
