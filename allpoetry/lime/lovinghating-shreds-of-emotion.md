title: "Loving/Hating: Shreds of Emotion"

His eyes kiss my skin
 while his lips burn mine
 
 His hands caress my shivers
 while his body weighs on my soul
 
 I know it's wrong
 I know he's wrong.
 
 His gentleness, his indecision,
 His need, his desperation
 
 To desire is to torment 
 And to love is to die
 
 He is that to me,
 a symbol of love and death
 
 My knight, yet inescapable
 murdering and saving me
 
 How can i push him from me?
 How can i stand his stares?
 
 How can i not yearn?
 How can i not taste him?
 
 To need someone so intensely
 And yet to hate them fiercely,
 
 That, tears me into shreds of emotion
 So please, finally, show me you.
