---
title: "Mind Numbing Sanity"
writer: "Lime"
slug: "mind-numbing-sanity"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/22538-Mind-Numbing-Sanity-by-Lime"
---

A small-town girl with nothing learned, nothing shared
Her soul lost in the mindless reverie of simplicity

Days pass, without even an opening of dream,
Spent numbingly with people without appealing features

Clones stare not quite at her, wide-eyed, and dense
Lacking slivers of windows into the soul

Merely watching not experiencing or feeling
Cold as the dull tiles covering the destitute bathroom

They see through her, into the open, cathartic air
Missing her sense of being, her essence,

Seeing only the same overshadowed, dull eyes
Staring blankly at the future lazily mapped out in front

Lines drawn to disenchantment and wishes squandered in dots
The girl knows she is lost, forced into the chains of routine.
