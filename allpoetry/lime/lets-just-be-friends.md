i'd like to say i'm handling it with grace
Of course that's in a perfect world
Because all i'd really like to do
Is shove it your god forsaken face

You said you'd never leave me,
You said you'd always be there
Since you broke that promise
It's time for you to see what i see

i really don't deserve this
and one thing i'd like to know
why the hell didn't you tell me
That our "thing" was one big hit-and-miss

Everything was one big lie
And i can't believe you told me
At lunch, at school, stating
that our relationship was bye-bye

So stay the hell away
Don't try to talk to me
Don't try to be my "friend"
At least 'til the spring of May.
