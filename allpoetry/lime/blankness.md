---
title: "Blankness"
writer: "Lime"
slug: "blankness"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

A quick glance sears into my waking heart,
A toothy smirk reaches inside and pulls me out.
Me, all I was before you, 
And the utter blankness i am now.

You stole me completely, wrenching my battered soul
from an already bruised body.
You took my confusion and my mind, dismantling,
Then tossing them restlessly as game pieces.

Now, I am empty, missing the light that rose my 
dreary sight, 
A mere glimpse of you pushes me back, 
ripping at time, begging for a need, a feeling

Hoping for an awakening,
Hoping to retrieve the broken pieces
Hoping for you.
