Greasy little faces
Smushed against the smeared glass,
"I want THAT", "Gimme THOSE"
Merry Christmas to all

Screaming babies, grimy kids
Weary, moneyless mothers,
Absentee, happy fathers
A lovely day at the mall

Toys and gadgets for sale,
All "priced low",
So it'll only set you back...
Just a FEW hundred.

Loooong lines that go back 10 moms
Wincing and snarling cashiers
Who barely manage to growl out
"Thank you. Have a nice day"

A religious holiday?
I think not 
As the kids reek 
Of consumerism!
