---
title: "Brought Me Back"
writer: "Lime"
slug: "brought-me-back"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/30188-Brought-Me-Back-by-Lime"
---

I know what I am
Nothing more than a door mat
I know you're better than that
You're better than me
You make things alright
You are the key
To all my sanity

In all my weary days
Everything of me kept shuttered
Until that spring in May
When I was lost, left behind
She drained me but you,
You brought me loving grace,
Brought me back here too

I'm not close to enough for you
I'm cold, frozen by doomed love
I lie, I cheat, I steal
After all this, I need to swear
I care for you for real
You make me believe all is right
You are the only key
To every bit of my sanity

In all my weary days
Everything of me kept shuttered
Until that spring in May
When I was lost, left behind
She drained me but you,
You brought me grace,
Brought me back too

There's no need for pretending
You make up the best parts of me
And I wanna hold your trust
No more hiding, no more lies

You're the only key
To all my sanity...

In all my weary days
Everything of me kept shuttered
Until that spring in May
When I was lost, left behind
She drained me but you,
You brought me grace,
Brought me back too
