"shhhh". He's comming, i can hear the car, 
I scramble into the corner i can see him from a far,
     He calls my name and turns on the lights,
Shouts abuse as i cry on this dismal night,
     My mommy is out and my daddy is home,
I'm laying here all alone,
     Scared and frightened, i cant help but cry,
He unstraps his belt looking so sly,
     I hold my head but in my hands,
Then see his face as he grabs my hands,
     He pulls me up with words of abuse,
I start to scream, and try to break loose,
     His grip is strong and my fingers turn blue,
I hope and pray but then it comes soon,
     I feel a fire in my throat, 
I feel the pain again and again,
     I scream "please stop" but he doesn't,
I try to break free but i couldn't,
     He pulls his hand back and then forth,
I feel my back getting so sore,
     I look at his eyes, I see only evil,
Then look up again and see betrail,
      He finally stops, with my eyes so sore,
I cry again, with the pain hurting more,
      I watch him walk away and then i sigh,
A sigh of relif as he walks to the door,
      This is what happens every week,
And all the time i only can weep,
      I can not tell but today i have,
I must tell you all before he stabs,
      These are my last words of feeling so low,
"please let me be i want to go!"
