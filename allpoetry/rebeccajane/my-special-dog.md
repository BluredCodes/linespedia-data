---
title: "My Special Dog"
writer: "rebeccajane"

I've never had a friend,
A friend like you befoe,
Who's always glad to see me,
When i walk through the door.

You never moan or frown,
Shout or ask for much,
Just glad o be in my company,
For a stroke and a loving touch.

There are times when all is turmoil,
We shout and argue too,
You hide away in a corner,
With eyes that look so blue.

But then when all is quiet,
Your head you'll lay on my knee,
With eyes that say "don't worry,
It's alright, you've got me".

I tell you all my troubles,
My worries and my woes,
You cuddle up beside me,
With a lick and a sloppy nose.

I've had you from a puppy,
Together we have grown,
We've run and jumped and rolled about,
Such fun we both have known.

Your more than just a friend,
You're really so much more,
I love you, I've never told you,
You're my special black Labrodor.

              Rebecca Jane
