---
title: "What If??"
writer: "Gomer"
slug: "what-if"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/16433-What-If---by-Gomer"
---

I sit here
in a lonely cafe
staring out
at a rainswept
parking lot
my coffee
steaming,
forgotten.
My eyes
Reflecting,
Dreaming,
Remembering.
A single tear
courses
slowly
down.
The wind
Whispers
and I think
of you.
Painting
a picture
of the days
gone by.
A brief vision
of eyes
endless depths
Sparkling
Stars 
Within.
A Vision
of a 
Face.
Always on
the verge
of Laughter
or Tears.
A vision
of a smile
Sometimes faint,
Hesitant,
like the first breath
of spring.
Sometimes Radiant
Bursting out!
like the Sun
after a rain.
A Spear of 
Longing.
Regret..
Pain...
Grief....
Spilled milk
in the sands
of time.
A Resolution
moving on
moving forward
but not without
a brief look
Back
to think
to wonder
what if?
