---
title: "Flames Dancing"
writer: "Gomer"
slug: "flames-dancing"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/16435-Flames-Dancing-by-Gomer"
---

As I sit here thinking of you
lost in memories
lost in the depths of your eyes
spiraling ever downward
looking for the eternal flame
of your soul
in the distance I sense a spark
my heart quickens,
my skin flushes,
my passion stirs,
my heart aches.
I feel so close
yet so far
but my soul-flame sees it's other half,
rushes madly through the darkness,
closer, closer, closer still.
and late at night, when none but dreams stir,
one pair of dreams slowly take shape
in the endless void of thought
slowly, carefully, tenderly, lovingly
begins a dance
a dance as old as time
a dance that started when the first life 
thought of other than itself.
slowly at first,
two embers of flame
moving faster,
brighter,
till with a crescendo of music they join, combine,
their forces are as one
one eternal flame
a beacon in the darkness
for all whose dreams travel there.
and late at night when none but dreams stir
two kindred spirits
though separated by distance
smile in their sleep.
