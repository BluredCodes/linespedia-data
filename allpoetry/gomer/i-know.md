---
title: "I know...."
writer: "Gomer"
slug: "i-know"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

As I stare into your eyes
I see the pain inside
I know....
I feel your heart breaking..
I see your soul shredding..
I know....
kissing your tears away
when you want to stay..
I know....
Listening to the agony in your voice
As you scream to escape
I know....
When you say you can't wait to leave
To come be with me
I know....
Because hunny, I share your pain
I feel your heart
I know your soul
I love you
I know....
