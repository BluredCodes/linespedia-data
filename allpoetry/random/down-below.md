---
title: "Down Below"
writer: "Random"
slug: "down-below"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/19896-Down-Below-by-Random"
---

Siting at a table
People look at me
Looking at this figure
They only see.

They don't care
What's down below
They see me
But they don't know.

They didn't know
What they did
But it's clear to me:
I just look big.

Anybody there?
Oh, now they've left
Left me with blankness
Blank from theft.

So what is now?
To continually go?
As nothing had happened?
No, I will know.

They thought they knew
They thought they saw
But it was not
The truth below.

Nobody will bother
Nobody will see
All that blankness
Left inside me.
