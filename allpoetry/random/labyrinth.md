Lost in the labyrinth
Of my Mind
Without an answer
I can find

Pathways fork
Here and there
Big decisions for me
Lie everywhere

Green plains and raging wars
I see through my two eyes
And happy times like partys
Then times when my friends die

Sometimes big roads
And many people I see
But I'm not really there
And they walk straight through me

Others persuade me
All the time
They say with them
I will be fine

I make mistakes
Along the way
But it all sums up
At the end of the day

Sometimes I get sick
Of this whole game
Especially times
I can't find my aim

But I just walk on
To the destiny
To find my truth
Then to be free
