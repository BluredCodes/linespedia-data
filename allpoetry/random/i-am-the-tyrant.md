---
title: "I am the tyrant"
writer: "Random"
slug: "i-am-the-tyrant"
source: "AllPoetry"

I am the tyrant
I set the code
From trees to rocks
And to the sea and the roads

I demand clean air
I say cars are bad
They smoke poisonous gases
And drive us mad

I banned driving cars
Now clean air will be filled
Anybody who drives
Shall then be killed

Then I tripped over a pebble
It tried to kill me!
I say that all roads
Should be pebble-free!

I hate the insects!
They infest the town
I'll just spray poison
They'll all go down!

I keep hitting my head!
Because of those stupid trees!
I'll cut them down with chainsaws
Then there'll be no more trees to see!

Then a dog bit me!
They're evil to people
They might carry germs
I'll kill them all!

There's a landslide!
It nearly killed me!
I'll flatten those mountains
And pour them in the sea!

Those fish really irritate me!
Too much peacefulness!
I'll use my poison again
For those fish's meaninglessness!

I hate the cattle
I hate the taste
I'll torture them all
To show them my haste!

Now there's a rally
But I'll tell them they're wrong
They can shout for now
But not for long!

The people in the town
They're too busy!
Walking everywhere
They make me dizzy!

I don't like seeing people
I don't like the talk
I'll shoot a big bomb
They'll crumble like chalk!

Those birds in the sky
They fly, but I don't
I'll get my rifle
And shoot them so they won't

Everyone's against me now
Killing me's my goal
But they have no hope
I'll kill them all!

I look at the sky
And what I've done
I thought it'd be good
But nobody said I'm a good man

And so I'm the tyrant
Of the world wrecked by me
In misery I'll die
For that's how it should be.
