title: "Left Behind"
writer: "Random"

The world left me
Behind
Leaving blank my soul
And my mind
And letting nobody
Look back
Left me in my world
Pitch black
Knowing that the light
Far away
Is not real, but I chase it
Every day
My once lively heart
Now slowed down
And hate filling it up
As if to drown
The one who was
Left behind
By the world that
Just didn't mind.
