---
title: "Window to the world"
writer: "Random"
slug: "window-to-the-world"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/26952-Window-to-the-world-by-Random"
---

I see this world
Just through this window
In front of me
And I can see people.
But no matter how loud I shout,
They never hear me.
I'm like a shadow,
Just that it's an invisible one.
The window's too hard for me to break
This door's never been unlocked
They don't see or hear me,
No way to break free.
I live inside the room
Inside my mind
Apart from the world
Until I get sick of it.
