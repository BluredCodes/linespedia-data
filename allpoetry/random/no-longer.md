---
title: "No longer"
writer: "Random"

Just like a silver morning crow
He rides into the night
His shield was scarred, his mail all rusted
And the sword chipped from the fight

He rides away from the rubble
Once, a town with liveliness
Now all broken and decaying
With voices crying in loneliness

He no longer feels the victory
In stead, loneliness in his heart
And the pain from all their suffering
Like someone had struck a dart

He rides through farmiliar woods
And an bird cries from a tree
Brings back the memories of the past,
A dream that will never be

Just like a jet black morning crow
He came in the morning
Where the village cheered for victory
When he felt sadness and mourning.
