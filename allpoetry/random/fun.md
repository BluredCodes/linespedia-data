title: "Fun"

I wake up
In the middle of the night
And instantly
I flicker on the lights

My mind fills with crazy ideas
Of lits of blood and killing
Puts a smile on my face,
Makes my hunger filling!

I think about
What to do tonight
All I know is
That it's going to be a fun night!

I walk down
To the big long hall
I shouldn't make a sound
I shouldn't stand tall

I reach the cabinet
I see the knife
So beautiful there,
Just shining in the night

I sneak back to my room
I lock the door
And seal the gap
Between the floor and the door

I take out the knife
Taking long slashes in grace
All of this
Puts a big smile on my face!

Not much blood at first,
It's always that way
It keeps me happy
And I cut away!

It drives me on
My arm starts bleeding
It looks so red, so pretty!
It keeps on leading

Now there's a fairly deep cut
On my arm, it looks so nice!
It's that fun that
Can't be exchanged for a price!

Now that's enough
Fun for tonight
What's for me
Tomorrow night!Here's something fun, for a change!
