title: "Time"
writer: "Random"

Time
Passes along
Bringing and taking
When they belong
And leaving things behind
Such as accidents and discovery
And always
The misery
Of the past to be
Experienced by the people
Who don't
Deserve it at all
When it leaves
It leaves its trace
The trace of destruction
Leaving no happiness or grace
It leaves behind
So much despair
To the ones who had hope
And the ones who cared
But it didn't even glance at them
Leaving them lost
With a different world for them
And as the days
Come and go
It brings and takes
Leaving people alone
