There's a world out there
Filled with happiness, trust and hope
Nobody there thought
That anyone couldn't cope.

They al seem to
Just chat and enjoy life with each other
And be happy
With themselves and no other.

To them
Nobody else seems important
They only care
For their significance.

They don't know of
Anybody else out there
Even if they did
They'd never care.

But there's an island
Filled with dispair, betrayal and fear
There's someone there
That person, is me.
