---
title: "I just didn't"
writer: "Random"
slug: "i-just-didnt"
source: "AllPoetry"

I didn't think you'd do that
To mess up my whole life
And "sorry" isn't the magic word
To pay for all my strife

You don't mean it, do you!
It's right in your eyes
I have had enough
Of your stupid lies

The first time you talked to me
When you asked if I had time
I sould have seen that cold face
Behind that made-up smile

But I just didn't
And now it all pays
You've left me alone and helpless
For all the nights and days

Even if I found another person
It would always be
That I'll end up in my loneliness
Now a simple routine for me
