title: "The last beam of light"

Stuck in a tunnel
I look at the light
I know I can't win
This eternal fight

I look down
Holes in the floor
Nothingness waiting for me
Not needing anymore

I still think
Should I move on?
I think again
Should I be gone?

I see holes in front of me
Some even seem welcoming
I can even hear some
Asking me to come in

It was too tempting
I watch the last beam of light
As I jump and fall
Knowing that's where I should be
