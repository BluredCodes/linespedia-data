Tapping of a pen
intensifies the loneliness
of a thousand absorbed men
ambitions buried under mess

Rattle of a can 
down a deserted street
echoes the future's longing
that their children should agree

Streaks of rain
coursing down the glass
iterates her pain
of images forgotton from the past

Tapping of a pen 
amplifies my confusion
that a crowd could be so near,when
I feel immersed in isolation.
