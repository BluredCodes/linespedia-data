title: "The Journey They Went"

The journey They went
while I stood stationary
The Anger I rent
upon the unwary

The changes They made
while many years, I strive
The Outrage I did save
until They were lithe

The army They established
while I contemplated
The Derision I endured
from Them, the narrow-minded

I the individual
