title: "tell me"
writer: "Why"
slug: "tell-me"

Broken promises
Forever creating hope
Lying to my face.
Treating me like a dope

Dad’s constant lies
Tales of a life more grand
However this life
Is miles from where I stand

Yearn to be told the truth
Not live a life of fabrication
But the society full of lies
Leads to social isolation

All I want is some certainty from him
To know where I stand
Maybe if I was more sure of life
I’d drop this knife from my hand
