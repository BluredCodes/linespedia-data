---
title: "steel red"
writer: "Why"
slug: "steel-red"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

strange attraction to my silver friend
staring at it's cold sharp face
slide it across my skin
wanting the memory to erase

it's been a long time
but back to my old life
not handling pain at all
and using my shiny old knife

ahh the stinging
of course it has to hurt
it covers the pain inside
blood runs down my shirt

i throw it far away
try not to again give in
but without even thinking another thought
the steel again hits my skin......
