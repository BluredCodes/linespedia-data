Again lost in the darkness
tiring of the fight
could I keep on fighting one more day
and maybe reach that light....

The TV droans on in the background
and the phone remains quiet
another endless day spent living
on my soul's emotional diet

My day is filled with longing
praying they will call
they won't claim their lost child
the one they all appall
