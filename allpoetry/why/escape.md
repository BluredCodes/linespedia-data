---
title: "Escape"
writer: "Why"
slug: "escape"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Shivelled in a ball,
can't stop shaking.
Hiding from it all,
my sanity it's taking.

Holding in my hand, 
a capsule that could stop it all.
But before i can start to climb,
i gotta go through the fall.

Can feel the evilness,
fighting under my skin.
The thoughts that are invading,
my head is in a spin.

What was i thinking??
hate this prescription crap.
Hooked on taking a pill,
got to escape this trap.
