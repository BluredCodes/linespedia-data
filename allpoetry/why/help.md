approaching another turn in life
make a choice of which way to go
take a risk on a new oportunity
or stay on the doomed road i know

the life i live is of ups and downs
one minute high in the sky
next minute i'm crashing to the ground
praying that i would just die

these doctors say they can solve it
the hole in my mind they can fill
but they can't fix anything
happiness doesn't come in a pill
