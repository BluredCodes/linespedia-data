title: "I Love You"

I love you and everything about you,
Your essence makes life feel brand new.
The sound of your voice sends chills down my spine,
I thank God we met, I'm so glad you're mine.

I can't imagine life without you or how it would be,
I feel I'm complete with you sharing life with me.
You light up a room with your beautiful smile,
When I'm with you I feel as carefree as a child.

I love what you believe in and all that you stand for,
This match is Heaven sent, I could ask for no more.
The most important thing is something you already know,
As long as we keep God in this together we'll continue to grow.open for suggestions,
on how I can improve
