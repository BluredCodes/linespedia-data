title: "Thanks Rick Springfield"

Hey look who it is, was all that she had to sayI remember that look, won't ever forget that dayIt was Rick Springfield, yep he was standing thereMy girl was now so happy, that I dragged her to the fairShe was staring at him, God I thought she was gonna pass outShe tried to call out his name, but all she could do was shoutShe looked like she had been transformed backwards in timeAll she said was wow he looks so great, sure wish he was mineI looked around and I saw other boyfriends looking around tooI guess I wasn't alone now, I guess all the other guys there knewThat this was a mistake taking our girls to see this superstarWe were all reliving our worst date, being left alone in our carI reached out for Mandy's hand, but alas it was nowhere to be foundWe had become deserted men forever lost inside Rick's FairgroundWith the music playing and Rick singing all his songs oh so loudI guess even I couldn't help to start to feel a little proudI had taken a chance, by taking my sweetheart to see her dreamGuess I had a good time also, so did the other guys it would seemAfter the concert, Mandy gave me a little kiss as I shut the car doorMr Springfield, thanks for bringing back the romance to us once more
