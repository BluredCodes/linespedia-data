title: "Chance To Kiss You Goodbye"
writer: "John J. Ruppert"

I got a letter in the mail today
After I finished it, I began to cry
It told of a lost lover, lost forever
I never had the chance to kiss you goodbye

It was sent to me by your mother and father
They told me that you would want me to know
They explained how you became ill and passed
And lying in your bed, about me you asked

I remembered back to those wonderful times
When we both shared a simple common dream
A life filled with love and empty of any pain
After reading that letter I'll never be the same

I remember you saying that you could never leave
Then I watched you walk away from me in disbelief
Neither of us knew what went wrong with our love
I would give anything to see your face once more

Through the years and the tears that letter will be gone
But that love that I have inside for you Veronica will go on
I wish I was there by your side to wipe the tears from your eyes 
And I wish I had just one more chance to kiss you goodbye

My Sweet Veronica
