Eternal High

You take me higher and higher,
for you Lord I have such a fire
blazing within this hungry soul,
feeding, filling, making me whole.

How I long to dwell by Your side
I relinquish all, my foolish pride. 
All I am, I give to You, my king.
You overjoy, making my soul sing.

There is no other place I'd rather be
than in Your presence and your glory.
What can I compare, all else is dust.
Only in You Lord, do I put my trust.

As You grant to me strength for the flight,
I open wings and find in you my sole delight.
Now I'm soaring freely above every situation,
elated by your perfect peace and admiration.

Flying high for You, never coming down,
I smile on my journey, not ever to frown.
Even if I fall, your hand would catch me.
My hope stays in your grace and loving mercy.
