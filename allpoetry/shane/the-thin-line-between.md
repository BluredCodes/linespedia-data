title: "The Thin Line Between..."

I want you , I need you
You hate me, I’ll kill you

Painful expressions, are worth a thousand words
They shoot my thoughts down, like innocent birds

Words are on fire, and yours are aiming at me
My worlds on fire, the only remedy is you

I can’t stand falling in love with you,
So look before you love,  or you’ll get run down

Don’t forget who you are, or what you were
When god made you
Love is a risk, it can pay off or kill you
