---
title: "I Know"
writer: "Shane"
slug: "i-know"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I know I’m not blind
So I
Guess I’m just stupid

I know I’m too kind
So I 
Think I’ll take it back

I’ll take my love from you
I’ll take it back again

I know I don’t need eyes to be
So I
Use common sense to see

I feel I’ve used up all my life
So I
Will give love a chance

I’ll bring me back again
Back to where fun was then

I see my reflection in the mirror
So I
See what I am

I know I’ll see myself
So I
Must be myself

I’ll do the best I can
To live againIt's poor it's short and it's here!
