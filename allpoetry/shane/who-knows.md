---
title: "Who Knows?"
writer: "Shane"
slug: "who-knows"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/49933-Who-Knows--by-Shane"
---

Everybody knows this is nowhere,
Everyone knows I am no-one

Nobody knows me,
I’m so alone…
That no-one needs to know

Nobody owes me,
A single thing
So I seek profit
In everything

Nobody knows this,
But it don’t make sense
My voice is my only defence
So I lay down my head, 
And face the consequence

Everybody knows 
That I never lie
But nobody knows
That fact is a lie
Nobody knows that
Everyone knows

I was born a hero,
But nobody knows,
In a world gone crazy
It makes no sense
To try to reason
In your defence
Because nobody knows
