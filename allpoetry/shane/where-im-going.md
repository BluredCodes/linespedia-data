---
title: "Where I’m Going"
writer: "Shane"
slug: "where-im-going"
source: "AllPoetry"

I haven’t been a sinner, but I haven’t been a saint
Sick of my impersonations, of people that I hate
I haven’t killed but I’m no saint, I don’t want to leave for hell
But it’s where I’m going

Looking for my mind, to give it back to myself
Seems like my only chance to avoid this hell
When it’s clear nothing can help
Because it’s where I’m going

I see a bright, though it shines it’s not for me
I seem to hide light, it’s all I’m good for
Though I can’t make a change, I’d like to replace
Where I’m going

So I guess it’s my time,
What could be worse for me god?
Betrayed again by your laughing earth
But I still love you man, no matter…	
Where I am going

Though I feel the need, to blame everyone but me
I can’t see the point, of contesting my reality
So tell me now god, tell me as a man
Tell me, just where I am going to?

So should you make the change,
I know that I can change
So your finished with me
My last wish in this life
Please tell me where I am going

Why am I going to hell?
Where’s heaven now
Please tell me where I am going
