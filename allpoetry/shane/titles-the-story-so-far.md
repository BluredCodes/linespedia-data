There's ‘Seven Shades Of Evil’ cast from a ‘Sky Of Darkness’
The shades of evil with the darkness as their harness
In a ‘City Of Spite’ down by ‘The Ancient Sea’
There’s only one way to set you free
You’ve got to ‘Trust In Me’ and learn to believe

‘Space’ and ‘Time’ won’t wait for you
But ‘Execution’ waits for you
Sitting ‘On My Own’ I just had to be alone
‘Don’t Wait Until Tomorrow’ to find your way back home
Marching to ‘The Seventh Symphony Of Death’ , Soldiers breathe their final breath

Into the breach once more, where ‘The World Is My War”
Tell me once more ‘What Am I Living For?’
Children hide your heads under your cover
A child without the ‘Consequences Of Colour’

I ‘Found Love - Lost My Mind’ , if you seek then you will find
But with no use like a picture to the blind
Hungry and sad had enough of your bitching
I took a trip to ‘Heaven’s Kitchen’
And tasted ‘The Soup Of Life’

In a day, where the children run away
In a day, Where a man gets on his knees to stop and pray

We’ve seen it all before
Like ‘The Crying Man .and. War’
Im loving you, Loving me
But ‘Alone With A Soul.s .- Insanity’
Gotta get your life in gear
Now ‘Alone .in. - Fear’ 
But from now till the end theirs no need to pretend
That we make ‘Good Friends .but. Better Enemies’

‘The Children .and the.- Birds’
Are filled with empty words
I know I’ll get this wrong
Like a devil’s  ‘Loveless Song’

Like ‘A Letter To My Love’
In ‘The Sky Of Love’
I can get it right this time

‘Wasted Time, Time Taking Time’
Why we wasting all this ‘Time’
Stealing my love like blood to a leach
Tell me ‘Whatever Happened To Speech?’
Have no shame but know that life is ‘The Game’

Like ‘The Dance Of The Butterfly’ 
‘Dancing Angels’ always ask why?
Does life have to be such a bore but it’s not that simple anymore
‘One Two Free! Four - Sending Soldiers Off To War’

Your living in a twirl and your wasting your time
Like the ‘History Of The World .and the.- The Death Of A Mind’
Your lives filled with holes and the perils of pain
It’s ‘Tormented Souls - A Song For The Devil’ again

A shot goes through you and then you are through
Become The Beast?, The Beast Becomes You!’
It takes persistence to keep winning and in the end
It’s about ‘Existence & The Beginning - Revenge’

I Am Myself - The Thoughts And Words Of A Madman
Be yourself, but you’ll end your life as a sad man
“He’s coming home!” said the pilgrim 
Like an ‘Old Peoples Home -.filled with. Children’

But like the slithering snail life spells hits trail
Of ‘Betrayal - If You Can’t Beat Them Join Them’
What God Is This? - The Creator!
Or merely a sadistic dominator

To a poor reception from the people he will flatten
The Reality Of Perception - Did It Happen
Until our end living at the blade of the knife
The End - Closing The Book Of LifeThis was a poem that included the names of every poem Dan had written at the time.
