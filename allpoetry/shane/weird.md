title: "Weird"

The sun came out,
He said to my face
“Hello, I’d like to burn you”

The hunger grew
As he said to you
“Hello, I’d like to eat you”

The school kid thought 
As he was taught
“Hello teacher, I’d sure like to meet you”

The open door
Touches the floor
“Hello, Can’t you silence me?”

The crooked hat
The man on the moon
“Someone tell me what I’m saying”

A lost meaning
Discovered in words
“I can’t help but be confused”

A feeling that’s gone
A lover that’s found
“Every time I look up, I see frowns”

Beautiful whale
Harpoon to the heart
“I guess they want to kill me too”

A meaningless poem
Lost within’ words
“I guess that I have to stop now!”
