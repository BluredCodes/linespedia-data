---
title: "Bus Love"
writer: "Shane"

Yeah it’s me who’s looking at you,
Watching your beauty live
Although I cannot move
I’d like to hold you now

Time in this place,
Is time with you
It’ll be over in 15 minutes or so
Until then I think of you as I let you go

My confidence is hidden,
It wants to touch your face
We’ll be apart when we leave this place
My friends they talk me, but you still have my eyes

I need in the quiet times,
The calm before the storm
I love you in my minds eye
Hoping it leads me with you through the door

This isn’t just a new crush,
I loved you long before
On the inside I want to
Speak to the space behind you eyes

You're just 2 isles away,
Might as well be
15 miles from me
I wish you could stay…
Until I have my confidence

Your hair is a dream for me,
The only dream, that I can see
God give me a chance
Give me an assured stance

Let me speak now,
Let me move now
Let me go to her
And let what must occur, go through

Now she’s gone,
And I think of all her beauty
But it’s hidden behind my fear
I spend my summer waiting, to see if she’ll be here next year
