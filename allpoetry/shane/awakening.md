---
title: "Awakening"
writer: "Shane"
slug: "awakening"

In the morning I wake up
And I feel dead
It takes me quite a while
To figure out my head

I’m trying,
To find what’s left of me
Searching for things, lost inside of me

In the morning it takes me quite a while
To become myself again
As I lye awake I miss my dream
Of living, A life I’ve given to me

I’m trying
To kill what’s given to me
I’m dying, to see what I’ll let me be 

In the morning, I hold my head in my hands
And I die again
I’m awoken by life itself
As it hit’s me, knocks me to the floor

I’m trying,
To live life given to me
I wonder, why I bother to be

In the morning it takes me quite a while
To clear my head, as I lye alone
I think to myself, I’ll die in the bed
Stay here for the rest of my days, until I’m dead

I’m trying
To raise from this cemetery
I’m dying, ‘cos my body won’t let it be
