After years of living, alone within’ myself
It’s clear that behind me, there was something left
As I look ahead I realise…
My face is changing before my eyes

As I look ahead I realise…
After years of waiting, I haven’t found myself
After years of trying, It’s someone else
After all these years I cannot see, I cannot feel

I realise that…
After all these years I cannot see my face
I realise that after all these years I’m dying in this place
I realise, What is mine, 
The freedom I searched has left me blind
Departed I’ve been for years, departed from my mind

No, I’ve no will left…
After all these years
I’ve lost the best
After all this, all these, reality becomes my fear

No I’ve no will left, 
Only a hole, where my mind was kept
Now I realise that after all these years
My prayers have fallen on deaf ears
