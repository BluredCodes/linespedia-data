---
title: "Deathbed"
writer: "Shane"
slug: "deathbed"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Memories surround my head,
As I sit here waiting in my bed
My friends they visit nervously
Almost as though they don’t know me

My hours are few,
But friends must meet their own curfews
They may never see me again,
But they can’t be late again

I hope I die in time for them,
Die for their convenience
They live like slaves,
I ask who’s dying really?
