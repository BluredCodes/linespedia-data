---
title: "Forever Untitled"
writer: "Shane"

Well, The epic song she is long
Beautifully powerful, wing of a swan

The epic snake, forever awake
Don’t close his eyes, barely trusts himself

The ancient snake, barely awake
Lying down naked near the epic lake

The powerful man, waving his hands
At the kill in the road, it’s life taken by his own fingers

The epic dad sure is glad, 
He locked his little children in the cupboard when bad

The juvenile child, running like wild
In a state of ecstasy
His daddy runs real fast
“What have you done to me?”

Now the miserable man, he does what he can
To try and forget the things that he did

Like the epic lake and the open eyed snake
He lives forever in fear and regret
