After years of waiting, I have found my place
After years of anticipating, I have seen my face
After years of complaining, I am rid of you
After years of training, I'm away from you

Now alone I live, I'm happy at last
Without you, I'm happy at last
I feel free now, alone
For the first time, because...

After years of waiting, I have found my place
After years of waiting, I have left that place
After years of training, I'm ready to be myself
After years of dreaming, I feel it within' myself

I'm free without you, happy, alone too
No longer circling around myself
Life is mine, full in my face
For the first time...
