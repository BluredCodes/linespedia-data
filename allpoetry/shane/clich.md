Brighten up things will get better
Cheer up, Life’s not always this way
When I was your age love was easy
You never know, love will find a way

There’s plenty of fish in the sea
But they’re swimming away from me
Tomorrow is just another day
For them to be swimming away

Do I love you, the answers in my eyes
They reflect the love that I’m feeling insideThis is an unfinished poem by Danw
