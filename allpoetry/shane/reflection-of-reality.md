title: "Reflection Of Reality"

When I see my face in mirrors,
I don’t look like myself

My reflection looks upon me,
Upon it I see a frown
A gesture of the life that found me
In it’s pain I drown

Glaring at my own reflection,
I see something’s lost
Looking back, It see’s my obsession
My want to be myself

Life’s not easy, when you don’t know who you are
Life’s just not easy when no-one knows who you are
