---
title: "Blind Love"
writer: "Shane"
slug: "blind-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I know I love you like I love the sun,
Forever your there but not always in sight
I figure I’m blind so I close my mind
A gift of life given, you’re the gift of time

And your memory it stays behind
Though we’re different behind the eyes
My memory pictures your eyes
Your with me tonight

I’ve loved and I’ve lost and I have compromised
The feelings they break me for reasons I don’t know
How can I love you when so little I know
I cannot see you, so you can’t be there, I guess I’ll go

Though my feelings get left behind,
My mind feels your here with me tonight
Your hair shining in the moons light
But only in my mind, do they say that love is blind
