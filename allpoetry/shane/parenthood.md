---
title: "Parenthood"
writer: "Shane"
slug: "parenthood"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

God damn that child, it ain’t what it used to be
God damn that child, keeps talking back to me
I raised you, I fed you, I loved you
But what does all that mean anyway?

God damn that child, It took my youth from
Left only pride, to take away, it gave away my life
The future ain’t what it used to be,
You ain’t gonna be happy till I’m underground

Feel it grow, watch him explode
In a fiery burst of rage, the targets set on parenthood

God damn that child, life was a gift from me
But the child threw it all away
God damn that child, He took youth from me
God damn it child, Old and alone I lay
