I’m so happy I’m crying
Or maybe I’m bullshitting you
You said we’re all born to die
Well I was born to live

Pills they take away my headache,
But you put it there
I just want you to know that…
I lost your key to the door

I hear you knocking but your not welcome in mine,
You screamed, you cried but I’m not giving up this time

The world is mine and now the pain is yours,
Well, How about that!?
