title: "What’s A Friend Worth?"

What’s a friend worth?
Any treasure on this earth?
Perhaps you have to admit
Friends ain’t worth shit

Your friends they aren’t happy
Until they get just what they want

They want you for something, 
Won’t stop till they’re done
Friendships just a hand me down,

I’ve got friends, but I’m still young
Speak to us later, and see what we’ve become

I got my friends and they’ve got theirs as well
Creative genius, well a genius that creates
That’s me defined for what I can tell

I can’t hear you but I feel the things you say
I don’t want to, but I can’t make them go away

Well I got my friends and they got theirs
Seems like I’m the only one who cares
What did I do?
To make them turn on you

You keep pointing the blame
Until it knocks someone down
Because someone said
“You like to get around”

What is a friend worth
A friend is worth less
Than you care to think
They’ll stab you in the back before you can blink

They me know me, but I don’t know myself
I’m living in a prison with them on my shelf

Friends are people who couldn’t care less
Trash your life and leave you with the mess
A friend is the one who will lend you a hand
A friend will be there for as long as you can stand

What’s a friend worth?
