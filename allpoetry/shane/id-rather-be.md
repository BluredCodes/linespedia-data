---
title: "I'd Rather Be"
writer: "Shane"

Sometimes, I’d rather be me
Than all of what you can see
Just a little bit
Of what’s me

Strong mind, I’m weak
Hard lines and my sunken cheeks
I’d rather be, someone than me
I’d rather be, Someone who matters

Abandoning the life, that got me here
I’d rather be, much more than me

I’d rather be, 
With an animal…
Inside, I’d be clean
With a criminal
I’d feel so clean

Just an animal,
Is all I mean
Individual, as a stereotype
Most of all, I’d fit in
