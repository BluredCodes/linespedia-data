---
title: "Broken"
writer: "Shane"
slug: "broken"
source: "AllPoetry"

Some of these things they work for me,
A bottle of pills take the pain from me
That ain’t the way to be free I know
But tell that to my friends who’re all suckin’ on blow

Pay my bills, I can’t pay my kids
The price you pay for living on the skids
They ain’t gonna look up to they’re daddy
They’re just looking for all that is free

Dead mind junkies lying in the street
Weeping of their minds battle and it’s defeat
Funny how the times have changed,
I used to be different now I’m just the same

My wife said she loved me for who I am
Until the day she found a better man
I’m sitting here just doing what I can
It ain’t easy when you’re a broken man

The lord they said he would guide my way
Well I’m still waiting for that day
People say you gotta take what you get
I’ll take my chance and get another hit
