A friend of a friend…
Is a friend I’ll soon forget
Struck by a new habit, but it hasn’t got me yet
Do you remember the day we never met?

We’ve been so stupid, Not a moment I regret
Known you for so long, but I still don’t know you yet
I’m leaving someday but I haven’t left here yet
Gets me to thinking about all the years we spent

Once just a friend of a friend,
Now you’re a friend of mine
Spent good times together
We had fun in our prime

As friends we’d be forever,
Though I have to say goodbye
Though I don’t have a reason
You’re a friend until I die
