title: "A Life Outspoken & The Restrictions of The Mind"

Like a story untold, the mystery never truly unravels
Confusing at first, we begin to except what life offers
Never truly alone but always in search of something new
We try to envision a better for ourselves

Winter time brings us the pleasure of pain,
The beauty of snow and the horrors of rain
The question is asked, but never understood
Beware that which you don’t know only venture where you should

Fear us or love us the choice is not yours
Consequence in sequence all things have to change
Set in stone at first but by time re-arranged
Follow me quickly and we will be free until we have continue our chores

Never truly evil, but rarely ever good
We as a people live as we should
A brief time for error, we fear doing wrong
A tale of life’s sadness is turned into a song

At the end of each dying sun
We rest our flesh and bones as one
Are mind lies in a state of delusion
A predetermined wishful illusion

Together are bodies and souls as one,
Like children softly play, we sleep
With the newborn sky to light the day,
We rise again with will to play

Repeating the past as though each day should differ from the last
The truth my friends is that it’s all a circle and then we die alas
