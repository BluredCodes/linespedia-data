My heart and soul, my fire
Just can’t get any higher
Cos my only desire is to be Family
In The River Of Fire!

Take me now, don’t leave me behind
The treasures of the world I’ve still yet to find

Mysteries that hide, The flames that light
It’s my burning desire, All that I require
Is lost in The River Of Fire

I couldn’t come down 
I got so high
Reaching up to the sky
In The River Of Fire

It’s a place where people know your name
And their names are all the same
Ooh, In the river of fire

You want to change but you can’t see
The life you live is history
The reason why’s no mystery
But you couldn’t get higher
Than you would, In The River Fire

Desire is the word from the heavens flaming bird, Griffin
Desire is my world and you could be my girl ‘cos It’s you that I am missing

Light the fire, burn the day
Watch the children run away
Never will they run away
From the evil mines that life does lay

‘They are safe here!’ said the liar
They are safe here in the River Of Fire

The River Of Fire runs long and bright
A supernatural flaming sight
Everything you seek is their
Don’t fear the flames they are not there
I guess I’ll wait for you until the war is through,
To join me in the river, The River Of FireThis one is over a year old and it shows.
