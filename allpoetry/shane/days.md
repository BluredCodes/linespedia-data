---
title: "Days"
writer: "Shane"
slug: "days"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

The morning she’s touching me,
She thinks that I’m that I have hope within’ me
The evening, I told I was god, told her all alone
And she believes me, believes in me

When I don’t believe myself,
I’m lying to myself

The night said he deceived me,
When he told me I was there
He cornered me, in false belief
To tell me, he never believed me

Disbelief, was all it took
To change me,
From my eyes winter fell
Like leaves from a tree

Winter was so cold to me,
She made me weep for being me
These are the pains of days gone by
Perhaps someday the pain will die
