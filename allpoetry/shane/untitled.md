Stoned, dead I've been lost within' myself
Lying motionless, somehow I'm not dead
Fortunate to be alive?
No it isn't me, I have changed

Sleeping all alone, on cold concrete sheets
Feeling nothing inside, dirt it burns the face
Stealing to stay alive, reality is the disgrace
No it isn't me, I have changed

Freezing, slowly amidst the warmth of news
On a park bench below the tree, someone reads the truth
Loitering it is not safe, unless you have no choice
No it isn't me, I have changed

Living, luxery I found myself reasons to be
Fast cars, walking beauty, I can help myself
Home filled, I am living like a man
No it isn't me, 'cos I can't change
