---
title: "Everlasting"
writer: "Shane"
slug: "everlasting"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I forgot the day…
When I lost the words to let you go again
But inside you feel the same
You feel ashamed of lies you told to my face

I took the pills to take the pain away,
But all they took were the memory’s we had made
In the sun when we were young, 
When we were one

So I cried the day I found you,
And I cried the day you left
For better or for worse the feelings kept
Inside me to this day

So now my life begins
Again I’m on my own without your hand
Without the reason I existed,
The reasons I’m a man

One day I know I’ll find you,
Once again
In a darkened day of destiny
I’ll share my hand and heart with you

When we meet again, 
I will tell you of the fun I’ve had since you were gone
And of all the worlds liars, 
I’d be the only one

Without you the world is different
I don’t like being changed
When I see your face before me
As I’m standing in the rain

On the corner of my windows,
At the bottom of a jar
I Haven’t quit love yet,
But I just cannot forget…

All the time I loved you,
All the time we shared
Of all the time I held you
Of all the things we dared

So I say so long my friend again,
But inside I know you’ll never die
Away from me your dreaming
Of a life I want for mine

So for now I’ll say goodbye my friend
I guess I’ll meet you at the end
Of love
So long, it’s so long
