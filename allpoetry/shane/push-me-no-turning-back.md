---
title: "Push Me (No Turning Back)"
writer: "Shane"
slug: "push-me-no-turning-back"

Push me to the limit and I will break again,
Dispel all the madness and unveil your evil plan
Your evil mission to fuck me till I’m dead
A new religion of wishing I was dead

Endlessly, immortality
I’m suffering convincingly
You laugh at me
Conceitedly

To cut a short story long,
I guess you got it wrong…

Well I’m your god,
Tell me who’s laughing now?
You walk alone
But I’m watching you somehow

You can take my body,
But you can’t touch my soul
Tear my skin, you’ll only find a hole
Of where a man, used to be

Forever falling down,
Tell me who’s your god now
Pushed beneath the ground
Tell me who’s afraid now

You thought you had it figured,
At the age of seventeen
Like a pack of wolves
You and your friends you thought you’d understood life

Well you don’t know shit,
You pushed me to the limit
And I ain’t afraid to die
You try to hide it with a smile

Well there will be no smiling now, you are destined to die at my hands
Like a lost sheep stray from the pack, there is no turning back
