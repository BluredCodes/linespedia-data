---
title: "...It Can Kill You"
writer: "Shane"
slug: "it-can-kill-you"

I’m here without a map,
I can’t see where I’m going
I’m lost within myself

I know I love you but I really hate you too
For all the little stupid things that you make me do
For every little painful thing that you have put me through
Without as much as a word

I’m not dead yet,
But I know I’ll be gone first
Lived through the heartbreak
I’ve lived throughout the worst

I know my love will kill me someday
And all the people who step in it’s way
I’ll have to take my life before it does more harm
I would like to say I love you, but now you know I can’t
