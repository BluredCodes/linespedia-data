It was the beginning of the day,
Or the end of the night?
I know this time, That I’ll get it right

Sitting here in the darkness
Wiping sun from my head 
Let the light be my harness
Until I am dead - until I die - eternal darkness

From temporary light to eternal darkness
My frustration lights my furnace
I’m just about to go and find the things that should be mine
Half a hundred dollars, And a non-sadistic mind

Looking to the left of me and seeing only frames
Of the pictures that life gave to me, and now take them back
I gotta get off this roller coaster, Put my life back on track
Looking to the right of me and all I see is black, But nothing seems the same
Looking right in front of me and nothing seems to change,
Take a look behind me and everything’s the same, 
Seems as though they’ve got no reason to try and make a change

Now I look ahead of me, I’m seeing only fear
And shadows of what’s left behind show me pain
Really gotta try this time to get my life in gear
The horror of reality and nothing seems the same

I rise so high, I’ve got so far to fall
Fall to ground, gravity tears me down
Spinning around in the whirlpool I have found

Equality, independently with poverty and misery,
Money to please men in medical suits
Spread disease with their chemical fruits

Children dying on the streets at seven,
No place for them in gods heaven
Smile when you think of it,
‘Cos it’s not you
Take a look again and your through

Memories of the future, a silent past
A wounded creature, who’s pain will last
Take a look through a blind mans eye,
And take a walk through a good mans mind
Look closely you’ll be surprised
With the madness that you find

Running from time, Awake in your prime
Keep running away to a better place,
Running away to a better day
But one thing remains the same
Cheat on death Cos life’s a game
Hide from time that won’t go away

Beating the clock,
Taken in your prime
Beating the clock,
