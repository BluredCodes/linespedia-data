---
title: "Lost"
writer: "Shane"
slug: "lost"
source: "AllPoetry"

I woke up bleeding from my head,
It wasn't liquid, it was not red
Seems my mind has left my head

I lost something I cannot give
It's down there somewhere with my will to live
