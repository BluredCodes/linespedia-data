---
title: "Sinking"
writer: "Shane"
slug: "sinking"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/46905-Sinking-by-Shane"
---

Is loneliness real?
Or have I just lost it?

If nobody cares, I can’t understand
The reason to be, the soul purpose of man

My mind is a river, a two sided story
On one side is fear, on the other is glory

I’m crossing the river, 
With my heart in my hand
The river flows empty
Why?, I just don’t understand

What did I do,
Why am I sinking?

The further I sink, the better I feel
The closer I get, to finally feel
