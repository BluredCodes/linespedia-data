---
title: "Aquarium"
writer: "Shane"
slug: "aquarium"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/48179-Aquarium-by-Shane"
---

His mother told him,
There was a sea full of them
She didn’t say, they’d run away
Form a line, away from him

He bides his time for wandering
As he slowly approaches them
Drifting in a state of fear
He resurfaces to the shore

He keeps drifting
He move’s by consequence
Until he moves he’s not a man
If he spits out his fear and swallows his pride
He’ll meet her on the other side

She’ll be standing,
Beautifully motionless
Like a ray of light,
His ray of hope

He’ll find himself staring
As he remembers the shame
He says “No more drifting…”
“I’ll stop my breath, I’ll take my chance”

Days would pass him by,
His only company was his own reflection
She changed his ways, put him in the right direction
He’s by her side, by himself

With the utterance of a word,
This fish will find his bird
Together they’ll be sunlight
Until the final curtain draws to a close

He’ll find the way, to her side
By the time he does, he’ll be a man
He’ll be one with the inner sand
He’ll be free from the sea and plunge into love
