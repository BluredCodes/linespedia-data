---
title: "Cell"
writer: "Shane"
slug: "cell"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/52204-Cell-by-Shane"
---

Setting trends to follow,
Just to follow them yourself
Tell me what’s the point…
Of life itself, when know one else wants you?

A darkened sky awaits you as you sleep
To wake into it’s sadness
Awake you’ll surely die, you wonder why
Sorrow says you have to die

Your feeble mind restricts you,
From you want to be
Your own opinion it conflicts with
What your capable to see

Your drive it slowly,
As you drive down the road of life
But who’s to know, where you’ll go… from here
The nation cannot see, following examples is not for me

You doubt it all, because you know it can’t make sense
You thought to end it all, but you missed the final fence
The final hurdle that keeps you sane
Keeps you warm and dry, from insanity’s rain

Words can be misplaced but you know just what you said
Follow in the footsteps of those who tread…
Those who would be sooner dead, than face dulling truth
So you wonder why, you can’t die within’ yourself

You thought of it, a way out of the circle of life itself
There’s a way out of this hell, but who can tell…
Where it lies within’ the cell of life
Who can tell, escape the cell of life
