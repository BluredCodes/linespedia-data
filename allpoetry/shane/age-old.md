---
title: "Age Old"
writer: "Shane"

Doesn’t want it
Doesn’t need it
Doesn’t need to live to see it, to feel it, to be it

Since the dawn of time, man has lived to surpass his prime
But what for?, for what?
Sentiment, irony, to live the righteous of equality

The clock begins and starts again
No time limit, When’s the end?
No limits set

Punched in at birth,
Punched out half way
Hard lines and pain are here to stay

It’s all downhill from here, unwanted, unneeded
A frozen image of the past is all that’s left of youth now passed
No answers lie in the age old glass, this time

We will survive this time?
The endless loop of life

The repletion of words spoken before,
Can’t stop the rain of days, fallen before

I’ll do today as yesterday, as will I do again
Doesn’t bother us now, not here somehow
I see what life is for
What it’s worth, priceless, like peace now sold
The cost of the life?, The price of growing old
