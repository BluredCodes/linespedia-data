Bide your time,
Your worlds apart
A broken watch
Is a broken heart

Bide your time
Whilst worlds combine
Take you time
Or the worlds will collide

A broken heart
Is a broken watch
Everything stops
And nothing works

Bide your time, make your move…
When the time is right
With your heart in your mouth
Check the time, it may be tonight

Make the move…
Time it right
And worlds apart become truly one
Then you can have some fun, for love has now begun
