---
title: "Black Hole"
writer: "Shane"
slug: "black-hole"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/47795-Black-Hole-by-Shane"
---

Black hole, swallow me
Take me from reality
Young one’s pray
For a better day

Black hole, spit me free
Into the world, where I can see
The love in the air
The hope for me, somewhere

Goodnight dark days,
Take me to the place
Where all the angels play
I want to see my face
With all it’s love in place
