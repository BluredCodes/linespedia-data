title: "This One (For Those Who Care)"

This one’s for all the helpless…
This one’s for all the helpless people of the world
My homeless friends my disciples
The one’s who created me

Would you look at me now?
Well, would you look at me now?

This one goes out to all those that I forget,
To all the people sitting crying and living in regret
This one’s for you,
It’s all for you

This one goes out to my friends on the street
Laughing in the faces of every people that they meet
This goes out to my spelling out of place
They put a smile on my face

Is it you, who forgot me?
Is it true, that you won’t look at me now?

This one is for all those alone
This one’s for myself,
For all those sitting weeping,
For regret inside themselves

This one goes out to all the people that I love,
The ruthless lawyer suing and the dead man above
All the folks that are drowning,
In a river of love

This one’s for you,
I don’t always speak the truth
But this one’s for you

This one goes out to the man who cannot see,
All the folks alone and those with the disease
This one is loved, this one’s enough
Enough love for me

This one’s not for those who’d marry me
For simple minded popularity
Not for those who paddle in the shallow end
For them there’s only one thing I recommend

Wake yourself, help yourself
Look at all you have accomplished
And all your fools gold wealth

This one’s for a mother, who lies dead with her child
This is for the love for her, that stays…
Until and past we’re old
This one’s for me, this one is meThis one kind of got derailed at the end, you'll know what I mean if you read it.
