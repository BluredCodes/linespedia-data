---
title: "Born Again... (I Wish I Was)"
writer: "Shane"
slug: "born-again-i-wish-i-was"

I wish I was born again,
With nothing on my mind
To be alive, to feel again
To live for all that time

Learn to walk, to love
Learn to talk,
To those who mean the most to me
And those who gave my life to me
Again

I wish I had, another try
To be alive, and feel the things I missed
This time,
To kiss the girls I missed, this time

Born again, I wish I was
But nothings new to me
Born again, I’m free
To feel, to be me

I’ll try, not to point the blame
Because everything would go my way
But I know, my luck would change
And I would say, Can I be born again?
