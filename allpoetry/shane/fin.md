Life is so boring
I spend mine ignoring
Everything that is fun

Footballers scoring
Cups of tea pouring
And they say I’m satisfied

Newspaper crinkling
Grandpa’s a thinking
About placing a bet on a mare

Mothers a sleeping
With secrets she’s keeping
She don’t want son to know

Grandma’s is crying
‘Cos she knows that I’m dying
 But I won’t tell anyone else

Daddy’s daydreaming
He knows I’m leaving
For reasons I didn’t say

Children are playing
And they are saying
“Why do you have to go?”

Thieves they are stealing
They take all the feeling
But they can’t take my pride

Now I’m just lying
Yes I am dying
To know what they think about me

So keep your tea pouring
I don’t mean to be boring
Just let me go

It’s the same in the end
So it’s goodbye my friend
I have to goThis one started as a nursery rhyme.
