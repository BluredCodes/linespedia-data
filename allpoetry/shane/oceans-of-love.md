---
title: "Oceans Of Love"
writer: "Shane"
slug: "oceans-of-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Wasted by the ocean,
Never mind
Finally, I’m feeling open
First of kind

Your eyes have got me blinded…
Pleasantly, 
With they’re light I’m guided
Blissfully,
I’m loving you

My heart has got me stranded
Without a hope I’ve landed
Beautifully,
In a world, where all I see is your face

Hopefully,
This will last forever
Eventually,
We will die together

No fear for me,
I will do all with you
Wonderfully
I’ve a life to give you

Together we,
Will spend all our feelings
Knowing we,
Will never lose the feeling

That you and I
Were made to die
But first live forever
Live together
