---
title: "Slave (If I Had A Gun)"
writer: "Shane"
slug: "slave-if-i-had-a-gun"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/46940-Slave--If-I-Had-A-Gun--by-Shane"
---

If I had a gun I’d eat it,
Swallow the bullets down

If I had a drum I’d beat it,
To the beat of the mindless clowns

Marching to the beat of the clowns,
Take their pills and swallow down

The world in me is a world confused,
I’m lost, I’m scared, misery is my muse

If I had a gun, I would just shoot myself
Fire at the marching clowns, and then me
But no-one else
If I had gun.
