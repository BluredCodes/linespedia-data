title: "The Thought Of Love"

Though I don’t love living
I love life itself
Seeing your beauty 
Reminds me of the line
“I want to be alive”

It’s the future I’m 
With optimistic eyes
The contest the glory
The beauty my prize
“The prize of you”

To be without you it brings me great shame
But the thought of you beside me
It fills me with pride
The future it tells me inside
“That you’re my bride”
