---
title: "Vertical Reading"
writer: "Shane"
slug: "vertical-reading"
source: "AllPoetry"

You, wanted everything
Said, you wanted nothing
You, took my everything
Loved, what you took from me
Me, Now I’m the one who bleeds

You, Took all that I had to give
Never, Seemed to give a shit
Loved , Was just a dirty word
Me, Is just a single word

You, are your everything
Lied, to no-one but yourself
