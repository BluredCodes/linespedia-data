---
title: "Desperate Times"
writer: "Shane"
slug: "desperate-times"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I know I feel sad,
But I don’t want to die
The feelings I feel
Make me want to cry…

But I can’t

The reason I live is the reason I’ll die
A foolish young wonderer wondering why
Who am I?

My friends will come to me and they’ll sail away
Lifting me up and lighting the day

I know I am lost but what can I do
With all this time?

My life has lost meaning
But the meaning is there
I feel it go through me 
Like the wind in my hair

Where’s my mind

I love the feeling of sun on my face
The comfort of emptyness in a wide open space
Yes I love life

Now go on without me
I won’t waste your time
Perhaps you’ll love me
And all the things beautiful

Read in between the lines

My feelings get left behind,
Blown away are the thoughts from my mind
Desperate times, I’m living inside
