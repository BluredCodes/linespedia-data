---
title: "God's World"
writer: "Shane"
slug: "gods-world"
source: "AllPoetry"

Just 'cos I ain't pretty don't mean I can't feel
When I feel shitty I’m not behind the wheel

The world she lied, who’s god now?

Lost my first son at the age of five
Run down by a neo nazi not fit to drive

The world she lied, who’s god now?

Your god, he took my sight
But I can still see the lies he told me

The world she lied, who’s god now?

They say I’m blind, but the black man he’s so kind to me
Because of your creation, that man he’s ashamed to be

The world she lied, who’s god now?

Mothers they’re weeping,
And their children, they cry to sleep

The world she lied, who’s god now?
