---
title: "Too Late (Coming Home)"
writer: "Shane"
slug: "too-late-coming-home"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/52733-Too-Late--Coming-Home--by-Shane"
---

I said I’d leave you 
But I’m coming back
A life alone
I know I’ve wasted mine

I only love the past
And that’s where I will stay
For the rest of my years
And all from this day

Slowly I journey to see you again
Inside I’m yearning to be me again
The oceans are turning as day passes day
Aged I’m learning and faithful I pray

The full moon is looking
For friends it can’t find
An old man I see it
It’s feelings like mine

Soon I will return
I hope all is well
I rescued myself
And maybe you as well

Back at the door
But your not waving this time
Nobody’s home and the light isn’t on
Sadness I feel as I left you too long

On my way back I’m wandering why
My sorrow grows bigger within’ my mind
I only had to try, and not lie to myself
I feel on the inside, I wish you hadn’t died
