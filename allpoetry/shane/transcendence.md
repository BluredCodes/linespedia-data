---
title: "Transcendence"
writer: "Shane"

---

So much to be written, yet so little to say
Confusion that only life itself can take away

Saviour transcending from the stars,
Mesmerised by the material world
Mesmerised as he improvises life

Squandered youth it makes me cry
Old mans time is bound to fly

One man changes as the days go by,
Wishing he was something else until he dies
From the cradle to the grave I was born to be saved

An easy life, is a life that’s lost
A good man dying, hanging on a cross

Sometimes you win, sometimes you don’t
Just because she doesn’t love you
Doesn’t mean that she won’t

It’s all a game so those that frown,
Put up all the pins and then knock them down

Time says your losing and you can’t win
Well set your watch backwards, 
And never give in

So much to say, but it’s not on my mind
Speaking the truth is seeing for the blind

Life is set, yet not defined
So take your chance, 
And take what you can find

Dogs will fight and dogs will die,
The cancer and a maniac still make us cry

All along the rules the same,
There ain’t no damn complaining
In the dying game

To live is die and it’s a crying shame,
The winner is a fool in the losing game
Called life

With all that’s said it makes me wonder why
There’s so much to be written, yet so little to say
