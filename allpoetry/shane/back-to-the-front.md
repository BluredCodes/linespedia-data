---
title: "Back To The Front"
writer: "Shane"

Back to the front
I’m at the front of the back
With a re-occurring feeling
That I’m here ‘cos I’m black

Front of the back
Of a line that’s white
Watching me as they kick my friends
What colour are we to fight back?

Front of the back
Black at the back
White at the front
Back to the front
