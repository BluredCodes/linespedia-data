Madness, he’s in my head
Madness, he’s under my bed

Madness, he’s in the back of my car
Madness, he says I’m going too far

Madness says I’m going too far into my head
Madness, he puts these thoughts into my head

Madness is out from under my bed
Madness is saying that I’d rather be dead

Madness is as madness says
Madness says I’d rather be deadThis one truly is a b rate poem, but I figured I'd post it anyway.
