The Color of Ocean

Who said it was blue,
By the way, 
Or green and flecked with foam?
Who is that color-deluded person
Who did not look
And see the myriad lights
Glittering in your shallows and depths?
Blue – ridiculous – turquoise is closer to the truth,
Green – absurd. There were emeralds strewn upon your surface
As if some careless jewel thief had dropped his cache in passing.
And of the line of charging mystical beasts rising with purity
To rush upon the golden shore,
What nothing word is this to describe them as foam?
