---
title: "You Fade"
writer: "Castaway"
slug: "you-fade"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/45776-You-Fade-by-Castaway"
---

How whispery white your skin has become,
how lack lustre your eyes which gleamed
before
like jewelry.

My dreams take me to you
when you were younger
and I was a child.
I remember the scent of you,
the heat of your skin in summer,
the way you used to sing,
the smell of gravy in the kitchen.

Your dark hair and freckly skin
were always a source of surprise to me,
and the way you laughed, 
carrying me away with you
as if I were a little boat on a river.

Behind those darkening eyes of yours
are the memories surfacing,
taking you back and away from me
until in the end
there will only be memories;
mine of you.
