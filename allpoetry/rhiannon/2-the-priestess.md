She silently sits alone in the small chapel.The sisters pacently wait for her return Knowing that she brings judgment and insightShe has traveled this path for a long time.A giver of grace and wisdom secluded in knowledgeThat has the power to restore and to delay.A lonely life blessed with higher callings.ForesightInspirationFrustrationShe relies on herself. Is it enough?Based on the Tarot cards.
