---
title: "Dreams of Time"

upon horses of devotion
ride destiny's warriors
seeking new foes of fate
to forever join in battle

in crowded solitute
sits silent lonliness
keeping company with compassion
and forgottens myths of god

hear the song of the wind
some what out of tune
and incessently droneing
with the lost lies of truth

dreams of time
dreams of time 
i think i am drowning in
dreams of time
