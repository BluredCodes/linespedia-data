With a determined glance he rises from the throneWith strong confident steps he leads the wayAnd I hope only to follow and share in his glory.He pauses only to listen to the will of the peopleAnd then finds the courage to make the hard decision.Our fates rest in his hardened, tested handsHypocrisy is never too far behind.ConvictionAuthorityIrresponsibilityPower is a dangerous tool in the hands of the wise and the foolish.Based on the Tarot
