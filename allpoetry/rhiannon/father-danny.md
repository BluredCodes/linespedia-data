Father Danny was a good man
He was strong in his Faith
He was righteous and caring
I shall miss him deeply.

I am not a Christian
Nor have I ever been
My beliefs follow a different stream
I bath in different waters

But Father Danny was a friend
And a wise one at that
He did not approve of my Paganism
But he approved of me

The point is we were different
Yet still we shared a bond
I don't understand this world sometimes
Goodbye my friend
