Dancing to his own unheard musicThe Fool spins and twirls on the cliff's edge.There is no time but now.What better way to spend the dayThan chasing rainbows and dreams?Live the fantasticalAnd know what life truly is.Dance SingDreamPerhaps The Fool is not so foolish after allThe first in a series (I hope) based on the Tarot.
