With the hand of the mighty MotherThe wheel continues to turn around us.The new replaces the old and the Queen dances.The songs of our spirit sing out with strengthThat she has given to us, her children.And yet when the womb is ungiving and unfaithfulThe Empress weeps great tides of unending woe.CharitableHarmonyInfidelityMother of one, Mother of all. Turn.Based on the Tarot.
