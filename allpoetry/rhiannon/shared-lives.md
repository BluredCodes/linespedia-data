title: "Shared Lives"
writer: "Rhiannon"

Sing to me old man
sing of the life you knew
sing of joys and laughter in a world of love
sing of heartaches and dissapointments
sing of your heart

I love the tales I hear in the wind
the soft truth in technicolor
surrounds my soul and excites my spirit
I can taste your food

To know the lives of others is to live them
to feel their heartbeat in your chest
to love their loves and fear their fears
To share a divine orgasim

Sing to me once more old man
sing untill the candle burns out
