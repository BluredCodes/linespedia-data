With a slight raise of a frail, weathered handAnd the whispers of a cryptic, lost languageThe Magician controls the unseen forces around him.Using his hard won skill, his wisdom and powerHe pulls the strings and makes the puppets dance.With a snap of his fingers he lights the wayTo Bliss or Damnnation, we are unsure.ImaginationIngenuityKnowledgeBut the price can be ever so high.Part 2 in a series on the Tarot.
