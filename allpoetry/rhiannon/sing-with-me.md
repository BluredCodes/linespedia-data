---
title: "Sing With Me"
writer: "Rhiannon"
slug: "sing-with-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/346-Sing-With-Me-by-Rhiannon"

Sing with me my friends
 sing of ancient wisdom
Of powers and magics that are no more

Sing with me my friends
 sing of melting rainbows
Of endless nights and moonbeams

Sing with me my friends
 sing of comets and fortunes
Of gypsies lost in elder woods

Sing with me my friends
 sing of soldiers and warriors
Of fairies and demons dancing together

Sing with me my friends
 sing of Kings and Queens
Of witches and dragons and vampires

Sing with me my friends

no, on second thought,


let's just watch MTV
