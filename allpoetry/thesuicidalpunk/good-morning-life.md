good morning…  life
breathing
walking
stares and laughing
pointing and gawking
humilation and mocking
messed up hair
and my own sense of style
the social outcast
all the while
listens to punk rock 
not a lot of friends
but the ones that I do have
will be there till the end
sleeping and crying
distracted and confused
feels like I’m dying
burnt out and used
deception and lying
abuse and destruction
fear and assumption
failing 
trying
goodnite… life
