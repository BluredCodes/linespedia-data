Tonight I’m standing at the edge of the earth
Or maybe it’s the edge of a cliff
I might not ever know
I only know I want to jump, and land below
Not on my feet
Who knows...
I hope I will die down there...
Wherever it may be...
I’m hoping someday...
People will understand, just who I am
I won’t come back...
So I guess this is goodbye
The hardest thing I could say
Who knows why...
All you have is memories when I’m gone
Maybe you’ll see me
Just think of someday
Dream of someday..
But for now I’m standing at the edge of the earth
Waiting for you to come back to me...
