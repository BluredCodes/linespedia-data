title: "[sitting and staring]"

sitting and staring
up at my ceiling
while no one is caring 
about how i’m feeling 
how they live
their happy lives
while i live mine
which is nothing but lies
themselves in love
when i suffer pain
not a care in the world
me, wondering if i’m sane
they will laugh
while i cry
alive they will be
after i die
