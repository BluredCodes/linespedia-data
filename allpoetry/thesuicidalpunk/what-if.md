What if I close my eyes
And your not here, when I open them?
What if I let go of your hand
And you never hold mine again ?
What if I don’t say I love you 
Will you still know that I do? 
What if  I take you for granted 
And one day you disappear?
What if hugging you right now
Is the last time that I’ll get to?
What if five minutes ago
Was our only time left?
