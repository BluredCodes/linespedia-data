---
title: "Achieving nothing by thinking"
writer: "thinking by silent scream"
slug: "achieving-nothing-by-thinking"
source: "AllPoetry"

I think I'll cry tonight 
over my unknown depression

I think I'll kill myself tonight
but not really because I'm chicken shit

I think I'll be sick today

I think I'll fail a test tomorrow

I think I'll day dream now

and I think I'll get a job next week

I think I'll lose 20 pounds
or probably gain 20 pounds

because with the way I'm thinking right now
I'm going nowhere to fucking fast.
