You're there when I need you
You listen to me bitch
You provide comfort
You fix my boo boo's
When I feel I can't go on,
the world just sucks to it's extreme,
you're there to reassure me
it's not so bad
it could be worse.
When he breaks my heart
time and time again,
you're there to piece it back together,
and remind me of what a dick he is.
When noone is there to listen,
you are.
When I have fallen,
I just can't pick myself up,
you are there, lifting me to the sky.
When I can't handle so much at once,
you're there lending me a helping hand.
You are my shoulder to cry on,
The comfort for my fears.
You keep me going,
keep me smiling.
You are an angel.
My angel.
Thank you.
When noone else would,
You did.
Without a single thought.
I wouldn't be here if it wasn't for you.
And in return;
I will be your angel.
I will fix your boo boo's,
Provide comfort for you,
Protect you from all harm,
Be there when you need.
I will keep you smiling,
keep you going.
I will pick you up when you fall.
I will listen to you,
Talk with you,
Cry with you.
I will be your angel
from now until forever.
Even after I have gone,
I will keep an eye on you
and help when I can.
You are my angel.
I am your angel.
What more could I ask for?


Dedicated to my angel...
