---
title: "A life that ended so short."
writer: "monkey angel"
slug: "a-life-that-ended-so-short"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/46255-A-life-that-ended-so-short.-by-monkey-angel"
---

He didn't get the chance to live his life.
He didn't get the chance to see his marriage grow.
He didn't get the chance to watch his kids grow up.
He didn't get the chance to fulfill his dreams.
He didn't get the chance to see the new millennium.
He didn't get the chance to live his life;
because he was killed.
He was hit by car.
He was so young.
He should have had the chance to live his life.
He should have had the chance to see his marriage grow.
He should have had the chance to watch his kids grow up.
He should have had the chance to fulfill his dreams.
He should have had the chance to see the new millennium.
He didn't get the chance because
A life ended so short.

Dedicated to Stan... RIP...I know it's repetitve, but that's how it's supposed to be.
