People can be mean, cruel, and heartless.
Just look at terrorists.
Look at the KKK.
Look at all the segregation.
People can hurt others without care.
It's like they don't feel the pain,
they don't feel the guilt.
Society is killing us,
     Killing our kids with drugs,
     Destroying our teenagers with cruel thoughts,
        telling them violence is the only way to go,
        telling them death is the only way.
     Killing the less fortunate by not
        feeding the hugnry,
        giving homes for the homeless,
        caring for the bum down the street,
        healing the sick and wounded without insuarance;
     Killing our students by not caring
       and not listening to each and every one of them.
     Killing and hurting each other in road rage and drunk driving.
     Fighting and arguing because of a difference in opinion.
     Letting arguing laed to hurt and sometimes death.
     We're taking guns to each others heads,
       not caring if the trigger is pulled.

When will this maddness end?
When will we learn there's more to life then killing each other?
Why can't we tell our children, everything's going to be ok and mean it?

This world would be a better place if we could just
     care for each other,
     compromise our different opinions,
     talking instead of fighting and killing,
     filling our kids heads with happiness and laughter,
     and not letting the worst get the best of us.Please comment honestly.  I can take the worst you've got.
