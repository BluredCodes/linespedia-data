In my world, the sun is shining bright.
In my world, there's a beautiful blue sky.
In my world, the birds are singing pretty love sucks.
In my world, the trees stand tall, full of leaves and full of lives.

As I walk down a path,
I look to my left and see 2 squirrles running around,
playing in a patch of colorful flowers.
They run up a tree, running up high to the sky.

I continue walking,
take a look to the right,
there is a fawn and his brother frolicking through the field.

I smile and laugh a little,
following the path to the end.
My world is full of happiness and joy.

I reach the end of my path,
You are standing there with arms open wide,
and a beautiful smile on your face.
My world is such a happy place because of you.

Because of you,
I can smile once again.
Because of you,
I can see the true meaning to life.
Because of you,
I can now understand why my life is worth living.
