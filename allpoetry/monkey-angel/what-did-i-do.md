---
title: "What did I do?"
writer: "monkey angel"

What did I do to get you so mad?
You just up and left.
Why?
Did I upset you?
I don't know what I did.
I'm sorry if I hurt you.
I'm sorry if I said something wrong.
What did I do?
Did I say something wrong?
I just want your attention so I can apologize.
I'm sorry.
Please don't be upset.
You are very important to me.
Even if you don't know it.
You are.
Please talk to me!
I'm sorry.
*sigh*
I'm sorry.I know it's not really a poem, but it's not really meant to be.  I'm just trying to reach out to a specific someone and get him to talk to me once more.
