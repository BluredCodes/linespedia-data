Mid-October as the trees begin their cycle
The leaves before me
They remind of the days in my life
One by one they fall, one by one they pass

A leaf falls
Never knowing 
Exactly where the wind will carry it
Before coming to rest on the cold ground 
A leaf lands

A day dawns
Never knowing
Exactly where fate will carry me
Living my life as best I know how
A day ends

Now as I look at the trees 
Naked, stripped of their leaves
Resting for the winter
I know soon that I too will be naked
Stripped of my days
Resting for an eternity 

'97 Michael D. Rhodes
