Based on an exhibition
dung beetles tandem flight 
full of sparkling merchandise

renewable human genome centers
all aspects of applicances
devoted to acquire the knowledge

largest sampled contractors
ever inducted into misadventure
internal guild of California

technical careers also market
sobriety and vocational training
to teach kids about the plight of 11,000 agencies
