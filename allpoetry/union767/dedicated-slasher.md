dream for this decay
images that guide our inner demons
the key to survival 
after a while get really annoying

promote strategic research
protect the cultural heritage
during the dormant season
organize nuclear saturday

look only 10 years later
the ultimate manifestation
low-pressure injection
don't take rejection

dripping fibers spread around
opportunity interacts with others
pedigree all people
blur the distinction
