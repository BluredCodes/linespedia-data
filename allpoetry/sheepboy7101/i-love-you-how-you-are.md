From the first moment we talked, I knew inside, your the one for me
Your words were sweet like warm apple pie, and your smile inviting me in.
But everyone can serenade you with sweet words, trying to capture your heart
so what was it really that made me stay, for the journey ahead?
There’s something about you, I feel it inside, but can not explain.
It's that something that has made you into the wonderful person you are.
