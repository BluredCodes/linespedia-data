True love can't be described by words, or shown through actions,
it is something that just happens between two people,
between two ordinary people, like you and I.
But who chose me... me over the rest of the world,
to be the keeper of a heart as great as yours.

True love is not just for the popular and the pretty,
but it's for everyone, including me and you?
Yes, It must include us, but what makes me so lucky?
I didn't empty my wallet, or show off my strength.
All I did was love you, and in return, you loved me back.
