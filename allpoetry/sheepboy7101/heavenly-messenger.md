Stars are little droplets of love
hanging in the heavenly sky,
just waiting for the chance to fall,
waiting for the chance to fly.

Through the velvet sky it streaked,
with all its reminiscent glory,
Past the window in which you stood,
to tell its own, unique, story.

"I was sent from above,
from the one you love
to tell you that they care"

"So please don't cry,
for now you know,
I am here to stay"

Away it flew, without much haste,
back to its home up in the sky,
where it waits, for another day,
