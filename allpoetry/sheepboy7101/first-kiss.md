I hear its supposed to be great,that first kiss with someone.A moment you'll never forgetbecause it's number one.But what if you made that someone,the person which you love.it'd be the greatest kiss everlike a gift from above.So when the day finally comes,and we make our first kiss.I will know deep in my heart,that this is true bliss.Ehh.... took long time to write, that's all
