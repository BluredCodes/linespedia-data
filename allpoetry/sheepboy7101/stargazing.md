Have you ever looked at the stars?  there are so many of them, so beautiful, and so mysterious; and as I gaze into your eyes, I realize the same hold true for you.  One could gaze into your glistening eyes forever, as if an an astronomer, and never find the answer to your beauty.  Your face glows with the splender of a million gallexies,  and that smile of yours, it can just melt a heart.  Your presence sweeps across me as if a shooting star in the calm azure night, so peacful, and yet mystifying.  Now have you ever looked at the stars, that look over us from above?  They remind me of you, just they're not quite as specialI havnt written any poems in a while, so it was nice to write to write again.
