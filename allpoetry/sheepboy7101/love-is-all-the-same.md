Why does it have to be this way?
Cant you understand?
Does it really matter how we met?
Love is all the same.

Every day you critisize me,
for choosing who I did,
I care about her anyway,
love is all the same.

You tell me to get a real girl,
why doesnt she count?
She holds a place deep in my heart,
love is all the same.

I hope, one day, you will see,
why I love her so.
For then you will know why I said,
Love is all the same.Well, Im proud, this only took me about 15 minnutes to write, unlike all my others taht took 3 hours, hehe
