Love is a funny little thing,Just showing up the way it does.Coming when you least expect it,from an angel far above.Filling all the wholes in your heart,that were always making you sad.Giving a purpose to your life,of which you've never had.You'll never need to worry again,about being lonely once more.For when love comes into your life,It's not going back out that doormeh...
