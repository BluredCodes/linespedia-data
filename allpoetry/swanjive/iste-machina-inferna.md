title: "Iste machina inferna"
writer: "Swanjive"

Spark of cathode raytube 
Sprung from glowing viewcube 
Stretches over yawning gaps 
To be there and not be there. 
A lying tongue of wires 
A silicone implanted AI remembers 
Things done but not done. 
Things moved yet unmoved. 
An almost plane of naught 
built upon a written word.

Both great and minute 
At similar times unveiled, a monster 
Receiving many imaginations 
Of stressed out workers daily 
Oh freight train of electrons
Or brain waves wash/entrain (?) 
Processing via gray matter 
Pulses and subsonic impressions 
The phantasm of an awakened state... 
From nebulous Discordia of limitless possibility 
Into the concrete forms 
That have come to ingest 
What strives to be 
A whole half 
A blank waiting to be filled in 
With knowledge of that which dwells beneath?
