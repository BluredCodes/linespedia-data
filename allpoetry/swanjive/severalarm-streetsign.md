On the road found I
A severalarm streetsign
Pointing in several different directions
With several mouths simultaneously preaching 
the brightness Of their respective directions

Both limbs flailing and
voices wailing wove
A silverescent opalescent 
aq'marine mist song

But after the song was over
And the mist had disappeared
To the insides of the hills
I looked down at my single leg
And sideways at seven tentecular arms
And down at the bewildered
Wanting to know
Which way they should GO!
