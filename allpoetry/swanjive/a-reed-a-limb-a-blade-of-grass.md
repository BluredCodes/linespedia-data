A reed 
 A limb 
 A blade of grass 
 Moving as 
 no lips 
 or pen 
 or words 
 except 
 the play of folding 
 hammered brass 
 swimming in a breath 
 speaks in silvery tones 

 Hammer Anvil hand arm 
 Speaker 
 Serpentine or Body 
 tweaker? 

 See how this transforms 
 Your body into animal forms? 

 Your wealth a glittering firmament spread 
 Arms as galaxies swirl about your head 

 unwrapped is the hidden key 
 The pulse itself has given me to the palace 
 Where these lips shall taste the chalice 

 A twist 
 A turn 
 A laurel thrown 
 on time 
 so stopped 
 so certain 
 so sweet.........
