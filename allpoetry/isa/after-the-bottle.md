Drown my sorrows
And ease my pain
An empty bottle
And it's back again

Night of pity
Night of shame
Come tomarrow
It'll still be the same

i can't remember
But I know the truth
It was me
And that's the truth

I can't go on
But this caosters going
Faster faster!
What am I doing

I've got pins and needles
I hear a ringing sound
My head is spinning
Round and round

Who's the bastard
that slipped me that pill
Who's the ass
Who hurts me still.......

I hurt me still

Drown my sorrows
And ease my pain
An empty bottle
And it's back again

Night of pity
Night of shame
Come tomarrow
It'll still be the same
