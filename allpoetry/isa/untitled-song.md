Its been a while since I've seen you
I saw your face yesturday
but that was not you
You were smiling
laughed at every joke i said
But deep inside i could see
You were screaming
screaming to be let out of the big big world
screaming at yourself
you think your so dumb
crouching in the corner
rocking back
back and forth
crouching in the corner
you were scared for yourself
you are so scared now

i know that you hide
but you can't hide those scars
I know you tried that night
to end your life
i can see it in your eyes

you are smiling
But you want to cry
And i love you

Your hiding
your trying
i see it in your eyes
you hiding that deep red scar
but i see it and i know
i see it and i know
that your crouching in the corner
rocking back
back and forth
crouching in a corner
and your scared for yourself 
and your so scared right now
but i believe in you
believe in you
woah i love you

You can't hide those scars
i know there that night
you took a knife to bleed
i see it in your eyes
stop smiling it's ok
i see your scars
And i know your scared
i love you
woah i love you

Stop hiding 
start crying, it's ok
You promised yourself that wouldn't
but it's ok
I'll cry with you
Nothing is ever the same
After you cry
Cry, cry, cry
Cry for the very first time

I'll tell you a secret
I got scars too
just like you
Maybe I'll cry too
I love you
woah I love you
It's been a while since i've seen you
I saw your face yesturday
But that was not you
Tell your self to have a cry or two
I love you
woah, i love you
