Some say hope
It is a ray of light
It'll bring you there
And give you flight
Some say hope
it is a rose
softness
heaven to the nose
But I say hope
it is my love
the love I have for you
You're my dove
Some say hope
It is a river,
Eases your mind
And there
love, you'll find.
