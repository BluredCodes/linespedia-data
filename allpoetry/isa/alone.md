you know, its sad,
that I feel this way.
I just want you to go away
plaese don't be mad

When I die here all alone
maybe you'll find peace inside yourself
Maybe the frusteration will leave myself.
When I die here all alone.

Leave me be.
Please don't ask anymore questions.
I'll die here alone with the confessions.
You'll know soon enough, all about me.
