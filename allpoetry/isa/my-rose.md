---
title: "My Rose"
writer: "isa"
slug: "my-rose"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/28521-My-Rose-by-isa"
---

I see her by my side
I want her there for all times.
She smells of roses
She speaks so soft
her touch sets me free
Free into a world im not sure of.
How can it be that a rose is by my side?
I see her as she moves closer,
i want to feel her touch again
It sets my feelings free
How can it be that the one i love smells of roses?
the one i love speaks so soft
How can it be that a rose is by my side.
