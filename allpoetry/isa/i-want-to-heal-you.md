---
title: "I Want to Heal You"
writer: "isa"
slug: "i-want-to-heal-you"

I can see the pain in your eyes
all the confusion and the lies
why don't you let me in your heart?,
So i can fix the broken part.

I see you there with your sorrow
and you seem so hollow
I want to fill that gap in your heart,
So me and you will never be apart.
