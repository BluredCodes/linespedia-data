Music and I
We get along
play the same tune
sing the same song

Music is my life
It goes with everything
A good day or bad day
I will still sing

When I'm down
I'll just humm along
I pray my life will change
With just one song

The crunching sound
drums beat my heart
the song bangs
into my heart

The soft sounds
are from the heart
the song flows
into my heart

The strums and strings,
stimulate my brain
The song skips
from brain to nrain.

The fast rymes
are rythmic from the heart
self expression
Enters my life from the heart

I bang my head
or just tap my feet
I can't resist
any good beat.
