Every passing day,I get a little closer to my endSo I'll just wait here for itMaybe its around the next bend.Today, maybe tomarrowWhen will I die?In what hell will I live?Or is Hell just another lie?How will it all end?With the blade of this knifeShall I cut my body open?Is this how I end my life?Am I supposed to liveSuffering in pain?No one understandsThat I've got nothing to gain
