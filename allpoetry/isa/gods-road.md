---
title: "God's Road"
writer: "isa"
slug: "gods-road"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I don't know where I'm going
I don't know what to do
I think I'm going down the right road
But when I get there what will I do?

Where is where in my life?
Who is now and who is never again?
Can I go on without these answers?
Is it worth it all again?

This meaning to my life seems pointless
All I do is run away
But still I find myself in the dark
God, please lead the way

I don't know where's up and down
Can't tell left from right
But I seem to continue on
Not putting up a fight

How is everyday so possible?
Full of Love
Full of joy
From up above?

I hold this candle 
Up to my eyes
I see through my pain
See through the lies

Everythings so confusing
Is this right road so right?
Nothing seems worth it
But never do I fight.

I find myself turning to God
A being so far away
I know he'll catch me
and lead me on my way.
