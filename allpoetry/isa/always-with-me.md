Last night I cried myself to sleepI was thinking that you left me for goodI didn't realize that you'll always be with meYou're in my heart and soulYesI will miss your touch and smellThe memories will always be aliveHow you touched my life...oh how sweetI never will forgetYou'll be by my side,Telling me to be strong and wise.So I'll dry my eyesbecause you're still with me.
