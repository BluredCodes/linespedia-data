I've got tomuch on my mind.
Everybody's yelling
Pump up the music
'till I can't hear myself think
My scars don't seem so real
The knives begin to disapear.
No one wants to deal with me
Yet I worry all about them.
Pump up the music
Sound drounds out the memories.
Base pumps out the pain in my heart
Pump up the music
from morning 'till night
I won't remember when I turn it down
all the pain hits me to the ground

Pump up the music
'Till I can't hear myself think.
It's to take a break from my life
To hide myslef from my own demons
They can't haunt me if they can't hear me breathe.
