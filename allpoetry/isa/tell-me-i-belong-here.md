title: "Tell Me I Belong Here"

Tell me I belong here
Tell me that You care
for I am about to give up

I don't know where I'll go
As long as I'm not here
The frusteration is killing me
Emptiness is my life's work

We all have to battle our own demons
You deal with yours
And let mine take me away
It's much easier that way

I could wait to see what my life will become
I will see it all fall apart.
So with this final breath
With this final tear -Good Bye

Tell me I belong here
Tell me that you love me
for I am about to give up.
