People say things on TV
and talk about their lives
 how perfect they are in every way
By saying that, they are wrong
they're not perfect
Not at all!

I wait for the day they stumble
 I wait for the day that they screw up
I wait for the day that someone laughs at them
I wait for the day that they realize the truth
They are not perfect

I want perfect hair they say
I need the perfect legs
I need that perfect red dress
I had the perfect day
I want
I want 
I need 
I need

Would you listen to yourself
Do you realize that you are the most evil thing
Do you ralize that a 16 yr old killled herself today
because she wanted to be perfect like you
but she couldn't

I hope that you'll realize the truth 
that you're not perfect

I'm not perfect
I'm not perfect
I'm not perfect
what am I gunna do?!
I'm not in the spotlight
my future is falling apart
I'm not perfect
I think I'll die now
Die now
I think I'll die now
Die now
Die now
Die now!don't ask!
