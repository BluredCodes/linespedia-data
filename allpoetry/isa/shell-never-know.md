---
title: "She'll Never Know"
writer: "isa"
slug: "shell-never-know"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

She'll never know
My hurt and why
The pain that knocks me to the floor
The misery that has become.

She'll never know
how dark, dark can be
She's never been in this abyss
I can't find my way out.

She'll never know
How much one can hate
Hate themselves 'till the end
I can bring myself to my end.

But they'll never know
That I know.
for I live in a dark place 
This place is hell.
