My dream is not to see any more tearsAnd to not see all the fears,Which fills all those who stood up and saidThey will see justuce before they are dead.My dream is that when a mother gives birthThat child will live a happy life on earthTheir lives should be filled with the sun's lightNot of the arkness that fills the nightMy dream is that all people will come togetherAnd accept pne another for what is inside no mater,What they look like or what they believe inWho their family is or what country they live in.My dream is that the world will surviveNot on living, but living as one.
