I want to go somewhere dark
where the wind talks with me
While the animals sleep
the moon peaks through the clouds
as the wolf cries to it to come down

I cry when the queen of day comes
they all open their blood shoot eyes
and reach for her in their beds

With the hellos comes the goodnights
with my king during the nights.
I want to stay in this dark place; night
Innocent ones run home
The boogie man comes out

i want to stay in this dark place
as long as it's dark I'll be awake
For soon there will be day.
