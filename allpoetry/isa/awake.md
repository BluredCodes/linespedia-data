I don't want to wake up one day
And find myslef even more lost
Knowing that I didn't fulfill my dreams
Find myslef being bossed
Don't want to wake up a drug addict
And go to sleep a slut in the afternoon
There is so much I don't want
Will I become my own stranger soon?
So many of us
Want money to much
They can't see
They smoke too much
Oh God, don't let me die young
What is it that I can't see?
Don't let me die in my sleep
I know there is someone I can be
Someday I want to marry
Make my own money
Keep being my strange self
Maybe paint the dark sky sunny
Don't want to go to the Moon
I want to see it from Mars
Never want to bleed again
But just hide these white scars
I'm awake now
I'm so scared to look around
I don't want to see this truth
I don't want to hear a sound.
