---
title: "Is it Possible"
writer: "isa"

It's time for all confessions,
release your obesessions.
Love just seems to be full of confusion,
just like dreams, they're all illusions.
Open you heart,
it's time for a fresh new start.
Be brave,
be strong,
please don't treat me wrong.
Hug me, hold me,
tell me that you care,
whisper in my ear and say you'll always be there.
I think I'm ready,
for this incrediable journey.
Where is this guy?
Who will spread my wings and teach me to fly.
Is it possible I can love again?
Will I ever find my soulmate,
who will open my eyes,
and show me how to love again?
Where ever you are,
know that I am here
screaming for you 
to come and be near.
Untill we meet,
and u sweep me off my feet,
here is a kiss send your way,
I hope that you come to me one of these days.
