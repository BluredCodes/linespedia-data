A hard sound
Crunching metal
A heaven from hell
The scent of sex
Smoking dreams away
Alive with the music
screams of pain and joy
Bodies bouncing up and down
Beer overflowing the mouths
ruining the next morning
Trojens in every poket
The night of a life
Forget the laws
Welcome to life
An unholy pssion
of sin, dirty mouths, and bodies
Pounds of highs
Drugs for all
Sweat dripping on the floor
A taste of sex in the car
the night of a life
XTC on the floor
Swallow and dance
Hips to hips
bodies all around
Scream aloud and laugh
It's the night of a life.
