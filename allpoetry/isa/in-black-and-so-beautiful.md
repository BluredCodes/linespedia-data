---
title: "In Black and So Beautiful"
writer: "isa"

In my dreams
You come to me
All in black, so beautiful
You whisper in my ear
"I love you"
Now wouldn't it be nice
If this came true
I could hold you all night
I'd give my life for you
Yes, I'm crazy
I'm crazy for you
I want to say
"I love you,
You are the one
The one that I love"
I will love you forever and ever
I can't stand to be apart from you
It hurts so much
Because I would love you so much
Now wouldn't it be so nice
If my dreams came true
I'd be the happiest woman in this universe
But for now i will just dream
I'll dream of you
In all black so beautiful
