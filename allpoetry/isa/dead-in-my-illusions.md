---
title: "Dead in My Illusions"
writer: "isa"
slug: "dead-in-my-illusions"

I called for myself in my sleep
Wake up I said Wake up!
This bliss is not real.
Just a figment of my imagination.
I walk into my house
feeling on top of the world.
Wake up I said wake up!
This happiness is not real.
Just a figment of my imagination.
Everyone is happy
smiling
They don't feel tempted by the knives.
Wake up I said Wake up!
But I didn't want to leave,
I wanted the illusion,
To live in a dream
I never woke up, never went back to reality.
I died in my dreams,
And I remain there forever
Dreaming.
Dead in my illusions.
