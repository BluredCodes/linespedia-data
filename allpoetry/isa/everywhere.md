I'm everywhere
I'm over there
I'm in yours eyes
I'm everywhere
I'm in that bottle 
that empty bottle
So good, 
so bad
I'm everywhere
I'm over there
I'm over here
I'm in your brain
controlling thoughts
I'm your emotion
I'm your depression
I'm those pills
That you pop
I'm everywhere
I'm what you see
I'm what you hear
I'm everywhere
I'm everything
I'm that pain
that sin
suicidal solutions
I'm oh so good
But to God I'm so bad
I'm everything
I'm everywhere
I am your thoughts 
I am your pain
sleep and caffine
I'm everywhere
depression striks at the vien
I hope i live
but i hope i die
I'm everywhere
I'm everywhere
