Writers Block
what a thought
Can't think of anything
All I can do is sing

"Get the party started...." It's not by N'sync
a song by Pink?
It's too catchy
but soon it will be sketchy

Too much MTV and JKJO
I forgot to finish my homework oh no!
It's 2:10 in the morning
Don't I wake up at 6:30 this morning?

Much confusion after I sleep
And I wake too a loud beep!
things don't want to go my way
they run far and further away

Don't try to edit this insanity
Don't say it's profanity
It's my mind at it's worst
Sleep deprived, now please despersce.
