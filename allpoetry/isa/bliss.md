title: "Bliss"

I always thought and knewThat I would die aloneHere on my bedroom floorI had cut myself to the bone.Laying in my own red blood,Holding my weapon of choice, a knifeI have pictured this a thousand timesHow to end my pitiful lifeSomeone walks in my room and screamsFor they see all my painThey should have realized, but they didn'tThat in life, I had nothing to gain.I am but a ticking time bombMy time is running outPlease don't try to help meI have to figure out what my life is all about.All my life I've waitedfor something better than this,But all i have come to figure out,That death is my only bliss
