---
title: "My wish"
writer: "isa"

I can almost feel my breath stop
I see them coming now
the deamons are headed my way
I can't hide anymore.
With them comes the pain
the pain is from the blade
it rips my flesh
it rips my soul
I just want to get away
they are tempting me
pushing me
stealing my only hope
there is someone else here
who is it?
I can barely see
It is God I see now
He's trying to help me
His faith in me is no morw
I fear that I am alone now
I cannot take anymore
Will you help me my friend?
I am lost and in pain
the lost abyss I am in is cold
I wish to leave the abyss.
By death I have fulfilled this wish.
