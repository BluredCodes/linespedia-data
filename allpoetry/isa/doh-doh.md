She runs in spasms

Runs into walls

When it's water she wants

A meow, she calls



She jumps on to the shelf

But before a yell, Doh Doh falls

Sweet doh doh

My cat trips on balls.



Not the smartest

way too curious

Sometimes I wonder...

Doh Doh?....Are you delirious



She's so cute

But so dumb

She needs attention

So I give her some.



She curls up

Into a ball

Purrs and seems to smile

Into sleep she will fall
