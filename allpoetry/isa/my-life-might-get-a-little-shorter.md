When the sky turns to gray,
when all the flowers go away,
My life gets a little more sad,
And my mind goes a little more mad.

When the clouds start to rumble 
And the wind begins to tumble,
My heart no longer sings in joy,
Because people can play with it like a toy.

When the whole world is blind,
When i just don't seem to mind,
My life starts to spin in a whirl
and it seems that I don't live in the smae world.

When it is colder than it looks outside,
When the only lights come from inside,
My judgement becomes a little more blurry
and I begin to seriously worry.

When the moon doesn't come out at night.
When the sun does not produce lighe,
My situation becomes a little more confused,
an my life seems to be used.

When my life jut doesn't seem clear,
When my future is not so near.
My life might get a little shorter
So my future does not have to be in any particular order.
