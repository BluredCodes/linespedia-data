Will I see my armegeton soon?
Is my end nearer than i thought?

Please Bin Ladin
Blow up my world
Get your guns and your bombs.

He's trying to bring it down
But he needs an army's help.

Help this world come together
Make us understand
that we're not loved by God
Make us understand that we're not worthy

We're not worthy
Make us understand
make us understand
Why the heaven's angels hate us

We're doomed anyway
So blow me up now
and kill my people
We are all going to die someday

Make us understand
That we are all stupid
that we are all self centered

Blow us up
Make us die
kill us all
Make us understand 
In the worst way possible

BLOW US UP!
