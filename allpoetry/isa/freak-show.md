Fade to black
Curtain down
The show is over
The stage stays set
The floor sweap clean
Lock the cages
Feed the entertainment
The freak

Use me for your show
Abuse me for your pleasure
I'm about to break
These bars are getting weaker

It gives me the creeps
How easily you seem to controll me
It's a shame
A shame that you're going down

I've made up my mind
No more freak show
I'll be the one cracken the whip
The one training you

I won't be your prize possesion
Your freak
Your supernatural  wonder
There will be no second chance
No apology to freedom for you

You wanted me
Now you've got me
From the inside of your cell
Your new home.

Now you're the freak show

Fade to black
Curtain down
It's my show now
I'm about break
