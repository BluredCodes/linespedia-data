---
title: "Don't Cry"
writer: "isa"

My friend,
Don't cry.
Now you don't have to worry
I am no more.
My friend,
Don't cry.
Now I'll be off your mind
I can't worry you to death.
My friend,
Don't cry.
I am ok now
Now I'm happy.
Isn't that what you wanted for me?
My friend,
Don't cry.
Cuz it don't solve anything.
You'll just keep crying
You river of tears will overflow.
My friend,
Don't cry.
I am happy now.
My friend,
Don't cry.
