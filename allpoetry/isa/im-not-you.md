Everyone's got their own reasons
For feeling sick, I'm gunna throw up!
Everyone's got their own deamons to face
Face to face
Everyone's reason's are different
Do don't tell me you understand.
Everyone's life is different
So don't tell me that you've been there
Curcumstances change and so did you.
You don't really know me
And I don't really know you.
But I'm ok with that,
why aren't you?
I'm not you
And you're not me
I'm not your little minni me
I'll never undersnd you,
And its for the best
I don't want to know whats going through your head.
We see thingy differently
The glass is not half full!
Pesemist, optimist
Athiest, Catholic
We're not the same in any way
How can I make you understnd this?
You live in your world
And I'll live in mine.
Everyone's got their own demons to face
Face to Face
You face your
And I'll leave mine alone.
