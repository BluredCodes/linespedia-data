title: "A Prayer to Erik"

To God I pray
That you will come back
That you will say
That you need me back.

Tonight I weep
I wait your return
I cannot sleep
I think of your return

I love you
more than I can say
God has given me to you
For you, anything I will pay

Please come back to me
Don't ignore our love
Just let the truth be
Thank the Lord above

I pray that you'll see
Our love is true
Come back to me
You know it's true.this poem was for my love Erik......aka....Tonerman
