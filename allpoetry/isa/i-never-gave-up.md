I never really did give up
never ended my life in vien
In the end, I succeed all
I got through all life's pain
All that the devil put me through
He tempted with sweet fruits
Took me to hell, I never saw it coming
Even stole my heart with lookin' good lutes

So now I sit out in the cold,
still afraid, biut  handful of hope
Which i never had beofre
i washed away my pain without soap.
Many times I truste a face A kiss made it real
I even felt redeamed at  moment
but sadness and anger I did feel.
