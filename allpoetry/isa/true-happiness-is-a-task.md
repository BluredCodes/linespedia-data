Today I should be happy
Everyone else is today
But not being happy, though I should
later there will be i price I'll pay.
I'll sit on my bed for hours
And ponder over my life
At theend of the day
I'll take out my knife.
It will be up to my heart now
Just a cut or should I dye?
I know the rutein by heart now
It's scary now many times I've paid.
Shivered in the night,
On the floor i laid.
For days and days
My heart was mad
If i was just happy that day
Imagine how much i could have
Maybe a little happiness
True happiness, not a mask
The truth is,
True happiness is a task.
