title: "I plead to you"

I came here to plead to you
Please don't follow me,
My path will lead you to destruction.
You know what our future would be.
Who will go first,
You or I?
With a fake smile on my face
I want to die.
You've got more going for you,
You should stay
Show them what your made of
because i know oh to well
That you don't belong up above.

Not just yet.
