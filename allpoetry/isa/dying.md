I have thrown it all away
I can't tke this anymore
Watching myself perish
And dye ever so slowly.
My heart has been torn a thousand times
I don't want to wtch myself dye
I just want to get it over with
Let em die right now!
The burn on my neck hurts
It feels so good to die
And the blood from my wrist is dripping
Making a pool on the floor
The empty bottles lay on the floor
I don't know what they were,
But my tummy is full.
I have thrown it all away
I can't take this anymore
Watching myself perish
And die ever so slowly.
My heart has been torn a thousand times
I don't want to watch myself die
I just want to get it over with.
