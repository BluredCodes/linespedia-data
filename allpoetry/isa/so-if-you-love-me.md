Stop the fighting
please, not today
I want someplace quiet all day
Can't you hold it in until tomarrow
Today is my day,
my special
So if you love me,
You'll stop your fighting
it's not important right now.
Give me a hug and kiss good night 
please not another drink, not tonight
please don't passout tonight, on the floor, not again
Cuz' tonight is my night
please don't runine it for me
wait until tomarrow to yell and shout
It makes no difference anyhow.
So if you love me
You'll stop your fighting
It's not important right now.
Tuck me into bed and kiss my cheek,
like you used to.
Please don't slam the door on your way out
I want some peace and quiet tonight
Tonight is my night,
My special night.
