Gods creation
Pure, untouched
Canvas of snow
Winds that blow
Wild dogs houl
Listen to the wise ol' owl
Wake up to fresh ground
Reborn everyday
Gods creation is breathtaking
His perfection is earthshaking
Rocks, hilss and trees
Birds, deer and bees
They live together
Except for one
Humans are corruption
Live in destruction
Now it rains differently
because humans live sinfuly
But it still snows beautifully
And makes humans think
What have we done
Gods creation is fun
How can I save the human sole
From eternity in hot coal?
Is it justifiable
Or is it to much gone already?
But in the end
Snow, God will send
To cool their minds
Because no amswer they will find
