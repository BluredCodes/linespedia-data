---
title: "I will not Speak"
writer: "isa"
slug: "i-will-not-speak"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/28450-I-will-not-Speak-by-isa"
---

So many words
In my mind
I see all of them
But you, I blind.
Hidden emotions
thet won't come out
stuffed in a corner.
What are they about?
Where do they come from?
I will not speak
my mouth is shut
the pain won't leak
into your heart,
it will hurt
you try so hard
and you're so alert
of any changing emotions,
a different smile,
the way I walk
and you smile
just in case
this is the day
that I'll speak.
You're so willing,
Willing to pay.
I'm so sorry
I will not speak
From my sole 
I will not speak
Not a leak,
not a peak,
Not a word
of true emotion.
I will not speak
of my death
I will not speak.
