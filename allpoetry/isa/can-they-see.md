---
title: "Can They See?"
writer: "isa"
slug: "can-they-see"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/17228-Can-They-See--by-isa"
---

Never before, have I felt this alone.
Even though I am surounded by those who love me.
I am the one who laughs the loudest
Just as long as they don't ask, they'll let me be.
But those who asked, know the real me.
They can see that through my mask there is pain,
They see the memories of last nights attempts that failed,
But can they see that I wear this long chain?
Link by link, my anger prefails
It never comes out until it is to late
Can they see what the pain really is?
Can they see that it is me i really hate?
