We're supposed to be friends
Mother and daughter 'till the end
I hate and love you
To eachother, love we send

We'll fight and fight
Without communication
But we make it anyway
Because of our dedication

You're my mom
Hurt you? I would not
You keep me alive
Your love, I've got
