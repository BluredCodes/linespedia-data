Am I a shooting star,
High above your pain?
People seem to notice a change
Things are going my way.
but somehow it's not good enough.
I've stopped being so masikistic.
I'm smiling
I don't want to die,
But all the pain is still with me
I just bottled it up deep inside
I have everything going for me
But somehow my flesh burns for more
More of the pain its felt before.
