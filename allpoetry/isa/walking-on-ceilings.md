I thought I had some control
My thoughts, actions, and feelings
I feel upside down, walking on ceilings
Even gravity seems to lack control
Over my life, I'm so confused
pain, inside myself it's planted
Hope from God i have not been granted
The Holy have been abused
From life to life I'll drift along
Maybe love; God given
And there'll be happiness to live in
but until then, I'll be singing this sad song.
Some day I'll have to let go
This depression can't last forever
At least I told the devil Never
Why am I walking on ceilings? I do not know.
