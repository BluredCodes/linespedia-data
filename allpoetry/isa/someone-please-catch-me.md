title: "Someone Please Catch Me"

Still bloody from the blade
Tears fall from my sore eyes
Does anyone see me
or hear my soft cries?

My wrists won't heal
Blood is what I cry for
It swirls, slides and slithers
Blood will settle the score.

I leave the blood on the ground
or on a book
I don't care anymore
I hope they look.

It's so good
But so not
That I'm bad
And never gotten caught.
