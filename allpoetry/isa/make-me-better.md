Hide the lighter
Hide theknives
Hide the blade
and save my life

Hide the toxins
hide the Pill
Hide them well
For looking I will

Lock me up
cut all my nails
I'll find my way out
put in an alarm that never fails

Padded walls
a padded door
make sure there's nothing there
So I'll rot and bore

No way out
No way in
Just a slot for some food
And make sure I don't sin
