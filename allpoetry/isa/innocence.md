Everything Ive said before
It makes no sence, I wanna die
Can't take this from you
Don't open your mouth and lie
IU wanna be pure and loved
Innocent and untouched
But I will never be that
It's me you toughed
Over and over
Day after day
We spend and spend
and soon you'll pay
