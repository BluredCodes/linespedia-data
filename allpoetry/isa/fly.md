Fly fly in the sky
Fly higher than a bird can fly
And please keep in mind
The peace of mind that you will find

Fly higher than the sky
Fly above the moon and sun so high
Never underestimate the altitude you can gain
But remember life's hate games and pain

Keep the wind of love under your wings
And watch out for the devil's dark and hateful things
Take a deep breath and taste the peace
Fly fly high and never seise?,
