---
title: "My Eyes"

My eyes have seen so much
But so little has been seen by my faith eyes
they never seem to catch my deamons and lies
So in god's hands, my eyes he does clutch
Seen cold, motionless, lifeless death
My eyes don't want to see this reflection
they want see a priest, confession
Seen a loved one give his last breathe
Seen  wife cry while holding his cold hand
Seen a hundred people say sorry to me
When I just want to be left be
My eyes never want again want to see that warm sand.
