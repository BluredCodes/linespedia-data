A simple number
A measurment of my intelligence
An accumulation of my effort
A BS grade
My 3.0

Morals don't apply
Spiritualoty is banned
Only book knowledge
Outdated texts
A home ec. teacher gone math
My 3.0

It's not failing
But it's not good enough
better than average
But not my best
My 3.0

I try so hard
and what for?
$100?
A hug from my mom?
What is it worth?
My 3.0

How can i go on living
It's a lousy 3.0
It means nothing to me
But it means the world to me
My 3.0
