I promised you all
And I let you down
I was scared I would fall
But all I did was Frown
And i know that you were scared for me
For my life
Is this all I would be?
Scars left by a knife?
I promised and i still hope
That I'll keep the promises I made
And I'll learn to coap
Because through out life, here in pain I've laid
