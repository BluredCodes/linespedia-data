title: "Chosen..."

If one could be 
Chosen to be with...

If one could be
Chosen to be happy with...

If one could be 
Chosen to share love with...

If one could be Chosen
to grow old with,
and share a life and family,
love and soul with,
I need search
no more.
I have found my only mate
and true love...
in You.And here is another one that I wrote for guess who...
