---
title: "Why Don't I Cry?"
writer: "Freewriter"

I want to cry,
but only sigh,
each time I hear your voice.

I want to cry,
instead I sigh,
whenever you are near.

I want to cry,
but only sigh,
each time you take my hand.

I want to cry,
instead I sigh,
I look into your eyes.

I want to cry.  Why don't I cry?
Because I only want your love,
and happiness.
And I could never, ever hurt you.Another for guess who... 
from our past. It is good at times to return to that which sparked the flame that burns within your soul, to add fuel to that bon fire...
