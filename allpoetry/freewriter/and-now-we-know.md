---
title: "And Now, We Know......"
writer: "Freewriter"
slug: "and-now-we-know"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/39991-And-Now--We-Know......-by-Freewriter"

And now we know...
but what this new found
knowledge is, and what it
means, we may not yet understand.

And now we know...
that deep down, I truly
do love Her, and this I say
with deepest regrets, I know I hurt Her.

And now we know...
that whatever I tried, I
I couldn't let Her go.  And
this too I know, that it will take time.

And now we know...
that as time heals all wounds,
that this too shall pass, and all
will be well again, but for how long?

What we can never know...
is how long anything will last,
or what tomorrow will bring... happiness,
love, hatred, despair, good, bad......

What we can only hope...
is of course the best, and 
to prevent any hardship, to be
prepaired for the worst........And all I can say now is that I only hope for the best of things, and for the best for all of you.  Thank you all for your support for ImmortalSoul, and perhaps we will be able to look back at this as a learning experience... I feel a little wiser for the hardship.  Then perhaps we can look at it as a decent soap opera/saga!!!
