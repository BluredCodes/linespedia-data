---
title: "Eagle's Spirit"
writer: "Freewriter"
slug: "eagles-spirit"
source: "AllPoetry"

My soul soars like the Eagle,
proud and high above the clouds.
But I am hunted, wounded, and wander now, blindly, lost.
I try to return to the sky!
But can not...
My wings are tied back,
my talons knotted in burlap!
I must free myself, I am too strong to
alow this!  Yet, I can not deny it.
