---
title: "Because I Still Love You"
writer: "Freewriter"

Because I still love you,
I am here.
Because I still love you,
I want to stay.
Because I still love you,
All else was superficial.
Because I still love you,
I'm sorry for what I did.

Even when I tried,
I couldn't erase all trace of you,
because I still love you.

Because I still love you...this is a poem in honor of ImmortalSoul, and is just a little bit deeper in meaning than the average poem by me...
