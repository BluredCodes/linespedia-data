Just imagine...
  A pathway headed West.
    An apple orchard lines each side.
      A gentle brook slips sedately beside you.
        Butterflies flutter among the apple blossoms.
          The sun is setting now.
            A collage` of reds and oranges dance upon the suns surface.
Just imagine...
