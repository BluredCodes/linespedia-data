title: "O rose"

O Rose,
so vibrant
and lovely to
my love, please
do a favor for me.
I wish you,
as bright and
beautiful as you
may be, to die before
my darling love,
so as to convince
her life is not permanent,
and to encourage her to
cherish every day of our love.
