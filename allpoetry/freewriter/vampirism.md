As you run through the darkness,
You ask yourself, 'Why me? What does he want?'
I'll tell you what I want...
I want your Blood, it's my Desire.
I want your Soul, it's my need.
I want your Body, it's my lust.
In other words, my dear sweet madam,
I want your Essence!
Why do I want it?  So I can feed on it, and on you.
To share
in the sensual
pleasures of giving
and of tasting our blood.
To spend an entire night together,
sealing our Fate, and our Love Eternally.
And, as the Sun begins to rise,
we flee the Light of day,
and return another night.
