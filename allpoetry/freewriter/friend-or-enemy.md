---
title: "friend, or enemy?"
writer: "Freewriter"
slug: "friend-or-enemy"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/32729-friend--or-enemy--by-Freewriter"
---

He's my best friend.
He's my worst enemy.
He's kind, caring, considerate,
sharing, a good listener, a good
friend, a great lover to his mate,
a gentleman in every respect.

He's short-tempered, violent, loud mouthed,
disrespectful to authority, and spiteful.
He's me...I found this one in the dust, and all I can say is that I am no longer the violent type.  And I still hold all the good qualities.
