title: "Remember"
writer: "Freewriter"

If you hear that
1000 people love you,
Remember that I am one of them.

If you hear that
100 people love you,
Remember that I am one of those people.

If you hear that
only 7 people love you,
Remember I am among them like Wednesday in a Week.

If you hear that
just 2 people love you,
Remember that I am the other one.

If you hear that
only 1 person loves you,
Remember I am that one person.

I will be the last to love you,
till our dying days.

Remember, because I always will...Always will remember.  How do we keep in mind all the little things that mean so much to us?  And why is it so difficult to express our true feelings when they NEED to be known, for the best results for everyone affected...?
