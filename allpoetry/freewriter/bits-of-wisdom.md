Achievement
~~~~~~~~~~~~~
The man who rows the boat,
generally doesn't have time to rock it!



Optimism
~~~~~~~~~~
An optimist is someone who
tells you to cheer up when things are going his way!



Success
~~~~~~~~~
If you have tried to do something and failed,
you are vastly better off than if you had tried to do nothing,
and succeded!



Friendship
~~~~~~~~~~~~
Never explain- your friends don't need it,
and your enemies won't believe it anyway!



Advertising
~~~~~~~~~~~~~
He who has a thing to sell and goes and wispers in a well,
is not so apt to get the dollars as he who climbs a tree and hollers!



Business
~~~~~~~~~~
We are all manufacturers... some make good, others make
trouble, and still others make excuses.



Satisfaction
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
If you don't get everything you want, think of all the things
you don't get that you don't want!



Experience
~~~~~~~~~~~~
Experience is a wonderful thing.  It enables you to recognize
a mistake when you make it again.
