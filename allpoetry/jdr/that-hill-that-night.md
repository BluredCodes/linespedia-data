---
title: "That Hill, That Night"
writer: "Jdr"
slug: "that-hill-that-night"

The veiled eyes of heaven.
Lurking long and vast.
We sit beneath a bleeding moon,
like logs upon the grass.

You tell me about astrology.
All that's on my mind is you.
We wait for dawn to take us home,
and bid the night adieu.
