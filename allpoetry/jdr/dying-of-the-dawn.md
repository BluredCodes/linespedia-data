So, what can I tell you
Red clouds have come and gone away
Like many mornings before this
I have nothing to say

The new day brings no answers
to the questions in my mind
Just like the breeze this moment
They will be answered, in their own time

And sunshine brings acceptance
that life still lingers on
I stand here in silence
Watching the dying of the dawn
