Somewhere, out among the stars.
The land of dreams chases the moon.
I dream that you are a fairy, and I am a wisp.
Making love on the wind.
Sharing a kiss, a caress, and a sigh.
High above the lands of mortal man.
We dance in golden shadows,
and laugh as sunsets never end.
Somewhere, within that land of dream.
I am a king and you are my queen.
All the beauty, serene, and warm.
Love blooms like orchids on soft earth.
And stay us there, forevermore.
Until time forgets to flow.
