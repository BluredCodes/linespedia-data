Leaves are stars
in the forest night
'neath twilight blooms
Black roses bright

The moon adorns
a violet sky
'twixt shadows dark
and light's pale eye

And so unfolds
the grandest scheme
unravels well
An elusive dream

The doom of night
quietly sings,
of heralded songs
and fairy wings

A fire burns
The ground aglow
Earth music plays
My heart doth go

Asleep I find
myself embrace
Awake to Day's
Besmirching grace

My heart enshrined
in night's calm cry
Where black roses bloom
and fairies doth fly
