The train stops at the station,
beneath a mountain torn sky.
October chill rushes through,
the hot July of you and I.

A new moon rises high.
And the center of life breathes-
life into foreign lungs.
Accepted as a breeze.

All our questions unanswered.
And all our dreams unmet.
The last train leaves the station.
Leaving no doubt, nor regret
