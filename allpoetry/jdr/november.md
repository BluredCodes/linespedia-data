title: "November"

I press your scarf
against my nose
Your smell is
gone from it
But still
it reminds me
of that
November
I wrap it
around my neck
and feel it
soft against
my skin
And I remember
all the mornings
I spent
holding you
waiting for you
to wake up
Or the way
you would step
on my toes
when we'd dance
I remember you
Sara
and I loved
being there
that November
And none since
have I not
pressed your
scarf against
my nose and
thought of you
