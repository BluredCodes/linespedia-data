title: "Storm"

Waves crash upon the shore
My heart pounding above the thunderous roar
One lonely being am I 
Underneath this blackened sky
No moon or stars to light the night
Just clapping thunder and flash of light
In that brief flash one can almost see
The turmoil brewing deep within me.
The thrashing, smashing, relentless waves
No match for my growing rage.
Why was I spared and not he?
My lover's grave site, the monstrous sea.
With a boom the rains from heaven release
Tears flow freely down my cheeks
And finally a sense of peace
As the storm subsides and I finally see
The life taken was not him , but me.
