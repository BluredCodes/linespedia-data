Souls entangled in love's dangerous game
Will they survive ?
Ever be the same ?
The same old story of lies and deceit
Told a thousand times over
And still one must weep.
Love found and then lost from no other fault
Than aggressive libido and no guilt to vault
Man's consciousness 
To something more deep
Than a pool of water at the end of the street
After a rain.
Is it time to change the rules ?
Re-write the playbook on love so cruel.
