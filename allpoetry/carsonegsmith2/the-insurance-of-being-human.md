I love and I need,
I am only human,
This heart of mine,
So confused with
How can I say?
Ah yes, 
Delusion.
My eyes so tired,
It shows
So dark they are,
They have become,
The center of me,
The center of all I know,
The core of my salvation.
Salvation,
A word with so much meaning,
Yet so delicate in desire,
I had salvation once,
It did not last,
It got lost in the social norms,
Somewhere between 14 and 27 years.
I cry inside,
Motionless I have become,
My mind numb with senseless dreams,
Corrupted with devilish feelings of lust,
This unknowingly power assures my body,
That I am only human.

Carson Edward George Smith
September 26, 2001
