---
title: "In the Hourglass"
writer: "Nathan Owens"

You gave me this
Who are you
I don't know where it's going,
What I'm doing here
At this time,
This horrid time

You destroyed this
Why have you
The sand fills the bottom
Of the soul machine
At this time,
This generous time

In the hourglass
Another soul is fading
I block out the present
And focus
On the past,
The hopeful past

And I recall
The many years gone by
I take a look around
At this wasteland
What have we become
What has become of our lives
