---
title: "Devilish"
writer: "Nathan Owens"
slug: "devilish"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/56-Devilish-by-Nathan-Owens"
---

conquered smiles and clouds of dust
you all stand clenching one another
then a loner comes out from the sky
and casts a shadow on the sun
a gust topples trees and you fall to your knees
hopeful for power, to have control

and you become gods
and no sooner than that has happened
they will have lost all that they had, except their spirit
which is what you wanted
you fear what you don't understand
and you must conquer what you fear

you hold me to the ground, i can never rise
though i'll be safe and sound
my brown eyes portray, but you'll never hear my cries
please, i'll make them sparkle anyway, whatever you say

you and your followers, here again
i crumble, just let me be
trying to rise, i stumble
laughter echoes through the midnight sky
head up, in trouble...do i smile
