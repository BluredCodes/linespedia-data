---
title: "Living Inside Myself"
writer: "Nathan Owens"
slug: "living-inside-myself"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/58-Living-Inside-Myself-by-Nathan-Owens"
---

a drip echoes through shallow corridors
on an identical path of an invisible beast
that melts into the earth
on a road with thorns, moaning
pacing in a sweat
trampling the sticky road
searching the agony of others
on a path of thorns
an army of reapers
to live on Satan's horns
if i close my mind in fright
don't detach the life inside
my sacred shell in which i struggle to keep tight
the suffocation if here but it's so warm in my hell
others just ignore themselves crying out
some try to help me but i help myself
their world is not for me, i cause my own destruction
i see past the demons of day into a world of my own
just living a dream in my hollow state, finally alone
