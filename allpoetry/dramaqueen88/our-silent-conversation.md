Why do you cry?
Why do you sit there, your red face wracked with pain,
your tears racing down your cheeks like falling rain?
Is it her?
Do you dream of her, your immortal beloved?
The one with the fields of golden hair,
and the lapiz for eyes?
The one who you love more than the heavens above,
and the sun and the moon?
Your Juliet?
Your Cleopatra?
The silence beteen us is deafining.
Are cold stares all that can occur between us?
Do not cry.
I don't want to cry,
and all the world knows that when you cry, I in turn must cry as well,
because when you laugh, I laugh.
When you hurt, I hurt.
Why do you remain with her if she can only make you cry?
You tell her that you love her, but does she know how much?
And how much she hurts you?
And I?
I want to smack you,
but I cannot hate you for loving her.
I cannot condemn a providence you cling to,
because you are the salvation I clutch.
All you love, I love.
