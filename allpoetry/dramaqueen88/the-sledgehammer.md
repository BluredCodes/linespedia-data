I am the slegehammer.
You are made of stainless steel,
of titanium, of diamond,
but I will crack you,
because I am the sledgehammer.
You think I cannot do it,
that your cover is too strong,
and I too weak.
Think again.
I am the sledgehammer.
I will smash you.
I will beat you and break you until you crack,
until you have no choice but to surrender to me.
I am the sledgehammer,
and I never grow weary.
