~The Hobbit/Elf is hotter~

The Potions Mistress: Excuse me can I talk to you for a minute?
Wanda: Uh hum, sure. Hey, I know you.
TPM: Yeah I know you too but umm I just wanted to know, do you know the hobbit named… you know his name.
W: Oh yeah. I definitely know his name.
TPM: I just wanted to let you know he’s hotter.
W: Huh… no no, the elf’s hotter.

[Chorus]

TPM: You need to give it up.
Had about enough.
It’s not hard to see,
The hobbit is hotter.
W: I’m sorry that you,
Seem to be confused.
Fine he’ll never be.
The elf is hotter.

TPM: I think it’s time we got this straight.
Let’s sit and talk face to face.
There is no way you could mistake,
Legolas for hottest- are you insane?

W: See I know Frodo may be,
Just a bit jealous of he,
But you’re just blind if you can’t see,
That the elf is a hottie.

TPM: You see I tried to hesitate.
I didn’t wanna say what he told me.
He said his hair was all fake.
It’s just a wig. Ain’t that a shame.

W: Maybe I misunderstood,
‘Cuz I can’t see how you could,
Wanna screw Elijah Wood,
When my elf is so damn good.

TPM: You need to give it up.
Had about enough.
It’s not hard to see,
The hobbit is hotter.
W: I’m sorry that you,
Seem to be confused.
Fine he’ll never be.
The elf is hotter.

W: Must you think the way you do?
Keep on acting like a fool.
You know ‘Lij is nothing compared to,
My adorable Bloom.

TPM: I think that you should realize,
And stare into his lovely eyes.
He has the power to hypnotize.
I know it’s killing you inside.

W: You can say what you want to say.
What he has Elijah can’t take,
From the truth you can’t escape,
Your boy’s eyes are so fake.

TPM: When will you get the picture?
You and the elf have no future.
I don’t know why you even bother,
And if you didn’t know, the hobbit’s hotter.

TPM: You need to give it up.
Had about enough.
It’s not hard to see,
The hobbit is hotter.
W: I’m sorry that you,
Seem to be confused.
Fine he’ll never be.
The elf is hotter.
