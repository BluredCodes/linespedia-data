I close my eyes
Toss...
Turn...
Wonder...

Stop it!
Stop thinking!
How do I turn it off?
My mind.

It travels
Without my consent
To destinations unknown
roads untraveled...

The thoughts won't stop!
Peace has never seemed so far away.
Peace?
Do I remember that feeling?

Will the end never come?I feel like this one needs more of a conclusion.
