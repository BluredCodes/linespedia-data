---
title: "Lost"
writer: "Monkei"
slug: "lost"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

We laugh...
She smiles...
He grins...
I turn away...
I don't want to see what I am seeing...
I don't want to know the truth...
The truth brings pain...
I don't know how much more hurt my heart will hold...
I don't know if I could take the loss again...
If I lose him to her...
Her to him...
Then I am lost..
