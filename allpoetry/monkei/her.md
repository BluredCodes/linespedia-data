---
title: "Her"
writer: "Monkei"
slug: "her"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/51874-Her-by-Monkei"
---

He holds me tightly,
But he smiles at her.
He kisses me gently,
But he laughs with her.
He hugs me fiercely,
But he leaves with her.
He likes me dearly,
But I fear he's in love...
With her.
