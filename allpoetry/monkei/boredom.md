Boredom...
Sitting
Waiting.
There is nothing...
The music plays over and over in my head...
My vision blurs...
I blink...
And refocus.
The phone rings..
Naturally,
It's not for me...
The door bell...
Not for me either...
I sit..
And wait..
Boredom..
