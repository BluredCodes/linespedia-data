I pull away,
He follows.
I begin to run,
He chases.
I gasp for breath,
He pursues.
I fall to the ground,
And he consumes my heart.This is part of another poem ('Love Again').  It was suggested that I put this part on as a poem in its self but it doesn't have a title.  It needs a title!
