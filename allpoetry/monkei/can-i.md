I know that when you smile at me
I will have to smile back
 I know that when you kiss me
I will kiss back
 When you hug me
I will hug back
 And when you look at me
I will look back
 But what I don't know is if..
When you love me...can I love back??I don't know if I managed to fully express my feelings here.  BAn uncertainty of love and loving another was what I was going for, almost kind of giving you a fearful feeling
