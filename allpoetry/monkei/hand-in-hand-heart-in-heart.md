title: "Hand in Hand, Heart in Heart"

It's all gone...
Not the emotion,
But the pain.

You opened me up,
Took away the weight,
And my heart began to beat again.

Long since melted,
It's raw now...
Open.

You're not though,
I see your step grow heavier
As your burden increases

Share it with me!
Talk to me!
Show it to me!

And then, together
We can walk
Hand in Hand...Heart in Heart.I just wrote this, kind of a random thing.
