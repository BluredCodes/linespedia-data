---
title: "I Am Alone"

I look around me
at the room full of familiar strangers,
smiling faces...
laughter.

I know these people,
I am like them,
Yet I am not...
They see it.

They sense the differences between us.
The void that seperates us.
The wall, visible to only me.
I am trapped, alone, in a crowded room.I didn't expect this one to come, but it seems to have come out ok.
