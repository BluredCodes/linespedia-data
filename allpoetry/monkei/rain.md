We were so in love
Every smile, every touch
Made a little more of us

But lately the skys,
Have been clouding over
And now the rain is pouring down

Each time the thunder clashes
And the lightning mars the sky
I feel like you pull a little more away.

I don't know what to do
I just want desperately,
To hold on to you.

But now every time she smiles,
I see you smiling back,
And I know I am losing you
If you are not already lost.I know what I am trying to express here, but I am not quite sure how to do it.  I had everything in my mind, it is just the putting it on paper part that gave me trouble.  Any suggestions are MORE than welcome.
