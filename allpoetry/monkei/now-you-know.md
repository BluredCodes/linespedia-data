---
title: "Now You Know"
writer: "Monkei"
slug: "now-you-know"

My walls are crumbling...
My heart is melting...
The rocks are falling...
The ice is cracking...

You broke through me...
Tore down my defenses...
Filled my heart...
And abolished my pain...

Everything I had constructed,
To keep everyone away
You passed through.
Leaving me open, vulnerable..

There is no pain where you have been,
You have restored my broken heart,
Mended my bleeding soul..
And now I've told you everything..

Everything I feel...
you know.
Every pain...
I've shared with you.

So now you know...
Was it a mistake?
Should I have poured my mind into yours?
Should I have shared my massive burden with you?

Do you know..
How much I have told you?
Can you comprehend...
What I've just given you?

Something I've never been able to before..
Now you know
Me..
Not my mask...
