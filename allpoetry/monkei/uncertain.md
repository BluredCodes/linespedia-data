I feel the loss,
Though uncertain...
I feel you pulling away,
Though uncertain...
I feel you smiling back at her,
Though uncertain...
I feel you holding on to me,
Though uncertain...
But I also feel you leaving,
This time, certain....This one started out good, but didn't end quite the way I saw it ending.
