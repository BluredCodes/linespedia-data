The flutter inside me grows more with each passing moment..
I don't know what to do
How do I stop it?!
The uncertainty swells in my chest until it feels as if it will burst..
Is this unecessary anguish?
Are you worth this much?
Can I outlast you?
Or will I die trying?I am quite obviously a novice.  This is the first poem I have really written like this.  Help me please!
