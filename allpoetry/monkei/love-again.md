---
title: "Love again"
writer: "Monkei"
slug: "love-again"

---

I pull away,
He follows.
I begin to run,
He chases.
I gasp for breath,
He pursues.
I fall to the ground,
And he consumes my heart.

I rise...
Empty...
Longing...
Afraid...
Alone...
He's gone...
I escaped...
But he stole my heart...
My life...
My love...

How do I love without a heart?
How can I love another without a heart?!?
Can I love another..?

The pain is fading,
He's taking it away...
The more time I spend with him,
We get closer every day...
My brick wall is crumbling,
Though not broken yet...
My frozen heart is melting,
Though not quite thaw...
He is getting in...
Breaking down my defenses.
Showing me how to love again...
I love, again.
