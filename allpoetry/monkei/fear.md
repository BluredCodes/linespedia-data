Turning every which way I see nothing but blurs
I stop, look, and see nothing
I hear something in the distance
But do not comprehend it
I feel something too..
Pain maybe..
It's hard to tell
It could just be desolation...
It's been too long since I let myself feel pain..
It's been too long since I let my heart thaw...
But now the water droplets are coming.
I fear the melting
I fear the feelings that may come with it..
