title: "The Only One"
writer: "Monkei"

I sit
Infatuated
Staring at the screen
Knowing someone wrote the words I'm reading
Knowing that an individual,
A person somewhere out there
Shares my feelings.

I sit
Now knowing
That I am not the only one.I am a novice poet, and I need help; but I like this one.
