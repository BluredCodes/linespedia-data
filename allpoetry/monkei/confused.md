You call to me from that direction,
He calls to me from behind,
They call to me from over there.
I spin around...
Confused...
Where should I go?
What path should I take?
Which one leads where I want to go?
Where do I want to go?I'm not very sure about this one in general.  If there is anything you notice tell me please.
