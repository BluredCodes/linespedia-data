My love for you

is a house of cards

built of dreams and longing

possibilities never realized,

certitude never proven.



In that house I waited for you

burning with unvoiced desire

for your body and your strength

to make me whole



I don't know what I wanted more:

to have you, or to give you

everything you ever wanted



then the wind arose and lay waste to my dream

scattering the cards

along with my hope



now in pieces

desolate and broken,

I ache for you stillNot a good idea to have an affair with one's supervisor...

For Eric Clark
