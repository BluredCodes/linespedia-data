this life throws at me stones of sorrows
tries to drag me down
and it succeeds.......oh it succeeds
i cannot be worse off than i am right now
but i must make it through somehow
For You
For You

i know if i can reach tomorrow it will all be fine
i also know that honestly that bullshit is a lie 
oh yeah
but i cant be too skeptical
of what i can barely see
so why do i believe in you?
i dont believe in me
but you do
oh you do
you do................you do

and i...........dont see.........why i'm............ugly...........inside..........of me...........i'm so..........ugly............to me........to me..........to me

but not to you.....no not to you
i dont believe you
i want to believe you
i do believe you
