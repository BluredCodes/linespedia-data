What do you see when you look at me?
I love you I loved you do you love me?
You can save me you make me feel alive
You can you did will you leave me you left me alone again
I slip back in my pain my hate
I slip back in to wait for fate to take my break my my life for you
I can't go on live on without you

Do you hear? Do you hear me?
Crying dying out to out through your ears your fears
My love encased in rage, in pain is let out to see you feel you now is shut back in again if you, when you go away

I have told you, hold you things that I can't even reveal to anyone else but you
Hear you fear for me, not me my life is there meaning something to you, for you, you love do you?
Please don't leave me, so scared so scarred of it of me my mind through me you press it down in me bring the good out
I am thankful my life to you for you saving me from myself
