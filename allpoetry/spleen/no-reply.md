---
title: "No Reply"
writer: "SPLeeN"
slug: "no-reply"
source: "AllPoetry"

door creaking open
they smell the smoke
somethings burning

voices raise
irritated, angered
no reply is heard

fingers turn the dial
the oven turns off
the pizza on the rack
no longer edible
they hear the music blaring

again

voices raise
irritated, angered
no reply is heard

feet climb the stairs
one after another
proclaiming they are home
something isnt right

one last time

voices raise
irritated, angered
no reply is heard

vision turns the corner
blood upon the floor
screaming now is heard
the soul is missing
the sky the child they ask why

forever

voices raise
guilty, pained
no reply is heard
