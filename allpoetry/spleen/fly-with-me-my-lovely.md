I………
Sorry for what
I am but what
God
Take me away
Fly……..
Into this hate
Fate is killing me
Slowly indeed
The knife driven deeper
Watch as I bleed
In you
In your
Eyes
My one
My true
Oh heaven tastes like you
Come into me lovely
Devour my mind
Watch as im dying
Kill me inside
Dark into not
Fuck drink to the head of nothing
Nothing inside
Dust is ashes to the mother
Of all
Of me
Just you
Come into
Come to me
My whore
I myself a whore
For you
So kick me
My blood flows for you
My love flows for you
Your whore
Im yours to
Abuse
This love is dark
My beautiful 
Im ugly
A whore
