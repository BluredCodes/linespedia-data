what is hidden from the world
is whats open to you
when i have no where to turn
i can always run to you
everything crumbles my dreams turn to rubble
this beautiful light is constant. this light is you
i could never relate, how much i truly need you
you would never believe, how much i truly love you
however the flower of this life happens to unfold
i know you will be there, i know you are with me
no one will ever glow the way you glow
