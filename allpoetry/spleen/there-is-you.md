title: "there is you"

Beautiful soothing words of reality spoken
Flowed forth from the mouth of all that is good
Adding seconds to the ticking of the bomb that is I
Sent by God, an angel, as glorious or more than all humanity
But not even an angel as you, so perfect, so lovely
Fate is set, the Hand of God predicts the path
Followed by all, but for every bad there is a good
For me, there is you
So beautiful, so free
All I can do is thank God that my path was intertwined with yours
To look for the good in my life, all I need is you
All I need to see is your light
I will be fine
