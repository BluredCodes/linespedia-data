my fuck
the one is a someone to you
but nothing is me
so im gonna fuck you in the head
i wanna fuck you like the dead
im gonna stay alive inside you
my flesh is what survives you
i have blood on my head
i have your blood on my tongue
you want to feel me?
ill feel you until they day we are one
its so dark inside of here
i cant see at all
but we are inside my mind
where is the light they thought they saw?
IM GONNA FUCK YOU IN THE HEAD
i wanna fuck you to undead
i have nothing else to give
i cannot stand to let me LIVE
I WANNA FUCK YOU LIKE IM DEAD
I WANNA FUCK YOU IN YOUR HEAD
IM SO GODAMMED FUCKED UP IN THE HEAD
IM THINKING WE COULD FUCK UNTIL WE'RE DEAD
