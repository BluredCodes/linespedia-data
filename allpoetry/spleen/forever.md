i look into your eyes and i feel this
i gaze at your beauty outward and within
i feel this beauty burning

i want to be with you longer
when i touch your hand
i feel this love grow stronger
when i feel your skin
i know i will love you longer
when i hear your voice
your laugh your smile
i will love you forever
i will love you forever
you make me smile in the middle of this darkness
i can only smile as i sink into darkness
the love i feel stronger
stronger
with each passing moment
the light returns
caught in time fixed in my mind
nothing can take it away
no none can take it away from me
forever i will be there
these memories
this love
forever i will be here
i will always love 
i will always stay
