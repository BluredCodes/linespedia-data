My pain can't be held inside any longer. 
I can't lift my head how can I conquer? 
My heart is still beating but I'm not alive.  
Soul barren and empty, how can I survive?  
No will to live, don't want to die. 
Can't find the truth, everything is a lie.  
Look over your shoulder, lock all your doors.  
Don't trust your neighbor's good nature 
when your neighbor doubts yours.  
Let us search blindly for the meaning of life.  
What use is searching when there's nothing to find?  
The blind lead the blind, the stray show the way.  
Soon their steps falter, they can't run away.  
You get what you give, if you even get that. 
There's only one way to live, and live that lie I can't.
