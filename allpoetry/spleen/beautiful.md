all this shit i wish i could show you
all i really want is to hold you 
all i really want is to know you
and have you stay

i wish i could retract the words i say
i wish i that i could go back to the day
i wish i could relive
everyday
i wish that i could just live
for one day

all this love i feel is for you
all its real and i could show you
all i need this photo of you
please stay

[chorus]

i wish that i could feel the way you do
so happy so free so beautiful
you are so beatiful
i am not beautiful
i am not beautiful
like you........like you
i love the way you glow
