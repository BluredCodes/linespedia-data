The floods came,
         so unprepared was I
     for the rushing, unrelenting
       torrents that threatened
            to consume me.

         My raft nothing more
         than empty promises,
           left me drowning
       in a sea of nothingness.

        And there was weeping
        and gnashing of teeth.
              No one near
           to share the pain.
       Trust, a distant rainbow,
         just another illusion.

         And the floods came,
        and there was weeping
            and the eternal
          scarring of a soul.
