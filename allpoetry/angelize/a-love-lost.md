---
title: "A love lost"
writer: "Angelize"
slug: "a-love-lost"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8199-A-love-lost-by-Angelize"
---

We promised one another the world,
held it in our hands, 
if only in our imagination.
We planned, plotted and schemed,
as we dreamed of our tomorrows.

Then, 
a distant sound of thunder.
Did you not notice 
when our rainbow
gave way to a darkened sky?

Yet, 
I think I can recall
when your laughter first rang hollow,
my own sounding with untruths.

My warning to you,
however,
was a single tear
that you laughingly wiped away.

You would have seen the emptiness
of our dreams reflected in that tear, 
had you but looked.

Now, 
all that we hold
between us,
is a love long lost.
