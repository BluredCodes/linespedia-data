---
title: "weakened by tears"
writer: "tears by angelz wings"
slug: "weakened-by-tears"
source: "AllPoetry"

I look into your eyes,
deception is what I see.
I love you so much,
that's why it's hurting me.

I'm trying to explain,
just how much I hurt.
I yell til' my breathe runs out,
anger flows in every word.

I  just can't seem
to get through to you.
I want you to hurt,
feel the pain, too.

"Why?" I ask you,
do you do this to me.
I can't stand it anymore,
Please let me be.

You've destroyed my happiness,
You've destroyed my heart.
I'll be doing better off,
If we were to be apart.

I turn away from you,
I can't bear to see.
What your eyes behold,
feelings,instore for me.

You beg me to stay,
you plead on your knees.
I have to be strong,
so my mind can be at ease.

Your voice is getting lower,
I'm shocked at what appears.
I look at you once more,
To see you choking on your tears.

I try to make you realize,
that my whole life is at stake.
But you continue to cry,
I wonder if it's a mistake.

I tell myself be strong,
but a tear rolls down my cheak.
The pain in your eyes,
is making me very weak.

I've failed to be strong,
failed to let you go.
I have to live with it.
Because I love you so.
