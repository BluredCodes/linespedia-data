title: "Arctic Blossom"

A rose locked solid in ice
Vibrant petals now frail and frigid
Embraced by a silent and deadly vice

Beauty eternal but not free
Never again will it dance in the wind
Feeling the cool breeze of the sea
For beauty is not in appearance alone
And so the rose ceased to be
