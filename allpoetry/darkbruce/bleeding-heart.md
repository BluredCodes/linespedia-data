---
title: "Bleeding Heart"
writer: "DarkBruce"

What has happened to us
Once so beautiful and pure
A rift steadily growing
Not knowing what to do anymore

I thought it was true love
Never questioned if we would persevere
Thought love would conquer all
Now my inner fear

If I can even call it that
My veins rupture
Arteries crack
Heart is shattered
Your words once spoken
Can’t be taken back

You destroyed me
Took my love away
Days of anger
Spouts of passion
Back to fighting
Typical news

The calm before the storm
The only days I can endure
The few I don’t abhor

Simple little annoyances
Causing you to explode
Abusing me constantly
But no scars
No bruises
No evidence of your torturous deeds

I sit and bleed internally
The life draining from my limbs
Becoming a puppet
No will to live

You have the nerve to come back
Crawling on your knees
Saying “I love you”
Eyes shrouded in tears
A façade all too familiar

One too many times
You have become possessed
By the devil within

Even now
As we are apart
I cannot escape you

Uncontrollable reminiscing
A flood of memories
Rushes through my mind
Returning my fondest memories of you

Once you held me
Cared for me
Loved me
Made me feel special
Alive

Now I am alone
Desolate Barren wasteland
Empty Heart
Crying soul

Not even my tears
Can wash away
This unbearable pain
Wounded,
And drowning
In this sea
Of emptiness.
