I wake up feeling pissed
I stick a needle in my eye
I want to feel anything but this
I want to feel alive.

Life drags me along with it,
An endless ride filled with suffering.
I’m sick of all this shit
I’m ready to jump off just to feel something.

I’m living on the fringe,
Chaos within but portraying sanity.
I want to pull out this syringe
But who cares about vanity.

I get another needle ready to start again
My life is spiraling down its last funnel
“Dying is not a sin,”
Echoes in my ears as I stumble down the final tunnel.
