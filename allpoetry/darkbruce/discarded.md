This bitter melancholy feeling
When will it ever end?
I thought we were in love
Now my heart will never mend.
I shared with you everything
And to your very will I did bend
But you abused me and warped my mind
I felt as though you were my only friend.
I was afraid to let you go,
To let our intertwined souls rend
But you cast me aside
And left me for myself to fend.
