I would do anything
To once again experience 
That intense love and tenderness,

Oblivious rapture in the present tense

Our turbulent emotions
Often created a tumultuous ride,
But at least with you I felt loved

I felt alive

Now I am nothing
But a broken down shell of a man
Barely able to survive

Unable to stand.
