---
title: "Give and Take"
writer: "DarkBruce"
slug: "give-and-take"

I give you my love
You don’t care,
I open my heart
You’re not aware,
I build you up
You rip me down,
I give you wings to fly
You leave me behind to drown.

You say you love me,
What a fucking lie.
But when I say I love you
You say goodbye

Now I’m alone
Crying in my melancholy world,
But what’s the point
You’re just a girl.
I gave you everything
And you deceitfully took it all.
Then you left me to despair
And take the pain of the fall.
