---
title: "Invisible"
writer: "DarkBruce"
slug: "invisible"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Transparent soul,
Blank face,
Bereft of emotions
Dead but standing in place.

This inert body,
This limp shell,
Only a corpse
Living in hell.

Life doesn’t exist
Except as a façade,
Consumed by death
Born beneath a morbid god.
