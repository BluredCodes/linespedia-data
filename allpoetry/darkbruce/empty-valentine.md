Two years ago this day this time
Our souls first became intertwined
Our first kiss and true valentine
I was yours and you were mine.

Last year this day brought much happiness
We spent hours together in rapture and bliss
I treasured your every touch and every kiss
Never did I imagine that you I would miss.

I have spent today in distress and fear
It’s hard to believe that you were ever sincere
On the corner of my eye a persistent tear
Spending this most memorable day without you near.
