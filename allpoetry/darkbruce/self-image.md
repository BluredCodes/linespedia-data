All my life
I have been looking for you
My soul mate and eternal love
Perfect, Pristine and ever-true.

I don’t worry about our future
No obstacle can hinder our emotions.
Our love is vast and endless
Like the beautiful deep blue oceans.

Before you, no one had ever accepted me for who I am
My entire life I had been forced to rearrange.
I was never good enough for anyone or anything
I had spent my life denying myself and trying to change

All my life
I thought I had been looking for you
And believed our feelings would never end
But your tainted love was anything but true.

We have no future
Long ago you told me you loved me, what for?
You have forced my emotions to dry up
And left me to drown in the murky waters of your shallow shore.

You say that don’t love me anymore
And that I need to change
Maybe I’m not good enough for you,
But I am who I am and I’m no longer going to rearrange.
