title: "Goodbye"
writer: "DarkBruce"

I take the knife
Feeling the cold steel
Against my wrist
Clenching my fist
Wondering
Just what would happen
If I took a little slice
And rolled the dice
The silence
And peace
No worries
No Fury
No thoughts
No nothing
Emptiness
Bitter cold and regrets
What’s that you say?
Fuck you 
You aren’t me
And you wouldn’t want to be
You couldn’t handle 
Just who I am
Being consumed by sin
By the burning rage within
You will never understand
I wouldn’t expect you to
Just leave this place
Get out of my space
Leave me to rot
In my isolation
Let me die
Don’t you dare cry
Not for me
Not for anyone
Life is too fickle a thing
To spend worrying
Over mundane details
That are forgotten
Over the course of time
