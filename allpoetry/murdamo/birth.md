Born ass backwards in the dark skin like bark eyes blood shot red with an ice cube for a heart my brain need's a jump start by a train or maybe heaven's rain I was pronounced clinicly insane now u feel the wrath of my pain which rips and tears at your vains like a heroin or cocane body's bloody and slain with a chain wich I wrap around my soul t'ill i'm dead and cold like a lost angel holding a pitch fork in a black coat with boney hands reaching out for a new soul's. p/s my friend wrote thisAP
