---
title: "what is real?"
writer: "murdamo"
slug: "what-is-real"
source: "AllPoetry"

Buying high priced classy clothes or wearing cheap shirts full of holes, doing what your personality asks or acting different and wearing masks, changing your life to fit in or being yourself not caring to slip in, this isnt me i just want u and your friends to like me ,this isnt kool its fake can u see, real is the way to be and dont let anyone chang u at all or u will living like a short lived water fall, running out of your true self,let the fake die and the real rise or u will end up living with empty eyes bored tired acting like your alive, wanting yurning for a way to feel ,so tell me what is real?
