The Thinking Time

Instead of falling into sleep,
I think about the world.
I think of things unknown to me.
I think of things I cannot answer.
I think of whether this is this, or that is that.
I think of my life,
I think of me, so young in the world.
I think of my family, my friends.
I think of this world’s horrors,
I think of its greatness.
I think of how it doesn’t revolve around me, I revolve around it.
I think of my home, its coziness.
I think of my bed, its warmth.
I think of sleep, so peaceful and deep.
I think…
I think…
I dream.
I dream a wonderful dream.
I dream a dream of happiness.
I dream a dream of life.
I dream a dream of wonder.
I dream a dream of flight.
I dream…
I dream…
I wake.
The day has begun.
Now, I must wait for that wonderful time of night;
The Thinking Time of night.
