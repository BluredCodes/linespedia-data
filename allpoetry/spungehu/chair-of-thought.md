I sit, suffering at my desk
Mind running loose through fields of possibilities
Books left forgotten, stretched open, pouring out their knowledge
Inky pen dripping permanent bruises on my papers
Homework lying down, scraped and scarred in an illegible scrawl
Silent pictures staring, showing times of happiness, wonder, surprise
Radio softly humming, as if strolling happily through a forest
Curtains open wide with smiles, showing me their wonderful world within
Me, sitting in my throne, my prison, my chair of thought.
