The ornaments are red
The angels are cream
We hope the holidays 
Pass by like a dream

The pine needles are green
Some paper bright blue 
During busy preparations
Holiday spirit is true

Santa is jolly 
His presence is felt
From the length of his beard 
To the width of his belt

During Christmas time
The carols are clear
Sung so merrily 
That all can hear

The candy-canes are striped
The applesauce tasted
During our family feast
No food is wasted

Snowy white days 
And hot, glowing fires
The cheerful, cozy unity
Makes us all inspired

And now we must end
With the warm, clear call
“Have a Merry Christmas,
