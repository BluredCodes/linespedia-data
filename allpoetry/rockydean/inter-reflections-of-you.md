---
title: "Inter Reflections Of You"
writer: "RockyDean"
slug: "inter-reflections-of-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

A few cords from the guitar some feelings written on a page,commas mark the spot where the cords are to change.Each line shines a little more through, where the cords carry a voice of inter reflections of you.These reflections cast your pallet and the words make up the paint,and when the cord draws higher the colors begin to change.When the octave drops from normal then color changes to blue,where the cord carries a voice of inter reflections of you.
