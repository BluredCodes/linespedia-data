---
title: "Love Is A Kingdom"
writer: "RockyDean"
slug: "love-is-a-kingdom"
source: "AllPoetry"

Love is a kingdom where there's devotion and delight,there's always affection and fondness like each breathe of life.Every minute away seems an hour for each on past,anticipation grows stronger until you see them at last.If love could be bottled it would be worth more than gold,nothing could take the place of the treasure you would hold.Its been said that love is in the eye of the beholder, is this true,so I'll end this with my idea and leave the rest up to you.To me love is forever and will be carried to my grave,because love is a kingdom and in my heart and soul it will remain.
