Go ahead commander cry it's OK,it won't be concerned a weakness not after this day.Your job won't be easy with the millions of lives in your hands,but we the people trust you as being a good man.We've seen the compassion in your eyes,when asked how you feel your lips quivered your eyes swelledat the lost of innocent life's.It's been said there's no winners in war,but America must defend itself that the terrorist will score no more.
