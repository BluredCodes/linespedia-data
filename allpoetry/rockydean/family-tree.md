Reserching my familes tree,
wow what a job this turned out to be.
I never knew much about my familys history
but I can't say that any more,
for with my computer and lots of hours
I've opened up genealogys endless door.
So many names some I can't even pronounce,
but I add them to the family tree and all that I find out.
Some times I hit them dreded dead ends,
but with querys in half the country
a new leed always comes in.
Theres always a thrill in finding a new cousin
from half way around the world,
like great grandpa's brother Tom's grandson's oldest girl.
For those who do this research they know what I mean,
for instance the other day I checked my e-mail
and there was a picture of my great great grandpa wow what a treet.
I've been asked why spend so much time on a family tree?
I can only answer theres a great enjoyment in it for me.
And when our work on this earth is finished,
there will be faces to add to them names of so many.
