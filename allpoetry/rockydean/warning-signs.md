There was a warning from the very first
when I inhaled my first cigarette,
I felt the fire going down my throat
and deep within my chest.
I gagged choked then I spit
its right then I should have quit,
but like so many of us who failed to ignore
those warning signs back then.
I never thought leaf wrapped in paper
could result in such awfull effects,
by lighting a match then inhale the smoke
a breath you never get back.
The stains upon the fingers
can't compare to those within the lungs,
these combinations of pure disaster
of damage that can't be undone.
I'm not trying to tell anyone how to live their life
I would be a fine one to say,
for I ignored all those signs
and now I have to pay.
