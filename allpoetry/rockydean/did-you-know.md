Did you know you're the first thought on my mind in the morning
when I awake from dreams of you,
for even the sun that sets in the horizon can't compare 
to your beauty shining through.
Your never far from my heart and you're image is etched within my mind,
your voice is like a burning candle, soft, yet strong enough for light.
