To become myself again
I must over come 
all the osbciles I've placed in my way

I must escape the fears I've stored inside
like the worries of this world
I've locked within my mind,
Though with the help of you my love
I'll release my hurt by tears uncried.
When I should shed these mortal fears,
and drown the sorrow I've hidden for years.
To find that man that I once was
the only one you said you loved
to admit I'm not the man I was back then,
but today I'm on my way
to become myself again.
