title: "The Fourth Finger Of The Left Hand"

I hold this band that I've bought for you in the palm of my hand,
it's not real diamonds or gold dear but I've done the best I can.
You see the sun has never shined brighter since you've came into my life,
and the words are not invented yet to explain my feelings right.
Though I promise to always love you and place nothing before your needs,
still my heart fears your answer as I bow here on bent knee.
Will the sun shine tomorrow or will the clouds come rolling in ?
as I waited for her answer  she smiled with a grin.
She told me I was a beacon that also gave light,
in this world so dark and oblique there was finally hope in sight.
Her words followed with tears as she reach for that little band,
then I placed my symbol of love on the fourth finger of her left hand.
