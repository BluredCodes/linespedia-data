---
title: "Come And Behold"
writer: "RockyDean"
slug: "come-and-behold"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/20542-Come-And-Behold-by-RockyDean"

Theres talk of a place some say they've seen,
and each with their story
of the beauty and peace.
They talk of pure harmony
and such serene light,
where some saw feilds of flowers
and trees that touch the sky.
Some saw a pathway that many walks along,
at a distance was a kingdom made of solid gold.
They heard Angels singing a beautuful song,
and a voice softly saying
come and behold.
