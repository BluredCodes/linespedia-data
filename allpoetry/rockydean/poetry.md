The pen within the hand,that writes with wisdom and seniority for you.The only explanation I've come up with,the heart leads in what we do.Sometimes the heart is happy and a laugh you may receive,but when the heart if filled with sorrowwithin each line it can be seen.The words will touch you the wisdom can guide you, if you'll listen to what the words sayfor rarely there's a pen in hand with a hear that Leeds the way.
