911 usually the number for someone in need,but today it marks the date of terrible deed.While we awaken this day on our everyday life's,local news station broadcast of dispecable crimes.America under attack by terrorist in the sky's,innocent people used as weapons a maniac has designed.While we watch on t.v. horror fills the screen,a plane strikes the World Trade building dear God can this be.911 used again as emergency people rush to the scene,hurring up the floors to help everyone in need.Fire fighters & Police save uncounted innocent lifes,but before their safe theirselfs another plane arrives.America watches helplessly as the second plane strikes,leaving a sinking feeling of dred in all America's eyes.Update from repoters terrorist crash a plane into the Pentagonin Washington DC, & a fourth plane crashes in Pittsburg reporters at the scene.The image of innocent people in distress beyond our reach,just then two 110 story buildings begins crumbling to the street.Our hearts hurt and tears filled this countries saddened eyes,the day 911 brought lost to innocent American lifes.
