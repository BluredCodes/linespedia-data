title: "Little Ryan"
writer: "RockyDean"

This old worlds filled with stories
sometimes good some times bad,
but few will pull at your heart strings
leaving you a feeling of empty and sad.
But this story I'll share today
has warmed my heart the made me cry,
you see it's about a little boy I never meet 
and his battle to cling to life.
They tell me he was sick alot almost sence his birth,
but for a few years he got better it seemed
and now hes gotten worse.
They said he liked lots of company
always smiling then he would shake your hand,
and before you left he made it a point
to ask you back again.
Well the first time I heard about Ryan,
they said he wouldn't make it through the night
but as the next few days rolled around
he was still putting up a fight.
To show where his little heart was 
he was worried about his mom and dad
he told them he would like a hamburger and coke
if they would eat one with him.
Now that was just a few weeks ago
and today they came with the news,
of how his life life had ended 
that his battle was finally through.
His family now grives their loss
of the nine year old boy I never knew
my heart broke in a million pieces
but I know its a better place hes gone to.
