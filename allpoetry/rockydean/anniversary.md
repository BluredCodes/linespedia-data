---
title: "Anniversary"
writer: "RockyDean"

All the days of my life to you I give my love,
Never will I forget to kiss you good night
Now and always angel my angel from above.
I've promised to be there through sickness and in wealth my love is true,
Vastly I hold these emotions inside when I'm away from you.
Every moment I'm away at work each day I feel so all alone,
Returning from work is a great feeling inside For you await at home.
So many times I've thanked my lucky stars for sending you to me,
Actually I feel I should say how lucky can just one man be.
Remember when we stood side by side when I told the world I love you,
Yes I love you with all my heart for on this day we said I do.
