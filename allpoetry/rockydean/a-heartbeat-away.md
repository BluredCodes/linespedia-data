---
title: "A Heartbeat Away"
writer: "RockyDean"
slug: "a-heartbeat-away"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/10202-A-Heartbeat-Away-by-RockyDean"

How far is heaven that God only knows,where our earthly virtue is rewarded in the city made of gold.Where no one cries in pain or from fear no more,it's written everyone on earth has a key to this door.Where no boundaries will surround us and lost loved ones will await,how far is heaven maybe just a heartbeat away.
