title: "Show A little Passion"

Has our passion for others faded away over the years,this worlds getting faster seems no one really cares.I know men who've worked hard for their treasures of little worth,but lost their passion for others on their quest to own this earth.Seems we've measured our whole existence on how much we can buy,from the size of house we live in to the type of car we drive.must we fill our pockets full then turn our back on friends,if we could only find our passion theres reward in the end.It's been been said in life were all delt with a loseing hand,but time will only tell whats in God's master plan.All the doors in God's house are open for us wide,but passion will be one thing we'll need to get inside.So can't we show a little passion help others when we can,try and love one another maybe lend a helping hand.Like the old cliche goes you rep what you sow,lets not lose our place in heaven for the likes of this worlds gold.
