Looking at old photographs
Places faded in memory
Have I been consumed by the hate inside me
I want to do the right thing 
But I am confused
I have been beaten and hurt
I try to have a gentle hand
but sometimes I am so goddamned cruel
Can I pay for all my sins
Can I put the pieces back together
Is it too late for beginnings
and past due for endings
I love my brothers and sisters
I hate them so damn much
I see red
Sometimes it is all monochromatic
God I could scream
But I am afraid I may wake myself
