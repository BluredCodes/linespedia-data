The Ground Trembles 
I try to hold your hand 
Please don't fall away 
I need you 
I want you 
I have traveled the world and time 
In you I have found what I sought 
Don't hide your eyes 
Don't hide your love 
Lay me bare 
See me from the inside out 
Hold me 
Understand me
Share with me 
I want to know your dreams  
I want to know your love
