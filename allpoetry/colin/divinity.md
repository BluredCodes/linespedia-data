---
title: "Divinity"
writer: "Colin"
slug: "divinity"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I spoke in dead languages to dead godsSearching for truthSearching for a light to guide me into the hallow halls of my ancestorsI found my mother divine goddess and mother to all lifeI found my father horned god of the wildI found myselfPeace at lastDoubts fall away like discarded robes and the fires of rage cool to bright red embersHere I find what I soughtIn my solitary journey I have found divinity
