I gave all I had to you
The things that made me smile
The things I loved about myself
My most treasured possessions and memories

You held them close
I felt safe 
I felt warm
I did not feel alone

Then you looked at me broken and bruised
I was not good enough
You walked away
I stood staring at the door
Thinking maybe you would come back
Maybe you would bless me a kiss upon the forehead and sacrament on my tongue
I know now some doors never open again and now I stumble and falter
Clumsily crawling through life knowing I lost the one thing I ever loved

Some people just are not meant to be happy
