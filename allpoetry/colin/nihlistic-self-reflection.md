title: "Nihlistic Self Reflection"

My mother was a whore
Selling her body for cocaine
I hate her she makes me sick
I love her and think of her often

My father was a drunk 
Forgot we were alive and it is better that way
I can't stand his idle conversations or his pats on the back
or his "good boy"s
He disgusts me 
I love him and blame him far too much

I hate myself going nowhere no education 
Too angry far too cruel
But I have a hart and the worlds pain hurts me
Tears on a childs face seem like blasphemy to the holy state of youth
My words never seem good enough
I can't help but think I could be a better man
