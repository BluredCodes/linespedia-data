I saw you standing in the shadows I saw you in the corner of my eye Blurred movement and a distinct smile I felt the music roll over me Guitar rifts and bass lines Pounding like a beating heart I knew I just wanted to dance with you I knew there was something about you Moving the through the crowd Bodies pushing me here and there Swimming up stream For you I want that beautiful girl with hazel eyes I want to dance with her I watched as you moved to the door Feet pounding on the floor Running in the rain Trying to make the 3 o'clock train Trying to find that beautiul girl with the hazel eyes She makes me feel alive Just want to know your name tomorrow Want to know your dreams
