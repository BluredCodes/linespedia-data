title: "An incedent with God"
writer: "Colin"

Rolling from the mountains
Sound and Color
Sunlight looks strange at night
God told me he was sorry
But I was disappointed
and relived
He had no shoes
Hadn't since Nam
His offering plate a sweat stained hat
He is ashamed of the pain
So he lives in a dumpster
A repentant Messiah
Eating chicken bones
I bought god shoes today
and the Holiest of Holies
Alpha and Omega
Said thank you
from behind the tears
