Hypocrisy is 
as hypocrisy does
How it is now
and how it was
Leaders from the start
during their time on earth
Hold delusions of grandeur
about their total worth
It's as if they can't add
even though they have tried
Subtracting human rights
as their riches multiplied
A failure to realize
they're not "untouchable" after all
Will result in a surprise
when others answer the call
Not as nuch a call to arms
as a call to do what's needed
An end to their reign
so the idiocy is impeded
If I hear that call
and I'm listening pretty good
The ending will be happy
just too bloody for Hollywood
