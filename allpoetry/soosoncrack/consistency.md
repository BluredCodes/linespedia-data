One afternoon at the table
while "Coors clutching" with a friend
My friend offered a comment
on her not to lie I can depend

She came right out and said it
"Eddie , you're an asshole"
This was nothing new to me
it was evident in my last poll

In fact to tell the truth
it's been this way since I remember
All through March and Spring
through Summer and till December

So I agreed wholeheartedly
and explained the following to her
First we're what we're supposed to be
and when we die it's who we were

Consistency is so vital
to getting along it seems to me
Unexpected reactions to situations
make life harder than it should be

Each type of person
that's in the world today
Can do their share for society
if their "ego" stays out of the way

I can handle anyone
jerks,morons and yes
Assholes are controllable too
once you get them to confess

Worrying how one will react
to a certain problem or event
Depletes the problem solving mode
not good use of time thats spent

The bottom line is this
whenever you knock upon my door
It's just me,Eddie the asshole
never less and forever more
