On the ____ day of Christmas Osama got from me:A shotgun blast to the kneeTwo crotch kicksThree left hooksFour dried up tirdsFive fractured ribsSix slaps for disobeyingSeven 'Seals' a swimmingEight 'Berets' land miningNine Taliban castrationsTen stitches to stop bleedingEleven 'Rangers' aimingTwelve direct hitseak © 2001
