Being a Football Fan(American)
is great this time of year
With some 26 College Football games
I've every reason to cheer

But why limit this "Bowl Mania"
to just the Holiday Season
It does'nt have to be Football
and we don't need a reason

Lets have a "Lethargy Bowl"
for teams like L.A.'s Dodgers
Who could play a softball team
coached by Pee Wee Herman and Mr. Rodgers

Mainstream society could have fun too
Police could play the "Law Enforcement Bowl"
A touch football game played
while eating donuts and cinnamon rolls

The "Executive Bowl"
would be exciting indeed
A totem pole of A-Holes
displaying their excessive greed

Our Nations Capitol
could also participate
With the "Deep Throat Bowl"
in honor of Watergate

Television advertisers must be represented
and their brains would be tickin'
It's the "Dog Eat Dog Bowl"
and they all taste like Chicken

How about our Prisons
whose populace grows by the hour
The "Soap on a Rope Bowl"
played in a great big shower

Maybe the "Viagra Bowl"
featuring women with D-Cups
Video-recorded while helping
George Burns to get it up

The "Intern Bowl"
another D.C. mess
Playing the winner of the "Viagra Bowl"
the one in the semen stained dress

This tradition's been with us forever
Adam and Eve invented it I'm told
The day they removed their fig-leafs
and played the first "Salad Bowl"
