It was "Good Times Bad Times"
no "Communication Breakdowns" in 68
We were all "Dazed and Confused
once "You Shook Me",I could'nt wait
Read a book called "Moby Dick"
about "What is and What Should Never Be"
Met a "Heartbreaker" who sang "The Lemon Song"
she's a "Livin Lovin Maid" to me
Went "Out On The Tiles"
my "Friends" had a "Celebration Day"
Headed for the "Gallows Pole"
sang an "Immigrant Song" all the way
Was "Going to California"
for the "Battle of Evermore"
Went to a "Misty Mountain Hop"
and a "Black Dog" evened the score
Sang a "Rain Song" over "The Ocean"
"Over the Hills and Far Away"
"Dancing Days" were here again
"The Song Remains the Same" to this day
Took a "Night Flight" to "Kashmir"
A "Black Country Woman" baked "Custard Pie"
"The Rover" was "Down By The Seaside"
"Sick Again" but still tried
Ordered up "Tea For One"
as I witnessed "Achille's Last Stand"
It was "Nobody's Fault But Mine"
"Candy Store Rock" is still in demand
Went "Southbound To Suarez"
like a "Fool In The Rain"
Spun from a "Carouselambra"
"All My Love" went down the drain
This dirigible still flies
it's "Presence" will always be
So sad a part found the "Stairway To Heaven"
now there are only three
