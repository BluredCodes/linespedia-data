I'm a "Bad Company" man
and as the "Seagull" flies above
It's for sure I'm "Movin On"
if you don't "Feel Like Makin Love"

I'm the "Youngblood" no more
but you're still my "Honey Child"
Can't get "Silver,Blue and Gold"
without "Runnin With the Pack" awhile

I "Make No Deals With Preachers"
seen many a "Shooting Star"
"Straight Shooters" love a "Whiskey Bottle"
"Good Lovin Gone Bad" so far

As the "Burnin Sky" glows
she's still "Everything I Need"
to "Do Right By Your Woman"
"Too Bad" both hearts must bleed

Should learn to "Take The Time"
so these "Crazy Circles" would stop
If she winds up "Gone,Gone,Gone"
I'll be "Bad Company" till I drop
