Me heap big smart manme like to believeBut lotsum what I doheap hard to conceiveMe workum hard and me workum lateStill not enough 'wampum'to take to 'Squaw Mate'I gettum cashme spendum cashIf me leave in pocketmaybe money turn to ash?Me always buy what wantnever what me needBookie wanting moneyme got mouths to feedSometimes go to 'Race Track'bet on big ponies that runMaybe lucky break evenbut plenty 'firewater' and funSometimes secret stuffhave to fill 'peace pipe'Must fill pipe withonly 'Grade A Type'Then go home to Squawshe ask where 'wampum' wentThen she yell and scream'Maybe I leave this tent'!That's how is right nowme always seem 'half-lit'So how are you todaymy name is 'Running Dumbshit' eak©2000
