Time is not of the essence
at least not to me
How it is utilized
largely dictates what we'll be
If it is wasted
it is difficult to grow
And the more we squander it
the less we will know
It breeds speculation
and comments made in haste
These un-informed statements
leave in my mouth a bad taste
To run one's mouth 
before one is sure
De-evolves that human
below animals with fur
The mouth should not run
until it knows the facts
It's  basically useless to all
except the flies that it attracts
So,I will try harder
I'd suggest you do the same
Keep your mouth shut
until you know more than your name
