Is there someone here
who's supposed to be the boss?
If so please step forward 
for your ass it's time to toss

Was this your first job
did you not learn from your last?
Because the way things are
I think we'll make this your last

If you opened your eyes 
could you see what's going down?
Or would your uncorrected vision
allow thoughts of hanging around?

Well I wear contacts
and my eyes can see
I've reached the conclusion
you don't deserve to be

Don't deserve a position
making decisions or rules
You barely deserve to exist
were both parents moronic fools?

The eruptions of stupidity
accepted,almost cherished
Situations can't improve
till fools like you have perished

Allowing so many illiterates
to assume "shot caller" roles
It's up the lunatic ladder
a totem-pole of asholes

People with measureable I.Q's
the distance between them vast
Not allowed in the "click" for years
cherish today it may be your last

Your last day in power
last day to be called number one
Prepare for the rest of your life
it's not gonna be much fun

A more cerebral approach
will quickly take over
Replacements will be easy
here comes one named "Rover"

Rover won't allow bulshit
so where will you fit in?
Don't worry too much
you'll not have authority again

So pack your shit up 
and try to keester that "Crown"
Because time's up Mother Fuckers
time for you to "Step Down"
eak 1998
