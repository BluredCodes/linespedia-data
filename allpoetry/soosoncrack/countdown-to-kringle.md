Snow is on the ground
Holiday spirits fill the air
Snowmen are popping up
noses of carrots and mops for hair

Children on their sleds
who can ride the steepest hill?
Just how things should be
in a season of cheer and goodwill

The day after Thanksgiving
is when the "countdown" starts
The years most anticipated birthday
and joyous spirits fill our hearts

The shopping malls fill up
on the busiest day of the year
The kids eat turkey sandwiches
and wonder when Santa will get here

Anticipation fills the air
our spirits fly so high
Up and down each street
Christmas lights fill the dark sky

There are reunions and parties
with drinks that are sweet
Courtesy seems more plentiful
in everyone we meet

Just for this month
times are like this,what a bummer
Attitudes return with the New Year
and stay with us until Summer

This Season I'm hoping
that it won't take us forever
Every day could be like Christmas
if we'd get our stuff together
