Never thought I'd sit down
and write a "Dear John" letter
But I'm all set to go
and the sooner the better

While writing I can't help it
I feel like I'm wasting time
I'm not even sure you can read
you ignorant,scummy bag of slime

You've committed treason
in it's most blatant and vulgar form
This poem is just a little rain
as you await justice's violent storm

You're no better than binLaden
as a matter of fact,maybe worse
I can't help but count the days
till you're both in the back of a Hearse

You make me so sick
you're a walking form of pollution
Just a walking piece of shit
a slow and painful death the only solution

Your Dad said you were a "good boy"
oh yeah,from the bottom of what barrel?
I've not seen or heard Mom yet
but Pops should've been sterile

People say you were "Brainwashed"
start wishing you were dead "Mr.Bubble"
I'd love killing a piece of shit like you
it's just too much freakin'trouble
