"Maybe I'm no Rin Tin Tin
nor a Labrador Retriever
Don't treat me like a Chihuahua
I'll turn you Poodles into believers
Straight out of Lady and the Tramp
I'm proud to be a Mongrel Mutt
Don't need your "blue ribbon" purity
to show you all what's what
Not applying for St. Bernard (hood)
I can't save all your canine asses
Most of you little yappers
ought to take some obedience classes
Don't blame it on your humans
because you did'nt train them right
After cornering some alley cat
and he kicked your ass that night
Stop being the typical "Fido"
with the "sit" and "roll over" crap
Start herding the humans with cattle
my seeing eye Sheperds made you a map
We're their best friends
but they are not ours
Some are mean and stupid
and others won't take showers
We are on a mission
my four legged friends
From Dachsunds to Great Danes
on us the future depends
So,if your situation is out of hand
don't just sit like a bump on a log
Call my Road Dogs and me to fix it
for I am "Blunderdog"
