title: "'Pied Piper of Politicians'"

Hello,I'm baaack...
do you remember me?
I was called to "Hamlin" once
to walk some rats to sea

Whoever called this time
did not leave their name
The request was really sad
in fact it's quite a shame

Business has been slow
for "Pied Pipers" these days
So I've been making money
in lots of different ways

But I'll be busy now
taking back my old position
Not ridding you of rats this time
but getting rid of your politicians

The rats were easy
I played music and they followed
By sundown I was done
as by the sea they were swallowed

Politicians may be more difficult
not quite so easy to fool
As deceit,lying and treachery
are their own favorite tools

Being environmentally conscious
leading them to water is not my wish
I don't want to be remembered
for poisoning all of the fish

There has to be a place
it has to be large indeed
To put these worthless worms
so humanity may succeed

For the Republicans I'll just play
Pink Floyd's classic "Money"
This will work on them
like trapping a bear with honey

The Democrats will follow
there are more of them to bury
This should require no more
than some Peter,Paul and Mary

This date with destiny
for these worthless blobs of scuttle?
The launching pad at Cape Kennedy
and a one way ticket on the Shuttle
