Rudolph the Red-Nosed Ranger
you're Patriotic,and it shows
This time we're fighting more than fog
we need you and your nose

That 'beak" of yours is sacred
each nostril,so tight and sweet
Just light the way for a little death
and your job is half complete

You'll make the right decision
of this we are quite sure
By the way,you replaced 'Gentle Ben'
my jacket was made of his fur

This should really be easy
but just between you and me
We're after some cat in a Turban
who was thrown from his own family tree

He'll lay low like a rat
and wait for a big piece of cheese
Once captured stick a foot in his ass
preferably up to the knee

This is only temporary
he'll gladly stay still for awhile
As things stuck up bin Laden's ass
have a tendency to make him smile

Osama is getting his very own Prison
brand new,by the Falls in Niagra
He gets three brand new cellies
convicted rapists strung out on viagra
