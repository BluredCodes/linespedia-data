It was 'All Hallow's Eve'Goblins filled the streetVampires and Werewolvesknocked on doors for Trick or TreatGhouls and Ghostsdon't miss any doorsAlong with them are creatureswho still walk on all foursPerpetual echo'sof door chimes and 'Trick or Treat'Made me stop and thinkdo Monsters get sore feet?I mean Frankenstein's bootsthey can't be that good for walkingForget the Trick or Treatingit's Dr. Scholl he should be stalkingAnd all of that sugarthey'll collect on this nightBy next Halloweenthere won't be a tooth in sightHow scary is a Monsterwithout any fangs?Just another Shirley Templebut with shorter bangsSo Monsters,drop the candytake a load off those sore feetGo get some foot padsand some Jell-o instead of meateak © 2000
