I set my "Strawberry Alarm Clock"
as I slept on the "Dock of the Bay"
"Vanilla Fudge" was my favorite flavor
I had the "Summertime Blues" that day

Had "My Generation" arrived
were we really "Born to be Wild"
If you went to "San Francisco"
you were simply a "Flower Child"

"Twenty Five or Six Two Four"
replaced "Love Potion Number Nine"
At this "Beggars Banquet"
we never "Spilt the Wine"

Climbed up a "Mountain"
and visited a "Mississippi Queen"
This was no "Imaginary Western"
the "Blood of the Sun" was mean

Wound up in a "White Room"
where "Tales of Ulysses"were told
A "Sunshine of your Love"
at the edge of each "Crossroad"

Was I "Experienced"
"Crosstown Traffic's" such a mess
Thanks for the "Purple Haze"
and refining the "Manically Depresed"
