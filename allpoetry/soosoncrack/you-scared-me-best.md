---
title: "'You Scared Me Best'"
writer: "soosoncrack"
slug: "you-scared-me-best"

Think way back
to when you were a child
Your actions not considered
rebellious or even wild

Yet once you were older
a name was attached
To attitudes or feelings
you were always over-matched

Being at this disadvantage
your elders beat you down
Both physically and mentally
your scared eyes viewed their "crowns"

These "Kings" and "Queens"
of imaginary places
Allowed the "pauper" in them all
to show in their faces

They'd put up facades
constructed of fear
Hiding hideous insides
they'd allow no one near

But they did not hide
whenever it came to you
This "game " was fixed
you did as told to

Remaining obedient
out of immense fear
Not as mush afraid of them
as of them seeing a tear

Now I see them
through mature,hardened eyes
One "steely" stare from me
now it's them that cries

Their awareness of a fact
that this "game" has changed
For all intents and purposes
"THE END" is re-arranged

Now it's so pathetic
the saddest thing I've seen
Scrambling to wash their slates
that will never come clean

The difference in them
a complete 180 degrees
Once they deem their slate clean
I'll bring them to their knees

I'll stare into their eyes
windows to empty souls
They'll look back at mine
into two dark holes

Speaking so softly
as I lay each to rest
Telling each they're  the first
because "You Scared Me Best"
