You never know what you'll seeon a stroll down 'Memory Lane'Never know who you'll meetwhat you'll like or disdainIt's a 'roll of the dice'on a table thats slantedIt's a botched circumcisionby a drunken Rabbi who chantedIt's a day without sunshinethere's no Moon to be foundA squared circle is the Starof this 'Theatre in the Round'It's the tirds in the front yardthanks to the neighbor's dog SpotWho should be slapped into a feverand slapped again for being hotIt's the fool in front of youthat won't get off the brakesThe one behind you tailgatesall stuff thats hard to takeIt's the check writing foolin the 'cash only' lineIt's wishing you could afford to kick his ass and just pay the fineIt's the 'check is in the mail'from someone with no addressIt's all a bunch of shitand makes for some real 'Stinky Stress'eak ©2001
