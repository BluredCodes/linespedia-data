It was time to 'Dream On''Walkin'the Dog' with 'Mama Kin'Once we 'Got Our Wings' and 'Rocks'we were 'Back in the Saddle' againThere were 'Toys in the Attic'we'd 'Walk This Way' 'No More'Whipped out a 'Big Ten Inch Record''Drew the Line' and hit the floorHad some down timeuntil we found a new 'Pump'Made 'Love in an Elevator'after a 'Crazy' yet 'Amazing' slump'Jamie Got a Gun'we threw a 'Monkey Off Our Back''Falling in Love'hurt our kneesour 'Nine Lives' cut no slackBut this 'Train Keeps a Rollin'through 'Swet Emotions' 'Other Side'Destination,Rock's Hall of Fameon a far from 'Jaded Ride'eak©2001
