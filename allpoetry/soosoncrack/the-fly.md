The view from here is great
if the scene were only as deserving
This would never pass as non-fiction
all these actions I'm observing

Destruction in its rawest form
degradation to the lowest stage
The same story every day
the only difference the number on the page

Go to work each day
an honest effort at hard work
Paychecks don't last a day
giving money away like a jerk

They may get locked up
have to do a little time
When actually their own identity
is the only victim in this crime

Is it suicidal?Is it physical?
Or is it just plain crazy?
I guess it could be all three
or maybe they just like it hazy

Being the proverbial "Fly on the wall"
let me tell you,I've seen some things
From sex and Hookers with Presidents
to "Parliamentary Pimping" by Kings

You humans can be fun to watch
though bent upon self-slaughter
If I ever thought I was turning human
I'd "kamikaze" into your fly swatter
