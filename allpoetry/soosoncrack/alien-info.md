All bells were tolling
the western front seemed quiet
A false sense of security
preceded a galactical riot

I sat at a local watering hole
everyone was imbibed and lying
From all the ones that got away
to working for the CIA spying

My booth was near a window
something outside caught my eye
Little whirling dishes of light
repelled from a jet black sky

They landed in the parking lot
hard believing what I saw
They suctioned to the ground
asd if bolstering gravity's law

Out got three dudes
you know,little green guys
With great big green heads
and one giant black crossed eye

I'm not sure which was the leader
but the smallest one started yakkin'
As he rambled on I just hoped
and prayed he was'nt packin'

I said,"hey there little fella"
and I'd never been more sincere
You are now on Earth
and you don't call the shots here

I thought you were more intelligent
I guess you've been over-rated
Now you're talking trash
and I'm getting aggravated

You've supposedly been here
on Earth a few times before
Even with just one stupid eye
why would you want to see more?

And as for these "probes"
that happen during these abductions
Don't do anymore without permission
and you'd better follow my instructions

No matter what part of society
rich,poor or middle class
Before starting an anal probe
remove human head from human ass

Y'all can stay till morning
do your explorin'and patrollin'
And seeing's how I like you
I'll only charge a buck per colon

Don't feel too bad
most of these fools won't ever forget ya'
Just don't let that door hit ya'
where the good lord split ya'
