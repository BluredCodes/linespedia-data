"Jack Flash" is jumping
"Shattered" rocks still roll
A"Nineteenth Nervous Breakdown"
thanks to "Brown Sugar" and a bowl

This "Machine thats my Head"
such a "Lazy" "Highway Star"
Canoes that "Smoke on the Water"
"Women From Tokyo" made my car

Always in a "Rush"
often a "Fly by Night"
If "All the World's a Stage"
"Tom Sawyer's" a pitiful sight

There are "Toys in this Attic"
This "Dude Ain't no Lady"
It's "Sweet Emotion"lotion
for him and Marcia Brady

So "Hot n Nasty"
may get "30 Days in the Hole"
So "C'mon Everybody"
why's my "Fever turned Stone Cold"

I won't "Ramble On"
I'll go read "Moby Dick"
No "Communication Breakdowns"
or I'll hit you with "Four Sticks"
