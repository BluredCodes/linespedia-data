Time to go shopping
for some things I need
Part of my preperation
a few bonghits of weed
This creates the stupor
that I must be in
This prevents one from calling
any next of kin
A combination of atrocities
Supermarkets hold for us all
Wobbly carts for one 
drive me up a wall
Then there's always
the great marketing technique
So I can't find anything
they move shit each week
As for those bonghits
they seem to increase in me
The need to consume
and partake of some groceries
The chips are so accessible
as much as they're for sale
And very easy to eat
when the Security Guard reads braille
Enough for the snacking
the Butcher is a jabroni
I'll just go back there 
and eat me some baloney
Well now...what's for dessert
those pastries look delicious
I'll tear into those Twinkies
as if I were Sid Vicious
With this mission completed
and my stomach nice and full
Next stop is my abode
and a bonghit for my skull
