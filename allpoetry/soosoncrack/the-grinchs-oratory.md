Bah,the Holidays
I hate this time of year
And you'll understand
after my story you hear

It happened long ago
I tell you,I was framed
But ever since that time
for the crime I am blamed

They called me a thief
they told me I'd pay
For being the guy
who stole Christmas day

I did'nt understand
for I had always thought
One got a fair trial
before one was shot

Before jury selection
deemed guilty as I could get
They'd have found me guilty
had we never met

I'd never do a crime
as heinous as that
I leave all that stuff
to the "Cat in the Hat"

For all these years
maligned in such stench
Forever now called
the "Evil Mr.Grinch"

Well listen to this
you tore up "Legal eagles"
You don't possess the smarts
of either one of my Beagles

Somebody else did this
and if I only knew
I guarantee by New Years
that they would be through

You don't think I like lights
and singing Christmas Carols?
Don't think I enjoy
drinking "nog" down by the barrels?

Trimming trees is fun
I used to enjoy that
But I can't anymore
thanks to some dirty rat

So as you all party
and bring in a New Year
I only speak the truth
really loud and clear

Whoever you are
that did this to me
One of these years soon
your ass belongs to me
