My soul is so tired
my spirit is dead
My wheels keep spinning
no matter what's said

I've trapped myself
and constructed a cage
Locked myself in
with diabolical rage

An element created
with help from just me
Unwilling to escape
a refusal to be free

A life which started
with plenty of support
As I poisoned my mind
the choice was to abort

Now my "missions"
are few and far between
Failing to realize
just how little they mean

This "Tunnel Vision"
I myself selected
Left the choices I had
behind and un-protected

Now through a haze
as I look but don't see
Plucking out both eyes
as I cannot face me
