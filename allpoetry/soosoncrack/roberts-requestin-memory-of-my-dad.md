It's been 25 years
a quarter century since the day
That's a long time Dad
since you've been away

After all this time
and due to my neglect
These overdue lines are written
from my Heart and with respect

You were'nt around a lot
but you were'nt a "dead-beat Dad"
I'll put you up against any Father
that anyone ever had

Circumstances dictated
that we'd be apart
But seperation holds no candle
to Love that's from the Heart

Mom and you divorced
when I was just over one year
But bad words about each other
I did'nt ever hear

Your explanations of why
a Marriage did'nt last forever
The visible Love between you
but just could not live together

I was constantly in awe
of your humor and your wit
Such an easy guy to Love
but very difficult to forget

One thing stays with me
of all the things you'd say
And it pops into my head
almost every single day

You asked of me one thing
out of all the talks we had
You'd say "do one thing for me Ed"
"Just don't wind up like your dad"

I'm very sorry Dad
but I did'nt come through
As every day that passes
I get a little more like you

It's not because I did'nt listen
to your single request
There's just too much of you in me
to ever pass that test
IN MEMORIUM OF ROBERT A. KESSEL
