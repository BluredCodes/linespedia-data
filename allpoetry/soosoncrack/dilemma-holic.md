There's a big time 'jones'inside me right nowI must find some 'dilemma'don't care where or howConflict and turmoilthat's the stuff for meIf there is no crisesno happiness do I seeWon't somebody pleasemess somehting up real badThis living without confusion is driving me madAt least a little conflictsomeone with whom to disagreeChaotic,wild and crazyis the way things should beThe calm before the stormabout bores me to deathAnd I will feel this wayuntil my dying breathLife is not worth livingif not shrouded in confusionWhen things run so smoothlyit shatters my illusionSo,trouble and woesdo not forsake me nowShower me with anxietybefore I have a fucking coweak© 1999
