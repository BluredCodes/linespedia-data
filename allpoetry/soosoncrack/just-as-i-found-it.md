There will be no heroicsno Grand Slam Home RunThings will be the sameafter all is said and doneNo fourth and incheswith time ticking awayWhen the clock runs outit'll be just another dayAccomplishments non-existentspinning wheels that still runFor tomorrow never comesso stop searching for the sunThe essence that was timedid'nt stop for any clockA mouth that's still runningbut it's nothing more than talkWhat once was so vibrantand thrived upon actionCancels destiny's appointmentas dreams are just distractionsThe artist that would escapelet reality get awayA would be masterpiecemelts down a canvas that's decayedNo comtributions will be madethere's no getting around itAshamed and so embarressedto leave this 'just as I found it'eak©2000
