---
title: "'E.T. Phones Home'"
writer: "soosoncrack"
slug: "et-phones-home"
source: "AllPoetry"

I'm but a visitor
sent here to study you
And I must admit
I'm amazed at things you do

This "sphere" you call a planet
a study in progress for my kind
We find it hard to understand
intellectually you're so far behind

Your surroundings seem to possess
tools to survive and flourish
Yet you run around in circles
what you'd call a whirling dervish

So many different theories
we consider this quite healthy
But you only have two types
there are poor and there are wealthy

This seems to be a problem
that's been with you forever
You can't seem to find a way
for the two to live together

As the wealthy hold their post
guarding their tarnished crowns
It's gettinng much more difficult
to hold the poor ones down

I've noticed many methods
the more fortunate have explored
Still the poor ones work
their situation still ignored

This has been the "status quo"
for as long as most of you know
What you fail to relize is 
like this you'll never grow

Not grow to a level
where you actually can "live"
Not grow enough spiritually
where you're able to forgive

I must admit sorrowfully
you have me entranced
To confuse me or my kind
you're not considered that advanced

But now I must return
with a message or some plan
Some sort of guarantee
we'll not fall prey to "Man"

Perhaps we should just do this
be rid of you once and for all
But then we'd be too much like you
intellectually very small

I'll just tell them what I've seen
they'll find it hard to believe
As for the Love of the God you worship
"Are you worthy to receive"?
