Nothing is minenowhere is meOut of placeamongst all I seeThe 'Missing Piece'?I definitely think notMy search is way off coursethis isn't what was soughtI was lookingfor something more sereneSomething more cerebralsomething far less in-betweenNot so much predictablebut logical and consistentA brave new worldwhere idiocy's non-existentWhere when one is huntingknowledge is the 'game'Where people are accountablenot pointing fingers of blameWhere 'To each his own'isn't just a hollow quoteAs long as it ain't my 'oar'whatever rows your boatBut I've been puzzled beforeprobably get confused againBut only until the dayI find the game I can wineak ©2001
