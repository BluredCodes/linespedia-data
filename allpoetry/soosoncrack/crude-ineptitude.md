If I were you
and I think I am
I'd straighten up
and get with the program

Instead of walking around
and sticking out my chest
I'd make an investment
in a bullet proof vest

The toes you've stepped on
really do not appreciate
Your attitude or actions
they will reciprocate

Your position as of late
one of power and control
You should've acted like you belonged there
not like an ignorant troll

If I thought you capable
of maintaining this position
We could have saved this visit
to your local physician

So,watch your back
and watch it good
You'll soon call me "Daddy"
as I stand where you stood
