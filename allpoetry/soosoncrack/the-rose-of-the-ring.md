In the red corner
black trunks trimmed in white
The big bad bully
"just call me Dolomite"

In the blue corner
lacy white trunks with tan
He's the "Rose of the Ring"
Stewart the sweet smellin' man

The fighters met in the ring
both jabbing and moving
Dolomite was tough
and Stewart's smell was behooving

Before each fight
while Stewart was alone
He'd use half a bottle
of his favorite cologne

On his way to the ring
all his adoring fans
Would scream and cheer
and wave deodorant cans

But on this evening
versus the "evil" Dolomite
He'd run out of "Old Spice"
it was an "Aqua Velva" night

Stewart got pummelled
the first couple of rounds
His cologne attracting "Poodles"
instead of the desired "Bloodhounds"

Late in the fight
a bottle flew into the ring
"Stew" took a splash from the bottle
and started to swing

As the bell rang
to start the final round
"Old Spice" was in the air
Dolomite was on the ground
