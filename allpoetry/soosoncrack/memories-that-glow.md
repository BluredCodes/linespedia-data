Thoughts at this timeare memories held dearOf times spent with Grandmaall crystal clearI recall CircusesOperas and ShowsA tender approachto solving one's woesI'll not forget placesthat we used to goThe Beach,a Museumor 'The Hot Dog Show'Holidays brought the aromaof baking and butterThe cookies we'd bakerequired every cookie cutterMusic was so specialsomething Grandma held so dearHer voice,it rang out to meso smooth,sweet and clearSometimes the Pianowould be our play for the dayResulting in yearningsto increase my stayThough as the years passedless time spent togetherThese memories with GrandmaI'll have foreverWith all these thoughtsof times long agoThank You Grandmafor these 'Memories That Glow'eak©1997
