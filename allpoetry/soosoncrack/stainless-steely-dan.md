title: "'Stainless Steely Dan'"
writer: "soosoncrack"

Time for a "Change of the Guard"
so dizzy from "Reelin in the Years"
"Turned That Heartbeat Over Again"
no more "Dirty Work" blood,sweat and tears
All kinds of of "Showbiz Kids"
departed from "My Old School"
Say "Bodhisatva" three times fast
the "Countdown To Ecstasy" for a fool
Took things "Night By Night"
"Through With Buzz" the asshole
"Rikki Don't Lose That Number" for bananas
food for the "Monkey in Your Soul"
Hit by "Chain Lightning"
had to see "Doctor Wu"
Wore "Bad Sneakers" on "Black Friday"
once "Katy Lied" I was through
Ran off to the "Caves of Altamira"
wore "The Fez" as a disguise
It was such a "Royal Scam"
But "Kid Charlamagne" still flies
Before they "Don't Take Me Alive"
my old friend "Deacon Blues"
Please find "Peg and Josie"
and tell them this news
Thank "My Rival" for this insanity
which allows "Time Out Of My Mind"
Even though I  "Can't Buy A Thrill"
this "Third World Man" he'll never find
