---
title: "'Me and Molly McGee'"
writer: "soosoncrack"

When the foot hits the door
a victorious heel over wood
Adrenaline is flowing
insuring it's kicked in good

Entry is the instant act
bearing the element of surprise
And for this instant
I'm like God in your eyes

You'll go where I send you
you will do as I say
To try and guarantee
that you survive this day

As I watch you sit
it's always amusing
The "why me" looks
why is this so confusing?

This obviously happens
when someone has been wronged
Maybe someone was hurt
for not being where they belonged

Whatever the story
the fact is,it's your turn
It need not happen again
if by this time you learn

I really can't help but laugh
as you sit all fat and jolly
As you put out the "welcome mat"
for me and my friend "Molly"
