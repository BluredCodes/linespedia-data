As time goes by
life's more like an examination
Ignorance and terrorism
their existence an abomination

Can it be stopped
this idiocy and it's stagnation
The world talks out it's neck
about "together" during a fragmentation

It's become unbelievable
a challenge to any imagination
How far we've all come
still low enough to cause frustration

World leaders and representatives
still trying to control situations
Now just second nature
to spew out mis-information

Fancy words to describe lying
the intentional spread of prevarecation
Their secret formula for convenience
a sickening addiction to fabrication

The bottom line is,they're lying
dissecting truth from situations
Blatantly beating humanity
awaitng society's capitulations

I'll take my own shots at convenience
as will the rest of the human population
Once transformed into the "powers that were"
we'll allow no more strangulation

Save yourselves and all of us some grief
pack things up for a permanent vacation
And do Me,Myself and I a favor
don't inform us of your destination
