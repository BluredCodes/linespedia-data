title: "'The Box'"

Something is missing
where could it be
It was not misplaced
and it belongs to me

Its contents at this time
not at issue here
I must take it back
I feel that it is near

This happened once before
and regrets still exist 
For sorrows and pains
borne of a dark mist

Light was shed on the perpetrator
another "Eve" if you will
She crossed over a line
and succumbed to it's thrill

She was not ready
none of you could be
For the contents of the "Box"
which was stolen from me

With pestulance a plenty
and a stronghold of disease
It actually was humorous
the "world" brought to it's knees

The world looks very different
as it grovels beneath one's feet
Emitting torrents of fear
hoping that one is discreet

A case was well plead
by the planet as a whole
But the powers that be
failed miserably in their role

Justice was done
as far as I'm concerned
My "Box" was back with me
and your world still turned

In this universe
there is so much around
For now it is mine
my personal "lost and found"

I'll now properly direct this
at whom it is for
You silly bitch Pandora
we've been through this once before
