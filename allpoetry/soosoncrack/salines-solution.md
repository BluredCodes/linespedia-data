The biggest lie on Earth?
Maybe it's "Big boys don't cry"
But just get back up
un-concerned with why

Fortunately for me
I was'nt raised that way
Yes,my heart is on my sleeve
and right there it will stay

My emotions are active
they'll be that way tomorrow
I'm thankful for the joy
but heed the reality that is sorrow

I may seem irrational
emotional spectrums run wide
Don't base a judgement of me
on whether I smiled or cried

It does'nt really matter
what people think of me
Maybe they should cry themselves
to rinse out eyes that cannot see
