Alcohol and cigarettes
the "legal addicts" pair
But profitable taxwise
so no one seems to care

"Illicit drugs" are different
oh yeah,why is that so?
It's for your own protection
we care for you you know

Come on now
lets try and be sincere
Do I remind you of a moron
is that how I appear?

Insulting me like that
is this system's big mistake
I see their eyes are open
does this mean they are awake?

Possibly in your dreams
if you dream in black and white
Everyone in the dreams 
just nods and says you're right

This is not a dream
it's here and it is now
Anything I wish to do
you want to tell me how

The very idea of you
running anything's amusing
That it's lasted this long
to me is so confusing

Chosen by the people
at least those easiest to fool
Then sit upon your ass
and make up silly rules

The time and money wasted
as politicians finger-point
If laws applied to them
they'd all be in the joint

When these "laws" are written
of sour grapes they're made
They are tainted at best
hardly worthy of being obeyed

So very many laws
filling thousands of books
Half of them written
by greedy elected crooks

So many of our politicians
have a strange idea you see
"I make the laws therefore"
"they don't apply to me"

We make them for you
all of our fellow man
We know they may seem tough
 but do the best you can

Then they disappear
immune from our detection
Not to be seen again
until the next election

If I could get face to face
and tell them how I felt
Some real severe punishment
is what I would be dealt

For this is the land
they say of "Milk and Honey"
Funny how most laws we have
don't apply to those with money
