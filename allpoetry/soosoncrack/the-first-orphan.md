---
title: "'The First Orphan'"
writer: "soosoncrack"
slug: "the-first-orphan"
source: "AllPoetry"

Do you ever get curious
of what's inside you?
Along with organs and blood
or should I say who

Inside it's mysterious
a "warehouse" if you will
Parts that give and take
even parts that thrill

Nows as good a time as any
for you to meet some of these
Some you will like
some will bring you to your knees

Here's one part
that makes you quiver
Like nails on a blackboard
that make you shiver

This part's glad to meet you
here come some more now
These parts like destruction
and love showing you how

All of these parts 
make up what is me
I am in you and of you
and I always shall be

This should'nt bother you
don't let it make you sad
Even though some will tell you
that I can be real bad

Don't listen to them
they know not what they say
I'm inside of them too
and they all will pay

You see,I'm an "Orphan"
the first one of all
My "Daddy" got angry
so I had a great fall

I do not need him
and neither do you
"Halos" are so silly
and "Harps" are too

So you just sit back
as I take the controls
"He" gave you life
I'm just here stealing souls
