This 'sphere' that is our homethe planet called EarthOur inhabitance herehas decreased its worthWe've raped Mother Naturetime and time againDestroyed a work in progressand what it may have beenHumans are supposed to bethe smartest of all 'creatures'Yet it seems we are the 'animal'most in need of teachersWe've declined at such a ratethat treachery is acceptedLets be honest herethe treachery is expectedIf you mix  with humansit won't usually take longBefore somebody screws up and does someone wrongPart of our original make-up?I don't know that for sureBut I have a suggestionthat's quite possibly a cureWhen people are 'out of line'they should be told so right awayThen convinced accordinglyby exactly what you sayIf no one ever tells themtheir idiocy tends to growAlways talking, never listeningso they may learn and growSo get out your 'Q-Tips'you 'Homosapien Jerks'Your methods were an embarrassmentlets see how mine worksYou'd better listenand you really should learnThat by being an idiot nowa kick in the ass is all you'll earnIf that does'nt workbend over for me and coughI'm stopping this 'sphere'and booting your asses offeak © 2000
