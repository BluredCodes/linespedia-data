You don't even belive in me
so I don't know why I care
But come this Christmas Eve
don't worry,I'll be there

Forget about the being good crap
there's other stuff you should drop
So I'll play "fortune teller" 
about some shit you should drop

The less we act like a-holes
we're closer to whom we should be
And it's just common sense
we'll have more under the tree

Too spun anymore to care
whether you're sleeping or awake
Come on now,grow up
be good for your own damn sake

When you were children
I filled your stockings with switches
Don't piss me off this Christmas
Ill have to fill your head with stitches
