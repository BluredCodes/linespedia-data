I may not be "Ms.Cleo"
these ain't Tarot Cards I'm readin
If you're here seeking trouble
let me tell ya,you're succeeding

I've got a strong feeling
it's real deep and probably true
Ive run into "homegirls" before
there's one in Lomas,looking for you

Don't let things get fuzzy
keep your mind and vision clear
Keep your hands on your own wheel
or she'll grab it and try to steer

You may not wake up dead
but for sure you'll be cryin'
You'll probably feel stupid enough
that you ought to be dyin'

You ain't the first
a safe bet not the last
There's been quite a few
fragile egos, still in a cast

Yet,she's alright by me
just does what needs to be done
She may just be working
but she's still having fun
