'Scuttle Pup Pond'holds happy fish and blue fishLots of number one fishand some number two fishThere are somethat have it all fishAnd some that have'nt the balls fishThere's someI gotta be me fishSome religiouson their knees fishThere are even fishthat cannot swimWhose chances stayreally,really slimSome of these fishthey speak from the neckIt's so funny to watchwhen they get in a wreckSome fish smoke dopesome fish pop pillsOthers huff acetoneright through their gillsThe most dangerous is the 'William Tell' fishWhom once arrestedis the 'my booty's for sale fishAlso the meek'I always get screwed fish'The victim of the 'what the fuck you gonna do fish'These are my 'constituents'I am Mayor McScuttleIf you don't like it herego piss your own puddleeak©2000
