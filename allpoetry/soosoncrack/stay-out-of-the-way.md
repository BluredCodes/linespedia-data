Some call it 'anxiety'
some say it's 'blowing off steam'
I call it awakening some fools
to a not so pleasant dream

Yanking heads out of the sand
slapping the shit out of those that need it
Instilling a desire to learn
hoping they're receptive when we feed it

This ain't the 'Land of Oz'
where it's 'Lions and Tigers and Bears,oh my!'
It's the asshole of the universe
pieces of shit dressed as Arabs,fall from the sky

Retribution is moving at a snails pace
there's not near enough blood spilling
Lets find that other gear
get over there and do some real killing

Lets stop running around the globe asking
if what we're doing is okay
All these other nations need to know
is stay the fuck out of our way

Lets not forget who started this
keep your opinions,they're not needed
There's plenty of 'whoop-ass' left for them
once this Afghany 'gig' is completed
