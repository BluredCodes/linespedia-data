Spooks and candyGoblins and WitchesLittle Princesses and Hobo'swith holes in their britches'All Hallow's Eve'or 'Halloween Night'Wolfmen and Mummiesand Vampires that don't bitePeople answer their doorsto all Trick or TreatersWho tomorrow will be sickbecause they were over-eatersCarved Pumpkins on the porchtoothless faces emitting lightMr Jack O'Lantern is Honorary Mayor tonightBlack Cats cross pathsas Witches ride their broomsThen familiar Zombiesarise from their tombsRemember on this nightwhile out Trick or TreatingHave fun but rememberit's Monsters you'll be meetingeak ©2000
