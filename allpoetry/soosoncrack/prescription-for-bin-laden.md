Where shall I start Osama?Perhaps a bullet through your kneeThen I'll get a baseball batyou're a pinata, hanging from a treeI wouldn't bludgeon you longor hit you in the headI must finish some thingsbefore pronouncing you deadI'd beat you just long enoughto break each rib you possessThen I would untie youto avoid a big bloody messBy this time you'll be thirstywhich I knew would be the caseI'd quickly bash your teeth outand gleefully piss in your faceThen as you struggledto catch just a few dropsI place the phone just out of reachdon't you wish you could call some cops?You can't dial 911you cannot rech the phoneIt's just you and Iand we're here all aloneOn with your treatmentplugging a curling iron inOnce hot and up your assyou'll confess everyones sinsNext I'll sever your organplace it in your bloody mouthThen stick you in a crateon a Freight Train headed southeak© 2001
