Hey Osama
you been watching the news?
See what's happening 
to your Al Quaida and Taliban crews
They're all movin on up
like the Jeffersons to the East Side
On a U.S airplane
for an eight thousand mile ride
We're de-lousing them
making them wear mittens
Sedating the bastards
so they're gentle as kittens
We're shaving their beards
and their shit filled heads
Bringing them to Cuba
to be questioned by the Feds
They have their very own "hood"
Guantanamo Bay is where they'll be
In cages with tarps for roofs
you will join them,wait and see
Come one and come all
you all belong in one spot
So we can line you all up
then you can all be shot
The Taliban,Al Quaida and you
have made some uncomfortable beds
Your arms up your ass to the elbow
just so you can scratch your heads
That's your future Osama
you too will have a shaved head and face
You maggot like pieces of shit
were never part of the "human race"
