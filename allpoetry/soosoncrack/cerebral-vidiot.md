It happened long ago
about thirty some years
I met my true love
a "black box" with "rabbit ears"

We were meant for each other
both heavy into pretend
And our romance flourished
on each other we'd depend

Early each morning
as the Sun would just peek through
All bright eyed and bushy tailed
time for "Captain Kangaroo"

Bugs Bunny and Daffy
Elmer Fudd and Yosemite Sam
The Road runner and The Coyote
"Wylie" the sacrificial lamb

And all of the "Cop" shows
man there were quite a few
Dragnet and Baretta
and "Car 54 Where Are You"

Then everyone got "soapy"
even late at night
It was "Dallas" and "Dynasty"
people would screw and fight

Yeah,we've been through a lot
and seen quite a few things
But the best part of all?
We don't need Wedding rings
