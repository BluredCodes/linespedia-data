title: "'Soaring Eagles'"

Some Eagles and a "Witchy Woman"
decided they'd "Chug All Night"
A "Nightingale" said "Take the Devil"
a "Peaceful,Easy Feeling" in sight
Moved on to "Bitter Creek"
awoke to a "Tequila Sunrise"
"Out of Control" on a "Saturday Night"
yet a "Desperado's" still wise
Went cruising in an "Ol 55"
"James Dean" was "Already Gone"
"Midnight Flyers" were "On the Border"
have a "Good Day in Hell" and move on
The "Journey of a Sorcerer"
allowed "Lyin Eyes" to see sights
We'd always "Take it to the Limit"
the "Hollywood Waltz" on "One of These Nights"
Felt like the "New Kid in Town"
"Life in the Fast Lane" no more than sport
A "Victim of Love" "Wasted Time"
"Hotel California" was the "Last Resort"
"The Greeks Don't Want no Freaks"
and "I Can't Tell You Why"
Don't wear "Those Shoes" on a "Long Run"
the "King of Hollywood's" my guy
There's no "Heartache Tonight"
still in that fast lane
Sure glad that Hell froze over
and that "Soaring Eagles" remain
