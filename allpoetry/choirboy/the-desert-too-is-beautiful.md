the desert, too, is beautiful

			(for Andy)



and you’ve got thorns

and prickly things

under your flowers,

i know.



but wandering across

endless, dry sands

i cannot but hope that

each mirage

yields you anyway.



crushing, blazing, blistering heat

eats me alive,

desiccating me

like drying blooms

left on the shifting floor.



and still i quest

the way

that gives to you…

cut you open, drink

you up,

live,

      live,

	live.



for the desert, too, is beautiful

if it doesn’t kill you.
