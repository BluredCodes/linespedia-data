season of sorrows



somewhere deep

(inside me)

a rain swirls

out of a wondrous light

baby blue at its

beginnings…



it brings tears

(mine)

to the surface

even as its beauty

creates

naturally…



and now i find

in backward glances

that i want those

long gone, who

moved me both much

and little…



May is the time

when the rain begins,

and that watering of my soul

drowns me, too.
