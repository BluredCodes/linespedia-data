newfound.

(a poem of Seattle)



shelter takes form

in a rainy dismal city

adorned, indeed fringed

with –only rainbows



and, long journey completed

I take hope

take sustenance

in home

not even yet seen



here Hill and Shore

are as much mine

as was all before



i own here

i am here

and in this nowplace

called to be



--only

freed.
