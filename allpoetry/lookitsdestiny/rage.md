The sharpened blade,
My untouched skin.
The incredible rage,
I am in.

I feel it tear,
The bloody cut.
I showed no fear,
I wanted to, but...

The large gash,
It stained the knife.
Just one slash,
To take my life.

The blood is dripping,
I feel numb.
The blade I'm gripping,
Helps me realize I was dumb.

I wanted to die,
I used the knife.
They say you fly,
