~*~*~*~*~*~

He knows too much,
makes the forbidden
touch
 ~ Hot ~
swaying in his decision
to stay afar

 ~ Fantasy ~

finds its way in
he lays her violet eyes
Down
 ~ Urgently ~
places her book
on the shelf
closes his eyes
 ~ Inside ~
he knows too much
to turn away
he is intimately

 ~ Aware ~

of her breaths
says her name 
repeatedly
in his head
 ~ Echoes ~ 
in his injured heart,
magnetized with need,
heart knows not enough
pleads to be relieved of
   *
 * Lust *
     *
to give freely to itself
love's golden gifts,
his heart 

 ~ Grieves ~

for the 
could-have-been lost

 > Forbidden <

because he knows too much.
