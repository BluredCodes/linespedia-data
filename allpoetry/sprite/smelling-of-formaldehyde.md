~~~****~~~****~~~

Invisible

Expressionless walls look at me
no personality
crashed inside
smelling of formaldehyde

Blank spaces

Van Gogh faces' solemn gazes
plead to be in other places
severed mail bleeding
smelling of formaldehyde

Irony laces

Butterfly nets and poppies
Degas summer playing races
spreading wings snared by gleaming eyes
smelling of formaldehyde

Fateful ending

Plastic unions all on paper
darkened cloud sending showers, 
heart flames' long ago sigh
smelling of formaldehyde.FORMALDEHYDE is EMBALMING FLUID. (For dead things.)
