Through  each glossy pane
cognate snow capped mountains gleam
Chinese porcelain

Early winter's dusk
a muttering of mallards
lost to river mist.The porcelain is as near as I can get to
Ming - as close as my dining room hutch
(unfortunately not Ming).  For dusk I had
to throw in the mallards.
