So gently borne on winds of spring
the morning stir of feathered things.
In grasslands round a sleepy town,
by rock bound shores and shallows,
a breathless, cooing soothing sound -
a muttering of mallards,
as day awakes and dawn abounds
with symphonies and ballads.
