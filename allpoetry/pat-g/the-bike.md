"Fie" they say to circular
"everything is cyclical"
So, here I lie… spoke released
ochre rimmed, jagged edged.
A worn muddle of rust, blue and green.
Fallen from once plump breathing care
in ravels of black rubber - staining hands
and fingertips.
Rock tattered, a former inner self of
seeping, seeping air.
