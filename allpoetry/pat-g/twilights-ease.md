So comes the soothing dusk to summer's day
Just past the fiery brush of sun's display
We seek the early evening's cooling ease
the gentle temperate hush of twilight's breeze.
Beyond the heated rush of work-a-day
such nourishment that loosens knots away.
We murmur words once whispered on spring winds
He clings to me; and I, I cling to him.Bo and I have been married 32 years.  We've
weathered all those years together and now
past spring and at summer's end we settle into
autumn.
