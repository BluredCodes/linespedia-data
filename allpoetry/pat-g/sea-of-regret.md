Here at last an angry sea
where the crashing breakers’ roar
fills all voids that came before
Here at last an angry sea.

Flights of words that soar and blend
into spray and sea again
Flights of tears that flow and streak
salty crystals down my cheek.

Grains of sand from rock and tide
fill the hollows left behind
Grains of sand my love and I
could not hold and could not bind.
Here alone in shifting sand
where the seaweed is entwined
building castles in my mind…

building castles in my mind.

I’ll hold back the setting sun,
grip the twilight from behind
til the shadows all are one
In whose darkness I might find
Just one final tear that lasts
wets the future, mourns the past.
Salty crystals left behind
on the weathered cheek of  time.

Midnight winds will find their voice.
stir the dune grass and move on
down the narrow path of choice
of regrets I’ve stumbled on…
