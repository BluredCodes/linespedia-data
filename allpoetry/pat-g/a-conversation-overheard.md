That he has left is not the worst to say
for in those hours of grief that came to be
I cried not o’er his loss, but o’er the way
he tossed aside the very best of me.
With casual disdain he cast off all
that was to come or did so come before.
All memories of love that I recall
All precious moments of our life and more.
All this he wrought with just 5 simple words
that paid the cost of what he thought was due
