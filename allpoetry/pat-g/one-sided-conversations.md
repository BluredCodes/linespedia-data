title: "One sided conversations"

There is in noisy silence
an accounting of each word;
mid hum and drum of syllables,
such scant emotions heard.
Each tattered and profaned
as innocence abraded.
The ebb and flow of intellect,
the pulse of love degraded.
So,
Speak to me in silence 
of how things used to be
unblinded by derision
when "one" and "one" was "we".
