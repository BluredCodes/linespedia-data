title: "Asphalt Tears"

Morning clouds, too gray, forlorn
     Shrug away their shattered tears.
     Grief pooling on the asphalt.
     Turned and tumbled, twisted leaves,
     Earthy gold and blood red tears.
     Grief mounding in the backyard.
     Passage of sweet summer wind.
     Lost crystal shades of autumn.
     Past the memories of green
