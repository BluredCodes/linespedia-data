Should traverse once succeed 
would soft luster wait me there 
so akin to oyster's gleam 
as that tiny breath of air 
that stirs those fluted notes 
cross the strings of Aeolean 
to the sleep of David's dreamInspired by a verse written by a poet by the unlikely name
of Stumpy:  His verse goes as follows:
And should traverse succeed 
would luster be awaiting 
akin' an Oyster's special gleaning 
or upon fluted notes 
of an Aeolean harp 
be carried into forgetfulness.
