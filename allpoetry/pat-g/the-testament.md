The ceaseless flashing of a traffic light
Re-echoing again off shattered glass.
In strident strobes that splinter back the night
Reflecting on a time that did not last.
That did not last long past the memory
Of well lit rooms and warmth sent spilling forth.
A beacon's left, now lost to reverie,
A package sits unopened on the porch
