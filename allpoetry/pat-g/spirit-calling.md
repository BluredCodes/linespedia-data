title: "Spirit Calling"

From the screams of things primeval 
     on the walls of darkened caves 
     in old tombs of stone and mortar 
     through to hist'ry's written page - 
     Spirit totems ghostly fading 
     change their shape, they're yet the same. 
     .... 
     Search the shades of virgin timber 
     dive to depths of ocean graves 
     they are carved in rotting cedar 
     they still gleam neath glacial waves. 
     Symbols of a "spirit calling". 
     Change their shape, they're yet the same. 
     .... 
     Grassy plains of floating umber. 
     shifting dunes of sun baked grains. 
     mounded hillocks still cached under. 
     steep'ng slopes where lava rains. 
     You'll unearth them - hear them calling- 
     Diverse shapes translate the same. 
     .. 
     "Come protect the most deserving. 
     Bless the race that calls your name."
