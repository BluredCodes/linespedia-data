first day of winter
sunshine and icicles
solstice drips away.

diamond chips of ice
worn and sparkling branches
winter is engaged.

nine to ten
early morning
snow plow noise

Silhouettes of white
line the playground path
angels passed this way.I cheated on 2 and 4 - they're old ones.
