Hovered o'er a silver mountain
lost autumn moon of frosted gold
spills it's pale light on my valley
as shadowed wings of night unfold.                     
Last hushed moments of aged twilight
spread a peace across this land
as newly minted liquid moonlight
cascades its coin upon the sand.
Petal shades of lingering sunlight
blush the sky as day retires
weaving music mid the pine trees.
