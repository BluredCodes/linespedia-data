Spring:

This unsettled day
when all that is for certain
is the weight of rain.


Summer:

One fence post
two crows
elbowing

Fall:

Picked before their time
not tempered by the first frost
just more sour grapes

Leaf by leaf
it passes
this bright fall

Winter:

Chaos in the eaves
three stellar jays discuss their
claim to one last seed

Telegraphed today
in mounds upon my doorstep
spring has been delayedSee Craig - I can even be long winded with
haikus.
