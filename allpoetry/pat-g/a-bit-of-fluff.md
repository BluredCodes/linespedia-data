title: "A bit of fluff"

This piece of fluff 
(it's belly lint)
records your way - 
It's true!
Some soft, some tough
each different tint
replays your day for you.
Yes, everyone 
should contemplate
give belly sand its due
Each mother's son, 
each head of state
should glance down and review.
And if you find 
your peace of mind's
described in shades of blue
the remedy's 
so clear to me
(and hopefully to you).
One subtle hint
just take that lint,
remove it from world view,
unravel it 
and start to knit
it into something new.
Reshape your fate
don't hesitate
till morning sets its dew.
Each extra night
prolongs the fight
and doubles troubles too.
Unlike this song
don't take too long
knitting many into fewThis was originally called:  Homework from the
navel cavity.
