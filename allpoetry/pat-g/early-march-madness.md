title: "Early March Madness"

Spring is the random 
wanderer of May;
yet, here in March 
a gypsy pulse is stirred
to seek a snow melt path
where primrose lay,
to hear the first new 
callings of a bird.
Transfixed a mellow
morning, umber dry,
in greening minutes
of a warmer day
where sun reflected
dust or snowflakes fly...
the innocence of winter
turns away.
