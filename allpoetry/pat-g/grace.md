Grace, it is the final blessing,
freely given, unsuspecting.
Men will say He is all knowing
yet will question His bestowing
when they open books of learning
seeking solace for their yearning
they'll intrepret their own meaning
to the lessons long time given. 
They must strive in their conversion
to adhere to their own version.
Pious people bent on trying
can forget that in all dying...
Grace, it is the final blessing
freely given, unsuspecting.Written after a household visit on my doorstep by two
people who were rigid in their beliefs.
