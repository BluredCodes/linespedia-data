Too many friends with bated breath 
have answered a battle cry 
Too many friends have stared down death 
and shuddered when others died..... 
     ------ 
Out of those graceless years of blood and wine, 
Too much of which we tasted on our own, 
We numbed our souls and cluttered up our minds 
while feasting on cold mead and whitened bone. 
Out of the ceaseless rocking of our souls 
between the cradle and the coming grave 
We live a life that never will be whole 
and gladly bear the weight of those who gave. 
Out from the valley of the noise and death 
mid endless echoes of our battle cry 
We hunger for the peace of one calm breath 
    ------ 
But, too many friends still die.
Too many friends still die.
