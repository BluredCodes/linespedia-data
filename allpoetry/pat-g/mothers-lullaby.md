title: "Mother's Lullaby"

Flow gently, flow gently wide river of time
soothe rapids and eddie to cradle what's mine
Spin slowly, spin slowly you spatial blue earth
weave dreams overflowing to capture her worth.
Glow silver, glow silver oh moon in night sky
beam cool paths with fireflies to light her way by
Dawn brightly, dawn brightly new mornings to come
bring dewdrops and nectar to sip with the sun.
Draw closer, draw closer Archangel of Might
guard her from dangers tha we cannot fight.
