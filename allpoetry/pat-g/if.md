If I could
but travel backwards,
fall through time
yet stay in place
In that flight
of time and fancy
would I change
my course of fate.
Could I choose
my blissful mem’ries
basing life
on only those.
Would I lose
those dreadful moments
that decree
how life now flows.
If I elect
to mold my his’try
could I do
as well as fate.
Would my soul
twist new additions
back to where
old evils wait.
Tell me if
the life I’m given
could affect
how others live.
Is it best
to deal with horror
and project
this love I give.
So that others
caught and tender
can believe
that joy awaits
just beyond
the price of terror
in the arms
of Mercy’s mate.Written a few years ago for a friend in crisis
