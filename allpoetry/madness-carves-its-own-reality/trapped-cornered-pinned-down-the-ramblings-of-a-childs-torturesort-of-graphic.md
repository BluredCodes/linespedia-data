You had me trapped beneath Your Unbarably Heavey 
Broad Frame of smelly Fat.
You used all strength to hold my Non-Stop 
kicking and screaming as I felt 
shame from deep within.
You shoved that filthy, selfish, and deadly diseased
sex-organ right straight up the decency of my
Bleeding Innocence.
You made me scream, as a Little One can for Mercy,
all the while No One in hearing distance understood
my Cries.

You gashed my aura with such a Heated Sexual Hatered
that I could No Longer make a sound, or move an inch.
All I could do was let the blood pour out as my
pain ran down with it in streams of Plenty, raging
right thru the center of the entirety of my
intriverted, UnEnduring Fear.

You litterally caused all physical Pain to cease
within a fraction of an instant.
You would not put an end to that Torturous Prison
engulfing my existance within feelings way beyond
Horror throughout all boundries 
of my Terrorized empty shell of a body.

I was Pinned down under Your Out-Of-Control
scumm.

Why did You have to Murder me that day??
