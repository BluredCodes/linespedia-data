*~*~ Winter's Glimmering Lace.....

---->> Fall upon my hair with Your cleansing Spirit.

---->> Fill my pores up with Your cooled Moisture.

---->> Douse me in an Amazing source of replenishing Nurishment.

---->> Lay, ever so still, beneath my eagerly wandering feet as I venture out upon Your astonishing "Fractals" that do refresh my mind in such an Awe-Striking fashion.

---->> Feel my LOVE for You grow with every Breath of mist I exhale into this Wilderness LandScape that surrounds Your Breath-Taking Livelihood.

---->> Watch mine eyes Light-Up and Sparkle within that Brilliance of extremed iced essence You bare.

---->> Notice those colours that reflect upon Your shimmer as the Sun rises to greet Your GraceFull fate of watering this Earth's WonderLand,  ....All for Nature's intent of growth throughout this Season's Majestic MasterPiece.

---->> Then as the Warmth of the Sun desolves right thru to Your destiny, all Humans, with this OutStanding EyeFull of Your Blessing, will touch and taste the Awesomely DeLightFull Miracles of Your Forever Changing World.

---->> You've Truelly scalloped my Soul with Pure Mystical InSight.  For,   ....Within each and every icey thin PieceFull Molecule, I can feel that TenderNess from Your Loving Touch.

---->> You soothingly layer every object within Your path's Life-Force, and I'll remember You, filling every boundry within my Spirited Being with an OutBurst of Excitement, all of my Life-Time.
