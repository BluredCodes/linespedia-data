Misty shades of pastel silhouettes filling Our Honor Savoured LOVE....

Whole-Heartedly Embracing each other's Spirited LifeForce....

Continuously melting within EnLightenment through the Brilliance that softly shimmers upon the gentle flowing motions of silver wings that purposely colours the Freedoms of a Dove....

Your TenderNess soothingly touches a supple WayWard Radiance throughout Great Desires within me "Extending", with ...Oh ...So ...Eager Needs, towards Your Genuine replenishing Aura of ~Never Ending Compassion~.

These Tantilizing dispositions of Your flavoured Passions roll, ever so fluently, across my absorbant Taste-Buds within such Delicacy of Our United Kindred Spirit's Nurishment.

....And with this HeartFelt awareness of the SlowDance all about Our Soul's intertwining Elegance, which shows quite the sensations of such Bonded SpiritedNess, We float with ease, hovering above this painted canvass of textured, Eternal "Unconditional LOVE",  ....soaking up that Gracious view within Our LifeGiving World.
