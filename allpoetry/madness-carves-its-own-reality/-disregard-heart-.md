How could I ever feel amungst this Earth's Population, my Friend, when all You do is toss my Heart as if it were yesterday's trash that looks as if it were to be obviously cluttering up Your LIFE??

You eagerly throw-up my name so willingly,  ...as I was Your terminal Illness, ...forever more festering within Your bosom.

How could I feel, at all, though I'm worth at least the Earth's scumm below Your feet??  For me to have that Nobility is only apart of "Your" Living NightMares.

I send You my UnDieing LOVE....
I surround You with tight squeezes to Embrace Your Soul....
I show You Faith in my ability to colour Your World with the Sun's Warmth.

With each syllable that I try to Lovingly soothe into Your Heart's path,  ....You desperately Spatt Back-Out at me.  For, that's all I'm ever Worth within Your Precious LIFE.

Descending from each steep step I can feel my knees buckle,  ....As I know that the UnStable foundation crippling my Soul becomes Your Ever-Waking sewer.

On a Non-Stop devistating day, within my Atmosphere, You becon towards the deep felt Abyss to deminish the remaining rubble admidst my feet.

You have Engulfed my Earth with Your burning desires of Torture, becuzz, I form what You seem to Hate the Utt-Most....

---->> "A blissful World, that I own, filled with "Unconditional LOVE".
