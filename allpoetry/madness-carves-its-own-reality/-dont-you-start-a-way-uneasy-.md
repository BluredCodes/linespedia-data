>> AuquaLung My Friend....
--->> Don't You Start A Way UnEasy!!
*******************************************

What's worse than smelling Your 
own Flesh burning away?? ~ ~

---->> Hearing this Lost, Desperate
LIFE of my Missing Brother's....

...Unfold within my Sunken Heart;
...All the while feeling each
shredded tear, bleeding from deep
within my Spirit, burn without end.

---->> Singing each Humanly Haunted verse....

...Knowing that a PainStaken Reality
Hides throughout each BloodY Creviss
within the NightMares that silently Lurk
all about my DayDreams.

---->> Clenching My Sullen Jaw Thoroughly....

...As I can Hear the Thunder from my Own
Wounded voice speak each word from this 
TruthFull Song, while seeing the Blood 
Flow from my Soul.

---->> As If I Were To Be In The Back-Ground's PainFull Shadows....

...Listening with that Agonizing Ear.
