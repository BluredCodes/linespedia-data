As my out of BreathNess sinks right into my laboring HeartBeat I endearingly graze slight warm breezes into the smooth arches of your tender ear, softly blowing earnest whispers of Your incredible name.  This WholeNess within my entity of stimulating, profound OneNess, bonded conclusively tight to You, is simultaneously being SetFree with such delicate ease, to be that quite PowerFull Liberty, feeling all sensations of Our complete LOVE grow even more Mighty Force as that magnificent splendor of Awe within Our tantilizing ties that Bind.

That opalesent glow radiating from Your mesmerizing glance is what tightly embraced the StillNess of my Heart's Captured Rapture, and does grasp that attentive shock of denial over Your Amazement as being my Angel In Shining Armor.

Such Rapturous Wonder has caught my shattered stomach as I feel as though the very pit of my sturnum enfolds quite the mass of Butterflies fluttering in such vulnerable motions, as they frolic over an Awe-Striking Sight of uncultivated fields mounding with undisturbed HoneySuckle.

The stimulus within astounding BondedNess, as We strikingly touch with waves upon waves of shivers flowing thru Our spines, is now fully OverWhelmed within a GreatNess of Arousal.

To brush up against Your unresistable suculant lips, as their savoury temptations melt with Passions of begging such Intertwined ContinuedNess between Our Soul's every seductive Want and Need, will NO Doubtedly bring on sensuating obsessions for One Another's World.

The earnestly provocative scent of Your pores actively roused quite the emotion, pleading from the start throughout the core of my Livelihood, as I thoroughly enjoy Your Aromic Haven for every depth my lungs could posibly create with that quick ease of excitement being contained for EnLightenment.

Just to lay by Your side is Treasured, unimaginably, to actually form such entire understandings throughout this Human World of Petty Mortality.  I could feel that astonishing rush through every thundering motion within Your pulsating chest of FirmNess,  ....so appeasingly Muscular,  ....such appealing softness within Strength,  ....intensely attractive smooth BrownNess, as though I could taste Your SweetNess of undeniable MilkChocolate that decadently flowed upon Your skin's Oh, So, Captivating Shine.  Thru Our mind of whimsical Journeys We can taste the Precious DeLight of Our savoured endearing Soul's release throughout that element quickly absorbing into Our one of a kind Kindred-SpiritedNess.

Our Souls that Bind such Breath-Taking, felt, Beauty give such the noticably pleasurable Journey within this LifeForce of shared Purity in GreatNess,

....FullFilling a saturating Essence of Genuine LOVE.

This Innocence of such a Rapture covers Pure Delicacy, strongly pulsating thru Our Desires.
