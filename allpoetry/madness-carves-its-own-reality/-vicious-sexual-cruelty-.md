You pores reak of flesh so full of nastiness,  ....completely radiating with OverWhelming SelfishNess that is Purely had by Your blinded Shame.

A vile scent,  ...Oh,  ...So,  ...Bursting with Cruel intent.
This disgusting aroma consumes my very innocence, as it stabbs violently, piercing into the Natural Decency of that ever so Deserving Nature of mine.

OOOHHHH,  ...What a sick, evil, and twisted game You play!

You polute my very Oxygen with the mere essence of Your roaming fingers.  How I desperately wished to bite each finger off at both dirty hands.

I Purely denounce Your membership into my Family-Ties.  How could You actually be apart of a Family that Truelly LOVES if You do Honestly condone Your incestual bonds that secretly tears apart a family through "Vicious Sexual Cruelty"??

Violent dry heaves do scower my throat as my heated anger pursues Your Own destiny of LawFull Justice.

I wish my intentions were just for merely venting feelings from an old past.  But, that would be, to me, like a Horrific Mistake to stuff these ever so PainFull memories far into my Subconscious, until Surfacing in a most Dysfunctional way, causing Havoc within me once more.
I now need to seek my Inner Child's Justice, and watch the changes take place within my World that deserves PeaceFullNess.

My Soul is shattered with Still PainFull memories, that is Bluntly inflicting my scarred mind with Dirty-Felt ShameFullNess.
And "Shame" is what DisRespects my Present OutLook with disgusting darkness.

I will do what I can to put an end to these kinds of WrongFull doings from abusers.

"Vicious Sexual Cruelty Will Win No More"...

...In my World.

Mary-Ellen
