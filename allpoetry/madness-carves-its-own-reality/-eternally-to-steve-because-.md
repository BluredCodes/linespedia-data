Because....

...My days are now filled with You throughout ever boundry of my Soul.

Because....

...You have the ability to set my Spirit Free with each and every expression that is Naturally radiating from the Brilliance of Your Aura.

Because....

...I see You at my side every day in an Amazingly Tender way.

Because....

...You and I were destined to be Litteral Soul-Mates forever more.

Because....

...There will never be a most Majestic LOVE like the one that You and I can't help but to share.

Because....

...I see in You "The Most Precious Best Friend" my Spirited Being has ever been Embraced by throughout the entirety of my LIFE's Journey.

Because....

...You are You,  ...I am I,  ...And, just that does make us Astonishingly Compatable to be with each other throughout all of Eternity.

Because....

...To know You is to know that Heaven does exist within our Souls.  If we could just let it in we could actually feel the Reality of LOVE's Miracle.

---->> Mary-Ellen
