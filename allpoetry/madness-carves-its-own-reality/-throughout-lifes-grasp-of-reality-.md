There's an Amazement throughout LIFE's grasp of Reality
That tinkles and twinkles within the Awe-Striking LifeSpan of my adventurous Journey.

Every shimmering partical, that represents the Purity of
LOVE we are Naturally to have, awaits my acceptance of 
its very meaning to Brilliantly touch this Spirited Being, of mine, with soothing warmth.

As the very Beams of Radiance extend towards my Livelihood
I am gifted with a Passion for LIFE itself, and for the
Humanity of Earth's ManKind.

As the never-ending horizon of the Magnificent Ocean 
Hypnotizes my mind with its astonishing Wonder....

...So shall the Awesome meaning of new LIFE forever
colour my World's phenomenal view of Agape's True
Creations.

A RainBow of Ethnic proportions fill every boundry
of this Earth's atmosphere with an OutStanding capability
to unite us all as "One"
....with the strength that LOVE gives us Graciously.

I respond to everything throughout my surroundings with
inner expressiveness to reflect my every need and desire.

I will hold onto this LIFE's grasp of a LOVED filled
Reality, forever more.  For,  ...I can honestly feel the Hypnotic Beauty it bestows within, and all about, the very things I absorb in thru my Soul.

Felt Thoroughly Within:
