"The weight of Your Sweaty Pores Murdered My Child Within"


You stoled my very Inner Child's innocence
with that ShameFull Filth You did cram right
in thru the Heart of what decency I was able
to squander.

Your, ever so large, body seemed disgustingly
heavey as You forced the LIFE from my Livelihood's
existance.

You, NOT Only, thieved away from me my Young Child's
LifeForce, but, as a Reaper of my Soul, You murdered
the LOVE that this Little One was born into.  The
Utter-Most cruelty a victom of Death could bare, as
violent sufferings, is one that lingers on throughout
what all others call LIFE.  Night after Brutal Night
I am slayed by Your crudeness with Flash-Backs of
BloodY-Shame.

Tearing all apart my inner-most Spirited Being had
driven my trust dirrectly head-on into the blinding
walls of such concretely Suicidal NightMares.

You needant strangle the Beauty of LIFE that was
handed to me as a well deserved Birth-Right, for,
....Just by knowing You existed in my LIFE has
litterally drowned out the future that I had with
my Children, and their Family LIFE with me.

Therefore, I will NOT waste anymore Precious time
with You stabbing deeply into my decency.

....Instead, I think I'll hunt down the very
essence of air that Breaths LIFE into Your veins,
so that ManKind's Journey throughout Your foot-
-steps will be handed that PainFull Noose.  Each
and every Molecule that makes up Your past-
-decaying deeds, Your present bleeding furry
against other Young Ones, and that Murderous
future amungst those Children of Faith throughout
their dangerous adventure with a Purity of
Gullability,  ....Will be sought-out and bluntly
blown away without the MerciFullNess You've
always kept from me, and my Inner-Child's
Reality of an Emotional NightMare.
