Reaching towards the sky with Sullen-HeartedNess....

Feeling consumed by SelfDisgust....

Being ever so deeply sucked into that DarkNess of an Abyss....

Violence echoes throughout my Shattered mind....

Keeping mine eyes on that tall, Hooded, cloaked figure with the rust covered, rounded blade....

Death has many disguises to suit my ever waking needs....

There's a small Light at the end of this Gloomy tunnel....

I can't see it any other way than a Way Out of this World that cuts the air off from my Livelihood....

I needent wait for Pain or Death to take this Emotional Distress far away, for, it has clearly been seen within my Mind's eye, that I will last here on Earth a Life-Time's Worth, No Matter what Suffering has in store for me....

So, I scream from the Highest mountain for that rest from LIFE,  ...and it Clearly Echoes back in strength a Thousand-Fold,  ...."I will remain here throughout this atmosphere with a Mission for the sake of LIFE ItSelf, but, also for so many others within my adventurous Path."

~ ~ And so I go On....

...Reaching for that Sun to Shine it's soothing Warmth throughout my Soul's Universe.
