“One and only”
Wrap your arms around me
Protect me from the cold
All I need is your body to hold
If tomorrow never came
I would not blame the sun
What’s done is done
And you are my one
(Chorus)
My one and only
When I’m with you
Never to feel lonely
Love you like no other
We can need each other
One and only
(End Chorus)
Looking into your eyes
Is a mirror to my soul
I know now, you complete me
And make heart whole
Say you love me once more
I know what I’m living for
(Chorus)
My one and only
When I’m with you
Never to feel lonely
Love you like no other
We can need each other
One and only
(End Chorus)
One and only, Second to none
I love you inside and out
No doubt in my heart
One and only, We will never
Break apart…
