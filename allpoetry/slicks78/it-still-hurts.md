Were broken long ago, I hide it but I think you know
Hurting eachother in innocent ways
Should've seen we had numbered days
Can't say anymore left here crying
On the floor
We can't work it out, I love you
In places I hate and doubt
(Chorus)
It still hurts you know my everything
When I shouldn't be bothering with your type
It still hurts we loved so much 
Now I can't stand your touch
It still hurts

Love is like flying you soar or die trying
Wanted to believe we could make it through
I'm everything and nothing without you
Caught between thinking or feeling
My senses are reeling
We made mistakes
One is all it takes(Chorus)
It still hurts you know my everything
When I shouldn't be bothering with your type
It still hurts we loved so much 
Now I can't stand your touch
It still hurts

It still hurts to know we loved 
With the flow of a water fall
And gave it all
I know your bad for me
You know I'm bad for you
It feels like heaven and burns like hell
To be free, and it still hurts meI wrote this one for a friend, so it's a bit lacking in the emotion I would put in it had I actually experienced what she had.
