---
title: "If I didn't love you"
writer: "Slicks78"

If didn't love you, what would I have?
An empty life, filled with half formed memories?
A destiny to trudge on the same old path
Laugh at my problems, cause it hurts too much to cry
I can't remember the first time, I said 'I love you'
But it doesn't matter anyway, I can't deny
That without you, there would be no I

If I didn't love you, What would I know?
How to numb myself, so feelings don't show?
It's true, I do that anyway
But you see inside, and I can't hide
From the love you give right to my heart
I feel, I could not live, apart from you

If I didn't love you, What would I do?
Waste anyway, until the dusts of today
Fade me into tomorrow?
Be filled with dull grey, and sorrow

It's true, I know now
If I didn't love you, 
My life would mean much less
If I didn't love you, there would be nothing
To remain for, live for, die for
Cause I love, you down to the core
And if you didn't love me
I don't think I would live anymore
And if I didn't love you
I would not, know what to do
