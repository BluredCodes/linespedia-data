“All I Want”
Happy memories never last
My better self faded into the past
Left me with a mold
That won’t hold anything but pain
The tear stains remain
And I’m just looking to be full
When I feel pushed too far, and pulled too close
(Chorus)
All I want, is to mean something
Left with a clean slate for once
All I want, is to be happy in my skin
I search inside, nothing within

Where do I start?
When I can’t even read my own heart
Stuck on what used to be
Feels like no one can see me
I’m just a shadow of the past
Trying to make what’s left
Last till I figure it out
(Chorus)
All I want, is to mean something
Left with a clean slate for once
All I want, is to be happy in my skin
I search inside, nothing within

All I want is to be myself
With a path that’s clear
All I want is to understand
Why painful emotions
Always get the upper hand
All I want is more of what I had
Less of what I got
That’s all I want..Based upon roughly on "Too Little, Too Late" but...not exactly.
