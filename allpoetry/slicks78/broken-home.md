I come from a broken home that remains together
Fill the cracks with glue, so long as you cover it with lies
They will believe everything is true
Screams ring louder from the down the hall
Doors slam and the pictures of us fall
And shatter, scattering my memories of happiness
Tomorrow wounds will superficially heal
And the broken home with pretend laugther is real

Say one wrong thing and you'll snap
You're retorted words are a harsh slap
In the face I've grown so accostom to
Why bother to reframe the memories
That yell out blame and prejodice
Let the glass cut your hands 
Go ahead and bleed for once
And feel the harsh reality of being alone, 
In your hand built, broken home

Cover it all with smooth words
Walk the walk, talk the talk
It's a token, of unspoken pain
Can't see the walls caving in
This is the broken home I have to live in
For now, for tomorrow, for forever
I can never escape, can't break the chains
The blood searing linkage, remains 
About my heart, I'll be strong outside
Inside I'll fall apart, this is the life
In a broken home.
