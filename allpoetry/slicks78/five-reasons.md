It's been five springs, summers, falls, winters gone
It's been five valentines that passed us by
And those who knew about you and I 
Said we wouldn't last, don't even try
I guess it was naive youth, That got us this far
I love you madly, need you badly, that's the truth

It must have been fate
That brought us together
Because you came into my life
Not one second too late
I can't even picture, where I'd be
If there was a me, without the us

Four seasons, Five reasons
Why I need you in my life

You hold me up when I want to fall

I love it when you call me pet names

You're the only one who can bring the sun
Into my heart, when I'm surrounded by dark

When I try to put our love into words, I'm speechless
Cause nothing could explain the emotions inside me

You were a friend first, though it was your love
I really did thirst for

Those are my five reasons
Though there's five thousand more I can think of
But to put it into five words 
I love you more than life...To the one I love.
