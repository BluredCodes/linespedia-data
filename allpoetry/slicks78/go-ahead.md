title: "Go Ahead"
writer: "Slicks78"

You strip me of anything I held true
You scream because I defy what you set as creed
But I always did read between the lines too well
You should know that, you taught me how
But I won't bow down to the religion of yourself
I won't worship you, because you have power over me

Go ahead and take my life away
It's a small price to pay
To know I am not your puppet
Go ahead and tell them your lies
They will know it's all false
When they look into my eyes
Go ahead and dig a grave
Big enough for both of us
I won't be your slave

You can cry a river to drown me in
You cannot break me, my walls will not wear thin
I'm sorry to say, I wanted to be just like you
Once in a day, so far away 
You abused me, now you use me 
Still tell me I've got dues to pay

Go ahead and take your payment
In the blood of my heart
Go ahead and farther distance us
We're already this far apart
I will not worship you as the great one
What was said, is said, what was done is done
So go ahead and hurt me more 
You can fight the battle, 
I will win the warA minor rambling... But sometimes the strongest truth is told through unformed thoughts.
