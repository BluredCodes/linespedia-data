I don't know what he saw in you, my father
Maybe you were a different woman then
But, all I've known is a time bomb
With the tick, tick, will you go off?
The next time I call out... Mom?

Drowning yourself, in new age health
In potions, and flowers
Crystals will make it all go away
You would say...
Here you said, it's two together
Just like the two of us
Hold  you said, touch it you said
So I did, felt it, I did, held it I did
Dropped it, I did, broke it, I did
You screamed, "why did you behave so?"
I took it, Brave as I could..

If it were different, I'd be so careful
Because, I guess I always did believe your words
"Things will be different now"
It was just a piece of rock, but it gave
Such a brutal shock
If it were different, if time could turn back
Would we have everything, that we lack?

We're both so different
Sometimes, words hurt less than actions
Silence, I can be cold as stone
I regret now just leaving it alone
would it have been different?
If I'd just let it go
Could I let you know my problems
And share my triumphs?

I wish it were different
But it's not, I live with what I got
Pushed away, I had no choice to go or stay
I'd give everything to change it
So it would be different, maybe we could love
Or maybe, I'd be somewhere, off above
And you'd have the life you planned
Before I came in, and put a stand to it 
But it's not different, and we are different
I love you, and hate you, for different reasons
It's true..This one is obviously about my mother. It makes sense to me... Don't know if it will to anyone else. Maybe I've had too much cold medication(damn colds!)
