You came to me that night, a look of fear
A pang of fright distant in your eyes
Though we both know, you did it to yourself
"Can I stay with you for awhile?" 
You forced a smile and choked a tear
When it had been more than a year
Since you'd smiled at me, truly

I heard the scream, I knew who started it
Numb, I did not want to be part of it
It was the same as any other night
A yell, a scream, a fight
In room I stayed, loud music played

The door creaked open
I dreaded the first word
Would I speak? No, I would go unheard
So you spoke, and I did not trust a single word
Leave, you told him, leave
It was him you say, him
And shouted, when you knew
I did not believe

Turn the light out, you said
"It burns my eyes, and hurts my head"
When it scares me to be in darkness with you
You're the monster of my nightmares, it's true
Words you spoke, of how it happend
Did you honestly think, I would believe
Such lies? Probably, I am not suprised

Hush, I said hush, it's better if you speak no more
You jumped, you cried, you yelled and asked
"Why must you continue our war?"
Why? I am not the one, I said
Who treats me like dirt, 
Then expects to me comfort your head
"We're too alike you know, it's really starting to show"
You said.. Alike? You think?
Perhaps, your blood runs through my vains
Perhaps your hair, a brown I have
Perhaps your eyes, sea blue, I own
But I am not like you, 
believe what you want I thought..
"Yes" I said instead, look what I got

You screamed, you cried
I tried to ignore, my own feelings
You asked "Do you love me?"
I nodded, said "yes", I lied

I held you that night, despite my own rage
At you, for locking me in your painful cage
I held you that night, so you would think 
We're alright
I held you, I held you
Even though, you hurt me in ways
Deeper than what I let show
I held you, what did I get?
Another anger filled fit
On another day, for I am a toy
For you to play
Still, I held you, I tried
And you killed me again, inside
