“Complete me”
You grow in stages
Have to leave some behind
As your life ages
Most have faded away
But you really did stay
I’ve seen more than my fill
Anything else is over kill
Youthful eyes start to grow
Now I know what I feel
You stuck around when we weren’t
Standing on solid ground
(Chorus)
I was a puzzle with missing pieces 
When it fell away
You were the one to stay
I found myself in your heart
You complete me in every part
(End Chorus)
You were there for the times
In between when there
Wasn’t much to say, or be seen
Lifes a downward climb up a slippery slope
You stayed through thick and thin
Gave me hope
(Chorus)
I was a puzzle with missing pieces 
When it fell away
You were the one to stay
I found myself in your heart
You complete me in every part
(End Chorus)
You set me free
Nothing to hide, or hold back
I hope you can see
What here inside this heart
Seasons came and we watched them go
