Grunt- a sound which has many meanings. Example "Hello, how are you" "*grunt*. Or "What did you do today?" "*Shrug* *Grunt". Also used for "I fucked up?"

Bleh- typcially used in place of a lengthy discription of something I dislike and do not want to take the time to explane. Example "Do you like Britney Spears?" "BLEH!"

Eh- Eh, a sound for which I cannot think of anything else to say. Or I am trying to be Canadian, can't you tell?

Bite me- Phrase used when is one being dissed and I cannot think of a better come back. Such as "You're ugly" "Bite me!". ...I do not mean to phsyically bite me, thank you.

Kickasstardly- a non-sensical word I use for anything that kicks major ass

Dude, or Man- I am trying to be cool, I cannot think of something else to say, Please move on. 

Gah- a word used to show pain, annoyance or anything else I see fit for using.

Cra-hap-happy- I did not just say Crap, I said I was happy asshole!

Coolies- the highest level of cool

Coolness- It's cool, but it's not THAT cool

Cool- so it doesn't suck, but it's rather interesting

Shut up- to be used at any moment, place, or time, such as now. Example, "I will not shut up"Part two, soon to come!
