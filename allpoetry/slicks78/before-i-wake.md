"Before I wake"
I cry dry tears,
Cause there's no more to give
Hardly the strength to live
Hanging on by a thread
That's ready to break
How much more do I have to take?
Before my soul can rest...
Haven't I passed the test?
Can't your eyes see, what this is doing to me
(Chorus)
Now I lay me down to sleep
If I should die before I wake
Hear one last plea
You could have saved me
The nightmares start before I wake
One more hopeless dream, I learn is fake
Before I wake, I'll break

Each breath hurts worse, Than the first
The next could be my last
Why does the past have to hurt so much?
You talk of it, like I try to make you cry
Guess you forgot, you stole my life away
Now I have to pay 

(Chorus)
Now I lay me down to sleep
If I should die before I wake
Hear one last plea
You could have saved me
The nightmares start before I wake
One more hopeless dream, I learn is fake
Before I wake, I'll break

Now I lay me down to sleep
Tears stain my pillow, I finally weep
So fragile you could shatter me
Pick the peices off the floor
Afraid I'll be no more, Before I wake
One last plea, you could have saved me
I'll break before I wake
