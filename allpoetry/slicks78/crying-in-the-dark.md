title: "Crying In The Dark"

Crying in the dark of night
Letting no one see, the fright
Not sure if there is a cure for what ales me
But the memories and thoughts, impale my heart
There's a place inside, I hide so well
As each day passes, it continues to swell

There's an echo in my mind
Of happy days, I long to find
I wonder if they were real
Or just an emotion, I pretended to feel
The tears stay behind my eyes
But they will not smudge my disguise
If you can find the right mark
On the right map, you might find
Me, crying in the dark

Innocense got me no where
Cause no one took the time to care
And pictures are worth 1,000 lies
I've been crying in the dark too long
Why do I not have an ocean?
To drown my fears in 
Tears don't get me very far

I won't let you in
Unless you give me good reason
To trust, Very few even want to
So I continue to build the wall
Around myself, and the tears 
Are locked inside with the fears
So here I am again...
Crying in the dark.
