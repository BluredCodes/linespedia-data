"Angel hidden"
Sometimes I'm scared, Of my own emotions…
So strong don't know what I'd do, If I ever lost you
Sometimes times are tough but we always
Make it through, nothing more to say
To describe the way I feel inside
You see things I've tried to hide
You tell me it's alright, I'll be here
As long as the sun brings light
(Chorus)
Are you an angel hidden?
So I won't know
If I look just right I swear
I see you glow
You're an angel hidden
A heaven send, I love you to no end

I said I'd depend on no one but myself
When I fell apart you rescued my heart
I was deceived by everything I knew
You were what I held true, you believed in me
And your love sets me free
(Chorus)
Are you an angel hidden?
So I won't know
If I look just right I swear
I see you glow
You're an angel hidden


You've been a savior
A true friend, you are
Everything I've been looking for
