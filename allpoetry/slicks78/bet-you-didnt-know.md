"Bet you didn't know"
A picture is worth a thousand lies
You think you know but you can't see
Anything through my eyes
Pain doesn't always show on the outside
It's so easy to hide behind these walls
My tears don't show at all
(Chorus)
This is my life, bet you didn't know
About the bruises on my soul
Bet you didn't know, how long
It's been since I felt whole

My closest enemy is blood to me
And I can't break the ties
No one will see beyond the lies
Just looking for the will to survive
When no one knows they took my
Reason to be alive
(Chorus)
This is my life, bet you didn't know
About the bruises on my soul
Bet you didn't know, how long
It's been since I felt whole

Bet you didn't know
Cause it never showed
You will never know, so don't say you do
What you see isn't always true
Bet you didn't know
