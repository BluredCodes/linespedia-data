“Words can’t”
Nothing could be as wonderful as you
When I looked into your eyes
I knew why the sky was blue
Rain clouds could cover me
In the dark of night, 
But you will always Be my light home
(Chorus)
Can’t find the way to express
My love, cause words can’t say
The way  I feel
Words are less than emotion
Words can’t stop me when I cry
An ocean, but love can

Try as I might, the emotions
Don’t come out right
The sayings are here in my heart
But they won’t come out clear
You can feel it when we touch
That means so much more than 
I love you
(Chorus)
Can’t find the way to express
My love, cause words can’t say
The way  I feel
Words are less than emotion
Words can’t stop me when I cry
An ocean, but love can

They said I would never
Find the one, that’s a job
That won’t get done
Words can’t tell me 
What I feel inside
Words can’t see, the emotion
That hides deep within me
And words can’t tell you
How much I love you
