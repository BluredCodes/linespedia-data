title: "Dream Eaters(On Music Row)"

I was young and innocent, with the oh so moldable personality
Easy to destroy, easy to cheat and steal
Handed the pen and paper, sign my name on the deal
It was all too late by the time the dates ran by
They said they'd make my dreams come true
They said they'd give me the stars, and my name in lights
They are just dream eaters, soul stealers, They took away my rights 
They are the low life of music row
I'd much rather they throw me to the wolves
I would have survived much better

I had so many years under my belt, For the number days I'd lived
Thought I could handle it, thought they were no match for me
Just pay up, name on the dotted line, I'll teach you what you need
It's all good, it's all fine...
Harsh words of criticism, "You're too tall...Not blonde, you don't have breasts like basketballs" 
And they are the ones that have the pictures on the wallls
Of stars, "I made them who they are and I can make you a star"
Believed what I heard, heard what I believed
Forced to grow, now years gone by I know
They are the Dream Eaters on music row

How naive I was, how naive they all were
I gave my money, my soul, my control over my life
For a chance at my name in lights
My words on paper, over the sound weaves
Don't believe false promises, You can do it on your own
Better off alone... 
Nashville, has it's fill of Dream eaters
Soul stealers, smooth talking dealers
Music row, The good,the bad, the ugly
I saw it all, found out the hard way
I'm better off today than I was then

Wouldn't wish the Dream Eaters on anyone
What's done has been done, I'm on my way
Just one more day to conquer, with the right people
Who taught me, not to trust the Dream eaters
Not to believe the Dream eaters, Down on music row
They'll sell your soul to devil, but keep the left overs for themselves
There are still flowers up in UA Tower, keep them dear, keep them near
They will win over those Dream eaters, Dream Eaters on music rowAnyone who has ever had a music dream, don't believe the dream eaters. If it looks too good to be true, it probably is. But take to heart, there are some good people who can help you out there. Just don't learn the way I did.
