“Be Together”
Lonely tears stream down my face
There’s a void in my heart
Only you can replace
Been a long time, since we met
Eye to eye, I hold it back
You won’t see me cry
All you wanna do is fight
All I want for us, is to make it right
(Chorus)
I can’t love you
The way that you are
I can’t hide it
There will always be a scar
We will never, Be together 

Same story, with another name
Too different, too the same
Your words still live under my skin
Love and hate, balance wearing thin
Trying to make it through
Too easy to see, I can’t live with you
(Chorus)
I can’t love you
The way that you are
I can’t hide it
There will always be a scar
We will never, Be together 

You see yourself in my eyes
I see the lies, under your warm disguise
It’s been shattered and scattered
Can’t we be, put together again?
Are you too like me?
Am I too like you?
I won’t believe it’s true
I always knew, we could never
Be together…Those of you that know me well, will probably understand who this about the best. But I won't say who it's about, because it could be about anyone, or anything- faceless, nameless... ..until you give it a name and face.
