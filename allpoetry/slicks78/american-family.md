“American family”
A broken home that’s still in one place
Broke their backs just to say they had some space
Try so hard to make ends meet
Just to keep shoes on their children’s feet
Their babies fight a war against society 
Son and daughter trying to be what they can be
(Chorus)
Meet the American family
Letting there wounds bleed for their country
All corrupt if you let your eyes see past national TV
Meet the American family

Every household has a gun
Children are now fugitives on the run
They are rich, Upper class well done
Mother has a fancy car, like a movie star
While her kids are at home with video games
Mother… Children… Faceless names
They don’t know, the single mother
With no place to go, but she still lets 
Her dreamless hope show
(Chorus)
Meet the American family
Letting there wounds bleed for their country
All corrupt if you let your eyes see past national TV
Meet the American family

It wasn’t all bad some of us are glad for the times we had
Do you feel the scorn, from the child born of poverty 
There’s the American family
Some of them are angry for the remaining pain
They have to feel, the rest of us learn to deal
We are joined together in this unwanted opportunity
We are the American family..I don't really know where this came from, just one of those times when you play and sing and something just comes out. Must say it's better with the music.. ...Seems a bit too much on the "Trendy" side to me. But oh well, here it is.
