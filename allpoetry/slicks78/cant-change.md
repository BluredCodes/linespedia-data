“Can’t change”
You could be so good if you tried a little more
You could have been my one, if you’d not
Done the things you did, closed the door on us
I wanted to see you for what you could be
Not what you are, I guess we were both blind
To see it all for what it was
(Chorus)
You can’t change someone
To fit your ideal mold
I needed you in my arms to hold
I can’t change, the way I see
Can’t change what you will always be

They say don’t mix lovers and friends
The story always ends the same
While I want someone to blame
We both did our fair share of not being there,
Wanted you to be a little less this a lot more that, 
While I’m gonna miss you can’t think
Of anything else to do, But say goodbye
(Chorus)
You can’t change someone
To fit your ideal mold
I needed you in my arms to hold
I can’t change, the way I see
Can’t change what you will always be

I can't change that we weren't right
Still I think of you at night
I was naïve to stay this long
What I felt was strong
You can’t change who you are
I remain here, you are still too far
Out of my reach, now I know
We can’t change each otherTo a good friend... You can't change people no matter how much they mean to you. But you can find someone who's right for you.
