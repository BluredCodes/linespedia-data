In sick they slumber
Death hiding in dark corners to claim them
Calculating it's numbers, who will it take first?
It's not blood, nor hate, nor pain does it thrist
But for souls, taken whole, taken by parts
It does not account for lost,lonely hearts
When loved ones are gone, we hope, they will move on
To heaven, and light, never to see
Another painful night

Death, it hides in the back of your head
Am I the only one, who wonders...
When, will I be dead?
No one knows, when they will be lead
Down the path, where death awaits
At those dark, evil gates

Death, it's black claws like a little sharp blades
Will draw forth your soul
Did you die from, Cancer, Cocaine, Aids?
Or perhaps you were old
Your grip on life, grown sallow and cold
Breathe in, Breathe out
Are shadows on your wall being cast?
It could be death awaiting you
The next breath could be your lastA bit dark I know. But, I just got to thinking while looking at a shadow on my wall(yes a shadow). How quickly life begins, and ends. No one knows when they are going to die, perhaps we have silly numbers and predictions. But does anyone really know when their last breath, is their last?
