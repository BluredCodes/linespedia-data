I was but a child when I met you
I did some stupid stuff, I was young
I guess we all do... 
When every other layer fell away
You were the one thing to stay

You were there for the moments in between
Those awkward phases that seemed then, to never end
But you were still there... It was then I knew
You were more than a friend

Into a woman I've grown, my whole life
I've shown you, Some parts not even
My best friend knew, ever lasting
You were there when the bad set in
Stuck with me, through more than thick
Much less than thin
It was then I knew I truly love you
More than words can express

Sometimes I wonder if you feel more or less
Than I feel, because you are too good to be real
If I lost you, I would lose my heart
Every part that has meaning...
It is now I know, my feelings won't fade

An old woman I want to grow with you
A life I want to share with you
Show you how much I care for you
It is now, as I sit and struggle
For the right words, when words seem
Of little value
I know, I love you, I need you
