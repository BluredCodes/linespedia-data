---
title: "I love you"
writer: "Slicks78"
slug: "i-love-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

You say I do things that,
Make you think, I am infatuated
That I’ll break your heart, when the love fades
But how can I make you see, this is not gonna die
Later on…
(Chorus)
I love you, it’s all I can say
Cause it’s true 
Maybe you think, I’m infatuated
Too young, Too sedated
But I love you, Yes I do

Now I wonder, if you’re the one
That’s fading, maybe sick of waiting
Just to think about it, brings painful tears
Spent too many years
Loving you, Caring about you
Over a misconception
(Chorus)
I love you, it’s all I can say
Cause it’s true 
Maybe you think, I’m infatuated
Too young, Too sedated
But I love you, Yes I do

I love you, it’s all I can say
Cause it’s true
It hurts me to think, you don’t trust
What I say and who I am
How can I make you see?
All the love I for you inside of me
