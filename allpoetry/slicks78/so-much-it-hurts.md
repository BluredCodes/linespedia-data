“So much it hurts”
You say you love me, Every part of my heart
Longs to believe you still feel strongly
But I hear it your voice sometimes
Would you do wrong to me?
You say it’s nothing but helping a friend
What if you feel more than what?
Could it be the end?
(Chorus)
I love you so much it hurts
I live for the moment we touch
It feels I’m losing you
You still say I love you
I love you too, So much it hurts


They say it’s dark before dawn
I’m afraid of losing you
Afriad you'll be gone 
Before the light shines in
I long to trust you, but it hurts to
Think of you in someone else’s arms
And it’s not just a friend if you
Like their pretty face
(Chorus)
I love you so much it hurts
I live for the moment we touch
It feels I’m losing you
You still say I love you
I love you too, So much it hurts


You say I’m taking this well
But I cover emotions you can’t tell
I’m trying so much it hurts
You tell me it’ll be over in awhile
Just to help a friend, it’s trial
I want to believe you
Cause I love you, so much it hurtsIt's amazing how much better you feel when you get emotions out. I wrote this the other night. And I now I know, I was just over reactting.
