“That’s why you love me”
You seem too good to be true
I wonder if I’m good enough for you
But I love it when you hold me tight
Make me sure, it will be alright
When I feel, less than perfect
Like a shadow in the dark
You’re there to tell me
(Chorus)
You need me, you want me
I’m perfect in your eyes
You say I’m beautiful when I cry
You know I can never lie
And that’s why you love me

I’ve been through tough times
I used to hurt inside and when
Tears wet my cheeks like rain
You’re there to comfort my pain
Even though it seems I ask too much
You say it’s all worth it, when we touch
(Chorus)
You need me, you want me
I’m perfect in your eyes
You say I’m beautiful when I cry
You know I can never lie
And that’s why you love me

You say, I’ve always been a friend
That’s why you love me
I’ll always be there, to the bitter end
That’s why you love me
It's been too many years
For a crush, hardly a rush
That's why you love me
And that’s why, That’s why I love you
