---
title: "What I love"
writer: "Slicks78"

Passion is what drives me
I thrive on the chill floor
The music pounding, I need more, more
Never quite good enough, heaven knows
I'm not made for this abuse
Without the movement, my life is of
Very little use
I feel myself bleed, cannot stop
Too much will to succeed
Surface wounds will heal
Naive to see the reality
They push me, push me
Just trying to stay one step ahead
When sooner or later, they'll see me dead
Competition, Repetition, 
The cold eyes, harsh words
When no one else is near
Make their intentions so very clear
I don't belong, who cares?
I'm caught up in this song
My body may break, but it will make me stronger
Anything to make the moment one second longer
This is what I love

The piano plays, a lullaby I sing
Just another note higher, lower
Conquering the challenge
Despite their bitter words
I cannot, will not give up
When the voice hums in my blood
Maybe not the best, trying to keep
A note above the rest
Hopping, Sweating, Wondering
If I'll pass the next test
I will make it, if it kills me
If I don't, I'll fake it
Cause no words otherwise will
Turn me away, From what I love
To my dying day

Another breath means another sweet tune
My fingers must move, faster, faster
A slip up, a miss calculation
Disaster...
A strum, a down beat
Hopping the next chord, will be
As equally sweet
A constant fight, to improve
I will stay till midnight
Finish the song, hope people
Don't think my words wrong
But I never really cared
My music, my movement, my words
Shared, hopefully not invain
Another way to share my love, my life, my pain
Call me a failure, call me a perfectionist
Maybe even insane...
But here it is, naked and true
This is what I do, This is what I love
