“Sneaks up on you”
I loved you in a moment of truth
You were the light in my misspent youth
When I made the wrong choice too many times
You were there to be a guiding voice
I can’t thank you enough
Can’t love you enough
(Chorus)
Love sneaks up on you
Changes shape too many ways
Sneaks up on you when you just
Scrape by, Love sneaks up on you
Can’t deny
(End Chorus)
You made me laugh, When I wanted to cry
I can’t help it, I can’t lie
You pulled me through
When I was sinking fast
You ignored my past
Only look towards the future
(Chorus)
Love sneaks up on you
Changes shape too many ways
Sneaks up on you when you just
Scrape by, Love sneaks up on you
Can’t deny
(End Chorus)
It’s a feeling I’d never known
Grown up and worn out old life
Didn’t know love, didn’t really care
Suddenly, you were there
And it, it sneaks up on you
You held me tight, made it true
