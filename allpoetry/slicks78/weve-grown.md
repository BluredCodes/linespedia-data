“We’ve grown”
Looking back at pictures of yesterday
Just another faded memory
Wish we could go back,
Can almost reach it, 
But it’s too far away
Faces disappear, the three of us
Used to be so near
And when I think of those days
I shed more than one lonely tear
(Chorus)
We’ve grown up
We’ve grown apart
Though try not to think about it
Cause your still in my heart
We’ve grown up, we’ve grown apart

We talk on the phone, I still feel alone
You’re not the boy I knew
The man in you shines through
You write me nightly wasn’t till
Last week, I learned of the love 
For me, that you didn’t speak
We try to stop from changing
Our lives are constantly rearranging
(Chorus)
We’ve grown up
We’ve grown apart
Though try not to think about it
Cause your still in  my heart
We’ve grown up, we’ve grown apart

I don’t know if I like the things I liked yesterday
We try to grasp memories that threaten to fade away
I didn’t mean to cut you from the frame
I feel for our broken friendship, I’m to blame
Time to face it, if we don’t time will replace it
