title: "Mungo"
writer: "squinch"

Do you remember when you were two years old?
Do you remember when you almost 
Drowned the cat?
Remember?
Remember what happened 
To Mungo, the prisoner,
Each time you flushed the loo?
Remember when Mum came upstairs
To investigate
Why you were so quiet,
What you were up to?
Do you remember lifting up the lid,
A screeching spiky ball of soggy fur
Leaped out of the loo and
Shot along the hallway
Howling?
'Mummy I'm potty training Mungo'
You said,
Quite happily,
Yet Mungo was trained,
You weren't.

Mungo didn't come back for days,
But I think he forgave you.
