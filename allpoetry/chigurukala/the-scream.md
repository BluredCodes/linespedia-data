The scream
That flashes in the voice
It’s for morrow’s sunrise
Looses If self-confidence 
Errupts intrepidity
The voice shrieks out
On any direction
A scream of emotion
A turbulent storm of anguish
Convulsion in nerves
Moment after moment an expansion
The troubles in uproar as waves
And turns around
Arrival of red spring as a greeting
Future forecasts
Let the sorrows freeze
Let the desires burn in life passes
Through childhood
Adulthood and oldage
In a body caution 
Scream entwining as a whirl
Time passes as an uproar wave.
