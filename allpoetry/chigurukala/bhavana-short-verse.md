It's a popular telugu short verse.Bhavana is a poetic expression of human nature. It consists of 3 lines and expresses whole poem view.


Pain

To be entangled
Eyes take its bath?
Causing shortness of breath.



Confusion

Roaring waves suspicion
Finds as unknown error
Maybe a troubled man


Mirror

Blooming-
Memories of the past
Is a lesson to future steps?
