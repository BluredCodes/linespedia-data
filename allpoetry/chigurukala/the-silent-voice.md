---
title: "The silent voice"
writer: "chigurukala"

The two lips in their hug
Dialogue is dumb
It does not unable to deliver
The ears are unable to listen
It ‘s in reticence and silence 
May be I’m in arrogance 
May be I’m in mischief with a suspicion
Our teacher may be in perseverance
In my palm of the hand red rose blooms
Bliss in his two eyes
But the silence doesn’t split
Some one’s word
It’s unable to disturb his silence
He doesn’t continue any interlocution
Words may be poured 
Words from his heart are in mute
The lips doesn’t disturb
Eyes of teacher are wet
And the teacher’s mercy word
I’m extremely sorry said in sympathetic manner
In my eye’s a brightening spark.
