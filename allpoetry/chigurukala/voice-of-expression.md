What is poetry? That it describes the subject of an object philosophy.  A spontaneous flow of emotions and it’s feelings of expression.  Every poem has certain theme and structure.  It means in poetry is nothing can be useless in this human nature.

     Every person has certain feelings and thoughts in their heart. But, it’s too hard to express their own views in words. In writing a poem it’s too difficult.  As a poet making a poem have to struggle to express your inner thought.


    Whenever you are writing a poem first we have to choose the subject and to study them in different angle to express in an innovative manner.  As a poet we have to remember the following fundamentals, writing  free - verse in an attractive manner.


     1.theme
     
     2. Structure

     3. Imagery and symbol

    4. Rhythm

    5. Economy of words.


      When ever writing a poem. Simply choosing the subject is not a correct process and the poem has certain motive. We don’t think it as taking a long sentence and making them into pieces and arrange them into order don’t mean it as a free verse.


     In poetry we found certain rhythm. Whenever we read aloud we can found them in an attractive manner.habit of reading poetry is an enjoyment in the poetic shower of words.


     Ralph Waldo Emerson states that “ there was never poet who had not the heart in the right place”.It's a quote to every poet.



 A poet has certain responsibility, when ever writing poem it’s not only inspiration to others and made them to think it over to find them a new world.
