---
title: "Recapture The Dream"
writer: "Jenni"
slug: "recapture-the-dream"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/49028-Recapture-The-Dream-by-Jenni"
---

Looking back at your photo,
Recalling the happy days,
Remembering all the good times,
When love was just a phase.

You were then a jovial kid,
And I was just a child.
Today, we are both grown up,
And life is no longer mild.

Many years have since passed by,
Many changes have taken place.
Yet, I can still recapture the dream,
Each time I look at your handsome face.
