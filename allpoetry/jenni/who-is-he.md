---
title: "Who Is He?"
writer: "Jenni"
slug: "who-is-he"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/51982-Who-Is-He--by-Jenni"
---

He's the first thing I think of when I awake..
He's the last thing on my mind at night.
He's sweeter than ice-cream or chocolate cake,
He's the one who makes my world seem bright.

He's the guy who puts a smile on my face..
And leaves me with a song in my heart.....
He's the one with whom I hope...
I never have to part.

He's the guy who can light up my eyes,
The one who brings me hope,
The guy who never tell me lies,
The one who helps me to cope.

He's the one with whom I can be myself,
And never have to worry
About leaving things on the shelf,
Or having to say...'"I'm sorry".

He's the one with whom I'd share,
The rest of my life on the net.
The one I give daily thanks for,
Since the very first time we met....
