THE HEART NEVER FORGETS

The heart never forgets the one it holds dear,
The heart never forgets the times you were near.
Those moments of anxiety and those of fear,
The words of encouragement and those that
	      showed you care.

The heart never forgets those precious times, 
That meant so much to me.
Or those other times that were not meant to be.
Those treasured memories that kept me going on,
Or your tender love that kept me from doing wrong.
