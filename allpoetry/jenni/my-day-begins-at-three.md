~My Day Begins At Three~



The day begins for me
When my clock strikes three.
That's when I come home to find you
As your day begins anew.....

I'm home from work, I need a break,
The noise at school is more than I could take,
I look forward to spending quiet time with you,
Hoping that you feel the same way too.

I log on as soon as I get the chance,
Seeing you makes my heart want to dance.
We chat for about two hours cool,
Until it's time to take your kids to school.

And now when my night is here,
I come back on and find you there.
It makes my heart so very glad,
You seem to take away all that is sad.

We chat again, we have so much fun,
We stay online till my day is done.
I sleep, then it's work once more for me,
Until my day again begins at three.
