---
title: "Our Fantasy World"
writer: "Jenni"
slug: "our-fantasy-world"
source: "AllPoetry"

Let's meet soon in our world of fantasy,
Where you and I can both be free.
Where skies are always sunny and blue,
And my body is always aching for you.

This new fantasy world of ours
Can be a garden full of flowers,
You'll kiss me gently on the nose
And very tenderly give me a rose.

It can be by the ocean so wide,
Where we can just sit and watch the tide.
Or it can be on a sheet of silk,
Where we can share our honey and milk.

I missed you tonight in Fantasyland,
Wanted to walk with you hand in hand,
And listen to music play all night long,
As we sing our favourite song.

In our world that was heavenly sent,
We can make love to our hearts' content.
While we're there together, you can be mine,
And I can be yours till the end of time.
