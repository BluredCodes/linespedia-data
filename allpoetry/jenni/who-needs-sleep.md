---
title: "Who Needs Sleep"
writer: "Jenni"
slug: "who-needs-sleep"
source: "AllPoetry"

Who needs sleep....
When I can have you,
Who needs sleep....
When our love is true.

Who needs sleep....
When you can be mine.
Who needs sleep....
When on each other, we can dine.

Who needs sleep....
When we're together all night,
Who needs sleep....
When you make me feel so right.

Who needs sleep....
When our poetry fills the air,
Who needs sleep....
When you are always here..

I
DO!!

GOODNIGHT!
