If tomorrow never comes for me..
I want the world to know..
That I have always felt so loved
And I was ready to go.

The love that my parents showed to me,
Could not be compared to any.
My family have all been kind to me.
Good times, I've seen so many.

The man I wanted to marry
Chose me to be his wife.
He has always been good to me,
All through our married life.

Friends I've made throughout the years,
Have brought me endless pleasure.
It is my wish to let them know
Their friendship I will always treasure.

So if tomorrow never comes for me,
Know that I've had the best.
Do not weep, but be happy for me,
For I have been nothing but blessed.
