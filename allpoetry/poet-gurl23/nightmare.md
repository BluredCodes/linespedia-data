I have this dream
What does it mean?
I feel so scared
Does anyone care?

Now I'm running 
Running far away
Far away from what
From what is chasing me

My dark past
Could that be?
Is that what
What's chasing me

I'm still running 
Running tiredly
I think it's catching 
Catching up to me

I pray dear God
Don't let me fall
No ground I've gained
Not ground at all

Is that an alarm 
One I should hear
Could it be over
Am I free from fear

Lying still
Emersed in sweat
Suspended in thought
Lost in the abis

It wasn't real 
Real only as dreams
Safe now I am
