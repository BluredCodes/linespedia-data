How long I have loved you 
You'll really never know
All the time and thought 
That I have spent on you

How long will it take for me to realize
That just because I love you
You might not love me back
Even though I pray it true

How long will this charade go on
I just can't keep it in
All the love for you I have
I have so deep within

How long I have loved you
Maybe one day you will see
Until that day does come
My secret is safe in me
