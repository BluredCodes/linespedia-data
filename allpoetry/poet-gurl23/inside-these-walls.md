title: "Inside these walls"

Inside these walls there's so much to see
Like a tiny house on the outside
A mansion when you walk in

Inside these walls dark secrets loom
Many of which no one sees
For the curtain is always drawn

Inside these walls pain wrenches the heart
Temptations lure the soul
The mind is forced to rationalize

Inside these walls the mask is removed
Vulnerability waits for the moment to strike
Leaving you unimmune to heartache

Inside these walls there's so much to see
Like a tiny house on the outside 
A mansion when you walk in
