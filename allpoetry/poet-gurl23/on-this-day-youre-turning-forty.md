On this day you're turning fourty,
remember age is a stepping stone to which wisdom is gained

Desire is subdued by realizing what's really important,
Family there through thick and thin,
The small kindnesses we usually take for granted,
and peace which tends to be cleared from our time shedule

Wealth in your collection of love increases,
True feelings come out
and relationships have the chance to flourish

On this day you're turning fourty,
Remember age is a stepping stone to which wisdom is gainedI wrote this for my mom on her 40th B-day.
