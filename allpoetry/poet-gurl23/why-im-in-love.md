Our minds and hearts on a one-way track
Our actions simotaneous and nsync
That's how I know I'm in love

Our eyes going from a distant place
To connecting from across the room
Our similar drive to win and suceed
That's how I know I'm in love

When the image of your handsome face
Can not be erased from my mind
When safe and protecting come to mind
I think of your embrace
That's how I know I'm in love

When my mind waunders 
It waunders to you
When your smile melts my heart,
Making my knees untrustworthy
That's how I know I'm in love
