The knife she’s holding in her handIs the one she stabbed in my backYes that’s my blood And the things she’s screamingoh so loudly-those areThe words aimed at my soulI close my eyes really tightAnd pray she’ll be gone when I wake upBut then I realizeI’m not asleepAnd it wasn’t A dreamShe is chasing meThrough my lifeIn my headIn my dreamsIn my mindParading Around With my heart On a stickAnd letting People admire myTearsThe OnesThatRunDownTo My Toes
