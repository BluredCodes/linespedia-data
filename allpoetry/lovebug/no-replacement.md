There never could truly be a replacement  For you  For me either I supposeAs for our friendship it’s strongOthers have comeAnd goneAnd in their absenceWe’ve grown strongerQuarrels here and thereKept us from intoxicatingEach others MindsFew differencesI have blonde hairAnd you brownYour eyes are brownAnd mine blueBlending togetherInterchangeable namesYou cleverly aggressiveAnd me subtly silentIt doesn’t make a difference thoughInseparable from day oneOur outrageous bird brain adventuresSpending six hoursSearching for doughnutsRunning out of gasAnd flashing all of MontrosePlaying green club gamesWith empty soda cansJust for kicksContinuous repetitionOf Backstreet BoysChinese fire drillsAnd almost cutting off my leg3 a.m. doughnut runsand highlighting for that oh-so-unnatural lookcan you believe the things we’ve donefrom 5 hour long preparation periodsjust to get washed awayin the gas station rainflooding our pantsextravagant drinking plansinventory and allour secret stashskinny dipping and fireworkshours of sobbingjust to throw upYUCK!All of this to deliver usWith the strongest friendshipAnyone could ever haveI know there are a lot of 'inside' phrases and events should they be taken out?
