---
title: "Hiding The Invisible"
writer: "LoveBug"
slug: "hiding-the-invisible"
source: "AllPoetry"

A scar that consumes
Eats away at the soul.
The rational aspect 
of covering what hurts
And hiding behind the wall
that seem to support
Blinded by the answers
To the mistakes
 that I have let
Claim my life
Unfaithful to myself 
and the future that 
I want
Lifting myself up
Beyond the presence 
Of your power
