title: "Crimson Dream II"

can you see me in the mirror?
can you?
can it be?
I tried to see you through the mirror
but I only see me

standing here alone
I dream of darkness
the darkness of a dreamer
who can never sleep

the dreams become darker
darker and darker as i--
as i pray my soul to keep

oh figment of my imagination
product of my fascination
why must i continue my persistence
in disproving your existence?

they tell me, in a madman's world i live
and they don't think i can handle it
but to them these words i'll give
for the flame to the candle is already lit

i am a dreamer
who dreams of darkness
the darkness of a dreamer
who can never sleep

look again the mirror
look again at me
look again at truth
and you i will see
