PickeringPassion is a worthy traitWhich we all possess some ofWe only have to use itTo accomplish our desiresIt is an elusive thingTo most on the journey ofThe wandering that’s called lifeBy those who decide they know.It puts you in a mire oftTill you’ve chosen what to do.To enrich this thing we callLife on the narrow channelIt can be used for all thingsWhether true or contraryAll depends on what we craveTo embellish our trophyIt might be worthier usedFor positive endeavorsUpon the trail of our livesTo gain all that’s desired.2000,
