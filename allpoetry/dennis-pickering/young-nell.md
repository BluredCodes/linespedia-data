This was composed on-line in response to her "Wrong Right Love."

© 02/02/2002 Dennis L. Pickering 

Nell, just remember that you're young  
And know also you have begun  
Your young life just is in the sun  
Do not go off just like a gun. 

Because you, Nell deserve much more 
Than some immature at your door 
Who can knock you down on the floor 
And out of your heart love will pour 

That Love will pour right through the grate 
That pouring might cause you to hate 
Those that are on your current plate 
Who are satisfying your fate.
