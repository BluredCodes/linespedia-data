© 11/22/2001 Dennis L. PickeringWinning is just the beginningOf your brief chances for grinningIt can be construed as sinningBy those not gaming in winningIt can be something you have doneWhen you are quick, on the runGetting things you want in the sunYou’re told you can have by your MumBut you do not have to be toldThat you can take things from the mouldAnd bring them into your abodeBecause they can be aproposWinning is getting what you wantBut be careful of what you wantBecause you might get what you wantPlus all things that go with your wantFINIS
