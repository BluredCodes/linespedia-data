©  February 7, 2002, 10:12 PM Dennis L. Pickering

She has got a lot to do
You’ve got to give her a hand 
Though she can make you feel blue.
Mostly fate banks on that band
You carry into the dew.

In the dew is beginning
Projects in your life to win.
Fate starts at the first inning.
Any project you begin
Because fate wants you winning.

I say, “Fate is influenced”
By our attitudes on life.
And fate can be influenced
By what we “think” of our life.
“Fate” needs to be influenced.

What’s that “band” your carrying?
The band is but your being.
Your being that’s harrying
All things that you’ve been seeing.
That you’ve not been burying.

Finis
