© 02/02/2002 Dennis L. Pickering 1:56 AM

You just have “faith” we are told
By the ones who want our gold
If they’d only realize
We have little for their prize.

They take little from many
Although it’s just a penny
Fills their coffers aplenty
When they acquire that penny

But Ben said, “A penny save.”
Is a penny that’s been made
To put in your bankrolls grave
Situation to behave.

As though it does want to grow
And replenish seeds you sow
But it can be apropos 
To increase your spending dough

Don’t know though you want to spend
Cash you have acquired on wend
Save it and around the bend
That cash will grow and append.

All that you might have on hand
Can gamely spend in your land
Acquire things life does demand
Like a fish that you do land

The routine that you enlist
Is the lifestyle you have kiss’t
Isn’t one you’ve caught in jist
And won’t ask you to desist.

But when you have faith in you
You will find a diff’rent shoe
That has a most diff’rent hue
You can wear to get your due

FINIS
