©  3/4, 5/2002 Dennis L. Pickering

There’s only one religion.
You can find within your glove.
Helps you more than a smidgeon
Aids you to get back above.

THAT RELIGION IS LOVE!
 
God is love and is within
Everyone upon this earth.
Is there such a thing as sin?
Loving causes us much mirth!

SIN IS CONTRARY TO LOVE! 

Religion’s about Balance
Harmony, Love in your purse.  
It can help you take a stance
With beliefs that you don't curse!

HARMONY IS ABOUT LOVE!

Programming creates beliefs.
Beliefs create attitudes.
Attitudes create feelings.
Feelings determine actions.
Actions do create results.

PROGRAM YOUR SPIRIT TO LOVE!
