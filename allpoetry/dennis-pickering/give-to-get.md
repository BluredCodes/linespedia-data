© 03/07/2002 Dennis L. Pickering

It’s easy to get the things you desire.
Those things that you want in this existence.
Giving you’ll find causes you to acquire.
Altogether, it takes your persistence.

Giving with abundant proclivity
To those to whom you desire on this earth.
It’s the sure way to bring activity
To connections to which you’ve given birth.

Using the "Giving Art" with diligence
Causes "Getting" much more than you do "Give."
Enthusiasm for the recompense
You’re obligated to leave in your sieve.

The "gifts" you acquire ensue in methods
And in ways of which you seldom conceive.
These "gifts" come from universe’s methods
Approaches it’s hard for you to believe.

The universe only wants to restore. 
Furthermore, increase what  you do convey.
That which you accomplish and don’t abhor.
All things you "Give" to others in your way.

FINIS
