© 1/02/2000 Dennis L. Pickering

We all have something weird
Which we have to explain.
If not but to ourselves
Then to one outside us.

Our weirdness is petty
It rests on who keeps score.
Weird means, “I’m unlike you”
And unlike are we all.

We all seek to attach ourselves
As though that is the thing to do.
Pleasing ourselves is not germane
Pleasing others is our purpose.

How is another overjoyed?
Ti’s easier than you might know,
Give LOVE freely to everyone
As though you acknowledged them.
