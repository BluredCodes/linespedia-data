© 7/04/01 Dennis L. Pickering

To journal is to record
Things in which your not accord
Or is it a welcome chord
You strum upon as the lord
Or the keeper of your ward

Wards are things we keep track of
Which are sent from up above?
Things in which we are in love
And these things can take the shove

But from those who care to do
All the things wanted by who?
It can capture thoughts by you
Of desires you want to woo

It’s better used for accords
Which you’re inline and aboard
The train of life or the hoard
It does count just get aboard

The journal is welcome here
And it’s welcome with your beer
It’ll take more than just a jeer
Because it is most sincere

For most all practitioners
Of the art of journaling
Those who attempt to record
Do gain things that they adore
