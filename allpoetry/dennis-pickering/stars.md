© 11/14/2001 Dennis L. Pickering

Are stars only in the sky?
Are they only what’s on high?
Do we sometimes say bye-bye
Without letting out a cry?

For the good that might have been
Though it might cause us to sin?
Or to relinquish a win
And place it upon our chin.

Where others can see it well
Sometimes they will often tell
Those that they meet of the swell
Things that this star’s done, Oh Hell!
