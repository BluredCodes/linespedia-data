© 6/30/01 Dennis L. Pickering

Save our housing, Section 8
Is our battle cry of late
We must open up the gate
That will satisfy our fate

Of fair-priced housing for all
No matter income great/small
Coalition has the ball
And their progress has been tall

It fights for housing that’s fair
For all people at this fair
If we’d but become aware
Of the others in our chair

Who strive to hold onto home
All they have to call their own
Though not covered with a dome
It’s still the place we call home

Finis
