© 12/29/2001 Dennis L. Pickering

The fairy tale you wanted was
A pleasure to compose because
It left so much room to create
A tale that is involved in fate

The story that you get is this
One you can welcome with a kiss
A kiss allows only what you
Do welcome with the morning dew

Once-upon-a-time there just was
This fairy that enthralled because
Beguiling’s what he knew to do
With every circumstance of you

He/It was looking for some gold
That It could most readily hold
And keep secure within his hold
In it’s kingdom low-and-behold

It found some gold the master had
Was stacked up neatly by the cad
Because in wealth he did abound
And kept it close just like his hound

The master, an unpleasant sort
“Alas,” you say, as you retort
He did do bad things just for sport
And welcomed all that does abort

Losing the gold, he had stored up
And kept within the loving cup
Composed of gold just for the sup
That master had most un-abrupt

The fairies in the land that’s in
Imagination deep within
And certainly can cause a grin
To reside just above your chin

As I said he had found the gold
A most welcome sight to behold
For him to readily enfold
And grasping, causing to be bold

Bold fairies are few, far between
Their residence tends to be lean
’Cause fairies mostly like to hide
In the country where they abide

He just had to obtain that gold
Such a sight for him to behold
Gold it knew and could get a hold
And delight his humble abode

Gold would adorn his meager place
And easily he then can race
To the place he washes his face
And keeps most things he does embrace

To obtain the gold he just must
Sprinkle it with some fairy dust
So, from his pouch he gets his dust
And acquires the thing of his lust

The fairy dust does make it float
Effortlessly into his boat
That circumnavigates the moat
Of the masters castle abode

Fairies can cause their boats to fly
Way up into the clear blue sky
Thus they readily say goodbye
To troubles they create on high

The boat flies but, is loaded down
Fully with that gold fairy’s found
The master formerly had bound
To his ambit that’s in the round

The fairy gets to his abode
It’s not even close to a road
It is placed in a tall, strong tree
Is said by humans to be wee 

Fairy proceeds to get out now
Start adorning his home somehow
Applying gold with some know-how
Fairy proceeds to take a bow.

’Cause gold makes his home look so fine
Fairy has no reason to pine
Because it now looks so divine
Fairy considers gold sublime.

More mischief fairy now must do
Not doing it makes him feel blue
He will attempt to get your shoe
Thusly making you go boo-hoo

When you have to saunter on rocks
Which are within, without the box
Just as is that resourceful fox
That is acting akin the Cox 

That will truly guide you to all
Things that you want upon your ball
Those are only within the hall
Not out of your reach but, just tall

Fairies also like to do good
Things that you can keep on your hood
And in your life just as you should
He does resolve the things he could.

He sees you riding on your ass
He thinks you’re a fine looking lass.
But, he can sometimes be crass
About thoughts that you often grasp 

He circumnavigates your head.
Brings fixations that you don’t dread.
These seven things he can bring.
Unity, Justice, Honesty

Respect, Kindness, Life, also Love
Unity equals up above.
Don’t worship idols just because.
These statements caused my rhyme/
metre’ to get out of wack
And to continue I must get back.
To patterns that I do follow 
Faithfully into the hollow

There, now I think I’m back on track
I must keep off of the sidetrack.
Because I’m speaking of the gnome
That we all need to give a home

In the location we reside
We cannot let the fairies hide.
Seeing as we welcome the ride
Onto which they’ll let us abide.

They sometimes can create a fuss
About which we can freely cuss.
Obscene speech is not on your bus.
Because you’re more proper than us

Back to the fairies I’ll now go
They don’t like to put on a show.
For Tom, Dick, Harry, or for Floe
They like to do things apropos.

They can give you all of their gold.
Which you may think that you can hold
But, maybe the gold that they hold
Is but LOVE, which is colored gold.

This color thing is not a tale.
It is just fact that I’ll avail.
To all of you who’re dark or pale
Seek the LOVE that is in the swale.

The Golden Love waits there for you.
Know it sometimes makes you feel blue.
However, what else are we to do?
We must get on the other shoe.

The Golden Love awaits you there.
It’s often found within your hair.
Hair’s only thoughts that you expend
From your mind when it’s on the wend.

That is all I have to convey
I will now be upon my way.
Just like fairies I will not stay.
Just leave my thoughts for you to weigh.

Finis
