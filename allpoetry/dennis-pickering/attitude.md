© 2001 Dennis L. Pickering

Attitude is our goal in life
We choose between what’s right or wrong
We are sometimes wrong, sometimes right
All depends on who is watching

The things we do to recompense
Ourselves or those not part of us
Are ever-present diversions
From that which we are here to learn

What is that thing we’re here to learn?
Which will align our attitude?
It can only be just one thing
That we are here to discover

That discovery needs to be
To learn to offer all you can
Of the single thing that all crave
Which is LOVE to/from everyone
