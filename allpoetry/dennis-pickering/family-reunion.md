© 7/31/2001 Dennis L. Pickering

The day to leave is almost here
We all will have a welcome cheer
Though attitudes are sometimes sheer
We get along upon the near.

Day that we all share a dinner
Makes us all be on the inner
Side of our lives as the winner
That we show every beginner.

Upon the journey of this life
We must leave home all of our strife
Because strife is not the sole wife
Upon the sharp edge of the knife.

That we all hold in our defense
Even fulfill our recompense
To relatives is given pence
We must all get off the fence.

It is a day that can renew
Ideas we harbor that are blue
And can undoubtedly change hue
And make relative strength anew.
