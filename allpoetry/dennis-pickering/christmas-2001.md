© 12/21/2001 Dennis L. Pickering
For Kathy

Christmas is a giving time
For us to put things in time
Or maybe to make things rhyme
Encounter things so sublime

Sublime things that we can get
Usually don’t make us wet
Upon that most fateful day
We can “GIVE” to those who stay

Within our consciousness
And we will not get duress
Upon our most Godly dress
Or in it ’cause God does bless

FINIS
