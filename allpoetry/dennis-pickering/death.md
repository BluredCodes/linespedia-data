© 2000 Dennis L. Pickering

Death is not real but often comes
As a thief will in the nighttime.
To claim victims to their demise
And to upset all their kin-folk

God only granted life to all
Making everyone just deathless.
How does it feel to know that you're
Immortal as the stones below?

Death is a term professed by men
To acquire what you may possess.
Upon this earthly world of theirs
Within a span of counted years

It never comes to anyone
Whether you want it or you don't.
It is but a misimpression
Given you by your persuasion.
