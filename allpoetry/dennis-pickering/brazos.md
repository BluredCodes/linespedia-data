© 1/04/02 Dennis L. Pickering

Brazos, I wonder if you’re still
Circumnavigating that pill?
That you have entered into still.
That “block” is an unwelcome shill.

That must be let go on its way
If Brazos is to seize the day.
And begin again to record
His most astute and wanted chord.

I hope you’re not feeling a pawn
Because your words are like the dawn
And not unlike the morning sun
That does encourage us to run.

With ideas we’ve engirdled
And consequences we’ve hurtled
Over on our forthright approach
Which do sometimes encroach.

Or maybe you have died and left
Poets without your thoughts so deft
I haven’t seen you on the chat
Although I’ve looked there like the cat

That wishes to go unnoticed
Where it walks on this fine lotus.
Has astuteness just like Brazos
Who does not need to feel morose.

Just because he’s suffered a “block”
Doesn’t mean he’s stuck in that crock.
I know that he will progress out.
That progression does make us shout.

For the joyousness we do know
When he again takes up his bow
To play on that fiddle of life
And help relieve us of some strife.

FINIS
