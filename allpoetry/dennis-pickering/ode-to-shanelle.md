© 2/17/2002 Dennis Pickering

Shanelle, this is what you just have to do
To position yourself out of the goo
This is what will make you feel most un-blue
Shanelle do this everyday just for “YOU”

Everyday look in the mirror and say,
“Shanelle, I forgive myself on this day
I always acquire those things that I want
Those things will never again be so gaunt.”

Everythings given by God, if you will.
Where’s this God religion says is not nill?
God is within you on your windowsill.
You just have to COMMAND to get your will.

This God I speak of is your higher self
You only need to take It/Him off the shelf
To increase your life into that of wealth
Remember wealth sometimes does require stealth

Pray tell, what is this wealth of which I speak?
It’s only things that don’t make you feel bleak.
And Shanelle can acquire upon that creek
That’s drifting by with all things that you seek.

And while you look in the mirror Shanelle
It’s time for you to ring that easy bell.
Remember you only have to tickle
Those glands in your brain, won’t make you fickle.

Remember HAPPINESS is what God likes
Chiefly when He/It sees them riding their bikes
Because He/It knows happiness will exude
And Happiness will no longer elude

Finis
