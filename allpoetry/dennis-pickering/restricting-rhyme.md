© 12/23/2001 Dennis L. PickeringBjorg thinks also that rhyme restrictsMe (from) with musings I believe can fixBut, I conjecture that if lines rhymeReaders concede them more of their time.Which is all that anyone can doYou don’t accomplish them with the dewBut you can keep them within your shoeUse these rhymings to enrich the hue.Of the others in your consciousnessWho often remain upon your breastWhere you can assist them in their questThough you might not get very much rest.What do I mean by “Upon your breast?”I just mean people close to your nestWho’re close to your influence at bestAnd readily listen to your jest. (quest?)Although you may not think it as suchJest can sometimes get to be too muchFor your acquaintances on this crutchWho you can alienate somewhatIn your attempts to get harmonyWith those others who are in your treeWho eat similarly with the beeLuscious honey in the familyFinisBjorg is a lady at a day enrichment program that I attend weekly.
