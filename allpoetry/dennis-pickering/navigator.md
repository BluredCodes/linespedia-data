© 7/02/01 Dennis L. Pickering

The navigator is the one
Who pulls together everyone
On every project that’s begun
The navigator makes it run

Projects need a navigator
To help bring in all the tators
Needed by the regulators
Who bring help to all potatoes

The help that’s brought ’s not over wrought
From any person that is brought
To the local where they have fought
For any desire that’s been caught

The navigator makes it go
Out of the stalemate from below
Forward to the future it blows
That the navigator does know

Which is undoubtedly for good
Navigators keep on the hood
All good ideas that they should
To accomplish all that they could

In spite of what others might think
The navigator rides the brink
Of all ideas that might stink
He can pull them out’s what I think
