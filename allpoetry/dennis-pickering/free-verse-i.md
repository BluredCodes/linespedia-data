© 11/17/2001 Dennis L. Pickering

Free verse writing’s a bunch of sh_t
It’s only prose and takes little wit
Some are nice stories but just can’t git
Reverence of a true poets wit

Since you think you’re a poet let’s have
Something besides “free verse” which is sad
In essence it comes from but a lad
From an immature girl or your Dad

Who may know not how to construct verse
With rhythm and rhyme which isn’t terse
Or travesty, withheld from a curse
Which you all acquire from this terse verse

This “free verse” is only for filling
Up someone’s hard disk, which is chilling
And totally unnecessary
Unless they are but seeking quarry

And if that is the case, which I doubt
It should cause you to certainly shout
“What an incomprehensible lout!”
Although that may be what it’s all ’bout
