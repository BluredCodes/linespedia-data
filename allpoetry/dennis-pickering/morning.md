© 3/17/2002 Dennis L. Pickering

Cockle-Doodle-Doo to you everyone
Is this the occasion to welcome sun?
And also to hit the bricks on the run.
That’s what is expected diligent one.

With diligence we all have to perform.
Not all believe that we must embrace norm.
And retain everything within the form.
That does grasp everyone it does transform.

Morning’s the instance for us to embrace
Conditions encountered in this rat-race.
Which are not often held as commonplace
By opinions our beliefs sometimes grace.

Morning is the time for most to arise
And recommence seeking after that prize.
Touted to be contained in the high-rise.
Has propensity to immortalize.

Morning is the spell we certainly start.
Obsessions most wonderful to our heart
Assets from which we will not step apart
Morning’s a fresh time to add to our cart.

Morning does continue at any Time
Can connote beginnings that are sublime
To those of you willing to do the time
Trigger yourself to be more than a mime.

FINIS
