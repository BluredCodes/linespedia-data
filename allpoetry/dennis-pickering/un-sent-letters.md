© 7/17/01 Dennis L. Pickering

Un-sent letters are a way
To get you out of the fray
Into which you’ve gone astray
Or have others lost their way?

Their way is but lost from you
Just try on another shoe
If it only makes you blue
But it gets you out of goo

Into which you have sunk deep
And for which you want to keep
Away from the things you reap
In your garden oh so deep

These letters are for your joy
They increase your joy, oh boy
And can always boost your ploy
For others in your envoy
