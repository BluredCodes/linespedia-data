© January 20, 2002, 8:40:48 PM, March 10, 2002, 9:20 AM Dennis L. Pickering Sunday, 

Sometimes things are more than they appear,
Tumultuous thoughts are frequently near.
Those unruly thoughts are Sometimes too dear,
Can be let go, don’t let them adhere.

Appearances are Sometimes capricious.
Though they are thought of as delicious.
Those thoughts are Sometimes fictitious.
Obsessions are Sometimes malicious.

Downfalls are malicious obsessions.
We Sometimes keep as our possessions.
 Maliciousness causes depressions.
To those we care for in our sessions.

Sometimes conversely, we get our want.
Even though wants can Sometimes be gaunt.
Sometimes, Sometimes, our lives it does haunt.
Isn’t Sometimes just unruly, daunt?

Sometimes it won’t let us ride our bus.
That bus we ride Sometimes makes us cuss.
Though we should Sometimes maybe discuss
Our options that are ambiguous.

FINIS
