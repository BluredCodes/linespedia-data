© 8/19/2001 Dennis L. Pickering

What is a wife, I but think
Only projects on the brink
And are kept out of the stink
Of garbage is what I think

Your description may differ
Except the word can infer
What we're taught as a young bur
Those teachings may be demur

A wife to me is just but
We want to keep from the rut
Want to keep the door un-shut
Attend feelings in our gut

Which will often tell us right
Circumstances of our plight
That we want all colored white
Which can keep us out of fright

Wives are what we hold dearly!!!

END

This WIFE is an idea or project not necessarily a female person though WIVES need to be held dearly!
