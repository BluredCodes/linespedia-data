© 1/19/2002 Dennis L. Pickering

1.  Cheerfully optimistic, hopeful, or confidant; a sanguine disposition, sanguine expectations

Would it not it be pleasant to be?
A sanguine monkey in the tree
Of this life that you do manage
And exploit to your advantage

Because we need to be sanguine
In our attempts to make life rhyme
Acquire what we think is sublime
Makes possessions in our life shine

Life wants to flow, make you un-blue
For you to engage the right hue
And with flow it can make you do
The correct things to fill your shoe

With all the things that you desire
To acquire that are on this wire
And things you need to set afire
Evolute your life-spell on fire

The tree that’s for sanguinity
Taken into infinity
Is where you need to occupy
Your place within this humble pie

What the hell is a humble pie?
It’s just the place in which you lie
And get the things you want on high
To have before you end and die.

FINIS
