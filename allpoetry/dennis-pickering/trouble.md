© 8/14/2001 Dennis L. Pickering

Please stay out of trouble
That was but my double
Who was in the rubble
Out of the nice bubble

That he had wandered in
That had dislodged his grin
He held not on his chin
He but wanted to win

All of life’s choruses
That he saw at Boris’
Which we all adoreses
Wanted for his choruses

Out of trouble he did stay
And that kept him from the fray
That was just upon his way
Into the life of E-bay
