© 3/10/2002 Dennis L. Pickering

Increase your vocabulary
Get yourself a dictionary.
Cause yourself to be more wary
Of the others in this ferry.

Like Perry who saught to explore.
And unfasten everyone’s door.
 Parallel to him I implore.
You reader to open mind’s door. 

Those people who don’t want to learn
Cannot liberate from that urn.
You do comfortably discern
And occupy just like a churn.

Well people, don’t you want to grow?
Circumnavigate that heave-hoe
And get yourself upon that bow
That waits for you to take the show.

You will comprehend that your boss
Is one who’s seldom at a loss.
Understands words you thought were dross
Can smoothly wrest them from the moss.

I once was told that I should write
For the audience that will light
With frequency upon this site
Has magnanimous oversight.

Oversight’s what I conjecture.
You have here don’t want to lecture.
Anyone in this bright picture
Who is upon this fine fixture.

Vocabulary does increase
Your paycheck now and it does grease
Your pocketbook that’s in your fleece
Or your stash kept in your valise.

Recall get that dictionary?
Just getting won’t boost your query.
Please make it just like that berry
You swore just one, then you terry.

Because if you fail to use it
Its purchase won’t increase your wit
And you’ll be stuck in the same sh_t
Out of which you struggle to git.

FINIS
