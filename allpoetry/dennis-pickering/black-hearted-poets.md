©  2/24/2002 Dennis L. Pickering

Is black the color of disenchantment?
Connotations inside the encampment  
Into which harmful meanderings flee
And fail to augment anyone’s knee.

Black writing is a pessimistic kiss
Of negative outlooks that seem to miss
And have been attracted from the abyss
Below this consciousness that we enlist.

It’s also full of negativity
Seldom gives any positivity
To those attempting to ascend the tree
And enrich their lives with vivacity.

I wonder why black writers illustrate?
Negative attitudes they propagate
Attempt to inflict them upon our gate.
Enlist others in their parade of hate.

Anger, I surmise, is self-fulfilling.
Was located here just for killing
Your happiness which can be most chilling
On decanters of life that you’re filling.

The word “black” here is used as negative
Necessitates things not superlative
The word takes no ulterior motive 
Is not intended to be explosive. 

FINIS
