©  2/22/02 Dennis L. Pickering

We live in an abundant Ever Land
It’s inside, let me take you by the hand
And help escort you to where you can stand
And settle in this self fulfilling land

You just have to nurture development
Of your abilities you thought had went
Left you without their full development
Cogitating that your dreams had been spent

Your brain is but your fantastic machine
You have to accept the knowledge you glean­
The knowledge is there and is not obscene
Your mind’s obsequious, waits for the preen 

Intuition is the key to enrich
Your existence in this slick, muddy ditch
Helps get accomplishments without a hitch
And knows what you thought you’d thrown in the ditch

Gleanable knowledge is easy to find
It just resides there asleep in your mind
An easy way for you to awaken
Is this easy way you’ve been forsaken

The way spoken of is the pendulum
Hope this fact doesn’t make you go “Ho-Hum”
It’s cited as the earliest brake-through
In science keeping us out of the goo

First must establish a distinct answer
Distinct ellipses swinging from dancer
One ellipse for yes the other for no
Answers you will glean to strengthen your show

Allow to happen is what’s essential
Finding answers that are consequential
Intuition is what you activate
Intuition helps you alleviate 

“Demanding” gets naught from your bride recent
Allowing to happen causes ascent
To your subconscious where pendulum went
Assistance for you is on what it’s bent

Finis
