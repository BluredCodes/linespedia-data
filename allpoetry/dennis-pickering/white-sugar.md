© 7/25/01 Dennis L. Pickering

White sugar is claimed by some
Desires that can’t be undone
But these desires only run
Just within the likely pun

White sugar is from cut cane
And adversely gets the brain
And makes one stay on the train
Is difficult to refrain

From the ride you have got on
Only makes you feel a pawn
Or maybe a crazy fawn
That likes to consume the lawn

White sugar, a nasty thing
Only will help you to bring
Your mortal life to the sting
Of the bee that it will bring
