© 6/20/01 Dennis L. Pickering

Do any of us have good sence?
Or do we sit upon the fence?
To satisfy another’s pence
Upon the journey from when whence

We all abide within our own
Consciousness we claim as our home
To satisfy our own desire
Which we want to keep out of mire

This we can do if we but try
Before the ultimate bye-bye
