© 11/13/2001 Dennis L. Pickering

What construes a lonesome fox?
It’s just something from the box?
That you have but constructed
To show your belief anew

Or maybe a fox is just
Something you’ve found on the crust
That you use before you’re bust
To gain the things of your lust

Lust is only your desire
That you wish to set afire
On this earth you call your home
And get it under the dome

The dome that is where you live
It contains things you can give
To all others in your life
They can join in with your wife

Wives are just what we hold dear
Our ideas that can be shear
On the cliff that is your time
Upon this earth, so sublime

Finis
