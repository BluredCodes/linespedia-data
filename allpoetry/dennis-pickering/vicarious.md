© 11/04/01 Dennis L. Pickering

We just cannot be vicarious
It’s something that can’t live within us
If we are to ride in our own bus 
And be put within the mortal rush

To accumulate what others think
Are the things we deserve in our sink
Although they may keep us on the brink
We cannot let them cause us to sink

Into the mass of ordinary
And bring things we don’t want to carry
That can trigger us to be weary
Which can keep us from being merry

We all essentially need that light
Can it always keep us from the fright?
That perchance we will happen to bite
Even though it might be light or night

But that is the only way we grow
We have to contribute to the show
We are starring in, oh don’t you know
We do not have to employ our bow

But when it happens to be employed
To shoot our arrows into the void
Acquire what we want not to avoid
Because we have to fulfill our void

The void that resides within us all
It can be but is not often small
Is taxing to enrich at the ball
Which happens to be held in your hall

The hall that we describe as our life
Metaphysically is our wife
That we have chosen as our duty
To fill it up with lots of booty
