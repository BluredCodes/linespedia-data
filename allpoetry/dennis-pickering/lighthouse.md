© 12/29/2001 Dennis L. Pickering

This lighthouse is only you
The light you shine must be blue
You will dawn another shoe
When you decide you can do!

Keep the ship you ride on course
From the rocks that do endorse
All of the negative force
That’s encountered in the door.

That you open in your life
Which can fill you full of strife
And make you forget your wife
That you’ve worked on all your life.

Wives not of the female kind,
Wives are projects you unwind.
Wives can put you in a bind.
Wife projects enrich your mind.

You, the lighthouse broadcast safe
Things that you don’t wish to strafe
In the ocean in your safe
’Cause them you don’t need to strafe.

Strafing only causes death
To projects that you beget
Though they may cause you to fret
Also they cause you to get

Getting things is why we’re here
Drinking from this cup of cheer
On this cliff that can be sheer
And is always ever near

For us to freely jump from
To rich life we rightly come
And we don’t want to be dumb
Or be underneath a thumb

Of some one who thinks they’re big
But we know they’re just a pig
Cannot aptly dance the jig
Or achieve upon their rig

Finis
