© 9/11/2001 Dennis L Pickering

Praying to god is but a ruse
To withhold people from a muse
That they can get a hold upon
And carry with them till the dawn

Of a new day upon this Earth
If it gets here before the birth
Of a new idea that’s had
Of anyone who might be sad

Praying is a supplication
To get something for duration
Given to you by someone else
Rather than get it for yourself

If you will only understand
You can possess things with a hand
Mostly from others in this land
Not from god that you think is grand
