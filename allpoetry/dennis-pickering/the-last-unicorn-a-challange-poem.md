©  02\16\2002 Dennis L. Pickering

The unicorn, a mystical beast?
Or is it one who has only ceased
Occupying this world that we’ve greased
With Anger and Hate not in the least!

I had seen one once when I did ride
On my Pegasus, we didn’t hide.
And by the rules we did not abide
Because those rules are like cyanide.

They occupy a different realm
Than the insane world that’s on our helm
Unicorns are with the Pegasus
Running freely not riding the bus.

Like an appaloosa they do look
Have a single horn upon their book
Above the eyes with which they do look
At other characters in this book.

Unicorns are just gentle horses
Like the Pegasus I adoreses.
The Anger and Hate is what forces
Unicorns and such out our doorses.

FINIS
