© 1/02/02 Dennis L. Pickering

Pegasus, a mystical beast
That you cannot ride in the least.
Unless you happen to know ’bout
Those divine planes that are about.

He looks like a horse but has wings.
It flies about, does many things.
I have just seen white ones pictured  
Here in this physical picture.

Hercules rode it seems to me
This mystic beast of burden, see?
He surely rode one to the gate
We presently know as the “Straight.”

Pegasus too is beast of Sag.
Take it from me, ’cause that’s my badge.
Though I’ve not seen one on this plain.
I don’t wish for you to refrain.

Of belief in Astrology
Which describes things under your tree.
Whether you believe it or not
It hits right on things you have got.

You can feed one that is so grand.
It eats softly out of your hand.
When you trust, it can take a stand.
Take you on trips that are not bland.

FINIS
