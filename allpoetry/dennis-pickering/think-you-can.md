© 1/2/01 Dennis L. Pickering

How do you get things done?
The only way I know
Is to just complete them
When you admit you can.

In order to admit this
You must do simply one thing.
That one thing is to honor
Your thoughts of the impasse
 
Fulfillment is the ticket
To accomplish your desires
Of everything you may want
Within this present life span
