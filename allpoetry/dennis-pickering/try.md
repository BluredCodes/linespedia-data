© 2000 Dennis L. Pickering

There is no such thing as try
You either do or don't do.
When are you going to learn?
Only do what you will do.

Trying is partly doing
And to partly do's a sham.
Do all projects completely
To accomplish all your tasks

On time is not important
To those who refuse to change
They don't choose to accomplish
Tasks that cause them to advance

For that's what life's all about
To gain advancement in your
Consciousness by learning love
Which is all we're here to gain.

To learn all things completely
Is the task that we've chosen
To do 'till we get it right
Upon our lifetime journey

That completes not when we die
For death is a travesty
Which hasn't been propagated
By all Christianity

To generate the growth of
A religion for riches
Which seems to be the goal of
Each and all religionists
