title: "'Together'"
writer: "pappy"

We were two lonely people
looking for someone to care.
We needed a world with love -
A world without cruel despair.

Then we found each other,
and we were complete.
We forgot the bitterness
while enjoying the sweet.

Our love is special;
It forever seemed right.
You took away the sadness,
and brought in the light.

Now we are happy,
our world is fine, 
You know you will always be
wonderful and divine.
