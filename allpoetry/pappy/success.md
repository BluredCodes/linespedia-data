title: "Success"
writer: "pappy"

Today you wear your cap and gown
you stand so tall and proud.
You make me so happy,
I want to shout out loud.

I want to tell the world
how proud I am of you.
How every day of your life,
you made my dreams come true.

This is your special day,
watching you, tears fill my eyes,
as you get your diploma -
As you say your last goodbyes.

Graduation is a stepping stone,
toward a bright future that will last.
I know you will succeed,
you always have in the past.
