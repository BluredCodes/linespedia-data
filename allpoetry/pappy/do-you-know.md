---
title: "Do You Know?"
writer: "pappy"
slug: "do-you-know"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/10338-Do-You-Know--by-pappy"
---

Do you know what life is likewhen there's no one to care?When you have an empty heartthat no one else can share?Well, I know what that is like,what loneliness is all about.Even when I'm in a crowd,I still feel quite left out.If you've felt that way, too,remember, you're not alone.Love can someday come to you,then you won't be on your own.You'll feel like singing;You'll feel happy all day.And good things surelywill quickly come your way!
