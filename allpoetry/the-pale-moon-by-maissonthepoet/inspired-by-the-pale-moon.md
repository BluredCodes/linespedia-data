i sit here waiting
longing for poetry to inspired by the pale moon
or should it be inspired by the pale moon at all
poetry shouldn't be pale
to associate pale with dead is not poetry
a deadened soul can not write poetry
poetry by a deadened soul is only claining to be
...deadened...
