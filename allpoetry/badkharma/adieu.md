title: "Adieu"
writer: "BadKharma"

Our time together is a faded road sign,
except in my mind...
There it is as brilliant as the morning sun,
as crisp as winter's breath,
as clear as night's sky
and as perfect as you...

The golden light of dawn,
a glimse of you...
A scent of vanilla,
a trace of you...
Any womans laugh,
a specter of you...
the winds gentle touch,
a memory of you...
The taste of night air,
a rememberance of you...

adieu
