---
title: "Silent Wings"
writer: "BadKharma"
slug: "silent-wings"
source: "AllPoetry"

I never heard them coming,

but it was time...



Angry at the world,

filled with spite,

tossing and turning,

all night...



I never heard them coming,

but it was time...



Late for work, 

late for life,

nothing good, 

nothing right...



I never heard them coming,

but it was time...



Worried about money,

searching for love,

never enough time,

hurrying along...



I never heard them coming,

but it was time...



Sped through the light,

saw the truck,

shattering glass,

passing into black...



I never heard them coming,

but it was time...



Red and blue lights,

distant voices,

but all I see,

is the summer sky...



I never heard them coming,

but it was time...



Floating up and away,

I hear their singing,

their gentle touch,

their silent wings.



I never heard them coming,

but it was my time...
