title: "The Clown"
writer: "BadKharma"

Like a puppet danced around on a string,
you controlled my every movement.
Walked me along your twisted plot,
comic, tragic and romantic...

Like a fool I worshipped your every word,
was easily decieved by your lies.
Sacrificed everything in the pursuit
of your every dream...

Like a mime I stood trapped in a box,
beating my fists against the glass.
I searched franticly for release,
as you watched my silent screams...

When the show was over,
you tossed me away.
I fell upon the earth,
like a crumpled ticket stub.
