When I look up in the sky,
is when I realize I have died,
a lonely traveler on the land,
my feet blistered in the sand.

All alone I stand...

No one there to lend me a hand...

Leaving me here to die all alone...

No one there, to take me home...

Over my grave no weeping face,
no tears for my disgrace,
just a line in a song,
just a poet dead and gone.

All alone I stand...

No one there to lend me a hand...

Leaving me here to die all alone...

No one there, to take me home...These are Lyrics to the first song that I wrote with a good friend of mine in college.
