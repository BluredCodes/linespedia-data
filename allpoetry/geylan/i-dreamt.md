---
title: "i dreamt"
writer: "geylan"
slug: "i-dreamt"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I dreamt
 	we sat on the sand
	barefoot hand in hand

I dreamt
	we watched the sun
	frolicking & having fun

I dreamt
	we bathed in the sunlight
	holding each other with delight

I dreamt
	with you by my side
	while she went on with pride

I dreamt
	were we together?
        were you ever?

I dreamt
	the sunset
