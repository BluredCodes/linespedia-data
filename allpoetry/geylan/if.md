---
title: "if"
writer: "geylan"
slug: "if"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/41617-if-by-geylan"
---

If someone had told me
	that I could dream
	and that true it would be
		I would dream

If someone had promised
	that I should know
	and that, that when I was held
		I would know

If someone had made me believe
	that I could regain
	and that my feelings  retrieve
		I would regain

If  someone had said 
	there was a, u
	and that if I prayed
		there would be u

If someone had promised
	that it would be u
and that, that when I was held
I would be with u

If someone had sworn all this
	that I would have
	and that, that all this bliss
		from u I have

I would have denied
	although within I almost believed
	and I would have tried
	and, and u appeared?
