And around…
Around me the dead.
Dead assassins of my mind,
hanging on the walls by pins,
trying to destroy my future –
even the sound, once I loved so,
is killing me.
Nothing reminds me of the past anymore,
as much as my mind itself:
a dim picture in the coldness of time.
