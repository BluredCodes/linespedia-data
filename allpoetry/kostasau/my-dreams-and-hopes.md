title: "My dreams and hopes"

My dreams and hopes,
I push you around in my life,
like the tramp pushes everywhere
a supermarket trolley,
full of goods he collects from the streets -
his whole fortune.
I don’t take my eyes off you,
neither do I shelve you -
even if I pay dearly for you.
