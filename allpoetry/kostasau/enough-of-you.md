---
title: "Enough of you!"
writer: "kostasAu"
slug: "enough-of-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

Oh no!
Enough
of you telling us
that these are birds
flying due south!
They are socks that the wind
snatched from the line:
we know it now-
it took ours too.
