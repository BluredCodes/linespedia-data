---
title: "Sea, my love"
writer: "kostasAu"

source: "AllPoetry"

I dive into her watery body
I reach the sandy bed
Untie the ribbon
And her hair flies loose
Spraying the rocky shore
With a fine salty mist

Next I kiss her on the mouth

Then the lighthouse flashes
And she blushes

As I come to my senses.
