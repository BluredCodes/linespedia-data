---
title: "Second Photograph"

Moon lands
on the shores of your forehead,
warms up her body in your blazing lips,
takes cover in the hollows of your eyes.
...
And, here she is, now,
working against time
stealthily on your face
sculpturing your feature
enriching it with shadows.
