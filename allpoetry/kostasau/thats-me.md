---
title: "That’s me."
writer: "kostasAu"
slug: "thats-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

My body, a bed sheet,
Spread out on the bed of pleasure,
Stained from the excesses
Of my senses.

My soul, a tablecloth,
Spread out on the table of knowledge,
Stained from the banquets
Of my mind.

That’s  me, a frail curtain,
Hung out on the line of life
Weathered by time.
