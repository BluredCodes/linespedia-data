The Poet can't just throw scattered words at random
On a piece of paper.
It takes a strong will to discipline words into an order. 
Solders of a wide variety of physical and cerebral ability
Will not fall into line easily.

When read-in quietly they fall into formations
Unique to every reader.
Each one interpreted to match their own feelings,
Like the inkblots of a psychiatrist.
