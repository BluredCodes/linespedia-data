Standing hereI'll always beThe light to guide you..straight to meI'll be the voice...that sings your soulI'll be the warm embrace..that won't let go~Chorus~And how can I express this love That I feel for youAnd what words can I use to make you know it's trueLook in my eyes and you will seewhat is meant to beYou'll never knowWhat you mean to meIt's so hard to showIt's easier to seeSome say I'm crazyFor feeling this wayNo matter what they tell me My heart will not obey~Chorus Repeats~You're everythingYou are my worldAnd I'd give anything Just to be your girlYou'll never know...How much you mean to me..it's so hard to show..It's easier to see..
