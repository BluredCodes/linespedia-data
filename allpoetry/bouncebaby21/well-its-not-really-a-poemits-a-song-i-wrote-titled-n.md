I used to think life was about
Happiness and true love, even blessings from above
but i was blinded by ideals
that love was really real
until i regained my sight, and realized i wasnt right
because our hearts cant truly feel, no its not really real

~Chorus~
It's just made from inside
Love is only in our minds
Because our hearts cant truly feel
no its not really real

Now i sit and wonder why,am i forever gunna cry
because my hope has just bee crushed
and ive lost all of my trust
and now i see how sad life is
but at least the truth is this...that its not really real

~Chorus Repeats~

Oh the world is so cruel
Where life is just a game
and dreams never come true
and love is just a name
Its just made up from inside
love is only in our minds
cuz our hearts cant truly feel
no its not really real...its not really real

here me when i say..just live your life each day by day
and dont u worry what your heart feels.cuz its not really...real..
