Never knew a wordThat'd send a tremble through my heartUntil the day I heardThen I knew right from the startThe sound was your voiceand it soothed me to the coreYou left me with no choiceI couldn't hold back anymore...~Chorus~Cuz I knew that you were 'him'And that you always would be thereAnd how I'd heed you every whimJust to keep u right where...Baby you should be..and you know thats with me..that's where you're really meant to beWhen you touched my hand fire shot right through my soulI've never known a manWho could make me lose controlI knew from then on outThat in my hearts where u will stayWithout a shadow of a doubtI'll love you more and more each day~Chorus Repeats~
