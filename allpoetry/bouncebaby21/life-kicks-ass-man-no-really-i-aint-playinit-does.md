I sit here and read all these sad and depressin rhymes
and wonder what is happenin in these  crazy times
everyone is sad and unhappy bout somethin
but look at me baby, i aint bothered by nothin

cuz i know just how cool life can be
if everyone will just take the time to see
that u can never be happy if u dont realize
u gotta stop using ur mouth and start openin ur eyes

People always say that times are rough
but things are only hard if u make them tough
so smile at someone who you dont even know
and soon u'll start to see a little bit of happiness show
  
so next time u feel like bitchin bout life
just be thankful u aint Eminem's WIFE
and i know my rap ya'll probably dont understand
but hey i dont need you to tell me my life kicks ass man!!
~Peace Ya'll~ and if ya'll dont feel my rhyme its cool..i still love yaz!!ah i just was spitting out the truth ya'll..so maybe its a weak rhyme..but the point was based on good intentions..
