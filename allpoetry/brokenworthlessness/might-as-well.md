---
title: "Might as Well"
writer: "brokenworthlessness"
slug: "might-as-well"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/33558-Might-as-Well-by-brokenworthlessness"
---

I'm already dying
you might as well kill me.
I'm already bleeding
I might as well be dead.
You are killing me slowly.
Killing me softly.
Just kill me already.
I'm already hated.
You might as well hate me more.
We all know what you want to do to me.
You might as well do it.
You might as well murder me.
I know I deserve it.
You obviously think I do.
Just kill me already.
I'm already dying.
I'm already hurting.
You might as well hurt me more.
