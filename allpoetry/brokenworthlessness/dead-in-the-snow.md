---
title: "Dead in the snow"
writer: "brokenworthlessness"
slug: "dead-in-the-snow"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/19517-Dead-in-the-snow-by-brokenworthlessness"

frozen and dead, lying in the delicate powder of the freshly fallen snow
blanketed with crystal tears and icy crimson blood
these are our hopes
our dreams
that lie dead in the snow
how they died
only god shall know
dead and cold
lost and unknown
the love lies bleeding
dead in the snow
