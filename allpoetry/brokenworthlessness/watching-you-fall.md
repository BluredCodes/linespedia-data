---
title: "Watching you Fall"
writer: "brokenworthlessness"
slug: "watching-you-fall"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/34185-Watching-you-Fall-by-brokenworthlessness"

Crystal tears
stream down your face
running your mascara.
Runing through 
your melting foundation
and washing away your make-up.
I never knew you had feelings.
I always thought you were fake
plastic
spoiled.
But you live
just as I,
you feel pain 
and joy
and hurt.
I always thought I would enjoy
watching you fall.
Watching your mask
shatter on the floor.
I never thought you were real.
With all that money,
with all those things-
I never thought you could be sad...
I thought you were immune
to the pain that I feel
that we all feel.
I guess it just shows 
how ignorant I am
how selfish I've been.
Insted of laughing
as I always thought I would,
I find myself crying
at watching you fall.
