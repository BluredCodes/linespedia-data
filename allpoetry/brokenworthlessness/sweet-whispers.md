I open my eyes,
and your sweet smile stares down at me.
Your hands caress me,
and your lips kiss me.
Sweet whispers of love
float in the air.
As you lie next to me,
under the soft silk sheets
warmed by the heat of your body.
Your fingers set my skin on fire.
Your intense gaze ignites my soul.
Sweet whispers
enfolding me.
Your muscular body
presses against me.
Your arms surround me,
reassure me.
I close my eyes again
and when I awake,
I see your handsome face,
sweet whispers grace my ears once again,
and I smile.
