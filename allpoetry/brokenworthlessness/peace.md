Look inside your heart and soul.
Look at the world around you.
Ignore the brutal fight for control
and know there is something that you can do
to fight for peace.

Without war or pain,
Without bloodshed.
There is nothing to gain
when you are counting the dead
killed in the wars you have forged.

No good can come from death or blood.
No good can come from war,
or from the dying in the mud.
But for some there is nothing more
then the thrill of the kill.

Peace is the only ecape
from the pain that they have caused.
To end the killing, end the rape.
If only they had paused
and thought of peace.
