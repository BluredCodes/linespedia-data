---
title: "Only a Child"

Don't tell me that I'm stupid.
I just might believe you.
You may be correct,
but you don't have the right
to say that to a child.

Don't tell me that I don't know 
anything at all.
I may be a child,
but children know
more than they let on.

Don't try to teach me
to follow your rules.
I will follow my own
thank you very much.
I have a mind of my own.

Don't tell me I'm wrong.
I know what I'm doing.
I know what I'm talking about.
I know what you're trying to do
and I won't let you steal my childhood.

I am only a child.
I still know who I am.
I know what I want
and i don't want you steal my innocence...

Don't call me stupid.
Don't tell me I'm ugly.
Just let me enjoy
the youth I have left.
I am only a child.
