There is so much pain in your eyes.
So much hurt in your smile.
There is comfort in the lies,
so misleading and so vile.
Your tears run red,
crimson as the summer sun,
with the blood that you have shed
for me and everyone.
We do not deserve your heart,
do not deserve your care,
don't deserve to know the part
of you that's true and fair.
Through all your days
you will always have  a friend
whom, forever and always,
will have a hand to lend.
