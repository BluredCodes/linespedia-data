---
title: "Waking Up Next To You"
writer: "brokenworthlessness"
slug: "waking-up-next-to-you"

The sweet aroma of you,
and of burning candles,
surrounds me.
The silky feel of your skin,
and of these silk sheets
I wrap myself in,
set aflame my desire.
I am in paradise
Waking up next to you.
Your strong hands
glide across my skin
as you wake from gentle sleep.
Your lips touch the back of my neck.
I roll to face you,
and your eyes stare eternally into my own.
You tell me that I'm beautiful.
You tell me that I am everything to you.
God, how good it feels to be loved.
You cradle me in your arms,
and hold me close to your heart.
The taste of you,
sweet as honey,
rolls over my tounge.
The sight of you is all that fills my sight.
A gentle smile plays over your angelic face.
My hands run down your chest,
trembling against your skin.
I would give the world
to spend my life
waking up next to you.
