---
title: "Strong Hands"
writer: "brokenworthlessness"

Tangle your fingers in my hair,
Put your strong hands on my skin.
Make me feel
as if the whole world
is just an illusion,
and you,
the only reality.
Wrap me in your arms
and hold me close to you.
Lay my head on your chest,
my head moving as you breathe.
Stroke those strong and soft hands
down my golden hair, 
and over my silky skin.
Lay your hands 
on my waist,
and make me feel
as if I'm flying.
Your strong hands
reassure me,
comfort me.
Never taking without asking.
Never asking for too much.
Your gentle smile
making me feel safe,
loved,
needed.
Your strong hands
making me smile,
making me moan
at the happiness I never knew I could have.
Safe in your arms,
with your strong hands on my skin,
the world cannot touch me.
I am finally happy.
