title: "Glass Hands"

I touch you
and you shatter.
My glass hands 
shatter with you.

You touch me
and I hope 
your hands shatter.
I hope they burn.

I don't know what I did.
I just know
I deserved what you did to me.
I still hope
your glass hands shatter.
From the blood
my blood
that you have spilt.

How could you have done this to me?
Why?
I didn't do anything to you.

I hope your glass hands shatter.
