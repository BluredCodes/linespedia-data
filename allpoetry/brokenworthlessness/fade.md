title: "Fade"

Nothing.

Fading away into the dark void of the world.

Knowing I will always be nothing.

Knowing I will never do enough.

Knowing I will never make a differance.


Falling away...


One by one you all leave me.

My friends are few and far between,

along with the glimpses of hope

that these tired eyes are allowed to see.


Watch me as I fade away into the bleak horizon with which I am faced. 
Watch me as I fade away, and wither without a trace.
Watch me with your forever closed eyes...
Watch as the whole world just flies right by.
Watch me although you don't care.
Watch me as I'm bleeding there.
Watch me through those silent eyes.
Watch me as I slowly die.

Fading away...
