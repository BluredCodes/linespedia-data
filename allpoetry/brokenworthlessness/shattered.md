title: "Shattered"

My heart.
My soul.
Shattered.

Millions of fragments of the glass that is my soul
the thin crystal that is my heart
are lying on the floor.

I fall onto them
as you kill me slowly.
The sharp shards
embed themselves into my back.

Energy.
Hope.
Life.
Escape from my being
As I am slowly
Shattered.
