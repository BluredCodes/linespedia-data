---
title: "Betrayed"
writer: "brokenworthlessness"

---

You tore out my heart
and stepped on it.
You ripped out my soul
and shattered it.
You stabbed me in the back
but at least you had the curtousy
to do it where I couldn't see.
You, on the other hand
stabbed me from the front
and smiled at my scream.
I'm sorry for all I've done.
I don't mean for you to think that way.
I just feel a bit
Betrayed.
