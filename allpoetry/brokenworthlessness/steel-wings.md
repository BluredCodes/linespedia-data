Wrap your steel wings around me.

Hold me close in your embrace.

Hold me close so I can see

The gentle curve of your face.


Oh, angel of pain

Oh, angel of tears

Just stop the rain-

It's lasted four years.


Your steel wings

stab into my soul.

Your gentle voice sings

and I loose control.


Kill me please.

Oh dear angel of pain.

I'm on my knees

It will never be the same.


Just wrap me in your steel wings

and never let me go.
