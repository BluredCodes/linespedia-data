title: "Take This Trembeling hand"

Lie your tired head
on sholders that will not shatter.
Fall into these arms
that will not break apart.
Take this trembeling hand
that will guide and never falter.
It will gently lead the way
and you will not get lost.
Ignore this broken heart, 
and let it lead you home.
Let it keep you safe and warm.
Let it hold you close.
Take this shaking hand
it's held out just for you.
Take this shaking hand,
and this shattered soul too.
This worn and bleeding heart
is beating for your strength.
For your silence,
For your hope,
And for your gentle smile.
