Please
you're killing me
Ever so softly
Please 
you're killing me now.

Please 
just kill me
I'm already dying
Please 
just kill me now.

Please 
you're stopping.
just don't stop yet
Please 
just finish me now.

I 
am falling
into peices
please
 just give me peace.

Please 
just kill me
I'm already dying
Please just kill me now.

Kill me now.
