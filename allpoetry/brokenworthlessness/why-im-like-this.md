title: "Why I'm like this"

I'm not like this because
I've had a hard life.
It's not the best, but I know
my life is heaven
compared to so many others.

I'm not like this beacause
I don't apreaciate what I have
I know 
I have all I could ever want.
I just don't have what I need.

I'm not like this because
I'm selfish.
I know that I am, 
I just know that
I would give it all 
just for you to be happy.

I'm not like this because
I want to hurt you.
I want  you to have 
what you need
and not have to deal with me.

I'm like this because
this is how I think.
This is me.
I am who I am.
I think how I think.

Stop telling me 
it is wrong.

Stop telling me 
I don't deserve
to be sad
about life
about me
about the world.

I am who I am,
I think how I think.
Stop telling me it's wrong.
This is why I am like this.
