F 
L 
Y 
I 
N 
G 

high above the stars 
the sky 

F 
L 
Y 
I 
N 
G 

rising up 
wondering why 

F 
L 
Y 
I 
N 
G 

and as I rise 
I fall to pain. 
I fall into the endless rain. 
I realize 
I cannot 
fly. 

I cannot fly 
I cannot fly
