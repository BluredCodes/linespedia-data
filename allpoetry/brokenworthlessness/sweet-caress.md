---
title: "Sweet Caress"
writer: "brokenworthlessness"
slug: "sweet-caress"
source: "AllPoetry"

Your hand caresses me softly
making my skin tingle
enticing me.
I need you
I wait impaitantly for your kiss
your warm touch
surrounds me
enfolds me.
take me away from this place
like I know you can
for you are the angel of death
