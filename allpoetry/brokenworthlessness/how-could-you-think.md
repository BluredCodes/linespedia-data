title: "How Could You Think..."

How could you think
that I do this to hurt you?

Do you think I want to be this way?

Do you think that I want to be me?

I don't want anything to do with me.

How could you think
that i make all this up?

I don't want to be this way.

I don't want  to think what I think.

You say I make it up.

God,

Can't you just listen to my aplogy?

Can't you just understand that I'm sorry?

I would die just for you to hear me...

just listen...

please...


I just want to say that I'm sorry

That I know I messed up

That I wish I could take it back.

I wish you could just listen...

It's me talking,

not my evil clone

not the one I pretend to be

not anyone

but  me.

So just listen

When I say

I'm sorry.
