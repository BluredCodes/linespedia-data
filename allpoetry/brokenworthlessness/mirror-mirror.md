title: "Mirror Mirror"
writer: "brokenworthlessness"

Mirror mirror
of metalic glass.
Why must you show me
that ugly ass.
Those eyes so empty
of peace or grace.
Why must you show me
this ugly face.
This pale skin
so frail and so bare.
Why must you show me
this ugly blonde hair.
Mirror mirror
on the door
don't show me this uglyness
anymore.
This ugly body
and this ugly soul
are slowly loosing
grip on control.
So kill me now
and kill me quick.
And with your knife,
please don't slip.
This ugly shape 
will cease to be
as you slowly and brutally
murder me.
