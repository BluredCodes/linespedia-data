title: "Greatfull"
writer: "brokenworthlessness"

I miss you.
I love you so much,
But I don't know
what to think.
I am so greatfull 
that you weren't here.
You missed that horrid day
by less than a month.
I am so greatfull 
that you didn't see 
what had happened.
Greatfull 
you didn't see
the pain.
I don't think 
you would've been able to take it.
It would have killed you.
At least you went peacefully.

We were all there.
although you didn't see us.
I said I love you.
Did you hear me?

Thank you 
for fighting so hard.
For staying
because we weren't 
ready for you to leave.

I'm greatfull.
