Jessica,
I just want you to know, that no matter what, I love you.

Sister, Dear
look around.
so many love you.
that is the truth.
ignore the rest.

Sister, Dear
I'm so sorry
you have been so good to me.
yet I haven't treated you
the best.

Sister, Dear
be good to yourself.
you have so much to offer.
you are a great friend
and a selfless sister.

I'm glad that you are my sister.
