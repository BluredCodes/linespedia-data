A new year is comming.

A year of love

New beginings

Friendships

Enemies

Pain.

Yes,
another year of pain.

Another year of seeing you sitting there
in the corner
all alone.
Not being able to help.

Another year of knowing
I am not good enough.
I will never be good enough.

Another year
of pasting on this
fake 
plastic 
smile
again.

Another year of being subordinate
to every living creature.
Everything that breathes
or once took a breath
of this pouluted
stagnant
air.

Just another year...

another lonley year....
