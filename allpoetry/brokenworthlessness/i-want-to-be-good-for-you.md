title: "I Want To Be Good For You"

I want to be good for you.
I would give anything
for you to smile
just because you see my face.
I wish I could make you long for me
the way I long for you.
For you to want me
so bad that it hurts inside,
so that you would know
how I have felt
for the past three years.
I want to make you smile.
Make you laugh.
I want to be in your dreams.
I want to be in your arms.
I don't want to hurt you again.
I want to be good for you.
Your smile,
Your laugh;
I would give it all
just to be the cause of them.
I want to be good for you...
Because you are so good to me...it doesnt rhyme and its not too good, but i just needed to say it.
