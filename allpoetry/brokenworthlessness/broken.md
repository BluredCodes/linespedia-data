You try so hard
to make me in pain.
But most of my pain
is self-inflicted.
You hurt me so much,
You wounded my soul,
and finally you have won.
You have broken me.
I lie here shattered
and broken
and bleeding.
Thank you 
for finally going through with it.
The suspense would have killed me
had you not.
I give up this fighting.
There will be no anger.
Only regret
for what you have done.
What I have done.
I am broken.
