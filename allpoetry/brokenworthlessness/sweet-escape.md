Peace.
Truth.
Love.
Trust.
War.
Lies.
Hate.
Lust.
Death
follows a life
forged in strife.
Sun 
after rain.
Drenching pain.
Rest
from this hurt
deep inside the Earth
when death offers It's sweet escape.It's not very good, but oh well...
