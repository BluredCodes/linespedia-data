Hold me up.
My legs are shaking.
I can bearly breathe.
I'm falling,
to the cold and frozen ground.
My trembeling form
crumples to the floor.
Catch me and hold me up.
Wrap your arms around my waist
and pull me near to you.
Don't let me hit the ground.
Don't let me fall.
I'm tired of falling.
I just want to feel your hand on my skin,
to see your gentle smile.
Hold me up,
I'm falling down again.
Lie me on the bed next to you,
and hold me close.
Slowly I am dying.
My lonliness is killing me.
I want to be happy.
Please let me be happy.
They can't make me happy
in the ways that I need you to.
I need you to hold me.
Hold me up,
and don't let me fall.
Don't let me die.
