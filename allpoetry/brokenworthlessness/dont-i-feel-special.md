---
title: "Don't I feel special"
writer: "brokenworthlessness"
slug: "dont-i-feel-special"

Hello?
can you even see me?
I am the one in the corner
crying.
But you are too preocupied
with everyone else
to see.
to care.

Hello?
can you even hear me?
I am the one on the floor
screaming.
But you are too hatefull of me
to notice.
to give a damn.

Hello?
can you feel me?
I am the one at your feet
tugging at your pant leg.
But you hate me too much
to ask why.
to see.

Now don't I feel special.
here all alone in the dark.
crying on the floor.

Don't I feel special.
being the only one
you fail to see.
