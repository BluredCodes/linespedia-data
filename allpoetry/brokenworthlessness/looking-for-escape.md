the silver blade sears through the pale silk that is my wristscrimson blood cascades onto the frozen groundseeping into the emptinessbroken and lost i fall to my kneeslooking for escape from the painlooking for hopelooking for lovefinding only hatefinding only bloodlost in this painlost in this bloodon my kneeslooking for escape
