title: "It's Alright"

As the sky falls down
And your dreams shatter like glass 
I'll be there to hold up the sky
and glue together your dreams.
Don't worry,
it will be alright.
As your hopes fall apart
And your soul grows weak
I'll peice your hopes back together
and hold you as you fall.
Don't loose hope,
it will be alright.
As your crystal tears fall
And blood falls from your wrists
I'll wipe away your tears
and bandage your bleeding wrists.
Stop your tears,
it will be alright.ok- so it's a little short.....
