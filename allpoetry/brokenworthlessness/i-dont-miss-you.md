---
title: "I don't Miss you"
writer: "brokenworthlessness"
slug: "i-dont-miss-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

I don't miss you.
I don't miss you.
I don't
I swear.

I miss love.
I miss us.
but I don't miss you.

I miss your hand
on mine.
I miss your friendship.
but I don't miss you.

I miss your smile.
I miss your laugh.
but I don't miss you.

I miss the way
you looked at me.
I miss your touch.
but I don't miss you.
