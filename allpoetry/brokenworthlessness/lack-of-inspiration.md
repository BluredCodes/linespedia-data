title: "Lack of inspiration"

I'm sorry
but there is nothing left
how can i write when i cannot feel
how can i feel if i cannot write
i've never been good at anything
never done anything worthwhile
i can't even write anymore
no
nothing left
nothing left but these voices in my head
nothing left but this emptiness inside me
this empty void that i have become
no one that i love enough for this
not for words
nothing that i love enough 
for words
nothing that i feel
for i belive i have lost my mind
i can't find it
because i cannot think
because there is nothing left
