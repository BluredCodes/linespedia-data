---
title: "Invisible eyes"
writer: "brokenworthlessness"

can you
hear me
when I scream

can you
see me 
with your invisible eyes

can you 
hear us
cry for you

can you hear us 
up there
in heaven
if there is a heaven

can you see
how lost
we are
without you

can you see that with your invisible eyes
your rain drop crystal eyes
that no longer stare into mine.

for you are gone.

I hope you can see
with those invisible eyes
how much I love you.
