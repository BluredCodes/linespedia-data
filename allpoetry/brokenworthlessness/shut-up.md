Shut up
stop telling me this
go away
you f-ing voices in my head
stop telling me to die
stop talking
just shut up
leave me alone
i know i am all that you say
but stop
stop saying that  i'm not good enough
can't i at least be good enough for myself
i guess not
not good enough for anyone
i can't think if you don't shut up
i can't think
go away
leave me alone
can't you understand
you're driving me crazy
i'm loosing my mind
and you know it
but still you won't shut up
leave
go away
i want to stop these endless tears
let me be
shut up
