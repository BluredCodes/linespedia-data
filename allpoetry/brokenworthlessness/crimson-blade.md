Crimson blade drags across my skin.
Burning pain.
From within.

Crimson blade-
you are my salvation.

You are strength.

You are love.

You are what I deserve.

My blood falls slowly to the ground.

Because of you.

My crimson blade.

Thank you...

No one loves me...

If they do I don't deserve it.

But I deserve you.

My precious blade.

I deserve the pain you cause.

I don't deserve to be loved.

I don't deserve to have friends.

I just deserve you;

My crimson blade.
