Let me die.

I don't deserve to live...

I never deserved to live.

I don't deserve to die.

I deserve nothing.

I don't deserve my life.

It's too good for me...

GIve it to someone else...

Give it to the one I loved...

His is worse.

I just want to give up.

Just let me die.

I can't take it anymore. 

Stop being selfish,

as I know I have always been,

and just let me die.

Just let me die.
