title: "Only Time Can Tell"

As the grains of sand in the hourglass trickle down
and the second hand on the clock slowly flows from number to number
around and around and around
i think about you
i think about what we used to be
i think about what you ment 
and i regret how i hurt you
i didn't mean to
and only time can tell
what it's done to you
what it's done to me
we don't even love eachother
anymore
and only time can tell
what it's done to our lives
but i know now
that i hurt you
almost as much
as you hurt me
and only time will tell
what it's done
to our lives
to our hearts
and only time will tell
if our wounds will heal
if  the rain will cease
only time will tell
