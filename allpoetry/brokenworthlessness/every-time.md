Every smile.
Every laugh.
Is so precious.
For I know 
One day
You will be gone.
You will die,
And join her in heaven,
If there is such a place.

Every hug.
Every time I say goodbye.
I fear it may be the last.
For I know
You cannot live 
forever.
You will close your eyes,
and never again 
open them in this world.

Every conversation.
Every word I say to you.
Frightens me.
For I know
It may be the last thing
I get to say to you.
You will know perfection
once again,
and finaly be releaved of pain.

I know you are not immortal,
but your soul is...
