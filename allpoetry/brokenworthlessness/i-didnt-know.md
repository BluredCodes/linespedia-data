I'm sorry.

I didn't know that I was hurting you so much...

I know I should have.

But I didn't know.

It's so hard to smile,

even for you.

I have no right to be sad.

Just take a good look at the world,

and you will agree...

I just can't help it.

God, how I wish I could be happy...

What use am I to you

If I am of no use to myself.

Thank you for helping me understand...

Now,

The only thing left is to learn to smile for you...

Learn to not be so selfish...
