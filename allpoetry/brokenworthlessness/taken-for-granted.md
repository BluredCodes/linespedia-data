---
title: "Taken for Granted"
writer: "brokenworthlessness"

I know I'm not much
of a person
a friend.

I know I'm not much
of anything.
I am nothing.

But I can't take
how you criticize 
the little I am.

I can't take
how you hurt me.
and say I have no reason to cry.

But I do.
You may not know now.
And you won't.
You don't deserve to know.

But I do
and I can't take 
how you take the little I am
for granted.
