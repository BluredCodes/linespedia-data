---
title: "Saftey Pin"

source: "AllPoetry"

Turn it in the fire light.
Shove in in my skin.
Feel the pain in the velvet night.
The searing pain from within.
Trading pain for pain.
At the end there is even more.
Watching the blood fall like rain.
Gently falling to the floor.
