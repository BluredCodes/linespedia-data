I know it's selfish 
to want something so self-serving
as comfort.
For wanting your arm around me
your hand on me
your strength suporting me.
Your soft lips
brushing against my cheek,
and your fingers running through my hair.
Just for one moment of comfort.
To lie in your arms,
feel you breathe,
hear your heart beating.
Your silken skin
streached taught over your muscle.
Your eyes
so blue I can see your thoughts...
see your dreams.
I need to find comfort in you...
