Close my eyes to the chill 
of the world and of life.
Bend my mind to the will
of the pain and of the strife.
Close my eyes and count to ten
and hope it goes away.
Sigh, and open them again
to another painfull day.
Close my eyes as tears roll down
needless as they are.
Dripping past my worthless frown
down to my worthless scars.
These needless selfish tears
from an empty, worthless life.
Painfull crystal tears
from imaginary strife.
