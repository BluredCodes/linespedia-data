---
title: "How could i"

oh god how can this be
i am so worthless
so alone
so ungratefull
i just want to die
escape
i never wanted to be like this
i never thought i was special
or maybe i did
so deep in my mind
that i couldn't realize what i have done
lost in my own self pity
stupid
selfish
how could i be so worthless
lost
lost in my  own selfishness
how could i 
how could i
