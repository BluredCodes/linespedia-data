title: "Thunder In My Head"
writer: "brokenworthlessness"

Thump.

Thump.

Thump.

Like the beating of my heart.

Thump.

Thump.

Thump.

Thunder echoing through my head.

Thump.

Thump.

Thump.

The sound is killing me.

Echoing.

Driving me insane.

Thump.

Thump.

Thump.

Just like your voice.

Painfully killing me.

Slowly taking all I have.

Thump.

Thump.

Thump.

Endless thunder in my head
