---
title: "I Promised Myself"
writer: "brokenworthlessness"
slug: "i-promised-myself"

Please don't leave me alone,
I'm tired of this solitude.
I've been alone so long,
I can't take it anymore.
I can't write,
I can't think,
I can't love,
I can't hate.
All I can do
is long for you,
and scream at myself for crying.
I promised myself I wouldn't cry today.
That I wouldn't miss you again.
I promised myself I would take it.
That I would get used to being alone.
But I can't live without you.
I can't live without your smile.
I can't live without your touch.
I can't make it
without you.
I can't smile,
I can't breathe,
I can't sleep,
I can't wake.
All I can do
is long for you,
and scream at myself for crying.
I promised myself I'd get used to this.
That I would learn to stand your crying.
I promised myself I wouldn't cry for you.
That I wouldn't bleed when you cry.
I promised myself that I'd let you go.
That I would let you walk away from me.
I should have learned by now that loving you
is no better for you, than me.
I promised myself everything
that I didn't do
and now it's gone.
