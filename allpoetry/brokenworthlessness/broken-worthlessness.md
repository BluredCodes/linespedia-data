my broken heart exposed and bleeding
lies on the frozen ground
broken as my shattered tears
broken as my tortured soul.
this worthlessness that I am
takes away all control.
knowing that I'm not enough
crying for this god damned world.
through all this pain, it is too tough
to keep hope in this world.
existing in this endless pain
broken in this painfull world
crying in this endless rain.
crying in this rainy world.
In this broken worthlessness
I cry, I scream, I bleed.
this is where I exist,
not getting what I need.
