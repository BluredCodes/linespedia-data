title: "Don't Worry"

Don't worry Grandma,
we're okay.
we miss you
but we're alright.

Don't worry Grandma,
for you have been
the candle 
in the night.

Don't worry Grandma,
don't cry for me
nor for my mom;
please don't cry.

Don't worry Grandma,
I still love you,
but I didn't want
you to die.

Don't worry Grandma,
I need you still
your smilling face
full of light.

Don't worry Grandma,
for you have been
the candle 
in the night.
