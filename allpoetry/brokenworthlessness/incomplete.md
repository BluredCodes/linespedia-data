Incomplete.

I don't know what is gone.
Something is missing.

I don't know what it is.
I just know 
I will never be whole again.

The empty void 
inside my heart
inside my soul
grows with each tear.

I don't know what I am
who I am
or what I will be.

I just know 
what I was...

It wasn't much.
I was
and probably still am
nothing.
Worthless
ugly
stupid
nothing.

I am incomplete.

Soon
there will be nothing left.
