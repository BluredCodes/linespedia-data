---
title: "No Clue"
writer: "brokenworthlessness"

You have no clue who I am.
You don't know what I need
what I want
what I love.
You don't know anything about me.
I can't take what you're doing to me.
I can't take how you treat me
like I'm a pile of dirt.
I wish I could hate you for it.
I just feel sorry for you-
sorry that you need to do this.
sorry that you think the way you do 
about me.
You aren't loosing much,
but if you want me to I will be your friend.
You don't want that.
I know you don't.
I just need for someone to care.
Anyone.
But you wouldn't know anything about caring,
would you?
