I didn't mean for it to be this way.
Not that you cared.
You just went on as normal
as I said goodbye forever.
I still want to be friends
but I know it cannot happen after this.
I meant to do it myself...
they just said it.
They told you in one sentence
what would have taken a mountain words for me to say.
Granted,
I don't know if i would have had the courage to do it.
Just that I should have.
I could have.
I'm sorry.
I didn't mean for it to be this way.To Justin although he will probably never read it.
