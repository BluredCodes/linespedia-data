---
title: "Just Out Of Reach"
writer: "brokenworthlessness"
slug: "just-out-of-reach"

I reach my trembling hand
toward your gentle face.
I want you to hold me.
I want you to trace
the curve of my waist with your hands.

Slowly, so slowly
you grow farther away.
In the back of my mind,
I hope that someday
I will be safe in your arms again.

I long to feel
the caress of your lips,
your hand on mine
and your gentle kiss.
Yet I know I cannot lie in your arms today.

I want you to tangle
your hands in my hair.
You don't love me,
but I know it is fair,
she deserves you more than I.

Oh but what I would give,
to feel your soft hands
travel down my skin.
To feel the warmth of your gentle smile,
and this fire that burns within
and to ease this frigd pain
that eats away my soul.
But for now I know
you are just out of reach,
where I cannot possibly go.
