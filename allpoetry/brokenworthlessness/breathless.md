You took away my hope.
You took away my dreams.
You took away my breath.

For now I have no hope.
No dreams.
I have no breath.

My heart beats slowly.

I try to inhale
but your voice
prevents me from doing so.

Echoing through my shattered heart.
Shaking the peices of my broken soul.

I am breathless.

For without you
I have no life.
I have no death.

I am nothing.

Your soft touch
brings tears to my tired eyes.

My crystal blue eyes 
shatter.

My crimson blood
freezes
as it hits the snow covered ground.

Without you 

I am breathess.
