Crafting with words the image i dream
closing my crystal eyes as frozen tears fall from my face
Talentless
i cry as i fall from grace
the grace i never had
the words so carefully crafted
have only been a lie
just Talentless words
in my Talentless mind
my thoughts
my hopes
formed into poems
are as Talentless as
this mind
all alone
Talentless
no words of magick
no images formed in your mind as you read
this Talentless poem
from this tallentless mind.
