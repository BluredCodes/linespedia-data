I feel as if
this blackened sky
is closing in on me.

It's attacking me.

It's killing me.

I feel as if
all the hate
all your hate
is slicing away 
at my already shattered heart.

I feel as if
this lonliness
is closing in on my soul.
leaving only lonliness

Only pain. 

Only hunger.

I feel as if 
this whole damned world
Is closing in on me.
stealing my hope.

Stealing my dreams.

Stealing my faith.

It's closing in on me.
Drowning me.
Sufficating me.
Killing me.
