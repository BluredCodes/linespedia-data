---
title: "flying"
writer: "brokenworthlessness"
slug: "flying"

flying high
above the sky
as i fall
down to the ground
flying off that cliff of mine
into the pain I've found
flying high above the blood
above the blackened clouds
i slowly fall once again onto the frozen ground
flying high above the loss
the war
but flying comes at a cost
loosing all i live for
screaming
falling
flying high
above the dark 
blackened sky
