---
title: "Savior"
writer: "brokenworthlessness"
slug: "savior"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

You
are my savior.
you 
are the one who holds me close
when I am crying.

You
are my savior.
you
are the one who kisses me goodnight
when it's too dark to see.

You 
are my savior.
you
are the one I love
and the only one who loves me.

You
are my savior.
you
are my future
my escape.

You 
are my savior.
you
are all that is
this world.

You 
are my savior.
You are death.
You are pain.
You are all I know.
You
are my savior.
