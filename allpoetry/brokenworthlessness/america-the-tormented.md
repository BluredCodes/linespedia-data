title: "America the tormented"

America
America
we are loosing all we are
we don't seem to care
that we are at war
we fight for peace
blood for blood
how right can that be?
meaningless blood
meaningless words
meaningless war
they need to know
that there is more
theres more we can do
theres more we can say
there is no reason it had to turn out this way
we don't need war
we don't need blood
it was meaningless war
it was meaningless blood
