title: "In The Corner Crying"

I'm sitting in this lonley corner
crying
rocking back and forth
singing mindless songs to myself.

I'm cleaning the blood off the floor again
scrubbing away at the blood
I have spilt.
My blood.

I'm falling off this cliff
loosing everything
gaining nothing.

I'm wiping these tears
off my dirty face
with these grungy hands
these 
pale
lonley
hands.
