I'm dying.
I don't need your help
but I'm wasting away into oblivion.

I'm dying.
It's been four years
and still I dwindle 
down to nothingness again.

When will I feel right again.
When can I live.

I just want to be happy.

But I can't be.

I'm a stupid

ugly

selfish

horrid

petrifying

revolting

disgusting

nasty

fat

rude

person-
the list goes on.


I will never be anything.

I am nothing.

I am dying.
