---
title: "Why Do You Hate Me?"
writer: "brokenworthlessness"

I know everything bad you say
isn't about me.

I'm selfish, but not selfish enough
to see it that way.

I know that I am stupid.
More than I can imagine.

I'm dumb, dumb enough
to not know why you hate me so.

I loved you, once
when you didn't hate me.

I am ignorant
to think that you ever loved me.

I never know what to say
when I see you glare at me.

I am rude, but not rude enough
to return your glare.

I never deserved you
even as a friend.

I am worthless
even more than I thought.

I don't know why 
you hate me so.

I know I am worthless...

I know I am stupid...

I know I am ugly...

I know I am selfish...

But I don't know why you hate me so much...
