this is the poem of a childthe mind of a child insidethis is the poem of the childthe child that's killing my mindthis is the poem of the girl in my heart screaming that this isn't truesaying that the pain will never partthat I'll never be good enough for youthe child inside that screams at mesaying that i am so losttelling me that this shouldn't betelling me to ignore the costsaying that i should finda way to make it up to youtelling me to be loving and kindtelling me what  to dothis worthless childin my worthless mindthe empty voice in my headtelling me slowlyscreaming so loudtelling me i should be dead.
