---
title: "No one loves me"
writer: "brokenworthlessness"

No one loves me.
No one even reads these goddamned poems.
No one cares what I think.
No one loves me.

I don't even love myself.
How can others love me 
if I don't even love me?

I'm lost.
Lost without love.
I don't even know
If anyone loves me 
at all.

I'm  so confused,
because I don't know
what I want.
I don't know if I want love.
I don't know
if I want someone
to love me.

I remember love.
It hurts.
I don't know
if I will ever feel 
that feeling again.

I miss that.
I miss love.
I don't know 
if love
is what I need.

but I want it so bad
that it hurts inside.

I know you hate me now.
all of you.

But once,
I was loved.
Once,
I loved myself.

no one loves me  anymore.
