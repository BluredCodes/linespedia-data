My skin tingles,
my heart ignites,
my soul sings
with longing.
My eyes sparkle,
my tears fall,
my lips quiver
with longing.
I want your hands on me,
your intense eyes
staring into my own.
I need your arms around me,
your lips upon my skin.
I need you to ignite
this dying flame within.
The fire of longing
burns away
at my weak and shattered soul. 
I need you to hold me.
I need you to take control.
I am yours if you want me
to do whatever you please.
I know you,
and I trust you.
You won't ask too much of me.
You won't take without asking.
You won't take to much from me.
To be held safe in your arms
is to finally be free.
You fill me with such longing,
for the love you have shown.
My heart it fills with longing
to no longer be alone.
