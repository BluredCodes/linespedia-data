---
title: "Touch Me"
writer: "brokenworthlessness"
slug: "touch-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

Push me down onto the bed, 
hold me in your arms.
Touch me with those strong warm hands.
Keep me safe from harm .
Don't let me be alone.
I want your hands everywhere on me,
your lips on my skin.
You don't know how much I need you.
How much I want you...
Please don't leave me alone.
I'm tired of being alone.
I'm tired of being cold.
I'm tired of being empty.
Please just hold me.
I'm frightened,
so scared of being alone.
Just hold me and tell me it's alright,
and someday, maybe someday,
it will be.
Kiss away all the pain,
and kiss away the tears.
Just hold me close
and keep me warm,
and touch me once again...
