It 

t
 r
i
 c
k
 l
e
 s

through my veins.
sending sharp needles
of stinging pain
through all of my body.

Starting at my hip
where the knife
drags across my skin.
already blanketed
with fading scars 
of previous fits
of pain.

Blood of fire.
filling my heart.
setting my soul aflame.

Staring at the knife
covered in flaming blood.
Crimson gold.
Flickering 
in the light.

Blood of fire.
Setting my heart on fire.
Burning my hopes.
My dreams.
My love.
Wait, not my love.
That has already left me.

Blood of fire.
Saving me.
Giving me peace
At last.
