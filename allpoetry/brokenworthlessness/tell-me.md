tell me why things had to be this way
i need to know
tell me please
i'm lost
please tell me where is home
where is truth
please tell me
just let me know who i am
i don't know
tell me 
please
tell me that i'm not alone
because i am
all alone 
tell me
please
where should i go
what should i do
what should i know
what is truth
what is love
what is pain
wait
i know what is pain
but tell me
please
how do i get away
get out of this rain
escape this pain
dry this blood
evaparate these tears
clean off this mud
wash away these years
tell  me please
tell me now
help me please
escape somehow
