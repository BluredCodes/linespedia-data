Stop telling me to wake up
from this ignorance I have come to love.
Just let me sleep.
Stop telling me 
that I can't rest...
It's my only escape-
you must understand that.
You say that I don't try,
but I try so goddamned hard
that it hurts.
I try so hard to resist
the suductive lure
of eternal rest.
Eternal escape.
Just let me sleep,
let me rest,
leave me to myself.
Please don't weep
when I rest,
and leave you to yourself.
Stop telling me to wake up...
let me sleep in my ignorance,
let me sleep in my blindness,
and If you truly care for me,
let me sleep in death.
