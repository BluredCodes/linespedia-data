I close my eyes 
and as the shooting star flies by me
I wish. 
I wish for the times when we were happy.
I wish for the times when you made me smile
instead of making me cry.

I cry too much anyway
so don't worry.

I stare up into that velvet sky
and wish I could rise into it
and fly.
fly away from here.
escape these memories.

I wish for you.
how you used to
make me feel inside
like I really could fly.

I wish so hard
on that falling star
that I could undo
the hate I've done.
just to be loved by someone.

I wish so hard 
and so long
that my head feels
as if it will explode
from all the tears
pouring from my eyes.

I wish they would stop.

I wish the world would be happy.
that's never going to happen
so i cry for the world as well.

I wish for peace.
but there is no peace
in this land of war.
so i cry for the country as well.

I wish for happiness...

I wish for hope...

I wish for the past...

I wish for peace...

I just wish.
