I'm dying...
Deep inside where no one can see,
I'm falling.
The world around me
crumbles,
shattering like glass,
and I can't glue it back together.
It melts and it cries,
and as it dies, I die with it;
I can't even save myself.
What good am I to the world
if I die.
What good am I 
if I cry.
I just can't seem to stay alive,
I can't seem to stop these tears.
Just hold me now
as I die...
Hold me and keep me safe
from myself.
The world dies.
It bleeds,
It screams,
It cries...
and I cry with it.
