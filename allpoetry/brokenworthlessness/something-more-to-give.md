I wish I had something more to give
for all I have done.

I wish I had something more to give.

You deserve more than I could ever give you.

More than I can give all of you.

I need you so much.

I just don't desrve you.

I wish I had something more to give.

I need you.

I need to deserve you,

because I cannot live without you.

I need you.

All of you.

I need your trust.

I need to be happy.

I can't help it.

This selfish need to have friends.

To be loved.

I wish I had something more to give.

Enough to be loved.

Enough to make a differance.

Before I die- 

my only goal

is to make a differance.

I just wish I had something more to give.
