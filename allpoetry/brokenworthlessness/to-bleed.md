Life is composed of many truths...
Love,
Hate,
Fear,
Lust.
But the truth and the strength
in all these things
are the tears
and the pain
and the blood.
To bleed is to know
that you are alive.
To bleed is to know 
you are real.
To cry is to know
that for which you strive.
To cry is to know
that you feel.
To be in pain
is to know the truth:
That all that is true
is pain.
To bleed is to know
that pain is love,
and tears, healing rain.
