---
title: "I Tried"
writer: "brokenworthlessness"
slug: "i-tried"

I tried.
I tried to let you go.
don't you understand?
you hurt me.
you deny it,
but you did.

I am not the only one
who promised forever,
you promised me 
you would always love me
but you hate me.
don't you understand?
that hurts me.

I know that I hurt you
but you can't say
that you didn't hurt me.

I tried to let you go.
I loved you
but now I know
that it was only out of pity
you didn't even love me
do you know how much that hurts?

I'm sorry,
but I'm not going to keep on apologizing.
I know I messed up
but so did you.

I tried.
I tried to be ok.
when you left,
I plastered that
fake
plastic
smile
upon my face.
I painted those
bright
glass
eyes
to cover the endless tears.

I tried to be strong..
I didn't want you to come back.
You wouldn't have anyway.
you don't love me.

I learned so much from you,
from us,
from what we had.
but I didn't know what that was
I never will.

I tried to let you go
but but now I know
it was only out of pity

I tried, 
I tried
