---
title: "For All Of My Friends"
writer: "brokenworthlessness"

You are everything to me.
My laugh.
My tears.
My smile.
You are what makes my life worth living.
You are few,
but mean so much.
Your smiles brighten the room
and my heart.
Your words calm the storm 
inside my shattered soul...
I only wish I could do more.
All of you have been so good to me.

There is the one who understands
everything I feel,
and she is always there to listen
no matter what happens in her life
that is filled with such emptiness.

The one whose smile,
whose tainted innocence is hidden to her own eyes,
and who always puts others before herself.

The one with such passion 
for everything she does,
who makes life more exiting, and was born to write and born to sing.

Then there is he,
with whom I cannot share my feelings.
He always makes me smile,
always makes me laugh,
but I have to admit,
the thought of being with him scares me...
I don't think he likes me back.
There are only a few other than these,
and they know who they are.
I hope they also know 
that they mean everything to me.
You are my life.
You are everything I live for,
and everything I could ever wish to be.
Thank you all for being there for me,
and for catching me as I fall.
You mean the world to me.It is not a fitting tribute for all my friends have done, but for now, it is all I can do
