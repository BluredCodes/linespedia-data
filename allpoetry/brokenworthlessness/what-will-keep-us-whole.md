title: "What Will Keep Us Whole"
writer: "brokenworthlessness"

What is that sound I hear
in the dark.
Whose is that silver tear
in the dark.
Where is that lonley voice 
to accompany my tears.
Can't it help me
to escape my fears.

Where is that warm hand 
on mine.
Whose is the blood that spills 
like mine.
Where are those lonley tears
to accompany my own.
Can't they help me.
I'm all alone.

What is that crimson gleam
in the night.
Whose is that scilent scream 
in the night.
Whose is that drop of blood
that stains my shattered mind.
Why is this world
so nieve and blind.

What are these empty words
in my heart.
Where is that spark of hope
in my heart.
Where is that gentle kiss
that will soothe my broken soul.
When will I find
what will keep me whole.

When will we we find
what will keep us whole.
