Please, God
I don't want much.
I just want my friends to be happy
the world to be happy.
I just want to be sane again.
I want the world 
to be like it was before.
I never knew 
people could be
so horrid.
I never knew 
I could be so horrid.
please, God
just make it be like it used to be.
Just make it go away.
Make it go away
