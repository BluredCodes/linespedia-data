I'm bleeding.
You don't see me.

I'm bleeding.
Can't you see it?
Can't you see the crimson blood
flowing from my veins
onto the snow covered ground?
Can't you?

It's turning the snow red
and you don't even notice.
Your feet and hands 
are covered in my blood.
You don't even care.

I'm bleeding.
The blood is streaming
down the curve of my hip.
The pain is blanketing my body.

I'm bleeding.
and you can't even see it.
