title: "Coffee"
writer: "brokenworthlessness"

You warm my soul.
You are my best friend.
You give me energy.
You make me smile.
You are my comfort when I cry.
You calm me down after a hard day.
Just like coffee.
Vicks,
I don't know what to say.
You are the best friend
anyone could ever wish for.
