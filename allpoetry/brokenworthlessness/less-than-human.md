You seem to think that I am less than human.
That the words you say do not hurt me.
If I did not know better I would think
you enjoyed watching my tears fall
from my bruised and bloody face.
Maybe I don't know better,
maybe you do enjoy my tears.
I am sorry for what I have done
whatever it is
to make you think
that I am less than human.
It would seem you think me a mouse
and yourself a cat
waiting to kill me.
I know you just wish I dissapeared.
Maybe I am less than human...
for if so many people think so
it must be true.
I am less than human.
