---
title: "Why Should I Care"
writer: "brokenworthlessness"
slug: "why-should-i-care"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/22077-Why-Should-I-Care-by-brokenworthlessness"

why should i care
that the world is falling apart
that no one gives a fuck anymore
when people die
just because someone else
feels they are not good enough to live
they are too good for that
for life
because life is hell
but why should i care
i have money
i have friends
but i wish it were gone
take this life
just take it
it's yours if you want it
i don't
i don't deserve it
but why should i care 
damnit
why
no good comes from caring
only pain
because there is nothing i can do
i am not good enough
enough to change
anything
but why should i care
that i can't
why should  i care
that i'm too stupid to change it
why should i care
