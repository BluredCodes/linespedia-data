The golden flame burns violently.
I watch it
f
   l
i
   c
k
   e
r

in the biting wind.

It touches my frail skin.
the spreading line of crimson covers my arms
and scorches my heart
as the flame caresses me.
Fire.
Beautifull Fire.
Caressing my soul.
Warming my heart.
Fire.
