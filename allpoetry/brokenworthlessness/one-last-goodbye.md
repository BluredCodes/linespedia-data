I said goodbye
though you could not hear me.
I said it.

I told you I loved you
though you couldn't comprehend.
I told you.

I kissed you
that one last time,
as your eyes fell dead to the world
I know you did not feel it.
But I did.

So now, 
althought your presence no longer graces this earth,
and your smile no longer brightens the world,
I shall say it one last time.

I Love you, Grandma.

Goodbye.
