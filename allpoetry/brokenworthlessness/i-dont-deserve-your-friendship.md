I don't deserve to be your friend.
You are so much of a better person than I.
Why do you think so much of me?
I am nothing.
You have always been there for me.
Always.
Through all this pain.
Through all this blood.
Through all these tears.
I don't know what to say to you.
I just don't deserve your friendship.
I am so selfish
so stupid
so unfair.
I never understood 
why you wanted to be my friend.
I never understood
why you didn't find someone better.
You always care.
You have always been there for me.
You are such a wonderfull person.
I don't deserve your friendship.Vicks....this is for you
