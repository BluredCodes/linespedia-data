"Hello", said I.

"Hello, Megan. You've been a bad girl"

"I know. I'm sorry. I don't mean to be such a bad person"

"Well you are.You are stupid and selfish", The Devil replied.

"I said I was sorry"

"Sorry isn't good enough. You deserve to die."

"You call that a punishment?"

"Where you're going, it is"

"I'm sure I deserve it"

"You do. You are so ignorant. So naive"

"Ingnorance is bliss. Didn't your mother tell you that?"

"I have no mother"

"Oh, so that's what makes the Devil so bad"

"Don't say that you worthless bitch"

"You can't hurt me with words, Satan. I call myself worse"

"Do you really? I can tell by the way you got here"

"I killed myself. So what? Maybe I like a little pain"

"You really are ignorant"

"Why is that you evil angel?"

"Do you know what you've done to your family, your friends?"

"Yes, and I'm sorry"

"STOP APOLOGIZING"

"I'm sorry"

"Damn you"

"I am damned. I'm here in Hell, remember?"
