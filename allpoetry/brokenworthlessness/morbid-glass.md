Crimson tears
dripping
onto tainted glass;
painting it with blood,
smearing it with tears.
Lighted images 
dancing
across the silver truth the glass povides;
consuming it with horror,
corupting it with ugliness.
A reflection of myself
dancing on the morbid glass.
Voices floating in the frigd air
surrounding me,
telling me
that the mirror lies,
But I cannot ignore
the repulsive face
that stares back at me
from the morbid glass.
I cannot ignore
the hideous form
that laughs 
and screams
and cries with me
from the honest,
morbid,
glass.
