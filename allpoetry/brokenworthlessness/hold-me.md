---
title: "Hold Me"
writer: "brokenworthlessness"

Wrap your arms around me.
So warm and so strong.
I can't take this anymore.
I need you.
Touch me softly
and pray to the lord to forgive you 
for what you are about to do to me.
Soft moans escape my lips 
as you touch my skin.
Fires of love and need
light from within.
God, how much I need you.
How much I need your touch.
I just need to lie down next to you
and fall softly asleep.
In your arms forever.
Never let me go,
Just hold me in your arms.
Safe in your arms
where the world brings me no pain.
Warm in your arms
where there is never any rain.
I need you more than anything
I need to feel your touch.
I need your hope, I need your trust
I need your love so much.
Just put your hands on me.
I don't want to be alone.
I need you to be there with me...
Please 
I don't want to cry alone.this is for the perfect man, who only exists in my mind, for he is not real, he does not love me.
