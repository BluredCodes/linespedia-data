---
title: "No Right To Cry"
writer: "brokenworthlessness"
slug: "no-right-to-cry"
source: "AllPoetry"

Crystal tears shatter
as they roll off my face;
Crimson blood seeps into the ground;
My screams echo off the walls;
Yet I have no reason to cry.
I feel worthless and selfish,
and although I am,
I have no right to bleed.
There are people bleeding already,
I don't need to add to the blood.
There are those who are dying,
and I don't need to add to the count
of the bodies found 
on the polluted and littered streets.
What right have I to weep-
to fall down on my knees 
and scream
untill I can no longer make a sound.
All those who die each day,
can no longer get down on their knees to pray
to the god that let them die.
I have no right to still be living,
for they are worth so much more than I. 
Take my life
and give it to them,
give them a chance
to live again.
Please, just kill me,
and give them my smiles,
give them my laughs,
give them everything I have,
just leave me with my tears.
