Blow me up.
Burn me.
I hope that someday
you can see 
what you've done.

Incinerate 
my dying soul.
Before you finally
loose control
of me, defeated.

Touch me
with that hatefull flame.
Kill me before
I go Insane.
You know you can't control me.

Fry my 
severed heart alive.
End its struggled beat.
Murder me and all for which I strive. 
I will celebrate your defeat.
