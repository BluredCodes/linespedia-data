---
title: "I'm Sorry"
writer: "brokenworthlessness"

I'm sorry
that I'm such a hastle
That I'm such a pain.
I know I'm not worth much.
I'm tallentless
and I'm sorry.

I didn't mean to make you cry.
I never ment to let you die.
I didn't mean to break your heart
again.

I'm sorry
That I'm so selfish
That I let you go.
I know I'm not very much.
I'm nothing
and I'm sorry.

I didn't mean to hurt you so.
I never ment to let you go.
I didn't mean to loose your trust
again.

I'm sorry
That I'm so stupid
That I am me.
I know I can't do much.
I'm worthless
and I'm sorry.

I didn't mean to be me.
I never meant to let you see.
I didn't mean to wound your soul
again.

I'm sorry.
