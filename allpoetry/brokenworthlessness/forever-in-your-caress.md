Forever in your caress.
In your warm, forgiving arms.
I want you to undress me,
please forgive these thoughts...
I can't be innocent anymore.
I tired of morals,
at least these-
I will always keep the rest....
forever and always,
even in your caress
where I cannot even recall my name.
Forever with your hands
gliding across my skin.
Forever with your kisses
igniting this fire within.
Forever worthwhile,
as long as I am in your arms.
Forever seeing your smile,
and forever life has meaning.
This one moment is forever...
Just one moment of bliss
can last a lifetime, maybe more
when I can feel your kiss.
I need your lips on my skin,
your hands all over all of me.
I need your strong hands
to touch me lovingly.
I need to be
Forever in your caress...
