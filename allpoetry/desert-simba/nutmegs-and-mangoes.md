title: "Nutmegs and Mangoes"

did you know how much I missed you?
Did you know how incessant the fragrance 
Of nutmegs and mangoes was?
Did you know that sleep had escaped me in your absence?
Did you know there were messages I wrote for you
In the air around your pillow? 
Did your heart read them?


I learned how much you love me, 
Even more then you could comprehend upon yourself.
I learned why passion fruit has meaning in your life.
I heard your lips tell me you love me.
Did you know those words sounded like 
It was the first time I had heard them in my life.


I desire your absence to disappear.
I desire your lips to be sealed to mine.
I want to wake up curled inside your heart.
I simply want to be where you are always.
Nestled lips to lips,
Torso to torso,
Heart to heart
Soul to soul.
Enjoined as One.
