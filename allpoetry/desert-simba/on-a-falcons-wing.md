title: "On a Falcons wing"
writer: "Desert Simba"

I have carried the sinews of this moment
Through the quietness of thought
I have stringed together each look of your eyes
And married them into the fabric of the day

I have walked beside you when you never knew I was there
I have stalked your dreams with the randomness 
With you walked through mine
I have simply become only love for you.

I have seen the wind play minstrel games with your
Flaming red hair
I have watched sea gulls dip their wings for you
And somewhere a falcon has tipped its wings with snow wind
To soar to a mountain top to bring you flowers that
Breed in snow.

And I a mere mortal
Touch the soft petals of your feet
And wish to bathe you in nectarine kisses
And want only for you to know
My love flies with the falcon……
