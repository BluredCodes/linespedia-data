---
title: "Healing wounds"
writer: "Desert Simba"

I have worried about your pain.
I have carried my unsaid words at the tip of my tongue.
Wondering if I can ease your heart with words.
Or do I simply touch your love soaked eyes.
Will you hear in my silence the concern?

I wish to caress your soul.
I wish to gently fondle your eyes
I wish to softly kiss your lips.
Sealing them to the pain and grieving of your heart.
And yet I see your happiness nestled into each gesture.

And I have made my peace with myself.
And if it helps I may tell you 
Your love helped me heal
I hope in some small way
My being there might heal your wounds.
