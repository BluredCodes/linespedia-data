---
title: "Angel"
writer: "Desert Simba"
slug: "angel"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Like a sleepless angel 
you shield my loneliness with laughter
and endless kisses.
You listen to my stories that have tired
many a soul.
You immerse yourself into my life,
delicately and hesitantly.
And I wait the messages that spring from your eyes.
And though you play hide and seek 
with your heart, 
there are some eyes where it cannot hide from.

	
I gather your eyes to the anticipated
instant of joy.
And gently I hear you breathe 
a new life into my being.
And you make me wait for moments
like peeling open oysters for pearls.
And in long gazes you drown me 
into a sea of love that makes drowning 
an ecstasy. 


In the new born morning light
you look like my sleepless angel
collecting a few moments of rest.
And did any one tell you what
that volcano in your belly meant?
And did you know that somehow I believe
my faith in love could only be restored
by an angel. 
Did you know it was meant to be you?
