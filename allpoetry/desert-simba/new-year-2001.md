---
title: "New Year 2001"
writer: "Desert Simba"

I have been blessed by a new sun
As we watched it blaze over sand dunes
warming our cold bones
and touching our hearts.
I watched you smile in the morning light.
I knew you were more beautiful than anything 
my eyes held before.
or indeed will hold ever again.

I awoke to your heavenly smile
and the tenderness of your soul.
Do you really know how lovely you are?
Do you really know what you do to my heart?
And sometimes I want to wander 
into the long stretches of silence that hold your love.


On some mornings I don’t want to move 
away from your embrace.
Just to lazily lie in the meditation of your soft breathing
watching a world go by.
To recall the flaming red hair you wear so well
which puts to shame the sun’s ball of fire.
Just like it gives life to the world,
your being there gives life to my love.


After a hard winter,
and a stormy silence 
I find the peace in your eyes
that paralyses me.
Wanting to turn that moment into a lifetime.
