title: "Ounces of Inspiration (Revised)"
writer: "UTTubby"

Tis not merely seen by the eye,
but feel it from the heart, whereas
one sees good deeds well done.

Judge others, not by eyesight
but by their footsteps taken,
yet, judge your own steps first.

You shall walk in peace and share,
expressing that what should be seen,
and not what is,
but what may become.

If you see your cup running over 
with peace and joy,
share a sip with your own neighbor,
because a full cup just swells.

Vision each other as a friend,
for one day We'll be gone.

Seeing is believing,
but know what you have seen
before you shall believe.
