---
title: "Sunshine in Prayer"
writer: "UTTubby"
slug: "sunshine-in-prayer"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/21081-Sunshine-in-Prayer-by-UTTubby"
---

Thank you Lord for the golden years
faithfully shown your light
And for your brilliance you've bestowed
shining come day and night

Thank you for your heart of gold 
With my trust in you I pray
And thank you for your rising this morn
as you smile upon me today

Thank you for our friendships
that reaches different heights
And for this light we share together,
I thank you Lord tonight

Thank you for your Blessings
come another day we share
And for your genuine love,
Humbly I thank you Lord in prayer.

Amen.
