Eye to eye... 
minds entwine 
touching hearts 
souls soothed 
reflecting vision 
spontaneity felt, 
combustion and arousal 
desiring wetness 
another reached, 
nearer drawn 
touching flesh 
bone melting 
dripping sweat 
drops forming 
uniting together 
passion to passion 
lust and satisfaction 
deeply breathing... 
exhaled life
in a puff.
