Further the field of butterflies where I lay, 
I drift into paradise-a blue topaz sea 
while my wild weeds sway-dancing 
softly to a melody. 
Bluebirds are chirping unlike impatient sea gulls 
perched upon wooden posts awaiting 
their morning feast. 
But, I gaze into a sunset in the bay, 
soothed by those rippled white caps. 
She appears swaying 
as I'm consumed by her sighs-her raindrops 
caress me like that ocean breeze  
mingles with my butterflies.
