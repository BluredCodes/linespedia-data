title: "Her Last Goodbye"

Her frail arms reached...paws first,  
limp body stretching, as if longing 
for another chance. 

And suddenly, our memory 
of her thick, shiny coat 
but her present bewildering look 
touched us turning our hearts to tears. 

I also remember a loving, playful kitten 
who absorbed life  
with her soft bright, round eyes  
that shone, embracing our smiles 
as she gave us her kitty-cat love. 

But, she was more than 'another pet' 
She was human. 
She was Kitty. 

And...then she hugged us 
and simply passed away.
