---
title: "Sundown and You"
writer: "UTTubby"
slug: "sundown-and-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/14801-Sundown-and-You-by-UTTubby"
---

It doesn't seem to matter 
if it's here or if it goes, 
but who knows if it will shine tomorrow, 
or if it does or doesn't show. 

Beyond that horizon, 
warm thoughts of you absorb my mind, 
for you're here with me today like sunshine, 
while I sit and reminsce in time. 

My tears of sadness are blinding 
like the sunshine through a smokey haze, 
but if you appeared here tomorrow, 
tomorrow would shine each day. 

I know you've drifted beyond my sunset 
'tis darkness surrounds me, too. 
My wish is to hold you forever in darkness 
so you'll see how much I love you.
