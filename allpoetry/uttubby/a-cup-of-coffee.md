Life's like a cup of coffee
Not too hot, nor too cold
Never boiled or sizzled served
Or chilled when set down on the table,
No more than a few teaspoons of sugar
No more than a little coffee creamer
For black appears too dark to me
And too much sugar seems too sweet.

Life's not perfect if a cup runs over
Spilled coffee leaves a stain.
When served if only a half-a-cup,
It's not enough to satisfy me.
Like a cup of coffee served just right,
life seems a little more enjoyable.

A cup of coffee served aroma
smells so delicious and inviting.
That morning sun shining through
And that aroma-scent smelling, too
seems so pleasant and full of life,
But...it's not much fun
when we're sipping all alone.
