He shines while staring 
through the clouds, smiling, 
as if saying good morning, but 
he just sits up there in the dark. 
I bet if he could talk 
the stars would meet me. 
I'd mingle. 
Perhaps I'd meet an angel or two. 
Who knows? 
Maybe I could reach 
those pearly gates 
and walk the streets of gold. 
I just sit and wonder what he'd say 
if he could talk. 
What would he say? 
What would I do? 
Who knows, 
but I just smile and wonder.
