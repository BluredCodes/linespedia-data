title: "Touched"
writer: "UTTubby"

My tears have rolled,
Touched my heart
felt it beat,
For such beauty
in the world
shown so bright,
A little baby
opened its eyes,
That first cry
without a tear
knowing I've none,
But felt it near
cause I was once
just like that,
I cried without a tear,
what happened to me?
Look at me now
after all these years.
