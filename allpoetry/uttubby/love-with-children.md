---
title: "Love with Children"
writer: "UTTubby"
slug: "love-with-children"

Beside the fireplace hung with stockings
A roaring blaze and a cup of coffee,
Wood is piled in its unique blend 
as smoke puffs in the night air again,

A Mistletoe is hung over the door with care
while children lay tucked beneath the cover,
A Grand Father chimes at least a twelfth time
as echoes and warmth draw romance nearby,

The stars twinkle in the chill of night
as the moon bids good-night to the chill,
And heavenly bells are heard and seen
They both entwine with loves old gleam,

And so the night wears well with jingles
Not candy or flowers mingle with the bells,
But the sweetest of dreams smell so sweet
Together alone while their two hearts beat.
