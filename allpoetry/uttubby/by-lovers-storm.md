---
title: "By Lover's Storm"
writer: "UTTubby"
slug: "by-lovers-storm"

The sunlight has shone true colors 
as red roses blossom in the breeze.
Two hearts entwine in tenderness  
while bees dance around the trees.

The wind softly whispers to my ear 
as trees hover over the leaves.
Eyes gaze into each others hearts 
yet butterflies are born to be free.

Darkness holds two echoes  
while raindrops danced with each other.
They're soaked beneath their skin
as the Sun's tucked beneath a cover.

The thorns hibernate in harmony
whereas frost will never be found.
Two hearts plucked one in passion
from a rose layed limp on the ground.

Held gently in her wet palms,
she kisses its dripping form.
His heart softly beats for hers
as she blooms within the storm.
