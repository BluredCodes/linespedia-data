My tongue hardens beneath you. 
Your sweat drips off my lips. 
You trickle down my yearning cheeks,
I have a hearty appetite.

I taste your juice 
dripping from my tongue.
Your moans echo in my ear
sweet in tenderness.

Your curves meet my erection-my
leaning tower.
It leans over for you.
You slide down its swollen shaft.

I feel you slipping,
covering me with your desire.
I taste each and every moment 
as I thrust deeply yet higher.

You turn around.
I taste you.
You engulf me with your lips.
We're consumed.
