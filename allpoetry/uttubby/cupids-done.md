title: "Cupid's Done"
writer: "UTTubby"

It's NOT just another day,
but a special one for

an arrow to strike- 
Aim and thrust from the bow
strengthened from his fiery 
finger tips aglow,

He's held gently by her eyes
entwined in her arms as
she's rocked the "cradle of passion"
smiling and caressing his thoughts.

She's fallen deeply from the thrust,
held gently by his eyes.
They unite beneath the stars, yet
kissed by the sun, yet
shadowed by moonlight, 

as they kiss.
Cupid's done.
