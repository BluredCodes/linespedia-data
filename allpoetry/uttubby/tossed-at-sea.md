title: "Tossed at Sea"
writer: "UTTubby"

Beside me she's perched 
as if awaiting a calmness. 
Sand drifts near our feet
as she's ruffled by the salty spray.

Her eyes wander,   
staring beyond the horizon.
I pray for silence-
merely a wishful calm sea.

She awaits patiently,
like a lion in the brush
pondering its next meal,
My eyes skim the water.

Together, we stare into nothingness-
a raging wind.
We gather our thoughts and toss them out
but they come back to us...wind-blown.
