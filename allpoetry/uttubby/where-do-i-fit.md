---
title: "Where Do I Fit?"
writer: "UTTubby"
slug: "where-do-i-fit"
source: "AllPoetry"

So...where do I fit on this little place 
you call earth? 
Is there a spot for me or am I wishing upon 
a star? 

Am I damned or dreaming? 
Is this my imagination 
because I'm scared, (shaking) 
like a pirate forced to walk a plank. 

Even though I walk on this land, 
I'm forced to ride the ripples. 
I simply shall not hide 
because I cannot disappear. 

Not all books can be opened 
like knowlege, courage, and wisdom. 
And so, I'll hide between two covers 
somewhere between the pages. 

Perhaps, I shall hide in a paragraph, 
in a sentence, or in a word, 
but I'll hide...and be resurrected 
by someone who loves to read.
