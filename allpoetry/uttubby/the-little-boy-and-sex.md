OK...Here's how it goes...

Sex shadows over me,
raped me before
my virginity-
age of Sex, Sevin, Ate,
but who's to know,
I can't even SPELL-
write or rong.
Who's to know 
he's on my back-
giving me nothing,
and taking it back, 
no attraction, 
or satisfaction
and kisses are yucky, too.
Oh! the shame.
What are kisses?

What's this...
I'm made to suck,
I don't know 
but it looks like me,
it's not him 
but another one,
preying on me
before my virginity.
Sex, Sevin, Ate, be Nine,
who's to know,
another one 
has joined this
show, cept he's 
closer to me like my...

OH! Help me!
I got a knife,
stuck it in a door...
pounding and screaming 
forevermore-
I'm reminded while I cry,
But, I would have killed him
anyway.
