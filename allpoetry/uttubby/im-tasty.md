---
title: "I'm Tasty"
writer: "UTTubby"
slug: "im-tasty"
source: "AllPoetry"

Well...Just call me toast,
slap some butter on me,
cover me up, hide my face
amongst a million other lil' crispys.

Take my words full of life,
spread them around with your
butter knife...
but don't drown me in molasses,
Oh...so sticky.

I can take a lil' criticism at best,
not to be burnt in your flaming hot oven,
and scraped right off...then tossed
away into the trash.

Eat me, cause my words are fine...buttery and sweet,
floppy not burnt, tasty from jelly,
when taken out just in the nick of time,
OH NO...I'm burnt!
