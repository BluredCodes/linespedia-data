---
title: "Best Friends"
writer: "UTTubby"

Throughout time,
the days and hours,
we've come to cherish,
for nothing compares
to those moments we've shared
and the dreams we've built upon.

Just like a sunrise 
and the sunset near,
my endless thoughts encompass you
because you are whom I adore.

Friendship is just a commonality
but so many never seem to last,
and so this I promise to you my best friend,
true friendship,
just like in the past.

You will never know
how many days pass 
that you linger on my mind,
just so you will know
if we must part,
you will always be the best friend of mine.

God bless you,
for when I'm taken away from this land.
I'll treasure your friendship forever,
for together... 
we were a part of God's plan.

Love,
Your Best Friend
