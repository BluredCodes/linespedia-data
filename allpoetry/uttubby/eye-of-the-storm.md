Darkness falls 
Clouds shuffle in
The Sea Billows roll 
Salt dances on air

The Moon still smiling
Stars merrily play
The Wind whistling
Rain trickling down

The Wind begins howling 
The Sea Serpent grows
The Moon frowns 
Stars disappearing

Eye to eye, Beware!
I'm feeling at ease
The gusts scattering
Stillness in the sand.
