Dedicated to my Grandmother

My heart she touched,
I felt as pure as snow
But snow have I none,
Nor does she know.

For the older she grew,
Forever in a day
There she was for me,
I'm thankful I do pray.

Never did I know,
A time there would be
She would come alive on print,
For others to see.

Time never stood still,
Like dust in the wind
Settle did it not,
Even near her end.

Even though she's passed,
I know she's at rest 
Tomorrow, if I died,
I would see her at her best.

I've heard when we pass,
Angels we do shine bright 
Purely and perfectly at peace,
Like a baby wrapped in white.

This Christmas be thankful,
And treasure your loved ones 
Share your love with them,
Forever...then life's done.
