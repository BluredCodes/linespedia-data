It's not just a note
Written in the stars
Never said never
Tomorrow's not that far,

It's not felt in a dream
Or spoken in the dark
It's never forever
Unless mean't from the heart,

It's seen by the eyes
It's felt in the mind
It's never too late
If it's played just in time,

It's not just words
Written on a page
It's stuck in print
If not read day by day.
