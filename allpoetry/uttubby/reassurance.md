I've walked beside you
each step...carried you
in prayer-answering your beckon call, 
wiping your tears and uplifting your spirit,
blessing you with miracles 
of strength and courage, 
of knowlege and wisdom.
Lest you forget, you've been blessed
with a life full of love and happiness.

With my arms open wide
and my nail-scarred palms out--"hold me, 
feel my heart, absorb my pain" 
and you'll know your in heaven.

So...wipe your tears and smile...sharing with
those you love, those who care or those who don't,
living each moment like it was your last.
I come swiftly in a blink of an eye.
