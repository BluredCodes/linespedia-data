an expression 
of true ways,
of laughter, 
of moments we share,
intimately and unconditionally. 

But, its NOT like a dream 
or NOT like a worn-out book  
to be placed upon a dusty shelf,
but, to be consumed with desire
when the cover is worn 
from reading it over and over again,
then back to page 1.

To Love; one must soar  
where eagles dare 
or sail across the stormy Seas
or rise above the true-blue mountains,

to treasure the gifts we share
soley spoken from the heart.
