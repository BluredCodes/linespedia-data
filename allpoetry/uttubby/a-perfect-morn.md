---
title: "A Perfect Morn"
writer: "UTTubby"
slug: "a-perfect-morn"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/23919-A-Perfect-Morn-by-UTTubby"
---

Let me breathe with you
till we're saturated by each breath,
faithfully till the morning comes,
midnight-soaked, then dried by the sun,

Your eyes held near to me in darkness,
you've felt my warmth and await the morn, 
Till this night has come and gone, 
Let me feel you till we're soaked and warmed,

Our dreams to mingle like the stars,
The sweetest ones twinkle and shine,
Till morning creeps through our window,
And the moon fades out of the sky.

Our hearts shall entwine,
playfully beating in rhythm,
Till darkness leaves on a peaceful night,
and our sunshine becomes a smile.
