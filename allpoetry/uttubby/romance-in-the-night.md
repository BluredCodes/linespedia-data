---
title: "Romance in the Night"
writer: "UTTubby"
slug: "romance-in-the-night"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/13708-Romance-in-the-Night-by-UTTubby"
---

Can you feel, 
a warm breeze tonight,
violins in Venice
beneath the trees or 
a singing gondola floating 
on cool calm water.

...two hearts in bloom
stoked by the fire   
beneath a full moon
lit by millions of stars, 
not written in Vegas on stone 
for money have I none.

...pounding in the dark,
awaiting to escape 
to a back yard or park,
on a beach or on a mountain-top,
merrily, as two heart-beats dance.

...lucious lips like wine
absorbed through the skin,
taste as sweet as honeysuckle vine,
succulent little kisses, 
by midnight, alone in the dark.

...flesh engulfed by flames,
drowning in pools of sweat,
soaked by the rain,
by sunrise, 
soundly, two hearts are asleep together.
