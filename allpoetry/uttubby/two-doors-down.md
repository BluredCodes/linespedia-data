---
title: "Two Doors Down"
writer: "UTTubby"

I remember NOT much about yesterday.
But,in 1967, once upon a CRIME,
the little boy in ME cried. 
And so the story goes...he was my best friend, 
two doors down we'd laugh and play.
But...I recall being enticed 
to lust at nudity.
He'd grab a book and turn off the light
and say "this is what to do, 
he'd do it to me" then done.
Pleasure or pain? I do not recall
or really care to remember.
But every time I envision that scene
it sort of smells and lingers.
So WATCH your children and WATCH their friends
to notice any frowns.
Let them know between right and wrong. 
Don't let them go "two doors down."
