---
title: "Oh!  Butterfly"
writer: "UTTubby"

Whenever a butterfly flutters about,
I wonder if it sees me smiling.
And when it passes by my eyes,
I wonder if it can hear me think aloud.

Whenever it lights upon my skin,
I wonder if it can read my mind.
And as it flutters away to another,
I wonder if it knows.

Whenever I see a butterfly,
I feel it dancing around.
And to feel it brush upon my face,
I suddenly come alive.

Whenever I see a butterfly,
It's so precious to behold.
And when I dream of a butterfly,
I dream of you.
