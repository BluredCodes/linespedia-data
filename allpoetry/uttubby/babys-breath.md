---
title: "Baby's Breath"
writer: "UTTubby"
slug: "babys-breath"
source: "AllPoetry"

A baby breathes life into a home... 
through nourishment 
and by molding us as they grow... 
through fulfillment 
by blessing us, 
enriching our most unpleasant moments 
with their smiles, 
when they giggle at the silliest things 
reminding us their human, too. 
They love unconditionally, 
watching us as we grow in love 
while we breathe life into each other... 
thus; a baby becomes a rose.
