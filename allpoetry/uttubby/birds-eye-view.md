---
title: "Birds-Eye View"
writer: "UTTubby"
slug: "birds-eye-view"
source: "AllPoetry"

In the still of the night,
Do birds really fly?
Do they really chirp or sing
or just whisper by any means.
Do they huddle in their nests,
comforting little ones, 
Do they dance in the trees
or just flutter in the dark.
Do they travel to and fro
till dawns early light, 
Do they simply rest in peace,
beneath a pale moonlight.
Do they see where they are going
or are they lost in the dark,
Do they pray to their maker,
saying prayers from their heart.
Do they wish upon a star, 
wondering who they'll meet
Do they know who it will be,
Do they love like you and me?
