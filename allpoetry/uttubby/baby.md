I feel just like a baby bird
jilted from it's nest.

Just because I lack feathers to fly
I'm filtered from the best.

Other birds chirp happily
while being fed juicy worms.

Me, I starve from "Mother Comfort"
because I've lost my home.

Now, I'm drained from energy
from chirping little cries.

Other birds spread their wings,
thus certainly they will fly.

If I could only climb that tree
to get back to my nest.

I'd be a stronger baby bird
flying high above the rest.
