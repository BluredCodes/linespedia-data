He sees her lying in the sand,
her silhouette stretched outward.
The sand and his light consume her.
She peeks at drawn threads of light 
from her neck to her feet.
He strips her cloth. 
His eyes dance over her nipples,
encircling them.
She surrenders.
He dances on her tummy,
down through her pubic hair-
left a soft warm trail.
She's warmed by his glance.
She dreams...His dance
rises beneath her,
spreading her legs,
soothing them by the glare. 
Slowly, he gleams,
arising to her attention.
She moans.
His light caresses her,
touching her wetness.
Her hair trails.
Slowly, he thrusts inside her-
An overwhelming light 
deeply inside and out.
She shines brighter than the stars.
Her eyes glow like the sun.
Together...they shine.
