title: "The Bestest"
writer: "UTTubby"

Daddy...You'd suppose we could go
and watch them clowns frolick around, 
or do you think we'll sit here... 
while I wander around the neighborhood
like a little lost pup crying for its mommy.

Oh Daddy...Please lets go 
to the Bestest Show on Earth,
when smellin' them old nasty cigars 
I'll hold my nose...but
smell the buttered popcorn
and not throw it at someone
yelling "get yer redhots"
as they pass by my seat
while I'm on your shoulders
tossin'peanuts.

Oh! Please lets go, and Daddy
I'd be good as cotton candy...I promise.
And Daddy, If I ain't good, 
you'd still be the bestest on earth.
