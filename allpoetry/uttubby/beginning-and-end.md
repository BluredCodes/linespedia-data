title: "Beginning and End"
writer: "UTTubby"

First, it's with the eyes and ears
As so the middle is from the mind.
The farther down the road you travel,
your soul you will surely find.
First, you learn to crawl then walk,
And, then you learn to speak.
Then, you learn to write alot,
and then you learn to think.
Death is only a matter of time.
