title: "The Old Folks and me"
writer: "UTTubby"

You'll never believe what flashed before me, 
BIG DOMINOES give me hives
84-version of 42 'cept with 6 players-passed away,
almost funny how time flys, I was merely 5, 6, or 7.

I sit...seeing them (statues) 'round the table,
perched like blackbirds upon her phone line,
Then, all the sudden, someone would jump and yell (84)
"sure is getting close to half-time"

"Oh, Boy" I said, "my favorite treat"
while reminiscing Saturday nights,
then Mamma would slice the chocolate cake,
pass the cookies all day she'd bake, and I loved that home-grown melon.

"A little more punch?", "No thanks for me"
I'd sit and smile, listening awhile 
sipping on grown-up coffee, cold Iced tea
or my favorite, "Dr. Pepper"

Time would pass between the sweat, no sign of AC,
just a big black-box fan, wringing of the sweat,
a phone that would make you jump off your seat
and my favorite dog barking...FLUFFY.

Another Saturday night come past 12,
I'm yawning being tucked in bed,
knowing I get to sleep late in the morn,
seems like a celebration...sad to watch them fly.
