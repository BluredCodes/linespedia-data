It's hot as blue-blazes
and I'm standing and sweating
with a tall cool one to chill the
summer sun.
And then they're off...
ripping and running as folks
roar while rising to the
occasion.
Those four-legged beasts just strut. 
But...one leads around the bend.
Then comes the finish so
the crowd stands
as I spill my drink
looking at my soaking wet ticket that
WON.
