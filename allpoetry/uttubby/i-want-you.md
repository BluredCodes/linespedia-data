---
title: "I Want You"
writer: "UTTubby"
slug: "i-want-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/24664-I-Want-You-by-UTTubby"
---

I want to be with you 
on the sand near the whitest of foam
In the valley amongst the tallest of trees
we'll carve love on the purest of stone,

On the highest of mountains to thrust
where the trees simply do not grow,
I want to share tenderness with you,
should it rain, hail, sleet, or snow,

We'll dine like a queen and king,
hosting a hearty feast,
But near a rustic cottage by the river
we'll lay just you and me,

We'll feel the tall green grass,
layed beneath our skin,
Spread out like the sunshine on the mountain
felt our love blowing in the wind,

I want you in the forest
to bask in the afterglow,
And feel the sweat dripping from our bodies
even if the sun doesn't show,

I want you until forever
till the clock has spun its last spin,
And if we should finish making love at best,
We'll do it all over again.
