---
title: "Love's Song"
writer: "UTTubby"
slug: "loves-song"
source: "AllPoetry"

So filled with passion,
two beautiful eyes peek on a morning dew.
Your inner-self gleams like the sunrise,
as you engulf me, I sweat.

I'm held, bound by you,
drowning from your heat,
caressed as you strip like the melting snow,
before my eager eyes, your complete nakedness aglow.

I'm entwined-wrapped in your beauty,
as you yearn for my nude sunrise.
Your very being encompasses 
as your touch subsides a chill.

Your eyes...My heart's warm,
like the sun rising over the mountains.
You sing, whispering a song,
as I hear an echo forever in darkness.

Your echoes soothe me.
