psst...C'mere
ssshhhh...But,
I just want to
say how much I
love your poetry.

You guys pour out
your heart and soul,
writing poems...that
make me smile, laugh,
or simply cry.  Yes,
I'm not ashamed to
share a tear for I
have a soft heart, sometimes.  
I

want you to know 

YOU 
all are great. I
mean it. And, one more
thing, please! Keep 
sending us your best.
This one's for you,
sincerely.
