title: "In Love"
writer: "UTTubby"

Just imagine your heart... 
tears washed away,
Then a beautiful light 
come sunrise or sunset, 
come your way.

Imagine your heart 
tis not to be broken,
and your smiles glow 
like the sunshine's rays.

Just imagine your heart 
dressed in white,
no tarnished dreams
from the sky at night,
no plagues or snares 
that would dampen your soul,
and all that's you 
feels like gold.

Just Imagine your heart's 
worst nightmare-a cast away,
to watch your sweet dreams 
come alive each new day.
Imagine your heart floating 
on a beautiful cloud,
sharing the best with another 
as life will allow.

Just imagine it beating.
