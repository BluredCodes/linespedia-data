---
title: "Carry Me"
writer: "UTTubby"
slug: "carry-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Lord...Paint me two roses-one bent 
and one upright, as if I'm bowing 
to my partner in grace.
Shower me with blessings 
filled with laughter and tears
to flourish this barren face. 

Flood me with rainbows
as a reminder 
you are whom I adore.
Never need I hunger or thirst
in this meaningless human score.

Light my stars
turning darkness to daylight
lest I become afraid.
And...take me to Heaven
on the wings of an angel
to the golden streets you've paved.
But Lord, meanwhile...please carry me.
