---
title: "Seduce Me"
writer: "UTTubby"
slug: "seduce-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

Kiss and caress me, 
while my eyes 
set you on fire 
with my tantalizing thoughts- 
your butterfly kisses I desire. 

Hold and feel my heart- 
pulsating inside 
while I breathe 
your very last breath 
of life through my soul. 

Consume me and taste  
my lingering scent, 
awaiting me to devour 
your every moment's last intention.

Passionately brush my heart
with your sighs,
moaning my name,
setting me on fire,
then soothing me 
with your everlasting flame. 

Then...seduce me 
while I seduce you.
