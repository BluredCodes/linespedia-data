title: "Without a Child"

I try not to cry,
thinking about those 
little unheard-footsteps
that only I've heard,
But...I cried this little "poem"
and it looked up at me
and told me it loved me
with all its precious heart.
Then, I wiped my sad eyes,
sighed, and I thanked
God anyway for my unknown love.
