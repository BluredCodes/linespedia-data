You're so beautiful and succulent, 
tasty to my senses as my tongue 
brushes your woven nature. I 
yearn for your nectar-sweetest 
sensation as you drive me wild 
with anticipating expectation. 
You melt between my lips tenderly 
and juicy while my eyes roll to 
the side. I desire your berry taste 
between my gums-full-filled my senses 
while you absorb my sighs. Oh! Precious 
berry fruit of mine, forever you will be 
my favorite one for your sensational. 
You're the best...my lucious berry, 
consuming my every desire with your 
compassion and love. 
My passionate berry, Touch me...Taste me 
while I taste you, 
and together we'll be united in Sweet Heaven, 
my Strawberry and me forever.
