---
title: "Portrait of Passion"
writer: "UTTubby"

Silver-lined clouds block the suns rays 
enhancing a colorful blanket of flowers.
Misty raindrops paint sweet pine
beneath the snowy peak mountains.
Two lover's cuddle by a blistery waterfall 
as love birds pose upon limbs.
Chilled waters flow briskly through my mind
while spraying mist upon their skin.
Two hearts entwine in moments of passion 
as the animals draw nearer to the heat. 
My eyes gaze upon the lover's passionate embrace,
thus; we are chilled no more.
