title: "Over the Top"
writer: "UTTubby"

My Thoughts Today
Simmer to the Top,
Boiling furiously
But the Pots too Cold,
They're Hot as a Desert 
But become Frosty and Cold.
The Rain begins to Pour
But then Water turns to Snow,
I'm Drifting over the Edge
But I'm Hanging on with my Fingers,
My Thoughts grasp to Breathe
Then the Chilly Air Tingles.
My Thoughts Today
Grow and Grow and Grow,
But, Ice Pricks like the Needles
Plagued beneath a blossoming Rose.
I can't Leap over the Top
For being Stuck in this Pose,
But, if I made it over
...As when the Story goes,
I would Reach over the Top
To Surpass Life's little Woes.

Then I could Smile.
