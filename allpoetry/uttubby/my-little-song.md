SHE sang...
Hush little baby boy
Don't you cry
Mommy's not gonna
Leave your side
Daddy's not gonna
Beat your butt
Big baby brother
Loves you much.
Hush little baby boy
Don't you fret
Mommy and Daddy 
Love you, 
You bet.
And, if You ever 
Feel it's the end
You can play this song 
Again 
and Again 
and Again.

Love,
Mommy
