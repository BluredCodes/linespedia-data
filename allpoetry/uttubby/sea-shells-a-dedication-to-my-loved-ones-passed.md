Seen through my eyes,My loved ones have scattered,Like bits and pieces of rigid edgesFloating to shore in the rippled water.Thoughts in my mind,They fade, Like the trail of the sun Drawn toward the horizon.Felt from my heart,They drift back to me, Like the sunriseGlaring into my eyes.Heard with my ears,They putter, Like the sound of a sea gull's footsteps- A gentle tapping in the breeze.Found In my life,They are silent,But, I see them everywhereResting along a peaceful shore.Seen through my soul,They live,Like the ocean tideThat washes them back to sea. © 2001 Dana Gallup (All rights reserved)
