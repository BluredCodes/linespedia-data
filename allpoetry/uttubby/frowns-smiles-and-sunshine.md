Whenever a frown
turns upside down
Frowns leave 
a weary face.

Smiles gleam 
Darkness fades
A good feeling felt
takes its place.

Even though easier 
said than done
Frowns will disappear,
Tomorrow's frown
Becomes a smile
brought about by good cheer.

Today's heart-ache
wears well
and tears flow endlessly
But never you mind
getting soaked,
Sunshine will shine
and take away any fear.
