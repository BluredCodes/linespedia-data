Paint me... 
slender and muscular, yet lean
blonde hair and tan like Hollywood
with blue eyes sparkling like pearls,
a back stretched out like an eagle,
soaring behind a mountainous chest 
with a waist-rippled like ocean waves,
soaking curved thighs 
with feet spread like sand.
But...paint me a perfect heart,
blood-red and carved like Valentine's
sheltered and blessed, 
caressed by love-to love 
and smothered from attention.
