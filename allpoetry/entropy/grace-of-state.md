Just there
Once again
Just looped
Again and again

Just us
One making gains
Just me gaining you
Again, for only me

Just me
Only alive in you
Just a grace of state
Against all odds

Just once more
Only this time stay
Just once again, for me
Again you can go

Just this time
Only take yourself
Just remove what you need
And leave me what we made 

And go away.

Leave me my Grace of State.

-C.A.Wooding.
