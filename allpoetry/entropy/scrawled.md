on my meager palette
there is Red 
and Blue
White near Black
even Green beside Brown
I have only this one brush
to attempt.
such a simple tool, to capture you all

What could I make
that was me, in each; our times
dried upon the cave wall
to imprison

one promise I sought and thus made me dream
Thrice.

-C.A.Wooding.
