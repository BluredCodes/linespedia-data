Colors bleedDarkness fillsThe sun sleepsThe moon awakensWarmth ceasesColdness risesLove is createdHate is promotedEyes are blindedIllusions are seenAngels guardDemons huntLife is givenSouls are takenNight is DayDay is NightNight livesNight dies.Here i attempted to describe the night with the least amount of words possible.
