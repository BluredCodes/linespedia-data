Why do I feel condemned?
Humbled before your lucidity,
I am lost...
I am yours.

Flawless to all pain,
The labyrinth of our past
Dissolves in your mind.
Teach me.

Help me see through your eyes.
You stand above me
Watching my every move,
Calming my every fear and anger.

Sitting here in the dark,
I try to imagine your face,
Your soul,
Your love.

What I would not do to see you,
To swim in your sweet memory,
To indulge every sensation upon those eyes
   which open the doors to my most hidden soul.
Come, bring me peace.
