I stand here alone, surrounded in barbwire.
A crystal world turned black.
My mouth is closed shut.
For the benefit of society, I am ignored, incapable of speaking my mind, like an invisible mime.
I look around at this estranged nation confused, and at the same time, curious.
Clones stand all around in their black uniforms, black wigs, and black masks.
A thick air smelling of blood hits me as I turn to watch these 'humans' eat their own flesh.
They eat like maggots, like flies, with one single purpose... to survive.
If only they could take a glimpse at my world.
They'd see a world engulfed with happiness, sadness, change, and color.
I have seen enough...stop.
I find myself motionless, lost in a complete state of apathy in this world which I thought was only fantasy.This was written for my English class back in 2000.  There were strict rules (and i mean rules which applied to EVERY line), and it had to be either about a utpoian or distopian society.  I had a blast writing this one.  enjoy!
