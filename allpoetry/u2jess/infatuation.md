title: "Infatuation"

Rejoice, love, for you have enlightened me.
Renounce love, and I can cause you no pain.
Cry for me, and let your tears fall like rain.
Sing to me, what your heart wants me to see.
Dream and imagine how you'd want us to be.
Pray everynight that your love is not in vain.
Desire the strength to keep your mind sane.
Dear this, and I have, but to a higher degree.
Look, my love, at what you've brought me into.
Understand that it is you I care for.
Come to me for you are my foundation.
Discover that you have made me anew.
Comply to my everlasting love or
I shall never rid this infatuation.Written for a class in 2000.
