Only one question remains...
Who pushed who over?

I asked for your attention
Seeked your protection
Desired your comfort.

And you...

You begged me to stop with these mind games,
Pleaded for my self-sacrifice,
Looked for a never-ending joyride.

Desires only complicate one's bliss.
Love became something it shouldn't be...
A fragile emotion.

Words were easily misunderstood,
Lies were hardly taken as what they were.
Respect was replaced with Lust,
Trust with Jealousy.
Laughter with Silence...
Love with Apathy.

So tell me...

Who pushed who over?
