---
title: "Forgive Me"
writer: "U2jess"
slug: "forgive-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Bewildered heart,
Gather your strength and
Ease the pain.
I know you're bruised,
Battered,
Broken.
Yet you refuse to extinguish that which scarred you...

Me.

I've thrown you to the lions.
Left you out in the cold.
Never put a stop to this.

Forgive me.
I doubted you.
Lied to you.
Underestimated you.

And look at what has become of me.

Now I, the strong one, stand here before you,
Lost.
Bewildered.
Bruised.
Battered.
Broken.
