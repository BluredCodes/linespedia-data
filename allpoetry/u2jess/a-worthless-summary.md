I would last all eternity if I
Were to count every way which I love thee.
I'm like a tear devoted to your cry,
A faithful breeze to your motionless sea.
You're like a scar deep within me. You've lived
In my dreams like an indelible ink.
You're like a cancer, a legend outlived,
You are the blush on the catch of a wink.
You're the rain to my sea deserted,
You've overpowered me. Now I'm like a
Vagabond, an atheist converted.
It is impossible to name all the
Thoughts, sentiments, and feelings you bring me,
But I hope, at least, my love you can see.


*A Shakespearean style sonnetWritten for my english class way back when.
