Beyond all physical,
Beyond all supernatural,
Stands the undefined between me and you.

This fire inside cries in vain.
Burning with passion, I excel.
You make me prevail.

Yet I see something.
I see a hole, the place where your feelings should be.
I see through you.

This confusion leaves me cornered.
Conform to this, never!
I will fight, I won't give up so easily.

Beyond all physical.
Beyond all supernatural.

Beyond you...
I will prevail.
