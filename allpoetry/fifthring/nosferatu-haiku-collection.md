"Temptation"

Feel the stare of eyes
Race of misty mind and blood
Hand reaches for me



"Possession"

Fingers on my chest
Sink in slowly to my heart
Beating in her hand



"Bouquet"

Warm red in her eyes
Sticky thick down her soft face
My life running down



"Taste"

Warm red on her tongue
Sticky thick slow down her throat
She hungers for me



"Exhilaration"

Wet hand clutches hard
Open mouth and gasping breath
Takes every drop



"Satisfaction"

Filled with thick hot wet
Releasing the flesh with sighs
Mind in soft etherI read a lot of haikus yesterday, so I guess I'll throw mine in.
