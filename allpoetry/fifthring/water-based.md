title: "Water Based"
writer: "FifthRing"

Black matted lace she peels away
though the red on the flesh does stay
to be painted with by a tongue at play.
On a canvas of belly flesh
from a pattern left by the mesh
so begins a picture fresh
Careful strokes begin designs
slowly through in clean, wet lines
inspired by sounds and pleasure signs
Until come lips that gently press
to offer a new creativeness
and leave the canvas a delightful mess
Strokes are made to clean
to clear away the madness seen
until the red had never been.
.
.
This one was inspired by a line from "In The Flat Field" by Bauhaus.
