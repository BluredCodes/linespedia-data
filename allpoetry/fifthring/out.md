*





i can feel them in my head

your eyes like poison

when you look at me

i die

then your spider lips

suck me dry

you float me

to another world

all filled up

then i'm just skin

you leave me floating

on a deep black sea

you're filled up with me



let me taste your eyes

cut my tongue

open wide

drown you in red

rain kisses

til you can smell

what i am

inside



squeeze me, squeeze me, squeeze me

till i'm falling from

your fingers

and your hands

are all too wet

and you're about as

drunk as you can get

on me



so take me till

i die

till we die

till we kill your every

cry





*Inspired by, and somewhat written to, "The Kiss", by the Cure.
