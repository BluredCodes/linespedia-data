Bad nights Better Days

Today was a bringer of sore news
I went along as normal as I could,
To only hear of this news,
It swept me away, as I was so unprepared.
To find out that today was a friends.
First flight  up there, 
Without a goodbye she went.
She was so close but now so far away.
I would give a many day to have her back to stay.
But this I know, that where she is, 
Is much better then where I am.
Although I shall forever want that last goodbye.
Even if it was right before she died.
And to this day I may never understand,
Just why she had to go but, 
I shall forever know that HE always has a plan.
And to this plan she must fit, for now she gone,
And we must all just sit.
At her funereal I shall watch her lie
When I Know she is actually flying very high above
Only to watch and feel the great love,
That we send to her high up above.



This poem is dedicated to a great friend of mine who pretty much raised me from age 1-15 she was our maid at home ( I am 17 now)  I found this out today as I came home from school November 06,2001. She has been on dialysis for some time now and has gone through a lot in her life time but she was like my second mom and this poem doesn't even do justice to her.
