By Then I'll Know

When I'm lost...
You find Me.
Whenever I lose My path...
You guide Me.
You're My reason for living...
I'd have it no other way.
Without You in My life there would be no need for Me to stay here on earth.
When My world was dark... 
You made things right.
I hope now that I'll never spend another lonely night,
But if We becomes I,
At least by then I'll know,
That there once lived someone who did love Me so.



* This is how I feel right now you know spirit of the moment type of a thing.*
