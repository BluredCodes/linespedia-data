Love You Better

In life we all suffer through trials,
We have our ups 
We have our downs,
All those other girls were your downfall but I can love you better

Money here is not an issue,
If you do for me, I shall do for you,
It's a 2-way street headed either to success or failure,
Are you ready for the challenge,
I will love you better

Material things will never replace love,
They'll only make it harder to cope without,
It'll make it a lot harder to find,
Will you or will you not be mine,
I promise to love you better



Lady Rage 11/18/01
