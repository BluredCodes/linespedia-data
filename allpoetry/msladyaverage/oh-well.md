Oh Well!		

You made me cry last night,
I vowed to get even,
You told me you fell out of love,
Is that your reason for leaving?
I don't know.

You gave me your last bid good-bye,
Said it would be unfair if you stayed,
Truth is I'd rather you lie a thousand times
Than having to live the rest of my life without you,
You knew and didn't care.

What am I to do?
What would you do,
If you were forced to where this shoe,
Oh that's right I know,
You'd say oh well Sh*t happens!


* I just felt I had to write this down because sometimes people can do  the darnest things to others and  then we end up hurt and confused in the end while the one who has afflict the pain upon us lives it up! (lolz)*
