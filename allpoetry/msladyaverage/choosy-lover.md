Choosy Lover


I thought I had my mind all made up,
I was going to say it's through with me and you for i've found another,
But it's too hard I'm torn
What am I to do when I want you both,
I can't just play the field,
Well let's just say I could but i won't.
I love you all, 
How can I be forced to choose,
It's so hard to make this decision I don't want to be  a fool,
For I am afraid that I may make the wrong choice,
How did I get involved with two different guys,
One feeds me things I would love to hear,
The other tells me lies,
you make me smile,
He makes me cry,
You piece my puzzle together,
He breaks them apart,
Yet I tend to forget,
You're one man with two hearts.I just wanted to say that this poem is about a woman who is in love with a man who has split personalities on one day he's nice then the next he's a beast. But either way she still loves him the same.
