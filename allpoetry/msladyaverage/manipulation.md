Manipulation


	I give you...
Pleasure,
happiness,
fulfillment
Love,
Smiles
togetherness,
acception

	You bring me...
Pain,
anger,
depression,
stressing,
tears,
loneliness,
objection,

	I trust you...
Truth
loyalty

	You betray me
Lies,
Deceit


I give you all of me with all honesty, But yet still you manipulate me.
