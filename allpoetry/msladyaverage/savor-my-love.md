Savor My Love


Take my hand,
Lead me to a place,
Where i'll live free from pain,
Free of worries,
And deprivation.

Lend me an ear,
Protect me from harm,
Give me your heart,
Instill your trust upon me,
Free me of my mistakes,
For once believe in me,
You're all that I need now and forever.
