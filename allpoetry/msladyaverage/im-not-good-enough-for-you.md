I'm not good enough for you

Don't waste your heart on me,
I'll only let you down in the end,
I'm not looking for a committed relationship,
I just want a man who'll walk all over me,
You'd never do that to me,
That's why i'm leaving you.
You talk of how you love me,
I know you're speaking from the heart,
And I love you too just not the way you ought to be loved,
I can't show you the love you want to see,
I got so many love scars and painful past experience's within me,
I want to give you all of me but I can't,
The effort you put forward is worthless,
I'm to afraid to open up to you so i punish you for the other jerks.
I'll live my life alone forever to be detained from love
But only  if it meant that I'd never have my heart broken again,
To love i'll forever be unknown,
My heart is too much like a glass,
I'd prefer a stone.


                               *******  This is to my Boyfriend I am not worthy of his love i tend to punish him because the things that happened in my past  yet he still thinks i'm worthy of his love**************
