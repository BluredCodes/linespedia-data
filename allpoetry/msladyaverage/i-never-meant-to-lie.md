---
title: "I Never Meant To Lie"
writer: "MsLaDyAvErAgE"

I keep saying I'm sorry but you tell me it's too late, 
For we've had the discussion and I still made the mistake, 
I was going to tell you that I lied one way or the other,
But you never gave me the chance
Now you'll never trust me again,
I've only added on to your confusion and pain,
I ask myself how can I make it better but there is no way,
My head says give up it's over but my heart says to stay,
You keep saying things will never be the same,
But yet I wonder if that's the way things end,
My intention was never to hurt you,
I know how it feels to be lied to,
But like you said  a lie is a lie,
No matter how you try to polish it up,
That doesn't mean that I will ever give up on you,
For then it'll be giving up on me,
Just thinking of how things would be if there were a you and me,
Hopefully one day you'll come around and realize,
That I never meant to lie
