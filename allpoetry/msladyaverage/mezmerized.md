Tell me you want me,

I bet I want you more.

Tell me you need me,

But I need you  so much more.



My life has become so precious now that you're by my side,

More than words can express you're a hell of a guy,

I never thought that each moment could be spent with thoughts of you,

May it be because I've fallen for you?



It's so amazing how I still feel your warm touch,

Words can't begin to explain what the future has in store for us.

It's as though you have me mesmerized could it be the way,

you walk, talk, smile, or the way you look into my eyes?

I don't know but you have my mind, heart, and body forever paralyzed.
