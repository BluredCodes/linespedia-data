I'm Too Strong For That

You think that you can walk all over me and then expect me to take you back,
but you can't,
You think you got me wrapped around your fingers,
But you don't,
I am my own woman,
I would never play the fool not even for the likes of you,
I'm too strong for that

You tell me lies when in reality your actually lieing to yourself,
When playing a game you are only obligated to the cards that you were dealt,
Keep doing what you do,
You do you,
I'll do me,
There's no one more immune to this than me,
I've been through this more times than I care to count,
I will not let you see me weep,
You're nothing to moan about, 
I'm too strong for that

Don't empathize me,
I'll be fine,
I knew your love was not there to stay,
My love for you will fade with time,
You think you got over,
But in time you shall see,
All the qualities you wanted in a woman,
You had in me,
That's the word from my heart now take it and leave,
I'm too strong for that
