Go!!!

Sit down and talk to me,
Look me in the eyes,
Tell me how you truly feel about me,
Spare me the lies,
It's a shame that I have to read your mind,
Be open with me let me know where we stand,
You won't make it any better by procrastination,
If you're really a man then you'll let me know,
If you're going to love me then love me if not then just go.This is something that I just have in my head about my current boyfriend. I've known him for four and some years now and we just got back together again!!!
