I'm Missing You


My days seem long now,
My nights are short,
Not a minute goes by without you on my mind,
Was it something I did,
Was it something I said,
What made you change,
Are you afraid,
Own up to your past you have to live with it,
I know what it's like to be used,
I know what it's like to be violated and abused,
Now take some time and apply it to you and me,
Then you'll see that i'm missing you and that you're missing me.I just wrote what came to mind at the time cause I write whatever I feel at the moment
