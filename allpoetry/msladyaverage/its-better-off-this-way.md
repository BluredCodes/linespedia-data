It's Better Off This Way


I  thought that things would be different this time,
I thought maybe you'd treat me better,
That i'd really have a chance at loving and being loved,
But I guess that was to much of me to ask.

I saw my life ahead of me,
You gave me reason to live,
Now you're gone and i'm alone,
What have I to live for?

There will never be another that can touch my heart the way I allowed you to,
When you said good-bye I was crushed but it was so easy for you,
How could you walk away from my life and leave me young, sad and blue?
I don't know how you managed to do it but I'm better off without you!



*** This is just something that I felt I needed to write. Never fall in love with someone who isn't willing to love you back. It's a no win situation!***
