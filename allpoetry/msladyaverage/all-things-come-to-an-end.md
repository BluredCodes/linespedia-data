All things come to an end


I see you looking at me like your self satisfied,
Proud of the way you told me those lies,
Promised we'd be together forever,
When you can't take me for who i am,
That's how boys grow into men,
Learn to except the things that you can't change,
By learning to make due with what you have,
I know that I am not perfect,
For even a girl who is perfect to the eyes has a flaw,
Who are you to judge me you're no god,
You thought that I'd be lost without you,
My life has never been more clear,
I thought you had imprisoned my love forever
But all things come to an end
