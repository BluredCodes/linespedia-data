I left you           

You'll never know how much you meant to me,
How you influenced my life in so many ways,
You opened my eyes to the world,
Made room inside of me for change,
I never knew how much negative mortality I had inside of me,
Then you showed me your love,
For it was thick love thin love is no love you see,
How could this be  I admit I was truly foolish,
To think that I could play with your mind,
I was afraid that in the end you'd do it to me,
So I took your heart and left you coldThis is about my ex-boyfriend who I met over the internet i had to leave him he was to good to be true!
