All It TookOne night of chemistry was all it took,Now i'm so hooked,When you speak of another,It makes me jealous,How did you do it, Boy why did i let youYou said you'll never trust another ever again,But how can I accept being just a friend,Knowing what a man you would be if we were together,I won't give up on you,Not now,No never
