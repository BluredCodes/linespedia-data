Ooooh!

Oooh!
I'm hooked on you scent,
Ooh!
How i long to taste you,
Oh!
The way you make me feel when your inside of me,
Whoa!
I can feel you in every inch of my being,
Man!
You full my hole up with yourself,
I'm stuffed by your fullness,
Man shoot there's only one ounce of you left!
