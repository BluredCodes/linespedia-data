Question
			
Why Does it always end this way,
Is the girl always the one to be burnt by the fire,
I never thought that you would be the one to instill such pain upon me,
But I guess I can say I am shocked but not surprised,
See I knew you were going to do this because at one point or another every man does,
I guess I never wanted it to be this soon,
You never even gave me the chance to make you happy,
I would've never disappointed you,
I only wanted  to love you and never to let you go,
But in life we suffer through good and bad times,
And unfortunately you were my downfall
