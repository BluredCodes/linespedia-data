More Than A Friend

I see the look in your eyes,
I feel the warmth in your touch,
It's all in the sound of your voice,
The pain you have in your heart,
The nervousness in your body at my touch,
But still you deny  it,
You deny me,
You deny our love
Is there something that I can do,
Is there something that I can say,
I do think of you in more than one way,
You're not just another man,
Actually you're more than a friendThat's how i feel about someone
