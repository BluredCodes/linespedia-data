I Promise

Yeah you think you got over on me,
You think that I am a fool,
But I would never allow you to kick me when i'm down,
I thought you understood me,
You said that we were just alike,
Two young  people in the need of love,
But somehow you forgot who was always there at your side,
I was your friend,
Your lover,
And a confiding guide,
But you used what I  told you to pull you through your struggles,
Then when timing got rough you just took off,
Now I am the one stuck and left behind,
But I will be alright,
For I shall not cry,
Even if I shed a million tears they won't float you back in my direction,
You are such a disappointment,
 I wanted to spend my life with you,
But one thing is for certain,
Everything you've done to me will come back around to you,
I promiseThis is not supposed to rhyme my emotions are all there in this poem it took me about two minutes to write this because it came straight from the heart.
