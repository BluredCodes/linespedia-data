Curiosity             

I never knew what to think of you,
You were always so oppressive,
I never expected to fall in love with you,
You wanted to give up on the love that brings you life
But I wouldn't let you.
What was it that discouraged you?
Can't you look into the eyes of the one you proclaim to love,
I'd give my all for you is that to much for you or not enough?


	*** Some people are never satisfied you let them have their share of say in things but sometimes that's still not enough***
