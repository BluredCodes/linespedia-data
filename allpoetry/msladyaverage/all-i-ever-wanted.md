All I ever Wanted

It's so hard to believe that you are no longer a part of my life,
I thought that you were here to stay but now I see that I wasn't right,
Lonely nights and days I spend waiting for your return,
Although I know that day will never come,
Why me I ask,
What has become of  my heart,
It's so hard getting over you,
Yet so easy to fall apart,
I thought you loved me,
But I was just your little toy,
Ready to move and listen at your every beck and call,
I thought that if I loved you with every bit of my heart you'd love me back,
Yet I know now I was wrong,
You took what you wanted from me never thinking to give to recieve,
Only giving what was a benefit for you as well as me,
But that's okay one day you'll grow into a man and you'll see,
All I ever wanted was to love you and for you to love me
