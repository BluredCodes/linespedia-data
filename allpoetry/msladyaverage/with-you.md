With You

Can't you see through my eyes,
I would never lie especially if it meant hurting you,
I want to hold you in my arms forever,
Protect you from harm,
Go through all life's changes with you,
Through the good times and the bad,
When you're right and when you're wrong,
Whether i'm happy or sad,
With you is where I want to be
When you're  in your darkest hours,
I'll be the one to pull you through,
Every breath that you take,
every step that you make,
At your side I will always be.I just had to get this out of my head! All  poetry is written due to real lif situations
