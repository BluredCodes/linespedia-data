U Made It This Way!

	

In all honesty,
I wish that I could say,
I'm forever to love you,
And i'm forever to stay,
So if you expect me to say these things,
I am terribly sorry,
I'm not here to lie to anybody,
Is it that you'd rather me lie,
Will it help at all to make me feel that way inside,
No the point is I don't love you,
Quite frankly i'll never love you again,
It was you who secluded me,
Who put shame to my name.


             *** This is to all the people who used me in the past and made me ashamed to be myself and who made me lost all love and respect for myself and I am still trying to work on getting myself back together again.***





Lady Rage
