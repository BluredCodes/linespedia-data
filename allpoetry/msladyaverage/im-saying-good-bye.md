I'm Saying Good-Bye

I met you a short while ago,
It seemed as though,
All my troubles went away that day,
I never thought that we would come to past,
But I guess that was fate's way of doing things,
Did I not show  you enough affection,
Was it all a game in your mind,
There's one thing for certain,
 I will end the battle this time,	
I will not cry over you,
My head will stay held up high,
Out of love and respect for myself,
I'm saying good-byeThis is about a boy who was more than a friend to me but to him I was just another girl on his long list of females.
