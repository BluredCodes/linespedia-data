---
title: "The Mask Which Hides"
writer: "Zanzer"
slug: "the-mask-which-hides"
source: "AllPoetry"

This is the mask
not the real me.
I fear for my life
can't you see?

I made this mask
to shelter myself.
So you see...
This is the mask
not the real me.

This is the mask
not the real me.
I fear you would run
and tell the others.
The others about ME!

So that is why
you won't give me a hand...
because of this mask
I have created for me.
