---
title: "The Dark One"
writer: "Zanzer"
slug: "the-dark-one"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/53659-The-Dark-One-by-Zanzer"
---

He lurks
He hides
He slithers
He slides
He corrupts
He destroys
He lies
He crawls
He laughs
He betrays
He removes
He extracts
He is the Enemy
He is the DARK ONE!
