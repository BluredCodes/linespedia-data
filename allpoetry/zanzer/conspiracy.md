---
title: "Conspiracy"
writer: "Zanzer"

They're all around
Above, below, behind, ahead,
around, under.
They're surrounding me.
They won't give me room to breathe.
It's like they're trying to kill me slowly.
These people with their nice suits,
and flashy ties.
They seem shady yet bright.
Bright enough to kill me without a single touch.
