title: "The Edge of My Blade"
writer: "Zanzer"

I feel anger and pain
So I shall make you feel 
The edge of my blade
No I will not submit to violence
I will attack from afar
With the edge of my blade
You're inhuman
You reck of hatred 
For those that are different
Simply because you are an orphan
It truly is sad 
Picking on others because of your insecurities
And now I am fighting back with
The edge of my blade
So... can you stand the stinging?
