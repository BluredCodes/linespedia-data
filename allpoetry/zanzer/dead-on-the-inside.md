---
title: "Dead On the Inside"
writer: "Zanzer"
slug: "dead-on-the-inside"
source: "AllPoetry"

What could have caused such a thing
For me to be empty
No thoughts, no feelings,
No emotion, no cares,
Is this really me?
No, it can't be.
But what if it is?
Would that be so bad
If I gave in and...
Became completely dead on the inside?This poem was written after looking back at my past. Basically I had become dead on the inside for 3 years of my life. No one could reach me, no one would listen to me, they would only give me the cold shoulder.
