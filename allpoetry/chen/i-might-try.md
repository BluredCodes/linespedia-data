---
title: "I might try"
writer: "Chen"
slug: "i-might-try"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/5575-I-might-try-by-Chen"
---

Confusion drives me insane,You get all you want to know,So UN real ,only on the papers You can express your deepest fears.And I love you so much,Like I never seen no one can be loved,And I know you so good,You cant imagine how muchLike an angel, all my songs for youIf I had to die for you,i am ashamed to sayI might try,As I'm already full with scars inside my heartAnd I don't know why,I think about your smile every second I breathAnd I don't know how,You're not here beside meAs it feels so right ,we both know it,Am I right?
