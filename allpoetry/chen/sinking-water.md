---
title: "Sinking Water"
writer: "Chen"
slug: "sinking-water"

So afraid of failing,So afraid of loving,Knowing the  sky are darkerwhen the sun is shiningShaking as she there beside meI seem to pretend i don't mind about my loving,Because the flowers grow greenerand myself sinking deeperOnce in a while ,My heart plays along the gameMaking other persons stronger,thats the way it seems to bewhen i'm so hurt in meAnd when I'm shaking and her arms beside meSaying she has no feelings for me,I cry inside my sinking water,But find a way to make myself stronger.
