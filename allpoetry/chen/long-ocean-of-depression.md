title: "Long ocean of depression"

How many times I've been toldTo let you goSuffer your pain by yourselfLearn the lesson of life itselfBecause that's the only way you'll learnto becomethe angel you seem to pretend you'reYour beautySmash me to the long ocean of depression,feeling coming closerWith no joy of smileOr spirit of real lifeThe reason of my excitement doesn't depend on you,I shall stopimagining you're the only air i want to breathAnd your breathing leads me to the way of loving you more than lifeitself.
