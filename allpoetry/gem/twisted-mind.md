---
title: "Twisted Mind"
writer: "Gem"
slug: "twisted-mind"

How could you just stand there
As I begged on my hands and knees?
I was only a 10 year old child 
And for you I was supposed to please

Why exactly did you do it?
Did it bring pleasure to your sick twisted mind?
I hope you torture yourself to death
When my body they should find

Do realize that you've taken me from my family
I can see tears welling up in their eyes
Did you really think they wouldn't notice or miss
Their only daughter, their princess, their prize?

In my head I'm thinking of bad things
Some so evil I have to scream
I'd never wish ill on anyone
But you are a different "thing!"

This is one of them situations
That I thought would never happen to me
But it has and it's destroyed more than my life
So congratulations, I hope you're happy.
