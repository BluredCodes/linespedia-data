I've just got back from the gym
I was there for a couple of hours

I could have had a swim
But instead I hit the showers

I'm feeling really fit now
And I'm looking kinda slim

Standing in front of the mirror 'WOW'
My belly's all tucked in.

Sadly my highs turn to a low
As I open cupboard doors

Hundreds of calories hit with one blow
A Snickers between my jawsThis describes exactly what I do when I've got back from the gym
