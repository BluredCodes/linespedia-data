You think you're right 
But you're wrong
You wasn't there 
So be gone
If you're asking for trouble
Come my way
I'll give you what for
If you don't run away
You make me sick
You're all mouth on the phone
I'll warn you now
When you're alone
I'll beat you till you're black and blue
There's no telling what I could do
So if you think you're hard enough
Come try me now
Lets have a scuff
May the best girl win 
That's what I say
You won't live to see another dayI wrote this poem when I was getting grief at 13 years old.
I found out yesterday that this 'Bitch' now wants to be my mate, 3 years too late!
