How can I explain what I feel inside
Something for so long I've tried to hide
I didn't want you to know how I feel
Because I thought it could never be real

I've always thought you were one of a kind
I considered myself lucky for such a find
This great of friend I knew was rare
To keep it going, I knew I had to take care

No matter how much I wanted to say it
Even the perfect words would undermine it
Nothing could say exactly how I felt
I had mixed feelings of the cards I'd been dealt

I've learned to live with just how it is
I know we will always be the best of friends
You have said you too will be there
Many great times together we will share
