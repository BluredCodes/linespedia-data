Shattered
Torn
Watching you walk away
Nothing will ever be the same

Agony
Depressed
Reading your goodbye note
Wishing it had never been written

Angry
Enraged
Seeing you with someone else
Wishing it was me

Sad 
Alone
Needing you by my side
Thinking of you all the time

Hurt
Grieving
Wanting to hold your hand
Longing to taste your kiss

Release 
Falling
Knowing I'll never mean as much
Giving up all hope

Torn
Shattered.......
