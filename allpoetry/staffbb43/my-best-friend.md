title: "My Best Friend"

It seems so strange without you nearYour sweet voice I never hearBut just the thought of you makes me smileAnd sets my mind at ease for a whileYou have always been there for meTo lend a shoulder when I was in needI’ve tried to be there for you tooTo help you out when you’re feeling blueThere is no greater gift in lifeThen to have you by my sideKnowing you will always be thereAnd that you will always careYou’ve touched my life from the startAnd went straight through to my heartYou will always be my friendAnd I’ll be there for you until the endEven when you’re no where aroundYour presence to me is always foundI feel your support where ever I goYou’ll always be with me I knowFor all of these things I thank youAnd for keeping our friendship so trueIt doesn’t matter the time or placeI can always see your beautiful face
