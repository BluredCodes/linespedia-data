---
title: "The Cold Hard Truth"
writer: "staffbb43"

Can't believe what I just saw
You arm around someone else
Although it's been year
I still need you like before
But you obviously don't need me

I want to hold you in my arms
And throw caution to the wind
Wish you loved me
As much as I love you
That you could feel
What my heart feels

I tell myself it's all a dream
I'll wake with you in my arms
Then I realize it's all real
Including this pain inside

I can't bear to see you with anyone else
Because I want you for myself
Somehow I have to move past you
And admit I have no chance

But I can't convince my heart
And face the cold hard truth
