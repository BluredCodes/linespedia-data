Thanks for all that you've given to me,
Love, hope, a reason to believe.
I'm not alone in this rat race
I can count on you to fill the empty space.

I know that we are far apart,
But you still hold a special place in my heart.
Of all the things I have ever seen,
Your beauty shines above everything.

The thought of you makes me smile,
Even when facing life's many trials.
Whenever I am feeling blue,
I close my eyes and think of you.

Your memory is never very far away,
It will always be in my mind to stay.
We have always been the best of friends,
And I hope that friendship never ends.

I do believe in love at first sight,
Because I felt it that first night.
The second that I saw your face,
I knew my life was not a waste.
