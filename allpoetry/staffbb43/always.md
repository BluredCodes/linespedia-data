title: "Always"

I always     Cared about you, even when I hated it     Will love you, even though you will never feel the same     Wanted the best for you, even though it's without me     Was there to comfort you     Felt deep inside you loved me too     Needed you close to me     Wanted to be with you     Led my heart to be brokenYou always     Meant something to me     Tried to make it seem all right     Resisted getting close again     Kept me hoping for a chance     Seemed to be okay with the situation     Made leaving look easy     Were beautiful to me     Broke my heart
