I cannot say how you've changed my lifeOf how you've ended all my strifeWho knows where I would be this dayIf you hadn't shown me the wayI'd like to think I've helped you tooGetting by and getting throughThe obstacles that each day bringsAnd overcoming all the little thingsOur ways I hope will never partSo you will stay close to my heartI knew it when we first beganBeside you I would always stand
