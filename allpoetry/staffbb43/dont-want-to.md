---
title: "Don't Want To"

Don't want to need you
Because I can't have you
Don't want to think of you
Because my desire grows
Don't want to talk to you
Because you would never understand
Don't want you to be happy
Because that happiness doesn't include me
Don't want to see you
Because that makes me need you
Don't want to need you
Because I can't have you
