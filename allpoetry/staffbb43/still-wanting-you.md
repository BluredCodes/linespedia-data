title: "Still Wanting You"

Oh how I rue
The day that I met you
You showed me how fast
Your love would come to pass
You didn't think twice
When you rolled the dice
And gambled on my heart
Then ripped it all apart
Despite all this
I still hunger for your kiss
Want to hold you near me
Close enough that you could see
What I have tried to show
So that I could let you know
What my true feelings are
And how I've carried my love so far
Just to show you how I feel
And that my love is for real
Sadly, though, this will never come
For you love another someone
Someone who doesn't share the same feelings
And has sent your heart reeling
I've been there to comfort your sorrow
But only had your love to borrow
Never will you be mine alone
No matter all the love I've shown
