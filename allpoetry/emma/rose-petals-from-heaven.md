Rose Petals from Heaven

Darkest hour
Fervent prayer
Restless child in despair
A Father hears, a Father sees
And sends upon a gentle breeze

Rose petals from Heaven

Outstretched hand
Breath Divine
Carried by the night mist fine
Wafting slowly, wafting sweet
Landing softly at my feet

Rose petals from Heaven

One by one
They fill the room
Crowding out the blackest gloom
Spirit Fragrant, Spirit Holy
Bringing peace and comfort solely

Rose petals from Heaven

Silky soft
Scarlet red
Each one a drop of blood he shed
Covering body, covering soul
Spirit broken, now made whole
By
Rose petals from Heaven

Grace and mercy
God sent love
Gentle touch of velvet glove
Rocks this troubled child to sleep
In Holy cradle laden deep 
With

Rose petals from Heaven
