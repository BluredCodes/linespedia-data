Solar System

Would you own me then?
Somehow thinking that I am within your grasp
As you turn, they turn
So should it be with me?

You are the center
Bright and burning
All is as you say it is

But
I will not be manipulated to 
Your will
I only gently circle
On the outskirts
And make a pretense

But maybe…
I’ll break free
And yet,
I know the thought of me
Hurling off into the universe
Gone forever
Would bring you no grief

Only…
Neptune 
Would follow suit
And he belongs to you
So  you believe

There is a universe out there
And here I stand
Forced 
Forever a silent witness 
To your white-hot raging

Tiny Pluto
Smallest of planets
(some say not even a planet)
on the edge of the solar system
That she did not create

Trapped by gravity
Caught in your orbit

But we both know 
That I am not here for you
And I do not feel or fear 
Your vengeful heat

Let mercury and venus serve you 
Worship you,Appease you 
Love you
Cursed by their proximity
 You are all they see
Let them burn in your tyranny and wrath
For they will never leave you

But I 
I am out here 
Far from you
Darkness and silence my cover
I am Ice and rock
Unmoved
Except by your distant pull
