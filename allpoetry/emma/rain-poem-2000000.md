Rain Poem #2,000,000

                             Rain

                        Here in the darkness
                        And only here
		        Nothing else

			The only sound I can hear
			The only thing I can feel
			Is the calling of the rain
			
			Don’t move or you’ll break 
			The spell	
			Don’t think or you’ll awaken
			
			Close your eyes
			No one can see
			No one can know
			No one can find you
			Here in the rain 
			 
			Some long dead philosopher raised a 
			Question
			If you can’t see something
			Does it cease to exist?
			Can I close this bedroom  door and know
			That once closed
			Nothing lay beyond it
			Just four walls, a door
			Darkness and the rain

			Forever suspending 
			Eternally paused
	
			 So I close my eyes 
			And try to find some
			Incantation, spell, word
                        Prayer
			To keep my world forever	
			Rain
