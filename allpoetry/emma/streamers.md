FYI:  Streamers are positively charged electrical streams that stand straight up during a storm.  They are everywhere, but are invisible to the naked eye.  They are at the very top of every object - trees, people, cars, etc.  They seek to connect with a negatively charged stepledder to create a lightning bolt.  A stepledder is a negative electrical discharge from a cloud.  When the two connect, a bolt is created and all the negative charge from the clouds is drained to the earth.  This process charges the atmosphere and creates more streamers to connect and create more lightning.  This poem is about this lightning cycle.                             



                                 Streamers
				
				She was created for him
				And in the howling wind and rain
				She stands rigid and alert
				Diligently searching 
				Urgently seeking

				He glances down from the heavens
				And sees her scanning the skies for him
				He considers his options and thinks
				This one will do nicely
	
				In a burst of dazzling light and speed
				He moves
				And tears the night with jagged edges

				The flash jolts her, shakes her
				Cringing, she raises her arms to meet him
				A destiny of conviction												
				In the park, an ancient oak stands
				In a microsecond, she stretches	
				And connects
				
				For an instant, they are one
				And she stands illuminating the darkness,
				Draining the heavens, as she draws    
				The stepledder down
                                For one last kiss

				The bolt goes through her 
				Through the core of the tree
				Incinerating the branches
				Splitting in two what was once whole

				He is finished
				His weakened current ebbs 
				Through the blackened roots of the tree
				Dissipating into the earth
				He returns to the heavens
				Charging the atmosphere as he leaves
				Giving rise to more who will serve his need
				
				A smile plays at the corners of his lips 
				As he glances downwards at his handiwork
				And chooses his next streamer 

				Their gentle, flickering light dots the landscape
				He reads their thoughts as the voice of one
				
				She was created for him
				And in the howling wind and rain
				She stands…..
