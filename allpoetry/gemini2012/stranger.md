Dear Stranger,I never knew you could exsist,Hi Stranger,It was a clue I must've missed,What do I call you stranger,brother, friend, your name,Do I know you stranger?You've been showered in fame,I never knew I had you stranger,I could see you a bit,Did you know I was there stranger,I can't just smile and sit,How are you stranger,I just forgot to ask,Yes I'm fine now stranger,You've found me at a task,Stranger is there something,Eatting you inside,You can tell me stranger,I don't want us to hide,We've found each other,And didn't know,Now we have each other,Whats to show,Listen to me now stranger,Now we know each other,We can stand now,Sister to brother.
