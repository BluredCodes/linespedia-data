title: "All That I can't have"

I've never shead a tear,
For your horrid soul,
I've never hurt a bit,
For the heart that I can't hold,
I've never thought of you as an Horrid man,
But I know you show that you hate all I have,
You can take my few last dollars,
My house is in your name,
But please don't take my heart,
and use it for your game.
