The clouds surrounded the moon tonight,

And looked at him as I've done before,

This him he looked down at me,
His eyes were in a glow,
I smiled at him,
My heart feel low,
I could dare get close to him,
Afraid of what I'd do if inside,
His arms close to his face,
Living in his sweet imbrace,
He looked at me,
And kissed my hand,
And in my thoughts,
Oh What a man!
I smiled and bowed,
And said hello,
But now my dear I have to go,
This moon was far from what I'd seen,
But my time would come you wait and see.
