To every lover who has a heart,3 don'tTo every drugie,4 aren'tTo every poet,20 aren't (as talented!)To every love you lose,40 getTo every rich person,700 aren'tTo every person who has a dollar,100 don't,To every person who goes to school,75 don't,To every time you say you're want something,250 have nothingand to every time I blink,I think of you
