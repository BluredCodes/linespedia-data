It's quiet,
To quiet to be where I live,
You never know,
who's behind the next corner,
waiting to stab your back,
you await the knife,
yet it never comes,
YOu think that they,
are behind the corner,
waiting till your weak,
BUt they never come,
it's as if their getting meek,
Silence Silence,
ringing in my ear,
are they going to kill me,
of is it all of fear?
Questions
