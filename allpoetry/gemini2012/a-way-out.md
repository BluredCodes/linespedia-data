---
title: "A way out"
writer: "Gemini2012"
slug: "a-way-out"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/15700-A-way-out-by-Gemini2012"
---

My Dad drinks his problems away,
He drinks away his fears,
Mommy says we mustn't stay,
Then bursts into tears,

My Dad drinks his life away,
He drinks away his dreams,
Mommy says just one more day,
And fills our hearts with screams,

My Dad drinks my hopes way,
He drinks away my laughter,
Mommy says we must go away,
To live happily ever after
