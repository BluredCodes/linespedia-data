War is a lonely place,
It's filled with pain and sarrow,
Without a happy,
Day tomorrow.
Can't you only find a heart,
And make sure that,
a war won't start,
Do you have a mind of good,
OOf course anyone would,
Now hold this dager in your hand,
And try to be a man,
try to stab me in the heart,
If you think you can,
This is the blindness,
Of a war,
Can't you see it burning,
Why don't you open up that door,
Is there anything conserning,
The way a war can kill,
Or are you another one,
Who cares less to nill.
