Silver she dances across the moon,

Her love and her heart shall gide her sweet way,

HIding from love it's all for herself,
Not willing to love or even share wealth,

No Silver is diffrent she's not all to pure,
She will soon show you she has no cure,

Her heart has been sent of keeping her pride,
Her smile is standing and shinning well wide,

Silver dear childe you're not what I thought,

YOu're in your own life,
Silver oh silver to none you're called wife.

Silver you're hear's not perfect or gold,
But silver oh silver here you stand bold,
