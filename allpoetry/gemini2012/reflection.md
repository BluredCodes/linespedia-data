You see a person you don't understand,
A person who's never in demand,
Someone who has the same exact face,
It always makes my mind race,
She always listens to what I say,
Never says a single word,
Never says that I'm obserd,
She listens very closely,
As I tell her every time,
She's a wonderful listener,
But the reflections still mine.
