It's something no one can replace,It's something of true grace,When things sometimes don't go your way,Your friends will be there come what they may,They'll always find it in their hearts,To forgive you and do their parts,To trust with always haope and faith,They're few things on earth so great,So treasure friendship from the start,And pray that it won't fall apart,For it will always leave a smile,This speical love makes life worth while.
