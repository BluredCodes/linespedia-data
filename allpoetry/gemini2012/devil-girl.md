The walls are red,Her heart is coal,Alone with pain,Lost in her soul.She kills to kill,She hurts to hurt,She dies to die,She burns to burn.She slits her wrists,She fights with her fists,She cannot make a friend,She's dead to the end,This ghoast girl isn't,What you think,Memories are always,Blank,YOu're scared of her,She's scared of you,And as the blood drain,YOu know you're through,I leave you now,With you inflicked pain,As your blood,will slowly drain.She takes the brush,and paints the sky,In the end,to the heavens high
