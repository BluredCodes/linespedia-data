title: "The Night I meant you"

I'm remembering the night I meant you,Even then I didn't have a clue,You gave me a friendly smile,Then we sat and talked just for a while,I could tel you were a nice guy,When I looked into your soft blue eyes,And now I am beginning to see,That you're far more than I thought you'd be,You've given me friendship, respect and love,Sometimes I'm convinced you're from above,I wasn't looking for love that's true,But love is what I found when I found you.
