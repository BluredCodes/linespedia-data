It was the night of the Blue Moon,
I walked down the Isle,
listening to my sisters Tune,
He watched me walk close to him,
I smiled at the thought of My moon man,
I slowly looked at Kim,
She was holdin the flowers,
All in a neat boque,
I really know she wants,
to go out side and play,
I stepped up beside him,
His face was all i see,
A blue Moon wedding,
Was odd enough,
My sister thought I was very tough,
She said I'd be scared for eternity,
For amrry a moon man,
That no one could be,
I smlied at my sister adnd said to her,
You don't understand,
I love him girl,
I pushed my blode hair,
Out of the way,
We said our I do's,
and kissed away....
