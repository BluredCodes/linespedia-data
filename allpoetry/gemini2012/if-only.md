If only I was in your arms,Sheltered from the night,My life would be just perfect,All would be just right,But you don't know my name,Not that I exist,So why do I still love you?Why do I persist?Our lives are different,Our paths don't cross,But when I think of you,My heart feels a loss,I wish we were a two,Just me and you,But still,I'll always love thee,
