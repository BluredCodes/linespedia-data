TEARS
Are you my shelter from the rain?
Are you the truth that never came?
To take away my shelfish fears,
And all my follish shame,

TEARS
Is what makes sadness bad,
And happiness, glad,

TEARS
Are what I call,
My sadened waterfalls,
Of denile,
Of hurt,
Of pain,
Of fire,
But tears,
Are what guide me,
though life,
