Color me a picture,
Draw me a pretty face,
Draw with me for forever,
I'll stay in your embrace,
I don't like the other crayons,
But you color them too,
You'll someday use me up,
That day is truely true,
The other pretty crayons,
are better without sight,
And if I'm your speical crayon,
You can Color me all night,
