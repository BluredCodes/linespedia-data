A true friend listens to what you say,
Lifes a sculpture friends are clay,
Friends are notes in lifes great song,
A melody that carried you along,
Wether it's joy or fear,
When ever life is hard their near,
In a world of darkness,
Friends are light,
So thank your lucky star tonight,
When life get hard and goals seem far,
It's then you'll know who,
your tue friends are
