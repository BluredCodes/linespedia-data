---
title: "Best Friend"
writer: "Gemini2012"
slug: "best-friend"

You never understood me not a tiny bit,
But yet it never bothered me I never could just sit,
I always was a loud mouth and never had it straight
I'll know you for forever when heaven opens her gates,
You think of me a fly in the web of your domain,
But once you really look at me my life was truely plain,
I'll never ever hate you and I'll love you just the same,
You are my very best friend in god's holy name
