Happiness isn't everything,
I found that out when He sliped on that ring,
Love is swift and love is sweet,
But if you're lucky you own't get beat,
Now listen here I've been through cold,
I've fallen fast and hit when bold,
I've loved and losted I've loved and fallen,
But now I know no one else is callin,
Now you watch your mouth you stupid child,
He'll hit you harder when you mouth
