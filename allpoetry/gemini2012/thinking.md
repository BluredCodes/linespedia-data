In my heart I'm thinking,
What am I to do,
In my heart I'm thinking,
Only about you,
You cannot keep your mouth shut,
Your posture isn't straight,
And what the hell did you say,
on our secound date?
The way you alway back talk,
The way you never say,
Ever time you stalk me,
in my heart I'm thinking,
What am I to do,
When I've some how madly,
Fell in love with you.
