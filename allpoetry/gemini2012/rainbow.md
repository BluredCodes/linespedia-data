I wish upon a rainbow,on every single hue,And pray with my entire heart,that one wish shall some true,I wish upon it's color,that together shines so bright,And if a single hue was missing,it wouldn't look quite right,The key to it's beauty,It's the way each color blends,If only the world were a rainbow,Then we could all be friends,I wish we could learn a lesson,From this beautiful bond in the sky,Together we could make world peace,The least we could do is try.
