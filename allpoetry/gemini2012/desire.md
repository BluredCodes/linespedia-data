Some say,I am defenceless,Against earths heavenly fire,But am I urning,With earths desire?Could it be,My life is changing?Yet so is my heart.Or am I,Moving so quickly to start,I fight against,earths wind,and earths heavenly fire,To find what in life,I desire,What I hope,What I wish,What I pray,My desire,Will guide my way,Desire is life,I have to live,Yet though life,YOu have got to win....
