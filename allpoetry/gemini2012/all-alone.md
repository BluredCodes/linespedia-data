I sit upon the roof alone,
With nothing left to do,
I cannot seem to think,
Of anything but you,
I wish you would've took me,
far away with you,
And if you took me with you,
I'd knew our love is true,

I'm kinda wishing now,
You'd have left my heart alone,
Because Now I'm darkness,
Fill me to the bone,
I'm sitting rocking back and forth,
Dieing all alone,
