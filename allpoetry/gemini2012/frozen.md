Don't die on me now!When I need you the most,You are my rock,And I am your coast,Don't leave me alone,When I'm so happy here,I'm much much braver,When you're standing near,I won't keep on crying,I move on forever,Knowing taht your not dieing,Alone in the cold,I'm trying to cope now,I'm to frozen to hold,My tears are like ice now,My worries are tears,Crying a river,Decending my fears,Kissing the air now,Smiling alone,I'm feeling a chill,From my skin to my bone,Why are you leaving,Me alone in the cold,How will I survive life,With out you to hold,My tears are freezing,Hell seems bright,I don't want you lost,Don't kiss me good-night,Miss me forever,Hurt me like time,I want you so much,If you only you'd stay mine,Don't die on me now,And leave me alone,I'm now slowly freezing,My heart to my soul....
