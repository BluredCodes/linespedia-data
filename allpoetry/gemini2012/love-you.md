Winds are stirring,Through the sea,Everything is,over me,My tears have fallen,My heart and dreams,And you are falling,With everything,I've fallen into,the deepest sea,And crying has gotten,used to me,Lost in,The will of your way,Nothing could stop,Yesterday,Maybe I'm lost,In your eyes,Everything is wrong,When we finally say good-bye,Can't keep pushin,Pullins to far,Maybe if we hush now,We'll know how we are,Love is no stranger,to the heart,YOu'll never fall,if we part,No love is strong enough,To survive the sea,And I can't help,You loving me,Let me loose my wonder,Never let me hurt,For another,Let me ryhme,Through our time,Love you,For the rest of my life.What seas what shores what grey rocks and what islands What water lapping the bow...
