I know now what you're telling me,
I know now what you see,
I now know the hate you hide inside,
That can't hold in from me,

You cannot tell me I'm lost,
You cannot tell me you're the boss,
You cannot hurt me anymore,
You cannot send me out the door,

I've tryed to hide you told me lies,
I've hurt my friends I wish to die,
I've killed my soul I've screamed in pain,
I've hurt, I've loved, I've gone insane,

You can tell me foolish lies,
You can make my heart feel cries,
Now you see me, now you don't
If you hurt me, No I won't

Leave me with my follish ways,
So I can see brighter days.
