---
title: "Stay"
writer: "Gemini2012"
slug: "stay"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Here fall the peices to my heart,
Don't look back or even start,
If you wish to hold my hand,
Well stand up and be a man,
The way you act is to far to fly,
Or ever even to pass me by,
I know that our love was true,
But now there's nothing we can do,
I'll sit and watch our lake of fire,
Melt away with deep desire,
And when you look my into eye,
I'll slowly croon a lulliby,
And as you slowly walk away,
I'll hold our love and hope you'll stay
