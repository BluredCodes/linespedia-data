You've always said to me smiling,
That you want to die silent,
I always reply that you won't
I'll be crying,
You always laugh at my emotions,
For I have what you don't,
And I'll be crying for you,
Even if for me you won't,
Don't tell me that it's all ok,
Don't tell that your fine,
And when you die,
I'll die with you,
Becuase you're the only friend,
That's true.
