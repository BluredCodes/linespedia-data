A cold dark heart,A ice cold hand,A hurt a pain,That's in demand,A love that's dead,A hurt thats said,To kill,To Sudece,To Die,To Bruise,A hurt,A pain,A hole,A drain,No love,No hope,No laughter,No folk,I have smiple words,For my simple pain,That almost driving,Me insane,My smiple words,My heart of coal,My dear lost friend,This world must end,Fire in blood,To the one you love,Death to the heart,Do never part,My simple words,For my simple pain,and as I fall,Into the pit of insane...
