You say you'll always love me,
Until the end of time,
But that's just on the surface,
Your love is such a crime,

We only ment the other day,
You're playing with my head,
I know that your just saying that,
To get me into bed,

I'm sure you have done this,
To many girls before,
They say that they don't want to,
But you still push for more,
I've told you how I feel right now,
And you just look away,
You say that I'm the girl for you,
In each and everyway,

But I know deep inside me,
What you're all about,
We've only ment a few days ago,
Yet I know you inside out,

Your only after one thing,
And you think that your all set,
But until you find respect for me,
That's on thing you won't get.
