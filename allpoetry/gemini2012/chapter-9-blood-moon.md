I got there extra early,
And stood in our sweet spot,
And I saw him appear with the mists,
And hoped he'd fall in love,
It was the Blood moon tonigh,
And death was on your door,
But I didn't give a crap,
I loved my moon man more,
I saw him walk up,
But he wasn't smiling,
like I wasn't standing here,
I sat down right beside him,
I whispered in his ear,
I leaned my head upon his shoulder,
and told him all my love,
And hope that I was the girl,
He was dreaming of,
He looked at me,
Know I had a secret to tell,
I knew he knew it but oh well,
I looked at him so deeply,
And sighed,
My mother wanted me married,
by the Blue Moon,
And I was really hoping that it would be you,
I'm gonna smile like nothing's wrong,
talk like everything's perfect,
act like it's just a dream,
and pretend he's not hurting,
But lets not ruin it,
He smiled back at me,
I guess I you know my answer,
Of course,
I say I do,
And now I knew his love for me,
was accually true,
He held me in his arms now,
Sheltered from the storm,
And his is embrace now,
I was unintesionally warm....
