I set off early,To my moon man,This was the Moon we'd plan our wedding,When he'd meet my mother,My family trailed behind,I ran into the arms of my moon man,He whispered you're always in my mind,My sisters jaw drooped,And there I heard,Her ego Pop,Kyle this is my Mother, My sister and My brother,This is my father,My cousin, uncle and don't bother,He smiled at me silently,And shook they're every hand,The night we had last shared,Was dancing in my hollow head,I felt tied to go to bed,Everyone left slowly,It was still middle night,I sat down right beside him,Just to hold him tight,i smiled into his deep eyes,And kissed his soft sweet lips,As he put his hands on my hip,I couldn't push away,I loved him to damn much,And this is why I say,I'll never hate him much
