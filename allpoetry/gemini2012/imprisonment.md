faces are pale,Sings the nighting gale,Hurt's only pain,That'll drive you insane,Imprisionment is nice,If you can roll the dice,moonlight fly by nightthe sight of sightthe plight of nightsun fall downgo round and roundget too tipsy and i fall downlife's a dreamwith a hint of gleamfend off flamesof war and hateburning lives of dark debatean unsure and questionable fateit seems to be a little latethe end is nearor to far awaywho am i to really saythe stars they stayremembering lives from far awayinside up above they mock methey lock meinto the great beyondpearly gatesi know noneif i saw them i would runthe stars are free up as far as you seei wish not to be enclosed by these gatesHere alone where everyone hatesa prision cell for eternity someday i shall be free to roamtransparentness my eternal home
