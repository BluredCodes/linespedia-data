An angel has fallen,From the heaves,To the earth,Far from her home,She comes to wander,Her heart is limp,Her soul is burt,Her love is gone,She's way to hurt,When she fell,She fell hard,Into her hurt,Hurting and crying,laughing and lieing,God didn't want her,She thinks to herself,He knew she was dark,Through all of her health,He sent her to earth,To help all the bad,She didn't let it happen,She felt so sad,The stars stoped shinning,The moon when dull,The sun doesn't shine,And shes not whole,She crys to herself,She's not praying at night,What's she saying,Nothings gone right...She smiles at her son,And walks to the bocouny,She stands at the edge,Feels so lonely,She jumps off the bocouny,Hoping she could fly,Hitting the pavement,Ready to die...
