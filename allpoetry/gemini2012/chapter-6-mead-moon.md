The crops were finally finished,
My work here was done,
It was nearly fall time,
And I couldn't hardly wait,
I ran right to the field,
Without a short delay,
I saw the moon shine brightly,
Upon is moon light face,
And I was dreaming of him,
HEart warming embrace,
I slwoly walked beside him,
My moon man was near now,
That's all I could ever ask,
I put my arm in his now,
And leaned against his arm,
You never told me your name,
He said with pleasing charm,
My name is Haruko,
I'm glad that you ask,
I wonder why you only come,
at every single task, (Moon)
He didn't answer the question only smiled so bright,
I loved the way the light,
hit his deep blonde hair,
I knew he probly had a wife,
And many many kids,
But in my deapest darkest dreams,
I know that I am his,
He looked at me and smiled,
I didn't care to dream,
It's as if he were reading,
My every little thought,
And now that I knew his name,
I knew the fates had saught,
I looked at him unwilding,
And looked into his eyes,
Now we both knew out names,
And we'll play no more games,
The moon man was my man,
No matter what they say,
I'll see you at the next moon,
I lve you in every way.
