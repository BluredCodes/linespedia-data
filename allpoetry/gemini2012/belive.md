Belive in yourself,
When everyone thinks your dumb,
Belive in your family,
Because your the shinning one,
You have your goals and dreams,
Make them all your own,
Never let your friends tell you,
What you have all alone,
You are what you will,
We'll love you all the same,
And when you make a mistake,
don't think your the one to blame,
YOu learn from your mistakes,
And live with all your heart,
And when you do something wrong,
It's not like life will stop,
You are what you dream,
You are what your belive,
And you can be whatever,
You want to be
