I'm not a devil,I'm not a saint,Sometimes I make,My best friends faint,With all the things,I do to me,There's just so much,You just don't see,You think I'm An angel,That's sent from above,But obviously,I don't share that same love,What you think Is so much different,From what I know,Some times I wish,That My life could go,Now that I finally,Have it threw my thick skull,I know now,That your love is full,You sent me to some place,To make me stop these,Terrible things,But you never know,What tomorrow beings
