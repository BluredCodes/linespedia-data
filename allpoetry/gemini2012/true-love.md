In the night,
In my heart,
I feel a love,
They shouldn't start,
I feel a burning,
Deep in me,
And everything,
Is feeled with glee,
I look into your soft,
Blue eyes,
and have no deep dark,
Hidden suprise,
The way you look,
The way you touch,
No way in hell,
Could this be lush,
