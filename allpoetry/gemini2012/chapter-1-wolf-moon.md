I walked out onto my door step,
And saw the moon shown so bright,
I loved the tint of the moon,
Unto the silky night,
I walked past the lake gaurd,
And into the forest deep,
To find a way to get,
A gental closer peak,
I steped into the river,
That's water was calm and slow,
I walked to the moon gaze,
As I ruffled my bow,
I saw a tall faint figure,
In the mists of the moon,
And as I saw it walk away,
I knew the legends were true,
I heard a wolf call to the moon,
As I have heard before,
But this time it was diffrent,
Because it didn't howl any more,
And as I walked back to my house,
I saw the figure wave,
I saw a smile creap upon his face,
And felt my young heart cave.
