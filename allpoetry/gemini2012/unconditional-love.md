Didn’t you know What I was going to say,
When you new life was in our way,

Leaving the woods to go to you now,
Can’t we find love though bleed in doubt,

Life has me hidden love shelter me,
Hiding beyond what people can’t see,

Loosing myself if what not and who,
How should I die when I’m bleeding for you.
