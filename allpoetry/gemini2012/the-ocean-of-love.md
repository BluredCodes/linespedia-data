title: "The ocean of Love"
writer: "Gemini2012"

He walks a mist the stars of night,He beams a bright celestial light,To set my lonely heart a flame,At the mention of his name,And helplessly I float away,I'm swept along confusions Bay,I'm drifting past the shore of chance,Where happy heart together dance,A whirlpool of forbidden thought,Where loves strong current has me caught,I smash upon the rocks of doubt,As I'm shaken up and about,I'm trapped within a sea of fears,I'm rocked by violent waves of fears,My boat is broken by the tide,My longing passion all subside,I'm emptied in a pool of calm,As the water sweeps my palm,Away into the thoughts of loss,And we as a pair twist and toss,And on the backs in angels light,His smile illuminates the darkest night
