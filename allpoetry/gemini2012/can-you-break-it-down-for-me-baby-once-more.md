Can you break it down to me baby once more,tell why we gotta break down to the floor,My hearts broken my tears ran dry,Baby tell me now do you wanna make me cry,Can you break it down to me baby once more,Tell me why you wanna show me to the door,Were you cheating on the love we had,Why you gotta make me feel so sad.Can you break it down to what's left of my heart,The small little bit you didn't tare apart,The small little bit that I thought that I lost,Hope that now baby you can find your own loss
