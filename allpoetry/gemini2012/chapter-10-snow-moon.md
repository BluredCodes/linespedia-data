The snow was beautiful,
Like a white palace,
Calling me out,
To dance,
I ran through the snow,
Just to get to my moon man,
I found him waiting for me,
I smiled,
I ran into his arms,
He smiled at me and said,
On the blue moon,
I will no longer be moon man,
I will live with you from then on,
I said you're what you can,
But you'll always be,
My moon man,
He smiled at me without a word,
For I was as happy,
as a bird,
Tonight was the first Moon,
Of a winters Dream,
And she was to live it,
By all means,
He lay her down,
In his arms she was,
And they were,
Deeply madly in love,
A breathless peice,
Of fleash they were,
through the night
Of long winters snow,
