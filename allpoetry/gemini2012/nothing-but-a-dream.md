life is nothing but a dream,What it is it doesn't seem,YOu never know your path untold,When you act alone to be so bold,You think that life is reaity,.When you are always tucked in a dream,And when you wake up for your life,You'll die alone without a dime,And when you see the reasons why,You'll be alone to weap and cry
