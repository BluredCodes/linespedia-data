a Mustange, Rides in the ashes that fall,Circling dust,An terrible call,Deadly voices in my head,Call out,Unto the dead,Ride poor mustange,Ride the wind,Run forever,Threw lifes sin,Run black mustange,Wild and free,Forever beautiful,For eternity,Keep you pace,To run from time,Run dear mustange,Free to be prime,Make your life,As pure as gold,So no trainer,Has your hold,Run free mustange,Happily,And someday,My heart might tame thee.
