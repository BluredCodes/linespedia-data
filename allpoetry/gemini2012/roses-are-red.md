Roses are red,Violets are blue,There's no way in hell,I could live without you,You can't understand it,Well neither can I,But when I look at you,My emotions run high,My hands get all shaky,My my eyes turn wet,And I want you to bad,Hey wanna bet,Roses are red,Cornals are yellow,I know from your eyes,You're my kind of fellow,I'm not going to hate you,If you turn me down,For if you hurt me to much,You're not the love that I've found.dont fall in love withsomeone you can livewith fall in love withsomeone you can't livewithout
