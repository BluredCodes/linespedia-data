A wilted Bouquet,
On my dresser drawer,
A lieing note,
torn on the floor,
A girl with tears,
Running down her cheek,
A boy with another,
girl to seek,
'Don't give me flowesr!
They'll only die!
Why tell me you care,
When it's only a lie?
I don't need your love,
That last just one day,
I don't need your affection,
Or that kind of play,
As much as you've already done,
You've hurt me to much,
To be the one
