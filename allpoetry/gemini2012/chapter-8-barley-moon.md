It was finally the next moon,
I raced to the fields,
I knew I was missing,
my sisters favorite tune,
I finally found him waiting,
In the exact same spot,
He smiled at me,
He was so hot,
I smiled at him and said,
Next mounth is Blood Moon,
Will you still be here,
He smiled and nodded at me,
I smiles and hugged him tight,
I knew that someday he would come,
And make everything right,
he place his arms around me,
And looked right into my eyes,
I looked right back,
And saw no hidden suprise,
In his eyes,
I saw his love,
He was holding for me,
And then he whispered in my ear,
Slow and gentaly,
When tomorrow starts without me,
Don't think we're far apart,
Cause everytime you think of me,
I'm right there in your heart,
I smiled at the thought,
And whispered back in his ear,
I'll love you for all time,
That night when I left him,
I felt much much better,
Now I'm writting every moment,
letter to letter,
