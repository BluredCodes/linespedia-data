When you told me we were through,
I smiled just to look at you,
And then I walked away from love,
Because you're sent from up above,
Now I say aloud,
The goddess must've loved you,
And Vowed to make you sweet,
She must've made you diffrent,
Yet kinda unique,
She must've wanted you happy,
because you're away from me.
