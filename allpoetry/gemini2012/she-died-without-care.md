She walks up to her friends coach,
And asks him for her home work,
He looks at her as if he didn't know,
What this was for,

She cleared her throat and frowned,
'You really didn't hear?
About the reck and,
the reason melly isn't here?

She was in this big oh giant reck,
she feel and nearly broke her neck,

She was with her friend who was really depressed.
her life was dead and her heart a mess,

So she decided to commit suicide,
so that in heaven she may hide,

thank goodness they came out ok,
A 4X4 came at their way,

Melly is in a coma,
and the other girl came out ok,
they're putting her in a hospital,
that's where she'll stay,

they say she waited for the reck,
they said suicide was on the deck,

they also said she hated life,
and it's all their fault,'
she yelled as her screams,
stabbed them all with knives.

'this is the worst news to hear,
from her former best friend,
but you know you can visit her,
room 404 at the end.'

Her class came to listen,
and heard every word,
they kinda felt bad,
for making fun of her.

The coach shivered.
'was there really blood?'

The class walked off with laughter,
she was soon forgotten,
she died later that nice,
no one even cried then.

Her spirit sleeps uneasy,
to know they didn't care,
only one of them had flowers,
laying for her to stare.

You know it's kinda mean,
they didn't know her soul,
And without her imagination,
their whole world isn't hole.you need to except people for who they are not what you think they are. get to know them and if you don't you never know you might've been in that car and awaiting the reck. but you know what you never know that clam silent kid you think laughs at the jokes and tricks you play on them, you never know the next day might be your end.
