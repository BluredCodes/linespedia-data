A mist my heart,Is a lonely song,One that I have found,So wrong,Longing forever,Hurting like me,Living a mist,The heart broken sea,Seat up the bow,Cut up the sail,I wouldn't need them,Once I'm in hell,I know I'm in pain,I won't be back,I'm happy to die,Just for that,The storm brew faster,The winds drew cold,And I was still freezing,With no one to hold,My destiny was told,My heart was going limp,And in this world,Of the annoying drip,I walk to the head,Of this mexican ship,The pain is driving,Straight threw my small hip,I don't care of life,I will soon find my death,In this unearthly world,Of myth,This is all I can do,Even thought you know,What's true.
