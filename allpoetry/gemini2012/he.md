title: "He"
writer: "Gemini2012"
slug: "he"
source: "AllPoetry"

I try my best to keep my friend safe,
but my efforts are futile.
He tells me not to worry about him,
but I can't help it.
I care deeply for him,
and want only the best for him.
He is a good friend,
and I do not know what,
I would do if anything,
happened to him.
