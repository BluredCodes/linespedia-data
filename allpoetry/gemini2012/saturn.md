Silence is deadly,
Like a thron in my boot,
I'll tell about Saturn,
As if I gave a hoot,
Saturn was a angry girl,
And a thron in my side,
Her eyes were dark and pretty,
Her Fear was miles wide,
Black was her favorite color,
And Chill her middle name,
When she married my father,
She covered me in shame,
She looked away at everything,
When she finally came,
She fills my life with inflicted pain,
Her hands are pure energy drains,
She tells my father,
Dirty lies,
That makes the woman,
To my despise,
I finally smile at my mothers grave,
As she whispers please be brave,
I softly kiss the grave stone,
Then slowly walk away...
