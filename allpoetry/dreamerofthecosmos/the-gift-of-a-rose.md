~






One single solitary rose
one stem 
        three leaves
                   and one beautifully perfect bud
                         just one thorn
But what could this mean
     the color itself tells you a little
 Whether red
            yellow
                  cream
                       white
                             or a combination
     But it is the giver who must tell it's true meaning

This tiny token can mean so much to one small girl
       this tiny token on any day becomes a blessing
            knowing someone cares for her would 
              help her feel human 

 A tiny token could me so much to anyone,
                           though some receive it more
                                     than others.
 

 Some would take it for granted, some would cherish the 
       tiny token.

To give the gift of a rose would be to show someone 
   you care
        Or to break a heart to not.


        If such a token is given let there be love 
                   along with it.





~


   




Happy Valentine's Day to all who have someone. And to those who don't I'm right in that seat with you. Longing for someone to hand me a tiny token.*tear*
A gift of a rose is the tiny token I long for this Valentine's Day from someone besides my parents.  I mean I love them and all. It's just I wish someone else cared enough for me to give me such a lovely little token.
