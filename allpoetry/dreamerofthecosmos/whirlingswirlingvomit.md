~




Wandering about the house aimlessly

in a delusional state of deslexic jittery

a cough & a hack, cold chills

swath me in a bath of bodily fluids

fluid which excretes itself from every pore of me body

"I need to A-CHOO." is said by the Giggling Nihilist
in the distance of my fogged contaminated, nausinated head

laying on the tilted, exhaustingly cold flooring of the lavatory

after a wretching cough made me fall when trying to vomit.
