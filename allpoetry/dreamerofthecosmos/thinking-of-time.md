Today is a day that will become yesterday.
Yesterday was once today and even a tomarrow.
Tomarrow is a today and yesterday.
So everyday is all day,
  next week will become a last week.
But it's what we expect from these day, what we hope to do,
          and wish to have taken is what makes the hours,
                   minutes,
                         seconds
                                become the         
                PAST........
                          .........PRESENT.............
                                                     ...........and FUTURE.This has been called Dime-Store poetry by a friend of mine. I really don't believe she found the true messege.  It's just an explination. Something more, but I don' think I've truly expressed it well.
