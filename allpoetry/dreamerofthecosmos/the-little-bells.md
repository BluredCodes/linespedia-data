The tiny bells fall so softly


As they play their little songs

quietly humming away as the filter about the sky
The soft pitter patter of them landing

the almost silent tinkle, as the lovely little bells sing
sing a song in the cooling midnight's moonlight
Collecting about hither and yon

blanketing the land in a cool and loving hug 
Wrapping one's self in this serene picture as it develops into a choir of little bells
night progresses and by the morn there are the remains of the classical natural song played by the bells covering the grounds


Snow has come to my townThere was a beautiful snow fall in my neighborhood last night and i thought I would share it with everyone.
