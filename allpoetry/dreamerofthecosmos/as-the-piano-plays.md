As the tiny pitter patter of the tiny chorus of bells 
is but a fleeting memory, 
I now listen to the piano's blues.

Beginning with the white gleam slowly turning blue then gray
Soon soft sounds play all around.
The soft blanket of bells is soon changed into a sheer silky sheet.
Playing along here and there. As tiny fingertips touch everywhere.

How is it the bells can be so warm and inviting
while the piano is cold? The Blues calms the soul 
as I rest here against a wall. 

Watching the piano play its song at my feet.
