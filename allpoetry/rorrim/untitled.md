At night I stand under streams
			Falling endless, upon
		   		the back of my neck
			  the top of my head
		  as I try to wash myself away.
		Lost in the constant noise
				I pray only to leave the world behind
		  as they have left me.
			Water enshrouds my head
		 and even the noise is lost
		    as reality slips away
		  for a heartbeat
			all is still and I
			    sleep.

		After
			finished, I turn
		  cut off the noise
				and begin
				   living.
