There she is!
			Walking this way?
		Nervous I stand, wait
				wringing mental hands
			hoping she'll come my way
	She's so beautiful
			kind but cold
				A mystery
	    She's here!  I
			can't believe it but it's as if
	I am not
			   Only a shadow by her side
	   What to say
			   How to stand
					Everything is fumbled
	So I wait
		And eventually
				she leaves
	   I watch her back
			My heart aches
		  I don't know what for.
