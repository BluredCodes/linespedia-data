Beauty is
				multifaceted
		shimmering in sunlight
			    melting in rain
	     A body, a smile
			 three kind words and a wave
	The ocean and trees
			Carefully sculpted clay
			    and painted paper
		   Towering buildings
			   blinking lights
	Equations
			that
				  run
					    across
							   the
								      page
	The touch of a hand
				on a sleeping brow
		love of the action
			    is beauty
