If there were ever 
One thing I 
Truly enjoyed,
It would be watching 
A boy concentrate.
The way his eyes
Shift and squint, his
Breath stands still,
Lips partially open,
Sometimes closing,
To indicate deep thought.
I would like to know
What's going on 
In his sweet head... 
If only for a moment.
