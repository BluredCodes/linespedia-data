Be certain to forget about the :

~Love gone wrong
~Mistakes you've made
~Rotten things you've had to say
~Silly drunken one night stands
~Tumor ? nope just swollen glands

Remember that :

~At least you've loved
~I've learned from them 
~It's said and done
~It was a good idea at the time
~Don't smoke so much and you'll be fine/00,
