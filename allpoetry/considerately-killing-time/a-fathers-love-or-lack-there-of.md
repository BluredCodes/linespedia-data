---
title: "A Fathers Love (or lack there of)"
writer: "considerately killing time"
slug: "a-fathers-love-or-lack-there-of"

You scream in my face
Telling of your regrets 
Mostly of the day I was born
I've endured your beatings
Swallowed your insults
Watched as you hospitalized
The only mother I have
Only to be left in your care
To be put through the same thing
To a lesser extent
I've forgiven you and your hate
I've wiped the bloody memories
From my mind hoping to
Gain some sort of respect
For enduring your wrath
As if knowing the pain 
You happily provide me
Will earn your love and
Prove to you somehow that
I've become better for 
Going through this hell
But as the years passed
I've realized that your
Tormenting me will never stop
And as you watch tv
Learning of girls being 
Raped and killed today
You shake your head and sigh
Turning a hateful eye and state
That they should have been me
