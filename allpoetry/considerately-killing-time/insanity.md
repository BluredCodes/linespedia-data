---
title: "Insanity"
writer: "considerately killing time"
slug: "insanity"
source: "AllPoetry"

My sanity is falling like 
              
A painful hail all around me 
                                                           
Reality is slipping through
          
The clumsy hands of time
      
Disease will paint my pretty portrait

Painting darkness all around me

Reality was nice to taste

If only for a little whileThere are times when I don't feel entirely complete, which is frustrating, so I thought that ending this poem when so much more could have been said went along nicely with the way I feel
