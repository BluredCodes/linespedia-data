as morning rises with the sunand all is said but nothings donei wake up to find all love is lostso why are you not gone?will you sing softly in my earall the songs I've longed to heari don't mind if the words are wrongas long as you are nearwhen the sun begins to diethe moon invades the virgin skyyou rape my mind with memoriesand wonder why i cryas the sun reappears with it comes my wasted tearsi hoped to wake without youso why are you still here?
