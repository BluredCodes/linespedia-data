---
title: "Special Friend"
writer: "considerately killing time"
slug: "special-friend"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/13246-Special-Friend-by-considerately-killing-time"

Through times of pleasure and times of pain
You've been there for me just the same.
There's nothing that I wouldn't do 
To show you that I appreciate you.
I really don't have much to offer
So read these words and know you matter .
Because knowing I have a friend like you
Is what makes the day worth living through.
So whenever you feel like no one cares,
Read this and know that I'll always be there.
