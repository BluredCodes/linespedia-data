I heard one day that they said you got so high
You ate up a chicken head and it was still alive
I had some friends they're swingin it's so dull tonight
Someday you're gonna get so messed up, 
you'll eat the head of your kid
And won't you be sorry Pot HeadOkay this is just a piece of the song.. but it's all I've heard and I think it's very entertaining =D
