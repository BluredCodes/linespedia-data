title: "Taming Worried Thoughts"

One night a dreadful thought occured to me
That caused my heart to cry.
My man asked me what the matter was 
And this was my reply:

"My dear I've loved you for some time
But soon you will be gone. I'll have no one 
To laugh with, nor to share my favourite songs.
You'd promised that you'd love me
Until the very end, that time is quickly coming,
I'm about to lose my love, my friend"

He put his arms around me and stroked
My worried head, he kissed my mouth so sweetly
And this is what he said:

"My girl, I've been my happiest while 
Holding onto you. We've shared good times together,
And they won't be ending soon. So let's enojy   
this moment, even if it may be our last,
And if that is the case, you'll always have the past." 

Wrapped within his innocence, he laid his lips
Upon my face, and as gently as this moment now, 
He entered me with grace, I felt his love 
Run through my veins, his lips against my flesh,
And when he finished loving me, he laid his head upon my breast

"So if you shall wake to find me gone, please remember
Where I'll stay, beside you in this common bed
Until there comes a day when two young souls will 
Reunite for eternity as one and until that 
Blissful day arrives I'll not be truly gone"
