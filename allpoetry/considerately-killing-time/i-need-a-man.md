A warm heart with strong desires, Lips that'd set my soul on fire,Eyes that make the sea look weakAnd eloquence when he does speakCreativity would be a mustOr he would not deserve my lustA complex man with simple needsA handsome devil who'd weaken kneesA sensitive, and caring guyWho wouldn't be ashamed to cry,Someone who could make me laughHe'd wash my hair while I'm in the bath If this sounds like a man you'd knowDon't you ever let him go.. But if you already have a manI'll take this one off your hands !!Not great I know.. but I think it's cute, and quite possibly what every woman wants lol =o)
