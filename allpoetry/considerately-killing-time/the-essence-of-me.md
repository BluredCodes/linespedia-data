Illustrious black hair 
And soulful verdant eyes
That shine through and surround
Warm, fragrant, soft ivory skin.

Small cupped breasts penetrate 
A delicate veil of confidence
Coaxing you into 
Splendid mild fantasy .

'Terrifyingly celestial'
That precious one,
She refuses to escape your notice 
A creature of pure magnificence,  
The picture of absolute beauty

Dazzling limbs that reach out for 
Something sacred to hold onto forever,
You secretly need that something to be you, 
For you, she would purify and make whole
