"Wake up sunshine!! It's a 'beauiful' day!!"
As I open my eyes with a smile,
Two little hands are cupping my face,
She's been waiting for this for a while.

I get out of my bed and we head down the stairs
To the kitchen for breakfast and tea,
As we sit at the table enjoying our meal,
This is what Karma says to me...

"Mommy I want to marry my prince,
I want him to come take me away,
We'll live in his castle and
We'll ride on pink horses
And, like daddy, he'll sing just for me..

We'll play on the piano, 
And he'll kiss my cheek,
And he'll tell me how pretty I am"
At this I can't help but grin..

"Is that right Karma honey?"
I ask my small girl,
Who is quite the romantic at three,

She smiles and she nods her excited head,
And at that moment my heart just breaks,
For when she finally does find her "prince"
In the long run her small heart will ache.

I wish there were something that I could do,
To stop her from growing up.
I don't ever want her to know what 
It's like to be hurt,
