---
title: "Daddy Dearest"
writer: "considerately killing time"
slug: "daddy-dearest"
source: "AllPoetry"

Your soul's been saved through ritual
Quite possibly the only way, 
You've made a promise to better
Though the pact was broke, and you're the same
You violate the minds of anyone who's shown you love
You contort the words of Jesus
To place your sorry self above the rest of us.

You anticipate to spread the hate
You've found a tool to hold you high
But believing isn't going to save you 
When the wars are raging in the sky
Like the rest of us, you'll rot before you die...
Like the rest of us you'll suffer
You will know no mercy from your God when you are choking falling, fading under
Your saintly mask will be ripped from your evil, hating face
Pretending doesn't matter when you're the first to fall from grace.
