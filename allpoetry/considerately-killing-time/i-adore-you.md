title: "I Adore You"
writer: "considerately killing time"
slug: "i-adore-you"

Voice of an angel 
Tattooed in my head 
So full of meaning. 
Every note sang 
Causes my heart to cry 
Out in utter joy. 
Never have I heard 
A more beautiful soul 
Disguised in a melody, 
Escape through the lips 
From the heart of a man.I melt everytime I hear this guy sing =oD
