title: "Princess Confusion"

Completely Me
A different face
My thoughts revealed
A different name
You love the way
I make you laugh
You're unaware
Of who I am
We talk all night
Until we sleep 
Our conversations
Running deep
You don't know
What's going on
Could knowing ME
Be so wrong?
You've fell in love
With me before
But years ago
WE were no more
So this I ask
(Answer honestly)
Would I light the 
flame of misery
If you found out 
it's only me?
