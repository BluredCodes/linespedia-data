title: "You're Only Seventeen"
writer: "considerately killing time"

A constant struggle
Freedom escapes me as quickly as 
The last breath of a dying soldier
I'm trapped 
I can't feel my legs
Fear sets in
Is this what it's like?
Is this how it's supposed to feel?
It's all happened so quickly
My life is over.
I'm screaming for you to turn back,
Go in another direction! 
But you don't hear me.
I guess this is the end for you too.
Why did you follow this road?
You saw what happened to me....Today, I found out that my little sister is pregnant...
