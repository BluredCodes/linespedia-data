title: "Said and Done"
writer: "considerately killing time"
slug: "said-and-done"

when consequences aggravate your simple state of mindwhen the walls turn grey, the floor grows cold your running out of time...don't hold your breath don't count on mewhen everything is said and doneyou'll be left to gather pieces of thedreams you've left behindI'll be the one who lets you downI'll be the one to hold you thereI'll be next in line to laugh at youI just hope you'll be preparedWhen you feel alone  just  know that  ignorance loves companyThe walls cave in, you suffocate Now won't you come and play with me promises are broken and the final fight was wonI'll be the one who's laughing  when everything is said and done
