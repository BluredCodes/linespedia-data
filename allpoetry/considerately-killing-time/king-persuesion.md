title: "King Persuesion"
writer: "considerately killing time"

I was once so innocent and great in your eyes
But now I'm hated because of your lies
You talk about stuff that didn't even go on
And now people whisper like I've done something wrong
I know what you said to that girl yesterday
Did you think the things you've said wouldn't come my way?
Though what bothers me most is you said that you care
And that you defend me to death when I am not there
Not long ago I had friends who were true
But now I am hated and it's all 'cause of youThis kind of goes along with 'Princess Confusion'.. It's a long story but it fits lol
