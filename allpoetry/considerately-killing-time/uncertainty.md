title: "Uncertainty"
writer: "considerately killing time"

You look to the past,
And it seems like your
Life has been burning away
Like someone's been sucking 
On you like a cigarette.

Nothing you've done makes any sense.
You've fought for causes that were lost.
You've worked your sorry ass off for nothing.
Well, not NOTHING, but certainly not 
Anything worth all of the energy you've dispensed.

So you look to the future, 
Hoping for answers to questions 
You'll never recieve,
Unaware of where you want to be,
Knowing that you want something.
To do something more than you've accomplished.


Though that could be anything.
