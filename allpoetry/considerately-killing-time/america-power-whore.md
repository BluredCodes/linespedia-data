---
title: "America: Power Whore"
writer: "considerately killing time"
slug: "america-power-whore"
source: "AllPoetry"

Whoring the ideals 
of political antagonism
in a mounting guerilla warfare of hatred 
American government 
lovingly fondle their dicks 
(in the name of patriotism) 
at the thought of using economic weapons 
and their power of intimidation 
excited to display their hostility
using nuclear teeth to
reveal strange interpretations 
of peace keeping
with offers of unquestionably selfish
conditional handouts to civilian war victims
in the name of comforting their own conscience 
while at home 
they send out brute policemen 
to enthusiastically beat demonstrators 
in the daylight on the streets as 
they protest against the powers who 
ferociously suck on 
the dignity of their own militant 
underpaid industrial workers 
and slaves to their plantations 
who 
in the process of dying 
are strategically chain-smoking their lives away
