---
title: "Take it Like A Man"
writer: "considerately killing time"

Regret, like bile, slowly rises
What the fuck have you gone and done
She's tied you up with broken rope
And you're only twenty one
A hostage to your own creation
You think about what could've been
She can't offer you salvation 
You're dancing in a morbid dream
You choke on the blast of reality
When life is shouting it's demands
You think about your independence
It's shattered, ripped right from your hands 

(you better take it like a man)I hate my sister's boyfriend.....
