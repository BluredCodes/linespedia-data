---
title: "Fist Full Of Pride"
writer: "considerately killing time"
slug: "fist-full-of-pride"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/11378-Fist-Full-Of-Pride-by-considerately-killing-time"
---

In nervous streets and 
shabby schools death 
is anxiously awaited
Cheap radios pour images 
of despair and denial
of the innocent blood 
awaiting unfair trials
You smack your lips at their 
tarnishing pride
your cultural superiority has 
propagated distress, 
propelling their hatred
a cheap way to salvation
While you're smacking your lips 
at their humiliation
they're scantily clad 
in a dress of privation
Surviving on hope 
that's provided in viles 
A fist full of hatred's 
your ticket to pride
