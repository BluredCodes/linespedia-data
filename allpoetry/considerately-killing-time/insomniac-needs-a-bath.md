I'v been up for three days now,
I've not gone to school,

I've not done anything,
And I act like a fool.

The imprint of my ass  
On my computer chair

I've not gotten a bath,
I've not brushed my hair.

I drink lots of coffee
And I eat Nutri-grain bars.

My head feels like it's swollen
And I look really bizarre.

I think that tomorrow 
I'll get my arse back in gear

But by the time 3 am comes 
I know I'll still be here.
