I'm going to hump a British boy
He'll be my sexy English toy
In a drunken state I'll use him
Then my number I'll refuse him

Who cares about the double deckers
All I want's an English pecker
Cuz to ride a British piece of meat
Would be such a special little treat

Let's forget about the Stone Henge man
I've got an even better plan
Let's find some guys with sexy accents
And reveal the mystery in their pants

Because to hump a sexy British boy
Would be such an orgasmic joy
On my stay in merry England
Hunting men will be my missionOkay, I'm tired...
