A tangled mass of dirty hair Hides a sunken, tired faceDark circles accent bloodshot eyesI've taken comfort in this quiet placeIt's later than an hour agoI'm not comfortable inside my skinI'll stop picking at the pallid fleshIf you'll inject the liquid sinI'm not finished this poem but I have to leave ASAP....
