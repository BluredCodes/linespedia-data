Oh, to be in an 80's Hair Rock bandWith spandex sporting loyal fansFoot high hair would be so glamI'd wear fish net stockings on my handsAnd to make myself look even betterI'd wear tight, glossy, patent leatherOh I would just be so much better Than White Snake and Poison put togetherOh, to be in an 80's Hair Rock bandWith spandex sporting loyal fansI'd drive girls mad across the landIf I were in an 80's hair rock bandI'm really bored, & I'M A FEMALE.... just so you all know =o)
