I spend the days urging a slower pace,
Collecting pieces of his mind,
Hiding in the contours of his imagination.
A bright red path leads to his eyes,
Dark, and full of meaning, like his thoughts,
His thoughts reflected in and reinforced by mine.
I saw him once. He's everything I'd imagined him to be.
Mountains of meaning collapsed at my feet.
Invisible, intangible, inaudible, but he spoke to me.
I've spent 19 years trying to find him,
It only took a day for me to recognize,
Who he was, he's exactly as I hoped he would be.I don't like this title AT ALL..... any suggestions?
