---
title: "Ultimate Revenge"
writer: "considerately killing time"
slug: "ultimate-revenge"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8233-Ultimate-Revenge-by-considerately-killing-time"
---

Each time you spew your ugly words in my direction,
like a missile on a mission to wound my softened heart, 
I sit silently, 
patiently, 
passively, 
waiting for the right moment 
to lash back at you. 
This moment will not come when you are done. 
This will manifest inside of my soul 
for days upon days and 
possibly months until my disgust 
for you is so much that 
I can no longer contain the way I feel. 
I question why someone, something like you was given 
the chance to breathe... 
I am thankful for these feelings, 
that you bestowd upon me, 
I hate you... 
And my attack against you won't be to scar you, 
to bring you down, 
to make you feel like the shit you are, 
I will never tell you how I feel... 
I will let this feeling grow 
until I am unable to keep it hidden 
inside the core of my heart any longer, 
today you will be spared 
for this time I  am able to 
immortalize my feelings disguised in beautiful words.
