title: "A Punk Rocker Please"
writer: "considerately killing time"

Oh, I love punk rocker's
They just blow my mind.
Most don't smell too perdy
But damn, they look fine.
Some sport crazy mohawks,
Some look like your dad,
But regardless of style
Their attitude's bad.

Oh, I love punk rockers
With lot's of tattoos,
They'll sing you a song
If ya give 'em some booze.
Some are anti-oppression,
They've got somethin' to say,
But some of 'em don't 
Give a fuck either way.

They're causin' a raucous 
An' they don't aim to please, 
And if you're a fascist,  
They'll bust out your knees.
Oh, I love punk rocker's
They just blow my mind.
Most don't smell too perdy
But damn, they look fine.
