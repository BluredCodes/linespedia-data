title: "This Is For You"
writer: "considerately killing time"

I'm so afraidEvery morning I wake up looking forward to you, I wait, almost impatiently, To know every word, Every thought that you produce, Regardless of how trivial those words, Or how briefly they're exchanged.I want to make you smile, To know what it's like to bring you joy,To be the one you know you'll find comfort inWhen you feel like the world has done you wrongTo reassure you that you are worth so much more than you thinkI picture myself with you, in your arms, Wrapping ourselves in quiet thoughts, Wondering what would it be like to experience your soul, To taste the essence of you in a warm, simple, kiss.Imagining how your breath would feel against my faceI want to know your faceI'm afraid that I'll never have the chance to know you.I am NOT in love.... just slightly infatuated =o) (Just wanted to make that clear) lol
