Covered 
In a blanket 
Of piss and ash 
Swollen eyes 
Struggle to see
A future in a 
Broken glass
That's been shatterd  on 
A warped floor of 
Hypocricy
Inspired by 
The sophistication
Of traditional
Ignorance that's been
Dangerously tailored 
To your twisted needs 
Hugging every curve of 
Your insanity while
The sour stench of
your elitist disease
And cheap imitations of
Designer decency 
Flow unimpeded
from a closet
of eradication  
In which social
