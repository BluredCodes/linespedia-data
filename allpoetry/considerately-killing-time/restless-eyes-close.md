Mother will you shave my headMy lips are crackedAnd nerves worn thinThe needle spinsWithout a threadI scream you're unaffected I saw you dancing in a dreamTattoos bleedingThoughts obsceneThings aren't always what they seemAnd no one understands meThis is about a crazy girl I met in a park with my ex who kept calling me mommy. I shaved her head with a twin blade razor because she didn't want her dad to know she was black, lol, true story.
