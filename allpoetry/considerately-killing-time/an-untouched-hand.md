---
title: "An Untouched Hand"
writer: "considerately killing time"
slug: "an-untouched-hand"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/13847-An-Untouched-Hand-by-considerately-killing-time"

Longing for a feeling
That I've never felt before
A fire that I need to feel, 
A pain I can't ignore
There's promise in 
An untouched hand that 
I can't stop reaching for

He lives inside my rainy dreams
Where long days are always grey
The night is thick with passion
And desires that will never fade
I'm hoping for a brand new love 
That this imaginary man will give

If you could see what I've dreamed
You might be able to understand
Please realize I have to go
I need to feel this untouched hand

I'm longing for a feeling
That I've never felt before
A fire that I need to feel, 
A pain I can't ignore
There's promise in his
Untouched hand that 
I can't stop reaching for
I'm homesick for a place that 
I've never  been before 
I'm longing for a brand new love
And his hand I can't ignore
