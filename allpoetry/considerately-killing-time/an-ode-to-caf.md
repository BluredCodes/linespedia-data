Oh Caf, Dear Caf,So good for a laugh,You brighten the gloomiest day!And when I'd sleep overYou'd act like Ol' RoverAnd on the floor you would stay!Seven years beforeYou used to knock on my doorAnd at the park we would playYou hit on my aunt andMy sisters spit on your pantsBut your smile never did fade!Oh Caf, Dear Caf,You're so good for a laugh!Remember the wine dance that day? (*wink wink*)Or the good days in PortIn Majewski's tree fort &The parties at Rachel's with Dame? So here's to Caf,(Who practically sleeps in his hat)Don't let your washboard abs go away!I'll never forget youAnd I'm so glad I've met you...My friend you always will stayCaf.. I love you man!! =op
