Steaming vapours of elegancePurify my muddled soul, withImages of blossoms, drifting slowlyBehind closed eyes with every Precious olfactory gift.Earthen coloured liquid withYour gentle scent, I've become Intoxicated by a magical kiss Of exquisite delicacy, all of meIs lost with every splendid taste.Faultless flavour of passion,Taming worried thoughts, you lingerOn unsatisfied lips, that ache for Your eternity, for a little more thanThe ephemeral paradise you bring.Tranquility infused in every breath,I've just embraced perfection.Vienna Opera Ball is my favourite tea =o)
