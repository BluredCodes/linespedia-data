---
title: "Swollen Ego"
writer: "considerately killing time"
slug: "swollen-ego"

He's not the kind of guy you've 
Seen a thousand times before
He knows he's somethin' different
And he'll make you feel adored
He's got a body like Adonis
And an urgency controlled
With encyclopedic knowledge
You think with him you could grow old

But suddenly his confidence 
Breaks into sickening conceit
You find yourself wondering just
What it was you couldn't see
A side of him that you can't stand 
You'd rather live with impetigo
It's crying a shame his dick
Isn't as big as his swollen ego
