title: "How  Could I Have Missed Him"
writer: "considerately killing time"

Just when you think your world is perfect 
You find someone to make it better
And he dances wildly to the music while
You watch him from the couch

No one's ever made you laugh 
Quite like this quiet guy can do

So you'll cook his favourite dishes 
Because that's when he gets excited
And when you see his smile
You feel you'll live forever
And when he hangs on to your body 
It's warmed by his favourite sweater
You play with his beaded necklace 
While he smells your dampened hair

You don't know how you could have missed him
When he's stood beside you this whole time

And when you are in his presence 
You have to be so thankful because
He's showed you things you would have missed
If you'd have looked for them without him
So you thank the Gods from heaven
That they took your hand to find him
And nothing could be better now for
You've found the one who's always held your heartThis is a song =o)
