title: "Inevitable Defeat"
writer: "considerately killing time"

Is this race worth finishing 
when defeat is inevitable?
Why do I try to fool myself by 
running in victory's general direction, 
Even though I know that, soon enough, 
victory will be just out of my reach. 
Why do I continue to run? 
Will there be some sort of 
satisfaction gained for 
being a part of this race? 
I don't want to participate anymore.
There's nothing to
Look forward to in the end,
Except for a participation ribbon.... 
Unless of course, you're in cahoots 
with the race co-ordinator.
Then I'd defineately be invited to the after party,
Hmmm, I don't think so.
All of that training...I can't devote
All of that time to ensure that 
I won't be left out of the winner's circle.
It's all a bit too much for me.
I think I'd like to end this race a bit early,
I think I would like my ribbon now please.
