---
title: "And All the Stars of Heaven shall Sing"
writer: "Keith"
slug: "and-all-the-stars-of-heaven-shall-sing"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8994-And-All-the-Stars-of-Heaven-shall-Sing-by-Keith"
---

Shepherds shiver where they lie
Snowflakes quiver in the sky
Silence blankets all the earth
At the hour of Jesus' birth

And all the Stars in heaven shall sing
The glory of the new-born King
And all the Stars in heaven shall sing
The glory of the new-born King

Cradled on his mother's arm
Free from sorrow, safe from harm
Wise men bring him in the cold
Myrrh and frankincense and gold


And all the Stars in heaven shall sing
The glory of the new-born King
And all the Stars in heaven shall sing
The glory of the new-born King

Factory chimneys belch and smoke
Flowers fade and rivers choke
The greed of man for wealth and power
Destroys our planet hour by hour

The stars of heaven dim and fade
Pity the world that we have made

From this world of death and hate
We must find that hill where shepherds wait
With one bright star shining on high
Out of a clear and smokeless sky

And all the Stars in heaven shall sing
The glory of the new-born King
And all the Stars in heaven shall sing
The glory of the new-born King
