How come your never homewhenever i call on the phone?how come we're never togetheryet you always tell me forever?When will the stories end?why can't we just be friends?when will i forget them alland why don't you ever call?believe in what i sayi don't want it to be this wayi have to make this choicei want to hear your voicenow and forever we will stayyou and me, together we'll melt awayforget the past, it is unknownthe future is what i want to knowi want to make this the best everi want it to last foreverwatch the tears fall from my eyeshear the sound of my heart diefollow your soul into my armsyou will not follow very fari am right by your side at nighti am right here in your sighttreat me like your one and onlypretend that we are in love totallyall my life i dreamed of youbut all i need is someone truebecome someone i once knewor just hand me your cluebut i know that would never happenand in a few i will be forgottengoodnight, goodbye, it is the enda letter to you i have to sendhave a nice life, i hope it is goodmaybe next time i'll be understood
