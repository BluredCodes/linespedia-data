---
title: "Every night i see..."
writer: "Lauryn"
slug: "every-night-i-see"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/19924-Every-night-i-see...-by-Lauryn"
---

every night i see
the sky lit with stars
     above you and me

every night i glow
with love
     i just can't show

can't you see the wonderful sea
of love surrounding us
     whenever we believe

i don't see what you see
we live in different worlds
     you and me

what it was to me to know that
you were the sun shining
     and you were the one crying

can't i see what's between you and me?
     the sky, the sun, the earth, you're the only one i ever knew
          yes it was you, and i won't ever love again
