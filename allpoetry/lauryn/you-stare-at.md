---
title: "You stare at..."
writer: "Lauryn"
slug: "you-stare-at"

you stare at the world with so much hate in your eyes
and no one can figure out quite just why
you feel alone even with a friend
you say you want to die, but no one has advice to lend
you carry a soul that is hurt and frozen
and you know in your heart, it's not the path chosen
you want to be the nicest person in the world
but all you are is a "depressed" little girl
no one understands quite where you come from
and thinking about the past makes your body numb
your mind is always filled with horror and dread
you are constantly paranoid and think someone wants you dead
maybe they do, but who even cares
you don't, you don't even think life is fair
why did god put you all through this?
your death to be soon is your only wish
you don't believe life is worth living
and you are tired of constantly giving
you want to live, but on the other hand you don't
and if you keep going on this way, you won't
