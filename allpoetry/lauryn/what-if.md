---
title: "What if..."
writer: "Lauryn"
slug: "what-if"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

what if honesty wasn't even a word?
what if trust could never be learned?
what if no one was there to listen?
what if you lost everything in a second?
what if you were to open your soul?
what if there was nothing untold?
what if the world came crashing at your feet?
what if you had no one but me?
