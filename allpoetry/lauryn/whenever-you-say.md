whenever you say my namedo you completely go insane?thinking about that one nightwhen i was in your sight?do you remember what i look like?remember when we took that hike?do you wish i was here today?do you wish i was here to stay?things will never ever be the samebecause when you called i never camei will never see you againyou have to live your life, so begini'm sorry i left you that one nighti'm sorry i fell out of your sighti didn't mean to hurt anyonei just couldn't live, and God woni'm sorry for all the hurt you have nowi want to see you happy, but i don't know howwhen you look at the stars aboveyou will see my eyes filled with loveyou weren't the reason that i lefti loved you from the moment we meti must say goodbye to you for nowwe shall part, but i don't know how
