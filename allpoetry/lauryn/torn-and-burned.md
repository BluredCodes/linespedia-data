---
title: "Torn and burned..."
writer: "Lauryn"
slug: "torn-and-burned"

Torn and burned
   with no disguise
You hold it close
   trying to hide

Flames burn high
   without dyeing down
Thoughts in your brain
   create a cloud

A solemn mist
   flows by your face
And places a frown
   as soft as lace

The light of darkness
   is never ending
Your heart and soul
   has stopped defending

The warmth from your heart
   turned away from cold
But nothing is there
   to turn to and hold...
