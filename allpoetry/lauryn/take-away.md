---
title: "Take Away..."
writer: "Lauryn"
slug: "take-away"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/29309-Take-Away...-by-Lauryn"
---

take away the pain
i have inside
take away the trouble
overpowering my mind

take away my soul
so that i don't dream
take away my voice
so that i don't scream

take away my body
that aches for you
take away my ears
so i don't listen for you

take away my emotions
let me live in peace
take away the heartache
you have given me
