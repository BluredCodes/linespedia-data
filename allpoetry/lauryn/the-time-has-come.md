title: "The time has come..."

the time has come to chose a path
chose the life you want to lead
decide which future you want
and how you would like to bleed

it's time to decide about life
who is worth your time
and figure out for yourself
what exactly to decide

chose the path well because
you can't ever turn around
but i hope at the end of your path
i am the one you have found
