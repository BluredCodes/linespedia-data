we talk like nothing ever happenedlike we never went that farand if only you could look me in the eyesyou would be wishing on the same stari know i'm the best you've ever hadbut you went and broke my heartyou said you don't want to get hurt like beforebut how,... if nothing ever starts?i just don't understand you at alland maybe that's why i fell for youmaybe that small mystery in your eyesled me to want to understand youi will move on to the darknesson out of your life from now onalthough every night i know i will see youbut i have to move on
