title: "My soul keeps..."

my soul keeps crying out to you
but your never there...
i start screaming for some attention
but still you are nowhere...

the tears begin to leave my face
and i don't have you here...
even in these very difficult times
you still aren't near...

i'm old enough to notice your gone
but she's still so young...
don't you know i miss you?
come home where you belong...

it doesn't matter if we're poor
as long as we're together...
we don't need a lot of money
just come home to me forever...
