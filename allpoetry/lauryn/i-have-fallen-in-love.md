i have fallen in love
several times before
and not have known
a thing about it

i didn't know whom
i didn't know when
i didn't know where
so all i did was wait

i still don't know
what type of man
or even the additude
but you seem to fit

it doesn't matter
how much longer i sit
because i know
i will be with you somedayas some of you may have taken it, it is about love... but please don't estimate what kind of love, this poem is about someone you meet that you fall in love with every thing about them, he/she shows you what kind of person you would like, and just sitting and waiting for the right one to come along, not necessarily waiting for that certain person.  So next time, please don't judge the meaning of a poem by it's cover (Michael Dennis Rivers).
