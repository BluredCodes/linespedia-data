---
title: "Hallow dreams..."
writer: "Lauryn"

hallow dreams fill your soul
as your body becomes cold
a cloud covers your eyes
as your family sits and cries
you don't know if you should go
but you know you must follow
you helped your family in every way
but not even they will stay
don't worry, they will not forget
because you are their sunset
just follow me into the light
it will soon be in sight
