---
title: "Respect me for..."
writer: "Lauryn"

respect me for who i am
     not for whom you would like me to be
love me for the person inside
     not as a person whom you need

watch me look in your eyes
     not the starry night
listen to my heart when i speak
     and please stop trying to fight
