---
title: "I would wait..."
writer: "Lauryn"

i would wait a lifetime to be with you again
i would wait in silence for it all to begin
my life is paused, it's on hold right now
because as for living, i just don't know how
catching only a glimpse of your smile
hearing my heart beat in your silence
it hurts too much to feel the love you give
it hurts even more to just live
