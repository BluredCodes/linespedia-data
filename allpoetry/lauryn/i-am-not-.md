I am not in love anymorei am sorry but i can live withouti am not drowning in tearsi am sorry but i have forgoti am not listening to your storiesi am sorry but they don't interest mei am not killing my dreams for youi am sorry but don't you see?You had the chance to love meyou had the chance to hold meyou had the chance to say i doand you know i would for youbut things are changed nowbut life is going on nowbut you will no longer survive on my loveand i will no longer need your love
