off in the distance i could seethe sun falling from the skyand as i looked towards heaventhe moon appeared with the blink of an eyethe wolf's howling made me shudderand i knew he wanted me to leavebut he had really said to himselfthat nature also belongs to mei heard the crickets music that they sangthe sweet lullabies that helped me sleepwhen i awoke i saw that the moonhad disappeared in the sky so deepthe sun reappeared floating upwardsinto the sky making the dayit floated to the spot in the skywhere the moon had once laid
