---
title: "A dreamer..."
writer: "Lauryn"

---

the world of a dreamer
     is always beautiful
their souls are happy
     to have something to hold

a true dreamer's dreams
     live past their death
and these souls
     they never forget

these dreams are windows
     into our souls
the dreams patch up
     our heart's holes

a heart without dreams
     is not a heart at all
a person whom doesn't care
     will surely fall

you may never know
     exactly what i see
but i love you
     because you do dream
