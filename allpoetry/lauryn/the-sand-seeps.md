title: "The Sand Seeps..."

the sand seeps between my toes
i remember being near you
the sun sets in the pink clouds
and then came out the dew

the grass was damp on our feet
yet we kept running the fields
and the animals they ran in the woods
as if we weren't going to yield

the stars shone bright above our heads
a shooting star crossed our path
and i made a wish to never turn back
why don't you do the math?
