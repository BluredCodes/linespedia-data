---
title: "I thought..."
writer: "Lauryn"

i thought i had found the person
perfect as he could be
but what i didn't realize
that perfect person isn't right for me

then i met you, not the greatest
but all the qualities i want
is what i see in you
but as far as telling you, i can't

i want you to know
that i respect your honesty greatly
and i need you to know
that i applaude your responsibility

i also would like you to know
of the feeling you have given me
it's greater than the perfect person
because you don't want to be

you didn't impress me by words
'nor by actions you made
you never tried to seduce me
and i don't care what your paid

all i care about is this feeling
it just feels soo right inside
but i'm scared to loose it
and that's why it has to hide...
