---
title: "I see you..."
writer: "Lauryn"

i see you sitting crying to the world
you are all alone, or so you feel
no one is there to wipe any of your tears
you will need plenty of time to heal

your life was shattered in a blink
i see your soul becoming cold
with only one ring of the telephone
you had no one else to hold

how do you go on from this tragedy?
how can you possibly survive?
when every one you love is gone
and your the only one left alive
