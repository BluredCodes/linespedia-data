They ask what i want to do with my life...
that's very easy, i can answer with one breath...
i want to be a nurse, help the needy, nurture,...
i even have some time left...

They ask what else there is that i would like...
they all know the answer, i want a family...
a large one, six kids, and maybe a dog...
and we will all live happily...

But they ask me want i want for christmas...
and this is the hardest thing i can say...
i tell them i would like you forever...
but all i can hope for is just one more day...
