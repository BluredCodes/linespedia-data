---
title: "When I look..."
writer: "Lauryn"

when i look upon your eyes
i see so many dreams for you and i
the silence kills me inside
and i don't know what to say, i can't hide
your smile warms me when i am cold
and i know i only want you to hold
i want you to know everything about me
i have so much more to say, so let me speak...
