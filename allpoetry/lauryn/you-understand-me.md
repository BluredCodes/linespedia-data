you have to talk to understand people
but for me, you just look in my eyes
to know what one is feeling you ask
but you always know when i have cried

you think of me sitting with you
and I get a sense of where you are
when i have a feeling you are upset
i talk to you through the stars

i know you pray for me
i hear it in dreams every night
i know you listen for me
but instead i walk into your mind

some people think they know me
but they don't, only you understand me
it was fate that brought me to you
or at least that's what i believeSo i don't get any more comments about something that the poem isn't even about... this poem is about a woman whom i admire, a true friend, someone that is very close to me, my love taa ta.
