---
title: "I know..."
writer: "Lauryn"
slug: "i-know"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/23688-I-know...-by-Lauryn"
---

i know every single face you make
i know i love you so very much
i know the words to our song
and i know that forever starts from now on

i know i fucked up
i know i did wrong
i know life is unbearable with out you
and the days go by so very long

i know life is hard now
i know your heart is breaking
i know my heart is breaking so much more
because of all my mistakes, i can't stop thinking...
