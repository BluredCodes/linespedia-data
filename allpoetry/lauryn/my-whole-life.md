my whole life i have waited for this
i wouldn't change one second
everyone watching every step
i will always have a hand to lend

it doesn't matter what they see
only what i know is in you
because when i told you my thoughts
you knew they were true

i don't need someone to call mine
i'm not after love, and you know
needless to say, i don't want to be another girl
but what i feel for you is beyond my control
