I remember when she left me, 
sitting there that day. 
watching her sadly
as she slowly drove away. 

I thought she'd be back shortly 
an hour at the most, 
but disappear she did- 
a lonely lovers ghost. 

She was my jeweled goddess 
arrayed in autumn mist- 
I thought that time would stop
every time we kissed. 

Months I sat there waiting, 
to find her on the lawn- 
Then one day I realized, 
my life was moving on. 

Pain can last a lifetime, 
but hope a soul can heal. 
So my soul shall tarry then 
love's subsequent appeal.
