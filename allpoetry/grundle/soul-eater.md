Like nebulous clouds
rising toward the ceiling,
this packaged cure-all
was passed around.
Day in and day out,
it floated toward the soul,
"I can't live without it!"
said one,
"Oh I'll stop when I want..."
spoke the other.

The vitality of vapor and water,
spiraling ever closer...
oh its green
so green,
lovely green
so green...
to touch it,
to taste it,
to feel it,
and love it...

Will they wake up?
probably not...
lost in the mist
of skunks and crazy lemons,
wandering through the mind
toward destination yesterday...
Their life so vivid, 
so exciting,
so touching,
so momentous,
without ever moving an inch...Any suggestions would be appreciated
