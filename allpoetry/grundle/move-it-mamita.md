who wants to bailar
when the musica se acaba?
is there any poder
cuando the dance se termina?

a mi mamita baile rica,
*boom boom*
shakin' your body
muevelo suavecita..
*boom boom*
el poder es contigo
*boom boom*
come baby come to me,
let your body go...

It happens in the music,
marching towards your soul..
the rhythm pounding moving
mientras losing control
these lights the sounds
se vayan from here
as groove makes way 
for desires to sentir.

a mi mamita baile rica,
*boom boom*
shakin' your body
muevelo suavecita..
*boom boom*
el poder es contigo
*boom boom*
come baby come to me,
let your body go...
