It was the strangest thing
when Dookle Naglog came between us...
after all those simple measures...
how could he have seen us?

Approaching innocently
he said he was a machinest,
alluding to how he could help...
acting in our best interest,

Actually he traded slaves...
we were taken to Venus, 
I felt particularly violated
when he grabbed.......my shirt!!!So the flow will go better the three periods e.g. "..." denote a PAUSE.
