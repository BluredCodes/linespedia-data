A hundred hours and a hundred days,
I have worked these fields, for what?
I make a dime and get some food
with each and every cut...

This land has been so good to me
for that I love it with my life
'61 was a bad year, you see,
but it saved my kids and wife.

My second love and second bride
she's happy as I sweat...
She shares with me in happy glee
A golden crowned sunset.Any suggestions would be appreciated
