Experience is the great provider,
For poets one and all...
living life will teach us,
our one specific call... 

I haven't even lived yet,
so what then do I write?
how can I explain it-
I have no written sight. 

so I guess I'll wait then,
for life to have its way...
later I can write it,
with pen and ink someday.
