here i am,
I wasn't before,
but like a wall of flame
I arrive on the scene.
i've never done this,
but i'm sure I will again
just hope it doesn't cause pain.

I observe, like a fly on the wall
I see nothing, yet I see what no one else does
things better left to the imagination.
any questions....?this is my first, but i'm sure they'll get good at some point.
