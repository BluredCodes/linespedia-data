don't we all wish for a simpler life?
one without the hustle and bustle of our daily life,
the traffic, pollution,
corrupt police, prostitution
of our resources. the stripping and beating of our mother earth?
take us back a few hundred years,
when politics were not the most important thing,
and there were no million dollar sports, 
no vegas casinos, 
no internet,
no phone.

a simpler time.
when man could commune with nature, and be one with the earth.
living off the gifts given us,
now long forgotten......

how long before we forget ourselves.....?
