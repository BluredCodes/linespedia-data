Roses 
Kisses 

A French accent 
Dollars and cents

A kind heart 
A tight butt 

Tears 
Beer 

A sense of humour 
Summer 

Muscles 
Loose morals 

Poetry 
Poverty 

Uniforms 
Boredom 

Tequila 
Mother nature
