You left me hanging up to dry 
While I tried my best not to cry 

I've never been one to beg 
Thought you were just pulling my leg 

Now six months and sixty wish-you-were-here poems later 
I still don't feel any better 

And you wanna hear something insane? 
I think I'm falling in love with you all over again! 

This is not how I had pictured it 
It was supposed to be warm and cosy and neat 

But six months and sixty wish-you-were-here poems later 
I'm ten years older and I know better 

Roses are not red, they are just thorny 
"You've got mail" is just a fairy movie 

And fairy movies don't come true 
At least not in the case of me and you 

Six months and sixty wish-you-were here poems later 
I still haven't found anyone better 

I still think you are The One 
My Friend, My Lover, My Man 

It's possible to love what you cannot see 
But to be loved back is not a guarantee 

I guess they were right 
I can't be on your mind while I'm out of your sight 

I guess they were right all along 
This "love" was bound to go wrong 

So six months and sixty wish-you-were-here poems later 
It's only my poetry that's better
