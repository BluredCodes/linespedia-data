Love is in the air 
Everywhere 
And I don't have a gas mask 
I'm afraid to ask 
For one 
So I'm choking 
Hawking 
Myself 

This day 
Is the day 
When most every girl has 
A gas 
Mask 
From dawn till dusk 
Everyone 
Celebrates the concept 
Except 
Myself 

To find a good gas mask 
Is such a trying, tiring task 
And with love being in the air 
Everywhere 
I am cruelly reminded that 
(Infinitely offended that) 
Two is better than one 
I can't breathe 
I'm alone, by and with 
Myself.
