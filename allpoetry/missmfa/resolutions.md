I need to be among my own 
Get out of this house, turn it into a home 
I need to find my intellectual clones 
Stop cracking my head against a stone 
I need to change my life's tones 
Make them repay my emotional loans 
I need to be alone 
Find someone with a shoulder to laugh on 
I need to remember why I was born 
Wipe out the things I can't condone 
I need no one's permission 
Do away with strings, remain with percussion 
I need to sieve and repackage my emotions 
Regain my rightful, hard-earned throne 
I need to go back to being an atom 
Rethink, reconsider, recognize, reason 
I need to update these resolutions.
