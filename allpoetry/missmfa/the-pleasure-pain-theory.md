Somewhere in this world 
There is a guy who has just gone bald 
After an "instant hair annihilator" chemical 
Happened to fall 
On his head 
Right after his fiancee, Emily, 
Decided 
To call off their wedding and wed 
Instead 
His number one enemy 
Ed 
Who had just succeeded 
In taking over Emily's - now former - fiance's 
(His name is Moses) 
Business. 

All this happened 
At the exact moment 
When I was looking into your beautiful eyes. 
So you better not kiss me or poor Moses will die.
