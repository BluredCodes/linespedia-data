Have a cup of me 
Hot, sweet and tasty 
You do look thirsty 
So quench your thirst on me 

Have a cup of me 
It's long overdue 
You look cold and blue 
Cups of me will warm you 

I've been stuck in this pot 
Fighting to stay hot 
Hoping someone would want me 
One day you came along 
Looking for something strong 
Looking for someone like me 

So have a cup of me 
Have two, even three 
You shouldn't worry 
'Bout running out of me 

Because I'm in plenty 
And I'm totally free 

So have a cup, please do 
So have a cup, won't you?
