title: "A sink full of dirty dishes"

(i)
A pot, a pan and a pile of plates
In various lewd and deplorable states
Giving me come-hither looks
And the spooks.

(ii)
I crawled out of the kitchen
And said to myself, "I think
I'll move." So I packed everything and ran.
Well, everything but the kitchen sink.
