---
title: "Curse you alarm clock!"
writer: "missmfa"
slug: "curse-you-alarm-clock"

May the cat get your tongue
So you'll never again utter a sound.
May you fall in fresh dung
Then gather dust in a "lost and found".
May your hands turn into feet
And may your face become uglier.
Do you plan to inherit?
Well then, may Big Ben, your grandfather,
Completely cut you off his will.
May a baby pee on you.
May you be run over by one gigantic wheel
Or even twenty-two.
And just when your batteries have gone dead,
May batteries be out of stock 
You rude, drag-them-from-bed,
sleep-interrupting clock!
