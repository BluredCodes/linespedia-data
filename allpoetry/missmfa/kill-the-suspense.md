---
title: "Kill the suspense"

Cut the strings.Or tighten them.
Give me wings. Or shorten them.

There's no in between.

Close the gap. Or widen it.
Where's the map? Have you hidden it?

Pull me closer. Or let me go.
Be my Lover. Or be my Foe.

There's no in between.

Take all of me. Or none of me.
Were we meant to be? You and me?

It's either a Loss or a Win.
But there's no in between.

There's either an Us or a What-might-have-been
But there's no in between.

So stop this nonsense.
Kill the suspense.
