February fun 
Has just begun 
Fanatically fun 
For all? For none? 

All year long it lies dead 
Inside them 
Then suddenly on one day, all turns red 
What a silly game! 

Why one day 
And not everyday? 

February fun 
Buy as much as you can 
For fortuneless fans 
Impose a ban? 

Fortify yourself if you're 
Unattached 
Some call it a day for the unsure 
Are the recipients really touched? 

Why one day 
And not everyday? 

February fun 
Every mother's sun 
Flowers, Chocolate, the Sun 
Anything that will stun 

It's not the thought that counts 
But the zeroes 
Found at the tail of the final amount 
Why? Who the hell knows! 

Why one day 
And not everyday? 

February fun 
After all's been bought and done 
From four to fifty-one 
When will they ever learn 

That the best things in life 
Are priceless? 
So friend, lover, sibling, wife 
Let's clean up this mess 

Why one day 
And not everyday?
