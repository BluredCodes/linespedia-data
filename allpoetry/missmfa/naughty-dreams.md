title: "Naughty Dreams"

I see him in my dreams and even out of them 
He gets me to thinking of changing my last name 
To his. 
He walks by in slow motion - or at least that's how it seems 
To me. 
He's an angel 
Just look at him 
He's a devil 
He makes me dream 
Naughty dreams. 

He's walking towards me! Oh God! He's coming here! 
I'm starting to tremble, my heart is full of fear 
And fire. 
He's standing in front of me, his heavenly eyes are looking 
At me. 
He's an angel 
Just look at him 
He's a devil 
He makes me dream 
Naughty dreams 

I see his mouth moving, I hear him call my name 
I feel his mouth moving, my body is all aflame 
For him. 
He kisses me in slow motion - or at least that's how it seems 
To me. 
He's an angel 
Just look at him 
He's a devil 
He makes me dream 
Naughty dreams 

My angel's wings 
Cover me 
My heart starts to sing 
Joyfully 
My devil's sting 
Delights me 
Bells start to ring 
Loudly 
Bells start to ring 
Loudly 
Bells start to ring 
Loudly... 

I wake up to the sound of my alarm clock 
My sheets are all tousled, my bed's a mess and I'm soaked 
In sweat. 
I'm aching all over - This is becoming familiar 
To me. 
He's an angel 
So out of reach 
He's a devil 
He makes me itch 
From naughty dreams.
