The sun is pretending to shine 
My Mother Just Showed Us Nine 
Planets are all aligned 
Cancerous, Scorpion, Leonine 
The Milky Way tastes like wine 
That is slightly alkaline 
The moon has a hold on The Brine 
Was that asteroid a sign? 
Believe or not, if you're so inclined 
But little green men are quite byzantine.
