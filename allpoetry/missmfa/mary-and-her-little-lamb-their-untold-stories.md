Mary had a little lamb
Its fleece was white as snow.
And everywhere that the lamb went
Skiers were sure to go.

~~~~~~~~~~

Mary had a little lamb
She went with it to school.
Not just for fun for show and tell
And the kids thought it was cool.

~~~~~~~~~~

Mary had a little lamb
But now it's a grown sheep.
It considered its options then decided to join
The flock of little Bo-peep.

~~~~~~~~~~

Mary had a little lamb
Its light kept her awake.
Oh! Sorry! My mistake!
I see it's lamb not lamp.

~~~~~~~~~~

Mary has a little lamb
But that's irrelevant.
'Cause a cute, little puppy dog
Is what she really wants.
