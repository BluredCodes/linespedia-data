Mother,
You have nurtured me
And taught me your ways
Because of this and more,
I will not forget you.
Sitakusahau.

But Mother,
I am now of age.
The world is an open stage
And I am ready to do my act
Far away from you.
Yet I will not forget you.
Sitakusahau.

Mother,
I will speak of you
With love and pride.
I will put into practice
All that you have taught me.
I will not shame you.
I will not forget you.
Sitakusahau.

Mama,
Ninakuheshimu.
Ninakudhamini.
Ninakushukuru.
Ninakupenda.
Ninaenda.
Lakini
Sitakusahau.
I will not forget you.
