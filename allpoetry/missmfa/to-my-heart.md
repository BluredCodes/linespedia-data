It will be alright. 
Soon light will replace night. 

Time heals everything 
It will lessen the sting 
By and by 
Blue skies 
Will come your way 
You'll be okay. 

I know you've been broken 
By words both uttered and unspoken 
By and by 
Your cries 
Will decrease 
And then they'll altogether cease. 

It will be alright. 
Soon light will replace night. 

Hold on 
Be strong 
Don’t turn into stone 
True love will come along 
And the emptiness will be gone 
And you'll no longer be alone 
And you'll no longer mourn 
So continue to sing your song. 

It will be alright. 
Soon light will replace night.
