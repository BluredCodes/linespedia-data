One and one 
Isn't always two 
Good looks can 
Lie to you 
Modern man's 
Like a baboon 
Go beyond the sun 
To reach the moon 
What's done is done 
Except if it's not 
Have fun 
While your blood is still hot 
Live and learn 
Talk a lot 
Or speak and span 
Over someone else's quotes 
Stay true to your clan 
Even in court 
Down with guns 
Up with the right to vote 
Get a tan 
To conceal your spots 
When will they learn? 
Well, as soon as they are taught 
My head churns 
When I have the flu 
Always have a plan 
To pull you through 
Don't pick up and run 
When your bills are due 
I've never been to London 
Have you? 
I'm a big fan 
Of all that's true 
Why can't she earn 
The same as you guys do? 
Half is better than one 
And one and one isn't always two.
