I walk through 
The forest of treasures 
Towering above me 
Displaying their pleasures 
For those who care to see. 
I climb one such 
Tree of treasures. 
I pluck its fruits 
For knowledge,for leisure. 
I eat. 

When perched on top 
Of the forest of treasures 
I can see far-away lands 
I can dive into the corners of the mind 
I can dine with Kings,hide with Fugitives 
I can witness the welding of two hearts. 
Without censor. 
Without measure. 

So, I use my library card. 
Frequently.
