I really like Strawberry.
I really like Chocolate too.

If I didn't like Chocolate,
It would be not liking
My very essence.

But Strawberry,
Oh sweet, succulent Strawberry!
Exotic, erotic,
Out of reach?

I already have Chocolate.
So I must have Strawberry
For dessert.
And for dinner.
And for breakfast.

Only Strawberry will satisfy
My Appetite,
Will quench my Thirst,
Will stop my Craving.

Chocolate is nice.
Chocolate is all around me.
But I want Strawberry.

Chocolate and Strawberry.
Ebony and Ivory.
