Are you deep enough for me 
To swim in the waters of your spirituality? 
Do you consider yourself human 
Because you're a good-sense-of-humour type of man? 
Are you a one-female male? 
Do you prefer dark to pale? 
Are you a hopeful romantic? 
Is there a love song that makes your knees go weak? 
Do you believe in brides dressed in white? 
Are you willing to be my White Knight? 
Is yours an open mind? 
Are you kind? 
Is music your soul's balm? 
Would you allow me to read your palm 
Even though I don't know how? 
Are you ready for true love? I mean right now? 

If you answered yes 
To all or to one (or less) 
Of the above questions 
Then, I'm open to your suggestions...;-)
