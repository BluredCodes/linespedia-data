Everyday 
I wake up sick. 
Just the thought 
Of being me 
For another day 
Makes me cringe. 

What can I say? 
I guess I'm weak 
I cannot 
Set myself free 
Except on the way 
To a binge. 

I don't believe 
Verse one or two 
If I did 
I would not know 
How to be and live 
Like myself 

The poetry I weave 
The things that I do 
Make my Id 
Change to Ego. 
I find I deceive 
Only myself. 

So everyday 
I wake up sick 
And the thought 
Of being me 
For another day 
Makes me cringe. 

What can I say? 
I guess I'm weak.
