title: "Self-love is not a sin"
writer: "missmfa"

My life is all that there is 
I don't believe in this 
I wish upon a fallen star 
In my car 
I think I'll fake it 

Even if it takes an eternity 
I will find my way back to me 
Take it out then put it in 
Self-love is not a sin 

I've spent a lifetime in the past 
The future was the worst 
I wish upon a fallen star 
In my car 
I think I'll make it 

Even if it takes an eternity 
I will find my way back to me 
Take it out then put it in 
Self-love is not a sin 

Look behind at where you've been 
Look beyond your nose and chin 
Look above then try to begin 
Look and you'll be sure to win 
Look, do you know what I mean? 

A life-long journey back to eternity 
I return forgiveness to me 
I wish upon a fallen star 
In my car 
I think I'll take it 

Even if it takes an eternity 
I will find my way back to me 
Take it out then put it in 
Self-love is not a sin
