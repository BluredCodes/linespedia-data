What you need to do 
Is grab him by the brain 
And shove him up against a wall. 
Establish mind contact 
And let him see right inside your soul. 
This will scare him considerably 
And he will try to break free. 
But don’t give him the chance. 
If you can put him in a trance 
You get bonus points. 
You can even try some brainwashing 
So that after all the cleansing 
The only thing he'll be thinking 
About is what he's reading. 
That is, your writing.
