Six-legged monstrosity
Sixty times smaller than me
You crawl by and I start to sweat
You fly by and I almost wet

Myself.

Exo-yucky-skeleton
(Move closer and I'll use this stone!)
The sight of you makes my skin crawl
And your touch does make me bawl

Real tears.
