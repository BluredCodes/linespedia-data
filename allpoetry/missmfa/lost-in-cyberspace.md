He was sent 
To lessen 
My innocence 
To intensify 
My inner sense 
Of direction 
To be my opposition 
A constant contradiction 
A benediction. 

He was sent 
To answer 
My questions 
To complement 
To supplement 
To re-establish 
Our connection, 
The one that existed 
Long before The Creation. 

He was sent 
To humiliate 
To elevate 
To complicate 
To levitate 
To sate 
To mate 
Me. 

He was sent 
I know. 
But I don't know
