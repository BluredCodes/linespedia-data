Oh no! 
Woe! Woe! 
Me, you 
Boo hoo! 
Price hikes 
More strikes 
No jobs 
Sob! Sob! 
No rain 
Again 
Ozone 
Half gone 
Mille- 
nium 
Ura- 
nium 
Disease 
Increase 
Racists 
Fascists 
More crime 
More grime 
No dimes 
Hard times
