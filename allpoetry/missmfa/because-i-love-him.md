Dormant volcanoes
Suddenly erupt.
Thoughts
Not at all apt.
Principles forgotten,
Yearning for his Eden,
Willing to turn Friends
Into Foes.

Dry riverbeds
Gradually overflow.
Touched
To my very core.
My life enriched,
Priorities switched,
Listening to my heart
'stead of my head.

Stormy skies
Presently calm.
He
Is my soul's balm.
Together we will travel,
And everyone will marvel,
At the light of love
In my eyes.(2)
