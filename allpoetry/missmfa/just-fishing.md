I have no bait 
At the end of my fishing line 
I plan to wait 
For the fish to choose to bite mine 

I have no bait 
I do not need to actively 
Participate 
For the fish to come hounding me 

I have no bait 
Cause my main aim is not to catch 
Food for my plate 
(But if I did, would I mind much?) 

I have no bait 
Cause my joy is in enjoying 
Life's sweet mandate 
So I'm sitting here, just fishingThis poem was inspired by an episode of "Touched by an Angel"
