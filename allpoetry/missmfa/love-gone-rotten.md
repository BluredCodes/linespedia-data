title: "Love gone rotten"

I see my precious dream 
Dissolve into a filthy stream... 
I hear my breaking heart 
Screaming from all the hurt... 
I smell the putrid stench 
Of love gone rotten... 
I taste the saltiness 
Of my tears of sadness... 
I touch the note you sent, 
The one that signalled the end... 

The last time I felt this alive 
Was the day you came into my life. 

My love for you is gone. 
My tears are as cold as stone. 
My senses overload. 
My prince, you are now a toad. 
My dream is shattered. 
My heart bruised and battered. 

But the last time I felt this alive 
Was the day you came into my life.
