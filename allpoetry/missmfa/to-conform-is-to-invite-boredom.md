You say I've changed 
That I'm different. 
I say thank you 
For the compliment. 
You say you don't 
Understand me. 
I say that's okay 
Just let me be. 
You say I'm bringing 
Down the whole clan. 
I say anyway 
The clan's no fun. 
You say I'm weird, 
Old-fashioned, insane. 
I say another compliment? 
Thank you again! 
You say I can't live 
In my own world. 
I say the word 'can't' 
Can't be used by this girl. 
You say I should 
Give up on my dreams 
I say I will but 
Only in your wildest, unimaginable dreams. 
You say join the club, 
Wear your uniform. 
I say to conform 
Is to invite boredom. 
You say your concern 
Is deepening. 
I say pardon? 
I wasn't listening. 
You say this and that, 
Here and there. 
I say what the hell, 
I don't care!
