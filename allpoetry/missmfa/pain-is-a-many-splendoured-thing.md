Pain is not a racist 
It does not discriminate 
Pain is not biased 
All must go through its gates 
Pain is multi-lingual 
Pain is international 
Pain is a many-splendoured thing! 

Pain can be chronic 
Pain can be acute 
Pain can be throbbing 
Pain can be mild 
Pain can be for a lifetime 
Or for just a nanosecond 
Pain is a many-splendoured thing! 

When someone hates you 
You might end up in pain 
When you love someone 
You might end up in pain 
When people are indifferent 
You might end up in pain 
Pain is a many-splendoured thing! 

Pain can make you cry 
Pain can make you scream 
Pain can bring you down to your knees 
Pain can dull your brain 
Pain can drive you insane 
Pain can bring you to your senses 
Pain is a many-splendoured thing! 

Pain may whisper to you 
Pain may shout at you 
Pain may hide from you for years 
Then one day jump out yelling "Surprise!" 
Pain may come from pleasure 
(And vice versa) 
Pain is a many-splendoured thing! 

Pain can come from a cold thing 
Pain can come from a burn 
Pain can come from a bad tooth 
Pain can come from a bomb 
Pain can be all over your body 
Pain can be all in your mind 
Pain is a many-splendoured thing! 

No pain is too small 
No pain is too big 
Pain is mysterious 
Pain is serious 
Pain is hilarious 
Pain deserves three cheers! 
Pain is a many-splendoured thing!
