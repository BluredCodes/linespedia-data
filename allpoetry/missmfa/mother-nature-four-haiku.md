Lightning 

Patterns of white light 
Painted on a black easel 
That is the night sky. 

----------------------- 

Erosion 

Multi-coloured stones 
Are Mother Nature's ice cream; 
Water is her spoon. 

---------------------- 

A winter's day 

A carpet of white 
Welcomes the yellow sunlight 
On a winter’s day. 

---------------------- 

Harmony 

Sky, wind, land and sea 
Nature is in harmony 
As we all should be.
