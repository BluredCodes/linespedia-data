Addiction to rhyme 
Is not a crime 
I do it all the time 
Cause it's sublime. 

And even if it were 
A crime worse than murder 
I’d still do it behind bars 
Just to piss of my captors. 

I’d yell, "I won’t be stopped! 
Even if my hair is cropped 
Even if my head is chopped 
I will still get doped!"
