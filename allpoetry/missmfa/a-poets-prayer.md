This road that has been chosen for me 
Is paved with broken glass and insufficient apathy. 

I pray for glass-proof shoes. 
I pray for tranquilizers for my Muse. 

This sky under which I'm required to fly 
Is dotted with fatigue and pessimistic fliers-by. 

I pray for a spare right arm. 
I pray for Sadistic Critics to come to harm. 

These waters that I currently live in 
Are lifeless and unclean. 

I pray for my very own Life Boat. 
I pray for a variety of pure thoughts. 

This prayer that I'm praying 
Cannot end without me saying 

I pray for a lifetime supply of AlphaBetiCal letters. 
I pray for a lifetime supply of pen, paper and publisher. 

Amen.
