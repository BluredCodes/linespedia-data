Alone again attacking away familiar feelings of aching
	Bloated bones bursting brain the secret signs of burning
	Consistent crying cursing craving wishing wanting crazying
	Emotional overload
	My soul is floored
	Feels like I've been hit by a lightning rod
	It's all coming out, everything that was stored
	Someone please decode
	This emotional overload
	Dangerous dark disturbing desperate thoughts tormenting deeply
	Empty echoes eliciting energies that torture endlessly
	Fanatic fantasies far-fetched fatalities ubiqutous and fruitlessly unfolding
	Emotional overload
	My soul is floored
	Feels like I'm being abhorred
	It's too much, I think I'll go backwards
	Someone please decode
	This emotional overload
	Groan!Gnash!Gurgle!Gasp!Cough!Choke!Gulp!
	Heaven!Hell!History!Hysterics!Omigod!Overload!Help!
	Emotional overload
	My soul is floored
	Feels like I'm speed-driving along a slippery road
	Just point me towards the Straight-jackets' ward
	Where someone will decode
	This emotional overload
