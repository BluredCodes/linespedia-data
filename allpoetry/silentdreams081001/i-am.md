---
title: "I Am"

I am your worst fear.
I am the one you dread.
I am the most frightful thing.
I'm the one that lives in your head.

I am your death.
I am your life.
I am the danger.
I'm the one that causes your strife.

I am your enemy.
I'm the bite you can't chew.
I am your stalker.
I am YOU
