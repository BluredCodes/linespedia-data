title: "Sing To the Lord"

I bow before you,I am small.You are great Lord,Be my all.I have done wrong Lord,need you to show me the way.Walk beside me Lord,beside me today.I call out to you,with my meek voice.Lord forgive me,your way is my choice.Take my weakness Lord,mold me in your hand.Make me better,a leader for man.Help me to bring the masses,to your feet today.All these things and more,in Your Name I pray!Does it need to be longer? And does if flow well?
