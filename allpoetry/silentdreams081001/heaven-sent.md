When I first saw you,something clicked.I knew you were the one,the one God had picked.Destined to love you,one-hundred percent.I went helpless to my knees,crying in my decent.A victim of chance,I fell for you.My 'walls' crumbled down,and my heat went too.Know you hold my heart,in the palm of your hands.And my body responds,to your wishes and commands.While we are together,I'm devoted to you.Helplessly, hoplessly, knowingly,forever too.Forever though,I hope we last.We may not,but you must know that you'll never be my past.You are my future,What i look forward too.Your my every hope and wish,and this is true.In my eyes,you are perfection.Your every move and word,giving voice to your reflection.You're my Heavensent,my glorious blessing.Without whom I could never,have escaped my depression.Every breath I get to breath,every thing I get to do,Every word I will ever get to say,Is only because of you!
