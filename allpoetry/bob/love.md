L-ove or Lust,which is it that will bring out the romance between two lovers
O-ptimism that makes one wonder if it was meant to be fate.
V-engence is the last thing that u can think of when he looks in your eyes and says "I love you"
E-arth - that someday both you and your lover will never be parted from this world
