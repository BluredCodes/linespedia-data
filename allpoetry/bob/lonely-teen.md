She sits in the dark corner,
while the jukebox blasts music into the walls around her.
The darkness engulfs her soul as the night gets older,
until a small light focuses on her face.

Slowly a shadow moves into the dark corner,
a young male with a smile to good to be true.
He places his hand in front of her,
beckoning for her to take it.

She remains still and silent, without a breath,
She looks at him and says "Im sorry, I can't".
The pain and agony of loneliness was to strong to combat,
even with an outstretched hand of help.
