She enters the room leaving a gasp,
Her long slender legs pass,
Leaving eyes glancing left, right and center.

She takes her seat next to the window,
the rowdy office place quietens down,
and flowers begin flodding the singles desk.

She smells the flowers and admires the cards,
but looks elsewhere for love,
love in the bottom of the heart that brings out the best in a person.
