Life is such a fragile concept,
The birth of a baby,
through to a death of a lover,
allow tears to develop and fall from the cheek.

Yet the fun times,
times of laughter,
times of excitement,
times of amusement,
Bring out the best in one.

Life is so fragile,
make the most of it.
