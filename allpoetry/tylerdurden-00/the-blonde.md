Such an explosion your name was to me,
	Like the one before you, you're a native to wonder.

	And such a wonder lingers, as I've seen those eyes for years.
	But to see two pairs now, well, Life grew on me.

	I've known you for so long now, but now that you're here,
	I welcome you with open arms, and hope to feed you some years.

	Your tounge has no place now, but trust me, it will.
	And your mind will surpass me someday, most likely in my wisdom. 

	And I'll be here for you, should life shed your fortune.
	I'll be here to dispense whatever calls your name.

	So the doors are open now, for awhile, anyway...
	I hope you extract the best of things while on this side.To Little Nicky
