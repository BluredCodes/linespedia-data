This world is a widow,Ravaged by fears.She can't render sorrow,Robbed even of tears.A widow of love,A widow of peace.A widow of smiles,A widow of ease.		A widow of God,A widow of faith,A widow of warmth,A widow of grace. She's a widow of ethics,A widow of the free,And I'm sure one day soon, She'll be a widow of me.Good times... huh? I thought up this happy lil'' sucker on the way home.
