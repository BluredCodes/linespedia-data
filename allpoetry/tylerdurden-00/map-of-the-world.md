I lost my map of the world.

	And now I face each hour disarmed of my will, disgruntled with such lack of vision.
	
The terrain of life proves treacherous and steep, yet abundant in warmth, when caught.	
	
I forget the direction that took me here, and only guess at the one I'm facing. But such matters lose weight in the midst of epiphanies.
	
I stumble on the sand, is it me or is it time? Perhaps constraint is in debt to both.
	
I hope to attain direction, yet hunger for it's absence upon discovery. Such a virtue cannot strive for martordym.
	
And now I look ahead... or behind, and march on. Eyes useless, feet trembling, my destination has no name, but will one day adorn one. 

	I destroyed my map of the world.Again.. bored.
