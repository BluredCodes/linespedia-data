---
title: "Message to a Mirror"
writer: "tylerdurden 00"
slug: "message-to-a-mirror"

Forgive me for such a seldom eye.
		For your flawless service leaks abasement upon my face.
	
I find myself fleeing to darkness, 
		For only there are you powerless.
	
And you erode me with such logic,
		Erode time with such ease.
	
	Can you not set aside yesterday for tomorrow,
		Instead of shedding it tonight?	
	
	When my vision befalls you,
		You befall all that dismantles me....
	
Cracks and seems that lied in the dark
		Now shine and bellow at your command.
	
I dread the truth that you inject into me...
		I dread the lines you have yet to draw on my face.

	Why can you not just lie?I hate looking in the mirror...
