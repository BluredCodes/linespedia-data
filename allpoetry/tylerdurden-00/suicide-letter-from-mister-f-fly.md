Dear World,	

	I just can't take the pain
	That this cruel world inflicts.
	I'm tired of being picked on
	By head lice and ticks.

	At least they can suck blood
	Which I cannot do.
	I'm just a fly,
	I'm attracted to poo.

	I get swatted at constantly,
	Which I cannot stand.
	I hover over urine,
	I throw up when I land.

	So please don't condemn me,
	When I say I wanna die.
	I just want to go
	To that big poo pile in the sky.

	And so now I will fly
	Into that big blue light.
	And when you hear a 'zap!'
	Then all will be right.
			

			Your pal,
				Mr. Fruit Fly
