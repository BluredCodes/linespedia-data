I just can't read the text
That the world has provided,
And I can't speak the verse
That leaves it divided.

My heart swims in sorrow
Against currents of tears,
And the picture once gloried
Now crumbles and smears.

I hear the music
Of the precarious song,
But find fear in the wisdom
That the lyrics preach wrong.

And when closing my eyes
Is my weapon of choice,
I find that their weapon 
Is the theft of my voice

So I sit in my grave,
Awaiting some Light.
Perhaps Wrong's blunder
