Just when you think all is lost
Something is there, glistening on the horizon
Untouched and pure
Waiting there, just for you

It’s your peace of mind
Back from the dead
Rejuvenated and ready
To save you from insanity

For me, that something is you
You were there, illuminating the darkness
Untouched and pure
Waiting there, just for me

Unexpected but not unwelcome
You saved me from certain insanity
Just by being there, being a friend
