title: "abuela, mi amor"

communication
a birth right
snatched
repossessed
taken back

except with me
to others, it is incoherent jibberish
they just nod and smile
make you a five-year-old again

you taught me
an innumerable amount
of life's wisdom and lessons:
don't give up
keep trying
keep talking
keep doing what's best for you

the torch has been passed
from you, to me
it's my chosen duty
to remind you of your life's lessons
i love you, grandmom.
keep doing what's best for you.my grandmom had a stroke on wednesday. i went to visit her at the hospital...she changed so much. this once proud, strong, self-efficient woman was reduced to a mumbling child. she couldn't hold a coherent conversation with anyone but me. raw emotions here, but i had to put them down. no critical replies here.
