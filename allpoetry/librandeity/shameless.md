title: "Shameless"

Waking for you
wanting you
wordless in my yearning

Laughing at our playful advances
knowing I crave more
more of your touch
more of your burning eyes...
My soul aches

Silently screaming
Touch me
linger
Take me
claim me as yours

...and never let gosweet flirtation - a touch there, a glance here - lover's torture
