problems wide & varied

emotions deep & intense

danger imminent



struggle as i may

to avert disaster

cooperation canna' be found

and so...

we are closer than i feared



displays of angst are due

bottled up instead

fizzling inside

hiding behind the glass

you scream the obscenities



warning:

glass breaks

and so do you.
