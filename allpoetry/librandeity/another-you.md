Blissful, happy, complete

But then,

Anger, frustration, defeat.

At first,

Your angel

But then,

Betrayed

Your feelings swayed...



So what now?

What to do...

I’ll go out

Find another you!

One who’ll pick me up

Only to drop me down

Proud to wear your bloody crown

Take my heart,

Rip it in two.
