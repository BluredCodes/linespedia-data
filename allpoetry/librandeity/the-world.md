The World
A place cool and dark
Everyone wrapped up in their own sense of superiority

The World
A place created by dreams
A place destroyed by insecure and ignorant idiots

The World
America
Our society is really fucked up

America
A "free" place
A place where the bars on windows seem like prison bars to children

America
A "diverse" place
A place where people are afraid to be different and sheild themselves in numbers

America
A place created by visionaries
A place where the real visionaries, doctors, teachers and mentors, are ignored

The World
This is my dream for you:
To become your current opposite
To become a place warm and light
A place where everyone's own ideas are preferred and accepted
A place where everyone has a heart, mind, body, and soul

World,
This is my dream for you
