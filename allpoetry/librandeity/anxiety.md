Arms left empty
Aching for their desire
Craving the fruition of their dreams

Arms left disappointed
Lonely in the dark
Mourning silently in your absence

Arms left dreaming
Fantasizing of nights yet to come
Anticipating holding you through the night

And so I lay here
With these empty arms
Waiting for you to come crawling into them
Here, where you are home.
