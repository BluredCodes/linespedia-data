lines

smooth curves, sensual sight

let me explore yours

leaving trails that mark my path

tracing every inch of your body



lines

wet and dripping

beads of sweat flowing down your back

and tears leaking

from the love that can't be contained



lines

marks of my fingers

desperately pulling you closer

...but never close enough



we are lines

tangled forever
