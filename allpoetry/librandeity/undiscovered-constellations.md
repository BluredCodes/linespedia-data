soft as a snowflake
glistening in the light
your touch flickers against my skin

gracefully floating
huvering, enveloping my body
your breath warms me
as you whisper words
dripping with honey from your heart

your soul is gentle
and always roams back home
to remind me
of how deeply you touch
everywhere

I feel everything
past, present, future...
all at once
as my heart rearranges the stars
to answer you with
Forever.for Chris
I love you...
