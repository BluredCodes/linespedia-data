I wait

And wait

And wait



For some ray of light to seep through

For a glimmer of hope that you're near

For a sign from somewhere that something will change



So I wait

And I wait

And I wait



Maybe you've forgotten I'm here

Maybe something is in your way

Something is not as it should be



So, I'll have faith in you

Faith that you'll come through

Faith that in just one minute, you'll walk through that door



And with this faith, I'll wait

And wait

And wait

But I'll wait with a smile, knowing I'll see you soonthis is so lacking something...help?!
