kisses and hugs

comforting...

and misleading



there to play

but never to be

childish games we must play

to make it seem worthwhile



there you are

everything i could want

nothing that i need



we play one more day

outside in the sun

until the weather changes



today:

sunny with clouds of delusion

tomorrow:

cloudy with drops of realityeh, something i just put together. how much does it suck? i'm thinking of making it a series. comments? suggestions?
