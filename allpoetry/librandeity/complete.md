Every time we touch
I’m reminded of why I love you
You enrapture me
Make me leave the rest of the world behind
Make me concentrate on you – 
Feeling you, loving you, healing you
In every way I can

You look at me...
In those sparkling blue eyes
There is such a caring stare
And while completely silent,
You let me know
Without a doubt
That you feel the same

I count on that loving gaze
To get me through the day
For as long as I see that sparkle in your eyes,
All is right in my world

When we’re together
Something inexplicable happens
I want only to make my love happy
Nothing matters but you

The electricity between us heats our room
Making it impossible to ignore
And we become one
In body, mind, and soul

In the simplest terms,
You love me.
Something that surprises me everyday
Rejuvenates me everyday
And reminds me everyday
Of why I love you.
