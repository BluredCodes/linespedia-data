I miss spending time with you
You’ve always calmed me down...

Your touch leaves me breathless, aching for more
Your eyes make me weightless with nary a care
I wish I could see them now...

Snuggling in your arms,
My nose nuzzled in your neck,
I feel like I’m wrapped in a protective sheath
Barred from harmful outside sources
Come cloak me in safety tonight...

You’re all I need
Why won't you come near?
Why are we forever estranged?

I’m overcome with love in your presence
Remind me of how that feels tonight...

Love me with your eyes
Love me with your soul
Love me with your words
Love me with your hands
Love me tonight!
More importantly, love me forever...
