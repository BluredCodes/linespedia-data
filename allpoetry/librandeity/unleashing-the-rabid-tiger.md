Unleashing the rabid tiger

Caught in my mind

Plaguing my heart with distrust

She is deadly

To all those not her



She craves that refreshing change

From pacing in her cage

She wants to thrash everything in sight

She knows no friend, only prey does she smell

Smells the fear she conjures up

And it brings a smile to her face



While I long to set her free

Along her path of total unending destruction

I can't bring myself to cut the chord...

And so she destroys only her surroundings

She destroys only me...i wrote this in frustration. i was fed up and angry at everything and nothing imparticular. i wanted to destroy, thrash, maim, but i couldn't bring myself to do it, which infuriated me even further...



so she destroys only me....
