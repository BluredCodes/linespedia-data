I long to feel your soft embrace
And the warmth of your breath upon my face
And the mystic sparkle in your eye
And the wholesome love that you supply

Only you have made me feel
Like you've melted my heart of steel
The way you talk; your words - so sweet
With only you could I feel complete

It's with you and not another
That I feel we could discover
The cool, crisp caverns of ecstasy
And the true love that is our destiny

It's in this love that I've drowned
And it's with this love that we're bound
And it's in this love that I feel
So happy and calm, yet so surreal

With this note, I hope I lure
The one I'll love forever more
It's with you that I lose myself
And it's because of you, love's the greatest wealth.This was my first love poem written specifically for someone. Unfortunately, I never got the chance to give it to him. Do you think he would have liked it, or would he have been repulsed by it?
