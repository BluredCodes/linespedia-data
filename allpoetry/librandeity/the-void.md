She walks around all day

With a smile upon her face

No one knows what’s going on

Something’s there...without a trace



She seems to be quite “normal,”

All giddy and upbeat

But only she knows the pain

That is her defeat



Every now and then

The hurt somehow seeps through

But she quickly tucks it in

And no one ever knew



The hurt, the pain, the emptiness,

They chew away inside her

On the outside, she laughs it up

And no one is the wiser



She thinks she’s so clever

She thinks that no one knows

But he’s go her figured out;

He’s realized all her woes.



That façade of a smile fades

And a genuine one appears

For finally love has found a way

To silence all her fears.
