title: "Camel Toe"

Why is it that it must show?
Ugly girls have it so

I love you like a dying crow
I see it everywhere that i go

whenever i see you grow
i want to blow.........chunks

your green and red and yellow
to me a friendly foe

tis you.....camel toe
