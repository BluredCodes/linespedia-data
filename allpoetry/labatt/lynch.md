Lynch I Loathe you
although to me you wanna do

Touch your lawn people every day
Touch Your piss chute in every way

why must you scare me and taunt me so
I have a penis that you must blow
you crabby old skank hoe

You scare the fuck out of me as u see
go to Ratazzis dance and pee

thank you
