Doo Doo Doo
Spew Spew Spew
Dew Dew Dew
Croo Croo Croo
Goo Goo Goo
You You You
Foo Foo Foo
Woo Woo Woo
Zoo Zoo Zoo
DOO DOO DOO

hows that for no meaning?
whores
