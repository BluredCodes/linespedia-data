I have a dog
Dogs are cool
sometimes...
my dog eats its own shit
Blah, pretty gross
i have 3 dogs
one is smart
another is dumb 
and the other one... 
eats its own shit
but i learned to except it
the way it should be
i just hope
it doesnt drink its own pee
