You are my friend
You Are my funnel.

Beer travels through you
Through your clear beautiful tunnel.

The Night we funneled while we camped
All of our blankets sure were damp.

But yet we funneled you with your beer
Simon drank 2, what a queer.

Aaron could barely get it down
no one laughed, not even a sound.

But Kevin Mike and Joe got it down good
We will remember those nights, funneling in the woods...
