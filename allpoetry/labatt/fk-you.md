FUCK YOU
you gay ass whore
you sit there and tell me what
my poems mean and nothing more
to me, eh, you are truly a slut
so FUCK YOU
and dont read them
FUCK YOU 
and all the rest too
FUCK YOU
and FUCK your mom
FUCK YOU 
you fucking deutche bag
FUCK YOU
and your family too
...Hey! guess what?
FUCK YOU 

goes out to all the people who bad mouth my poems...wait til i read yours, stupid deutche bags.
