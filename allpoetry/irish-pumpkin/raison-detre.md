~



Reflecting back on the night
secret kisses infiltrated by moonlight~

Laughter swaying with the gentle breeze
delightful shivers tickling my skin~

Serpent tongues caressing my ear
nimble fingertips undressing~

Heart bleeding tendermost adoration
shameless thirst satisfactorily quenched~

Shared warmth annihilating the bitter cold
plucking dried leaves from our hair~

Relishing glory in the moments
mere words cannot do justice proper~

Thinking back on the magik
morning prolonged from breaking~

And wond'rings of what shall come
bringing smiles to our inseparable lips~

The future spreads her silken wings before us
past joys eclipsed by every new dawning~

You, my Love, are my Raison d'etre.



~Raison d'etre is french, I believe.  It translates as reason for living or justification for being.  Dedicated to my Irishclam, who brings me new happiness with each moment spent in his presence.
