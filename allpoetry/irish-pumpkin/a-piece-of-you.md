~



Leaned back against your knees~
warm and safe in your strong embrace.

You're tracing delicate spirals~
upon my energized skin.

Lights down low~
comfort in our closeness.

You whisper sweetly into my ear~
telling secrets of the future.

I stare into your blue eyes~
losing myself in the depth of the orbs.

Time reincarnated~
once an enemy, now an ally.

A meeting of minds~
melding of kindred hearts.

Two become One~
you keep a little piece of me.

Lonely void conquered~
I own a piece of you also.

Bullet-proof glass~
Protecting our singular soul.
