title: "Throwing Away Your Life"

Your happy life is coming to an end.
For you have chose to drink your life away.
Drinking has became an everyday chore,
And you wonder why I'm not around you anymore.
You've had one to many chances.
You don't deserve anymore.
You were an abuser, and a drunk, you never had time for anything else.
You hurt us to many times,
To many tears I've cried.
Living with you was driving me into depression.
All this does not compare to the hurt  you caused me on my grad night.
For im glad you couldnt make it, for it prevented a big fight.
I brought you back a rose,
And I gave you a hug,
You reaked of nothing but booze.
Tears started to run down my cheek as I slowly approached the car,
Then that very night you went out to the bar.
You know how much you mean to me, 
And I wish you would get some help,
But you say you don't have a problem although I know for a fact you do.
If you really cared, 
You would of changed your ways,
But now its to late for I am no longer a permanit part of your life.
