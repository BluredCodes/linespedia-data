I tell you all the time that you're a beautiful girl, 
But you can't seem to get that into your head.
You're always putting yourself down,
As if you wish you would die,
For if anything ever happened to you I would cry.
You mean the world to me,
You know how to lift me up when I'm down,
But whenever I look at you all i see is a frown.
Its as if your always sad,
Nothing no know one can cheer you up,
But behind all your sadness there has to be some happiness.
So keep trying your hardest to turn that frown into a smile,
Because then you will realize,
That all your effort was well worth the while. 
Look up in the sky oh so beautiful and blue,
And think about all the people who love and care about you.
