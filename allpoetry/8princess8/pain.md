title: "Pain"
writer: "8princess8"
slug: "pain"

Does it every stop,
Do things ever get better,
When will I ever feel happiness again.
All the fighting and screaming,
All the cuts and the bruises,
All the bad memories still cross my mind.
All the tears that have ran down my cheeks,
All the sadness I've felt.
All the times I have thought of killing myself,
Just to much to handle.
