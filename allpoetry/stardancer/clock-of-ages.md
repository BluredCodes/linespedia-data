The ebony clock
Darker then the forbidding sky
Belongs to fates cruel hands
Holding time captive
As it guides the heavenly cosmos
Setting the sun
Yet hiding the moon
For eternity is strives amongst the darkened shadows
Hurling the arms of fortune
Through the mists of lost destinies
Mocking those who are blind
Disillusioned in perfect lives 
Stealing the breath shrouded in gold
Leaving but tear drops to float upon the whispering winds
As it’s sovereign cloud ascends the clock of ages
