title: "Destiny"
writer: "Stardancer"

Dancing breezesCarry smokeOf agonizing screamsAs a water droplet in an oceanThey drift away From hearts of the belovedMysterious soulsCarried in the heavenly currentFlying freely through Their untraveld pathsFate with out an ownerStands waiting and yet is never satisfiedFor deaths murderous ovensHave burned the innocent stargazes And forever they floatUpon the cheek of destiny
