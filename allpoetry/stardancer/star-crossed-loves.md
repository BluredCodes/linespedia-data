title: "Star-crossed loves"

Star-crossed lovers,

Destined to forever be together

As there feeble hearts intertwine

Under the radiance of the heavenly sky

 

Hearts filled with joy and hopes

Hopes that one day will be for another

For the perfect romance

Can be the worse

 

Lies and under hidden truths

Mists of emotions twirling

To yet another

As the lost one stares

 

Wishing that once again 

For the perfection that was never there

As once the humans are crushed and left to pity themselves

In the deepest chambers of the heart
