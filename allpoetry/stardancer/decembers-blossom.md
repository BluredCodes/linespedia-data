title: "December's Blossom"
writer: "Stardancer"

The slumberous haven
That hath been covered with crystallized white
Pale moon beams
Dance upon the serenity
The lonely life
Stubborn to subject
To the harshness of fate
Deaths kiss doth 
Play upon the petals
Beckoning it to rest
As the valiant blossom
Refuses to surrender to
The slings and torments of lifeBasically about never giving up no matter how hard it is not to surrender.
