title: "Dreamers eyes"

Blue moon eyes
Gazing upon 
A white satin veil
Of heaven's innermost dreams
Kissing destiny's fortune
And playing with fates anger some wrath
Inverting reality into wishes
Covering the magnificent stars
With its own beauty
Claiming the visions of one
While stealing with fears
With one celestial breath
