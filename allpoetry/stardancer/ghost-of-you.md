Curse the never ending night
That lets the tears kiss my cheek
Bitter sweet tears
Of true loves joy

The nights gentle breeze
Dance upon my skin
With no tender embrace to hold

My heart shatters
With the sound of the closing door

Baby you should have known I’d cry.
You should have known I’d be lonely.

Now I sit alone
With but a Ghost of youThis is the first Sad love poem i have written.... i hope you all like it.
