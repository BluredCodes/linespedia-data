The straighten path of destinies trimFall into the twisted in the sandOf corruptions hollowShadow of darkened lightShrouds the fallen wishesHate’s blossom sown in the soulCracking the dry earthAs the thorns force their way to be seenAgony’s cruel tearsCrystallize and burry the earth In Serene whiteFreezing the compassionAllowing the weakness to be purgedAs the hideous beast of hatred evilHobbles its way down the pathInto the world………
