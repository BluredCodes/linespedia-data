---
title: "Heaven's Wander"
writer: "Stardancer"
slug: "heavens-wander"
source: "AllPoetry"

Star lit dipped moon beamsKiss the bleak morbid skyBitter serene tears from heavenDance upon the croaked fingers of fateWhile Fortunes lustful lipsBreathes calmness upon the hill topsGolden laid roadsDiverge into destiny’s webClaiming untold promisesOf what lies aheadWhile the lonesome wanderChooses oneFor one is all he may take.
