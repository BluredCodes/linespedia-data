title: "911"
writer: "Stardancer"
slug: "911"
source: "AllPoetry"

Twin brothersOf a pale faced childFall in chaotic rageFace no brighter then the moonWith oceanic eyesThat steal splendor from the starsLong glorious locks of brilliant redThat dance upon the childlike faceFire flickers in her eyesAs hate explodes in the 45 degreesThe brothers fall helplessly to the unforgiving groundAs the steal innocent soulsSatanic smoke covers the blazing sunAs chimes of help arrive yet are in debt to timeSweet silver tears fall from the eyesWill all the glory of hopeHer suffering will be raisedUpon the ever soaring eagles wings
