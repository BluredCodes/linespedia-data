Ghosts of past lust and desire
Dance upon the tainted parchment
Naked canvas of the ingenious artist
Yet to be clothed witth the goddess's beauty
The hour of ectasy
Soon shall toll the clock
As lover's embraces soon entwine
Under a starbound sky
Ghosts once again streak past
Leaving but memories upon the wall
