title: "Love's Bliss"
writer: "Stardancer"

Two souls embarking on a joyous journey
Guided by their hearts
And lead by destiny

Their hearts beat
As to form a symphony of love’s songs

Together they join in the greatest of all bliss
Ruby red lips
Meet as to place sin upon the other
As they steal a sacred kiss

Neither afraid
Yet both anxious
To feel the great serenity
Of the stream of love

Their hearts are blinded
By a brilliant joyous light
Blocking all visions but of the other
As they share in the greatest of all human emotions
Love…..
