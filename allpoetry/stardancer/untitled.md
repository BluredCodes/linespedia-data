What formality
Lies hidden
Behind the obscure door of lies
Only hearts of purity
Could avoid the pool
Of corrupt lies
Sovereign youth
Yet to be intrigued
Into the murderous circle
Of degeneracy
Though as the hands 
Slowly circle around
Corruptions croaked hand
Shall wind its way
Into the innocent hearts
Taking root and growing
Into a blossom of hatred
A nightmare now reality
Now say
Do your eyes see what I do
With all your gull can you look into my face?
