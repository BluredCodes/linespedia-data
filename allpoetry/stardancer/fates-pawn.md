title: "Fates pawn"
writer: "Stardancer"

Promiscuous hiderStolen by shadowsWith gray fearful eyesPraising upon the locked doorPawn in fates brilliant gameJester at the feastDrunken with ryeStealing the mask of fortuneDrinking the bitter devastation of fateTaking the final wishOf omniscient power Golden leaves dance upon the breezeAs eminent gates paint the horizon
