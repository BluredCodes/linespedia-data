title: "We......"
writer: "Stardancer"

source: "AllPoetry"

We  wear the make upBecause all the popular people wear itWe get  the shirtsTo look like a famous personWe buy the stylish pantsTo fit into the groupWe buy the shoesBecause everyone else has themWe wear our hair a certain wayTo mirror a magazine imageWe get in shapeTo be fit like our role modelWe don’t speak our mindsBecause others won’t like itWe sell our originalityTo be like everyone elseNot my normal style... I'm trying something new but i think this is ok...
