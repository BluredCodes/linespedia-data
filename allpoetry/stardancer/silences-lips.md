title: "Silences Lips"
writer: "Stardancer"

Perilous rubbleShourded by scarlet tearsBlanketing the infernois earthDiclexic with the hate and sorrowStarving for justices holy vowWithout judgment upon their soulsThe mirrors were shatteredTimeless sand stolenAs debt to time and luck runs dryRivers of souls, break the em battlementsBitter sweet tears,Float into the sea of silence.
