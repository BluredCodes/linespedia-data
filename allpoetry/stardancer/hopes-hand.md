title: "Hopes hand"
writer: "Stardancer"

Unnoticeably standingAmongst numerous souls.Yet still aloneAs though the whisper of silence,Has breathed upon me.Waiting for a hand,To steal me from the dark silence.And guide me to the lightened joy.  As the joyous light grows more and more distant.Darkness encases me to its solitude.A beacon of light,Shines bleakly in the darkness.And a hand steals me away.The curse of silence,Was stolen from my lips. By just one single kiss……
