Silver drops of SerenityFall slowly to the slumberous earthThough to cover it with a veil of lifeOver the pebbles of humanityA triumphant cry roars outAs to claim victory of the fire goddessWile flashes of hate streak the morbid sky.Driven in uncertaintyTo the very core of my essenceHidden by valiant masksYet surrounded by fear aloneWhile the celestial monarchy Claim victory, or suffers defeat.
