title: "Painting with the colors of the wind"

Upon the naked canvas
I bless the mystical colors
That do paint the celestial universe
Brilliant Red that steals the beautiful evening sky
Majestic Orange that kisses the morning sun
Gleeful Yellow rays that dance upon lovers lips
Royal blue that flows from the waterfall thrashing upon the parched rocks with serenity
Calming green grass on the plains that blows in the gentle breeze
Gracious black that steals the night in its darkened complexion
Freezing white crystals that kiss the slumberous earth
The colors whirl amongst the wind
Blessing the harsh earth.
