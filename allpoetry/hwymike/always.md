Always               As you slip into bed to rest your beautiful soul               Letting your ears ring of our passion fling               Willing your dreams to be filled of us (Thrilling)               As feeling the heated breeze of my breath               You and I in necking or nookie the never-ending need               Sweethearts in bedtime affairs, feeding each other cookies...Copyright © July 2001 Michael L.
