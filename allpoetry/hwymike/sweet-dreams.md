Sweet Dreams

Sweet dreams little ones.
Close your eyes to sleep.

All through the night,
holding Teddy bear tight.

Sweet dreams little ones.
Keeping us playing insight.

Tomorrow’s things will wait.
Till we awake for breakfast.

Sweet dreams honey.
Lie close let’s snuggle.

I’ll be your sheath,
Yours truly comforter.

Sweet dreams family,
All tucked in tight.

Hope you are ready,
I’m turning out the light...
