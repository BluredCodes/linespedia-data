Monumental

As I stood there looking at the broken home
I was eight and realized the change of fate.
In patience dreamed of what I wanted to be…
 

“Love of mine” I became a “Pen Man”
writing my “Pennies” of thought’s
“Thirsty” for “Loves Wealth” with 
the “Lover of My Soul”  shinning
like a “Diadem Gaily”

Then in “DAYJAVOO” 
“When” I unveiled the “Bull of the jungle”
“The Fathers wish,” a “Sky Love” was “Wings”
for “Freedom” as “On running” the “Push”
was “Shattered…” “A Days Ways” “Reaching” 

To “Understanding” all “Distant Night” with 
“Terse Faces” “ Encompassing knowledge”
of “Sweet Dreams” through “Country Summers”
“Time-less love…” “Keeping If” round and bright
as “June Moon Full”

Of the “Sacred” movement “Surface Change”
Making “Hello in Goodbye” from “The Skillet”
Flaming the “Fallen State” like the up-rise for-seen
In the “Princess Parade” around “The Store” under
“My Midday Sun”

Still in the “Shadows”… “Stars and You” reigns 
“Following King James” whether “Lost and Found”
while writing the “Note of the sleeping”
admits the “Moonlight Madness” battling the 
“Soul Bowl” winning “Always” surrounding

“In My Eyes” of thy heart theme’s “Dead Man Touches Living”
showing bright in “The Lover Of My Soul” 
created the “ Lovers Lane” to the golden 
“Monumental” telling of fifty of 
“My Midday sun” glow of father above…
