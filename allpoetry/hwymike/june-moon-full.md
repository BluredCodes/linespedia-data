Oh moon in the sky surrounded by cumulus 
                         clouds tall and dark 
                       Behold you’re showing through a crack with 
                      bold lighting so bright 
                       Capturing secrets in a glow covering 
                     The ground I drove along
                   Lingering clouds,laced the earth's face 
                    Streaming shadows with darkened lie’s
                  That lay covering shiny but mourned face’s   
                   Tailed times thus the trailing winds 
                     Moving the veil off and on
                      Like thieves’ hearts so cold then 
                       Stale as the June night air
                      Almost held strangled in the pureness of 
                       a hearts beat
                        Feelings began seeping through my mind of 
                       how many tales Could you’re sacred face tell
                        The hoodlums haunting laughter singing 
                         in my ears
                      A fear rushing my heart with feelings of crafty 
                       characters shadows moving across my roof top
                        Through the night rattling my rafters 
                         DR. Jekyll as MR. Hide or Frankenstein 
                      stumbling across my floor creeping and popping
                    After reading Poe’s revealing poetry lying silent 
                   like the scurrying mice of the fields
                    Terrified of the night owl and the wolfs howl 
                      In nights of June under full moon…
