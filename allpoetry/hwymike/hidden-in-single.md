Hidden In Single

It’s not for a moment
It’s a commitment
Like strings break
And rings fade.

But in the heart
It remains,
The torment of
Being alone…

Monday morning
Got that growling
Moan of alone
Misery full blown

My head full of 
Loving dreams
But my body 
Screams…

Like the throbbing
Of my feet 
My heart so needs 
To reach yours;

My thump, thump
You’re pitter-patter
Partners in comfort,
Strength our fortress

Tuesday morning;
Legs and feet lucky
Enough to sleep 
Out of the summer heat

Close to living on the 
Street, mind and soul
Are beat… Any thing
But work seems Greek

Your king waits,
No queen…
Just you in 
My dreams

When it’s the day,
Grace sends you my way
My heart maybe unrestrained
To say hoo-ray...
