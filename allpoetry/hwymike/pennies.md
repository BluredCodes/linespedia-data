Pennies

Pennies for your thoughts,
These are some of my thoughts on pennies.

Pennies have heads,
But they can't think.

Pennies have tails,
But they are flat.

Pennies are made of copper,
But some are called wheat.

Pennies: shinny or dark,
Found on sidewalks or under swings in the park.

If you are smart!

You will count and keep, 
all the pennies you find through out.

This is how pennies are pinched.

"Ouch"!

Don't be silly,
Pennies can't shout.
