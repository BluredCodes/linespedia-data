---
title: "Wings"

Wings of wisdom
                              Create successful flight

                                   Wings of wit
                                keep friendly skies

                           Wings of mothers and fathers
                            Shelter sons and daughters

                         Wings of metal or Medals of wings
                            These are more than plain,
                         they show some of life's greatest

                    Wings with wide span or wings that fast     flutter,
                            With friends we fly better 

                                 Wings of love
                         The height’s with everything in sight

                                  Wings
                             Are the caring things

                        Wings, we all have the ability to fly
                      Sometimes we need a lift to spread our wings

                                 Need a hand…

                      Let me share some of my love in flight
                          Hold on tight little wings
