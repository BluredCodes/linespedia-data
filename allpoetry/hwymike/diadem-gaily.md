Diadem GailyWhen you walked into the roomGrace fell bestowing divine love,Seen the glow a diva when youTurned showing meCrystal brown eyes ablazeBetwixed the sleek slopeAtop comely cheeksStarting my knees weakeningBeginning the bond so fondIn bedchamber beyond my loveLonged to find youAbove you from your ceilingMy soul seeping,seeking reflectionEffect shining bright in a coned rayFilled with majesties desireGlorifying ecstasy. The renowned Spirit speachlessIntervening you sound asleepWith astonishing ravish apparentAloof dreams shadowing my heartsAllureIn heavens above your spiritI spin on a pedestalUnderneath oceans of emotion,My treasure chest cherishedCrashing me crush, coining in the Craziest kiss, that I'm so glad theRest of the world missesMy goddess, my queenDiadem Gaily...Copyright © June 2001 Michael L.
