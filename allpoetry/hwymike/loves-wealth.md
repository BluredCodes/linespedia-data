Loves Wealth

Poverty nor riches,
What is integrity?
For money or love?

Is the past a saftey deposit box,
And is the future a checking account?

Behind every great man,
there is a graceful woman!
Beside every good woman,
is a grateful man!

In front of all of us is knowledge!
Success is bound with togetherness!

The hugs are they the hearts?
Kisses are they the fruit?

The eyes say look!
But the arms they feel!
To say, 
YES,YES!
It is real!

The words they are the way,
The tone of voice,
It is the light!

Hold fast the words!
Keep the tone for instruction!

Learn from destruction,
But consider the triumps of communication!

If it is needful for me,
Then it must be needful to others!

So to say one will prosper as the other!

   IN LOVES WHEATH TOGETHER!
