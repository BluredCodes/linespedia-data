Writers Proverb 1


In a block, but mind is round
The heart utter, then just a mere
Shaky fingers of a well pen,
A delousing dread…

Placed palm upon rambled thoughts
All wrong. Themes seem gone,
With scattered stanza stilled
Achieving anishitive to accomplish…

With a fist full of ink, the eyes clinched
In blink, as bitter in seeing a clean sheet
Pin pointing punctuation… The ever rise in 
Blood pressure…

Then through the red rays and strobe moving
Fumble the writer stumbled in swaggered…
Wayward of mind… With hand swat or pondering
To squat (Not to pee) but write something all would
