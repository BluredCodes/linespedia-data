Chariots of love willed wagons  covered in purple satin   sewn with real silver. Rubies attaching the windows  pearled white lace.Wheels of fine gold, royal in strength. As they roll over rocks still   never loosing pureness and shine.The door framed in diamonds  with a crystal handle.  Inside the Princess is soft and warm  with smile and charm.Raising her arms to wave with her  hands to the people in praise.The queen’s daughter is paraded in  prize and will someday be queen.She tis’ the fountain of future fame’s.                         In my hands I will hold the flame of glory gliding with glee, gallantly striding the front   of her parade.    Up top teams of horses with shoes of gold.      Thine coats shining like the silken dress    covering her heavenly body.  Of the finest cloth in the lands.  Hoping someday to be the leader of her everyday paradise parade...Copyright © July 2001 Michael L. Chick07/-/01,
