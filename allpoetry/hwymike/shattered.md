Shattered

When you can’t run from yourself,
nor hide the pain.

Been driven out of your mind,
over the strain.

Oh can’t they see this man,
trying to make ends meet?

The tears run in loving
fun memories…

These streets so winding 
with so much on my mind…

Working overtime for little 
more than a dime…

Oh can they see the footprints
of this shattered man…
