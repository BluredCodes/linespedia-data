THIRSTY

Thirsty is the day,
Hungry is the night!

Thirsty mind is full,
But empty!

Ever so thirsty hearts,
Pumping blood!

Thirsty for more of everything,
But not just anything!

Thirsty for sight, sweet or bitter,
Either or just to swallow!

Thirsty souls craved,
As the ocean never full!

Life's streams and rivers,
Full flowing!

Still man is thirsty!
