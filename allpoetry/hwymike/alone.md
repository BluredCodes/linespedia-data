Alone I travail to no availDreaming of the face behind a valeI so long to kiss the lips of…Alone I pass to and froIn my soul, foul and bitterOut of my grasp seems fitter…Fluttering my heart, like a capturedButterfly, in the sky my tears are not dryRolling like a river my skin quivers…Alone dreams a-drift drowning desolateSolo in remembrance, my member differenceComprehensions for correspondence…SOS low or high my sadness has no subsidenceThe desert storm has rid me of my mindThe mountain peak makes me shriek…Pondered if I’m a freak Or if it’s just I’m uniqueAlone I’m lone-a or loony...Have you ever felt like just...
