Sacred

      In the cool night out of the heat I sit perched,        
        plugging  away (Anxious) to unite with 
       the creature I seek underneath the surface. 
        Painted in rare sculpture, the beauty of nature 
     captured in my eyes sight; the sky laminated with the
   darkened greenness of tree tops shading the bone white
    skeletons glow on the banks,wild grass growth covering the
   steep drop going down to the breath taking reflection I
                       glide across.
     Graceful in peace of mind reflecting society’s only  
   remnant, the sound of eighteen wheels rolling down the 
 distant Hwy...  I cast away all my frustrations every fling of
      My six foot six-inch sward of glory in sacred war away 
                  from the world's zone.
     Entering into the perspective of my pursued pleasure,
      paralleling the waters edge, I nudge with indulged  
   reeling; steadly feeling the rhythmic humming of the blade
    spinning sending the waves to call the lurking predator of
                 the deep I so sacredly seek.
     Secretly I propel my soul beneath the surface to 
        relatively unveil what structure is there so I can  
      present the bait I feel will make the massive attack to
    feel the (Striking) jolt as the predator is my prey and
         will be captured in the war of my peace fight,
                            sacred...
