Love Adrift

Furthest of near be my loving dear. I feel the need to breed.
Sharing it all with you. This is so real but you’re so far away.

My eyes so slowly close, to open my arms
Heart too, as you slip from thin air into them.

Running in my mind on our souls this love does travel.
As well our mouths moan and groan sail adrift on the winds.

Through the standing still… Earth shattering… Heaven moving…
Timeline on fast forward mission of our ron-da-voo (lapsing time)

Floating us in my hearts glass casing bubble that I hold only you.
Where your lips never move but say a million words.

Here there is no them… Only us our lighthouse lit glowing of our souls
Bestowing for our eyes comely enchantment.

So much time, so little loving you.
Touch too much, feelings of needing you so much.

Faiths driven, liven the loving you for real dream.
Like a silent scream…(Souls confessions realm.)

Every night my site takes us to this place it feels so right.
Feeling the sensations of your absent touch.

Through out the day in my hearts eye I see the stress in yours.
The tears that stream down your face I taste on my lips.

Locked in secret ron-da-voos of me and you,
All the things I love to love about you.

To turn the key we close our eyes, open our hearts 
And send all our love through our souls secret ron-da-voos…
