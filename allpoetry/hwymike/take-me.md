Take Me

I stand here all alone
No queen on the other throne.
Feeling like a dog chewing on a air bone
Be gone my frozen heart, let my soul to fly.

Take me my love, kiss me endless
Run your hands lengthy through my loving soul
Bowls me over in ravish treat my tarnished being
Shine the way to our fulfilling passion place.

Graceful queen, glean your touch release my tortcher
Recapture our united desire; let me feel your wanders
Let me fulfill your empty loving cup, supply loving fire
Taking the flames on to a higher stretch.

Take me my love
I’m yours.
