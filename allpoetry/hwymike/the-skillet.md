The Skillet

Round in shape with handle for hands of grace.
Inside its rim becomes a masterpiece to eat.
Flambayed, Sautéed or Simmered Pasta,
In a cream sauce or Shrimp with Rice, then
Blazing hot to Blacken Fish or Steak.
.

When the cook has a masters hand in dish dining.
Savory it is to have it handed to you still hot.
With the smells of seasoning's and spice well blended
Making the mouth water and tummy say, "Yummy."
Most skillet dishes only take a minute.

With a shake of this and a pinch of that , stir then
Simmer shakes and add something that has been baked 
Or fried it’s all up to what the cook has in mind as to 
What you will find in the special skillet that now lay
On a plate or platter that awaits the diners devour.
