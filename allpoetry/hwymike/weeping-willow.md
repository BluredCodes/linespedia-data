Weeping Willow

Standing tall in seasons
Before the fall, nature’s
Beckon for natural change
Life’s cycle, like in a cage
Aging ever so going…

Forever weeping as the wind
Bellows across oceans then
Meeting the land, still traveling
Going on so as to where ever
It may cycle life’s age…

Days wallowed: through wilting
weather, when till 
life passes like shedding souls
For when the strong winds blow
The leaves do so go…

The inner rings of the trunk
Like the face’s wrinkles
Show the secret tails of seasons
Living through lives changes of
(Us) "Weeping Willows..."Hope you enjoy the growing I chose to portray in 
this poetry!
