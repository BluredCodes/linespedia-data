title: "Terms"
writer: "Hwymike"

Terms of endearment...
Endless meaning enchanted.

In whispers: wilting
His or her inner being.

Terms interpreted...
Perceiving is perception!

Fresh roses in a vase,
Or tender kisses upon the face.

Terms with interest...
Big or small what percent trusteth?

Terms of love,
Strings break... Rings fade.

In the heart 
It remains truelove's enduring flame.

Life's terms; fame or shame
A vivid gallery of valor.

Coming to terms
Terms coming to end.
