Destiny


              The fall collage painted the hillside,
               I stood foot propped upon a rock
               This stag roaming and grooming,
                      With heart a gloom…

                  Then out of berries I so ate
                 My doe did gravitate, gradually
                  emerging, excitingly I raised
                   My head examining her…

              First I smelt the scent of pleasant,
              Then felt the grace of tender plight
                 Finding the delightful sight
                 My manliness craving her…

                  Fur so soft, eyes of allure
                 Her innocence my full cure
                Pulling at my being becoming
                     Sworn only to her…

               Sunset shaded clefts, grounds
              of our courting (extolling fringe)
               Let the flaunting begin… ‘Tis
              honor to chase, twas loves fate...
