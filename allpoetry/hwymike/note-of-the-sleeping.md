Note Of The Sleeping

                       Two, four, six, eight, you just wait.
                        Meanie, meanie, mow. I got something 
                         to show...

                       Tickle, tickle my name is Michael.
                        It's no pickle in my pocket.
            
                       Honey be quick, honey don't be late.
                         It's you for dessert.

                               I can't wait....

                       One, two, In a hour or two.
                         We won't be through.
                          Three, four, in the shower  
                           or floor...
                            Five, six, sex is the intent.
                             Seven, eight.

                               I still wait...

                        Nine, ten, I'm ready to begin.

                       Wild thing, wild thing, give me loving.
                         Touching me tendering.

                        The ink is red, my eyes are blue.
                       All I want to do is make love to you.
                           You're late, you're late.

                             Asleep I wait...

                       Wake me for snoring is boring.
                          We'll make love,
                            till morning...
