Sky Love

In the sky above the ground 
Our faces are held captive 
The smiles miles high 
God’s grace placing our 
Great love’s face glowing 
Free feelings in toss 

Round we zip
Dipping us down
Gripping fingertips flip
Gasping whip to zap
On a sky map 

Throughout the clouds 
Our hide and seek 
The lasting caress
Touch-less 

Our youthful love
Never weak
Flying butterflies
Always unique

With a bird’s eye 
View of lovie dovie 
I’m your bad boy hubby
You’re my sweet honey

Sprung souls in our 
Moving, motionless
Speaking, silent
   Sky Love
