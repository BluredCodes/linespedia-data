Love of mineLove of mine,Words to describe and say the meaning of you in my life.In the entire world, all the things upon this earth,Only you it's all I need to fulfill all that I could ask for.In the heavens, it's you the heights!Salt of the earth, it's you the composure of my wits!For as the depths, in the deepest of oceans,It's for you, all my undying love full of emotion.Love of mine 'tis this touch to express?My, it's ours, the best of friendship!Dancing in the dark.Is the music orchestrated by the rhythms of our hearts flutter?Love of mine,Constantly concerned to let you feel my love.Love of mine,Anytime I need to free myself.Love of mine,Anything, anything at all, it's nothing to reveal!The inconcealable the unforgettable you!Love, you are where ever you are.But here I come to find you!I want to dance,Be face to face in an everlasting embrace.My heart explodes over and over,To shed the distance.My soul, as an Eagle in the sky,It's on the fly to you.All the gardens beauty, all the sky's blue,All my love I send to you!Greatest of great, the way to describe the rockets launch.The touching down of my spirit upon you.As a Hurricanes rush, as a Tornadoes twist,My desire is to wrap my love around you.Love of mine, Oh love of mine!
