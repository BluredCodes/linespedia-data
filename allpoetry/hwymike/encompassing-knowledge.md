Encompassing Knowledge

                    Proverbial mind paralleling,
                     the overall production.
                    Horizontal the horizon,
                     levelly levitating life.

                        'Plus or minus'...

                    Latitude stretched attitude,
                     degrees longitude suitable.
                    So to encompass,
                     endowing endeavors.

                     'Wisdom's narrowed mirth'...

                    Placed angels aligned,
                     defining earth's divinity.
                    Dimensions, roots squared,
                     reviewing man's regards.

                       'The balance'...

                    Minds turning round,
                     earth's inhabited face.
                    Kings determine mass,
                     masses of kingdom.

                      'Forever heaven'...
