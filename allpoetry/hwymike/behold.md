Behold

           To be held / healed
          Free breeze as I knelt
          Filled with pure cure
          Sacred from the mere

              Upon the low
            It was bestowed
             Hoary my head
            Through thoughts

         In a rush I did so bond
          With the silent armor
       Twas’ shielded in a sparkle
       The earthen pressed diamond

       Jeweled in the lace round my
        Mind covered in placed face
     Loves warmth, tender in smile as
      Trembled steps turn weight-less

        The fear has loves caress
          Then to me professed
          All wanders/ ponders
            With out a word

          Whispered on the soul
         The wisdom I did behold
           As I am healed/held
            recieved beloved…
