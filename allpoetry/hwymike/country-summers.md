Country Summers

                      Early light, late sunsets,
                       Glowing orange rounded
                        in purple to blue,
                      White clouds, spotted
                       sailing adrift across
                        dark green tree tops...

                      After splendoring day's
                       marching for morels,
                        wild onion weeding,
                      Along creek bottoms,
                       wildlife blossoming,
                        in wanderous bloom...

                      Off to fields and pastures,
                       filled with flowers...
                       On stallion, Marvelous his mane,
                      Gallantly gallopping, to run
                       the scent's of clover 
                        and evergreens, through the air...
    
                       The Whippoorwill singing song,
                        Bugs A-Z blowing in the breeze,
                         Smells of, brauts, burgers,
                        wings and beans, brushed 
                         with BBQ sauce...

                       Garden fresh melon's sweet,
                        tomatoes, corn, onions too,
                         I love thee... in my life,
                       Feeling free, playing on hands
                        and knees through,
                         Country Summers...
