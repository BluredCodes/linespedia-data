Fancy Face

             Set in curls-swirl, girl your life’s thrill
          Sleek with rosé cheeks, touching them sweet
        Glossy lips shining sensual, shocking with a kiss
           Eyes of sparkle marking my mind eternal
        Anytime, anywhere closing my eyes you’re there
        Feather kisses, creating my lasting wishes placed
           Gifts of pearls is your teeth; lying beneath
       my eye lids…Life to my casting-away keeping sanity
      Face of fancy, fantasy frolics and lipstick “God” I can’t
          quit fancying my frill on your beautiful face…
