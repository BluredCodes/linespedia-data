The Fathers Wish

In the times I held you in my arms 
My heart was always humbled
Watching you stumble with sounds 
Of baby babble still lingering my ears 
In sleeping dreams fears you steered 
The way to lay beside me

From tricycle to bikes and 
Spills filled with thrills
I always seen in you 
The me trailing and smiling
With feelings filled hugs as you tugged 
At my neck to heal the scrapped knees
Or “Please, please” as you wanted to be 
Wild and free
Giving you my best wishes in giving kisses 

Seems like yesterday it was sharing home work
Now we share our homes work
My want for you to always know 
I hold you in my dreams  
As you fulfilled my every command of god 
When this fathers wish came true
I got you...
