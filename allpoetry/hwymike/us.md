Us

When traveling through out the day 
while listening to the radio waves
       
When advertisements interfere I will hold you, 
my attributed adversary
     
  When I think of our love:

It could win a Grammy but honey
our secret is safe with me

Strong enough to be made Tops as 
our cards are always saveing each other 

When feeling sweet as cake you’re 
the icing rich and dreamy

When the maxing out house walls 
are filled to the rim with honey do’s

I promised to slip in time for little kisses 
I recall us standing in the shower together 

The Zest to give it our best, as we stood 
in the mirror Close-up on the Scope 

A detailed floss telling boldly Mr. to clean,
tossing the junk flooding the yard of your craftsman 

When buttering you up to Aleave the Cheer
I’ll take the kids to the park…
