The Store

Chain of events distinct in
the distance, (Fast paced) The 
sights and sounds of people
parking.

As the people push the carts
I restart the chain by chasing
the (Runaway) soon to be door ding
flings.

As I talk with face after face,
kids with candy all over theirs
Parents with candid direction.
I stride with perfection.

Still taking the time to do the service
of keeping the circle in motion,
showing customers friendly emotion.
Through all the commotion…

Taking the time to be kind, 
help to lose the itch to be so 
stiff. Feelings of domestic
acceptance…

It’s the grace that
keeps the blessing
flowing in this dizzy
place.
