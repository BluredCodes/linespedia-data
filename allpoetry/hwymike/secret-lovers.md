Distances travel with time lapsing as the kiss
Swirls, with tongs as snakes in tumble
Humble the hearts desire in this heated fire
Souls scream in silent sail when dreams 
And affairs affiliate so intoxicated

Fixed on desire to be in the light of higher
You found me asleep filled my dreams
And took me to you pleasure palace
On a cover and pillow pallet we lay
Discovering the inner power of love 

You wrapped me in your loves scarlet sheet
Tickled my feet with your warm toes
Your lips like glassing let the tall cold drink pass
From your being to mine blood red and sweet 
As a yellow roses fine scent divine

Behind my neck your fingers caress
Fondling my hair as I slip of your brassiere
Whispering in your ear
(Bis – nay- tay- la-po- niss- cumba)
Diadems do so I ever melt from your touch

‘Tis thus fate our secret relation 
For nor shall it reveal disastrous
For whom this heavenly bell tolls
When our souls collide in the chambers
Of passion set aside for only our hearts desire…Standing with hands to heaven in aww- vation!
Bravo, Thank you heavenly father!
