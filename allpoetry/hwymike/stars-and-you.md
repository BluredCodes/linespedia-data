Stars and you

Stars are bright,
But your love is out of sight!

Stars may glow,
But your love is my hallow!

Stars may fall,
But my spirit hears your every call!

Stars may fill the sky,
But in my eyes, you are both day and night!

Stars need night,
But I need to feel our bodys close and tight!

Stars are far,
But in my heart you are!

Stars are many,
But couldn't any compar to you!

If a loved one gave you this would you smile?
