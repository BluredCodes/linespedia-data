Lost And Found  Lost in times,I'm found when looking in your eyes!  Lost in day games,I'm found when you say my name!  Lost in loving you,I found myself when I found you!  Lost in empty space,Then I found all the beauty in your face!  Lost and alone,Found in a bliss when we kiss! Lost the fading thrill!When you found the will to feed our hearts for real! Not just mind reel!   Lost the blues!When I found my sunshine!SUGAR,HONEY,DARLING AND DEAR!        THAT'S YOU!Copyright © March 2001 Michael L. ChickJust think I been single for 4 years now!
