---
title: "Love and hate"
writer: "Hwymike"
slug: "love-and-hate"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/7014-Love-and-hate-by-Hwymike"
---

We so love to hate, and 
then we'll hate to love. 
Bitter is the sweet 
heated blue flame 
of love and hate? 
Like a torch 
the scorch 
to sum-sort 
is it to short? 
Has it lost the thrill? 
What’s the missing part 
in my heart: 
of our minds 
from the beginning of 
time till the end! 
What is the difference 
color, heritage, or hair of 
bright fluorescent streaks? 
Maybe the skin is painted, 
marked in taboo of tattoos. 
What can or can't be determined 
disposable or composable? 
To be scattered and battered 
in hate with love; 
We seem to hate ourselves 
with the things we so love. 
The love of another we can hate, 
but our love... Still I see is to hate.
