“ Faces of time with timely faces”                        Pictures hold faces in time                    Time leaves marks on remarking faces                 “ The shaping frowns and smiles turn round”                    Watering image reflecting the reshaping                          Water on faces wakes                            Wakes of water                            Change awareness                      “ Saving face faces more saving”                   Faces of books veiling the faces of view                    Textured faces facing the books text                    Faces doubled in duel dwindling life                  “ Living faceless lie’s less of more life”                   Embellished embers remembering cherished                   Childish youth heavens eternal face lift                    Tearing faces endlessly evaluating                                                                'Terse Faces'...
