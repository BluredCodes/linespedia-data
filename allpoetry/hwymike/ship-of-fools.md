Ship of Fool's                       (1)Ever so the tides, washing sand away in time.Even as the soul shedding of fleshly desire.Every breath seeping from fools like the sand.Vary slow rocking, the shocking effect ever so…I sail, you sail, somehow passing of fail.I said, you said, brethren swing the Gail.In the storm of a fools folly.Light the fire’s…Let the winds blow…Least the beast fight…                  (2)One-steps up standing to battleOne sits down from fear One mans the steering.No one try’s,No one cry,No matter how high…Everyone rides on a ship of foolsEverything’s so said secretEvery time you defile in denial…   Copyright © January 2002 Michael L.
