As I opened my eyeslingering stars like fire-flies in the skyIn the distance I'm captured in the glowof trees in fall bloomThe sun shines on throughthe gray clouds gloom withtwo rainbows archingDoubled imaged in the mirroracross the silent, stillmaking my heart shrill surfaceColors surprise, enlightenmy consumed eyes,Waken up to natures awe.Was fishing all night woke up with this!
