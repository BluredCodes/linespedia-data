DAYJAVOOPulled in the drive at a quarter after five.It’s on my mind, how to stay alive.Driving my flesh to blood to fill Everyone’s want for my bucksStrolling to the steps, (Yep all is empty)Called to memories in mind flash back,    Cats on the carpet…Dogs, barking at door…My pride and joyPlaying in the floor.(Reality) “No ones home but me”Don’t see any reason to keep bucking the bills.Life has lost its thrill, no love waiting here.To fill this working mans needs.My repentance scream.Three years running no time for finding love.Just tears over lost love covering my face.At a quarter after five pulling in the drive.Dreams through my mind sights and soundsOf my little family is around and crowning. Fulfilling this working mans needs.Cats on the carpet…Dogs barking at the door…My pride and joyPlaying in the floor.“I’m home it’s me.”I swear I heard her say“Every body daddy’s here”(But then again)No ones home but me…Copyright © July 2001 Michael L.
