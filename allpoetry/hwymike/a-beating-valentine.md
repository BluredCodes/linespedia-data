A Beating Valentine

In this man only for 
you within my chest.

Treasures of for you…

 Words to describe.

Lots of pearls,
but only one girl…

Many diamonds,
lots of tokens.

But your love key
can open it.

Under the flesh’s depths
glowing peeps of…

Your arms round my neck,
the perfect necklace.

Our joining hands, 
a heavenly cross.

Floating souls,
we ride the surface.

But from me to you…

So scary, it’s hairy,
majestic and
full of shimmering
Jitters.

A glossy surface
filled with love of 
your beauty.

Your face etched,
Covering this place.

The treasure chest 
That lies with in
