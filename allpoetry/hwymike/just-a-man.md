Just a Man


To my unfound cherished lover,
with all my emotion as everything of my all…

“I need you here, not there…

Share love with me always and forever
Teach me the meaning of a woman’s true love

Souls intercepting in this real man…

Lonely as a million square miles of open
Untamed madness that’s taken its toll

Pointless it seems this house, a prison…
Thy bed a desert no tears for rain I’m drained

On my own, what more as a tomb stone
With love letters of my heart, meant for you

This mans broken heart bleeds with need…

For you to kick in the walls, break all barriers
Walk into the me that needs, wants nothing
More than to hold you near, craves more everyday
To say in more than words surely I’m “Just a Man”

“Just a Man in love with you!”Just a secret letter to my soul-mate!
