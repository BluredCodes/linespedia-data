Just right (part 4)

As I stand above you, You lie upon our sex but more 
Complex love layer. I look at you sound asleep after
Our wild rampage, in but out of control love scape.
I pinch myself to make sure (us) is not fake, or I’m not
Dreaming!

I see you dreaming wandering if  ‘tis of me you dream.
As I slide into my pants, slip on my socks, I so want to talk
To you and tell you of my (Erotic) drunkenness you so inflated
my infatuation. Then it strikes me “Mike” she’s everything you 
Wished for!

Vary my best friend, the one I can trust. As well in the facts
Of my lover, the only one I feel safe with under the covers.
Then the only one my soul sets with in Gods loving hand.
Tears stream down my face not like any-other but my 
beloved  you. For all the ways that you are form fitted for me.

Like Eve’s I pronounce in my summer’s for / in basking of you.
Or like the hollows eve/ the ivy through the trees capturing me
In all your beautiful colors when falling in love with all of you.
Or the eve when the winters chill reminds me of your shrilling “YES”
For in my heart body and soul tis this rings every eve’s … ever for broadcasting…
(Just right part 4)Twas in effect (Erotic) and for my reality of 
Parts of my endless desire for real taking it 
past just love...
