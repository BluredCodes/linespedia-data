Just right (part 5)


Soon your swooping soul so wanders to touch me from afar
Your arms round my neck, feels so real (has me at kneeling)
Chilling to the bone the wilting waves of love so divine
Captured in the tenderness, more at submission of confession
Lovie I find more so a man past in- love… 

A woman within thines being with at will run through me
Running to me then taking me away … 
Tales to tell of the messages meaning standing still times     
Your voice tender the sounds render tears of aftermath
Actions of realness relived throughout the day dismaying me

The secret smile I see when reflecting right of the drivers’ seat
As the breeze of the window brushes my ear like you’re whispering
Better yet when licking my lips, lushly lingering to a secret french kiss
Then your touch, fingers through my chest hair underneath my shirt
(Can you feel me lifting your skirt, and then my loving you so much it hurts?)

I see in a different light (through you) the things you don’t want me to miss
Can hear the secrets you so long to tell me later feel the melting of soul 
As you feel my desires upon your flesh when apart, taking your hand in dance
Spinning you as you prance in a day light out of sight tango tasting of tangerine
Living out real life in a fantasy flings….
(Just right part 5)This is a series of mine(perfection in relationship)
