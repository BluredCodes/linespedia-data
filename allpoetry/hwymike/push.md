---
title: "Push"
writer: "Hwymike"
slug: "push"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/7385-Push-by-Hwymike"
---

Showers of flowers along the desert sand.
Bathing at the beaches in suits of little.
Gliding on a carpet round the treetops.

My imagination the roller coaster up,
down and all around. (Butterflies)
In my belly as I float adrift on dreams.

My breath heavy, head swimming, as 
I inhale deep holding air like a balloon.
To carry me afar, in self-affair…

Relation retroactive then pushed with exhale.
Is letting my soul free. Pushing to self-extreme.
In my passion as my finger tips down my chest,
(Pinching) the pink nips under my forest of hair.

As the sweat, like dew has newness I confess.
The pushing is a cushion as I fondle tired thighs.
I can feel the (fiery) spark of my eyes. 

Fleeing the darkness of feelings restrained,
released in a push as I tightly grasp my body
(Full and hole) with this push…
