ReachingSeeking or findingheart and mind…Reaching the startthen reaching to end…Reaching-in to reach-outtouching the inner…Out-reached, far fetchedto relate to them in-reach…Raised in-reachinghands to heaven…Spirit reaching up,to be touched…Reaching down to pullthe low - up high…Grace reached asapexing Mount Zion…Reaching through the clouds,reaching around earth…Reaching to have new birth,reached life through death…Copyright © August 2001 Michael L.
