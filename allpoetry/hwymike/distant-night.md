Twas’ the moon rising in the sky full…Glowing as a candle in the dark of nightWith your face holding glitter gleaming,Eyes radiant in the moon beamsShining from the sky’s surfaceReflective embrace, our faces so closeLeaving everlasting fire in the heartFlames spreading to a smile miles wideEngulfing the minds on past life’s plain Into the forest of our first kissMidnight’s flame steaming the nights dew…Winds blew to send the steam of our uncoveredCaress… Day light comes, our spiritsSoon run to be distant stars, A shooting rainbow of divine hello…Copyright © July 2001 Michael L.
