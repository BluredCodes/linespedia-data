---
title: "My Midday Sun"
writer: "Hwymike"
slug: "my-midday-sun"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/6293-My-Midday-Sun-by-Hwymike"
---

You're my brightest love light.
You're healing touch to my flesh.
Earth quaking thy kiss...
Surpassing my soul's
     boundary...

Lost then found in your genuine 
emeralds, starry divine.
Thy beauty etched, branded,
tranquil upon my
      being....

The electrifying apex,
momentous measure maxim.
From head to toe. Bold Burgundy
flowing free with in my
      veins...

Spirit igniting Zinfandel,
reuniting frivolous flames...
Burning ever so bright.
Shelling supernova of
      flesh...

World ravishing ship toss,
Senses totaling mountain tumble.
Our pressing hearts purge.
When you're in my 
      arms...

Wondrous 'tis my desire.
To taste, feel and indulge
my biggest addiction.
Love sick I am
     for you...
