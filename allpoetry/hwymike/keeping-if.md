Keeping If

 
                     If keeping your affection
                      May I ever desire another’s

                    If keeping your needs in my thoughts
                      May I always be in yours

                    If keeping understanding
                      May we always talk of what is real to feel

                    If keeping your friendship
                      May we always walk together

                    If keeping our love
                     May we never take it for granted

                    If keeping things from pushing us apart
                     May we always run to each other standing together

                    If keeping you means giving all of me 
                     Then I'm yours, 
                     and may you keep giving me all of you

                    If we keep all of this then we may share
                     Life and love

                       All to keep from losing each other…
