Does it

When my mind revs, 
Your heart stops?

My pain swells,
Your ego swoon?

The fright of death,
Please your ambition?

My blood on the paper money,
Amplify whooping?

Pinning the shrill,
Your smiles thrill?

Please I beg of you,
Beggars no appease you?

My souls cry,
Sparks in your eye!

My secret hearts desire,
Does it ever be heard?

Told to me… 
Nobodies fault but mine.

Ever does it seem? 
Means of redemption.

Damned repetition,
Relatively demanding!

Geez…  
Does it?Really though don't you agree?
(Does it?)  This could be theme 
for many, many poems on so many 
subjects!
