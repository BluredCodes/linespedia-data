Eve of Val

              Value the eve.                          Trust and believe
          Long to see and feel.                  In loving realization.
        Valor with gathering it.          Rings true eyes green, blue
      Gallant shewn light of you       Every day this loves on anew
     As rose petals litter in heart    Placing a trail to lighted loving
   Covering the ground we share, glares eve on love beneath arched
  Times defined divine the blush and wine give the perfumed candles
 Ample time to burn with the oxegons from our explosive pressure the
Pleasure of excitement blasting, lasting, massing and totally surpasses
You are my flicker, glimmering, shimmer of eternal flame shamelessly
Everlasting full metal strength bonded of molten soul rivers a hot love
 Yet flying on the wings of queen-bees gathering honey for feeding us
   Sting and stung this is such a beginning before when we always flow
    The light shining through you hair has heavenly gleam of an angle
      Fragile yet agile mystifying with flying me upon high and in sky
       I’m your guy loving my lady shading you with my soul as you 
        Glow from the beauty of your face showing and bestowing a
         Goddess essence ever for revealing the magic of our days I
           Display secret kisses and hugs formed from hearts drum
             Beating in-time pounding in my mind the high of time
                Seeking to find the alone time for you and me on
                  A line calling to you saying “Be my Valentine”
                    Take me never let me go nor thus be alone
                      Thy queen is the life to nest me in safe
                        Always from the alone fright in night
                          Intertwine our souls kiss of fine as
                           Aged wine “Be mine always and
                            Always” Waves crashing time
                              Yet making divine shag 
                                Here’s a lifetime of love
                                 On a card or in a bag 
                                  Value of love never
                                    To be mistaking
                                      But making us
                                         One to one
                                          Value of 
                                           Priceless
                                              “Us”"Man... twas hard and well I tried!"
