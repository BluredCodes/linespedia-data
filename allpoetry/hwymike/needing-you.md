Needing You

Its 4:45 in the afternoon and soon as the evening is here 
I’ll be UN-near to you my dear. I will be in the work gear!
As my mind puts together plans of sharing life with you
Loving you from afar reminiscing of times…

 I’ve seen you at the bar
Or kissing you in my car 
as I drove reckless in the parking lot
dreaming of our passion so hot!

It never stops you and me running free 
As the world turns our love so burns…
 
The thirst, it’s quench my every inch
The taste crazy as wild berries
You, the touch it says so much
The motion it releases the ocean

The sight of you fills me with so much might
Sounds of passion the lift off the ground
The desire sends it even higher. The smile it 
Says for more, just a little while more!

Oh how I do adore, this is more than words
It’s in the flesh giving it our best leaving the
Simples to the ones that need to rest
Others sunsets is our dawn.

Each others breast we caress, yours so soft
And fun to taste, The faces of time standing still
In our endless thrill, our will it’s the best it’s what 
Separates us from the bills!
 
“Endless distress my longing for you!”Wow!
