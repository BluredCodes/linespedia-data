A Days Ways

                         Pouring thoughts relaying emotions
                          How well can we tell the other
                          Or show our self of theses things 
                         To express fast or opinionated slow 
                        Take it to the moment of achievement
                     Tears of endearment or trials of experience 
                       Thrilling is it spilling the confessions

                       Who is to willingly undo wearing any shoe
                                    Sandals or boots

                   What is the desired destination of day in, day out
                                    Slacks or jeans

                               Who will fit the appointment
                   At the start in the smarts to get the right remarks

                                    Beginning to end
                      Can you tend the limbs of your tree of life

                           Leaving is it the shedding or covering

                                    What's the motive
                                  Centered and rounded 
                                    How square are you
 
                              Prepared to do what it takes
                    Two shake's and in a moment it is broken or built

                    It's to be the ability to stride balanced and sturdy
                                    On rocks or sand

                       It's how well we deliver and accept shock
                                    In a days maze...
                                
                          All of this is how the road is paved 
                                    In A Days Ways...
