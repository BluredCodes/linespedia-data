Timeless Love                       'First sightful second'.                          Love was endless...                       'First minute', power                          of presence...                       'Hours honestly',                          beloved meaning...                       'Days go', captured...                         sensational.                       'Nights turning',                          beside ourselfs.                       'Weeks', willing heart.                       'Months living', with you.                          All thoughts and dreams.                       'Years', lifes tears                         shared distant.                       'Turning century'.                         Unconditional,                          momentous moment.                                                     'Of', moving                              love.
