Insight

Time there are many seconds as min, hrs. and days
Seasons as weeks of the months making changes in yrs.
Over decades centuries of Christ

Streams lead to rivers as they travel to the seas.
Clouds gather the toiling chain of rain. Grateful
Is the earth it replenishes (Just as repenting souls)

Even with the winds and shaking quakes, still the 
Earth standing strong to trust in Christ’s test of time
In-site we see the circle, sun rise and set

In our foil unfolding tis our understanding of what
has been will be again. As we spend our time
Is our pleasure in the coming of the rapture

Our reward in our wayward ways for why should we not
Tend to the things that free flow to our souls from every
Thing we feel with knowledge of fathers loving grace

Replenishing others with the gift of loving spirit
Twas cherished our birth, tis nertchering friendship
Our living sailing on dreams of divine coherence


Established on the corner stone of faith to battle through
Time and changes, dreams maybe many, desire maybe much
But the sum of such as all things turn over and over

Sun carries from end to beginning and is the warmth of love
The lord approves the motions, as before as it came so shall it go
And come again!Just a revalation I had one night 
as I lie after reading in the word!
