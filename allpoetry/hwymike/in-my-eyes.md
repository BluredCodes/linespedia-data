Leather and lace, sassy with classSensations pass to and froThe blush upon your face breezes my soulSolemn the secret caress as you touch your dressGraces as every sunrise the well-toned thighsFirmly encompassing UN defiled groundSounds of your pressing, send tremors throughThoughts of mine that do divine your essencesPower of presence, priceless the endless duskDawned by your prevalent elusive allureEntered prized night by day inBombarded cascade flashes Rolling thunderBlossoming bloom Consumed summerWith winter's chill racing along our fleshHeart abounding expressions withMoaned fulfillment’s asMoments of motionless endearmentUnited in breath  In my eyesCopyright © June 2001 Michael L.
