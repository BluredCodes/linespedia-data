---
title: "Yes, it's you!"
writer: "Wooky"
slug: "yes-its-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

From the moment i first met u, i knew you'd be the one the one I'd cut it loose with, with you I'd have my fun i could be myself around you, i wouldn't have to change you would enjoy the way i was acting, even if it was strange i can come to you with problems, i know you're always there seems when i feel so alone, i always know you care you make me feel so special when you are arounda really special person, which i am glad that i found such a special person and nothing less than kind such a beautiful friendship, I've tried so hard to find I'll tell you this right now, i never want this to endNothing could ever change this, you are my best friend
