---
title: "Strong"
writer: "Wooky"
slug: "strong"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/19365-Strong-by-Wooky"
---

How can you possibly find someone to comfort you?Sometimes the pain is too much.I wish I could comfort her!But I don't know exactly how she feels.I wish that I could get inside her feelings and help her through the pain.It seems like she's strong though. . .I know she'll be alright.No one can break her.They might make her a reject.But to me. . .She's the strongest person I know.No one can break her.No one can make her crumble.She's strong. . .She's my hero!
