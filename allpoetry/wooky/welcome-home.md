The bus pulls up and i get off.Welcome Home, i say to myself.This is my house.This is my family.No one is home,well he is but he's sleeping.i take off my shoes and bag,and go to my resting place.There i sit in silence, thinking how much money she owes me.Why did i give it to her,why did i fall for her scheme.I love her and i want to help her.But can't she realize she has a problem.She keeps telling me she'll pay me back.Am i just selfish. . .Then i sit in more silence thinking about him.Is he going to say the wrong thing.Is he going to piss her off.Are they going to fight again.I hope not!But that never happens.they always fight.  i always sit there, in the silence,wishing they would stop.
