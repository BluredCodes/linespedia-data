Why do they have to say mean thingsWhy do they have to argue about dumb thingsWhy do they hate each other over stupid thingsWhy do they put me through this SHIT!If they only married to make our family happythey got problemsI don't even know if they love each other anymoreWhy?Why do they argue about dumb things?She's always naggingHe's always not listeningWho's side am i supposed to takeI love himI love herI Hate them together!
