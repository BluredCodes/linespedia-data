title: "The Kiss"
writer: "Wooky"

The Kiss They Had.I wonder if it meant anything.Why are they trying to confuse me.They hate each other!Why do they fight, But then kiss the next day.Are they going to forgive each other?Probably not! So why are they screwing with my mind.I wish they could forgive each other,I wish i could hear them laugh and have fun.But that's never going to happen.I wish they would get a divorce!
