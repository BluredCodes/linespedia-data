This goes out to my new found friends who really like CLAY!i thought i was cool until i found them?the one guy with the guitar, well, best said by someone, he's got the confidence to knock down the Empire State Building.  The other guy with the bottle of Vodka and the hidden Mike's.  He's just the BEST!!  there's a potential friendship there.  The girl with the orange shirt, the one with the belly-button showing.  Every time we talk, we cry.  That's something I'll never forget.  Then there's the guy who i was mostly scared to tell.  tell my secret.  but with a little help from Mike, we worked pass that.  But we looked pass Mike and he was totally okay with that.  Which i should have known.  There's the girl with the cool dad.  the other girl with the really strong coffee(hint hint, nudge nudge, KISS KISS?)  the girl with the purple hair, and not to mention sluttly shirts.  BUT THAT'S COOL!!  we're eEnemiesbut we understand each other.  them there's her.  The one that i said i will forget all our memories.  the one who i made out with on the floor when we were 12.  the one who helped me through this the most.  I love her!!Anyway enough with sappy love.  the reason i call this what a high.  is because my new friends make me High on Life!!  What wholesome fun we have!!
