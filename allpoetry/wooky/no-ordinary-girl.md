She no ordinary girl
She got something different about her.  
  Maybe it's her smell. . .
      Maybe it's her hair. . .
          Maybe it's her make-up. . .
I don't know why she's so nice to me. 
I can't believe how much I've come to like her
She's there to comfort me and tell me everything is going to be alright.
She's there to help me decide what's best.
She's my student Council Buddy!
Who loves me for who I am!
