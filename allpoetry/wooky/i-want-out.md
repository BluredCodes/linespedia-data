title: "I Want Out"
writer: "Wooky"

I don't got much to do
Just sit here and think
think about tommorow,
what will happen tomorrow?
think about yesterday,
what happened yesterday?
and i think about today.
today was my day.
i couldn't do it,
i couldn't tell them.
everytime they ask me about it,
i deny it.
Why?
It's like I afraid.
Well you told me not to be.
You told me the closet was full.
You told me to come out and. . .
But i can't
Something is holding me in!

That damn closet!
