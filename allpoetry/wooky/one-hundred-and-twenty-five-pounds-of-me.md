How can you guess someones' weight when you don't even know them.Maybe i Am his reincarnation, that's cool!!Am i the fish or the bird?i don't see anything in you, except for a really nice guy,who really cares for me.Maybe i am the captain of the ship, that's cool too!I was once a hundred and eight pounds of fear, But now I'm One hundred and Twenty Five pounds of Me!!Maybe it's because of you,or Blue Pixieor maybe even viloet.snowBut i thank you for not letting me sink.  Because without that I'd probably still be only One Hundred and Eight pounds.I'm working on being my own light!!Thanks
