title: "Sniper"
writer: "Wooky"

The Sniper sits at the lot of the hill.
The snow is glistening off the sun.
It's almost blinding.
The man stands at the bottom of the hill
The snow covered trees make a delightful sight.
He thinks of his family and the holiday that is near.
Why is the Sniper looking down at him!
Why is the Man so oblivious to what's going on.
Wake Up!
The gun is on you!
The target is set!
The man looks at a photo of his family.
The sniper pulls the trigger.

THE END
