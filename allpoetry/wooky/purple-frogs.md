For anyone who lets their imagination run free!

Uh-oh! It's happening again! 
I swear its not a sin!
But those purple frogs are out again. 
Orange spots all on their backs, 
Really, I swear I'm not whack. 
Who knows what they're after, 
Maybe they just like the sound of laughter. 
But once in a while everyone should see
These purple frogs that are after me.
