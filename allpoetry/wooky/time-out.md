title: "Time-Out"

Sometimes I just wish that yesterday never came, today never happened and tomorrow is many many breaths away. Sometimes i wish that i could yell Time-Out and the world would stop, and for those moments that the world's paused i could talk to myself, ask myself what i am doing, making sure I'm doing things right.Sometimes I just wish that right now, in this very moment, I myself could last forever.  My thoughts could be heard and known and i could be the soul power of the world. yet, somewhere inside myself I realize that yesterday came, today was a very bad day, and tomorrow, inevitably, will come.
