---
title: "Sentenced to Death"
writer: "wildbrushfire"
slug: "sentenced-to-death"
source: "AllPoetry"

You're nothing! I wish you were never born!

Your words branded my self esteem,
your fists bruised my soul,
weak and disoriented
you expected me to be strong.

Disgusted, I stare at my perceived
disfigured reflection in the mirror
smashing my fist into its cold surface
shattering it to pieces on the floor.

A piece calls out to me.
You're nothing! Do yourself a favor
end it all. Put yourself out of your misery.

The jagged edge rips
deep into the flesh of my being.
My garnet blood pours out years
of self doubt and confusion. Doomed
to spend eternity with you in Hell's icy grip..
