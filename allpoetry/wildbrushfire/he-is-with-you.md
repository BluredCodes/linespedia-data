---
title: "He Is With You"
writer: "wildbrushfire"

Life is one big mystery,
You don't know what will happen,
You don't know what you'll see.
Through love, hate and heartache,
         He is with you.

Guiding you to your salvation,
Through the skies above,
Through entire nations.
When you try to achieve a better life,
          He is with you.

Regrets, frustrations and fear will subside,
You won't have to weep,
You won't have to hide.
When you share peace and happiness,
          He is with you.

Achieving strength and peace of mind,
Feeling wonderful,
Feeling divine.
The mystery of life won't matter because,
          He is with you.
