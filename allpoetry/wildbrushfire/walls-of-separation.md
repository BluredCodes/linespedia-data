title: "Walls of Separation"

She built walls of doubt and confusion around herself.  Brick after brick plastered together by fear and indecision.  Slow and determined I scraped with love and compassion, chipping away years of mistrust and pain.  I see her lovely face as a mop of tears sweeps lines down her dust covered cheeks.  A silent weep barely escapes her pouty lips as I blanket her in my arms and taste the salt on my tongue from her kiss.  I rock her gently telling her everything will be fine and silently hoping to convince myself.
