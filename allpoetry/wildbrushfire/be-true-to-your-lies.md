title: "Be True to Your Lies"

Lie to me...
tell me you want me.
Lie to me...
tell me you need me.
Lie to me...
tell me I'm the only one.

I met you on a train
your eyes bore right
through me. 
You breathed life
into my lungs,
I cannot live 
without you.

Lie to me...
say you'll marry me.
Lie to me...
tell me you care.
Lie to me...
tell me you'll always be there.

You told me we would
be together forever
but now you're gone.
I wanted to believe you
but once again
I was wrong.


You lied to me...
you told me you loved me.
You lied to me...
you told me you cared
You lied to me...

When will it end?
And will I ever
