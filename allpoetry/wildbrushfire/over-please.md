My Dearest,

   Blah…Blah…Blah.

At the bottom, 
as always,
there you were.

			Over Please

Followed by an arrow
(As if) I didn’t know
you continued on the 
other side.
You couldn’t keep it short
Or even leave me in suspense
With

			To Be Continued…

Over Please?  Please!
