Baby, don't you remember me?We met at the bar last week.Your caresses made my body quiver,Like ripples rolling through a river.You told me you weren't married though.I guess I should've known.A man with a body as sexy as yours,Has to be followed by a whore!But I'm not mad, as you can tell 'cause,Temporary insanity will send you to hell.Along with that bitch you call your wife,Whose probably been frigid all her life!Which is why you came looking for me.I can understand, 'cause I am supreme.But I have to admit, I lied to you too.I'm married as well and he's looking for you.
