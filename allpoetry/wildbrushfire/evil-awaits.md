Sometimes-
My heart pounds in pain.
I want to scream at the top of my lungs,
"Why! Why do you torture me so!"

Satan-
I have stared him in the face.
Hell's fury burns depression into my brain.
What keeps me going? Do you know?

Will-
Power

Kill-
What God has created he'd say.
Oceans of emotional waves crash through my mind.
Life is loss and loss is pain.

Life-
Squeezes the breath from my lungs.
Can't breathe. Want to die. Kill me, suicide is a sin.
Release me from it before I go insane.
