She stares alone across the universetears are her airshe cannot breathe with out them Drowns her with it's pleasEverything was trueit could have been a storyHope was a daggerLove was a poisonSadness a familiar songNo one can get inside herNo one can bare her painShe is lifeBut only in darknessTake her hands they are covered in bloodShe has killed herself againNightmares keep getting better and bettercatch her if you canShe can't keep going this wayEverything she wans is the sameRape her like a child christened in bloodPainted like a lost saintThere is no hopeThe voice is deadIf only she could lovePlease say the right wordsNo one holds her heartNo one holds her handThe sadness gets better and betterShe roams this world aloneNothing left but pain
