---
title: "Missing You"
writer: "Pseudoprincess"
slug: "missing-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Even though we weren't dating
There was something between us
OR so I thought there could have been
But I messed it up
I did wrong, and lost your trust
I miss speaking to you
I miss being with you
It may not have showed how much I cared
But I did, the feelings were there
If I could go back and change what happened
I would in a second
People said I would be stupid if I did
But they don't understand
Because the feelings I had for you I hid
