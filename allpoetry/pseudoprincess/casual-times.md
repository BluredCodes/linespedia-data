I really don't understand youYou are the boy that I fell forMany months agoI still have a thing for you And I'm not sure why!I really thought you were differentBut it's all an act all a lieI'm the one that messed it all upBy hanging out with your friendBut every-time we party we seem To have what you would call A very 'casual relationship'Just for that night Then you don't talk or anything in the endIt's happened beforeAnd it may happen againYou probably just think I'm easyWhen really that's not the caseMaybe I just fell for your sweet faceI have a thing for you and like you alotSo I do what I do hoping..That something in your head clicksAnd you begin to like me again tooBut I guess it's just all about getting a pieceAnd that's all I amSo really what I need to do is get that through my head.
