---
title: "You don't know my friend...."
writer: "Pseudoprincess"
slug: "you-dont-know-my-friend"
source: "AllPoetry"

You think too much of me
I'm really not all you say
Which I know you know this
So I don't understand why you said 
what you did
There are things I wish I could change
Really, I still could but I'm too scared
to do so for fear I make a mistake
And you don't feel the way you say anymore
Graduation is coming up fast
You've been there for me through 
good times and bad
I don't want to loose the bond we carry now
I know we will promise to keep in touch
But I know what's going to happen
We will losse that touch, 
it will slip by without notice
As will I
You'll find new friends to share your 
thoughts and worries with
To share your good times and bad
I know I will always remember them
No matter who comes in and out of my life
There will always be a little piece of my
heart that is already filled with memories
and thoughts of you
You've always meant so much to me
I wish I was someone that could share my feelings
Because I want you to know, 
But I'm not
So you may never know just....
How Much You've Meant To Me................
