---
title: "Gone"
writer: "Pseudoprincess"
slug: "gone"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

How can you just stand there 
And pretend like nothing happened? 
How can you be so cool about things 
When my heart is aching? 
I would have done anything for you. 
I would have killed for you, 
But you left, 
You left sore scars in my heart, 
For the rest of my life. 
I washed away your fears with my aching tears, 
I listened to your lies, 
I yearned for more. 
And now you are gone, 
You are gone to another, 
Another girl... 
A girl so innocent, 
Another girl in love with you.
