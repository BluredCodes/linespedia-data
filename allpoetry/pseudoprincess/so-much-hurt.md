---
title: "So Much Hurt"
writer: "Pseudoprincess"
slug: "so-much-hurt"

You have cause me so much hurt like everyone said you would
Our relationship ended more than two months ago
You moved on a week after we called it quits
For some stupid reason I still think about you all the time
I can't even go out with guys because the whole time I think about you
I compare these guys to you
You aren't what I want in a relationship
You are so booty hungry 
In fact that's all you want in a 'relationship'
But for some reason my feelings for you are so deep
Your kisses were so sweet
But it's all over and while I should be having fun with these wonderful guys
Guys that actually treat me right and actually care
I can't have fun cause I'm comparing them to the things I loved about you
Even though you were only in my life for four months
My heart is still aching now that we are apart
I gave up so much for you
Which was stupid of me to do because I knew how it would end
If I knew that I would still be thinking about you this much
I would do everything differently if I had the chance to do it over again
