As we stand thereYou move closerConversation stopsMy stomach turnsButterflies flutter aboutI gaze deep into your eyesAs your sparkling eyes gaze backYou wrap your arms around my waistPulling me closer against your bodyI wrap my arms around your neckOur heads tiltMoving closerOur eyes closingOur lips touchYour lips are so soft So tenderGetting more intenseOur lips open slightlyThe touch of your tongueIt send me into a form of ectescyWe've been seeing eachother for some time nowI get this feeling every time we kissI don't just love youI'm IN LOVE with you
