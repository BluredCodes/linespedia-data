Darkness fills the room your voice ascendsNot envying the living or idoling the deadBroken dolls inside your heartLike shattered glass at your feetThe dark wave comes to those who listen and To those who care about the nightYou arise from the mistMoving under lightsYour laugh is for those who wanderWho dare to enter your domainBut you bid everyone welcomeThe UN-dead all look the same
