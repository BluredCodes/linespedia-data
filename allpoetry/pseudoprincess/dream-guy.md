---
title: "Dream Guy"
writer: "Pseudoprincess"

He will have dark hair
Every hair will be placed perfect about his head
So easy to run my fingers through
he's eyes will be a deep blue or soft green
They will sparkle like stars
I will easily loose myself in them
He's mouth will hold perfect full lips
Lips so soft and wonderful to kiss
These lips will form a gorgeous smile
That can brighten the darkest day
This smile will form the cutest 
And most defined dimples ever
His voice will be so soothing
That when I hear it I get butterflies in my stomach
His arms will be so strong 
So he can hold me tight
I will feel so protected, so safe.
His hands will be soft
They will be big but not too big
Just big enough to fit mine perfectly in his
His chest so broad and smooth
So my head can rest apon it 
With my arms wrapped around him as we lay beside on another
Yes, I made him up........
He's too perfect to be true.......
