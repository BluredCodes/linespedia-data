I write my feelings down
That the only why 
that I feel that I can express them
You tell me to open up
You tell me I need to talk about my feelings
You are there for me
You'll understand, that's all I hear
But that's just it
You won't understand what's going on with me
These feelings have been there for so long
I don't even know what's going on
They keep building up
More and more each day
I don't know how to deal
So I close out the world
It's better for me this way
Better to be alone
Because I don't understand
So I think, How will you????
