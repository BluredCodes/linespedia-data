---
title: "What I Want......."
writer: "Pseudoprincess"

Is it so much to ask
to find that special guy?
I've waited for so long
time just keeps passing by
I want someone to share my secrets with
someone to talk to
I want someone to ask opinions of
to tell me what they would do
I want to look at them
and get completely lost in their eyes
I'm so sick of the guys I've been with
I'm tired of their lies
I want someone to 
wrap their arms around me 
Someone to hold me tight
for all eternity
I want someone that 
is fun to be with
Someone that when I'm feeling down can
take those feelings and have them lift
Maybe just something as simple as 
just being kind
It's incredible how 
hard that is to find
Really I want something so warm
so pure like a dove
What I really want 
is to fall in love.
