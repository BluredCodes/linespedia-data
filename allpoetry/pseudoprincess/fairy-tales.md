When I was little I loved the stories of Cinderella, Snow White, Sleeping Beautyand even when I got a little older I loved Ariel, Belle and Jasmine.Such wonderful storiesAll different in their own little wayBut all so much the same.....Beautiful girls feeling like there is no hopefor finding true loveBut by the end of that two hour movieThey all have over come their problemsand found prince charmingwho just happens to be handsome,wonderful and has swept them off their feelReally this is greatif only it were that easyAs a child I wanted to be a princess, find my prince charming and live in a wonderful castleBut that's why they are called fairy talesin reality that doesn't happenI wish I was a child againso that I would still think that's how it would happeninstead of the disappointment I feel nowKnowing it's not that easy at all.
