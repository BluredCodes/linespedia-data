I think I might have confused you moreI'm not sure if I gave you he wrong impression or notIf the feelings I have for you are as deep as I show then I didn't But if they aren't and I just wanted to try and seeThen I definitely gave you the wrong impression!I have fun when we are together really I doI tell you everything and we get along so wellBut deep down I just think you are my Best FriendI don't know if it's because I still have feelings for the last boy?Even though he has moved onOr if it's because I'm afraid of loosing you because we are leaving for college in a weekWhen you asked if you could kiss me goodnight my eyes lite up as I said yesSo what does that mean????Do I have feelings for you like you do me or am I just confusing both of us by doing so?I guess my real problem is that I compare you with the last boyWhen really I shouldn't do that at all.There were things that I loved so much about himBut his priorites were all wrongWhere as yours are so rightBut the things I loved about him aren't there in you!So I dont' know what to do!I think I just love you because you care so much for me and no one has cared as much and you, plus you love me too.
