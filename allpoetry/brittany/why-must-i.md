---
title: "Why Must I"
writer: "Brittany"

Why must my head
play these childish games?
Why must my eyes
tear at a simple thought?
Can't I just be happy
lying in your arms?
Can't my heart be at ease
when you say you love me?
Why must my soul
be tormented so?
I just want to be free.
Free from doubt...
free from heart ache.
So why must I keep circling
and never find an end?i hate doubting every relationship that i'm in.. i hate running...
