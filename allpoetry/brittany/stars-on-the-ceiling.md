title: "Stars on the Ceiling"

I lie in bed at night
unable to think to sleep
I stare at the stars glimmering
back at me from my ceiling
they whisper sweet nothings
that they heard only a short while ago
words that you whispered
loving utterances spoken over the phone
hey make me forget all the bad
your poetic verses destroy all the sad
simple stars implanted on my ceiling
know all the love I feel
they know the dreams i dream at night
and the tears I cry
because you are so far away
but in those stars
are a piece of you
so now the tears that I cry at night
are finally okayyes, you heard it right!
stars on the ceiling!
they are the glow in the dark kind!!
