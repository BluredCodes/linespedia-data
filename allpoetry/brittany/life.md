Life is stupid. 
Life is not really around us.
It is a dream our minds dream for us
to lie about the harsh reality
of what life really is about.
Don't concede.
Don't believe.
Nothing is really there.
It's only put there to falter and to fail.
Don't concede.
Don't believe.
Nothing is really real.sorry that this is such a short piece.
