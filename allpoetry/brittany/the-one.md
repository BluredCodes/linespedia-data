---
title: "The One"
writer: "Brittany"
slug: "the-one"

you're the one in my life
the one who makes everything alright
the sun rises and sets by your command
the moon radiates by a wave of your hand
you hold such power
to unlock the key to my fearful towers
the stars know not such beauty
as that found in your loving need
you are my only one
the only one I'll ever loveI love you Josh!!
I always will!
