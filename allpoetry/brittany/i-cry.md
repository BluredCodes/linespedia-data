---
title: "I Cry"
writer: "Brittany"
slug: "i-cry"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/19275-I-Cry-by-Brittany"
---

Life is stupid
love is pain
that's why we live
in all this vain

Dreams are tarnished
hopes are killed
that's why we live
as though we will

Smiles are lies
friends untrue
that's why we live
and still we lose

Broken hearts bleed
envy turns green
that's why we live
that's why we need

Depression make us weak
poverty at a high
that's why we live
that's why I cry
