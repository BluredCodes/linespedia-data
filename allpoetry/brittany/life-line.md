---
title: "Life Line"
writer: "Brittany"
slug: "life-line"

sinking deeper
sinking down
if only I knew
please show me how

*falling into hell
drowning in its sorrow
throw me a life line
help me see another tomorrow*

walking alone
walking scared
have no place to go
wishing my life would be spared

*falling into hell
drowning in its sorrow
throw me a life line
help me see another tomorrow*

dying slowly
dying along
scared of what's to come next
no one would even know

*falling into hell
drowning in its sorrow
throw me a life line
help me see another tomorrow*would anyone even care if i died? seriously? would they? or would they just pretend?
