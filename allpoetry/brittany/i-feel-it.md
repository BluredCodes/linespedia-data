---
title: "I Feel It"
writer: "Brittany"
slug: "i-feel-it"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/43062-I-Feel-It-by-Brittany"
---

depression
sorrow
pity
I feel it everyday
I don't know any other way
tears
blood
pain
I feel it everyday
and yet, I have no say
burning
draining
drowning
I feel it everyday
please let it just go away
