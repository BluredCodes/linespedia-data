title: "Return to Me"
writer: "Brittany"

thinking back in time
remembering the way
that life used to be
wishing it would go back
return to me again

* come back to me
return to me
say that you love me - again
I need you
I love you
return to me again *

feeling so empty now
afraid to lose anymore
living life by each moment
dying alone inside
return to me again

* come back to me
return to me
say that you love me - again
I need you
I love you
return to me again *

crying my tears of pain
feeling as though I will
please tell me you need me
that you'll love me 
return to me again

* come back to me
return to me
say that you love me - forever
I need you
I love you
