fear
doubt
misunderstanding
my love for you is strange
i fear what i don't know
but i fear it to end too quickly
tears
heartbroken
is this what will beocome of us?
will we stay as we are forever?
empowered
love
living
please let me forget my doubts
i only want to be with ou
these ramblings are killing me*sigh*
