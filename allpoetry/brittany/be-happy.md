---
title: "Be Happy"
writer: "Brittany"
slug: "be-happy"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/38653-Be-Happy-by-Brittany"
---

It hurts me
to see you like this
to cry and weep
to fear and worry
all I want
is for you to be at ease
to love and be loved
to laugh and enjoy life
there seems no other way
I see only one choice
I'll give you mine...
my hapiness it yours
I can live without
I have for some time now
it's yours, no hassle
so please be happy
it's all I really want
just please be happy.
