Evil conformityin all its glorywave your ugly handmake your standFaceless dummiessmoke your weedwallow in ignorancego forth henceEnvious of each othershameful of your brotherlook awaydon't be scaredPerfection always teeminghyperactivitysleep all nightfree of frightSimpleton mindsyour all alikeexpose your conformityin its evil meansconformity... PUKE!!
