---
title: "Filthy Sins"
writer: "Brittany"
slug: "filthy-sins"
source: "AllPoetry"

Dreams are gone
life isn't real
pain has filled all
filthy sins
death and destruction
tears of blood
what have I become?
killing what I hate
hate what is killing
despair beyond repair
crying through closed eyes
ignorance has overcome
in these filthy sins
hurting outside
all inside is dead
stars untouchable
wanting what can't be touched
vain in angelic faces
nothing is real
pain insurmountable
through these filthy sins
