---
title: "Happy Face"
writer: "Brittany"
slug: "happy-face"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/37943-Happy-Face-by-Brittany"
---

Confused
lonely
afraid
these feelings have become my own
they never detour
but to many, remain unknown

Happy face
masked in fear
of what they'd find
would they pity?
to just laugh?
there's got to be another way.

I remain confused
lonely
afraid
but never without my happy face
to conceal
these feelings.craziness
