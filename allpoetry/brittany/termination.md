Plotting my termination
I need to find a way out
I can't breathe anymore
I smoke half a pack a day
so that I may suffocate in my sleep
I take ten times too many pills
hoping I'll pass out and leave
the pills relieve all feeling
make me feel empty and uncaring
but it's better than what I have now
I can't stand to live any longer
not where my life stands now
so if you have any suggestions...
please tell me howseriously though...
