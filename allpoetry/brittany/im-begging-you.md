---
title: "I'm Begging You"
writer: "Brittany"
slug: "im-begging-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/48596-Im-Begging-You-by-Brittany"
---

I'm begging you
please take this life
take it and do what you wish of it
I no longer need it
so why should I live?
I'm begging you
do it however you wish
pain is not an issue
as long as the goal succeeds
I can't stand it any longer
I'm begging you...
