I can't breathe
suffocating on my own demize
scratching my nails on glass
killing my ears to remind me
that I'm still alive
cutting my skin
to remember pain still exists
I can't breathe
drowning in my own tears
overdosing on pills
to numb the stabbing pain
ten times too many
to feel no pain for an hour or so
I can't live
burnng my soul eternally
no lnoger able to believe
to trust their lustfull glances
nor to care that they love
love me or any other being
I want to no longer be here
and yet...
I can't die*sigh*
