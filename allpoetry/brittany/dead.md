---
title: "Dead"
writer: "Brittany"
slug: "dead"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/34828-Dead-by-Brittany"
---

died long ago
still walking somehow
alive only in body
spirit gone now

evil entering
purity is unknown
beauty lost to all
unknowingly still shown

time to end this pain
throw it out the door
tired of all of it
tired of being a whore

take me now
death master, sir
take this living body
to live in the dirtpain.. oh how it's kept me alive
