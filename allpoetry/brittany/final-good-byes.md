---
title: "Final Good-Byes"
writer: "Brittany"
slug: "final-good-byes"
source: "AllPoetry"

Good-byes are the hardest to say
when it's to someone like you.
The tears in my eyes make me wish
there was some other way.
Your friendship was always true
even in the hardest of times.
How can all this be ending?
The answer - I have no clue.
Trying my hardest not to cry
for you when you leave.
I'll summon all my courage
to finally say good-bye.I hate good-byes.
