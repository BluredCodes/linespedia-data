Petty bitch
who do you think you are?
walking the way you do
talking the way you do
still treating me like shit
you used to call me "friend"
what happened to you THEN?
I miss her
but I hate YOU
I wish I'd never talked to you
if I knew this was you
what happened to you THEN?
please come back to me...
I miss her
