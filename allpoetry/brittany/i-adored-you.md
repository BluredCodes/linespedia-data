---
title: "I Adored You"
writer: "Brittany"
slug: "i-adored-you"

I adored you
would have done anything to please you
but you were stupid
you beat her...
your own wife...
my mother
you about killed her
did that make you feel good?
did it make you happy?
to see your love bleeding?
helpless on her own
and yet, I adored you
but when you took fate in your hands...
when you shot that gun in your head...
you killed a piece of me with you
I am alive once more though
and I no longer adore you.this piece was about my father. yes, he did beat my mother, and i had to witness it at least once a day. very hard on a kindergarten at the time.
and when he did committ suicide, i blamed it on myself.
i finally opened my eyes .. and saw that it wasn't my fault. it was his, no one elses.
that's why i hate him now.
