sadness
pain
i feel it everyday
isn't there someway else
to express how i feel 
when you are not around?

depression
broken
i feel it everyday
this all must be surrmountable
it can't go on forever
you have to come to me soon.

please some back to me
i need you so bad
don't you understand?living 1,000 miles from the one that you love is very hard.
this was kind of just on the spot.. so it's not very good
