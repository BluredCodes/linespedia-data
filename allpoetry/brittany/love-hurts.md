---
title: "Love Hurts"
writer: "Brittany"

Love hurts 
there's no other way 
Love kills 
and takes nothing else away 

Love hurts 
it kills me all the same 
Love shatters 
and destroys all I ever wanted 

Love hurts 
though it'll never pass 
Love cries 
and leaves me wondering whylove hurts.. i hope you understand my reasoning
