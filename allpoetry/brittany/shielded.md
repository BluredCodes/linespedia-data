Sturdy
Confident
"Normal"
The model of perfectness
A shield built of glass
Built to protect
Protect me from being "different"
Glass shield packaged "fragile".

Tip it over, watch it break
Break this shield
Set me free
Now I can finally be me.This piece is about being yourself. No matter what anyone else thinks of you, you have to look past that. Where I live, if you don't act a certain way, then you are condemned. Take advantage of being able to be yourself.
