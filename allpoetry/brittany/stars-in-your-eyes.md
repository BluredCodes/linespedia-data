---
title: "Stars in Your Eyes"
writer: "Brittany"
slug: "stars-in-your-eyes"
source: "AllPoetry"

the stars cannot compare
to the light I see in your eyes
they are nothing
they mean but a guide to lead me to you
mountains hold but majesty
but nothing like that found within your heart
you fill me with such bliss
but to have the first kiss
love is so undescribable
the way it is when I'm with you
cupid wouldn't even be able to understand
the stars that I see in your eyesanother one that i wrote to my boyfriend when we were in our poetry war!
