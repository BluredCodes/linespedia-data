---
title: "Lone Ranger"
writer: "Brittany"
slug: "lone-ranger"

Dusty trails behind
I see nothing but more ahead
I'm lost and confused
the Lone Ranger
alone for all times.
My head is spinning
with feelings of betrayal
I'm hurt and immune
the Lone Ranger
alone for all times.
No one could feel the way I do
I'd never wish it upon them
I'm torn and broken
the Lone Ranger
alone for all times.
The dirty wind of life in my eyes
filling with tears of understanding
I'm crying and still
the Lone Ranger
alone for all times.
