---
title: "Turned Around"
writer: "Brittany"

Dead to the world
unloved by all
my world turned around
and put against the wall

Darkness changing light
weight off shoulder
my world turning around
and moving the depressing boulder

Lies seeming truthfull
smiles making kind
my world turning around
and beginning to unwind

Strange but yet happy
cold warming glances
my world turning around
and finding hidden chances

Hate through all loving
confusion feels intelligent
my world turning around
and making all magnificent

Once dead to the world
unloved by all
my world turning around
and no longer ready to fall.
