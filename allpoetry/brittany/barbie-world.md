These childish gamesyou play every dayring a round the rosiemy head keeps spinningYou're all the samedon't try to blameyou make your fatethere's no debatePorcelain dollsbeautiful and tallangelic facesperfect acesBarbie heavenperfect tengo to bedthen do it againYou're all so fakethere's just one wayI'll take my chancesjust leave me with glances.Conformity. PUKE!
