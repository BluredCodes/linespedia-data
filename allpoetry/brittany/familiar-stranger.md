Familiar stranger
growing closely to my heart
I think that I know you
but then I doubt
Familiar stranger
why do you love me so
I fear what I don't know
Familiar stranger
make me forget my doubt
I'm afraid I don't understand
how I possibly can
Familiar stranger
I wish I knew you better still
though I know I surely willI love you Josh, but you know that I doubt.. it's just in my nature!! I think that just makes me love you even more!
