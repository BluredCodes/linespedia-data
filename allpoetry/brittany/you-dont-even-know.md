---
title: "You Don't Even Know"
writer: "Brittany"
slug: "you-dont-even-know"

I loved you with all I had
And you didn't even get mad
That I flew as fast as I could go
You didn't even know.

You took me back for number two
But I did the same thing to you
I flew as fast as I could go
You didn't even know.

Third time's a charm
I couldn't do much more harm
But time did show
I didn't even know.

My games were too much
I played with only a hunch
You showed the waters still flow
I didn't even know.

You got me back with full force
But I'll let love run it's course
I'll go farther than man could ever go
And you don't even know.
