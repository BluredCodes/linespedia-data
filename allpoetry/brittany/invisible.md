have i become invisible
to everyone around me
you don't seem to hear me
so does that mean you cannot seem me
has society gotten to all of your heads
so that your eyesight is failing
i don't quite understand..
i'm still hear
i can feel your presence
i can see and hear you all
so why am i an exception
why am i so invisible
to those who really ought to be?i hate life.. and those who used to claim to be my friends..
