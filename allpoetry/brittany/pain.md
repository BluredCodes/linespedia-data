Pain is mountingI feel it comeThrough my lifeThere's always been one.Crying with no tearsCrying for hopeNo one cares nowExcept the poke.Waiting for tomorrowWanting something otherTake this life from meTo give but another.This ache I feelEvery day - every nightWill never fade awayEven after life.Surmountable screamsYearning for comfortNo one left to helpEveryone is hurt.What is this life we live?What makes it so hard?All is burning through this painLook how we've come so far.This poem is about depression. Life seems to become impossible at times, and this is about the point where you are ready to surrender.
