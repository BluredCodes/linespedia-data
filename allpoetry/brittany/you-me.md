title: "You & Me"

Love can move mountains
faith can part the seas
so imagine what could happen
with you and me.

My love is never-ending
you are a time-less love story
more than I ever wished for
perfect for me eternally.

Mountains and stars are just things
hearts and souls feel
beauitful as you really are
my love shall never leave.i wrote this to my boyfriend the other night. we had a "disagreement" so we were making up at this point. everytime we make up, we right poetry on the spot. it's really romantic. this is just one of the pieces i wrote there.
