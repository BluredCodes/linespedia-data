---
title: "I'm Through"
writer: "Brittany"
slug: "im-through"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I loved you so
didn't think life existed
outside of your love

You hurt me
ran from me time and time again
left my heart broken beyond repair

Now you come to me
asking to love you once more
my mind is left at unease

I loved you then
you were perfect for me
but I found life does exist beyond you

I've found someone new
someone who won't run
to make my heart feel at ease again

I can't love the new you
you aren't the same as you were
I loved him...not the new

So please leave me be
I've lived in heartache enough
find someone to fill your needs

I'm through...I cannot love him anymore..
I've battled it far too long.. and I can't do it anymore
