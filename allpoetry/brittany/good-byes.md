---
title: "Good-byes"
writer: "Brittany"
slug: "good-byes"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Now that you'll soon be gone
I can finally admit
that I've done some wrong
and I'm truly sorry for it.

But I'll never regret
that you were my friend.
Whenever I would fret
a shoulder you would lend.

You mean more than words can say
to me in every way.
So I really wish you'd stay
and never go away.

My tears may neam no longer
what they had in the past
because they flow stronger
when you run away so fast.

Good-byes are the hardest to say
when you're put to the test.
But you can't change 
that I've tried my best.
