---
title: "You Saved Me"
writer: "Brittany"

I thought my heart was dead
broken and tattered on the floor
you picked the pieces up with care
made it safe to love once more

I owe you my life
my love and strength is yours
you saved me from myself
allowed me to be reborn

I love you more than I knew I could
completing the void I held within
you love me when I thought it was impossible
showed me I could love again

I've been afraid for so long
never letting anyone know the real me
you carried me away
giving me courage to finally be.

I owe you eternally
would do anything to please you
you saved me from myself
shared your strength to make me newokay, i know that i usually say that love sucks and that it hurts so much, but i still beleive that. i just usually go through these huge mood swings, and i guess today was just a good day. i love my boyfriend more than i ever thought possible, but i'm still afraid of love and of being tossed aside as i have been so many times.
