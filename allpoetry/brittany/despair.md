deathdyingsicknessfallingsinking, sinkingcoldpainpounding, poundingin my brainsicknesspaincoldwetshivers, shiverstearsfallingdowndeeper, deeperdeathhellfeinds awaitingpeople screamingburning, burningsouls yearningcryptic laughingdevils dancingfire, firehearts poundingchildren cryingmad thoughtsnever endingwanting deathThis poem was written by one of my very good friends. I think that she deserves to be recognized for the feelings that we both share, but I am not gifted to put to words.
