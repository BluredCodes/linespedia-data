Don't let me cry these tears of pain.
Don't let me heart feel ahamed.
Hold me hand, look into my eyes
and tell me everything is alright.
Life is not meant to hurt so bad.
My heart is not supposed to feel so sad.
I've cried for too long.
Tell me that life does go on.
These ears need to hear
something other than tears.This poem can be interpreted in any fashion. Anyone that has had something painful in their life should be able to relate to this feeling. I feel this everyday.
