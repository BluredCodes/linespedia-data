I worry about you
I worry that you will hurt yourself 
out of spite of others
I fear that the cutting will get too deep
and the blood will wash away your life
I fear you will never be happy again
I wish for you to have all you ever imagined
to have all the riches your heart desires
I wish you would take my words to heart
and save your own life.Melissa, I love you girl!
I worry about you so bad!
And don't think that you're a burden on me, cuz you're the farthest thing from it!!
