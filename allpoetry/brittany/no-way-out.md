---
title: "No Way Out"
writer: "Brittany"
slug: "no-way-out"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/19391-No-Way-Out-by-Brittany"
---

Suicide is it
there's no way out
anger
frustration
pain
suffering
death has become us
there's no way out
conformity
fright
hate
disgust
we live our lives
there's no way out
