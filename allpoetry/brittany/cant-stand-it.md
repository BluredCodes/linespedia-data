title: "Can't Stand It"

I'm annoyed by everyday life
can't stand to be around the people
that I used to love
that I used to live be with

I'm tired of life
can't stand to be "normal"
by going out and living
by going into public the way that I am

I'm disgusted by everything
can't stand to live anymore
to be alive and breathing
to be around anybody or anything

I just can't stand it anymoreI'm in a whole in my life.
Gonna have to start seeing a counselor now.
My mom's freaking out on me again because of it.
I cried myself to sleep again last night.
Geeze.. I hate life!
