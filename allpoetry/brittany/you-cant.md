---
title: "You Can't"
writer: "Brittany"
slug: "you-cant"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/16331-You-Cant-by-Brittany"
---

Pain so deep
it isn't real
Hurt so hard
it can't be true
Breaking pieces
bleeding hearts
Anguish is inevitable
Suffering can't be overcome
You brought this on yourself
Don't you wish to take it back?
You can't
