i laugh and smile
while all i want to do is cry
i try to please you
but yet you continue to question
i persist that nothing is wrong
when all along, i'm dying inside
but you don't need to know that
i don't want to burden you any longer
i'd rather just die
so i'll continue to smile
and laugh to look happy
for you
but i'm only laughing
to keep from cryingi do this all of the time
smile, trying to hide the tears welling up in my eyes
