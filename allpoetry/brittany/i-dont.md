---
title: "I Don't"
writer: "Brittany"
slug: "i-dont"
source: "AllPoetry"

Why should I care?
no one else cares for me
you act and smile
yet lie behind my back
Why should I care?
when all true life
has been drained from my body
I'm but a zombie
walking around
and still not living
Why should I care?
my pills take away all feeling
my head spins and I fall
but it's better than living
So why should I care?
I don'tand i seriously don't care about anything anymore..
