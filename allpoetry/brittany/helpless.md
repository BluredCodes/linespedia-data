---
title: "Helpless"
writer: "Brittany"

Pain so deep you cannot bleed
other pass by and take no heed
you welcome death
though it will not come
treasure all things lost

Chained down
they can't hear my screams
they spit on me
ignore my pleas

A prisoner in my own mind
no way to escape
those thought that bind

No tears to shed
they are not real
the pain lasts
never to heal

Never let anyone in
never let anyone know
ignore what they tell you
never give in and fall

There's no way out
you're forever trapped
gasping for airThis poem was written by one of my very good friends. I think that she deserves to be recognized for the feelings that we both share, but I am not gifted to put to words.
