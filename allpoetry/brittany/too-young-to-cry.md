---
title: "Too Young To Cry"
writer: "Brittany"
slug: "too-young-to-cry"
source: "AllPoetry"

Afraid of all the unknown
Afraid to learn too much
Please let me free to go
I have no purpose here
Lord known I've seen the such

*Don't take my wings just yet
I'm too young not to fly
Don't take my wings from me
It's still too soon to cry*

Sitting on the sidelines
Afraid to make another step
Unsure of the reason why
Please let me free to go
I'll land before it's dark

*Don't take my wings just yet
I'm too young not to fly
Don't take my wings from me
It's still too soon to cry*

I've made my mistakes
I'll admit I have
We all do - we're only human
forgive my wrongs
I knew no better

*Don't take my wings just yet
I'm too young not to fly
Don't take my wings from me
I'm still too young to cry*corny, yes... but it's from me, so would you expect anything less?

this is about being forced to grow up too fast, but not sure that that's what you want. childhood is great now and then.. but it's something that's essential for us all to experience. i've hardly had the chance.
