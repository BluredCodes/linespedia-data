---
title: "You Said You Loved Me"
writer: "Brittany"
slug: "you-said-you-loved-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

My head is spinning
can't think or do
you said you loved me
but I know that can't be true.

You were with her
the other night
she kept getting closer
but you didn't fight it.

You didn't make love
but the cheating is there
you're guilty of it all
and you say you still care.

I feel betrayed
my body has gone limp
I shouldn't be such a baby
I shouldn't be such a wimp.

How could I love you
and let my guard down
oh yeah, that's right
you showed me how.

It's all been a lie
none of it could have been true
you said that you loved me
now what am I to do?
