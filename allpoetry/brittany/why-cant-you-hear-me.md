silent screams
going unheard
why cna't you hear me?
I'm yelling
and crying
asking for sactuary
yet you don't flinch
don't even blink
why can't you hear me?
my voice is breaking up
going hoarse now
my pleas for help are but whispers
but my tears are holding strong
you can't make me quit
so why can't you hear me?i hate this feeling...
it's what will kill me in the end
