---
title: "At A Distance"
writer: "Brittany"
slug: "at-a-distance"
source: "AllPoetry"

Looking at a distance I see
all that life could ever be.
But the distance keeps me
from ever being free.
I'm balled all inside
waiting for time to pass by.
And the ball never will slide
even when I ask why.
All true feelings are kept near
so as no one can hear.
These feelings - my worst fear
to let them slip with a single tear.
Staying with my best bet,
keep the distance met.
Everything inside is kept
where the distance is meant.
