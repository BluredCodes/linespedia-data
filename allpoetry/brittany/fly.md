---
title: "Fly"
writer: "Brittany"
slug: "fly"
source: "AllPoetry"

Traveling down the same
dusty trail I've seen
for far too long already.
The road feels too comfortable.
My guard has mistaken its position.
I must leave this place.
Find a road to suit the new me.
Sprout wings to escape.
Fly away into the night.
As long as I fly...away.
Dust the pain away
start a new beginning.
Fly away into the day.
Looking to never return.
Only to fly...ahhh.. the dream of flying away but to never return.
