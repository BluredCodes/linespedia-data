I am filled with hate
I tried to fight it, but it was too late
I looked around and I did find
Chaos is a state of mind

I realized there is nothing in my life
I can't be freed from this plight
I write a poem, make sure it rhymed
Chaos is a state of mind

I want you to tell the truth, for my sake
I know politicians are nothing but fake
I look and see; yet I'm still blind
Chaos is a state of mind.
