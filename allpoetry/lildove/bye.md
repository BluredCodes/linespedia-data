Across the lonely ocean
Here the mournful cry
An orphan seagull, all alone 
Whose family just said 'bye'
They did not say it directly
But she reads between the lines
She hurts so much
All she has ever looked for is a loving touch
But she marches on with no complaint
Or even a weary sigh
But later on, she cries some more
As her spirit starts to die
