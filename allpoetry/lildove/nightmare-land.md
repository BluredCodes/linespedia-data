When the sky falls down,
Courage is lost
My face in everlasting frown
My hopes and dreams, gone
My soul will be dead before long
If only I could see the light
Through this never-ending night
Pit of despair, Dragons lair
Strong like the big oak tree
Once blind, I now see
I do apologize,
I hope you understand
I was dreaming when I wrote this
In Nightmare Land
