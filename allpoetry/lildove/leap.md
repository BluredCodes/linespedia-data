Help me, what am I to do
I hope my strength will see me through
I wish I didn't have to leave
My life is here like the air I breathe
The loneliness shone upon my face
The day my life went to waste
No matter how hard I try
The day will come that I must fly
Bird from the nest
Separate from all the rest
This is the time that I must wait
For the inevitable coming of my fate.
