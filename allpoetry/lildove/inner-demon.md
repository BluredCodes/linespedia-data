Hear the siren song of spite
Make people undo what is right
Hear the shrieking banshee scream
Then pretend it's just a dream
Vampires come, eternal life
Sinking in to pools of strife
Werewolves, mummies, evil ghosts
Of all the demons I've met,
I'm scared of myself the most.
