writer: "LiLDove"

The saddened end will take a toll 
A vigil mind has lost control 
One missing part now to mend 
This broken heart cannot defend 

Lost to time and time alone 
Take a mark that hasn't shown 
Yearn what's left and hope to see 
Left my heart aside to be 

Gripping to these memories 
Breaking down onto my knees 
Using thought to pray for past 
Till the future I await, alas
