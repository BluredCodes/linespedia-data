As I look through the window, longing your embrace
I think I hear you call my name, yet you are so far away
I turn around, hoping to see your face
But all I see is emptiness
The emptiness of my soul
I beg you not to leave me
But you say that if you leave, you will no longer torture me with my own love
But every second I miss you
Every minute I long to kiss you
Every day I love you
You are destroying me
My heart aches and bleeds
