Play with my mind
I think not
Because inside you shall find
A nightmare no doubt
My sense is now burning in hell
As we now speak
Sinking in to an empty well
You are just far too weak
To see what's inside
It's a fight
Couldn't even if you tried
Never in me, find even a hope of light
