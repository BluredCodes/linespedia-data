---
title: "Love laugh"
writer: "LiLDove"
slug: "love-laugh"
source: "AllPoetry"

I hope my soul will soon be dead
Stop these demons in my head
I gave you my heart; you broke it in half
Used my love for a laugh
Couldn't fight this feeling, I did try
Nights were my time to cry
Alone, disconsolate without you
Can't trust anymore, I don't want to
