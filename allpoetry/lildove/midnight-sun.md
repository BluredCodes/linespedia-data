---
title: "Midnight Sun"
writer: "LiLDove"

In the land of the living
Midnight sun,
Where their blessed
Love begun.
The stars shone brightly
Against the sky,
Glistening lights
That never die.
Upon the deserted
Desert sand,
Where they laid
Hand in hand.
Sharing thoughts
And making dreams,
A fire in their hearts
That still gleams.
She with her head
Upon his chest,
Listening to
His body rest.
Him with his fingers
Twirling her hair,
Had never seen
Skin so fair.
Here in the land of the midnight sun,
Where two hearts
Melted to one.
Here where the stars
Still shine bright,
No matter where
They are tonight.
