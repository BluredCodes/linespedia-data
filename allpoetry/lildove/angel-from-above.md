All I want is for you to hold me in your arms
Hold me; never let me go, let you protect me from all harms
You will be the best for me
'Cause when I'm with you I can see
I can see my reason to live
My reason to give
And it's you
You have no idea how much I want to hear "I love you too"
You're the best thing in the world for me
When I'm with you, when I hear your voice, I feel blissful and free
I've loved you for so long
And I'm trying to hold out and be strong
You're my baby, my love
The one true love I've found, my angel from above
I'm not perfect; I'm not the most beautiful
But whenever I'm around you I feel such a strong pull
I want to wrap you in my arms, and give you a passionate kiss
That would be my life dream, my one moment of true bliss
And I just want you to realize
That you're different from other guys
And the one who will be standing there when everyone leaves
Is me
