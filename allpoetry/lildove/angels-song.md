title: "Angel's Song"
writer: "LiLDove"

Morning sun,
Evening rain
Signs of smiles,
Followed by pain. 
What a life,
I have led
Oh, the tears
That I have shed.
Far away
In the mist,
"Angel's songs"
The dragon hissed,
"Are of Love,
Life, and hope
Up the steep,
And curving slope"
