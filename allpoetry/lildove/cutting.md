title: "Cutting"

Tear the flesh, open wide
Try to see the pain inside
Then when you find a way
You start to do it every day
On the surface, see the blood
Coming in a torrential flood
Starts to hurt like hell's own fire
Slice the arm with a wire
End of pain, start of life
All on the tip of a knife
Fall down, start to sink
Life flies by in just one wink.
