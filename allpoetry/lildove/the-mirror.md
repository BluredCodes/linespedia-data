I looked in the mirror one dark day
And saw a little girl,
Of her slowly darkening mind too scared to say
Then I saw a young child
Helpless and so alone
Who dreaded the thought of school to home
Then the mirror went dark and I did see
A healthy young woman
So happy and free
That's when I realized
The young woman…was me
