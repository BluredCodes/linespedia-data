No where to run, even less places to hide
Got no place to turn, for the pain is inside
It slowly grows, this sickness in my heart
And it gets worse every moment we are apart
Spreading along and my soul cries
For my head forgot the color of your eyes
With each passing second that you're away
The light gets fuzzy, I can't see my way
You are the only one who can cure me
Antidote that'll set me free
Stop the pain, I'm begging you please
Save me from this love disease
