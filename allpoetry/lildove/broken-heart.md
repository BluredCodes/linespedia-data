title: "Broken Heart"

I think my heart just broke in half
If this keeps happening, my heart won't last
He said something so cruel, I didn't think he had the means
But it's exactly as it seems
I love him so much that it hurts inside
Now I have no hope and only him to think about and cry
So many memories flash through my mind
So many more tears fall down my face
There's so many sweet times with you I need to erase
