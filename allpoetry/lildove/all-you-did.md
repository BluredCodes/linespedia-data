All you did was hurt me
All you did was lie
All you did was see to it that every night I'd cry
All you did was make me feel like I wasn't worth your time
All you did was leave me here and say that you were never mine

Well I no longer give a shit
No longer care at all
Without you in my mind
My lifes a total ball

You dragged me down
Would not let me go
Until you were sure i loved you so
And then you let it show
You didnt care that I loved you
You said straight out you werent true
And on the spot
I got rid of you

Now i have found someone new
A love more than perfect and true
He will never decieve me, never play me a fool
Never leave me just to be cool

All you did was mess up and play
So all i did was send you away
Now your crawling back to me
Saying that you are so so sorry
All i have to say to you
Is get away from me, and Fuck You Too!!*grins* here ya go shawn..have a nice life!!
