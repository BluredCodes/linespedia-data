title: "Since you left me"

Ever since you left me
There hasn't been one night I haven't cried
'Cause the day that you were killed
My soul, it died
I feel as though my soul is gone
And although it's been a while
The heartache is still so strong
I wish so much that you'd appear again
So things can stop being the way they've been
And I just want to let you know
There's not one night I don't let my pain show.
