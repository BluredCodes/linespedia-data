---
title: "Distant Love"
writer: "LiLDove"

I look at you, you look at me
I hope one day that you can see
You're so close, but still a world away
Out of reach in every way
Try to hide my feelings
Your gaze leaves me reeling
Your spirit so close to my heart
And one day we shall part
In my heart is where I'll hide
Please never leave my side
Even though your leaving me all by myself
Love will pull us through
Through heaven and hell
