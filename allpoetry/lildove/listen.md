title: "Listen!!!"
writer: "LiLDove"

Sometimes I feel like you don't listen
As the tears falling down my face glisten
You never cared
My only friend is my teddy bear
I try to tell you
What's wrong with me
And yet you push me away
You will later see
The problem that was bothering me
Was nobody would ever listen to me!!!
