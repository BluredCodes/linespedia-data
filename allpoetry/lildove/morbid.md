Gogh sent her his ear, 
Would've sent his eye
All to never see her cry
Romeo died
Went and killed himself
Smiling, book goes back on the shelf
Lovers leap, there's a girl in her teens
Jumped off a cliff, it's almost obscene
Want to see more stupid stuff people do for their lovers?
Love is morbid, yes its true
But that is the way I feel for you
