I drag the blade across my skin
And watch the blood flow thin
This morbidly fantastic feeling overwhelms me
And I feel this is, finally the end, I can see
Yet as I sink in to this pool of strife
I wonder if I really want to take my life
But then I remember all those who lied
And I continue on with my suicide…
