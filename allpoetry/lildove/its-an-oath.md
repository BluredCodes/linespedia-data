I knew I loved you
But until today I didn't know if it was really true
I knew I was in love, but not to what extent
I wasn't definite that when I said I loved you its what I really meant
But today I thought I was going to lose you
I became petrified that the cheating conversation was true
My heart hit the floor
I ran away, crying hysterically
And collapsed, halfway out the door
Now I know that everything I've been saying came from the heart
Because when the threat of losing you was here
I knew I wouldn't live if we were to part
The thought of losing you just brought on an unbelievable amount of fear
I can't lose your love
You're my baby, I'm you little dove
My heart belongs to only you
Now I'm sure, my love is true
I think these feelings go for us both
Now when I say I love you
It's not a promise, it's an oath.This is to the love of my life..i love you so much Derek...And i know you will never hurt me...Youve always been there for me..I Love You
