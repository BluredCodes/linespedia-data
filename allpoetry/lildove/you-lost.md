title: "You Lost"

Hello bitch, look at me
I've made it through, so you've been beat
But you're not like me, your hearts not bleeding or tore
I've lost the battle, but you've lost the war
I want to hold a knife to your head
But I'm the better person, so you're not dead
You still long to torture me, 
But I've found a way to set myself free
So when you look at me
You will see
The hatred that rapidly consumes me
