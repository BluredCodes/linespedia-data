When we met it was like paradise
Like when the light hits the horizon at sunrise
At first sight it was love
That goes over, beyond and above
When I see him I begin to smile
Because I know he'll go that extra mile
Then he smiles back
Lifts his shirt and shows me his pack
My heart goes wild, my mind crazy
And the world around me becomes hazy
I don't want it to end
It's like a wound in my heart beginning to mend
As we become one
We indulge in something we've never done
Something that takes away all pain
Enough to drive a girl insane
When we met it was like a dream come true
We'll stay together forever, me and you.
