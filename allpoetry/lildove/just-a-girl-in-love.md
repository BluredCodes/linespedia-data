title: "Just a Girl In Love"
writer: "LiLDove"

I loved you, you know it's true
You confuse me, leave me feeling dazed
Every time I see you, 
Unshed tears make my eyes glazed
I used to talk to you every day
Then you vanished
And I missed you in the most horrible way
Now you no longer love me
Although I was hoping so much we'd one day be
As days turned to nights
And night to day
I figured you must not have loved me, no way
For why would someone like you love me?
The reason for you loving me I just couldn't see
I figured you must have just lied
When I thought of this, I felt as though my soul had died
So I gave up hope, I thought it was just as well
And grabbed the next guy I could get
To get my self out of this love-based hell
Yet night after night
For peace with myself I continued to fight.
Now every time it begins to get dark, I sit by the phone
A shred of hope left, thinking maybe you'll call
Tears gleam within my eyes
I'm just a girl in love…waiting for the time she'll fall
