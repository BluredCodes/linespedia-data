Said no
Slap across the face
Hard blow
He left his trace

Bruise from the top of her forehead
Down to her chin 
Just because of what she said
Weak girls never win

She was taught to say no
She didnt love him
He said 'so?'
But she continued to refuse, suddenly a lot of pain
Her head throbbing, the lights became dim

She was left lying there
All alone
Feeling violated and bare
Afraid to make what happened known

So scared
Shes afraid
She cant believe he dared
Just because she wouldnt let him get laid

Not by her anyway
Shes so hurt
And still to this day
She has so many problems...tries to cry them away

But she never succeeds
They're still there
her broken heart always left to bleed

Twelve years old
Now fourteen
I'm still so scared and alone
