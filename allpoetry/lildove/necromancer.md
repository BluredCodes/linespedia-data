Call up a spirit
But you can't hear it
Toy with a ghost
Soon to be its host
Dance with a demon
No longer a free man
Go and draw your sword
Attempt to capture dragons hoard
But even as you ride on a beautiful white horse
You fall in the mud, Things get worse
In your mind and in your soul, goes up a wall
Will you still catch me if I fall?
There it goes, my silent cry
Will you save me before I die?
