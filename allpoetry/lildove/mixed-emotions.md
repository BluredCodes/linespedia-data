There's mixed emotions running through my head
When I realize it was you who led
Me straight in to this black abyss
Yet every time I see you I want to give you a kiss
I'm all confused and dazed
And you're not even in the slightest bit phased
I love you so much that I hate you
But that makes no sense, Cause when you're not around I'm always blue
You've disrupted my heart and mind with your perceptions
Jumbled my thoughts because you make me ask so many questions
I just feel so strange
I feel so unwanted and deranged
I have so many thoughts, so I'm just stuck here fuming'
Cause I feel as though you think I'm inhuman
There's mixed emotions running through my mind
And soon I hope, peace I'll find
