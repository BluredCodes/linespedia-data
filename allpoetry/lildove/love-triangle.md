I love him so much
I just want to feel his loving touch
But he loves her with all his heart
He never wants her and him to part
She loves me but doesn’t know why
She says her strange feelings for me make her want to cry
So do I
She has him wrapped around her finger
He’s loved her for years, but still I linger
Waiting for the day
He will send those feelings away
And realize that it’s me he wants	
My love for him continues…my heart it haunts
He should realize it is I who will keep him from being hurt
But right now whenever I leave him, I think about my time with him
And I feel like a piece of dirt
Why does he say he loves me even though he loves her more?
Why does he get my hopes up just to let me fall?
Why, whenever he’s in trouble, it’s me he’ll call?
When he loves her more than me
But she loves me more than she loves him
A confusing love triangle torments me
Frustrated and confused
