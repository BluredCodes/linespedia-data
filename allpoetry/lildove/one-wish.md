If I could have just one wish
It would be to hold you forever
Night and day I see your face
The urge to touch, stopping, never
A kiss is worth a million songs
And sonnets interminably long
Your embrace heals me
And from the world takes every wrong
This may be weird
This may be sappy
But in your arms,
Is where I'm happy
So hold me hun,
Don't do me rotten
And think of things, 
Long forgotten
