Your face is carved from smoke
Your arms numb the world in a single stroke
Your eyes are the darkness beneath a storm
Your chest is the mountain that keeps me warm
The sound of your voice makes the planets collide
The weight of your lips changes the pace of the tide
You make me whole 
You tear me apart
Your perceptions of life
Unsilenced my heart
