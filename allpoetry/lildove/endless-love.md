title: "Endless Love"
writer: "LiLDove"

I love you so much
All I want in life is to feel your love filled touch
I have no idea how you did this to me
But I feel like I'll love you endlessly.
I wish these strange feelings were not mine
But I can't deny it, I think about you all the time.
I know you don't feel the same way I do
But I can't help myself…I'm still in love with you
I wish I wasn't, because I know I'll just wind up in pain
Because in me loving you, there isn't much to gain
Although I may not get your love, 
At least you'll know I think you're my angel from above.
At least you'll know you have some one to call
When you feel as though the world around you is about to fall.
I hope you know that all of the above is endlessly true
It's not good for me, but I love you. 
I really don't think you believe what I say
I don't think you realize I really feel as though I'll love you until my dying day
Your eyes twinkle like the stars own blaze,
I melt even under your quick gaze
I can't take the miles
That we have between our hearts
I can't take not seeing you smile
Whenever we're apart.
The sky has lost its color
The sun has turned light gray
At least that's how it feels to me
Whenever you're away
I crawl up in a corner, to watch the minutes pass
Each one brings me closer
To the time I'll see you at last.
I sound as though I'm obsessed with you
But I know I'm not
The sky is just a different blue
When I haven't seen you a lot.
Talking to you is like hearing angels talking in my sleep
Each of your 'I love you's makes me want to break down and weep
Yet at the same time, I am so blissful and free
Believing for a moment that you love me.
For everything is okay whenever you are talking
Cause' on top of clouds
Is where I'm walking
This stupid little poem
Was to show you how I feel
Although I know you love somebody else,
I just wanted you to know my love is more than real.
