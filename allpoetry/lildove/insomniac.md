I can't sleep, I'm alone
I lost my spirit; I'm just flesh and bone
Close my eyes, lay down in bed
Try to force these demons out of my head
Stare in to darkness, I can't tell
Where I am or if I'm well
This must be some kind of sorcery
Because hate and nightmares overcome me
Morning comes, doves cry
And in my bed, awake I lie.
