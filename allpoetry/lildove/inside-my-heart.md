---
title: "Inside My Heart"
writer: "LiLDove"

I said I would do fine without you
Without your love
So unperfect and so untrue
In times of hardship to you I stupidly flew
Yet without you the sky isn't as bright a blue
I said I didn't need you
I guess I lied
Can't stay away
No matter how I've tried
You walk past and I went to hide
While deep inside my heart, I cried
