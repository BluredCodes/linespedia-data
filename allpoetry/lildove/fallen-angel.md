Down to Earth 
Feet on the ground
I look straight ahead
Don't turn around
In all I do
I'm here for you
I'm your fallen angel

Stretch my arms
Reach to the sky
My wings are broken
But I need to fly
Look at me, 
I'm not what you see
I'm a fallen angel
Fly high
Where the eagle sings
Fly high
Fix these broken wings

A breath of wind
A whisper of sound
I rise through the mist 
With my feet off the ground
Wont you come with me
Cause I don't want to be
A fallen angel
