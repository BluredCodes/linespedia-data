You say you don't want to hurt me
So you turn and walk away
Love or logic, no one can tell
What is wrong or right we say

You leave me alone, try to save us both some pain
Lonely nights and daydream days
Without you my world isn't the same
Think with your head or think with your heart
Nothing lasts forever; we might just grow apart
But this isn't puppy love, I know it's true
I can't believe it, but I love you

Loving you is hard, this I know now
But if we stick together, we'll get through it somehow
And without you in my life
Nothing can be right
With the darkness
Alone I have to fight
I sit by the phone, waiting for your call
Tears glisten in my eyes, waiting for a chance to fall…
