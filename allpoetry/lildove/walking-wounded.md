---
title: "Walking Wounded"
writer: "LiLDove"

Spiral downwards, depths of hell
Fear infests where hopes have fell
Staring in to black abyss
Searing is the flaming kiss
World around crashes down
Reality knocks it to the ground
Dance with demons in lethal dose
We are all the walking wounded
Yet you smile at me in spite

My mind brims over, full of searing pain
I close my eyes, its still the same
Wake up, go back to sleep
Try to escape the world you see
Make everything bounce off your skin
Even love cant get within
Stumble but stay on your feet
Try to keep the pain discreet
Why has everthing gone wrong?
We are all the walking wounded,
Yet you smile at me in spite

Scream and no one will hear
All you have is pain and fear
Twist and turn in fates flame
Stop and your gonna take the blame
No ones here to help you live
You've given all you have to give
Human touch is not worth th pain
We are all the walking wounded
Yet you smile at me in spite
