Nameless and Unimportant 
I walk among the crowd
I need to get out of this hole but I can't
Depression is covering my mind like a shroud
I walk blinded
I can no longer see
I need arms in to which i can flee
I need a rope, someone to help me out
But help is something i don't know how to go about
I need someone to not listen to me when i say 'leave me be'
Can't you see? My heart is screaming Help Me!!
