Dreams and demons walk hand in hand
Through it all right here i stand
Aphasia joins in the crowd
We all walk silent, theres not a sound
A mist settles over the group
Left, right,left, right watch our troop
Our heads covered by paper bags
Outcasts of society, dressed in rags
Dont try to fight against despondency
We still walk slowly, no bright or fancy
We walk forevr, children of the night
So turn away, ignore our plight

Were different from you
We know that now
We know the truth
While to idols you bow
Distorting reality, you taught us hate
Well stay ourselves, wont take your bait
We tried to conform, it didnt work out
You never knew what we were about

You locked us down, we still prevail
And we survived to tell the tale
You tied us down, would not let go
Until the day that we would show
The feelings that were deep inside 
When all we used to do was hide
We wont learn, we already know too much
You use ignorace as a crutch
When the time came, we let you in
You took advantageof our wrongs and sins
You have no clue what were feeling or doing
It doesnt matter if you think were inhuman
