Stoned Again
Where have I been
In the midst of the past few hours?
I’ve been looking at the sky
Wondering why
The clouds are shaped like castle towers
Why the stars never fade in their glow
Why they give such hateful beings
Such a wonderful show
Why we don’t realize we are blessed
I never realized until now
That the world we have today
Is amazing-it’s like ‘wow!’
We are blessed in every way
So as I continue to look at the sky
I continue to wonder why
Why when I wake up tomorrow
After my night of being baked
Why I wont remember to forget my sorrow
And smile a true smile, not fakedStOnEd AgAin...For all those stoners out there, you ever had these feelings? When your stoned, your happy, and you try to remind yourself that life is great and we are blessed...then, when you wake up in the morning, you just cant do it!
