Perfect in every way
'Thug' as some people may say
I can barely describe how he makes me feel
I feel…
As I may kneel when he walks by
Or cry 
And I know why
Because he doesn't love me as I love him
And it hurts so badly
But then I look again
And can think nothing but glee
For he,
Is perfect in every way
'Thug' as some people may say
