I whisper a silent prayer
To a God I cannot see
I don't know if he's even there
And listening to me
I wanted you for my own
But that was not to be
I gave my heart to you on loan
But you gave it back to me
It was broken to bits
In a million shards
Now it beats in painful fits
I knew you dealt the cards
They were never in my favor
But I will be okay
If I ignore the bitter flavor
Because I love you anyway
