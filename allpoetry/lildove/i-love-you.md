---
title: "I Love You"
writer: "LiLDove"
slug: "i-love-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

Every time you look in to my eyes
I know it's true
I love you
And every time we touch
It may be too much
But I can't tear myself away
And then I look in to my soul
And see everything I've been living for
The love that I have found in you
Will always assure me and see us through
