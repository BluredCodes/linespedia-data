---
title: "My Love"
writer: "LiLDove"

As I looked in to your eyes
I saw a world of pain and saddened sighs
Now I look again and see 
Your undying love for me
You helped me stretch my wings and fly
Now hopefully I can help you in to the sky
At night I dream of our future together
Forgetting about bad things that once were
For now I see nothing but blue skies ahead
As I lay next to your sleeping head
You make my worst days seem like nothing
And as long as I live, I want you to know you are my king
Now I can stop my endless search for love
For I've found my angel, and you've found your lil' dove.
I feel like I'll love you even in death
For I can see myself telling you this when I breathe my last breath.
