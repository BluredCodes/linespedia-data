---
title: "Shell"
writer: "LiLDove"
slug: "shell"
source: "AllPoetry"

It's lonely in here
But it's lonely outside
Get me out of this shell
No I don't want to hide
Want to fly
Want to feel the air beneath my wings
But I'm locked up in here
In this shell that I'm in
My home, my body, my mind
Just me, myself, and I
