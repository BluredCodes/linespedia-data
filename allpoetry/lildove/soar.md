I looked at you
You met my gaze
I thought my feelings were just a phase
But I was wrong
You helped me be strong
I didn't want to trust again
So things would never be the way they've been 
Then you came along and found your way in to my heart
You healed it and gave my life a new start
I feel much better than I used to feel 
Your so perfect it seems unreal 
You stretched my broken wings, taught me how to soar
Now I'm content saying you are the only one I want to fight for
I love you more than words can say
You make me feel awesome in every way
As the sun goes down and it gets black
I feel happy knowing sleep will come without nightmares waiting to attack
I can't believe how wonderful you are
Although the distance between us is so very far
I know that we will make it through
Because you have me, and I have you
