title: "Shattered Mirror"

Spiderweb cracks all over my face
Pieces missing-let me out of this place
Punch the glass, watch it fall
How and why did I come here at all?
Cant feel the pain, my open hand split
It starts to bleed, I don't give a shit

Shattered, Shattered mirror 
Shattered image of my self
My soul screams, can you hear her
Dancing in the glass that fell

The scars appear, I start to feel
The world around me becomes real
I'm frightened and alone
Is my heart really becoming stone?
