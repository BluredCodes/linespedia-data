You seem to think that I did wrong
Because our relationship didn't last very long
That's not true
And you should know
That in some way I have to let my feelings show
You are mistaken
When someone hit on me
I always made sure they soon knew I was taken
You said that I fucked up
That's a lie
You said I flirted too much
You're wrong
I have never set my mind to not cheating or flirting the way I did with you
So its bullshit that you say I made things fall through
I never ever cheated on you
When guys hit on me I did stuff to them that was cruel, and mean
So you know what?
You fucked up…you just want me to take the blame
Now I have to deal with the shame
Of you basically thinking that I am a slut
Don't say that to me
I need to maintain my sanity
I never tried as hard as I did with you
To make sure things did not fall through
So when you say that
It hurts so much
Because, except for yours, at that time, I denied mans touch
So when I say hi to you
Don't even act like I'M the one that MADE you do what really you just wanted to
