I'm sorry I hurt you so
I'm sorry I had to let you go
No I do not have love for you anymore
I never deserved you
You're the best I could ever do
But now I'm dumping you
Please move on from me
I know you wanted me to stay forever
But you got to find someone else
I'm sorry you thought I was the different one
I did once love you so
Or so it was thought
I did once care for you
And I'm trying so hard not to hurt you
But no matter what I do
You just seem to never let me go
Forgive me but we can no longer be
Why? You ask
I need to just get away and be free for a while
You will always love me, you say
Now I know your love was always true and stronger
Than I was ready for
Please forgive me; there is one out there who will love you more than I could 
You will find her
Until then don't remember me
And please forgive me
