As I walk away
I already miss the three words you say
You make me feel so special inside
When all I used to do was hide
Now I'm not afraid to be just me
You're the one that helped me be
The person I've been hiding within
Now things have stopped being the way they've been
I'm happier than I used to feel
I feel so joyful, so normal, and so real
And although, I know
That's a lot to put on one guy
I just can't help it
When you hug me, I want to leap up and fly.
