There once was a Grandmaster Kung Fu Monk
One day he fell into a real funk
Monk said, "I do declare all my students suck!"
"Rather than teach let me get hit by a truck!"
All he asked was that it happen early in the day
Then he wouldn't waste his last day that way
The next morning Master Monk went to cross the street
Poor thing got plowed down by a Mack Truck fleet
Now, poor poor Monk, he did not die
Oh no, you see, now in the hospital he does lie
You see, Monk had all his 200+ bones broken
Now for 6 months he hasn't spoken
His moronic students drop by every day
And he can't even tell them to stay away
The most unfortunate part of this little story
Is that although he was in his prime and glory
And although Mr. Monk was really quite strong
His manhood is no longer very long
It was partially cut off when he got plowed down
He looks at it every day with an angry frown
And although he lays there at his "leasure"
His wife has to go elsewhere for her pleasure!!For you Haikumonk!!!!!!!  You gave me the idea!!! Better be careful what you say :)
