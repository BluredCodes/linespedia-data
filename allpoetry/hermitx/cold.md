The clock slowly ticks away
Time drifts into the distance
I am left behind to find 
My way to where you went
Snow blinds me to your path
A chill penetrates
What can I do?
I gave all my warmth
To keep you going
A long ardous journy
I will not make it
But the cold only reminds me
That I made the right choice
Hope yearns and burns deep into the soul
Love will make the ice disappear
The body falls but the heart only sharpens
To the pain I feel when you're not here
Spring is only a day away...
