The sweet smell of your room beckons
I find my body unable to resist
Heaven draws me in with lights blinding
An angel takes my hand and caresses my soul
Fires dance and flicker left and right
Never knowing which way they want to go
Here I am walking blind
The allure softly grabs at my heart
Emotions flood the room 
And I have quickly been pulled under
My breath slowly slips
No struggle, no resistance
I die in your arms
With nothing but a smile on my face
Your presence surrounds all of me
I have found eternity in your heart
Forever stuck in a web of your skill
I'll never leave you
