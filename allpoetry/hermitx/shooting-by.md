A glance above only reveals
The truth we both know too well
I walk slowly towards your room
Pushing the door open slightly
I can't resist going in
I find you there
Tempting me 
No reason to hold back
I already checked my logic at the door
My hand reaches out to touch you
Is it an illusion?
Does it even matter?
No, why should it?
I am here and you are here
What more could I want?
But none of this is important
I take the moment by force
You are waiting for me
The distance quickly disappears
In each others arms
Emotion drips from your lip
A sweet embrace as our faces meet
What is to stop us?
Passion amuck
Will it, dare I say, lead to suck?
Perhaps I have said too much
For a dark fantasy should never be shared
I cannot help myself
Desire is uncontrollable
I must have you
The stars above twinkle for all
But the shooting ones are only
For those who are quick enough to see
Gone as quickly as they were there
As quickly as there was usrandom, very confusing
