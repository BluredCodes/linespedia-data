---
title: "What Am I?"
writer: "HermitX"
slug: "what-am-i"

I am your safety net
You only see me when you look down
I am the white noise
You only hear me when you can't sleep
I am a blanket
You only need me when you're cold
I am your friend
All the time
I am your love
Never.I have issues.  No, really I do.  Anyway...
