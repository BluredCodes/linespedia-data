Why does the sun rise?Why does the moon glow?Why does it rain?Why does my heart skip a beat?Why do the stars shine?Why do birds sing?Why do gems sparkle?Why do my eyes stay glued to you?It's all been heard beforeThe same old rhyme and timeMy feelings are set in stoneRadiant goddess if one ever shoneI am not myself around youI am something so much betterI was so alone before I met youI was lost before your light hit meI could never leave youI could never feel any lessI would give anything to be yoursI would die happy near youWhy do I love you?The answer need not be saidIsn't it obvious?Maybe it's just me?I'll always be what you need me to beI'll never stop chasing youI'll always be trueI'll never quitI'll always love youUhhhh =?
