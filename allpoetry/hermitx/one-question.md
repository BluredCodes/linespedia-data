What does she ponder?
Carefree as the air
Eyes closed tight but gently
What thoughts fly in her mind?
A smile breaks through
Like the first hint of spring
Solemness sprinkled with a little bit of joy
A lockett of hair trickles down her face
Across the unbroken ice of an eye
Like a kitten she bats it around
What happiness dances around her conscienciousness?
A light glows from a tiny ember
As a swift wind ignites it's passion
Soon a roaring inferno will burst forth
And the ice will shatter and melt
What action will it bring?
A careful stare met by unerving heat
So too do I melt in the inferno
But, what now does she ponder?I dunno
