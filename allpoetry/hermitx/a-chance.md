A glance across the room
A smile flashes, blinding my senses
We exchange thoughts
But are they our own?
Imaginations know not of reality
I stumble clumsily toward you
Is it just a dream?
My heart is exposed 
One last chance
Was I mistaken?
Did I not understand
I hope that I am right
The future beckons and you await
Here I go
Wish me luck fateA crush
