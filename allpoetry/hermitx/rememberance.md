---
title: "Rememberance"
writer: "HermitX"
slug: "rememberance"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Will you remember me now that I am gone?
Will you still run to me when things are upside down?
I was always there
Times weren't always the best
But I never gave up
We may have changed but
Does that really stand in the way?
Was I too much to handle?
Was I not what you expected?
All I can say is sorry
But even that won't help
There are no more chances
How long will you remember me?
