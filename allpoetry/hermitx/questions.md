I gotta ask why
Where did I make the mistake
Was it something I said
Am I just not the right kind of person
Why did I have to be the one out
I don't understand 
Why don't you notice me?
Did you not see me screaming?
I was always waiting
Fate said no,
And I said so?
Where does that leave me?
You could never really be happy to see me
It was all a lie that I never saw
The vortex dragged me blindly
As you sat there never willing to admit
That I was a friend
Misguided by a magnetic compass
Alone in the dark because the hand 
That reached out never felt
The warmth as it didnt exist...Figure this one out.
