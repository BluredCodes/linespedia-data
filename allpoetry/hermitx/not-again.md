Not again
The screws tighten
The eggshell is about to crack
I can't stand the pain
Visions of what I can't see
Scare me into a paranoia 
That cannot be defined
The darkness is slowly creeping
And I've run out of corner to back into
The ball has been dropped
As I dive for it I see
That I'm comming up short
I make mistakes
But I'm not allowed to fall
Too sensitive to hold
A fragile mind
I know I'm wrong
And that I shouldn't take it hard
But I cannot offer anything less
Because what you see is what you get
Oh no, not again
