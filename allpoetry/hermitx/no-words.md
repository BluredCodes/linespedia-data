---
title: "No Words"
writer: "HermitX"
slug: "no-words"

Silence fills the air
With only thoughts in the way
Of the sound I want to hear
I am afraid
To speak my mind
I fear time is swiftly
Finding the exit
With me nowhere near
There are no words to say
How much you mean to me
No action could ever define
The impact you had on me
There is simply nothing
