It's a quaint little place that few know about
Only a handful of people reside there 
With me included 
People come and people go
Often leaving their mark here and there
As for me and my 2 cents
I must say that it's a great place to be
It has that secure little feel
I love those rolling green hills
The quiet little brook
With secrets never brooding
Sometimes the weather isn't the greatest
Occasional storms and knee deep floods
But I never said I hated rain
The wind always whispered what needed to be said
Sometimes I wonder why this town is so deserted
It is beautiful and full of life
Of course, then I realize
That people never come to appreciate
All there is to see and do
In this wonderful little world of ours
Where the best kept secret is you.=)
