All are wandering souls
Undeniably lost on the path
Signs point left and right
But nothing will tell truth from fiction
How can one continue?
Beauty and passion await
The pot of gold at the end
Of the road that all must travel
Don't cry for me,
I am already gone
All I can leave is a simple message
Which you might just find
In the last place you look
I love you always.
