Why is it......
   You can make me laugh
       when no one else can,
   Make me see clearly
       when my eyes are full of sand,
   Make me smile 
       when there once was a frown,
   And stop the tear drops 
       from falling down,
   The answer is clear
       as the sky is blue,
   Theres only one reason why
       its because I LOVE YOUThis was written for my love of my life, Lee.
