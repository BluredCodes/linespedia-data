Why do I always end up with a broken heart?
It never sseemspossible right from the start
It sseemsas if everything is going great
But I always end up with a heart ache
Everyone laugh at me with despise
I can even see it in there eyes.

The things that come to mind
Are not always so kind
I'm saying this for the very last time
Love me for me, Not just for a 'good time'
I have hardly any heart left
Barely enough to live with

So if you get the rest of my heart
Take it, Don't break it right form the start
These words that I say 
Are here to stay
Today, Tommorrow, and AlwaysThe last part I think is bad can you tell me if it sounds Al right?
