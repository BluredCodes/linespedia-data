Your my friend my best friend my love along the way sweet and honest to this dayfrom the day we met its you i feened with the first words we spokeI knew we were meant to beA couple, A love, your heart and mineSwitched places as we fell in love for the very first timeWith this poem i want to sayfinding you has made my dayno matter how far apart we aremy heart will be yours and yours will be mine
