I'll never forget the times we share
In the love-filled springtime air
Its 1 AM, the time is right
I now sneak out into the night
The field by your house is where we meet
In the tall grass we take a seat
We throw out our arms and embrace
The silver moon lights up your face
We talk for hours on end it seems
Sitting in this field of dreams
This is how I want my life to be
You always here, close to me
We lay together your head on my chest
This is when I feel the absolute best
Knowing you have complete faith and trust
Loving you in a definite must
I hope this moment never ends
But I must go home before day begins
We press our lips together for a kiss
A wonderful feeling of pure bliss
This is the highlight of my week
Being with you is what I seek
The field is our secret on one knows
That is how the story goes
I hope you like it, I know I do
Willa, baby, I love you
