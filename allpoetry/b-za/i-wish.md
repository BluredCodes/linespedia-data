I sit here all alone tonight
Sitting here under the moonlight
Gazing at the stars above
Thinking of the one I love
A shooting star passes by
Its magnificent glow lights up the sky
I wish I may, I wish I might
God please grant my wish tonight
I wish that just for a day
I could have things my way
That Katie could be just mine
Just for a small amount of time
I don't think its too much to ask
I just want a small kiss that will last
Just a kiss oh so tender
Just a moment to remember
If I could have her just for a day
Then I would sweep her away
To a land filled with passion
In a very loving fashion
Our two souls become one
Just before the day is done
I wish I may, I wish I might
God please grant my wish tonightThis is dedicated to Katie, my bestfriend's girl. That's why I can't have her. But she likes me and I like her so... I dunno. If you have any suggests before I read it to her let me know. Thanks.

b-za
