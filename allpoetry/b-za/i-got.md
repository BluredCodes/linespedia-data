I got the windIt beginsBy blowing my kisses to youThen you'll hearIn you earMy sweet whispersI got the starsVenus and MarsCan finally agreeWe were meant to beHand in handAcross this landYou commandMy thoughtsMy every little actionI got the rainYou stop the painOf a bad dayWith your bright warming smileA tender kissIs pure blissCupid's arrow It didn't missI got the moonThe month of JuneVacation togetherSuch great weatherYou are my sunshineYou are my good timesYou are the best thing in my lifeI got all thisCause of youYou are my whole worldAnd I love youI wrote this one November 24, 2000.
