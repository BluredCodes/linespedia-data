You never know when love will come alongBut when it does, it comes on strongYou could be walking around at the mallAnd there she is, thin and tallYou could be in your brand new carAnd you see her from a farYou could she her at the poolAnd you start to act the foolYou could she her in a dreamAnd then there she is, the real thingShe could be the one with the crushAnd she keeps her secret hush-hushYou never know when love will come alongBut when it does, it comes on strongI wrote this June 26th. I wrote it while wondering where my soulmate.
