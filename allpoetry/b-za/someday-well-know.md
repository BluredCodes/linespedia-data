Someday we'll know if love can move a mountain 
Someday we'll know why the sky is blue
Someday we'll know if Gilligan got off that island
Someday we'll know why the sunk Atlantais
Someday we'll know loves dies sometimes

I bought a ticket to the end of the rainbow
Watch the stars crash into the sea
If I could ask God just one quetion
Do broken hearts heal

Someday we'll know what killed all the dinosaurs
Someday we'll know why the dark is scary
Someday we'll know what makes legs hairy
Someday we'll know why we have have ten toes
Someday we'll know why we have one nose
Someday we'll know why I wasn't meant for you

I bought a ticket to the end of the rainbow
Watch the stars crash into the sea
If I could ask God just one quetion
Will He keep you safe for me

Someday I'll go dancin on that rainbow
Someday we'll know who decides our fate
Someday we'll know why humans hate
Someday we'll be up in Heaven laughing
Someday we'll know if true love is once in a lifetime
