I believe in destiny
I believe that you and me
That we were meant to be
Just you and me

I believe in fate
I believe when we meet that date
That something brought us there
And when we began to stare
Deep into each others eyes
That we were meant to be

I believe in horoscopes
When the paper says
"Gemini: you had a fight
A few flowers will make is right"
I buy a dozen roses 
Send them to her house
With a note that says
"I sent these roses to you
so they can see what real beauty is"

I believe in love at first sight
Cause with once glance at you
I suddenly knew
That my heart belonged to you

I believe in destiny
I believe in fate
I believe in horoscopes
I believe in love at first sight
I belive that you and me
We were meant to be
