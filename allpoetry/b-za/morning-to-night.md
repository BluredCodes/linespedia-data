I lay alseep in my bed
Visions of you dance in my head
But its time for day to begin
So my dream must sadly end
You are the sunshine sneaking in
And with you my day begins
The perfect start to everyday
Things are already going my way
There may be some problems during the day
But with you they fade away
You are everywhere I want you to be
You are the shadow that follows me
You are the warmth in the air
You are my one and only care
You are the ground on which I stand
You are everything I love in this land
All day spent thinking about you
The sun begins to fade from view
Like watching the sun set on the day
Your beauty takes my breath away
Now let the party begin
As I begin to dream againthe 2nd poem written for annie my love
