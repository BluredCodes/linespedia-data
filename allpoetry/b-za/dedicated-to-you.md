I dedicate this poemTo the girl that I loveHer name is Grace and she's an angel from aboveShe's a princess too ya knowAnd I love her head to toeGrace you are my inspirationI'll always love you without hesitationI think its fateThat has brought us togetherAnd nothing could be betterThan being in your armsBeing in your loving graceSeeing your beautiful faceI can't stop thinking about youEvery minute I want you moreYou make my world spin on its topA wonderful feeling that will never stopI know its hard cause were far apartBut I know deep in my heartWe can do it, we can make it workThey say love conquers allThats why I dedicated this to youBecause I love you like I do
