I used to want you oh so muchHow I wanted your gentle touchJust one small kiss on the cheekCalling you to hear you speakYou were as close to love as can beThat is what you said to meI felt the exact same wayI fell for you more everydayYou were in control of my heartI hated it when we were apartI thought about you day and nightSurrounded by a golden lightI thought you were the girl of my dreamsBut that is not the reality of thingsYour boyfriend is my best friendI can't take you away from himSo what do I doI stop my emotions for youI stop thinking about you the way I didI put my emotions in a garbage can and close the lidI used to want you oh so muchNow I don't need your gentle touchDedicated to KatieI wrote this one today and yesterday. I just don't wanna be the guy to take my bestfriend's girl away from him. He loves her so much. I liked her alot and she liked me alot, we talked alot on the phone, but I can't do it.
