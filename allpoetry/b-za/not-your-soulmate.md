Blake, I'm sorry, you're not my soulmateBlake, I'm sorry, its not our fateThat's what you said when we broke upThat's what you said when I said 'sup?'Us being together is a mistakeI can't love you in that way BlakeThe more we are together I'll grow on youYou can learn to love me, that's what we'll doNo Blake, that's not the way it will beWe just weren't meant to be, can't you seeWilla is special and knows these thingsSo we're breaking up it seemsI wish I was the one for youBut breaking up is what we must doFate is something only God can decideFrom fate we cannot hideWe are friends though, to this dayAnd friends we shall always stayI wrote this poem this summer (early June) after me and Willa broke up. It just wasn't meant to be. Also read 'I'll Never Forget / Field of Dreams' which is also dedicated to Willa.
