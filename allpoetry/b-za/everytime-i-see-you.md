Everytime I see you
My knees they get all weak
Everytime I see you
My heart it skips a beat
Everytime I see you
I fall head over heels
Everytime I see you
I know how true love feels
Everytime I see you
My heart it seems to know
Everytime I see you
My love begins to grow
Everytime I see you
I know everything is right
Everytime I see you
I think of you all night
Everytime I see you
I know you are my soulmate
Everytime I see you
I know it was our fate
Everytime I see you
I know it was meant to be
Everytime I see you
I'm glad its you and meand the final poem for annie
