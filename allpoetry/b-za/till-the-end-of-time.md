All the emotions I have within
All come out with the stroke of my pen
The only feeling I have is love
There is no need to push and shove
The love belongs to only one girl
And to me she means the world
I'll say her name and say it loud
ANNIE! ANNIE! I say it proud
In my heart there is a fire
With one burning desire
That Annie knows my love is true
And that I can always be with you
For I love you more than words can say
I'll love you forever and a day
That is the meaning of this rhyme
I'll love you till the end of timepoem #3 for my love annie
