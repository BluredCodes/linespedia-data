You say you don't want to hurt him
You say that's the reason why
But if you would just give me a try
You are the one who brought it up
So I know deep down you want to
So if you would make this wish come true
I swear you won't regret it

You say you don't want to hurt him
That's your only reason why
But if you would just give me a try
Let's not call it a "fling"
Although we've had one once before
Let's stay together and make this so much more
How do you know I'm not better than him
How do you know we're not soul mates
How do you know its not our fate
How do you know we weren't meant to be
Let's get together and then we'll see

You say you don't want to hurt him
You say that's the reason why
But if you would just give me a try
You decided not to do it
It would hurt him way too much
But now I'm the one whose hurting
And you will be too
When you realize I was meant for you
In the end it'll be you and me

You say you don't want to hurt him
You say that's the reason why
But if you would just give me a tryI wrote this poem November 24, 2000. This poem is for this girl, Hillary, who was thinking about cheating on her boyfriend with me.
