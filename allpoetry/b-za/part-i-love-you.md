Let me tell you what I wanted to do
I wanted to write a poem for you
So I started to write
And I stayed up all night
I wanted it just right
But that proved to be a fight
Finding the right words is tough
So this poem is not good enough
None of the words will do
Not even "perfect" can describe you
I could list all the reasons I love you
But that would take forever to do
Describing your beauty would be a chore
I could always write more and more
I hope you get the message I'm sending
My love for you is never ending

This poem just will not do
So I have another for you...i know this is not that great, thats why i have another coming
