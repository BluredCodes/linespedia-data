Greatest Gift Of All 
      Copyright 2001. Scott James Used by Permission Only. All Rights Reserved 
                                     

                           If I was to explain love 
                         What words would I use? 
                         To describe what it means 
                         And I learned that love 
                      Doesn't have the ability to choose 
                          An age, gender or race 
                     It just hits one day with amazing grace 

                     Sometimes love can be right or wrong 
                    And sometimes love can leave you weak 
                   When other times love can make you strong 
                     But most often, love just causes a pain 
                      That lingers on, inside of our hearts 
                     Yet still we yearn, to fall in love again 

                        Love is the greatest gift of all 
                       It can make our spirits soar high 
                         It can catch us when we fall 
                        Let's rejoice, the voice of love 
                         And the mysteries it holds 
                     the power to make us run walk or crawl 
                  That's what makes love the greatest gift of all 

                 If it's so great, why is it the hardest word to say? 
                      Why when you give your all to me? 
                        Does it take my breath away? 
                     Don't go through life, denying yourself 
                          The chance to fall in love 
                         You'll regret for not trying 
                   Even if it fails, dont give up you will prevail 

                        Love is the greatest gift of all 
                       It can make our spirits soar high 
                         It can catch us when we fall 
                        Let's rejoice, the voice of love 
                         And the mysteries it holds 
                     the power to make us run walk or crawl 
                  That's what makes love the greatest gift of all 

              You'll know you're in love, when you look at each other 
         The feeling you will feel, you'll know why love was meant to be forever 

        Oh baby, don't you know, don't you know  (love is the greatest gift of all) 
               It can make our spirits soar high (soar high as the sky) 
                 It can catch us when we fall (lord knows we fall) 
                  Let's rejoice, the voice of love (sweet voice) 
                  And the mysteries it holds (it holds a mystery) 
               the power to make us run walk or crawl (it's in control) 
                   That's what makes love the greatest gift of all
