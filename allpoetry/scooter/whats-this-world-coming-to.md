What's This World Coming To?
Copyright 2002. Scott James. All Rights Reserved. Used By Permission Only

I turn on the TV
Only to see third world children dyin'
They don't have a drop of food to eat
It breaks my heart and I start cryin'
Then suddenly I realise that life can be unfair
But does anyone even care?

I turn the channel only to hear dozens are dead
Some emotional teen brought a gun to school
And shot them all in the head

What's this world coming to?
Where did we go wrong?
I keep hearing if you believe in love
You'll have the courage to carry on 
Was this really what God intended for us
When he created mankind?
Or would he say "I'm disappointed in you"
Tell me what's this world coming to?

No matter where you go
You can't escape the tragic news
Two men were beaten badly
Only because they were Jews
What about the boy that was tied to a fence
Back in the day, because he was gay

When we realise that black, white or blue
We're all the same in God's eyes
And that's the honest TRUTH!

What's this world coming to?
Where did we go wrong?
I keep hearing if you believe in love
You'll have the courage to carry on 
Was this really what God intended for us
When he created mankind?
Or would he say "I'm disappointed in you"
Tell me what's this world coming to?

Days like September eleventh
Are a constant wake up call
It taught that no matter how invincible we think we are
In a matter seconds we can fall

What's this world coming to?
Where did we go wrong?
I keep hearing if you believe in love
You'll have the courage to carry on 
Was this really what God intended for us
When he created mankind?
Or would he say "I'm disappointed in you"
Tell me what's this world coming to?
