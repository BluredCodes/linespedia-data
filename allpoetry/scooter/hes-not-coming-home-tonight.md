He's Not Coming Home Tonight
©2001 Scott James ® All Rights Reserved

Days have passed by the pain still not gone away
Keep hearing on the news, it's gonna be another one of those days
She goes on searching refusing to give up her fight
He's not coming home tonight

She sits by phone waiting for it to ring
The chance he'll walk through the door are now becoming very slim
She keeps telling herself that she'll be alright
He's not coming home tonight

She lies awake, wondering how much more she can take
Before the world she knows 
comes crashing down in front of her eyes
Still, she tries her best to be strong
But she can't deny, what she believes to be true
Her heart keeps telling her 
That he saw heavens' white glowing light
No, he's not coming tonight

She grabs a cup of coffee, sits in his favourite living room chair
She just won't admit it but inside she's so scared
She keeps hoping for the day she'll hold him again tight
He's not coming home tonight

Then suddenly she cries out a loud moan of pain
Across the bottom of the T.V screen, among those dead they flash his name
She begins to cry, it doesn't seem right
No, he's not coming home tonight

She lies awake, wondering how much more she can take
The world she knows 
Came crashing down in front of her eyes
Still, she tries her best to be strong
But she can't deny, what she knows is true
Her heart told her the truth 
When it said he saw heavens' white glowing light
No, he's not coming tonight

Still, she tries to be strong
But she can't deny, what she knows is true
Her heart told her the unfortunate truth
He's not coming home tonight
Oh no, he's not coming home tonight
