I've wrote you letters, 
But I can't send them
I've checked the phone book
But there's no address for heaven

I'd like to call you 
But there's no number
To hear your voice again
So I could remember

The day, that you left me suddenly
And how we're now worlds apart
Everytime I reminisce, 
There's an emptiness
Inside this broken heart

Looking back I often wonder
Why did this have to happen?
I get by knowing you're an angel
It's so enchanting
Silently sitting
Wiping away all the tears I've been crying
If I was to say everything was okay
I'd only be lying

But the day, that you left me suddenly
We became worlds apart
Everytime I reminisce, 
There's an emptiness
Inside this broken heart

But the day, that you left me suddenly
We became worlds apart
Everytime I reminisce, 
There's an emptiness
Inside this broken heart
