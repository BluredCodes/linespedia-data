Wherever You Are
Copyright 2001 Scott James/David Trott. All Rights Reserved

Loneliness, has taken my heart
It left me here all alone
It's tore my world apart
Now there's nothing left to see
But broken dreams and promises
A love that used to be
One I thought I would last forever
Between you and me

Wherever you are
I know I'll always remember
The love tha tyou and I made together
Wherever you are
In my heart is where you will be
Always a part of me
I'm just so afraid let go and fall
Wherever you are

And I, I can't get you off my mind
Baby you'll never begin to know
The hurt I'm feeling inside
You opened up my world
Then slammed the door shut
I can't believe you're gone
How quickly you moved on
Leavin' me far behind

Wherever you are
I know I'll always remember
The love that you and I made together
Wherever you are
In my heart is where you will be
Always a part of me
I'm just so afraid let go and fall
Wherever you are

I would give you my last breath of air
Call my name, baby I'll be right there
The hero who will take your fears away
I'll be the shoulder on which you can cry
But when I look into eyes
Don't be surprised if you hear me say
I'm still in love with you

Wherever you are (wherever you are)
I know I'll always remember (Baby I just can't surrender)
The love that you and I made together
Wherever you are
In my heart is where you will be (it's where you'll be baby)
Always a part of me
I'm just so afraid let go and fall
Wherever you are
I know I"ll always remember
I just can't surrender
Oh baby oh, wherever you are
