MOMENTS
©2001 Copyright Secure Scott James All Rights Reserved

Thinkin' of you, of you again tonight
The times we had together, the times I held you in my arms so tight
But now you're gone, and the moments become memories
Memories that slowly begin to fade 
 As time passes by, I can't deny

I long for the moments
The moments that came to a sudden end
I can't comprehend, so I close my eyes and pretend
This is a dream I'll wake up from
A moment that tore my heart apart
A moment I can’t believe came true
I'd give my world, to have you here with me
So I could share the moments, the moments of my life with you

With each day, each day that passes by
I’ll do my best to be strong, I will try to move on
Here I’ll be waiting while you explore your new endeavors
Soon the time will come when you and I will be
Together, now and forever

I long for the moments
The moments that came to a sudden end
I can't comprehend, so I close my eyes and pretend
This is a dream I'll wake up from
A moment that tore my heart apart
A moment I never thought would come true
I'd give my world to have you here with me
So I could share the moments, the moments of my life with you

I know you're looking down on me, from the heavens up above
Baby, please answer my prayers and send me your love


I  long for the moments
The moments that came to a sudden end 
I can't comprehend, so I close my eyes and pretend 
This is a dream I'll wake up from (a dream I'll wake up from)
A moment that tore my heart apart
A moment I never thought would come true ( I can't believe it came true)
I'd give my world, to have you here with me 
So I could share the moments, the moments of my life with you
