He's a six foot one, blonde hair blue-eyed poster guy
All the girls go crazy for him
Still I can’t figure out the reasons why
Sure he has the body
But, baby does he have the brains?
The fact still remains, 
He’s nothing more than a puppet on strings
Always being put on display
Seven days a week, twenty-four hours a day

No matter where you go it’s all about sexuality
You can run, but you can’t hide
From all the insecurities you’ve been holding back
Keep hearing people say to fit in
You can’t be overweight you have to be thin
But that’s just not reality
Why’s it all about sexuality

Popular magazines focus too much on looks
Pictures of Pamela Anderson Lee, or Britney Spears 
Grace the cover of Vogue
They’re not even real so what the hell is the deal?
Is this an incentive for us to run out and have plastic surgery?
Well today I'm making a stand and I'll say
That I'm not about to become a beauty queen
Just to impress society

No matter where you go it’s all about sexuality
You can run, but you can’t hide
From all the insecurities you’ve been holding back
Keep hearing people say to fit in
You can’t be overweight you have to be thin
But that’s just not reality
Why’s it all about sexuality

When you’re looking like that
It makes me really think
I’m glad I’m happy to be myself
‘Cuz I’d never want to look like that

No matter where you go it’s all about sexuality
You can run, but you can’t hide
From all the insecurities you’ve been holding back
Keep hearing people say to fit in
You can’t be overweight you have to be thin
But that’s just not reality
Why’s it all about sexuality
Sexuality
