With You Baby Is Where I Belong  
 
 Copyright 2001. All Rights Reserved. Used By Permission   
 
 
Verse One:
Sittin' by the phone, feeling so alone
Wishing that it would ring, to hear your voice once again
Tryin' not to think about you is harder then I thought it would be
The emptiness, I feel inside of this broken heart of mine
I can't sleep, can't this up for much longer baby
I need you here with me so I can make you see

Chorus:
With you baby is where I belong
I'm tryin' to be strong without you
It felt so right the night I held you in my arms so tight
And when I woke up by your side 
I looked deep into your eyes and I knew
With you baby is where I belong

Verse Two:
Gonna find a way to be with my baby again someday
I'll do whatever I can do, baby to prove my love to you
I'd fly a thousand miles, just to see the smile upon your face
I want to embrace, the happiness I feel whenever you're near
The day you walked away, I begged you to stay
But I knew you had to leave
Now I'm left hoping for the day when we'll be together again

With you baby is where I belong
I'm tryin' to be strong without you
It felt so right the night I held you in my arms so tight
And when I woke up by your side 
I looked deep into your eyes and I knew
With you baby is where I belong

Bridge:
Gotta be with him
Wanna know what he feels inside
I wonder if he still loves me
Or has time changed his mind

With you baby is where I belong
