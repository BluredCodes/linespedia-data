Copyright 1998. Scott James 

Another day passes by
Without you in my life
The memories I hold inside
Still bring tears to my eyes
I'll never forget, that cold winters day
When I woke up to find out, you were gone away

Like a dream, I thought could never come true
God proved me wrong,
When he sent the angels down for you
It broke my heart, I couldn't believe my eyes
You were Gone, you were gone
Before I had the chance to say good-bye

As the weeks, turn into months
The months turn into years
I'll face my greatest fears
And I'll cry a billion tears
I've learned that 
At times, life can be so damn unfair
It eases my pain to know
God welcomed you home with open arms

Like a dream, I thought could never come true
God proved me wrong,
When he sent the angels down for you
It broke my heart, I couldn't believe my eyes
You were gone, you were gone
Before I had the chance to say good-bye

I hear your name, and it brings back all the pain again
Still so hard to believe you're in heaven

Like a dream, I thought could never come true
God proved me wrong,
When he sent the angels down for you
It broke my heart, I couldn't believe my eyes
You were Gone, you were gone
Before I had the chance to say good-bye

God proved me wrong, 
When he sent the angels down for you 
It broke my heart, I couldn't believe my eyes 
You were Gone, you were gone 
Before I had the chance to say good-bye
