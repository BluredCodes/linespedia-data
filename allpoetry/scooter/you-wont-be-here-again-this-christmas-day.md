You Won't Be Here Again (This Christmas Day)  
 Copyright 2000. S.J.N All Rights Reserved. Used By Permission  
 
Another year is about to creep on by
Without you here where you should be
Surrounded by love, 
With your family by your side
It's been a tough year, 
But I've managed to make it through
Now it's Christmas time
And I can't get you off my mind

I'd trade every gift I had beneath the Christmas tree
Just to have you spend this special day with me
But no matter what I do or say
You won't be here again this Christmas Day

An empty chair
A missing gift with your name
From now on 
Christmas Day will never be the same
If only you were here, we'd cry some happy tears
Missing you hurts so much more this time of year

I'd trade every gift I had beneath the Christmas tree
Just to have you spend this special with me
But no matter what I do or say
You won't be here again this Christmas Day

If only I could go back in time
I'd try to convince God to change his mind
From taking you away
You won't be here again this Christmas Day

I'd trade every gift I had beneath the Christmas tree
Just to have you spend this special day with me
But no matter what I do or say
You won't be again this Christmas Day

But no matter what I do or say
You won't be here again this Christmas day
