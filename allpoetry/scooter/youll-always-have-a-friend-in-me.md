You'll Always Have A Friend In Me  
 
Copyright 2001. All Righs Reserved. Used By Permission Only  
 
 
Verse One:
When you're lost and so afraid
I'll be right there to help you find your way
And when you're feeling hurt and all alone
I'll be the light to brighten up your days
I will make all your tears, disappear
You have no reason left to fear
My task here won't be complete
Until I can make you believe

Chorus:
You'll always have a friend in me
Whenever you're in need
I will make you understand
How much you truly mean
And no matter how far apart
In my heart is where you'll forever be
Soon the day will come when you will see
That you'll always have a friend in me

Verse Two:
When the time comes for me to die
I'll be the angel
Who looks down upon you from heavens' sky
Unfair as you may think it seems
You'll see me again in your dreams
Just close your eyes but don't you cry
I may have left, but this doesn't mean good-bye
I know your heart is full of pain
But I promise you, that together we will be again
  
You'll always have a friend in me
Whenever you're in need
I will make you understand
How much you truly mean
And no matter how far apart
In my heart is where you'll forever be
Soon the day will come when you will see
That you'll always have a friend in me

Bridge:
And no matter night or day I'm just a heartbeat away
I'll be there to answer your prayers to show you I care
I'd walk through hell and back again
To cry your tears, to ease your pain
You don't have to think twice
I'll always be by your side
For the rest of your life

You'll always have a friend in me
Whenever you're in need
I will make you understand
How much you truly mean
And no matter how far apart
In my heart is where you'll forever be
Soon the day will come when you will see
That you'll always have a friend in me
