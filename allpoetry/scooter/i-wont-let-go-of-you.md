I won't let go of you
Copyrights 2001 All Rights Reserved Scott James

Never thought I'd see this day
Never thought I could ever feel this way
Feeling so hurt and alone
I still pinch myself to make sure it's real
Still waiting for my broken heart to heal
One day I know that it will
It feels like only yesterday I cried all these tears
But it's already been three long, lonely years

It still hurts
To think you won't be here anymore
There's still not a time 
I don't think of you as much as I did before
Is the pain supposed to last this long?
Or is it time to move on? 
Until I figure out what I should do
I won't let go of you

Last night I had dream about you
You said I had the strength to make it through
You were an angel with wings (angels with wings)
I told you how much I missed your smiling face
You said you couldn't be in a happier place
And how everything would be alright
I woke up suddenly, rubbed my eyes
Only to see you standing there
Smiled, then thanked God for answering all my prayers

It still hurts
To think you won't be here anymore
There's still not a time 
I don't think of you as much as I did before
Is the pain supposed to last this long?
Or is it time to move on? 
Until I figure out what I should do
I won't let go of you

Hopefully I'll see you again in my sleep
Another memory of you I'll forever keep

It still hurts
To think you won't be here anymore
There's still not a time 
I don't think of you as much as I did before
Is the pain supposed to last this long?
Or is it time to move on? 
Until I figure out what I should do
I won't let go of you
