Forever In My Heart
Copyright 2001. SJN. All Rights Reserved. Used By Permission 

                                          
Days go by without you
The hurt begins to fade
Time doesn't have the power to erase
The memories we have made
 I try to be strong without you
 Yet my heart feels the pain
 I can't believe you left me
 The second he called out your name

 And teardrops fall down from my eyes
 No amount of words could describe
 What I'm feeling inside for you
 A part of me died, a part I won't regain
 And no matter where life takes me to
It just won't be the same
The clouds have rolled on in
 My world has fallen apart
 I promise to keep you
Forever in my heart

Soon my eyes will shed their last tear
The emptiness I feel will disappear
 So many nights I've dreamed of
The times I shared with you here
 They now become just memories
Lingering in my mind
I may move on with my life
 But I'll never leave you behind

And teardrops fall down from my eyes
No amount of words could describe
What I'm feeling inside for you
 A part of me died, a part I won't regain
And no matter where life takes me to
It just won't be the same
The clouds have rolled on in
 My world has fallen apart
 I promise to keep you
 Forever in my heart

And there's just no looking back on what was
it's out of my hands, so in God I trust
 Spread your wings, fly through heavens' sky
And remember that I swore to never let you go
You're forever in my heart (in my heart)

And teardrops fall down from my eyes
No amount of words could describe
What I'm feeling inside for you
 A part of me died, a part I won't regain
And no matter where life takes me to
It just won't be the same
The clouds have rolled on in
 My world has fallen apart
 I promise to keep you
 Forever in my heart

Days go by without you
The hurt begins to fade...
