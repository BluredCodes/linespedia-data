Just Shut Up And Let's Kiss

 Verse One:
Another night spent with you
It's where I wanna be
And all I really want to do
Is hold you in my arms, til the break of dawn
It's where you belong
You're the strength that keeps me goin' strong
You're the reason I know my heart can't be wrong
So before what we feel is gone

Chorus:
Just shut up and let's kiss
Can't fight a temptation like this
Let's give in, time to begin baby
Just shut up and let's kiss
It's a feeling I know I can't resist
Something I wouldn't give up the world to miss
To feel your lips next to mine 
Just shut up and let's kiss
