Who I am Inside
Copyright 2000, All Rights Reserved Scott James


Why do I
Have to hide, who I am inside
Without a million looking down upon me
Everyday I pray, that today I can be myself
Unbelievably, this cold world forces me to play a certain part
Just to escape a broken heart

When will they finally see
That no matter what they say, this is me
When will they accept that this
Is who I am inside

If this is me, then tell me why am I
So afraid to stop living this lie
And stand up for what I believe in
I know one day, I'll find the words to explain
That through our differences
We are all the same 

When will they finally see
That no matter what they say, this is me
When will they accept that this
Is who I am inside
Just open up your eyes
Listen to me and maybe you'll realise
This was not a choice I chose
It's who I am inside

So I'll hold my head up high
I won't let what they say make me cry
And I'll believe in myself without needing a reason why
No longer will I conceal, who I am and how I feel
I'll show the world the secret me
I've hiden inside for so long

No more pretending that I am
Something I'm not for the rest of my life
Maybe now you'll realise, that I am who I am
See that this is who I am inside
