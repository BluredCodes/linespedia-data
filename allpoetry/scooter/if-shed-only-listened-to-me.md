Together, they walked through the airport
He said "Baby why do you have to go,
I have this feeling inside of me
I think you outta know"
She said I don't have time
I need to be on my way
Don't worry I promise everything will be okay

She gave him one final hug good-bye
Honey I love you, but I can't be late
She picked up her luggage
Blowing him kisses, as she ran through the terminal gate

He said If she'd only listened to me
Maybe she would have been able to see
By getting on that plane, 
 Life would never be the same
What more could I have done
To make her agree
When she's always been the stubborn one
If she'd only listened to me

As he watched the plane take off
A thousand feet his heart fell
Slowly he walked away
He knew all too well
She'd fly right to heaven
Away from this cold cruel world
Away from the love they would have forever shared

Driving home, he turned on the radio
When we heard a sad voice say
Flight 84 to Charlottetown
Had engine problems, and came crashing down

He burst out into tears
He screamed life is so damn unfair
God please take care of her
Until  I can make it there

If she'd only listened to me
Maybe she would have been able to see
By getting on that plane, 
  Life would never be the same
What more could I have done
To make her agree
When she's always been the stubborn one
If she'd only listened to me
If she'd only listened to me
