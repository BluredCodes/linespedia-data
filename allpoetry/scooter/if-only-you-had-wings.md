If Only You had Wings
Copyright 2001 Scott James All Rights Reserved

Lately I've been thinkin'
Bout the days you've been gone
Still here I sit, tryin' to be strong
Memories of you are what keep me holdin' on

I see your picture, it hangs upon the wall
I cry when I pass by 
it doesn't seem fair at all
I just want to scream, wish it was a dream

But I'm wide awake, that's how I know it's real
If only you knew, exactly how I feel

If only you had your wings.. 
you could fly home from heaven 
and I, I'd cry a million tears.. 
To see you once again
if only you had your wings

Have you ever wished, 
you could take my place
To embrace life like it was
, before you had to leave
Though it won't come true, 
it never hurts to make believe

We're so close, yet we're so far apart
Always together, forever in my heart

If only you had your wings.. 
you could fly home from heaven 
and I, I'd cry a million tears.. 
To see you once again
if only you had your wings

I know I'll see you again, i'm just not sure when
Years could pass us both by, until then

If only you had your wings.. 
you could fly home from heaven 
and I, I'd cry a million tears.. 
To see you once again
if only you had your wings
