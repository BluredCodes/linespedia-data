From The Moment That I Fell In Love With You
© Secured 2001 Scott James ® All Rights Reserved

Everyday that I wake up by your side
One glimpse into your eyes
And I just can't begin to explain
What I'm feeling inside for you
When we're together you make me complete
Baby, there's nothing for you I wouldn't do

From the moment that I fell in love with you
Without a doubt I knew
I'd be with you forever
No matter what we go through
You changed my life for the better
From the moment that I fell in love with you

There are so many times, times that I wonder
If you're an angel sent from above
'Coz I feel blessed, 
To be the one to have all your love
My heart belongs to you baby
That's the honest truth

From the moment that I fell in love with you
Without a doubt I knew
I'd be with you forever
No matter what we go through
You changed my life for the better
From the moment that I fell in love with you

I'd breathe my last breath just to show you I care
I can't conceal all the love, all the love I feel for you inside

From the moment that I fell in love with you
Without a doubt I knew
I'd be with you forever
No matter what we go through
You changed my life for the better
From the moment that I fell in love with you
