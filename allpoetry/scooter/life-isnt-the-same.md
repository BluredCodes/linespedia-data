LIFE ISN'T THE SAME
Copyright 1998. All Rights Reserved Scott James!

Walking down this long cold road
Where you lost your life that day
The hurt and pain I hold inside
Words could never begin to say
Just how I feel within my heart
Tryin' to pick up the pieces
But just not knowing where to start

Life isn't the same, no life isn't the same
Today I'll face my fears
Wipe away all of my tears
I'll accept that you're gone
And try to move on
But still, life isn't the same

Down at your final resting place
Next to your name, lies a picture of your face
The tears come pouring down, like falling rain
It brings back all of the pain
On January twenty-fourth  of Nineteen Ninty Eight
The angels took your soul
Through heaven's golden gates

Life isn't the same, no life isn't the same
Today I'll face my fears
Wipe away all of my tears
I'll accept that you're gone
And try to move on
But still, life isn't the same

Life isn't the same, no life isn't the same
Today I'll face my fears
Wipe away all of my tears
I'll accept that you're gone
And try to move on
But still, life isn't the same

No no life will never the same
