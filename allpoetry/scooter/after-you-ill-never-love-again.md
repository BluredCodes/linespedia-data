After You, I’ll Never Love Again 
                   ©2001 Scott James.  All Rights Reserved. 

                     The last word I heard was good-bye 
                 As you walked out the door, tears filled my eyes 
                            You were my life, 
                        Without you I feel I have died 
                          Oh baby, I need to know 
                        How do I get by without you? 

                     My heart knows it’s time to move on 
                   I can’t find the strength I need to be strong 
                         You left me here so weak, 
                          That I can barely speak 
                       Still wondering what I did wrong 

                        After you, I’ll never love again 
                          It’s not worth all the pain 
                              What if I try? 
                        And it only turns out the same 
                     I couldn’t bare another broken heart 
                    It would tear my world completely apart 
                     That’s why I made this vow to myself 

                       That after you, I’ll never love again 
                      There’s no body else I want more 
                   Everyday I pray, you’ll love me like before 
                         I know it won’t come true, 
                        But that’s what wishes are for 
                        After you, I’ll never love again 

                    A million lifetimes can’t make me forget 
                     I’ll always remember the day we met 
                         Though it didn’t last forever 
                           it’s better then never 
                       I had the chance to be with you 

                        After you, I’ll never love again 
                          It’s not worth all the pain 
                              What if I try? 
                        And it only turns out the same 
                     I couldn’t bare another broken heart 
                    It would tear my world completely apart 
                     That’s why I made this vow to myself 

                      That after you, I’ll never love again 
                      There’s no body else I want more 
                   Everyday I pray, you’ll love me like before 
                         I know it won’t come true, 
                        But that’s what wishes are for 
                        After you, I’ll never love again 

                        After you, I’ll never love again 
                          It’s not worth all the pain 
                              What if I try? 
                        And it only turns out the same 
                     I couldn’t bare another broken heart 
                    It would tear my world completely apart 
                     That’s why I made this vow to myself 

                      That after you, I’ll never love again 
                      There’s no body else I want more 
                   Everyday I pray, you’ll love me like before 
                         I know it won’t come true, 
                        But that’s what wishes are for 
                        After you, I’ll never love again
