Why did you do it 
Just leave me here waiting 
Wondering why it went wrong 
With no one to turn to for answers

Why 
How could you do it 
Just leave me so easily 
Destroy something so perfect and fragile
How could you seem to be so innocent but really on the inside 
Be lieing the whole time 

I dont like the world without you 
Its too big and scary 
To lonely without you by my side 
To sad without you here 

I want to feel your lips once agian
Pushing agianst mine
Like they always did

You told me one day it was over 
No answer why 
No feeling at all
How could you do it to me 

I feel so alone in this world 
I cant stop crying without your face 
I see a big blur 
I dont know what its supposed to be 
My end?

Metion how you loved me 
Metion anything from back then
And watch the weather change from grey to blue

I know you wont be coming back
I dont know why I even wait anymore
Your with him
You dont need me anymore
I am obsolete

No longer in use 
I will put myself there 
Into good use
Good for my heart and soul
Good for my being 

I cant stand to see him with you 
So I took a picture
I cant stand to see you sad like this 
So I kept a memory 
I cant stand to be without you 
So I finished what was started

The weather will never be back
As it slowly turns to black
I cant see what is happening 
But without you 
Whats to see 

From blue to grey
Grey to black
I slowly pine away 
Into the darkness 
To which I was thrown
Alone
