As I stand here alone
Breathing your aroma
Seeing your pictures slowly fade
My tear soaked hands become warm

We were so peacfully great 
No complications
No problems 
Not one 

Why did you think it was so right 
To do what you did
To make me suffer these consequences alone
How could you 

I cant do this alone
I need you 
I need to kiss you 
Hold you 
Feel the memory that haunts me now 
Touch the reality that once was

You cant come back to me now
All I can do is see you every winter 
Waiting 
For your avrival
Why dont you ever come

I stand here 
Above you
Crying 
Trying to find you 
Finding nothing but more memories 

Please help me 
I need your help
I need to feel your lips 

Come back to me 
Hold me 
Kiss me 
Feel this memory that is burning within me 
I cant do all of this alone

I cant do this 
This isnt reality 
It cant be 
I has to be a test

"Please come back"
I cry out 
In tears 

I know you are near 
I can see your title
Remaing 
Tauting me to do this 

As I stand here alone
I feel so betrayed 

And I take the razor from its ivory sheild
And I watch it as it slowly does its work
I cant bear this torture
Looking at your name 
Engraved in the stone
Knowing your a few feet away

I know that I am finally coming back to you 
I am going home
