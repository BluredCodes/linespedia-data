Am I just a pawn in your plan
Nothing more than just a mere toy
What do I mean to you 
Anything?

Why do you toss me around
Why do you treat me like this
You said I deserved better
I thought you meant yourself

Pain, Distress, Dismal, Depressed
Am I just a loser
Well here I am
Its all inside me

All the pain
All the torment 
Everything you said I should try and get passed
Well how do I pass you 
Tell me 

Tell me who to be 
Tell me what to do
Tell me how to ascend
Tell me how to live

These refelctions are fading
Everything is going black
Tell me now 

Please dont lie
Please dont act like I am a pawn
This isnt a game

Im sick and tired of being your little playtoy
But what can I do
I am of no importence

Why would you care if I died
Your game would be ended
But there are others for your game
You will just move on

So here is where it ends
So this is where my salvation is over

Well be that so
GAME OVER... 
YOU WIN
