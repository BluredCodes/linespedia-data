When you left me that day 
I had not a clue why
I was destroyed
I was killed

Not outside 
but inside
Now I should finish the job
Your no longer there for me

Your the love that walked away from me
It takes time to mend the wounds of all this suffering
Time i dont have
Look at me now 
Im miserable and weak

You left me 
And Im left wondering why
THeres nobody left
and Im left rotting in my head 
ALONE

Here i stand now that im alone
With no one to comfort me
One set of footprints in the sand
No one to take my hand

Memories in me 
cacooned in misery

The darkness overcomes 
Soul soars to the other plane
Existince past the door 
I sail through purgatorys bay

Receive me or cast me away
On the other side 
the time has come
lock and load
Im going home
