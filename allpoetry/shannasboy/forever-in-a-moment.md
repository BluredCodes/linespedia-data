title: "Forever In a Moment"

As cold as ice
Your lips touch mine
We stand alone here in this place of final life

Everything like it should be
Alone as it should be 
The winter wind pushes us together
So very warm

We stand in the snow
So white 
So pure
Untouched by any other
Just like our hearts

We have been waiting for this moment 
For so long
No longer waiting we embrace it

The cold hits us like water on a ship
And the tombstones tell us all of there secrets 
They tell us to love 
To kiss
To embrace this loving moment as if it we never 

We shouldnt have to drag ourselves
Out this far
Just to accomplish our dream
 
WE should stay right here
Motionless
In this moment forever
