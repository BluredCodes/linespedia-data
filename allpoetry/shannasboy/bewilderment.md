title: "Bewilderment"
writer: "ShannasBoy"

I love you 
I cant stand to be without you 
I need your essence close to mine
I need your lips pushing unto mine

Why did you leave me?
Was there a real reason?
I dont see what it could have been

After saying you loved me so much
After everything we had together
Everthing that was still to be
You left

How could you 
You pulled me out of everything I was
Then left me hanging by a thread
How did you do it so well
With out me having a clue

You have killed me 
You never have cared 
You never will 

You never loved me 
And you never will

Is this what you wanted
Is this what you truely had in mind
Because this is what you are getting 

A life devoted to hate 
Pain
Suicide
Killing 

My cold being was never meant to be 
I have lived this lie long enough
Its time to finish off what is already dead
Its time to kill the last of what could have never lived

Falling 
I reach out to embrace 
Whatever may come to be  
I wont be here much longer 

Its bewildering to me 
To just do this to the one you loved so much
It makes no sense
But what makes sense now that your gone

There is not one reason to stay here
Here in the cold 
Without anyone to comfort me 
Without anyone to be mine forever
Without you
