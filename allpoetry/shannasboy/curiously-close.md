---
title: "Curiously Close"
writer: "ShannasBoy"
slug: "curiously-close"

And as I turn to the end
I embrace what I might find
I kill what shouldnt have lived

I have come to close to the end
Beneath the holes you dug around my life
I fall
Defeated, I withdrawl

I may find it better here for me
I might find peace within all of this emptiness
How pitful can I be

And in my moment of truth
You speak to me
You tell me something more

You tell me you love me
I still listen to these lies 

You never loved me
I could never believe you now

And as I move closer to my eternal doom
As I put the razor to my wrist
You plead
You cry

Its to late 
You never cared for me
Only for him
I shoud have never listened

Now look at me 
Feeble and frail
No one to comfort me
Alone in this hole you dug

I was stupid
I let you touch me
I let your words spill into me
I let them pass right through the barriers
You brought out hope and reason within me
But it was all a lie

How could you do it 
Just lie to me so well
Make me fall in love with you 
Then throw me away
What did I do?

To suffer this fate 
The last one to suffer
The worst

You tell me not to
You say its not the right way

Your lieing
stop
Please 
I love you 
Why dont you notice this

I have come curiously close to the end
To close for comfort
I dont wanna die
Save me
Please

I have done the math 
Enough to know that this was a mistake
You were a mistake
I was a mistake
I should have never loved you

As I reach into the darkness 
Into the endless forever
The blackness I call home
I tell you one last time 
I love you
