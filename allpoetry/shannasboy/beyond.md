title: "Beyond"
writer: "ShannasBoy"
slug: "beyond"
source: "AllPoetry"

Cold and lifeless
I lay upon the ground
Wondering what comes next
Wondering why you did it

I dont understand
How you could just throw this all away
Watch me die and bury myself 
You dug the hole for me 
I fell right in

I dont get why you are so cold
Your heart tainted and worn
Your soul black and full of negitivity
Your eyes veiled by the false truth you portrayed
How could you do this?

I want to live agian
I want to live in your arms
Come back to me 
Awake me with a kiss
Pull me up within an embrace
Open my eyes with your loving heart

I didnt want to die
I wanted to go on
I wanted to live my life with you 
Knowing all I know now 
Its never goning to happen

I loved you 
How could you been so mean
To strip me of my life
To kill off your one true love
To cause suffering on the one you claimed as your own

I dont know why 
But I like it here in my hole 
I may find happiness here 
I may find some peace within all this emptiness

How pitful

I couldnt go on in denial
Please come to me 
Hold me 
Kiss me 
Touch me 
Feel me
Be mine forever 
Just like we planned 

I see you up there 
Crying 
saying your sorry for all you did
But what you caused was only the begining 
I freed myself
I felt the blood run down my arm
I know what it is like to die

Now its your turn
I see you up there in the snow 
Burying your face in your hands
Looking at your wrists in disgust
As you pick up the knife
With the white handle
I feel sorrow for you 
I feel sympathy even after you killed me 
I feel sorry for leaving you 

And in the last instant
You pause before you go
To see a butterfly land on your blade
And break it into pieces
You know what I am telling you 

Because even beyond the grave
Ill still say
I love you
