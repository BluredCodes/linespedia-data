Leaving this world was hard for us to do
But we both know it was for the best 
We know the reasons for what we did
All was right and just in our head

We both know that 
What we had was last chance 
No more 
It hurt to bad for it to turn out the same
We needed to escape all of the pain

You started liking another 
And when you broke the frist law of commitment 
I was killed 
You felt so guilty 

I ended it 
You said it would never happen
How can I trust you now 
Knowing all of this 

You asked me if we would ever be back together agian
I answered you short and simple 
No
Never 

It hurt to say those words to you but what had to be done was 
You suggested suicide 
I was more than willing to go 

You told me on the last day here 
To ride your bus home 
I did so 
When we got there
We kissed so deeply 
Knowing what was going to happen

I slowly picked up a knife 
You did the same 
We couldnt live without each other 
But we obvioulsy couldnt live with the pain either 

We slowly gave each other our wrist 
We looked into each others eyes
Liped I love you 
And said goodbye 

With that last final kiss 
The knifes did thier work
We embraced 
Holding each other so close
We kissed 
O so deeply 

You were the frist 
I looked into your eyes 
Said I love you 
You liped it back 
And your eyes slowly rolled back

I starting crying harder and harder 
I laid you down 
I took my place by your side 
Slowly touched your face as the blackness overcame me 

We did what we had to 
To be together 
No rules 
No outside feelings 
No restrictions 
Just pure love 

And now we lay in our eternal resting place 
Same confiding casket
Same stone to read 
Lips lightly touching 
Hand touching 
As it will always be 
In a place called forever
