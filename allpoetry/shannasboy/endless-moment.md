A moment is like mere seconds with you   
A never ending moment is what its like when you are not near
I loved what we had 
But I cant go on any longer 

You left me for him
Despite all the vows 
All the promises 
All the love 
You left me 

How did you do it?
Veil your lieing face
With truth
How did you trick me so well
How could you?

I knew my fears were childish
I knew they could have never come true
It seems I have been shrouded with lies all along

I didnt want to believe you that day 
I wanted you to walk back through that door into my arms
Tell me it was alright 
Kiss me lightly and tell me goodnight 
But believe what I may 
Your no longer here to protect me from the unthinkable threat 
The threat you bestowed upon me the day we frist kissed 

I should have never fell in love 
I should have never said I love you the way I did
I dont want to live a life without you 

You tell me I'll make it 
You say I'm strong 
Lieing agian

I cant go on without you 
I dont want to 
I have no will to carry on anymore 

The razor sits on my bed 
Yelling and shouting for me to slide it across my wrist
Screaming and fighting I try to maintain myself 

These tears are so cold 
Without love they cant be warm
Each tear reminding me of that night 
That keeps playing over and over in my head 
I cant stop this anymore 

A moment is endless without you 
I want to end all this 
I want to satisfy what has been yelling 
I need to finish what was started so long ago

Sitting here in my room
With the razor in hand 
I finally end 
This endless moment
