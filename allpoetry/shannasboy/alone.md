As the cold blood trickles down
The razor drops
I fall to my knees
Wondering what you are doing to me

I see the look in your eyes
As I slowly collapse
And fall to the bed of spikes

Its slowly getting dark
You smile at my pain
And laugh at the terror in my eyes

But somehow I feel in touch
Feeling everything around me
The suffering of the blade
All felt within me

As I slowly die
You walk away
I cry out to you 

I cry for you to save me
But you look back in shame

What did I do
WHat did I do to deserve this horrid fate
How could you just walk away

You were my light 
SO come back and light the darkness that has been bestowed
Come back and save me
Show some mercy 
For whatever I did

Please come back
Dont be the final touch of my death
Be the frist touch of my birth
As you once were
Before you killed me 
All alone
