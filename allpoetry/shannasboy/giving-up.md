---
title: "Giving Up"
writer: "ShannasBoy"
slug: "giving-up"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

While we stare at the sun and wonder
Wonder what happened to us 
Wonder what went wrong
I guess sometimes things dont go as planned

What we were was magnificiant 
Unmatched 
Unbeaten
We were the gods of each others lives

But something went astray
Something fell out of place
We know of it not
It just happened 

We undid what took so long to do
We finished what could have been great
We destroyed the love that bonded us

I cannot go on like this 
I can't stand to see you with him
You told me to wait for you but this is too long

Im giving up
Severing the entity that could have been a part of you 
I want to live my life with you 
But you dont want the same 
You want him
You dont care about me 
And thats unbearable

Thats a torture I cannot live through
Im leaving the prison you set up for me 

As the tears roll down my face
Stream unto my bloody wrists 
I put my hand onto the seat where you should be 

And as the moonlight slowly touches my blood filled fist
I say goodbye 
To the lie I once lived
