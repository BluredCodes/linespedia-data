title: "Heading Back Home"

Do you know what its like to see the face of your own stablity 
Suddnely look away
Turn its back on you and never turn around agian
I do

I also know what its like to be left empty with the dead and the hopeless


You dont seem to understand
Why dont you see that I love you 
How come you dont trust me

I have led you step by step
Inch by loaded memory
Shown you the glow you produce 
Fed you the reflections I hold so dear

You move me with a kiss
Open me within an embrace
Draw me to you with the promise of pleasure

Under this dead December sky 
I am and will be waiting 
Defending my love for you 

What is this but my own reflection
You have never cared 
Why dont you see I love you 

I have shown you all I know 
I have given it my all
Nothing seems to work
So I am headed back home

Seeing this place agian
Realizing I dont wanna be here
Its too late to be saved 

You left for him
You killed me 
I gave you my all

Staring down the hole agian
Holding the razor concealing my fate
Survival is no longer an option
And I'm terrified of what may come

Just remember I love you 
Even as I shed these tears of pain
I will always love you 

Hurt
Numb
And lost
I'm heading back home
