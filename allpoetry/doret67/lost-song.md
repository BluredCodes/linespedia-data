The fire burns, the thoughts run wild.
       A touch, a kiss remembered, fans the flame.
 The warmth endures, though dreams grow cold.
       Longing to hold you close, I breathe your name.
 
 
 Where lies the dream that once lived bright?
       Buried 'neath the shifting sands of fate.
 The dream survives, still buried deep,
       The wind to stir the sands, I still await.
 
 
 A song once sung will still be heard,
       As memories spin out the magic spell.
 The story told will live again,
       As songs unfold and weave the tune to tell.
