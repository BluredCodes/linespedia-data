You love me still , you say to me
 But is love what you feel?
 How do I know what game you play
 What part of you is real?

 You took my heart, gave me a plan
 Then took that plan away
 Was it a joke, or simply fate
 What is the plan today?

 Will you be true, be mine for sure
 Or will dreams fall to ruin?
 Will we be lovers for all time
 Or find an end too soon?

 I know that trust is what I need,
 But how to find that calm?
 My heart is torn, it needs to heal.
 Where is that healing balm?
