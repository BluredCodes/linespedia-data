If  I follow my heart will I find you
At the end of the long lonely road?
If I follow my heart could I bind you
With a love that will never grow cold?

Will my life take a turn to the sunlight?
Will I know that your love is my prize?
Dare I trust that this feeling is so right
When I find I am lost in your eyes?

If I follow my heart
If I follow my heart.

While the world with its anguish pursued me
And the rest of my life seemed forlorn.
Did the love that I seek now, elude me?
Or can memories still be reborn?

If I follow my heart will you leave me
Alone with my darkness and fears?
To lose you forever would grieve me
Far more than you'll know from my tears.

If I follow my heart
If I follow my heart

If I follow my heart will I find you
At the end of the long lonely road?
If I follow my heart could I bind you
With a love that will never grow cold?

If I follow my heart
If I follow my heart.....
