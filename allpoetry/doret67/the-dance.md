Dance with me. 

Hold me in your arms and sway with the music. 
Let the rythmn take over our souls as we move together in time. 

Dance with me. 

Hear the beat of the drum as it matches the beat 
Of my heart, and the tempo of our love rises and falls. 

Dance with me. 

The music sweeps over us and the ecstasy of the dancing 
Reaches the crescendo of the sound... 

Before the dance is ended.Written as a response to a challenge by Poetic Fanatic
