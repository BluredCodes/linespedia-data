---
title: "Love?"
writer: "Doret67"
slug: "love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/14400-Love--by-Doret67"
---

Duality, duplicity, lies told in darkness, burnished in the light.
Sweet sorrow, cold fear, 
Deep feeling, a warm tear.
Life shared, dreams broken, the sharing only skin deep.
My needs, your needs...who's counting?
But love won't die, even if
I doOriginally part of an add a line..
