Why cry?  The tears won't ease the pain.
Besides, the tears won't come.
I sit, and memories fill my heart like rain.
The rain is warm; my heart grows numb.

I love him still, but does he care?
I think perhaps he may.
He told some lies but life is fair
and true love comes to stay.A bit trite.. but still holds a truth for me nearly 40 years later.
