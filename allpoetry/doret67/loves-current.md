Softly into my life he stole,
A peaceful presence, gently felt.
As time passed by he touched my soul
And depths of frost began to melt.
At last a river flowing free,
Love's sweet current moved in me,
Love's warm sweet current moved in me.

Then changes came, a wandering eye?
I gave my love, what could I do?
A need in him I can't supply?
Perhaps the dream was never true.
The river now has found a bend.
Love's current cold, warmth at an end.
Love's warm sweet current found its end.
