Why?  I love you.
   Why?  I feel your touch.
Why?  I remember your lips.
   Why?  I hear the sound of your voice.
Why?  I feel the softness of your cheek.

The touch of your hair upon my hand, remains.

Why?  I love you.
    Why?  I feel your glance.
Why?  I want you near me.
    Why?  I feel the tears in my eyes.
Why?  My heart aches for your love,

And nothing matters to you.
