---
title: "I seem to be..... but I really am"
writer: "Angeleyes"
slug: "i-seem-to-be-but-i-really-am"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/20908-I-seem-to-be.....-but-I-really-am-by-Angeleyes"
---

I seem to be crazy.
But I really am searching for a way to hide how I really feel.
I seem to face reality
But I really am living in my world of illusion 
I seem to be in love 
But I really have no idea what love is
I seem to be looking into your eyes
But I really am far away, lost in my dreams
I seem to have peace of mind
But inside I really am at a battle with who I am, and who I want to be
I seem to have all the friends in the world
But I really am lonely
I seem to be happy 
But I really am searching for happiness
I seem to know where I am going
But really I am lost…..I wrote this for a creative writing class, just a spur of the moment thing, sorta ended up really expressin my feelings.
