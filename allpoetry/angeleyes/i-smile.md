---
title: "I Smile"
writer: "Angeleyes"

I smile, the disguise of tears.
As i wish to be free from a life of worries and insecurities,
Unable to escape this empty feeling
Caring about a world that doesn't care about me.
What is this feeling called happiness,is it real?
Or is it just another disguise in this world of illusions?
We all pretend to be something we're not,
Or never can be.
So i wear the mask of what my quest is for.
Happiness, strength, even inner peace,
As i hide behind my smile.
Someday i will find answers, or a meaning to life,
To the existence of my being.
Maybe through these answers i can also find happiness,
But until then all i can do is smile
