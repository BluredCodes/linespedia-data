I am afraid
That in the morning when I awaken
I will face my worst nightmare
That I will no longer be able to breathe the air of peace, which brought such comfort
But that of war and hatred which was forever brewing in our mist
From racism to terrorism
Who then shall we fear
God, man, or both?
Nations shall rise against nations
Will you turn against me too my friend?
Will you ignore my cries?
Delight in my pain
In a world where we live in fear
With nowhere to hide,
Nowhere to be safe.

As we turn to face man
We turn our backs on God
