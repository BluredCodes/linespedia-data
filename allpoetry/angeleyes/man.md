Well I can't blame you
After all you are male
So I have to pity you instead
Cause I have realised that u know no better
You "mature" looking for your perception
Of the famous word of illusion
"Perfection"
Which u wish u could achieve 
But u have one major fault
Testosterone.
Looking to fulfil your pants instead of your mind
.

34-28-32
Man's greatest fantasy
When will u realise that this form of "perfection"
is found only on the shelves of a Toys R Us store
So I wait for man's exception to the rule
With less testosterone
And more common sense.
To accept my less than perfect smile
.

Watch thou for the mutant
Who appears in many a disguise
Man is one.just any general comments
