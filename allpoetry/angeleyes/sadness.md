Sadness, to the depths of my soul, 
Tears which pour like rain, 
which you try to wipe away, 
but it's impossible 
Because these tears are for you, 
For I have fallen 
In a way which brings change, 
change we cannot accept nor understand. 
My eyes are clouded with your smile 
Your eyes light up in a way that I have never seen before 
Yet in a way which hurts me 
Because that light is not for me. 
There is no light for just a friend 
we're to close for lights 
since we have a "bond" 
A bond i would forget instantly if I could be with you 
Yet emotions I cannot reveal 
Because of....HER 
or is it because of me? 
I am too afraid to let you know 
That my heart cries out for your touch, 
that my lips beg for your kisses 
That you should be with me, 
Instead of the one who will never know how to love you
Only I know
Yet we are just friends 
So my soul cries...this is a poem i wrote to one of the challenges. It was great writing it.
