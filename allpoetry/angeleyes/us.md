---
title: "Us"
writer: "Angeleyes"
slug: "us"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

From that day we first met
Everything fell into place
We were meant to be
Friends, soul mates, 
Together, forever.
Two wandering hearts searching for comfort 
Which we found in each other,
Two souls searching for that special place to grow
In warmth, in love.
Gazing into those eyes you took me away
To a place of refuge, which I found deep inside
Where I created my illusion of security

Memories of the past becoming obstacles
To obstruct the path of a brighter future
Falling between stepping stones of faith
Yet not surrendering and falling into 
The waters of discouragement 
Encircling us,
Attempting to drown us in insecurities
Destined to tear us apart

But we held on to hope 
To that supernal bond which could never be broken 
Souls entwined, you have become the essence of me
Placed me on a pedestal, made me your queen 
In the castle of your heart.
We have created our definition of love
Which will never die
All because we never gave up
