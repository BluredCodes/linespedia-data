Those words unsaid
Which flow between the lines
The soft I miss yous 
Lost in the darkness.
The misunderstood words 
Which destroy the connection
Or reveal the lack of correlation
Between minds
Between souls.
Lost
In futile attempts to speak,
To capture the mind through poetic flow
In return for philosophical reply
As feelings remain hidden
Behind barriers of defence 
As I am left to wonder…
