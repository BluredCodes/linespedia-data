Hurt,
That's all I seem to feel now,
I no longer feel comfort in those arms
In which I once felt secure
Now there seems to be just coldness there.
As I slowly walk away 
I don't hear your voice calling me back,
Telling me you love me,
Asking me to stay.
It no longer feels the same,
You have changed, we have changed
And it hurts that there's nothing I can do about it,
But cry.

Loneliness,
That's what I feel
When I think about us and a future that will never be.
I no longer see love in your eyes,
I wonder what you're thinking
But I'm afraid to ask.
Part of my soul has left me
My heart now feels so empty 
Like it has no reason to go on
I am no longer happy
Because I have lost you.just general comments i guess
