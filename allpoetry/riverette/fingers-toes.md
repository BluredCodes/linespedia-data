---
title: "Fingers & Toes"
writer: "Riverette"

Ten little fingers and ten little toes
Two tiny ears and a small button nose
Now grows inside me, this bundle of joy
The only reminder left for me from that boy
He took away my innocence, made me feel so low
With no more self respect, only my belly to show
How can I deal with this? I'm only sixteen
I was gonna go to college, I had so many dreams
But now my life is ruined because of one mistake
Now I must quit school for my baby's sake
Other girls sleep around and never get caught
But I do it once and now my lesson has been taught
If I could only go back in time, I would fix it all
And know that I again fit in walking down school halls
And I promise, God, I'd make it better if I could do it again
I would do the right thing now, but I was so stupid then
I had my whole life ahead of me, now I'll soon be a mom
Won't get to graduate or find a date for the prom
So I ask all you girls out there to think with your heart
Don't fall into peer pressure and you'll remain smart
Don't think you're different from me, that it can't happen to you
With only a few minutes you can be in my shoes
With a baby to take care of all on your own
You'll lose your friends and family and be alone
So please listen to me and don't take off your clothes
Then all you'll have to worry about are your own fingers and toes
