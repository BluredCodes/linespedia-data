dedicated to Mark Hoppus

I know it seems like a fantasy
But I just want him to be with me
It could be great
But I'm too late
'Cause he's found someone new
As I gaze into his eyes
I kinda wish that some other guys
Could smile the same
Without his fame
And make me feel faint with just a look
But it'll never happen
Not to me anyway
I'm no trophey girl
He can put on display
No blond bombshell
To sit in the front row
With a lighter in hand
And air kisses I'd blow
But I'll still have my dreams
Where everything's perfect it seems
Where he'll give me a kiss
Giving my heart such sweet bliss
And sing to me with his sexy voice
And I'll meet him every night when the pillow hits my head
Wishing he is here with me in my cold bed
Then I'll fall asleep
And I will keep
Him close to me in my dreams
We'll hug and kiss
And reminisce
Only in my dreams
And I'll whisper in his ear
How I love him so dear
Only in my dreams
And he'll tell me
He does agree
Only in my dreams
Only in my dreams
Only in my dreams.....Sad really, but it's about my obsession for the bass player from blink 182
