---
title: "I need you"
writer: "Riverette"

You're my life, my everything
And I need you
But are you just lying
Or do you need me too?

I could make you so happy
If we were more than friends
My fantasy of us together
I don't want to end

When I tell you my secret
What will you do?
Will you kiss me
Or diss me
If only I knew

I need you
But do you need me?
Or am I just another girl
You toss away casually?

I left my heart open
But how could I have done so?
You just ripped it out
Made me feel so low

You're such a player
So selfish too
Then why, I ask myself,
Do I need you?
