---
title: "Web of Lies"
writer: "Riverette"
slug: "web-of-lies"

I pity you
For you've fallen in
To this web of lies
Your "friend" did spin

You only hear her words as true
You don't see what I see
Blinded by unending trust
I want to set you free

Is it the truth?
How can we ever know?
For her heart is now blackened
No emotion she'll show

I see her lie to your face
With no remorse at all
Trying to protect herself from our words
She builds an emotional brick wall

She says "I didn't do it"
But we all know it's untrue
You don't deserve her anymore
Her apologies are too long overdue

So I'd tell you to leave her
But all you'd do is disagree
And look into my eyes and say
"Well she's never lied to me"
