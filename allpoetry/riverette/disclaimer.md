Today I send you out
Into the real world
With a disclaimer on your chest
Note: I am a jerk just like the rest
Fall in love with me
I will break your heart
Cause I’m now a college boy
Only interested in you for my sex toy
Go out with me
But you’ll probably be used
Just letting you know
My flirting’s a show
To get into your pants
And then I’ll ditch you
Cause I’m a true player
My main goal to lay her
So this final warning:
Stay far from me
Cause the chick who wrote this
Almost died from my poisoned kiss
