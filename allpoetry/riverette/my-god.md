---
title: "My God"
writer: "Riverette"

Ancient scriptures
Words so wise
Yet the whole damn religion
Is shrouded in lies

A book of opinions
Many base their lives on
A book written thousands of years ago
Is now just a con

The "Good Book" they call it
Though to me it's mostly bad
Full of contradictions and deceit
These verses make me mad

How dare you tell me who to like
Who to hang out with, who to talk to
Gay people, girls who've had abortions
Are they not human to you?

God is very loving
Yet all Christians do is hate
People with different beliefs or morals
Against them you discriminate

Go to Church or go to Hell
My choises are quiet slim
But MY God is not judgemental
You'd believe me if you really knew him

Church is not a sacred place
Or one for quiet contemplation
But a scam for the trustworthy
A way to make money from adoration

To get you there on Sunday
They strike fear in your heart
Coughing up cash out of guilt
It happens to even the smart

But MY God doesn't care
What I think about Church
What I think about gays
What I think about abortion
MY God loves me for who I amJune 2000, What can I say? I'm a teenage punk who lives to question authority.
