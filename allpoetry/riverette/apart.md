---
title: "Apart"
writer: "Riverette"
slug: "apart"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/29109-Apart-by-Riverette"
---

I take her by the hand
And squeeze it very tight
I know that it is time
No longer can she fight

The tears start to come down
My pain begins to show
I feel her hand getting cold
Her spirit will now go

As she leaves me here alone
I sense an emptiness inside
Without her here to talk to
In who will I confide?

I go home from the hospital
Get dressed all in black
All the time being selfish
Wanting her to come back

But she is in a better place now
Of this I am sure
Where she can be with her mom and dad
A place so white and pure

Now every day the pain grows more
As I try to remember her face
That of a great beauty
Such a beauty time can never erase

But I know I can get through this
Even though that's not the way I feel
For death is just a part of life
For which everyone must deal

But on some lonely nights
When I know she's there I say
"I love you so much, Grandma,
And I'll be with you someday."
