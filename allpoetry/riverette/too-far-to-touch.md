---
title: "Too far to touch"
writer: "Riverette"
slug: "too-far-to-touch"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/29141-Too-far-to-touch-by-Riverette"

What can I do from here
To help you stop your tears?
You’re too far away
For me to touch
You don’t even know me
But I love you so much
You’re married and 30
And it’s really become dirty
Being a teenager
And chasing you
I’m retarded
You’re so cool
And the saddest fact of all
I could never give you a call
You’re such a sheltered celeb
And I’m among a million fans
Never be even the smallest part
Of your life long plans
But I’ll still be alive
Able to survive
With just the thought of you living
Always playing your bass
I’ll be able to go on
With a smile on my pointless, stupid, lonely face
