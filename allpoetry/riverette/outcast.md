---
title: "Outcast"
writer: "Riverette"
slug: "outcast"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

She sits in the corner
Concealed in darkness
The only mourner
At her own funeral

Crying these tears
With no shoulder to lean on
Her hopes and her fears
Bottled up inside

Bad or good
She gets her attention
I'd help her if I could
But is there anything I can do?

She is an outcast
Hated by all
If only I could erase her past
Give her a fresh start

All she needs is someone
Someone to love her
Someone to need her
Someone to listen to her

So for a short period of my day
I stop and chat with her
Soon she turns and looks my way
I think I can see her smile

I get strange looks as people pass
They point and coldly laugh
For all I care they can kiss my ass
I don't care at all what they think

She could be helped tomorrow
Because of what I said today
Now gone is all her sorrow
From a cordial greeting

Yesterday she was ready to die
But now she's pulled back from the edge
From a word such as "hi"
This girl has been saved
