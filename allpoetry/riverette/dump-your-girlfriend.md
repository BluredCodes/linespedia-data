---
title: "Dump your girlfriend"
writer: "Riverette"
slug: "dump-your-girlfriend"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/42940-Dump-your-girlfriend-by-Riverette"
---

Sometimes it makes me sick
To see you with that girl
She’s sweet, funny, and pretty too
Her smile makes me want to hurl
You think she’s the one for you
And I know it looks that way
But how can you be so sure
When I’m the one that’s here today
It was so important to you
That she come out and be here 
But now I am the only one
That will stand up and cheer
I see you glance around
To see if she will show
And my heart brakes for you
Cause that bitch, she really blows
But you love her still and brag to me
About how great she really is
How you’ve met that one special someone
And you want her to have your kids
Though you must be blind
Because I know you can’t see
How crappy she treats you
And how great you’d be with me
So here’s my simple solution
Dump her and I’ll ask you for a date
It’s such an easy line to think
But I can’t say it to your face
Cause I know somewhere deep inside
She makes you smile behind the tears
And if I caused you even an ounce of pain
I know I’d want to die for years
So even though it hurts me
I’ll have to ask this from you
Chose the girl that makes you happy
Cause I’ll be happy, alone, just loving you
