---
title: "With a Glance"
writer: "Riverette"
slug: "with-a-glance"
source: "AllPoetry"

It's been like this for far too long

With only one in my sights
Pulling me through these cold nights
Thinking of him evermore
Wishing to be the one he adores

It's been like this for far too long

So long that I don't remember
How it feels to have that ember
The ember caught when you fall in love
And the way you feel he fits you like a glove

It's been like this for far too long

And I don't think I'm ready
To again feel so unsteady
To put my heart back on the limb
And to feel the desire to kiss him on a whim

It's been like this for far too long

I loved him for six years so how can I love again
I just want it to be like it was then
When he didn't like me and it was all great
But now I like someone new who could be my mate

It's been like this for far too long

And I wonder if this other guy
Will like me too, or will he lie
Before it was better when I new I had no chance
But last night my world was changed
With just one glance
