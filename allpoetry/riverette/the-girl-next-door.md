title: "The Girl Next Door"
writer: "Riverette"

wish you could see it my way
I'd love to be in your shoes for a day
I'd throw my self at all the guys you get
And do some things that I'd regret
But you're not like me, you're such a tease
You don't care for their feelings, you do as you please
They kneel at your feet and kiss your toes
And buy you bouquets of carnation and rose
When they've fallen for you, you break the news
You don't like them like that, they were just used
As they sulk in the corner you slip in a short skirt
You'll find some new guys and with them you will flirt
Then when I finally like a guy he confides in me
He says with you is where he wants to be
It sucks to be the one who loves and never gets loved back
I feel like every good quality you have I somehow lack
Guys see you as everything they want and more
But they just see me, simply, as the girl next doorMy first song
