---
title: "I've Lost the Battle"
writer: "Riverette"
slug: "ive-lost-the-battle"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/29107-Ive-Lost-the-Battle-by-Riverette"

I pick up the knife
Wanting to die
Cutting the strings
So I can fly

Fly to a place
Where it won't hurt
Fly to a place
Where I won't feel like dirt

"A life without love
is a life not at all"
I will never see love
So tonight I shall fall

Alone I will wait
For my courage to come
As I think my last thoughts
My memory I draw from

I look deep inside
And reach for the good
But all I see is darkness
Cracks in the wood

In such deep depression
I try to stay strong
But with so little hope left
I can't wait very long

To give up or to fight
Which one will I choose?
To give up or to fight
Either way it seems I loose
