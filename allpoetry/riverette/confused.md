---
title: "Confused"
writer: "Riverette"

Love lasts forever
But I can only chase you so long
Soon it gets boring
And I feel loving you is wrong
I look into your eyes
And see there will never be an "us"
There will always be
Just you and me
And I just try not to make a fuss
I finally get over you
At least that's the way it seems
I'll have to wave goodbye to my heart
And bury my beautiful dreams
I hold my head high
As I look for someone new
Your image fades away
Beyond your face there is a view
What?
You liked me?
And you think you still might?
How dare you tell me this now!
You're feelings are not right!
There are others in my world now
No room for you
And if this is the truth
How could you be so untrue?
Untrue to me
Turning me down
When I shared with you my feelings
You just led me around
I can't deny
Though I really want to
That I still have these feelings
That I still love you
What should I do about it?
I can no longer get used
Or handle the rejection
I'm just so damn confused.....
