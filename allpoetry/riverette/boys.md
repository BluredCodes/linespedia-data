Think with your dick, you, selfish inconsiderate bastard
Because that’s all you’re good for anyway
I don’t need you or your childish make-me-cry mind games
Hurt me one more time, you don’t wanna see the price you’ll pay
Kiss my ass, bite me jerk, go straight to hell
And make sure to wipe your shoes on the way out
Leave my heart a sopping wet muddy-ass mess
Please don’t turn ‘round to see me fighting not to pout
I don’t want you to see me like this
I don’t want to be, like this
Tell me what to do
Tell me what to see
Tell me why you’re a dick to me
I don’t want to be associated with your kind
>From this moment on, you’re in my life no more
So pack your clothes and leave my life forever
Take all you shit, and go out my fucking door!
GOOD BYE
