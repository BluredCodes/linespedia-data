---
title: "Where I am"
writer: "Riverette"
slug: "where-i-am"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I've finally come to a place in my life
Where I can truly be happy without all the strife
I remain single, but it doesn't bother me at all
I stay on cloud nine hoping that I won't fall
I'm content with my friends, both girls and guys
And I don't have to hide behind a cheap disguise
The word "boyfriend" is just a title and nothing more
A guy friend with privileges, only wanting to score
But I just want to be friends, so that we can talk and smile
These relationships that I keep are actually worthwhile
When I'm with these guys I feel nothing but love
I feel as if I'm not there, watching us laugh from above
And my life feels so perfect, I want it to stay the same
Should I have any problems, I have only myself to blame
I pray day and night that this world is no sham
For I love my life now, right where I am
