---
title: "What I want, what to do?"
writer: "Riverette"
slug: "what-i-want-what-to-do"
source: "AllPoetry"

Jesus Christ
I don’t know what to do
Do I want to win
Or do I want to lose
Do I want it plain
Or with special sauce
Should I follow the path
Or do I want to be lost?
I can make it easy
But I kind of like it hard
A new and virgin canvas
Or one that’s filled with scars
Jesus Christ
I don’t know what I want
It could be so easy
But it’s the way you flaunt
Your great personality
And your gorgeous smile
Is it your sense of humor
Or your sense of style?
And sometimes I wonder
If at night you lie and dream
And wonder if I wonder
Could she ever like a guy like me?
