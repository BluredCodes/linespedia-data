---
title: "True Love"
writer: "Riverette"

He's the greatest love I've ever known
The sight of him makes my heart skip a beat
Thinking of him leaves me short of breath
I wonder "how can a guy be this sweet?"

My parents say it's not really love
My friends all don't believe me
But in my heart I know it to be true
To my soul he holds the key.

His sandy blond hair sways in the summer wind
His blue eyes glisten in the moonlight
Looking at him I know he's the one
I dream of him every single night.

How can I describe this love?
How can I express it to you?
Being with him makes me so happy
Without him I'm so blue.

I tell you now this love will last
My life I'm willing to bet
There's only one flaw to this great relationship
I haven't met him yet.This is my first poem.....not my best work
