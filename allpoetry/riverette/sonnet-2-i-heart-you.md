title: "Sonnet 2: I heart you"
writer: "Riverette"

Sitting and waiting for the snow to melt
Wanting so badly to be in her shoes
Wishing these feelings would never be felt
Waiting to hear from you, good or bad news
Four hours, twelve minutes, three seconds ago
I told you about how I had a crush
Fear dwells in my heart, you finally know
Next time I see you, can’t help but to blush
Your away message has been on for too long
I am feeling antsy, please come back soon
Deeming “why bother” as my new theme song
I sit and cry by the glow of the moon
But I don’t have hope, I know how you feel
And I should start right now, trying to heal
