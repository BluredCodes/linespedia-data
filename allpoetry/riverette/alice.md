Hello my name is Alice
And I like this boy you see
His name is Jimmy Alexander
And he's the one for me

Captain of the football team
All around gorgeous guy
When I dream about him day and night
I am forced to ask myself "why?"

Why don't I make a move
Or ask him for a date
Yes, I think I'll go for it
And then follow the course of fate

Now I must find the courage
Somewhere deep inside
I seem him in the hallway
But I want to run and hide

Here goes nothing
It could be my lucky day
As I take a deep breath
I think of what to say

"Hi Jimmy, It's me, Alice.
From Biology 101.
Would you like to maybe go out Saturday night?
I think we'd have some fun."

All of his friends turn to see
He's got a smirk on his face
Suddenly I feel cornered
I need some serious space!

Now my brain clicks on
In come clouds of doubt
Please, I don't want to hear the answer
I just want out!

My tears drowned out his shallow response
I turned and began to run
Then he shouted over the laughter
"Now this is what I call fun!"

So I plot revenge
In my room alone
All I see are shades of gray
Everyone speaking in a monotonous tone

Now I think I've got it!
I'll make you feel pain
Never again will you take a girl like me
And mark her as your gain.

So when the hurt is stabbing you
Like a thousand knives
Know this is what you've done
To so many innocent lives

Up on your pedestal
You will forever stay
But six feet underground
Is where I will eternally lay.Rejection can be cruel
