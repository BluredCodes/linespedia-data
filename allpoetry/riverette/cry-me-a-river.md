---
title: "Cry Me a River!"
writer: "Riverette"
slug: "cry-me-a-river"

So, you've got problems?
Well I've got 'em too!
Stop whining to me
I'm sick of it, screw you!

I just can't listen anymore
To the cries and complaints
Because I'm crying too
Though it may be faint

I try to help you out
But then you quickly go
Without healing my pain
Leaving me with only scars to show

So cry me a river
Build a bridge and get over it
For I will no longer listen
To your pointless shit

So stop talking to me!
I don't care a bit!
Just leave me the hell alone...
Never mind, I'm over it :)
