---
title: "Land of Make Believe"
writer: "Riverette"
slug: "land-of-make-believe"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/29124-Land-of-Make-Believe-by-Riverette"
---

I don't need to dream to see
This wonderful world in front of me
It's there for everyone although they may not know
Blinded by their own pity and woe
So they find a new way to open their eyes
They reach for some drugs and they improvise
It takes them away so they can finally find
This magical place, buried deep in their mind
They're always so happy, and I envy them for that
But I have no reason too, where they are, I'm at
They needed drugs while I got here alone
And I found this make believe land, all on my own
But there is a difference between these two lands
One discovered accidentally, the other one planned
And most importantly, their land is fake
Just a simple charade, make no mistake
My land is different and it is my ideal
Because my land of make believe is 100% real
