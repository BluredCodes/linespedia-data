---
title: "Loser"
writer: "Riverette"
slug: "loser"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I don’t know the difference
Between love and a simple flirt
I would ask you for a date
But I refuse to again get hurt
Everyone loves you
That’s including me
But why can’t, for once,
You just return the love to me
I’m sick of playing games
With this one sided die
I smile whenever you’re around
But then you leave and I soon cry
It sucks to be the one
Who never wins the game
I’ll always be the loser
Because I’m just so lame

What I need to find is a loser just like me
What I need to find is a loser just like me
What I need to find is that special guy
Who just can’t win
Cause he’s a loser
Just like me
