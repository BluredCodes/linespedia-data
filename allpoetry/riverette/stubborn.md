---
title: "Stubborn"
writer: "Riverette"

You cry and you cry
And say how hard it is
How he hurt you
How he left you
But the fault's not all his

I know that you're aching
But did you ever stop and think
That your words hurt him too
You helped break the link

Well get over it! He's your Daddy!
And life's just too short
Apologize for the family
You have my support

You can call him or write him
But no, you're too set in your ways
You must make amends, and do it quick
For short in number are your days

He's not getting any younger
And soon he will die
And then what would you do
If you couldn't say goodbye?
