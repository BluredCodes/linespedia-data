---
title: "Sell-out"
writer: "Riverette"

We’ve been playing the same punk music
Haven’t changed our style in years
Never ditched our punk attitude
Still chasing the chicks with blue hair
Just like hanging with my friends
And playing my guitar
Didn’t know it would cause such a stir
And go this far...
Sometimes I wonder
What was the price of my fame?
Loosing my fans
And feeling such shame
Why can’t they just accept
My video on MTV
And my song on the radio
But they’re not happy for me
It’s so lonely
At the top
When all your fans
Think you’re a flop
You should finally be happy
But all you can do is pout
Cause to all your former fans
You’re just a punk-pop sell-out
