I'm Daddy's girl
And he loves me
He says I'm the prettiest girl
On MTV

Daddy says I'm beautiful
And talented too
He says I'm really smart
I'm just better than you

Daddy says I've got talent
At singing, I'm the best
And Daddy says you should listen to me
And not glare at my chest

Daddy says I look great
Dancing on stage
Trying to exite my baby Justin
And thousands of boys not of age

Daddy says I'm innocent
A sweet southern bell
He says it's not my body, but my voice
That makes the records sell

Daddy says I'm just like the other girls
I just show a little more
He says you can't make fun of me
Or call me a whore

Daddy says I'm perfect
And I'm 100% real
You're just jealous
That you don't have my sex appealI admit it....I'm jealous of her
