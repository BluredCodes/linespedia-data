Can I share with you what is on my mind?
It’s been bugging me for a few weeks now
I need to tell you, but I’m not sure how
The way I see it you’re stupid or blind
She’s beauty, brains and talent all combined
She loves you, she’s already made that vow
Your feelings for that girl, I can’t allow
A better match for you, you’ll never find
As my good friend, I know she’ll treat you right
Because she worships the ground you walk on
And all she really wants is to be with you
She will dream of you every day and night
But make your move cause she’ll never act upon
These feelings she’s got, even though they're true
