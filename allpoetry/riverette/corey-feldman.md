**Corey Feldman**


I fell in love with you the first time I watched Stand by Me
You were the hottest child actor that I had ever seen
You were so sexy smoking by the fire, Teddy
You even showed us your tight ‘n white skivvies
Corey Feldman (oh oh)
Tonight will you be mine?
Corey Feldman (oh oh)
Your ass is so fine
I saw you in the Goonies,
co-starring with Rudy
And in Dream a Little Dream
For me you shook your booty
Corey Feldman (oh oh)
Tonight will you be mine?
Corey Feldman (oh oh)
Your ass is so fine
Nerf Herder wrote a song about you on their “How to Meet Girls” CD
They said they’d do anything, even roller-blade with you for me
But that’s the last thing on my mind when I see you baby, 
I want the two of us to star together in a porn movie
You could bring me my Christmas tree
And then you’d make sweet love to me
COREY!Yes, I am obsessed with Corey Feldman and I'll admit it!
