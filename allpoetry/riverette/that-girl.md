---
title: "That Girl"
writer: "Riverette"
slug: "that-girl"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I hear someone call my name
But I don't turn to see
Because I know they don't want me
They want the other girl
The one who is fun
And peppy
And makes them laugh
Not me
Her
Because if they knew the real me
They wouldn't call my name
They would give up
Because what's the use?
Talking to a girl like me
So somber and solemn
Who wants nothing more out of life
Nothing more
Than to live
I don't want to be happy
And smile
And make people laugh
I want a life worth living
So I don't always feel
So low
And worthless
So if you call my name
And I don't turn
Then you will know
Once and for all
That the girl you called for is dead.
