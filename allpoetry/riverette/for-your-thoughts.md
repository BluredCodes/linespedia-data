---
title: "For Your Thoughts"
writer: "Riverette"

A penny for your thoughts
And then maybe you'll share
Why you keep your head down
And cry with such great despair
I'll give you a nickel
Then you can tell me whom
Killed and locked you in
This premature tomb
Maybe for a dime
You'll decide to say
What problem's you've had
That have ruined your day
What about a quarter
Will you tell me then
Or will you just run away
Time and again
I'll give you a dollar
Ok, I'll pay five
Just tell me what I can do
To keep you alive
I'll give you a hundred bucks
Is that enough
Or will you just shrug and tell me
"Being a teenager is rough"
I'd give you all I own
My life savings and above
But now I've finally realize
All you want is love
