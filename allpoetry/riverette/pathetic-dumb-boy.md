---
title: "Pathetic Dumb Boy"
writer: "Riverette"
slug: "pathetic-dumb-boy"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/38831-Pathetic-Dumb-Boy-by-Riverette"
---

I wish you could see the scars on my face
But I cover them up with a layer of disgrace
Cause I can’t imagine my life without you
Making me feel the way that you do
But how can you change your mind so fast
I was your goddess one day in the past
Now I’m nothing more than a face in the hall
I reach for your hand but just get shoved to the wall
Your smile was so sweet; it made my heart melt
Like ice cream in July, each degree being felt
And I thought there was nothing I’ve ever wanted more
Than to be the one that you so adored
But I see now that you deserve to be alone
Waiting for a ring from your silent phone
But don’t count on me to give you a call
For you cut out my heart and watched me fall
Didn’t call a paramedic or dial 911
You just laughed with your friends and thought it was fun
So here’s my revenge, you pathetic dumb boy
You can watch me leave with my look of joy
It’s fake now, that’s true, but it won’t be for long
Cause you’ll soon weep when you see that I’m gone
