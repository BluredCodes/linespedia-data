Flagiciously
We are friends
We'll be together
Til the end

She'll pick me up
When I fall
She'll be there
To here my call

She is my mentor
My teacher, my friend
My jokes she'll laugh at
My wounds she'll mend

But most of all
She helps me through the bad
She listens to my problems
Makes me smile when I'm sad

I love her so much
It is no lie
I will always remember her
Til the day I die

One of my best friends
We are always together
And she'll be my Evil Twin
Until the end of forever!June 2000, for one of my dearest friends Emily
