title: "Still"

I still remember
In 5th grade when you moved here
I still remember
The way you drew my heart near
I still remember
How you made me believe in love at first sight
I still remember
How you made me feel all right
I still remember
Every word you ever said to me
I still remember
The way we used to disagree
I still remember
The way I loved to hear your voice
I still remember
That you were always my first choice
I still remember
How you looked so perfect in my eyes
I still remember
You were so different from all the other guys
I still remember
That you were my first true love
I still remember
You fit me like a glove
I still remember
You made my life have a clue
And after all these years
I still want to be with you
