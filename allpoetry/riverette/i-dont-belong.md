---
title: "I don't belong"
writer: "Riverette"
slug: "i-dont-belong"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/29135-I-dont-belong-by-Riverette"
---

Look into my eyes
And try to justify your lies
Because now I know the truth
For I've seen through your disguise
I feel so sick
Like I swallowed a brick
And there can be no more trust
After I have followed your tricks
It's over now
So go take a bow
Cause you've messed up
And broken the friendship vow
I can't trust again
You'll always lie about men
Let's just face the facts and see
It will never be like it was then
So goodbye and so long
No matter who's right or wrong
Cause your life and your future
Is a place I just don't belong
