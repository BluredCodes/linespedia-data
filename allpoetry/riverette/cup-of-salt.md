---
title: "Cup of Salt"
writer: "Riverette"
slug: "cup-of-salt"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/29136-Cup-of-Salt-by-Riverette"
---

Will I ever again feel the way
I felt when we first met?
I looked in your eyes
Slipped and fell
Hit my head
And I’ve never felt better
Every scrape and cut
I received
Felt good
Because you gave it to me
But as soon as you left
Those sores you gave me
They ached
And  I needed you
To dress my wounds
But by then it was too late
You had moved on
To another girl
And injured her
While my blood still dripped
Now who will pick me up?
A suffering girl
Bloody
On the side of the road
Not you, or any other
But you will always be there
To lend me
A cup of salt for my wounds
