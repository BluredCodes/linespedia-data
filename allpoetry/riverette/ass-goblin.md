He hurt her then
He'll hurt her once more
All he wants from her
Is to score

He'll treat her like dirt
While he screws another chick
Never thinking with his heart
But thinking with his dick

After a month of this crap
She will again dump him
But then will soon after
Return on a whim

My shoulder she'll come to
When she needs to cry
I'd do anything for her
For her I'd even die

She's my best friend in the world
But this guy is just trash
To this world
He's an infectious rash

I just want the best for her
Why can he not see
What a wonderful person she is
When it's so obvious to me
