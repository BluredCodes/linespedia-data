title: "Waiting"

It started with your smile
The love I had for you
And when I looked into your eyes
I thought you might love me too

It grew with the scent
Of your sweet cologne
You made me feel
Like I wasn't all alone

I told you about it
And scared you away
I cursed myself for months
For making you stray

I tried to ignore it
And call you a jerk
But the feelings, they stayed
The insults didn't work

I kept it a secret
When you found someone new
We didn't talk to each other at all
And I really missed you

It started to go away
When I found you wouldn't love me back
I tried to figure out what it was
What qualities I did lack

Then, my love for you, it died
The pain had finally come to an end
And where I once saw my love
I now see a best friend

Now my love is up for grabs
As I sit patiently
Waiting for the one guy
Who will love me for me
