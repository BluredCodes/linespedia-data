I dragged the blade over my veins,
hoping to spill my venomous blood.
Why do I mutilate myself?
Will I ever find out?
It hurts to think,
I have no place to go.
Since you've slammed the door into my face
aching my soul.
But searching on my own,
I have found my true meaning.
Silently I crossed over
into the light.
Realizing that my pain,
can't be expressed in such self damaging behavior.
And with a smile on my face,
