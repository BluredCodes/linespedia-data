The rose captured her thoughts
and emotions,
bringing out the best;
Locking away her tears of sorrow
forgetting all the rest.

Now when she sees the red,
it strikes her as her capture;
It took away all her security
left her in the open.

More things hurt her now than ever.
Always feeling alone.
So never again
shall the rose be a friend.
But the lover who stole her thoughts.This one I made after my friend drew a picture of a rose with a childs bruised, crying face inside. It made me feel sad. I hope you like it. If anything needs changed. Please tell me!
