---
title: "Alone To Murder"
writer: "Barbii"
slug: "alone-to-murder"

Alone on a planet
no one could see her die;
I took this chance 
to watch her beg for her life.
Sticking long needles into her skin
watching this beauty flinch;
Ripped open her soft fingers
from the tip;
Bathed her in her own blood,
watching it run,
being driven mad
couldn't see anyhing so sad,
being forced to die,
alone with a murderer by her side
a person could laugh
with such a pathetic unneeded glee;
Turning white,
from the loss of blood;
Oh no! She's dead!
Oh well, next please;
