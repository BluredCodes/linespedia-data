They sacrafice our feelings,
to make themselves feel good.
Hate and pain surge inside
to bring us o today;

Darkness covers our bleeding hearts;
And burns inside our souls.
Trash and evil lives inside
and revolves around our hate.

No one treasures the sweet, sweet suffering;
Bringing themselves to an end;
Feeling their pain inside 
as they drag the blade
across their veins;
Hoping to spill the blood,
in which is full of venom 
that lashes out to their tongues.This is one of my first poems ever written. So please comment.
