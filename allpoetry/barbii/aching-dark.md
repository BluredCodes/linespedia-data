---
title: "Aching Dark"
writer: "Barbii"
slug: "aching-dark"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/32325-Aching-Dark-by-Barbii"
---

I wish upon a lonely night;
and pray for the darkness
the darkness that absorbs my heart
and pains my soul;

Never will the light brighten my fright,
for the screams that seep into my nights;

Taking away the pride I need;
Killing me,
till nothings left
not even a breath,
for the darkness has breathed life
upon my death;

People celebrate the way I ache,
and I celebrate the way they'll die,
for causing the pain that lives inside;
