Imagine, 
just picture your brain becoming 
a world.
How will your mind look?
Would there be a scenery?
Or just those black pits.

Look down my fields,
see the gray?
Just like my life, between the lines.
Did you find the graves?
Where I locked away
my emotions and happiness.
Maybe you saw the line,
all the way up the cliff?
That was me, all six hundred,
killing myself over and over!
Just look!
Please remember.
Because soon enough,
the smell of burning flesh
will prove 
your now in my hell.Please tell me how this sounds. As adults you might not like it. But most teens do feel these ways.
