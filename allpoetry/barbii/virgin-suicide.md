writer: "Barbii"

A girl,
a girl as perfect as can be
secrets,
secrets she holds inside.
Oh why?
Why must she hide behind,
hide behind a scattered world,
full of wicked lies.

People can trick her,
her pain is unknown.
Unknown to the rest of the world who hates her so.
Surely,
surely if you must behold,
behold before you,
she suffers,
suffers coldly,
coldly suffers;
Do you need an explanation?
Explanation over why she lies awake;
Lies awake and thinks of you,
you and the torment you put her throught!
Maybe one day,
one day maybe,
you could leave her alone,
to live!I need imput people! Tell me how it is!
