No one understood exactly why,
she hid beyond her mind;
Locking the key to her emotions;
Allowing only hate to leak through
from the door to her heart and soul,
conquering it all;

Peace no longer lives inside;
Hate sometimes deserves to cry
Full of fury,
anger and despise;
The girl goes to her room alone;
Alone the world sees her as a challenge;

Knocking down her only pride,
making it hurt inside;

Her special tears to cry alone,
never near or by a soul;
All play to say,
they understand her pain;
But never will they know of her silent
screams,
