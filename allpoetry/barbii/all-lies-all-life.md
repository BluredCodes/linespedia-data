---
title: "All Lies All Life"
writer: "Barbii"
slug: "all-lies-all-life"
source: "AllPoetry"

I hate my life.
The way I live.
It was a choice,
a choice I did.
My stupid lies,
The lies I said.
Made my life
so hard to live.
