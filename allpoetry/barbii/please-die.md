---
title: "Please Die"
writer: "Barbii"

How would you feel 
if I asked you to die,
never to yell;

Amongst the rest,
stop thinking your the best;
Take a deep breath,
now...
picture yourself rotting in a grave,
no hair left and very little flesh
maggots and worms crawl in and
out of your eyes;

Sound fun?
Then lucky for you,
you'll be there soon.
