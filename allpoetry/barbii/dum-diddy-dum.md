Dum, dum, doo, dum
I like to suck my thumb.
Dum, dum, doo, doo, dum
looking at you makes me want to throw poo.
Yum, pum, clum;
My eyes were shot with cum (hehe).
Zoom, toom, shoom
my girlfriend went into full bloom,
Shit, fuck, rotting-like truck;
he haw who he wah eh;This was my frist poem written as a joke. Please enjoy!
