---
title: "Weapons"
writer: "Barbii"

Everything today's a weapon.
No matter what you're seeing.
They say no weapons in school
but then they allow us 
scissors pencils and pens.
Don't they know,
if a child's ready to blow
he could hurt his classmates,
make them beg for their lives,
while he stabs pens in their eyes?
