he said that we won't last forever 
so i wonder... should we even be together?
everytime i spend time with him my heart just dies
what is love in a mans eyes?????
he told me he loved me before
he changed his mind,and i don't know if i should walk out the door
we used to be so close until that one night
that night we became so intanint, i don't know if it was right
now all we do is arguee of stupid shit
and i still wonder... is the flame between us still lit
it took me over a year to get him back
and now all he's doing is throwing my life off track
i can say i love him
because i know it's the truth
well i guess this is the end of my story and we are still together trying to work things out
