he says he loves me
but is it true
how are you supposed to believe
thoes heart stopping words i love you
i've been hurt to many times
it's hard to believe, in love is it a crime
to honestly think you've had enough 
he says he's blinded by my beauty
that i am perfect in every way
but when i look in the mirrior all i see is me
why am i so afraid 
he says he's the luckiest guy in th eworld 
to have me as his girlfriend 
like that's something i havn't heard
everyother guy never loved me in the end
he says he's not like other guys
that sounds so fimilar......i wounder why!
