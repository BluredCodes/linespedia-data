today is the final day
the ending of the perfect begining
my hearts screaming for a delay 
but the begining keeps on ending
i startr to cry 
but for what perpose
because i have to say good-bye
to all my friends i'm going to miss
we finally made it
and were proud to walk away as freshmen
just something doesn't seem to fit
is this the way things should've been
i stand strong as they call my name
and smile as they cheer
things will never be the same
a new begining starts for us next year
i can't stop myself from crying
because i can't figure out if this is the ending of the perfect begining
or is it the begining of the perfct ending
