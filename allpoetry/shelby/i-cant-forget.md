i try to forget that night
but how can i forget what i know in my heart is true
your the first thing i think about each morning and the last thing each night
how am i supposed to forget whan i can't forget i love you
how can i forget the first time we kissed 
or our first dance
how can i forget all the memories i miss
oh, what i wouln't give for a second chance
i'm not saying i don't want to forget half the time
i wish i could forget all the times i woke up alone
all the times i spent crying
or cursing on the phone
i can only take so much 
all the pain i've had to endure
just wheni think i've had enough i end up having to deal with more
i'm not saying it was always rough
that we were always having firghts
but i know when i've had enough
and i still havn't had enough that night
