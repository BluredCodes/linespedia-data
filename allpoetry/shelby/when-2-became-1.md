---
title: "when 2 became 1"
writer: "shelby"
slug: "when-2-became-1"
source: "AllPoetry"

when 2 became 1
you said we could be friends
when 2 became 1
i had to start all over again
when 2 became 1
you made lies about me
when 2 became 1
you suddenly became my enemy
when 2 became 1
you never treated me the same
when 2 became 1
you making me cry started to be a game
when 2 became 1 
i wondered how to make it 2 again
when 2 became 1
i couldn't even make you my friend
