i have all these thoughts running through my head
all the good times we had
but you want some other girl instead
and i feel stupid for feeling sad
i keep thinking if i wouln't have pushed you so hard
and just loved you a little more
show you all my feelings i've been hiding
it would make you forget about the other girls before
if you didn't care about me then why'd you even bother
i wish i could make you happy
but i guess the only man safe enough to love is my father
what did i do to deserve this 
all i ever did was love
i wish i could trad in this broken heart for one more kiss
and teel you once more i love you
