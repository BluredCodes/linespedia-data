i miss you calling
i miss the sound of your voice
when you said it was over i started balling 
everything in my heart  was destroyed 
everybody trys to  make me feel betterv 
and say i'm to good for you
they say i don't deserve to hurt this way
and i need to get over you
it's too hard to  forget
we had alot of happy memories
but it's hard to get to the next step
when your in love so badly
they tell me one day you'll come back
but we both know that's not true
i need to get on the right track
and say good-bye to you
