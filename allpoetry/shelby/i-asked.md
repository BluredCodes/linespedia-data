---
title: "i asked"
writer: "shelby"

i asked for a dream came true 
and you gave me broken promises
i asked for a real man and i found you
i became full of empty wishes
you built up my hope just to let me down 
i fell inside your heart 
and you didn't help me as i started to drown
i wanted a knight, but you wouldn't play the part
i wanted you to love me
no one else
i wanted you to help me see  
but your heart belongs to someone else           
i wanted you to look into my soul 
but you were blinded
and too busy playing me for a fool
now it's to hard to get behind it
i want a true story 
but you were to caught up in lies 
i want you to adore me
but i'm not even gonna try
