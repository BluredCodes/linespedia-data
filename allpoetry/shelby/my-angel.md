it started to rain and storm
but as lucky as i am an angel was born
when i was falling into the sky
he held out his hand and told me to fly
he saved me from my dangers of hell
but who is this stranger that saved me before i fell
his face a marvalous glow
his eyes a mystick twinkle
i don't understand your world below
then the rain started to sprinkle
why do people do such stupid things
how are you expected to fly when you have no wings
you have unbelieveable problems i'm sure
horrible diseases that have no cures
but why you want to end your life
because of your problkems and unending strifes
i could see his heart was like a childs
because he never missed an oppertunity to smile
i might not be able to save you again
so don't end your life when your head starts to spin
if you just believe
he will help your goals to be achived
you will all be judged
and your hearts will have to testify
just because you make a smudge 
doesn't mean you can't try
he will help you when it starts to rain
he will end your problems and treat your pains
i wanted to throw it all away
but this angel wanted me to stay
who was this remarkable man????
instead of falling he helped me to land
now i wake up every morning with a picture in my head
of this strangers face
if it wasn't for him i'd be dead
it was like i had a whole in my heart
and he filled the empty space
he saved me before i died
and for that i owe my life
he taught me that my feelings don't have to hide
and because my hero was so brave
he had the courage to help me be savedthis peom is about someone who wanted to kill themselfs b\cuz their life was to hard but god helped save that person
