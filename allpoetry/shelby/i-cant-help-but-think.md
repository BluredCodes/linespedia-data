i see you lok at me
and i can't help but think
what is it you see 
everytime i see you blink
hoping somewhere in your heart
you feel the same way about you that i do about you 
hoping your feelings for me are as strong 
as my feelings for you
if only you could see what i see
and feel what i feel
then maybe i could make you love me
without question of eye appeal
i made a wish for you 
and i thought it would never be
but i guess my wish came true 
because somehow you came to love me
no one wiil ever understand
why i do the things i do
and i will never understand
why i love you the way i do
