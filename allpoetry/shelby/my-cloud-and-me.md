---
title: "my cloud and me"
writer: "shelby"
slug: "my-cloud-and-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/34215-my-cloud-and-me-by-shelby"
---

they say every cloud has a silver lining
exspecialy to a mind and body tired of fighting
that's where i long to be
up in a cloud
only me
watching the people down below
rushing around as they go
i'm happy up here all alone
in my fluffy spot, no where to roam
i have everything i'll ever need
my cloud and me
