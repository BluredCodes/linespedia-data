when we first started out
neither one of us thought we would make it
we broke away and never thought of a  2nd chance
something told us we didn't fit
we both got stuck in someone elses trance
we wern't fair to our hearts 
because ther was still something there
something inside us from the very statr
how could we have been so unfair
here i wsa longing for love
and i found it, but not from the person i was thinking of
all this time i was waiting 
and not noticing the truth 
the whole time i was sitting here 
i never relized that i loved you 
i tried not to see your flirting
and i tried not to care 
but deep inside it was still hurting 
knowing you weren't there
we're finally together again
and now i don't have to be scared to say i love you 
i'll always remember when
you said i love you too!!!!this poem is to my boyfriend b/cause we have been together off and on for 3 years
