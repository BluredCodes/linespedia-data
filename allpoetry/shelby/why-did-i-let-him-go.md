---
title: "why did i let him go????"
writer: "shelby"

i sit in my room thinking
why i made that mistake
and when i left 
your heart is what i had to take
i thought i loved you so much
but something told me my love for you was a fake
now i want you back
but you don't want me
as life goes on i try to get my life back on track
but without you life goes by so slow
and i wounder ..........
why did i let him go???????/
