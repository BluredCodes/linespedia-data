why do parents tell their chindren fairy tales? is it some hurtful way to get their hopes up? fairy tales are about true love and dreams come true so you ecpect things to go good for you. but in reality who's going to have to tell that innocent little girl that prince charming is really an asshole in disguise? And do you want to be the one to tell that poor lonely boy that theirs no christmas this year because daddy's been laid off? truth is theirs nothing wrong with the children it's the parents who fuck their kids heads up.see kids are vonarable they think everything their parents tell them has to be right. but one day when your son or daughter walks up to you  asking nothing but the question of why???? what are you going to tell them? to just have faith and keep on believing beacuse things will only get better? do you think he or she will be willing to forgive and try to undrestand what you did after their hopes and dreams come crashing down to the depths of reality? will they come out stronger?
