just when i thought you didn't love me
i saw the twinkle in your eye
and in that moment of magic you came to me
and promised me never again would you lie 
you stole a peice of my heart 
and even though you never said it 
i think i stole a peice of yours too
even if it was only a part 
i knew inside it was true 
you gained my trust 
and became my dreams 
you were more then enough 
because you came to love me
