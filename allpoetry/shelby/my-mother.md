---
title: "my mother"
writer: "shelby"
slug: "my-mother"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

you gave me a begining
and a deeper meaning in life
you told me stories with happy endings
you always taught me to do what's right
you gave me your eyes, your charm, your smile
you gave me guidence and diciplin when i needed it
you made me strong and still so fradgle
you gave me spunk and that clever wit
whenever i'm sad it
s hard for you not to know
because i change my smile into a grin
it's hard not to have my true feelings show
to keep everything bottled in
she may not do alot of things right
but she always comes in my room to kiss me good-night
and most important of all she loves me
a greater love then a sister to a brother
the strenght of a woman who helped make me
a woman who i can only call mother
