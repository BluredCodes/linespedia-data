you said you loved me, i said it too
i should have known it wasn't true 
you lied and played me for a fool
i didn't know love could be so cruel
you cheated me , broke my heart
and that's the reason we fell apart
so many nights i would cry
when i thought of you and i 
you said our love would never die
but you were wring fairwell good- bye
