---
title: "only if i could"
writer: "shelby"

how do you save a broken heart
a blurry vision of a dream
how do you save it from falling apart
and making everything as it seems
a lonely heart with a passion to love
a haert that's been through agony and pain
that through all the hurt maneged to somehow reach above and make everything right again
how do you learn to love after your hearts been broken
how do you stop crying
how do you make your words outspoken
and your heart undying
do you ever get used to being hurt
if it happens so many times?
is the time with that person worth it 
does it make you sublime?
how do you know when to give up
or even if you should
i never gave up
only if i could
