were all supposed to be diffrent and we should never feel out of place
it shouldn't matter the color of our skin or the detail of our face
why are people discriminated by the color of their hair
or the expression of the clothes they wear
the biggest problem our world will ever face
are the problems of haterd of human race
wether you are a blond or have brown hair
wether your eyes are blue or green you shouldn't have to care
don't ever think that your not good enough
because life isn't always this rough
it doesn't matter what style you like
as long as you do things in your own way
because no one's supposed to be alike
everyone has their own role to play
it may seem like your problems will never end
and that you don't have a single friend
but to me, everyone should be trated the same
blacks, whites, and even the lame
you were made to be whoever you want to be
and you'll always be a friend to me
