title: "he loves me not"
writer: "shelby"

we were together, but you broke it apart
and in the process you broke my heart
wew were as one but you changed it to two
i lost all hope in my dreams that were all brand new
wew were a couple but you chattered my dreams
all of our plans were not as they seemed
i tried to get in, but you pushed me away 
and now we never talk as each day passes by
and now i wish you would still love me
but that's a useless dream
so please give me back my heart
it means alot to me

As i lay in a bed of flowers 
i think of you and all loves powers
i pick a flower and pull a petal
he loves me
i'm overloaded with joy
he loves me not
you have no idea how much i love you boy
he loves me
being with you has to be right 
he loves me not
i know i'll dream of you tonight
he loves me
as i pull the last petal, all my hopes are shot
it falls limp to the ground
he loves me not
