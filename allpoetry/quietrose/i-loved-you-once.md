I Loved You Once


I hate you for the enduring pain
like the dreariness of a never-ending rain
I hate you for the darkness
like the thunderstorms that never rest
I hate you for the sorrow inside
like the blue moon that tends to hide
I hate you for the constant ache
like the hurricane that will never break
I hate you for tearing my world apart
like the avalanche that will never part
I hate you for the shattered dream
like the wind with its piercing scream
I hate you for the constant anger
like the lightening bolts with all its danger
I hate you for my unhappiness today
But I loved you once on a sunny day
