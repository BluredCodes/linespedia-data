Today I laid my head down and cried.
I was stuck in the middle I had no pride.
Today I looked around and no one was there.
I reached our for help but no one cared.
Today I saw a knife all the pain came  to mind,
I couldnt deny the hatred inside.
Today I saw my fourth grade picture.
Remembering how things were so much easier.
Today I learned my problems are for me to fix.
The choice that I make I have to live with.
Today I learned that freinds turn there back.
That caring for each other is something they lack.
Today I learned that trust is earned.
That its not a gift or something, you give in return.
Today I learned that life got ahead of me.
That I couldnt cathc up and make sense of anything.This poem was written and if u have a problem with it to bad... These are my feelings and I can't help how I feel....
