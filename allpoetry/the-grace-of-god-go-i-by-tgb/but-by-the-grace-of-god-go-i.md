A doorway for his shelter
a blanket for his bed
discarded, forgotten soul
better of dead

Begging for change
no money, no one cares
ashamed to hear the cynics call
drunk! junkie! A waste of my clean air!

Sitting there shivering
brought it on himself?
wrapped hungry in a threadbare shawl
In empty pockets he carries his all

confidence cast out
dignity shattered
shuffle to the bridge side
nothing now matters

climb on the railings
rivers torrent below
he questions,
would he be missed, were he to go

to jump and fall
to turn and flee
to give up on life
as it gave up on he.i hate seeing homeless people, we have a lot of the in Scotland. it makes me sad.
