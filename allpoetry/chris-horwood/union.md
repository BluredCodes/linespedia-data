UNION

simple needs, my complex heart
a happy flower this day to start
which was begun short nine years prior
from here evolves as we aspire
the sharing of a treasured life
from now through then as man and wife
so tender, care this gentle dove
I cede to you my soul, my loveI wrote this for a precious friend who today married her partner of nine years. I love you Julie.
