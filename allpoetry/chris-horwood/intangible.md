INTANGIBLE

Was it my eyes upon you falling felt
that turned your glance to me
some curious part of feminine self
that knew intuitively

Was it that smile to me so sensual
which took my breath away
just as it had, did I suspect
in some distant yesterday

Was it your turning around permission gave
a chance might we explore
that feeling which unspoken said
perhaps we'd loved before

Or was it just imaginings
a sense of deja vu
just wishful thought upon my part
perhaps you'd sensed it too

Well for now I'll muse the mystery
in those enigmatic eyes
and speculate in self-debate
what secrets they disguise

Until perhaps in future times
or other worldly place
the paradox of futures past
perhaps falls into place
