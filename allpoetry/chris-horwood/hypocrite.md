HYPOCRITE - Graphic Language Warning


you call me for a loser
cause I view things differently
well fuck you and your narrow mind
don't hang your shit on me

your blatant lies and backstab ways
and you dare critisize
as shallow as an empty pool
the loser in my eyes

and you've the hide to rag me out
because I dare be gentle
with your violent sporting pseudo-gods
well who's the fuckin mental

you preach me that my drug of choice
will get me into strife
well at least my drug don't make me wanna
beat up on my wife

you view me like some ferral beast
and tell me I'm inferior
but screwin people to build your wealth
sure don't make you superior

you say you care for fellow man
as the environment you plunder
if this is how you raise us up
you make me fuckin chunder

so next time when you sneer at me
from over lowered glasses
don't wonder why I've no respect
for you upper class of arsesWalking through the streets of Sydney a while ago, there were a group of young people hanging out, minding their own business and sharing a few laughs. They weren't being loud or offensive, just enjoying each others company, yet this suit-stiff walking past took offence, apparantly just because of the way they looked [the usual "cool dude" gear] and starts giving them a serve. As they stood and looked at each other in disbelief, I couldn't help wonder what a lot of young people think about the older generations [as if we don't know,
and is it any wonder?]
