Devoid

                             touch me
                              plea s
                          is anyone care
                    scream cries on stone ears
                an agonizing cacophony of silence

                             isolated

                      the totality of misery
               even poor loneliness pity thinks me
                    too desperate concern with
                        dreams fallen round
                       convictions detritus
                the flotsam of shattered existence
             flushed up putrifying on hopes shoreline
                              
                              enough
            that which spawned life will now consume it
                         down to the ocean
                                D
                                            
                                O
                                W
                                N
