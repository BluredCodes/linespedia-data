PUSSY


so
the moment of truth
I'll bet you've jacked yourself
senseless thinking about this
well here's your chance big guy
no bullshit, the facades stripped away
just the three of us
you, me and your fantasy

therapist/theRAPIST
and power?
well, you paid for the privilege honey
