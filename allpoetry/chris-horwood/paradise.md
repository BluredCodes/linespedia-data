PARADISE
[for Malcolm]

In a lush little valley at the base of Mt. View
Quite a trek along Springvale Track
Lies a bushman's retreat that proves hard to beat
"Briney's Farm" and Malcolm's old shack

Of galvanized iron, stone chimney in place
Your typical bushman's abode
A verandah with views that are unparalleled
And the sheep keep the grass nicely mowed

A cordial bottle and piece of old poly
For a downpipe will substitute
An old copper Rheem complete with a tap
As a water tank, it's a beaut

The abulutions consist of an old metal dish
And the mirror from a wreck on the wall
The loo with a view is "dig your own"
Just over behind that deadfall

There's parrots and 'roos, echidna and trout
Wild duck and cockies that shriek
And the sweetest spring water you ever will taste
Flowing down along Springvale Creek

Inside the shack is an Aussie bloke's dream
Washing piled up near the wall
The larder's well stocked, tinned peas and an onion
And there's coffee and tea if you call

Out back of the shack, an assortment of chairs
And a fence to keep the sheep out
An old camping cot, it's canvas length-ripped
One bean fart too many, no doubt!

There's jerries and chainsaw, knick-knacks and bits
All manner of tools set around
Woodpile and axe, rams head on gate
And rabbits springing out of the ground

The ex-posties bike, at the back door she waits
Old reliable to her master
When it's footy tips time, or a thirst to be slaked
Down the hill to the pub he will blast her

Now some might think the shack a bit rough
And not quite to their liking
But it'll do me any day
Well worth a full marathon's hiking

Cause you're always made feel welcome there
In that bushman's paradise
And in these days of 'up yours mate'
That's pretty bloody nice!This poem is dedicated to and inspired by a friend of mine who lives a true bushman's lifestyle in the mountains of the high country in southern New South Wales. I just hope that the "Australiana" terms that I've used in places aren't too colloquial, so that they lose their meaning/significance to those from other countries. What do you think?
