Bulimia for Hire


looks to thrill, looks to kill
looks to create illusion
the air-brained toys of marketing boys
purveyors of delusion

strut and prance, catwalk dance
pooncie little poses
a bulimic parade of stick insects
with dogshit up their noses

wasteland frames devoid of brains
the pretentiousness of chic
emotionless cadaver faced
anorexic little freaks

cameras whir, journo's purr
the socialites go orgasmic
flash them a sneer and a bit of tit dear
as we loosen their wallets of plastic

super models, super cool
or a super bunch of farces
well if this is what high fashion's about
they can stick it up their upper classes
