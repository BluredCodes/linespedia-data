GONE

so

smart at last
finally got the piece of paper to prove it

you can admire it now with both your faces
speak of it with both your tongues
gloat over it with both your ego's

but that piece of paper is as transparant as you
and the daggars you use to carve off
slices with which to impress your friends
will eventually be sheathed in their back's too

and your new found wisdom
will still not help you understand
why they all eventually leave
and you'll still be
the most intelligent fool I know
