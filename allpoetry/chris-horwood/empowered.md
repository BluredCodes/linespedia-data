EMPOWERED

kiss me    kick me
lose yourself in my love
treat me    beat me
abuse the gentle dove
loser    accuser
objectify your hate
tough guy    piss arse
power trip masturbate

fuck you

i'm out of here

and you're alone

unspoken fear
