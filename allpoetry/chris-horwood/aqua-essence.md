Aqua Essence

In an aqueous
medium you danced
infectious spontaneity
beckoning my participation
invitation unconditional
and daring imagination to
consider the possibilities
of former existence
shared ancestry

You were complete
and I momentarily
envious, until the
overpowering empathy
of that moment
reinforced that
this was not new to
either of us

for we knew each other
well
