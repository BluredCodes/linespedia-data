Perspective
         (To the dragonfly with apologies)

Dragon the frame through blue space
A duplex of gossamer suspension
All so different up there

Feel in
From the vantage of conscious detachment
Marvel back

So/We're the only ones with it?

What arrogance

Well/That's consciousness, hey
So much for the pinnacle of the evolutionary process
