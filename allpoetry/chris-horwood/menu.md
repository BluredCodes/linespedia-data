MENU

off the wagon today 
with a mighty thud
from the heights of achievement
back down in the crud
self promise and others
with oh such conviction
to succumb once again 
to your old friend addiction

yeah you bullshit so good
in control, in denial
and who gives a shit
it's your life to defile

but remember your promise
those words that you said
time to wake up
keep going, you're dead
