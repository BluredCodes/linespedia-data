IDENTITY


when I cease desire your adornment be
and you look to my face for your mirror but see
not your egotist image reflected in me
will you help me or turn me away

when the words in my throat cease your echoes to be
and the thoughts in in my mind from your shackles break free
when the needs of my own seek their own needs from me
will you love still or seek a new clone

well?
