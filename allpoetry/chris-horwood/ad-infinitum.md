AD INFINITUM


that perfume!

I smell a memory and
search the room
and though devoid of 
your presence again I
feel your arms envelop me

sweet erotica
the bliss of first
loves naivety and
passionate its embrace

as petite breasts
pressed firm into 
the chest of youthful
anticipation

as the thrust and
glide of torsos against
the moistened glaze
of silken sheets

as the electric
intensity of
breath blown soft
upon parted lips

so attuned to
the song of hearts

and so receptive to
forevers promise

anticipating the survival
of more than just

memories


FOR SOMEONE
A LONG TIME AGO
