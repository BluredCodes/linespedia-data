B-Bay

suck in air
and the rocks disappear
tides encompass

gulls turn
and bicker over morsels
a thrashing of beaks
the madness of wings

thought and sea-eagles
compete for altitude
the sand in the hourglass
has all evaporated

and all the while
the melody of La Mer
for the one-legged tern
