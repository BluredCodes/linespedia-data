REMEX

we walked to the top of the world
and danced on the pinnacle of existence
arms outstretched
as birds
and their thoughts were our minds
felt the wind 
and knew it
soared to the treetops
and swayed as the branches
smoked the fires ancient allure
and thought into it
trod the earth
and walked upon ourselves
searched the skies for the universe
and discovered it within
contemplated time
and were as one with the continuum

and knew ourselves
everyone
everything
