title: "Made to be me"
writer: "GiftedGoddess10"

I wasn’t meant to be skinny
Size 2 or size 3
Because when I was created
I was made to be me
I wasn’t made to be gorgeous
The finest face you’d ever see
Because when I was created
I was made to be me
I wasn’t made to have hair silky & smooth
And flowing down my back
I wasn’t made to have flawless skin
That was either too light or too black
I wasn’t made to be the most humorous
Not everything I say is funny
Because when I was created
I was made to be me
I wasn’t made to be the smartest
The most brilliant the world would see
Because when I was created
I was made to be me
Just a piece of advice
For all eyes to see
Love me for who I am
Cause that’s all I’m gonna be
