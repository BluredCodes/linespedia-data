I hate to have to tell you thisBut now I guess I have toFor about the last 2 years I’ve been cheating on youI didn’t know what to sayI didn’t want to make you cryI couldn’t stand to hurt youIf I just tried to say goodbyeIt all happened so fastIt was a big mistakeI was planning on telling you beforeBut I guess my planning came too lateIt was at that partyYou’re birthday party in factThis guy just pulled me closeAnd then sat me on his lapHe started kicking this sweet gameI never wanted it to endI guess the alcohol had my brainI didn’t know it was your best friendBut the rest of the days togetherI really can’t excuseHe knew exactly what to sayTruthfully, he’s really a better lover than youI’m really sorry babyIf I’m offending you with my wordsBut you never bothered to apologizeWhen I caught you with those birdsSo I guess we’re through nowYou go on with your lifeIt was fun while it lastedBut it’s better now with Mike.
