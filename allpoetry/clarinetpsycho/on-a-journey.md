---
title: "On a Journey"
writer: "clarinetpsycho"
slug: "on-a-journey"
source: "AllPoetry"

Traveling alone on a journey,
the journey called life.
I travel alone on this journey
looking for answers to questions,
questions that seem to have no answer.
Traveling this lonely world
looking for those answers.
I have seen many things,
and seen many people.
But none of those things or people
have seen me.
I travel unseen,
but I see everything.
Through everything I have seen
I have found many answers.
But the answer I have found
are for other people’s questions.
All the people say thank you,
and say they will remember me.
But over time they forget me
and don’t remember who I am.
So I start to travel 
this journey once again.
I travel it alone, unseen,
and unremembered.
Still searching for answers,
answers to my own questions.
I know as long as I stay
on this path I will find those answers,
but for mow I travel this journey
unseen and unremembered.
