title: "i am a man"

i am a man

crooked, awkward line drawing of

a sobbing puppet.



symbology, we can dream of meat-eating

dinosaurs and trundling epitomies.



where you draw the lines,

where you draw the crosses

and the dollar signs, i will stand

within my box and be yours.



sobbing puppet. no fun toy.

measley little nothing thing,

artifact



of discrepancies. i am a man

crooked, sobbing line drawing of

my shameless fantasies. actor

for your wet stage and soft legs.



actor for your actors' guild.

all right. blood in the veins.

get it rolling. i am a man

tired, wonderful and stick man version



of a young boy, caged, crying.this is one of my older pieces, dating way back to 1998...
