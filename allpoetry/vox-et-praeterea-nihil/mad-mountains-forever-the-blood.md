title: "mad mountains (forever, the blood)"
writer: "vox et praeterea nihil"

mad mountains crawl down my

veins and i want to be within you



inside you and ruthlessly

swallowed. there are hearts of

thunder, bred and broken through

the mandibles of this employ,



the terrorism of my own body. mad

torrents of torrent torment my flowing

moments. i want my veins to drive



circles, into, out of, around, beneath your

skin. beneath your surface (wet; within)

and when your villainous fingertips

run villainous fingertrips down my

visible drain,



mad mountains crawl earthly 'round my

sane and rational treason. remember,



this will not last forever, the

blood will go



forever, the blood

will go



forever, the blood
