title: "ceramic"
writer: "vox et praeterea nihil"

i've never had you on my face,

ceramic and wet, chilling

and when i saw you in your sleep

it wasn't how i wanted it

it wasn't capable and free, empty and engulfing



though my arms did grace you

though my hands held fast...



growling ceramic wet,

she has my dreams locked in siege,

a struggle, a mandatory struggle,

will against will.



scouring ceramic surfaces,

surging out

captured

sighing about the possibilities

singing about the parlance of our times

will against will, a mandatory struggle

a pause...



growling ceramic wet,

she has locked me within my dreams,

without reprieve, where i can see you on my face,

where you are rose and warm

where you are short and powerful,

moving and symphonic, orchestrating masterpieces,

masturbating capable and free,

empty

and engulfing

and it's how i want it

seeing you in my sleep



growling ceramic wet,

moving and moaning princess,

subtle and God Damn This Fantasy seductive,

behind me



though my arms did grace you

though my hands held fast...



sighing about the possibilites

singing about the surging surfaces

and disturbing suffrances.(not my best but my most recent)
