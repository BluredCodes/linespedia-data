---
title: "whispersweet sheath"
writer: "vox et praeterea nihil"
slug: "whispersweet-sheath"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8928-whispersweet-sheath-by-vox-et-praeterea-nihil"

under the

many waves

in

dreams and

under hiding it

sits



such

unusual power

and yet

voracious hunger

it holds, as

though it could

eat away the world, and

rightly should



my own hungers

are

driving me toward it, to

eat and savor such

ripened and

elaborate tastes





does it

usually taste

like this? so

comfortable

i don't want to

stop



let me stay

in here,

guide me

under the folds

right where

i want to be,

right where it never

ends



longing for

it swells in me,

never wanting to stop, but

gradually i must

escape,

right where it never

ends
