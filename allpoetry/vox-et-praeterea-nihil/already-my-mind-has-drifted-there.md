already my mind has drifted

whither which all wandering away,

a thousand innuendoes are playing with my

fragile toys, my mind

explodes (and girls and boys,

already my mind has drifted boundless

off, often lost in faithful fluff)



and boys and girls, i'm stuck

(here rutted) dreaming of the things i've wanted

since the day

since the nice moment we began

ever since ever since,

and boys and girls, this random world

this enemy, this fruit has fallen off the tree.



her legs, her eyes and blue and blonde,

her momentary collapse, her standing still dance,

her laugh and everything, and



already my mind has drifted from the past,

the present's folded future maps,

the man and woman song, the is it right or wrong,



and girls and boys (so painlessly) i

accidentally set it free, and bodies touch

in all my thoughts, and trouble starts

its building up, its many-sided twist

into the only thing i hide from you.
