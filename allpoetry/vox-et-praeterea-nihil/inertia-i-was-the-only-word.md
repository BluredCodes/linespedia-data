i was the only word in her vocabulary,

in the structure of the simple

the structure of the simple punctures

piercing little needles digging dirt into the flesh

i was the only word in her vocabulary,

the structure of her acupuncture

the minutes we return to rest

the mind we set to spinning tests



i was the hand that flexed the hand around

the dirt that soiled up her ground

running myself around town to keep up

running myself into the earth

digging deeper

digging digging deeper for the same old shame

the same old wasted sort of game

the mind we set to spinning tests

to put our minds again at rest



she sucked the life from him

she swallowed

she touched her legs around him 

when she followed him home from school

when she hollowed herself around him

drunk and duplicate

and i was the ease she put herself to

i was the minute the sea touched shore

the meat around her apple core



and already she has gone away

and i'm the last thing on her list today

i'm the list of last things meant to do

i know the little piercing needles digging dirt into

i know the middle structure of the subtle angry acupuncture

i was the hand that flexed with her fingers

i was the arm holding nothing around



running dirt into the ground

running soil around the fear

with wishes never ceasing here



the mind we set to spinning tests

to chase away whatever is left

i was the only word in her vocabulary

but moving objects come to rest
