---
title: "Never A Greater Loss"
writer: "Danw"
slug: "never-a-greater-loss"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/45807-Never-A-Greater-Loss-by-Danw"
---

A friend of mine, a friend to all
We gave him love he gave us more

Just a taste, was all he had
A bite from the apple he cupped in his hands

Met him like we all in school
Oh the wonder we had found
Everything seemed so much easier
When he was around

The best was all he knew, Good was all he’d do
But now he’s gone, without so-long

Taken from those who loved him
From those who care, given to god above
A child that the angels now doth bare

I should have spoken to him, shook him by his hand.
Instead we all now miss him, and all that he had planned

Gone but not forgotten, left he a story to be told
Story, soul, a friend remembered till’ the day we all grow old
