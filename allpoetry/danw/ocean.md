title: "Ocean"

I’m down below you
I’m surpassed at best

If the oceans made you, who made me?
I can feel fear, it can’t fear me
I can’t see it, it’s inside me
I can leave it, Leave it in a glass

Alcoholic substance,
Makes fear take a pass
For a few hours of bliss
But they’d never last

The oceans made you,
But they’re drowning me
Drowning me, in a sea of democracy
