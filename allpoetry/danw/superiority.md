Nice watch, mines better
Scream distance, I’ll just send a letter
Cry for help, I’ll help myself
Go to school, I like to teach ......myself

Myself, slowly watching your withering confidence
I watch, as your leaving the soul of your habitat
What for?

Your family’s dead, Mines a reincarnation
You feel as though you can’t win the battle, 
Saddle up son and get with the rest of the cattle

That’s not say that I think I am better but......
I am
Not to say that im wealthier than...
A pauper like you my good man

I don’t mean to sound rude, 
But I wouldn’t spare you a crumb of my food
You should have been careful, 
You shouldn’t have cared at all

Now with many you rest
Slumped below, behind the best
