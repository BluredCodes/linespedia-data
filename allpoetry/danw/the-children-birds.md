The small children danced in the sky
The olden ones looked on in disgust wishing them to die
The small children are falling from the sky
Olden ones laugh.................

I bring you not hate only love
The love of  a blind man to a friend, The love that brings people together

Did you hear the song of the birds?
Their beautiful sound unspoken as words
Their song has been sung through many lifetimes
They sang as young men lined up to die for their country
They sang as small children crawl through the beatings of childhood

When the world is in need of beauty, these birds hide
Where is their beauty when brother dies?
Perhaps the beautiful song is no more than a death march for eternity.
