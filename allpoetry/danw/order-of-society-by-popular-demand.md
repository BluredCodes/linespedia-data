Relax
Relief
Ok
Time to believe

Reproduce
Replace
Remove
Put to rest

Equality for some
Inbalance for us all
Developing only what we wish
Educated in, what they say
They do, they tell you too

Abuse
Abused
Misuse
Misused
Un avoided
But somehow screwed

Incompetance 
Idiots
Insects
If I crush you
There is no judgement

Space
Spiders
Speed
Height
Haunted by your fears

Crush
Crushed
Crashed to the ground
No more
Playing around

Imitation
Leads me to feel
Frustration
Leads me to see
Impersonation

Eradication is the only way to go
Just like I said, I told you so

A million times before, a thousand times again
What’s done is done, whats said is said

Pre-heated to condtion
Humanity is flawed
Flawed by repetition
The explantion to the problem
Is to have patience, 
Not await what you are told

Told
Forced
Must do
What I say

Love 
Hate
War
Not fare
Warfare 
Who cares?
Not me said I
And she with he
When asked to be
Possibly free

Controlled
Enrolled
In a life
Like every other
Just like your brothers

You are human too
Do what you 
Wish to do!
It’s up to you??????,
