title: "River Of Knowledge or The Allegiance Lost?"

Glorified, petrified, living life
On the narrow rivers edge
The calm sea loose like mountains ledge
Falling down, never reach the ground

Time to drown

Exaggerated, imagination
The operation, more powerful than man
A procedure overdone, gods wisdom undone
His wisdom is denied, his rules have been defied

By those who set them
Glorified, to be petrified
Water seething like
The devils new born child

Meddling metaphor,
Unvoiced to be ignored
Runs from the razors edge
Inevitable like the mountains ledge

Can you put a price on life?
You can’t, so just forget about peace
Just forget about it
All, is lost, forgotten last has one

But we must rule and rise as when
Adventures end, the wars begun
The beginning of the end
Like letters soldiers sent

To die

For king and country
Like slaughtered cows
To be hungry, the brown cow!
Who’s laughing now?
