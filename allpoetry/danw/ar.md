A.R?

What did they do to poor Sandy Baloo
An object of merriment like the ones in the zoo
We took her to Dr two, they told us the cure
Took my dog for a walk, she fell to the floor

What did they do?, why is she sick?
“Prescription is the remedy”
To cure disease, so why did she fall to the ground?
She’s gone, oh no

Whatever happened to Harry the H
Said they would help him
Then we saw the ape
Smothered with blankets
His outsides were in
Syringes were scattered the sign read.....

Chorus

No Entry, not for you not for me
Not for you, me to see
What they did to the dogs
What they’re feeding the hogs
It’s not for us to see

Bridge

Resting, quietly
While it seems they’re
Testing quit violently
Children crying
Nature joins tem

Chorus

No Entry, not for you not for me
Not for you, me to see
What they did to the dogs
What they’re feeding the hogs
It’s not for us to see

I don’t want to be the one, who’s tested on
The one, the one the one
Just one
