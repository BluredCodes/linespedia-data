---
title: "Mother?"
writer: "Danw"
slug: "mother"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/45816-Mother--by-Danw"
---

Beauty defined, the worlds rendition of gods silhouette
Fear it can consume us, it only gets in the way
But when the fear has faded all that’s left is your face

Never bettered, not even contested
Like a child that’s unborn, in a life that’s un tested
United together to celebrate this day
Forever together, in a sentimental way

For lies grow old, and with youth is brought truth
Dispelled are the lies as you listen to cries
Of those for whom you care..
Of those who’s everywhere, is empty without you
As fictional as life is, but forever true

You think you heard a whispered voice?
Well hear this mother fucker, Hear this mother fucker
