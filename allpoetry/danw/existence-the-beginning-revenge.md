title: "(Existence & The Beginning - Revenge)"

Young children stood laughing by the fireplace
Slowly burning in the tides of time
The eagle embarks on new land, for life
The hunter breathes a sigh of death as he reaches for his tool

The animals gathered to watch the sea consume the shore
Watching the sands slowly sinking toward the sea

Angels danced on hells highway
Whilst shadows burnt in the fire of daylight

What has brought us to this hideous place?
The days diminity brings forth night upon the old village
The people of it’s tender caring, pray for forgiveness as they tear it down
We have killed our dear sister, our home , our lives

A man enters the room..!
Who is this young friend, we open our caring arms to tend for him
Fill his life with our love and spread our joy throughout his soul
‘Your people live in the village of the damned, now you must become exactly that’
The filthy man tore his weapon from his pocket
‘I have slain your people for freedom’

You hear my screams but your minds do not want to hear
You feel my pain but you pretend as though they are not of existence
You have caused my terror and now you must die!
Leaving behind only the shattered remains of your lives dream’s
