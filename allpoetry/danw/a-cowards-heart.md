I only see you on beautiful days,
Because you make the days beautiful

I see you only on beautiful days,
Because it is you who makes them beautiful
Like a drop of rain on the desert sand
Your a miracle blessing the land

A day wasted is a day spent without you
A day with you is the way to light the day

The truest truths of life are spoken on the night,
That will one day be for you,
A sentence incomplete, words unspoken too

Time will only amplify the beauty in your mirror
Time will pass, Im standing by streak down my back is getting clearer
Incomplete is to be without you, like god above beauty surrounds you

A day will come when I am brave, for both of us a tale to be told
No longer scared your mine to hold
Until that day I write to you, with a pen and transparent ink
My loneliness confining me, a time to stop and think

Another day and midnight passes an empty room and worthless classes
Empty bottle two unfilled glasses, should this be forever?
