---
title: "Re-Believe"
writer: "Danw"
slug: "re-believe"

Relieve me,
Believe me
In my time… of weakness

Believe me
Relieve me
From the pain that never graced my soul before

Console me,
Control me,
Take me back to how I felt before I loved

Don’t leave me
Believe me
I didn’t realize what I’d done before

Be with me,
Just fill me,
Make me whole again

Don’t love me,
Don’t leave me,
Just don’t hurt me no more

Don’t you, hurt me
Try to feel me
Try to keep me in touch with her no more
I don’t want this life, to hurt me anymore

I never felt like this before
I never felt un-loved before
I still believe, there’s something left
Still can’t believe, the day she left
