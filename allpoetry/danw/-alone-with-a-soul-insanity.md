title: "( Alone With A Soul - Insanity)"

Did you hear that?
Did I say that?
Can you feel it?

Am I alive?

The horrific flames surround me burning my skin,
The cries of terrified children haunt me 
Why do they scream?
The screams are getting louder, almost deafening
My safety has gone, the flames draw closer
The walls are getting smaller but the flames still rage
Closer ......Closer

I cannot hear you 
Darkness imprisons me and all that I see
Please god help me!
Yesterday will soon be here and tomorrow will be history
The past is the future and the future is the past but the present brings only pain
Of people slowly slain, their tortured souls fill my head

The flames have now consumed me, tearing away my flesh and haunting my fragile soul.
Am I dead?
Everything is still, and dances
