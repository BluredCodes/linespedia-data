---
title: "Mama"
writer: "Danw"
slug: "mama"

Take a break, take some time to think
Of all the places and the things you’ve seen

Mama never said
There’d be skies of grey
Mama never told, that you die someday

You feel the hunger, So you
Stop and wonder,  What your hungry for

Mama never told you, why daddy had to leave
Mama never taught you
About the tricks that were up his sleeve

Slowly sip your wine, 
Vodka at eight, and your feelin’ fine
Or at least you think so

Mama never told you so,
The places your not supposed to go
Mama, yeah she said so
Theirs a place you shouldn’t go

You can’t get up from your chair 
The wheels are keeping you their
Look in the mirror and it cracks
Reflecting memories of things you can’t take back

You never knew before
Mama said, yeah she said
Never stop, no she never said
Don’t go, just don’t go

Sleep and the darkness is enclosing
Sanctuary for last time, a lifeless spectre
Takes you down into his parlour
Takes you down to meet you father

Mama never said, your daddy was dead
But he’s alive, you been tricked
Trapped alone, your rendered useless
But she never said to fear thoughts!
