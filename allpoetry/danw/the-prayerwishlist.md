Dear God
Please take away those who beat my brothers,
Please take away those who raped my sisters
Grant my family some money,
To feed my children, and rub salt in my blisters

Dear God
Would you let me have a go
At doing what you do
Just so I could know
Exactly, what it is your thinking

Dear God
Can you forgive our sins?
Forgive my Asian brothers, ‘cos they thinkin’
Forgive the prisoners for committing no crime
Forgive you, forgive me for making us live a lie

Dear Satan
What the fuck is your problem?
Democracy, Poverty
I can’t see or at least it seems to be , that way

Who put the price up for prayer?
Who put the price up for prayer?
Like the man in the box, like he even cares

“I listen to your shit”
I’m raking it in
The money, the stories that your telling
I’m raking it in

I wish I rich, I wish I was famous nah
I wish I was an English man, with niggers blood upon my hands, nah
I wish I was an Equal man, I’d hold my colours hand in hand

Dear God
Nah, But equality was never for you
You saved the foolish
And punished those that do....
What they want, independence
What they want!
