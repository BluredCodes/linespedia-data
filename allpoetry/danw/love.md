Express your emotion, Your  unconditional devotion
Love is my poison and I hold the solution
To love!
The solution......
To love!
The evolution
Of Love!

Express your emotion, Your  unconditional devotion
Your love it is my potion, lying safe behind the ocean

Holding on to you, until the day love is through
Clinging on to me, Until we can be free in a sky that is true
In a sky for me and you!

It’s not me your offending so just stop your pretending,
Nothing remains the same, now that everything has changed
It’s not the same.......

Part 2

Two hearts that shouldn’t be together
Closely holding love to be forever
Two minds that shouldn’t talk together
Holding onto their love forever
You came from a town
Where birds love the sky
You smile but never frown
Cos you were made for I

Your the light in my life
Your the flame that lights my eternal candle
Lay your picture on my lonesome mantle

Should it come the day you die
Time will heal my wounds that hide,
The mourning hollow sky provides
My love!

Tender town love
Backwards and forwards, Love is true
Never enough
But life is just a game to you

Birds that fly out to the sea
Counting sands of love for you and me
But it’s much to late
When the feeling fades
Face to face,
And I could not even talk to you
Just can’t talk to you
I just can’t talk to you
Look what I’m going through
I don’t need to talk to you

Because it’s over!
