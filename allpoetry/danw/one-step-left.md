I don’t mind standing in line, to meet the man, the president
But I can’t stand to be powerless, when/while my families dying far away

Never questioned my ........
Bad intentions .........

I don’t mind waiting in line, 
No I don’t mind waiting in bed
If the mouths that speak aren’t being fed

If it’s not an act from the heart,
It’s another thing to tear the world apart

Powerless, but still hurt
Can’t see the reason, but it still hurts

Faceless stranger, looks just like me
How can they be, sick as Satan?
How sick can they be, as sick as Satan?

One step left, they get to hell
