title: "Deserted"

I loved in days before now,
Before love was cool
I saw the signs that ordered
That we forget somehow

I’m sorry but I’ve something to tell you
Speaking from the heart, the missing part
I swallowed my pride to keep on trying
You said loves dying, well I’m not

Colours on the wall
The lights are shining on
My friends they’re 10 feet tall
They said that I was wrong

I’m writing these words
And I’ll keep on trying
Until your love for me
Stops hiding

She loves me, she loves me not
It kills me so much, she loves me not
I feel your crying, like rain to the dry desert sands
Let me be, all that I can be, let me be your man
