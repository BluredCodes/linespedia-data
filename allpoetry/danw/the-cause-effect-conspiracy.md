War makes mice men
To the shrew in the field
Like the cows become cattle
All slaughtered in the battle

Never once is once enough
One time be loved
One time be judged
Be born ...just once

You are to us as he to she
As they to the, inferiority

What’s seen, we judge
What’s owned we prostitute
The wrong we persecute
No longer execute

An opinion to me
Is not for you to see
Stay put, cease thoughts
When spoken, they are ignored

Speak up, step down
Slow down, back up

If you can beat your friends
And there’s no way to join them
Strike back, when struck upon
React to the reaction

The chains attached to everything
Respond equal oppositely
The cause effect conspiracy
It’s happening to me

Pick your wounds, they bleed
As you think of what you’ve seen
The cause effect conspiracy
It all reacts, that’s plain to see
