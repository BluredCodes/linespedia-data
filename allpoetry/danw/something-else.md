So I begin again, and so the world it lends a hand
Yet receives a bloody stump, my life was blunt
And to the point, gnawed to the bone
My loneliness the only friend I own

Shine your light for me,
Just for the first time
See the light in me
This is my last time

So I begin again, not for the first time
My minds inside mine

Watch me die each day….
I am only one, yet before the day is done
I am born again
Forced to live again
