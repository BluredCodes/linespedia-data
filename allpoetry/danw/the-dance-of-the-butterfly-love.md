title: "(The Dance Of The Butterfly - Love)"

I love your walk, like I love the dance of the butterfly
Your hair is beautifully golden as grains of sand on a beach
The wonderful sky and the unparalleled beauty of the sun 
Hold their heads low as they are nothing when compared to your beauty.

Your beautiful eyes light up the skies,
Filling happiness into my life
Your great beauty is the light that guides me through the dark of night
The summertime smiles as it casts your sweet shadow in it’s light
Your face is a sweet rose and your body the sweetest flowers of gods garden.

The sight of you lights the candle that shows me the way,
Without you I would be blind, with only the thought of you to guide my way,
I write to you with words unspoken, So my heart is not broken
Forgive me for not presenting you with poetry in person with pride
But your intimidating beauty would force me to hide

As long as the sun shines and the moon lights the sky
I will know forever the reason why......
God made such a creature as you, for I
