---
title: "School/Liar"
writer: "Danw"
slug: "schoolliar"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I just said I love you,
I never said I liked you
Abused and misused
An object of shame

I said I love you,
I never said I liked you
I said, I’d touch
I never said I’d care

You said you knew me,
And I said that I knew you
You said you loved me
And I said that we were through
