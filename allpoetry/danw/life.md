title: "Life"

You begin, just another mouth to feed
When it ends, as just another fuck to bleed

Take all I've got and all I have
Go feed your kids, livin' in the ritz
Watching me sink right down
Below your feet

You take my cash
My clothes, my shoes my loved one's
You watch me rot in my defiance
While I die

It's your idea,
To feed some of those folks
Who you are starving

You taxed my car
And before long you'd taxed my babies
Have you taken the time
To watch reality lately?

Reality to you it could be quite alarming
I'm hating you and all the freedom your disarming
