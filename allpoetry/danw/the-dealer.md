title: "The Dealer"
writer: "Danw"

You can roll a dice
Play the cards
Place your bet

I bet you this
It’s best to bet
All your life
One at a time

Your life is set
Like Russian roulette
Only one winner
(Only one holds true)

So take the dice
Throw it down
And throw yours away
Don’t even think
That there will be
A better day

Bet your house
Bet your car
I bet you won’t
Get to Mexico
Won’t live that far
