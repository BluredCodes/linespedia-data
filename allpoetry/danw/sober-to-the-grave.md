---
title: "Sober To The Grave"
writer: "Danw"
slug: "sober-to-the-grave"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/46208-Sober-To-The-Grave-by-Danw"
---

Changing ways, they make him a better man
The devils cards, deal him a better hand

With just a touch of innocence
Just a pull of the clutch, to prevent the incident
But it’s not enough to be the same
Improvise in life the game

He waits till the day is over
Three drinks from being sober,
Just one away, just one way
From saying it’s OK, and he wanted it that way

Never does he fear
And never does he feel
His future like a boat
Memories of his past are quickly sinking

Ain’t no shame in being fair,
He’s got no shame with having cared
For the girl he left behind

New towns, new faces
Changing places, switching in his mind
He blames it on his friends
They blame it on the time

Blame it on the alcohol,
But never on his soul
Just a drink from sorrow
It’s the drink that keeps him whole

Never short on change,
But it’s something he can’t do
Empty like a bottle, feels his life is through
It’s seems OK to him, just seems strange to you

Arms wide open, with his hands in the air
Mind forced open with the drink in his hands
And his time becomes frozen, like the heart of the man
Lives his life equal but always it’s unfair
No shame as he, to watch him, for him to
Cry, as he sighs
Watch him, and cry, as he dies

The glass was empty but the bottle was new
Drank his life away at twenty two
Never cared for the thoughts in his mind
Stayed devoted to those left behind
