---
title: "Revenge (You Lie)"
writer: "Danw"
slug: "revenge-you-lie"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/46115-Revenge--You-Lie--by-Danw"
---

Alone I sit in my cage
Depressed, sadness cures my rage
We live, we love
We die

Alone, I’ll die
My insides cry
I live, you lied
We die

With you, I’m alive
With you, I survive
I live, alone
You love, you lie
We die

Feelings are numb,
You took, the breath from my tired lungs
You love, you life
You kill me
You loved to lie
We die

You love, to lie
But now, must die
True lies, real life
You lied, you die
