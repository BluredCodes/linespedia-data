---
title: "Fade"
writer: "Danw"
slug: "fade"

Staring blankly at the sun,
Wondering if he could be one
One day maybe the child, will run
So far forward, he’ll reach the sun

Staring scared toward the sun
Wonders when his time will come
The weary man, now old and frail
Seeks relief from his lifelong jail

A time will come when I must leave
Behind me cast, my shadow breathes
For how much longer will it last
Until it fades into the grass
