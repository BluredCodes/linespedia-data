There I am, I’m trapped inside television
There I stand, caged inside the window glass
I don’t feel, when I’m twisted round the shining brass
I don’t break when I’m bent inside the mirrors glass

I’m twisted, I’m crazy
I’m not in love

White lines, dark, dark liquid
Mysterious needle, being injected
Old ugly man, negatively reflected
Needle injected, somebody cut me..... some slack

There I am, trapped again
Here I am rehab is my name

Crying babies, an un-caring stare
I’m missing life, I’m missing all the feeling
So I sit reflected
