title: "Simplicity"
writer: "Danw"

We are man, the lands simplest man
We are man, the world craziest man
Man oh man, create mechanical
Man oh man, puppets to the hands

God the lord, pulling on mans chord
God the lord, Crazy, imperfect flaws
God the man, wandering guides the lost
God the man. Killed upon a cross

Man the god, he destroyed the land
Man the god, he made our world the damned
Christ the lord, has done nothing since
Christ the lord, Forgotten the world which was once convinced

Lord forgotten, Species wonder why?
Lord forgotten, Does he wonder why?
