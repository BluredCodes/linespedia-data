title: "(And) Above All Else"

Nothing left to give, No reasons left to live
All the reigns have slipped away
And been soaked up in the mirrored pools below
Nothing left for me, it’s all washed away

There’s something, There’s something I can’t find
There’s a choice, A choice farther down the line
But I can’t wait to take a chance time
I can’t wait to take a from life

I don’t want it,
To be petrified, by what may lie ahead
Or what may not
I’ll skip the game instead

I hear the lies, beneath me
Can’t take the pain, within’ me
