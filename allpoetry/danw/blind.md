title: "Blind"
writer: "Danw"

To a child the world is new,
To the mind the world is closed
To the blind, the worlds are clothes

Waking in the silence of the invisible night
Forever resting their eyes
Focused only on what could be
What could have been

Lost is the memory of colour
Spent is the image of life’s colour
A split second between sight and sound
Sound light travels the speed of sounds

Forever lost is my way to see
Still no signs, cannot stop my will to be
Death is to be without words
Unspoken sounds, deaf is tone not heard

Trust your mind, 
Dreams of visions are what you dream
If it was only that way
Can’t be your eyes, but I can give back to you
Sight in the lines of your mind
I’ll find your way from here to where?

I love you!
