---
title: "Don't Call Me"
writer: "Danw"

Don’t call me, 
Don’t even look my way
Don’t even say my name
Don’t call me

Don’t send me your frozen images
Nothing you say will remind me
Of when times were good, 
When we were one

Don’t call me
Don’t dial my number
Don’t love me
Don’t touch my life again

Don’t say my name in your sweet tone
Don’t call my phone, ‘cos I’m not home
Don’t call me
Don’t hurt my feelings

Don’t call me
Don’t try to win me back
Don’t hurt me no more
Just, Don’t call me
