title: "Me"
writer: "Danw"

Buy one and get one free,
The name of my life story
Religion tells you, just what is what
God made you, so you could watch me wasting all I’ve got
Self destructive is my middle name
Suicidal is my last

Don’t be afraid of being alive,
You know what you can do, now do what you can’t
Build a child from a watered plant
It cost me, more than money
To sell my mind,
Profit in peace is a profit lost

Do what you want,
Do what you can
Don’t be afraid of being a man
Don’ be afraid of not giving a damn
