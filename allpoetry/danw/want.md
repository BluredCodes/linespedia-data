I don’t want to die alone,
Reminiscing on my own

But I don’t want to be just one of those
One with the ‘we’ as they propose

I don’t want to die alone
But I don’t want to live alone

Nor do I want to live my life
Like a stranger with a neighbours wife
Avoid confusion, I miss the confrontation
Never there, never aware of what im missing

Watchin’ the man, of whom she’s kissing
From the distance over there
She can’t see, doesn’t even care

Volunteered to give my mind,
The package comes for free

Never enough to say
Or at least not in the way
My thoughts they spell the words that I mistake
