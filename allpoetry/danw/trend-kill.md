---
title: "Trend Kill"
writer: "Danw"
slug: "trend-kill"

---

Cliché, Consistent without change
A trend set, To follow, without use of brain

Do as I tell you too never as you wish to do
You’ll just fall flat, on your ass

Following the path, with closed eyes
Your only fear, to improvise
It’s a curse, not a fucking religion

I, I’m laughing not with you
Conspiring, to free you
Conspire to lead them, in a new direction
Conspire to leave them your not my fucking possession

A glance in the mirror, brings tears to eyes
Of you and every man before, who never thought to try

Stray from the path, for inside of your head lies 
Something improvised
