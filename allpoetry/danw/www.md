Read a letter today, written in German ink
Said It wants to be read, and it made me think
50 years passed since the war,  Heroes died before the war
The letter said he missed his family, tragic is,  that now there dead

It’s funny how time changes things, from a man with a stone in his hand
To a finger on the trigger of a gun
He asks me ‘What went wrong’
If I could only tell him, what a change his sacrifice made
I’d be a liar, the worlds the same

He finished the letter, like his life
Before it’s time, with love to his wife
