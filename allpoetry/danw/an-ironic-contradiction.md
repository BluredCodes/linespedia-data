---
title: "An Ironic Contradiction"
writer: "Danw"

An ironic contradiction as amusing as the prison of the mind,
It offers freedom to all confined
As foolish as a master, His genius is only surpassed by the thoughts
and laughter in his mind

Front of the line, I’m at the back of the queue
Seeking only the things, that in time will come to be true
If life is a game, then man is the player
God, is unholy insane human slayer

On a dark, dark day when all is the light,
It’s meaningless to think your right
Belief is an illusion of the mind
Blind is all it has to say

Nothing’s worse, than when your soul delight
Comes crashing down, slowly softly it’s cushioned
To suffocate you, deprivation of air
Reminiscent of where, the fish like to swim

Don’t fight against it, just fight to give in
Don’t swim through, when the river’s within
The river within’, the air without, self controlled
Suicide, deprivation, self en-fuelled suffocation
