title: "The Twisted Empire Of The Sun"

As the thunderous storms raged outside,
His fear grew larger
The tiny collisions of invisible clouds
Kept him warm on the inside

Cautiously like a hunter he reached for his tool of death,
Daringly he lunged and sealed the creatures final breath
His tremendous victory…
Had left him at a loss for words

A killing of mass proportion,
So little that the shot was never heard
From whom would he steal life from next
An innocent creature of the wild?
Or a destined to be evil satanic child?

Only his tight mind could tell the truth,
Of the lies he held, within’ the noose
Of the rope that gave him pain
The coils that stole his life
