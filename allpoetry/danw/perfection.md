---
title: "Perfection"
writer: "Danw"
slug: "perfection"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Perfection is a lover on a winters day,
Perfection is another lost, come back to play
Perfection is a mirror showing only good
Showing youth forever, loving like it should

Perfection is a lover on a winters day,
She is my friend who went away
Perfection is my dream
Perfections gone but it never leaves me

If all you seek, is all you’ve lost
Perfection takes the morning frost away
If all you want is what you see
Perfection is reality

It’s either free or it’s not there
I know it seems unfair
Just think, It could be me or you
I feel, perfection’s me and you
