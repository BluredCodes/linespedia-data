---
title: "A Thought"
writer: "Danw"

The weight of thoughts, They’re crushing of your head
The sounds of thought, Echo through your life
The stress of words, Of head confined
Lost in the storm of your mind

The beauty of the dead, And all they leave behind
The silence of words said, Like writing to the blind
Hidden lines of life, Are scribed within your mind
Ironically they laugh, At the errors left behind

You can’t stop time itself, you cannot stop time
From doing what it has to
From taking from your mind, As it creates a world
A world within’ itself

A thunderous wave of thoughts, Surf to explore you mind
The weakening of your soul, Leaves a privileged empty hole
Heart drumming like a madman, For fear of fear itself
Your thoughts are to help others, though you cannot help yourself
