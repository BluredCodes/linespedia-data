title: "(The Crying Man - War)"

The man cries once more
Begging you to open the door
Let me into your beautiful lives
Save me from the stabbing of their knives

You disgrace me from your thrown
Although you know I am one of your own
A son of your creation cast to the whore of nature

Let me in to the house once mine
Do not leave me here to die
My life is running out of time, Why have you forgotten me?
Dig me out of this dark underground hole
Have you lost your souls?

I know now I can never return bearing the scars of loneliness
Now time must take me forever..!
