title: "Disease"
writer: "Danw"

Travelling like disease in a land of blood
The poisoned liquid is deformed by the infection of society
Fallen angels crash and burn on the side walk
Whilst the devils spawn run rabid through the streets

Apocalypse is nigh, The evil make their stand
The good now they all die, lay buried under land

When you said your mine, Your mine
Not giving nothing up this time
Put your vows up to the test
Big deal I cheated
You broke the rest

Broke your vows
Twist the knife
You know some how
You’ll get by
Twist the knife
I got nine lives
