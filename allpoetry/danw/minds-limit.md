Waking up, falling down
To sleeping silent in the ground
The gain of progress, the pain of life
Progress today I’ll take mine with a knife

Violent mind, restless sleep
Time to lose what’s mine to keep
Mother calling, Heavens crying
Now’s the time to say goodbye to life again

When I find, what I need to set me free
Tell me can you believe, the words that can’t hear
Written on paper quickly burning, can’t that time is going back again

Going back again,
To heavens arms, The thrills I killed
Take me back again, 
Needles lost, Shuffled cards and can’t be found

2

Alone with holes again
Empty space that was my soul
Empty place it makes me fold again

I think I’ve lost my mind
I thought it was my friend
Losing, losing, losing

What’s gone is gone
What’s done is done
I said why would you take peace from me
What’s done is done I wanted it that way
Done is done, Done is done, Done is done, Done is done, Done is done, Done is done,

3

Now it’s over can’t pretend
The soul he sold, it got him in the end
Hungers for life, but he’s full
Can’t you see the man, He is a fool????????,
