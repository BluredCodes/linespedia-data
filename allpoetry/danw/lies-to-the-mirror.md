---
title: "Lies To The Mirror"
writer: "Danw"
slug: "lies-to-the-mirror"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/45787-Lies-To-The-Mirror-by-Danw"
---

Can you believe all the bullshit today
Flying, circulating everyday

Relieved of duties unforeseen
For a fear of not being the same
Under pressure to qualify
An obsession they can’t deny

Does as he’s told not as he wishes
Paralysed by their vicious conditions
Where’s he now, he’s dead

But they don’t care
Where is he?
Somebody’s dead
But they didn’t know
Where is he?
He’s dead, he’s gone

Gone but not forgotten, a false term
Forgotten but not gone he feels like that’s the one

Well I stopped trying to make a difference,
Don’t you, speak about, false implications, me you speak about, in past tense

I wish for nothing more than peace, 
A peace of mind that keeps me open
Freely as I run, towards the ever laughing sun
