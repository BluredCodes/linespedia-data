Do you not hear the song of the birds?
Can’t you hear their sweet song slowly changing
The beauty of their music rivalled only by the setting of the evening sun
Their song never changing while all around them fails to stay the same
The beautiful birds sing their song whilst all around them dance
But wait all is not as seems if you look closely and not glance 

Butterflies dancing in the sky
Being scorched by the sun
Soon they will have to die
Cannot you see they have not fun
Only pain until come fourth the rain to take their pain away
The water eases their pain but slowly tears away at their beautiful colours

Suddenly everything stops
But their song remains the same, only now quiet
Saddened by what fills their fragile minds
The butterflies no longer dance as they are fooled into the sky
Now the butterfly sings a song, but he does not know why

‘These evil demons have tricked us my friends’
‘But wait it’s too late, now we must meet our end’

The evil vultures scream their song a voice to guide the blind
But again the birds have tricked him for his home he’ll never find
You listened to smiling you stood the whole day through 
When in their world of insanity they were watching you

Do not trust these demons they are devils in disguise
I’m afraid my friend they’ve killed again right before your eyes
What can you do to stop song kill them and go to hell
Many of us just sing along, now we are sick as well
