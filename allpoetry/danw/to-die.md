---
title: "To Die"
writer: "Danw"
slug: "to-die"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

You must die in my world
In my mind

You will suffer in my head
Do the time
Paybacks are a bitch
A bitch is what she is

Dying in my head 
I must be dead

Extensions of the thoughts in my soul
Change my thoughts death, suffering enrole

Succession of pain, the nightmare trapped
With the eyes of the whores of jericho

Miscellaneous ramblings, can’t tell me what to do
Can’t tell me what to say
You waste your time, You waste your time
And time is not to be wasted
Sooner or later your gonna have to face it
To walk in my shoes you gotta do up the laces

You can’t teach a blind man to see
Trying to teach a dead man to be
In your mind you got the belief
But your eyes were born to deceive

If you don’t see me, then I don’t care
Just another victim, crumbling to your death
But I will never believe
I will never believe
I will never believe.... In you

You cannot see
The best of me
Until you take the rest of me
