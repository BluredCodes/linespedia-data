I sit here waiting for the inevitable
What lies in store for me
I imagine what will happen and I can see
That the course of event seem unavoidable

On a night of celebration disaster strikes me
With a firm grasp on my neck I am thrown to the ground
But who is this?
A tall brave stranger
A friend to rival the beast before me
He puts his pride aside and stands tall in support

But that was before
Now I feel the day of redemption is at hand
Perhaps I too can escape lives doomed circle and enter the safety of confinement
Everywhere I look the beast awaits me, no matter where I hide
Time is against me, waving it’s ever present arm to stop my progression
The meaning of life?, perhaps to receive what you deserve
‘Do unto others as they do to you’, a certainty for pain
Hate consumes the beasts body as it prepares to destroy me

The wise man awaits his fate with a plan in his all powerful mind
Perhaps the beast has a heart, or perhaps I can appeal to it’s needs
And provide him with natures liquid cancer
Time will tell, albeit sooner or later
As the once safe man became no more than a hunted animal
With only his mind to save him
