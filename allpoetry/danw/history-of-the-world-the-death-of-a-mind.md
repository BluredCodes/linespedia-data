The world in my mind , my world is my mind!
And in that world all is controlled except time, 
Time is control itself and we are all a slave to it.
Open your mind and embrace god?
Close your mind and hide from the world that you control.
Language is speech is spoken is what once was now broken

I can’t hear so how can you be talking to me , without sound what is reality?
Without reality what is matter or mind or even sanity?
Pain is what we fear and to some what they cheer but inside pain I fear
So close my mind and leave the world behind.

Take a long walk with your mind and you will be changed with what you find
Only thoughts that pictures could portray a dream/vision so real that it surpasses and becomes life itself only to fool your body into death.
I’ve seen faces before and recognise them again although forever blind I see all.
