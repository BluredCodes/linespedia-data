---
title: "Different"
writer: "Danw"
slug: "different"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I lost track of time the day I lost my mind
I fear the unknown day will come again
To take my soul,
To take the rest
But it missed it's chance 
There's nothing left of me

So you'll take a hole
Once filled with me
Now the hole is dirty
And without a home

Sometimes I feel I want to kill myself for you
Just to prove myself to you
