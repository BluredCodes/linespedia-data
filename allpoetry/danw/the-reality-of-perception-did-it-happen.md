title: "(The Reality Of Perception - Did It Happen?)"

If a tree falls and no-one is their to here it did it fall at all?
When brave heroes of war come home to their once peaceful homes
How must they feel to hear such corrupted words spoken from their loved ones
Disrespect oozing out of every crack in the wall
Did it even happen at all?

War time veteran becomes no more than just the hero of the day
His friends who fought on and lived to see him prevail respect him
But what of others, surely ‘The scars I bear are for my patriotism and fighting”
As with the tree only few can see that this man, this hero has lost more than his limbs
Somehow by defending his homeland, respecting his queen and loving his family
He has become both mentally and physically less of a man

You can not petition this mans intentions once held by ignoring his sacrifice
If the man bears the scars of war, he not only fought but he became a hero

Just as when the tree falls, it does not fall silent
The tree comes crashing down to an almighty thud that sends shock waves through the ground into the heart and soul of everyone around it.
Does the tree not provide oxygen, oxygen allows us to live
The tree is our hero and as heroes fall side by side they may be not visible
But the respect felt for them will forever scar their people mentally
