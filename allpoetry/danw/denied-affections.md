---
title: "Denied Affections"
writer: "Danw"
slug: "denied-affections"

Just a view of you and my heart is in my mouth
Mumblings of the words of the words that can’t come out

Beauty is not enough of a word for you
But somehow, just a glimpse a my chance with you is through

If it’s not my fault, I’d like to know just whose it is
Who’s had me going through all this
If it’s not my fault why can’t I take you home

Memory of her with me
Only in my dreams I see
Maybe I could take her out
Squeeze emotions, get them out

It’s not time today,
It’s never time
It’s just the way, she doesn’t say anything

Her smile is reflected in my heart
My confidence behind so far
Her hair, waterfalls from the rainbow sun
I wish I was the only one

But can’t happen
Im not in heaven
Her holy land
Not for this man

Until the devil has his day
Has his filthy way
Maybe just of wonder
To fulfil my endless hunger

Shy like child I run away
See my hero everyday
Never shamed he gets hit way
Why not me?
I feel like running home, just to be alone
The opposite it seems
To what I’ve hoped and dreamed
Maybe, someday!

Reaching out and giving in
Can’t seem to find the strength within
Maybe I am like the child
Feel so strong but I have to hide

Dreams of empty heaven
Fields of you and me

When I see her dreaming, I feel so brave
But I can feel their laughter, so I run away

Some things were meant to be
Some broken dreams were meant to be
Some things weren’t meant to be
A parted sky for her and me
Just weren’t meant to be!

I cut to the chase forgive my gruesome face
Relieved now that I believe anything, everything
Everything is worth a chance, Take the evenings final dance
