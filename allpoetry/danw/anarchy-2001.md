---
title: "Anarchy 2001"
writer: "Danw"
slug: "anarchy-2001"

---

Got your high school kids, 
With their high school tongues
Kindergarten killed by his high school gun
But he didn’t know the truths
The storm took him when it came
Kid with a gun he killed someone but whose really to blame?

Left home at fifteen and tried to make his name
But by four O’clock he’d never be the same
Took his fingers from his pockets
And his bullet from his gun

Killing in the name of all that is innocent
Never meant to be more than an incident
Now the jailers, judge they have they key
To his life’s remains, his memory

Left home at seventeen he tried to love he prayed
Maybe that he’d come home someday
He’d come home, but it’s too late

Left prison at sixty from beginning to end
More of man than me and now he see’s
The world around him changes, 
Filled with unfamiliar unrecognised faces

But he can’t do nothing, that’s the way has to be
Jailed at seventeen, imprisoned from the start
Juvenile delinquency, you know he’s not the one
This isn’t justice It’s Anarchy 2001

They say that prison changed him, 
But the streets are who they blame
And from the minute they let him out
He waits for death but he takes his time

To take another life
No child, full grown
So sad another life
The life he took, his own!
