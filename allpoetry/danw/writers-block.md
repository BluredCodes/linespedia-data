Writers block,
Keeps staring at the clock
His analogy becomes reality, 
Confused, interpretations miss-used

Got to get the ideas flowing,
For the populations they are growing

Can’t find the words to say,
To help myself
Express myself
Confess to myself, I’ve hit the bottom of the rock
I’m stuck......

Can’t find the words to say,
To help myself
Express myself
Confess to myself, I’ve hit the bottom of the rock
I’m stuck, with writers block

I’ve lost a part of my memory,
Could this possibly be the end of me
Wake up in the middle of the night, 
What I feel It just ain’t right
I don’t want to think I want to write

Sometimes realise
I can only be as good
As I’ll let me
Give me reason,
And words to make a stand
And I’ll write the words I feel and let them break through

Break through,
Break through the block
I look up the clock and it’s spinning
The time is wasting but winning

Have to find the remedy,
Restoration of my memory
If I write then I can be a man,
Give me your patience and you’ll understand
