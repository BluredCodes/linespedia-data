title: "A Picture Of Life"

A picture of what used to be
The sun it shines unusually.....
The grass as green as polluted sea
The trees trembling like a child in the street at night

A hollowed house unoccupied, 
Like the derelict buildings which give homes tonight
A picket fence around the house
Like barbed wire fence would surround the law

It’s sky a shade of blue,
Only seen in the eyes of something new
The aged and torn corners of the old picture
Represent all that is wrong in the world
