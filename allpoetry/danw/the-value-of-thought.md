---
title: "The Value Of Thought"
writer: "Danw"
slug: "the-value-of-thought"

Can I be the hero just today?
Feel my wrath, as I turn to stone
Would you care to hear what is said
By the people who once were dead

Just today, can I be
All that I am, all that I need
All that I see, Oh can I be?

Why be sorry, when you did nothing wrong
Why worry, about writing a stupid song, for me
Would you mind to tell me why?
When the sun shines on you,  the rain seems to drown me out

Whispering quietly dying out the screams of those who care
About whets thrown away, maybe it’s best that way

Maybe that’s the best way,
Maybe that’s the best way,
Maybe it’s the best way

Pitying the poor, it’s for the best
Worship those with more, It’s the only way
Praying to the dead, Perhaps it’s best that way
Your wasting away, opportunity

Apologies, the value of the fly
Lies in shit and then it dies
