---
title: "Meet Again?, Goodbye!"
writer: "Danw"
slug: "meet-again-goodbye"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/45331-Meet-Again---Goodbye--by-Danw"
---

I’m the next to go, without a shout aloud
To siren where and why, I go
I can’t comeback, I can only go forward
Can’t look back

Dying from the inside, 
I’m loving things that I’ve already said
Crying on the inside
I’m thinking things that I’d already said

I didn’t get all I could get,
But I ain’t living in regret
Just so long, my beautiful friend
I love you now, as ink to a pen

And now comes the end,
Don’t forget me, just say 
“We’d forever love him anyway”
I still love you, forever, always

If Im alive to see the 4
I’ll live to be higher
If I alive, I’d live to inspire
If I can’t seem to take much more

Don’t forget, the way
Where we in heaven met
