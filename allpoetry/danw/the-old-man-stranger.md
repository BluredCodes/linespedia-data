---
title: "The Old Man Stranger"
writer: "Danw"
slug: "the-old-man-stranger"

Strangest names in the strangest places
Confused looks on the simplest faces
I had to ask them, I just had to know
Where is this place that you have to go?

His answer was the strangest thing
A guitar man would ever sing
This is what he said

My back is broken, but my spine is strong
Now I’ve spoken I must sing my song

Take back your money, I don’t need it any way
I sit here streets just praying everyday
That my time will come, next time be the one
Yes, I’ll be the one

I returned to that place it must have been a thousand years
All the people were different and they didn’t want to hear
So they took my story, sold for glory in the only way they 
Pawned my wisdom for glory
Just like the old man had foretold, He was the one

Angels, fallen Angels falling
He was the one

Truly I’ve been thunderstruck today
The old man once spoke to me now he’s me
Lost my youth and I can’t find it today
Somebody took it and they hid it away
