---
title: "If I Die Tomorrow"
writer: "Danw"

If I died tomorrow...
Would you say you loved me?
If I died today would you have the time?

If I died tomorrow would you mark the day?
Would you remember all the things I used to say?
About life being nothing, just something in the way?

If I said I loved you, would you go away?
I was drowned out in my sorrow
Now I've gone away

If I died tonight,
Would you take the time to say hello to heaven?
You'd care then, but you didn't before
Like my conscience, you ignored

If I die, I'll take a piece of you with me
I'll do it in my dreams and in my sleep
I will watch you from above when you break down
I will look upon your crying eyes and laugh at my grave

Living like this, leaves me paralysed within
Laughing at you when I'm gone
At your every sin

Today I left you space inside
Now you know the price of my life
