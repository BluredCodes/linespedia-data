title: "You"
writer: "Danw"
slug: "you"

Treat you like shit, I can’t do that
I’d rather watch you as you sleep
And dream of the life you want
Better that than the things you keep

Eyes like diamonds, priceless
Yet more valuable than any mere miracle
Hair like soft golden waves gently pressing against each other
Dependent on you to be their mother

Your lips like Ice,
Your words so cold
But the feelings nice

And I’m spent
