title: "Slave To Forget"
writer: "Danw"

You're a slave to your friends,
You're a slave to mine
You're a slave to money, 
You're a slave to your mind

You're a slave to thinking 
And you're a slave to drinking
Your a slave to your money, you friends and your mind
Your a slave to them man and your wasting my time

Believe in Jesus, he’s your saviour
Believe in life down under, believe Australia
Believe in all the things you care to think you know
Correct the man, when he’s wrong, I told you so

Well, forget about money
Forget about your worries and forget about mine
Your living in a date now get with the time
Run fast, but don’t hurry my friend

Drink from the bottle, until you drink to the end
The end of time, end of your mind
Enter your mind, your mind is blind
