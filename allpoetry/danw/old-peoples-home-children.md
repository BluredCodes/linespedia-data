title: "(Old Peoples Home - Children)"

Open the doors and let him in
He will not harm you for he is the only one
He slumps crippled in his wheel chair as small demons laugh at his youth now lost
They laugh at a hero now worn and tired, his achievements provide a sedative
But their laughs burn his tortured soul, his body weak his face withered

He prepares to die respectfully and quietly
Since the evil ones have diminished his pride to a state of depression
‘Ha ha ha’ the children laugh as the old hero fails to reach his goal in time
‘He’s pissed himself’, he cries internally as he hears their thoughts
For one last chance at pride he stands tall and smiles at the children
With a finger extended and held high
The old man leaves for eternal rest in the knowledge that he will have forever.....
