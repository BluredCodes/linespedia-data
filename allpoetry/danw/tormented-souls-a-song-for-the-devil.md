Welcome to the man with different faces,
A lover to the family of which he disgraces,
He laughs when they cry .
To make them learn
He hides as they die
To watch them burn

To god we pray in desperate measures	
Gone insane with the pain of pleasure
Death we pay, is the price for life		
Livin’ on the edge of the blade of a knife

Watching people die for leisure
Tormenting of their souls brings pleasure
Your covers keep the demons out	
But who is their to hear you shout

Close your eyes and hide your fear
Close your mind cos’ the truth is here

Lights on , Lights off 
Can’t get away
The memories gone
But the pain remains

Lights on , Lights off 
Can’t stop the pain
You can wash it away
But still it stains

Close your eyes , and open your mind
To he who seeks , The beasts he finds
Never get away can’t leave it behind
You cannot escape the demons in your mind

In your mind
You know you can’t stop.........

Lights on , Lights off
Come beg on your knees,
There’s no escape ,
On your fear it feeds

The beast , come feed the beast
Feed the beast
Yes , Feed the beast
The beast in your mind
