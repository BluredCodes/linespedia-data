Shadows lurking everywhere
Wondering why they can’t see their eyes
Why they can’t see, the things to make them see

Candles burning in the darkest room
Flame is saying that he dreamt of a day
A day, that he would dream they’d put him out

Ghost is yearning to find a friend
Not visible but his thoughts pretend
He needs someone, he needs

Alarming as it may seem, I never wanted you to dream

To leave me again, would be a sin
Why sleep again?
To leave me again, would be a sin
Why sleep again?

Why are flames burning without any air?
Shadows lurking still but theirs no-one there
The ghost is crying now, putting out the flame
It doesn’t bother them but he still needs her

Man is dying by the side of the road
This man is dying, why? he just doesn’t know
Perhaps one day he will, never be alone

But now he is well aware that the flame is out
Now he is well aware that the flame is out
Now he’s out

But to leave me again, would be a sin
And now she’s leaving again, 
Now she leaves me again
And to leave me again, it would be a sin
