Hmm
What was that?

It was life!, Did you enjoy it?

Yes, or at least I think so

Oh?

Well, It was a little tasteless at times and some of it was hard to swallow

Would you like some more?

Perhaps later, I’ve had enough for now
So this life of yours

Yes!

Did it cost you much?

The cost was greater than you could ever imagine sir

Would it be possible to have some more then?

Yes sir,
It is invaluable but easily reproduced, sometimes wrongly produced or not intended!

Indeed, but what of my family?

What of them Sir?

May they have some,?

They are having it now!
They’ll be up to see you when they are finished, they may finish at different times though Sir

That’s ok, I look forward to seeing them all again

Are you full Sir?

My stomach feels full yet I know it is empty

I must admit Sir, I put a little more spice in your children’s life, I hope you don’t mind

No that’s perfectly okay, as long as they enjoy it
It seems I have an addiction, my hunger for life grows

Im very sorry Sir, I have ran out of life for you

That’s okay, a man can only have so much after all!
