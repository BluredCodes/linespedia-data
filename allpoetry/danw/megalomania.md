---
title: "Megalomania"
writer: "Danw"
slug: "megalomania"

I’m not demented, I was just deprived
From cradle to the future, I’m a present day child
As I watched you dying, I thought I’d survive
Let my caged bird go and watch him fly

Meets the horizon, Tries to do more
Tries to be like me, and falls to the floor
Explosive like volcanoes, I’m still in control
Feel my wrath and watch me roll

I am man perfected, Can’t be anymore
Unlike all you people, 
Scratching and fighting for a place on the floor

By my light your blinded, 
To see what lies beyond
Without me your not guided
In a cold dark room without a light

Im not scared of fear, in fact I’m fear itself
Yes, you’ll learn my lesson
I’ll beat you, teach you
Learn you well, learned to well

Now learn this lesson son,
And you’d better learn it well
I’m a man, a machine, unstoppably mean
You can’t beat me, not even close
