---
title: "Epic Reduction"
writer: "Danw"
slug: "epic-reduction"
source: "AllPoetry"

The violent storms raged outside
Inside the peaceful sons sing
The old man divorced his sins
As the young girl became one with her love

Today it was the eternal pain of life
Yesterday it had never been so uncertainly sweet
The sun rose to give them life
As the moon fell upon their heads like the gallows of time

The homesteaders ascend the great mountain
Visitors descend into the open space
As the rain beats down on the bleeding ground
The sun above heals their wounds

The literary visionary stood tall
A hunched dwarf hid his loose knowledge

I wouldn’t say it’s love, I just hate everybody but you
In a storm I see just where I’m going to,
But I can’t see the rain
