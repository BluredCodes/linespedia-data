Fuck the minority, screw the majorities
I don’t take orders from anyone
Drive blindfolded with my hands in my pockets
I don’t need no respect for life

Your obsessions, hate my direction
Withheld aggression aimed at you
I just want something to see me
I just want to kiss it goodbye

Class recession my confession
The order of above was failed by me
Bring the class down, tear the walls down
I’ll do it with my teeth

I can’t see, nothing to stop me
I won’t be someone who follows suit
It’s too small for me, What I wear, I feel free
Predetermined occupation, 
Mom and dad didn’t leave you far from the.....
Proverbial tree

It’s not like rain is to seasons
I can’t find someone to reason
With me

All I seek is, good behaviour
And a little advice, on the things that make me happy
Or put my head in a vice
And I’m not happy, with the rest of them

It’s not easy, acting responsibly
For others at it is to me
They seem content living the pack of cards
And each of their hands, the same to me

They just need, someone to free them
They just need, some independence
The worlds corrupted, ‘cos I’m....Interrupted
By everybody but myself

Wolfs in packs can’t seem to remember
Why they’re attached to a lead
Birds in box’s, can’t remember why the gun makes them bleed
I just need someone to see them, I just need someone to die for them

Bleed, bleed, bleed, bleed, bleed??????,
