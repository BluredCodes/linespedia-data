The thunderous lightening erupts like a volcano of fury as it explodes in the sky
This magnificent occasion marks the sensation felt by both you and I in love
The creatures in the dark shadows stand still because we both are too young to die
Together the tranquillity of our timeless love shall always be enough
Time is on my wrist but not in my hands
Soon the time will come for us to pass our eternal torch of light to a new generation
But not before elevating ourselves to extreme levels to excite our fans

Our time has come, the time has come
Now we our one, we two are now one
But first..... lets have some fun


Our river has run dry, now we too must die
Beneath the ground we two must lie
Goodbye my sweet loving earth, goodbye to a world not sane
Now dead and forgotten, We will rise again

Our people have watched us die, watched us enter eternal sleep through flames if hate
They care not and look on laughing with no remorse
Resurrection they must die of course

You must die when I say die, so die right here right by my side now die
You must die I heard you lie, so die right now I hear your cries
This my friend is not The End, but your end

The slow dark ocean washes their young unfulfilled corpses out to sea
As the ocean unleashes hell upon their once healthy bodies
Swimming so quietly, Singing so silently Dying so violently

New life is thrust upon the world, I have returned but what of my bride
Awake
A spirit appears
‘I was cold amidst the cold night with the drums beating a song into my heart’
The music of a returning mortal, 
People shaking at the knees, Now disturbing their peace
Let the violence cease, The mouse ate the cheese!

War - Gunned them down
Love - Broke them down
Hate - Knocked them down
Slavery - Forced them down
With not a frown pushed them down to our fair town.



The kingdom is safe my friends but the war not over
We gather here today to sacrifice and put our lives in jeopardy
For the love of our slain king
The men rode off into the night and under poisoned influence did they sing

Pollution has corrupted the purity of our fair planet, It’s icecaps meting away
In our short existence we have killed each other from rival to brother
We have made rules and broken each and every one
Perhaps our time has come is this The End?
