title: "(Dancing Angels - Social)"

Upon entering the grand hall way excitement filled my body
My body was temporarily paralysed with the electricity that filled the air
The music’s deafening song embraced all those who strayed upon it

The exotic floor conducted the movements of the wonderful people
They danced into the night as Angels with no reason to exist but to dance
Dancing alone but at the same time always together
The lights lit their musical catwalk as each one showed their prowess
One by one the mystical creatures shone like shooting stars

Like birds set free, they flew across their golden highway
But wait..... The music’s over!
The dream is corrupted with the foul aftermath of a once beautiful night
As the music diminishes the once golden creatures crawl to escape
Now drunken with their own sense of mortality
Fear fills their once confident bodies and their outer shells give off bad emotion

The man who had provoked these demons and once friends was filled
Filled only with the poison that he thought would bring him closer
Closer to the very people whom now despise him, why such a change?
As the Angels were dancing one golden and mysterious creature was not present
The emotion in the room changed as his heart began to shatter leaving only poison

With the poison in control the man unleashed his rage upon poor unsuspecting angels
With his pride and reputation diminished, he was slain by the beast
As the sweet Angels flew off to their domains in the clouds
When will my Angel come home?
