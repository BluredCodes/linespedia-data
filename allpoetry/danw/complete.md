Knowledge like rain,
Dropped into his open collective mind
To fear he was no stranger
Strange, that fear he left behind

He marvelled at the sun
His vision to become
One
He lived while he was young
Gone

Lost can’t be the way
No signs, just feelings
No traffic just confusion
On the highway of the mind

Thoughts of life became madness
All thoughts now left behind
