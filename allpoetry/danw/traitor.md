---
title: "Traitor"
writer: "Danw"
slug: "traitor"

Don’t destroy yourself in spite,
Destroy others for what they have become
For what you have made them become

Unprecedented unattainable heights,
The heights of simplicity, out of reach for the feeble
Feeble minded, strongly built

Humour taught to beast stands tall,
Forgets his mentor, he’s nothing at all
Hatred replaces dislike, crime is unkind
Kill the innocent and obey the guilty

My best friends in life are inanimate, their mine
My life is pointless, but it’s my time to waste, my time

I never said I liked you!, Because I don’t
Never did, never should, never have, never could

Wasting my time, wasting my breath
Not on you, I’d rather be death
