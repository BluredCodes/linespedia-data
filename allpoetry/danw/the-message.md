There’s a message in the street,
But It don’t tell you what to do
It’s a warning sign when your feeling blue
A life support when there’s nothing to do

It’s love

There’s a message in the face,
Of the people that you meet,
Of the homeless children, 
Dying in the street

It’s love

There’s a man upon the floor,
Who couldn’t take it anymore
Left his wife and kids 
He’s gone away

With his beautiful children
In the street they lay
With a message

It’s love

There’s a message in my words,
In the song of all birds the singing
There’s a message…
It’s love!
