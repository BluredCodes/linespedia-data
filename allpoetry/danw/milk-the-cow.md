---
title: "Milk The Cow"
writer: "Danw"

Milk the cow
Feed the need
Spike the vein

Feel the buzz
Lap the high
Take a day
Walk a mine within' your head

Snort the line
Glaze your eyes
Lose your mind

This ain't shit
I doubt you
You doubt me
You doubt yourself

Light a J
Reach the high
Slow me down
So I feel alive
