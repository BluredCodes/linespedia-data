Everyday your body dies
Cigarettes, corruption lies
Speak too soon you lose
Your life

A privilege soon withdrawn
Respect for imminent death
Date (still to be announced)
And all must still die
And although you cry 
Nothing can be done
Your just another one

Tragedy turned to statistics
Jokes of death makes them sadistic

Let’s do it again
Do it again

Life dropping down, beneath ground to overhead
Win, ing nothing but ceased existence
