---
title: "Time Out!"
writer: "Danw"
slug: "time-out"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/45320-Time-Out--by-Danw"
---

If time is valued
Then value me
For all I've wasted
Not up to me

Time takes time
And time takes you and me
For a joy ride ensuring that we're never free
Time is a joy ride, crashed into a willow tree

Time may be money,
But neither can I see
Except my aged...
Reflection killing me

Time says your pregnant,
Time says your dying
Time is the difference
Between confinement and joy

I have no joy left,
No life for time to destroy
What man created,
I just can't take it
