-Before The War

One...Two...Three....Four
Walking proudly out the door  
Five...Six.....Seven.....Eight
I and friends go off to war
One...Two...Three....Four
Drinking gin the day before
Five...Six.....Seven.....Eight
With ladies war makes men more

-During The War

Shut up two...three....four
Sending soldiers off to war
Dead alive six....seven.....eight
A dead man rest upon my plate
Shut up two...three....four
From his mouths his lungs did pour
Dead alive six....seven.....eight
Surely this cannot be fate

Shut up two...three....four
Somebody get me out of hear
Dead alive six....seven.....eight
To save my soul or is it too late
Shut up two...three....four
I see my friends lie on the floor
Dead alive six....seven.....eight
They aren’t moving any more

Shut up two...three....four
Coughing, now my throats so sore
Dead alive six....seven.....eight
I cough my blood upon the floor
Shut up two...three....four
Time is running out for me
Dead alive six....seven.....eight
With only bullets to set me free

Shut up two...three....four
Dead alive six....seven.....eight

Shut up two...three....four
‘We honour soldiers lost at war’
Dead alive six....seven.....eight
‘These young men who lived before’
Shut up two...three....four
‘Their mothers hearts aching and sore’ 
Dead alive six....seven.....eight
‘No longer with them anymore’
