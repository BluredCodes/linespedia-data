You told me to call you
But I never did
I was afraid of being true
So I curled up and hid
I didn't know
What to say to you
I still don't
Know what to do
And I am bleeding
My blood is red
I wish I chould understand
All of those things we said
But I can't
And I cna't get you outta my head
You sau that you love me
You need me forever
Am I the only one you want
Will we stay together
And why do you not talk to me
In front of your friend
And then when we're alone
Our conversation never ends
When it's just us on the phone
We don't have to pretend
You say I don't need anyone
Not a single soul
Just a promise that the next day brings the sun
And a little bit of control
But I'm locked in this car
With no brakes
No steering wheel
I've come too far
For a heart break
I'm not made of steel
And if I love you today
Will you love me tomorrow
Just promise me you'll stay
Don't bring me sorrow
So when you ask me what I'm doing
Over the phone
I'm thinking of these things
So I don't feel alone
