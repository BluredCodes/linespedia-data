---
title: "I'm Only Lonely"
writer: "Merules36"

Look at me 
Here all alone
I'll neve be free
My body's turned to stone
I'm without you now
I want to get you back
But I just don't know how

I'm only lonely
When you're gone
I'm only lonely
When I can't go on
I'm only lonely 
When I sing my song
I'm only lonely

I wish that you could understand
Everything about me
No one else can
But, oh God please
Take me from my lonliness
Take me from this place
Take me from the hollowness and the sad look face

I'm only lonely
When you're gone
I'm only lonely
When I can't go on
I'm only lonely 
When I sing my song
I'm only lonely

The page is blank
But everything's on my mind
I've got nowhere to go
But I got all the time
I guess I'll be okay
When I walk off alone
It's alright cause, I got nothing to say

I'm only lonely
When you're gone
I'm only lonely
When I can't go on
I'm only lonely 
When I sing my song
I'm only lonely
