I've sailed across the sea
To rescue you
Now you're leaving me
And I don't know what to do
You just walked out the door
No hello, no goodbye
We can work it out
Or we can at least try
Bring me hope
Bring me pain
Start to cope
And stop the rain
New and strange
Yes it will be different
It's time to move on
It's time to change
Time to change my heart
It's been awhile
But it's still so new
Cause I'm starting to smile
