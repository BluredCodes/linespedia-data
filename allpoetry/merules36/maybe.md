You get outta the car
On this lonely, Friday night road
You said it should've never gone this far
And now I am alone
Driving on those dark, dark streets
Those yellow lines over and over again
They lead me to where your heart beats
And you won't let me in

Maybe I should turn around
Maybe it's time to let you go
Maybe I should just break it down
Maybe I should let you know

There's no souviners for this trip
Nothing but painful phone calls
Everything surrounds me
In the same four walls
The streetlights map out my directions
But I only wanna go to you
You're my only sweet obsession
And I'll give anything to you

Maybe I should turn around
Maybe it's time to let you go
Maybe I should just break it all down
Maybe I should let you know
