---
title: "What Do They Know"
writer: "Merules36"
slug: "what-do-they-know"

If you could only see
How much I love you
With your eyes of blue
Your hair of gold
I want to spend my life with you
Until I'm peaceful and old

They say we're only children
They say we can't know what love is
But they'll never know when they fall in
Cause we've already fallen

Watch us grow up
And stay together forever
Even when my eyes are shut
I know we'll be together

I need you in my blood
I need your heart to keep
I need your love
I need to be in your dreams when you sleep

They don't know anything
And I don't care what they say
What do they know about our love
Anyway
