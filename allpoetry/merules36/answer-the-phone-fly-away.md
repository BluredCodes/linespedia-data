title: "Answer The Phone (Fly Away)"
writer: "Merules36"

I look for a direction
But the burnt map
Flew out the window
Destination
Unknown
And I don't know
Just answer the phone

Fly away
Like a kite
Fly all day
And through the wild night

Take me
To a higher place
Everything's so perfect
With the sweet honey taste
I wanna get you alone
Just you 
And me
Just answer the phone

Fly away
Like a kite
Fly all day
And through the wild night

I reach my hand
Into the sky
I bring down the stars for you
And you still say I never try
I wanna go home
To where I belong
I'll sing my song
If you just answer the phone

Fly away
Like a kite
Fly all day
And through the wild night
