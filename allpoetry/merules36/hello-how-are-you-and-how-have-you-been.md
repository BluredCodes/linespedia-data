Hello 
How are you
How have you been
I'm standing outside
So please let me in
I love you 
I need you
Don't treat me bad
I love you 
I need you
Yet you make me sad
I'm running in circles
Lap after lap
I walk in the hall
And give your shoulder a tap
I say hello
How are you
I'll wait for your call
Why do I let you
Break and bend me
I am just so confused
You say you love me too
And I dream of you at night
When I'm alone in my bed
I hope that you're alright
That I'm the only one in your head
Please forgive me
For all that I have done
Baby, for me
You're the only one
So today when I see you
We'll say again
Hello 
How are you
And how have you been
