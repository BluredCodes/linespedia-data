---
title: "When I Look At You"
writer: "Merules36"
slug: "when-i-look-at-you"

I like to watch you breathe
I like to watch you live
I don't know why
I look, dream and sigh
I wish I could have you
And you to love me
I am so in love with you
If you could only see

I'd die if I could touch you
And kill for just one look
I'd carve off my skin if you knew
That when I look at you
I fall in love

I've got a crush on you
A secret little longing
All I wanna do
Is see you
I see you everyday
And you say hello
And I want to stay
There's something you don't know

I'd die if I could touch you
And kill for just on look
I'd carve off my skin if you knew
That when I look at you
I fall in love

I get up early in the morning
To come and see you
Just looking at you makes me happy
And if you only knew
I'd give up every breath
If that's what you need
I'd do anything for you
I'd cut myself to bleed

I'd die if I could touch you
And kill for just one look
I'd carve off my skin if you knew
That when I look at you
I fall in love
