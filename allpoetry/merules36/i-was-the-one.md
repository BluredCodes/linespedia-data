---
title: "I Was The One"
writer: "Merules36"
slug: "i-was-the-one"
source: "AllPoetry"

I was the one
Who made the wrong
I was the one
Who was never strong
I was the one
Who always wanted you
I was the one
Who was never true
I was the one
Who made this mistake
I was the one
Who caused all this ache
I was the one
Who walked through this
I was the one
Who made this wish
I was the one
Who should've known better
I was the one
Who wrote that letter
I was the one
Who made this mess
I was the one
Who had to confess
I was the one
Who really loved you
I was the one
Who actually thought it was true
