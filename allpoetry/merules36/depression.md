This isn't 1920
Or 1925
This is just a normal day
In my everyday life
I can't eat at times
And I can never sleep
In my dark mind
My dark thoughts I keep

No one really knows
The pain
Inside
All my symptoms show
I'm insane
And its something
I cannot hide

It's a scratching that won't leave
Me alone
Begging me
Screaming at me
Yet still goes unknown

I really can't explain it
And they tell me
"You're sick."
This is my life
And I can't change it
