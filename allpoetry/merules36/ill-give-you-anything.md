---
title: "I'll Give You Anything"
writer: "Merules36"
slug: "ill-give-you-anything"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/23635-Ill-Give-You-Anything-by-Merules36"
---

The sun goes down
And in this bed
I lay
With you at my side
With you in my head

All the silver stars
I make them shine for you
And the silver moon
I make him come
Everynight
Just like the yellow sun

And you come to me
You look into my eyes
You and your disguise
And ask for more
And I'll give you anything
In this world
I'll give you anything

I drain my blood
Kiss the earth
Swim through the floods
And still there dearth
Left with you
Instead of one
You want two

And you come to me
You look into my eyes
You and your disguise
And ask for more
And I'll give you anything
In this world
I'll give you anything

And now I'm blind
So you can see
And there's all your dreams
Faster than the speed of light
And I've given you everything I have
Tonight

And you come to me
You look into my eyes
You and your disguise
And ask for more
And I'll give you anything
In this world
I'll give you anything
