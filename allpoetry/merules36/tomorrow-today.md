I'm trapped in a room with no windows
I can see nothing from here
I'm not living my life anymore
I'm waiting for everything in fear
Fear from the truth
Fear of myself
Fear of you
Fear of Hell

The lights fade down
As dusk turns from dawn
I'll come around
I'll keep moving on
Cause I can't feel a thing
I gotta think about tomorrow today
