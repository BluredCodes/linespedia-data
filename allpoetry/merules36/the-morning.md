---
title: "The Morning"
writer: "Merules36"
slug: "the-morning"
source: "AllPoetry"

Walking on the beach
In the early morning sun
The pinks, the purples, and the blues
The waves shoot up
Like a bullet from a gun
The silence is frightening
But it makes me feel peace
The cold morning wind biting
At me for release
I sit down
In the sand
I look around
And think about
God's plans

Lying with you
In the early morning warmth
There's nothing to do
As we watch the sky
Black
Grey
Pink and blue
I think about the past
And how lucky I am
The world
It moves so fast
I have worked so hard 
With my own hands
I never thought I would
But now I know I can

I look out the window
And see all the busy people
I wonder if they know
How to feel
They're always running
And driving too fast
I wonder if they stop
And wonder how long
Their lives will last
Take the time to look at the flowers
And watch their children play
In the morning they spend hours
Trying to get to their busy days
Have they looked into the eyes
Of their sons
Thir daughters
They might seem to busy
To even try
