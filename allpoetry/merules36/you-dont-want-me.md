---
title: "You Don't Want Me"
writer: "Merules36"

In the morning when I wake up
I look in the mirror
I try to keep my head up
But I wish that you were here

And I miss you so much 
I can't breathe at night
If I had you to touch
I'd be alright
I see the stars in the sky
And I walk down the street
And I wonder why
You don't want me

What do you want from me
I'll give you love, my heart
And I'll make your dreams come true
I'll give my life to you

And I miss you so much 
I can't breathe at night
If I had you to touch
I'd be alright
I see the stars in the sky
And I walk down the street
And I wonder why
You don't want me

In the middle of the night
I crawl outta my skin
I would be alright
If you would only let me in

And I miss you so much
I can't breathe at night
If I had you to touch
I'd be alright
I see the stars in the sky
And I walk down the street
And I wonder why
You don't want me

I want you to find me deep in your soul
Hold you and love you all through the night
I want you to lift me out of this hole
I want to bring you the sun and the light
