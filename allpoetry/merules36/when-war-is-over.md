---
title: "When War Is Over"
writer: "Merules36"
slug: "when-war-is-over"

Everything's quiet
Since you're not around
We made our choice
To lay it all down
I can't say anything
But I wanna scream
Everything's ruined
All of my dreams

When war is over
When dusk comes from dawn
When the souls are all gone
When we all move on

Everything's over
And you're already gone
I thought it was forever
We can't keep moving on
But it's all done
The sentence has been read
I crawl outta my skin
Just to get outta my head

When war is over
When dusk comes from dawn
When the souls are all gone
When we all move on

The tears still fall
After battles are gone
I gave you my all
I gave you my song
But I'll remember our best days
The days under the sun
I'll remember everything
When we're all done

When war is over
When dusk comes from dawn
When the souls are all gone
When we all move on
