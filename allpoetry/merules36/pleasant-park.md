It's three o'clock in the morning
And everything is fine
It's three o'clock in the morning
And I still got mine
And if things were meant to change
Here after dark
I know right now it seems strange
But it's coming from my heart

My heart
And Pleasant Park
After dark
Hanging around 
Down
In Pleasant Park

We run through the dark
When it's just you and me
We run through my heart
We run where it's free
I'd give up everything
If I could keep this moment forver
And I know that thing
Are gonna get better

My heart
And Pleasant Park
After dark
Hanging around 
Down
In Pleasant Park

And when the crystal blue sky
Falls to the ground
I'll have you with me
I won't make a sound
The night holds me for ranson
And the stars and the sky
I can never see the sun
And I don't know why
