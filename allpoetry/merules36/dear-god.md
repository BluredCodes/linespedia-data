Dear God
Can you hear me
Can you see me
What happens next
What's our destiny
Dear God
Will my dreams come true
Is there anything I should learn how to do
Is everything gonna be alright
Will I make it through the night

Dear God
Can you see the light
What time is it up there
Do you cause all the fights
Do you even care
Dear God
Why do people die
Too much money
Is Heaven really sunny
Will I even know why

Dear God
Why do things go wrong
Is that how it's supposed to be
Should've kept going on
Are you even listening to me
Dear God
Does Jesus really love me
If he asks who wants to know
Just tell him
His friend, me

Dear God
Why is there pain
Is it right that people suffer
Every single day
Just tell me if things will be okay
Dear God
There's so many people
That don't believe in you
Why do they hate someone they've never seen
What will the world do

Dear God
Where are we going
Are all the stories true
Is it true God
That you love me too
