It's bleeding
And crying
And shouting "Help Me!"
It's needing
And dying
Yet no one can see
And piece by piece
I take it apart
Analyze and release
My complicated heart
I don't think I'm depressed
Well, maybe I am
I'm not happy
If I want to be I can
And I never will undersand
Why I hafta break down
I built it with my own hands
It makes my smile and my frown
And I leave it in the dark
For days at a time
But my complicated heart
Knows it's only mine
Why do I have these feelings
Of hate
Of love
My mind always starts the fight
The debate
With my complicated heart
