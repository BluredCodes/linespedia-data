---
title: "Gumby"
writer: "Merules36"

Gumby is a guy
Who has no clue
He makes girls cry
He makes them turn blue
Gumby he thinks he's cool
But maybe he's not
He's the onle who wounded you
He fired off the shots
Gumby he's smart
And he can play it off
But he's breaking girls' hearts
Without a single thought
Gumby's no bright one
No shining star
He pushes her everyday
And makes her life so hard
Gumby is sneaky
He's cruel with his games
He'll break your heart a hundred times
But just saying your name
Gumby you see
Don't mess around
Gumby you see
You're what makes her frown
