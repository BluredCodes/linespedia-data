title: "I Can't Imagine"
writer: "Merules36"

I have touched the beauty of the world
I have seen the eighth wonder cry
I have held the summer in my arms
And I have heard the answer to the question why
I know what the moon is made of
And I know the true meaning of love
And I've tried so many times
But it never plays through
I can't imagine my life without you

It wouldn't seem normal
If you were gone
Never seeing you again
Just seems so wrong
I know the one person
That I'm supposed to be with
I'll be with you always
It's my only wish

I have touched the beauty of the world
I have seen the eighth wonder cry
I have held the summer in my arms
And I have heard the answer to the question why
I know what the moon is made of
And I know the true meaning of love
And I've tried so many times
But it never plays through
I can't imagine my life without you

I don't know how to live
In my world why you're gone
I need you forever
I need you to move on
To wake up in the morning
And not see your face
Could only mean
That I'm still lost
In a dream

I have touched the beauty of the world
I have seen the eighth wonder cry
I have held the summer in my arms
And I have heard the answer to the question why
I know what the moon is made of
And I know the true meaning of love
And I've tried so many times
But it never plays through
I can't imagine my life without you
