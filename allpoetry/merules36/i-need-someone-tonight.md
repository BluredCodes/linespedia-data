---
title: "I Need Someone Tonight"
writer: "Merules36"

You wait for me at night
To tell me all your stories
Stories of love and life
And stories of glory
And the moonlight shines down
To the bright blue and green earth
And the elders tell their sounds
Of how they were here first

I need someone tonight
To come and rescue me
I need someone to make it alright
I need someone to set me free

It's a love and hate game
It's a game of love and lies
The rules are never the same
And you play until you die
It's all about your soul
It's all about you time
It's all about you
It's about what you do
