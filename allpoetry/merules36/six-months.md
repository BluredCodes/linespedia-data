It's been six months
Since you last called
And I wish I could hear your voice
Since your engine stalled
I come home
And I light the candles
I pray to God
I wish I could get a handle
But you've been gone
For so long
I feel so lost
Like I can't go on

You told me at the start
That it wouldn't work out
So I gave you my heart
And you gave me your doubts
Maybe it was just one big game
Who trying to beat who
Every night I whisper your name
Cause I miss you

And now I sit here
Alone in this room
Wish I could take the wheel and steer
With you and the wheel we're bound for doom
I will live
And I will go on
It's all because
You've been gone so long
