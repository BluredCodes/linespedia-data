Take my hand
As we venture the dark
Venture my land
And venture my heart
Everything's okay
As long as you're near
We're leaving today
We're leaving from here


If we're quiet
We can hear the wind
It's a wild night
It's a wild sin
Don't listen to what they say
Cause they don't know
What do they know anyway
About where we wanna go
