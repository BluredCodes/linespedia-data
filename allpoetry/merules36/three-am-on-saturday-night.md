Three AM on Saturday night
She finally decided
It was too hard to fight
She was tired of hiding herself
From the rest of the world
She was stuck in a box
And she had no escape
Time to unlock the locks
In her fragile state

Burnt, bruised, and beaten
Her heart wanted out
There was nothing she could do
The world would soon be dark
As her skin turned pale
And her body cold
A complicated female
Barely sixteen years old

She looked into the mirror
And wrote her letters
Mom this ain't your fault
Dad put yourself in my shoes
Sis leave my room alone
I love all of you

She opened up the bottle
And she took out her knife
Eighteen pills and the blood poured
To end her short life
