We sat there for hours
Contemplating the truth
The only thing I knew
Was I wanted you

It had been so long
Since I'd been in love
So many lonely days and nights
I'd had enough

So I sat there and I spoke to you
"Baby, I love you."
"Baby, I need you."
I read off those five words
"Will you go out with me?"
And I know that's what you heard
So I waited and I waited
About to die
When you said something
That made me cry
"Yes I will."
"I love you too."

Now my life is complete
I don't know what to do
I am so thankful
I have the love of you
