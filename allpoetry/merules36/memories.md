So young and innocent
A different man
If you ever knew him
You'd hate their plan
He is not my brother
Nor was he my friend
But he was God's child
With life brought to an early end

Memories
Of how things used to be
Memories
Of when you were happy
I will miss you
You've helped so many to be true

That world has healed you
As you were killed
That could fence in Laramie
Left your pale face chilled
And as you prayed
God made sure you were saved

Memories
Of how things used to be
Memories
Of when you were happy
I will miss you 
You have helped so many to be true

This shepard that we loved
Has come and gone so fast
To Heaven up above
But out love will still last
Matthew is a hero
Yet still goes unknown
