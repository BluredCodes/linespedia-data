---
title: "Clear The Rain"
writer: "Merules36"

Walking through
This hell
Watching you
Doing so well
I really do try
Everyday
I'm not gonna die
I'm gonna be okay

Clear the rain
And clear the sky
Clear the pain
And wonder why

Getting outta here
Is taking too long
Can't hold you near
In your heart it's wrong
Was it too hard to lie
And to pretend
Was it too hard to try
Cause we both knew it was the end

Clear the rain
And clear the sky
Clear the pain
And wonder why

Don't talk to my friend
And don't talk to you
Everyone pretends
Don't say nothing about you
Walk outside
Just for awhile
Keep feelings inside
And give 'em a smile

Clear the rain
And clear the sky
Clear the pain
And wonder why

I get ready for bed
And turn out the light
See you in my head
And miss you tonight
Pull up the covers
And wonder why
There were so many others
But with you I tried
