The first time I saw you
I knew it was trued
I was meant to be with you
I never said a word
Smile
Hello
Then I look at the stars
They shine a bright yellow 

I would go home
And feel my heart
I knew of my love
That say in the dark
So I said
Get it out of my head
I reheased our conversations
From my heart
Bleeding red

I looked into the mirror
To see my soul
All I saw was a big black hole
No future
No past
Nothing I see
I have you
You have my disguise
It's only your blue eyes

I watch the sunrise
In your arms
I look into your eyes
And see the worlds
Nothing matters except you
Watch the purple sunrise
And know I love you
