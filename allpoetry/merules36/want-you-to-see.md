---
title: "Want You To See"
writer: "Merules36"
slug: "want-you-to-see"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I watch you all day
And wait for you to see
I fall in love everyday
You make me so happy
When I see you in the hall
I try to pretend
You're nothing more to me
Than a real good friend
But I'm lying to myself
I'm lying to you
I beg for your help
I wish that you knew
You're so special to me
You're my favorite guy
Even if we're not "together"
Be my friend til the day I die
Even if you're not mine
I want you to fall in love
I will love you til the end of time
Til I'm taken up above
So I just want you to know
That you mean the world to me
I never want you to go
I just want you to see
