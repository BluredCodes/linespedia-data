Let you go
One more time
Cause I know
That you ain't mine
There's no more kisses
And no more hugs
And now my heart already misses
You
It misses you

Let you go
The last time
Been down so low
And I'm too beaten to climb
I am no longer yours
And you're no longer mine
I guess sometimes people
They run outta time
I look at the clock and I see
You don't love me
We ran outta time

Let you go
To fly away free
And now I know
You and I weren't meant to be
I can't say
That I don't love you
It's okay
Cause we both knew
I watch you walk out the door
And turn to smile one last time
We're not in love anymore
You're no longer mine
