Magic eight ballWhat do you seeWhat does the futureHold for meI can't tell the worldHow I really feelBut in my heart I know my feelingsAre true and realAnd I can't talk to Who I loveAnd can't even see youI've had enoughMy friends know meBetter than anyoneThey say that I don't want thisI'm not happyAnd they're rightI do wishThat I could beAlright With myselfI wish I was okayAnd the caving in wallsFall on meBefore I can runMagic eight ballHow can I be happyWith someoneAnd I'll wait for my callTo set me freeMagic eight ballHow can I be happy
