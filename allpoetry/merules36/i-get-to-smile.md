title: "I Get To Smile"

I used to be happy
I used to smile all the time
Now it seems as though
I'll never reach the end of the line
My eyes have turned
From brown to black
I just want myself
To come back
I can't say I haven't changed
Cause everything about me is different
I feel so strange
My life's twisted and bent
I don't know why
This must be my path
It's too hard to try
It's been so long since I've laughed
And I can't sleep
I'm up all night
If I could release the secrets I keep
I might be alright
I keep running
Forever and a mile
How long until
I get to smile
