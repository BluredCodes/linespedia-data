Your ice cold blue eyes
They wounded me deep in my heart
When I see you I die
You make me stop before I start
And I'm sorry that I did you wrong
I'm sorry that I gave you pain
I wish that I was strong
And I'll never tell your name

Baby, what happened
One day it all just changed
If I could answer that question
Maybe I wouldn't feel so strange
From the first time I saw you smile
I knew you had to be mine
But our love was empty and cold
We ran outta time

I know exactly what I wanna say to you
But don't know how to say it
And after all we've been through
I'm tired
I'll love you forever
And kiss you goodnight
But for now just tell me
That everything will be alright
