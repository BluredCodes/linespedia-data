title: "Drive You Home"

I can't give you everything
I can't change the world
I can't do lots of things
And I can't even sing
I can't make a difference
And I can't fall in love
I can never make sense
But I can drive you home

I can drive you home
Tomorrow or today
If I wasn't so alone
I'd drive you yesterday
And I can feel you in my heart
As the darkness comes down
And in the dark
I know you're coming down

I can't hold you up above the sky
I can't bring world peace
I don't know why
How do you find out these things
I don't know how
To do must things
But right now
I can drive you home
