Another page has been turned
Another book begun
Another fire has burned
Like the red summer sun
You took my hand
And you looked deep into my eyes
You led me to a new place
And then you had to lie

You came and left me
And you're never coming back
THe heart theft
Left me blinded and I can't see
I lie here
Waiting in my circle of death
So much fear
And you came and left

You promised you'd never break my heart
But I guess I always knew
You'd leave me in the dark
But there was just something about you
If you would only trust me
And you could just return
And there's one thing 
That I've learned

You came and left me
And you're never coming back
The heart theft
Left me blinded and I can't see
I lie here
Waiting in my circle of death
So much fear
And you came and left
