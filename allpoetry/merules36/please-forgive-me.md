title: "Please Forgive Me"

I sit and stare
Outta my window
Does anyone really care
Will I ever know
I notice that you
Haven't come today
I don't know what to do
Or what to say
But I'm sorry

So if you're still there
And you can hear my voice
Please come home to me
Please forgive me
I know you don't care
And I know I'm outta your life
You're still the only one I see
Please forgive me

I cry my own blood
Cause I need you soft skin touch
And I swim through the mud
Cause I miss you so much
And I don't know why
I ever hurt you
But I promise I'll try
I promise I'll be true
