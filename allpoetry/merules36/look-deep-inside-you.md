---
title: "Look Deep Inside You"
writer: "Merules36"

You don't look into my eyes
You don't look into my soul
You feed me with your goodbyes
You throw me into a hole
The water falls
As the earth turns
Fire takes walls
As it burns

Look deep inside you
Look deep inside me
Look deep inside the truth
Tell me what you see

You've fought all the demons
You've started over new
You've left me with none
You gave me something so true
You've taught me to live
You showed me how to give
I taught you how to feel
I showed you what was real

Look deep inside you
Look deep inside me
Look deep inside the truth
Tell me what you see
