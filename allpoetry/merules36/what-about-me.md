And what about me
What about what i want
You stole something
And returned memories to ahunt
And you said that it was okay
That I wasn't in trouble
On that day
I never knew what I would get
And you hurt me
And you did me wrong
I was too young to see
What was going on
And what about me
What about what I want
The world can never see
That you cheated
You lied
The undefeated
All at sometime die
And I wear a smile
Like I do my shoes
Both walked the extra mile
In the end
Both used
And why did you pick me
Out of all of the others
Were you afraid to see
The world unhappy
What about seeing me
Unhappy
Did you think that's what I wanted
It hurt me everyday
A secret
In the dark
You told me it was okay
But I knew in my heart
What you were doing was wrong
End to start
It kept going on
Days and weeks
Months and years
It made me weak
It gave me more fear
Too many burdens
For one to carry
Your commited sins
Happened again and again
What about me
What about what I want
Did you ever think someday I might be
Scared
Afraid
I was the only one who cared
That my heart
My mind
Had no maids
To clean up after you
From the start
To when you left me behind
I was so young 
What could I do
Evil's tounge
Talking to me
And I was tortured
What about me
What about what I want
You beat me
Until I broke
No one could hear or see
The things you did
The words you spole
I was twisted
I was bent
After all that time
I spent
And no one ever knew
It was all because of you
What about me
What about what I want
Oh, I'm supposed to 
Take it
Grin and bear
What about you
And what about your secret
Started with a corner tear
And then you tore it in half
And I hope all you can hear
Is the world ridicule you
Can you hear them laugh
I never wanted to
What about me
What about what I want
