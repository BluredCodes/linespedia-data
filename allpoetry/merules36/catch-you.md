---
title: "Catch You"
writer: "Merules36"

I'll never know
What would've been
I can't catch you
And you won't let me in
I'm gonna fess up to everything
I'm tired of being blue
I'm gotta write and I gotta sing
Cause I can't catch you

I just can get you
I can't even say hello
I can't catch you
I guess I'll never know

You think you're special
You think you own me
I'm locked down in Hell
And you walk around free
I still want and need you
But I will do without
I can't catch you
And I want out

I just can't get you
I can't even say hello
I can't catch you
I guess I'll never know
