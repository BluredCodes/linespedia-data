---
title: "Leave It All Behind"
writer: "Merules36"
slug: "leave-it-all-behind"
source: "AllPoetry"

Watch the man
Next door
Take his wife
By her hands
And beat her life
To the floor
And then she's left
Crying
And in pain
She wishes 
She was dying
Cause everyday
When he gets home
He goes insane
Leaves her alone

She knows if she wants to live
She's gotta
Leave it all behind
If she had anything to give
She would
Leave it all behind
But the silent movies
Play over
And over again
In her mind
She's gotta
Leave it all behind
Leave it all behind

Three shots
Three minutes
Three cops cars
Swerve past my house
Then the young boy
Runs his punk ass
The opposite direction
Fired the shots
In a shuffle
With a gun
Like a toy
And his own dirty past
Never knew
Any other way
He only knows
He to live today
His dad's at home
And he never understands
He beats his mom
With his own hands
The poor kid
Only wants a way out
So this is what he did
Now he's got his doubts

He knows if she wants to live
He's gotta
Leave it all behind
If he had anything to give
He would
Leave it all behind
But the silent movies
Play over
And over again
In his mind
He's gotta
Leave it all behind
Leave it all behind

Young girl
Only seventeen
Can't deal with the world
Or what she's seen
She watched her friends
They all seemed
To like it
It was a new end
For a permanent dream
This is the third
Try
She knows
Everything she's heard
But she's 
Ready to
Die
Her brother is the punk
With the gun
Her father the drunk
Who don't give a damn
'bout his son
Her mama gets beaten
Everynight
She's tired of being told
It'll be alright
So she takes the needle
And she heats it all up
She makes another scar
For that last high up
Fifteen times too much
And it ends her life
It all wouldn't be so bad
If her dad
Didn't beat his wife

She knows if she wants to live
She's gotta
Leave it all behind
If she had anything to give
She would
Leave it all behind
But the silent movies
Play over
And over again
In her mind
She's gotta
Leave it all behind
Leave it all behind
