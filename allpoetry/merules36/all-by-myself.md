title: "All By Myself"

Something so perfect
Something so fine
Love lasts forver
Need you to be mine
What went wrong
In the love we had
Was I never what you wanted
Is it something we could never have
What did I ever know
Thinking we could do this
I always thought we could
And I still dunno what this is
I'm missing you now
I'm all alone at night
I don't know how
But come make me alright
Turn off the light
And brush my teeth
Close the window tight
And wish myself goodnight
