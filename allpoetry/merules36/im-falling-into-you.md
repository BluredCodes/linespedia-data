You smile back at me
When I give you a grin
As far as I can see
You want me to come in
There's nowhere else to go
I wanna stay right here
And all I know
Is I wanna have you near

I think I'm falling into you
And there's nothing I can do 
About it
Cause I think I'm falling into you
And I don't know
What I'm going through
Cause I think 
I'm falling into you

And when you hold my hand
I finally understand
And everything works out
I know what my life's about
I wanna be with you
And I know you feel
The same way too
You knwo I'm falling into you

I think I'm falling into you
And there's nothing I can do 
About it
Cause I think I'm falling into you
And I don't know
What I'm going through
Cause I think 
I'm falling into you
