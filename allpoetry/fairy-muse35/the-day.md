i woke upon 
a bed so cold
what i had lost
was about to unfold

i was freezing as if
I was stone 
i lifted my head
i was all alone

then i knew where i was
i had added up the clues
i was in a coffin
i  had rose from the deep blues

i wasn't sure who had done it
but i was going to get 
my revenge
