I have a cold 
A runny nose
It kind of acts like a hose

I have a headache
It's not much fun
I hope it soon will be done

My body aches
It's turning numb
I can't even chew my bubble gum

I have a cough
It's hard to breathe 
I hope I don't have to sneezeI wrote this when I was very young and would like to know if it is any good at all.  I would really appreciate it.
