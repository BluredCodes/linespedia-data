A mind wanders through the night
--ponders up a little flight
--wonders what is weighing right
--thunders in a sea of light,

It clouds up skies with thoughts that flow
--eyes so bright they seem to glow
--cries of love in darkness grow
--fly so fast, yet go so slow,

In the air a warmth does mass
--where the fear is in the past
--there in silence seals the cast
--pair a couple to always last,

As the days start their incline
--wieghs the hearts through the time
--pays each night without a dime
--lay two lovers, sweet as wine,

When the sweet life lays to rest
--beat by only ages test
--meet again on endless quest
--repeat the love that they knew best.This was a hard one for me to write, I wonder if people will get lost in it (in a bad way), please let me know.
