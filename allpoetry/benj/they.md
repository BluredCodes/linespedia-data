title: "They"
writer: "Benj"

They came last night with concealed weapons;
an invisible rifle, an obsolete knife,
They stole my wallet, then took my muse
They went for my thoughts and stole my wife,
They cleaned out my home, ran off with my pants
They broke my dishes to feed their strife,
They put spikes in my bed, took the food from the fridge
Then they ran ou the back, and disappeared with my life.
