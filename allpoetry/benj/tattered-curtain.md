The dreams you've castseem a little uncertain,I suggest you leave the past,emerge from the tattered curtain,So the fork in the roadwas replacing the spoon,and your search for the codeleft you under the moon,It's time to face the mirrorstrace the forgotten yearsthrough your drying tears;the ones to come, lay the biers.Step from shady tree;indulge in the Sun;see where you'll be,a motionless run.Reel in the dreams of lovecast with desired burton;replenish your soul;emerge from the tattered curtain.2001,
