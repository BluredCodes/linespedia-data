She steps to the edgeof a vivid region,Looks o'er the ledgeof Her purple legion,One hundred miles; vast-she's walked in sorrow,Trudging through a pasthidden by tomorrow,Held for years beneath his hand,stripped of tears, a lonely daughter,Bottle fears have been her land,the only cure's this waning water.1999, I just want to make sure this flows well for others.
