The pupils have hiddenin a blink of the eye;a dynamic changein the American Pie;The violence has spreadacross the mourning sky,and when asked how,the only answer's why,It seems we fall furtherthe harder we try,and talk walks tallwhere actions lie,but we better act fast'fore any others die,'cause life comes and goesin a blink of the eye.2000, Ending seems rough, could just be me.
