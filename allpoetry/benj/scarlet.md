She came down from the golden palace
Scarlet hair of the bleeding fire,
Battled with the devil's malice
Won the soul of Eden's desire,

Eyes taken from the shining sun
Born from the the hands of love,
Innocence of spirit hath come
Born from the angel above.1997,
