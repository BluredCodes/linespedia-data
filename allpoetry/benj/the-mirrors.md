It's not the years in your life
rather, the life in your years,
It's not how you've cried
but how many tears,
It's not a matter of strength
but how you face your fears,
It's not what they see in you
but what you see in the mirrors,

Who do you judge?
Who judges you?
I'd let you know
but would it be true?
For my words are a gray
not a crisp clear blue,
and I have to admit
I've judged mirrors too.1999,
