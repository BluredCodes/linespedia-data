---
title: "Mother's Land"
writer: "Benj"
slug: "mothers-land"

Tell me of your golden gate
Grazing ground of eternal fate
From dirty dish to silver plate
Is there room, or shall I wait?
Tell me of this gracious land
Endless beaches of rolling sand
Creatures walking hand in hand
Is there room to sit, or shall I stand?
Tell me of the united one
the bluest oceans, the rising sun
The clear air, the endless fun
Where no one's lost and no one's won.

Then unmask mother's face
Erase the stained industrial trace
Look at where we run the race
Haven't we already ruined that place?1998,
