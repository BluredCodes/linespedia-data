---
title: "And it Rained"
writer: "Benj"
slug: "and-it-rained"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/35286-And-it-Rained-by-Benj"
---

Children played
on their playing ground.
A dirty dog
rambled around.
and it rained,

Mothers walked
in an empty park.
Fathers walked
within the dark.
and it rained,

Cats dug
through filthy cans.
Mice washed
their miniature hands.
and it rained,

Blood spilled
from an open wound,
Then washed away
with the flowing gloom.
and it rained,

Rabid men
bit kind rats.
Mice chased
frightened cats.
and it poured

And it poured for days
And it poured for nights,
And it washed away
the sweet little sights,
And it washed away
the safe little lights,
And if flooded the streets
like a mighty tide-
Then it died...
off...
a little...

And flowers grew
from the moistened ground,
A dampened dog
rambled around,
Time stopped
without a sound.

And it rained.
