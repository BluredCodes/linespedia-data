I am writing in silencehence, just my imagination,The words scattered across this pageforming this collaboration,Are symbols of the patterned alphagiving sentence no indication,And all these words in coalitionare just an act of cerebration.I am writing sol within this frameand here beneath this lumber,While o'er there across the floormy children lie in slumber,And in tomorrow, if it may befather time will call my number,But for today I'll urge my mindto think of that less cumber.I am writing here in naturesurrounded by earth and air,Underneath the bluish lightand the sun's settle glare,I am writing here with sea and treein the center of all not fair,Enjoying all the flowing landwithout a single care.1997,
