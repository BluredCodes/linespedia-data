---
title: "In a Field of Fire"
writer: "Benj"

The moon rises over a field of fire
as the wind sweeps across the flames,
The rain falls and the light grows higher
while she walks along her path of pains.

A tear falls from her saddened eyes
fear lingers in her broken sighs
here she stops and here she lies
near the end she waits and cries,
The beat of rain, in her head, does pound
defeats the hope that was around
streets of thought now lost from found
heats the memories now coming down,
And night pulls over its blackened shade
light from the stars begins to fade
Fright is here with dues unpaid
sight is found inlaid in jade,

So she walks away from the pouring rain
while the moon sets on a field of fire,
Burning up the remaining pain
and leaving behind her only desire.1996,
