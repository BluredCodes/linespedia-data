---
title: "The Novel"
writer: "Benj"
slug: "the-novel"
source: "AllPoetry"

A poor man sits to write a story
Not of heroes, wars, or glory
Not of hardship, pain, or worry
Nothing bloody, nothing gory,
Not of a loss, life or love
Not of the skies or the clouds above
Not of a player with a golden glove
Not of the nature, wolf nor dove,
Not of a night bitter and cold
Not of a woman he'll never hold
Not of a warrior strong and bold
Not of the dice he too often rolled,
For when he writes, he writes instead
of all the thoughts left in his head
all the dreams asleep in bed
all the books he never read,
When he's done he'll quietly go
out the door, and into the snow
holding a classic he'll never show
A number one seller that no one will know.1997,
