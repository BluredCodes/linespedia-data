The mirror of my soul
lies dormant in your eyes,
The spirit of my heart
holds its placid disguise,
The spark of your smile
'cause the flames to rise,
The words from your lips
always seem to energize,

I look into your feelings
the delicate emotional ball,
And I see the sinister Fear
fleeting down the hall,
The thoughts and ideas
are tearing down the wall,
We've found our fountain
We've answered our call,

And through it all
you've been in me,
All I've seen
is what you see,
A standing ocean
A raging sea,
Forever, together
we'll always be.Is the ending too unoriginal?
