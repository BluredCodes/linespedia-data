I can see
Each glittering light
Every ray, all beside
I know my heart
Wants to say
A simple word
That is day
But I cannot 
For my dreams
Is my only
Crystal stream
All language
Of my words
Does not know
How to form
From this silent,
Silent time
In this long
Starry night
Please forgive me
If my hands
Creates untrue
Our beloved language
Of our words
All today
For tomorrow
Gone yesterday.

-Nick
