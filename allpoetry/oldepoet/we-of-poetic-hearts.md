title: "We of poetic hearts"
writer: "OldePoet"

I close my eyes
And write this on the side,
I know nothing of the words I write
But only that my dreams is right,
My fingers hover above the keyboard
While my eyes roams its view outside to find just one more,
My body hungers just for things to say
So seek, we of poetic hearts, to find a way.

-Nick
