If I leave something untitled
Why does that want to bring me home?
If I left something undone in my day
I would be held, and looked in the eyes
And be told, “You shouldn’t have done it that way”,
I know that sometime things needs a lift
Which people would call it a subtle fingerized gift,
But sometime I just want to leave it on the ground, in rain or sunshine,
I just want to leave my window untitled 
And the world be its own story.

-Nick
