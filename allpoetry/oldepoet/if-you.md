If you want to wonder
Let the stars give you things to ponder

If you want to roam
Wait till spring for the grass to form

If you want to dream
Take life at its unseen seam

If you want to hear
First see what your heart holds dear

If you want to snooze
Go out in day and do as you choose

If you want to read
Go learn the color of everyone’s deed

If you want to stay in your holding past
Go with the future to make it last

-Nick
