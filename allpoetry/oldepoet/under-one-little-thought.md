Under one little thought
And sweeter then the flowers I brought
Is this most lovely dream
This steady crystal stream

I open my arms
A melodic surrender
To this beautiful forever night
Where the stars sings their own light

I want to love you
My sweet dear Mystery
I want to embrace your dreams
Your oh so clear star bright

But my eyes can’t hold
What the night have told
I know you are all so cold
In this lonely night of old

But I must be
Where my flesh roam free
And I must be
Where my love loves me back

It is a simple time of majesty
That your glamour becomes this night
But my limited future ahead
Is forlornly brighter then your star light… 

-Nick Audet
