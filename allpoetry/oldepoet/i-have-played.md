I have played
Into what my dreams will keep
I have lived
In what my reality will not remember
I have thought of questions
In what mystery wont let me hold

I have dreamed
Into what I will play
I have made reality remember
In what I have lived
I have made mystery let me hold
In answer of what my thoughts had in question

Changes have been done
With our ability to change
The existence of what believed cant be changed
But in the changing, something has endured.

-Nick
