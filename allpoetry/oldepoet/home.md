The sun has set
And the wind blows
The heart is still
Where your home is

Stars sky
And lake of crystal hue
Surpassing love
Holds no bounds

Rainbow rains
And falling grounded clouds
Friendship all
Truth holds deep

Entwined lovely nature
Combined human beauty
A lost art
In our worldly virtues...

-Nick
