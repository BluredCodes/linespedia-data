I know people watch a poem
Form under words I write

I know when I shape this ‘round
It is something hopefully never seen before

I know I have the power in words to say
To your eyes one a star

What does my poem means
Behind all the bravos and reprobate

I love all the words of well done
And I listen to all disliking

But it’s the poem I compose
That I want to see in their eyes

It’s their frown and smile
That makes a world in my poem

And here I write
An end I cannot finish.

-Nick
