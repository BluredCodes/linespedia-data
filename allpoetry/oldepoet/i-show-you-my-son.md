title: "I show you, my son"

I show you my son
A one little star
For it is you
They dream all about

I show you my son
A blade of green grass
It is the life in one
That grows in all!

Here I will take you
And here I will show you
That the river sweet stream
Is the giving love upon all life

And come here my dear son
Lay upon me is my failing health
For its you who will now show
The love for all to your dear son.

-Nick
