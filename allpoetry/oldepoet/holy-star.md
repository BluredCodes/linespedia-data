Holy star
In this holy night
Here I am
Beneath your guiding light

Holy star
That graces the sand
Yet I ponder
Where do I stand?

Holy star 
In this holy night
Under the guiding light
Came a shining knight

Shining knight
With a solemn face
Declare his place
With a formal bow

Shining knight
Under the guiding light
Stood his height
And spoke his heart

"You are me
 And I am you,
 Your my past
 And I am your future"

"Your words are wise
 And your heart is pure
 Follow the guiding light
 And you will know why"

Holy star
In this Holy night
Here I am 
A shining Knight.

-Nick
