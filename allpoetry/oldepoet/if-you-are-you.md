If you are you
And he is who he knows he is
What that make me,
If the third is not the same?

Each eyes and hair
Every limbs and size
And every color of skin tone
Is such the world we abide in, such is fair

Every open hand
And in every open smile
The lovely open acceptance is of
No mind the many miles

Its kindness in every gesture
Its all the living smiles on every face
It’s the open heart in these warming days
That makes our ground below our walking feet

We all stand somewhere, all around
We stand up there high or on the humbled ground
It is an existence so profound
It is no question that we are of human kind…

-Nick Audet
