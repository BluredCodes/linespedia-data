title: "SILVER BOXES"

Our words should be like beautiful
silver boxes wrapped with shiny bows;
then each time we gave them to another,
they'd do more good than anyone could know.

Think about silver boxes the next time 
you want to speak you mind;
and how nice it would be if a silver box
you might also receive in kind.
