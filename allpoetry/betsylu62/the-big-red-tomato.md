A big red tomato makes me think about my Mom.
A slice with some salt & pepper,
and she's humming a happy song.

I wonder if she knows
how easy she made it all seem...
being a wife, a mother and living
the All American Dream.

My Mom can be happy without requiring a lot;
It's as easy as a big red tomato
that really hits her spot!

Mom, I'm doing my best to like tomatoes too;
I still have a hard time swallowing them;
but I keep trying out of love for you.
