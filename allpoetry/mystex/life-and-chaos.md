What a mad existance is life,
Chaotic and Uncontrolled.
While we like chaff
Float before its raging winds.

Born into this world
Not of our own desires.
We strive to bring order
Into the chaos of our lives.

So it is that chance and happenstance
Seem to rule all we do.
We rever luck
And try to court her whims.

For in her fickle whims
She embodies chaos.
The very nature of life
