In all I see and do,
You are there with me.
Every sunrise you show me
The simple beauty of the world.

Things I take for granted,
Simple yet so complex.
It makes me wonder how I missed you,
How I couldn't see you before.

Your the engineer of all I see,
The artist for who creation
Is your greatest canvas.
Yet only now do I see you.

Only now do I realise
That everything is you yet not.
In my life I see your hand,
Your touch affecting me shaping me.
