---
title: "Heavy Heart"
writer: "zanna"
slug: "heavy-heart"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I sit alone and wonder what to do,
Do I just sit back and watch all hell
Break lose, or do I say what my heart 
Really feels. Would it hurt you or would 
They just be words that don't matter at all.
The words in my heart come from love 
that I feel, if that matters 
it shouldn't hurt to say them to you.
I have put up a guard to shield any pain 
that I know lies ahead and will soon 
come into play.
For my heart grows heavy 
with each passing day, 
how do I stand by and watch another 
career go down the drain. 
I can not find the right words to say 
but I know one day I will find a way, 
for if I don't the distance between us 
will be far to great, for us to find 
our way back to what we had yesterday.


Bobbie Brouhard
