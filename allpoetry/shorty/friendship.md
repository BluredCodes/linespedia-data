Friendship's like a circle....it goes around and around.
One tells another a story, and the other wallows in the sound.
Listening to their problem,
story, gossip or whatever.
But how come sometimes I feel like mine,
just don't seem to care about others?
They might care about their parents,
Some family and some friends.
But then how come I'm getting a feeling,
like my friends don't care about being my friend?THIS HAS NOTHING TO DO WITH MY FRIENDS THAT ARE ON THIS SITE, DONT WORRY! lol i love you guys!.....just upset about how some of my friends treat me....whatever
