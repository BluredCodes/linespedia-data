Everyone has 5 senses.
You can see,hear,touch,feel, and taste.
What if you had a 6th sense?
What if it was a sense for love?
A sense that could only be sensed with that one special person.
That one special person that could make the world stop.
Make the world spin backwards.
That could make the world spin completely around him.(or her)
A sense that gives you butterflies in your stomach that just won't go away.
A sense that can give you the courage to say those three little words...
