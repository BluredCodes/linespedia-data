Shaina is my puppy,
i love her very much.
She's cute and cuddly and so fluffy, its very addicting to touch!
She plays with her toys,
and loves to eat cheese.
Will someone shut me up now....please!
I love my puppy and she loves me,
but it pisses me off, 
when in my room she decides to pee.i wanna write something funny!!!! lol a lil off my serious poems hehe
