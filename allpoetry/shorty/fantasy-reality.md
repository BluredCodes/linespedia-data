---
title: "Fantasy, Reality"
writer: "shorty"
slug: "fantasy-reality"
source: "AllPoetry"

Im sitting by a fire, doing a puzzle with you.
This is my fantasy.

I'm sitting at your funeral, I've never seen so many people crying.
This is my reality.

Yard work while everyone's inside relaxing,
This is my fantasy.

And then I open up my eyes, and I see my fantasy's gone....
And here comes my reality....Ever had one of those days where you just piss a grandpa or another family member that's passed away? Ever wish your fantasy of what life was would come back? just one of those days rite?
