Lean on me,
when you need a friend.
Lean on me,
I'll be the one who'll attend.
Lean on me,
when you need a hand.
Lean on me,
I'll help you stand.
Lean on me,
when you feel small.
Lean on me,
although I'm short, I'll help you feel tall.
Lean on me,
when you need a hug.
Lean on me,
and I'll show you true love.
Lean on me when you need somebody,
I'll be that somebody,
