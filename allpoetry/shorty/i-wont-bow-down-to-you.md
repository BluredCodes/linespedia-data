title: "I won't bow down to you"
writer: "shorty"

You can say what you want,but I won't bow down to you.You can do whatever you want,but I won't bow down to you. You can spread rumors across the world,but I won't bow down to you.So no matter what you do,I refuse to bow down to someone as low as you.I got bored in case you didn't notice....Not one of my best work again
