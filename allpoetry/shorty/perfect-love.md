You put my hand in yours
and everything seems just right.
The night, the scene, everythings perfect,
not even a hint to a fight.
A light kiss upon my lips 
I'm in total bliss.
how I am right now
is how I want to be forever
with you by my side,
this is a moment I'll always treasure. 
you without me means misery,
me without you means a mystery.
without eachother, we're forever lost.
our happiness together can't be put on a cost.
for the price would be up to the moon,
and very soon, the day will come.
when you'll finally see how much you mean to me.
I love you so much,
always and forever,
and any other guy won't even matter.
the love of my life is infront of my eyes.
and this cannot be a discize.
the love of my life, standing tall and true,
sweetie, I can surely say, that i love youI love you, you knwo who you are
