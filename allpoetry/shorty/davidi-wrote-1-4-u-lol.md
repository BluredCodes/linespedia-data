I have a friend named David.
His personality is highly rated.
If you sit by a couch and talk for a few,
you'd be amazed by the things he'll say to you.
He's funny and sweet, and kind to meet.
What more is there to ask?
I mean, he's NOT an ass.
He'll stand by you and help you stand tall.
Although he's SHORT, he'll help you feel tall.
Although he'll poke you, or the "masked poker will,"
But deep inside I'm sure he'll still love you.
So I have a friend named David, 
queerness and all.
But hey, think about it?
What would the world be like, 
if there wasn't a David at all?
