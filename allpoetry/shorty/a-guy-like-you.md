I looked up into your big green eyes.
I saw passion, love, something I didn't see for you.
I had just met you a few weeks before,
You called me after a squirrel. 
How could you fall for a girl like me?
How could I say yes to a guy like you?
I must've been crazy,
I must've been sincere,
For I don't care for you as you do to me.
I'm sorry, sweetie, I care for you so.
But I don't like you like that, I don't know what to do.
So I guess we're over, the breakups done.
I hope you can accept this,
