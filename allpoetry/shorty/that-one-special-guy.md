"I never would have thought you two'd be together"
That's what everyone says, since we've found eachother.
While kissing in the crowded halls,
everyone there just disolves.
Another world.
Another time zone.
That is where I'll be.

With the love of my life right next to me,
I really dont care, can't you see!?
No matter what you do or say,
NOTHING will stop me from feeling this way.
With my special guy right by my side,
where else could I possibly want to be?

He holds me tight and I feel weak,
I wonder what's holding me up on my feet.
He holds me with such love and care,
falling in his eyes, all I can do is stare.

I see some chick trying to hit on you,
all I want to do is go and steal you.
So I can hug you from now till tomorrow, 
cause without you, I'm in total sorrow.
From now until many years to come,
you'll always know where my feelings come from.
From my heart I will be true,
