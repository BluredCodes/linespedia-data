---
title: "I hate you I love you"
writer: "shorty"
slug: "i-hate-you-i-love-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

You always do that to me! You say we'll hang and we never do!
I hate you!
You talk to me and tell me how much you care about me.
I love you.
You say we'll get back together, yet we never seen to.
I hate you!
We talk about how we're both single and we should get back together.
I love you.
No matter how much I hate you, I still seem to love you. 
What the hell am I supposed to do, when I'm stuck on a guy like you?
I hate you!......Yet I love you.
