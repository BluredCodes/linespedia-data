Sisters.
Sisters are supposed to be there,
no matter what, threw and threw.
She's supposed to stand by you and make you seem all tall.
She's not supposed to yell at you, 
making you feel extremely small.
She'll listen to you no matter what,
she'll listen to your bullshit.
Comparison.
Mine yells at me 24/7,
I feel so far from Heaven.
I'm supposed to feel like someones always by me, 
yet I feel like a spikes are duggen into me.
Why do you have to yell?
Why do you have to spoil all that we have going?
Why do I have to have a sister like so?
I love you so, although you don't love me.
No matter what you do, I guess I'm stuck with you.I'm in the middle of a huge fight with my sister:(
