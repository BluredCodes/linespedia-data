title: "Together"
writer: "shorty"

You held me in your arms,
I felt like I was in no harm.
Whenever I was to be with you,
the clouds opened up, and everything was blue.
All we had to do is touch,
right there and then I knew this wasn't a crush.
I love you so much,
you'd whisper in my ear.
Suddenly, I had no fears.
Together we were, and together we'll be.
Hunny, even though you always tell me,
I know you'd never leave me.
