title: "Don't Dump Her For Me"
writer: "shorty"

Don't dump her for me, 
for i'm not worthy.
I don't know what you see in me, 
I'm just another girl.
Another girl looks your way,
a girl who you know would always stay.
We are best friends and that we'll be.
But how can you even think of dumping her for me?
You said so yourself, you don't want to hurt anybody.
Yet if you stay you hurt yourself, if you leave you hurt her.
What to do? what to do? 
I really do not know.
I'm here for you, 
I've always been.
Always have.
Always will.
No matter what you do,
no matter what happens between us,
I still have and always will love you
