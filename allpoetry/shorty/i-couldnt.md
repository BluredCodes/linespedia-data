There was so many things I wanted to say,
but I couldn't.
I wanted to kiss you,
but I couldn't.
I wanted to hug you and hear you say everything would be ok,
but I couldn't.
I wanted to stand by you in the hallway of the school forever,
but I couldn't.
I wanted to say I really care about you too,
but I couldn't.
I wanted to say I love you,
But I couldn't
