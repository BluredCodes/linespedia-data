Love.  What is love?  People use it so frequently, who knows anymore.
Is it when you finally find that one person where no matter what guy looks at you, you can't even turn your head?  Is it when you find that one person that keeps you going and throws you out of bed to go to school everyday? Well, I never believed in any of that. It was all bullshit to me...that is, until I met you... You've changed my life, I believe in love. Thankyou for bringing that special something into my life.
