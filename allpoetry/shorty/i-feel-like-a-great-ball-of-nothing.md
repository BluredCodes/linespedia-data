title: "I feel like a great ball of nothing"

A great ball of nothing,
thats what i feel.
like everything i've worked so hard for,
may never be for real
i feel like a great ball of nothing.

I've spent so much time trying to make the night perfect!
Nothings going right, we're not even doing anything good.
i feel like a great ball of nothing.

I spent years working on my reputation, 
minutes later called a slut.
I feel like a great ball of nothing.

I searched and searched for good friends,
yet i seem to find the ones that blow me off a lot.
Why can't i find a one true friend?
I feel like a great ball of nothing.

When life's got you down, and you feel no emotion cause everythigns so weird,do you ever feel just like a greatball of nothing?just how i felt
