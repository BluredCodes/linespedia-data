Have you ever felt that sudden rush?
When you and your true love touch?
Your heart begins to race,
Man! What a pace!
My entire life, I didn't believe in it,
but now, I've found that one special "it"
I noticed it tonight,
the moon shone so bright.
I saw it in your eyes,
our love really is devine!
I looked up upon your face,
my heart started beating at an unstopable pace.
I felt my heart suddenly expand,
my hearts fallen for you oh so my i almost can't stand!
So this is what it feels like to be in love?
Now i see what all the commotions of.
I always thought "oh how silly" when i heard of a person in love,
now all i want to do is have my love for him grow and grow and grow.I spent tonight with that special someone...i guess it suddenly out of nowhere clicked how much i really do love him....reality check!! lol...almost scary.....this entire poem is based on my "reality check"....and how i never wanna go back to how it was:) i love you soooo much dogan! what would i do without you??
