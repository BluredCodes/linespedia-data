In the dead of night
A crash in the house
Who’s there?
My heart pounds
I’m scared
Soft sounds outside the bedroom door
Slowly the door opens
A dark shape enters
Frozen, I can’t move
It’s just you, unexpected
Looking lovely as always
All dressed in black
A strange look in your eyes
Something hidden behind your back
What’s the purpose of this visit?
Your hand moves, revealing a gun
What’s the gun for?
You point the gun straight at me
What are you doing?
No! Someone, please help!
No one but you hear my last cry
Just the two shots in the night
