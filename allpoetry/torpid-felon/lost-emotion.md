Lost emotion caught in the dance of the world, Waltzing the feelings adorned by millions, caught in the net, The net of hatred , The net of hurt, They rhythmically move around mortals heads, Confusing, abusing and distorting the sense of feeling. Emotion: something you lack greatly in.00,
