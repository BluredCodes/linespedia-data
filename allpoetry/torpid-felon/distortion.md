A HEARTACHE OF DISTORTION DROWNS ANY LOGICAL IDEAS,NOTHING EVER FITS MY BROKEN PANE OF GLASS,ETERNAL FURY DOMINATES MY SHORT SPURT OF HAPPINESS, SCREAMS DROWNED BY THE HARMONY OF UNCOMFORTABLE SILENCE, A SUFFICIENT AMOUNT OF SMILES ARE STORED IN MY HEAD, MY HEART BEATS ARE LIKE A CLOCK TICKING AWAY TIME.01,
