title: "pondering"

i sit here wondering what my life would be with out you
lonely, scared , wondering
hoping that this day will never come
we will go through trials and tribulations
which together we will resolve
nothing will come between us to break us apart
communication is the key
so why do i sit here wondering what life would be with out you?
