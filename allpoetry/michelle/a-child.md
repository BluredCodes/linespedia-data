---
title: "a child"
writer: "michelle"
slug: "a-child"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/2553-a-child-by-michelle"
---

you feel the need to bring a child into this world

you have an empty space in your heart

you need to look honestly at yourself

the truth then will be revealed

your self esteem and mistrust are getting in the way

the way of a promising future

to thine own self be true

dont make a child suffer in this world because of you
