---
title: "true love"
writer: "michelle"
slug: "true-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/2549-true-love-by-michelle"
---

the times that we fight
you always take to flight
you say you want it to last
then why do you always run?
listen, really listen to what i am saying 
let your guard down
let me break those walls you are hiding behind
let the past be just that
forget all the hurt that they have put you thru
you must know by now
my love is true
