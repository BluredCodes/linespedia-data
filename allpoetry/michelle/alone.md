SITTING IN THE DARKNESS
THE ONLY SOUND I HEAR
IS THE CLICKING OF THE CLOCK.....IN MY EAR
TICKING AWAY THE HOURS,MINUTES AND SECONDS OF TIME
WHAT NOW?   WHERE DO I GO FROM HERE?
SCARED.....SO SCARED
TO START ALL OVER AGAIN
