there was a time when i thought i could never love againthen you looked my way with a wink and a grinmy heart went a flutter and i melted like butteryour sweet, honest, warm hearted and kindthose are qualities that are hard to findwe have both been hurt in the pastthis would be the reason not to go fasti need to tell you what's on my heartto see if you feel the same and maybe together we could make a new start.
