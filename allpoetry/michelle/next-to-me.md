i just want to say that i don't easily give my heart away
the first time i saw you there was a certain something       that kept my heart a thumping
i often sit and wonder how it  could be between you and me
the times we could share together would be remembered forever
there's an uncontrollable desire that sets my soul on fire
i can't pinpoint it.... the flutters just won't quit
my wants have turned to needs....this isn't a game or a tease
you might find this hillarious , but i am totally serious
my heart is racing and i find myself pacing
waiting to hear your voice and to tell me that i am your choice
the whole day through when i think of you
there's a smile that lasts for a long while
the place i want to be is with you next to methank you tina :}
