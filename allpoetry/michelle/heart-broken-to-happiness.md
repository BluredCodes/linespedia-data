title: "heart broken to happiness"
writer: "michelle"
slug: "heart-broken-to-happiness"

A BLACK CLOUD SITS OVER MY HEAD 
AND MORE TEARS HAVE BEEN SHED
THE WORDS I HEARD JUST BROKE MY HEART
MY WORLD ONCE MORE TORN APART
THE FEELINGS I THOUGHT WE BOTH SHARED
SHE CAN NOT BECAUSE SHE IS SCARED
SCARED OF LETTING GO
SHE'S FEELING SO LOW
HOW CAN I CONVINCE HER THIS IS NOT THE SAME
HOLD YOUR HEAD HIGH WITH NO SHAME
HAPPINESS WILL FIND YOU
IT'S ONLY A HEARTBEAT AWAY
PLEASE LET ME IN AND SHOW YOU THE WAY
