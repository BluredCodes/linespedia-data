at one time i thought it was such a lonely wordbut now i know that is absurdall by myself is another way of saying ALONEby giving myself time i have seen how I've grownI've grown not only in mind but also in souli am now able to fill the once emptied holeuntil now i didn't know who i wasi never seemed to give myself a chance....just becauseslowly taking one day at a time, to explore my inner self....is an experience all in its selfi need to move foreward, no time to slacki have now made myself a pactto be honest to myself and to be truenot to hide from all that is newcontinue my life as i want it to beto find the unlonely ME
