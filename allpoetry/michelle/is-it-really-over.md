i ask the question time and time againit's a losers game....nobody winswe had something once....it was greatnow it looks like it's too latethings were done and said we can't look backit seems like a constant attackwe have nothing in common as we seeso it's time to let go and be freeto find happiness and to go on our wayit won't be easy as they saythere's alot of memories ....good and badand at times we will be lonely and sada beautiful daughter we named shannon leighwhich is the bond and the keythe key to happiness and love we once sharednothing definitely can compareshe's the love of our lives and will keep our spirits aliveso when we are down and feeling low feel like we have no where to gowe will just remember her sweet innocent smiling faceit will always bring us to a different place
