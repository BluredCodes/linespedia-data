---
title: "loving you"
writer: "michelle"
slug: "loving-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/2003-loving-you-by-michelle"
---

there are times when all i can think of is you

i can't keep you out of my mind it's true

if you've noticed , I'm around all the time

yes, i am guilty

but is this is a crime?

i long to see your sweet face

nothing or no one can take your place

we will be together forever

this i can promise you
