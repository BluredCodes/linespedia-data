LIFE IS SO FUNNY
ONE MINUTE YOU HAVE IT ALL...NOT A CARE IN THE WORLD
THEN THAT DEADLY POISON SEEPS OUT AND BANG
YOUR WORLD, AS YOU KNOW IT, HAS ENDED.......
UNHAPPINESS
HURT
ANGER
DESTRUCTION STRIKES
RUINED LIVES
HATE
JEALOUSY
NEVER FORGOTTEN OR FORGIVEN WORDS
ANXIETY
FEAR
CRYING
SORROW
YOU JUST CAN'T GIVE ANY MORE
YOU HAVE TO GIVE UP
TOO STRESSFUL
ALL BECAUSE ' YOU DON'T NEED ME ANYMORE'
