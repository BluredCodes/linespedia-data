---
title: "if i can't have you"
writer: "michelle"
slug: "if-i-cant-have-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/2005-if-i-cant-have-you-by-michelle"
---

i don't care if the skies are blue
let it rain down on me
let the wind blow thru me
life is not worth living
if i can't have you

you are not a possession
nor are you an obsession
you are like a dainty flower
that gives me so much power
i need your heart
mind
and soul
to make me whole
without you i am nothing

you are my destiny
you are so dear to me
i just can''t let you say goodbye
life is not worth living
if i can't have you

you and me together
my fantasy forever
how have i come this far?
how do i go on without you

emptiness and darkness
when will there be light
life is not worth living 
if i can't have you
