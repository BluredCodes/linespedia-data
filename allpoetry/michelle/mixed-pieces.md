title: "mixed pieces"

THE TOUCH IS POISONOUS

THE LOOK IS DEADLY

TO FEEL IS DANGEROUS

THE WALK OF LIFE IS PUZZLING

EACH PIECE PLACED INTO THE PUZZLE ....DOES NOT FIT

EVERY PIECE TO COME INTO CONTACT IS DESTROYED

TO LOVE IS UNNATURAL

THERE IS NO SUCH THING AS LOVE.BUT THERE IS A DEADLY POISON SPREAD AMONG THE PIECES

THE PIECES END UP SHATTERED AND SELDOM CAN THEY EVER GO BACK TO PLACE. BUT YET THE ROADS TO DISASTER ARE MORE LIKELY

TO FINISH THE PUZZLE THE PIECES WILL END UP WITH A LIFE LONG EMPTINESS THAT CAN NEVER BE PUT BACK TOGETHER THE RIGHT WAY AGAIN

THEY'LL ALWAYS BE THAT ONE PIECE MISSING THAT NO ONE WILL EVER BE ABLE TO FIND

ITS OUT OF PLACE AND CAN NOT BE REACHED

NO MATTER HOW YOU TURN THE PIECE IT WILL NEVER FIT INTO PLACE

EVERTHIS WAS MY VERY FIRST POEM WRITTEN
