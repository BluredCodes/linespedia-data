---
title: "a love once forgotten"
writer: "michelle"
slug: "a-love-once-forgotten"
source: "AllPoetry"

the walls are beginning to tumble down
slowly falling to the ground
piece by piece; they are going so fast
i have found a love to last
someone who loves me for "who" i am
no more judging; no more scams
happiness: something i have forever longed for
is right here, right at my door
i just need to open it; open it to you
for "you" are the only one who can show me a love that is true
