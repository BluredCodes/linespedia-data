---
title: "How to be Hip"
writer: "Rio Child"

Well, you must understand that motion is
just a filler between the times we rest.
So that all that is cool
and all that is down is
to stop beautifully and to do it often.

Because everyone moves,
and everybody sleeps
but very few know the art of stopping
and fewer still...Think of a Janet Jackson dance routine.
