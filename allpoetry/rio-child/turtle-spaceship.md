In the land of the blond boy rapperand the strait-laced girl playing cello,all enemy lines shall be shattered to the beat of unification.Listen to the dragon as it bellows,for decades dormant it has now woken.To answer the national crescendoit has finally surfaced from the Han.The Fatherland's chains will be brokenwith mere micro-chips as libation.This morning the masses have spoken:their Word a sunrise over the land.The reunification of Korea will rock the world!
