The source of happiness? To be yourself

always!

A stone? a stone; a simple hope? unsaid
Two birds? not four; each star? to shine somewhere.

And yet,

by birth a ROSE a thousand times you're blessed

for

by being such your joy is ours to share.Does it flow at all?  I mean, it does to me but than don't mean much does it.
