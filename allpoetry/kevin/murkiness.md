midnight clouds -oh-
noontime's face
glowing trees & love of race
from our lives our souls have fled.

in darkness, where sweeping willows wave
lie naked white breasts floating, 
bright against against darkness,
their newness of experience leaving
a glowing trail home, to hansel and gretel,
and the other children.

ask me to stay one more time and I will:
but sex is a drug like any other,
and in partaking I worry that this
already fragile, sinking self,
will not be myself, by morning.written: , revised 6/25/00
