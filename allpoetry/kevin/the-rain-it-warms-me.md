title: "[the rain it warms me]"
writer: "kevin"

the rain it warms me
wet, enveloping
helps me shed my leaves
releases my tension
with wet hair, and
tears streaming,
I cackle at the sky's
insanity,
for I am humankind.

the drizzle is light enough
to inspire,
kind enough to
welcome me home.
Releasing, forgiving,
caressing intellect
towards nature's common end.

11/98
