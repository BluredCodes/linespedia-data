ta play tease ingnessly hope,
dreamingnessly true are you
between the contract of eyeglance
rip papers, tear away virginity
twist me towards fate
bend me to your will
but watch the catapult recoil

tempo ing hotplate, boiling
7 skinny cups of blood, boiling
teeth falling out and your old enough
the first look and last are always the same
ta play tease ingnessly me dream
twist and then and bend and recoil

end! lifewatch, love snap truth coil
serpenting is dreamstreaming hop
help find dog under yourself a birdflight
dream, dream, falling anger, rise happygetting in touch with that wild side
