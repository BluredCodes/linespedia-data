title: "silly jumpinghop kids"

opening with silly jumpinghop skip kids
orange beneath the moon, we skipjump
forward into blackholes of love
leaving empty milk bottles behind
dreams of playing in the dust
all the buildings gone, only parents
feet planted as giant oaks in moist desert soil
towering above and leading up, up
skipjumpinghop with them, then alone
falling, falling, caught
by a rope with a unique not.
