I.
plummet to the depths
of light at the end of your eyes
another dream and the phantasms will outnumber reality
of love like a cancer, I can't cut it out
a root so twisted about my spine
I scream 'pull it out', dying, dying.

II.
a thousand horses pulling backwards
mud slipping in the rain, no directions
only BACK, prevent the single natural action
prevent the destruction of the dream through action
on the threshold of becoming, I believe in you.*sigh*
