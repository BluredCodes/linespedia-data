---
title: "understanding a new friend"
writer: "kevin"
slug: "understanding-a-new-friend"
source: "AllPoetry"

I met you and your beautiful honesty surprised me, 
our golden strands were thoughts alive within the truth
explaining dualisms, finally beginning to understand
the median between romantic ideal and individuality

analyzing this sort of thing has melted golden moments
in the past - I can't fight my nature - relax:
see the struggle of life falling away!

maintaining the romantic ideal, movement presses onward:
more dualisms: wanting distance, needing to be an individual
our cultural ideals are so cruel!  Its a wonder
anyone finds happiness this way, these silent people

left with some lingering pleasure, after compact thought
the ability of life to continue moving despite pleasure or pain
though I can't decide if I've been happy, I know
life will only be getting better, that change is good,
that in finding a friend I increase myself, remain myself.
