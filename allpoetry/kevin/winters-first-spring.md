patience - sleeping
you awaken me, myself too
as winter's first spring,
hibernation at finally, finally at an end.
now i can focus
on our oneness, twoness too.

:awaken: wild animals, wildflowers
crown my lover with thorns
this kingdom we unveil, reveal
