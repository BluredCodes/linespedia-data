the last two dolphins in all the sea
met their fate tonight within the waves
when the only two souls in all the world
collided in a flash of blinding light
( blind like the love
  that fell to the floor
  and was buried beneath the sands )why are ALL of my poems in the angst category?  Ugg, I shouldn't be so tormented...  I'm writing again, you can guess that something happened :)
