or rather, too many
Their words and bodies have been piling up
for centuries, in one massive, smelly, untomb
and blocking the way to the truer treasures
of heart, living.

reality can seem so far away
getting so stuck in creating
Mom, I've got to go color,
said the 5-year-old-son (me)
laughing as he felt the coldness,
the capitalist fist closing
around his throat.
