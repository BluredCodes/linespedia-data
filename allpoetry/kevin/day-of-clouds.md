ringed around with leaves of goldthe live center of frigid winteralone at stormwatchbooks, wordsyour breast & a fire burning happiness on page 28beauty my shadowy companionleave whatever dreams behindthey got in the way of lovethis is all we need?how long to wanderthe streets of restless poora deeper love - foundationsliving takes so longbecoming human,lead the shadows to their darknesslight outside ambition, brilliant mindsapplied to everyday - for love!leave work alone, come back to beddine in the clouds, love us instead11/98, revised 10/11/01
