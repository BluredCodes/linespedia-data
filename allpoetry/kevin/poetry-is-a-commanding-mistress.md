no breaks and no pay:
my life is just a tool
to start with nothing and end with something,
the earth spinning mindlessly,
here I am creating:
stopped three times on the way to class
I must write it down!  or I'm not alive
but who is this emotion, whatAmI, 
and when the teacher asks why I am late,
I just smile
thinking, knowing, "Poetry is a commanding Mistress."
