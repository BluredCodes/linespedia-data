neonlit halfwaydone bookdream your way into the circleI'll call you back if I'm interestedsign up here, have you loved before?countyourkisses, average per hourhow long will you be staying in this area?and the purpose of your visit?  (you know thatall romance died with Juliet)stand here, call it a balconythrow down your short cropped hairthe gallant trip back down, stumbletwist the bloody pulp of your hair. fallback to earth with a bruised conscience.all around you the flowers spring uppick some and I'll yell it was my gardenbut love me, love me, love me doso long as you try, it might be you.
