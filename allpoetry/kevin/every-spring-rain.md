the soft tree songs
my eyes fill with
their sad melody

the earth moans
ear to breast, I
hear it's low sigh

my moon wanes
existence flickers -
still, I disbelieve

the twisted silence
of souls turned away
from the sky

life amidst death;
my hope: within
that always-songTHIS is why I am depressed :)
