title: "smoking emptiness"
writer: "kevin"

The smokers sit with me, in me,
their words of change, emptiness
like a crow crying vainly
against a terrible blue sky
against an ugly world full of beauty.

"you're dying" I observe
blank faces respond, unassuming: 
"so are you."

black tar inhaled into their lungs -
rather like the darkness of my poetry
both deadly, it’s the passion that counts,
my dreams flowing into words,
their anger into smoke, both floating away

snuff it out and move it on,
life's too precious to waste.
