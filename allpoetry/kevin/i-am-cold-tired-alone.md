title: "[I am cold, tired, alone]"

I am cold, tired, alone
from work to work
: I must escape
to my world of
books, writing, computers
to enjoy spare time
to contemplate love
how long will I wait?
until I close up
like a butterfly backwards
regressing into childhood.

I am beautiful now -
don’t make me ugly.

11/98
