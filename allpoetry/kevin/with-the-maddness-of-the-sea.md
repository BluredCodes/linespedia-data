title: "with the maddness of the sea"

My eyes again wander in the green sea,
of blindness and love's shared sorrow - 
I wonder where you went away to
and if agony persists on the other side.

youth's complex madness, 
finally become as empty as it began
let's lend each other hands, start anew -
we might touch the rain-tears

each grain of sand cries out at the loss:
each magnificent rainbow of each overturning sail
each darkness grows, at the end of a day

from darkness, amazed by the persistence of the sun
I remember the rolling green hills of Tuscany
and wish I’d found my love there
and that it would never flee againsometimes, in death, things hurt more than they did being born...
