title: "blissful shall the children find us"

I understand that smile now
of history and growth and chance.
I respect our differences,
your individuality is safe with me.

all I want is your trust, your love, your faith
all I ask is understanding, communication, emotion -
let us transcend everydaylife and, through love, dance among the angels.

the rest? blissful shall the children find us.
