I am a good story, I am the red girl with black hair leaving home.
a montlake drainpipe was dry and warm, but it wasn't home
I left myself behind with the family pet - each dependency sickens me,
again a hired killer, each screaming drip echoes into

the silence I bought as I removed the headphones for the first time that day,
what could I understand without music?
No more the twisting melodies into the echoing church
No more the soft whispers of lost children along the bony corridor
It's an underground-alone

counting the dark paths I can't remember
entire lives full of anti-capitalist ramblings, love and it's gloss
so badly needing a haircut
each step towards the wall, I abandon again the urge for homehmm
