the sword anduril
leaps toward my throat
stabs the third eye,
of foresight,
telling me to  r e l a x
that happiness can only be found through
r e l a x 
and that we need to be satisfied 
with helping people, notjust
being betterbetterbest

but idripaway
when you pull out
my spine
