show me your blue toe (your secret)show me what you meant by lovedidn't leave my too-tight tie at the doorthis strangling dream real purple mist:our slick tongues talkingthe crystal broke, I said stopties to unhappiness: words '$', symbols: heartglowing embers down the drainwhirlpool sunset - a single treea single leaf, a single word
