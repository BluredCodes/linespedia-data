two northwesterners
walk
      together
long hair,
sweet smiles,
jokingly serious - 
hardened, evolved women
at one with snow & ice & rain.
kind still through the hardwork
we change and we

      forget

adjustments make easy
old comfort to present pain
we enjoy, we thrive.

      (& survive)
