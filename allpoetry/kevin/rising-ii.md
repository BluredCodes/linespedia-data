as each rising ember lends itself to me
and the flames remind me of the theme
when a bird, rising, found himself in flame
at each turn he saw his end approach
but really, dying isn't such a bad thing
since you become yourself again
and the moon you lost is your own again
and dreams are so suddenly reachable againwatched a fire tonight.  Trying to be inspired.  it is _so hard_ to think and write at the same time!
