the sinking thought in me
at seeing all the lonely
sitting by themselves
each ringing their own bell
but not listening for each other

when will we see
that happiness is free...Just a short emotional study.  Like a sketch, only with a poem.  Does anyone else do these?  Have an idea where I should take this in a longer work?
