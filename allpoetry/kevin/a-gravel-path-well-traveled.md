shadows play overhead
looking around, I
can't find which direction is up
point me home & give a push
two dreams turned to stones underfoot.

I am daily outdone in greatness
history's leaders looking down, laughed
&, appreciating yesterday a day late,
I sink into the muddy stream of mediocrity.

tell me, teacher, which meaning
is the right one, which path?

can't walk away from passion", "be
