I'm just trying to learn -
don't let the sword fall
my shoulders are hunched
I await your call.

the call of midnight, rising
the thousand rainy days
crouched in this warm bright castle
the error of my ways

become immensly clear, its the,
the darkness that I need
bring me love's obscurity
I beg, I beg, I plead.
