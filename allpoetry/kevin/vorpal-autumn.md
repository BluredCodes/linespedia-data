title: "vorpal autumn"

maple leaves floundering in puddles
this years rain proven too much
I'm swallowing it, trying to save them
as if taking so much upon myself
could be good for anyone.

its only as we really try
that things begin to break down
the floundering after success
the one to look out for

this madness youth slipping away
my dreams to truth, die innocence
snic snak, snic snak, cut it off
blood spouting, a third eye socket

helpless cries in midnight air
screaming silence, I know fear;
these things I have said before
hear as the dreams begin to roar
