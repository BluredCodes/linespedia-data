title: "standing and leaning"

I need to be able to
       stand on my own two feet
and yet lean on you comfortably

There is a fear
       I shall jump and
   hope you'll catch me
but you won't be able to
              (I am too heavy)
   and I shall fall
       and it shall hurt
so I
 need to be able to stand alone
 but touch and speak and listen
              in a way that will
              augment myself.
ultimately, we are selfish
but for now, kind.
