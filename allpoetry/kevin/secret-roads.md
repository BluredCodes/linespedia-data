whichway do you lead today?left hand up, stuff the right in your pantsat your frown, my heart bends, twistingaway into some secure other universeevery dissapointment echoes to yougreenroad to red: watch the autumn decaythis merry path of socalled friendshipa fire's center, oblivious of it's edgewhere is this going?
