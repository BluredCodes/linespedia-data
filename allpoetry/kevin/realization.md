I walk by, and
your red&blue lights flashing, and
heaven's gauntlet settles over me.friend got a speeding ticket.  *wave* Jon.
An interesting quote: "Hey Jon, you're going twice the speed limit"- Geeda
"Isn't that a federal offence?"- Kevin
-- 2 minutes before Jon got pulled over
