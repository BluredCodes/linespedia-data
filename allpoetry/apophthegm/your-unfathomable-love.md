title: "Your unfathomable love"

I never cared to look into your eyes
To fathom its love for me,
 Never had I felt it won’t demise-
  My ecstasy was your only glee.
You wanted my shoulder to cry upon
And I never heeded your tears,
Your compassions for me weighted a ton
But I never even called you ‘my dear’.
Your worries for me never stopped;
I deemed it so inane,
Now that my heart got well mopped
I grasp that I was all profane.
The lusts I craved for was dead as bust
Was only a fleeting delight,
And to ply me with bliss was your only lust
I was never lost from your sight. 
I love you now, I’ll love you forever
I swear I’ll never stop,
Please come back and don’t go ever
I vow that my love won’t clog.
I cry in vain and you’re there unstrained
In the dome of the virtuous souls;
I shed my tears at your epitaph unstained- 
Our love had never reached its goals.
