title: "Me and my solitude"

Here I am, on my sole- At a chat with my isolation.Me and my solitude, Gossip about the ambiance,Listen to the sound of silenceAnd hear the never heard music-The music of seclusion.All the chatters I know ofMay leave me now or then,Yet my solitude never isolates meWill always listen to my tale-Of love, of life, of tearsOf charm, disgust and fears, To him I keep no privacy As he is my only empathyMy loneliness is my sole ally I talk to him at my impasseOnly my heart beats encompassOur ambiance; I love my isolation, But for him I’m zilch (same am I to him),We’re at a sweetest bondageWe share a pain and gleeAnd we speak the same languageAs both of us is ‘me’.
