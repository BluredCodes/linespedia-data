Life is a ride to heaven or to hell
Life is a rose with brief, fleeting smell,
Life is a yo-yo with its ups and downs
Life is circus, with emotions as clowns.
Life is the superset of all joy and tear
Life is a vehicle, with God reining the gear;
Life is a toy with which kismet plays-
If nemesis is a hunter, then life is its prey.
Life is short, shorter than a kiss
Life is a chance that we often tend to miss
Life is a boon to the blessed and cheery, 
Life spells doom to the fateful and gloomy,   
Life is a chrysalis that looms and expires
Life is cobweb- all mortals are wired
Life is a law no being can defy
Life is a puzzle, you solve it and die.
