Love is the portal that leads to bliss
Love is your sweetheart’s passionate kiss
Love is the network connecting all hearts
Love is the potion that sweetens all tarts
Love is the beam in the time of tears
Love is the screen that hides us from fears
Love is a merchant that offers us dreams
If life is an ocean then love is its stream
Love is the magic gripping our souls
Love is the catalysts stirring us to goals
Love is the hermit that sees no evil
Love is the saint that drives away the devil
Love can be pain still we find it pleasant
Love can pour rain to the hopeless peasants
Love is the epilogue to the epic named life
Love is never a prologue; it welcomes no strife.
