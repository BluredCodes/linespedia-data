---
title: "Melody in desolation"
writer: "Apophthegm"
slug: "melody-in-desolation"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/17471-Melody-in-desolation-by-Apophthegm"
---

Grayness plagues my soul,
In the desert of despair I am all alone
Left with a water sack, whom you call hope
Love- my camel to ship me towards redemption 
I hear melody, amidst my desolation
The melody of love, faith and fancy
Globules of hope keep me swarming
Alive; into the direness of agony
The tune of love tells me to go on
Few more miles to go and glee is my tenure
Mesmerized with that melody I continue my chase 
Enduring the tang of fear, pain and dereliction
I heave my body to an unknown destination
Where life frees me from all sobs, 
When the bag of hope dies, I die
With a set of pains and gains I collected
Tuned with the music of life,
Booed with the terror of strife
Yet the music sovereigns the house of the souls
 I know pleasure triumphs
Enthralled with that melody, charged by hope
Zilch desert turns full to me
A wreath of joy and a carpet of glee
