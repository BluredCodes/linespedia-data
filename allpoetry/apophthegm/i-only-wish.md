---
title: "I only wish..."
writer: "Apophthegm"

I see you crying, forgetting yourself 
Against the photo of your love,
You scream against your fate; 
You were destined to mourn.
For no one could hold back Death,
I see you beat your head 
Against the wall, screaming your love’s name-
My name; but you can never see me-
I see you; in your black mourning dress, scarf
Eyes swollen with tears, shaking red lips-  
You still look pretty 
To me, you will forever be, if only you could hear 
My words, which had been a melody to you.
I walk beside my grave, I cannot cry, cannot howl,
Don’t need them anymore, yet I feel it more painful 
Than death, to see you crying for me whilst I am with you, 
Will always be, our bodies weren’t in love, our souls
Uttered the oath of being together, forever .
I don’t complain my death,
Only feel the pain of seeing you without your smile
May be you’ll redeem it, time changes everything
Let’s hope it does, until then miss me, but not my love
For it was never made to die,    
Soon my time with you shall be over
And I will start a new life, a life as a dead
I have nothing to complain God, 
For He blessed me with your love, 
Yet a man’s want never ends, I only wish
I could stay with you a little longer
Love you a little longer, I only wish...
