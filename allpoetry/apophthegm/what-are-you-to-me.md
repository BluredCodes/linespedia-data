title: "What are you to me...?"
writer: "Apophthegm"

Do you want to know?Why are you to me the only vow?A gem that will forever glowIn my heart.From where should I start?You are the joy that always makes me smileThe very best chapter to my profileYou are to me the only motto,A sweet apothegm, a captivating photoFramed in my vision,The jewel of my dreams.I win my race,Through the portrait of your marvel faceThat boosts my speed at a wondrous pace.To me you are all glory.Amazing how my life went onWhen we hadn’t met- Yet I know without you now,I will drop down dead.Even if I die,The story of you and me shall never fade,As long as love here rules this space,Our love shall remain.
