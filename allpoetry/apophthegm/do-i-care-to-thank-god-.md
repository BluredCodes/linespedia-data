---
title: "Do I care to thank God ?"
writer: "Apophthegm"
slug: "do-i-care-to-thank-god-"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/42732-Do-I-care-to-thank-God---by-Apophthegm"
---

I’m still breathing, when a million breaths are stopped,
I’m still eating, when millions can not!
Still I pray to God that He grant me more,
Instead of thanking life, I place all my abhor.
I lurk in the sunlight and still I need more light,
But there are people, who have darkness still to fight!

I dread a mere insect, when millions die of war,
I curse that blackhead, but thousands have battle scar.
Still I say to God that life should give me more,
Even I curse this world when her weather is too cold.
There are millions who, live barely clad in winter
Still they struggle to live, they die but never surrender.

I have hands to hold, but millions are orphaned,
When I catch a cold, the environment is blamed.
I have my rights read, many don’t even know theirs;
I only snob about, without shedding true tears.
Do I ever thank God, that I am well-off than a lot
The best I do is blame and curse my lot.
