The letter of my heartbreak
Lay on the bedside table.
Newly finished; carefully inked name not yet dry.
I stared at it, wondering what it might mean.
What it could mean – the possible explosives carried in a single white       Hallmark envelope.
When writing it, everything had made perfect sense – 
The reasons for our parting, the regret, sorrow and love.
But now, as the prospect of losing
What I agreed, in those words, should be lost
Became real entities of pain,
I realised I could not afford to make a mistake with Us,
And tore those hateful logics away.
