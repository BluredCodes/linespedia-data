I'm sorry for everything I've done to youFor all the pain I've put you throughYou helped me with my problems again and againBut all I would do was comitt the same sinYou searched your heart and soul for ways to help meBut I was the one that held the keyI would always choose the wrong path to takeAnd as I would do this your heart would breakI broke your heart more and moreThen one day it finally toreWhat a selfish person I must beTo let your heart break because of meThis was the worst thing I could ever doBecause the last thing I had in mind was hurting you
