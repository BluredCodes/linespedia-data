title: "I Don't Need You"

My mind is surrounded in complete darkness
I don't want you to tell me how I feel or what you think
I don't need you in my mind
But I do

My mind is surrounded by the light you gave me
I don't want you to ask me whats wrong or tell me that you care 
I don't need your help
But I do

My mind is surrounded by hate for myself
You know this, but I didn't tell you, you found out on your own
I don't need you to know
But I do

My mind is explored by you, the darkest secrets that I keep
You know of everything in my mind that I hide from you
I don't need you in my mind
But I doOK hmm.... well this is the first poem that I have written without rhyme.... I'm actually pretty proud of it.... I think that this is probably my deepest poem. It's basically about the same person I wrote "I'm Sorry" "Thinking of You" and "I Wish" This person has helped me so much - she means the world to me.... she is always in my head, I can't hide a thing from her.... and thats probably a good thing!
