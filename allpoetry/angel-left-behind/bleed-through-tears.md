---
title: "Bleed Through Tears"
writer: "Angel Left Behind"
slug: "bleed-through-tears"

As I bleed through my tears
I erase all my fears
Fear of not being loved at all 
Fear of getting up just to fall
Tears run from my eyes
And I forget about all the lies
Lies I've told to people that care
And everything I've done that was unfair
Tears release me from everything inside
As I bleed and let go of what I hide
