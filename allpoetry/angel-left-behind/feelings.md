title: "Feelings..."
writer: "Angel Left Behind"

Feelings of hate, feelings of guilt
Like a wall these feelings are built
As time goes by, the wall gets taller
My faith in myself getting smaller

Feelings of sorrow, feelings of pain
All are depressing, just like the rain
Longing to cry all my emotions out
But all I can do is scream and shout

I wish I could be happy, I wish I could be free
Free from all this guilt, free from this misery
I wish I could be free from the dark side of life
It's so very cruel, just like the edge of a knife

These feelings will not stay without a fight
I will try to overcome them with all my might
My soul will be free, my soul will fly
But only if, I kiss these feelings goodbye.
