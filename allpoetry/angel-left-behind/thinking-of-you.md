title: "Thinking of You"

I think to myself, “What should I do?”
And I can’t help but to think of you
You’re always giving me great advice,
And this I will not sacrifice 

When I think of what you would say,
All my doubts are washed away
And confusion is left behind,
For the answer has been defined

You have always told me what is right
When it is dark you show me light
You help me see through clear eyes
And you always hear my silent cries

One day I know that I will see 
All that you have given to me
And all the things I’ve gotten through
Just because I thought of youThis poem is about my teacher/friend/therapist. I don't know where I would be right now if she wasn't there for me...
