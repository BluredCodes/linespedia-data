It hurts me to know you feel this way inside
And behind your smile is the pain that you hide
You shut everyone out to deal with this alone
And I can't tell if you're ok over the phone

If you ever need me I will always be there
I will always listen, you know that I care
I can't tell you I know how you feel
But I can tell you from this you will heal

Don't let yourself slip back to the cruel dark side
Where your feelings and pain you hide deep inside
You don't deserve the treatment that you got
But hopefully you see the lesson that was taughtI wrote this poem for my best friend who just got out of a bad relationship....
