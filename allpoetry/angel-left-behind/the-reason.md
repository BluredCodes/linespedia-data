I am overwhelmed in all my painAs I intensely stare at my veinHow much I want to cut it and watch it bleedBut there are people who care and this they don't needI could easily give into the knifeOr even cut deeper and end my own lifeBut this would be breaking God's plan for meSo the reason I am living is for He??,
