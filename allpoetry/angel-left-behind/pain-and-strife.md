---
title: "Pain and Strife"
writer: "Angel Left Behind"
slug: "pain-and-strife"

As she lies awake, failure fills her mind
Her reason for living she cannot find
In so many ways she's messed up her life
And all she's given is pain and strife
She is so sorry, does anybody know?
For so very long she's felt so low
Nobody can see that inside she's crying
She can't even see that her spirits dying
All she can see is people don't care
She doesn't know, but this makes her heart tear
Her heart is broken, tears fill her soul
But in the end she will pay the toll
The toll for causing all this pain and strife
Oh how I wish this wasn't my life
