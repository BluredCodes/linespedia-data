---
title: "Touched"
writer: "Angel Left Behind"
slug: "touched"
source: "AllPoetry"

You talked to me when I was feeling down
You made me take a look around
Forcing me to see what I couldn't
And helping me to see what I wouldn't
When I feel like no ones there
You always have a way of showing you care
When I felt like there was nothing to life
You were there through my pain and strife
You made me feel like I was worth the while
And you are always able to make me smile
For everything you've done, I thank you so much
For my heart and soul you have deeply touched
