As the blood drips from my wristThe vampires creep out from the mistI am now his new obsessionHe wants me to be in his possessionI hear you creep in the dark of nightSo I guess I will give up without a fightI am too scared to tell you noSo with you I must now goSoon I will be part of the nightBlood I will crave and I shall see no more light
