I wish I never lied to youI wish I would have kept the words I said trueI can't believe I did this, You mean alot to meYou cared so much and I was too selfish to seeBut it's too late now, your trust for me is toreBut your the reason I don't hurt myself anymoreIf it wasn't for you I might not be livingSo thank you for your kindness and all of your giving
