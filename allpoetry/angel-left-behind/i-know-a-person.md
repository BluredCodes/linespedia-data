---
title: "I Know a Person"
writer: "Angel Left Behind"
slug: "i-know-a-person"

I know a person who treats me bad
Fills me with guilt and makes me sad
Takes me to a place so black
It is the happiness that I lack
I am not good enough, could it be?
I wish I knew why I am drowning in misery
It makes me live in an eternity of Hell
Could it be, I am an angel that fell?
I know a person who makes me cry
who's life is nothing but a lie
This person tries to buy my love
with so many things, such as a dove
It is not my love that needs to be bought
It is loving that needs to be taught
For I am not the one who's love isn't there
He is the one that just doesn't care
I know a person who makes me sad
This person is my very own dad
