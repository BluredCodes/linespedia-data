Ode To Rexanna    Look to the east this etheral night,   Orion is stalking the sky.   Jupiter rules with her children supreme,   Commanding, Demanding from on high.   And if the stars seem brighter,   brighter, ever so slight,   Perhaps it's because an Angel gave in,   And Rexanna joined the Heavens this night.   She was still a child, with a cherubs smile,   Spreading happiness all the while.   Too young to be burdened with the loathsome striff,   That came to her from the Netherlife.   Rexy with the big brown eyes,  Ivory teeth,  and puppies thrist for fun.   Rexy you darling dark haired beauty,   whose life had just begun.   Go rest in the Bosom of Jesus,   your demons are nevermore.   And maybe someday, we'll meet again,   As we pass through Heavens door.   RIP Rexanna
