She doesn't believe in miracles,
she's never really tried,
she's never heard 
the fluttering wings,
of angels passing by.
How can anyone 
live their life,
without believing in anything?
Because if you don't believe in anything, 
what makes life interesting?I wrote this in my summer school accelerated english class...i'm a nerd i know..but it wasn't for class.  This actually stemmed from a person whom i've strongly disliked all my life.  She doesn't believe in miracles and that got me upset.  I was like how can you not? So I wrote this...
