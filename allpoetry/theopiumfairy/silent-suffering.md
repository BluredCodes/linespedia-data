---
title: "Silent Suffering"
writer: "theopiumfairy"
slug: "silent-suffering"

I hear you crying
But I cannot help.
I hear your voice, 
full of suffering
I just want to hold you in my arms,
safe away from harm.
Your memories come back to haunt you
And I cannot ease your pain.
I love you so very much
But you are so far away,
in a place of misery and pain.
I just want to make you happy once again.
I wish that there were ways
For me to help you dear,
to keep you from retreating to that place,
a place of pain, sorrow, and fear.
I love you very much,
but I cannot ease the pain
Please help me to understand
So I can show you,
to love again.
