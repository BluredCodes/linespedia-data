Why won’t this shit stop? The voices in my head just keep ranting and probing at me, asking why? why? why? why? and all I can ever answer is I don’t know, don’t understand, don’t care.  I just don’t care. Just leave me alone for a change.  I don’t need everyone’s fucking concern.  I’m fine.  I know exactly what’s wrong with me…and with you.  I don’t need help.  Just because my head is out of the clouds and I have a grip on things doesn’t mean I’m a fucking psycho.  It doesn’t mean I have to be locked up.  Fuck you all.  I mean it.  Go take your naïve heads out of the clouds and get back to the real world.  What are you afraid of? That you don’t exist?  You do.  But existence is all the more painful.Um this was really weird for me to write.  It isn't directed at anyone in particular.  I think that it may have been my subconciouss speaking, so don't flame. Thanx!
