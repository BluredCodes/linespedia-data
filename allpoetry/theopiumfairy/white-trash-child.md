title: "White Trash Child"
writer: "theopiumfairy"

Look at me, 
discarded white trash, 
with dirt on my face, 
and blood on my hands.

I'm not good enough 
to be around you.
It's written on my face 
that I'm raggy and used.

I know that you're ashamed 
of what I am like,
but you caused the disaster,
that is my life.

I won't go away,
I won't disappear, 
you can close your eyes 
and I'll still be here.This is about people constantly blaming me for their problems and about people that just look at me and don't see hwta i really am...it's kinda mixed up.
