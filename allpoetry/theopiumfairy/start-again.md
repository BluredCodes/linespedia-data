I can’t take this anymore.
I just want to stop trying, 
I need to give up.  
Everything I do is wrong,
No matter what it is, 
How hard I try.
I don’t want to be me anymore.
I want a new face,
A new life,
I want people to see me for my efforts, 
who I am
not what they see.
I may be different, 
but yet I am the same as everyone else inside.
Except
I try.
But you can only get so far before you fall, 
and have to peel yourself off the floor, 
and start again.I am having alot of trouble lately with peoples expectations of me, and so I decided to write a poem on this subject...I won't go into alot of detail...all I vcan say is that I had a rough day.
