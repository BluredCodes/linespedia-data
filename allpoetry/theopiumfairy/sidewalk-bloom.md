title: "Sidewalk Bloom"

Struggling to grow
between the cracks
of the pavement,
the little flower stretches
up towords the sun.  
But as it grows,
beneath the suns rays
the pavement begins
to put pressure on it's root of being.
The flower wilts as it gives up,
and the petals start to fall,
the sun loses it's brilliance 
and the flower wilts and dies.
