title: "Watch You Die"

I want to beat you
until your eyes swell shut
and blood flows from your lips.
I never want to hear
your voice again.
I never want you to 
ever touch me again.
I want you to burn.
Better yet, 
I want to watch you burn.
I want to watch your skin melt,
before my very eyes,
your bones turn to sand and ash, your eyes explode.
I want to hear you fat
hiss and bubble, as you descend to hell.
And I want to put you in your coffin.
For Good.
