---
title: "Puzzle"
writer: "theopiumfairy"
slug: "puzzle"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/41975-Puzzle-by-theopiumfairy"
---

Pieces of you 
are within me,
pieces I can never see.
Hidden from my eyes, 
leading secreet lives.
Never letting me be free,
never letting me be me.

Pieces of you 
are within me, 
pieces I can never be.
Taken away, 
leading me astray,
never taking me the right way.
It seems impossible to get away.

Pieces of you
are within me,
peices everybody sees.
The freedom I will never have,
the life that I will never lead, 
all because of you,
all because of me.

Will I ever see, these peices of you in me?
