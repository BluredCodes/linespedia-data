This is just a test poem.  
Then again,
I am just a test person.
You can rip me open, 
see how I work,
I won't care
just as you can
look deep into this poem,
and see nothing there.
You can cut me,
and watch me ooze blood,
just as you can squeeze this poem,
and watch the emotions seep out.
You can smash me up,
you can kill me.
Just as you can kill my words,
by crumpling up this page.
