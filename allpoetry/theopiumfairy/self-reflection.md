I'm taking my time not caring.
I won't take any abuse.
All I feel is numness.
All i am is used.
I am a void, your creation. 
Designed to take up space. 
Souless, empty, misted insides. 
Care to take a look?
It'll be one hell of a ride.
Because it will be like
looking inside your own reflection.
