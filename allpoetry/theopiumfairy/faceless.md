The power of persausion
clouds the mind
of faceless wonders
nobody is left to stand out
of the crowd of lost souls
Broken and jealous hearts mingle
everyone else is just there
to pour salt on open wounds
that are trying in vain to heal
For once there is no voice 
to pull you from the midst
because everyone is the same
nobody is above
and only those who you imagine
with your disillusioned mind
are below
dellerious and starving for hope
the only person you can depend on
is your old self
and your old self 
is a fabled, wistful memory
of what life should be like...
but isn't
This is the way life is
you must be self deceptive
to live a pathetic illusionummm...i was kinda pissed off at all the narrow minded people at my school and stuff when i wrote this...alot of people don't understand it...if you don't that's my fault not yours
