It’s not you, it’s me
The line I’ve heard too many times.
Why couldn’t you just see, 
What you meant to me,
How much you changed my life 
but then made it crumble and shatter?
You chose another girl,
Not knowing how you’d change my world.
What am I to do?
Say it’s over and we’re through?
Well I can’t do that
You were the only one I ever loved.
To me, you were an angel,
sent from above.
So you’ve left me behind,
put me out of your mind.
I loved you once before.
Now I don’t love you anymore.
