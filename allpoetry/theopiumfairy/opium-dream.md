title: "Opium Dream"

Soft clouds of opium
lift me high on ecstasy, 
suck me dry
of all that's me,
just to feel alive.

Come crashing down,
the clouds dissolved,
the high is gone, 
no more peaceful bliss,
simply dead reality.
No more ignorance.

Slip into another dream,
I wander through the tree's of green,
I see the rainbows
brilliant colours,
the unicorns glistening mane.
Beyond there is a castle,
high up in,
the opium clouds.

Below me is my body,
waiting for my mind to come back,
so I can awake
from this opium dream.
