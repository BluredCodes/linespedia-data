---
title: "I'm Here"
writer: "theopiumfairy"

I wish I could say that I'm worthy to be in your presence.  But I'm not.  I guess that's why people avoid me.  Is there honestly anything wrong with me? I'm friendly enough...am I too friendly?...but I never get close.  Am I worthy to be anyones friend?  Or am I here just for decoration?  I don't want to be a fly on the wall...don't you see?  LOOK AT ME! I'm here...
