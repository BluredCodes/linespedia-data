Oh,love that will not let me go,
emblazoned on my heart
since the toy soldiers vied,
and a Steiff was at my side,
tenacious through storms of disbelief,
sophisticated reasoning,
and cold indifference.
Thomas?
Oh, I knew Thomas well,
understood what he was about,
but Thomas learned from his doubt.
Essences spiritual can't be viewed,
a deep, deep faith is imbued,
a peaceful calm I now know,
oh, love that will not let me go.
