---
title: "Rumors of Heaven"
writer: "Jane"
slug: "rumors-of-heaven"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/38130-Rumors-of-Heaven-by-Jane"
---

Did we laugh as much
as my heart remembers,
and slay nights' dragons
with tomorrow in our arms?

A love warm enough 
to shame the sun, as it
bound us soul to soul,
with rumors of Heaven.
