The Cardinal seeks
in deepening snow,
seeds that winter
cannot grow,
his vibrant plumes
of brilliant red
recalls blood
that does not flow.

A winged clot,
he comes to call
at the feeder
in winter's pall,
to sate himself
above the frost;
congealed blood
upon a cross.
