title: "Roses"

I bought roses today-
tall proud ladies in pink,
that shed no tears,
that know no fear,
only the beauty of God's hand.
These ladies comfort me
in a world changed forever
in a twinkling of shattered glass,
debris, fire, and hate.
Inert in beauty, they whisper
that some things stay the same.
I bought roses today.

            ( Sept. 14, 2001)
