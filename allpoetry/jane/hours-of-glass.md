---
title: "Hours of Glass"
writer: "Jane"

Determined tides
of orange summers
lap at the years,frothy
with yesterdays' moons,
while feet of feathers
make footprints on a cloud.
Seamed leather skin
tanned by time,clings taut
over bones, and shrinks
from the wind, to hide
behind hours of glass.
