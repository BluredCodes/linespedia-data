---
title: "Brainwashed"
writer: "Jane"
slug: "brainwashed"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/7925-Brainwashed-by-Jane"
---

To be brainwashed
by those timbers crossed,

with promises of crystal chimes,
to see again those long lost,

and gather back old times,
would make Heaven gold,

as the legions have told
who had visions of those climes.

To be brainwashed
by those timbers crossed,

and have faith in the story,
keeps the soul from being storm tossed,
as it awaits the final glory.
