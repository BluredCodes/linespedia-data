---
title: "It's Hard to Say Goodbye"
writer: "Jane"
slug: "its-hard-to-say-goodbye"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/7168-Its-Hard-to-Say-Goodbye-by-Jane"

A basketful of fall
overflowing with red and gold,
a shawl of diamond snow,
and never feel the cold,
a necklace of spring,
a ring of summer's glow,
to take along earth's seasons
when it's my time to go.

Deep pockets full of love
lasting through the years,
a purse with children's laughter
coming after tears,
memories of music,
that stirred my heart so;
precious earthly baggage
when it's my time to go.

For what is left behind,
I'll meet God with a sigh,
and wonder at His Heavens,
but it's hard to say goodbye.
