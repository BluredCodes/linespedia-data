They must have smelled it in New York,
certainly in Washington DC,

the stench of burning flesh
that roiled the clouds,and

fouled the trees day, after day
month after month, year after year.

They must have heard them in New York,
certainly in Washington DC,

the screaming pleading voices
that trailed to nothingness.

They must have seen it in New York,
certainly in Washington DC,

plumes of smoke rising higher, higher
to disperse generation after generation

of innocent Jews who, have haunted
to this day, those who chose to look away.
