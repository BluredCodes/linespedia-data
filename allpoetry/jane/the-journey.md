title: "The Journey"
writer: "Jane"

How long will the journey take?It will take seasons,and seasons of changeWill there be smiles or painful heartache?Whatever you, and the fates arrangeWhat scenery on the way will unfold?Meadows of sun,and dark waters deepIn swirling tides, how will I keep hold?By love and strong faith you will keepHow will I know when the journey is o'er?When the heart with memories singsWill I find rest on that final moor?Yes, beautiful sleep that eternity brings.
