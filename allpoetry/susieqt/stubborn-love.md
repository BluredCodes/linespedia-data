It's your stubborn love that never lets go of me 
That brings out the very best that I can be 
It's your tenacity that holds on to this woman 
And never lets her be a fallen one 
It's your persistent love that keeps me going
Like a river in its flowing 

Your stubborn love embraces my failing heart
Making it a place to ne’er depart
It’s your enduring love that keeps me together
Helping me to survive stormy weather
It’s your unwavering faith in me that keeps me
On the path to a life that’s free

This stubborn love of yours enriches my soul
Touching places and making them whole
It’s your ongoing love that shows me the way
Keeping me safe from life’s fray
It’s your unselfish gift that changed my life
Protecting me from trouble that’s rife

Stubborn love changes the very heart inside
No need for one to run and hide
Stubborn love that holds on in tough times
Supporting those fragile minds
Stubborn love never lets go when put to the test
It’s where I have found my inner rest
