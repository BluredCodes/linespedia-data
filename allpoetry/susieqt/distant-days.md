Distant days lost in a far away haze
Dawns cast adrift in endless oceans
Sunsets weave their magic potions
Nights of dark and dismal gloom

Weeks of sorrow unfurling now
Sen-nights of harrowing eternities
Months move in a strange slowness
Time is speedily lagging behind

Memories dim as time travels on
In some way I notice you are gone
Alone today for yesterday is done
Will my tomorrows never come?

Alone again in spaces unknown
How quickly the time has flown
Standing aloof and left all proud
All I want is to hide in the crowd

Distant days lost in a far away haze
Realities dreams have now gone on
Left behind am I in time immortal
Where then did spread its portal?
