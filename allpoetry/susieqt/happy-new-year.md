Ring in the new and toll out the old
Come one and all and now be bold
The new will soon be here
Don’t waste it all in fear

Celebrate with love and giving
Now is the time to start living
A new year on the horizon
Time now to go a’blazen 

The future is looming just ahead
No time now to laze in your bed
Shake off the old inhibitions
Make some new traditions

Happy New Year we do now shout
As we usher the old on its way out
Come on and have some fun
Before the old is really done

New days are just around the bend
No time left to borrow or to lend
Auld langsyne we will sing
This new year in, we bring
