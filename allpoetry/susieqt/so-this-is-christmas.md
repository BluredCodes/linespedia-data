title: "So! This Is Christmas"

So! This is Christmas, that time of year
When we wish each other good cheer
A time for thanksgiving for the gift
That was sent to us to bind the rift

So! This is Christmas when we all hear
A Savior is born, no need now to fear
He came as a babe in a lowly state
All our sins now laid upon his plate

So! This is Christmas a time of good will
The gift he brought then is available still
Shepherds in the field were a little afraid
The angel told them He will be your aid

So! This is Christmas, a peace on earth
He came that we might have a new birth
Wise men from the east followed a star
They traveled with their gifts from afar

So! This is Christmas, what will you do
With the gift that was given just for you
He left all the splendor of heaven to come
To die for you even if you were the only one
