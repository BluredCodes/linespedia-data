title: "Who Am I ?"

I often wonder as the days go by 
Who am I?
Am I the woman others see
Or am I ... me.. the one I choose to be
Where is she, the one I want to be?
Where is the woman deep inside??
Is she buried in all the lies: 
In all the needs of others
Will she arise from the ashes of time 
Like the Phoenix ,,,,,in her prime

There is so much that pertains to me
A wife, a mother, a friend  and a lover
How I long to discover,
The woman deep inside of  me
To let her be free, to rise and take her place
For all the world to see,
The one that is really me
The truth of my Soul and my Spirit
That is part of the all that I am
I will arise and be all that  I can

Can I dare to let you see all that is within
Will all my passions be in vain
Or will you be the one to gain
All that I am and am called to be
So can I let this woman free
To be all she wants to be
And not what others need her to  be
Can she fly this woman in me
Will she touch the heavens
And learn to be free?

Yes  I will arise and go forth to discover
All  that I am and all that is really me
For, yes,  I   like this woman I   really am
For I am just me,  no  more no less....
I will arise and go free and be who I want to be
And let no man or woman stop me
From being the woman 
I am destined to be
I am Woman, wild and free
Being  all that pertains to me
