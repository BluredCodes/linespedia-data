pace upon the shoulder of my heart
those glimpses that smother my kindling thoughts
sucked into an oblivion
that is you

but like oxygen 
you rush furiously through a roaring mind
the creator of dancing, flickering flames-
red in jealousy but an internal being
that is a faint blue-white with purity
burning into my soul

ever lingering- you steal away
silently as you had first came
in the midst of one fairy night
for she manipulates the winds
she commands your heart
