'SHOWERS IN THE SUNSHINE' 

Windows open. 
Dark cumulus hovering above. 
A crack of light. A sonic boom. 
A sudden gush. A resurgent spray. 
Drops asunder. Distant thunder. 

The month of June. Back to school. 
Gumdrops and gumboots. Huddled buses. 
Crowded benches. Moistured play. 
Muddy football. Boats at bay. 
Drops asunder. Distant thunder. 

A nubile breeze. A flush of love. 
Windswept rocks. Crashing waves. 
Hand in hand. Lips on lips. 
A parasol sole. A momentary slip. 
Drops asunder. Distant thunder. 

Picnics in office. Rainy treks. 
The smell of soil. A steady patter. 
Walking on tracks. Potholes gauge. 
Climbing peaks. Coming of age. 
Drops asunder. Distant thunder. 

Two become one. Treacle moon. 
Shores call. Tempests rage. 
Beads of sweat. Fertile land. 
A fresh crop. Printed sand. 
Drops asunder. Distant thunder. 

June comes soon. A new monsoon...

- Iznogoud.
