I have a twin. 
He's inside me. 
Sharing my quarters. 
In those dark grey recesses. 

I think. He speaks. 
I cry. He laughs. 
I'm bounded. He flies. 
I wake. He dreams.

Sometimes I'm he, he's me. 
But never are we one. 
Which is sad. 

Because he's living my life. 
In a mirror. 

Double-faced. 
Insincere. 
Almost pretentious. 

I'm not a schizo. 
But, he's one. 
I'm not me. 
He's he.  

I want to lose him. 
But, he won't let go. 

And last night I realised. I prayed. 

Prayed, he doesn't have a twin.
