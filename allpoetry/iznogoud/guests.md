'GUESTS' 

They've arrived again. 
From a place I've never visited. 
Unwanted. But loved.

They'll steal a piece of my room. 
Invade my private space. 

They'll be pampered. 
Delicious treats. Clean bedsheets. 

They'll be shown the sights. 
City streets. Bright lights. 

Guests are like that. 
But then I guess, we're all guests too. 

And when it's time, I'll visit another place.
