'A DAY'

I rise. 
To the smell of yesterday's ash. 

I walk. 
Towards a still unlit one. 

I breathe. 
The clouds in the morning sky. 

I live. 
The day, stub by stub. 

I retire. 
Another fag strewn.  

I sleep. 
Extinguished by the night.
