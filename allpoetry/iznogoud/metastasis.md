‘METASTASIS’

I hated the person I loved. 
She used me. 
Feeding on my soft tissues. 
Malignant, while I was benign. 

A tumour embedded in my heart. 

She lapped up whatever I gave. 
Spreading under my skin. 
Till, I played surgeon. 
Deft laser strokes.
