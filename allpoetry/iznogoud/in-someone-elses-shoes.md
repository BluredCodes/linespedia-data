If I could be you, 
what would I see myself as? 

A lover. 
A loner. 
A loser. 
Perhaps. 

If I could be you, 
what would I be to you? 

A pillar. 
A mirror.
A shadow.
Maybe. 

If I could be you, 
what would you be to me? 

Some questions beget no answers. 

- Iznogoud
