'LYCANTHROPE' 

Dark moonlight. 
Silent beds. 

A ravenous wolf. 
Feast of flesh. 
Fresh dripping crimson. 

Padded paws. 
Night vision. 
Muffled growls. 

Dripping dribble. 
Plastered breath. 
Makes his way. 

Little Red Riding Hood. 
Beneath a cosy hood. 
He pauses to lust. 

Feeds. Ravages. 
Incestuously destroys. 
The last vestige of innocence. 

Night after night, the soft spoken man 
Unleashes the beast within.
