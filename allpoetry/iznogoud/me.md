‘ME’ 

I write. 
Words that fill my plate. 
Words to change someone’s fate. 

I write. 
Stifling creativity. 
Fuelling consumerism. 

I write. 
For others, in public. 
For my soul, in private. 

I write. 
For someone else. 

Hence. 
I write. 
My own epitaph.
