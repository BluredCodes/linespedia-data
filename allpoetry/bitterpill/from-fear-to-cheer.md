title: "From Fear To Cheer"
writer: "BitterPill"

Fear!
I had never felt such intense emotion,
as I walked into that gray, dismal room.
It was filled with nothing but sadness,
remorseful tears and gloom.

There they were,
side by side,
just as they had always been.
My Mother in her lacy white dress,
Father in his Sunday best,
and me, alone to contend.

No smiles graced their faces,
as I stood gazing in awe.
No comfort or compassion,
as they had given me,
so many a time before.

How I yearned to feel my mother's arms,
in a loving, warm embrace.
My Father to take his handkercheif,
and wipe the tears from my face.

Standing there, so alone,
I realized this journey, 
will be long and concrete.
I can tread this path
with courage and strength,
love will guide my feet.
