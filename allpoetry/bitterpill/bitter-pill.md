I've felt so ill lately,having my dose of a bitter pill.Sadness, anger and enlightenment,it all began to unveil.Humanity is suffering,far beyond human comprehension.For this bitter pill,was a dose of pure resentment.A heart torn apart,with enormous loss in life,Brought the bitter pill,so much pain, sorrow and strife.Perhaps it is better,to take a bitter pill with a grain of salt.It slides down more easily,with a wonderful lesson taught.dedicated to my cyber mom.
