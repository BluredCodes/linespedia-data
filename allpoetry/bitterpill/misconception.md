---
title: "Misconception"
writer: "BitterPill"

A terrible misconception,
that adults seem to perceive.
Is that all is well,
when you are young,
no troubles or responsibility.
From experience, I can say,
this be far from the truth.
Cause my life has been flipped,
turned upside down,
while I travail my youth.
Just as adults,
I have to contend,
with pain inside my heart.
Filled with misery and grief,
cause my world has been torn apart.
So, if you are living adulthood,
don't be fooled and believe,
that just because I'm a young lad,
my life be free from trouble and grief.
