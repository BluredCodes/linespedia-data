title: "Letter Of Questions"

And so I cry.
Each and every night,
bitter tears,
pour from my eyes.

If the God of Heaven,
could answer this prayer.
I would be so blessed to know,
that you still love me,
that you still care.

Mother, Father,
can you hear?
Do you listen at night,
while I shed these tears?

Do you see me,
while I speak with my friends?
As I tell them of your horrible, tragic end.

Do you feel it to, 
this pain deep inside?
How I loathe to live,
without you to abide.

Gram and Papa,
are doing fairly well.
But in all honesty,
my life is a living hell.

I'm not allowed to speak of you,
or utter a single word.
I suppose that is why,
I have so many questions.
Even if they are absurd.
