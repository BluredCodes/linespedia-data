I'm still standing
Standing here in the shadows

The shadows of the universe
The shadows of the world around me

But most of all the shadows of you
My future with you may not even exist
It is unforseen

I can't predict the future
But I know what i would like it to be

Give me some assistance
Give me the final say
Cause I can't stand in the shadows
ALL DAMN DAY!
