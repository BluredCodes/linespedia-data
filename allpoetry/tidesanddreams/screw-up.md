---
title: "Screw Up"

It would be nice to know where I am headed
So I don't make more mistakes
I can't be used again

I won't let myself screw up another time

So you aren't the person the person that helped me screw up
But I can't let myself fall into another trap

There is something different here
Something special about you

I wish I knew you well enough to know what those things are

I can't fall and screw up again
