my closeness to you grows greater and greater everyday
i am at a loss for words, don't know what to say

all i feel is complete comfort when you are near
i sense not a single fear

you hold me close thats the part i love the most
you make me smile, that's all i need to boast

a gentle kiss, a single touch
close, so very muchI am a little rusty, haven't wrote in a while, but i will take any comments.
