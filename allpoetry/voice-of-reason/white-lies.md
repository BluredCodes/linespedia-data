---
title: "White Lies"
writer: "voice of reason"

If truth be told
instead of lies,
would we all lead
much better lives?

Little white lies...
Necessity!
To live in peace
and harmony.

If noses grew
for each lie told,
we all would stand
out in the cold.

"Honesty hurts!"
is my belief;
better to lie
than cause you grief.

Relationships
would quickly die;
could not survive
without white lies."Everything I say is a lie, and that's the truth!"....
So, if it's true that everything I say is a lie, can you believe what I just said or not?"

It has been said that in a normal conversation, a person lies every five minutes.
