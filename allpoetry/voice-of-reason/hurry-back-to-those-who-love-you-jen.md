When you're down and out,
Know that we care.
 
Without a doubt,
In spirit, we'll be there.

We long to comfort you,
To be by your side,

To wipe away your tears,
To heal the hurt inside.

Picture this!

See all of those who love you
Surrounding your bed.

Listen!

Someone's singing a love song!
You can't get it out of your head.

Believe!

Things can only get better!
You will be strong, come what may.

Know!

When you're feeling low,
Tomorrow will be a brighter day.

Rest!

Take it easy, mend and then....
Hurry back to those who love you, Jen.
