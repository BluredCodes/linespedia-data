Jealousy
causes so much misery!

It always seems
that others
have it better
than me.

Popularity!

How sweet 
it would be
to have everyone
love ME.

I wish…
When I blow
out the candles

I wish
upon a star

But no matter
how much I wish

I'm still not
popular.

When I die,
my epitaph will be

Here lies ?
Who the hell was he?Only the lonely
know the way I feel tonight.
Only the lonely
know this feeling ain't right.
~Roy Orbison~

Don't know how to deal with the problem of jealousy.  I'm jealous when a friend likes another friend and spends time with them instead of me.  I'm jealous when someone writes a better poem than me and gets a lot of applause.  I'm jealous when I see people who look better than me.

Jealousy is a curse.  Anyone who has never felt jealous should consider themselves blessed.  People don't often admit that they are jealous.  I do.  Good trait--honesty.  Bad trait--jealousy.

I will be jealous of the person who wins this contest if it isn't me.
