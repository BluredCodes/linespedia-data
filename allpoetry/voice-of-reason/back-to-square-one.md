We were having so much fun,
Until our love came undone...

Back to Square One.

Making plans, holding hands,
Then your wants became demands...

Back to Square One.

We had everything under the sun,
Our lives had just begun…

Back to Square One.

Footloose and fancy free,
Together forever, you and me…

Back to Square One.

Destroyed your demons one by one,
Just when I thought I had won…

Back to Square One.
