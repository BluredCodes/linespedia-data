---
title: "'Tale of Seclusion'"
writer: "Frank"
slug: "tale-of-seclusion"
source: "AllPoetry"

When one is devoid of happiness and desire
When humanity remains and woeful events transpire
Before the cleansing by Saint Anthony's Fire
When life is a catastrophic vortex of distress
The world shall feel winters bitter caress
When rivers run dry , and Mountains tumble
The laws of society shall begin to crumble 
Chaos controls the hearts of the naive
Through flesh and bone shall the demon cleave
Darkness spreads , a virus most voracious
Death plagues the land in a manner quite predaceous
Sorrow's cry echo's through ones essence 
Quietus of existance , a universal evanescence
Lasting silence , a peace so profound
The hollow vastness of nothing , emotions abound
Fated to remain alone with thoughts so dreary
Haunted as well as daunted , i grow weary
A conclusion befitting a tale of torment
A life forever bound to a sorrowful lament
The tale of one's eternal seclusion
A tale with a forgone conclusion...
