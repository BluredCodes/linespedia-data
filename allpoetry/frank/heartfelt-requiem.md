---
title: "'HeartFelt Requiem'"
writer: "Frank"
slug: "heartfelt-requiem"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

With the wind , my whispers ride
Secret prevarications , held in delight
Untold tales transmitted by the morning tide
Requiem of ruefulness , my heart's plight

Phantoms wail , their wretchedness perceptible
Atrocious disinterest my only protection
Desolate soul , halfhearted and susceptible
Ethereal fiends , a common mutual affection

Dross tears shed for envinced felicity
An aeon of torment upon this phantasmagoric celestial sphere 
Yet i smile , a dull infernal eccentricity
A toilsome and arduous life , nevertheless i persevere

The odious infection of ignorance , the corruption of mortality
An inglorious life amongst the typically judgmental
The wandering abnormality , seized within reality
An ostracized vagabond most unsentimental

Disinclined to aid the alleviation of my grief
I retain an unabated perception of affliction
The future enshroud by uncertainty and disbelief
One matter remains indisputable , my constant dereliction
