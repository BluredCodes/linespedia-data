Silence
The battle was won in our favor
Yet i do not....
Nay , i CAN not , see this as a victory
The bodies of my comrades
Lay strew across the snow covered field
The surreal image of red and white snow
I am alone , no feral fiends , no noble allies

Our God weeps for the lost souls
Trapped in an eternal war
Their ethereal souls forever bound to their armor and unsheathed blades...
His tears bless the bloody battlefields with unrivaled fury
How is it i came to live?

Am i cursed O'Lord?
To remain on this Earth a wounded knave
No escape from the guilt of not saving my compatriots
I could do nothing for them....
The wind blows across my tears

I am bound to honor
I shall not leave my friends to suffer
As i unsheathe my blade , i gaze at the majestic rainbow
So beautiful , the last vision i shall see
All goes dark , I am free
My comrades , hold the battlements steady
I shall join you oh blessed friends....
