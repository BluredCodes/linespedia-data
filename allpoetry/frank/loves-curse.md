---
title: "'Love's Curse'"
writer: "Frank"
slug: "loves-curse"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I see her from my corporeal prison
My Mortal Goddess , happy and joyous
Love fills her azure eyes
Will she grace me with her presence?
Or will she vanish with the Sun's first light
Ethereal blades
Impaled in my mortal soul
As she holds another in a warm embrace
The crystalline tears i weep remain unnoticed
My heart implodes with a brilliant fury
Such agony i can not bear
I fall to my knees crying out in anger and sadness
My beautiful mistake...
The ironies of life , the perplexing results of trying
As i lay awaiting Death
Reflecting on the effort wasted
An attempt to be happy and in love
Only revealed to this wretch an unfathomable anguish
Remember me well Goddess...
The love you murdered with the travesty of happiness
Remember me well...
The memory of the fool in love
May it bring you such agony
Fare thee well wench....
