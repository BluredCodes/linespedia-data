Cruelest pleasures of modern society
To ostracize those with different opinions
Negativity towards modern conformity?
Of course , its the path I've chosen
Why conform to appeal to the weak
Their views matter not , they'll never affect a person's life
Yet others conform , they bend till they break
For what reason?

Conformity is a curse among men
Yet its a blessing as well...
When others conform...
To an abandoned idea or conception
Or to a forsaken opinion from the mind of a pariah
The dreamed conceptions become reality
When others conform out of fear , their weakness is shown

Cowardice amongst the feeble dissolves respect
No one truly understands , what they see is what they accept
Shame yourself , to please others who do not matter...
Its the path many have chosen...
A path that will lead them to inner misery
Once you abandon yourself , your notions
Thoughts and Ideals...
You're left , no more than an empty husk
Not worthy of pity...Opinions are welcome , Idiocy is not.
