"One's Pain, One's Fear"

I have to tell a story, that I hold so dear
Its all about the pain that I have endured the past few years
I'm giving the respect to those, which is long past due
Everyday I'm always thinking of you
But the pain seen through you has increasingly grew
The pain inside seems in can never be put rest
Till I get this off my chest

Sam and Jason it all started with you
You were murdered, what ya mystery, and everyone you- knows its true
Then Jimmy came next in line
What a shame you lead to suicide, dude it just wasn't your time
We never got to be good friends, just so you know
I hold you within tho 

This small group was just the first
As I go on it gets a little worse-
Next was buddy of 5 years who's name was Adam
Killed in a crash that should have never happened
News of your death brought tears to my eyes
What was harder was I never said good-bye

The one that hurt the most, is the time I lost a good coach
It was a horrible way to see you die, knowing you had cancer just made me cry
Only the worst hadn't come yet till you died
The worst for me, was when I laid you to rest
because I knew you were the best and nothing less

You think is done there, but there is still more yet
My good friend John had to come next
Fast asleep, in your sis's car
She got into wreck and you now know the rest
because you are in heaven among the best

Finally, I just recently got shocking news
My friend D's mom wasn't doing to good
Cancer was the sign and that's never good
Hopefully she soon she'll be doing fine 
But in the meantime she'll be on my mind
In my prayers and in my heart

Its funny how life can be, its like a game of live and learn
You'll never know when the dice are rolled and its your turn
But in the meantime I'll just think of you
Your all in a place that know you belong to
With friends and family and one day I'll be there too
"D Don't worry things will be fine"
To those that I lost, Until the day we meet again
I'll hold you all in my heart till the very end!!
