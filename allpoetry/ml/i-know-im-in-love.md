title: "I know I'm in Love"

What do you consider love?
Is it the feeling in your heart?
Or the knot in your stomach
Can it just be the thoughts of your mind?

Well how can you tell you’re in love?
Might it be the look when your eyes meet
When your heart skips a beat
Or maybe just maybe the very thought you just know

Whatever it is, when I see you 
When I feel you
I never want to let you go
Keep you in my arms
So you’re always at my reach
Through all this
I know I'm in love
