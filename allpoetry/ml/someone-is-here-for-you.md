---
title: "Someone is Here for You"
writer: "M.l."

Pain hits you hard when you hear the news
Before they consume you
God is on your side and won't let you down
Just take a deep breath
Don't give in, but stand up
Close your eyes
Begin to pray
Your words will be heard, so don't fret
He won't let you down
But you have to take the time and 
Say you want him by your side

Then Look to your side and find your friends
Your true friends will be right there
They will be praying with you
Those are the friends you want for life

With all those people on your side 
The pain will lift and will be no more
Thats how you take the news
Never alone
There is always someone there for you
