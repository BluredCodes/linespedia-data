---
title: "Live a Day at a Time"
writer: "M.l."

Close your eyes
Say good-night 
to another tiring day
Get your rest 
so you can begin another day
Life is short
so live up to its full potential
For when you die
you can look back
and say I gave everything a try
Regrets are painful
So never keep them by your side
So when you look back you
You know you lived one day at a time
