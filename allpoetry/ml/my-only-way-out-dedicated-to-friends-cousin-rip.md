title: "My only way out (dedicated to friends cousin R.I.P.)"

You want respect,whats that
after what you did to me fuck that
everything I say you turn it back on me
I am living in a hell, which create for me
which I stuck with for eternity

All I have is fear of losing you 
but the stress you put me through
makes me want to get rid of you
only I can't turn away because life would turn grey

Chours
When we took are vows 
 you told me, you'll never leave my side
as I am about to die, 
I lose sight of you in the hazie sky
love you, but you turn your back to me
now I need see the light of my only delight

Tears of pain stream down my face
as I drink again for another lonely night
Drunk as I can be, I fall to my knees
Praying and hoping I can see another day
my mind is all mixed up and I cannot think clear
I am losing touch of everything I hold dear

The only way to set myself free
is to use the gun right next to me
holding your picture in my hand, I begin to cry
because I know I am about to die
without you here to tell me no
with one slight squeeze I let myself go
