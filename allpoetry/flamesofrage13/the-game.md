A gameis what we play.Going round andround.Move after move.Watching,waiting,to pouncewhen the otheris weak.Gaining strength.Trying to win.Win what though?AAcceptancepopularity?It's RIDICOULUS.In the end,it doesn't matter.When were old,no one will care,or even remember,if who we dated,or if we were crownedprom king, or queen.Or even if we wore headgear,and snorted when we laughed.But until we stop jugding,the game will last.So I say,we play the never ended game.I was having trouble ending it.  Did I drag it on to long?
