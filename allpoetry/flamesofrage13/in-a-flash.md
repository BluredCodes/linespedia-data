Hate 
I try and relate,
to the trends
that pass by in a flash.
Without warning 
I am left,
in a cloud of dust,
grasping
for a foot hold.
Searching for a place
to make my impression,
on the world.
Wondering what
will be enough
to make them,
remember me.
