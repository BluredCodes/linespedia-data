I can't stand it.
I want to scream.
Silence just isn't my game.
I want to tell you,
what I think and feel.
But when I try,
You push me aside.
"Not now,I'm busy."
Comes your reply.
Lets talk about polotics,
or the sky.
Lets talk about food,
for all I care.
Let's just have a conversation,
you and I.
Before times slips by.
