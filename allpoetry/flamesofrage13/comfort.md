The blood comes gushing out.A smile graces my face as i watch.The sight is both pleasant yet distasteful.The slits down my arms,are exits for my pain.All the stress and anger,I hole up inside,comes rushing out, with each drop of blood.I wash it away with my tears,of misery and shame.Tomorrow will be the same.As will everyday after.Until the day I cut to deep,and I'm found in a pool of blood,and my pain is gone forever.
