When you said 'I love you'
I felt like somebody.

When you called me beautiful and touched my body,
I felt wanted.

When you told me you'd always be there,
I believed you.

Until one day when I reached for your hand,
and it was gone.
I tripped.

And tumbled down the stairs of our love,
each one hitting me harder than the one before.

Each stair brought back a memory of you.
They made me see the real you,
that had been there all along,
I'd just been to blind to see.

Now I sit at the bottom,
staring at my broken heart,
lying on the floor.
