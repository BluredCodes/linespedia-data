---
title: "~THE POWER OF MUSIC~"
writer: "ray of light"
slug: "the-power-of-music"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/968--THE-POWER-OF-MUSIC--by-ray-of-light"
---

The rhythm of music cures my pain.
It reaches for my heart and removes the stain.
The verses push the thoughts outta my mind.
And invade the area; settle there for a while.

The tone of the music makes me melt,
Into a strange feelings which can only be felt,
By me.
It runs through my veins, and my sorrows
Melt with it.

I carry a whole lotta worries with me,
They are trapped, i cannot set them free.
But when listening to the verses,
They walk right through me and are lost.

The beats penetrate down into my soul,
Relieving the stress burried in it.
The chorus that sings along,
Makes my soul dance with it.

**Listen to music with your mind, heart and soul,
And then see what miracles it can do!**
