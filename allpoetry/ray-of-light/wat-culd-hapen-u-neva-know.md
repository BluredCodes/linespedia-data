What could happen, You never know.
In summer, it could snow.
The sky could turn green, and grass blue,
You never know what could come true.

What would happen you never know,
In winters, hot winds could blow.
Eggs made in factories and chicken grown on trees,
You never know, when the world could come to a freeze.

What would happen, you never know,
In Autumn flowers could grow.
The aliens would visit us, and we would visit mars.
You never know, this couls happen as time is not far.

What would happen you never know,
In spring, leaves may not show.
Robots becoming lors and man becoming slave.
You never know when the world would turn into a grave.
