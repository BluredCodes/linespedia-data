What is life?
I do not know.
Is it a long narrow road?
Or a stoned path to follow?

Is it already there?
Or do we create it?
Is it hard or easy,
To make a way through it?

Is life a pleasant journey?
Or is life a roller-coaster ride?
Is life smooth or bumpy?
Is it something we should put aside?

Is life a thrilling mystery,
Which we will have to solve?
Or is life a novel, after each chapter,
New meanings evolve.

Some say life is a stage,
Where everyone play their roles.
Is life a staircase,
Where on top lie the goals?

Life is a game, is that true?
Some win, some loose,
Some don't know what to do.

Is it that confusing,
To figure what life is all about?
It has been fifteen years,
I still don't have a clue.
