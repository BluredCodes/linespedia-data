Wrapped in silence,
I couldn't say a word.
Staring into your eyes,
I saw the depth.
When i had you in my arms that day.

I have always known,
That you are so precious.
And i was holding,
The most precious thing,
When i had you in my arms that day.

To hear your spoken words,
And to see your charming smile.
My heart brimming with happiness,
That very clear night.
When i had you in my arms that day.

A little dream I had,
To spend some time,
With only you and me.
Everything i dreamt came true,
When I had you in my arms that day.

Thinking later of me and you,
I might have cried a tear or two.
With sleepless nights, with dreamy days
Never will i forget the moment,
When i had you in my arms that day.
