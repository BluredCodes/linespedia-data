Dear God,
People seek you for help,
But i seek you to thank you.

You made flowers bloom in my life,
But thorns were also found.
I am thankful for that to you,
As that has brought me so much close to you.

You shone the sunshine my way,
But also the dark clouds drifted in.
I am thankful for that to you
As that has decreased the distance,
Which existed between us two.

You added sweet moments in my life,
But i tasted some bitter too.
I am thankful for that to you,
As that has made me believe in you more.

You made my lifeso lively and fresh,
But rotten parts existed as well.
I am thankful for that to you.
As that has made me trust you.

PS: keep giving such things to me, As i never want to go far from you.

Your's lovingly
