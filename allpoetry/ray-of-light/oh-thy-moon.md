---
title: "Oh Thy Moon~"
writer: "ray of light"

Oh thy moon,
Who stands above the Earth.
Glistening in the dark omen,
Thou are my only light,
Thou are my only light.

Thou can see me pray,
In insanity each day.
For once, shine on thy life
Make thy worries disappear,
Oh make thy worries vanish.

The world sunken in a potion,
Thy call the potion of insanity.
There art no one i shall trust,
But only you,
Only you thy moon.

Each day, i shall look up at the sky,
I swear by thou, oh thy moon.
I shall give thou my soft hands,
And thou can dance upon them,
Dance upon them thy moon.

Oh thy moon,
My lovely moon.
Glistening in the dark omen,
Thou are my only light.
So shine on me.
