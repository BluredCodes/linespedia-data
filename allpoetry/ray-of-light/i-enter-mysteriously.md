---
title: "I Enter Mysteriously.."
writer: "ray of light"

I enter mysteriously,
Surrounded by silence,
Yet you can hear,
The beat and rhythm I create.

I enter mysteriously,
Surrounded by darkness.
Yet you can see me,
Hiding under shadows, I make.

I enter mysteriously,
Surrounded by lonliness.
Yet you can feel,
The precense I create.

I leave mysteriously,
Surrounded by emptiness.
Yet you see,
me depart without a single trace.
