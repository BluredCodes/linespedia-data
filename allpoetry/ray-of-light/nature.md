It's stupid of us humans to rob ourselves from our own tomorow...anyways here is my poem describing the beauty that nature posses...just feel it.

The heavenly clouds give way to the morning sun.
The sunlight kisses the morning dew on the flowers.
Each petal unfolds, and reveals it’s hidden beauty.
The insects buzz around, flying in the air of freedom.

The light summer breeze blows, trumpeting a tune,
The leaves change color and dance with the wind.
The birds appear to fly through the silky spring,
Playing catch on the distant seven colors.

The richness of the soil, the aroma of it’s freshness.
The river turns and twirls on it’s journey through the soil
The old man at the river bed, bends and cups his hands,
And drinks the water of nature.
