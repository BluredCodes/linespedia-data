Yesterday...
        My life was like rippling water,
        Everything was so unclear.
        Like the dry autumn leaves,
        The wind had blown me here and there.

Today...
        My life is like a mysterious puzzle
        Some peices scattered here and there.
        With hope as my only companion, I move on,
        Gathering them one by one.

Tomorow...
        My life shall be like the rising sun,
        Illuminating the path of a new start.
        It will be a whole new world,  
        With a new difinition, with a new meaning.
