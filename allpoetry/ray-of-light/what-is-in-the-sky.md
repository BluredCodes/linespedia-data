title: "What is in the Sky?"
writer: "ray of light"

Why is it when i look up at the sky,
A smile blossoms on my face?
What is up there, so special...so dear?

Why is it when I do look up,
My intense worries vanish..they disappear?
What is that feeling, which makes me
Kiss the sun and hold the clouds so near?

Why is it when I look up,
I see the brightest star twinkle?
What does it signal, what truth does it bare?

Well folks i have the answers,
To my own questions.
My future lies in the sky,
It all is written up there.
