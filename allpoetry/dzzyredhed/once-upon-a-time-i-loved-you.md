---
title: "Once Upon a Time I Loved You"
writer: "Dzzyredhed"
slug: "once-upon-a-time-i-loved-you"

Once upon a time I loved you,
in the misty fogs of morn,
way before the dawn of history
and before the earth was born.
Somewhere else we were together,
as old as time must be our love.
There is no other explanation,
it drifted down from heaven above.

Once upon a time I held you
in my arms and in my heart,
locked within my soul forever
and although we had to part,
we knew we'd find each other
in another time, another space,
for we two are locked together
with a love time cannot erase.

Once upon a time I lost you,
wandered through time without a will.
A love like ours does not diminish,
I loved you then, I love you still.
Once again we've come together,
found each other at long last,
knowing our days of separation
are completely in the past.

Once upon a time I loved you
with a brilliance rivaling the sun.
That love for you still is shining
our two hearts still beat as one.
Forever we will love each other
for it is our destiny.
Always we will be together,
walking through eternity.

by Alexandra Webb
