Weep not for me
Waste not those precious drops of pain
For I am fading
Fading like sunshine under a heavy rain

The road is short
With many many miles streched out behind
Time grows quick
Memories flood the rivers of my mind

I fear not Death
I have known his face for many years
I return to the Earth
Waste not on me your troublesome tearsNot to fear Death is to live from under his shadow
