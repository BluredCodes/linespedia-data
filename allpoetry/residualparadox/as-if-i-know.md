What is it with existence?
The high pinnacle of a mountain, then
Deep aquatic murkiness, obscurity
Then again, the victory of overcoming
Where does it start? Who knows the end?
I know it’s in my pocket,
I just don’t care
I am lifeless
I am dead
The sweet smell has departed.
