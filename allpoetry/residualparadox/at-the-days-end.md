I awoke yet the sun didn't rise.
My astigmatism keeps me blind while all others see.
It was a trap
they knew and escaped.
But...
the room closed in and crushed me.
Due to my astigmatism, I did not see.
The sun did rise,
but not for me.Please include any criticism in your comments. I would appreciate it and find it very helpful.
