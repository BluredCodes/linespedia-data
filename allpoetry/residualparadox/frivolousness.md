The scorn of all men,
looked down upon by those who are esteemed.
Worn away like the fist imprinted, dusty old 
sandbag beside the place of barbaric brawls
chipped away like the paint on moldy tenement walls
This is humanity!
Everything luminous is a mirage
The false, yet alleged, but still false
Knowing our destination, we wander still
Having all of our provisions laid before us
We wander empty handed
Boundless is the trouble of our misdirected fury.
