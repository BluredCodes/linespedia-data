Br(o)od

Sometimes I feel lost in this big world,
roving forlornly through a sea of darkness
Sometimes I feel blinded in the fog of depression,
the thickness of it even suffocates me
Sometimes it’s as if the weight of the whole world 
has fallen upon my shoulders 
as it crushes me beneath it

The people are all around,
however
It is loneliness that consumes me like a fire.
Even my own family are as strangers.

Misery has lain her eyes upon me and has never looked away since
I look for ways to sooth the gray,
only to have it become thicker,
endless.

The joy of it all, the fervor within,
SNATCHED AWAY!!!!
HELP ME GOD!
I am numb…Please criticize and comment.
