Hope burns bright,
Beyond the burning windows,
Of rage lit in our eyes.

There is no destruction
Not of the self,
And as the rubble falls,
So too fall those,
Intent on destroying,
The fabric of success.

For anarchy rages in the hearts
Of those who seek to further life
Nurturing one view
And destroying all others.
Life reacts against life
Which cannot see the one
Must always be part of the all.
