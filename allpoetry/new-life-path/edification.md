title: "Edification"
writer: "New life path"

The truth we seek but rarely find
For the fabric of life within us it binds
Down the long winding road
That we travel in time,
We're so caught up, Involved,
That veracity's lost.

What's the truth behind life,
And its tender embrace
That beacons us follow
With the seed of our race?

How can reason transcend,
All the pain that life blends,
Into each textured soul,
Whose deep grave seems it's goal?

But this quest is well hid,
At the meal which we're bid,
To drink deep of life draft;
To kill fatted calf.

For we're born with greed,
Thus compelled to feed;
Does our futile reflection,
Only aid our digestion?
