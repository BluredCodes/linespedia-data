---
title: "no longer distraught."
writer: "again i go unnoticed"
slug: "no-longer-distraught"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/18274-no-longer-distraught.-by-again-i-go-unnoticed"

the painful words have been said,
the aching now long gone.
no more do i regret my statements,
and my decision to let you go.

for awhile i was distraught
wondering if i had done wrong.
but now i see the picture clearly,
and know i've done right.

you can bring me down with your words
and try to break my strength.
but nothing you can say 
will ever knock me down.

you may be hurt,
but such is life.
nobody's perfect, 
life isnt's fair.

don't dwell on the past.
move on to the future.
what's said is said,
and won't be taken back.

all i can say is
are you happy with the results?
you brought this upon yourself.
i hope that your happy
you really deserve it
this will be best for us both in the end.
