title: "i, you, and us"
writer: "again i go unnoticed"

measuring my seconds, minutes, hours
until i can see you again.
the you which once i loved.
the you which now is no longer.

i miss you. 
you've been missing for days.
your smile has been misplaced.
not a gift to me anymore.

you have stolen my heart. 
and run away with the goods.
i pray you will return.
and give it back to me again.

the air is silent.
my mind is stopped.
just waiting for the moment,
that you will speak to me.

i wish i hadn't told you,
how i loved you so.
it just complicated us.
the us that i miss the most.blah. this is just about my whole situation with my boi james and how after i told him that i liked him, he hasnt replied or spoken to me since.
