a winter wonderland in the palm of your hand.give it a gentle earthquake, and see white dots begin to float to the bottom. a scene of happy people, iceskating, singing, dancing and more,these people in the globes are ideal in every way.under a clear shelter,dancing in plastic shake up snow,no worries and no problems,just happiness abroad.just watch the pleasant smilesof the figures left inside,and feel an instant kindnesswash over your mind. if only they weren't fakeand were part of reality,we could all live in a sheltered worldmade of shake up snow.hehehe. my friend jen told me to write a poem about snow globes so i did.this is by far NOT my best work at ALL. lol. so please, go easy on the comment with me. :)
