the calm before the storm is soothing to the mind and at the same time does a number on the nerves
what’s in store?
will dying life be replenished or thrashed?
questions race through my head but sadly not all are answered
the rain begins
there are those who seek shelter and those who welcome it
what shall i do?
 . . . 
there you have it
ironically while in the midst of my thoughts the rain has gone
nothing is left except the sweet aroma of the moisture that has been left for us to saver
maybe next time when it rains i will make a decision
because it will eventually rain again.dont let oppurtunity pass you buy.
