as vast as space
open meadows
deep blue oceans
scorching deserts
clouded skies
a dreamers dream
limitless
concoctions of imagery
thoughts painting scenery
calmly creating worlds
distant bright stars
draping reality with the blindness of fiction
invasions of night
piercing through sun lit canopies
looping over and over
played through fantasies
falling through delight
wakening to desolation
to the already established world
‘till the sun convenes with the horizon
and the shadows known as night return
goodnight sweet dreams
goodnight
