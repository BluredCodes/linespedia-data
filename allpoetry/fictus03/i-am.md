---
title: "i am"
writer: "fictus03"
slug: "i-am"
source: "AllPoetry"

I am the never ending emotion of deceit
The floor beneath your feet
I am the lone tree in an open meadow
The knowledge that compels you to say “yeah I know”
I am the calm before the storm
A sculpture without form
I am the day and the night
The dark and the light
I am the frustration of elation
The blatancy of evasion
I am the effect of vision
The choices and the decision
I am a book without pages
An idea that’s outrageous
I am the very reason
The change of season
I am an artist
He who tries his hardest
I am lyrics without a voice
The sound of rejoice
I am an empty canvas
The verdict dubbed unjust
I am the very question
The lost with no direction
I am the pen that won’t write
The undercover insight
I am the flash behind the photography
The map of geography
I am the breeze on a hot day
The one who came to say
I am the soil under the plant
The reason for words “I can’t”
I am a prisoner of war
The remembrance of that which you swore
I am a revolutionary
The burden that you carry
I am the silent
The main event
I am an every day man
The limits of the horizons span
I am a mere thought
The one they forgot
I am . . .
