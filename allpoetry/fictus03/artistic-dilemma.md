its been a while since ive written anything beautiful
since my pages have danced with
happiness
joy
and smiles
the suns light doesnt reach out to touch my pages anymore
words like
gracefully
lovely
beauty
are strangers to my pages
its been a while since ive written anything beautiful
since my pages drew laughter
since people use to get the impression that i was content
the impression that i lived
words like
delight
bliss
elation
dont  flow from my pen anymore
its been a while since ive written anything beautiful
my pages arent  what they use to be
theyre filled with words like
sorrow
depression
uneasiness
the clouds have faded the sunlight
the sunlight that use to reach my pages
the pages that use to contain contentment
its been a while since ive written anything beautiful
discomfort
grief
mourning
all flow from my pen
with dark written ink that has turned my pages melancholy
still works of mine
still read
still poetry
but not enough
draw a smile
a laugh
anything but sympathy and a tear
its been a while since ive written anything beautiful
its been a while since ive written anything beautiful
