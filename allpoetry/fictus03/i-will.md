---
title: "i will"
writer: "fictus03"
slug: "i-will"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/52261-i-will-by-fictus03"
---

i will advertise mankind
with a pen and paper
i will expose conscience
with a pen and paper
i will craft visions
with a pen and paper
i will devastate mainstream
with a pen and paper
i will question motives
with a pen and paper
i will awaken the imagination
with a pen and paper
i will undermine popular belief
with a pen and paper
i will redefine metaphors
with a pen and paper
i will impact society
with a pen and paper
i will reminisce of history’s glory
with a pen and paper
i will bring skepticism
with a pen and paper
i will make the world love me
with a pen and paper
i will make the world hate me
with a pen and paper
i will do all this
with a mere pen and paper
