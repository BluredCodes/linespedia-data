I hide all the pain I ever knew
We can never part
For if we do – my wisdom lost
And if we don not – we become one
Thoughts, feelings, and love in unison
I will not let go of my anger
There lies my strength
There weakens my character
I gets the best of me
And tears go unseen
Hidden for what
PRIDE
I have so much to cry for
And no one knows but me
The fine line is blurred
All I care to know
On which side do I standone of the worst things to do is to keep your anger bottled up inside.
