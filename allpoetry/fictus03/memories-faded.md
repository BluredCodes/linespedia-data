title: "memories faded"

the laughs we use to share
the environment that use to be so familiar
one fall parted an entire family
the bickering and petty arguments
possessions so precious faded by greed
rather than embracing the memory
the will was read
and the schism of my life followed
a part of me is now missing
but i find it in the memories
the den where we once were
a room filled with conversation
now filled with the silence of memories
nothing more
i try to keep them with me
but everyday they fade further
and i fear they will soon be lost
