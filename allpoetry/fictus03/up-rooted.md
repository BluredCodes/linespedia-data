title: "up-rooted"

When the ivy meets the water and greets the given life it lays in memory
When the drop hits the floor and it sees no more we embrace our fate
Everything in between is but a moment and oh such a sweet moment
The growth we create breath by gentle breath shows no fear after the waiting is over
The condensation of morning dew
The sun will rise
The blades of grass become warmed
And the dew becomes no more
Welcome neighbors of mine
This day was made
Made for you and I
Bask in the ocean of warmth
And enjoy the cool breeze
When he stops by
Days come and go again and again but where will you be from day to day
We rise for the day and fall for the night
‘till the dew returns to comfort us as we wait for the sun to arise
Then the ivy begins to rot and the air turns stale
The drop hits
The river runs dry
The leaves turn and fall
The grass misses the dew
The soil abandoned
The night remains
