---
title: "my one faithful"
writer: "fictus03"
slug: "my-one-faithful"
source: "AllPoetry"

they say they love me
they say they care
but i know better
when it comes down to it
only one has remained in twine with me
my depression
faithful and loving
and love returned
stopping by every now and again
just to let me know she still cares
where i was once lost
she showed me the way
and what more can i do
be grateful
be thankful
what can i say
no she's not welcome
but when i turn
there she is
she's here to stay
she will always
be here
for me.
