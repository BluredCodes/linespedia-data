The same moon that shines through my window once shone through yours
I miss seeing you but I don't miss your presence
You are still here with me 
And the last time we saw each other you asked me not to shed tears on your behalf
So I didn’t
Sadly I do not the extent of your legacy
But our conversation is not yet over
There is more to discuss
Whether it arises on the front porch or in the kitchen
The moon witnesses so much and says nothing
It sits in the vast space maintaining its comforting glow
Rest assure
