am i wrong to want
if so then death
to deny my wanting is to deny my nature
it is the the set tone for the herd
i know my mistress
but her name i don not know
and i wish to share a word
but i can not share that which i do not have
a far off dream
too far from reality
no harm ever came from dreaming
but infatuation is another story
and one to be told
after all what else is left
