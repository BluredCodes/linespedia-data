---
title: "the end of the tunnel"
writer: "fictus03"
slug: "the-end-of-the-tunnel"

they say that there is a light at the end of the tunnel
a light at the end of the tunnel
at the end of the tunnel
the light is always at the end
it would serve me better now
to have a mere glimpse of light
then to wait till the end of the tunnel
why
why wait in anticipation
till the end of the tunnel
to drive away the shadows which hang over our heads as the very sky we live under
rather now than later have some light
given i live now
living dodging shadows
escaping the night
but running tires
and tired i am
where is my light
at the end of the tunnel
far from now
distant from present
not knowing when
waiting impatiently
not knowing
uncertainty
at the end of the tunnel
pure light
till the end of the tunnel
shadows
night
unseen light
at the end of the tunnel
so
till the end of the tunnel
i run 
pace unbroken
lighting matches
candles
torches
to see in the dark
hoping that light will reach out from the end of the tunnel
and show me the way
to light my path
to drive the shadows away
to leave me in the light
