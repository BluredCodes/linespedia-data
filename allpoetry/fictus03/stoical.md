title: "stoical"
writer: "fictus03"

tears are shed from eyes around me,
and i do not move.
birds sing their songs around me,
and i do not move.
i view death falling around me,
and i do not move.
words are distributed around me,
and i do not move.
weather constantly changes around me,
and i do not move.
violence presents itself around me,
and i do not move.
happiness is shared around me,
and i do not move.
individual moods vary around me,
and i do not move.
everyone smiles and frowns around me,
and i do not move.
time continues to pass around me,
and i do not move.
the world is falling in around me,
and still, i do not move.
