---
title: "my vehicle"
writer: "fictus03"

it would be nice to fly away one day
to get away from it all
leave it all behind
and enjoy the winds grace
its only too bad we cant
and thats just the way things are
even still why remain grounded
although we have no wings of our own
im not just gonna sit here
i plan to fly
far away from this very spot
i will paint my wings
i will paint them from the fruits of the forbidden garden
i will sketch them from the pencil that refuses to write
i will outline them in the tears of the sky
cause I refuse to stay grounded
the sky is my limit
i plan to reach it
whose to say that without wings we can not fly
i intend to
i will write my way to the sky
these words can take me anywhere
through the depths of the ocean
across the African plains
up to the moon
beyond the jungles of south america
into the conscience of the soul
past the veins of the body
just outside the sight of the mind
and back
the written word will be my vehicle
my path to the imagination
and with these words
I can go anywheresometimes it helps to use poetry as a "vehicle" to escape for a while.
