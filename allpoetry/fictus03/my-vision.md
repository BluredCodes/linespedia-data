caught in the reality
that i know as my thoughts
lies a vision.
this vision is mine and mine alone.
it belongs to only me.
no one else can view it or understand it,
but i can.
it’s the scenery as i travel through the lacandon jungle
of mexico.
it’s the breath i take before exploring the depths
of the red sea.
it’s the wind i feel as i stand on the highest mountain peak
of the himalayas.
it’s the sound of the coqui in the rainforest 
of puerto rico.
it’s the sunshine on the great plains
of africa.
it’s the scent of a freshly cut rose
from my mother’s garden.
bottom line
it’s mine
and it’s whatever i want it to be.find your vivsion and stay true to it.
