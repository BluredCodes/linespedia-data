title: "a walk in the rain"

it seems no matter where i go
i go walking through the rain
no one to me sunshine
or even a newspaper to escape it
and there im left
another victim of the endless rain
prayers are far from salvation
due to altering stars
and independent minds
damn the ungreatful skies
whats next but go on
to the next puddle in the course of this walk
a walk in the rain
a burden to live with
a reacuring moment
be gentle great skies
i do not crave rain
but prefered over hail"life is something to keep your mind off dying"
                                       -fictus03

live your life to the fullest.
