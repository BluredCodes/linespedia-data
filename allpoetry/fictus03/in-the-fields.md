displeasing echoes
hitting one wall and continuing to the next
to much work
im tired
not enough time
another assignment
all i do is work
now sitting there left in wonder
the echoes unheard
of how working in the fields is back breaking labor
of how being free is back breaking labor
of how a better life is back breaking labor
of how supporting a family is back breaking labor
not enough echoes of thanks

i thank my ancestors
all those who came before me
all those who gave me
their sweat
their blood
i thank those who gave me
the opportunity to do this “hard work”
i thank those who stood up
those who made my future
those who gave me the right to do this “hard work”
without those who came before us
without their standing up
without their sweat
without their blood
without their back breaking labor
we would be left in the fields

thank you for the “hard work” you gave mebefore you complain about the hard work in school or long hours at work, think to yourself, it is a freedom to that hard work and be able to work those long hours.

your ancestors are to thank.
