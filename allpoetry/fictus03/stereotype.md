my whole life
its taken me my whole life
to develop the person i am
five seconds
it takes you five seconds
to think you know the person i am
with one quick look
over my apparel
my hair
my skin color
you recall the myths
about how people like me are
you remember our slang talk
our very rude manors
our laziness
our lack of education
our behavior
our unwillingness to do our best
in the back of your mind you think
and you paint your picture with the wrong colors
youre not blame
you cant help being ignorant
so let me help you
no i do not speak using slang
allow me to get this door for you
excuse me you first
my plans for today
im gonna go home and lie on my couch to watch T.V.
just kidding
im actually going to walk my dog around the block
followed by partaking in game of basketball
uneducated you say
i can teach you a thing or two
as far as behavior goes
thats my personality
deal with it
i have goals just as everyone else
and I will achieve those
next time just ask
it makes you look and sound a whole lot smarter
and remember
i am a contradiction of belief
