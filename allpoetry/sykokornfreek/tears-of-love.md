---
title: "Tears of Love"
writer: "SykoKoRnFreeK"
slug: "tears-of-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

As I sit here all alone
Pondering in my never-ending pain
A wondering vision blurs my mind
It comes unseen at first, but grows in strength with time
It’s tall and dark
Mysterious and mournful
Although the lights are very dim
I can still see the glow that lies deep within him
There is something about him
This being
Something I can’t describe
He stands motionless as I begin to cry
His long, black hair falls into his face
As he looks down at me from his towering height
Unlike anything I’ve ever known
He bends down and places his hand on the back of my head
I look up from my once covered eyes
And look at his face
He doesn’t speak
Silence fills the air
I try to speak, but my throat becomes dry
I look down, to try to break his trance
But I am forced to look back up
Slowly my eyes cross his again
And a chill shoots up my spine
A start of a smile crawls across my face
He stairs deeply into my eyes
And I can’t help but look back into his
He stands again, but this time not alone
He takes me by my hand and pulls me up
Even standing he is much taller than I am
We stand there together
No more than six inches apart
I start to cry again, not really knowing why
He starts to cry too as he wipes my tears away with his hand
Breaking the silence he whispers
“I love you”
I look down, confused at first
Now everything makes sense
I look back into his eyes and reply
“I love you too.”
