title: "Constant Contradiction"
writer: "SykoKoRnFreeK"

The more I love you
The more I despise myself
The more I need you
The more useless I feel
The more I adore you
The more I resent myself
The more I want to be with you
The more I want to loose myself
The more I begin to care for you 
The more careless I am towards myself
This makes my lone for you slowly fade
Which makes the hate for myself diminish
I slowly stop needing you
All I need is myself
I don’t adore you
I resent you
Why would I want to be with you?
I don’t want to disappear 
I don’t care about you
Maybe if you cared for me that would change
But as long as you are who you are
Then all I have is this constant contradiction
