I watch how the moon sits in the sky
As I look into your eyes
I pull you closer
Into a kiss
The void opens
Once again
And we fall
Holding on breathlessly
Stealing the moment
We don’t let go
Why would we?
To be a fool
But we are in love
So far into love
We have long passed the E in the word LOVE

We’ve been here a while
We know what we’re doing now
And we don’t plan on leaving anytime soon
We like it here in this place
Our void of nothingness
With so much emotion
Together at last
Scarred hearts being mended
Mended by our love
Our compassion
Our comfort

Slipping out of the kiss
We return to reality

Look at what we’re doing
What we’re doing is serious
No games to be played
We both begin to cry
How overcome we are by our love
Both alone in time
Alone in a moment that is stuck in a void
Of nothingness
Far from the reaches of the real world
Not going back
Some would call this a fantasy
