As your blood slowly pours from your arm
Onto my lifeless body
You begin to cry

Do you realize what you have done now?
Do you realize what your fear caused?
It caused our death

Or do you simply want to prove a point?
To get back at the world

Who was so mean?
For you to cause this?

Was it me who was always there?
Or was it him who hurt you so bad
You had to hurt others?

What is the color of your soul?
It’s not white and pure
Like I had wished

It is a black from the depths of hell
It’s so resonated
Nothing could compare

Don’t you see?
I had to get out of it all
To set my body free of your torture
I was left with no other decision

Are you aware of what you did?
You caused pain unlike any other

Look at what you are left with
Nothing
The decision of death
Because of mine
The last decision
