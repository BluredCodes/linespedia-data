Look at me 
Tell me what you see
Is it love? 
Is it hate?

Or is it pain?
Pain is what I see
Pain is what you have given to me

Pain is all I know now
Everything you say is a lie

My heart was so new
So unused
So beautiful

But then you came
And destroyed it all
Took down my barriers 
Let nothing in
And took everything out

How is one so cold?
Why does everything get destroyed?

Has it come to my final goodbye?
Is this how it ends?

I am dead now
My heart is in too much pain
I can’t stand it another second

I have no guiding
I have had to deal with everything

Nothing is left for me to gain
I will soon be going away
First I must thank you 
Thank you for destroying me

Now I drench myself in my own blood
And finish your delicate craftwork
Then I end my pain and set my heart free
Free from your prison of hateone of the poems my boyfriend, Eric wrote
