---
title: "Never Before"
writer: "SykoKoRnFreeK"
slug: "never-before"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/26512-Never-Before-by-SykoKoRnFreeK"

These feelings I've never felt before,
Are the ones you make me feel.
These dreams I've never dreamt before,
Are the ones that you make real.
This heart that has never truly loved,
Now beats just for you.
These words that once were only lies,
You have now made true.
These lips that have never been kissed like that,
Are burning from your touch.
This body that has never wanted anything,
Is yearning for you so much.
This soul that once was solely mine
Now also belongs to you.
This sky that once was a dull, dreary gray
Is now a bright, brilliant blue.
You are the one I truly want,
The half that makes me whole.
You are the one, to whom I surrender
My mind, my body, and soul.
