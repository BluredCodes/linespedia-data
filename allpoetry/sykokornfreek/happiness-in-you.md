I’m tired of all the ceaseless lies
The lies of hate and torture

So I walk up on high
And step to the ledge
Seeing through your lies
I laugh at myself
As the tears roll down
Because now it’s the world I know

You pull me back and say I love you
I know this for one is not a lie you hide
As you do all the rest of the time
You hold me close, then kiss me deeply
While I hesitantly go along with you
I’m afraid of you now
For all you’ve done in the past
But I know someday you’re going to make it all go away
And I’ll finally have some happiness in youA poem my boyfriend and I wrote in an im 8)
