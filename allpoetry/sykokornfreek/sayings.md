Waiting for the bell to ring,
Let me out of this boring jail,
I don't mind if anyone cares,
Not one soul seems to even want to bail,
What am I doing anyway?
It seems like everyone wants to do the same thing.
Just sit here with their head on their desks,
Bored out of their minds.
Teenagers these days,
Are just procrastinating and not caring,
That their actions speak louder than words,
And their words contradict their sayings,
That change their perspective on life.
