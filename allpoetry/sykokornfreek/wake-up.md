Am I nothing to you? 
Do you know the pain you produce?
Do you now the hurt you give unto others?

I don’t think you fully realize 
The importance of the situation
I think you need to wake up

You need to realize how much your words hurt
And also how much your words heal
Use this power to make me happy

It’s been like this for too long
You act like it is some kind of fucking game
Why don’t you get it?
Why don’t you just grow up?

I found in you that empty truth is full of lies
I discovered that you are just a little kid
Wake up
Your in love 

And whether you are or not 
You’re stuck with me
You can't stop it 

But you can stop all the hurt
You can stop all the pain
You can stop all the torment
You and you alone

Please stop before it’s too late
Wake up while there is still someone to wake to
Be with me
Kiss me 
Hold me
Touch me
Before I am gone

I don’t have much time before my heart says enough
I don’t know how long this can keep going on

Please help me
I fear for my life
I fear what will happen
Help meone of the poems my love wrote
