---
title: "The Second For You"
writer: "SykoKoRnFreeK"
slug: "the-second-for-you"

Once I yearned
And twice you blockaded
Now I weep
And you will always mock me
Mock my sorrow if you have to 
Laugh at my tears
Bury your guilt beneath my sadness 
Let the years
Make you forget 
You never will 
I will not let you
Laugh if you must
But never forget the trust
You molded into my soul
the pain that you left
Growing and growing still
And growing strong 
I will make you remember 
My voice forever today
Twice you blockaded
And forever you will pay
