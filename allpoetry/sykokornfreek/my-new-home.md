title: "My New Home"
writer: "SykoKoRnFreeK"

I don't know where I am
Everything went black
My eyes are peering out through this blanket of darkness
You did this to me
Tell me where I am

Three walls for me to look upon 
Or write upon
With no pen to write with
I tear at my skin
Revealing blood, I start to design
Tiny letters that form words

This cell is small
It's cold and I'm scared
My body lies numb against this scolding pit
Why did you have to sabotage my life?
Turn my smile upside down?
I loved you

It's getting harder to breathe in here
How could you be so cold?
You did this to me
You and you alone
Put me in this small compartment

My new home
Six feet below the surface
