Tears come to eyes
As I say goodbye
My final one
The one I have tried to avoid

There’s no other way
It must happen like this
You love me I know
But you have hurt me for the last time

I am sick of this all

I’m tired of singing the same song
Pain, pain go away come again some other day

Pain is life
And my life is pain
I’m done with it all

I’m done with you
I have to get away
I don’t want to leave you
But what choice do I have?

And when I’m almost gone
You enter my mind
A tear forms in my eye

I hit the ground
Before it leaves its sanctuary
As I have now done...yet another poem my boyfriend, Eric wrote :D
