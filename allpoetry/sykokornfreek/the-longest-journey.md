Our longest Journey
Into each other hearts
To each others aid
And to our love’s rescue

The longest journey of all
Is the journey of the miles that part us
But most importantly, love

We have come far along the path of love
So close to the end of the yellow brick road
Into a life of happiness

We need each other more and more as each day slowly ticks by
Time
It’s a killer of lovers

We have been bestowed with the love of all loves
We are the lucky ones
It’s us who will make it

They don’t know us
They don’t know how far we have come
To come this far
We must have been made for each other

The consequences of our past actions
Have been plowed under the earth
Ashes to ashes
Dust to dust

We are here now and happy
That’s what counts

We have conquered the journey
We did it all on our own
And we couldn’t be happierone of the poems my boyfriend, Eric wrote
