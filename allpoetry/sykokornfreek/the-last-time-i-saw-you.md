title: "The Last Time I Saw You"
writer: "SykoKoRnFreeK"

The last time I saw you
You had the same shining eyes
I love looking into them
Through our happy times and goodbyes

The last time I saw you
My love for you grew
It happens every time we hug
It wasn*t anything new

The last time I saw you
You seemed angry or sad
I couldn*t help but notice
The way you said nothing when I asked why you were mad

The last time I saw you
We got into a fight
You were over there and I was here
The feeling I had wasn*t right

The last time I saw you
We made up and cried together
That*s when I knew we were okay
And I know we will be forever

The last time I saw you
I hugged you many times
Each time is a special memory
Like a beautiful song full of rhymes

The last time I saw you
I dreaded saying goodbye
I knew it would be hard
And I knew that I would cry

And the next time I see you
It*ll all be the same
Everything will happen again
But I guess that love is the one to blame
