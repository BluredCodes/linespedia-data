title: "They Didn't Listen"
writer: "SykoKoRnFreeK"

My tears fall, 
But no one notices. 
I complain, 
No one listens. 
I'm in pain screaming inside, 
No one sees. 
I can't take anymore, 
The pain is too great. 
I take matters into my own hands, 
I grab the knife and hold on tight. 
I cut myself, 
Blood flows, 
Tears fall. 
Now I am dying on the outside, 
As well as the inside. 
I feel a great relief. 
I lie down, 
I die. 
People mourn. 
What could they have done? 
What was wrong? 
Why didn't I say anything? 
My soul looks down upon the Earth, 
At the mourning people. 
At my lost life. 
They could have done something, 
So much was wrong. 
I tried to speak, 
They did not listen.
