When you enter my room you face my bed
Turn then you see my desk instead
On my desk is my computer and things
Like pictures, papers, and a song that Koßn sings
All over my walls are pictures of Koßn
And information on them, like where they were born
Turn into the bathroom and more Koßn you see
What*s that smell?  I think it*s poparii
The sink is white with silver lining
And the floor is tiled in a blue that is shining
The shower is enclosed in purple and green
It*s the weirdest bathroom I*ve ever seen
Then back to my room on top of my TV
Is my lava lamp and pictures of Jeremy
Then is a stand with my stereo and CDs
And a picture of Jeremy and I together
On my computer you will see AOL
I*m always online when I*m home, usually have mail
Talking or not my phone is close to me
It*s never on it*s stand where it should be
With my collected shot glasses and beanies
Then you see my door which has sayings on it
One of the sayings is “go away!” so let*s try it
