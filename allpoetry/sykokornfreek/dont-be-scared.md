---
title: "Don't Be Scared"
writer: "SykoKoRnFreeK"
slug: "dont-be-scared"

What’s wrong with you?
There’s nothing to fear
Nothing to hurt
Nothing to feel
But love

I love you
I won’t hurt you
You’re the only one
No one could top what you’ve done

Is it the world that surrounds us?
Let go of the world
They can have it all to themselves
We’ve created our own
You will never need to face the world alone

You fear me betraying you?
How utterly incorrect
Wake up
I’m in love with you
Nobody else could compare to you

You still feel the pain?
Baby, that’s behind us
We made mistakes
Now look at our perfection
We’ve established a new life together
This time around will be different
I promise

Here
Take my hand
I’ll guide you through this maze
To the finish line
We’ll cross it together

Look down this windy road
It’s me at the end
I’m falling fast
So run to the bottom to catch me
I need you

You have nothing to fear
As long as I’m here
I’ll be around forever
And I’ll tell you this
Until you’re sure fear has forgotten your name
I’ll keep your mind from going insane

Please don’t grieve
About our past
We’ve both done foolish things
We’re past them now
That’s over

Look at this portrait
Two beings occupy it
You and I
No outside feelings this time
My love

Pull me into our void
You say you feel truly content here
Let’s stay here then
Until you’re sure you’re okay
I’ll gladly stay forever with you
In this void
It’s peaceful and, you’re right, content

To love is to care
To care is to want
To want is to need
And I need you here with me
So don’t give up
Don’t give in to a little scare
It’s just an emotion that brings sorrow
We don’t need interferences we’re all to ourselves
I will never hurt you, my love
You have nothing to fear
