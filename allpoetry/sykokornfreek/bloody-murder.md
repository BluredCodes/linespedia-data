---
title: "Bloody Murder"
writer: "SykoKoRnFreeK"

I lay here with my wrists all bloody  
Pondering in fear  
You committed a crime, you committed murder  
You severed my heart, you wrote on my grave  
You’re just an elusive killer  
I don’t know what’s with all of this cutting  
All it does it cause more pain
Someday maybe you’ll design a cure
Then deep within my soul you’ll embed it
My thoughts fully revolve around suicide
I feel like something is missing
You can’t just say that it’s ending
What must I do with this feeling I’m enduring?  
If you walk away in this moment I’ll eternally suffer
Faded scars have evaded my arms
It’s cold inside me, because my heart refuses to beat
It finally has came to this tragic reality
Permanent separation
I’ve lost everything, except for my tears
There is no relief, I feel too used
I’m a prisoner of your blood-ripping soul
I have nothing left, but facing this bloody murder
