The hour came and passed
He gave you up
I couldn’t be happier

He threw you so close to the pit
But I pulled you out before you fell in

You started to see what I realized months ago
It took just one look
To see you were the one for me

I saved you one night
It was so perfectly planned through our improvising
And after I did
He lost you
I saw my chance

I knew it wasn’t right
You with him
Not me
No

I wouldn’t accept it
So I tried
And finally
A year and four months later
I had my moment of victorious glory

With one kiss our world was turned upside down
We were born again
We fell in love

We both suffered losses
But we came through for each other
And here we are so perfect
So right
I know I will make you happy
Now and forever

You just didn’t notice back then
It hurt to know
Trying my best
Wasn’t enough?

But I would not give up
Where there is a will there is a way

You were my will
And I found my way
You finally got me
And I got you
And we couldn’t be happierone of the poems my boyfriend wrote
