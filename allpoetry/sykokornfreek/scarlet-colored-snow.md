Among a desolate graveyard in the snow
Alone before you kill me
Before you ruin this life
You smile
I cry
Then you say goodbye
I die
If you can’t have me, no one can
And I seep into the snow
Slowly and painfully
Leaving only my scent
It’s enough to drive you over the edge
As you take the gun unto yourself
And tightly close your eyes
You pull the trigger
And one silent, scarlet tear falls down your face
