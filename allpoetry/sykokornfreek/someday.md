---
title: "Someday"
writer: "SykoKoRnFreeK"
slug: "someday"

There is something missing inside me,
But when I'm with you it's always there.
Like I'm missing a very important factor,
When I'm not in your arms, playing with your hair.
I think that my feelings for you,
Are more than they seem.
I am longing for you always,
Even when I dream.
I hate the miles between us,
They get longer each passing day.
I hate the constant pain inside,
But I love you in every way.
Every morning when I awake,
I long to hear your soothing voice.
Just so I could reassure,
That I have made the right choice.
I hate to telephone you,
It hurts to say goodbye.
When I am away from you,
I only want to die.
To be in your arms again,
As we fold into a kiss.
And to look up and see your face,
Would fill me with bliss.
When I'm there next to you,
Playing with your long, black hair.
It gives me the most perfect feeling,
As it blows freely in the midnight air.
When you look at me,
With those beautiful, sapphire eyes,
The feeling over-powers me
And I know they tell no lies.
When I'm there close to you,
Tickling your cute little nose.
I know we are right for each other,
Standing in a perfect pose.
Whenever I make you laugh,
You have the sweetest smile.
It glows so brightly,
That I could see it from a mile.
Until the end of our lives,
I hope we stay together.
Because being with anyone else,
Could never be better.
