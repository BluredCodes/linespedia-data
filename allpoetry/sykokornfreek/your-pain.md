---
title: "Your Pain"
writer: "SykoKoRnFreeK"
slug: "your-pain"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/26811-Your-Pain-by-SykoKoRnFreeK"

I feel your presence when you enter the stage,
I understand your hypnotic cries of rage,
I see the look of pain in your eyes,
I can almost taste your strength rise,
I hear your mesmorizing thoughts,
I feel your dark barriers,
I watch the depressed look on your face,
Lots of people feel the same way,
But that won't take away my love for you which I will never sell,
But that won't take away your endless pain that you know so well.
