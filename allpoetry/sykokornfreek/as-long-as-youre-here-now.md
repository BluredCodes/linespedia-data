What did you know? 
You weren't here then 
You didn't know him
You didn't know me when I was lost 
You don't know how I felt 
You don't know what I did 
You couldn't feel my pain 
You couldn't even imagine what it was like 
I went through everything without you 
I suffered loss after loss 
I lost myself without you 

But I found it in you 
You opened your arms to me 
You showed me another way 
You told me not to give up 
You tried to help me 
But ended up loving me 
And I guess I did too 

So we started growing together 
I started forgetting about the past 
I guess it really doesn't matter 
What happened then
As long as you're here loving me now
