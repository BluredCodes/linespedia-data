Delicate snowflakes dance as they fall
Onto our hearts
As one
Feeling this way

The tombstones all individually placed among the grass
Everything topped with an ivory shield
We lie atop the frail blades of grass
There we kiss forever
Adoring the moment
Not letting go
Our hearts screaming I love you

This has become our garden
Our garden of love
Of happiness
And bliss
This garden of ours never broken
Could there be any greater feeling?
Or even a greater emotion?
If there is we will soon top it

Nobody understands us
No one we’ve approached
For a love like ours is unconquered 
Because we’ve topped all the rest

They just don’t get it
But why are we fighting them?
There is no reason
Let them have their fun
And we will have ours
Together
As one
Under the snow
That dances as it falls
Gently onto our hearts
Burying them
Together
