Let’s make a deal
We’ll end PDA
You and I together
Don’t you think it’s gay?

Stupid whores
It’s all their fault
All they do is get in the way
If they were slugs we’d get out salt

We can’t kill off sluts
But maybe a rule 
Or I could just be dreaming
But that would be cool

You have to admit, it’s got good points
We don’t see people makin out behind a trashcan
But aren’t they going to far?
Hugging and holding hands isn’t to ban

School isn’t terrible
It’s a way of seeing you
But that’s not fair
I’m teased by what we cannot do

Pushing you away
Telling you no
Holding it back
We should be able to let it all go

We aren’t going to shed our clothes
We’re asking for a simple hug
Maybe also a small peck
God, PDA is a bug!

I love you
Therefore I need you
Public Display of Affection
Yes, I want the public to know!

Let’s call and complain
To the board of directors
Let’s take the school to court
And barely make it through all the many sectors

What’s so wrong with a hug?
Or the touching of the face?
Sure, a kiss would be nice too
But just enough would be a simple embrace

T’was a good day when it was allowed
Everyone showing their emotion
Now everyone is wrapped up in themselves
Make-up, fingernails, hairspray, and lotion

PDA
Needless to say
Is very gay
I’m not trying to play
Even in May
Shouldn’t we have a say?
At the end of the day
It needs to go away
No need for it to stay
Even, some money we’d pay
Take a trip to the bay
Or eat some hay
We’ll even send it a bouquet
Okay?
Yay!
