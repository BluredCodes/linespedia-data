---
title: "Don't Let Me"
writer: "SykoKoRnFreeK"
slug: "dont-let-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/27114-Dont-Let-Me-by-SykoKoRnFreeK"
---

Let the water
Forever flow from the wounded rocks

Let the trees 
Forever grow strong in the brush

Let the graveyards
Forever collect more tombstones

Let the tears
Forever fall from my eyes, in happiness

Let the blood
Forever pump into and out of my heart, for you

Let the snow
Forever fall delicately onto our hearts

Let my heart
Forever burn for you touch

Let my soul
Forever reach out to touch yours

Let you and I
Forever be happy together, as one

But please don't let me suffer
Forever in the pits of your prison

Please don't let me crumble
Forever under all the pressure

Don't let me die
Forever in the thought of your kiss

Don't let me ponder
Forever in the thought of fear or betrayal

But most of all don't let me think
That everything can amount to forever
