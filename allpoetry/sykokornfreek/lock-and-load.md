---
title: "Lock and Load"
writer: "SykoKoRnFreeK"
slug: "lock-and-load"

Feelings of frustration
Knowing all is wrong
These wounds display the pain
Crawling into my soul

You can’t look past them
You know you can’t 
Stop trying

I’m too far gone now
You had your chance
You blew it
How could you?

Seeping into these veins
You’re a stowaway into my heart
Tearing at my tattered being
 I cry
You try
I die inside

I’m forced to look upon my reflection
Disgust
Disease
Demise
Dismal
I hate myself because of you
You destroyed me
Nothing can lift me now
Not even love

So with this
I ascend
Forever to the depths of never

Let’s lock and load
I’m leaving here
