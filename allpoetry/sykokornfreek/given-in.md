I’m sorry for all the tears
I’m sorry for all the insanity
I’m sorry for taking advantage
I didn’t mean to

I won’t do this again
I have finally given in
You won’t feel anymore pain from me
Not again

I didn’t mean to hurt you, my love
And if you die, I will revive you
And go to hell, I will save you

You have nothing wrong
Nothing at all
I seek no revenge
The pain stops here
I love you

I have been hurt time and time again
It's all I know
But now it’s plain as day
I love you, I have given in

I will never leave you
Nothing can keep me from you
Not now
Not now that I know how much I love you

There will be no more pain
No more tears
No more questions wondering why?

You say someday I'll be hurt
Well I'll be with you
So it will be you who hurts me

I have given in
Now you get what you asked for
I love you
I cherish you
I NEED YOU

Your my everything
Your the answer to my fears
I cannot live without you
Let me kiss you deeply and hold you near

I now understand what you have been trying to tell me
I’m in love with you
I have given in
This is new to me, But I’m not afraid

I love you
I’m not afraid, not even a little
You will not betray me, And I will not betray you
I will just be here loving you, for eternitymy boyfriend, Eric's poem answering to my poem, Not A Lot
