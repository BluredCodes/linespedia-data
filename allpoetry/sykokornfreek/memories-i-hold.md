So quiet
I hear the rustling of the leaves
As my mind soars
Through passionate memories
I see your face
Inside my mind
To nothing I can compare
When you smile lovingly at me
While you run your finger through my hair
When you look into my eyes
I feel a sudden sense of bliss
When you wrap me in your arms
And pull me into kiss
Your soft hands
Against my skin
Caress my face
And soul within
To my heart
You hold the key
To set my soul
And body free
But now you're gone
You may never return
Without you
It feels like my body burns
My love for you is strong
Forever I shall hold
Without you here
I*m left in the cold
I stumble through darkness
Alone and afraid
For t*was so long
Our farewells we did bade
I feel I must end my life
With the swiftness of a blade
As the image of your sweet face
Slowly begins to fade
As you begin to fade
Our future lies untold
For although we are seperated
Our memories I still hold.
