Like the wings of a bird unfolding
My heart opens
With my eyes, I lay beside you
My love

The day's first light finds its way
Into our bed and touches your face
Pain seems to fade away
My love

Then you smile and touch my face
My soul races to another place
My whole being is in a daze
My love

My love's wings lift me high
My spirit soars, I begin to cry
For you, Shanna
