I had seen him before,
But we didn't speak.
He was so cute,
It made my knees weak.

The day he came up to me,
I didn't know what to do.
He looked at me and said hello,
And then I realized it was true.

He was tall and dark,
With long, black hair.
He was thin, but not skinny,
With eyes so fair.

He told me his feelings for me,
I felt the same way.
He told me he liked me,
And it swept me away.

Why he was doing this,
It did not show.
But if he would have stopped,
I still would never know.

And from now until forever I know,
That there will always be.
A special place in my heart,
For my beloved, Jeremy.
