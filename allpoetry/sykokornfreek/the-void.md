Frozen moments in time
Stopped hour glass
Alone with you
In a black void

A black, empty void
Filled with all the love I can give

And as we slowly go into a kiss
The same fear builds up in me
Will this be our last moments in this empty void?

I don’t want to leave
I shouldn’t have to
I love you

I know we will eventually go back to it
But it’s when we are out that I am dead
I feel so alive in the void with you
Outside I’m lost to being

They shouldn’t make us leave
We can always live in the majesty of nothing
It’s like I have been touched with love by my girl

Let the bastards rot in time for making us leave

But here we are again
In the void
But when we were done kissing
I opened my eyes
And we were still thereone of the poems my boyfriend, Eric wrote
