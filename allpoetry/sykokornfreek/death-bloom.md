I’m sick and tired of embracing reflection of past thought
I should be with you 
I should be holding you

But instead I’m lead into a life of misery and hate
A life without you 
I don’t know why you hurt me
I don’t know how you could be so cold

I was left with one choice 
To embrace what I had
To satisfy my own being

Fuck the details
Fuck the pain
Fuck everything

It’s dark for the fear of failure
And gloom as wide as an eye and femunting
The hate is roiling
I have a death grip on my veins
Flowering forth foul nectar
Space between a blink and a tear

Now as a last request 
I request you stay here as I take a vacation
From which I won’t return

Stay here live out your life 
Hurting every pathetic fool you can 
As you have done me

Now I pack my bags and leave you 
Thank you for what you caused
