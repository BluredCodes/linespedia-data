---
title: "Different Worlds"
writer: "SykoKoRnFreeK"

The muffled screams of silent voices,
The bad happening from all good choices.
A day and age where people get off on sadistic fun,
When fathers don't care about their only sons.
The world today is like throwing flesh to the wolves,
It's everybody for themselves, anybody who thinks different is a fool.
But come into my world and you'll find out what twisted is all about,
Where the fabled illusion of promise is all in doubt.
Where the demons are imprisoned and where angels die,
Where the doves don't fly with the crow's constant cry.
Where the sorrow and sadness out scares the fear,
When all the pain in your heart sheds black tears.
My life is scary, in which you will never understand,
I am forced to sit back and watch everything I've created crumble into the sand.
