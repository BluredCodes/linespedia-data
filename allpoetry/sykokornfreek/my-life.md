You showed me nothing but love
In return I gave you nothing

I should have cared
I should have been sincere

I could have fallen in love 
But instead I rejected the idea

I could of had you 
But instead I threw you away

And now you are gone
And for good this time
I don’t blame you

But now I realize what I had
A little too late

I feel so alone 
The way I felt before
I no longer want to feel this way

Forgive me 
I knew not what I was doing
I need you

I need you now
I love you now
I care for you now

I’m sick of being alone
I just want it all to go away
Just want to run away to die

Take it... 
Myself...
My life...one of the poems my boyfriend, Eric wrote
