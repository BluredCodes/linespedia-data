---
title: "Nothing Can Compare"
writer: "SykoKoRnFreeK"
slug: "nothing-can-compare"

We share a kiss for a few moments
Then I run my fingers gently down your face
As we stair into each other's eyes
I feel your unbreakable embrace
I love every second of being with you
Every time we part, my eye loses that special tear
It's the tear, not just from my heart, but also from my soul
When we are together my heart races
It's the way you say; "I love you" and the way you make me feel
While we set together in almost darkness
I feel the steady beat of your heart
As I lay my head against your chest
I know our love will never break apart
I know my emotions for you could never be greater
As I notice the loving look in your eyes
I run my fingers through your long, black hair
And look deep into your eyes, to get trapped in your memorizing barriers
I've finally found the right guy for me, and nothing can compare.
