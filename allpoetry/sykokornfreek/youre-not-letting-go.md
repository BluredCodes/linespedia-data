You’ll never let go
Never, no never
I won’t let you
This time

Never again
Will I let you push me away
The way you did last time

Never again
Will it happen the same way
It can’t happen
I won’t let it

One of these days I’ll show you I’m right
By not letting you let go
Let go of it all

So what, you’re getting fed up?
I’ll show you today
We will get through this
Together
Somehow and some way
I’ll prove it to you

I won’t let you push me away
When the sad and sorry day occurs
You will be shown
Shown that I love you
Shown that I care
From the core of my soul
And the depths of my heart
I love you
And I’m not letting you let goThis is a poem my boyfriend and I wrote together in an im :D
