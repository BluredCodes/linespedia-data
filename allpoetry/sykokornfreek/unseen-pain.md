---
title: "Unseen Pain"
writer: "SykoKoRnFreeK"
slug: "unseen-pain"
source: "AllPoetry"

It's not fair, what they think of you 
It just doesn't make any sense 
What did you do to deserve this? 
Why can't they change their minds? 

It all seemed so simple 
Everything worked out alright 
But they hold it against you 
And punish you even more 

You didn't do it on purpose 
"It was an accident," you said 
But they will never believe you 
Even though you never lied 

You sit at night and cry 
You wish they could see the hurt 
But they don't even know you  
You're just a crazy teenage boy 

So you call me up at night 
And you tell me that you're sorry 
I listen closely to every word spoken 
And talk you through the pain 

You see, I could never hurt you 
I can't stand to see you this way 
So I'll promise you this right now 
And I'll never go back on these words 

You are the only one here for me 
I love you with all my heart 
So I'll stay here right beside you 
I'll help you be strong again 

It will all work out some day 
We'll be on our own together 
We won't worry about the little things 
And we'll be happy together forever
