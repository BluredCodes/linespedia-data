As all await the midnight hour
I get anxious for your call
Your voice is far and distant
But can be heard as always

I don’t know what you are doing 
Something proven to be perfect
Glowing deep
Within your illuminicent being

I need your voice 
It’s all I can have
I wish for more but what can I do?

When you call 
I know it will be soon
Only an hour more and I will have you 

You speak of unspoken plans
I hope it’s what I’m thinking 

At the 12 o’clock hour
I will receive a call from the one I hold dear
The time is coming close 

I hope I do not hear of any feelings besides mine
I know I will not
For what do I have to fear?
Nothing

It’s been 3 minutes 
The time is drawing near
I just hope we talk 
I hope it will be long

When you have to leave me I will cry
When you call me again I will stop
But in between I will be thinking about you
