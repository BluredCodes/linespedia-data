---
title: "I Love You"
writer: "SykoKoRnFreeK"
slug: "i-love-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/26504-I-Love-You-by-SykoKoRnFreeK"

I love you with all of my heart,
And pray we will never be apart.
I think of you all day and night,
Because I hate when you’re out of sight.
I want to be with you,
I know you want the same too.
I want a kiss,
The feeling fills me with bliss.
Soon you will see,
I want you here with me.
So I can whisper, "I love you" in your ear,
I want to be with you year after year.
I know it is true,
Believe me when I say, "I love you too."
I can feel its power,
It just gets stronger every hour.
There will be no lies,
As tears fall from my eyes.
I can't wait to be with you,
All my love for you is true.
Forever is long,
But I know it’s not wrong.
And I know I will love you forever,
For I am rather clever.
