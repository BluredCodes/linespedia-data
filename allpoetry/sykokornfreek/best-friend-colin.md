Hey… it*s Colin!
We have a lot in common
He*s my bestest friend
And he will be until the end
He comes from Collinsville
And he was born in a big windmill
Orange is his favorite color
We*re so close, I call him brother
Soccer is what he likes to play
And No No No he isn*t gay
He thinks Jon Davis is a queer
And he tells me that without a fear
Because he knows I won*t care
Cuz getting mad at him, I wouldn*t dare
He doesn*t dress like me
He dresses like a preppie
But being best friends isn*t about what you wear
It*s about how much each of you care
And the good times you share
Colin hates me, and I know it*s true
But that doesn*t make me blue
He*s my bestest friend
And he will be until the end.
