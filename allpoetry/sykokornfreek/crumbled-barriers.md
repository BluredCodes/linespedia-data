---
title: "Crumbled Barriers"
writer: "SykoKoRnFreeK"

My barriers were up
You weren't getting in
No not ever
Sure, I loved you
But I wouldn't give in
I was fearful of pain
Anguish and sorrow
My barriers stood tall
Proudly they blockaded
Against your explosions
I wouldn't let you prevail 
No not again it wouldn't happen
But it did
You prevailed
I barricaded
But you still crumbled my barriers
Burnt them
Then threw them in the ocean
The ocean of pure love
I had given in
Scared to death I got closer to you
Trying to rebuild my barriers
I tried to keep my distance
But it was too late
They were no longer of importance
You were
We were
I began to actually love you
With my whole being
And now that it's too late
I will never go back on these feelings
And I'll never have to build another barrier
Again
