title: "The Least You Could Do"
writer: "SykoKoRnFreeK"

Like a fairy, you opened your colorful wings
And lifted me high up into my life
Your eyes looking into mine
Sparkling, telling me you've touched the stars
Your shy nature
Just makes you seem so mysterious
Your unique make up of the world
Tells me that you've been everywhere
You're beautiful face flutters around inside my heart
You sprinkled your pixie dust unto my soul
Turned my life around

I'm living with you in your kingdom in the sky
In your heart and your majesty 
Nobody could be happier than I
Unless they too were touched by your magic
But it's forbidden unto any other
For you committed it to me
You said it was the least you could do
If it truly was, then I'd love to see what you could do
If you gave it your all
