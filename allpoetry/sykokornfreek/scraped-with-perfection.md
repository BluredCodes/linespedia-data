---
title: "Scraped With Perfection"
writer: "SykoKoRnFreeK"
slug: "scraped-with-perfection"
source: "AllPoetry"

Death, torment, demise
Killing, torture, crying
Blood, scars, pain
Hurting, fighting, trying

That was my life
Up until the point
You rescued me from my prison
The prison inside my mind

You reached deep inside me
Touched my very soul
As we started to spin
We fell into a void that began to open
A new world was created
And I was born

Instead of death
There was eternal life
No more torment
My life was stagnant
I felt no demise
For I had you to hold

No more killing
Besides, who deserved it?
Torture was seized 
Replaced by total bliss
The only tears that fell from my eyes
Were of only happiness

No blood was shed
Not in our eyes
My scars faded away
Washed away by day
The pain was silenced
By your delicate hands

The hurting, what hurting?
That's how it seems today
The constant fighting
Has been replaced with relaxing
Trying isn't hard
When your lips are always near

Life, smile, love
Living, bliss, happiness
Pretty, wonderful, delight
Care, relaxing, caress

That's how I feel today
You've poured yourself all over me
Drowning me in love
And I'm cherishing every drop
You did it with one, single kiss
Now it's done
I have been scraped with perfection
