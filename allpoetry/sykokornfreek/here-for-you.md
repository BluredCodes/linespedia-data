---
title: "Here For You"
writer: "SykoKoRnFreeK"
slug: "here-for-you"

The pain you feel,
The words you say,
I am aware of them,
Every single day.
I hear you scream,
All of your pain.
Wanting to help you,
Makes me insane.
I want to hug you,
And let you cry.
Please take my shoulder,
Don't be shy.
No matter where you are,
And what you do,
Always know,
I'm here for you.
