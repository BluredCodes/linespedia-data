---
title: "Let Go of Me"
writer: "SykoKoRnFreeK"
slug: "let-go-of-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

There is something inside me
I can't destroy it
Can't pull it out
Anymore

Only one soul has seen it
He's on hold
Today

Digging to find it
Tearing away my existance
Let me be
Let go of me
I was content
Before you came

You are a menace
Tracing my moves
Tickling my atmosphere
With your solitary touch

Let go
I don't need you
I'm in love
With another

Sameone who won't hurt me
Won't kill me
Like you did so long ago
I should be over you
I'm not
Why not?

Let me be at rest again
This elusive pain is killing me
You're dispicable
A low life
I need you not
Never again
To mess things up
With my love

I won't be yours
I won't hold your hand
I can't today
I'm sorry
I'm in love
I'm happy with my love
This time

Get over me
Let me go
Let me be free
Like I was once before
Without you
BeforeI met you
The day before
Let me live then
With him
Anything
As long as you're gone
