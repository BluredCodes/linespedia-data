Death bells ring, are you listenin?
In the lane, all is glistenin
A horrible sight
We’re frightened tonight
Walking in a killer’s wonderland
In the mall there is murder
Chaos and furor 
Psycho clerks, tall
Want to hurt all
Here in a killer’s wonderland
In the meadow, we see a gallow
With a freshly tied noose
We know what it’s for
Somebody has got a cooked goose
Later on, we’ll perspire
As we roast, in the fire
Our skin starts to boil
It’s raw and smells spoiled
Here in a killer’s wonderland
Some cannibals, few
Got in the act too
Here in a killer’s wonderland
