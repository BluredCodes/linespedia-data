---
title: "Death Grip"
writer: "SykoKoRnFreeK"
slug: "death-grip"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Look unto these eyes before you
Dissect the emotions
They revolve around love
A love forbidden unto you
Against this law among us
Illegal
Who knows what will become of us?

You smile
I melt
You kiss
My being tingles
You laugh
I can’t help but feel the admiration in you

I feel like dying
You’re never how you seem
I feel I could never be happier
Then I’m disappointed again
What happens each time?
What goes wrong?
Is it love?
Is it just a phase?
We’ll see

Disgust
Adornment
Fore shame
Enlightenment
It doesn’t make sense
The way I feel with you

Study this soul of mine
It’s at ease because of you
Because you’re calming my heart
With your eyes

I had a death grip on my veins 
Until you shaded my existence
Changed my outlook into love
I can’t do this
It’s wrong
No

I love you
I care about you
I want you
I need you
I want to kiss you
Touch you
Everything in me I want to let you feel
I love this feeling I have
When I’m with you

You’ve corrupted my mind
From being in pain
From seeing eye to eye with suicide
I got thrown so close to the pit
But you helped to pull me away
Just in time
 I returned the favor
The night you said you wanted to 
I wouldn’t let you let go
That way
Alone

I told you to go
I dared you to 
In my mind
Because I would have surely followed
So I could look into those eyes again
But you left suddenly
Left me wondering
What to do

You loosened the grip I held
Upon my veins
You helped me see it through
Of what to do
I thought I’d take this time to thank you for always being there
And putting up with my confusion
I love you for it
I always will

I hope you thank me too
Because I have tried to help you as much as I could
But what could I do?
Being so far from you, emotionally

Whatever the circumstances 
You know I’ll be here
Ready to show you yourself through my eyes
I know you’ll see it in mine
Because I feel you inside of me
Loosening and loosening still
On this grip I hold upon my veins
