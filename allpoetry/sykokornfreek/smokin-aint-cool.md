People who smoke bother me
Walking around smelling like exhaust fumes from a car
Thinking they’re awesome
Scum of the Earth is what they are

2 grams wood alcohol
½ gram candle wax
4 chips of a sewage pipe
2 grams swamp gas

4 grams butane
2 grams formaldehyde
4 grams arsenic
3.4 grams of pesticide

Seeing a guy
Inside a cloud of smoke
Is not at all appealing
It makes me want to choke

Yellow fingertips
The breath of a bear
Dreary, drooping eyes
Greased and skanky hair

I hear my friend always talking
About how smoking is so cool
He just doesn’t seem to understand
It makes him look like a fool

Smoking causes internal damage
Like cancer in your lungs and mouth
Leaving permanent scars in your body
Never being able to live them down

Death will come at an earlier age
Than when it was meant to be
Each cigarette takes 7 minutes off your life
Not being able to keep a memory

If you think to try smoking
Forget the idea
It’s stupid and a waste of life
Besides, it makes you gross!
