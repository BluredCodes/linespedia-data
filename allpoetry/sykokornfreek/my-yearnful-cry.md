---
title: "My Yearnful Cry"
writer: "SykoKoRnFreeK"
slug: "my-yearnful-cry"

I love you
What else can I say?
You’ve made me what I am today
Please, don’t go away
I want you to stay with me forever
This moment is so perfect
Just like you and I 
Without you I would surely die
Pour yourself all over me
And I’ll cherish every drop here on my knees
Our chemistry is identical
Sure, through the years
We’ll see some tears
But we’ll conquer fears
And last forever
I want your heart
All to myself
I want you to love me
Like I know you can
Look behind your own soul
And tell me who you see
I’m sure you’ll find you and me
We may be a little different
But in my heart’s defense I’m telling you
We’ve got enough in common to bond us forever
I don’t seem to see
What makes you think you have to leave me
This can’t die
I yearn to live
When you mentioned leaving
I felt my heart go up in flames
Put out this raging fire
Tell me that your in love with me
Look deep within my eyes
Gently touch my face
Drown my emotions in a kiss
Hold me in a warm embrace
Tell me how much you need me
Be here when I’m lonely
I know you love me
How can you just walk away?
You’ve done so much for me
In this short period of time
I love you so much
These words are too simple for the way that I’m feeling
Don’t sever my soul
Don’t dig deeper, just to crawl out
You’ll leave your touch
Permanently embedded in my soul
My heart, you stole
