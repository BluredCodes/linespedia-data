Every night you leave me
leave me wanting more
always with goodnight or goodbye

What’s good about it?
You leave me and I’m sad

Slowly I drift off
Slowly I dream about you
Slowly I wake crying 

I can feel you there next to me
But I need more
I need you next to me
I need to touch you
I need to kiss you

And when I cry
I pick up the phone 
Slowly dial your number

Then I think of you sleeping
So peaceful
So correct

I hang up
I wait for you to wake
I wait to tell you I love you
But I’m tired of waiting
I need you next to me

How much longer can I wait
Not much

Time doesn’t know how it kills me
It’s like it enjoys my pain

When our day comes
I will cry
But this time 
I will no longer need you
