title: "Darkness Unrestrained"
writer: "SykoKoRnFreeK"

I am the God of your life
I am the bringer of sorrow in your loved ones
I bring to you, a vision of the unexpected
I am easily feared, as well as I should be
I am the last
It's just a matter of time until you meet me
I'll be here forever
You feel me get closer to you
You are always thinking of me
You think I'm terrible 
You'll see me face to face someday
I am not first
I will never betray you
I am the one whom the world sees unfit to live
I am the hatred that bleeds from the walls
I am the black-hearted soul that prowls in the alley
I am the tears of all that have cried
I am the blood of all that have died
I am the reason people fear the night
I am the reason nightmares were created
I am the embodiment of evil
I am the master of puppets, both live and dead
I am the ruler of the world of night
I am the killer of all things pure
I am the creator of war
I am the slave master of the universe
I am darkness unrestrained
I am the final judgment
Do you know me?  
Will you recognize me when I stare you in the face?
Will you know it? Will you know me?
I am eternal 
If you fear me… 
Then you know me…
And to know me is to embrace me…
To embrace me…
Is to except me…
Rest now and sleep 
It's my gift to you.
I am none other...



Than Death
