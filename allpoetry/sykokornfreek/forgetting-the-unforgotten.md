---
title: "Forgetting the Unforgotten"
writer: "SykoKoRnFreeK"
slug: "forgetting-the-unforgotten"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/26241-Forgetting-the-Unforgotten-by-SykoKoRnFreeK"
---

I hurt so bad inside
I’m trapped within you
Your soul is a kidnapper
And I’m its prisoner

You may think it’s all in the fun
But I’ve got some news for you
This isn’t a game
You should be ashamed
I love you
I care about you
I’d die for you

I wish you could see it through my eyes
Everyday it gets worse
The pain, the hurt, and the sorrow

I’m drowning in your eyes
Please stop this raging flood
Raise my spirit high
Stop shedding my blood

I saw you smile
As I slowly melted like a lighted candle
Then you left me
All alone
I felt the fire in every bone

I’d do anything to see you smile
Even if only once in a while
But I’ve been visited by reality
And only seen betrayal

When you left me you forgot something
You forgot to unlock this cage
The shackles chained to my legs
Are still squeezing tight
You left in a rage
Forgetting the unforgotten
Your love
Me
Crying alone in her prison
Your prison
Your soul
