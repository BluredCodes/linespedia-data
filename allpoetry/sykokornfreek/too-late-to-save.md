Digging into my soul
You left a permanent impression
You left your love
You left yourself

Why did you leave me?
Why are you gone?
Why did you hurt me so bad?

I used to look at you as my god
But now I’m no better than a Christian
Worshiping something that isn’t there

Now I am condemned to a fate 
A fate you sealed me into

It was so perfectly planned
You didn’t have to lie so well
You could have kept me at bay 

But from day one you had it all planned

Why do you hurt me?
Why is it so constant?
Must you be so vile?

I have to get away
I have to save my heart
I must leave this world 
I must set my heart free

I’ll leave with a bang
And you won’t care
It will be so perfect 

Maybe someday you will feel remorse for what you did
Maybe someday you will care
But it will all be too lateone of the poems my love wrote
