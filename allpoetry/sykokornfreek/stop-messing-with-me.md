title: "Stop Messing With Me"
writer: "SykoKoRnFreeK"

I thought you loved me
I thought you cared
I thought you wanted me
I thought two hearts, we shared

Then I saw, what I didn’t see before
You didn’t really want me
You just wanted to hurt me
And now you’ve come back for more

Stop messing with me
I’m only accused
Not the true enemy
You’re just confused
There’s no way you could enlighten me

I had no freedom
I was trapped in slavery
Deceitful honesty
I’m a human
Not your machine

You can’t now say you love me
After what you did to me
I loved you more than words can say
And yes, in fact, I still do

There are now a lot of things I don’t understand
All the times we were together
You always seemed to be happy with me
You also always talked about forever

When it would come near parting time
We used to always cry
Were your eyes just telling lies?
Or did I mean something to you?

No!  You’re just playing mind games
You’re trying to get me back
So you can hurt me even worse this time
Your soul is black

I’m not  going to fall for it again
You think you can easily win me over
And then get rid of me even easier
No, you’re the one who’s disappointed now
