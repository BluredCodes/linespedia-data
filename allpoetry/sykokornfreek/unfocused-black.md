As we slowly go into our first kiss
You wonder where I am taking you	
You wonder why I brought you here

As the unfocused black swells around us
We get closer and closer
We’re almost there

We take one look at our past life
All we see
A noisy roller coaster, kids screaming, an old man asking for change

Pandemonium, chaos, poverty
It’s all we used to know

But as our lips slowly touch
We start to spin
And we finally kiss
We are reborn

We start to fall
Holding each other dearly

This was meant to be
We are perfect now
Nothing could stop us

We forget what we were
And accept this overwhelming feeling

Everything disappears
Everything but us
With just one kiss

We are left alone in this unfocused black
Together
