---
title: "It's Not So Bad"
writer: "SykoKoRnFreeK"
slug: "its-not-so-bad"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/26247-Its-Not-So-Bad-by-SykoKoRnFreeK"
---

You tear it all away
And leave me with nothing
But then you repent
And it's not so bad

You break my heart
And trample on the sadness
But then you embrace me
And it's not so bad

You strike my pain
Bring unhappiness
But them you call me
And it's not so bad

You taste my sadness
While marveling in my misery
But then you kiss me
And it's not so bad

You bring the blood
Throw salt in the wounds
But then you hold me
And it's not so bad
