title: "Going Home"

When you left me that day
I had not a clue why
I was destroyed
I was killed

Not outside
But inside
Now I should finish the job
You’re no longer there for me

You’re the love that walked away from me
It takes time to mend the wounds of all this suffering
Time I don’t have
Look at me now
I’m miserable and weak

You left me
And I’m left wondering why
There’s nobody left
and I’m left rotting in my head
Alone

Here I stand now that I’m alone
With no one to comfort me
One set of footprints in the sand
No one to take my hand

Memories in me
Cocooned in misery

The darkness overcomes
Soul soars to the other plane
Existence past the door
I sail through Purgatory’s bay

Receive me or cast me away
On the other side
The time has come

Lock and load
