Resistance is futile
When it comes to loving you
The love I hold is much too powerful
Why even try to stop?
Why even try to hold back?

We could hold it back
But it would be inevitable 
Until our mourning
Our pain
Our yearning
Ran our eyes dry and flushed blood to our cheeks

Like a scrapbook from Hell
It would break us
And we would collapse
Our stagnant hearts would slowly be eaten away
By the maggots of inutility

Look closely
Believe in us
You’ll see we’ve succeeded
My love

Hold me close
Fold up in me
Don’t hold back
Just give in
I know very well you’re scared to death of me right now
But give in and trust me
Let the feeling flow
Let it run free

But what if I let go of you?
Let you fall alone?
You’d be lost and afraid
Then all the fear was lifted off of you
Would you be brave enough like you say?
To pull the trigger?
And end your life right there and then?
Or would you find yourself and keep the rage at bay?

Let’s give it a shot…

Look at you now
That’s pitiful
You’re worthless
Why should I bother with you?
You suck!
You’ll never amount to anything

How do you feel now?
Hurt and alone?
Now time is coming to a close
Decide 
Pull the trigger?
Or keep yourself at bay?
Which will it be?
