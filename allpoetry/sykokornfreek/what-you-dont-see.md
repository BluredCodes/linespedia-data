---
title: "What You Don't See"
writer: "SykoKoRnFreeK"
slug: "what-you-dont-see"

Look into me
Tell me what you see

My eyes
Full of depth and perfection
All you’re seeing is deception
They’re full of lies and mistakes

My lips
Plush and full of pretty secrets
How deceiving
They behold my tragedies and my pain

My hands
Cute and full of creativity
No way you mean the same hands that have hurt me
Many times before

My heart
So pure and full of love
I Doubt it
Chipped and faded
Drained of life
Chocked full of misery

My soul
You see something clean and untouched
Wrong
It’s worn and tarnished
Weak and frail

Me
You see a little child filled with schoolgirl dreams
Well, to tell you the truth
I’m full of pain, sorrow, angst, and death

Just let go of everything I seem to be
And realize this insanity
I’m not all you think I am
I’m much more, but negatively

Look into me
And you’ll see everything
The emptiness
The hate
The darkness from within
Will seep out of my veins unto you
You seem to really care 
But you aren’t seeing all that’s there
My pain, my anguish, and my tears

You think I’m strong
I’m frail and weak
You think I’m warm and content
But I’m frozen and morose

Look into me
Don’t you see?
I’m not what you’ve expected of me
I’m death and demise
Not your most valuable prize
I’m hateful and discontent
Not passionate and loving
I’m deceiving and filled with lies
Just look into these tear-filled eyes
I’m dying inside
I can’t see what you see
So I understand how you can’t see the truth
This is all what you don’t see
But what you’re seeing
Isn’t me
