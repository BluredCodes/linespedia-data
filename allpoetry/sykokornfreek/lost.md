Lies, anger, hate
they shoo love away
I build the walls to shelter us from them

Come on
Step out
I’ll be your strength

Focus on your love
Release
And run to me
You can never look back on the visions from the past
They wilt and fade in time

You have to just trust me to hold your hand through it all
Then we turn and walk away from the feelings of hate and dismal

And we sever all ties
And then hate and dismal come between us
Then I decide to become the sacrifice
Anything in order to save you

So run away, I’ll be ok
Don’t turn back
Don’t have regrets
I’ll be fine

I’ll catch up
I won’t get lost
I have you to lead me

And if I do lose my way
I’ll sit and cry
Because I have then lost you
And I’ll never get you backone of the poems my boyfriend, Eric wrote
