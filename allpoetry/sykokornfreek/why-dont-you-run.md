---
title: "Why Don't You Run?"
writer: "SykoKoRnFreeK"

If there's a plane leaving town 
I hope you're on it
If there's a train moving fast down the tracks
I hope you caught it
Because down here isn't where you need to be
So, catch a ride
Or catch a cab 
But don't you walk 
Why don't you run?
Cut a path across the blue skies 
Straight in a straight line you can't leave here fast enough
Find a truck and fire it up 
Lean on the gas and off the clutch
If you don't have a suitcase 
Grab a box or an old brown paper sack
Pack it light or pack it heavy
Take a truck or take a Chevy 
There's a shortcut to the highway out of town 
Why don't you take it? 
Don't let that speed limit slow you down
Go ahead and break it
Just remember I don't want you walking
So why don't you run?
