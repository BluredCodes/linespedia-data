A Thousand Stabs Into My Heart

You told me that you loved me
You asked for my heart
You said no one could brake us apart
I felt so loved 
I felt so needed
We talked every day 
You said you loved me
You said without me
You don’t know where you’d be
I felt that this was forever
I turned around and in your arms
Was one of my friends?
I felt a thousand knives
Stab my heart
And once again
I am in pain
I thought you were different
I thought you were cool
But I guess I was merely a fool
You thought this was a game
But I know that my heart will never be the same
Or was that what I thought 
The last time
But now I know 
That my heart was not to be mend
Now and forever
