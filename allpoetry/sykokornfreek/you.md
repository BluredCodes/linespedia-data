All my walls are falling down
Pain’s aloft misery

I’m sure the lessons were learned 
I’m sure the punishments went well deserved

In my life, meaningless
Life is loss
Look at me

I’m sure someday I’ll wake up
I’m sure that some day I will wake from my dream

I focus 
Tunneling to your light
Your loving smile
I wake up

I wake with you beside me
Loving me 
Taking care of me
Comforting me

I love you for what you have done

But don’t feel sorry for me
I don’t need any sympathy or empathy 
Just your love
Just you
