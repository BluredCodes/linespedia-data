Surrounded by glass on all sides
Something keeps you from breaking through
You see the one you love on the other side
You scream
You cry
What can you do?
But watch her teary eyes
As he takes her away from you

Not just away from you
But away from home
From love
From peace
From happiness
From feeling

He ties her up
And gags her

You think bad things aren’t supposed to happen
What happened to your plans?
Your dreams?
He dug a whole into your bond
As you realize why he did
You notice the glass was never there
It was a mere illusion
To keep you back from her

He takes out a knife
Holds it to her throat
You scream
He jabs it into her
You cry
You immediately thrive to die

He won’t kill you
He won’t even try
He likes your pain
So you attempt it yourself
But he won’t allow it
He’s taken control of you
He shackles you to a wall
All you can do is cry
Your heart is up in flames

He makes you stare at her lifeless body
Burning like ashes falling
Tears fall from your eyes
You break the shackles
Run to her aid
Knowing it’s too late
You hold her and begin to cry
As you kiss her lightly upon the cheek
Then you fall to her feet

Grasping the knife lying upon the ground
You gaze back at him
You tell him thank you
What else is left to say?
You tell her you love her
Even though she can’t hear your voice
And she doesn’t reply
You know she feels it somehow
Someway

You take the knife
In your shaking hands
Turn to look at him
And say look what you’ve caused
As you’re body grows weak
You lie upon the ground
With the faint dripping of blood
