One more kiss before we die
And eternally close our eyes
Soon we will be together forever
In eternal life
I wish to stay, and not to go
But what else can we turn to?
To die like this
With this last kiss
Is falsehood’s flame
And eternal shame
But you’ll never know
And neither will I

You won’t let us die?
You won’t give up?
What shall we do?
Try

Face to face
The passions breathe
Without us
They will die as well
So, with this last kiss let’s say goodbye

Farewell unto you
With this closing 
I kiss you
Deeper than ever before

Eyes closing
Lips not letting go
As we are right now
Hearts coming to a close
Souls touching blackness
We part from this life

As the spinning stops
We enter a new
Eternal bliss
And happiness
With that last kiss
Forever more
Together at last
With no interruptions
The way it should be
The way it should have been

I feel like I’m flying
But this time I truly am
Up here in the sparkling skies
With no one else to turn to
But to you
My love

I can now tell you I love you
The way I’ve always dreamt of 
And you can do the same unto me
And we can be happy forever

And to think it was all thawed out
