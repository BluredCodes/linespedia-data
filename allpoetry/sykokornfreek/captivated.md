"I love you," you breathe, giving mass-less sound
The utmost weight, as it dances around
Inside of my wondering head
The chains have been forged, and I slip them on
Like a new silk shirt; a smooth, cool prison
Created by words you just said
Your eyes lock me down, your smile turns the key
Your soft, warm kiss takes all freedom from me
And for this, I'm twitterpated
Though you’re my jailor, you’re my hero
I've got comfort untold, with your love within
By your love, I'm captivated
