By: D. Clark

Written: 15 Feb 2027

“Final days of the City of Babylon”


I can only hope this time capsule reaches you and from its contents teaches you,
For the knowledge inside is so vast greater then this asteroid that is to hit in ten days,
So much information each time decade and age, most events took place in the new age,
Never give into hate, greed, anger, or rage it will only spread and destroy you in the end,
Learn by our mistakes during our most bloody times of war and political hell look past you shall,
What is inevitable should be a reborn of a New Earth and a peaceful one at that, as it should be,
For no more fears of a primitive childish mind will hold such weapons of mass destruction,
For all such weapons are all in orbit in space right now, I will give you all I can in the next ten days,
In approximately two hundred and forty hours my own and countless others lives on this earth will be done,
I have accepting this fact for a long time now as first word became light in the year two thousand-one,
I feel so much love in my heart now more then ever in my fifty-seven years of life have I felt this,
So much spirituality in so many people right now, of all races from all nations never thought would come,
Seems prayers are being said twenty-four hours a day right now, God is being flooded with calls,
I can feel all the energy right now, sense all intense emotions, I hear thoughts of people as I walk near them,
Many have accepted their true divine spiritual gifts of insight these days so much trust right now,
A greatest of times was right as they said we will be living in I will not regret them and will relive them,
I welcome you to this soon to be New Earth as Gods new born children, I hope when you read this I will be right there,
I will join the rest and fly like an eagle of this great Nation and World that is united as one, as I soar into the clouds of eternal peace,
Just so free from this captivity right now as so much is waiting for you to venture, with a sky as no longer a limit as we will be with you,
Never be afraid of expression for it’s not a tangible possession it is yours like your soul never keep any hate inside,
Keep your pride for it can only go so far but too much will bring an indifferent unbalanced change one should not make,
Included in this wonderful packet are a series of sounds of nature from the songs of birds to the calming ocean waves,
I only hope this is not the only of nature left if so there are several duplicates of each recording which will bring you pure light,
So it came as each in their life sees a fallen angel, as such to bring onto a lesson of beyond value to be learned,
So he shall earn his key to unlock each closed door of the past, such value bares no currency or a sense of urgency,  
It will burn in our soul after such as one becomes one with mother earth as earned pain of trials of clairvoyant tendencies,
For what will come are later days of cherished expectations and divine enlightenment when our exam is placed before us,
I hear the heart of this City of Babylon beating with pure intensity now, as the last will and testimony of its spirit cry out in anguish,
As you feel or live days of great suffering as unbearable as your soul can take look up in the sky and you will see the one I see now,
You will see a sky of hope and future as my sky shows immeasurable catastrophic proportions with a demons face that nears,
I will carry you as I pass before the forever soon to come light, as you are our own newborn children raised from our parenthood,
Be cautious of the forged tongues that shall come of how mankind caused such devastation as some inequities could come about,
So many singing praise and glory to the lord and savior on all networks has been running for weeks now with great energy flowing,
People from all corners of the world right now are all in prayer as the rejuvenations have spread to every last soul during this hour...
