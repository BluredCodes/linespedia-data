By: D. Clark

With this second year of this new age from repeated dreams come words of a possible near fiery warning,
With this shall come deep beneath with the beast to growl will rise a red sun filled morning,
As a glance to a sight that was seen before as the great mountain we view of the cold dove awaken,
With the cries of his mother onto those chosen to hear during the sign out of sleep they are shaken,

As one who dreams of each advance of the beast with his dreaded attempt to control nature like before,
With the City of the Cosmic Needle as asleep as the great mountain but with ferocity awaken once more,
As the first sign is seen so clear as the first roar sounds as the mountain sheds its fiery tears, 
Then shall come such dark clouds as not from man but a true dragon unleashed massive fury of deep fears, 

With a power beyond the Childs most destructive toy as nature shall not weep as the beast leaps out of his cage,
 As the glory of the lessons we are taught during these days shall put out most of the fires of the beast and his rage,
So behold these words of warning have not read as truth make no mistake for beyond it shall be a new day of sun,
If such day is to come let it be another challenge as many with a strong faith in Jesus our Lord shall not lie down and be done...
