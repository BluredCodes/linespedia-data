By: D. Clark


As Einstein’s theory of relativity brings a distance of great longevity,
Comes soon of a world united becomes of such new energy prosperity,
When this hidden after year’s sheds joyful tears greater then our walk on the moon,
When beyond each brother as man leaves his mother a new world shall come soon,
 
When this leap as instant beyond sight as the new system found shines a star so bright,
Then with shields of absorption with this massive gravity distortion faster then light,
As the dream of many of this human race to finally journey deep space is one we chase,
When so it comes as a day to hope to see as it’s meant to be a greatest discovery to face,

As this energy pulsates rapidly accelerates a propulsion of gravity said less then zero, 
Then as the eight of this day seen but not too late as they venture beyond any hero,
As all this comes a sign from ancient of time of a possible race left a face on mars,
With this being the key then a golden heart to see past the long imprisoned behind bars,

So if this day shall bring this change over night with a test of a speed of light,
What a day this will be as the final conquest we see of mans greatest flight,
If this day is to come it will take many changes of all before our journey has begun,
A day of discovery as we begin our recovery for all as one second to none we become.
