Subject: Replenishment of Babylon (after seven hours of silence)



As difficult as any position that comes of us in life of our duties and responsibilities no such one will be as great as this. Seven hours of pure silence was given in the wake of a most horrific calamity this human race has ever faced. Just as unexpected as most events took place as unreal as we watched them during the past twenty-five years. This will rank at the top of any that proceeded in human existence. I took time out during the past seven hours of the aftermath of this great catastrophe to read the many hundreds of thousands of messages that flooded our data system during the final minutes of the calamity. I stand before the few who are left on both colonies of Mars and Lunar base. With the greatest of all emotions flowing as this task placed on all of the survivors will be of the greatest of importance. Our fate will be determined on the strength of our faith. So much energy is being felt right now with the rest of the survivors as if all the countless lives that were gone in an instant somehow filled all our hearts with incredible divine love. Our spiritual gifts of insight have greatly increased over the massive event that occurred over seven hours ago. A gift has been given to this race once again. New Earth will rise up from the charred remains of all those who fallen. Immortal is what will become of our planet once more. During these past decades of mans inevitable succession of survival has given even us such hope that we will live on. There will be a New Earth, a better world to live in. Our journey has not stopped but only has speeded up. We have not fallen but have risen. Our limits are no longer of which we look up in our skies no more. A full replenishment of Babylon will be a long and challenging path. It’s a challenge we shall welcome and will be again the words many have said during one titanic moment a voice from the grasps of death yelled Victory will always be ours! A new golden age of man has come on us as many said would; we are the parenthood of tomorrow, a New Earth of peace love and indifference. A new glorious world we will see once again reborn, as one of many, one of all these new timeless marvels built with the golden heart will not fall!
