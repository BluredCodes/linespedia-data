By: D. Clark

Written: 02 Feb 2027

"After the One World Legion formed"


As the final challenge of the third cycle came,
About this time words from such poetry showed its fame,
With all nations become as one and a new leader called upon the entire world as United,
For many were filled with either great fear hate some were greatly excited,
Chaotic as it seemed during the rise of one other leader of greatness Bush who succeeded,
As most hate and evil in most lands condemned to their tombs as the floods receded,
There was a new globe during these days after both north and south poles tilted,
Great spiritual awareness hit with immeasurable force during an awakening of many gifted,
As the voices shouted out such unity to bring forth a race of one community,
When these words are read from the year two thousand and twenty seven,
For they have been sealed into the capsule protected from the beast that is to fall from heaven,
We have over come such apocalyptic times of man and nature after many great millenniums,
From seen of this day as timeless Pyramids bare a sign from a great never Forgotten Egyptian time,
With mans struggled questions who will not come unanswered as on the latter of the mind he climb,
 So as the time comes faster bringing this cosmic beast closer the entire world on a last call to avoid its disaster,
Just as the child with his most destructive toy matured as grown and changed its use for a greatest of joy,
Faiths became strong like a mothers and infants bond as it occurred the exact time of birth,
It had to take such a coming of One Titanic Moment to bring this day with a fraction of it showed during Sept 11th,
So final days become hours then become minutes as this most powerful weapon of this race is now in orbit in space,
All the world in silence await as the dinosaurs with no chance as us of their final fiery fate,
While I write this I have in my hand my bible as its read from front to back as each page turns if not to late,
Now being said a calculated trajectory of this furious asteroid to hit holy sands of once ancient Babylonian land,
A final order is to be giving as oddly an unthinkable one to launch as much nuclear power to create a sun,
So I shall ride this endless roller coaster of great emotions with the entire world in one seat of pure excitement not defeat,
How amazing to watch this event unfold as it don’t seem real on high definition television as so clear we see this object near,
Such a great picture as it comes in all stations of one show, a season finally of the human race as hope sits in orbit in space…
