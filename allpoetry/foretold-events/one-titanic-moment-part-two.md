By: D. Clark

Written: 11 Feb 2027

"Beast nears The City of Babylon"

As there is no point on changing the channel any more I now reflect on past’s shore,

I hope for our example to pay such a great price before the hope that will be reborn,

As I write these words for those from possible many years from now will rejoice somehow,

So much has been learned as we look back from the past as lessons to later glory earned,

I look outside and see the trees and the birds that fly for they will never ask or care of why,

It seems they are better off then we are for they somehow accepted what is coming from space,

Shall they shed one tear if even nature will as this falling beast strikes and kills an entire race,

So much was learned during the past two decades so many events took place with many disgrace,

A conquest of time proved once again of the need for this race to venture out to space,

We will move on for our seed has been planted into the cosmos with many, who were chosen,

There are few of all minds and cultures that will watch this as I live it on Mars, as I face my end,

Such vast minds who are safe now on refuge on the fourth brother away from her dying mother,

I feel my fate is easy for I shall be gone in an instant, when it hits half the earth shall be incinerated,

I write words for future generations to understand what struggle man has undergone, how we must change,

How I know so well now of all those who died before us in all three world wars as they died for all of us,

Now I see a brighter side to this, as it’s my turn to pay my dues, as soon I will see a light so true,

So much spirituality has come to me over the past 26 years; I pray to God that no one else has a day as this,

I ask for those who find these messages too keep your faith intact and cleanse your mind to later find,

We are all one race a human race never to be prefect but always share one form of light, which is love,

So calm outside a radiant sunny day out as I walk the empty streets, a few outside preaching the gospel,

I know as you read this you will have many questions that I hope will be answered, as all is possible,

What I learned is without writing, as it became my dream now it seems more important then food or sleep,

Seems most took this news well, I knew of this before it came as I wrote a poem just of this time,

So in a way I prepared myself about 26 years ago when it first was told to me of an alleged rumor,

But now it has become more then even a cancerous tumor in our hearts with only one slim chance,

I can see the angle of death over the whole earth his shadow all over all lands to plague us of life,

For this life has been more enlightening then imaginable living as said of the greatest of times,

So no birds that fly in our ending blue sky will even cry only man will during these days,

A Beast nears the City of Babylon as each second passes, each letter I type each word you read...
