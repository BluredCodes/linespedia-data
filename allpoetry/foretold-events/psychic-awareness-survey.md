By: D. Clark


Would you say time is an illusion of one we can alter?
Will Time catch up, as does our understanding?
Does each letter come from thoughts as seen just ahead?
As you drive your car you see ahead a red light,
But you do not stop for you know it will be green,
As you hear a song played constantly in your mind,
Then you turn on the radio it plays at the exact part,
When you toss a coin and think hard enough,
That coin will land on the side you want it too,
If you say the first idea that comes to you,
Just the second before you meet someone you don’t know,
It could be very possible it is exactly what the other is thinking,
If you seem to be drawn to a movie or of its idea or story,
Then later to understand why it’s burned in your mind,
As this movie becomes reality as a form of warning when,
Time was able to catch up, a lesson from this for time after,
If you are able to sense emotions without talking to someone,
Can guess exactly what the person is thinking even on the Internet,
Just by reading a simple poem or letter you can sense their emotions,
If you can sense and are drawn to certain people of an interest you have,
If you can sense somehow a persons religion without knowing, 
Guess or know somehow if a person is lonely or in need of love,
Just by one look at them or a simple line of text on a text chat,
Have you suddenly lost a fear of ghosts and spirits and of the paranormal?
Do you see sudden small flashes of light in the corners of your eyes?
Somehow you will know what I mean as it will suddenly come to you,
Will songs without words bring you a flow of uncontrolled knowledge?
Will these words as they come to you seem beyond what you would write?
Do you find yourself for hours in a trance state in a deep thought or conversation?
Is this conversation more important then sleep, food or even work or money,
Are you able to feel others that are in pain feel the exact same pain?
Can you relate to any or all of these, and why are you shocked now?
Will you see this as a waste of your time or of a great interest?
Is all the above you read something you somehow always knew of?
Will you now see those flashes of light in the corners of your eyes?
As I do right now while typing this, will you have that cloudy feeling?
Will you have such dreams so real that when you awake you feel dizzy?
As if you have just re-entered your body, or as if your heart stopped,
But your heart began to beat again as the total numbness clears your body,
Have you ever felt this like when your circulation from your hand,
As it falls asleep it hurts and you cannot move it as it has fell asleep,
But have you felt that same sensation all over your whole body?
How many times have you felt this during the week, and during dreams?
Are you thinking right now that Foretold Events has lost it totally?
And are going to leave a rude comment out of a reason that maybe,
You can actually relate to one or all of the questions said?
Or leave this comment as honesty as you can, honest, as it should be.
