By: D. Clark


Once a calm shall blow onto this land from above down below,
Never will be this day be forgotten of such wounds suffered to bring,
Every living soul from land to sea to witness this New Age as meant to be,

With these days showing us both heaven and hell in a pattern,
One color of blood will bleed these wounds of this race,
Rivers will flow like the gentle glow of this new star,
Lives will change intensely during these times as a shift occurs of consciousness,
Devolvement of the mind from young to old will drastically excel more then any time,  

Good and evil will battle more in all aspects of life in all of us and all over the globe,
Once this one world community is established after such blood, as did our civil war,
Venom from this black plagued serpent will come of a cure already know from HIV,
Eventually truth will come from this over fifty years of cover-ups about extraterrestrials, 
Right now still silence during each day with a real truth buried until the precise moment,
New lands, new laws, new justice, new freedoms, new world will become then all die,
Memories of this once proud country will haunt many for times to come not forgotten,
Evenings of sharing changed days thoughts and wisdom given from surgical implants,
Never will a justification be said or heard its hidden like the secret black ops,
Trust in God will be all we have for trust in man will be a great tyranny of defeats, 

Before this shift in land and sea and all that was once free to see its meant to be, 
United We Stand Divided we fall from twin towers to furious eruptions of nature to all,
Silence is golden as also consent; we will see both sides of silence these days,
Hope will come from the eyes of the new born child brought into new lands of America.Try and find the hidden message.
