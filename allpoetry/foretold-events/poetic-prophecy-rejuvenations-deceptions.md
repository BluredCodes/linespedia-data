By: D. Clark


As to those unexpected during a time when words were undetected, 
Is when you finally accept doors of the past with keys while others rejected, 
You feel words like these and you accept them and after you stand corrected, 
As these past lessons need to be learned as wisdom is earned shall be protected, 

As is now such faiths that begun those as united as one with uncertainty here, 
With our thoughts onto others not of only self will become a new page so clear, 
After the swift rumble brings a shift amongst all eyes shall be a forged tongue, 
With eyes and mind open we will see pass the disguise as this evil looks young, 

When those awake with the lion as eyes earn courage is strong and effective, 
Is when this fallacy said by those of the tenth brother speak of a false directive, 
Begin as those fallen from the sky a great deception as all is still with false tears, 
As those see passed such fallacy as others not then quick changes to great fears, 

These words shall be poetry now and later realized as a day to live words of warning, 
For those fallen of beyond this time attempt to create the darkest day of earth in mourning, 
So these rejuvenations felt during these times must be revealed as unexpected they come, 
Then listen to your heart for my wound bleeds your blood instead of death live as a race of one.
