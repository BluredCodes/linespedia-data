BY: D. CLARK


"My words shall haunt those who read upon them, for times to come. What is to be deciphered from here on shall be met with judgments and ridicule of others from enslaved minds. Be Advised... This is could be my final Poetic Prophecy in the series for I have reached the End. More could come. Prepare for the greatest forbidden fear that you shall have come as images as you read. A true test of faith in Jesus Christ our lord is to come. Love and light shall prevail always! May 2003 will be a date to keep in mind."  




During this new age after such days of the greatest of terrors unknown,
Come to all who walk in the steps with shadows of inescapable death shall moan,
Your ground will shake, as the earth shall tremble as all mans homes will crumble,
From all the great ones from below rise from the beast spread with the greatest plague of pure death and chaotic rumble,

Rivers of pure blood will rise above the tallest mountain to turn all skies a dead of red,
Those fears of the flood of blood with the great fires not only mans but of nature instead,
With all the unleashed hell onto the lands from the beast shall swallow all air and light,
As for many days scores with less then a breath as the severe cold brings to many frostbite,

During each second passes without a means of returning as always felt a running out of time,
As this One World Legion forced under chaotic control slaughter all who want freedom as it becomes a crime,
Take note of such grave words of warning with each one possible with such unspeakable doom,
When during a time of a sign when soon an object in our night sky shall be brighter then our moon,  

So those who shall never see words as these who shall ridicule me I shall bring out to you so clear,
That if not being buried alive, smothered in a volcanic eruption, or drowning in titanic floods brings fear,
Then shall come as the easy way out being death for the final chapter of his judgments will come,
Cast down those of only self-preserving in all evils to eternal hell with faith in god brings a glorious new sun.  


God Bless you all.. God bless the world. We are one race of one who bleed one color of red. Make this a Century of Discover not of Disaster. For the sake of mankind we must all pull together. Or this will be our future...
