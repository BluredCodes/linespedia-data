By: D. Clark


During this New Age, Death will surround those who cause or wish it upon others,
Times will move faster as Sons and daughters will lose their fathers and mothers,

Hate, anger and greed will breed to exceed what the mortal soul will care to tell,
Those who will put blame on God for these years of great fears down to hell they fell,

Great tense urgency comes a one-world currency as both job and stock markets hit zero,
Winds these days blow still but hearts beat rapidly till during a third war to bring a new hero,

Then in dismay in a deceiving way this One World Evil lie to its race about aliens from space,
On the last page of the seven-year stage Gods judgments will come before all life’s case,

Faith must be stronger in any form of religion or even in ones self during these trying days,
For if faith is given in any form of love beyond the norm will come blessings in many ways,

Peace must be won of this race of one to win this final challenge that could fall from space,
We all have only one mother in this system of nine brothers of beautiful grace a glorious place,

Forget all the fears shed only joyful tears keep your soul intact to hell and back with a golden heart,
There will be no end only a new time will begin with a cleansed soul transcends to a brand new start,

Ocean waters will rise as tall as the blood filled skies but hope and glory never dies, it will be cherished,
A word will be heard all over this world, this word shall be “Victory!” while evil in all forms, perished,

So view her fiery dance warning of a never forgotten morning when global peace returns his son,
Because in my heart without a doubt ends all foes begins new friends for Tribulations Has Begun!
