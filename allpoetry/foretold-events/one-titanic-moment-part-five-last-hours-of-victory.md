By: D. Clark

Written: 26 Feb 2027

“Final hours of the City of Babylon”


There is still a great amount of thoughts that will be left unsaid for the time has come now as all begin then must end,
As always said there is no end only a beginning, this will be proven as words lived as you read these words you will live them,
I find a loss of words at this time as the hours are passing by as each line of text is written, the last hours of my own life are given to you,
We will always be there as you will never be alone, I and countless others will be there to help guide you through,
As you read these words that will survive against the demons of time and of the beast its self you will see the many centuries,
Many centuries will pass by the time you find this capsule; many will come from the fourth brother of the Mars colony,
Do not fear those who shall come down from the sky, as you will know in your heart for they are of the same human race,
They shall come seven days after the capsule seal is broken for a signal will be sent the moment it is open joy will come,
A great gift as the times of the ancient disciples will be given during this day, a gift of pure light as will tell of your insight,
Within the embedded code of life is written the world of the ancients great disciples of a stellar race from beyond,
Beware that ignorance is bliss of all existence as the true will become still, with knowledge becomes liberation,
Thoughts will transcend into actions from clairvoyance when troubled times come, a sight of a timeline will be present,
This timeline is one of possible in terms of a premeditated extent from guidance from the paranormal domain always present,
Each sight will come just before the occurrence but no make is to be made of such an emotion given from this as fear,
Fear will become a distraction, as it is an emotion that will bring control at times beyond your own will of control,
As the ages have come and gone one still remains clear of this New Age of Information how it will serve its purpose,
With the rise of the light of information from all lands a technology that is now given to you as in this capsule lays the key,
For those who endured the greatest of suffering as I feel mine is easy in comparison, those left on both colonies that are near,
Mars and Lunar colony were established to ensure the replenishment of soon to be New Earth, show welcome to such as they come,
For they have ventured a journey as great as yours as being the great ancestors who triumphed in solitude of our moon and fourth brother,
When they come they will bring great gifts of beyond your own time, they will be so far ahead of time its self, your heart will tell you,
Such joyful days of love, light and peace will be yours on all lands of the white dove if you keep faith of such they will always be,
Such a peace will prove its immortality in you and all who love each other and his mother, as it’s all we will truly need to live,
Hate, greed, anger, deceit, and adultery are the cause of such times for even these evils of human nature caused one outcome pure love,
Love and good always conquers over darkness, so brings this cleansing from the cosmos I can see this massive object in the night sky now,
For this could be my last sunrise for this is the final hours of the City of Babylon when the greatest times end to bring a New Earth,
As the asteroid enters our atmosphere it will create a great shockwave, such an immense wave of sound never heard in all human existence,
This final order of the full-scale assault is said to be in its final stages, less then two hours left until the word is given and hope will rain,
I now say once again to never give into hate or face the days of sanitations, for man from land to sea shall not escape what is meant to be,
My final message will be enclosed in the data systems, as comes my One Titanic Moment of over one thousand suns of blinding light comes,
If you do not by chance receive my final message the outcome will already be known to you and I will always be there to guide you thru,
Farwell always love and light lessons learned brings glory earned as true of the new he shall see, this capsule is now being sealed,
Hope faith emotion devotion a race of one has just begun we bleed one color of red live a life of discovery not one you shall dread.
God Bless all the people of New Earth may peace and prosperity fill all mans hearts from here to space.
Be proud always as all one of this great courage of the lion of this one race as the voice will never stop shouting on this day and for all that will come V-I-C-T-O-R-Y!

"Third Cycle is complete, all challenges were favored well."
