Inspiration came from one of Nostradamus' writtings. From century 10, quatrain 72.... 

The year 1999, seventh month,
From the sky will come a great King of Terror.
To bring back to life the great King of the Mongols,
Before and after Mars to reign by good luck.




By: D. Clark


With the cold dark rage during the fifth month third year new age, 
Brings more then what the eastern city bleeds sets the seven night stage, 
Then comes the still silence to all children and mother as the seconds of gore,
As the long nights pass by during this blood red sky strengthen faith in god more,

For what is to be seen over three thousand years ago like before as false it shall,
As this king of terror brings its false lie to all a truth shall be our sun many will tell,
Then those fallen angels shall rain down a red sky as weightless they fly,
As those with out faith shall not see down on one knee begging mercy and cry,
 
As the child listens to her mother and not a false tenth brother then many learn,
If any accept this great lie soon in our sky then king of terror from cosmos has return,
Many with sight beyond defeat shall not give into those giant of deceit they breed,
For our anger hate and greed today shall give them more to feed,

As called Nephilim in Hebrew or Sons of Anak in the bible even then they were resisted,
For these said to be from space said to have created the human race as words are twisted,
So if comes to pass the return during this shar past our ninth planet by far bare mark,
Then repeat this amazing tale of shifting poles of a man who knows as Noah and his Ark...
