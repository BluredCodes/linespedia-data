By: D Clark

As the sudden shift in all forms beyond the clouds of the mind occur with a global intensity,
With a momentous step taken by those with out a means of returning becomes gifted with a awaken ability,
When this catastrophic bolt of pure divine knowledge strikes those selected who can see beyond fear is tranquility,
Only when both eyes are open to see all doors of a distant past as lessons learned then shall earn nobility,

During great times of rejuvenations when the forecasting of each climate calamity which bestow onto earth a true hell,
Is when the selected can tell which keys shall open the doors in threes while other minds closed could not and down they fell,
Into dreams of them as they show not or give into petty fears while in dreams the greatest from the sky shall shed such tremendous tears,
When all seen by him from above and below weep not of horror or sadness but pure joy as others chaotically face their greatest fears,

When the gifts flow as comes to pass a great awakening as only those will see a true light of sunshine during an afflicting morning,
Become words like these and truths from the biblical Revelations of an End of Times of old to tell of birthing pains of a sobering warning,
For not all only some shall be blinded to the final chapter on the last page of man as dwell on steps of death he shall as prophets tell,
While our future is not written as prophets come to light during these uncertain times of the greatest global change for better it shall,

As my Poetic Prophecies will not be proved true or will I ever say likewise as my only purpose of such writings is to open some eyes,
With my final wish upon who read my words strengthen their faith in Jesus our Lord to bring the greatest love and light to all dark skies,
For my eyes were closed for so long I stood blinded until a time of great blessings came as new each day brought to me believe you will see,
As all my tribulations from my time as young were needed as I look and welcome any challenge with faith as you shall as its meant to be.
