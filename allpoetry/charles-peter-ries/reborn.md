Alighting for still moments
Sun shining through
Late morning mist
Butterflies and bees
Sipping nectar, pollinating
Fucking and fertilizing
Flying to new lovers
Tulip shaped lips quivering
Sleek ivory colored stems calling
	“have me,
 	 taste me, 
  	 make me blossom and
  	 you will be reborn in the April rain.”
