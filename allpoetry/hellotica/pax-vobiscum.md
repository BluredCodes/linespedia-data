Pax Vobiscum! (Peace Unto You!)



                   God No!
                   I wouldn't kill you!
                   and you wouldn't kill me!
                   for friendly and non-violent
                   is the way we really like to be!

                   That is until or unless
                   some adverse situation
                   just happened to arise?
                   with deceit,skullduggery,
                   provocation or lies!

                   Like a war or a police action
                   and you were on the other side
                   why then I'd have no trouble to decide
                   to rip and tear your offending hide!

                   Or to shoot an AK-47
                   at your traitorous head
                   or garrot you till your
                   eyes bugged out dead

                   But then maybe you,d
                   blast a bazooka at my car
                   or toss a molotov cocktail
                   into my crowded bar

                   Or on me and the kids maybe
                   you'd drop your blistering napalm,
                   disperse your killing chemicals
                   or spray some obscene nerve gas bomb

                   Or maybe I'd have to torture you
                   for some vital information
                   with pliers, a propane torch
                   and some bloody flagellation

                   But then otherwise - Hell No!
                   I wouldn't kill you
                   and you wouldn't kill me!
                   for friendly and non violent
                   is the way brothers really like to be!What ever happened to diplomacy and negotiating "Peace"?
