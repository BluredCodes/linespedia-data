Born With Resolution


                   I was born with resolution
                   and with resilient constitution
                   I was born to fight!
                   to forge this vision
                   I see as right!

                   Mankinds ignorance will
                   be my battlefield!

                   Truth and Justice!
                   will be my shield!

                   ...Onward Yo!
