The Old Bald Game


                   Bald is Beautifull!
                   or so they say?

                   Well I guess if you,ve
                   had a falling out
                   its not such a bad play!

                   Rather that than pay
                   to wear a plastic weave
                   and elicit enough
                   snide comments to make
                   your poor pate grieve!

                   But on that whole
                   shining surface,you say
                   you don,t give a fig?
                   no matter how nasty
                   and dark the dig?
                   you still treasure
                   the thin security
                   of your glaring wig?
