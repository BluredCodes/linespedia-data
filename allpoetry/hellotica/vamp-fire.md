Vamp-Fire Vanquishers
1313 Castle View Lane
Transylvania TRA-13

To:C.Dracula (or Occupant) 

         Dear Sire!

                   May I enquire? If you're the Vampire
                   who is currently on fire?
                   If so? Then that news will certainly be dire!

                   But then luckily! I have an extinguisher,
                   thats presently available for hire!
                   and if I do say its a bargain,
                   then I'm not just singing out of choir!

                   So if theres some urgency to your
                   current funeral pire-mire?
                   Then please phone,or do send a wire!

                   
         P.S. Also have you received our latest flyer?

                   Thanks 
                   Dawn B.Reaks
