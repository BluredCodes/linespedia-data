The Sifting Sands



                   Jace left without a trace
                   not even a face
                   would ever recall him

                   Wayne left on a train
                   he loved so but in vain
                   now resolved but insane
                   he wants only to find her

                   Will searched on a hill
                   lived for the thrill
                   but ended up a kill
                   for the winter chill
                   that easily found him

                   I knew a man half alone
                   sifting sand splitting stone
                   now lost in a quagmire
                   of bleaching bone
                   written on a marker was
                   you can never go home
                   (but who would want to anyway)

                   Round the bend and broken hearted
                   I wish these things had never started
                   or from the past I'd never parted
                   I'm like a cold stiff body
                   thats been carted
                   to someplace it knows not where
                   but doesn't want to be
