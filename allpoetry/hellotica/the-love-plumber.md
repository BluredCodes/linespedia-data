The Love Plumber:

         I'm the love plumber
         I'm a roto router pro!
         I can unblock all your passages
         and make your passions start to flow

         I'm the love plumber
         I like to work long and late
         I can squeeze into every nook and cranny
         till all my pumping plunger skills abate

         Yes I am your love plumber!
         Oh I long to bolt and screw
         but flushing out your system
         is what I really love to do!

         So I'll let you handle my faucet
         if you'll let me snake your drain
         and I'll keep recharging your tank
         until these flood waters start to wane!
