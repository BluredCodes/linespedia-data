Day to Day




                   For  girls  and good times
                   we impatiently waited
                   age advanced, dreams evaporated

                   but theres something always
                   pushing us on
                   years of youth suddenly gone

                   The lucky enjoyed themselves
                   had it all
                   others knew misery
                   and took its fall

                   It all works out
                   so they say
                   but what it meant
                   it never did display

                   just day to day

                   and then day to day

                   only day to day
