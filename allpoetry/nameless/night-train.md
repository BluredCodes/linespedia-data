---
title: "Night Train"
writer: "Nameless"
slug: "night-train"
source: "AllPoetry"

How can you find love?
Aboard the night train.
Everyone is gone at the end of the day.
But you keep riding.
In search of love.
But I'm right here at the train station.
Waiting for you.
But you dont come home.
Aboard the night train rolling down that lonely road.
Out the window.
I see the darkness.
And that game of life from which we come.
But you keep running.
Running high.
Headed for a better place that you'll never find.
You want love? 
I'll make you cum.
I give you all of me but it's never enough.
Waiting for you.
I stand alone.
Wondering how to stop this crazy train.
How do you stop this train?
