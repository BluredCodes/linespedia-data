Hello

Good morning, hurt me again
Before the day begins

So I may not see the joy
Of the first few minutes

I can bask in the misery
For yet another day

And set the stones
That pave my way

And if my answer to god
Is 16 guage wood and steel

Then maybe the world
Would know how I feel

But the simple question
I ask you all

Is how hard, oh how far
You will let me fall
