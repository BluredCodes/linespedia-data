title: "Digital Man"
writer: "Nameless"

Energize me.

Can you see something that I couldn't see?
Can you make me feel something that I need?
Can you take me somewhere I haven't been?
Leave behind that state of mind... virtual reality

A digital man will take command.
As technology sweeps this land.
As I have lived, I will die.
Starting over, atomized.

You're telecommunications are breaking down
repeat
You're telecommunications are breaking down

Teleport, then reappear.
I'll burn a hole through the atmosphere.
Ride the radio waves, digitized,
Feeble eyes, realize.
You create an ocean of electricity.
I dwell upon an island,
Exist on ecstasy dreams.
Make a world of all my own,
Save it on my pc.

See no blood then open my eyes.
Execute once electrons fly as..
I am increasing my broadcasting plans.
If I am the one to become digital man.

I am - digital man

You have given me all of your energy.
I am recieving your command.
I thought you had vanquished me,
But still I am.
