---
title: "Digitluv"
writer: "Nameless"
slug: "digitluv"
source: "AllPoetry"

I'd give my soul for just one moment.
A chance to touch you, just to pull you close.
Far away through a screen I'm speaking.
You've got to hear my voice - Digital.
I dream you're real when I close my eyes.
We walk along as our electrons fly.
You tap the keys to say how you're feeling.
I know you're lost, enter the words to heal it.
