title: "CentreStage"
writer: "Nameless"

I picture my life
With paint upon my face
They have written upon my back
It's been left there for all to wonder why

Oh why
I try as I try
I can't always hide
Yes, please let me die.

Do you see me
Like I do? 
Would you believe it
If it was the truth? 
Deny me what I strive for
Blind to why I am I.

I hate you
You hate me
That's the truth of it
To escape you
I run to solitude
Please I beg you
Please let me be
