I dont know why I try
It falls to pieces everytime
You act like I'm not alive
I have no feelings left inside

You're voice leaves meaning
I sense your lies
And still you run
And spread you're wings to fly

I'm alone here
Left alone here

Noone hears my pain
I can't take it again
And the question remains
Is it to end this way

Is it to end this way
Is it to end this way
Is it to end this way
This way
This way

There's nothing like a suicide is riding shotgun
I want to be free of this pain this insanity
I lost my mind, not realise in just whats happening
I've lost a dream, you'll lose a me, please God just let me.. 

When all alone, I see my friends, but I can't make it
When all depends on you, I lie alone
I just can't take it, walk out on me
I'm left alone, all alone, I can't believe it
I'm left alone, all alone, I just can't take it.

Noone hears my pain
I can't take it again
And the question remains
Is it to end this way

Is it to end this way....
