Weve known eachother all of our lives
weve been there for eachother too
then one day the worst thing happened
a kid brought a gun to our school
he pointed the gun straight towards my friend
and then he pulled the trigger
i ran to the floor where she lay there shot
and i looked her in her eyes
and i told her "no u cant leave now"
"no , no u cannot die"
she looked back into my eyes and she said
"ill miss the fun we had"
it was like a  movie playing back in my head
of the fun things that we liked to do
and in seconds my best friend
my best friend she was dead
and there was nothing that i could do.
