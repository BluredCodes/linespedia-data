Every time i look at you
I know that i can see
everything that's missing
deep inside of me

I miss the times you said to me
"I love you, i always will"
I'll miss the days we spent together
And the way you made me feel

But i know that ill never feel that way 
For you or no one else
I know that i cant seem my love
givin to you of any one else

You said you'd always care for me
and never break my heart
but i see my heart is broken into three
and i know that u have lied

I tried to say he's not hurting me
He's just so mean and unkind
I told myself there's other guys
But i couldn't get him off of my mind

so i told him i still cared for him
ad he said he felt that too
then why am i still feeling 
unhappy and mad and blue?

I think that's just the way
My life turns out to be
then when i start to love some1
they end up hurting me
