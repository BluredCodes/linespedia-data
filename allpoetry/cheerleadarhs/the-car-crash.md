title: "The Car Crash"
writer: "cheerleadarhs"

I walked outside 
feeling brood and fret
i sat on the bench 
where we first met
i said all the things
you said to me
i could feel all the tears
run down to my knees
i took in real hard
and tried not to breath
i let out some air
and felt a cold breeze
I looked to the ground
and felt all alone
i shouldn't have let you
drive home all alone
with drugs in your way 
and beer in your car
you crashed near my house
it wasn't that far
when i heard the loud crash
and the knock on my door
my mouth couldn't talk
i fell to the floor
when i finally got up 
and ran where u lay
i couldn't look at you
i didn't know what to say
i walked to your body 
masked with my tears
i looked in your eyes
it was my biggest fear
to have my best friend
laying in front of me
and nothing i could do 
could bring her back to me
i looked all around
and saw that your car
had smashed into a tree
yet here you are
with blood all around 
your hairs all matted
and your wallet on the ground
what were you thinking
how could u do something so dumb
i wanted to yell
but my body went numb
i heard the police man
in back of her car
telling her parents 
of what he had saw
they said they did
all that they could
and if there was more
they were sure that they would
As i heard the bad news
i turned to my friend
and looked at her eyes
i would have rather been dead
she leaned into me
and whispered these words
"i love you girl
i always will"
with those simple 6 words
i grabbed her warm hands
and felt her last breaths
and the grip of her hand
but then all of sudden
the grip wasn't there
i hugged her so close
and stroked her brown hair
the tears came down 
like they just would'nt end
i placed her back down 
and said "your my best friend"
i took of my coat
i placed it on her
and cleared up my throat
i told her i loved her
this wouldn't have happened
if i told you not to go
if i said don't leave now
there's no need for you to go
but no i was mad
and told you to leave
and look what happened
you died in front of me
so as I walked outside 
feeling brood and fret
i sat on the bench 
where we first met
i said all the things
you said to me
i could feel all the tears
run down to my knees
i took in real hard
and tried not to breath
i let out some air
and felt a cold breeze
and wondered will i ever 
see you again
will i ever hear or see
my only best friend
