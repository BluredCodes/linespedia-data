---
title: "New School"
writer: "seashore"

Scary,gloomy,
Creepy,weepy,
Happy,clappy,
On a bright new day,
Opening doors, following a trail - you dont know were it could lead,
Looming around looking for friends.


(Be nice, I'm only nine!)
