HYSTREIA RAGES FREELY
WITHIN A TORTURED MIND
DEVIATION, THE REASON 
FOR ABANDONMENT.
NEVER ONE TO STICK IT OUT,
YOU'RE STILL SEARCHING FOR AN ANSWER.
COWARDICE SURFACES ITS UGLY SELF
KNOWING HARD TIMES AHEAD.
QUIT - YOU CHIKEN HEART!
NO - STAY AND BE BRAVE
CAN'T FACE SENSAI NOW.
NEED HELP, NEED REMEDY
BEFORE IT GETS OUT OF HAND.
FIND SOMEONE STRONG TO PULL YOU THROUGH.
SEE SENSE, SEE SANITY,
SEE THAT TRUTH WINS
IN THE END.
