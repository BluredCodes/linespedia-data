You stood by me when I was feeling low
You were there when I had nowhere to go
You made me smile when I wanted to cry
You helped my pain go rushing by.

Selflessly you've helped me through these days
Enabled me to see the sun's cheerful rays
You've given me life just like the sun
Thankyou so much - don't change for anyone.for mils - thanks for pulling me thru!
