---
title: "Man Made"
writer: "melmac"
slug: "man-made"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Stark across the heavenly skies
Virtuous in existence
Surrounding us every day.
Intertwined with Mother Nature
Like a feather tickling His toes.
Earth stacked high - 
Grand and bold
Intrusive yet majestic.
Piercing steel lays about
A busy operating buzz.
Billowing smoke, clouds of steam
Beautiful faces with no cares in this world.
Glass flashes a beaming smile -
Tar gratefully accepts the warmth.
Hundreds of eyes wink on and off
Roundabouts and roadblocks 
On the busy highway of life.
All this grandeur and splendour
Created to satisfy
Humankind.
