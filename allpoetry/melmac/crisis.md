Where am I, who am I
What am I doing?
I've been here, I've come back
Where am I going?
She's started, she's stopped
I'm stranded - but why?
We've talked, I've asked
But where are the answers?
I'm young, soon to be old
When will everything fall into place?
We keep secrets, we share thoughts
What does it all mean?
We can wonder and ask
But why don't we get far?
Because there is noone in this world
Who can give us
AnswersThings have fallen into place for me, now...
