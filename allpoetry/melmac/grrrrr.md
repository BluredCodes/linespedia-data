-they say vanilla calms the nerves-

Shut the fuck up.
All you care about are
Your loud, rude selves.
Go home all you conceited 
Assholes.
How many damned people do
You want to hear you?
Too many self - centred friends
Doesn't make you a better person.

-why aren't I calm yet-

Raucous laughter
Echoes through my nightmares.
Slurred, fuzzy speech - 
Isn't 'consideration' 
In your vocabulary?
Go back
To where you came from 
If you love it so much.
So now just shut up 
And leave me alone.
Self-righteous people
Don't get a second thought
In my lifetime
