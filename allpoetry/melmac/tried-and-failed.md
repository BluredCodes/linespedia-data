---
title: "tried and failed"
writer: "melmac"
slug: "tried-and-failed"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

HURTFUL,VENOMOUS WORDS
ESCAPE THE MOUTH OF THE ENEMY
RIPPING POSITIVITY FROM
A WEAKENED SOUL - 
THE SOUL OF A CHILD
HOPING, SEEKING FOR 
AN ALTERNATIVE

TENSION, TORMENT, THREAT
GO BACK TO THE ROOTS.
UNMISTAKABLE SYMPTOMS - 
FELO DE SE.

FOREIGN, TASTELESS OBJECTS
IN THE MOUTH OF A CHILD
WAHED TO GREATER DEPTHS
BY A SEA OF WARM, SWEET TEARS.
INGRATIATING ITSELF TO GOD
THE CHILD'S EYES CLOSE - 
UNAWARE OF APPROACHING ANXIETY
TO KEEP ALERT A MIND FAR GONE

UNSTOPPABLE SHEETS OF SALTY WATER
CUTTING THEIR WAY ACROSS
FLESHY PINK HILLS
A HEART RACING
SEEKING SOLACE
IS CALMED BY A PEACEFUL VOICE
REMINDING IT THAT SOON ALL PAIN
WILL DISAPPEAR

THE VICTIM OF
UNSURPASSED TURMOIL 
AND MALEVOLANCE
WITH THE RISING SUN
WILL BE FOUND AT PEACE.
