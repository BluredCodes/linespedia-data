---
title: "For a Lifetime"
writer: "melmac"

source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/45082-For-a-Lifetime-by-melmac"

My angel watches over me
Whether he be far or near
And in my heart I love him so
There's no need for fear-
For fear is weak
And our love so strong
It is certain
