title: "Away"

Shall we fly far away-
Away from the hustle and bustle
Of everything we're conditioned to be-
From misconceptions
From lives shed
Strewn far and wide...

Do you want to walk a rainbow
To find our pot of gold?
Or take a ride on a shooting star
To find our jungle paradise?
We could visit the man in the moon
And discover the secrets of the earth.
Ruby red slippers glittering hope,
Seeds sewn to form new lives-
New, beautiful, smiling lives.
We'll discover the most magnificent places
Yet undiscovered.
Then to lay back in the arms
Of someone you love.
Madness, love, freedom, happiness, beauty
Feelings unchecked, 
No right
Or wrong...

Let's grow wings to fly away
Away from hardened views
And narrow ways
To live for ourselves
In our own
Beautiful Existence.
