---
title: "Perplexed"
writer: "melmac"
slug: "perplexed"

A young soul,
A restless heart - 
Feelings so unrefined.
An expectation of life's fruition
Unbroken in your mind.

Far away places 
And exotic desires
Call to you from beyond.
Perched on a ledge
Don't know where to go
Scared to unfasten a bond.

Tethered to tedium
By harsh, greedy strings
Of this hungry land,
Yet drawn to the dream
Of an unspoken oath - 
Hungry for the freedom to stand.

A displeased crowd
Might stand in your way,
Don't let them brandish a knife.
The real pressure on you
Is to make it come true
And love You for the rest of your life.
