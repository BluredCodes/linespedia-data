You lay upon my bed
Your complexion a golden hue.
While you breathe contnentedly
My eyes watch over you.
This is peace and serenity
As I lay by your side
Hearing your sweet breathing
I know it's to you that I'm tied.
My heart is so happy, so full of joy
From my face beams a radiant glow
And as has been said many times before
I cherish you more than you know.
So sleep, my darling, sleep
And dream sweet dreams of night
While I lay down with my dreams of you
Knowing that we're just right.
