---
title: "Futile"
writer: "melmac"

You were born - 
You brought joy.
You once cried - 
You then smiled.
You showed love - 
You were loved.
You shared your life - 
You were admired.
You had fun - 
You made fun times.
You were a great friend - 
You lit up my life.
You were here - 
Now you're gone.
