title: "A Path For You..."

A whirlpool of indistinguishable thoughts
Heckling a precious soul
Realities of absent relationships
Beginning to take their toll.
See through the hardships
The torment and angst - 
To look past these visions
You must grant yourself thanks.
Now follow the meandering path
In whichever direction it leads
Then let your quest for immortality
Be the only thing you need.
Stand with your friends to guide you - 
Strong with your head held high
Discard any negativity
And look toward the sky.
Be guided by your heart
And let your emotions flow - 
When your soul is at peace within
You'll be the first to know.
