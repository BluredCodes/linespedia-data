Precious memories of you
Are buried deep within
Your bright blue eyes, 
Your cheeky grin

You were taken away
To a special place
Somewhere for you
To be given your space

Where have you gone - 
To a place in the sky?
Or a place in your lifetime 
Where the eagle did fly

You showed me your palm
And told me you'd go
You knew it was coming
How did you know?

You told me to smile - 
to always be glad
And that life is too short 
To always be sad.

So maybe one day
I'll meet you out there
With a bottle of rum
And a dye for your hair.

Until that day, though
I'll cherish your time
Remember me fondly
And let your happiness shine.
