You pulled me back from the dark dreary days

And gave me light

You helped me 

Even though I tried to fight



You helped me up when I fell 

Your eyes told me you loved me

I could tell

Even though I wanted to be free



You gave me courage to step up to my fears

You were with me all the while

And wiped my tears

You made me smile



You carried me when I fell sleep

And showed me the way

Your heart I keep

This very day



You pulled me from the end

And gave me freedom

For that you are a friend
