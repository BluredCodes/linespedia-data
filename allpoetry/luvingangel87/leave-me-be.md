Leave me be Demon Slayer
you gave me protection for only a second
Leave me be tender boy
you gave me comfort for only a minute
Leave me be optimistic talker
you gave me happiness for only an hour
Leave me be beautiful singer
you gave me music for only a year
Leave me be gift giver
you gave me a life
Take it back
I choose my mistake
