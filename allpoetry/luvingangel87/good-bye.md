---
title: "Good Bye"
writer: "LuvingAngel87"
slug: "good-bye"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

look into my eyes
hear my cries

why was this done?
he's still too young

sucked from his prime
it was a serious crime

his body sucked down below
and among the stars his spirit may glow

I can still look up at night
and see his light burning bright

Although I might not look to go on
for his life is now presumed gone

I'll remember the days
we spent in good ways

frolicking about
may we not have a doubt

that they are full of joy
forever our little boy

we'll miss him so
and now we must woe

the loss of a friend
but it's not the end
