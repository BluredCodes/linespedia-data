The lights flashed
The specks of dust fluttered through the stale air
The eyes watched anxiously
My breath hastened
It's mist rising before my eyes
My hands trembled
In my own mother's hands

Then she let go
And I made my way out
Alone and frightened
Out of place
But this is where I practiced
Though it doesn't feel the same
It's the eyes
They frighten me

The music starts
It flows through me
It carries me through
And gives me confidence
Each jump they gasp
Each turn they sigh
They are now in love
With my soft movements

I no longer feel their eyes
I no longer an alone
I no longer feel afriad
This is where I'm supposed to be

The music stops
I feel empty
This is my home
Where I belong

On the Ice...
