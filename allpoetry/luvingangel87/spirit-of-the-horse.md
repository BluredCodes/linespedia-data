title: "Spirit of the Horse"

The hooves beat fast
wavering past
my heart skips a beat
as I nearly lose my feet
eyes wide
as they flied
on the ground
nimble and sound
eyes a fire
heavily they perspire
long leg stretched
a minute caught
neck arched fine
never out of line
ebony coat
dark as smoke
long flowing mane
wisping through the reing
tail wavy and long
like a beautiful song
when in your seat
I feel your beat
I can sense your power
but gentle as a flower
you know I'm true
and same to you
you run with me
and we pretend to be free

you are never lost...
you are never found...
you are never tame...
you are never wild....

...the spirit of the horse....

An ode to Beau, may you forever run free
