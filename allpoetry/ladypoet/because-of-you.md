---
title: "Because of You"
writer: "LadyPoet"

No sunset will be as beautiful 
as the ones I watched with you 
Nor the walks along the beach 
listening to a seagull or two 

I will never sit in candlelight 
without thinking of your smile 
Or feel the heat of passion 
if only for a little while 

Because of you I can appreciate 
the rising dawn of each new day 
A tender kiss, a soft touch 
clever words you use to say 

The grass seems so much greener 
trees taller as they gently sway 
The little things most don't see 
as they scurry along on their way 

You've shown me my inner strength 
and how dreams really do come true 
I surely would have missed all this 
had I not shared my heart with you
