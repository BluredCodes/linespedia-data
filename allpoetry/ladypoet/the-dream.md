title: "The Dream"

In a meadow of dancing wild flowers 
she laid basking in warm midday sun 
Dreaming of kings and their castles 
and if her prince charming would come 

She envisioned him atop a black stallion 
as sounds of thunder echoed each stride 
The crest of the king he wore proudly 
a jeweled sword safely kept at his side 

Her intuition warned of great peril 
the battle was now close at hand 
In a glimmer she sensed his life fading 
as he died to protect the kings land 

Trembling she knelt down beside him 
bewildered to how this could be 
She was dreaming of a life everlasting 
in a castle she was yearning to see 

A touch and at once she awakened 
her eyes quickly adjusting to light 
Surrounded by dancing wild flowers 
and a black stallion fading from sight.
