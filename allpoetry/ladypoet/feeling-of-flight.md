At the end of a dingy hallway 
in a corner just out of sight 
She sits with her flame and needle 
waiting for that feeling of flight 

The world outside flows past her 
a life she chose to leave behind 
In her existence there are no others 
Only the demons occupying her mind 

Her clothes are ragged and dirty 
red hair hanging in matted clumps 
Her face thin from lack of nourishment 
Scavenging for scraps at city dumps 

Fear and desperation her companions 
As daylight surrenders to night 
She scrambles back to the hallway 
Again seeking that euphoria of flight
