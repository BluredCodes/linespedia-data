title: "I will kiss you there"
writer: "scenarist"

I will kiss you there! 
as you swell, and where 
I can feel your heated body quiver 
the place where only my tongue can deliver 

you will see my red heated lipstick kiss 
from below and above your belly and will not miss 
the view, the touch, the sound, the taste 
up from your toes, to your sweaty face 

I will make you moan 
and in between your groans 
you'll excitedly guide my passion 
and beg me not to give you rations 

I will fulfil your wildest dreams 
complete your fantasy and make you scream 
make you cross your eyes and toes 
make you smile, and watch you spread your nose 

I will hold you as you sleep 
love you and my promise keep 
to always be the one for you 
faithfully devoted--undeniably TRUE
