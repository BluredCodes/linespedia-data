IF TIME WAS OF NO ESSCENCE 
AND if "IF" COULD RESOLVE ALL PRESENCE 
WOULDN'T IT BE WONDERFUL "IF' WE 
I'M TALKIN ABOUT YOU AND ME 
COULD BE (((REAL))) 

WITH ONE ANOTHER--YOU KNOW EACHOTHER 
AND WE'D NEED NOT HAVE TO HIDE OUR FACE 
I MEAN YOU AND I WOULD KNOW OUR PLACE 
AND WE'D HAVE NO NEED OF TIME, OR SPACE 

"IF" 

VULNERABILITY WAS ACCEPTED AS STRENGTH 
AND WE MEASURED NOT WEAKNESS 
AS WE MEASURE YARDS,OR LENGTHS 
AND WHAT I SAID WAS SAID NOT TO HURT 
AND WHAT YOU SAID WAS NOT HARSH OR CURT 

AND "IF" 

I COULD SAY "I LOVE YOU" WITHOUT ANY CONDITIONS 
AND MEAN IT 
AND YOU COULD SAY THE SAME WITHOUT CONTRITION 
AND JUST BE IT (LOVE) 
AND I COULD BE THERE FOR YOU WHEN YOU NEED ME 
AND YOU WOULD KNOW WHAT'S UP--CAUSE 
YOU COULD READ ME 
(LIKE A BOOK) 

AND "IF' TIME WERE OF NO ESSCENSE 
AND if "IF" COULD RESOLVE ALL PRESCENCE 
WOULDN'T IT BE WONDEFUL "IF" WE 
I'M TALKIN ABOUT YOU AND ME 
COULD BE REAL?
