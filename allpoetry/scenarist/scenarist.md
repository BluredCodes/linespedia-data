LIKE A CORTESAN 
I SPREAD MY SELF THIN 
UPON THE COLD WHITE PAPER 
WAITING TO BE CONSUMED, 
RAVISHED,EMPTIED,SOLD OUT 
NAKED, AND VULNERABLE 
TO THE LUSTFUL EYES OF MY BUYER 
CALCULATING THE SUM 
I WILL OBTAIN 
AT ANY COST 
WHIPING LONG, SEDUCTIVE, ENTICING PROSE 
WITH MY PEN 
WITH THOUGHT AND SKILFULLY SWEET ABANDON 
TEASING, AND TEMPTING THE VOYUERS MIND 
AROUSING THEIR INTEREST 
AND 
PROVOKING THEIR EMOTION
