Because you don't hear what you want me to say 
Don't judge me and let your thoughts run away 
With how cruel or decieptful I am and ruin our day 
there is a reason I am quiet and that is okay 

Because I don't respond to things you think I should 
May be I desire to react and would if I could 
My feelings are very tender and not hard as wood 
But I am afraid, and uncertain, BUT IT IS ALL GOOD! 

Because you are confident,self sufficient, and sure 
and I hide in the shadows and am intrigued by your lure, 
Don't let my reservations upset you--I know I'm demur 
Just continue to be yourself, and I'll grow--YOUR my cure 

Because I cry easily and am moved by the slightest breeze 
And am highly affectionate you think I'm a tease 
My commitments are strong this should put you at ease 
My Love for you is strong and with you I am very pleased!!!
