Oh what a perfect gift you gave 
Your WORD –FAITHFUL THAT PROMISED 
Of eternal life you made 
To me and all of us like doubting Thomas 

Your voice you wrapped in sinless flesh 
To dwell among your chosen 
And saw HIM accomplish every test 
Faith through Action spoken 

YOUR LOVE, YOUR LIFE, YOUR FAVOR and GRACE 
YOU give exceeding and abundantly to all that are in need 
ALL you ask in return, is repentance from sin, and Faith 
Responding to even the smallest of faith—that of the mustard seed 

We ceremoniously celebrate this gift calling it Christmas Day 
Yet we fail to Honor YOUR GIFT-- to thank and give YOU praise 
We disregard the purpose of CHRIST birth and go on about OUR selfish, futile ways 
Assuming debts for the pleasure of flesh, when the debt for flesh (OUR GIFT) was so AWESOMELY and selflessly paid.
