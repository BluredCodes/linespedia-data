I WANT TO FEEL YOUR BODY NEXT TO MINE 
ENTWINED 
I WANT TO FEEL YOUR BREATH UPON MY SKIN 
AND THEN 
LISTEN TO IT'S PACE 
AND FEEL 
IT HOT AGAINST MY FACE 
I WANT TO FEEL YOUR DESIRE 
AS YOU SWELL UP--LIKE FIRE 
AND BEGIN TO MOAN 
I'LL GROAN 
WATCHING YOUR PASSION FILLED EXPRESSIONS 
TAKING LESSONS 
FROM YOUR GUIDING HAND 
URGENT AND NEEDING 
YOUR EYES ARE PLEADING 
FEEDING MY NEED 
TO PLEASE 
YOU
