title: "Selfless love"
writer: "scenarist"

tangibly visible, open, nude,--VULNERABLE 
no hiding of feelings or fears are you able 
to allow yourself the freedom to be 
(((YOU)))without any concerns or anxiety 

genuine, honest, faithful, and true 
all these things I desire how 'bout you 
unaffected by peoples thoughts or perceptions 
straight from the heart, ALWAYS REAL, no exceptions 

DEFENSELESS,susceptible to the throwing of darts 
IN THE RAW,unprotected even to the breaking of heart 
void of all inhibitions because there is no need to care 
because to hurt or to harm you I just wouldn't and couldn't dare 

all these things i could be just for you 
if allowed the opportunity to prove 
there is no need for deceit or the shelter of internal walls 
because i am certain i can be as constant as NIAGRA FALLS 

i was taught to give love and love you would receive 
unconditional love is in what I truly believe 
if self for love is for a momment denied 
there'd be no reason to hide,or to tell any lies 

give until giving takes your last breath 
give until all you have given is your ultimate best 
give until all the words you can say or confess 
is that LOVE IS MY RESPONSIBILITY AND I'LL EXCEL IN IT'S TEST!!!!
