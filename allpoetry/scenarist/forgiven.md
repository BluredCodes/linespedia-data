Saved by Grace and not ashamed to say 
The wrong I've done and even do today 
Was payed for at a cost I would never pay 
The Sacrificial Blood of Christ redeems in every way 
God told Moses, "I AM All you'll ever need" 
"I will send MY ONLY SON through Abrahams seed" 
"Emanuel" "God with us" if only you'd believe" 
Condemnation befalls the fool whom chooses to be deceived 
"There is no God" a fool says in his heart 
Rejecting "HIS" first and perfect LOVE from the very start 
As a sinner I am extended grace daily, and unto me MERCY GOD departs 
You'd be wise to accept HIS gift of life--really you'd be Smart 
To accept forgiveness for your wicked ways 
Accept redemption from Our Lord and sincerely say 
I Believe HE died and arose on the 3rd day 
Proclaim Jesus as the SON OF GOD and bow your knees and pray 
and ask the ONLY LIVING GOD to keep satan and his demons away 
All that GOD ask of you is to believe JESUS CHRIST IS THE ONLY WAY!!!
