---
title: "WE"
writer: "scenarist"
slug: "we"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/22318-WE-by-scenarist"
---

IF not for me who would it be 
to love,to touch, lead the blind to see 
to encourage,to hug, fill the void that is empty 
if not for me who would it be 

if not you who else will do 
the things that only you can do 
bring a smile to a face and brighten the blue 
if not for you who else will do 

if not for us who else would fuss 
argue the point and do what they must 
to ensure that all was not ludicrous 
if not for us who else would fuss 

it's up to "ME" to take the first step 
to allow "YOU" to help me pay the debt 
for "US" all to gather as one--make a net 
that binds us together as "WE" --let's not fret 

so lets "ALL" make a differance in life 
put away hate,envy and strife 
love one another with all our might 
NOW WOULDN'T THAT BE OUT OF SITE!!!!!
