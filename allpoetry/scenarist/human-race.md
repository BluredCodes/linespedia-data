Can a person be a color, or a race 
Only if we need to differentiate 
When we look at the glory of open fields 
Do we ask or take notice of what it produces or yields 
Or just marvel at the beauty of it all 
watching it's changes summer through fall 
Black is a color that White has none 
Brown is a hue like Yellow is one 
Red is warm and radiant too 
All just colors blended under skies of blue 
You don't look like me, nor do I look like you 
Our differences is what makes us striking to view 
Why can't you accept my individuality as I respect you? 
Can a color be a person, or a race have a face 
Is our difference our Glory or a reason to hate?
