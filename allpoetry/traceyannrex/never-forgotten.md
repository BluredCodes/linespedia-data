title: "Never Forgotten"
writer: "Traceyannrex"

Looking back over the years, I see myself determined and strong.
The decisions I've made in life - some were right, some were wrong.
The one that wasn't wrong was being pregnant with you.
Living with a new life growing within was wonderful and new.

Imagine my shock when the unthinkable came my way.
I went from joy to sorrow in the course of just one day.
I may never know the reason why this happened to me,
But I know that God holds your soul that my body set free.

There is nothing I can do to change the fact, to relive that day - 
to not lose you - and bring you back.
Though we're separated by time and space, we'll meet again one day in that heavenly place.

My healing needs a place to start and I'll begin with my broken heart.
But until we meet again when the timing is just right, 
remember that mommy still loves you with all of her might.
