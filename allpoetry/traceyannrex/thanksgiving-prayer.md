I'm thankful for second chances and rekindled romances with my husband and my Lord,
With two men in my life we're tightly wound like a knot in the three stranded cord.
Thank You for my wonderful friends and for what they do comes from the heart,
My life wouldn't be the same without them if we would ever be apart.

I'm thankful for our children with their souls still pure in spirit,
As the kids sing Your loving praises let everyone around them hear it.
Let these young ones live from example and reap harvest from the seed,
Because after it's all said and done it's the children who will lead.

I'm thankful for my job that Lord provided for me,
The money for bills, the money for God to accomplish His Perfect Will.
I thank You Lord for my home as I get in my warm toasty bed,
I fall asleep thankful that I have a place to lay my  head.

I'm thankful when I make mistakes for You teach me better ways,
I give You thanks Dear Lord cause You are worthy of my praise.
So thank You Father for what You've done so far in my life,
Keep me close to You to be a better mother and a better wife.
