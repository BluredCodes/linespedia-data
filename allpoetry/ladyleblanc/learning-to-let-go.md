I'm letting go,
 
       or trying to,
   
            attempting to forget you.
But you've weeded deep
   
     into my soul
       
           torn into my heart.
  Now the memory will not die,
 
      while I die each day around you,
        
            without you,

                denied you,   my oxygen.

 You've won,
    
       beaten me,

           left me behind,

                moved on,   not looking back.

  I'm stranded, abandoned,

       and stuck,
 
            and desperately

         learning to let go.
