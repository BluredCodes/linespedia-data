title: "Forever, My Dreamer"

Rest sweetly, my Dreamer
Evade this dark world
and welcome the light
you see within sleep.
Sleep deeply, my Dreamer
Replenish your soul
with pictures and thoughts
not real in this world.
Forever, my Dreamer
come, be with me
and leave this dark world
on the light of my wings.This is for the comfort of those of us who have lost our loved ones before we were ready to say goodbye.
