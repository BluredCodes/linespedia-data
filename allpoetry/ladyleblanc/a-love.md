A love
so strong
no man could break it.

A love
so deep
one Man could take it.

A soul
so black
but cleansed to whiteness.

A soul
so white
it shone with brightness.

One God
One Man
one sacrifice.

For me
for you
for eternal life.

A love 
so strong
no man could break it.

A love
so deep
