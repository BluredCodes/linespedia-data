What love has he
to break my chains.
A brave man could 
only do it.
For once I was
in bondage of the heart.
He had no fears.
No regrets.
No second thoughts.
I had rejected
so many before him.
Men feared my name,
but he loved me
Always was he
persistent,kind,
caring, ever-loving.
He came to me.
He melted this 
cold heart of mine
with a single 
gentle caress.
Ah, what wonders 
can a sweet,
warm kiss do.My inspiration for this was the relationship between my father and step-mother. She told me about how mean she was to him, before she discovered that he is the most perfect man on this earth. :-}
