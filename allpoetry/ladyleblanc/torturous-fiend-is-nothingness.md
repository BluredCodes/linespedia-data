Torturous fiend is nothingness!
A drought has fallen inside me!
The light is dim,
I fear I've lost the way.
Inspiration is nearly extinct!

Muses cascade upon me!
Give me love, or forbidden hate!
Or fear, or acts
Of selflessness to fill the great abyss.
Torturous fiend is nothingness!Having troubles...
