The autumn breeze
lifted gently my hair.
Raindrops fell
and mingled with my tears.
Not long ago
this is where you left.
I must stay
and remember you forever.
It grows late, 
the sun has set for us,
and I live
in twilight eternal.
Do you weep
as I must?
Would it be
better if you returned?
Please, come back
and we'll write a different ending.
Alas, you won't
and all I have is autumn rain.2000, I know it needs some work. I have tried for a long time to make this one better, but I can't seem to think of anything. Any suggestions?
