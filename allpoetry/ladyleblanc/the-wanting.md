title: "The Wanting"

You see them every day
the lost, the hopeless, the lonely.
But you do not stop 
to offer a kindly gesture.
You walk with them through life
the desperate, the needy, the suicidal.
But you do not stop
to encourage them to go on.
You think that there is nothing you can do
for these wanting people.
But you can stop
and become a friend,
a voice,
a hand to hold.
You see them every day
the lost, the hopeless, the lonely,
the desperate and forlorn.
But you do not stop,
and they do.
