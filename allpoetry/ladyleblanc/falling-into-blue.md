---
title: "Falling into Blue"
writer: "LadyLeBlanc"
slug: "falling-into-blue"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/15198-Falling-into-Blue-by-LadyLeBlanc"
---

He comes during the night,
to kiss away the pain,
sending gentle, grasping fingers
massaging at my brain.

A thousand grains of sand,
each purer than the last,
pouring down my eyes and throat
in a single, silent blast.

I'm falling, flying, fleeing,
below, above, behind.
That is the time he enters in,
at least, into my mind.

He brings me to a world of dreams,
a place that cannot be.
And yet, this world does exist,
and it's this that sets me free.

We're flying over land and sea
to the somewhere he calls home,
but suddenly I'm rising
back to the home I call my own.

Waking to a strange, same world
where true dreams can't come true,
waiting ever vigilantly
to fall back into Blue.
