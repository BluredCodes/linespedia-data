She stares at him
with dim unseeing eyes.
She loves him still
and cries as he cries.

This cruel old world
will tear them both apart.
This consuming cancer
has crept into her heart.

Where are the fates
that brought them both together?
Where is the cure
for this girl, delicate as a feather?

Love is the one thing
that binds her to this room.
Love is the thing
that makes him want it over soon.

Her breath grows short
he holds her hand too tightly.
Her spirit leaves
he weeps fiercely, quietly.

He reaches out,
she is beautiful in death.
He breaks his heart
and draws a final breath.Is the ryhming alright?
