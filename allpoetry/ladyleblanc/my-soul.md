This overwhelming numbness,
this coldness of the soul,
something passes through my heart
and in leaving leaves a hole.
The wound is very painful,
and doesn't seem to close.
My tears form a silent stream
which flows from eyes to nose.
I put on a fooling act,
pretend that naught is wrong,
and yet within my heart
I sing a mourning song.
I feel so lost and lonely,
like I'm losing control.
But I've heard of an everlasting Father,
who will love and save my soul.2000,
