Did you see the sun set today?
Sweet poetry in itself;
the sky sang 
in fiery hues of golden pink.

He painted it for my inspiration,
one of His masterpieces on a fading blue canvas,
but I have not sufficiency to begin its description.

I watched in awe, and slight disappointment,
as the great glowing orb
melted into the horizon
in a final explosion of gold.I saw a most amazing sunset on my way home from my grandparents house. It was the last I saw for the entire year, and it was INCREDIBLE. So, I decided that perhaps it deserved a memoir!
