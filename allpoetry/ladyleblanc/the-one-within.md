As the world turns around you,
  you are forced to sit back
     and watch.
Your friends are leading their own lives,
   filled with their own joys,
     and seem not to care.
There is a void, it seems, within you
   that has been filled with 
      empty,  aching, loneliness.
Though the sun is shining,
   you do not feel its glory 
      upon your face.
But take heart, my friend, search deep inside
   and you will know someone awaits you
     who can't be described with words.2000, This is all mine! I wrote when I was feeling "oh so very alone", but then I remembered..........
