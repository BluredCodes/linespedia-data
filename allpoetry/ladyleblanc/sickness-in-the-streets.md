Whither will I wander 
In these dank and dreary streets? 
For I'm a creature of the night 
And the alley is where life leads. 

Sell your body to the darkness 
Catch the going rate 
In my world of last resorts 
where corruption is your fate. 

This life is bathed in shadows 
Much darker than the night, 
An ever-spinning whirl pool 
Of shame, and cold, and fright. 

Don't pity me for my choices. 
Set your troubled mind at ease. 
The streets are full of sickness
