She's flirting with Death,
and cheating on Life.
She walks on the edge,
plays catch with a knife.

Infatuation turned obsession,
her blood flows hot and red.
Seduced by chilling darkness,
she puts a noose above her head.

Life tries to win her back,
but Death has caught her heart.
She takes his hand, jumps off the cliff,
and now her marriage starts.Ok, I wrote this as a companion poem for a story that a friend of mine wrote. I'm not a dark and scary person obsessed w/ death and dying. ;} so here ya go Angela, I hope you can use the poem!
