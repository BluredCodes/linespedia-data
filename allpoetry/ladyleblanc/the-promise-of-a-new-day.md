A well, too deep,
I now call home
Desperation
has trapped me here.

All dark, no light,
no silvery warmth
Just
Desperation, black and cold.

I scratch the walls,
I fall back down.
It
seems I can't escape.

But, here! The sun 
is peering through
Perhaps
the dawn of a new day.
