I'm waiting for the day
when the sun will join the sea
For though I cannot stay,
you said you'd marry me.
How I'd much rather be
still safe by your side.
The morn I'd rather see,
is the one when you're my bride.
Our country is in trouble dear,
and dark is the night.
The time is drawing near
when I must go and fight.
Hon, please know I love you,
and I'll be home soon.
Whenever you get lonely,
look up at the moon.
All answers must be found,
and all victories won.
If I don't quickly come around
then you'll know it's done.
But whenever you get lonely,
look at the moon and see
my face looking down at you.....
