The shadows 
in a room
full of darkness
consume me.
They wait
in anticipation
of the prey
they desire.
I enter
and am struck
by the din
of silence.
My ears
ring in fear,
my back tingles
with tension.
The wraiths
send out tendrils
to capture and
bind me,
to bend and conform me
to the will of the dark.This is a piece of junk!!!!
