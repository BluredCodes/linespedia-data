Dearest baby brother,

    I am off at college now,
    and you're only two years old.
    Still, I love you very much,
    I hope that you've been told.

    I miss you all tremendously,
    as maybe you can tell.
    Give Dina a hug for me,
    and your mom and dad as well.

    I'll be home for Christmas 
    and maybe New Year's too,
    You want me there for Spring Break?!
    Well...I'll see what I can do... ;-P

    Are you growing up too fast?
    Are you getting to be tall?
    Have you learned your letters yet?
    When you do, give me a call!  (You can do that anyways though)

    I'll bring this to a close,
    don't want to keep you up too late.
    I'll call you up this weekend.
    (Unless I have a date! Hehe!)

    Sweet dreams, my baby brother,
    don't let the bed bugs bite!
    I'm thinking of and loving you,
    and say a prayer for you each night.

                                 Your big sister,
                                   ~*Kaleigh*~
