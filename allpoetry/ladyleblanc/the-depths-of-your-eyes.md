Looking deep into your eyes
I see you have no soul.
The depths of your pupils continue receding
into a bottomless hole.

Emotions buried deep inside
slowly consumed your heart.
It is a fatal disease you have,
which must be stopped at its start.

Anger and hatred are all that you know,
your true colors rise.
I should have seen it long ago
looking deep into your eyes.2000,
