In lonely darkness here I lie,
and wait for you to hear my cry.

My mind, it aches in loneliness,
my body yearns for your caress.

Would you prefer it you that I seduced.
passions then, in all, produced?

My lips, they are in ecstasy
when captured by yours utterly.

Lioness ever will I be,
until your dearest face I see.

Mine heart I freely give to thee,
him that first would have tamed me.I wrote this when I got bored in English!
