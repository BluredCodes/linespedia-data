Clear as glass,
deep as forever,
Come inside
before your welcome is over.

Fragile as porcelain,
it breaks on the floor.
All that is left
are remains of the door.

Hopeful, then broken,
then sewn back together,
my porcelain heart
can be cracked by a feather.
