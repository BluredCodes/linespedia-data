Your simplest touch 
   fills my body with fire!
A graze on the shoulder
   sends jumping, dancing,
       writhing flames
Living fire! through my veins,
   freezing my heart for split seconds of eternity.
I live for the fire that you ignite in my soul...Challenge Description: write a poem about fire, no more than eight lines, good luck!:)
