---
title: "All of Me?"
writer: "LadyLeBlanc"
slug: "all-of-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

The Desire washes over me
fills my mind
day and night.
It is so very strong
that it lays a thick blanket
of sticky sweetness upon my tongue.
Am I unholy
to Desire so much?
To want so desperately
to be more than what I am right now?
There is so much
to be done,
differences to be made,
and I Desire a part in the drama.
Is this all of me?
These simple words
which will one day fade?
Or, if I Desire hard enough
will I become my words,
and be more than what I am
right now?
