---
title: "Bent"
writer: "Highland Dreams"
slug: "bent"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/27973-Bent-by-Highland-Dreams"
---

when I'm bent to the side
pain rushing through me
and I stare up at you
you stare back at me
not seeing, not feeling
I can't help but laugh
as my tears fall slowly
and I just want to hurt
to hurt more and more
til I can't feel at all
because it pains me
to see you so close
yet I can't touch you
I can't feel you there
I can only hear your voice
mocking my longing
teasing my soul with song
with that bloody voice
and those eyes so pure
I'm afraid of you
so shiny and hard
behind that glass so safe
hiding away in that box
making the world my prison
‘cos I can't get in
I'm dying deep inside
bent to the side again
whilst pain rushes through
