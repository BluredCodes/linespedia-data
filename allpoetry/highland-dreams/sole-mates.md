---
title: "Sole Mates"
writer: "Highland Dreams"
slug: "sole-mates"
source: "AllPoetry"

We were like
An odd pair of shoes
Mismatched and worn
Breaking down the arches
And burning the soles
Looking perfectly well
On the outside
But never really a true fit
Given up to charity
In hopes of finding 
The perfect fit
A true sole mate
