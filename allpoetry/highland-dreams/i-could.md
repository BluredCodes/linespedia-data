---
title: "I Could"
writer: "Highland Dreams"
slug: "i-could"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/37867-I-Could-by-Highland-Dreams"
---

I could do with you right now, 
open the door, find you standing there,
smiling just for me.
It’d be just the best present, no more
thoughts, only reality, do more 
than just dream about you.
To hold you, to feel you,
to taste you,
‘tis the best of dreams.
One day soon, it’ll happen, dreams
can come true. Until then,
waiting here, I am, for you.
