---
title: "Here For You"
writer: "Highland Dreams"
slug: "here-for-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I see you, standing there with those stars in your eyes.
Yet behind those smiling eyes are the pains of loves lost.
So full of hope and dreams. How can I protect you?
I could share the inner most depths of my soul,
Or should I silently slip away and wish you the best?
Will the pain of my past, erase the hopes of your future?
Perhaps, one day we'll find that crystal ball in which to tell our fortunes.
Until then, I'll be here for you whenever you need a shoulder to cry on
Or lend an ear in which to whisper your deepest secrets and desires.
After all, what are friends for?
