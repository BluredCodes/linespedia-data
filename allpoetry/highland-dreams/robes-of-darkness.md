wretched blackened sophistries
eating all the painful cries
tempting sweat and pornography
bleeding out through their eyes
diseased and rotten catalysts
linked with their bodies sex
slick and tainted orifice
with deceitful plans to annex
listen to the moans and calls
watching with those gaudy queens
lovely souls of washed out pain
broken tramps of sordid dreams
swaying in the foul warm winds
the robes of station tattered then
bloody skins of putrid violation
carnal lusts and growing sin
strung out on desirable creation
and all that god's loose vanity
enticing drops of euphoric tension
slick, slipping ropes of insanity
this animal's reign of disorder
lapping poison from the wounds
gray dogs at retribution's hands
greasy salvation comes too soon
enjoying all the wicked sport
twisting as they watch the rapes
masturbating to the droning sound
sensual motion within the shapes
those angels broken by the fall
forward in the line of sensation
such a prostitution of power
brought by horror to elation
languid traces of their ill intent
mouths wet with intense thirst
scan the flesh for scars and burns
draining souls just to be nursed
glassy nipple of hungry seraphim's
leaking venom into the broken maws
attached to all the maliciousness
bloody stitches fester in their jaws
watch with horror from the throne
laughing while his new children feed
this god rests his fattened hands
and watches the slaughtered lambs bleedMy apologies, I'm in a rather dark period at the moment.
