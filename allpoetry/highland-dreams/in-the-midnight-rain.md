---
title: "In The Midnight Rain"
writer: "Highland Dreams"
slug: "in-the-midnight-rain"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/25959-In-The-Midnight-Rain-by-Highland-Dreams"
---

I whisper to you
through the drops 
of the midnight rain,
as I dream of you
finding me in the
dark-
and making my 
world bright again.

I wish for you
under the light 
of the brightest star,
and I know you'll be
able to
hear it-
No matter how far.

I can feel your body
next to me
as I lay 
in the night,
and I can imagine your
lips on mine-
When my eyes are closed tight.

It's morning now,
but my thoughts of you
still remain,
and I can't wait
until we meet again-
In the midnight rain.
