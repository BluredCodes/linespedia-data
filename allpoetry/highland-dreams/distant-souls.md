---
title: "Distant Souls"
writer: "Highland Dreams"

Looking into a distant horizon
Away to that far off place
Awaits  a gentle loving spirit
Whilst sunlight and warmth bathe my face

Peering across a lonely flat plain
There is another soul, wishing the same

Trepidation, gazing, waiting
Knowing time moves it all around
Loving spirit hovering, yearning, waiting
To be delivered from it’s earthly bounds

Another lonely spirit, also struggling to be free
Though many miles separate my love from me

Over cascades of snowy mountains 
Through the thin ethereal air
Across rolling plains he waits
Calling for his maiden fair

Over the hills, past windswept  rivers of desire
Loves compelling passion waits beneath the city spires


In starlight, a valley sleeps in silence
The winter air is crisp and clear
Only a heart sounds through the night
Time to consummate our love draws near

Now, not so many miles between the two
Yes, my love .....now I’ve come for you
