---
title: "Lost"
writer: "Highland Dreams"
slug: "lost"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/28296-Lost-by-Highland-Dreams"
---

Like a lighthouse
to a storm-tossed ship
on a merciless sea
a piece of bread
to a child 
with a pencil-shaped body and plate-sized eyes
a strong rope
to a climber
dangling above nothingness
your love came to me

And like a grain of sand
sucked away by autumn's winds
I lost it again
