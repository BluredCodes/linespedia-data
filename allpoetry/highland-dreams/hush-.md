Hush .....
Don’t say a word
Let your eyes say the words 
My heart needs to hear

Don’t say a word
Just hold me close 
My heart needs to hear
Only you

Just hold me close
If only for tonight
Only you
Complete Me

If only for tonight 
Let your eyes say the words
Complete me
Hush .....
