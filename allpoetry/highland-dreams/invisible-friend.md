---
title: "Invisible Friend"
writer: "Highland Dreams"
slug: "invisible-friend"
source: "AllPoetry"

I’ll lend and invisible hand
To your visibly broken heart
I’ll send this invisible tissue
Before your tears can start

Tomorrow will bring a better day
I will hope that you can see
That you have a visible friend 
Even if you can’t see me!Smile, it'll be okay.   : )
