Here stands the conqueror
Before the morning sun
He puts on his trainers
Preparing for his run

Cold wind in his face
Chilling his rosy cheeks
Big race is coming up
In a few short weeks

Through rain and snow
His pace does propel  
Not only his body
But his mind as well

Shattered yet refreshed
At the end of the run
The race has yet to start
But it’s already been wonFor James Angus Mackay (tgb) who will be running the London marathon in April.  Best of luck to you James, we're behind you.
