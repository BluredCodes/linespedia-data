---
title: "The Punishing"
writer: "Highland Dreams"

What punishment is this for choosing what is not right?

What darkness is this...will I ever see Light?

What pain is this that no one can understand?

What wound is that cannot be healed by hand?

What lashes are these that has been done to me?

What scar is this...that no one can see?
