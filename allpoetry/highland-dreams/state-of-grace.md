---
title: "State of Grace"
writer: "Highland Dreams"

There is a state of grace
That happens every day
It’s a feeling I thought 
Was lost or locked away

This time no pretense
No more playing games
I’d walk a thousand miles
Just to hear you say my name

Kiss me softly and slowly
Lets get lost, like only lovers do
Hold me closer, love me tender
I get swept away, by all that is you

You’ve awaked something 
Deep inside my soul
Every moment, every second 
Completes me and makes me whole

We’ll make love freely
As we watch the new sunrise
I’ll lose myself in your embrace
And the beauty of your eyes

You’re all my dreams rolled into one
I can hardly believe you’re true
I’d live a thousand lives 
If every one I could live with you
