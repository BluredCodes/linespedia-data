---
title: "You Have"
writer: "Highland Dreams"
slug: "you-have"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/24332-You-Have-by-Highland-Dreams"
---

You have inspired me
With your poetry and the words within
You have touched me
Without ever having felt my skin

You have moved me
With the kindness of your heart
You have given me
The hope of a brand new start

You have comforted me
When I felt so all alone
You have written
What only my heart has known

You have listened to me
When I needed to be heard
You have shown me
Through the beauty of your words

You have mesmerized me
When I have never even met you
You have made me dream 
I hope that you've dreamt of me too
