whispers
from the dark loam
of soft shadows
fantasies of love
reaching 
the quiescent
vale
traveling 
deep into
the hidden crevices 
of time
where love once 
bloomed
in shades of blue
and lovers lived
their sighs 

plunging
like a falling dream
into the valley
of tears 
that embraces
their pain
destined to wander
in solace
whilst nature
sings its
dulcet refrain

do you hear me
whispering
your name
