---
title: "In The Thick of Things"
writer: "Highland Dreams"

My mind skips across reality
Like a flat stone across a glassy pond
Throwing thoughts to clarify
This bed of confusion
I recline on

In my imagination so clear, 
Yet unseen lies my craft, 
My boat into tomorrow
Pondered, though realised
There is no harmony without sorrow

To surround yourself with atmosphere
Or submerge yourself in oceans
Take your time to dance around
In the thick of things
Time abounds

The beginning always ends so sweetly
And the finally only starts to glow
Fires inflame and questions bother
The answer is ...
We’ll never know
