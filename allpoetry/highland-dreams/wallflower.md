title: "Wallflower"
writer: "Highland Dreams"

All alone  . . .  in a room full of people
Looking for that familiar face
One that will cause my heart to leap
And quicken its timid pace

Just the mere sight of him
Can make my body blush
Oh, this insane attraction
What a bloody rush

I merely catch a glimpse of him
In the corner of my eye
Such a vision of perfection  . . . 
I let out a bit of a sigh

Will he even notice me?
Will he take the time to speak?
Oh the mere thought of it
Makes my legs go weak.

But I shrink into a corner
Unable to be heard
As my dream passes by
. . . I utter not a word
