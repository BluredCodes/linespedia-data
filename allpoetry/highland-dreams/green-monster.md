---
title: "Green Monster"
writer: "Highland Dreams"
slug: "green-monster"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

creeping, cowering 
like a hunter 
yet, we are the hunted

always being pursued

sneaking up 
biting us 
attacking from behind

eyes glowing in the dark

it smiles as it senses our pain
claws sink deep into our flesh 
as it hangs on

a tough beast to conquer

it's fiery breath always upon us.
always lurking
searching, feeding

he's a very clever villain

you think you've prevailed
but you've only just 
b e g u n

the fight is long
and in the end
only with 
y o u r s e l f
