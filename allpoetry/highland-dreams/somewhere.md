---
title: "Somewhere"
writer: "Highland Dreams"
slug: "somewhere"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I see the promise in a smile as yet unknown to me
The hope shining in eyes as yet unseen
I feel the breath in a mouth unkissed
And sense the touch in hands as yet unfelt
I hear your speech in a voice unheard
In my soul, I can feel your presence
When pale pink tickles the sky and calls me to wake
With the rising sun proclaiming day has begun
In the smell of freshly cut grass, and earth after the rain has passed
Whilst watching the clouds o'er snow covered mountain tops
Or in the break of the waves in the heart of a storm
When darkness descends and the lights on the ground compete with stars
The calm before sleep when all is restful and quiet
When twilight falls, and the moon descends
In these very things that call to me
Somewhere I know you wait.
