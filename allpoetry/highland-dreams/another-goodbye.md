---
title: "Another Goodbye"
writer: "Highland Dreams"

In the glow of the early morning light
	Our eyes are locked in a silent embrace
                      
            	I feel the  warm rush of tears 
                       	As they start to stream down my cheeks

Your heart beating against mine
	Fingers intertwined, clenched hands

            	Only a bead of perspiration separates us
                       	Two bodies move in a rhythmical dance

As sweet spiraling pleasure consumes us
           Emotions sweep over me like a warm breeze

                       Your passionate cries of love
                                  Muffled in the tender kisses on my brow

Silently we lay in each others arms
           Knowing what the daylight brings

                       I bury my head in your chest
			          Praying that tomorrow never comes
