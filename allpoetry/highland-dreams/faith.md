---
title: "Faith"
writer: "Highland Dreams"
slug: "faith"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

See the signs, madness all around us,
Somewhere I hear sirens, wailing through the night.
Read the Times, it makes me wonder,
Is there more to life than black and white?

I never thought I’d question
The doubt I’m seeing everywhere
Standing here beside me, you’re the answer.
When I lose my faith, you’re there.

The more you have, the more you want,
It leads to nowhere.
I should have learned by now,
Life is nothing, without someone to share.

You can count on me to keep you safe,
I’ll be your shelter from the storm.
Come lay down beside me, and
Let our spirits melt us into one.

With everyone uncertain
The future’s never really clear
Standing here beside me, you’re the reason,
I found my faith again with you here.
