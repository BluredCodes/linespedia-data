title: "Sometimes"
writer: "Highland Dreams"

Sometimes I wonder if you can see inside me,
With your beautiful eyes
Because when you look at me
I feel as transparent as glass
But, you can't can you?
Because if you did,
You would know how I feel
How I'm reborn every time you touch me
How I'm rescued with every kiss
How much I need you
How can we be together?
Is it just me?
Can you see it too?
If only you would tell me...
Because every time I hear your voice,
I die knowing you're not with me.
