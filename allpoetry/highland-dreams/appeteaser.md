Tonight it’s just a table for two
Don’t need an oven to heat things up
Your body is what’s on my menu
And I’m not going to stop til I get enough

Tonight I’m going to feast on you
There won’t be any cooking to do
And just when you think I’m through
I’m really going to start pleasing you

Come on in I’ve set the table
On my body you can feast
Tonight I’m going to be your waitress
I’m going to serve you a little treat

Would you like an appeteaser?
Let me whet your appetite
A little taste of something sweeter
Served just the way you like

A bit of whip cream on top
Served with a  taste of my wine
I’m never going to stop
Until your lips are on mine

I want to pleasure you
Until you beg for more
Any way you want me to
Til you’re ready for the main course

I’ll work my way down just a little lower
I wanna kiss your body right
And just when you think its over
I’ll make it last all night
