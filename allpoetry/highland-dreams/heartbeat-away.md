---
title: "Heartbeat Away"
writer: "Highland Dreams"
slug: "heartbeat-away"
source: "AllPoetry"

A dying 
heartbeat 
heard
but 
miles away.
a soul 
lost
trapped 
in the fold

confusion 
sets in 
as 
emotions
mean more 
than their words.

So close
yet a warm 
embrace
too far apart.
It seems that 
never
will the body 
be found 
to join
the beating 
of the 
heart
