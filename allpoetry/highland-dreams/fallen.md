---
title: "Fallen"
writer: "Highland Dreams"
slug: "fallen"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

she sits inside an empty room, 
all she does is cry
her life was left torn apart, 
without a reason why

he left behind a broken heart, 
she said that all was through
she told him that he must go,
that he had be untrue.

a perfect love was all she knew, 
. . . was all she understood.
her life had been like heaven, 
she did everything she could.

now this angel’s broken wings, 
could not lift her from this pain
she fell down from her heavenly home, 
and would never be the same.

the life she lived was all but gone,
nothing left but an empty space,
living inside a barren world, 
with the memory of his face.

the stars that used to touch the sky, 
had fallen to the ground
the birds had lost there voices, 
and no one made a sound.

she shut the whole world out, 
what else could she do?
she left behind the life she loved, 
she said that all was through.

but with one broken heart, 
another’s love can mend,
her world began to open up, 
once she allowed it begin.

she looked beyond her memories, 
of her loves lost in the past
began to open up again, 
didn’t want the pain to last.

she decided to move on from him, 
to find another life,
she walked on with a graceful step,
and left behind the strife.

though she was forever disappointed, 
and told she would never fly
even with her broken wings, 
she will once again ascend the skies.For everyone who has been there .... and back.
