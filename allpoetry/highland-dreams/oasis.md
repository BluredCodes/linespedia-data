---
title: "Oasis"
writer: "Highland Dreams"
slug: "oasis"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/7117-Oasis-by-Highland-Dreams"
---

As the setting sun sinks wearily
On a seemingly endless day
I know that I cannot carry on
Continuing to live life this way

In a world so bent on destruction
Filled with hate, corruption, and greed
I fail to find the beauty in life
And I seek out to find what I need

Across the vast wastelands I travel
Seeking the life of my many dreams
Finding no refuge or comfort
In the mirage's cool clear streams

The sands of time stretch before me
Behind me are the steps of my past
I really know not what I am seeking
But I will know when I've found it at last

My mind and body grow weary
And my heart struggles to carry on
I know that what the future holds
Is just beyond the dawn

As the sun rises in the distant sky
I can't believe what's in front of me
My mind struggles to figure out
If it's a mirage or an oasis I see

It is everything that I imagined
Filled with love, beauty and more
No more man's inhumanity to man
No starving children, poverty, or war

A world where there are no colours
No sickness, no corruption, or greed
Where hate and anger have no home
Our spirits and souls are freed

Tomorrow I will wake to find it all a dream
Back to the pain, the anger and the hate
My hope is that some day soon
This dream will be our fate
