Love, 
from the 
snow capped peaks 
of my heart
echoes ring 
through the valleys
below.

Songs of 
Abelard and Heloise,
distanced 
by ignorance and shame,
and all those 
whose flesh wanders
the wastelands 
of separation:

you
are the
winter rose
hued 
on the breast 
of new fallen snow.

~ ~ ~ ~ ~ ~


Echoes 
of unrequited 
love
reverberate
through the glen 
of a broken
heart.

Silent strains
of devotion,
estranged by
unruly circumstances,
seeking to destroy
the bonds 
which can never
be broken:

you
are the
radiant sun
that
delivers the rose
to existence.
