---
title: "Penetrate Your Mind"
writer: "Highland Dreams"
slug: "penetrate-your-mind"
source: "AllPoetry"

Close your amazing eyes
Lay back for a little while
Let me ease your mind 
And leave you with a smile

Let me be the very first 
To penetrate your mind
With my tongue I will explore
Every inch that I can find

Reach out your ardent hands 
Touch every aspect of me
Submit and I will show you
How pleasing love can be

Give me all your consideration
Allow me to have my way
I will drive your body wild 
With the games that I will play

I’ll turn you on like never before
Lose yourself in the bliss
Passionately making love to your mind 
With my lingering kiss

I want you screaming my name
The look of pure ecstacy in your eyes
As I deliver you to nirvana
Whilst savouring your sweet surprise
