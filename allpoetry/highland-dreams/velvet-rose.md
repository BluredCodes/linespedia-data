Behold the mystery of the velvet rose
	My eternal gift of love to you
		Explore it with your mind and body
			For there is pleasure in it's beauty
				Brush your fingertips o'er it's petals
				As it transforms in to full bloom
			Petals moist with the dew of passion
		Fragrant with the aroma of ecstasy
	Let your tongue revel in it's nectar
For it is for your pleasure only
