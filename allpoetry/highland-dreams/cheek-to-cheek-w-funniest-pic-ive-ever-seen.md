I love my little sister,
I well and truly do.
And if you don’t believe it,
I’ve got a pic for you.

I gave her a little make-over,
and coloured from cheek to cheek.
I must admit the site of it
Made mummy’s knees grow weak.

I love my little sister,
With all my little heart.
I just don’t think me mum
Appreciates my work of art !Still laughing !!
