title: "How Forever Came True"
writer: "Highland Dreams"

As the sunset disappears 
Across the northern sky
I look forward to forever 
In your beautiful eyes

‘Tis all that I can say, 
As long as I have a voice
I’ll thank heavens above 
That I was your choice

If lying in your arms 
Is the last thing that I do
At least I know that 
I’ve been loved by you

A hundred years from now
When my love letters are found
Long after my days are done
And I’m buried in the ground

Someone will read these words 
That were meant only for you
And then they will truly know
How forever came true.

So let the years roll on by
I’ll just smile when my days are through
These are the best times of my life
Knowing that I’ve been loved by you.

 . . . and all my dreams came true.
