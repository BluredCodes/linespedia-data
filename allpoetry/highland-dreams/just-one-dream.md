---
title: "Just One Dream"
writer: "Highland Dreams"
slug: "just-one-dream"

If I was limited 
to just one dream,
it would be of 

Y
O
U
.
 
Your enchanted words 
are like 
a celestial invocation. 

Y
O
U

give deliverance 
to my captured soul.

My benediction 
rests 
upon your shoulders 
for nirvana 
is a part of 

Y
O
U
.

My only torment 
would be 
to awaken.
