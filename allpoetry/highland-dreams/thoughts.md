---
title: "Thoughts"
writer: "Highland Dreams"

Silence is golden, but no gold for me,
my thoughts are far too loud.

Have I screamed it out yet?

Has anyone heard my thoughts,
they seem so conspicuous.

I wreak havoc on myself,tear myself apart,
surely I must not deserve you, certainly you must be mistaken.

when alone I wonder . . . 
why do I deserve to be alone?
but when with you . . .
I wonder why I deserve someone so special.

I hope that someone can be you for a very long time . . .
I'd hate to break your heart, but somehow you're breaking mine.
