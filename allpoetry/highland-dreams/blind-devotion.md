---
title: "Blind Devotion"
writer: "Highland Dreams"

A righteous path meant to follow,
                                Amongst the bramble to define.
                                Beaten over hill and hollow,
                                With certainty walk the divine.

                                Where onward to paradise bound,
                                The righteous walk in fervent stride;
                                For no longer lost they have found
                                That which the forest dares to hide.

                                Through the thicket they've seen the light,
                                Upon this path for them alone;
                                Now interpreting wrong from right,
                                They will follow to the unknown.

                                This righteous path as tracked by men,
                                Where most narrow and easily led.
                                So it's followed time and again
                                By those who fail to use their head.
