Oh Connor, Oh Connor,
Don’t believe it’s true.
Sammy didn’t really find,
Your Teddy bear for you.

In a dark little room,
Underneath my stairs,
Your Teddy is waiting,
Bound and gagged in his chair.

Teddy will be here,
Until the ransom has been paid.
If you haven’t figured it out,
