Just a load of broken promises
And a great big pack of lies
How could I have ever fooled myself
Thinking you were not like other guys

I thought this time was really it
A chance for love and a bit of romance
I guess that I really should have known
You couldn't keep your dick inside your pants

I hope that you're happy with yourself
That a quick orgasm was worth losing me
I pity you for what you have lost, because
You'll never be the man I need you to be

So sleep soundly tonight in the knowledge
That I won't be losing sleep over you
I'll find someone who can love me as I deserve
And whose love is really true
