---
title: "The Moment"
writer: "Highland Dreams"
slug: "the-moment"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

this is the 
moment
I want to share
when yesterday 
was forever
and tomorrow 
is unreachable
a moment in time
when seconds
stand still
and all is
perfect and serene

a moment to 
share
to reflect on the past
and focus on
the future
a moment when
nothing 
can go wrong
any step
be it backwards 
or upside down
is right. 
these 
are the moments
we live for
