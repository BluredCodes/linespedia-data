---
title: "Dreams Unheard"
writer: "Highland Dreams"
slug: "dreams-unheard"

to another world
another time
another people
dreams unheard
stolen by the underworld
societies crime
warring people
dreams like words
twisted and twirled
into broken rhyme
and left unfulfilled
the red and the blue
sometimes conceal what is true
bought and paid for
with blood and sweat
of the third world nations
someday rebel
to return the hell
that was worn
with a smile by
an innocent child
corporations prioritization
profit at all cost
in the end peace is lost
my children's,  children's,  children
will be left to pay the cost.
once again opportunity lost
and dreams deferred
staining the white
with what isn't quite right
hearts and minds that see the light
yet unable to shine 
through greed's gleam
dreams deferred
