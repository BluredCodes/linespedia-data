---
title: "~Paths"
writer: "Highland Dreams"
slug: "paths"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/39230--Paths-by-Highland-Dreams"
---

I want to go 
Where no one’s gone before
My faith will find the way
I’m not afraid anymore

So many times 
You lived with my strength and my pain
You were my friend
When you had nothing to gain

I’m ready to walk that line
Ready to fight 
For I’m not alone anymore
You’re always in sight

For all those years,
You suffered for my love
I love you more
Than you can ever conceive of

For once in your life
Let your soul shine through
And this time I will go
That extra mile for you
