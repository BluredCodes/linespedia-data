writer: "Highland Dreams"

I'm glad you liked my poem ...
Or at least you said you did
But, when I saw a 'new' work by you
I nearly flipped my lid!

The stanzas were far too similar
Even though they didn't rhyme
You didn't think anyone would notice
But you'll get yours in time!

I've contemplated what to do about you
I thought about writing a letter
What pisses me off more than anything ...
Is that I liked your poem better.You know who you are ... I'll be watching!
