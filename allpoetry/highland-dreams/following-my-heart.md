---
title: "Following My Heart"
writer: "Highland Dreams"
slug: "following-my-heart"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I meant to fight it to the finish
But you made me forget
Though it’s possible I’m dreaming
Don’t wake me yet

You know how I feel
I can’t see the day for night
Just can’t seem to win
No matter how hard I fight

I twist round, and turn round
Then I give in
You’re in control of me
My head’s in a spin

You take me and you break me
You do what you please
I stumble and I tremble
I’m weak at the knees

I can’t last another night
Every moment is an eternity
From the depth of my soul
I need you here with me

I can’t wait another night
For every secret your heart reveals
So that you and I may get to know
That it’s true and that it’s real

I’ll follow my heart to the end
As far as the eye can see
I’ll not settle for anything less
I know where my arms should be
