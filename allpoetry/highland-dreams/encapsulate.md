---
title: "Encapsulate"
writer: "Highland Dreams"
slug: "encapsulate"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/38922-Encapsulate-by-Highland-Dreams"
---

Eyes close,
mind floats
into another dimension.
Acutely aware,
feeling the slightest touch,
magnifying,
peaceful.

Breath shallow,
softly caressing,
zephyrs of desire.
Ageless words,
encapsulated in the air.
Tenderness,
silence.

Tracing fingers
over skin,
the intimacy heightened.
Exploring,
touching in a new way,
gently,
daringly.

Skin touches
warm flesh.
Total surrender
of self.
Two are as one,
thrilling,
confiding.

Eyes gaze
into beauty.
Carnal beauty,
shines through.
Seeing into the soul,
totally,
revealing.

Lovers close,
outside time,
enjoying the moments
of ecstacy
drowning in the bliss,
forever,
memorized.
