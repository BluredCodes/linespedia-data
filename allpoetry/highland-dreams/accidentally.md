---
title: "Accidentally"
writer: "Highland Dreams"
slug: "accidentally"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

as I stroll in
                                     the woods absorbing
                                     the beauty of nature
                                     I accidentally see you
                                     but you do not see me
                                     watching you bathe in
                                     the crystal blue lake
                                     I become a spectator
                                     in the grove of pines
                                     on the bank of paradise
                                     I am hypnotised by
                                     the beauty of your
                                     wet shining skin in
                                     the sun of summer
                                     I imagine myself 
                                     in the blue water
                                     diving for the treasure
                                     hidden deep below
                                     the oxygen from your soul
                                     gives me life as I dive
                                     deeper into your pool of lust
