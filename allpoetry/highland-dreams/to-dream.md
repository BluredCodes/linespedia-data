---
title: "To Dream"
writer: "Highland Dreams"

Alone again I lay down my head
                          Where shadows fall and darkness reigns
                          Praying for the night to come and take me
                          To the love that awaits me in my dreams

                          Slowly the mists roll in with the tide
                          Carrying me along to meet with my destiny
                          Over turbulent and wind swept waters
                          The velvet hands of night stretch out to greet me

                          In this moment I feel helpless as a newborn child
                          Until I'm once again safe in his embrace
                          My worries and my fears fade away
                          I see the tenderness and the love in his face

                          My body trembles with his touch
                          His fingertips know the way to my soul
                          Taking my mind and body to higher places
                          It is only with him that I feel whole

                          The peace and rapture I feel within his arms
                          Will soon fade away in the morning light
                          Once again I will be alone and
                          Frantically I try to hold on to the night

                          To a cold and lonely place I drift
                          Back to the aches within my heart
                          How I long to go back to my dream
                          Where love and hope can start

                          So I keep this dream inside me
                          Because I know how much he cares
                          For when I need peace and love
                          My dream will still be there
