---
title: "Wings Of Love"
writer: "Highland Dreams"
slug: "wings-of-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/6958-Wings-Of-Love-by-Highland-Dreams"
---

Preparing to take flight
From far across the sea
As on the wings of a dove
My true love waits for me

This dove has no feathers
Not a beak, or even eyes
To guide its lonely path
Through dark and foreign skies

This creature has no blood 
Yet it carries with it my heart
On glistening wings of white
The long awaited journey starts

These wings carry with them
My fondest hopes and dreams
I watch and not so patiently wait
For an eternity it seems

Seconds seem like hours
Hours seem like days
My heart beats in anticipation
Of meeting his loving gaze

My heart now ceases to beat
If only for one minute
As I watch the dove descend
Knowing all my love is in it

Tears of joy fill my eyes
As this wondrous bird does land
I turn to see a loving face 
Emerge with outstretched hands

Hazel eyes that pierce my soul	
Now upon me gaze
I can't wait to hold you in my arms
I've been waiting for this for days

The bird of love sits idly by
As we kiss and head on our way
For five short days of heaven
I wish he were here to stay

The days they pass so swiftly
We wonder where they went
Five days and nights of passion
Never better my time been spent

Time together has quickly passed
Once again my eyes fill with tears
It's hard to deal with the emotions
Of my hopes and dreams and fears

My heart cries "Baby, please don't go"
But my mind knows he can't stay
This thing doesn't look like a bird of love
But rather a bird of prey

We say our tearful goodbyes
And complete them with a kiss
I know with all my heart
It's more than his touch I will miss

This day the bird doesn't seem so lovely
I think it looks nothing like a dove
I know now it will take him away
But it can never carry away my love
