To manicure or just grow wild?
No ... this is not about my lawn.
You will never even notice,
If I keep my knickers on.

The subject of this poem,
Is female pubic hair.
I'm wondering which is better,
To trim or just go bare?

Some choose to shape and sculpt it,
Others choose to leave it be.
I choose to shave completely
It shows off my jewellery!!Something silly ... from girltalk with a friend at AllPoetry.
