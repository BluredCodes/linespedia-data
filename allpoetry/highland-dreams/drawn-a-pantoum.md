---
title: "Drawn (A Pantoum)"
writer: "Highland Dreams"
slug: "drawn-a-pantoum"
source: "AllPoetry"

I’m unmistakably drawn to you,
As if a moth to a flame.
My heart does beat true,
At the very  mention of your name.

As if a moth to a flame,
Will I be warmed by you?
At the very mention of your name,
My soul is torn in two.

Will I be warmed by you,
Gently warmed by your fire?
My soul is torn in two,
I am filled with desire.

Gently warmed by your fire,
I long to spread my wings.
I am filled with desire,
For so many things.

I long to spread my wings,
Although I fear being burned.
For so many things,
My heart has truly yearned.

Although I fear being burned,
I cannot leave this be.
My heart has truly yearned,
So please come fly with me.

I cannot leave this be,
I’m unmistakably drawn to you.
So please come fly with me,
My heart does beat true.First attempt at this style of poetry ... I think it needs some work .. but not sure where to start.  Any suggestions are appreciated.
