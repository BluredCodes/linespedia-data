---
title: "Perhaps .... Perhaps Not"
writer: "Highland Dreams"
slug: "perhaps-perhaps-not"

Perhaps
it was something 
I saw in you
when you lay 
close to me
and whispered 
so sweet
so terribly sweet
that I needed to hear more.

Perhaps
the touch that ran
down my spine
as we lay supine
was more than I deserved.

Perhaps
this excitement
will become a bore
and I shall fall into a decline.

Perhaps
this craving for you
is like having a desire
for strong mints
or chewy nuts.
But then, I have always fell
for such easy sentimentality.

Perhaps not.
