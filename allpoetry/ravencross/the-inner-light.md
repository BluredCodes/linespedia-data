The world will never be the same again,
From now on things are going to be different.
You inspired us and that will always be reflected,
but somehow the ground fell from under us.
The sun rises and the sun has to set,
Or the sun would never rise at all.
Maybe now you've seen the inner light,
And did you find the further you travel the less you knew?
Maybe now you're looking and know you changed our world.
If it weren't for you, where would we be?
We'd stumble in our footsteps without a path.
You're the one lit the match when it was dark,
but now it's dark again.
And there goes the sun.This is for and in memory of George Harrison, in case you couldn't tell. :( It's so sad... he was only 58 and SO talented and such a great person who fought what he believed in.
