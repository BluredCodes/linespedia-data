Bitch
You're the one that told me blood was beautiful
It was you that taught me how to hate
You're the one that taught me to fall

Bitch
You told me sugar coated words every day
Words about blood tasting like cherries
Words that were lies that made me believe

Bitch
I should have know it's always the quiet ones
It was you who taught me how to kill
You're the one that taught me to crawl

Bitch
You knew I didn't want to be like you
That's why you longed so much to make me see
You said angels aren't special until they've fallen

Bitch
I never imagined this world you created
The world where the living fed off the loving
And you laughed when they all went down in chains

Bitch
You climbed into my brain and played around
Made me think thought I'd never have thought
Made me do things I'd never have done

Bitch
You believed in a world where there was only you
There was no place for no other and that's what you wanted
You sang with a huanting voice lyrics that made the world cry

Bitch
You crawled to me when you ran out of time
I took you in and raised you up
So you took me down and taught me your ways

Bitch
Red was always your favourite colour
You painted your walls in the blood of your victims
Innocent bystanders of your insane outbursts

Bitch
You always smiled when I came around
There was always that thought I'd do your dirty work
I'd paint the walls and you'd pave the way

Little fucking bitch
