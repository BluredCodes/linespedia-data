---
title: "Go away"
writer: "RavenCross"
slug: "go-away"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Be careful when you're around me,
I may look sweet but I do bite back.
Somebody taught me how to fight back.

I never want to be near you anymore
This isn't what I came here for
I don't know who told you this is what I wanted,
In the simplest way to say It's not what anyone wanted.

I'm not a little girl and I'm not stupid,
I have eyes and they're following you everywhere you go.
Take your time or find a pace my eyes won't wander.

I never want to be near you anymore
This isn't what I came here for
Nobody made you God and God didn't make himself,
So get off your horse and stop livin' on a shelf.

I have a mind of my own and it's powerful as hell,
You don't want to get in my way since now I'm not afraid.
I might look nice but someone taught me how to fight back.

I never want to be near you anymore
This is not what I came here for
