title: "Sin for Me"

Sin for me,
Take a drop of my blood,
Prick me with the thorn of a red rose.
Lie for me,
Break my outside shell,
Enter into my shattered world.
Steal for me,
Leave your sinecure world,
Take things that don't belong to us.
Kill for me,
Scavenge for all the remnants.
Never admit the things you've done.
Run for me,
Get away from me,
I don't want you where it isn't safe.
I'm certainty not the one to be around.
