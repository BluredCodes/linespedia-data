Dear God what have I done
There's blood everywhere
On the wall and on my hands
I heard screaming but now it's fading

God Save the Children
Who haven't been corrupted yet
God Save the Children
Who know not better than to walk in my footsteps

I don't remember much of it
All I know is there was chaos and there was pain
Everybody everywhere put their lives on hold
To find out how things would end

God Save the Children
Who's souls are still free and remain untainted
God Save the Children
That the world hasn't gotten to yet

Now I know I'm not the only one who'll never be the same
Seems like the whole world is up in arms
I can't look forward
After all here the blind lead the blind

God Save the Children
The ones who aren't yet slaves but still need to be set free
God Save the Children
Who haven't died and who haven't killed

By the end when it's all been said and done
And the walls are washed and my hands heal
When people walk these halls will they know
All the screams and all the blood this place has seen

God Save the Children
Who look at the world and don't understand
God Save the Children
Who didn't cry when I took this place down in flames

Teach them how to cry
