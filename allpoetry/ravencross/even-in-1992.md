I knew that this convertion,
was only a diversion.

Keeping you away,
Doing anything to lead you astray.

Even in 1992,
I can say for sure I knew.

You're not as strong as some might say,
It's actually easy to keep you at bay.

In the light of this new era,
Some have gone as far to call you the new Hera.

Do you really believe all those things you claim,
Or are you just out of names to blame?

You're ripping apart from the heart,
But I know, you're not very smart.

Even in 1992,
I can say for sure I knew.
