---
title: "Asleep at the wheel"
writer: "RavenCross"

I feel asleep at the wheel,
'Cause you were sitting next to me.
In the passanger's seat.
And you were driving me mad.

I feel asleep at the wheel,
'Cause you were talking but said nothing.
In the backseat of my car.
And you were driving me off the edge.

I feel asleep at the wheel,
'Cause you were boring me to death.
From that big mouth of yours.
And you were driving me up the wall.

I feel asleep at the wheel,
But it was my turn to drive.
So I drove into the wall.
