---
title: "The Dark Horse"
writer: "RavenCross"
slug: "the-dark-horse"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/16193-The-Dark-Horse-by-RavenCross"
---

Purely gothic pessimistic questions,
Sadistic outtakes from your daily escapades,
Nobody anticipated the dark horse might win.
Unraveled souls to reveal the vengeful show,
Defunct without ever being aware.
Long-lived tales prevaricating themselves away from factuality.
You wouldn't have guessed the dark horse would come out on top.
Damned to ceaseless torment, bound by chains.
Horoscopes wicked as mortal nature,
Your living funneral at the dark chapel,
In the gloaming is when the change occurs.
No ones ever presumes the dark horse.
