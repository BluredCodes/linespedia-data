title: "Inside my head"

You put me through so much pain
Wrapped me up in ten thousand chains
You knocked me down but it made me stronger
You gave me a torch but you knew it would never light her

You're  the one that taught me how to hate
You're the one that taught me how to show no mercy
On my broken arms I will no longer carry the world's weight

Twisted man, yeah you didn't like me
Come at me cast in restraints, You taught me to be ready
I won't fall unless I'm sure there's no one to catch me
You taught me so long ago how evil I could be
