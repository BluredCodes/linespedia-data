I am Bloody Mary,
In the mirror.
Looking Out,
And bleeding in.
I'll scar your face,
and attack your mind.
Just call my name,
13 suspenceful times.
Spin in Circles.
Turn on your light,
or you'll end up like me.
I am Bloody Mary,
This is my life,
as well as death.
I'll torment you,
You asked me to.
I'l do to you,
what was done to me.
I'll make you bleed,
And I'm make you scream,
I'm Bloody Mary and you tempted fate.This poem might have more meaning if you remember playing Bloody Mary at slumber parties and summer camp.
