You're watching and just standing still
It's not your concern and you simply watch in shock
Nobody's innocent when blood is shed
And people stand around and no one helps
There's no such thing as innocence when you
Coulda done something
Coulda fought someone
You were scared and I don't blame you
But you're not an innocent bystander
Just a bystander nothing preceeding
You didn't add pain but you wouldn't take it away
You're not afraid to fight when it's your fight
I thought it would be your fight
When you see someone uncousious still being kicked
Until there's more blood then there was before
And nobody has helped still
It's not brave when you're not scared
Nobody was in the mood to be the hero of the day
Just don't try to convince me
