You say you're mad at me
Ok, I know you're mad at me.
You just assume I know what I did wrong.
You know I'm not just singing this lame-ass song,
I'm actually trying to figure it out.

Maybe I could fix the whole,
If you'd tell me which wall I struck.

You say you're mad at me,
Ok, I know you're mad at me.
I just have to say I never meant to hurt you.
In case you don't know, for me this is so new,
I'm actually trying to figure it out.

Make me carry the cross.
Make me nail in my own bleeding body.
Make me die that way.
Just don't make me figure it out.
