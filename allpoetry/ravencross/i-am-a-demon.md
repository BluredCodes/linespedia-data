---
title: "[I am a demon]"
writer: "RavenCross"
slug: "i-am-a-demon"
source: "AllPoetry"

I am a demon,
That crawls under you skin,
And makes you hurt from the inside out,
Until you scream and your lungs bleed.
You're bitter.
You're hollow.
There's nothing there.
Entwined and mismatched.
You strangled me.
I am fragile.
Bones break and I'm on fire.
You're nocturnal and I trust you as I sleep,
But secretly I have always known.
You're cold.
You're empty.
You're just a man.
Unfitting and  undefined.
You suffocated me.

I am a freak,
That crawls into your mind,
And makes you so confused that you can't think,
Until you scream from the loss of refrence.
You're dying.
You're alone.
There's no one on your side.
Paranoid and culled.
You smothered me.
I am weak.
Thoughts fade and I'm drowning.
You wear a blindfold but I trust your aim,
But secretly I have always known.
You're stained.
You're void.
Unbalanced and unleashed.
You choked me.

I'm crawling under your skin and through you mind,
I'm trying to get this to work but I just feel worked.
I've seen the future and it's just like now.
You're still gagging me then.
