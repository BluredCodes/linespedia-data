It sends shivers up my spine,
I can hardly breathe these days.
It's just intoxicating, so exhilarating.

I guess that you're just jealous,
I'm the best there's ever been.
And,
I'm so cold.
Sorry, Mom and Dad,
I know you're not too proud of me.
Things are differnt now,
I'm not your good girl anymore.
I will always be cold.
It's not my job to watch your back,
I will always be cold. (Ice Cold)

I'll never really give a damn,
not even when Hell freezes over.
I guess I'm sorry for all my wrongs,
but that doesn't mean I'm going to stop.

I've broken so many hearts,
I've fought so many fights.
I've cussed so many times.

I hate being told I've hurt people,
People have hurt me every day,
since I was born.
Everybody hurts everybody,
don't play saint on me now.

I guess that you're just jealous,
I'm the best there's ever been.
And,
I'm so cold.
Sorry, Mom and Dad,
I know you're not too proud of me.
Things are differnt now,
I'm not your good girl anymore.
I will always be cold.
It's not my job to watch your back,
I will always be cold. (Ice Cold)

The lights are off now,
and maybe you think I'm morbid.
All I am is human.
Take a look in the mirror,
you're just as cold as me.

I guess that you're just jealous,
I'm the best there's ever been.
And,
I'm so cold.
Sorry, Mom and Dad,
I know you're not too proud of me.
Things are differnt now,
I'm not your good girl anymore.
I will always be cold.
It's not my job to watch your back,
I will always be cold. (Ice Cold)

Never forget,
you're just as cold as me.

And I will always be cold.
I will always be cold. (Ice Cold)
