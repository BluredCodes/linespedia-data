---
title: "I think I found it"
writer: "RavenCross"

What am I supposed to say when you laugh at me,
And you've changed since I last saw you.
Now you're so much a worse person,
But I still wish you'd hang around more often.
When I was little I used to chase rainbows,
and sometimes I'd watch the stars until I feel asleep outside.
It's been years since I've even glanced the stars.
But it doesn't matter, since stars would only make me cry.
I'm such a romantic I still think we'll be together at the end.
I wrote a poem trying to tell people what I felt, I lost that paper somewhere.
Later I heard some rock band singing my words.
Nothing to claim and it's such a shame,
but you're still the highlighted lines in my script.
Sometimes I yank my brain around and think,
I think I found that thing that is the limit of what's possible.
I don't think I can go any further,
I found the thing I've always been looking for,
And so I have no reason to look anymore.
If I find heaven on Earth when I die where do I go?
