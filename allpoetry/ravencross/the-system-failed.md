The system failed
They cuffed the pot smokers and locked them away
But the baby killers are running free
Better not make a living selling sex
'Cause what you do that's hurting no one
Is the governments concern
Still they haven't found the antrax distro
But rest assured flag burners are behind bars
You're paying for the jail cells too
The system failed you and the system failed me
The system failed us allThis is... ermmm, kinda an affirmation. I guess... I'm not a druggie or a hooker, by the way, since people seem to think becuase I believe in rights to do certain things I must be doing them. X.x
