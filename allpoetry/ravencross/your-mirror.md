Get your mirror.
Break the glass.
Shatter your refelction.
Shatter my soul.
Break me.
Put me back together.
Leave cracks and scars.
Make sure that the world knows.
I've been broken.
I've been shattered.
I've been crushed.Hmmm... I left this short becuase making it any longer really took away from it.
