History always repeats itself
Don't you know you're just playing the game
There's no thought in your head that hasn't been thought before
They'll put your name in history books
But your story's been printed before

Why don't you read the story
After you've thought it through
Try not to change what you'd planned to do
Didn't it sound almost prophoric
