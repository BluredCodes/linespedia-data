title: "Sunshine and Maggots"
writer: "RavenCross"

This is the place that they were all talking about
This is the life that they said we should try

I wanna see the sunshine with you
I wanna crawl underground with you
I wanna see the maggots with you
I wanna be perfect and I wanna be consumed in flaw
I wanna burn the ashes down further with you
I wanna ride the waves til the sun comes down with you
I wanna search the desert and when I find you you'll be my water
And If you were water then I could bathe in you

I wanna be there to take the bullet for you
I wanna be the first one to not abandon you
I wanna see all the beautiful reasons to love you
I wanna go down when you go down We can work our way up
I wanna close my eyes and wake up in your arms
I wanna trash the world and save my love for you
I wanna dive underwater and you can be my oxygen tank
And if you were air then I could take all of you in

I wanna love you
Simply put and plainly said
