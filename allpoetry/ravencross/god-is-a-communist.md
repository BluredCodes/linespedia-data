Also understand this is not THE God, but YOUR God. Not yours specifically, but the one a lot of people preach.The only thing they didn't tell you in Sunday school,Is that God is a communist.God gives everybody the same thing,Even when you worked so much harder than everybody else.Your God is a communist.You're out there worshiping a communist.Funny, they never told you that.The things your God does makes Hitler look like a nice guy,At least consentration camps someday end.When you disagree with God and if you beg to differ,If you don't play by his conformist rules,He'll burn your skin and it'll never end.I bet you never heard God compared to Hitler.I bet you never heard Hitler turn out kinder.You tell us if we don't worship your God,If we don't always go to Church,It doesn't matter what's in our hearts or in our minds,but we're doomed to flames and we're doomed to chains.God saves those who believe Jesus died for us,But what's he save you from?I'd like to save myself, I'd at least I'd like the option.God created you but you are not in his debt,And you shouldn't ever bow down to save yourself.What a cowardly way to live.If you don't have evil in your heart,Don't let them make you believe that you're evil.No one ever talked to God, and he never said,'Follow my rules or forever suffer.'If you fall in love and they love you back,You're expected to love God above them still.A God you've never met and can't prove exists.More than the one who's been there for you,Even though you never asked to be created.And if you're not thankful even when everything goes wrong,Then you're a sinner and you will pay.If they didn't tell you then, You must see it now.God is a communist.
