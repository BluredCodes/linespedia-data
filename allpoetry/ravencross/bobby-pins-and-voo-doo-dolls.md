You know I want to take a bobby pin and break your soft skin
I'd like to see you bleed from a point so dull yet so painful
The pin that is blunt with a bigger point bringing more pain and more distress
I wanna make you bleed like I bled

You know I wish I had your Voo Doo Doll
I could shove a sharp pin into your simple skull and fill you with agony
You see I could give you a headache and pierce your vital organs until you break down in misery
I want the agony to hurt you like mine does me

I want to make you unstable in every sense
I'd like to drive you to catatonia and the edge of your sanity
I want to make you experience it all from paranoia to dependency till you can't take it no more
I want to make you the lunatic I have become

You would never even come close to all the things I'd put myself through for you
