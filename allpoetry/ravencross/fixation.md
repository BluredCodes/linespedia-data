---
title: "Fixation"
writer: "RavenCross"

You're the message from God,
The Promised Saviour to rescue my soul.
Don't misunderstand, Don't get me wrong,
But you exist inside my wandering mind.

I've got this fixation with you.
It's like everything you do, 
fascinates me from my cultivated world.

Stare.
Give me a look from your personal Utopia.
Let me take a glance.

You're my hero in a blurry world,
You're the one they all look at as you walk by.
Don't misunderstand, Don't take this the wrong way,
You'll always be my everything.

I've got this fixation with you.
I can see the scars from the past that haunts you.
I can make the future that will keep you.
