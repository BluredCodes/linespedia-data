You've got to stop denying it
You're just like me
You'd so afraid becuase I'm such an outcast
And you fit so perfect on a K-Mart shelf
On the outside you should break that famous face
And get down with reality since you know
You're just like me inside
You're scared and broken and we need each other
There's a place on your left eyebrow
Where the hair doesn't grown and there's a sacr
And you know I think it's sexy that way
It's the part of you that you hate so much
'Cause nothing on a K-Mart shelf is different from the rest
On the outside you have no other flaws
You know we're the same way
You know we need each other or at least I need you
Stop trying to claim conformity
when you know you're just like me
