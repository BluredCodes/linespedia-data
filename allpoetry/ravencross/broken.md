Yeah, the government stole your heart away
And you can't fight back without a gun
Yeah, you're broken and malfunctioning
but I cannot help you
Oh, you've reached what they call rock bottom
Now you understand the metaphors
Torn to 10,000 pieces,
covered up like a conspiricy
Shaken up until you can't think straight

Can you take it
Can you live through it
Will you still be alive when rock comes back again
Duck and cover You'll be safe 'till rock comes back again

Yeah, the government stole your heart away
And you can't fight back without a gun
Yeah, you're broken and malfunctioning
but I cannot help you
Oh, you've reached what they call rock bottom
Now you understand the metaphors

You couldn't ever stand your ground
Taken past normal expectations
Standing through their support
Falling only when you get kicked
Obeying without thinking
Breathing but not alive
Taking everything to a deeper level
Making so many more wannabes
You'll never have a kick like this
Even drugs can't get ya this high

Yeah, you're broken and malfunctioning
but I cannot help you
Oh, you've reached what they call rock bottom
Now you understand the metaphors

Ripping up your old photo books
The world abandoned us
