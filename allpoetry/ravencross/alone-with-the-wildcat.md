---
title: "Alone with the Wildcat"
writer: "RavenCross"
slug: "alone-with-the-wildcat"

Timber eyes shaped like cresants,
His existence merely to prove me wrong.

In this barren forest of my cascaded past,
I stand alone with the wildcat,
The part of me I wish I didn't have.
The crimson bloody tears intangible to most,
The secret he and I share,
Isn't so discreet in that very trice,
When I am alone with the wildcat.

Crawling back for another glance,
Some would climb mountains just to see,
The wildcat inside of thee.
