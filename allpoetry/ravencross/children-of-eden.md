Children of Eden,
We have stories to tell.
Memories, Descirptions, Interviews with the darkness.
We take the sun away at night.
We're the ones that make paper cuts sting.
It's us who opened Pandora's fragile box.
It's us that let the evils out.
We're the ones who let the demons roam.
We are the Children of Eden.
