---
title: "Malice"
writer: "RavenCross"
slug: "malice"

You took it out on me
The fact that you can't face reality
You took it out on us
We were the ones that wound up bleeding
Bleeding blood Bleeding Words and Bleeding lies
You took it out on me
Cause I was standing up when you fell down
Cause I was surviving when you were dying
You just took it out on me
Assumed I was going forward
So you were going backward
Made yourself a victim of circumstance
Paranoia isn't just a state of mind
You took it out on me
You know I was strong and you were weak
Primal instinct takes you over
I'm advanced while you're still simple
Your existence is a malice
It's almost laughable
You took it out on me
The fact that you're being manipulated
You took it out on us
We're the ones that wound up crying
Crying tears Crying songs Crying dust
You took it out on me
Cause I was loyal and you can't be trusted
Cause I was gripping as you were losing ground
I was gripping as you were losing ground
You took it out on me
