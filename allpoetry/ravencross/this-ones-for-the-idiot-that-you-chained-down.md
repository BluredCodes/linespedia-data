---
title: "This one's for the idiot that you chained down"
writer: "RavenCross"
slug: "this-ones-for-the-idiot-that-you-chained-down"

Their lips are cherry red and their eyes are bloodshot
Their flattened minds limit them down to mainstream networks
No one likes an idiot
So you strapped them in and chained them down
They'd never heard this law but it was enforced

This one's for the idiot that you chained down
This one's for the mind that you caged in
This one's for the day when
You won't fear the idiot as you do

Their skin is pale and they're shaking from the cold
Their simple ideas didn't put anyone in envy
No one likes an idiot
So you strapped them in and chained them down
They're the ones who didn't move when the rain got heavy

There are idiots on every corner of every street
You can't chain them all down but you know you'll try
They're standing in the hail and getting hit in the head
And you're ten feet away getting out your handcuffs
No one likes an idiot
So you strapped them in and chained them down

This one's for the idiot that you chained down
This one's for the mind that you caged in
No one likes an idiot
Maybe you should wonder why no one likes you
