title: "All the Pretty Faces"

Too many to count,
It seems that everywhere you go,
There's another pretty face.
It's just so easy to trust a pretty face.
It's just so easy to love a pretty face.

All the pretty faces,
That fill the streets of every city.
All the pretty faces,
Shaded under your eclipse.

On all your walls,
All the pretty faces,
Found in so many differnt places.
I found the prettiest face of all,
Ever since I cannot see the faces at all.
