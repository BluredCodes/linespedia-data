---
title: "Can't Crawl"
writer: "RavenCross"
slug: "cant-crawl"

You always were as alone as I was
Cold, matted, locks of your hair
That's all that's left over now
It's all I'll have when I visit your grave

I can't come crawling back to you
No not this time
I won't come crying at your grave
No I'm gonna lift myself up

I can only wonder where I'll be when I get over you
Frozen, lonenly, pieces of your soul
I still have that, to remember you
Ever if I can never show it off

I can't come crawling back to you
No I'm through with that
I won't come crying at your grave
No I'm gonna make it alone

I've really got to get over you
I'm not in a healthy state
My vision is blurry and you're on my mind
Even though you're six feet under

Everyone else has long since forgotten
The smell of your hair and the touch of your skin
But I'm still trying to take my first step
I can't catch up with all these expectations

Silence is Golden
And how I long to crawl again
