The honeymoon is over but we haven't even boarded a plane
I remember when I used to want to be around you
You just need to shut your mouth
Stop giving me that look
Stop assuming and stop concluding
If you would shut your mouth and open your mind
You could see all the beauty
and learn to love the flaw
If you would shut your mouth and open your mind
The world wouldn't be so hard for you to grip
You really should shut your mouth
You should stop teasing
Stop feeding me your bullshit
I told you I'm not in the mood
So stop feeing me your bullshit
I don't want to look at you anymore
Just the thought and just the sight
Is enouph to make me hurl
Just shut your mouth
Try not to lose control
Shut your mouth and open your mind
I remember when we used to hang out
I remember when you used to have an open mind
But now you oughtta just shut your mouth
No one cares what you have to say
All your words are always the same
So we wouldn't miss anything new
Just shut your mouth
Try not to lose control
Just open your mind
There's so much for you to learn
