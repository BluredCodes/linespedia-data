---
title: "Simple"
writer: "RavenCross"
slug: "simple"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I am beneath you,
You are above me.

I wish it was that simple.
I don't mean to make you pull.

I wish I could be your willing slave.
I don't know why I can't just behave.

Life isn't a simple story,
Written on the back of your hand.
Paranoia leads you to believe,
Complex means betrayal.

I wish it were that simple.
If only I would submit to you.
I wish you could wear my like a shoe.
But it's never that simple.
