title: "Give me a Hug"

I think maybe I just need a little affection
I don't wanna fight right now
I don't care if you don't agree with my opinions
Give me a hug
I think I just need someone to hug me
I'll give you a hug
And we can look each other in the eye
Maybe we can get along
