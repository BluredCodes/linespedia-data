title: "Make me a Goddess"
writer: "RavenCross"

Get down on your hands and kness,
I want to be your entire world.
Beg for me to know you're alive,
I want to know you worship me.

Make me a Goddess in your religion.
Make me the Queen of your whole world.
Make me Royalty in your kingdom.

Feel the pain I would have felt,
Take my hold away from me
And make me a Dominatrix.
Make me the hero in your book
Don't define with just one word.
Make an entire Dictionary to define me.

Make me a simple girl.
Will you still love me,
If you don't make me a Goddess?
