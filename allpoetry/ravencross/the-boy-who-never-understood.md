title: "The boy who never understood"

It was so confusing,
Way to complicated, too many twists,
For such a young mind.

He was the boy who never understood,
When Mommy told him Daddy moved on.
He was the boy who never understoood,
Why Daddy would ever want to leave.

The laces seems pulled so tight,
So perfect, Free from Flaws.
Little boy never knew.

But it always felt like Mommy loved Daddy,
But Daddy didn't even send his child support.

He was the boy who never understood,
Why Daddy moved away with someone else's Mommy.
He was the boy who never understood,
Why Daddy was calling that little girl his baby.

He was the boy who never understood,
Why Daddy packed up one day and went away.
He was the boy who never understood,
Daddy wasn't coming home again.
