By way of setting a scene,
Kitchen, table, bacterial gleaning,
Burnt breakfast with frozen coffee and
Vitimins.

Our subject felt a slight guilt,
As he sat back for one last morning,
Day; one last concession,
He threw back his head.

Swallowed the tablets which hit his brain
Like a guided missile.

The diode, the transistor;
The neuron that never fired,
8.0 mgs of alprazolam, xanax;
And your brains a lost and found.

[electrophysiological relationships between cognition and volition are
chemical]

Sinking back into the car seat,
Devil red and torn but cold,
He rolls back the embankments of sedation;
Peering at the tele-spectral image of a dead world.

[Or a world obsessed with death]

Symptoms of a secular society:
When we have given up on the idea of God,
A booming economy of 'life prolongers',
Smile from every billboard.

A mania for dietary supplements,
Personal trainers and multivitamins,
An ardor for herbal remedies and
Weight programs.

The cawing of crows;
The tired anorexic daughter and
The biologist mapping DNA strands.
