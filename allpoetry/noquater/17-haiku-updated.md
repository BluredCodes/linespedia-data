These were written over a period of some months.  Some of them are by a friend Michael.  We wrote them at work for something to do.  There are seventeen of them, some of them are humor, they are not meant to be related to each other in any way.  Hope you enjoy them.

periphic car tire
burns aside Texan orgy
waiting to go out
	
Japanese porn star
awe struck by my wool car seats
burns sugar cane tears

papyrus morning
anaesthetized against the port
of all that’s calling

beyond my windshield
All of the space between us
Becomes squashed bug juice

every time I look 
at mirrors all I see is
a mirror strangely

I didn't write these words
because I am a small owl
and we can't write words

tranquillised mantis
spider lives in the dark shed
when summer exploded

cigarettes and lime
remind me always of time
yet to pass us by

my dear love fear not
I never left my chamber 
pot on the car floor

I will protect you
from cockroaches even if
they are made of bark

lake Disappointment
pissing with my new hip flask
autumn is charming

I gut common gods
invoke amethyst storm clouds
wrath eyed drunk mister

the somnambulist
wet marsh road, green amber glass
split heels for a sip

I have a fortress
a pleat coat, chicken coup shack
lank hair sick of brain

Mike wrote:

to this I confess
baiting the cheese trap with mice
sheepishly grinning

all lines lead away
painting insides of balloons
circling inwards

I lost my lunch money
then I found my appetite
they would have been friends
