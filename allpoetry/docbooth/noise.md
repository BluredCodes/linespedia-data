Noise

Big noise,
Banging on the ear drum,
Beating out its tom-tom message 
Crashing and jarring on some inner nerve.
Little noise,
Tapping softly
Whispering in the ear like a wisp of wind.
Crying noise,
Bleating in the distance
Catching the ear of an anxious mother.
Laughing noise,
Dancing round the head
Skipping a merry message to a happy soul.
Angry noise, burly noise.
Little noise
Crashing noise,
Banging noise.
        Shouting, laughing
        Weeping –
        All is noise.
