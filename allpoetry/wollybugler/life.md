Life can be beautiful,
Life can be a bitch.
One day is beautifully bliss,
One day is a damn darkness.

It is like a bed of red roses,
Loving and caring, and graceful.
It is also like a bed of serpants,
waiting for a good kill.

sometimes life is so wonderful,
and you just want to embrase it.
sometimes you just want to 
take life, and squash it dead.

But, overall you learn to 
cope with everything you
have been given, whether it
is good or bad.

In the days of my life, 
anyway...
