Tomorrow will bring a lot
Of sunshine and sorrow
It will bring a beautiful breeze
Or it will bring a blazing storm

Storms of tomorrow are 
As bad or worse than ever
Sunshine of tomorrow can 
Bring beautiful happiness

The loneliness of the storms
Can show many storms hatred
The love of the sunshine
Can show beautiful bliss

Even though everything that 
Is around us everyday my
Have the consequences of 
Tomorrow and what it may bring!
