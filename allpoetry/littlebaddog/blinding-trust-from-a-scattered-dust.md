~~~ 
External crust 
of blinding trust 
withdraws 
from span deployed 
Edges 
curled 
its smoke 
unfurled 
unleashed 
but not destroyed 
~ 
Prediction gleams 
within our dreams 
withdrawn 
deploys our fears 
Sobered 
fooled 
with emotions 
ruled 
then stripped 
by the strength of tears 
~ 
Wisdom draws 
from unending flaws 
utilized 
to the wound we're sealing 
Shrinking 
scars 
in effect 
requires 
adding worth 
to the course of healing 
~~~I think we've all experienced emotional scars ...
