---
title: "'beyond 20/20'"
writer: "christy"
slug: "beyond-2020"
source: "AllPoetry"

you are the eagle
always focused
lense to brain.
you see 180 degrees of 
brilliant distance,
a lover of detail
never failing to 
be aware of the 
slightest movement
difference
absence.

thought it was perhaps a gift,
some beautiful abnormality,
a genetic trait passed 
in cracked blue ice
to lay in the heads of
our children.

you told the story
face twisting into fear,
body language turning childlike,
lashes dripping memories until
you were eleven again and
shrinking away from the strap
biting your back,
face forced into the mattress
by the daddy monster who said
you should have payed 
closer attention.

your clarity was
payed for with skin and blood.
i couldn't save you then,
but today i can start by
allowing you to, 
for once,
close your tired eyes.
