---
title: "'Slumber'"
writer: "christy"
slug: "slumber"
source: "AllPoetry"

Sleep my child
and make this a time
for sweet release.

Bathe in the warmth
of bubbling dreams
and recapture some peace.

Taste the feast
of tranquil darkness
laid upon your table.

Free yourself
to float a while
now that you are able.
