title: "'Indifference'"
writer: "christy"

The late sky is warm,
blushed like
naked shoulders beneath 
gorging eyes.
Soon night will emerge,
stretch, and hover as a
butterfly discarding
the golden cocoon.

wings glossy with the
blood of time
will paint 
the air with 
memories, tragic moments
waking as 
thirsty vampires
who forget these veins
ran dry long ago.
