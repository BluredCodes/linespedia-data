---
title: "'Your Turn'"
writer: "christy"
slug: "your-turn"

Shock of neon pink 
burning shadowed sky;
a perfect backdrop for
nearing vultures
mad with the stench
of bleeding.

Closer and closer they come;
ravenous hunger 
flaming their eyes.

Whip of inky feathers,
like a ticking bomb,
grows louder with
each shrinking second.
Panic pricks  
a dying heart.

Funny how this vision paints
me with sinful laughter,
strokes an evil
inner flesh,
as I watch them prepare
for descent.

One last look back
and then I step aside,
for this time, my dear,

they have come for YOU!
