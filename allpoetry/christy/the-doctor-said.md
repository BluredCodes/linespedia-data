the doctor said
for seventy three dollars
i can inject you with darkness,
fashion and paste you a silly 
smile and empty eyes to
greet him with, but you'll need
a driver to transport you
to your own bed
where his scent lies like
lead in the frame.

when you wake, 
take these little pills
in moderation
to mute the thunder. i can 
give you numbness and cloudy 
glass to look through.  i can
give you smoky grays and
whispers, i can
give you peace in needles 
and childproof bottles
of goodies, but
i can't 
give you back the soul
stuck to the 

bottom of his shoe.
