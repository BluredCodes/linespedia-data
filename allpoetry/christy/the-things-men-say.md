title: "'The Things Men Say'"

I see
mischief dancing behind 
your lids, round the curl of
your lips; latent yearnings
turned plunderous in
my company.

I breathe.
Your scent slakes my senses.
You are spicy winter rain
endlessly sluicing, rising,
becoming osmotic. 

You touch,
devilish whisper on
skin, reaching for some naughty
thought to slip through your mouth,
a chocolatier's sweet
utterance.

"You feel so good
I wish I had more than 
two hands".
