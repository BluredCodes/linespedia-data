---
title: "'After I've Gone'"
writer: "christy"
slug: "after-ive-gone"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/24260-After-Ive-Gone-by-christy"
---

When the sparrow sings atop my headstone,
will my face, your mind still see?

When the worms have burrowed through these cold bones,
will you still remember me?

When the years pass by and these days are long gone,
will our love, your lips then decree?

When gray streaks your hair, will my voice carry on,
or will your heart set my memory free?
