Night is a sadist,
growling and writhing;
a hungry lover who
opens herself across

the heavens,  
where colors meld and blur 
into a darkly painted fresco
looming overhead like a 
restless vulture.

Even the moon is 
a silver snake,
coiled and hissing above
the rains...
rains that shiver  
the length of your spine 
and douse the stars to 
milky smoke.

Somewhere in the thunder 
you are trembling, 
burning light.
