title: "'Broken'"
writer: "christy"
slug: "broken"
source: "AllPoetry"

you said..... 

goodbye 

with as much warning as if you'd 
pulled the grenade pin 
and yelled, "RUN!".  it was the shock 
of being set afire in your sleep, 
sentenced to death without a trial. 

but i really don't remember the shape 
or sound of your reasons, 
don't remember the voice in the 
dryness of your eyes, 
don't know if i was sitting or standing 
at the time or if you smothered me with
your stare. 

i only recall the explosion 
of a train breaking and derailing 
within my fleshy hull, 
wanting to snuff out the light 
in my head, give myself 
to the worms, 

and the woman-scar that throbbed 
beneath my fingers, 
signifying the untimely death 
of a womb sacrificed to give you  
the babies who watched sadly 
as you left, 
the babies I would have to breathe for 
when they can't understand  

goodbye.
