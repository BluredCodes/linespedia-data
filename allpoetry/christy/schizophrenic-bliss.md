(in loving memory of my days as a psych tech at the state hospital)


I met my other side today.

She said, "Life is easy
when you just don't give a shit".
(I think we're gonna be good friends)

She said, "Smiles come a dime a dozen
in my world".

I said, "Cool, I've got some change".
