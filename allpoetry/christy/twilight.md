The sun is mango lava
flowing down
a purple cone.
Darkness wakes
to peer through ten thousand 
blinking eyes.

Evening's scent descends,
moving wolf and owl 
to sing as Luna
strokes their heads
with silver fingers.

Crape myrtle ignites
its amethyst torch
drawing curtains of insects
in gray chiffon,

and night and day
merge briefly
like two lovers
stealing time.
