Just standing there blowing smoke rings
without a care in the world;
cigarette dangling between chipped nails
and daisies in her eyes.
She breathed contented sighs.
Weathered sandals strapped on her feet
to match her ratty bag;
she takes another drag,
cuz life to her is sweet.
A smile plays on her lips,
and there's a lightness in her stride,
for life is just a ride
and she's ready for the trip.
