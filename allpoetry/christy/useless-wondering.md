title: "'Useless Wondering'"
writer: "christy"

He pops the top on another beer
still marinating
in last night's urine.
Sweat beads on his brow
like seeping venom,
and he takes another sip
of the child support 
he never intended to send

...wondering why he never sees his daughter.

She lies still
as the last clean diaper is fastened
with hands tired from a second job.
And, while mom looks in the mailbox
for the check that never comes,
a child fumbles with makeshift toys

...wondering why she never sees her father.
