---
title: "'Dead Again'"
writer: "christy"
slug: "dead-again"

Lies are your wicked children.
They crawl from your 
pretty mouth to
maim and claw and
pound my skull
till I'm crushed to heaps of
ashy bone.

Did my eyes ask too much?

Did I summon them here to
murder me again?

For days I have been 
rotting in death, 
yet, my chest is still
thumping
........rising
..............falling,
and while recumbent in
the open tomb of day, I think

you could have at least 
closed the fucking lid this time!
