---
title: "'Last Night'"
writer: "christy"
slug: "last-night"

last night we sucked dry 
the liquid moon  
with our eyes and skin and tongues
till we gasped neath
the silk of darkness. glowing
and giddy, you gave to me
your gifts of freedom and 
blank pages for rewriting myself.  
you winged my mind with 
white feathers, turned gravity to 
a weak whisper void of language.

last night you set me 
adrift on a sea of nothing 
and everything, let me dissolve
into warm fluid, made me careless
and alive.  there, in the graveyard  
of questions, you showed me 
a mirror that for once

knew my face.
