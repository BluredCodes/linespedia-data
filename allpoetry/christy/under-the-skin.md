You said:
'Look beyond these many faults,
scrape the ugliness away.

Reach inside to find the man
who only looks this way.

Peel back the insensitivity,
dig past the selfish lies'.

So, these things I did in search of you,
but found to my surprise

that nothing lay beneath the filth;
there's no one there to see

just as YOU will find this time
when you reach for me.
