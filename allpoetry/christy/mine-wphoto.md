(for Skylar)

I am
swallowed up in the belly of
his love,
fuel to the blue flame licking
sclera,
the one possession he can never seem
to share.
I am 

the watermelon slicer.My son's two favorite things in the entire world are me and watermelon...too bad life can't be that simple for the rest of us...hehehe!
