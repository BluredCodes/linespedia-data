wanted to touch you 
as you slept
curled, unshowered, still wrapped
in wrinkled work clothes.

wanted to whisper something 
unforgettable,
draw focus, shock you into love 
again,

wanted to break something
carelessly, rousing you not
so accidentally, a chance to 
relieve my disappointed skin.

wanted to see life blink in
your bottomless lakes, now
stilled in the strange shadows 
of our bedroom.

wanted you to want
what i need to live again,
wanted to slaughter the sandman
with our rusted daggers of fire.
