title: "Still"
writer: "christy"
slug: "still"

Fate is a moody bitch,
giving you to me , taking you,
giving you, taking you.

You are always within me
rising and burning like
tears that never surface.

Funny how everything has
turned bittersweet while 
our dreams are still 

lukewarm. Your heart,
black as a murder of crows,
thumps in my stomach

wanting to know more
than I am allowed to give,
but will anyway to

your mind, your eyes, 
your hands, whatever 
reaches for me,

surrendering to that
which stirs me, haunts me,
promised me tomorrow.
