title: "'Talking to Myself'"
writer: "christy"
slug: "talking-to-myself"

Sometimes when I ache,
when I fall face-first
into the ass of anger,
thoughts gush passed my teeth
drowning ghosts.

My voice is wind whipping
flame, earthdark tongue
cracking stone.

I speak the words to taste them,
practice their parade in
rusty metal,
gratify my ears with wicked
hisssssssses.

I spit them at memory, 
at nothing, at a reflection
where smoke trails 
blacken lips before 
they are once again 

swallowed into my brain
for safe keeping.
