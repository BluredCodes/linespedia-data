You clot the veins 
of a gray world
where bones won't bend 
and blood knows 
no apology,
where remorse is vague 
and wakeless, and fear is a
shiny tool.

You are an evil child
with a begging cock,
you stink of hate and lies
and dirty hands raping
flowers.

Shhhhh...
I'd like to play with you,
maybe hug you with the 
bumper of my SUV,
caress you with gasoline kisses,
drop a match.

I'd like to show you
how it feels to be
the magnolia, flesh
spoiling flesh dark as death,
dark as her face 

burning at your hand.This was written after interrupting a man beating up his girlfriend over the weekend.  Let's just say he won't be smacking anymore ladies for a while.
