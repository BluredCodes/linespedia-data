funny how the slightest detail never escapes you.
i think that's why you loved me and maybe
why you left me too.  but, last night 

you quizzed me relentlessly about strange 
tire tracks in the mud-soft driveway 
(perhaps the meter reader?).

you observed smutty remains powdering
the fireplace that i ALWAYS sweep spotless
(sorry, proccupied with our separation).

you even commented on the flowering
tops of the queen's tear towering on the porch
(said they needed a splash of water).

so, how the hell did you not notice the 
frost glittering on my lips 
when you kissed this corpse you once called

wife.
