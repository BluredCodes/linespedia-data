My love for you.
I wrote tihs poem when i was 13..my second powm. I think it shows, but thought i would include it anyways.



I look into your bright blue eyes, 
And all I see is love, 
It feels so very good to me, 
Like the heavens are above, 

Then I start to talk to you, 
And it ends up in a fight, 
But all the thoughts of losing you, 
Make me see the light. 

Then we kiss and talk again, 
We know we'll always be best friends. 
"Cos this love, 
Will stay forever, 
And if it left, 
Our hearts would sever. 

I think that you have, 
Lots of proof, 
That when I say I love you, 
I'm speaking the truth, 

But my one wish, 
Is don't ever leave me, 
As my love for you, 
Will always be. 

There's one more thing, 
I'd like to say 
I love you till the end...the very last day.
