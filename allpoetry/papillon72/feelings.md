Feelings(by Folma)




Of young Jasmine, so pure and sweet, 
how I think of you, when I bed down to sleep. 
Of the pain you must fight, every day, 
how I wish I could help, in any way. 

You inspire me, with your battle so long, 
your imagination rife, and mind so strong. 
The words that you write, can only astound, 
and my love for you, knowing no bounds 

Because we live so far, the miles divide 
the yearning for you, I can not hide. 
It is not pleasures of flesh, that I seek, 
just wanting to know you, and finally meet. 


There is nothing I own, that I would not impart, 
to spend just one day, with you sweetheart. 
You taught me well, with your words that I cherish, 
that life is to short, and your love so precious. 


I feel your pain, when you tell me you hurt, 
and I am sorry dear Jasmine, if I am being to curt. 
You have broken down walls, that I put up around, 
and for the first time in my life, a true love I have found. 


So please dear sweet lady, all I ask of you, 
is to continue the fight, as I no you can do, 
With the love and help, of your family and friends, 
you can beat this dear Jasmine, please let it not end.
