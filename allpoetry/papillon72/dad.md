This is the first poem i wrote...i was 13 and....well its self explanatory.My dad disappeared for the next 4 years till i finally tracked him down. I had actually lost the third verse and today when i was messing with it, the verse was just right there in my head.WOW! I have pained over that for years.

I always wished, 
From the very start, 
My Dad would leave, 
My parents would part, 

When it did happen, 
My heart set a whirl, 
My Daddy was leaving, 
I clammed like a pearl. 

I went in my room,
I cried and i cried,
I felt almost like,
A part of me had died.

Now all I can say 
Is that I miss you Dad 
And memories still haunt, 
Those wishes I had.
