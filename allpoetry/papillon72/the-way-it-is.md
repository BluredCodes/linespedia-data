The way it is(by Folma)




From the day I was born till the day I will die 
a biker from birth, the apple of my grandpas eye. 
It's in the blood why we are what we are 
and thats why we roam so near and so far. 

We don't expect you to understand how we live 
for it's our lifestyle and ours to keep, 
We seek nothing from you and your lives of burdon 
to us your lifestyle sucks, thats for certain. 

So if you see us with our hair in the wind 
look at our faces and check out the grin. 
it's the pleasure we seek as we just cruise along 
on our rythmical beasts singing their own song. 

You look down upon us with your eyes of horror, 
but if you knew us you'd know we are men of honour, 
for honour is the code by which we must all live, 
for to break the code, we would never forgive. 

So when you see us riding in packs, 
as we carve up the road with the sun at our backs 
we are riding to live and living to ride 
upon our hogs that we take so much pride.
