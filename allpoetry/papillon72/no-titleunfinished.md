I wonder if you feel
'Bout us the way i do!
Does your heart skip a beat,
The way mine does for you?

When i know you're coming
Around to spend some time
if not only for those moments
You might just be mine.

Do you hear the phone ring,
And hope it will be me?
Do you smile the way i do,
When my name is on the screen?

Do you get a funny feeling,
When you're on youe way?
Bubbling on the inside,
not knowing what to say?

Do you hear a song, 
That sends your mind away,
To a time when we were together, 
Only just the other day.

Do you often wonder,
where this is leading to?
Or is it just the way you said,
" No committment here from you!"

It doesn't really matter
What your answers are,
'cos thats the way i feel,
Even if its from afar!

I'm not really sure,
Exactly what it means.....well i have since found out that he never felt those things...still dosnt, but im waiting right now to see if hes coming over tonight! Better the devil i know...i hope!
