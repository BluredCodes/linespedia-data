---
title: "Lonely without Love"
writer: "English Rose"
slug: "lonely-without-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/31623-Lonely-without-Love-by-English-Rose"
---

My world is so desolate and lonely
No arms to hold me tight,
No gentle voice to soothe the night

A whisper of regret fills my mind
Of what has passed before behind

I think of many times alone and hope the day will come of sweet repose
I need a love of such a might to fill the void of pity and spite
