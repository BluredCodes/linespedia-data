---
title: "Love's Spite"
writer: "English Rose"
slug: "loves-spite"
source: "AllPoetry"

Abandoned love, no place for it to go
No one had wanted her, he had said so
Was he right?   I wish I didn't know
He invades my mind and soul

As he walks with his new love,
I can finally give up hope, 

How stupid to think he would
How stupid to think he could
