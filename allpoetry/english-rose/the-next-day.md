---
title: "The Next Day"
writer: "English Rose"
slug: "the-next-day"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/31681-The-Next-Day-by-English-Rose"
---

Maybe I was wrong about you,
you came into my life like a bullet from a gun
bringing my dreams to reality, my life into view
With promises of love you seduced me
With kisses deep and long you made me yours
All night we kissed and all night we played like lovers on a shore

      You are my present but not my future
       You drift like leaves on the floor

Knowing this was true, I still loved you
Through heartache and tears I believed your lies
My love for you ran true

Next day you went away to another love
who cared and treasured you.   

      My love for you died that day.

But the next time you took me to where we used to lay,
you stopped me from hating you.

My love for you grows strong, but just until the next day.
