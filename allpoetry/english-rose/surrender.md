title: "Surrender"
writer: "English Rose"

It happens to me nearly every day
A new influence comes my way
Full of promises and ideas,
it makes me believe everything it says

I hate my life, my dreary days
my work,  my play
I wish it would all just fade away

One happy moment to be free from life

Free to do my heart's desire
Free to dance in Hell's fire
Laughing at the world's plight
I surrender and give up my fight

The Devil can have me
He said he will set me free

To live , to die in Ecstasy
