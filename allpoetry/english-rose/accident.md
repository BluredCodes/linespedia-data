I nearly lost him today
the love of my life
the man of my dreams
I nearly lost him today

The phone rang like any other time,
this is the paramedics, a problem with Chris
car in a ditch all smashed in

My breathing become laboured
my head spun around
tears rushed down my face 
so quickly they barely left a trace

Is he ok? I pleaded with them
Does my love still beat strong?
I just knew there couldn't be anything wrong

And then came a voice so familiar
sounded vague and unsettled
Everything fine, I love you 
The tears got worse

To the hospital I went.My husband was involved in a car crash today, really makes you realise how fragile life is and how important love is.
Update 3 Feb my husband is safely at home with a broken wrist and concussion.  The car, unfortunately is dead :(.
