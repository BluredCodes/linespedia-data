Alone with my baby
feeling left out of life
I ponder my future 
as a mother and wife

Should I return to my career
where challenges always appear
Or should I sit here every day
and wonder my time away

Motherhood can be so boring
The days melt into one
Hours of playing the same old game
Can this really be fun?

It's a tough decision to make

My child needs her mother

But I need a break!Written today, when I was feeling low and sorry for myself.  Since giving work to care full time for my daughter I have fely very isolated.
