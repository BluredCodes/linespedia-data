title: "Our life together"
writer: "English Rose"

A mixture of pleasure and pain in a fusion of love and hate
Violence and the smell of alcohol on his breath
The verbal abuse on his tongue and his mind
Feeling like a piece of merchandise in a shop window

        No Love
        Only Hate

Feelings of resentment rose in my blood
Pictures of happy families came to my mind as I watched his hand beat down
The fear of approaching danger fills my thoughts day and night
Screaming and shouting are his only form of communication

Mother, Mother what is happening?
Crumbling down are my dreams for happiness
Father, Father why are you doing this?
You came in the night to beat me into loving you
Love, Love what does it mean anymore?
Hatred is far safer

Worthy or Unworthy, no time to tell
You have made my life a living hell.
