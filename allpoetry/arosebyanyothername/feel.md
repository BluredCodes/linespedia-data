Don't lie to me anymore
It's you that tore
So many holes in my heart
I'd wish we'd never be apart
Now i wish that I had never known you
I'm a  fool for believing that you'd be true
You've told me nothing but lies
And that I can do nothing but despise
Although I'll love you forever
On a scale beyond every measure
I wont let you hurt me any longer
For I'm not dead, only stronger.
