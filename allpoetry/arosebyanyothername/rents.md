Why do you treat me like a child?
Why can't you see how I've grown?
All these feelings inside me have been reild.
I'm not allowed to speak in a certain tone!
You ask why I want to leave.
Isn't it obvious that I'm going insane?
The way you treat me is hard to believe.
Why do you feel that you must keep me on a rein?
I have to get out of this house!
I need to be on my own.
I feel like I'm caged, some sort of mouse.
There's even a curfew for when I talk on the Phone.
I need to be independent.
Although I realize the responsibility.
I'm going to have bills, lots of things like rent.
But now I know, I'll be free to be me.
