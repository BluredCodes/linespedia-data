---
title: "Music."
writer: "arosebyanyothername"

I could listen to the melody,
For all eternity.
I love the harmonies that I hear.
It absolves all of my fear.

I love this symphony.
The notes are poetry.
Music wipes away all the pain.
My passion for music shall never wane.

I'm consumed by the beauty,
It's all I can see.
The notes cicle around,
The music does surround.

I feel like I'm in paradise
If I had nothing else, It'd suffice.
There will never be 
Anything that means more to me.

For we have all been blessed.
We have been made a guest.
To the choir of angels singing.
Filling up all our being.

I could never ask for anything more
Music makes my heart soar. 
High above anything that you could create.
My love of music goes far beyond any fate.

Nothing is stronger.
The more I hear the longer,
I wish to stay
I will never be turned away.

Music is a bond that holds all of us together.
It makes the world and everyone even better.
For after hearing it's perfectness,
We realize our selfishness.

And we grow more 
then we've ever have before.
Music makes me whole.
And it completes my soul.
