title: "You're love...for someone else."

I look at you
And I see your pain
I love you too
But it's not the same
If only you knew
What was going on in my head
I know it's true
But it's not what you said
All these feelings are coursing through me
I feel so much it's hard to explain
I only wish that you could see
The reason why I'm going insane
I know that you're not mine
And I can't pretend you are
I realize now that it's time
Because your love for someone else surpasses me by far.
