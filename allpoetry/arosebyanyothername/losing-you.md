title: "Losing You."
writer: "arosebyanyothername"

I see you smile,
I wish I could stay here a while.
Although I wish it to be true,
I know I can't be with you.

I don't know what is real.
All I know is how I feel.
I want to be 
In your arms 
For eternity.

How long can I deal with this?
When all I long for is one last kiss.
I need your love.
To carry me up above.
I am crashing down
Hard upon the ground. 

When I see,
What's right in front of me.
The reality!
Is that you don't love me.

Trusting you was a mistake.
I wish the words I could take
Back from you.
You let me down too!
