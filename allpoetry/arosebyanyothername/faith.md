---
title: "Faith"
writer: "arosebyanyothername"

How do we differenciate
Between the love and all the hate?
I know there's something bigger than me.
The love of God has set me free.
We all have conflicting ideas
But they don't have to end in tears.
I know you many not agree
But life without God, I just can't see.
I feel His presence deep inside.
In the deepest place where I often hide.
Something inside my soul always knew
That God loves me and will always love you too.
You many not agree with what I say
But your ideas I dare not attempt to sway.
I'm sorry that you may not feel
All the love that I know to be real.
For I know I am made complete
Maybe in the next life, we may meet.
I do wish this to be true
For I have an emense amount of love for you.
To tell you of love is why I write.
Love so strong, beyond touch, taste, and sight.
