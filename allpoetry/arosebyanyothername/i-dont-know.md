---
title: "I don't know."
writer: "arosebyanyothername"
slug: "i-dont-know"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/53522-I-dont-know.-by-arosebyanyothername"

Why do you ask me so many questions?
Why do you assume I know everything?
I don't know anything!
I'm not smarter then you. 
Never said I was.
You think I just know everything off the top of my head.
I don't pretend to have it all figured out.
So why do you think I know.
I'm just like you.
Confused and messed up inside.
I got my own problems
Without dealing with yours.
I help you all I can,
But you still want more.
I don't know what else to do.
I'm not your mother.
Stop making me crazy.
You think I have nothing else to deal with!
I don't have to deal with my nonexistent father.
My screwed up joke for a mother.
No, I don't have to deal with my delinquent sister.
You think I have the perfect life.
It's not that simple.
I'm just better at hidding it then you.
So stop comparing yourself to me.
Stop making me feel guilty. 
I have too much pressure without more from you.
Just give me a break...and stop complaining cuz you don't have to deal with half of the things I do.
