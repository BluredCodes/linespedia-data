---
title: "Know the End"
writer: "arosebyanyothername"
slug: "know-the-end"
source: "AllPoetry"

I always kill myself for you,
To myslef I'm never true,
Should I die to myself?
Or triumph over your ideals?
I'm tired of pretending to be
Something that I never will become.
I refuse to continue this falsefied life.
Cuz your not worth the pain and strife.
If I do not stop becoming what you want me to be,
Then I will lose who I really am.
I will never return to what I was before you.
I can't waste my time
Trying to figure this out.
If you don't love me without these lies,
Then there's no reason for me to be here.
I need a love that's worth everything.
A love that I would die for.
A promise that is never-ending
With a perpetual allure.
We will never be this way 
Although we did try
Now is the time I must say
Good-bye.
