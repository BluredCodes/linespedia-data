---
title: "Move on....not forget."
writer: "arosebyanyothername"

I'm feeling the present slip away
Everything around me fades.
Although I wish upon the brightest star,
I know I can't make you stay.
I can't undo what I've done
and I can't make everything alright again.
I don't know if it's my fault or yours
So I'll just say it was no one's.
Memories will live and never die.
Just hearing your voice makes me fly above the clouds.
I wish and I wish...
It seems as if that's all I ever do.
For we can't go back and undo the past. 
Yet I know we can't live on in the future. 
It seems our paths have been joined,
However, now must part.
Maybe I will see you again sometime,
When we've matured and made our seperate lives.
But I know, even then,
I will love you 
There will never be a moment when I think of my life
At this current time and not think of you.
Even if I fall in and out of love 
A million times, 
It will never be as sweet as your love.
For you have been there 
And I have given up
I was too blind to myself
that I didn't realize how I was hurting you.
So before we both get hurt any more
We will move on...Yet we will never leave the love we have.
