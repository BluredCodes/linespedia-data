---
title: "Bittersweet."
writer: "arosebyanyothername"

We have lived here most of our lives.
We can tell you anything you want to know about each other.
But now it's time to break these ties.
For now we are moving further and further.
It's time for us to move.
Discover what makes us different.
My tears you have soothed.
We must adapt to our new enviornment.
Our lives here in our little town are full of memories.
They will live in our minds and we well cherish them.
I will never forget they way we used to tease
Each other, and little notes we used to send.
Laughter fills my face right now.
But soon enought it will be filled with saddness again.
To lose our friendship I refuse to allow.
Know that you will always be my friend.
You have a special place in my soul.
And I will never forget our long conversations about nothing.
But like in Cinderella, the bell now does toll.
We can hear nothing now but the sound of everyone crying.
Althought this is a happy time, it's all too bittersweet.
For as much as I want to leave this place, I want to stay as well.
In our lives, I know again we shall meet.
Therefore, I will not say goodbye, See you soon is all I will tell.
