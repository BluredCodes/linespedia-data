title: "Take a minute and listen to yourselves."

Why can't you stop fighting?
Don't you realize we're supposed to love one another?
It doesn't matter who's wrong or right.
The only thing that matters is that we're there.
I can't pretend I don't see the hurt in your eyes.
I refuse to cover up the pain I feel inside.
The pain in your voice, burns in my ears.
I want you to love each other.
Don't put me in the middle of it any longer.
I love you both,
And I wont live like this.
If you don't care about each other enough to listen
Then how can you claim that you are capable of love.
It's impossible cuz if you loved anyone, 
Then you would see how much torture you are causing each other.
We don't have much time, 
Can't you see she's dying.
Don't be blind,
Cuz time doesn't care.
You wont have another chance, 
So don't let this go on 
How can you let this continue.
Would you just listen to each other.
There's so much pain
But we give up now.
We've been through so much already
It's such a waste to erase all of the memories we have.
You tell me that it doesn't matter 
cuz there never was any love to begin with.
I can't believe that!
Cuz I've seen your love,
I know it's there. 
You have to let go of your pride and die to yourself.
If you don't then you will lose each other
In this live and in eternity.
