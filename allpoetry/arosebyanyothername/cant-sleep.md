---
title: "Can't Sleep"
writer: "arosebyanyothername"
slug: "cant-sleep"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/52291-Cant-Sleep-by-arosebyanyothername"

On angel's wings, you fly unto me
On clouds of a memory
The smile that fills your face
Your elegance, and your grace
All this is in my head
Everything that once was said
Your love surrounds me
And all I feel is free
I love you for who you are
And I wish upon a falling star
In time I will see
my love for all eternity
