---
title: "Dreams unrealized"
writer: "arosebyanyothername"
slug: "dreams-unrealized"
source: "AllPoetry"

I heard your voice today
Although I know you're not really here
And here now i lay 
Everything is so unclear
How I wish you were with me
That I could hold you in my arms
Oh if only I could make you see
How much love for you is inside of me
You can not comprehend
The pain that I feel
I will no longer pretend
I will not turn away from all that is real
You are inside of me
You will never leave
You will stay with me forever
No matter how much i try 
I will never be rid of you
I will always feel your presence
And I will hear your voice inside of my head
I wish that this was all 
Something that i could change
But as the stars fall
From the sky
So did the love your love and I
We'll live on forever 
Wondering what happend
And why I never
Could feel your love
I was too naive
Thought love came from up above
Maybe from a fairy tale 
Oh my heart set sail
As you walked with me
I only wish I could have seen what was to be.
