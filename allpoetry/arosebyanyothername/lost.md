I'm lost in a maze,
Stuck in the haze,
Lord, I'm amazed,
That I'm still here.

I'm breathing,
It's the only thing to do.
I don't know how to cope.
Somehow, I've lost all hope.

I can't see straight in front of me.
Is this how it's always going to be?

Life and love and Happiness,
Oh How I wish.
Will these things be,
Stripped from me eternally?

I'm struggling.
I'm losing grip
To this cup of life
As I take one final sip.
