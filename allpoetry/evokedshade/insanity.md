title: "Insanity"
writer: "EvokedShade"

Confused insanity struggles from within,
Insane feelings that will begin,
The day they leave thing will be all right,
I hate it when I have to fight.

Fighting with myself and struggling to find,
Find what’s right and not hollow filling sin,
My mind plays tricks on me and I don’t understand,
Why my head feels heavy again.

Someone so close to me and someone near,
Loves me so I can feel,
The feelings within are real and true,
I can’t seem to find a way to you.

Getting past what’s in my head,
I go lay down in my bed,
Close my eyes and it happens again,
The damn things struggling within.

The harder I try to get by,
The more inside I want to cry,
Cry out everything in my head,
Wanting to be relaxed again.
