---
title: "Strange Existence"
writer: "EvokedShade"
slug: "strange-existence"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Slipping through the lines of reality,
Mind sweeping in and out of existence.

Shifting and changing the way I see,
My mind is now playing tricks on me.

I look in front of me and all I see,
Is my existence coming on to me.

From the shadows I crawl up in,
Finding the sights I once was in.

Crawling away from me I see nothing but fear,
In an instant my heart is racing near.

Near the end of all I know,
Near the end I now can show.

Shooting up straight in the air,
I'm blind to see the one that's there.

If once I was really there,
I would not see the one who cares.
