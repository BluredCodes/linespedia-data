All I see around me,
Are people who judge what they see,
What they see is not who I am,
Or who I want to be.

This is around everyone,
This I know,
It causes depression to thouse who know,
Feeling broken and all alone.

Look inside and see whats there,
And then you will begin to stare,
Once you look inside of me,
You will see whats inside of you,
Is inside of me.
