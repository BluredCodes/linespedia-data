title: "The Way I Feel"
writer: "EvokedShade"

There are many things i will do for you,
And you will wonder why,
I will do them and be silent,
And you may even cry,
But what I do for you I do it from the heart,
I will do anything for you,
You tell me you are ok,
But I think another way,
One thing will always stay true,
I am always here for you.
