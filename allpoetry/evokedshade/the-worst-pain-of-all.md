title: "The Worst Pain of All"
writer: "EvokedShade"

As the needles scrape and pierce my heart,
I can feel it pushing through,
The pain within grows and the sharp pain stabs,
Begins to pull my heart from my chest,
It wants to eat it for breakfast.

I get a shock like I'm scared,
Afraid that I have lost everything that has ever meant anything to me,
I fall back and my head hits the floor,
I wake up and I've been drug to the door.

A pain so great that moves me when I'm unconsious,
So great it could move any object in any world,
A pain you feel all around you.
