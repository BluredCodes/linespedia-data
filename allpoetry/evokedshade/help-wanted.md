something big happend today,
I got ignored and went insane.
Friends who pushed me away,
And in this moment I feel astray.
Blind they are and do not know,
The kindness inside that I begin to show.
I help as much as I can when they need it the most,
Give them adivce, guidence, and hope.
Show them that not everyone in this world is crude,
And when I need some help I get an attitude.
They say they're busy and maybe thats true,
But I drop everything to help them through.
Through the tough times in they're lifes and homes,
Tough and hurtful things people begin to show.
And when they say 'Thank You' I begin to glow,
So the light begins to flow.
Ironically when it's time for me to ask,
I'm placed on the shelf within a flask.
A flask of help when they need to take,
Indeed they are making a mistake.
When I finnaly find something to break,
I know inside it's something that acks.
I need to get out and let them know,
I need the help in which they can bestow.
Until the end of all this use,
Until the end of all the abuse,
They will always make the same excuse,
This whole time I was only used.
