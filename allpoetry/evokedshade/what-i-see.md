title: "What I See"

Shifting inside of me,
Is the world I see,
And when I look at you,
All this anger builds in me.

Though we've been through a lot,
This I now can see,
What builds up inside of me,
Is something you must not be.

Blind to what I feel inside,
Something that always will rise,
A heat of no remorse,
I feel with you as my source.

Inside all I feel is pain,
And after all this it still remains,
Closed up inside it will stay,
To you It'll always be the same.

Close to my heart you will always be,
Even though you riped it away from me,
Knowing I must get free,
Inside of me you'll always be.

Near the end of all I know,
Someone comes along and begins to show,
Shows me the brightness of life beyond,
As a reflection within a pond.

Seeing this I must believe,
That within myself I can retrieve,
The only thing that makes me relieve,
Is the past in which I must leave.refering something that happend last week.
