Lifeless Shadows and oceans of soot,
Falls from my face and covers your foot,
Dark inner strength of smothering beauty,
Taints my soul and strikes at my feet.

Feelings within strong and bold,
Comes across as I bestow,
A dismal infection inside flows,
Growing now I feel you pull.

Closer to me you find a way,
To infect me again with your plague,
From within the past in which you came,
Creeping up on me I feel within.

Chilling pools of fierce dilutions,
 Fill my thoughtless head,
A void within begins to grow,
Shaping me to believe I might be dead.

My heart grows cold as I begin to stare,
Into the void I see you there,
Heartless inspections and thoughts of grief,
Inside you feel I am your relief.

Release me from this infected place,
I see you no more and now will face,
What’s in front of me I now can see,
No more blinded I set you free.
