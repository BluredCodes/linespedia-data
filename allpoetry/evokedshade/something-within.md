title: "Something Within"
writer: "EvokedShade"

When you see the one you love,
And all your fear begins to fall,
Once that happens than you'll know,
What it is we all try to show.

Deep in our hearts we'll always feel,
The ones who make us smile,
feel warm,
And real.

A feeling so deep you can't describe,
Something inside you can never hide,
If you don't see it and others do,
When its the time, you will too.

Inside so deep this will always be,
For true feelings came back to me,
I finally found the one who cares,
The one who makes me smile,
Inside without a care.

My care for pain has now been lifted,
My lonely heart has now been gifted,
With a feeling I can't describe,
Even though I know I've tried.

Close to my heart she'll always be,
For inside us all there is someone like me,
That feeling inside you'll never hide,
And If you do you'll loose it too.

Let it be free and flow through you fast,
Feelings that are real will always last,
Even if you think it'll never be,
What's inside of you someone else will see.To my girlfriend, This one is for you. *smiles*

To everyone else..When you find the one that's for you, she/he will see your love before you. You can even quote me on that. *sticks his tongue out*
