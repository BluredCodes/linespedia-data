title: "Through With You"
writer: "EvokedShade"

Day in, Day out,
I'm oblivious to whats around me,
People speak but I do not hear,
The voices in my head are nothing but a fear,
twisting and turning aways to the left and to the right,
I had everything than it all disapeared,
You walked in the door and then turned around and now your gone,
Who I am is not who I used to be,
I was here for one reason and now its gone,
I'm through with this world and im through with you.
