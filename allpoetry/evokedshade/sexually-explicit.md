Shaved skin of soft beauty,
Sweet nectar forms within,
Hot breathing of desire,
Sex you can taste on your skin.
One gentle touch of a breast,
Brushing your chin,
Hardened movement of sin.

As the moment of happiness gets close,
You wonder if its there,
Not yet,
But all most near.

Movement like rippling water,
Smooth, Soft again.

You grab, Pull,
Touch me softly with a grin.
I can taste you now,
Taste you from within,
I can taste you now,
Your stillness is growing thin.

You move wild,
With excitement in your eyes,
Your eyes close slightly,
As i give you a surprise,
With a loving expression on your face,
You happily say to me, "again"
