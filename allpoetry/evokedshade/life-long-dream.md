---
title: "Life Long Dream"
writer: "EvokedShade"
slug: "life-long-dream"
source: "AllPoetry"

We've made it through this far
Wished for life on a single star
Inside of us we let something grow
Opening our hearts, We begin to glow

One feeling inside we can never hide
Inside us both we feel is right
We look around and see the light
Inside each other we feel it bright

For all the tears we cried
For all the fears we hide
For all the time passed
For all the feelings that last

With each other we long to be
Close to one another we will see
That soon enough you and me
Will be together feeling free

Living life not letting it pass
Being close feeling it last

For all the tears we cried
For all the fears we hide
For all the time passed
For all the feelings that last

Nothing is easy and that we know
We've made it through this far
Our feelings is what we show
To each other forever it will flow

If we don't give up we will grow
Living a life letting go of the past
The feelings we have for each other
The one true feeling, will always last
