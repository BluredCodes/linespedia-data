writer: "EvokedShade"

Every day its just the same,
I talk to you and I go insane,
Things around me are just not the same,
Life means nothing not even to her.

Around in a circle I want to vomit,
Sick depression is not even close  to how I feel at this moment in time,
When I feel the chunks begin to rise,
I just think back in time to a day I was happy and felt no change.
Sweat dripping from my brow,
I feel nothing not even now after this,
Life is just a big puddle of piss that needs to be wiped off the floor,
As I walk out the door I hear the words running through my head,
No more,
No more.
