title: "Love On My Mind"

Twenty four seven,
I feel like I'm in heaven,
Thinkin of you nonstop,
You are the only thing in my mind,
With a touch so soft and lips so sweet,
Its hard to beleave I'm still standing on my feet.

Smooth skin and hair soft like silk,
Beautiful eyes like stars that fell from above,
Smile so sweet you nock me off my feet,
The way you look at me girl gets me every time,
When you start lookin at me i stop on a dime.

Lookin right back with a smile,
A smile so bright it lights up the sky,
For a eternity of light within a smile.

Happy and so free,
Me with you and you with me,
Flying so high only the 2 of us can see,
The love between is so strong,
Even the birds sing us a song.
