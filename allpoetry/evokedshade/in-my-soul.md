title: "In My Soul"

I have a hole in my soul,
That I can't control,
And when you're around,
My life is so full.

I can't let go of you,
Even if you break me in two,
It's so hard,
Not being with you.

I have no control,
It's all up to you,
I have no control,
I want to be with you.
