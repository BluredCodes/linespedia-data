Life is a trip in wich you take,
Along the lines you'll make mistakes,
And once you learn than you will know,
What it is that I try to show.

Once a world fun and new,
Makes its way back to you,
Things in the past that you have lost,
will make its way at a cost.

The cost is the one you fear,
The one thats closest,
The one thats near.

The last one on your mind,
Like a splinter in your eye,
Fast and clear you will see,
The unknown truth shall set you free.
