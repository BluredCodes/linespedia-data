Waking up and falling out of bed

Speaking words inside my head

Unleashing what has been hiding inside of me

Feeling things you would never believe



Seeing what's really there

Sacred and pure I feel it now

Inside of everything 

and everyone you know



I am good, I am evil

And everything in between

I am the balance in which you seek

I am the words you'll never speak



With you I will always be

A balance in which you can not see

Something you do not have to believe

Always there no matter what you need



I am the things that you fear

I draw you closer I bring you near

I push you away I bring you pain

I am the balance that will remain



In everyone I stay waiting for a day

When someone discovers my existence

One day you will all see

What makes you who you are is me



I create life, I bring death

I am the chaos draped across your breast

I am the tranquility inside your head

I am alive, I am dead



Inside of you I will always be

Creating selflessness, creating greed

I am the one you will always need

In you I will always breed
