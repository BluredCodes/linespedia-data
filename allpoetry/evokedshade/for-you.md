title: "For You"
writer: "EvokedShade"

In one ear and out another,
Just like the emptyness in your head,
Shifting and changing no matter what you do,
I'll always be here for you,
I'll always be here for you.

In good times or bad,
No matter how scared or sad,
If there is one thing than this is true,
I am here,
Just for you.Wrote this when talking to my depressed friend.
