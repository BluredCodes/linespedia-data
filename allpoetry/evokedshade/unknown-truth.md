title: "Unknown Truth"

Calculating imagery pressed upon your mind,
Shows that you indeed are blind,
Blind to the fact of truth unknown.

Lifeless and limp you will not know,
What you see in front of you is me,
Showing all to you that you can not see.

Be free above the skies you call near,
For all who does not know will always fear,
Follow your heart and you will see,
That gradually what I was you will now be.
