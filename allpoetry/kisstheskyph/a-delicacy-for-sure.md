---
title: "A Delicacy For Sure"
writer: "KissTheSkyPH"
slug: "a-delicacy-for-sure"
source: "AllPoetry"

Let me be your fuschia daisy
Bloodstained and imperfect
Reaching towards innocence
And drooping under the weight of my own
Magnificence.
Brush my petals with rough affection
And I’ll give all my diluted
Honey to your fingers.
Just promise that you’ll 
Pluck my petals
