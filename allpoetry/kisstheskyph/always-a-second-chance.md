title: "Always a Second Chance?"
writer: "KissTheSkyPH"

Let’s go and fall in love again.I’ll take your breath awayAnd you’ll sweep me off my feetAnd together we’ll topple, laughing, into the sandFrom the sheer awkward weight ofAffection.Let’s go and trade hearts again.I’ll twinkle your eyesAnd you’ll paint a smile on my lipsAnd together we’ll eclipse the bright skyWith the sheer light energy ofInnocence.Let’s go and find happiness again.I’ll make you forget the worldAnd you’ll make my laughter ringAnd together we’ll drown out the gentle wavesFor the sheer sake ofHeaven.
