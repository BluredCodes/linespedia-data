I could drown in your eyes.Their clear blue depths,Wherein your beauty lies,Swallow me and steal my breathAnd bear me toUn-dying death.I could melt on your lips.Their gentle kissings,From which affection slips,Restore to us eternal sighsAnd bar our lipsFrom sad goodbyes.Fall 1999,
