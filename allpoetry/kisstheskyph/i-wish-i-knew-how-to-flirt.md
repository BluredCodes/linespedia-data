I love your faded jeansThat match your faded smile.With a heart as rich as wine,This has to be a signThat we’re meant to be together.You see my tongue subduedAnd my cheeks turning rosy huedBut you can catch a nervous eyeAnd see I’m only shySo please introduce yourself.
