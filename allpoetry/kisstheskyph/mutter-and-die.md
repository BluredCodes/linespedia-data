title: "Mutter and Die"

Suggestions of you
Are slowly causing me
To kill myself.
I don’t know
How much longer
I can pretend
I still have something--
Anything--
To hold onto
(Now that you’re not
Holding me up.)
I drove the knife
With excuses
Of freeing myself
When all I wanted to do
Is rid you of the cancer
That was
Me.
(all I’m left with)
I can’t subject you to
My
Homicidal habits
(slowing down my suicidal tendencies)
I’ll just return my 
Decomposition
To fast forward
By my own hands.
