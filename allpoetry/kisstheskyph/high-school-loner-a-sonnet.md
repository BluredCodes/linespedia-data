Let those who are by petty things enraged
Make to their slender rings of whispered news
And keep themselves in such affairs engaged
As keeps them well equipped for social cues
And while they feed their virtue to their roles
And dance around ambition tainted whims
Pass judgment on pretended mirror souls
Absorbing consequence in sun burned limbs.
But I myself will stay among the shade
Which better suits a soloist inclined
And drives back elements that seek to fade
Fair reason and the diamonds it may find.
     More happy thus immersed in lonliness
     Than those in their endeavors to impress.March 2001, This was a school assignment to emulate a Shakespearean sonnet.  I just like it.
