It's back again.
A simpler solution,
But I'm not brave enough to
Back out;
Not brave enough to be a 
Coward.
Honest thieves
Have guilty souls
When they steal bread.
Better hide the aspirin, Mother.
Wish that I could rule myself,
But I think I've spun around,
And I don't want to hurt
You.March 2001,
