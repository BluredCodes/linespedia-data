---
title: "I Don't Want To Die"
writer: "KissTheSkyPH"

I need to leave the sickly city lights behind me
And venture off where no one else can find me
Where the affectionate embrace of stone and water can’t be lost upon my ears.

I need to find a shady seat among the dancing trees
And greet a river’s constant flow while quiet on my knees
Where the overwhelming feeling of humility can’t be lost upon my heart.

I need to lose myself in the celestial blues above
And make myself a rapt apprentice to the veterans of love
Where the innocence of every divine exchange can’t be lost upon my mind.
