---
title: "Touch Me?"
writer: "KissTheSkyPH"

I believe I got lost again today
And found myself.
I’m catching demons in the
Corners of my bloodshot eyes.
I need to steady myself
And freefall through my passion
I want to be empty again
But I long to be filled beyond the brim.
I want it to hurt
I think I’d die if I could
But that would be a sad disappointment—
The anticlimax to the dead action
I’ve been living for so long.
