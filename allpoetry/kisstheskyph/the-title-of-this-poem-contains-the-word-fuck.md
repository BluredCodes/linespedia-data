Discoveries yet to be made
Stand tall in shadows of lust.
Constitutions yet to be laid
Are anchored in puddles of dust.
Is controversy yet to be played
Or is it beginning to rust?
In this world where piety's paid
How do you know what to trust?

Irreverence is our deliverance
Tear down the horizons of our potential
Religion that gave birth to ignorance
Dictates which morals are essential.

Oh my, what offensive words I have!
All the better to cure you, my friend."Take a month
and show some kindness
for the folks
who thought that blindness
was an illness that
affected eyes alone."
-Maya Angelou, "Take Time Out"
