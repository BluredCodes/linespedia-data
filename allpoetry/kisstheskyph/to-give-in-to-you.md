title: "To Give In to You?"

I found you at arm’s length.An ocean of reasons divide usA landslide of protests deride usDiscouraging feelings inside usThat echo in stillness of speechArms’s length plus one.A canyon of interest assails meAs reciprocation fails meAnd I know that the feeling that hails meIs all lonely locked up in my mindUncertainty shouldn’t dance around the impossible.dammit I'm having a confusing day
