---
title: "Can't Be"
writer: "KissTheSkyPH"
slug: "cant-be"

You can’t be 
The cloth that wipes the blood from my wrists
The food that will concede to pass my lips
The pillow that can lure me into sleep
The tonic that can teach me not to weep.
We can’t be together.

I can’t be
The rose that brings a tremor to your eye
The hope that kills your need to wonder why
The song that eases all your worldly fears
The light that guides you till the peril clears
We can’t be together.
