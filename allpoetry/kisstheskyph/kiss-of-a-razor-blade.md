Tonight the world falls apart
For the fourth time this week.
Chilled metal carresses my legs
And burns hot against my cheek
As saline rivers mirror
Red streams seeping down my thigh.
I can’t shake myself to life
But this bleeding gets me high.
When the world reveals itself 
In crazy black and white,
These scarlet stripes of passion
Can make me seem alright.
