title: "Waking Up"

Conscious first of my pillow’s kiss--
I cradle it like a lover
Whose arms I never want to leave.
Whose face I never want to see.

Peeking out between leaves of dark
(my first adventure of the day)
I see that the lesser of two evils lies in open eyes
And so I venture into the dreadful promise of a new day.
