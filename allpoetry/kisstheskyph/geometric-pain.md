Eight even incisions,
Intersecting if only they were infinite
Instead of
Segments stopping short of one another.
Can’t quite reach…
Eight angry brows of Euclid
Glare up at me
With ruby frowns.
I suppose I shouldn’t
Unleash Geometry
On my skin.
