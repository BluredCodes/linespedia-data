title: "I Hate the World"

I hate the world.
It holds the razor from my wrist.
I hate the world
Because it tells me I’d be missed.
I hate the world.
It won’t hand me a gun.
I hate the world
Because it frowns at those who run.
I hate the world.
It locks away the pills.
I hate the world
Because it mourns the ones it kills.
I hate the world.
It won’t let me fade away.
I hate the world
Because it won’t stop loving me today.
