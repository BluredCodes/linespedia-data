title: "Bleed Happily Ever After"

Bleeding into the grail of absolution
I feel lighter at this severing from your forevering.
At last I have some resolution
Where numbness doesn’t play a part within my heart.
I’d rather end my life than do nothing at all.just me playing with rhyme and thinking over the happenings of tonight.
