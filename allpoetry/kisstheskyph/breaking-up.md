I can’t trust you unless you hold a pistol to my brow
I won’t believe your eyes unless you hold my life in the palm of your hand
Prove me to myself and seal fate as if there is such a thing
Pull the trigger and I won’t be able to live without you.
If I know you can strike the final blow
I know that your kindness is by choice and not necessity.
Mercy is a powerful aphrodisiac.
Make me live to do your bidding.
Who’s eager to please when there’s no effort involved?

You’re dying to run your tongue over the
Poison of my delicious secret life.
You’re the blindfold and I’m the executioner
It’s a unanimously undesired suicide pact,
But you’ve tasted my wine and want to drink me
Until we’re both too intoxicated to see the knife I hold.

In the same boat
But on different sides of the sun
If only you knew more intimately the tar through which I pull my oars.
A shadow is cast upon my toil toward our common goal.
Before you know it my side has sprung a leak 
And my failure to notice anything at all
