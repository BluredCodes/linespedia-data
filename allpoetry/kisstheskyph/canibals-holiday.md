Fiona Apple says to save the turkeys
Martha Stewart says to stuff your apples
Up the turkey’s butt.
Gobble
Gobble
Gobble
Gulp.
The turkey’s cry of freedom
Is roasted to domestic holiday perfection
So you can give thanks to your gut.poor turkeys!!!  Thanksgiving dinner is like some Satanic ritual!
