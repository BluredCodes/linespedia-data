If you didn’t want 
A kiss to last a life time
Then you shouldn’t have
Blown me the words
That matched ecstacy
With “till death do us part”.
You never meant
To hold and to have
And now your revocation
Rings through my ears
But can’t surgically remove
These strings across my heart.
You embroidered “forever”
On my soul
And then dropped me like a rag
But my eyes still bear the crest
Of our initials.
You know I’ve always saved
My brightest colors for you.
Just let me wipe the strife from your brow
One more time…"Take thy beak from out my heart and take thy form from off my door!" -Poe, the Raven
