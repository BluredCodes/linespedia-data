Thinner and thinner, 
Letting the liquid of my soul 
Escape in any way possible. 
I can't let myself become any more infinite. 
I want to feel small. 
I want to notice the confines of my own skin 
By no longer having any. 
I'd ask you to help me take in the seams 
But I'm afraid you'd only comment 
On why life is too big for me again. 
I'd reach out a hand to you 
But I'm afraid you'd see the 
Red trickle painting my wrist. 
Soon I'll be nothing but air. 
You'll breathe me in and know everything, 
But at least then your desire to help
