Plastic bag over my headI’m suffocatingAlmost deadYou remember everyWord I’ve saidAnd fester everyWound that’s bledA leash that nowIs turning red.As you try to getInside my headWhere hope was killedWhen reason fledSo I could turnTo you insteadFor exorcismOf the dread.Reluctant slaveAs I was led.To choke the darkDefense you fed.Fall 2000?,
