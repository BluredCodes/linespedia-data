Pink shadows of the love you used to give me--
You brought two dozen to my mother,
Now a shadow of the youth I've conquered.
Are you always going to lurk in the shadows,
Trying to remind me how much I need you?Be gentle--this is just a vent of confusion and indignation, but I still want feedback.
