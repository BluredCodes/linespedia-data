I know I shouldn’t wonderAt the meaning of the meaninglessBut last night I kissed your best friendIn an image in my mindAnd woke up feeling guiltyThough you’ve already left me behind.And last month you said hello to me.I’m still wondering what you meant by that.Is there really nothingness beyondOur day to day nothings?I wonder what that means...I'm in a random mood this morning.  Whee! my cold has gone to my head!  I really should perhaps sleep more.
