A pink sparkle pierces your lower lip
And a soft blue baby hangs from your neck.
Working a hundred jobs a day,
You hate the world
But laugh as if you own the sky.
So soft that no one can break you,
You’re who I want
And who I want to be.I'm beginning to do portraits of people I encounter.  This is about a co-worker I admire.
