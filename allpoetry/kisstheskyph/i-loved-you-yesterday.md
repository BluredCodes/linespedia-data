title: "I Loved You Yesterday"

While you’re busy dancing around our problems 
I’m dancing on table tops, 
Jumping into bed with perfect strangers, 
Soaring through the stars, 
Imagining freedom 
As I trace sand dollars in the sand with my shoes. 

While I’m busy wishing away the present 
You’re putting diamonds in my eyes 
Tracing lace upon my skin, 
Weaving a cage of love, 
Digging through my ears 
As you give me those pathetic puppy dog eyes. 

I don’t love you today. 
Maybe tomorrow.
