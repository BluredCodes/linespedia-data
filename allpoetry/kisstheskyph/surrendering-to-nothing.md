If you’ll set me softly by the sea
I’ll want to drown myself--
But not for contempt of my flesh.
The visions I see in the sand swallow me whole
And I want to go to the place
Where my land meets that ever-merging
Rendezvous of elements;
Take a step toward the waves
And be digested
Into the everything that looms
Beautiful before me.
There is no ecstasy like that of
Eternity.first draft again.
