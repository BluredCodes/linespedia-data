"CALL ME IF YOU NEED ME."
"ok"
"I MEAN IT."
"i know"
"YOU WON’T BE BOTHERING ME."
"i bother everyone"
"THAT’S ONLY THE DEPRESSION TALKING."
"we share a voice"
"PEOPLE CARE ABOUT YOU."
"but i don’t exist"
"CALL ME."
"i can’t i’m broken"
"I GIVE UP."
"so do i"
"STEPHANIE..."
"sure thing..."just another prattling experiment; me entertaining myself.  this is a side note to the better stuff i'm working on, but i get random thoughts.  i'll probably come back to this some day and actually make it something other than jibberish.
