I’ve lost my buoyancy.
I’m not sure I can recover from this fall.
Give me some air.
Give me a break.
Give me a moment
To look into your eyes
And find something more there
Than absence of the spirit.
I can’t pull myself from the bottom of this well.
It’s walls are too familiar.
They cry the juice of my philosophy
And bar my fingers from
Groping their desperate way.
Toss me a line.
Toss me a promise.
Toss me a crumb of your compassion.
Think of it as your annual
Act of charity.
Dance around your motives and incentives,
But please,
Before it’s too late
Save me from
Drowning in myself.Help me help me help me.  I don't know what to do. I don't want to bleed another night away...
