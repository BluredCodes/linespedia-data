---
title: "I dont Understand"
writer: "beutifulbeuti"

I dont understand, I dont get it
Why did you say those words, if you never meant them.
I guess I love you means something different to you.
Was she good, was she pretty, did she smell nice?
I tried, I gave, I loved, I cared I still do.
It hurts so bad i cant stand it, i want you to touch me.
Tell me your sorry, tell me you love me.
Emotions, I cant stand them I dont know what to do with them.
I dont want to feel them anymore.
Make it go away, take my pain.
Just take my life.My head is a mess, i dont even know if it makes any sense?
