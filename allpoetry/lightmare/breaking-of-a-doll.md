ivory skin, slender limbsdoe-eyed, honey haired waifdancing a knife bladelooking for herselfbanishing idealsreplacing them with naughtbuffeted by hope into breakers of despairpale fingers clawing at lovereturning scarred and achingperfect little doll--broken
