Witty comebacks, a saccharine smile
bubbly perky happiness…
is that what you want me to be?
A woman without her character,
a smile that masks the pain?
I know that my poetry can scare you
yet in my life I’ve learned:
it’s too easy to be that happy persona
that masks the pain within
and now I abandon that practice
