sleepy… angry…
emotions wash over me
as I struggle to stay awake
pain (mental and physical)
my body drained
WHY DO WE HAVE TO BE FRIENDS?!?
smoothing my eyebrows
my eyelids
so sick of it
of YOU
why can’t you just let it die?
blinking, exhausted
who said we had to be friends?
why can’t you accept the idea that
things just might not work out?
letting my hair down
