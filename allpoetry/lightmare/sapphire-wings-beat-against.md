title: "[Sapphire wings beat against]"

Sapphire wings beat against
merciless crimson tongues of
dancing flame
Emerald leaves shuddering on 
chocolate limbs

Scalding updrafts the only movement
Save the silent pulsing of the
Shimmering wings
Fear thick as tar, ebony dark
Emanating, Radiating

Futile hope, miniscule waves of crystal blue
Spiraling upward
like leaves returning to the tree
randomly flitting
to avoid scarlet blasts

Higher and higher
Home destroyed
Flies the small butterfly
Soaring on the clouds
Away from destruction.
