---
title: "I am"
writer: "Lightmare"
slug: "i-am"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/5164-I-am-by-Lightmare"
---

Corrupt me
if you can
for my mind is strong
Hate me
if you want
the loss is purely yours
Despise me
as you wish
for being what you’re not
Loathe me
if you feel it
for the strength that I possess
Desire me
as you should
for everything I am
