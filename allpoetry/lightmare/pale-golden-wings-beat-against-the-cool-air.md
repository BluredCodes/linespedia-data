Pale golden wings beat against the cool air
as the small butterfly flits back and forth
amongst the crimson flowers
settling on an emerald leaf,
she stretches her wings to the sun
and basks in the warmth of the afternoon. ©  on Mar 14 2001 01:28 PM PST   0  • 10
