title: "[Praises I can deal without]"
writer: "Lightmare"

Praises I can deal without
trite stressed syllables
flowing unnecessarily from
agony stained lips
My joy is in myself
this conglomeration of
insanity
before you
Eyes and hearts
are linked;
can you see
the fire in mine?
Ivory kisses
to ebony heart
scarlet flames
consuming rancid green demons
Purging heart and soul
the glorious blaze
sets free the nymph
to dance in the breeze
Back turned to praise
Eyes closed to contempt
Watching the freedom of the forest
In the heart of this nymph.
