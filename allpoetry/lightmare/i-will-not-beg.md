---
title: "[I will not beg]"
writer: "Lightmare"
slug: "i-will-not-beg"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/310-I-will-not-beg-by-Lightmare"
---

I will not beg
my head held high
I can wait
as long as it takes
for you to see
The importance
that I place
and the way that I do
My time is better spent
mind wandering amongst
angels
Than
waiting
for dawn to find you
Yet from that realm
I shall look down
and wait for you to see
