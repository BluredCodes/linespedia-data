title: "[Fingers extended all the way]"

Fingers extended all the way
So that they don’t retract
And claw the pale skin beneath them
Pain eases the emptiness
The hollow void gets filled
The crimson stains shall fade away
And time will find the scars
Long and jagged
Across the wrists of fate
