---
title: "[Close the damn door]"
writer: "Lightmare"
slug: "close-the-damn-door"

Close the damn door
is that so much to fucking ask?
Does anyone hear me screaming
or is it just the noises in my head?
Tears to flood the oceans
welling in my eyes that SHALL NOT fall
Churning stomach
Straining hands—longing to shred
at pale flesh
in a futile attempt to rip the last
shards of hopelessness from my ragged soul
Cleansing not there
nowhere 
There has to be a way
to end the pain
that doesn’t involve death
Where is the gate to happiness?
Who stole the key?
murmurings of rage
boiling in my blood
crimson agony
scarlet torture
Waves of scathing anger
washing away the sanctity of 
peaceful soul
Find me, oh dove of serenity!
