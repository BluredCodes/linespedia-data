Friends around me run
…whilst I sit
Friends around me dance
…and I wonder
Friends around me sing
…I hold still
Friends around me laugh
…and I dream
Friends around me smile
…my body shakes
Friends around me play
…and I cry
Friends around me rejoice
…my pain leaves
Friends around me live
