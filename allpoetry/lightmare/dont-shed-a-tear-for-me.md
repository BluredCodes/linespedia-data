Don’t shed a tear for me,
don't bother
I mean nothing to you,
I’m just that quiet voice in the world
that quivers ever so slightly with boundless pain;
but don’t think about that
why ruin your day?
I’m a nobody, a nothing
something and someone you’d rather ignore
I feel the pain of this hollow life
as I look at the emptiness that surrounds me
all I ask of you
