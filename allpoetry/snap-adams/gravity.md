i want to turn off gravity
and float through your blood

your eyes are calling me in
i fell into them just so easily, it felt so right

i hope i'm doing the "right" thing
who knows what's good, certainly not me

i just want to talk all night long
i strung a piece of string, in between two tin cans, to your heart and to mine

i want to hold your hand
and talk to you, and take you out into that field, and watch the sunset, 

hold you in my arms through the night
wake up in the morning, as the sun rises, and know that you are the most amazing person i've ever met.23.2.02,
