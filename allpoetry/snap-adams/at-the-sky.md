i look into your eyes
you jump in the car
we drive till we find a field
where we can stay

we lay down
stare at the sky
at the clouds
my hand holds yours

you fall asleep
when you wake
i have flowers for you
to let you know i love you

when you wake up
i'm back again
i kiss you on the cheek
and we stare at the stars

the cold comes
the sky is dark
i want this moment to last forever
i kiss you and carry you back to the car, and drive you home
i don't want this to end10.2.02, THIS REALLY BLOWS! yes i know.
