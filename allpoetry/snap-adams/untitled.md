my memories are painted in my brain
your picture is painted in my heart
i just want to see you
you don't know how hard it is to be apart from you

i just saw you today
i want to go back already
i didn't want to leave
you're so pretty, and you make me have a bliss feeling

my blood burns
when i see you
my head is light
and my eyes don't feel heavy

and i want to thank you
you are the most wonderful girl i've ever met
you're a blessing sent to me
we both take each other for how it is

thank you.18.2.02,
