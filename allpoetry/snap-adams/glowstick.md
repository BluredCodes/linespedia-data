have you ever seen a glowstick glow?
have you ever seen a lightning bug shine and shimmer in the night sky?
you've bent me just the right way
i'm glowing inside

i don't know what you see outside on the skin
i'm glowing inside
it burns
but its great

when i see you
i have a rush
a feeling of this warm blood flowing through my vains
the blood once red, now glows, i don't know if you can see

i'm blushing i'm sure
you can see me, even though i'm not even there
i feel like i'm the sun
shining for everyone10.2.02,
