i want to get in the car
and drive away
far away

i want to start a new life
where no one knows my name
no one knows that i'm such a bad guy

where no one knows that i've messed up so bad
where no one knows that i'm such a bad friend
where no one knows that i'm such a jerk

i need to see a brand new sunset
with a brand new crew
start up a whole new life

a new beginning
a new ending
a whole new story
told in slow motion23.2.02,
