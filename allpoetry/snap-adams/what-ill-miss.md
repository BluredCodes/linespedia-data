what will I miss?
i'll miss your little things you do
the kiss on the cheek,
the while you laugh

your smile, your personality
walks in the summer rain
being carefree, looking at the stars in your backyard
the totem pole, the way i feel when i was around you

your cold hands
tickling fights
i must move on

i let go first
i broke the promise
i ended it
i gave up
