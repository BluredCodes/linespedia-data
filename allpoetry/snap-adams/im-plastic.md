writer: "snap adams"

i-feel-so-odd
i'm-broken
i'm-plastic
i'm-glass
i've-been-thrownaway
into-the-dust
i've-made-a-toy
i'm-a-toy
nothing-more
i'm-broken
i'm-dead
i'm-heated
ready to be molded
into-whatever-you-want
