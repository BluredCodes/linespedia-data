There is always that moment
Before you make the call...
Your heart pounds
as your fingers press
each number slowly,
excruciatingly painful
The very thought
of hearing his voice
As he answers the phone...
What will you say?
How will you sound?
As you baffle through 
the endless corners of your mind...
Or the very thought
of the silence you might encounter
just the sound of your breathing
or of his...
Or even worse
when all of this is built up
in your twisted little mind...
and no one picks up.
