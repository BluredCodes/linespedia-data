I love youbut you're now so confusedso I will waitas long as it takesuntil we can be togetherlike it was meant to be.There will be othersof that, I am sureBut none, will I be theirs, trulyFor my heart will not be theirsMy heart is not even mineMy heart, forever, will belong to you.And if, by some unfortunate chanceOur hearts never collideKnow that you have always had mefrom the beginning, until the endIf we can't be together,like it was meant to beI did not wait in vainfor no one could be more worthyof my heart and soulthen you, my only true love.
