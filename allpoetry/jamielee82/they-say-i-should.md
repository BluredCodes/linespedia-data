title: "They Say I Should..."

They say I should see someone
he will help me
he will make me happier
So I will go
but they don't know
that no one can help
these feelings deep inside

They say he will make
the sun come up brighter
over my gloomy heart
So I will go
but they don't know
that the sun has set
When will it rise?
Never
