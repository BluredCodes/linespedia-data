title: "Daddy"

I thought we were over
the hurt and the pain
the treats and the fighting
the tears and the rain.
When I hung up the phone
the words fresh in my mind,
I search for the answers
of which i can't find.
Do you really hate me
for the things that I say,
for the person who I am
who I've become to this day?
Did I turn into someone
that you can not bare?
Or do you still love me, daddy?
Do you still care?
