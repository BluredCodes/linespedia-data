When you had medid you cry?thinking maybeyou would dieif in the futureyou did seesomething happening...happening to meOr did you justgo alongthinking nothing...nothing's wrongWell Mother nowcan't you see that something is happening...happening to me...and now I cry cry crythinking maybeI'm the one to die.
