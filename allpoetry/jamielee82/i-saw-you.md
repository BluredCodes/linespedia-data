I saw you walking by today
I saw "you" the person I have always known
A glimpse of the boy from yesterday
A moment of time, traveling back
Back to the past 
childhood days
When you were my love
and I was yours
And life was so easy
So filled with hope
I closed my eyes
and I breathed a sigh of relief
Thinking, believing you were back
And you were,
but "he" was not
So often I forget
That life must be lived
And with experience, disapointment,
growing up...
People change
You have changed
But now I know
that I have changed too
And maybe today
You closed your eyes,
Hoping that "she" was back
But "she" is gone
And "he" is gone
And two strangers stare at eachother
trying to hold on
to a life lived long ago.
