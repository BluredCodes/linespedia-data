You let them have you
so easily
so soon
They smirk 
as they leave your bed

Did you catch 
his last name?
As he walked out of your life
forever
Did he promise you the world?
And then leave you crying?

Your better then that
Don't you know?
They see you as a prize
that they've won for their shelves

And you are a prize
to those who love you
to those who know you

But not to them
Don't give in 
resist temptation
for temporary satisfaction
replacing long term effection.
