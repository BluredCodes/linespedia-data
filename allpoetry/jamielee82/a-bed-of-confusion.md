title: "A bed of confusion"

i lay with him,
in a bed of confusion
not knowing what will
come next
Never expecting
the unexpected
Never realizing
the wasted time
The heartache

And then it hits me
A blow to the heart
right to the bottom
killing me slowly

He has to "behave" now
like I am something he 
is doing wrong.
I never knew,
that I wasn't first
Never knew that there
was another
Laying with him 
in a bed of confusion
not knowing what will
come next 
Never expecting
the unexpected.
