Today I couldn't breath
Just like that
A passing thought
A fleeting memory
and my heart stopped
My hands began to tremble
The tears began to form
A trip back through time
Feelings of long ago

But then, I realized
The future can be bright
If I believe it to be
So, I sighed...
I was relieved...
I could breath again.
