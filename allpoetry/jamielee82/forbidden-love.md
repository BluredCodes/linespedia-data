title: "Forbidden love"

Forbidden is 
this love we dare.

but why?
I ask this world we share.

because more years
you have then me?

more experience in life
more wisdom you see?

They cannot be hidden
these feelings inside.

I wish we could tell them
I don't like to hide.

When my heart is so full
it can't take much more.

'Till it bursts open wide
and then we will soar.

For the days I am with you
are beautiful and real.

Its more then infatuation
this love that we feel.

So I am young
and older are you.

this is not strange
this is not new.

One day soon
all the world will know.

That age has no limits
and this we will show.
