---
title: "My Friend??"
writer: "Jamielee82"
slug: "my-friend"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/4137-My-Friend---by-Jamielee82"
---

So close we were
that we were one
growing up together
always and forever
the bond we shared
could never be broke
so we said to each other
to make ourselves believe
that friends like us
were few and far between
never to understand
that time can change everything
can break that bond
can crush these friends
until we were nothing
just passing by
not knowing each others thoughts
that we were so adept to before
so are we enemies?
or nothing?
Or are we true friends
separated by the present,
meant to be together forever
in the future
like the past.
