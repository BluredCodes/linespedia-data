So now I sit here cryingin desperation on the floorstaring at the ceilingsearching for something more.I feel my grasp loosening on the life I once new wellI see my self crumblingin this twisted horrid hell.The desperation looming over meis one that I can't bareI look far into the futureand only see the darkness there.If no one can be trustedwith these feelings held insideMy soul will burst with all of my secretsand the tears that I have cried.
