There are so many changes
In this complicated world of mine
Am I still the same
girl I was before?
Or did I transform
into someone I don't know?
Whom no one knows...
Will I become bitter?
A victim of my circumstance?
Will my heart freeze
to any signs of love?
Merely because
I was so hurt before?
A broken spirit?
With broken dreams
of living happily ever after
in a world of perfection?
Please let me know
and accept
That I can not be perfect
This world can not be perfect

For perfection is imperfection.
