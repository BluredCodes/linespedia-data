The first broken heart, 
that I have ever known
was not from some boy
whom I pledged my love to
that wouldn't come ' till later

I remember the day, the moment,
so clearly
Him sitting at the table
telling me he was going away
Far away
I cried, I pleaded for him to stay
I broke his heart, like he was breaking mine
But it didn't work, it couldn't work
For he was already gone.

The first time that my heart broke
It was not by some guy
I thought I would be with forever
But by the man who comforted me
when I had a nightmare
THe man I thought knew everything
could do no wrong

My father broke my heart
when he left me that day.
