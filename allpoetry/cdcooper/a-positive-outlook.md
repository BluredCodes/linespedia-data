A Positive Outlook
By Christopher Cooper

It may not be right
To waste your time
Thinking of happy thoughts.
And yet I say even the darkest day
Is interrupted by the sun lights play.

Some will say all is lost
And nothing is worth saving.
They say to look at what it cost
And not to waste your time
By letting your life go on by.
But I say there is a brighter day.

They say there is no one to trust.
That no man is left upon this Earth
Who is worthy of this lowly curse.
At every turn you'll find the worst.
I say even lonely men can learn.
Yes lonely men need to be heard.

You say I'm just an optimist
One who lives life on a pray.
Someone who believes
In giving life its share.
But I say that even on cloudy days
The sun is just a cloud away.
