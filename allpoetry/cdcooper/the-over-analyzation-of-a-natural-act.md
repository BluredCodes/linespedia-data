The Over Analyzation of a Natural Act
By Christopher Cooper

Swaggering into a room
of bland floor covering
and uninspiring wall tile,
searching for the privacy
of public anonymity.
I count the wall fixtures
and carefully choose the one
where I can remain unseen.
Urgently and with purpose, I address the fixture
standing with feet shoulder width apart
one foot slightly in front of the other.
Pretending not to be aware
of my own reflection in the tile's glaze, 
I separate my clothing in an un-sexual fashion.
and start to hum.
I am a middle-age man ... I am an old fart.
The sound is needed to help in direction guidance
not to ward off unwanted suitors.
As a young man
seeing the source
and hitting the target was never a challenge.
Now I have to rely on memory
and pray the target is within reach.
The question now is can dignity be found
while your tinsel strength abates.
Pissed at my inner voice wishing forever-lasting youth.
I stop the progress of my task
and ask the stranger next to me.
"Is it my age or my prostate intellectualizing
that makes this act so complicated.
