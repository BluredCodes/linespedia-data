When a man smelled like a trade
I had the odor of charred rosewood 
With the scent of copper oxide.
If I was really good,
I could add the fragrance of a fine vintage wine
Or the frigid aroma of a refreshing monk's beer.
But if I was bad,
I would have to settle for the stench of vinegar
Or even worse, the common smell of Norwegian fish..

With the boys
Life is lived in a freeform style.
Leaving me to be the annoying scent of scotch-broom
And the sarcastic smell 
Of last night's economical adult beverage.
Even though I masked it with an aborted Irish Spring shower.
I find my aftershave only complicates it.

With the wife
I smell of sweet molasses
Warm angel food cake iced with chocolate mousse
And slices of strawberries. 
My breath is the reverent whiff of antiseptic Lysol.
And my comments are the amazing bouquet
Of lemon fresh Pledge.

How do I smell right now?
Who's asking?
