Loosing a Bout with Life
By Christopher Cooper

As I wander though lifeless existence
Waking though streets of despair 
I am a bout lacking emotion    
And a sea with waves that won’t share 

So I run to the curl of the pitied
Finding sorrow that bears the weight of the time
Every day is lost in the wind
Sing out direction to the un-hearing mimes

I ask my mother so to try to please
I hate the correctly written word
I tell my father so to try to explain
It never says what I want to be heard
 
So I join the misplaced rain washed amours
Whose colors have run down their lost faces
Remember what my mentor had told me
In life the further you reach.....

My arms are too short
and the trigger too close......
