Don't even talk about my acne
These zits make people attack me
and crack me,
They use their swollen knuckle's
Then they take their belts off and hit me with their buckle's,
Damn! My foot kills!
I see any big guys outside and I get the chills
I get diarrhea,
Blood spurts out of my ear,
This is raw fear
How am I going to have a career? 
When I cant even talk to a stranger 
I get so paranoid,
because my life is one big void
No friends.
No confidence.
No life.
