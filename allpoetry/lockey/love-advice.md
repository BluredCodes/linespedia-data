Ok so you're in love
Don't be scared - embrace it
It might not happen again - face it.
Now you have got to make it happen
Go to the girl with your cap on
Her shoulder you got to tap on.
Put your hand on your mouth and make a little cough
Now grab her arm and pull her off! 

Go down on one knee
No! Get back up she might think your silly
Just tell her you are in love with her
If she laughs at you say it was a dare
Then run off and hide anywhere.

But if she says she's in love with you
You will be shocked that came out the blue
Don't go insane or were a tutu
Just do what comes natural
Throw away your satchel
So what if people laugh at you 
Kiss her!
Ill always says you make a lovely pair
