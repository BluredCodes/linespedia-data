Today I saw a fight at school
As usual everyone crowded around - maybe that's the fight rule
I could see the enjoyment on everyone’s face
I heard the thump as it hit that lad’s face
The lad ran off - the instigator took chase
The main block was the finish line for this fight race 
As the crowed realized what was happening

They started shouting and chattering
I usually find fights so fun
Jumping on peoples backs 
I'm usually the first one 
I'm usually the first to react
Not even for a second did I enjoy this attack

I'm usually buzzing as someone gets their nose broke
And cheering as each of them put their opponent in a choke
This time I just stood there in silence
For some reason I hated this violence
The instigator was a guy named Tommy
I know next time to make sure he’s away from me 
His opponent was a tall lad - who didn't fight back 
Maybe he knew about this attack

I walked off as the crowed followed the fight
I knew that lad would bleed before the end of the night
