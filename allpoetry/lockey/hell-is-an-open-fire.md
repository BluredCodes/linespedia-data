Welcome to Hell that is the open fire
And I must admit I do tire
The days get longer and the heat from the flame
Is burning my eyes and increasing my pain 
And the height of my pain seems to get higher
Until at last I no longer tire
Because the smoke from the fire
Is crushing my lungs and making me respire
I am crying.
My body has given up... 
dying,
The flames have now realized I am lying
I know the flames won’t engulf me
My faith will protect thee
But the ghosts from the past still haunt me
And the life in front still daunts me
