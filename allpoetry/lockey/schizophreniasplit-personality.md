I’ve got split personality
Messin with my mentality
Don’t know the difference between dream and reality
You smile at me
Nod your head
You’re plotting for me to go in the padded room
I would rather be dead
You say I am sick in the head!
Oh no it's started!
My mind is going all strange
Hi I am lockey: Deranged 
Loves eating human flesh
Got to be tender 
Got to be fresh
No don’t inject me
I'll be good I swear 
Right come here see how you like me pulling your hair!
Or sticking you with this needle
I am here to scare!
Oh I'm getting a tingly feeling
What do you expect I'm dealing
With split personality
It’s breaking my mentality
Ok it's Steve here 
It's time to do things right
We can’t inject somebody in the day
It has to be done at night
Let me shed some light
On who I am now
It's Steve! 
The sensible one
A bit chubby but very strong
I know how to kill someone 
I won’t get it wrong
I won’t get caught
Throw the dead body in the seaport
Where there are sharks and fish
Don’t you understand me I'm speaking English
If they fight get a knife and stab them in the gut
If they scratch kick them with your foot
Now do you understand me? I'm not what I seem am I
Look me in they eye
I said look me in the eye! 
You will realize 
I don’t care if I die
