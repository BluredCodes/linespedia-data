Crimson Liquid 
Pours from my veins 
Red Richness 
Succulent Salt 
Dripping from wrist to tongue 
Yum 
Yum 


Dark Red Life 
Pumping 
Pumping 
From Heart to Vein 
Vein to Heart 

Crimson Liquid 
Pours from my veins 
Draining my life 
as I let go of the reins
