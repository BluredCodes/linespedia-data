---
title: "Ultimate Fear"
writer: "Blah Blah"
slug: "ultimate-fear"
source: "AllPoetry"

I fear the loss of these fingers 
the ones that play piano 
magic tunes 
spewing forth 
like lightning bolts 
my music is my release 
my closest friend 
my most loyal lover 
without these fingers 
life is bleak 
it means nothing 
for my music is my only retreat 
my only savior 
that saves me from pulling the trigger
