Why must Television suck so bad
If fifty nine channels were all I had
Every one would be so dull
There is nothing to watch at all
Even the Olympics are silly these days
With curling and ice dancing as the new craze
I can't deal with late night cable
I'd shoot the set if I were able
I flip and flip and still come up short
Last night I watched MASH and then Night Court
I long for the TV of my childhood
Only three channels, but they all were good!
