Violets are blue
Roses are red
With her sister
He went to bed

Violets are blue
Roses are red
He hurt her so bad
She wanted him dead

Violets are blue
Roses are red
She put a bullet
Through his head

Roses have thorns
His blood was red
She will live
In prison, insteadI got this idea while watching a soap.
