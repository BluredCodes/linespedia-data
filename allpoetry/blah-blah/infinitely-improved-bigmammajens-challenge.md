You taught me how to love myself 
when no one else cared 
and I was alone and crying 
wallowing in my own self pity 
You slapped me out of it 
and told me to be proud of who I am 


You held my hand 
when the world turned it's back on me 
and you said you would stay with me 
and fight with me 
because I was worth it 

You said  "If the world wants to stomp on you, 
...........then they stomp on me, and I won't 
...........let the world trample us down!" 

I will never forget that 
you made me feel like I belonged 
you made me feel like I was worth something 
you made me feel SPECIAL 

Thank you for being my lifeboat 
when I was drowning in confusion 

I love you, Grandma!
