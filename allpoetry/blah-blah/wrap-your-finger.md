---
title: "Wrap Your Finger"
writer: "Blah Blah"
slug: "wrap-your-finger"

I told you I would leave you today
so wrap a string around your finger
so that you remember why you don't see me
when I should come home from work

Wrap your finger
so you remember this feeling
as you realize I'm gone
and I'm never coming back

Wrap your finger
so you remember this day
the day I took my soul back
and I gained my sense of self worth

Wrap your finger
so you remember this emptiness
as you understand you are now alone
and you will never have me back

But most of all
Wrap your finger
so you remember how you lost me
because you didn't know how to treat me
and make sure you never treat another woman the same!Grow up and learn to respect women!  Until then, you will never be able to make her happy
