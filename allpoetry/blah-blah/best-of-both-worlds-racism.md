Wisdom comes to me from families
One Black
One White
My strength is manifested from my struggles
Of being neither Black nor White
But both

I can see the best in both worlds
because I am so closely connected
and questions arise in me
about prejudice, racism, and stero-types

Do Black people really know?
what it's like to be part of the most hated race in the world?
Do they see how much a white person has to struggle
to prove that they got where they are
because they DESERVE it and not because they are WHITE?

Do White people really know?
what it's like to have unsolicited stero-types attached to their skin?
Do they know what it's like to walk into a store
and to be followed around so they don't steal?
Do they know what it's like to deal with racial profiling
almost every where they go?

Racism is still here and it's still a problem!
I wish people would stop labeling others by the color of their skin
but rather by the size of their heart!

I can see both sides, because I am connected 
to a Black Family
and 
to a White Family

And I see the beauty in both!
