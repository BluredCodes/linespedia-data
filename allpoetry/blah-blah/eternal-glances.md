title: "Eternal Glances"

Heart stops....
A glance from across the room
Eyes Lock....
Illuminated by the midnight moon
Hips sway....
Calling for his attention
Lips play....
Causing her strong temptation

They meet....
He beckons her with charm
And takes her to his home
Glad for this magical moment
To have her alone

They kiss....
Unaware of the rising sun
And as tongue meets tongue
The cold blooded murder is done

He stabbed her....
His true passion revealed
Her crimson blood on his hands
Hunger temporarily healed

He tastes her blood....
An evil smile creeps on his face
Cuts a lock of her hair for keeping
And hides it away in his secret case

He is satisfied....
But only for a night
He'll be on the prowl again
In her gruesome death, he'll take flight
