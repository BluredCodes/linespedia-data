Challenge Description: Look through a dragon's Eyes. Tell me how he feels & thinks (50 points)



----------------------------------------------------------------------------

In those fire red eyes 
burning flames of torment 
hide behind fury 
The dragon yearns to be loved 
longs to be accepted 
but is only hunted for the sake of the hunt 
he is only searched out for the sake of the find 
and his lonliness becomes hate 
his cries become screams 
his pain becomes rage 
and behind his eyes 
you can see the bluest flames 
ablaze with revulsion and repulsion 
smoldering lonliness is hidden beneath the surface 
waiting for someone to see him for who he is 
waiting for his chance to live in freedom 
but he sees no end to his torture 
he sees only hatred in the eyes of those he meets daily 
and their hatred manifests itself into his soul 
until he too begins to believe 
He is EVIL 
He is BETTER OFF DEAD
