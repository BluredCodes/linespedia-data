I know you want me
I used not to care
But now when I see you
I want to smell your hair

Your lips are decieving
Your taste in my mouth
Cause last time I saw you
I had to go south!

You are the one
I want to make love to
You are the one
I will screw and screw

Sorry to be forward
Sorry to be bold
But yours is the member
I want to hold
