People say I'm neither black nor white 
and I can't disagree without a fuckin fight 
Well I say you're wrong 
and you can't handle that 
Kid, I'm fuckin human 
What's the crime in that? 
You want me to say I'm black 
and renounce my mother 
but it's depth you lack 
cause you can't see 
I'm not one or the other 
Others say I should claim white 
and turn my back on my father 
and forget the African plight 
but you can't see 
with your ignorance, I won't bother 

I stand PROUD 
I stand STRONG 
Say it LOUD 
The road is long 
I keep it real 
wit all my peeps 
I am Bi-Racial 
But my soul runs deep 

Flesh of my Flesh 
Blood of my Blood 
I'm a mix of two skins 
I was born out of love 

Stop trying to tell me 
to be what I'm not 
I be what I be 
and I got what I got 

I won't let you stomp on 
all my hopes and dreams 
Squash that Bitch! Now walk on 
worry bout your own damn schemes 

I stand PROUD 
I stand STRONG 
Say it LOUD 
the road is long 
I keep it real 
wit all my peeps 
I am Bi-racial
