Challenge Description: simple love story. you all know how it goes. write me of love. it's not a hard challenge, so have fun with it! (50 points)



--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Love has captured me 
and held me prisoner 
in the heavenly abyss 
opening my wings of passion 
exploring Desire and Compassion 
making my once dead heart 
Alive with emotional bliss 

I can fly within your smiles 
and I can soar within your laughter 
Let me swim in this emotional ocean 
where your voice is my lifeboat 
and your eyes are my lighthouse 
Make my once cold heart blaze with fire 

You have taken me beyond the edge 
where sanity is just my imagination 
and with every touch of your hand 
and every sound of your voice 
my heart beats ever faster 
until I feel like I can fly on this love alone 

Yes, I am a prisoner 
A prisoner of LOVE 
and You are my warden!
