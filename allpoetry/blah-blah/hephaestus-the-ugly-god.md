Challenge Description: Write an ode to a greek god... any greek god... I don't care... no brownie points for choosing me! Has to rhyme and be very imperial. I want the essence of my brothers and sisters... their whole spirit... and I want to be incredibly impressed (30 points)



--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

O God of Peace and Loving Hearts 
Bring us your Fire 
and spare us your facial arts 

You are hideous in physique 
with a face even Mother can't love 
but you provide your great armor 
and protect us from above 

You have strength of heart 
because of your ability to prove 
Your worthiness of a God 
behind your parents' reprove 

Look not to the mirror 
For it only shows 
Beauty that is skin deep 
as your beauty within grows 

So lend us your stregth 
and give us your fire 
You are Hephaestus 
God of peaceful desire!Hephaestus is the son of Zues and Hera. He is the only god that is physically ugly. He is the god of fire and forge and the armorer of the gods
