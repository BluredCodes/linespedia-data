title: "My Pile"
writer: "Blah Blah"

Like a heap of clothes on the floor
my secrets and lies are piled high
too BIG for me to ignore

The stench of my lies
are too much to bare
like a pile of old laundry
just sitting there

I try to clean it up
then the lies force their way out
adding to the pile
of scandals, lies, and doubt

Now the pile has grown 
too BIG for me to ignore
and I'm stuck in my own stench
lying here, crying on the floorThis is about little lies becoming a BIG deal, because you have to keep covering each lie up and the trail of your lies leads to confusion
