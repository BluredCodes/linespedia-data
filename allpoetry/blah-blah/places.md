I have seen your place
so full of THINGS
Nic Nacs and Gadgets
but all these won't give you angel's wings

So what of my place
not much to show
but I spend my money where it matters
but you don't care to know

You show off your possesions
but they are merely things
you can't take them when you leave this world
so what if it's fit for Kings

In the end
all you'll have to show
is your black, empty soul
and your residence down below

But though I have not much
I wish for nothing more
for when it's my turn to go
I'll take my golden soul to Heaven's doorWell?  heh
