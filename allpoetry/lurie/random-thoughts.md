title: "*~*Random Thoughts*~*"
writer: "Lurie"

Quiet
sitting
all
alone,
dog
barking
on
the
lawn,
Brain
aching
at
the 
tone
of
the 
ringing
of
the 
phone,
kids 
screaming
in
the
room,
splitting
headache
feels
like 
doom.

Lurie*~Suffering from an exscrutiating headache when I wrote this~*
