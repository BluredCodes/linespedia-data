It's snowing,
the fire is roaring,
our minds are one tonight,
In the fire glow I see
you,
the vision sends my heart soaring.
With the couch cushions
all over the floor,
no one but us at home...
no one at the door,
we'll slowly undress each
other,
and nestle underneath the 
cover.
Loving every inch of skin,
You hold me close to you,
knowing that I love you so
and this life wer'e in.

Lurie
