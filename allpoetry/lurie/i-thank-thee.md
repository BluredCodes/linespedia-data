title: "I Thank Thee"

I feel thy love
on the breeze.
I hear thy love
in a bird-song.
I smell thy love
in a field of wild-flowers.
I know thy love...
you gave your life for me.
I see thy love
in the pages of your word.
I hold thy love
with every beat of my heart.
I thank Thee for Thy Love.


Lurie
