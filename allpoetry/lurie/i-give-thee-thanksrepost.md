title: "I Give Thee Thanks~{Repost}"

Inspired by the stars above,
the sun that shines...
to show His love.
the clouds so white
The trees in bloom,
the winds that blow 
away the gloom.
Tender buds that soon will flourish,
He'll send the showers...
earth to nourish.
The deer that stand in the field,
the streams that flow 
and never yield.
For all these things,
I give Thee thanks,
and for the wild flowers 
that grow
on the river banks.


Lurie
