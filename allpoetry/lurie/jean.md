title: "JEAN"
writer: "Lurie"

Letting all his fear release,
allowing anger to find it's peace,
Knowing that they loved him so,
keeps his hearts fire aglow.
Yes, he'll miss them, mom and dad,
but he knows they don't want him sad.
They still watch him from above,
The suns rays bring down their love.
He will always long for his mothers embrace,
holding her hand, seeing her face,
His fathers wisdom to partake,
just pray to God, he will not forsake.
He's opened his heart to bare his soul,
with light from above, he will be made whole.
This darling boy, iv'e learned to love
so now I am his cyber mom.

Lurie
