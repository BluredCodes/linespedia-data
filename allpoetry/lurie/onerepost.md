---
title: "ONE~{Repost}"
writer: "Lurie"
slug: "onerepost"
source: "AllPoetry"

You love me,
I know,
I love you,
you know.
Clasped hands,
playful lips kissing in secret.
Two minds,
two hearts,
one soul.
One life to share.


Lurie
