What this woman needs
Is all of your love
What this woman needs
Is your heart your soul
What this woman needs
Is the looks you give
What this woman needs
Is the soft kiss hello
What this woman needs
Is someone to be there
What this woman needs
Is all you stand for
Without all of that this
