They say love is purely chance
But is it or is it planned
I'm not quite sure 
There is one thing I do know
And that is that I do love you
I know you don't love me
I just don't see why not
I did everything for you
None of that ever seemed to matter
It was always that way
Just look back at the past year 
Is there one thing that I haven't done
Or something I did wrong
If there is I am sorry
I thought I was doing right
Then again I am always wrong
