Waves thunder 
through time weary tides 
swelling, 
pregnant 
spewing fertile foam. 
Lunar light 
stretches 
magnetic glistening fingers, 
dragging hesitant sea water 
swirling with sand. 
Placid, popping foam 
seeps into granular silence. 
A brief embrace 
the moon gently kissing 
a silver evening tide.
