Regarding the crime 
retribution reigns 
fallen souls select solace 
innocence lingers in a muddy place. 
Ambient debate clamours on, 
sharp eyes haze like a sultry 
summer day. 
Faltered hearts keep tempo 
thudding a banal beat. 
Death beckons an eager host 
reality marries ambivalence 
the gown blood red 
spawning a new precedent. 
Guilty limbs reside, silent.
