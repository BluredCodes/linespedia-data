RATED TRIPLE XXX

CONTAINS STRONG LANQUAGE VIOLENCE

Johnny Farkracker became the devil's dream
When at 7 He pawned his ma's wedding ring
Treating his girl friends to a candy fling
At Eight the Devil's mirth he pleased
Disposing of his Granny's false teeth
She kept in a pickle jar beside the sink
But He really aroused the Devil's glee
Seeing Johnny sneak on Christmas Eve
Cursing last x-mas calling Santa a creep
Not bringing any girly mags for him to peep
So when fat old Santa got stuck in the fleu
Johnny Farkraquer knew just what to do
But not before peeing on Santa's boots
He stoked the fire and heard Santa's cry
Shooting up the chimney like 4th of July
After grabbing the toy bag he had seized
Johnny boy hid while Santa's ego seethed
When Santa came back looking angry and glum
Johnny zapped him with his mama's stun gun
Santa screamed and his conscience concurred
No more presents for Johnny Boy on Earth
For the lad scorched Santa's family jewels
Soiling his pants as up the chimney he flew
Sleighing away the fat man roared a boast
Soon now little Johnny boy would be toastJust a little nonsense folks!
