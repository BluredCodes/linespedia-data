(Into the great unkown alone)

Hear the whispy falling snow
In their icey gowns enclosed
Into the great unknown alone
The secret its silence tolls
Of The God who is but isn't
Living silent within your mind
Words are a living person devine
Sleeping awakening within your mind
The key that gives life meaning
'You won the billion dollar lottery'
'She loves you her words define'
Words are a living person devine
Sleeping to awaken within your mind
Flowing rivers of golden rhyme
Pure and clear as angel dreams
A mystery defying space and time
Hear the song the singing proclaims
Like warm and cleansing gentle rain
A rose so sweet a rose to keep
Forever locked in space and timeI feel the name of Jesus has been terribly maligned through profanity , so I try to write poems that put him in a good light using biblical metaphors.  Happy New Year to all.
