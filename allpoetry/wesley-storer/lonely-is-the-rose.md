title: "Lonely is the Rose"
writer: "Wesley Storer"

A crimson shrine
A lyric devine
Soft royal lilting
So furtively kind
Her satin lips
Soft velvet tips
So richly define
Seeping her wine
With tinted lyrics
Blandishments vine
Within your mind
The lonely she knows
For all she glows
Myrrh cedared May
June clovered hay
The Lavender sage
Maroon flowing gaze
Invitingly stays
Kiss the lips
Of a lonely rose
Forever to find
She's a branch
Of holy devine
Forever thineI hope you all dig roses, because there's
just something about their unassuming beauty
that turns me on!  Pen onward oh Bardsters!
