---
title: "Glass the color of Gold"
writer: "Wesley Storer"

I'm just an old guy now
Haunted
By glass the color of gold
My Mother's old coffee cups
Of long long ago
The finest things she owned
Their color gave her pleasure
In times of grief and woe
Lord she had plenty of those
Nothing it seems will atone
Nothing ever turned to gold
And I'm an old guy now
Living alone
Thinking of Mother long ago
And glass the color of goldLike most people everywhere, I wish I could have done more for my Mother, but unfortunately, I was too stupid.  I'm afraid I was quite a problem child.
