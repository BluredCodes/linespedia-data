title: "The Pilgrim Heart"

True love lonely love
Faithful billowing breeze
Sailing upon the Sea
Poor little Mayflower
Yearning to be free
From vexing iniquities
Setting the Pilgrims feet
In a different land
Where no royal courts
With royal decrees
Come to tax your home
Make their God your own
Away far distant shores
To future firesides bright
Winds listing their might
Hearing the sound thereof
From whence it comes to
Where it goes no one knows
But its name is wonderful
This the pilgrims' believe
From now to eternityI didn't get to sleep at all last night, so I lay on my bed writing this poem in my head.  Maybe someone will like it as much as I did writing it.  Bon Voyage!!!
