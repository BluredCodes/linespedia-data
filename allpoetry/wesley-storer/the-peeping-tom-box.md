It was a little black and white t.v.
Dirty and grimey with a faded screen
Old with a screwed up vertical hold
I felt it was messing with my soul
It was always going out of control
I taped a sheet of paper to its front
'peeping tom box - requires adjusting'
Took it to the 4th floor garbage room
amid the squalor of the nut house hotel
Then the the fire alarm went off again
I think it was diseased I feel better
It was driving me crazier than hell
Besides I think it was watching me
Now I just lay there on my bed
Blubbering lines of poesy rhyme
 In my head
