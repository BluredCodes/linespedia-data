Death of the flying Flowers
Monarchs 
Slain by cars
Planes and trains
Kids and storms at sea
It was accidental I guess
No one for anyone to blame
Killed by the Greenhouse effect.
No wait a minute how can that be
That doesn't make any dam sense at all
They were way down South in the warm
Is everything losing its temperance?
the Darwinians say many species 
Throughout history are extinct
Rationalizing all agonies
Into driveling trivia
Rotten hearted liars
How I wish my soul
Were as light as thine
O princely Monarch Butterfly
Sailing sacred skylights devine
