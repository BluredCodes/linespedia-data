title: "Sunny Days in Darling May"

The satiny skies of Spring
Bring leaves of floraled green
To sunny dayed darling May
Opulent silver clouds opaque
Whistling winged Geese array
Over cachets of dewey sachets
Roses maroon of blooming June
Golden slippered silouette moon
Pendulum cameo ebulient loom
Sweeping sparkling star festooned
Pure lights eternally alluring
Morning brightened fragile dreams
Overwhelmed by blinding beams
So snowy starlite bridal veils
Under steepled railing bells
Old Cupid's arrow strayed 
From ardor's place of favored grace
With heart's true affections feigned
Love's harpish lark gone harping
Ignoring yonder weeds of decay
Covering forsaken bachelor graves
Love's foolish favor did not claim
Once they wallowed in the muck
Once they groveled in the mire
Hiding silent tears suffered
Tender dreams slowly ruptured
Pleading the tender darlinged May
Not by loneliness to be consumed
But alas their loneliness resumed
Not grown wiser with the moon
