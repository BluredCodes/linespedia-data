title: "Once i kissed a Porcupine"

Once in a forest dark and deep
I fell from my journeys asleep
And dreamed of a lady sweet
Dreamily nudging my cheek
Rolling over upon my hip
I soon felt her kissing my lips
Then I awakened in a fright
Being in a forest's dark night
Backed away to the flash light
Turning it on I saw the porcupine
Slowly take flight into the night
God what a lucky guy am I!!!
How many guys can truthfully say
They've been kissed by a porcupine
As I look over the years of my life
I wonder how I ever made it through
I guess there must be a miracle 
A miracle called GodI lied!!!
