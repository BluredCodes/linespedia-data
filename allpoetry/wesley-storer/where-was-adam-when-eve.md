Where was Adam when Eve
Peeked from the flora leaves
By the sleepy little stream
And the tall dark Stranger
Stood beaming there with glee
She looked so intrigued
So innocent so naive
Our little flower Eve
So naive our little Eve
And purty as a prayer book
And then the wind began to
