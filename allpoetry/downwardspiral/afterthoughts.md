Sprawled legs, thrown carelessly 
across the bed,
The evidence of you left slipping silently 
from me-
My eyes half closed, my lips curled
Into a cat-like smile…
All cream for this kitty, please…
A soft purr escapes from within-
Memories only moments old
Flood my mind, my body-
Weaving a warm coverlet over me---
You demanding my eyes remain
Open-
Insisting I watch us in the mirror
On my dressing table.
You smiling as you study my face--
The hunger in me growing,
Burning...
and...  at last,
Igniting-
White Hot--
Begging for your mouth
Between my legs…
Your tongue at THIS spot--
The room filled with the scent of my sex-
Heady and strong
Beckoning to you…
Aching for the magic
That is your mouth
And you--
Trying to hold back,
But wanting it
Needing it
Reveling in the thought of sliding in deep
To taste it--
Sweet
Hot
Wet---
I arch and moan,
The sound of my pleasure deafening
Within my mind-
Then you're above me, 
Hovering-
On me,
In me-
Cutting through me like steel,
Hard and Unyielding-
Your eyes closed,
Face awash with bliss---
Deeper,
I cry to you-
Deeper Still,
I Beg--
And as you bury yourself
In my secrets,
The room spins,
I call your name-
I float
and…
then
Fall Slowly---
My lips curl once more,
Even afterthoughts of you
Make me wet.
