Andrew my love
Your the best in the entire world
You make me reeeeeeeeaaaaaaaaalllllllllyyyyyy happy
And you smell really good
Andrew my love
I have one thing to say to you
Your name must me Campbells
Cuz you are mmmmmm mmmmmm good!!!Love you bunches man!!!!!
