---
title: "Paint my Love"
writer: "my latin lover"
slug: "paint-my-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/34544-Paint-my-Love-by-my-latin-lover"

I'll paint my love in shades of blue
Paint my soul to be with you
I'll sketch your lips in shaded tones 
Draw your mouth to match my own
I'll draw your arms around my waist
And then all doubt I will erase
I'll trace a hand to wipe your tears
A caring look to calm your fears
I'll paint the sun to warm your heart
Swearing that we'll never part
I'll paint the stars in the evening sky
Draw their light into your eyes
I'll use a brush so light and fine 
To draw you close and make you mine
