title: "\"Sonic Alchemy\""

We paint with sound, this mortal world, each movement to inspire
The heart and soul and mind of man - to here fulfill desire.
The rhythms pulse, vibrate, and glow, if man will only hear
With more than an unconscious mind, and with a listening ear.

So few attune the inner voice, or grasp the cosmic sound
Thus, in a world of two dimensional thought, they remain bound.
For hearing must be active - listening with all our intent -
To gain the understanding of each sound and what is meant.

Enhancing life - this sonic world - is what our music makes...
The words we speak, the thoughts we think, emotions we create.
Polluted thoughts and words produce a dissonance - discord -
Intruding on our peace of mind to in our cells record.

We suffer from amnesia - wellness in our cells, suppressed -
And through the power of the ear and mind, it is addressed
Through "bio-tuning" - unifying body, mind and spirit
To eternal Light harmonics... if we'll only hear it.
