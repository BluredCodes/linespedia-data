'Tis sad, the loss, but greater stillthe 'circumstance' which guides the willof mortal man 'til he's undone...and loses that which he had won!We oft' bemoan this mortal state,yet fail to judge and here relatethe 'circumstance' to reckless choice;one leads, one follows its own voice.Man can't, alone, improve his lifewhen 'circumstance' becomes the strivethat keeps him from a cherished goal.'Tis sad, indeed, to lose one's soul!
