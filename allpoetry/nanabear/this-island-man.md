---
title: "THIS ISLAND, MAN"

This island, man, a stellar light adrift upon the sea
of time and space continuum, a complex galaxy
of systems within systems, light, harmonics, frequency
attuned to truth eternal, or to static vagaries.

A complex structure intertwined, entrained to God's own word,
to resonate, within this sphere with all that's good, when heard.
Imbued with the capacity to grow, progress, assured
of immortality and exaltation... if preferred.

Concentric egos, striving oft' against the Master Plan
for non-productive reasons, simply just because they can;
myopic lasers, unaware of everything they scan,
producing incoherent light... to wit, this island, man.
