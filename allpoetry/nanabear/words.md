title: "WORDS"

Without the Spirit and intent, words are with danger fraught.
As structures built upon the sand, they fall and come to naught.
With only wisdom of mankind, mere words cannot express
The majesty of holy realms, nor visions, there, address.

All words fall short and miss the mark of what the soul desires,
But music, filled with Spirit, raises men's souls and inspires
Their minds and hearts to nobler truths and acts, with good intent;
Until they find their life's a joy, each moment blessed - well spent.

Choose carefully, then, the words you'd use, intent upon their Spirit,
Thus fill your life with majesty so that all men can hear it.
Praise Him who gives all things to you and bless His name, The Word,
Until all heav'n and earth resound as one that they have heard!
