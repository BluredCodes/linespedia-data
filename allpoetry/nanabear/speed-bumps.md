---
title: "\"Speed Bumps\""
writer: "NanaBear"
slug: "speed-bumps"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

From pole to pole
on poles we glide
throughout the galaxy,
in search of life
on other spheres,
perhaps the same as we.

We dance around 
our globe that's bent,
like actors on a stage,
and leap across
the starry skies
with words upon a page.

Our thoughts fly past the speed of light,
exceed beyond our grasp,
and fail to see
the needs at hand,
while mysteries we'd clasp.

It's great to see
how far we've come -
advancements in great jumps -
but our progress
is oft' undone
when we ignore speed bumps.
