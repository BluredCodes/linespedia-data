Sometimes I think I think a thought
that's new...
But it is really not.
Just something that I had forgot -
a thought of something I once knew.

Sometimes I've thought a thought to think
for fun...
When I am on the brink
of boredom - mind that needs a "drink" -
a thought complete before it's done.

Sometimes I think I think a lot
but don't...
It's just that I am caught
off-guard by what I once had thought -
and thoughts I bid come forth that won't.
