title: "\"Within The Stillness\""

Within the stillness of my soul,
I hear my Father's voice, 
Can feel His arms surround, embrace,
And in His love rejoice.

Within my heart he touches me
And often, moved to tears,
He comforts every anguished doubt,
And silences my fears.

Within each organ I can find
The sacred laws He gave,
As promised to His valiant ones,
That he would there engrave

To be an everlasting part -
A witness to their souls -
And guide upon this pathless shore
To lead them to His goals.
