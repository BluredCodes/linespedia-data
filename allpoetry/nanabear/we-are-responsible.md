---
title: "We Are Responsible!"
writer: "NanaBear"

Liars, thieves, and murderers of truth rule in the land,While the 'silent majority' still in their silence stand.The rule of law lays stripped and raped by those who lust for power;Our Constitution's hanging by a thread this evil hour.The legal rights of every state are daily trampled onIn such a blatant manner that they're buried, dissolved, and gone!Corrupt and biased judges now make laws and set asideThe very judgments, laws and statutes by which they should abide.Thus strife, caused by the thirst for power, intensifies and growsThroughout our towns and countryside and civil unrest sows.Perversion, first of minds and hearts, translates into destructionOf our very nation as it fills up with corruption.There are no Statesmen left.  We're led by men who commit treason;Who're bought and paid for by a foreigh power for one reason:To weaken and tear down our Constitution and each fenceWhich guards our sovereign nation; to destroy our self-defense.The sad this is WE are responsible for this disgraceBecause of immorality; becoming all that's base.We have perverted even laws of nature to abuseOurselves and all our fellow men, which God will not excuse!
