---
title: "\"To Sleep...\""
writer: "NanaBear"

to sleep until all turmoil's gone...
past night-
past day -
past endless dawn -
until even God can't recall
why He made me...
the least of all.

to sleep until there's nothingness...
no pain -
no grief -
no sore regrets -
which cause such anguish to the soul...
this i would choose...
ultimate goal.

but then a voice speaks in the dark...
"You can't! -
"You won't! -
"You have the spark!"
and breaks away the deadly black
which steals my soul...
to bring me back.

Once reason, lost, returns I see...
the past -
the now -
the future me -
I am amazed how close I came
to losing all...
with self to blame.

For I asked God to let me "feel"...
the pain -
the grief -
the senses reel -
that others have, while here below,
to better love...
