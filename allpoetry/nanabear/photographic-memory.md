title: "PHOTOGRAPHIC MEMORY"

My photographic memory's out of film, I'm sad to say.
Can't seem to get a picture of one thing I did today.
I'm fuzzy 'round the edges about how my time was spent,
So don't expect clear snapshots of my friends or where I went.

My past is more in focus than the meal an hour ago,
And I can sing the "Big Band" tunes, but days and months don't know.
Some faces look familiar, but imagine my surprise
To find out we're related!  (You sure wear a good disguise!)

Alone in crowded rooms where people talk, but not with me...
Can't follow conversations, so they all just let me be.
It's lonely - being perfect - in this odd and unique way.
I'd love to share my "albums", but most people shy away.

So here I sit and contemplate where all my film has gone,
And worry that my eyesight will not wait to tag along.
If you find where I put it, please return it - quick - to me...
