Where, O freedom, have you fled?  You're hunted round the globe.All countries have expelled you, Europe's 'stranger'; England's 'robe'Has given you a warning to depart and not return.Asylum of mankind, America, to you we turn!'Novus ordo seclorum' now appears upon your seal;Once purchased by much precious blood, a broken law to heal,The Declaration of Independence and Constitution stateThe principles, specific provisions, all men need to be great.O freedom, promulgated by your Declaration madeIn heaven, ratified on earth by blood, both nobly paidAnd freely given, it is sad you are by fools replacedWith vices, avarice and greed; cast down, defiled, disgraced!Though you now hide your face in shame, there soon will come a timeWhen you will be enthroned again, by men who are divineAnd worthy of the blessings now all fools hold in contempt.Then Christ, the rightful King, will reign... when all His wrath is spent! ©  on Aug 26 2001 09:38 AM PST   0  • 9
