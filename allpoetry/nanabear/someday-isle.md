Someday I'll respond to truthin an appropriate way.Someday I'll connect with you.and know just what to say.Someday I'll be 'good enough'to merit more respect.Someday I'll attain my goalswhich now I just neglect.Someday I'll have patience, butI don't have time right now...I'm stranded here, on 'Someday Isle','til I can figure how!
