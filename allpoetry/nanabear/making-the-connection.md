From fear, for power, or out of love...
Do we make the connection
Between our actions and their cause,
Acceptance or rejection?

If we fall short at the last day,
Below our expectations
Of where or who we thought we'd be,
Do we make the connection?

Until we recognize the cause,
Found by great introspection
And change, to do all things for love,
We'll suffer disconnection.

We can't progress from where we are,
Bring our flesh into subjection,
Until we each become like Christ
By making the connection!
