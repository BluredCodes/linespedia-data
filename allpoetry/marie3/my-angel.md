I'm laying in this bed alone,
And I'm thinking only of you.

I wish that you were here, 
To fill the space beside me.
      (Inside me)

Baby, you're my angel.
Will you save me tonight?

Save me from this darkness,
And the loneliness inside.

Come and be with me.
Bring the warmth of your love.

Hold me all through the night,
And let me here the beating of your heart.

Let me feel your breath on my skin,
And the depth of your soul within.

Angel, only you can save me.
So come to me tonight.
