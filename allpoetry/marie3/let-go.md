Somewhere deep inside I'm dying
I can feel it slipping away
Slowly seeping out of me
I'm drowning in this pain

Reaching out to everyone 
But know one's there to hold my hand
You see me disappearing 
And you don't give a damn

You insist that you love me
You tell me that you care
But when I really need you
When are you ever there?

It would be so easy 
To make it all just go away
Take this knife, slit my wrists
and let go of all this pain
