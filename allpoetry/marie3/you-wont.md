Lost within the shadows of this dark realm
I call out your name and plead for help,

But you won't hear me.

I stand before you broken and bleeding;
Wishing only for you to hold me,

But you won't see me.

My soul cries out in anguish 
And you know what pain dwells within,

But you won't care about me.

I give up, let my soul die 
And let the darkness engulf me. I am dead,

But you won't even know I'm gone.
