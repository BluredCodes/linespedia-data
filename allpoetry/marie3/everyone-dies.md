---
title: "'Everyone Dies'"
writer: "Marie3"
slug: "everyone-dies"
source: "AllPoetry"

The vodka bottle comforts me
Tells me, don't you cry
I am left here alone 
And my spirit begins to die

I wonder if you can hear me
Or if you know my pain
Do you see the heartache?
And the tears that fall like rain?

I start to wish that you were here
To see the sorrow that fills these eyes
The windows to my soul fill with tears
As I look to star-lit skies

Knowing I will never touch them
Or live to see another sun
I take one last look 
Knowing that this life is done

I don't want you to be in pain
And I don't want you to cry
Because I am just a person
And every person has to die
