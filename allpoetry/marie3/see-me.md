I really wish that you could see me 
As I stand before you every day
As I stand there heart and soul bleeding
Screaming your name

But even as the tears fall and I crumble
You look right through me
You don't see the torment
Or the f*cking pain in my eyes

How can you be so blind
That you do not see 
The darkness inside
That is engulfing me?

It has torn apart 
All that once lived inside
And taken from me all I ever loved
All I could have been

Now there is nothing left of me
Nothing but this broken shell
This lost and empty being
That stands before you now
