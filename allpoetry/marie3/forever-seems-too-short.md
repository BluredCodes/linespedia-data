Your eyes of senna,
And your lips of pure silk.

Your body warm and wanting.
Your heart beat so thrilled.

Hands so gently touching;
Words so soft and sweet.

As you lightly kiss my breasts,
I pull you close to me.

Making love to you,
The world disappears.

There's no time for tomorrow,
And no time for lonely fears.

As the sun begins to rise,
We lie here close to sleep.

Your heart beats beneath my ear,
And forever seems too short to me.
