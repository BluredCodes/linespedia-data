You don't know my torment.
You don't know my pain.
Don't attempt to understand
What you could never comprehend.

You don't know how I live.
You don't know how I feel.
Don't try to walk in my shoes.
You wouldn't even last a  day.

Could you deal with the emptiness?
Could you deal with the hate?
Knowing no one loved you?
Or would give a fuck if you died?

NO?

Then don't try to understand me.
Or analyze my depth and my pain.
Because you couldn't stand to see the world,
Through my eyes.
