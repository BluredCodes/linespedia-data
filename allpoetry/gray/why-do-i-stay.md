---
title: "Why do I stay?"
writer: "Gray"
slug: "why-do-i-stay"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/164-Why-do-I-stay--by-Gray"
---

He rests my heart
on the tip of his tongue
and every day he swallows.
I choose to sacrifice
everything within my skin
that is righteous and mine
for pieces of his affection.
I cry phantom tears 
that would drown my cheeks
if I could let them.
Now all I have
is some restless sleep.
