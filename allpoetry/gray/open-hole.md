So sad, so sad to think that I am
anything but a coward.
Only fearful children play with such
fire in their hearts as I do.
Maybe this intoxication will make me honest.
To keep going and going, never knowing
when to make yourself stop.
Not realizing the words
that flow from these lips,
the secrets that flow from this body.
A temple long since crumbled,
nothing much left to lose.
I can't remember a time when
