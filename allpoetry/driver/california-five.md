title: "California Five"

Sacramento in the rear view mirrorsSouth bound on interstate fiveEight wheels humRound and roundWhere we stop...The grass plushA photo I've never seenOrchards were only storiesOver coffeeI never thought I would make itThis far anywayDonners passTested meMothers snowWas hellI held onLike the othersAnd the ones yet to seekCalifornia fiveWhere we stop...
