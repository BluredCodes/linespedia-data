North out of Nogales18 wheels roarWelcome to America brotherCactus wave helloDesert heatMakes the interstate gleamHere I come PhoenixCan you hold me?No wayBecause I've got to rollFlagsaff in the morningThen I'm gone againSt. Louis has called my nameGateway to AmericaWelcome
