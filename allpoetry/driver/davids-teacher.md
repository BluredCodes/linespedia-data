title: "David's Teacher"
writer: "driver"

He just turned three
Innocent to the world
If he only knew

I'll teach him 
Dream my son dream
Candyland
And baseball
Cartoons
Milk moustaches
Man, I have to laugh

My heart sinks 
To see him grow
I still teach
Big boy ways

Buddies forever

From a son
To a father
Lessons learned
And forgotten
But always a common bond...
Dreams

Buddies forever

I love you David.
