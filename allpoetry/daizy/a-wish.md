I am content,
With the way my life is.
Full of happiness,
And sadness too.
A little bit of madness,
To keep me true.
But still I wonder,
At times, Why do I get so bored.
And so I want,
And so I need.
Something a little more.
Is it a pine,
Is it a wish,
Is there really anything more
