---
title: "A lifetime with you"
writer: "daizy"
slug: "a-lifetime-with-you"

The way your eyes sparkle,
The way your smile shines.
The way you could light up a room,
Each and every single time.

It was easy to fall in love with you,
You were so trusting, perfect and true.
You held the gift of promise,
A key to open my soul.

You loved me so simple,
Never asking for anything more.
Than what I am, or what I will be.
And what will bring that was better than before.

Every second of everyday,
I want to be in your embrace.
To feel all your love,
To look upon your face.

Never for a moment,
Will I ever doubt you.
I will always know inside my heart,
That you are the truest, through and through.

I know nothing lasts forever,
But for some reason, 
I won't believe that true.
Because as far as I am concerened,
I am going to spend a lifetime with you.
