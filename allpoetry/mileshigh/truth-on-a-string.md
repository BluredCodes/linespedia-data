title: "Truth on a string"

I have got your truth,
your truth on a string.
Have i seen it all?, 
    Seen it all slip away?
How would you know if i did?
How would you know if i could?

If i told you lies,
you would smile. And i would laugh.
If i told you truth?
I would cry.  And you would pray. 
But i have your truth, 
your truth on a string.
