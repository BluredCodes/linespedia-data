title: "Sunday"
writer: "mileshigh"

In the majesty of Sundays, mourning.
I am thinking back of days.
When all those things misunderstood,
Were as actors, playing for me.  
Climbing through logic, 
In search of hard truths.

So now as I sort, 
back through thoughts passed.
The lessons learned, and those that were taught.
I praise you for making a man, unafraid.
To dream of angels, while standing in ghettos.
To think of earthworms, while walking on clouds.

And in this break, between here and the next.
I need you to know,
that of all the things unsaid.
I have become.
