title: "Love"

A liar from the very start,
fueled by fear, disguised by heart.
I talked to you about the pain,
of abandoned love, with ice cold veins.

But I am not so hard at life,
I've hardly lived, there is no strife.
I gave you what I could not bear,
The fact that mine is all too clear.

You have seen through both far and wide,
and surely through my empty eyes.
And if you could stand, to see us through
I would lie again, to be with you.
