You gave it all to me,
and i never saw a thing.
I Lost all the magic, 
In looking for the strings.

I may have lost the chance,
to save you from the pain.
I cannot change the past,
only my love for you remains.

But now i see the forest,
lost among the trees.
I threw it all away,
You gave it all to me.
