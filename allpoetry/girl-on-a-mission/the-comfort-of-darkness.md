title: "The Comfort Of Darkness"

The night moves on into eternity, as it cloaks my wailing soul
filling what I know now is a very deep hole.

Fill me up oh sweet night, cloak and hide me from the day
for it's in your comfort that I sink, never caring what others think.

Falling deeper into you, and you into me.
