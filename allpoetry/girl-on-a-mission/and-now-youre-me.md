---
title: "and now you're me"
writer: "girl on a mission"
slug: "and-now-youre-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/24605-and-now-youre-me-by-girl-on-a-mission"
---

just wanted to belong to befriend and have friends.
but got instead taunts full of lead.

ripped me apart and i wondered why even when i'd 
done no wrong, i carry such stains in my heart.

stains of cruel words spoken without thought.
time passes i've grown.  by some strangeness
we meet again and you're all broken up and
beaten down.

but unlike you i use my words to build you up
and encourage you to go on for I can see that
you are now me.
