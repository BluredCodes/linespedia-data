I've just been swallowed by a deep abyss.  
The dark flows over me in waves of sadness 
indescribable.

I'm angry now wanting to escape but lost 
not knowing which how.  I'm confused and 
don't know which way to run!

I'm terrified, "What was that?"  Paranoia 
takes hold so you think I'm strange?  Well 
maybe you're right.

But you don't matter nothing does...
just me in this hole falling...falling...
falling...OK I know this is like deeepressing!  I wrote this during a time in my life when I felt very bad!  I'm thankful to God that I don't and never want to feel that way again!
