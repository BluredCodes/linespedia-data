Hey, ever wonder what you missed when you couldn't 
even say "hi"?  Didn't want to meet that person 
who was so "different" than you?

Hey, what if that person held the key to your
lonely heart?  Maybe fulfilled your deepest
fantasy, your forgotten dreams, your deepest
wishes?

Might have been your greatest lover to which
no one could compare!  Or maybe the best 
friend you'd ever have like no one else
could ever be!

Maybe...maybe taught you things untold mentored
you to greatness!  Ever wonder what you missed
because you decided to stay locked in your own
little world allowing fear and prejudice to 
hold you there?

Ever wonder if you had just accepted that
invitation to meet that "different" person
what your life would be like now?

...EVER WONDER?The "prejudice" discussion on the discussion board inspired me to submit this poem.  It was written nearly 3 years ago.
