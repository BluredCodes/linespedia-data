The whisper of your soft breath is
barely audible. I hear you with my
heart and feel you deep inside.

The ocean of my love has been penetrated
by the fire of your passion.  Take me
sweet love and make me totally yours
if that's possible, for I am and always
will be one with you.

My pulse is rising in time with your 
deep thrusts, I can't contain the 
overwhelming pleasure pouring forth
from our love.  

Oh listen and hear the longing in 
my emotions wanting more and more
of you to fill me to explosion
beyond reality in a climax of 
untold, unknown, orgasmic ecstasy.
