title: "Hello, Can You Hear Me?"

I whisper in your ear so close
"hello can you hear me?"
There's no response...or is there?
But then you walk...you walk away.

I see you often you know, but you 
can't see me...or do you?  But
then you walk away...you walk away.

I watch your hair dancing as the 
wind teases and tangles it.  I see
your beauty so enchanting, I want
to embrace you, you can feel me...
can't you?

I wait longing for the time you will
return.  Will you come back...you will
won't you?  I reach to caress your face,
you pause as if...as if...but you won't
believe, you just move on.

But not before you gently place the
scented roses on my hard gray stone.
Bye...for now my love.  I wait with
deep longing for your return!
