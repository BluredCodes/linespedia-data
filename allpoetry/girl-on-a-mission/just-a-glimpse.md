title: "Just A Glimpse"
writer: "girl on a mission"

In a flash I saw you but that's all it took.
You let down your guard and I stole a look.

You pretend you don't care with that
nonchalant stare.

But in one tiny glimpse I caught your stare
full of desire and lots of love too. Don't
close up now...it's too late for we both know
this is our fate.

So kiss me now and hold me too for I have
caught a glimpse of you.
