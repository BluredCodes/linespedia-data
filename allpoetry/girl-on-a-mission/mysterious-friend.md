---
title: "Mysterious Friend"
writer: "girl on a mission"
slug: "mysterious-friend"
source: "AllPoetry"

Your dark fingers caress me all around, I shiver deep inside.
You seem to evade me but yet...yet I love you.

Now I feel the warmth of your breath upon my face, I sigh with great contentment!  The cries of distant memories and voices oh so faint.

I search for you on the left and on the right...where have you gone?
The dawn has come, you've gone away.  I sleep restlessly awaiting your
return, longing to frolick and dance once again in the night!
