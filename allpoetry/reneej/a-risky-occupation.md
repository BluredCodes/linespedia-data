Poet,
a risky occupation -
baring your heart,
a curtain drawn
for a play.

A tap dance,
sometimes without music -
just a phrase
over and over
only you can hear
and that can drive you
to the edge.

A harried spouse
waiting in the wings,
not understanding
your love affair -
never comprehending
your endless quest
for the perfect word.
