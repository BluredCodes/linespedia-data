title: "Christmas Every Day"

I string words together
like pearls
in praise of our children,
our grandchildren, and nature,
too often leaving you out -
you, whose smile
is like the rising sun,
whose goodness makes my life
a Christmas every day.
I cannot capture
the essence of you
with words,
but when I watch you,
the man you are,
the love I feel
is a burst of jewels
unstrung
and glittering my world.
