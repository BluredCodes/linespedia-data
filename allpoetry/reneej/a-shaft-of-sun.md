I walked in a dim forest
at noon.  Capes of moss
draped on me from trees
overhead, as if in sorrow.
No birds sang.

All at once I came upon
a long, open space.
A shaft of sun shone
on me.  I heard a swallow
sing!

It was like the day I grew up,
being able to walk
the path ahead
untethered, listening
to life's music.
