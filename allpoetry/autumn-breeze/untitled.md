All I ever seem to do
is reach for love from all of you.
If you look, then you will see
how empty it is inside of me.
My love was taken and thrown away
by someone who I'd rather not say.
If you think you could rise above,
and restore in me, my endless love...
then I will surely fall for you 
and love you like you want me to.
