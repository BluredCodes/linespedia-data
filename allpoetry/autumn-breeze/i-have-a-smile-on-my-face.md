title: "[I have a smile on my face,]"

I have a smile on my face,
but happiness is not the case.
I smile just to make you think
i'm happy that we aren't linked.
If you could see beyond these lies,
you'll know that I am dying inside.
You should have stayed, why did you go?
Please tell me cause I want to know.
I wish someone could make me see
that it's to late for us to be.
You see this smile on my face,
but happiness...is not the case.06-01,
