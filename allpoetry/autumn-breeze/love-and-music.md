---
title: "Love and music"
writer: "autumn breeze"
slug: "love-and-music"
source: "AllPoetry"

I hear a sound from across the room
that immediatly catches my attention.
I can't imagine a sound more beautiful
than the delicate vibrations of this one.
I close my eyes and try to see what this beautiful sound would look like.
Just as I realize what I am seeing,
I feel a deep wave of chills run throughout my body.
For what I am seeing is purity in all of the world.
I know this really does exist, because I feel it
when I hear this sound.
Nothing in the world can be more beautiful
becasue to me, this sound is love.
