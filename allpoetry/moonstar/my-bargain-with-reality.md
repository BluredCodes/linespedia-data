give me the chance
to have a dance
on a quiet night
I'm in a trance

allow me to twirl
like a little girl
with easy skin
and soft brown curls

to see through a scope
the moonlight elope
with a meadow of dreams
alive with hope

to fly to the clouds
and sing out loud
and mingle amongst
paradise's crowds

please deny your greed
cruel Reality
for one night loosen
your grip on mein other words....

GET ME OUTTA HERE!!!!!!
