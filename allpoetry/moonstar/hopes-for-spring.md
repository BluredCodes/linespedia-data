---
title: "hopes for spring"
writer: "moonstar"
slug: "hopes-for-spring"
source: "AllPoetry"

I see the clouds
collecting in your eyes 
A cold wind blows through me

I am surrounded
by trees of harsh words
There is no shelter in your forest

The life around me withers
from the winter that you bring

So i grab a warmer jacket
pull up my hood
put on my gloves
and brave the storm

Because winter is just a season
sure to tire and end
And then I'll be free
to approach spring
cold and drenched
but unbroken
