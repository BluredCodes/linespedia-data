you tossed back your hair
   and watched with a smirk
      with beautiful eyes full of sin
you were sure not to loose
   but you couldn't take me
      with your cheaply used lines and a grin
said you'd drive near and far
   in your piece of crap car
      but you were just shit outta luck
'cause i saw pretty quick
   that you think with your dick
      and really just wanted the fuck
i sat and observed
   as girls kept getting used
      and i watched it go straight to your head
still i'd smile and nod
   at all the right times
      at the sickening lines i was fed
so just as i thought
   'twas a matter of time
      till you did what i knew you would do
though you spoke very sweet
   when all's said and done
      i knew it was just a cheap screw
so i can say
   it was no shock to me
      when the next day "the speech" came to pass
because from that first night
   'till the very last day
      i could see you were simply an ass
for now i'll be nice
   i won't play the bitch
      i'll battle my urges to kill
but if karma one day
   doesn't take it's revenge
      you can bet your sweet ass that I WILL!!
