---
title: "my trip to Morrow"
writer: "moonstar"
slug: "my-trip-to-morrow"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/48683-my-trip-to-Morrow-by-moonstar"
---

my heartache will turn
to anticipation,
the hollow spot
will bein to fill
with thoughts of you,
with the warmth of knowing
when my plane to Morrow
has landed
I can once again
know my love
in the flesh.

I'm awaiting my trip
to Morrow
to that place
where todays longings
are yesterdays news.
the yearning
is nearly unbearable
but I must find patience.
for I know
no amount of money
could bring me 
to Morrow
today.
