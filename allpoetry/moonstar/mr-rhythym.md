there once was a crazy old quack
when he danced there was no turning back
any music you'd choose
he would wear out his shoes
with his freestyle, ballet or tap

then he ran across crazy miss rhyme
she had her eye on him quite some time
when he saw she danced faster
he found them a pastor
since then they've been living sublimehee hee
i almost titled this "how to amuse yourself at work"
