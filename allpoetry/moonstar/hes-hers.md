---
title: "he's hers"
writer: "moonstar"
slug: "hes-hers"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/46038-hes-hers-by-moonstar"
---

She inhales and his soul is sucked from his body 
   joining her collection-
She reaches out with claws and touches his chest-
He shatters like broken glass-
Sharded fragments fall to the floor sharp as razor blades-
She gathers them and holds them in the palm of her hand-
Light reflecting through her-
And blows them into the wind of never-
And he watches his death played like a film in the reflection 
  of her eyes-
