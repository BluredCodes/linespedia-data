---
title: "watching you die"
writer: "moonstar"
slug: "watching-you-die"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/48684-watching-you-die-by-moonstar"
---

your affliction
no one but you can conquer
your addiction
and yet we all must suffer
condemnation
you lie about your so-called
"motivation"
and in the end you'll die

loved ones helpless watch your dance
you're fascinated with tempting death
you start, then quit, then start again
why don't you slit your fucking wrists
and finally get it overwith

hold the knife
just like you hold the bottle
end your life
your cravings supercede your
masquerade
why don't you grab the fucker
by the blade
you're killing yourself either way

loved ones helpless, hopefull still
you'll see we all can feel your pain
you'll see we all have weaknesses
you'll see the world for what it is
and maybe then you'll rather live
