title: "you don't know me"

tell me again
how well you know me~

know my every move,
my thoughs before i make them,

each curve of my body
and how it fits to yours,

the shape of my eyes
like the back of your hand,

my soul 
inside and out~

tell me you know
how i feel,

where my mind
will lead me next~

tell me i'll be back.

and while you're at it
tell me this~

if you know me so fucking well
