---
title: "my everything"
writer: "moonstar"
slug: "my-everything"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/50142-my-everything-by-moonstar"
---

if only i could spend forever in your arms
feeling your warmth against my cheek
the strength of your arms
sheltering me from even my own bitterness
your heart beating against my temple
almost in tune with my own
it courses through your chest into mine
with a rhythm of pure, untainted love
my eyes close
and for a moment innocence is recaptured
everything is you
and life is good and simple again
