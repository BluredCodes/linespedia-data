i have this need to tell the story
of a friend that i now have
for though i know him 
very well
i'm baffled by him constantly

because i never liked him much
i'm getting more and more confused
that here i am
surrendering
myself unto his gentle touch

my heart is beating wildly
i must be under some dark spell
with his lips soft
against my neck
my body is deceiving me

i hate that he's a little dim
but smiles at me so charmingly
so innocent
and so i had
no choice but to have sex with him

and so i'm warning you my friend
you must beware these evil men
so you'll be more
prepared than me
when the pussy burglar strikes again!!!!!!
