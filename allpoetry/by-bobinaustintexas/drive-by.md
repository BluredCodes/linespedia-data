---
title: "Drive by"
writer: "by BobInAustinTexas"

shotgun blast
drop really fast
breaking glass
cover your ass
other gun shots
seeing blood drops
windows gone
45 drawn
pictures fall
on the floor crawl
mom screams
feels like a bad dream
dog barks
broken tv sparks
sis is crying
glass is flying
can see them now
their lives disallow
squeeze the trigger
with all of my vigor
the car speeds away
house in disarray
will see them real soon
by the light of the moon
will show them my fury
their families will bury
the bodies of sons
killed by my gun
