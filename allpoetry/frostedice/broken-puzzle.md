The universe is made equal,
	Every being is made twice.
	There is an equal for everyone. 
	And a love for every heart.

	Once you find your other half,
	Your puzzle will eb complete.
	Your heart will be whole,
	And your life full of joy.

	I have found myself,
	Out there amongst the stars.
	I let him go, and now I'm incomplete,
	Like a puzzle with one missing piece.Any comments welcome.
