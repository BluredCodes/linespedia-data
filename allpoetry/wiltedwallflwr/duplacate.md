in my day
in my life
i am all me
and noone else

who can 
be somone else 
but themselves

when they cour
and hide them selves

with a mask

and theyd listen
to my words
and keep them in 
their memory 
for the time needed

when thay can rip me off
of myself
and be pasted onto 
somone else
