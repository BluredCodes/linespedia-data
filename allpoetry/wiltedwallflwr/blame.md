when she breaths
she wonders why
  and her soul cringes

her hearts fake
wishd hed suffer
  blood the color of forever

since that time
innocence lied
  taken from her verry being

gone away
to be denied
  they know its true

its alwayse true
but no, they dont believe...
  -the timid kind-this is toa friend who went through somthing really awfull... im not at liberty to say what but i wrote this to comfort her.
