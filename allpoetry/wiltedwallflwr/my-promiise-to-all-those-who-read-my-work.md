Ive got words. 
    words made for me. 
words made for everyone. and ill let you know what those words are, in any way i can, in any possible way. 
    Ill be myself, in your eyes. 
so that you can understand me. understand my words, my mind. 
 
    but my thoughts are for me. and my days are for my journal, and  my words are for you.. for me. 
 
     hear them 
     tell them 
     exorb them 
     let them effect you 
 
feel what i felt -i ask 
    and tell me your thoughts. 
 
 If you do that, ill tell you, everything i know and feel. 
 in any way i can. 
    for me.this is why i write
this is a promise i promise anyone who reads a poem of mine.
