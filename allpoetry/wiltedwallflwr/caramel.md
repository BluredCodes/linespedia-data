Caramel puppy
Barking loud
Wanting free,
Mister won’t you please feed me?

Little girl
Want to dance
Want to dance,
Wallflower this may be your last chance.this is one of my verry few organized and rhyming poems... im verry proud of it, tell me whet you think?
