I’m being the person you said I couldn’t be
I’m forming a different ocean; I’m creating the quicksand sea

Ill become the person I promised myself I wouldn’t be...

I’m sculpting a boarding artwork of clay from my hands
I’m sending letters to second grade friends

If you don’t mind your messing up my plans...

I’m loving a saint in the midst of hell
I’m not in 6th grade anymore


I’m turning out woman you said I couldn’t be.
