---
title: "I Remember You"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "i-remember-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I will always remember
I will always remember you
you are the love of my life I remember
we had good times together, I loved
I will always remember being with you.

I remember the years we would've had together
I remember the feelings I still have for you.
I remember the love that we always shared.
You took me from a child and made me a woman, I remember.

I remember that I still love, and my feelings will never die.
The best thing about remembering is remembering that I had you.
I will always remember the good times, and try to forget the bad. 
Just thinking about us when we were together, it always makes me real glad.
