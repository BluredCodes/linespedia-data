---
title: "You Can Tell I Love You"
writer: "Anganiesha"

There's nothin' much to say, it's self-explanatory,All you have to do is look at my face. You bringSo much joy, and warmth to my heart, I know that You cannot be replaced.I know that you feel the same way I do,Because it is written all over your face.You make me feel real good, you makeMe feel loved, you brighten my day with your grace.You know I love you, I love you to death, youMean the whole wide world to me. If it wasn'tFor you it wouldn't be me as far as my eyesCan see.I tell you all the time there's no one else for me,I want to give you all of my love.From high up above I know we're in love,And that shall set us free.
