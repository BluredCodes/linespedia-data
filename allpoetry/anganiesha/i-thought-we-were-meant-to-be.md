---
title: "I thought we were meant to be"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "i-thought-we-were-meant-to-be"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8232-I-thought-we-were-meant-to-be-by-Anganiesha"

I tell you how I feel about you, but it seems like you spit in my face.I think about all our memories, because they cannot be erased.You know that you were my first, and I would do anything for you.I loved you with all of my heart, without you I just fell apart.I tried to change my ways so I could be with you.But it seems like I did it for nothin, and I became your fool.I used to cry and pray, because you left me all alone.I cried all day, and cried all night because I wanted you to come home.I used to have a lot of dreams, dreams about you and me.I remember the first time we met, It was in 1993.Then we got together in 1996.You were my dream come true, and I cherished our first kiss.Our relationship was going well, then you changed your ways.I always tried to work things out, but you never had nothin to say.I never understood what went wrong, you said it was not me.Then I started to put everything together, I thought we were meant to be.
