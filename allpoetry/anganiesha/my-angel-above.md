---
title: "My Angel Above"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "my-angel-above"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

You said the words “I love you”
I always said them too.
The love that I have for you,
Is oh so very true.

Hearing your voice always makes me smile,
And I can get through the day.
I’m always happy to hear from you,
Especially the things you say.

I know about you very well.
Everything in and out.
I know what you are going to say to me,
And what they are about.

You know that I know your laughs.
I know that you know my cries.
You know that I love you,
And without you my heart just dies.

I know when you are happy.
You know when I am sad.
To me you are my angel above.
That’s why I give you all of my love.

You know I love your kisses.
You know I love your touches.
You know the way I feel,
And you know I love you so much.

I always had the time to say,
I’m happy to be with you.
I reminisce on everything we been through.
That’s how I know I love you.

Every time I close my eyes, I always see you holding me.
Hugging me, and kissing me, and saying we are meant to be.
Sometimes I cry myself to sleep, because you are not here.
But every time I am with you, I seem to have no fears.
The flames I have for you right now, the wind cannot blow out.
Everyone in the world knows I love you.
I love you without a doubt.

You are the air that I breathe, the sight that I see.
I know we are meant to be.
From high up above I know this is love.
And it shall set me free.

I will never forget you ever in my life,
Because of the times we had.
I think about the past, I know it would last.
You always made me glad.

Your face is like a sunshine, because you brighten up my day.
That’s how I know you’re my angel above.
I know that in every way.
