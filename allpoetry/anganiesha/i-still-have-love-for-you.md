---
title: "I Still Have Love For you"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "i-still-have-love-for-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8297-I-Still-Have-Love-For-you-by-Anganiesha"
---

No matter what I go through, you are always on myMind.I wish that we could get back together, because youAre a one of a kind.When you told me you were coming overMy smile stayed on my face.Deep down in my heart I know for a factThat no one could take your place.You make me feel good; you make me care about the worldYou know that I am always here for you.I’m always willing to listen; you know I’ll always be your friendI still love you and I cannot pretend.I think about your smile that always stayed On your face.I think about when you came in the roomYou blessed me with your grace.To let you know one more thingI really love you with all of my heartThinking about us when we were togetherAlways make my heart fall apart
