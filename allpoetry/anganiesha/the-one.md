I always knew you were the one
For me.
The one for me, we’re meant to be.
I knew I would love you for
Now and forever.
It was the happiest day of my life
When we got together.

I knew you were the one who would
Brighten up my life.
The years we spent together, I hoped
To be your wife.
I know that you are the one for me,
I know that because without you I can’t see 

That’s how I really know that you’re
The one for me. 
Because no one else can replace you,
And in my heart we’re meant to be.
