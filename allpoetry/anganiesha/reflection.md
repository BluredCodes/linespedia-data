---
title: "Reflection?"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "reflection"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I look in the mirror
I think that I see you, but
No it is only me.

The reflection of myself
But yet I saw you standing there
Smiling and looking at me

I close my eyes and open them again
To see if I was out of my mind
I saw you beside me you looked right at me
But yet it is me that I find

I rub my eyes and start talking to myself
Because I know that you were standing there
I look in the mirror, it is still my reflection
But I could’ve sworn that I felt you near.

Am I going crazy? do I miss you that much?
I think I am going insane.
It feels like I’m dreaming I need to wake up
It feels like I’m in some kinda game
