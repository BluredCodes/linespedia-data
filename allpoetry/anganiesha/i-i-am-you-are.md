---
title: "I…   I Am… You Are…"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "i-i-am-you-are"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8387-I----I-Am--You-Are--by-Anganiesha"

Feel all alone
Feel miserable
Feel sad
Love you so much
Don’t want anyone else

Very sorry
Not the same
Irritated 
Motionless
The blame

My heart
My life
My every thing
A special person
The only one who can dry my eyes
