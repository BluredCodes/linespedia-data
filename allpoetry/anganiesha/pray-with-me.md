---
title: "Pray With Me"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "pray-with-me"

Pray with me.  Do you see what I see?
Feel what I feel? 
Experience the true meaning of life itself.
Life without you isn’t worth living.
You made me realize so much about myself.
You made me understand love, and things that I thought that I would never understand.
Pray with me once again. I miss you and only you. I want you back in my arms
That’s where you belong.
Can you feel my pain right now? I feel so much pain without you in my life. When I hear your voice it makes me strong for the moment. Not to long but long enough.
Pray with me. I want us to get back together. I want you to forgive me and give me another chance. I learned from all of my mistakes. My biggest punishment is not having you in my life. You are so special to me, and I will always love you. Love you eternally.
Pray with me one last time. I know deep down that you still have love for me, and I know you still care. I also realize just because you care about me and love me that you do not have to be with me. Just because you care about someone doesn’t mean that you have to get back together. But I’m praying. Praying silently that you will come back to me. You’re all that I see. I only want you to be with me. So please I ask that you pray with me. I know the good Lord will make everything all right
