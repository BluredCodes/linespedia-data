---
title: "Do You Remember"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "do-you-remember"

Do you remember…
The 1st time we said, “I love you”
And the promising of being faithful
Do you remember…
When I gave my heart to you
And the commitment we made

Do you remember…
When you made me 1st cry,
Your 1st lie,
And when you said good-bye
Do you remember…
How hard I cried,
Remember I wanted you back,
Remember you didn’t have time for me

Remember now…
I don’t want you and don’t want you to have time for me
I don’t miss you
I don’t want you to care or worry about me
Remember now…
I’m never coming back

Remember now…
I don’t care how hard you cry
It’s my turn to say good-bye
I won’t call you if you were the last person on my mind
Remember now…
Don’t ask why
Or where I’ve been
I want you to remember to…
Forget me like I forgot you
Remember don’t call for forgiveness

I want you to forget…
The times we had,
When you made me laugh
I want you to forget…
My smile, my love
How tight you held me
Everything we had
I want you to forget everything, and remember that you had me
