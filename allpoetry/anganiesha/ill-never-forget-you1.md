---
title: "I’ll Never Forget You*1*"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "ill-never-forget-you1"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8190-I-ll-Never-Forget-You-1--by-Anganiesha"

At times I feel like a shadow,That you have left behind.My heart hurts so bad, from all of this pain,My sight will forever be blind.You touched my heart with your presence,And you blessed me with your love.You kept me happy over the years,And from that you will be kept above.My love will never die, my flames are still lit,Only you can blow them out.When I hear your voice, it makes me realize that I love you without a doubt.I wish you could see, you're everything to me,And I will never let you go.I know that you love me,you know that I care,We are meant to be from what I am told.
