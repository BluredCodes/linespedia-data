---
title: "I Know"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "i-know"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I know we’re not together
But I want you here with me.
I want you to know how much I miss you
I want you to really see.

I know we had our good times
I know we had our bad.
I wish that we were still together,
Because I know that we would last.

Every night I think of you
When I picture your face.
I know I need to just move on.
But no one can take your place.

I feel this way because I know
I love you with all of my heart,
But since I know we’re not together.
My heart is torn apart.

I know that I will never find someone
As special as you.
I know that I will never love
Anyone except for you
