title: "Nature Reminds Me Of…"

Love reminds me of a tree blowing
In open winds with colorless
Leaves disguising its true
Meaning of happiness.

It also reminds me of a summer
Breeze gently moving the hair
Across my face, seeing is believing
The wonders of life

Roses are beautiful like the first
Day we were born
Into the scared world of loneliness
That we will never fully understand

Watching the clouds move unevenly brings tears to my eyes because
It makes me think about that
Everybody has to die.
