---
title: "It’s Just You"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "its-just-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

You say that you love me, and say you stillCare.Well you know that I love you, and I missThe love we share.I think about you all the time, especiallyBefore I go to sleep.You know that I would never cheat on you,And you know I’ll never creep.When it was me and you I always hadA smile.But now there is not us. I have no reasonTo smile.If we got back together I’ll show you allMy love.Just to let you know, I put no one else above.I loved the time we spentWhen we was alone.I miss how we always talked, and laughedUpon the phone.All I have is memories, of how it used to be.I miss how we were together.I miss how it was you and me.
