title: "Untrue Love"
writer: "Anganiesha"

I never really understood loveUntil the day that I had met youNow I wish that day had never cameBecause your love for me was untrueAt the beginning you made me feel lovedThen it started to fade awayI should’ve known that this would not lastI don’t even know why I stayedI should’ve left after you first liedBut I gave you another tryWhy? Because you criedI didn’t have the heart to say good-byeBut now I’m saying it, so byeI don’t even want to hear a response nor a replyBecause all it is, is a lieDon’t ask me nothing, no time for whys
