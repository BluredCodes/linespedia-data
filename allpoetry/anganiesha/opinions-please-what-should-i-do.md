IF you didn't read my poems "My Abusive Relationship and Before & Now" please read them so you understand what i am talking about.

but for those who have read them. i want to know should i give him another chance or just leave him alone? we were together for 2 1/2 years. that is my heart. that was the first time that he had ever put his hands on me like that. i don't know what to do.
he apologized and told me he felt less of a man. should i believe him?? would i be stupid if i got back with him? honestly? i relly nees to know because it is bugging me. he is a jealous type of man. i wouldn't do anything to disrespect him though. i just need some advice. so i would really appreciate if you would let me hear *y'all* out on what y'all have to say.
thank you
