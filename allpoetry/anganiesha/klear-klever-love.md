---
title: "Klear & Klever Love"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "klear-klever-love"

Our love is not blind it’s klearThe thought of us apart makes me shed a tearYou are dear to my heartI’ll never let you goTime will only tell the love for you that I shall holdI call it klear with a “K”Because only our love is so pureI have the cure for your every tearThat ran down your face and made you depressedI’m the one you need without getting you undressedImpressed are you on how I show you my skillsYou know that I will never lie to you, I will only keep it realTo the end because you’re my male friendI wouldn’t let nothin happen to you.True we are friends and nothin moreYou know if you ever had a problemI’ll always have an open doorfor you and only you, no one elsebecause you’ve been there for methat’s why I say our love is kleverhowever I feel this way about no one elsethat’s how I know you’re special and I love you like no body else.
