Every time I close my eyes I start toShed a tear.Wondering if we’ll get back togetherWondering if you still care.I always had a dream, a dream aboutyou and me.But now my dreams have gone awayWhy did you let me beEvery time I think of you, I alwaysHad a smileSince I know we’re not together, It seems like I can’t even smile.I miss the times we had, when it wasyou and I.I always cry myself to sleep.It feels like my heart just diedEvery time I see you, I just wantto ask you why.Why did you hurt me the way that you didIt feels like I want to die
