I tell you to stop callin'I tell you to leave me alone.I tell you that it’s overYet you still call me on the phone.You come by unexpectedI still tell you to leave me aloneYou ask can you talk to meBecause I won't pick up the phoneI told you that it's overBut you just don't understandWhy can't you get it through your headThat you are no longer my manIf you would've done me rightWe still would've been togetherEverything that you say to meFlies away like it's a feather.I blocked your number onceDon't think I won't do it againI don't want nothin' to do with youI don't even want to be your friend
