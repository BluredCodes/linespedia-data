title: "My Love For You"
writer: "Anganiesha"

I wish you understandI only want you as my manWhy can’t you just seeThat you mean the world to meI can’t wait till you come homeSo we can argue upon the phoneYou left me all aloneBoo I miss the way you moanI miss your toneI look at your picture and smileThen say you’ll be home in a little whileI dial your number and bug your gram,Damn! I miss you. I can’t sleep at night.I miss the way you argued and the way we used to fightI wanna hold you tight, I’m mad cause you’re not hereI wish that you could catch each and every one of my tearsI have fears-fears of loosing youThat’s why I try to tell you I appreciate everything thatYou do, you my boo, and I’ma love you to the endI want to let you know that I will always be your friend,Your lover, and someone to talk to, I’ll never turn my back.You know I care a lot boo, and I will always turn back, to you andOnly you cause I love you
