A new morning
A new day
A new day has come
For me to start all over with my life
Yesterday is long gone
Tomorrow is today

Today I can see how beautiful things are
And now I can sang a new song
I don’t remember my pain
What I can remember now is my gain
Many storms have traveled my way
But now I stand and face another day

A new morning
A new day
A new day has come
For I know that I’m not the only one
Who has lived through the pressures of life
And the enormous sacrifice
I will no longer be stressed
I smile because I have been blessed
God has given me a brand new day
He made me stop crying and took my tears away 

A new morning
A new day
A new day has come

By Anganiesha G & Lisa Watson
       Feb 9 2001
