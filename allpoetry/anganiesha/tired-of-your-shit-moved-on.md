Don’t you know that I am tired, tired of hearing your sh*t
I’m not worried about the things you’re doing, so go somewhere and make a wish
I’m tired of unkept promises, and swearing you’re going to do well
How many times do I have to tell you? I want you to go straight to hell.

You made me have so much pain, and made me feel so low.
You used to call me out of my name, and stayed sayin that I was a hoe.
I took so much sh*t from you, and I took it for the last time
I meant that I would never get back with you, a person like me you will never find.

I have to much respect for myself to let someone who don’t bring me down
I have stronger walls that you can’t knock down. I promise you won’t make me frown
So go somewhere and play your games, ‘cause your games have been terminated here
I am so glad that we are not together, from that I made my own mother shed a tear
