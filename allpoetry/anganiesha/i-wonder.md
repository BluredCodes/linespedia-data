I wonder what you are doin’		Before I call you on the phone.I know we’re not together,But I wonder if you’re alone.Sometimes I think your not,Because of the way you answerThe phone.I really hope that you are,You are all aloneI wonder when you go out,Do you think about me.How things were, and how theyUsed to be.I wonder if you see my faceEvery time you close your eyes.I wonder if you think about us,I hope it’s not a surpriseI wonder if you knew, withoutYou I just die.I wonder if you knew, all I doIs cry.I wonder if you see how muchYou mean to me.I wonder if you care, if not justLet me be.
