---
title: "Wanting you to know"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "wanting-you-to-know"
source: "AllPoetry"

I wake up every morning, and I think about you.
	I miss us being together, and I know you do too.
	Every time I see you, I think about what we did.
	I remember when we first met; I was just a kid.

	When we got together in 1996.
	I knew you were special even before our first kiss.
	I fell in love with your heart and your style.
I knew that we would be together for more than a little while.

Every time I see your picture, it makes me wanna cry.
Some times I feel I can’t go on, then I just want to die.
My heart is weak my soul is dying, because it is not you and me.
I wish you knew how much I miss you.
I wish that you could see.
