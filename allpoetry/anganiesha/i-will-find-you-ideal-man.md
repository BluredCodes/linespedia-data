---
title: "I Will Find You( Ideal Man)"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "i-will-find-you-ideal-man"

I will find you.
I will not look for you
But I will find you.
I know you are out there
Looking and waiting for me.

I will find you.
It doesn’t matter how long it takes,
Because you are worth the wait.
But I guarantee that I will find you

And when I do
I promise I will never to let you go.
Let you go, no no
You are my ideal man
Someone who understands and
Will not take me for granted

I will find you,
And when I do
Everything will be so perfect
Us together, just me and you

I will find you
Because you are worth
The wait
