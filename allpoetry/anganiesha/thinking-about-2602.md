I keep writing ‘cause I can’t sleep
I’m thinking about 2/6/02
When you put your hands on me

I don’t wanna cry, well actually
I can’t any more
You used to have a place in my heart,
But now I have a closed door

I want to sleep but it’s like I can’t breathe
I shut my eyes for a minute
Just so I can feel a breeze,
And feel this burden lift off of me

I’m thinking about February 6
It all happened so fast
I don’t have to worry about you any more
Thank god everything has passed
