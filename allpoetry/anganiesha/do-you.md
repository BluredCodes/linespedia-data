---
title: "~Do You~"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "do-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8185--Do-You--by-Anganiesha"
---

Do you know you make me cry, and you’re the
Only one who can dry my eyes.
Do you realize that I love you, and you’re the only
Guy I experienced true feelings for
Do you understand you were my best friend
Before my man
Do you think about the years we spent together,
And I never cheated on you once
Do you know that I love you with
All of my heart
Do you realize that I’m hurting because
We’re not together
Do you understand that I don’t want
No other man
Do you think about the 1st time
We got together
Do you know I miss how you held
Me in your arms
Do you realize that I’m not the same cause we’re
Not together
Do you understand that I will hurt until you
Come back to me
Do you think we’ll get back together
	~Do You~
