---
title: "I Lay Alone In Darkness"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "i-lay-alone-in-darkness"

Darkness is all I gaze into,When there is not you.I lay alone in darkness.Without you I turn blue.Not talking to you cuts my heart.And I cannot go on.I miss the way you kiss and touch me.So what did I do wrong.When I hold you, it’s like a dream,A dream that will go on forever.You remind me of a crystal rose,A crystal rose that clever.You will never go away, you’llAlways be in my heart, until god takes me away.No matter what happens I’ll always love you,And I am here to stay.You know my love will never changeIt will stay the same.I lay alone in darkness,Because I feel I am the blame
