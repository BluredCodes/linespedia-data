I realize that I make you cry more thanI make you smile.I just want to work things outSo I can see your smile.I realize you love meI do love you too,It’s just hard for me to show my feelings.The reason why it’s hard, because I’ve been hurt, I’ve Been stumped and rolled over like dirt.I realize you care and love me to death,I don’t know why I treat you so bad.You mean everything to me from the bottom of my heart,I don’t mean to make you sad.
