title: "I Love You"

Only I can tell you what youMean to me.No one else can replace you in myHeart we’re meant to be.I hope you understand I’m sorry forWhat I have done.I never meant to make you cry, I just wantedTo have some fun.But now I realize my fun is pain, I didn’tMean to hurt your heart.I hope you forgive me because I am sorry,I didn’t want us to be torn apart.
