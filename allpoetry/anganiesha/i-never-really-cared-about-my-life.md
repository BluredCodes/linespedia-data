I never really cared about my life, until I seen whatYou went through today.I thank the lord that you are alive, and for that I haveA lot to say.You mad me laugh, you made me cry, today I prayed andJust said why.Seeing your face, hearing you talk dried all the tearsAway from my eyes.You’re a good friend, we talk everyday; we laugh about thingsNo one understands.We’re to young for problems; we go through it all, we need a manWho does not demand.Gia g, my GG, I’m glad that you’re alrightNow we can laugh all night on the phone.I never really cared about my life until now.
