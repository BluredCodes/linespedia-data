---
title: "I Cry"
writer: "Anganiesha"

Every night after I talk to you
I cry myself to sleep.
I pray to God to let you see
Without you I can’t sleep.

I cry at night I cry when I,
Cry when I think of you.
I don’t understand what went wrong
What went wrong with us two

You know that you mean the world
to me. So why did you make me cry.
And leave me with questions that you
Can’t even answer, all I wanted to know was why

I’m sad without you, I’m glad when I’m with you
But I still know that we’re not together.
Still not together, it makes me cry
Only you can dry my eyes.
