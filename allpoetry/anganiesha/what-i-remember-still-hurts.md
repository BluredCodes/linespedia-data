The last time I saw your face was…I don’t rememberBut the last time I heard your voice was…I think SeptemberI remember the last time you held me in your arms…But now I am deafI’m deaf because you took your loveThe night you had leftWhy did you break your promise to me, andWhy did you always lieWhy did you ever stop lovin’ me,When all I ever did was tryIf I saw you today, I’d shake your hand,Because to me you were no type of manYou did me so wrong, from that I became strongBut I still think that you wouldn’t understand
