---
title: "I Will Never Love Again"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "i-will-never-love-again"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8372-I-Will-Never-Love-Again-by-Anganiesha"
---

The pain you put me throughMade me realize I don’t need a manYou thought that I needed youBut you really didn’t understandI don’t even want a friendBecause I’m afraid of being lied toThat’s how I really know I won’t loveI won’t love anyone else againI’ll never love again because of youYou lied to my heart and made it turn blueI thought we had loveBut I guess I was living aloneYou don’t have to worry about me cryingOr calling upon the phoneI’ll never love againBecause of the memories I have of youI will never be the sameCause all I see is youYou are the blame for my sorrow and sadnessThe hurt and confusionMy hate and my lonelinessI don’t want anybody else…Because I’ll never love again
