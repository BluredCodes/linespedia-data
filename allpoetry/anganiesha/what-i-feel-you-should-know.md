You make me feel unwantedBecause of the way that you treat meBut then you say that you love meThen you want to let me beI don’t understand what I did to you To make you feel this wayYou know that I love youI tell you each and every dayI need to leave you aloneand stop callin you on the phoneBut without you I feel aloneI need to talk to you on the phoneYou are the only one I care aboutYou still make me smileI’m going crazy cause we’re not togetherWe’ve been apart for a little whileThis love I have for youI will have for no one elseI make this promise to youBecause I don’t love no body elseYou always accuse me of cheatingBut you’re the one lying to my faceYou’re the only one that I’m worried aboutCan’t no one else take your place. You know I care about you a lotThat’s why you act the way you doBut I just wanted to let you knowThat I am nobody’s fool.
