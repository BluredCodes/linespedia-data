title: "3 Letters I Received"

I received the 1st letter,Without a name nor a reply.I knew that it came from youBecause the first sentence you asked me why.You’re the only one I knowWho would send me a letter with no replyYou wanted me to come backYou couldn’t accept my good-byeThe 2nd letter I received You wanted to talk to me on the phoneYou told me the time you were going to call And you said you knew I was aloneI wanted to know how you got my numberYou called and I asked you from whoYou said “don’t wonder I know more than that”I hung up, I didn’t want to talk to youThe 3rd letter I received…You wanted to meet me at a small placeYou left the time and day you’d be thereBut I didn’t want to see your faceSomethin told me not to goSomethin told me I should stayI got the courage to get up and see youWhen I got there you had nothing to say(I don’t want you no more)
