1* To never hurt me/2* Never leave me/3* Always love me
4* Never cheat on me/5* Never change
6* Won’t lie to me/7* make me happy
8*He won’t do it again/9*be disrespectful

I’m tired of broken promises, you know promises
That you can’t keep
I hope you don’t think I’ma wait for you, you
Know sit around and weep

You promised you’ll never cheat on me,
But once again you have lied
You look me in my eyes, lie to my face, I’m
So used to it I can’t even cry

Why should I try, why should I promise
When you’re going to continue on lying to me
You’re not going to hurt me, I deserve someone better than you,
So I decided to let you be

Since you can’t keep promises, I’m not going to keep mine,
I want you to know how it feels.
I don’t want you as a man, you’ll never understand
Leave me alone, Why? Cause you’re not real
