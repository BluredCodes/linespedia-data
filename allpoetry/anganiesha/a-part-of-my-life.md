---
title: "A Part Of My Life"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "a-part-of-my-life"

If it wasn’t for you, it wouldn’t be me.
I told you once before that we are meant to be.
I love everything about you, especially the way you make me feel.
Sometimes I look myself in the mirror and ask if this is real

I thank God for you, because you are the best thing that ever happened to me.
If I’d loose you, I’d go crazy, my sight will no longer see.
You are a big part of my life; I hope we will never end.
I can’t face the fact of us not together, and also you being my friend

God knows that I was hurt, and I deserved someone better.
That’s why he had found you, and he made us get together.
You are a big part of my life, and you will always have my heart.
I hope that you don’t break my heart, and then try to tear us apart

Everyday I wake up, and smile because I have you
I reminisce on the times we shared, and when you told me you loved me too.
I remember the day we got together, I told you we were meant to be.
Ever since then, our love has grown; you mean the whole wide world to me

You are a big part of my life; you kept me together, and showed me that I can love again.
I love you more than my life, more than my soul, more than you will ever know
You changed my life around, you made me smile when I had frowned, you were there to wipe my tears away.
You told me you’ll never leave me, we’ll always stay together, and our love shall never fade away
