title: "Dream Come True"

I yearn to live with you in my life.My dreams at night, you ask me to be your wife.Come hold me in your arms, hold me real tightNever let me go, and don’t say good nightYou’ve tinted my life with joy and gladnessYou’ve chased away my pain, sorrow and sadness.With your soft heart and your lovely faceA smile so beautiful, it cannot be erasedCan’t help but sleep with you on my mindWithout your sweet love my heart is so blindCome here my boo, come take controlCome drown in my heart and stay in my soulSleep beside me, as I whisper in your ear“You’ll never be alone, I will always be here”I tell myself I can’t live without you nearIt would kill my soul with just one tear.I lay in darkness alone in my roomI miss you so much, all I hear is a sad tuneMy tears start crying, my heart turn blueI’m glad that I found you, and I’m glad that youLoved me too.
