---
title: "You’re Back in My Life"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "youre-back-in-my-life"

I missed you so much; I missed hearing your voice,Something told me to pick up the phone and call.I did what I felt, you answered the phone,You sounded like you were all alone.I couldn’t believe it was you, I asked how you wereAnd if you had thought about me.You said you were fine, and that I had crossed your mind,And people told you we were meant to be.That put a smile on my face, I heard the same thing,I miss how it was you and me.You always kept me happy; we were together for so longYou were the best thing that ever happened to meIf we got back together our life would be better,Because you know I would never do you wrong.We can start where we left off, December 30th is five yearsThe love that I have for you is still strong
