---
title: "Sounds of your voice"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "sounds-of-your-voice"

Every time you talk to me, it feels like I’m going to melt.
I like the things you say to me, the love for you I felt.
Even though we broke up again, I liked to hear you say.
“We will get back together again if it was meant to be that way.

More and more I miss you, especially hearing the sound of your voice.
I would never break up with you, because that is not my choice.

If you felt what I felt when you talked to me.
Our love would grow so beautifully; there would be no other girl in your life but me.
Every time I think of you, I would hear you say.
You go your way, and I’ll go mine, I didn’t want it to be that way.

The sound of your voice is mellow and sweet; I miss how it was you and me.
The sounds of your love are so beautiful, I think we are meant to be.
