---
title: "The Shadow You left Behind"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "the-shadow-you-left-behind"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8307-The-Shadow-You-left-Behind-by-Anganiesha"
---

Every time I hear a sound I turn around
but it's just the wind.
I try not to miss you, but I cannot pretend
you were more to me than just my friend.

I closed my eyes and counted to ten, wishing
that you were here with me.
I open my eyes and there you were,
no! It’s your shadow that only I could see

I dried my eyes and tried to be strong
promising to keep you in my heart.
It makes me sad you left your shadow behind
not even knowing you were still on my mind

I wish I could hold your shadow forever
instead of it fading away
you left your shadow behind along with my heart,
and leaving me with nothing to say.
