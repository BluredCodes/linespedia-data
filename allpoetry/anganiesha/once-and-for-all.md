---
title: "Once And For All"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "once-and-for-all"
source: "AllPoetry"

You know what I ask myself, why do 
I Stay around?
You never make me happy, and all I do
Is frown.
I only blame myself, because I let myself
Go back to you.
The more and more I think about it I know
I had become your fool.

I put my trust in you, I gave you my heart,
You said we would never part.
The lies begin to come, I wasn’t number one,
I thought that you have given’ me your heart

2 ½ years was a waste, I want to spit in your face,
I realized your face isn’t worth my spit.
You caused nothing but pain, I don’t even want you to
Remember my name.
I wish we never met
