title: "How To Release Stress"
writer: "Anganiesha"

Every time I feel stressed out
I just pick up a pen
I write down what I feel at the moment
And what ever else happens then.

I begin to feel better when as I start to write
Because I feel my stress going away
I express what I feel, the pain goes away
From with every thing that I had to say

When I write, it feels as though
A burden was taken off of me
I thank the lord for helping me out
And guiding me so I could see

When I'm done writing, I think about what happened
And say, "why did I let it bother me"
I'm a strong woman, I shouldn't be stressed
There should be nothin' that's wrong with me.
