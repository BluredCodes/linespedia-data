I didn’t mean to make you leave.I only wanted you to stay with me.I didn’t mean for things to get so out of hand.You were my one and only man.You’ were right about when you said I got my priorities Mixed up. I was to blind to see.Now it is too late. You are gone out of my life.I hope not for good. You are my strength. You make me go on through the day.I didn’t mean for you to leave.You gave me the world.There is nothing that I wouldn’t do for you.I wish you could take away my pain. I want your forgivenessI will never lie to you again.I will never cheat on you.I changed everything around.I hope it’s not too late.I didn’t mean to make you leave.I just want you to believe.Believe that I was so blind to see that someone Cared about me so much. It was right in front of my face.And I ignored it. Not intentionally, just accidentally.I wish you only knew how much I am suffering.I am not myself. I need you here with me.I just want you to give me another chance. If you do, you will never have to leave again…I didn’t mean to make you leaveDo anyone feel how i feel right about now
