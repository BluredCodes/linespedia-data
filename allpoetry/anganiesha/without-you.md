---
title: "Without You"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "without-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8230-Without-You-by-Anganiesha"
---

My heart fell apart.Words have no saying.Love does not take part.My soul dies, when I am not with you.Sounds aren’t worth hearing,Because they are not from you.Touch turns into nothingWhispers fade awayAll I do is cry, with nothing else to sayLaughs go away, smiles fade away.Without you my heart just diesBecause I can’t go on another day.
