I will never stop caring about you.
I will never stop loving you.
I will never stop missing you.
I will only stop missing you when you come back to me

Come back to me, I will show you so much.
Part of that much is how I have changed.
I changed for the better, and to also prove to you
That I always loved you: You for you.

Ps.
I’m sorry and I love you
