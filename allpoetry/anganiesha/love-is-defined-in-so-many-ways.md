Love… love is defined in so many ways that
People cannot truly express a real meaning for it.
But the way that I feel about you, is a way
That I have never felt about anyone In my entire life.

You are beautiful inside and out, that’s what makes
Me love you so much.
It’s not just about your touch. It’s how you make me feel when
I’m around you.

Around you, it’s like a gift from heaven. I can’t really
Express how I feel about you because I don’t want to
Scare you away. I don’t want you to take things a whole nother
Way.

When I close my eyes, I can see you. I picture
Your face on everything that I do. It’s true
That I really do love you. I can’t live with you
And can’t live without you. 

I smile when I hear your name in the streets.
Because I know people want to be me
And they want to have you in their life. But they
Can’t because I will always be there no matter what the situation may be.

No one compares to you. You already know my deepest secrets.
You know more about me than I know about myself.
I know you in and out also. It’s like we were made for
Each other.

I love you more than you will ever know.
I will never stop telling you that I love you.
At times it might be hard for me t express it, but I’m sure you know
That I do. I will do anything for you.

When you cry, I cry. We share each other’s pain.
I always hold you in my arms because I love you.
I just love you. For you being you, and holding on to what we
Call a Relationship. And from that, I will always love you.
You made me the person that I am today.
