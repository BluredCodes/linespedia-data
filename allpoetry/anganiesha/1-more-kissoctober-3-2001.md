title: "1 More Kiss…October 3, 2001"

Can I have 1?Just one?Just one more kiss before you are finally out of my life.Life as I only knew it was with you…But now you’re gone.My life isn’t worth living.I know I need to move on,But with me is where you belong.Can I have a kiss?Please just give me one more.I ignore the fact that you are not in my life.I don’t understand, what is a harmless kiss?Are you afraid of what might happen after a kiss?Don’t be. All I’m asking for is one before you walk out of my door.My heart is on the floor, because you no longer want it.All I wanted was a simple kiss. Even if it doesn’t mean anything to you,It means everything to me.Well can I kiss you?Just one time before everything that we ever knew is gone.Just one more kiss, if not I only have one wish.That wish is… that you don’t walk out of my door.You will stay with me at all times, reaching your arms out to me,But no more because you’re gone. I hope it’s not to late.To late for me to have just 1 more kiss.If so I will leave you alone and never bother you again…For 1 more kiss
