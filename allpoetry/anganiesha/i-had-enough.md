How can you say you love me, and don’tKnow what it means,Because all you do is tell me lies.Well I have something to say, so listen up clearlyI’m done with you now goodbye.I’m tired of cryin, I’m tired of hurtingI can do bad all by myself.All I want is a man who will treat me rightInstead of arguing and trying to fight.I can’t take no more of your selfish waysEverything’s always about you.Well you know what, you got it,It’s all about you,Because I’m happy that we are through
