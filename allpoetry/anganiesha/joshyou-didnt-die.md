title: "Josh...You Didn't Die"
writer: "Anganiesha"

You never had a worry
You always had a smile upon your face. 
I can't believe you're gone
No one can ever take your place 

I saw you the day before you died
You smiled and said good-bye
I always hated the word "good-bye"
It made me feel like somebody had died.

The next day I had heard
that Joshua Rodriguez had died
I said yeah right as I cried
Because I didn't even say good-bye

I got real sick and I had to go home
I called my mother upon the phone
I didn't believe it, Josh was dead
I was in denial that Josh was dead

His smile is gone forever
I'm not the same I will never be better
For Josh he was so clever
No one hated him, hated him ever

I looked up in the sky
As I cried and asked God why
Why did he let Josh Die
Without us even saying good-bye
