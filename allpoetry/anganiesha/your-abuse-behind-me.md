---
title: "Your Abuse Behind Me"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "your-abuse-behind-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

I can’t explain the way I feel, it’s
Like I have no emotions inside.
I cried my last tear, got rid of you as my fear
I will be damned if I shed another tear

So I’ma kiss you’re a** good-bye
I’m done trying to try
You have really pushed me beyond my limit
The one time I couldn’t breathe, you asked me to leave,
Then asked if you can come back to me

I feel blank inside, my love turned to hate,
You will think about me more than I will you.
I can’t believe you said you loved me, and wanted me to say it
Back, for what? The love you had for me was lust
