God only knows…Why you treat me like thisWhy you say the things you sayGod only knows…Why I still put up with you after you abuse me by verbal phrasesGod only knows…Why I love you so muchWouldn’t cheat on youAnd give you the respect you don’t deserveGod only knows…Why I took you back all them timesWhy I apologized when it wasn’t my faultAnd shut up when you ask me toGod only knows…Why I cry andStay hurt instead of leaving youGod only knows…Why I tell you I love youTry to prevent argumentsAnd listen to youGod only knows…That I would never cheat on youNever lie to youAnd tell you what ever you wanted to know.God knows…That I am a good womanTrust worthyReliableIndependent womanHonestGod knows…That I deserve better than youI made youI can hurt you worse than you hurt meI can leave you at any timeGod knows…I am strongAnd most importantly…God knows I don’t have to write this to express how I feelHe knows my achievements
