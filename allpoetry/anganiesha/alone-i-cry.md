---
title: "Alone I Cry"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "alone-i-cry"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8187-Alone-I-Cry-by-Anganiesha"
---

Why do you get so mad at meI tell you I love you so endlessly.You get mad off of men that I don't even worry about.You make me feel like a child, teary eyed and then even poutI cry alone because you don't trust me, you think I’ma do you wrong.You have to remember that I’m not you; my love for you is very strongI cry alone cause you don't realize all the men don't compare to you.I always been faithful, I never even cheated, I always stayed loyal to you.But you still don't realize that I cry alone, Stop accusing me when you're the one who's doing wrong.All I want is to be there, you need to cry alone,You need to really realize that my love for you is very strong
