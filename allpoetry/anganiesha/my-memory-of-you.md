---
title: "My Memory Of You"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "my-memory-of-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8300-My-Memory-Of-You-by-Anganiesha"
---

I never had the chance to tell you, that I’m
Sorry for making you mad.
I wish we were back together again
Because without you I am sad

I spent a lot of my life with you,
And without you it’s like I am dead.
I will never forget when we first got together,
Remember I was just a kid.

You’re the first guy that I ever loved,
And the first to make me cry,
The first person I experienced true feelings for,
And the only one who could dry my eyes

I thank the lord that I was with you,
He blessed me each and everyday.
If I had one wish, my wish would be
That our love would never fade away.
