---
title: "Lovers Song 2001"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "lovers-song-2001"
source: "AllPoetry"

1As of yesterday
2I tried to say
3I wanted you to …stay.
4You’re on my mind
5You’re one of a kind
6My past I kept be…hind

7I woke up today
8With a lot to say
9 I’m glad you’re back in my…life
10I want to be with you
11The whole way through
12Without you my soul is… blue

13A thousand tears I cried
14No more good-byes
15 I’m left with questions like…why?
16You know I’m not shy, is there a reason why
17That you make me …cry

18I love you so much
19I miss your touch
20Do you think our love was …lust
21You said you want to be with me
22 I’m all you see
23And that we’re meant to …be

24You take me away, from the pain
25And you bring me paradise.
26I loved you
27All so much, and you know I miss your touch.
28You make me see the light, and the stars that’s shinning bright

Repeat Lines 1-6 2 Times
