---
title: "The Love I Feel"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "the-love-i-feel"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8227-The-Love-I-Feel-by-Anganiesha"
---

The love that I feel for you is true.
	The love I need lies within you.
	I still remember the day we met.
	From then to know
My love has kept.

Freely flowing love, the channels of my soul,
The love that I feel has began to grow.
Touching my heart as
Well as you did.
I fell in love with you, when I was just a kid.

Before I met you,
I stood alone,
Not talking to anyone
Just friends on the phone.
Now there is you,
The empty space has filled
The love that you’ve given me has such a thrill.

For it is you who brightens up my life.
My will to love
I cherish no one above.
My heart could not breathe
I could not live.
You know that I love you, and I would never fib.

Pain and agony has gone away.
I thank god for you, in each and every way.
For the first time in my life, I am complete.
You know that I am here for you, and I will always be your treat.

Knowing you are here,
Brightens up my day
Knowing that we were together
Makes me happy in every way

Something that I always knew is that it would be me and you.
No one else would believe me, because they never had a clue.
