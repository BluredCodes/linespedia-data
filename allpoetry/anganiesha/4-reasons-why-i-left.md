---
title: "4 Reasons Why I Left"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "4-reasons-why-i-left"
source: "AllPoetry"

You are a liar(Everything that came out of your mouth was a lie)(You couldn’t remember what you said 10 minutes ago)You are a cheater(You cheated on me when we were together for several months)(When you asked me not to talk to any men I listened, but you still were receiving numbers)You hurt my heart(Because you said you loved me, and then you did me wrong)(You said we would always be together)(You let the other women lead you to believe I was cheating)I am tired of crying (Because of every time you did me wrong(Because you stayed out all night)(Because you lied)those are the 4 main reasons why I left
