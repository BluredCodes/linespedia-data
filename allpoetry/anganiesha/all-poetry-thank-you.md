Thank you for your honesty
And the advice you have give me.
I’m glad that you really cared about what happened
And gave me the strength to let him be.

You all have made me feel better
In a way I can’t describe.
I used to be scared and wanted to cry
Now I don’t feel like I need to hide.

Thank you for your support
And the comforting you have given me
You opened my eyes, I thought I was blind
Thank you for making me see.

Maybe he did love me, Maybe he didn’t
Maybe he was just playing with my heart
I’m glad its all over, I won’t have to be sad
Now I have a chance for a new start.

Thank You
