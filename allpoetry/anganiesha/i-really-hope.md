---
title: "I Really Hope"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "i-really-hope"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/39648-I-Really-Hope-by-Anganiesha"
---

I hope we get back together
Again.
Hopefully you know you’re my
Male best friend.
Hopefully I can show you I
Changed me ways.
Hopefully you can tell, and I
Wish you would stay.

Hopefully you can see how much
You mean to me.
Because without you, my heart
Could not be.
I hope that you can tell, that you
Are my heart.
The only guy I loved, and I fell apart.

Every time you close your eyes, can you
feel my pain.
Hopefully you can take my pain away,
And make my strength regain.
Hopefully we will get back together again.
Together again you and I.
I hope that you do understand me,
Because without you I just die.
