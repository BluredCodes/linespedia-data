I am free from you nowThere is no burden upon my heartThe memory I have of you is gone.I left you with no good-byeI didn’t even have to tell you whyNo reply, surprised that I didn’t even cryFree from you I will no longer cryScreaming I wanna dieWhy? I ask the lord to tryCalming my nerves from every one of you liesFree from you I will no longer be stressedUndressed from your eyes that lieGod bless you for not asking why,I told you too many times I’ma fly Fly away high in the skyI no longer feel unwantedI no longer wanna dieMy life is too important to be ruinedBy what you’re doin because you ruinedYour chances on being with meBut you’re the once who decided to plead the 5th degreeOh please shut your mouth I’m almost doneI always told you you’d miss me, I told you I’m #1
