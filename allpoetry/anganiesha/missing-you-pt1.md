Can you change the way that I feel about you?Sometimes I wish that I never met you.I don’t mean that in a bad way, I’m just saying, that I wouldn’t feel the way that I feel now.I miss you so much. The only way that I feel better is when I write.When I write about you.I feel better when I write you letters and put my poems in there.I feel a burden lifting off of me. Like the Lord is telling me that everything is going to be all right. The lord will never fail me. I hope you never fail me.I hope that we can get back together and work out our problems.You can’t change the way I feel about you. You can be as ignorant as you want to be, but it will still not change the fact that I still love you.
