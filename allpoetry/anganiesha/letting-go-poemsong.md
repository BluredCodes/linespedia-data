---
title: "Letting Go (Poem/Song)"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "letting-go-poemsong"

Don’t you know that I loved you from the start
From my heart I hoped we’d never part
I had love but I never had love like this
I cherished us, and I hoped for no eternal bliss

Close your eyes can you see me rubbing you
Holding you, kissing you, oh ooh ooh ooh.
I loved you, but it’s time to let you go
You hurt my heart, and you lied, you made me feel low

I supported you, and tried to give you what you need
You made me feel like a cut that always bleeds
Unborn seed I felt so far away
Sometimes I wish that I would’ve never stayed
I got played ‘cause I put my faith in you
This was a lesson in love that made me hate you
