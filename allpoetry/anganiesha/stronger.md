stronger i have become since i told 
you to get away from me. 
a shattered heart no more 
since i've shown you the open door 
Don't try to come back to me 
Because you and i will never be 
stronger i have become 
my eyes can finally 
see 
Shut is the door, will always be 
Never to be opened, I am totally free! 
Stronger, yes I have become strong 
For I've put you right where you belong. 
Now, forever you shall stay outside, 
Never again to walk by my side. 
By my side you will never be there 
hurting my heart 
and making me shed a tear. 
i learned my lesson 
and it made me stronger 
you're out of my life 
our relationship will not be longer 
I will go on, past, is my mistake 
But in learning came my big break. 
I've learned this lesson well; that I am worthy 
Even though you tried to make me feel dirty. 
I'm worthy of so much more love than you gave 
You can not keep me in your
