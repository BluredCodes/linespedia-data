---
title: "Let Me Tell You"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "let-me-tell-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8360-Let-Me-Tell-You-by-Anganiesha"
---

Let me tell you...
That I am tired of being mistreated
Lied to
Abused by verbal abuse
And constantly accused

Let me tell you…
That I know you’re the one cheating
Denying who you are talking to
And what you did today

Let me tell you…
That you are no longer going to treat me like I’m nothing
Nor use me for your personal use
And try to keep me around while you do your dirt

Let me tell you…
That I am a woman not a toy
I have feelings and emotions just like you
I cry every time you hurt me

Let me tell you…
That I love you
I care about you
And that you will no longer hurt me

Let me tell you…
I put up with your lies for the last time
The hurt you caused within my heart
And the suffering that was caused without a reason
Will never happen again

Let me tell you…
That you will always have a place in my heart
But I will no longer be with you
Comfort you no longer will I do
Care about you always will

Let me tell you…
This is that last and final time I will talk to you
The last time you will hear from me
The last you will ever see my face
Let me tell you…
The pain I feel now will no longer take place
I will find someone who will treat me how I want to be treated
Not half stepping everything 

I am done telling you…
