---
title: "Forever Always"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "forever-always"
source: "AllPoetry"

Forever always would be you and I
Forever always is what we have
What we have is special
Forever always my candle will still be lit
Still be lit, my flame will only burn for you

Forever always you will be in my heart
In my heart is one person, and it’s you
Forever always I’m going to love you
Going to love you I will never stop

Forever always we will be together
Be together until we die
Forever always you will be someone special to me
To me you will always be loved
