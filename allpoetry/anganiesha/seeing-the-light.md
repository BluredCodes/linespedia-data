---
title: "Seeing The Light"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "seeing-the-light"

I really thought that we had something special.
But once again I was wrong. You made me strong by doing me
Wrong. Thank you! I finally see the light. I’m tired of our fights.
You’re ignorant, self-centered, and low.

I don’t know why I ever wasted my time on you.
You’re not worth it, not even a quick glance.
I don’t miss you, I don’t care: I don’t like you,
You made me hate you.

I spit on your name, I don’t know your face
I erased you from my heart. You no long have my key.
You’re weak. You try to make me mad by mentioning other females’ names.
Your game is over, just like our relationship.

I won’t call you, I won’t say I love you: you’re the last person on my mind.
I will never hurt again. We will never be friends. Someone like me you will never
Find. Remind yourself that you messed up: we could’ve had something. Nah, we never
Had nothin’; that’s somethin’… that’s the way love goes
