---
title: "So Deep"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "so-deep"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8291-So-Deep-by-Anganiesha"
---

My feeling I have for you
Are so deep.
With you in my life, I feel
So complete.

Every time you're near, 
I don't shed a tear,
But when you are not here
All I feel is fear.

When we were together
My love was so deep.
My love was so deep 
My heart felt complete

But now that you went away
My hearts not complete.
My hearts not complete.
But my love is still deep
