title: "You’re Sorry?"

I always accept your apologyThen you hurt me all over againI don’t know what to do with youI know I just can’t win I’ve always been understandingAnd always willing to forgiveI don’t know how much more I can takeI don’t know how you can even liveAnd keep telling me lies,I cry not knowing why you hurt meI never done you wrongOnce again you say you’re sorry.Then begin to sing a songYou’re not sorry,You can’t even spell sorrySo I’ma spell it for youI’ma spell it “so-sorry-I-met-you”And leave you alone for good, I know I should, it’ll make me feel betterI know you’re sorry now cause we will never get back togetherHowever if you know the meaning of sorryI might decide to forgive youBut I realized you won’t, so I just wanted to sayThat I… am really sorry for you too
