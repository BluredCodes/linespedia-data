---
title: "Best Thing"
writer: "Anganiesha"

I don’t know where I went wrong: I never was that strong,
I knew it time and time again.
To be more than honest, you meant a lot to me.
You were one of my favorite best friends.

You always made me smile, made me laugh,
When I felt like I was at the end.
I thank you for everything, everything you have done
And for that you will always be my friend

I can’t pretend that you were never the 
Best thing that ever happened to me.
When I was down you made me smile,
The way you made me feel was free

I thank you for listening: I thank you for caring
And always being there for me.
I’m sorry for everything I’ve done, to me you’re number 1,
I miss you, please come back to me
