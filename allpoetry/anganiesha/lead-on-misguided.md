title: "Lead On & Misguided"
writer: "Anganiesha"

Cry...I'm not going to shead a tear,
cause i know no matter what happens
you'll always be here.
If I'm in pain, i'll make sure to regain.
Regain my strength from my sadness, because
my happiness will always remain.

Cry...I'm not going to shead a tear,
I thought no matter what happenes that
you will always be here.
I thought i would be crying, because
of your lying, i'm always trying and whining
cause i cared, your love was blinding

Cry...I'm not going to shead a tear
i finally had to realize that you 
didn't want to be here.
you're always hurting my heart and taking
my kindness for weakness
i thought you had changed so i stayed then got played

WHY EVEN LOVE??
