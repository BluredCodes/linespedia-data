---
title: "Sorrow and Sadness"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "sorrow-and-sadness"
source: "AllPoetry"

Pain is all I feel, when I am not with you
No one else can replace you, because the love we had was true.
Every time I close my eyes, I start to shed a tear.
I know we have our differences, but I’m just showing you that I care.

The times we spent together were the best years of my life.
We used to talk about marriage, and I would be your wife.
Now those moments have faded away, and left me alone crying.
My heartaches so bad from sorrow and sadness it feels like my soul is dying.

I ask the lord to help us out so I can make you see.
All the things that we went through are just saying we're meant to be.
