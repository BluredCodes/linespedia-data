I love when you make love to my soul,
I behold the truth that lies within us.
You mean more to me than anyone that I know.
Making love to my soul is beautiful,
You love me for me. I love you for you.
I make love to your soul also.
I cherish everything that we do, and that we have ever done.

I begun to realize that making love to you is all I want to do.
I only want to make love to you, physically, mentally, and socially.
I emotionally cry when things go wrong. The love that we have for each other
Is beautiful. I love making love to you. You make my body feel so right in your arms.
Your gentle touches that mean so much.

I love making love to you. You know every spot in my body.
My body only recognizes your touch because I am used to making love to you.
To you and only you. Once again emotionally I cry when things go wrong, but from that
I became strong just like you did. Us making love becomes better.
Forever I will remember how you made me feel when you made love to me.


Both of our bodies touching, and we’re yearning for more.
I store my fantasies within, not letting you know my secrets, about the art of
Making love. I breathe harder as you breathe harder. We’re realizing that we need
Each other more than we expect it. I accept the fact that the love that we have ever
Made was beautiful, and for that reason. I will only make love to you.
Physically, mentally and socially.
Emotionally I will cry out for you. Because the way that you make me feel
When we’re Making Love
