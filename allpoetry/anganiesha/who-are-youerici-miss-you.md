You’re the air that I breathe
The sight that I see
You mean so much to me

I wish you were here
I wish you were near
To dry my every tear

I love you so much
I miss your touch
You loved me so very much

I miss your face
Your style and grace
No one can ever take your place

I can’t move on
I’m trying to be strong
I’m sorry for doing you wrong

You’re perfect to me
I can’t let you be
Baby I just want you to see

I promise no more lies
And no more whys
I promise that I will always try

You are my heart
I am torn apart
I hope I am still in your heart

Please say that you care
You know that I care
I miss the love that we shared



I cry and cry
I know the reason why
That you really didn’t want to try

It will never happen again
You are my best friend
To me you’re a # 10

I have been blessed
I’ve been put to the test
I don’t want to be like the rest

I want to be in your arms
I feel safe and warm
Do you miss being in my arms

To end this poem
I’ll call on the phone
I don’t want to be alone

You’re on my mind
You’re one of a kind
I wish that you were mine

Can you be here with me
I want to make you see
That we are meant to be
