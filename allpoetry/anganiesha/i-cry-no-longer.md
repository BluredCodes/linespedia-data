---
title: "I Cry No Longer"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "i-cry-no-longer"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8191-I-Cry-No-Longer-by-Anganiesha"

I cry no longer, because you made me stronger
You wiped my tears away, and told me you were going to stay
I cry no longer, because you made me realize
You made me realize that our love could only get much stronger
I cry no longer, because you held me in your arms
You kept me safe and warm, and far away from harm

I cry no longer, because I have no more tears left to cry
God already knows why, he knows how hard I tried.
I cry no longer, because I am with you
All I see is you, and I just want to stay with you.
I cry no longer because, my heart is filled with love
I put no one else above, it’s hard for me to love

I cry no longer, because we been together for so long
Our love does not seem wrong, cause us together makes it strong.
I cry no longer, because there is no reason for me to cry
I’m all out of whys, and the feelings that I want to die
I cry no longer, because I am not stressed out
Depressed, lonely, frustrated, mad, sad, teary eyed, and hated

I cry no longer, because the lord took my pain away
He showed me a better way, and he told me the things to say
I cry no longer, because I’m older
I understand things a lot better, my feelings aren’t as colder.
I cry no longer, because our love only got much better
We will always stay together, me and you always and forever
I cry no longer…
