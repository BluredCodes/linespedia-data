---
title: "I’m Sorry"
writer: "Anganiesha"

The way that I’m feeling right now,Is a way I never felt before.The things you used to say to me.It seems like you don’t care about me any more.I know I used to snap on you.I was completely wrong.Please give me another chance.I want to make our relationship strong.I’m sorry about everythingI’m sorry for making you mad.Boo please will you forgive me.I promise to make you glad.I love you more than anything.More than this whole world.I would cry for eternity.If I could no longer be your girl.You’re the 1st guy I ever loved.The 1st to make me cry.Please say that you still care about me.If you don’t, I’d just die.
