I'm taking the time to let you know you are the best little brother in the world.I still remember the 1st day you were born,when I was just a little girl.I don't see you much, I miss you a lotyou are growing up before my eyes.I remember when I held you, yousmiled real big, reminiscing makes me wanna cry.You're getting so old, the years went by, Iremember this like it was yesterday.It makes me sad cause I don't know whereyou are, I miss you each and every day.November 26 was the best day of my lifebecause that was the day you were bornI can't even call you to say happy birthdayI cry because my heart is so torn.I wish I could call you, and you tell me howyou are, and what’s been going on with you.I want to hear every detail, I want to hear you tell a joke, and I want to hear you say you love me too.
