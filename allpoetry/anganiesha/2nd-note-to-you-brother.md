---
title: "2nd Note To You Brother"
writer: "Anganiesha"

I remember one day, I kissed you on your head,
you looked up and smiled at me.
I was so proud when you said your 1st words
and when you tried to call me your sis.

You always stayed by my side, you always crawled over to me,
laughing while you were on your way.
I remembered when I helped you take your 1st steps
you looked at me and decided to stay.

It made me laugh, because you wobbled when you tried
to walk over to me.
When you finally got there you sat on my lap,
so I decided to let you be.

I blew on your stomach and tickled your side
every time you started to cry.
I let you know that I wasn't going anywhere
as I wiped the tears away from your eyes.

You fell asleep on the couch, so I covered you up
and kissed you as I said Good-Night
I went in my room and before I closed my eyes
I thought about holding you tight.
