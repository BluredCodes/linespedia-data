Over all…Our relationship had itsUps and downs.Not sayin that I cheatedOr even fooled around Over all…We got along very wellYou used to ask me if I was in loveAnd I said only timeWould tellEven through you always had an attitudeI always kept it realI told you I loved you everydayAnd expressed to you how I feel.Over all…I was good to youActually always good to you.I never told a lieWhat about you, what’s your replyOver all it was goodI wouldn’t change anything if I couldI would tell you all of my feelingsBut again I don’t know if I should
