---
title: "I Think About You I Care"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "i-think-about-you-i-care"

I wonder if you care about me,
The way that I care about you.
You know that you were my
First love,
And you know that you’re my boo.

I think about you everyday, I
Think about you and me.
I think about all our memories,
And how it used to be.

I reminisce on the first time,
The first time that we met.
I cherish all your love, the
Love I have I kept.

I hope we get back together,
Without you there’s no me.
It’s like my vision has gone
Away, I have no sight to see.

My happiness has gone away,
Because you broke my heart.
But without you, you know
I can’t go on, and I just fell
apart.

All I want fore-ever is for
You to stay my present.
I know god will let that happen,
Because, to me you are sent from heaven.

You’re my male best friend, I can tell
You anything.
I feel like that came to an end.
The reason why, is because you hurt me,
But you will always be my friend
