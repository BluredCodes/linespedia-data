---
title: "Tell Me Why"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "tell-me-why"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Tell me why my heart hurts so bad, I cry
Myself to sleep, you mean the whole wide
World to me.
Without you I am sad, I will never be glad
Because I miss the love that we had.

Tell me why I cry, the tears that I cry when
I’m all stressed out.
I feel all alone, alone
By myself, because you left me all alone.

You know that I love you, I never meant any harm
I miss you all so much.
It’s just not the same, I feel I’m the blame, I 
Love you and I have no shame.

Then I began to realize that I can’t go on without you
Being apart of my life.
You’ll never understand the way that I feel, but you know that I love you on the real.

Tell me why can’t I see, that it’s not you and me, but
I still feel like we’re meant to be.
Just give me a chance, you know I won’t lie, all I ask you is just tell me why
