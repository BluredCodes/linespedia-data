---
title: "I Hate You"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "i-hate-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8358-I-Hate-You-by-Anganiesha"
---

I hate you
I hate every thing about you
I hate the way you smile it brings tears to my eyes
I hate the way you laugh
I hate the way you hold me
I hate the way you kiss me and make me feel so warm
I hate when you tell me you love me
I hate when you do something and it makes me cry but I always
Take you back.
I hate the day we met
I hate when it was you and I alone in a room
I hate how long we were together
I hate the way you make me laugh
I hate the way you hurt my feelings
I hate you because you said we’d always be together
I hate the way you call my name
I hate when you take me for granted
I hate the way you look at me
I hate when I believe you
I hate when I go see you
I hate when you come see me
I hate you because I love you
