Plain and Simple:You treated me like Sh*t.Don’t ask me for a kiss.You put me through hell.-you cheated-you lied-you betrayed me.-you treated me like dirt-you made me cry-you lied to my face-you hung up on meand exedra So for all that pain and hurt you causedFu*k you! I mean that from the bottom of my heartPlain and simple;I didn’t deserve the way you treated me.I’m happy without you. You’re the one who will be miserable.What you’ve done to me I hope you’ll get the same… so from the bottom of my heartI just want to sayFu*k you!You will get it worse than how you did me
