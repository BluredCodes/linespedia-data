---
title: "My Feelings"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "my-feelings"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Throughout this whole relationship you
Want every thing to be about you.
It takes two to make a relationship
But if you want it can be just you

I try to do the things you say
To prevent us from any arguments
But when I ask you to do some things for me
You look at me like I don’t have any sense

You know I don’t ask you for much
All I ask is for your time and love
It’s not like I ask you for money
I just want you to put me above

I love you with all of my heart
Without you I am torn apart
I know that I can’t be without you
I know that because you’re in my heart
