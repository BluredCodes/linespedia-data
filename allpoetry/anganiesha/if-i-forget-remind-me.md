---
title: "If I Forget Remind Me"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "if-i-forget-remind-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

The thought of you makes me cryI’m through with love, I don’t wanna tryI can’t take no more of your liesIt’s sad, now I’m saying good-byeSo if I forget…Remind me not to rememberRemind me not to call youRemind me that there are men out there who will treat me betterThan youRemind me not to care.When I start to reminisce, remind me that I don’t need youAnd you are not the one for meYou make me insane, every time I try to forget you, itReminds me that I am still in love with youI know I say one thing and do anotherBut I am certain that I can leave you, leave you alone.But then you begin to whineOnce again I give inI know that I am strongBut what I really want to know is what do I do that is so wrongSo…Remind me not to mention that I love youRemind me to be sorry about the day we metEven though I adore youI need to be reminded to ignore youYou are the one thing I truly regret.So when your lies begin to blind meRemind me to put you behind meDon’t let me kiss you, just remind meMy love is gone with my memories behind meSo as long as you don’t forgetTo remind me you are my regretMy love for you I will forgetThe past is what I regret
