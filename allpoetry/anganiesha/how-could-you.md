---
title: "How Could You"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "how-could-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

I don’t know what I want to do with you.
I know that I want to be with you
But I keep thinking about you cheating
And that makes me not want to be with you

We make each other promises
The ones that should last forever
Then you be the one to break them
Now my trust in you is gone forever

I try to believe the things you say
Then you want to come back with a lie
I always told the truth to you
Why do you want to see me cry

You’re the second man I gave my heart too
And the last one to receive my love
The reason why I feel like this
Should I tell you, well just because

You already know my feelings
They are written all over my face
I still remember when you cheated on me
I remember when that took place

And for you to say you love me
Then turn around and do me wrong
I wonder where the love has gone
Did I do something wrong
