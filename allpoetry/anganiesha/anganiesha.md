~A~lways willing to forgive but never forget when I am truly hurt by an individual~N~ever give up in hard situations even though it seems the best at times~G~enerous to peoples that’s less fortunate because that makes me happy to see them happy~A~ngry when someone tries to run all over me and thinks that I’m naïve, and I’ll never know~N~ever bite my tongue for anyone, I will always keep it real, real is who I am, and real is who everyone knows~I~ntelligent person, I always stay on task~E~ncourage others when they feel that they can’t go on, I make them strong~S~hy to a certain extent, but don’t let that fool you~H~appy when I help someone achieve their goals and they succeed~A~nd for all of this, that’s why I am known as ~ANGANIESHA~
