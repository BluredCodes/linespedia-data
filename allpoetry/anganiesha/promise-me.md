Promise me that you'llNever leave from my sidePromise me also that youWon’t make me cryMake me believe that that I am the only one.And I’ll make a promise to you, to never fall out of love.Promise me when you leave, that all you'll think about is me.I hope that you keep your promises.I promise to never let you be.I always keep my promises, and I hope you'll do the same.Promise me that you'll never cheat on me, and our love shall always remain.*Only If You Promise Me*
