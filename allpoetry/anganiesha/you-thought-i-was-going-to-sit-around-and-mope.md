title: "You Thought I Was Going To Sit Around And Mope"

You must really think that I’m going to sit around and just mope.You really didn’t deserve me: do I miss you? Well honestly nope!I’m tired of the disrespect, and tired of the lies, you always tried to hurt my feelingsAnd always made me cry.Well I’ma find someone who will dry my eyes, and never see me cry.I thought loving you was incredible. However boo it was a mistake.I never hurt so much in my life: I never shed that many tears before.Therefore why should I let you see me cry?You will never get the best of me. I’m free from everything that you put meThrough. Why you? Why did I have to love you? Everything happens for a reason.I will never go back to you. I promised myself that I would leave you alone and move on with my life.Being with you was a set back. The fact was I was to good for you.You had someone who would do anything for you, and loved you for you.But you never saw it. So it’s your loss and my gain. I’m sane cause you are out of my life. You must have really thought that I was going to sit around and just mope.
