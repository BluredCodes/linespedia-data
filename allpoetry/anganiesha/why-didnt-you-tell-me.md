---
title: "Why Didn’t You Tell Me"
writer: "Anganiesha"
slug: "why-didnt-you-tell-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

I asked you why you wouldn’t cheat on me.You said because you loved me.Then you told me you couldn’t lie anymore.You told me you cheated,And kissed another girl,It just happened, but you did keep it real.I didn’t know what to do I just cried on the phone,Because you lied and I felt all-alone.Throughout the relationship I never even cheated.I sat here and cried all alone.I wish I had cheated, because you wastedMy time, by doing nothing but tellin’ me lies.I wish I would of known, what I know now,Because I wouldn’t be cryin askin why.
