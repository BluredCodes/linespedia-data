---
title: "Before & Now…You Hurt Me"
writer: "Anganiesha"

Before you were the only one, 
The only one who could dry my tears
And be the person that makes me smile,
And takes away all of my fears.

But now you can’t dry my tears
Or take away all of my fears.
I can’t stop my crying; my heart feels like it’s dying. 
Remember when you used to catch my tears?

You’re not the same person you used to be
I can’t believe that you put your hands on me.
Then expected me to stay around, 
I stayed around long enough.
I took your cheating and your lies, don’t you think enough is enough

You’re not the same person you used to be
You used to be there for me
I remember that like it was yesterday
Now I just see you beating me
