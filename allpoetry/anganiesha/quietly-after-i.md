Quietly after I kiss youI start to walk out the roomPromising to returnAnd to come back real soonI hate leaving your presenceBecause of that look you have in your eyesAs if you know I’m not coming backAnd then you want to ask me whyQuietly after I touch youIt makes me wanna stayAll alone, lookin fineBeside you I wanna layQuietly after I tell youIt’s only you that I loveYou quietly respond backIt’s only me that you put aboveI stopped talking quietlyAs I told you I am in love with youYou smiled and replied backThat you are also in love with me too
