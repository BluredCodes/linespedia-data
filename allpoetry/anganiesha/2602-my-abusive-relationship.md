Was it my fault that you put your
Hands on me?
Punching me like a man and
Wouldn’t let me be

I really thought that you had loved me
I guess I had to find out the hard way.
I thought we were going to work things out
And our relationship would always stay.

I couldn’t believe that you hit me,
I was just worried about protecting my face
I never thought that you would get out of hand
I’m glad you didn’t hit me in my face

You called trying to apologize to me, and
Saying that you felt less of a man
I believe that you do, I’m hurting ‘cause of you, I really
Just can’t understand
