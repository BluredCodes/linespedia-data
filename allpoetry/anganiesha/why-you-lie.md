title: "Why You Lie"

When I’m over your house,A girl calls on the phone, you try toAct like it’s one of your friends.I pay it no mind, because you think thatYou’re slick.I act like I don’t know I just pretend.But when you hang up, I just give youThat look “like go ahead and lie to me,”The first thing you say is that is wasn’t a girl,But we both know she wasn’t a he.Why you keep lyin’ to me, we been together for too long,I thought you were keeping it real. I guess I was wrong, I should of known that wasA lie, I need to wake up and just say goodbye.
