title: "Waiting In The Rain"

I am waiting for your calling
My sweet sweet darling
I am waiting for your sign of love
Because you're my angel from above
You're my angel of love
You're my angel of light
Your my angel tonight

I've been waiting
Throught my darkest hours
Waiting in the rain
I don't know how you've got these powers
You've got me waiting here
Here in the rain

YOu said you would always love me
But is this true
You said you would come back for me
But is this true
And now as my cloths begin to stick together
I am wondering where is my love
Where are you

I've been waiting
Through my darkest hours
Waiting in the rain
I don't know how you've got these powers
But you've got me waiting here
Here in the rain

And now it's getting darker
I'm wondering where are you
Darling, where are you
So now you've sent somebody to tell me off
Babe
How could you do this to me
How could you break my heart

So I've been waiting
Waiting in this wretched rain
Waiting in my pain
