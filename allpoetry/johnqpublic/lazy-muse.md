She kicks off her shoes
and tells me
"No work for me today"
despite my anxious pleas

To please her 
I rub her shoulders
(always soft & warm)
then brush her tangled hair

Butterflies & bumbleblees
float from between her curls
I pay no attention

I speak to her
(with my ever desperate "please")
she replies with sounds
of flutes & fiddles
I do not listen

For her happiness
I've built strong stone walls
to enclose the lush tropic garden
where she lives
She only climbs my ramparts
seeking freedom

She seeks assurance that I love her
and that I'm not just using her for pleasure
but she responds to my sweet talk
with bitter tears
leaving me wet & shivering
& seeking shelter

She knows how I love to touch her
but to tease me,
she flees from my embrace
& when I give chase
I only stumble
then lie bruised
among the fragrances
of her rainbow colored blossoms

I can only hope
that very soon
she will return to me
with her sweet caresses...Sorry folks; writer's block for me today. She'll be back; she knows how much I love & need her.
