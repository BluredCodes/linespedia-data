though sated for the momemt
it remains
undimished

eating through my soul
like flames
roaring through a doomed house

the curves of her
invade my dreams;
i wake to reach for
the emptiness
where she was

skin like caramel
to be licked & kissed
(but not by me, tonight,
my mouth trembles with
the craving)

eye contact on the street
makes me shudder
there are
lethal beasts within those orbs
waiting to absorb me

i dream of her hair
(like silk from ancient china)
in my dream, i wait
for her to release her locks
like rapunzel
so i can climb her wall

to her second story window
where i can find 
the touch 
i crave

at her touch
i burn
in the broiling tropics of her

skin deadly as a viper's vemon
to me, but still i crave
to be enfolded
in her curves
