It was a chilling night all over the city;
the internet explorers' ship was grounded.
The city was plunged into darkness
as all the explorers' screens
faded to blue.
meaning
no more

instant stock quotes
marching across screens like little green soldiers
as signals of the city's massive wealth;
no more
come-ons from cigar-chewing lenders
inviting broke explorers
to mortgage their houses, cars, dogs, & wives
for fat wads of instant lucre
no more
hot babes with glistening lips 
inviting horny explorers to come hither
no more steamy chats with with vixens who hide their warts
behind the universal vanishing cream
of anomymous fingers tapping on keys
& no more
ego massages for lonely poets
who'll have to resort to dead tree pages
or forfiet their dreams

All over the city the hue & cry & hubbub
focused on one thing
who's to blame?

The courageous explorers launched a probe
& the head net detective finally stabbed an accusing finger
at those evil
cybergremlins!

Their malevolent tentacles had ensnared
the vital machinery of the net.
Their quest:
Destruction and domination!

They aimed to bring the mighty city to its knees
and leave the valiant explorers in quivering heaps,
pleading for mercy.
The hierarchy came to a consensus
to enlist the city's the aid of the city's powerful cyberhero:
a mysterious figure in black mask & cape,
whose name is spoken only in whispers:
The Webmaster.

Blazing across the landscape in his grand silver chariot,
The Webmaster wrathfully wielded his great cruel blade
leaving legions of cybergremlins' shattered bodies
scattered across the earth.
The surviving fiends howled in rage and agony
that The Webmaster had single-handedly vanquished their conquering force;
first triumphant, now dejected, they knew
that their sole option was to flee

Suddenly, all over the city
darkened screens blazed to life
and the internet explorers' tremendous vessel
rose in triumph above the ground
and resounding cheers rose from the populace;
the city's way of life had been saved from certain doom.
