---
title: "I Hated Poetry"
writer: "johnqpublic"
slug: "i-hated-poetry"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/26045-I-Hated-Poetry-by-johnqpublic"

I hated poetry

dreams distilled in graceful words

I hated
the honeyed voices
of poets

who stole the musings in my head
& made them 
as beautiful as stars
that fill a cold night sky

I despised
the dancing strings of words
fueled by despair or triumph
lust

or love

they made me feel
so inarticulate
as if my mind were made of dust

poems I saw & heard
where like sky diamonds
that I could not touch

Those could create these things
were a mystery to me-
gods that walked
the same earth as I
yet it seemed
their feet did not quite
touch the ground

poems were cruel to me
touching feelings I wanted to hide inside
a sacred vault held deep within my heart

I hated poetry
until a time of despair & loneliness
moved my hand to reach for a pen
and draw diamonds in the sky....based on a true story.
