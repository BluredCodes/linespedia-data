title: "Prayer For Jen"
writer: "johnqpublic"
slug: "prayer-for-jen"

I know you don't hear from me often
not even in times of crisis
but, please hear me out; I don't want much
just a request
for a gentle friend

the one I turn to
when I need to turn my sorrow
to smiles or laughter

the one
bursting with love and kindness
for distant friends

the one who brings
the light and warmth of sunshine
to this place

Today, she's in a place
where fear and sorrow
rain down on her
tears flow down her cheeks
and in all the distant lands 
where she is known
tears flow for her

My request:
peace for her;
please
dry her tears and give her a reason
to smile

hold her hand and tell her once more
that she is loved

give her hope 
that she will survive these days
as she's lived through darkness before
and come back to light
with added strength

bring out the courage she needs
to fight this latest battle
against tragic circumstance

and bring her back to this place
so she can continue
to share her joy and wisdom
with friends like me

and lastly,
when this time is over,
grant her a reprieve-
too many sorrows have fallen upon her, please
stop the rainA clumsy effort, but heartfelt.
For bigmammajen, and those who love her.
