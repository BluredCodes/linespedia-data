title: "Chaos Child"
writer: "johnqpublic"

I will not pretend
I can relate to you;
with your orange & purple hair 
& fifty acres of tattoos

I watched you dance so wildly 
to your linkin park songs
you invited me to join you;
I said no, I cannot spin like that

That was when you were joyful, manic;
moments later, your mood is tragic
melancholy drama seeps into your voice
& you adjust your crown of thorns
like a bloody tiara

& you cry when I tell you 
you don't look so good in black

Your rages, to me, are soulless theater;
I recline in your balcony, munching popcorn
& waiting for your tide to turn

& then it turns:
with gale force gusts & heaving seas
trying so hard to drown me

I remain aloof
you pout (thinking it looks seductive)
& then we ignore each other for a while.
You hide your ears behind
headphones bleeding traffic jams
I retreat to my kitchen
(in case you're hungry)
& we both slink away from ourselves-
you, in envy of how cool i am
I, miserably in love
with the chaos that you are
