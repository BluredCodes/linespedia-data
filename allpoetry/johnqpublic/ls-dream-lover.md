---
title: "L's Dream Lover"
writer: "johnqpublic"
slug: "ls-dream-lover"

The lover he writes about
has silken skin
& a voice like honey

she whispers softly in his ear
as her delicate hands
caress his body

Never does she have a harsh word for him;
only words of love & kindness

He is a poet respected in many quarters;
he's read his works on the evening news
and he can be seen in the green corners of his city,
pen & notebook in hand,
recording dreams about his lover

He covers her in roses
(the kind with no thorns)
& spills his devotion
over closely scribbled pages

A bright young woman who'd just heard him read
the praises of his sweet love
marveled at the tenderness
in his graceful words

When she asked who was so beloved
to inspire all these words of passion
he mournfully answered,
"I wish I knew."...based on a true story. And no (in case you were wondering), this is not about me.
