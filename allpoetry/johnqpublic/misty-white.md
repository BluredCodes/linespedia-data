Two feet of snow
coat the ground where she lives;
through her window,
she sees houses, cars, & trees
shrouded
in misty white

Her heart is cloaked in winter's gloom;
from a thousand miles away
I pray
that the joyful child
hidden deep within her
will run outside
and carve an angel.
