There's a jazz band playing
in my head
(& man, these cats sure do swing)

I hear them every time
I read the work of Wendy

Words stand at attention until
Wendy plays her tune
& then they dance
swing, ballet, & sometimes tap
all enslaved by Wendy's rhythm

she is playful often,
sometimes seductive
(never will I admit the way
she turns me on)
& she knows how to bring a flood of tears
to even a hardened heart like mine

Her tune continues;
& my restless feet
tap a drumbeat on the floor
& my head shakes in admiration;
