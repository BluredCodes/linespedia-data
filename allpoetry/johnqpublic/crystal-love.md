---
title: "Crystal Love"
writer: "johnqpublic"
slug: "crystal-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

She wants a little drama in her blood
(just a sniff)
she receives it,
then she dances
through her liquid world, intensified
by the drama in her mind

[Flashback]
Golden Gate Park
(her idea of a posh address)
was the scene of the drama of her
taking shallow breaths, as she awaited peace-
the sleep that eluded
the girl that danced with crystal love

scotch on the rocks
sipped from a mason jar
failed to soothe

forty Xanax
she thought,
might do the trick,
trim the weeds of drama
strangling her brain

finally, she slipped into a dreamless sleep

Later, she was rescued,
her body purged of poison
and bundled on to a plane
soaring back to Virginia
[End of Flashback]

It is late
flashing colored lights
dazzle her eyes
a booming techno throb
moves her feet
her partner in this dance
is crystal love
who shakes and twirls her stoplessly
through a moment 
that feels like forever
till it's over

Now,
she is brittle,
a china girl

cracking

a tremble finds her hands,
then rolls through her,
to grip
arms
legs 
head
body

and she is shattered

the pieces of her strewn on a grimy, sticky floor
no-one there to pick her up;
her glassy-eyed companions
all dance
with crystal love

She has a little drama in her blood
and a voiceless scream burbles forth
from her throbbing head-
cutting through the fog inside her mind
one thought razors through
she needs someone 
to mend
her bleeding brain
