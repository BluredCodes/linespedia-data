---
title: "The Dreams of an Entity"
writer: "Avalon"
slug: "the-dreams-of-an-entity"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/50867-The-Dreams-of-an-Entity-by-Avalon"
---

The world as we know it seems natural
But really the world is endless
Alternating realities,
How do we know we exist?

For all we know we are merely a dream
A dream of a supernatural entity
Performing the acts of his twisted mind
But what happens when he wakes up?

Will the universe we live in stop turning
Pause mid-air, mid-breath
But the important question is
Will this dream return?

Will we ever exist again
Will we continue these 'lives'
If we do exist in the dream of an entity
What does that make us?

We know we're not immortal
Therefore the entity is mortal too
And if we only exist in his dreams
What happens when he dies?
