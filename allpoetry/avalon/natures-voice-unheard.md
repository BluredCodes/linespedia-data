---
title: "Nature's Voice Unheard"
writer: "Avalon"

Come sit under the billowing tree
Listen to it's call
It speaks to you, tells you truths
Truths a thousand years old.

See the butterfly flittering
Gliding between the flowers
It has a voice, can you hear it
A voice of magickal powers.

Caress the silken waters of this singing stream
Listen to it's song
It sings a harmony of wonder and beauty
A beauty a million years strong.

Feel the winds blow gently
Whispers delicately in your ear
Let it carry your spirit through the clouds
Let the softness melt away your fears.

Speak to the dainty bouncy squirrel
Hear it's little story
It can tell you things you never knew
About the majestic natural glory.

Nature speaks in it's voice unheard
A voice of hope and love so grand
If only we could listen
If only we could understand.
