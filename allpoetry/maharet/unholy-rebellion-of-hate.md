title: "Unholy Rebellion Of Hate"

Child livin' for the taste
of the bad man's posion candy.

The virgin whore, mother of jesus, mary.

The dirt feels good
I admit I sin for a living.

I live to die.

The angel disobeying god's command
Satan's revenging smile behind the curtain scenes.

I am the dog.

The infection of uncureable rabies
destorying within your inner being.


I am the silver butcher knife.

Penetrating sword through the heart of love
I hate the beauty.

Devil's creation.

I am the work of evil
I am the hated thing.

Rebellion, Renegade of the new age.
