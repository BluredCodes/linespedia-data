title: "Sexual Insanity"
writer: "Maharet"

As I study your sleeping body 
Such Things I crave
I want you
So badly
When I’m near to you 
I have to feel you 
Every inch of you 
Every curve on your body 
I need you 
I want to taste you 
The sweetness of your skin 
Against my lips
Lusting so powerful

The beast inside me 
urging to come forth 
and just take you
Get you down
Make Love to you now

Must have you 
desiring all you are
wanting you so badly
inside me 
You must be with me
beside me 
hand in hand
Just you and me 
I give myself to you 
but will you share yourself with me?
let me have you
let me breathe upon you
Don't torture me now

Can’t get you
Out of my head 
Your everything 
That drives me mad 

Sexual insanity 
I must be with you 
in some way or another 
You’ve got my spirit and soul 
cold chills shiver down my spine
Oh you’re so fine 
divine as mankind
you sweep me off my feet 
Take me off the ground 
until our faces meet 
tongues twist and turn
Connected together 
our bodies burn 

passionate love 
Night till dawn 
I’ll Always be by your side
If you love me or not
