---
title: "I Wanna Hold You"
writer: "Maharet"
slug: "i-wanna-hold-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

I wanna be there now,
Beside you anyhow.
	
        I wanna lay you down,
	watch you as you sleep.
I wanna pet your hair,
Speak whispers into your ear.
	
        I wanna hold you now.
	Don't make a sound.


The limits break me
Trapped inside a crate
and shipped too far away.
I can hear your music
even if so far away,
It plays through my brain.


I wanna see you now,
The smile upon your face.
	
	I wanna touch you some how,
	Feel all that soft lace.
I wanna share your breath,
Twist our tongues in a race.
	
	I wanna hold you now.
	Don't make a sound.

Come to me now.
Be with me now.
	Arms open wide.
	A place for you to hide.

Lay among me now in safety.
As I want to hold you.
