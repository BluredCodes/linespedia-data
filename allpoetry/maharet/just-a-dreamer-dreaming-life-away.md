title: "Just A Dreamer (Dreaming Life Away)"
writer: "Maharet"

Cloudy skies over head 
Wind's slippery kiss chilling through my veins 

I will rest upon the grass 
And the heaven I'll have in my eyes 

As I sit and wonder 
All my days slipping away 

I am just a Dreamer dreaming my life away 
Sleeping on the drugs of emotions 

Dreaming to escape what is on the outside 
My devotion to getting away 

In my mind I see the wonders of the world 
In my dreams secrets of life are revealed to me 

I sink into my soul 
The shield covering over me so protectingly 

The story behind the abusiveness 
The truth behind all the lies 

The world shall know my name 
They will cherish every breath I take 

I am just a dreamer 
Dreaming my life away 

Men will fall to their feet 
Bow before me and want me for their bride to marry 

They will all love me 
Rapture all for me 

I am just a dreamer 
Dreaming my life away 

I will have all the knowledge I need 
Every question I will answer at ease 

To know everything to say 
They'll claim me their goddess within 

I am just a dreamer 
Dreaming my life away 

A big mansion 
A life so sweet 

A family so perfect 
Everything so great 

I am just A Dreamer 
Dreaming my life away 

No more rules 
Nothing I'll have to obey 

I'll be an angel 
I'll have the wings to wander away 

Just a dreamer 
Dreaming away 

Silly things 
Will never come true 

For a dreamer 
Wasting her life away 

Everyday 
Just another day 
To dream her life away
