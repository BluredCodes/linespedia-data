title: "Release Me From You!"

Shoot a bullet to my head 
let the blood flow through... 
Leaky hole just in my brain 

Take the knife 
Stab it into me too! 
All the juice spilling over... 

Pour the blood for me 
Take it out of me... 
Because I dont want to 

Push me off the cliff 
Make me fall... 
So far I break when I hit 

Release Me! 
Release Me From You! 

I'm sick of being with you 
I hate you 
You hate me too! 

Tired of your lies! 
Fed up with your excuses! 
Dont care what you think! 

Cannot escape you 
I despies you! 
Dont wanna like you! 

Kill me now 
Or i'll make you! 
Maybe i'll kill you too! 

Release Me! 
Release Me From You!
