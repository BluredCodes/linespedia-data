---
title: "I Want You"
writer: "Maharet"
slug: "i-want-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Soft... 
Your lips red 
Must be sweet as wine 
I need to taste you 
I want you. 

Unseen... 
Fore i've lost myself inside you 
To the point of no return 
I need you to find me again 
I want you. 

Warm... 
Breath down my neck 
To feel your presence beside me 
I need your touch oh so much 
I want you. 

Lust... 
Flesh rubbing against flesh 
Pleasure with pain 
all together 
I want you. 

Prey... 
I'm watching you 
Sleep so peacefully 
Lay beside you so easily 
I want you. 

Daring... 
You look good enough to eat 
My lips part among your skin 
and the blood I crave comes within 
I want you. 

Child... 
I promise to take care of you 
Take away your pain 
Rid you of your maddness and hate 
I'll take you... 

Take... 
What I want 
and desire 
what I take 
simply done
