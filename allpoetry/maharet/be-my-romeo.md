title: "Be My Romeo"

Be my Romeo.


Take my hand.
And show me your land.
Wonderland.

Let your words teach me.
Just what I should be.
Your Lady, maybe.


Be my Romeo.


Wipe away these tears.
Fight away the dragons.
Cure my fears.

Mend my Broken heart.
Caress my breast.
And lay me down to rest.


Be my Romeo.


Climb through my window bravely.
Wrap your arms around me.
And hold me, so soundly.

Warmthen my coldness.
Awaken me from my death.
And say that you love me.


Be my Romeo.
