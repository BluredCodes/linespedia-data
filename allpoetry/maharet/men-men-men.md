Pulling down highway 101 - all revved out - havin' fun, 
Music blastin' - as I drive - oh man - I feel so alive, 
Wheels squealin' - as I go - screechin' round corners - 
ain't goin' slow - my old engine's - bout to blow. 

Chorus ..... 

Rollin' down the street - with my windows down - relaxin' to 
the beat - ain't screwin' round - just actin' like - a natural woman - 
while these skate boardin' - low riders - - give me that look 
and laugh at me - ain't none of these suckers - ever goin' be free. 

All home in bed - near the midnight hour, 
Lookin' for hags - in their porno mags 
Diggin' on hippy - new pop kickin' chicks 
I think they're all - just plain dicks. 

Look at me - I got the looks and style 
These men want me - for a little while 
They want to - climb my skinny frame 
and ride and ride - till I go insane. 

I aint no whore - don't sleep for money, 
Don't pleasure guys - unless they give me honey 
They think I'm clean - but I'm rough and dirty 
My mind's out of control - be dead before I'm thirty. 


But I got the rhyme - and I do the beat, 
Just watch the clock - as I rock my feet, 
I'm in the mood - to have some fun, 
But I'm so sorry - not with you hun. 

I'm independent - ain't got no - leash 
Can do what I want - I'm single as can be 
Oh I love to have - what girls call fun 
but with men who don't run - when they're done. 


They leave you alone - feeling left out - all used up - thrown 
down the drain - abused in emotional ways - but we're so forgiving, 
So pick up the phone boys - wait till the tone - dial 6-6-6, call 
some bitch, dial 9-9-9, shame online - dial 5-5-5, oh your mine. 

Gotta love men - but jeez - can't they do something more 
than tap tap tap upon my door - then bang my ass upon the floor? 
Oh let's stay young while we can - cause these men ain't easy 
It's like a disease - all they want is - sex, sex, sex, sexual ease. 

Oh ladies they don't know - our needs - make them get down 
on their knees - and lick, lick, lick, as long as you please, 
Let's forgive them for being - such sick minded things - 
and enjoy the happiness - their sickness brings. 

Repeat Chorus ..... 

Rollin' down the street - with my windows down - relaxin' to 
the beat - ain't screwin' round - just actin' like - a natural woman - 
while these skate boardin' - low riders - - give me that look 
and laugh at me - ain't none of these suckers - ever goin' be free.
