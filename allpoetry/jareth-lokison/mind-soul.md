---
title: "Mind & Soul"
writer: "Jareth Lokison"
slug: "mind-soul"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/53188-Mind---Soul-by-Jareth-Lokison"

I take you in my mind
Or you take my soul
I'm never sure
But I can never let go

Thoughts of you stay with me
No matter where I am
Or what I do
I'm never away from you
