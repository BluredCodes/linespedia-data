Kiss my sins with bated breath -
Your taste was never mine.
Rebuke me now for words unsaid;
Praising me for your demise.

You were born to bleed, you say,
Cut by dull hand-me-down knives;
Slash your wrists to lick them dry
And its never quite enough.

Your innocent blood.
My fingerprints.
Your smile.
My cries.

Whatever makes you happy.
