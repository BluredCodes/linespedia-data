title: "Evaporate"
writer: "cynicalsaint"

Catscratch claw marks 
Reeking of injustice
Found in harsh words unsaid.
How typical of you.

Think you're so great.
Wanna change the world
With your cut-me down regard
And never-meant-it nicities.

Hypocrite, eternal cynic -
Try me one more time.
I'll change your world;
Poison you with reality.

Tell me one more time
How you think I'm immature.
Watch me as I slice through
That carefully painted fascade.

Your insecurities bleed through.
Flawless presence melts away
Into an inconvenient puddle...
Left to evaporate and leave you bare.My oh my what a wonderful mood I'm in....
