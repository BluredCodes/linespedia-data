Believe that I can change
But still be the same.

Believe that your love will
Never be a hopeless cause.

Believe that I tried and
Am trying harder still.

Believe that my breath
Has its own ring of truth.

Believe that my world
Resides within you.

Believe that my past
Does not change our present.

Just believe in me ... 

Oh fuck it. 
Believe what you want.
