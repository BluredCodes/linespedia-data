I can pretend to like youIf you want me toI can put on my happy faceAnd say meaningless words like 'I Love You.'To please you...If you want me toI can kiss youAnd you drain my life awayTo make you happyIf only to please youLike you want me toI know we should quit this game...So that we can separateAnd say meaningful words like 'I Hate You.'Like we should doYou don't want me to?Would that please you?
