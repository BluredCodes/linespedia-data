---
title: "I tryed so HARD!!!"
writer: "StarGrrl"
slug: "i-tryed-so-hard"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I tryed so hard,
And got far.
I thought so long.
I tryed to stay away from the wrong.
But in the end,
It doesn't even matter.

When good comes to good,
And bad comes to bad.
We think hard.
But in the end,
It doesn't even matter.

I could be good,
I could be bad,
But I relized,
In the end,
I doesn't even matter.

I listened to the good,
I forgot the bad,
But in the end,
It doesn't even matter.

I read the books,
And got the marks,
But in the end,
It doesn't matter.

So I did the drugs,
Acted horribly,
Because in the end,
It doesn't even matter.

I doesn't matter,
Because we all die in the end.
It doesn't matter,
Because life sucks.
