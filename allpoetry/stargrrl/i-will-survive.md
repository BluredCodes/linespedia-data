---
title: "I will Survive"
writer: "StarGrrl"

Life circles around us,
Like a black void.
Life punishes the poor,
And rewards the rich.

Life circles around us,
Teases us,
Mocks us.
Life is not happy,
For the poor.

So go ahead,
Tease us,
Mock us,
Just try to drown us!
But you will never succed with me.
I am stronger,
Better.
I will survive!
