The vibrant colours,
Soaring quietly through the air.

The way of wonderful life,
Eat or get eaten.

The beautiful birds,
Soaring quietly through the air.
Calm me in all my anger.

The vibrant colours,
The way of wonderful life,
The beautiful birds,
Are free.
