School is the place,
Where I am smart.
School is the place,
Where I must speak.
School is the place,
Where I am noticed.

Musical Theater,
I'm small,
I hide,
I go unoticed.

Jazz band,
I'm good, 
But not that good.
I'm smart,
But their smarter.
I try,
But not enough.

At home I am a writer,
But know about almost nothing else.
I am pretty,
But I could loose some weight.

Why am I not good anough for you?
I try so hard,
I really do.

Why can't you accept me?
I may be different,
But I don't bite.

Why, Why, Why?
I do know why though.

Because,
YOU ARE DUMB!
YOUR GOALS AREN'T MY GOALS!
I'M BETTER THAN YOU.
BE QUIET,
SIT DOWN,
LISTEN TO ME FOR ONCE!
I'M NOT PERFECT,
I KNOW THAT.
BUT I'M GOOD,
GOOD ENOUGH,
for me.

So please accept me.
Wont you?
