My life changes constantly.
It soares in and out of blisful happyness.
The bunt of all my troubles,
Is my crazy, psychotic family.

LET ME LIVE MY LIFE,
I am no child any more!
LET ME MAKE MISTAKES,
I will make them anyway.
LET ME GET ANGRY,
Don't calm my rage,
You only make it grow,
Into devilish, death thoughts.

My life changes constantly.
It soares in and out of clisful happyness.
The bunt of all my troubles,
Is my crazy, psychotic family.
