---
title: "Untill the morning comes"
writer: "StarGrrl"
slug: "untill-the-morning-comes"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/45037-Untill-the-morning-comes-by-StarGrrl"
---

Untill the morning comes,
My dreams are true.
Untill the morning comes,
I can live my life.
Untill the morning comes,
My life is perfect.
Untill the morning comes,
The monsters are gone.
Untill the morning comes,
Life is very sweet.

When the morning comes,
The day could never go faster.
When the morning comes,
All dreams must never be shown.
When the morning comes,
My life is run for me.
When the morning comes,
My life is full of dread and warning.
When the morning comes,
The monsters attack visously.
When the morning comes,
Life is a crude joke, 
That only I don't get.

During the day,
My life is run for me,
My life is full of dread and warning,
The monsters attack visously,
All dreams must never be shown,
The day could never go too fast.

During the night,
My dreams are true,
I can live my life,
My life is perfect,
The monsters are gone,
Life is very sweet.

Life is a crude joke,
That only I don't get.
But it is MY life.
I will live it MY way,
NOW!
