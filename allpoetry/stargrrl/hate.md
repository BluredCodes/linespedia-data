I hate them with a passion.
The way they walk,
The way they talk,
The way they think their better.

I always want to kill them.
With a knife,
With an axe,
With anything I can.

Because I am better.
Batter than them,
Better than their parents.
Better because I am kind.

Because I am stronge,
I am smart,
I am kind,
And I will prevail.

No matter what they tell me.
