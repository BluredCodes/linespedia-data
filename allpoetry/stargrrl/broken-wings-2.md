In my life I am alone,
Silent on my broken wings.
Once lying on sweet, soft grass,
Now sinking into that deep dark pit.
My future holds no happiness,
It holds what I know not.
I have no spiralling crystal stair,
I only have these broken wings.
I want to fly into the hights,
But it hurts my shoulders with the weight,
Because i am bound to this earth, and life.
I protect all,
but not myself,
