---
title: "Independance Day"
writer: "StarGrrl"

They tell me I'm crazy,
They tell me I'm dumb.
They push me away, 
With only one thumb.
They don't know why,
I cry and pout.
They don't know why,
I scream and shout.
They think their right,
They think I'm wrong.
They think I'm crazy,
To write these poems.
They'll lie,
And make me cry.
And I know ,
They only care about their show.
But I won't cry now,
I'll push them away.
I'll be independant now,
I'll be the one to play.
And when I leave,
And never come back.
I will be relieved,
But their lives will turn black.
Yet I'm not that evil,
Even though,
They tell me so.
I have a kind heart.
Even though,
It's been torn apart.
I am independant,
And will remain that way.
Because this is my day,
Of independance!
