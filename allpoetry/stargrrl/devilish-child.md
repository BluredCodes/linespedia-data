---
title: "Devilish Child"
writer: "StarGrrl"
slug: "devilish-child"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/43435-Devilish-Child-by-StarGrrl"
---

She thinks of death,
Of violence,
Of blood.

She thrives to hurt,
To sacrifice,
To kill.

She feeds off sorrow,
Off malishousness,
Off murder.

She lives in a world of darkness,
Of evil,
Of night.

She hides herself,
Inher world.
She is the devilish child.
