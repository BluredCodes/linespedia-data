Dissapointment is when you expected more.
Dissapointment is when you are concidered too dumb to do something.
Dissapointment happens when people make you feel small.
I'm dissapointed,
Because I wanted a bigger part.
I wanted a chance to get something good.
I wanted to be considered smart for once.
I didn't want to be idnored.
I didn't want to hide in the crowd.
For once I wanted to be noticed.I wrote it after a dissapointing class.
