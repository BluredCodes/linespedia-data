It runs like a lighting bolt through the vast mountains,
To meet it's true love,
To save it's loves life.
The fire burning behind them,
Killing, destroying everything in its path.
The fire does not care for their love,
It only cares for death, pain, and suffering.
They run for days and nights,
Not knowing where to go,
Because the fire would not stop.
The fire laughed it's wicked evil laugh.
The fire was burning horribly fast, they had to act quickly.
They ran for the water as fast as they could,
The fire, 
So determined,
So horrid,
Lept into the cold water,
And laughed for the last time...
