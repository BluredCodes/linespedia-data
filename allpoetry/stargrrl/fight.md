The growing flame,
Inside ones body.
The evil glare,
That starts it all.
The first words spoken,
Are WHAT IS YOUR PROBLEM?

The first swing,
That brings on the adrenalin.
The rush of it all.
The quick release.
The way of that life.
Then, one final blow ends it all...I was angry with a co worker. Can you tell?
