title: "Rage, Anger, Madness"

Rage fills the vulnerable body,
Erases the memory of all proper thoughts,
Envelopes the eyes that see the wrong,
And kills the golden, pure heart.

Anger builds and holds the rage,
Pauses the pure mind,
Makes the wrongs less important,
And blinds the golden heart for a short time.

Madness eats slowly at the body,
Interferes with the smart mind,
Makes rights from the wrongs,
And eats away at the pure heart.

Rage, Anger, Madness,
Body, Mind, Heart,
Release the rage.
Release the anger.
Release the Madness!
