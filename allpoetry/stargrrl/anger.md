Anger fills me beyond,
Belief.
As they scream,
And yell,
And repeat themselves a hundred times.
As their faces go red,
And they point,
And try to make me feel dumb.

They think I dont get it.
But they dont get it.
they wont let me xplain,
Let me move,
Let me talk.
What I say must be wrong. 
Right?

Well, they can scream,
And yell,
And point,
And make me feel dumb.
I dont care any more,
Or should I?
Should I try to explain?
Should I try to talk?
I dont know any more.
Maybe I am wronge,
Maybe I shouldn't explain,
Maybe I shouldn't talk.
NO!

I should explain.
I am right.
I should talk.

But I always,
Just sit there and take it.
I listen to them,
And the anger,
Builds inside me.
The anger builds beyond,
Belief.
Untill the anger,
