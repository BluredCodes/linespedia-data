The pretty colours fly around,
Around their swirls and hopes.
They are different, amazing colours,
There only to please me.

I know their for me,
Because only I can see them.
They swirl and twist and change,
Just for me.

I am their creator,
I am the one they worship.
Because they are pretty colours,
There to entertain, and illuminate the room.

I love my pretty colors,
I love them very much.
Because they are my pretty colors,
Only mine.
