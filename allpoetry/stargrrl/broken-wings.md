In my life I am alone,
Silent on my broken wings.
Once lying on the grass,
Now sunken into the deep pit.
My future holds no happiness,
It holds what I hold not.
I have no crystal stair,
I only have broken wings.
I want to fly,
but it hurts,
because I am tied down.
I protect all, 
But not myself,
Because I am Alone.This is the first poem that I've actually ever told anybody.
