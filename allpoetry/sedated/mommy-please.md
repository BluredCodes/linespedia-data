---
title: "mommy, please"
writer: "sedated"

the beast it sleeps inside of him 
and it awakes when the light gets dim 
his clutches tear young flesh apart 
they rape the grace of an innocent heart

the sweat it smells like fear alive
in this little house on the park drive 
a mind wrapped up and thrown to confusion
the prosperity of love turns to a strange illusion

mommy, please leave the light on... 

blindness in the light of her eye
she won´t dare to ask the question why 
she thought it was included into her providence
but the fortune teller lied with every chance

smile, lady, smile, maybe soon you can forget
that a life has been destroyed in your daughters bed
smile, lady, smile, what would the neighbours think
when they´d know about your husbands favourite drink

mommy, please leave the light on...

see this fragile one rocking herself to sleep
hoping for salvation, trying not to weep
in her arms lies Old Bob Teddybear 
she´s sucking on her thumb,
hping that Daddy isn´t there...

mommy, please leave the light on...
mommy, please leave it on
