here she is lying now abused her face, a swollen and bloody messanyone would cry now, but she is lying stilllistening to the sound of her breaththe street lights sparkle through the window shadecreating tiny fireworks on her body, little lucid marks all over her skin, she kinda shines now, she kinda shines......she´s free now !
