wet warm unresistable desire burning fountain inside a boywalking this world unconsciousyearning to be a dirty boy toyhe followed him to the restroom their they stood eye to eyelooking deep into each other until one started to satisfy a lighter of wildly flying embers skin to tongue and tongue to skin hidden inside this little chambersweet confessions in silent moaningbut still covered in denial they turn and walk away and what happened here today never happened anyway
