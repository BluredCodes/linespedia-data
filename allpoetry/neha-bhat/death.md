Death. Death. Death.

Everywhere just
Death. Death. Death.

Nothing but
Death. Death. Death.

Littered on streets
Death.

Staring in your face
Death.

Creeping from behind
Death.

Stabbed in your heart
Death.

Only death
Nothing else
Just
Death, death and
More death.

Up and down
Right and left
Across divides
Across lines
Simply death.

Gory. Ghastly. Scary.
