---
title: "I have not"
writer: "neha bhat"

source: "AllPoetry"

i have not written
these past few days.

and i am feeling sad

i cant though help it.
as my pen refuses to move,
and the thoughts want to remain thoughts only....

their wish
i cant force them out.
