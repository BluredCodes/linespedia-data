title: "some unforgotten memories"

A bad past
And some unforgotten memories

Is all that I have
To show to you
To give to you
As me.

Can you accept me
With this baggage?
Or
You also will
Leave me?

Like others
To fend for myself.

With 
The burden of
Just a bad past
And a few unforgotten memories…
