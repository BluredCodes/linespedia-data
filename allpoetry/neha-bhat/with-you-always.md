---
title: "with you, always."
writer: "neha bhat"

Lights went out
And I decided to take 
A walk.
Down the road.

Cool breeze blew,
As I strolled around.

I could see
Couples walking hand in hand
Ahead of me.

And I lamented
My partner’s loss.
Who was lost—
Somewhere
I don’t know where.

Then suddenly,
Things looked up
And 
I heard a voice
That engulfed its arms around me
And said,
‘I am always there for you.’

And slowly the loss faded.
Away.

I wasn’t alone.
Anymore.
