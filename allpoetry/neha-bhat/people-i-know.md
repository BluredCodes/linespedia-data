---
title: "people i know"
writer: "neha bhat"
slug: "people-i-know"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/32303-people-i-know-by-neha-bhat"
---

they understand me better
and we laugh with each other 
a lot
cry also.

together,
we hit off
like long known kiths.

the only hitch is
i haven't seen them...

but is a vision really necessary?
