title: "WAIT"
writer: "neha bhat"

when i know
that i am not going to get
what i want
when i want
and as i want

then why
why do i still
linger on hoping?

that perhaps
my wait 
will yeild something.

when deep inside
the heart is sure

that the wait
is useless...
