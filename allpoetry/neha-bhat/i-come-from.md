title: "i come from"

I left behind many memories.
Where? I don’t know.

Home is what?
Unknown to me
I have not had the
privilege
Of having a home.

People were there…
But all have now
left.
Just as sand trickles down 
From one’s hand.

Past perhaps was a
Fantasy. 
The bygone was 
Perhaps
Just a dream.
Where is come from?
I do not know.

Maybe is am just born
Right now…
