---
title: "my own epitaph"
writer: "neha bhat"
slug: "my-own-epitaph"
source: "AllPoetry"

I knew not 
That I was writing

My own death sentence.

With my own hands.

But then who knows?
Do you, for instance know,
When are you going to die?
