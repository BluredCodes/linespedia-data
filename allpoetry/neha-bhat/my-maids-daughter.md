title: "my maid's daughter"
writer: "neha bhat"

She was truly
A replicated version
Albeit a younger one,
Of my maid.

Playing around in my backyard
Under the winter sun,
Sitting on the white garden chairs
Then standing and running away.

Then again, coming and sitting down,
Quietly. Smiling.
Picking and placing at a different place,
The pickle jar.

My backyard was truly her whole
World.

The feeling has just sank in,
In those moments with her,
I was witnessing 
Innocence.
