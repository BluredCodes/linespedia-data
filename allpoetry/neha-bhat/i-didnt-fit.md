---
title: "i didn't fit"
writer: "neha bhat"
slug: "i-didnt-fit"
source: "AllPoetry"

The system
Lacked a place for me.

I lacked adaptive strength 
To accommodate them.

But we coexist.

We both ignore each other.
Don’t cross each other’s paths.

After all,
One doesn’t have to die for another…
