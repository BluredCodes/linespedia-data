---
title: "death of innocence"
writer: "neha bhat"
slug: "death-of-innocence"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/46837-death-of-innocence-by-neha-bhat"
---

innocence here 
died long back
when? 
don't ask
time here isn't committed 
to memory.
also, i don't remember.

but 
i know it is dead.
how?
well, i attended it's funeral

i saw it's pyre being lit.
i saw innocence
being burnt.
to death.

yes innocence
being burnt to death.
