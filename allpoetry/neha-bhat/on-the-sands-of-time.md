title: "on the sands of time"
writer: "neha bhat"

We left behind many imprints
On the sands of time.

Now,
Time available,
We went back to them.
For the want,
We just revisited them.
And we found,
That unable to cut ourselves
From what went
We need to come back
Again
And again.

As
It is here,
On the sands of time
That we find
All those cherished, loved moments
Of our lives.

It’s here
On the sands of time
That people we lost
On this journey
Are found.

It is here
On the sands of time
You feel
And find the etched
Portrait of 
Yourself.

It is here
On the sands of time
You cry
You laugh
For what went right
And for that what
Went wrong.

It is here
Where you lose
You win.
You live.
You exist.

On the sands of time.
