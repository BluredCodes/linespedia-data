i was walking along the path
when
suddenly
i turned around
and came back a full circle.

to where i had
started from

there, 
i met my old self.

i was angry for being that way.
but what could i say?
Mute, hushed I witnessed myself. 

after all as of now,
the person is me
and before too
the person was
me...
