title: "epitaph -- distances"

Born with lot of dreams 
Broken one by one were all.
It didn’t take lot
To tell
That life was like this.
Always. 

Distances were there too before—
But never touched like this.
From an infrequent rain 
They became a smiling sunshine.
To core.

Something knocked its hands on my door
And I realised that I needed
To flow ahead.
I did. 

And someone realised too late though—
Distances had increased too much. 
But now, like before 
Distances couldn’t be reconciled. 

And life?
Life left them nothing—
Except nights of dreams.
Of distances before.

Now,
Even they don’t exist…
