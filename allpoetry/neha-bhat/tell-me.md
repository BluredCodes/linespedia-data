---
title: "tell me"
writer: "neha bhat"
slug: "tell-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

tell
me something
do we need
anyone or do we
not? does anyone need us or does no one need
us? if yes, fine. life's purpose is served. but if
not, what is the 
reason we are
here. tell
me.
