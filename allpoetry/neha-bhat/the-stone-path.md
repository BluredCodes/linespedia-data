Yellow radiant rays
Eliciting me out,
The stone path that stood,
testimonial to my childhood. 

Uneven, unconnected stones,
Sprawled all along the
Pathway.
You stumble, yet be steady 
On them.

Memories of childhood
of scuttling beyond and
Ahead on
this path,
Surge forward
Pouring out,
Effortlessly.

This path,
From my early days
Until now,
Has been 
my only constant companion.
