---
title: "Not a poem yet"
writer: "neha bhat"
slug: "not-a-poem-yet"
source: "AllPoetry"

i just dont know what to write
so i just wrote this

but it still hasn't become a poem
yet
the word flow...as in a story
not in poetry...
not flowing
sentence like....

a story not is what i want to say...
but show my feelings.

and that is a poem...
from my heart...

but not a poem yet this is...
as what comes is a story and
not a poem...

yes, not a poem yet...
just a story...
unfinished...
