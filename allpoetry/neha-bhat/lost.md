my love 
is lost somewhere.

can you find him for me?

i can't
that's why i am asking you.

you see,
he doesn't listen to me.

not anymore.
he used to earlier.
but not now.

and even i don't listen to him.
for that,
he doesn't speak

so how do i look for him
when i don't even hear his voice?
