i looked into the mirror
and it showed to me
the ugliest face i had ever seen.

unruly, unkempt and burnt
not beautiful at all.
pain sorrow and wounds
inflicted everywhere.
where the eyes could run.

who was it?
rather,
who was she?
i thought i didn't know

until when
i looked deeper
and to my horror

my mirror revealed 
myself
to me.
