i stayed put 
around here
for a very long time.

but
no one noticed
me.

and so
all alone
i decided to leave.

but suddenly
people surrounded me
from left right
and
everywhere

and leaving from here
was a thought
left somewhere
far behind....

i found everything
so why search for it
anywhere else?please suggest a title people

cheers
neha
