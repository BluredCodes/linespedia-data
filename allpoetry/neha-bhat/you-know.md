It was after days
Weeks
And maybe even months
I haven’t committed time to memory yet…

That I etched out poetry on my lined paper.

The mind raced
And words stared back at me
It seemed as if 
Water just  flowing down.
Cool. Calm.
Yet fast…

Know what?
I feel as if I can still write…
