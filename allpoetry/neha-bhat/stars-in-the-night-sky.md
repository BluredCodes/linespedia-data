title: "stars in the night sky"
writer: "neha bhat"

Shining silvery
They are.
Stars in the night sky.

Luminously white.
Bright. 

The lap of the sky filled 
With
The ringing of
Their thousand laughs.

Alive with splendour.
Alive in beauty.
Is the stream nearby—
With rippling reflections of the
Shining stars in yonder sky.

One here.
Another
There.
Seems as if
Playing hop-skotch with me.

Smiling down on me.
Leading me with ease on my path.
Their glow
Makes my path radiant.

It is easy to pass this road.
The stars grant me their company.

Without them
The road seems so
Endless.
Abandoned.
Lonely.

With them the night ended when…
Who knows?
I reached my destination when…
Who knows?
Only the stars.
