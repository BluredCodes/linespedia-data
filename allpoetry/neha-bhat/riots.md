I was told and
Am always still told 
when I ask
Peace brotherhood and
Love
Are our second name.

Then why?
Why the smoke
Rising from
These burnt edifices?

Why
The stench of burnt 
human forms?
What was the fault
Of those innocent
Young kids and
Those women?

Why hurl stones
And mutilate people?

All is
Is it our peace?
Our love?

Intolerance shows
Out from each 
Expression on us.
Hatred written
Clearly on
Our visages.

Are just a temple or
A mosque 
The things we are fighting 
for ?

not seeing 
that these riots
kill
those for whom the
temple and mosque are
being built.

We need
To wake up.
To hard reality.
Practicality.

We are destroying
Our civilization by our own
Hands.
Fear we embed in
Young innocent hearts.

Scared eyes 
Stare back
Tears well up
In lost eyes
Tears of loss, sadness
Plight.
Mouths remain 
Speechless.
Except for muffled cries
Of pain.

Nothing
At least not positive
Comes out of riots

But we are mortals 
We will know not
What we have lost.
Until we are done with the riots….i know its verbose
but this is the truth the current situation of my hometown where communal riots are going on
just pray all of you that things get fine soon

thanks
