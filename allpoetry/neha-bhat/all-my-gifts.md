---
title: "all my gifts"
writer: "neha bhat"
slug: "all-my-gifts"

I wanted the stars
And wanted someone to pluck me the moon

But when did anyone get such gifts?

I wanted 
Something that was truly me,
Something sublime.

I wanted me to be reflected

I wanted all my joys, pleasures
None of the sorrows
Nothing sad.

But when have dreams
Ever come true?
So I thought.

Until,

One day
One by one all my gifts

Were sitting pretty in my lap.
