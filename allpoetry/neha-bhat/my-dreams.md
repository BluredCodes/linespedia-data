---
title: "my dreams"
writer: "neha bhat"
slug: "my-dreams"

i had so many dreams
settled in my eyes

and then suddenly 
i woke up

and the dreams 
one by one
all of them
just disappeared

and told me before they went
'we are resettling somewhere
else.'

and then my dreams left me
all alone...
