She just sat.
Under the naked, blue sky.

Drenched in the blazing
Rays of the sun.

Gleefully playing around.
With birds flowers and her soft toys.

Her reflection felt on the grass. 
Neatly edged caricature of hers.

Around her, 
There was so much beauty
Splendour and brightness.

Not for her.
She had no way other than touch 
To feel everything
Why?
Because she couldn’t see.
She,
Was blind…
