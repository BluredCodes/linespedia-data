---
title: "you and i"
writer: "neha bhat"

You and I
Are a picturesque dream.
Like an angel coming 
and blessing a child’s
every dream.

You and I
We are two of its kind
One is the heart
And other? Is the mind.
One is what breaks
And other is that binds.

You and I
We’ll see each other
Through. Leaning on each other
We’ll hold unto
You and I.

You and I
A dream perfectly nurtured,
Smile to each other 
When we open our eyes.

You and I 
We are each other’s solace.
Drops of tear that
Fall from the other’s eye.
You and I 

A perfect reverie.
