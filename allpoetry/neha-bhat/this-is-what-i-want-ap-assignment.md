this is what i want
yes this is what i want

a little peace
a little world
a little love

all for myself.

just me. 
all alone

this is what i want.

i want a life of peace
where i am what i am

no pretence
no hurry
no worry
no lies
and no different faces 

just me

yes 
this is what i want
