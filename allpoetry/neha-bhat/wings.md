---
title: "wings"
writer: "neha bhat"

Give me my wings.
Cause I want to fly
Far far away
And then?
Then come back,
Sit in your lap
And tell you,
‘See, I went to far away places
Saw every human race
Met people just like me 
And
even like what I couldn’t imagine them to be.
Then
By and by
I grew tired 
Of the same old people.
The same old places.
Same old faces.
Grew tired of my wings.
And I just didn’t feel like 
Flying. 
Anymore.’

And I looked up to you—
‘Now I don’t want to fly. Anymore.’But wait
Don’t take away my wings yet. 
I don’t want to fly right now.
But who knows?
I might want to start again all over
Tomorrow…
