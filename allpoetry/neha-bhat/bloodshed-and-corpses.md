---
title: "bloodshed and corpses"
writer: "neha bhat"
slug: "bloodshed-and-corpses"
source: "AllPoetry"

bloodshed bloodshed and 
more bloodshed
one corpse, two corpses and many more corpses
and in this way, i reached the other end of the road.
