---
title: "sleepless nights"
writer: "neha bhat"
slug: "sleepless-nights"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/45982-sleepless-nights-by-neha-bhat"
---

The eyes just dream on.
Open. Seeing vivid imageries
Of what went by

Someday I would like to
Tell you too
Where all I have been
All hellish things I have
Lived through
Escaping unscathed but
Assuming it to be
My last moment
Alive…

The wish for a night’s sleep
Has remained a wish.
Lashes don’t droop
Eyes don’t shut.

Sleep lost
In pain and anguish
Sleepless nights 
I have had
On someone else’s worries
But my own?
Not until today.

The heart weeps
Tears drop from the eyes,
So much sadness, sorrow
Unhappiness.
Everything here lies entwined.

Sleepless nights
On my worries
Worries?
What all do I have?
Many perhaps,
But not telling
Mute, hushed.

Words don’t form
Tongue doesn’t speak.
Only eyes see visions
On sleepless nights.

Every night 
Is a sleepless night.
Scared, frightened
And terrified 

Are my sleepless nights.
