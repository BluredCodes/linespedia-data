---
title: "Eighteen Again"
writer: "Gina145"

I'll never be eighteen again,
I'll never get the chance
To get up on an empty stage
And dance a carefree dance.

I'd love to be eighteen again
With the knowledge of my years,
But can you have the knowledge
Without the grownup fears?

If I were eighteen again,
What choices would I make?
Perhaps I'd be a doctor
And heal for healing's sake.
Perhaps I'd be the lawyer
Who'd bring justice to the world,
Maybe just a plain hairdresser,
Keeping Madonna's fine locks curled.

I'll never be eighteen again,
Perhaps it's just as well,
I'd still be simply me again
Through my heaven and my hell,
I'd never be a doctor,
I can't stand the sight of blood,
If I lived another's dream,
My life would be a dud.
I'd never be a lawyer,
Nor hairdresser to a star,
If I can't be Madonna,
I'd rather watch her from afar.
