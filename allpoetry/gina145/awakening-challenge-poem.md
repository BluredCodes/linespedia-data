Out of seemingly endless darkness
Came a brand new day
Not yours this
But mine alone
And I gazed in wonder
At all its living colour
Spring green, the grass grew lush
Carpet to my still raw feet
And overhead the brightest blue
With just a fluff of white
That I’d walked on once
With you
And purple flowers growing wild
A red dress on a dancing child
And on and on
My eyes were drawn
To all dark’s hostages free
Until I felt I’d burst
