I dare to walk beyond all horizonsI try to fathom the depth of seaI dream to fly up above the zenithThe sky, the watersThe free breezeI embraceDreams unlimited,I workStars will tell my story...When I posted this poem, I didn't thought that this would be required. But for the sake of some people, I am here to explain some things. Well, one can blame me of being a snob. But this poem is not one from a snob. I just wanted to convey that I am going to make my way to my destiny. I am going to work endlessly to reach there. What I aim might seem a dream, a castle in the air. But that's where they should be, isn't it? I am going to put foundations below my castles. I am going to give a try, by heart. And if I am true to my heart, my soul, then I feel that I would reach the horizon that I seek. That pinnacle, that zenith would me mine, one day sure. And then THE STARS WILL TELL MY STORY...
