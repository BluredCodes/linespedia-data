A wall

So high

A child leaps

Knows

Father will hold

Trust...



Autumn

In the woods

Bare trees

Wait for Spring

As leaves unfold

Hope...



The hilltop

Song of Larks

Gentle breeze

Sun rising

Hearts behold

Peace...



A tired man

Sleeping

Resting at ease

Mother's lap; remembering

stories she told

Bond...



Eyes touch

Silently talk

Moments freeze

Unspoken promises

Hearts make, as, you I hold

Love...Just my heart's voice...
