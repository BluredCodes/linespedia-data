Lap of Nature

I am lying

The river murmurs

Trees whisper

Birds chirp

Colours of the rainbow

I behold

No thoughts

No brain at work

Heart content

Just one emotion

Its so beautiful!

Is this Heaven?

This is Heaven!

Echoes the heart

Is this Dream?

This is Dream!!

But has come true

I can feel the serenity of the peace

Its love everywhere

Says the river,

Say the birds

Softly says to me every tree

This love of Nature

It sets my Soul free...
