Closest to me, nearest to heart u would always be
This closeness i feel, but cannot see
Wish we r friends today n' forever,
And scale horizons higher than ever
Only time would tell where we reach, how we go,
But on voyage to success, together we'll row.
Friend u r revered today n' always,
For what u r with your lovely ways...
