---
title: "I wish..."

My heart open 
I embrace the world
A niche of love
I dream to create
A nest of care
I wish to make
O dear
Let us be friends
Let us spread love
Let us walk together
Spreading smiles
Embracing hearts
Enlivening hopes
In the hearts deep
That the world of love
We will reach
Before we rest,
Before we go to sleep...
