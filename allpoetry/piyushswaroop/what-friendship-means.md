title: "What Friendship Means..."

Friendship means hearts beating together, 
Friendship means pains we make light as feather, 
Friendship means unspoken promises that friends keep ,
Friendship means unspoken bonds hearts deep,
Friendship means smiles that friends spread,
Friendship means tears together friends shed,
Friendship means you feel I am there,
And I need not say I do care.
Friendship means warm hands in moments tense,
Friendship means cares n' shares hence,
Friendship --- so easily felt, but be defined never,
Wish we are friends today and forever, 
Friendship means warmth of day, light of ray, 
In the end so I just say, 
Friendship means dreams unlimited that we'll see,
Friendship really really means you to me...
