I hate Terry's mom
because she's a bitch.
I hate how she works at
Abercrombie and Fitch.
I hate how her voice sounds
When she picks up the phone
And how happy she sounds
To tell me Terry's not home.
I hate how she's not shy
About wanting my dad,
And I hate how she says things
That make Terry sad.
I hate Terry's mom
And have all my life,
I know it sounds bad
Because she's my wife.This just popped into my head as I was falling asleep and I felt compelled to write it down.  Feel free to tell me how much it sucks.
