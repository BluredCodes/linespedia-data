I’M SO INCONSIDERATE!
WHY DO I BRING EVERYBODY DOWN?
HOW IS IT THAT PEOPLE DON’T BEAT ME UP EVERY DAY?
I LOVE ME.A lovely little blurb I found hidden somewhere I had intended to forget about.
