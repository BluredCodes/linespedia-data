Someday I'll tell you everything you want to hear.
Someday I hope you feel the same,
Someday when I'm ready to dive in again,
Someday when it's okay to speak her name.

Someday I'll let you rearrange my room.
Someday I know I'll hold you all night long.
Someday I promise it will get better.
Someday I will write you a love song.

I hope someday she will forget me.
I hope someday I see you every day.
I hope someday my family will forgive me.
I hope someday we'll be okay.

Someday I'll show up nervous, holding daisies.
Someday I'll take you walking in the rain.
Someday I'll hold you when you're crying.
Someday I'll take away your pain.

Someday we'll travel across the country.
Someday we'll share the faces of our past.
Someday we'll become best friends in the world.
Someday I'll know this sort of thing can last.

I hope someday you'll hold my hand.
I hope someday to wake up next to you.
I hope someday you'll say you love me.
I hope someday I say it, too.This is my life right now.  The most personal thing I've written on here.  I know the "Someday" thing is corny and drags on.  But that's because what I feel is corny and drags on.  So there.
