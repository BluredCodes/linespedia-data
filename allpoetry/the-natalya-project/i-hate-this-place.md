---
title: "I Hate This Place"
writer: "The Natalya Project"
slug: "i-hate-this-place"

Thoughts and feelings, they are nothing.
Daily choices form our lives.
I'm in shambles, feeling everything.
Only by choices can we survive.
The feeling's mutual, I hate myself
For bringing nothing to this place.
It won't sink in that I'm an asshole,
A broken life, a shattered vase.
Timing's wrong, so walk away
From things you never thought you'd find.
It makes no sense for me to give up,
But there is no reasoning with my mind.
Photographs and frozen moments
Remain emblazened on my soul.
Waiting here for my good friend,
Goddam, it's getting cold.wrote this this morning before school.
