It was difficult then
It’s difficult now
You’re not voicing your thoughts
You’re not making a sound
You were so secure then,
What’s happened to you?
Why do you lose your head,
And why don’t you answer?
You have so much to give
Please don’t turn away.don't remember writing this, but then again, I don't remember writing lots of things in my notebooks.
