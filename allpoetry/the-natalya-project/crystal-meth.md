title: "Crystal Meth"

So blind and beautiful.
Queen of the storms in my world.
Far-fetched and based on nothing.
Knock down, drag out girl.

Touch like the fire of a dragon,
Stare like the kiss of death,
Power of total Armageddon.
Sad like crystal meth.

Chained up in parties and bars,
you are your own friend.
Make up and drown all your feelings.
Spiraling down and to what end?
