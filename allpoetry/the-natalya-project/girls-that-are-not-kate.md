Hey Donna, if you wanna,
We can pick up a bite at the Whattaburger.
Now, tell me how
To get there 'cuz I'm drunk with hunger.
Hunger for your love and hot french fries.
I see forever and a milkshake when I look in your eyes.

If it were meant to be, I wouldn't have to pee.
Let's take this as a sign and let's throw it away.
I know wheat he meant: in a penny arcade is where we spent
Our pennies - there weren't many - 
But it's over now, so just get lost.

Oh, Sarah, Scarlet O'Hara
Would look like Marilyn if she stood right next to you -
that's what you do.
You make everyone feel like Norma Jean because
Darkness and self-importance are the key.
You see an empty wallet and kitchen when you're looking at me.

If it were meant to be, I wouldn't have to see
You drive into a sign that says to slow down, turbo.
I know what it was; you just ignored it because
You're evil, Evel Knievel,
You stole my bike, so get on with your life.

How many bank tellers must I date?
Lots of fish in the sea, but none on my plate.
I showed up to my own Bar Mitzvah late.
What I would do if I could just have Kate...
