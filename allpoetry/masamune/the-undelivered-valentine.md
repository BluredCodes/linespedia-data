---
title: "The Undelivered Valentine"
writer: "Masamune"
slug: "the-undelivered-valentine"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/40653-The-Undelivered-Valentine-by-Masamune"

i've felt this way for a long time
and yesterday i thought you did too
all i wanted was to hold you
and i hoped you wanted to feel your lips next to mine
as badly as i did

it all seemed so perfect
that look in your eyes
that sound in your voice:
soft, gentle, scared a little
but strong as always
i thought for sure
you held the same desire as i



but no


you don't feel for me like that
i don't blame you
i know i'm not your type
and i can't make you love me


but still


i loved you before i understood what love was
and i still don't understand love
so i love you now
more than ever
regardless
that we can't be
we
just me as me
and you as you
