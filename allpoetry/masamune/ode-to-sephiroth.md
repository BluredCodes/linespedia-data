who would've ever thought a polygon could mean so much?
long silver hair
long silver sword
the beauty of the bishonen.

if only you were real..
i'd lock you in a cage
or perhaps chained to a wall
my dark little mage

oh why did you go crazy,
oh why did kill,
my sweet polygonal clone?
