walking in the park i noticed the path
a foot path
not often trodden
something over there called me to it
and i beckoned you closer 
as i stopped and looked down the overgrown path
you came
asked if i thought we should follow it
i nodded

we went down the path in near silence
not creepy silence
and i had things i wanted to say
but had no words for
and then we came to a fork
"that way"
i pointed to the right
there was a tree hanging low over it
you went in first
and then silently told me to be quiet
i followed
questioning it all
then i understood
hundreds of them
butterflies

it was an odd shaped clearing that my path lead us too
a nearly perfect circle with only one way in
thick trees all around
i had never been here before
we stood in the center
and watched as they fluttered around us
in silence
but not the silence where neither of us had words
just there was nothing to say

i pondered that night what had led us there
had you conjured up something
as a sort of reverse present
so i could think i found it and gave it to you
or had i led us there with some subconscious force
or was it pure chance
i don't know
and i odn't care anymore
the memory of that beautiful sight 
will stay with me forever
and i thank you
for holding my hand during it
it makes the memory warmerthis is a dramtization of acual events
we did see a....swarm....of butterflies in the park
they were beautiful
