---
title: "self doubt"
writer: "Masamune"
slug: "self-doubt"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/28436-self-doubt-by-Masamune"
---

i know i'm not perfect
but i fake it pretty well
maybe that's my problem:
who i want to be
who i can't be

i tell myslef i'm real
a human with a mind
but i fear i'm trapped in the bullshit:
that i lie to myself
that i lie to the world

i know i'm not perfect
and i know that if i try
i'll just be another one of them
of empty-mind whom i so despise.
