---
title: "what you get"
writer: "Masamune"
slug: "what-you-get"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/37100-what-you-get-by-Masamune"
---

i hate you for breathing
i hate you for the sound of your heartbeat
i hate the way you raped me
took away my soul

promised so much
yet given so little
i feel naked and broken
bruised and bloody
and it was done by your beautiful hands

so now when i walk away 
you'll still be cold
same as always
and i'll tell myself you're hiding tears in your laughter
as i walk and wipe away my own
