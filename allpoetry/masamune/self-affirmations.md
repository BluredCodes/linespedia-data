writer: "Masamune"

you think i'm pretty?
i hope you do
but if you don't
it's your own fault
i'm good enough for me

do you think i'm worth it?
i sure hope you do
cause if you don't
you don't know what you're missing

do you think i'm strong?
you be stupid if you didn't
for i have already proved it to you
over and overthis isn't really to or about anyone. it's just there to be read.
