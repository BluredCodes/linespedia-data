It's cold inside
perhaps my heart 
has frozen over
just like yours
maybe all my dreams
have burnt out 
with the flame
that was my
passion
could my reason 
for living
have escaped my
being
was it 
that night
was it the
words you
lied
that changed me
from the warm person to
this empty cold
being
with nothing to live for but
a dream that
has faded beyond
nothingness
am 
i like
you know
shallow and fireless
just another 
figure
with
out
purpose
my tears
are colder 
than the ice
in your soul
that i 
thought
i melted
it's empty inside
hollower
than you
shallower
than the pool of tears my cold
bedside
am i as empty 
as you now
with your lies
and your beauties
all fake
just like
lovenot my usual style. i was trying to prove something with it. tell me if it works.
