---
title: "It"
writer: "Masamune"
slug: "it"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/25550-It-by-Masamune"
---

i feel it in your palms
that power.
that warmth.
the one that has no words to describe it.

i can taste it in your tears
not just saline.
not just sadness.
tastes like strength on its knees.

i can see it in your mind
all its trials.
all its pain.
but still it lingers.

i can hear it in your breath
the stability.
the strength.
never quavering

i can smell it off your skin
so sweet.
so pure.
yet never to be beheld.

i know it is with you.
though it has no words to be spoken.
i know it is with you.
though i know not what it is.please don't put a four's or five's if you don't mean it.
