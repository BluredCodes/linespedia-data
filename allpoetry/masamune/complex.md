---
title: "complex"
writer: "Masamune"
slug: "complex"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/25035-complex-by-Masamune"
---

i love you.
lie, love is just hormones to you. 

i've never felt this way before.
truth, but have you ever felt anything?

i want to be with you forever.
lie, there is no forever.

i'd die if i saw you crying.
lie, you have before....you're still alive.

i need you.
truth, but only because someone needs to need me
