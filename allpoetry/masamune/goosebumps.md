---
title: "goosebumps"
writer: "Masamune"
slug: "goosebumps"
source: "AllPoetry"

i have those goosebumps again
the ones that come without reason or warning
and don't leave till they've covered your whole body
then my eyes start to water
and my world get blurry
so i rub my face
with my goosebump-y hands
till the tears are streaked across my face
then my goosebumps are gone
but my face is full of saline
so i wash it in cold water
and get goosebumps againlook at it as a metephor
