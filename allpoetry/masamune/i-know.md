---
title: "i know..."
writer: "Masamune"
slug: "i-know"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/44853-i-know...-by-Masamune"
---

i know it doesn't help to say things
things like
it'll be ok
some day
one day
just not now
grit your teeth
it'll be ok

i know it doesn't help to cry
cry over nothing
your problems
your life
your pain
its not mine
it is more than nothing
but it's not use to cry

i know it doesn't help to hope
hope for things
that may never come
dreams
wishes
prayers
for tomorrow
will you be here then?

i know it doesn't help to say i love you
but i'll say it anyways
cause i do
and
maybe
it might help
and that's the truth my dear
