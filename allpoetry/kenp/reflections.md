Reflections

I gaze in this mirror
day after day.
Not thinking much
of what i see.

Hi there.
I am your mirror
and i am here,
whenever you need me.
Through thick or thin,
on days you can't win.

When things are low;
I will be there 
to watch you cry.
Not a word i will say.
I will watch you 
resolve it,
in your own way.

When things are high,
I'll be there too.
Now this is better 
than seeing you blue.
Your eyes your smile,
brighten my day;
I hope i will see you
more this way.

All secrets told here,
remain within.
For no one else to see,
Just you and me.

Reflections are
all that i see.
Inner beauty,
and all you can be;
are real possessions
i wish i could see.
For true reflections
can only be
from within;
your personality.

Take me with you
where you go;
peer deep within me,
into your soul.
The mirror within you
will show a truer view;
of all you can really do
and be.

Goodbye for now;
I know not when.
But I'll be there 
within you;
and here waiting
waiting till then.
For you, only you.
Till then.Any and all comments are welcomed.
