title: "Visions"

Through the small 
viewfinder
an aborable vision appears,
a very striking woman
sitting in her chair.
Long light auborn hair
softly flowing over
tender shoulders bare,
soft hair gently parted
off her forehead where,
gentle steel eyes focus
on the task that 
to her is crystal clear.

Her delicate soft nose
partially conceals the view,
of velvet crimson lips
where a hint of a smile
is showing through,
to warm the hearts of all
who stand within any room.

Long sleek tender fingers
rap out their tapping tune
on the ivory keyboard
since mornings early dawn.

A gentle downward pressure
then a smooth release
and this lovely vision
is captured for all time,
imprinted deeply
in the emulsion of my memory,
secure there for all time.

Now I'm frequently drawn
into that darkroom in my mind
printing out this picture,
this vision in my mind
