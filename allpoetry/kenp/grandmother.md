My Grandmother
Was quite short and stout.
Had to be to accommodate her heart.

My Grandmother
Lived through live poor.
Yet was the richest person I knew.

Mt Grandmother
Had very little education.
I learned more from her any schooled learning 

My Grandmother
Never showed any anger.
Maybe that's why I am so easy going.

My Grandmother
Never talked of the many problems that she met.
Could be that's why I too am like that.

My Grandmother
Has long since gone.
But in my heart I still sing her song.
