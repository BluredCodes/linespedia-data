title: "The Smarter Gunslinger (Ballad)"
writer: "kenp"
slug: "the-smarter-gunslinger-ballad"

Out from the saloon the stranger came
packing two guns, this man with no name.
Out to challenge for claim to fame,
me the fastest gunslinger in this town called Blaine.

Was there when I called him out into the street,
dust balls were a swirling all around our feet.
Noon sun was high and shining it's white light,
above and behind me, off to my right.
There we stood the two of us ready for the fight.

Eyes ablazed a-twitchin
fingers there an-itchin
feet they were a-twitchin
standing in the heat.
Out there in the middle
of a hot and dusty street.

The glinting off my badge was causing him to blink,
my guns were out and on him faster than a wink.
Bullets rush towards him faster that he could think,
blood is a-flowing as I turn to finish my drink.

Eyes no longer a-twitchin
fingers no more an-itchin
feet they're still a-twitchin
dead there in the heat.
Laying in the middle
of a hot and dusty street.
