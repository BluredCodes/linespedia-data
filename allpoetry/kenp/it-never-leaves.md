It Never LeavesSilent terror breaks,the stillnesswithin the room.Cooing softly:Done  then  gone.      And:Then left lying there,naked;violated and bruised.Not yet old enough,to identify;with the hurt that just passed through     Then:Softly sobs,when she hears,a mother's whisper:'Are you OK dear?'      Now:These words and thoughts,return crystal clear.Even today;when death is near.I am just a beginner, so any comments and help will be appreciated.
