The school that I used to attend,way back then,was smallcompared with schools of now.Problems within were somewhat similarthen;we had our bullies too.Ralph for oneappeared large and strong,to be feared and avoided by all.Pushing and shoving;sure there were fightsbut once they were overyou were still ableto make the home hike.It was different then;back then,there was no lunch money to steal.Brown bags full,these were the bully's catchback then.Today, hell,a much different storyto tell.Power, guns, knifes, sex, crack, potand more.These are what they strive fornow.This is what bullies are all about,rapist and murderers outstalking the streets,created every second fromsocieties;Henry Ford factories.Where will it end?Hell, who can tell.Ask them.The bullies alreadyhave the bomb.PLEASE CRITIQUE IE:  This  suxs, this is what i see wrong, go back to driving a truck, etcAny and all are more than appreciated.
