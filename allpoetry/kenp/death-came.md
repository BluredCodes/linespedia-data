A Town's Lament

Death came,
and with it went:
Logging trucks to pay the rent,
homes for shelter,
monies for families content,
business for the anchor,
mills that needed workers.

Death came,
and with it went:
the life of a small town.
Where did it go?
Ask the Liberal government!
