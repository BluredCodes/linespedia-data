---
title: "Promise of a friend"
writer: "zee"

We used to pledge this promise long ago,We said where you go, I will goI will follow in your steps close behind,A more devoted friendship none shall findO we were young girls of nieve minds,When once we pledged such vows to one another,But still I hold this promise in my heart,And still I vow that we shall never part.Though the road winds uphill as it goes,And every step seems harder than before,We travel this journey as friends,And together we'll reach the end.Now we are women with our separate lives,We have our separate cares, joys and woes,Have we betrayed this bond of trust we spoke?The answer I would give you here is No.For still we share together the things most dear to us,For still we share our dreams and our hopes,And still we walk together the same path,That leads us through this stretch of time called life.Though the road winds uphill as it goes,And every step seems harder than before,We travel this journey as friends,And together we'll reach the end.
