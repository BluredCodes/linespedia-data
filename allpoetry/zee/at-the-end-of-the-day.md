title: "At the end of the day"
writer: "zee"

At the end of the day,
I have you and you alone,
I know that nothing else shall stand.
I could build myself a palace, 
In my own perfect world,
But I know that it would crumble in the end.

If all I'm living for is you,
Then I will not be disappointed,
If I'm living for myself I'll end up empty.
Nothing in this world,
Can satisfy the way you do,
But your love endures throughout eternity.

I want you and you alone,
More than anything else,
Come and be the rock on which I stand.
Build for yourself a kingdom,
Of 'Jesus People' on this earth,
Who together on your grace alone can stand.

You are God, You are King,
You are Lord of everything,
You're the breath I breathe that gives life to my soul.
You're my life, You're my strength,
You are my perfect peace,
You're my vision, You're my purpose, You're my goal.
