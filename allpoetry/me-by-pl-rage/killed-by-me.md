title: "Killed by me"

I walk through the silence of night
Shrouded in brooding darkness
Hide now my dear
Going to take your life
Run away from me
I will set you free
Desperate words of love
How can I believe you now?
Never before did you care
Scream for me now my dove
The sweet song of agony
Pure is your blood, I see
Red as red, as me
This shouldn’t be
Blue is your color, queen of ice
Have I made a mistake?
Are you true to me?
No!
Your tricks won’t work tonight
No illusions will stop my blade
Let done be done
It ends here and now
