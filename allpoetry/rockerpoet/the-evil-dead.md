title: "The Evil Dead"

Evil is positive 
In its existence 
Grab thyself a light 
For it has come close 
To transform thy soul 
Into a mess of bones 


Run nowhere my friend 
This is the beginning 
Of the extreme end 
Just a joke thou play 
Ends up with eternal regrets 
With only dust left 


Cry baby cry 
No one is in sight 
If being alone thrills 
Then bear the fruits 
And sleep real tight 
With the evil dead tonight
