Dark So damn dark Is that your heart From deep down inside Fear So much fear Is it in your eyes From where they turn blind If your evil Is my good And my evil Is your good Can we reach a compromise If we do Will it be Brutality Nudity Vulgarity Cruelty All in one War within a war I was told To 'terminate with extreme prejudice' Yet to discover My greatest fear Did not come from your destruction But from within my dangling heart In this chilling time Of horror2000,
