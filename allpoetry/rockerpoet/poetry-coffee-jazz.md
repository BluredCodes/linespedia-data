The mood Of enjoying that cuppa This good A dozen of tunes to couple Captivating lines Like the drizzling raindrops I am blind To all that urban props This is living Without lies A fool's loving Never diesTo all fellow poets:Ain't this what all of us are doing?Live thy life.
