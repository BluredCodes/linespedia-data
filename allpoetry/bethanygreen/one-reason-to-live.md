title: "one reason to live"

They were there right beside you
Then they dissapeared 
They were supposed to be there forever 
Then they were torn away and taken
They objected to the gesture 
But the mother always wins
You cant see them anymore she says
As you watch your life crumble
You wonder if there is any reason left to live
Then you turn around and see that one smiling face
That one reason to live
You can't leave him alone
You know he'll help you through it
