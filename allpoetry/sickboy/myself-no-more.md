---
title: "Myself No More"
writer: "SickBoy"
slug: "myself-no-more"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/5130-Myself-No-More-by-SickBoy"
---

Gnarled heart within my chest.My ravaged body the world detests.Twisted soul upon my visage.Sunken eyes void of presence.Nothing but a memory to all.Sanity refrained I do recall.I shall vex no more.To fade is my wish.So bade farewell to this currish.
