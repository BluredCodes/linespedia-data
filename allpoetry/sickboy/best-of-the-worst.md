---
title: "Best of The Worst"
writer: "SickBoy"
slug: "best-of-the-worst"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/40826-Best-of-The-Worst-by-SickBoy"

You my best of friends yet my worst.
Your always there with a vile of posion.
I hate you but you make me happy.
Please let me go so I can stay.
With out you theres no one. 
With you theres death.
Feed me your lies your pains,
so I can steal a piece of your soul.
So I can live without living.
