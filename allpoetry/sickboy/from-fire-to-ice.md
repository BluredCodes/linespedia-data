---
title: "From Fire To Ice"
writer: "SickBoy"
slug: "from-fire-to-ice"
source: "AllPoetry"

Drops of ice from tiery eyes from when my pain douth arise.
My will broken down, my heart renounced.
Forsaken all others through pain to pleasures.
My fobb offed love to her eternal foray.
