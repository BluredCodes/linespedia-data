title: "I wanna Live Forever"
writer: "Renokhan"

B. Franklin Smith has died, and everything is gone
No estate, no legacy, no young to carry on
B.F. Smith is dead; no one will know him again
Just like he never existed, his memory whispers in the wind
Smith left the world a man alone
A world no worse without him home

In this world of physical things
All that is eternal is human beings
The ones that remember and create the past
The ones without which nothing would last
