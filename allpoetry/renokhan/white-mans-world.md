---
title: "White Man's World"
writer: "Renokhan"
slug: "white-mans-world"
source: "AllPoetry"

The angry black man screams "why?" 
The suburbanite man says "don't cry!" 

The shinny white lady knows today is great 
The disillusioned dark woman feels only hate 

How can they get together 
And attempt to weather 
This injustice of life
And allow them to entice 
The level of freedom the white man feels 
Instead of the weight of a white man's heels?
