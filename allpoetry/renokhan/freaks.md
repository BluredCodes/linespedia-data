---
title: "Freaks"
writer: "Renokhan"
slug: "freaks"

To them I may seem a freak
The ones that follow like sheep
To live my life how I choose
To show them I’m not to lose
The ones who have a good time
Laughing at freaks different in mind
That their time spent is hollow
Because all they do is follow
