I can't stop staring at the walls

She's dead, she's dead, she's dead, she's dead
Is all that keeps repeating in my head

I can't stop waiting for the fall
I can't stop staring at the wall
All the regrets are mine to keep
All the tears are mine to weep

Fuck this rhyming poem
Doesn't it know that I am not the same anymore?
