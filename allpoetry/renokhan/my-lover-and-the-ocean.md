---
title: "My Lover and the Ocean"
writer: "Renokhan"

Seagals call
I find
You falling away
Eclipsing the moon and muting the ocean

I reach
Catching you softly
With my guitar
Playing on a silver line

I see moonshine pale
And Trees bend
Beneath your sunlight smile

Tomorrow's the day
That we have met a thousand times
Under your breath

Still the sun does not rise
The sea switches tides
I let you free
But still you fall to me.
