So happy being sheep are sheep
Told when to eat
When to sleep
Told to find a mate to keep

A shepherd's there to light the way
Keep the sheep from going astray
Shown which path is good 
And which path is bad
Respect thy mother
Love thy dad

To be a sheep is a happy life
To be unaware of life's ugly strife
To stand in lines without know why
To waste a life waiting to die

Are we sheep today?
To act like sheep in every way
I rebel with all I am and nothing less
Which makes me a black sheep I guess
