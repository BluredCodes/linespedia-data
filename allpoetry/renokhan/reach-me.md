---
title: "Reach Me"
writer: "Renokhan"
slug: "reach-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/7501-Reach-Me-by-Renokhan"
---

She approached me out of the race
Walked up and with a word expressed
God's grace upon her face 

She held truth and desire in opposing hands
Forcing me to take a stand on life and love 
And things I knew nothing of 

I tried to reach her words
How like an angel did she speak
All the wonderful things she had to teach 

But I paid them no heed
For she has what I need 

We talked
We danced
We made love again and again 

But none of that made sense to me
I wanted something she did not have
I just wanted her to make me glad 

She said she had no strength 
To carry any more than what she keeps
That I had to stand on my own and learn to speak 

But I paid her no heed
For she is all I need
