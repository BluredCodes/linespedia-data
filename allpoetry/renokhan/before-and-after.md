---
title: "Before and After"
writer: "Renokhan"

Before as a child, life was easy.
I never knew strife, and I hardly knew pain.
A world of wonder and mystery called to my brain,
Like a sponge soaking up water,
I could never get my fill.
The sky was huge and I had nothing to do.
Birthdays were special and a year seemed forever.
Life was good when I was a child.
After I grew up the land I live in 
seemed so strange.  Like a change 
had occurred and there was no one 
to blame.  Love and lies and murder 
and death gained meaning and life grew 
beyond wonder and into disgust.  Never again 
became a recurring theme.  Life moved 
further from the dream.
