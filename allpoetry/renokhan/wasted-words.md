---
title: "Wasted Words"
writer: "Renokhan"
slug: "wasted-words"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

All these words do nothing for him 
Sitting idle in some poetry forum 
Wishing the world would fix itself 

But Stephen's tired of the vagrants' stares 
Forcing him to the edge of tears 
While he fights his way to work 

Yet he never returns their pleas 
He hides his eyes until he leaves 
Saying "God I'm glad that's not me" 

But Stephen has time for sex 
And idle days to waste at rest 
While he should be making a change

And Stephen remits his duty to men
He hasn’t the time for a soup kitchen
He just can’t get beyond himself.

He has a lover to efface from his skin
So his powerful words are wasted on revenge
Instead of helping his fellow man

Because we all have time to eat 
And such nice places to sleep 
We never think beyond ourselves
