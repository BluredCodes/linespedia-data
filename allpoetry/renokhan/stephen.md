---
title: "Stephen"
writer: "Renokhan"
slug: "stephen"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8800-Stephen-by-Renokhan"
---

Sorrow kept but unexpressed 
Selfless judgment, past regrets 
Empathy too grand to stand 
Lost Lost lost in this land 

He's got sweetness, he's got sweat 
He's got sorrow, he's got shit 

Mismatch socks, deep deep thoughts 
Wrinkled hair and clothes in knots 
Dreams and cares that love not dare 
Writes of truth, gets back stares 

He's got pain, he can't explain 
He's got pity, he won't fit 
He's got time but he still quits 
He's got pain, he feels drained 
He has questions he can't exchange 

A daydreamer caught in thoughts his own 
A man who's fought to keep his throne 
A boy who's scared to be alone 

He has sorrow he has sweat 
He's got memories he's got shit
