The war I lost, it was my own
I lost it being away from home
It's not the car, it's not the smile
It's not the sex, or his style
It's the way I waited knowing you'd be there
Then seeing so clearly back in your stare
That I was defeated; done
Some other guy had won
I lost the war then
But not because of him
