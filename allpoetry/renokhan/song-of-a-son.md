---
title: "Song of a Son"
writer: "Renokhan"
slug: "song-of-a-son"
source: "AllPoetry"

I've seen your freedom 
and it's better to forget 
the submissive behavior 
that somehow you've kept 

You see him standing 
with that new wife of his 
yet you smile and wave 
and hurry the kids 

Oh all those things 
you dreamt to say 
unused, you let them 
simply slip away 

The act of respect 
that is his game 
you let him play it 
while you act the same 

He calls you Honey and 
suddenly you're floored 
though he uses you still 
his attention you adore 

You loved him deep 
and probably still do 
never able to escape 
that he doesn’t love you 

So you live life free 
to do as you choose 
but when it comes to Dad 
you will always lose
