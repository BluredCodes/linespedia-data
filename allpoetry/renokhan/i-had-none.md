---
title: "I had None"
writer: "Renokhan"
slug: "i-had-none"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

You waited all day 
For words that lay 
And creep 
And stall 
Yet live 

I waited all night 
For the heart light 
I saw a cat crawl 
I saw a dream end 
I saw, I saw, I saw 
So many things 

We’ve traveled far along 
Had many passengers 
With smiles and songs 
And many ideas of love 

I saw tomorrow fly 
I saw dreams die 
I saw, you saw 
Oh so many things 

I saw the hollow sky 
I saw men lie 
I felt a warm hand 
Grab my arm 
Calling me to creep 
And to stall 

And all the passengers wait 
For the ride, the ride, the ride 
To end, stall, live 
I sang you a song 
You sang along 

We traveled far 
Into the dark, dark, dark 
We sat awake waiting for the lark 
We had seas to cross 
And lives to part 
But we stalled 
I lied, you cried 
We were just too afraid 

All that in just one day
