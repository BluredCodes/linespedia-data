title: "Apathy"
writer: "Renokhan"

I strive in cultural weakness
The holes made by too few having way too much
And no one understanding the other

I step from light  I fall into shadows

I walk in the dark recesses of a sacked society
I watch the vagrants life unhinge
Lying in the bright light of day
As Godfearing men clean the streets of trash
And the dark faceless forms that invade their commerce
With their boxes and weather newsprint

I see the darkness  I'm blinded by light

I see sights that angels abandon in pity and wives revolt in disgust

I tread in darkness  I walk in light
One can not see the other

I invite the light to come and see the swallowing whore
As she gets plunged by lewd cocks in anonymous forums
I invite the darkness to see a new mother's child suckle at her breast

Both are repulsed
Both are quickly forgetting the other
