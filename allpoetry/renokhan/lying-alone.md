title: "Lying alone"
writer: "Renokhan"

The evening was pitch and I got lost 
Tight in my sheets, I turned and tossed 
Those Fantasies of you, and you with me 
I mixed of truth and lies with ease 
I saw your vision, I made it blue 
I found myself and thought of you
