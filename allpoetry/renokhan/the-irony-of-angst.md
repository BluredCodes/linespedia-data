---
title: "The Irony of Angst"
writer: "Renokhan"
slug: "the-irony-of-angst"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/6454-The-Irony-of-Angst-by-Renokhan"
---

I hate people because I hate me
I hate noise so just let me be
I hate color because life's too dark
I hate love cause it breaks my heart
I hate my friends
I hate all that ends
I hate skies, blue or gray
I hate tomorrows as much as Mondays
I hate everything I can see
That's because I hate what's inside of me
