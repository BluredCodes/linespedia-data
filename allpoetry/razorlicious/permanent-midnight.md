The consistent thump, pumping of blood
And beads of sweat run down icy flesh
“Tu es blessant, Jasper,” she said tonight.

A blooming canker of heavy desire
Lowers the threshold and burns the skin
“Tu es blessant nous, Jasper,” she said yesterday.

The hallucinations dance and close around
A withering body that longs to collapse
“Tu es blessant votre individu, Jasper,” she said last week.

Tremors dictate a transient psychosis
As dilated pupils glaze over with haunting death
“Tu es blessant votre vie, Jasper,” she said last month.

Plagued by the piercing of a thin metal arm
That will kiss the internal wounds dry 
“Tu es blessant, Jasper,” she said again.About heroin withdrawal. French translations: You are hurting, you are hurting us, you are hurting yourself, you are hurting your life, you are hurting.
