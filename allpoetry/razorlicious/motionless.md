---
title: "Motionless"
writer: "razorlicious"
slug: "motionless"
source: "AllPoetry"

His limbs malnourished
(skin discoloured)
His tired eyes
(sickly expression)
He walks jaggedly
(wavering about)
His skin clings to
young but old bones

His lips lifeless
(seldom smile)
His motionless words
(slurred pleas)
He stumbles about
(directionless)
His greying hair
makes him older

His body deteriorating
(tobacco-stained fingers)
He commits a slow suicide
(barely cares)
His family breaks
(falls into tiny pieces)
His last hope topples 
to the groundFor my uncle.
