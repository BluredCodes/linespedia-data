title: "Pale Fragility"

crystallized
icy roads, car windows fogged
shimmer of frosted green
powdered trees no longer
   sing peacefully
a soft cloak–
mother of pearl
leafless, wind-tattered
   they stand
crevices packed with white

the air that nips
   flushed cheeks
frozen tips of flesh
nature is pale
   fragile

breath dressed in smoke
against the faded sunlight
the world looks
   blurred
naked
simple.
