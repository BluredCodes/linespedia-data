---
title: "Forever In Flight"
writer: "razorlicious"
slug: "forever-in-flight"
source: "AllPoetry"

The perfect angel, strong
sweetly arranged
Her wings silver-lined with delicacy
Her eyes like chips of 
emerald ice, framed in gold
An elegant smile to grace us gently.
Her guiding light still
burning brightly,
soft spoken words touch their 
tender hearts.

She places her wise hand upon
my shoulder, wrapping her love 
around my wonder:
"It will be all right."
I trust her dearly, for she is the angel. . .
The angel in flight 
flying among bright-clouded skies,
over darkened waters, corrupted land.

Sing your sweet melody of hope,
forever I pray.
It will end. . .
Only when the light fades 
from the precious sky,
and the green is plucked from 
the fresh grass.
When the clouds are stolen from 
the Heavens, and sweet, 
contagious lyrics no longer flow.

You keep them alive. They flower before you.
I thank you, Angel, for your healing guidance.
Your wings will be 

   Forever in flight.
