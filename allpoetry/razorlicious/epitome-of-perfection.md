title: "epitome of perfection"
writer: "razorlicious"

blindness in a stranger's eyes
an unknown perfection true
insurmountably love's wish
the deep-rooted beauty shines

each cheek gold-dusted with faith
two shimmering soul windows, pools of blue
words grip with desire tenderly
bidding release with articulation

every waking hour a mind's prey
devouring silently the indefinable charm
it creates all safeness in life
assurance of one's own fate

the softest flesh upon touch
the sweetest musk breathed
the epitome of perfection merely
   a sense of human solace
