title: "the clerk"

she whiles the hours patiently away
a curious smile playing upon her lips
a secret uttered not but in hushed tones
whispered quietly in her joyful dreams
in long lingering gazes, in soft sighs
and how she makes it through the dreary day
stroking the keyboard with her fingertips
amid the bustle and the screaming phones
god only knows -- or so it only seems
she might be wiser than we realize
