these ramblings
fall from my dry lips
like small scattered crumbs
confectionary nothings
at whose fingertips
your weakness succumbs
here you pour
forth your restless soul
head upon my knee
plainly unaware that your
broken hunger stole
her thunder for me
and you breathe
all i have inside
comfort offered here
conspicuously sown seed
unabashed to hide
the purpose made clear
intentions
far from noble but
so unable to
resist these machinations
a scrumptious pursuit
this snaring of you
