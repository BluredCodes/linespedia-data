---
title: "ah, but"
writer: "lilanais"
slug: "ah-but"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

you must judge me
dear one
for this mess
i have made
and what
i don't explain
and wouldn't try
tied still by nothing
and this
you must tell me
mahal
for it is in your nature
and i am still a woman
of course
you must know by
now my contradictions
make me what i am but
you must love me
fair boy
and all i am to you
as you said
despite.
