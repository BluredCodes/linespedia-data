harder some daysthan this, though i've knownbetterand last call washours ago it seemscompany kept and nowalonei watch wisps waft and wonderafter you;does she make you smilein precise waysand is it her beautythat keeps,in delicate intricaciesi could never matchand wouldn't trytied here by nothingso i thinkwill this hold on the morrow?we shall see(from the june chronicles)
