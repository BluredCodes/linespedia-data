---
title: "untitled (impromptu)"
writer: "lilanais"
slug: "untitled-impromptu"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/31874-untitled--impromptu--by-lilanais"
---

overfed 
and spitting 
lies
somehow turn
to feathers but
that's all 
you see
that's all it
will ever be to
you
and i
wretched now for
nothing and
he
still miles away and
reeling
somehow
was what he said
not someday
but i'll take it
come
as it may
