It's the nearness I can't bear
When I'm alone with you,
As if your stare might pierce right through 
My entire being there.
And how, with carelessly chosen words
You can disarm me so
As I stand helpless while you throw
My dignity to the birds.
And you may never comprehend
My masochism for 
Your power over my vulnerable core--
