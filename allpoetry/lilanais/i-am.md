---
title: "i am"
writer: "lilanais"
slug: "i-am"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/39545-i-am-by-lilanais"
---

you
asked once what
i wish for
today
i am
considering
strangely that
you know me through
this silence as
i am
and how
dare i do this
for you
all light and heart
have shared
and see in you
i am
flustered
hands and feathers flying
(the wings broke long ago
as did my pretty
little circle)
but still tasting what
you take into
you and i
ache and dull
alternately
for this
we don't define
and have
made it
a way of life
so presently
to answer i
shall decline
for now
i am
beat and wretched and
wish no more
than to be
you


(for june)
