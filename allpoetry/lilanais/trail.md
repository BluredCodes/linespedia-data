---
title: "trail"
writer: "lilanais"
slug: "trail"
source: "AllPoetry"

oh hello
kind friend my
smile is bleeding
well today i
thought of you
and maybe
you could be proud i
drank in kindness
in small doses
good for the 
soul they say
makes the drummer 
stronger
and they should know
and well
today this grew
and gave again
perhaps
beginning the end
of what was never and i
wonder
sometimes how
nothing
casts such a
lovely shadow
and how 
much longer my
face will hold
for today
my smile and i
are cracking at our
corners and my
secret
place is quietly
caving in
