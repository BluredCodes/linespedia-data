title: "birthday song"

this is not a birthday song.
you don't mean to be unkind;
i am spatially aware--
see, i sit where i'm allowed,
and i am completely fed
where i watch you from outside.
all is well and nothing's wrong;
this is not a birthday song.

i think now it won't be long.
these are pretty things i find
lounging on your kitty-chair
once where i was borrowed.
these are things i do not dread
for i've nothing left to hide.
no, my love, it won't be long,
and this is not a birthday song.

22 may 2001
