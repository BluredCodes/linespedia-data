---
title: "muses"
writer: "lilanais"
slug: "muses"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

least of them now
still patiently
watches the line 
grow steadily

the faces of
his deepest sense
a lovely sea
of eloquence

here, one knows not
her constant hold
the rest silver
she ever gold

one, always praised
a princess true
on pedestals
he himself drew

another, still 
revisited
and yet to grace
the borrowed bed

and last, she waits
on his lost prides
least of muses
and worst of brides
most fortunate
for smallest shows
of devotion --

but still it grows

and once almost
the first in line
she dreams of love
and does just fine
