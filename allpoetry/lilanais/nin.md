she is alive and well.
i always say i was born too late,
but she tells me otherwise.
she lives inside and through me,
through all of us
who scream
to be free.
today she has broken loose.
she assured me of who i am
and that i am fortunate
for this.

i know her not well,
but i know her kind
for this
is me.i am flying a little tonight, albeit rather low. please pardon the whimsy and the blind bravado.
