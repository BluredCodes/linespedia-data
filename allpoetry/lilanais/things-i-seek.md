---
title: "things i seek"
writer: "lilanais"
slug: "things-i-seek"
source: "AllPoetry"

trouble here, you see
when i find certain
things i seek inside
your
such vicious eyes

sometimes capture me
thought i could remain
nonchalant to hide
my
concealed surprise

seeing as i may
fall right into this
and i should refuse
you
i should, because

much to my dismay
your so careless kiss
causes me to lose
me
as i once was
