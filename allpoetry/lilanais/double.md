here it begins
here it is made flesh
and now it blooms as
she said a cut
and forgive me today
as i must borrow
starved as i was for
this i sought
but for certain misgivings
and souls undressed
onto the kitchen table fast
(you little slut)
come as they may
caught in the undertow
into the tragic metaphor--
hell, why not.
