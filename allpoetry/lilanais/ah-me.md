---
title: "ah me"
writer: "lilanais"
slug: "ah-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

opened
an old one
today you said
you have not forgotten
shall i pray
her month is over
oh but
never such luck
ah me
and my indiscretions
new heights now
and driving
hard and fast
into these
begging hands
beating my selves
into
voluntary submission
always the best
slave and star
of one's own
freakshow
funny
can of worms this
unwanted blessing
bitter sweet and better
left most adamantly
closedah, dammit. he made me cry last night. this is one of my "boys are stupid" poems.
