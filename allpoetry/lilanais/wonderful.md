title: "wonderful"
writer: "lilanais"

how nice
to for a 
small time be
allowed here
inside you
and she
wonderful
small blonde thing
fragrant and
intoxicating
purity she makes
me feel this
blessed
beyond language and
consideration oh
how nice
to wear this
love we don't
explain
how free
to breathe this
open space
bigger inward
bareboned wide
and i
may try to deserve this
but i don't well
and humbly
i resign
i sigh i
say
how nice
