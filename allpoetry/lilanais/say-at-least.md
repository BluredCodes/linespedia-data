---
title: "say at least"

what i was
thinking i couldn't
say at least
not as i
normally
would but then
this
never was a
normal thing
as i
unaccustomed to such
purity such
nakedness
bared too much
and now quite
humbled.
what then to
do with this
moment hanging
in the air and tracing
my bewildered lips
--amazing--
what a fool will do.
