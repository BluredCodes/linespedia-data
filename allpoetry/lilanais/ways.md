(for mahal and the pretty girl)


this was
mine into the night:
cautiously speeding and shadow
in meticulous tow, making
excuses in my head as 
to why this visit was really
necessary, feeling
heart and swollen in spite of my
conscious self.
and hers:
weary and watering,
on hers to
bed and your
voice to soothe, without
me again and always
oblivious as to how she
has softly deconstructed
me with her
selflessness.
i on mine, we
exchanged pleasantries and
spoke no deeper than
where we sat, where i burned
and she billowed,
casual as a cat
in her comfort in
us -- and so each
full blown and blustered and
in selves absorbed we
into the night thus
went on our ways,
she coiling into dreaming and i
spinning deeper into love.
