(this is pure whimsy, and impromptu besides. and two beers and an empty tummy later, perhaps not a good combimation.)

for the webdreamer, and for Morpheus.


do you notice how the stars,
one by one, go to bed
leaving you with nothing and 
you
to wonder things you never wonder
in the sun?
where do they go, 
my weaver of dreams?

and as my turn
to slumber nears,
why does the one star that matters
suddenly turn up?

how does this happen?

the night, she's a tricky one.i am sooo drunk.
