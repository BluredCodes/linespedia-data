what fearyou askwhysheets to changesecrets washed awayalong withmeand she on the morrowwhile i fadesix days of solitudeand two withand afterwho knowswhat fearyou askthese unseen quiversfail to showthis devotionwon't applyat least not in your eyesuntil next timeagain you askbut answersfutiletwo months gone and stillthis indifferencea ring missinga belly flauntedperhaps no one'sbut mineif onlywhat feartruth pleasebut truth stands fearedin returns unwantedpromises unaskedfears unfounded?you tell me(from the june chronicles)
