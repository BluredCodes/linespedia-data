---
title: "that day. this night"
writer: "lilanais"
slug: "that-day-this-night"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/38878-that-day.-this-night-by-lilanais"
---

oh you.
with your buttoned-down eyes,
chest in rhythm and
lines made smooth by Sandman,
lit by the moon and
sedated by Skyy.
by day you yaup fearless
at the world,
wrecking shop and wreaking
havoc on helpless
little poet girls.
afternoon finds you
here again maybe
taking me down in silence,
naming your muses one
by elegant one
as now beneath,
now across your
tongue traces cracks
into my soul.
here this night is softly
swallowing your brow,
and we are three years into 
becoming
the stuff of legends.

and you.
you are not so ferocious.
