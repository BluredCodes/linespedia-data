---
title: "the cigarette break"
writer: "lilanais"
slug: "the-cigarette-break"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/23240-the-cigarette-break-by-lilanais"

frozen fingers frustrated
attempting this posterity
ink takes time to freely flow
as i lose my clarity
here under this cruel night
and one sustaining thought to bless
that of your warm waiting eyes
to hold me in their kind caress
such long minutes now waited
shall continue if need be
soul without a home to go
save for where you would lead me
and presently under stars bright
here illumed, i must confess
i'd wait a lifetime i'd surmise
frozen here nevertheless


for d., december 1999
