sold me short
again today
still cannot quite grasp
what holds me here 
and so
absorbed in this
whatever
we've been through this, he said
but i
just won't let it go
perhaps the pain is only
comfort
and company worth keeping
not so and still
relations pending
but this will be the last
yeah, right.

(from the june chronicles)
