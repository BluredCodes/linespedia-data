(for mahal, and for anne sexton.)


here


where i sit
and this
flashing white
box tells me not
much of you or your
philosophy today but i
am thankful and
not complaining; here
where i sit and can
dream of you
hands and mouth and
impertinent tongue,
hard eyes and
everything else in a
flash before me
dizzyingly
trying to keep with
your
wit and tender sharp
eloquence; here i
still sitting and
you now gone and
i lost in thought and reeling
from what you
left, gathering
into my body and mind
what is
spilling through my now
sore 
fingers.
