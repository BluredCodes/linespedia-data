---
title: "in retrospect --"
writer: "lilanais"
slug: "in-retrospect-"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/23238-in-retrospect----by-lilanais"
---

here now in 
this
same changed place
seven and five
and decades dryer
none the wiser 
than
when we first --
then
we poor
strangers rich
in feeling through
all into an
uncautious wind,
nothing lost and
speaking
in common tongues,
bought freedom with
nothing and what
we thought was our
sufficient
philosophy --
but here now
and again i
find me blooming as
you have said
away
from you
and you
no longer hear my eyes --
and thus we
strangers even now have
broken
pedestals and replaced
them with
stages and soapboxes,
dreading what we do not
hear and losing it
in translation.
