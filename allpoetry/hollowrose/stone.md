title: "Stone"

Staring out into the spaceAn infinite blank stare that never leaves my faceA swirl of confused emotions rages on insideThe never ending sadness I'm always trying to hideLife becomes mundaneand I don't know what to doSo I'll keep on being sadToo bad its nothing newI have become a canvasPaint me as you wishIt won't affect me anywayI won't notice if something's amissNot knowing what love isOnly knowing how to cryMy happiness has gone awayIt left the day you said goodbyeFeels as if I'm deadBut I'm very much aliveSo I'll do the only thing I know how,rock back and forth in my corner and cry.
