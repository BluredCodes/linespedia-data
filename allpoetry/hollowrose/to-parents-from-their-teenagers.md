---
title: "To Parents from their teenagers"
writer: "HollowRose"

source: "AllPoetry"

Don't tell me I can't think about nothing
I know damn well I can
You don't know what goes on in my head
You don't know who I am

I'm different from you
I know that for a fact
Don't say you'll understand
When you'll never comprehend

I may be of your flesh and blood
And that's where the likeness stops
I pose this question for you
Answer it if you can

When will all the sadness stop?
When will my sorrow end?
