title: "Over"
writer: "HollowRose"

Sadness I keep deep down inside
The feelings of sorrow I can no longer hide
Life turns boring and colors fade to gray
Reaching for the knife, something to make it go away

Watch as the blade sinks in
Its possible that death may win
A cry for help I hear them say
Looks like death won't win today

So for now its back to my corner to cry
Not anymore will I ask myself why
Maybe one day the blade will sink in
Far enough and death will win.
