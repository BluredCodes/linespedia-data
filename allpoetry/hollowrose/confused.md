---
title: "Confused"
writer: "HollowRose"

Sitting here doing nothing
simply staring off into space
So many thoughts inside my head
and I don't know what to think
Things were going good,even I was doing fine
but all that changed the day that you came back into my life

Made to wear a mask they know that I can't stand
everything changed so fast I don't know what to do
To me you feel like a stranger although you weren't gone for all that long
Even though the bad things happened and all the felt was hatred I was the one who still hung on
Clinging to a dream I didn't think possible,
I knew wouldn't come true

I threw it all away,gave in and felt the hatred too
She became my friend and guided me along leading me away from you
when out of the blue my impossible dream came true
I turned to my ally only seeing she sided with you
Now I'm all that's left and I don't know what to do

Do I stand my ground or do I side with you?
I don't want to think,I don't want to move
so instead I'll sit here and stay confused.
