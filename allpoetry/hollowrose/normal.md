---
title: "Normal"
writer: "HollowRose"
slug: "normal"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Tell me that you're different
There's no one else like you
A diamond in the rough you say
Yeah right,that'll be the day

Truth is you're just another creation
Made from that same old perfect mold
Like a bomb about to explode
Hatred inside with nowhere to go

An unearthly scream this anger's now rage
I feel like an animal,I'm trapped in a cage
Someone help me please I don't know what to do
I just wanna be normal,normal like you.
