---
title: "Betrayal"
writer: "HollowRose"
slug: "betrayal"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Whispered words not meant to hear
Secrets revealed a trust betrayed
Told in secret not meant to be revealed
Another life I live full of hatred and despise

No one to trust,anger kept inside
Screaming on the inside
Crying on the outside
Thought you were a friend,realize you lied

Repeated everything I said to ears not meant to hear
Betrayed my trust,two faced and deceitful
Get out of my face
Get out of my life

No reason to know you
You stabbed me in the back
Nothing to me now
All the feelings of hatred I can no longer hide

My chance will come
I'll be on top
You'll have the face full of dirt
And the knife in your back
