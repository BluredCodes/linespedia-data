---
title: "Too Late"
writer: "HollowRose"
slug: "too-late"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Didn't wake up soon enough
Didn't realize it in time
All the things that I did wrong
Now you're lost to me
Swept away with the tide

Too selfish and too blind to see
The things I put you through
Wishing I could take it back
Make it all like it used to be

You've left and gone forever
Left me in the cold where I deserve to be
Not worthy enough of living
but also not worthy of dying

All alone to realize my mistakes
and to hope one day you might return,
but now all I have is my sorrow
and a single memory of you
