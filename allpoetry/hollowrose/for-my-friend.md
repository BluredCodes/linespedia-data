---
title: "For my friend"
writer: "HollowRose"
slug: "for-my-friend"
source: "AllPoetry"

Thinking of you brings a smile to my face
Makes me remember our first embrace
Something inside begged you not to go
But how much of my feelings do I show?

Cautious to bear a secret held deep inside
Here goes nothing my soul's open wide
The answer I got I didn't expect 
while you keep me guessing what will be next

Kindness that comes from deep within
The emotions we share,I feel like a twin
We relate on a level that only we share
Your warm brown eyes that show me you care

These feelings I feel seem all brand new
Glad to be sharing them with someone like you
Our future lies undetermined ahead so for now
 I will sleep with dreams of you in my head.
