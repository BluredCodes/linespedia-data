---
title: "Going Down"
writer: "HollowRose"
slug: "going-down"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/21526-Going-Down-by-HollowRose"
---

Lying,trying,coniving bitch
Hope you die,hope someone flips the switch
On to off as easy as that
Won't bother me anymore
No more of your bullshit

Older than me but acting much younger
Get over it its over between us
You threw it all away
All away the day that you betrayed

Stabbed me in the back left me for dead
Didn't even look back
Realize now how much you didn't care
Your words were lies
Your comfort empty promises

Never cared you used me as a pawn
A rung on the ladder to get higher up
Not anymore its time to come down
Down off your hill of lies
Time to wake up and realize

I can't be pushed down
You can't stop me now
You're going down
