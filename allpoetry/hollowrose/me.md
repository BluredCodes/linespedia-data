title: "Me"
writer: "HollowRose"

Mournful unyielding stare
Painted on her white stone face
A statue absent of all emotion
An angel fallen from grace

Watches as the world goes by
and she still remains unseen
Silent words she often whispers
and no one knows what they mean

She grieves forever silently
Over all that she has lost
Determined to feel whole again
No matter what the cost

Her silent cries are never heard
A tear rolls down her cheek
Her identity no longer a mystery
This girl of stone is me.
