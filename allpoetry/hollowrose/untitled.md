A glimpse into my soul
so sad you'll never know
what goes on inside my head
or what I think about as I lay in bed

Vacant eyes lost deep in thought
a blank and empty face
the window to my tormented soul
a mind forever caught

Emotions torn between good and bad
I don't know what to do
too scared to reach for help
afraid to release the truth

Helpless and confused
tears of sorrow that I shed
nothing left worth living for
my heart's already dead
