---
title: "Deep Inside"
writer: "HollowRose"
slug: "deep-inside"
source: "AllPoetry"

Crying on the inside
Smiling on the outside
Feeling strangely sad
Yet looking genuinely happy

Masses of confusion
These feelings I keep inside
Too afraid to show how I feel
I'm scared and so confused

I don't know what to say
I don't know what to do

For now I'll keep on acting
Pretending all that I am not
Until the day will come to be
When I'll show you the real me
