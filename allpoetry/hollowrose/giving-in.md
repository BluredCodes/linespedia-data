---
title: "Giving In"
writer: "HollowRose"
slug: "giving-in"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/42744-Giving-In-by-HollowRose"
---

Light fades into darkness
changes all that's bright into blackest black
emotions stripped completely clean
now everything's all the same
no more joy to enter my life
my happiness slips away

Tears of sorrow become a normal thing
self-inflicted scars,the band-aid for my pain
loneliness my only friend
my happiness has gone away

Searching for that certain something
to change the black back into white
the one thing that will bring my emotions back
and send joy into my life

My band-aid scars multiply by the day
no longer can I hold it back
Giving in to this consuming pain
and now my battle's lost
The blackness I fought now gathers me in,
and takes away my life
