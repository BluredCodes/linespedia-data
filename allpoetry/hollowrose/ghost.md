---
title: "Ghost"
writer: "HollowRose"
slug: "ghost"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

A whisper in the dark
more words go by unheard
I speak yet no one listens
No one hears my cries
of suffering,pain,and agony
would you honestly care if I died?

A ghost is all I am
A ghost is all I've become
forgotten and discarded
Tossed aside and left for dead
Left to deal with the pain inside
A silent being that walks these halls

Sad eyes watching all the faces
Realize I'm invisible to everyone I knew
Anyone that cared has gone and walked away
Silent screams escape my lips as the razor sinks farther in
My blood and I become one on the floor
And I realize I was right,you wouldn't realize if I died.
