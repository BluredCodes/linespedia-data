---
title: "For You"
writer: "HollowRose"
slug: "for-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Stuck inside a place
filled with shadows,in the dark
right and wrong pull at each side
and pain consumes my heart

Ever present sorrow
the tears roll down my face
Thoughts of all that I've done wrong
now an angel fallen from grace

Wish that I could take it back
make it right with those I've hurt
Inflict their pain upon myself
a scar for every face

Unable to give a reason why
for all the things I did
Just know that I am sorry
and I'll understand if you can't forgive.
