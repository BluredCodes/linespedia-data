---
title: "Stand still"
writer: "HollowRose"
slug: "stand-still"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Sitting here deep in thought
My mind it seems as if its caught
Between a place of light and dark
I'm all alone thoughts left to wander

Repetitive life now getting boring
Without me the world would go on turning
Stuck in a trap I just can't get out of
My minds dark depths simply swallow me whole

With no where to go and no one to turn to
I pick up the knife, just what will they do?
Nothing as usual I'll just join the ranks
Of all those before me whose faces were blank

Left to paint a picture they couldn't see
I almost wish it didn't happen to me
Not knowing where I'm going or where I'll end up
What they want me to be I just can't fulfill
My destiny lies when my heart stands still
