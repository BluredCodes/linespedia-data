---
title: "Trapped"
writer: "HollowRose"
slug: "trapped"
source: "AllPoetry"

Buried deep insidea dark cataclysm of my mindMorbid thoughts swirl aboutprepared to consume me wholeDrowning in my own emotionsMy live I've left behindForgetting how to feeland even how to interactMyself has become my only friend,the only one capableto understand and comprehend,what goes on inside my headMy identity long forgottenmy body merely tossed asideby those who determined I simply couldn't surviveMaybe they're right and maybe they're wrongbut how are they to knowwhen they can't begin to understand?
