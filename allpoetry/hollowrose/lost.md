title: "Lost"
writer: "HollowRose"
slug: "lost"

Thinking thoughts of yesterday
Painfully remembering the day you went away
Wishing and hoping you soon would return
Without you here my soul is left to burn

The nights I sat in the corner and cried
All the while slowly killing myself inside
Wanting so much just to go on and die
Knowing you wouldn't want me to,the least I can do is try

Try not to go and reach for the knife
The only thing now that brings joy to my life
I fear one day I'll think of you and give in
To the freshly sharpened blade as it slices through my welcoming skin

I don't know what to do to stop the sorrow and make me whole again
Can't go on living with all this pain,so I guess it means I'm giving in
Letting it go as my blood hits the floor
My world turns to black,my heart beats no more.
