title: "Your Sunshine Love"

What are my chances for your love? What do you need to stay?
What would it take? What must I do? What must I say?
Must I roll the dice with Lady Luck?
And throw all my cards to the wind, spend my last buck?
Should I wish upon a four-leafed clover?
Or walk through an orange colored poppy field, dream over and over.
Darling, what are my chances, to sit in the sunshine of your love?
