---
title: "I know who I am"
writer: "willowsight"
slug: "i-know-who-i-am"
source: "AllPoetry"

Eyes that see
what others can't,
The goddess sings to my heart
her beguiling and loving chant.

The goddess within
brings out my soul,
Blessed Be for her
for my life seemed as black as coal.

The goddess bathed in moonlight
harkoning to her magic call,
Powerful and all knowing
in her shadow I feel so small.

She brings my lonliness and desperate soul
the essence of her being,
To a glimmering harmony that lights my way
as shows me my future for my wellbeing.

She lets me see that she'll be there
each dark and tattered day,
to discard away all my anger
and throw my fears away.

She envelopes me in her watchful eye
like a mother cares for her child,
As moonbeams shoot through my soul
I know my life has been restyled.

So to her and Jesus I know
that my life can be sublime,
If I enjoy each blessed day
and simply take my time.

BLESSED BE/AMENI hope that people like this poem because it is very special to me because of what I have been through in my life. I owe everything I have to Jesus and everything I am as a person to the Goddess.
