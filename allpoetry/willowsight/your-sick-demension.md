title: "Your sick Demension"

Cold hands 
sick mind,
my thoughts 
do find.

Your desperate and twisted
and you dont care who you hurt,
your hands double fisted
as you tear my shirt.

Black velvet
surrounds my heart,
tears and sorrow 
no love from the start.

Decieving,screaming
hurtful lies,
A man of faces
I know your disguise.

Once love,now hate
the drugs posess you,
how did pain 
become my fate.

Will this torture never end
who are you?
I loved you
I thought we were friends.

Tissues crumpled from tears of fear
I thought we would last,
the horror I think when you are near
you were different in the past.

You don't give a dam about our girl
so i'm leaving and you'll regret,
all my crying,begging and bruises
and i'll get revenge some day-that you can bet.
