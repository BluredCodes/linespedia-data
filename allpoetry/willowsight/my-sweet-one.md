title: "My Sweet One"
writer: "willowsight"

Inside me you grow
I feel you there,
Your going to heaven
that i'm aware.

I love you
this is so unfair,
the pain and the loniness
it's more than I can bear.

I know god needs you
thats why you must go,
i'm so sorry you were sick
and I love you so.

I dream of holding you
in my arms tight,
but instead its a nightmare
I get each night.

I pray for the day 
that i'm with Jesus and you,
to know what would've been
pink or blue.

I pray that my tears
will be erased with delight,
when I leave this world
to you I reunite.

Willow
