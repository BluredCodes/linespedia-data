My little Hannah with eyes like a crystal lake
and hair like glimmering gold,
My flower child baby
that never intends to grow old.

A mouth made for talking 
and a book always in her hands,
writing in her journal
of faraway lands.

She's only eight
but acts like she's thirty,
she's always willing to clean up
when things get dirty.

She's the one that takes care of me
if ever i'm not feeling good,
I'm so proud that I have her
and I don't thank her enough like I should.

The treasures inside her 
full of sunshine and love,
Imagination and wonder
is what she has a lot of.

I know that her future will be very bright
and a great writer she'll be,
I wanted her to know that I love her
and this poems from me.

WillowThis is about my oldest and I want her to know that I love her and that I appreciate how she always helps me around the house, and her little sisters. I also think someday she'll be a famous writer, but whatever she is I love her very much.
