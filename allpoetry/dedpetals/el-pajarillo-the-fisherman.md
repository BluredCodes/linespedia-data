title: "el pajarillo & the fisherman"
writer: "dedpetals"

you should live underwater
if you're tired of all this rain
it doesn't rain inside the ocean
it won't rain on your head.

you should take advantage of this
i can't believe we can't walk
we can't talk
you should live underwater...

above ALL this ICE
lies a pond
are you trapped?
under ALL the ICE
underwater...blow bubbles...

you should take advantage of this
the fisherman will see you
el pajarillo will hear you
they will help you.

trapped UNDER the ICE
they're bound to break you out
if you're tired of all this rain
they'll break you out and let you...

BREATHE...
