GOD DAMNED!!!
hot damn
these people scare me!
flipping thru my channels
i stopped on your "show"

shes a Christian woman
flashy earrings, tacky gaudy gold
big ass hair, shiny alligator boots
bejeweled dresses, plastic face-lift
is that what "my maker" is made of???

she has tears in her eyes
am i supposed to feel sorry?
they all ORGY in their ways,
hairpieces, glitz, glamour
and u say jesus is weeping 4 me??

i'll send as mch as i can to u...
how about thousands??
how about millions???
anything to see old ladies 
with bows in their hair
tits doubly-done
makeup caked

and we're talking God & Jesus
& Bible & Heaven
& Hell & Satan
& Peter & Paul
& Mark & Luke & John!!!! AUGGH!!!

STAGE SHOW u pay to get in
you'll never ever get out
false idols
covered receding hairlines
tan from a bottled pill
is this what God wants?

AHHHHH UTOPIA!!!???
freakdafied circus side-show!!!???
heaven???
hell...whadda ya think...no offense to any one christian, i just came across one of those PTL jim and tammy tv evanelistical thangs one night and got pissed lol...
