title: "the boy next door"

the thin wiry legs of 
the boy next door
have made made you want to reach out
and touch fire.
the fire burns deep, the fire burns ever-
lasting
in all worlds and words
that fire is thee fire of the boy's protection.

one leg for stability, the other for attraction!does it flow?
