some people
are only taken away from their own minds
in their car.
so they drive unaware to pedestrians and crosswalks and red lights and yield signs.
singing away gleefully, not noticing the tall buildings surrounding them.
they don't notice the trash on the sides of the road, will he slow down just for one moment while i carefully fasten my seatbelt?

some people
become so manifested in what they are doing "NOW", that their lives become so totally wrapped up in that moment, that they can not see out of the corner of their left eye nor "right" eye those tall buildings surrounding them, or all that trash on the sides of the road.

some people
have become quite blurry...dontcha think so?
guess i just won't ever know?
