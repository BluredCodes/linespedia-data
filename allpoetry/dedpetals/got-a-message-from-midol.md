a merry merry message from ms. Midol
she's been hammering away
boyfriend knocks with a deficeincy

about, above, across, after, against, among, around, at...

such chritian beliefs
she's been bathing in her own dirty water
NICE girl, travelin' up that hill
Lily in a pond, a pool of her own...
kind, kindness

mama said "don't make no mess, don't take any shit Tess!!!!"
when will she love herself as much as i do? when will she SHOVE herself down that hill?

merry message from Midol
she's honey & homespun
boyfriends in the back and racks her

before, behind, beside, between, beyond, by, down and during...

jesus never came though
she climbs into her own dirty water
NICE girl
trekking through that (burning) bush
Lilys in a puddle, a puddle of her own...
sin, sinner

mama said "don't take no mess, don't make any shit Tess!!!!"
when will you love yourself as much as i do? when will you SHOVE yourself down that hill?
