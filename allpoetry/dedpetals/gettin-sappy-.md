are we sure we even exist?
or...is this just a mind-less situation
undoubtedly real
i touch u
i feel u
like u r real, marshmallow feeling(s):)
i could not make this up!
it's 2 wonderful...
ancient in my league "i know"
skin and the feeling of touch
senses r never made-up
sooooo wonderful i heard your voice
i couldn't make this up
it's 2 wanderlust
my ears i felt 2 make sure each was there
they felt like clouds
undoubtedly i touched them, i felt them
like they r real, airy feeling (my oh my)
i couldn't make this up!
it's all 2 wonderful...oh
ancient in my league "i know"
skin and hearing your voice
senses r never made-up
