BOY, is your toilet reallllyyy inviting!
BOY, is your toilet really inviting (me in):)

Loud-Mouthed Juliet
screaming in your ear
she has quite a BIG mouth that Juliet dear
she wouldn't let u go 4 a second
wouldn't let u go 4 a second...

BOY, your toilet is realllyyy an inviation!
BOY, your toilet is inviting me in (really)!!!

But...what if i came 
and took you
in my arms?
would you be able to resist my feminime charms?
and what if i came
& whisked you away?
would you just call it "any other day"?

Loud-Mouthed Juliet
shes screaming in your ear
shes got a large hole
that Juliet dear:)

just open up 
shes got shaky hands

shaky hands...
can you handle her FOREVER???
