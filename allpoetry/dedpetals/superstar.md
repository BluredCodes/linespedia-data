red dye in your hair
twinkle twinkle
little stars in your eyes
you must be a superstar girl!

where have all your legends gone?
they've gown down girl...
gone down girl...go down girl once more...

Nellie girl
with snakes in your hands
backwoods Maine has brought you
thus far...far away from your "homeland"
you don't look the type
go and change!

twiggy legs, scars and bruises
Girl has a way with the animals!
have YOUR way with the animals!!!!

twinkle twinkle
scratch those starry eyes
think "superstar" girl!!
