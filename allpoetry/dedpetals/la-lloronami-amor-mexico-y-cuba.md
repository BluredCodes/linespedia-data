thick, plump cubans and mexicans
have touched my lips...

as caressing as they are
why does there have to be an abandonment?

i'm converting catholic figurines
in my bedroom
conversating with the saints
ghost just passing thru
they can be moody...
and they're including me
in ALL their plans...

as i pray to Santa Maria
as caressing as she is
my eyes twinkle and
tear
she has to leave

why does there have to be an abandonment?
im moseying along
in this headroom
walking, writing on...writing on
ghosts from Guadelajara and "La Llorena"
just passing thru
mauling & including me in,
in ALL their plans...what do y'all think im feeling in this??? let me know id be nterested to hear your thoughts...:)
thanks,
dedpetals
