SHE
a little liar
pleased & plotted
her way into my ocean
5 leagues under-
stands on her head
upright she is
to passerbys
shivverriinng SHE is
in her own Atlantis
frozen & fallen
shadowy & sodden
SHES hung brightly
for all to see

touch her dress
feel her face

quite remarkable 
SHE is 
one little liar...
