Ol’ school brother in the house, since the birth of Christ
Chimney to chimney but I’m afraid of heights
I am getting pretty old want my retirement plan
Money ain’t a thing but still I can’t pay Uncle Sam
Only decent job I get and it be at the mall
Little homeys on my lap, lined from wall to wall
Pukin’ cryin’ cheatin’ lyin’ greedy SOB’s
If it ain’t little toy trains they mess with the Barbies
East-West everybody wants the presents from the Clause
But I ain’t givin’ noone jack and that is just because

Hook: All you people y’all make me sick
I am tired of your whole clique
If my laws you try to attack
You’ll find you’re hunted by the pack
If my sleigh you will not abide
You will answer to the --------

All the ladies want some from the big guy from the top
I love to say I’m not a player I just crush a lot
One time, weeki-weeki and I always travel far
But I can’t wait until I get the new company car
No disses to my reindeer boys they is a bunch of jets
But I would rather find myself behind a new Corvette
Da milk and cookies as my pay it’s such a load of crap
Dessert for breakfast, lunch and snack, I ask whaddup wit’ that
I service everyone on Earth I’m here to save the day
Even McGwire/Sosa hittin’ bombs from here to Monterrey

Hook

All you people y’all make me sick... Eternally
Watchin’ my lady Mrs. Clause up in her bikini
Why she ain’t mad at me is still a mystery to me
Look where we livin, (Where I’m chillin’) place is as cold as can be
Tol’ me to be a doctor we could have a family
I said, “No, I’ll be immortal” I think now what’s wrong with me
She still loves me though I think cuz she’s still sleeping in with me
But our bed’s been the sofabed since 1963
I digress anyone else they woulda thrown me in the street
Only way she’s getting paid is million mailmen that carry
Million letters with “Dear Santa” written very carefully
By million kids who want my gifts they behavin’ so beautifully
In the morning they will find ‘em and be shouting out with glee
It be one more night in the life of my gift giving partyDue to the obsolete nature of the chorus, it will now remain unfinished until I can come up with something better...

The Great Immortal Zeus
