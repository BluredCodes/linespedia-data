With my thunderbolt skills, I’m the nicest ever
Tonight’s endeavor can prove that I’m just twice as clever
As the next guy, his mom, his dad, or girlfriend
Good in the ring, but on the Mic’s where your world ends
Cuz it’ll take a sec for us to sign the truce
I never fake a threat and you’ll regret your rhyme with Zeus
I’m undefeated, invincible mouth of pure steel
So advanced in my battle your loss is a sure deal
So take a few moments to erase all that
Before I get to swinging crazy with this baseball bat
I’ll make your face fall flat, and while I chase mallrats
I’ll pick up seven phones in Harlem and have Mase call back
You could dis me in June, but I’ll always remember
My temper’ll spark before we reach the end of September
And that’ll forever be known as your cripple day
When the immortal makes his lyrical triple play
