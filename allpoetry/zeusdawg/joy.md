I’m not a player but I love the girls
Not an egomaniac but I’m above the world
Sky’s the limit, no point in being stuck in today
Just reach high over the sky, up, up, and away
Think of flowers and grass while your cape flaps in the wind
And let the happiness that you create happen again
Trapped in a whim, on a high with no substance attached
Full color film in this realm with vivid prints to match
Sitting bare on rocking chair to relay on the days
When simple things made happiness from the way you were raised
And pray for the grays to fade from the top of your head
Delaying decay that spreads so it stops at your bed
Heaven on earth Eden reopened jazz in the sky
The death of cynicism time for folks to catch in the rye
And all the while Mt. Olympus opens up once more
So that the world’s ambrosia and nectar drink comes forth
