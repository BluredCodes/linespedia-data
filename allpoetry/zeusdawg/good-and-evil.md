Deep in the realms of Time and Space
Where Void was in evident reign
The Beginning flashed...

A Year in a second
A Millenium in a minute

Void had the calm, euphoric thoughts of Good


Good doubted it's King, Void


Doubt, turned to Evil


That is the beginning of Doubtful Good, and Confident Evil
