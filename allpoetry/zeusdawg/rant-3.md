Who are you to tell me what to do?
You can rule my work but not my life
Everything you do to everyone else
Is fine until they do it back to you

You can't promise that you'll help someone
While trying to destroy them on the side
You build a lavish castle with your hands
And underneath destroy it with your feet

What goes around tends to come around
No matter which way you try to escape
You need to treat all people with respect
If you want them to treat you just the same

One day you'll see your kingdom all across
And realize the citizens are gone
And then you'll see your evil has destroyed
All the things you thought you had worked for

BUT IT'LL BE TOO LATE
