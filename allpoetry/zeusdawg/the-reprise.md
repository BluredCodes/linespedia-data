Late last night you told me
I was your one and only
I went and kissed you slowly
That makes your skin a-glow
Three days ago was going too fast
Our love and problems grew past
You left how could you do that
Wanted to talk to you
Last week the drama with the phone call
I watched a wall of stone fall
My cries and your new moans crawled
And I felt so alone
All month you took the game and played it
"I love you's" that were stated
The meanings that were faded
Which brings me to today

Chorus:
It's very strange, I know
For me to dwell on ago
But this time I don't think it is that hard
So my dear I'll depart
From matters of the heart

Bridge 1:
And I hope my feelings carry me
My dear so when you marry me
Or else I don't think we'll get very far

Now I'm hearing this for too long
Say I'm leading you to do wrong
Inside the hurt is too strong
Cuz since I married you
On a warpath is where you're heading
Our end this trend is setting
So I'm never forgetting
You leaving me for dead
Each time brings lies and explanations
For outside fornications
You were my inspiration
Now you inspire pain
Some day you'll ask how could you do it
Our break up will be fluid
I'm sad for going through it
Got nothing else to do

Chorus

Bridge 2:
Happiness is what I strive to gain
So I hope that I'll survive this pain
Cuz right now it's just tearing me apart

I'm sorry that I missed your birthday
I miss you in the worst way
No matter what the hurt stays
Allowing me to curse
The day we married way to quickly
Forever came and hit me
I'll bet the fates just tricked me
The bait was never switched
See you're the best thing that could find me
These other girls remind me
I can't treat you unkindly
You're always on my mind
Please realize this ain't cemented
My heart is still quite dented
But love is unrelented
I'll see this to the end

Chorus
