Waiting
He sits there
While forever pacing
Smiling at destruction
Laughing at despair
Gleeful at the extinction of mankind
As old as time itself
Fallen from the stars
Right hand of evil itself
Waiting
He sits there
For the day when he shall be set free
And burn the villages
He sees himself stomping
Laughing
Soon he will be released
And death will come
Until then he must remain
Sitting there
