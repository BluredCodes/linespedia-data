I know a girl with hair as soft as silk strands
And eyes so blue they’ll make the sky feel guilt and
One look’ll make the crazy postal guy a milkman
This spilt can of worms, demands to be heard
She’s amazing, determined, and I feel for certain
That she’s special, I’m curtains, brain’s no longer workin’
She’s got me, crazed not knowing what I’ll say next
Blank looks at the grocery store buying playtex
Lord knows that I’ve just fallen, I’m so far gone
That it wouldn’t be shocking if the world was playing our song
My senses just went out the door, vamoose
Cuz the great Zeus Dawg just became the dog, Zeus
But what can I say, if I’m to lose my brain and my senses
Stain up my lenses with rain from the trenches
Of the war of my heart, I might as well just sing
