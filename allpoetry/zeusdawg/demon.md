I used to hit people
I couldn't help it
There was something inside me that wanted pain

I'd just see somebody
And start to swing away
And make is so their blood flowed away like rain

Until I came to a Eureka!
I didn't have to do it
I'd just ignore all the stupidity

But

Recently I've had feelings
For a long time now
People provoke me, "Are you kidding me?"

Why are you stupid?  You know I'll kill you!
I just can't help it! I warned you! 
And now you're in for the ride of your life!

No... stop Jeff... but I can't
I've already started swinging
And the demon wins the fight for my strife
