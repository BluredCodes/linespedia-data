---
title: "Humans"
writer: "zeusdawg"
slug: "humans"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I hate the way they smell
I can sense it from miles away
Makes me nauseous

I despise their selfishness
They have no sense of duty
I don't understand them

I detest their love for hate
I was created for war
And I don't like it

I envy their free will
They do whatever they want
I'm bound to duty

They make me so angry
Sometimes...
I want to kill them all
But sometimes...
Secretly...
I wish I was one of them
