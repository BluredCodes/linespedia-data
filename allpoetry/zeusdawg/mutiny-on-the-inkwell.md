title: "Mutiny on the Inkwell"

Easy pen, come on I don't know why you don't write
Anything clever for me it's just paper it won't bite
I'll get you the finest inks from Budapest
Come on write the first word, my brain'll do the rest
At least I hope so, right brain? You'll find the will
To give me good rhymes and things while I have time to kill
Maybe the pencil should cooperate a little better
Where'd I put it?  Please just give me one little letter
I'll fill the rest of the word out, for real, I promise
Nothing on this planet will keep me from being honest
Damn, no response, maybe a song and a C chord
But my music book's full and I can't plug in my keyboard
I used to be able to write about some beautiful lips
That made me shake and bite my nails to my cuticle tips
But now I can't string two words together my largest threats
Are writer's block and ADD when they discard this vet
