Workin’ on my lyrics to catch my dream tour
My verbal broom’s gonna leave a clean floor
The immortal on his way to make what you’ve seen sore
While avoiding three Buds and about fifteen Coors
Paint a green door and make the door seen
Count to fourteen and wipe with chlorine
Take three hits of valium and morphine
Talk about whatever make it clever
I figure I can rap about gats, AK-47’s
Nine millies, bazookas, Tens and Mack Elevens
But then I can’t tell you ‘bout what made my hat new
Or the ten cans of paint that made my flat blue
And concerts, I’m catching late flights
Stopping hate riots, and keeping late nights (ha ha)
Now face right, walk, bend down, take a breather
Turn around, run, jump in my two-seater
Ha! Get out of the way of the side mirror scanner
Keep hands and feet inside, mind your manners
Watch out now, we gon’ be doin’ one-forty
Down the freeway, cuttin’ off trucks, scarin’ shorties
Wearin’ sporty threads, in Fords and Benz
The roar defends and it leaves you short of friends
So watch out before they say you’re loose in bed
Pay attention, you better get with Zeus instead

Better stay tuned for rule number one
Don’t make fun of the guy with the gun
Cuz he ain’t about fun and he’ll go nuts
You missing for a week’ll cause a big fuss
No ifs, ands, or buts, this always rings true
You can’t figure out which girl to sing to
So keep a log book, don’t move the wrong rook
Checkmate by Scooby-Doo makes a dog hook
But when the smog’s shook you’ll have your clean air
Little jog?  Look! That guy has no hair!
Cuz I’m a man-myth-legend type pal
Got a Sprite can with seventy five cals
Drink and my thirst is gone but don’t blink
Sugar hyper Zeus’ll turn your skin pink
My verses make other cats think
“Why he stay afloat when everybody else sinks?”
Cuz I have tight flows and even the mic knows
My rhymes got more energy than a light show
Don’t try to insult my dissipate sound
Open your ears, and you’ll get hit with the breakdown:

Dome brights, long fights and flashlights
Foghorns, creamed corn, and dog bites
Trust in you and, reinstate, set your fame
Farfignewton, commensurate, picture frame
Not the same, little house with eight trees
Bringing back, rentals with late fees
Owing debts, they’ll make your knees pop
While you’re screaming out to please stop
And houseworkers, they make the mop squirt
Like I said, clean floors and not dirt
With beach girls taking their shirts down
Lewd conduct, makes Ernie and Bert frown
But the King, he was a hound dog
While Teddie whispered and owned a sound log
The people who wanted peace wrote a lock truce
The same people that named this Dawg Zeus
