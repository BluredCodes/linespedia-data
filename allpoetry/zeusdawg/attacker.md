What am I? Man or a beast?
Machine or primitive? Ready for most but with plans for the least
Loose lips cause everytime Zeus flips
Cuts up your squad watching as red juice drips
Thunderbolt slicing the air like a ragin’ saw
Electrocuting everyone that breaks Meaghan’s law
Burn and roast like black toast from my wicked skill
Never cauterize the wound and watch the liquid spill
To mix with pills and start up my electric drill
A missed hit will prolong the length of this quick kill
My list splits till I lay your former district still
And then the missiles milk the earth of these ballistic hills
Five minutes back you said I lost my mind
Until the beat was laced with all my verbal cyanide
Now try and hide pass the time to find yourself luck less
