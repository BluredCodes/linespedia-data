I made myself crazy but the world made me snap
Uber human, twice as strong and four times braniac
Physicist extraordinaire, strategist with skills to spare
No attention span, dyslexic and still aware
Enough to win a game or two of chess while shooting the breeze
And read a book on medicine to find the roots of disease
My knowledge, power and talent are all worthless to you
Unless you can stop me from killing cutting my stress in two
And free my brain from stupidity that always surrounds it
Making me finish humanity ‘fore my days are counted
I now inject my soul to wherever I flow
A saturate my conversation with whatever I know
I try to seep some knowledge out there so one-eighty I.Q.’s
Can find their way into the brain of some lady like you
And then perhaps I’ll calm myself and not chill to the bone
Cuz then I’ll be able to breathe and not feel so alone
