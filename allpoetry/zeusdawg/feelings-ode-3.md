Fell in love with her eyes, and ignored my insides
She began to spin lies that cut me like twin sais
Brain on spin cycle already been tried
Convicted and thin sliced, hung to bring mice
Tried to save my broke heart but fat ladies sing twice
Made a joke start from standing on thin ice
A charming guy dedicated to romance
In a world of pure snakes where lovers have no chance
She preferred getting played to having a slow dance
I slipped even though I grabbed it with both hands
And wanted no part of "I didn't know" chants
Found the truth with indi-glo lamps, skipped the program
Gave up on all the games, stopped listening when she would call me names
And took her off my list of holidays
This immortal's worthy of this truth
Cuz you should be fortunate just to love this Zeus

She was a girl with hair as soft as silk strands
And eyes so blue they made the sky feel guilt and
One look made the crazy postal guy a milkman
This spilt can of worms, demands to be heard
I brought her to my stress spot and held her near
I was trembling intensely when I felt her fear
Connected to mine these emotions were truly unique
I better act now cuz I'll soon be too weak
Looked in her eyes again felt the tremble skyrocket
The burn started coming out of my eye socket
Till I pulled her close and made my lips touch hers
My heart spilled so much blood that the Crips just purred
I lay there in all my mental rhapsody
Glad to be, a part of gentle blasphemy
She kissed me back I put my arms around her head
And thanked the stars, that I had finally found a friend

Looking back, I'm too used to people changing their minds
Leaving broken-hearted Zeus Dawg saying good-bye
Praying the sky would pity this lost adonis
Sitting on number two like a foster mom is
Out of sight, out of mind, out of bed
Ready to face the world like a blind powder keg
Flying doubt is dead, the heart resurrecting
All competitors better start genuflecting
I'm definitely in a Boyz II Men mood
What better to expand your choice of venue
The more you ask for the louder the spray
Of gifts and lifts for twenty four hours a day
And many more flowers'll stay on the right track
It's time for this nice guy to fight back
Ain't nobody ever gonna treat him like that
Zeus'll turn the next one into a midnight snack
