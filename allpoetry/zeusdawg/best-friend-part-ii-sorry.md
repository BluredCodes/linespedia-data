---
title: "Best Friend Part II: Sorry"
writer: "zeusdawg"

Sorry
For not seeing who you were
Because I see now who you are
And feel foolish for having hated you

Sorry
For not understanding your motives
And following my childish ways
I know now I was ignorant

Sorry
If I ever caused you harm
Even a little bit
It was unnecessary

Sorry
If I ever hurt you
Even though I may have meant to then
I now know I was only hurting myself

Sorry
I don't know how to make amends
But I will repay you someday
And carry this burden forever

Sorry
