Those words still ring inside my head 
As I'm writing this from within 
This brain I call my home 

Relationships are weird, she said 
I'm just biding my time with him 
It sucks to be alone 

I didn't think much of it then 
She found me and loved what she'd known 
To be her true escape 

But she went back to him again 
I guess It Sucks To Be Alone 
Would truly seal my fate 

The coming days my heart would roam 
As she loved him who she despised 
And left me here to wilt 

I loathe IT SUCKS TO BE ALONE 
It places fear and pain and lies 
Where your heart love once built
