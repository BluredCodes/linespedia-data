The Haiku:

Planet full of snakes
And backstabbers everywhere
Need eyes in my back

Fighting depression
Falling faster to the floor
Need a strong shoulder

Immortality
Disconnected from the world
Someone throws a line

Thanks to my partner
Life is easier to live
Thanks for all your helpJust a little something for one half of the greatest duo in history...
