I wasn't put here to bow to humans
I was created to live your life perfectly and show you morons how to do it
You morons think life is so painful to live
Try doing it forever skipping death and straight to the crib
I don't complain about my job or my eternal fate
Surrounded by idiots that can't see or learn to wait
I ain't complaining anymore so I'm just taking what's mine
I don't care anymore so I'm creating a line
Between your world and my existence, my closet hell
I'll make it thick, so if you're dumb enough to cross it, well...
I’ll skip that little detail and let you figure it out, fools
The point is I’m taking my world back, with or without youA shorter version... my temper's been acting up lately
