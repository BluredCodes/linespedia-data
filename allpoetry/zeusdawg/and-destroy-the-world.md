title: "And destroy the world"
writer: "zeusdawg"

I can't bring a disk into the library
Because I might create a virus
And destroy the world

I can't walk around my old high school
Because I might disrupt a class
And destroy the world

I can't stay out very late
Because I might not sleep tonight
And destroy the world

I can't show up five minutes late
Because I might do something unproductive
And destroy the world

I can't be smart without a degree
Because I might prove you wrong
And destroy the world
