Did I
Lie about my true intentions
When I visited you and amplified my own dimensions?

Did I
Get on the bus instead of driving
Forget the directions to the contract that you were "signing"?

Did I
Forget to play the bad guy to your bad script
And not allow you to keep being a victim addict?

Or Did I
Just step on the scene at the wrong time
And refuse to wait on your ridiculous long line?

Maybe I
Took my heart away to quickly and locked it
Cuz I'm too big to fit in anybody's back pocket?

So Did I
Get to be too smart for your fun and games?
That's too bad cuz now I get to dance on what remainsArg.
