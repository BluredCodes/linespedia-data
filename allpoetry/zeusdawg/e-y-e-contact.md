Well, whether at the club or the first date
Pub or the birthday, caught in the worst way
Highly affected by this thing called a lead plate
Can't keep my eyes or my gaze on her head straight
Gazes wandering away from her face
To the tight stuff giving her such a nice touch
Check it, I try to tell her she's the best of the best
Not too convincing when I stare at her chest
Cause it's blessed with hypnosis, wall to wall
Those beach balls from a shirt a size to small
Fall, victim to the lure of her dance
Try to talk to her face but my mind's entranced
My head's weighed down, my eyes way down
My brain's now wond'rin' how I can lay down
And score, somewhere other than the dance floor
Whisper a few sweet nothings, watch my chance soar
But I notice her frown as she makes my hands sore
If I don't look at her face I know I can't score
