If you think I’m gonna fail, don’t give me the assignment
Any word you write on that page, I can rhyme it
I’ve got scholars from the universe reading my rhyme schemes
While I sit and sip OJ at my autograph signings
So you’re mad cuz I’m hyperactive
That’s the only thing that you can say when I’m distracted?
You better stop, cuz you’re staring at a colossal one-eighty I.Q.
That wouldn’t think twice before rolling through some lady like you
So stop and think before you reach and degrade my book
Jeff may be my given name, but Zeus the name I took
Is coming for you, while screaming an incredible laugh
That makes you sound crazy explaining to the medical staff
Let me say that thunderbolts ain’t no joke
And electricity goes from me to the hayters causin’ smoke
So I’ll work on my swaying, and my quick wit
So you can work hard on trying to catch up to it
