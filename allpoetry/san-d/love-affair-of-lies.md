"Love Affair Of Lies"

Why won't you leave? 
Why can't you see?
Our love can never be.
I have not the strength 
to say good bye,
to this love affair of lies.

I try to run,
I try to hide.
Try to bury these feelings, 
down deep inside.
But you keep coming back to me
You will not let me be.

You have someone
else at home.
Your heart, I do not own.
This is not a life for me.
Why won't you set me free?

You say you love me.
You say you care.
Then why am I
in such despair?
First your here, 
and then your there.
This love you give 
is so unfair.

I try and try
to break away.
But you come back
to my dismay.
I try so hard 
to hold my grounds.
But it's too hard 
when you come back around.

I have not the strength
to ease my pain.
Or the will to say good bye.
Can't stop this love 
that's deep inside.
Can't stop these tears I cry.

Why won't you leave?
Why can't you see? 
Our love can never be.
I have not the strength 
to say good bye,
to this love affair of lies.
