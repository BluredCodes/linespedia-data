"Just To....."

Just to wake up to tomorrow.
Feel the sun upon my face.
Feel the warmth that's in my heart.
Thank God! A brand new start.

Just to see the smiles 
on the faces of  those I love.
Hold them in my arms so tight.
A chance to make things right.

Just to smell the fragrance.
Sweet nectar in the air.
Smell all the gardens flowers,
in the month of April showers.

Just to hear the sparrows,
in the early morning hours.
To hear their precious melody, 
in their modus operandi.

Just to taste the sweetness, 
of your kiss upon my lips.
Taste the passions, 
of your perception.
Taste the ecstasy
of my submission.

Just to wake up to tomorrow.
Feel the sun upon my face.
Feel the warmth that's in my heart.
Thank God! A brand new start.

By: Sandy/San-d
