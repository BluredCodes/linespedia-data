'Old Sammy'

When I was just a toddler, 
say four or five years old.
We had a dog named Sammy 
who loved to watch us so.
A Collie mixed with Shep 
with a shiny coat of gold.
He loved to watch us as we played 
and he lived to watch us grow.

We never had to worry 
if a stray came near our yard.
He always kept an eye on us 
and always stayed on guard.
He never let a stranger 
come near us as we played.
And right within a few feet 
is where he'd always lay.

I never will forget Old Sammy,
when he looked into my eyes.
With love that sparkled from with in 
the day he passed and died.

It saddens me to think of him 
and all the love that Sammy showed.
For he lived his life to protect us........
and then he had to go......
