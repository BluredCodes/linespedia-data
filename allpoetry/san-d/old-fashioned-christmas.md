"OLd Fashioned Christmas"

An old fashioned Christmas
Is what lingers in my mind.
Colored Bulbs and tinsel
of every shape and kind.
A Christmas tree with an Angel
It's lights that sparkled bright.
And we waited up for Santa
We stayed up half the night.
Cookies on my night stand
A glass of milk to quench his thirst
We knew before he'd
stuff our stockings. 
He had to have this first.
We'd watch to see if Santa
Flew across the evening sky.
Would he forget about
our house and accidentally 
pass us by?
I'd see the snow flakes falling.
And a twinkling star so bright.
I knew it must be Santa's reindeer
On Christmas Eve at night.
And then I'd climb into my bed
and softly say my prayers.
My heart it pounded lightly.
I could hear it softly beat.
As I quietly drifted off to sleep.
As I prayed my sole to keep.
I'd wake before the
morning's light.
Creep down the hallways floor.
So quietly I'd sneak to see
the gifts up unde the tree.
The Christmas tree
was all a glow.
And presents stacked 
so high.
I'd sit there very quietly
And wait for morning's light
A good old fashioned Christmas
is bedded in my mind.
And memories that will last me
until the end of time.

By: Sandy/San-d
