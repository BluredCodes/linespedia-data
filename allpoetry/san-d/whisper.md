"Whisper"

Whisper to me the words I long to hear.
Tell me how you long to have me near.
Hold me tight and don't let go.
On a summers eve when the winds softly blow.

Lets drift away, go somewhere and hide.
Sail away with me on the oceans tide.
Soothe me with your tender touch.
Whisper you love me, oh so much.

Stroke your fingers through my hair.
Caress my body, for I don't care.
Send me shivers up and down my spine.
For my love for you is so divine.

Touch me in the depth of my soul.
Give to me your heart and make me whole.
Fill me with passion and uncontrollable desire.
For you are the only one my heart admires.

Let me feel your breath against my skin.
As the flesh of your body touches me again.
Take me to a place called ecstasy.
Whisper your love for me.

 By: Sandy/San-d
