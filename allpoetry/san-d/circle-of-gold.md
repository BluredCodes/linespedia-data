'Circle of Gold'

This circle of gold with one tiny stone.
Once shined like the stars in the sky.
But somewhere in time it has lost its glow.
Like the glow, I once saw in your eyes.

I took the circle of gold, off my finger today.
Releasing the bond that you held on me.
But this circle of gold entwined round my heart
has me chained to your memory.
