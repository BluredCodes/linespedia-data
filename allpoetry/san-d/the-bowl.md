"The Bowl"  

Why do they make these things so tall?
Why can't they see, I'm too small.
And then they wonder why I miss.
When on the floor is where I piss.

So I just aim and take a chance.
I dare not move, I keep my stance.
And if the bowl I do not hit.
It's just to late, I cannot quit!

By: Sandy/San-d
