"Angel Wings"

The years will pass but not without,
A heart that feels your essence.
And deep within this soul of mine,
Is where I'll feel your presence.

I couldn't wait to hold you. 
See this gift, God gave to me.
I waited so impatiently,
You filled my days with glee.

I loved you from the moment,
I first felt you in my womb.
I never knew we'd never meet.
For God, took you way too soon.

I didn't get to see your face, 
or caress you in my arms.
Or count your tiny fingers,
Or get to know your baby charms.

You must have been so perfect.
For God, to want you so......
He guided you to heaven.
It broke my heart, to let you go.

I miss you so and love you.
It saddens me so much.
To go on through life without you.
Miss the warmness of your touch.

I know that you're in heaven.
Far better off than I.
I know you have your angel wings.
In heaven above blue skies.

Remember that I love you. 
Save me a seat upon your pew. 
For one day we will meet again.
When my life on earth is through.
