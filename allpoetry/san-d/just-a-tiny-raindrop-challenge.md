"Just A Tiny Raindrop"

I'm just a tiny raindrop.
trying to find my way,
to the under nourished pansy,
that has lost its sweet array.

But on my way I landed, 
Ore the branch of hickory tree.

drip drip drip
down down down

Unto another tree branch,
of the big old hickory.

Staring down unto the ground
The pansy I could see.
It was I, the tiny raindrop,
that would tell its destiny.

And then a breeze hit from behind,
I had no other choice. So.....

drip drip drip 
down down down

But another branch got in my way.
Oh man, this just was not my day.

Then old Mr. Blue Jay,
perched himself near by.
He flapped his wings 
and spread to dry. So......
 
drip drip drip
down down down

He swooshed me off the side.


And yet another tree branch
ahead of me I see.
Wonder if I'll ever get, 
to the pansy that waits for me.

drip drip drip 
down down down

Ouch!, I finally hit the ground.


By: Sandy/San-d
