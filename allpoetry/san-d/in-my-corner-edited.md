'Snowed In'Night has descended the moon nestled behind thefacade of misty snow.White outside my windowmakes the dark an evening glow.In my corner with a firedancing in the room.I lay and watch the snow fallto the scent of burnt woods perfume.Snowed in by the crystal blanket already nestled on the ground.Heaps of mighty snow driftsin my sleeping garden and all abound.Trees that lost their covernow coated all in white.Crystals dangle from its limbssparkling through the night.Snowed in this wintry blisswith a wine glass of pink Chablis.Ease dropping on the echoesof the winds whisperingthrough the trees.Silence that succumbs mein the midst of a wintry storm.Snowed in, lying by my fire,in my corner, where I stay warm.By: Sandy/San-d
