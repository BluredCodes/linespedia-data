title: "Twilight Across the Horizon"

Twilight across the horizon,
where land and sea meets
skies of blue.
Captivated by the rays of
sunlight, 
with orange 
and yellow hues.

To the East a
distant city.
Seems so peaceful
from where I stand.
To the West, a sail boat
calmly drifting away 
from land.

Twilight across the horizon
Captivated by the rays of
sunlight, with orange 
and yellow hues.
