title: "Beauty and Grace"

She walks down the isle, holds her head up high.
Takes in a deep breath and lets out a sigh.
Memories of childhood dance through her mind.
For a moment she travels back through time.

With so much beauty and full of grace.
She looks like an angel standing there in her place.
She trembles a little, a tear falls from her face.
She gracefully walks and doesn't loose pace.

The sun through the pane shines directly on her.
She glistens like stardust as her eyes are transferred.
To the man that is waiting so handsome and tall.
Her seemliness beauty has him mesmerized in awe.
 
A golden ring on her right hand passed down from her Mother.
And now on the left he places yet another.
He pledges his love, "until death do us part."
He's mesmerized with this woman who has stolen his heart.

He stares in her eyes at the love that she shows.
He sees past her smile, the love deep in her soul.
With pride and honor she slips the ring in it's place.
He loves this woman filled with beauty and grace.

By: Sandy/San-dThis verse was written and dedicated to my daughter.
