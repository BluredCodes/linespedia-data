---
title: "Precious"
writer: "San d"
slug: "precious"

---

Precious is the child born,
The life with in ones soul.
The bond that holds us oh so close, 
is the warmth of love that flows.

Precious is the pride I hold
and I love them each within.
God, keep them all within your reach.
From me, your word is what I'll teach.

Precious are the feelings
that keeps them close to me.
Deep within this heart of mine
is where they'll always be.

Precious is my first born.
He taught me how to strive,
and gave to me my inner strength,
to fight to keep him safe.

Precious is my second born.
She brought back to me my faith.
For when life gave me so much grief.
The Lord gave her to me.

Precious is my third born.
A miracle in itself.
She gave to me the ability 
to reach for all my dreams.

Precious is my fourth born.
he taught me how to fight
for what the Lord had given me
and treasure it for life.

Precious is my last born.
God sent her unexpected.
She's the image of her Mother.
A blossum that's been perfected.

Precious are my children.
All gifts from God to me.
Left for me to guide them,
and then to send them back to thee.
