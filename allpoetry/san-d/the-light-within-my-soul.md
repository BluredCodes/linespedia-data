---
title: "The Light Within My Soul"
writer: "San d"
slug: "the-light-within-my-soul"
source: "AllPoetry"

You found the light within my soul.
You make me shine, you make me glow.
You touched my heart, like a summer's breeze.
You made my love, your destiny.

We've traveled many places.
Where only two can go.
In a wonderland of ecstasy.
Like river's we flow.

You take me in your arms again. 
And pull me close against your skin.
This warmth, this passion,it's gentle and pure.
For you my love,it is secure.

Your words of passion, softly whisper in my ear.
Oh, how much I need, and want you near.
You set my heart, my soul afire.
My love for you, a burning desire.

You found the light within my soul.
You make me shine.
You make me glow.
