Moonlight 

Staring through her window 
moonlight casts it's spell 
She's searching through the shadows 
of the Parkway Inn Hotel 

Illuminated by the moonlight 
a walk into the midst 
of hiding in the shadows 
escaping all the gist 

Lovers in the moonlight 
where their passions then unfold. 
But always in the light of day 
it is friendship that is showed. 

By: Sandy/San-d
