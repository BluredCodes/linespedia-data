We awaken to a new day 
that is filled with melancholy
Grasping onto what was once
a day of peaceful harmony

And in the midst of days to come 
our thoughts we must conceive
Our loved ones that will have to fight
against the blind that are deceived

Our minds are all in wonder
and our hearts are all in pain
How can so many follow
A man that seems insane.

So we live our life from day to day
we pray that we can cope
We turn to God for guidence
and cling to only Hope.

By: Sandy/San-dDedicated to all those who lost their lifes on 9/11
