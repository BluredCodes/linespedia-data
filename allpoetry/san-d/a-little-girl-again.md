"A Little Girl Again"

   Sometimes I wish I was a little girl again. 
To dream of princesses being carried off by 
the handsome prince. Or to wish upon the 
twinkling star and watch them glow so bright.
 
   Sometimes I wish I was a little girl again 
to see my wishes, hopes and prayers come true. 
With the twinkle of an eye or the flutter of a wand. 
To sit upon my mothers lap, feel the warmth within 
her heart that took all my fears away. To know that 
she'd be there to protect me forever. 

   Sometimes I wish I was a little girl again so 
I'd never had to feel the hurt one grows to know. 
When hearts are broken, dreams are shattered. 
When life isn't at all what you had dreamed.
 
   Sometimes I wish I was a little girl again, 
when scary things were only in bad dreams and 
would disappear by the warmth of someones touch. 
When tears would fade away just by the 
tender softness of a kiss by someone who cared.
 
   Sometimes I wish I were a little girl again 
to recall the feeling a child feels when she sees 
her first snow flake fall unto the ground, and 
to romp and play in the snow as if it were the 
last she'd ever see. 

   Sometimes I wish I were a little girl again, 
to never have to know the hardships that's in 
this world today, but to only know the love 
and beauty a child feels before she's grown
and her dreams are gone.
