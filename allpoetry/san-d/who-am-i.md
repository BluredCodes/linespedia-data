"Who Am I?"

Who am I ?
Not perfect by far
I know

A sinner 
from my first breath

living my days 
here on earth

fighting to redeem 
this soul

to smother 
the fire and brimstone

fighting the temptations
that keeps me from
being whole

Searching for the path
that will one day
lead me to my home.

Who am I ?
Not perfect
by far I know.
