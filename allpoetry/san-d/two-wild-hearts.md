"Two Wild Hearts"

The music is playing a little on the wild side.
Two wild hearts caught up in the motions.
His uncanny ways has her mesmerized.
As she's drawn towards his evil potions.

He moves to the rhythm of the devil's dance.
His lust is enveloping her style.
Ruby red lips, long shiny hair.
As their eyes meet in a glance.

Slender and tall she sways to the beat.
Her body a temple he's craving.
Two wild hearts beating out of control.
Leading where? On this road they are paving.

The music it softens and slows down the beat.
He moves cross the floor to her side.
With a smile that wins her she's lost her defeat.
As he leads she submits to his vibes.

Two wild hearts intertwined in the midst.
Of a night full of liqueur and wine.
When the night turns to day and they wake
to the light.
They will look at each other and sigh.
