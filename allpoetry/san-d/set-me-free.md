---
title: "\"Set me Free\""
writer: "San d"
slug: "set-me-free"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8106-Set-me-Free-by-San-d"
---

Let me close these weary eyes,
unleash these ties that bind.
Take away the pain I hide 
in the shadows of my mind.

Let me rest these weary bones
that have weakened through the years.
Free me from this bondage
that this body has had to bare.

Let me close my eyes tonight.
Let me sleep in harmony.
Take me to my mansion
and in heaven, set me free.
