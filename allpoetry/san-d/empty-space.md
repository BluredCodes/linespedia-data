Empty Space'

Eyes as blue as the heavens skies
Yet sparkle like stars through the night
You're lost some where in there,
in that empty space.
In the depth of your darkness.
Like a mad man that has no soul.

Will you ever return to reality?
Will I ever see life in your eyes?
Or will you just stay 
in that empty space
in the depth of your darkness?
Like a mad man that has lost his soul.

By: Sandy/San-d
