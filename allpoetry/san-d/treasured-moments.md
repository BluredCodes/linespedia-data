"Treasured Moments"

Treasured moments of 
childhood hugs.
Warm in her arms like a
bug in a rug.

Remembering her touch
and missing her much.
I treasure the moments
of childhood hugs.

The touch of her hands,
When bathing me clean.
The sparkle in her eyes,
Oh how they gleamed.

I remember the love 
In her eyes when she smiled.
I treasure the moments,
When I was a child.

The scent of her hair,
The softness of her skin.
When she'd hold out her arms,
And tell me to climb in.

I'd cuddle beside her
And snuggle up close.
A kiss on my nose,
Then she'd tickle my toes.

This Mother so special
And held deep within.
She molded me into
The image that I am.

These are the treasures 
I cherish so much.
The childhood moments
Of childhood hugs.
