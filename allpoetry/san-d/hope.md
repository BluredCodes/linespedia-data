"Hope"

I cling to her memory
and I long for her smile.
Remembering the sensations
when I was a child. 
Hope is the future,
that when my life is gone.
I'll walk in the heavens 
where she's been for so long.

I know she's an angel
and clothed with a gown.
Her wings are of white,
upon her head is a crown.
The Hope what I cling to
one day will appear.
This heart will no more
shed all these sad tears.

I hunger for her laughter,
to hear her songs that 
she sang.
Her whimisical nature,
that would light up my days.
The Hope that I'll see her
when my life must end.
In the heavens, a new life
for us will begin.

Life is so precious and
and abundently so.
Life can be taken
before we're ready to let go.
This life here on earth 
is our own Heavens's trial.
This Hope is the reason we
must not live in denial.

I know she's in Heaven,
she is waiting there for me.
Smiling from her castle,
above all the seas.
Watching over my shoulder
making sure I do right.
Guiding me through, 
all the dark lonely nights.

She's waiting so patiently.
Leading me down the narrow
path that is straight.
Guiding me through all the 
triumphs I won.
Holding my hand when 
I fail at one.

Hope is what I cling to 
and what Heaven has to offer.
An everlasting life,
when we no longer have to suffer.
A life with no sorrow 
and a brand new beginning.
A life without pain and
a life never ending.

I hope to hear her laughter.
I hope to hear her song.
I hope in a everafter
and I hope that I'll belong.
I hope to see my angel
and I hope to see her crowned.
I hope to see my Mother,
in her snow white evening gown.

By: Sandy
