"Empty"

Empty are the shadows 
   searching for the light.
     Dark and lonely yearning
       in this cold cell deep
           into the night.

Empty is the soul,
   long gone is all the hope.
       Faded with all the
         memories
           of his innocence
             of long ago.
           
Tick Toc Tick Toc
    Final hours dwindling by.
      Heart that's beating rapid,
        waiting for its time to die.

Eyes filled with emptiness.
     He walks at his own pace.
       Then strapped into his death bed
          and yes, a tear upon his face.             
 
Emptiness envelops him,
     darkness overcomes.
        A plea for his forgiveness.
             His days on earth are done.
                        EMPTY!
