"So Sweet, So Warm"

So sweet, so warm, 
can you be real?
I can't explain, 
what I so deeply feel.
You hold me tight. 
You take away my fear. 
When I'm with you, 
everything seems clear.
When I look in your eyes, 
you seem to know, 
everything about me,  
I'll never let go.
So sweet, so warm, 
is this for real?
