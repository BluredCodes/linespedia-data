"For Those Who Feel No Guilt"

Sow the love of kindness 
for those who feel no guilt.
Keep your heart with purity,
forgive the dirt they've dealt.

For we are not the ones to judge,
nor we the ones to condemn.
For those who feel no guilt today.
Shall regret on judgment day.

By: Sandy/San-d
