"Wintery Storm"

The shadows of the tree limbs 
dance upon on my walls
The light of day shines through my window
As the snowflakes begin to fall

I hear the echos of the birds 
preparing for their tasks
Getting ready for their journey
to a place where they can bask

The ground is softly covered now
as the snow flakes keep falling down
And the essence of this beauty
brings on a silence to our town.

The sun is slowing fading now
as the day is winding down
But the snow outside my window
keeps falling to the ground.

I sit and watch the children
building forts and men of snow.
I watch them make their snowballs
and then watch them begin to throw.

It brings a smile upon my face
to see them full of glee
I watch them romping in the snow
that by now is to thier knees.

I sit here at my window
As thoughts run through my head
In awe at this wintery bliss 
of the snow covered winters bed.

Across the street I hear the sounds
shovels scraping hidden concrete
It's just a way for them to play
and yet can be discreet.

Something bout a wintery storm
That brings a calmness to the air
It makes me want to cuddle
takes away all my despair.

It makes me think of nature
and all its wonderous ways
For even on this wintery day
A smile came my way........
