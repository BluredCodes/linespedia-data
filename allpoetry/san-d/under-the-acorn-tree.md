"Under The Acorn Tree"

All the golden leaves have fallen.
It's time to rest our limbs.
For time has taken a toll on youth
under the acorn tree.

Oh, I never knew the golden leaves,
would one day fall and die.
While playing under the acorn tree.
I always loved to lie.

We'd take the branches from the ground
For our horse, this is what we used.
We'd run and stride them through the woods
to keep us all amused.

We had only the acorn tree,
to play among it's leaves.
We'd romp and run and do our thing
Under the acorn tree.

It's acorns were our supper
and its branches were our ride.
All its leaves up toward the sky
is where we'd hide for cover. 

Now just like all its golden leaves,
it's time to rest my limbs.
For time has taken a toll on youth
under the acorn tree.
