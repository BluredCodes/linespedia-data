"Symptoms"(writers block)

Mind goes blank,
thoughts run wild.
Try to sit,
relax for awhile.

I write some more,
try to think.
Does no good,
can't concentrate!

Scratch verse out,
start anew.
Can't find a word, 
to rhyme with blue.

Want to go to bed! 
Can't contemplate!
Nothing in my head,
sure is getting late!

I go into a daze,
I need to take a break.
My nerves get short.
My head begins to ache.

Have to get a brew,
take a pill or two.
Then I get a thought,
but I loose my 
point of view.

Guess I've caught,
the writers block.
I've lost my train of thought
Nothing in this head,
Just the tick tocks
of the clock.

By: Sandy/San-d
