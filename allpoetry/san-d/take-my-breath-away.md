'Take My Breath Away'I lay awake each nightwith your image that will never fade. I close my eyes,and feel your touch.It takes my breath away.The years have slowly driftedand time has healed my pain.Deep within this heart of mine,a part of you remains.These memories that lingerof a dream of yesterday.What happen to the moments when you'd take my breath away?I can hear your word's of passion,whisper softly in my ear.I cling unto my pillow,your voice I long to hear.You touch my heart, with ecstasyYou promise that you'll stay.You  hold me in your arms so tightand take my breath away.We travel to many placesthat only you and I can go.We move throughout this ecstasylike rivers we do flow.As I pull the coldness over me,I want to feel you against my skin.This desire that's inside of metakes my breath away again. In this place of darknesswhere nights take place of day.You set my heart and soul afireand take my Breath away.By: San-d
