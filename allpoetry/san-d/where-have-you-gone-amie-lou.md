"Where Have You Gone? "Amie Lou"

Where have you gone, Amie Lou?
Are your eyes still misty with blue?
Do you miss me tonight, yearn to hold  me tight?
Or is it my memory, you fight to subdue?

Where have you gone, Amie Lou?
Do you dream of the love we once knew?
Do you stroll down the paths, we walked in the past?
Do you cry as I do?
Do you know, I Love you?
Where have you gone, Amie Lou?

By: Sandy/San-d
