"Scents You've Perished"

The scent of love still lingers.
   Aroma fogs my mind.
     A smile in my eyes,
        leftover from the years
          gone by.
Scents of passion, the 
    love that perished,
      breathing in, 
       desperately trying to
          recapture the scents of
            the love I cherished.

                 By: Sandy/San-d
