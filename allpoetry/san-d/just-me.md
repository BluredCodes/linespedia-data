"Just Me"

See through me, 
deep into my soul.

Touch me, 
in the depth of my heart.

Hear me,
whisper in the echos of your mind.

Breathe me,
the essence of my sweetness.

Lead me,
where there is no path.

Soar with me, 
where no one dares to fly.

Love me...................

Just me...................
