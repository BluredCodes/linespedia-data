---
title: "\"Fly\""
writer: "San d"
slug: "fly"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/7848-Fly-by-San-d"
---

Movements from
within a mother's womb.
Mesmerized by 
first glance.
This miracle 
of life.

Precious, this
tiny soul whose life
depends on her.
Love, trust, faith
shared through
a mother's touch.
 
Treasuring all 
the moments when
innocence is pure.
She knows one day 
this little miracle
will spread its 
wings and fly.
   
By: Sandy/San-d
