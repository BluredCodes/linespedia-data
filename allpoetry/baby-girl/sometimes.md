title: "Sometimes....."
writer: "Baby Girl"

source: "AllPoetry"

SOMETIMES - When I make a wish and I have no one to tell it to
I miss you!

SOMETIMES - At night when angels sing me to sleep with a lullaby
I miss you!

SOMETIMES - When I hold my teddy bear and stare into his kissable blue eyes
I miss you!

SOMETIMES - When I look into a mirror and I don't like what I see
I miss you!

SOMETIMES - When I cry and all my tissues are gone and a shoulder too
I miss you!

SOMETIMES - When I'm driving to work and "our song" comes on the radio
I miss you!

SOMETIMES - When Hugs and I love you's just isn't enough
I miss you!

SOMETIMES - On my couch, your scent still lingers from the night before
I miss you!

SOMETIMES - When I wake up and realize life isn't worth living without you
I miss you!

SOMETIMES - when my journey becomes a mystery which leads into a legend
I miss you!

SOMETIMES I miss you to much
SOMETIMES I miss you a little
SOMETIMES I miss you way to often!
