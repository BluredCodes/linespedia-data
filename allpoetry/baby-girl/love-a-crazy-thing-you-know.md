---
title: "Love ~ A Crazy Thing You Know"
writer: "Baby Girl"
slug: "love-a-crazy-thing-you-know"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/37944-Love---A-Crazy-Thing-You-Know-by-Baby-Girl"

Love ~ can be cruel
Love ~ can be genuine
Love ~ can be directful
Love ~ can be the nectar of the finest red wine

Love ~ sometimes comes
Love ~ often goes
Love ~ can make us do crazy things
Love ~ seems to always know

Love ~ brings us peace of mind
Love ~ makes us fall
Love ~ makes our knees shake
Love ~ can make us stand proud and tall

Love ~ is often scary
Love ~ can drive us insane
Love ~ can make us worry
Love ~ will leave others to it's blame

Love ~ has no age
Love ~ has no remorse
Love ~ can heal old wounds
Love ~ is sometimes the cause of divorce

Love ~ to hate
Love ~ is blind to see
Love ~ can completely change your life
Love ~ is around us even when we don't want it to be
