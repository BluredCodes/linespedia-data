Baby Baby..
Our love is as pure as a beautiful rose forest
So wonderful, the angels sing us a harmonious chorus

Baby Baby..
I can't help my heart to feel this way
When I am near you you make me smile all day

Baby Baby..
Words come easy to me like times like this
Every second your away from me it's you I truely miss

Baby Baby..
In your arms is where I long to be
It's a comfort zone that you secure in me

Baby Baby..
Your cologne on my shirt.. oh, how it lingers
You have my everything wrapped in one around your sweet fingers

Baby Baby..
Your special and I pray our love will never end
You will always be my First, my Best and my "Always Friend"
