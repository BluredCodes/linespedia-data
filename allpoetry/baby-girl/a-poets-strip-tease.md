High pitch horns draw everyone's attention
Behind a glass wall lurks a sexy lady
Skimpy lingerie and a silky top hat
Only true vision is her silhouetted body
Slowly undressing herself to the crowd
Flicking her shoes off in the air with just the tip of her toes
Wiggling
Slithering
Begging for more action
Taking her clothes off completely
One inch at a time
Slowly arousing those watching
Sucking on her fingers
Teasing the audience
Sliding down a wet pole
Twirling
Spinning
Onto an abandoned chair
Sitting spread eagle 
She pulls a string from above
Water rushes all over her now naked body
She looks for a towel to dry off with
But, she hasn't any luck
She grabs a tie and wipes the dampness off her tits down to her belly button
Teasing
Grinning
Standing high and tall on a nearby chair 
Shaking her body to the music
Giving a show the men want to see
What they had paid their hard earned money for
As the lights begin to come to
Gradually
Becoming brighter
Brighter
The wall collapses
Naked to the world
There stands me wearing nothing, but a hat and a tender smile
