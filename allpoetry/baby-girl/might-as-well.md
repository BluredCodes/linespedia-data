My mind S K I P S a beat
My heart races with impure thoughts
Tongue tied I have become
Time stands the ultimate test
Tick..    Tock..     Tick..    Tock..

My Laughter overcomes my fears
Anticipation takes over perspiration
Anxiety questions.... What if????
It's a blessing to belong
A yearn to be desired
A million and one reasons to hide

Manpower
Watchtower

Overlooking the wrongs
The should I's
In The Conclusion
When Destination comes to call....
"MIGHT AS WELL"
