I dance to the angel with the magical tune
I twirl and swing about inside my room
Infront of my mirror/rorrim ym fo tnorfnI
I dance 
     and 
       dance...

Thinking of you 
Our sweet romance




I dance to the angel with the magical tune
The angel so dear that brightens my room
A dove on her wrist, a smile so BRIGHT
I Dance
     and 
       Dance...

This angel like you
Such a wonderful sight




I dance to the angel with the magical tune
You and her both share my love in this room
I think of you everytime I play her my friend
I Dance
     and  
       Dance...

She is done singing
I must wind her up again!
