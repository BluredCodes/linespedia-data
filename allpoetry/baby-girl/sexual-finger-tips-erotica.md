I lie silent and breath in the moment
A bliss of ecstasy times a thousand
My body responses to his pulsating finger tips
Such a timid tender touch of sexual sensations
Purging into a desire beyond my control

~@~ 

My legs quake 
My breath shortens 
Quickly, my heart races for an escape
My claws dwell on a object to pierce
Begging to sink deep into submission
I close my eyes as my flushed face reveals itself

~@~

My mind clears and disappears into a foggy haze
Caught in slow motion atmosphere trying to find myself
a minute passes and I return to reality
Slowly, coming to a world so bright so clear
My mind frees itself
My body comes down from its trembling tremors

~@~

I open my eyes and see him staring down on me
I smile brightly
He smiles proudly
All the other guys had played me a fool
But recently, I finally found a man who is an exception to that rule!!!
