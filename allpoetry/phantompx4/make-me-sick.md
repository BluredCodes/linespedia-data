So here I am spilling my guts to this guy,
He can relate so very well to what I say.
Even though I know he is lying right away. 

He tells me things will be ok, 
And, that I should try things another way.
What the fuck does this guy know?-

What the fuck goes on in my world?
This stupid lying prick, can go suck a 
Dick.

How can you relate to my jailbird song?
When your life is so perfect. And so heavenly sung.
You don’t know squat. Yeah you don’t know shit!

When did you go home and see your mom lying there 
Unconscious. And to know that your father did it,
Goddamn it admit it!

You don’t know what its like to have a shity day.
Your fucking girlfriend is perfect, and your captain 
Of every team

Ohh yeah, I'm a friend, some one to hang out with so
I can be compared to how much you shine over me.
Get the fuck away from me

All you prep's, all you rich snooty fucks.
All of you only like me for how I tell it.
You all don’t really know me and never will.

This is what I scream in my head and I go to bed. 
You all make me sick,
Enough said.
