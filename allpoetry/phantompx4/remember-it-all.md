It's so funny how life comes out.
When you get over things
You thought you could not live with out.

Yeah I really think it is,
When you move on, and forget the pain.
Of the time, when she shouted out someone else's name.

Wasn't that a bitch? Yeah you still remember it all,
No, but really you're fine.
Remember the time when you got in that fight with your mom?

Wow I bet in a hundred years you would have never 
Ever thought you would hit your mom! Wasn't that a sight to see?
Do you think the drugs have gotten a hold of me?

Look at what you've done!
The house is such a mess and the noose undone.
No, really I think your going nuts.

See, the room is still spinning. What are you going to do?
Ugh, I think I'm going to spew.
OH GOD, WHATS HAPPENING? Did you just see that?

Dan! DAN! You're tripping stop choking the fucking plant.
Opps, that's it you're done with.
Quit trying to swallow your Tongue.

Taking drugs is not the answer.
You have to reach out go talk to the pastor.
Things don't go well for us; we need to get some help.

You've known this, why do you go with out.
Yes, you do need to get some counseling.
It's more then your trauma over your Dad.

Well just thought I'd try to help.
Besides you're really weird,
Because you are talking to your self in second person.
