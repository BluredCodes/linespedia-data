So were over now, done with?
Ok that's fine there are some things
I was never all right with.

You think you're something special?
Yeah, I bet you think you're hot shit.
Well hot did you feel when you found out-?

That he only used you,
And then you were with out.
No, You never could get it.

Yes, I loved you all along.
But you just never could see 
what you allready had.
And now I'm gone.

I tryed all I could to make you feel 
like a queen. Because. . . . . . . . . 
Thats what you were to me.

But it's cause I had things to do,
That you did not understand.
Don't you see I was still your man?

It's your selfish pride that keeps you blind.
No you are not at all the type of person
to go back.

You are afraid to admit you're wrong.
And it's because of it you will never see the
light. 

Yeah, I like to think it could have been real good,
Hell I know it would have been great.

Even though my love for you is true,
I'm left with a fake perseption 
of you.
