---
title: "the end?"
writer: "to some i am a poet"
slug: "the-end"

it seems like all i ever see is an illusion
intruding into me...a confusion.
i can't get rid of this intrusion.
i guess my only solution
is a slow and personal execution.
now i sit in seclution...
my mind filled with pollution.
my heart skips beats from this contusion.
must i race for my conclusion
just because of this delusion?
no.
