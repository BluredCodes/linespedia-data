---
title: "no title"
writer: "to some i am a poet"
slug: "no-title"

with no certain direction,
a hurting expression forms.
a brain storms.
drastic,
sporadic,
and sometimes a sarcastic
thought addict.
indestructable walls constructed.
life's abducted.
knife's reluctant to
cleanse the infection.
to remove the imperfection
from nature's selection.it doesn't sound finished, does it?
