---
title: "it's not promised"
writer: "to some i am a poet"
slug: "its-not-promised"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/51993-its-not-promised-by-to-some-i-am-a-poet"

tomorrow...
the promise from yesterday.
so sure to wake up after dreaming of far away.
it doesn't always happen like that.
it's a fact,
the hopes are pulled at...
the promise can fade.
just live for today,
forget about the promise that was made.
