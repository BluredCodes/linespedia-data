---
title: "another wet day"
writer: "to some i am a poet"
slug: "another-wet-day"

as a teardrop explodes,
my eye reloads another shot.
my mind implodes another plot,
twists and pulls itself into a knot.
my knuckles are stained white,
fists are clenched tight
and exist only because of this fight.
sun-kissed in the darkness,
lost in a place without light.
a face without sight.
just a disgrace in it's own right.
i can't let it bring me down,
but there's just nothing i found.
nothing around me to get away,
so the tears fell on this...
another wet day.
