---
title: "Piss on Love"
writer: "JensPen"
slug: "piss-on-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

Something far away calls you
And you feel it to be real
   The aching won’t stop though
 She chides you
 Breaks away
 Lets your heart lose meaning
 Licks the purple flame 
     The heat of a strange land
Hands feet touching
And she isn’t yours any more
   Her mouth cleverly changes and the kiss once sweet, now foriegn
Die
Fall
Fade
  Good night
