I saw you cry
choke on little seeds
spit and sputter
like the broken empty husk
   that you say you are
    that he says you are
 you want little smiles to follow you
you want bent looks, and sideways stares
you need hugs and kisses full of fake roses
  i miss you mama,
where did you hide when they took it away?
   i don't want to follow you
i don't want spit and roses

she turned her head in  the direction of the sound, the aged twinkle of chimes and the grey summer heaved through the windows, her hair spun and tangled it's self away from the sprigs of light falling around her.  

did you hear me-
i miss you mama,  i miss you
