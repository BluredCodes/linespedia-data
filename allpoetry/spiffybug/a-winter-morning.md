---
title: "A Winter Morning"
writer: "SpiffyBug"

It’s one of those times in the morning
When it seems like the whole world is asleep
Except for me
And perhaps, the trees

They sit immune to the drifting snow
Huddled deep within themselves
With only their pencil sketch limbs
Dramatically clashing against the sky

And I, I stand on cold linoleum
At the sink with a glass in hand, and gaze
On the dreaming world outside of the window
And the peaceful scene seen from inside

It’s one of those times in the morning
When I can be so bold as to think
That I’m unique
For I’m all I know that’s not asleep

But time has a way of changing things
And before the moment’s lost, I think
I’ll leave it here so that I’ll find it again
Some winter morning when I’ll rise

And the world will be peaceful and asleep
Except for me, and perhaps, the treesEver have this happen to you?
