It was easy enough to pack my things and go.
I had to learn what he had always known.
You have to keep some self-respect and pride.
It's when I lost them that I knew,
And now the moments come too few.
I don't want to let him go,
But I need to restore my soul.I was digging around in my paper piles and found this. . . so I figured, why not?
