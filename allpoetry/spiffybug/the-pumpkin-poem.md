My Halloween pumpkin is wearing my wig
And sadly it's looking much better in it
The pink ribbons make it annoyingly cute
And purple was never really my color

Pumpkin-head has brighter eyes
And clearer skin and her smile
Doesn't fade like mine
Like the color from the autumn leaves

But pumpky-dear will soon be rotting
On my porch step. I'll be plotting
How I'll dispose of her body
At the local compost pile

Left out with the other trash
Such beauty never seems to last
Especially with winter nearing
Frosting faces that once were learing.If you get confused...just think of it as a giant metaphor.
