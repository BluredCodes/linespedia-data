title: "Sing a Song, Posey"

Sing a song, Posey
Sing a song for me
'Cause I feel too alone tonight
And I know myself too well

Make it smooth and easy
Hum a while to calm me
Make me think I'm happy
And someone who could learn how to love

Sing a song for me, Posey
Wrap your arms around and hold me
'Til my heart doesn't feel so heavy
And there's hope after all

And while you're singing 
I'll be dreaming of a wonderful world
And I'll leave this awkward body
And joy and tragedy will be under my control

At least in my dreams...

Say a prayer, Posey
Say a prayer for me
'Cause Faith is feeling weak tonight
And nothing needed comes easily
