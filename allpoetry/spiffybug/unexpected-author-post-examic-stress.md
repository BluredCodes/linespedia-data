Orwell? What the Hell?
Where did he come from?
I strained my brain and missed a party
Jiggy with Kipling; Down with Hardy
But it seems I wasn't done.

Orwell? What the Hell?
Someone shot an elephant
Now I've got to pay for it
My prof is acting like a git
And changing what she wants

Orwell? What the Hell?
All good intentions were a myth
I'm officially quite pissed
Thank God this class is over with.Uh...I guess this could also be angst...but I doubt it would help the situation any. :) *back to relaxing*
