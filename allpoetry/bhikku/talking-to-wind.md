---
title: "talking to wind"
writer: "bhikku"
slug: "talking-to-wind"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/7522-talking-to-wind-by-bhikku"

firefly light guiding
contrast to all this gray
all is fog that i just breathe into
life from wounded lungs
making an effort
stamped my palms in trial
barbwire crucibles
between me and God I know
but I seek outside company
a 3rd person suggestion
a voice from up high
down below
or inside
as long as it makes some sense
don't really care for cardinal direction
as long as its coming back to me
magnetics
black hole framed
vacuuming all senses to this tongue
lapping at the world
providing temporary comfort to flame
to mushroom clouds and depleting halos
worn crooked like gang member attire
making it personal
like scars or belief
