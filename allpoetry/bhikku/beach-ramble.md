shadows of the cross 
i riped off my chest last night
doing its graveyard thing
salvation in the bend of limbs
call me yoga
cuz i like to sit on my ass all day
and meditate on nothing
and stop watches i don't pay attention to
let the minute hand loose carbonation
get stale
