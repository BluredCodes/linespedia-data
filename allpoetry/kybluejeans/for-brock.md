For Brock

                  Did you know you were leaving
                  Why did you have to go
                  Did you know I'd miss you so
                  Put into life from my womb
                  I watched you become a man
                  Remembering your first hunt
                  And your first deer
                  Seems like yesterday
                  Except you've gone away

                  When God gave you to me
                  I thought you were mine to keep
                  Never thinking I'd have to give you back
                  But now as I weep
                  
                  There are so many things I'd like to change
                  If I could go back in time
                  I would change the day God took you away 
                  From these arms of mine
                  I would stop the rain
                  The car you were in
                  Not wonder if you felt pain
                  I'd hold you close
                  Wipe my tears
                  Instead I call out your name

                  I know you're in heaven
                  That's were angels live
                  I asked God to keep one at your side
                  My only son
                  And that's what he did
                  

                 I look to the stars and watch for a twinkle
                 Cause that's your sign to me
                 Your ok God's with you each day
                 And soon he'll send for me

                  Dedicated to Denise
                       For Brock
