A Marriage

           A story book romance wrote for you and me
           Tales of a love some would only dream
           A lifetime together of serenity and trust
           Moments spent together learning about us
           A single rose to show that our love is true
           Remembering the first time we shared I love you
           Rare in a lifetime do you find your mate
           I believe our love is truely fate
           A promise for forever until one must die
           Gardens of flowers I will plant where you lie
           Eternity together planned for you and me
           A Marriage
           For all the world to see
