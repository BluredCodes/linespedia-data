Hiding Away
           
               They think I am the strong one
               With a smile of steel
               Hiding away all the things I feel
               I take each day one by one
               Trying to survive and hold on
               Then you walk in some ole place
               You look like an angel
               A smile comes to my face
               I can't let you see me with tears in my eyes
               I won't let you hear my heart as it cries
               You stop where I'm sitting and say hello
               Your looking like what I'm feeling 
               But it's not so
               I try to speak the word's don't come
               So as I sit there I go numb
               I watch as you make your way back to the bar
               Your love left me with a lasting scar
