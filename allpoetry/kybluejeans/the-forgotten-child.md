title: "The Forgotten Child"
writer: "kybluejeans"

Shadows on a wall cast pictures of the past
              Childhood memories forgotten
              A painting framed of glass
              Words of broken promises
              Still whisper in my ear
              Take me back to a time
              A child filled with fear
              Daddy won't you hold me
              It's the worse storm this year
              I'm just a little girl
              And my mommy she's not here
              How do birds fly
              Why is the sky up there
              No answers for my questions
              Just shove me out of here
              Daddy do you love me
              It's not my fault I'm here
              I'm still just your little girl
              The forgotten child still hereany and all comments always welcome
