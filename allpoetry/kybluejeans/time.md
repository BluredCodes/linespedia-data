Time
    
               Thinking about the time we spend together 
               Seems I never get enough
               If I had to live my life without you
               It wouldn't be the same
    
               You've put laughter in my heart
               That was once filled with pain
               You've put love in my soul
               That once was afraid
               I want you hold you
               Till the morning sun warms me
               with it's light

               Your so diffrent
               Unlike the rest
               The little boy in you
               So funny, so wild, so dangerous
               You've excited my dreams 
               Sent chills down my spine
               Driving in circles, always on my mind
               No not your wife I ask to be
               Just your lady through eternity
