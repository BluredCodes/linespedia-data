Knowing

                 I thought I'd searched the whole world over
                 Looking for my night in shinning armor
                 I gave up and ran away 
                 From things that didn't work the right way
                 I had decided to live my life
                 Never being anyones wife
                 I figured the most important thing 
                 Was to get it together and hide it away 
                 I'd always be safe that way
                 Then one day I meet you 
                 The sun came out and lifted the blues
                 You slowly took my hand and I knew 
                 No more would my heart be used
                 We decided to give it a chance 
                 This may be the one that last
                 So here we are babe takeing the days one by one
                 Here for each other to lean on
                 I never thought there would be a day
                 Someone like you would come my way
