Again

              Something in the air
              Changes everywhere
              Words with broken lines
              Stories out of time
              Faces without names
              Old familiar games
              Been there before
              Now please close the door
              See me standing strong
              Wake me I was wrong
              Hear me say goodbye
              Watch me as I cry
              Down that road again
              Hello my old friend
