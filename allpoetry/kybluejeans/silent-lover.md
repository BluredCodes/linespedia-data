Silent Lover

                     My Silent Lover dosen't know I exist
                     He has other Lovers I'm last on his list
                       
                     How do I show him
                     Life with me could be so grand
                     How do I show him, his my only man

                     My Silent Lover I'm waiting for you
                     My Silent Lover with you love I'd be true
                     Only in my sweetest dreams are you mine
                     That's where I kiss you lips of wine
                     My Silent Lover you are the greatest to me
                     My Silent Lover you are better there you 
                     I close my eyes now and fall to sleep 
                     In my heart Silent Lover
                     Your here for keeps
