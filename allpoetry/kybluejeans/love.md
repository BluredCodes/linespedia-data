LOVE

             Lay me down beside you
             Your breath upon my face
             A feeling of forever
             Begining to take place
             Music softly playing
             With no radio on
             Just the harmony of two bodies
             Joined as one
             An ecstasy of pleasure
             More exciting then Monarch Pass
             A love for one another
             That is sure to last


                   DEDICATED TO
                       Dale
