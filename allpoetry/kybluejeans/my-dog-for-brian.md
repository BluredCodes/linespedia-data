My Dog (For Brian)

     My dreams that once lay heavy on my shoulders
     All my desires stepped on as I grow with wisdom
     The problems I gave myself starting to grow with age
     As I tip toe through life wondering what's next
     I remember the past and your words of wisdom

     I can see myself as that old man
     Who walks his life alone
     Except for the dog at his side 
     No one else is home

     The dog understands and gives his love
     Wanting nothing in return

     My children grown and moved away
     She greets me at the door
     Tail waggin momma's home
     Never dreaming I'd end up this way

     Now years later I am the one
     Who's all alone
     Except for the dog at my side
     Know one else is home

        Dedicated to Dixon
