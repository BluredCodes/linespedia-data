Doin Hard Time
         Lovin a man in prison held by steel bars
         Is like loving lonliness it cuts deep but leaves a healing scar

         You know they're coming home but still it never ends
         You close your eyes and try to sleep as lonliness snuggles in

         Your days start to early from no sleep the night before
         You wish you could kiss him as you leave for work 
         Close the door

         Then you get a phone call and try to be so strong
         When your insides are crying cause it's been to long

         He tells you he loves you
         You pray to God it's true
         Doin hard time for someone
         Is the hardest thing you'll do
