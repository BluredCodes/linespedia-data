A Dream
 
I saw a passion burn in your eyes with our very first kiss
I felt a hunger in my soul I fought to resist
My need so strong to feel your body pressed to mine
I wanted to feed this hunger of mine
You touched me with a tenderness so strong and in control
I felt the fire between us burn my very soul

In the heat of passion we fell hungerly to the floor
I gave myself to you you stole myself control
Each time reaching higher
Higher then before
I hungered to feel you deep inside of me
You held me tighter with every breath I'd take
My body aching for you I felt the earth quake

Then I lay like a baby in your arms
I turn to call your name
Tears begin to fall
Your not beside me
Only a dream
