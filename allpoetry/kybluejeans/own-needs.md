Own Needs  Sad to hear of your life and your broken dreams  Anyone who knows you wears scars from your needs  To look into your eyes one could never see  How many lies that you tell to feel your own needs  Now that it's your turn I admit I feel shame  Because I know I'll never see your heart wear the pain  Life is a lonely road you now walk alone  Who will be there this time just before you fall  I have a new life now you must crawl  Save your lies for someone who walks without need  I am the one who now stands strong   To feel my own needs
