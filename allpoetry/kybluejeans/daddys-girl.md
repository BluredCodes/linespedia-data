Daddy's Girl

         Remember the first time we were introduced
         At your granny's house
         You looked at me with a hate in your eyes
         That poured all over me
         Your dad and mom just divorced
         And you your daddy's girl
         Bound and determined I was not gonna take
         What was left of your world
         Taking your daddy was the plan
         You had decited for me
         When really all I wanted was
         His love for me
         I tried to explain calling your name
         I told you the way it would be
         Always your daddy he would be through out eternity
         I was not taking your daddy
         Please try and accept me
         He and I just share a love
         Some only dream
         Now you've grown up
         A beautiful woman you turned out to be
         I only hope I am there that day 
         You too plan eternity
         I will stand on the side
         You will be a beautiful bride
         My only regret you see
         Is that I am just your stepmom
         And a daughter you are to me

                  Dedicated To Crystal
