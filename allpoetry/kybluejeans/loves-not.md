Loves Not

              She made love to a stranger
              He wasn't even a freind
              After she went home
              The same heartache set in
              Most of her evenings were spent alone
              Except for the nights she took strangers home
              She was searching for something
              Pass the eyes and in the soul
              Something in life her heart had not known
              She hides it but cries into the night
              Wondering is life worth the fight
              Loves not a stranger she holds at nightany comment is greatly appreciated
