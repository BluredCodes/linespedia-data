A Promise Of Tomorrow

              When you looked at me and said hello
              I felt your eyes searching for something in my soul
              What was it in life your heart had missed
              You were trying to express in your tender kiss
              

              As you touched me and held me near
              I could feel as you caressed me some kind of fear
              Someone must have hurt you so deep inside
              Any chance for love you just set aside

              I can't make you a promise 
              That this will always last
              I won't ask you to love me 
              With the scars of your past

              I will be here to hold you
              When your scared and all alone
              I will try to listen
              When you let your feelings known

              With a  promise of tomorrow
              You will not be alone
