HER
                  Her heart carries a dagger
                  Her soul tattered and torn
                  Bruised and broken promises
                  Her smile very worn
                  Whispers of silence
                  Secrets never told
                  Fantasies abandoned
                  Her mind raped and cold
                  Stories of a woman
