Waiting
People at the front door
Someone's on the phone
Nether one is you
So I'd rather be alone

My friends keep asking 
What's going on with me
They say I ain't the same
As I use to be

Once I loved to party 
I still love to dance
I use to go out looking for a little
Romance is something 
I found at every bar
Sittin on stools, playin pool
Out back in a car
That life is fun 
I've had my share of flirting
Hearing those lines
And taking any dare

Now I find myself at home
Sleeping with no one else
It's hard to believe 
I'd rather be alone

Jody's at every coner
He wants to take me home
That's okay Jody 
Tonight I sleep alone
I'm waiting on my baby
Soon he's coming home
