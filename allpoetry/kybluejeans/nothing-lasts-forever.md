---
title: "Nothing Lasts Forever"
writer: "kybluejeans"
slug: "nothing-lasts-forever"
source: "AllPoetry"

I have a feeling in my heart my mind can't explain
I have a feeling in my soul it's calling your name

Last night I had a dream you went away
These are the words I heard you say
Nothing lasts forever and dreams don't come true
Don't love me baby I ain't good for you

As the dream continued I heard an erie sound
Blue lights around us
You laid laughing on the ground
We had fun while it lasted and the times they were good
Next time around baby you'd wished you should

They finally got me now come hold me tight
Looks like your rebel has lost his last fight

As you kissed me you slipped away
These are the words I heard you say

Nothing lasts forever and dreams don't come true
Please love me baby
I ain't good for you
