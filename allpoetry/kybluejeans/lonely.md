Lonely

         Sometimes I get so lonely and I don't know what to do 
         I just lay back, close my eyes and fill my mind of you
         So many days have passed since I've kissed your lips of wine
         So many nights I've buried these tears holding this pillow of mine
         Sometimes I get so scared
         I start thinking of your never coming home
         but if I can't have you I'd rather be alone
