Karen

                            Stop feeling the pain
                            This one might last
                            Stop feeling the shame It's in the past
                            All of your wishes and all of your dreams
                            All the promises left undone 
                            All the beginnings gone with someone 
                            Let your heart begin to grow 
                            With a love you'll never know
                            Feel your shattered heart meand
                            At his mercy your lover and friend
