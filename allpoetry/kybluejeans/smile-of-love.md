Smiles Of Love

              Smiles of love happy and bright
              Make me glad
              In your arms I will lay tonight
              Soft spoken words and your touch
              My destiny in life
              To be at your side on a 500 mile ride
              Is more than I could ask
              Loving you for the rest of my life
              Will be an easy task
              For you are the one God has choose
              To help me lose my mask
