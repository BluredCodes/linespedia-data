A Teardrop
                 A teardrop on my cheek
                 Is all it seems to be
                 A teardrop on my cheek
                 Shed for you and me

                 A teardrop lost and lonely
                 As I am for you
                 A teardrop slowly sliding
                 As I fell for you

                 A teardrop being wiped away
                 Easily as I was from you
