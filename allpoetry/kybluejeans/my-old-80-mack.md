My Old 80 Mack

           Three miles out of Uniontown
           Stopped at a bar to drink one down
           You were on my mind again
           So I pulled over and began to grin
           I remembered the way you smiled
           How you helped me on my way down
           Now I'm out here just the road and me
           Seeing things I've never seen
           Ain't it funny the way things go
           In love one day and the next alone
           No more promises and no regrets
           Just a life of one town to the next
           My new love has a 400 cat with a jake 
           She's just an old Mack
           She never hurts me or let's me down
           When I'm lonely she's always around
           No child support payment no alimony
           Just your occasional maintence matrimony
           She never ask's where I been
           She never cares where we go
           She's my gal
           And now you know
