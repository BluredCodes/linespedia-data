Rebel Soul
            You never gave me a promise
            I never gave you a line
            Boy you got a hold on me
            I hold it deep in my eyes
            Not sure you'll be around tomorrow
            At least that's what I am told
            So if you don't mind
            Could I take the time
            To know what some would call a rebel soul
            I know I could never hold you
            That's what has held me all this time
            You and I already know
            There is just no taming
            A rebel soul
            So I will grab the days
            Hold the nights
            Till once again I'm alone
            I could never be happy with the kind of guy
            Who loves me so
            Some say I am foolish just waistin away
            Cause you will always leave me hungry
            Hungry for words I dare not say
            So please never add a change
            To what I'll always know
            As the man with a rebel soul


                        DEDICATED TO
                          Clinton
