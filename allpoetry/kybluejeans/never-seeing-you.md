Never Seeing You 

           I'd been running around living it up
           Never thinking about growing up
           I'd heard all the lines and broke all the rules
           Took some time to discover I'd been the fool

           Now there where some lookers who took the time
           To run off with this heart of mine

           As I played you stood by 
           Ready to hold me when I'd cry

           I kept looking but never seeing you
           My nights became lonely then my days too
           I needed someone 
           Still never seeing you

           I'd heard I love you from every man I knew
           Always believing but ending up blue
           Still never seeing you
