What I see                When I look at you                What do I see                A young man                You're growing too be                The smile of an angel                You wear on your face                So much fun                With no time to waste                Playing with puppy's                Dancing in place                Matchbox car's                You always race                Lot's of hugs                 And kisses too                Dalton I wanted you to know                You are a special you                                  Dedicated to Dalton
