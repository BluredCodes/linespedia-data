Destiny
           In our lives we sometimes become too scarred
           To cross the path of destiny
           With know trust in ourselves
           And the scars of life
           We become trapped in a shell of loneliness
           As time will pass so will the pain
           We capture our own souls
           Holding to the promise we make ourselves
           To be alone is much better
           Than being alone
           With the truth of our own eyes
           We long to touch and be touched
           Anger overcomes us if we open that door
           Yet if it remains closed       
           We become cold and life's destiny
           Will pass us byany and all comments welcome
