Reflections

                      A was within myself
                      Bruised and broken dreams
                      A fairytale of things
                      That would never be
                      A scralet letter left unread
                      Stories never told
                      A lifetime of promises
                      Waiting to unfold
                      A reflection from my eyes
                      A reflection of my soulsometimes I wonder if my writing is to deep
