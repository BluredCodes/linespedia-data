Memories

               When I woke this morning
               Before the dawn of light
               Again you were there
               Crossing the memories of my mind
               As I layed there and thought of you and me
               Tears fell from my eyes I couldn't sleep
               I thought it was special
               Our love was true
               Now I am thinking what do I do

               Putting it behind me
               The memories of us
               I know longer want to live
               I know I must
               I'm thinking this time
               I will feel no more pain
               All I wanted was you 
               I didn't ask for your name

               Love...if this is what it's like
               No more for me
               I'll continue to fight

               I guess it's best you went away

               People can give untill they just give up
               I am giving up baby
               The memories of us
