Change the Rubber

     Sittin at the station bidin my time 
     Tryin to keep an eye on that baby of mine
     I told him those tires we'd have to change 
     We're going down the highway doing a hundred and three
     He just looked at me and said it's all the same
     He yells out if I keep doing this they'll be looking for me
     I know the tires we're on just ani't no good
     We wouldn't be in this spot if we'd sone what we should
     Flying down the highway as fast as hell
     My baby looked at me and ain't going back to jail
     We better take the time to stop int his station
     We better take the time to change               
     Now it was dark and gettign late
     My belly was growling I hadn't ate
     The guy at the station was almost done
     My baby looked at me then pulled out a gun
     Ya know they didn't fight us or try to resist
     Five hundred dollars richer glinched in our fist
     We got out of there as fast as we could
     Again we were doing what we shouldn't of
     The law would get us if they knew what we'd done 
     I knew it was time to fet ride of the gun
     I tossed in a ditch and that's all to say
     Changed your rubber if you need to
     Before you end up this way
