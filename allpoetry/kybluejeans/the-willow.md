The Willow  
          I'm not looking for love
          Or just learning to play
          I've had my share of one stands
          I'm done with that game


          I've been touched by Romeo
          And loved by the best
          I layed beside Shakespear
          And put a dagger in Draculas chest

          I've worn the scarlet letter
          Lived Love Story and laughed
          Wrote a poem about my first love
          And left it in the past

          The oaks limbs will break
          Yet they are strong
          But the willow withstands all that comes along
