Mask Of Pain

     We've opened our heart to pain again
     Both to scared too let the other in
     Our wounds have scars our cuts to deep
     Our hearts alive but wants to sleep

     If we feel nothing but a touch to the skin
     Never will our heart feel love again
     Must we laugh as our soul carries shame
     We wear a mask to cover our painall comments and suggestions welcome
