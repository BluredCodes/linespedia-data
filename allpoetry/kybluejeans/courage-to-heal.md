Courage To Heal

             To many years and tears
             And the fear of being alone
             Makes a person scared but tired
             Of always being strong
             Learning to lean on someone else
             Letting them feel your pain
             Leaves you feeling naked
             Showing your scars of shame

             To many times of being let down
             Builds back up the walls
             Taking one brick out at a time
             Takes courage to heal
