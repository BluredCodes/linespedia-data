The Last Chance
               As I think about my life
               All my trials
               All my mistakes
               All the heartaches that somehow healed

               I think of past lovers
               The time we shared
               And how I felt when it ended
               I was caught in a cycle of ups and downs

               Never knowing where I was going
               Never knowing how I would get there
               Never caring when I finally did arive

               Then I met you
               God's fate to join us together
               It must have been

               For when I hold you
               I feel all your trials and mistakes

               As I look into your eyes
               I can see all your heartaches that somehow healed

               I believe God has sent you to me
               I will always respect what has been given to me
               My last chance to love



                           Dedicated to
                                Dale
