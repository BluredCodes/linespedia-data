Six billion perspectives

Seeing
Feeling
Hearing
Knowing…

Familiar faces 
Forgotten love
The smell of coffee.   
Never seen from above. 

The world at large, seems too vast to see all. 
Knowledge of all, beyond our comprehension.
God’s comprehension is infinite
Human spirit is infinite.

The floating blue Orb houses all
Destined for greatness
Some may see, but many will fall
So close to perfection

This is just a human spirit school
Passing to the next level will explain all.
Reality will envelop imagination
And Six Billion perspectives will seem as one.
