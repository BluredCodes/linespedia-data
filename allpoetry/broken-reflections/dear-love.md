title: "Dear love"
writer: "Broken Reflections"

Dear love, please listen, for i have deep words, deep thoughts, deep feelins that u have never heard before
I have words of pain,thoughts of hidden desires, and feelings that will never be seen
Because they are locked into every dark corner of my mind, because im afraid to let them see light
Im scared to feel you love,to give you love,to let someone love me in return
And truthfully why should I feel you, You left me, You let my heart without even saying good-bye
And now im supossed to trust you, when you betrayed me, when you let the people who say the love me, betray me
Love i deserve to ignore you, forget your exist, forget you, like you forgotton me
So what do you have to say to yourself now love, and dont say sorry, because sorry doest fix all the nights my innocence, my purity was taken away from me
Sorry does not make this infested, poisonous body clean again does it
So thank you love, thank you for listening to these deep words, deep thoughts, deep feeling that u have never heard before and never hear again
