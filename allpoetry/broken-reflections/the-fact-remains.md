---
title: "The fact Remains"
writer: "Broken Reflections"
slug: "the-fact-remains"
source: "AllPoetry"

Its not much more say to reach your heart,
Theres not much more i can do to make you love me
But now the fact remains that im tired,
Tired of being the one who keeps trying,
While you find it so easy to just walk away
But yet you say that you love me, want me
Need me in your life, that you cant get by without me
Yet you hurt me, and yet i sit there and take it
But love makes you do some crazy things,
And i did a lot of crazy things to stay with you,
Not that you took time out of your buzy life to notice,
But for the first time i'm taking my power back
I can no longer give so much of myself,
To the point where i start tolose myself,
Because im to the point where caring for you feels wrong
So for now on you can do what you want,
And be with whoever you want to be wiith,
Because the fact remains that im gone,
And im never comming back
