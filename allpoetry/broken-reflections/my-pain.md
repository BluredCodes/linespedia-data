---
title: "My Pain"
writer: "Broken Reflections"
slug: "my-pain"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/53500-My-Pain-by-Broken-Reflections"
---

I wear several masks
So no one can see
My life of pain
My life of misery

It hurts to write
It hurts to feel
So i hide my feeling inside
Because its the only way i know how to deal

So i walk around a tortured soul
On the outside looking in
With pain unimagainable
never knowing where to begin
