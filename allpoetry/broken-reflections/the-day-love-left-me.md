title: "The Day Love Left Me"
writer: "Broken Reflections"

There was a time when i couldnt stop saying that I love you
And now i find it hard for me to even say your name
Is this some evil plan love, to get me to come so close, 
To get me to believe that i have come so far,
To just pull me right back to where i was in the begining
Because this time instead of pushing you away,
I needed you love, only to find out that you didnt want me
That when i layed in front of you, in one true moment of weakness
That my vunerablilty wasnt enough for you to want me,
For you to hold me,
So is there anything i can do to bring you back
Because i never wanted you to leave me feeling so alone
Or at least more alone than i already am,
I feel like im going crazy without you,
Because there was a time when i couldnt stop saying that i loved you
And now more than anything i wish that day would soon returni know that it sux, sorry
