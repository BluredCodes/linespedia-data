---
title: "If This is Love"
writer: "Broken Reflections"
slug: "if-this-is-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

If this is love, than all I can say is wow,
I really dont want it,
Because it takes up too much time, 
Too much patience,
Too much thinking 
Just a little too much feeling
Not to say that your not worth it,
I just dont rather not care
Because sooner or later we are going to get tired of each other anyway
And slowly each one of our faults will start to show
And this love that felt so warm and good, 
Will start to feel more cold and distant each day
So if this is love,
Please just take it back, because bascally i dont  want or need it
Give it to someone who deserves it, or at least cares enough to do something with it
