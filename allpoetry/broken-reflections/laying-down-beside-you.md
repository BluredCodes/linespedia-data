---
title: "Laying Down Beside You"
writer: "Broken Reflections"
slug: "laying-down-beside-you"

If you would let me lay down besides you
Let me feel your heartbeat so close to mine
I would give you my life, give you my world, give up all that i am
For one night to live for your touch
For one moment to die for your kiss
I would bring down the sky, wrap you up in the stars,open up the gates of heaven
Just to let you know how much you mean to me
Because every night i dream about you, of taking in every breath of your soul, of just being next to you
And everyday that love becomes more real
So if you would let me lay down beside you, let me feel your heartbeat as it fuses with mine
I would lay down my life,throw away my whole world, discard all that i am
For one second to live for your touch, for one moment to die in your arms for one sweet kissum i know that its not that good, sorry
