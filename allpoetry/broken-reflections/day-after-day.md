---
title: "Day after Day"
writer: "Broken Reflections"

Day after day my pain rises like the morning sun,
Holding my fears up for the world to see

Time after time, i cry to god, my voice forever uplifting,
To take my lifr, kill the pain,, but my cries go unheard

Hour after hour,time holds no truth for me,
As im close to my end, but no further away from the begining

Tear after tear,they just keep falling like the evening rain
As the darkness dispenses the last of my holylight

Fear after fear, they grab my mind and fill it with darkness,
As i softly caress the caring hands of death

Nite after nite,my hope for happiness dies like the sunset last rays
Killing whats left of my soul, leeting my soul slowly drift awayi know this is dark, but what can i say
