Hello?
Can you tell me who i am?
Hello?
Can you see me?
Are you blind or something?
Why are you looking right through me?

Hello?
Are you listening?
Why wont anyone talk to me?
Because i seem to have lost myself?
Hopefully somewhere where you can find me

Hello?
Do you know me?
Can you tell who i might be?
Can you tell me who i was
Can anybody reconize me?

Hello?
Can you see me?
Can you tell me who i am?
Hello?
Are you blind or something?
Why are you looking right through me?

Hello?
