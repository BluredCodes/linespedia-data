---
title: "Burnt Ashes"
writer: "Broken Reflections"

Can you please find me,
Before I lose myself again
Before I get lost in the burnt ashes,
That I once called my memories
Before I start to become someone I don’t remember
Someone I hate,
But who every else loves
Because in my life and in my dreams,
I sometimes fall victim to the shadows that hide behind the walls
I fall to the point where I don’t know who I am anymore,
Do you?,
Because I just want everything to end 
My life,...my dreams, 
I just want it all to stop,
I just need to stop pretending,
So if you could please find me,
Before I start to lose what's left of me again
Before I get lost in the burnt ashes,
That I wish that I didn’t have to call my memories
Become someone I don’t remember anymore
Someone everyone loves, 
But who I seem to learned how to hate
Because sometimes I fall victim,
To the shadows that rapes me behind closed doors
Fall to the point where I don’t know who or what I am anymore
Please tell me that you do?,
Can anyone just find me
