If at anytime your heart longs for me,
Close your eyes and make a wish 
And i promise that not a moment later.
I will be laying beside you
Not in body, 
But in mind and spirt
I'll be there holding you, 
Doing everything and anything to make sure you never feel lonely again
Because slowly but surly your inner light has found my inner darkness
And now that they intertwined 
I feel as if i can do anything
Scary enough, i can actually feel,
And now that i do i dont to stop,
So if at anytime your heart ever longs for me, 
Just think of me
And i promise that i will not a moment later
I will be by your side
