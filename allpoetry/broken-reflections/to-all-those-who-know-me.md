To all those who know me
Know whats really going on inside
Know that behind all mt laughter
All i want to do is cry
You seen me happy and depessed
But i know through everything
You wonder if one day you will get to see me cry
Wonder if i'll let myself feel anything
But i cant, i wont,i dont't
Pain and crying is for the weak
And my pride keeps telling me,
That strength is what i must seek
So to all those that really know me
Really know whats going on inside
Pray that one day instead of making you laugh
One day i'll let you see me cry
