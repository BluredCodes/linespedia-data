I feel your touch down my neck
I want so bad to turn away
Rememberd feelings of love wash ovewr me
Cant help but want to stay
But i dont wanna lose myself
I cant once again get lost inside your pain
You just cant come back
And expect thing to be the same
Because you didnt think that i could live without you
I bet u thought that i couldnt get by
But i learned to love myself
And to live without your lies
So no matter how bad i want to kiss you
No matter how bad you want to kiss me
I know that all you will do is cause me pain
So my heart must set you free
