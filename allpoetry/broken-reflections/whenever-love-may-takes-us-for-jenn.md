Wherever love may take you, i will never be to far behind
If it brings you into my arms or into the arms of another
I'll always stand by you without hesitation
For what i feel is deeper than just love, its loyalty anf honestly to the fullist

Wherever love may take me, i know that you will never be to far behind
If it finds me running into your arms or into the arms of another
I know that you will always be there holding my hand
For noone knows what the future holds but i know it holds me closer to you

Wherever love may take us, we stand tall against the world
If it finds us laying in each other arms or the arms in the of another
We will be there for each other without question
Because nothing can change the connection that lies deep in our heartsi know this isnt one of my strongest poems, but i wrote for you Jenn my only true sun, moon and evening star
