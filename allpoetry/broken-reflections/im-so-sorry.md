---
title: "Im So Sorry"
writer: "Broken Reflections"
slug: "im-so-sorry"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/52796-Im-So-Sorry-by-Broken-Reflections"
---

Im sorry for loving always loving you
For always standing by you, 
And for never judging you

Im sorry for for always being your friend
For always telling you the truth,
And for always trusting you

Im sorry for all the poems i wrote you
For all the songs i sang to you

Im sorry that you decided that you dont love me anymore
That im not even good enough for you to talk to anymore

Im sorry for making you smile,
Im so sorry for just trying to make you happy
