---
title: "True Feelings"
writer: "Broken Reflections"

My true feeling are what I hide inside
Thrown away with the pain I left behind
I run away the minute my fears begin to rise
I only hurt myself by pushing you away
To many problem, to many ways for me to say I care
To many reasons for me to tell you that i love you
Though the words run through my mind a million times
When the time comes im still dont know what to say
That when you touch me,my whole world stops
That when i see your face, the coldess in my heart melts away,
Because i never met anyone who made me feel so free,
Who made me feel so safe,
But my true feeling are what i hide inside,
Thrown away with the pain i left behind
