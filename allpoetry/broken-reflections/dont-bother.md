---
title: "Don't Bother"
writer: "Broken Reflections"
slug: "dont-bother"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/51214-Dont-Bother-by-Broken-Reflections"

Its easier for me to say that im not ready,
Ready to fall in love, ready to be with you
Though your touch brings shivers down my sopine
Dont bother feeling anything for me
Hold me, but dont love me, want me, but dont need me
Because i can never love you, not the way you deserve to be loved
For theres a fire, an anger inside me that you could never understand
Let alone want to love
So just walk away with your pride,
Before i walk away with your heart
Because the chains that bind my soul arent yours to break
I will never let u see inside of me
I will never give you the chance to love me,
So dont even bother
