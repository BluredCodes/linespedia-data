writer: "Broken Reflections"

Please dont love me,
Dont think about me,
Matter of fact dont even like me,
Dont waste your time on my heart
Because all im going to do is hurt you,
Well if i dont let you hurt me first
So just leave me alone
Find someone who cares 
Because belive me, i DON'T
