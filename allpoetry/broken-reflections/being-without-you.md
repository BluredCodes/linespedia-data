---
title: "Being Without You"
writer: "Broken Reflections"
slug: "being-without-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/52824-Being-Without-You-by-Broken-Reflections"
---

I am so alone,
I can't stand this feeling of being without you,
It's like im losing control,
I cant sleep without you here by my side,
I stare at your picture to pass the time,
Just trying to find some reason in my mind,
Why you are gone
