---
title: "I Didnt Need You"
writer: "Broken Reflections"
slug: "i-didnt-need-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

I didnt need you to care about me
I didnt need you to complete me
I didnt need you as a friend
I didnt need you to complicate my life
I didnt need to fall for you
I didnt neeed to feel so connected to you
I didnt need to care about you so much
I didnt need you to love me
I didnt need your pity, for you to feel anything at all
I didnt need you in my life
I didnt need you at all,
But most of all i didnt need you,
To leave me feeling so alone
