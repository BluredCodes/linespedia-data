If i was a poet,
Could you imagine the things i could write,
The things i would say,
The emotions i would make poeple feel
The joy and the pain i would get,
From writing all my emotions on paper

If i was a poet,
Would everyone feel a lil of my pain,
Understand that i have a heart,
That through my words i know how to feel,


If i was a poet,
Could i make the tearless cry?,
Make the heartless feel pain?,
Could one of my poems change the world?

If only i was a poet
