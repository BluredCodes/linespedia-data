Can someone tell me if this is love,
Because if it isnt,
I dont know what else to call it,
Because I think about you before i go to sleep,
Your my first thought when i wake,
I think about you hoding me, me holding you
About our first kiss, about the first time we make love
When i look into your eyes there is no place i'd rather be
So if this isnt love, please tell me what it this is
Because i just dont know what other word to use to define it
