title: "Special"
writer: "Broken Reflections"

Could everyone stop saying that they love me
Because everytime i hear those words it hurts my ears
Because you know what he told me that he loved me too
He told me that was special
So special that he just had too cause me so much pain
Guess thats the price of being his favorite friend,
Guess that the price of letting someone close too you
For letting myself feel something
Because everyone loves to take, but in return gives so much less
Want love and trust, but is never willing to work for it
And because of that i will never give of myself freely again
So can you please stop saying that you love me
Just stop becasue those words bring pain and hurt to my ears
Because as he touched me, he said that he loved me too
He said that i was is favorite lil' buddy,
And with a smile he told me that i was special
