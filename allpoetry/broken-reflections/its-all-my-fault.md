title: "Its All My Fault"
writer: "Broken Reflections"

I would say that i didnt ask you to love me,
But i did, and im sorry for that
Because its all my fault for trying to give you a heart,
That was a lil' to cold to give
But that doesnt mean that i never loved you,
Or that i didnt even try,
That doesnt mean i no longer care about you
Because we both know that would be a lie,
So now i have to let you go,
But in the end all good things fall apart
At the end of every fairly tale,
The hero really does die
So i wish that i could say that i didnt ask you to love me
But i did and im sorry
For pulling you in, and that just as quickly letting you go
But no matter what,i know that its all my fault
