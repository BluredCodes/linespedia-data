writer: "Broken Reflections"

Im sorry,
But no matter how hard i try,
I cant say that i love you,
Shew i having trouble getting pass the word "I"
But please dont take offence, its not you, its me
I readily admit that im afraid,
Afraid of loving you,
Afraid of being loved in return,
Afraid of being totally open with anyone besides myself,
And even then i have issues
So im sorry that i cant say that i love you
Because i dont think that i could ever love anyone
