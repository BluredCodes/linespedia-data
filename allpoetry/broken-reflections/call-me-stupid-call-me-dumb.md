Call me stupid,
Call me dumb,
Call me whatever you like,
As long as you know,
That no matter what you call me,
I cant help but still be in love with her
I know that she hurts me,
And I know all she does is cause me pain
But i just dont know how to walk away,
I dont even think that i want to,
Because through all the pain and hurt
She still makes me so happy inside
Even though she uses the fact that she has my heart
I still just dont want to be her friend,
I still cant help but want something more,
Because no matter how hard you try,
You can't comprehend the way she makes me feel,
The feeling that run throughout my heart,
That run throughout my entire soul
So call me stupid,
Call me dumb,
Call me whatever you like,
As long as you know,
That no matter what you call me,
I cant help but but still be in love with her
I cant help but still want her in every way
