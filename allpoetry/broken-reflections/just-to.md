title: "Just To......."
writer: "Broken Reflections"

Just to touch you
Just to kiss you
Just to hold you

Just to see you smile
Just to make you laugh
Just to make you happy

Just to be your lover
Just to be your friend
Just to be your everything

Just to know that you think of me
Just to let you know that i only think of you
Just to know that we think of each other

Just to be in love
Just to let you know that im still in love with you
Just to hear you say that you are still in love with me
