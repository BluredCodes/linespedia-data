writer: "Broken Reflections"

When i see your face ,i wanna touch you, feel you,love you, intrigue you, caress you

I want to float inside your arms forever as the world slowly melts away

Because you give off this heavenly light the the whole world can see, 

But only i truly admire,

This smooth rythem inside you that has me hooked on to your every move

Has me wishing for the day when your angel eyes will speak only to mine

When they will sing a simple love song that only you and I alone can hear

Because i cant help but want you, need your body oh so close to mine

To touch you, feel you, intriuge you, 

To want to entice all of your every five senses

Because you give off this light that the whole world can see 

But only i truly understand

And though you are not yet mine, 

I cant stop dreamin' of the day they when you will be

When your angelic eyes will speak so softly into mine

When they will sing a simple love song that only you and I alone will hear
