---
title: "The Loves That Kills"
writer: "Broken Reflections"
slug: "the-loves-that-kills"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/52166-The-Loves-That-Kills-by-Broken-Reflections"
---

Between the lines of broken promises,
Hangs a thinner line of love that neither one of us can see
Is it you, or is it me, that just keep pushing away
Untill we are right back to where we where in the begining
Which is nowhere,and thats is where we will forever stay,
Untill one of us is willing to step forward and say what lies in our hearts,
Because a love that started out so magical,
Now ends in deafining silence,
And  if i was meant to lose you, 
I refuse to lose you like that,
But if my love,..if my heart isnt enough to make you stay
Than i must respect that,
So if this is love, that it must be the kind that of love that kills
Becuase i feel like watching myself die,
As I slowly watch you walk awaythe end came and now i cry alone
