---
title: "Alone"
writer: "Broken Reflections"
slug: "alone"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/52463-Alone-by-Broken-Reflections"
---

No matter how far i fell,
I was always able to pick myself up,
But it never crossed my mind,
Not even once,
That i would one day fall way beyond my arms reach
That by my own free will,
I would find myself alone
