---
title: "Nightmares"
writer: "Broken Reflections"
slug: "nightmares"

If You could see the nightmares 
That run through my head
How they cut through me like a knife,
Leaving no words to be said

Could someone kill me now,
So i dont have to see the light of another day
Because i'll do about anything,
To make the pain go away

Because pain is all that in my heart
And it wont let go of me
Hate is all i can feel
These feelings wont let go of me

So i put a gun to my head
As the world turns dark
This is how my life ends
Before it even starts

And i know you think this is wrong
But this is the only thing that feels rite
So i slowly pull the trigger
As the world says good-nite
