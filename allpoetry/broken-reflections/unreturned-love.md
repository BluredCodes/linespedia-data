I promised to give you everything
To bring down the moon and the sky
To give the whole world to you
I promised to love you till the day i died
But so quickly did i relize that,
No matter what i promise, 
You'll never love me back
I gave you my heart, gave you my soul
And yet that meant nothing to you
I gave you my life at your feet,
And i still dont know what to do,
To make you see,
How my whole world falls apart,
When your not near me
And though i keep telling you how i feel
The words in my heart wont make you love me
No matter how hard i try,
I know bwing with me will never make you happy
I cant make you love me if you dont
Cant make your heart feel what it wont
But i once promise to give you everythinhg
To bring down the moon and the sky
I would have given you the world, to make you love me
I could hav loved you till the day you died
But so quickly did i relize that,
No matter what i give you,
You will never love me back
