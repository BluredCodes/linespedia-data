---
title: "If You Could Read My Mind"
writer: "Broken Reflections"
slug: "if-you-could-read-my-mind"

If you could read my mind,
Tell me what would you see,
Would you see how much I love you,
How much  I want you,
Could you see how much you hurt me, 
When you say the negative things you say
The pain, the heartache
The trials and tribulations I go through
Just to be with you,
Nah, u  would probably just over look all that,
And casually tell me how much you love me
As if that is supposed to erase all the hurt,
But I'm sorry it doesn’t ,
And I know that I'm only hurting myself by staying
But I just don’t know how to walk away
So if you could read my mind,
Tell me what would you see,
Tell me could you still treat  me the same way
Could you look in my eyes and still hurt me,
If only you could read my mind
