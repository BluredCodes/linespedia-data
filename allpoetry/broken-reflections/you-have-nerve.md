---
title: "You Have Nerve"
writer: "Broken Reflections"
slug: "you-have-nerve"
source: "AllPoetry"

I cant believe you have the nerve to tell me,
That i never loved you with my whole heart,
But let me ask you this question ,
For this whole time what the hell do you think i was loving you with?
I wrote about you, thought about you like all the time,
Did whatever it would take to make you smile,
All i wanted to do was to make you happy,
But i didnt love you with my whole heart,
What more did you want,
Becasue whatever it was i willing to give
After all i did to stay with you,
I just cant belive that you have the nerve to tell me,
That i didnt love you with my whole heart
