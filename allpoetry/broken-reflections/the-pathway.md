I can spend forever looking into the eyes of the one i love,
 
And never find a glimpse of that same love returned in his gaze

But you see im not like im wishing a mircle, 

Im hoping on a dream, the light to show me pathway to his heart

Though i know he feels me like the tide feels the magnatism of the moon 

Like the stars feels the velvet abyess of the sky

He hides his heart from me, he hides his soul

So i'll stand here waiting, wanting to spend forever looking into the eyes of the one I love, 

Just waiting to find my love returned in just a single gaze

But you see im not im not hoping on a mircle, 

Im living for this one dream, to find the pathway that to his heart

Thats burned deep within our souls

A pathway he can no longer b so afarid to open
