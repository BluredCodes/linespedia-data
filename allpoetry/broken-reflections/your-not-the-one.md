---
title: "Your Not The One"
writer: "Broken Reflections"
slug: "your-not-the-one"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/53203-Your-Not-The-One-by-Broken-Reflections"
---

I dont need to be in love
I'd just rather be alone
Because you dont know who i am
Your in love with someone you dont see,
Your not the one, as much as i want you to be
You cant melt the coldness in my heart
Theres no hope of me opening myself to you
So just leave me alone
Because ill just turn around and hurt you
You cant fill in the emptiness that lays inside my soul
Theres nothing inside me that you could love
You cant see inside me, you dont even know who i am
Your just wasting your time,you just dont understand
The pain that eats and sleeps inside my mind
The nightmares that run through my head
So i dont need to, or dont want to fall in love
I'd rather just walk away and find myself alone
Because your in love with someone you dont see,
Your just not the one, as much as i want you to be
