title: "Why Do We Keep Holding On"
writer: "Broken Reflections"

Theres not much for us here,
But my tears, and your pain
So can you please explain this to me,
Why do we keep holding on ?
You just keep promising me the world,
I keep promising you my everlasting love,
But neither one of us is willing yo collect on those promises
So here it comes,
The look of two people who thought that they loved each other,
(But  really only loved themselves)
Look at each other as perfect strangers,
With more comptempt, than love
But for some strange reason we just keep holding on
But to what,
The memories,
because that all we seem to have left,
And even those arent worth holding on to anymore
So im sorry but i tried my best to stay strong
But now all i can do is say good-bye
