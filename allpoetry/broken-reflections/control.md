---
title: "Control"
writer: "Broken Reflections"
slug: "control"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Would you please not touch me like that
Because each time you do,
I cant control the feelings that run through my heart,
That run through my entire soul
The feeling of wanting you, but knowing i cant have you
Because you say that you dont want to be had
But than why do you make me feel this way,
why do you fill my heart with so much joy
And if you dont want me, than why dont I just walk away
I know, because somewhere in the back of my mind I think im in love
Or at least happy that i can finally feel something,
And now that i do i just dont want it to stop
I just dont want to stop wanting you
So than you know what, I change my mind
Would you please touch me again
Because I like the fact that i can't control the feeling that run through my heart
That run through my entire soul
