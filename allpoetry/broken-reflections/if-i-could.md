---
title: "If I could"
writer: "Broken Reflections"

If I could tell you how I feel, 
I would do it without hesitation, 
Yet every reason in the world tells me not to
The love that you give me makes feel so important, 
Yet at times that same  love can make me feel so inadequate
There was a time when your touch would make everything better, when you would make me feel as if we were the only two in the world 
When a single glance could take away any hint of loneliness I could have ever felt
When your presence would make me feel so complete, as if you were the onlyone for me
Now those feelings have disappeared, 
Our love for each other has slowly faded away
So if I could tell you how I feel, 
Would it make a difference, 
To a love once had, 
To a love now lost
But if I could tell you, would you understand, 
Could we ever find a way back to each other's hearts.
