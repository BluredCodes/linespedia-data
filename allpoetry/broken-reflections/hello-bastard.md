Hello bastard, 
Tell me....
Did you ever think that the shoe would be on the other foot
Did you ever think that i would have a gun to your head
So come on quick, tell me what your thinking
Are you wishing that you never touched me,
That you never raped me
Tell me, are you watching your whole life passing before your eyes,
Naw your probably labeling yourself as the victim, a victim of circumstance
My circumstance
But wait dont you wanna hear what you did to me, 
Dont you wanna knowwhat you taken from me,
How numb i am inside out because of you
How i dont know how to feel,
That i dont know how to function in a simple reletionship, because of you
You dont, 
Hey, im sorry, maybe you wanna hear how you robbed of my soul
Oh come on, 
Dont you wanna play, 
Remember all the games you made me play
Even when I didnt want to, i remember saying no, dont you
But now we are playing my game, and the stakes are much higher
Because in my game, 
When i kill you, I can finally kill a part of myself
So you see than you must die, 
Because in everyone ofmy dreams i kill you,
