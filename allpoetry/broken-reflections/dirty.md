---
title: "Dirty"
writer: "Broken Reflections"
slug: "dirty"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Have you ever felt so dirty,
That no matter how much  you washed yourself,
You  know that you will be clean
That no matter how hard you try ,
You can't rub off the stench of his cologne,
Can't erase his invisible fingerprints off your body
You just know that you will never be clean again,
And that you will never feel safe inside yourself
Because you don’t even know who you are
And you don’t want to know
Because you're afraid that you just might define yourself in him
You just might become him
Well I don’t want that,
I don’t want to feel, to remember,
I want to stop hearing his voice,
I want to stop feeling the touch of his hands
I just want to be clean 
I just want to feel safe inside myself again
