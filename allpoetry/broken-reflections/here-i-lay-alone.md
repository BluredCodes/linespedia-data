title: "Here I Lay Alone"

Here I lay alone in my bed and all i can think about is you
Its like a reaccuring dream that I cant get out of my mind
Where i can still feel every faint touch, 
Taste every sweet kiss, 
Hold on to every soft whisper
And once again your laying beside me like an angel that I can only hope but to touch
Though your eyes said that you wanted to stay forever, 
Your heart told me that you must go
So here I lay alone, only thinking about you
Its like a reaccuring dream that tries to hide so deep inside my mind
Like a memory i hold oh so dear
So i cant help but reach out for every soft touch, 
Still cry out for every daughting kiss, 
still charish every chilling whisper
Wishing that once again i could live inside your arms
Because even though your eyes wanted to promise me forever, 
I knew your heart could no longer love me
So as the nite turns into day and once again into nite, 
I lay alone in my bed, 
With the silent, quanit memory of you
