---
title: "You Love Me"
writer: "Broken Reflections"
slug: "you-love-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/51629-You-Love-Me-by-Broken-Reflections"

Even in my darkness, i feel your light
Even in my pain, I feel your love
Because even though it took me so long to relize,
I know that with you by my side i will never be alone
That you will never hurt me,leave me, lie to me, or betray me
You love me for me, and thats all that matters
