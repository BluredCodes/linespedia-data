---
title: "My Fears"
writer: "Broken Reflections"
slug: "my-fears"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I fear love and happiness,
And the simple fact of knowing that when you get it,
It always seems to slip away

I fear being completly open with anyone,
Scared that no matter how much you trust,
The person you love is always going to hurt you

I fear the pain and anger that i hold so deep inside
Though the world does not see it,
It's always there

But most of all i fear you,
The way you make me feel,
Down to your very touch

I fear that you will never undstand me,
That you will never see the person,
Standing rite in front of you
