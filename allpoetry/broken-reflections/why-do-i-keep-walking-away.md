Why do I keep walking away,
To only find myself coming rite back
Do I just like the drama?,
Or maybe I just like to watch the enviable happen,
You hurt me, I try not to hurt you
And once again the endless cycle is born
Or maybe  you just want to see me in pain
Maybe you get some sick joy out of watching me cry,
Whatever it is  I just keep finding myself coming back for more
But more of what?,
Love, if that’s what you like to call it
Because that’s not what I feel when you hurt me
So than tell me why do I keep walking away
To only find myself comming rite back ?
