Whoa, who said anything about love,
Because for damm sure it wasnt me,
I mean you are a great person, 
And a even better kisser
But i was just using you,
You know to just pass the time,
And i know that sounds mean,
But i told you a long time ago that i cant fall in love,
Shew, im lucky if i feel a basic emotion,
So im sorry you fell in love with me,
But thats your fault,
And basically your problem,
Because now that I know that you love me,
I have no other choice,
But yo just let you go
