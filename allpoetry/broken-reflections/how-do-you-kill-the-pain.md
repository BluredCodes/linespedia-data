If i knew how to kill the pain
I would do it right now,
I wish i knew how to kill the pain
Could someone show me how

Becasue the world keeps on moving
While im still livin yesterday
Keeps on moving on without me,
Moving faster than i can say

The nightmare that run through my mind
Before i can say how i feel
So i can finally stop lying to myself
So i can finally be real

With who i am
With who i thought i might be
If i explained, would you listen?
Sometimes it hurts to be me

So i put a smile on my face
So the whole world can see
The person i thought i am
The person i thought i had to be
