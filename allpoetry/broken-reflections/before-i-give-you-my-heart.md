---
title: "Before I Give You My Heart"
writer: "Broken Reflections"
slug: "before-i-give-you-my-heart"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/53457-Before-I-Give-You-My-Heart-by-Broken-Reflections"

Before i give you my heart,
Can you promise to love me as much tomorrow,
As you do today?
Will you always trust me, no matter what?
Will you always make sure that i can trust you
Because even though you dont think so,
I feel the answers to my questions are important to know
Before i let myself fall to deep,
Because before i can say that i love you,
I have to know that you will be giving me the right kind of love
I have to know that you will always be there to weather the storm
Hold my hand when i cry, and be there when i need you
So before i walk this life journey with you any further
Before i just hand you over my fragile heart,
Please promise me that you will never break it
Look into my eyes and promise me forever,
Tell me that you will love me as much tomorrow,
As you do today,
Because even though you dont think so
I just have the answers to my questions,
Before i just give you my heart
