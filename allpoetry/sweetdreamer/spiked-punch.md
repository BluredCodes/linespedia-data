I raise my head from the side of the ships railing when i realize i shouldn't be here. It's late and there's too much drinking. Anyone could get hurt.

 "Hey r u okay?" someone asks as they messege my shoulder. 

 "yea I'll be fine I'm just a little sea sick." 

 I say trying to get away from him as I regain control of my sight, and of my legs.

 As I try to walk below deck I can feel my legs start to crumble underneath me and my arms feel like jello. I can see people just staring and pointing at me as they laugh behind my back. 

 It's horrible I can't see straight and I'm weaving back and forth and I feel nausiated, dizzy and whatever word you can come up with.

 I finally make it down and I feel for a place to sit because I can't see anymore and I find a hard place to sit, but it's to late I pass out.

 Bt my last thoughts were Ishould of took my mom's advice, never set down your drink and then go back to it after a couple of minutes.i realize this probably sounds more like a short story.

                 sweetdreamer*
