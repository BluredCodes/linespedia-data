title: "Behind the Sorrow"

Behind the tears of sadness,
Lies a heart of mirth.
Awaiting it’s release
To spread joy for all it’s worth.

The sorrow like a locked door,
With a missing key,
But can be slowly opened
After a soulsearching journey.

Joy erupts its own burning light,
Like a spirited flame,
As the door is opened once and for all
And glistens once again.
