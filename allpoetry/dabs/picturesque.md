title: "Picturesque"

Of snow, of ice
Of tainted silver lace
A Dainty snowflake
falling with grace
A crystallised hue
A luscious diamond shroud
An ice-bound tundra
The ceiling dappled with billowy clouds
The indigo sky
An endless unwavering ocean
Scintillating, stilled fireflies
Scattered delicately at random
The softened zephyr whispers
The eerie nights chorus
Shadowed looming pines
Are ghastly gathering ghosts
Hush, Hush
The landscape at rest
The vast horizon, tundra, and surrounding territories.
Picturesque.
