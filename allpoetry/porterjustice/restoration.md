The beginning of time 
is but a moment of unexpected awareness 
cascading down the walls of centuries, 
evolving into a momentous concentration 
of life and strength and enduring remembrance.  
Once, the walls were open to exploration 
by the bold and faint-hearted alike.  
Now, closed to all, they are but the reflection 
of a memory once held by all, 
now forgotten to the masses.  
When shall we again obtain 
the declaration of life's most honored purpose?  
When again shall we reach the apex of understanding
which comes with the contemplation of things eternal?
