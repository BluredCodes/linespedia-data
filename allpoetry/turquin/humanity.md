title: "Humanity"
writer: "turquin"

Humanity,
living, breathing, pulsing;
of warm earth-hewn clay;
eternity enclosed within
tender, silken skin!
Creature of questing,
of passion, undulating,
seething, teeming,
tenderly touching;
a multiple flame
of sex and jealousy, ignited
and of Heaven, inspired.
Enabled for destiny
that's timeless and noble.
So why loll in the depths
and dregs of despair,
O crown of creation -
Why trade soul for cankered trinkets
O fellow heirs o' the cosmos?
