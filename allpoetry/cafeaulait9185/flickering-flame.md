A violent storm rages around me, 
Claps loud and flashes bright 
Branches pounding my window as 
The rain pours down in the deep night. 

I light a single, tall candle 
Blowing the match out with care 
Watching that little flame flicker 
While I climb into my chair. 

I pull my legs tight to my chest 
Imagining what could have been 
Thoughts playing across my mind, 
On strings of an imagined violin. 

My eyes return to the tiny fire as 
It ignites a spark in my heart. 
I am surrounded by dark fury, but 
These black clouds will soon depart.
