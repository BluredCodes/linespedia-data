There is a long road before me,
Some of which I have walked.
I have traveled parts fast and others slowly,
But the rest is yet to come.

There is a winding road before me,
A new adventure around each turn.
Each curve presents a different mystery,
Which I soon may solve.

There is a hilly road before me,
Great dips and gigantic highs.
Occasionally the path will become icy,
But I can always climb up.

There is an imperfect road before me,
Having its share of potholes and bumps.
But so often it has unbelievable beauty,
I wish to travel it forever.There is a lot of symbolism here--this whole poem is a metaphor.
