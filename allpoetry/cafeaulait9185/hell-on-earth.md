title: "Hell on Earth"
writer: "CafeAuLait9185"

Told to go home,
But with nowhere to turn,
All she could do was cry out.

Her dreams had died
With the slams of his fists
And the pounding of flesh in the night.

She's broken and bruised,
But no one could care less
About this innocent girl's pain.

She needed the help
That no one had offered
She'd die by his hand--or her own.

Dark sunglasses
Cover her eyes, not the tears--
She can even see through it herself.

She weeps in the street
Since she cannot with him
Or else she'd get hurt even worse.

But the agony of defeat
Of this poor woman's soul
Can be remedied only by death.

She'll take his gun,
And put an end to it all.
Maybe she'll be happy when dead.

No one will know
Of this horribly tragic tale--
None of them have interest in her.

Just a weak girl
With no future nor past
Who's been living in hell all this time.

She'll be out of the way,
And he'll do it once again
To someone with no clue what he is.

Sleep well, sweetheart.
Let your soul be at peace.
You don't deserve this anymore.

But be sure to leave room
Once you've arrived in heaven--
His next wife will be joining you soon.The horrors of marital abuse.
