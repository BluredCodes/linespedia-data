In these moments before I sleep,
I pray to God my heart you'll keep.

Of you I'll dream before I wake~
On a sandy beach my hand you'll take...

...Or maybe we'll float in sunset skies~
On a cotton ball cloud forever we'll lie...

...We could stroll over the sloping hills~
At the end of time we'd be there still...

...Visit a faraway land on a flying rug~
On it we'd cuddle, snug as two bugs...

...Travel all of the seven seas~
Just as long as you're there with me...

...And in the morning when I arise
I'll remain all day in this paradise.This is so cheesy it's almost embaressing...
