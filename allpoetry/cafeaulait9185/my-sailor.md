I never thought you'd leave me there

On the platform in the rain.

My coat was drenched, my hair was soaked

And you just boarded that train.



The only choice you'd given me was to

Shed a few more tears

While I watched you speed away to

Leave me with my fears.



You had promised that you loved me and would

Stay for my whole life

And I was certain that you'd have soon

Asked me to be your wife.



I was being punished for a war that

Never was my fault

But because some strangers started it

My life was put on halt.



A year ago one of those strangers visited 

To bring an envelope for me.

But still I haven't broken the seal for 

I know my sailor is lost at sea.I should note that this isn't my personal experience... it's writing from a character that I created.  My greatest apologies to all who thought this was a true story.  Thank you for reading.
