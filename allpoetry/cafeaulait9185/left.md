I am the last of the living.

Walking corpses suffocate me,
Hollow eyes staring through my soul.
My heart aches for the lost.
The rest have ceased their beating--
No longer do I hear the thump of pulse.

I am the only one giving.

Forever wandering with no destination.
They'll recognize the place when they arrive.
Green fungus destroyed them.
It had always been life itself--
No longer do I hear the jingle of change.

I am slipping.

Their trivial temptations overcame them.
They are no happier now than before.
The sun has been blackened, flowers have withered.
This lonely, parched planet crumbled to dust--
No longer do I hear the song of laughter.

I had been the last of the living.
