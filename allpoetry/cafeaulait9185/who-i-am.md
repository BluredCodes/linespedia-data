I am no one but myself.I am not a teacher, physicist,or Internet tycoon, any more thanI am an insane scientist.I am who I am.I will not always be content,I might tell lies or the truth,and sometimes my patience is spent.I cannot be changed.I do not like the color pink.Though love is supposedly blind,My heart, once in a while, starts to sink.I have my hopes and dreams.They may not always make sense.I don't like phony people andCan't stand any false pretense.I need to be accepted.All I want to be is freeFrom that illusion put forward.All I want is to be me.
