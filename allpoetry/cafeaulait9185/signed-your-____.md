I am tired of this treatment from you.

You have stepped all over me
and you have dirtied my soul.

When I get the least bit worn out,
you simply discard me like nothing.

You will open my mouth and
pull out my tongue.

You tie me up so that
I don't wander away

You're controlling and will
only let me travel in a pair.

Men will give us no attention
if we are tall.

You'll abandon me if I'm not
the right color or size.

And as soon as I start to smell nice,
you throw some powdery stuff at me.

I am tired of this treatment from you.

Signed,
