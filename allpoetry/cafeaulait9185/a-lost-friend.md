title: "A Lost Friend"

You listened when I needed help
And did your best to give advice
You always treated me so well
You always seemed so nice.

Sometimes you made me worried, but
I gave you endless support
We had an incredible friendship
Until it was cut short.

One day when we were talking
Something said just didn't fit
And then I finally figured out
How much truth you did omit.

I'm hurt, I'm sad, I feel betrayed
In my eyes you have died
All this time I thought you a friend
But all you did was lie.
