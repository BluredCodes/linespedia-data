We walk beneath to meet below
Branches of our weeping willow

Velvet shadows hold us, hidden-
Love's empassioned dance forbidden

Kept apart by parents unkind
Yet here we lie, bodies entwined

The beauty of your moonlit face
Enchanted in our warm embrace

Kisses deep and touches tender
Two hearts lost to sweet surrender

Linger here in blissful crime,
In moments left untouched by time.First love poem in a while....  First one I actually like.
