title: "No One But You"

I once thought about you-
Now you own my thoughts;
I once wanted to be with you-
Now you contain all of me;
I once hoped to be loved by you-
Now you barely give me a glance.

All this time, I have dreamt
Of no one but you;
My eyes have been set
On no one but you;
My heart keeps beating
For no one but you.

You have bewitched me,
You have consumed me,
But you have never loved me.

She has entranced you,
She has engulfed you, 
And she has always loved you.

Now I watch you,
Happy together;
Now I miss you,
In all your splendor;
Now I leave you--
Farewell forever.
