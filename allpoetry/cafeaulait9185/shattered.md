title: "Shattered"

You played the strings of my heart
But it didn't mean a thing to you.
You smashed me like a guitar in a rock star's concert.

You left me with nothing to do
But lie shattered on the floor.
You didn't even stop to pick up the destroyed bits.

You just kept going on your way,
But I couldn't move at all.
You moved on and all I could do is watch in pain.

You have been gone for a long time now
But all I can do is hope you'll return.
You left me broken, but I wish that you'd break me once again.
