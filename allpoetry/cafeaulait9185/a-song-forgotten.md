There's so much noise
Getting harder to think
The dirt turning turquoise
The cloud becomes pink

Life's meaning has left
All of it's mixed up
Overwhelmed with cause and effect
Inside this sorry dump

I'm losing this battle
No matter how I fight
Being led like cattle
In this low moonlight

Swimming against the current
But it keeps dragging me along
Drowning but burnt
