I'll poke holes in your walls,
For I may be caged--
But I can still BREATHE
And you can never stop me.

Long after my body dies,
My heart will still PULSE.
The sound of drums
Will deafen your ears.

And so, the beat will go on,
Although drowned by the tears
That fill this sad place.
I SWIM from the boundaries,
Away from your petty rules.

Not only can I 
Think outside the box--
I AM outside the box.
I am UNRESTRAINED.

These are your tears,
Not mine!

Consider me gone already.
Gone by choice,
Not by chance!
