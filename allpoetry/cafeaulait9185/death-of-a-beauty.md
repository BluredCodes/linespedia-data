Sparkling sapphire
Dangling from a golden chain
Swinging from

Side to Side

With no escape,
Just going

Round and Round

To no avail
And losing her shine
With every movement

Back and Forth

Until there is nothing
But a lonely, tarnished rope
And the stone-cold shell
Of what was once
A gem.Not a jewel...
