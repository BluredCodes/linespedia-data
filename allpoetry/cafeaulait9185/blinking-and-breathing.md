He stares off into space
At the life of the world to come.

Swallowed by the deep end of life
And waiting to be spit out.

His soul is bitter, and so are his lies,
For he has been abandoned.

Forgotten and angry he sits alone
With only the room and its air for company.

Tears stream down his beautiful face,
Yet another victim of quiet desperation.

But as he looks around,
There is a smile
In his wet salty eyes.

His cheeks brighten
As he blinks
And breathes
And knows:
