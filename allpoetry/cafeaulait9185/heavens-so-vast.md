I lie alone in the soft green grass,Staring up at the black velvet skyWhile overwhelmed by emotions of glass.Gazing at stars placed by faeries, I dream of our old times togetherAnd long for you to return beside me.I wish to be a glittering jewel,Far away from this lonely planet.How could you leave and be so cruel?A bright white diamond flies past.I think of you and begin to wonderWhere your place is in these heavens so vast.I'm not sure about this one...
