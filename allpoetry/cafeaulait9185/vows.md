Marriage is a bond between
couples young and old.
To show your love, to say your vows,
exchange your rings of gold.

When you're home, you'll share your lives,
your families, your hearts.
The way you love each other so, 
you'll never want to part.

Together you'll walk down a road
of both love and pain.
Together through the sunny days,
and through the clouds of rain.

Together you'll be forever,
many times to be grand,
As you're walking with each other
down that road, hand in hand.I wrote this when I was only 13 for my uncle's wedding, and my mom made me get up and read it before the ceremony.  I was NOT happy about that...  :-)
