Sleep (noun)-

The sole experience
In which you can see
With closed eyes
And not even do it
On purpose;

A period of time
When you cannot be plagued
By the troubles
Or the dilemmas
Of everyday life;

The best form of earthly escape
And the only one
Available to all--
Rich, poor, old, young--
(especially the bored);

The experience I desire most
But is just out
Of my grasp,
Which almost comes
but slips away.




To: My neighbor's dog,
Which obviously doesn't know
What sleep is
Or we'd both be doing it
Right now.LOL don't ask
