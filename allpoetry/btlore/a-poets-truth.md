Would you forgive a poet,
  His eyes fixed on romance,
For letting truth escape his stare
  And honesty his glance?
For always seeing clearly
  Those things not really there,
For saying that he loves you,
  Confusing love with care.
It may be true he loves you
  Although it should be known,
Poets have no flare for truth
  So they create their own.None
