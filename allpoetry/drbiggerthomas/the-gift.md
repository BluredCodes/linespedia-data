MY 1st gift
to u will
be the soft sound
of my honest whisper
a loving glance into ur eyes
a chance to feel 
a waterfall within
ur sensuously thickilicious body
   
My 1st gift to u 
will be understanding
warm lips planted on ur cheek
firm hands caressing
spots enlivened with hurt
a promise
sealed with 
the gentility of these words

My 1st gift 
to u
will hold
in the sky of ur mind's eye
will dance  bewteen the slow 
rhythms of a new love's
heart beat
will cleanse
will exhilarate
will build small pieces of
pulsation
on warming flesh

my 1st gift
to u
will
be like u
special
drenched with intelligence
beautiful, caring
full with incessant
possibilities
my 1st  gift to u
will be ..........
something like this
