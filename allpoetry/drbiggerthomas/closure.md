title: "closure"

close those eyes     which have seen    so many tired passers bye     rich with bullshit, promises, and games     of which u never deserved      close those eyes    and feel this     distant hand massage    the wounds of ur well protected heart   close ur eyes     and know that these words   were given birth   by the loveliness which is     you    deep like drawl which u hear around those   parts     close those eyes     and think of nothing     which does not honor     chersish     or celebrate   the divinity of your strength   the wonderment of your love     the moistness of your lips   close those eyes  and know     that love is just a    click       or point     away smiling     as you closed your  eyes
