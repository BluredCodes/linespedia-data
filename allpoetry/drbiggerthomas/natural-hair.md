I stare into this convivial darkness
absorbed and woozied
from the extended stilled flashes
of a faultless smile 
perfected skin
pure unadulterated attractiveness
mixed with silky black natural hair
my fingers ache
my soul soon will tremble
my imagination boogies
with mysterious
caramelic rhythms 
silky black natural hair 
accented by 
lips
lissom, tendered, soften
internal murmurs call to me
from some indistinct place
where strangers laffs
at large smiles birthed from childly exhilaration
my eyes enravished by its incomparable beauty
black
silky

hair
