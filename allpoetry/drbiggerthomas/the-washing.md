i will wash u

in my love

gently

from ur the tips

of ur toes

to the cresindo*

of ur breasts

warm and supple

easing away

ur need

to sip from the ashtray

of ur past

i will

tend to ur arms

with a special

wata

made by the tears

of the greatest love

with

one aim

to wake up

and gaze into

ur soul`s windows

4eva

to whisper

my secrets

my insecurities

my affection

i will wash

ur belly

with strong has

filled with

responsibilty and humor

caress ur neck

with this

scented soap

that i will only

share with u

i will

wash

aways

everything

that does

not make u

tingle

wet

smile

i will.....



come kiss me

in the dark caliginous

crevices of your mind

with lips sweet

as morning cups of Southern KOOL aid

skin as malleable as the Lord' love on Sunday

come kiss away

my ruff spots

feel the tongue

of my life congregate

in the estuary of

Firsts&

love,

come kiss

me

like lovers who loved

loving

like angels doing

the messiah's industry

come kiss me with

all that you have

til your pulse increase

til your flesh warms

til  your toes curl

come kiss me

like i was the last mickey

on earth

like i feed your love spirit

wetting

umbra

come kiss me

see my future

canoodling

into the the nite dies

and orgasms are born

within the walls

of your untouched chambers

come kiss me

please

step out of this

life

and

come kiss me
