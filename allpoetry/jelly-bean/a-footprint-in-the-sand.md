title: "A footprint in the sand"
writer: "Jelly Bean"

A footprint in the sand is like
A soothing voice in the dark
Like the unknown path you've stolen into my heart

A footprint in the sand is a simple I love you
So in my reply I have to tell the truth
That I've always loved you and will past the day I die 
And in this I tell you I swear I won't lie

Footprints in the sand are secrets untold
Or anything beautiful that you constantly behold

Footprints in the sand are dreams that come true 
An example of that is “My finding you.”

A footprint in the sand is a day gone past 
But that doesn't mean the memory won't last

And in these footprints I've made clear to you
I hope to make more walking right beside you.
Kelly Denise Oharah
