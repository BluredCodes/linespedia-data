title: "Kiss 1"
writer: "Jelly Bean"

The first kiss is usually the worst,
You're so full of hormones you could just burst.
You feel as though you're the only two in the room,
Anticipating his touch that will be so new to you.
You've been holding hands all night and day,
your palms sweaty with no words to say.
So nervous about that first move
You advance ever so slowly to see if he will approve.
At last you lose all patience and dive right in,
Thinking, "at last I've experienced a heavenly sin"
But in moments you ask yourself what have I done,
And in this same instance your heart falls to your stomach like a ton.
Should I open my eyes or keep them closed?
And in all the commotion you've squashed his nose.
Should I keep it simple or use my tongue?
I'm not quite sure if that was how this is to be done.
Then when it's all over and you think you're through
you don't believe that girl kissing him was you.
And when you're really sure that it is done
You've forgotten the new feeling and how it was won
