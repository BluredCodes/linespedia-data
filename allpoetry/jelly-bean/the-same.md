---
title: "The same"
writer: "Jelly Bean"
slug: "the-same"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/38001-The-same-by-Jelly-Bean"
---

Life and death are so much alike,
You never know when either may strike.

Two teenagers in the back of a car or
a middle aged man dies in a fight at the bar.

Newly weds on their wedding night,
Or an old man dies holding your hand so tight.
Life and death are never mistakes,
They are planned out in our sub-conscious as our fate.

What happens, happens.
What's done is done.
Think about what you've read because it's true and you can't run.
