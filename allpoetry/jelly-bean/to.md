TO:
The father I never knew,
I want you to know that I love you.
I hope you think of me sometimes,
I hope you love me too.
I wish I could of heard your voice
or reflect on a memory 
but all I have is a single picture that Janet gave to me. 
Sorry this is so short but I have to go. 
I think of you always,
sure as the wind will blow.
LOVE,Kelly(Amanda)
