You used to hate me,
We used to really cuss,
Then we became friends, which would be surprising enough,
I gave you everything, which was my trust,
You were my best friend for always,
Never again was our hatred discussed

I helped you find love with the one you wanted
But you never realized the jealousy of which I was taunted
And when she left you I searched for new pieces to put together,
When I knew in my heart that mine would fit better,
But now were finally together,
No more jealousy
And all that matters to me now is that you love me
