title: "Liv-Confused"

The day I began to live was also the day I died
This was a tragic step because I was just five
Now I'm nearly 18 an adult who knew too soon
my definition of an adopted child growing up would be;confused. 
I just wish I knew where to start 
For 18 long years my father and I have been apart
Does he remember me, think of me,or care that I care
Daddy come back to me oh I wish I could know
Maybe you know him so I've described him here just below
He's Theodore Price with blonde curly hair
He's got 5 children I tell you I swear
3 girls and 2 boys whom he left behind
I'm sure if he came to see me I really wouldn't mind. 
I hope someday to hear his voice,
although others think I shouldn't,
that would be my choice.
