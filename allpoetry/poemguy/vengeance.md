Bombs raining downWithout a soundPeople screaming chaosIn our corrupt worldVengeance is hailing,Falling on the peoples browA end is in the makingWith many souls up for the takingSay good-byeAnd one last prayerSave your lost soulsShed your tearsBlood is pouring downFrom all around this landGod bless us all'Cause we're about to fall.
