title: "Daddy Gave Me A Bath"

staring out my window
voices fill my mind
reminding me of the place I went
when I was only nine

daddy took me to the bathroom
for my daily bath
but this time it was different
i didn't play and laugh

daddy made me touch him
in places i didn't want to
i don't know why he did it
i guess it was punishment

i didn't know daddy could rape me
i was a boy to
how could he have done this
to me his son

i screamed and yelled
and he wouldn't go away
some days i think about that
why i couldn't be strong

i tried pushing daddy away
but he just kept holding on
"leave me alone!" I screamed
but all he did was laugh

i don't think i'll ever forget the day
daddy gave me a bath
