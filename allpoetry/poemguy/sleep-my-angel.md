sleep my angel
dream away
take yourself back
to yesterday

find peace and rest
in the mornings dew
think of us as we were
not as we are

sleep my angel
don't go away
we need you here
god wait another day

my love has breathed her last
this day as it came to pass
a calming look pierced her brow
as i sit repeating

sleep my angel, sleep
