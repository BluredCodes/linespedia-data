staring out my windowlooking at the cloudsfeeling the hate and angerthats developed somehowstreets are filled with hatredbusinesses covered in greedthis is the devils worldhe is here to succeedwhat can i do to stop the violence?what can i do to stop the pain?how many lives will be lost?how many will die in shame?is their an answer for the world today?or do we just lie awake questioning as we pray?we are a free peopleyet we try and drag that astrayamerica where are you?stand above this landmake us proud of youas you show the world your mighty hand
