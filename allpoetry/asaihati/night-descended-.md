[]Night Descended []

>

When night descended,
And
The universe got narrow in my eyes,
The stars shacked,
The moon hided between the clouds and the elegiac poetry,
**
There was a ring in my finger,
Brings delight ness to who sees it,
In it, sets a red corundum,
**
She drank form my soul,
And got drank form the wine,
I water its' grapevine the purest waters,
**
And when night descended
At once the eyebrows blinked,
continually
I stopped,  monitoring the sky,
**
Gloomy takes sides with sorrow
more then the pureness,
**
I went to explore
the exits of secretes of the sky,
**
I brought a pencil,
Papers,
And started collecting the picture,
**
Its colors, mixed the dark and the bright,
**
What a Perplexity I was in
I wish I did not look at the sky
**
My body trembled,
And the litters shacked at the say,
My sides crossed,,
**
The ring striped from my finger,
And the red corundum denied it,
**
I turned back,
No papers,
No pencil,
No ring,
And
No drawing on the water,
**
I weathered back smothered teardrop,
***
**
*
I did not cry for the shacked words
I did not cry for broking pencil,
I did not cry for turned paper,
****
**
Do you know
what makes me cry???
**
Do you know
why the teardrop, smothered???
**
Do you know
why the ring striped from my finger???
**
Do you know
why the red corundum denied ring???
**
And do you know
why the Gloomy takes sides with sorrow,
more then the pureness???
**
And do you know
why the eyebrows blinked, continually???
**
Do you know why
the moon hided between the clouds and the elegiac poetry???
**
Do you know
why the universe got narrow in the eyes???
*******
*****
**
*
When you are legible to know
you will weathered back with a smothered teardrop,
**
And you will know
this is your cry when you said good bye.
**
for me
its enough drowning my steps to fly in another sky
**
You might or you might not see me again,
and
If it happened I wont be your gain.
