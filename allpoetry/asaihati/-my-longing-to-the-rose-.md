I have a longing to the rose,From the time kept appearing on the way,Dismisses me a sweet longing,**Remained me of hills,Some hours past,My soul,Became a chilled,Between valley, and streams,**there is a sad cloud,splashes of perfume,some time on the rose and some other time on your face,**Hitting the rose and give her a wet cool look,AndHitting your face,AndThe soul of love,AndRoads get the love,**wet ten the valley,to make it a sweet garden,love puts on the cloth of love’s soul,**Quite hearts meet,Shake,Quench all the thirst of the passing by(s)**My Rose,Your cute face,Look at the cute love,-------------------------------------------------}--------------------------------}------------------}Sun of Sa
