[] Contraries []



>





Hidden my feelings

I cant,,, for you,

Your droppings the ball

My love it true,,







When I learn how to fish,

I throw the rope farthest and get the biggest fish,,

Called just my lines,

The trees should sew,,







You know me well,

my love is true,,

If you needed one,,

my self is due,,







With you

my life, for me and you,,,

Love me

Loving you,,,,

Confirming the contrary is true,,
