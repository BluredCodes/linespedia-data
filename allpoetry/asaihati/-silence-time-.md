[] Silence Time []

>

Nights and nights passed by,
our link,
a strong wall,
its love link,
**
If I say I love you,
My voice will be hanged,
And
If I keep my mouth shot,
Sorrow will attaché me,
**
after and when I was,
nights and nights passed bye,
and our link,
its love link,
I would be strong enough to pass the hardships,
Now I know my power,
**
but and to let the silence time leave,
