[] Roses Rise []







There was a secrete in my chest boiling,

I prevent it and hide it,

So no one can see it,



And when ever it appears  in front of me,

The fear fell me and luck my tongue,



As if I'm at revelations of sea waves or a volcano,



I did not hide it, but I fear the people of my time,

And who is like me also feared others of his/her time,



My heart could not hold accumulate my secrete,

Nor the singers accumulate my soul,

I headed to the farm and it was dark and I was lonely,

To Barry  my secrete,

As if they Barry a devil,

I saw night became my victim,

It cried and made me cry,



There are tears for the nights no one sees them,

Even the eyes cannot see,



I fight with my felling,

As if we are in war,



Time of been away is gone and time of getting close came,



I bandaged my soul,

And what worries the people worries me,

I don't see in the drink a meaning,

And how many meaning in it,



I felt as if I'm the machine of the era,

Or any of the dishes,



I became like the rock with no feeling,

And the builder and destroyer are the same,



The garden covered my secret and all my wishes are covered,



Its lost when some thing is lost from my soul,

And I think my soul is lost,



And one day I had another secrete that took me to

The same garden,



When I reach to the place where I barred my first secret

I seen a rose been grown there



And I believed in my moment

if you

plant a rose you get a rose
