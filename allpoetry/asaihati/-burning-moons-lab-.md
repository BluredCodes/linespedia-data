Heat is generated inside my heart
Like a burning fire

***

Left the chest boons like ashes
*
I became a class room and laboratory for the time
Experiencing
hearts and levers must be burned
*
How does the heart get safe or be in peace
*
Been used to the sorrow
 And
 dissolved in it's components
With out the feeling
I
Did not get the pain
I
wish this heart
Is
A solid substance
How won't I cry
While there are tears in my eyes

***

How won't I cry
And the rowans and burns in my heart

***

Only few people
have patience
*
Just a moment from when I start laugh
My laughing becomes cold and siring
*
And my long asleep
become my awake ness
*
God
when you
 created those affecting things!
Why
 did not you
 created hearts from soled metals?
*
At
 every time I say
I found my love
And my life
Will have no sorrow
A burning moon comes around
and leave us a part
*
After that
 I gain extra
And deeper
 wounds and burns
*
my long sleep wont be a sleep
*
my frit end heart well be strong
*
Flow whenever
 love
flow
Patience,
Tears,
Blood,
Or,
fire
And
Become
A Burning Moon's Lab
