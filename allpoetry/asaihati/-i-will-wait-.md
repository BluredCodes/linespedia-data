[] I will wait []

>

The night went so smooth and fine,
I have on mined lots of the day’s loads,
My head is that much heavy to carry,
When I come to put my head on the chair to relax I feel so pain,

The sweeties thing a man do feel is to rest after the hardship,
But what is the reason I could not put my head on the chair,

Surprising ,
What did I eat,
What did I drink,
What did I inhale

The odor of perfume is nice,
It make the senses really relax,
Something in the soul, called the spot of the smell,
This thing have sometime that make it different,

I’m sure it is the perfume of love,

I just don’t know,

All I know is love’s perfumed killed my pain,
I like it
I inhale it,
I hug it with both wings,
I just cant sleep,
I want to wait tell I meet again,
Non the night or the day,
Could do what this perfumed made
So
Let me stay tell the time we meet again
