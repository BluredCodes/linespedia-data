[] Strawberry Words []

>

LOVE ME,
It is the deepest word of what I said,
I removed the ties from
My soul,
**
LOVE ME,
It might be our fates,
Gathers us,
And
Your branches,
Rooted in me,
Stitches,
**
LOVE ME
With out lies,
Or cheating,
With no words,
**
LOVE ME,
When,
Winter comes,
Under the words of love,
Cover me,
**
Show me,
What the souls’ revels
And
the clay,
LOVE ME,
FOR
Heart in me,
Is a Master,
