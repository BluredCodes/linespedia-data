[]You Planted the Longing []



  

You Planted 

the Longing Flowers in my passion

Words and exhaustion, that roves inside



You water it

 form your eyes light Pure, prime 

choice, of pen inks,you given the whole



hope,you put

the hardest time in front co-op,

you keep affection shaded,and opened the 



flowers fields,

Longing aperès in your heart,Fly

in the air,Grads of the sky protects you,



you offered 

a soul,Your eyes, the Shining stars,

Lend some more to the stars,make their 



light up and

glow,decorateroads in my may,I swear 

your plant is green,Your waving flag white



When time

passes by,You planted 

the longing,flowers in my passion
