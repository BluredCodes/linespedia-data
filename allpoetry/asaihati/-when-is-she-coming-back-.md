[] When is She Coming Back? []



A repeated question



Feels sorrow with letters





I asked the rain

I asked the shore sand

I asked the sea and the waves

I asked the trees and the levees





All of them showed their sadness and homesickness





At that time I heard my sound reflected to me, but

The sound of bird joined and made it stronger,





My love has the bird’s 

sound with the rhythm

When she whispers

 the letters are love story,





When I remembers her teardrops 

floods worm form my heart,

My wishes the effect 

and the wends put the fire off,





The bards flock together

I asked them where you are from

And do you know





The calmness shredded my soul

Left me to the stars with my sites

Look in the miseries of there paths,

She might be there in our sky,





Or might be hidden behind the seen,

Together with the lonely stars,

Or maybe she is gone

 and never will come back,





I Asked her star

Where is she?

Where did the softly sound went,





That angle’s look where to be found,

The sites slowly went down

The promises are  not from the features of the unknown.





My chronic wounds did not leave me

Burning flame between my chests,I sewer If you come back,

And the calendar days covered with love,





My boat will fight the waives

and if not, I will be going back 

asking the days,







It’s a repeated question feels sorrow with letters

There is only one answer

Will always say,





The heart and the soul,

fly with hearts and souls,

It’s not me, who started your burning fire,





It's just became your song

When ever you repeated it,you activate love in your heart, and your soul,keeps on asking



When is she coming back?
