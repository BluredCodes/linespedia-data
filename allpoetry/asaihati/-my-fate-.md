title: "[] My Fate []"

If the fates went away from me,
Be,
The water of my fate,
**
and tell me with out shyness,
and tell me with out a tie,
**
you became a master,
and
with fates you get made and play,
you
raise a roof,
no harmless, and hardship,
**
from the fate
what purest,
lovely rain at the dawn,
**
from the fates,
a hope,
design the mentality, and the thoughts,
**
If the fates went away from me,
Be,
The water
of my fate.
