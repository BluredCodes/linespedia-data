[] A trip in my life []>A strong wend,andextracted the sea dock the waves,extracted all the greens and dry,people move out for the seek of safety,I moved too,I found my self between a peal of sands and sands,I used to have a garden in my home,I cut the biggest flower, and kept it to dry,when people were moving it was in my hand,I pored from my eye water,form the goodness we are in,I found the flowers started growing,and a garden in the desert is something,just hang on there,you'll read lots of those wonders when you know the taste of love,---------------------------------------------------------------------}----------------------------------}------------}Sun of Sawonders when you know the taste of love
