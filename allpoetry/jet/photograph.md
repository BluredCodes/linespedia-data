title: "Photograph"
writer: "Jet"
slug: "photograph"
source: "AllPoetry"

A snapshot is taken in the blink of an eye,
The image lasts forever, imprinted with dye.
With time it will yellow perhaps even tear.
But it will live in the memories of those who were there.
Through the madness of time, it will remain;
Through earthquakes and floods, always the same.
A picture will stay, through reason and rhyme,
Will stay just a memory, a moment in time.
