---
title: "The World Stood Still"
writer: "Jet"
slug: "the-world-stood-still"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/13732-The-World-Stood-Still-by-Jet"
---

Space and time collide.
The world comes to a halt.
Time stands still for just a second.
And things have never seemed so right.
When I open up my eyes
I see your loving gaze.
Looking down on me so tenderly,
With that smile on your face.
Still glowing from your touch,
I lay my head upon your chest.
And that night when the world stood still
We both learned what love meant.
