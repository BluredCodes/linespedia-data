title: "Another Dumb Blonde?"

I can see it in your eyes.
In the way you talk to me.
It's written all over your face.
The judgement I've been dreading...

Let me guess...

Just another dumb blonde?
I've got nothing in my head.
Easy pickings for your sport...

You're all the same.
Well let me make one thing clear...

I am NOT another dumb blonde.
I am not your tv slut.
I am not a prize for you
to display on your mantlepiece.

I will not sit quietly
As you destory my mind.
I will not let you degrade me
And strip away my self esteem.

I am not another dumb blonde.
I know what I want.
Come see me in ten years or so...

Then we'll see who the dumb one is.Okay..it's late. I got one blonde joke too many and this is the result...let me know if it sucks. :)
