---
title: "You"
writer: "Jet"
slug: "you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/36593-You-by-Jet"
---

If you...
Hold me tight.
If you...
Make things right.
If you...
Whisper in my ear.
Then I...
Have nothing to fear.

If you...
Will always be there.
If you...
Have love to spare.
If you...
Love me for me.
Maybe you...
Can set me free.

You...
Are the one that I love.
You...
Are the one I dream of.
You...
Set my heart free.
You...
Complete me.

I love you.For a special someone. :) I know it's cheesy...Emotional mood.
