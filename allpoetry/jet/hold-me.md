---
title: "Hold me"
writer: "Jet"
slug: "hold-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/24191-Hold-me-by-Jet"
---

In your arms I find my comfort.
Your smile is my hope.
When my world falls down around me,
Only you can help me cope.
In your eyes I see my soul.
Your kiss alone sustains me.
When I'm trapped by my depression,
Only you can set me free.

Hold me.
Hold me through the night.
Tell me everything will be alright.
I don't care if it's all a lie.
Just hold me when I need to cry.

Be there for me when I need you most.
Wrap me in your arms and hold me close.
Wipe the tears off of my face.
Take me away to a better place.
You are my love, you are my light.
My hero, my own daring knight.
I give to you my heart and soul,
For only you can make me whole.

Hold me through the night.
Tell me everything will be alright.
I don't care if it's all a lie.
Just hold me when I need to cry.
