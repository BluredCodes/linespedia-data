Silver strands of moonlight
Through cotton clouds do show,
To dance with the soft starlight
Among the waves below.

Perfect silence wreaths the night,
Save for the waves' soft sighs.
As they tease sandy shores,
Beneath the watchful skies.

The merest whisper of wind
Stirs leaves into a silent ballet.
And ov'r the flickering lights
Of my fire they do play.

With the moon as my guide
I become one with the night,
So peaceful and perfect
