---
title: "Nightmare"
writer: "Jet"
slug: "nightmare"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/13709-Nightmare-by-Jet"
---

Another sleepless night.
Tangled blankets tossed aside.
I can't keep doing this to myself.
Waking up in a cold sweat,
A scream still dying on my lips.
Images as real as anything I've ever seen
Still stuck behind my eyes.
The dawn will not bring comfort.
The light can't chase away these fears.
Only you, with your voice, your touch, your love;
Can rescue me from this hell I've discovered.
Only you can save me from myself.
I want to laugh at the irony
But I'm scared I won't be able to stop.

Do you see what it does to me when you leave?
All these fears manifest themselves
Conspiring against me
Until I don't know whats real or not.
And I wait for your call,
Wearing holes into the floor.
Fighting demons only you can dispell.

Until the phone rings at last.
So loud in the sudden silence,
And finally I hear your voice.
I feel the terror leave me
As it flees your comforting words.
Only to return when you leave again.
And you can never understand it.
