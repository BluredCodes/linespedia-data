title: "Dreaming of Tomorrow"
writer: "Jet"

Dreaming of tomorrow
You never thought it'd come so fast.
But while you're dreaming life moves on
And today becomes the past.

Today you'd never think
That things could change so much,
You never think you'll say goodbye
Or have friends fall out of touch.

But now tomorrow's here
And it's time to say goodbye.
It's time to pack up all your things
And give something new a try.

Do you like it in tomorrow?
Have all your dreams come true?
Or do you still look ahead
To what's in store for you?

Always dreaming of tomorrow
You'll never get to see today.
And while you dream of better things
Your life will slip away.
