If angels dance do people laugh?And what if angels cry?Can people feel an angel's tearsOr hear the angels sigh?What happens when those angelsNeed an angel of their own?Who's there to pick them off the groundAnd set their feet back on the stone?Who comforts angels when they cry?Who wipes away their tears?Stays up and hold them through the nightAnd tries to calm their fears?Even angels can have heroes.Even angels need to cry.And sometimes all they really needIs the strength to learn to fly.To me this kinda sounds repetitive...But I can't really find any other words that flow. Any thoughts?
