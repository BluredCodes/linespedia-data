---
title: "Goodbyes Are Not Forever"
writer: "Jet"
slug: "goodbyes-are-not-forever"
source: "AllPoetry"

A silvery tear on a porcelin face. 
A sad little smile held tightly in place. 
Together they watch, together they wait 
Together they worry for their little girl's fate. 

Trying so hard to remain strong 
In the face of the moment they've dreaded so long 
The hatchling is free to fly from the nest 
Their part is over, she must do the rest. 

Tender hands healed broken hearts and skinned knees. 
Gentle voices gave talks of the birds and the bees. 
Killer homework was dealt with in a logical fashion. 
Comforting hugs, laughter, joy and compassion... 

A silvery tear on a porcelin face, 
As she works to maintain her composure and grace. 
I love you Mom. I love you Dad. 
I'll be okay...don't worry too bad. 

A silvery tear on a porcelin face 
Letting go of that last embrace. 
Through your tears you tell me never 
Believe that good byes are forever...In respone to Sunshine's challenge. It was too good to pass up. Love you mom and dad!
