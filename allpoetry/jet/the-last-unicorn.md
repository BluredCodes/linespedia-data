The last unicorn has fallen.
Her spirit sings no more.
Her beauty and her magic
Belong now to myth and lore.
Elves and dwarves have left this land,
In search of better things;
A safer place, where unicorns
May fly on glittered wings.

In a world where hope is a whisper
Of a dream lost long ago.
Where shattered dreams and promises
Adorn the ground below.
Can one believe in fairy tales
When all the love has fled?
Is magic even possible
When innocence is dead?

The last unicorn has fallen.
