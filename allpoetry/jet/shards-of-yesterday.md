Drowning in a sea of memories.
Where shards of yesterday
Can still hold their sting.
And the hunter becomes the prey.

Persued by the demons of my past.
Hunted by dark wraiths,
That linger like the echoes
Of a long lost faith.

Specters of pain
That I once thought were dead.
Have returned once again
To mess with my head.

Visions of a hurt buried long ago.
The silence broken by screams
And the soft tinkling
Of shattering dreams.

It's only a game.
Idle amusement for you.
If only you had a taste 
Of what you put me through.

The ruins of my life
Collapse around me.
The night steals my hope
