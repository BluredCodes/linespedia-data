I'm begining to wonder,
Just why I bother
Pretending to be 'me' for you.
Why won't you accept
That I've changed who I am?
I'm no longer the 'me' that you knew.
You remember me as
The shy little girl.
Who would let you walk over me.
She grew up sometime,
While you were away.
Is it really that hard to see?
Don't think you can change me.
I've made up my mind.
Some things my life can do without.
So if you can't accept
The new me as I am,
