In the darkness of night 
The shadows gather 
Watching, waiting... 
Ignored by the light. 

Soon time will come 
When the shadows will rule. 
Under cover of night 
The brave will succumb. 

Saturnine hate 
Will fuel this fight. 
Old furies unleashed... 
It's already too late. 

Out of the ashes 
Of wars long forgotten. 
The dead will rise 
To partake in the clashes. 

The more gallent ones 
Will fight for the light, 
But hellspawn and demons 
Have no fear of guns. 

The last vestiges of light 
Are smothered by rage. 
And the dark gates of Hell 
Embrace the eternal night.A response to Fortold-Event's challenge to write a phrophetic poem. :)
