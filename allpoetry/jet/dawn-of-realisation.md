title: "Dawn of Realisation"

In the soft glow
Of the dawn of realisation
I see myself.
Alone.
Unbound...
Unchained...
Unhindered...
Free of all my pain.
Doubt.
Guilt.
Hatred.

Free from you.

As the dawn brings new light
The shadows of your pain
That once wreathed my heart.
Dissolve under the warmth of the day.
And dreams blossom
Once more.
In the fertile soil of my soul,
No longer
Tainted by your poison.

Never again.
I tell myself.
Never again
Will you control me.
Hurt me.
Not you or anyone else.

The dawn has come.
Night will flee its touch.
Creeping back within its shadows.
Never to return.
