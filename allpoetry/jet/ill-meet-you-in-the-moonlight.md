title: "I'll Meet You in the Moonlight"

I'll meet you in the moonlight.
Under the moon's soft glow.
We'll dance among the stars tonight.
And forget the world below.

A picnic in the sky.
A romantic night for two.
A walk through dreams and memories
Of my time spent with you.

We'll ride the comets through the skies
And claim this night as ours.
We'll dance until we fall asleep
Upon a bed of stars.

Do not despair when morning comes,
I'll still be in your heart.
I'll meet you in the moonlight
Whenever we're apart.Take it as you will.. :)
