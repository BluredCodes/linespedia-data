---
title: "Why?"
writer: "Jet"
slug: "why"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Darkness sweeps over me
Leaving me alone.
A shell of what I used to be
With a heart like frozen stone.

A cold and barren landscape
A mere shadow of my life.
Harsh reality with no escape
Every word a twisted knife.

In silent barren fields 
I wander without aim.
Wondering how you got past my shields;
Just how and when you came.

You left my heart in pieces
Lying shattered on the floor.
Not a thought for me you left me here
And walked right out the door.

Why did you leave in such a rush?
Were you afraid to see me cry?
Afraid of what I'd try to do?
When all I want is 'why'.

Why you did the things you did.
Why you felt you had to lie.
Why you tore my heart to shreds.
All I want to know is why.
