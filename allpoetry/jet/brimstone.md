Brimstone eyes keep watch
From their post amid the skies.
Molten tears trace steaming tracks.
Fiery breath heaves silent sighs.

Brimstone eyes, strong and proud
Weep for an age lost to the past.
Leaden heart in a shell of scales
Aware that he's the last.

On guard until the last
Until his wings betray him.
Snarled cries of self denial
Echo like a requiem.

Forever and a day 
He will stand on gaurd.
Perusing the heavens
With his mournful regard.Posted as a response to sidewinder's challenge to write a poem through a dragon's eyes. Way too much fun. :)
