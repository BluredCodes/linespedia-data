---
title: "Broken Wings"
writer: "Jet"
slug: "broken-wings"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

An angel fell from heaven.
And lost his wings along the way.
The scars he bears will tell the tale
Of the time he went astray.

Sweet angel all alone.
In this cold, cruel world.
Broken dreams on broken wings,
That betrayed you once unfurled.

Fall from grace into my arms
Silent tears upon your face.
In mourning for your lovely wings
And the dreams you can't replace.

I will mend your broken wings
And help you learn to fly.
Watch you spread your wings of gold
And leap into the sky.

I will be there when you fall again,
Your golden wings in tatters.
Together we will face the days,
Our love is all that matters.:)
