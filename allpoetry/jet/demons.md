Screaming on the inside
Where no one else can see your pain.
This festering pit of pain denied
Is searching for release.

Hurt like none I've felt before
Tears at me with taloned claws.
My heart is frozen to the core
With a chill I cannot shake.

Monters live in the opaque light
Too horrible for words.
They're reaching out for me tonight.
In my prison of regrets.

Screaming wordless screams
In a cold and empty void.
Trapped amid my broken dreams
