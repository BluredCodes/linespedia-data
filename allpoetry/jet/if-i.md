If I ignore your words,
Will it make them go away?
If I paste a smile on my face
Will you stay another day?

If I cried a thousand tears
And begged you not to go;
Would you stay right here with me,
If I let my feelings show?

If I stand and watch you go,
Will you understand my pain?
If I let you walk away,
