An ordinary friend
Is that all I am to you?
Just someone who is always there
Beside you through and through.

Just a friend you've known forever
Someone to share the blame.
Who knows your darkest secrets
But loves you all the same.

Our friendship just keeps growing
Getting stronger every day.
But still there are some things I feel
Some things I just can't say.

I can't tell you how I love you.
I can't say how much I care.
Cause the simple thought of losing you
Is more than I can bear.

So I leave the words unspoken
But my feelings still hold true,
Hoping that someday soon
You might love me too.May 2000,
