---
title: "Shimmer"
writer: "Jet"
slug: "shimmer"
source: "AllPoetry"

Phantom fingers caress my face,
Echoes of a lost embrace.
Whispers sigh into my ear,
Suddenly so crystal clear.
Faded memories take my hand
And lead me to that forgotten land.

Lost in a dream of long ago,
Delicate as the falling snow.
Siren songs inside my heart;
Silken memories they impart.
Scarlet dreams adorn the ground
In that wistful sea I drown.

Phantom fingers caress my soul.
Reminding me of what they stole.
A part of me was left behind
Into those memories now confined.
My physche once again laid bare
By a shimmer in the air.*shrugs* A walk on the lighter side...sorta :)
