As the embers and ashes spiral 
Ever upwards on the wings of a sigh. 
They leave the flames to watch from far below. 
Wanting only to be so free as they. 
In his rage the inferno burns bright. 
White hot anger sparks into the offending sky. 
But the crackling protests of an earth bound flame
