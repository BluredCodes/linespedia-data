Some say love's an angel
A shadow never seen.
And some say she's an echo
Of things that might have been.

Some say love's a myth
Just a shimmer in the air.
Some say she's a specter
She isn't really there.

Some say she walks among us;
Sweet whispers in our ears.
Some say she stands before us;
To chase away our fears.

Some say that they have seen her.
Some say they've felt her touch.
Some say they've heard words spoken
Of peace and joy and such.

Some will say don't trust the angel
The road she paves is rough.
But love sent you into my life
