title: "Oops?"
writer: "Jet"

source: "AllPoetry"

So you screwed up good this time.
Took it just a tad too far?
Hit the line and kept on running
Never pausing to look back.
And now she's got that look.
The one you've come to dread.

When sorry just won't cut it.
And 'oops' isn't enough.
When she's spitting mad
And out for blood.
What's a boy to do?

Offer pride as sacrifice.
Throw your ego to the wolves.
And admit that you were wrong.
So what if it's not manly.
So what if it's not 'tough'.
It's not that hard a thing to say.
And maybe, just maybe...
It will be enough.Sorry..I know it's not a great poem. Just venting a little...a certain someone hasn't learned this lesson yet.
