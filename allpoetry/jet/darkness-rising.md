Darkness is coming...
Can you feel it?

Darkness draws closer...
Creeping up bit by bit.

Ignore it.
Forget it.
It won't happen here.

Don't worry,
Darling.
There's nothing to fear.

Darkness is brewing...
In the back of your mind.

Darkness is stirring...
Yet you remain blind.

How far
Do they
Need to go to be real?

How many
Young lives
Do they need to steal?

Darkness is moving...
Under cover of night.

Darkness approaches...
Soon hell will ignite.

Go on
Pretending
There's nothing wrong.

Darkness 
Is coming.
You knew all along.

Darkness is coming...
