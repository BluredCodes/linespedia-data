---
title: "Wish Upon A Star..."
writer: "Jet"
slug: "wish-upon-a-star"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/22327-Wish-Upon-A-Star...-by-Jet"
---

I used to stand below the stars
And make my feelings known.
I'd tell the rocks and moutains
How I wished you were my own.

And so the stars above me
Heard all my pleading cries.
It seems that all the fabled myths
Were really only lies.

For it seems the stars are broken.
They no longer hear my prayers.
But watch with cold indifference
As you walk by me unawares.

My faith in love has vanished.
The stars betrayed my trust.
Each time you kiss another's lips
You grind my dreams to dust.

You never knew my love.
You never knew my touch.
All I wanted was a moment.
I didn't ask that much.
