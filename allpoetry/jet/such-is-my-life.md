title: "Such is My Life"

Christmas is coming
But exams will get here first.
I'm in my dorm, away from home
So stressed I think I'll burst.
Sadistic profs take pleasure
From making students squirm.
So many tests and papers.
How I dread the end of term.
Running out of time,
Nothing sticking in my head.
I can't write this! I can't do this!
My heart is full of dread.
The exams come round,
It's too late now.
You can only sit and pray
To make it through somehow.

One down, five more to go.
Will this never end?
All I want is to be at home
With my family and friends.
And so the cycle continues
With no relief in sight.
I'll think of you this Christmas
As I study through the night.:) Exams got the better of me this year...
