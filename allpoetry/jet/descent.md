title: "Descent"

The dream is the sameDesperate. Lost. Alone.So dark it's blinding.So bright it's oppressive.I'm lost, flying, fallingFalling faster.Can't stop. Can't stop!I'm lost in a swirling sea.Sea of words, of emotionsOf 'what ifs' and 'could have beens'It crashes down and buries me.Rips at my soul with clenching fingersCold claws of darkness grasp kicking feetAnd pull with determined mightDown, down, ever downward.Unstoppable spiral into nothing.Abandonned. Alone. Forgotten.Adrift in an ocean of thought.
