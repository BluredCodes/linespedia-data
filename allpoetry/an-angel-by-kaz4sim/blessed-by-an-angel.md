I’d always wanted a daughterTo share special times with meA beautiful little girlWho’d make my life completeWell an angel must have heard meAnd thought I deserved the bestHe chose you my little princessPicked you out of all the restHe gave you love and kindnessAnd beauty not just skin deepHe gave me you my darlingAnd said she’s yours to keepWell that angel kept on watchingAnd that’s when he sent me youA beautiful baby girlThat made my daughters twoHow could I be so deserving?What had I done to earn all thisTwo sweet and loving baby girlsThat made my life so blissThat angel kept on lookingAnd he thought our family funHe put the apple in my eyeWhen he blessed me with a sonNow that angels gone a wanderingTo bless another heartWith love beyond comparisonTo help that family startSo thank you dear sweet angelI’ll not forget you, no, not everThe bond between me and my childrenAre impossible to sever
