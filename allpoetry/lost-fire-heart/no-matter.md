This poem has a special dedication that cannot be ignored. It is dedicated to Anthony Charland. I will always love you.
----------------------------------------------------------------------------

Through good times and bad,
I'll always be here.
No matter where you go,
I will follow you.

No matter how hard you try,
I'll always love you.
And in loving you,
I'll be the closest friend.

I'll open myself up,
if you promise to accept me.
In hope that you'll share yourself
with me too.

In the way that I care,
I hope it brings a smile to you face.
A small shred of joy
in my world full of pain.

No matter what you say
my feelings will not alter.
They'll stay constant throught
your life and mine.

I'll be here no matter
how hard you push me away.
You can't close the doors
without my love creeping through.

Weither you in pain
because you've been hurt,
or happy with your life,
I'll be here to share it.

Weither were sittig in silence,
or having a good time,
or sharing a deep conversation,
I will never leave you alone.

I can't begin to explain it,
this elation that binds me to you.
I'll always love you,
and always be here for you.

No matter what,
I love you Tony.
