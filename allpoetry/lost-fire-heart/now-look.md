writer: "Lost Fire Heart"

Now look what's happened.
You've wasted so much,
and only been let down.

You didn't let me warn you.
She's done this before,
so many times.

HAd you only let me,
before you went
behind my back.

Now look what's been wasted.
My heart now in pain
and it could have been avoided.

So much could be different,
and easier
and less painful.

You had to go 
and do that to me.
I hate it.

No look what happened.
I could've been saved,
and been happy.

You though it would be better,
the way you thought it would be,
but it's not, is it?

How stupid
and nieve you were
to think that.

Now look what's been wasted.
All those feelings,
and thoughts of good things.

It's not f*cking like that now
is it?
Why, god-dammit, why?

Now look what happened.
Everyone's in pain,
when we didn't have to be.This was written a long time ago and has nothing to do with anyone now.
