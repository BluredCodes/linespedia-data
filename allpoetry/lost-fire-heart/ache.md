---
title: "Ache"
writer: "Lost Fire Heart"
slug: "ache"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Your simple look,
turns me
inside out. 

The way you walk,
makes me
take notice.

The way you smile,
makes me know
that I'm in love. 

When you stop and take notice
of little old me, 
I feel special.

When you come to talk, 
and make me feel better, 
I learn to open up. 

When you listen to my words, 
and offer me advise,
it eases my soul.

When you tell me
that you still care, 
my heart skips a beat.

But, my heart still aches,
for a simple look, 
or your touch.

It still aches 
when you walk by, 
and don't say hi. 

And it still aches 
when you smile,
and I know your not mine. 

Make the ache go away.
Ease my pain.
Love me again.I love you Tony
