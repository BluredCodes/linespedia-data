---
title: "Yes, No"
writer: "Lost Fire Heart"
slug: "yes-no"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I'm sorry, 
How many times must I say it. 
Can I take back
the mistake I made?

No, I can't
and you won't let me fix it. 
Yes, I do care,
I do love you. 

No, I know I hurt you,
time and time again.
Yes, I promise
It will never happen again. 

No, you don't believe me. 
How can I prove it?
Yes, I will try.
I won't do it again.

No, you can't trust me. 
Do you still love me?
Yes, I love you. 
Only too late. 

No, we cannot go back. 
Time has passed us by. 
Yes, the future is here. 
It will be our time. 

No, it hurts you too much.
We can ease the pain.
Yes, it will leave,
you will feel again. 

No, it is too soon, 
but I cannot wait.
Yes, I have discovered my love,
we must act now. 

No, I do not care
what everyone thinks or says.
Yes, I will devote myself,
to you and ONLY you. 

Yes, I do love you.
