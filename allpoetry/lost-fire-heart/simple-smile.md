I used to cry,
late at night,
and all alone,
sitting in my bed.

But now,
there are no more tears
left for me to shed.
I smile instead.

I smile,
when you call
just to say hi.

I smile, 
at the thought of you.

I smile,
because I know
that soon I will be in your arms.

I smile,
at the first chance
that I get to see you.

I smile,
when you kiss me
and my heart melts.

I smile,
when you hold me close,
and I can hear your heartbeat.

I smile,
even though
we must say goodbye.

I smile,
because I know
I'll see you soon.

I smile,
because I know
I'm in love with you.I love you Tony. You make me smile.
