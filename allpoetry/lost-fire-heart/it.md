---
title: "IT"
writer: "Lost Fire Heart"
slug: "it"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/44014-IT-by-Lost-Fire-Heart"
---

Too many ideas,
and thoughts
and feelings, 
and answers,
all shot down,
from the pain
it's been through.

This scared,
abandoned,
and alone creature,
that dosn't think
before it acts.

Too much pain,
and sorrow,
and hurt,
and anger,
has gone through
it's body,
heart,mind,
and soul.

The answers,
and clues
we shall never know.
Why or when
these feelings
came and went
through it's pain.

The reason
is not known,
nor shall it ever be.
Known to others
who know not
of it's thought's
and pain
and anger.I love you Tony, Wholy and truly. Forever and ever. I will never hate you.
