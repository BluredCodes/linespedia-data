He broke me.
Broke me in half. 
No way to go on.
I'm torn to shreds.
Never knew,
how much I love him.
Now I'm torn.
My hearts in shreds,
strewn across the floor. 
No way out.
Down a dark tunnel.
The candles gone out.
I can't find the way.
Confused and alone.
Not knowing
which way to turn.
Cold walls all around me,
closing in.
no way to fix it.
A shattered glass,
there on the floor. 
Light gleams
on the sharp edges.
Calling to me.
A pain in my stomache.
A hole in my heart.
Right through my body.
The pain shoots.
No stopping it.
Never ending.
Pain from him.
The glass is there.
As I slide it across my wrist,
the pain disappears.
I'm whole again.
Till the next pain wave comes,
and the glass calls me again.I still love you Tony. I'm not going anywhere. i just need you to be there for me forever. Your my everything.
