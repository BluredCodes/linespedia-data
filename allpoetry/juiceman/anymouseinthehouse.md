Anymouseinthehouse has opened my eyes to the world
I can be the bigger person and just ignore that girl
Say what you want about canon cus I really dont care
I guess all of your feelings canon could spare
I promise he wont write about anyone unless the push him to
and as for anymouse, I will never write about you
You and some others on this site are like angels in my mind
Thank all of you for reading my poems and ryhmes
Dont push and I wont shove
Juicemans writing this mush and showing love
thanks anymouse
welcome to my house anytime
to sit back and chill and write ryhmes
peace
