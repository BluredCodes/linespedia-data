title: "to my friend anymouseinthehouse"
writer: "Juiceman"
slug: "to-my-friend-anymouseinthehouse"

one of my first friends on this site,
you saw potential I didnt see.
you always had something encouraging to say.
You were always lookin out for me.

I selfishly left,
and didnt even say goodbye.
Im sorry playa i didnt leave for good,
I just needed sometime.

Juiceman was my soul,
canon was my heart.
I let them come together and fight,
canon tore me apart.

I never knew that I had had such an impact,
I figured i was just another, you know...
but you and a few others never put me down,
I really appreciate all the love that you'd show.

You too influenced me,
you showed me that soul is the heart of rhyme,
I thank you for teaching me,
I realized it and integrated it in mine.

Playa im sorry, 
i didnt mean to leave you alone.
tell me what you need and im here.
your my dawg and I want it to be known.

were veterans of this site,
and long time ryhme boyz.
playa lets collab some time.
make these words our toys.

gimme a shout out,
for anything you need.
rhyming grew in me cus of you,
you planted the seed.

props to my boy...Anymouseinthehouse...bow down to him, a god of a man
