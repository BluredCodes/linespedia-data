title: "I love you more than you Know"

September 29th will forver be one of my favorite days
Its like a repeating movie that forver and ever plays
You told me you werent falling in love with me no more
you've reached the end of the fall, my love started to pour
I couldnt stop smiling, my life is forever changed 
this feeling of love is different and somewhat strange
it is the best feeling though and i never want it to stop
I feel like im sitting in the clouds, the ones at the very top
that wasnt the only surprise you had for me though
you werent done making me happier then ever , oh no
You told me that you wanted to be my girlfriend
I told you that my love for you will never end
October 1st will be the first time you say the words
my love for will flow like rivers running in heards
I know that you love me but you want to say it to my face
Where you say those words, i will always cherish that place
It is time i create the future of never ending happiness for you
I almost died when i found out that you feel love for me too
now we can be officially together for as long as you love me
I fall more in love with you everytime you look, kiss or even hug me
These words i have written for you dont even begin to express
There are no words that i can say that will tell you the rest
So all I can say is that you are perfect, I love you, and I love everything about you
I dont know how i would feel act or be if i had to be without you
I love you baby..I always will
