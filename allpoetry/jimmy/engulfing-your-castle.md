Engulfing Your CastleYour castle of sand, stands tall on the beach,my early morn tide, can barely yet reach,I wait with the sun, as it burns through the day,I know that I'll reach you, and soon find a way,Surrounded by love, from all those that past,your structure stands firm, with hope it will last,As creatures run in, and out of your shade,your castle of love, watch steps as they fade,My tide grows an inch, as the night starts to fall,I reach your love castle, and lap at your wall,Then finally break through, to set your love free,I began to feel close, as you fall into me,Then with all my might, I push to the beach,I will surely embody, with tides I do reach,With one last good swell, I swallow you whole,	for now we are one, both body and soul.© J Fossier 2/991999,
