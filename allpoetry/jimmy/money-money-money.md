Money Money MoneyLooking like a losing soul,I walked into the glory hole,To find my riches, quick and clean,To live my most outrageous dream,I roll the dice, 10 thou to spare,Wrist pops quick, as all eyes stare,Rack the velvet wall with grace,Both dice slow, an even pace,Bursts of yells, pierce the table,High rollers, big smiles, designer labels,Bosoms pop up, at the smell of the money,Ladies crave, this high roll-in honey,Money buys power, it hides away fears,May break up a family, it may start a few tears,It's only a tool, to make our minds trite, Controlling our lives, away from the Light,So caution yourself if you fall into riches,It comes with a cost, full of soul hitches,Just send it my way, so I'll save you life's test,I'll do all your suffering, and live with the best!© JFossier 6/01
