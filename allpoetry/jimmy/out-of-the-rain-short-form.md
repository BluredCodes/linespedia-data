Out Of The Rain - Short Form

Pocket marbles, dolls and beads,			
Children's toys, plant their seeds,
Guns and knifes, stained with blood,
Screaming nights, end in mud

Simple things, make minds grow,
Or caskets buried far below,
Spit of knowledge, mixed with cheer,
Or smoke filled holes, served with beer

We're the ones, show the way,
Past is learned, apply today,
Videos, electronic gear,
Books filled with dirty smear

Hopeful futures, shine real bright,
Serene hearts, sleep well at night,
World's breathing, chaotic pain,
Troubled gangs, go insane

Running packs, everywhere,
Vicious rage, minds don't care,
Confused and hurt, feel despair,
Where to hide, no one's there

Live on streets, learning life,
Evil touch, world of spite,
Deadly darkness, harden heart,
Children's lives, bust apart

Noah's here, can you see,
Do not run, hide, or flee,
Think real hard, deep in mind,
Straighten your back, follow your spine

Days of past, I remember the time,		
Ran with the pack, saw all the crime,
Finally grew up, knew what was right,
Learned through life, follow the light
						
Love all children, if you are smart,
Open your arms, your soul, your heart,
Light up their face, chase away pain,
Gather them close, out of the rain

Harvest the children, back to the home,
Comfort the heartache, make them belong,
Dry all the sorrows, cast them a light,
Build them a world, a home that is bright
