'What is Love'Love is passion seen in eyes,Love is heaven in your heart,Love is never in despise,Love is sometimes far apart.Love is something we all give,Love is hard to be without,Love is where it can live,Love is simple and no doubt.Love is happy in our souls,Love is sad but never seen,Love is most of all our goals,Love is never in-between.Love is here for those who want,Love is something never bought,Love is what we want up front,Love is always in our heart.© J Fossier 9/98
