Skip Wide Eyed	Join me in my fantasy,dance naked in my heart,Bare your skin against my breast,and hold me from the start,Smiling bright eyed nymph,taste my face aglow,Skip your beat into my life,feel my blood a flow,					Sheer my shell with burning eyes,sip my salt of joy,Love seeps deep into my pores,with thoughts of once a boy,Resonate your movement,as you flicker in my flame,Shadows dancing wildly,between each drop of pain,Stoke my warmth of amber lust,heat my depths desire,Burn your face within my mind,set your blaze a fire.JAF 1/99© J Fossier and friend
