Living 60's HistoryShouts of pain, deem mindless views,grieves, screamed thick in air,radical truth rake the scenes,mankind neglect to care,Confusion was the motive,aggression pushed the point,politics chased their own,hippies found the joint,Themes, flowed the message,abstract art was cool,music made its mark in time,soldiers made the fool,Rights pounded on the road,oppression made its mark,blood rushed against its blood,forecasts seemed so stark,Times spawn change, across the nation,journalism began a power,life moved along again,only standards turned all sourer.© J Fossier 2/99
