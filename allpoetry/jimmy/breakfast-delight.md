Breakfast DelightButter sizzles in a pan,Standing on her naked feet,I walk across the floor a man,Looking for a morning treat,Eyes affixed upon my goal,Ignoring all she really can,I want to tang the food of soul,Tasting all her eggs and ham,I try her fare, and roll the dice,She finally joins me in my feast,Her thought of making dirty rice,Brings steam within, this morning beast.© J Fossier 12/00
