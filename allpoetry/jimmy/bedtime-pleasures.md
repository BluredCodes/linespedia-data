Bedtime PleasuresAs I slip into the sheets, my foot meets yours.With destiny forth, you seek my touch.Your loving touch, so gentle kind,excitement begins, to unwind.Aroused by hands, so softly sweet,brings me to, a fiery heat.As I feel, wet supple lips,I move my hands across your hips.Your back is arched, your moan divine,both knowing, it will soon be time.To seek all pleasures in ourselves,induce forth bliss upon all else.You smile a grin, and eyes not wide,this is when you cannot hide.Your true feelings, will be known,to all that hear, your passion moan.We grow complete, as we pull close,never know, who wants the most.As we touch, more and more,down we slide upon the floor.Excitement grows, with each new move,there's nothing more, that can improve.For we know, no better time,for each of us, to feel divine.As we peak, we fall to earth,rejuvenated, full of worth.© J Fossier 8/98
