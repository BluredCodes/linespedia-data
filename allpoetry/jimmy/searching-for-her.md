Searching For HerYour attention is mine,that is my goal,To be with you always,to open my soul,To wipe away sorrows,like no other man,To have you alone,Every day that I can,Feeling your warmth,deep in my heart,Sharing our lives,so we never part,You, but a dream,I know who you are,Thoughts in my mind,and nothing so far,I will never give up,believing one day,You will be mine,mine here to stay,When that day comes,you'll know that I've long,To be with you always,just like we belong.© J Fossier 8/98
