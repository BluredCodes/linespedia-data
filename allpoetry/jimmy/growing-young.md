Growing Young	Through smiling faces, pure as gold,we watch our children's lives unfold,Young ones sprout, grow so fast,they learn and play by stains of grass,Bringing laughs, or dash of tears,we share their lives and drink our beers,And leaving thoughts of work behind,we deem their Love's not hard to find,When looking deep into our past,we find the fun that didn't last,That growing up, can take away,and make your life another day,To loose the child inside our heart,leaves life so stark, and not to smart,For joy is what gives us love,a gift from God, sent from above,So cherish all your kids at home,and be fulfilled and not alone,Lapping love, and feeling young,your slice of life, yet to be sung.© J Fossier 11/98
