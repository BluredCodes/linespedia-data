Richness of LoveLaying on my cold flat sheets,I wonder if my feet will always be chilly,I wonder if I will taste the bliss of warmth,I wonder, will she lay her leg on mine.I want the touch of her to share my bed,to be her blanket in times of winter,to warm her soul in times of hurt,to lick her wounds in times of loss.Someone who shares the joys of life,feeling her smiles embrace my heart,feeling caressed by her aura of love,feeling fulfilled through her piercing eyes.For all the gold and riches of the world,cannot replace the void of amour,cannot paint the heart with jubilation,cannot fill the ear with sweetness.So count your money wisely,for richness of coins will spend away,for richness of life will die someday,but richness of love, will pave your way.JAF 4/99
