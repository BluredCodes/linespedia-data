Softness of PillowsLying together, on softness of pillows,Glances of eyes, peering on skin,	Surges of passion, envelope our minds,Brushing of touches, excite deep within.Caressing all parts, of body and mind,Fondling all creases, bringing her higher,Bearing her thigh, moving toward warmth,Movement in rhythm, to nurture desire.Feeling affections, throughout all the air,Nude figures entwined, sprawled end to end,Sensations embraced, deep within bodies,		Emerging with bursts, of passionate grace.	Lips joined in unity, fill up our cravings,Stoking with pleasure, exciting my lust,Creations of love, tightens all muscles,Moistened with savor, to share between us.			Devouring reality, of world and all thoughts,Strains; all voided, in passions of heat,Lying together, on softness of pillows,Only to leave us, so both complete.											© JFossier 9/98
