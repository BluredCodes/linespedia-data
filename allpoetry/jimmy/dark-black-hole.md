Dark Black HoleLook deep inside that dark black hole,the place you call your holy soul,for many are so scared to seek,it's not for sheepish, or the meek,Deep inside those dirty walls,look inside if you have balls,the bad, the ugly, the tarnished ways,the wants, the times you've hid for days,The dark black box you're scared to open,holding times of evil spoken,a place tucked deep within,conceal to hide, your deep dark sin,Light avoids this hole of hell,digressions burn and festers swell,where hurt and anguish come with pain,it marks your soul with devils stain,All must wash these marks away,to feel His grace and walk His way,on your knees you pray and say,give me peace and life someday,Through His son we wash our soul,in efforts to avoid the hole,you must be true in all your ways,to rest with Him your final days,So start today before your soul,leaves this earth for that black hole,share your goodness to those around,so dying brings you heaven bound.© J Fossier 10/98
