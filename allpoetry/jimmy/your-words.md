Your WordsLapping up, your words of love,encircling like a graceful dove,Cascading down my heart in kind,impressions etched atop my mind,So sweet of virtue, to the bone,will you join me on my throne,You make me feel, like I am king,I'm core enveloped... crave your sting,Our distance pairs us, far apart,not the same for in my heart,It reaches lengths, both far and wide,surrenders all, and swallows pride,My passions feel for you.... my §weet,to lay my head upon your feet,Rejoicing love.... your every sound,commingled souls, entwined and bound,Your niche embossed inside my heart,could never leave, should you depart,Unlocks my spirit.... sets me free,spawns precious life inside of me,My hunger smells your calls of love,scent, floats around my clouds above,Lush swooning words, fixates my soul,chants.... come to me, pay my toll.JAF 10/98
