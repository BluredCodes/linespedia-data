Lovely PrincessYou are my lovely princess,you sleep the days away,for dark is just another dream,for you, to laugh and play,Your smile is ever tender,your grins are quick to leave,for love, you seem to give it all,for seen in eyes of gleam,The voice, you do not have,the hands, that cannot hold,gives special love to life,gives soul, to those so bold,My air is yours to have,my life is yours to spend,take my love within your heart,take smiles to help you mend,When days are gone forever,when time has passed us by,I'll tell you how I loved you,I'll say it with my cry,For you're my lovely princess,for one of God's true friends,the diamond in the roughest form,the coal that shapes the end.© J Fossier 5/99To my lovely special angel.... Tina.Tina was my special child who passedMarch 24, 2000.  She was sixteen yearsold, and could never talk or walk, but she taught volumes of life to all those who came in contact with her during her life inthis world.
