'Are You There'Are you thereTo touch my words, softened by your eyes,Are you thereTo taste our day, and feel sensations on your skin,Are you there To embrace expression, flowing deep within our minds,Are you thereTo feel alive, to melt our lives together,Are you thereTo pawn desires, as we join as one,Are you thereTo share our hearts, diving deep inside our souls, Are you thereTo brave the truth of life, to bare our inner being,Are you there tomorrow?© J Fossier 2/00
