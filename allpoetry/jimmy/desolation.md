DesolationFor I died today,no passion, no more,my soul has gone away,leaving despair to score,Emptiness surrounds void,my pores spilling air,no place to hide a smile,scattered sadness everywhere,Not hate, nor joy,sits inside this shell,pleasures once enticed,is now replaced by hell,Pain is not my hardship,no reason to believe,this time, no change,can take me from my grieve.© J Fossier 6/00
