Double-Edged StormSleet, rain, frost, snow,nature hurls its mighty blow,Warming bodies huddled tight,fires burn throughout the night,Winter freezes life about,cold numb kills, without doubt,Trees are fallen, nests on ground,icy streets, spinning round,Dropping branches, one by one,weighted danger, needs the sun,Snapping sounds, crystal clear,sirens blasting, fills the ear,Nature shivers, down the spine,brisk winds blow, swaying pines,A forceful power, howls with might,brittle death, creeps in fright,Food is gone, birds in fear,destruction brings, frozen tears,Dripping drizzle, makes its mark, sun goes down, as day goes dark,Majestic storm leaves's all bright,brilliant shine, refracting light,Tormenting gale, flees the beast,leave living art, for eyes to feast.© J Fossier 12/00
