Kosova's JewsHistory painted, before our eyes,men's spilled blood,cause children's cries,Shelters bleak, rain for sun,danger looms,no where to run,Tents for home, huddled tight,tearful people,lost in plight,Families gone, tears run dry,hope seems grim,few want to die,A man alone, reins folks fate,he feeds his troops,with lies and hate,Fueled by scorn, can this last,repeating fears,from history's past,Mankind's loss, all must learn,destruction breeds,the devils' burn.© J Fossier 4/99
