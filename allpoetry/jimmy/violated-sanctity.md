Violated SanctitySanctity and faith,tarnished by beer,pangs the heart deep,releases a tear,Sixteen years few,a bond did envelop,two persons; one spirit,began to develop,Built on alliance,foundation, with trust,a union, once solid,divided by lust,In mind, or in matter,a breach; is quite clear,unwinding the rope,jilts, hatred and fear,Soiled; dirty laundry,hung in the air,embarrassment, none,limp heart impedes care,Human; so cold,fancies oneself,nudges love wayside,hoards in the wealth,One; who stills loves,pays a huge price,the other, grows callous,a cool drink with ice,Myopic brain vision,an ocular illusion,single important,religious confusion,Life lesson, now learned,love; stripped of its bond,old wounds, always fading,but never; quite gone.© J Fossier 2/01
