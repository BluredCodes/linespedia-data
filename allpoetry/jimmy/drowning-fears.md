Drowning FearsLonely figure, humble frame,stands alone,on 9Th and Main,Misty streets, slouching boys,walk on by,to deal their toys,Dim lit shadows, casts a blank,their moments gone,life borne all dank,Sleepless nights, turn to day,no place to go,they have no pay,Plastic bags, create a home,huddled down,no one to phone,Turn the wheel, shift a gear,glance on back,escaping fear,Faces lost, cold hard stare,memory notes,A-void life there.© J Fossier 5/99
