Parallel Deathdancing winds,across mycheekbones,lays palenessof cold,onto my face;my tempo faster,aiming to escapeits bite and sting;as a bitter northernerbegins to swallowits victim,It slowly eatsbody warmth;chilling bloodfor dessert;inducing its path,to numbness;frozen snotblocks offmy innerair,no finger holes,to live;fused ice glue,covers mysquinted eyelids;peering throughfrosted slits,wandering blind;a pale manwho cannot seeand cannot breatheand cannot feel;will die aparallel death;thus twice feeling;pale, blind, breathless and numb.© J Fossier 2/01
