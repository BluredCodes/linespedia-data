Fine Wine

Women, compared to
Fine wine
aged to perfection
Smooth

The complexities
tasted, felt, heard, and smelled
as the glass is tipped
to my lips

She pours forth
her Vintage
from rounded hips
Barrel-like, strong

The cask has held
Children, 
and intoxication left
from harvested love

Meals are now shared
with the Cabernet
of deep conversation
and understanding

William Dawes
January 6, 2002
