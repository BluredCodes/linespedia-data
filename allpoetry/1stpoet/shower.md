shower

As the steam in the bath
thickens
through the fog I see clearly 
a glistening body

mirrored am I in your eyes
my fingers cascade down your torso
following the trail of the water droplets

I pause at the curve of your breasts
my senses aroused by the fragrance of your flesh
and honeysuckle
with each bubble that forms
I wish upon those rainbows shimmering 
in the candle light

I bathe you with kisses
washing away all of your cares

we dry ourselves with passion’s heat
