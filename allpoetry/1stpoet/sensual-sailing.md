Sensual Sailing

I have felt passion’s fire so
deeply the marrow
in my bones became
ash
her lips were lava
as they coursed the landscape
my body presented
and as steel in a vice I bent to her
touch, soft as a feather
the sun was coal in comparison to the brightness
her eyes shone upon me

she, my world, quaked within
her thighs, the red sea my fingers floated upon
parted
her back arched in waves, and I like a ship
rode each crest
her breathing caused me to sail, uncharted
beyond the rocks that before kept me anchored
