Existence

Will I be gone tomorrow
like a warm front that passed
bringing sunshine and storms
within fleeting moments to your mind

Not living, nor dead
male nor female…..
just thoughts that entertained you
wined and dined you, brought you to tears
of ecstasy, only to leave you cold in the morning

when you touched me, you touched yourself
I live only within you. You give me breath
life, and movement. Flesh that feels
the energy my mind possesses

I live only for this communion we share
within words I write
We are one in spirit, and mind
for that is all I am, a muse within this form

In your eyes, I am loved
I live
I am eternal, in my existence
