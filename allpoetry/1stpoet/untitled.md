Shadows (a poem on being deceived)


I see you in the shadows
my love from days gone by
then those emotions come crashing
I fall to my knees and I cry

My mind is looking for light
yet there is darkness all around
in music and in thunder
your voice is the only sound

sometimes when I am sleeping
and dream of castles in the glen
I roll over on my side
and you are with me once again

I often wonder of the freedom
of love and moving on
but then in the wind I hear your whisper
and all becomes undone

I know that you are with him
and that I should not care
yet my mind goes on a fantasy
is this something I could share

without a shadow of a doubt
I mustloose you from my life
the only questions that remain
is it as friend or enemy....not wife

you open up your heart
and see shadows in your thought
you come to me for answers
are we doing what we ought

I only have so much strength
the sunlight is growing dim
will you rest calmly in my shadows
or go on seeing him
