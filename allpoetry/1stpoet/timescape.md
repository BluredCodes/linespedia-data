Timescape

If we have come from worlds distant
times of old, transported to this moment
we find life to be a glimpse
of dreams

I, a knight bold in valor
chivalrous, I shine in the eyes of your heart
I ride steady on your thoughts as you dance
upon clouds of sleep

You, a fair maiden that a squire
would desire
your love is my armor, for
it keeps me secure in times of separation

Each second that passes
we change to settings
strange yet familiar in texture

the clear air of the mountains
surrounds us
I, the strong warrior upon Mustang
bronzed by sunlight
You, singing by the river
washing hair as long as my arms

I long to reach out and touch you
to smell the sage upon your body
pull the ties of the buffalo skin
and watch it fall to the ground around you

your body shining in the moonlight
and then

I am taken back to this moment
where I see your brown eyes watching me
there
as I write
