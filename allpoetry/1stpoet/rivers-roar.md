River’s Roar

Rivulets flow through ridges
of cerebral cells
love cascades over cliffs
that edge of sanity so closely clung to
as sensuous sensations are surrendered to

on the brink of release to the wonderment
man and woman float on the flowing cloud of fantasy
as fragrance from heated flesh
enters with every breath 

diving into the shimmering depths of 
eyes, steaming pools
a-mist from the bliss of plunging
into the whirlpool of embrace

bodies sink into each other
tossed by the rocking waves of passion
together
	we
		DROWN
