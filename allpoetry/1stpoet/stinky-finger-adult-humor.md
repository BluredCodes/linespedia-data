Stinky Finger (adult humor)

I was called to do this
a need to please myself
and satisfy you

you could not move until
I used my skills
to allow you again to spin like the wind

my hand found the warm wetness
slight oils still left on the surface
folds of flesh, parted and allowed my 
finger
to 
find
its 
target

damn, I hate when I drop a spoon
in the garbage disposal

William S. Dawes
