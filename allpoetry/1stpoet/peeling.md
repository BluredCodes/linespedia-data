Peeling

Pain is the paint you wear so well
your face and eyes darkened
by the shadows cast from your mind

is there a candle
a flicker of hope somewhere to be found
to spark new thoughts of belief
that you are good
worth life
and the love of a fine man such as me

if I could take this skin
the tarnished white that coats you
peel it and sand down the cracks and crevices
that are etched in your soul
Fill you with smooth tenderness
edges rounded….no more sharp corners giving pain

How do I brush this new color
blush of life
green of desire
yellow of hope
a rainbow for you to wish upon again

reach out
let the rain bend my sunlight
peel away the clouds from your eyes

I am that pot of gold you wish for
