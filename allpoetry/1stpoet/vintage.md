Vintage

I will see you in the twilight
the morning hours
when the only light in the room
comes from your eyes
still glowing from the love we shared

the silence of slumber was watched
your body still twitching
from the 
tremors
 of
  spent
   passion

oh, to be the pillow your head rested upon
to be the clouds your dreams danced upon

the sheets now, still damp
sweat, perfume
and sweet tears
a concoction of consumated passion
it wakes me like coffee
quenches me like water
and intoxicates me like wine
  these juices from pressed bodies
as your whines fermented in my ears

your eyes, my dear
are the crystal goblets
shim
    m
     ering

I drink my fill
of love.
