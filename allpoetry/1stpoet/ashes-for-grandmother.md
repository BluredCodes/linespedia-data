ASHES (for Grandmother)

Her life now hung 
fragile
like the ashes on the end of a held cigarette

for years her stature seemed to dictate
that one must smoke, those clouds
swirling around her as she moved
as chaotic as her life really was

now she lay
wrinkled and misshapen
resembling the butts that were crumpled with fingers
before the next link in the chain was added

those fingers and lips
the only flesh colored parts that remained
stained
by years of tars absorbed…..
now clawed and gasped for the last remaining moments 
life was sparing

the mortician awaited
soon she would be
ASHES
