Transcendence


You have begged for entrance into my head
to experience my joys, and also my dread
plunging your fingers into wounds that have bled
and tasting the salt in the tears I have shed

Asking to see the world through my stare
comprehending life, being truly aware
to experience this, of what to compare
into my world I take you, ensnared

You touch my words penned with black ink
further within my body you sink
Like a vampire, my lifeblood you drink
at the edge of my soul you stand at the brink

Ingesting flavors of bitter memories
yet you want more, you shout “Oh please”
So I take you deeper, you’re down on your knees
I’ve captured you now, and I hold the keys

As you feel life that I’ve lived in my brain
you flow within, expanding my veins
my audience you are, and I entertain
from the pure pleasure, I am sustained

I live within words I scribe to the page
perhaps to enrapture, perhaps to enrage
but combined our thoughts are truly engaged
you may be chilled, or yet, be ablaze

Communion of flesh, spirit and mind
thoughts that traverse, enter, and wind
your consciousness I know I can bind
I own you, if the right words I can find

William Dawes
