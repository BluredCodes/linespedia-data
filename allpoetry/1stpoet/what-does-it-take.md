What does it take

What does it take
to have your name mentioned in movies
have segments of sonnets recited in song
and see a name on a binding

What does it take
to touch a heart
woo with words
and make love to a mind with thought

what does it take
to hold the world in a vision
pen it to parchment
bringing to life a moment in time

what does it take
to temp desire
burn a heart with a fire
eternal

it is poetry my friend
that takes all that there is
and gives it in gift
of reading
