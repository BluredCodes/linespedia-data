Three J Hell

I came home with my three friends
I pray the night quickly ends
Jose, Jack and Jim kept things alive
Now to the toilet my head does dive

I was earlier feeling no pain
Those three held all of my brain
For I was tougher than Dirty Harry
Smarter than Einstein
And sexier than Ricky Riccardo

The bed is moving across the room
How do I end my time of doom?
I made it home, where is my car
Which one of us drove from the bar?

That worm I ate to win the bet
Is now my biggest regret
Elephants pink, and frogs galore
Are dancing on my bedroom floor

This hell I am living in
Why did I let it begin?
Jose, Jack and Jim you see
Alone are fine, but not all three

Maybe I should find a new friend
Rum and tequila, you think they blend
Or maybe something mixed with coke
No, the fizz would make me choke

I think I will start a fad
Mix vodka with Ole Grand Dad
Call it an old fart, or maybe King Tutt
That drink would knock you on your butt

No, if I end up on my can
I just might smoke Jamaican
Then I won’t have bottles when I’m done
They say the green stuff is lots of fun

Nope, again that starts with J
That joint to get me through the day
I will stick to booze because
It’s perfectly legal to catch this buzz

William Dawes
July 2000
