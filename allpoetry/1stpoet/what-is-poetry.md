Look outside
see the sun dance upon the water
the bees suspended like helicopters
above fragrant flowers
and picture laying upon a sandy beach dreaming

see the children
kicking a stone down a brick road
and watch a man struggle
moving a heavy load
feel the hurt of a bride crying, left
standing at the altar

lips burn searing
as a kiss touches the core of your soul
and you taste the sweet look of love in eyes
looking innocently back
Hold a star in your mind
   Wishes can come true

Poetry is life
Poets live, knowing they can touch
     the heavens with words.............
         and a heart: with Emotion

William S. Dawes
