Racing Mind

My mind is set for speed
I question if I shall succeed
on capturing the seed
of inspiration

oh thoughts, please don’t erode
for the rubber must hit the road
within, I feel emotions explode
words form

My heart sets the pace
there is a smile upon my face
I start filling the blank space
on paper

I move my pen with motions intense
I feel as if I am riding near the fence
from opening my self there is no defense
to rhyming

and as I round this one last curve
I pray I won’t loose my nerve
I must stay focused and not swerve
and crash

so a pen forms line by line
this victory feels so fine
I get drunk as if on wine
from writing
