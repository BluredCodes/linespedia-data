Misled

My heart wanted love
and took my mind on a journey into darkness

what was behind the eyes
the skin
and the mouth of the one I was with

it was darkness
cold
barren

no love was there
a heart as stone was within walls of ribs
without a door to be opened

my heart gave my mind thoughts
that if I gave more, I could win her
thaw her
and bring her to life, living, and loving again

but there was no pulse
no signs of energy in a mind that sat
silently dying, and bringing me to decay

do not trust your heart
you may be
Misled

William S. Dawes
