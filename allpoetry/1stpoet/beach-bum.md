Beach Bum

She lay on the beach, not near me
Trying to get a dark tan
I saw her string bikini
And started devising my plan

Somehow she must notice me
As to not catch her completely off guard
Do I approach by sand or sea?
The decision is really quite hard

If I swim through breaking waves
With one hand I'd be holding my nose
And to walk across invisible flames
I could burn off a few of my toes

I guess I'll just sit here in my chair
And figure out something to do
Maybe she will just notice my stare
And all of these dreams can come true

Her walking up here with legs so brown
Oblivious to heat of the sand
I picture her in white wedding gown
Life on this beach is so grand

My visions of delusion are gentle
Over thoughts long I never do toil
I easily can become mental
Seeing women covered in oil

William Dawes
