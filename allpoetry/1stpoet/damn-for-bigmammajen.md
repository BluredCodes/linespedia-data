Dam (N)—for Bigmammajen

I see the heart has been hurt
that the blood of life and dreams
streams
from tears through eyes red as blood

tears shed in sadness
not so much because of badness
just that human nature, and love
are callous in actions

we attach ourselves to others
drifting on the parachute of emotions
that carry us aloft as life spins like the earth
underneath our dangling feet

the cord, when cut
causes us to spiral downward
where only the previous scars of memory
soften the blow

if I could reach you
give you my thumb to hold back the waters
that stream down cheeks
soft as clouds blown upon winter winds

you my friend will survive
your heart will become again strong
there will again in your mind
be a song

of LOVE

William S. Dawes
1st Poet
