Pub Crawlin

She said “You love me now, 
but will you like me later?”
I replied “I’m not so sure
but you’re my ego inflater!”

So beautiful and blonde
I pictured her in my car
but for now we were relaxing
in the bar

I drank my Killians
she; Southern Mist
and I wondered just when
her lips had last been kissed

we held each other
on that dim dance floor
and I knew in my heart
I wanted much more

her sweater so tight
and soft in my arms
could I persuade her
with all of my charms?

to see her in the morning
sun shining in her eyes
after this night of drinking 
would be a surprise

yes I was so happy
feeling no sorrow
I was with her tonight
would I still be tomorrow?
