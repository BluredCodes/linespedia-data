Skeletons

I thought today that skeletons 
have been in my closet
people, places, things of the past that
I wished would turn to dust and dissipate
the memory blown upon a sigh

I heard the sound of rattling bones
disturbed again by the stirring
my brain was giving to resting haunts

then I pondered upon the idea
that I too had been around people
who now have skeletons concealed

am I perhaps a walking reflection
that I do not recognize in the mirror
a ghost that wanders distant minds
