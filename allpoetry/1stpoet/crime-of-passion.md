Crime of Passion

I stand before you guilty
Of the crime I do admit
I will endure whatever punishment
I am judged, do not acquit

If my charge is that I love you
I shall endure the chair
For no man made device
Of you does it compare

To go by electrocution, I would surely burn
For within my mind you charge me
And I give my energy in return

Lethal Injection does not phase me
For you run deeply within my veins
No torture on this earth exists
To make me drop the reigns

In a chamber now I sit
Waiting for gaseous toxins
And all I can think of
Is your fragrance in the mist

If by rope I must swing
As the tightness grips round my neck
My senses will feel your soft kisses
And the angelic songs you sing

I have become lost in you already
So the guillotine does not sharply cut
The way a word on my ears
Causes me to become heady

I know that living for you
Is dying within myself
Only to be re-incarnated
In your arms

William Dawes
June 18, 2000
