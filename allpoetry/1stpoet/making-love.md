Making Love

The times when passion rises
and bodies mingle and melt
is ever oh so special
when the fruits of love are felt

as the sun breaks through the window
in those waking hours
the fragrance of glowing skin
humbles all the flowers

after a day of working
covered in grime and sweat
together in the shower
sharing the joys of getting wet

after eating a hot meal
when strength has been regained
the unbridled moans of passion
for hours can be sustained

as night falls down upon the land
under covers soft and warm
the two consumed in love
held in each other’s arms
