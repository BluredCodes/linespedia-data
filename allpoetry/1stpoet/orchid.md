Orchid

I long to taste the sweet dew
from the petals of an orchid.
Begging for my entrance, nectar so sweet.
Protruding bud lying just beyond open fronds
Curved, open tunnel, inviting.
The savored sweetness encapsulates my tongue
Glistening crimson delight….a rare flower
Mine alone to drink from.
It feeds me with beads of euphoria
as I lap them hungrily
I must enter completely
to feed it with my pollen
So again it will open, and await me.

William Dawes
