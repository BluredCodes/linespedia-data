Infrastructure

Roadways reach around the city
the way my arms reach around you
no area uncharted
a driving force causes thoughts to travel
within the highway of my mind

my love flows as water through a main
you tap into it at will
hot to bathe in, or cool and refreshing
you drink of me, quenching thirsts

the energy I have for life
a current you take hold of
you become enlightened by the power I have over you
and your smile becomes brighter

I am the strong tower you take refuge in
when life’s storms grow black on the horizon
you run within my walls

If one day, the earthquake of Alzheimer’s comes crashing
my roadways collapse, the water pipes burst
and the lines come tumbling down

what will you see?

rubble and dust blowing in the wind

And what shall I see
what will I be
Will my thoughts still think of you
will the waters pour out into the sand of time
as when the main breaks, and the ground around washes away
will fires burn in dry whispers
words you don’t understand spoken by lips that once charged you
ignited you in passion, now you trace the lines with your fingers

my body, crumpled sitting timelessly in a chair

I will see you smile
hear you call my name

but like Atlantis
I will remain unfound, yet still exist…….somewhere
