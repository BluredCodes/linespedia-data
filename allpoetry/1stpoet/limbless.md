Limbless

I am called a vagrant
slovenly seated upon my chair
Vagary is my only hope
for dreams are all I have

My legs, gone, I no longer run like the wind
more so with the wind, breezes blown through lips
as hips ache 

Arms that long to feel again
reach within my mind, fingers clutch at memories
of how you felt

As the wheels of dreams past roll by
Softly spoken come your words
and I am moved

The shell seen shatters silently
as you kneel and kiss my lips
I feel refreshed

I take you with me upon my chariot
cobblestones become as clouds
and together we ride the wind
