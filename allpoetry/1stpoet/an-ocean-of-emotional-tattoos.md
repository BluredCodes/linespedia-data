An Ocean of Emotional Tattoos

My arm bears the image
indelible ink
A shark, my tattoo

It swims in an ocean
cerulean lines
In waves, his world

My mind has such markings
conception left
of thoughts I've had

An ocean of deep emotions
Engulfing me
I swim through them

Waves of thought ripple
washing words
from pen to paper

You read my poetry
floating, bathed
my ink leaves its mark
