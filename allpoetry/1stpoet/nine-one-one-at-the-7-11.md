9-1-1 at the 7-11

Through darkness flashed blue and red
past my house, quickly he sped
through the silence the sirens did wail
was someone soon going to jail

Is there an accident, a loss of life
perhaps a husband beating his wife
maybe even a cat in a tree
I got in my car to go see

He turned left, and then went right
across town quickly in the middle of the night
I thought perchance someone was shot
until he pulled into the parking lot

he ran rapidly up to the door
then made his way to the back of the store
with his hands he grabbed a donut, glazed
my jaw dropped, I was amazed

nine-one-one should have been called
at his reckless abandon I was appalled
into the air he fired a warning shot
and said "Don't bother me son, my coffee is hot"

Next time you need that man in blue
here is all you need to do
Stop at Seven-Eleven for a candy bar
for by the curb will be his car.

William Dawes
August 21, 2000
