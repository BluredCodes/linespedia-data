A toxic baby is what I am
Born and raised in a garbage can
I breathe acid fumes and toxic mists
Because of the lifestyle that helps me exist

I have deep scars on my arms and leg
For clean air and water I must beg
There is no end to my miserable plight
It’s been 10 years since I have seen pure sunlight

The food I eat, full of DDT
Choking fumes are surrounding me
I feel as if my life is a lesson
That with Mother Nature we shant be messin'

My eyes seem blind, I can not see
Is it really better living through chemistry?
for there was a time I was a great dancer
Now I anguish with the pain of Brain Cancer

This poem may be morbid, and I hope that you  grasp
the meaning so for clean air you won’t gasp
I only have strength to type this last line out
For my supper today was a mutated trout
