I am your muse


Forget me tonight as I leave
Do not let my memory linger long
For I am just a muse
Hoping to touch your inner thoughts momentarily

I only want a moment to enchant you
To stir a desire to once again turn to the page you have forgotten
And to allow you again to create your works

I am an imaginary figure, who has taken on this shape
For if I were to come to you in a song
Would it be any less potent

Like an intoxicating fermentation, my effervescence enters
And I take complete control, but you are unaware
That it is I who holds you captive in this moment

I will leave you soon, yet will always be in you
For my fingers go deep and are not
Easily removed

My only love is you, and what you sing
With the music I give you, 
And your own memories combined

You will soon again be strong
Captivation of the song you long to sing will be a reality
And I will still be here, yet forgotten.

William Dawes, May 21, 2000
