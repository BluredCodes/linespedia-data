Blessings 
(WHEN READING THIS POEM, READ THE CAPITAL WORDS DOWN AFTER THE COMPLETED TEXT)

SOMETIMES I sit in silence, watching you
THERE in your silence, sitting
ARE you hurting in ways
HIDDEN behind eyes clouded by tears
MESSAGES you send to my loving heart

PLACED now is your hand
WITHIN mine, I tenderly touch you with
PHRASES I speak as softly as my skin embraces yours

THAT touch, those words
REACH you at your core
AND stir to life embers
SOFTLY blown by the breeze of my voice, You
TOUCH my face tenderly with outstretched hand

A smile forms across a face moments ago somber
HEART beats now change, to that more of happiness
IN time you see that I truly am the man you
NEED to give your all to

OF life’s most wonderful blessings
HEALING your hurt is my gift
