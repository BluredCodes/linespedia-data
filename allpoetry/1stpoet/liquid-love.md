liquid love 

Can I touch you tenderly
tempting thoughts to dance on the floor
of your soul

to spread wings of love dreams
with the touch of wind on feathers
dipped in ink

dripping the bold black oil
onto parchment
whetting desire, your lips glisten
as you wet them with the tip of your tongue

eagerly you lap
the liquid from the page
with hungry eyes you devour me

I love your tender touch
