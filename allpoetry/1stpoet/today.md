Today

Today the tears are tapping the keys
each droplet directed to vowels and consonants
constantly pouring words onto the page

you told me I was the means to paying bills
and he was the one who held you
while your ring stayed in the drawer

questions still ring in my ears
in the tone of your voice
the wanting
the needing to know my every move
so 
Yours
would be covered, like footprints in sand when waves wash them


Today my tears are torrents of triumph
for I know the clutter of distrust
is flowing like a river from the crevices
down cheeks, the salt (sweetly tasted upon my tongue)
is not the bitterness of reality I faced hours ago

You said you would never lie……
so not telling, or avoiding the questions kept you in truth
when in fact your mind was wallowing in the depths of despair that
you could not face me with
for fear your own tears would give you away

These tears are my cleansing
the clutter is gone
and now I can collect my thoughts……..

Today, I am a new man
free
and you will continue to drown
in tears never cried
