Brown Eyes

I remember the look in those eyes
when after two years apart 
you caught me by surprise

on a drive I was that day
I passed your house, you waved
and asked me to stay

at one time those eyes were sweet
a rich chocolate that would melt me
but this time I should have taken retreat

for those eyes held despair
broke and loveless you were
yet I was captured by your scent in the air

fine perfumes blinded my eyes
tears of seeing you again
flowed freely, and I heard your sighs

now three months after the I do’s
what was not seen in brown eyes
has given me the blues

I was the hope of rescue you had
a job, insurance…help around the house
but you treat me so bad, not like a spouse

I would be willing to do anything
if only I would hear the words
“I love you”
you only say you were desperate, and I was again a mistake

your heart is cold
and your touch has put an ice jam in my river of love
jagged shards cut my soul with every heartbeat when we
are together

2 more weeks until I again
will be apart from you
papers signed and delivered

will I ever again be able to look at brown eyes
or will I think of you…and the pain.

William S. Dawes
