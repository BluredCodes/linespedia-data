Arrows Fallen
(in memory of Jane)

Jane married an abusive man, and I lost a friend.

Hand in hand you walked down the aisle
then that love turned so vile
smiling as you became bride and groom
then pots and pans flew across the room

forever and ever were the words you said
then your only escape was to be dead
when asked the question, you said I do
then the colors of love became black and blue

Cupid’s arrows shot to your heart
that was how you got your start
now to the ground fallen, the tips…Shattered
each day beaten, tortured, Battered

there were the excuses you made up
I fell off the stool while getting a cup
or I ran into the stove while baking a pie
that is how I got this ugly black eye

when at one time you and I could share a laugh
now I see a life fallen, a broken shaft

William S. Dawes
