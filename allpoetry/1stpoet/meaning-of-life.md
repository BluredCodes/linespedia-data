Meaning of life

I ventured to the mountain
To climb and find the guru
Who held the meaning
Of life

Through ice cold streams of thought
Over boulders of suppressed emotions
I trod upwards
Searching

Through clouds of stormy memories I passed
Into the glow of happier days
The warmth of my life
Before me

I saw skeletons that had been long dead
Now remembered for an instant
As I dusted the bones
Yet left them undisturbed

And upon reaching the summit
I found the guru
As I sat content
Beside myself

William Dawes
