Nirvana Interrupted

It is amazing how the surface that we see of life,
 is so calm, is so deceptive of the depths and the turbulence
 that lies just a scratch deeper

smiling children, stained faces from ice-cream and kool-aid
hiding bruised souls, scarred hearts
innocence taken away, never to be returned

victims of sexual abuse, a war they fight with no guns
there are no tanks and bombs, but destroyed homes
the castles of imagination and youth, gone

placed in a world, where guilt rules the mind
fences are the faces that peer upon them, 
holding them tightly in the confines of confusion

love is not supposed to hurt
imaginary friends cannot protect
and family does not believe the truth of babes

not able to grasp the thoughts of those that inflict
the child sinks within dreams of better places
only to be awakened by nightmares when darkness comes

there is no safe place anymore
will they grow to be the walking wounded, the cycle to repeat
or will one touch be like Hiroshima, laying all waste, to be reborn
