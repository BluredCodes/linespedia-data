Just to let you know

Just to let you know
these words I write, I taste as the ink
is licked from fingers 

the sorrow of the loneliness
is sour, like the un-ripened grapes of love
that never were able to ferment
and give the sweet wine

the fragrance of the morning sunrise
is sweet, like the honey 
upon my croissant at breakfast

the dreams of being touched
by love
are savored
like the richness of chocolate and cream
melting upon my tongue

and I know that until I find 
the soulmate who exists in clouds of dreams
I shall lap up bland moments of solitude
waiting
