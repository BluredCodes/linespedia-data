Nursing a Cold

Sitting here sipping tea
with lemon and whiskey
a touch of honey
bitter-sweet swallowing…..to soothe an aching throat

Life can be a virus
giving us pain and masking
true images of who we are

there comes a time when honey does not 
coat and cover the raw realities around us
no love can be sweet enough
not even thinking of the garden that it came from

sour is the flavor of regret
wishing one would have made other choices
moved on quicker and not been stuck
squeezing the last drops of time
only to be left as a shell

drunken one can be
when all around is bliss
tasting the oaky-richness of a true kiss
meant to stay forever

sometimes I feel my best
when I seem to be at my worst.
