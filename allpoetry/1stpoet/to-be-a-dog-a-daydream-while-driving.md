To Be a Dog

Oh those dog days
romping and tromping
sniffing and chomping at butterflies

days spent lounging in the sunlight of summer
basking on the grass
belly up to the warmth
then running and jumping in the lake

swimming, chasing ducks
barking and running after cars and trucks
sniffing crotches (and getting away with it)

yes, to come into the house muddy and wet
from a rainstorm
and not get scolded (sorry I missed the rug)

to lift a leg on the tree to pee
or take a dump by a stump.......


There is the sign
37 miles to the next rest area.

William S. Dawes
