Mental Optometry


I went to the doctor today
hearing- ok
heart- ok
muscle tone to body fat- good
cholesterol- excellent
I even have 20-20 vision

it is my mind’s eye that needs glasses
sometimes I see what was never there
and other times I walk into invisible walls

memory and emotions will do that
after a night out you call your wife “Myrtle”
yet her name is “Angela”
a shared chocolate shake (2 straws) took you back to high school
and that first crush

oh, I have been there before
describing a painting, a zoo
perhaps a poem
only to find it was a dream left over
from last night……the sleep lingers

I am 40
I have gray hair
245 poems penned in two years

if I didn’t write
I would think myself blind

William S. Dawes
