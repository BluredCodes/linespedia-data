Ode to My Muse

You give me a word
or a thought, feeling, fragrance
Perhaps a color or a name:
and expect me to create from it.

I, a meager poet
otherwise paralyzed,
Your commands move me
to life again

You say "LOVE," only love
and I must go from there.
Is it lost love, new love, 
or a love of myself...I am lost!

You oh Muse, 
Powerful and All-knowing
show me the color of ink, Blue
and "Lost love" is the answer

I write
not one line, but words flow
no effort goes un-rewarded
the page soon fills

I smell the memory
of the fragrance she wore
on Your breath, and tears
dot my page

Your fingerprints carry
and swirl words into structure
readers: Detectives
see these and study

Touched they are by your 
Grasp on my creation
And I humble, 
Owe all to you

Thank you, Oh Muse
You are my love
My new Love
My only!!

William Dawes
