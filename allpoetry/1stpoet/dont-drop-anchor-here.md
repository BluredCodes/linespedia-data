Don't Drop Anchor Here

I was looking for safe harbor
calmness to surround me
balmy breezes, slightly cool
and a tinge of salt.

She was alluring
arms outstretched like channel walls
Eyes signaling a sense of safety
as only a steady beacon could do.

Yet a tempest raged
beyond
the shoreline of her lips.

Every "ic" and "ac" known
would toss my vessel towards
Jagged rocks.

Alcoholic, Schizophrenic
Nymphomaniac, Kleptomaniac
Insomniac

And I said
"Don't drop anchor here"

William Dawes
