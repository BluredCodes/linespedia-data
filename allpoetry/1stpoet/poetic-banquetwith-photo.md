Poetic Banquet

Oh to dine 
on words so fine
laid out as in a feast

where the tongue
forms thoughts into spoken words
as eyes capture flavors from the page

an horsd’oeuvres
the first line, temptingly taunting
begging for one to bite
finger foods carved so delicately

then  the blend
where emotion and desire
want and rhythm all come together
as a consommé
swallowed deliciously
the hunger grows, and the reader goes on

to the main course
where the meat of the poem
robust, hot, and juicy awaits
the tender tendrils traverse through the mind
causing satiation

in the final lines
the sweet icing upon spun words
bring closure to a banquet
