Duality

The battle of good and evil prevails 
deep within the entrails
of man and beast 
who for the least
may stand the test of time

there is yin and yang
and innocent do hang
for sins they do not commit

to one a man may look like Jesus
to another that Manson named Charles
but he is somewhere in the middle
where duality, dost him ensnarl

the tongue is an organ of senses
a compilation of all that is
for whether sweet or sour
bland or of salt, this gets sorted out

with poetry and words of wisdom
the depths of the mind is touched
but what is heard in a word
can become quite obscured
when emotions come into play

for a person may mean no malice
no harm does that one convey
the hearer thinks twice, saying that was not nice
and angrily goes on his way

then where does the circle come rounded
where logic and intellect meet
I have searched in my heart, and then for a start
I went looking upon the street

Ne’er did I find the answers
to those questions that rattle my brain
for one day  I feel quite stable
and on some days I think I’m insane
