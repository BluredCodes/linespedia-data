Changes

Those words that were written
the me of long ago, penned to the white parchment
the black blood of emotions spilled
filled verses

now lost and gone, drifted off
into boxes or buildings left deserted
like the memories of those days
sometimes resurrected 

I wished those corpses would sleep
rot away, 
bitter tasting were the thoughts that haunted me
childhood, my youth tainted
tore holes through innocent desire

I know now that I am more than those words
ever would have been
that I survived and grew, (maybe not stronger)
wiser just the same

I am no longer living the death
that trapped me, 
the sting of fear now gone

it is not time that heals wounds
but more so the release of the worms that filled the brain
and ate away at dreams that longed to live

dreams must be kept growing like fragrant flowers
weeds that choke sanity must be pulled
and the water of tears
must be cried
 upon seeds dried
 bringing life again

changes blossom
