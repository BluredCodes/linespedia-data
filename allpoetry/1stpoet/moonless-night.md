Moonless Night

The night is now moonless
I donned in Black
The stars were numb to my presence

I sailed to the heavens
Found lunar orb
and captured it

Love, in my life
has left darkness
no glow of fullness, no smile of crescent

I wish upon stars
my only recourse
for moonless, my mind has caused eclipse

If you wish the moon
Returned to the night...Stars
Send me love
Light my darkness.

William Dawes
