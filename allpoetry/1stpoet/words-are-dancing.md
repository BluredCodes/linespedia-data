Words are Dancing

this morning I woke 
with new music
in harmony we slept last night

the caresses of dreams kept me warm
as your body lay next to mine
we were not spent from love making
but glowed in moonlight magic
comforted by communication

in short weeks we have begun
to emerge as a spring flower
My mind brings color to the blossoms
of thought that hang like rainbows
as early showers of affection dampen us

this morning words are dancing
as my heart is romancing
and I breathe the fragrance still left on my pillow
songs of hope drift 

in awe I am of what this is
for your touch brings strength
your words bring calming
and your smile brings warmth

as words dance
I see you in each line
the curves of the letters
could be molded as clay
to form every inch of your body

I sculpt you with writing
framed in thought.
