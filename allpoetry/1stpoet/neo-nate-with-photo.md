NEO-NATE
(please read author comments first)

If not for the miracles
of modern medicine
there was little hope 
of holding my child again

when he was born
there was a hole in his heart
that in itself
almost tore me apart

He came into the world
three months before his time
was there hope
he would live to his prime?

With tubes and wires
hooked up to his frame
my cheeks became flushed
tears to my eyes came

now thirteen years after his birth
I realize the miracles, and what they were worth
a child so fragile, he fit in my hand
is now vibrant and strong, a handsome young man.
