IN DEAMS

Her eyes reflected starlight
the way wet pavement
reflects traffic lights
at night

I stopped my thoughts
my breathing
and my heartbeat as
I found heaven in her gaze

we walked upon the shore
the sand felt as clouds under our feet
the breezes off the ocean gave my lips salt
her sweetness became even stronger

The Halo around the moon was
a reflection of the aura she produced
for this angel on earth
gave me temporary escape from Hell

When I awoke
Upon my pillow I found a solitary 
white feather

A quill left behind
for my ink to bathe
and write of the night
I rode to the stars on an Angel's Wings.

William Dawes
