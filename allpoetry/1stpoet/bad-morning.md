Bad Morning

When I awoke today
my stomach was feeling tingles
for at the party last night
I ate 15 cans of Pringle's

now this may not seem
to be that much of a quandry
but what I thought to be a dream
is giving me loads of laundry

after a restless night, hours eroded away
in the mirror I caught first sight
egad, I said, this is a frightenly bad hair day
just another part of my plight

my intestines were feeling particularly perky
and I had a fear of stepping outside
the rumbles of gas were getting quite jerky
but I had to get more Tide

I ate that last chip, tastingly scrumptous
with salsa and Velveta combined
and I know that I am not being presumptous
to the bathroom I may be confined

Oh god, not now.....my nose feels a tingle
please don't let me sneeze
for if I do there will be more than a trickle
my shorts will be full of cheese
