60's Born Poet


My poetry is the door
to my mind
Thoughts strung like glass beads
through which you enter
Black light illuminates emotions
Casting hues contrary to the norm
I paint with fluorescent words
So on my incense you are carried
To the world in which I live
Shelter
Is just a shout away
And my words scream
Begging you to come deeper within
Once inside you kiss the sky
As you ride a magical carpet
While Neil Young sings
"Old man take a look at my life"

William Dawes
