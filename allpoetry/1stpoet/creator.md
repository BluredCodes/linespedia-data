Creator	

Creator I am
of words 
that create

A world formed
by my pen
within you

Light and darkness
both seem blended
within mind

Emotions, like 
oceans
swell within

Mountains of fear
climbed, moved
and turned to sand

I do this
with a touch
of my hand to pen

Creator of 
life
within words
