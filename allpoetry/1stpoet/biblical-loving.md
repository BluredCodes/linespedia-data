Biblical Loving

In the beginning, I was void
darkness moved across my surface
chill air was the atmosphere around me

The light of your eyes and
the warmth of your smile touched me
it was as if the heavens had opened, 
your angelic voice brought the rain of tears
that separated the dryness in my throat and heart

oceans of desire soon moved upon me

your kiss was not that of Judas
as lips parted like the red sea, your tongue like a chariot
charged forward into the waiting paradise of my mouth
walls crashed behind me, as the dust of my past vanished

there was a covenant between us
a bond taking us beyond the stars
no ending, into eternity we would breath life into one another
forever living in a garden within our minds and souls

I had been like Lazarus, but your hand, your voice
raised my death like state 
I again felt alive

This resurrection, finding a new body and mind
breathing the freshness of your fragrant flesh
as we make love by the moon’s glow
and wake to birdsong in the morning

the fires of hell could never touch me with heat
the way your touch burns, and tortures me
I want more, I beg, and you comply

only 144,00 shall enter the gates of heaven
I know with you I am there, in your arms
for your heart is paved with gold
