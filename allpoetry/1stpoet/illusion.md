Illusion

Silk sheets tangled
On toes
Pillows shoved to the headboard
Blankets disheveled, tossed to the floor
Waves
Rolling within a rubber mattress
Due to perpetual thrashing
Sweat rolling off glistening skin
Moans and groans, muffled by clenched lips
And hair tangled, stuck to moist flesh
Fingers curled inward grasping, 
Every muscle taught
Nerves, alive: electric charges racing through the synapses
Eyes are rolling behind closed eyelids

Wake up-
You are having a nightmare

William Dawes
