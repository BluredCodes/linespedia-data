Sexed by Dr. Suess (you guys made me do this)

I don’t like green eggs and ham
but I sure like going down on Pam
she has these thighs that shake and quiver
who’s the taker, who’s the giver
 
someone asked about this guy named Horton
said he had somewhat of a shortin
but the lil folks seemed not to care
for it stuck straight up into the air

if there is a such a thing as a cat n a hat
when in a raincoat my thingy is fat
oh, that was maybe in a tad bit of bad wit
but believe me baby, we’d make a grand fit

so I sound like a bit of a Grinch
well, seducing you might be a cinch
if I could possibly tickle your fancy
but then again that risk is chancy

William S. Dawes
