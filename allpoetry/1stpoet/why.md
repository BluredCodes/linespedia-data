Why

Why do we live in the turmoil
of trying to find love’s sweet taste
and why do we deal with the heartbreaks
when we fall into love with such haste

why does the touch of another
seem to be that wonderful bliss
and love and death my dear friend
come to you with a kiss

if darkness is what comes after sunshine
why does not laughter come after pain
I think that in future endeavors
from the search for love I’ll refrain

yet in absence of it I so hunger
for a love that is wholesome and true
now I ask in my life long quest
why have I not come across one such as you

William S. Dawes
