Love Art

You lay before me naked
asking me to create art with you
But with what medium am I working?

For if you are canvas awaiting my strokes
to paint you blush and crimson with moisture
I must first frame you with candles and rose petals
so you will be outstretched, taut for my work

Are you clay? 
able to be molded into statuesque beauty
begging for my hands
to caress and shape you, 
shall I heat the kiln?

I find you now suspended
floating off balance, you need equilibrium
I attach myself, offset
we move

Encircling each other in candlelight
our gyrations matched
floating on the scent
of fresh roses

William Dawes
