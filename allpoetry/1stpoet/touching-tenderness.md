Touching Tenderness

He was touching tenderness
thumbing tears from blushed cheeks
his words had blown on the clouds
to her heart in that moment

He was touching tenderness
his mind opened as her eyes
shut softly
she fell into his arms

he was touching tenderness 
his hands holding her softly
One would not want to bend
tender angel’s wings
