Being

If you only had eyes
you could see the world as I have seen it
the darkness of night within a mind of a person
trapped in fear

a fear of living
not that death would be welcome, or a release
but the fear that in living one may taste life
the bitter realities that pain and hurt
loom around the corner, even though the moment holds pleasure

If you only had a mind
could you understand my thoughts
Why I ask questions to simple realities
think of ways of hiding my feelings towards others
because they too, may not understand what fragments of shrapnel
wicked words have been

would your feet fit the shoes
that I have walked in for forty years
Callused and cold at times, stuck firm in a dreamscape
for my feet have not touched earth for many days
I long to live in this place I go with wings
there is no more pain
there is no more love
there is nothing, only me

in death

death, you do not see
you do not feel
nor walk or think

yet you are more kind than those living
