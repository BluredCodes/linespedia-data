Angela Anaconda; Exotic Dancer

Satan took the form of a serpent
Beautiful
to tempt Eve, 
and there she was before me
beautiful
Angela Anaconda

sidewinding on stiletto heels
a rhythmic motion like a tongue
sensing the air around for prey
she spies me
Angela Anaconda

she clings to the brass pole
defying gravity
Breasts firm, pyramids of the god’s
reaching for the heavens
as her back arches
hair touching the floor
and her legs, like a boa; constricting the pole
Angela Anaconda

slowly sliding downward
slithering upon stomach towards me
eyes fixated
does she sense the heat I am producing
Angela Anaconda

she strikes
her touch is venom
I become rigid in my chair
I am her next meal

Angela Anaconda

William S. Dawes
