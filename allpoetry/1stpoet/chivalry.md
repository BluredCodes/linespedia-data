Chivalry

Forever young I will stay
From drinking at the bay
Of tranquillity you provide

You refresh my sapped strength
For you I would go any length
I venture on quests of love

I have no fears of dragon fire
Every move in me you inspire
I ride armored with your thoughts

Captive am I in your confines
You intoxicate me like vintage wine
Drunkenness is not a crime

Say the word, and I answer the call
All of your enemies I will make fall
My sword is sharpened by divine will

After all is said and done
Apart as two, together as one
You alone complete me
