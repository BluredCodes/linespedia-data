Red Flannel

I love her in red flannel
color of a bright apple
and that of my eye she is
the delicious taste of her kisses
my mouth begs for those juices
and I hunger for her beauty

I love her in red flannel
fire’s heat captured in her flesh
we make love and the embers of embrace
warm me
on cold days I cradle her closely
and the glow from her eyes
melts me

I love her in red flannel
a relaxing reflection of the sunset
riding upon her cloud-like whispers of love
blown upon my ears
I rest with her, as night comes
