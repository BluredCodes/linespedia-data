Love???!!!

The thought of love
was as complex as washing my hair
with cooked oatmeal
I could feel the warmth, the wetness
and the existence of what seemed to be lather

for having True Love
seemed as plausible
as teaching a hen to make chicken soup
Cannibalism came to mind
for it would be ingesting love to give it in return

Falling in Love
looks as safe as catching foul balls
with the tongue
Is the prize worth the taste of fresh blood?

Possibly yes
for love gives one the feeling
of being Invincible
Having no fear of powdering a nose
or brushing teeth with an ax

but Holding love can be like
carrying foxes in a berry basket
Well fed and resting, easy
disturbed and starving…they look for escape

I want love
for my life now only has the flavor
of Plastic Grapes.

William S. Dawes
