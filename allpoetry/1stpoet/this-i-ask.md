This I Ask	

Why does it take
a nation brought to its knees
to bring the people to their knees
in prayer

if love is stronger than hate
why does it take such hate
to bring out the love
of people

if we are the free
why do we live in fear
that our freedom is so vulnerable
when it is tested

violence will not squelch
violence
but cause the wheel to spin faster
when will the spinning stop
I feel dizzy

If our motto is “In God we Trust”
why take action ourselves
“Vengeance is mine says the Lord”

September 11, 2001This piece was written the morning the World Trade Centers collapsed.
I penned this about 5 minutes after the first tower fell. My co-workers were all going nuts about the end of the world....getting on their knees, and saying we needed to go after those who did this.
I started asking myself these questions...a self exam if you will.

WSD
