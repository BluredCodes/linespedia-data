Poetic Pondering

I write verse that ventures to my mind
at times when coffee, or sleep have wakened
thought and my muse
 
it seems in my writings
that people read the words
and want to place me at the scene
of the crime
the bliss
the love
or even the seashore at sunset

I have not a dog, nor a lover
yet to see and touch pleasures
pictured in ink is easy

in incense I can smell perfume
I can see a plant and think Jaded by Jade
in sleep I can ride dragons and slay giants
and in words I can take you to all of these places

Yes I ponder on poetry
I delve into thought
to tempt fingers to feather the keyboard
bring black to the white page
and allow you to see my world

however real or imaginative it is
it lives
in Poetry
