I Thought They were Stars

I looked out my window
across the frozen lake
saw the glimmers in the ice
and took them to be reflections

fallen snowflakes of light from the heavens
stars that twinkled in the moonlight
coming down to me

I walked out onto my balcony
reached out my hand
and felt the chill turn to droplets
as these stars died with my touch

each particle wished upon
solemn tears that were frozen in space
now captured by mortal man
I cupped my hand
raised it to my mouth
and tasted moon milk, and the spun honey of comets

the stars were brighter tonight
as I walked back inside
climbed into bed
and found myself no longer thirsting.
