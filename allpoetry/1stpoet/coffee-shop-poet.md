Coffee Shop Poet

Sitting, sipping
dreaming, writing
time drifts in the smoke

in the steam rising from the black coffee
words whisper softly to me
the language of my muse

from Istanbul  to Thailand
Iceland to Ireland
dialects are dipped into as my pen moves

distant lands traveled
by the mapping of words
a roadway drawn by ink

the smell of Indian incense
and the hot sands of the Sahara
fill you, and scorch you as you read

In the ashes on the floor
you see my footprints
yet I have walked within your mind

you taste me
the clove in the air
Black cigarettes, oiled sweetly

I am the coffee shop poet
I am the world you live in
I am the words that you read
I am eternal
