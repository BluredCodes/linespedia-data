WORDSPACE

If rhyme is the reason for writing
One may surely find a word
But it must make sense when seen
And even when it’s heard

Some say meter is what it takes
To get the point across
Throughout the words upon the page
There are those little breaks

Image is used to paint the scene
A canvas to our eyes
Moving like a movie on a screen
More meaning it implies

Emotion, oh that word
The reader it can touch
Heart strings pulled, the mind awake
Emotion does so much

Narrative or drama, well
These are also used
It helps one to follow along
And not be left confused

Some other tools that one can employ
Are intellect and wit
Shakespeare, Auden, and Tennyson
Seemed to make it fit

Now once the work is completed
Spelling and grammar checked once again
My sermon now is finished
All can say AMEN

William Dawes
July 2000
