---
title: "All I'm Asking"
writer: "BreakingSanity"
slug: "all-im-asking"
source: "AllPoetry"

Will you hold on tight,
if I ask you to let go?
Will you tell me,
if I don't want to know?

Will you live forever,
if I ask you to die?
Will you weep for me,
if I beg you not to cry?

Are you ready to hold on,
as I scream and kick?
Are you ready to comfort me,
through thin and thick?

Are you ready to love me,
when I don't want you to?
Are you willing to wait,
it's all I'm asking of you?
