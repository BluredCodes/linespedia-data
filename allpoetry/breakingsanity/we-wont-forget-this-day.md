title: "We Won't Forget This Day"
writer: "BreakingSanity"

Our tears will fall and our buildings will crumble,you may think you kick us down, but all you get is a stumble. Our pride stands tall and proud,you think you made a ruckus but I swear we're just as loud. Our children wear our colors; red, white and blue,those who don't support us are few. Today we're here to mourn the lost,but tomorrow we'll fight at any cost. Those who did this surely will pay,but whatever happens, we won't forget this day.
