---
title: "No Choice"
writer: "BreakingSanity"
slug: "no-choice"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/1998-No-Choice-by-BreakingSanity"
---

I can't be the beginning 
and I refuse to be the end.
It's not what we thought,
these lives are dead ends.

It was so long ago,
that all of us were here,
together we ran,
through life and fear.

Now though it's changed,
it's not the same,
you're an addict, I'm a whore.
But you're right it's the end that came.

My downfall won't hurt,
I won't loose my life,
but you, you can't stop.
And it may end your strife.

I wish I could change it,
hold true to my pledge.
You know I won't do it again.
But you were tipping off the edge.

There was nothing else,
I had no choice but to tell.
You think it'll kill you,
but dear you've already fell.

It's not fair,
the mistakes that we make.
But I swore as your friend,
your life it won't take.

I'm sorry you hate me,
but at least your alive,
now you have the chance to ignore me,
you wouldn't if you'd died.
