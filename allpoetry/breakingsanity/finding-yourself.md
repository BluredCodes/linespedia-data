---
title: "Finding Yourself"
writer: "BreakingSanity"

You say no one understands,
you say no one really cares.
You keep all of those feelings bottled up inside.
How do you expect to get any where?

You've tried so hard,
to be who they want you to be,
you lost yourself,
you know you'll never be free.

Trying to be the best,
isn't what you feel.
It's finding your happiness 
that shall make you real.

Make yourself,
don't let others lead.
Follow your heart,
and you'll find what you need.

Don't let yourself regret
all of the things you do.
Live you life like you want,
to yourself stay true.
