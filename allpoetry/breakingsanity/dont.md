---
title: "Don't"
writer: "BreakingSanity"
slug: "dont"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/4922-Dont-by-BreakingSanity"
---

Don't lie and tell me you'd do it all different,
when you know it would all turn out the same.

Don't look at me and say how much you love me,
when you know it's all a game.

Don't tell me it's all going to be okay,
when you know it's all gone to hell.

Don't tell me your still standing,
when you know you already fell.

Don't laugh when I say I love you,
when you know it's true.

Don't cry when I say it's over,
when you know it's all on you.
