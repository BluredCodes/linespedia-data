title: "Given"
writer: "BreakingSanity"

---

You spend all your time,
trying to be different
but in the end, 
you only end up the same.

You've given all your time,
trying to forget
but you've only found,
that you can't remember.

You spend endless nights,
trying to be forgiven
but you only find yourself,
falling asleep.

You've laid in your bed,
wondering what you've given
and in the morning,
you've given it all. 

You found the answer,
it's there the whole time
all the hours spent dreaming,
were dreams dreamt in vain.
