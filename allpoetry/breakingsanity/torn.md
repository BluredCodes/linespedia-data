---
title: "Torn"
writer: "BreakingSanity"
slug: "torn"
source: "AllPoetry"

I'm torn between what's wrong and right,torn between my heart and sight.You say to always follow your heart,but at the same time you say we'll never part.I've fallen between my dreams and what's real,no matter how much I say I love you, I don't know how I feel.My eyes can't see you anymore,I need to find what this life is for.
