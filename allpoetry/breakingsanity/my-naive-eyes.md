title: "My Naive Eyes"
writer: "BreakingSanity"
slug: "my-naive-eyes"

Don't ask me what's wrong, don't try to comfort me. Your looking at me like you give a damn. You've built your wall of lies very well.For so long I saw you as perfect,but day by day your wall has begun to fall. Now I see through you.You aren't the man I thought you were.You aren't my everything,don't do me any favors.Don't swear you'll be by my side.Don't offer to make it all OK,when you know it's all your fault.
