title: "A Little Bit Harder"

Wipe that smile off of your face.
Hold in those tears,
walk away.
Try to breath 
hold your breath.
Watch the words that you refuse to speak.
Try not to fall so hard,
or run so fast.
Don't stare so hard as you walk past.
Hold me closer,
let me go.
Don't try to convince me,
I already know.
You say it's nothing,
but it's everything to me.
You ask me to stay,
so I'll just let you be.
