title: "I Wanna Wake Up"
writer: "BreakingSanity"
slug: "i-wanna-wake-up"

Maybe things will get better if we let them.Maybe the rain will pass if we just wait it out.Maybe we have hope yet,maybe it's not as important as we thought.Maybe you don't hate me as much as you think. And maybe I don't think as much as I should.Maybe neither of us can stop and listen to the beautiful silence which surrounds us.Maybe we were both to blame.Maybe neither of us care as much as we should.Maybe one day I'll wake from this dream.
