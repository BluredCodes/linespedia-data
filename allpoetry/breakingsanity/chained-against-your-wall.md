title: "Chained Against Your Wall"

I'm standing here chained to this wall.
The chains that bind me are the words you spoke.
I'm looking out the window in my cell.
The sun is always setting
and the stars are always falling.
I don't know if I could make it out there.
The only thing I know for sure,
is that this wall I'm chained to,
won't fall.
You've built it strong,
with hateful words.
Meant to hurt me,
beat me break me.
Now the only thing I know that is real,
are the chains you've placed upon me,
and the wall you built around me.
