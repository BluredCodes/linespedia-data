---
title: "What Tomorrow Brings"
writer: "BreakingSanity"
slug: "what-tomorrow-brings"

Your fear of today
is hardly something you can say.
What has happened through the rain,
things will never be the same. 
Some say we can only blame ourselves,
but say that as you look at our pictures on the shelves.
If tomorrow shall come,
and we hear the beat of the war drum,
let us not forget today,
and what we shall forever pay.
