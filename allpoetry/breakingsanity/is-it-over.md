---
title: "Is It Over?"
writer: "BreakingSanity"
slug: "is-it-over"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I don't know what to do anymore.
My eyes are telling me one thing,
my heart another.
I see him and I see my future,
I look at you,
and I see my past.
You were so wonderful,
you showed me things I only ever dreamed.
But now we fight more,
and you don't seem so happy,
I don't seem so free.
I can't lie and 
say I don't still love you, 
how could I not?
When I look into your eyes 
I see my smiling face,
I can see thousands of kisses we shared,
and the nights of just sitting, talking.
I see the past few years,
but I don't see the next.
Maybe it's finally time to move on,
cuz everytime I see him,
I see you 
and the way you looked at me 
years ago.
