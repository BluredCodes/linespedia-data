I search my soul,
I scour my heart.
Walking through the years 
I wonder.
What do I do?
My heart and soul,
everything I am and want to be,
all is searched,
all is wished clean.
The pain
and sorrow.
The torment
and hate,
all willed away.
In all the years lost,
I've traveled so far,
so tired I've grown,
all the while,
on a quest for innocence.
