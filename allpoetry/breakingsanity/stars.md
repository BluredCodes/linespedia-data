---
title: "Stars"
writer: "BreakingSanity"
slug: "stars"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/1617-Stars-by-BreakingSanity"
---

You stand outside,breathe in the fresh air.Hold on to everything you wish you had. Don't cry those tears,they won't help at all.Gather all your courage,follow me.I know it's going to be different.But nothing ever stays the same.Try to be strong,they'd want it that way.Remember them all,those who've passed before.Look up at night,at every star that you see,for one day you know,those stars will be you,those stars will be me.
