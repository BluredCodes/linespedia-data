title: "Staining Me"
writer: "BreakingSanity"

There are droplets of you forming in my eyes.
You're falling down my cheeks now.
You have no respect for my sensitive skin. 
You're staining me all of the colors 
I thought you'd wiped away.
Everything you have become 
is exactly what you'd helped me kill.
I wore you on my sleeve,
I figured you'd stay there.
Funny, how I've seemed to have lost you now.
And even though I thought you cared,
I could have sworn I heard your laughter,
behind my tear-filled eyes.
