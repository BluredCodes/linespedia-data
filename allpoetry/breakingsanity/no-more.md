title: "No More"
writer: "BreakingSanity"

I tried to reach out to you,
I did everything that I could do. 
I prayed and cried, 
oh God, I've tried.
In my head I hear the words you speak.
Whispering the broken promises I was supposed to keep.
You say I stabbed you in the back,
you cry because of the qualities I lack. 
But I've got a few words for you,
You've made mistakes too.
I've asked your forgiveness a thousand times,
but you can't forget my thoughtless crimes.
I forgave you,
for all the pain you put me through.
I refuse to stand and watch you hate me,
I'm done apologizing, I have to set myself free.
I didn't want it to end this way,
but I'm not going to watch you walk on me another day.
