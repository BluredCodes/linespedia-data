title: "You Don't Know Me At All"

What will you say to me when I beg you for a reason?
There are no words,
but you can't stop talking.
I don't even hear you anymore. 
Raise your voice,
I still can't hear you! 
Don't scream at me,
when all you do is lie.
I've played your fool.
but I've never been good at acting.
You think I'm so stupid,
you can run all over my heart. 
You said you know me so well,
funny, you don't know me at all.
