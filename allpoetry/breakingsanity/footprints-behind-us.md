---
title: "Footprints Behind Us"
writer: "BreakingSanity"
slug: "footprints-behind-us"
source: "AllPoetry"

I was walking down the beach,
holding on to your hand.
As we got farther down the coast,
I realized how distant you had become.
Your eyes looked far beyond me.
So I cast mine out to sea,
letting my guard down for just a moment.
Now, all I have is that moment,
that moment and the footprints left behind us.
