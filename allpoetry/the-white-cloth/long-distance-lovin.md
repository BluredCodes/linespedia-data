Our passion drips between us
Our hearts beat fast and sure
Our breathing comes in mighty gasps
As we feel this love so pure.

The miles seem to melt away
As I listen to you moan
Our voices travel this lonely night
You come with me by phone.

You inspire these sexy words in me
And then the words inspire you
To scale such heights of ecstasy
I so gladly talk you through.

Your exciting sounds of pleasure
Lustful melody to my ears
They melt away the loneliness
And they stop the bitter tears.

As it gets hard to talk right now
Indeed, it’s tough to breath
I know that were you here this night
For sure I’d never leave.

And the love that’s great inside us
The hearts that beat so hard
Remind me of every kinky thought,
Fantasies in which you’ve starred.

So that when this so great distance
Has forever melted away
We’ll look back on this fondly
On what passions we’ve shared today.

With the chance to then renew our fire
While never being alone
We’ll hurl ourselves into the depths of love
Like we’ve just done on the phone.Sometimes when you can't be with the one you love... you "reach out and touch" them... ;-)
