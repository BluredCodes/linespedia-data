---
title: "I Found You"
writer: "the white cloth"
slug: "i-found-you"

I’m not sure how you found me 
Or is it that I found you? 
I’m not sure we suspected this 
But from such things our love grew 

I’m not sure I was a free-man 
But I know I’m now a slave 
My heart and soul have sold me out 
My permission I would have gave 

I was standing in the chains of loneliness 
Even though not quite alone 
But my heart could sense this magic there 
Through the email and then the phone 

I was bound in my self-made dungeon 
Naked for the world to see 
Thinking there was no hope and then 
You came and rescued me 

And I know this feeling’s mutual 
And I know the things you’ve said 
And I know the miracle of this love 
Has raised me from the dead 

And I know just how my heart leaps 
At the mere sound of your voice 
And I know the thoughts that fill my soul 
And then cause it to rejoice 

For my eyes behold your beauty 
And my ears capture your sound 
My mouth has tasted of your sweetness 
And you’re the purest taste I’ve found 

And my hands know how to love you 
And when you tremble and call my name 
I lead you through to passions rich 
And I’ll do it all again 

I would travel the world over 
Crawling on hands and knees 
If it meant a moment of joy to you 
I would cause all hell to freeze 

I will never cease to love you 
Not so long as I draw breath 
And my spirit’s vow is longer still 
Love does not have to stop at death 

I know you’ve felt it’s power 
I know you’ve touched it’s flames 
And I know that you’re amazed like me 
Destiny has called our names 

Is it that we were yielded vessels 
Is it that our needs are unique 
I do know that my life before 
Had gotten so very bleak 

But now I’m so full of passion 
My heart has renewed desire 
My life has found it’s direction now 
My soul has soared never higher 

And it’s all because I found you 
Or is it that you found me? 
Where we blessed by happenstance 
Or were we meant to be? 

It’s not all that important now 
Our love is in full bloom 
I happen to know ‘twas guided by Love 
Birthed of a holy womb 

But this magic that so enthralls us 
Has upon us etched our names 
And the Love that so blessed our lives 
Now fuels our eternal flames 

And it is all because I found you 
And it’s also that you found me 
And the time was right and we were ripe 
Like sweet fruit upon a tree 

And I know we’ve both had failures 
And we’ve known our share of pain 
But through the fires of hell we came 
Sweet peace we did attain 

And I know your name’s here on me 
Etched right here on my heart 
And I know I’ve left my mark on you 
With love I did impart 

But that love was fueled by you 
Your soul cried out to mine 
And mine answered the call you gave 
Urged on by Love divine 

And I’m glad that you did find me 
And I’m glad that I found you 
And I’m glad that passions filled our lives 
And bound us as with glue 

And you know how much I love you 
Though the words fail to convey 
Every time I try to tell you more 
The hours just slip away 

But I know that you can sense it 
As our spirits have their commune 
Two hearts that have now beat as one 
Two souls that are in tune 

And I know just where to find you 
And you know for sure I will 
I’ve had this empty, vacant place 
That just your love can fill 

And I’m so very glad you found me 
And I’m glad that I found you...January 2002, This is one of those poems that wrote itself... I just sat and let the words leap from my fingers to the keyboard... and had I not forced myself to stop, might STILL be writing it!
