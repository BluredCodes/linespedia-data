---
title: "Sweet Waters"
writer: "the white cloth"
slug: "sweet-waters"
source: "AllPoetry"

From the depths of our hearts 
From even deeper; our souls 
We’ve found this sacred fountain 
From whence this sweet love flows 

The waters that are bubbling 
Like a spring for me and you 
Refreshing our very spirits 
By the things we say and do 

I drink of your love daily 
And you bathe yourself in mine 
We nourish one another 
And our hearts do seem to shine 

Each day we have each other 
Gives Heaven a new name 
Each night we share our passions 
Another spark to this great flame 

Your eyes they do excite me 
Your flesh drives me so wild 
Your voice is music to my soul 
And I feel quite like a child 

Your hands explore my body 
And mine find their way to yours 
And you wonder just what you might find 
When you rummage through my drawers 

Some nights we find the kinky side 
And others we know sweet peace 
This magic love just seems to grow 
Those sweet waters to release 

One thing I’ve come to count on 
To know beyond a doubt 
Is that the love we share between us 
Is what life is all about.
