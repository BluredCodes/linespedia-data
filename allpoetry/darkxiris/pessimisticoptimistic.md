The higher I climb, the further I fall
Everywhere I turn my shadows stand tall
You may say i'm something
But you treat me like I'm nothing at all

No more kisses:
You're feeling sick
I am too:
I'm pessimistic

I fall asleep, my insides scream
I find no release inside my dreams

Feels like everything we have is falling apart
Bitter thoughts I wish they'd never start
I found I'm not the only one inside your heart

Look and listen to me for once, I'm battered
The heart of glass I gave you has been shattered

Cut my nerves away, I don't need them anymore
And I don't need you,  my angelic whore.
