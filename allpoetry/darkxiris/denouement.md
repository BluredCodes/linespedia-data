title: "Denouement"
writer: "DarkXIris"

If you're going to kill me 
make it fast and quick
This lie I'm living is
starting to make me sick
I'll cut myself
until I cannot feel
Everything I thought we had
proved to never be real
I wish I could feel love
just a little bit
But I find myself giving
and never receiving it
I wish this weren't the
way it had to end
But I'm already gone
and I'll never love again
