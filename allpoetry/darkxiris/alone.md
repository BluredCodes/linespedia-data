title: "Alone"

I found the prettiest girl
She asked my name
She gave me a flower
I was never the same
We fell in love
We were happy
The sun shined 
We were laughing
Months went by
Winds became cold
The flower withered
Our love grew old
Then that flower died one day
The sun didn't rise, skies turned gray
"My love, our flower died, may i have another?"
"Of course. I'll give you a special one, my dearest lover."
I grasped it tight, and my hand bled
Blood trickled, my skin turned red
I dropped the flower
Tears ran down my face
Is this the real world
Or just a sour taste?
I glanced back at my love
Found only to see
Her and another boy:
She didn't care about me
I looked at the flower
Lying on the ground
It was covered in thorns
and I lost all I had found
I lost the only love I had ever known
Since then, I have never felt so alone
