title: "Bloodlips"

All I ever did was give
all she ever did was take
And she couldn't keep this promise
she said that she'd never break
She read my feelings
just like a book
She had a secret
at which she never let me look
She broke my spirits
as often as she could
She watched me suffer
because she knew I would
She laid me down 
as I gave up
I closed my eyes
and I never woke up
