I had a dream
Everything I feared and worried about came true
But I never woke up:
Now I can barely stand to look at you
The sky is slowly 
falling down on me tonight
You used to be my candle
but you've lost your light
When ever there is sun
You cast on me a shade
It has grown bigger
with every broken promise you have made
I'm leaving:
Nothing is a perfect as it once seemed
And I the closest I ever got to you
was in my dreams
