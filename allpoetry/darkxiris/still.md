---
title: "Still"
writer: "DarkXIris"
slug: "still"
source: "AllPoetry"

I:
Shaking at the end of this tunnel
on the ground that I stand
I'm blind in the darkness
Reaching out for your hand
Many times I've stated my case
But you don't understand
What do you think of me now?
Do you think I'm a man?

I've been searching for someone
I've been searching for you
Searching through darkness
too black to see through
Ironic: I think I'm dying
Still so young in my youth

II:
Still living in darkness
Still living in pain
There's nothing left for me
Nothing to lose or gain
You've given me love
You've given me a taste
You've shown me a reason
Life isn't always a waste

But I'm still searching for someone
I'm still searching for you
Still searching in darkness
Still too dark to see through
Still think I'm dying
Still think I need you

III:
I'm still a bit damaged
Tourmented by pain that we cause
The pain digs deep into me
Like razor-sharp claws
I still think I love you
I still think you're strong
But I don't know if I've lost you
Or if you're already gone

I'm still searching for someone
I'm still searching for you
Still searching in darkness
Still too dark to see through
Still think I'm dying
Still think I need you

IV:
I won't turn my back
I have no regrets
You gave my life meaning
That moment we met
I knew what I wanted
My mind was set
I wanted you since that moment
That moment we met

I've been searching for someone
I've been searching for you
I was clouded in darkness
It was too black to see through
These feelings you've had for me
Before, I never knew
But I found a light shining
That light: it was you
