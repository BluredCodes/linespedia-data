Please don't stare
I'm feeling strange
While you're blissed
I feel deranged
A dirty secret
I never knew
Another worry
I will abuse
Feel me up
I'm feeling used
Tarnished by the damage
That we do
A mist stays
Once was see-through
I need this feeling
I need you
Sugar-sweetened
Baby names
You look at me
I don't feel the same
Once exciting
Now is lame
Once was dirty
Now is tame
Once a comfort
Remains a strain
Once a love
Is now a pain
Tarnished by this damage
That we cause
Like a problem
That we'll never solve
Give me a kiss
I'll call you God
I'll be benefit
To your cause
Don't leave me now
It's not too late
But you're already
Trying to escape
You love to tease
You love to stare
At other boys
I can't compare
Now you give me
Another use
Another lie
Yet a new excuse
Now look at my
Broken heart
So exquisite
You'll never start
We've been here
Wasting time
A bitter taste
Of lemon-lime
A bit tarnished
Still you shine
I'm just another person
Standing in your line
Wipe my blood
Upon your drapes
Give me reason
Give me taste
I'll do my best
Won't go to waste
My broken heart
Will mend with tape
This feeling of
Another shape
You feel nothing
I feel raped
