title: "Polygamy"

I'm still alive
But still drowning in the water
I thought I had found the girl of my dreams
But I lost her

It all disintigrated
and she left me here to lay
I made my biggest mistake
and watched her walk away

Listened to the voices
What did I find?
Somebody else holding her
I thought her heart was mine

But I should have known;
This is so typical of me
I'm left feeling used
I wish I weren't so naive

She beats me with the hand i used to hold
She was my goddess: my ultimate influence
When she let go, my heart grew cold
and I've never been the same ever since
