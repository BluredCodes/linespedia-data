I recently discovered how rude children are these days...
If you're not a cheerleader, volleyball player, basketball player, etc.
Means you are nothing, to kids these days...You can't hang with them unless you are in there little clicks..
Picking on others because of the clothes they wear. One child may be dressed in all black and pitch black hair..To everyone they think the child is a freak..When in reality, the child is expressing their own feelings through their ensemble..Just because you wear fancy clothes and name brand doesn't make them any better than the ones who dress different..Let people live their lives how they want instead of how others want..
