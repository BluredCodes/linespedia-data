---
title: "True Love"
writer: "Lovergirl"
slug: "true-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/47674-True-Love-by-Lovergirl"
---

I finally found someone who loves me, for me
Brought together by God’s hand
Knowing what I had always wanted
Love can be right before your eyes
Never realizing it, until it is gone
Love taken for granted, lost in vain
When it is true, you will know it
The one you will cherish for life
Feeling the warmth between you and your lover
Something deeper more than words can say, is there
When the world turns its back on you
Your true love will always be there for you
Understanding your every need
