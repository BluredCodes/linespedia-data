---
title: "Blank paper"
writer: "solange"
slug: "blank-paper"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/7444-Blank-paper-by-solange"
---

Patchwork thoughts appear
on paper
Phantom shadows from
my mind
Come and go like
morning vapor
Leaving part of 
me behind
In the silence 
of a moment
Just before a
poem is born
