You were the most popular girl in school
I could never see what you saw in me
My heart ached when you broke the dating rule
Other boys danced with you and you could never see
I wanted you to be free and I did not say a word
All I asked was for the last dance to be with me
I loved you so much my happy little bird
I knew you might flirt but your heart belonged to meThis poem was inspired by the song "Save the last dance for me" by the Drifters.
