At drive-in moviesRolled up steamy windowsBack seat mystery'sCold sweat all overShe says her period is lateYou just hold her handFake ID buys beerGet sick and lose all your lunchForbidden delightsTake a drag my manNo it wont do you no harmListening to liesI want to go momMy country needs me to fightShe just weeped softly
