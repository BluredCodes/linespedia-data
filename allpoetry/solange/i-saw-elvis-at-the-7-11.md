He was buying a dozen donutsand a quart of milkWearing sunglasses and a shirt made of silkI spoke up and asked himif he was the kingHe just smiled sadlyand mumbled No,I don't sing He turned an ambled outthrough the doorI thought my jaw would bounce off the floorHis pink caddie shown in the bright sunOn the vanity tag werethe words 'Hound Dog 1'
