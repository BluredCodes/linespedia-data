title: "Nocturne to her ebon eyes"
writer: "solange"
slug: "nocturne-to-her-ebon-eyes"

I saw the sun one morning
It smiled and seemed to say
Why do you shield your eyes
as you go on your way
Do you not gaze longingly 
toward the rising moon?
Do thoughts of falling stars 
send your heart to swoon
I humbly replied pray do not
plague me so the reason to display
For love rides on wings of fantasy
till stolen by the day
All night my mind is 
free to dream
My memory strolls along a 
running silvered stream
Once I knew a gentle girl
with eyes of ebon black
Only in the dark of night 
will ever she be seen
In dreams we walked in fields
of stars 
two lovers hand in hand
She walked along beside me
Sweet scented was her hair
I heard her pleasing voice
float lightly in the air
She seemed to fade as each 
star grows dim at dawn
And slips back into my memory
as silent as a fawn
