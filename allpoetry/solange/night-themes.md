title: "Night themes"
writer: "solange"

Soft velvet
darkness on the land
Nights caress

Starlight shines
A carpet of gems
Sky treasures

Night time fears
go bump in the night
Robbing sleep

Dreams flow by 
Pictures in the mind
Saying much

Rest in sleep
Restoring your body
Till the morn
