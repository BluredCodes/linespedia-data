title: "Don't ever Tell"
writer: "solange"

People who have a secretEven one they can never forgetDaddy why do you touch me thereI don't understand why you say you careFamily's torn apart by this sicknessIn behavior they coldly dismissLife's for ever destroyed by an actEvil spawned will never retractI will not point fingers but the persons who are guilty will get the message.I don't have to name the ones.Their own words will uncover them.
