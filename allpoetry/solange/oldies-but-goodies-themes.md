title: "Oldies but goodies themes"

Slow unsure shuffling
With her head on my shoulder
When I fall in love

Arms and legs frailing
Wild gyrations on the floor
Pulsing tribal beat

Light hand on her back
Swaying with slow rhythem
Close,but not too close

From across the room
She sits with all her girl friends
Will she say no thanks

Music time machine
Each song brings a memory
Oldies but goodies
