Sweaty palms
Even with three showers
Meet her dad

Sitting there
in the cars back seat
Mother drives

Movie was bad
Arm on her seat back
Her nearness

At her door
you shake hands and smile
Date me again?

Back at school
waiting for your bus
You see her

Being shy
You just grin at her
She knows
