I once was a boy
Then I was told my name
I am a maggot

Buzz cuts crown our heads
We are shorn sheep
lost in a big herd

Inspections are not fun
Perfection is never reached
They always find faults

Marching builds team work
The hard asphalt hurts my feet
Keep in step and sing

I made it through
A lean mean fighting machine
The rights of passage
