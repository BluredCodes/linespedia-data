Rant about howIt ain't fair.Neurotic obsessionwith suicide.Rambling angry poemswith hatred inside.Scream about howevery one is blind.Yet ignore yourown narrow mind.You want your freedom?The pay your dues.Open yourself to some one elses partLivings not easywith out any cluestry an open mind.Now thats a start.
