If Wishes Came True...

I've wished she would just leave
  then my anquish would surely end
I've wished she would hurt him
  and then they would come to an end.

She doesn't really love him
  I've seen his heart in pain
Heartache stacked upon heartache
  that I know I can take away

My love is his honest love
  I've wished he could really see
my open, fragile heart
  yearning to set his free

I've wished for a bonified miracle
  to open the doors for me
I've wished she would vanish into thin air
  at the least she could set him free.

I've wished that he would leave her
  and come to me for life
instead of sneaking stolen hours
  secretly in the night

All my wishes are in vain
  I knew it from the start,
but if wishes came true, and he was mine
  I'd have a happy heartJust don't say I told you so.
