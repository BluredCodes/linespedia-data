Ebony tears I have shed for you
down pale ivory cheeks, tinged with blue
ebony thoughts laced with dull red
anger bubbling inside of my head
choking myself until I fly high
on ivory wings into the sky
lost in clouds of ebony storms
my madness has so many forms
pain until my mind is blank ivory
wiped clear of thoughts of you...
how it sets me free.I wrote this last night and.. I'm still not sure what it really means. I think it's kind of bad, though. D= Or it could just be that I just got done reading some of "The Oddest"s poetry. Anyone would feel unworthy after those exquisite pieces.
