The sound for the deaf,
the sight for the blind,
something to tickle the senses
of the paralyzed.

Seen only by the observant,
heard only by the silent,
felt only by the still,
a land of wonder for all.

The place of unicorns,
where ogres dwell in castles,
and knights rescue damsels,
a world of imagination.

So sit still and wait,
close your eyes to see,
plug your ears to listen,
the wonders of life are here....eh. Comment as you wish, rate as you wish. It's for lack of anything else to upload...
