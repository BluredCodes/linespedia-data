title: "Enigma"

Glistening black eyes
like the midnight sea
the mystery of the ocean
staring back at me.

Secrets like sharks
ready to rip you in half
hiding in dark waters
to leave a bloody aftermath.

Laughter like shoals
of bright silver fish
to hear it more often
is just what I wish.

Sadness like shadows
of deep liquid caves
unexplored and endless
unable to be saved.

And then just eyes again
staring into mine
I love their enigma utterly
they're yours, my love divine.I love Jake's eyes. :)
