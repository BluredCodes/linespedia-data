---
title: "I'm Sorry"
writer: "Spoot"
slug: "im-sorry"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I
didn't know
what I had when I
held it in my bitter hands.
What a perfect thing
I possessed
you
were just mine
such a finely wrought
person, held at a distance
without thinking I
crumbled your
soul
unknowingly
brutalizing the
delicate architecture
of your caring heart,
I was so
cruel.

I'm sorry.I miss my Jeff.
