title: "Sunrise"

Shades of gold
paint the sky, burnt
rose diffused and entwined
with pink clouds swimming in color
brushing the sky with orange and mauve
to chase the blue of the sky away, along with
the  blues  of  the  people  below  it,  this  morning.No real shape or form.
...
I get bored.
You don't even have to comment on it.
...I need a life.
