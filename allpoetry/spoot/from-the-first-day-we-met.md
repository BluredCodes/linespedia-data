From the first day we met
I handed you my heart
gave you my love in a smile
before the crowd tore us apart.

Wondering, wanting.

The second time we passed
in the busy halls that day
I walked up to you and requested your name
you told me politely and went on your way.

Dreams in the night.

In the morning, before class,
you walked towards me with a grin
your sapphire eyes captivated me
my soul that day you did win.

Hoping, praying.

We held each other in slow steps
the night of the senior prom dance
it was perfect as a fairy tale
a wonderful story of romance.

Love goes on.

From quiet night to still morning
we held one another with tender affection
knowing for certain, without a doubt,
that we had found the entirety of perfection.

Soul mates.

After a night of dinner and candles
you got down upon one knee
my eyes grew wide, my heart stopped beating,
you always brought such joy to me.

Wedded bliss.

Late one evening my stomach jolts
and the next whole day is blurred pain
my only memory is your warm hand
through labor, you kept me sane.

First child.

Now all of our children are at college
your golden curls are steely grey
now at the sunset of our lives
I still turn to you at the end of the day.I think the whole thing's weird and the only reason I'm putting it up is because Matt said that if I didn't, he'd get off the phone, come over here, and drop another cookie down my shirt.
