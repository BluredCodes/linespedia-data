---
title: "Drowning in Honey"
writer: "Spoot"
slug: "drowning-in-honey"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/53531-Drowning-in-Honey-by-Spoot"
---

Sweet honeyed words on the tip of your tongue.

Up to my neck in thick, golden lies, brewed
to perfection in the wax catacombs
of your bitter soul, crafted to fool me.

The mellifluous deceptions of love gone wrong.

My lips are sealed with poisonous syrup,
silencing me from speaking out against
your hollow, sugarcoated promises.
