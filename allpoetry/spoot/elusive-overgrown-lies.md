---
title: "Elusive (Overgrown Lies)"
writer: "Spoot"
slug: "elusive-overgrown-lies"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/47705-Elusive--Overgrown-Lies--by-Spoot"
---

How elusive your love is.

Elusive like the wind,
and dandelion seeds
spread to the breeze.

Elusive like sand
held in a child's
tightly cupped hands.

Elusive like the water
strained through golden
rays of sunlight.

You keep running away
leaving me with no stigma
your love trailing behind you
like a camoflauged string
through overgrown grass
I try to catch it
so I can keep you close to me
but each time I grasp
my fingers close on weeds
grown large and complex
like your childish lies.

Please stop running
let me hold onto your love
like a cherished relic
holy to my being
I promise I won't hold too tight
confining your freedom
I promise I won't keep you too close
I'll give you free reign
just let me find your love
if it still exists
in this overgrown
entanglement
of little white lies
grown into tall, dark tales
that you add on to
until nobody remembers the truth.

How elusive you are.I'm not particularly sure what I think of this one... I wasn't really paying attention when I set it down, I just wrote it.
