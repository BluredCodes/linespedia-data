---
title: "Reborn"
writer: "Spoot"
slug: "reborn"

Raindrops pounding onto my head
encasing me in opalescent aqua
until I see the world through a prism.

Prism refracts their personalities,
rain washing clean their layers of paint
and leaving their true colors exposed.

Exposed in the glory of sunlight
beautiful people shine through
deception washed clean.

Clean and reborn by the wash of water,
sweet drops peeling away their lies,
leaving them pure to start fresh.Rain makes me happy. =D
