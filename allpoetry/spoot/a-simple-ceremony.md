title: "A Simple Ceremony"

A simple ceremony
for we are not wealthy
in private testimony
that our blooming love is healthy.

Surrounded by fresh flowers
a sweet rainbow on display
the wedding lasts for hours
a romantic soiree.

The day passes in a dream
of your eyes and your hands
unconditional love, our theme
exquisite promises, no demands.
