Today, I am lost

Sifting through items that make up my home,

those "treasured" possessions,

desperately searching to find ME.



I have become scattered.



Scattered about a temporary world.  



Guitars beneath MY wrought iron bed

George Winston CD's, stacked on MY floor

a watch, in a pocket, thought lost.



It's the "stuff" that tells the tale.

All of those things that express my manner

express my soul

compose my being

representing the life I lead, and who I am, incompletely.



That tentative life I live

time spent, time gone

vague memories

fallen on the floor next to 'December'

 

Scattered.
