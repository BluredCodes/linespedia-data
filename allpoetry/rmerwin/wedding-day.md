Arms outstretched
drinking in a sky so vast
it was as if I’d died
and gone to heaven

You were there, of course
encompassing it all
and the moment just kept coming

As I saw the altar
distant, as if in a corner somewhere
it was unreal and unimposing 
I looked to you

Then I prayed

Not for god,
for the pursuit of him

For the first time
I hoped for long life
and eternity

We took our vows
and we were married.
