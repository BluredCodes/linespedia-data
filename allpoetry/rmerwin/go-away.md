Grey

It was always grey
and so were you

You cared so little
but meant so much

The time between us
each tender touch
had slipped between your fingers
passed right through your brain
and all the love I'd given you
had all been love in vain

Wasted

It was always wasted
and so were you

Like the whisper of a mother to a sleeping child
who gently sighs
fast asleep
life passed through you with good intention 
and you never knew
"I" passed through you
and you never even knew

Wasted and grey
is what you've become
and I am becoming like you
A wasted grey and sensless existence
is all you've left us to

Go away.
