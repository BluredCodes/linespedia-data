We were skipping rocks that day,my father and Ithose little flat flurries of hopehovering for a moment then plunging out of our sight into an unknownI wanted so much to go farther in lifeskip just one rock farther than all of hiscareer, house, car, wifethey would all be better.I've been throwing rocks for a long time,it only makes me unhappy, I think I'll stop now.I could always pick the best rocks, you know,He was just stronger and smarter.If you have something on this... let me know
