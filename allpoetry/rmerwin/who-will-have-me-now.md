She had dismissed me out of hand again.Just as the welling of my eyes had come,swiftly, abruptly,I'm leaving her.My love for her waning.Waning like a moon that lives to dance among the trees, with no regrets.Disappointment blurring innocence, blurring ignorance.Dis heartened, bleeding innocence, pleading ignorance, pleading me.She'll place me in her heart, and in her hand again.Please me with her charms, and with her love again.As the welling of my eyes had become dry,I calmly and completely left her side.Who will have me now?
