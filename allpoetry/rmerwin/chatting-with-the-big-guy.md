God and I had a chat tonight
He told me what he thought
I'm not sure how I feel just yet
I'm still in a bit of a shock
Seems I'm disillusioned
thinking the life I live's okay
He showed me all my faults
and then went about his way

Seems that I've been dreaming
feeling in control
Seems I lost my way back there
not on the path at all

God and I had a chat today
I'm sure I'll be alright
I just need to follow his heart, you see 
when confronted with the fightjust a quick thought
