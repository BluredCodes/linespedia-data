Time,
lingering in a moment so long
it becomes permanent
like a tatoo.
Stationary, on an ever changing canvas.
Young 
   taut 
     skin 
       grows 
          old.
Moments of permanence
ink vivid memories on the canvas' of our minds.
Fresh and sharp, 
muting with age,
the moments remain unchanged.
Suspended, like tatoos,
these portraits are false eternities
to be lost forever in the passing of lives untold.
