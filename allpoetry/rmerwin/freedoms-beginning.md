As his great hands finished removing the training wheels 
the seat was the only thing left 
a small adjustment, then off we'd go 
to a life full of freedoms limitless. 
I remember him holding the sissy bar 
assuring me I was okay 
certain he's right there behind me 
steady I ride on my way 
I look over my shoulder to see him 
knowing he's holding me safe 
my heart jumps, he's in the yard cheering 
I remember the look on his face. 
My mother was jumping and screaming 
Go Ricky, you're doing great 
I remember how much it meant to us all 
I'm still thankful for that time today. 

In that moment, I was free for the first time. 
I could go anywhere 
do anything. 
Maybe even fly.
