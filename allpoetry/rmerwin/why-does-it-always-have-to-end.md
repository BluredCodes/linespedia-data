She's said it nowthat irreversible tinge that lingers in my stomach it is over.What about the promisesthe plansthe furniture we shopped for to make the home we both dreamed of, forever?we were so close.Only details remain nowthe distribution of emotional severances, heaviness of heartthe apologies.What about our familiesthe holidaysthe gifts I had so painstakingly selected and subsequently hidden for so long?Why does it have to end?Why does it always have to end?
