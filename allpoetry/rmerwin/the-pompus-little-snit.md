I've read your scithing pointed words 
not works of poets 
like scrapping little girls
squeeling and fighting in dresses just pressed
home to their mothers in wrecked sunday's best
I've listened a bunch and I've spoken a bit
now I am tired of this pompus little snit
So I'll exit the venue of those who don't hear
In leaving I'll make myself perfectly clear
whatever is done or whatever is said
we're all here together
one earth till the end
So show me some tolerence
cut me some slack
and give me some credit
for giving it back.
