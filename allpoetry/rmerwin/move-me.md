Move MeTeetering in the balance of a far off conversation  I sit and ponderwho must I be to join the world?  Persecuted, scrutinized, never really justifiedI sit and wonder.  Balanced at the edge of some climax yet to be exploredI wonder who will save me  Who will come to defend MY side  my innocence, my ignorance   who will teach me how to 'be' in the face of a world that forsakes me?Stumbling at the brink of decent to who knows what lowsand who cares, really.The rising tide of individuality 		stemming from brutalityam I so different?Standing strong on my plateau of contempt justified in my strength, 		my stubbornnessI am a rock, now,MOVE ME.edited thanks to a special friendLemme have it.
