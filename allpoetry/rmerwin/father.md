He didn't say he was leaving
and then he left, I'm not sure why
He left my mother, left our home
their three kids all alone to die

He didn't leave us anything
he walked right out the door
didn't give us any chance
we're sleeping on the floor

His brother called here once or twice
His mother too ashamed
His sister gave us money once 
you know it's all the same

Can't he be responsible 
for the lives he's cast away
can't he bring himself to face
the mess he made that day

Now that I have grown up
I hold my children near
the family we have brought forth
withour fail, I will be here
