Your magical words woo me
little dashes and curves 
pencil upon paper
you are affecting my mind
again.

Where are we off to?

Descriptive little notions 
unfolding the finest nuances of the mind and spirit
those little things we do
you recognize us all.

What manner of emotion will you unweave
what symbol of wisdom to believe
where simple men, of simple minds
drawn out from shadows, no longer hide?

I am not afraid.
