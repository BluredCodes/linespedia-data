Crashing to the streets a moments fury 
casting shadows on our hearts as they both fell
the bitter taste of terror shaking strong men
the crippling deeds of cowards bring forth hell.

Young men called upon to do their duties
children cower helpless, in the womb
A nation torn with anger, torn with grieving
vengeance coming swiftly
calling loved ones forth to doom.

So fight the good fight,
do your civic duty,
buy Christmas gifts, and turkeys
all the while
remember those who hate us
and sleep lightly
those who teach their children bitter lies.

Give blessing to the ones that live with hatred
compassion for each loved one left behind
no victor without bloodshed, no good reason
remember those who lose thier lives and hear their cries.

And please God don't condemn the children
share your love and give them tenderness
deliver our world from all this needless hatred 
in your great name I pray this, God, amen.
