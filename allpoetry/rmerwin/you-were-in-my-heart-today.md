You were in my heart today
the child you lost was there too
My pain for you both just a fraction
of the pain that I know it's caused you
I can't tell you enough 
how I'm sorry
the loss you endure is not fair
Hold tight to the candle you burn for him
I'm sure that he senses you're there.

My deepest sympathies.  I wish I could do something to help you.  I'm so sorry I'm helpless to comfort you. God bless.No one should ever loose a child
