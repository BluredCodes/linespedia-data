I'm here, you see
my little pen
my little light
in a corner of the room 
scribbling.

Words pour forth
mind
pen 
page.

Heart break
falls in tears
upon tiers
upon pages, never shared.

Secrets
painstakingly laid to rest
now tucked under the mattress 
safe,
so I can sleep.
