It is I a tourquoise...
(don't ask me what that means...LOL)
Hmmmmm Bandersnatch!
(my kinda stuff!)
Unite!
(now what with that?)
Stealth is my middle name!
(remember there's always someone watching!)
and if i watch, i see!
(but, who knows what i'm watching!)
let me in! let me in! let me in!
(but what do i do when i get in?)
