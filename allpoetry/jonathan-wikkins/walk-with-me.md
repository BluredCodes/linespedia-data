title: "Walk with Me..."

There was a day,
we walked hand in hand,
together…
Hopes and dreams, all around…
But now,
she waits for me,
patiently…

For her love for me,
goes beyond the physical
and
the emotional,
and
touches
upon the spiritual… 

Just out of reach,
she is…
But there,
just the same…

For when my day comes,
she will come for me,
her smile full and bright…

She will say to me:
“I have come for you,
my love,
take my hand,
once again,
and
walk with me…

And we will walk
through eternity,
together,
hand in hand,
as before…
