Halloween is coming soon…

     For most it’s a happy time…
     A time for ghosts and goblins, 
          harvest moon, jack-o-lanterns, 
               and the like…

     But not for me… 

     A time to say gladly, 
          “trick or treat”.   
     What I got one Halloween, 
          was neither trick nor treat…
     It’s even a time for hot air balloons, 
          snuggling with someone special 
               against early winter’s chill…

     But not for me… 

     It's a time to plan 
          our winter activities,
               skiing, snowmobiling and more.  
     It is a time to dust off 
          snow shovels, 
               and make sure your winter clothes 
                    fit and are still warm….

     It’s time for All Saint’s Day
          and all that entails…
     All these things do is remind me 
          that the first of November
               is almost here,
                    and all that goes with it.   
     It is a day 
          that will be burned
               into my memory
                    as long as I have one… 

     It is a day that, 
          for the foreseeable future,
               I will go to a very special place…
     There I will sit upon a stone, 
          and remember…
     I will remember 
          what was good in my life,
               and what was not…

     I will remember what happened… 

     I will cry, 
          for it is a sad day indeed, 
               for me…
     It is a day
          when all my hopes and dreams, 
               were dashed into a million pieces…
     That awful day, 
          when I looked out 
               into the sunrise, 
                    and said:
     “Baby, you missed a beautiful sunrise.”   
          and she seemed to say to me: 
      “No I didn’t, my love,
           I was here, and shared it with you…
      Even though you can’t see me, 
           I’m here with you.”…

     I feel I have remembered 
          many other things 
               she seemed to say 
                    at that moment…
     For you see 
          on that dreadful day, 
               the day after Halloween
                    that year,
               I lost something special in my life…   

     That day, 
          when after a valiant fight, 
               the love of my life went away, 
                    never to return…
     And I will visit that special place, 
          from no matter where I live, 
               where my wife is buried, 
                    on the first of November, 
                         the anniversary of her death…
