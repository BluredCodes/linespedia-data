At times in our lives...

Love grows,
    becomes an evolution…

For with time
    things must evolve…

When our lives find love, 
    it comes in many ways.

The best love, can come unexpectedly;
    it may simply fall into our laps.

Sometimes a “hi” is all it takes.

How we meet is unimportant.

It’s our souls that know what and who
    we’re looking for.

Our souls meet in mysterious ways,
    and we never know how it happens,
          or why it happens.

We just have to accept it and realize,
    there may be a bigger plan,
          that we are destined to follow.

Love can start off simply,
    just as two people who may meet,
          in line at the grocery store.

Or two people who’ve known each other for years.

We never know, if our soul mate is,
    the next person we may meet.

It’s our souls, that determine,
    who we’re drawn to do, in our lives.

Then love just simply seems to evolve from there,
    almost naturally, easily, without question…
