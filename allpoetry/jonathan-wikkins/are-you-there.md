---
title: "Are You There?"
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"
slug: "are-you-there"

It is I, 
     the muse… 
I have returned 
     once again…

This time I have returned
     with a message, 
          and a request…

It is time 
     for you
          to awaken…
It is time for 
     the forces of good to unite…

You know who you are,
     some of you…
Others will awaken 
     when their souls 
          are spoken to…

The alliance is not 
     what is expected…
Those of you, 
     who witness,
          must find it
               within yourself…
To know that the alliance 
     is what is necessary…
It was formed millennia ago, 
     when the war began…

The alliance has been active 
     through other battles,
          for other worlds… 
Now is the time 
     for the alliance to awaken… 
Now is the time 
     to fight the battle 
          for this world… 

The battle will be difficult…
As battles between good and evil 
     always have been… 
Trouble is, 
     the outcome is not certain,
          it never has been… 

For those of you 
     not destined to fight, 
          do not despair, 
               do not judge…
Do not think ill 
     of those battles 
          you witness, 
               even if they do not appear 
                    to be battles… 

For evil has done its job 
     very well,
          as it has in the past… 
It doesn’t want you 
     to realize the true battles, 
          for then, it can deceive you, 
               deception is its greatest weapon,
                    for then it makes
                         your mind its own… 

And if it succeeds 
     then you fight half its battles for it,
          and you don’t even realize it… 

The battles you witness, 
     if you do…
Will not all be battles 
     between flesh and blood… 
Many will be battles 
     between principalities of power…

Sometimes they will be obvious,
     two entities, 
          one human, 
               one not…
In a sword battle to the end… 

Other times they will be hidden 
     within your mind…
Or in the guise of “progress”…
They may speak of a “One World Government”,
     or a cashless society…
Or even something as simple 
     as an “ID chip”…

Many battles will be fought 
     in your homes…
Siblings against siblings… 
Mother against father… 
All against each other… 

Sometimes evil spirits 
     will inhabit your homes, invisibly,
          creating havoc in ways 
               you’ve only seen
                    in science fiction or horror… 
Sometimes it will even be visible…
But mostly 
     it will be within 
          one or more minds 
               within your homes… 

Now to you,
     the alliance… 
The message I have been given 
     to say to you is as follows… 

Follow your heart 
     where it takes you…
For it shall know 
     where to lead you…
To find yourself, 
     for the fight that must 
          take place… 

It is I, 
     the muse… 
I shall return…
When the time is right 
     for the rest of the message…
