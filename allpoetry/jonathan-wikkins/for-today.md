---
title: "For Today"
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"

For today we are all gathered here, 
     to witness something special… 

A wedding of two people, 
     who always wanted something special in their lives, 
          and now they know they have found it…

Love, honor, respect, and admiration… 

They’ve looked for it all their lives,
     but now they have found it, 
          in each other,
               for today, tomorrow, 
                    and all other tomorrows of their lives… 

They will live as one…
Laugh as one…
Stand steadfast against the storm… 
And love as one…

Ttill the end of their lives…
