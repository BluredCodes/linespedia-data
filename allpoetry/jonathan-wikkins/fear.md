---
title: "Fear"
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"
slug: "fear"
source: "AllPoetry"

At times of our lives,
     fears of the unknown,
          present themselves to us…

Sometimes those fears are justified 
     sometimes they are not…

Sometimes we find out,
     that what we feared comes true…

Sometimes it is the simple fact,
     that what we fear, 
          is in fact, fear itself…

Fear, it can damage us…
