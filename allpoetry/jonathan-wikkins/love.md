Listen well my dear friends… 
     
For the muse,
     has arrived…

I have been sent, 
     with a message. 

That message is love… 

Love comes to us in many ways, 
     and from many sources…

Life is passion;
     love is life…

Allow that love,
     to enter your life…

For it is I,
     the passionate muse,
          who brings it... 

Love of your fellow man,
     love of a child…

Love from the creator,
     of all life…

Allow passion for love,
     to permeate,
          the very depths of your soul;
               for that shall be your salvation…

Save yourself through love,
     not hatred…

Take that love,
     and allow those around you,
          to know…

That love shall conquer evil…
