---
title: "Tools"
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"
slug: "tools"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

In the beginning
     of our lives,
          we are all given
               a set of tools…
The set changes
     from person to person…

It is how and when
     we use them, 
          that determines
               the outcome of our lives…

Sometimes the same tools
     can be used
          either for good or for evil… 
It is simply how they are used… 

The greatest
     of these tools
          is love… 
We are all given love
     in varying degrees…
And when used with love,
     all other tools,
          can be used properly…

It is said that with love,
     all things are possible. 
          and without it, 
               nothing else matters…

Our tools are given to us,
     when we seem
          to need them
               the most…
Some present themselves
     early in life… 
Some present themselves
     after good times…
Some present themselves
     after great loss and pain…
