---
title: "In My Heart"
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"
slug: "in-my-heart"

In my heart
     there dwells
          a special lady… 
She has watched over me
     for quite some time now. 

She constructed a door,
     to my heart, 
          she knew one day,
               she would open it, 
                    and welcome someone in… 

Recently she did,
     for someone knocked
          upon the door to my heart,
               it was opened wide, 
                    and she welcomed her in, 
                         with open arms… 

She is someone special,
     someone new,
          she will dwell there,
               till the end of my days… 

Together we will find
     happiness,
     love, 
     respect, 
          and 
     goodness in life… 

We will live life together, 
     one day
          as man and wife… 
We do not yet know,
     which one of us
          will go first,
               of course… 
But when it happens,
     someone will be waiting,
          on the other side,
               for the one of us 
                    that goes first,
                         and 
                    there they will wait, 
                         for the other one… 

We will live this life to the fullest…
We will say “I love you”
     many, many times… 

With a hug,
     and a kiss, 
          we will spend our lives together,
               we will know that,
                    whenever the one
                         who goes first,
                              that we were loved,
                                   above all else,
                                        and
                                   we will be waited for…

We will know this,
     because,
          that is what love
               truly is…
