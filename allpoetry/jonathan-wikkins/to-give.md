Some people take all that is given…
Some people give all they can…

When we take more
     than we give, 
          others become 
               less important
                    to us than ourselves…

When we give,
     we place ourselves
          beside others…
Then they feel good
     about themselves,
          and return what is given
              even more…
Give and it shall be returned,
     take and be selfish
          and others resent…

Which is better? 

Decide for yourself…
