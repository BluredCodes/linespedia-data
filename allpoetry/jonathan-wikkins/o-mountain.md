---
title: "O Mountain..."
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"

O Mountain, 
     what am I to do? 
I am so alone now… 
Have I done 
     the right things for me? 

O Mountain, 
     has she been 
          that little voice, 
               within me?
Trying to steer me straight? 

O Mountain, 
     what am I to do? 
There has been one since, 
     is she the one for me? 
Did we do it right,
     or just think we did? 

O Mountain, 
     if she is not, 
          then lead me 
               to the one for me… 

O Mountain, 
     if she IS the one, 
          for me, 
               then lead me…
To where, 
     I need to go…
To what, 
     I need to do…
To what, 
     I need to say… 

O Mountain, 
     lead her also…
To go, 
     where she needs to go…
To what, 
     she needs to do…
To what, 
     she needs to say… 

O Mountain, 
     if she is NOT the one,
          help us both… 
For we will need, 
     all the help 
          we can get…
For there will be 
     much sorrow, 
          much pain, 
               much anger, 
                    and 
               many, many other things… 

O Mountain, 
     what am I to do?
