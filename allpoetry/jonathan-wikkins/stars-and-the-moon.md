---
title: "Stars and the Moon"
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"
slug: "stars-and-the-moon"
source: "AllPoetry"

When the moon is full,
     and the stars are out,
          and the night is upon us,
               our love blooms…

In the moonlight, 
     our hearts are filled…

When we gaze, 
     at the stars, 
          our eyes see things…

They see love…

They become content…

They become each other’s…
