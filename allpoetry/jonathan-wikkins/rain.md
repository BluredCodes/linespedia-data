---
title: "Rain"
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"
slug: "rain"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Does many things
Rain fills what is empty
It waters the earth
It allows plants to be born
It washes our faces
We look
We feel
We know

And therefore
We live
