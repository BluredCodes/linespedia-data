---
title: "Eyes"
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"
slug: "eyes"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/11253-Eyes-by-Jonathan-Wikkins"
---

Eyes
the windows
to our souls
~

We see
many things
with our eyes
~

We look
we gaze
~

Our eyes
can see
each other
as we truly are
~

Our eyes know
~
