title: "There is Another"
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"

There is another,
          who yearns for my heart,
               up until now,
                    someone else has
                         held my heart,
                              someone I lost,
                                   just a short time ago…

     When she was taken,
          it was so sudden,
               so complete,
                    I never could say
                         what I wanted to say…

     I never lost the love,
          I felt for so long…
     For it was not destroyed
          by bad times that happened…

     So the love remained,
          till now…
     But I must release it,
          for another wishes to have it…
               so that she can experience
                    the true love I felt,
                         for someone I lost…

     I never wanted to release you,
          from my heart, nor
               to stop loving you
                    as I did,
                         before all this happened…
     But I know I must,
          for I cannot move on,
               with my life…

     In time my love will fade,
          for you…
     In time I will find what I miss,
          so very much…
     So I ask you now,
          release me from your love…
     But keep it, 
          for one day, 
               if I need it…
     I may need your help, one day,
          and I ask for it in advance,
               just in case…

     Please release me,
          for I release my love,
               for you…
     Wait for me however,
          I’ll see you again,
               one day…

     Goodbye my lady love,
          I wish I had had the opportunity,
               to say this to you just one last time…
     And no matter,
          how many times we said it,
               which were many…
     And even though I release it,
          I release it out of love…
     What I would like to say is this:  
     “I love you, I love you, my lady,
          Goodbye”
