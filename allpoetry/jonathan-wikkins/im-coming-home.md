title: "I'm Coming Home"

You know all my reasons…

You know what I must do…

You know all my dreams…

You know all my sorrows…

You know about my loss…

Tonight begins a process,
    a process I have dreaded.

As you know…

The memories that dwell within me
    are quite vivid at this very moment….
I must somehow find a way
    to leave them behind, and start a new life…
It is my hopes, as well as my dreams,
    that life will include you for many years yet to come…

I’m coming home for all the things,
    that I have missed these last few years…
Since I have been here,
    THERE has always been home….

Some say:
“Home is where you hang your hat."

I say:
“Home is where your heart is.”

And you know where my heart is…
As I know where your heart is…
My heart is with you; as yours is with me.
It is time…

I’m coming home…

When we gave each other our hearts,
    so very long ago…

We did not know then
     what it would mean to our future.

Now we will have the opportunity,
    the opportunity to find out
         what our lives will bring now…

We do not yet know what that will be.
But, the love is there,
    as it has been all this time…

As you also know it is now possible,
    to begin to fulfill a dream.
A dream we have both had,
    for a long time…
Let us both do our best
    to keep that dream alive.

We may experience many things
    in the months ahead.
It is my hopes, our dreams
    can still become a reality.

We have talked about my process
    I feel must happen.
That process began months ago.

The process of turning the final page
    on this chapter of my life.
As I began to turn that page;
    I also began to turn the first page,
         on a new chapter of my life, 
              that chapter will be about you.
I must admit, I have peaked at the first page…
It is filled with love…

Always remember this, my love:
    no matter what happens,
         no matter how it goes,
              always know this, my love,
                   always know this: 

I’m coming home,
    to YOU.
