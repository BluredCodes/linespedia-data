---
title: "Out in the Garden"
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"
slug: "out-in-the-garden"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/15297-Out-in-the-Garden-by-Jonathan-Wikkins"
---

Out in the garden
Two lovers are in love
The flower’s, and grass’ scents are intoxicating
Laughter and sweet nothings are heard

Two lovers are in love
Swinging together, more deeply they fall
Laughter and sweet nothings are heard
The sounds of two lovers in love

Swinging together, more deeply they fall
The flower’s, and grass’ scents are intoxicating
The sounds of two lovers in love
Out in the garden
