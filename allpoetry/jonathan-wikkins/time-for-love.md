---
title: "Time for Love"
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"

Dear lady, you are the one for me, now
for two to live as one till forever…
Your heart is one I shall enter with plow 
to furrow through your heart as I enter…
For the love and life shall be ours all time 
enter with me now, for our life shall be… 
Our love for each other, shall be sublime 
to the storms of life, go we now with glee… 
As the years pass, let’s always be in love 
go we now together as one, not two 
Let us now find love’s precious treasure trove 
for in the end of life, we’ll not be through 
Our love shall be known, for all eternity 
we will walk together through time, with glee…
