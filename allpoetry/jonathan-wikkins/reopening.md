---
title: "Reopening?"
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"
slug: "reopening"
source: "AllPoetry"

What is that I detected
     in your voice?

Has love returned?

Did it ever leave?

Is that an opening
     of your heart, I feel?

Continue to open,
     if you can…

Let love fully reenter…

I know it will take time…

Just always remember…

Love becomes you…
