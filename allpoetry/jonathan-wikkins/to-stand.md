---
title: "To Stand"
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"
slug: "to-stand"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/15489-To-Stand-by-Jonathan-Wikkins"
---

To stand exposed
     before one another…

Nothing hidden, 
     naked to the soul...

We stand before each other,
     bearing ourselves, totally...

Nothing is hidden…

As love enters,
     nothing can hide...

To weather the storms, 
     and strife...

Our souls,
     must know...
