Good evening dear lady, our love needs filled 
For our hearts desire one another's now 
Our passion causes our desire to build 
To each other, love grows for us and how 
Our love grows for us without subsidence 
To the chamber of love, love comes to us 
Our bodies need love for our subsistence 
We cannot end of this, for want of thus 
Go we now, throw our hearts on to the fire 
For the fires of desire burn in our soul 
In the end of this, our needs are dire 
Let us be for us, like the times of ole 
Our desires build, give them to us, we must 
For we know our desire, for which is just
