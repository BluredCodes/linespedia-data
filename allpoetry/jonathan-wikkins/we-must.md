---
title: "We Must"
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"

Why are we where we are, today? 
 I do not understand... 

Why must I fight you, for you? 
 I do not understand... 

Why did we loose, what we had? 
 I do not understand... 

Did we ever truly have what we thought? 
 I do not understand... 

If we did, can we find it again? 
 I do not understand... 

Have we lost, what we wanted for so long? 
 I do not understand... 

What do we do, if we find out we lost it? 
 I do not understand... 

What happened? 
I do not know... 

But we must find out...
So we can either move on,
or back together... 

We must...
