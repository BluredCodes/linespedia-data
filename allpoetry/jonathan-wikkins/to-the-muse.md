---
title: "To the Muse"
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"

To the Muse... 
          Go we all, at times,
               he is there... 
          When love is needed, 
               we find him…

To the Muse...
          In times of trouble, 
               he is there, 
          to make our hearts become whole…
               For comfort, 
          to open our eyes... 

For the Muse... 
          About it he writes, 
               love, and Life... 

Go the muse... 
          Into life's troubles, 
               he lives it, 
          he gives us comfort of all…
               For he knows, 
          who he is till now... 

When at times,
          life passes us by,
               comes the muse... 

When at times,
          our life needs sadness with love, 
               brings the muse... 

When in love,
           for gladly we go, 
               to the muse...
