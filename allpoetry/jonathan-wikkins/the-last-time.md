---
title: "The Last Time"
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"
slug: "the-last-time"
source: "AllPoetry"

When I saw you last, 
     I was overwhelmed 
          with emotion…
I was left alone, 
     with you, 
          for the final time…

To this day,
     I do not remember 
          everything that I said, 
               just bits and pieces, 
                    but, 
               what I do remember,
                    Is what I did,
                         the last time I saw you,
                              and some of what I said. 

You were lying there,
     motionless, 
          your skin cold to the touch,
               no expression on your face…
Your wedding ring, 
     I gave you was still
          on your finger, 
               where I had placed it,
                    so many years before. 

Then just before you left,
     I took off my wedding ring, 
          and placed it, 
               in the middle of your chest….
Our symbols of our undying love, 
     remain there, 
          where I placed them, 
               to this very day…
And there they will remain,
     throughout all eternity. 

When they came
     to take you away, 
          it was my eyes
               that saw you last…
With these words, 
     that I do remember,
          on my lips…
Your love for me, 
     still in my heart, 
          where it also remains, 
               to this day…

I said: 
     “Dream well, 
          my love, 
     dream well… 
I will always miss you…
Your name will be 
     on my lips, 
          when I join you, 
               where you are now. 
My love for you, 
     will always be 
          a part of my soul…
Until the day when I join you
     on the other side,
          where you are now…
Please wait for me, 
     look over me,
          help me, 
               whenever you can…

Goodbye 
     My love,
              Goodbye”…
