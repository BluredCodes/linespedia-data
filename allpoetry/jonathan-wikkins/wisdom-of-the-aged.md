---
title: "Wisdom of the Aged"
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"
slug: "wisdom-of-the-aged"
source: "AllPoetry"

It is I,
     the muse…
I have returned
     once again…

This time with
     Wisdom of the Aged…
The source of which
     escapes me…
Allow the words written,
     to enter your soul…
For I,
     the muse,
          am only
               the messenger… 

“Take heed, for the end is near,
     the evil walks amongst you…
Do not listen to its words,
     for it will destroy your soul…

“There are those amongst you,
     who know the truth,
          seek them out,
               for they are the way,
                    take heed… 
There will be those who deceive you, 
     those which will bear false witness…

“Pay attention to your heart,
     as they bear their words,
          for it knows the truth…
But, you must listen…

“When love presents itself to you
     in one of its many forms,
          know this,
               it is from your creator…
For with love,
     you shall prevail…
And that love
     will show you the way…

“That way shall not be easy,
     for it is fraught
          with danger and heartache…
Yet have faith
     that love is with you…”

From whence these words arrive,
     it appears there
          shall be more, forthcoming…
When they appear,
     I shall present them to you…

Yet, it is up to you to listen,
     pay heed…
