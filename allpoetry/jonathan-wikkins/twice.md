title: "Twice..."

It happened…
Nothing I can do, 
     will take away this pain…
Some simply say: “Give it time”…
     but, how much?

Twice…
A heart has been broken… 
A soul must rest… 
Time must be given… 

Just a year’s time… 
Twice… 
A heart must mend,
     for life to be good, again…
There is new life,
     new love, out there,
          somewhere…

But, why, just when things
     were looking good?
Just as dreams were about
     to be realized?
You broke my heart… 

The pain is almost unbearable…
Yet, hope will heal…
Life will renew…
Love will reenter…

And soon…
