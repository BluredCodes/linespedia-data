title: "Dreams..."

There was a day…
Hopes and dreams
     were before me…
Life was grand…

Then, it happened…
My life as I had known it,
     changed overnight…
Lost all hope,
     dreams were shattered…
Went through the motions
     for a while…

Later, new dreams appeared,
     a new hope rekindled…
New love, returned…
Hope…
Alas to dream…
