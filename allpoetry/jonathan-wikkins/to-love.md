I love the way it feels

     to have you in my arms…

     to see you look deeply into my eyes…

     to go to sleep beside you…

     to wake up and see you laying there…

     to hear you say "Good Morning, I love you"…

     to share a life together with you…

     to know our lives have become whole again…

     to be your "one and only"…

     to help you around the house…

     to hold you when you need held…


It feels wonderful, again... 
     now that we know, 
          love is there...
