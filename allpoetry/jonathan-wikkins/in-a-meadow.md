title: "In a Meadow"

In a meadow,
     by a stream
          two lovers
               are together…

A blanket is laid out 
     for their picnic
          for two…

The stream’s water
     flows by
          romantically babbling…

Their eyes are
     upon each other,
          words spoken softly…

Hearts are together,
     hands held…

Two who were lonely,
     are no longer…

Electricity is in the air
     for love is shared
          between them…

Words of desire are exchanged 
     special feelings abound…
