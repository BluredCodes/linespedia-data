Why did this have to happen?
Perhaps it is not to be known…
Perhaps one day, 
     it will be known,
          when the sea no longer 
               rolls with the wind…
When the sun no longer 
     shines upon my face,
          when I owe no more 
               to the future,
                    when my job is done 
                         here on Earth...

Perhaps then I will receive an answer,
     it may not be the answer 
          I would like...
But at least I will get an answer...
To WHY???

WHY were you taken from me?

WHY so suddenly?

WHY so completely?

Without even a chance 
     to say goodbye...
