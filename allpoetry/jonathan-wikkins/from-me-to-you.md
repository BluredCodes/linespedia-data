---
title: "From Me, to You"
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"

When we met,
     years ago,
          did you feel what I felt?
Did that little voice,
     inside,
          say:
“Hang on to this one”?
     Mine did…

I have thought,
     many times,
          of late,
               would I have continued,
                    had I known?
The answer,
       has always come back:
“YES”

You brought something out,
     within me,
All those years ago,
     that I never knew…  

Our life,
     was somewhat a fairy tale,
Could one reason to my question: “Why?”,
     be, because it was too good to be true?

Or could it be,
     what is happening now?
