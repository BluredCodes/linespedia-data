~

It BEGINS...

The QUEST…
The PROCESS...
The NEW LIFE...

~ ~

It ENDS...

The SORROW...
The PAIN...
The OLD LIFE...

~ ~ ~

The old life
     will give meaning
          to the new…

The new life
     will give purpose,
          to the old…

~ ~ ~ ~

Let life find its purpose…
Let love enter, as it is due…
Find happiness…

Begin the QUEST...

~ ~ ~ ~ ~
