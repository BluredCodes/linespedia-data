Life has a passion, 
     all its own…

Passion comes from within, 
     from many places, 
           in many ways…

With a passion for life, 
     returns the muse…

Fear not, for all is well,
     life's passion is upon us…

"To thine own self be true." 
     passion is self, be true to it…
 
Take life, and look upon it, 
     live each day, as if it were our last…

Out into the fray, goes the muse... 

All is well...
 
For love goes with.,
     and passion follows... 

Passion for life, 
     Passion for love, 
          and for happiness...
