---
title: "They Won't Come..."
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"
slug: "they-wont-come"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

The words, that came
     so fast and furious
          for a while, won’t come…
We all experience it,
     from time to time,
          writer’s block….

One day, soon,
     it will come to an end…
Is it changing directions?
Finding heart to write again?
What will happen,
     this time? 
That’s anyone’s guess… 

What will be the inspiration?

What will be the genre?

What will be the category?

Who will be written about? 

Will it be love poetry, 
     or short stories,
          or novels? 

Who knows? 

Time will tell…
