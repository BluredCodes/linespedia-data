---
title: "Just a Breadman"
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"

Just a breadman, 
     nothing special... 

A breadman, 
    who walks amongst others, 
         he is nothing special… 
              
Or so it seems... 

He has a heart, 
    as big as the sun, 
         
Just a breadman, 
     nothing special... 

He had something special, 
    and he lost it... 
         
He found something special, 
    and looks like he'll have it…

He found a love,
     like he missed…

He lives it, 
    thinks about it, 
         puts his thoughts on paper…

When people read them, 
     they seem to like them... 
         
Just a breadman, 
     nothing special... 

Writes about his loves, 
     his loss, and his renewal. 
    
Just a breadman, 
     nothing special... 

Who writes poetry...
