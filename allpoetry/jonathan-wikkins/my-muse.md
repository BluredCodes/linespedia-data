title: "My Muse"

In the morning, 
     to my muse,
          I speak…
I’m asking for wisdom,
     and the words to convey it…
Finding my heart,
     to pour into those words… 
My muse,
     he speaks! 

He speaks at the strangest times, 
     usually when the mind is clear…
A pen must be taken,
     and ideas written… 
So pen and paper,
     are always at hand… 

He is speaking at this very moment… 
As with other times,
     sometimes he presents all,
          sometimes just a title… 

Sometimes a dark cloud enters,
     sometimes erotica, 
          sometimes love,
               sometimes sadness…

My muse presents all… 

And he knows all….
