---
title: "Time"
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"
slug: "time"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

It travels
          on by...

     Life
          happens... 

     Time
          it will tell all…

     Sometimes
          life, time,
               takes us by the hand, 
                    and leads us,
                         or follows us…

     What we do
          with our time,
               is up to us…

     What will happen? 

     Time will tell…
