I’m here,
I’m back home, again.

What I don’t know is
WHY?
Why is a mystery.
Yet, it feels
like I never left.
Something is waiting,
something good.

What is to happen here?
What lives am I to touch?
What shall I do here?
I do not know.

But, it is home.
Renewal
Healing
New life

I’m here
