title: "New Life Begins"

It has begun,
     life, new life…
A new purpose,
    a new place,
          a new love…

When life gets us down,
    we must move on…
Moving on, sometimes,
    is the most difficult thing to do…

Find new happiness,
    for it is out there,
          for us all…
