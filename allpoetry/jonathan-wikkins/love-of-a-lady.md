---
title: "Love of a Lady"
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"
slug: "love-of-a-lady"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/14644-Love-of-a-Lady-by-Jonathan-Wikkins"
---

When a lady, 
    loves her man, 
          her soul loves. 

When her soul loves, 
    her heart is his. 

When her heart is his, 
    his becomes hers. 

For the love, 
    of a lady, 
          knows no other…
