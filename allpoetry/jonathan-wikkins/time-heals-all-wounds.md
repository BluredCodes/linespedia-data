title: "Time Heals All Wounds"

Time has a way
     of healing
          what happens to us…
In time,
     life gets better again…

Wounds can be deep,
     like watching your soulmate
          die in your arms,
               at the age of 47…

Wounds can be wide,
     like watching a relationship,
          you thought you wanted,
               disintegrate right before your eyes…

Then just as that one goes…
Another one comes in…
Like it was supposed to be…
Like it was meant to be…

Healing comes in many ways,
     all it takes is time…
I was told
     “time heals all wounds”…
All I could say was
     “how much time?”

But it does… 
     and the time it takes,
          is up to us…
So we just simply
     give ourselves
          time to heal…
