---
title: "Sunrise and Sunset"

At sunrise, 
    the colours we see, 
          the reds, 
              the oranges, 
                    the blues… 

All the hues, 
    the beautiful hues, 
          inspire us, 
              cause us to grow still, 
                    make us ready for the day. 

When they return, 
    at sunset, 
          we become at ease, 
              we grow still again, 
                    ready… 

Prepared for the night, 
    when love enters, 
          our hearts again, 
              our souls unite, 
                    we grow together…
