---
title: "I Can't Wait..."
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"
slug: "i-cant-wait"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8195-I-Cant-Wait...-by-Jonathan-Wikkins"
---

I can’t wait,
M’love
I can’t wait
…

I can’t wait,
to gaze into your eyes,
as I feel the love you feel,
for me,
once again
…

I can’t wait,
M’love
I can’t wait
…

I can’t wait,
to hear your voice,
as you speak the words,
“I love you”
as I hear the love you feel 
for me,
once again
…

I can’t wait,
M’love
I can’t wait
…

I can’t wait,
to take you into my arms,
as I feel the love you feel
for me,
once again
…

I can’t wait,
M’love
I can’t wait
…

I can’t wait, 
to see you gaze into my eyes, 
as you see the love I feel 
for you,
once again
…

I can’t wait,
M’love
I can’t wait
…

I can’t wait,
for you to hear the words I speak,
“I love you”
as you hear the love I feel
for you,
once again
…

I can’t wait,
M’love
I can’t wait
…

I can’t wait,
for you to take me into your arms,
as you feel the love I feel
for you,
once again
…

I can’t wait,
M’love
I can’t wait
…

I can’t wait,
for us to be together,
as one,
once again
…

Hand in hand
…
Eye to eye
…
Face to face
…
Heart to heart
…

I can’t wait,
M’love
I can’t wait
…
