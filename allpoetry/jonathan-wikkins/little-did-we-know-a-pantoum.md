Little did we know, what would happen later
Our day was normal, except for a few minor things
Friends were coming for the weekend
The day was changed, for accommodation

Our day was normal, except for a few minor things
Our life was good, content
The day was changed, for accommodation
Hot air balloons, filled our thoughts, and would the air, soon

Our life was good, content
We spent time with our friends, our kids with theirs
Hot air balloons, filled our thoughts, and would the air, soon
We went to a party, all seemed normal

We spent time with our friends, our kids with theirs
That night, you would leave for good
We went to a party, all seemed normal
you died in my arms, your name on my lips, love still strong in our hearts

That night, you would leave for good
Friends were coming for the weekend
you died in my arms, your name on my lips, love still strong in our hearts
Little did we know, what would happen later
