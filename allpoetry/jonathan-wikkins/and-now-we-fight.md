title: "And Now, We Fight"

The Powers of Evil
     are out there… 
The Lord of Darkness 
     is upon us… 
What must we do? 

FIGHT… 

The Powers of Good
     have gained strength
          to fight…
Not against simple flesh and blood,
     that is far too simple…
But, against Principalities of Power,
     a much worse fight…
For those battles
     occur within us… 

The powers of Evil
     thought they could win…
For they gain strength,
     from within those they conquer…

Be careful, 
     what you think…

Be careful, 
     how you feel… 

For Evil wants,
     your very soul…
