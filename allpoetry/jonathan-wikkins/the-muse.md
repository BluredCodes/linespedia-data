Out into the fray, 
     comes the muse, 
          to weather life's storms...
     Says the muse:
"Passion speaks passion." 

"For life is passion,"
     speaks the muse...
"Passion begets life." 

Storms come to us all, 
     life passes...
          How we handle life for us, 
     storms and all,
gives us growth and love…

At times life gives pain,
     but for love, 
          life moves within us…
               Life's passion is upon us, 
         for passion is life…
     Comes the muse, 
speaks of love and life…

Out into the fray, 
     goes the muse…
          All is well, for life is grand…
     Passion determines life, 
passion for life's joy…

"For life is passion," 
    speaks the muse, 
"passion begets life…"

At times life gives pain,
     but for love, 
          life moves within us…
     Comes the muse,
speaks of love and life...
