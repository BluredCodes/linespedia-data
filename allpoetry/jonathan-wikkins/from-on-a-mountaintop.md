---
title: "From on a Mountaintop"
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"
slug: "from-on-a-mountaintop"

From here miles can be seen,
     in all directions. 
          it seems there is no one else
               in the world…

From on a mountaintop, 
     nothing else matters in the world, 
          just you and your companions…

From on a mountaintop, 
     it seems you are
          looking down on life…

Feelings are with you,
     that you are insignificant, 
          in the world…

From on a mountaintop, 
     cares slip away…

All that is thought
     is the beautiful sight
          all around you…
