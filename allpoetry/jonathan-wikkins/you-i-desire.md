---
title: "You I Desire"
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"
slug: "you-i-desire"

When I draw near, 
     our hearts beckon each other’s... 
          our desire surrounds the other... 
               neither can resist,
                         the other’s want... 

Our arms ache for the other’s touch, 
     our lips need lips. 
Our eyes say: 
               “You I desire...” 

Allow me dear lady,
     to lead you away, 
          from your daily troubles,
               from life apart, 
                         to love of each other… 

Take my hand, follow me, 
     allow your troubles to subside… 
          for now
               is the time for our love, 
                         to be expressed… 

Physical desire grows, 
     our skin needs the touch of 
               bare skin… 
Allow that sensation 
          to fill our hearts… 

As our passion ensues, 
     let our movement commence 
          to life, love is all that counts… 
To love, you are mine… 
               as I am yours. 

When juices flow, let nothing 
     be between our bare skin, 
          as passion subsides, 
               let love be a part of our lives… 
As the afterglow comes over us,
                         let our love renew… 

Till the next time my love… 

And soon!!!
