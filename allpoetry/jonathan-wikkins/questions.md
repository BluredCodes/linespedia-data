The day approaches... 

That dreaded day... 

The anniversary... 

Have I done it all right? 
Has moving on past,
     come too soon? 
What should I do? 
Has my life truly renewed itself? 

Or am I fooling myself? 
Have I done it all wrong? 
Why all these questions? 

Why did this have to happen? 
These answers, 
     I seem to be getting, 
          are they for real? 
Or just false hopes? 

TELL ME PLEASE! 

For mistakes at my age, 
     can make things happen too late...
