Moon...
How are you tonight? 

We come to you as two, 
     who wish to be as one…

Cast your sensual glow, 
     upon our faces…
Our hearts wish
     heightened desire…

As we bare each other's bodies
     our breath quickens…
Bathe us with your sensual light,
     bring our hearts desire…

We are yours,
     as each other's…
Our bare skin glows,
     as you fill our desire…

Allow our desire to wax and wane,
     throughout the night…
In evening's afterglow, 
     bring peace, serenity, and newfound love, 
          to us both...
