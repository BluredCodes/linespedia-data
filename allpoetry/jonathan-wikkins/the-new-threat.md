---
title: "The New Threat"
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"
slug: "the-new-threat"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/24112-The-New-Threat-by-Jonathan-Wikkins"
---

It is I, 
     the gentle muse… 
I have returned
     once again,
          this time with a message
               of hope…

The forces of evil are gathering…
Good will conquer,
     for it is written… 
It will be a terrible fight… 
For good has always 
     conquered evil. 

Measures have been taken, 
     time is of the essence… 
The end is near… 
Pay head to your heart,
     for it knows
          what it must do…

The one thing evil
     does not have,
          that will cause its defeat,
               is love, for love 
                    shall always conquer evil…
It will not be easy, 
     but good will be triumphant… 

There is a new threat, 
     one that has been written… 
One the world has never seen… 
But there are those,
     who shall conquer,
          in time… 
Do not allow yourself to be deceived… 
It will not be easy, 
     the marks are well hidden,
          and organized… 

Have faith, those forces
     that shall defeat,
          have the one thing 
               that their enemies do not… 
They have the love, 
     that has passed on down,
          throughout the ages… 
Love of their fellow man, 
     love of a child,
          love of each other… 
That love, comes only 
     from the source of all life,
          the creator… 

His name means love… 
His name means life… 
His domain, 
     shall be your soul’s,
          if you listen… 
His forces shall be triumphant…
