---
title: "Forlorn..."
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"

Forlorn, we gaze upon the past, oh no...
Could it be, our lives have moved on past us now?
What is it? You might ask, I do not know...
Seek ye, for life is grand, if we allow.
O'er time all can be well, give it we must;
Through time, all can be healed, what needs have we?
Come time, love is waiting, to be with us...
Given time, love shall make us want to be.
What is life? We may ask, what do we do?
Our hearts bleed, we feel our lives are through...
Troubled, we loose ourselves of love's appeal.
Till now, our love has been unbroken, true?
When our hearts break, we know that we must do...
