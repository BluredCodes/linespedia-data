~ ~ ~ ~ ~ 

To my muse… 

Thank you for the words, 
I am about to receive… 

For I am only the messenger, 
YOU are the true writer… 

Please pour the words,
into my heart,
where each one
shall pick up a piece,
as they flow
through my fingertips
onto the media
where they are read…


~ ~ ~ ~ ~ 

To myself… 

Dig deep within your heart, 
find the words,
the muse has given…

Allow them to flow forth, 
so that others may feel,
and some may heal,
through the words,
you were GIVEN…
