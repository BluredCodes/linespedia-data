---
title: "Moving On"
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"

When you left, 
          you took with you, 
               so much of me, 
                    there wasn't much left... 

   I have rebuilt much of that, 
          but the hole you left in my heart, 
               remains till this very day... 

   To move on... 
     To find what I miss... 
     Difficult, at best... 

   For when you lose, 
          what I lost, so quickly, 
               so suddenly... 
                    for seemingly no reason... 
   A part of you, 
          dies too... 

   Then one day, you realize, 
          what you must do... 
   You MUST move on... 
     Find again... 

   For that is what, 
          they want, 
                    for those, 
                         they left behind... 

   Moving on... 
     You make mistakes…
          You're vulnerable…
               You can easily 
                    be taken advantage of... 
   But, still, you must move on…

   For if you do not... 
          You become, 
               The living dead...
