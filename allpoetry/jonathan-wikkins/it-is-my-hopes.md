title: "It is My Hopes"
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"

Life happens,
     does it not? 
Things change,
     do they not? 
This change,
     this life,
          between us,
               of late,
                    has not been pleasant,
                         has it?

Perhaps it is only temporary,
     and our life together will renew…
I know it will take time…
It is still my hope,
     it will change again,
          for the better,
               back to love…

We are about to have
     the opportunity for
          what we have both wanted,
               for a very long time…

What will it take,
     to renew?
Time?
A break? 

Our hearts are still
      in the possession of the other…
Will we give them back?
Can we ever rebuild,
     what we had? 
If we give them back;
     will it only be temporary?

It is my promise to you,
     as I hope yours to me…

To try again… 

One day…
