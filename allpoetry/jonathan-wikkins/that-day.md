---
title: "That Day"
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"
slug: "that-day"
source: "AllPoetry"

I went somewhere one day, 
     that day…

It is a place where I had been, 
     many times before…

But never for why 
     I went there, one day…

It is nothing but a piece of ground, 
     but, I went there to dedicate it 
          to someone I loved, 
               more than my life itself…

Most people who do this, 
     just simply plant a stake,
          so the crews know 
               where to dig…

But I went to this special place,
     for a special reason…

To dedicate, 
     that piece of ground, 
          to you…

I don’t remember, 
     all that I said, 
          that day…
But I know it was long, 
     and gave me much comfort…

But in the end
     of what I had to say…
I dedicated 
     that piece of ground, 
          to you…

For your final resting place…
