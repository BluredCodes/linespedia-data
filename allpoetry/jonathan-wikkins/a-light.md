title: "A Light"

Into the darkness,
     pierces a light…

What is the light?

Is it truth?
Is it hope?
Who holds the light?

What is the source?

Is it a candle?
Is it the moon?
Is it the sun,
     through a hole
          in the clouds?

Give into the light…

For it has need…
