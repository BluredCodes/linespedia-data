---
title: "To All of You, Here..."
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"

In the short time,
     I have been here
          many things
               have happened…

Poetry, is one…
Lots of it…

I have seen
     the rise and fall,
          of a controversy…

As a result, 
     some poets quit the site…
A few have returned…

I am still learning how,
     to pour my heart,
          into every word
               I write…

To all of you,
     for all the encouragement,
          you have given me…

I say: “Thank you,
     from the bottom
          of my heart…”
