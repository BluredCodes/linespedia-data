---
title: "Love Is Up To Us"
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"
slug: "love-is-up-to-us"
source: "AllPoetry"

Love

Catches us,
brings us together
~

Love

Completes us,
tells us who we are
~~

Love

Can be good for us
~~~

Love

Is all we need,
to be who we are
~~~~

Love

There is nothing else,
if there is no love
~~~~~

Love

Can be found or lost
~~~~~~

Love
Is up to us
~~~~~~~

Jonathan Wikkins November 7, 2001
