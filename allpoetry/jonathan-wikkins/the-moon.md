Into the night, 
     we gaze upon you, 
          with your face shinning brightly…

Illuminating our souls, 

Throughout the ages,
     you have inspired time, fear, and most of all, 
          LOVE… 

For time, 
     the ancient ones, 
          marked their passing… 

For fear of the unknown, 
     you disappeared,
          and then returned… 

For lovers gaze upon you, 
     to further their gifts,
          to each other…
