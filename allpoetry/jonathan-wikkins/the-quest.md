---
title: "The Quest"
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"

Wide open spaces,
     mountains, valleys,
          memories…
Some good,
     some not,
          some horrible…

I must leave this behind,
     and start over…
What my life will bring, now,
     is anyone’s guess… 

I now begin a quest,
     a quest for love,
          contentment,
               honor,
                    joy, and happiness…

What might I find along the way?
Heartache,
     pain,
          uncertainty,
               and broken dreams…

One thing is certain,
     that I will find in time…
Something I may
     have never found before…

For I have found love,
     honor,
          contentment,
               joy, and happiness,
                    in my life, before…
I have also found heartache,
     pain,
          uncertainty, and broken dreams… 
I know those things quite well,
     in fact…

Yet the one thing,
     I feel I have lacked
          all of my life,
               I may have never found…
Yet I will,
     one day…

In time… 
I will find… 

Me…
