---
title: "Here I Am"
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"
slug: "here-i-am"
source: "AllPoetry"

Here I am
     just a man…
          
A man,
     in love with a woman…

Today I am here as someone, 
     who wishes to love,
          as I know how… 

I hope you can accept it,
     for I wish for you to have it… 

For if you can,
     you will find, 
          a love as you may
               have never known… 

It will be a love,
     given freely, 
          with just a small hope
               of return…

It will be a love,
     that lasts for all eternity…

Please find a way
     to accept me as I am…

For I will accept you,
     as you are…

And I will love you
     like no other, 
          has ever been loved,
               truly loved… 

So here I am,
     offering something to you…

Please consider,
     for it is only for you…

I have released my love,
     that I have felt, 
          for someone I lost,
               suddenly, completely,
                    just a short time ago…

Please accept the fact,
     that I know how to love, 
          truly, honestly,
               for I learned how,
                    so long ago…

Here I am,
     just a man…
In love with a woman,
     who wishes to say: 
“I love you.” 

I now and will always say this
     to you, with the conviction
          it deserves…

I love you completely, honestly,
     with all my heart…

I love you…
