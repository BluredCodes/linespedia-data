A new love… 
It has awoken… 
One never expected, 
One never thought possible 
One that should last till the end of time… 

Life sometimes hands us bad influences, 
Throws us the wrong direction, 
Hands us sorrows, and pains… 
But if we look upon the bright side, 
Wherever it is… 
We can find happiness, love, and eternal joy…
