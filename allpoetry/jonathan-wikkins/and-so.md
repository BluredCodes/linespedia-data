And so…
It ends...

The Dream…
The old life…
The old sorrows...
The loss…
The old love...

And so...
What do we do?

We move on…
We find a new dream…
And we follow it...

What will we find?
Who knows...

The QUEST has begun...

And so…
Allow the soul to rest...

What is sought?
New life...
New love...
New Dreams...
