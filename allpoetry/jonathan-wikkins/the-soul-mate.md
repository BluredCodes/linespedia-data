---
title: "The Soul Mate"
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"

We look for love,
     true love,
          in many places,
               sometimes it is never found…
But look we do…

To a lucky few,
     love seems to come
          easily, naturally…
Without effort,
     or so it seems…

Often, we ask ourselves,
     Where is love to be found?
Therefore we look,
     and look some more…
And one day,
     one day,
          a soulmate appears…

We don’t “find” a soulmate,
     our souls do… 
When our eyes meet
     with someone who is
          our soulmate,
               our souls connect,
                    in ways we never knew…

It is as if
     our souls know,
          what we are to find…
And they put us
     in the right place,
          at the right time…
And our soul mate
     appears…

And then…

It starts…
