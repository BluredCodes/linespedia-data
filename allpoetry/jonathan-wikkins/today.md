Today is the day... 

That awful day... 
     one year... 
          one year ago today... 

It started off like any other 
     just a day, you know... 
Today... 
     Remember? 
Our friends were coming up... 

We sponsored a hot air balloon... 
     we were so happy. 
          content... 
               in love... 

Today... 
     Tonight... 
          Tomorrow... 
               and 
                    the 1st of November... 
The second worst day of my life... 
     
Today is the first anniversary 
     of the first day 
          of that awful nightmare
               that ended with your death…

I've moved on MY LOVE... 
     as you would have it... 
          but I still wish you were here... 

Today... 
     is the day... 
          The one year anniversary... 
               Of the day…

You died in my arms...
