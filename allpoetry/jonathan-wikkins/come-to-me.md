title: "Come to Me"

Come to me
my love

Come to me
…

Desire fills our hearts
love fills our eyes
skin needs skin
want fills the air
…

We need each other
…

Come to me
my love

Come to me
…

Night is young
moon is bright
stars fill the sky
fire burns within us
…
    
And we are ours
…

Desire's night is here
and belongs to us
…

Come to me
my love

Come to me
…

Let us give of ourselves
and
speak softly
to each other
…

For desire grows within us
…

Come to me
my love

Come to me
…

Moonlight illuminates us
furthering desire
casting a sensual glow
upon us
…

Our bodies smile towards each other
…

Come to me
my love

Come to me
…
