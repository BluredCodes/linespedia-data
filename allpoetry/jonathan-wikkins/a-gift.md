The gift of love,
     is the greatest gift
          of all…
When a man
     loves a woman,
          and the woman
               loves him back,
                    there is no greater love
                         in the world…

The gift of children,
     to one another,
          is divine…
Let love grow
     for all the years
          you will have together…
And you will find peace
     amongst life…
