title: "A Different Drummer"

For I walk to the beat of a different drummer
When life gets me down, I take offense to it
To love, to life, to happiness!
I let life take hold of me, and live it one day at a time!

When life gets me down, I take offense to it.
I live each day as if it were my last.
I let life take hold of me, and live it one day at a time!
Do you do the same?

I live each day as if it were my last.
To love, to life, to happiness!
Do you do the same?
For I walk to the beat of a different drummer.
