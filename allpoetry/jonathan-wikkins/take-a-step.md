I am not asking you to run,
yet
…

Just take a step,
a step in the right direction
…

Do not lose sight,
of your dreams,
so, take a step
…

For if you do,
you might just find
what you seek,
true love,
happiness,
joy,
honor,
and commitment,
so
…

Take a step
…

And then another
…

All that,
is there for you
…
All you have to do, 
is 
WANT it
…

Badly enough 
to keep taking steps,
forward
…
