title: "New Times, New Alliances"
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"

It is I, 
     the gentle muse…
I have returned…

As I have said, 
     many times…
I shall return, 
     when it is necessary…

This time with news, 
     and a request…
Where all this information 
     comes from escapes me, as usual…

There is an alliance, 
     a new one forming,
          an unlikely one, indeed… 
For there are things 
     which must be told… 
For deception 
     is Evil’s greatest weapon and ally… 

Awakening is happening 
     amongst those destined to fight…
Some are aware, 
     yet others are not…
Little or no blood 
     will be shed…
The battles shall be 
     between principalities of power… 
Some of that power, 
     will be unseen, unheard, unknown 
          to the masses… 

Yet this fight 
     was not supposed 
          to happen 
               within our lifetimes, 
                    not this one anyway… 
But someone, 
     the very being that was given 
          this gift in the beginning,
               changed the timing of prophecy,
                    for his own advantage… 
Those he is to fight, 
     were not to awaken 
          during this lifetime, 
               at all… 
Their souls were to learn, 
     from this lifetime, 
          as in their previous lives, 
               and awaken during the next,
                    prepared to fight… 

But as usual, 
     the battle for this world, 
          will be tough, 
               as in other worlds… 
Some have been won, 
     others were lost… 
Only time will tell, 
     whether Good or Evil prevails… 

As you all know, 
     there are prophecies 
          that lead to the end…
But what you don’t know, 
     is Evil has its own prophecies… 
Prophecies that speak 
     much differently of the end of time… 

There are many writings 
     that lead to prophecy, 
          some which are hidden 
               within popular literature… 
For those to whom 
     they are directed, 
          will see the true message, 
               and meaning behind the words…

All those that preach for the good, 
     no matter what the faith, 
          have the same goal in mind, 
               just merely are on different paths… 

Beware, however, 
     of those who preach, 
          yet tell how you should believe…
It is what is in your heart, 
     that should tell you 
          what is right, for you… 

All major religions 
     feel they are 
          the one way…
Yet, all are right, 
     in that, they are all 
          on the path towards righteousness… 
Just merely a different path, 
     the goal is the same… 

Some of you 
     know who you are…
Others will merely be 
     drawn to these words…
Yet, awakening will happen 
     to those towards whom 
          they are directed. 

As you read these words, 
     let them enter your heart…
If it is to you, 
     to whom they are directed…
Then you will know what to do… 

It is I, 
     the muse, 
Who one day 
     shall return…
