(A welcome to my son, schitzo)


You are only 15,
     yet you have
          such talent, son,
               untapped talent…
I know we have 
     not been as close
          as I would like, of late…

My heart as you know,
     has been broken,
          in the last year…
Not just once,
     but twice… 
That you may not have known…

But now,
     that will change…
We will teach each other,
     learn what
          is truly important…
As you know
     my life has changed…
More than you even know,
     yet…

That change,
     that you know,
          brought about something,
               something I never knew…
Something untapped
     within me that 
          I never knew
               was even there…

Welcome to you…
Keep up the great works…
Learn…
Teach…

And most of all… 

Continue to WRITE!
