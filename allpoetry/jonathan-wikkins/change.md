Today, it begins!
Change!

The old life is ending...
The new life is approaching...

A fade has begun,
     a fade into obscurity...

Soon...
No one will see...
No one will know...

This change is necessary,
     for a life must be left behind, 
          and a persona must begin...

When life gives sorrow, and pain, 
     life can end, or it can change...

Allow change!

The muse is watching...
 
Waiting...

Writing...

     and...
    
SOON...
    
To speak!
