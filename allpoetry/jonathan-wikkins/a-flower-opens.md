title: "A Flower Opens..."
writer: "Jonathan Wikkins"

When a flower opens, 
     many things come into bloom…
But many more things, 
     must happen first. 

First a kiss by the sun, 
     which allows it’s eyes 
          to reopen… 

Then a simple caress, 
     by a gentle breeze 
          upon it’s ear… 
Like fingers caressing it’s skin, 
     softly ruffling it’s hair…
The breeze can be warm, 
     or cool… 
And it can be placed, 
     anywhere along it’s body… 

As it opens, 
     its nectar flows freely, 
          pooling where petals 
               come back together… 
Sometimes this nectar can be sweet, 
     or just a little sour… 
It all depends on what 
     it wishes to attract…

No two flowers wear 
     the same fragrance,
          for different flowers, 
               attract different things… 
But flowers have 
     the same purpose, 
          to attract something 
               they always attract…
The petals soft as silk, 
     all have a purpose… 
To attract something it must, 
     in order to set seed…
