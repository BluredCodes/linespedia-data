It's time to move on,          to make a clean break.A break from the old life,          to the new life.A release must happen,          so i can move on.Find happiness again,          find love again,          move on past...Away from what I have lost,          on to what i have found:New love...          New happiness...                    A NEW LIFE!!!(C) Jonathan WikkinsAll Rights Reserved
