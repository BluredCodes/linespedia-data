title: "Take My Hand"

Take my hand 
    walk with me… 

In our way 
    stands nothing… 

Love fills our hearts 
    as together we step through life. 

On our walk through life, together, 
    those who see us, 
        know love is full within us. 

Our hearts connect 
    with each other, for all time. 

No matter the distance, 
    the connection remains….
