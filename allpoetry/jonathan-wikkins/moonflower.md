Moonflower, Moonflower
open before me
…

Your nectar's sweet scent flows
bringing desire
…

Open freely,
moonlight's shaft again is here. 
Bathe the night flyer
with taste's desire
…

Its shaft wishes to penetrate
your petal's depths, lubricated
with pooled sweet nectar
…

As clouds arrive and withdraw
again and again
the night flyer again wishes
nectar's sweet fragrance
…

Till the opaque morning dewdrops
burst forth in your petal's depths
Prepare to open
another night comes
…
