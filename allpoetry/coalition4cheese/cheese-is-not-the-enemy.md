---
title: "Cheese *is* not the enemy"
writer: "Coalition4Cheese"
slug: "cheese-is-not-the-enemy"

We here at the Coalition 4 cheese
Merely aim to please
If you do not like cheese
Well have it your way
You can even write some poetry
To rant your release
But we believe
There is a kind of cheese
For every man woman and child
Some is strong
Some is mild
Some will put you to sleep
Some will drive you wild
But we at the Coalition believe
Armageddon is nigh
Unless we can find the means
Of mass cheese production
Spread the good news
Yet detractors say
Cheesecake is sinful
And a ticket to all that is awful
No no we at the Coalition say
Cheese is a peaceful way
We do not turn to riot
We do not turn to violence
Our chefs are the best in the world
Here, try some spinach and ricotta canneloni
And lasagne too
Pizza with all the extras
And cheeseburger made from cows that once went moo
Ok ok perhaps perhaps
We all belong in a zoo
But cheese is not murder
And is good for you
