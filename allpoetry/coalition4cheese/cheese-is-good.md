title: "Cheese *is* good"

Now look at mice
How clever they can be
All because they know
Cheese is good for you and me

Now you won't find a mouse
Starting world war three
Ok ok I know you're thinking of the Brain
And his sidekick Pinky

Now here is some friendly advice
Try giving your cat some cheese
And look at it purr
And beg for more please

The moral of the story
Is animals know a good thing
So eat up eat up
And admit that cheese is king
