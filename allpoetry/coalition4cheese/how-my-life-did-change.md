title: "How my life did change"

My life was boring to say the least 
I was walking down the street 
When a round of gouda hit me 
And I realised what it was to be free 
Eating that yellow stuff 
Smooth, creamy and a little tough 
And that ladies and gentlemen 
Is what cheese did for me 
Now I formed this coalition you see 
To help all those who love cheese
