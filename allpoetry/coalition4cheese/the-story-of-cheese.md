Cheese is big these days
But it never started out that way
Many, many ages back
It was just another mouse snack
Very lowly and humble
Mice had no need to grumble
Because there was enough cheese
Even in winter's deep freeze
Now what happened then
Was a matter debated amongst men
Some say it was a Judas mouse
Oh what a horrid louse
Giving the secret of cheese away
In the fateful month of May
The secret could not be kept
But there came a time where many wept
Armaggedon was threatening
Because cheese supplies were dwindling
So this coalition was formed
To find the means of mass cheese production
And spread the good news
Of cheese and its wonderful ways
Its multiple uses
And so Armageddon was abandoned
Because everyone had discovered the joys
Of cheese
And the world sank into a slumber
Of beautiful peace
The peace of cheeseIf everyone believed, there would be no Armageddon
Cheese is *not* the enemy
