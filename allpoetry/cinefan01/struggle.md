Sin.
A bitter taste
Upon my delicate palette.
Right.
Wrong. 
Gray.
I try to fight 
The urge 
To be lukewarm.
Be it better
To proclaim
The title "Christian"
And sin publicly
Or
Resist labeling,
Live the best I can,
And try not to 
Lead anyone astray.
What does it all mean?
Where does this leave
My soul?
Where does it leave me?
