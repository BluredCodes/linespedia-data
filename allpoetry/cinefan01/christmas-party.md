title: "Christmas Party"

Staring into a sea 
Of smiling faces,
I feel awkward
And ill at ease.
I mean,
They're family.
I know about as 
Much of them
As they know of me.
Once a year,
I see them.
Never quite comfortable,
Never quite fitting in.
They see each other
Much of the time,
Knowing each other better,
Never quite knowing me.
Hard to feel 
That family love,
That family warmth...
With strangers.
