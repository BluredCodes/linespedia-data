Slave to my passions, Bound by my desires,Tortured by carnality.As I cry out in yearning,My body quivers anxiouslyAs you,My warden,Stalk meLike a panther Hunting its prey.You stand before me,Glistening in your sweatAnd masculinity.My body pulses,My heartbeat poundsIn my ears.As you loom over me, My face gleams With perspiration. I lick my lips greedilyAs my eyes swallow you whole.Not enough description? Not vivid enough? Is something missing? I feel like this poem is lacking in something that I am not exactly sure of.
