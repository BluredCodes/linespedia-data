The emptiness in her soul
Was apparent in her eyes.
The fire in her,
That had ignited her passions
For life and love,
Had been ground out
By the heel of Time.
Her life seemed to be lacking
In excitement,
But mostly in meaning.

She's gone now.
The stitched wrists
Cross her lifeless heart
In an empty pit
Six feet under.
Her soul has flown away,
Searching for meaning on Earth
Before heading to the Pearly Gates.Be Honest!
