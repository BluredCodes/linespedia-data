---
title: "My Beloved"
writer: "Jhai"
slug: "my-beloved"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/15360-My-Beloved-by-Jhai"
---

I think of you all day and count
the minutes we can be together again.
I remember how you made me feel the
last time we were together and I smile.
I run to you my beloved.
I can only be happy when I'm around you.
You don't expect anything from me,
you just allow me to be me.
Sometimes when I come to you
my feet are dragging I need you so bad.
Usually by the time we part I feel better
even though I don't want to go.
You're many things to many people
but to me you are mine and mine alone.
The time I spend with you is precious.
I know you will never turn me away
and as long as I respect you,
you'll respect me and never hurt me.
I'll never leave you my beloved
my beloved sea
my turquoise blue
Shore of Free
