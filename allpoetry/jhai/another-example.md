You sit there and write 
stuff on your pad.
I tell you how 
I'm feeling bad.
You force your 'shoulds' 
down my throat.
But I'll throw them back up
and watch them float.
I don't want to be
another example
file or caseload which
I'm sure your ample.
I already know I'm not
a good mother, wife or anything.
That's why I reached 
for the final sting.
So what am I doing here?
Because I have no one else
to talk to except my
pen and paper so I have to
pay for attention and 
shamefully I mention
why I feel so bad or mad.
You sit there and write
it on your pad.
K
