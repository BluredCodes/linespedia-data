I'll be wearing mini-skirts
Black lacy hose
when I am 81 years old.
I'll swim at the beach
each sagging breast will attest.
I've lived my life
I've done my best.
Lifes' too short
to give it less!
I'll belly dance for you
because that's on my list
of things I plan to do
before I am St. Peters guest.
Pierced nipples, navel, eyebrow too,
stilleto heels and a heart tattoo.
We'll have a margarita and
talk about the good old days.
Relive those memories!
So look for me, I'll be the one
that greets you with a toothless smile.
Living life and having fun, 
while I still have a while.
