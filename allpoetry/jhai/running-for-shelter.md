---
title: "Running For Shelter"
writer: "Jhai"

Gray animal shaped clouds
crying like sad angels
sending people running for shelter.
Deep muddy puddles
reminding me of conversations over coffee.
Safe and dry inside
I watch the rain splatter
on my steam filled windows.
A cozy fire, yarn slippers and a good book.
The wonderful world of rain.
But there is another storm inside.
Tears falling, sobs of sorrow.
Sending my emotions running for shelter.
Even though I will never be with you 
ever again
I will always love you.
K3-10-98,
