title: "Operation"

I am laid out
on the operating table.
The light illuminates 
a red reflection.
My chest is pinned
in the open position.
Watch it slowly beat.
Dr. Fate works deftly,
he has never seen a
heart this broken before.
Tragically all realize
that it's a hopeless case.
Nurse psycho puts make-up
and fake smile on my face.
Wheeled to heal at the
intensive care unit.
Then the asylum.
