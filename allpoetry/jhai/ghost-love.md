Specter of my SoulTwisting and turning through my grey matterHaunting my heart your goalChasing my sanity like the Mad HatterCajoling meEnticing meI succumbGhost Love is what I feelWhen I think of youMemories render surrealDo you feel it too?K4-9-994-9-99, Thank God I am NOT haunted anymore. What I felt for this ghost is not more but I still like the poem.
