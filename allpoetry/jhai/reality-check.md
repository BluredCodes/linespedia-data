"Have a good one!"
Said so easily.
The welcome mat pulled in.
Have a good what?
Life?
"Things couldn't be better"
I could hear your voice smile.
I'm back at square one.
Death?
"There's no hope"
How could you mean that?
You told me you loved me.
Oh God....the pain in my soul.
Reality sucks!!
