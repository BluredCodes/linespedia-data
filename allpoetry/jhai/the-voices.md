title: "The Voices"
writer: "Jhai"

I've got a demon on my shoulder
He's just a little guy.
As heavy as a boulder
About two inches high.

He says he is my friend
He likes to give advice.
My business he attends
With gruffy angry cries.

I also have an angel
So pretty and petite.
So lightly does she dangle
Her tiny little feet.

Her voice is soft and sweet
In whispers she would say
"With God you can defeat
for you the price he paid".

Seldom do they agree
On what is right for me.
Often when they fight
I cannot tell who's right.

Life is full of choices
It always has to be.
I listen to the voices
In the end it's up to me.
