These days you wouldn't recognize me
Walking down the street.
I'm chumba wumba to the max
I can't even see my feet.

Eating is my refuge
My comfort and escape.
No longer do I taste the food
As I scrape it from the plate.

I eat to fill the void inside
The place you used to fill.
Confused emotions now collide
With memories I can't hide.

No magic number no perfect size
No glass slipper for my feet.
Prince Charming your a fantasy
So all I do is eat.
