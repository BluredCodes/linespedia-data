title: "Revelation"
writer: "Jhai"

My dear schizophrenic sister
who has been touched by various angels
gave me advice the other day
as I nursed my heart all blistered.
"You should read the Bible...
the Book of Revelation. Pretend you
are the Great and Mighty Whore. 
Use your Imagination"
I laughed so hard cause if she knew
how true the words she spoken.
For my spirit it is broken, hanging frightfully.
I'm a powerless small old closet drunk you see,
reforming as can be and looking for that perfect 
balance to stop the white trash slut inside of me.
I'm so domestically challenged too.
I'm the one with your husband see,
so blind and spiritually bankrupt.
I thought I was so spiritually sown,
found out I've got Spiritual Downs Syndrome.
I want to know the Will of God and I want to know 
Right now! But How??
Guess I'll read the Book of Revelation and pretend
I'm the Great and Mighty Whore.
Just use my imagination.
What a revelation!
