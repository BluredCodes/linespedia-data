If I am mad they come out red
Smell them and you'll wish you're dead.

If I'm deep in thought about you
They come out misty midnight blue.

If I'm feeling mellow
Airborne mass of pastel yellow.

Looking for trouble, when I feel mean
They give me away by going green.

Sometimes they leave a pretty pink
But watchout! Those really stink!

Rainbow hued puffs of air
Breathe those in if you dare.

Those are the ones that smell like roses
Following me all kinds of noses.

Mine are never SBD
I always set them free.

Never a doubt when I'm around.
It's been said my escaping farts,

are truely magnificient works of art!
