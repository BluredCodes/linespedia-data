RUNShe didn't know about the drumsThey didn't know about the funRun and thinkNo, don't thinkSpinRun and spin and laughThey beat their drumsThey face the seaAnd watch the one who runsShe spins and runsTo the beat of the drumsLand....Sea.....Land.....Sea....Beating in the backgroundThe fading drumsK7-25-977-25-97, I used to run barefoot at the beach after work. It was my release. There were these teens with big bongo drums. It was real cool to run by them. I would spread my arms out and run by them. Sounds weird but I had alot of fun. K
