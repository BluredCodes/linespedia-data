In my deepest darkest hour
You were there.
You held my head above
the threatening black seas.
The pain I could not bear,
Fetal position, head in knees.
You held my hand
When all I could do
was sit and stare.
You stroked my hair and
Soothed the pain of life.
Reassuring words said
spoken gently
counteracting what I heard
In my head
Wishing myself dead.
You helped me through
The hell of depression.
Always giving me the impression
Of tender loving care.
You were there
Even if I wasn't.
You helped me survive.
Now I'm coming back alive
And I can see
Your Love is rare.
Thanks for being there.
