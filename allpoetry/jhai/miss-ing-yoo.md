title: "Miss Ing-Yoo"
writer: "Jhai"

When Miss Stakes comes knocking at your door
usually invited by something done before,
her visit isn't pleasant.
Like when you see that witch Miss Eree.
She gets all in your business
I really must confess.
Miss Trust won't leave your home,
it turns into war zones.
Never invite Miss Conduct,
you'll really be out of luck.
She's in your face
like pepper mace.
Be careful what you do
or they'll come visit you.
I know because I tried.
Now I'm stuck with Miss Tee-eyes.
I'm forever Miss Ing-Yoo.
