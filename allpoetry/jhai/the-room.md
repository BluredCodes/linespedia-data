---
title: "The Room"
writer: "Jhai"

Happiness is a role
because
inside of me there is a hole
so 
I journey down into my soul
to 
know myself is my only goal
but
I fear there's nothing there
just
a blank and desolate stare
and 
my mouth gaping in open fear
for 
all the skeletons adorn'd are
here
in the room where no one has a key.
gasp....
I open the chasim and set them free
in 
hopes that they'll stop tormenting me
now 
they're exposed for all to see
and
silently they laugh at me
as 
I crawl into the corner
of 
the room where no one has a key
