---
title: "Gray Day"
writer: "Jhai"
slug: "gray-day"
source: "AllPoetry"

How do you describe a sky so gray and full
of gloom that you see but cannot be?
(The way I feel no emotional connection)

How do you spell the sound of the wind,
blowing in your ears at different rates but
at the same time?
(The way I always talk to and answer myself)

What about the big and puffy clouds being blown
and pushed aimlessly around?
(Like I push my emotions around when I try 
 to deal with my loneliness)

I've tried to run from my life,
Every road is a dead-end.
(I didn't see the signs)

In this gray fog I stagger. What I see
Is not what appears to be.
(Or is it?)

Cold, Damned, Empty, Lost, Gray
