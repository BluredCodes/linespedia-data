---
title: "Ohhhkay...?"
writer: "Jhai"

Monkeys in ruffly underpants
Watching green turtles dance.
Gorillas wearing lipstick
Posing as diva chicks.

Polar Bears float by
on puffy clouds blue sky.
Elephants play hide and seek
Kangaroos acting like geeks.

Underwater seahorse spins
swaying to musical fins.
The mermaids dine on clams
pretending they are eating spam.

These are bits and pieces here
of what happens in my dreaming sphere.
What it means I really can't say
So I wake up and just say....Ohhhhkay...?
K
