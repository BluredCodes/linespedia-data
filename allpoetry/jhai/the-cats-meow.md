---
title: "The Cats Meow"
writer: "Jhai"
slug: "the-cats-meow"

Come on over Mittens and make your bisquets on my tittens.
I'll make some grunion with parsley and chives
but I gotta warn you,
It's a good thing you've got nine lives!
Come one! Come all! We'll have a ball!
I'm alreaky practicing my cat calls.
Don't whisper to anycat a single mew...
and we'll make my special human stew!
Ewwww!!
Just kidding! Just joking! Just pulling you paw!
Ha Ha! We're gonna eat them raw!
Come on down! Come on over!
We'll tease all those city goofy rovers.
I have a special bag like Santy.
Full of duct tape and a ruffly panty.
Don't pack, don't plan...get going now!
Yeow! Wow! It'll be the cat's meow!!
