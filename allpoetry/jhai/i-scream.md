---
title: "I Scream"
writer: "Jhai"
slug: "i-scream"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Like that damn ice cream truck tune
thoughts of you go through my mind
over and over again.
I chase that truck but
I can't catch it as it turns the
corner of fate. 
I'm left standing
on the streets of desire. 
On my own rocky road.
As I scream.
