Don't judge a book by it's cover
Read me
See where I've had to kiss his ass
to make it a love story.
Then there's the pages torn out
now it's a mystery.
Read me
I'm exposed in my pornography.
Different places on my face
underlined
Not a bestseller but
sure to make you see the irony.
Read me
K5-13-99,
