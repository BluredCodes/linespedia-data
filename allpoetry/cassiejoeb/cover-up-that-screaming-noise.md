title: "Cover Up (That Screaming Noise)"

Immature, lively, feeding wolves
Pale, unsecured, mindless tools
Jealous, hurting, striking arms
Memory loss and breaking charms
Bruising, burning, laughing marks
Shaking, swallowing, bigmouth sharks
Stealing, stinging, perfect toys
Cover up the screaming noise
Please cover up that screaming noise
Steal away the hatred gaze
Appear so sane and laugh in day
Then you cover up the midnight craze
Cover up as you take me away
Cover up and give display
Hide it all
Hide it all
Take away what you gave to me
Cover up what cant be seen
Bloody shadows line your fence
Masked it all in your defense
Immature, lively, feeding wolves
Pale, unsecured, mindless tools
Cover up the screaming noise
Please cover up that screaming noiseThis was going to be for a new band my friends starting...its not exactly on beat or anything...more or less a scrap metal song..
