Running down an ally,
I don’t know where to turn,
They want me dead or alive,
Either way I’m going to burn.
They chase me down the streets,
And into brick wall dead-ends,
And to think these were the people,
I used to call my friends.
They are hiding in the shadows,
They can’t leave me alone,
I’m searching for a new life,
One that I can call my own.
I don’t know why they are involved,
I don’t even know why they care,
I don’t mean something to them,
It’s the lies that I can’t bear.
They tell me these things,
That I don’t want to hear,
They are just completely blind,
I know there is plenty to fear.
So please, anyone but them,
Answer me when I scream,
Please do something,
