Every where I turn,
There is another hell,
I feel torture and regret,
And the pain I know so well.
Every path that I followed,
Has another dead end,
More lies, and more hurt,
I am my only friend.
I cant stop myself,
I hear the voices cry,
Couldn’t I just give it up?
How easy it is to die.
Every time there is bliss
I try to get away,
Hide myself in all the joy,
But they can find me anyway.
They take my all dreams,
Steal my heart and brake it,
All the tension in my mind,
I don’t think I can take it.
Every time I tried to go,
I get back where I was,
No one left to set me right,
And never someone to love.
