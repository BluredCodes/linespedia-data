---
title: "Laugh At My Suicide"
writer: "CassieJoeB"

Don’t make me loose it
Your driving me insane
You force me to have to hate,
Give me so much pain.
You don’t care how it hurts,
And you watch but you don’t say,
You can see me suffering,
But you just stay away.
You laugh when I fall,
Try to make me fall again,
And the looks on your face,
I cant seem to ever win.
And you watch me every time
That I am wrong and you are right,
You force me to stay in the dark,
When my heart strives for the light.
And you just stand back when I scream,
Its never your fault, not you.
You can smile and laugh.
But if only you felt it too.
You cant feel anything,
I wish you could feel my pain,
The hurt so bad it makes you shake,
Youd want to die and never live again.
Your driving me crazy,
And you’ll watch me die.
You wont care, you might laugh.
When I finally commit suicide.
