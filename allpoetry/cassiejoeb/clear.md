title: "Clear"

The evil that lives inside me 
That wakes me up at night 
I thought it was gone. 
Here it is again 
It is dark outside again 
As clear as the stained bloody sleeve 
I can feel what he’s after 
Why does he want me? 
His hunger hurts my hands 
I can’t control them 
As clear as the knife I hold 
It rapes my mind 
Everything is his 
As clear as the pain 
Life is suddenly empty 
So clear I can see to the end 
I see through life 
To my destiny in the fire
