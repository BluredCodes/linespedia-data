title: "Always Sleeping"

See through my eyes
You’ll see the ceiling
Because I’m being walked on
While I sleep on the floor 
I don’t care what happens
Long as you don’t follow me
Think I’m a dead on the ground
Somewhere I’m living
But I don’t want you to find me
I don’t want you lying next to me
Us both in an idiot sleep
I’m oblivious to the universe
Because I’m always sleeping
