In my dream I see you walk away
Smiling, while I beg you to stay
Fading in to nothing
Leaving me to be nothing
I see you never look back
I feel you getting fainter
Drifting off to a new world
Where I am not welcome
Losing you 
I am lost
You forgetting me
I embrace our memories
Together never again
You letting my hand go
But not staying 
To watch my first step
Carry me to the stars
But it’s a one-way trip
Alone out there
You go home without me
I stand in the vast emptiness
I open my eyes
