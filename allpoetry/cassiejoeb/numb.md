I cant be anything
Because I feel nothing
Just walking numbly down the hallway
My feet move automatically
I don’t do anything anymore
It just happens
Without will or want 
I just go on through life
I just live and I hate it
Slowly dragging on 
Watching the world around me
But I don’t care anymore
The world has people
People are fucked up
They numb me
