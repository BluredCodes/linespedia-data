It all burns in my soul
Even by the fire I feel cold
This life's left such a hole in me
Don't hate me if I leave this place
Sometimes I pray
Not to wake up the next day
I'm just waiting
For a blackout to take me away
I opened my eyes and I killed my sleep
You left me so broken
You left me living
So I lye here
Listening to nothing real
Just the things over in my head
They don't make noise
They just make me feel
I don't want to feel you again
The pain you left in me
I hear it over again
I experience it over till Im crying
The pain is just so dark
There is not light in the world
Everything there was, you cast a shadow on
So Ill never move
You took the reasons to live
There was none
I only thought there was
No I know the truth
And I'll lye here forever
Broken
I'm just waiting
