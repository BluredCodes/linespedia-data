---
title: "You Said"
writer: "CassieJoeB"
slug: "you-said"
source: "AllPoetry"

You said we would always be together,
That friendships like ours last forever.

You said through thick and thin,
Together we would fight to win.

You said, you were going to be there,
When I need a shoulder or someone to care.

You said, you believed in me and my dreams,
That you know how hard it sometimes seems.

You said, you would forgive my errors,
And you would cure all my terrors,

You said, we were as close as could get,
Closer than anyone has ever been yet.

You said, you would always blame the other guy
And everyday stand close by my side.

You said, to always believe in you,
And everything you said was true.

You said, you could never tell a lie,
Secrets weren’t kept and laughter would never die.

You said we were meant to be together,
I thought these promises should last forever.

Then you said I lived in a universe,
Full of bad luck and curses,
I was where you couldn’t stand to be in,
Some dark place deep and within,
You said you couldn’t understand my cry,
But you could, though you didn’t even try.
