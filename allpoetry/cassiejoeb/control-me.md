The whole worlds against me,
Like they want me dead,
Giving me all this shit,
Negative thoughts to fill my head,
Everyone’s a critic towards me,
Have no respect for my view,
My life is never up to me,
They tell me what to say and do,
As if I belong to them, 
It’s like my life was never mine,
I want to get away from it all,
But there’s no one to hide behind.
They all want to control me,
Like I am some sort of toy,
To play and have their fun,
And in the end destroy.
But finally I don’t give a damn, 
So stop telling me what to do,
I’d already be dead,
