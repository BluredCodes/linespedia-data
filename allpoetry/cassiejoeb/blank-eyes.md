Looking at the mirror,
Glaring at the wall,
Searching for a soul,
I see nothing at all.
They are empty,
Theres nothing in my eyes,
Just the wetness of tears,
And that never dries.
Blank and so gray,
Where there once was light
They seemed to have dawned,
And there is now night.
My eyes were happy and full,
But now there nothing there,
What happened to me,
To make my eyes so bare? ©  on Jun 01 2001 04:47 PM PST   0  • 10
