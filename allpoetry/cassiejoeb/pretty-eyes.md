title: "Pretty Eyes"

Strangling now on this dirt
The path that I wanted 
Your pretty eyes can't take the pain
Your pretty eyes are so dim now
Vacantly searching in my own soul
Sorry that it was never a bad dream
Something never seen before
It's always harder the first time
Your pretty eyes never glow anymore
You always have a reason to look away
New pain that invades from the backdoor
Never knew things came that way
Creeping up the step to slaughter
Your pretty eyes are crying for me now
Know that soon I'll stop breathing
I love your pretty eyes
But I don't want your pity
