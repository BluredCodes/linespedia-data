title: "Spinning In My Mind"
writer: "CassieJoeB"

Sometimes late at night
I like to close my eyes
Lay my head on the pillow
Think of you and cry

I think about how we were
How close we used to be
And when we would sit alone
The way you would say u love me

*I get lost in these dreams
That I imagine all the time
Now it’s all a nightmare
Spinning in my mind*

I thought about the way you were
Before that one last day
When I gave my life to you
But you gave your love away

The day I was ready to kill
I remember how you lied 
If it weren’t for all my friends
I swear I would have died

*I get lost in these dreams
That I imagine all the time
Now it’s all a nightmare
Spinning in my mind*

I was so scared to live
I couldn’t go on without you
I thought I had real love but
All this time it was untrue

Lying on my pillow and crying
And here I am again after you’re gone
Maybe I’ll get over you
If not, I wont go on

*I get lost in these nightmares
That I dream of all the time
I wish instead they were peaceful dreams
Spinning in my mind*
