---
title: "Stay For The Baby"
writer: "CassieJoeB"
slug: "stay-for-the-baby"

I don’t know if you’re the one
That’s I will love for all my days
But for now please don’t leave
I need you in so many ways
And Im not sure if we can make it
Out there in this world alone
Together, is it strong enough?
Better than being on my own
Im still holding on to you
Because I am so afraid
I don’t know if I love you
But a baby’s on the way.
And Im sorry I really am
That I need you for support
Don’t run away from me
Cut what little we have short
Now I know for sure your not the one
But you’re the father of the boy
But don’t leave because of him
His heart is not a toy
Don’t leave because hes coming
Its your fault too
How would you like your dad
