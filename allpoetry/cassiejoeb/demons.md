title: "Demons"

I tried so hard,
But I lost the fight,
Will all the demons,
That torture me at night.
I’m worthless and lost,
I am to believe,
As the razor they gave me,
Drips blood down my sleeve.
I pass through the day 
Without feeling emotion
I try to hide in the land
Leave to the ocean,
In the dark they all return,
Never a night of peace,
And no one else ever could,
Understand what I see.
I ignore what they cry,
But I cant get away,
They harass me, and hurt,
Till the sun rises for day.
But they can still find me
By the scent of my tears
Stealing love and happiness
Stealing hours and yearswritten: that night
