---
title: "For My Brother"
writer: "CassieJoeB"
slug: "for-my-brother"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I am your older sister
Do you know who I am?
You may never speak
I might never hear your words
I wish you could tell me 
Because I don’t know you
I see you but we have never met
You don’t comprehend what I say
You look at me with blank eyes
And you never recognize me
I want to be close to you
Like brother and sister
But you’re so far away
And I may never really meet you
We could always be apart
But in the same room
I tell you everything
About life and people
How I love you so much
But you never understand me
It makes me cry to know
You’ll always be there 
But never here with me
