Touch stars beyond the borderStill true desire that nothing lightsI can never be sane this wayCloud ends crash and make thunderStanding in the rainI patiently wait for the world to endIf I could find the one thingThat would make me completeThen I could stop waitingBut until then I'll waitAnd I'll pray
