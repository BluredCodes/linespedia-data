I walk around today 
And you think I am here
But I am gone
I have been so dead
For the longest time
Hopeless and angry
Angry at what
I don’t know
Just feeling the heat inside
Pure aggressive pain
From the games they played
They took my life
And it belongs to them
They ate it like wolves
They beg for more
They wont get me this time
I will burn them before the go near
I play with fire
I get caught again
Standing in the room
There is no door here
My hand moves through the wall
Its not there
I just see it
Like me
I’m not there
