---
title: "Have I Changed?"
writer: "coyote"
slug: "have-i-changed"

What gives you cause to say I've changed...in any direction?
You have known me again but a short time.
Who is to say our first re-meeting was not a show...a put-on
I tried to correct for my having been away.
I used manners and customs I had previously known, but have used very little.
You see me now as different, less clean, non-habitual.
Please, look at me. See me...reliant, loving, caring.
I may no longer be your ideal of well bred, but I am sensitive and particular.
I still want the best, or whatever it is that will satisfy me.
I am human and cannot explain all things, especially one person to another.
Not me to you.

San Pablo 18 Oct 1971
