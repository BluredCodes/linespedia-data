---
title: "2 Hearts Beating"
writer: "coyote"
slug: "2-hearts-beating"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/2759-2-Hearts-Beating-by-coyote"

2 hearts happy
2 hearts beating
1 heart loving
1 repeating
2 young lovers
2 hearts leading
2 each other
2 hearts beating.
