title: "Somebody Changed the Channel"
writer: "coyote"

I was watching the world as the clock ticked by.
The picture was clear, not a cloud in the sky.
Just enjoying the scenery that filled up my eyes--
then somebody changed the channel.

Just a second ago it was clear, blue and bright.
And now, of a sudden, it's darker than night...
and the noise that it made caused a terrible fright--
'cause somebody changed the channel.

Attacked from behind by a cowardly clan.
Destroying and killing as only they can.
Sacrificing so many just to carry out a plan,
Please, somebody, change the channel.

Now the ashes have settled; our spirits have, too.
But we have to rise up and then start anew,
for the flag's flying higher for me and for you...
and it's flying on every channel.
