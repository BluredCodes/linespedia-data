As soon as I asked the question, 
I remembered where I put the fuses--duh!
Why does this always happen--
not so much that it happens, I guess, 
but why at such inopportune times
as this where she is sure to find out?
Like the time I was enjoying the sound
of rain beating on the window. 
I was even watching it run down in sheets,
admiring it's intensity.
Then she walks in, dry as a bone, and says,
"a sprinkler head has broken off in the yard.
Can't you hear it on the window?"Sequel to Sitting Alone.....don't think I'm just making all this stuff up.
