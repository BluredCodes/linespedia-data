There was a helicopter crash today.
It was only about 150 feet off my post.
There were four men aboard--all gone.
It wasn't burning when I started, 
but an explosion went off as I neared.
When I got there it was all aflame.
I could hear movement, and a guy screaming for help.
I couldn't even see him let alone help him.
When the fire was put out I saw the remains of them--
all burned to death.
The pilot still had his head but it was all burned--
his lips were cherry red.
The fourth man was under the turbine in a ditch...
no arms, legs or head.
I even cried when I saw him.
What was I supposed to do?

Tan Son Nhut  15 Mar 1971
