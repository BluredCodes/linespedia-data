Okay, maybe the water thing wasn't 
strictly my fault, but it sure made 
me look pretty dumb.
That's what this is really all about,
looking dumb at the wrong moments.
I don't need any help in that department--
I do fine all by myself.
Like driving a car. My wife doesn't like
backing up a car, especially while parking.
I do it all the time, either on the road or 
in a parking lot--it's really no big deal.
She's always saying that it must be a "man 
thing", because she sees so many men do it, 
and very few women.
So the other night, as we were leaving her 
mom's house, I put HER car into reverse so 
I can pull around a car parked in front of us.
She makes a comment about backing up and I
immediately hit a truck that I hadn't 
noticed before (not a drink passed my lips).
There wasn't much physical damage, but there
didn't have to be, did there?Why Me?
