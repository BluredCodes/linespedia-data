---
title: "No Threat, No Sweat"
writer: "coyote"

As I gaze into the distance,
and center my sights,
I try to get a sharp visual...
Damn these cloudy nights.
Then there's movement,
just a hair off to the right, 
so I squeeze the trigger,
and my muzzle flashes bright.
That's one less threat to worry me
on this lonely, rainy night.
