---
title: "Can You Feel It?"
writer: "coyote"

Can you feel it, can you move? Does your mind reflect this motion?
Will you give what you receive or does your heart cast out emotion?
Is it simple to survive; can you tell yourself to forget?
Did your love make you want to live...or have you tasted it, even yet?
When the world resolves this matter, saying who should live or die,
tell them you should go on living, all your life is only giving,
and that alone should be worth saving.
You should live, not I.
I have found my peace, you must still try.

Nha Trang 14 Jul, 1971
