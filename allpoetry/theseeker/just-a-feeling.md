---
title: "Just a Feeling"
writer: "theseeker"
slug: "just-a-feeling"
source: "AllPoetry"

A cold morning watching the sun rise.
A wish on a falling star.
A touch from a friend.
All just a feeling we can't explain.
A new life born.
A life passed on.
A kiss of love.
All just a feeling we hold inside.
A world of possibilities.
An impossible task.
Love.
All just a feeling that we hold in our heart.
All just a feeling...
