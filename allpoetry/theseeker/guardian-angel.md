---
title: "Guardian Angel"
writer: "theseeker"

Held to life
By the ones he loves
Holding on for the hopes
That he can help save them too
Hold them up
Make them feel special for a moment
And give a little hope to people.
Holding everything back
Making others feel important
Giving love away 
But who guards the guardian
When the walls come crumbling down
The tears come flowing out
The trickle of blood forms
When the hearts gone...
The guardian angel is all alone.
