---
title: "Believe"
writer: "theseeker"
slug: "believe"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I may not know
The pain you feel right now
But I do know 
That seeing you hurt 
Is the saddest thing for me.
I care about you,
Miss you,
Love you.
I want to see you make it in this world.
Even though I know,
I can never be with you,
You can always trust
That my heart will be with you.
I'll go on fighting for you,
And you can always believe
That I will care about you.
I love you,
With all my heart.
