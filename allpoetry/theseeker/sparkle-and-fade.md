---
title: "Sparkle and Fade"
writer: "theseeker"
slug: "sparkle-and-fade"
source: "AllPoetry"

Happiness.
Love.
Life.
A shining moment before it all fades away
Who would have thought,
After all this time,
We're chasing after those stars that are just
Going to wink away into nothing.
It's all going to sparkle for one great moment
And fade away into nothing.
Sparkle and fade...I kinda got the idea from the Fuel song Shimmer, but hey.
