---
title: "With You"
writer: "theseeker"
slug: "with-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

I don't know what it is
But I know it feels good inside.
Everything seems better,
The world looks different,
I don't feel so trapped in my shell.
Glimmers of hope on the horizon.
The shadows of life fade for one moment.
The world seems full of possibilities
And my life, for once, worthwhile...
With You.
