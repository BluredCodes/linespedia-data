title: "Will I Ever Know?"

Will I ever know…
What made me visit your website?
Your sense of humor was pure delight.

Will I ever know…
What brought us together?
Maybe it's just "Birds of a Feather".

Will I ever know…
Why I have such strong feelings for you?
A day without contact can make me so blue.

Will I ever know…
The feel of your kiss, the touch of your hand?
The miles between us are hard to stand.

Will I ever know…
How it feels to have your arms around me?
I can hear you tell me to wait patiently.

Will I ever know…
How does it feel to be the shower of your affection?
I'm positive it's close to shear perfection.

Will I ever know…
If these feelings for me you share?
I, at least know, in some ways you care.

Will I ever know…
What it feels like to be secure?
The distance between us makes me feel so unsure.

Will I ever know…
If I need help, will you be my guide?
All I need to know is that you want to be by my side.This was written to an internet friend who I felt very close to,
