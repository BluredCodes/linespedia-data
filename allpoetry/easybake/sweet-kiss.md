---
title: "Sweet Kiss"
writer: "easybake"

We stood under the stars, you're hands on my waist,
The crickets chirped loudly, almost in haste.
You moved in closer, you're eyes locked in mine,
I longed for your kiss, for just a moment in time.
You're lips met mine, and I felt the sensation,
No longer did I wait, to give into the sweet temptation.
My knees went weak, my palms became sweaty,
I want to go back to that place, where you've taken me already.
Where the world disappears, and all that's left is you and I,
so come again to me soon, so that I can touch the sky.This is for a friend who lives a little ways away and who 
came to spend a very special birthday with me.
