When I needed a shoulder to cry on,
When I needed a willing ear,
When no one else wanted to hear it,
You were there.

When I was sad and depressed,
When I was rejected and disappointed,
When I was angry or lonely,
Only you were there.

Through the good times and the bad,
When no one seemed to care,
I've come to notice, that you were always there.This was written for my best friend of 10 years.  When I look
back on the dark times in my life, she was always there for me.
