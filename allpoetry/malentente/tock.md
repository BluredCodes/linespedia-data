Caring but helpless . . .
. . . shackled feelings abound.
Mind becomes tired . . .
. . . hear the same old sound.

Marching quickly, nowhere . . .
. . . always turning around.
Looking deep into mirrors . . . 
. . . only sadness is found.

Movement like a pendulum . . .
. . . forwards is backwards found.
Always coming closer . . . 
. . . but never reaching ground.

Time loses relevance . . . 
. . . nothing seems to astound.
Clanging ever slower . . .
. . . life's clock has come unwound.I'd had a veeerrrrryyy bad day.  It got better and I rewound my clock.
