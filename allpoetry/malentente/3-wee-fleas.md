Three wee fleas . . . livin' on a pup.
One began bitin' . . . wakin' Rover up.
Scratchin' and itchin' . . . he couldn't find a one.
Laid back down . . . a sleepin' in the sun.
Second little flea . . . makin' not a sound.
Started chewin' hard . . . round an' round.
Rover turned over . . . usin' a back paw.
Diggin' an' thumpin' . . . stopped it with a claw.
Just as his eyes closed . . . flea three began to work.
Makin' that doggie wake . . . with a twitch'n an'a jerk.
Havin' 'bout enough . . . he headed fer th'water.
