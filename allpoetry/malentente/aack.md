title: "'aack!'"
writer: "MalEntente"

Bullys abound and always will
Life deals out a lot of swill.
Things don't work out, more often than not
And when you want cold, you somehow get hot.

When it says push, you have to pull
The best food is served, after you're full
You're never prepared for a bloody nose,
And you'll always get sprayed if there's a garden hose.

Your car won't run and their mouth won't stop
You only break laws when you're around a cop
Somehow things break whenever you're around
Your grass dies fast . . . but your weeds abound.

Losing your temper you break what is dear
The classiest drink you can think of is beer
Forget your wardrobe, it's out of style
Besides it's all dirty and stacked in a pile.

All in all, only nonsense . . . there in your head,
You might as well give in . . . and go back to bed.
