There's a silly question that I've always kept.
Whether Teddy Bears dreamed whenever they slept.
And, if they do, what do they see in there . . .
In the night's darkness - perhaps their soul-bear?
And when they dream, can they walk around?
And can dreaming bears make any sound?
But the most important question is . . .
. . . can dreaming Teddy Bears . . . really kiss?Of course.. the Poem NAMED itself!
Again - here I go mixing Teddy Bears and soul-mates . . . geeeezzz.
Think I have a fixation?
