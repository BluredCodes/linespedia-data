title: "'Soft'"
writer: "MalEntente"
slug: "soft"

The touch of a baby's first little reach,
Fresh, furry fuzz, on a ripened peach,
A whispering breeze, blowin' here and there,
The cute round tummy of a teddy bear.

The warm fur of a sleepin' kitty cat,
A newborn puppy's paws - goin' pitterpat,
A butterfly kiss, lightly on your cheek,
A slow floatin' feather, playin' hide'n'seek.

A sweet summer rain - tappin' at your window,
A cute little wink . . . without innuendo,
My thoughts drift to you, like a gentle dove,
This, and more, is how I feel . . . when I feel your love.As with all my poems, they were named after they were written.  This poem literally screamed it's own name to me.  See if you don't agree!
