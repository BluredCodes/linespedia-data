Long and lacy, silk starts dripping,
From sheer - off shoulder - slipping.
Gravity pulls, curves relinquished,
Clothes to floor, touch established.

Fingertip traces a long outline,
From arm to shoulder, over to spine.
Softness shimmers in dimmist light,
Distance diminishing into the night.

Intimacy wrapping around and through,
Heat inside bringing window dew.
Blended hearts for a moment in time,
Speaking only . . . in pantomime.

Moments blur . . . but last forever,
Wanting, willing, never to sever.
Gentleness demanding anticipation,
Protecting both . . . with honest elation.we all have our moments.. that BURN into our memories with love and deep emotions.  Enjoy!
