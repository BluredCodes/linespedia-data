..
...
boom
bah boom
Your heart 
speaks in rhythm
to my ear, pressed against
your warm shield of a chest,
and a syrup sigh escapes my lungs
as I press my lips to your shoulder.
I’m caught on you like a leaf on a fence
Our love becomes kinetic--
our souls are magnetic,
like two pieces of a
whole, severely
longing for
completion;
compassion,
rash sensations
invoke my rampant hips.
I lightly brush your lips,
you kiss my aching fingertips..
Primal drives collide with infinite true love
and combust in the sky as our limbs enfold.
Your hands rove to secluded regions
invoking their treasures
with magical strokes
and spellbound, we 
slip together to
tumble into one
single rhythm
bah boom
boom
...
..
