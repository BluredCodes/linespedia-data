Jesus^2
that would be Jesus x Jesus
so what would that equal you ask?
well as everyone knows we can’t solve this equation solely with what we are given.
But let us understand that Jesus^2+ 84= 88
then we can simply deduce that Jesus = 2
Then lets say that we have a circle with radius (Jesus) of 4,
find the circumference (A Kitten)
well that would be um... well I don’t have a calculator but it would be
A Kitten = pi x (2 x Jesus)
simpler being
A Kitten = pi2Jesus
Through this mathematical equation we can deduce that Jesus is directly related to A
Kitten through their mutual similarity in pi.
As everyone knows, pi is the same as Pie (my favorite is key-lime)
As holy scripture states, Jesus is the son of god.
Because of Jesus’ brotherly relationship with A Kitten  A Kitten is the son of god as well. 
(or if the Kitten is a female, daughter of god)
because A Kitten and Jesus are directly related to a piece of Pie (key-lime to be specific),
then logically we (as the educated community) can deduce that,
Key-Lime Pie is God and the father of Jesus and A Kitten.
Thank you for your time and god bless.unknown,
