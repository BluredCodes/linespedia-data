A friend .....
A= attends you
 B= believes in you
C= cries for you (washes away your pain w/ all their tears)
 D= does time for you (heaven forbid)
E= explains things to you
 F= flies to be close to you
G= goes to extremes for you
 H= holds the door for you
I= interjects when you say negitive things  about yourself
 J= jokes with you
K= kills for you (heaven forbid)
 L= loves you like no other can
M= makes room for you
 N= novrishes your needs
O= opens up too you
 P= prays for you (sell their sould for something prue&true like somebody   like you) 
Q= quiets your fears
 R= revolves around you
S= steals for you
 T= throws away all the pain your living
U= understands you
 V= violates all the love your missing
W= waits for you
 X= xcepts your weirdness
Y= yells at you when your doing wrong
 Z= zooms to your rescue
