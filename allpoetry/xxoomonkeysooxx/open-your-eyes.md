Open your eyes,
tell me what do you see?
Open your eyes,
and describe it too me.
If you open your eyes,
you will see how I care.
Open though eyes,
and I will be waiting there.
Open you eyes,
and embrace me.
Open your eyes,
can't we live happily?
Open those beautiful eyes,
and see how much our love will erupt.
You should of opened your eyes from the start,
and gave me a special place in your heart.To Jason, I love you babe.
