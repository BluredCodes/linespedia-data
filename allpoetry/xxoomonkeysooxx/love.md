title: "Love"

I love you
   and you love me.
Is it a miracle?
   How could that be?
Love is a precious feeling.
   Hold it to your heart.
Never let go.
   Never will we be apart.
How did faith bring us love.
   Only the answers lie up above.
I make everyone see, we are in love.
   Two in one harmony,
love for two perfect people.
   Love is endured and so needful.
Just remember one thing is true.
   I will always, forever in my heart...
         LOVE YOU.
