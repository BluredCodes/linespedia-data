I said,"I love you."
You said whatever.
I said,"I want to stay with you forever."
You just said, ya right.
I said," I want a baby."
You said,"NO!"
I wonder why???
I guess you took me as a joke, and I took you as a joke.
It turns out we were both serious.
We had nothing in common.
We still stayed together.
Afetr we worked things out for about a year,
we got married.
We had two twin boys.
I started working.
You stayed at home with the boys.
Except when I got home, you'd go out.
A year later I found out you were having an affair with my best friend.
I kicked you out and she took you in.
I hate you and she loves you.
I moved on, moved out of town, and re-married.
I married a doctor, one who knows how to treat a lady.
One day I saw you out on our street.
You were calling out my name.
Wondering where I was, I was in the house right in front of him.
You were desprite and wanted me back.
It turns out my best friend was to "exotic" for you.
You heard I for into a fight, and my marriage "split".
Now you want me back, but theres something I want to say ....
......."You screw you lose!" Sorry I'am happy the way my life is ....
...... with out you!!No I am not married. By friend got married when she was young, and this in away happened to her.
