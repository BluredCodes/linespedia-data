As you sit there writing,
I sit and stare,
at your warm, swwet smile, and wonder if you'd care.
I wonder if you feel the same way,
I wonder if you like me too,
Do you ever look at me?
Do you think of me as I think of you?
What's on your mind,
who are you thinking of,
all these questions I ask myself as I sit and wait for love.
Who do you dream of,
when you lie in bed at night?
I dream of you as I lay and hold my pilliow tight.
I can only wonder what you're thinking,
as I sit and stare at you,
hoping one day you'll notice me,
like i've noticed you.
