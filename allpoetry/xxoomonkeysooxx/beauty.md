Love is beauty.
Love is sea.
Love is a wonderful feeling,
between you and me.
Love me never let go.
Let all your feelings show.
Love is beauty.
Love is thought.
Love will make you see.
Love can not be bought.
Love is beautiful don't deny,
give it a chance.
Let me try.
Love is always a beauty,
when two people share it,
especially you, and me.
