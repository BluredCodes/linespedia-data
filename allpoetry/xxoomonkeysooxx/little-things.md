Your the little thing that matters.
Your the little thing thats shattered.
Your the little thing that holds me here.
Your the little thing that I love so dear.
Your the little thing that makes me mad.
Your the little thing that I make sad.
Your the little thing that smiles glad.
Your the little thing that makes me wear plaid.
Your the little thing that touches me here.
Your the little thing that I touch there.
Your the little thing that cried for me.
Your the little thing that let mr beg on one knee.
Your the little thing that stays inside of me.
Your the little thing that I want for eterity.
