As I look out the window, a tear falls from my eyes.
I start to cry thinking about you.                             I feel you in my dreams,holding me so near and being sinceare.  Looking out the window I stare up at the sky looking at the bright stars: wondering who you are thinking of as the night goes by.
I wish upon a star that one day my wish will come true.
This I can dream forever.... to be with you.
