I broke your heart
without me knowing.
I broke your heart
without it showing.
I said I love you,
you didn't respond.
I say to myself,
what have I dont wrong.
You wrote me letters, 
saying good-byes.
I wrote you back asking why?
You said we have changed,
that our love will never be the same.
I told you NO,
that you were wrong.
I said our love,
will grow forever strong.
I begged and begged, 
but got no mercury.
I even cried a poor mans pity.
Now here I am, 
lost in this world.
I want you so bad,
to have and to hold.
I promise I'd never do wrong, 
if only i gotten the one that I love.
