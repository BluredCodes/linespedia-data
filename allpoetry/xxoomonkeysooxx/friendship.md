Your friendship is very kind
Im glad your a friend of mine.
You were the only one there for me when I needed you.
You always cared.
You are a definition what a friend really is.
I wish I had more.
I wish every friend was like you.
Thanks for being good to me,
when I was wrong.
You always made me see even when we had a fight.
We kept a friendship.
Everything turned out alright.
I'am grateful that you are my friend to be with me, till the end.
