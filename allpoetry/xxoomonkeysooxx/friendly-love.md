title: "friendly Love"

This friendship I love so much
this gentle heart
i hold 
waiting for your soft touch
a dream to uncover
to unfold
I love the way I feel when I am with you
a feeling none can steal
my love is forever true
No matter where we go
i know where my heart may be
becuase you taught me to grow
and showed me how to be.
Remember when you are wanderingalone
don't complain or despain
for you're never really
on your own
because in my heart
I will always be there.
I love you
with all my heart
and will never let go till the end.
I pray we'll never be apart
becuase you are my very best friend.
