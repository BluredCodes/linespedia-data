First of all I want to thank you,
Jesus almighty.
Only you ever knew.
Thank you for always being by myside,
always loving me
even when I lied.
It is true what people say.
God you do work in a mysterious way.
When I lost all faith and hope I wanted to die.
Just hang myself with a rope,
but your love surrounded me.
It went to my heart,
and made me live peacefully.
Thank you for all the things you've done,
you never let me hold back, turn away, or run.
Without you who knows where I would be.
Thank you Jesus for everything you made of me.
