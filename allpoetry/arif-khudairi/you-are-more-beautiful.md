---
title: "You Are More Beautiful"
writer: "Arif Khudairi"
slug: "you-are-more-beautiful"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/6191-You-Are-More-Beautiful-by-Arif-Khudairi"
---

You still 
The same,
My love.
You have not
Changed much.
You still
The same,
As before:
Your eyes
Still look
As wide
And blue
As the skies.
Your lips
Look like
Red tulips.
Your hair
Still looks
Like spikes.
Your cheeks,
Your neck,
Your laughter,
Your smile
Look today
As they did
Yesterday.
You still
The same,
My love.
You have not
Changed much.
You still
The same
As before.
You only
Have become
More tender,
More elegant,
More beautiful
Than before.
