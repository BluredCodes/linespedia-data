---
title: "Woman"
writer: "Arif Khudairi"
slug: "woman"
source: "AllPoetry"

Woman!
What would we
All be
If it was
Not for you?
What would 
Our life
Look like
Without you?
A gloomy,
Long night,
With no stars,
With no moon?
Or a dry,
barren Sahara,
With no streams,
With no trees?
You are
A precious,
Sacred gift,
With which
We are blessed.
Every day,
Every night,
You shower
Our life
With your light,
With your charm,
And delight.
You give love,
And pride.
You give sense
Of belonging.
You give hope,
Sacrifice.
And for this,
And for that,
And for more,
And more,
How can we
Repay you?
As a symbol
Of every
Beautiful thing
In our life?
You should always
Be treated
As you deserve:
With love,
And respect,
With gratitude,
And pride.
