---
title: "Token of Love"
writer: "Arif Khudairi"
slug: "token-of-love"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/13501-Token-of-Love-by-Arif-Khudairi"
---

Like a camel,
In a Sahara,
As vast
As my heart,
I will travel
All my life.
And like
A cloud,
As white
As the snow,
I will drift,
Day and night,
Trough blue,
Wide skies.
I will sail
Across sacred,
Deep rivers.
I will cross
The seven seas.
I will reach
All the shores.
I will wander
In the cities
Till I find
The shore
Of your eyes.
Then I will
Set my ship
To fire.
I will throw
The oar
Into the air,
And the sail
Into the waves
Of your sea.
And I will
kneel down
On my knees
To place
My heart,
My dreams,
My life,
At your feet,
Hoping that
You may
Accept
All of them
As a token
Of my love
For you.
