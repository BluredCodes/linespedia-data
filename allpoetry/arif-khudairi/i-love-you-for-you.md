---
title: "I  Love  You  For  You"
writer: "Arif Khudairi"
slug: "i-love-you-for-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/8487-I--Love--You--For--You-by-Arif-Khudairi"

It is true 
I love you, 
Sweetheart, 
Not because 
You have 
A pretty face 
With large 
Blue eyes 
Like the color 
Of the skies,
And delicious 
Sultry lips 
Made of soft, 
Red tulips. 
I love you,
My beloved, 
Not for 
The music 
In your voice 
Which falls, 
From your lips 
Like sweet, 
Passion fruit.
Nor for  
Your elegant 
Long neck 
Like a swan. 
I love you, 
My darling, 
Not for this
Nor for that; 
For this
Kind of love
Is not
A real love. 
I love you, 
Dearest one, 
For what 
I can see
With the eye
Of my heart 
Deep within 
Your skin, 
And beyond 
The color 
And the size 
Of your eyes, 
And your lips. 
I love you, 
My love,
For the sake 
Of love,
And for you.
