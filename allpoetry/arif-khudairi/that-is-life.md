---
title: "That Is Life"
writer: "Arif Khudairi"
slug: "that-is-life"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/13999-That-Is-Life-by-Arif-Khudairi"
---

That is life:

A mystery,

A riddle,

A paradox.

No one

Understands

The real secrets

Behind

The waves,

The forests,

The clouds.

Spring comes,

And leaves,

Then autumn

Soon arrives.

Laughter always

Follow cries.

Tears spring

From smiles.

Pleasures lead

To pains,

And sorrows

To delights.

Night gives

Birth to day.

And day

Gives birth

To night.

Life is

Nothing but

A shadow,

A dream,

A slumber,

We fall in,

And awake

Only when

The birds

Fly back

To paradise.

Think not

Too much

About it.

Life is

A mystery,

A riddle,

A paradox.

Take it

As it is.

Pick up

All the fruit

That you can,

From the tree,

And enjoy

All the time

That you have.

Love,

Sing,

And smile.
