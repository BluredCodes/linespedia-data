---
title: "I  Love  You"
writer: "Arif Khudairi"
slug: "i-love-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/6709-I--Love--You-by-Arif-Khudairi"

I love you
For your eyes
Make me
Love the seas,
The rivers,
And the skies,
And dream
About you
All the time.
I love you
For your lips
Make me
Love the roses,
The flowers,
And the tulips,
And fly
In the sky
As a drunken
Butterfly.
I love you
For your smile
Makes me
Love the sun,
The moon,
And the stars,
And wait
For the dawn
Every day
In delight.
I love you
For you
Make me
Love the birds,
The streams,
And the trees,
And swim
In a river
Made of musk,
Of charm,
And dream.
