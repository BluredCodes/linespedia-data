---
title: "When Peace Comes Back"
writer: "Arif Khudairi"
slug: "when-peace-comes-back"

When peace
Comes back
To our life,
When smile
Returns
To our lips,
When sun
Rises up,
One more time,
And the clouds
Of sorrow
Disappear,
When birds
Fly freely
In the skies,
Without fear,
When spring
Puts back
The green leaves
Upon the trees,
When flowers
Put on
Rainbow colors,
And wear
Their sweet scent,
When love runs
Like rivers
In our hearts,
When children
Are hungry
No more,
When they are
Happy,hopeful,
And secure,
When soldiers
Come back home,
Throw their weapons
Into the seas,
And sing
The song 
Of peace,
When our life
Is filled
With beauty,
With truth,
With goodwill,
When our dreams
Are fulfilled,
I will sing
My love songs
For the birds,
For the flowers,
And for you.
