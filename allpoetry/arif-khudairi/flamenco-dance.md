Dance!Dance!Never stop.You were bornFor your hot,Flamenco danceWith style,With charm,With delight,In the lightOf the stars,Or the rays Of the sun,Dance!Dance!Never stop.This nightIs for us,Here with you.Stretch the neckOf the swan,Raise your arm,Lift your head,Turn to the right,Twist to the left,Hit the groundWith your foot,To the tuneOf the guitar,Do your elegantMerciless moves,Sway your body,With vigor,With delight,With content,Throw your sharp,Provoking looks,Send the fireTo the chests,With your wideSpanish eyes,With those round,Golden earringsMade of sun,With your longnighty waves,Falling overYour soft,Milky shoulders,With your full,Naked breasts,Whose tiny,Brown nipplesLook likeWild grapesUnderneathYour silky,Red blouse,With lips,And cheeks,As redAs the tulips.Dance!Dance!The Andalusians,With glittering Burning eyes,Clap their handsIn delight.Dance!Dance!Wave your hands,Shake your hips,Never stopYour erotic,Gypsy dance.You were bornFor flamenco,And the nightIs for us,Here with you.
