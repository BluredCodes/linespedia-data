---
title: "When You Were In Love With Me"
writer: "Arif Khudairi"

Yesterday,
When you were
In love
With me,
Everything
Looked different
In my eyes.
Life looked 
As beautiful
As paradise.
At day,
Sun looked
Even brighter,
And the moon
More tender,
At night.
I picked
Up the stars
With my hand.
I touched
The clouds
In the skies.
And the dawn
Always beamed
In delight
Like the beam
In your eyes,
And the roses
Looked like
The color
In your cheeks.
Yesterday,
When you were
In love
With me,
Everything
Looked beautiful
In my eyes,
And then,
Everything
Looked different
From now.
But my love
For you
Is still
As fresh,
As genuine,
As strong
As it was
Yesterday.
