i was chillin at my crib with my homies
when this one hoe strolled in
her name was shaniquewah 
and she was lookin for 8-ball
she was looking for some hook-up
8-ball said yo yo yo hoe
we have no hook-up
so we no hook-up dealers
so go home hoe
