title: "Kids are Cruel"

They say kids can be so cruel,
what about adults
the ones that made the rule

They seem to be
just as mean,
maybe you can't see.

You're the ones that started it,
you said the words
you threw the first hit.

If the kids learn from you
and you start the words 
what are they supposed to do.

If you can't get along
how come the kids 
have to sing this happy song.

Maybe if you would change your awful ways,
Kids wouldn't reuion future days.
