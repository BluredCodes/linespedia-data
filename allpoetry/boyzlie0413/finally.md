I knew you would come
and here you are
I have waited so long
yet haven't gotten so far

you treat me like a princess
like you're in love with me
I love it when we're together
yet I wish I could make you see

please don't leave
don't break my heart
you say you're differnt
you have from the start

I'm not sure if I should trust you
you seem so sincere
but what if it turns out like the rest
and I'm am forced to shed a tear

I think we should be together
and I want this to be real
but it's hard for me to let you in
and it's hard for me to feel

maybe I will eventually come around
if we give it enough time
I just want to be happy 
but I also want you to be mine
