why do we always fight?
why do I always cry?

why can't we get along? 
why can't you love me?

why did you say that?
why do you want to hate me?

I want to be with you,
you want to hit me. 

I want to love you, 
you want to hate me.

but you can't hate me, 
you can't hit me, 
because deep down you do love me.
