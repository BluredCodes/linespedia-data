what are you thinking?

are you thinking about me,
or about another girl?

maybe you're thinking
about how much you like me.
maybe about how much you like her.

do you like me, 
or someone else?

i wish i knew what you were thinking,
then i would know what to do.

do i talk to you 
or should i sit in silence.

if you like me 
then i should talk,
if not i should say nothing.

so i sit here and wonder 
what do i do.

i figure i have nothing to lose, 
so i ask you,
'what are you thinking'

you're only reply,
'i was wondering what was for lunch'
