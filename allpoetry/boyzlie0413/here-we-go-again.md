One more time,
here we go again.

More people fighting 
more people dying.

no one really knows why
why we can't just get along
why we can't live in peace.

innocent people must die 
just so someone can prove there point
what is the world comeing to,
and when will it stop? 

my answer is never 
because everyday, and with 
every new act of violence
we teach the kids to do the same
they fight, they refuse to live in peace 

the children are scared to go to school
the adults, scared to go to work.

scared to live,
here we go again.
