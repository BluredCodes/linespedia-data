title: "I miss him..."

Why do I miss him all the time,
I want him so bad,
but he's already mine.

We hardly talk anymore
It's like we're not even together.
My heart is so sore,
'Cause he promised me forever.

I want it to last 
but I don't think it can.
There's just no way 
he can still be my man.

We never get to see each other.
It might not be his fault.
I just want us to be together,
this is tearing me apart.

I don't want it to be over
but I'm afraid it is.
Now I can move on 
and try to get over this.

I'll find a new man
to take your place
one that will love me for me 
not just for my pretty face.

I'll miss you at first 
and I hope you'll miss me too 
but eventually I'll get over you.
