how could you?
you turned on me,
you said you loved me.
now I know it was a lie.
if I could take back 
the things I did and said,
I would in a heartbeat.
I gave you a peice of my heart,
of my soul.
I gave you something 
I can never give again,
and I can never get it back.
I trusted you. 
I loved you.
you used me, 
for something so wrong.
why did you do it?
so you could brag to your friends.
you are such an asshole.
I hate you now. 
you shattered my heart.
you ruined it for me.
you mean nothing to me now.
