title: "why can't you see"
writer: "boyzlie0413"
slug: "why-cant-you-see"
source: "AllPoetry"

I get these thoughts in my mind, I'm beginning to think it's a bad sign. 

All I do is think about you, people are starting to notice it, too. I 

talk about you and all that things you do. I see you everyday, yet I can

never find the words to say. I get so nervous around you yet I still 

want you to stay. You always say the right words, although they hurt me

like swords. You always pick on me and it hurts, I try to hide it, I dont 

want you to see. Everyone says I look good but you must not think they 

should. I try to be pretty for you and this is what you do. Everyday, 

I pray, I want you to look at me, why can't you see.
