---
title: "I am over you"
writer: "boyzlie0413"
slug: "i-am-over-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I am over you,
and you're over me too.
I don't love you anymore,
because my heart is still sore.

I have someone new,
I just always think of you.
I'm not jealous of that girl,
it's just when I saw her my heart tore.

I can find someone better than you,
I just don't heal like you do.

I am over you.
