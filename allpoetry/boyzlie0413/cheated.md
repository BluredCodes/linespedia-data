you cheated on me.
did you think I wouldn't see?
you thought I wouldn't find out.
did you assume all I would do is pout?
you think you are so good at this evil game.
you can't even imagine I would ever do the same.
I can tell you think I am weak,
and can't live without out you because I am so meek.
but I have news for you,
I have someone else to.
