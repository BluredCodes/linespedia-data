---
title: "I hope you know"
writer: "boyzlie0413"
slug: "i-hope-you-know"

I want to do it 
I want to pull that trigger 
I want to end my life 
a bullet right through my flesh and bone
straight to my brain
for if I do survive I will be so far gone 
that there will be no reason to live
what am I saying, I have no reason to live now
not a soul in the world cares 
would you even notice if I was gone
if I just left this world, would you care
I don't think you would 
I think you would go on like nothing happened
as if I had never existed.
When in reality just as you were sleeping
in your perfect home with your perfect life
I was putting a bullet through my brain
and all because of what you said
all becasue of you.
as I lay there and die I hope you realize that.
as my family griefs I hope you know that.
as you keep living your perfect life I hope you understand that.
