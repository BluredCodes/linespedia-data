will you shut up already. im tired of hearing about it. i didnt do it. it
wasnt me. so move on. stop talking about it.  cant you get the hell away 
from me. im trying to aviod you. yet there you are again.talking to me like
i want to be your friend. i dont. you screwed up you always do.why do you 
have to be like this. i have done nothing to you. you're a bitch thats why.
you cheat on your boyfriend. and i am supposed to cover for you. then i do 
and you turn around and dump him. what do you think he thinks about me now.
well i dont care. ive given up. moved on. im tired of everyone. all these 
people are assholes. they wont leave me alone. they all have turned 
against me so i am now turning against them. no one understands.
all they do is talk about you behind your back. i have no friends.  
i have no one. fuck the world.
