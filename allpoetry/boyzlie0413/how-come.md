How come I loved you,
and thought you loved me.

How come you said that,
and thought I wouldn't hear.

How come you did that, 
and thought I wouldn't find out.

How come you used me,
and thought it would work out.

How come you hurt me,
and thought you'd get away with it.

How come you said we'd still be friends,
when you knew it could never be the same.
