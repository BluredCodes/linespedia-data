Everyday I sit here and wonder,
what it's like to be you,
through all the rain & thunder. 

I want to play your game,
I want to be the popular one,
with all the friends & the fame.

You don't understand,
how differnt we are,
you have a man, a life, and a car.

Me I don't have a soul,
my life can't compare, 
or even pay toll.

I am the one no one sees,
as I sit here and dream, 
about how my life is ripping at the seams.

I just want it to go away,
I want what you have, 
and I want it to stay.

But I am the loner, 
I can't be like you, 
I can't have the things, you do. 

I like being alone,
and keeping to myself,
in my own little zone.

There is only one thing I don't understand,
why am I so lonely,
maybe I just need a man.
