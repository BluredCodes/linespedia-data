---
title: "*the words you say*"
writer: "boyzlie0413"
slug: "the-words-you-say"

The words you say, 
they hurt more everyday.
You act as though you don't even know, 
that you're the reason my self esteem is so low.
You tell me not to take it so bad,
but I can't help it, you make me so mad!

Every word you say,
makes me change my ways.
It's because of you I refuse to eat,
and dislike any treat.
I wear makeup to look good, 
even though some don't think I should.

I try not to give you anything to laugh at,
then all you do is say my smile is to fat. 
So now I don't smile
maybe you'll pick on someone else for a while.
