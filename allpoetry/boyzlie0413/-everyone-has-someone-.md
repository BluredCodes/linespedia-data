They say everyone has someone
and one day you'll find him,
just be patient.

My question is how long?
How long is being patient?
And how long is being desperate?

Because it seems like 
everyone does have someone,
but me.

I'm the only one, 
without that special someone.
So how come they don't have to be patient?

When I think I have found him
it turns out I was wrong,
he's really someone elses.

Then all I hear
is 'there's more fish in the sea,
you can find someone better.'

I realize I'm young
and I have a long time to look
but back to my question,
Why doesn't everyone else have to wait?
