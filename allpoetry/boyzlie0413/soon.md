title: "soon"
writer: "boyzlie0413"

one day you love me
the next, you love her.
what should I think?
you toy with my heart.
you act as though you want me'
and just when I think it's true,
I see you with her.

I know you don't see it,
and I would never tell you;
but it hurts,
to see you with her.
to hear you say her name.
I know its over,
and will never be again, 
but it still tears me up inside.
I want you to love me, 
I want you to be mine, 
and only mine.
but I can see,
you like her now.
I dont see why
we are so different.
yet I wish I was like her
then you would like me again.
then I wonder 
what did you see in me,
anything???
you must have seen something.
yet, it seems you like nothing now.
have you changed that much? 
or is it a show,
for her.
I know the real you,
and I like it. 
so if it is a show,
I think you should be yourself.
then if she is in her right mind
she will see what I see,
and will then love you for you. 
in a way I want you to be happy
and if she will do that for you 
then I'm happy for you.
but still hurt and alone.
maybe it will go away,
I hope so.....soon.
