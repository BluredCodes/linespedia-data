When he says I love you 
does he really mean it,
or does he just say it 
because he knows that's
what I want to hear.

He says he loves me,
but he doesn't show me
I want to be loved but 
I don't feel loved.

So I wonder,
if it really is love or, not.

I don't know.

Maybe time will tell.

but if it is real love wouldn't
I already know it?
