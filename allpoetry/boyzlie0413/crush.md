I try so hard,
to get you to notice me.
I want you to see me.
I want you to like me,
everything about me.

I want you to be mine.
I want you to be with me.

You sit and laugh with your friends.
I sit alone and watch you.
Hoping that just for a second you might talk to me,
but no you keep walking, 
you never even look up. 
You and your friends are having a great time,
when im dying inside.

I have a crush on you,
I can't help it,
it just happened.
I want to be that girl, 
the one who always gets the guy.
but no, instead I just get to dream
and hope, wish and pray.
It never works out,
I always get hurt.
Maybe, that's why they call it a crush.
