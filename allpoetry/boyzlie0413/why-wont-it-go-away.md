title: "Why won't it go away?"

why won't it go away
the pain, i mean 
it hurts so bad
everytime i see you with her,
you and her look so happy
it makes me so sad
i want it to just go away
they tell me 'give it time'
well how much time
its been along time 
you've moved on,
why can't i 
i want to 
but i just can't
everytime i see you it gets worse
it's like you've stabbed the knife in me
and now you just keep on stabbing
one more time, over and over again.
it hurts, still,
the wounds have not healed 
i have not found someone new
and i don't think i can find someone better.
