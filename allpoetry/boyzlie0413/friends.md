I feel like my world is crashing down upon me
like I'm in this giant fog and I just cant see
If everything that happens has a reason 
then will you be my friend for just a season?
we have come so close yet we are still so far 
like I'm driving on this endless road in a beaten-up car
You are such a good friend that I will always love 
you are the one I can relate to, that other lonesome dove
but who's to say when you will fly away
You come into my life yet I can't tell if you will stay
you came just in the nick of time
I'm so glad, I was beginning to lose my mind
I want you to stay with me and be my friend
I need you to help me through, not just now but, till the end
so friend please don't leave me in my time of need
for my life is changing so much and at such a terrible speed
I need you now more then I would ever say
so just listen to me and be with me today
