half empty?

half full?

it depends did you take the time and pour half the glass?
or, did you simply drink half the glass?

for if it was poured half way it is half way full,
and I say work a little harder and you could get a whole glass.

if you drank half the glass it is half empty,
and I say you're half way to dyeing of thirst.

although, either way it's 50\% to mush glass.
