title: "*you're to blame*"

you did it 
it's your fault

you said you loved me,
you wanted us to be together.

you lied to me,
you cheated on me.

I forgave you,
I said we could put this behind us.

I wanted to move on,
I wanted to still be friends.

You held on to something,
you wouldn't get over it.

You got mad at me,
you wouldn't let us get past this.

I gave up,
I wasn't going to put up with you.

You hurt me,
I am the one who suffered.
