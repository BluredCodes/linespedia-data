title: "bullet"
writer: "Dilionn"

yeah, I see you
like some lothario
whispering promises
telling me you'll make it all better
if I'll just trust you
you'll give me peace
release from all this bullshit
but the brass gives you away
I know you're selfish
I know you lie
you think you'll talk me into it
get me to smile as I lock and load
I'm not falling for it
not today
