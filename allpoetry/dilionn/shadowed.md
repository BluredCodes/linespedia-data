title: "shadowed"

night terrors
in waking 
hand of shadow
grips my ankle
ball and chain
boogeyman of my own creation
this hill is too steep
hauling that weight
mule for discontent
climbing toward the sun
etching dim pictures
in my millstone 
to pass the time
