title: "Marionette (some strong language)"
writer: "Dilionn"

I am a marionette
the puppeteer's dead
I hang inert
strings tangled
over the shadowy rafter
hollow, wooden
eyes to the hardwood
quiet stage
dust settling in my
painted hair
I long for someone
to twitch my strings
I want to dance
I want to fight, fuck, spit and laugh
rip the world a new asshole
I want to sing, cry
exult in simply living
one day 
when the worm-eaten stage
collapses into dust
and wind whistles through
shattered walls
some lost tired soul
will find this battered
broken thing
mend it with careworn hands
and give it life
once more
