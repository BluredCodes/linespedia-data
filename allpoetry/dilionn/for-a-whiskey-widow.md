---
title: "for a whiskey widow"
writer: "Dilionn"
slug: "for-a-whiskey-widow"

you must have asked yourself a thousand times
what you saw in that angry man from mizzou
rawboned and never weaned, sucking death 
from a glassine teat, raging vengeance 
for some long-forgotten childhood slight
forever beyond his reach...but he held you close
held you by the throat, justified your black eyes
by telling tales of woe to rotgut sympathizers
feeding your children despair...long-suffering,
you survived the scars he carved with blind fists
helpless with horror, damaged children never put right
a woman tortured but still alive, that long-ago beauty
smiling from yellowed paper, not yet broken
sometimes I see her in your eyesThis is for and about my Grandmother, who survived 30 years of spousal abuse.
