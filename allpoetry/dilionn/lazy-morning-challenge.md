I wake 
open eyes and 
spy you 
a sleepy smile 
reach out 
stroke your 
cream-soft cheek 
entwine wandering fingers 
in your liquid-gold hair 
you turn your back 
against my chest 
my arms enfold you 
your hand like 
tiny china 
wrapped in mine 
let my thumb brush 
idly against your hand 
and drift away
