when she left
I climbed into 
this little box
closed the lid
blotted out the sky
so I wouldn't see
her face in the stars
in glittering twilight
hear her voice 
birdsong on an ocean breeze
feel her lips
as unwelcome sunlight
brushed my cheek
just for a little while
safe and warm
dark and protected
trapped in a womb
created for myself
the darkness is so complete
sense of time
sense of direction
gone
can't recall the way
escape forgotten
this little box
will be my tomb
