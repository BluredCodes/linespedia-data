title: "baisers de fantôme"
writer: "Dilionn"

my orphan heart
lives inside you
it will not come home
until you rest
in my arms
whispering, it will 
haunt you
with gentle sighs
speaking in daydreams
until you rest safe
in arms that tremble
incredulous at the touch
of such beauty
I would never sleep
if I could not 
feel you
like a ghost 
kissing me goodnightFor Tina, who revived me from a long death.
