---
title: "ode to a long-ago home"
writer: "Dilionn"
slug: "ode-to-a-long-ago-home"
source: "AllPoetry"

break the window
slip catlike 
into haunted halls
desperate domicile
for radicals
runaways, rebels
simple fools
wry jokes 
creep past the skeletons
empty iron walls
swirling scent of
grave dust
ancient floorboards
adolescent frustration
climb into shadowy warmth
poor man's penthouse
watch shadows dance
metamorphose
glassy fevered eyes
unutterable longing
a tissue-paper kiss
