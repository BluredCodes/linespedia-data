---
title: "stockholm syndrome"
writer: "Dilionn"
slug: "stockholm-syndrome"
source: "AllPoetry"

she sits quiet
waiting for him to
come back to her prison
throw her a crust of sex
a cup of cruelty
at least it's attention
he tells her it's love
and she starts to believe him
thinks she's fucking up
and he only hurts 
to show her the way
so she learns how to 
do things the "right way"
his way...
he says he's only playing
when he throws her to the floor
when she cries he says 
baby I'm sorry
baby I love you
and she begins to believe 
love is pain
apologizes for his cruelty
takes it on herself like
a battered Christ
shrouded in tear-stained 
wedding-white chiffon
absolves his sin
loves her tormentor
even as he hammers
nails through her wrists
she is his prime defender
as he strips her self away
like birch bark
