snowblind
living in a crystalline haze 
reflections of reflections 
your smile in the 
corner of my eye
elusive but always
always there
keeps me sane

icy wind howling
through my straw limbs
I feel your
whiskey-fire warmth
spreading from my chest
gentle flame licks over me
around me, through me
consumes and protects me

in the darkness 
I was always lost, but
with your hand in mine
I fear no evil
your laughing eyes
light the way 
to rebirth...
the springtime of my soulFor Tina...I don't know if I like this one, I hope you do.
