title: "seraph"
writer: "Dilionn"
slug: "seraph"

shattered angel in a cage
wings bruised so many times
she fears the very thought
of spreading them wide to take flight
tear-swollen eyes blind her
she can't see her walls are glass
I stand outside, throwing spark plugs
cracks craze the surface
still the wall holds, tentatively
if she stood, and simply tapped
the tinkling sound of freedom
would ring in her ears
trimmed wings would fledge
grow strong and open
and she would fly away
laughing like a tiny bell
