---
title: "daydream sunset"
writer: "Dilionn"
slug: "daydream-sunset"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/51060-daydream-sunset-by-Dilionn"
---

come with me
let me enfold your fragile hand
lead you along
white sand under conch-pink sky
chase the surf back into the sea
run, laughing, from its counterattack
fall softly into the soft beach bed

kiss me
let me tangle my fingers in your
liquid-amber tresses
tongues dancing gently
salt on your lips, the taste of you
filling my senses like the rushing sea
wavelets tickle our feet, we lie
contented and unafraid

night falls
picking up driftwood as you chase me
through the white-noise blackness
sit by the newly-built fire
your head resting against my chest
firelight flickers in your gem-like eyes
we taste the wine of contentment
fall asleep under inky star-smeared blankets
holding fast to real lovefor Tina, who never ceases to inspire me.
