Even after all this time
these things I remember
entering our room
finding it dark 
save for a single sunbeam
falling across your body
stark contrast
against the black sheet
even now I can smell 
grapefruit perfume
hear the small cry 
when I pull your hair
feel your pulse against my lips
in the hollow of your throat
I remember laughing like children
entwined in sweaty sheets
the way your breasts looked
like vanilla ice cream 
with a perfect strawberry on top
I remember entering you
the way my world seemed to end
each and every time
and begin anew
slender legs shockingly strong
holding me close with a force
neither of us knew you had
your lips like pillows 
made of rose petals
melting into mine
you never knew 
how amazed I was
every single time
the look of surprise
on that impossibly lovely face
as we tumbled over the edge 
always made me feel
