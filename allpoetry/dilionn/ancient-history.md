if I'd known
noticed the obvious
transparently fatal 
decisions, actions, words
maybe...
	maybe...
		too late.
time is a treadmill
can't walk backward 
cure the wounds
already inflicted
unravel a tapestry of regret
each passing moment
an iridescent thread
woven into years
in the end
we are nothing
but our memories
