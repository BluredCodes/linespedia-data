---
title: "Anastasia"
writer: "Dilionn"
slug: "anastasia"

In that crowded hall
it sounds so cliche 
but the glitter
in your smoky eyes
made me forget everything
nights spent laughing
crying, cutting
eating dreams and drinking despair
I will never forget
when you disappeared
just for a moment...
but I was so afraid
peering through the blinding rain
cigarette smoke stinging
in my eyes when
suddenly I saw you
in that globe of light
stark against the night
you looked like Ingrid Bergman
waiting for Bogey
your rain-soaked hair
clinging to your pale cheek
your eyes wide to see the night
your laughter like a carillon 
as I ran to you
I couldn't breathe 
without you near
as I caught you in my arms
breathed your lips, your hair, your skin
I could see us there
under the streetlight
kissing in the rain 
like black and white 
movie romance
