---
title: "Being?"
writer: "Dilionn"
slug: "being"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

it began s l o w l y
self-protection gone awry
let hope fall away
beauty is pain
trust nothing
salt the earth
until love can't live
pain and fear and joy
compressed into dull 
grey vacuum
sometimes
I wish I'd bleed
FEEL something again
black hole existence
collapsed on itself
infinite gravity
falling forever
into a sea of self
far above
I can still see
a glimmer, nothing more
half-light wavering
fainter with each passing moment
deeper into the abyss
each day I wonder
will I ever surface
again?
