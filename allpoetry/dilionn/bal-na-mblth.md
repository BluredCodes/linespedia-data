heartbeat thrumming
silent flutter
lashes brush tender
moon-pale flesh
oceanic rhythm
rosewater blood
infrared telegraph
whispers direction
guides me, trembling
to the mouth of flowersOk, this is a piece I feel weird about.  It's...barely-rotic in any traditional sense but close enough I felt it best to mark it as such.  The title (and inspiration for the concept) is a place-name in Ireland which I think is the most poetic in the world..."The Mouth of Flowers".
