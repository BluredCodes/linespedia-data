tonight we spoke
after decades
or was it days?
breathless
addlebrained
stunned beyond words
so much I planned to say
my clumsy words 
trampling one another 
emotional stampede
both trembling with
ecstatic love-panic
your soft words 
ensorcel me
making me flush
gasp for air
laugh out loud
when you said
"I love you"
I caught the sound
laid it gently down
in the safest part of me--
--on ice-age insomniac nights
I will draw out
those three 
        little 
           words
spoken softly
in your precious voice
sleep safe
and dream of youPour T...votre voix m'enchante
