title: "morning"
writer: "Dilionn"

I sleep
and in dreams you come
you dance with me 
I breathe you in
absorb you into me
I hold your body 
you cradle my spirit
like a hummingbird
but when I exhale
eyes unwilling
flutter open
I watch, helpless 
as you fade into mist
real but incorporeal
I wait, until the
warmth of your body
fades from my arms
and I waken fully
longingFor T...je vous ai donné mon coeur...traitez-le doucement, svp.
