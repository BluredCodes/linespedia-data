title: "walking away"
writer: "Dilionn"

saying goodbye 
long decade 
heartbreak and high 
every emotion relived 
as I sift through 
detritus of a life wasted 
memories shoved into 
black plastic bags 
praying the future 
isn't another illusion 
letting go 
as I climb onto 
silver bullet 
bound for 
))parts unknown(( 
through crushing waves 
cold unknowing sea 
hope floats, and 
I cling to it 
driftwood to my 
sailor, spluttering and 
shipwrecked 
eyes to the horizon 
searching for green 
salvation
