---
title: "my heart a home"
writer: "Dilionn"
slug: "my-heart-a-home"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/51059-my-heart-a-home-by-Dilionn"
---

she lives inside me
setting up house
under my leaky roof
inside the scarred walls
of a battered heart
touching me only in dreams
longing beyond comprehension
mine, in all the ways that matter
I'm stupid sometimes
let insignificant others
make me insane
I look at her picture
though I hardly need to
her face is etched in my mind
her lips like petals 
of an undiscovered flower
beautiful beyond words
somehow seeing her with my eyes
soothes me in a way
seeing in my mind's eye can't
her laughing eyes bring me peace
serenity in the depths of her soul
floods into me, a warm deluge
a new smile never discovered
until she set up house
in the long-empty rooms
of my heartfor my lovely Tina, whose eyes reflect the beauty of her soul
