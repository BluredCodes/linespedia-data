at -last-
longed-for touch
we are set alight with 
phoenix flame
reborn in the flesh
our eyes meet
love-warmth 
swirled passion-spark
a sly smile, you
lean your head back
I taste desire
on your lush lips
drink passion
let it warm me
like slow magma 
t r i c k l i n g 
throughout me, I
lay you down gently
hands trembling
lazily tracing the 
map of your skin 
s  l  o  w  l  y
my lips brush
delicate satin throat
pulse points
send morse-code messages
through my lips
throughout my body
my mouth wanders
over rose-petal flesh
fireflower kisses 
marking the paths of desire
pausing...
my tongue encircles 
taut pink nipple
teases with a flick
a slow
soft
sssuck
moving...
my lips blaze
a heated road
climb lightly over
gently curving hip
warm inner thigh
a soft kiss...
a tiny bite hints, and
lithe legs part
I drink liquid heat
nectar electrifying
burning and quenching at once
white-hot flames 
crackle through me
I roll and pull
your body covering mine
your mouth to mine
your heart in mine
your gentle hands
pin down my strong shoulders
my rough hands glide 
gently over smooth soft skin
coming lightly to rest
on hips that promise delight
you slide back...
and down...
full breasts brushing
my trembling chest
white heat fills you
a high-voltage thrill
arcs through us
and we are one
at -last-
your flesh melts into mine
blending...a singularity
your cries 
echo inside our kiss
you bite my lip, arching 
into sweet hot flash
we are enflamed--like a nuclear blast
we are consumed--burned to ash
we are reborn--phoenix unifiedFor Tina...as always my inspiration.  With love...
