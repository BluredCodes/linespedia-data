i don't understand
what kind of person
takes net in hand
to "collect" a butterfly
only a corrupt soul
could abduct
bright-winged 
living treasure
press it in a book
metamorphosed, flattened
into a lifeless trophymaybe it's just a poem about a dead bug.  maybe it ain't.
