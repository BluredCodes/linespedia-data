---
title: "good night, baby sister"
writer: "Dilionn"
slug: "good-night-baby-sister"
source: "AllPoetry"

good night
my baby sister
straw-haired 
angel, never 
got a chance 
to do wrong
learn life
beyond a little town
little boyfriend
little time you had 
nature cannot abide 
one so pure in heart
if not in deed

good night
my baby sister
taken by that thief
who stole your voice
made you someone else
made you terrify me
betrayed by the one
who should have saved you
vanished, the only trace
long-dead flowers
leaning against dead stone

good night
my baby sister
abandoned by the one 
who loved you most
who could not lose you
and threw you away
i was a coward 
and a fool
let you cry
because it was easier
than listening 
to your death
change you

good night
my baby sister
15 years past
yet to numb the pain
i failed so completely
a brother's duty
should have done
something
anything
kicked ass 
and taken names

good night
my baby sister
before you were taken
and i lay you down
your face so different 
against white satin
in your wooden bed
i forgot to tell you

your big brother loves youdedicated to my sister, Erika Rose...she would have been 29 today.  I hope she forgives me.
