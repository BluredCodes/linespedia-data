tee vee preacher
spins lies to his fold
sleazy simoniac 
screaming about ssssssssssssssin
pounds his Book
autotheistic avatar
demanding tithes
to his personal God
hollow promises of
prayers for remission of
illness, dementia and sin
follow him to Zion, children
this presbyter in polyester
his faithful flock
pure in desire to 
touch the face of God
pour fruits of life's work
into his gullet
again the moneychangers 
have taken over the temple
where is the Messiah now?Um...wow, a lot of comment on this one.  First of all I want to make it clear that this is NOT an attack on Christianity or on anyone's faith.  That is one reason that I have included biblical references...the other is that they felt right.  This IS, however, an attack on the sleazy bastards who use the human desire for spiritual connection to make themselves rich on empty promises and outright lies.

Now...I also want to provide definitions for some of the more unusual words I've used here.

Simoniac:  A person who takes money to "intercede with God" on the "customer"'s behalf.  A practice often said to have ended centuries ago.  Turn on your TV and see what YOU think.

Presbyter...essentially a synonym for priest or minister.  I just thought it sounded good.
