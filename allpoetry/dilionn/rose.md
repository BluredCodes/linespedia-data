title: "Rose"
writer: "Dilionn"

I remember 
when we were young
love-blind 
never let  
little things like
two years
two thousand miles
incommunicado
stop us
do you still remember?
I called you
payphone on the 'Ave
freshly arrived from
transcontinental hitch
riding hard with
methamphetamine cowboys
pushing diesel quicksilver
down night's gullet
lonely sons returning
crazy asian man
waves his gun at me
afraid he'll be shot
your voice over the line
half-drowned in
alternative humanity
when I heard your voice 
I knew you still loved me
and cried
