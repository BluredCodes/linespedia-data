last night in my
long time home
staring at snow
tv has deserted me
listen to old songs
drink cheap beer
perforated with my 
lucky lager
church key
pretending ten years 
never happened
daydreaming about being 
young and stupid and 
fucking free
wishing for cops 
to come bust up my 
one man party
make me feel 
like a rebel again
it beats the truth
starting over
ten years too late
heading off into 
some kind of dream
praying to bacchus and jehovah
simultaneously
it seems experience
is more than the
sum of its parts
because this time
it's just not the same
kabuki girl and catalina 
failing utterly
to make me feel alive
listen to hope and think of you
my only hope is
down a long road
and I pray again
give me my dream
