sitting here twitching
chomping on orange
flavored but not masked
cigarette-butt gum
hands like puppies
roaming over everything
desperate for ACTION
maybe just 
cut my own eyes out 
with a plastic knife
cough up lung butter
damn it why can't I breathe?
isn't this allegedly...
good for me?
I think I can stay sane
don't think about it, just
keep BEATING my HEAD into the KEYS
