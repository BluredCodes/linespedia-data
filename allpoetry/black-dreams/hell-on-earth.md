Hell on Earth

I went through hell
one night
I cried for hours
No one was there
I had no shoulder to cry on 
No one there beside me
I was alone
With a soon tear soaked blanket
And an old teddy bear
To help me through 
Another night of hell on earth
Music brought the tears
Words brought more
I cried for hours one night
And no one was there to care
To lift me
And cheer me up
To say the least
I felt like shit
SO i wrote a poem
One in which you are reading
Sleep never came
It was replaces by endless hours of tears
My hell on earth

~black dreams~
