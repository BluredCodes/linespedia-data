It Hurts

Ive never known
to be played before
it hurts
it kills
all feelings are dead now
gone
destroyed
no one can help now
its over
there is now only 
empty space
filled with dark memories
and rumors
rumors fly
every day
most are ignored
left alone
but some stick in peoples memories
they realize 
this might be true
so it travels further
sometimes changed
to be even worse
i cant help 
what is said 
but i can choose
either to believe
or not

~black dreams~
