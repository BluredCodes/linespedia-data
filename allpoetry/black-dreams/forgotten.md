FORGOTTEN

I don’t know how to feel
I’m lost, 
forgotten.
no one seems to care anymore
they just take one passing glance 
then keep on walking
My life has turned so dark
so dreary.
No one seems to care
where I am 
or where Im going.
It’s a living hell.this is my first time writing... tell me what you think - truthfully. i need some criticism in this stage of my writing.
