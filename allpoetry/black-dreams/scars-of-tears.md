SCARS OF TEARS              

I need to cry
Tears of fury, weakness, frustration
Gone
Lost
People dont understand 
The way I feel
Day for day
Night for night
I cry
Tears stream down my face
Invisible scars of sadness mark my face
Until I cry no more
And no one cares
The scars continue
Until I have no face
The tears have taken over 
Blinding me
Killing me
Slowly
I blink
And live no more
