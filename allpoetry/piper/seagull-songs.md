***

Seagulls 
Flocking,

In search of sea prizes,
Along the shore they fly.
Hovering on reeds of wind
Aloft in the evening sky.

Angelic white they seem
Paddling the air with ease,
Looking for a tasty morsel,
Something drifing in the seas.

The birds dive and call, each
A different tune to finish.
They glide in dips and circles
Diving in to catch their fish.

Daylight wanes to a peach glow
While winds settle into a chill.
I sing along with the seagull songs
And wrap myself in blankets twilled.

Seagulls 
Resting.I love walking on the beach watching the life of the waves.
