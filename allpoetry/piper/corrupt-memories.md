.............

Pressed inside my album
Dry and withered up
My memories of you
Have become corrupt.

Thinking our love was real,
I saw only you.
But you had hidden plans,
Never to be true.

We fems are unworthy
In your egoed eyes,
To deserve loyalty,
Be your only prize.

To the very smart ones
Truth in this is plain,
Cheating on us at all,
No need to explain.

You're out of my dear life,
Just what I wanted.
Alone is better than
Uncaring flaunted.

So, bye, bye my blackbird
Fly yourself away.
You've lost the very best
Cheated luscious me, and will pay!

Because you will never find 
Anyone better, pray,
Than the one who fled, and
Found her own sweet single way!men!
