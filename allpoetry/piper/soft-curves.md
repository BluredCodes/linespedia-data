***

As I sat
Across 
From you,
You stood there 
Staring rivets 
With your eyes,

Probing 
Deeply 
Into me here
And there,

Gently touching
My hips, a knee,
My lips
With your fingertips,
Searching my lines like
An architect planning
Renovations...

Soft curves, 
You said,
And smiled.

I loved that.

Say it again...
