~*~  ~*~  ~*~

Sandpiper beaches
Scorching fragile Summer feet
Tiny bird steps hop 
and flee.
~ ~ ~ ~Fly away
Sandpiper sunset
Ocean coolness creeping up
Bird eggs in 
Seagrass baskets
Stay to enjoy the waves
And what they bring in.
Sandpiper heaven
Satisfying
Morning scents of fish 
Peeps flowing in the wind
Feather calm.sigh~I love the beach
