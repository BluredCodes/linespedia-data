~~~~~~~~

Painted angels 
in the sky

Maroon 

like old dried blood

~Winging~

to dark pathways
where they find

[[Reasons]]

but no causes...
pressed against
The great clock

Booming

it knells
dropping gravel
into the armpits of hell

Gnarled inside
Pride
Foretells it's doom.
