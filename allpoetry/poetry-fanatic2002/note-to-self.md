Maybe in time your f***ing attitude will change towards people that constantly keep screwing you over... Maybe you will realize that no matter what you do they will never change. Maybe at that point you will see that some people are moochers and pushovers and f***ing screw-ups. Maybe that’s when you’ll realize that not everyone is as nice and considerate as you like to think they are. And maybe about that time you’ll realize that you HAVE to stop trusting those kind of people. But that brings up another issue. You now have to actually learn who is that type of person. Now that’s a hard topic. How can u possibly judge someone on his or her first impression? You know yourself that first impressions aren’t everything. First impressions are what people use as excuses when they don’t like someone because of the type of person they are. So what’s a girl to do when she needs to consider whom she can trust? That one is easy… she gives up! She gives up and hardens her heart from everyone, even those whom she does trust at this moment, because who knows, they could screw her over some day too.2001,
