WATCH IT BURN, 
WATCH IT FADE,
 WIPE YOUR CHEEKS AND WATCH IT BURN.
 FEEL THE HEAT RISE TO YOUR TEMPLES. 
FEEL IT ENTER YOUR HEART, 
FEEL IT GO THROUGH YOUR VEINS. 
FEEL IT TAKE YOU OVER. 
FEEL IT TAKE YOU, 
STRANGLE YOU FOR ALL YOUR WORTH.
FEEL IT TAKEYOU FOR GRANTED 
AND TAKE THE LIFE FROM YOU (or what you wanted to believe was life). 
FEEL IT TEAR YOUR SOUL INTO, 
AND BURN YOUR VERY TOUGHTS. 
FEEL IT GO INTO YOUR DREAMS, 
LET IT SHATTER EVERY ONE, 
FEEL IT SEARCH YOUR SOUL. 
FEEL IT GO INTO EVERY PART OF YOU. 
FEEL IT KILL YOU, 
FEEL IT MURDER YOUR LOVED ONES. 
FEEL YOUR TEARS COMING FASTER AND FASTER. 
FEEL IT? 
DO YOU FEEL IT? 
WATCH IT DISTROY EVERYTHIG AROUND YOU, 
EVERYTHING THAT YOU WANTED TO CONSIDER YOURS. 
FEEL IT? 
I HOPE YOU DO. 
IT HURTS, 
IT REALLY DOES. 
DOES IT RIP AT YOU, 
TAUNT YOU AND SCARE YOU? 
DOES IT CAUSE YOU TO CRY SO HARD AT TIMES? 
DOES IT MAKE YOU WANT TO SCREAM INTO THE NIGHT? 
DOES IT MAE YOU FEEL LIKE YOU HAVE NO SELFWORTH? 
DOES IT? 
DOES IT TAUNT YOU? 
I HOPE IT DOES. 
DOES IT CAUSE YOU SO MUCH PAIN THAT YOU WANT TO SLEEP ENDLESSLY? 
DOES IT? 
DOES IT WANT YOU TO? 
DOES IT MAKE YOU AFRAID OF EVERYONE, 
OF EVERYTHING? 
DOES IT FEEL AS IF IT HAS COMPLETE CONTROL OVER YOU, AND OVER YOUR BEING? 
DOES IT? 
DOES IT FEEL LIKE YOU CAN’T GET RID OF IT? 
DOES IT FEEL LIKE YOU WILL NEVER ESCAPE IT, 
NO MATTER HOW LONG OR FAR YOU RUN? 
IS THAT WHAT IT FEELS LIKE? IS THAT IT? 
IS THAT HOW IT FEELS TO BE DROWNDED WITH FEAR 
TO THE POINT WHERE YOU CANT SERVIVE NO MATTER WHAT? 
I GUESS IT'S ABOUT TIME YOU LEARNED THAT.
