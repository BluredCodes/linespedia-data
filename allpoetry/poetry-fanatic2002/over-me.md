Your devilish eyes make me shiver, 
and my every hair stands on end. 
Your face is cold and lifeless, 
your hand are just the same. 
The smirk you wear upon your face, 
it sends my every nerve into shock. 
As you walk by a cold chill jolts my senses. 
The hiss in your dark voice 
it causes my every mussel to become tense. 
You try to tempt me into your world of sin. 
You can't figure out why I wont listen. 
My ears are depth to your words, 
and my eyes are blinded by your actions. 
Time after time you come back, 
Using my weakness as a pawn, 
You try to capture me.I resist you each time, 
But you never back down. 
You try to find a way to consume me. 
You never give up. 
But I keep pushing you away. 
You tried so hard to win me over, 
eventhough you know, 
you have no power over me!!!2001,
