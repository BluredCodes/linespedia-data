One of these days you’ll realize that lying to me, hurts both of us.

One of these days you’ll realize that you should have called when you said you were going too.

One of these days you’ll realize the pain you are causing me.

One of these days you’ll realize that these tears are real.

One of these days you’ll realize that you can’t cross bridges that you have  burned.

One of these days you’ll realize that you do mean everything to me.

One of these days you’ll realize that I cant fight my demons without you.

One of these days you’ll realize that I cant accept you in my life any longer.

One of these days you’ll come back, crying.

One of these days I’ll finally stop caring for you,
                   
Cause the Lord knows I’ll never stop loving you.
