Sometimes it appears that those who feel that they are really “in love” are just acting, as though they are performing for those around them. This makes me wonder is love is simply an emotion as opposed to a set state of mind. Is love simply lost and discarded as a bad mood? Or is that just simply the way people choose to use the word? Maybe that’s all it is, a word. A word like any other, such as dog, tables, or face. But then again those words have meanings.
