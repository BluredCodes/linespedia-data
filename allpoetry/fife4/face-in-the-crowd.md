title: "Face In The Crowd"
writer: "Fife4"

The light fades on another day
 and my eyes feel weighted.
 Did I change the world today?
 Anyone I loved? Anyone I hated?
 Reflections of the day echo in refrain.
 Nothing worth knowing.
 I felt no happiness, sorrow, anger or pain.
 None worth showing.
 Will this be my life?
 Am I doomed to live in wonder?
 When will I feel strife?
 Shout with the bursts of thunder?
 Change the world with this hand?
 Easier said than done.
 My life is just a grain of sand
 On the beach for all the None.
 I am now just a face.
 Just pointless in my being
 Look away from this place. 
 I am not worth seeing
