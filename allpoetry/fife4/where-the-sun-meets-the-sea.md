Did you know that we earthly creatures
                          Could behold such splendid features,
                          To see Godly creation
                          During sunset formation 
                          And we students could become the teachers.

                          Then why has no one presented
                          This theory so new invented?
                          No more wondering how
                          Or why we're here now
                          And hate could be forever prevented.

                          Could it be we have all been blind
                          To the beginnings of all mankind
                          Because no one has seen
                          The space in-between
                          Where the sun and sea intertwine?

                          So when the sun sets once more
                          Miles past the vast ocean shore
                          You look to the space
                          Where fire and water embrace
                          To the grandeur for you to explore.
