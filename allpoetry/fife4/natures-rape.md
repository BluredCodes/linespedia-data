Nature produces miracles.
Clouds dance across the sky.
Brisk air's fury;
and anger I cannot deny.

Blades of grass bend
at the wind's boastful command.
Flowers arise
out of the rich, lush land.

Can beauty continue
through our distrust?
Or will it fade 
as all beauty must?

Land is raped
by man's savage lies;
Leaving no anticipation
for the majestic sunrise.True opinions please. And check out my other poems!
