---
title: "Dance of Man and Nature"
writer: "Fife4"
slug: "dance-of-man-and-nature"
source: "AllPoetry"

There is a music in the wind,
A beat in the earth as it turns.
The rhythm to the water
and the fire as it burns.
We, as humans, ignore
The symphony of mother earth
All the time confined
to the illness of our birth.
We were made to move
Faster than the wind can blow.
And thus our two worlds
Can never live in quid pro quo.
The world spins one way
And we want it to change direction,
Oblivious to the fact,
We live in a harmonious connection.
So, as the wind whispers
And the fire burns in dance,
We will yearn to hear the music
If we only had the chance.
