I am keeper of the wind.
Guardian of the breeze.
I cause the grass to bend,
And trees to flail their leaves.
I draw pictures in the ocean 
And make sculptures in the sand.
The wind is my tenure.
It is mine to command.
And when the time comes
For God to let me die,
My soul shall ride the wind
Across the clear, blue sky.
I shall travel 'round the world
Try and reach for the moon.
I will be the hurricane,
Tornado and typhoon.
But until that time,
I shall stay on the earth
And I alone shall know 
The value of my worth.
