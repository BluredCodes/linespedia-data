Who is that ball on the bathroom floor?
Is that you, Susie May?
Your cheeks are soiled with dirt. 
Where have you been today?
Your hair is stained red
And your skin, black and blue.
Tell me, Susie May,
Where did he touch you?
Don't cover your face.
You have done nothing wrong.
You must show your smile to the devil
Show him that you're strong.
Can you even look at me?
Please. Don't turn away.
Look at the sky.
The sun is shining today.
I know you are lost
In your shame and your fear
But please do remember
I'm here.
