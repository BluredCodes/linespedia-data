title: "A Sinner's Death"

Come crashing rain.
Come flooding thunder.
Stop my pain!
Erase God's blunder.
He made me wrong.
I am not man.
My life's too long.
HURT AS ONLY I CAN!
My smile is grim.
My frown too bright.
I long to see Him.
The forever night.
Please don't leave.
Stay til I dream.
I choose to deceive.
We are not what we seem.
Blinded by night.
Lost in the day.
My soul takes flight.
I am fading away.Tell me what you think.
