If You were in my life
I'd give you everything
I'd always be there for you
I'd share with you anything.

If I could hold you for a lifetime
And keep you in my arms
I would hold you there forever
And save you from all harm.

I would treasure every moment
Spent there next to you
And then I would smile
Cause it'd seem like the thing to do.

I'd remember every move
And keep the memory with me
I'd treasure every smile
That I'd happen to see.

If you were in my life
I'd promise you my world
And then I'd kiss you softly
Just to let you know,
I'd always be your girl.All I'm asking for is a chance to give you everything you have ever told me you always wanted...friendship can be more, if we let it.  it's your call babe!
