---
title: "I Love Him"
writer: "Trisha K."

I love him so much
How can I make him see?
He should know by now,
He means the world to me!

I'd talk to him more often,
but he never seems to care.
Sometimes when I've needed him,
He wasn't always there.

I understand he has to work,
to keep us clothed and fed.
Each night I pray he comes home safe,
Before I go to bed.

He doesn't always see the world,
From my point of view.
He sees things from a higher level,
and to me it's all so new.

Over the years, we've drifted apart,
but when did all this drifting start?
I used to be his little girl,
Now we're in two separate worlds.

When I'm with him,
I don't know what to say.
How do I break the silence?
I have to find a way!

I want to talk to him,
but sometimes I don't even bother.
What else can I say?
I love him...he's my father!I love my daddy!
