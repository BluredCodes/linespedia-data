---
title: "What Might Have Been"
writer: "Trisha K."
slug: "what-might-have-been"

I saw you as you walked into the room,
I smiled, cause I didn't know what else to do.
You came over to talk, but not for long.
Then you took her to dance,
cause they were playin' your song.

Someone must have saw the hurt in my eyes,
as you walked away, hand in hand,
With the new love of your life.
Then they asked me how I felt, or if I even cared.
I looked at them and simply said,
"If it was anyone else, the pain I could have bared."

For some reason, you meant so much more to me,
then any guy before.
For so long, we were the best of friends,
but started to think of you as something more.

I wanted to be that girl,
the one you would hold,
the one you would hug,
the one you would run to
when things got tough.
But instead,
now, I was just barely a friend.

She's the one you think of,
when you hear a certain song.
She's the one who can make it better,
when it seems like everything is going wrong.
And when you close your eyes at night,
she's the last thing on your mind,
but did you know,
you're the last thing on mine?

She seems to be better in every way and more,
I don't even know what I bother trying for.
The only thing I ever wanted
was just one chance with you.
Then just when I thought I had the chance
to make my dream come true,
She came along and stole you away.
So, I guess I'll just have to find a way,
to move on with my life.

But, before I do,
There's one thing I would like to tell you...
No matter what, I hope happiness you find,
and maybe one day, when the time is right,
you will look back, and remember the past.
Then you will see what might have been,
If only you'd given me the chance.09-26-01,
