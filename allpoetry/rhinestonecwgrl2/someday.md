I like you, but I don't know why
Your words seem to have me hypnotized
I can talk to you night and day
And won't even miss a word you say
You make me smile
And you make me laugh
It all kinda gives me a hope in a way 
I guess you could say 
That maybe I'll find that guy 
The one that's ment for me....
Someday
