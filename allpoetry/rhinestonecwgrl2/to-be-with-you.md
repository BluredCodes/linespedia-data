title: "To Be With You"

When I'm sitting alone
I think about you
Your cute smile
Your pretty eyes
Your sweet personality
And I think about how you hold me in your arms
And I can smell the scent of your cologne
And hear the sound of your voice saying, "I love you"
How I wish I could again hear your voice
And I wish I could again be in your arms
You mean everything to me
And I wouldn't give up the love I have for you
Not for anything in the world
I just want to be with you2000,
