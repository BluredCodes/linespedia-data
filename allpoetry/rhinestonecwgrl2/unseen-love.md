I told you but you didn't listen
I tried to show you but it seemed as if you didn't see it
You didn't see it at all
Not one bit
I wish you would see
I wish you would see that I'm in love with you
The only way you will see is if you open your eyes and ears
You will probably never see but I only wish you would1998,
