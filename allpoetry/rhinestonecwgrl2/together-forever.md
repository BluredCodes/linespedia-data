When I looked upon his face
I thought I saw God's grace
We were in the very place
Where our hearts began to race
That's when I knew we'd be
Together forever
My life was filled with fate
But when I called on him I knew he'd never be late
Even though my mind was out of state
To him I was always great
That's when I knew we'd be
Together forever
When I failed to even give him a glance
He always gave me a second chance
When he heard our song he never failed to ask me for a dance
That's when I knew we'd be 
Together forever1998,
