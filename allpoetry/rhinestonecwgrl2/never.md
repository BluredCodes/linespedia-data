You tell her that you love her so, 
And that you'll never let her go.
She tells you that she loves you too, 
And that no one else will ever do.
You go out almost every night, 
And you rarely ever fight.
When she goes to see her favorite band, 
You're always with her hand in hand.
You two will say you were ment to be,
Others tell you, "maybe, we'll see".
She tells you lying is her pet peeve, 
And you tell her you will never leave.
Then one day you up and go, 
She thought you said you'd never go.
But you tell her you'll still be friends, 
Even to the very end!
