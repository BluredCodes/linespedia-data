A friend is someone you can tell things to;
Someone you can trust;
Someone to keep your secrets;
They're there when you need them the most.

A friend is someone who is always there for you;
If you're happy, 
Or if you're sad,
Or if you're in between.

Sometimes they may hurt you when they really try not to;
But they sincerely apologize, 
You forgive, 
And then you forget.

When you find those true blue friends,
Take them while you can,
They may be there for you,
Even to the end.
