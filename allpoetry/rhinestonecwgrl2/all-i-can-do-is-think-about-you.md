When I look at you my heart skips a beat
Everything surrounding me seems to disappear
All that is there is you
I just can't stop thinking about you
When I look at you
I see your gorgeous smile and eyes
When I hear the sound of your voice
My heart just melts
There are many times when all I do is think about you
And how I wish that I was yours and that you were mine
But I know I can't have you
I just wish you knew the way I felt about you
And wished that you felt the same way about me
All I can do is think about you1998,
