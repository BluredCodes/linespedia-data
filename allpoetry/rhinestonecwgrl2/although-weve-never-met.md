title: "Although We've Never Met"

Although we’ve never met,
It seems as though we’ve known each other for years;
You seem to know so much about me, and me about you.
Although we’ve never met,
You care about me, though I don’t always know why;
But I also care about you the same.
Although we’ve never met,
You can make me smile and laugh;
Even when I am sad and crying.
Although we’ve never met,
You listen to me;
No matter what my mood may be in or what I may say.
Although we’ve never met, 
To me you are one of the sweetest guys I know;
That makes you a special person to me.
Although we’ve never met,
You are one of the best friends I have;
And I like that about you.
