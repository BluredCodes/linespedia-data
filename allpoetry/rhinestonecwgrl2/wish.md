When I look into the dark night sky
I wish upon a bright star that you were mine
I wish that I could be with you
With your arms tightly surrounding me

When I look into the dark night sky
I wish upon a bright star that you were mine
Your eyes looking into mine
And mine looking into yours

When I look into the dark night sky
I wish upon a bright star that you were mine
Your voice wispering in my ear
All the words that I want to hear

When I look into the dark night sky
I wish upon a bright star that you were mine
Your soft lips against mine
We share a passionate kiss under the moonlight

When I look into the dark night sky
I wish upon a bright star that you were mine
Talking with you every night and day
Being together at any moment that we want

When I look into the dark night sky
I wish upon a bright star that you were mine
I know that all won't happen though
Unless your feelings change for me

When I look into the dark night sky
I wish upon a bright star that you were mine
Please just give me a chance
I wouldn't give any other guy even just a glance

When I look into the dark night sky
I wish upon a bright star that you were mine
Just say that you'll be mine 
I promise I won't let you down
