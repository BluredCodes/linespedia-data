It was that day
When I found my way
To the valley of loss
I was determined to go
And I thought….

Allah

I am a stranger overhere
I am a stranger overthere
I am a stranger everywhere
And I wish to stay here
In the valley of loss
Please let me stay
This is where I belong 
Nostalgia is my song

While talking to.Almighty Allah
I found lots of others
Sisters and brothers
Trying to find their way
Out of the valley of loss….

They were calling…..

Allah

What if we pray
What if we obey
Can we not stay
In this valley of loss

And there was no reply

I said…..
Nostalgia is a fate
No need for bargaining
No need for debate

But the pain is great
Replied all of them

We were born to be fun
Here is a desert
Everything is dim and yellow
No moon no sun
No single tree

I said yes….
But….
It is easy to be left alone
With memories and losses
It is peaceful overhere
It is safe ….It is lonely….
It is definitly sincere….

They replied….

You know nothing
You are only new
What you think of the valley
Is not true
It is hard it is lonely
It is full of pain
Try to pray
Try like us to obey
May be you can find the way

At this moment whispered
Alsheikh….. 
from beyond the doors
Of the valley of loss

Children of the pain
Strangers of the desert
Leave the stranger alone
You know nothing better than him
Let him have time on his own
You all need time on your own
You need to find your healing alone

Children of agony and despair

He said

Learn how to love yourselves
Learn how to love each others
Learn how to discover the gifts Allah gave you
Learn how to stop mourning over your pasts
Learn how to open your hearts to forgiveness
Learn how to honour yourselves
Learn how to believe in almighty Allah
Teach your hearts to be tender
Teach yourselves how to surrender

To find your way out and away
of the valley of loss

Healing is a lot of effort
Replied all of them

No way back to eden
Replied alsheikh

You all know that
No way back to eden
Through the desert you have first to go
Your spirits should first grow
So open your hearts 
And try to take the last chance
Try to believe in yourselves
In the mercy of Almighty Allah
Open your hearts to the angels of light
To the grace of insight
Sent to you from almighty Allah

And believe and believe
That Allah will always protect you
And will always love you

And believe and believe
That one day….
You will recognize your way
Out and away …..
from the valley of loss

Allah

Called the stranger
I am afraid
I am lost
I am extremely hurt
My heart is full of pain
Please …..
Let me stay
Till I find my way

Stranger of the desert
Whispered alsheikh

Do not ever worry
Open your heart to Allah
Believe…. my poor child….
Cause when you believe
The fear and the pain will fly away
And you my poor child will find the way…..

soumaya

*alsheikh is the man responsible for teaching religion in islam
