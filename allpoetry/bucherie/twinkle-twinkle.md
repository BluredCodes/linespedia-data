Twinkle twinkleLittle starHow I wonder Where you are*HabibiAre you nearAre you farHow I wonderWhere you areNow my dearIt is more clearAway from you Life is sadIn your armsLife is madWhat can I doOh cherieHow I love youI stay all nightCounting starsThinking of allThe sheherayarsI saw in youAll the sheherazadsI was for youOh habibiHow I miss youHow I longeFor your smileHow I missYour styleYour  heart so tender How I longeFor the dangerFor the riskFor the adventureHow I miss you cherieOh my sheherayar*If I can just find the starThat would tell me Where you areI would give herAll my wingsI would ask herTo go so farTo get you back to meTo my armsTo my realityMy sheherayarAlone without youI am just a fantasyA lonely dreamerAnother sheherazad*A princess …so sadI don’t know When will you comeOr where you areSo sheherayarI shall waitIn my fantasy landCaressed by The waves and the sandI shall wait habibiIn every fairytaleI shall waitOne day you come backAnd give me back my wingsAnd the kiss of lifeOh sheherayarLet me count the starsOne …two…three…Habibi keep looking for meUn …deus…trois…Tu es la raison que je suis toujours laFive..six …seven…OopsI missed a star…It might be your star…I have to start again…Wahed…etnein..talata…*Habibi…Tesbah ala khair…*Good night cherie….et bon nuit…Soumayahabibi=my love in arabictesbah ala khair=good night=bon nuitwahed..etnein..talata..=one.. two.. three = un.. deus.. troissheherayar & sheherazad r 2the 2 legendary characters of one thousand nights
