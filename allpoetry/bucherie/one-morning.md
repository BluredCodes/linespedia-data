One morning

A bonjour
A mental game
A laugh and a smile
What a blessing
So stimulating...so fresh...
so tender...So caressing
What a fresh breathe
What a rhythm what a style
When we met that day
I got off my way
To hear this bonjour
I was willing to say
Cherie pour toujours
How I love your bonjour
How I wish
To explore your land
How I wish
To see your home
How I wish
To come close
How I wish
To stay…..

One morning…..

Un adieu
My heart is full of pain
Back on the road
Same old stranger
Alone again
I vision my circles
Same old circles
My face is wet
Tear drops or the rain..!!
How I miss this bonjour
How I miss the smile
How I miss the fresh breathe
Of every morning
The rhythm and the style
What can I say
Nothing I can say
Alone in my sacred lonliness I guess
I should go back to stay.
May be… one morning… may be… one day…

soumaya
