The wild horse

Run wild horse
Run wilder and wilder

Set fire in my heart
Keep my spirit free
And never surrender

Run wild horse….
Run,…. explore,…
Love and wonder
Do not fear the pain
Do not fear your nature

And…
Dream of the gain
Of the sky , the mud 
The road, and the flowers
Dream of the rain
Keep your loud laughter

And run and run
Faster and faster

Invade the deprived
With dreams of love
Invade like thunder
Destroy their castles of fear
Break their walls
Drop their barrier

And run and run
Wilder and wilder

Believe in your pride
Do not ever hide

Keep your head raised
Keep your beauty praised

Open your heart…
Let go spontaneously….
And keep being tender

And…
Run and run
Wilder and wilder
Do not forget my dream
Do not ever surrender
And…
Keep setting the fire

Keep the passion
Keep the desire

Shine like diamonds
Be precious like rubies,
emeralds and sapphire

And….
Run and run 
Wilder and wilder

Teach me how to feel
Teach me how to be real
How to keep my glory
How to treasure my appeal

Teach me wild horse
Teach me how to believe 
Teach me how to be true
How to be free as you

Teach me wild horse
And keep teaching me
For my life to be brighter
For my dream to come true

Soumaya
