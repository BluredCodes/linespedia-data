Once upon a time...

A lion king met a kittie cat

They talked they laughed

They loved to chat

They walked across his forest

And visited her garden

The king was really nice and modest

And her roses were golden

Along their beautiful walks

Harmony started to exist

And no one could resist

And roses grew and grew

And hope and happiness flew

Not only in his forest

But also in her garden

Till one day….

Something has happened

The king decided 

He should go to his cave

He said kittie cat ….

I will leave you to my cave

Be good till I come back

Promiss me to play here

I will always be near

Overthere in my cave

So promiss to behave 

She gave him the promiss…

But…

Staying there alone

The cat got angry at him

She started to miaow

Miaow miaow

Come out my king

Grrrrr grrrrrrrr

Kittie cat I said behave

I need to stay alone

For a while in the cave

Miaow miaow

But I miss you my king

Grrrrrrrr grrrrrrr

I miss you too my dear

But I need to be here

I need to be alone …

In my wholly cave 

So try to have patience

Don’t act like women

Be quiet and behave

Miaow miaow

All right all right

I will try to behave

I will try to be brave

But…..

do you have to be away

Without even a good morning

Or hi or how do you do

Even every other day

I used to like our daily hello

Anyway….

Ok I will go…

I promiss my king

That I will try to understand

your moments of silence…..

I will try to obey

Miaow miaow….miaow…

But most of all days

Today I can’t obey

I won’t tell you I miss you

Cause that I am sure you know

But my king….

Please wait and listen..

what I really want to say

is ….

Happy valentine day….


soumaya
