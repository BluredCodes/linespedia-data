She says with a smile

I like what you say
I give you extra stars

How I used to love the stars

She says with a strange belief
I can heal myself
I can catch a relief

So hard what she says

Such faith

est vraiment dure

with your heart yet tender… and pure

tu peus pas echaper de l’amour
Tu connais l’amour

When it hits you deep down in the bottom
When your roses start to blossom
When you recognize the taste of love

You can say lots of things
But you can’t actually be brave
As she thinks….

You just turn blind
And the most painful is
You wish you stay blind
You wish you never have insight
You wish you never see the light
Cause the light hits painfully right here
In this sacred place hidden in your chest
This ever lost planet that is called the heart…
Cause it tells frankly that you should have been the one who have left
You should not have waited till being hurt

Yet…you see…

on peus rien faire
C’est l’amour
Et tu connais l’amour
So sweet…so tender
Et enfin tres dure

But you know I used to love stars
I shall try to earn her stars
And may be another trip to the moon…

soumaya
