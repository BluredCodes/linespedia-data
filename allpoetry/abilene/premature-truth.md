title: "Premature Truth"

He's over the bridge now
And it's burned like all the others

My tale is a long one
And it's name is no further ours

Never imagined he'd go back to the green past
Sick with deceit & noxious fume

Memory always finding it's way back 
To the womb
Only reason to justify the things
He's done

Poor lad, my creature has sunk
Say, finally it is so- Curtain,
descend for the final time

I'll say it now- this premature truth-
that the only element we have to share
Is the sand under our shoes.
