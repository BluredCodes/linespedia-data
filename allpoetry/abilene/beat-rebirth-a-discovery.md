title: "Beat Rebirth (A Discovery)"

We may be the next beats
The Sal and Deans

Torment-stricken, it's so odd to see
Someone as miserable as me and me

But here I am, and again, you are
Though not as a venomous root,

But a seed inside the hand of fate
Plucked from a scandalous fruit

And if I choose to remember you
Three hundred years from now,

It'll be in my grave, with a decanter saved
For the immortal me and you.
