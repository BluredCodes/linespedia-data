title: "Limp Salad Lust"

I'm not here to eat you,
Salad Lust
I don't know what I'm 
Going to do with you.

Those weren't really my nails 
Scratching your neck
And the black hair in your eyes
Wasn't mine.

You sure ain't ripe for
A person your size
And now my body is known
To yours.

I think I could go to hell today
I could try to talk myself out of it
But I'd rather see your face,
Because mine is full of guilt.
