---
title: "Art & Sadness"
writer: "Abilene"

Pondering on the duration of these meetings
While imagining a place where the wind 
Can lift me up and take me afar

Are you inside my haze?
Alive with my pain...yet?
I write, you see, this is my life

I write for my life
And it's bound like a hard secret
A secret to be told and broken
And I broken, my face in heartfelt ruin

Majesty Secret, a large meaningless whorl
A whorl of intoxicating kiss and forever branded guilt
My life- I write for art & sadness.
