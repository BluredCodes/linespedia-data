writer: "Abilene"

Watching the way the man dances
He is an old man, dancing & prancing like a child

And there are many of them
But only he is dancing to the music 

And the girls in the band look cold
They're dancing only to stay warm

The band didn't bargin for him
His big belly peeking it's way into their thoughts...

They're thinking of a thought that's cold
