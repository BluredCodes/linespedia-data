---
title: "For Future"
writer: "Abilene"
slug: "for-future"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I’m caught between two wars- or worlds.
I’m alive- wearing time loosely around my waist,
And your turmoil like a widow’s black shawl.
Let me go, release me, let me come apart.
Don’t blame me a fool, ‘cause then I can’t be rich in heart
I can’t hide the diamonds in my soul, I can’t live this way.
I’m fallen, I feel the tank weight of discord
Aligning such a deep part of me-
Such a part that despises cocaine.
This episode feels final...
The devil cries for me,
And I cry for future.
