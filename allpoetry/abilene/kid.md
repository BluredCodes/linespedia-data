title: "Kid"
writer: "Abilene"

I looked at that kid sitting in his desk,
And suddenly he became a symbol 
Of my generation.

He was big, adult-like, and it scared me
He scared me because I've come to find out
That something in life is amiss...

Then, I knew, my childhood was gone.


(9-18-96)
