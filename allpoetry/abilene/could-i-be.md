---
title: "Could I Be"
writer: "Abilene"
slug: "could-i-be"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/20514-Could-I-Be-by-Abilene"
---

I feel like I've died
Oh, no that's not a bad word
Something has died inside.

The pang in my side,
The hole in my heart
Something seems to be missing...

Tension is thick
But the time has gone by,
And could I be wasting away?
