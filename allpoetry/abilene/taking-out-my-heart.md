title: "Taking Out My Heart"

I'm drunk & sick but happy & strong
And we're all horny as owls & toads
Lying & crying for my own chance at freedom
Falling from a vast sky of liquor
Stupor of mistakes, but I don't care
He's not a good boy, never loved me
So I'll make him & brake him
Until he bleeds as I did
I believe I can say this, I can!
Alive in the laps of 1,000 boys
A savage to ravage such types as these
A purple eye, a subtle sky
It's me & him & them & I
