---
title: "Set Up"
writer: "Abilene"
slug: "set-up"

Love broke from my glass eyes

Love broke the smile from my face

I don't understand such a man

With no graces exposed, only the ugly vines

Yet my heart still reaching deep inside

A taste true to my tongue & bittersweet

I've been set up. 

Hurt as my witness.
