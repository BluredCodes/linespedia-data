title: "Longing America"
writer: "Abilene"

I work & sleep & work in America
I work with people who've grown bitter
With what they're here for
I think of my boss- an over-grown child
And bad sense of humor

I work & sleep & think of America
Landscapes, the tide, the magnificent skies
The immortal highways stretched long
And wide away from every place 
I long to be

I work & sleep & covet America
I wish to be beatific inside this place
Moving towards the grey city's dawn
Feeling out the lines of the past
These lines that appear drawn on our face

I work & work & and dream of America
Although inside, I feel I'm only looking through
My will is strong but temporarily hung up
I may just become eccentric, unhinged with great spirit
To feel free from the storming lack of my aura
