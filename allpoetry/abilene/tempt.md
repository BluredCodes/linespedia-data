I can’t tempt his hard-on
Because lately our souls have been too weak.
But I fixed an angelic hand on his hair
To tell him that he’s my only one.
Fool me- I’m a fool
But I found your magic place.
Only because I pretended
To be Nancy Drew.
It’s sunup and I’m here 
Wishing you were my coat.
