title: "The way I feel about everything (1997)"

Stop dying!

But I keep dying, and can't stop it...
Every night, another death.
A part of me dies every night...
The week is too slow, I can't bear it...
My headache grows & splits, every night....

I can't stop thinking about the day you'll come home
And change the way I feel about everything.
