title: "Bottle of Sweet Depression"

The hard truth lies within 100 cigarettes
One hundred thoughts or more
Inside 16 ounces of glass.

Shattered was the memory 
On my weather-beaten window sill
Battered was my heart,
residing in humid gusts of grief

The lonliness I know sits in that bottle,
Homicide too, homicide & rain.
My body is crushed within
That little, despicible bottle!

The way it's shaped,
Isn't and can't be trusted,
And can't ever be forgotton
My sweet depression.This was written at a full blown adolescent stage, where feelings seem so strong- (homicide?- I was too gutless)
