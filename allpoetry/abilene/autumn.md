There’s no autumn here in Florida.
And nothing that compares to the mighty pine trees or cedar.

The fixed season fails to bring a cooler wind,
Not even a differing smell.

I seem to be alone and wishing
There was some way to bring autumn to me

I remember how the chill in
The breeze would blow a kiss
And the ground felt
Like cold silk under  my feet.

The feeling could be summed up in
Jim Croce’s voice- the intimacy
In the blow of the breeze on my cheek.

There’s no autumn here in the bright sunshine
No call for a change of season

Then I ask what my reason is
For depriving myself of any change.
