title: "Trance"
writer: "Abilene"

I love you
The day is long
And sweat for the money
I love you.

You're in my skin
My will to be
Our futures price
When lovers meet.

Pure, your soul
Mine, not so
But a glimpse of you
And I let go.

Blink me an eye
Prance me a dance
Do what you do for me
A trance, a trance.
