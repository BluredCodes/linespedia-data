title: "The Lonely Paraschitzanoid"

I open my mouth shut,
staring when you're not,
my conscience a gauzy sheet,
you're everything I‘m not.
I’ve betrayed the voice of reason,
planting its seeds like benevolent disease,
suggesting that everything’s one,
that I should just let it be.
Why should I listen to reason?
It never has bowed to me.
