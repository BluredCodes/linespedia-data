title: "Jonesboro, Ar."
writer: "Neurosine"

Infectious, delicious, deteriorate.
Surreptitious, enlighten, ameliorate.
Disdain, despair, belittle, berate.
myself leaving me in this state.
What a fucker I am to decieve me,
what an idiot to even believe me.
When I know I want what's on the plate.
And I'm afraid the next moment,
might just be too late,
for me to grab a taste of something,
a little less plain,
I disdain until I must agree:
it'd be interesting to be,
just a little bit different me.
I'll pay...that's the way...
but on another day.
Anything to stave this boredom away.
I fucking just hate this place,
I'm afraid I'll look at my face,
and find I've changed into one of it's minion.
Blinders and sayings, parables and praise,
to a discorporate being,
pretending it's me with those wings.
A sheep in the ocean of fate...
washing me away.
