I can jerk off all that I want,
yet refuse to converse with my hand.
It’s a strange enough relationship…
But Uncomplicated,
a matter of hormones and glands.
My hand lacks valor, 
And sophistication,
It’s completely in my control,
No surprises,
No demands.
I don’t think it really loves me.
I don’t think it really can.
It’s a limited relationship,
But perhaps I can get it a tan,
Teach it to can-can,
Buy it cosmetics,
Give it a band….
But still it’s only a hand.
Don’t think though that I don’t appreciate it,
I know that it does what it can.
