A notion can eat your heart out,
a notion can bend your brain,
a notion can place you a few steps ahead,
or knock you a whole world away.
Reasoning leads us to tautological conclusions.
Complications invariably arise,
we mix up the chatter into some stew,
that comes spilling from our minds. 
Nothing is real that can help us,
understanding comes with great pain,
to know the world and the universe,
you must be completely insane.
Actuality is a strange dimension,
untenable and sublime.
An ungraspable fluid,
we just swim right through it,
and it swims through us in kind.
I think we could learn how to treat us and it,
if we only had the time.
