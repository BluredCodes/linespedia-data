Infinite Daze,in the ceaselsss emotions,
We justify the paths we walk,
 Close the doors, listen to pre-fabricated peace,
Deeply wanting the sweet surcease,
 to die, to die,
something more than high,
to be released.on the edges of the infinite skies,
Alive, breathing, desiring our life.

	Our peace is tenuous at best,
Our minds lack wisdom, want critical rest,
They need to face the day.
They need to face the day.

	We lose sight of the way,
We're conditioned; imbued answers.
And don't really make it all better.
They lie, and they lie, and they lie.

Only to make them better.
They take the letters,
They spell pleasing words 
To make the shit smell like pretty brown flowers,
To guild the cage with the gold,
stolen our so precious stones,
They use  lacking reason,
claim gods open the seasons,
let themselves rest in illusions,
base their lives on lies.
want it to be just the same,
For you and I,
No wisdom,
You and I,
No reason,
You and I,
Myth in the eyes,
Let’s take their powers away,
Let’s l;eave them powerless in their decay.
