title: "Conspiration Spectre"

The deluded,
so close to the truth,
closer than the norm,
take a step in the wrong direction.
Is is conspiracy,
is it mockery,
or is it a lie?
Is it the movies?
Almost they understand the lies,
they almost pull off the disguise,
then schitz out like some abstract painting,
but close enough to see the lies
call a truce.
Is it some betrayel to the common bond,
calling up spectres,
you may become if you seek,
anything that's beyond,
common fallacy,
we belong,
somewhere they don't even want us to imagine.
