---
title: "Self-Molding Clay"
writer: "Neurosine"
slug: "self-molding-clay"
source: "AllPoetry"

Pathways of interaction,
interfacing energies,
internally touching reality,
shaping, being shaped.
An organism far reaching as the universe,
eternally changing actualities face,
shaping, being shaped.
This infinite reaction,
a being bathed in existence,
persisting, insisting to change.
Learning the way of the waves,
shaping, being shaped.
Clay molding clay molding clay.
