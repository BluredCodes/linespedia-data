Shedding the skins
I worked hard to believe,
now their existence is baggage to me.
Emphatic with morals,
riddled with lies,
luckily this time,
I got out with my life.
Nothing in this place deserves my belief.
I just knew you'd be the last to deceive.
wrong in the heart and wrong in the mind,
I'm supposed to just drop it and leave it behind.
Somehow I still find that it all follows me.
Emphatically claiming that this can not be.Written October 11th, 2001 ©  on Oct 11 2001 06:23 AM PST, Neurosine    personal  • angst
