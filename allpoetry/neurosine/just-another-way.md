Douse me with infinity,
wander through my lies,
eat me, taste divinity,
samples for the kind.
I'm just a book,
so open me,
Read me,
take your time.
Another nook in which to peek,
inside of your own mind.
translate boundries of 
Monochromatic, sepia toned,
mundane things we're told,
and sold
just like that,
a vision stole,
weep...and grieve,
because it replaced,
truth you've always known.
I'm asking you to see,
your visions, your truths, and your mind,
are very...very old.
They know,
they need not be told.
