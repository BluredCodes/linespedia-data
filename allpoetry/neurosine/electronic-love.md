Your synapses composed,
in just such a fashion,
you're always ready for electronic action.
Diodes exposed and transistors of passion.
Your all positronic, and you want satisfaction.
You're pulsing like fire,
and you'll get it yet,
my electronic darling,
just don't make me sweat.           ©  on Oct 11 2001 06:32 AM PST, Neurosine    other  • adult  • humor
