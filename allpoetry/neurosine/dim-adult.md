Light a candle,
lick my wounds,
you know that I've been dying too.
You lie to me,
and I'm your fool ,
why do I waste my time on you?
Fairly wasted,
every hue,
drains of color,
drains of you.
I miss you,
want to fuck you,
kiss you,
but I need nothing more,
in my life,
than to simply despise...dismiss you.
I 've weighed this stain in every way,
it clouds my vision every day.
I can't decieve the deeper why,
I can't believe I want to die. 
Please just go the fuck away.
I cannot stand this slow decay.
This is a terror pain frustration piece from some of my bad bad days.
