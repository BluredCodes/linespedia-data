title: "Free Time"
writer: "Neurosine"

Time wrestles thoughts,
trestles infinity,
above opens doors,
used to remind me,
with pictures,
dyslexic snapshots,
glimpse of serenity...
fluffy oblivious divinity,
lost in another soul,
didn't know,
I drowned there.
Couldn't move or speak freely,
groove within me,
know or see all the needs,
up in me.
Slowly I lift above this darkened cloud,
unprotected,
and allowed,
anything I care to imagine.
