title: "Antimerant"

I hate me, I hate me,
I want to negate me,
Disdain and berate me,
distend and deflate me,
i rate me, below,
i pull me in tow,
suffer me to believe anything,
that I know.
I'm so lame,
and a shame,
a sad fucking blow.
a life form so low,
below so below.
so incredibly dumb,
that I don't even know,
I mean nothing,
and dying is so,
such a favor for everything.
