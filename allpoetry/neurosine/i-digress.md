---
title: "I digress"
writer: "Neurosine"
slug: "i-digress"

Indebted to nothingness,
for every bit of my laughter.
I sip at the pool of understanding.
Nerd extraordinaire.
And I pretend I have no cares.
And everything is no where.
A nihilist in denial. 
Nothing can be fair.
Ambiguity is truth.
Where did I leave my underwear?
God...I don't know.
And y'know, I don't care.
They're everywhere.
Under that facade of laughter,
and nobility.
On the chair.
I could even be wearing them.
What matters is that they're there.
Anyhow...
