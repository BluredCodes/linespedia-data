All queries,
Reduced to metaphore,
Living in the blasphemy of it’s own lips,
The metaphore will surely die.
No Reprise for the true metaphore,
For it cries that it doesn’t exist
And it cries....and it cries.....and will mourn ever-more,
For as the metaphore strives to justify its existence,
The metaphore surely dies.
