All synchronized, purely evident,We must see when we peel through ourselves.Nothing left but pure synchronized…Energy in vibrating intricacy.Nothing but passing lies.Yet another, another surprise.All through time. Through another disguise.We sheer through all we despise.All this just to realize.It’s only one mind,somehow alive.
