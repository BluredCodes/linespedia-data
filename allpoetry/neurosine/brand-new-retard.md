I'm a retard,
in a phase.
Seeing mosaic,
through a multi-hued haze.
My soul also retarded.
I know just where,
it started.
With quivering lips,
I stand on the edge.
35 Miles Per Hour...skateboarding.
Right on the edge...and I knew it.
My eyes had no freedom but to watch intently.
Let me decide.
As I fly under the truck...skateboard mutilated.
Doors open for me.
I'm a retard. 
Or a lamer.
Or loser.
Or crime scene.
Or another alien.
Jolted into submission by the man.
The Fucking man, I say...
shamelessly unafraid.
Illuminati already hunt me.
They're making me one of babbles children.
even they say...I'm totally retarded.
wise beyond a single life span.
as my body gets older,
I will be free from all pain.
Concentrating on the masses. 
Peyote...psilocybin...MDMA...
welcome to the real world...
they say...
then they fade. 
And you wonder if they were there anyway.
But you certainly feel retarded
as the whole world blows away.
