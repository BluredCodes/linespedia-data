Light to dark obfuscates all with disguise.
Configurations that blow through your mind. 
Neanderthals or stalagtites aren't so blind,
that they are not in the moment...
completely. 
We fall a second behind,
letting our thread untwine,
until it bursts away,
many times per day.

II
I've lost myself somewhere,
Yes, I likes to hide,
and subtly tugs me along
I imagines I chase it,
But I might be wrong.

III

Inevitibly I catch up, to me...
With a bump I step into my life,
how could I have been so wrong?
What a waste of time to wonder.
Where to shift the blame?
Everything is the same anyway.
Every time it's the same: 
I became,
I became,
then withered away,
,again and again and again.Somewhere I picked up that MU was chinese for well...kind of zero, but more like a third option in binary option, 1, 0, or MU?
