Chase all your dreams,
chase them away.
Fuck 'em: You'll be rich someday,
then you'll have your say. 
Enlightenment sits on your shoulder,
complain and it will stay;
kiss it, and it will show you love,
then it will go away.
Arduous reason is not your domain,
nor is anywhere simple,
nor is anywhere sane.
Draining your brain,
into every orifice,
you soul has always been stained.
It's a meat to be chewed very lightly,
beautiful in self restraint,
graceful in composure,
glorious in passion,
chasing illusions all through the day,
chasing all of those dreams away.This was about a girl I knew, then it took on its own life.
