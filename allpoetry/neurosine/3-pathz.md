There are three paths always available to us. 
One leads to the world people often think they want and succumb to. It is the realm of dreams, and only inadvertently effected by reality.
The second is the limbo of waking dreams, a life of anxiety and preconceived notions, wishful thinking. It is the most frustrating because the denizens delusions and expectations lead to a great deal of mental anguish. It’s dificult to second-guess a huge mass of oscillating energy to grant wishes. God is a manifestation of this realm. Most people exist here.
The third path is when the dreamer dreams with reality, dreams themselves into the dream, indiscernable from the rocks or desire. This travelller intuitively senses a personality of the universe coming out of the ground and sky. They listen to the significant conversation the universe transmits to them from infinity, and waits for the natural response to motivate them. This requires a peace of mind only attainable by meditation and the constant alertness which marks a person completely enveloped in the present moment. Fear is no longer intimidating. Death and birth just a part of the never ending change. The concept of self only a notion. Inextricably connected with the infinite universe on every infinite level of size and dimension. 
	If we can unlearn the fears instilled into us, we can allow ourselves to take the third and most natural path. Everyone is a citizen of the universe, all of time and space. Few people have attained the wisdom to really appreciate that fact.
