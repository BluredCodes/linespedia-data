title: "Unconscience Circumference"
writer: "Neurosine"

Sledding out to sea,
my ego and me,
can't leave sadness alone.
I must respond, correspond,
and ask why?
Why does it seem I have died?
Why does it feel like a lie,
why didn't you,
why didn't you try?
Intimate with no one,
my alien wanders around,
wondering why nothing I can grasp,
exists in the sound,
of one hand clapping alone.
Inevitably I begin to rationalize,
but can not escape within that disguise,
I think Maya's laughing at me.
Though I love her....
I still see her veil fall away,
only horror remains.
To eat and to stave,
off day after day after day.
Another thing to despise.
