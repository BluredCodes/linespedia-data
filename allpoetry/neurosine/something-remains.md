---
title: "Something Remains"
writer: "Neurosine"
slug: "something-remains"

Your eyes were a glimmering passage, you were certainly that illusive 'she,'my dream girl, restoring,my shattered love of my flawed sense of humanity.I had no doubt that you and me,would live and die as naturally,as we came together.Easy as breathing,easy as walking,as falling,as bleeding.My impulse to call as I see you receding,fills me with such misery. I know you won't listen,I know you won't pause,for the picture you've painted of me.Stuffed in a box,labeled, discarded,you won't even look to see,the hurt angry mind,shamed to tears for a dream,another victim of mistaken reality.As much as I've wanted to degrade you,and break you,hurts do fade, but love stays.You'll always be in my family,and I'll never be the same.I only hope I'll grow stronger,as I sift through the meager remains.
