title: "Perusing Infinity"
writer: "Neurosine"

The model now melts,
skeletons unglued,
sputtering laws in disjointed divisions,
to explain inexplicable stew.
In contrast
my tranquil emotions and rythms,
sing a synchronous symmetrical tune,
transending hesitative,
stifling decision,
the universe beats its tattoo.
It views itself in my third eye,
I'm the moment,
awake,
so alive.
I try capturing;
to file this state,
(for tasting some other time.)
It then falls out of pose,
the cataract closed,
on a cacophony of patternless light. 
Photons arrested in flight,
inadequate to express a true vision,
of the moment as it was comprised.
Inexplainable keeping,
the inexplicable secret,
I can't tell you,
but you can find...
The whole universe in a square inch of space,
all cosmology within your mind.
