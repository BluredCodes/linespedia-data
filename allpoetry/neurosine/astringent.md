---
title: "Astringent"
writer: "Neurosine"

The astringent didn't completely keep me from licking the wound,
I think it may go deeper.
The contingencies I took didn't quite keep me from peeling,
away all the layers.
I can still clearly see all the pain.
I opened the wound in hopes rife with healing,
now I'm squinching my eyes, and I'm feeling,
that salt you threw away,
right into this gash,
I thought was fairly obvious.
But it wasn't at all that you didn't notice,
it was just that you simply didn't care.
I know that you'll never be there.
Got to find a better healer.
You suck the pain,
only sharing with me,
the burden of your cross.
With your deceptive phrases,
and your mindless prayers.
You've made a sort of joke out of me,
despite the pain, I can laugh too,
in some twisted way...
it's quite a scream.
We never ever want to awake,
from a beautiful dream.
