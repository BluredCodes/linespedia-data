title: "The Spiral Caress"

A kindness untouched,
by the harshness in life;
Good cheer and charity follow in love.
Aware of the oneness of this world,
Love in his eyes.
That soft voice spoke fondly of people.
His Kindred.
His love still flows true.
His energy circling around within memory.
His experiences breed skill,
in the blood of descendents,
lessons pervade their memories.
Birth isn't a beginning death seemingly ends;
we can perceive only a moment of infinite
nature dissolves,
as it occurs,
as it evolves,
Again...and again...and again...
Eternity has no beginning,
love has no end...
