Grounded,
okay and normal,
community, and love.
No, I'm just this awkward weirdo,
home lost long ago,
if I'll find one again,
I don't know,
Living lonely.
Relieved of the delusion,
but heavy with apprehension.
Wondering where my life may go....
afloat and I don't understand me.
I'm a mess,
can't talk to anyone...
oh fuck, I'm so weird.
I have to confess,
I need to meet some people like me.
To touch, feel, and trust...
in love and in lust,
other aliens to surround me.
