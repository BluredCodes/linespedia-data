Dance away into nothingness...
trifle strange,
notions,
only,
guiding your way.
Following the pretty colors and lights.
So angry when they hit you,
so mad when they shit in your face,
so unaware of the consequences,
they just hurt you.
Dance away into a bloody mess.
Don't you have s0meth1ng to confess?
Our juices are flowing,
we'll devour your sins,
take it into us like some bitter candy.
Then spit it in your face haha. 
How does that taste?
We think it's delicious.
We're suspicious of you,
want to get into you,
maybe uncenter you,
then splinter your core.
That and more.
We'll show you the score.
Then let you in the door.
Then we'll all go and do it some more.I couldn't even begin to explain to you what this is about. It's a menacing disjointed abstract.  Analysis hopeless  . Spewing poison.
