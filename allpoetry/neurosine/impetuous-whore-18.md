---
title: "impetuous whore (18+)"
writer: "Neurosine"
slug: "impetuous-whore-18"

I walk in your shadow,
I shout out your name,
but you don't give a fuck,
and it's just the same thing,
all alone,
on a phone,
talking to no one who is listening,
sweat is glistening,
though I know how you are.....
all alone,
on the phone,
with yourself,
on a shelf,
with dust ages old,
yet I care....
I don't know why.
Do I still want to die with you,
shall I stagnate alone,
for a lie,
for a phone,
for a call?
Is this all,
I can see anymore?
What a whore...
what a whore,
I became for you.
