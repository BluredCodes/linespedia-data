I’m arraigned, and I’m so innocent,
I don’t know the rules of this game.
But don’t think I’m playing the victim.
It’s not all…
It’s not all the same.
Children of the universe,
The differences flow between us…
As we recognize them,
They transist.
Travel on,
Their fates and destinies,
Arranging you and me,
In our soup,
And our soliloquy,
Lacks substance…
Pathetically incomplete,
Stoned…
Here’s what we own,
This moment,
I’d rather not spend it alone.
It’s hard to make friends,
With the rocks and the stones.
Sometimes I need kinder company.
Someone I haven’t yet known.
