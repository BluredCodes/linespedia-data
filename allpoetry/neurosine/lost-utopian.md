title: "Lost Utopian"

If the stiff lactic acid filled chi barriers,
would let my energies flow...
I could search for something greater...
become and admonish all doubts and all fears.
In a moment...just long enough to tear it,
grinning and laughing,
chewing up its energies,
my ends could find me...
on my way.
But these paths are uncertain.
The roads rife with misinformation...
how does anybody win,
but with blind faith,
and determination.
Stupidity has its place...
ignorance it's fortitude,
denial can be a master,
of thinking men. 
We're ruled by the stupid.
That acid seeps in again.
I really need a friend.
Someone who won't just pretend...
to understand the misery,
of loving such a dissapointing humanity.
Where are the rest of Utopias children?
Working on electronic devices,
hacking the systems,
harrasing the masses,
offending the snobs with their own minds....
presenting their evil to them.
Though the messenger gets the blame.
Where are all my friends?
