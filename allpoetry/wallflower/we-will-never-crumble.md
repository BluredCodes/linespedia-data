title: "We Will Never Crumble"
writer: "Wallflower"

The city which once held
The torch to light the way
Crumbled into pieces
In just a single day.

But America cannot be broken,
America will be proud.
We will show our strength
And let our pride be seen all-out.

We mourn the loss of many
For a much unneeded cause.
They sliced right through the nation
Like a newly sharpened saw.

We will never crumble,
We will never fall.
They may have bombed our nation,
But haven't weakened us at all.
