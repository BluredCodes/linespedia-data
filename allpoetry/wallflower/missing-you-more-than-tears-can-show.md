title: "Missing You More than Tears Can Show"

I remember the breeze
Through your silver hair,
and the memory's so real
It still feels like you're there.
I miss you more
than I can bare..
But now when I need you
You're gone, You're never there.
How could god have taken you
Knowing how you're needed?
How could god give you so much pain
In this life you have exceeded?
I know that it
has been three years
But still I have to 
cry these tears.
Wishing for a chance
To thank you so,
Missing you more than
tears can show.
