---
title: "You Should Know"
writer: "Wallflower"
slug: "you-should-know"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

You should know,
These tears I cry
They are for him,
As I watch him die.

You should know,
This poem I write
Is because I
Slowly die inside.

You should know
This is goodbye
To him, The
Reason why I cry.

He's going on to god
Because only the good
Die young.
And, love, He is too young.

Of course, I'm going to cry
When I place 
just one red rose
Upon his coffin-side.

Of course,
Inside I'll die
As I take that one last look
At his caring eyes.

Of course,
He'll pass 
on judgement day.
We all knew that anyway.

You should know,
These tears I cry
They are for him,
As I watch him die.
