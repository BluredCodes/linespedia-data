---
title: "I'll Be Your Everything"
writer: "Wallflower"
slug: "ill-be-your-everything"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/5059-Ill-Be-Your-Everything-by-Wallflower"
---

You mean so much to me,
Not even words can tell.
I can imagine life without you, love
It would be a total hell.

And if you were to leave me here,
Craving for your touch..
I have to tell you, sweetheart
That I would miss you way too much.

You see, my love, I need you so
Like a seed, I need your sunlight.
You took me from the shadows
And saved me from the night.

I know it may be a challenge,
I know that we may not always agree.
But if we reach a bump in the road,
Just give me a chance and stay with me.

I promise i will love you,
I'll make each day worth living.
I will promise to be there for you,
And I will be your everything.
