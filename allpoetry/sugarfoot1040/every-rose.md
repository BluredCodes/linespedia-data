---
title: "Every Rose"
writer: "sugarfoot1040"

Every breath with you,

Every wish for you,

Every rose petal from you,



You loved me until the end of time

as you you told me in my dreams,

You walked by me the ground moved 

me in a mystery way,

never stop loving you,



Every breath with you,

Every wish for you,

Every rose petal from you,

Every breath

Every wish 

Every rose

of the love you sent me,



The colors of life glows around our love,

Our love for each other is strong,

You loved me until the of time,

never stop loving me,



Every breath with you,

Every wish for you,

Every rose petal from you,

never stop loving me

Every breath

Every wish

Every rose
