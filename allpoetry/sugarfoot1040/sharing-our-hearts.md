The first time we met,
I shared my heart with you,
and you shared your heart.

I know from that moment on,
you was the one that god sent
for me. The heart that shall 
share with me, and my heart 
shall share with his,

We walked hand to hand,
We loved each other,
Filled the empty spaces
in our lives.

We won't take granted of 
each other.
We comforted each other in 
the time of need.

As we shared our hearts,
we got to know each other
better than a friends.

From that day,
I loved you 
unconditional, and you
loved me unconditional.
