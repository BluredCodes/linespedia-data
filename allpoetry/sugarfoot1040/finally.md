Finally I can walk away from you,Finally I can go on,Finally I stopped wishing for you,Finally I realized that I have a life after you,Finally I feel good about myself,Finally I can be myself,Finally I stopped crying over you,Finally I can say it is the past,Finally I stopped dreaming about you,Finally I can face my fears,Finally I can love someone else,Finally the day goes by without you,Finally I listen to love songs without you in my mind,Finally I understood why god did this to me,Finally I can put your pictures in the shoe-box,Finally I'm free,Finally I'm over you!!!!!
