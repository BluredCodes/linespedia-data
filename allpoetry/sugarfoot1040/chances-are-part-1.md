I have a love story to tell you, it is like a fair tale, the good, bad, and the ugly but, this love story you couldn't keep a secret.   They fell in love at first site, the girl felt the butterflies in her tummy. she asked herself can this be true, she finally mite the man of my dreams,one of her dreams come true, but they both was so young to understand what the true meaning of love. They did anything and everything against their parents wishes, so they could understand the meaning of their love for each other.    They was so dumb and young to understand what they have done, and they lost a little something that they created together, and they paid dearly for their sins. from that moment on she blamed her man for her sins, she blamed everyone in her way. She thought she was smart, but she was to young to understand the life that she was walking into. She was driving her man into an world away from herself, they was going separate ways, she couldn't understand why his father hated her so much, she paid for her sins.       he started wanting her to be around all the time. His attitude started to change but not by her hands, he changed on his own free will. She begged him but he won't listen to her at all, so she fellowed him on his choices in life............
