I can see the other side of my life, can i touch it?
I can be the one of understanding you,
I can walk without holding on,
I can run from danger,
I can be myself,
I can be a friend,
I can be there for you,
I can be light to show you the right way,
I can be the best for you,
I can be your lover,
I can be a boss,
I can be me once again.
