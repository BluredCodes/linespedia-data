---
title: "Sitting Here"

As I sit here in a lonely quite room,
all I can do is think of the times
we spent together.

Loving one of another

Thinking of you is my favorite hobby now,
since you left me without saying good-by.

You said I love you until we was miles apart.
I felt lost and lonely without you arms
around me, and singing a love song
to me

I hated you since you walked away from me
Left me standing there crying my eyes out

all you had to say is I Love You

My mother said you would always leave me standing
in the pouring rain-sad, lonely, and confused

This is it, This is my life, This is the new 
beginning to start all over again without
you standing beside me.
