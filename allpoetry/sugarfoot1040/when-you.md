When you said good-bye to me,
My heart fell into a hole,
and started to bleed,

When you went out the door,
the tears fell down my face,
to reach it's faith,

When you wrote that letter,
it took my soul and 
stabbed it twenty times,

When you called me,
that took my breath away,
and stopped my heart to hear
You say Good-bye forever.
