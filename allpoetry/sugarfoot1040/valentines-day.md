Today is special,
Today is sweet,
Today is when two lover meet,
They hold the other's hand,
They can face the world,
This girl and her guy,
They share something special
no one else knows,
This love is forever,
They'll never let go,
What they have is priceless
more than a diamond or a pearl,
This girl and her guy,
This guy and his girl.
