She know in her heart he wasn't ready for a true relationship, so she wanted to take her time because she don't know if she was ready for the challenge of her life.
They was doing so perfect, until the old flame, the one he married to wanted him back. He told her that she had nothing to worry about, that he was happy with her. he loved on her, but he never told her how truly he felt about her, but she knew what he was thinking, just because the way he smiled at her, the way he told her that she was beautiful, the way she moved him, the way she understood him. He said he would never break her heart again.
But another day came, he never called her again, never to say it was over, or to say i'm sorry again. she was left alone again with a broken heart again, she said to herself will there be another chances again god. months went by her, she dated around, but she was still in love with him. she know that she needed to go on with her live. her friends helped her out alot, by helping her to forget him, and that there was a man out that will love her for who she was, and not to use her. she tried and tried, until one day. she looked up, and she saw her life flash in front of her face, she know from that moment that life does go on without him, and that god has someone out there for her, and it might take time for her to get there, but she walks on......
