Each day gets harder to walk on in my life,I feel this weird feeling in my heart tellingme to stay by your side, and never to let go,but on the other hand my mind tells me to walk on,I guess time is my witness in life, or I'm just fooling myself, or you are fooling me?I tired of running, I'm standing here in thisspot, and one day you will see me loving youalways.The storms might blow me over, but I can get up,and dust my knees off without a cut,Please turn around to me and see me crying over you,and stand here in the storm with me, and embrace mewith the love you have for me,Because I know it is there.
