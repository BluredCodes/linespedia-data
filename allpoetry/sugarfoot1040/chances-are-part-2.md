....... She started to grow older and opened her eyes, and asked herself why am I fellowing a man like a puppy. She couldn't leave him because she loved him with all her heart and soul. The day came when she had to think hard for herself 'where her life was going'. He stopped spending time with her, and betrayed and lied to her. her parents yelled and screamed at her to stop crying over him, and asked her what she wanted in her life. They was so worried about her because they wanted to poprotecter from the pain of a broken heart. The day has came when they went separate ways, he went his way, and she went her way. She cried for several months, exex speciallyolidays, it was the hardest. Her family was there with their husbands and wifes, and her standing there all by herself. the whole time they was there she would hide in her room, and cry. He tried his hardest to get back into her life, but she didn't know what she wanted. so she told him to go on with his life, and he did, and he married another. She was so heart broken because the man she loved had finally went on his life, and all she had was a lonely bar stool with her name on it. She dated around but nothing compare to the love she had with him. Years went by, he chased her, and she chased him. Finally a day came for that second chance to make it right.............
