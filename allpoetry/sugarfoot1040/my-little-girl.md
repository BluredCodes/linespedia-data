You was too early,
I never wanted this to
happen to you.
I think about you every second,
and minute of a day.
When your birthday comes around
each year, I never forget the pain, 
and your beautiful face and how 
little you was.
But I wish that I could put little bows
and ribbons in hair.
Your memories live on within my heart.
I never got to say good-by, or I love you.
I hope that you hear me in the heavens.
I wished for years that you was here,
but I know that you are being taken care
of up there.
baby if you hear your mommy, I Love You, 
and I'm Sorry My Little GirlIn the memory of Diamond Lynnann Pyatt passed away August 9, 1992
