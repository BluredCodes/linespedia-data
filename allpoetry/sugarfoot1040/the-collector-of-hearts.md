---
title: "The Collector of Hearts"
writer: "sugarfoot1040"

source: "AllPoetry"

You walked in my life,We loved each other,We walked hand to hand,You put a smile on my face,You collected my love,You collected my soul,You collected my feelings,You collected my heart,You walked out of my life,We part on a stormy night,We walked away from thought of each other,You broken my heart,I see you playing with others hearts,You collected her love,You collected her soul,You collected her feelings,You collected her heart,You are the Collector of Hearts,You walked out her life,You left her in the streets,You walked away from love,You broken her heart,Playing the Collector of Hearts,You collected my love,You collected her soul,You collected my feelings,You collected her heart,You collected my heart.
