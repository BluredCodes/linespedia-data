mucklar
mucklar
the world has gone mad
and its full of arseholes

self pleasuring, self fulfilling, souless arseholes
there every where on the streets, selling bullshit
to buy more bullshit

yes ( dear ) the world has gone mad
( and no-body gives a fuck )

the people i used to know have shrunken to tiny specs of dust that no longer speak or write to me

my generation
what do i know about my generation
i know nothing about no thing

and i sit comfortably
( thats right )
comfortably in my reluctance to mingle
