interlaced, interwoven
thoughts that bump and grind

to many sides to the puzzle
and release is reluctant to comply

it's tricky and spiky this thing called life

i do not know it's secrets but i hope one day i might

everythings now doubled sided, multifaceted, multifaced, mulitchoice, multi tasking

to many, to much stimuli

my eyes hurt but there is no stopping progress

development
or maturing
