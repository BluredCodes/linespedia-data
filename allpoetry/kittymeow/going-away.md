going away, gone, leaving, diappearing.
long time to get back. in the air, thinking
thinking I hope of me.

days long and nights longer
trying to smile, trying to celebrate this time of the year.

sunny out, smog blanket covers city, ugly place to walk under.

time keeps ticking
I'm so acutely aware of it, it hurts my insides.

packing best tops and jeans and jocks into small square suit case.

I must remember to eat breakfast
wonder when rach will drop off her stuff.

tomorrow never comes
timeless to know the difference
and uninterested in the difference

it all blends together to form a weekly smoothie.
