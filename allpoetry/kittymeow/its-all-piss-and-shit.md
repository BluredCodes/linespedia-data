its all piss and shit
everything can be divided into these two catagories
if its piss its bad
if its shit its really (dam) bad

where has the light goodness gone?

the cats are fighting again
i hate there fighting screams, there attack noises

i call the alley cat Bukowski cause he alwaays has scratches on his face just like the real Bukowski

the cat he fights with is a sun loving, roof dweeling cat
maybe its a female and Buk thinks he is in love but they just end up beating the shit out of each other

all i know is that it sounds scarey and aweful and primal and i just want to make them stop
i just want them to be happy and friends and they could do cat things together
you know, lying around, eating, sleeping, coughing up fur balls and preening

that would be much better
but Im  pretty sure they don't care what i think 
they just go on fighting and I'll see Buk tomorrow in the alley and I'll pat his little head and see afew more scratches to add to his Bukowski image
