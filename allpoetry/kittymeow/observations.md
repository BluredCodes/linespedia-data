light filled, sun filled room.
I feel awkward sitting on second hand chair.

I smell sunlight and freshness mixed with cigarettes and dust.

my legs feel smooth, I shaved them last night.

I see christmas decorations, small mounds of tinsle and sparkling red tinsle wrapped around 1.5 menter CD holder.

stuff is everywhere on dirty, dusty carpet, shoes,socks,books,bags and bits of paper.

my back hurts, feels like my spine is curved in the wrong direction.

hands slippery from moisterising my legs and my mouth tastes of coffee and cigarettes.

observations......................
