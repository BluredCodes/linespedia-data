STILLNESS CAN CRACK YOU
LIKE SILENCE CAN RIP YYOU OPEN

UNSPOKEN WORDS CARRY MORE BAGGAGE

THAN SPOKEN WORDS COULD EVER

MUSTER THE COURAGE TO OPEN MY MOUTH WIDE TO EXPOSE THE

YUCK........ OVERFLOWING
