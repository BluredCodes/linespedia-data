dream the dream, it's your to keep

hey mister on the corner with your cock in hand and your heart on the floor

what ya doing?
where ya been?
long time in nowhere, doing nothing

did ya find what you were looking for???????
and did you find it wanting more????????

hey mister on the corner
flipping quaters
give it up

slowly dying, weeping crying, mourning
did ya forget the time???
throw away your watch

hey mister laying flat with your thoughts exposed
did they steal your soul????????

is that why it's always so cold here !
