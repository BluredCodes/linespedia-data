forget regretsyour time is upleave quicklyleave quietlydon't mention itdon't remember itdon't try to understand itthings and places take shapelike cloud picturesoh beautiful cloud pictureswhere are you now ????my special friendimpressing people with your madnessi suspect             or impressing yourself with the shapes inside your headyour all crazyi leave now cradling my memories in my folded arms.
