PLAYFUL LIKE A KITTEN
THATS ME
HARD LIKE A ROCK
THATS ME
SOFT LIKE A FEATHER
THATS ME
SAD LIKE A LOST CHILD
THATS ME
HAPPY LIKE A MADMAN
THATS ME
EXPRESSIVE LIKE AN ARTIST
THATS ME
DRUNK ON LIFE AND FULL TO THE BRIM
THATS ME
THAT IS ME, IS ME , IS ME
