old junk
old junk
the yard is full
the mind is full
the eyes are full
overflowing, decaying
tired and perpetually bored
don't dare tell me fate has a plan
cause I think he fucked mine up
I want my money back man
it's all full
it's all filled up
no room
no space
be gone
unless you come bearing gifts
of chococlate and cigs and poetry books
by young freaks...............................................
