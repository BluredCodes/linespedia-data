a street paved
a creature delight
an army of flightless bugs with voices that echo through stimulants.

a poster book away
a picture dissected

the white collar man looks confused, disorientated, mess, ridged and frigid.

where's his high heels and surspenders
a kinky mind fuck
a gender perversion

click, snap, crack

coloured cloth adorn his once innocent body

tie hang up
