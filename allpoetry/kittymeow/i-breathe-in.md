---
title: "i breathe in"
writer: "kittymeow"

i awake
i breathe in

i rush
i do the things that have to be done
i hold my breath
i hustle
i bustle
weaving in and out of the hungry hoards of hurry happy fools

i rush, i stress , i panic
i breathe in, i hold my breath

than the sun retreats
and the blackness falls upon us

finally
i breathe out
