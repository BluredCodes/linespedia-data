title: "feel it"
writer: "kittymeow"

tongue fuck
fist fuck
mind fuck

life - mouth open, eyes closed
pretend that it's not you
pretend you don't exist

your a still life painting
a crooked frame
a decaying building
a frozen moment

if i told you it's okay
would you feel it ??????

where it's missing.
