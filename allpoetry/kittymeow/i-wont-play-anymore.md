title: "i wont play anymore"

words misplaced
displaced
misused and abused

it's a crime you know
to be such a fuckhead
it's a crime in some states

it's a crime
to projected your childhood shit
onto me into me
into my fragile mind
you have no right
and i don't want to play anymore

you hear me
are you listening
you mother fucking monster

i won't play anymore

anymore
anymore
anymore
