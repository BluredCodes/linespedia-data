inbetween realms
something is missing
a look
a stare
the color of your eyes
images and burnt onto my brain
I cannot rid myself of them
in my dreams they manifest
reminding me to look harder
to look closer
to examine
embrace the random images
they are mine to keep forever
mine
day light makes no sense
the slither of the moon seems farmilar
like a memory trapped in reason
logic is so over rated
it has delivered me from nothing
given me no-one
bouncing of interior walls
no-one sees the inside
and thats where all the action is man..............
