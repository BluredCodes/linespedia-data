your given me a fucking brain ache man
and a tooth ache

you sit there with your malajusted stares
hollowness is your very being

your given me a fucking brain ache man
and a tooth ache

too much bullshit has erupted from your half red lip sticked lips

stop projecting you freak
you fucking arsehole mother fucking lost daughter
blame only lasts so long dear child
your running low on excuses

don't you know it
don't you know it
don't you know it
