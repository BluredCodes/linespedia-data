go fuck yourself HARDsilent theory MANtake some time out to go mad and to remember what it feels like to be free.OH the structure you paintwith dull gray hues.you are but a rational monster, stepping on imagination with your big black manly boot.a kick in the groinoh i insistit would be my pleasurego fuck yourself HARDsilent theory MANyou sit in smug silenceit is your weaponi don't understand you
