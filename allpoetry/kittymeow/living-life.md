living life in paper cup
the plastic wrap is to tight

scream, release
cool strangers in the night
dark, fright images
cruise along my bedroom walls, deviating from there corner happy homes

why have i come here
to this dream palace
this self manifested reality

what lurkd inside these illionary walls

its all strange and out of place

take back my senses
give me back my eyes
and broken limbs

i awake and nothing has changed
