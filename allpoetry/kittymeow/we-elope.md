across the room you stare
eyes blazing

who told you I couldn't be somebody,
I know I shut those pixies up 
so it couldn't be them.

The sky matches your shirt
your such a stellar being

Don't tell me things I've said before, expecting me to recall them.

Tell me nothing but give me your eyes, your lips, your brain
I'll take good care of them.

You see I have nothing else, nothing tangable to please you with.

In the mits of my contemplation
your eyes catch mine and for a second we elope to a place where none of this matters
