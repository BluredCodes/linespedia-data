a trapped, drenched mind breaks, collapses under the pale sky and searches the darkness for deaths soft handshot motors rumble and old feet step time and time againa letter in the post carries news better not redeyes trace around faces, processing data, memory bank of madness reimbursedtired eyes and swollen limbsthe people travelplastic in handa cheap thrilla multicolored destructionmoist lingering nonsensea voice from the past traps and cages a memory in flight pathlight beckons through pin holes and the mind becomes numban overdose
