I remember strange nights and ridiculous chatter
when time lasted forever
when second felt like days
a single moment changed us forever
frozen time
slow motion movements
limbs reaching for things, firm and plush
lips moist and red, blood filled joy
talking in tongues, a language we understood
let no-one else in
the night must be ours and ours alone
we must save it for special occassions
for when we are lost in daylight
when we are confussed by rules and structure
jumping on tables
arms stretched
like wings
we wish to fly
to fly away from ourselves
for a moment of peace
quiet comes too quickly
liven here takes to long
I wont back the timeless moments
from you I wont back the chatter
the senseless chatter
that made me feel understood
when nothing makes sense
nothing matters
but your sensations
the tingles
the vibrations of life
