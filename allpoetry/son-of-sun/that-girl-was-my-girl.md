Memories from the past 

When I was with her 

Life going so fast 

The end was unfair 



Now you holding her hands 

Now you are her man 

I know I should understand 

But I can't 



Spending your moments with her 

Kissing her lips,smelling her hair 

Taking her here and there 

The smiles are everywhere 

And I'm here all alone 

And my faith was gone 

I know life is unfair 

That girl was my girl 

* * * 

Writing my pain by my tears 

And my dreams will disappear 

Around me closing doors 

Life isn't the same anymore 



Now I'm holding the loneliness 

And I feel of the emptiness 

I don't have to think,or to guess 

Now my girl with someone else 



She is your own 

Shivering her bone 

By your heart's beats 

To make love complete 

I know it's a nightmare 

That girl was my girl 

You shouldn't know something about her
