title: "The Son Of The Sun"
writer: "Son Of Sun"

Is this the beginning of the end 
or the end of the beginning 
I think I'm so careless 
and I'm counting the days 
with every moment 
to leave this sadness 
I don't want anything from this life
and I think it's time to say "Goodbye" 

I will forget my days with you 
I will forget my love for you 
I will forget my days with you 
and I will remind everyone 
I'm the son of the sun 
* * * 
My heart became strong like a stone 
and the memories reminding me 
Days are my way,The way of thorns 
and I'm still searching for me 
Don't think my heart burning like a fire 
you don't have anything could bring my desire 

I will forget my love around you 
I will forget my dreams about you 
I will forget my pain without you 
and I will remind everyone 
I'm the son of the sun 

I will run through unknown 
Not afraid of being alone 
and I don't want you to be with me 
Once again I will rise 
you will see it by eyes
and I will live for eternity
