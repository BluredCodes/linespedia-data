---
title: "Gentleman"
writer: "Son Of Sun"

Some people think, love is, 

childish and foolish things. 

but I think it's a myth, 

when I'm with, 

A girl with a golden wings. 



I still wondering, 

what it would be,if I didn't meet you. 

'cause love is amazing, 

when I find that you have it,too. 



I will be your man 

who give you all he can 

and always understand 

I will be your gentleman 

always kissing your hand 

living with you on every land 

I will be that man.
