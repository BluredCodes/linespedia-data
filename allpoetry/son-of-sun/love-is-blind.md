---
title: "Love Is Blind"
writer: "Son Of Sun"
slug: "love-is-blind"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/33855-Love-Is-Blind-by-Son-Of-Sun"
---

Beats from my heart 
now it will start 
were waiting for years 
to touch your ears 

I tried 
so hard to hide
feelings you can't find 
but love is blind 
to be with your side 

Now I'm here 
with you my dear 
whispers all I can hear 
when you are near 
to forget my fear 

You're special and unique 
like unknown lovely music 
you're hard to find 
I know love is blind 
to be with your side
