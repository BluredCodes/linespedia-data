title: "Every Time"

When I touched your hand 

I thought that I touched flowers 

And I need to understand 

Why the moment without you is like hours 



I kissed your tears 

I touched your lips 

Oh my dear 

You give me the power of missing 

Even if you walk two steps 



Every time I pray 

Wishing you would stay 

Waiting for you every day 

To live with you away 

I feel the pain if you will sigh 

With you, the love will never die 

I will be yours, and you will be mine 

In every day, in every time
