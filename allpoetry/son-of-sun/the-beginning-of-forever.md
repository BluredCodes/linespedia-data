title: "The Beginning Of Forever"
writer: "Son Of Sun"

I still remember 

The last time we were together 

Wanting to discover 

The beginning of forever 



But I knew that would be in the end 

And we will be apart 

So many kisses I can't send 

After what you did to my heart 



What can I do... to make you love me? 

What can I say... to make you believe me? 

It's only you... who I want to see? 

Every day... so set me free 



I lost my way 

And my heart was withered 

In a game I played 

Pretending I'm the winner 



So please return to the past 

Return to your man 

I said "I love you" at last 

Wishing you would understand 



It's true 

I still needing you 

But you must know 

That I'll never let you go
