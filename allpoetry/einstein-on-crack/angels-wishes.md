title: "Angels' Wishes"

Now we’re past the season
When people show good will
Momentary peace on earth
A time when all stands still
When angels’ work is minimal
The world it seems is one
Many happy people
Sharing in their fun
Of course there are exceptions
Perfection it is not
Christmas is the time of year
That cannot be forgot
Then the seasons over
Calm is in the past
Returning to life's troubles
Across our world so vast
Think of children’s wishes
I wonder if someday
Our attitudes will change somewhat
Have Christmas every day
Perhaps then in the future
Our worldly woes will fade
Bitterness and hatred
Cast into the shade
Angels then could concentrate
On those who need them more
So live all year like Christmas
Our world needs it for sure
