Humanity - A strange word
It encompasses all
All of us who live here
Can't listen to 'just one' call
The World is full of everything
Love - Hate - Peace and War
People full of harmony
Others who try to score
Another oxymoron
To which our lives digress
How can another human
Hurt just to impress?
We are one clan together
Under one big sky
Why can't we be happy?
And let divisions go by
We're split into our sections
Politics, Race and Faith
Within them subdivided
Humanity hard to trace
It may seem idealistic
To hope that at some time
Our world could be 'all peaceful'
Completely free of crime
Perhaps the word 'Humanity'
Should be the first we learn
So future generations
Will give World peace a turn
Humanity is all of us
Living together as one
Breaking down the obstacles
That stop us getting on
So next time you see this word
Or use it in your life
Think about it's meaning
Of Xanadu not strife
It's up to us to change it
Each and every one
For every time that someone does
The first steps have begun .....
