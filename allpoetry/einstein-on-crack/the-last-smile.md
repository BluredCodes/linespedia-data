---
title: "The Last Smile"
writer: "Einstein on Crack"
slug: "the-last-smile"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/31271-The-Last-Smile-by-Einstein-on-Crack"
---

When I reach my deathbed
My smile will be preserved
Because in life I found a love
Much more than I deserved
To find that special lover
Who fuels you with desire
That never seems to ebb away
A true eternal fire
She is my one true soul mate
Someone who shares my dream
Who takes me to the places
Where I have never been
I must thank my Angel
For leading me the way
To find that special lady
Who from my heart won’t stray
That’s why upon my ending
A smile will be there
Caused by all the happiness
I felt beyond compare
