‘Twas on the day
I lost my life
The check-out line
From trouble and strife
My soul did rise
From its mortal frame
To my surprise
I seemed quite sane

Leaving my body
When I did
My Earthly demise
To my eyes were hid
The gradual rise
Through the tunnel bright
Made the journey
Feel so right

The calm arrival
At Heaven’s gate
A sharp contrast
From my mortal state
Led gently by angels
To a viewing room
Then to wait
To an angel's tune 

My life’s work
Shown in colours
The pain and hurt
I put on others
To feel the pain
I put them through
To make it right
I could not do

I also felt the happiness
The joy and hope I gave
Sadness of my mortal death
Should die within my grave
The pictures left
My soul was clean
The next step on
Seemed like a dream

Angels led me
Through the gate
To a place 
Then asked to wait
The beauteous glow
Of Heaven’s light
Surrounding all
But not too bright

Before my eyes
The scenes unfold
The story of Heaven
In tales are told
A beginners guide
To Paradise
Do not ignore
The good advice

The old life over
A new one started
What awaits me
And my departed
What kind of time
Does Heaven keep
What will I do
With my ‘Eternal Sleep’
