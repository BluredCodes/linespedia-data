Challenge Description: Write me a poem about the war in Afghanistan, but write it from the opposite view point than you hold. Don't cheat. I'll know. (50 points)

The piccy is 'sick' but in keeping with the challenge ... no offence is intended to any one ... Andy

I'm fighting for my one God 
The one that I hold true 
Because it's what I'm here for 
It's what I have to do 
The path is clearly written 
I must face my fate 
Running with my destiny 
For which I cannot wait 
I'll face up to the infidel 
To drive them from our land 
Fathers, sons and brothers 
Together we will stand 
No matter what they do to us 
We'll stay until the end 
They have no right to be here 
My country I'll defend 
Bombs and guns don't scare me 
Fate is what I fear 
Not defending 'the true way' 
Will cost my family dear 
So off I go to battle 
I'll fight with heart and soul 
I may not be a soldier 
Though freedom is my goal 
Fighting off oppressors 
Who do not know our way 
Soldiers of the nations 
Defenders of decay 
Looking back at my family 
Knowing I do right 
The smiles I see them wearing 
Fills me with delight 
They know whatever happens 
A hero I will be 
By risking life and injury 
I'll win eternity 
For that is what the enemy 
Does not understand 
For we are true, proud people 
Who'll die to save our land
