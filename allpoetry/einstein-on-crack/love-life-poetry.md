Love, Life and poetry
All mix into one
Full of rich emotion
Tasks to overcome
Souls enriched
Hearts a-fire
Written words
Which may inspire
Angst filled pages
Echoes of life
Hurtful feelings
Cut like a knife
Beauty unbounded
Visions so pure
Words that are healing
Thoughts that can cure
Read all you want to
The guidebooks are there
Written in poems
From joy to despair
