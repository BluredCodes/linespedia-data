Hand in hand, the future lies
Time is our real friend
The feelings that we share as one
Will take us to the end
Our love was born of friendship
Beginnings tried and true
My world became a better place
Once that I met you
Feelings grew between us
Now our worlds are one
Taking steps together
Although the race not run
We’ve still got cause to nurture
What we feel inside
Constantly still learning
Our fears we shouldn’t hide
Happiness engulfs us
We need to take our chance
Friends, Lovers, Partners
Sharing in life’s dance
A bright and rosy future
Is waiting for us both
Working hard towards that
To you, I give my oath
I know you love me deeply
My views are just the same
So let’s go forward step by step
And let our true love reign
