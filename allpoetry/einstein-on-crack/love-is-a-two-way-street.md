Love is a two-way street
It must be given
As well as accepted
Too many times in our lives
The love we wish returned
For a moment of need
That has been previously given
Is not reciprocated
Some people have found it
Two-way traffic
On a road to happiness
There are those who are still travelling
Down one-way systems
On the wrong side of town
Looking for the right turning
That will bring them the joy
That they richly deserve
It’s there – Somewhere
No map will guide the way
For those of us searching
We have no route-finder
Searching down blind-alleys
Praying that this time
Our path is true
Getting our hopes dashed
Then suddenly!
Crash!
Stars collide!
Heaven appears
Happiness complete
But that isn’t me
I’m still driving
Down roads and alleys
The tank is still full
Waiting …
Hoping …
Praying …
Stop!
Wait a minute
What’s this?
Another road to try …
Maybe this time
Maybe not
Let’s give it a try
