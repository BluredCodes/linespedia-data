The intrigue that inspires us both
Burns deep inside our souls
Reasoning deserting us
Images of passion overwhelming
Mistress, Mother, Wife and lover
Picture and thoughts transpire
Rational thinking disqualified
Eroticism abounds
Thoughts and dreams
Revolve around you
Whilst awake and when asleep
You’ve entered my soul
And my brain
Awakening long dead desire
The burning desire you feel
Is present inside of me
Can reality match fantasy?
Only one way can it be found
Would it end in disappointment?
Or heaven’s high reached?
The game’s afoot
We’ll play it though
Maybe someday
We will find
Truth or consequences
Can be fun
But also contrived
I’ll leave the rest up to you
No easy choice to make
In your heart of hearts
You know friendship will survive
So it’s over to you
Make up your mind
Whatever you choose
I will be kind
