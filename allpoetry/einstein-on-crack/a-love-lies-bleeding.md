title: "A Love Lies Bleeding"

The bitter words I heard tonight
Ran round my head, my chest felt tight
A dagger plunged deep in my heart
When did this deception start?
A love so special, you’ve destroyed
I’m too distraught to be annoyed
How could you act so deviously
Not taking love so seriously
My heart left damaged, out on the floor
As you left, you closed the door
You didn’t listen to my pleading
Now you’re gone, a love lies bleeding
