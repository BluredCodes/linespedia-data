---
title: "Starlite"

The blood red sky
Fades to gloom
As night comes down
The stars they bloom
White glowing lights
The shimmering grace
A bright white moon
In a cloudless place
The dark black curtain
Contrasts the scene
A picture postcard
Where no man has been
