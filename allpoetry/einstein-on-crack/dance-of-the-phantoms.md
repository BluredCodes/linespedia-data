title: "Dance Of The Phantoms"
writer: "Einstein on Crack"

Toe to toe
Body to body 
Gently rocking 
Swaying ....... 
Embracing ....... 
Rhythmic and sensual 
Engulfing ......... 
Enraptured
Bodies entwine ....... 
Enveloping 
Minds dancing....... 
On waves of ecstasy ...... 
At pleasures that will be ...... 
Crashing on shores of passion 
Lost moments in time 
Thunder crashing overhead 
Lightning strikes 
The earth rumbles 
Stars collide 
The light show begins 
Exploding into oblivious splendour 
Louder than the roar of a cannon 
The ebb and flow of the music from the soul 
Then peace ...................
Release 
Silence of passions ardour 
Sweet and sated 
The moment when heaven and earth merge 
All is right with the world 
Accented by a pumping heart 
Tingling soul 
Warm breath on heated skin 
A place where words are unable to reflect 
Emotion experienced 
Physical feeling felt 
Heart exposed 
Laying open .... raw 
Yet glowing 
Where the tender touch sends shivers o'er the senses 
Cradled, safe and at peace...at last 
Until the world once more returns to envelope us in reality 
Praying that we can return here again together 
Again to embrace the dance ...
