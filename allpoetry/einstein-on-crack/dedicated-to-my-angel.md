This is for my angel
The one who keeps me sane
Who comforts and inspires me
And never lays down blame
She's always there to offer
Everything I need
Even words of warning
From her I always heed
Truly Heaven's angel
Because she never scolds
Even as I mess up
As my life unfolds
Heaven must have sent her
She's loving, true and kind
Helping out with problems
When I'm in a bind
We all have OUR angels
I know that I've found mine
If you havn't found yours yet
You're certain too in time
