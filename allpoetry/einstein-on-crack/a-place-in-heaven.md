(A Eulegy To No-One and Everyone)

There is a place in heaven
Set aside for you
With people up there waiting
Friends and family true
Guided there by angels
Your journey they will ease
So when you reach St. Peters’ Gate
They’ll hand you o’er the keys
For you to meet your loved ones
Who’ve reached that special place
Also meeting close friends
The type you can’t replace
Those you’ve left behind here
Will miss you that’s for sure
Legacies you left with us
Make memories endure
