---
title: "I Am Me"
writer: "Einstein on Crack"

I am me!
I know me
I know want I want
I know what I feel
I need for nothing
I want for nothing
I am happy
I take what life gives
I take what I need
I am me!
I share my happiness
I share my sadness
I give support
I accept support
I am human
I make mistakes
I can get hurt
I can hurt
I can love
I can be unloved
I am me!
