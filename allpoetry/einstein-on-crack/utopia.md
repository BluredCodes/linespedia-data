writer: "Einstein on Crack"

Wouldn't life be great
If we all lived in Utopia
No violence or tension because of race
Hate and persecution - Non-existent
Joy and happiness in abundance
Poverty would not exist
Race, Creed, Colour or Politic
Causing no problems
Everyone gets the food they need
Fat-Cats would not be
Wealth would be shared by all
Crime would not happen
Life would be perfect
Utopia is a dream
Unrealistic in our world
Humans are emotional
Need and greed go hand in hand
Life is balanced between good and bad
You need one to know the other
Thats why it's merely a dream
It's simply not possible
As much as we would want it to be
Improbable .... Impossible ...
So let's take the best bits
Try to send our message out
We are one on this planet
Let us love, share and live in peace
At times it will be tough
But let's not hurt one another
Why not try talking it through
Violence is compounding problems
Crime will never dissipate completely
Until greed is forgotten
I don't think that will happen
Utopia is a dream
If we all dream hard emough
Maybe some of our ideals
Will happen for everyone
Peace can then reign
A farewell to arms
Would be heavenly
Even then we would see
Our dream is beyond reach
Half-way there would be a blessing
So let's keep the dream
Keep on dreaming for perfection
To see where it takes us
Seeing how good we can make it
Without reaching Utopia
