As I lay here quietly
Contemplating my thoughts
Memories of my life
The ups and downs
Highs and lows
Opportunities lost
I see where I am
Where I’ve been
Dreaming of pastures new
Here I lay in this
Sea of Tranquillity
Rebuilding and learning
Pondering ….
Do I need anything else?
Why should I spoil
This happiness I have
For uncertain dreams?
Should I leap once more
Into the unknown?
Searching for greater happiness
When I already have some?
The peace that surrounds me now
Will help me choose ….
