Said the angel to the poet
You haven’t written much
The poet said I’m not inspired
I think I’ve lost my touch
A swift reply the angel sent
Listen to your heart
There’s people waiting out there
For words that you impart
Thinking for a second
On what the angel said
Thoughts they ran around and round
And filled my soul with dread
I gave the words a second thought
That sweet angelic voice
Maybe I should listen
I didn’t have a choice
Let people know they’re not alone
With their trials and woes
Tell them of the passion
When true love overflows
I’ll carry on my heart-felt thoughts
For those who wish to hear
Just for me – My comfort is
My angel’s always near
