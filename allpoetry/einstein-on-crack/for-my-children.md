For David And Lisa ... I love You****************I'm not the perfect FatherThats what I've never beenThere's one thing that I hope forYou're ready for life's unseenI've always gave you freedomSo you can cope with lifeEven through the hard timesWhen the family went through strifeI know that I live far from youAnd I don't see you muchThere always is the telephoneFor us to keep in touchTo my Son, who is the thinkerI see you doing wellMy pride in your achievementIs more that words can tellFor my Daughter who's a MotherYou also make me proudYou've learned from all life's lessonsMistakes - You are allowedI wish that I could be thereEvery single dayTo help you and support youIn every kind of wayThat just isn't possibleWith the life I leadAlthough I know I'll be thereIf ever you're in needSo once more for my childrenI hope you realiseYour deepest hopes and special dreamsWhen reaching for the skiesMy thoughts are ever with youYou're never far from awayMost of all - I Love You BothThere's nothing more to say**************************Copyright © Andy..Harding
