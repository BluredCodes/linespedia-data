title: "I The Druid"

I the Druid
Call on spirits from the air
I the Druid
Call to spirits of the earth
I the Druid
Call to spirits of fire
I the Druid
Call to the water spirits
Come feed our land
Enrich our soil and lives
Give us a bountiful harvest
So we may be strong for you
I the Druid
Call upon my people
To offer thanks for our blessings
I the druid
Call upon my people
To work hard
So our spirits work is not in vain
I the Druid
Call upon my people
To offer what is ours to our spirits
I the Druid
Wish us all prosperity and health
The ability to overcome obstacles
So we may return again
To give thanks once more
I the Druid
Wish you farewell
Until we meet again
