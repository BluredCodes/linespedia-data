---
title: "My Angel & Me"
writer: "Einstein on Crack"
slug: "my-angel-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/28878-My-Angel---Me-by-Einstein-on-Crack"
---

There's an angel watching over me
That much - I can tell
When I need some good advice
Her words I'll hear her sell
She leads me to my future
To where my life should be
Through my deepest, darkest hours
Helps my eyes to see
Always ever close by
Never far away
When I need my angel
She's there both night and day
To take me to the future
Full of joy and hope
Making clear my vision
Well within my scope
Her ever present aura
Feeds me when in need
This will always happen
Until my soul's been freed
Even when that happens
She won't be far away
Because I know - She's with me
Until my dying day
