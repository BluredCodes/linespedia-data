Lost souls and happy lovers all 
Living in the same world
United under the human race
Colour, creed and language 
There are no bounds
We’re all the same inside
Emotional suffering and pain spare no one
Love and happiness is there for everyone
Our lives complicated by cosmetic unrest
If everyone were to be happy and loved
Maybe there would be a little more peace
This can cure not everything
But a minor step in the right direction
Learning to deal with our inner mind
With our relationships towards others
One big step on the road to harmony
This builds from inside of us
If you can’t love yourself
Who can you truly love?
Spare a thought for the lost souls
Sending our love out to them
So they may find peace and contentment
We’ll change the world later ….
