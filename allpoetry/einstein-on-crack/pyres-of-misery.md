title: "Pyres Of Misery"

Burning seas of misery
Scattered ‘cross the country
Livelihoods eaten by flames
Charred carcasses litter the landscape
Plotting the March of an invisible enemy
Spreading silently-The reaper moves on
Confusion and fear in its pathway
Death and destruction in its wake
Scarring our green and pleasant land
Its relentless campaign rolling on
Not knowing when to stop
The country brought to its knees
By the silent assassin
Rampaging through the masses


(Inspired by the Foot and Mouth Crisis in the UK which started Spring 2001 and was only declared free recently .... Livelihoods and whole areas have been decimated both financially and socially from this invisible and indiscriminate disease)
