Like a place viewed through opaque glass
Silhouettes litter this twilight world
The Creeping mist encompasses all
Creating the effect of a Ghost-Town
It’s eerie silence and dampening feel
Like a classic gothic scene
Unseen voices break the silence
They come and go – unobserved
Your range of vision decreases
As the curtain grows quietly thicker
Enveloped in an uneasy cloud of white
Moving faster towards your destination
The fear inside growing as your vision decreases
Streets and sights you know – their presence unnerving
Made strange and scary by the encroaching veil
You reach the doorway and enter swiftly
Switch on the lights – I’m Home! – Safe and sound!
