All who walk the labyrinth of life
Some get hurt by the trouble and strife
The select few find a gilded path
On the Road to the fatted calf

The ones who battle their whole life through
Should give the chosen an open view
Of life’s cruel torments that they bear
Or would they even bother to care

Do life’s elite pause on their journey
Stop and think about Tom, Dick or Ernie
Those who reach the Utopian Goal
Do they bother to search their soul

Who really understands the race that’s run
What price to pay for those who’ve won
Could they have contemplated life’s true cost
For those who tried and sadly lost

Those who try and come off worse
For you I dedicate my verse
To the few who reach their grail
Spare a thought for those who fail
