title: "Sparkling"

Like jewel encrusted waters
The sun lights up the sea
Gems that are so precious
Yet they cannot be
For they are illusion
Mother Nature’s gift
You’ll never capture these gems
If you cast your net and drift
The bright white sparkling diamonds
That float upon the scene
Look like they’ve been crafted
Out of sweetest dream
As the day gets older
The sun begins to set
But Goddess of all nature
Hasn’t finished yet
Her glistening jewels are changing
In colour and in hue
As the sun get lower
You get a different view
Of red and gold and amber
Caused by sinking sun
Chinks of light then flicker
Night time has begun
