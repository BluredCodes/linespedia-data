I’ve been on the other side
And looked on over
To the place I saw
Where the grass was greener
Life wasn’t too good
Where I was standing
So I set off to leave
Where the grass is greener
The journey was long
It didn’t look far
Then I reached the place
Where the grass was greener
I arrived from my quest
This place seemed familiar
Looking around this place
Where the grass is greener
To my surprise and disdain
My life did not change
Maybe should have stayed
Where the grass WAS greener
