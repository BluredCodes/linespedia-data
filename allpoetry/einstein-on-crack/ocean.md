A deep blue ocean
Azure in hue
Looks so inviting
It's welcoming you
To enter its waters'
Pristine and clear
Yet teeming with life
That lets you draw near
The fish swim so close
To Humans who swim
Down to their world
A planet within
Rich coral landscapes
Plant life so rich
Palettes of colours
In a deep water ditch
This sight's so tranquil
But do not forget
There are hidden dangers
Which just maybe met
A danger that missed
Because of the scene
Gazing at beauty
Dry Land's never seen
To appreciate life
In all its disguises
On land, sea and air
It's full of surprises
The ocean so clear
Life so extreme
It's hard to take in
Nor is it a dream
