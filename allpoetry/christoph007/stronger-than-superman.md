---
title: "Stronger than Superman"
writer: "Christoph007"
slug: "stronger-than-superman"
source: "AllPoetry"

Leap tall buidlings
Is not what he can do
To be a real life hero
Is job filled by very few

He can be hurt
He can even cry
He can be stopped
He can even die

But what makes him a hero
Is something so small
Knowing all this himself
He is not afraid to fall

Superman can't be brave
Harmless is his trait
But this man can be killed
You can easily seal his fate

But he goes out anyway
Stares death in the face
With no special powers
To evil he will give chase

He wears no shiny cape
He wears no dark mask
He simply doesn't give up
On his life long task

So if you ever kept going
When the end looked far
Remember those heros you loved
Will never be as strong as you are.
