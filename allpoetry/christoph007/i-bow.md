I bow to you
And no other
You are still my queen
And forever shall be

Patience may be gone at times
But loyalty is never out of sight
And shall ever be your shield
And I will be your sword

Forgive me
Forgive you
Friends forever
Heroes to each other
These will be our actions

Eye to eye we may not see
But heart to heart we still are
And will not be broken
I bow to you

And as I bow to you
I can see you secretly
Bow to me
I just didn't look hard enough

We bow to each other
Come what may
I shall be loyal to you
Till my dying day
