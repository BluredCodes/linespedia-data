---
title: "The Existence of the Beast"

Dripping with sweet air
The two lovers dance
In the forbidden circle
Shout out to the moon
Fears arise in one's heart
Let loose the devil
Is not an option
Strength in two
Is better than one
Passion is not a monster
Who's existence can be stopped
It is what we are
It is the fire that burns
Within the depths of our souls
Torn and wet
Burned and dried
Fight the night
Let it not overtake you
You have the power
Dream the dream
Where lovers fly
And darkness hides
Sweet somber innocence
Is all in the eyes of the child
Monster?
Demon?
Beast?
No such animal draws breath in your heart!
