Jade be thy name
In tulips love we dance
You smell the burning fire
Of sweet innocense
You dine on the rich's expense

I am coming for you Jade
For your darkness
Is my truth

I am coming Jade
If tomorrow be thy name
You shall sing it
In the rising dawn

Touch the wet burn 
Of your desire
For it shall devour you
And then you shall rest
In it's womb
