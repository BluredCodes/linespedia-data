The beconing of sweet fear
Is not upon their lips
For they know not darkness
But only truth and light
They serve but one
One above all high
Strength of thousands
Are no match for them
Grab the sword of forgiveness
Pick up your shield of mercy
And take arms to the call
That is our order

We are the Knights of the Order
Hope is our ally
Fear, hatred, darkness
We repel such animals
We shall bring you love
We shall bring you faith
We shall bring you him
The one on high
His love
Is your survival!
