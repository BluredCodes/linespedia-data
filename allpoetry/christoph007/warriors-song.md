Do you see your power
Do you see your truth
The world becons you
I shall help you answer
For the fire of light
Is your true strength
You know not what great things
You are set forth to do

But the warrior can cry
The warrior can die
But just take my hand
And I will help you fly

Darkness is not truth
Truth is only you
Find that light
That is your fire
Rage against the fight
Do not fight alone
Look to the shadow
For I am right there
Your silent friend
Your eternal companion

Though the fight may be hard
And the night might be long
Together you and I will stand
And we will sing the Warrior's Song.......
