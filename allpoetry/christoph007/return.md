A shadow of a boy
You once knew is upon you
His smile can almost be seen
But the dark still conseals it

SHOUT OUT!

For once upon a time
There was a boy
That you knew so well
But he was lost
Now its time for him
To make his return
He struggles to appear
For he knows not how
To be truly seen

Who is he?
Tell me!
Who is he to you?
And what place does he have
In a world that seems to have no vacancies?

Laughter and heart
Smiles and jokes
Innocense and depth
Do these make up 
Who he is
or is it more?

He loves you
Did you know?
For he loves his two young warriors
That act beyond their years
He loves his leader
No matter what the circumstances may be
He loves his pool partner
Though she triumphs over him at a often rate
And he certainly loves his Empress
Despite whatever differences they have

Perhaps it is not things that make up the man
But rather people that do
So now he must make his return
That smirky school boy
That witty treasure cat face
That you grew to know and love

RETURN RETURN RETURN
