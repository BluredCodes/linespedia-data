---
title: "Eternal Secret"
writer: "Christoph007"

It has me now
Sweet smelling innocense
Fresh as morning dew
I can feel it inside
That burning
That hunger
And I obey
What other choice do I have
I stand by the river
With my body in flames
For I know not the temptation
That is the burden of my soul
Redemption is not an option
For it is only one path
On a highway of heaven or hell
I smell you now
My thrust is upon you
And you are within my grasp
Fight me not
For you will only sink deeper
Into my waiting arms
For it is your prison
That you long to be locked away in
And now you are mine
The darkness hides
Our elusive and dangerous entanglement
And in the end
There is only silence
To hide our eternal secret, my love
