In shines the light
From darkness's womb.
I embrace it with love
And it's spirit is my tomb.

The light is so bright
To it I answer to
Wisdom and power it shows
And a life I never knew

Now I am the light
And I shine so true.
I am looking for one in darkness.
Perhaps that one could be you.

Christopher David Tripp
