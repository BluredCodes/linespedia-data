---
title: "Eyes of the Angel"
writer: "Christoph007"

I rage against the night.
It is the fool of dreams.
It holds no answers for me.
I rage and scream and fight.
For my sword is buried deep
Deep inside my heart of justice.
I am coming for the night.
I am it's only companion.
For it knows not the woes.
Of man's true evil.
Who knows what shadows.
Can hide in the depth of their womb.
I am sorrow's cry.
I am pain's enemy.
I am greed's payment.
I am the liar's truth.
I am the end of the enemy.
For I am the eyes of an Angel.
