title: "Hallowed Ground"

Tread reverently upon this hallowed ground,
Where the blood of valor can be found.
 
Here innocent brethren died.
For every creed and race we cried.
 
Their hopes and dreams in ashes cold
As we watched their world explode.
 
Brave are the families of those lost,
Wives and husbands now tempest tossed.
 
Their sacrifices will not be in vane,
For Liberty and Freedom will once again reign.
 
Tread reverently upon this hallowed ground,
This sacred land where tyranny will be bound.
