Reminiscent of a tin-type image		 Sun streaming copper beams		Across a dream soaked field						Flowers ripple in the warm breeze.	Butterflies and bees drip like jewels.Praying Mantis offers up petitions. Specks of pixie dust wisp gentlyVivid pansy petticoats gently swishSuch days are fables in the making.
