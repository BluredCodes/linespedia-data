Amid a flowering garden along a country lane . . .The Fairies held a carnival upon a fragrant summer’s eve.  Arriving luxuriously upon Dragon Fly limousines.Under a canopy of fireflies they frolicked and they played.  A stately group of Indian Pipes formed a stunning colonnade. 	Sliding down Rose Petals to land in gentle Buttercups, Then springing forth to swing along the Ivy entwined above.  Mushrooms made the most delightful trampolines While Bleeding Hearts formed-up merry little swings.	They took romantic snail rides around a reflecting pool While crickets serenaded with their chirping little tune.They feasted upon blueberries, raspberries, and tender sweet peas.  Then washed it all down with dew from the trees.Throughout the silken night they laughed with joy and with delight.  Then, gone were they with the morning’s first ray of light.
