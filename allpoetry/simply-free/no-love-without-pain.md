I thought being with you forever was my destinationAll it was, was just plain infatutionI think the reason I'll love you forever Is the simple fact I've never felt this way beforeYou made me realize I was so blind and nieveThat there is nothing left here for meWhy couldn't you have proven me wrongTell me you loved and needed all this timeEven though you gave me tears and painYou gave me the most amount of happinessFor that...I never could love another moreI chopped my really long poem, tell me what you think of it all now. 2001'
