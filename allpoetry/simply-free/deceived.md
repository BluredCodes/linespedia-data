I don't know why you treat me this wayFirst I am nothingThen I am your allWas this all a game Or is your mind out of controlYou seemed so sweetThen you prove me wrongWhy can't you see You needed me all alongI can't fight itIt's like a bad dreamWith no where to runExcept back into the armsThat I had come fromAnother about my only love... feel free to comment.
