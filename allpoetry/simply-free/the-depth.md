title: "The Depth"
writer: "simply free"

I am buried in you breathI am warped into you mindI am so twisted in this painBroken and BleedingWaiting and BelievingHoping for your diluted lies Drowning in my tearsLeft in this terrified nightmareMy heart is beaten and batteredMy soul is dime and darkNothing from youNothing for meHelpless and SufferingHidden in this blurred sceneI am trapped here with youWith no where no run Without you by my sideBlinded and boundTo all the fakness you bareCruel and Empty Is what you are to meBut I'll be forever yoursEndlesslyPlease comment my poem. '2001'
