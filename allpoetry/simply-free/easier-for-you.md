Filled with a bit of sadness,
But only showing you
What you want to see,
Hoping to make you feel
A little more a ease 
For what you have done to me;

Trapped in this pattern,
Knowing that nothing will ever change
If I stay here in this miserable game;
You say everything that I long to hear,
Very next instant taking it all back,
Leaving me so very confused;

I never meant to love you, as much as I do,
Do you think I like all this pain?
All of which you put me through,
You don't know how many times I wish I could take back,
All this love I put out there for you
But life without you is what I lack;

All that is left here
Is the hurt and pain you sent me
I just can not see
Why I beleived so deeply
You'd stay near til the end
Knowing very well, by my side
