title: "To Much To Take Back"
writer: "simply free"

Shattered Heart,Broken Dreams,Is all that is leftFrom the love I gave to thee;Why I love you so?I expected so much more,After throwing my heartInto the pits of hell for youI thought maybe you would see;'See' what you ask?Maybe that you do, Care for meAs I have always Cared for you,More?That you love meThe way I imaginedLove always would beOr even to love me at all,Do you still declare more?Or have I given you allAll that which I neededThere is no way,No way at allFor you to make upFor all of the pain All of the tearsThere is no way To rebuild my heartAfter it has been brokenShattered onto the floorWhere you sweeped it upWhile looking into my eyesAnd so easlyThrowing my love and my heart awayThis was the hurt emotions I felt while my only love broke my heart so many times. Comments please!! '2001'
