I've wished for your love
I longed for your touch,
I never thought I'd love you so much
It's hard to hide the truth
Especially written 
All over my face;
Your every move makes me smile
Your every touch makes me weak,
But the truth of how you feel
Is heard in your every word;
You tell me you don't love me
That you only care for me
Though you can ripe my heart apart
Never thinking to how it makes me feel;
You took me for gantit
And thought that was ok with that;
I could never stop loving you
Though you'd never love me in return,
I could wish for a thousand things
The one thing I long for 
is... for one last simple kiss
bit that would leave me asking...
