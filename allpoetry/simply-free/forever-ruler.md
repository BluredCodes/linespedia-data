I may not be letting it show
Deep down you should already know
That I hold you very dear to my heart
For there you left your mark
I wish I could put you in my position
Hoping for the next opposition
Instead I hope there was a place I could live
Where you would give me everything you could give
I still catch myself waiting for the sweet things you never swore
I think that is what will keep me here alone ever more
Desperate to keep you here
I think I will lose you, and that is just what I fear
My future in the hands of destiny
Inside I know that all that has happen was all that was best for me
I wish I could be so very cruel
But My heart is what you'll forever ruleI kept this last bit the same. Please comment on all of my poems and tell me where to improve. Thank you. '2001'
