The thoughts of love rush through my mind
Trap in this illusion with no where to hide
I can't find the reason to why my heart keeps me so blind;
If there was a dream left here to achieve 
It would be the dream of you at last loving me
To know why my heart beats only for you
Would be the very reason to my endless misery
Your love is needed here
My heart can't beat without you near
My life seems to have no reason 
But to stay here believing you care
I can't let go, I am to far gone
With all your fakness you lead me on
Even after through all the pain and tears
I still believe that it was all so real
All of which you made me feelThis poems is about the only person I have ever loved, and loving them even if they had no intention of loving me in return. Oh feel free to comment on my poem. '2001'
