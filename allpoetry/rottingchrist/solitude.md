I walk alone, this lonely path,
Born without being asked,
No one knows my true self...

Wake up every morning,
Not happy to be alive,
I hide myself in the shadows,
Until nightfall are open eyes.

Thoughts of despair,
Driving me to insanity,
But nothing can I do,
To quell my soul within me.

Who really cares about me anyway,
They don't even know me,
Tears of sorrow fall in the dark,
Suffocation I cannot breath.
