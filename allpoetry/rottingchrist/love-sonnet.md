Shall I compare thee to an autumn's day?
Beauty that runs so deep through the mountains,
Rivers catch your beauty if I may say,
Love so strong that my heart pours as fountains.

The glowing sunset lights upon your eyes,
The warmth from your face melts away my heart,
Your sweet kind smile brings life to the skies,
My love for you will last and never part.

A million colors falling from heaven,
A million wishes for the best of thee,
The cool breeze brushes across your face then,
I see you walking away out of me.

Ever lasting will be my love for you,
Until then time reflects it's course to view.Inspired by Shakespeare.
