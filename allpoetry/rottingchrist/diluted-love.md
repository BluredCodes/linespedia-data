---
title: "Diluted Love"
writer: "RottingChrist"
slug: "diluted-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

I can see your reflection off the ocean water, lit up by the pale moon in the
Sky, I see myself but no reflection, what I see is silence, silence of myself 
And no one to open my eyes to see you.

The image of you is cloudy, though the clouds look dark and sinister I feel I 
Need you. The dim light shining from your eyes will guide me to your home where
I may become strangled in your grip.

Struggling to breath, I cannot cut myself loose from your beauty.  I am forever
Trapped, enclosed by the vivid images of your mind, wanting to be released, but
Held for amusement.

The roses of your lips prick me with pain as I try so hard to forget you, 
Encircled by my blood as I lay on the stone cold ground, I whisper my love for 
You as you look down upon me, smiling as I take my last views of the real you.


Nothing is real but you, the dimensions of reality bend and perplex in shape as
I wonder across the vast open plain of nothingness.
