Truth be told in our dreams,
Honesty can be seen in your desires,
Fear touches me from afar,
I linger on hope.

The smooth embrace of your smile,
The extravagant sparkle of your eyes,
The brutal aching in my heart,
The actuality I cannot perceive.

My dream becomes a nightmare,
The truth impales me again and again,
The honesty from your lips seals my hope,
Life becomes fragile.

Frail and lost in a sea of obscurity,
Struggling to breath in the truth,
Grasping for reality,
Suffocating from your deception.

Perception becomes murky,
Silence becomes my companion,
Sensation disintegrates,
A cloak of dusk surrounds me.

One last breath to remember you,
One last thought to dream of you.
