What can be said about hope?
Hope doesn't keep a man alive,
Hope keeps a man always wishing,
Wishing for what cannot be.
Hope makes a man realize something,
It makes him realize a fantasy,
A fantasy is nothing but a dream,
A dream is nothing but hope...
Hope is nothing but pain.
