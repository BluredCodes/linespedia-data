It is in my mind day and night, 
This eternal struggle I cannot fight,
I wish that i could be released,
Let my mind go to be at peace,
Wish I could fall into this abyss,
Ever growing darkness my one true bliss,
This life was not meant to be,
Why was I born? why do you torment me?
