How I wish I could bend the structure of time
To be with you forever,
To go back to the first day I met you.
Everytime I am with you my heart beats
Faster and faster.
It's like an aura of unresistable fragrance
Luring me to you.
Everything is still as I see your beautiful
Eyes light up.
All the most gorgeous sites in the world
Could blend together in a spectacular
Array of colors and yet, not even come
Close to matching your exquisite splendor.
Sorrow seeps my veins whenever you're not near
But I mustn't weep,
For you are enveloped in my mind and just the
Very thought of you brings a tear of happiness 
To my eye.This is a poem I wrote to a chick (yea yea cheesy).
