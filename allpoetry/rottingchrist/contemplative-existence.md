---
title: "Contemplative Existence"
writer: "RottingChrist"
slug: "contemplative-existence"

I'm alone in the dark,
No one to hear me cry,
Cannot escape this reality,
My life is just a lie.

Dreams of fictional reality,
Engulf me in a river of tears,
Dreams of what cannot be,
My future will never be clear.

Contemplating my existence,
As I lie here in the dark,
Thoughts unravel in my head,
I wonder through this hellish park.
