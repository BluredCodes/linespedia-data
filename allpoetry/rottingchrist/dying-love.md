---
title: "Dying Love"
writer: "RottingChrist"
slug: "dying-love"

We are bonded by our blood,
If you bleed, then so shall I,
And the blood that runs down my body
Is so red like the burning passion
I see it in your eyes.

Your flesh against me is cold,
Cold as a blackened earth that will soon
Become of my heart.

Your kiss is like a 1000 thorns of pain
Stabbing at my dead heart.

I am saddened everytime I think of you,
I wish you did not choose to take your 
precious life.

I should've helped you in your time of need.

My mind was empty just as your heart was,
I regret telling you my true feelings,
Everday now is a cold dark curse,
Soon I will be with you and leave this gloomy place,
Your touch will be mine again,
Before it was deadened by sadness,
I will feel your love as the razor slices my veins,
So please come and get me,
I am waiting for you because I love you.
