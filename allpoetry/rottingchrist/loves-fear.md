---
title: "Love's Fear"
writer: "RottingChrist"

I am hopelessly, desperately in love 
With a beautiful girl that cannot know,
She must not know for fear of rejection
Of myself which will make my heart ache so.

Sorrow seeps my veins and spreads through my body like a contagious disease..
Destroying me bit by bit, piece by piece.. The waves of torture only amplify
In my mind as I remain in this state of stillness.
