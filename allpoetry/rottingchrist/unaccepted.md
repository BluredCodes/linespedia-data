title: "Unaccepted"

My mind will collapse as they stare at me,
Studying me with disapproving eyes,
One by one they take their passings,
Condemning me from within.

I can't flee...
I have no where to go.
I must take their glaring punishment
While I slowly crumble in the wind.

I look at the rippled water,
A million eyes staring back,
Disapproving eyes within me,
No way to mold myself right.

I can't hide...
I have no place to stay,
I must punish myself more
To make myself disappear.
