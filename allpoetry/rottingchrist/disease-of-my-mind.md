---
title: "Disease of My Mind"
writer: "RottingChrist"
slug: "disease-of-my-mind"

What is this pain I feel?
Gripping my mind so tight,
Clawing it's way inide of me,
Its soul black as night.

What is this hell I feel?
Growing through my body,
Destroying my feelings inside,
Depriving me to be somebody

What is this misery I seek?
To be alone with this plague,
You are my only friend,
You are the only one, the only way

Why do I continue to live?
I wish I could let myself go,
Why won't you let me be?
Why do I carry myself so low?

Why do I bother to fight?
There's no point to resist,
I will let myself go,
This misery will no longer persist.

Why am I still alone?
Blackness covers me everywhere,
An eerie opening appears,
There I can see Death's glare.

I am finally taken away,
Oblivious to this land
My mind turned to nothing,
My soul led by Death's hand.
