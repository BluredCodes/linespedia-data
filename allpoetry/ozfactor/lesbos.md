---
title: "lesbos"
writer: "ozfactor"
slug: "lesbos"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/45003-lesbos-by-ozfactor"
---

i. 
mnemonic reveries drift in the guise of legendry 
on melancholy wafts 
to the platonic nostalgia of the setting sun 
which tints the roaring sea in scarlet red, 
shining reflection of the transient illumination, 
gleaming lustre of hephaistos' fires 
in revering memory to aphrodite and the amazones. 

ii.
golden shine the curves 
of barren hills along the shore 
as daylight flees swiftly to unsighted lands 
and darkness settles to caress the face of the earth 
with the fingertips of vibrant hands, 
ignite the spark that sets the night aglow 
which engulfs tides of ecstasy rising below. 

iii.
through the darkness of the night, 
allied protector of unspoken truths, 
pierces the groaning sigh of unchartered seas 
as a thousand eyes gaze golden from cloudless skies 
down upon the forbidden sight 
which hides in wild commotion from the tarnished light 
to shell the brazen sands in enraptured bliss. 

iv.
to the soft caress of the glimmering sea 
the seething shore sighs in treacherous hiss 
in anticipation of the simmering summernight's kiss 
as the ocean waves with floating tongues 
moisten the salty lips 
of impassioned seas 
wildly like the emotions unsettled beneath. 

v. 
lured by midnight murmur, milky foam rises to the crest 
of ecstatic ocean waves 
through which drifts within the bare chest 
the rhythmic palpitation of a heart that craves 
as the brooding summer air conveys through passionate gust 
a vociferous burst of amorous lust 
as the gleaming water flows 
along the shore of the isle of lesbos.
