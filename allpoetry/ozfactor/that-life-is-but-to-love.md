title: "that Life is but to Love"
writer: "ozfactor"

that Life is but to Love, 
that Love gives Immortality, 
that i was born to Love 
but lived and loved not yet enough 

that you are mine 
and all of mine is yours, 
that but Love is Truth 
and Truth be Immortality 

that those who seek but Life 
yet know not that Life is but to love 
miss true life in all its Beauty 
and ponder the burden of Mortality 

that Life is but to love, 
that Truth is pure Beauty 
and Love the Life of Truth, 
Life in Immortality, 
that Life is but to love, 

this i promise thee.
