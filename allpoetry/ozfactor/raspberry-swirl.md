she was a strawberry blonde 
better than chocolate 
she called me shortcake 

cut to the chase 
like the icing on the... 
or something like that. 

said she didn't want it 
said she didn't 
didn't want it 
don't 
don't 
but you don't really mean it? 

wow what's the point in all of this 
and what's the reason for all that 
and this and that and this and 
and strawberry cheesecakes 
and meretricious fakes. 

taste the sweet sweet juice, 
crunch the pearls 
the pearls, the whipped cream 
whippedcreamcreamdream WHIPPED.
