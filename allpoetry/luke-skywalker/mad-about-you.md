---
title: "Mad About You"
writer: "Luke Skywalker"
slug: "mad-about-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

My love for you outshines any other love
For its purity outcompares that of the doves
How I love you no other woman will ever have
For only with you my soul soars to heaven's above

How I care for you no man will ever know
Because the way you care for me makes my heart glow
Wherever you are deep concern in me grows
Now I know I've been hit hard through cupid's bows

How I miss you no song can ever tell
For its tunes can make an angel out of hell
Memories with you lingers like a magical spell
In my loneliest days it can make me well

My love for you I offer in a mystical wreath
For only you can take away my breath
My love I give you will fade not yet
For I will love you still till life after death
