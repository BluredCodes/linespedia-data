title: "Oh Never Again"
writer: "Luke Skywalker"

Oh never again will I ever findThat flower abloom in each day's decline,With petals in crystal mists atwineAnd hues enfold in Art's divine.Oh never again will I ever seeThat blossom blest as fays can be,With a scent in forlorn hauntings pleaA yonder's quest in a murmuring lea.Oh never again will I ever findThat lovely flower blest in Art's divine;Oh never again will I ever seeThat fairy blossom is but nestled in thee.
