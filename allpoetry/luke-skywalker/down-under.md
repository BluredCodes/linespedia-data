title: "down under"

down under
darkness is what i see
around me, embracing me
deep within me, senses of awakening
are ready to squeal a new beginning

endless days passed before me
yet here in this creepy world i laid still -- waiting
i can hear faint voices above me
crying and moaning -- all grieving

arcanum silence came and wrapped me
it was so loud it almost stunned me
i screamed, but nothing came out of my mouth
i swooned, and there i drifted to the abysmal south

then i woke from my noxious reverie
only to find darkness still gripping me
now i can feel beings crawling over me
tearing and eating every part of me

here i laid for countless days
hearing others scream of death
here in this lifeless world everyone pays
a chilly blue sanguinary death
