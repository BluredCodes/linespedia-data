title: "Shoot to Kill"

Bang...
And the birds in the trees flew;
Their wings flapping against the wind
Heading towards Ra's hue.

Bang...
I ran as fast as I could;
Fear piercing my soul,
Hiding amongst the woods.

Bang...
And it hit me hard;
Blood gushing out of my body;
My head, the Hunter's lone reward.
