---
title: "Thy Shadow"
writer: "Luke Skywalker"

My love I give thee
In ev'ry turn of the day.
Through thy heart I see;
Through thy soul I'll be;
With thee in each sunny ray.

When the morn turns gray,
With thine eyes thee cannot see.
Seek what thy heart say;
Walk and find the way;
Be not scared, I am with thee.

And when the Ra shines o'er
The placid waves of the sea.
Heed the gods aver;
Fore'er and fore'er;
Thy shadow I'll always be.
