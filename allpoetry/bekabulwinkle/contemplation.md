title: "Contemplation"
writer: "Bekabulwinkle"

Have you ever had
20/20 vision in
Hindsight.
Picked it up to late.
Knowing it but not.

Have you ever had
The Cinderella chapter
Knowing your destine for
Greatness
But stuck in a rut.

Have you ever had
So much pain
That you thought you’d
Never survive
But you did anyway.

Have you ever
loved with all your
Heart
Knowing they cant
Love you back
But you loved them anyway.

Have you ever
Distorted something
That you knew could save you
But you did it anyway.

Have you ever
Hated someone so much
That you couldn’t
Live with them
But you learnt too.




Have you ever
Fought against the tide
Sinking but swimming
Knowing that you’re different
But not caring.

Have you ever
Hidden such a secret
That it hollowed you
Out from the inside
But you kept it anyway.

Have you ever
Wanted something so bad
You thought you’d
Explode without it.
But changed your mind when you got it.

Have you ever had 
A friend who was your best friend
Until she got a boyfriend and then decided 
You just weren’t worth the effort
But she always came back when he dumped her

Have you ever
Fallen in love
With a green eyed man
Because I know I have.
