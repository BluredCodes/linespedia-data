title: "Difference of two worlds"
writer: "Bekabulwinkle"

Myself I blame – You I pity.
As I saw darkness – You saw light.
When I fought – You sat bright.
When I cried – You laughed with delight.
These are all memories of mine
The way you would smirk as I cried,
The way you loved the sight of my pain.
Now I’ve broken through that dark cloud.
I can honestly say –
You mean nothing to me
