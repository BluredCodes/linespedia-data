Here I am
Just sitting
Watching, following, stalking
I see you in the morning
In that place,
When your still sleeping
But you’re awake.
You keep me awake
Coming into my dreams
Traveling where your not wanted.
Finding all my secrets
Invading all my memories
I’m tired
So tired now
I don’t sleep
I find you wherever I go
Living off
Unknown memories
Seeing
Unknown faces
Creating unknown places.
Do you know that you haunt me?
Traveling into my dreams
Bringing nightmares with you.
I scream at night,
But no one comes.
You have opened old demons
And given’ them new light.
Fears burn so deep
They’re forgotten
Until you visit me
I want you too go
But I need you to stay
This is my weakness,
You expose my failure.
You show me myself.I cant remember,
