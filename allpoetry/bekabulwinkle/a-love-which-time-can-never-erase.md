title: "A Love Which Time Can Never Erase"

As I look back upon those years,
I should say it started in
Year eight.
My life upside down –
No one for me to love - No one to love me.
Then I saw you,
Something good came out of something so bad.
At twenty-five you were to old,
For a child of fifteen.
Your heart so exposed so open
I knew you would never hurt me, never leave me in pain.
We became so close in such a short time.
Some people made up lies –
Their imagination could conceive what was really going on.
Their lies and deceptions tore us apart.
You left me alone - crying at a wound
I knew I could never heal.
Now I search the crowds for your face –
And look for you wherever I go.
Just to hear your voice, see your face one last time.
To smile and say that I love you.
Only now I cry at night
And live in a life of memories.Have no idea,
