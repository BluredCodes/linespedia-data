I feel like I’m drowning,
Slowly falling through the merky water.
I’m gasping for air,
Struggling, clawing, wishing
To get up for air.
I can see the light fading
As I feel someone grabbing me.
Pulling me deeper and deeper
Moving to the bottom,
If there is a bottom.
Then I see them,
Slaves to the gatekeeper.
I see them all,
Their bodies blue and bloated.
It is then I accept my fate,
And then I feel them let go.
I ascend, towards the new light.
Its becoming brighter and brighter.
I wake with blood all around me.
Acceptance fades out and pain takes its place.
Here I am, lying in my own blood,
Drowning in my own life.
