Flashing lights,
Guitar riffs
Tight leather pants
Revealing neck lines
Words no longer put to paper
But twist and sway
In the dancing of the lights music
The image is more important
It enslaves our emotions
These are all parts of our
Modern day Poet

Love, hate, distrust
Anger, passion, sorrow
All tools to be used
And abused.
Teenage depressions
For all to hear
Melody and Harmonies
Developed and polished
This are all used by our
Modern day Poet

This Modern day Poet is no friend of mine
They reveal all our secrets
As they were to be shared.
Can you honestly say this was your childhood?
Did you suffer so much?
Were you always picked last?
Weren’t you loved enough?
What excuse can you come up with next?
Are you going to tell me you know my pain?
You know my emotions,
You know how I feel in the darkness of the night
Laying by myself?
All right you Modern day Poet
Polish my life for all to see
Make it happy, perfect families.
These Modern day Poets are no different from the last
