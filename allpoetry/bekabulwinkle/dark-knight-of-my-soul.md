Dark knight of my soul
Creeping inside of me.
Haunting timelessness,
closing inside this
hollowed emptied
poisoned body.
Sliced intermediates
Pounding rain of redness
Smooth lies.
Dark knight of my soul
Are you waging this battle for me?
Chiseled out fame
Silent cheers
People can’t understand
This symbiotic dependency
Meltdown this gold,
Dissolve all this rage
The emptiness had leaked into
Wondrous flights of fancy
But this sword is too sharp for me.
Our life prism seeps towards distraction.
Dark knight of my soul
Can you hear me?
Are you there?
Will you ever come and save me
