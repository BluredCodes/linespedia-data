Nothing is solved – Nothing changed
I am still the bad guy
In this scenario – just as I always am
The fall guy – to take the blame
Because it’s easier than realising
Who’s at fault.
A habit forged so long ago
We will never be able to break.

Easy targets to hurt
Easy since you don’t see
The pain you inflect with your
Careless actions – careless words.
You spill your venom and then
Leave without a care.

With each passing day - you take
From me something ohh so precious
But in your mind – there not even a second thought
I wonder if there was even a first.
Did you ever consider my feelings –?
You complain that we don’t make an effort.
But would you keep making an effort
If time and time again you were pushed away
You can only take so much
Before you come to the conclusion –
You’re just not wanted.

I always thought we would be friends forever
We would look out for each other,
I’ve been told blood was thicker than water
But now I see its not – blood doesn’t mean anything to you
I’ve been stepped on enough, made to feel inferior
Made to feel I’m not worthy enough
Not good enough – just not enough for you.

I’m not writing this to make you feel bad or
Lay a guilt trip on you
But to let you know – how I feel
So for once you can see – so you can open your eyes
Too my pain, hate and agony that’s etched upon my face
Etched upon my heart, but I think it’s getting too late
To save me, I feel like I’m going under
And I’m not too sure I want to be saved.
I don’t want to play these silly games
Politics in families – stupid pride
Is killing us all. 

So I’m asking you to open your eyes
Open your heart to this
If you don’t care about me – fine.
If you don’t care about this family – fine
But don’t take from me anymore than you already have.
Don’t keep me dangling upon this tight rope
Tell me to go away – tell me I’m not wanted
Just tell me something.this was written on nov 22 2001
