I wait in the shadows of your dream's,filled with hazy thoughts.As the moon chases the sunand the night air tastes of blood.I can smell the questas I stalk the night lifemany centuries I’ve traveled in the light of shadows.Lost immortal's thirstsas you drink from my throbbing heartand wail in the pleasures.So you shall learn the realms of orgasmic unknown’s.Tear my flesh and feast upon my empty soul;join me in eternity’s snaredoomed to the shadowsand a life lived in misery.i cant remember,
