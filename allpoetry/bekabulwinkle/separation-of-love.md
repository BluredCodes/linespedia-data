---
title: "Separation of Love"
writer: "Bekabulwinkle"
slug: "separation-of-love"

My emotions on fire,
Racing, tearing me apart.
I love you so much
I hate you.
Pushing you away
I cry inside.
Tormented mind
Of a unnoticed
Genius.
Wanting you so much
But knowing your
Just out of reach.
Open your eyes
Look past your life
And see mine.
Selfish
I know.
But do you love me?
Could you ever
Need me as much as I need you.
Ark Angels
Devils inspiration.
If I was different
If I could change my past,
Would you be in my future?
These photos are useless now,
No memories to spare for you.
Smiles pasted into place,
Undercurrents of fear,
Emotions destiny.
