Sitting here,
My stomach churning
My head spins
I have no idea
Where to start.
I’m not going to tell
You a sad story
Or bring tears to your eyes,
I’m not going to talk
Of a poetic story
Setting happiness in your heart.
I’m telling you the truth,
It’s filled with
Love and hate,
Lust and revenge
With a little extra dash of pure evil,
And a pinch of sorrow
