People all seem to stop and stare –
I tried to fit in,
Be as everyone else.
I dressed like everyone –
Spoke like everyone –
I even thought like everyone else.
Everyone, that is;
But me.
Trapped in myself.
Wanting to get out –
Silently screaming to be let free.
Then it happened – I found me.
Standing alone – crying.
Knife slicing – biting into my skin
Feeling free, freer than I ever had been.
Blood mixing with tears 
I finally found me.
