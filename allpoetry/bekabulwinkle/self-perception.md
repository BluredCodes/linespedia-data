Unique, Unfailing, Imperfect
Wannabe freak.
Friendships before Cliques
Needy, clinging, hermit.
Anti-social crowd loving show off.
Solace withdrawn complicated
Post-teen.
Fearful, bold self absorbed
Always there, helpful
Paradox.
Easy to get along with,
Hard to understand
Recluse. Clever, evasive
Need to belong, detached
Psychotic Poem writing
Dream heeding, weirdo.
