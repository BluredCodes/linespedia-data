Here I am
Just sitting – Watching – Following – Stalking
I see you in that morning place,
When your still sleeping, but awake.
You tire me so easy now days,
You float into my dreams
Traveling where your not wanted or needed.
Finding out all my secrets, talking to my memories
Distorting my life.
I’m tired now - to tired to literally care.
I find you wherever I go – Living off unknown pasts.
Seeing unknown faces - creating unknown places.
Do you understand how much you’re hurting me?
Taking all my dreams and replacing them with nightmares.
I scream at night – but no one comes.
Opening my memories – opening all my demons with them.
Fears burnt so deep – they’re forgotten.
Until you visit me.
I want you to go – but I need you to stay.
This is my weakness,
You expose my failure.
You show me myself.
