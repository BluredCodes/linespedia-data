---
title: "Puppeteer"
writer: "Bekabulwinkle"
slug: "puppeteer"
source: "AllPoetry"

As I look into your eyes
I find myself sinking
Deep within their coldness.
Between your hate
And where your twisted love decides to sit.
And I take a look around
Into the blackness of your heart
And the darkness of your soul
I search to find life.
A glimmer of the man I know you could be
But as I sink deeper into your world
I find each emotion tweaked
Taunt for the playing
And I wonder who is playing you.
You’re a sad case, no one to care for
No one to care for you.
Not a word is spoken between us
I try to smile but it comes out all wrong,
And another moment is lost.
