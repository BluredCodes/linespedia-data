With the smallest of movements
with the smallest of sounds
I know you are coming
I know you are around

twisted dreams, demented lies
can you hear this screaming
or is it in my mind
with each slice I seep with red

The tiles are cold now
under this body of mine
I float above this scene
and wonder will anyone morn for me

This pain I feel it tells me I am real
Because with all this emptiness inside
I feel like its going to swallow me
I’m full of emptiness inside

My mind starts to drift
I can see them all now
there tears as fake as their smiles
Oh how if they only knew, if I told someone it would of been all right

But who would I tell - you?
Would you of listened?
Would you of cared?
Would you of helped me?

This secret has hollowed me
From the inside out
so before I fall back asleep
I see my manifesto

My life’s work

It reveals all my lies
and your too.

He's here now
It’s my time to go
so forgive me
Because I just couldn’t stay any longer

My last breath has come and gone
And I see myself smiling
As I lay up against the blood stained tiles
I wish it didn’t have to be this way
That you didn’t have to find me.

Your screams permeated my lifeless ears
As you tried to fight the moment
But it had already passed.

Your tears 
I never thought your tears would engulf me so
Such sadness – so hollow
So it was you who mourned for me

Shame flooded over me
It wasn’t just me
It wasn’t just me who would die that night

The night drifted into day
As the police floated in and away
Yet you still stayed.

I could heard your thoughts
Your curses – your pleads
Your questions why

And I hated myself for not being able to answer them
Hold you in my arms and protect you from them.
I was I too weak to go on?
Or was I strong to end it?

The silence screamed
As I watched over you
People trying to comfort you
Tell you it would be all right.

As the sunset displayed another of its mysteries
I watched as it glinted in the fading light
And your mind was hollow with grief

Laying upon the frigid tiles 
You went to follow in my footsteps
I cried for you to stop
I screamed for you to see sense

But your ears never heard my plea
And I saw the life drain from you 
You were so close – but I couldn’t let it happen
I couldn’t be responsible for another wasted life

Crying as only the dead could do, I prayed 
I prayed to whoever was there – 
I prayed for your life
Because I knew this was not you

You are stronger than I am – You can live
As I open my eyes I saw yours
I saw the man come around the corner
I saw his cry for help – the way he held you
It was then that I knew
He was that man for you.

Time has past since that day
But I still watch over you
Watch over your children
And you know it

You hear my whispers on the wind 

I will forever be grateful to the man you now love
Because he not only saved your life
