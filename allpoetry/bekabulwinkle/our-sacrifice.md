It’s hard to say goodbye
When all you want to do is
Hold them close and never let go

It’s hard to let go of memories
That have been so distorted
You can’t tell what’s real anymore

It hard to realize that you’ll always be the ugly one
No matter where you go
You’ll always be pushed away because of your face

It’s hard to say you’re sorry
Because it’s admitting
That you’re imperfect

It’s hard to give in
When all you need is one more fight
But you have no more to give

It’s hard to love
When all your energy has been
Focused on hate

It hard to forgive
Even when you need to
So you keep on lying

It’s hard to go on
When you know you’ll
Always be alone

It’s hard to let go
Even if it means killing the thing you love
Because can we be sure it will come back

It’s hard to be left behind
But you’ll soon get use to it
I did

It’s hard to be used
Because when you give all your
Heart and soul, you’re open for all to see
