title: "Forgiveness Is Not An Option."

A year has past – Even two
The pain still rips apart my broken and bleeding heart
I yearn for just one look,
Just one quick glance.
Just to make sure he remembers.
To see the pain in my eyes
And for him to realize – he did this to me.
For him to remember that starless – rainy night
When he took my love from me
And never gave it back.
