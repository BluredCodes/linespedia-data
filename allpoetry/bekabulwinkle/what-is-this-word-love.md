title: "What is this word Love?"

Are you in love?Or are you in love with being in love?Can we really truly love?Or is it a fear of being alone?Is love a tangible thing?Can we touch it?Or is it like faith – something we just need to believe in?Does love make us who we are?Or is love just a catalyst for who we want to be?Do we have unconditional love?Or is it we just want to be in love so much we over look our flaws?Can you show me what love is?Or do I need to find out myself?Can you explain how we fall in love – How we know that person is the one?Is love monogamous?Or can we really love more than one person at a time?What is the difference between loves?I can say I love you and mean it with all my heart –But then turn around and proclaim my love to another.Why does love hurt so much?Why do people play with love?Why is it used as a power, a weapon?Can you sell love?Can you fake love?Can you tell me about this enigma?Or is it something you must immerse yourself in?Let it engulf you so fully that you lose yourself in it?Can you tell me what love is?I can not remember when this was written sorry,
