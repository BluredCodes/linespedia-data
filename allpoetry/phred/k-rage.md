Angry Man broadcasting on K-RAGE
Radio of the mind
Thrusting 100 million watts at you
               poor sucker
               infected with the repression virus
that won't let you act human -- more like a sheep

Can't you see that your main CPU is shutting down?

I can fell your conflicted emotions
pour out of you like heat waves
your brain cells popping like kernels
turn bright red with repressive fusion
                                repressive misery

and yet you still try to suffocate the righteous message of......
Angry Man broacdasting on K-RAGE
Radio of the mind

The repression virus will be your downfall
Unless you listen.

Let the K-RAGE  sound break down the barriers, 
                                                    set you free......
