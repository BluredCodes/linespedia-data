The last time I saw you

was the day you stepped into oblivion
                stepped into obscurity

and even though the others might 
have forgotten your name
               your face
               your soul

a day does not go by, 
where my thoughts don't turn to you

And although I don't know why you did
                         what you did

I'll keep searching for an answer...........
