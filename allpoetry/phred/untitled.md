"A screaming comes across the sky. It has happened before, but there is nothing to compare it to now."


Telltale signs of --

The night sky filled with glowing trails
					  howls of rage
pushing against the wind, the stars
				
perhaps it is depserate measures
a last chance at glory
			or apocalypse

And the seconds tick away
a cacophony of wails and screeches

the surface glowing
	serious friction
	as it slides through the air

the citizens crane their necks 
in response to the spirialing chaos

and in the final seconds
there is silence, 

silence smothering one and all

and then the world erupts in white light
					    white heat.....

"Now everybody --"







--Quotes from Gravity's Rainbow, by Thomas Pynchon
