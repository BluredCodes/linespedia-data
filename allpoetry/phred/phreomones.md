title: "Phreomones"
writer: "Phred"

Does it trouble you to think of me...
...as something you desire...

...though you sit there in your  phermone suit
and slap away the drones who hover about your feet
and you act unconcerned about the ones who want to mate with you
even though your eyes are on me
and I don’t care what scent you give off
because I know the consequences of mating with you
I know all about your lethal sting...

I have your attention...
your phermones aren’t working on me
it’s the raised eyebrow and the corner stare 
giving you away

and I can move in any direction
maybe towards you,
or far away...
I’d see you slink after me
using all your wiles to entrap me 

You don’t understand what’s happening to you
do you?
it’s all about pheromones, you see...
and mine are stronger than yours
and you won't realize that until it's too late....
