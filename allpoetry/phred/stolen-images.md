title: "Stolen Images"

Stolen images
tiny fragments of vision
patterns of color and shape
coming together, falling apart

muffled noises under the haze
	a cry, a piercing note

the scent of cinnamon
a whiff of your perfume

the touch of silk against skin

only unsure of where I am
underwater, in the darkness of my mind....
breaking the surface
dragged down by my own demons

my own undoing
a personal disintegration
I feel the splinters pulling away
leaving only vague sensations:

stolen images
remembered scents
soft caresses
plaintive cries of hope.....
