---
title: "In My Head"
writer: "Phred"
slug: "in-my-head"
source: "AllPoetry"

In my head, I see myself as lonely, 
Tattered self image dangling from a pole.
Unsure what is to become of me, my life, my mission

In my head, I see you as prey,
frightened, hamstrung, defenseless
and I am the beginning of your end

In my head, I see them as fools,
jumping through hoops like trained animals
Mindless thoughts, deeds, actions

In my head, I see myself as alive
true survivor of the world
with scars to prove it.

In my head, I see you as a victim
stalked by the masses
Persecuted for nothing

In my head, 
thoughts swim in a swift river,
forever colliding
forever moving,
forever drowning....
