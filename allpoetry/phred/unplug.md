title: "Unplug"

Unplug me 
	take me away from people
wanting my attention,
asking for direction,

Unplug me
	don’t want to know
why you’re upset
about your corvette

Unplug me
	disengage my mind
just want to get away
not tomorrow or  today

Unplug me
	curl inside myself
observe my own thought
cannot be sold or bought

The chattering, talking, yammering, complaining, whining, bitching,
pontificating, opinionating, declaiming, babbling, commiserating, 

I don’t need it, not right now
want some time to myself
away from people
away from the universe
away from the noise

Just unplug me......
