A parady of It's my Party.

It's my due date and I'll cry if I want to...
Cry if I want to
Cry If I want to
you would cry to if you had baby in you.

Nobody knows why this baby wont come
but I really want her right now
Oh what a pain waiting is
I want my labor right now

It's my due date and I'll cry if I want to...
Cry if I want to
Cry If I want to
you would cry to if you had baby in you.

Lets go on long walks and here drink some tea
I cleaned the kitchen last night
oh why won't this baby come
Contractions should start tonight.

oooh oh oooh oh

It's my due date and I'll cry if I want to...
Cry if I want to
Cry If I want to
you would cry to if you had baby in you.
