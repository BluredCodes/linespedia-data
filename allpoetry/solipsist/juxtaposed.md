In the window
Lies the lady
I can hear her
Say no 
In the sidewalk
Lies truth and illusions
And I 
I don't want to be with you
I don't want to be with anyone
Go away 
And leave me alone
Why?
I chose this life
I didn't choose to come here
See the couples
See the couples
I see the couples in the park
See the couples
I smell love in their hearts
OH!!
I can't feel anything at all
I LIE way too much
But so do I
Schizophrenia
In his/my head
Our head is diseased
Lock us up
Catch me like the animal he is
He'll love ya for it
I'll love ya for it
