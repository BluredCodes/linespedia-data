Just a poem 
A poem or a 
Song 
To keep me satisfied 
A piece of a rotten sandwich hanging from 
The ceiling 
To keep me from melting 
And you disappeared real fast 
Without saying goodbye 
And what you didn't do 
Lets me hang on to myself for 
Just a little longer 
Couldn't give me more than a moment 
Awfully tormented aren't you? 
I suppose I was never aware 
That life existed outside of my room 
But now... 
The song is over 
And so is this poem
