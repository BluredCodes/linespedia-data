title: "'Pieces Of Dreams'"

discarded memories of being four
believing in angels and wanting to fly
days of ballerina dreams
peanut butter and Peter Pan
wishing for my Neverland
that I would never see

no pills no tears no problems yet
my innocence as whole
as I, a little girl
with pieces of dreams
and fragments of feeling
to splinter in my mind*sigh*


i miss being little... and believing that everything would be ok
