my lethe, you are
that river from which i drink,
inside this lovely hell
your cool waters remind me to breathe
cleansing away the pain

my lethe, you are
the river in this foriegn land
solid and eternal
ethereal, though you will always be
the road to my damnnation

my lethe, you are
the sweetness of peace
the stronghold against fate
which would have me remember everything
in your waters, i forget my mortal's pain
