title: "'To Kitten, For Her Jealousy'"

Tears for father, tears for mother,
Tears for sister, tears for brother.
There was nothing left to lose
Until she came along. 

Torn from the ones I loved, 
I was lost at sea, adrift in tears,
Resigned to three more years 
Of this Purgatory.

A knife was my God, 
Death loomed like an impending bomb 
On my concience.  I was alone, 
Except for Kitten, so far away,

She, my soul's lover.  I loved her
Through a telephone wire,
Now and forever, Amen.
My lovely Lethe, my soul's mate, 

I call you Kitten with love, 
Remembering your Asian eyes, and lashes long,
Bearing no mascara. 
There is nothing left to lose except for you. 

I will lend my tears to my kin
And my smile to the world, 
But I would give them all to you.
