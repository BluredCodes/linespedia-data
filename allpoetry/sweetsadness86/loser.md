Don't want to lose your identity
You know you're halfway gone
And that you're never happy
Unless you're doing something wrong

The risks you take amuse you
Because they let you lose yourself
To the people who'd abuse you
And abandon you in hell

Stabbed in the back one too many times
Nothing's ever good till it's gone
Punishment for another's crimes
Miracles can always go wrong

All the times I tried to reach you
I knew you needed a friend
All the lies you couldn't see through
You were living for revenge

I knew every chance you got
You put your anger on parade
You didn't care who saw you 
If they weren't part of your charadei wrote this for a friend in the seventh grade. ironically enough, we have a lot in common now
