title: "'Vertigo Reality'"

life was a black hole sucking me in
raping me of dignity... 
then robbing me of faith
when your face is full of scar tissue 
and the tears are stinging your cheeks
it's hard to feel pretty

so i let my brain go in circles 
and my body was left coping with the pain
and i separated from my being
so that i could float on an imaginary cloud
where i was already blissfully dead

truth was a specter beating on my door
stabbing at my vertigo reality
taking the only sheild i had left
until acidic tears had burned it full of holes
and th blood of my wrists fled for a better place

it's easy to lose your reality
when sanity is so insane
i'm thinking that i need someone to hold onto
but the only person who sees me
is on this same ledge... arms spread. 

in a vertigo reality you can fly...vertigo reality is where my brain goes when someone is hurting me.
