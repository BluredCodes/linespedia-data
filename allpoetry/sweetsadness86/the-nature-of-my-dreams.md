---
title: "The Nature Of My Dreams"
writer: "SweetSadness86"

It feels like a dream now,
Those few stolen hours of life for living.
I want to see you again
The way you were when I first saw you
Standing there, with your ironic smile,
Bad posture and black leather.
I wanted to touch you and hear you say my name.
I want to taste you and make you feel the same.

It feels like a dream now,
As I remember that moment we met.
I want to be there again,
To go back to the way things were.
You eyed me through your long dark lashes,
Hair falling in your face like a curtain from the world.
I wanted to see you with nothing falling in your eyes.
I want you to talk to me without this disguise.

It feels like a dream now,
That I kissed you once, then twice.
My forbidden fruit, you were gone so fast.
I swear someday we'll make it last forever.
I wanted to stop the world and touch you again.
I want to turn back time just to see you again.
Amongst the world we stand alone,
But I know this is not the nature of my dreams.
