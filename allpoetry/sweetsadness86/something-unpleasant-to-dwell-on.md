I wished upon a plastic star
As far from God as God was far
Looked for something unpleasant to dwell on
To pass the days that were already gone
I'm knee deep in lies that I told myself
Sinking inside an empty hell
Of greedy eyes and empty smiles
But what could I do, there's not a soul for miles
So I ran away to a brighter place
Where everyone lied and talked at my face
Unable to see deeper than the pools
Of salty tears from shallow fools
I am knee deep in things that I haven't done
Wading in memories from when I was younger
Dreaming of the things I'd never be
But you can't remember what you'll never see
So I made a wish on a toxic flame
But it flickered out before I could say
All the things I cannot let myself hear
Cuz I'm too afraid I might have nothing to fear
As far from God as God is far
Another wish upon a fallen star
Another bitter pill I chose to take
Another heavy chain I chose to make
Another empty day inside my shell
Halfway between heaven and hell
Something unpleasant to dwell on to make the days go by
Something I told myself so that I could cry
