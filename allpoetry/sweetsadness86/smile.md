Never bothered to ask me
Just why am I so silent
A shadow in a corner
A warm body

I've been waving red flags
I've shot up my last flare
Your eyes are still fixed on the TV

Never bothered to ask me
Just why don't i smile
Nature's punching bag
Society's pincushion

I've been running through the crowd
I've been swimming against the current
Your eyes would never rest upon me

No one ever told me
That I've got a beautiful smile
Now I'm not pretty
Now I'm not real
