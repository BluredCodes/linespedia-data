Mother,
Sometimes I think
That I could return to you,
The comfort of your womb,
Days without tears,
Growth with your promise of
Warm childhood years,
Now lost.

Father,
Sometimes I think
That I could fly, faster
Than a speeding bullet, 
A bird, a plane.
If only I were not
Your fetal Superwoman,
I'd fly...
