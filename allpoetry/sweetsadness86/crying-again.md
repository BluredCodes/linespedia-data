empty words tumble from my lips... 
salty tears fall, always falling...
the excretement of a heart overful.

cut open the veins that hold this lie...
life? just another long, winding road... 
where is the broken trail?

ugly and sad in a room of mirrors...
stream of unconciousness
or the lack thereof... haunting me

another bloodied knife...
maybe this is growing,
maybe this is love?ech, take no notice of this... just me being stupid.

but i just had to go over it again
