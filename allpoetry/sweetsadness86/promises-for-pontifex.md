broken promises
empty, greet my SOS
dim my only flare

his hand extended
an endless sea can't drown out
the sound of his voice

we two survivors
tell our sad stories and hope
someone might end thempontifex and i have been exchanging IM's... he is so kind in his concern... i am from an abusive family, i am surviving... he says, in short, "Run away from the pain." such kindness...
