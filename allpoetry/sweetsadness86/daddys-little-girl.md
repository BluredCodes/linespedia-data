I used to be your starry sky
Your little girl who you adored
You used to know I was alive
Child of your seed who never cried
Once upon a time you would love
To tell me I was beautiful
I turned 13, you grew out of me
Trust and love are a fable of the past

Daddy loves a pretty girl
With no complications
Daddy still loves my little sister
But that will change when she turns 13

I used to be your pride and joy
You would show me off to your friends
Talk about my reading level
How I buried my nose in Gone With The Wind
Once upon a time I was pretty
As long as I brought you your coffee just right
I started growing up, you grew tired of me
Now that I'm not begging for ballet class

Daddy wants me to be his pretty girl
He wants me to stay that perfect age
With no boyfriends, no thoughts, no worries
No one really wants to changepossibly the nicest and most friendly thing ive ever felt or written about my father. he just didnt care after i became a teenager.
