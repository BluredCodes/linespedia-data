from the corner of my eyes, i see
life rushing past, a mile a minute
trampling the ones left behind.
fifteen years i have been
fifteen years i have seen
false beauty triumphs always, while
the glow of the midnight moon is forgotten

beginning my end, i am
lining up the pills,
two at a time. then
nirvana was vaccumed out 
of my burning, nauseous stomach

from the corner of my eyes, i watch
life tripping over its own feet
and blaming the ones left behind. 
fifteen years i have cried
fifteen years i have tried
but pain wins over love once again
and the joy of a blossoming child is forgotteni HATE it
