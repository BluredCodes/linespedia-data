If I only had the means
I would lock up these troubled screams
That tumble from my mouth,
Agonizing,
Tantalizing.

I would take this mental vertigo
Seal it up in a box,
Padlock my disease in a safe, 
Tied with the chains of faith. 

But from you, pain is love
Send it as your peacemonger dove,
Gift-wrapped with ribbons and venom.

Stop!
Stop your yelling. Does not it echo
Inside me enough?

Stop! 
Stop pretending. In this glass house
Of recycled trust.
