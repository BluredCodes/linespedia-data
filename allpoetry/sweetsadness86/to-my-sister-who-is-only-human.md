valiant, warrior of all 
that is almost unreal
haven't you learned 
that nothing is fair?
she watches hours of Disney 
but can't understand
Why no one is really 
like the Even Stevens
Haven't you learned 
that the world is ugly
That your sugar coating 
is only saccarine
That all love will fade 
in the face of strife
Haven't you learned that
The worst always happens
That it will probably 
rain on your Sweet Sixteen
Haven't you learned 
to keep your mouth shut
Keep your head down 
and learn to evolve
Don't you ever let them see you cry
If you must then lock the door

Haven't you learned 
to fight for yourself
Cuz no one else will fight for you
Haven't you learned that 
reality is not pretty
And you have to learn to deal with change
