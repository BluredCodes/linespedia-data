when did this begin? suddenly aware,
i feel for my wounds and find them
healing, for the moment. then i, 
incensed, resolve to heal, 
for a brand new god. 

when did this begin? nothing new, 
this emptiness, the wounds are
back for another year, now
that my new god has gone
back to hibernation.
