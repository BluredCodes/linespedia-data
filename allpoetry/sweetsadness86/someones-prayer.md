title: "'someone's Prayer'"

I cry, sometimes at night,
I cry too often now.
I see you in my mind,
But you never make a sound.
For something to hold on to,
I'd give up anything. 
But I'm so sick of waiting for 
What tomorrow never brings.
I wish you were here.
I remember when I cried on your shoulder,
And you told me everything would be O.K.
And you told me not to cry,
To save it for another day. 
I wish things could have been different,
Like when my world turned over...
A disturbing peice of history that used to be us
I forgot to tell you I was faking being tough.
There's something I'm missing that I can't seem to place;
Another priceless jewel that I can never replace.
A hole in my life that you used to be there
Or are you someone's prayer?
