I am a jasmine, blooming in the darkness.
I am the desert rose, the pride amongst the briars.
I am the oleander, my poison is your grief.
Cause my wounds, I dare thee, strike off my flesh and see! 
I am the Scorpio, I will grow another tail.Don't ask.
