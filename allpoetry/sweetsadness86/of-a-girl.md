a ghost inhabits an empty vessel
of a girl
she could cry tears of blood 
but she'd never stop

hide away, hide away the pain
run away, run away again
i'm not worth your time

a ghost mourns his many chains
in a girl
she could whisper all her thoughts
but you'd never know

hide away, hide away the tears
run away, run away from here
i'm not worth your time
