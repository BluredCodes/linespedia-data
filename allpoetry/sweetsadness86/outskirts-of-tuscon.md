---
title: "'Outskirts Of Tuscon'"
writer: "SweetSadness86"
slug: "outskirts-of-tuscon"

heavy on my mind
a picture of a child {woman
of unearthly beauty
that i alone can see
ghost to haunt me
in my sweetest memory}
hair falls in her face
{i want to brush it back
let the world look in your eyes
epitamy of madness and 
melancholy sighs}
she'd smile at me
{like i was someone
you made me beautiful
when i'm so far away
on the outskirts of tuscon
i wish that i could say}
everywhere i go...
{tatooed on my heart
an image of you smiling
hand inching up my thigh
silken whispers and soft flesh}
even on the outskirts of tuscon
you're always on my mind
