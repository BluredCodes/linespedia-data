This somber silenceIs as loud as your yelling of before.I hear everything you do not say.You have said it all before.The words echo in my mind,Leaving scars on my heart,Bringing tears to my eyes.At this moment,I just want to run, far far away.At this moment,I wish I could wake up and find that it was all a bad dream.At this moment,When the air around you is poison gas,And your eyes shoot bullets through my soul,I just wish I could beBullet Proof
