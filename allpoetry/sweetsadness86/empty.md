i lost my chance to find myself
that dying spark into the wind
i was looking for a better haven
large enough to house my sins
i am just an empty glass jar
transparent as the air you breathe
i know you can see right through the
lies tears scars i've come to be
the irony of nothingness
comprehending what i'm not
cling to shreds of emptiness
and lose hold of what i've got
nobody can see me
and they keep looking away
in this search for dignity
i fall flat on my face

birth to life to death to black
set back up for a new fall
another thing i cant take back
the worlds so big but i'm so small
how did i get so small?
