I never wanted this to end
So silently, or
So quickly.
I wanted a long goodbye,
To see your tears, 
For you to say what I've always wanted to hear,
For an eruption of words.
The wordsthat never came,
They could have been my ticket to something more,
To grow beyond this. 
But what are so crucial to me
Are only words to you.
