title: "'Just A Kid'"

You besiege me with commands
You don't care how I feel
With your cold and ruthless hands
Only pain is real

"Go make me some dinner, honey
Go find me some beer and the remote
Go get me something with which to hit you
So I can prove once again my control"

"Come here sweetie," he said with a smile
"Come here, give Daddy a kiss."
"Get over here, bitch," he'd said just last night
"Bend over, take your punishment."

I am hurt and no one cares
I might as well just dissapear
It doesn't matter what I say
No one is listening, anyway

I am hurt, but he doesn't care
What am I still doing here
It doesn't matter what he did
After all, I'm just his kid
I don't matter, I'm just a kid

"Now hug me goodnight, pretty girl
Tell me I'm your number one
I'm protecting you from this cruel world
I'll come to your bed when I'm done"
