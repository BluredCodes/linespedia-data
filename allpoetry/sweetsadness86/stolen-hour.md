it was  3am and all was asleep 
the world was black and cold 
only when the sun was down 
and the wind sang a mournful song 
the city stars were distant eyes 
i'd be swaying to the sounds 
the air smelled of a far-off sea 
where you are still, awaiting me 
with the breeze in your hair 
and the moon in your eyes 
we exist within our memories 
of a 3am when i felt alive 
freed from the sun's burn 
i hold our stolen hour insidesonnet. this is a challenge poem.
