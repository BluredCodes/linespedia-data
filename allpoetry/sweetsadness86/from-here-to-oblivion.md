how long is the road
from here to oblivion
where i've been hiding
waiting for you to awaken

you tell me about your god
the ultimate sacrifice, 
the ultimate sacrifice? a son?
while orphans die in the gutter
the children of no one, 
so they're not a sacrifice, i suppose.

how long is the wait
from now to oblivion
which i will spend
waiting for you to awaken

you tell me about your love
your beautiful muse? 
a beautiful muse is a crying child
with dirt on his face.
a child born of sin
so he's not beautiful, i suppose

how long is the road
from here to oblivion
how long will i wait
to see my truth awakennot only do i not know what this is about, but i dont especially like it... its so muddled. it needs work.
