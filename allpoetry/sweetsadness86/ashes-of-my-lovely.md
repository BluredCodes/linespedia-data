ashes like the pheonix
  she combusts each time

...sensual smoke curls...
  wraps her legs about my mind

such a sweet seduction
  as she roars to life

eats the barriers away
  ...we are here for your consumption...

and you rise from your ashes
  more beautiful each time

won't my goddess of fire and rain
  makes her home with me on the moon

{tonight?}
  home of silk with your sunshine

i bathe upon your skin
  curious fingers inside your firekisskiss...
