title: "'My Own Patch Of Snow'"

took a leap of faith
off a cliff
and fell on my head
on someone else's patch of snow

put socks on my hands
cuz i couldn't afford gloves
got snowballed in sleet
from barbie's backyard

running and jumping in a dirty park
the clouds were white
but the world was dark
and bleak to me and my falacies

playing in the cold
in someone else's patch of snow
snow angels and wishes 
that don't come true

when you have to play alone
in someone else's patch of snow
and the whole world is cold
i just wanted my own patch of snow
