i am watching as you aim
poisoned arrows through my heart
in your sleep, unerringly
hitting the target.
 
i am sitting about with my tears
crashing from my eyes
the end of an era if
you leave me now

i am keeping crossed fingers
hoping that we can turn time back
erase all of those things we said
if i could take back those angry words

that tumbled out of our 
lips... i'd tell you all the things
you were afraid to say
like how i feel...

with you gone the world is bleak
people call my name but i do not reply
i am watching the world from a distance
feeling like it should just stopi know i know... it sucks... but i just needed to say this... blah blah blah.
