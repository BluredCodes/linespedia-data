title: "'Paper Doll'"

Would you like me better if I read the Wall St. Journal?
Would you like me better if I were Daddy's little girl?
Would you like me better if I gave you free reign over all?
Stop trying to make me your perfect paper doll
Would you like me better if I had no Black friends, 
Asian friends, good friends?
Would you like me better if I wore frilly white dresses, 
had long golden tresses,                  
and I never made a sound?
Would you like me better if I gave you free reign over all?
Stop trying to make me your perfect paper doll
Would you like me better if I stayed 4 years old forever?
Would you like me better if I were at your beck and call
I would like myself better 
If you didn't think of me as your paper doll
So stop trying to make me your perfect paper doll
