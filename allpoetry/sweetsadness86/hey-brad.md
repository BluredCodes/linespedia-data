close your lips  
  your mind and your heart
hey brad, you've unforgiving ears 
  hey brad you turned your back

i'd tell you i'm sorry
  but i dont think you would hear
hey brad  i dont know why
  hey brad you're cold and polite

and courteous as ice
  in the middle of winter
hey brad i need to be cut open
  hey brad found something to hurt me

must be nice to hate
  but i get sad instead
hey brad i'm sorry
  hey brad where are youhey, ...
you didnt want to tell me, 
you're cold and cruel and impeccable
i almost wish you were an asshole
so i could put you down
i cant say good riddance...

what did i do?
