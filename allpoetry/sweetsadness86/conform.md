Look at me laugh at me
Point your fingers at me
tell me that I am wrong
Tell me I don't belong
Tell me I'm not worthy
To hang out with you
Conform, be normal
Don't dare deviate
Conform, be normal
Just do what we say
Talk to me about me
Point your fingers at me
tell me that I am weird
For the way I appear
Tell me I'm not worthy
To hang out with you
Conform, be normal
Do what we say
Conform, fit in
Don't dare deviate
{wear what we wear}
[think what we think]
{laugh when we laugh}
[do what we do]
