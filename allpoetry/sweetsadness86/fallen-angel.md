title: "'Fallen Angel'"

what happened to my faith 
gone like dust in the wind 
the moment that came over me 
defiance washes away all sins 
love lost and love borne 
a fleeting moment so forlorn 
so quiet in my madness? 

i don't know what i'm feeling sometimes
but i know it's not for lost 
in your pride and passion and lust 
how can you say you are saving me, 
am i your cross? bear me then, 
for i am merely wood, 
like pinnochio, i am not really real

if i am to be your cross than
you are to be chained to me, 
don't cry when they nail you up 
it was meant to be 
and god is your father, 
the one who divorced your mother 
and left you with this, i, your cross... 

pray for forgiveness and absolution 
and still i will be there, for there is
no solution, no antidote to me
