---
title: "'Pretty'"
writer: "SweetSadness86"
slug: "pretty"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/38201-Pretty-by-SweetSadness86"
---

like a leach or a vampire
i feast upon the love of another
bend you to my will
so i can triumphantly reject you
and lift myself up... to stare down at you
so you will make me feel pretty
twist the beauty into something i understand

like a chasm or a black hole
i sucked the world into my existence
let them become my substance
i see myself inside your eyes
i pull you all down to my darkness
fashion the world after my image
twist the beauty into something i understand

like your everyday rejected lover
i revel in my sweet sadness
a raucous reverie of tears
tearstained, on my knees once more
pleading for your attentions
so you will make me feel pretty again
twist the beauty into something i understand
