pisces anti popstar
said he loved us too much
a fish out of water
drowned in the undertow

needle in his veins
gun against his head
we are too alike
we were born to die

make him your messiah
staple him to your cross
pisces anti popstar
another fading flamefor prettyhatemachine

and every KC fanatic
who would glorify his weakness
and take his shit for gospel
kurt was weak and stupid for what he did
and so am i for what i've done
but hes dead now.
