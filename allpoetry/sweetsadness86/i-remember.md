I remember
When we would laugh
Untainted,
Undaunted,
We were happiness embodied,
In the midst of silent desperation.
Just the four of us, 
My sweetest friends,
Against the world.

I remember
When I felt beautiful,
This beauty lost
Without you there.
Now I'm so alone,
But never alone.

I remember
When my heart was like
An air bubble,
Full and swelling.
But now you are gone.
My bubble has burst.
