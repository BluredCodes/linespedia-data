kept a knife under my pillow
cried to it like you cry to god
replaced my soul with empty words 
that i told myself

guess that makes me defective

but i've always believed
that everything is pain
with a mask and chemicals
the kind that my brain craves

i've danced around the chasms
i've fallen in their depths
i almost pulled myself out
but my soul is caught inside

guess that makes me defective

but i've always believed 
that i was merely a doll
made of plastic, defective
missing some of my parts
