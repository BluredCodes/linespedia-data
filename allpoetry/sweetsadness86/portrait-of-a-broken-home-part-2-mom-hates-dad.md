Another day
The air is thick with
Animosity, pain
The tears of a child

She glares, resentful
She picks at her food
The silence is so loud
I heard everything they didn't say

Dad hates Mom
Mom hates Dad
Living with them is like 
Playing catch with grenades

She uses me as a weapon
They throw me back and forth
I want to cry but 
I cannot make a sound

I cannot break this silence
This cruel, ugly quiet.
But this is what life's like
A mock paradisethe mock paraidse thing... its about how childhood is supposed to be the best part of your life and its really not. and its also about how form the outside family is something good and shiny, but if you look inside its ugly. hence, mock paradise.
