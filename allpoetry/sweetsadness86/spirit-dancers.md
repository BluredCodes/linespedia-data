we pull back layers and expose them

to the eyes of an unforgiving mother

who passes her cancer on to you

with a kiss and a blade

stand naked in the cold wind

that whispers of your faults

and you believe the lies they tell you

that you're ugly and inadequate

can you see your reflection

in my bloodshot eyes

goddesses of the moons and stars

of your faraway nexus nebulous

with white light in your eyes

from a shattered dream or two

you rise above the ashes

your fire doused by your tears

blind to your own beauty

don't listen to the ghoststhis is dedicated to those who feel ugly
unworthy, even though you glow

kisskisss...

you are always loved
