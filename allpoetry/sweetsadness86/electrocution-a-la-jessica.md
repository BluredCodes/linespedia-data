title: "'Electrocution a la Jessica'"

Affection casts an electric light
To electrocute my angst
[Drown me kindly inside brown eyes]

Psychoanalyze my love,
Take my sad thoughts and burn them
To ashes, rip me to shreds
Until you can love me again

We are an unsolved equation
An uneven line, words twisting and curling
Into fragments of meaning, to
Fall empty to your adamant ears.

Don't be angry, my lovely.
Don't be sad [that's my job]
Love me [and we all fall down]

Your words metamorph into a velveted
Dagger to kill me softly [inside brown eyes]
Emotional whore, not beautiful,
[I'm beautiful for you alone]

Don't you see I'm waiting here for you?
I'm waiting up. Will you come back home?
Erotic fantasies revert to a memory
Of my lips upon yours, a leaping flame.

Emotion taking shape between quaking lips,
Two warm bodies, now cold
[Don't fall into my rut]

Didn't you know you make me
Happy? Didn't you know you make me
Warm? You make me...
Sorry.

Will you be happy if I am?
I am happy when I hear
Your voice... [tell me it's OK]
Tell me you love me.

Look at me, needy, hoping
Electrocute my angst
[Drown me kindly deep inside brown eyes]
