I stand still, though life has forsaken me
Pondering the next step I might take

I wonder where these roads will lead me
If sorrow will ever be in my wake

Naked, as the world would strip me bare
Once more, my astral wanderings take me

That sanctuary where my soul rests for now
Inside your arms I find all that I should need.

Love is like a memory I cannot lose
A whisper that is haunting my silence

A ghost of everything I could have been
Telling me of what I'll never know

Happiness is love with a better mask
fleeting ever further from my grasp

Miles between us... you who hold my heart
In hands I see but in sweet memories
