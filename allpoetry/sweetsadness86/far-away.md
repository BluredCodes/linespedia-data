The haze is everywhere
Opaque smoke that covers the city
Fog invades every car window
And casts a pall over the neon light
My thoughts are far away
In a place where it is always raining
And I am a pale dreamy mademoiselle
I dance in the rain
The air is dense and thick like butter
It presses into me, a tidal wave of pallid mist
It smells of a forest in a fairy tale
And of your last ciggarette, too soaked to light
My spirit is in a land far away
Cloaked in white and contemplating the rain
And I am a shadow, a ghost
I sway to the rythms of the wind
Reality is a grey haze of screaming wind and mournful rain
I dance upon the clouds and watch it fall
