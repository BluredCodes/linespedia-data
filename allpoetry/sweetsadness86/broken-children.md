make way for the crying child 
pat me on the back, 
hand me your false sympathy 
make up for all i lack 

a parade of broken children 
marching through the jagged days 
watching me with buring eyes 
begging me to come out and play 

i wallowed in the playground 
wishing that i were whole and real 
i wallowed in their sympathy 
i cut myself so i could feel 

if only i were invisible 
if only i were like you 
a woman who had no childhood 
A child with no one to turn tochallenge poem
