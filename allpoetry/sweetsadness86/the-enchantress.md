a spell of autumn and spring
cast upon the frigid darkness

of the world, the eternal flame
of this purgatory, life... see

the child who cries sparkling diamonds?
jewels and pearls fall from her lips,

uninhibited... arms open, she gives a kiss 
of a stranger... the touch of a earthbound soul. 

thirsty for all she gives. 
i would hand it to her... for all

my shiny tears have fallen
into the hands of a loving warrior

fighting for the good side, or the bad...
for the difference between each.

the serene goddess who dries my tears;
a specter lamenting my fate

i swim in her words, that keep me afloat
that life-giving breath of love

ethereal... i drown in her eyes
and dance upon her gravefor jen.
