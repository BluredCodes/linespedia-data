It's my problem, it's your fault
It's all relative now
And I feel there's no way out
I want to run but don't know how
How did I get so sick and tired
Even though it's nothing new
Yes, of course I am at fault
Oh well, I'm blaming you
I want my own identity but
The past has got me chained
Ignorant of choices but
I'll find some new complaint
