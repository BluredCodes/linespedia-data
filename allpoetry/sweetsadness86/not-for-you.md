---
title: "'Not For You'"
writer: "SweetSadness86"
slug: "not-for-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

You
Don't care if I'm crying
These truths you keep denying
Someday you'll be burning
You will comprehend
The things you've put me through
The love that was untrue
Knowing that I can never let it show

But this is not for you
And I am not for you
I have feelings too
But they are not for you

You
Never seem to understand
Were never there to hold my hand
Now you cling to me
But I turn away
You cut me with your words
There's nowhere I can turn
You're always there to wound me in some other way

But I'm a person, too
I have feelings too
I see what you would try
It's just another lie
These words are not for you
And I am not for you
