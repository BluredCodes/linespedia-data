Sometimes I think that 
My city is beautiful...
Grey blue of the sky. 

Rain, rare, exotic
Air heavy with misty breath
Wind singing of free

Spirits, roaming, watching
A poet with her pen, as
She tells their stories.

Night, the heady smoke 
Sensual, swallows the stars
Neon lights blinking

People go by, not
Seeing the fairies, inside
