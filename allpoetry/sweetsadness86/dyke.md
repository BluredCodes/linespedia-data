Someone called me a dyke today
Hurling words to shatter
Grenades against my soul
Like a God would smite his foes

But this God wore a GAP tee shirt
This God had acne scars
This God sneered from his high horse
And stabbed an angel in the back

Someone called me a dyke today
Enamel cross around his neck
God, your father, must be so proud
As you spear him with your words

Someone called me a dyke today
The thorn upon his crown
He proudly sits on his plastic Jesus
and digs his talons in
