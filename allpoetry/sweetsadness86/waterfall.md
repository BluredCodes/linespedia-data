feelings are like a river
too many all the time 
crashing against the sand of my conciousness

huge and furious and beautiful
made up of rain and the sea 
foaming waters and salty tears

glorious sorrow and crisp coldness
as i swim for calmer waters
i only find more torrents to come

emotions are the mighty rapids
and this is the waterfall
as i pass over this cliff and into 

more water, more emotion, 
more physical need for rest and respite
and you
