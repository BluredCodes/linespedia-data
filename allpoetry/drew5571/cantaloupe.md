I'm trying to talk to you

but you are too busy

eating cantaloupe

and drinking coffee

with a cube of sugar.



Communication is not

what you really want to do.

You eat cantaloupe

and drink your coffee

with a cube of sugar.



I'll just read the newspaper

and figure out the

crossword clues

while you eat cantaloupe

and drink coffee

with a cube of sugar.Written december 2001 as a 2 minute exercise. I had to use three words, communication, coffee, and cantaloupe in a poem. I apologize for its simplicity.
