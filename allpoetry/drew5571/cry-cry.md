---
title: "Cry, Cry"
writer: "Drew5571"
slug: "cry-cry"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/5756-Cry--Cry-by-Drew5571"
---

Baby,
there's a sadness
in your expression,
there's a silence
that I wish would stop.
For a moment,
could you open up.

Cry, cry,
let the rain fall
over me.
Cry, cry,
maybe it would
set you free.

Baby,
there's someone here
to console your heart.
These arms would hold
you when you
fall apart.
How do you feel?
Please let me heal.

Cry, cry,
let the rain fall
over me.
Cry, cry,
maybe it would
set you free.
