Hands and tongues are ready to pounce
on the thoughts that spring from my mind.
There comes a time for nothing said
or written upon the blank screen.
When everything has been done
by those who have come before me,
how many ways can one describe
the sacred and the profane or
the obvious and minutia?Late night improvisation-please be kind.
