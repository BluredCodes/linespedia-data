title: "Sentimental Cliche"

There's something about your style
that makes knowing you worthwhile,
the way you talk,
the way you walk,
how you hold my attention.

I'm never one for love songs
for I'm often proven wrong,
maybe tonight
I could be right,
do you hear my intention?

You'll simply throw this away,
thinking I've nothing to say.
My words are true,
I do love you,
do I see a reflection?

written 2/1994
