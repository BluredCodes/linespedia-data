---
title: "Exposing The Negative"

Pictures of
smiling faces
I once knew,
they are memories

brought to light.
As I look with
new wonder
at my youthful times,

hindsight brings
an awareness
that can be
expressed in this thought.

There is no
returning
to the past
that was never there.

It is just
the surface,
you know not 
what they really think.
