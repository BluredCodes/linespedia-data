---
title: "Why not?"
writer: "Riceman"
slug: "why-not"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I jump around, you say no.
I talk loud, you say don't.
I say no, you say yes.
Why not?

I can do whatever I want,
who are you to tell me no?
And all I can think of is,
Why not?

You want and want and want,
then if I ask you for something you say no.
One question pops into my head,
Why not?

Some of my requests are a little too much,
but you still don't do anything for me.
When I ask for peace and you say no, I ask,
Why not?
