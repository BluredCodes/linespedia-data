---
title: "What's wrong with you?"
writer: "Riceman"

What's wrong with you? We used to be tight. Now you insult me and don't like me anymore? What's wrong with you?
We hang out, we do stuff together and this is how you repay me? What? What was that? No forget you! You don't deserve a friend like me.
You and your childish arguements is what brought our friendship to an end. Arguing about the dumbest things! You brought this upon yourself, not me.
Don't blame me for your mistakes! How dare you say that! What's wrong with you?
