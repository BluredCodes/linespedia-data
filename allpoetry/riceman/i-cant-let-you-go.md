---
title: "I Can't let you go"
writer: "Riceman"
slug: "i-cant-let-you-go"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/50495-I-Cant-let-you-go-by-Riceman"
---

I see you everyday, think about you, even though I don't talk to you much, I can't let you go.
Your face lights up my day, your eyes blue like the endless ocean,
I can't let you go.

You are so beautiful, soft and gentle, nothing can compare to you,
I can't let you go.
Standing tall, no one is better than you, I want you, but it's hard,
I can't let you go.

It feels like I've known you for so long, but you don't notice me,
I can't let you go.
God made you a very special girl, anyone can see that, I just want you,
I can't let you go.

When you enter the room, I don't know what to do, you are the best thing that happened to me,
I can't let you go.
If you decide to come looking for me, you know where to find me,
Please don't let me go because I won't.
