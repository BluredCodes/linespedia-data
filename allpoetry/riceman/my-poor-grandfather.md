---
title: "My poor Grandfather"

This is a true story about my grandfather.

He was the best thing I ever had,
Everytime I went home he'd be there waiting to give me a hug.
He was the nicest person I knew, especially since I was only 5.
I loved him so much.

Even though he has passed away for a while,
I can't keep mourning about him.
At least now he is in a happier place,
While we sit here waiting to die.

He never got to see me grow up into a teenager,
Never saw me change, he just had to pass away.
He was my one and only grandfather,
my poor old grandfather.

I really miss him.......
