wish i was in a movie 
                   things are so easy 
            break ups lead to friendships 
                          and pain and aches turn out fine 
and          once it's over, 
you    turn     it    off    and    walk   away 

                                life could be so simple 
only l    i    t   t   l   e hectic problems 
                                              that scurry around, 
                           that are figured out 
   with 
      quick wit

                       defined characters that never dissolve 
nothing changes 
                       and if it does, 
                          there's always a s   c    r   i   p   t 
                                  written eloquently, 
thoughts are put on display 
                                       a drama performed 
                          
                                   to perfection
