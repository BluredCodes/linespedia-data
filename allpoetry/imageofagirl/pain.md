title: "pain"
writer: "imageofagirl"
slug: "pain"
source: "AllPoetry"

you slowly move your body 
        onto mine 
     and comfort my frailty 
you possess in your hands 
the ability to     kill me    with    words

you have all the power 

i am your minion 

i will follow you 

and wait at your feet 

        walk all over me and 
toss me aside 
      as though i am nothing 

i will be nothing 

just 
to 
be 
with 
you
