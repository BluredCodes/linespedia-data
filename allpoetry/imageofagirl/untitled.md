fast paced living 
       has taken the life out of       me
   ripped my soul in     two 
  i've been run over 
      by an on coming     truck
   too many       times
  almost            daily
     i have been mugged
    and laughed          at 
   
               teased, tested, and tried
    
      I    have    fallen  off    of     the 
    tallest building
                     all just trying to live my life.
