someday 
i   will   become   comfortable   in 
my    own    skin 
and  
   someday   these
bones         won't feel as though they 
weigh more than the entire world 
and      someday i can be proud 
of the way i smile 
and i    won't have to bleed myself 
  dry 
someday    i won't wear myself thin

and someday the toilet won't flush 

away all my pain 
 and someday   someone will  tell me 
        everything is fine, 

             and someday hell will freeze over.
