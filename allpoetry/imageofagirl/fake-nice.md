title: "fake-nice"
writer: "imageofagirl"

overcoming the comlexities of standing out in the crowd
afraid to shy away, 
           and people will notice
   the one who does not conform 
    doesn't wear pink 
when pink is in
    doesn't listen to blink 
now that they're pop 
    doesn't ooh and aww 
at every hot guy that walks her way 
    doesn't smile and pretend 
she likes the world and everyone in it
     doesn't act as though she knows nothing
only marbles rolling around her head 

                the blond dye hasn't crept into her brain 
    hasn't destroyed every cell and made her the same 
yet she is too afraid
     to raise her voice
too afraid someone might listen
   afraid that they'll mock her 
for being a brunette with 
                        two left feet, 
         who likes geeky 
     guys with thick black glasses, 
  who wears plaid pants, and reads 
 philosophy, and who only likes old blink. 
       but she doesn't care 
   gotten over all the complexities 
and realized just how     simple   it   all   is

        they'll wind up barbie dolls 
      working at the closest supermarket
with bag boy husbands, and        two       kids, 
             and one in the oven
she's glad she went to school 
                      glad she did the work 
     glad she only acted fake-nice
        and never conformed.
