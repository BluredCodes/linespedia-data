are they here 
         its hard to tell 
if they question 
   this statement
  my dress 
  my shoes
           they have a folder
 that is thick 
      as a tree 
       all about the life 
of little ol me 
they judge me 
based on my 
report card
on my 
group activities 
on my 
love life 
or lack there of
do i fit their 
demands 
and have i 
become what they 
need
or am i something
they can't have 
a screw
to clog the system 
can't i just 
not worry 
about all their 
needs 
because they demand me 
i do not want them 
i want what i once 
had 
and what i once loved
want to go back to when 
everything was easy 
and nice 
but i can't ignore 
the silent ramblings 
of the elder 
what they have to say 
decides my future
i can't control it anymore.this is not applying to a school, it's about applying to life. what ppl think of me, decides my social status or if i will be popular. it's annoying yet true.
