---
title: "love tainted"
writer: "imageofagirl"

i hate the way

you neglect me 

and make me feel like it's all my fault 

my heart can only be thrown around
so many times

before i feel the need to leave

if i let you have all of me 
i fear that you'll ruin
what i have 

and taint all that i consider my temple 

if i give you only some 
and you destroy it without consideration
break the bond i agree to

you'll kill me with heartbreak 

and that i won't allow.
