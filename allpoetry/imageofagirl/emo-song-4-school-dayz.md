your expectations leave me open for mistakes 
all of your questions are lined with answers I can't fake 
I can't let you control me decide when I should wake 
you are stupid how can you decide what I should make 

oh these days have gotten too long 
and I can't wait until the time passes 
I am too old to go off for six hours 
and fill my mind with useless knowledge 

when will i  ever need it you, say that I will 
close your mouth I've come to the conclusion, I've had my fill 
all of my dreams stretch past that window sill 
if I don't leave soon i'll soon have to kill

oh oh oh 
these days have gotten too long 
oh oh oh 
and I can't wait for them to be gone 
oh oh oh 
I am too old to go off and listen 
oh oh oh 
six hours have never been this long. 
 
you say i should learn it 
school is for geeks 
give me a six pack and some cash 
and I will be content just to sleep 

I'm leaving I'm never coming back 
now I still can't sleep in works here 
when will i ever get to close my droopy eyes 
and rest 
r i p 
r i p 
will mean only sweet sleep for me. 

oh take me back to the time of sick days 
where snow days where a common thing 
where movies took up the time that we had 
and useless thought ran through my head. 

take me back 
life is too hard
they didn't prepare us for anything.
