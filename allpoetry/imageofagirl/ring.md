title: "Ring"

I'm calling you
ring ring ring
can't you hear that
ever persistent noise in your head
doesn't it pound through that 
        three or 
              four times
before silence begins
do you deny me 
of a simple conversation
how can you not want me
when you tell me you do
it's started all over again
           cat and 
                 mouse
I chase to find you
why won't you answer that noise
don't you need to know it's me on the other end
don't you care that i'm alive?
