am i visible 
       amongst all of this dust 
                 just another lemming ready to make the leap 
   can you be my savoiur 
          and teach me how to become perfection? 
   be nice     and       kind 
and    holy and    christian 
            and a great big slut, 
become what everyone wants 
    I can try my hardest 
                and put up a good fight 
 
     but my personality always wins 
                         I just want to be popular
   wearing tight jeans, and a tommy shirt 
               following the flock 
                     believing all that is put in front of me 
   and never questioning anything 
              can i mimic your great achievments
          your pretty hair and high pitched giggle 

                      with no brain i won't have to think 
   no standards to live up to 
                 except for the do's and don't list 
d on't wear      white      after labour day 
or      pink       nailpolish on wednesdays 

don't care about the rest of the world 
or those dying 
                   suffering is not something that will enter my mind 
                     
it's so easy being you 
    and don't tell me it's not 
                               sign me upsarcasm
