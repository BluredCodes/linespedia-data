title: "highschool bureaucrats"
writer: "imageofagirl"

i want to say
more then what i can 
but every time i
try to free the small 
minds they seem 
to swallow me up 
inside their hollow eyes

is their anything going 
on inside their heads
any activity, 
when i smile i get no response
but if they are told they
will smile right back at me 
something is funny only 
if it has a laugh track at the end
the best clothes have to be 
store bought trends

they commit themselves
to raising their hands
and doing as much as they can to 
pass 
sex drugs and lies
are the highlights of their 
lives 
there is so much more 
for them to see
past their broken reality 

but i fear the worst
i sit too far out of the 
crowd
the majority will rule in the end
and i will be pushed down 
power has no mind. 
if this is who will 
represent us in the end

i fear graduation
