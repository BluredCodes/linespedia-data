Hello its me   Do you remember me?       Do you remember my face?          My hair, my smile, my laugh?             Do you remember how guliable I was?           How simple, how silly? How stupid I was?        Do you remember the way you treated me?     The way you lied, you cheated,  And then tore out my heart?I remember you, do you remember me?
