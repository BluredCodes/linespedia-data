title: "for ryan"

i am sorry 
      for judging you 
   and saying that you 
             don't   know   how   i   feel 

       saying you can't relate 
  and  
       saying that you don't feel the same 


sorry if i hurt you 
     the   blame   was   not  meant  to  be  placed

                     solely in your hands 
it's an easier way out 

         then to accept it all for myself 

it would save a lot of tears 
                                       or so i thought. 
but without you 

i would cry the             
                   nile 

be with me forever 
                  i need you 
  words are stupid things that pour 
out        of          my       mouth 

    waterfalls of lies and unbridled thoughts

for what i fear in the end 
                          is losing you
