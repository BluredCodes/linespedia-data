---
title: "or would you"
writer: "imageofagirl"
slug: "or-would-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/10855-or-would-you-by-imageofagirl"
---

Or would you
fly away 
or would you
cling tight
or would you 
run as fast as you can
or would you 
hold me close
or would you 
tell me you love me
or would you
pass me off as another
or would you
cry if I died
or would you
laugh with pleasure
or would you.
