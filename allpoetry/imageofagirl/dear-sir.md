dear sir;
there is something undesirable about 
the way that you walk 
and waggle your toungue
i wish it would fall upon the ground
so i could squish out 
all of your stupid little thoughts

i lived everyday of this life
being opressed by your face
the way you smile 
devestates my family
your gut reactions lead to 
me living on the street 
your greed and needs for
all of the good things in life 
leads to my tortured soul
making down payments 
at the bank for your happiness. 

if you are for the people 
why do you kill us 
little by little day after day
if you care only about our wants
why must you toss them aside
and when we form the ability 
to stand tall in front of you
why must you knock us down 
with torture and pain
if this land is ours
then why is it only you 
who is free
