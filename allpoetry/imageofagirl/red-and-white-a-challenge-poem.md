tonight 
      i will sit at home 
and  cry  my  eyes    until  they  are 
                                              dry 
          as    the    desert 

i will    tear    up your picture 
            and ignite it in flames 
  as the ashes  float away 
so will my love 

                                i only hope that this will cleanse me 

            give me the redemption that i need 

sweets can not sweeten my disposition 
                  flowers can not bring life unto my death      
the pain i have tonight 
                      will not go away 
                                  
                                      until  
                                    you 
                                      come 

                                      back
