you are the poet i'll let your words wash over my soul
your hands have been molding what your eyes have been seeing
out in this darkness my breath has escaped me 
lead me into your transluscent dreams

anger has enraged me until my blood boils hot 
cool me down with the waters of calmness 
into this area i have never ventured 
I am trusting that you will not lead me astray 

out in this starry night 
your eyes hold all the answers 
you and i can wait for eternity
on this mountain top i can see the ground
the fall is much too steep 
but i would fall a thousand times and
 worship at your feet. 
So let me be 
with you for ever 
can you not tell that you complete me 
do not walk all over this 
body can not take it 
don't tell me no no no. 

times have shortchanged me and i have been the carpet 
people have stepped all over me and dirt is entrenched inside 
show me I am something give me the knowledge 
feel that this is so right. 

out in this starry night 
your eyes hold all the answers 
you and i can wait for eternity
on this mountain top i can see the ground
the fall is much too steep 
but i would fall a thousand times and
 worship at your feet. 
So let me be 
with you for ever 
can you not tell that you complete me 
do not walk all over this 
body can not take it 
don't tell me no no no. 
and you are 
every thing that I could need 
you are just what I wanted 
you are all that I can ever see me being with 
I am changed.....
and out in this starry night

your eyes hold all the answers 
you and i can wait for eternity oooh oh oh
on this mountain i can see the ground
the fall is much too steep 
but i would fall a thousand times and
 worship at your feet. 
So let me be 
with you for ever 
can you not tell that you complete me 
do not walk all over this 
body can not take it 
don't tell me no no no.
 don't tell me no
