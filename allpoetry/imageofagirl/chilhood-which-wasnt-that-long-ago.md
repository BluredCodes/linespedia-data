every morning i would wake up 
cheerios in hand 
my pajamaed feet snuggled 
in my protection blanket 
the one that warded off the evil spirits 
i turned on the picutre that let me 
see in to my make belive world 
that opened my eyes 
hidden by little coincidences 
and funny practical jokes 
bugs bunny, that silly ol' rabbit 
i would contrive my skills to sneak about 
duck tales taught me the lessons of economy, 
the simpsons, opened my eyes to 
real life 
so many lessons learnt over a bowl 
of cheerios 
in a cool dark dawn.
