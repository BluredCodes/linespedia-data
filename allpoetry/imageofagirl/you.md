hide behind 
      slowly unfurling lies
pretend as though be both love 
    the way that we are 
inconspicuos little details 
cannot      bring      us    down 
   we will live on for eternity 


pretty     little    lies 
line up
 one  
   by 
    one 
and as i knock down all 
of my prejudices i am 
shown,   that 
all of the money 
can't      buy    a    world 
where i can have what i once did 

the past was so simple 
carefree  in the      way that i      loved 
but    beauty changes into            hate 
and    hate is    what rules the world 

hating you only ruins me 

and i can not fall 

i would rather push you away 
and      move     on 

but in the end 
i would crumble with out you.
