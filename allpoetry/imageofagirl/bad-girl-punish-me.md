i've been a bad girl (punish me)
      i speak too loud 
    so that all can hear me 
      and inside theres 
   an extra special yearning 
  to be like you in every way 
     but i will go against it, 
   and be the way i wanna be 

i've been a bad girl(punish me)
   i will not stand for this
      intended conformity 
      i will play my cards 
     and cling to my dreams 
  i will hold onto my intentions 
 and your follower i will never be 

i've been a bad girl (punish me)
    because i am not just like you 
   i have been naughty in all the 
             wrong ways 
   i won't sit back spreading the
         legs of injustice 
       i won't let you rape me 
        of my civil liberties 

i've been a bad girl (punish me)
          because i hate you 
        and because i don't need you 
              and your army 
        i don't need your painful 
              considerations 
          even if they are plenty 

i've been a bad girl 
        because i've been good to me
