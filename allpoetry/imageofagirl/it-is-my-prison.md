title: "It is my prison"
writer: "imageofagirl"

my heart so swiftly breaking 
can be healed 
in the blinking moment, 
time too soon wants to pass 
ever moving shadows 
lie heavily on my mind 
breaking over gently 
pushing me over the edge 

the fall 
is too much 
this drop will 
leave 
me 
broken bodies 
why would I die for you to be 

redemtion is over rated 
it is not what i need 
sympathy can't save me 
it is time for me too bleed 
dripping dominoes crash down 
onto my life 
to knock down another 
an augmented scheme 

the fall is 
too much 
this drop will leave me broken 
bodies why would i 
die for you to be 
free free free 

enslave me 
i will be 
all that you need 
chains can't keep me 
this is my prison 
lock me 
and beat me 
push me down 
               down
                       down 

the fall is 
not enough 
this drop will make me whole again 
forever i would 
die for you and me.
