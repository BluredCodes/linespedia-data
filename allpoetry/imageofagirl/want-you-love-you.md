title: "want you love you"
writer: "imageofagirl"

My body is your canvas
paint me the way you want me to be 
and I will promise that I am all 
that you will every need

Underneath this twilight
the sun surrounds me
the starlight dances accross your face 
In my dreams you are around me 
you surround me 
      cuz I love you 
            I love you

trace the lines of my body 
let your hands be your guide
travel all over me 
see all the pretty sights 

underneath this heat 
your breathe takes me away 
In my dreams your all around me 
you surround me 
      cuz I want you 
           I want you
