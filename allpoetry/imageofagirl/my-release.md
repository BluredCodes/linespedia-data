---
title: "my release"
writer: "imageofagirl"

drug me for the cause of salvation
pin me down in the name of resurection
let my mind fly away 
until  my body pulls it back 
I am needing just a second
to rearrange my requirements
on what I feel should be 
my life 
my standards are set so low
the dust can fly right past me 
I am in pain from the beauty i see
just let me have one more hit
blasphemy is only a secondary regret
the pin will dissolve into my skin
the liquid can cleanse my soul
cause in this broken body
my tragedies have no where to go 
let my senses become clear
until I reach the final nirvana 
let me have my release.
