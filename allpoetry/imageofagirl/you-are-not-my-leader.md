i'll scream 
at the top of my lungs 
       as it echoes of the wall 
it lunges into your ears 
                        but you don't care 
                    with all of your power 
you let it flow right through you
        it will disrupt you 
                     and you're to busy with your life to care 
about the truths 
                 and consequences 
          of living closed minded 
                            in your little suburban life 
    you pay 10 bucks for a meal at McDonalds 
             100 bucks for your tommy jeans
           and 1000 bucks for your IBM 
corporate rule has consumed you 
and bled you dry 
                            "who cares" you say 
           "it's all the same 
                there's nothing I can do anyways" 
answering back 
       with searing wit 
                "       thank god they took the time 
and brought down hitler, 
           or what if they said it was too hard, 
and that he's a good man . would you be where you are today? "

                  just don't let them decide what you see and hear 
make your own decisions
   
                           do not be the follower of a hidden leader 

  who hides behind celebrities faces, and cooky cartoons
              who pays millions to find out how you think, 
     and then makes thirty times that amount selling it back to you
