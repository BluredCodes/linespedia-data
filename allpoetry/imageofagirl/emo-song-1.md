on this little street in nowhere land 
lives a woman who pays two dollars for a ride 
she is wrinkled and she's lost all of her coins
on this broken side walk is the wonders 
of lost men who have faded from their prime 
sitting knowledge lays within gently drifting from their mind 

if you meet me out here 
I will hold your coat for you 
if you want i will be 
exactly what you need
so much traffic has passed us 
too many cracks have broken our backs 
in this city lost in time 
in our hidden land. 
I will go on 

On this corner stands a girl dressed older then her age 
her lips are stained with pastel pinks 
her dress raised too high her shirt too tight 
On this road drives cars that fly by fast like time was nothing 
until it catches up to them, 
they can dissapear from life cycles 

And if you meet me out here 
I will hold your hat for you 
if you want I will be
exactly what you need
so much time has passed us 
too many lines have crushed our weak spines 
in this city lost in time 
in our hidden place 
I will be forever. 

And if you want 
to hop into that car 
drive faster and further 
it will catch up to you in the end 
just choose now to be with me here 
It's not that hard hard hard 

And if you meet me out here 
I will pay for your fare 
If you want I will be again 
so much shit has happened
too much is on our sholders 
we can't carry the weight of the world 
but come with me out here 
in this city lost in time 
In our hidden place 
I'll be yours and you'll be mine.if you have any stuff you think I should add or better words for things write a note. I am trying to expand into lyrics and stuff. thanks everyone.
