she has regrets in her life
how many times she has
given up 
how many times has she thought
too much
how many times has she passed the chance up
She knows its her fault
she allows herself to be a little puppet
in his game of life
she smiles when she is told
'jump'
'how high?' is her response time
and time again. 
She has strings attatched to her arms and legs
she has eyes following her everywhere
He can see what she does
He can stop what she does
he can punish her, beat her down
'your worthless'
' i know' is her response  time and 
time again
but in the back of her brain
some where deep down inside
where his prying eyes can not find
lies a prayer that someday she can 
cut those strings 
take a leap
'Where do you think your going?'
'Goodbye'
