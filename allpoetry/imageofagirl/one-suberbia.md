a scene of perfection 
     the landscape of life 
no more inclinations 
    to see it fade 
suberbia is a mild mannered
             woman swathed in blood 
over the loss in her sons hockey game 
   
daddy walks over me (do i allow him?) 

he plays me like i am his        slave 
  tells me life will be alright 
   in my perfect house 

picket     fences     squash   hopes    that    i   have 
 and they trenscend into      time 

let me have my freedom. (do i want it?)

my brother beats me till i bleed 
        for secrets i know 
he tucks them away 
    spices on the back shelf. 
out of reach 
           to the common public 
the smell floats through 
    a scent that is lost in a quiet scream

        my boyfriend fights for civil rights 
 yet can't form the ability to be civil 
contradictions are his business          

    he kicks and screams 
     tells me i need him ( do i really?)
and then pushes me away . 
(temper tantrums for the well mannered)

did your mommy tell you, two wrongs dont' 
            make a right (switch around for todays society) 
       

             time can not fix things
       the day is dawning 
      time for change in all directions
