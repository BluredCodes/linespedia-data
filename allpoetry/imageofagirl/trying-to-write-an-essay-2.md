words they want to flow like a river 
yet there is a dam blocking the rush 
and my mind flys a million miles an hour
over time and space pull and push
my mind jumps back and forth hopping to and fro 
and yet it leads me back to you 
back to my home 
when will this night end 
and when will dawn arrive
all i know is that it is gonna be 
a long long night.
