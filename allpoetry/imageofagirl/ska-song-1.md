do you want me 
do you need me  
crying late at night 
can you feel me 
stepping inside of your bones
make them ache all night 
I am seeing 
everything clearly 
i'm in a perfect state 
I know you left her 
just to have me 
but i am not what you need 

can I escape you 
can't i run away 
where ever life may take you 
let it take you away from me 
walking by my house 
and waiting till the dawn 
you can't change the fact that 
you and I are not one. 

I can see that you love me 
you're obsessed and i can't see why 
you must have me 
you must touch me 
but I am not your kind 
I am funky 
you are plain 
you are stupid 
I am sane 

Can I escape you 
Can't I run away 
where ever life may take you 
let it take you away from me 
walking by my house
and waiting till the dawn 
you can't change the fact 
I am leaving, I am gone 

I don't like you 
I never will 
I think I kind of hate you 
your face makes me ill 
stop with this crazy stalker like fashion 
your endless please do not attract 
leave me alone and keep you insanity intact 
some where else, 
far away 

Can I escape you 
Can't I run away 
where ever life may take you 
let it take you away from me 
walking by my house 
and waiting till the dawn 
you can't change the fact 

i don't think your fun 
you're not a cool guy 
you don't like my music 
you're not a nice guy 
you break hearts and abuse them 
just cuz i'm pretty doesn't mean i'll absorb 
all your wanting and understand what you need me for 

can I escape you can't i run away 
where ever life may take you 
let it take you away from me 
walking by my house 
and waiting till the dawn 
you can't change the fact that 
you and I are not one.
