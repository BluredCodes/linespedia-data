the cold of this december 
as ice enters my soul 
i am frozen deep within 
only the heat from your body 
casually moving along mine 
will melt my heart 

my body and my mind 
have experienced so much pain 
these last few years 
but in your arms i find 
the embrace to make it 
all disappear 

the strength and your charisma
move into me 
and hold onto the frailty 
and turn it all away 
to make me see what 
there is out there for me 
you would give it all up 
on this cold dcecember 

you can no longer come home 
when i need you 
yearning can't make the
time pass 
the guilt i feel cannot 
make your travel home 
but the need makes me ache 
into my very bones. 

this last gasp of in ability 
the fear i have inside 
i need you hear to silence them 
tonight. 

loneliness is the state
 that i have been in
this cold decemeber 
so much to offer in the 
times of warmth, the summer 
offered solace inside your arms 
but now the cold has reached me 
and i can't fight anymore 
take me over 
on this cold december
