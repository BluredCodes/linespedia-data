I am so sorry to disrupt
    what you have in your life
           Apologies do nothing
only endles ramblings of a 
       foolish person
I have put all your perfect dreams
   Imperfectly in disaray
       I have messed up what you have 
   I am such a foolish person
 Do you know why I do what I do
    do you care why I forgo all I have for you
 Do you even question why
  You throw me away like a passerby? 
Well oh no you don't 
     What little secret do you have that I can find
what don't you want me to unlock
    what deep seeded fear 
  is hidden deep with in
everyone has skeletons
   who dance with muted mouths 
 What do you not what out
  that is hidden deep with in? 
let me see.
    your life was perfection
but I must shatter your little world
    abuse me an hurt me 
but you'll never win
I'll break you 
my condolences
