hard to build up this sanity 



and     have      it     ripped     away    from    me 
          

    i can only hold on for the ride 
                but if you wash your love over me 


   you can cleanse me with all of your lies 

     pretend as though you 
         care 
           for 
               me 

just  heal       me      for       a      day 

when days turn into eternity 
only 
     then 
        will 
            i
            
                            be saved.
