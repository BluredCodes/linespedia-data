slowly revealing 
      piece  
                     by 
                                piece 
                            the puzzle that is you 

as   i come     closer 
to          the         point          of 
 
    no 
  return 

the quest for knowledge 
                           in the end scars. 
         
        it's worth it. 

afraid and scared of 
         whats     there     deep       within 
       
        but i want it. 

the beginning caused this
         a domino fell       over 
  one 
               by 
                           one 
i have reached 
        a time where i       
                      can't go back .

                i must accept the inevitable 
you         are         an            enigma 
       
                  impossible to solve 
 
                      i need to 
but      i  
do not
 have the          key.
