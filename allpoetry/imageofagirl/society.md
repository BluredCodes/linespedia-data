this reverie
is personally understanding
the shadows that creep by 
punish me in the night
I have too little to live for
but too much to leave
can't there be a compromise
I am headed on a one ticket ride
to the gutter 
a girl who no longer exists
spit on and used
beaten and abused 
not a whole person
the escape is less then I wanted
it is not all I could need
for I am quick to jump to anger
I was too quick to leave
I am spiraling down in this darkness
committing the ultimate crime
scorching my anger
I die at what price
The commercials all warned us
jump in it is too late to escape
I have fallen so hard
it had gotten too late
I am in over my head 
but no one cares
I am no longer a reality
one of societies little mistakes
