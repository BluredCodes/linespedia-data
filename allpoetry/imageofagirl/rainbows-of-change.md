---
title: "Rainbows of change"
writer: "imageofagirl"
slug: "rainbows-of-change"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/22262-Rainbows-of-change-by-imageofagirl"
---

the shine of their faces
plastered amongst their fears
living normal lives
all they can do is
pay the bills
 as they pile up, 
they are supressed
they are held down 
their dreams are lost 
in a breeze of children
their hopes of fast cars
and beautiful women 
their tedious tasks
keep them sane for a while
robots in the clothes of men
they're apprehensive about change
when their body aches for it
can you hear me 
my little glass men
when the world falls 
apart and crashes
you will be broken 
shatter into pieces 
i'll sweep you from the floor
and place you where you belong
i'll wipe away your lost innocence
and when light of reality shines upon, 
you will be swathed in rainbows
