---
title: "On your knees"
writer: "imageofagirl"
slug: "on-your-knees"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

can you guess the little questions 
that protrude out of my head 
can you see right through this 
common entity? 

are you too perfect 
is your ego too large 
do you recognize me? 

I am every girl who 
has ever knelt down 
at your feet 

can you see me for what I am? 
I will succumb to your every wishes 
and want to fulfill all your needs 
I will praise the way you brush your teeth 
the commoness of the situation 
that you perform with such complex ease 

every movement you make seems 
to performed in the tune of symphonies 
every picture you have taken 
your smile escapes 

I will lay my body down for you 
I am here for your taking 
the fruits of my soul 
please take the path 
and overcome your ideas 
of what you need 
fill my empty promises 
call me on my declarations 
I will prove them all 
when you kneel down on 
your knees and 
praise me.
