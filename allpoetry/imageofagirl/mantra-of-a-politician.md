title: "Mantra of a Politician"

gotta be a winner
the first in the line
step over backs 
and crush their spines 
gotta be a winner
always on time 
strive towards the best 
devour all the rest 
gotta be a winner 
this trophy must be mine 
the end justifies the means 
and only the win is what is seen.
