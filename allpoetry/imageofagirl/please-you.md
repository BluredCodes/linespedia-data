title: "please you"
writer: "imageofagirl"

you slowly undress me 
    until i lay here 
                  naked 
   for you 
   so you can mold my mind
   i     am    yours
make me into a masterpiece

                        you rudely awaken me 
       and take away the beauty 
      of all that was once 
         but again 
       can  never be 

     the carpet is then 
  pulled out from under
        but on the table 
    i lay broken 
                        i am not a magic
  trick 
        i am a person 

slap me with reality 
            it is something 
i can not deal with 
                                    i am a dreamer

if i bare my soul 
                 for you 
  lay it upon a 
  plate 
   you will take your 
  fork and knife 
and cut it up 
      until it is unrecognizable 

               i would rather dream.
