title: "nightime descends"

out there, 
i can here you 
so many whispers have left me 
helpless for you 
your body 
makes me yearn and ache 
and every passing day 
i am left wondering 
sadly and alone 
i let my mind wander 
through pictures and ideas 
concepts and conclusions
your image chases me 
through endless hours 
it crosses through my mind 
imprinted in the creases 
i have yet to succumb to your 
wishes are many yet 
I am strong like a castle 
unfallterable i stand 
unattainable yes, 
waivering yes 
I am ready
