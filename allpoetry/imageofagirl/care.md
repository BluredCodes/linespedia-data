care is a four lettered word that you haven't become acquiainted with 
yet you know the rest 
they role off of your tongue with such ease, 
greasy and sweaty miserable murmurings 
of a miserable man 
your hair is slick with the heat
as you kneel down 
your body is fat from too many hotdogs 
and beer guzzling contests
you whisper disgusting words 
into her pretty little ears 
pleading and praying for forgiveness 
you smell of tarnished hearts 
and old chili 
a mixture that confuses and degrates 
you move towards her telling her 
its all her fault that you live 
in a nickel and dime shack with 
ten children and a dog 
you tell her it's her fault you can't 
get a job, that there is no food on the table, 
and that she caused the beginning and the  end to all wars
                  if she could just see it your way. 
                    everything would be all right 
you promise if she becomes your yes man 
you will worship the very feet you kick 
and step on 
you will love her smile, the very one you tell her 
to wipe off her face, when you screw up. 
her body will be your temple, the very temple you 
moleste until she begs for your forgiveness
for she has sinned
its never you 
its always  her. 
care is a four letter word she hasn't become acquainted with.
