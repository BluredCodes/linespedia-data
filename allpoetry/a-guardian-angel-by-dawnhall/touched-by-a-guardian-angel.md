Touched by a Grardian Angel

I have earned my wings
I am flying in Heaven
I can do many things.

To Jesus I blow a kiss,
To my friends I give a hug
But Mommy, you, I really miss.

How can I be here and be there?
Jesus I ask you for an answer
How can I be everywhere?

Thank you Jesus for showing me how
I can give your blessings to others
I can be a Guardian Angel Bear, wow.

The heart of a stuffed bear am I.
Look Mommy and all children near,
I can kiss away your tears, don't cry.

I can pray with you
Just pinch my hand,
You will no longer be blue.

My halo lights your way
See it flicker and glow,
I am here to stay.I have some beautiful Guardian Bears and they say a prayer and their halo glows.  Then the Lord blessed me with a poem for them as I share my bears with others.
