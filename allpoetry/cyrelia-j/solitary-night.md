Solitary Night

Don't tempt my dreams only for a night 
And steal away my heart and leave me dead inside
Please don't look at me and see nothing but a life 
A soul 
My tears 
To take away. 
I know what I see in your eyes of myself- 
All of me 
My very being
Spare me this pain 
Your lies 
Your contempt 
And leave me to die in my solitude
And if you won't scrape the ashes of my soul from the ground 
As I lay desolate and broken hearted on your shadow 
Then I no longer accept this! 
I no longer accept this torment 
This hell 
This complete and utter loss of self
And yet- 
If that is what I must pay 
Must suffer this agony for all your whole affection 
Then it is my choice
One I make in my full lucidity 
With all of myself 
Then I no longer care about this damnéd existence or my life! 
Are you happy if I cry?
And should you laugh...
Then let these tears of mine forever decorate the heavens 
Like so many shimmering diamond stars
Until the day that I die!
Always-
Always for you my love
