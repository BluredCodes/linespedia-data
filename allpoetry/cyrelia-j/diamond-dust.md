---
title: "Diamond Dust"
writer: "Cyrelia J"
slug: "diamond-dust"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/23589-Diamond-Dust-by-Cyrelia-J"
---

Fiery eyes
Immortal Soul
Spans the universe
Taking its toll

Searing volcanoes
Fall to the sea
My ruby sweetheart
Tis’ a harsh world for thee

Escape the blackness
Of this dead earth
My jade sacrifice
The place of thy birth

Under this frail sky
Clutching to vanity
My emerald dreamer
Transcends frail humanity

Waves crash on the surf
Seeming a dream 
My crystal pearl
Trapped in this ravine

Shadows engulf life
Tearing this plane
My sapphire goddess
Driven insane

And yet still no name
But “anonymously”
My ebony treasure 
Mine eyes never see

Fading through darkness
Seeing me distant
My amethyst watcher
Erratically constant

Driven mad lover
Things aren’t as they seem
My diamond desertion
Who destroyed my dream

Yet it is certain
Lover mine lost
My Onyx betrayer
Must suffer the cost

Now who is fallen
Shattered, torn, broke
My Citrine survivor
Arose from the smoke

Though when we end this
Thou shalt not see
That knife in thy back love-
It was from me.

“Payback’s a bitch ain’t it?”
