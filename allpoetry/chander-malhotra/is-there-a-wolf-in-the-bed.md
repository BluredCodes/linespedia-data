title: "is there a wolf in the bed?"
writer: "Chander Malhotra"

What tragedies await us now
as we stand  pacific upon our contentment?
What hidden dangers lurk
in life’s daily quirks?
How many muggings am I marked for?
Which bearded fiends await to beard me?
Are there financial disasters impending?
Is someone somewhere a nasty email sending?
Is already a virus eating away my files?
Is my boss planning my retirement?
Which sky is going to fail an aeroplane?
How many wives right now are absconding?
Which joy ride is going to crash the railing?
Will the next phone bell be news from hell?
Will I wake up tomorrow?
Is there still hope or just sorrow?am i a poet
or am i just flattering myself?
