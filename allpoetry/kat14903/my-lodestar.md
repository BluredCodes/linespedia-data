I sing praises to you,
Oh LORD.
I am forever grateful,
for your protection over me.
You are my LODESTAR.
My ever constant,
GUIDING LIGHT.
In the darkness,
your light shines
directly in my path.
You provide direction,
for when I've lost my way.
As I follow your light,
I place my trust in you.
   ALMIGHTY FATHER,
All loving and All Powerful.
I need not be afraid.
Your Light will always 
guide me home.
