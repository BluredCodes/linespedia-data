---
title: "My Wish for You"

When you are troubled,
I wish for you a guiding light.
When you are feeling down,
I wish for you simple pleasures.
When you are lonely,
I wish for you Love.
When things seem hectic,
I wish for you peace.
When things seem worthless,
I wish for you hope.
I dedicate this  to you my friend,
to let you know my love has no end.
To the good Lord I lift your name.
And I know for me you do the same.
As I gaze at the stars above,
I thank God for you my friend.
Remember always, my love has no end.
