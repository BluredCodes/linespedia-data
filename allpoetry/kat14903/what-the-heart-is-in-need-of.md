It is not in knowledge alone,
but also in understanding.

It is not in "a time for,"
but in an assurance beyond all time.

It is not just in asking,
but an awareness of having received !

It is not only in repentance,
but also knowing one has been pardoned.

It is not in searching for the Holy,
but in having invited Him from within.

It is not in feeling unworthy,
but in accepting His Grace's fullness

It is not only in loving Christ Jesus,
but in fully welcoming him in your life.
