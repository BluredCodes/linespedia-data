We all must face trials,
at certain times in our lives.
And there may be days, when it seems
that life is much too hard 
to bear alone.
At these times, it's such a blessing 
to know that we can depend on
the loving comfort,
and support of a dear friend.

MY DEAR FRIEND,
You've been such a great 
support to me. At a time
when all I could see was darkness,
you opened my eyes and showed me light.

When I needed to cry you allowed me
to express my hurt.
Then you genty brushed away my tears,
and even managed to get me to smile.

When I needed to talk, you listened
with an open mind. And only when
I asked for your opinion,
you didn't hesitate then  
to lend it.

When I was angry,
you allowed me to vent my frustrations.
Then ressured me that things
would be ok.

I cherish the time 
we spend together.
Each moment that we share.
In good times, those special days
filled with joy.
And even in the sad times.
When it seems like the bad times
will never end.

You share your love 
so unconditionally my friend.
And I'm grateful that
your a part of my life.
I thank God, each day
for sending my
"SPECIAL ANGEL"
in my life.
Thank You My Dear Friend....
God Bless You Always,
