I hear the song of Autumn
echo a bittersweet sound.
As its brilliant leaves
float gently to the ground.

A vast spectrum of color
red, orange, yellow, brown and gold.
A beatiful story,
has yet to be told.

As soft winds blow
a crisp scent fills the air.
Whispering promises
to always be near.

Next a melodious tune
plays softly on my heart.
As the cold bite of winter
begins its slow start.

Life all around me
soon comes to peaceful rest.
The earth blanketed in white,
and ice covered vests.

Then as March winds blow
and bring on April rains.
Spring makes its arrival.
Life begins again.

Flowers in bloom
as the robin returns.
In search for a mate,
the love that he yearns.

With the approach of summer
comes sunshine and rainbows.
Romantic nights sitting,
by a campfires glow.

Freshly cut grass.
The smell is so grand.
Vacations at the lake.
Building castles in the sand.

Autumn the returns.
A new cycle begins.
Seasons in Time 
that will never end.

So as i reflect back,
and look at the sky above.
I gaze at its beauty.
A reminder of God's Love.
