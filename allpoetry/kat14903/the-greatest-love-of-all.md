FAITH
always believing
and never ceasing.
Trusting in God's word.

HOPE
blessed assurance.
A steadfast course.
Promise of a future.

LOVE
unconditional devotion.
Upon the Cross.
Christ died for us.

{Now, these three things remain:
Faith, Hope, and Love;
and the greatest of these is Love}
(Corinthians 13:13)
