You entered my life and became a friend.
Because of you, I can smile again.
After years of anger and tears;
unable to trust anyone.
For I no longer cared.
I stopped living.
Sheltering myself
in the darkness of self pity.
I could no longer see,
for living meant nothing to me.
Then you walked up and said 
a cheery "Hello"
And with your warm friendly smile,
you made me laugh.
Soon after, 
my anger and tears disappeared.
I was able to let go,
the pains of my past.
You offered your hand, 
and a listening ear.
As i opened up and shed 
those final tears.
Your friendship has touched my heart.
You've been such a blessing
from the very start.
I'm ready,
and I want to start living again.
Thank you Friend,
for making me feel special.
For unlocking the door,
I told myself I'd keep locked forever.
