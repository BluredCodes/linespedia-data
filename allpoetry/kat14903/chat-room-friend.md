So far between the miles,Yet so close i feel our friendship.Albeit behind a screen,You dear friend I've never seen.I turn on my box and see you there.My heart lights up, as we begin to share.Our thoughts, our day, what on our minds.Little secrets, you are so kind.You make me laugh, (£Ø£)And help me smile :-)Our time spent hereis all worth while.And in my prayersI thank God for you my friend.And I ask the Lord to keep you safe,Throughout each days end.Though miles may separate us,whenever you need a friend,I'm only a click away.
