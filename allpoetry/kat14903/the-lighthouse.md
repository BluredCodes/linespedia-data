In the distance stands 
a Lighthouse.
It shines for you and me.
The Heavenly Father
leading us there.
Showing how to 
live life Eternally.

When the storms of life 
consume us,
and darkness starts to fall.
His Light shines even brighter,
as we answer to his call.

For when we lose direction,
He calms our every fear.
We find peace and comfort,
knowing His loving 
Grace is always there.

When our hearts
become filled with sadness'
and life becomes
too hard to bear.
Our Heavenly Father 
holds on to us,
and wipes away our tears.

In the distance 
stands a Lighthouse.
How we long to seek 
this place.
When we shall meet
Our Lord Jesus Christ,
and see him face to face.
His Holy Light,
it shines so Bright.
For everyone too see.
Salvations Gift,
His Saving Grace.
So that we may be set free.
