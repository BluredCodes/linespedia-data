title: "The Forgotten Toy"

I have only one eye.
The young child tore off the 
other one when I was young.
My stuffing is coming out.
The child played tug of war
with the dog.
I have only one ear.
The child decided I 
needed a haircut.
The child would pick me up
by my legs.
I would be so dizzy,
by the time I was set down.
The child would pick me up,
with those peanut butter
and jelly hands.
That is why my fur 
is so mangled. 
The Child, he has grown up.
Now he is away at school.
I have been forgotten.
Here I sit in
this dark dusty room.
On this same old shelf,
all alone.
Waiting....
For a new child to come 
and play with me.
