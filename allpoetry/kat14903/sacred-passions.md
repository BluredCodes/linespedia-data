Let us take tonight my loveand make it ours alone.With our bodies entwinedin lustful desire,so does our sacred passion burn.Seductively your eyes meet mine.Our lips touch, our tongues explore,so pleasurable and so electrifyingto our most sensual parts.Foreplay our most beautiful weapon.Our heartbeats pounding fasteras our bodies press hard together.Deep penetration with such ecstacy.We move in perfect rythm.Filling each other, completely satisfied.Over and over our tryst continues.Breathless as sweat pours from our flesh.Reaching together in the ultimate climax.Over come with joy, the perfect union.Embraced by your warmth and in our love.For tonight you belong to meand I belong to you.Nothing else matters at this moment.The world around us is quiet.Our yearning deepening still.
