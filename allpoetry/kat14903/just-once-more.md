Just once more.....
I wish that I could see your face.
Your bright friendly smile
that could warm the coldest heart.

Just once more.....
I wish that I could hear your voice.
Filled with such compassion,
like when an angel sings.

Just once more.....
I wish that I could feel your embrace.
So tender, yet so  full of strength.
Reassuring me that life is good.

Just once more.......
