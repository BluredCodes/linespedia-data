As the clear blue sky 
gives way to darkness.
The sound of distant thunder,

crashes  with its grand roar.
A Single Flash of light,
brightens the sky.
Followed by a moment of silence.....

Then a single raindrop falls.
Suddenly followed by a 
bold fierce storm.
The sky unleashes its anger
over the earth.

With boundless fury,
the clouds cast forth
their shadows.
Anger, destruction, and hurt,
over what man has done.
Sadness, over what has become.

Next a soft breeze gently 
weakens the storm.
As the glow of sunshine,
gleams through the clouds.

The LORD brings forth 
a beautiful ribbon
of colorful Glory.
The Rainbow...
God's Promise....
A dance I call Heaven's Tears..........
