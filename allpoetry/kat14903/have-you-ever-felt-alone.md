---
title: "Have You Ever Felt Alone?"

Have you ever felt alone,
and thought that no one cared?
Or had just one of those days,
when it seemed like no one was there?

Have you ever felt like 
crawling in a corner,
because life had let you down?
Have you ever felt like crying,
because not even 
your best friend was around?

Has there ever been a time
when life has seemed unfair?
And everything around you,
was filled with much despair?

Has it ever felt 
like the world around you,
was just about to fall?
Has it ever gotten to the point,
where you felt like ending it all?

Have you ever felt alone......?
