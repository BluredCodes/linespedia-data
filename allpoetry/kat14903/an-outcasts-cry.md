A young child cries out for help,yet no one seems to hear.On the outside appearing happy,while inside he hides his tears.Made to feel like an outcast.Taunted by bullies at school.In silence he keeps to himself,as he questions the Golden Rule.Inside him burns a secret pain.Seeking revenge he creates his plan.Before is peers,he takes his final stand.If only someone had listened,and had taken a moment of time.To lend a hand of help.Preventing this horrible crime.....
