I climbed the window,
and opened the stairs.
Said my pajamas,
and put on my prayers.
Turned off the bed,
and got into the light.
All because he kissed me goodnight.

That morning I woke,
and toasted the news.
I put on my cereal, 
and ate my shoes.
Even though things weren't
going quite right.
I was happy because,
he kissed me goodnight.

Then evening came,
so I played cards with the phone.
I talked to my puppy.
Threw my brother a bone.
My life's all a flutter,
yet I feel such delight.
All because he kissed me goodnight
