---
title: "For You....."
writer: "kat14903"

For you, I'd capture the sun,
and warm you with its rays.
To show you the right  direction,
even on your darkest days.

For you, I'd seize the sky,
and give you a place to be free.
To spread your wings to fly,
so you can be all you wish to be.

For you, I'd gather a rainbow,
for when your luck has run out.
Its radiant colors, giving you a smile,
when you feel low.
Giving you hope instead of doubt.

For you, I'd catch the brightest star,
to place it in the path you take.
To guide you wherever you are,
and protect every step you make.

For you, I'd give life's simple things.
A bright red rose, a gentle kiss   
and quiet tears.
A field of daisies, a flowing brook,
a bird that sings.
A soft breeze, a gentle hand,
sharing fears.

Most of all, I'd give my Love,
to you my dear sweet friend.
Whether here on earth,
or Heaven above.
May our Friendship never end.......
