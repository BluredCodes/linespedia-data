title: "Before You O Lord"

All that is within me,
is known to you O Lord !
Every thought and every action,
remains ever present to You.
Before the will has chosen,
to identify what the heart does hold.

So I now remember
without ambivalence,
that your love and pardon 
are never bestowed in vain.

Even the smallness of the heart
and the scars left by sin,
fail  to become barriers 
to the abundance of
the love and mercy
which You, O Lord,
so gently have provided.

Now,
a quiet thanksgiving
fills my being
and in the light within.
Your gentleness and pardon, Lord,
have now healed my soul.
