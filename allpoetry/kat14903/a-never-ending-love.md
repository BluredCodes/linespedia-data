---
title: "A Never Ending Love"

All through the days, they laughed
All through the nights, they sang
All through the years, wedding bells rang

Savoring moments of romantic bliss
Embraced by a love so strong
In each others arms, they truly belonged

Tender kisses, special moments alone
Sacred vows, together they shared
Erotic expression, with passionate flair

A promise made to stay together
A promise made of love forever
A promise made to leave thee never

A torrent storm shattered precious dreams
Neither knowing, it would be their last
Their love becoming a thing of the past

They held each other close
He drew his final breath
Tears fell from her eyes as he met his death

All through the days, she mourned
All through the nights, she cried
All through the months, wishing she too would soon die
