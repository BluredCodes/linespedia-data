When I need a friend,
You are there for me.

When I shed tears,
You see them and wipe them away.

When temptations consume me,
You are my Strength and Shield.

When life is filled with burden,
You help me understand.

When my heart cries in sadness,
You are my comfort.

When I sin,
You forgive me.

When I repent,
you are my hope.

When I am lost,
You shine your light to bring me home.

When I call your name,
You answer me.

When I'm filllled with selfishness,
you open my eyes and show me your way.

When I come to You,
You receive me unconditionally.

When I receive You,
You love me even more.
