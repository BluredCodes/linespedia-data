As I walk along the seashore.
I feel the wet sand beneath me.
And the waves carressing my feet.
The cold dark wind,
blowing against my face.

I look across the horizon.
And I watch the sunset
in the distance.
Slowly....
My thoughts turn to you.

My eyes  fill up with tears,
as the memories flood my mind.
Lonliness fills my heart.

Wondering still, 
why you were taken away from me?
Swept away by a viscious storm.
Swallowed up,
by the waters before me.

The wind becomes stronger,
as clouds roll in.
Another storm begins.
Lightning flashes in the distance.
As the white caps grow bigger,
they call your name.

I step out further,
allowing the waters to rise up
over my body.
"TAKE ME ! TAKE ME TOO !"
"SO I CAN JOIN MY LOVE !"

Suddenly all is quiet,
and the night becomes still.
I wipe the tears from my eyes,
and breath a heavy sigh.

Reaching into my pocket,
I bring out glass bottle.
with a  message inside.
One last kiss,
before I cast it out
into the waters.

Then I watch as 
as it floats away.
The message I'm sending to you.
Goodbye my Love,
I'm able to go on now.
I Love you.....
Until We Meet Again.........
