OH, the many people
that we meet.
Simply by typing 
a few words across a screen.
From different walks of life,
as we join together in one room.

Some names we recognize,
while others are new.
Always there to encourage,
and share a laugh or two.

Even tears and hugs,
are sometimes shared.
Telling innermost thoughts.
Knowing that there's
someone  who cares.

For you never know
just who you will meet,
on this wonderful thing
we call the net.
You may end up with 
the best on-line friends,
that you know you'll never forget.

Though distance may separate you,
those little words on the screen
are what brings you close at heart.
You'll always be friends.
Even though you may be miles apart.....

(this poem is dedicated to Eddy and Tina.
Two of the dearest and heaven sent on-line friends ! God Bless you Both !)
