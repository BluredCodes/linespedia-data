Oh Great Sunset
How wondrous is your fiery furnace.
Dissolving all the sorrows of the day.
Providing comfort,
consuming all life's pains.

Oh Great Sunset
Whose beauty fulfills all passion.
Sparking romance while colorful rays
dance on the hearts
of the young and old.

Oh Great Sunset
Watching your flame blaze accross the sky.
Casting downward into the ashes of dusk.
I anticipate your return
