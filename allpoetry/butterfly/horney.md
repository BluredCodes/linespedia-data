I want it!
I need it!!
It's all I think about!!!
Give it to me now, hard and fast, 
Make the first time the best time.

I stop,
I can't.
To many reasons,
I shouldn't,
I couldn't.

To much to lose
What will I gain,
I sit alone with the emptyness,
The pain.I know the title is kinda out there, but I wanted something to catch your eye, haven't done anything like THIS before, but needed to get it out of my head and onto "paper."
