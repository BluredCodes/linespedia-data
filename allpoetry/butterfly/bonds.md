A Hand,
A face,
A touch.

Apart,
they are nothing.
Together,
They are everything

One girl,
One boy,
One man,
One woman.

Apart they exist
They survive.
Together,
they live,
they thrive.July 18 2001, I'm not sure if the title works for this or not, feel free to suggest a better one
