---
title: "He touched me . . ."
writer: "Butterfly"

His hands
Soft and gentle upon my body
Like kisses from the wind.
 
His lips,
caressing my skin,
pulling only moans from my lips.
 
My body clings to him.
My soul yearns for him.
I call his name.
 
And I awake
Alone,
Empty,
But hopeful for the next dream.
