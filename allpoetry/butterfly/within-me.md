title: "Within Me"
writer: "Butterfly"

I feel your body held within mine
I feel you deep within me
Ours souls and minds become one
 
Heaven and Earth,
Fire and Water,
All become one - as our bodies join.
 
Is it fate?
Destiny?
Chance?
What has brought me here?
What has allowed me to feel you deep within me
Within my body,
And my soul.
