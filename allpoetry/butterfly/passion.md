It burns within me
My life's fire.
 
This passion,
I can not feel it with my hands.
I can not touch it with my face,
And yet it is there,
Always.
 
Burning
Yearning
 
Beating within my heart
And soul
 
Wishing for me
to 
just
let 
go.
