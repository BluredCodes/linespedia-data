Out in a dry, cracked field,
I look to the sky.
I see the storm clouds gathering,
And I hope 
I stand a field,
And I hear the thunder roll.
It shook it me to the bone.
Then as I watch,
The sky opens up,
And the rain begins to fall.
Let it rain,
Let it pour,
I don’t care anymore,
Let the rain fall down on me.
The earth needs the rain,
The plants need the rain,
The animals need it too.
I need it to live, I need it to survive,
And so do you.
I need it to live, I need it to survive,
And so do you.

Let it pour,
I don’t care anymore,
