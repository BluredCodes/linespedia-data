---
title: "My Heart"
writer: "Butterfly"
slug: "my-heart"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/30216-My-Heart-by-Butterfly"

Upon my sleeve
I wear my heart
A heart of 
gold
stone
ice
fire

A heart full of
love
hate
passion
need

A heart that needs
everything
nothing
challenge
safety

Please,
Tread carefully.
