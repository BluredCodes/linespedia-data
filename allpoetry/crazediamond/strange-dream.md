ALL WAS SURROUNDED WITH BLUISH LIGHT.
THE EARTH WAS GOING TO DIE.
IT WAS RAINING.
BUT,
RAIN DROPS TURNED INTO GREENISH CHEMICALS,
AND THAT BURNED THE SKIN OF LIVING BEINGS.
EVERY ONE WAS RUNNING FOR REFUGE..
HOUSES WERE NO MORE SAFE..
WE WERE TOO RUNNING AWAY FROM OUR HOUSE.
I TURNED BACK TO HOME.
DAD! CALLED ME..
I LEFT THE HOME,
WITHOUT CARING FOR ANYTHING.
CAUSE I GOT CONFUSED.
WHAT TO PICK?
PEOPLE WERE SCREAMING..
NO ONE KNEW WHERE TO GO.
I SAW THEIR PALE LIPS 
IN THAT GLOOM OF HOPELESSNESS.
WITH HORRID SENSE OF 
DECAYING.
BURNED BODIES WERE SCATTERED EVERY WHERE.
AND DYING REMAINS..
