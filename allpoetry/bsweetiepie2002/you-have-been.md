---
title: "You have been"

You have been my support since
I could remember
You have been my back bone
since day one
You have been the answer to 
all of my prayers
You have been my angel 
in the sky above
You have been my shield 
when I get in a fight
You have been my strength 
when i was weak
You have been my umbrella 
through all my storms
You have been the cane
that I will walk with 
You have been the listener
to all of my many problems
You have been the audience 
to my speeches that i gave
You have been the light of my life
And i love you for that
