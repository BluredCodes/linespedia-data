What if I never had of meet you?
What if I never told you i cared?
What if I showed you my feelings?
Would you still be there?

What if you never called?
What if you never fell in love?
What if you proved your love to me?
Would I still hang around?

What if we weren't lovers?
What if we weren't friends?
What if we gave our eveything?
Would we even matter?

All these questions
arose in my mind
things i've searched in my heart
that i can not find

Maybe you can help me
tell me what it seems
Tell me the true words
and tell me what they meanI was bored.  So be gentle with the comments.  I couldn't think of anything else.  I am kinda mad.  So i wrote something close to nice.
