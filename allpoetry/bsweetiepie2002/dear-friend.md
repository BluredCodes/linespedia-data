Dear Friend,

Its been 50 years since the day I met you,
walking to your class.
I knew that I'd be friends with you,
the moment you walked pass.

I knew you'd be the perfect friend,
to share my secrets with.
And in my heart you'll come in first,
No you will never drift.

I'm glad that you are in my life,
The friend I grew old for.
When I became my husbands wife,
you were my counelor.

It broke my heart to lose you,
The day GOD called you home.
But I'll be there too,
And then we shall never walk alone.

I'm missing you dear friend,
my prayers go out to you.
My love for you has no end,
