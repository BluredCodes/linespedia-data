---
title: "My Nightmares"
writer: "BSweetiepie2002"

When people go to sleep,
that's just what they do.
What I'm about to say
is between me and you.

Things seem to happen,
not only to me.
The things that I witness,
a child should not see.

Sometimes I see death
knocking on friends and loved ones door.
The things that I dream,
I can't hold inside no more.

Sometimes I see people 
that I will meet in life.
I've even seen things like
who will be husband and wife.

I've wished for dreams
like the grass is green, then sun, and this.
But I get dreams like
 a bloody murder or a body in the mitts.

Sometimes I am afraid 
to go to sleep.
But for years and years
I've never said a peep.

My dreams are starting to calm down
but I dare not tell a soul.
But until the dreams stop,
my life will be on hold.
