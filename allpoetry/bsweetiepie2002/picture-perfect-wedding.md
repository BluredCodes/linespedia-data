Could this be foreal
Could this really be true
Is this the day
The day i say "I DO"

I'm starting to get the jitters
I'm getting butterflies
My stomach is upset
And the hot tears are burning my eyes

I hear my queue
There goes the music
Everyone's in awww

The doors are open
Their eyes all wide
Under my veil I hide

I want to runaway 
But I see his face
So ready for this to happen
I step with love and grace

It seems it took forever
But I finally made it there
He moved the veil and smiled at me
With oh, so loving care

And when the time came 
for me to say "Sweetie, yes I DO"
It came so easily you see
Because, baby I love you

I'm proud to be your wife
I'm glad you married me
I've waited several years about
Until you set me free.
