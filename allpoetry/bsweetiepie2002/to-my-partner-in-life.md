title: "To My Partner in Life!"

He's the only man I see,
the man that is with me.
He gives my toes a great curl.
I've known him since I was a little girl.
I love this mans smile.
I also love his style.
From his head to his feet.
Everyday when we meet.
It's like 2 kids at the park on a  swing.
He'll always be my number one KING!
This is to my partner in life
Love Always, your loving wife.
