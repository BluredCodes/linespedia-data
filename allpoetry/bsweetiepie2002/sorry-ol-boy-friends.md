Why is it every time
I get a man
he says "Baby your mine"

But what he's 
tryin to say is
"Baby we need some 
space and time"

Leaving me crying 
after sex
Then callin me
his ex

I don't understand
this game thats he's playin
This brotha aint the man
So why this brotha hatin

I gets a phone number
yeah its a mans
but you know dang well
not to stick yo hand down these pants

Brothers I'm sick of you 
screwin then leavin
Why don't you be a man
if you say you not cheatin

You say you love me
sweetie was that true
Or are those all lies
tryin to make me boo-who

I'm done with the lies
I'm tired of the games
I'm tired of your sorry butt
sayin you gon changeThis isn't for all the guys. I do know that there are some good guys left.  And i think that i have the best one!
