I cried all day tears
told you all my fears.
Gave you all my love
flyin free as a dove.

You never loved me
unloved is what you came to be.
When I should have held my head tall
you made me feel, oh so small!

I've done kartwheels and flips
I've done it all  but tripped.
You gave me too many facts
and yet you didn't appreciate that.

If you haven't figured it out
exactly what I've been talkin about.
Pack your stuff boo
cuz I'm breakin up with you!
