I've watch you grow
and take your first steps.
I was there when you said 
your first words.

I've lived next door to you 
for years and years.
We've spent countless hours
telling each other our secrets.

You were at all my 
slumber parties.
Even when your sister
didn't want you there.

I remember the trouble
that we would get in.
But you was always be by 
my side, even from the beginning.

I remember playin jump rope, 
mister freeze, and all the other games.
I remember hangin on the front porch
when it got late.  
(cuz thats where our folks expected us when the street lites came on)

But the memories are just that now
your far away from me.
But I'll join you in heaven one day
and you can show me it's inner mystery.

I'm missing you dear friend
My young, but fallen star.
I know that you will be safe from harm
And in my prayers at nite.

~In loving memory of Anna Marie Petty~
