When I didn't wanna talk to people
you were there to make me laugh and smile

When I didn't wanna stay at home
you were the one to encourage me to stick it out

When I didn't feel the love I should
you showed it to me, and gave it to me.

When I didn't care what was going on around me
you gave me something to care about, you!

When I didn't wanna say I love you anymore
you gave me that little breathe to say "Sweetie, I love you"

When I thought that I couldn't go no more
you pushed me all the way to the finish line. 

And when I thought all the odds were against us
you proved me wrong, by showing me just how strong the word love could be.Thank you sweetie! For showing me things that I know that i would have never seen if it wasn't for you.  I love you Seth, with all my heart and soul!!!!  =)
Love your Baby
Jennifer
