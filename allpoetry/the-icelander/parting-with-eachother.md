title: "Parting With Eachother"
writer: "The Icelander"

Everytime we imbrace eachother with a hug, We leave in tears...

Everytime we imbrace eachother with a kiss, we leave heartbroken...

Everytime we see eachother, we part in sorrow...



Until the next time, your name is on thy lips...
