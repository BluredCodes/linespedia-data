The days long, The nights sleepless.

Confusion is all that I know in my life at the moment.

I am at a four way stop,

Wondering in which direction to go.

Each way with an unknown future, an unwritten past.

The choices are unclear to me.

Not knowing what each choice would bring.

This is an average day in my world.

What about yours?
