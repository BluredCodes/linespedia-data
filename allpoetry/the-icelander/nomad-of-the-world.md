title: "Nomad Of The World"
writer: "The Icelander"
slug: "nomad-of-the-world"

The unknown path sees to come closer with each day that passes by.

Uncertainness of the unwritten future holds many questions,

In which the answers are more blank then the question itself.

Where will I end up next?

The ghosts of my dreams are haunting my thoughts,

Even when I'm awake now.

Feeling like I don't belong anywhere, makes my existence hard to handle.

I am what is called a Nomad of the World; also I'm known as a teenager.



It's hard being a teenager in the times that we live in today.

Not always knowing where you belong.... or your purpose.



Listen with your heart and let it guide you along the path of the unknown.
