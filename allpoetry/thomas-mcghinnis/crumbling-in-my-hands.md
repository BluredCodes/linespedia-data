title: "crumbling in my hands"
writer: "Thomas McGhinnis"

When I grasp life in my hands
and truly know what it is
I feel so happy 
but then it starts cracking
shattering
breaking
CRUMBLING

Friends die
parents pull away
no one loves 
no one cares
life crumbles in my hands
and slips through my fingers like sand

Thats what life is like when its crumbled, crushed and worthless
