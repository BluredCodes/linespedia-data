I had an epiphany once that I could be the best
but the people of this illiberal earth showed me wrong
they took me for granted and harshly overpowered me
I thought I had good ideas 
but they thought through single-tracked minds
but one day I might  show them
show them a great epiphany
an epiphany that will change the world....


forever..........
