title: "My Love For You"

My love for is deeper than the seven seas combined into one depth
It reaches farther than the most distant star and is more vast than the universe itself
It is eternity in every beat of my heart
It is the lick of my lips and the smile that draws you in

My love for you can never be compared
For it is uniquely absolute in it's nature
And impenatrable to any wicked charm
I will never give anyone a mere chance to destroy us
I would turn and walk away before they could even speak

My love for you can and will survive any and every battle
It can be shot at, burned, wounded almost to the point of death
But I assure you that it will never die but fully recover, with more strength and vigor than before

My love for you can withstand any trial and can correct every error
I will always find a way to make our love more complete, more sustainable
And I'll rival anyone who dares to stand in my way

My love for you will conquer and devious act or malicious deed that tries to falter us
It will filter out all forces that conspire to work against us
It is eternal and will follow you through every lifetime, with or without me

My love for you is the seductive sway in my walk, the grace in every step
It is loving words that I pen on paper and emotion in my voice when I speak
It is the sensuality I feel when you whisper my name
And the warmth that envelopes me when you are near

Loving you, being in love with you means everything to me
Without you there would be no us, there would simply be no me
I am lost in your essence beyond forever, and there I will always remain
Until thee end of time. I promise & I love you madly.

       Love always
     Your blushing bride,
