Locked in the deep confines of human imagination
    I can reach through the walls and touch the sky

'I'm not just the voice in your head, I'm the corruption in your blood.
Coursing it's way through your veins into madness, eliminating  all sane control.'

    I'm losing my grip on life.
Penetrating Darkness
    Falling, floating, my body has become invisible

Lost in my own mental domain
  Searching, finding and feeling nothing
Except the cool sweet Autumn breeze on my face
 
I wander
Oblivious to my actions and words

In my world you don't exist
In my world I can do anything I want to do
I can materialize any fantasy, make any wish come true
   
   I dream what I want to dream
I live on the moon
   I am every star in every universe
I'm the night, the fire dancing blue
   And I am the daemon caged away inside of you

          Dare to find me?
       Well take the long road through eternity
   I am beyond ever coming back to sensibility
       Fine in my liquid time of reality
   I am.....
       ....   Insanity.


Lady Dame
