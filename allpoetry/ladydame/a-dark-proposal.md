Move me with your wicked liquid soul
And embrace what you will be
I have been in love with you for seven thousand years
And now it's time for you to recieve
My truest gift, immortality

My love, do not be afraid of me
You were born for the purpose of becoming my queen
But your face insists that you do not believe
Look into thine eyes and all your pain I will relieve
Let me kiss you with my darkness, so you can see
That you an I, under this midnight sky
Are designed for eternity, and destined for her boundaries 
I love you so madly my beauty

Please, take thy hand
We'll flee from this mortal land
I wanna dance with you forever under the moon
Come along, light will be calling soon
We must breach our impending doom
Before we can fall asleep in each other's arms
Below the surface, in my room!!

My Deep Angel, rule at my side
Be my lady, my lover
Your secrets in me, can confide
Nothing from you I have to hide
Any doubts you have please subside and put aside
I will love and protect you until the day that I die
Remember what I say and why
It is because I have never been so in love, as I am with you

Thousands of lifetimes 
I have waited
Just for you
And now that you are here
I know exactly what I want to do

I wanna seize your mouth with mine
I wanna mate with your lips
I wanna make your soul cum with me to heavan
And from my crimson ecstacy, give you a sip
Know this...
All my power is yours, as long as you love me too

For I am the lord of this...darkest domain
Commit to me and as royalty you shall reign
Pledge your fealty, and you will never come to any bane
Your charm can unlarm any man to be insane
A single glance your way and all my control you drain
It has been hard enough for all these years to refrain
From stealing you away in the night and your virtue, obtain
As well as your affection, gain
Now that I have you, every eve together we shall lain
I promise with you never to play any games
Trust in me and all that I am saying
I am looking forward to being yours and you being all mine!

Oh, such sweet eternity.A little bit of the darker side of romance for you, ehh?!.
Enjoy
Lady Dame
