I am the Mistress of the nightI'll weave you in to my spell so tightI can hear your thoughts and play your gamesI know all your secrets and the twist in your veinsYour corrupt insideYou can run but you can't hideIn the mirror it is I who ride the tideAnd am the shadow lurking behind your sideI'm the color green in the iris of your eyesAnd the crafty ways by which you abideWe have been one since the beginning of timeYou are a part of me and I am one of the DivineThis is our destinyI belong in your bodyDeep inside you will find meDancing along the cliffs of paradisaical insanityThis life is meant to beYou freind...  Are my eternity.Lady DameJust sprang up one day.
