---
title: "You and Me"
writer: "LadyDame"

I've never known such pleasure before
As the sight of you when you walk in the door
Because I know exactly what your gonna do to me
Your gonna bring me to the land of the darkest ecstacies
And...sensitivities
I know what your thinking when you look at me that way;
Candlelight, some cherry wine from the cellar
My hips just swayin' away
Your hands in my hair, the scent of me on your skin
Entwined in magic released from deep within
I give you my soul with no reluctance
You my love are the most ultimate substance
More drunk in love could one ever be
Together we've made a crucial mark in eternity
And have wrought out our destiny
Hand in hand, just you and me
On our way to fortune and fantasy.

Lady Dame
