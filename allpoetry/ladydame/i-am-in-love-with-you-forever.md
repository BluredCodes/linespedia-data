title: "I Am In Love With You Forever"

Will you kill me in the night
And feed on my blood with such pleasure and delight
Or would I you save
From the dark crypts of a desolate grave
If you were to offer me your immortal kiss
I would so willing accept such a corruptive bliss
For my blood is your wine
Let me drink from your wrist
And I shall drink from thine
Lavish yourself in my red passion so sweet
My body is here, waiting for you to greet
I invite you in 
Please come with me to play in my sin
I want to bury myself deep within your blessed soul
And never let go 
I am yours forever, please know
Let me show you that I an the Master of seduction
I hold the highest rank in soul corruption
Can you hear my voice in the darkest hour
I call upon you for my pain to devour
Release me from these demented lands
I know my sorrow you deeply understand
My dark, dark Angel 
Can you taste my pain
Will you carry me on down to your maddening domain
I too wanna play your wicked little game
And know your pleasures so insane
You know how high passion I reign
In the presence of the Faust
For you madness I will feign
Take me behind the bloody rain
The innocent evil no longer from I refrain
I love you so madly that it's hard to obtain
Such awful and tedious pain
You've captured me into your vampire eyes
This absence of your presence I madly despise
In the winds can you hear my cries
When the time is right , we shall rise
Into the Darkness, here we come
Immortal souls never die, the deal will be done
He calls upon us now to please come
Into the cold cold world, where there is no wretched sun
And I am in love with you forever
