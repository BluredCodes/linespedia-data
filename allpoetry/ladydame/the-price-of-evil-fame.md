My Dark Lord has sent for me, to carry out a phrophecy
His loyal bride, of evil eternity
Bred from loins of ancient royaly
I willingly come to enforce what was meant to be 
The world will be stomped out by a fire legacy
Look into thine eyes and know that this very being
Will be the Queen...of Chaotic Catastrophe

I've watched the world through my scrying mirror
And have seen the pins of pain
I know all abour self-destructive greed
Mass murder and being insane
Protected in my palace
From all of life's twisted little games
I've now risen from the South,
To bring back the ones noted for their treacherous claims

"Your evil deeds have caught up with you. 
Now your shit out of luck and your payment is due!"
Rage filled ripe and purely black souls
Hundreds of thousands to be brought down low

You've all chosen your paths 
And have sealed you fates
You can fight,  or you can cooperate
But either way I'm gonna march your ass on down to the devil's gate

Locked up to be tortured and tamed
The price is very high for the name of evil fame
But you just had to have it
The pleasure of inflicting pain
Now Darkness is your only comforting companion
And music, the rain.


Lady Dame
