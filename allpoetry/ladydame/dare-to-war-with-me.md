title: "Dare to War with me"

Yeah you just try my patience, an enrage my mind
Test my strengths, push me past my limit
And prepare for what you will find

I guarantee you won't like what you see
Play your games on my grounds huh?
I'll deceive you destiny and destroy your every fantasy
Dare to war with me?

I've watched you lie and scheme
With a dark motivation left unseen

What is it that you thought you could accomplish, by becoming my rival?
Go ahead, battle with my wit
But soon you will realize how mentally unfit you are
For this trial of survival and expertise
Dare to war with me?!

I've played this game
And am quite noted for my fame
The outcome is always the same
I win, your remain
Everyone is momentarily entertained

I've seen right through your antics from the first
You have no image with me, I know you at your worst
But still you push, prod and pull
Always looking like the fool
But never knowing what quite to do
Except my every man be used and left confused

I pity you, but not enough to be your friend-ever again

You've sinned your last sin with me,and have told your last lie
I don't befriend people like you, who are nothing compared to I

Malicious act I knew you wrought, but I silently decided to you, tolerate
I should have counseled with and listened to my thoughts
Who bade with you not to communicate

But with your words, my trust you lulled
So I gave my friendship for you to hold
Then you decided as to be so bold
And slander thy name in a friendly household
Well our friendship quickly doth unfold
To my dislike you have been sold
Ugly in my thoughts, spiritless and cold
Unworthy you are of any human mold

In this case I was the fool
I was the one who had been had
I gave in to you even though my conscience knew
That you were deviously, deviously bad
And would eventually make me mad

Your own anger and maliciousness will fall back upon you
Karma will eat you alive for the havoc that you do
Drama Queen in your name to everyone that your introduced
People like you need to be locked up, strapped down and subdued

So much for giving you a chance to be in my privileged life


Lady DameThis poem is for all the miserable people in life that try to make all the happy people miserable too.
