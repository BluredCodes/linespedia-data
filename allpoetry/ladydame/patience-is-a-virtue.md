The Boundaries of Hell are in sight and sound
I can hear the screaming bodies hit the ground
A Death Angel leads me to the gates
There for my wedding ceremony I will wait
The Dark Lord has summoned me
And ordered that I am his bride to be
A black lace veil covers my pure white face
It hides my fear but gives me grace
I carry the head of a weak-filled man
My Lord ordered him dead and brought to his land
So I did the deed to please my groom
And he was quite amused that I carried it out so soon
That night to darkness, I was wed
An after the ball, I was in our evil bed
Doing the deed of giving my Lord a son
For him to teach how an army is run
But what nine weeks later
In my dark red room
A devil's child grows fast in a woman's womb
Our son was born with power in his gaze
He was Satan's heir, and he we would raise
Thousands of years flew by and we eventually got old
The Prince of Darkness was ready to take control
He argued with his father and demanded to rule the lands
His own son dared to defy him
So out of his father's land he was cast into the burning sands
There he would remain, until the death of his Lord
Until the control was his right
He would not walk through the palace doors
500 years passed
No death or decay
100 years later 
The prince was still cast away
350 years the after day
He was invited back to meet with the evil king
In a short time the prince arrived on his black horse with wings
His father decided to let the prince have command
For he was growing old and weary
To aged to demand
The prince graciously accepted
He did what he had to do
Very slowly the young king learned
That patience is a virtue
