A desert storm settles against the nights shadeAmongst the gallows trees our faces creep and fadePure white masks with dark demoniac eyesAs we lick our lips, pass the bloody criesPure bred death equals pure deadly mindsOur skeleton like hands...clawed and definedSearching through the nights, seeking worn out soulsOur raging right come and goThe lust in love for blood and painCaptures their minds to play our gameInsanity courses through their veinsA Demoniac Romantic as they claimBorn into darkness, ripe and meanPassion and blood is what we fiendQueen of the Stars, Goddess of the nightEnvelopes her children within a spell so tightCome with me, taste the desireStrike up inside your burning fireOur only life is the blood red potionA heartbeat on your lips, bounds a devotionFeel the living and steal their graceBeauty the innocence that covers their faceRule the world with our Ancient Power      An I, the Priestess...  Will watch from my tower.Lady Dame
