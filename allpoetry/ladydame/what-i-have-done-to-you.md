title: "What I have done to you"

I am the Master of your Dreams
The Soul Contriver of all things
I can see into the dungeons of your mind
And twist up time to do my bidding

In the palm of my hand
I hold the Fates quick command
I can steal your soul through your eyes
And you will never actually realize
     What I have done to you.
