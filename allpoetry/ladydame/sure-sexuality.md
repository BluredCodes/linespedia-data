The seductive sway of a naked womans hips
soft moisture on pale crimson lips
midnight moon, lunar eclipse
passion swelling within the air, fire lit
In all this, sure sexuality

The scent of fire and forest on a man
a warm wool flannel and big husky hands
exposed ebony flesh, lusting in demand
drugged with desire, soul screams in silent demand
Give me, your sure sexuality

Our bodies entwined in sensual sin
the power of pleasure cloaking us within
reaching higher & higher, stirrings of climax begin
melting moans move minds, thoughtlessness wins
In all these ingredients are in, sure sexuality

Sweaty and sated, lying in the candle glow
closing thine eyes, slowly calm takes force & flows
moon rapidly setting, the sun about to show
heaviness claims me under as the wind rustles my hair...
     blowing from the window
Even in sleep my body craves to be known, by sure...sexuality.
