staying by your side at nightwaiting for the golden lighti wished on a star above my headand i wished that i would soon be deadi can not hold up anymorethere's nothing to dream fori wondered about my heart and soulsearching in a deep dark holewatching the stars in the skyhoping that i soon would flyi need no more despair tonighti need no more reasons to fightmaking sure the sun may shinei wish i could say i'm fineand even though the world is turningi know in hell i will be burningi can not hold up anymorefor i just may fall into the doorof the light tonight in the skymy heart, my soul, please go flydoves flying east and to the westover my head while i lay to restgrave stones by my side next to mefinally, at last i do feel freethe starlight sky overheadthanking god for i am deadmy life has been lost in the nightmy family's faces were a sighti hear the voices from up abovei can see faces, once filled with lovei want to say goodbye with lovebut i will be watching from up aboveas i walked toward the lighti turned to look at the saddened sighti looked at my body at resti guess it was truly for the best
