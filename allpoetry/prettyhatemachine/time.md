title: "Time"
writer: "prettyhatemachine"

the dark hour passes
the sand falls through
quiet stand the masses
as they witness something new

the coming of the age
the breaking of the mold
the burning, red rage
the fall of the bold

twisting in chains
locked in a cage
unbelievable pains
death takes the stage

the last word is spoken
a death for all
another heart broken
into the fire we all fall
