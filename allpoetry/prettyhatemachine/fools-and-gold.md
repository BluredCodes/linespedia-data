fools and gold


do what your told 
dying is for the old
spend your riches
like fools and gold

time can only tell
your descent from heaven to hell
whenever you smile 
the fools always frown

ashes come from fire
the weak begin to tire
they never know what's there
their wealth is their only care

reap what you sow
the stories are told
how fools overlook happiness
to polish their goldthis tells many things about me and about the world... 2 different stories are in this
