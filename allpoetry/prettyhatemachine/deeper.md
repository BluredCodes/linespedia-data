deeper and deeper i fallsick and tired of it allneed not sorrow but a prayer looking for someone to be therecan't find what's needed to sayeverything stands in my wayaddictions so strong they tear me apartbeing alone, out of sightdeep within the darkthis hole seems never endingbut i'm still scared as i'm fallingreaching out for a handnobody reaches back they just watch and standas i fall into the blackcant help otherscant help myself for now i'll take everything i have to giveand put it on a shelflet me drown, let me fallsmash my head against the walli feel there is no turning backi'm heading further into the blackto all my friends i hope you learnor eventually you'll have your turnyou'll be drawn into this trapunable to turn backas you fall into the black
