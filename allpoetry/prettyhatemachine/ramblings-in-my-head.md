title: "Ramblings In My Head"

help me, please
you run
i fall
leave me behind
leave me again
drown, i
sorry
i ask you
kill me now
you did it before
why not again
running still
the vision of you
it grows smaller
i grow weaker
drown, i
leave me here
is what i ask now
and let me feel
going byeif i'm left alone nobody will care for me to go
