thinking like no other
her mind is twisted yet beautiful
her thoughts, so wonderful
yet some so black

feeling like no other
she understands the head trips
her care and love is true
she still feels pain like others
in which some feelings are blue

seeing like no other
she dreams in red
her dreams, so wonderous
and some so dark

hearing like no other 
she always hears the music
she hears what i hear
singing me a song
whenever she smells my fears

touching like no other 
her caress is soft and warm
and the words that she whispers 
could calm a storm

her beauty, so wonderous
so perfectly crafted is her body

the winds cry her name
and the candle flickers when she walks in 
but like the rest of us she still is not without sinplease give me an idea for the title
