i'll find an angel one day
if it's the last thing i ever do
and to feel her touch upon me
would be a sensation that's always new

only she could save me from my sorrows
and make the rest of the world go away
i would feel comfort in her presence
and she would brighten my day

together we would walk
and love would be our guide
and when we both have passed
we'll be laid to rest side by side

i'll find an angel one day
if it's the last thing i ever doi'll find an angel one day... or not...
