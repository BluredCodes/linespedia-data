i smoke another cigarette
and i feel it bring me closer to my life's debt
i focus away from the bad and i can only think of her
but now it seems as if the future is a blur
and i lie awake in bed
and i can only dream of holding her close and kissing her head
and bring her the happiness that everyone craves
i wish i could be with her all the way to the grave

i wake up to another day
outside, i only see gray
i see myself alone 
my fate was carved in stone
just to see her face 
let's me know of God's grace
the touch of her hand
is a soft caress like the eroding of sand

i'll stand alone forever
never to be with someone and say "we're together"
i walk this path to nowhere
all by myself with no one to share
it seems as if my island in the sun
only comes with room for one
if only i had the chance
