why do you look then turn away?
i'm left to drown in this hole

forsaken in your smile
i feel the need to hide

i said i will not give in and not conform
but i can change for you

how do you and i still move when time stops
and everybody freezes?

and then later it was just a dream
i see now but i'm afraid to live it

slowly i fall apart
how can i let it happen when my guard is up?

i begin to drift away from you
and i disappear 

further away from you
