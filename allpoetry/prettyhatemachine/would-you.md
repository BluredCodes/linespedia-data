---
title: "Would You...?"
writer: "prettyhatemachine"
slug: "would-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/32846-Would-You...--by-prettyhatemachine"
---

i ask you not to baby me,
but to give me love endlessly

i wonder if you would care
or if you would ever be there?

i ask will you be the one to wipe away my tears
and wash away my fears?

would you be the one to hold me tight,
lay beside me, and kiss me goodnight?

i know only you could bring me such blissful pleasure
and i cherish you more than any metal treasure

i know i love you with all my heart,
but not knowing the way you feel tears me apart

i know i would never be blue,
if we could share a love so true
always together,
me and youkill me please
