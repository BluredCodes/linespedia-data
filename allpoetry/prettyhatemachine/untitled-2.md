these words that lie here 
they help me release my pain
they wash clean away the blood that stains
they destroy my fear

a heart so cold 
but it wants to be warm
lonely as it grows old
it also grows torn

a soul so lost 
it calls to be found
but no one is around

this call for someone to release me
it goes unanswered and may never be
i am a fool to think 
someone would be there for me

alone i am 
twisted is my soul
these words help me
