locked down
no breaking free
trapped within these demons inside me

badly beaten
i lie face down
i watch as my blood runs on the ground

mentally tortured
these thoughts inside me
cover my eyes in blood where i cannot see

sitting still
i stay in my cell
i'm trapped inside my own hell

unknown feelings
what i cannot control 
tortures my mind and my torn soul

lacking love
makes the path i walk even more narrow
i now know the thing piercing my heart
is a broken arrowi wrote this a lil while ago, but i remember having a crazy feeling when i wrote it and it was hell... i wrote my thoughts as usual
