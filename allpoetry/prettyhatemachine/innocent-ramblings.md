tea cups and razor blades
oh, blood runneth over

pictionary and charades
it's hard staying sober

bullets and knives
it's hard living this way

loss of lives
i think it's time i go away

table for two
it's me and the knife

grave for one 
i took my lifea mixture of depression and spontaneity... it kind of tells a story though so it doesn't seem like it's spontaneous to me because i know the story in my head... i hope this one makes you think
