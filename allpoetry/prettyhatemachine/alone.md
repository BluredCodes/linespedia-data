title: "alone"
writer: "prettyhatemachine"

i will never be mended
this heart is forever torn
my needs, never to be attended
into loneliness i am sworn

never reaching to another soul
i'm left with this gaping hole
i will always walk alone
my fate is carved in stone

a dream never to be fulfilled
a hole in my heart is drilled
out of all the fish in the sea
there will never be a "you and me"

isolated i will stay
feeling empty each day
never to be held
in love is what i have failed
