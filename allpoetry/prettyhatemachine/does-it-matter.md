---
title: "Does It Matter"
writer: "prettyhatemachine"

i am here for you
or does it even matter?

all this life to live
more dreams of mine to shatter

am i supposed to care?
or does it even matter?

why am i here?
this, in my mind, does clatter


my existence is false
you don't want me here anyway

i'm tired of feeling this
and being lonely each day

now i will cut everyone off from me
so i can slip into my own eternity

you only have everything to gain
once you have lost everything

and now that i've hit bottom
i want nothing

and when i leave...
well, does it matter?
