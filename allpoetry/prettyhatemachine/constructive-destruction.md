title: "Constructive Destruction"

tragic desire
released from fire
beautiful sin 
screams from ire

wonderful lies
the hateful disguise
silicon words
in deceitful eyes

soothing syringe
injecting cringe
surreal high
visions of tinge

divine solution
healthy pollution
lively death
self execution
