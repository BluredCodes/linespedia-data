this blood that is taken from me
the pain and the agony
the tears and pain
the cold wind and rain

these flames that burn my flesh
the the sensation always fresh
the burning of my soul
the hurt takes it's toll

all the times i've cried
wanting to feel alive
but i know i truly have lived
since i carry this pain insidethis poem is kinda inspired by nine inch nails songs, "hurt" and "something i can never have". i have my own reason for these words up there, but i think other people will find reasons they feel and live in my words above. i hope this will make you think or make you feel. thanx
