need to feel mental bliss
i lie awake between reality
and a dreams kiss

need to feel it
take away the pain
lost in time
comfort as the blood stains

the burning stops
my eyes are still closed
red is the blood
but blurred visions are all i know

i dream now
my thoughts so real
a knock at the door
this high, so surrealthink what you want... i get a bad sleep... i write about how it makes me feel...
