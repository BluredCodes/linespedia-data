i see everyone and they are upside down
all their smiles turn to frowns

there i see
all the world is frowning upon me

black turns to white
heaven fades away
day turns to nite
skies turn gray

i see everyone and they are laughing
all their smiles turn to cries

there i hear
the world's screams from it's fear

mountains turn to dust
the world sighs
gold turns to rust
all hope diesthink wut u will
