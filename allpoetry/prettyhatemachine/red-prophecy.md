title: "Red Prophecy"

cure my eyes for what they have seen
and cure my soul for where it has been

seek the river, the crimson waters
blood shines through, the virgin daughters

look to the sky for it is now red
look for help, but everyone is dead

ah, the time is upon you
it is time to go

don't put up this fight 
you can't win, you know

this prophecy of destiny
uncoiled throughout your life
a damned epiphany
ends you with a knife
