your apathy
my apathy

all of us could care less what each other thinks
no matter how many times i justify myself
all you can come up with is "because i said so"
or "because we want you home tonight"
we never do anything anyway
no matter what you always come up with something so incredibly stupid

can you not tell we are totally different?
can you not tell that i dont like you?

it doesn't matter if you were a teenager once
times have changed
do you not understand that?
your closemindedness annoys the living hell out of me
what makes you happy is that i'm alive and endure no physical harm

but you dont understand what goes on inside
you never will

you may think it's just a phase
you may think it's just because i'm a teenager

age doesn't matter
no matter what you do and who you are
your excuses, so ignorant and stupid
you will never understand
we will never think alike
we agree on things so common
but we'll never come to terms
with the things we always argue about

although you've much experience 
it makes you blind to other's reality
i wish you could understand 
and maybe one day we can talk and have an intelligent and interesting conversationmy parents annoy me... they are too closed minded to understand what we argue about and what i feel and what i am going through... it doesn't matter if they were a teenager before, they don't live as a teenager in today... and to all you parents out there who are reading this, please understand that your child will be a teenager one day if they aren't now and the best thing you can do is support them and try to understand them and not let ignorance and closed minded, self righteous shallowness get in the way... IT'LL SAVE YOU MANY ARGUEMENTS
