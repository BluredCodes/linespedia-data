---
title: "Run Away With Me"
writer: "prettyhatemachine"
slug: "run-away-with-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

come with me
let's take a trip 
to a land in the sea
let's run away 
and be free
just you and me

we'll never look back
we'll look to the sky
and watch as the moon goes by
on this island we'll stay 
until we both die

just me and you
and the ocean, blue
the sparkling water
it's paradise
just me and you
won't it be so nice

but you're only a dream
and away i shall put you
this dream of an island
and me and you too
