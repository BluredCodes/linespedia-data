sorrow anger fear and lack of motivation
many a cause for all this frustration
things are long gone that will never be back
and i feel so many reasons to attack
attack this anger, attack this fear
i wish i could draw close friends no matter how far or near

wilted is the rose
no longer it grows
another will come along 
as the planter sews
but the rose will not be the same
it will not, cannot replace the one 
that had grown when the planter had begun


perfect little world everything is right
how can that be when someone's always feeling this fight
the thoughts of society are the evil of today
attack them with justice but they never go away 
pity do I take on all of the mortal kind
for instead of attacking straightforward we attack from behind


wilted is the rose
no longer it grows
another will come along 
as the planter sews
but the rose will not be the same
it will not, cannot replace the one 
that had grown when the planter had begun


scared by the corrupted and immoralized
stand up and fight back against their lies
be not of machine but of your mind
share the wealth with all mankind
forget this candy coated life, i ripped mine bear
no longer do i see lies because the truth now lies there


open your eyes
there is no truth in disguise
society is what i despise
