title: "where my thoughts lie"
writer: "prettyhatemachine"

far beyond the dimmest view
my thoughts lie

in them i see me and you
thoughts soar and fly

these thoughts then become dreams
and all my hopes die

my mind tears away at the seems
and my heart pains once more

already hopeless
i lock my heart's door
