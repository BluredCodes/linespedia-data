i just don't understand 
why we cannot hold hands 

why you don't want it to be this way 
makes me wonder and sigh each day 

how can we be so close 
but yet away, so far 
i wonder if we ever look at the same stars 

you make me so happy 
i dont know if you see 
but would it not make you happy 
if it were just you and me? 

oh, how i love you 
your very presence 
brings me to my knees 
and only you have my heart to seize 

i want to be with you forever 
i really want us to be together 

why you do not want it to be this way 
makes me wonder and sigh each day 

i'd never do you harm 
and i'd never do you wrong 
all i want is to stay wrapped in your arms 

i just don't understand 
why we cannot hold hands, 
why we can't be together everyday, 
and why you don't want it to be this way*sigh*
