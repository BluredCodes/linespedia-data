the beat of the drum
steadily beating
time is constantly fleeting

now is the time
dont ask why
tomorrow may not come 
tomorrow may die

grasp hold of the present 
for now is what matters
the future may hold
dreams that are shattered

the war inside isn't over
and it can't be easily won
take control now
