title: "The Rain's So Cold"
writer: "prettyhatemachine"

the rain's so cold
and i'm so lonely
the skies are a sad gold
and my tears are falling constantly

my pain is getting deeper
reaching the core of my heart
it's like climbing a hill that gets steeper
and it slowly tears me apart

the night cries loneliness
and the day weeps of sadness
and i'm always set up for the fall
hurting and bleeding for all

and the last day for me
is when i fall victim to the sea
where i'll drown my sorrows of today
and make the world go away
