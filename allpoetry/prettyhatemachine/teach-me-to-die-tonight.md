title: "teach me to die tonight"
writer: "prettyhatemachine"

tonight it's just me and you
and i have one question to ask
can you teach me to die?

just point your gun to my face
and blow me away
please, i wanna fly 

i want to be the wind 
and i'll carry the bullet on my back
tonight is the night my blood runs dry

can you feel my heart racing?
the eagerness to feel eternal bliss
it's all carried out with the pull of the trigger
and the bullet's kissthis poem flowed so well as i wrote it. i ask that you please psychoanalyze it and think about it when u read it. i felt the poem reach me after i wrote it. i get a crazy vibe and feeling from it.
