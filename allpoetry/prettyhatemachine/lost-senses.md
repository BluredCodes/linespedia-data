---
title: "lost senses"
writer: "prettyhatemachine"
slug: "lost-senses"
source: "AllPoetry"

look into my eyes
since yours are so blind

touch the skin
that crawls from beneath these wounds

taste my mind
the one i lost so long ago

hear the thoughts
of those abandoned in your hell

smell the wretched odor
the stench of your fucked up reality

now open your eyes 
and meet my plague
which is your fall into death's black poolyou have everything to gain, once you've hit bottom
