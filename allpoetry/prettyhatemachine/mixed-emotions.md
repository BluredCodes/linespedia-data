lives faded
judges jaded
known hate
not stated

piscean panic
depression, manic
all rituals
now satanic

blindly trusting
sanity rusting
in me,
sword thrusting

help cries
living lies
heart blackened
soul diesi'm not satanic or anything. i dont know how to tell you my feelings in this one, so it is supposed to make you think. don't harshly criticize it for being so abstract.
