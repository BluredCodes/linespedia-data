title: "I Wish Away All These Gray Days"

sometimes i wonder
when i find sunshine
how it still rains and thunders

the clouds turn from white to gray
and the flowers wilt
then there goes my day

the sky is always crying
and it's always falling on my head
now is one of the times i wish i were dead

why does it have to pour on me?
everytime i stand in the glow of my sunshine
i find another reason to be unhappy

i dont ask much
i just wish i was more easily pleased
i wish away all these gray days
and that life wasn't such a teasewritten in a down and depressed state but i have nothing to be down about... i really wish this wouldn't happen because it's annoying as hell... ah welp
