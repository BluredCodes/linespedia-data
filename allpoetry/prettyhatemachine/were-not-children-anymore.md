title: "We're Not Children Anymore"

on the playground
the swings rock back and forth
because of the wind

the abandoned see saws
lay dormant

the merry-go-round,
bare of hands on the handles

the desolate slide
left to rust

but yet we still wish we could play on them and be happy

happiness may be a choice
but you have to find it

ignorance is bliss
being young can be missed

but sophisticated happiness wins our desirewe're not children anymore and it's hard to be easily amused... that's all
