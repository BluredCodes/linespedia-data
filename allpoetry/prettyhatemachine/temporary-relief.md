a divine needle
syringe filled with liquid
a high so wonderful

an empty place
filled with the injection
happiness occurs in short but sweet moments

a hard fall
coming down from heaven
meeting hell after such enlightenmentthis is not exactly about doing drugs... it's more of showing an escape and choosing an easy but temporary relief... i felt it was easier to show a easy and temporary relief by using drugs... just think hard about this one...
