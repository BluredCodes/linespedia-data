title: "I Want To Be A Teddy Bear"
writer: "prettyhatemachine"

i know this sounds funny
but i want to be a teddy bear
i want to always be there for someone
i want to make them feel better and put a smile on their face

i know this sounds funny
but i want to be a ray of sunshine
i want to shine down on someone when they are low
i want to give and show them light when they feel lost

i know this sounds funny
but i want to love someone
i want to let someone know they are the highlight of my day
i want to hold someone and have someone to hold me

i know this sounds funny
but i want...i guess the meaning is obvious...
