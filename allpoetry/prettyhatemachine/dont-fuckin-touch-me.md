your arms reached out
and you tell me to come here
no matter who you are

DONT FUCKIN TOUCH ME

you bullshit liars
you hate
you're against me

i stand just to fall
for all of you
i'm tired of being the same sacrifice

dont tell me it's ok
dont tell me i'll get over it
dont tell me you're sorry
 
just 
DONT FUCKING TOUCH ME

i'm sick of it
give me the damn gun...i dont care if it has fuck in the title... i want isolation... i wanna go away... i dont wanna bother them anymore...
