---
title: "My Unspoken Valentine"
writer: "prettyhatemachine"

i'm yours but yet you're not mine
i want to call you baby, you're so fine
this one's for you
it's my unspoken valentine

i love you for everything you are
you are most important to me, by far
and i'm always waiting and i'll always stay blue
in my heart there's always room for you

and though we may always be apart
i write you this valentine
and i give you my heart*sigh*
