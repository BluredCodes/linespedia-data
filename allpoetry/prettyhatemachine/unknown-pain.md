without you everything falls apart
you shoot your arrow through my heart
you are somtimes filled with happy lies
but let me rot and be covered by flies

you can be many different things
sometimes you're round and small
sometimes you're human
no matter what you always leave me to feel ruined

i know you hate me but i still love you
this unknown pain makes me so blue

although you torment me
i feel i still need you 
you bring me up and take me back down
but the feeling is always so newdo you ever feel like you're missing something that you feel you need to keep living, and you keep living just in case you might find it?
