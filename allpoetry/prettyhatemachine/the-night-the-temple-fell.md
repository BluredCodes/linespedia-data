as the night went through
he walked from temple to temple

mentally beaten
he forfeited the battle

he tried to find his place
and he found another temple

he stopped for a rest
and thought of the battle

thinking it was best, after all was considered a loss
he gave in to another war
he put the gun to his temple
then fell to the floori'm pretty sure we all think about suicide at one point in time. some people actually go through with it. i wrote this to describe some of the motions in depressions or anxiety.  i know i used the word "temple" a lot but it's showing different things each time it is used.
