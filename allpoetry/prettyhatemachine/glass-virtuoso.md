title: "Glass Virtuoso"

music, was given to me
it was a brilliant escape you see
it has taken over everything now
and to it i give my vow

but yet i'm a glass virtuoso
i can stand tall to live music
but i shatter as soon as i'm hit 

and i would trade music 
for the rest of my life 
to be happy

...and all the glass fell to the floor
and i am shattered once more
