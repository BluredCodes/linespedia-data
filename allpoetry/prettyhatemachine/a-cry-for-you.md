---
title: "A Cry For You"
writer: "prettyhatemachine"
slug: "a-cry-for-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/33816-A-Cry-For-You-by-prettyhatemachine"

whine
whimper

where are you?

hold me please

sniff
sigh

where did you go?

do you not wanna be here?

tear
cry

i want you here
i love you
stay here
dont... leave me... please...another lonely day
no one to wipe these tears away
no one here, no reason to stay
