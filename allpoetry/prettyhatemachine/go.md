title: "Go"
writer: "prettyhatemachine"
slug: "go"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

force the river to flow
give illness to the fever
make way for the plague

give way and inhale
take the high
spare the gray

take your time
there is no clock
just speed of the moment

lay your head down 
and forget
let it end 
feel no regret
