---
title: "The white line (homeless)"
writer: "wtk82"
slug: "the-white-line-homeless"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/26391-The-white-line--homeless--by-wtk82"
---

Across the white line walks a man with no home.
On the side of the road walks a man carrying everything he owns.
With no real purpose, with no real shelter,
he just keeps on walking along the white line.

Across the white line travels a man with no direction.
On the side of the road travels a man carrying nothing but confusion.
With no real purpose, with no real destination,
he just keeps on traveling along the white line.

Across the white line moves a man with no family.
On the side of the road moves a man carrying only his insecurity.
With no real purpose, with no real love,
he just keeps on moving along the white line.

Across the white line passes a world that he doesn’t know.
On the side of the road passes a life that no one sees.
With no real purpose, with no real hope,
he just keeps on roaming along the white line.
