title: "To become a child again"
writer: "wtk82"

To go back in time,
to become a child again.
To search long and hard
for that once youthful soul.

Just ot be a child again
to be able to have no stress.
Just wanting to run in circles
and skip rocks down the street.

Learning to ride a bike again
would mean so much.
Wishing that I could still
play hide and go seek.

Just be a child agin
to be able to have no worries.
Just wanting to love Christmas Eve
as I struggle to go to sleep.

To ride without training wheels
would be much the same.
Filled with so much excitement
and experiencing the thrill.

Just to be a child again,
to have a care free life.
Just wanting to hide Easter eggs
and experience finding one myself.

Just to be a child again,
to have that once great imagination.
Just wanting to color pictures
as I try not to go outside the lines.

Playing outside all day long
unitl someone says, "It's time to come in"
To be able to yell, "Just 5 more minutes"
would mean so much.

Just wanting to go back in time
and become a child once again.
Only wishing to find
that so called youthful soul.
