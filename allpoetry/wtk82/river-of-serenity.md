---
title: "River of serenity"
writer: "wtk82"
slug: "river-of-serenity"
source: "AllPoetry"

Upon the banks
I bathe.
In the silence of nature
I pause.
Staring in the eyes of Serenity,
I walk farther away from shore.

Seeing my reflection move,
with every breath she breathes.
Feeling the ripples travel
with every word I hear.
Drifting downstream
with my head facing the clouds.
The sky crystal clear.
The wind echoing through the trees.

Above the sun shines,
painting the river
shimmering shades of pink.
Glistening with every touch,
and glowing with every word.

Upon the river
a waterfall appears.
Surrounded by a mist
a rainbow within.
Touchable
as the mist seems alive
Unreachable with every 
grasp, still I try.

An echo gathers around
as she whispers my name.
Her hand touching my soul
guiding me downstream.
Upon her heart I ride
drifting for eternity.
Lost in her love,
a river of serenity.
