---
title: "Moments like this"
writer: "wtk82"

It's raining and cold inside.
I get lost in the clouds.
There I stand above your grave,
Today is the day, here’s my goodbye.

There he walks along the road,
a poor old man, with no place to go.
I wanted to stop, but had to get home,
I didn’t even try to say hello.

and it’s moments like this I can live without.
and it’s moments like this I don’t need in my life.

I wake up to see the sun rise,
there beside you as your body comes alive.
I hear your first yawn, it’s a reminder,
To turn over and see you smiling.

I wake up to see the curtains sway,
there beside you as your eyes find their way.
See the first light as it travels through the air.
So I turn to watch as it paints your hair.

and it’s moments like this I can’t live without.
and it’s moments like this I need in my life.

Tonight I dreamed my grandpa was still alive,
there beside him as we talked all night,
His voice echoed to the sound of the wind,
I awoke alone, wanting to hear him again.

Tonight I dreamed about starting a family.
There beside her, stood all of my children.
Still in bed, I’m awakened by laughter,
kids running in, just wanting their daddy.

and it’s moments like this I can’t live without.
and it’s moments like this I need in my life.
