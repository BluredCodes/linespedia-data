title: "Grandma's rose"
writer: "wtk82"

wondering within
a field of daisies.
Lying in the flowers,
a place of no explanations.

Resting gently,
in the garden of eternity.
There she stoops,
the angel of Serenity.

The warmth felt
between her feet
Engraved into the dirt
of the ground beneath.

There we stand
exchanging tears.
Embracing in this moment,
from my heart to hers.

Reaching down
to the endless meadows.
There she stops to speak,
“from me, a grandma’s rose.”

“A gift from my heart
for the one you love.”
“Tell her it was grown by
an angel above.”

There we stand
exchanging goodbyes.
Embracing in one last moment,
before I open my eyes.


Wondering within
a field of trees.
Lying in their shadows,
a place of peace.

Resting gently,
in the garden of eternity.
There she appears,
earth’s angel of serenity.

The warmth felt
between our souls
Engraved into my vessel,
my heart overflows.

Reaching out
to her radiant glow.
There I stop to speak,
“from me, my grandma’s rose.”

“A gift from my heart
for the one I love.”
“Grandma says
it is from the gardens above.”

There we stand
exchanging tears.
Embracing in this moment,
from my heart to yours.
