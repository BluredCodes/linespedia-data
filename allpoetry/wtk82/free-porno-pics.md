Free Porno Pics

A simple phrase,
but a powerful one
indeed.

with 3 words,
actling like a 
magnet to freaks.

these 3 words,
the future 
of the internet.

as it seems,
to draw attention,
but fails,
to have real meaning.

The cyber generation
our new name.
place of freedom
or a place of waste.

forgeting such phrases
as A PEOPLE PERSON,
SOCIAL, OUTKAST,
IN CROWD, OUT CROWD.

With one click 
of the mouse,
you are the popular one.

No love at first sight.
How about love at first click.
Missing out on the best times: 
getting to know someone.

but with one click,
and a peep into a profile.
you are cupid, 
and faces are useless.

what is next.
how bout cyber sperm.
yea that is right
online pregnancy,
hey it has a catch.

caught up in the moment
of cyber love,
forgetting,
that it is a keyboard,
your making love to.

but hey it beats you hand 
right.

No u are still using you hand.

limited time only.
you get the cash
we'll show the ass.

free lives
free lives

lost in the world
of cyber porn.
