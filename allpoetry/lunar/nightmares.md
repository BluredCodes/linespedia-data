title: "Nightmares"
writer: "Lunar"

When the calmness of the sea, and moonlit skies combine,is when our dreams take on the forms, of what is in our minds.These images in each of us, are how we feel within,they strive on all our love and hate, our goodness and our sin.The nightmares come so vividly, and fairy tales unclear,the creatures prey upon our souls, and fill our hearts with fear. Our dreams a mere reflection, of the images we see,if we fear that we're doomed in life, it's what will come to be.If imprisoned in a eerie swamp, Grim Reaper right behind,and you fear not a death today, and answer you shall find.When at night we close our eyes, and surrender at the gate,pay no mind to what they say, the do not hold your fate.Give no power to the form, that stands in front of you,take it on, let go your gaurd, is what you have to do.You must have faith and must believe, that you can make it right,turn all bad dreams to fairytales, turn darkness into light.For each and everyone of us deserves to have the best, to live each day in happiness, and nights in peaceful rest.
