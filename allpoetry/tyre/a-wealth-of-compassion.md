---
title: "A Wealth of Compassion"
writer: "Tyre"
slug: "a-wealth-of-compassion"
source: "AllPoetry"

Open unto me the windows of heaven
Flooding my soul with abundant wealth
In nurturing my heart with contentment
That I may have to give unto my world

My world is filled with pleasant memories
Of loving folks who give of their talents
To enrich the lives of those they encounter
Unto the benefit of the Master's charm

Passion lives within the heart of a soul
Driven to perform at their peak desires
Expressing their worth to achieve success
Marked by the goal of their intended quest

The depth of my passions, lying deep inside
Which motivates my soul with hunger
Is to deliver unto my encircled realm
A peaceful co-existance for all to attain

Enter my world and abide in my realm
Where comfort envelopes your very soul
With peaceful surroundings for you to enjoy
A wealth of compassion waiting for your entreat
