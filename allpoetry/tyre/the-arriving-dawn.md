---
title: "The Arriving Dawn"
writer: "Tyre"
slug: "the-arriving-dawn"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/3780-The-Arriving-Dawn-by-Tyre"
---

Dawn arrives with your tender touch
Searching for passion to be renewed
As I awake unto your gleaming sunrise
And burst into the shimmer of morning dew
I reach out to draw you into my soul
That together we may delight upon the dawn

Oh heavenly sunrise
Abide forever more
Give life unto eternal treasures
Which delights these gentle hands
And dwell richly in the company of my love

My love
My sweet passion of life
Graze upon the tapestries of our noonday song
And sing your heart out
As we arrive deep into the day
Until, at last, the setting of the sun
Where we embrace each other
And cuddle unto our slumbering sleep
Waiting for the new dawn to arrive
