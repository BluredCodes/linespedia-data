---
title: "Beneath the Ancient Elm"
writer: "Tyre"
slug: "beneath-the-ancient-elm"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/5387-Beneath-the-Ancient-Elm-by-Tyre"

Enchanting passions drapes the veil of dawn
As whispering winds dance across soft meadows
In a distance the ancient Elms cast their shadows
To soothe the chatter of waking birds now gone

Smell the aroma captured in the morning dew
And listen to the brooks sing their lovely song
While you drift into dreams meandering along
In quiet streams of beauty deep inside of you

Feel the satin of dense grasses beneath your feet
Coaxing you into laying down and view the sky
Where fluffy clouds cross as mountains gone by
Until visions of castles mark your dreams complete

There you will find me with my arms opened wide
Delighting much in your want for a lover's caress
That we may deliver what our passions express
Unto each other in the whirlwind of hunger's tide

Enchanting passions, breathe life into our dreams
And let our worlds reunite us in our want to be free
In catering to our desires beneath the ancient Elm tree
With our hearts knitted together in passion's streams
