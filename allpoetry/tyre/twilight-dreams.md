Twilight dreams, a night of screamsI couldn't get to sleep last nightGrave images of tortured facesDeath still holds a veil of frightThe moon is full, over the poolRunning red with the blood of ghostsThunder roars upon agony's doorCharlie still craves my bitter hostsDays roll by, yet another tryI strive to bury the dead againWhat hope have I to overcomeOr at least be able to bargainOld gray streaks of AutumnWhispers carried off in flightTwilight dreams, a night of screamsI couldn't get to sleep last night
