Oceans of waves singing their endless songsIn soothing rhythms as begging you to comeAnd rest warmingly in my arms tonightThat I may delight in your want for my loveLet me bathe you gently with scented watersHeated for the pleasures wanting to take holdYet slowly building with eager anticipationWithin each step given to excite your passionsLet me massage your body in all its fullnessAs I straddle your naked butt resting before mePaying careful attention to incite your desiresWhile relaxing each part unto its total submissionThen with steady movement, I wrap you fullyKissing your face until you beg for my enteringOnly to build your madness with each denialWhile I descend ever so slowly unto your nestThere I shall send wave after peaking waveOf satisfying floods of your endless wantUntil I finally enter you eagerly from behindPounding explosively until our strength is spent
