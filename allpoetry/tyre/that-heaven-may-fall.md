---
title: "That Heaven May Fall"
writer: "Tyre"
slug: "that-heaven-may-fall"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/4543-That-Heaven-May-Fall-by-Tyre"
---

Take my hand and abide in peace
Let not worry fall upon your face
In my arms I will comfort your heart
Walking beside you at a steady pace

In times of trouble, I will be a shield
To buffer the arrows hurled against you
That you may have a chance to stand
Without fear of falling against the rue

You are a precious gem unto my soul
With luster sparkling against the night
More brilliant than mere eyes can see
As an endless source of purity's delight

I will love you dearly with all my might
As longing to touch your soft, tender lips
And caress the fullness of your appetite
Though lost in the midst of a total eclipse

I will gladly endure in this living twilight
In hopes of capturing even a fallen residue
Of your rapturous splendor if only just once
That heaven may fall from deep inside of you
