---
title: "A Dream to Fulfill"
writer: "Tyre"
slug: "a-dream-to-fulfill"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/12071-A-Dream-to-Fulfill-by-Tyre"
---

Explore the regions of a newly found world
Where life exist to surround you with its love
That you may wander without knowing fear
And capture the beauty released from your soul

Engage its laughter which strengthens your heart
As you appear in the midst of the apple groves
That leads you through rich gardens of dreams
Unto a cottage waiting to be called home

There you will find me with arms opened wide
Eager to embrace you unto tender romance
As a hand grasp your face to entice you with kisses
While the other pulls you close to secure you near

Embrace my dream and be filled with content
As your deep, enduring passion bubbles to explode
And I shall receive you as one deserving the best
From the Lord of the castle unto his Lady's nest
