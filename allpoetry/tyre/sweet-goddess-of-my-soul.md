---
title: "Sweet Goddess of My Soul"
writer: "Tyre"
slug: "sweet-goddess-of-my-soul"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Empty breezes expressed in the still air
Often follows a hollowed heart in despair
Grieving over the abandonment of emotions
Viciously trapped in its ever growing cycles

What hope has a man to regain his strength
When none are willing to comfort in his affair
With renewing compassion in a gentle wave
Of passionate acceptance for him to brave

Sweet goddess of my soul, I call upon you
To descend upon my cravings for you to clutch
In hungering desires to explode with your touch
And build a bond together for life to endure

Oh sweet, passionate goddess, your love I adore
In your passionate acceptance I am now reborn
With a newness of life from deep in your tenderness
Evolving into an endless longing for evermore
