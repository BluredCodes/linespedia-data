title: "Welcome to My World"

Welcome to my world sweet passionate soul
I have been waiting for you for so long
My life has been lonely for many years
Because it is in your heart that I belong

I have longed to be with you, yet unknown
To caress the beauty laying next to me
And dream of you waking up by my side
Eager to embrace the world cheerfully

Like a safe harbor we may build our home
To provide comfort within its cove
A nest to house our ever growing peace
With gardens leading to a special grove

There we may rest to view our home afar
And talk about what our hearts may decide
Or walk in silence just knowing we care
Very deeply with one another's pride

Let our world be filled with your tender love
Blending richly with all that I have to give
That together, forever, we may know
The true meaning of the love we live
