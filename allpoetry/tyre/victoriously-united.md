---
title: "Victoriously United"
writer: "Tyre"
slug: "victoriously-united"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/41025-Victoriously-United-by-Tyre"
---

Enter into my soul laid open for you
And abide in the passions of my heart
My hunger screams for your tender mercies
To sweep away the pains of solitude's might

Let me fulfill your need for compassion
With a gentle hand to embrace your desires
Which aches intensely with anticipation
For a lover's heated entanglement

I am a man who is strong in his passions
Desiring greatly to become your delight
As one who is wanting to be at home
Eager to establish our loving abode

Together we can build a mighty fortress
Able to conquor all of life's challenges
With the force of our two loving hearts
Victoriously united as a single soul

For it is your heart I longed to secure
With a beauty unmatched by any other
So hear my plea to become one with me
That victory may abide with us forever
