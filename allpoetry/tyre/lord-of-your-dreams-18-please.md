title: "Lord of Your Dreams            (18+  please)"

Awake now my precious love of life
And let your eyes be fixed upon me
As my heart aches for your passionate touch
To sweep over my body tenderly with kisses

Devour my soul and feed me with a feast
Of your enchanted beauty tasting sweet
That I may fill all my hunger in you
With your eternal pastures engulfing me whole

Let me graze upon your tapestries of gold
Until rapture swells in endless rhythms
Of mounting pleasures sinking deeply inside
With heavenly thrusts exploding in relief

Exhaustedly we collasp upon panted breath
Pulling you into my fold, secured for the night
Until dawn awakes us to begin again
In an endless dream fulfilled with delight
