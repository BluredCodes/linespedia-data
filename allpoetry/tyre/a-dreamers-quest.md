---
title: "A Dreamer's Quest"
writer: "Tyre"
slug: "a-dreamers-quest"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/27339-A-Dreamers-Quest-by-Tyre"
---

I have travelled so far throughout the years
Wondering if there is someone who would care
Enough for me to give of her uttermost self
As a fountain of love's passionate devotion

A woman who would give her all selflessly
Without thought of gain through vain glory
Or dominance through deceptive measures
But fully from her heart as unto her Lord

For unto such a woman shall I give my all
That she may never lack in love nor substance
But shall flourish in all things under heaven
With all the majesty bequeeved unto my Queen

Your life shall I cherrish with all my heart
Yearning to embrace you with tenderness
To feed and feast upon our togetherness
Devouring each other until all is spent

Oh precious woman, make yourself known unto me
That I may prepare a home for our hearts to dwell
And draw you deep into the hearth of my soul
Vanquishing for all time, solitude's living hell
