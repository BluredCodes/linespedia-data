---
title: "Kindred Soul"
writer: "Tyre"
slug: "kindred-soul"
source: "AllPoetry"

Breathe in the passions of a kindred soulAnd let your heart feel what it has instoreThen explore the depths laid before your eyesThat you may know the beauty deep insideHarbor the essence of undying faithNested around straws of knitted cravingsWhere you may graze hungrily at your leisureAbiding in the wiles of love's interlaceEmbrace my longings for your soothing careAnd let me ignite your passions with a touchThat I may bathe you with soft, flowing kissesEager to taste your sweetness burning brightYou are the essence of my intense desireThe enchanting delight of all my dreamsWhirling me about in your impassioned cloudsOf rich beauty in mountainous splendorMy kindred soul, abide forever moreEngulf me with your enchanting delightsWith dreams to endulge our need to fulfillThe ever growing desire to be at one
