---
title: "A World Without a Home"
writer: "Tyre"
slug: "a-world-without-a-home"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/4157-A-World-Without-a-Home-by-Tyre"
---

Passion swells deep into the heart of a man
Whose solitude punishes his every move
With dreams unfulfilled and fears from finding
That no one is willing to hold his head

Lost in the fields of rivers left crying
He host the hopes of their haunted dreams
His wisdom calms their fears running over
Only to find that they will wander away

Left alone he writes his enchanted dreams
Ever seeking yet never finding his world
Where his passion is granted by his love
United with two worlds becoming one

Oh passionate soul, your dreams are at rest
In solitude your life is doomed to become
Emptied by the lack of a willing soul
Whose love is aimed forever at another

Fear not for the emptiness in your life
As many have benefited from what you've taught
And continue to give without gaining in return
Alone in the remains of a world without a home
