---
title: "When We Unite"
writer: "Tyre"
slug: "when-we-unite"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/3849-When-We-Unite-by-Tyre"
---

Let me gaze upon your passionate beautyWith your eyes sparkling its enchanted glowAnd a warming smile that melts deep intoThe very heart of my most intimate soulSpeak unto me with your celestial voiceLet me hear the whispers of your soft danceWhich captures my attention with eager focusEngaged in my cravings of wanton desireEmbrace me fully with soft, flowing kissesKindling waves of intense, fiery pleasuresUntil lost in a unity without boundariesSatisfying each other with explosive intentWrapped in my arms, let me cradle you closeTucked securely away into the folds of my loveAnd rest contently within a deep, sound sleepDreaming of pleasant worlds where love is realAwake with me and stroke the embers anewLet each day begin with a passionate touchThat through the day we may be filled with delightWaiting with anticipation for when we unite
