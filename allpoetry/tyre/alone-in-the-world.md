---
title: "Alone in the World"
writer: "Tyre"
slug: "alone-in-the-world"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/9244-Alone-in-the-World-by-Tyre"

Alone in the world, my soul craves attention
From a woman who has the will to implore
With her heart endearing unto its desires
In engaging a union with deep passion

Let our souls unite with intense delight
Eager to deliver unto the other's need
While enhancing the pleasures in our quest
Hungering to be filled throughout the night

As I gaze at you, my appetite climbs high
For your beauty instills a want for caress
With soft kisses flowing across your face
While searching for treasures beneath the sky

Let our wanderings soar unto endless explore
Bringing life unto new worlds yet untouched
With imaginations reaching unto new heights
That passions may behold our want for more

Such are the dreams of my longing intent
Delivered on page in hopes for your response
To wrap me in your arms with all your might
And flood me with waves until life is spent
