---
title: "Only By You"
writer: "Tyre"
slug: "only-by-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/19079-Only-By-You-by-Tyre"
---

Quietly I awoke unto peaceful skiesWith dreams filtering through the clouds of dawnOutside my window I hear the sounds of morningDrifting through welcomely with greetings of calmSlowly I arise from the warmth of my bedEyes holding sleep heavily as to hinder the dayWanting me to stay in the beauty of momentsBegging me to deny such needs as to continueSuddenly the aroma of coffee fills my nostrilsAdding strength to engage movement from restKnowing that soon I may shower unto full awakeAnd capture new beginnings with vigor and mightAs I listen to sweet Celtic songs and sip my coffeeI dream of far away lands in the times of oldEnjoying the precious moments given in solitudeBefore engaging the world with their many problemsOh how precious are these quiet, peaceful morningsWhat a pleasant pace they set for each new dayTo capture such pleasures can only be surpassedWhen shared with such beauty as I see in you
