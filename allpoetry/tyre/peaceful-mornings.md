---
title: "Peaceful Mornings"
writer: "Tyre"

Quietly the night twilights into dawn
While waking birds chatter their cheerful songs
Dew begins to settle upon the earth
With crisp air exciting senses awake

The sun is rising over the mountains
And life begins their morning rituals
With a sense of calm delighting the soul
Unto blossoms of fresh, clean aromas

Down by a creek singing its melodies
A fawn, playing, kneels down for a drink
His mother stands near with a watchful eye
Guarding against dangers lurking about

In a distance stands a Tudor cottage
With smoke rising through its chimney above
A faint odor of breakfast now cooking
Brings hunger into the picture now clear

Awake and enliven, the day now begins
The pace is set with its proper greetings
Time to go forth with newness of life
And capture the fullness offered in peace
