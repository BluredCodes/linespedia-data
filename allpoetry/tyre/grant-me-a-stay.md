---
title: "Grant Me A Stay"
writer: "Tyre"
slug: "grant-me-a-stay"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/19194-Grant-Me-A-Stay-by-Tyre"
---

Grant me a stay in the midst of your dreamsAnd bid me welcome into your chambers tonightThat I may be privileged with your desiresAnd indulge in the wonders of your delightCall upon me as the Lord of your passionsJourneying through nights deep inside of youWith wanderings of anticipated pleasuresThat I may make all your dreams come trueNow let me abide in the depths of your soulWhere reality demands the life of your dreamsTo grow with purpose unto living together as oneUnited by the flooding of love's endless streamsFor you are the maiden of my soul's requestEncouraging my hopes for our souls to nest
