---
title: "Grant Me A Night"
writer: "Tyre"
slug: "grant-me-a-night"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/10327-Grant-Me-A-Night-by-Tyre"
---

Deeply alluring and hypnotic eyesEnchants unto tantalizing appetiteFor sensual pleasures that do appeaseStarvation's hunger for your delightWhat is your wish that I may performTo satisfy the longing you create in meYour effectual charm excites my soulWith cravings mounting for all to seeI beg you my dear, beautiful maidenGive me relief for my hunger in youAnd grant me a night beyond compareFor dreams assuredly do come true
