---
title: "Queen of My Eternal Heart"
writer: "Tyre"
slug: "queen-of-my-eternal-heart"
source: "AllPoetry"

Let peace reign deep inside your soul
Bringing forth dreams worthy of heaven
Delight with passion in caring for others 
That they too may be consumed in your love

Dream sweet dreams enabling you to cope
With rich songs sweeping across your heart
In waves of soothing comfort unto the dawn
That each new day begins refreshingly strong

When in times of trouble, cast your eyes fully
Upon my soul with hope embedded inside
Reaching out for the comfort given to you
Knowing that I eagerly give wholeheartedly

It is for you that my life is given completely
Ever evolving unto new heights supreme
Wanting with great desire to be a part of your life
To hold you deep within the bowels of my soul

For you are the Queen of my eternal heart
Enchantress of my soul's hungering enrapture
In you I have the fullness of heaven's delight
Worthy of all that I have in my heart to give
