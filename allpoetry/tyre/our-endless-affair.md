---
title: "Our Endless Affair"
writer: "Tyre"
slug: "our-endless-affair"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/5380-Our-Endless-Affair-by-Tyre"
---

Abide in my heart dear, passionate soulLet me bathe in your want for adventureWhere dreams fulfilled are just a step awayPlunging deep into heaven's lost treasureHungry for a taste of your sweet nectarI longingly await for your entreatTo engage in a course for love's domainAnd establish our goals to be completeYour beauty I crave to caress and holdWith kisses flowing across heaven's doorKindled by your desire to hunt and grazeEnriched with a passion for eager exploreEndear yourself unto my wanton questAnd let me be fulfilled within your careThat we may abound in our want for moreDaring to dream in our endless affair
