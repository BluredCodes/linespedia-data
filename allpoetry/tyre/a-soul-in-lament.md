---
title: "A Soul in Lament"
writer: "Tyre"
slug: "a-soul-in-lament"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/16178-A-Soul-in-Lament-by-Tyre"
---

Loneliness strikes an abandoned heart
Whose passions grow cold against the night
In watching the youth as they travel by
Onto sweeter dreams to fulfill their delight

Drained is a soul who detaches the world
Whose arms stretch out unto a grain of hope
For the touch of a gentle hand in response
Only to find that no one is there to cope

Shattered dreams now lay wasted by the shore
Broken by waves of relentless oceans
Embittered by a desire to remain alive
Yet unforgiven am I of its notions

I remain alone unto my last breath
With no tears to comfort me in my death
