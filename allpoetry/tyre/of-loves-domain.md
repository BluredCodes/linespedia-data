---
title: "Of Love's Domain"
writer: "Tyre"
slug: "of-loves-domain"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/26046-Of-Loves-Domain-by-Tyre"

Let me hear your voice sounding in my heart
And feel your breath dancing upon my face
Your soothing touch ignites my soul's desire
In want for your tenderness to embrace

Let me bathe you in lavender and gold
With soft petals of rose floating abreast
To carry smooth, white pearls before your eyes
That you may behold the rich beauty in you

Let me gaze upon your silky delights
As you move passionately before me
Enticing sweet pleasures longing to start
Enchanted by graceful sways of your arms

Open unto me as needing my impart
To satisfy your cravings for my love
Wheeling intensely deep in your crevices
Unto shattering explosions of ecstacy

You are the passion of delight in me
My soul and reason for giving my all
To secure your everlasting devotions
Upon the greater depths of love's domain
