---
title: "Escaping the Minefield"
writer: "Tyre"
slug: "escaping-the-minefield"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/6369-Escaping-the-Minefield-by-Tyre"
---

Beneath the blanket of stars shining bright
Lies the breath of my soul's longing delight
To escape into the world's enchanting dreams
Freed from destruction and life's midnight screams

What fear induces others to stay clear
With bayonets blazing whenever they're near
Or even my own engaging distress
As frozen in a minefield without rest

The answers must lie somewhere deep inside
Where rivers join against the rising tide
Which washes away both hope and despair
Bathing together as though each would dare

The rising tide has no conscience to hold
And can only do what it has been told
So I must determine what I must do
To defuse the minefields before morning's dew

Time gives no warning to the steps we make
So approach with care for your life's loving sake
That you may escape from the minefield's strife
And enter into the dawn of your new life
