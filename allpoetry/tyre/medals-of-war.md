---
title: "Medals of War"
writer: "Tyre"
slug: "medals-of-war"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/3928-Medals-of-War-by-Tyre"
---

The pain too great to remember,
The pain too great to forget.
The pain too great to sleep,
The pain too great to stay awake.
The pain too great to hold,
The pain too great to let go.
The pain too great to endure,
The pain too great to give in.
The pain too great to live,
Dear God, I can't even die!
