---
title: "Enchanted Dreams"
writer: "Tyre"

Enchantment surrounds your passionate beauty
As my eyes indulge upon its magnificent array
Dancing like a sultry goddress in a waxing moon
With passions focussed to delight my very soul

Whispers fill my wanton ears with glee
Enticing sweet allures of your intent
To encompass my heart with tender joys
Quietly seducing my being unto your desires

Zestfully tease my heart in want for more
Eagerly returning with great hopes to nest
Within your intriguing capture of my delight
Vieing for your affections to rest deep inside

You are the vision of my enchanted dreams
Living in the depths of my soul's hungering
To devour your insatiable appetite longingly
As I digest your nector screaming with pleasure

Enchanted dreams, may I never be awakened
But ever abide within the realm of your keep
That the joys of our encounter may last forever
Richly united in the harmony of two hearts in one
