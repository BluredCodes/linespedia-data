---
title: "The Rising Dawn"
writer: "Tyre"
slug: "the-rising-dawn"
source: "AllPoetry"

A cry of hope sparkles within the rising dawn
Whose beauty enchants even the deepest heart
With passions flowing freely from a love divine
As hope may be fulfilled from a willing impart

Let your passions arise within the morning sun
Your comfort shines as within the bright of day
Reach out unto the hearts with such tenderness
As compassion has depth within a lover's array

Set course now unto the rising of a new dawn
That hope may arrive upon a passionate shore
For hope fulfilled with loving care in your hearts
May add strength unto our soul's loving adore

Into a new day we shall boldly walk the earth
Ready to reach out with our most delightful cheers
With such passion never before witnessed by those
Who could only show their lusts behind hidden fears

Our lives shall be a lighthouse in their stormy seas
Guiding troubled ships into the safe harbor ashore
To aid in their comforts throughout the bitter nights
Unto a new day rising, rich with eager explore

Now the time has come to abide within the new day
As our hopes and dreams are awakened and drawn
To promote the awakening of old worlds lost in sleep
By the showing of a new world, within the rising dawn
