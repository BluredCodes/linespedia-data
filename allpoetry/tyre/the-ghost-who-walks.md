---
title: "The Ghost Who Walks"
writer: "Tyre"
slug: "the-ghost-who-walks"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/16181-The-Ghost-Who-Walks-by-Tyre"

When help is needed, I will lend a hand
In your times of trouble, I will be a friend
I am the Ghost Who Walks without a sound
Never intruding, yet will always be around

Hidden from triumph as no longer in need
Life will move forward unto the next plead
Then as trouble arises, causing grieving pain
I will enter your world to help relieve again

Let not trouble dwell from deep inside of you
Don't hide your grief and I will help you through
Life is too precious to abide in sorrowing despair
So call upon me as one who truly cares

When your grief returns to trouble you no more
And victory abounds upon your triumphant shore
I will fade away again without a memory to cast
As the Ghost Who Walks upon an endless past
