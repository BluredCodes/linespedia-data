title: "Rain Dragon"
writer: "Tyre"

Rain dragon weave your mist and cry your tears
Let heaven open its bounty free
I laid apples under a willow tree
That you may feast and bring to me your cheers

A brave knight has lost his passion for fight
Surrendering instead to live a ghost
As one whose memories harbor a host
Never seeing the dawn rise from the night

Wilting sorrows bring heavily laid sleep
Only to exhaust in wandering alone
Seeking peaceful solace for hope to keep
Before swallowed in war's torturous zone

Rain dragon weave your mist for me inside
That dark memories may have a place to hide
