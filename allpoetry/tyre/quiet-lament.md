---
title: "Quiet Lament"
writer: "Tyre"
slug: "quiet-lament"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I am an empty soul with only dreams to bare
All the passions that lay dormant deep inside
With so much sorrow in an heart longing to quest
And become accepted by such beauty found in you

I passionately dream the sweet dreams of loving
Such grace laced in a pattern worthy of a goddess
Whose intimate heart is match only by a soul
Laid open to the hunger which fills my loneliness

Visions of your smile softens my troubled life
Wrestling to become worthy of your compassion
With hopes that someday I may finally become
One who will become your Knight in shining armor

Alas, my dreams are only but an empty affair
Lost in a world unseen by your beautiful eyes
Quenched by the sorrows too intense to abide
And buried to cover all the shame left behind

Quietly I move forward unto my last breath
Never knowing what my heart has been denied
By the grief of a past too horrible to describe
Nor willing to punish you with its hideous glow
