They act like rain drops
They're frozen like hail.
They think beautiful like models
But they're skinny like death.
They like long blonde hair
it looks like scraggles of straw.
Their shoes 6 inches high
Their legs 3 metres long
Their hearts are burried long from the outside
Their skin and bones wrap them up like blankets
They do not go to the gym
They have their own
It doesn't involve excersise, 
consuming food is a hazard to it,
and you need very long fingers.
Their ice only turns
to fire
Their brains were never there
'I don't like this fat here or this fat there'
well bitch you're lucky; some of us don't have the option of choosing which fat we like and don't like it's just all around and we've learned to live with it. Be fucking thankful you even have a life after putting yourself through that. You don't know how many other stupid girls would kill for your body just to be another of the worlds' wonderful sex toys.
