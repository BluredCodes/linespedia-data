---
title: "lovely woman"
writer: "silent scream"
slug: "lovely-woman"
source: "AllPoetry"

Please don't break me
I know you really do love me

Respectful
Honest
Loyal
I am 

You are me 
and I am you

Everyday is a new day 
and every old one a memory
we will both grow old 
carry memories 

but together we will always stand

Forever and never ever forgotten
you're in my heart
you're a part of me
we will never be lost from each other.
