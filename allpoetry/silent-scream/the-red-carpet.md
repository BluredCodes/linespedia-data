title: "the red carpet"
writer: "silent scream"
slug: "the-red-carpet"

just let me cry just let me free don't hold me any longer ghost of cage entrapment please let me be alone I have nobody left just me myself and I can I plead on my knees? can I watch you take me away to that blue little room can I hold myself in from you and the fear I can't have those white walls are caging me the blood stained carpet on the stairs is toturing me at this very moment I want to kill you bleed you bastard I control myself or do I? so walk me down the royal blood stairs through the hall with no walls the room of white crumbling mass it's tearing me but I won't show it not to you nonono I can't nonono it is possible for you to see a living being smile?
