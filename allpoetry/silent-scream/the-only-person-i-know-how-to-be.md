The other girls hold their heads high
with their deceiving eyes i hate.

behind the scenes 
fingers in their throats
no food on the table
and no life to live.

I'm not like them
how is it possible?
it's the mannicans
their bones stick out

the models on T.V.
the new Barbie doll singers

vacuum the waste
fill it up with air

I purge in food i love
I don't fucking care

I can't help it
please accept the way I am

because the only person i know how to be is me.
