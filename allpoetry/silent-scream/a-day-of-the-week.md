why
do you try this hard?
and how can you feel good about 
what you've done?
it wasn't supposed to be like this
it wouldn't have started
it wouldn't have ended
but we're here now
 no knowing
  no stopping
   full blowing
To break the cycle
put into play
it happened 
nobody stopped it
and look where it landed
a dead end street
where nobody lives or dies 
   F
  A
    L
      L
       I
     N
        G
into silence
which will break
with voice of no light 
between loss of sight
future reference not needed
for being has not been read
tell us you'll fall into it
tell us you'll fall
and will not look back
into the last peice of oblivion.
