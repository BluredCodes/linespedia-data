---
title: "Cold Blood"
writer: "silent scream"
slug: "cold-blood"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/29896-Cold-Blood-by-silent-scream"

Blabber on
her mouth moves
speaking nothing
thinking meaning

towards her
cold blood
blue blood 
freeze

long shot
understanding
ramble, ramble on
stop the world

thinks she's good
hating everybody
loves herself
too much

        Bitch.
