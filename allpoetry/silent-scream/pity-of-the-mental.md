I will not wallow
 in
  self-pity
Pitiful to myself
 but why
I don't 
  Understand
I'm living
breathing
loving 
  and loved
Depressed I will find
  myself
for not a reason
 Trasaction
from
    fun to sad
It's not amusing
I'm wallowing
in self-pity
pity of emotions?
pity of alternatives?
I'm pitying myself 
not for a reason
To enjoy this
  i n sa n e
To want this
      Me N TA l
To break down 
        and cry
the only solution
I have yet
 come up with

So I'll sit 
and I'll cry 
I'll saturate 
in self-pity
for no reason.
