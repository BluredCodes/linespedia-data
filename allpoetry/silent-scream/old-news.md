title: "Old New(s)"
writer: "silent scream"

The old me
is empty
drowning in water

The new me 
is happy
happy like no other

Brand new life approaching
slowly and
not yet knowing

I hope it never ends
like a river
which always bends

I know it's not all open
there are boudary lines
but I'm hoping that will change
because I'm willing to cross these mines.
