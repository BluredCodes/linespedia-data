Music is:
Imagination
A fantasy light
A joy for new hope
A world beyond the world
A new person
and place
It is my life
I live for the senseless
peace of music
when I open my eyes
I will
sing
close them 
I will
humm
You will never feel 
anything better
then what music  
can make you feel
Lose control
of body's first motion
and hold back your head
in delight
sit down 
to tears 
of distress
and press play
full blast
serenity from
the horrors of the Earth
Pick what you want to hear
not what they tell you to
It feels like the tingling 
of rain on my body
The warmth of the 
hot roasting fire
The current of 
the cold river
The feeling of floating
on clouds
in the sky
Listen to music
the sound of it all
it will help you 
through the day
But wait I've thought long and hard
I believe it will
help you
 through all of your life.
