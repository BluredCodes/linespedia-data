---
title: "Hero"
writer: "silent scream"
slug: "hero"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/31498-Hero-by-silent-scream"
---

those words you use
where do you get them?
why do they form there?
and why do i love them?

searching, searching
found
the person that i want
and can't have

you're pasted on my wall
so i'm with you all night
pretending you are real
is what gives me the creeps

warm like a blanket
soft like a pillow
I think you are my hero
solely and alone

I miss you
but how is that possible?
I don't know you
I want you
and that's very wrongly possible

I'm hoping that one day
I'll find someone like you

he may not be as intellectual
he may not be as lustful
but god dammit
he'll be my hero.
