title: "there's a hole"

there's a hole
in the ground
over there
see it?

it's watching you
look out
it's holding you
better run

don't get stuck
they're coming
coming to get you
to haunt you

hide
while you have time
leave
before it's too late
scream 
and let someone know
          that you're gone
