I am labelled 
a punk
i sit with them
those kids...with spikey hair peircings

I'm not cool
I'm not friendly
I don't have a life
and I don't have feelings

I listen to shitty music
I don't wear good clothes
I'm stupid
because I'm not like everybody else
and I hate every fucking thing on the planet

Do you want to be my friend?
