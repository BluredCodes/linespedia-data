---
title: "Party"
writer: "silent scream"
slug: "party"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/32477-Party-by-silent-scream"
---

"Under the influence"
I've never known it more
then party.

"Boy meets girl"
I've never seen it more
then party.

"Puke 'til your eyes pop out"
I've never been disgusted more
then party.

"Take it 'til the sun comes up"
I've never known it more 
then party.

"Don't let them come"
I've never heard it more
then party.

"Hold me 'til I fall asleep"
I've never wanted it more
then party.
