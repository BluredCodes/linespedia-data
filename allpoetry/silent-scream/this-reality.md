A way to look inside a girl
and see a real reflection

a real guy who comforts
who enjoys the embrace
of you in his arms
and the look upon your face

someone who understands you
completes how you feel
even thought when you read this
you know it'll never be real

pressure for sex
will always be alive
but I don't give a shit
this is something i can survive

this guy will never be found
someone who undrstands me

because i have not been found
and i don't understand me.
