Scars on my body
Danny left one over there

        Luke left it over here
  Bob left it right about there

              Around on my back is Beau
on my side is Jeremy
    
       my right ankle, look it's Justin

   My forehead....it's Tyler
           Put on my sweater only to see Jaime's on my hands

      Cover my feet only to find Matt
    And if I look inside
inside my mouth
  d
   o
    w
     n
my throat 
to my red beating heart
    only to look up and find
  the next heart breaker
I now find it very hard to be with someone and think we'll last for a long time. Lately I've been hoping for more than the best and believing it will work.
