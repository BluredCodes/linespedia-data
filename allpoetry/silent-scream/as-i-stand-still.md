title: "As I stand still"

My blood boils 
to the point of agony 
so
I burn holes
to rip the demons 
    a  w  a  y
My blood turns 
to fluids
so I slice a line 
to let the sparkles
fly 
 a  w  a  y
My blood freezes
80 below
so I stand in the cold
no movement 
just frozen thoughts
of frozen life
in a frozen world
and all I can do 
is stand there
and watch
as my life slowly
