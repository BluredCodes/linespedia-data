title: "I might pee my pants"
writer: "silent scream"

This funny expression
I always use
actually brings cheer to my life

not often do i say something so simple
and find myself laughing so hard

I use it all the time
and people may get sick of it
but what they don't know is 
that it helps me cope with everyday life

I just might pee my pants

oh, the laughter carried out
I know it's immature
I know you may not approve
but it brings with it to me a light of hope

It shows me that I'm able to laugh at even the simplest ties.
