writer: "silent scream"

Filled with regret
look I've started
Another heart beating
another cold and blue
never meant for it to be this painful
I thought these hearts 
were both beating 
in sync
Red and glowing 
beating with butterflies
A letter
so scared
runrunrun
can't believe you cab possibly make yourself
feel so horrible
I hate myself
I'm so cruel
to be part of human nature
I'm not natures' human
I'm a being 
with too many emotions
I'm so sorry.
