It's so funny to them
yeah, I'm a whore
what's considered  whore?
hmm?
'cause I don't believe I am
I can't help the feelings I have
real depression 
    real pain
         real angst
and yet the fun doesn't stop
not for me
There's no such thing
Unless it's unreal
and I'm not real
according to the world
FAKE!!
      DESTROY!!
feel my fucking pain
I'm not some sleasy whore!
OKAY!!This may remind some people of jokes from last night and I'm not trying to yell at them. I just have not been coping well with anything lately and very very little things have been really getting to me and that's what this is all about. I don't want them to think I'm mad at them I was just hurt becasue I didn't know what was real.
