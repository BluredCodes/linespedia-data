title: "Misty Bubble"

Please pop
that misty bubble
holding me in
breaking me up

I swirve away
I try to go through

directing from there
I open my face lights
spot nothing
but the misty bubble

not being my hair
nor the raindrops falling down

the knife in my side
slowly pulling up
shreiking I'm not holding
inside is where I'm moulding

taken me in
never release and pull me out
love me too much
hate me over the limit

pressure is too hard
grasp my own last breath
and I will break away.
