I don't see
smiles
I don't see
laughter
I see miles and oceans
of pain
cold
hearts
soul
breathe in 
the stale city air
breathe out 
what could be
your last
b re a th
convulse
to them, no needles
no money
bastard you say?
do not blame yourself
do blame yourself
what's the real difference
when your outside 
in the cold
alone.
