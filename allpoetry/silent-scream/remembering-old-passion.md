I want your hands in my hair

let them slowly sweep my neck

slide your lips down my chest

hold me close with every breath

sweet cheeks of innocense

can change easily with passion

the soft silky blankets that cover you

the feeling of being in lust not love

is very undescribable

to have someone to hold

to have someone to kiss

to have to share all night with
