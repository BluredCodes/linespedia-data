Just drive me
into the next level
of the game

no lights
no corners
no back alleys
just straight aways

Just drive me
into thin air
where I belong
in the stars

no people
no worries
no pain
just smiles and warmth

Just drive me
somewhere peaceful
away from home
and away from here

Just drive me 
to a fantasy world
far, far away.

Red lustful beauty
Orange hot fire
Yellow sparkles 
twinkling in the blackness
Green ribbons of flowers
falling from the abyss
Blue drops of petalled rain
soft and light so smooth
And purple fingernails
I'm feeling amazing tonight.
