title: "Pictures in my mind"
writer: "silent scream"

A video maze
see the man in the corner
I hope you do
meet your life's guide

He's there all the time
whether known or not
and he walks me around
in halls of pure rot

This place he says he knows
but is only half true
is a place I know
is very much like a zoo

He controls your view
of life in his range
and I hope you've told him
he's really quite strange

Oh the things he'll make you do
can be both good and bad
doesn't matter how hard I try
he makes me so mad

All about the things 
he says should be
but he doesn't know the truth
in reality

So if you ever see this man
sometimes he's a little blue
tell him I say hi
he'll probably say fuck you.
