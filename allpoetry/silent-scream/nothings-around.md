title: "Nothing's Around"

By our lonesome
nobody's here
it's the noises
that we fear

Cover ourselves
in eachother
reaching out
for nothing but feelings....

All I want
is to be with you
on our lonesome
alone and true

There is much warmth
between you and I
the t.v. is on
and we are sleeping


Soundless
silent
sweetly
softly


I can't stand 
not having this feeling
of you
and me

silently 
sleeping.
