writer: "silent scream"

I was born
on Monday
Saw the world
on Tuesday
Made it home 
on Wednesday
Cried all day
on Thursday
Said hello
to Friday
Got a boyfriend
on Saturday
Slept all day 
on Sunday
repeatrepeatrepeat
2 weeks in my life
like a train
running off 
its tracks
I can't see
Tuesday from Monday
Or Sunday from Saturday
But I know it'll always be 
the same.
3 weeks
4 weeks
5 weeks
nothing will ever change
but it's the basics
the inside details
will always look different
on the drawing
I said
and it's true 
I can't stop it
and neither can you
it just happens
and we wait for it everyday
who will live?
who will die?
and who will never know?
it hurts to think
you might not see another day
speak another word
or cry another tear
but if that doesn't happen to you
life goes on sucker
we all have to pull through.
