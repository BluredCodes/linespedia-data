---
title: "Soap"
writer: "silent scream"
slug: "soap"
source: "AllPoetry"

Try so hard
phone calls at night
just to be sure
everything is all right

Slippery
the way they're so easy to lose

Slimy
makes you feel like grunge all the time

Refreshing
comfort is always needed

Squishable
it will always end up this way

Breakable
hold it close for as long as you can

Every good relationship is like soap.
