---
title: "For we've been lost"
writer: "silent scream"
slug: "for-weve-been-lost"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/29904-For-weve-been-lost-by-silent-scream"
---

Respect for you
making me stronger

you're clearly beyond intelligent
I know not much
for we've been lost
from each other just a while

but now we're here
and I know you once again

accepting I am of you
of course I am
unlike some
which can be more than frustrating

I hope you know just that
for anything you ever need
I'm here if you need a listen
I'm here for you if you ever need help.
