title: "Abuse"

Close your eyes
sweet little baby

Please don't cry 
sweet little baby

I hope you die 
sweet little baby

Because she won't cry
she'll hold you tight
and after they find you
there will be another fight

But at least you won't be there
to be thrown against the wall
to be shoved around and kicked around
just like a ball

abuseabuseabuse

She had no strength to stop him

So don't blame her
she did put up a fight
and don't worry little baby
you'll see her in heaven tonight.
