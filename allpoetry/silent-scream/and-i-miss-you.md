---
title: "And I miss you"
writer: "silent scream"
slug: "and-i-miss-you"

About a year ago
a little more than friends
we had so much fun
and I miss you

Lying in your back yard
those hot Summer days
I remember your skin touching mine
soft and warm as I recall

The warmth
holding you close
a dream
never taken away
and I miss you

Advice from you now
like a very good old friend would
and I miss you.
