title: "Mr.Smiley the shoe"
writer: "silent scream"

Said Mr.Smiley to his girl
you run
I step on you 
and smile again I will

Mr.Smiley let it go
says she
for I'm not able
to stop screaming

Your stepping on my toes
then on my knees
when your at my head 
please let me be

Sadly being mistaken
Mr.Smiley does not want you
he just wants your feet 
to walk

Walk over there to your good friend
let him take over from there
Mr.smiley and his pale white face 
do not give a care

His blue veined
Blood shot
Horrible self
Is nothing to the eye

He needs you there 
crushed your feet
coiling his fear
'til sails eye hers will meet

When Mr.Smileys' done
you can go home
try and escape and he'll find you
he finds himself good at that

When Mr.Smiley looked at you
that day upon the peir
I know what you saw
and I know how you feel

Though I can't explain it
he's never very clear
but his face with that look
will never let it go

The glimmer in his squinty eyes
the razor edge of his yellow teeth
the feel of hands
on your waist and breathing on your neck

Never let it happen
lock yourself up tight
Mr.Smiley stays around
this shoe watches you all night.
