---
title: "Find the meaning"
writer: "silent scream"
slug: "find-the-meaning"
source: "AllPoetry"

I'm not good enough
not strong enough
happy enough
or pretty enough
to suit you
how cruel can people be?
cruel enough
to cause
suicide?
I can only be 
so many things
so maybe strong 
isn't one
nor happy
nor pretty
But, I am me
and if I'm not 
good enough for you
I'm good enough for him 
and I'd rather
have it
that way.
