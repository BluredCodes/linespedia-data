---
title: "And Sometimes"
writer: "silent scream"
slug: "and-sometimes"
source: "AllPoetry"

Sometimes
you make me feel
 like
   an open door
 with
  cold air 
 rushing through

Sometimes
you make me feel
 like 
   a wave
 of red smoke
  twisting
 all 
  around
your skin

and sometimes
you make me feel
 like 
  I've known you
 my whole life
   and you can
 make everything
    All right.
