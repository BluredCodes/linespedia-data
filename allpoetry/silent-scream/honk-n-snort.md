Neither with a waist 
just fingers in their stomachs

The whining of the voice boxes
and smiles upon their faces
no room for a heart
inside they're cold and black

Dove feathered ravens
wanted sexually
why?
to put up with their attention craving lives!?

In a past life I would envy thee
now the word makes me sick
I envy no one but myself 
for having a real life

yet, envy
I hate the word 
as I hate them

You judge
you honk 
you snort
you scream

Oh look there you are
dye your hair for everyone else

Shave your genitalia
to wear your jeans low cut

I hope somebody rapes you 
allthough thats horrible to say 
oh well it's only you two 
it'll give you another something to hide away

your lives are so fucked up
spitting in your sister's cake
looking like you do just to please them

Only to please T-H-E-M!

Dickteaser! Dickteaser!
hold me back please
square like a cardboard box
to hard to please

So wear your jeans low cut
wear your shirt up high
your tits stick out the top
your genital out the bottom

I hope you think you look good
as i would surely abort
but nothing would give more pleasure 
than to kill honk n' snort.
