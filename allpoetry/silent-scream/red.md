Red
for me to hate 
and feel rage
funny how it can also mean
love and compassion
This is my favourite colour
(next to black without feeling)
if I could paint the house red 
I would
if I could hold you in my arms red
I would
if I could love, hope, hate and move on
with red
I would
Make me a spirit
to travel long away
red wings
red hair
red clothes
brown eyes
hmm brown eyes?
well red eyes mean evil
hatred towards everything
you've learned from the world
that there's one side to everything
Try to learn from yourself 
and real people
not learn from not existance of others.
