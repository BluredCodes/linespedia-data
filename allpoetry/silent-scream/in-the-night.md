1am
thinking
about how i wish my life could be
my future unknown
my past unforgotten

2am
drinking
coffee's keeping me alive
staring at the stars
they look mysteriously divine

3am
wanting
a 'guy' to kiss my lips
holding me close
and feeling my hips

4am
s  lo w l  y  slipping...

i
 n
  t
   o   

madness.

5am
uncomfort
rendering me silent
tossing, turning, flipping
until i so desire

6am
piss...yes i need to pee
just like any normal person
or not
whatever you can conjure up

7am
soooooo d r  ow s y
slight loss of control
the comfort of my pillow
conforming into a hole

8am
sleep
deeper
deeper
dream all that i can manage
into the unseen

2pm
awake
wrestle with the light
of knowing that today
is closing in on tomorrow night.
