---
title: "i don't wanna...."
writer: "silent scream"
slug: "i-dont-wanna"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/29611-i-dont-wanna....-by-silent-scream"
---

somebody likes me
for once in my life
i chase and i chase
but now i'm running

it all seems together
now it's all falling apart
it's just another guy
another fuck to break my heart

it feels right
my insides are beginning to turn
the fire's burning
somethings happening

i'm getting that feeling
that tingly one i hate

the boys all make the girls cry
it's long to tell
they like it though
you can see it in their eyes

so to do it or not to do it
they all say take it slow

i'm afraid 
i don't wanna....like someone
