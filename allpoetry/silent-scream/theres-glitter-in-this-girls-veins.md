there is a gift 
behind the eyes
behind the skin 
behind the heart

never will i let it go
i hold you close
to close for you to know

a blue shimmering rose

raindrops        
             sunshine

     mist
    
you are strong
and wonderful
so caring 
and loving

i love with all that i own
i cherish you forever
please be honest
with me all the time

     and you 'll always have a place in what's left of my heart.
