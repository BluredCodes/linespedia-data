---
title: "Machines"
writer: "silent scream"
slug: "machines"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

When you feel the worst
the best things happen
And when you feel the best 
the worst things happen

All of this contributes
to life
as a whole

The love between people
the pain of a break
between people
the thought of someone
makes the energy spin

Machines.
With feelings
emotions
senses
and integrity
Used for work
play
and selflessness
Or at least that's what we think
But, not everybody works
plays 
and rarely will you find 
a selfless person.
The world is supposed to be 
beautiful
funny how I'm leaving 
to another country
to feel at ease
People in that country 
probably want this country
Same with the next
and the same after that
It continues down the line
We are not sane machines
not any one of us
We all have our breakdowns
we all have our ups.
good days 
bad days
It plays itself out
and when it doesn't 
we can't figure out what to do
Some will die
suicidal.
Some will leave us
mental.
Some will just carry on
half sane.
And isn't that what everyone hopes for?
Life is made out 
so simple 
and easy
But it's only the fake 
that need to explain this
These are the really 
disfunctional machines.
They make a fake world
so the real one
isn't so bad
in their eyes.
As soon as reality kicks in
It's over.
Every machine 
deserves an instruction manual
but not every machine 
comes with one.
If you are one of those
who needs to make up 
your own instruction manual
mkae sure you look it over 
and perfect it
Because some of the smallest details
can turn your life for the worst
Although some can turn it for the better
Just know that
you are a machine
your library is what's making you 
understand this
Don't take advantage of what you don't own.
