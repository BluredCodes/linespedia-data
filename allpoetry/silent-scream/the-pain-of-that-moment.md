title: "The pain of that moment"
writer: "silent scream"

How can you be so thoughtless?why do you say and do those things?especially to those you care about I said faggot I'm so stupid how could I be so inconsiderate?!I don't know how she feels about it I didn't know what to say
I didn't know what to do.to sit and stare at the wall forever would be pointless.everyone can be sensitive; maybe that's the most sensitive part of her.how could I do that?fucking society.making homophobic statements.they're so common.and never taken care of.it's so sad that so many machines didn't come with feelings.like me.what the fuck is my problem?
not only do I feel like shit,I'm obviously causing other peopleto feel so bad. I don't mean it.I'm so sorry.sometimes it just happens,but it's no excuse I'm sorry.
