title: "The only time I'm happy"
writer: "silent scream"

Being there for friends
friends back there for me

Having little groupie parties
talking in between

Dust is pillowed on my phone
the only good thing about it is 
I know I'm not alone

Not that I have many of them
But most feel the same
we're sick and tired of the world
and playing this stupid old game

That's why every night
I sit and stare at my wall
waiting for a better day
when I'll actually get a call

If I could see my friends every night
life would be just grand
because we talk about eveyrthing humorously 
and it's not like taking a stand....

I don't really know about this
my writing is not so clear
all I know is what I hope and that is
for my friends to stay near.
