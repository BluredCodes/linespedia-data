---
title: "Ringless me"
writer: "silent scream"

It's waiting for you
go on inside
don't worry about me 
I'm still trying to hide

For my answers aren't as clear as yours
so go on be yourself
what are you trying to prove
I'm sorry it's not allowing me to move

For I am a burden
I'll take to my wings and fly
on my fairytale horses back
and with him I shall cry

See that hole
beyond them far
that's where I belong
not here at the bar

With destiny at one end
and force at the other
I choose neither
for they aren't either my mother

And destiny's eyes move slowly and subtle
Where force and his eyes know nothing but trouble

So here I am 
I'm ringless again
please don't look back
I'll be fine, me and the holes' end.
