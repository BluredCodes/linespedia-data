---
title: "a million times i thank you"
writer: "silent scream"
slug: "a-million-times-i-thank-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

a million times i thank you
for being at my side

all of you
for telling me 
i am not alone

for telling me you care
not to be afraid
to be myself
and let me love

it's a very slow process
but i'm trying very hard
to accept the way
bring it into view for others

yet i don't know how to do this
i find myself lost again
this is a very long road 
i'm not near close to approaching the end

i don't know why i am here
i don't know where i belong
i don't know what i believe in
and i don't know how to trust myself

so this really makes me wonder
who am i??
