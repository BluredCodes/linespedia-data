title: "did they really say it?!"

did they actually say that?
these boys who think they're better
did they really tell me
that death is more than just sexy
they want it
"she's so hot"
as her melting breath of poison
walks by
hips jutting out
belly baring all
and once more
the boys heads all follow 
in her direction
mmm her hair
let it drip
d r i p
d  r  i  p
those long string bean legs
the pinky fingers wrapped up 
inside tissue paper
'hotty mchotterson'
she's the same as everyone else
and they claim they are too
but they're not
they judge the same
they act the same
they talk the same
surprise!!!
they are the same
so fuck them.
