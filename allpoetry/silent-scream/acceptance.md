Why can't it be accepted
it's not the same any longer

no more posters
no more rebellion
and no more wreaking chaos

I don't mind you
staying the same that you always were
but we're growing up in a different age
and reality is setting in

Now we're getting looks
from tables across the room
and I'm not positive how you feel
but just why can't you accept it?

I thought we would be allowed to have new friends
it's not like they're even new
I've known them forever
and from you I'm getting quite confused

But if this is how you want it
then to please you that's it can be
but for now
let me figure out my life

and we'll discuss this in between.
