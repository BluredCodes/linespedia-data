An assertion was softly spoken to me
that I have overcome my past

I disagree (peacefully) and I declare 
I avow in contrast

To overcome means to beat
It means to conquer, overwhelm
It means to crush and overthrow
It means to defeat

I say instead let it be known
that instead I accept

I embrace and believe
I understand and receive

I welcome and I take it home
I take it into my soul

I make the past a part of me
I don't have to crush it or defeat
I don't have to win or beat

I choose instead to embrace 
I choose to understand

Knowing its contribution
helped me become who I am

The person I have become today
A person I would like to stay

I know that I will be okay

I know that I will liveThis came to me as I read comments from other poems I have written.  My hopeful thought for the day....
