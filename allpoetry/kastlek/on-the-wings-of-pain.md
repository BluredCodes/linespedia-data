On the wings of pain
A child is born
A girl
A daughter 
A ray of sun
A joy
A problem
A weapon
A toy
Criticized
Hit
Blamed
Never herself
She grows
She tries
She is:
Mother
Daughter
Wife
Friend
Never herself
Always trying
Always striving
Perfection
Never achieved
Scared
Hurt
Sad 
Angry
She is Becoming
A Person
Growing
Learning
About life
Herself
She never knew
She has needs
She helps
She loves
Always giving
Never taking
Trying
Disappearing
Appearing
The past haunting
The present hurting
She is learning 
To Become
To break free of:
Roles
Pain 
Fear
Grief
She is grieving
Hopeful
Joyful
Sad 
Learning
About herself
She never knew
She exists
She matters
She has a voice
She proclaims to the World
To herself
I AM
I will be
I count.
