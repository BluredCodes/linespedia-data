---
title: "I Am You"
writer: "KastleK"
slug: "i-am-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

I am all things beautiful and true
I am lovely and intelligent
I am ugly and dark
I am obtuse and ignorant
I am sparkling white light and rainbows
I am the dark hiding shadow
I am green meadows and pasture
I am the wild running Mustang
I am the caged bird
I am the eagle that soars
I am nightmares and cold night
I am warmth and comfort
I am pain and suffering
I am pure indescribable joy
I am blue sky and white clouds
I am hurricane and tornado
I am compassion and empathy
I am all selfishness
I am judge and jury
I am unconditional love
I am ever changing
I am constant
I am reality
I am clarity
I am complex
I am simple
I am human
I am spirit
I am alive
I am lies and duplicity
I am the sum of all truth
I am you
