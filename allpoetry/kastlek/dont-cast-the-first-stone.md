title: "Don't Cast the First Stone"

A judgment place upon a feeling or poem 
Is a judgment placed upon a heart

It pierces to the very soul
With a comment biting and oh so cold

Why do they not want to hear
The undistorted facts thus told

Does denial run deep in their veins
Do they hide their own aches and pains

Hurts that they must have endured
No one gets through life unscathed

Have their tender feelings gone underground
Nevermore to be found?

Let the one who says, truly, only love they've known
Be the one to cast the first stoneI wrote this because I have experienced the first-hand damage that comes from being judged for feeling.
