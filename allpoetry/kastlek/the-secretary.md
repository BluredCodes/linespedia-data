As I sit and stare into space
At the smooth expanse of my empty desk
I cannot help but wonder at the emptiness
of a world of professional nothingness.

Without a document to fix upon
Nothing really exists.
Wait until something arises
To end this nothingness.

A world of desks, a world of suits
A world of lunches and computers.
What would happen if I just stood up
And walked out of that door?

In this world I am as useful or useless
As the computer that sits upon my desk.
And still I sit and stare into space
Trying to look industrious.

Without this world of nothingness
I cannot pay my rent.
Without this world of nothingness
I cannot afford to eat.
Still, it doesn’t really seem to matter
Who sits in my seat.Just a little thought from someone bored to tears at work.  I'm not a secretary anymore, but it still reflects on the seeming irrelevance of the workers in our society.
