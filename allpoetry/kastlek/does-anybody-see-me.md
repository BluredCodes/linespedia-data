---
title: "Does Anybody See Me?"
writer: "KastleK"

How are you?  they ask (often for show)

As though they really want to know

I respond I’m okay or I’m fine

Because their message is get out of my way

I have someplace to go, do you mind?

It’s really important don’t you know?

More important than the state of your heart or soul

I stand there and whirl as they pass me by

I hate it when my eyes fill

I hate it when I begin to cry
