---
title: "The Shattered Mirror"
writer: "KastleK"
slug: "the-shattered-mirror"

Defeated by the department store mirror, again

Surrounded by the shroud of substance once needed
Hatred, despair, spread through her mind
Coursing with a life of their own

Pulsating, vibrating
The shame settles like a cloud around her mind
Devastation once known is known again

Frozen in time

The shame of long ago consumes her
She views herself through a shattered mirror
Seeing the wretchedness of others

The squalor of a time gone past

Her face is marred with anguish
She sees the depravity of their actions
As though it is etched indelibly upon her face and form

Impaired by the brutality inflicted
Unable to perceive her own image
She smashes the mirror

Watching as shards fall to the ground

Sweeping away the shattered fragments of her life
Reality returns as she resumes her existence
