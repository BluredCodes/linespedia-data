Is there no restNo rest for the rainFalling to it's graveUpon the misty window paneAs I watch the worldFrom inside my cageNever leaving with triumphOnly returning with rageSee the marvelous moonlightIt lights the wayThrough the darkness of chaosWhile the rain washes me awayAway to the nothingnessThat creeps behind my backAway to that placeThat place in me thats blackI seek only for restFrom drowning in this sea of painIs there no end to this lonlinessFeeling restless as the rainThe Lonely Poet
