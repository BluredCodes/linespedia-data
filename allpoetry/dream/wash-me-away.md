The choices we makeLike the drugs we intakeAnd the tears fill their eyesLike rain clouds fill the skiesForever on my ownFeeling so much more aloneSee the beauty in the skyAs tears pass through my eyesWashing away the painLike the blessed blessed rainWanting to be freeWanting to find the other side of meI'm lost inside you're smileMissing you all the whileI'm so scared of what I faceGrowing more to hate this placeSave me motherSave me fatherI am falling farther fartherInto the pit which I have dugAll the pain and all the drugsRain come back to meCome back and set me freeForever feeling trapped,A piece that never snappedStruggling on another dayOw please rainWash me awayThe Lonely PoetThis poem tells a story about how people can indulge in material things....every formed thing is impermanent!
