---
title: "Something Pure and True"
writer: "Dream"

Pushing away the tears
Just leave me to my own
I took the words
And put them in my skin
I'm living a lie
And the truths found within
I don't need the words to say
They're already there
So pay attention to the waves
That lie behind our sight
Soon enough we'll see if we are ready or not
Who knows how the end will come
All I have is the skin
The skin I hide behind
So don't look at me
Like I have nothing you want
I'm filled with the magic
That can never be touched
And it feels like you're so far away
I guess you forgot the words
That I wished you would say
we're too much the same
But so much unlike
But now I'm left cold at night
Were are the covers
That kept me so sheltered
Were is the love
That mede me feel
So much better
I was never there
When I thought I was
The truth it hovers
Just above
So take this pain
And shove it in you're veins
Feel the pulse of torment
Bleed from your own face
Good bye to the good times
They are all fucking spent
I thought things were real
The things you said
You never really meant

The Lonely Poet
