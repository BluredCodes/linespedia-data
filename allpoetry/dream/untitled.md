Goodbye blue skiesAlone as a spirit in the rainTo the ends of the earth I travelRunning from my painSailing off with the breezeThe world within my handsSomeday I'll follow my footstepsBack to were I standAll that you struggle to makeWill fall to pieces in your handsThere is nothing in the endMaybe then you'll understandAll that remains is memoriesExperiences relived againThe rest of you will die awayAll that's left is your experiences in the endFill In The Blanks....On this one?I dunno just thought I'd share. Take care.~Dream
