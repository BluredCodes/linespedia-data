title: "Sweet Child"
writer: "Dream"

Life is just a fantasy
And nothings what it seems
Wishing all you hold within you're hands
Wasn't just a dream
Waking in the silence of a dark empty world
Knowing self-destruction is slowly grabbing hold
Feeling that sensation that nothing is this good
Feeling like happiness was a story you once told
Always scratching the surface
Never finding whats inside
Innocence is something of the past
Something that long ago died
These words inscribed upon my soul
This heart no one can hold
Like a fairytale
Life's a story I've been told
So forgive me I have lost my way
Forgive me for not believing in you
When you said evrything was nothing
To me it now seems true
Sweet child so shiny so new
What has the world done to you
There you are
Behind a dark disquise
Wipe away those tears
That dround your precious eyes
Of all the chances
You had so few
You've seen the world
Look what its done to you
Everything so bright 
Soon fades away
Never set free
Like the words I wish to say
So please forgive me
For doing you so wrong
Feel what nothings like
For my childhoods now gone
