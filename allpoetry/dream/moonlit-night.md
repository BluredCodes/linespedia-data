title: "Moonlit Night"
writer: "Dream"

I curse the darkness
That swallows the light
Alone to face the silence
Knowing nothings right

A prisoner of my own mind
Trapped behind words I can't say
Afraid you might not understand
Why I am this way

I beg for you to comfort me
While you leave me here to die
I lay in the darkness
As I silently cry

I can't change what is
With all these tears
Knowing it won't matter
When they'll too dissappear

The night spreads it's darkened wings
Swallowing the evening sky
Magic as a moonlit night
Beneath the stars I die

The stars that fill the heavens
Woven into my dreams
Waking to nothing
So sad it all must seem

So I stare into the darkness
Were lonliness is seen
Wishing I've awoken
Awoken in some dream

I once felt the warmth of love
Pass through my brocken soul
And as I stood in silence
I knew I'd have to watch it go

So here I am
With nothing whole inside
I knew something was there 
Before it died
