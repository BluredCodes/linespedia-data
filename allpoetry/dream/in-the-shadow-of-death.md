Finding comfort in chaosIn the time I spend aloneFeeling like a newborn childThrust into a world I've never knownArising like the sun at dawnMy fears growing highNot knowing how to climbLow and cold I cryWho will love me now Now that I have grownWho will know my HEARTFearing that I will die aloneHow could I have been so blindI had the flame to light the wayHow could I just stand asideAnd watch the pain tear me awaySo now I am the chaosleft behind to die aloneFinding comfort in the darknessBecause fear is all I've ever knownThe Lonely PoetThis one is dark and deep.  I hope you feel the same deep puncture.  Please tell me what you think. Dream
