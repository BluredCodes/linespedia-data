I can never get this rightBut the words I can't defineLife is a never-ending fightAnd death is so divineSo what's to be accomplishedWith words I cannot sayMy life has been demolishedIn every dying dayJust tell myself to let go of hateBut I must change what I believedSomehow I missed my fateOr have I simply been deceivedI take a journey back in timeWhen to me things seemed so greatOr was I just blindThe pain has taught me how to hateIn ways I try to hideBurdened by my hateSomehow the child in me diedAnd now I know that mistakeSomething that's been lostCan never be retrievedMistakes I made had no consequenceThat's something I believedAt time's I've been aloneTo face the worst of fearsLiving with all this pain on my ownI hide behind these tearsCannot show my painKnow my true weaknessThe world can be so vainI can't find my happinessMaybe tomorrow will change my mindMaybe these answers I will findI know yesterdays a dreamDeath seems so divineThe Lonely PoetHey I wanna know if ya guys like my writing.
