There is nothing I can writeFor all my words are meaninglessAnd what is there to sayWhen all my thoughts are emptinessThere is nothing left for meFor my world is lost awayThere is nothing to start over withFor nothings left to stayYet feeling something somehow in all this nothingnessSomething I could only feelYet feeling all that uglinessI wish nothing were so realAnd my insanity was life to meYet my life won't stay the sameAnd living with all that crazinessOnly craziness I becameSo can I ever move beyondWhen the worlds so far behindAnd will anything be gainedWhen nothings all I findWhy can't someone tell me why it isWhy for me life seems nothing more but hopelessnessWhy can't someone explain whyWhy I'm left with all this nothingnessThe Lonely Poetjust so ya know I wrote this February 28th 2000 2 days after my birthday, 2 days after my brother attempted suicide, while I was in a treatment facility....:( just so ya know....~Dream
