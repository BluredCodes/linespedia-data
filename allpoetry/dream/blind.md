title: "Blind"

I see what you see not
This world of darkness you have brought
Shadows dancing in your eyes
Feeding on my hopeless cries
Seeping through your veins
Consuming all your pain
Strength slipping out of sight
Don't let go I'm holding tight
Dying flames drowned by your mind
What you've feared has left you blind
