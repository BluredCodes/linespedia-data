Don't expect to pierce the darkness
When you close you're eyes
Don't mistake the silence
That listens to your cries
Don't fight with the darkness
That fill inside your head
Don't try to revive
What's already dead
Don't say that it was nothing
When you know that it was you
Don't depend on anything
No matter what you do
Don't say I didn't warn you
Because you know the truth
Don't say it's not your fault
For what's happenned in you're youth
Don't forget to say goodbye
You owe the world atleast that
I have walked in the same places
And sat were you have sat
Don't you see that you are nothing
And slowly nothings taking hold
Don't you see the bottom coming
When the misery has left you cold
Didn't you see it coming
Or did you blink you're eyes
And did you pierce the darkness
Only to feel the torment in you're cries
Well don't expect me to pick up the pieces
Don't expect me to scrape you off the floor
There's nothing there when you close you're eyes
Don't expect for me to care when you're not here anymore
