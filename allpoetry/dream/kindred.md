Nothing is real anymoreMy heart smashing on the floorYou stole my thunderPulled me underForever trusting in the rainShowering down like my painThe darkness I faceSuch a fucking disgraceThe love's been passed out and there's none left for meSo I take my spot in the crowdBut my heads still in the cloudsMy soul kindered by an eternal flameBut no one care's to speak my nameSuch a loser I don't know when I winThe pain it hides behind my skinFlesh and boneIt's cold as stoneLove come's after lustBut like evrything it too returns to the dustSmoke circles in my headHelping me forget the fucked up thing's you saidMy sorrows to deep to drink awayTo old to be back in the gameTo young to make my nameCan't escape changeMorbid and derangedEverythings how it wasn't supposed to beA kindered soul for eternityThe Lonely PoetThanx for your comments and for rating Divine so highly.
