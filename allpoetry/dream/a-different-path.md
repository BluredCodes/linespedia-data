She walks a spirit of this earthHer eyes flaming greenSearching through you're soulOnly darkness can be seenYou have felt the coldAs you walk in the rainFeeling numb to the worldFeeling numb to the painYou have a story to tellYou're eyes have so much to sayYou've walked this earth foreverAnd forever you will staySome are blind to see you're beautyFor you disguise it so wellSome are blind to see the truthTheir live they live in hellHell on this earthFor those who cannot seeSee the beauty behind the darknessSee the living breathing meThe pulse of lifeRunning through our veinsFeeling touched by the beautyFeeling numb to the painSo many paths to travelEach one a different endNever knowing what awaitsBehind each approaching bendBut the struggle aloneIs what we have so greatThe drive of lifeThe love we re-createFeel it inside you For their it's always beenSomw still blind to the truthEach path a different endThe Lonely PoetI hope I haven't posted this one already.
