title: "Holding On By Nothing"

Once an unsuspected desire
Added to the burning fire
Flames glaring in your eyes
Love's a dying flower
Something sweet gone sour
Dying like my hopes and dreams
The higher it gleams
Dancing flames
Of all my pains
Hopeless lies
Inside your eyes
There it hides
There it dies
Too much concern
You make it burn
Adding to the fire
Wanting desire
Heart I broke
Burns up in smoke
So here I stand
While you demand
I know you feel it
I can't help but see it
For I've gone inside
I know were it hides
So your a little scared
Afraid that I've cared
You say you mess up my head
Wanting that fire dead
I no longer have inticipated
You seem intimidated
By the ropes 
Attached to all your hopes
And I still fear
Tha you will dissappear
That you'll stop caring
And we'll stop sharing
That day my last goodbye
For I'll be dead
Trapped by the fire in you're head
Added to the maze
Added to the blaze 
It's not what it seems
It's my dying dreams
But who could care
There's no one there
And I'm holding on
To something already gone
I'm falling in the hole
You dug deep inside my soul
Afraid I use
And you confuse
So forget how it used to be
That's what's killing me 
You are nothing
Hiding something
As I fall inside
I see the last glimpse of you're eyes
No more a blazing fire
You've lost your desire
I NEEDED YOUR HELP
Now I'm stuck with MYSELF
