title: "Impermanent"
writer: "Dream"

Incapable of love
How unchanging it seems
Sweet as the bitterness 
Of unspoken dreams

I feel you gaze
You're eyes a river that endlessly flows
And I find myself drowning
In the streams of your soul

Have I lost the wind beneath my feet
Only for daring to fly so high
Have I yet to feel the impact
Where the earth kisses the sky

Suffering is bliss
When it ends with satisfaction
Desire is only pleased
By a spark of passionate interaction

Can't stop comparing
This life to the one in my head
Can't stop re-living
All the emotions we fed

The eternity of each instant
Every moment spent together
Brings me inner peace
A peace I won't feel again ever

The feast of love 
Leave's you only the more hungry
Craving for that sweet taste
Sweet tasting misery

Now this stillness
Between the time of our meeting
Begging silently 
Quietly pleading

One by one they seem to fall
Like a tree's fallen leaves
How foolish I was to follow
While my idols blindly did lead

I knew better to follow such desires
Struggling in vain still against them
You always seemed to shape my heart
I can't touch that joy again

So now a stale existence
From the life that you left
Learning how closely related
Love's pleasure's are to death
