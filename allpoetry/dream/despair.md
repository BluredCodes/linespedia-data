Never is there never Ever and ForeverThis hate that turns inside my headHolding on to what's been saidDie with my despairNo longer can I careNever is foreverForever is never thereA little piece of the noise that constantly fills my head.  Any of you guys ever just make up line's outta nowhere? This is one I put together.....~Dream
