a wrickled time
a basketcase mind
all to the end 
just be there for me
i'm missing the point once again
with my logic so flawed
what a waste of 15 seconds
what a waste of 20 years
enter now
so i have you fooled
i'm not that good
not like you say

planet drifts
my mind drifts
think about the all around
places, people, politics
getting sick
getting sick
where do i stand
in the midst of it all
-it's all in your head-
--it's out of your control--
people, places, politics
and above all myself

i feel next
due to extra skin
now i have everything i need
i beleive
beleive in what?
myself? my surroundings?
where is this place now
i stand at the zeinith
but a peak nowhere to be found
i'm still waiting
still wondering if this is logic
if this is what i originally came here to do
i forget
act saved
for those who surround you
nothing comes to mind
where are your reasons?
where's your cover now?
when do i find out how?
where is the white-out?

beaming with laughter
he looks out the window
"pityful" he says
joy creased his face
humbled by the masses
stored for safe-keeping
he looks you in the eye
and he just plain doesn't like you
