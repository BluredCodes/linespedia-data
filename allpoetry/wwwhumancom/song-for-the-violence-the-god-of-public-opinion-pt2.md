buisness as usual
reaction is swift
did we stop for a moment?
think?
there's a better way
integrity saved
6,000 of us
6,000 of them
we can trade back in forth all day
but not in my name
not in my name

be carefull with the gun, it's loaded you know
it's tangable and real
everyday i see the American flag
and someone pissing in it's honer
and telling me i should do the same
this is not what it means to me
this is not what it's supposed to mean to anyone
"but we will win and this is our cause"

age of ignorance never ended
hate swells all around
"we're a country united"
we're the masses united
in hate and fear
is that something to be proud of?
if a man is arab
middle eastren decent
he's not welcome here
fucking America
they are them
we are us
that's why

"patriatism"
hate
"a nation united"
united in hate
"a war on terrorism"
and anyone around them
patriots or nazis
everybody's flawed
everybody's flawed
everybody's flawed

not in my name
not in my name
not in my name.....don't let the screams for vengence drown out the voice of reason....they way i see it if they come over here, kill 6,000 innosent people, then we go over there and kill 6,000 innosent people, our cause is no longer justified. there are ways to go about bring these people to justice that wouuldn't jepordize innosent afghan lives. but aperantly that isn't worth it. because they are them and we are us therefore they deserve to die and we do not. the tradmark hypocricy of America shinning proud these days.
