when time or dreams give outan empty box in an empty room
maybe it's better this way
maybe now it'll make sence
close my eyes for the first time
taste disease
suck it out
suck it out
fucking rip it out
stone faces melting
confussion coming over me
come crying back
crawl back
crawl

all i wanted was something beautifull
something not swinging a fist
pour silence down on me
i can't handle another drop
all i wanted was something beautifull
time can teach me this
shame in all i am
if i can exsist at all

push me down
keep me there
right where i belong
what did i do to desurve....
"shut up"
be down
face it 
crawl
crawl
crawl
crawl
crawl

where does malice come from?
wher can it go from here?
i don't see wher i come into play
though i know i shouldn't ask questions
this is where time begins
this is where time endsthis isn't my best work. deal with it. it isn't my worst eather
