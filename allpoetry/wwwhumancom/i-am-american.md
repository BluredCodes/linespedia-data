i hide behind God 
i hide behind guns
i am American
you are a disease
you are not me
i am American

it once was said
"all men are created equal"
but what's the value
of our lives vs. theirs

i once said
"this is my country too"
you responded with
"you sure don't desurve it"

what does it matter
it's just a dead bbug
no worth, it's just one less
we build a country on certain principals
turn it off when it surves our needs
we don't care who's guilty
you look like one of them
the hate America breeds
"they say God is on their side"
"but God is on our side"
therefor i must be wrong
God proclaims it

"sometimes blind vengence, war, and yes even hate in needed"
there is no excuse for hate
no situation justifies it
"it's war, deal with it"
it's the war i have a problem with
you can't say it is because it is
and we can't just turn off due prosses
because they are different
"well they should have thought of that before they joined a terrorist group"
what if they didn't
that's what the trial is for
innocent untill proven guilty
not here 
not in America
not anymore

"in the past we've done the same kind of things"
so that makes it right
terrable atrocities in the name of democracy
manifest destony
slavery
imprisenment of Japanese Americans in WWII
is our past that much to be proud of
but the spoiled brat always thinks it's right
debate with you on chatrooms
all you have is stupid comments on my typing
refuse to discuss facts
just stupid 3rd grade insults

i hide behind God
i hide behind guns
i am American
you are a disease
you are not me
i am American

i am American
or just the KKKall the things in quotes were actually said to me, eather in letter form in responce to a series of letters to the editor that i had published in the local newspaper, or in political debate on verious AOL chatrooms. i actually was in a chatroom for an hour trying to talk intellagently with people and all they were doing is making fun of my screen name and my horrible spelling. no one had anythinng intellagent to say in their defence.
