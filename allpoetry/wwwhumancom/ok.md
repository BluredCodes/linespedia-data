getting over losing
know that it's won
i drank from the river that's now my home
so calm
can fate be perfect?
i'v found nothing perfect yet
but i know that i'm trying
and i think that it's working

dead babies in a wrinkle in time
dead prophets standing in line
i'm nowhere
i'm now
i'm all that's between
calling out ot me 
in the dark
this is me
i know i'm home
not so alone anymore
i can let it go
it doesn't matter too much about the things that surround me
all i know now is that once in a while
i'm ok

fallow the wake
the point where the wave broke
rolled back into the sea
i'm not perfect and neither are my days
but i know i'm not dying
not anymore
karma cashing in
finally
with honesty and an mind open
my life and my music
it's my reality
craft it to my will
the world belongs to me
the world belongs to me

dead babies in a wrinkle in time
dead prophets standing in line
i'm nowhere
i'm now
i'm all that's between
calling out ot me 
in the dark
this is me
i know i'm home
not so alone anymore
i can let it go
it doesn't matter too much about the things that surround me
all i know now is that once in a while
i'm ok

it not that it's never dark
but it's not always dark
and in the end
i'm ok
i'm oki'm a little more content with my life then i was about a year ago. i still have my days where i'm depressed, my moments of darkness. but due to my girlfreind, my freinds and my music things are a lot better now. i have the ability to smile now for the first time in a while.
