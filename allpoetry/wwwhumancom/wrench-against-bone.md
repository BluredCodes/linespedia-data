title: "Wrench Against Bone"

driver says "get out of the way"
"i don't have time for this"
i'm standing in the pauses
between moment and time
searching out ways
counting the days
i killed enough pride
for a weeks worth alone
i'm trying but i failed
i failed my own

a vision, a cure
a phone book lying open
i'm keeping on time
now i realize
the projection apears
enter, be filled
hear a saint call out
"i'm drowning God"
"i have his head on my wall"
it means nothing to you all
the way things come down
keep it out of your mouth
but the gun just seems to fit
the all-imposing sides
eye me breathless
i stand in my pause
and wait

wrench against bone
i'lll make up my own
i hate to go outside any more
my own little space
where pain delays
i'm all out of waste
i'm all out of waste
take me to higher ground
try to be humane
most weakness comes from within
where can i find it?
maybe someday i'll wake up
maybe someday i'll wake up
maybe someday i'll wake up....it just kinda happened...i didn't even mean to write this poem. i was just writing down lines that i didn't want to foget and eventually it became a poem
