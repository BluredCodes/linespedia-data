i wear my crown 
in the ruins on myself 
i thought i was the ocean 
i thought i was the wind 
in the cool waterways 
the whispers drag me down 
i thought i was the ocean 
i really was a puddle 

i wear my crown 
high on broken head 
wastfull was the day 
but always saphire blue 
baptized in moter oil 
the nature of the cold 
living in the summer 
i see what i am losing 

i wear my crown 
but mocked by children wearing gold 
i see it in the mountains 
with my hanging means 
mirror image of the dead 
but i thought i was the ocean 
keeping with my dark days 
i pledge i'm standing down 

i lost my crown 
feeble minds in asprin haze 
people are the meaning 
God has let me down 
breathing naked fire 
complacent in the sun 
clear in frail light 
the dogs go marching by 

i lost my crown 
breeching codes of men 
look through cracked skin 
and leave no stone unturned 
i never beleived that no one cares 
dust on curtains held 
i'm living with the tide 
and running from my ghost 

i wear my crown 
on face i crave that's missing 
fill me up with evolution 
nothing to make a hole 
i thought i was the ocean 
i really was the Dead Sea 
generic by definition 
and crippled by my own hand 

i wear my crown 
dawned on demon's head 
bleeding fists apon the wall 
and enlightened by the pain 
i feel i need to leave 
i fell asleep and stayed 
it's cold out in the ocean 
and it's hard to keep a flame 

i lost my crown 
to shrink with the ages 
here's to a new life 
one of driving minds with me 
i light a roman candle 
and put it in my mouth 
a flare of briliant lights 
and a world gone astray 

i wear my crown 
drinking water as pure as gold 
clear the darkness 
and shower in my own tears 
complaining to the sunrise 
the sky comes crashing down 
living with a facelift 
and a lifesize crown 

i lost my crown 
in a river flowing fast 
i thought i was the ocean 
with dead wind apon my sleeve 
growing grayer by the day 
baptized inside myself 
i listen to the ocean 
with needles stabbing down 

three hands and a chair of colors 
i beleived that i could fly 
now i see a ghost 
with a baptized crown
 
the three hands that bless the sky
remove their fingers
float apon the ocean
a gun to my head
and finally remove my crown
