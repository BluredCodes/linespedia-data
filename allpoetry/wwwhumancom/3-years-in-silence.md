put the gun in your mouth
c'mon, you earned it
a life that's so empty
a mother that's so cold
your child is sacred
not profit
you fucking whore
i watched for 3 years in silence
as he wasted his life
wrestaling with knives and talking him down
fits and tantrams
and 10 mile walks at 2:30 in the morning
"you're going or we'll get rid of this baby right now"
"i have my ways. i'll lock myself in the bathroom and you can forget about it"
using him
using her
using me
i tell him to snap out
she's only white trash
and eventually he did
so go on. you earned it
the world needs a favor these days
