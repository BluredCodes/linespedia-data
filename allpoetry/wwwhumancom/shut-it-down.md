come down
give me the benifit of the doubt
i grew apon but so far down
i know i'm sold
i know i'll go
soon enough
as the day retires 
no faith come alive
not for me
not ever
i'm not another
i'm not even me
wait
drop the silence
reading of the fake star
laying in my backyard
perfect
final
over
on my knees
giving head to the machine
it's been all me

a one day watch of covered bridges
a two day trip to the big unknown
a three day wait for things to come together
a four day waste of time

i'm so saved but i exagerate
i'm so hungery but i can't move
the days become hours
the days become seconds
the days become nothing at all
stop coming
the waiting can crush me
waiting for what?
a bloody noes and a headache
i'm still dreaming
no wait i'm just asleep
be forthcoming with those who are stalking you
be forthcoming with me and me alone
the metal's so cold against my skin
the world's so cold against my kin
for this gift wait
for this gift wait

i stole it away
and i don't feel guilty
i stole it away
but lost it soon after
god can't reach me
prophet can't teach me
it's all the same to me
cannot find it
replace it with days
slip it back unnoticed
i'm crying for help
i'm not listening though
i'm crying to death
"give me it all"
"give me it all"
"i want the world"
"no catches"
"no strings attached"
"i want the world"
"i want it all"

i know now what i can't be
i know now what i can't be
i know now what i can't be
