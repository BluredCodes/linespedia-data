witch hunt, witch hunt
throw away the dead
exchange looks, burn the books
kill the towelheads

hide behind guns
hide behind God
hide behind government
say anythhing to prove me your right
"don't like it get out of the country"
well ain't that the American way
we toil day and night over vengence and hate
we scream at you "child don't speak to me that way!!!"
"god is behind us"
a cold wind hits your back
what makes you think whatever higher power
is on a side at all

witch hunt, witch hunt
throw away the dead
exchange looks, burn the books
kill the towelheads

i grant you a power
maybe a few more
but just for the time being
give them back after the war
wait breathless for the climax of the story
you'll be waiting forever
when does it end
"when terror is dead"
i guess we just fucked ourselves then
we don't have to wait for them to destroy us
we'll just do it ourselves

male age mid 20's
i want to ask you a few questions
for the safety of the nation i need to detain you
1997
an American woman tried in Puru
American government complains
it's unconstitutional
(it is)
but now it on our side
"we'll try them in tribunals, they desurve to pay"
but we found american with them
"well now what do we do"
"God help us if we deny an American his rights"
"God bless America and the American way"
i disagree....
"get out of my counntry"
"we don't want your kind here"

witch hunt, witch hunt
throw away the dead
exchange looks, burn the books
kill the towelheads

innocent untill proven gulty
not in America
nobody seems to realize
that's the point of having a trial
the government told us their goal was to destroy democracy
but we beat them to it

witch hunt, witch hunt
throw away the dead
exchange looks, burn the books
kill the towelheadsthis is about the hate, this is about the racism, this is about the compleat disrespect for peoples rights except for those who sypathise with the masses. i get told to get out of the country if i disagree with the same people who dare to call themselves american. America is so caught up in vengence for sept11th that we don't really care if who we catch are really guilty or not. if we cought them and they are middle eastren then they are guilty enough. i turned the rhyming on and off. i'm not sure why. it just seemed to work.
