an echo in my mind
my voice just a whisper
tag me, mother
in home of contept
i'm gone
    bring again so i can see 
    the rotted flesh inside of me
    toning down of being mean
    a slave to love, a slave to me
it'a never what i wanted
face riddled with emotion
i don't want to sleep
i guess i'm impossable
i'm done
    i dropped my eyes to be so blind
    to lie in bed and kill some time
    i take this life and say it's mine
    i hope it's not hope, don't say goodbye
hands to face and what am i
but certain pain for you
i crawled out from under my rock
and now i'm deadwaight
i'm sorry
    i fake a smile and dress the blade
    but wake to dream when it's today
    i can't show you another way
    i would die for you to stay
i beat myself it does no good
scream at the mirror
"stop me now"
"before i do more damage"
i clutch the phone
"don't talk like that
"save me"
"save me"
"save me"
"save me"

someday i'll find
this place in me
but i'm obsessed
with uncertainty
covered in cuts
that don't want to bleed
severed myself 
from reality

it brought me to my knees....i had a bad day
