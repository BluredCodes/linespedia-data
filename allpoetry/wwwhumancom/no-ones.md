never fuck where the dead children lie
never fuck where the dead children lie
where the pain has sown the dispised
never fuck where the dead children lie
'cause they're not dead in their eyes

only hope now
but human doesn't matter
face down 
bullet sown to skull
we lead, we inspire
kill and maim
"they're not the same"
"they're not the same"
mind-numbing how you can go through with this
can't see, there is no whites in their eyes
a faceless victom
"but he was one of them"
born evil
due to herratige

this is America
this is the free
this is the ground we built to stand on
"this all is only for me"

now i'll take this American flag and i'll burn it
as you stand on those silent ruins
and now i'll take this American flag and i'll burn it
there's nothing left to beleive in anymore........

never fuck where the dead children lie
never fuck where the dead children lie
where the pain has sown the dispised
never fuck where the dead children lie
'cause they're not dead in their eyes

we're not fighting a war
we're living a war
a fixation on the prospect
there is always more to do
wipe them out, they're different from us
they don't desurve to exsist
hate rules the world
but that's the way it's been
sence the first human's inseption
we are a shame....

life belongs to those who can ruin it
destroy it and crush it
choose to take it away
when does evolution kick in?
when can grow to be better then this?
it doesn't happen
we're only human
what more can we expect?
from the annels of time
this is what our kind is bred for
we're not that intellagent after all

never fuck where the dead children lie
never fuck where the dead children lie
where the pain has sown the dispised
never fuck where the dead children lie
'cause they're not dead in their eyes

this is the world
this is the free
this is the ground we built to stand on
"this all is only for me"
