bartered dreams of silence
cold embrace and watched all ripped from me
you call simple pain as my life
the flies on my corpse
digging deeper
kill to feed it
holes in eyes make great getaways
and i'm in that hole now
so change it all
be ready and final
insertion of life
and come fallow me

what if i did?
coming real
coming me
what if i did?
try to feel
try to be
what if i did?
to reveal
broken lip
what if i did?
harber vowel
soaked in shit

aim to me
colered black in time
that pained look will fallow me
lick the envolope
seal my fate
eternal glass
half full or half empty
i feel the pinch
the welt stayed for weeks
i'm just living on emotion
a fucking grain of time 
all the while i stand
short-winded to the sun
the dead hand on my shoulder
a voice says "you're coming home"

what if i did?
coming real
coming me
what if i did?
try to feel
try to be
what if i did?
to reveal
broken lip
what if i did?
harber vowel
soaked in shit

so dead, cold the world in suspended animation
i killed the one thought that crossed my mind
i wash the metallic taste from my mouth
for another moring has come
wishing can't bring back the night before
dreaming can't fully re-create
what the facless need to be
a breath escapes my lips
shhh
the morning will hear you
open eyes and come for me
one message is true
the others not sure
one fist in the air
two more in the gut
i'm wasting something beautifull
i'm going back to sleep
i'm going back to sleep

what if i did?
coming real
coming me
what if i did?
try to feel
try to be
what if i did?
to reveal
broken lip
what if i did?
harber vowel
soaked in shit

soaked in shit
to vomit dreams so tall
soaked in shit
to purge me of it all
soaked in shit
to save me from the fall
soaked in shit
and curl into a ball

please excuse my broken lip
