booze and violence
scream out
 "turn your head"
 "children should be seen and not heared"
goddamn this worthless face in the glass
when dark nights turn darker
i take it in my own hands
"if he comes in my room i'll kill him"
"i'll kill him"

gone is empty folds
that lay waste in this dream
there's more then one way to rape, father
look at me and remember
remember the drama
fucking drama, i'm sick of it
why do i keep reliving it?
lost sleep in the fetal posision
in the corner of my room
fearfull of the unseen
all i know is he's after me
how old was i?
5...6?
asking "when will tommorrow begin"

i was always lesser 
i was always nothing
a pawn, a bargoning chip
kidknapped to a picnic
 "you'll come stay with us"
a bleak stance
"i'm tired, i want to go home"
 "but you can't"
"i'm scared, i want to go home"
 "but you can't"
 "they don't care about you"
 "you should stay with us, we do"
grandpa tracks us down
11:30 on a school night
yelling and screaming through locked car door
with a confussed 8-year-old in the passenger seat
he reclaims me
then he turns to me
 "they don't care about you"
 "come home with us, we do"
i'm confussed

close my eyes 
i hear them again
i stand at the open window
only 8 years old
"what would happen if i jumped"
"would i fly away?"
"if i died everyone would be better off anyway"
i stand there
hours pass
fear of death meets fear of life
someone's trying to get me
someone's trying to kill me
what did i do to them
i exsist
i shouldn't
that's what dad wanted in the first place

i'm now 11 or 12
visit my step-dad
trailerpark upstate
a freindly joke over cookie dough ice cream
a psudo-kick
 "oh, i'll kick you back"
 "yes", "no", now he's by his throut
glasses fly
 "are you happy now?"
i ran off in tears
midnight i wake
find i'm the topic of conversasion in the next room
 "he's a brat, we all know that"
i know when i'm not wanted

mother has someone new
white trash don't like me much
from the coutch
 "you lazy, worthless failure"
 "get off your ass and clean up around here"
 "i'll raise you right yet"
i say i'm going to a concert
he respectfully disagrees
 "keep it up"
 "i'll knock your head clean off your shoulders"
i was always lesser
i was always nothing
i stare silent into his eyes
behind me on the table lay a steak knife
now taken into hand behind my back
i stare silent
thinking "one more step and i'll bary this into his skull"
one more step
i stare silent
begging for one more step

demons flood me, many years
can't escape the flying beers
demons flood me
internal
external
thick skin cannot save me
a loving mother cannot bathe me
she cannot touch me at all
demons in, vomit out
internal
external
it doesn't matter anyway
amensorry it's so long. i was telling a story. and i'm aware of the mispellings. i can't spell worth shit and i can't get the spellchecker to work on this thing
