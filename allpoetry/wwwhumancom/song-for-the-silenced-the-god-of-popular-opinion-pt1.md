an explosion
then silence
this is what they wanted
so let's turn the world grey
"there are sensitivities"
"shhhh"
with lips sown shut
carfull not to offend
they won't break us
we'll do that ourselves

silence screaming in my ears
("let's try to live a normal life")
giving in to all the fears
("let's try to live a normal life")
let's go back
maybe learn from mistakes
the words can't be burned, only the books they are in
so this is America to you?

fly's gather
decay sets in
nothing moves
it all becomes stagnent
we live in the land of past nazi crypts
just to keep us in control
uneducate yourselves
sence the sick that none of your concern
i will bleed for this
this is my war

silence screaming in my ears
("let's try to live a normal life")
giving in to all the fears
("let's try to live a normal life")
let's go back
maybe learn from mistakes
the words can't be burned, only the books they are in
so this is America to you?

the silent objective
an excuss, a ploy
kiss the ground, forget it's name
follow blindly a fucking idiot
they're not his words anyway
use tragity to perpetuate selfish motives
and let big brother into every aspect of our lives
feel thoughts, say words
but you can't
throughout the ahes they say one thing
mean another
my word is my bond
you can't take that away

silence screaming in my ears
("let's try to live a normal life")
giving in to all the fears
("let's try to live a normal life")
let's go back
maybe learn from mistakes
the words can't be burned, only the books they are in
