one more night bursts into flame
and i'm too weak to think
i sit quietly in TV's glow
waiting for the phone to ring
such a sinking feeling
such a sinking day
i'v been saying for hours
wouldn't have you any other way
tell me over and over again
"i am not, i'm not like this"
"you should move on with your life already"
"i got a whole lifetime of this"
it stinks of passion, another late night
cleanse the body with water from tap
wish away another fight
i can't see from where i'm at....

    where am i going wrong?
    a face in the glass
    i'm trying
    god, i'm trying
    where am i going wrong?
    i'm just waiting
    for the truth to fall
    getting out of the outside
    my words fall like rain
    i pour my soul into jumbbled-up sentences
    re-arrange the thoughts
    coming like a flood
    press stop
    we'll pick this up some other time
    
    don't let the eyes go vacant
    i can't see without the other
    i will go blind without you
    this is my
    beautifull green eyes

    getting out of the outside
    my words fall like rain
    i pour my soul into jumbbled-up sentences
    re-arrange the thoughts
    coming like a flood
    press stop
    we'll pick this up some other time

my words fall like rain
this is my
beautifull green eyesi didn't have a good night last night
