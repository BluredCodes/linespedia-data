strange events with placid smiles
play for keeps
play for liers
i am the frantic death
beat beside while tumers rot
i mistook the meaning
i thought i was dreaming
it's all the same
both night and day
to grow the way takes patience and time
to be it is more
nurture and refine
take me
to peace, or peices
i know not what follows
just that i am here
guide me
reverse the roles for a moment
the texture of space can wait
while i re-collect
re-connect
re-direct what's left
i am the moving peice
in the gears of the planet jammed
i grow tired of faining interest
i'm just pretending i'm waiting
it's not so long as too far down
and i wait
be safe
afraid
just a small smudge of the truth
and i'm not sure if it was for my own good
i play it too safe
i'll never be bothered
so now it's too late to be the same
it's something new
and no one knows or suspects
be awake to me 
and understand
held hands
blind dance
and i'm awake
to see me safe
still afraid
still awake
still insane
still dead.....

i will never know the meaning
i will never be for sure
the coldest hour apon me
i'm in a rush for the freezer
i can't see the world awake
it's dreaming, cold and afraid
who are you?
where do you begin?
it's all a matter of time
just a matter of time
