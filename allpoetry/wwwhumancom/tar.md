i listen to the winds
God, kill me for this
hold the sward while i fall
the flames can envelope me
and i find it so warm
drop to knees
slit the throut and scream
die for me
just die for me
it waon't take long
just a moment or two
i know now how the book ends
this place is not safe anymore

i see the scars
i see the tar
i see my days in wasted hours
i see the haze
was lost for days
i see my soul inside this face

disgrace......

a castle of sand and dead leaves
a stupid little trick to see what i see
i am lost in a dream
all my making
hold me under
i didn't mean to exstst
stare out the window
i see nothing
i see me


i see the scars
i see the tar
i see my days in wasted hours
i see the haze
was lost for days
i see my soul inside this face

disgrace......
