can't see it for what it's worth 
living with dangers of obsene birth
i'm more of less
more or less
sweet caress
to lay to rest
buy a peacefull place
my warning light 
i'm burning out
burn me a peacefull place
and let me back in
when the cold can't effect me no more

a caring touch
but what can i do
the world in an oyster
and i am the cancer
it tears me up to watch this
reality grasps
rips me apart
weakness
it stands before 
everything i see
is no more then a dream

dance for rain but it comes no longer
i stand
with raw emotion
the core of my being 
exposed
with water
to wine
i call for God when God's too busy
he strikes out to find
what has fallen
i blame myself
i am myself

reality from the watchtower
feared from a distance
forced myself down to the beach
baptized in moter oil
i open my eyes
open my mouth
take in a gulp
this is what i desurve
i'm wishing for a future
i don't know where i am
i think i'm going home
i think i'm going home
i think i'm going home......
