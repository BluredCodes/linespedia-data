so soft the breath
she speaks, i have a hard time hearing
living proof things change for the better
press her forehead to mine
"your mine..."
exhale softly
and draw her closer still

a word escapes me
for the time being that is
spread out on my bed 
wearing nothing but my shirt
her eyes dance, radiate
even when she doesn't want them to
she hides her face
dreams of better days
but i'm here today

her eyes
i quit
i'm blind
and full of shit
her eyes
she stays
my mind
amazed
at her eyes

try to keep from being long-winded
cleiche and tired
but i'm not too good at these sort of things
i'm a disease, a reflection
dense and undeserving
this gives me something to be
when before i was nothing

waited a long time
for that single moment to arise
she looks at me longingly
i never that that was possable
sleeping in her warmth
legs tangle and i know
that shhe feels the same

her eyes
i quit
i'm blind
and full of shit
her eyes
she stays
my mind
amazed
at her eyes:)
