profit, prophet
pointing fingers all the way
love thy naighbor
kill thy children
manifest destony
blood on christian hands
grasp the cross at the base
and with a thrust
through the hearts
of all of God's little children
you so easily kill off when they aren't like you
forget the earth
forget the mortal life
as God shakes a finger at you
from a thousend miles away

folding in the name of Christ
grab your gun, he refuses to beleive
witness once too often the truth
enslave....

you drop the razor
it's too cold outside
Christ cuts his wrist in a fit for attension
judge not lest ye be judged
homosexual
-kill him-
pagan
-burn him-
jew
-destroy him-
non-conformist
-well push him away-
-free-thinkers not welcome-
take an ancient faith
older then society itself
and bastardize it for the means to conform
enslave the minds
masses huddled in fear
hold them down

folding in the name of Christ
grab your gun, he refuses to beleive
witness once too often the truth
enslave...

God speaks
man follows
the blood on their hands
who's to blame?
the bones of the children
baried in the textbooks past
disconnect from the earth
the past 
the beginnings
follow the trail of blood
the trail of tears
just forget
move on
become their sheep
