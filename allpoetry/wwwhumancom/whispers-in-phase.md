discuss group function
I've all run out
pain will treat me
and life will eat me
my eyes follow veins of time

so many reckless lies endearing
tumors replace the blood that's in me
I'm waiting for another day
another day to come my way
it came to be the one forgotten trust
another prayer scraped and turned to rust
all encompassing the rules of the game
but it ended days ago

mispronounce, misspell and alter
close my eyes, stumble and faulter
speaking tounges and dropping names
a distant rememberance of a game
all in line, ahead again
remain the same, remain my freind
remain the game, rename the dead
dear God don't stop until the end

follow
wispers in phase
the moment of haze
retreats
so fear the child
grown so small
for in the end it's fate
