a violent twitch by which we judge things
perpetuating hate in which we know
with all things said i stand before your judgment
i care not for confrence with the angels of the quicksand

BOCES learned and bred
mind set on just exsistance
violence, sex and drugs
and your sad little world
what made you top step on the latter?
pants sagged and ebonic talk of gangs
in our quant suburban town
ask me if i'm going to blow up the school
threaten me with violence when i say something too intellagent
for you to understand

a violent twitch by which we judge things
perpetuating hate in which we know
with all things said i stand before your judgment
i care not for confrence with the angels of the quicksand

look around, the perfection is weak
but yours are the confadent genes
utopia is a dream
and we are innocent
a disgusted look from across to street
with Old Navy and pop music by your side
concerned with make-up and shoes
and knowing your better then me
a stupid giggle and a comment about the "fashion victom"
but i know i look cooler then you

a violent twitch by which we judge things
perpetuating hate in which we know
with all things said i stand before your judgment
i care not for confrence with the angels of the quicksand

the ones most closly related
tend to be the most judgemetal
jump apon the psudo-punk rock band wagon
and cheer their stupid little bands
"what's up with them"
"why do they look like that"
and then we get the same stupid questions 
that everybody asks us

a violent twitch by which we judge things
perpetuating hate in which we know
with all things said i stand before your judgment
i care not for confrence with the angels of the quicksand

the angels of quicksand
the hand of the ape man
suburban child of Compton
the punk rock hot bed
welcome to Horseheads
the land of the weak
all humanity united
"get out you fucking freak"i'm sure there are many of you who can relate to this one.
