Valentines Day.
It used to mean something.
To me it's just another day.
I look at other people.
Happy to have one another.
I used to have that.
Now im just alone. 
It's relly depresing.
I see the girl I really cared about.
With a new boyfriend.
He's twice the guy i'll ever be.
I know most of you reading this thik.
Damn he complains alot.
But some of you have now idea the lonlyness I feel.
If you have some one make them feel special.
If you dont find some one.
For me it's hopeless.
I hope you all got something out of this.
I guess all I can do is.............
