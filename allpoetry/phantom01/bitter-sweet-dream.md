A bitter sweet dream.
A day I live like im normal.
A day I dont feel death.
A day people are glad to see me.
Hell im popular.
Heaven hasnt forgoten I exsist.

Y sit by my self at lunch.

God really does care.
Oh crap.
Dammit.

Im awake.Your comments are valuble.
