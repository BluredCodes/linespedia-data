title: "Through the Eyes of a Beast"

The air is crisp and dull
The wind whips at my face
My hooves hit the hard ground
And crush the stones into sediments
Some would see this as a MIRACLE
But I do not... 

One of my own runs behind me
as a rope swiftly flys, but misses
Some would see this as TRAINING
But I do not...

Another disappears in the darkness known as death
He will not return
Some would see this as a long DREAM
But I do not...

The prison is small, but up in the air I soar
Across the rails of redeement
Some would see this as FREEDOM
But I do not...

I must go back, I think
to the one I love
Some would see this as STUPIDITY
But I do not...

Something moves
With wheels that turn
Some would see this as MAGIC
But I do not...

A whinny is cried 
but I am so close
Some would se this as a CHASE
But I do not...

I run to the door
And unhook the latch
He is away at last
Some would see this as SUCESS
But I do not...

The air is cold and threating
The road is long and rough
Some would see this as a SET BACK
But I do not...

Over a hill, to a gully
And into the waters we jump
Some would see this as a THRILL 
But I do not...

No horse and saddle dare jump
and freedom is so near
Wildness is apon us
And life as we know it goes on
Some would see this as a victory
As do I...This poem was written from the eyes of a horse, one that is trying to be caught to be taken to be slaughtered. I wrote this poem for a contest in my guild, but I thought I'd add it here as well... Any critique would be appriciated... Paige
