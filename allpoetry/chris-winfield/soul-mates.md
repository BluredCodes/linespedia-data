title: "Soul mates"
writer: "Chris Winfield"

Why can’t you see? 
That you’d be good with me? 
Need, want, love must be possible 
Wanting, needy, me for you. 
Soul mates reaching across the ether 
To out-distance the distance 
Which separates us. 
Laugh? Cry? 
Love? Die? 
Inevitable separation becomes 
Togetherness at last .
