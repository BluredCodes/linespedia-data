title: "An apology"

I have a thing to do 
I have a thing to say 
I never meant to hurt you 
Please don't leave me this way. 
I'd climb a mountain for you 
I'd paint the sky above. 
I'll run a marathon for you, my love. 
To see you in my arms, 
To sample all your charms 
I'll be true and good 
Trust me if you would.
