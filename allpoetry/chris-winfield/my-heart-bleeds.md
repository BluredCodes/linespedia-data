---
title: "My heart bleeds"
writer: "Chris Winfield"
slug: "my-heart-bleeds"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/11888-My-heart-bleeds-by-Chris-Winfield"

My heart bleeds for you everytime you go away My heart sings for you whenever you are near I love you with a love so true I love you each and every hour My body aches to have you here But you are distant from me, so far away I wish you could be with me, every night and day In my bed and in my life. I just wish my life away.
