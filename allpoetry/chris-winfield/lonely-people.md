title: "Lonely people"
writer: "Chris Winfield"

Lonely people 
Meeting, passing, never touching 
Always needing, wanting, never finding 
Lonely people. 
Crying, laughing, searching until the end 
Lonely people. 
Looking for love, peace of mind 
Securty evades them 
The world seethes with 
Lonely people. 
Needing to reach out but 
They will not touch the hearts 
Of one another.
