Little kitten small and brown
Watch her tumble and fall down
Shaky legs and squeaky meow
Sinks to happy dreaming now
Mother’s licked clean her fur
She settles down begins to purr
Small round tummy filled with milk
Brown coat as smooth as silk
Little kitten brown and small
Captures love from us all.
