I lie in my bed and see 
The green bellybutton monster looking at me 
He tickles me toes 
Then moves to my nose 
He's me bedtime friend you see. 

I found him one night 
There was something not right 
I itched where I just couldn't see 
I poked around and there I found 
Him grinning up at me. 

He's small, he's green and hides away 
For no one else to see 
My bellybutton monster 
Who really does love me.
