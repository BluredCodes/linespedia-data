---
title: "My cyber love"
writer: "Chris Winfield"
slug: "my-cyber-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

I dream of you in my bed at night 
Dreaming of you holding me tight 
Kissing my pillow instead of your lips 
Drinking your love with gentle sips 
Wanting to hold you so close to me 
Knowing our love was meant to be 
Waiting to meet you in real life 
Hoping you’ll make me your loving wife 
Knowing that you, my cyber love 
Will dream of me in the plane above 
Bringing you over the seas to me 
Hoping to turn you and me to we 
Tomorrow we’ll meet for the very first time 
When I’ll be yours and you’ll be mine.
