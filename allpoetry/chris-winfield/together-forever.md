---
title: "Together forever"
writer: "Chris Winfield"
slug: "together-forever"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/19719-Together-forever-by-Chris-Winfield"
---

The rain blots the landscape
As my tears blot this page
Although you're never with me
We are never far apart
I carry you with me hidden deep in my heart
Nothing will ever part us
No-one can come between us
You're' the prince of my dreams
Let me be your queen.
