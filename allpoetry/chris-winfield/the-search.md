Do I walk the narrow path? 
Looking for contentment 
Seeking happiness 
Resisting temptations 
Or should I wander the winding way 
Succumbing to pleasure? 
Does the winding way lead to your door? 
Or will the straight road get there faster? 
Is happiness behind that door? 
Or is it just a dream to lead me stray? 
Dreaming I walk the way 
Looking for fulfilment 
I meander, knowing not what lies ahead.
