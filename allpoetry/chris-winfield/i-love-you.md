---
title: "I love you"
writer: "Chris Winfield"
slug: "i-love-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/17100-I-love-you-by-Chris-Winfield"

Everybody is lonely sometime 
Everybody is blue 
How can I be lonely 
When I've got you? 
When you are here I'm alive 
When you are gone 
Desolation 
Grief is mine 
How can I live apart from you?
