---
title: "The fool"
writer: "Chris Winfield"
slug: "the-fool"
source: "AllPoetry"

I am the wise fool
You met yesterday
A fool for you
A wise man to resist
Forgotten feelings fill
My heart, tired but refreshed
Young but old, seeing you
I love but hate
Hate but love you
