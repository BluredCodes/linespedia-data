---
title: "Times of trouble"
writer: "Chris Winfield"
slug: "times-of-trouble"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/12156-Times-of-trouble-by-Chris-Winfield"

If to God we cannot turn in times of trouble, times of stress 
Who, then will comfort us in our deep distress? 
Bringing peace to the anxious mind 
Calm to the heavy heart 
He surrounds us with his love 
Omnipresent he guides us 
By his wisdom to a new and better life 
Great joy and happiness
