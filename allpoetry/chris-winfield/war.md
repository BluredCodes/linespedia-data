title: "War"
writer: "Chris Winfield"

source: "AllPoetry"

When war comes 
I shall sit down and sing 
About the ones we lost so long ago 
And the ones we are losing now 
When Peace comes 
As it surely will 
I will stand up and shout 
Of victory for the losers 
But they will never hear 
Their eyes are blinded too 
Only the winners are left.
