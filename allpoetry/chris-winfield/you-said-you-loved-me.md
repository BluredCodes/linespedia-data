You said you loved me
You know you did
That day when we
Talked for hours
You said that now
I should know that
All your words were true

Did I believe you? Or
Were your words like
 Water through a sieve?
Your words were carved
In the marble of my heart
And your absence dripped
Pure acid
I struggle to neutralise
To regain some semblance
Of normality.
