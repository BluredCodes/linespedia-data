---
title: "For my Father"
writer: "Chris Winfield"
slug: "for-my-father"
source: "AllPoetry"

I remember the magic of my early life
While you remember betraying your wife
You taught me how to shoot and fish
And I’d obey your every wish.
In my teenage years you were lost to me
Now you’ll be gone forever because of big C
Your nighttime filled with fear and pain
And yet we might never meet again.
Reconciled in my later life
Love cuts through bitterness like a knife.
Whatever your sins, you don’t deserve this
I hope that you’ll go to Heavenly Bliss.
My sweet memories will keep you for me
As young and handsome as you used to be.
Father, dear Father, I wish you could know
How much I’ll miss you when you go.I heard that my father is dying and this is for him
