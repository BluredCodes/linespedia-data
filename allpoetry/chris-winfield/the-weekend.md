Another lonely Friday night
Sitting here, getting tight
Drinking doesn’t clear my sorrow
The way I feel there’ll be no tomorrow
You’re out again, not by yourself
It seems you left me on the shelf
But when you want your laundry done
Then it’s to me you’ll quickly run
I’ll iron and cook and sew for you
Please tell me what more can I do?
My love for you remains so deep
But you’re not with me when I sleep
And so I end another day
At home alone while you play.
