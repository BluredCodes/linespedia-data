The dawn, the dusk and scent-filled day 
Serve to remind what 
Was nearly thrown away 
Down dales and vales up hills and moors 
Glad thanks go out 
To those of mine and yours 
Who died that day 
Stay awhile and gaze 
For sights such as these 
Don't often come your way 
Rest awhile and see 
The magic of the world 
Revealed to you. 
The curtains drawn to all but you
