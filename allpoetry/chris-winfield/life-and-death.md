---
title: "Life and  death"
writer: "Chris Winfield"
slug: "life-and-death"
source: "AllPoetry"

Life is long and deadly
Death is short and swift
Life is death

Death is long and
Life is folly
Folly and fun
Death is solid, reliable
Dependant
So why prefer life?
