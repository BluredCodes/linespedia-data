title: "On a mortuary table"
writer: "Chris Winfield"

When I'm alone my thought turn to death
Not mine but yours
I see you lying on the table
White, flaccid, pallid, uninteresting
But I'm not crying, 'cos
 I've icicles in my heart
I didn't see you as living but
As a living hell
Cold death becomes you well
The only pain I feel is when I'm pierced by
Icicles in my heart.
