title: "Hidden life."
writer: "Chris Winfield"

The shadow hides the pain
On the faces by the grave
He will never laugh again
Nor will he shout and rave.
His hidden secret life
His abuse of son and wife.
Will never now be told,
Not even when they’re old.
To tell would bring them shame
And renew shafts of pain.
But through the deepest shadow
Hope pierces like an arrow.
That time will heal all ill
Far better than any pill.
