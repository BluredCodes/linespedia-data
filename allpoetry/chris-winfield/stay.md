---
title: "Stay"
writer: "Chris Winfield"
slug: "stay"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/40309-Stay-by-Chris-Winfield"
---

Through out the long darkness
Stay with me my love
Touching, gently touching
Stay with me , my love
Side by side we lay
Stay by me, my love.

In brightness we awoke
Stay with me, my love
He stirs, He moves
Stay by me, my love
He leaves
Please stay with me, my love.
A warm hollow remains
And now alone I know, He will never
Stay by me.
