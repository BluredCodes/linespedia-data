I call upon the heavens above
The stars and planets too
To guide me in my quest for love, 
Hopefully leading to you.
Blackbird, sparrow, hawk and dove
Birds of every hue
Please sing and shout
To tell the world about our love.
There is no manner of doubt
That meet we must.
So put your trust in me my love
As I put mine in you.
