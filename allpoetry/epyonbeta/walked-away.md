---
title: "Walked Away"
writer: "Epyonbeta"

Everything I have been taught seeems to be a lie
Everything I see I wish I could deny
I want to run away to a place and be on my own
but I know one thing bout life, I will never be alone.

Fate is playing a joke on me
Laughing at my face when I cry
I had told you everything
This fact I wish was a lie
I bared my soul for you
But all that you could do was wonder why I feel this way
It's something I can't explaine
This love for you I can't contain.
But I bet you think that I am lame
I am cast down into shame.

So I just hold inside these feelings I've contained
As I see you everyday
Wishing I could tell you the truth
I just walk away.
