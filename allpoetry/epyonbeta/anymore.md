Do you remember the time we were alone
We held each other in our arms
I kissed you on the head
And you kissed me back
What has happened since then?

I wonder what happened then.
I remember a happy time
I was happy then you understand
But now I feel this emptyness inside
But then I lift my head up.
A smile comes on my face as see you come.
I ran to you and and tryed to lift you up
But you vanshied these illusions confusing me
I drop to my knees and held my head in confusion

I shouldn't have wasted the time I had wih you
Now you are not here any more
I cant bring you back any more
I can not hold you any more
I just want you back
