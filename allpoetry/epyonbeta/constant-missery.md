I cant understand this sorrow that I feel
It grows inside my heart
All I know is this feeling is so unreal
I dont know how to start.

 I just know I want you know
To bring the joy back to me
All this sorrow has pulled me down
this constant missery

 My life has meaning or so they say
Then why do I feel this way?
All this anger in my head
making thought dead
I want to realease it
but I cant for you see
you mean everything to me
I want you back right now
my life is a spiral in time
a peity worthless thought.

 I just know I want you know
To bring the joy back to me
All this sorrow has pulled me down
this constant missery

 Now my story of love loss ends
I have moved on for once
this feeling that i have gained
is the feeling of ones love loss.
