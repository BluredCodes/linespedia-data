title: "Where do I search?"

I looked up to the sky that day
My mind is so confused
My head bent down
My mind in shambles
I want to go to theplace you are

I want to see that angel that invades my mind
The one who makes me smile
I want to see that angel
Who makes my heart fly higher
I know your out there
Somewhere, I just dont know
Where do I search? How can I find this piece missing in my heart
I want to hold you in my arms and whisper in your ear
I want you to be here with me and chase away these fears.

I look to the west now.
Wondering what your doing and how you are
Then I close my eyes and continue to walk
Your the angel in my mind. I will find you some day
Just you wait. I believe in Fate.
I wont let you get away.
