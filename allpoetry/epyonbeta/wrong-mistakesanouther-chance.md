The night is dark and cold and damp
I wish I could escape
I hold the point to my head
and hoped to never wake

I pulled the trigger to the gun
but all I heard was click
My eyes flew open and I opened the gun up
Five bullets fell into the lake
Where was the one that was supposed to end my sorrow and unhappy thoughts?
Why did I get another chance?
I do not understand
What I did was horid
My life has been so bland

I tossed the gun into the water watched it sink strait down
I walked away with a smirk
The gun began to drowned
