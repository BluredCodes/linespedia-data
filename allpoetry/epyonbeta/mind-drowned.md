title: "Mind Drowned"
writer: "Epyonbeta"

All you can do is bring me down.
Make me hurt inside
All you do is contridict my ideas
Make me want to hide
From all this anger
All this hate I dont know what to do
So I run away to a place 
To a place where I feel happy
A place where I can hide
A place where I can escape life
And build up joy inside

But you always seem to find me no matter what I do.
So I keep on running away from something
And that something would be you.

Get that thought into your mind
I do not want to run no more
Just leave me in piece and leave me alone
But all you want is more
To feel my anger
Feel my hate
To make me be someone you know I won't be for you.

So now I found a place to hide.
I am sure you'll seek me down
And my my life the misery
To make my mind stop and drowned.
