In my life there's been one voice
one person to chear me up
one life, one soul, one inspiration, to fill my heart right up
You made me cry, you made me fall, but always fixed mistakes
I thought real hard and now I see your everything to me
You helped me through this time in life
the one I did not want to see.

I tryed to resist this feeling I felt at first
I thought it was ment to hide
But now I know this thing it was can not be indentified

I love you now as much as before I can not say just why
I want you back in my my life today
I want to live to feel this way
for you to be my one true love
for you to be my radient dove.
