I can't believe what's going on
I feel so alive
I used to be so lonely
That I could not survive
But now my life is happier now that I've got you
An angel has entered inside of my heart
Now a smile comes onto my face
I will no longer feel as if I am falling apart.

We walked down the halls in oposite directions
Then I caught a glance of your face
My heart jumped and began to race
Your eyes were beautiful.
Your hair was just as great.
Then you looked back at me with a smile
I knew that it was Fate

A few weeks later we are now together
and when I am lost inside your kiss my life seems to last forever
I hae never felt love from an angel
I have never felt so pure
I have never been loved by angel
Until that kiss your Heaven killed my Hell
