title: "Glass Shard"
writer: "Epyonbeta"

As I lay down in my bed
The millions of thoughts swirl threw my head.
What they want, I do not know.
All these answers I wish they'd show.
All I want is to go to sleep
Put all these thoughts behind me
But these images just keep coming back
haunting my mind.
Making me snap.
So as I lay down on this cold sheeted bed
the confortor covers my head
the pillows are stiff and hard
These thoughts keep inguring me like a glass shard.
Piercing my hope
Piercing my dreams
piercing everything I hold dear to me
I want them to leave but they wont go away
Why do I have to fall asleep this way?
