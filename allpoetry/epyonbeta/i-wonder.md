---
title: "I Wonder.."
writer: "Epyonbeta"

I wonder all the time what happened
I wonder where I went wrong
I wonder all the time what happened to us
I just can not see my faults

I gave you my heart
my soul
but like a gun you looked away
decieved and shot me in the heart
Causing my heart to sway

So why do I feel the way I do
I wonder all the time what I have to do
I just want to know one thing about you

Do you ever wonder what might have happened?
Where did I go wrong?
What might have happened to us?
What were my faults?

I did everything for you
And yet you just tossed me away
You would walk and leave me alone
You mistreated me everyday

Why did you do this to me?
I do not understand
You shot my heart into a thousand pieces
This fact I do not comprehend how any one can be so cruel
This fact I can not turn into a lie.
