---
title: "Love's Fury"
writer: "Epyonbeta"
slug: "loves-fury"
source: "AllPoetry"

I can feel this distance that has grown between us.
You feel so far away.
I want to hold you in my arms
But you do not feel that way

So I continue to try to break the walls between us
My iron fists bleeding now
I beat on the walls chipping them down
But now my fists are in pain
I can not continue this much longer
Before this fury turns to shame
This ambition grows inside me
I need to get you back.
I avoid all of the pain
I just need you back

I can feel this distance that has grown between us.
You feel so far away.
I want to hold you in my arms
But you do not feel that way

So now that I am broken I fall to the ground
I hold out my hand and look at my fists
Blood is driping on the ground
These walls that held you from me was still up
Do you know what this feels like
To know no matter what you do
Your love has to stay inside.
