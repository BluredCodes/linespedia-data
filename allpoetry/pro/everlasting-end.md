as the moon eclipses the sun,
in the darkness im filled with fun;
i stare at the ecplised suns rays,
i begin to go back and remember the days;
when life used to be all good and great,
but now my life i really hate;
i think about the things i used to care for,
thinking and thinking, i can nolonger bear anymore;
the moon moves making the sun reappear,
i stand as i watch in fear;
eternal darkness will be coming for me,
i think about this and laugh with glee;
i nolonger have my life to defend,
here comes the everlasting end.
