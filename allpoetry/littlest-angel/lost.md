WRITTEN 2/09/02


My child was lost at 6 years old
No more for me to have
No more for me to hold

Never to watch grow up
Never to be there when she needs me 
Never to hear her laugh

I saw her one last time to say goodbye
Promised the world
And received nothing in return

Got told we would take her
She will know who you are
You can write and call
And got nothing at all

The gifts I did give
She had to hide
Don't let anyone know

Grow up little one your mother 
Is dead to you

The heartach 
The hurt oh let it go away
Was very real and true

She suffered 
I suffered 
All over a man
