written  2/04/02


THEY CAN ONLY TAKE SO MUCH
THEY GIVE TO YOU ALL THEY HAVE
BUT IF YOU CHOOSE TO THROW IT AWAY 
THAT IS ON YOU

DON'T PUSH A GUILT TRIP THEIR WAY
DON'T MAKE THEM FEEL THAT THEY ARE THE ONLY ONE THAT 
CAN HELP YOU STAY
THAT IS ON YOU

YOU HAVE A MIND
YOU HAVE A LIFE
IF YOU CHOOSE TO KILL YOURSELF
THEN DO IT

BUT DON'T MAKE A SISTER,FRIEND,
FEEL THAT SHE IS THE ONLY ONE THAT CAN HELP
NO ONE BUT YOU CAN LIFT YOU UP
NO ONE BUT YOU CAN TEAR YOU DOWN

YOU CLAIM TO BE A SISTER, FRIEND 
BUT YOU DRAG HER DOWN
THAT IS NO WAY TO TREAT A SISTER,FRIEND

CUT EVERYONE OUT
BE MY GUEST
THEN TAKE YOUR OWN GUILT WHERE IT BELONGS
NO ONE DESERVES THE GUILT THAT YOU ARE 
GOING TO GIVE THEM
ESPECIALLY NOT A SISTER,FRIEND
