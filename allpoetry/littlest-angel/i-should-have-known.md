Two years ago on Valentines Day I gave my mom a pig
A cute little pig
She loved pigs

Then that saturday she came over 
Doing better I thought
Walking better
Getting up from a chair better
She looked good

Thursday I should have known that something wasn't right
7:10 am driving my bus.
My chest hurt
Having problems breathing
My arm hurt
What is going on

Called on the radio I can't drive
Someone came and got me
Took me back to the lot
Told don't come back until you see a doctor

Went home called the doctor
Got an appointment for 10 am
Got there early
But funny thing is the computers went down
Waited in the little room
It seemed like forever
Finially the doctor came in at 10 am

Went back to work.
Could have gone home
Could have relaxed for awhile
Could have done anything but gone back to work

Went back to work
Everything is fine
Pager hadn't gone off all day
Nothing to worry about

My little one there
Myself no one else 
Fifteen calles on the caller ID
From my dad's house. How odd
An emergancy surely he would have paged me I thought

Called the number
My brother answered
Come to dad's house now
He repeatedly said
Have to wait for husband I said
Why, What's wrong? What's going on?

Then he said mom died this morning
I should have known

The death certificate says she died at 10 am
I know she died at 7:10 am
I should have known
