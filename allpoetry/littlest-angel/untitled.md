written 2/4/02


Someone who thinks mor a a man than she does her own child.
She sees him a little. But he still knows her as mother.
No one can ever replace that for a child
But now she doesn't care. 
All she cares about is the man.

She has no respect for anyone and how they feel.
Weather or not anyone else cares what happens to her.
All she cares about is that the man left.
She is wollowing in the pain and the hurt.
All she cares about is the man.

No matter how she feels or how she hurts she needs to grow up.
Everyone has lost. 
People have lost more than she will ever know.
All over a man

She thinks she is being cute.
By all the pitty party's she is playing.
Planning on making someone else feel bad
If they can not help her come out of this.
All over a man.

Don't make others feel guilty.
Because you want to do what you want.
DO IT!!!  DON'T PLAY!!!!
Don't put someone else in the fire.
Because all you care about is you.
All over a man
