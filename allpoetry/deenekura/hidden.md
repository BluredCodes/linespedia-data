The simple things can be worth while,A joyous laugh, a loving smile.We take these things as if they were free,But some have secrets that others can’t see.Behind that laugh, a troubled heart,A tortured soul that’s torn apart.Behind that smile, emotional distress,A pleading pray for final rest.Some have pain that destroy their life,No relief from hardship, a continuous strife.The world is full of actors that could win a gold prize,But keeping in such emotions will be the start to their demise.So before you think a smile is pure, think of what may be,Think of what may be hidden, what you cannot see.Dedicated to a friend who doesn't always tell me what's wrong.
