From afar...You looked so gracefulYour shining brilliance brightened my darknessWithout touching, I could feel your purityEach breath from your lips was a blessing...Yet...With each closer step was a changeYour light seemed to fade a bitYour purity seemed taintedBlessings were turned into curses...What happened to my angelWas I always this blindCould looks have deceived meWhere did you go...I never got to touch your skinYou disappeared from my sightI thought I loved you...but...I don't understand what happenedFrom afar...You looked like perfectionA dream that I could reach towardBut as I looked at you deeperYour beauty dissipated...la la la...I hate guys, yes yes I do
