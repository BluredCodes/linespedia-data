title: "Masquerade of lies"

A never ending circle, a never ending disguise
This forever lasting carrousel, this masquerade of lies
A cycle of deception, an entire world of deceit
A masquerade of lies that will lead to our defeat
The murder of an innocent, the constant cry of pain
The never ending masquerade with absolutely no gain
Our horror show of invention, our breeding of illusion
The tolerance of trickery, the constant flow of confusion 
A masquerade of hidden secrets deep inside the heart
A tear between the truth and lies, a smaller gap apart
The two collide as one concealing the honest division 
All boundaries of the truth and lie, end with that collision 
We live this masquerade of lies, without the torment of guilt
Our society thrives upon these lies that we ourselves have built.Ah...The world today?
