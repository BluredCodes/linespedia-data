Shaken by the sudden changes, but caught in the brilliance of it’s light
Abashed by how everything looks, but in fear to look from the sight
It’s hard to understand why things in life shift
But you can’t be paralyzed forever because of the simple driftChange happens everywhere, don't let it get to you.
