The silence stretches out forever
In your heart, you'll hold me never
With your eyes you'll see me not
I am not the one you've sought
Details lost in sands of time
Like a window of broken rhyme
Dead and cold without a sound
Buried deep within the ground
Lost without a single trace
Fallen far from purest grace
Bleeding from a broken heart
Torn because we're far apart
Damned to walk alone in life
Abandoned to my lonely strife
Forgotten for an ancient error
Left behind in forlorn terror
Wishing hoping for a love
With no answer from above
Discounted pain within the soul
Losing sight of a single goal
Ignoring life with full completeness
Filled up with absolute bleakness.Errrr.........?
