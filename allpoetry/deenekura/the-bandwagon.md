Everybody sees me sitting, everybody staresEverybody sees me crying, but nobody caresAll the world can see my pain but none of them step nearEach of them just stay and watch, and keep away in fearAlone each day I face the stage, all alone I cryAlone each day I walk along, and all alone I'll dieAll the world laughs at me every time I fallEach of them just point and grin anytime I callEverybody sees me cower, and they all attackEverybody sees me reach, and they push me back...For all of those who don't follow the crowd
