As I lay on the floor, my blood running clear
I reach out my hand, and suddenly you appear
Your untouched face, your unloving eyes
That gentle smile that I so much despise
You deepen my wound, my blood soaks the floor
All you did was caress my flesh, yet it tore
Rotting inside, with illusions of hate
Killing my dreams till everything's much too late
Love like a blade, cutting me deep
Stealing my soul, all the benefits you reap
Lies your dagger, stabbed into my eyes
Your room filling up with my screams and my criesErrr...I just wrote this spur of the moment at like, 4am.  No, it has no significant meaning or anything, it's just a poem.
