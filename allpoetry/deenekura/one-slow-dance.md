As our hands touch, my heart skips a beat
Our surpressed passions begin to surface
I cannot stop my loving looks towards you
Please don't stop
We dance under the warmth of the covers
In eachothers arms, I see the world for the first time
Your heart beats in sync with my own
Please don't stop
Our souls become one as our bodies entwine
Hand in hand we will face the world
You speak lovely whispers into my ears
Please don't stop
"You and me dancing the night away"
I let you take me to the ends of the world
"You can my heart beating so hard"
