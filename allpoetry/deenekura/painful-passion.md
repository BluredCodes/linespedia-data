A single slap
That's all it will take
A single fist
It will carry all your hate
Dug into my flesh
I can see it in your eyes
Struck across my skin
It reflects all your despise
A single slap
I can feel it in my soul
A single fist
Pushing me back into my pathetic role
Dug into my flesh
You can't hide my pain
Struck across my skin
My blood falls to the floor like rain
A single slap
Everything is over in an instant...no comment
