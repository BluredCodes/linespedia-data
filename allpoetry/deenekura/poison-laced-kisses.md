title: "Poison Laced Kisses"

Confusion circles it's way deep into my heartLetting out cries of horror as it consumes meNever-ending agony tearing me apartReality slipping away like water in my handInsecurity passing through my skinUnhealing gashes bleed till I can't standFalling into an abyss of nightmaresMy senses filled with your bestowed miseryAlone with the path of your bloody dreamsMy broken voice, only your historyAshes of life poured from your golden glassBloody memories framed on your wallOld horror stories flashed with your passOur shattered pieces cutting my soulArgh...Some people SUCK
