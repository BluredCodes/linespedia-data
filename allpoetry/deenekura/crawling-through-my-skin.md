Emptiness...That is what I feel when you are not nearLoneliness...That's what consumes my heart when you're not hereHeartbroken...That is what happens when I cannot touch your faceShattered...That's what becomes of me when you name me a disgracePain...That is what hits me when you say goodbyeAnguish...That's what fills me as I strive not to cryTorment...That is what my soul feels as I walk aloneMisery...That's what my mind feasts on as I steps toward the unknown...it's dumb!!!!!! -_-
