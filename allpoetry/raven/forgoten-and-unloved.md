as i lye alone in my grave forgoten and unloved
in my cage is where i am now
waiting for what is to be
in the distent sunset sets a seting memory
dont care dont fear do not fear me
for i am alone in my cage in my lost memorythis is a quick example of how it feels to be in a crouded place and feel so forgoten at least thats what i think i hope this will speak to u because i can only wright something like this when im really really sad so its a rare thing

-Raven
