it was the second day of high school for that year
it was grade nine and i was there
but when she walked in late that day
i thought i was traped away
entranced by the way she walked
i was unable to talk
it was imposible to see
what would come of her and me
but it happaned what can i say 
she broke my heart the very next day
who would have thought she was so crule
to cheat and to lie and tell rumors at school
all i ever did was treat her right
and all i wanted to do was hold her tight
that day under the tree ment nothing i guess
when me and you held each other and kissed
where did the love go....she didnt see?
its all because she cheated on me...this is a story abought a girl who i thought loved but turned out to have a secret life of danceing naked for guys and haveing 12 dozen or so boyfriends all i did was bow to her because i thought she was a girl who i could actualy marry and she just didnt want that only latter i found out abought it all and then the realationship ended please send me your comments and rateings everyones count :)
-Raven
