---
title: "Pain's Ante"
writer: "ktzulu"

Sitting forever comatose
Neurologically raped by televized pollution
Mother Nature has cut my umbilical cord
Relinquished my bond of peace
Denied existence in the humble society
Only the slef-indulgent hermit
Forgets about his crumbling walls
As great cities digress into oversized cemeteries
Nations become spectators, on the stage
Thier siblings providing the entertainment
As their peoples hurl stones back and forth
No longer wanting to aid in humanity
Rather gamble on the final score
We relish the thought of some others pain
When sitting several borders away
