---
title: "Rebirth"
writer: "ktzulu"
slug: "rebirth"
source: "AllPoetry"

From the well of hope,
Vibrant colors flow
As the water dazzles
And glitters sheepishly
Into the fading sky,
As bars of music whistle their way
Free from the ground upon I stroll
The trees, dancing carefree
And the sky, An amazing shade
Of purest purple
Slowly life goes by,
Grasping at reality
The hope well begins to crumble
From the cracks flows not Nature's lifeblood
But polluted mire gels forth,
As the ground's only trembling
Is it's soul-searing gasping of breath
The trees no longer hold their mother intact
Having been axed into extinction
Our sky, no longer timid and beautiful,
Filled with cumulus anger, seething forth hatred
Raining down Nature's,
Shards of liquid pain
Bellowing forth anguish
As the rebirth begins
Impotent minds,
Returned to her womb
Forever forgotten
In her ashen abdomen
