In the night of darkness, When hell-hounds yell
The demons be shrill and the heart races like hell
Are you scared of what beholds 
When headstones bare your name, Does the blood still flow
In the hours of decease, Alone in the time of evil
To stand strong or fall headlong
All eternity stands to watch as limbo runs downstream
Faith struggles within
One for all, none for you One, Corrupt religious leech
The crucifix feeding frenzy, Stains replaced by rot
Pedophilic prayers dream priests, Satan in a suit
Cross-bred firechildren, Sacrifice ruining sweet virginity
Chaos visits for coffee, Anarchy is fastest anesthetic
All being amalgamated, Swept into the inferno
Losing touch with all
Only thing left being...
