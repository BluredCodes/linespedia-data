Alone to carve my prison
Even my thoughts have deserted me
Evicted early from the womb
And thrown knowing towards the tomb
A peasant in the court of noblemen
Kicked in the teeth
Till my soul bled forth
Scrape me and rape me
This world will never break me
Dancing with death since birth
Even he won't take me
Pain is my only pleasure
All that I've known
Walking on the waters of emotion 
Should I fall and feel
I think I'll drown
Estranged from guidance's eyes
I have lost my wayOct. 2001,
