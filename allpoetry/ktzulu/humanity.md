title: "Humanity"
writer: "ktzulu"

A minute iota in an infinite cosmos
We so foolishly believe in our superiority
Basking in our own ludicrous ignorance
Arrogantly we lay claim to the celestial skies
While still skirmishing over tracts
Of the earthly homeland
Humanity is collapsing as society decays
Power-mongers bark out commands to pawns
While lounging in the pearl iridescence of ivory houses
Insanity has run amok in the race for technical knowledge
In an era of steel birds and titanium sharks,
Only the innocent suffer
With a martyr's blood seeping into your facial pores,
Where do you hide from the truth?
With political assassins elected to the throne,
Who will you trust?
Countries committing genocide while accusing others,
Where do you live?
And when all partake in the atrocious acts,
Who will judge?
