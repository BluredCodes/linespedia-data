Languishing in the nether void
Faceless in the utopia of million-dollar mugshots
Infested rats in concrete mazes sniffing out Prozac
Puppeteers yanking strings of golden illusions
Diseased dystopia the lifestyle of high sobriety
Milling out of lavish palaces to homes of cardboard walls
Chase your dreams of heroin induced fantasy
Humility is the first step in recovering from stupidity
Useless wisdom is the cornerstone of insanity, and
Corporate bureaucracy corrupts judicial foundations
