---
title: "Trapped"
writer: "ktzulu"

Constantly led through the maze
Everywhere leading... nowhere
Fighting for sweet release
Hallowed passages running backwards
Lost in anxiety, dusk is swallowing all
Hallucinatory illuminations frighten the stoic
Electricity thunders down, and
Rain trickles like a salted wound
Eternal vastness encompassing mass confusion
Spirits whisper tales of wisdom
When the smoke floats gracefully through
Narcotic philosophy edges out narcissistic indulgence
All are lost in the trap of life
