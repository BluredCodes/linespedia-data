---
title: "Placidity"
writer: "ktzulu"
slug: "placidity"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Cannon sounding
Shells screaming
Soil flying
Soldiers exploding
Blood spurting

Winds blow
Grass sways
Calm,
Quiet,
Gone

Fear,
Terror,
Pain,
Blood

Sunshine,
Fields,
Placidity,
Graves

Soldiers scared,
Rifles shrieking,
Cartridges piercing

Poppies,
Horizons,
Tranquility

Cries and stains,
Haunting

Peace
