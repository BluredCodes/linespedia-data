---
title: "Wisdom's Curse"
writer: "ktzulu"

When we pass, do we go beyond
Or are we stuck forever here
Even when names are forgotten
Deeds will remain in story for others to tell
When none care any longer,
We will ride the winds of the reaper
When one helps one,
We become invincible and miracles will amaze us
A wise man said that evil will come to pass
When no one stops evil, will it pass
When men no longer fear shells of lead
Mankind will cease to exist
Pillaging and raping are common in streets of fire
Cannons break the silence of graveyards
When we're all lost, is there more to come
Of all the indecencies, morality hurts the most
When equality is bequethed upon all
Then we shall be free
