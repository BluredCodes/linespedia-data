Captivated by the iridescent aura
Translucent placidity shimmering uncorrupted
Demon spells delicately caress the water ripples of fate
Voodoo shamans chanting quantum witchcraft
The ivory stairwell to heaven is more tedious,
Then the flaming rollercoaster to oblivion
Manipulatory spirits guide us in hidden arts
When the frigid winter air is pierced
With the shattering shrieks of midnight creatures
All are silenced in the path of darkness
With the crimson light fading to a glimmer
And horizons steal away behind twilight
All are pinched in prejudice's vice
