---
title: "Speechless"
writer: "ktzulu"
slug: "speechless"
source: "AllPoetry"

Scurrying abot in squalor
You see the rats of pestilence
Yet we are poverty's peasants
Not your rodents of disease
We provide sustenance, not demise
Humanity's cobblestones we have become
Trampled into oblivious mediocrity
The masses of mass indistinction
High society's elitists have stolen our voice
