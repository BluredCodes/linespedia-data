---
title: "Daemon Childe"
writer: "ktzulu"
slug: "daemon-childe"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/32637-Daemon-Childe-by-ktzulu"
---

Bred and borne of scorned fury and fire
With the nobility of a peasant for thy sire
Thrust into thou's world of blazing turmoil
And to the Christ tempted to foil
The child cloaked in elegance
Misguiding thee naive worshippers of reverance
Lambasting the prophesized fortunes of days long old
Laying oppurtunistic claims of deceits therein told
Befriended of pagans, Thy was raised of the blade
As mere papal entrance was fiercely forbade
Working the rituals of ancestors forever since past
Thy was branded as daemon and swiftly lashed
Though thier accusing fears didn't ring far from true
For the crimson of devil was thy's inner hue
Laying waste to the experiences of the word
And teaching thou, thy messiac vision blurred
From forth the seed of daemon thy sprung
And from atop thou's throne in heaven thy wishes thee flung
Thy spot in the fires thoroughly assured
With ravenous incantations of the absurd
Behold the child of the blackened flame
Educated that thou's Christ and daemon are but the same
Hurling spears of blaspemous insult
With intentions to lace old wounds with salt
Forgive thy not, Thy knowest what thy do
Thou's grail in the mire was thy that threw
With blood on thy hands of the famous son
Of daemon child, Thy am one
