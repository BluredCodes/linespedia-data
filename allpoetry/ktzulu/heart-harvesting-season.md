title: "Heart Harvesting Season"

Crying out in livid anguish
You have broken my spirit
Paralyzed my heart
And decapitated my soul
Amputated our life of two
Before it blossomed into one
The lacerated exterior barely conceals
The shattered emotional interior
My spiritual tree has grown
And was thoughtlessly harvested
Before reaching the sky
The storm of Apocalypse
Rendered me to the soil
With on one around
I'm petrified to crawl on my own
Only to be struck down again
