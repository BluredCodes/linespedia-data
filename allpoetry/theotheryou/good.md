title: "Good"

I walk as the steam rises from the sidewalk
The sun burns through the black around me
But I don’t even feel it
My mind is distracted
My eyes are fixated

And I can’t help but think that one day we’ll be good again
My thoughts drift and I forget how to breathe without you
Teach me to exhale

I talk to your photograph and suffocate
I try to swallow reality
But I just keep on choking
My soul is in pieces
But my heart won’t back down

And I can’t help but think that one day we’ll be good again
My thoughts drift and I forget how to breathe without you
Teach me to exhale

One day we’ll be good again
Someday I will remember
Everything I’ve forgotten
And I will be forced to learn

And I can’t help but think that one day we’ll be good again
My thoughts drift and I forget how to breathe without you
Teach me to exhale
