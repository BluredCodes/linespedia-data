For My New Old Flame 

Let’s find our way back through the years
To when love was something more than smoke and mirrors
And let’s go back to a time when you and I
Suddenly felt love deserved a try
Circumstances, however, prevailed
And my bleeding heart was left unveiled
And in all that time my feelings wouldn’t fade
There was nowhere to hide no shelter, no shade
Around every corner there was only despair
I longed for when I was sharing your air
Recently, though, I’ve felt like I did
Those years ago when we were just kids
And it seems like we both have come full circle
And our hearts may be bruised and colored purple
But if you promise me that you’ll still be my friend
Even if this love again has to end
Then I promise you that I’ll do what I can
To make my love reach across desert sand
And if I have to I’ll get down and crawl
Across every mile and fight through it all
Just to make us last as long as we should
And so we can stand where we once stood
Way up above everyone on the ground
Eyes closed and lips locked, we’re not looking down
I say we let us be us again
As the words ‘be mine’ flow through my penmy cheesy valentine's day poem
