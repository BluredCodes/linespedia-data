I've thought about it all and come to a decision
I'm just gonna let you suffer and i'll never interfere
Cuz that's just who i am all cold and heartless
And if i'm only selfish then why am i so afraid?

It's almost beautiful the way the classic story plays out
I see through all the smoke and skin and comprehension
I'm bleeding again, give me a tourniquet to cut it off
Home is lost forever now that pity has been destroyed

The fading colors of yesterday still fresh in my mind
I bite and teeth only to destroy all i've created
Manifesting and festering all this evil within me
Eyes are gone before you know you're blind but you are

It's all just symbolic the way you sell yourself
This conquering device couldn't unclog your mind
Unattainable due to my lack of structure and brilliance
You design my divine eyes, we're just cold and heartless

It's prolific that you're gifted and i don't deliver
All i've sacrificed will never relate to you
You've delayed the fact that you’re slowly dying
It’s a vague description of the cold and heartless soul i ownnot even i know what this is about...and i wrote it!
