There is a thing that i enjoy
  you might know it as food
   i love all kinds i really do
hopefully it wont make me blue
sometimes i eat too much 
and my tummy is all in a bunch
i like to eat it with my friend danielle
eventhough we sometimes swell
wow this is fun
i hope you like my pun
hahaha i like food it is great
and now i'm done
