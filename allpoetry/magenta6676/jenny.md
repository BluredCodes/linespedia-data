there is a girl with blonde hair and blue eyes
she is so cool and she even has a pool!
this girl is ditzy
but we love her anyway
she has a best friend- her name is danielle
when there together they alwayz pwell
shes really ditzy 
shes very pitzy
but shes especially nitzy!
