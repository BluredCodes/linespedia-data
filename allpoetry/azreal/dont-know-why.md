---
title: "dont know why"
writer: "Azreal"
slug: "dont-know-why"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/2959-dont-know-why-by-Azreal"
---

when i hate myself,its not me who cries
 when i stab my arm,its not me who bleeds

its my soul......
and i dont know why
but it cant stop crying 
and neither can i
and i dont know why
but it cant stop screaming
that it just wants to die

and it begs me to do it
and it begs me to see
that if i cut my life short
ill finally be free
and it begs me to do it
and it begs me to see
to cut my fuckin hand off 
and watch my arm bleed
and it begs me to do it
and it begs me to see
that only by death
can i ever be happy
and it begs me to do it
and it begs me to see
but i cant
cuz i dont have it in me
and it hates me for that
and i dont know why
