My Special PurposeI'm a tiny, falling raindropthat glistens and shineslike a multi colored prismin the sun's bright glare.I am only one among many.You may think I'm not important.My time here on the earth is short,but I've been given a special purpose.I give the needed moisture to these vivid green leaves,as I drip from branch to branch.Without me, there would be no life.Soak up my precipitation,draw your existence from me,for this is what I was created for. When the sun's warmth beats again,my life here will be no more.But when the crusted earth grows parched,you'll find me out on another day,faithfully fullfilling my mission.©TinasHeart 10/28/01
