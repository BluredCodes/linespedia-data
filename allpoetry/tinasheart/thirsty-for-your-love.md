Thirsty For Your Love

I am dry like the desert,
thirsting for the moisture
of your love to fall on me,
like the dew, so sweet and pure.

Satisfy me as only you can do.
Come, rain down on this sand,
let your love shower over me,
flood this dry and thirsty land.

When your body's close to me,
swaying in rythym with mine,
your love saves my soul
for you are my lifeline.

Your love heals this parched soil,
the water I need to survive,
I drink deeply from this source
and my heart is revived.

It's been so long, come to me now,
fill the longing in my soul.
Fall down upon me in big drops,
I'll take my fill, 'till I am whole.
