Love Has Found A Home

Your love has found a warm home
you'll never again have to roam.
So stay right here by my side.
In my heart let your love reside.
For Baby, I solemnly promise you,
my love for you will always be true.
I'll make your home a cherished place,
that will always be filled with grace.

Forget what your life was like before,
for you will never be lonely anymore.
In me you'll see a fire burning bright,
to keep your coals glowing in the night.
I will fulfill your every wanton desire.
Come with me now, I'll take you higher.
No need to travel down a lonesome road,
for now your love has found an abode.
