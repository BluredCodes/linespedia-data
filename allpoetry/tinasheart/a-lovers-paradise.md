A Lovers Paradise

We walk the sandy beaches
of this enchanted island.
Nothing is more beautiful
on God's fascinating earth
than this Heavenly lagoon.

Sand seeps through my toes.
Gusty sea breezes whisper,
blowing through my tresses.
The ocean intensely bellows,
as waves crash against banks.

Admirably magical, this place.
Here, our love is intensified.
As you embrace me close to you,
gazing deep in your brown eyes,
I know assuredly you feel it to.

Wildly my heart begins to pulse.
Feel the rhythm of my heart beat,
as you passionatly kiss my lips.
Our yearning never more intense
than now on this tropical Paradise.
