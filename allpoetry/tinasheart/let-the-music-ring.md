---
title: "Let The Music Ring"
writer: "TinasHeart"
slug: "let-the-music-ring"
source: "AllPoetry"

Let lively music emphatically ring,
as joyful praises to you we bring.
Let it sweetly fill the whole earth,
singing of our celebrated new birth.
Unspoiled creatures we've now become.

So let us sing, we desire to shout,
telling the world what you're about.
Your unfailing compassion is flowing.
Souls are yielding, faces are glowing,
shining in the splendor of your glory.

We will fearlessly dance in the streets,
lifting holy hands, we'll keep the beat.
We'll tear down walls, destroy strongholds.
Your name will be declared, make us bold.
Nothing can stop us from joyfuly singing.
