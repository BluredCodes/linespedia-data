Sweet Surrender

Lord, I give you all, my life, my heart,
my soul, my hopes, and dreams.
All, I lay down at Your feet.
You can have them all,
for they were never
mine to keep.
All I ask
in return
is for Your
peace.

I give You my marriage 
with every fairytale
or illusion.
With or without him,
I choose to live,
for I know Whom
I live for.
I trust You
to see me 
through.

If You should take everything from me,
every gift You've ever given,
I'd still have this peace
to comfort me.
I do not ask You
to change the 
circumstances.
All I ask is that
You change my
heart.

Give me peace
in the midst
of
the
storm
and
I 
will 
go 
on!

TinasHeart 02/11/02
