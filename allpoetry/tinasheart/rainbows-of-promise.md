Rainbows Of Promise

Looking back over my life's incidents,
there's been both good and bad times.
Days I would not ever want to relive.
Obstacles that were so painfully hard,
I didn't think I would find the strength
I needed to endure even one more day.

Now I know that You've always been with me,
even before I knew You or Your loving ways.
You breathed life into me and gave me power.
You helped me brave the perplexing storms.
Together we conquered each raging onslaught.
Lord, without You in my life, I am nothing.

With each storm comes great blessings.
Each day You're teaching me to be like You.
Even when I am not faithful, You remain true.
After the storm, there is the bright rainbow.
The sun comes shining through the dark clouds.
Once again I see the promise for better days.
