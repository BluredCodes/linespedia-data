The Keeper Of The Stars

There is a God in Heaven
watching over you and me.
When your days seem dark,
feels you haven't a plea,
Remember He cares for you.

I know my friend the world
can be so cold and cruel.                       
At times we wonder where                     
are the sparkling jewels,
that God has promised us.

When you can not lift up
your troubled head to Him,
look around, I'll be near.
I'll help you find the gems
that glisten like no other.

You are never forsaken.
He has not forgotten you.
He truly knows what's best.
We must continue to persue,
to know His perfect ways.

For His ways are not ours.
His plans are not our plans.
All your hopes and dreams
are forever in His hands,
where He holds your future.

Do not look for false pearls,
made by the hands of evil man.
For God has something better.
The best for you, is His plan.
For you are special in His eyes.

Your shining moment will come.
It just takes time and effort.
Keep inscribing hope upon hearts.
for when others only fall short,
Your words bring light and hope.

He has given to you His voice,
words of wisdom and healing
flowing forth from your pen, 
not bouncing off the ceiling,
but affecting many broken souls.


Just remember this, my dear brother.
Though the stars in your skies may
not enlighten everyone on the earth,
in God's eyes and mine, you're a ray
of light that touches many hearts.

So shine on!!
