---
title: "My Friend (for Poetic Fanatic)"

My friend, you are always there,
showing to me how much you care.

We've been together for so long,
what we have could not be wrong.

No, this is a gift from up above.
Just one way God shows His love.

As the days pass, closer we become
To me you have become such a chum.

You are there chasing the blues away,
with a kind word and a smile each day.

Isn't it funny how we seem to just know
what the other thinks as the more we grow.

We share a common bond so precious and rare,
I think as friends, we are quiet the pair.

When I say a prayer of thanks, you come to mind,
for to me dear friend, you're the perfect find.
