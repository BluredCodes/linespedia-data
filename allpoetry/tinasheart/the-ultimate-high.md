It can be sensual, but not any sexual content. The Ultimate HighYou lift me higher than I've ever gone,higher and higher 'till I reach the peak.Together we stand on this mountain top.We've felt all the love one could seek.On this beautiful day we exhausted all,just to get to this elevated plateau.Now we can only lie here remembering all the magical events as we gaze below.We endlessly swam the ocean's depthsas waves of pleasure washed over us.Surging tides of joy did not stop us,we swam on untill we reached success.You set me high upon an eminent ridge,then continued to ascend 'till you cameto the highest point of accomplishment.Together we erupt into the ultimate aim.©TinasHeart 10/25/01
