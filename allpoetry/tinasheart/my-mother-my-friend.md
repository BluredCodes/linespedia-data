My Mother, My FriendAs I reflect back on the years,of memories of you I hold dear,I recall the tender waysyou displayed in our younger days.You always showed you cared,through the hardships we have beared.Making it all seem so fun,your resolve would not be undone.You taught us to be steadfast,that setbacks would not last,to never give up on our dreams,no matter how hard it might seem.Now I have children of my own.I'm staking out into the unknown,yet you're always there with advice,your enlightment so concise.Under your nuture I have grown.I know I am never alone,not with you as my friend,and your wisdom to extend.©TinasHeart5/13/01
