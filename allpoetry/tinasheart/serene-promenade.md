Serene PromenadeThere's a quiet promenade I like to gowhen my soul's weary with a heavy load.A splendid place where all is at peace.As I breathe the fragrance of blossoms,I feel my strives float away on soft winds.There's a shimmering brook that glistensas the sun caresses it's shining surface;like sparkling lights, the sunbeams gleam,as snowy white swans fairly glide in accord.It's a magical site for my eyes to behold.Vivid greens exquisitely array the borders.An ancient columed arch graces the walkway.Sweetly scented lilacs lavish the curvature.Climbing tea roses elegantly line the walls.Delicate aromas waft and penetrate my senses.Beauty encompasses me in this tranquil parka still calmness gently captures my essence.I lay my burdens down, surrendering them all.I am refreshed and filled with thankfulness,when I leave this wonderous place of serenity.©TinasHeart 10/29/01
