This goes out to a special friend. You know who you are! :):):)

Chasing The Clouds Away

When the world is bitterly cold,
seems you haven't got a friend,
I give you something you can hold,
my heart full of love I will extend.

My arms shall reach out to you,
give you love wrapped in a hug.
I can not stand to see you blue,
may my friendship keep you snug.

I give to you my caring heart,
hoping you see the love inside.
I'll always try to do my part,
to help you push your cares aside.

When you are feeling down and out,
keep in your heart a happy tune.
My friend, do not whine or pout,
for this storm shall pass soon.

I know the song within your soul.
When you can't hear it's melody,
find contentment to make you whole,
I'll sing the song you've sung to me.

This peaceful ballad I sing to you,
may it fill your heart with cheer.
A friendly song to see you through.
May it's timeless message you hear.

Sing aloud, chase those blues away,
if you seek, you'll always find grace.
That sadness is not here to stay,
So it's time to put on a happy face.
