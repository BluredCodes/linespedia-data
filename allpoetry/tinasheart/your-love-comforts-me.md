title: "Your Love Comforts Me"

Ever endearing to my heart,that look of love within your eyes.It chases my despair away,and sets my spirit soaring high.There is no place I'd rather be,than lying here in your embrace.To hear you whisper in my ear,expressions of your tender grace.Just to hear your soothing voice,to feel your arms enfolding me,conveys contentment to my soul,knowing you love me completely.©TinasHeart6/28/01
