Principe De Amor(Prince Of Love)

My prince of love enraptures me with his voice,
warmly whispering romantic words into my ear.
He utters seductively in his native tongue,
"tu eres esposa mia, la corona de mi vida.
Como las estrella brillan en de noche, asi brillas tu.
Ho mi amor, como enciendes las sensaciones de amor.
Tu amor me hace vivir en este mundo de maldad."
He hastens me to the edge of ecstasy.
I've fallen once again under his loving charms.

Prince Of Love

My prince of love enraptures me with his voice,
warmly whispering romantic words into my ear.
He utters seductively in his native tongue,
"You are my wife, the crown of my life.
Like the stars shine at night, so you shine for me.
Oh my love, how you start the sensations of love.
In this cruel world, I live for your love."
He hastens me to the edge of ecstasy.
I've fallen once again under his loving charms.
