Whispered Secret

Years ago a word was whispered to my heart,
a message of love and hope filled my being.
My days of childhood, spent dreaming of you.
Grasping the promise of love that came to me,
faith, built upon a dream I would not let go of.

You came into my life, the past drifted away,
years of longing had passed and then I knew
I had never loved before, for you were the one
I had been waiting for my whole life through.
The gift of love, sent to us on Heavenly wings.

A message was sweetly whispered in the wind,
heard only by my heart, now filled with joy,
a word flew into my spirit from God up above,
my prayers had been heard, answered through you.
I knew you were the one I'd spent my life with.

Two souls that were destined to be together,
preordained before the hands of time began,
now joined in unity, God's plan now complete.
To this day, I still hear His word in my heart,
inbedded in my soul, a promise none can take.

Through years, we've passed through raging storms,
times we were barely able to keep our heads afloat,
but the strong love I hold for you never left me.
Storms continue to blow, yet our love is unshakable,
the foundation remains steady, built on higher ground.

In a world that's ever changing, one thing remains,
my love for you shall ever remain steadfast and true.
In my heart, a love that shall not ever be destroyed,
it is deeply rooted in my soul, planted there by God,
continuing to grow more complete throughout our years.

My heart keeps growing fuller with my love for you
trials will come, but our love will stand the test of time.
With Heaven's blessings in our midst, we'll stand strong!
Keep this promise in your heart, knowing my love won't end,
this is the secret that is whispered sweetly in the wind.
