Spring

spring has disembarked;
a renewal in my heart.

gentle breezes waft;
I breathe the freshness of Your love.

birds pleasantly warble;
my heart lifts in song to You.

emerald grasses germinate;
Your love takes root in my heart.

flowers gracefully blossom;
a new truth blooms in my heart.

the sun shines in a cerulean sky;
Your love penetrates darkness.

my eyes behold the beauty of nature;
once again I'm animated by Your grace.
