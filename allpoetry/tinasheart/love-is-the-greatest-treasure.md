The Greatest Treasure

Your love is the greatest treasure,
my most renowned pleasure,
generously bestowed by God above,
sent on the wings of a gentle dove.

He knew just what I needed.
When He made you He exceeded
all I could ever want for,
a gift of love that I adore.

I want you more than silver or gold.
Why can't your eyes behold?
I'd cast all earth's wealth into the sea,
just to have you here next to me.

I am wealthy beyond compare,
for you my love are so rare,
more exqusite than any gem.
It is from you my joy stems.

Many may desire to have gold.
I desire your love to have and to hold.
Some may grasp riches in their hands.
We have love that will forever stand.

One day all things will pass away,
gone tomorrow, here today.
Of all the things I could attain,
love is the only one that will remain.

For love is the greatest treasure.
