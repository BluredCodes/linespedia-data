Hunger

Like the cry of the howling wolf,
there is a cry deep in my soul,
a longing that only you can fill.
Come and satisfy this gaping hole.

Take me on an intimate journey,
to the essence of your heart.
To be lovingly familiar with you,
we can now make a fresh start.

Tell me can you see my longing?
the love I have inside my being,
daily it haunts and torments me.
This ravenous hunger is unfleeing.

There's no sacrifice I wouldn't make,
to have your heart enfolding mine,
to be placed in the highest postion
of your esteem would make my eyes shine.

The relationship we have is wonderful
but let us always continue to stride,
untill our love is all that it can be,
with less, let us never be satisfied.

This is the hunger deep in my soul,
that cries persistently for release.
Hear my wailing deep in the night,
that the love we share will not cease.
