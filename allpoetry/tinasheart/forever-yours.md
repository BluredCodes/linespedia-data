Forever Yours

All my life, my heart, my soul,
I give all only to you, my dear.
Untill the sun no longer shines,
untill all the stars disappear,
that's how long I'll be yours.

Untill time stands still,
I will hold you close to me.
'Till I take my last breath,
you'll know this guarantee,
untill then, I'll be yours.

For you are everything to me,
my sole reason to go on living.
You've loved me like no one could,
through years, you keep on giving,
that is why I'll always be yours.

If ever you should go away,
the sun would no longer shine,
the stars would no longer gleam,
from living, I'd surely decline,
forevermore, I want to be yours.

If you should walk out that door,
it would break my heart in two.
There is nothing I would not do,
just to see your love rising anew,
for you know, I'll always be yours.

Let's rekindle our love once more.
We both know, a love like ours is rare.
From the start, we were destined to be,
my love, with only you I'll share.
I am faithfully yours, forevermore.
