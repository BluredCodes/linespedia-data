Never cease to dream,
never give up hope,
Heaven's glory will stream,
and all your dreams will come true.

****************************************

Dreams Do Come True

Once I wished upon a star.
I petioned God above.
I envisioned your face from afar.

I knew you were out there,
somewhere, thoughtful of me,
hoping for love to share.

I imagined the day,
my prince would approach me,
with all your charming ways.

Substance better than a dream,
I've fallen into your arms,
where our love reigns supreme.

We'll be together always,
handsome prince and princess,
in love for all our days.

Dreams can come true,
I know this in my heart,
for I have found you.
