Oneness

The ocean air so fresh,
so invigorating and clean,
the intake of salty air
makes me feel so serene.

Here in the open air, with 
sea gulls flying freely,
I am at one with Nature,
feeling so alive and carefree.

I'm the waves that wash over you,
breezes blowing through your hair,
the sand beneath your feet.
This oneness with you I'll share.

Just to carress your body,
I'd be the wind whispering sensually,
the sand kissing your feet,
waves of love that bathe you tenderly.
