Like a winged angel,each time you touch me,my heart takes flight.Journeying to ecstasy,with each sensual caress,soaring faster and higher.Carried on wings of love,passion, my Heavenly attire.As we intimately unite,my soul extends to the skies.Embracing you closely,together we reach Paradise.©TinasHeart8/10/01
