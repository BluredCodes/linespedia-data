Joy Has ComeHis anger endures but a moment; in His favor is life: Weeping may endure for a night, but joy comes in the morning... He has turned my mourning into dancing. He has taken away my clothes of mourning and clothed me with joy. Psalms 30:5,11*********************************************Joy Has ComeThrough the darkness of night,thunder rolled resonantly in my mind.Infinite tears have been shed,Despondancy at it's height.You rescued me from the mire,sorrow transforms to joyful dancing,rags of mourning stripped away,arrayed in pleasing attire.Morning light splits duskiness,a new song settles in my heart,praises flow from my lips,for merriment has progressed.Permeating in the crisp breeze,lightly scented, perfume sweet,the fragrance of Your presence,that brings me to my knees.You adorn me with a crown of grace.As morning glories divinely unfold,the beauty within begins to bloom,loveliness reflects on my face....for joy has come!!©TinasHeart3/24/01
