What You Mean To Me

Have I told you today,
how special you are to me?
Did I reach out my arms
to hold you tenderly?

Did I send a smile to you,
tell you all you long to hear?
Did I listen to you intently
with a heart that was sincere?

Let me take this opportunity
to show you my heart of love
for special friends like you,
a gift God sent from up above.

I will take the time right now
to tell you how much you mean
to me with each day that passes,
you've been a friend on whom I lean.

A friendship like ours is rare,
something so beautiful and pure,
to be treasured untill the end.
Through time our bond remains secure.

I want you to know these things now,
for of tomorrow we hold no guarantee.
The grass withers and flowers fade
tomorrow may be whisked away suddenly.

If by chance we do not meet again,
at least on this side of eternity,
May you know without a doubt
how special you are to me.

I will stand in Heaven above
watching and waiting for you,
on billowy clouds white as snow,
'till at last you make it through.
