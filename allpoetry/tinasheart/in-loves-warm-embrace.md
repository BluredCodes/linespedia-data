In Love's Warm Embrace 

I walk with you hand in hand,
upon the golden sand.

See the waves crash against the banks,
for this beauty give God your thanks.

Smell the salty air,
feel the wind blowing through your hair.

I hear the ocean roar,
my love begins to soar.

I kiss your lips so sweet,
in the summer heat.

I look deep into your eyes,
underneath the painted skies.

Tonight as we sit by the fire,
our love is reaching higher.

Taking us to a place,
in love's warm embrace.

With you I want to go there,
where our souls we will bare.

We will become one,
and know our love has just begun.
