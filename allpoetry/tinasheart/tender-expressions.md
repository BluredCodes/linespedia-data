Tender ExpressionsJoyful adorations are ever flowing.May they caressingly reach your ears.I desire to be pleasing in your sight.May I bring unto You great enjoyment.I offer up my essence unto you, my King.May it be as a sweetly scented sacrifice.Touching, bringing you much pleasure.May my praises be as honey on your lips.That look of compassion on your face,your eyes shimmering with love for me,reassuring words spoken softly to me,tender expressions of eternal fondness.My heart is humbled in your presence.Your loving ways bring me to my knees.Rapturous tears stream down my cheeks.Lord, you are all I have longed for.©TinasHeart 10/02/01
