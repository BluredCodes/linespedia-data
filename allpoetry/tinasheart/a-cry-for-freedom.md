A Cry For Freedom

Many times when sleep claims my weary body,
I drift into a world of wondrous dreams.
Foes and loved ones may try to inflict pain,
yet I lift my arms and float above
where nothing can touch me.
I feel my spirit rise within me.
Freedom and peace flow through me.
I hear a voice from within, saying,
"nothing can harm you, my child".

Visions, peaceful and serene through the day,
of the mighty eagle resting upon a mountain top.
Victory has soared through his great wings.
He's free anytime to take flight,
yet chooses to remain in close fellowship
with the Creator of Heaven and earth,
high above all earthly struggles.

Lord, grant me wings that I may fly.
When pressures weigh heavy on my heart,
lift my spirit up high, taking me
peacefully into Your presence,
where nothing can cripple me.
Let Your being rise through me,
leaving behind all doubts and fears.
There I shall wait to hear Your Spirit, 
gentle as the whispering winds 
sweetly sweeping through my quills.
I will not succomb to be weak,
Strength, I've only imagined shall be mine.
Lord, it's Your power I will seek,
As I anticipate deliverance from you,
freedom shall ever be my cry.
