Love Eternallove that shines brightdeep into the night;in your heart abides;never leaves your side;brighter than any star;whether near of far;carried in your heart,so you are never apart;two hearts become one,never to be undone;forever soul mates,for this is fate;joined eternallyyou and me;my one true loveordained by God above.©TinasHeart4/19/01
