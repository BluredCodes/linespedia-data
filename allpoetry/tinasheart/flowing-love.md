Flowing Love

There is a river flowing,
happily shining with love.
Gentleness emerges forth.
It caresses your essence
and floods your substance.

Ever streaming to your side.
Let the waters wash over you,
cleanse you, make you whole.
Refreshment in weariness,
made to complete and help you.

Look deep into my tender heart.
Soft compassion can be found.
Can you see it glowing there?
Crafted uniquely just for you,
by the Father's loving hands.
