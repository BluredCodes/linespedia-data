Simply Magical

Lying in your comforting arms at night,
I drift into a peaceful realm of dreams.
A mystical glow of iredescense streams.
Taking your hand, we reach a regal height.

Vivid, enchanting colors our eyes behold,
as we float through the starry skies.
We can go anywhere our hearts surmise
and glimpse our fantasies as they unfold.

We shall capture a luminescent moon beam,
make a sensual wish upon a shooting star,
I will blow you a heavenly kiss from afar,
take our passion to the greatest extreme.

There is nothing for you I won't do tonight,
for yet again, you've charmed with your ways.
Each time you touch me, I'm in a magical daze.
Held captive under your spell, it feels so right...

For each time we make love, it's simply magical.
