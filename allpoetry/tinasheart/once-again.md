---
title: "Once Again"
writer: "TinasHeart"

You gazed into my eyes,
as you softly traced my lips.
I heard you huskily whisper,
'When I close my eyes, I see 
you surrounded by flowers and
looking lovely as an angel.'

You held me in intimate embrace.
My heart disclosed wings,
and encased ardently around you,
as our bodies and souls
interweaved together
in heavenly exultation.

As I close my eyes, I can
dreamily envision your face.
As the sweet scent of roses pervade,
thoughts of your sensual embrace linger.
So entranced am I by your love,
how deeply you affect me.

Enfold me in your strong arms.
Murmur loving words to me,
your angel encompassed by damask roses.
Satisfy me with sweet and tender kisses,
underneath the cerulean skies for I long 
to feel your gentle embrace once again.
