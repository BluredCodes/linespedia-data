This poem is written for my husband, "Principe De Amor"

Fire In My Soul

Fervor
within my soul
shining brightly for you.
Only you can light the candle
of the fiery passion that burns in me.
You keep my fire glowing bright, 
how could I ask for more
than your touch of
fervor.
