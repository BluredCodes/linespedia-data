Love's Purity

Your spirit runs through my veins,
pumping essential life to my soul.
You're the essence making me whole.
The intensity of our love is pure,
forever faithful, true and sure,
without blemish, spot or stains.

Our marriage bed remains undefiled.
Descending on wings of an unsoiled dove
destined and ordained by God above,
is the sweetness of our integrity.
White as virgin snow, glistening in purity.
When together we unite, I'm always beguiled.

Soaring high above, embraced in heavenly hue,
all the wonders that are new, we'll explore,
experiencing blissfulness, like never before,
restored, like roses in the silvery moon's mist.
Love's angelic melody always sung in our midst,
"There shall be none before you, none after you".

Our love, heated by passion's firey glow,
yet delicately intricate like fine lace,
wild and unleashed, tenderly filled with grace.
In marriage, there are no bounds that I can see.
I belong to you, my heart, I give you freely.
Eternally yours, all my love, I'll willingly bestow.
