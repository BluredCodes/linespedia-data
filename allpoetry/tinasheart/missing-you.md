Missing You

Thoughts of you cloud my vision.
I long for your touches on my skin.
To feel your arms enfolding me
would make my heart joyously spin.

The way you love me is purely magical.
To savor the sweetness of your kiss,
hear you whisper lovingly in my ear,
would be a taste of Heavenly bliss.

I want to lie within your embrace,
run my fingers through your hair,
murmur seductively in your ears
my heart and soul with you, I'll share.

How I want you now to be with me.
The hours seem to go by so slow,
Hurry back home to me, my dear. 
for you know I am missing you so.
