Mermaid's Song Of Love

When there's a full moon hanging low
in the skies, stars twinkling bright,
they say a beautiful, mystical mermaid
comes out in the still of a quiet night.

Resting on the rocks, lifting her voice
in a heartfelt cry of sorrow and desire.
From deep within her woeful soul a cry
rises in angelic voice, for love to acquire.

Gazing at the moon in great longing,
as if it holds an answer to her need.
Yearning to be held in love's embrace,
her song goes out in a vigorous plead.

All her nights are filled with lonliness.
If only she had a lover, she'd give him all.
She has so much love to give within her heart.
When she finds love, his heart she'll enthrall.

Her magical kingdom of enchantment lies
within the depths of the ocean's floor.
Of all of the kingdom, none can be found,
as a suitable partner for her to adore. 

So when the moon is full, listen for her cries,
that blend in with the roaring ocean's bellow.
Look closely, you may see her in the moonlight,
as she sings out for love in a voice so mellow.
