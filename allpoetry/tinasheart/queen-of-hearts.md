Queen Of Hearts

So, you think I am the queen of hearts?
Well, I will always do my part
to paint you vivid word dreams,
show you my heart, the love that streams.
For this I have found to be true....
that dreams can come true for you.

You're playing with the queen of hearts
Do you think that's really smart?
'Cause joking is the only thing 
that will do any good for you.

You know I have a lot of fun
the poets here are number one.
When someone writes about me,
an honor I consider it to be.
I figure we might as well smile,
for crying is not worthwhile.

You're playing with the queen of hearts
Do you think that's really smart?
'Cause joking is the only thing 
that will do any good for you.

I always enjoy a lot of good humor
some nuts prefer to spread rumors.
I just laugh them off, it's all in jest.
No used getting stressed.
I know the truth inside, that's what matters.
So batter up, it's just a bunch of fun chatter.

You're playing with the queen of hearts
Do you think that's really smart?
'Cause joking is the only thing 
that will do any good for you.
