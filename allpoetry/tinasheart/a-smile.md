Ok, here's a little bit of humor to brighten your day. :):) 


A smile

A smile
I give to you
to cheer and make you glad
A simple gesture of my heart.
When all is cold, let my smile warm your soul,
bring you comfort on weary days,
to chase away darkness.
I would stand on my head,
to see you with
a smile! :)
