Expressions of LoveLying here next to youin the mornings blush of dawnhearing the pulsing of your heart,our tender love's perpetually new;zealous like the surging sun,rising each new glorious day;When the sun begins it's descent,our love has only just begun.Burning intensely with each embrace.Kisses lovingly seared upon flesh,loving words uttered affectionatelyleaving a contented smile on my face,and intensifies the gleam in my eyes.Intimate moments shared with you,touching expressions of our love,constant and true, knowing no demise.©TinasHeart 9/22/01
