This poem is written for only one man. My husband, the love of my life.

Seduction

Gaze deeply into my turquoise eyes.
They are bottomless salty oceans
surging with infinite love for you.
Let soothing waters wash over you,
getting lost within my affection.
Swim below, see the hunger there,
a craving that only you can fill.

Oh how I long to mesmerize you 
with soft and gentle caresses,
reveal to you peaceful visions
of a magical, unihabited land
where the sun sets tranquilly.
The sound of my cries will echo 
between majestic purple palisades.

I'll entice you with moistened kisses,
send waves of pleasure down your spine
until you are putty in my hands.
Anticipating words I long to hear 
escaping from your seductive lips.
I'll revel in the warmth of your touch
as you entertain my every desire.

As our passions begin to mount,
gentleness will no longer exist,
except the soft coohs eluding my lips.
We'll feel the molten heat within,
fancy the mountains with fiery glow
rising higher with each fierce stroke,
Until in unison this volcano erupts.

Take my hand, Dear, I'll take you there.
