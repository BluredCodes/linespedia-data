Evanescence

A sadness surounds my heart today.
My life seems to be in disarray,
wondering if I can ever change, 
thoughts seem to be deranged.

Longing to make things right again,
for happiness and peace to remain,
instead of the pain I often feel
Lord, to me, secrets reveal.

I have made such a mess of my life,
at each turn, grief and strife
longing for Your sweet peace
on my life to be released.

These simple words of love You impart
whispered sweetly upon my heart,
nothing I can do to make You love me more
nothing I've done can make You close the door.

The windows of Heaven opening for me,
with Your love, flowing freely
falling down, sweeping into my essence,
sadness will become evanescence,

as I trust in You.
