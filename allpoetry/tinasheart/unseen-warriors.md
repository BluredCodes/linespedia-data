Unseen Warriors

God's heavenly angels are ever in flight,
bravely fighting battles left and right.
Intensly keeping watch over you and me,
Have some faith in what you can not see.

If only you knew the danger they spare,
when to God you lift up a little prayer.
You did not crash into that car today,
an angel intervened, pushing it away.

An angel is at the foot of a child's bed,
battling pressing trials that lie ahead,
ministering him safely through the night,
defending that child with all her might.

Drawing their sword, holding back forces,
keeping you in the light, their only course.
Receiving cuts and bruises meant for you,
'till safely, the trial you make it through.

Many times while in battle they're scarred,
from wounds recieved while keeping guard.
Fearless warriors are these winged beings,
in another realm, that we are not seeing.
