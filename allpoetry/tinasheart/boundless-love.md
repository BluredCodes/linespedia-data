Boundless Love

Each night as the sun sets on the horizon,
I will meet with you in our secret place,
our secluded garden where true love grows.
Here, my dreams come true in your embrace.
I know the sensation of being loved by you.
I find in you all I've longed for and more.
The love you share, something I never knew,
exceeding all that I have ever hoped for.

Just by the slight stroke of your sweet lips,
my heart overflows with rapture and delight.
When you hold me close and look into my eyes,
all my fathomless passions fiercely ignite.
I know the freedom of love without bounds.
It has been said that marriage is a prison.
Only in sacred union, endless love surrounds,
releases the soul, so love can be newly risen.

So enter in with me, my dear into this freedom.
With our love so true, we'll reach beyond the sky,
freely soaring high above all earthly constraints.
We'll build a castle of love extending way up high.
The shimmering stars shall illuminate our distance.
You truly are the man that I anticipated for,
you are my life, my breath, my very subsistence.
With you, I'll explore all the wonders love has in store.
