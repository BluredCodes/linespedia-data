River of Love

I was drowning in a sea,
lost in despair.
Your love rescued me.
My heart You began to repair,
as You entered into my soul.
Every pain You saw.
You set out to make me whole.
When my heart was shattered and torn,
when my body was weary and worn,
when my hope was all gone,
You gave me strength to carry on.
You always saw in me,
something I could not see.
You knew right from the start,
that deep inside my heart,
past the binding chains,
past the hurts and pains,
burried deep within
was a river of love,
just waiting to be set free,
to flow into the sea,
drowning out dispair.
My eyes were open as I began to see,
life is not so bad after all,
when into Love's arms we fall.
