Love Will Come To You

How many times have you wished upon a falling star,
hoping that your one true love would come from afar?
Have you stopped believing in magic and fairy tales?
Don't stop believing, you must not give up hope.
For love comes in many different forms.
Seek the love of family and special friends.
Discover the laughter seen in a child's smiling face.
Above all, seek to know the love that comes from above.

Your soul is wounded from another broken relationship,
seems you've cried a river flowing with unending tears.
Hold your head up high, you will find the rainbow.
Weep no more for what was never met to be.
You should not settle for less than the best.
Remember, good things come to those who wait.
Your special someone will come to you, on the wings of love.
When you least expect it is when love will come to you.
