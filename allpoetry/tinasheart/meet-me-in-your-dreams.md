Meet Me In Your Dreams

Tonight as you lay sleeping,
look for me in your dreams.
Meet me in our secret place,
where golden sunlight streams
through the enchanted forest.

I'll be there beneath our tree,
You know the tree I speak of,
for you carved our intials there,
near the wild roses I so love.
There, I'll wait in anticipation.

Together we are as one spirit,
shining the light of endearment.
I am with you always, my love,
even in sleep, true love's glint
shall not ever loose it's glow.

I will blanket you in warmness,
fervently kiss away your cares,
listen intently to your desires.
Tonight you will know no despairs,
for I will fullfill your eagerness.

Here, beneath the canopy of trees,
we will become one, body and soul.
The cares of this world shall float
mystically away as we're made whole,
tonight in our magical dream realm.
