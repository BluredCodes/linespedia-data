Always There (for my buddyy) :)

I have got this friend
who is always there.
I think he's swell,
showing he cares.
He comes across the waves,
whenever I need a hand,
he's always there,
to help me stand.
We are really a strange pair.
Who would have thought 
we would be so close
yet, he's all I've ever sought.
He helps me through tough times,
always there seeing me through
without you, my friend,
whatever would I do?
For all the times you've made me laugh,
when you helped me through tough times,
for always being there, no matter what,
I want to say thank you in a rhyme.
You are a friend 'till the end,
One that I will always cherish.
From day to day we remain,
friendship like ours will not perish.
This goes out with lots of hugs,
for all you've done for me.
You're always there buddyy,
a pal in the highest degree.
