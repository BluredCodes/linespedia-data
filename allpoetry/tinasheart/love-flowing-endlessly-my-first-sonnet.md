Love Flowing Endlessly  (my first Sonnet)

As rays of sunshine stream through western skies,
with bright tints to behold as the day ends,
I reflect on wonders, God's hand supplies.
I count the blessings, each day He extends.

You surface from the shadows of my mind,
splendor of your love, lavished tenderly.
The essence of rapture, clearly defined,
by the way your love freely ebbs to me.

An endless rushing of passion cascades,
impelled into the ocean of my love.
The force of our unity never fades.
It rushes freely with a mighty shove.

Waves of thankfulness wash over my soul,
for strong love surrounds us and makes us whole.
