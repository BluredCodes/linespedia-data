Look Up

My friend, you're always there,

never late, though it may seem.

Right on time, You show You care,

Your great love begins to stream.

Why should I carry my burdens alone?

You are there to help me each day

Your faithfulness You have shown.

Though emotions be in disaray,

my heart shall never turn away.
