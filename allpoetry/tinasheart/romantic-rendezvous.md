Romantic Rendezvous


A full moon hangs

in the midnight sky.

The ocean bays;

frosted, foamy waves

crash against seaside.

Pina coladas

being sipped

in refreshing breeze,

as the band plays

Spanish roundelay;

softly swaying

to tune our hearts sing.

A love so strong,

eternal rapture,

lasting and true.
