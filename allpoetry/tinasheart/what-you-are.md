You Are...

You are the sun that rises,
bringing forth a joyous new day;
the radiance on my face;
a bright and cheerful ray.

You are the rushing tempest,
that showers on my heart.
You submerge my being,
as flowing love you impart.

You are the love I breathe,
the essence of my soul.
I relish each moment we share,
for with you I am whole.
