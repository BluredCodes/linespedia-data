~In all my dreams, I never thought,reality could feel this good.~****************************************Heavenly PassionIn the dark hours of the night,I hear you calling out my name,ravenous hunger in your voice,as though you saw into my heart.I reach longingly out to you.Gratefully, you receive my love.Your kisses stoke a flame within,this fervid urge must be filled.Sheer ecstasy as you rush in,elevated on Spirit wings,ascending to heights unperceived,grasping Heaven's love here on earth.©TinasHeart5/19/01
