Can You Hear the Cries?

Through the deadly silence,
a woeful cry splits the air.
The cry of the nations
can be heard across the land.
Tears are streaming down,
for the blood that was shed,
the innocent lives that were lost,
on this day in history.

In the dead of night, when all is quiet,
a soulful cry can be heard.
Can you hear the cries 
coming forth from graves
made of rubble and debris?
Listen, hear the cries
of survivors crying for
lost loved ones on this sleepless night.

When will the hate end?
When will the violence stop?
Is love for our brothers and sisters,
just a thing of the past?
We fall down on our knees,
oh Father in Heaven hear our pleas.
Destroy hate, restore peace,
as we cry, "God bless America".
