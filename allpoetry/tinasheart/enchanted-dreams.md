Enchanted Dreams

In my enchanted place,
there is only you and me.
Radiance shines on your face.

There are only clear skies
and magical rainbow dreams.
Love glistens in your eyes.

On my enchanted plateau
bitter words are never spoken.
From your lips only honey flows.

Kisses ooze like sweetest wine,
passions run free and wild,
indulging in the fruit of the vine.

Where has the glimmer gone
that once sparkled in your eyes?
My prince has withdrawn.

I've strained to hear the song
your lips once sang to me.
My love you've been gone too long.

Come with me my love again
to this enchanted place
where our love forever reigns.

Moonlight has begun to stream.
I lay my weary head to rest.
I'll look for you in my enchanted dreams.
