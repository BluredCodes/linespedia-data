The Only Way To Go

While driving down the road today,
I was touched in a special way.
On the radio a song was playing.
Touched by the words it was saying,
as though God was speaking to me
in an answer to my heart's plea.
As I listened intently to the song,
I knew in trials, He'd make me strong.

It sometimes takes a lot to learn,
that God's love is at every turn.
How many times must we climb this hill
before we triumph in His perfect will?
He gives us several tries to advance.
In His mercy there's a second chance.
Though It seems a hundred chances to me,
Yesterday's forgotten, cast into the sea.

We may remind Him of our many mistakes,
how many times our hearts seemed to break,
He does not know what we are talking about
Standing in the way, only unshakable doubt.
Our sins have all been covered by His blood,
even when painful memories begin to flood,
drowning out the peace that's our's to keep
For His love, as wide as an ocean and as deep.

In this life, choices we must make,
Though our heart may painfully ache,
His powerful strength, we will avail,
because His kindness does not fail.
It is Your will, I must now choose,
with this choice I will not loose.
With all my soul, this I truly know,
that Your way, is the only way to go.
