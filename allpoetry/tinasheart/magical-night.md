Magical NightStars shimmer in the midnight sky.Romance weaves her enchanting spell,swishing and swirling in the air.Wanton desires pervades my essence,for I've fallen under your charms.With just one glimpse into my eyes,Visions of you cloud my thoughts,do you know how deeply you effect me?I await your soft touches on my skin.On this magical night, you will be mine.You embrace me tenderly into your arms,smoothly uttering ardent words in my ear,Then you generously kiss my quivering lips.I can no longer wait for our intimate union.Show me now, everything I have wished for...beneath the whimsical skies.©TinasHeart 9/5/01
