Angel Of Inspiration

At night while you lay sleeping,
from above, one is sent to instill.
A special angel comes peeping,
resting on your heart's windowsill.

She is the angel of inspiration,
and if you leave an open window,
she'll whisper words of aspiration.
Soon creative ideas begin to flow.

Words of love rush to your soul,
visions of fairies dance in your head,
gentle words of healing to console,
dreams interwoven by a single thread.

Tonight as you lay your head to rest,
leave an open window and heed her voice,
for she will aspire you to do your best
to create a poem in which you can rejoice.

Be sure to listen with an open heart,
as she softly whispers, images to bestow,
inspiring you to create those works of art,
for poems are only a thought untill they grow.
