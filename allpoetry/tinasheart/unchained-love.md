Unchained Love

Tonight you embraced me so tight.
Caressed by the gentle moonlight,
tenderly beaming in your eyes,
it was there I saw eternity arise.

This love was written in the stars,
a love that shines bright like ours,
by the Father in Heaven is foretold,
tonight that promise begins to unfold.

You reached within, touching my core,
holding the key that unlocked the door,
knowing behind that door was a stream,
endlessly flowing with a love supreme.

Sweet and pure love comes rushing out,
flooding the essence of all my doubts.
Now I'm free to love without confines,
all because you read between the lines.
