---
title: "Sage Brush"
writer: "TinasHeart"

Sage brush
on lofty hills,
rocks, clay, sand storms swirling.
From the open plains to tall trees,
these things I see out on the open road.
Collapsed roofs on old rustic barns,
cows in open pastures,
green and lush like
sage brush.
