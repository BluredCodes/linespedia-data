Each Time With YouEach time in your embrace,pleasure fills my heart,allured by your grace.Each time our lips meet,my physique shudders,swiftly my heart beats.Each time you softly speaksweet endearing words,my soul is ever meek.Each time that we unite,infinite pleasures found,your passion my delight.Each time with you,joy beyond compare,our love is always new.©TinasHeart
