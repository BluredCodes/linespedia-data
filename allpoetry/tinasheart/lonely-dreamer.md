Lonely DreamerAmongst Heaven's hostis an angel so fairwith lustrous golden hair,cascading on ethereal wings;beaming sapphire eyes;rose blush cheeks,and cherry-red lipsupon peach soft skin.This animable angelis a lonely dreamer.She will often dwellalong a tranquil creek,by emerald grasslands,her solitude to seek.She mimics monarchsdancing among daffodils.She dreams of romanceas she sings aloud,lost in her whimsical waysdreaming a handsome ladwill hear her song of love.Captivated by her scent,he'll murmur her nameand consent to kisses sweet.While the other angelsare busying themselvesfighting battles and such,she sits indulging fancies.She heeds not their reprimands.She knows her dreams can not be,still she dreamily invisionsimaginary castles in the air....for she's just a lonley dreamer.©TinasHeart2000
