Our SanctuaryIntimate moments shared with you,they are the times I cherish most.When you hold me tenderly close,filling my heart with love anew.Murmuring words of luring passion,those trusted secrets I hold dear.Whispering endearments so sincere,as my heart beats with compassion.Tucked closely between the sheets,of our blessed sacred sanctuarya spiritual high my soul carries.A union like ours is so complete.Each time that you enter into my gate,on wings of purity my heart transports,rapidly we move past the outer courts,come now with me to a Heavenly estate.©TinasHeart 10/26/01
