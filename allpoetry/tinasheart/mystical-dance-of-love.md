Mystical Dance Of Love

There is a place that's filled with magic,
an enchanted garden of wonder and delight.
efflorescence never looses it's beauty,
for it has been told, when all is quiet,
a fascinating faerie princess comes to life.
You will find her out each starry night,
with gossmere wings flying in the breeze,
she'll be there, dancing in the moonlight.

Wherever she may go, she leaves a trail
of purple mist and magical faerie dust.
No one knows for certain why she does it,
some say, it is for longing and for lust,
still others believe it's her only passion,
one thing is for sure, to dance she must.
If you listen closely, you'll hear the melody,
streaming in the air, when strong winds gust.

I believe her prevocative prances are for love,
for often forlorn tears flow down her face.
She awaits for her charming faerie prince,
longing to be held in love's alluring embrace,
to share her sweet seduction through the nights,
knowing one day, he'll meet her in this place,
so she continues her dance with all her might,
awaiting the day her true love comes, her tears to erase.

Beautiful little faerie princess, take heart,
your true love shall come flying through the air,
He will capture all the stars within your eyes,
sweetly sweeping you off your feet in great flair.
With all the charming and romantic ways he possesses,
he will bestow to you all the love he has to share.
So continue with your sweet seductive moonlight serenade,
keep on believing and let not your heart despair.
