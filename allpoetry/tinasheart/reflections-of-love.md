Reflections of Love

Gaze into the windows of my soul. 
Mirrored there, a reflection of love 
that my heart holds for you.

As I stand before you, 
I glimpse your amorous eyes, 
an echo of the devotion I have for you. 

Picturesque impressions of you 
forever imprinted on my heart. 
The years to us have been kind. 

Images of our love ever on my mind. 
The likeness of our admirations, 
a beautiful sight to behold.
