A Precious Gift

A gift from God up above 
you have now received. 
Sent down with love 
for all that you've grieved. 

A glorious new day begins, 
darkened clouds roll away 
see the sun as it grins 
in illuminous array. 

A brand new day with great glory 
God's placed within your hands. 
What will be your life story, 
will you walk in His plans? 

As a fragile flower in your care, 
only He can unfold each petal 
when we try, they only tear. 
Let His will on us settle. 

You want to know His plans for you? 
And walk within His perfect will? 
Then this is what you must do, 
Find a time to be still. 

Then, with Heaven's angels you will shine, 
feel the brushing of their mighty wings, 
with powerful protection, so devine. 
Know you're a child of the King.
