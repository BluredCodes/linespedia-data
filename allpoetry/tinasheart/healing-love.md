Endless Love

Your kisses sweet and loving,
stir such intensity within me.
A touch, a feel, a knowing,
of love eternal.

*****************************



~Healing Love~

Lay me down soft and low,
cover me with velvet kisses.
Restoration begins to flow,
within your warm embrace.

Rapturous murmurs in my ear,
sultry breath upon my neck,
all my heart longs to hear,
the depth of your affections.

The healing I find
as you hold me close,
gives me peace of mind,
by you I am loved completely.

Enclose me with your love.
As we join together,
upon the wings of a dove,
my spirit is lifted high.

For you I expand my wings,
freely I discover paradise,
for you are everything
my heart has longed for.
