Love Beneath The Moonlight

Moonlight
beams on your face,
starlight shines in my eyes.
In your warm embrace, time stands still,
my heart races, tremors run down my spine,
the heat rises, passions ignite,
love's fiery glow holds us
here beneath the 
moonlight.
