Thank YouLord, I just want to say thank you!I just want to praise Your Holy name.I love You with words that are true.From era to era You remain the same.Your promises are faithful are sure.The whole earth will tremor and shakewith joyful adorations ringing forthall for the glory of Your name's sake.Hear me as I shout aloud Your worth.May my rumble be pleasing in Your ears.I just want to lift my voice to You.My soul rejoices for Your great love.My feet will rythmically dance anew,as on the wings of a pure white dove.Thank you for all that You have done.©TinasHeart 10/28/01
