Enchanted Dreams Of You

There's a special place, in my realm of dreams,
that comes to life beneath shining moon beams.
A mystical garden scented with nature's perfume.
When dew drops and moonlight caress the blooms,
they glitter magicaly, tinted with Heavenly hues.
I watch in awe as opaque light begins to suffuse.

A magical array of splendor captivates my eyes,
mesmerised by the magic, my soul begins to rise
in longing, when at last I see you in the mist,
with your body shimmering, you, I cannot resist.
My gaze fixed on your form, desire floods my being.
Of all the beauty around, you are all I am seeing.

I long for you to take me into your warming embrace,
with loving gaze fixed upon me, my sorrows to erase.
Every hindrance floating endlessly through the air,
My heart takes wing, for no one to you can compare.
You are my inspiration, what makes my heart fly free.
With your charming ways, you enrapture me effortlessly.

I am entraced by you, cast deeply under your spell,
with just a slight touch, my body begins to swell.
In all the earth, no one could make feel like you do.
Of how deeply you affect me, do you even hold a clue?
You're everything I could dream of and so much more.
It's only your face, I'll see in my dreams forevermore.
