I'll Meet With You In Heaven

The saddest day I know,
was the day you left my side.
God called you home one night.
Still I know our love abides.

I miss your gentle touch,
I miss your endearing smile.
I miss your loving ways.
For me you walked the extra mile.

As I lie here staring at the ceiling,
I remember the times you held me near.
Who's gonna hold me now that you're gone?
Who's gonna call me dear?

Goodbye my love,
I know you are happy there.
You are with the Lord above.
But I'm lonely down here.

Only God can wipe away my tears.
Only He can help me to stand.
But still I long for the day,
that once again I'll hold your hand.

I know God is here.
He will lead me through,
untill that beautiful day,
when in Heaven I meet with you.
