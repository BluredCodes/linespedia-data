Within My Heart

When darkness pervades my thoughts,
Your light shines, allowing me to see.
When lies are whispered to my soul,
Your truth prevails, setting me free.
Greater are You Lord, within my heart.

When the enemy rushes in like a river,
Your love is the ocean roaring in might
and nothing can keep me from Your love.
I will mount up on wings, taking flight.
Greater are You Lord, within my heart.

When I am limited by the infamy I see,
You look deeper inside, seeing my heart.
You're showing me my essence in beauty.
The gentle love inside is a work of art.
Greater are You Lord, within my heart.

Let the soft compassion burn the chaff,
Untill all that is left is the love inside.
A gentle heart streaming with rapture,
this is Your great love that will abide.
Greater are You Lord, within my heart.
