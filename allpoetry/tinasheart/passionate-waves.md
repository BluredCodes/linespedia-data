Passionate Waves

The sultry sun sets
intimately close to the earth.
Effervescent shades of dawn
entwine organically in unison
at the climax of the day.

The whispering wind
moans his seductive cry,
lustily wailing, briskly shifting
through the arching willow.

The swelling ocean
bellows her tempting plea.
Plunge into her depths,
taste her salty flow,
as waves of ecstasy 
wash over you.
