Kiss Me In The Morning

When the sunlight begins to stream,
each new morning before you arise,
I know you watch me while I dream.

All my heart longs for is your kiss
to softly, sweetly carress my cheeks.
In my days, your touches I reminisce. 

I snuggle your pillow with devotion,
for there, the scent of you lingers,
permeating my heart with sweet emotions.

Planted sweetly upon my brow
a simple gesture of your love
shows to me your faithful vow.

Then I'd feel your love through my day,
giving to me a special, warm feeling
knowing you're love even when you're away.

Please touch my cheek before you go,
sweetly whisper to me words of intimacy
speaking of the love you tenderly bestow.

With each step that you take today,
feel my soft cheek upon your mouth
and know my love will never go away.
