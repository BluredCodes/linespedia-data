Ocean Of Love

You are my soul's echo.
Gazing into your eyes, I see
the way your love flows,
endlessly into this sea.

My soul, a fathomless ocean,
surging eternally unto you.
You, an echo of my devotion,
that rushes the tides through.

Love's tidal waves are rife 
flowing endlessy over you.
On the shores of this life,
it's passion remains true.

This ocean will never run dry,
nor loose it's might.
There's an endless supply
for it's formation is tight.

I'm drowning in this love,
washing endlessly over me,
taking me on the wings of a dove,
to a powerful degree.

I am lost, in your essence,
submerged deep into your core,
awestruck by it's quintessence,
and never wanting to go ashore.


We'll stay in the ocean's floor,
loving endlessly all our life,
seeking the treasures in store,
infinitely, our love will survive.
