Passionate Glow Of Love 

Evening falls in great splendor. 
God skillfully paints blue skies 
in shades of pink and heliotrope, 
exposing picturesque expressions 
of His creative affection for us. 

Sweetly scented roses shimmering 
in the sun's blushing cerise rays, 
matching radiance upon our faces. 
Passionate sensations delight us, 
encompassed in the colors of love. 

Plush emerald green trees swaying, 
to and fro in the chilling breeze, 
mimicking the rythm of my heart, 
beating wildly with fervant love, 
raised by the warmth of your touch. 

As the sun sets this romantic 'eve, 
zealous emotions are intensified, 
embraced in a radiant glow of love. 
Lavish me with intimate caresses, 
holding me in the warmth of rapture. 

I look around me at God's beauty. 
My being floods with thankfullness 
for all the gifts God has given us. 
Of all these blessings, in my eyes, 
the greatest of all these is you.
