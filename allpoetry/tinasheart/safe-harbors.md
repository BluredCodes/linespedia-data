Safe HarborsI walk the Eastern shoresjust before the sun sets.Frosted white caps collidewith the rocks and sand.Salty breezes permeate,blowing through my hairand exhilarating my senses.Majestic colors fill the sky,A masterpiece designed by God.Like the old lighthousestanding tall and firm,is my Savior's love for me.He rescues me from any storm,reliably leading me to safe harbors.©TinasHeart 9/17/01
