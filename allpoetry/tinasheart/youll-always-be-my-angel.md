This poem is written for my daughter, Angel. Just wanted you to know, I love you and I always will. 


You'll Always Be My Angel

Sweet tender child of mine,
how you make my soul shine.
You feel up all my days,
with joy in all your ways.

I love spending time with you.
It matters not what we do.
To hear your voice so bright,
fills me with great delight.

I see you look up to me,
wondering what you'll be.
Seeking my admiration,
in your life's exploration.

It makes my heart arise,
as I see the love in your eyes.
Your heart in tune to mine,
is a comfort so divine.

You touch my heart more than you know,
with all the love you sweetly show.
Butterfly kisses on my face
keeps my heart in warm embrace.

I watch you grow before my eyes.
With each year that passes by,
you become more of a pleasure,
each year with you I treasure.

You'll always be an angel to me.
My heart will hold you tenderly,
even when you are all grown
and have children of your own.

My hand may release you,
as the world you enter into,
but my heart never will.
when you're grown, I'll love you still.
