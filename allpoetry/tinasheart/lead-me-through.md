Lead Me Through 

Lord, I am trying so hard to trust you. 
I know You have my best interest at heart. 
I know I have a marriage made in Heaven. 
Yet sometimes, it seems to fall apart. 

Then I wonder, will my tears ever run dry? 
You are everything I need in this world. 
My days were always filled with bliss, 
then the enemey's anger was unfurled.

To kill and destroy is his mission.
He wants to sever the gift you gave.
I know that You're greater than he.
I give my life to You knowing You save.

I know I must continue on, trusting in You, 
that you're working all things for my virtue. 
When the trials seem too much for me to bear, 
sweet Jesus, hold my hand, lead me through.
