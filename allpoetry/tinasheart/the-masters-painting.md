The Master's Painting

My eyes observe the wonder 
of God's grandeur before me.
A saffron sun illuminates
cloudless azure firmament,
above a ultramarine ocean.
Olive green palm branches
gently waver in the breeze.

Inspired by nature's beauty,
my oppression is blown away.
My spirit arises in flight
touching Your receiving heart, 
as my voice raises in praise.
Lord, You are the Master artist,
all creation is Your canvas.
