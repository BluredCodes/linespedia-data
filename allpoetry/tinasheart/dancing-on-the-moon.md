Dancing On The Moon

Sweet lover of mine,
set your mind at ease.
Come away with me,
this moment we'll sieze.

Cast aside your cares,
invision with me,
a place so serene,
a flight of fancy.

Drift into the sky,
among the bright stars,
fly away with me,
Paradise is ours. 

We'll have a grand ball,
waltzing in the air,
you and I will be
quite the charming pair.

I'll be your princess,
dressed in royal gown,
sitting on the moon,
looking quite renowned.

You my handsome prince,
sweep me off my feet,
dressed in tuxedo,
looking fine and sweet.

We'll dance untill dawn,
loving all night long,
within your embrace,
is where I belong.
