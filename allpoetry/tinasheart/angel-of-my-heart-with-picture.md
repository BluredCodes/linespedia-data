Angel Of My Heart

Sweet angel of my heart,
you fill me with delight
as you have from the start,
ever dear in my sight.

Go to sleep little one,
dream magical pleasure
untill the night is done,
those no heart can measure.

Dance on the silver moon,
capture a falling star,
Hear Mother's loving tune,
that's never very far.

Rest your head on the clouds,
sleep peacefully, my child.
May every joy allowed
brim in your heart so mild.
