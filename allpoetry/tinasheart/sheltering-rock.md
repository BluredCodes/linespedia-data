Sheltering RockThe fierce storm rages.In a sea of intense wrath,waves crash destructively.They threaten to engulf me.Dark clouds encompass,spouting viscous, ugly lies,that fuse with desperate tears.Yet my focus remains on You.You set me high upon a rock.Whenever I am dismayed,I will dwell safely in the hiddenshelter of Your mighty arms.©TinasHeart2/11/2001
