Sunshine In My Heart

The sun begins to rise above the hills.
Rays of light shine through my window.
The house within is peaceful and still.
As my sleepy eyes begin to open I know
the familiar longing to be by His side.

I listen to the sparrows sweetly singing.
I hear my Savior speaking softly to me,
His tender, constant love He is bringing.
I open the windows of my heart quickly,
to let the sunshine of his love beam in.

Within His Holy Word, there is a fountain.
His words to me bring such joy to my soul,
much more than I could ever fully contain.
Reaching to the depths, making me whole.
My eyes shine with the knowledge of His care.

When storm clouds try to hide the sun's glow,
there's always a glimmer of hope to guide you.
When we grow tired and weary, Jesus knows.
His faithful love for us comes shining through,
dispelling the darkened hopelessness in the air.

Even on those days when the drenching rain,
thunder and lightning split the darkened earth,
crashing down unyieldingly upon my window pane,
the storm will soon end, I'll see my promise birth.
Jesus is the sun that brightly shines in my heart.
