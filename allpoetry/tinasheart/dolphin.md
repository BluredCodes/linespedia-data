As many of you know, I just came back from vacation.
One place my family and I went to was Sea World in 
San Antonio, Texas where we saw lots of shows. The
dolphins were so cute, I felt they deserved a poem.:)
That is what inspired this poem.


Dolphin
swim fast, fly high,
make your magical sound,
twist and turn beneath the water,
dance, prance and put on a show for us.
Sleek, elegant and beautiful,
wise creature of the sea,
you charmed my heart,
dolphin.
