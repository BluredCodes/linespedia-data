Evensong

The sun begins its decline,
exuding eminent pigments;
orcherous and helitorpe
enchantingly interweave,
arranging a magnificent
tapestry in the Heavens.

Inhale the fragrant aroma
of lilac and honey-suckle;
sweet and delicate scent
of floral perfumes combine,
tantalize, and arouse senses,
cultivating a calming nature.

In the serenity of twilight,
just as the day grows dim,
casting violet hues in sky,
crickets harmonize contently; 
attend unto the evensong,
as another day purely resigns.
