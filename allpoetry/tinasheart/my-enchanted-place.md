My Enchanted Place


There is an enchanted place 

filled with loving grace.

Here I feel warm and secure,

all else fades away in a blur.



My heart fills with gratitude.

It does not matter the mood,

tears of sorrow, tears of bliss,

my every care floats amiss.



I want you to take me there

for nowhere else can compare,

to the place within your arms,

where I find your loving charms.
