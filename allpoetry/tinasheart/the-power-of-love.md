The Power Of Love



The power of love

so mighty, so strong,

no hardship can overturn.

Tears flow from my eyes,

for love has overcome.

Through trials of life,

true love remains firm,

it never leaves your side.

Hurts are hurled at every turn

we cry out to God above,

He hears our every plea.

Though the love never left,

it is deeper still,

fathomless as the ocean blue.

I hold a promise in my soul

that no one can take from me.

The roots are ever strong,

it can not be plucked away.

It will always come back again!!

Love is strong. Nothing can stop it's power.
