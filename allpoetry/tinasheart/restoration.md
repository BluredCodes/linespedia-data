Restoration

I need your ardent affection
like a tender blossom urges
for the pleasing spring rains.
Without the gentle sprinkling,
the shoot will wither and die.

With the fresh morning dew
comes arousing restoration.
Just one droplet of your love
could never be enough for me,
It leaves me longing for more.

Let your love shower over me,
stimulating me like a rainfall
upon a parched dry ground.
I want to flourish in only you,
stand beside you true and tall.
