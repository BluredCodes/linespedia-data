Kiss Me In The Morning

Each new morning when you arise,
as the sun peeks through the window,
streaming rays of light into our room,
I know you watch me as I sleep.
All my heart longs for is your kiss,
softly carressing my cheeks.

The alluring scent of you lingers,
permeating my heart with warmth.
A simple token of your love
planted sweetly on my brow,
I'd know your love throughout the day,
Untill once again you're in my arms.

With each new day, I desire you.
Please touch my cheek before you go
intimately whisper words of love.
With each step you take today,
feel my cheek upon your lips,
and know my love shall never leave.
