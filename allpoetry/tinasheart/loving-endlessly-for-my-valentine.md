Happy Valentines Day, my Prince of Love!

Loving Endlessly (for my valentine)

You, my prince came into my life,
upon a starry, mystical night.
You rescued me from the darkness,
slaying the dragon with might.

You swept me off of my feet
with all your charming ways.
You captured my heart with love,
and set my fervant soul ablaze.

You placed me upon a white horse.
Together, we rode off into the sky,
to our magical castle in the clouds,
where only love is the needed supply.

Many times I have pinched myself
to see if this is a dream or real.
You are not just a mystical fantasy,
yet the love we share seems so surreal.

In the night, you hold me in your arms
filling up my soul with such bliss.
Tonight, I want you to take me way up high,
arousing my senses with a lover's kiss.

As we unite, we will reach lofty hieghts,
abundantly exceeding every mystical dream.
We shall spend our nights loving endlessly,
reaching the sky, where love rules supreme.

Tonight all your dreams will become reality.
Endless waves of pleasure will wash over you,
for tonight your every wish will be my command.
I will show you passion like you never knew.

My love, take my hand and fly away with me,
on a magical journey of intimacy and glee.
I will always be your princess, you, my prince,
and together we will keep on loving endlessly.
