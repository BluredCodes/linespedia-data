Nature's Fiery Passion

The sun spreads out wild and free,
across western skies in the evening,
opening for the entrance of the moon.
Shades of passion, hot and torrid,
so fervid, the sky begins to blush.
The fever burns on as the sun moves 
rapidly into the depths of Heaven,
a seductive oneness so devine.

A beautiful array of splendor appears
as the sun and the moon intwine in the sky.
Untill, exhausted, the sun sinks below the earth.
As the fever passes, the earth begins to cool,
twinkling lights fill the sky, and the moon appears
with a magical afterglow, shining in the light of the sun,
At that one special moment of the day when
the moon and the sun come together in unison.
