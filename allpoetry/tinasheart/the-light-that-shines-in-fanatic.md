The Light That Shines In Fanatic

There is one among us who dazzles. 
Though darkness be all around him,
he shines like a star in the Heavens.
He writes for the good of man kind.
Once engulfed in a sea of blackness,
God mercifully rescues from the flood.
Now, standing boldly in great radiance,
he shares with others the light of God.

He's a man of God, wanting only to share
the wonderous joy that he has discovered.
It is not his own glory that he shines,
but the glorious light of God on high
shining brightly for all the world to see.
It does not matter what you have done,
he will forgive and make a new friend,
to show the gracious mercy of our King.

The sweetest honey drips from his lips,
which are words of God's greatest truths.
Your self esteem, he will build highly,
with his words full of life and strength.
Always encourageing you to do your best,
helping others to succeed in their works.
There should always be a place in our hearts,
for a friend such as he, standing bravely for truth.
