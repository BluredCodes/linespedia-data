Only you

Only you...
with love so true,
touch my heart
the way you do.

Only you...
have made me whole,
filled the longings
of my soul.

Only you...
impart delight 
satisfy me 
all my nights.

Only you...
will I treasure,
give you love 
beyond measure.


Only you...
make me feel wanted,
for our love 
remains undaunted.

Only you...
take me through life,
make me glad 
to be a wife.

I’ll know, 
when my life is through,
joy of love,
in ...only you.
