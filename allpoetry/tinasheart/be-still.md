"Be Still"

When all the world is cold and uncaring,
I hear a voice speaking ever so tenderly
within my troubled heart, "Child, be still".
I'll seek a place of solitude and serenity,
a quiet place where I find comfort and rest.

I hand my troubles to the One who cares,
pouring out my heart and soul, tears flow.
All though He knows, I tell Him everything.
"Be still", soothing breezes begin to blow.
Sweet, gentle peace settles upon my soul.
