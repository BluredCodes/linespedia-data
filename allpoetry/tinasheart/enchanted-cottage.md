Enchanted CottageWalking down a winding lane,vivid scenes unveil before me.Images of an enchanted cottage,nestled in the essence ofa lush emerald green grove.Underneath a stony bridge,a shimmering sapphire brook,where snowy white swansgracefully glide in accord,with gentle flowing drifts.Livid blue and rose floralborder the glistening water.Tea berry and honey-sucklegrace the arch way entrance.Sweet aroma wafts on the breeze.Such wonder my eyes behold,on a clear Summer afternoon.It sets my spirit soaring high,penetrating me with admirationfor nature's magestic beauty.©TinasHeart7/26/01
