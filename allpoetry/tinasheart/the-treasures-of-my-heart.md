The Treasures of my Heart

In my heart there's a chest of treasures,
seeking finest jewels brings forth pleasures.
Memories collected through the years,
vivid immpressions of you appear.

Gazing deeply into your brown eyes,
watching illustrious stars arise,
that sparkle with perpetual love,
gently soft as a pure white dove.

Hearing you whisper into my ear,
as in your embrace you hold me near.
Coursing my fingers in your wavy hair,
as I listen intently to your every care.

Savoring sweetness of your lips,
as you gently carress my hips.
To lie within your sheltering arms,
knowing safety, guarded from harm.

I walk in and see your face alight,
mounting on wings, my heart takes flight.
Sharing a message as our eyes meet,
those secrets between you and me, discreet.

Standing by my side, you've been my friend,
happy or sad, a strong hand to lend.
Traveling down our journey of life,
I've been truly blessed to be your wife.

Our love is lasting and sincere,
Growing stronger every year.
The gems I cherish make me whole,
they are the treasures of my soul.
