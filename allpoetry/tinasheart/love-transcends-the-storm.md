Love Transcends The Storm

We sailed the currents of the raging storm,
violently jolted by the tides of this world.
We set forth on a peaceful voyage on the sea.
Then the fury of the typhoon was unfurled.

The tempest, so intense, It seemed we'd drown.
By the enraged seas, we were nearly devoured,
barely able to keep our heads above water.
But love's vigor can not be overpowered.

The tides of life can be tranquil or angry.
God never promised it'd be all smooth sailing.
He promises to be with us through the storm.
We can be assured that love is unfailing.

Many raging waters cannot quench true love.
Through the storm, a peacefulness descends.
Through every roaring storm that life hurls our way,
I'll trust and believe our love will transcend.
