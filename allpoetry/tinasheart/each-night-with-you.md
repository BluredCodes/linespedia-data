Each Night With You

Each night with you,
I feel the gentleness of your touch,
and breathe the essence of your love,
I need you so much.

Each night with you,
is a pleasure beyond compare,
as I taste the sweetness of your lips,
and run my fingers through your hair.

Each night with you,
it's for your love that I plead,
for in your love I am complete.
You are all I'll ever need.

Each night with you,
I'll meet with you in our secret place,
to recieve the pleasures of our love,
that lie within your embrace.

Each night with you,
your love becomes sweeter than before.
I want to lie within your arms,
and love only you forevermore.
