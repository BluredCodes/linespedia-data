Waiting For A FriendHow I am missing you today.Friends have a special way,of chasing the blues away,sending happiness your way.Putting smiles upon your face,by their tender loving grace.They enclose a special placeinside, no other can replace.If you find a true friend,they'll be you to the end.with strong hands to lend,many troubles will ascend.These things I found in you,a comrade who is always true.Now my faith I must cling to,as you sort things through.I must wait and give you time,till the bell of freedom chimes.I pray that higher you'll climb,and things for you turn out fine.It is no easy thing for me.Days seem like an eternity.God help me, hear my plea,as I'm here on bended knee.God grant my friend, wings to flyswiftly, through the storm's eye.Let him hear not the enemy's lie.Be ever always his great supply.I need God's grace to wait for you,for that is what a friend would do.I will be here, just like you knew,When this trial you make it through.©TinasHeart 11/30/01
