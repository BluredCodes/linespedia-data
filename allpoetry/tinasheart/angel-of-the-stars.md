Angel Of The Stars

Sweet angel with starlight sprinkles,
in your eyes, Heavenly joy twinkles.

Those sweet rosy angelic cheeks kissed
by God, for you're surely in His midst.

A halo upon hair that shines like the sun,
beauty God put into you, cannot be outdone.

Play melodies for us on harp of gold, 
sing of Heaven's great love you hold.

With a special sweetness all your own,
God's wonderous grace you have shown.

Fly with gossamer wings in skies above,
sprinkle stardust and God's devine love. 

Keeping us forever within your sight,
watch over us and guide us to the light.
