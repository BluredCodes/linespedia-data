The Blind and weary Walker...
His sword a burden to carry.
The Tremble of his hand...
        A wisper's wind.
Cursed to follow.
        Kind to tame.
Ever Darkness.
        Lit beacon, faultering dim.
Willow spirits instill.
Sadness     shame.
        River soul.
                   Moving Conscience.
Strike down the love, Cast life         away.
The sword a burden,
                   The hands a witness.
Blood that stains,
                     The eyes, skin.
Soul.
Anger, a two edged blade.
             Strike down the love,
       Dignity shrinks to shame.
                                  To shame.
