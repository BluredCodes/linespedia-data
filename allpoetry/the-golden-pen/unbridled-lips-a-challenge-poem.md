L ips unbridled and free! 
I ndependance has cut the chains of silence once and for all! 
B Lessed liberty! 
E xpression to speak our minds,without censorship nor fear 
R inging loud and clear, 
T he voices of democracy, 
Y ours and mine....One voice echoing the songs of Unity! 

Mary Gonzalez
