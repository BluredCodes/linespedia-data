~My Romance~


Soul, there’s a Royal Ballet
Within my heart this very day,
Leaping, twirling and dancing for utter Joy!
In rhythm my entire being sways,
While invisible feet does pirouettes
To the cadence of my romance.

In a minuet my body dances in time to the tune of my romance,
 Entranced by the passion, mystery and pageantry it performs a Royal Ballet,
My heart twirls and leaps and does pirouettes.
In celebration of such a delightful day!
My mind melts, my body sways
My entire being radiates with love and joy!

Soul, you are entranced by the melody of my romance....
In your ecstacy you sit and watch the Royal Ballet
Enchanted by each ballerina’s pirouettes,
You are enchanted by this extremely romantic  day.
In rhythm your entire body sways
With bliss, with love, in utter joy!

You are caught within the overture of my romance  
As if by magic, you become a ballerina performing in the Royal Ballet
Leaping, Dancing doing pirouettes,
Overwhelmed by the glamour, the magic, the opulence of this day
Overtaken by sheer beauty and elegance...your entire body sways
Delighting in the rapture, the charm...the joy!

 Soul, experience the elegance, the splendor of my romance
Enjoy the grand repertoire  of the Royal Ballet
Dozens of ballerinas in pink tutoos twirling in dainty pirouettes
Celebrating the majesty...the royalty...the passion and the  enchantment of this day.
In elation their entire body sways
Enthralled in utter happiness and joy!

There’s an exotic flavor in my romance
Poetry in motion captured by the performers of the Royal Ballet 
It’s in the flair of the ballerina’s pirouettes..
Its in the ambience of this very day
It’s the rhythm and cadence that makes your entire body sway
You could smell the jasmine in the air as your soul delights in utter joy!



Applause! Applause! My being gives a standing ovation beaming with such Joy!
Such performance demands an encore! Bless this most enchanting day!The curtain rises! The house brought down! A stellar performance by my romance!


            Mary Gonzalez
