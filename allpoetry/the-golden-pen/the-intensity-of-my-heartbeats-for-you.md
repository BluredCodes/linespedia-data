My sweetnessWords are not enoughTo reveal the depthOf the loveMy heart contains for you;   Never in a million yearsCan you ever guess for sureHow many tearsI’ve shed over you; for my love for you is pure   My love is an ocean of purityIt is real and genuineIt is not a weak love, but one of true vitality and potency...intensityMy love shall never fail you, angel of mine!   SweetnessYour soul is like an essenceWhich fills my life with joy and ecstasyYour spirit renews my entire soul with blissYou fill my entire world with so much pleasureMy love for you runs through my veinsWith so much pressure   My heart sings out a song for you....A song of gratitude and appreciationMy love for you is pure and trueIn it there is no deception   Sweetness...In me you will find an ocean of honestyIn me there is no darkness nor painWhen you look at me you will discoverA galaxy of love and passionAnd a gentle heart full of love, admiration, and respectMy love for you is a lasting love...an everlasting love that will never ceaseWon’t you acknowledge this immense and pure love, please!!!
