From up above the clouds,
My soul hovered near
To Almighty God, and covering my naked soul with His ermine shroud,
I humbly whispered in His omnipresent ear.
I whispered softly in a solemn prayer,
The kind of soul I chose
On Earth to be my mother...
I asked God for a heart tender as a rose,
A soul purer than solid gold,
A voice like the cherubims
That sings you the sweetest hymns.
A pair of loving arms that so tenderly does hold,
My soul forever in a loving embrace..
A strong spirit that will carry out her maternal tasks till the very end,
A woman with a gentle face,
With a radiant smile more brighter than the sun,
A woman with an iron will
That will stand firm, ready to defend
Her child at all cost, no matter what danger lies ahead.
A woman with an undying love and passion for her children.
