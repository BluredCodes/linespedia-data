Homeward Bound
I look upon the celestial night above.
A trillion stars light the way
A beacon of love...
A lighted pathway...
That leads me homeward bound; 
 
  
When chaos gets the best of me...
When dark clouds shade my mind...
And all I can see
Are heartaches and the burden which the heart does bind
The spirit downward
and anchors it until it falls into an abyss...
I close my eyes
And release all cares mundane
And find a mystique peace
Then look heavenwards towards the brightest star...
And sprout wings to where you are!
