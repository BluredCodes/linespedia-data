When God designed the Universe
He knew that man would be a creature
Predestined to perdition;
So even before he drew up the blueprints of Creation,
Before He even laid out the cornerstone
Of this place man now calls, ‘home’...
He set about in heaven
To create a celestial race
He fashioned them out of His own loving essence
Out of innocence, love, and pure phosphorescence.
He gave them a special role...
To be men’s spiritual companions
And guardians of the soul.
They guide us through our darkest days
And even though they are invisible
Their light shines on us from their celestial dome.
Like the sun’s rays..
To lead our poor misguided steps
To the boundaries of Paradise
Should we pause and heed their celestial call.
