title: "If I Lost You"
writer: "The Golden Pen"

I was a closed door unto the world, 
Afraid of letting the sun seep through. 
Indeed I was a frightened little girl 
Looking at life from deep within with spectacles of blue; 

Slowly I lost the dream and hopes of finding True Love, 
Convinced that love and happiness was just a myth... 
I've been taken advantaged of 
For so many years that I had learned to build a strong tower 
To protect me from all this. 

Eons went by 
My spirit drunk by all the misery of life 
Sick of all the pain and the lie 
Of what true love was really all about.. 
I kept myself safe within my tower.. 
Far from the fear and self-doubt. 

Yet as if some miracle or cosmic destiny 
This man came along 
So bold, so lovingly sweet...patiently 
Singing a love song 
He slowly began to tear down my walls. 
Until I felt the sun shining on the other side 
and saw for the very first time 
The beauty of love like the sun's warm rays 
unleashing  its power 
it enveloped me like like a mantle 
Unveiling love's enchanting magic that very day. 

If I ever lost you, My Love 
You'd take away the energy 
Of Love's healing rays 
My spirit will be lost forever 
In a dungeon dreary, dark and grey! 

You are my Hero, the Prince that has set me free.. 
If I lost you my heart will wander forever 
Throughout eternity 
A lonely wanderer 
Still searching for a dream impossible to reach 


Please, sweet Prince 
Embrace me with your eternal love 
Annoint me with your kiss 
Convince me what true love is really made of 
Show me the way 
to true happiness and bliss... 
But don't you ever walk away!
