title: "Christmas in My Heart"

Snowflakes silently descend
Like glistening angels from on high...
Rejoice my soul for God did send
A Gift of joy...my whole being quivers! It sends  a jovial sigh...
Rejoice! There is finally Christmas  in my heart!

Clouds of doubt depart..
As a new dawn slowly rises
With glee and joy in my heart...
I celebrate this Miracle with its share of surprises..
I have much to commemorate...
There is Christmas in my Heart.
					


In exultation my heart dons festive apparel..
My entire soul swells with music..
The sounds of angelic carols..
Mingle with the wonder and magic..
Exultation! Rapture! Awe..
Within the contours of my being reigns a celestial peace..
Replacing all the darkness, uncertainty...flaws..
With a love that will never cease!

In His Mercy God above
Has sent me down an angel,
Presenting me with the Gift of Love...
My soul has witnessed a miracle!
With joy and a radiance within
In  gratitude and thanksgiving , I sing!
I now rejoice...letting Christmas seep in....
God performed a Miracle, Darling..
I thank my lucky stars above
For every day, sweetheart...
There is Christmas in my heart.
