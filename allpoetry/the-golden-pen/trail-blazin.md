---
title: "Trail Blazin'"
writer: "The Golden Pen"
slug: "trail-blazin"

Follow me along my journey 
Through golden grass 
Along our way 
We'll hold our hands together... 

Nature all around us.. 
Butterflies on our faces 
Blades of grass tickling our feet beneath.. 
The wind kissing our countenance.. 
Walk with me 
Through trails of sycomore and pine trees.. 
Where days follow nights and you and I 
Trail on and on forever 


Mary Gonzalez (c) 2002
