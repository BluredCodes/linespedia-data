title: "Incandescent.."

Alive, Blazin' with a fury.. 
With golden yellow eyes that burns through any metal. 
Hot as the coals of Hell whose candidates must walk through without mercy. 
Transfiguring, purifying and consuming... 
Reducing everything to ashes and charcoal.. 
a symbol of death and destruction, engulfing all within its path 
Yet a symbol of life in all its glory.. 
Loving, nurturing, yet angrily devouring anyone who dares laugh at its wrath!

Mary Gonzalez (c)2002
