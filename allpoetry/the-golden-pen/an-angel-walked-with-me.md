title: "An Angel Walked With Me"

Once upon a heartache, When life to me seemed like a burden, 
And my heart was hurting so that I thought that it may break.. 
Somehow I found solace,
And the strength to carry on! 
Though my world was tumbling down
And my heart so heavy I thought may touch the ground... 
I've always felt a loving presence
Telling me 'Darling..It's alright!' 
I've always felt some invisible arms around me.. 
Holding me ever so tight... 
And a soothing voice to calm my sobbing eyes.. 
All through the years I thought I walked this godforsaken earth sore afraid and lonely... 
When all along life's unsteady path.. 
An angel walked with me! ..... 
When my heart was bound by chains of brass.... 
I swear that an angel walked with me
