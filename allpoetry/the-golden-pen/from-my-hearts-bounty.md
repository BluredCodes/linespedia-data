A while ago I gave you my heart, wrapped in pretty Christmas paper....

A heart-felt gift given with love and much sincerity

For you to keep forever;
 

I am not an Indian giver...

One who takes back what I present.

When I gave you my heart it was forever...

A gift for you to treasure; a unique and joyful present!

To unwrap again and again and again

To cherish, love and enjoy for your very own pleasure

With no thought of recompense nor gain;
 
 

So, darling....Please do not tell me you can't afford my gift to you...

Do not question , return, nor reject what is freely given.

Keep it forever safe inside  your soul's coffer.

Seldom but   a chosen few who are dear to me...

Have ever had the honor of receiving  this great gift I have to offer...

The undying passion and devotion I share with those I truly value!
