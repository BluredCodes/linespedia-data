---
title: "Love's Purple Rose"
writer: "The Golden Pen"
slug: "loves-purple-rose"

Darling, I give you passion's purple-petaled rose, Wildly wet with morning's dew Sweetly scented to delight your gracious nose; Press this purple-petaled rose, Dear Against your bosom.. As a loving token and souvenir Of a love deep and moist that will forever blossom.
