Child of Venus..
To you alone I owe my heart's debt
And all the love which overflows in excess.
My heart once cried in deep regret
Over many lonely heartaches and despair,
Yet you alone took the impossible task of
Awakening someone's affection with just one single glare
By piercing his heart with your poisoned dart..

Cupid, you clever marks-man, you!
You've hit your victim smack in the middle of his heart,
And magically colored my heart pink from it's usual shade of blue.

Cupid I will eternally imortalize your image
Building you a statue
Where years and years from now people will form a pilgrimage
To venerate and honor you!


Mary Gonzalez (c)
