Soul Mates            Soul to Soul together,            Two souls entwined forever            United to one another             By a piece of cosmic thread..             Heart to heart we gather             As we journey on ahead..             We’ll sail through bright and stormy weather             With nothing to fear or  dread...              With love, hope, and joy to share              Nothing can separate us...we are joined forever.              Seam to seam we two form a perfect pair!             	 (C)2001 The Golden PenAnother soul mate creation by thegoldenpen
