Looking deep into each others’ eyes,Beneath a golden sunset,Two lovers exchanged their most cherished prize...Love’s perfect matching amulet!       Taking her hand, he placed his gift upon her open palmAnd with the greatest love and pride,With a bright smile and the greatest calm,Addressed his future bride:   'Darling, as long as you have this...You shall always have me by your side.It shall always, always give you many years of bliss...But take good care of it, my love...my bride!As it is my very soul you carry'   Drawing her hand upon her purseShe drew out a small bundle'Darling, This is the greatest treasure I possess...It shall keep you company...and forever warm your soul!       As token of their deep affection and commitmentThey offered each other their own heartsTo wear as some great ornamentTill Death do them PartMary Gonzalez © Copyright, 20002000, To my Darling, Alex Aris
