title: "Brave Heart"

The day I heard your voice for the very first time, 
I felt as if heaven opened up its gate for me. 
My soul felt heavenly chimes! 

You dazzled me with your sweetness.. 
And made my heart rejoice 
Now my life is filled with sunshine. 
I am illuminated by the sunshine of your smile! 
Our conversation seemed to last an eternity 
Even though we talked for just a little while. 

Across the miles our lives have come together 
By an invisible, cosmic force 
It is finally our turn 
To reap the joys of love and wonder.. 
This relationship is so sweet! 

My life has been blessed because of you. 
You've made my heart complete. 
Our souls fit like a puzzle, too, 
Which forms an exquisite pattern 
  

When God brings to your life something of great joy and value 
Like He has given me in you 
You must take a calculated chance 
And fight a never-ending battle 
To make the dream come true!
