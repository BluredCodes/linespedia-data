Two distinct Continents unite 
By invisible fingers that carry two diverse cultures through 
The realm of cyberspace. 
In this modern age 
Of microchips and electronic gadgets galore, 
It is quite amazing what can be achieved 
In such a short amount of time and space; 
A thousand years ago today 
No one could imagine 
A small world could come together 
By a small electronic device 
Called E-mail. 
Now with the aid of this modern miracle 
East could now meet West. 
In yesterday’s archaic world 
The postman was a Neanderthal 
As compared to this modern beauty 
By just writing few lines of cyber text 
You can send a message half across the globe 
And in an instant East meets West... 
So advanced is this technology 
That what used to take a lifetime 

Can now be accomplished with a few keystrokes and a click of the mouse. 
What’s next, electric sex?
