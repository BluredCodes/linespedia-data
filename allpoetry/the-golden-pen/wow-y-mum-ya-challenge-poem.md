Wow-Y, Mum-my! 
Why's Daddy top o YOU? 
Are you cold? Must be it! Daddy's keeping Mummy warm! 
I scratch my head, Don't know why what's going on here tonight? 
The bed is shakin'...Daddy must be dreamin' he's a captain of a ship! 
The room is spinning, Mummy's yelling! Are you alright? 
I just woke up to get a sip 
of water, but on my way over I had to pause, 
Cuz...I heard a thump and thought a mad man was in mummy and daddy's room.. 
Brewing up a Storm!

Mary Gonzalez (c)2002In the mind of a child, catching your parents in action is truly puzzling :O)
