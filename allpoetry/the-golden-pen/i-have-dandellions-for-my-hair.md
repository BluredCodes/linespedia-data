ARRRRRRRGGGGGGH! Goodness Gracious..Woe is ME.. 
                  Call the men in white...I'm in deep lunacy!!!!!!!!!! 
                  I believe I'm going to have a nervous breakdown! 
                  As I gazed in the mirror the hairs on my spine stood out...completely!!! 
                  NOT another frightenly BAD HAIR DAY? 
                  I'm not lying....I ressemble a mad lion 
                  From worlds never seen before! 
                  Fluffed all in disarray, 
                  Scattered to and fro 
                  My hair won't budge today! 
                  No matter how I style it; It will not obey!!!!! 
                  I could whip it into shape 
                  or better yet....blow it to smeethereens.. 
                  Goodness, Gracious...I'm in Despair... 
                  I have Dandillions for my hair!!!!
