Your Beloved comes on the Wings of a Dove,
                      Through gales and rain,
                      Steadfast to declare his love.
                      Have faith, This is a Match made in Heaven...

                      Your Beloved is coming soon
                       With tokens of love and affection
                       He’ll be singing you a love tune
                       That will declare his devotion...
                       As certain as the skies are azure
                       Your Beloved is coming for sure..

                       Heaven and Earth will rejoice along with you,
                       As angels above
                       Bear witness to this Love so bold and  true
                       Patience, my dear....listen; The stars above
                       Are heralding the coming of your Beloved
                        Destiny  foretold...
                        You will soon meet your True Love..
                        Behold...he is coming real soon with a band of gold
                         On the Wings of a Dove!

                     
                 
                       

                       Mary Gonzalez (c)2002To: My True Love  whom I know is coming soon to my heart and soul to be my husband...Alex Aris
