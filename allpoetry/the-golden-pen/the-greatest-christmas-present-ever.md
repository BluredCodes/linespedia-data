I shall wrap for you a box full of love
And shall use my finest wrapping paper..
One with Christmas cupids and doves.
I wrap it with all my heart and with pleasure...
I'll tie it with a scarlet ribbonThen place a satin red bow on top, my love
My heart feels that this is the best present it has ever given...
I shall place it underneath the christmas tree
For all the world to see!
This present is a very valuable presentIt is priceless...
You will find it sweet and pleasantIt will extend itself throughout eternity...
The gift that will always keep on giving...
I am sure it will fit you, sweetie...
I am certain this will be the greatest christmas present you will ever receive...
It will wrap itself around you to keep you warm all through the days and nightsThis gift has a lifetime guarantee
Free from defects and it doesn't require batteries...
Enjoy my Christmas gift to you...
Eternal membership into my very heart and soul!Dedicated to my Darling Love of my Life: Alex Aris
