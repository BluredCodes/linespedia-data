title: "The Origin of Sin"

Sex is not sin, Heavens...NO!
There is divinity in pleasure,
Giving your skin a healthy glow,
And your soul much rapture!

Sin was born rather out of blame
When Adam blamed his wife in Eden
Out of a lack of shame
To own up to his resposibility...
This was indeed the seed of sin;
Implanted within the womb of humanity
By an angry God who only wanted to hear the truth
Rather than the thorns of denial.


Sex was the ultimate fruit of life,
The food of paradise for Adam to enjoy
But he forfeited this fruit to listen to his wife..
Who in turn listened to the Evil One instead of God's voice
Temptation was his downfall....Denial... his sin...He had a choice!
As a result of their disobedience..they unleashed the power of Death..
