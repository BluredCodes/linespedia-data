On a starry night I look above
And count a gazillion celestial luminaries.
Each star brags and raves about him whom I love.
And counts all his virtues up for me
Like a heavenly abacuss.
The sum of which makes whole new galaxies
Which live within my heart.
The celestial bodies aren't big enough to chart
All of the sum that adds up to my beloved's essence.
He alone is his own infinity
Of charm, wisdom, and goodness!
