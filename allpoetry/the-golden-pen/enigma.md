Each of us Is only a portion  of the Divine.. Scattered here in part To search for our other half in this lifetime. Each of us is a piece of the Divine Enigma with soul, sinew and heart But incomplete.. Strewn together in chaotic disarray. We err and suffer And live in fear and dismay... Only when we link together Each, contributing his unique piece Can we take part in the Bigger Picture Forming the Divine Puzzle that makes us whole Do we discover the beauty of Inner Peace And the True nature of the Soul (c) 2001  Mary Gonzalez
