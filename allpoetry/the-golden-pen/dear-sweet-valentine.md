Dear Sweet Valentine of mine,
Doorway to my heart's desire!
A little piece of the Divine
Dwells inside you; Your soul is an all-consuming fire
That keeps me warm and toasty inside
and my cheeks all rosy;

The day I met you the heavens smiled wide
As the sun seeped through the very soul of me
Warming and stirring my juices deep inside
As in the brewing of sun tea
On a sunny afternoon.

You and I together
Against life's stormy seas,
Will sail on and on forever
Throughout eternity.
