title: "Tritan's Lair"
writer: "The Golden Pen"
slug: "tritans-lair"

Foamy and mysterious.. 
A woman's velvety blue-black hair 
Tossed this way and that way in a body wave of passion, sexy..lusterous 
Whisked by a nutical wind with a certain Nordic flair; 

Her face shines brightly against the mirror of a full moon. 
A flirty, shiny look upon her eyes, 
Causing the stars above to swoon. 

The horizon sings of her ageless beauty 
And the shore brags of her fame 
The lighthouse advertises her mystery 
And the wind calls out her name 
But the seagulls all know her secret lies 
What lurks beneath her surface
