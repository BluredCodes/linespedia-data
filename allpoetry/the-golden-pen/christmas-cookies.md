---
title: "Christmas Cookies"
writer: "The Golden Pen"

1 ounce of wholesome hugs
1 Cup sunshine
1 Cup Christmas Cheer
10 0unces milk of human kindness
2 Partridge eggs
a dose of kisses
2 Cups of sugar-coated sweetness
a pinch of mirth
1/2 cup of buttery fingers

In a large bowl cream the sugar-coated sweetness with the 
buttery fingers. Add the partridge eggs. Blend well. Add the
Cup of sunshine, Christmas cheer, milk of human kindness, mirth and
mix till harmonious. Fold in the hugs and kisses.

Preheat oven to 375 degree Farenheit.

Drop cookies by loving tablespoons unto an ungreased cookie sheet.

Bake for approximately 8 to 12 minutes

or until Santa Claus could smell them from the North Pole.


Take them out of the oven and let cool completely.

Serve with cups of Christmas Cheer!
