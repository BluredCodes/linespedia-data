I've dressed my heart up in Chantilly Lace,Decked it with my finest jewelry,And endowed it with my graceI've placed it oh so lovinglyInside an enevlope the color of  baby blueSealed with a kissI send this sweet Valentine to YOU!Mary Gonzalez (c)2002Happy St. Valentine's Day from the goldenpen
