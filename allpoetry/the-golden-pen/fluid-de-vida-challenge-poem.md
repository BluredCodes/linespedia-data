Scarlet liquid of life, 
Everflowing in silent waves, 
Clefting 'gainst the Brawny Muscle in vibrant fury, alive, 
Flowing constantly through crystal vessels, 
stretching across eternity.. 
Oh, cup of vitality, 
That revives the soul, 
With the taste of tomatoes, pepper and a dash of salt, 
Cocktail of vitality that makes us alive and whole.. 
We toast to thee!
