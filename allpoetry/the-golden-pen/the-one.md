---
title: "The One"
writer: "The Golden Pen"
slug: "the-one"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

In delight and ecstacy you make me rumble..
Darling, You're the one
Who makes me tremble
By just the utter of your name;

Love's eternal flame
Burns with a constancy
That puts the stars  to shame.

Non other than you causes a sensation..
My soul moves and quivers under your touch
Your love and devotion
Has conquered me with such
power and fierceness,
Making me melt under the puissance
Of your existance.


Mary Gonzalez (c) 2002
