title: "From This Day Forward"

Two golden bands entwined...
A token of a Love whispered long ago
That is now a promise Divine.

Two lives now interwoven,
A future, bright and rosy!
Man and woman
Coming together to share a life together,
Building their own home all snug and cozy..
From this day forward
Now and till forever..
Two lives become ONE!
Forever faithful one to the other..
Trusting and working hard
To keep the flame of love alive..
For richer and poorer
Now their lives have really begun..
Man and wife...
Until our lives are done.

Mary Gonzalez (c)
