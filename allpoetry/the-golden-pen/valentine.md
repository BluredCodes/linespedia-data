A winged cherub, blind

With golden arrows armed...

The cosmic planets all aligned

By Cupid's beauty, the entire world is charmed.

With a love that swoons the birds to sleep within their twiggy beds

And makes the mountains quake and fall unto the sea..

A love that paints the roses red...

And makes hearts sing with glee!

 

 

After a cruel winter... a reprieve..

The World welcomes back the Sun...

And rejoices in relief

There is love and joy in the horrizon

A day of friendship and romance

A day when hearts fall within Cupid's armorous trance.
