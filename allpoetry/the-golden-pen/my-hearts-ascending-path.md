My heart grew wings instantaneously. 
Like an infant bird 
My heart soared finally, 
Triumphant and undeterred, 
After many hours of discipline; 

Letting a higher voice within circumnavigate 
My heart flies at a steady pace, 
Confidant that Fate 
Will lead me to a better place. 
My heart used the pilotage of Faith.
