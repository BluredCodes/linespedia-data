Dearest lady, clad in velvet red, 
My cheeks are drunk of the wine from your ruby lips, Divine 
Oh, regal queen that reclines upon a satiny-cushioned bed, 
Queen of my heart, I humbly admire those rosy cheeks of thine 
and wish, oh how I wish that you are mine. 

Lady, Your body is a temple 
That stands in the midst of a moonlit garden, 
A pearly shrine, to pay homage to your soul 
With Daily pilgrimages by many lads and maidens 
Who honour you with deep reverence; 

Lady, night and day you torture me with your fragrance, 
Your exquisite yet intoxicating perfume that lingers in the air forever.. 
Sweet lady, with your pearly dewy smile and regal elegance.. 
Your essence poisons my heart with an immortal poisonous mystery 
Once you whispered your name to me.. 
But my memory fails..I can't remember.
You have enchanted me with your beauty.

Mary Aris(c)2002 ---2010
