To My Darling Valentine, Alexander:



 I want to be the One

Who you think of 
 The one you call upon when you need a friend
I want to be the one that you wish showering you with my love...
 The one that listens to your each and every woe
 And understands the nature of your soul.
I want to be the one whom you’d want to grow
Day by day together for the remainder of your days;


I want to be the one whom you want to hold your hand
The shoulder you reach out to...
The one who will stand
Behind you
wiping the tears away
When you’re sad or blue.

I'd like to be the one whom you crave for in the night,
The one that makes you sweat with salty pearls.
I want to be the one that makes you squeal with peals of delight,
The one who makes your mouth water for more...
I want to be that girl..
The one that you adore!


I want to be the one who makes your heart skip a beat or two
By mere utter of my name.
I want to be the one  that lights the fire
And shines your darkest days like a flame..
The one who kindles the candle  of your desire...
I want to be the one that you can hold
Forever dear and true..
I want to be the one purer than gold
Who will give all herself for no one but you...
I want to be the one who loves you through and through
In good times and in bad times...Always there for you!
