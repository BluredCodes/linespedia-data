Venus, the Goddess of love had an affair 
  And out of this was born the Rose 
  With a beauty and grace beyond compare 
  And an eternal fragrance 

  Envious of this child the other flowers 
  Kept their distance altogether 
  In a rage, Venus with all her powers 
  Decreed that her child was to be Queen forever 
  And made every bloom to bow and honor her daughter.. 

  By Royal comand the child of Venus was crowned Queen. 
  On her head was placed a tiara of dew-drops and pearls 
  Dressed in a gown of Royal Velvet green 
  
  She, daughter of a goddess.. 
  Reigned supreme with beauty, grace, and elogance 


  Mary Gonzalez (c)2002
