Tiger, Tiger

Tiger,Tiger all through the velvet blackness of this sensual night...
With a burning passion glowing bright...
Burning embers in your eyes..
You bring out all my passions primeval
With your fierceness....wrapped within a striped diquise.
Tiger,Tiger..
King of my jungle
With a wet and mighty appetite..
You bring your kitten to new sensual heights!
.....Mary Gonzalez (c)2001
