title: "A Lover's Symphony"

My whole being tingles!
My entire body quivers with delight...
Words are not enough
To describe the way in which my man touches me...
Just one utter of his voice 
Transforms my soul into a symphony!
My heart and soul become an orchestra..
In Rhythm my body sways to and fro
In harmony, cadence and harmony!
My soul rises into a crescendo
as invisible fingers stroke my body 
as if it were an electric guitar
