---
title: "No One Like YOU"
writer: "The Golden Pen"
slug: "no-one-like-you"

No one like you can sing to my heart, 
Soothing its heartaches with the chords of your love. 
No one like you could ever pry open apart 
This soul, revealing all the jewels hidden within my heart's treasure trove! 
Your kindness and your love were the key 
That unmasked my soul's face.... And with your love you've released all the beauty 
That was hidden beneath the surface!Dedicated to Alex Aris (c)2002
