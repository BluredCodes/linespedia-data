A little bit of you grows deep within my soul,
 Every day I care for it tenderly with loving care 
Nurturing the love so beautiful 
That grows within my heart...a Love you and I share.... 
A love I've come to cherish deeply with every given day 
A gift so special 
Is a sin to throw away!Written for my soul mate, Alexander with love from his other half on Valentine's Day!
