title: "Love..Under the Sea"

I've searched the ocean far and wide, 
I've raised storms and have stirred the tides! 
Still I couldn't find in the deep blue sea 
Someone who would compliment me! 
I've searched in the farthest corners of the ocean floor 
Hopelessly searching for a sweet amor 
Someone who would swim forever side by side 
Making love bubbles under the Sea; 


Suddenly out of the blue.. 
Swimming in the same pond as me 
A fish appeared so sweet..I'm so glad that I've found YOU! 
We will swim the whole ocean together 
On this fantastic journey 
Flapping our fins in unison 
Forever making love bubbles... 
Under the sea!
