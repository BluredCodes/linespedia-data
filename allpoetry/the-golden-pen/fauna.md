title: "Fauna"
writer: "The Golden Pen"

Lovely lady in your teal evening dress, 
With your floral tiara upon your golden head, 
Kiss Nature with your breath, 
And revive her child that once lay dead 
Upon a crystal coffin 
Lying On display, 
Mourned by all creatures..the wren, the hare,the robin 
Waiting for the day 
That you would come and with your love resurrect her once again. 
Oh maiden of hope, Come and renew the Earth 
With the colors of life, joy and rebirth! 


Mary Gonzalez (c)2002
