I should have known it was Destiny 
When he came knocking at my door that afternoon, 
I felt a mighty sesmic tremor deep within the core of my body! 
I caught my heart humming a familiar tune 
And skipping with delight. 

My face lit up brighter than the sun, 
Strangely my knees didn't quake 
And yet my stomache whirrled and went off like a gun. 
For God's Sake! 
I thought I had died that very day 
My head buzzed like a swarm of hungry bees 
The essence of ether made me sneeze 
It was then I realized I wasn't dead 
There was no funny business 
I had nothing to dread. 
Quite simply 
That very afternoon Destiny and I had a rendezvouz 
That very day I found my true love, my ultimate happiness!
