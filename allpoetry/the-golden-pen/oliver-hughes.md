Oliver Hughes
Oliver Hughes..
Can never EVER learn to tie his shoes!!!

Oliver Hughes,
Oliver Hughes,
His shoelaces are spaghetti strands,
Ties them together with buttered hands
Oliver Hughes,
Oliver Hughes..
I wouldn't want to be caught dead in his shoes!

Oliver Hughes
Oliver Hughes..
I saw that boy on the evening news..
Oliver Hughes,
Oliver Hughes...
He tripped over his running shoes 
Now poor Oliver is all black and blue! :o0
