The flaming torch that keeps the flames of life burning bright..
With glowing embers red throughout the course of the frigid night
The spark of electrical current flowing constantly
At the speed of light
Traversing each cell and veins of every mortal
Restoring, renovating vigorously
The human soul
With a potent strength and vigor that does strive
To maintain the Divine Factory of humanity alive..
With energizing passion,
The sheer combustion that furnishes creation
With the spark that keeps it going strong...
The incredible daily tonic that keeps the spirit supple and forever young.
