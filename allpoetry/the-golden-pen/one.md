---
title: "ONE"
writer: "The Golden Pen"
slug: "one"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/25821-ONE-by-The-Golden-Pen"
---

Two people... One heart whose blood flows from the same river,
Forming an eternal bond,
Pledging their love for one another
That no one will ever break...
Two souls that through eternity and beyond
Will keep each other company

Two halves forming a whole...
Perfection...the reincarnation of Adam and Eve
Purged of their sins from their FALL..
Two souls merging..body and soul
For Love's sake!


Mary Gonzalez
