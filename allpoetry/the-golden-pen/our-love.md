Our love was written by angels above 
In the clouds of heaven 
In the Language of Love 
The stars brag about us as they shine 
Revealing  our story to a hopeless world below 
Darling, our love is an omen.. 
A sacred sign 
That two souls were destined to come together 
To form a lasting bond that will last from now until forever! 


...Mary Gonzalez! (c)2001To Alexander whose love has made me whole!
