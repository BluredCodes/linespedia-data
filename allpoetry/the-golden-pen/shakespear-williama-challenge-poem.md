Shakespear, William 
With his golden pen 
Sat he by 
The edge of my bed, 
With vivid imagery 
He touched my head 
William Shakespear 
He fills my head 
With sonnetry 
And poetic images 
He takes me to Elizabethan journeys 
Back in time 
To the London theater 
He shows me hostile oceans 
and beautiful valleys, 
Showing me tragedies 
and humourous comedies... 
William Shakespear 
He takes me to England 
and flies me to a foreign land 
And he teaches me the trade of literature 
He fills my head with vivid poetry 
with words spun in gold, rich in imagery..written in fine penmenship.. 
and kissing my lips 
He fills me with grand inspiration... 
Making me write with much passion 
Turning this folly into my life's vocation! 


Mary Gonzalez
