---
title: "I'm your home!"
writer: "The Golden Pen"
slug: "im-your-home"
source: "AllPoetry"

You're inside every cell 
A part of me forever,
I am alive and well...
You live inside of me.....in every single fiber
Of my existence.
My heart beats in harmony, 
Pumping the
nourishing rivers of your love!
You feed my soul...
Your love is the fuel
That warms my soul..
Adding beauty and charm with your essence....
You fill every nook and cranny
With a love so rich and bold...
You live inside of me... 
You're everything I am...everywhere I tread...
Your love I'll dearly hold..
Forever and a day...
I have nothing to dread..
You're inside of me
And there you will be..forever!

Thanks to you..
Forever you will be
A part of me...
I am  your home...
Here's where you belong, no need to look any further
I'll hold you close to my heart..from now until forever!
