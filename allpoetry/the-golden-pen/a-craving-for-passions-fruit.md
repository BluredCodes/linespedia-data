title: "A Craving For Passion's Fruit"

Ripe and fresh off the vine, Suculently  moist! Divine! Juices flowing....flesh so tender... Wets my appetite, stirs my hunger! Seducing me so wildly Enticing all my senses! In anticipation My body, ravenous for instant satisfaction, Craves the Sweet Fruit with a ferocious passion! Mary Gonzalez (c)2001
