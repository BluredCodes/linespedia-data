title: "Awakening Eros"
writer: "The Golden Pen"

On many a moonlit night it seems

My heart said goodnight to my weary soul

Whilst my spirit, like a spider spun many interesting dreams..

That my whole body and conscience did enthrall






Asleep in such a restful slumber,

I spent my entire childhood in repose;

Whilst the whole earth rejoiced in Splendor

Enjoying nature's beauty and love's resplendence:




In hibernation I remained all winter long

Underneath a golden sky....

Until the morning I heard that ethereal song

Sung by a nymph in some distant lullaby,

Whose brilliance blinded me completely!

Making my eyelids flutter like mystified butterflies

On a sunny morning, glorifying the beauty of a dewy rose.

Refreshed after a long rest, at long last , Nature did awaken Eros!
