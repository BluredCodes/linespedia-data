Everybody has an interview with the Dark Knight;
Some sooner or later.
We all have a contract, legal and binding
And at the end of our lease
He'll come to collect what is rightfully his;
 
  
Fear not the Dark Knight
When he comes to claim what is His.
He'll stab you with his two-edge lance
And greet you with His icy kiss;
 
  
Some say he's a demon
Others say he's a caped crusader on a heroic campaign;
I say he is an angel of mercy,
So embrace him with a banner of courage
For he will guide you safely
Through the valley of your darkest hour.
 
  
There is a light at the end of the tunnel
Each cloud has a silver lining...
After each deluge there is a rainbow of hope.
The Dark Knight is the Lighthouse which will guide us safely
Through our darkest night
