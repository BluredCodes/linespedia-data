On the dawn of my Birthday
I do reflect upon my life
And I am grateful for each and every day
The good days as well as the ones of strife
My life has been a mixture of bad days and good
But mostly I remember 
The good days that I've been given
And I reflect upon the reason of my personhood
And then I look within my heart and find the answer:
I have come into this world to do good
If I can be of service to humanity
Let me do it now...for tomorrow i may not be here
I only come this way but once
And I must make each day a masterpiece!!!
