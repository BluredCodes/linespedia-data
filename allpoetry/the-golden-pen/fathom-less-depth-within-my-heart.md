My heart is an ocean'Tis seven-thousand leagues in depthAnd when love's ship sails in motionIt does create waves of magnetic strengthMy heart is like the sea---a womanOf extra-ordinary beauty, allure and mysteryOnly heaven knows for certainWhat treasures lay hiddenIn the endless depth of passion   Quite a menagerie my heart does holdWhich throughout the ages have sunk below the ocean floor'Tis spell-bounding what an acquatic tale can be toldAbout the boundless wealth in storeFrom my heart's bottomless resevoirDedicated to the Heart of a Woman which is as deep as any oceans!...thegoldenpen
