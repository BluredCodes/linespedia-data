---
title: "Love's possession"
writer: "The Golden Pen"
slug: "loves-possession"
source: "AllPoetry"

My Beloved is mine
And I am his
Together we will be
Sharing love and bliss
 Together...throughout eternity.

Forever our love does shine
Like a diamond in the sky
Illuminating our love divine
Throughout the heavens where angels fly;

My beloved is mine
And I am his....
Together we’ll live eternally
Partaking forever of love’s sweet kiss,
We will live most happily 
In perfect wedded bliss.
