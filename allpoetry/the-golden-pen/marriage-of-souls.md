title: "Marriage of Souls"

Two souls sharing one Destiny, Sharing the same Cosmic Dream.. Two hearts, Beating in unison and harmony, Pumping the same Love between; Two sets of eyes Looking at the World through the same lens... Turning the mundane grey images of life into a colorful paradise; Two sets of hands Holding each other tight, uplifting one another Throughout Life's many adversities and  plights; Two seperate entities... Joined and bound; Two players coming together Forming a team, Trying to bulid a Life of joy and beauty Out of the love they've found. (c)2001 Mary GonzalezWritten in honor of my love, Alex Aris!
