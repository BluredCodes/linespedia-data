Life is full of endless beauty.
Like no other jewel of divine grace.
Life is full of radiance...
Life is the supreme jewel of the human race.
It is the Jewel that we must learn to wear
With pizzass and ingenuity;

No other jewel can you find...
No other jewel can outshine
The puissance and magic of this precious jewel so precious and rare.
There's no substitute
For life is irriplaceable.
Wear it with your birthday suit
and you shall be irristable!


Mary Gonzalez
