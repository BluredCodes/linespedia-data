title: "For the Love of Alexander"

He comes to me In the warmth of sunshine, Like a ray of light so heavenly; Streaming with Love He dazzled me, dousing my essence With Liquid Love raining down me from Heaven's trove; My life hasn't been the same My days and nights were darker Before he came, God heard my prayer And sent an angel towards me By the name of Alexander. For the Love of Alexander My heart unveils Revealing its true essence.. For once not afraid to bare Its true self, it is free forever Flying like a bird above the heavens For the Love of Alexander I am whole He has come to me with a love that forever Will embrace and protect my soul From all that is preverse, dark and evil.. For the love of Alexander I am grateful and will return that love a thousand fold. Mary Gonzalez (c) 2001 Dedicated to my boyfriend, Alex Aris
