Dancing in mid-air
Underneath a gilded sky,
You and I draw near
with  burning embers in our eyes;

Together we glide
Beyond the moon...
Two silhouettes that side by side
 move to a heartfelt tune
Of passion and delight.

Giving ourselves completely to one another
On this special and exotic night
When we pledged our love forever..
Engaging in this dance of intimacy.

 Each year we celebrate our Anniversary
Dancing cheek to cheek... Entwined together
With love, romance and our vow to spend eternity
In the company of one another.
