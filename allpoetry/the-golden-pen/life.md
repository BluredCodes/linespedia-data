~ LIFE~~


Life is one big motion picture.
We are its academy-award nominated actors and actresses
And the Lord is our Director.

We are cast into many different roles
And day by day we play life’s many scenes
Life is one motion picture
With many different characters
and diverse plots and situations.
Each of us must learn to play our parts skillfully
For we only get one take.

Life is a long-playing series
With many types of situations
If we only play our parts convincingly...
We might even win the Oscars!
