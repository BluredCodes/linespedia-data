A poem is many things, a wonderous concuction, 
A painting done in an array 
Of different hues in a pallet of style and diction. 
What makes a poem a 'poem' you say? 
It is quite difficult, You see... 
Any body can be a poet if he takes pen in hand 
And out of the studio of his heart paints words into poetry. 
True poetry, my friend.. 
Is but a menagery 
Of emotions felt a while ago, painted on a page 
Inspired by the Muse with a fury 
captured in bright imagery 
in a kaleidoscope of lanquage!
