Today I dedicate this heart of mine
To guide you, Love 
I plead you, Valentine
Consider this poor heart of mine
For it is fragile like a dove
It can break quite easily
Today I cover you with sweet and tender hugs and kisses
Remember...
Solitude is cruel
And the nights are cold in deep December
Only this poor heart of mine can keep you company
And warm your frigid soul
This great passion that I bear
Is vaster than the blue horizon
And deeper than the deepest ocean
Today, Love I render this poor heart of mine to you, dear
You’ll find me day and night here
Dedicating my whole life to you
With nothing in return save your happiness and ecstacy
