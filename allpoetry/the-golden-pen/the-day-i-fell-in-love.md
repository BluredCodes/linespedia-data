I should have known it in my heart, 
My body gave me sesmic warning 
Yet right from the start 
I knew this was leading up to something! 

I felt my heart do summer saults in mid air! 
My stomache unleashed the butterflies 
It hit me like a bolt of lightning when I caught his stare 
It was then I realized 
He's the One who was sent from above, 
The best day in my life has been..the day I fell in Love.The greatest day in my life has been the Day I fell in love with  Alex Aris! :O)
