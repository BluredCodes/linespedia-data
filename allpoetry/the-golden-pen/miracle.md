---
title: "Miracle"
writer: "The Golden Pen"

source: "AllPoetry"

You are the Gift Life has given me... 

A wonderful Jewel that adorns my heart with joy and 
glee! 

More radient than the sun, 

You shine in me and make me sparkle! 

I treasure You forever and beyond 

You are the Gift that God has awarded me... 

I will keep you in my  Treasure Chest 

And guard you with all my heart and soul 

For You are my Miracle... 

My Love, My Prince...my Shining Star..My prized Jewel!This is dedicated to the LOVE OF MY LIFE, 
My Wonderful and Precious Boyfriend, Alexander Frederick Anthony Aris!
