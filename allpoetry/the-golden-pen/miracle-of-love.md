I was absolutely dead until This immortal creature Performed a Miracle! Touched by the light, I was reborn by this wonder-healer! Breathing in my nostril, He dispensed something magical..vital...quite potent stuff! Curious, I asked him to reveal the ingredients of this medicine. In a whisper, he echoed in my ear...'my child, it's called, 'LOVE' Transformed by this miracle potion I became animated...alive... and dancing on the wings of doves! I never knew such joy, strength, power, vigor until This heavenly messenger from above Breathed in me the True Essence of God...This Miracle called Love! Mary Gonzalez (c) 2001To: My Darling Alex Aris with my heart and soul
