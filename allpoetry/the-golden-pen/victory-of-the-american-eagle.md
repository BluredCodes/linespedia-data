Out in the distance
The American Eagle glides
Triumphantly across the sky
With the Spirit of patriotic valor..
His mission...to establish liberty
and defend the cause of justice;

At a glance
I see the mighty Eagle passing by
At an alarming altitude
I see him returning safely home
With an air of poise  and a fierce  attitude
He sits victoriously upon that purple-mountain peak
Savoring the sweet taste of victory
His prey clutched tightly within each talon!

~~In Memoriam We Honor Those Whose Blood Was Shed~~

They were young servicemen of twenty-two
And on their suits they wore Red Badges of Courage.
Young men and women who gave their souls for glory
In the name of freedom; in the name of victory.

They spilled their guts over Germany
And in the havoc of the Vietnam War
They risked their lives in the Persian War
And Death came knocking upon their door.

Today we honor such heroic deeds of valor
Though they are long gone, they will not be forgotten
As long as the American flag waves over a country of Democracy
Their names will shine in splendor–
In Memoriam we honor our valiant servicemen
In our hearts there is a white carnation
That will never fade.
We will gaze up to the heavens
and see their faces amongst the constellations
and the fifty stars which represent this great Nation.
