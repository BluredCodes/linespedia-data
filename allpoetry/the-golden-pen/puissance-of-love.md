---
title: "Puissance of Love"
writer: "The Golden Pen"
slug: "puissance-of-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

The power of love is so bold 
That no mere mortal can break its chain. 
Love is the strongest emotion in the universe. 
Love never goes out of style. 
Love is eternal. 
It is very assiduous. 
Love is not prejudice. 
Love is not blind, nor is it condescending ... 
Love is the greatest gift of all! 
  
  
  

Mary Gonzalez (c) 2001
