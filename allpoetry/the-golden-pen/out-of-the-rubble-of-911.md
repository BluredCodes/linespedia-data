Amidst the calm, Indian summer morning, 
Calamity struck in less than one hour, 
The whole world was in mourning.. 
Twin brothers fell..the horror! 
The chaos and confusion.. 
The blood loss..the Terror! 
From within a distance the enemy rejoiced, laughing with pleasure.. 
Ahhhh! Victory! Defeat of America! Success!!!! 
The sudden thrill of death.. 
Blood smeared on his hands... 
The coward hid safe beneath the earth 
As millions of innocent people died in this Tragedy.. 

A nation stood still 
Everywhere brother consoling brother.. 
A sea of American flags furled everywhere 
To show the enemy 
That a Suicide bomber or two cannot maime or kill 
The spirit of brotherhood... democracy.
America, like a great phoenix rises out of the ashes 
And lives forever!In Tribute to the victims of September 11
There lives won't be forgotten!
The world will defeat the one responsible for such an act of violence!
