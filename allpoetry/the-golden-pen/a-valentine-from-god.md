The snow fell down like silent tear drops 
From a scarlet sky, 
Covering with a white blanket a field of snowdrops. 
A sudden chill frosted my eyelashes and made me realize 
The beauty and wonder of nature 
On this one cold, snowy afternoon in mid January. 

My cheeks glowed a crimson colour 
As I felt the snowflakes descending softly upon my face, 
I saw a hand divine in this ancient splendour... 
As I felt God's pre-season Valentine 
of creamy Chantilly lace, 
Falling silently like angels' wings kissing my nose 
and covering my head like a white woolen bonnet.. 
Winter has this magical effect, 
going unnoticed by passers-by 
Who neglect to see the real beauty of God's design 
And fail to see the lovely picturesque scene of a winter's day...
A Valentine from God!
