title: "The Magic of Your Smile"

Enchanting! Radient! 
A beacon of joy...
A Gift..heaven sent
To light the path of my heart; Dear boy..
Your smile shines upon me like the bright morning star
Lighting the darkness of my  soul
Smile, Keep smiling , Love, with mouth open ajar..
Your face beams brightly and beautiful...
Enchants and enthralls me...
With a beaming unique style
and a power so bold and wonderful..
At first glance I simply lose my identity!
