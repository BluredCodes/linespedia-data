My heart overflows with love; it pours like tea, Onto a dainty teacup, Resting on a lady bug's lap! My heart, a symphony... Sings songs of praise to my dapper, young chap; My soul...a butterfly With gilded wings a flutter, Gracefully glides on velvety cushions Entertaining its audience With its regal minuet! Nature rejuvinates itself by such a performance.. Love's grand repertoire... Aphrodite's 'ROMANCE', Live...On Eros's Golden Theater! (c)2001 The Golden Pen
