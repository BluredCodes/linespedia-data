title: "Obsession...."
writer: "The Golden Pen"

My mouth waters with anticipation.. 
My body quivers with desire 
At the thought of your luscious body...my ultimate temptation. 
My flesh perspires 
I long to taste your lips against my own. 
Savoring the essence of your breath 
Completely melts this heart of stone 
To live without you is sheer torture 

Your smile is the sun that warms my days 
Immersing me with its sunny rays, 
Your hair is the silk that carresses my naked skin. 
Your tongue is the fruit that provokes me into sin 
Your body is the temple and the shrine 
That keeps me there in adulration.... 
Your presence is sheer heaven, 
Without you I will simply wither 
You're my obession.. 
My whole being craves you twenty-four-seven!
