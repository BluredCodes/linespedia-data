title: "The Christmas Waltz"

Another wonderful year of love,
Another year of passion and delight...
I look back and remember 
Our first rendezvous...our first hello!
 The two of us exchanged our love that deep December,
Exchanging tokens of love and deep affection...
Was it destiny that brought us together...
Or a Blessing from above?
Non-the-less, on that night so long ago..
Music played...a sweet serenade of love..
Our hearts danced with each other...
Hands entwined, bodies set in motion..
Our tongues twirling to the tune of our devotion,
Sealed with a kiss...Our love began.
Two people...woman and man
Found a love like no other
Through space and time..
We, two, danced in rhythm and in rhyme..
Lost forever in intimacy...
Words too few to comprehend..
The politics of this romance..
I thank the stars forever
For uniting us, My Darling!
Tonight I thank the heavens above..
With gratitude and thanksgiving...
Tonight as we celebrate our anniversary 
Let us seal this bond we formed
With the sweet kiss of ecstasy
and the wine of dulce amour!
