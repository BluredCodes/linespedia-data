A lit birthday cake all ablaze,
A room full of presents.
One look upon your face
melts my heart and I thank the stars above for the blessing of your presence, 
Love....
Everyday I thank the Stars and your Mum, too...
For the priviledge and wonder of you;
Everyday of my life there is love and joy,
it's true...
Since I've known you, Birthday Boy,
My life is sweeter, richer and by far...
A better life thanks to you...
Each night I thank my lucky star
and make a secret wish for you..
That every year of your life you'd be doubly blessed, too..
This is my Birthday wish for YOU..
May God give you the love and joy
You have given me
And make your days and nights forever
joyful and happy!
