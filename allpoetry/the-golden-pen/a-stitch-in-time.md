A Stitch In Time
Sitting here, needle in hand
I attempt to make a masterpiece
I pull the cloth tightly against the band
And thread the needle with ease
In my mind i’ve created the perfect tapestry
A beautiful design
of intricate pattern and artistry
My hands, dainty and small
commence its’ task
And carefully pulls with great care the needle
And begins to embroider with yellow flask
some design that is soft, femine and beautiful
In time I embroider a red, red rose
With a kelly green stem 
Oh!! I am out of white floss
A fine crafter I am...
I run out of material
Before my project is done!
