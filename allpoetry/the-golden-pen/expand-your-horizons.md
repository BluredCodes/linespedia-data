Expand your horizons
And don't hesitate
Express your potentials
As much as you can.

There are so many avenues that lead to success.
With the adequate skills and knowledge you can possess
The kingdom of  glory
And pure happiness
It just takes strength and courage to progress.
Don't be discouraged if things go astray
Just learn to laugh all your problems away.
Try and you will see
The road to victory
Go ahead and follow your dream.
Express all your knowledge for the whole world to see...
Just move on and on; 
Don't delay
Expand your horizon
And taste the sweet wine of victory!
