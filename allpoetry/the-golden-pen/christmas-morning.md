Dressed in whimsical clothing, Nature wakes us once again
Unto an enchanting morning..
Beckoning to all children..
Young and old to come pouring
Down the stairs this Happy Day!

Underneathe her petticoat, the Christmas tree reveals
What treasures Santa Claus brought on Christmas Eve.
All throughout the living room the sound of squeals
From little John and Steve
With eyes ajar and mouth wide open
They dive underneathe the Tree;

And Father and Mother, arm in arm, look on
As wrapping papers, ribbons, tags fly
And gladden hearts smile with happiness...What fun!
Ah, the wonder...Oh, the joy!
Christmas has begun,
Mother serves hot cocoa! Merry Christmas, every one.

Mary Gonzalez (c)2001
