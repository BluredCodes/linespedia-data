Dance with me in the moonlight...
Underneath the starry skies.
Let's delight in dreams that come  our way
In the stillness of the night.
Within my lover's womb
I am safe and sound.
Oh, cover me inside your womb, Lover!
And hide me from the world  so I cannot be found!
Only lost inside you forever
I hover between joy and safety.
I swim within your foamy oceans
So vast and deep,
As I breath in the air of your essence...
Our tongues dance together
An intimate dance
Twriling round and round
To the rhytm of passion and romance.
And I can hear the monotonous sound
Your heart's very own tune;
From the comfort of your womb, I don't ever want to leave
But linger
Let me dwell in there forever!
No, I don't ever wish to see the light of day...
But revel in your protective clasp
Whilst I sit there helpless like an infant on your lap
As you rock me gently to a sensuous sleep.
