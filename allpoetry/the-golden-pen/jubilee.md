I   celebrate this sacred new beginning,  Love.... 
                                        So shiny, new, and avant-garde. 
                                      I celebrate and thank the stars above 
                                          With lyres, flutes, and bards, 
                                 I celebrate in grand style ...The Eden of our Love, 
                               Born of a crossbreed between friendship and desire; 

                            Conceived of a single seedling of amity within Venus's womb, 
                          Our love took shape immediately on that blessed September morn. 
                              Nurtured with God’s sustenance, slowly, it began to bloom 
                  First a seedling, then a green, young sprout, and then finally a fully blossomed  Rose. 

                                         We two formed a sacred alliance, 
                                               Ever since Day One. 
                                        I hope that in a thousand years hence 
                                   Our Love will grow stronger and linger on and on 
                                               As when it first began.
