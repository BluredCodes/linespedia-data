Love's spirit proceeds the bride and groom,                       
  As it lingers there, a phantom of sweetness.                         With Sweet aloes and precious myrrh                         It fills the essence and aroma of Paradise.                         Fair the true beauty of such tender bride                         As her Father unveils the true essence of                         innocence                         Before mens'  eyes.                         The gallant young groom cannot help hide                         His desire to set his hands on his well                         -earned prize.                         What a precious sight this is,                         That before God's angels and men's view                         The couple utter their 'I do's'                         And seal their nuptials with a precious kiss                         Oh, what a lovely token of true love..                         Love that can only be conceived by God                         above!                             Mary Gonzalez (c) 2000
