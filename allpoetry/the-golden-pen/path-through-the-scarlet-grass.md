In the past we have taken the erroneous trail 
                Leading to nowhere in particular 
                But in endless travel 
                We have failed 
                To forge ahead upon our journey; 
  
  

               How my feet ache! 
               I am weary! 
               Treading forever through miles and miles, 
                Afraid of making a mistake. 
               How long will it be until 
              We find the one,  true  path? 
  
  
  
  
  
               Will it take us forever to discover 
                The path through the Scarlet Grass? 
  
  
                Never fear, Love, never fear 
               For the end of our weary journey is near... 
               We’ve discovered one another... 
                We are half way there!
