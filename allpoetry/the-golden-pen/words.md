Words, Words, Words, 
Dancing 'round my head.... 
Flying like a flock of birds 
waiting to be read. 

Words, Words, Words... 
They come to life, I dread! 
Conceived in silence unspoken and unheard 
In the poet's deep imaginings words  imbed, 
Coming to life instantly with each flow of the Author's pen! 

Mary Gonzalez (c)2001
