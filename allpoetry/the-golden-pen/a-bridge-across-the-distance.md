---
title: "A Bridge Across the Distance"
writer: "The Golden Pen"
slug: "a-bridge-across-the-distance"
source: "AllPoetry"

There is no distance when two hearts connect.
In spite the distance in touch they keep
Whether via phone, snail mail or Internet.
There's a bridge that bonds them though rivers deep.

In spite of boundaries,borders, or space,
Their kindred hearts shall in time rondezvous.
Countries all unite in Cyberspace
Love will form a bridge across the oceans blue.

When two souls come together
Though they live far, no matter the distance;
Love forms a lighted bridge across life's river.
Countries all unite in Cyberspace.

Love forms a bridge which fuse together..
Souls by distance torn the whole World over.
