title: "The Aquamarine"

The ocean reeks inside of you, 
The spitting Image of Posidon! 
I look upon your surface of greenish/blue 
And see the depth of the ocean 
Clear as a mirror 
Revealing the mysteries of ocean floor 
Reflected upon the blue horizon 
With reverence and awe I listen 
To the sound of the waves against your shore 
And behold your ancient beauty 
Instilling in me a calmness so soothing and serene. 


Deep blue and crystaline, 
Your beauty radiant, eminating love and peace, 
With a breath-taking sheen 
Giving off vibrations that will never cease 
to amaze the wearer of this aquatic stone 
Engulfing the wearer with a wave of mystic blue 

The mermaids know of this precious metal, 
Cultivated under the sea 
in ancient aquatic mines that no mortal 
has dared to reach this precious commodity 

Guarded by dolphins, 
The goldfish miners dig all day 
Wagging their irredescent  fins 
being careful of a foray 

The ocean shines in different hues 
From rich deep green 
To shades of blues.. 
Radiation from deep below the ocean's surface, 
The sea reveals its secret... 
Spitting pounds and pounds of aquamarine 
Unto the seashore bucket after bucket!
