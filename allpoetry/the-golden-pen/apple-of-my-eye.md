A lazer light displayHaunts the sky...Transforming night to day;In the spotlights the secret image lies!In awe the Earth strains its neck to viewThe wonderful display of lights above.My heart smiles as I project the wonderous image of you..The apple of my eye...The heavens danceMy eyes reflect the beauty and elegance..The World entranced..enchanted and romanced;I celebrate the Wonder of you,The one I truly loveWith a lazer show display for all to view..The world below can witness from above..An enlightened skyThe One who makes my soul shine bright..And my heart twinkle with delight..The Apple of my Eye!Mary Gonzalez (c) 2001Dedicated to the Apple of My Eye...my boyfriend, Alex Aris!
