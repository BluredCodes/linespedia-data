To my Soul mate 

When I look at you it’s as if looking in a mirror... 
In your eyes 
I see my interrior.. 
Your presence has made me realize.. 
You’ve helped to shape the woman I’ve become.. 
You complete me; 
Together we are whole.. 
Yin/yang..inseperable 
Throughout eternity! 
You and I forever,
Together... two souls becoming One!
