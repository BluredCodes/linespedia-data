True Love 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------


  



Love was born of innocence 
Whose only begotten seed 
Was sown exclusively 
In heaven’s garden. 

Love is a luminescent star, 
Dazzling in shining splendor. 
Love is bright...its shining splendor leaves one in awe. 
Love abides by its own law. 
Love governs with equity... 
It holds no boundaries. 
Love is not grudging nor holds rancor. 
When love is injured, love does pardon. 
Love is slow to anger 
And quick to embrace. 
Love is the flame 
That warms the heart with joy. 
  
  

Mary Gonzalez (c) 2001
