Butterfly
Dance the dance of freedom's song
You flutter your wings and away you fly
Your wings of gold are dainty but strong
Like a beautiful ballerina you do your plies
And off you go to blue, blue skies
Butterfly
You dainty dame
You add beauty to this earth
Without you life won't be the same
For your presence adds grace and lustre to the universe
