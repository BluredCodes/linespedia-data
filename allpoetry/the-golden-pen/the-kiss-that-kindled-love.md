title: "The Kiss that Kindled Love"

Awake, Love..
   I stole Love’s first kiss
   From the angelic treasure trove;

   Guilty as sin,
   Stirred from within
   By passion’s exotic hand!

   Gazing at my beloved’s eyes
   I awoke him with a gentle  kiss
   Purged out of paradise?
   I dream not of...
   My sin was mainly the quest for bliss!



   In a blissful dream my lover smiles at me..
   Longing to taste again the sacred wine...
   The stolen loot of ecstacy
   That kindles love’s exotic  flame

   This is not sin,
   That renders to love
   A joy that swells from within...
   But a gift of love, Divine!
  


   Be merciful, Heaven..
   Put me not to shame,
   I will not steal again!
   Do not exile me from Paradise..
   For it is here that my beloved lies.
					
   Behold, my beloved wakes unto me
   Face all shining with delight
   Tell me, what sin lies in this...
   But to brush the cobwebs of last night
   With the sweet wake-up call of  morning’s first love’s kiss!
