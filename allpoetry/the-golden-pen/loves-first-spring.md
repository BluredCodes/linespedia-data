---
title: "Love's First Spring"
writer: "The Golden Pen"

You've enriched me forever with love's morning dew! 
Like December's first snow my soul was virgin and pure 
But the warmth of your heart gave me a love that endures! 
Love, You are my first! 
Your lips were the only lips that like an eternal river has quenched my thirst.. 
Your body the only one that has come close to mine 
Your voice was the first song my heart heard sing.. 
You are my one and only True Valentine... 
My soul lay in deep winter 
Awakened from an eternal slumber 
By the warmth of Love's first spring...
