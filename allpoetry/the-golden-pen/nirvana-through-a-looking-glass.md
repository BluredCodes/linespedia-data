title: "Nirvana Through a Looking Glass"

Like a seagull my heart soars. 
Lifted on light cushions of sheer love, 
It hovers upon distant shores 
Watching the dolphins dancing on the waves , harsh and rough; 
Your spirit is a lighthouse which guides my flight, 
Illuminating the darkness...a beacon of love that takes me to where you are. 
You are my haven that shelters me from the coldest night... 
By the light of your eyes my path is lit far brighter than the North Star; 

Your smile is the rainbow 
That colors my day. 
I love the way it radiates a multi-colored glow. 
I love to slide off this celestial slide like a child at play. 

Your soul is my horizon... 
Wide and spacious, Love, 
Your heart is warmer and wider than the sun, 
Your voice, softer than the cooing of the dove; 


Your body is my vessel 
Which carries me, 
Setting sail 
Upon this cold, chaotic journey 
Through the crystal waters of your devotion. 

I lie on this picture-perfect beach 
Watching you make the heavens kiss the sea. 
And prove once and for all that no star is ever out of reach!
