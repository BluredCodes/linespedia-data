title: "Exchange of Hearts"
writer: "The Golden Pen"

Inside me it's your heart I wear 
beating joyously within my chest 
pulsating forever 
eminating within my soul...all of you at your very 
best; 

You in turn wear eternally 
My own heart within 
Feeling my love internally 
Flowing through your veins 
I in you 
and you in me 
Together we remain 
a part of each other 
for an eternity 
We live for one another 
vowing to take care 
Of the loving part 
That dwells within one another 
With the common goal 
Of one day uniting these two hearts 
That when merged together finally make a whole. 
Mary Gonzalez (c)2001 


Dedicated to my boyfriend, Alexander Frederick Anthony Aris
