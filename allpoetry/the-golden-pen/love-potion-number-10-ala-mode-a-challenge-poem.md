Tonight I'll whip you up something sweet, 
My Lil British Love, divine... 
You'll smack your lips with my perfect dish, good enough to eat, 
It'll taste like strawberries with cream mixed in sweet zindfendl wine. 
For this British Dish I'll need: 
A pound of lip-smacking strawberries, 
A bottle of sweet pink Zindfendl wine, 
A box of confectioner's sugar, a pound of chocolate covered cherries 
A lovely doily from an old Valentine... 
A crystal serving dish from Grandmother's cupboard and a silver spoon. 
Half a gallon of chocolate and French Vanilla ice-cream 
Heart's Desire....just a teaspoon! 
I mash the strawberries with my lips and fold in a half a cup of kisses, 
Then measure and pour the wine into 10 different dishes, 
While I cream the milk and sugar and turn it into whipping cream 
Then pour the wine into the cream, folding in the chocolate covered cherries! 
Not forgetting the teaspoon of Heart's Desire. 
In the serving crystal dishes I pour this sweet confection, 
Topped with chocolate and vanilla Ice-cream and a dollop of whipped cream.. 
MMMMMMM, My Cheri Amour...this sweet concuction of mine 
Is called, "Love Potion Number 10 A La Mode"...It is quite delicious! 
You will simply smack your lips as you lick away the dish, 
And then drift off to an amorous and passionate sort of dream!
