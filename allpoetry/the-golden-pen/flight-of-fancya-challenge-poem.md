My soul, like incence filled with silence 
As I listened to the voice deep within me 
In a trance 
I heard the spirit sing its song of liberty 
Casting all my fears and shadows 
into the rivers of tranquility. 
My eyes projected green meadows, 
bedecked with primroses and poppeys. 
A voice like a roaring water fall 
loudly whispered to me... 
Soar, Soar like the Ealge beyond the sycomore tree. 
Take flight,my child and travel 
Where the horizon meets the sea! 

Mary Gonzalez (c)2002
