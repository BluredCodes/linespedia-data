---
title: "Beautiful"
writer: "The Golden Pen"
slug: "beautiful"

My heart is an open canvas 
And my emotions, an Artist's pallette 
My soul takes a deep, deep breath 
As it captures your image against a golden sunset. 
My Darling, you are absolutely an inspiration... 
Your beauty leaves speechless 
The most articulate of poets 
My love, You are truly beautiful....a priceless Masterpiece! 


Mary Gonzalez (c)2001
