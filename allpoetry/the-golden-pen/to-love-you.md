title: "To Love You"

The greatest joy,
The deepest ecstacy..
You've given me to fully enjoy..
Unconditionally 
You've given your heart and soul
And immersed me in the magic of your affection

Like an artist, you've painted my world
With the colors of love....
You've made this girl
Shine brighter than the sun above;

To love you, boy
Is my greatest task...
With great honor and dedication..
I will bathe you in a greater love, with a golden flask..
I'm going to devote the rest of my life
To give you back what you've given.
To become your wife
Is an honor that I dream of
To Love you is a Promise
I will keep forever..
Sealed with a sweet and solemn kiss.
