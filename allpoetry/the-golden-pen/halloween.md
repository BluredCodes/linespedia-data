When the cool, crisp air of October
Tosses the harlequin leaves around...
When there is a certain eerie chill in the atmosphere..
You can be certain..Halloween is near!
The skeletons rattle their dried-up bones
In a ghostly, macabre dance
In the graveyard you won't stand a ghostly chance
For the ghouls and imps and all the Devil's cohort
Party till the break of dawn,
Gorging on all sorts of gory treats!

The moon casts an eerie fluorescent glow
Against the velvet black of night
And from above you can listen 
To the cackle of a hundred witches on their Samhain Flight!

When you are greeted by the florescent grin
Of a creepy Jack-O-lantern,
And as you trample through the night
Your mind plays evil tricks on you!
Every tree is a phantom
Trying to capture you,
And the wind becomes a sinister spook's howl!
Your heart races upon the hooting of the Great Horned owl!
On this scary,scary night in deep October...
Best be on your guard tonight...
Against all unsightly sounds and sights you may encounter...
It's best to not be seen...
Beware! Beware! Beware of this horrible night
