Darling... As we, hand in hand embark together on love's journey... Rest assured, my love...You won't walk alone on distant shores Or be a stranger in a strange country... We will sail together As we venture unto this new frontier. The journey is long and endless And there may be gales, turbulence and a few delays But, my love, our love is boundless And I am sure you will rave about it for the rest of your days; I will sail with you upon love's grand adventure And share everything there is My Heart's golden key to you I will render And grace your countenance with Love's most sweetest kiss. I will walk with you a thousand miles And share with you all my joys and woes. We will go through sunshine and gales But through it all our love will grow. And in the end we shall sit and reminsice Hand in hand in matching rocking chairs With joy and glee and happiness Knowing that through it all we've managed to stay together ~Mary Gonzalez~ (c)2001
