On a starry night I look above And count a gazillion, celestial luminaries. Each star raves and brags  upon him whom I love And counts all his virtues up for me... Like a heavenly abacuss The sum of which makes whole new galaxies Which live within my heart. The celestial bodies aren't big enough to chart All of my beloved's essence.. He alone is his own infinity Of  charm, wisdom and goodness! Mary Gonzalez (c) 20002000,
