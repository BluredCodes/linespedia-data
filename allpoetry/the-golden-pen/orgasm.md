Mount Olympus errupted...
In a most erotic way!
Earth and sky moved, interrupted
By the sonic vibrations unleashed today,
Awakening and enticing the gods with pleasure
Moved by this delightfully splendid display
Of heat,motion, and sky-rocketing sesmic vibration...
the Gods were pleased in every measure!
