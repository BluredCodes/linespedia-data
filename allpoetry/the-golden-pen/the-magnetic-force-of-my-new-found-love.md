You have made me look upon life
With new eyes
You have removed upon my life all strife
And filled my soul with so much wonder and surprise
That now my whole being sings in harmony;
 
  
You have made me whole
My days are so much brighter than the sun
Your love has healed my entire soul
And now I am a whole new person.
 
  
Thanks for making me see the truth beyond the lies
Thanks for showing me the true beauty which lay hidden
Inside the darkness of my soul 
You have brought out the very best in me...
Now my soul emanates such wondrous love and energy
That it empowers whole entire cities
With the energy of your love
 
  
You have changed my whole life perspective...
You have given me a new attitude
Now I am more receptive
To all the avenues of love
Your love has warmed me up inside
That it has thawed my heart completely...
I am no longer the bitter person I used to be
You have filled my life with everything that is good
 
  
Your love flows through me
Like a radiant energy field...like some energizing force
And it surrounds me with good vibes
This new-found love seeps through my veins
And allows my life to run a smooth and constant course
My life soars to new and electrifying heights
Thanks to your love , my life is now a fountain
Of happiness and light
 


The Golden Pen (c) 2006Dedicated to my husband, Alex Aris.  The day we met my heart thawed completely and I knew that he was the ONE to set my destiny in motion!
