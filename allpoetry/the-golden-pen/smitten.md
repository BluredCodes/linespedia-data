It started as a friendship, nothing more.. 
I still remember the first conversation we shared.. 
As time went by, I simply began to adore, 
The way he laughed, the way he cared; 


Like a sharp knife plunging in; 
Deep burning in my heart...Desire never to part, 
Holy Obsession.. almost like a deadly sin.. 
Pledging my love for him.. eternally... 


I made my move... 
Exposing my deep and open wound for him to heal, 
Risking rejection, my desire for him to approve 
Took all the guts inside my soul..
