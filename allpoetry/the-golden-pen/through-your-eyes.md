title: "Through Your Eyes"

I never thought it possible 
To come face to face with my Destiny... 
I now understand its oracle... 
"A bright future I forsee". 
Gazing upon the twin looking glass, 
I see you and me together throughout eternity 
Engaged together in a cosmic, infinate dance 
A marriage of two souls... 
A fusion of hearts merged together as One.. 
The consummation of a Sacred couple 

Mary Gonzalez (c)2001
