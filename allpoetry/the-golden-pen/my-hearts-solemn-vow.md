title: "My Heart's Solemn Vow"
writer: "The Golden Pen"

A pulsating heart of gold 
That speaks your name with every beat; 
A pair of loving arms to hold 
And lift you when you stumble and envelope you like a sheet; 
A Spirit, lively and bold 
That will comfort and become your eternal friend.... 
An unquenchable passion to consume your loneliness 
An eternal pledge, tinged with Love...a Love that will not bend 
But endures through every wind and gale with fierceness... 
This I Promise YOU!
