The barbarians are at the gate. The demons want to detonate
And hold our feet to the fire of money burning.
The holy roman empire and the tang dynasty, 
My friends, this is history,
A pendulum back swing through paradise.
As the spoiled seal their minds away air tight
In plastic wrapped pride, attempting to preserve
What was prestine, only now feels dead inside.
If only my wallet wasn’t so full of blood
I wouldn’t be swimming in the streets
And tumbling with the weeds
Of a ghost town.
