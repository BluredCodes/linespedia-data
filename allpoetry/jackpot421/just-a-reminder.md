Wires form our currents, born from nature’s green
Nurtured living urethane gardens and concrete know everything machines 
So calculating. As natural as our creations, we know everything
In our info nation tech ideology speed dialing American dreams
While sleeping at our cubicles
Waking to the machine gun attack, to disrupt your attention deficient
Of an instant message, a memo, and fax

*JUST A REMINDER*
1. Plug in on Sunday
2. Spirituality is not exempt from tax
NOTICE: Lines 1 and 2 are never subject to change.

Here’s a questionnaire to help us help you leave the important issues 
To the specialist guru, Starbucks, sport utility
Efficiency greater than any human being
And any questions thereafter
Can be filed electronically, replied instantly
To leave you to wonder if you were talking to reality
Though it doesn’t matter, trust us
You can sleep easy at night.
