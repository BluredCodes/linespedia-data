HOW APT
THAT A PERFECT PUNISHMENT
FOR PAST INDESCRETION AND INFIDELITY
HAS NO REVERSE GEARS.
ONE MAY SHED AS MANY TEARS;
BUT THIS PESTILENCE THAT LIVES AMONG US
CUTTTING DOWN THE YOUNG STURDY CROP
WITH SUCH INDIFFERENCE AS WE ALL DROP.
IT HAS LED US TO BELIEVE WE'RE IMMUNE:
ME ,DIE FROM AIDS , IMPOSSIBLE!
TIS IRONIC THAT THERE'S NO CURE,
NO SECOND CHANCE.

PROPAGATED BY THE KNOWING AND UNKNOWING,
IT WILL WIPE OUT MILLIONS.
YET WE FEEL OURSELVES DETTACHED FROM ITS REALITY.
FRIENDS, NEIGHBOURS AND FAMILY DIE FROM IT,
AND YET WE SHUN IT FROM OUR CONSCIENCE.
OUR MISDIRECTED EMOTIONS ARE THE CONDUIT
OF ITS GUARANTEED DISTRUCTIVE FORCE.
LITTLE DID I KNOW..

THAT I WOULD ONE DAY 
FALL ITS PREY.
THE DISBELIEF;
IN SLEEP I FOUND NO RELIEF!
I CRIED BY DAY
AND PRAYED BY NIGHT.
FRIENDS TOLD ME TO PUT UP A FIGHT!
HOW COULD I HAVE GONE SO ASTRAY?

I IMPLORE YOU, DEAR POETS;
THIS IS AS BAD AS IT GETS:
REGRET, REMORSE,RESIGNATION.
AS  POETS WE MUST TAKE ACTION.
IN OUR WORDS WE MUST URGE CAUTION.
FAR TOO MANY PEOPLE VANISH BEFORE OUR EYES,
THERE ARE NO SECOND CHANCES

WE ARE UP AGAINST A PERFECT KILLING MACHINE.
IT GROWS WITH US;
IT STALKS US
TILL WE'RE RIPE.
AND WITH A SWIFT SWOOP
IT GOES FULL BLOWN.
GNAWING AWAY
AT EVERY VITAL ORGAN,
WEAKENING, BREAKING DOWN,
AND FINALLY IT ATTACKS OUR INTELLECT,
THAT WE MAY NO LONGER BE MASTERS OF VERSE.
INSANITY SETS IN,
AND THE BRAIN IS MALFUNCTIONED,
