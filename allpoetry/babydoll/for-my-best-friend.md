---
title: "For My Best Friend"
writer: "babydoll"
slug: "for-my-best-friend"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/46652-For-My-Best-Friend-by-babydoll"
---

I wish for you
That I could share
The thoughts of my heart
The thoughts of my hair.

I love you so much
And wish you could see
I'm here for you
Can't you be here for me?

You got caught up
In drugs and boys
You treated them as if
They were just merely toys.

All I want
Is for you to be
The special girl
I used to see.

Where is the smile,
And the beautiful hair?
The happy girl,
Is where?

Please know that I'm not here to judge,
The choices you make,
Or the things of your past,
Just know to be careful with the risks you take.

You said you were changing,
And the bad things would stop,
I am here to help you,
Please, don't let my dream pop!

For I love you,
More than you know,
More than blue means sky,
And white means snow.
