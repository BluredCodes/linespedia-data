---
title: "Friendship?"
writer: "babydoll"

A time in your life,
When you most need friends,
What usually starts,
Almost always ends.

Look around,
Who can you trust?
Not many, for most are about
A superficial, selfish lust.

How sad,
That many can't find,
One friend in the world,
Whose words will be kind.

There are always those,
Who feel excluded.
The feelings they bare,
Are usually eluded.

My question is this,
for you to ponder,
Who are you to
decide the fate of another?
