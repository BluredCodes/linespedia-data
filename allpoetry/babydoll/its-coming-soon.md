---
title: "It's Coming Soon"
writer: "babydoll"
slug: "its-coming-soon"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/46655-Its-Coming-Soon-by-babydoll"
---

You've taught me so much,
More than you know.
Things of the heart,
Things of the snow.

So the time is now
To give thanks to you,
For all of your time,
And all that you  do.

You are my best friend,
My true soul mate.
There is nothing about you,
That I could ever hate.

You mean so much to me,
And I don't know how,
I am getting along,
Without you right now.

But the future is coming,
It will soon be here,
The time will have passed,
And you will be near.

So cheer up my love,
Let's wait for the end,
It's only two months and,
You will be my all the time best friend.
