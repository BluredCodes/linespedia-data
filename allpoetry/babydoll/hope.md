As I sit here and write
With tears freely flowing,
You're 800 miles away,
Completely unknowing.

I miss you so much,
That's all I can say,
Except that I'm waiting,
Waiting for a new day.

A day when the distance is by car,
Not plane,
And I can reach you easily,
By simply driving down the lane.

Soon you will be home,
The torment will be over,
We will be free,
To run through the fields of clover.

To run through the meadows,
And across the stream,
Not to wake up,
To find it a dream.

You will be here,
To sleep with me.
Replacing the nights,
I was so lonely.

When two soul mates meet,
It is hard to deny,
The love that they feel,
And how hard they try.

My soul mate you are,
So the world should be aware,
I love you,
I will always care.Eric, it's almost over.  Stay strong.
