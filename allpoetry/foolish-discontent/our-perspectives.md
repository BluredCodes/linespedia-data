~Our perspectives~

Im beginning to see the things around me
the way the evil hides on my shadow
When something is pure, I lift the vail
to reveal the corruption that has taken tole
And insipid words are strung along
with memories falling off at every corner
 the picture fades as every sun sets
every day looses value
But I keep the music playing
hoping that one say
I will begin to see the things around me
the way you want me to
