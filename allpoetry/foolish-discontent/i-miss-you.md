I miss you

My missing of you is larger than the clouds 
that carry all of the angels and their mighty wings
My yearning for your company
is mightier than the all of the Giants at the top of the beanstalks
My love for you cannot be measured by angels on clouds
or giants up high
The only thing big enough to describe it
Would be all of these things combined, at least.
