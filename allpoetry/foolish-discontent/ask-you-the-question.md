~Ask you the question~

I often wonder what you would say
if I asked you the question that I long for the answer to
If I asked you if you loved me, 
I wonder how you would break the news
that I am just your friend
Still, for some reason beyond my control
I keep this crazy thought in my imagination
that if I ever ask the question
You might just say you do
Then the question that would come next is,
Am I dreaming?
