---
title: "Ender"
writer: "foolish discontent"
slug: "ender"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/43637-Ender-by-foolish-discontent"
---

You are the ender
 at the end of the line
 on the back row
without the dream
The end is the lonliest place
everyone seems so happy
Yet you are at the end of the line
 on the back row
and cannot find your dream
But just remember
Without the ender
There is no line
And when they happy fall sad
You stay strong
and step up, go forward
and alas, you find the front row
you reach the front of the line
and your dream is granted
For now you are the Starter
Now get back in line
