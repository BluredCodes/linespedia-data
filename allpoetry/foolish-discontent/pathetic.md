~Pathetic~


You will never get it
And I am glad you wont
Since you have no idea
That I am pathetic

You have no idea
that while you tell me she is wonderful
how much I wish to be that she
and how I am pathetic

Still, through it all
I am sure that you dont share the obsession
and it makes my spirit barely alive
Yet, I still fawn for you
Isnt that pathetic?

Cause I am the pathetic girl
The role I swore to never play
