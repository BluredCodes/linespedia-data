~A Story Never Told~


I see your face,
I see your spirit
But you cant fool the world for much longer
Within those eyes, behind that smile
Its beginning to surface:
your story never told.
Its okay to have a secret
You dont have to hide your past
I will love you, faults and all
And dont be afraid to be known too well
If you just let me in
to your story never told
I promise,
My story is waiting for you.
