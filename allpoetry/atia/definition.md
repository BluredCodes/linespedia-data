title: "Definition"
writer: "atia"

Peace-
The language politicians speak
(or so I've been told)
Ambrosia-
Delectable dreams for those deprived
Yet what the rich eat everyday
Life-
Oh but half a breath
And what of death?
Inevitable..
But who defines truth?
Lies bleed every which way
My subjective tears go astray
And I laugh at "facts"
Like houseflies really-
latch on to the ones
You find the least irritating
And then go buzzzzzz.........
Breath your objective lies
Like half the world never cries
More really
But they don't exist 
We don't want them to
Justice-
just another word.
It's all relative
So where am I?
Stuck somewhere in between
Of what we will and will not to see
If you want I'll be alive
Or else I'll be another number
One you're not aware of..
Honestly-
By whose definition?
Mine!
So I dare you
Define me..I don't know if this is good at all or if it created  the effect I wanted. help..
