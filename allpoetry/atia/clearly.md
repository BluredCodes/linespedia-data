---
title: "Clearly"
writer: "atia"
slug: "clearly"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I can say I saw you
The first time
A thousand times
But I never saw you clearly
I can say I heard you
Just as everyone else does
So, come again
I want to listen clearly
I can say what I believe
I know I feel different
But after all that has passed
I'm a fool clearly...
