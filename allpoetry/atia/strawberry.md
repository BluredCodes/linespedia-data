It's one of days
You go hunting with your eyes
For a desperate bolt of extraordinary.
You sit behind a desk
Thinking, "I feel pretty."
With peace in your face,
And a smile that can give to life.
You run around asking for things
You find you already have,
And you head outside to eat
Only to give in to the wind.
The pond that's frozen over
Makes you want to step in.
You wonder why you stumble often
As you stumble again.
All it takes is 
One bent out of shape strawberry.
Perfect!
Makes you laugh 'cause it makes no sense
And reminds you of you.
It presses against your hand
Telling you the world is yours
For the taking.
Only, you don't want to take.
You want to give.
So you bite in,
Giving meaning to it's life.Today, this was me :)
And I really want honest critical comments.
