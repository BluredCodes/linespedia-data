---
title: "Spangled"
writer: "atia"
slug: "spangled"
source: "AllPoetry"

There are times
When the stars twinkle in my eyes
And the orange yellow of the world
Beckons me to call
I don't just walk anymore
I jump, I skip, I hop down life
Determined to get every leaf
To hear it crumple beneath my feet
And scream my very name..
I walk on the edges of sidewalks
With my arms up like I were an airplane
Singing songs only my parents would dare
I sense the wind chill my cheeks
And thank God for life
(despite cursing myself for anticipating the cold)
I look up at the night
And wish I knew more of constellations
Or else my bespangled sky would take
Some shape or form..
I feel the world 
Reaching out to me,
Needing my crazy childishness,
Since that's what keeps us sane,
Loving me for who I am,
Knowing it can't keep me tame...
