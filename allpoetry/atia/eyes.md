---
title: "Eyes"
writer: "atia"
slug: "eyes"
source: "AllPoetry"

Why show me a glimpse
Of all that I can never have?
Why make me fly so high
And fall so fast?
..I don't think I can take it in..

So, Must I comprehend?
Can I not wander off searching,
screaming and shouting and crying?
Tell me is it really true?
Or what is true at all
I'm sick of all the lying.
Tell me you see it inside me
That my crazy world is real
Because the moment I step into yours
I can't get myself to feel
Don't tell me I'm clueless
That I live on a pack of lies
As for the world you live and breathe
Look at it through my eyes...
