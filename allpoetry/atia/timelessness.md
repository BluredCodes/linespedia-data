Seriously, I need this time
I need to sit here
restless,
senseless,
pointless,
but still alive...
Clocks tick away from me
And I away from time..
So where are we going
Should I care?
Nah!
I like this feeling of Timelessness
Tomorrow,
I shall have to pay
Tomorrow
I shall..
Don't I deserve it?well, this gets a little more across...
