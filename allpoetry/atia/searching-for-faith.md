Trace a path that I can seeA reality from which I can not fleeFor this is life and nothings freeLet me believe some things can always beEven if it's not for me(But I want it to be for me)I wrote this at a time when my whole world was tossed around and everything I thought I had was lost. I've had to start over and I can't help but contemplate my loss...But every time I do, I'm afraid of being ungrateful for the things and people I do have left.
