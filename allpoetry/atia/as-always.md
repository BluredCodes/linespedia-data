Yes, It's me again..
Out of words
And out of luck
Broken Down
And Torn apart
Used and abused
By choices 
That I made
By paths that I followed
There lies my soul
scattered here and there
oops.. 
There goes my innocence
The snatching winds fly by

But what do you care?
I'm here aren't I?
