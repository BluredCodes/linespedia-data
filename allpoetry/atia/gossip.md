Fried eyes Glare
Empty eyes stare
So you'd best better beware
Before you're swallowed..
Lies all lies
Filthy expectations
Kick back and get sucked in
Or better yet bitten 
By the bite of death
Venus Fly Trap
Suffocate within my breath
Amidst all the fervor
Think me not condescending
Just fascinated by your life
And critisicms unending.
Just a busy bee
Hanging on for the buzz,
Dying to hear the rip
Of myths and tales unsung
Longing for life and dreaming
Of your taste on my tart tongue..I loathe gossip...
