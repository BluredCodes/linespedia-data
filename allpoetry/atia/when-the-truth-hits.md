...Dazed...
My mind seems to fly away.
I find myself unable to think.
The music plays loudly and I
Sit staring ahead blankly and blink.
And then it dawns on me,
Everything you had to say.
I don't like the way you look at me
So turn your gaze away.I dont like this one much except its important to me cos it regards someone i did know..
