---
title: "Thinking of You"
writer: "atia"
slug: "thinking-of-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/10081-Thinking-of-You-by-atia"

Keep me inspired
Keep my eyes blazing and my tongue fired
Keep me alive
When you look in my eyes, look inside
Keep me awake
I don't care for what's at stake
Let me believe
That all you said was true
Keep me talking
Keep me hanging on
Keep me thinking of you!
