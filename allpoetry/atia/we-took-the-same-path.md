How can I believe
That you led me astray?
When I walked along beside you,
Every step of the way..
I’m supposed to be disillusioned,
Well, that’s what people say..
So what if I don’t know
The year or month or day?
When I speak, 
I’m told I’m blind..
Why can I not see what’s true?
But how can they not see?
That if I’m lost,
You’re lost too…
