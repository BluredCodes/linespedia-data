New visions spawn from
Restricted views under wool.
My cure for unsightly hair
Blocks out a world that glares,
that scares me away from sight.
I need strain to earn
A little more of life.

Seems like I have a choice.
Feels almost like I care
For how I am surrounded
In the soft and harsh places.
I need just ignore the faces.

This passive little shelter
Sports a fancy rim of dread
Patches of protected vision
Cushion my unbalanced head.
And yet I will to reach
From underneath a warm fuzz.

Thoughts often wander and leave
There's depth to that weave.
Life is short bursts of meaning
Between the eye and the storm.
But to speak of why I wear a lid,
