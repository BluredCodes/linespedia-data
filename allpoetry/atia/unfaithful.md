Rollercoaster
What goes up must come down
And what goes down
Well it's a ride
And if it don't come up?
What then?
Hopelessness..
So what of life?

"I'm doing Good."
"There's always hope."
"Things can only get better."
"Keep the faith."
"Hang on. Great things will come."

Yeah right!

I feel like a run on...
I feel so ungrateful for life..
I feel ashamed
Of myself..
