Why can't we choose
when we want to be born..
Why am I alive to know 
That I missed so much?
Standing in the shadow
Of this scary old mansion
No shadows, no ghosts 
Tell me of what had past...
I can only imagine that
The wind that moves through me..
Moved through you..
When you were
And I was not..
Why?
Why not?
When there was adventure
When there was love
I promise....
I wouldn't have taken it away..
