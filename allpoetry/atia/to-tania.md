title: "To Tania"

Life is tight on choices
I spend my days
Wondering how the words I say
Shall choose to condemn me
Heads fill with voices
Too loud for me to care
I'm so afraid to bear
Who I really am out loud
But then I catch a sight of you
And we can spend all day
Dreaming of the loves and lives
That may come our way
You're the only one I know
Willing to decipher all my verses
And read between the lines
Of never written curses
You're the only shrink I have
When I break down, that's often
But any and all the strength I have
It's 'cause of you I've gotten
So how can you ever be down?
When you are why I smile
Don't care of what other people say
Honey, you have your own style
Life is a crowded room
It's so hard to wade through
But I pray and hope that at the end
You'll find someone just for you :)This is for my best Friend Tania cos she's been down a whole lot lately..
