---
title: "Scar vs. Sting"
writer: "atia"
slug: "scar-vs-sting"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/25544-Scar-vs.-Sting-by-atia"
---

Beware of evil bees
Old memories swarm back
I've been stung too many times
To know..
We are the walking wounded
Some of us will never heal
And the skin-replacing scars
Will show..
Existence and then life
Too many lessons to be learned
I can only pray for a better memory
And bargain sting for sting
In hope that I will not be burned.
