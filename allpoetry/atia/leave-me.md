---
title: "Leave Me"
writer: "atia"
slug: "leave-me"
source: "AllPoetry"

Leave me alone
Drowning in the abyss
Maybe I’ll find whats amiss
Or whether its worth at all
Let me fall
Into the black hole thats pulling me in
Cause it feels the same under my skin
Let me lose the game I know I can not win..

Can’t you see you're setting me free?
Leave me be...
