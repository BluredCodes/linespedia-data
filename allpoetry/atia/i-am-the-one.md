---
title: "I am the one"
writer: "atia"
slug: "i-am-the-one"
source: "AllPoetry"

I am the one who is afraid
To speak in front of a crowd.
I never used to be this way.
I am the one who 
Turned quiet,
'Cause I had nothing to say.
I never used to be this way.
I am the one that walks by
Deliberately,
Just to see you smile
Back at me,
Wondering
Am I crazy?
Do you care?
Why?
I never used to be this way.
I am the one who pours out her heart,
Choosing to be misunderstood,
Who laughs and cries at the same time.
I never used to be this way.
I am the one who hides
And then errupts,
Reaches out,
Fingers growing numb in the cold.
I never used to be this way.
I am the one who feels,
Who searches for eyes
To pierce through her soul,
Who wants to reach out and touch.
I never used to be this way..
