I wont even tryTo denyThe fact that you hurt me..And Lord knowsI tried to forgetIt was easier than forgiving.Here was my new lifeA million miles awayWithout youAnd all the hassle I forever thought you were.But you chose to go furtherWhere we can't even reachYou died..Now I don't know what to think..Except I know I have no choiceThan to forgive you..I'm the one left with a voiceHow can I speak my mind?How dare I now?I'm sorry...This was the shortest way to vent out all the emotion I have been feeling since I heard that he was dead.
