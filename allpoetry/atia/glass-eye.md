---
title: "Glass eye"
writer: "atia"
slug: "glass-eye"
source: "AllPoetry"

Creep
Pass
Leave
All at the same time.
Or I could just sit here
And read my world away
Read the lies from off your face
As though I were blind.

Tears fall like stars could only dream
Above me, a moving mass of sky
Bluer than the eyes of truth
But I haven't seen those in a while
I wish I too were a cloud
Flying high and breaking 
Away from the eye of your storm...
