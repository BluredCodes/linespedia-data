title: "Looking up"
writer: "atia"

Squeeze every ounce
of patience out of me
And what will you get?
Your head knocked off..
haha!
Like I dare.
I care too much.
I wish I didn't but that would be you,
Not me.
I live for the day
You too may regret.
That perhaps
I too shall be re-assured,
That the moon shall hang near,
Complete me with itself
As its entourage of stars disappears, 
As do mine.
Atleast this way I'll be looking up...
