Fleeting thoughts
Running in and out of my brain,
Do not flee before you are caught,
Made permanent in black and white,
My pinstriped slaves on paper.
There can never be enough
So let me hoard some more,
Unleash you from my soul 
And fetter you down on pages.
I shall call you Poetry
And you can call me cruel...Any suggestions for the title?
