Is that what you told her:
It's ok for me to die?
Where do you think it will get you?
Let me think a bit
Did you just call me ruthless?
Cos I need to be sure
Oh yes of you but too of me
Let me open my eyes 
And watch you prey on my heart
Let me breathe in the stench
That rises from my starved soul
No, I'm not a masochist
I just want to know
What its like
What you call
Being free...
..cos that's what you told me..This is dedicated to all those who support the war. It gets to me that we can sit in our comfortable homes and hear lies bellow from the television set while many innocents die.. all in the name of freedom. XCan we even imagine what it feels like to look to the sky for salvation and meet only death..
