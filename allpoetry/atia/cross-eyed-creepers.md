title: "Cross-eyed Creepers"

Honestly, I hate cross-eyed creepers
Who the heck are you staring at?
And why do you hang around?
Confess!
What is it about me 
that keeps you stuck
Is it the charm...haha
Or could it be I provide you 
With plenty to laugh at
hmm...
All this attention gets me thinking..
too much...
muddled up....
gee..
I hate cross-eyed creepers
Don't pretend to see through my soul
When you might not care
It makes me expect to much
Perhaps a little understanding
perhaps a little care
Perhaps hope for my dreams
That you now carry in your hands
Or fear that 
They shall fall..
Only to bounce back..
Unfulfilled..
Like you...
And stare at me...
Laughing at all I never am...
Or perhaps can never be...
