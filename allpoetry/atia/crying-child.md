title: "crying child"
writer: "atia"

Sometimes I wonder that
We shouldn't have been taught morality
Wouldn't it have been easier
To accept all that we see
Somehow I open my eyes to hate
And I think its all wrong
I drive myself crazy
I can't make sense of the world
I feel like a confused little child
Lost in a flea market
I can't rationalize 
Whats been done to me?
When I look at the world
I see all that it should and could be
But it's not
And it hurts...
