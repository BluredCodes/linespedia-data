So I decide
To run out of songs to listen to..
Like that was even possible.
Did you know that rain was the first song?
And that tears compare all the time?
But nothings impossible really,
At least not forever.
I just want to do something
That is not possible now,
Even if in my head.
Do you think if there was something
That just I knew,
I would be lonely?
Well I am,
Even though I blurt out
I have nothing to conceal..
I am only waiting
For you
To give me something to hide :)weehee.. I'm on a roll here
