title: "Flight"
writer: "atia"

I don't get how you can be there for me,
Unconditionally.
And that's what confuses me
I choose no path that's set for me
Let me make my own
And if every possible route is taken
Every grain of sand tread on
I want to take to the sky
You'd have to be my wings.
But if you're ever gone
I'd fall back to earth,
Already discovered.
My world would be the same as everyone else
And I would be as everyone else,
Fallen...


(28th March '01, I fell)
