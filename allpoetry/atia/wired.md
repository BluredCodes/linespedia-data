Did I ever mention
There were pretty meadows outside this box..
And the words crazy and weird 
Seem to blend in..
I can see what you don’t dare 
To ever want:
“Too free a mind
To be bound by anything but herself
Or that she dares to give away…”
Awful thoughts reside
And parks sing songs while I flutter by
Along with all the butterflies
Down to the espresso machine…Have begun to discover the wonderful world of caffeine.. :P
