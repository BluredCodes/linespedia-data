Find me a quiet spot
To sit calm in the shade
To hide
To wait
For the storm to abate
I don't want to be in the action
I don't even want to watch
People falling all over each other
Like there was a stampede
It's just so ridiculous
You'd think with all the earth has given us
We'd have enough space
OK, 
So you say
I can't have my way
Or else I won't live
But what good is life
If I look out my window
And die each day?This is my response to what I see happening all around me...
