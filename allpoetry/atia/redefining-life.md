Life does that sometimes,
And I go on about it,
Hoping to discover something new
For myself,
But perhaps already discovered.
Feels good though..
So this is a hard time for me.
What have I got to lose with honesty?
What have I got to hide?
What have I got to say
That cannot be said when I write?
I am for the world
And the world is for me,
Washed out and wrung out
Many a times
But not quite enough,
Than is meant to be.
Today, I know.
Tomorrow, I will know better..
