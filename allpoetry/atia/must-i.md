---
title: "Must I"
writer: "atia"
slug: "must-i"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

The walls seem more familiar now
Though I am still molding
Myself into a newer world.
Things must be different 
'Cause of the differences 
I choose to feel..
Yet I must be me,
Demanding a place among the driftwood
Without seeming too anxious
Or haunted by the very 
Existence of my breath.
If now had any meaning
I would not have to wait 
To be wise or feel older.
The moments I live
Are always too young,
My future always far away,
And my past -
Always Undiscovered...
