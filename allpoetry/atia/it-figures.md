title: "It Figures"
writer: "atia"

Where's the humor in hurt?
Or the laughter in hate?
What am I and what are you?
Refusing to look beyond the dirt
Piled high before our eyes
By choices, deceit, lies..

You and I survived...

But was it worth the fight?
Life cares not for us
Who dare to dream too much
But sits and watches
Our bubbles burst all over the place
Like it were a game
With childlike innocence
Whatever it has left...

It makes me think,
"Hey!
If I can do that to life,
If I can knock 
Some sense, some reason, 
Some practicality
Out of it,
I can change the world."
