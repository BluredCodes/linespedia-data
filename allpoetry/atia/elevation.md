Tainted eyes and tainted souls
Loving caged in locked up rooms
Freedom flying stories high
The world screaming only love
Stars twinkle signs of life
Waves lull life to sleep,
Then swallow whole and lash the beach
Leaves rip away from life
Fire falling kills the sky
Tremors shake the earth to bits
Yellow swirls, orange surprises
