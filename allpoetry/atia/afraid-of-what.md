---
title: "Afraid of what?"
writer: "atia"

She lies back
Takes the music in
And wonders
If death was to come now,
Would the feelings be frozen in time?
A relentless calm pervades
This is what life had been
Acts for which she'd be accountable
She had always known
Shouldn't she have been fearful?
And she shakes her head,
'God forbid!'
And wonders if it really matters
Perhaps a better place would wait
Perhaps nothing really ends
But then, she was never any good at taking risks
Uncertainty had found a good home in her 
It's funny that no one had ever dared 
To stare into her eyes and ask flat out:
'What are you afraid of?'
Everything!For some reason, I couldn't get myself to write this in the first person. I suppose that would make it much too personal than I can handle right now..
