title: "In the wake"

I never asked you
To create my world
Never asked you
To make sense of the whirl
The life around me
Or maybe just the lack of it
Oh heck, who cares?
I do
Who gave you the right
To mess with my reality
To lie and keep me tame
How can you live to know
that you're quiet
HUSH!
I'm not supposed to know
Another world exists 
Thats harsh
Where parents don't know
Of sawed off guns in their kids' rooms
Or that 'Hey you're wrong!
But I shall kill in my playground.'
How high were you planning to take me
Had I not run from the drug?
When were you planning to let me fall?
Or would you keep me
high on you and your lies
Forever and ever
And watch me feed
On your self proclaimed righteousness
and your home grown sensationalism.
I'm speechless
I'm blind too
But that's what you did to me
The eyes shall open though
To see the holes 
That bore through your skin
And show all you are -
empty
Is that what you wanted me to be?
Hollow? 
Devoid?
Alone?
Thanks for the future
Or the lack of it...This poem is addressed to the news media and is in response to a paper I am doing research for..
