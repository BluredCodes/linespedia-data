---
title: "Renegades"
writer: "atia"

We're all for sharp turns
And tides that turn
Along with tables,
Aiming to be the leaves
That never fall from trees
Despite the changing seasons.
We appreciate the struggle,
Anticipate the vertical drop,
Do all the right things
For all the wrong reasons,
Thinking maybe a heartache
Is the key to soul searching
And perhaps there's a bigger reward
The bigger is the hurting.
So we look into barren eyes
For a little self assurance
After all, what is life
If not a test of endurance?
