title: "Fateful"

Fate's Dagger
Plays with my reality
And plunges,
Cuts me out of the noise,
Delivers me with ecstasy.
I bleed freely.
Benevolent fate gives
To me,
Tears that line the sunset,
And a heart
That has given voice to ink.
Who here dares
To grab the hilt of fate?
Not I.
I fear tomorrow I shall wake
In yesterday,
For the sake of tranquility,
Only to find 
I do not know who I am
Again.
