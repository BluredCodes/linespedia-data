Sulk
All the world's a stage
And today I shall be beggar
Asking for more
Where is the land I have been promised?
Where is the truth behind the lies?
Now that I have seen the smog
Where are my endless blue skies?
Let the tears roll
Your twists and turns have wound me up
And I can not know what to do
So where is my winning cup?help with the title please :)
