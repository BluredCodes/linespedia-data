Hey I'm all for labels
Some used to call me a bitch
But they never really knew me
And those who know me now
Just like to call me crazy
But who really knows me, knows me?
Who truly cares?
Who dares?
Not many really.
So be full of all the talk
Tear me up, bit by bit
I'm your problem, sure ain't mine
See if I give a shit!I think this poem sucks but what the heck..
