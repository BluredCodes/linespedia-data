title: "Hungry Poet"

Feed me
Give me the will
Within my breath
To carry on
To speak, to scream
To bear the daggers 
Shooting from your eyes
Let me bask
under the sun of my dreams
Let me bathe
In the calm 
That I will to be me
Let me rise
Like a storm
Let me fall
Let me have it all
Breathe me
Feel me
Save me
While I save you...This is what poetry means to me and thank all you poets for being there :)
