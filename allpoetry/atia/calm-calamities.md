---
title: "Calm calamities"
writer: "atia"

Recently,
I have walked down stairs
Without thinking of falling
And I laugh
Without forgetting why..
If only the emotions
Wouldn't hit like a wave
With my back turned
I wouldn't be dragged
into the undercurrent
I wouldn't be so overwhelmed
I wouldn't hide my sentiments
Saying it accomplishes nothing
And then want to explode.
I feel numb
I need a paper and a penMy sister tends to think that as I do not show or talk about my emotions, I am simply devoid of them..
