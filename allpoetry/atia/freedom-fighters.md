This is for the freedom fighters
Cos I thought of them today
And I don't think much of them at all..
This is for the freedom fighters
who don't go to fight the war
They fight for the truth
Unveil the lies in the eyes
Of those who never blink
Of the secret stories 
The media only dares to make public..
Of the lame excuses enclosing us all
Who allow us to look beyond
And allow us to feel
This is for the freedom fighters
Who have given me sadness
beyond compare
On seeing the REAL state of things..
This is for the freedom fighters
Who have let me heal...
