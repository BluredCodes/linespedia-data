---
title: "Deja vu"
writer: "atia"
slug: "deja-vu"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/31810-Deja-vu-by-atia"
---

'There it was again;
I was there again.'
For a moment back in time,
I sang the same old rhyme.
And when I closed my eyes,
The silence whispered sweet lies.
Lost in the ocean of memories
Risen somewhere from the past.
Don't say another word
Just let the moment last...
