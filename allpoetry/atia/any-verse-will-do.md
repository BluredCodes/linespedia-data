---
title: "Any Verse Will Do"
writer: "atia"

When the world seems to have taken
Everything from me but my name
And left me a package full of tears
And left me nothing but my shame
I take a look at my bleeding heart
And know the wounds will heal in time
I look at my life and I sigh 
I sit and I scribble a rhyme.
I do not like that I've changed
I do not like what the world has done
It's taken my sanity and my strength
It's taken my love and left me none
It's times like these I'm helpless
It's times like these I want to die
I need a reason to move on
And my verse gives me a reason why
The world does not have to care for me
My heart can still be true
I do not need anyones empty words
Cause any verse will do!
