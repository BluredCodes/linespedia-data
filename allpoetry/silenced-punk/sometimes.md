Sometimes I wonder why god picked me,
To endure all this suffering and pain,
What have i done to deserve this?
Other than take his name in vain,

Sometimes i wonder why people are so cruel,
And treat me like they do,
Why don't they even give me a chance?
they just use me,abuse me,and throw me away like a worn out pair of shoes,

Sometimes i wonder who i am,
From my experiences i don't know myself anymore,
who am i?
A different person up to the very core,

Sometimes i wonder why i'm still alive,
With my negative way of living im surprised,
Why am i still alive?
Just to live life and hope there is a worthwhile prize,

Sometimes i wonder,
Don't you?
