---
title: "Cold"
writer: "SiLenCeD PuNK"
slug: "cold"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/34046-Cold-by-SiLenCeD-PuNK"
---

Life without love can make your heart cold,
For my heart was an icicle only so bold,

When you came into my life,
I saw that my heart started to thaw,
But the jagged scars of pain still showed you my flaw,
I gave you my heart so fragile and cold,
For my heart was an icicle only so bold,

You dropped my heart and then it shattered,
Into little peices that all were so small,
you of course tried to help me pick up them all,
For your heart was full of love and not so cold,
For my heart was an icicle only so bold,

As you try to pick up peices of my broken heart,
It just makes it fall even more apart,
I try to hide the pain of this painful dart,
So i can salvage the shattered peices of my broken heart,
So fragile and cold,
For my heart was an icicle only so bold,

I had so much pain that i tried to hide,
I could take only so much,
From this hellish ride,
For the peices of my heart were so cold,
For my heart was an icicle only so bold,

I don't know when I am to explode,
Or pop from the spears of pain,
As i stay and pick up the peices of my heart.
During the silent rain,
As the clouds silently lolled,
For my heart was an icicle only so bold,

The night was still and silent as it rained,
For that resembles my silent cries of pain,
As the night grows dark,
It also grows cold,
For my heart was an icicle only so bold.

There is nobody that i can now trust,
For my heart lay broken in the thick dust.
As i pick up the peices of my heart,
So fragile and cold,
For my heart was an icicle only so bold.

Each peice of my heart has a memory so true,
Each peice of my heart has a part of you,
Each peice of my heart so fragile and cold,
For my heart was an icicle only so bold,

You are the one who is leaving to start a new lease on life,
I'll just be here on my knees picking up the peices of a heart,
That feels like its being peirced with a knife,
My heart is on this floor shattered and broke,
With each peice i pick up i need to learn to let go,
For my heart was an icicle only so bold,
And my heart now is a scar so very cold.
