Love,
What is love?,

That feeling,
When you're with your lover,
by the lake,
and when you look into her eyes,
you feel like you have butterflies in you stomache,

That Time,
Where you take like to the next level,
Nothing can stop you,
Not even the devil,

That moment,
Where sparks fly,
You are so full of joy,
You sometimes can't help but cry,

That thought,
That you will stay together,
with your lover,
and be with her forever,

That place,
In your heart,
Where you opened up,
You gave it to her,
As freely as an empty cup,

To make love work,
It only takes two,
I have three words to say,
And those three words are,
I love you
