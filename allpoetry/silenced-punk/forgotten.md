---
title: "Forgotten"
writer: "SiLenCeD PuNK"

Forget love.
I have experienced it and i always get heart broken,
Theres no reason to go through it,
Because love is just a state of the mind,
Not a stupid emotion, 

Forget friends,
At least for me,
They are never true,
When i least expect it,
they give me the cold shoulder and flee,

Forget life,
I never win,
There is no happy ending in real life,
It's just a bowling game with human pins,

Forget Money,
It can't buy me happiness,
It can only buy me stuff,
Stuff can help at times,
But not when we have it rough,

Forget me,
I am the unliked,
And My heart is rotten,
But I am not noticed,
For i am forgotten.
