It can be yellow and it can be white,
But no matter what,
its to this kids delight,
This kid likes it too much,
For it he will even fight,
This kid is jimmy and cheese is the delight,

He will eat it anywhere,
This child will chew it here and there,
The sickening smack,
Of his lips as you stare,
At him gnawing this snack,

Oh but its not a snack,
Its a feat,
For this disturbed child,
Would prefer this over meat,
This to him a marvelous treat,

This is not only a story of cheese,
But of jimmy pareese,
But thats not his name,
It doesnt matter,
Because all jimmy's are the same,

Jimmy was eating a block of cocoa,
when he first saw that cheese,
He went loco,
He chased after it,
He dove through some glass,
He hijacked a car,
And put on the gas,
All for a peice of cheese?
oh please

He was determined,
He was absurd,
if you saw what this boy would do,
just for a peice of cheesy stew,
you would think of him crazy,
you would think of him insane,
I just say hes jimmy,
And to think all the jimmy's are the same!
