All the things i love about you,
theres so many to share,
Why am i still talking about talking about you,
I shouldnt waste another minute in dispair,

Your eyes,
They twinkle and shine like a newborn star,
I should know,
I got lost in them and i didn't even have to look far,

Your smile,
So warm and full of happiness,
That smile makes me smile,
More than anything does and never less,

Your playfulness,
The way you mess with me,
its always fun to play with you,
who is so full of energy,

Your sweetness,
Like nothing i have ever tasted,
My favorite fruit is a strawberry,
But you are too sweet a strawberry to be wasted,

I love your every quality,
I love your every move,
i love everything about you,
But most of all i love you.
