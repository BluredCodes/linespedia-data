When i first met you,
You swept me off my feet with your beauty like a nymph from heaven,
As i lay on the ground I was too lost in your bright starry eyes to get back up,
But after recovering from the emotional coma i stood up slowly,
And cherished the blessing that came to me from up above,

Whatever you are,
An angel from heaven,
Aphrodite goddess of beuty,
A tempting siren,
Or a simple human being,

Whatever you become,
A demon from hell,
The devils apprentice,
Or even the devil itself,

Whatever you are,
Whatever you become,
I will always love you.
