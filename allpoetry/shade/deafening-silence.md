title: "Deafening Silence"
writer: "Shade"

My mind is dull
My body numb
And something wicked this way comes
My eyes are heavy and downcast
I hold no hope for you 
Only memories of another man’s violations
So you’ve forced your heart away
Through the crowd we watch each other with pain
Remembering a dreamed kiss 
My lips tingle through the poison
Then the sensation fades with the memories
And my mind
Darkness; The ground races up to lovingly embrace me
Instead she breaks me
