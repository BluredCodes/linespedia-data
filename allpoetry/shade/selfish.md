title: "Selfish"
writer: "Shade"

It’s like cloudy oil in your mind. 
You cannot wash it away 
You can’t do anything but sit there 
Feeling disgusting alone tired. 
Your whole body aches 
You feel like you are dying on the inside 
While your soul suffocates in it’s own grief… 
And they call suicide selfish. 
They have no understanding of what it feels like 
Having the daily torment of life 
Be powerful enough to over shadow the love of those close to you. 
They can’t understand though... 
Because to understand is to be there and to be there is death.
