Never speak a word in my defense
For every lost cause there are martyrs found
Whore or Slave, Prince or Popper

You told me that love makes the world go round
You betrayed my trust 
Don’t say another sound
One and the same, one and in shame

Just forget about me

If I see beyond the filthy horizon, what lays ahead of me?
Perhaps my own destruction
Baptized in blood
Breed more corruption

You forgot about me

My heart is broken, my will is gone 
I'm not scared but I can't move
And I ramble on and I ramble on
