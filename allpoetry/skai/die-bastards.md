---
title: "Die Bastards!"
writer: "Skai"

Tear out your hearts
Gouge your eyes out with sticks
Rip out your windpipes
Chop off your dicks.

You deserve it all
You fucking bastards
I want revenge
For your disasters.

You spilled innocent blood
So I'll drain your love of pain
By drilling some holes
Thru your worthless brains.I wrote this because two 16 year old guys who live in a nearby city mutilated and burned four kittens, killing one and nailing another one's feet to a board.  Three lived, but they only caught two of them.  I want to kill those sick bastards who did that to them.
