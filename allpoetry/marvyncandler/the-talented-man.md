title: "The Talented Man"

A talented mind
Holds no bounds,
It has no illusions
Adrift in the clouds.

And talented hands
Have no answers to find
And no negative thoughts
At the back of the mind.

But bereft of achievement,
Bereft of esteem,
The talented mind
Becomes brittle and weak.

A talented man
Has the world to roam,
But the talented man
Struggles alone.1997,
