I want him to love me
Love? Yes I said Love
For I have never been loved before
And all I have ever desired is...
Love

I want to find that person
That person who makes your day whole
That person whom makes you smile,
Even when you don't want to

I want to live a life of great joy
With the one I have yet to meet
I want to live, period

Life has been nothing but hell
And all I've gotten from it is pain
And I daily ask myself, why?
Why is it just me?

Is there anyone out there?
Anyone to cope with me?
I just want answers,
But cant see them
For I am blinded by life

Maybe one day,
Just maybe, I will find him
And he will change my life around
For better?? who knows.. But I know he is out there
