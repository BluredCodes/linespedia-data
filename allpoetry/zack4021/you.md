I have never loved someone like you
There is more to you that I haven't found yet
More to you that is waiting to be found

Some say Love is a strong word
Some say Love Is used loosely
I say fuck them
I know my feelings for you are strong
I know that you are going to be there in the end

At times I miss you
The touch of an angel
The smile that brings joy to my life
I miss the way you stare at me
Those heavenly eyes of the Angel

For You have turned a life around
Both yours and mine
You make me think of how much more can I get?
I have everything I want
But you tell me there is more

For the day waits for me
And I wait for the day
That we will be one
That we will interlock hearts
The day all my secrets will be revealed

For When I'm with you I feel
I feel as though I'm in the arms of an angel
Life is nothing but perfect
Life is YOU 

I dream of the day I get to wake up
I get to wake up, and you're by my side
You are there in all your glorious wonder
I Cannot wait till the day comes
For it shall be a great day

I love you for being "You"
