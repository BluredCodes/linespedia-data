Life goes on and on
Just getting worse 
Worse with each day that passes
With each setting Sun

My feelings are mixed..
Tossed, and thrown around
I feel as though I'm lost
I feel like I can't do anything

People depend on me
Depend on me to do their work
Stress drives down
Drives me through the ground

I feel like dying
Like life has nothing to offer
I get nothing but pain
Pain that hurts and stays

I spend all my time waiting
For the day that comes
The day comes and its alright
Alright to let go

My soul says keep going
My body says stop
Like a game of tug-o-war
I am pulled every which way

Im tired, lonely, and abandoned

I feel like no one listens
No one knows what I feel
Rejected and alone
That's what I am
