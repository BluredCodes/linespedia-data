Today you called
You called with news I didn't want
You called to say
To say you got a job

I am happy for you
Yet I am sad
For I now I know
I know we wont talk much

Communication...
It's another word for relational-bond
It's what keeps a relationship together
And without, there isn't "us"

I get home, and I wait
I wait and sit by the phone
Waiting for the call from you
For you to call and say "I love you"

Your beautiful voice
Caressing my ears
For I want more
More of your wonderful words

Please don't leave me
Don't start a new life without me
I want to be there always
Take me in with you

ok, ok! 
I accept the fact
That you're getting a job
Just know that we will work things out!

Just start "working"
