You're familiar with my weakness
You're the one in whom I trust
Trusting with all my heart

I feel as though I'm devoted to the end
For my life without you is not complete
And you will bind it together

Your eyes, deep like el cafe
Starring deep into life
Showing me the way

You come to me with your problems
Your problems ... my problems
For we are one

I feel that your love knows no end
"Save my life from these troubles" You say
As I return with "One Day..."

For one day you WILL see
You'll see that things are better
Better than you could have ever imagined

With me holding you in my arms
You shall be safe
Safe with me

For One day
