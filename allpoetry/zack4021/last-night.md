Last night
The greatest night of all time
The night my dreams came true
The night I saw my true love

We held each other for what seemed an eternity
It felt as though I was floating
Floating above the night sky
Starring down at him

He showed me what love is
For love is not a word
It's a feeling
A great feeling in which only comes by once

He kissed me!
A kiss of love
A kiss that felt like sugar on my lips
A sweet sweet sensation

In his arms I felt
I felt secure and safe
For he will protect me
And I shall return the favor

Last night was a great great night
The feelings being wrapped around me
Like a warm blanket on a cold night
The feeling doesn't get better

I cannot wait for the day
The day in which I get that feeling
That wonderous Feeling every night
What a day it's going to be

His touch is so different
The kind that sends tingles down your back
The kind of kiss that makes you wonder
Makes you think of how it could get any better?

His voice, his smile
Everything is heaven in my eyes
And he is the Angel 
The Angel in which has come to save me

I spent all my time waiting
Waiting for him
And he showed up in all his glory
Last night
