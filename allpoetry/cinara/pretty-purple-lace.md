Pretty purple lace


Just as the redbud spatters woodland edge
with pretty purple lace
And dogwood pinks and whites in bloom adorn 
the woods with charming grace
So does your love surround my heart
With splendid hues of rainbow arc

Just as the rays of filtered sun fall through
the forest canopy
And sun-drops dance upon the ground to rhythms
of the wind and leaf 
So does your love enhance my soul
With new dimension to the whole

As mountain springs release their fill, and water wends
through hilly seams 
Just as the clear spring waters rush and gather more
from other streams
So does my love forever grow
Just as the sparkling waters flow

As hummingbird his nectar sips
So do I long to kiss your lips
