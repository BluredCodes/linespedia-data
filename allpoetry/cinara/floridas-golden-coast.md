Florida’s Golden Coast

The hot and bustling southeast coast of Sunshine State
A megalopolis of palms and interstate
Accommodating more and more incoming mass
As alligator lurks below the overpass

They come from all directions over land and seas
To live in sprawling tropical communities
A mega-city built between the Everglade
And coastal waterways that God and man both made

A haven for the super rich and super poor
And Spanish speaking immigrants from other shore
They come by private jet, and overcrowded boat
They bask in wealth, or simply pray to stay afloat

Luxurious yacht that’s docked along the waterfront
Material product of the manmade wealth and want
Undocumented immigrants seek lifelong dreams
An underground economy is bursting at the seams

The night lights up with Latin rhythm and with song
And fine cuisine and wine beneath a royal palm
A glittering diamond necklace strewn from Palm Beach to the keys
While man remains a mystery to the wondrous Manatees
