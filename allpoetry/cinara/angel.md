ANGEL
(Dedicated to SolitaryTearOfAnAngel)

Never seen and never  heard,
Nor answers to the spoken word
But touches you with  whispered breath
And harbors you in times of stress

She hovers over you in sleep
In hours when dreams are real and deep
And spreads her wings above your bed
And shines a halo round your head

She stands behind in vigil’s hour
Protecting this most fragile flower
She walks before the path you plod
And reaches out the hand of God

In restless nights of sorrow’s  gloom
She shines an opalescent moon
And casts a light upon your soul
That you may see the inner goal

Then in a dream it comes to you 
This sorrow that is real and true
It must be lived, it must be felt
The pain and stress that you’ve been dealt  

For sorrow is a valid part
As joys that linger in your heart
In time your eyes will shine anew  
In all the love surrounding you

The grief turns into memories
And whispers through the swaying trees
The past that’s etched into the eye
Is joy and love in days gone by 

If I could have an angel lay
A wish upon this coming day
I’d ask that you be wrapped in white
Of angel feathers soft and light

That splendid tunes would dry a tear
An angel harp to touch your ear
An opal moon to shine you light
A whispered breath to say good-night
