The Will of the Wind 

You can’t always blueprint the course of the day
With schemes and designs to determine the way
For there are some forces from which you can’t hide
It’s the will of the wind, and the drift of the tide

Sometimes you go forward with zest in your soul
Determined to reach that one reachable goal
Then unforeseen happenings come into play
It’s the flow of the waters, the storms of the day

Some days you wake up with your heart full of dread
With sorrowful melodies fixed in your head
Then the requiem turns to a jovial tune
It’s the tilt of the earth, and the pull of the moon

You cannot predict what tomorrow will bring
What dance you will dance and what song you will sing
For no matter how great your resolve from within
It’s the drift of the tide, and the will of the wind
