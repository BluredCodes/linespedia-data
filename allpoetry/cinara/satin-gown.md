Satin Gown

Jewels of blue enhance her eye
Golden hair reflects the sky
Flush of pink on silken face
Satin gown with flowing lace

Crimson red to wet her lips
Pearly glaze on fingertips
Finally, to complete the whole
Poetry adorns her soul
