Two Lovers Met

Two lovers met one summer night
Enchanting  me in sheer delight
As I sat still beside a stream
They came to meet as in a dream

A little moonbeam softly dropped
On whispering stream and there it stopped
And in contentment stayed to rest
Upon the water’s gentle crest

The water murmured out of love
And only I and stars above
Were witness to the splendor there
This tender touch of loving pair
