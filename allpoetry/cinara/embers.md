Embers

Lost in red of glowing ember
Eye in mesmerized remember
Sees a picture painted in 
November’s frigid chill of night

It was by a warming fire
Night of longing, heart’s desire
That her staring eyes were deeply
Mired in the flickering light

Long ago this youthful splendor
That for years would cruelly render
Painful thoughts and visions to a
Tender heart of purest gold

Love still burning in the deepness
Of the night, in taunting sleepless
hours the lonely heart in weary
weakness still the past beholds

In the fire the face of passion
In desire’s embracing fashion
Love that once to this sweet soul
Was rationed within embers’ keep 

Now she stares into the ashes
Glowing embers, little flashes
Til the blinking of her lovely
lashes fall into a sleep

He slowly enters, stepping lightly
Lights again the fire nightly
Still he watches o’er his love
From brightly shining stars above
