Make Me a Memory

Dream me a fantasy, write me a tale
Fly me to clouds on a white feathered sail
Flutter a butterfly yellow and red
Paint me a memory to etch in my head

Snow me a blanket of white crystal ice
Bake me aromas of cinnamon spice
Warm me a fire when the sun has gone down
Sleep me a dream wrapped in pillows of down

Sail me on oceans of powerful tides
Swirl me in whirlpools in roundabout rides
Dare me adventure upon the blue sea
Make me a memory of wind blowing free

Grow me a garden that sparkles in dews 
Spatter the petals in bright pinks and blues
Rain me a drizzle to feed them with wet
Delight me a garden that I’ll not forget

Dream me a love who is honest and true
Wrap me in pleasure the way that you do
Heat me in passion with words from your pen
Kiss me a memory of love once again

Cry me a tear from the pits of my soul
Laugh me a giggle and reach me a goal  
Wonder my mind at the wisdom you write
Make me a memory, dear poet, tonight
