Challenge Description: Write a poem about Spam, the food! Turkey Spam, Ham Spam, do you like it? do you love it? do you want some more of it? mmmmmmm spam!

          (S.P.A.M.)
Specially Processed Appetizing Meat 


Spam is the best thing a dollar can yield 
Especially the very last dollar you wield 
Folded down in your pocket as deep as you can 
Abandoned til hunger spurns memories of Spam 

The thing you were raised on, the meal of the day 
The staple upon which at grace you would pray 
Most modest of sirloin, the super mom’s fix 
The dinner she served in a five minute mix 

So great and so versatile. Nothing can match 
The meat that demands a tin key to unlatch 
A treasure of nourishment locked in a can 
Marked in bold letters the golden word “SPAM” 

It’s great in a sandwich, or broiled like fish 
So tasty in omelets or a casserole dish 
At galas and cocktails, each time that you dine 
Dip it in caviar, serve it with wine 

“How quaint and endearing“, your guests applaud 
While trying to swallow the treats that they laud 
The kids say your sandwich is the  best thing around 
Just add mustard to leftover Spam that you ground 

With fried eggs and grits, it’s a real tasty treat 
It fills up your innards with good processed meat 
Next time you are hungry, and the limit’s a buck 
Don’t worry, my friend, cause I think you’re in luck
