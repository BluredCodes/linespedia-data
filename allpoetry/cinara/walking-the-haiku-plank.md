Haiku too hard
Cinara takes the plunge
Tries anyway


Walking the Haiku Plank

_____

Old rocking chair
Squeaking on summer porch 
Cricket responds
_____

On linen sheets,
Moon, the only witness  
As life begins
_____

Crystal snowflakes
landing on a coat sleeve 
Stars from heaven 
_____

A sun-lit web
quivering with dewdrops
Architect hidesPlease critique
