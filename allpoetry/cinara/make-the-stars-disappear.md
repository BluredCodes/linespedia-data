Make the Stars Disappear


Make the stars disappear, make the moon go away
I can no longer watch how they taunt me this way
For the moon lays so gently on pillow and bed
And the stars twinkle love-light on white quilted spread 

I beg of the little stars lighting the skies
Please wipe out the memories of love from your eyes
For you were the witness to love’s splendid bliss
Please undo the lullabies, un-kiss the kiss

Dear moon, will you listen to heart’s gentle plea
Please un-glisten opal white beauty on me
Un-glitter his eyes with the gems from above
Undo his caresses, unmake all the love

You two were the ones who enchanted his heart
Now please un-bewitch this one so torn apart
Unfurl all the darkness of midnight‘s black sky
Unravel the dreams of a fool such as I

Close your eyes, little stars, bow your head, moon of grace
For shame should envelope your beautiful face
Bestow no more splendor upon this, my soul
Please un-kiss the kisses that made me feel whole
