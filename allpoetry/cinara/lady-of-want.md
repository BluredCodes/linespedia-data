Lady of Want

she is on the constant brink of 
standing on
the edge of desire
sometimes she sits on the moon
and smiles down at me
asserting her independence
like a confident feline
sometimes she floats just ahead of me
within the whisper of my breath
tumbling and twirling like a butterfly 

but once in a while
 she allows herself capture

she walks, she floats,
she smiles, she gloats
she’s there, she’s not
so elusive
inconclusive
but she is real,
at least she is for me
and she is always beautiful

she’s quite visible and clear
til she walks into the mist
and hides
but I walk in her shadow
always following her
in constant persuit 
and she changes form, and hue
and direction
but she’s always there
at least she is for me

she has put me on a pedestal of pride
she’s thrown me into the abyss of sorrow
she’s taken me to the height of ecstasy

they always called me a dreamer
and that’s what she is

a fantasy

a wish
something I aspire to
something I fight for
something I live for
she is a dream

and I have caught her once or twice
and lost her many times
but I never gave up
she’s still there
sitting on top of the moon
and just a sudden twist of fate
could bring her spiraling down
 into my hand

she walks, she floats
she smiles, she gloats
she’s there, she’s not
but she is very real

at least she is for me
