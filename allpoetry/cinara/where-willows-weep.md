Where Willows Weep

Beside the lake where waters sleep
And dreams of love in silence creep
We watched the evening shadows fall
Upon the stillness of it all

Sweet twilight laid across the west
Its veil of dark on hilly crest
The night descended, waters cooled
And bathed the stars on rippled pool

The moon was spread with halo mist
And shone where love and dreams exist
Beside the lake, now edged in frost
Sweet love was born and love was lost

Deep waters sleep and dreams have crept
Where willows weep and I have wept
