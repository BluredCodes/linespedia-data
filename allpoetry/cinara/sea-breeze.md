Sea Breeze

Coconut palm is swinging, dancing,
Swaying in the breeze
Heavy leaf of mango
And the old banana trees

Everything is moving
To the rhythm of the seas
That stirs the mighty ocean waves
And brings a welcome breeze

It blows the golden of my hair
And warms my tearful face
As I prepare to leave you now
And all your charming grace

For I am homeward bound today
To a far, far colder place
And I will miss the sea breeze
And your comforting embrace

Please tell me that you’ll wait for me
Please put my mind at ease
And I will come again one day
To winds that never cease

I’ll see you in my dreams, my love
Beside the churning seas
Where everything is swaying
In the gentle ocean breeze

Coconut palm is swinging, dancing,
Swaying in the breeze
Heavy leaf of mango
And the old banana trees
And everything is moving
To the rhythm of the seas
