Again

I cannot doubt the path I chose
though piercing thorns have marred the rose
For I have won a garden’s fill
of violet and daffodil

But if I lived by my desire
I‘d not have walked through stinging briar
My days ill spent would only yield 
a daisy meadow’s yellow field 

Though wisdom triumphed in the end
I’d love to try it all again
