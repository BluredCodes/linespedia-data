Silent Are The Dreams

Silent are the dreams that come on tiptoes in the night
Silent are desires flaming in a candle’s light
Silent are the songs that have been dwelling in my heart
My room is wrapped in feather sounds since we have been apart

Quiet  is tomorrow’s hope that  rustles in the trees
Hushed are whispering verses that are blowing in the breeze
Soft is someone’s trembling voice that settled in my ear
Unheard are my illusions that are ever drawing near

I can hear the murmur in throbbing of my heart
Sounds of forest creature’s in the blindness of the dark
The babbling of little brook that springs from hillside wells
The clear and lovely carillon of  country chapel bells

Life is filled with music, though it’s silence that I hear
The sound of words unspoken that are loudest to the ear
I look into the midnight sky for Jupiter and Mars
To chime the love that’s in your heart - but silent are the stars
