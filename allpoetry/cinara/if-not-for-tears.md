If Not for Tears

What is this sorrow, but a fallen tear
That washes heartache onto pleasure’s face
As eyes unveil the misery and fear
Unleashing pain held in an inner place

Then, what is joy, but teardrops in the eye
Cascading over waterfalls of strife
That all may for a passing moment spy 
Through laughter’s open window pane of life

What is love, but moments when we weep
For sentiments we harbor in the heart
The joys and sorrow that sogently creep
Into the night when lovers are apart

If not for tears, our hearts would never prime
What’s hidden in the deepest wells of time
