Same Time, Next Life

The days are short, the hours are few
I’ve not much time for loving you
We met upon the sunset hour
And witnessed love begin to flower

The body has grown ill and weak
The mind too fragile dreams to seek
But hearts are beating ecstasy
In rhythms of the pounding sea

Love has come to us too late
A lover’s loss, a wicked fate
But time will bury all our strife
I’ll meet you here same time, next life

Just stand upon the ocean pier
The loveliest evening of the year
Against the red of sunset sky
A soul that loved but did not die

Your face will not appear the same
And I won’t even know your name
But this I swear to be the true
Within my heart I’ll know it’s you 

We’ll be as one, as man and wife
Just meet me here same time, next life
