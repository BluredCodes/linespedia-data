Challenge Description: Mission if you accept it. You have had your awakening or calling from God. You gift of Prophecy has been awaken. As a new prophet you shall under go the most trying of Trubulations and Revelations of all the Trials of the coming Event of pure Calamity. You have constant dreams of them, visions and words come to you from many spirit guides. Time is drawing near for so many global Calamities are coming and you must warn as many as you can before it comes or will it for ever rest on your soul! Write as deep as your soul will let you, as if you are channeling God or spirits themselves. Write in such Divine words that even yourself will feel great fear. The future of Mankind will rest on your words, choose them wisely and make sure the message comes out as deep imagery as you possibly can. You are now a Prophet so you must do your job or suffer eternal Hell. Write about an Earthquake, Flood, Nuclear War, Asteroid Impact or any other Disaster. Do not hold back. A true prophet shall come before mankind is done...



The Final Toll 

Earth is our inherent dwelling 
Borrowed for a given time 
Time is ever passing, swelling 
To the brink of the sublime 

That is when earth, disappearing, 
To it’s origins of naught 
Tolls the bell that some are fearing 
Of the end that souls have sought 

Walk the way of forest treasures 
Tread the richness of the earth 
All the wealth cannot be measured 
In its true aesthetic worth 

Nor can life nor love be weighed 
Or calculated in degrees 
Nor the green of meadow’s blade 
Or churning waters of the seas 

The beauty of our place of birth 
Has been a gift for ears and eyes 
And nature speaks the songs of earth 
In rhythms of its lullabies 

The earth was lent in trust to man 
And only God will give the sign 
When sea and forest,  hills and sand 
Will disappear by His design 

There will be a day, and hour 
When He sets our spirits free 
To be unchained from earthly powers 
Into His eternity 

Balls of fire will fall from heaven 
Cast down by the hand of God 
Destroying  every man that’s ever  
On a forest pathway plod 

From infinity above 
The vastness of a space that’s void 
Will come a glaring signal of 
A giant mass that God’s employed 

To slice the earth in bits and pieces 
Opening its fiery core 
This will be the death of man 
The life of soul forever more 

The fire from heaven will not cast 
A look of fear upon your face 
But rather visions of your past 
And all the love that you embrace 

Walk the way of forest treasures 
Look toward heaven with eyes of trust 
Reach with spirit toward tomorrow 
Leaving garments in the dust 

Listen to the chiming toll 
For it is beckoning your soul
