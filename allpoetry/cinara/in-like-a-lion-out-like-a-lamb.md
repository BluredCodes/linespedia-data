In Like a Lion - Out Like a Lamb

You came in like a lion, so pompous and proud
Scanning the room for the usual crowd
Talking and laughing and flaunting your charms
Facing, embracing each one in your arms

I waited my turn just like everyone else
My eyes so entranced by your wonderful self
Oh, how I cherished you, equal to none
And was saddened to think I was not number one

You spent the whole evening enchanting the room
With your laughter that every one tried to consume
So charming, so witty, so handsome, so smart
Capturing many a soft empty heart

The women all catered to your every whim
And I could feel jealousy brewing within
They stared at you endlessly, so mesmerized
By charisma that twinkled with joy in your eyes

By the end of the evening, you finally came
And gave me a kiss as you whispered my name
Then you closed your blue eyes and your one-year-old charms
And left like a lamb in your daddy’s warm arms
