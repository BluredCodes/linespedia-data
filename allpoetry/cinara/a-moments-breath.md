A Moment’s Breath

Of bliss and sorrow, this I know
That both can penetrate and flow
In equal measure of unrest
Like stinging lightening through my chest

A moment’s breath of reminisce
Can launch from memories of a kiss
Sweet spear of sorrow piercing me
With burning pain and ecstasy

But what remains in all above
Is love, and joy at having loved
