Dreamers

I’m standing in a line, it seems
With other souls with other dreams
Each one awaits his given turn
As moments tick and candles burn

And all the eyes are fixed upon
The silver gleams on field and pond
Reflections on the trees and hedge
From dreams beyond horizon’s edge

So patiently we wait our turn
Each with an inner dire yearn
We stand and wait in starlight’s beam
Each one in line to catch a dream

If I could have but one desire
I’d not allow the candles fire
To waste the wick til I have seen
All those in line obtain their dream

A dreamer is a dreamer’s friend
And all possess a poet pen
