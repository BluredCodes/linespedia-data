I AM 

I am the one to give you birth 
To validate your place on earth 
To tell the tales of who you are 
And hold the beams of evening star 

I am the one to give you life 
And understanding of its strife 
To live, to die, to seek what’s true 
I am the one who teaches you 

I am a giver of delight 
In glow of evening’s silver light 
To speak of sorrow’s tender trace 
I am the one that you embrace 

I am the one that you respect 
For vicious moods I resurrect 
I put your mind and soul to rest 
In rocking cradles on my crest 

I am the one to give you strength 
To venture forth at any length 
Providing you with endless means 
To seek the bounty of your dreams 

I am the place where God abides 
Where blessings flow with changing tides 
I am the one He chose to be 
The rhythms of eternity 

You touch me softly on the rim 
You dive the depths of passion’s whim 
You ride the waves on golden sails 
Beside the beauty of the whales 

I am your friend, your foe, your love 
Reflecting moon and stars above 
I am the force from which you came 
And you have christened me a name 

         I am The Sea
