That’s All I Know

Each dawn begins with purple skies
Each evening bathed in lullabies
Some days bring joy and some bring strife
And that is what I know of life

I see reflected in one’s eyes
The light of truth, the blink of lies
Deceit can often start in youth
And that is what I know of truth

I’ve manned the helm upon a sea
With no direction guiding me
Not one kind soul was standing near
And that is what I know of fear

I lost the best and felt the worth
Of what was lost upon the earth
But in the end I know the best
Is understanding spirit’s quest

The light of God shines on my face
He taught me all I know of grace 
The time is near my final breath
And that is all I know of death

I’ve opened up my spirit’s door
In verses etched forevermore
I’ve told the truth for all to see
That’s all I know of poetry

My book may well remain untouched
Stored in a box in attic’s must
Forgotten on a dusty shelf
But I was always true to self

My deeds I’ve done, the seeds I’ve sewn 
That is the life I’ve always known
The dreams we wasted, that were ours
That’s all I know of moon and stars
