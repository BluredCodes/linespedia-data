A Dream Exists

A dream that has not yet been kissed
Remains unseen, but does exist
A vision that is undefined 
Imbedded in a dreamers mind

It lives as sure as dawning light
Erases moons and stars of night 
Forgotten in the daytime sun
Remaining on the path they’ve spun

Just as a tulip bulb will rest
Beneath the snow in winter’s nest
As warmth of sun and springtime mist
Give life, so does a dream exist 

The flower lives and worries not
Which season is, if cold or hot
To stars that fly the Milky Way
The day is night and night is day

A dream is visible and clear
Though not to eye or hand or ear
It lives within the heart and mind
As sure as rambling roses twine

Just as the breeze remains unseen
As barren trees will turn to green  
As stars will disappear at dawn
As fairies sprint across the lawn
