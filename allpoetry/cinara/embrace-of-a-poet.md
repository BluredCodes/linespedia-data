Embrace of a Poet

A greater debt I’ve never paid
Than bitter thorns upon me laid
More dark the canopy of strife
Than all the twilights of my life

My tears have worn a weathered hole  
Into the deepness of my soul
Where rivers still meander through
A canyon of the deepest blue

A poet wrapped in simple grace
The words of truth in warm embrace
And granted payer of the toll
A river’s ride through misty soul

I saw a stream in deepest wells
Of blessings churned in joyful swells
And in the currents of the stream
Still flowed the essence of a dream

Let waters flow, let sorrow pass
The joys I know will far outlast
The tears that dwindle to an end
Swept by the words of poet’s pen
