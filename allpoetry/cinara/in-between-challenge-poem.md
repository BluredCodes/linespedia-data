In Between 

In chasms twenty fathoms deep 
On ancient floor sea creatures creep 
Descendants from a former age 
Surviving turn of every page 

Translucent blues like gemstones gleam 
In currents of the ocean stream 
Where porpoise glides in water’s flow 
As forces swiftly move below 

On surfaces of sparkling green 
Atop the oceans sunlit sheen 
Above the fathoms lies a bed 
That glows at dusk in sunset red 

‘Tis here I float upon a craft 
And watch the sea from vessel’s aft 
As wind and water touch my face 
I know full well my proper place 

Above the sea, the purple sky 
Fades into dark as hours go by 
A sea of stars then veils the black 
With twinkling lights from eons back 

From ocean’s depth to heaven’s height 
From sea to sea in moonlit night 
A man can only dream a dream 
And live in Nature’s “in between”
