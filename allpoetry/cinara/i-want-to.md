I Want To….

I want to sooth you as the mist has bathed forget-me-nots of blue
As nighttime haze drops honey wetness drizzling in dew
I want to warm you as the rising sun dissolves the morning mist
Radiating through to flowers wanting to be kissed

I want to touch you as a butterfly would brush a powdered wing
Against the petal softness in the balmy days of spring
I want to kiss you as a honey bee lingers ever near
To summer flower’s  treasured nectar, sweet and moist and dear 

I want to hold you like a morning glory wraps its twisting vine
Around each crevice of the trellis as it slowly curls and winds
I want to love you as the raindrops drench each petal of a rose
Dipping it in nourishment of wetness as it grows

As lilac spreads its soft perfume
From lavender and purple plume
As humming bird so sweetly sups 
From honeysuckle's golden cups
