Day and Night

The day speaks loudly of itself
in brandishing its face
The night slips silent as an elf
in shadows of its grace

The sun will force the eye to gaze
at splendors all have seen
The little stars will only dance
in eyes that have a dream

The sun puts on a glorious show,
from dawn ‘til evening  comes
The moon shines but an opal glow
to which the heart succumbs
