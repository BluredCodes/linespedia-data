Something Small

Sometimes our eyes are struck in awe
at splendors trivial and small
Sometimes the sweetest music heard
is in the trill of winter bird  

The steps of faith are not immense
to visit God’s magnificence 
The grandeur of His mighty power
is in the silence of a flower
