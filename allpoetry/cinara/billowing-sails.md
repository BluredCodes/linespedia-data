Billowing Sails

Billowing sails in the soft morning breeze
Capture the scent of the blue surging seas
Only the wind and the water and I
Know of the freedom below mist sky

Skimming through waters of bottomless depth
Feeling the wonder of life in each breath
Changing direction as sea winds will blow
Shifting the wind driven sails as I go

Nothing can settle my spirit’s unrest
Like soaring on sails over silvery crest
With wings of an eagle I float in the wind
Relinquishing sorrowful feelings within

The sea is my freedom. The waves , my delight
In redness of sunset or blackness of night
All of this pleasure runs deep through the vein
‘Til sails turn my heavy heart homeward again

If I could live all of life’s seasons afloat
Upon open seas in a safe sturdy boat
Weathered by time and by wind driven rain
Sheltered from sorrows most deeply carved pain

I’d go where life leads me on waters of blue
For joy is there drifting in memories of you
And only the breezes , the sky and the sea
Know of the measure of love within me
