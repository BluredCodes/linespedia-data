Magic Moment

The dusk brings a sense of beginning and end
When sky’s reds and purples and aureates blend
In silence the heavens announcing that this
Is the day’s final moment of glory and bliss

The earth and the season  have somehow produced 
The timing of swallows to gather and roost
A signal for crickets to fiddle a tune
When bat silhouettes flap across silver moon

I sit and observe evening’s drama unfold
Though there’s no final peace in all I behold
Til the moon rises up in a soft globe of white
And little stars come out to cover my night
