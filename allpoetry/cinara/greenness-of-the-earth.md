Greenness of the Earth 

Step light upon the greenness of the earth
For treasured gifts of natures realm are such
That floral field and velvet forest fern
Are meant for only wondering eyes to touch 

Take pleasure when a gliding wing of white
Draws circles of contentment in the sky
Memorize the elegance of flight
And photograph the eagle with your eye
 
Touch gently on the petal of a rose
Stroke the powdered wing of butterfly
Capturing its iridescent glows
Not with the finger tip, but with the eye

Bathe your feet in waters of the stream
Let its movement flow right through your veins
Just as it gushes through the planet’s seams
Fresh and swollen from the spring time rains
 
Let earth and stream and fragrant scent of flower
All living things of true aesthetic worth
Caress your soul with joy of nature’s power
Step light upon the greenness of the earth
