Challenge Description: Write me a poem about your favorite fruit. I want to see a lot of visual imagery in this & delicious descriptions (teehee) have fun! 



Sweet, Sweet Wine 

The drink of sweet serenity 
The vineyard brewer’s cup of tea 
In crystal stemware’s sparkling shine 
Is poured a mellowed ageless wine 

The drink of friendship, love and life 
The brew that quenches pain and strife 
Stirs all the passion of its balm 
Into a mix of midnight calm 

The rim of goblet slowly tips 
To pour its fragrance on your lips 
A tea that stewed in barrel’s well 
Where smooth aromas churn and swell 

The grapes that rendered such a brew 
To warm the blood inside of you 
Were grown with care from nourished earth 
Then crushed for fruits of other worth 

The future potion for the mind 
Now hangs in clusters from the vine 
To be the purple, red and white 
Of midnight moon and star’s delight 

My laden goblet lifted up 
Saluting fruits of potion’s cup 
Here’s to the grape that has bestowed 
From vine’s abundant mother lode 

The passion held within my glass, 
The rendered joys as moments pass 
My cup contains a vintage tea 
Of land and earth, of sky and sea 

In soft delight of warming brew 
My mind recalls sweet thoughts of you 
The balm that settles on my lips 
Is savored taste of tender kiss
