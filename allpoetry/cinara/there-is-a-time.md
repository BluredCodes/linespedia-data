There is a Time 

For every man there is a time to ponder his belief
There is a time for butterfly to spread its rainbow sheath 
For new-hatched bird the time will come to stretch a feathered wing
For every soul there is a time to hear the angels sing
 
For every heart there is an hour of sorrow to endure
And with the darkness comes awareness unperceived before
For every night there is a dawn that’s new and fresh and clean
That brings a bright perspective that the eye has never seen
 
A passing cloud above the field for moments hides the sun
The knots of gnarled grief in time will also come undone
So great will be the joy that we will cup our hands to hold 
The splendor of a lily as its velvet wings unfold
 
Darkness turns to laughter at the time the heart appoints
All that passes is a plan the hand of God anoints
There is a time to wipe the wetness out of sorrow’s eye
There is a time the timid wing will soar into the sky
