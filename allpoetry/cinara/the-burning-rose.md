The Burning Rose


The rose, the rose, the lovely rose
The red of flame and passion grows
So sweetly on the rambling vine
Just as the love that once was mine

Oh, rose of beauty, crimson red
Your petals speak the thoughts unsaid
You stir the heart and singe the mind
With embers from another time

Every bloom displays a name
That’s burning there in scarlet flame
A love that laid a rose of red 
Across a lover’s moonlit bed

The rose, the rose, the flower of bliss
The fiery page of reminisce
Is opened as the petals turn
And gazed upon with eyes that yearn

Silently your blooms unfold
The story you had left untold
And now the flame has been reborn
On vines abundantly adorned 

Oh, burning rose you please my eyes 
For on the arbor sweetly lies
A silken touch of velvet’s lure
A soft caress at memory’s door
