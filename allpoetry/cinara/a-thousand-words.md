A Thousand Words

A thousand words upon a page
Can never quite amaze
As a simple line of poetry
A lovely silken phrase

A glittering field of fallen snow
Can never mystify
As a simple crystal snowflake
That stays etched within the eye 

A universe of stars above
Cannot evoke delight
As a single candle glowing
In the blackness of the night

A book is like a treasure chest
To open up the mind
A poem settles in the soul
And stays the rest of time
