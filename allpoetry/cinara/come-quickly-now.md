Come Quickly Now

Come quickly now and quietly
And bring the coming day
Twirling in the spaces
On a slipper of ballet

Around a fiery sphere you twirl
And gracefully unveil
The floral blooms of April
That are left upon the trail

The universe is savoring 
The fragrances of spring
That spin from off the blossoms’ 
Golden petals that you bring

Come fill my garden up with joy
And green on every tree
And fill my eyes with colors
Spinning into ecstasy

The time is right, the earth is tilting 
Into springtime pose
Balancing and whirling 
On the tip of ballet toes

It’s time to show your glory now
It’s time to cleanse my soul
With sense of new beginnings
As sweet daffodils unfold

Come quickly now to fill my head
With lilac’s scent and tulip’s red
