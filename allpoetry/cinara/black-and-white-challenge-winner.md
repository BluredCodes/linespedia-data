Challenge Description by Silent Wolf: Ok... I don't want to sound too picky but my 2 year anny is comming up and I'm looking for a real sweet poem about a wolf and a dragon (they are mates) The longer the better. The winner will be the one that sparks my interest or hits me the hardest. Character info avaliable in my poem "Wolf and Dragon" or "Silent Wolf" (Dragon's name is Crying Wolf's name is Silent) 


Black and White 

Shiny and soft is the coat on her back 
Star laden sky lights her garment of black 
Reflecting its sheen in the wintry cold 
And two mystic eyes, of florescence in gold 

The night is her element, ebony twin 
A friend and a sister, their spirits within 
The night winds will howl through the evergreen trees 
The black wolf will answer the ancestors pleas 

In daylight she’s silent, at night she’s alive 
In nature and darkness her energies thrive 
As she thrusts her head upward and howls to the moon 
The spirits come gently at her echoing croon 

They sing through the winds of a dragon of white 
Who is maker of crystal ice painted in white 
Born in the clouds and laid soft on the ground 
A dragon of calmness in beauty’s surround 

They whispered a secret to wolf of the wild 
They said wolf and dragon are each God’s pure child 
Pursued by the humans, escaping their plight 
Both creatures of nature, both loved by the night 

One evening, the black one at midnight had found 
At the edge of a meadow, deep snow on the ground 
And there in the woodland a dragon of white 
A soul mate of nature, a friend of the night 

They came to each other, and stared in the cold 
White dragon with black eyes. Black wolf, eyes of gold 
From the very first moment, they felt a strong love 
Blessed by the spirits who watched from above 

Black is the color of hours of night 
White is the wind driven crystal delight 
Happy are they in a haven of bliss 
Black and white love on a night such as this
