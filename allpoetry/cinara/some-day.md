Some Day

Some day you will unveil the heart that is blind 
As night spreads before you a polka dot sky
When moonlight has laid down an aura divine  
You’ll see fires burn in a woman’s dark eye

Someday in the future when darkness has crept 
And clouded your dreams with the blackness above
The little white stars will dry tears you have wept
And perhaps you will notice the one who’s in love

I’ll wait for the time when you long to embrace
In the warmth and the comfort of midnights sweet balm
For sooner or later your footsteps will trace
A deliberate path to my doorway of calm

As soon as you see how I bathe in the stars
Whenever you notice desires on my face
Lit up by the Milky Way drifting afar
That descends to earth’s nearness when two souls embrace

Perhaps you’ll regret that somehow you let go
The one left unnoticed, as love passed you by
Though you’d stay if you’d unveil the passionate glow
Of fires that rage in a woman‘s dark eye

The two will be wrapped in the sweetness of night
My eyes will reflect joys of polka dot sky
Descending upon you invited delights 
As you gaze in the deepness of love in my eye e
