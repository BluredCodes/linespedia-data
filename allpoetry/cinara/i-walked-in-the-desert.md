I Walked in the Desert

Dusk in the desert where quietness lies
Indigo blue under lavender skies
Young Golden Eagle in breeze of the night
Last beams of sunlight on wings edged in white

Sun drops down slowly behind mountain’s ledge
The last golden beams fanning out from the edge
Canyon is silent, the heavens are strewn
With bright little stars and a glorious moon

Skies light the footpath of silvery sand
Where I linger to view evening splendors so grand
The dawn creeps in slowly with joy to the eyes
The mountains of purple beneath crimson skies

So very few flowers, yet beauty unfurled
In this ever surprisingly colorful world
I walked in the dessert, alone in the sand 
God stood there beside me with paintbrush in hand
