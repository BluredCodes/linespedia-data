---
title: "Do you truly know me?"
writer: "taracer 1986"

Do you truly know me as you claim you do
can you still gaze into my eyes and know my every thought and dream.
Do you feel the rate of my heart quiken 
when you walk into the room?
Do you sense that my one true purpose is simply to complete you.
Do you think about me throughout the day
as you haunt my dreams at night.
Do you desire me with the same passion
that once caused you to continue to fight.
Do you need me to be near you as my body craves your touch.
Do you require only me to find the happiness life demands.
