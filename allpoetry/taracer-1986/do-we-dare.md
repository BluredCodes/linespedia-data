I can feel your eyes watching me,
like warm loving hands against my skin.
In the back of my mind I wonder,
are you thinking what I'm thinking?
I just can't stop myself from wanting
the things that only you seem to be able to offer.
Constantly craving that feeling of satisfaction,
to know that you're completely mine.
So am I living a fairtytale,
a distortion of reality that I need to be true?
It may be just me thinking,
but somehow my thoughts won't stop.
Something in your eyes tells me
that its not simply a dream 
or just my imagination.
There's something there,
but will either of us move forward?
Do we dare ask for more
and risk what we have now?Let me know what you think......i appreciate constructive criticism.
