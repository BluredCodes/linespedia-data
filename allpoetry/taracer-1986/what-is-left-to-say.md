---
title: "What is left to say?"
writer: "taracer 1986"
slug: "what-is-left-to-say"

What is left to say
when we look into each other's eyes
both of us lost in tears
and both aware of what must be the conclusion...
What is left to say
when we've said our I love you's
made our promises
knowing that we can never be together...
What is left to say
when I awake at night missing you
wondering if at that moment you're missing me too...
What is left to say 
when my heart is breaking
and you're the only one who can stop the pain...
What is left to say
when I've tried to move on and can't
when I can't forget
or don't want to...
What is left to say
when you're holding me and I never want you to let go...
What is left to say
when all is said and done
when I'm trapped and I can't find the words
to make things ok...Please let me know what you think and any critisims you have.
