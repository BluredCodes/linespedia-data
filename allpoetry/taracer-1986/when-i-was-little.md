title: "When I Was Little"

When you're a little girl
      you have millions of fantasies.
Dreams that one day
      will somehow become your reality.
Simple pictures in your mind
      of how the world would one day be.
The perfect view
      the way that you believe things are.
An ideal of where you will end up
      maybe ten to twenty years from now.
Traditionally you'll be married
      to your one true love, mr. right.
Together you'll have a couple kids
      living happily ever after.
Then one day you awake
      woundering what to do with your life.
Constantly chasing your fantasies
       that once seemed so simply real...What do you think?  Can you relate?
