Writing is an expression of my deepest thoughts.
To be able to put into words 
my ideas, my longings, my feelings.
Describing everything that mean anything.
Reflecting on important events,
occasions that require more of an explination.
Viewing things in a different light
and comparing them to something else.
Writing is a talent,
the motivation comes freely.
The ease comes with experience,
practice, and perfection.
A gift to cherish
positive self-expression.
Writing for me,
is more than mere words on a piece of paperAgreed?
