I watched you cross the street today 
I knew that it would be the last 
I tried to soak of every detail 
For when the moment passed 

A bus pulled up and blocked my view 
You were gone to another life 
In spite of sadness, I had to laugh 
I thought you’d be my wife 

The light turned green and the bus was off 
I was left in a cloud of gray 
The thick exhaust explained my mood 
More than words could say 

The last day of our daily routine 
Not coming home tonight 
I will miss everything 
You told me that and you were right
