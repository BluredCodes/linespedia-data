I miss you More than a snowflake misses its cloudCrying crystalline tears all the way downMore than a candle misses its flameRobbed of its light which it cannot reclaimMore than a train misses its trackWhen it’s passed and knows that it cannot go backMore than an angel misses its GodAnd misses the heavens from which it’s outlawed More than a man that misses the thingThat he found in one fall and lost in one springI miss you
