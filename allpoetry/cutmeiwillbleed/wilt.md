---
title: "Wilt"
writer: "CutMeIWillBleed"

You picked me up and brushed me off
And carried me to a place where I could die
It's so deceiving, there are flowers everywhere
But flowers always decay with time

They're so striking until they wither
But that's the cruelty of life
And the greatest lesson I've ever learned
Is that everything beautiful dies
