A Part of You

Drinking each word like the booze that makes her numb
She's a drunk trapped in the bounds of time
In each tear is the anguish of a thousand tears
And, wrapped in the arms of relief, she cried
She listens to you and hears you as well
Swallowing your words like they were lost pieces of her
Like those words were a puzzle piece in the picture of us
And every fucking word you said was just to drag her down
She's an addict to pain, her drug of choice
She's addicted to you like you were her fix
And, throughout her suffering, she'll be enveloped in you
As unreal as you may seem, in you she'll find solace
In you she'll feel livid
In you she'll find torment
She'll crawl inside herself 
And be a pill in the bounds of you
She doesn't know what to feel anymore, just that there's nothing left

All she knows
Is that she is desperately in need of you
