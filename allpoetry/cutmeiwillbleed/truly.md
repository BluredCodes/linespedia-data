Truly

Just feel my heartbeat
It's renewed and crawling with hope
This sensation is euphoric
This feeling of alive I've developed

Then my dreams wither and die 
As the sun descends inside my head
The dark hides me away
And I am filled with dread again

Just look into my eyes
They're a window to my soul
A magic looking glass 
To feel the desire we hold

A lust for life
I don't want to die
Just to be whole again
To truly be alive
