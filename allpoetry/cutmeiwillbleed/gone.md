Gone

You're so sad
And you make sure to let me know
And to let me see inside you
You scratch a hole in your soul

And if I saw you in Heaven
I'd be sure to let you know
How fucking tired you made me
And that it dragged me down

Will you even remember me
Or think about me when I'm gone?
You make me feel unwanted 
And I don't go where I don't belong

It's our last dance
Before reality sets in
Dancing where the long road ends 
And stopping where the end begins

It's finally time
I'm unravelled and dead on the floor
Because there was a strong person inside me
But she doesn't live here anymore
