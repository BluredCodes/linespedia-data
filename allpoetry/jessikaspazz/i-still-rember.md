---
title: "I Still Rember"
writer: "jessika.spazz."
slug: "i-still-rember"

I don't want to grow up.
Eternally young.
No responsabilities.
Long days in the sun.
I wish i could express myself,
I wish someone would listen.
Making others happy used to be my only mission.
Confused and worried about what lies ahead, 
The future is something
I most often dread.
Although the present is no better,
For I'm going nowhere fast,
Now that i think about it, 
I long for the past.
Trying to find myself,
Lost in a dark and endless night,
Yet i keep driving along,
I have no head light.
No one understands the way that i think.
Not appreciated for listening,
As if i were a shrink.
Wish life was as simple
as it used to be,
I want to be in the barn again
Together just you and me.
It seems so long ago,
And yet I still remember.
Build a fort
Out of hay
Chase the chickens milk the cows,
Make believe all day.
But those days are gone now,
They're locked inside the past
And together we moved forward,
But the future came to fast.
So fast infact, I lost you.
Somewhere along the way.
And i I guess i just wanted to tell you
I still think of you today.yea old old old.....im not afraid of the future anymore.
