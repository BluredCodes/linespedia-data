I'm stuck inside,
Please let me out, 
I can't put my emotions into words.
If I could
I'd tell you how i feel.
Maybe you'd understand, 
And say you feel the same...
But I don't think so.
I've thought too long,
And laughed to hard.
False happiness protects me from the truth.
I can't explain whats inside me now,
Hoping you'll know where i am.
I can't find myself
I'm lost in a dark place.
There is no light
at the end of this endless tunnel.
Impossible to see,
Impossible to feel,
Impossible to touch,
Impossible to heal.more old stuff...(apologies)
