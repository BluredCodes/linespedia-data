---
title: "Watch Your Step"
writer: "jessika.spazz."
slug: "watch-your-step"
source: "AllPoetry"

Solid ground is hard to find
and when you do, there's peace of mind.
Don't waste that time, or throw it away,
For upon your feet it's hard to stay.
The ground will rumble, the earth will break
Your stability is found at stake.
If you should fall, then who knows when
You'll find that stable floor again.
So watch your step, don't ever fall
Or you'll find yourself as a bouncy ball.
But whats the point of a life like this?
What's a hit without the miss?
