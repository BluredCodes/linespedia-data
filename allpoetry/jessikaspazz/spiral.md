Indescribable feeling.
Not happy, tingly, exciting and light.
The other kind.
Angry, tight, confusing, frustrating and heavy.
You may know it.
You may not.
I do.
I don't like it.
I'm scared.
Of the feeling, and of you.
You are superior.
I am nobody.
I am no one.
You are.
And with you, so am I.
Please don't leave me.
I need you.
You don't need me.
Leave.
You should.
I would.way back....
