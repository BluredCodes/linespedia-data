---
title: "What Can I Do?"
writer: "jessika.spazz."
slug: "what-can-i-do"

Fuck you
I hate you
I can't fucking take you.
I can't stand the feel of this place
I can't stand the sight of your face
Do you think you're blessed with grace?
Do you strive for me to feel disgrace?
Use me again to get up off the ground
So again you can strut with your self-awarded crown.
Why you treat me this way
I can't understand
Because i try and i try,
I give you both of my hands
A piece of advice, a shoulder or two
but the abuse never stops
So what can i do?
