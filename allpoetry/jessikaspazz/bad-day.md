No where to run,
No where to hide.
No one who’ll comfort,
Or stay by my side.
Wish I could show you,
The pain that I feel,
Wish you would help me,
Help me to heal.
But instead you’ll leave me all alone.
And I’v been thinking about, 
Not coming home.
I’ll sit inside,
And think of you,
Pretend my daydreams,
Are all coming true.
I know you think I’m crazy, 
And maybe that’s ok,
Perhaps I’m just having,
A really bad day.
I wish you could see,
My point of view,
Then you might help me,
Help me get through.?/?/98, this is old....
