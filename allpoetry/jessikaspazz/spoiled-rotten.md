title: "Spoiled Rotten"

Two-fifty million kids
In the world today,
All working for the same
Indecent, sleazy pay.

Some kids start out at five,
Then they’re working everyday.
With children in the workforce,
It leaves no time for play.

Parents are in debt,
They’re in trouble and now they owe.
Put the kids to work,
Rob the childhood they’ll never know.

All their lives they’ll work
And waste their days away,
Only to be hungry,
Never getting proper pay.

All that work from dawn ’til dusk
In filthy, fourth hand jeans
Crouched, confined and cramped,
Education’s but a dream.

They’re easy and they’re quiet,
And they don’t know their rights,
If we can keep it down
We’ll never cause a fight.

When I think of all the clothes I wear,
And what I wear them for,
I wonder what the real costs are
And why I paid the store.about child labour (obviously)
