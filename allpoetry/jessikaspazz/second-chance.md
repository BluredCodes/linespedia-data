Why is it that when I try,
I get so nervous,
And act so shy?
I wish I had a second chance, 
Confident and calm, 
I’d make my advance.
Then I’d tell you how I feel,
You understand!!
It seems so real.
But reality burns,
And stings my eyes.
I soon awake, 
And start to cry.
I remember how you looked at me,
It was nothing like I’d drempt it’d be.
My happy thoughts were sent away,
They left me here,
In present day.
And now I’m left without much choice.
To sit and write poetry,
With my inner vioce.
Or run to my room,
Where I can hide.
Or turn to a friend, 
In whom I confide.
Love is great,
Or so you say,
But just you wait,
