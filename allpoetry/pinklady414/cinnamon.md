I want cinnamon kisses again.Flavorful kisses from you.I crave the flavor of your lips.How the taste lingers on my tounge.I want to know that taste.To indulge myself in it.To know your touch and your taste once more.To absorb that flavor.To feel your lips, And how my mouth waters at the thought of your taste.I take any kind of comments. If i don't like them, I will ignore them :P
