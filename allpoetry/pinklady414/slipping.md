I pretend its not there.I am different.The more I pretend that its not there,The WORSE it gets.I slip further.Further into my insane imaginary land.I am losing my grip on     the edge of reality.My fingers are getting tired.I am weak.I can't hold on much longer.But once I let go and fall,   there is no coming back.Well here go!
