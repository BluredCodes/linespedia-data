---
title: "Well shit"
writer: "PinkLady414"
slug: "well-shit"
source: "AllPoetry"

I just want to break down and cry, like a whole hour of sobbing and soaking in my own tears.Just break down and REALLY CRY. Like get all up in the fetal position, knees to my chest, and let it all out.Cry over all the big stuff and all the little shit too.Your boyfriend is and asshole or you stubbed your toe. Let it all out.Open the flood gates.Just let it all out, soak in your tears, into your skin into you soul.I wrote it on the fly
