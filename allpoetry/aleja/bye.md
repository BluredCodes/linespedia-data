-In my mind
i always tried 
to think of you
but im such a fool
you dont deserve me
you deserve the moon
the moon that a fool cant give 
and i just cant live like this
i have to live you, i dont love you
-Why dont you... danm cant hold you
-I dont love you, leave me
-Ill live you because i love you, but unless let me call you
-Do what ever you want
-Alright then, BYE...
