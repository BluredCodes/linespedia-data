Some people want a fairy tale life.
A world of luxury,
no tears to fall on pillows at night.
All smiles and laughter,
without knowing pain or heartache.
Yet i cant help but to want the bad things.
To be able to be strong,
when life tries to render me weak.
I want to know i can rise above pain,
and to stand when heartache knocks me down. 
Most of all to have the knowledge that i can smile,
through desperate tears.
For if i never experience suffering,
then how will i ever know what true pleasure really is?
