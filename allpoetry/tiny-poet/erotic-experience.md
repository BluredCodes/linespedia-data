TOUCHING,TASTING,INDULGING,IN THE SWEETNESS OF THE RHYTHM.TWO SOULS MELTING INTO ONE.PASSION OF LUST AND TRUE LOVE,MIXES AS OUR BODIES GRIND TOGETHER.MOANING,SWEATING,WANTING THE HEAT,THE SOUL,MIND,BODY,ARE COMPLETE.LIPS CARESSING EACH OTHERS SKIN,EXTACY MAKES ME TREMBLE WITHIN.CHILLS RUN UP AND DOWN MY SPINE,AS YOU AND I INTERTWINE.
