Here i lay,Poison attacking my liver.panic attacks,cause my body to quiver.liquids from the i.v,dripping into my veins.swallowed 91 pills,to try and stop the pain.yet still im here,its a miracle i didn't die.but the pain was still there,every time i watched my husband cry.every time he thinks about it,the hurt always shows.if i was asked was it worth it,i would have to say no!
