Cherishing every moment you are nearHappiness and joy is what you giveReveling in your sweet loveing kissesIrrisistable charm is held withinSmile that is sweet and simpleTouching you makes me feel completeOlive skin thats gentle like a dovePretty brown eyes that shine like starsHope and love are bountiful in your heartEverlasting peace is captured in your laughterRemarkable, wonderful beloved husband.
