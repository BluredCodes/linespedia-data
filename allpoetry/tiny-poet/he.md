HE TOUCHES ME
flesh gets chills
exploring my every sense
the night surrounds us
our nakedness exposed
so lost in each other
we forget the world
HE TEMPTS ME
wanting his caress
i surrender to his warmth
bodies exploding
with flames of passion
we fade into one
climaxing together
HE HOLDS ME
hearts still pounding
heavy breaths escape our lips
kisses lingering
gently he smiles
and its then i realize
HE LOVES ME
