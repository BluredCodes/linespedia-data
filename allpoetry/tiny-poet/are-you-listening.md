THE CRICKETS ALWAYS CHIRP.IN THE MOONLIGHT THATS GLISTENING.THE TREES RUSTLE IN THE BREEZE.BUT ARE YOU LISTENING?THE BEES BUZZ AROUND THE FLOWERS.A BLUE BIRD IS WHISTLINGTHE GENTLE RAIN IS FALLING ON THE GROUND.BUT ARE YOU LISTENING?FROGS ARE BUSY CROAKING.A WOODPECKER IS CHISELING.NATURE IS SPEAKING LOUDLY.BUT ARE YOU LISTENING?
