they call me tiny
they pet my fur
for my lunchtime meal
i rely on her
when im in a good mood
i rub against her leg
she thinks im being sweet
but its only an atempt to beg
im smaller than the rest
i have a button nose
sometimes im so hungry
i want to chew on her toes
i growl at other kitties
my meows sound like squeeks
when it comes to being a cat 
im anything but meek
im grey with black stripes
i have big ears
when i come around
i bring sunshine and cheer.
