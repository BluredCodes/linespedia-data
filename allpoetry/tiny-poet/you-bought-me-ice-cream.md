on this gloomy nighti was feeling saddesolate tears i criedin my heart i was madyou called me on the phoneduring your break at work you said comforting thingsbut still i remained hurtso you knew what it tookfor me not to be upset anymoreyou had ice cream deliveredright to the front doorit didn't cost muchjust a few centsbut you'll never knowwhat that little gesture meant
