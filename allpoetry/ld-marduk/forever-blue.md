title: "Forever Blue"

Neath a sky…forever blue,My heart lies heavy…for tis true,I loved thee more than life itself,Yet twas but something that thou felt.Thou could not e’er return to me,Nor…with all sincerity,Feel within thine heart—Thus it was thou said goodbye,Leaving me, alas, to try,And come to terms with losing thee,And of the love I felt—Yes neath a sky…forever blue,My heart lies heavy…and tis due,To feeling love…yet having none,Because tis love I have for one—Who could not e’er return to me,Nor…with all sincerity,E’er feel within her heart.
