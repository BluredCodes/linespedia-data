I walked along a nameless shore,In a place…I had…n’er been before,Looking out…at stars…and sea,With but one thought,That thought…of thee—For twas a time…I didst believe,Thou in thy way, Didst care for me,But fate decided otherwise,I nevermore…to see thee—Thus here I am…I know not where,Perhaps lost…yet still aware,That fate it was…took thee from me,Two lost souls…eternally.
