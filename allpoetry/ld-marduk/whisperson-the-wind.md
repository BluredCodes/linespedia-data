---
title: "Whispers…On the Wind"
writer: "Ld Marduk"
slug: "whisperson-the-wind"
source: "AllPoetry"

There be whispers…on the wind,When alone…tis that I hear them,Soft spoken voices…murmuring,Of days that art…no more—Of souls once known,And held quite dear,Now departed…so that there,Be only whispers…on the wind,To mark…they’d ever been here—Loves desired…and fulfilled,And ones…alas, That never shall,Each adrift…upon the wind,Departed now…n’er to be…again.
