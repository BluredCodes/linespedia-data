---
title: "It Seems"
writer: "Ld Marduk"
slug: "it-seems"
source: "AllPoetry"

It seems like only yesterday,I fell in love with thee,And I have done so ever since,For thou art precious to me—It seems…like only yesterday,I bade thee…marry me,That we might be together,Unto eternity—It seems like only yesterday,My, how the time has passed,And thou art still…the one I love,No need to question that—For it seems like only yesterday,I fell in love…with thee,And it shall always be this way,For thou art precious to me.
