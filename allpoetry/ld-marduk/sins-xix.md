Passion and desire,how compelling...heart and soul,and the hunger...and the yearning,That makes me lose control—For whenever...thou art near me,my blood pressure...starts to rise,and I but long to hold thee,and gaze into thine eyes—For thou...art warm and gentle,thy kisses...sweet like wine,and...to but make love unto thee...Would be...but so divine—For thy skin...is soft as satin,and warm unto the touch...and to hold thee, in my arms...Would mean so very much—For passion and desire,are compelling...heart and soul,and mine are lost in thee...for thou art beautiful.
