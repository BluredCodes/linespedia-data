title: "Sadness"

‘Tis a sadness e’er to know,the feelings that I feel,are ones but unrequited,and thus…but made….unreal—‘Tis a sorrow even more so,to truly understand,that such shall be so…always,though oft I but pretend—That such is not my fate,nor…my destiny,even though the pain,haunts me endlessly—For the feelings that I feel,are ones…but unrequited,and for eternity,it seems…’twill be so always,for ‘tis my fate…and destiny.
