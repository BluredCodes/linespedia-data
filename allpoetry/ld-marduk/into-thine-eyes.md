title: "Into Thine Eyes"

To but look…into thine eyes,The tears there shed…I realize,Art of my doing…I am to blame,Now nothing will ever be the same—I see the pain…upon thy face,The fault be mine…’tis a disgrace,No right have I…to have thy love,What I put thee through hath stripped me of,Any hope…to again…have thee mine—I do not ask thee…to forgive,Though I should wish it…long be it I live,In hopes…before I die,I may find absolution,For the pain, I caused thee,And love lost…unto pride.
