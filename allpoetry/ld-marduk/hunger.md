title: "Hunger"

Oh, how I hunger…yea, indeed,Desire that…which can n’er be,Thus I must guard…my heart and soul,From…e’er yea revealing—What is forbidden to be known,For such…would surely damn me,Yet I cannot…e'er help the fact,To do so…is compelling—For tis but my desire…to but openly,Express my feelings unto all,yet alas…tis sad to say:To do so would be most unwise,Perhaps…be my undoing,Thus I guard…both heart and soul,From ever yea…revealing—What I know will e'er consume me,If left…yea unfulfilled,For to do so…would be most unwise,And perhaps…be my undoing.
