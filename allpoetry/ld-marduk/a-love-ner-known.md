---
title: "A Love N’er Known"
writer: "Ld Marduk"
slug: "a-love-ner-known"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/2530-A-Love-N-er-Known-by-Ld-Marduk"

A broken heart…A tortured soul,Remnants of a love n’er known—Desired, yea, and longed for too,Yet e’er…unable…to pursue—For both…art cautious…and with reason,For each has suffered in due season,And thus…art vulnerable—To suffer such…but yet again,If not careful…to defend,When love is but the goal.For desired, yea, and longed for too,The heart and soul…known to pursue,Love that…they n’er shall know.
