Come hither now my love…say I,Come hither now…to me,And let us but rejoice,Before I must…to sea—Come hither now…and don’t delay,For with the morrows sun,The sea will be awaiting,And I will needs be gone—Come hither now my love…say I,And shed but not a tear,And promise me…when I return,That ye’ll be waiting here—For the morrow soon must be,And I but gone…put out to sea,So let us now rejoice,In the hours that remain,For it will be…. A long, long time,Before I’m home again—So come hither now my love…say I,Come hither now…to me,And let us say our last good-byes,Before I must…to sea.
