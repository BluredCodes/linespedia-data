Each day I wake…with thoughts of thee,For in all the world…there couldna be,Another soul…Tha I love more,Nor careth for…so deeply.And as the day progresses,My thoughts of thee remain,Bringing sunshine even when,The day hath nought but rain.And as the day but endeth,I‘ve thoughts of thee…in dreams,Tha’ make my nights…but pleasant ones,For to me…thou art everything.
