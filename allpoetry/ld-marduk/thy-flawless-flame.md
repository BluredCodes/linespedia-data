Thy flawless flame…
That holds me there,
Heart and soul chained…
And yet unaware,
That such a sentence…
Such as it be,
Is more akin…
To fantasy—
For to envision as I have,
Thy visage…
Here before me,
To have it thus…
Be thy slave I must,
So would I…
Willingly.
