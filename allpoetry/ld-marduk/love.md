title: "Love"
writer: "Ld Marduk"

I know not where to find it,I fail…at this task,And though I long to feel such,Tis something that I lack—Thus, long has been my journey,And difficult…indeed,Because I search…for the unknown,The possibility—Exists I may n’er find it,Or even if I do,May not know it for itself,And…to tell the truth—Fear, that in my failing,Tis that…n’er shall I,Know the pleasures of it,Before my time to die.
