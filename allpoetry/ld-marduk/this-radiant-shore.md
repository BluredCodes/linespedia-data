title: "This Radiant Shore"

Oh where is this place…This radiant shore,Where I may dwell, And wander…no more?Yea once, I thought, Twas here…with thee,But such…alas…twas fantasy,For thou wished not,Nor did desire…ever my company.So where is this place…This radiant shore,Where I may dwell,In peace…once more?Yea once, I thought,Twould be with thee,But that turned out…mere fantasy.
