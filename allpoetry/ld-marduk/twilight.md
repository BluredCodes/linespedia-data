The hour of twilight…has now come,I watch the sky…the setting sun—And my thoughts…they drift to thee,and I wonder…pensively—Where thou art… dost thou still care,or do I seek…What is not there?For long…have we been parted,yet my feelings are the same,yet I know not how ye feel,or if, ’tis but a dream—For the hour of twilight…has now come,perhaps, forever more,for long have we been parted,of thy feelings…I’m unsure—Thus…perhaps, I dream,all of this, a fantasy,my hope…that thou still care,As I do, for thee.
