I’ve never seen thy face,Know not…each line and curve,Nor heard thy voice…’cept in a dream,Yet it sounded soft…and sweet—I’ve never held thee…in my arms,Nor at night…have watched thee sleep,Even so…though it sound foolish,I am in love…with thee—With all my heart…and all my soul,Yea…every part…of me,And though our paths,May never cross,Feeling else…could never be.
