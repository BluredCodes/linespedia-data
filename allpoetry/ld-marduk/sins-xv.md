The taste...of thy sweet lips,is that...which I hunger for,and my soul....does burn with lust,And the desire to but know—The pleasures...of thy flesh,as sweet smelling...as the rose,and the softness...of thy touch,when I...but hold thee close--For thou art...sweet as honey,and I...would have my fill,for great...be my appetite,and greater so...my will—For thou art...my desire,and what...I hunger for:Is a taste...of thy sweet lips,and...but something more--The pleasures...of thy flesh,that are...but thine to give,for they...are the source,that is my hunger,and shall be...evermore.
