I watch thee...quite contentedly,patient...always, to the end...for I know that, such is fate,And therefore...genuine—What I speak of, are passions, and desires...heart and soul,that are thought of, as obsession,For I cannot let them go—For to hold thee, in my arms...in a passionate embrace,to woo thee...and seduce thee,And kiss...thy pretty face—Is a hunger, so consuming,that it makes of me a slave...and I wonder, for a moment,if it all is fantasy?
