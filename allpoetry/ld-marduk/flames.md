title: "Flames"
writer: "Ld Marduk"

Flames of passion…desire,Consume my heart…my soul,With unrequited love denied,That doth torment me so--For therein lies a hunger,That never can be filled,A Maelstrom of emotion,That haunts me…even now--A yearning…an obsession,That drives me fore'er on,T'wards a line I dare not cross,Tis a place…I don't belong--For that place is where thy heart lie,And thou n'er hast granted me,Any hope at all that I…Will e'er…eventually—Thus…I am consumed,By passions…and desires,Unrequited love…denied,I so wish…had but been ours.
