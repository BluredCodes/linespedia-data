A poets phrase of feelings,Expressing more…Than words,But emotions…Of the heart and soul,Art chiefly the concern—So as to better understand,The mixed emotions…That oft can,Confuse and often blur—All manner… Of one’s feelings,So as to sometimes err,Upon the side of caution,When tis love, one seeks to share.Yea a poets phrase of feelings,Expresses more…Than words,But feelings of one’s heart and soul,And the passions often stirred—When one cannot express…In actions or in deeds,What the poet says…with eloquence,To garner…the same thing.
