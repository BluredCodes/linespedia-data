title: "My Answer unto Thee"

Ye asked me of my love for thee,Ye wondered…would it end?And as my answer unto thee,This is what I said—When thunder does not echo,When the stars…no longer shine,When the oceans cease to ebb and flow,Until the end…of time—So shall it be…I shall love thee,Wish only such…in kind,For I am thine…both heart and soul,This I tell thee…so ye know,Such shall be…this love of mine,Now and always…for all time.
