title: "Whispers in the Wind"

I whisper softly…into the wind,So that it…may carry,The words thou never got to hear,And for that…I'm sorry--I took for granted…all the times,Such things I should have told thee,Believing that tomorrow,Twould be a better day--Alas…'tomorrow' never came,For thou were taken from me,And so I whisper…into the wind,Just how much…it was…I loved thee.
