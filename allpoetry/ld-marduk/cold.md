---
title: "Cold"
writer: "Ld Marduk"
slug: "cold"
source: "AllPoetry"

Cold is the heart that hath no love,
And bleak is but the soul,
A barren wasteland…nothing more,
Where nothing tender…grows—
A harsh and somber place indeed,
Full of anguish…misery,
Stark and quite depressing,
A lonely place…to be—
For cold is the heart that hath no love,
A place…that should be pitied,
A bleak and barren wasteland,
Full of anguish…misery,
Stark…and quite…depressing,
A lonely place…indeed
