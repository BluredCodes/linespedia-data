Ah, my soul, in rapt attention,Findeth her a quaint possession,Wanting…yea…but n’er once daring,E’en to approach her—Ah, my heart, with some conviction,Seeketh but from her direction,A chance for eyes to meet—Thus my soul, but now, must ponder,Whether (having fallen under),Her spell was wise indeed—For my heart, can wait no longer,Truly wanting…but to know her,And yet…knows not the words to say—Thus my soul must find a way,To aid the hearts condition,Lest such feelings remain…unknown,And love be…forever missing.
