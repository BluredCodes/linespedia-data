title: "Silent Cries"

Thinking back…on days…no more,I hear the silent cries,The tears of the broken hearted,For the loves…they’ve been denied—And I feel their pain…and anguish,For I too…have known it well,For it…hath been a part of me,Making life…a living hell—A torment…I have long endured,For a time…that knows…no end,For I hear the silent cries,For I know them…all too well,They are the sound…of breaking hearts,In which…no love…be found.
