I once…by thee…was enchanted,Bewitched…were the truth e’er told,And to gaze…into thine eyes,I found…quite mystical—To hold thee…in my arms,Was to take my breath away,And to have thee…in this life,Was something…long…I prayed—Yet fate takes many turns,And the end…is often different,As in the end…we learn—For enchanted…and bewitched was I,As a part of my life…thou became,Not…as I’d envisioned…but one can’t have everything.
