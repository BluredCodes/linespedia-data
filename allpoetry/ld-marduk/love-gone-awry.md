---
title: "Love Gone Awry"
writer: "Ld Marduk"
slug: "love-gone-awry"

Myself, in sorrow…awash with tears,E’er in anguish…over countless years,Tormented by a love…but gone awry—A heart torn asunder, A soul…in torment,Unable to…so comprehend, The reasons…how or why—Yet, each day doth bring the same,And yet there’s no one to explain,Though ‘tis quite oft…I ask…they try—For am I e’er to thus believe,That ‘twas fate…’twas destiny,And all my sorrow…and my tears,Were only real…to me?
