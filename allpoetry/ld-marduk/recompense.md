Alas…such sweet emotion,As my heart and soul possess,Findeth no companion,No means of recompense—For open as my heart may be,One must…first…have eyes to see,Thus…it is…I am alone,For there be no consideration,Of my heart…and yea…my soul,And of how they art…but feeling—For alas…such sweet emotion,As my heart and soul possess,Findeth no companion,No means of recompense—No means to share…nor one to care,And thus…art damned forever,To exist…but nae to flourish,For I’ve no one…my dear.
