Because I breathe of love for thee,My very life depends,Upon the hope…that one day,Thou wilt but understand—Because I breathe of love for thee,I must e’er make amends,Because I must not take for granted,Thou believe this genuine—Because I breathe of love for thee,My very heart and soul,Ache with the desire,Unto the world to show—Because I breathe of love for thee,Thou art my night…my day,And I pray thou understand that,It could be no other way—Because I breathe of love for thee,I must but say again,That having thine returned to me,My very life but doth depend.
