I harbor a longing…deep within,A desire so strong…it border on sin,With an open heart…that seeks…ye see,To win thine own…so that…we be—Together…eternal,Like the sand…and the sea,Like the stars…in the heavens,Two hearts…with one beating—This longing…so deep,This desire…so strong,Could but move mountains,If thou wished it…be so,For thee…I would do such,If thine love…were my own.
