---
title: "I know not…Anymore"
writer: "Ld Marduk"
slug: "i-know-notanymore"

I know not anymore it seems,Whether all the hopes…the dreams,That long I’ve harbored…deep within,Will find a soul…to be shared with—For all the souls whom I have known,Have never found…such feelings…shown,To be…but of their liking—Thus…unable to express,Such feelings…heart and soul…possess,I know not when…or if…or e’er,A soul will even know I care,If such hopes and dreams…are lost,Because such souls believe them not,As being genuine.
