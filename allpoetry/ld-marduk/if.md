---
title: "If"
writer: "Ld Marduk"
slug: "if"
source: "AllPoetry"

If I were…thy love and free… I would walk the skies to be with thee,From the Four Corners of the universe,Lay the stars of the heavens before thee-- I would…in tender song and verse,Of love…sing unto thee, For if I were thy love,Such…I'd do…willingly--If thou were…my love,Didst choose…to be with me,I would cross the farthest points of hell,Simply to be near thee--For I love thee more than life,Would surrender such…to thee,For if thou were my love…I thine,Let it be…eternally.
