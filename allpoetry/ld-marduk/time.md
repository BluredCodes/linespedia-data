title: "Time"

Our time together…is so short,For such exists…in dreams,And yet…one lives a lifetime,In a moment of ones sleep—For there I hold thee…in my arms,Can tell thee…that I love thee,And though tis but a single grain,In the sands of eternity—Such time to me…is precious,How I long that it could be,Time spent in the hours,That are…reality—For our time together…is so short,For it exists…but in my dreams,A single grain…in the sands that are,All of eternity.
