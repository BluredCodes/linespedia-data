title: "Eros Unbound"

The fetters and the chains art loosed,My heart and soul…now free,I voice the feelings long denied,That I have held for thee.No more afraid…of consequence,Of such I say…be damned,For long I’ve hungered for this moment,And now…I make my stand—For the gods in this…art with me,So in this…please understand,I’ve loved thee since we first did meet,It’s grown much stronger…since then—For now I hunger…and I burn,Desire…openly,To take thee in my arms at last,never…to release thee—For the fetters and the chains art loosed,My heart and soul…now free,Tis time thou understood at last,My feelings felt…for thee—No more afraid…of consequence,Of such I say…be damned,For I have waited long enough,Tis time I took thy hand—To hold thee in my arms at last,N’er to let thee go,For I have loved thee all my days,And oft have told thee so—For now the fetters and chains art loosed,My heart and soul art now free,Tis time I say…to all the world,I AM IN LOVE…WITH THEE!!!!
