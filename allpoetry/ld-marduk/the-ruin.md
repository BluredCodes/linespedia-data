title: "The Ruin"

Here…amongst oblivion,The ruin…of heart and soul,I shed the tears…of anguish,Over loves…I’ll never know—For though I’ve searched,Both far…and wide,It seems my fate…to be denied,That…for which I’ve longed—Thus, I cry…such bitter tears,Languishing…for untold years,Never to know…of love at all,It is my heart…and yea…my soul,Lie amongst the ruins,Of the loves, I’ll never know.
