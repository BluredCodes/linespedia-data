Now…are merely ashes,a love, held deep within,that once, I had thought, eternalbut now, is never more,for we have gone, our separate ways,and I, am now, alone—For what had once,been dear to me,became, quite literally,merely ashes, and no more,for thou didst go,leaving me…Alone.
