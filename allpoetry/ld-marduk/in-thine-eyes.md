title: "In Thine Eyes"

Thou see me nought…as what I be,In thine eyes…tis differently,And I know nought…nae…at all,Just what I mean…to thee—I call thee love, For such thou art,For such…is how I see thee,But how it is,Thy see thyself,Only thou…can tell me—Yet in thine eyes…when tis I look,They seem…but dark, and empty,I get noe warmth…of presence there,When thine eyes art cast…at me.
