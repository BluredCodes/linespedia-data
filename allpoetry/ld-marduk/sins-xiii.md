Thou art...but a vision,that fires...heart and soul,thy beauty...is of legend,And I desire thee so—To hold thee...in my arms,in a passionate embrace,to woo thee...and seduce thee,With a longing...that I say—Is a craving...and a hunger,and thus...a deadly sin,for it leaves me...ever wanting,And yearning...once again—To hold thee...in my arms,in a passionate embrace,to woo thee...and seduce thee,To but make of thee...this day—The vessel...of my passion,for I long...so desperately,to take thee...in my arms,And but make love to thee—For thou art...but a vision,that fires...heart and soul,and thy beauty...is of legend,and I desire thee so—With a yearning...so compelling,that I...am but its slave,and, alas...I am condemned,to forever feel this way.
