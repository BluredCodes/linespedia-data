title: "Lines"

Lines I’ve written…feelings all,Of love…and pain…which I recall,With such detail…that to this day,I feel the pain…of thy going away—And many lines…of prose and verse,Express first hand…how such had hurt,Because it was, I loved thee dearly,Yet thou said thou could not…of me—And so I sought…upon reflection,To pen the lines of my conviction,That I shan’t get over thee—Yes lines I’ve written…feelings all,Of the pain of love…gone unfulfilled,And of my conviction,There for all to see,That I shall not e’er…get over thee.
