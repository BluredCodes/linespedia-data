In a wilderness of emotion,where love is oft belayed,where one kind heart…cannot be found,and souls but lose their way—In this place…art feelings lost,or perhaps…but gone astray,to merely then be banished,from the hearts of mortal men,lost within the wilderness,to n’er be seen again—So what is to be made of this,in time…will we understand,or will the wilderness claim all,so that…but in the end—Not one kind heart…is ever found,and all men’s souls art lost,damned for all eternity,Because he knew not love.
