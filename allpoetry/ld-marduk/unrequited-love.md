---
title: "Unrequited Love"
writer: "Ld Marduk"
slug: "unrequited-love"
source: "AllPoetry"

I ache from love…that be denied me,And of the tears…cried silently,My heart lain open…for all to see,And yet…thou seem…unwilling—To accept…my love for thee,As true…and genuine,Thus unrequited…it remains,No matter…all I’ve done—And I ache from love…so long denied me,My heart and soul…confused…in pain,Would rather die…than remain this way—For my heart lay open…for all to see,The love within…e’er meant for thee,Now seems destined…to never be.
