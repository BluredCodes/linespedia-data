title: "Need"
writer: "Ld Marduk"

In thine absence…I hunger,In thy presence…I burn,Needing thee always,Is a constant concern—For my heart and my soul,They yearn for thee,Wanting…oh, so desperately—To voice their need and longing,Their desire…to hold thee near,For thou art an obsession,One…that is…quite dear—For in thine absence…I hunger,In thy presence…I burn,With a longing…a desire,To but hold thee…in my arms,For thou art precious unto me,Tis for thy love…I yearn—For thou art…an obsession,One…that is…quite dear,One that doth possess me,Every minute, every hour,Of the year.
