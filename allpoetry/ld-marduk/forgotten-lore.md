title: "Forgotten Lore"

Love (it seems)…it be no more,Save within…forgotten lore,Tomes of verse…doth speak of it,But only as…past tense—Can it be…am I that dense,Or be it mere coincidence, That I feel…as I do,More an exception…than the rule?For ‘twas my passion…my desire,E’er discretely to inquire,But on this subject…none would speak,And for my efforts…thought a heretic,Because I dared to speak my mind,And to share feelings…by others denied.
