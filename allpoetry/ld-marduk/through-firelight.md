As the sun doth ebb…And the night draw nigh,I watcheth thee…Through firelight--And as the moon…Crests overhead…I speak of things…Left long unsaid--I sit beside thee…Hold thee tight…And in soft words, confess--That I love thee…Heart and soul…Desire n'er to let thee go--To cherish thee…Throughout our lives…Would such…Dear heart…But thee surprise?Now the day…Tis 'bout to dawn…The firelight…Now nearly gone--And through it all…I've held thee tight...As we slept…Beneath starlight--Confidant…What had been said…Will keep thee…By my side.
