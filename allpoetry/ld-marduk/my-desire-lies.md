title: "My Desire Lies"

I tell thee now…my desire lies,Hauntingly…behind thine eyes,For long has it been…I’ve wished to gaze,And to lose myself…as I while a way—Moments spent…admiring thee,For in my eyes…thou art heavenly,And yea have I longed,To hold thee…in my arms,And to whisper sweet nothings…to thee—Yet...I wish…that such should be,Something agreed to…mutually,For though ‘tis I love thee,My desire lies…hopelessly…behind thine eyes.
