---
title: "Shards"
writer: "Ld Marduk"

Broken hearts…are hard to mend,
For oft…in pieces…much depends,
Upon the skill and care afforded,
To insure…that each shard’s molded—
Crafted once more…as a whole,
To be much stronger…than before,
So it can…again…but hold,
Such feelings and emotions—
As were the cause of its condition,
So that it needed such attention,
Which very few…will give—
For such skill and care afforded,
To a heart that has been broken,
By a love…not meant to be,
By another…willingly,
Should not be…discarded—
For much stronger…than before,
The heart can venture…but once more,
To seek that love…its long desired,
Yet never had…before.
