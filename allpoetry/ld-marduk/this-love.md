---
title: "This Love"
writer: "Ld Marduk"

This love must be hidden,Kept shrouded and cloaked,For such to be known,I cannot e’er hope—Tis tempting…forbidden,And yet sweet…as is wine,Yet n’er can it happen,Must e’er be…confined—For this love is a hunger,A longing…a fire,Which torments my heart,With its endless desire—Yet such must be hidden,Kept shrouded and cloaked,For such to be known,Is too much to e’er hope—Tis tempting…forbidden,And yet sweet…as is wine,Yet n’er can it happen,Must e’er be…denied.
