title: "Loneliness"
writer: "Ld Marduk"

Loneliness…I know it well,So very well indeed,For it has been so much, An integral part of me—No love… Hath ever touched this heart,Now cold…And quite unfeeling,Cursed with passions and desires,That I was e’er concealing—Because I could not share them,Only loneliness…survives,And all because…I could not share,The love I felt…inside.
