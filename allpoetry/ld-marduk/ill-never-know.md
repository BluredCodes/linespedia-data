---
title: "I'll Never Know"
writer: "Ld Marduk"
slug: "ill-never-know"

I'll never know…Not e'er again…The warmth that is thy touch,And now have only memories,Of that kiss…that meant so much.Alas…a life…together,For us…was not to be,And yet I often wonder,Was it fate…that sealed me?Into a love…I could not share,Nor cast aside…completely,So lost in thee…had I become,That to this day…I am found weeping--Tears of sorrow…and remorse,For a love…long I've been keeping,Unrequited…though it be,In my heart I keep believing--That there be hope…for thee and me,Though there be no proof of such,The only proof…tis I require,Be that I love thee…yea this much--Even though…I may n'er know,Now…or e'er again,How it feels to hold thee,As oft I did…back then--Nor the feeling…of thy touch,With thine hand in mine,Still have I the memories,I shall treasure…for all time.
