title: "Lost"
writer: "Ld Marduk"

From the moment my eyes beheld thee,I knew that I was lost,That my heart had been enslaved,And all the world forgot—For in thy presence…Time matters not,For I am lost…in thee,Yet…the feeling’s…not mutual,And for eternity—From the moment my eyes beheld thee,I knew that I was lost,That my heart had been enslaved,Yet the feeling…was not mutual,And thou sent me away—For in thy presence…Time mattered not,And I was lost…in thee,But thou felt not…as I did,And could not e’er believe—That from the moment… My eyes beheld thee,I knew that I was lost,That my heart had been enslaved,And the world…for thee…forgot—Yet in thy presence…Time mattered not,And I was lost in thee,Alas…the feeling…’twas not mutual,For thou felt such…n’er for me.
