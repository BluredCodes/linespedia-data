When I look into thine eyes,I’m painfully aware,That though I love thee…heart and soul,For me…there is nothing there—No warmth, nor tenderness,For thou know not…that I care,Or if thou do…thou tell me not,Leaving me…to fear—Of dying lass…from loneliness,Because I cannot share,Such feelings as I have for thee,That long have but been there,For looking deep…into thine eyes,Thou know not…that I care.
