title: "Perdition"

An isle of despair,where hearts and souls are damned,to spend eternity,seeking but…amends—And yet, alas, ‘tis hopeless,I know this personally,for here have I been sentenced,Because of love for thee.For it was but a tragedy,a curse upon my soul,to love one who could not return,such feelings to anyone—Thus, I agonize,over feelings once possessed,but lost when thou condemned me,to endless loneliness—Thus the isle of despair,is now where I reside,for my heart and soul are damned,and so, alas, am I—To spend eternity,alone…and but unloved,for such, here, is forbidden,And not to be thought of.
