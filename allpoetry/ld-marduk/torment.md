My torment…yet…be greater still,For tis that I…can still but feel,The warmth...that was her touch,So soft...and yea quite gentle--And though the days have long now passed,It doth seem...as yesterday,For she hath both my heart and soul,Enslaved...eternally--Thus, am I aghast,To know that...at long last,She confided love for me,For though I spoke oft...endlessly--Of such feelings...felt in kind,Long didst she but wander blind,Seeing...without seeing,The love for her,That was mine.
