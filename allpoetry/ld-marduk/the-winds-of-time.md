title: "THE WINDS OF TIME"
writer: "Ld Marduk"

Adrift upon the winds of time,my heart and soul await,the coming of a love renewed,to sorely test…my faith—For there are doubts,and there are fears,that I dinnae understand,for they art great…in number,An’ I…but a mortal man.Whose knowledge is but finite,no god but would I be,an’ yet I pray, this love renewed,is thou, comin’ back…to me—For great were the feelings,In my heart….e’er a part of me…that long for thy return,Though it test my faith indeed.For thou were precious…unto me,in ways thou n’er did know,thus I couldna understand,why thou e’er did go—To leave me e’er in wonder,an’ full of doubt…indeed,as to whether why it was,Had been because of me.
