---
title: "Lost For Words"
writer: "Ld Marduk"
slug: "lost-for-words"

Tis not often, such I be,But so…I often find myself,When it comes to thee and me –For though I love thee dearly,Wish only such…in kind,There is a gulf between us,That is widening…all the time –Once it was…I thought it bridged,That we’d reached…an understanding,But it collapsed…because of words,So we’re back but where we started –So all the years…have been for nought,And it seems…that now thou hate me,And lost for words it seems I am,That could express to thee…I’m sorry –For though I love thee dearly,Wish only such…in kind,There is a gulf between us,I doubt we can bridge…this time.
