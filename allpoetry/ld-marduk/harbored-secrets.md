title: "Harbored Secrets"
writer: "Ld Marduk"
slug: "harbored-secrets"

I cannot…though I long to,confess my heart to thee,for such feelings that I harbor,art best kept secretly—For in thy presence…tis not wise,to lower my veneer,for thou might not even recognize,the soul that thou see there—Thus I keep…unto myself,what my heart but feels for thee,for such feelings that I harbor,art best kept secretly—For in thy presence…tis not wise,to show such…openly,thus…I keep it to myself,though quite reluctantly—For I cannot…though I long to,confess my heart to thee,thus…must be content,to love thee…from afar,Perhaps…eternally.
