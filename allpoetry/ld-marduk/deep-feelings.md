Tormented by silence…My heart knows no rest,Bereft of all hope…of love to possess,It cryeth in pain…even more so…in vain,Over loves that…have long since…been lost—For my heart…hath deep feeling,A concern…over such,It struggles…and strives,To comprehend every loss,In hopes…can be found…some middle ground,So the lessons of each…won’t be lost—For this torment…be maddening,And my heart…deeply saddened,When the loss of love…it hath caused.
