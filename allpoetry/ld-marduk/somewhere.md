title: "Somewhere"

Somewhere…in my lonely heart,Perhaps…as a memory,Lies the remnants of a love,I had longed to share…with thee—Yet it seems that fate…is often cruel,In its deals with mere mortals who,Desire only…to be loved,But…for whom…love is doomed—Forever to be nothing more,Than dream or fantasy,For somewhere…in my lonely heart,Lies the remnants of a love,Which fate dist cruelly steal away,Never to let be,A love I’d longed to share…with thee.
