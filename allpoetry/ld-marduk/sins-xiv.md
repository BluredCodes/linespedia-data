Can you feel the fires burning,deep, within my soul,and the passions, and desires,So compelling...that I know—That to hold thee…in my arms,in a sensual embrace...to woo thee, and seduce thee,Is…the only way—To bring about seduction,and the lust within my heart,to catch thee...in a web,of a wanton appetite—To possess thee...heart and soul,As night does...of the earth—For my passion...would move mountains,and for thee...it would be worth--The selling...of my soul,for to make love unto thee,is a desire...I have harbored,Yet, I know...‘tis destiny—That the fires in my heart,should burn eternally,with the hunger...and desire,I sought to find...in thee.
