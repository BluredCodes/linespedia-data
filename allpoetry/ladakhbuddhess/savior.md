Like a divine gift from the heavens,
Oh! angelic creature,
save me from destruction.
Your beauty flourishes before
my naivete.
Salvage my sanity!
Desperate as the dead, gnarled knobs
of winter's limbs
I beckon to you
oh, utopian splendor!
