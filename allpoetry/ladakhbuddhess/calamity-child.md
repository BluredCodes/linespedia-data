title: "Calamity Child"
writer: "LadakhBuddhess"

The rise and demise
of delirium,
will prove us to be strong.
A coked up country boy
will prove them to be wrong.
He's falling in line for you,
but what difference does it make?
You're looking at the moon.

A calamity child
expulled from paradise.
Would you have rolled the dice?

Foes and fiends...
Death after new beginnings...
He's already broken,
go ahead, take a slice!
Of the boy with a thorn in his side.

...'cause what difference does it make?
