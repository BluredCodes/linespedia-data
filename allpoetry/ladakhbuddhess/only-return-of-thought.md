title: "Only Return of Thought"

Winter'd hands sparked her sunset,
seen through aged eyes.
"What world's? What shores of what worlds?"
Winter'd hands, the signature of yore
groom gnarled notions of
yester-year's youth.
Won't we ever reach dawn?
