She devours moonbeams.
Luna lies with ashen angels...
Condensed thought drips
from the tongues of beauties,
thick as golden honey.
"They have minds like gems," she says.

On wings that fly
through demonic dreams,
they leave... 
crowned with laurel in oblivion.
And she weeps, tears of silver
for loves long gone.

But her song is not over,
it has only just begun.
