The luscious grandeur of 
melancholy horror
trickles through my brain.
Our cage has been locked,
and they've thrown away the key.
Our fate is set.
Slow death through brain shrinkage.
A drought of pure knowledge.
Corrupt fingers poke at our past loves, until
the written word has no meaning.
Hitleresque tasks.
We must follow the pied piper 
to our utopian facade of a destiny.
