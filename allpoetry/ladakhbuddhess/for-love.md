title: "For love"

I want to taste your soft
sweetness.
Catch the golden honey that
flows from your lips.
Stare into your glacial pools,
lit by enchanted moonlight
which peers from under 
the ashes.
Once gray,
now alive.
Your temple moves me, for I
have never before seen such beauty.
