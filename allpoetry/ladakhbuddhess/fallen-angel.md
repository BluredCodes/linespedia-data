---
title: "Fallen Angel"
writer: "LadakhBuddhess"

Reach for the heavens, she said.
There is no place so lovely.
Love is not so beautiful,
flesh turns to dust.
One more medicated blissful moment...
I want nothing greater
than heaven! she said.
Just give me one last peace...
Her dust blows in the wind.
