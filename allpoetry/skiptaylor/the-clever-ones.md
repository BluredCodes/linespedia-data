title: "The clever ones"

Many faces amongst us
None of which we can trust
It's troubling to know
That we could never show
Who we are

The most clever trick
Is to make others believe in you
When in the end you were never true
Leaving those that trusted in you
To thrive in revenge

Hate and envy is common 
But it's not from strangers we don't know
It's from those that love us
Or better yet
The clever ones
