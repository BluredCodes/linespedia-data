title: "the devil behind angel eyes"

I remember the first time I saw you
So mild and timid, so innocent
You gave me faith in people
For so long I had none

Our friendship grew into something unexpected
You made me fall in love with you
I gave you my world, heart and soul
You gave me feelings that I've never known

Years passed by and I believed we'd be together forever
But you didn't see it that way
You were tempted by the fruits of another
And left me in anguish and feeling spiteful

I let you back into my heart
Only to have you stick a dagger into it again
You have left scars
Wise people have said that only time will heal wounds

I don't believe that is true
Because from now until i leave this world
You will forever be on my mind
But now I've learned there is the devil behind those angel eyes

If it happens once, shame on you
If it happens twice, shame on me
