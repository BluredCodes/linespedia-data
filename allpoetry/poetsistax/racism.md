i hear people calling racist names
i see people playing racist games
when is racism going to end
can't we all just be friends after all its just the colour of the skin
so come on people racism belongs in the bin
i am black you are white
so lets get together to unite
to hear it once
to hear it all
to rise together through pride and fall.
