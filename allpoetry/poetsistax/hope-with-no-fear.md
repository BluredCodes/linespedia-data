Hope with no fear

What do you see
When you see me
A person who hears voices
A person who hears strange noises
Tell me what do you see
When you see me
A person who relies on medication
A person without any dedication
I’ll tell you what you what you see
When you see me
A person who has hope
A person learning how to cope
With life when it comes around
With things that often get me down
So what ever you see
Don’t think I’m weird, neither strange
I’m a person with hope with every new day.
