I am the eagle, the bird of prey
always upwards, looking down
on the specks of humanity below me

Detached, determined, destined to never
run fingers of flesh and feeling over smooth smooth grass
lord of the empty sky

I shall remain here, where I can see everything,
know everything; think everything 
If I am ever to sink from my lofty kingdom to the realm of man, it is only to kill, and swiftly I return.
