The following are some Haiku I've written under the pressures of extreme ennui. They're not good, but they passed away some of my time, perhaps they'll do the same for you.

Concerning 6th Period:

Eternal stasis
confusion subdues my soul
why must I take trig?

On 7th Period:

Class is very dull
I absorb nearby beauty
this occupies time

Of matters in 8th period:

The hour I dread most
my wits are ground to nothing
the abyss presides

Relating an account of a rather special girl:

Olive skinned seraph
her laughter falls like light rain
on my soul's soft shell

In regard to a close friend:

Steadfast survivor
smiling eyes hide quiet strength
proud humility

Composed for a lost acquaintance:

A little cynic
laced with simple good nature
I still miss her smile

Inspired by a great fellow:

Maelstrom of knowledge
ecstatic vastness of mind
kept in modest frame

Written because of a missed opportunity:

Fire encased in lead
fear and hesitation swell
the flames slowly die


Demonstration of how easy it is to write bad poetry:

I sit here at night
in front of a glowing box
and tap many thoughts
