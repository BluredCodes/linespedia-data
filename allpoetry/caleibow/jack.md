Jackrabbit
Hoping along the side of the road
Jack rabbit jumps
Stopped in  mid jump

Flat Jackrabbit on
Jack rabbit road

Two jack rabbit ears are 
Left up,
not ground into
the asphalt.

Like TV antennas on top of an old Set 

Desert heat rises off the road, in waves

Rabbit ears
sway in the desert wind. 

not another car for days.
