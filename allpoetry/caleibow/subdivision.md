---
title: "Subdivision"
writer: "caleibow"
slug: "subdivision"

I was standing
12 years old 
dust covering me
the trucks bounced like burros
across the ruts
truck after truck

old men 
with bandannas around their
necks 
would wave
truck after truck after
truck loaded with
earth

I used to play
now I stand
and watch 

We would play for hours
scamper up screes
Climb trees to get closer  to
hawks flying in circles

at times
when we were tired of talking
we would lie in the grass and look up into
the blue sky that vibrated like hummingbirds wings -
a times when we were still we could hear 
each others breathing
the hawk breathing,
breath, blood and root. 

The trucks came in waves
and I stood against them
drowning
watching
not breathing
unable to catch my breath
as they took me away
one truck at a time
one hawk at a time.
