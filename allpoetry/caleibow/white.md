title: "White"

Snow outside
the white filters into the room,

Listening to love songs in Spanish
I dream of Chilean snows
and hot tea.
Looking out over stampeding seas
The wind knocking at the door,
but you won’t let her in.

I take you, like the waves 
take the sea.
And
in the white foam of sheets,
I’m stranded on the shores of
your belly

I drown in
the white light.
