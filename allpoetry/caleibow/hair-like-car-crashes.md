She sits next to mehair like car crashes -her eyes are like park benchesworn and green -I lean back like a broken chairI touch her shoulderShe turns and kisses metakes my arms and ties themaround hershe wears me likea dead mink She takes me homeher house is a nest of crowsshe throws me on her unmade bed of strawdevours me -then discarded by the unkindness of Ravens
