My grandfather sold shoesSold spats, laces, and wing-tipsHe always wore wing-tipsTook meticulous careHad all the accessoriesFor his shoesHadn’t sold shoes for 40 yearsbut still carried a shoe hornMy grandfather died in 1993We put him in a wallFour up and two overLike a pair of shoes in a storeroom 'A size 9 please'Down the hall    up the ladderFour up    and                two overWe roll over the wheel'd metal ladder from against the wallFour up    and                 two over'my grandfather please.'This too was just written.  Does the ending work?
