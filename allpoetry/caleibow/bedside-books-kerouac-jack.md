Beside books,Kerouac Jack.Poetics and spontaneous prose-In the moment before knowingWhen you touched me,Unconscious, unknowing, bewitching me in a moment,When you said…I can’t remember what it was you said.There’s something about youThoughtsWordsBodyUndefineable, undeniable, unknowable –Unhaveable?It’s like a dream tumbled tossed twisted and interwoven,Or the realityOf the moment, a head of the movement,Sensual, raw,MonumentalMounting movement-Rocking racing raving ranting words,Wanting now to be now Wondering wonder of fearing old fearsBludgeooning hope, Budding beatitudesExpecting nothing wanting nothing, needing nothingExpecting everything, wanting everything, needing everything.Needing the needing.
