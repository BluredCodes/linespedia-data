Gravity brought my great grandfather to this country
It was my great grandmothers will
Who wrestled the moon, 
Changing tides
Disturbing orbits.

She brought him here, 
but he stayed in Germany.
He became a suit in a chair.
He’d smoke cigars and exhale great
clouds of  regret

She brought him here, but he
Never dreamt America
Never put his shoulder to the wheel,
Never learned English,
Never spoke again -
