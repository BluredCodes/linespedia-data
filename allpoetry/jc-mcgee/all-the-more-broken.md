---
title: "All The More Broken"
writer: "jc mcgee"
slug: "all-the-more-broken"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

icy fingers reach
the landscape falls away
trapped within the shards
a colder hell today

bury brittle bones
beneath a sea of frost
suffering alone
where warmth and hope are lost

frigid and pale
the body is frail
and the spirit all the more broken
bleed to be free
this dying in me
goes beyond all the words i have spoken

swept into the sleep
that spans a thousand years
hollow wounds so deep
shedding crystal tears

a whisper from the veil
so softly tempts my soul
my cold, erected shell
begins to take its toll

frigid and pale
the body is frail
and the spirit all the more broken
bleed to be free
this dying in me
goes beyond all the words i have spoken

can you see my face
underneath the glaze?
fingers claw in place
against the icy haze
i was drifting
through debris
wanting something true
loneliness
is killing me
where the fuck are you?
