My wild mountain of beauty
what secrets do you hold
Your home to natures creatures
Hidden in your folds.

The song bird trilling sweetly
Or Fox with shining eyes
Old Wombat trudging onwards
Kangaroo hidden by your disguise.

Guardian of Gods creations
Protecting them from man
From those who shoot for glory
Bringing distuction to this land.

My wild mountain of beauty
This promose I make to you
Here, no harm will come to those you shield
For my love of them like yours is true.
