Gremlins in the computer
Alas have struck again
Snuck in Oh so quitly
Driving me insane.
All I did was download
A program known by all
Innocent it wasn't
Soon we headed for a fall.
Ate up all my files
A hungry gremlin was he
Next onto my mail
Nothing left to see.
Address folder empty
Friends all disappered
All floating round in space
In the cyber hemisphere.
Three days of a blank screen
No poetry, messages or chat
No more downloads for me
I'm very sure of that.
