title: "To Forever Share"

This love we share
Never meant to be
Hearts didn’t acknowledge
Your love was not free.

Magnetic forces 
From your soul to mine
Though we fought these feelings
Our lives now combined.

As love grows stronger
With the passing of time
Passions rise to heights
Never expected to find.

Your love is a Treasure
Priceless beyond compare
You’re my heart my soul
I pray with you life to share.
