Chains of love
Unbroken by time
Two hearts forever linked
Your heart to mine.

Though circumstance may try
To bridge a gap between
These links won't be broken
Of our love kept unseen.

Longing and wanting
Each and every day
Our love never wanes
As the same prayer we pray.

We pray for the day
Our hearts can be free
To rejoice in our love
For the world to see.
