title: "Love  Eternal"

Love eternal
Hearts on fire
Invisible chains
Forever as one.

Memories of passion
A touch so tender
Love filled nights
Under starlit skies.

Bask in the wonder
Of a love so true
Souls uniting
As bodies yield.

Forever your love
Always my love
Eternal love
This love.
