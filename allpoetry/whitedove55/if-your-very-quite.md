If your very quite
And carefully sneak outside
You could be very lucky
But remember you must hide, 
Look down in the garden
What a sight to see
A hundred little Fairies
Dancing beneath a tree,
Holding hands and twirling
Twisting every way
Tiny wings a flutter
You don't believe you say?
Well let me tell you now
That Fairies do exist
In the bottom of my garden
Playing in the mist.

For you Jess
