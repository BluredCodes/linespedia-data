---
title: "My  Love"
writer: "whitedove55"

Your eyes show passion
No words need be spoken
Your touch so gentle
Conveys loving emotion.

The thrill of a kiss
Warmth of embrace
Passion ignites
As your fingers trace.

Two souls as one
Hearts intertwined
Love as never before
Again impossible to find.

Though fate may intervene
As it did once before
All we know is this moment
And of this love we are sure.
