title: "Walk  With  Me"

Lord grant me the courage
To stand on my own
When all from my world I love has gone,
Lord grant me the strength
To hold my head high
Put a smile on my face
When my eyes want to cry,
When my minds in turmoil
Despair my friend
Show me the wisdom 
To know it will end,
Walk with me Lord
Show me the way
Help me get through 
Till the next sunny day,
I know I'll survive
If you lead me along
And stand by my side
Till all's where it belongs.
