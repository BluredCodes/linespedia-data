Mist in the morning
Silent, eerie
Hiding secrets
Behind a blanket of white,

Slowly rising
Releasing it's captive
Back to the heavens
From whence it came,

Earth shining shimmering
Awakens the beauty
A new day beginning
A joy to behold

Incredible colour
Sights to astound
Ever changing, amazing
This great land of ours.
