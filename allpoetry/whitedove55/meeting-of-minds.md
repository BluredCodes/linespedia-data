Meeting of minds
Across a crowded room
Passions rise
As eyes lock in love,
Words not spoken
Fear of exposure
Only memories now
Of happier times,
Gone now dreams of starry nights
Then, not a sound
Nor a stirring
Only beating of hearts,
We wait we watch
Always from a distance
Still the meeting of minds
Lingers on.
