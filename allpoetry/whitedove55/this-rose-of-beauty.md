title: "[This Rose of beauty]"

This Rose of beauty
Given with love
To remember days now past
Petals velvet soft
Like your gentle kiss
Or the caressing touch of your hand.
As I tend this beauty
I feel your love surround me
Knowing I'm in your heart
This Rose grows and blooms
As does my love for you
For our love is eternal.
