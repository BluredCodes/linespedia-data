Just one more drink is what he said
Just one more drink with Jim or Fred,
Dinner on the table cold as ice
All in bed it will not be nice
Just one more drink,

Just one more drink is what she said
Just one more drink to clear my head,
Dishes in the sink no food on the table
Wait awhile kids untill I'm able,
Just one more drink,

Just one more drink the young boy said
Just one more drink behind the shed,
Head spinning, legs are shaking
How many days will this boy be waking?
Just one more drink.

Just one more drink between two mates
Just one more drink could be to late
That last drink should never have been
Now only memories of what could have been.
Just one more drink.
