Hey little Blondie
You mean the world to me
Your little heart so full of love
I wish the world could see.
Your happy laughing eyes
That smile upon your face
The way your always in a hurry
As if every days a race.
Some say you'll be in movies
Seen by near and far
But to me my little Blondie
You are my shining star.
You always know the right words
For the times when I am down
You say Nanny how I love you
Your a joy to be around.
Oh yes you have your moments
When so naughty you can be
But Blondie when it's tallied up
Your sweetness wins you see.
Hey little Blondie
Let me tell you how I feel
My hearts so full of love for you
Some times I wonder, are you real?

For Jessica the light of my life.
