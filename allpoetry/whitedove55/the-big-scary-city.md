Of to the big city tomorrow
Oh what a scary thought
Nerves are all a jangle
For in the rat race I'll be cought.

With lots and lots of people
All of them in a rush
I have this awful feeling
I'll be lost forever in the crush.

What really worries me
Silly you may think
Are the traffic light in the city
As they change colour and blink.

For in our tiny village
The same goes for our town
No matter how hard you look 
Not a traffic light to be found.

Three days in the city
How will I survive
I pray someone will take pity
And offer for me to drive.

If I could take a plane I would
But we just don't have them here
So it has to be by road
The nerves are getting worse I fear


Please think of me tomorrow
As this journey begins
That I'll survive this city experience
And soon be back home again
