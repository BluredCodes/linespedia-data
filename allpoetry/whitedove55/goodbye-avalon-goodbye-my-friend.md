Goodbye Avalon
So long my friend
What a race you ran 
Right to the end.

Magnificent grey
You were are sight to see
Then as age crept on
We set you free.

Days of racing over
The paddocks you roamed
Then a call from me 
Would bring you home.

Often I would watch
As alone you would race  
I guess memories of your victories
Your age would replace.

With your gentle ways
And loving heart
I knew someday Avalon 
We would have to part.

Your gone now old friend
Life wont be quite the same
But where ever you are
You’ll race once more to fame.


Dedicated to my Beautiful 
Old ex race horse Avalon 
Who lost the battle today
At the grand old age of 23yrs
