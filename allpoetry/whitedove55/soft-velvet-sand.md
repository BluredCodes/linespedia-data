Velvet soft sand
Beneath a starlit night
Lovers walk hand in hand.
Promises made
Love sealed with a kiss
Under a golden moon.
A dream realized
Two hearts forever joined
Hearts rejoice in loves beauty.
No past no future
Just a moment in time
A moment on soft velvet sand.
