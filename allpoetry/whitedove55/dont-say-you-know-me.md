They say I can handle
All of which comes along
They say I have strength
To always be strong,

They don't see the real me
The one that I hide
They don't see the turmoil
Churning inside,

It's all just an act
Well done I agree
But sometimes I wish
They would just look at me,

Please don't say you know me
You can't really you see
How can you know me
When even I don't know me.
