Gentle little lady
Did I here you whisper again?
Is it just imagination
When my hearts so filled with pain.

I think I heard your voice
As I believe I have before
Or was it just the breeze
Mum, I really can't be sure.

I heard the words be strong
Wipe those tears away
Words you used before you left
To join the Lord that day.

You said before you went
You would be watching over me
Mum I need you here now
My heartache can you see?

I must believe I heard you
That your watching from above
Whispering words of comfort
Wrapping me in your love.
