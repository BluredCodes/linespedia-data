Blow gentle breeze
Blow my worries away
Bring peace to my vally 
Bring happier days.

Ease my troubled mind
With your whispered sigh
Banish pain and heartache
As you quietly pass by.

For so long I've hidden
Sad memories inside
Gentle breeze carry them away
Help free this mind.

Gentle breeze my friend
How you soothe my soul
I know you'll be with me
Untill again this life's whole.
