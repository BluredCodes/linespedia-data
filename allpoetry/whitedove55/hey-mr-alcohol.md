Hey Mr Alcohol Why did you pick this man?
you could see he had a family you didn't figure in their plans.
You eased your way in silently pretending to be a friend
You quietly destroyed all that was good as you did of many men.
A hell you turned their world to made all afraid to speak
Although they loved him dearly around him they would sneak.
You laughed as you watched this man abuse those once held dear
Humiliation Rage and Cruelty into their hearts you instilled such fear.
Mr Alcohol you soon got your way his heart and soul possessed
You filled his head with demons this man once was the very best.
Mr Alcohol he's gone now you finally got your way
A good man before your friendship
God knew, and now with God he stays
