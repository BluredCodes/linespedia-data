title: "Leave This  Angel"

Please don't use this child 
As the object of your hate
She didn't ask to be here
Don't use her as the bait.

I watch you fill her head
With your anger and your spite
Turning one against the other
Bringing unrest to her nights.

I sit and rock her gently
With soothing words and love
This tiny little girl
Such a blessing from above.

If harsh words are all you know
Go ahead fight then if you must
Just don't envolve this Angel
Without you who can she trust.
