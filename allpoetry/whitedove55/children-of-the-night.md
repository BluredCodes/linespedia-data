title: "Children  Of  The  Night."

Children of the night
Never seen by day
Floating through dreams
Bringing memories.
Memories of times
Happier times
When all was well
As was meant to be.
Children of the night
Arms outstretched
Embracing, loving
As days gone by.
Awaiting darkness
Welcoming dreams  
Awaiting the return  
Of Children of the night.
