I wait 
The phone rings
Praying I answer.
Tears fall
Heart breaks
Never their voices.
I watch
Scan every face
Never those loved.
How much can a heart take
Before it shatters
How long can I go on
With this pain inside?
Will there ever be a time
Life is whole again
Is it ever meant to be
They return home?
