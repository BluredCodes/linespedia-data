title: "Don't  Cry  For  Me"

Don't cry for me
If you see a tear
I'm glad you see
Just to have you near.
Though dreams maybe shattered
By those loved now gone
With you here beside me
I know I'll be strong.
Don't be sad for me
While I reminisce
Of happier days
Oh how those days I miss.
So if you see a tear
Quitely slip by
Don't worry my dears
Soon again I will smile.

I lOve You Both.
