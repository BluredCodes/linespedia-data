There's a Wombat in my garden as big as big can be
He showed him self tonight scared the life right out of me.
Now I knew I had some Wombats that's expected round these parts
But one as big as this he gave me quite a start.
Lucy my little dog in a barking frenzy was she
So fearless me torch in hand goes to have a look see.
Much to my dismay Wombat and I are face to face
Both frozen on the spot then I bolted with great haste.
Three foot long Two foot wide a foot and a half he stood
I watched from behind the screen sure I'd be his dinner if he could.
Now I know he's there I'll keep looking all the while
For to be a Wombats dinner let me say is not my style.
