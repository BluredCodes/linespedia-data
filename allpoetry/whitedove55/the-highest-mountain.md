I climbed the highest mountain
When I thought I heard your name
The only thing I found
Was a reflection of my pain.
I swam a raging river
When I thought I heard your voice 
I battled through the current
Then had to make a choice.
Do I continue on my journey?
Until I find you Son
Or turn back in despair
Accepting defeat has won.
Only a mother knows the answer
The journey carries on
Until you come home to me
Then the journey’s done

For .S.
