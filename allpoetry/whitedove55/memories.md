Memories of nights
Beneath starlit skies
Vows exchanged
As souls unite.

Memories of passion
Bodies on fire
The thrill of a kiss
The rapture of a touch.

Memories of a love 
Never known before
Love now parted
Still lives on.

Memories only memories
Still mine to hold
Till memories reality
I will love you.
