A faded image 
Photo tattered and worn
Brings memories of love
From days long gone
Velvet touch as hands explore
Passions rise, explode
As souls become one
Bodies entwined
Ecstasy unequalled
In a bond of love
A bond carried through time
An image now faded
Though love lingers on
Waiting to unite.
Then again one.
