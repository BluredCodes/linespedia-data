title: "Fantacy  Of  The  Night"

Is this a fantasy of the night, two faces smiling
Eyes showing love asking to enter
My arms outstretched to embrace those once lost
Memories of long age invade my mind
Hearts rejoicing as once again reunited
Hearing the words for so long missed
Is this real has the dream come true?
Are you standing there or do I awake to reality
A new day dawns reality once again
As I go on waiting for this fantasy of the night.
.
