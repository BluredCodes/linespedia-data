title: "Yesterday's Tears"

Tears of yesterday
Linger near the surface
Forever watchful 
Waiting for the moment,
A fleeting memory
A face in the crowd
A haunting tune
Or an innocent word,
Unexpected, unwanted
Tears of yesterday surface
Released from imprisonment
Returning the pain,
Forever watchful
Always waiting
Waiting for the moment
Heartache begins.
