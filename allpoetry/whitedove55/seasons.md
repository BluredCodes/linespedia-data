Leaves of Autumn
Silently flutter
Blanketing the land
In a carpet of gold.

Warm blue days
Drift to cooling nights
Mountains shrouded in mist
As the new day dawns.

Each season unique
A magic of It's own
A beauty to behold
This gift from God.

As Autumn slips in
Making way for winter
I Rejoice in the wonders
Of the seasons.
