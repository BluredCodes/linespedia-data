How did we let it slip away
That love so young and new
What happened to the dreams
Dreams shared between we two?
How could we know this love 
Would one day end in sorrow
Left with only broken hearts
Gone, hopes of more tomorrows?
Had we know the future
Would we have shied away in fear
Afraid to take a chance
Never to show how much we cared?
Although there's no tomorrows
And no future here with you
I cherish memories of the love we shared
When our love was young and new.

For~W~  With the Lord.
