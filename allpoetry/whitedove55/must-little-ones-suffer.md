Must little ones suffer
Harsh words spoken
Words spoken in anger
By those who should cherish.

How do they comprehend
Hate shown by those loved
Bewildered confused 
To young to understand.

Stop these harsh words
No more raised voices
Though you may love no more
Remember their love for you.
