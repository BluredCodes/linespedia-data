River of tears
Mountain of sorrow
Do you see my pain?
Which path should I follow?

Do I follow the path?
Where I know he awaits
Back to his arms
Back to heartbreak.

Or do I be strong
Turn and walk away
From my only true love
For the answer I pray.

River carry me on
Show me the way
Mountain lend me your strength
Help me this day.
