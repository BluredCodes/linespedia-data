Send me a Rainbow
Of colors bright
To banish the darkness
And ghosts of the night.

Send me a song bird
With a voice so sweet
To calm any Demons
Should I happen to meet.

Send me a stream
Running freely on it's way
To wash away the sadness
In our hearts this day.

Then send me the answers
To why It should be
So many hearts were broken
Again to never be free,
