Everyday I walk on thin ice
Everything I say makes you uptight
Nothing I do ever seems right
I'm tired of crying tears at night

And as you make me cry...
My tears will not be in vain
I've earned my purple heart
I will feel no pain

Every opinion I have is wrong
You criticize my favorite song
It's been like this all along
I've put up with it for far too long

And as you make me mad...
My tears will not be in vain
I've earned my purple heart
I will feel no pain

I see your anger everyday
I ache inside from things you say
I forgive the things of yesterday
Just to get more thrown my way

And as you hurt my heart...
My tears will not be in vain
I've earned my purple heart
I will feel no pain

I'm finally done, I'm giving up
I've had my last, I'm shutting up
And when I'm gone you'll call me up
Don't be surprised when I hang up

And as you made me cry...
My tears were not in vain
I earned my purple heart
I will feel no painI wrote this one when I was really pissed at a certain someone. It's one of those where the emotions just spill out... Writing this was great for me, I felt so much better after I'd gotten it all out. Song-Poem. Written & posted in September, 2001.
