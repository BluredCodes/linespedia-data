Break free, I say
Break free and see the sun
Break free, my love
Break free and run

Break free, my baby
Break free and see the world
Break free forever
Break free, dear girl

Oh, freedom is forever
Freedom is the sun
Freedom ain't no privilege
Just break free and run

Break free, I say
Break free and see the sun
Break free, my love
Break free and run

Break free, my baby
Break free and see the world
Break free forever
Break free, dear girlThis is what I like to call a song-poem. I will probably be writing a lot more like this because I am a very musical person. Anyway, enjoy!
