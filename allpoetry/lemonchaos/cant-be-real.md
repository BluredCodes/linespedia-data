Can't Be Real
(B. Bailey)

It can't be over yet
I am not ready for this moment
Tell me it isn't true
It can't be real

This cannot be the end
Of a decade so perfect
Tell me this is not happening
Tell me please

I am not ready for the end
I am not ready for it to be gone
I am not ready
I guess I never will be
But I know this can't be real

It can't be time
I am not ready for the heartache
The tears burn my cheeks
As they flow down

This cannot be the close
Of a phenomenon so true
Tell me this is not happening
Tell me please

I am not ready for the end
I am not ready for it to be gone
I am not ready
I guess I never will be
But I know this can't be real

All I know
Is that this is my life
All I know
Is that this is my soul
All I know
Is I need this to survive
How will I ever... 
How will I ever go on

I am not ready for the end
I am not ready for it to be gone
I am not ready
I guess I never will be
But I know this can't be real

I am not ready for the end
I am not ready for it to all fall down
I am not ready
I guess I never will be
