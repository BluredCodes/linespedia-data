In this world of sin
No one can win
Freedom of speech
Is kicked to the curb

An opinion is made
An opinion is stomped
An opinion is rejected
An opinion is punished

In this world of sin
You cannot say a word
For if someone disagrees
You get branded for life

What I think is bad
What I think is wrong
What I think is irelevant
What I think is ignored

In this world of sin
Why do we even bother?
They say we have the right
But then they take it away

What is the point of this?
What is the point of life?
What is the point of speaking?
What is the point to try?Freedom of speech no longer exists.
