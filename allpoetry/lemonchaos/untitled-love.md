Love, such a strong wordPain, such great sorrowWhen your love is gone, I feel painHeart, feels the soul’s emotionsHands, feel the skin beneathMy heart is in your handsThis one came to me while listening to the song 'Misery' by The Moffatts. It's an awesome song, you should listen to it sometime. November, 2001.
