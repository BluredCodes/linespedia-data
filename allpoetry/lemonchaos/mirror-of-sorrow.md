title: "Mirror of Sorrow"
writer: "LemonChaos"

I am the girl in the mirror
A perfect reflection of you
I live my life as you would
Just from a different view
The world around me shaded
In your sadness and your tears
A raging storm of emotions
Building over the years

I keep it all locked away
From you, so you may live
A happy life, sans all the pain
This to you I give
But when you seem to get lost
You step right through the glass
The pain and sorrow fills you
Within this world of brass

Everything is tarnished
From crying tears of old
Your flesh begins to crawl
As it is filled with cold
Your body can not stay
Forever in this place
It will slowly kill you
Scar and mark your face

People see it as you pass
A marked woman you stand
I will help you to get back
Just take hold my hand
Take this one step for me
No - take for yourself
Only you can make it back
Place sorrow on the shelf

Once back into your world
Much better things will seem
Within your life the happiness
Will begin to gleam
Never let the sorrow of
My mirror world show through
It is not fair to your own soul
