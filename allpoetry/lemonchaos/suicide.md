Rusty razor cut through my skin
Pain and death come rushing in
Beautiful blood flows from my veins
I've lost control, I've dropped the reins

My life is spiraling steady down
Past and present swirl around
My life is flashing 'fore my eyes
Dark and dismal turn the skies

A single thought runs through my head
It's too late now, I'll soon be dead
No longer can I change my mind
I now must leave this world behind

Rusty razor fall to the floor
I have not strength to hold you more
The light is slipping slow away
I will not live another day

This is it, I must let go
If then I knew what I now know
I would have stayed to try again
I would have stayed and tried to win

Slipping slowly farther down
If not from pain I'll surely drown
The water covers up my face
Finally, alone, I leave this placeThis poem goes along with 'Alive in Death' and 'All in Vain'. All three poems I wrote tonight... I must be sorta down.
