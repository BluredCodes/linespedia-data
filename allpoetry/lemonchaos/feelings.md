I hear you call;
Your spirit, your soul
Calling my name
No need for spoken words
Your heart knows what I need to hear

Your hand on the small of my back
Your eyes which pierce into my being
You read my thoughts before I think them
A link between us
A bond never to be broken

A love too strong to ever be voiced
You'd give your life for mine, I know
I've seen you try
I've seen the way you care
I've seen the feelings you try to hide

You bear it all to me with one look
One tearfilled look
That's all it takes for me to knowThis poem, as alot of mine are, was inspired by my two and a half year obsession The X-Files. It's how I view what Scully feels about Mulder (as in she's speaking the words). Written & posted in October, 2000.
