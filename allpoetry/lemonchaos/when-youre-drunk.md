When you're drunk the world spins 'round
Take a step and fall to the ground
Smack your head and bust your ass
Cut your wrist with a peice of glass
Everything blurs and seems the same
Everyone has gone insane
You just want to up and run
You just want to have some fun

When you're drunk everything is fine
Get more beer and drink more wine
Get in the car and drive to the store
Don't you worry, you can buy more
Have another drink, it goes fast
Who cares if you hit the gas?
Twenty miles over, who the fuck cares?
Just a little faster, go anywhere

One more drink and then you're done
One more drink and we'll have some fun
It's not over 'til the fat lady sings
Drink some more until your ears ring
When you're drunk you just don't care
You could be happy being anywhere
When you're drunk you don't give a fuck
You'll get by, just rely on your luck

Another drink, just for the hell
One less penny for the wishing well
Nothing makes sense, you are too drunk
You think an ugly fuck is really a hunk
It's all good, you're feeling real high
If you jump far enough you'll touch the sky
But don't you worry about a thing
In the morning you'll forget everything

And it's all over, you've hit the ground
No more fun, the world's stop spinning 'round
Your friends have to drive you home
Or you'll sleep outside all alone
And in the morning your head will ache
The light will hurt, the earth will quake
But it will be all worth the while
Because you got drunk, and that's you're style

Now that is all I have to say
Have a nice night, and have a nice day
I am too drunk to really care
About anything or about anywhere
And in the morning  I won't even ask
Because I'll know I've been an ass
Because when I drink, I know I suck
