title: "I'll Never Let You Go"

My love,
Dear love, sweat love
You are starlight
Shining down on me
So bright
I hold you close
All through the night
Soaring through the sky
High as a kite
You set me on fire
Forever blazing bright
Like a butterfly
Free and taking flight
I'll never let you go
I'll  never let you go

My son,
Dear son, sweet son
You are my life
You are there
Through all the strife
I would die
Without you in my life
It would wound me
Cut deep like a knife
I love you so deep
More than my own life
I'm here for you and daddy
Loving mother, loving wife
I'll never let you go
I'll never let you go

My brother,
Dear brother, sweet brother
You were always there
Through all my hardships
Forever did you care
I was bound and broken
And still you were there
All of the tears and pain
You and I did share
I thank you all so deep
And proudly I will wear
A sign of my love for you
Upon me I shall bare
I'll never let you go
I'll never let you goThis originally just started out as a love poem that came to me as I was listening to Janet Jackson's song 'Again'. Then for some reason I decided to write the second stanza. Obviously this is not about me because I don't have a son or a brother. This is about a character in my stories named Julianna Taylor. She "writes" this after her husband and son are killed.
