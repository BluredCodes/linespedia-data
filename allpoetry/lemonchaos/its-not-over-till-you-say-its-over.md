It’s Not Over ‘Till You Say It’s Over
by Dustin Moldoon

It starts with you...
staring in the mirror...
look at yourself...
don’t look at me…
help the world,
the end is near,
it’s a hideous sight...
call me crazy...
the truth can hurt,
but lies are worse...
hear the crying,
make it better,
take back the freedom,
love and happiness...
you’re not perfect,
do your best,
speak your mind,
let go, take chances,
make mistakes, get messy...
no more games,
no exceptions... 
follow your heart,
chase your dreams,
friends are friends forever...
don’t worry about me,
I’ll be okay...
don’t let me down...
thanks a lot...
And remember,
"It’s not over ‘till you say it’s over."
