Have you ever stayed up so late
You feel drunk
It's like an adiction
It's like a drug

When the world is spinning
And you hold your head tight
It's now morning
It's no longer night

Have you ever stayed up so late
You're no longer tired
It goes beyond hyper
It goes beyond wired

When your whole body shakes
From cold and from madness
And your mind will not focus
On anything but sadness

Have you ever stayed up so late
That this is how you feel
And inside your head
Anything seems real

And it's all you can do
To keep yourself sane
It's your way out of the maze
The sunshine through the rain

It's all over
You're okay
The sun comes up
It's another day
