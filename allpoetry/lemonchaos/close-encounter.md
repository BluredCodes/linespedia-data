Walking along in the shadows one nightI dared to glance at the skyA single craft hovered there aloneWithout a reason whyBrilliant lights shining downWarm upon my faceIn my mind a question arose-Were they from another place?My hopes and dreams of this momentNever could compareTo the feeling I felt insideWhen I saw it thereAnd as it turned and flew awayLeaving me in the darkI knew that my close encounterWould always be in my heartHere is yet another poem inspired by...
