Can anyone hear me?
Is anyone there?
Do I even matter?
Does anyone care?
If I died today,
Would they even know?
And if I knew they wouldn't,
Would I turn and go?
Is my life to be
A simple one, alone?
Will I go on forever,
Never to be known?
Is this how I must live,
In the silent dark?
Knowing I could end it all,
With a single spark?
Can anyone hear me?
Is anyone there?
Am I just a nobody,
Someone the world can spare?
If I ran away,
Would they know I'm gone?
If my chair was empty,
Would they just move on?
Is my life worth living?
Will I ever know?
If I run away,
Where is there to go?
Who would dare to help me,
A street child on her own?
But which life is the better one,
Ignored or all alone?Is this about my life? I honestly can't answer that. Yeah, sometimes I feel like this, but doesn't everyone? Written & posted November 19, 2001.
