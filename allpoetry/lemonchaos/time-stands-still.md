Time stands still
Amazing beautiful love
A perfect pair together
A match from up above
Time stands still
A simple kiss of longing
A feeling of perfection
A sense of just belonging
Time stands still
A dance among the stars
Peaceful time together
Healing all the scars
Time stands still
A never ending bond
Forever strong devotion
