Lower, baby
Almost there
To the left
Just a hair
Put it in the hole
Hammer it in
"Oh my god!"
"It's perfect, Ben!"
The picture hangs
Upon the wall
That's what we were doing
After all!Perverts! What the hell did you think they were doing? Hehe...
