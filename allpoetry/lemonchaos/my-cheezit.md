My Cheezit

Sqaure
Salty
Perfect

My Cheezit

Yummy
In my
Tummy

My Cheezit

Orange
And sunny
Bright

My Cheezit

Perfect
Snack
Delight

My Cheezit

Yum
Yum
Eat 'em up

Gotta
Love
Them
