One word is all it takes to make my heart soarOne word from your perfect lipsPerfectly shapedBottom pouting slightly more than topMy name they formA heavenly soundAll it would take is one slight touchYours to mineI silently wish for that one touchFrom the perfectly shaped lipsOf the perfectly made manThis poem, of course, is about my main man David Duchovny aka Fox Mulder. This is definately how I feel about his lips. Written & posted in October, 2000.
