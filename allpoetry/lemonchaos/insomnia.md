(Drum beat)

I sigh and lay my head down
For me to take a rest
Twenty Z's for me to catch
A lovely sleepy fest
I've been up for thirty
Hours straight - no lie
My head is pounding
Sleeping sounding
I feel like I could cry

I cloes my eyes up real tight
And think about my day
Maybe I can bore to sleep
And dream the night away
But my head is hurting
And it will not let me sleep
I can not dream
I wanna scream
Awakey I must keep

I toss and turn for hours
At a time in bed
The clock reads 5oclock am
I grab onto my head
Just two more hours, baby
And then I can get up
And go to school
All the while drool
As I doze like a little pup

So here I am in math class
With my head down on my desk
And when my teacher wakes me
She asks about my rest
I ask her what she speaks of
The word foreign to me
Insomnia
Is my game-a
But then I fall back asleep

Oh, insooooooomnia
Oh, insooooooomnia

It messes with your head
Makes you feel dead
Makes you just wanna crawl
Like a fly on the wall
Lay around on the floor
Awake eight hours more
Nothing that you can do
Sleep evades you!

Oh, insooooooomnia
