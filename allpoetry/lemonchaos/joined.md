In the dark I hear his voiceSoft, gentleIt caresses me deep withinI am overwhelmedThe slightest touch sends shivers up my spineI am overjoyedThe ghost of a kissSweet, loving, tenderI feel love, desire, and passionAs he and I are joinedHands intertwined, heats afireLove, lust, escalating desireMoving as a river, flowing togetherHe and I are joinedThis poem was kind of an accident, I spit it out while trying to write an X-Files fanfic. I think it turned out pretty good, though. Written & posted in October, 2000.
