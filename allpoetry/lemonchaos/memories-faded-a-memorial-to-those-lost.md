Inhale the death like smoke
Blow it out and watch it swirl
The curling whirl makes my stomach churn
Towering inferno cause everything to burn
All around me lay chared down
Chared black like coal
Blue sky brown

Remember the ones long lost
Spirits crumble as you pass
All their hope and resolve gone
Wondering desperately what they did wrong
Forever gone as you disappear
Whispers in the sky
Are you here?

All alone now... they cryLet us never forget those lost almost five months ago... May they finally rest in peace.
