When I'm lost in the dark
And I don't know where to turn
And there's not a candle
Left to burn
I just reach my soul out to you
And I know you'll lead the way
Once I'm safe in your loving arms
I know I'll be okay tonight... tonight

Well I've been traveling this road
Both day and night
And searching for a way
To make things right
But when you reach your hand out to me
And help me to get home
I know deep down in my heart
I'll never be alone again... again
'Cause

Chorus
I know love will... keep me warm at night
And love will... make everything all right
And love will... keep me close to you
And love will... be a part of everything I do

Forever alone
I always thought I'd be
Until you came along
And rescued me
So now I know
The meaning of true love
You are the one
That I dream of always... always
And

Chorus
