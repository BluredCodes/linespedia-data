No one reads my poems anymore
It's like they kicked me out the door
Pulled the mat from under my feet
Shoved my ass onto the street

That's alright, that's okay
I'm going to write them anyway
No harm done, I'm just fine
Keep on writing these poems of mine

Comments dwindled from five to one
Instead of alot I only get some
And when they tell me I really suck
I tell them I don't give a fuck

That's alright, that's okay
I'm going to write them anyway
I won't wine, I won't cry
Keep on reading to find out why

I write my poetry for myself
Not to satisfy anyone else
The world is lucky I decide to share
A little nicety would only be fair

That's alright, that's okay
I'm going to write them anyway
Will not worry, will not fret
I will get my comments yetThis is not a complaint (even though my comments *have* been dwendling), I just thought of it when I was realizing that at first everyone was reading my poetry, but as soon as I started writing some comedic stuff it went down the drain. I guess I already jumped the shark! OUCH! But, I'm going to keep writing because it's what I love. Eventually someone is going to come along and read it and say, "Hey, that's really cool." That's what this poem is all about.
