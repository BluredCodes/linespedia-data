I am so hungry
Hungry with desire
The flames of love
Lick sharp at my heart
The pangs of regret
For not speaking my mind
Hit home like nothing else
And I can do nothing but live
Nothing but love
Nothing but let you know
You and you alone
Always you and no one else
Just one look
And I melt
Melt, melt away
I love you
If you could only see
If you only felt the same
Because I am so hungry
Hungry for you
Hungry for your loveFor someone special...
