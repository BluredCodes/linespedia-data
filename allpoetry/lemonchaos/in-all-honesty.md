In all honesty, I am just a girl
Not to be accepted by this great big world
A great many others tend to agree
There's got to be something the matter with me

Straight red hair, plump ugly face
God should send someone else in my place
Not to be here, not to be loved
But to be bound and gagged and gloved

A fungus, a plague, an evil thing
A screeching sound above a gentle ring
Don't take me serious, I am not true
You'll be infected if my skin touches you

Listen not to those who say
I am not evil; I should not go away
Lies I say, lies all around
Force my ugliness into the ground

Beat me, kill me, make me bleed
All of humanity must be freed
I am not worthy of this world
