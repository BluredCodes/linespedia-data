Day one:
Shining inside
I see the light in your eyes
Amazing beauty
Cannont be described
The smile on your face
It gives you away
You are perfection
I love you today

Day two:
Deeply longing
It is your touch I crave
Patiently waiting
Keeps me in a daze
And now that you're gone
I can only wait
Sitting in silence
Alone in my crate

Day three:
Secretly wishing
To feel the touch of your skin
Everyday wanting
Never giving in
But on the slight chance
Your hand may brush mine
I'll know in that moment
If you will be mine

To be continued...Started 1-30-02, This is about that special someone... And yes, it's supposed to be 'Guil' not 'Guilt'. I will try to write a stanza every day until one day there is no more to write.
