I can't explore the world from inside my cell
I can't lie to myself and say that I'm well
I may fool the world but I'll never fool me
I'll never quite see the way others will see

My life is my own but I choose not to live
I take what I get but I get what I give
All alone, screaming from within
"I'll never give up, but I'll never win"

Life is a lesson that we all must learn
The flames of desire are easy to burn
Love letters written and love letters lost
Life is not worth the ungodly cost

Take as you get, easy to receive
Anyone and everyone once will deceive
No one can really be called a true friend
Everyone will be alone in the end

Alone I shall be, alone I am now
Won't ask myself why, won't ask myself how
A last letter typed, a last page has turned
The candle of life has finally burned

Take me down, don't follow me past
The weeds have grown tall, get lost in the grass
A final adieu before I must part
An echo of me shall remain in your heartJust something I needed to write. Another one will probably come soon, I've hit a poetry streak. November, 2001.
