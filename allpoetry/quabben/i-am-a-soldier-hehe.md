---
title: "I Am A Soldier (hehe)"
writer: "Quabben"
slug: "i-am-a-soldier-hehe"

I am a soldier in command of my men
We move with stealth so silently
Blood pressure is up, the mission is on
My team is tense our goal is near
I signal by hand, positions take up
We’ll wait till we see the white of the eyes
And finish this mission with no loss of life
In a short moment our goal looks at me
With the speed of a cobra I use the net
With the speed of a mongoose our goal moves back
The ladder then topples, my team are in fits
I’m flat on my back, my command in a mess
No longer soldiers my team are my kids
Giggling and laughing and making much noise
Which calls to my wife, she comes outside
‘Billy’ she shouts ‘stop fooling around
You’ll frighten that budgie and scare him to death’
She raises her arm and Bluebeard flies down
To cheers from the children she takes him inside
I am a soldier, but not today
Today I’m a father just out to play
