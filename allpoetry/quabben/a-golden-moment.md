It was just a glanceWhen I caught sight of herA golden moment by perchanceThe sparkling movement of her haircaught my attention as in a tranceAs she passed by my eyes just staredI couldn’t move or turn my headWhen out of view how I despairedFeeling like I’d turned to leadWorrying that her image blurredBut, her picture was not lost to meThe golden moment in my soulI watch each day if I can seeHer pass this way, as she’s my goalAnd maybe then with her I’ll be
