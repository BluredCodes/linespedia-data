title: "Jimmy's Last Mission"
writer: "Quabben"

We had been friends for quite a few years
Long before the military asked for our ears
Convinced by their story we signed the pledge
So many times we went to the edge
For God and for country we stuck to the task
When missions were over our hands we did clasp
The day he was taken I remember with dread
How my friend Jimmy, ended up dead

The mission was dangerous, search and destroy
Covertly seeking to find our boy
The villagers told us he had long gone
A young girl praised the deeds he had done
Jimmy then questioned the thoughts of her words
She didn’t like it and shot him right there
I lost my friend Jimmy on that fateful day
In his last moment I blew her away
