---
title: "To The Dogs"
writer: "Quabben"
slug: "to-the-dogs"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/29817-To-The-Dogs-by-Quabben"
---

Low lies the cloud tonight
darkness all around
Low lies my heart so tight
joy has run aground

Freezing is the cold tonight
bitter is this life
Freezing in the small dim light
Lonliness is rife

Better now you have come home
light is all around
Better now you have your bone
good dog, my faithful hound
