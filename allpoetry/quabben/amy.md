Amy is a shepherd
of a German line
She is my friend
She is my guard
A shadow too
Where’er I go

I give her time
and she takes mine
I give her food
and she takes mine
I give her a bed
and she takes mine

Full of trust
she gives her love
Faithful thru’ and thru’
I worry though about
her love, I think she
goes to far

For I am almost certain
She wants to marry me!!This is no joke!!
