An Indomitable Spirit

As the darkness of night closes in on me
I reflect on my life that’s past
By circumstance or sheer bad luck
I’ve had my trials to bear
Battered and bruised, I bore the pain
Couldn’t stand straight; but my spirit unbowed
Broken and bloody, I bore the pain
Couldn’t stand straight; but my spirit unbowed

Family and friends, lovers too
Have gone to the other side
Beyond the veil of the silent night
Out of my reach for now
Through grief and sorrow, I bore the pain
My heart so shattered; but my spirit unbowed
Through rivers of tears, I bore the pain
My heart in pieces; but my spirit unbowed

How do I survive? You may well enquire
I cope; with the help of a friend
An indomitable spirit; God’s given to meI see lots of sad poems on this site, maybe this will help someone in their sadness.
