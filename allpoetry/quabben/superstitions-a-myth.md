I walked under a ladder today at the mall
what was I thinking of, not looking at all
Last week, a mirror I dropped on the floor
in shattered pieces a crystal no more
Then the black cat that crossed my path
I knew I should never have put on my cap
The salt was a slip, my hand went amiss
All goes to show, superstition’s a myth
