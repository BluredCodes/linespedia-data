You asked just who the Quabben was
I’ll tell you now but you must believe
Since days of old he was with you
His silence somewhat subdued
Mighty were his deeds perceived
You finding it hard, can you conceive
He stood for right and sure would fight
Your case he would defend
His name was written before our time
Before we all were born
He stood before the hordes of hell
So strong and battle worn
Never there but ever here
Just where he does belong
A friend in need a friend in deed
With power and might should he need to fight
Call his name, yes call his name
He’ll stand beside you there
For Quabben is and ever was
A friend to fight the foe
Your inner strength, your other self
For Quabben lives in youdianes asked what a quabben was, i hope this answers her question
