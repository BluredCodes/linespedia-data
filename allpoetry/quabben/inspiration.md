When darkness falls into your life
And shadows drift across your mind
The light is dim, the spark is gone
Now is the time to start the fight
And look again to find some bright
Take hold tight with all your might
And let your heart do just what’s right
The dark will dim into a light
And shadows’ll drift into the night
Until your glow becomes a sight.
