---
title: "The News"
writer: "Quabben"
slug: "the-news"
source: "AllPoetry"

More death and destruction, even more.
Disease and starvation, a plague or two.
Mayhem and murder, with pestilence sure,
Diseases from nature and from man too.

What am I doing with these things on my mind?
It’s not just the sadness or even the blues.
The longer I wait I get more of this kind.
What am I doing? I’m watching the News.
