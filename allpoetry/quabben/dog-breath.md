Dog Breath

My garden has some lemon trees
that fruit the whole year round
Bright yellow edibles bathing in the sun
Sweetly smelling and sharp to taste
they never go to waste
If by chance a ripe young fruit
should escape my eager hands
Amy, my dog will leap straight up
and take her share to ground
She treats her treats just like balls
until they’re mashed and well sucked dry
Amy has the sweetest breath
Of any a dog you’ll find.A bit more about Amy, a dog I think wants to marry me.
