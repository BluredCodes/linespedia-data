Restless

Nights are forever
in the land of gloom
Desperately waiting
for day to dawn
Hopelessly tossing
breaking your heart
Fitlessly turning
fro to and fro
Needing to rest
the impossible quest
Hurry oh light and
kill this damn nightJust a simple little write that isn't really me its more what I picked up from some of the sad writes I read on the site.
