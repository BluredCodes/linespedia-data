title: "Heaven Is No Place To Fear"
writer: "Quabben"

Out of the blue
from over the moon
that lump of rock
came flying through

Impact soon and
then our doom
no way a bomb
much worse for us

Now its dark
and full of gloom
the day has gone
our time has come

My Lord! Its light
it is not night
we are all here
Heaven is no place to fearI've been watching to many disaster movies
