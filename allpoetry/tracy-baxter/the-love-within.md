I am so lucky,
To know this one thing,
That love is within us,
And can be found again,

Although we may hurt with a pain deep inside,
Love is the strength to break down the walls in our lives,
It surround us with a light to show us the way,
Towards pure happiness and contentment,
For which we need in each day.

So hold it in both hands and don’t let it go,
For it is our essence,
And we are never alone.
