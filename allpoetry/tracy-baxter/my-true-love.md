title: "My True Love"

If I never love again I will know this one thing,
That I had what all have searched and few ever find,
A true kindred spirit,
A given heart so pure and kind,
Whose eyes shone innocence and whose heart belonged to mine,

But I did not see this gift in my life,
An offering of pure happiness,
A true love that would never die,
And I pushed it away,
Searching for something already found,
And lost you forever, 
The only light in my life,

You are now gone,
Never to be a part of my life,
And this love is all but a memory,
