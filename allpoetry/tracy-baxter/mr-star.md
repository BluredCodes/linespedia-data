Have you ever held the star of your life?
Did it sparkle brightly in your hands?

I have held my star, 
It did not shine,
It crumbled in my hands like sand, 
I tried to keep hold I did, 
But the more I tried, the faster it slipped away,

I’m so scared now, with each breath I await the darkness that will become my life,
My star has gone,
