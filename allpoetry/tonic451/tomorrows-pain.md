---
title: "Tomorrow's pain"
writer: "Tonic451"

In my hated past and so I hate it more today 
Life was cruel, too unjustful, so unjoyful
For me to take and bear it's cold harshness

These days were so long as if it were an eternity in which
I lived in a deeper sorrow than most have ever lived
My heart so poisoned from my own hate and rage
Against all the world, I torn my soul to save myself
From this misery I cursed everyday that gave me such pain

Can tomorrow bring a better day, I would ask life so mournfully
Why hate so passionfully and not so passionfully love?
Love is something I can only fathom but never truely know

You're eyes are wet, which makes you blind to love's sight
Weep no more, tears be gone, then you'll see love's light
