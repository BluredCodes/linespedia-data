title: "With blind eyes only"

Somewhere
I drift between
night and day
restless and selfless
chasing after love
with blind eyes only 

I fell that night,
she left me with mental scars
she left me pissed at the world
she left me with my jaw still open
she left me damaged

I get lost in the memories 
of her and I sometimes 

I can't forget the pain
of when.....

she left me sick and starving 
she left me crying and fading
she left me praying to God 

To take back the memories
of her and I
 
memories,
that are like 
little pictures hanging
in the back of my mind

They won't go away

Take back the memories of
her and I
