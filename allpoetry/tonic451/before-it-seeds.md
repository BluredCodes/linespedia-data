Youthful minds sway towards such violence,
'cause of hate within kept in silence 

Violence, the result of vain hate 
which I will not let it be my ending fate

What nurtures this kind of hate to such an end?
Does it come from the ones we call a friend?

Maybe its in the things we see or hear
that could bring people to hate and fear

Many victims of this wrath and violence lay dead
and their names rest on the tombstones to be read

Painful memories and scars will never fade away
in every ending night and in every rising day
I guess it's the price I must pay

All I can do is learn to forgive the deeds
of others and let go of the hate that feeds     
the violence of our wrath before it seedsthis poem is for the victims of the school shootings
