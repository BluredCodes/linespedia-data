What only a few words can describe:

Somewhere along the creation of a dream land of peace called life,
The entrance of darkness crept in, consuming all that was once good.
Though life is what one makes of it,
The truth still stands that what one sees, is not always what one should believe.
With so many large words, one can still only describe a small bit of what life might have been,
But never will be…
The fact is that people are never afraid of death.
No, not death…..For death is nearly the end of another chapter in this dream.
It is dieing that frightens all and creates such a wonder……..
And so I say…….I am not afraid of death…….I am afraid of dieing.I wrote this a while ago and thought though it was short... it is the truth I know all too well.
