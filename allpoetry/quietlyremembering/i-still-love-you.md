title: "I Still Love You"
writer: "QuietlyRemembering"

Some people say they have forgotten who they were when they were little,
But I have not.
I still remember the way you held me and loved me,
The way you cried when I was hurt,
And the month you slept by my hospital bed just to make sure I could see a smiling face in the early morning.
I remember the ducks by the pond that ate bread until they sank,
And I remember the bird who nearly bit off my finger,
But I loved them all just the same.
It seems like yesterday we would laugh at the pig who just couldn't understand how to stand on 4 feet,
And I remember how you told me to be strong,
And I remember how you held my little arm every night before I slept,
And sang me a song just because I asked,
And I remember how you were always there even if you were so many miles away.

I remember everything we had together,
And I will always remember...
The day I forget is the day I relive them with you all over again.

Until then....
I still love you.Dedicated to Betty Elaine Strote (1921-1997)
