My Only Teddy Bear (Part 2.. Teddy Bearing Gift.)

It may have been the best day of my life,
The only day dedicated to loving and nothing less.
I thought it would be forever,
You told me it would be forever,
But you lied, and I believed you.
He meant so much to me,
And you tore him apart.
He was my love packed into that little heart he held,
With my grandmother's braclet shining so proudly.
It was for you,
And it was for love.
You tore his heart apart the second after your lie had surfaced.
You savagely destroyed everything he meant,
Everything he was.
You still hold that braclet as if it were your own,
But you don't deserve it,
Nor did you deserve him.
You can call me what you want,
But I took him back,
And now he stares back at me,
Next to the other Ted,
And all I can see is his empty bear brown eyes,
And the emptiness that once held his heart...
