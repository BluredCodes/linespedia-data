When I come home I know you won't be there,
You never are.
You are never there when I need you,
Or when I ask for your help,
But when you needed guidance,
I lent all I had,
But it never was good enough, was it?
You always tell me how much you love me and need me,
But when I turn on the light you are dissapeared,
And when I close my eyes at night you are forgotten.
In the morning you are still not home,
And it seems you no longer live here,
Because I have forgotten your true face,
And I have not yet seen your true smile.
You tell me you are depressed but I cannot see why,
You have everything life has to offer and more,
And I will no longer stay around to be your pawn.
I have no sympathy for those without a heart,
But even less for those who's is stone and shattered.

When I come home you are never there,
