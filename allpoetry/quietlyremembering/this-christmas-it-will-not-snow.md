This Christmas it will not snow,
The Earth is too broken to support such a magical existance.
You have forgotten all that you give and have been subdued to lifes simple mistakes,
Pretending to be something you're not.

You fight for something that is not there,
Have you fogotten who is your real foe?
Or do you choose to run from all those who truely love you,
Because you are so lost among a crowd of fools.

I wish I could help you now,
But somethings just need to be done on your own,
And when you return I will be waiting by the door,
But if you do not plan to stay just forget ever stopping by and walk right past,
For I cannot bear to see you.

Can't you see me standing here,
I wish you could only know,
But when you come through please give me a call,
I cannot sleep until I know you are home,
And once again safe in your own bed....
In your own home.

Please forgive all the mistakes I have made,
I have done them for you,
And only because I know how to love,
But you do not love me too,
Because I am nothing more than a whisper in your ear.
I don't even know if you can still hear me,
It seems I am fading away.

~~~~~~~~~~~~

It is true,
The Earth is broken,
And it is true,
You have forgotten,
And this I know...
This Christmas it will not snow,
What doesn't exist doesn't show.

Or does it?It won't snow... because it is just too warm... you try to figure out what that means.
