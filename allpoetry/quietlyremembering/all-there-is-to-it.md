Everyday striving to do my best,
Yet I know I can do better.
Every moment working to succeed into a higher,
More splendid life,
But every night before I close my eyes and sleep a dreamless oblivion,
I wonder...
Is it worth wasting all my life for this one second of greatness?
Is it worth everything I do to be better,
Every second of my life being better than the next...
But who am I anymore?
A mad, crazed, power hungry, ego?
Yes... I am,
And I am proud of it...
If I can make one second of my life wonderful,
Then all the rest don’t matter...
That is all there is to it.humm... yup
