---
title: "Just an Emotion"
writer: "QuietlyRemembering"
slug: "just-an-emotion"

I wish I could choose who I loved,
Then I would never lose a minute of sleep at night,
I would never dream of you,
Never feel different from everyone else.
I wish I knew why my heart always chooses the wrong directions,
But then again, love is just an emotion.... right?
Love is just something you are born with and cannot change no matter how hard you try,
Just like hate... you can't teach people to hate or to not hate...
They choose themselves...
I'ts love all the same... right?

Maybe someday love will find it's own way home,
But for now I am stuck loving you...
Or so it seems...
But love changes everday...
But one thing doesn't...

Some truths are better left untold...
I'd rather lie than be dethroned.::sigh::
