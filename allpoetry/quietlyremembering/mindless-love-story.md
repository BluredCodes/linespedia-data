---
title: "Mindless Love Story"
writer: "QuietlyRemembering"
slug: "mindless-love-story"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/28532-Mindless-Love-Story-by-QuietlyRemembering"
---

It seems now I'm talking advice from all the wrong places,
From those who's love is very short but vision seems so great.
You never really can tell who will inspire you until you let people talk.
Now it seems I'm caught in this circle of confusion however,
And beleive me:
If I could break free, 
I would.

Lately I've learned that you can't choose who you love,
Or who you hate.
It's just something that happens.
And what happened today is just one of those things you try hard to deny,
But just can't seem to do it.

My brother asked me a question.
It was simple I mind you,
But It made me think.
"So.. do you like her?" he said,
As if he knew me,
And he did.
"No, I don't think so,"
I replied,
But now I know the truth.

Now I truely know that you can't choose who you love...
That's a fact.
The only problem is that I find love in all the wrong places to...
I suppose that's just what love does though...
Kinda funny, Huh?

P.S.  Isn't it funny that red is both the color of love and the color of hate?  ...Just thought I'd point that out.hmm... ::sigh::
