---
title: "You Don't Know Me"
writer: "QuietlyRemembering"
slug: "you-dont-know-me"

Many say that family is the most important thing to them....
I envy their every move,
For I cannot find a reason to believe such and ignorant statement.

Many tell me Christmas is their favorite holdiday....
I only wish I could say the same,
But to me it just another day to sleep in and forget yesterday's troubles.

Many are rich and many are poor....
I am neither,
It is the worst: You expect to have comforts but when they are gone you miss them more.

Many don't understand me....
But I'm not as complicated as you might think,
I'm just less predictable.

Many judge me....
Don't judge me,
I am much different than you.

Many people hate...
So do I.
But differently than you.Heh... Merry Christmas I suppose.
