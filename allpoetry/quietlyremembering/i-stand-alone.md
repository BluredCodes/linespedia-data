---
title: "I Stand Alone"
writer: "QuietlyRemembering"
slug: "i-stand-alone"
source: "AllPoetry"

In a world infatuated with hurting others,
Even when they try to help,
I try to stand alone.
In a world where people kill themselves because they cannot take it....
Where eagles no longer soar because we do not let them,
I try to stand alone.
In a world bound by limits and tests,
I try to break them and pass them with flying colors,
I try to stand alone.
In a world where hate is said to be freedom of speech,
And white doves can be shot down from a red sky,
I try to stand alone.
I try to live my own life,
Away from your pain and suffering,
Becuase that is the only life I can stand to keep...
Inside I am much weaker than I appear,
But still I will stand alone.
I will not bow down to the richest of kings,
Or be enslaved by a might god,
Or be controled by the very barriers that have been set for me,
Because I stand alone,
And to stand alone I must break laws and conquer mountains.
To stand alone I must be true to only one person,
And that is myself.

Inside I am much weaker than I appear,
That way... 
I can anticipate the next corner.
