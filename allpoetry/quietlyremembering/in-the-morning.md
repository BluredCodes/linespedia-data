---
title: "In The Morning"
writer: "QuietlyRemembering"

The morning is the only time I have alone,
In the still darkness of the night before,
The moon dissapeared but the stars still glow,
And even though soon the sun will shine,
For now I can be happy...
By myself,
In happiness...
In darkness...
In forgotten pains and over-ventured sorrows...
In hope and in defeat...
I am happy being here...
Here I am safe.

What more is there to say?
