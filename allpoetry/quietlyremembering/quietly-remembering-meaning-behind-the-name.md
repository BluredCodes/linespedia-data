Every day that I live,
Solitary,
Confined to my own self,
My own thoughts,
My own mind and my own desires.
Every second breathing another breath,
Crying another tear,
Forgetting the last second's pain,
Hoping tomorrow won't be quite as bad.
Every step I take,
Striving towards perfection,
Crawling towards infinity,
Pretending to be everything,
Achieving nothing.
Every moment I still think of,
Every tear and every fear,
Every need and every want,
Everything you said to me before they took you away,
Stole you from me,
Destroyed my life,
Lacerated my wounded heart.
It's been so long,
But I think of it every moment,
Every sleepless night,
Every hopeless day.
Throughout all those forgotten memories I still remember one,
Still think of you every day,
Still hope you'll return to me again,
Still cry for your forgivness every moment,
Still live without you.
Still, every day,
I remember,
Calmly,
Alone,
Remembering,
Quietly.just tell me all your thoughts...
