I have been with allpoetry for but a little time but I have read so many wonderful poems from many wonderful people.  

With all my heart I wish you guys would recosider but I can see now that there is much greater things that you must accomplish.  I wish all of you luck wherever you take your poetry and please keep me posted on what's going on with that new poetry site.

To all those who fight for the cause and not the reward I thank you for blessing me and everyone with such great poetry and even greater hearts.  Only a few many can be so pure.  

Dedicated to all those who never give in to ignorance:

So many tears that are to be cried,
So many hearts that are to be splintered.
There are only so many words that can be said,
Before the last tear cascades down a broken cheek.
I stand up for all that I can secure,
I hold true all that I know.
Thank you... for being better then I could ever be.
Thank you... for being better then them in every way you can.
Thank you...
For not giving up...

It's all I could think of... im too shocked to write anything grand.  I just wish those who must leave luck and forever will I be your friend.

...Good bye
