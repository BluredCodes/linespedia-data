---
title: "Will You Love Me Just Tonight"
writer: "QuietlyRemembering"
slug: "will-you-love-me-just-tonight"

I suppose I'm supposed to be your crying shoulder,
But what tears have you to cry tonight...
When everything in life is perfect,
I must ask why you stare into the dark night sky with such fright,
Such terrible fright.

Do you not beleive that the very same sun will rise once again in the sam place?
Do you not see that you have everything you will ever need...
And even if it's not me you love,
There are others more willing to fight...
Are you willing to fight?

Do you not realize that the tears you cry aren't sacred?
They have lost their meaning the last time you cried...
The last time you forgot what everyone has given you,
And even though I know your life isn't everything it should be,
I know you don't try to make it either.

Do you not realize what I tell you,
Or are you too frightened to answer me in return...
When all I'm looking for is an answer,
Will you return the light and hold me true tonight.
Will you love me just tonight?
