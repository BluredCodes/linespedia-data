Im sick of all those people who tell me how to live my life,
Tell me how to be free.
I hate those people who base their lives on religion,
When all religion does is produce hate and destruction.
Don't lie to yourself,
You so called god is nothing but a make believe figure,
The perfect human so to speak,
And that's all he will be,
Nothing but an undying character in the biggest fiction book ever to be published.
I just wish you could see so you could stop wasteing your sundays getting up early,
Stop wasting your holidays in a church.
I wish you could see so you could stop wasting your life... preeching about your death.
If you don't remember, life is supposed to be short,
So work things out,
Get a life,
And stop dreaming about make-believe characters like little babies...

*Out of the kindness of my heartDon't mind me... im mad.
