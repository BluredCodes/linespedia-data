---
title: "I Just Need Me"
writer: "QuietlyRemembering"
slug: "i-just-need-me"

So you say I'm your friend but you walk away,
So you say you care but you only talk about 'me'
So you say you love me but you don't even know the meaning.
So you say you know me but you don't know shit.

So, you think you're my friend,
Then why doesn't anything matter to you?
What I think,
What my dreams are,
What I am.

If I'm your friend then why don't you care?
If I'm your friend then why are you over there,
Instead of over here?
If I'm your friend,
Then you need a new one,
Because I've had friends,
And they always do the same.

I don't need friends...
I just need me.For a special someone who doesn't even know me.
