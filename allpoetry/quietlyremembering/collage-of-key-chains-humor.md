What a strange thing this is,
So many key chains,
So many sayings... 
What to do... what to do...
Collect them all,
Of course.

There's this one; my favorite,
I don't discriminate,
I hate everyone'

 Then theres that one,
'Do I look like a frickin peoples person...
I didn't think so.'

And I don't mean to harass,
But 'could you drive better if I shoved that cell phone up your ass?'

Then there is my motto,
The one that describes me interally...
'To save us both time,
Assume I know everything.'

And trust me,
As time goes by...
I will have more...
But until then remember:
'Don't drink and drive,
You wouldn't want to spill your beer.'Trust me.. more to come.. more to come.
