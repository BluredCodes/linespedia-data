You think you're so special huh?
You're nothing but a dumb son of a bitch,
Who can't talk unless it's yelling,
Who can't reason unless you get your way.
You think I'm gonna look up to someone like you? 
I would never want to be you...

I hate you....

You think you're better then me?
You're nothing but a low level scum on the bottom of my fucking shoe,
I scrap you off and throw you away....
You mean nothing to me.
You are nothing to me.
You never have been.

I hate you....

Why don't you just go the fuck away,
Leave me alone!!!!
Why don't you take that proud ass of yours right out the door,
'Cause sooner or later you'll be sorry.
I promise, above anything....
You will be sorry.

I hate you....

Stop lying,
Stop yelling,
Stop screaming,
Stop hitting,
STOP CURSING,
STOP SWEARING,
STOP SMACKING,
STOP BREAKING!!!!

I HATE YOU 
I HATE YOU
I HATE YOU
I HATE YOU
I HATE YOU
I HATE YOU

I HATE YOU!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!Sorry... I really had to get that out of my system.
