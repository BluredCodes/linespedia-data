It's funny how things come and pass so quickly,
And you don't realize what you had until you have lost it,
And you don't understand what life is until you have died.
It's funny how things never go the way they are supposed to,
But they always end up being something great in the end...
You just can't see it because you are blind,
But when you wake up tomorrow you will look back upon now and realize that you had it all,
And you threw it away for something you thought would bring you happiness,
When you had it all along,
You just couldn't see it.

It's funny how the memories play, rewind, and play again in my head,
Every day as I survive,
Every night as I lay in bed wishing I hadn't...
They just keep repeating,
Over and over and over,
Calling to me,
Yet not as cliche.
They are all I have left of times when I was truly happy.
Then again...
I might be happy now and not even realize it until tomorrow morning,
But by then it will just be another memory and I will waste the rest of my life wishing I could relive my life,
When I am reliving it every moment...

I just wish life were a movie...
