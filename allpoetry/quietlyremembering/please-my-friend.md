I never really knew how to tell you how much you mean to me.
From the first day I say you staring at me through crystal aqua eyes,
The most pleasant smile resting on such a beautiful face I knew you were different than them,
Better than them.
Now that smile hides behind a frown.
I wish I could show you how to be happy,
I wish you could know how much it means to me to see you smile.
I never knew how much that quote meant to me until I met you:
"You shouldn't frown because you never know who's falling in love with your smile"
I wish I could tell you in words how much it means to me,
How much it has always meant,
And always will.
Please don't lie to me,
I know you hear these words that I speak so bluntly of.
I love you in every way a friend can be loved,
All I ask is that you love yourself.
Don't submit to the cruelty of others,
The pain in the world.
You are beautiful to me, 
And I remember once when that was all that mattered,
I wish it did now.
I wish you could see.
