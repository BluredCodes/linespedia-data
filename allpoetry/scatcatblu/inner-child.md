---
title: "'inner child'"
writer: "scatcatblu"

stagnant pools of wasted dreams
drowning every night it seems
shadows follow the path I take
falling from a darker age
long and slender stalks of doom
reach for me in darkness bloom
torn asunder running wild
clinging to my broken child
seeds of darkness fall to earth
weeds of past times clog my path
interweave and breed once more
tempting blossoms hiding thorns
every path I journey on
scars of darkness bleed me strong
force me wary trust unknown
on this path I search for home
hold my child asleep within
safe from darkness hate and sin
when I break stumble and fall
free my child her wings will soarSep 2001
