~^..^~


skip the beat
slip the beat
drop the bass
slide over here

beat the rhythm
meet the rhythm
take home blues
plays me free

rhythm of a chance
given me a chance
clap your hands
sway your hips

chance the music
dance the music
close your eyes
and breathe
