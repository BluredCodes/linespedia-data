Brought to being by the caterpillars' song
Who sang through the night and the next day long
Slept in a cradle rocking on a thorn
Looked upon stars and there was I born

Sown together by fine silk thread
Wore I the garments that the angels did send
Taught by the fairies who came without light
Tucked me in and left with the night

Into the world I was sent
The form of a person to me was lent
The gifts of a past time I had not known
Hidden in a body that has around me grown

I know of something that can't be true
Within myself I know not who



(circa 1985 after reading way too much Shakespeare)
