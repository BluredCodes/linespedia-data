---
title: "'bi the way'"
writer: "scatcatblu"
slug: "bi-the-way"
source: "AllPoetry"

smiling eyes
personas' delight
naive lips
tempting to bite
look upon you
talking earnestly
and all I can do
mentally dream
restraining thought
your insensitive touch
bursting my dam
of urgent lustthe more you try to deny the more it occupies your mind
