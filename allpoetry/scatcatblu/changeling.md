---
title: "'changeling'"
writer: "scatcatblu"
slug: "changeling"
source: "AllPoetry"

if I were you and you were me
what intricacies of self would we see
what reflections of pain and enduring fights
of hardened shells and scars that bite
of feelings bought and thrown away
of lessons learnt and lonely days
what pearls of wisdom hidden beneath
dreams forgotten in realms of sleep
and joys portrayed amid flailing kites
loveless lives and blood stained nights
would we promptly exit or linger to stray
captured gazes on mouths afraid
