'little tug boat'

sometimes at work
I feel like I'm the only one
holding things together
steering the fleet
with my efficient little tug boat
while every other minute 
of my life
I feel like I'm subsiding
about to throw myself on a reef
nearly drowning
while I look for my own
little tug boat
to throw me some slack
and tow me to safety



'perchance to dream'

time and effort
sailing through the cosmos
out of reach
trailing behind
the patience and wisdom
that I doubt
I'll ever achieve
