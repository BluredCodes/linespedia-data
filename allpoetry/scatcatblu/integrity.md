~*~

sounds penetrate
reverberate
pass through
waves travel
expanding
signaling electrons
action
reaction
progression
concentration
productive state
pleasantly agreeing
over compensate
aggravate
placate
work ethic
charts display
efficiency
reliability
character
responsibility
diplomatic
constructive discourse
positive attitude alleviates
stress strain exhaustion

blank
erased
resurfaced
formatted
taken myself aside
renegotiated a settlement
seeking down time
on hold
externally coping
dying inside

~*~
