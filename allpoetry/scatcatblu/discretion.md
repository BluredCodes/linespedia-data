~~^~~



extract myself unnecessary

despicable to the end

walls of introspection

gotta stop banging my head

captured myself in cliche

some bad ass attitude

grinding hips to confusion

dancing moist interlude

extensive rehabilitation

implied surrender defeat

sculpting bones of contention

disdainfully indiscreet

sanctify my redemption

sharpening wit artfully

insomnia of interception

bleeding obscurity

transmission of perspective

echoing insomnia beat

responsive interaction

nobody fucks with me

change the music baby

kiss me trucefully

reaquaint my ignition

use me this time for real

roll the dice be playfull

submerged in your disguise

curiosity is a virtue

stop frustrating me with your eyes
