title: "'evolution'"

The petals fall from the yellow daisies that you gave me
on my desk they sit and glare at my indifference
much like you do when I want to be alone.

You give them to me always to cheer and remind me
of a love that I no longer know
but that you still hang on to tenaciously
hoping I will again come home.

Momentarily I look at these yellow petals
falling on my keyboard and wonder
if I can not feel this love that you await
then how can you be sure it is not gone.

The thought itself sad does not touch me here
I pick the petals from the letters and staring
uncomprehendingly at this collision of worlds
drop them in the trash where they belong.

I can say the words that describe the feelings
that I am supposed to show and understand the momentum
that drives our lives in the direction that you want to go
but inside is still numb.

I am sorry I am a self absorbed bitch
and if you are intent on waiting
you had better endeavour to be patient and strong
evolution takes time.7 Sep 2001
