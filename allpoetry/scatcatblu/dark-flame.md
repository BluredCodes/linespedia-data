title: "dark flame"
writer: "scatcatblu"

hey little shining light
hey little star
hey little blazing sun
hey little flaring gun
hey little flame

stop trying to touch my insanity
stop trying to cure my inadequacies
stop trying to take away my pain

i breath the light i close my life
i taste the tears i run away inside
i fill the silence with null and void
i drink the guilt i dance in the flames

hey there sunshine
hey there rain
beautiful blue
sky over green hills
watching rolling waves
crashing and caressing
jagged rocks
coming down again

darkness, oh sweet darkness
i prefer your velvet stage
i devour your moonlit silence
ecstatic to embrace your quiet rage
envelope me, protect me
spare me from this other reality
give me your strength and ambiguity
let me lose myself to your dark touch
let me share with you my pain
