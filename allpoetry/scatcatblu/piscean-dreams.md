~*~

sky light  day bright
star light  night sight
moon light  dream flight
selflight
insight

pontificate confusion
filtering reality

charming persuasion
contemporary craft

displaced misnomer
alignment deferred

projecting ascension
limited reserve

casting allusion
paper mache wings

temperate conditions
countdown begins

telemetry vantage
velocity ensues

cosmic connection
elemental blues

relatively seeking
piscean dreams

astral spectrum
insight foreseen

~*~>
