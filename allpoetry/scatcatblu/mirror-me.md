~*~

the golden pattern shines
sun and moon entwined
sparkling yellow starlight
dawns hushed eyes aglow
the craftsmanship is unique
quality for none to par
the handles intricate carvings
a warm life flows when held
I trace my fingers so gently
and caress the fine outlines
the central surface shimmers
radiating the patterns life
even in this shadow room
the object glints and moves
like a creature of nature
I imagine warm blood flowing
within its inner grooves
the urge I hold against me
I can not deny once here
I dance to hold within
those flames of day and night
I chance a finger straying
to the inner surface coiled
the smooth metallic feeling
even this seems to carry warmth
ever silent I breathe more slowly
I chance not a mist of fear
I firm my grip on wood
etched with lifes own grace
and in that moment of movement
I realise my doubts draw near
what if I look and again see
that reflection of nothingness
that dwells where I glance
and lays my numbness here
for all the world to notice
I lay this prophetic object
creature of lifes true light
face down on this barren scape
and wait for another chance
a possibility of a new path
with my fate shimmering intact
instead of endless shadow 
an abyss of endless black

~*~
