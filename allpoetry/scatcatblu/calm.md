---
title: "'calm'"
writer: "scatcatblu"
slug: "calm"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

i cant sleep i get up i write expelling the pain i lay down i can't stay down i wander through the rooms i listen to the night i sway with the wind more words invade my brain somersaulting and elongating inside i contemplate i pour further thoughts and emotions into black and white my heart dances and my mind reverberates in time i pace and stop listening to my thoughts wandering with myself enjoying the closeness craving sleep and soft comfort i lay down again the night has chilled my skin i snuggle against your warm side i sigh my body relaxes but thoughts swirl uncontrolled in sleep you reach out and hold my hand your sweet subconscious love calms my mind
