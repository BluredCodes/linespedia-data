I am alone
I like the darkness
in darkness I can see
I am unknown
I reach out
to find
what is not yet there
I am surrounded
crowded in
I can't be found
only what is wanted
I didn't make
none of it is me
I am seen
I am not here
although I am caged
I am free
I have nowhere to go
I am not here
I don't know where
I should be
the hope I have
does not know me
it will be lost
before I can reach
what I don't feel
I am alone
I torture myself
I am in vain
I want to help
the one that is alone
she will not talk
she will not feel
she is alone with me
in the darkness
hope is what I seekThis one is an oldie, way back when, but I still carry some of the same feelings and thoughts even though I have moved on in other ways!
