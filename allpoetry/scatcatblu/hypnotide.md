---
title: "'hypnotide'"
writer: "scatcatblu"
slug: "hypnotide"
source: "AllPoetry"

My hand presses against my heart
the thumping reassures me
I don't know why
reassures me of my own reality
sleep evades me
the sound of waves
crash through the night
they usually lull me
but they call to me tonight
I stand still
I hear the waves
I close my eyes
I feel them crashing over me
I feel them inside
I revel in the darkness
I listen to the quiet
punctuated by the waves
accompanied by my heart
