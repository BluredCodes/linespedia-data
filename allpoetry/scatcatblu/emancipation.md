~~*~~

woman
undone by a glance
i stop almost afraid to move
then you whisper me to your side
and i glide as on silken wings
unfamiliar with your casual repose
i feign disinterest of your subtle style
more used to suits and immaculate poise
i nod and smile looking for a diversion
accepting cocktails and small talk
delving into modern tax regimes
and baudy office discussions
extracting myself from your presence
i move towards safer ground
secure in a seated position
watching as the poseurs dance
reciting oft used incantations
hoping to spark a bite
relaxed by fruity concoctions
slipping into my comfort zone
settled in upholstered warmth  
i assume my indelible guise
peering nonchalantly at the fray
you slip unwarded to my side
beauty never smelt so sweet
as juniper and honey laced with lime 
you're so pleased at my presence
not my scene anymore
preferring to avoid the flimsy
who skulk daily in my doorway
with their foreign ideas of real
and you say how we look as sisters
both dressed in monochrome
your arm slips through mine
and your hand strokes my palm
we laugh and talk of escaping
possibilities of a midnight swim
or perhaps a more intimate club
where lesser sharks dwell
all the time gently stroking
naively glancing under perfect lashes
i find myself relaxing intoxicated
and i wonder how innocent
of my reactions to your presence
you really are
