~O~



inconsequential

thoughts scatter like bird seed

littering lifes path

caught up in revisions

framing scars

avoidance reaction

standing on broken ground

standing in open road

evening glances down

momentarily frozen

reasoning mistaken thought

pause to look beyond

comprehension

stop to witness

theatrical debut

the first star I applaud

searching the moon

reaquainted

meeting on the asphalt

moonlight soothes memories

fresh night air sparks life

revising destination

follow a star lit path



~O~
