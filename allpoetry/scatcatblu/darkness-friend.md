there's a journey waiting for me
a brilliant journey unlike any
I have so far seen
take the light away from me
darkness helps me see
wraps me in its tenderness
envelopes and softens
this world for me
darkness, my friend
with your shining moon
and twinkling stars
watching over me
you show to me the wonders
wonders from my dreams
dreams from my
sleep stained nights
nights you lingered with me
I once dreamt to be
the shining light in your midst
Now it's only your cold tenderness
I scream to kiss
watch over me my darkness friend
for in this nothing life I dwell
your painful love
is my lovely hellNov '95 - a dark time
