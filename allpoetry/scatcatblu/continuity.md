passion plays me mistress

never coming first

caressed by wrong intentions

bonded by relief

hypnotised expression

bed of unmade sleep

held silent in reflection

love lies lost in grief

waking from aggression

silent weapons drawn

mingled with my tears

your sweaty body shines

rusted armour fallen

bathed in cold demise

succumbing to the darkness

thankful numb am I
