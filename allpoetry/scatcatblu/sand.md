here again

why bother leaving

struggle to overcome

prove to myself

chance the disappointment

there's something more

than being numb



here I stand

find myself static

in this perpetual madness

filling up inside

with all the feelings and emotions

that I can never share

or touch to understand



here in the darkness

the pain and fear assault me

like the tide in an endless wave

eroding every fibre

plunging me deeper

into this dark heavy cave

where all my senses echo

reverberating and hollow



here all alone

not moving

afraid of breaking

shattering into complete nothingness

unrecognisable like the sand

tiny bits of never knowing

gritty and compounded

shards of empty discarded life

slipping through my hands



here I fall

lost or released

forgotten my original form
