~)(~

spilt milk
wiped up and eradicated
before spoiled

words describe my feelings
parlaying my insides
painting pictures
of black
blue
and grey

wallpapering a fountain
useful
it ain't

I see and hear
adequately

I think and speak
enough of my mind

I walk and manouvre
throughout life

offering congratulations
and condolences for those
who wander the path

going either way

I am full of compassion
insight and joy
for everybody I know

I seek justice and love
temper the anger and hurt
of friends and those I touch

I am always just fine

OK

perfectly happy with myself
couldn't be better actually

in a good place

I survive 
I compromise

I admit nothing else

~)(~
