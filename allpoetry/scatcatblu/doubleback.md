~**~


fear me
enbodying your lack
of substance
I partake
of your inequality
starving
my mind
your ignorance
despising
the air
inhaling stench of decay
tracks
wayward traveller
hidden transformation
fear
smells of hate
taint your path
lead me
astray
den of remorse
paths
strewn harsh regret
stumble and break
stream
cool and fresh
advancing I await
reflect danger
your inability
flounders icy depths
drowning
shadow mist
escapes
your exit
halving
my fate
