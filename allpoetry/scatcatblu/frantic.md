i drive too fast
the music has to be loud
rushing from race to race
singeing myself inside and out

creating perpetual stress
running myself dry
empty of all respite
never catching up

no glance behind
silent screaming
feet in action
frozen smiles

stumbling inadequacy
never contemplating

pause

stop to think

miss one frantic beat

i'd die
