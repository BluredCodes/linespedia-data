title: "'bleeding silence'"
writer: "scatcatblu"

When darkness rules the day as night

and numbness creeps its way full flight

across my stoney face appears

nothing

and I touch everything with my silence



 hands in the air

 feet on the ground

 ok I'm ready

 you can lift me up now



when sleep evades and toys with me

and wakefulness is a weary dream

I continue by the least facade

empty

holding on only by my silence



 gift me something

 I can use

 make a difference

 save me soon



when balance hangs as far as stars

and sanity lingers stroking scars

hate and pain create thinning walls

crumbling

accompanied by my silence



 soft as silk

 gossamer wings

 left behind

 forgotten realms



when wishes dream lifes fantasy

when flying oceans call to me

when darkness wakes the light set free

when candles burn I bleed silently
