---
title: "'motion'"
writer: "scatcatblu"
slug: "motion"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/23365-motion-by-scatcatblu"
---

light
delight
pale moonlight
ray of sunlight
dawn

speak
to me
my mistake
stop to listen
still

dew
soft wet
morning breath
fresh and gentle
flame

warm
sunshine
penetrate
unforgiving
move

sun
respond
banish sleep
put in motion
life

wake
reset
hear my mind
concentration
night
