Candle light so pretty yellow bright
softening and warming, dancing and performing
translating shapes and shadows
elongating memories
moments that glow, flickering thoughts
mesmerising me
touching my dreams
tingeing them with buttery warm delight
whispering fragile phrases
seducing my eyes
lureing me beyond my own insight
tempting me, pre-empting me
to believe in shadows of fantasy and flight
fairies and moon beams
ancient power and prophecies of paradise
candle light, charming little friend
full of bright joy and delicate companionship
a vessel in the night
glancing off the darkness
travelling in the currents
carrying a cargo full of dreams and fantasies
sharing with me your erattic art
leading, following, accompanying my mind
guiding me towards your secrets
suggesting the wonders there to find
candle light brave glowing hope
resisting, staying true to your self
displaying the pleasure and fulfillment
pervading your lovely crazy style
daring the world to experience
or imagine just one moment to compare
with the brilliant energy and dancing joy
that exists in your radiant flare.yep I think I stared into the flame one too many times, got lost, and never quite found my way back again :)
