title: "'do you know'"

you're kind of strange you know how your eyes never smile and your lips never speak what your heart never feels and your voice hides the sound of the shattered pieces of your broken mind that never sleeps





do you even know what you see when you cast your eyes at your reflection and the numbness creeps over your whole being and you can't even look beyond the facade that is so hollow and lonely that you don't even see your own pain





do you know the screaming inside your head is so loud one day the dark silence that you live in may consume your inability to speak your own name in an attempt to remind yourself that you are nurturing the process that makes you insane





do you know you have bordered up the darkness inside with you when you fled the destruction of your being as a whole and now you are feeding it in the comfort of the only place you thought you could hide in alone



 

DO YOU KNOW
