title: "'snack'"
writer: "scatcatblu"

light hangs me for a fool
the colour where i die
salt encrusted repetition
savoury spoken lies
add myself collected
lifes insidious stare
dialogue detected
flashing buyer beware
changed myself midsentence
scripted false belief
degradating essence
ambiguous relief
dollop me unconscious
spoon me with your lies
inhabit my existence
taste my sweet demise
