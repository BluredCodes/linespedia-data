---
title: "'darkness in disguise'"
writer: "scatcatblu"
slug: "darkness-in-disguise"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/38086-darkness-in-disguise-by-scatcatblu"
---

do you dream me
when you close your eyes
or do you just imagine
conjure up a tragedy
your darkness in disguise
is that why I stumble
confused and out of place
caught between reality
and your unfamiliar face
you never speak
eyes focused far beyond
leaning towards you
the whispering is gone
do you know me
am I here at all
I feel insubstantial
your existence flawed
am I close to you
out of the corner of my eye
I glimpse sudden movement
like a shimmer in the sky
can you hear me
are these thoughts my own
is there understanding
together we are alone
can't you feel me
can't you recognise
the screaming inside of me
these pounding thoughts of mine
obviously you relegate
my existence to your mind
explain my mental anguish
with dreams and lapses of time
I feel you unaware
your love falls to the ground
you're made up of pieces
and my piece can't be found
 
dance with me
let us be one
enter my existence
kill me to become
take the blood of my soul
spilling from your mind
wash yourself in darkness
as reality unfolds in timeNov '99
