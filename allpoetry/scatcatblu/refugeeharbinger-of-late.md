i don't have your answers

i threw them all away



they singed my lips

and stained my hands



darkened my heart

with winged embrace



raped my intellect

with greed and haste



stole my thoughts

and spat in my face



without repent

they slunk away



i don't harbour your answers

i don't wield your fatei bet you think this one is about you - don't you?!
