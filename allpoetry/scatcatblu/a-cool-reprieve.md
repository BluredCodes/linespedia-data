~)(~

floating
how would that be
in the depths of the ocean
moon drowning in love with me
fish swimming
perpetual motion
in all directions
waves gently nudging
spilling froth
salty flakes of unshed tears
across my swollen face
gliding
unrestrained movement
not caring for warmth
or a cool reprieve
casting aside all doubt
letting go of fear
a release of memories
bubbling to the surface
dispersing silently
a gentle swaying
lulling to sleep
floating
unable to speak
dreaming the ultimate dream
answering the call
the tidal pull
setting myself free

~)(~
