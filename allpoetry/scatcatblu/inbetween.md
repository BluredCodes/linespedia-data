title: "'inbetween'"

~)(~



slander me via your progression

you pander to my lifeless form

what hope of reconciliation

what strength is there in sand

mend me with acceptance

carry me forward and beyond

cancel the debt of long ago

without absolution

tomorrow never comes

I guessed at your surmising

you never truly understood

I swept the demons bedridden

they grasp and scratch

the darkness attracts

they hold me frozen

in lifeless form

you don't see my ghosts

menacing stealing hope

I tried banishing from existence

the scars hold them strong

birthmarks of contention

I play my part insubstanial

life never seems to deliver

the goods are unresolved

delayed or on back order

often never received at all

I no longer voice opinion

opposition or reach for help

the unending cycle - is unending

no proffered help - actually helps

by day the ghosts surround me

at night demons lurk under my bed

and inbetween is you my companion

but you don't see to understand



~)(~
