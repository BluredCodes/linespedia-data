~*~

wrap me indelible silence
risk adventure game replays
happy ending withstanding
escape intricate detail
edit despair unending
shallow grave undug
conceptual display overtaken
riddled dilemma of love
bonded union unbroken
hearts strung knots unfurl
catching ends unravelled
patiently weaving anew
laugh this life misbegotten
tender hearts play words
forgotten loves true token
casting stones of hurt
wrap me arms wide open
risk eyes shine me lost
happy bond understanding
escape myself in your love

~*~love weaves and holds its own existence
