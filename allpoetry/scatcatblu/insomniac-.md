~O~

everything quickens
then slows to a beat
i know that im tired
but cant seem to sleep
pushing my body
to painful extremes
a lacking overabundance
physchosis in screams
the energy rating
has dimmed to a flicker
im combusting internally
in animated suspension
im starting to think
dark circles round my eyes
are kinda pretty
they go with my smile
i have to sleep i have to sleep
goes through my head
im a walking insomniac
the dreamless undead
the darkness follows me
a shadow unresolved
refusing to envelope me
and carry me home
im not sure where i stand
the decisions im making
in trancelike stupefaction
malingering sensations
clock ticks in sad misprint
plunging me edgewards
stealing inhibitions
stumbling
blithering
cant sleep
cant sleep
cant sleep
chanting
derision

~O~zzzzzzzzzzzzzzzzzz
