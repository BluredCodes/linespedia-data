~~
colour co-ordinate
marvel creations
hanging obscenities
aesthetic appeal
capture illusion
transported artistry
objectify lifestyle
framing ambivalence
location ideal
detail dimensions
surface arrangements
shelving practicality
glitter and gleam
cathartic symbolism
stop gap enhancements
picture perversion
decor for real
sample pot sarcasm
downsize dimensions
relocate spacially
extensive enhancements
creative ordeal
~~this is what happens to your head while renovating ggraarrrghgh :[
