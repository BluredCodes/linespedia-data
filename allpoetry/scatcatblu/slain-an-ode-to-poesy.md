title: "'slain (an ode to poesy)'"

I read you like a shadow
your elusiveness
excited my challenge
I slipped on your notoriety
pausing to lick the embers
wipe the ashes from my eyes
gift me a glance
so I can assume your reality
flinching at eye contact
scorched in ecstasy
I disintegrate
pleasured to extinction
smouldering with relief
discarded
left behind
an apertif
for the next challenger
in an army
of misbegotten whores
vying for your punishment
while undressing themselves~~ for those I read ~~
~~ offering me a slice of their own ~~
~~ beautiful cocoons ~~
~~ scorched by their own enigma ~~
~~ emerging ~~
~~ gargoyles solidifed ~~
~~ holding translucent butterflies within their souls ~~
~~ I dreamt of you all ~~
