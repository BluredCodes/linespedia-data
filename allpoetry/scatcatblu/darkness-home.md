---
title: "'darkness home'"
writer: "scatcatblu"

do you hate me
hate me
don't care for my reasoning
hell will care for you
demons think inside my brain
use me up
caress me insane
did I say that
insanity is the art
of being in touch
with your true self
scared
don't be scared
fear captivates your heart
intensifying those emotions
that bond your soul
to the dark
darkness
I glory in your style
delve into your velvet arms
I can't breath
panting with desire
touch me darkness
awaken the pain
punish me
for loving you
drive me hard again
love me
do you love me
do you want to possess
my soul
love is a shadow
of darkness
demons in control
touch me
no, don't be shy
innocence is a commodity
let me drown
within your naive smile
do you hunger for me
do you want
to taste my appetites
they are already owned
by darkness
and darkness has
many friends
the demons are restless
their hunger
is unresolved
I must feed my demons
I am compelled to give them
what they desire
the moon is my goddess
she speaks to me
in waves
she is a demon goddess
an altar
to the depraved
do you really hate me
or is pity on your mind
come
walk with me
I have of nothing
to be ashamed
come with me
let me take your hand
it's burning to touch
to join and caress
a darkenss soul
do you want to leave
go
there are many doors here
let go first
you must go alone
and I have no desire
to leave my demons
or foresake
my darkness
home
