I'm a kitten
watch me play
cute and vulnerable
stroke my fur all day

I'm a cat
see me pounce
playful and mischievous
you're my mouse

I'm a tiger
hear me roar
vicious and unpredictable
beware of natures' law

I'm a feline
fast and sleek
adaptable and devious
I only prefer to sleephee hee - don't wake me now :]
