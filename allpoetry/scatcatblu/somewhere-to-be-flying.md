Somewhere to be flying
lose myself in sleep
I'm going to fly
in the blue green sky
with the white foam
clouds rolling over me

Fly with the creatures
that live in this sky
make it my own
when I learn to dive
deep down into the sky
flying in the deep blue greenthis has many meanings for me and is possibly a little window to my inner being
the two sides of me conflicted (as usual) the deep desire for life and death eternally entwined
swimming in the ocean and flying in the sky - drowning and soaring *sigh*
can you tell I'm a pisces :)
