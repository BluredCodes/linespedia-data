Dragon Tears

Would you catch a Dragon's tear? 
or watch it fall to the ground? 
Would you scream and show him fear? 
or crouch without a sound? 
Would you see inside his mind? 
Would you dare to touch his soul? 
Would you show him thoughts of kindness? 
or would you run without control? 
I need to know the answers, 
to the questions that I ask you. 
Because behind this Dragon's tears, 
Are my eyes of baby blue.
