The Devil's Kiss

I swallow your love and honor 
as I take the things you need. 
Then I give you emptiness and hatred 
with a dash of pain and greed! 

I plucked the wings from Lucifer 
as he tumbled helpless from his grace 
I put that look of terror 
on your father's frozen face. 

You confuse me in your ignorance 
not knowing who I am... 
So, I will tell you... 
As the lion told the lamb. 

I am your altered ego 
and I hold you when your high 
just to watch you beat your soul 
as you slowly learn to fly.March 08 1999,
