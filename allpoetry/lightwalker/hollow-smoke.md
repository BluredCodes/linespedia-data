Can you see the face,
	the one that stares into your soul.
It peers from hollow smoke,
	And begs to take control

The horns of death are faded,
	and murky in their distain.
The fires rage on forever,      
	as people leap to end their pain 

 If you look a little closer 
	you will see the tattered forms.
As people fall unto their silence.
	to signal comming storms     

The skies will rest once more
	after a reign has cleansed the land
and the shores will be united
	as the people make a stand!
