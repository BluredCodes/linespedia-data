title: "Passing One's Shadow"

Look into the moon,

hold it's precious ring,

grasp the energy within,

listen to your heart sing!


Do all that you can,

the time, it won't be long.

one moment a straining seed,

the next, a tree that's strong.


The words I speak are true,

I lived each one in vain.

I flew above the eagle,

then dove to feel it's pain.


I thought that I was great,

I even swam the sea,

but the farther that I got,

The less I was free!Sorry bout the poem ...It was there before?
