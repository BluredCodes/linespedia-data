undevoted

pain
the serenity of quietness
impressions of dreams dwindle away

reflections of secrets yield
enchanting the minds darkest caves
venturing to the deepest of thought

emotions linger forever
filled with sadness

sculpted by there gazes
straining to get away
to a place no one knows

seeing forgiveness
almost begging not to let go....
slipping from there tenuous grip
i fall....

into nothingness, 
i guess this is where i belong
portraying the part of a dramatic lie

believing should be a sin
it only leads to thoughts that reveal the inner self
reflecting dark caverns of empty space

suffering consequences
slide through shaeless threads of truth
hiding from itself...alone
breathing nothing but untold secrets

silent voices
seeking the unknown

avoiding all objectives
keeping all realities caged
standing alone.....again
