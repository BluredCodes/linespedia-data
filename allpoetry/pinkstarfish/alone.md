alone

the overwhelming feeling of loneliness
seeks truth, which lies in the darkness of someplace unknown
forgetting the beauty of passion and love for life

care leaves all words behind
nothing is shared or said
unwillingness fills the world before me
whispers flow threw my ears
lies.....

inside....you...
revolve around lies
to hide the truth
for fear of optimistic eyes
waiting for the mortifying moment to lash out

the temptation of death
can conveice unreal thought of insanity
oblivious to the mind
impeccable to the outside world

whispers dragging on
in the mindless tunnels of the heart
ripping memories from there places
shattering any speck of happiness left
leaving dark...black....hatred thoughts

inner fear escapes threw the eyes
i wish i could just be away
i'm tired.....

always isolated
waiting....
wanting th empty moments to end
feeling alone.....
again....

closed eyes see the vastness of dark
imerging fears....
lies told to truth
believing
only
words from the mind
seen to be real but are not
