title: "The World's Fetish"
writer: "PrincessOblivious"

Have you ever heard the screaming

of so many broken hearts?



Have you ever seen a sea

made by so many salty tears?



Have you ever seen so many bodies

killed by disappearing self-worth?



Have you ever seen so many mourning

over the life they never had?



Here I sit,

on a curb,

wondering what can be real.



Here I sit,

on a curb,

wondering how people can keep a faith.



Here I sit,

on a cloud,

of so many other’s misery.



Here I sit,

in a daze,

remembering there is no God.



I see the eyes,

staring at me through miscolored glasses,

as though I’ve gone insane.



I see the fingers,

pointing at me,

as though I’m their every blame.



Then they hear the screams,

see the tears,

see the cuts and scars.



Now they know,

that I am made,

of what they are.
