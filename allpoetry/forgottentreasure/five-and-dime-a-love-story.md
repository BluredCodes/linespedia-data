at the five an dime i bought him a toy 
nothing special 
just a little note of my affections 
loving him is all i know how to do 
but then, how can you not? 
been there for me through all my pain 
making it all better with a simple kiss 
throughout my life, he was by my side 
played together as lively children 
sweet summer heat blessed the relationship 
cooling down by the lake side 
not a day goes by i do not see him 
he exists in me, as i am sure i do in him 
even if i go away 
he remains by my side in spirit 
i long to see him again 
so we may play once more 
at the five and dime today 
i bought him a toy 
nothing special.... 
only a dog bone and a new collar
