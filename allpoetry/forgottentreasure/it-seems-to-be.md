title: "It Seems to Be"

she sees the world through clouded eyes 
her genius lay hidden under years of dust collected on her soul 
inner bravery unseen, for self-loating overthrew happiness 
a cynical twist to the christmas tree-- 
--her image one of poison candy canes 
so sweet and inviting...yet dangerous to untrained heart 
she'll taint your blood with piercing words 
then perform a transfusion with gentle caress 
sew your stitches with mint flavored dental floss 
rip them out with rusty needles 
or so it appears to be 
her amazing voice serenades your very mind 
bringing forth unknown theories of human nature 
for her words can prove to be contagious... 
...more appealing than any book-taught lesson 
an inhuman wit courses continuously through her veins 
searching as a hawk would for any opportunity to descend 
victims cant decipher the incident-- 
--sarcasm or thought of purity? 
brought forth from past hatred and further past hurt 
an anger boils under the surface..pushed away just far enough 
as to hid the bitterly apparent tears flowing 
her soul bleeds with the need to scream...to  kill the demon within 
yet it is hard to drown the sorrow with happiness 
when an hour of laughter equals only a flicker of tears, anger 
a veil of mystic engulfs her every breath, each waking step 
draping her words with illusion, her actions with diversion 
she remains a lead tipped butterfly-- 
beautiful to the vision...harmful if not careful 
she'll taint your blood with piercing words 
then perform a transfusion with gentle caress 
sew your stitches with mint flavored dental floss 
rip them out with rusty needles 
or so it appears to be 
clouded entity flows eternally closer 
a wonderful companion...teaching me lessons each moment 
trusting those whose appearance may fool 
breathing with love for another, diverse soul 
who cries under the same moon, whose blood is red with the same oxygen 
exterior so cool, within warm as blazing fire 
an ice cube with a core temperature of 85 degrees 
she'll poison your soul with genius thoughts 
make you believe your life is true 
clean your wounds with deepest love 
set your heart aflame 
...or so it appears to mewritten for my best friend, Casey...she asked me to describe her in poetry form-this is what happened
