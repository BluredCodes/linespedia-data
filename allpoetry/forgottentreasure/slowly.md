each new dawn births darker fury 
as each lie cuts deeper 
your silky fingers lightly caress, 
sending flames through the tips 
burning my veins, smoldering my blood 
for your love is killing me 
toss another sultry glance my way... 
make me yearn for your skin 
your very presence a temptation, 
my muscles ache for your body 
yet your simple touch is poisonous 
i feel your thoughts like swords of rage, 
driving though my fragile soul... 
ripping me limb from limb, 
your smile ever standing 
melt me with your acid rain 
feed my angst with passionate promises 
as you run your fingers through my hair 
my heart collides head-on with reason, 
and my brain explodes with colossal confusion 
for your love, my love, 
is slowly killing me
