whisper of a wind
concealing all the hate
a drop of a bomb, 
shattered soul
hidden
-oh so gracefully-
by a southernly breeze...
droplet of thunder
exploding,
creating a sensual song
against the lonely tin roof
ecstasy shivers...
a moment has past,
moment's relapse
given birth to abuse...
father time and mother may i,
shielding their abuse
world's ungodly evil
-unspeakable disgrace-
hidden the truth,
so horrid
inexplicable...
a veil of lies
woven by grandmother tempation
protect innocence
preserve humanity
-scoffable-
cushion the ride,
play mercy to rolling boulders
crushing all the beauty
destroying all the purity
cliffside daydream,
washing away rusted edges
only to leap to an untimely death
escape?
close your eyes
eruptions of rainfall
drown sorrows,
cause spirits to rise
truly creating grace
while all seemingly crumbles,
gossiping winds envelope
smothering any indignities...
state of calm
state of peace...
pain, suffering
blown away 
lost forever..
