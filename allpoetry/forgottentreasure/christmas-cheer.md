christmas cheer
reflection of a brandnew you
shards of realism dance above the mist
creating within you perpetuity...
ill believe in your forever
listen to the tears,
forsee the sadness in your tremors
calm your weary heart..
if you could only listen
beating of a lost soul
rain pounding against a heavy shadow,
dark impressions for only blind eyes
deaf murmurs echoing through the catacysms
sending vibrations
horrible hangings of yesterday's sorrow
linger, placed halfheartedly throughout your unconscious
...ill soothe your bitter wounds
be a shoulder when all others cut you deeply
hold a hand to hide the glaring light
create a realm of purity
if you could only listen
step aside
-outside yourself?-
never cast aside
forget
regret
for nothing remains untouched,
my soul still shines in your wary presence...
love can never die, be extinguished
only morph to suite circumstance
forever can exist,
-though maybe not as you had planned-
friendship houses the kindest hand
if you could only listen
