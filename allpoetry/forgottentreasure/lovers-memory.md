remember the day
we declared our love
our souls coming close together
and we were higher than heaven? 

remember the evening
our lips first met
akward realization of a moment
and pure joy trembled in our eyes?

remember the first day we met
you called me beautiful
and curled up next to me
rested your head on my lap?

do you remeber these simple occurances
which set up the tone of our romance?
did you take notice
of the smile you brought to my lips
disappearing only to kiss you
destined to return again?

do you recall 
falling asleep
waking up to eachother
held night long in a tender embrace,
a sweet "good morning" kiss
to give pleasure to each new day?

your hand, your face, your lips, your skin
etched eternal in my mind
and each simple smile
wawrm, loving touch
means more to me than the existance of angels,
more to me than gold

i'll always remember 
the moments we've shared
my liberation from life's woes,
introduction to love's salvation

and all the glorous pit-stops
we've experienced along the road,yet another poem for school...an "i remember" poem...
