silent cries beget bleeding tears
your unspoken evil tears my soul
i crave your touch...sweet caress
under the pale moonlight, my dear,
my invisible valentine
dreaming of the destined day
our two worlds finally become one
as i scratch from within my cage
waiting for your rescue, my love,
my invisible valentine
able to taste your air...sweetest murder
my voice unable to call to darkness
hoping to end this horrid suffering
to end in your lovely protection, my only,
my invisible valentine
mind aches for i curse my thoughts
bringing agony to my forever mending heart
these explosions of passion destroy my will..
...my ability to love any other than you, 
my invisible valentineback when i hated Valentine's Day because i never had a Valentine..
