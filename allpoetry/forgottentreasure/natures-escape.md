a tortured soul needs a place to roam 
a retreat to live without the teardrops, 
to dance beneath the rain 
run today to the land of the gnomes 
hide your pain under the mask of the faeries 
sing away your blues 
the wind of the willows will lull you to sleep 
purifying your soul with each warm gust 
silver moon your window to heaven 
guiding you closer to the stars above 
where your happiness awaits 
love life once more 
learn to breathe with your heart, your soul 
let the perfume of the air swallow your sadness 
turn your tears into waterfalls, 
soothing wounds with their rhythmic song 
nature's magic can erase your pain 
relieve the torture in your life 
as you run to the land of the caterpillars 
drink from the pools...ever self cleansing 
and sing a song to the rising dawn, 
watching your fears float away with the southern wind
