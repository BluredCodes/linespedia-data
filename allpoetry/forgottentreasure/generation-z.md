close your minds
save your breath
for all you say wont matter
emotions stirred,
angst instilled
forget to say your thoughts
drop your views beside yourself
become generic like the rest
create a lie,
learn to live anew
eyes clouded,
vitality severed when you were silent
your soul an empty void
constructed by the television you so religiously watch
Jerry Springer your new found God
a forest only something Discover Channel can provide
for youre a product of the 90s
fatherless children, motherless nothings
youre happy to fall into line
lose your indentity, lose your mind
lose all and everything individuality can provide
so drink you Cola, watch your MTV
become a clone of society
for youre a product of the 90syet again, a stab at social commentary...written because of the ignorance and un-individuality of the people i see around me...
