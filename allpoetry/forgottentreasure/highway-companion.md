traveling along this desert highway
my mind drifts back to you
fond memories of love
unfaltering, pristine, true...
and soon my eyes begin to weep
unable to withold my emotion
no longer can i lie
for time could pass,
the road might dampen..dust
worn from weather
-yet my love for you could never-
our flame burns so strongly
brightly, a beacon in the night
for you i'll walk these lonely miles
surrounded by the fog
for your embrace ill conquer climate
love will move me swiftly along
along this beaten, barren path
my mind slowly wanders back to you
and i'm no longer alone
