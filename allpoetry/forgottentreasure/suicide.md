where have you gone 
i search aimlessly 
my heart thumps with the power of a thousand doves 
caramel candy rests on my bedside table 
remnant of our sweet beginnings 
sucked of all its magic 
i again have misplaced my life 
you weren't swallowed in the confusion 
it isn't possible 
i need you here alongside me 
you have left me before 
oh, how it hurt temporarily lacking a soul mate 
now you are gone once more 
and it kills being without you 
maybe you only ran away 
what they say cant be true 
i wont let it be so 
i talked to you just yesterday 
you seemed as though you loved yourself once more 
the hard times finally over 
then this 
what went wrong, hon? 
you are so brilliant 
cant you see how many love you and care? 
it isn't true 
i saw you YESTERDAY 
this isn't you in this self-induced slumber 
it isn't 
you killed me alongside you 
that gun pierced my soul before penetrating your skin 
how am i supposed to cope? 
you are all i ever had 
all i ever needed 
you are me, i am you 
it cant be true 
the police, they lied to me 
this picture of a blissful girl... 
the pills, the blood, it isn't real 
this doesn't happen to people you know 
people you love 
time has exorcised the demon in both of us 
only i am here to reflect upon it 
you ARE alive,babe 
i saw you only yesterday 
you were so happy, too 
this person being lowered into heaven is not my kindred spirit' 
i look, maybe it is me being introduced to the Ultimate 
a girl can hope 
yet somebody has to be in there 
because it isn't you 
it cant be you 
for i talked to you just yesterdaydont worry, this isnt based on a true story...:)
