killing  all  the  runaway  turtledoves
strangle  them  of  all  their  love
tell  me   not  to  lie
i  love  to  hear  your  laughter
hate  to  hear  you  cry
killing  all the  runaway  turtledoves
all  i  could  do  was  laughi actually dont remember writing this...so i cant explain what i was thinking...im not sure if i like it or not, even i cant make sense of it...
