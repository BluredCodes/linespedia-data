after such a while
the beaty of it all 
jumps out and attacks me
head on like a dagger in search of blood
pricked once or twice...
...the slow realization never set in
depression always masked the truth
now bitterly apparent
lucky to breathe, to smile at jokes
to have a roof, bleed when struck

how could i have let it go unseen?
blinded myself with a ribbon of lace
lovely lilac.. chocking my life
away from happy love
i know the truth of it all now
the wonderful poetry..sung by the wind
as the raindrops seduce my windowpane
and the sweet melody of the fan
keeps the heat from my heart
but not from my soul
this heat for life, 
lust for love
driving me closer to eternal bliss
my only nirvana...
...found on a sheet of blank paper
marked with some ink...
drawn from my soul


august 4, 2000
