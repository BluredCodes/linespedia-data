cloudy misfortune envelopes the moon
i stand alone beneath her murky eye
sedated
filled with glistening, beautiful poison
viens course, unable to extract...
cruise with me throughout the wild
make the leafy coverlet your bed...
this daze the purest ecstacy
tantric screams, electric quivers
caused...created
illustrious cloud greying her intensity
a film of romance to fill your heart
to engulf a soul, to lose a mind
alone, surrouned by life
totally consumed with hazy fire
as a gloomy shadow bleeds through the glare
to soothe the eye, caress the senses
i stand, hands outstretched, to greet the clouded moon
and lose myself in the bleeding romance
