lovely girl dancing down eerily calm avenue 
happily reminicing the fascinating days events 
astride her weather-worn horse 
a fear of nothing breezes through her soul 
as she swiftly passes a stranger, 
worn as badly as her noble horse 
noticing not the rapier he witheld so quietly 
waiting for a beauty just this grand to pass him by 
she fades wooingly into the crescent moon 
this foot dirty man creeps behind 
stopping only to straddle his fresh young horse 
as ebony as the forest hidden from the barely there moon 
the prince's daughter remains unaware while her ride is yet undisturbed 
her gliding mare softly evades any stones, any protrusions from the earth 
the path was a trove of demons, unseen in the darkest night 
her eyes a peircing grey...cooler than the thundrous wind 
they search surroundings for any intruder to her peace... 
...stopping on the stranger from long ago 
his rapier now gleaming under the fading moon, a twine string flowing to his side 
fear striking her soul so suddenly, frozen as her horse in headlights 
the dark soul of the barefoot demon shone stronger than his eyes 
being a deep green, so soothing and relaxed 
she tried to persuade her horse to turn around: "Delphi! Around!" 
yet she as well became entranced with the glow...the rapiers song 
as she jumped off her beloved, her gown became entangled...trapped 
the stranger advanced slower than prior thought possible 
girls heart racing faster each tormenting second 
grey eyes darting for an escape...any way to survive 
silent stranger reaches her horse....her gown...slices her free 
his rapier so sharp it seems to melt the fabric 
under the waning moon he ties her arms above her head 
as she desperately struggles against time, her eyes afixed upon his dead, cold stare 
her arms twisting vainly, trying to find freedom within these ropes of death 
the increasingly calm man agown in royal robes changes his pace 
throwing her shaking body onto his horse's body, he gathers his supplies 
her horse stands bewildered until deciding to run into the birthing dawn 
the night stranger rides quickly off into darkness, opposite the rising sun 
the girl's thoughts blur together, meld into one vision...fading into unconsciousness 
awakens she does in a new place, the warm mid-day sun tanning her nude body 
noticing the constant trickle flowing from her face, arms, stomach and pelvis 
hands now knotted to the wooden  beam behind her 
wrists bleeding where the rope digs into her soft flesh 
now beginning to think of the man from the prior night,  cold man in heavenly robes 
he returns again, his rapier replaced with a sword...his rope, a molding sack 
this daughter of the prince dies here, alone in the glowing sun 
the man of stangest evil calmly ends her suffering 
buries her under the coldness of a new night 
his rapier now replaced with a steel shovel, the scraping echoing into the darkness 
while the midnight moon watches in deadly silence,
