a peaceful embrace
blissful
eternity
forever
as a blade of grass emerges
months of bitter decay
your love releases me
soaring above clouds of reality
giving a glimmer
explosion
final burst of electric passion
your smile 
creates heaven
a moment
pure nothingness,
existance,
love
bound all together by your embrace
hold me
touch my heart through the night
make me..
again
completeness...
once more
for youve done it all
made me love
shown me the light
for ive seen it now
found it
love
i've found youa stab at beat...a simple love poem deep within
