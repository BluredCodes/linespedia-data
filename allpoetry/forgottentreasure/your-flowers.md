a bundle of flowers rests
here, crumpled in my lap
a few dandelions, a daisy or two
enough to fill my heart with pride
purest gesture brings a tear
complete with pussy-willow bouquet
my angel came soaring
into my life, carrying a smile
a pocket full of laughter
youre missing a few teeth,
knocked out by childish fun-
perhaps an apple?
hard to believe
such pure love can be delivered
in a package as dainty as you
my little sister forever,
a haloed angel into eternity
producing a new reason to live
each day you smile, a tear is born..
for i deeply love your flowersthis was inspired by the hard work my then 5 year old sister put into gathering me a bouquet of flowers from my backyard...she could only muster a few dandelions and daisies, but she spent hours searching for the PERFECT ones...it was so beautiful i had to pinpoint the memory

April 16, 2001
