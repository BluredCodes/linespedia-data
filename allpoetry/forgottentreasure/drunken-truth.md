please excuse the delerious ramblings
i've simply had too much to drink
my mind is a blur with liqour-laced lies
and my mouth has lost its remote control
i'm stuck on play, youre forced to listen
too tempted to leave, imagine what i might reveal!
your lover's hidden secrets?
dear friend's scandalous ways?
perhaps it is wisest, my dear friend,
for you to take your leave of me in this state
because i'm certain your ego-hungry ears
would not be eager to hear
the thoughts i have of youthis was an "apology poem" i had to write for school..i think its funny that all the apology poems i write end up being non-aplogy poems..oh well...
