all your rotten inbred minds
trying to feed me your poison
religious battles, death for God...
self destruction for the almighty above
murder is murder
whether for God or for..Bush
Bush is not God, nor would God approve of Bush
his slaying for his namesake
his death for science's pride
all his toxins, all his greed
not what God meant to create
youve destroyed your ideals by electing a criminal
given into final evil
forgotten how absolute love feels
youre a society of bloodthirsty villians
never caught for death is government approved
kill or...kill
no choice youve left
for empathy, morals
escaped from all reason, all perceivable logic
destroy the world for your God, your country
lose your soul to evil's burden
poison the minds of all your mindless fellow citizens
and your idiocy will be the death of allyes, i warned you...not patriotic in the slightest...if you were offended, i must repeat..i warned you
