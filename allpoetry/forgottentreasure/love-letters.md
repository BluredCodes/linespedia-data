There is only a persona held by myself which lies alone.  The day that I slay the dragon within is truly the day I wither upon the corpse my living self houses.  Knowing that I am truly not alone is my barrier to all evil brought by my lies.  Waking up each morning with you by my bare side brings the single smile to my quietly beautiful face.  Thank you for living the holy lie with me; I am in debt to your grace and power.  The self assurance you wiilingly posses throws me into a fit of lust and envy.  Cries of joy heard by my lonely ears are ever heard by the gothic crows awaiting my departure into their realm.  Being with you keeps me alive, tears of sorrow are nonexistent in your surroundings.  Each evening without you brings a thought of anger and fear into my barren soul.  Lighting my fire each day, the torch burns when you leave my side.   My crystal blue eyes realize the deep sincerity to your puriy.  I may be the only one to notice the love you hold for all living souls, I may not be.  All I can be sure of ,however, is that I am the luckiest person to peer into your heart.  
	Day by day my love grows stronger.  Eve by eve my terror diminishes.  You are my angel, the one who protects me each breath I take.  I cannot express in the words of thousands the depth of my passion for you.  Your simple caress sends a thrill down my slightly deformed spine. I do not know how to tell you all this, my way with words is not exceptional.  My writing is not as great as you honestly say it is, so I cannot pen a letter to be read by your amber eyes of innocence.  For years we have been by each others side, holding the other tight from the undying , relentless rain.  Seek me as I have found you, please realize the love I hold for your sexy mind and complex body. The day I cry for my fear is the only day I need you compassion.  I cry each day for you and the fears you house in your self.  The most important person in my life is the one in you.  
	Thank you again for being there for my teenage mind to spill to you.  My soul rests in your presence,or in the thought of your presence.  I love you for all eternity and live upon each breath you breathe.  Sweet dreams.

love, 
  your nichole
