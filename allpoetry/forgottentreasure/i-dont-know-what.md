The wound constantly deeper than before piercing my very soul....needing you touch me please.....make it better simple ways of living hurts to have you be gone nowhere to be seen..... the tears are rolling for the lack of i don't know what looking out my dirty mirror..seeing only myself clouded in a world of confusion behind the shadows; lurking in the corners.....deeper lost to my realization---- of i don't know what is there a simple life anymore? children laughing at pure humor...giddy to the bone? together as one entity again a river of briny liquid overtakes me engulfs me in her rage the want of a better existence give a life to take a soul needing to have a passionate kiss a freshly plucked rose holding the world together keeping my sanity in clear vision the broken window sewn by tender gestures glance over in my direction, lady luck restore my lost belief, please in i don't know what
