Oh Miss Graizy, you arent really lazy
as you sleekly speed down the lane
your speckled shawl, your southern drawl
i noticed as you were lifted with a crane

you may be quite fat, but as i sat
i see you deeper within
infinite wisdom inside your horned head
how else could you operate this clump of tin?

i later went home, where my mind began to roam
the earlier thoughts of you
even though you left me mystified
i couldnt help the urge, that night i ate beef stew...

Oh Dear Miss Graizy, your vision is now hazey
and as i quietly reflect,
i laugh aloud, for a large speckled cow
speeding route 6 is  not what you normally expect!!
