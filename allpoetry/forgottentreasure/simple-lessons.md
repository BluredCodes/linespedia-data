plant your feet firmly down
recite the lesson of the day
do re mi fa so la ti do
understand the hypocrite
lie to the man hidden in the rain
speak to the people of the sand
learn the ways of elves
play with the gentle fairies
take caution with those who dare not believe in miracles
become a legend in your life
save the mind of ONE soul
brighten a smile, decorate a lonely Christams tree
put up with the crying baby
grin and bear it
listen to your boss today
enjoy time spent with animals
become a child, play in the mud
sleep in a bed of roses
carefully remove the thorns
take your money, give it away
now children, what have we learned today?a time when i was desperate for innocence..things seemed to be lost and all i wanted was for people to realize the simple beauty in life

Nov. 30, 1999
