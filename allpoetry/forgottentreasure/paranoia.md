paranoia, paranoia
mistrust all you trust
hold the flame closer
protect yourself
demons of today, 
lusting over your shoulder
crawl away...
breathe no longer acidic oxygen
coursing around your head, your soul
tie the hands of lovers
bound the hearts of friends
youve pushed away all others
keep your secrets at bay
slowly the facade crumbles
youre left alone, naked
no warm hearts
open arms
spitefull glares greet your presence
broken potential bonds
destroyed caring souls
paranoia, paranoia
no one left to trustthis is an old prewrite...its about being paranoid in general..i hope it fits.
