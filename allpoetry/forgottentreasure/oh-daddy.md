Oh, Daddy

i was your little girl...
took me hunting, brought me fishing
all was crystal...beautiful

each drunken fist battered my soul
"Please! Leave Mommy alone!"
voice of reason...a three year old

youve tortured me..
hurting my fragile mind every twilight
why did you love the beer more than us?

guns and knives...sticks and stones
truly they hurt..AND break bones
learned that at five and a half

Oh, Daddy,
remember your personal moving day?
greatest budren lifted...serenity

you twisted my soul...killed me alive
suicidal little me...8 years old

i love you so much, daddy
and it hurts to want you deadi did not write this when i was 8; alas, i wrote this October 12, 2000...
