Im sorry
for all this pain ive caused
you have washed away
for all the lies i breathe
you have cast aside
for all the love ive given
you would not believe
so sorry that i loved you 
so sorry that i cared
but you would never want to hear
such simple, truthfull words
instead i gave you sorrow
bruised your shallow ego
tried to bring your eyes to open
...you were blinded by the light
i'll never try to bring a smile
brighten up your day
never again show you tender love
for ive always been pushed away
the tears that came when i left
all the pain i caused
could have been avoided
youd be left unharmed
Im sorry that i loved you
Im sorry that i cared
but your actions caused your sorrow
your ignorance caused your despair
