**written for my dear friend who lives on the land, drifting from house to house, bed to bed, never a stable home...all my friends hate his ability to charm his way into our wallets and our of our homes, but we love him...that is why i chose "love"...**

alone again
this tumbleweed of society
floating aimlessly
his next meal, warm bed
drifter, loner, social butterfly
lover of all things beautiful
yearner of all things pure
brighten your day
his sweet smile so charismatic
personality so temtping
falling in love
-a neccesary move?-
believing his lies
longing for his freedom
scared to leave security
follow this whimsicle image
he'll bring you through his ferris wheel
toss your emotions, a hurricane's force
you'll love every second
wish for more
you'll love to hate 
this tumbleweed of society
