SIDE ONE::

a child's love
sweet and innocent
a flower bud
unfolding, a glimmer of hope
bathing in your glory
a sunbath
your innocent love


SIDE TWO::

falling down, cannot breathe
dying
poisoned
slain by the dagger
your lovethese are actually two different poems written two different days...these are my feeble attempts at beat poetry..ive been reading Ginsberg and Kerouac and Burroughs lately..and i love the style of the beat generation..i hope im doing well..
