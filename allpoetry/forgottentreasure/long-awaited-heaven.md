bleeding on the inside, calm facade to all melting within you,  rock hard love guide me to that sacred heaven, the holiest ground on which you tread each and every thought you dream brings us closer to light -quivering, losing control porcelain shattered, sudden short of salvation carry me with you, take again my hand.... ...you've conquered my heart once more enslave my body into your arms, lead  me toward that mystical beyond the celestial light which shimmers each moment your eyes meet mine -crying again, wishing for your touch smooth the wrinkles of age-worn battles wasted away before my calling, disintegrating shell kindergarten paste could heal all wounds pasted me together time and again then blown afar by winters taunting wind steal me away from a horrid present-past remove me one final instance tremors of love birthed by  my soul longing to be saved...ever present evil anticipating your luscious  kiss, your holy hands sliding me toward  eternity, one step closer to god an orgasmic glance, a climatic gesture, leading my soul nearer yours... ...long awaited heaven
