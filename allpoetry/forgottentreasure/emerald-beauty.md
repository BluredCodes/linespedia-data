title: "Emerald Beauty"

your name consumes me with a fire
so passionate i cry
you are where i want to be...
... within your angelic gaze
each passing second tears away my flesh
exposing the lonely girl inside,
wishing she were with you
my soul has been captured in your spiderweb
willing to cast life towards deaths door...
....for a second of your contentment
i wish to fall into your memory
leave a tattoo for all to see...
...you to forever cherish
each passing journey brings my heart to you,
wrapped in lilac cellophane for aesthetic pleasure
nightfall reminds me of your amazing beauty,
for you shine a brighter light than any midnight star
eternally i live within your crystal web,
wishing to again be consumed...
...wrapped once more in your emerald cloak
your amber eyes glow when i cry,
when i bleed your soothing hands caress,
eliminating all the pain...
i love you,
i am forever in your debt...
for you have given me life
