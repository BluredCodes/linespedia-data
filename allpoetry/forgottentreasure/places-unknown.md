dream with me
away we'll fly
over mountains
ill kiss your lips
cover you with golden rays
heat of forever set you ablaze
for i am yours to hold
take me
this place we'll leave behind
hit the road
souls to the pavement
travel along dusty highways
make love at all-night diners
wine ourselves to bed each night
live of the earth
bare-backed,
anew to the sun
where ill be waiting
for you to awaken
and kiss your lips
in places unknownjust a little love poem..forgot what it was like to be in love...:)
