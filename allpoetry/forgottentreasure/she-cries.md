her heart beats silently, pounding through her chest
as she lay forced down upon the damp leaves
quivering below his powerful muscles
she cries
for her life she begs this demon
set her soul free again,
allow her to live purely
without the burden of this memory
yet he pushes only harder,
thrusting his hips up into her
and they toss and turn as lovers would
he wants her body, he delicately whispers,
this wont hurt at all...
tries to soother her furrowed brow
while this hellish imp penetrates her secrets
she cries
pleads again for her afterlife
(for her life has been ruined)
screams to beast, begging for emancipation
yet her voice is drowning in her tears
he cannot understand her sorrow..
nor would he care to listen
the battle however is soon ended,
for he fills her soul with his dirty seed
and caresses her sweaty hair,
as a doctor
"that wasnt so bad"
upon untying her hands he leaves
this innocent woman shaking alone,
her body blood from her fight,
soul unpure from the Devil's encounter...
still feels his horror all over her
can taste his infected air,
and alone she trembles in his aftermath
a discarded toy from years before...
closing her eyes to the ugly horror
only to see his face...
she criesJune 11, 2001
