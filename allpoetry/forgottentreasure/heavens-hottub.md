title: "Heaven's HotTub"

laying besides you, 
the heat of our hearts melting the ground below us 
creating a private bit of heaven, 
designed for only two... 
your gentle embrace carries my soul to the moon, 
steals away my fear... 
long after my eyes leave yours, 
i can still envision your energy, feel your taste... 
the sweetest candy exists within you 
and as an addict i crave my next fix, 
anticipate your caramel intoxication, 
long for your peppermint purification... 
when your eyes meet mine i soar away, 
lost in a realm of love, a world nine clouds above reality 
until your soothing touch pulls me down again... 
complete me once more with your beauty, 
fulfill wishes with each passive glance, 
pour your soul into mine...i'll give you my everything... 
traces of your hand remain on my body, 
providing a memory of your wonder, another reason to cry... 
my love for you sets all logic aflame, 
diminishing any remnants of coherent thought 
for my mind cannot function, will not obey... 
within your arms security arrives, 
protected from the evils of hate, the demons of the earth 
your powerful aura, crippling love 
keeps my life eternally safe, totally unharmed... 
when i leave you the tears do flow, 
a river of agonized separation pours into my ocean of love 
forever feeding this flame, energizing the longing, 
this ever growing craving for you... 
complete me once more with your beauty, 
fulfill wishes with each passive glance, 
pour your soul into mine...i'll give you my everything... 
laying besides you, 
watching our secret space in heaven, 
my heart glows...my soul bleeds.... 
as you hold me forever into the night
