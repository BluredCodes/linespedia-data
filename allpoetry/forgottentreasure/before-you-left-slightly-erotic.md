home created ecstacy
i forever long for your far away touch
when will you return to me?
i envision you and i together
before you went away
your gentle hand caressing my body
trembling with anitcipation
your mouth exploring every curve
our hearts beating faster every second
i turned to you, it was my turn now
i know your body's map by heart
slowly i researched this guideline
my mind unable to think
our lips together form a seal
so wholesome and so secretive
you told me i had the perfect body
my beauty reamins unsurpassable
as you agian soothed my soul
stroking my summer tanned skin
i wanted you so badly then
your body should have been mine
i realize now, with you gone
while i am wanting you more and more
that perhaps i did the right thing
physical pleasure means nothing
if emotionally, emptiness exists
when you return,
i'll still be wondering
wanting and waiting
and always remembering
that steamy summer night
the night before you went awaythis was written about the last night with my boyfriend before he lost any emotional attachment to me..
