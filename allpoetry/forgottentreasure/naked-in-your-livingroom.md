cotton underwear dangle above
slightly out of reach
my frostbitten thighs long for consolation
gripping to each warm, tender kiss
...another monday passes calmly...
roses longer than empire state
soft to the touch...silken lips
melt upon contact--icicle grip
push-up bra disintegrates
as my mind fades to summer thoughts
...attach my fins so i may swim away...
world withough neccessities
internal combustion soothes the pain
turns grey skies blue--a crimson clover
denim jeans fly away
floating from my slippery grip
rug-burnt knees tremble in the cold
turn to thoughts of religious fury
sacreligous escapade...
form-fitting shirt explosion
tempting my faith with beautiful visions
my numb mid-rift crystalized
thawing under the taste of a lowe lip
dull pain insists to persist
ripping the words from my hands
shoot me in the temple...
...yesterday, so i can evade the blow
flannel knee socks scamper away
hide under my lace-y bed
each bitter toe craves attention
escape to heaven--escape to summer
warmer nights, bluer skies
being held by the one you love
as my naked body absorbs the heat
under the harvest moon of may
awaiting the simple caress--
thaw me from the inside
create an internal ecstacy
so i may remain together..intact...
..completed by you..
in the sweet summer bliss?,
