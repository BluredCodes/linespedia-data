title: "Do I..."
writer: "HypurGurl"

Do I truly love you,
you asked me just today
I put it into these words, 
and it came out this way
To me our like an angel,
sent from above
You'll never be lonely ever again, 
you'll always have my eternal love
I will dry your tear drops,
whenever they may fall
I'll be there by your side, 
whenever you should call
I will be your lighthouse, 
that guides you through the storm
When the cold wind blows, 
I'll be there to keep you warm
When the rain has stopped, 
I don't have to look at the sky
I can find my rainbow, 
when I look into your eyes
I'd give you the world, 
if it were mine to give
I'd even give you my life, 
so that you may live
Yes I truly love you, 
but if you should ever doubt my love is true
Just close your eyes and remember, 
these words I've said to you
