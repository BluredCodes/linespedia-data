Why did you have to leave me
 Why did you have to go
I lay my head on my pillow
 My tears begin to flow

You told me that you loved me
 I believed that it was true

But then you went and left me

Is it cause theres someone new?

   You really mad me sad 
when you left me that one day

 It really is confusing 
 when you have nothing
       to say

So please tell me now
 and tell me true
Is this the end of me and you

By Larissa (BitchyHypurGurl)
