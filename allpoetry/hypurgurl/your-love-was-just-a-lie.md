Can I say goodbye how do you expect me to let go
Something that was so important to me just decides to go
What am I expected to say when your just standing there 
This all seems so wrong, it just doesn't seem fair
My one true love is walking away 
And there is nothing I can do to make him stay
Can't you see we are meant to be 
Can't you see your one true love is me?
We always said we would be together
For always and forever
But forever wasn't true, or you would leave me never
so turn and leave before u see me cry
and eventually ill make myself believe this love was just a lie.

Bye
Larissa

Bitchy
