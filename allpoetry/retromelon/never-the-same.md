Coherent thoughts:
First thought:
Whats going on?
Second thought :Oh shit is this really happening?
Third thought:
Dear God what now?
In our generation
MINE
Terrosists attack
True evils face, oh so nefarious
Clearly marked in the smoke
The causlties still
So very still on the rise
The United States of America's symbols
of ecnomic power and domination
gone,vansished,collasped,
of military power
Also a part collasped
Hi-jacked planes
Act as bombs
Loaded with fuel
From east bound west
3 planes
smoke billowing
Things crumbling
Papers flying though what seems to be a ghostly town with appartions of what happend
Want to:
Run
Scream
Cry
Die
In my enerity of existence on this planet
nothing so horrible
I was so very sure
war was something
war was yeah, something 50,60 years in the past
Atleast for America
Such as like when 
our territory
Pearl Harbor
Was attacked purposely by the empire of Japan
Rocketing the U.S into world war 11(2)
But don't you see
This is far worse
Cilvians, our civilians,3 locations loss
Unlike Pearl Harbor, whom we knew was surely attacked
by the empire of Japan
We are still hunting suspects
Visions burn in my mind, body and soul
Watching it uncome live
The noise , deafining, over audible, loud
Before media edits
 People in utter shock, Running for their lifes, seeing the firefighters disspear in the smoke
Never to be seen again
Of people free falling to their already inevtible deaths
To avoid, unspeakble pain from where they were trapped
100 or so stories up
of the towers collasping
People along with glass, steel, falling like snow
Ashy, choking,demonically evil human snow
People mutulated, children oprhaned, people widowed
clothing, shoes, scattred
That golden childlike untouchlbe innocene lost
Yesteredays monstreous problems oh so insignifant now
Helpless
The words run through and through your mind
Oh god is this truly real
Seems like a cheezy movie to me
No defintely not reality, nightmare, nightmare, nightmare...
I close my eyes so tight it burns this outragouesly horrible burn
Leaving my head down on American Goverment work never to be finished
Slowly opening, looking up
Its still thre, don't cry,don't cry, don't cry
Then its the media fooling us yeah
We have extraorindany speical effects
But why? 
It is real
Blink back images, blink back horrendous imagees
We are in war
Maybe World War 111(3) and nothing
will ever be the sameA/N this was wrote on like September 14, 2001 so take into account I had no idea what was to come...
