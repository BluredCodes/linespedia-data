Always



   I always knew
Far before I met you
You were the one for me
You make me sick
Angry and mad
You annoy me and make me cry
But then you make me feel safe 
In the wretched embrace that brings our prophesized doomed fate
Who knew to love so true
It was for fairytales for mindless children
Not
For us 
The two who can’t make it
We have something real
Everything is so confusing
We both have been through so much separately before we met each other
More when we did
And even more to add when we split
Don’t think this means I don’t love
I think I always will
Like I said I always knew
Our love is true 
You know how I know
It hurts
It make me want to scream
It makes my blood boil and cry out
But at the end of the day it is always the same
I know you always loved me
It wasn’t ever I thing  I had to doubt
True love isn’t all sunshine and bunnies and it doesn’t make things wrong right
There is a hole in my heart that you took away
For the better of me
And for the better good
My soul reaches for yours
You are its mate
This is how I always know
So when you asked me all those years ago
When we were going threw a hard time if I was still your girl
I said always
And to this day
Through you said to move on and I believe me I have
It is forever and always
