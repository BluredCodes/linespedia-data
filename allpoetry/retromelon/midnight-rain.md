Its a eerieish calm
Black sparkled
with little whte orbs of gas
A moon, full and orange playing shadows
Around my hollow form
Not in size but more inside
I move easily
Steathlily through the night
As I am accustomed to
I feel safe
cloaked in darkness
Free to be me
The smell is fresh 
And clean
it lets me think
why was fate so mean
I try to divert my thought
to something more
It begins to rain
Splashing like the tears my anguished soul cries
God I love rain
I feel safe even enough to cry
In the dark
In the rain
I can't just be vain
The water drips from my air
It seems to appear the rain stopped, mid-aire
I wipe excess water from my face
and head'home'
No one knows
or should
Of my sorrow
In this midnight rain
