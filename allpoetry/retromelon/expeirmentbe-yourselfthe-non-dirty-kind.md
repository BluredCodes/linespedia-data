Why does everyone try so hard
To be what everyone wants them to be
And never once find themselves
Or when they say be diffrent
So you change your personality
Is anyone what they seem
Even if you have the ablitly
To flaunt your true colors
There is always hidden depth
Something no one should know
Those voices that echo in your head
Everyone has loads of secrets
Never to be shared
So why can't we be ourselves
Not diffrent, not cool
Not popular, but ourselves
Who gives a flipping damn what anyone thinks
It will lessen the burden on your heart
Its a relief
You can focus on more things
If you don't stop to think oh, what do they think of me
Why must you all strive to be friends with people
Putting out a fake personatilty
To people who don't like you for you in the first place
Makes you wonder does anyone ever fall in love
Or just with the masquerade the other is holding
I hope there s soulmates
the ones who see you
Cause you can't know love
If  there is nothing real to love
If you thinks something is awesome
But oh that person doesnt...
Screw it and do what you want to
I am not saying be reckless, and break rules, or have sex
You may have some morals such as I
I am starting to sound like a hipprocritcal bitch 
But be yourself, and live to your morals
DOn't waste most of your life , acting 
Unless of course you enjoy it , do it on stage, or infront
Of a camera
But not in front of the so called family and friends
who sya they love you
Even for a expeirement
One day
one time
be yourself, and whomever 
Sticks by you, is your true friends, love and what
You can call family
