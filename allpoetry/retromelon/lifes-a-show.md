Life's A Show
We all are handed a role by God
We do mostly out of improv
Some of us get kicked off
In death, sucide, diseases
Plagues,war murder which I am to famliar with
The list always go on, our favortite perfect characters 
in our lifes dropped like a fly
Life's A Show you don't get to rehearse
No time to prepare
Cause the script is already wrote
The big moments all laid out for you
Some little and subtle
Some aren't, but you just go along with it
In your improvastion
Its what you do afterwards that counts 
Anyways right?
Thats what makes the character you
It what gives us purpose fullfilling,
But we already are cast
Our friends, family and battles
We can't stop, only the selfish alterntives of
Self-infliction of death
Theres heavan
Limbo
And hell
Guess where we are...
Limbo
We play are roles right
We get our second chances in the 
Form of reincarnotion
Then our final desitnation comes
Then we either go to heavan or hell
Or of course we have apolyspes
The End Of Days will come
But we won't live that long
Cause in our script...
Humanity is what kills off humanity
But since lifes a show
We all do play our parts
But maybe
If it comes out wrong
Play it like a musical
Sing a happy song
Pray that it works
Lifes a show
Hope your friend doesn't die
In any form
Lifes a show
You have no f* clue
But were are stuck in Limbo
And every choice we make
Leads to the ending scene
I am here
I am not going to leave
God gave me a role to play
And when I die 
I want to die
No living longer for me
No being scared
Its weird
In my reality
I am just really not scared of dieng it self
I know its already written
I am just more scared how it is going to happen
But I remember Lifes A Show
And I have been given a role
I will play it right
Show my true self, and not mock what others do
I don't try to be diffrent
Or alike
Cause I have been given a role
And every verse I sing
Can make it that much worse
I am me thats what I have been chosen to be
One day
I will never sing
Give me something to sing about
Because I refuse to have to cover what I am
Go on in being yourself, take what the writers throw at you
The death, the pain, the loss
It will be over one day
Its what you choose to do after every single big moment that counts
Because if you give up your choice is made
No more limbo
No heavan
Cause you have to remember
Lifes but a show
Life is just this
Wounds can only heal
By living
If you go on
One day it'll stop
And you realized I was right
that lifes just a show
