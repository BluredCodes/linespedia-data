Am I what you want me to be
Doesn't matter if you tell
Cause I'll never try, I'll never be anything but me
We have huge authority figures, and they tend to be
Very narrowminded, racist, discrimnative, sexist
Ectetra,ectetra,ecectra
To them there is black and white
Good and Evil
As told to them by generations before
And tell the new generations again
It leads you to
Hating this world
Cause we can't break the vicious chain
Everyone seems to think everything is one-dimensional
That between the black and white, not the shade of gray
Look at him
He is diffrent, very sardonic
He stares at you the wrong way and you hit him
Given no real reason
Look at her she's a freak
She's believes in witchcraft
And has her own ideas, that vampires walk the earth
Hey guess what, doesnt mean she is one
Every human and non to think of it
Have feelings beleive it or not
Look at me
I am usally hated or liked immeditaly
I come up with my own theorys through ancestors, the bible
everything I know
I am hooked on Anne Rice, Edgar Allen Poe and watch a show
About a vampire slayer and a vampire with a soul
That could be the only reason you think I beleive
In what I do, 
You look at me and the sivler necklace that rests on my 
Non-existant breasts
And simple pentacle
Its not inverted, it doesnt believe satanBut you see good and evil
Black and white
and figure I am on the wrong side
Why don't people open there damn eyes
What do you know about the long funky colored brown haired, green eyed
Scrawny girl
Or the quiet so called freak
Open your eyes and don't judge someone by there looks
No one is good
No one is evil
No is one-dimensinal except for maybe the devil
No one is white
No one is black but fall in array of colors
From pigment to exxegrant souls
Take off your damn glasses and look at true colors
Yeah she may be a bitch
But most likely there is a reason
Yeah look at him, he's a prick
But  you don't really know him
Shades of gray
We got to break
Shades of gray
Have always stayed
If everyone could see outdie of he box
even just for a moment
Our world would benifit
We are just leading ourselves lower and lower
Sending men into a battle they'll never win centuries ago
And centuries to come
Teaching our kids these horrible things when the future lays in
their(our) hands
I am here to tell you
I am not black and I am not white
I am not perfectly good and sunshine
But not evil coated in darkness
Probally not even fitting in the one of the shades of gray
I try to describe
But I am me, and thats how it will always be
Don't label, we all have something in common
The day to day battle to make it through
to try and understand why we are heare
Next time you are to judge, shes a freak
Satanic, he's stupid, she is a nerd, he's a prep
Get to know them, understand why they are the way they are
We all have a right to breathe the same air(or not breathe)
this air to walk the earth
Dont try to tell me why I wrote this
just try to apply it to yourself
Cause do you know what you are missing?
Spend your life seeing black and white
When the world is a interesting place
If you dont ignore the shades of gray
