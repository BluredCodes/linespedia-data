The End
 Don’t you see
I do love you
I sit seconds from my death
Where in the hell are you
I gave you everything
My Life
My love, innocence
My blood
I sent you to hell so the world can go round
And yet you left
I have to go now
I have to sacerfice my self
So a loved one will go on
I look around
I can always sense you
 screw the distance
You put between us
But even that faint tingle is gone
Are you dead 
Will you be on the other side
I jump feeling the portal crushing every bone in my body
I grit my teeth as my body shakes violently 20 feet above the air
Blood rolls in a flood over my tounge
Its going to end soon I can tell 
Finally the portal stops
And all that is left is a tiny bit of strength as I see the ground rush from under me
I am gone before my body makes a thud on the rubble
I hear all my deceased beckoning and look at my bloody body
All my friends and family gather around, some shocked
Some unconscious, since it was one shitload of a battle
And some just fall down to the ground as grief overtakes them
But they are safe thank the lord I am about to meet
I feel the peace and warmth wating for me
And I know I am never going to see you again my love
Just no as if my death has nothing to do with you
You could of spent my last moments with me
I forgave you for everything you did a long time ago
I love you
But now I must go
