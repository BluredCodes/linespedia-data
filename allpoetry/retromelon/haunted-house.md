They say
True love
Is your blissful escape
from all bad
as safe haven
bt for us
its a haunted house
I walk through
the hallways 
of your murky soul
totured , tainted, not with gold
Overwhelmed by the bitter scent of guilt
And then the truest love
that flows strictly and un-clotted
For me
I can't find the safe light
Here
My eyes
Like two hazel orbed flash lights
can't seem to light it up
In the gentle heated embrace of you lover
they say its home
safe, and warm, full of sunshine and romantic rain
it couldnt be no more dangerous, a deep dark horrible storm
Raging inside you 
For us
While I want to escape
I can't stop
Entering my haunted house
Thats created for
Our two souls entwined
And we can't just to be together
give into our desire
the only true I could ever expeirence
All the while the haunted house won't go away
No natural diaster can wipe it off the earth
not now
not ever
because it
and you
will always be connected to me
why oh why is fate
so damn nasty and cruel
not to let for once, a rarity even, two soulmates
sharee a house of love
instead of a cold haunted house
Haunted by apparitions of us holding hands
kissing,making love just that once, showing our enternal love
That we ourselves are now forever
restricted to act upon
they say loee
is your blissful escape
for us its a prison
we we be enternally locked in
you and me in forever
