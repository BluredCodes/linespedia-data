---
title: "man of my dreams"
writer: "Still Sweetpea"
slug: "man-of-my-dreams"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/14560-man-of-my-dreams-by-Still-Sweetpea"
---

sitting here with nothing to do
my thoughts wonder and immediately they find you
i truly love you,which comes as no suprise...
i could go on forever,but this i'll summarise...
in just a few short weeks
you have completely swept me off my feet
i've never met anyone quiet like you
you amaze me with the things you do
you're sweet,loving,and kind
you have an amazing body,as well as a strong mind
i love that you always see things differently
you see whats there,not just what they want you to see
your confidence i envy
always so sure of yourself,i wish i could be
i feel i could learn so much from you
if you'll take a minute to explain a thing or two
right now i have nothing to offer you
my word will just have to do
one day we will be together
and stay that way forever
you are just perfect,or so it seems
you are the man of my dreams
