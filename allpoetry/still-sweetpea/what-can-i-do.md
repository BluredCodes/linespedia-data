---
title: "What Can I do"
writer: "Still Sweetpea"
slug: "what-can-i-do"
source: "AllPoetry"

Now all of a sudden
I dont exist to you
You have no idea what
you're putting me through
Everytime I see you with her
rage overcomes me
I have to wonder....
How could this be

You'd said you'd love me forever
and I thought it was true
Now you've taken 'forever' from me
What can I do

Have you forgotton the times we've shared,
the love we made
or did you ever care
these are the thoughts I try to evade

It was supposed to be just us
me and you
Now you've taken 'us' from me
What can I do
