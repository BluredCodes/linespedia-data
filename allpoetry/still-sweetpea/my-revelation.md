Lost in love or drown in lust 
these strange feelings I dont trust 
I cant figure out what keeps drawing me to you, 
do I stay,go, what should I do 
Knowing your real intentions would be of much help.... 
If only I knew how your truely felt 
My feelings are strong and clear 
losing you is my biggest fear 
I love and miss you so much 
I mess the secutity in your touch, I love the intensity of your eyes 
I cant stand yet to say goodbye 
I'll hold on to you for as long as I can 
always wanting you to be my man 
All these feelings are strange and new to me,never felt before 
Im not sure I can do this anymore 
Loving you is the hardest thing Ive ever done 
I had really thought you were the one 
I thought you would please me,mind,body and soul 
now I realize I have to let you go...
But you were never really mine you see,
but perhaps one day you can be....
