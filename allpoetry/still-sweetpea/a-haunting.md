---
title: "a haunting"
writer: "Still Sweetpea"
slug: "a-haunting"
source: "AllPoetry"

the ghost of our love still haunts my heart
which is not completely torn apart
your memory lingers in my mind
i'd hoped that would pass in time
but no,im convinced its here to stay
threatening my sanity more each day
i cant make it stop,though i really try
i cant stand to suffer this pain,i'd almost rather die
no existance is better than a loveless one like this
you cant contradict me,you know it is
love is supposed to make you happy,not cause pain
you have everything to lose,nothing to gain
I'm placing my heart under lock and key,shutting everyone out
until i can figure what this whole love thing is really about
