im tired of the bullshit
im sick of the drama
damnit im so tired
of you fucking with my mama!

why cant you leave her alone
and stay way...
its okay though
ill have my day

i wanna see you suffer
i wanna see you cry
i would almost like
to see you DIE!

ive never hated with such passion
you literally make me sick
i would love the pleasure of
seeing you get your ass KICKED!this would be my mamas exboyfriend who will not leave her alone
