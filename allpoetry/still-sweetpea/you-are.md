---
title: "You Are"
writer: "Still Sweetpea"
slug: "you-are"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/14295-You-Are-by-Still-Sweetpea"
---

you are my rain 
during a drought
my cheer
when i pout

you’re my security 
when im scared
my resistance 
when im dared

you are my eyes
when i cant see
my reality 
when it just cant be

you’re my air 
when i cant breathe
my light 
when its too dark to see

you are my comfort
when im feeling sad
my joy 
when im feeling bad

you’re my voice
when i cant talk
you are my balance
when i cant walk

you are my music
when i want to sing
you are my life
you are my everything
