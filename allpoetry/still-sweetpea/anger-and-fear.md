---
title: "Anger and Fear"
writer: "Still Sweetpea"
slug: "anger-and-fear"

My mind swirls with Anger and Fear
stay back,please, don't come near
for they are entwined, and are a storm of madness
and if embraced cause nothing but sadness

The wrath of Anger is unbearable
of escaping it,no one is capable
It feeds on your number one weakness
leaving you paralyzed and speechless

The claw of fear grabs and constricts
there's no comparison to the pain it inflicts
It'll drive you crazy,
even steal your sanity
