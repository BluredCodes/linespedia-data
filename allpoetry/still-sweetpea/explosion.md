I keep my feelings bottle up
never spoken
My heart shattered
and broken

Loneliness now my unwanted friend
it's time for this madness
to come to an end
I keep it quiet so no one knows
It's too much now
Im about to explode:

I cant take it anymore 
the shit's about to hit the fan
The bitch knows better
than to fuck with my man
I've tried to be mature about this
and just let it go
But I just cant stand it
Im'ma whoop that hoe!this was when my boyfriend and cousin were messing around
