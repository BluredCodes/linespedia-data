title: "the way it has to be"
writer: "Still Sweetpea"

you know what he's trying to do
he's being nice to me,only to get to you
but i cant let him back in
he'll only hurt me again
i hope you see
This Is The way It Has To Be
i feel im constantly standing guard for both you and me
its a shame This Is The Way It Has To Be
you are all i have and i wont let him take you from me
i'll do whatever it takes to make sure you love safely
i'm not trying to tell you what to do,its not my place
but is this what you want to always have to face
ya'll fight day after day
but it doesnt have to be this way
take this as you will
just remember you're not the only one who has to deal
i want nothing more than for you to be happy
and thats The Way It Has To Be
