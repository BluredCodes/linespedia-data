---
title: "I Think You're the One"
writer: "Still Sweetpea"

You say you love me and I want to believe
my doubts only you can relieve

For real love my heart yearns
I want to feel the eternal burn

I want so badly to be in love
you know the kind, heaven-sent from up above

I want something  as simple as your kiss
to put me in total bliss

I want to go wild from just your touch
Im starting to wonder if there is a love of this such

Im told,slow down, just wait, your prince charming will apprear
but will I know it when he gets here

I think you're the one who can give me what my heart desires
I think you're the one who can set my hear afire

I think we should give our love a try
and if we're not happy;we'll part and say goodbye
