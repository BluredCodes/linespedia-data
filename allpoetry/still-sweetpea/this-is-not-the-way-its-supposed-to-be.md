My last thought before I go to sleep
My first when I wake
Im so unsure
I hope my feelings arent fake
I think about you all the time
I dream of when you'll be mine
You seem to have me under your spell
Being without you has made my life hell
I dont know what to do
I dont know what it is about you
I cant get you out of my mind
My heart is stuck in this bind
Why cant I make you love only me
This is not the way its supposed to be

She seems to be here to stay
always threatening to take you away
I wonder if she loves you the way I do
Why is it you have to love her too
I know you are the only one for me
This is not the way its supposed to be

What is she doing for you that Im not
What does she have that I havent got
Being without you I feel so lost
I want to be with you whatever the cost
I dont know how to make you see
This is not the way its supposed to be
