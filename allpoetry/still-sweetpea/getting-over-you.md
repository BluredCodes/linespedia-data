---
title: "Getting Over You"
writer: "Still Sweetpea"

They say there is a thin line between love and hate,and Im starting to understand
Im not sure how much more of this I can withstand

One minute I love love you and the next I dont
sometimes I'll want to be with you and then I wont

No one can change how I feel
not even I can change my feelings at will

All I wanted was for you to love me
but my heart you decieved

Now all I want is to put you in my past
I'm getting over you at last

It's taken a while for my heart to get where it is
but its better for both of us like this

I'm saying goodbye,its time for this to end
I know our love we can never mend

Its not been easy to do
but finally IM OVER YOU
