i know this is not my decision to make
but im not sure how much more of this i can take
all the fighting has major effects
your unhappiness isnt hard to detect
i know all couples have their ups and downs
but sometimes i dont even want to be around
what you decide effects not only you
but there isnt much i can do
i want to be there for you,but dont really know how
tell me what to do to,i'll do it now
i love you more than anyone in this world
i want to always be your little girl
if you think its the right thing to do
i'll stay,and stick it out with youthis was written when i wanted my mama to leave her now ex boyfriend
