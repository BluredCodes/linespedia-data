title: "What you Want"

If love is lost, can it be found
Im not sure you should even stay around
The last thing I want it to hurt you
but who knows what I might do
I've been burned in the past
and just dont know if we can make love last
I want to be with you
but wonder if its the right thing to do
Its in me that I have all this doubt
Im not even sure what this whole love thing is about
Do you think I can give you what you need and want
Im scared that I can not
If even I dont me,shouldnt you
tell me now,are you sure this is what you want to do
