I have something to tell you,my confession
I love you so much;its almost an obsession
I love so many things about you
your sense of humor,confidence,and personality,just to speak of a few
I love that we talk for hours and never tire of each other
both listening carefully to one another
I love that you can always make me laugh,keeping me happy
there are so many wonderful things I want everyone to see
Your dominance turns me on,making me hot
you know you do that a lot
I just wanted to take a minute to tell you I appreicate everything you are and do
and make sure you know that I truly love you
