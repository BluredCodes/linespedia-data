---
title: "A New Angel"
writer: "Still Sweetpea"

An angel came and took me by the hand
he told me it was time to leave this land
He was gentle and kind
he brought ease to my mind
He told me everything was alright
and then all of a sudden this light, so bright
A glare in my eye
I knew it was my time to say good bye
At first I wasnt aware
but I looked up and everyone was there
Standing all in a row 
everyone's face aglow
Everyone from yesterday
everyone who had pasted away
The most beautiful sight
then all of a sudden I was an angel in flight
I'm always watching over you
I see everything you do
One day when it's your time to leave this land
I'll quietly come and take your hand
