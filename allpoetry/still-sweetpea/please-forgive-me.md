---
title: "Please Forgive Me"
writer: "Still Sweetpea"
slug: "please-forgive-me"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/14305-Please-Forgive-Me-by-Still-Sweetpea"
---

You're not going to like what I have to say
Please,dont take this the wrong way

I have something to confess to you
I dont know how you'll take it,or what you'll do

I've told you something thats not completely true
I've exaggerated how I feel about you

I really care for you,maybe more than I should
I dont love you yet,although I know I could

I want to love you,and I know I can
Buts lets take some time,and later try this again

I dont know why I felt the need to lie
But I feel so much better now;I can let out a big sigh

My intention was never to decieve you,though im sure you think that must be,
but please find it in your heart to forgive me
