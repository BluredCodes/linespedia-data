---
title: "The End"
writer: "Still Sweetpea"
slug: "the-end"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/14297-The-End-by-Still-Sweetpea"
---

Crazy thoughts fill my head,
fury over comes me
I cant believe the betrayal,
its so unnecessary

She was family and a friend
or so I thought
Apparently I was wrong
she is not

She knows how I feel
but just dont care
I cant believe shes doing this
how could she dare

We had been together 
for almost a year
Shes caused our relationship to end
or so I fear

I guess now I have no choice
I have to move on
Its so hard,
I've cried everyday you've been gone

I'll love you forever
just like I love you now
We'll be together again someday
I'll figure out how
