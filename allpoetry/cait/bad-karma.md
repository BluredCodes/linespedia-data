---
title: "Bad karma"
writer: "cait"
slug: "bad-karma"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Liquid heat passes throughcrystalline veins.Overwhelms me.Corrupts me.Incites riots and resurrects karma.Breathe deep and lower lids.The past plays picture perfect against the backdrop of closed eyes.Spiders spin silk from my socketsencapsulating my helpless pupil;forcing the show.Nerve endings send falsesignals and my skin once againcrawls with his cold caress.So rage envelops me in its volcanic embrace.Overpowering, I'm impotent.
