---
title: "electric impulses"
writer: "cait"
slug: "electric-impulses"

Anything to drown them out.
Turn up the music, take another 
hit of sound.

Focus on the pain they've inspired.
Its metallic fingers tightening
their vice grip.
Their sadistic phrenological exam.

Allow everything to expand past 
all logical porportions.

I think therefor I am...
They are my reality.
My obsessions.
Give them form and free reign.

Focus on this act and 
the next word, but when 
they're back to full volume
don't forget to write them down!

Into the journal, mustn't forget!
quickly now!

Before they have time to 
mobilize.
Bunch up.
Become an inpeneterable fortress.

Trapped from within,
they'll be back
