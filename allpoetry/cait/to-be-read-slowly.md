Fingers splayed over soft flesh,
left to their own devices to 
explore every fold.
Every surface.

Feel days old stubble 
turned to soft fuzz.
Feel short rough curls
and tendrils to force
shivers.

Swirl of an index,
dip of a thumb,
guide tongue to
salty flesh and small
pert pink nipples.

Taste and lick 
and suck all flesh,
navel, inner thighs,
and essence of you.

Fingers and tongue 
guide you, 
excite you,
Harden you.

Mold me to you;
I'm waiting.did you read it slowly...?
