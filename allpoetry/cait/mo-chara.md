what do we mean?
Trusworthy and trusting?
Honest and reliable?
Surely it's more than a good dog?

Is there a waiting period?
An informal probation?
Should there be?

An' if it harm none...
Shall we break our probation?
what then?
Who decides? 
What if your racket is broken?
Who serves the ball?

What if She said you're crazy?
And HE calls you neurotic?
What if they're right?

At what point does cynicle skepticism ferment to bitter regret?

What if it's happened before?
What strange sense of humour is this?
Why,with what She said, and He said, and You say, and I think,
do we still try again?

Why do we choose to trap ourselves in this vicious cycle of 
Codependancy,
Usury,
Entertainment, and
Need?

But they say jump, and I can
never stick my landings.

So hutt!  hutt!

Mon Ami,
My Friend,
Mo Chara.what am i talking about?
