The sound of my dog snoring happily beside me 
and the soft feel of my cats fur against my cheek.
The ecstatic "happy dance" of Blippers the Magnificent!
(A.K.A. Bill the ferret)

    All these have comforted me,
    These things I have loved.

The sound of my mothers voice
and the warm body of my sister dreaming next to me.
My Zaidy impersonating a fiddler with his daughter's 
ancient violin.

    All these have kept me grounded,
    These things I have loved.

The sound of a throaty pleasure moan
and the feel of hot lips and sharp teeth against my skin.
A pair of warm arms to hold me close.

    All these have loved me,
    These things I have loved.

This hap-hazard tapestry of subtle moments
framed and displayed for my heart to enjoy,
hangs a testament from the past 
to the future and for all
These things I have loved.gotta love homework?
