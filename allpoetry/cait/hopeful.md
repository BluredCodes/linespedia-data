The season changes and 
brings with it new possibilities
for hope,
for beginnings,
for closure.

Cleansed by pure white liquid 
Love.
Enveloped and renewed.

Celebrate!
Run joyous through the
streets and scream
Freedom!  
from the hilltops.

The option is there now.
Take my hand and
Run with me.
