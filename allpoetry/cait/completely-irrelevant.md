I am one with my nose.



Our relationship has transcended

the mundane sybiosis of everyday 

existance.



I am now agonizingly aware

of my aching Proboscis,

stuffed and wet.



I cannot see,

think,

or feel past my own 

nose.



It thought i should write this down for posterity. 

(gaizunterheit!)no deep meaning in this one.
