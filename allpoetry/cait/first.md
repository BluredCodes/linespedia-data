at their old apartment,shuffling and lingering,You Came.attempts at singing, guitar stringshumming and vibrating,You Played.later at my plazafidgety and glancing,You Forced Yourself.now, on a downtown streetshuffling and lingering,You Come again.using the same friend, and youreaching and desperate Get Me.but now,I am in control.and you will be Goneman i really don't like the subject of this piece.
