Channeling
 
Free with your purchase in the next 20 minutes!
A new life, 
Face, 
Body,
Soul.
JUICE!  Right?

I am in control.

A million children died as casualties of war 
Last year,
But  I  can change the channel.

As the minutes turn to hours 
They lose previous form and logic.
It is no longer 5:14 am.
It’s the end of the first commercial break in
Martha Stewart Cooks.
Finally I know how to make a tureen of foie gras.

My life is closer to perfection.

I am now halfway between the first commercial break
And the next break from commercials.

I am calling the Prayer lines.
Someone will be waiting for my call.

I am buying the AB Roller,
(No CODs please)
I am adopting a child of war,
I am 
Britney,
Enrique,
Sum 41 and SONY

Martha’s ending, but Sally will 
Pray for me,
A child will be saved,
My abs will be firm, along with my
Buttocks, thighs and hips.
I will know how to dress,
Do my hair,
MAKE my self UP.
The music to listen to and
The direction of tomorrow
Leading to tomorrow night.

Thank GOD
(no CODs please)curious vanity always wins.
