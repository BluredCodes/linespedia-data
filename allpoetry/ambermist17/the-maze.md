---
title: "The Maze"

Secrets shared
behind shaded smiles
Knowing eyes
meet across the room
The link between us
strong & deep
you come closer
stand beside me
take my breath away
We feel the same
and hide it away
behind closed doors
with no key
an endless maze
of sweet surrender
from which I can't escape
I don't want to
I'm lost in you
