---
title: "The Walls"
writer: "Ambermist17"

source: "AllPoetry"

All these rules
the walls so high
tower over me
hide my desire
but through the door
all can see
my struggle
I struggle always
with my desire
my feelings for you
never leave my mind
sleepless nights
endless fantasy
it hurts so much
it never ends
no hope in sight
no end to the pain
I'm helpless before you
I'm on my knees
I need you to set me free
