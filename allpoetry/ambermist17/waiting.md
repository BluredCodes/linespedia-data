Constant pain
alternates with constant joy
linked together
they blend into one
one desperate emotion
from which I can't escape
my companion
in the light of day
and the dark of night
and every time in between
I wait
wait for relief
wait for a resolution
wait for you
no end in sight
stretched tight across this chasm
pulled in two directions
towards you & away from you
suspended
over heartbreak & love
