---
title: "Your Storm"
writer: "Ambermist17"

up and down
round and round
tossed on your storm
your beauty blinds me
your voice haunts me
your touch thrills me
your presence shatters me
you blow my mind
send it swirling like the wind
you break me down
and build me again
with a casual glance
a whispered word
it's so easy for you
and so hard for me
my knees buckle when you look at me
when you touch me lightning strikes
how can I handle these highs & lows
I need to stay away
away from you
but I can't 
can't stay away
