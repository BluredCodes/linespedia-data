---
title: "Following You"
writer: "Ambermist17"
slug: "following-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/4967-Following-You-by-Ambermist17"
---

Awkward yet so powerful
the uncertainty in my heart
at moments beautiful
and yet terrible
a dark secret hidden
yet out there for all to see
they all know
how I feel
and I can't lie, but I can't tell the truth
I follow you
with my eyes, in my mind
always a step away
you turn and look
make my heart stop
and dance away with a smile
you toy with me
give me hope and take it away
you're so cruel
but I can't stop wanting you
a thoughtless word, a lingering glance
shatters my world
you know what you do to me
and yet you don't care
You swim in my misery
and enjoy your power
my traitorous heart--my life & my death
yearns for you still
when will I realize
it could never be
it will never happen
you move toward me
the tension escalates
and you turn me down
again
I won't ask anymore
I've been more bold with you than anyone else
and you turn me away
I know where I stand
I'm playing the fool
in your kingdom
give me the strength
to turn my heart
