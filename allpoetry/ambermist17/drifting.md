---
title: "Drifting"
writer: "Ambermist17"
slug: "drifting"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/39053-Drifting-by-Ambermist17"
---

Lost in the mist
that envelops my senses
The velvet mist
of dark surrender
caresses my skin
and fills my ears
with soft cries
of need
Aching to give in
I give up control
and lose myself
in it's soft clouds
adrift on the wind
that carries me to another place
so high & so sweet
I can touch the sky
Borne on the wings
of total abandon
shattered
by passion
