Behind your eyes
is a knowing smile
you know
I know you do
the secrets I hide
My heart lies open before you
yet you make no move
Do you feel something 
something small, desire
for me, or nothing
Am I a joke to you
a young girl with a crush
Do you tell your wife 
about me, my smile
Or am I your secret
your hidden desire
Do you wonder how I feel
Do you watch for me
look for reasons to approach me
If only I knew what lies
Behind your eyes
