title: "Climbing Your Pain"

Climb & climb
to higher peaks
ever onward
to your destiny
what is it you are climbing for
why do you try so hard
one misstep knocks you down again
back to where you started
it's that infinite hope
that keeps us alive
that keeps us striving towards that peak
up and down
and round and round
spins the yarn of our lives
it's the moments of gold
that perfect space in time
that rewards us for all of our pain
our indecision and insecurity
and then we start again
climb & climb
