I woke up today
certain in my uncertainty
content with not knowing
a feeling so free
strong in my conviction
that this is the right path
I know not where this will lead
I know not where it came from
I only know it is
and it's existence was destined to be
always there,
waiting,
for that moment of revelation
wherever this may take me
my life's been changed forever
enlightened by surrender
