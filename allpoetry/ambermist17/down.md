title: "Down"
writer: "Ambermist17"

---

And here I am again
in a place so familiar
tossed aside by your laughter
down, and down again
how do you do this
take me so high
only to throw me off that cliff
you're unfeeling
blind to my pain
it would seem
but you know
and I'm a toy to you
you don't care
you wound me
shatter my smile
so I feel alone
alone in my misery
how can I cope
what can I do
to make you want me
