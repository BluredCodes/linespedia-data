---
title: "Night Cry"
writer: "Ambermist17"
slug: "night-cry"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Across the miles
in the rich velvet of night
a voice calls you
who do you think of
what is in your heart
what is on your mind
I want to know you
your hopes & fears
your secrets
how can it be
I know you already
is this an echo of the past
or a future that's yet to be
