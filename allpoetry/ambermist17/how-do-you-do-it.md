title: "How Do You Do It"

How do you do it
how do you rule me so
you break me with your silence
and build me with your laughter
How did you do it
slip past my defenses
expose me to you
you shine such light on my darkness
I want nothing more
than to give you all of me
How do you do it
draw me in with your eyes
and push me away without a word
How can I break free
from your chains
when all I want is to be your slave
I'd do anything for you
fulfill all your dreams
chase away your fears
if you would give me a chance
How do you do it
deny me what I want most
