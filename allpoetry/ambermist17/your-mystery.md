---
title: "Your Mystery"
writer: "Ambermist17"
slug: "your-mystery"
source: "AllPoetry"

You mystify me
with your inscrutable eyes
they reveal all but say nothing
your light & your darkness
your humor & your strength
your thoughts you hide inside
so deep no one knows
how you feel
a mask to the outside world
How do I know you
is it something so real
we both turn away
afraid of the unknown
so penetrating
a shaft of light
within the deep night
a star for guidance
to remind us of what could be
a pool on the dry earth
to quench your thirst
a constant search
always looking
for what you don't know
wandering, drawn to the flame
the heat of desire
a certain burn
on your soul
and you don't know
why you can't say no
