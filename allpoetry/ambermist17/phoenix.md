Smoke risesthrough the depths of nighthigh above the trees towards the starsfull of magic & mysterywhile below a fire ragesflames of destruction & desireengulf all that I have knownall that I wasand burn it into ashyet I standamid the ruinsreborn, evolvinginto what I am meant to belike the phoenixI spread newfound wingsand follow the smokethat risesthrough the depths of nighthigh above the treestowards the starsfull of magic & mystery
