Many tears fall on the glass
reflects the tattered state of my heart
chipped & scarred, but pulsing still
lit within by your hope
striving forever for that golden star
beaten down by life and pain
the way is dark but knowledge gained
allows me to smile through the hurt
All that happens serves a purpose
though it may not be clear at the time
It's our job to live & learn
and learn to live again
