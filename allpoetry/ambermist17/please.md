In the soft shadows
I feel you
the satin heat of your lips
scorches my skin
makes me burn for your touch
your hands, so strong
stoke the fire
and I melt
yearning
to taste you
a need so strong
overtakes me
breaks me
I cry out
for you to release me
to show me
the heights of our ecstasy
unable to stop
I beg you
"please..."
