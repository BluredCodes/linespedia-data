Uncertainty
steals my breath
makes my heart pound
My stomach tightens
you look at me
from across the room
look deep into me
...and smile
sends my soul soaring
my heart takes flight
until I realize
it's not me you are looking at
but someone else
and my heart plummets
to the depths of despair
my eye tears
I look away
unable to gaze at the vision of you
laughing with someone else
laughing, I feel
at me...
