title: "Love's Question"

How is that what feels so right
is so awfully wrong
Is wrong defined by your heart
by your mind or by someone else
Do you bow to the restrictions
or fight for what you feel
against all odds
everything stacked against happiness
is it only for the few
with the strength to fight
Love's not easy
never has been
are all great romances
tempered by the flames of despair
by tragedy
or is it all fiction
a fantasy within all of our hearts
something to strive for 
but never achieve
a constant question
that brings never ending hope
Is this what defines humanity
falling & struggling to our feet again
is this what love is
a life to live or a 
lesson learned?
