---
title: "Hunger"
writer: "Ambermist17"

source: "AllPoetry"

---

a fierce & devouring
hunger burns through me
a constant craving
for you
your touch electric
and powerful
shoots through me
like lightning
a thousand storms
rage inside
and shatter my control
destroyed
by your kiss
your passion
adds to the fire
that burns always 
ignited by you
bound by the intensity
mesmerized by the flame
aching
for you
