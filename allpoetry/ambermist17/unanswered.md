---
title: "Unanswered"
writer: "Ambermist17"

source: "AllPoetry"

---

A thousand knives
shred my soul
they leave me in tatters
an empty shadow
of all that I should have been
my life a constant reminder
of all that I can't have
the storm of pain
whips around this place
tosses me back 
into unforgiving waves
of frustration & loneliness
humbled
I face the walls
that hold me back
that leave me begging
for something more
for what I don't know
I cry to the heavens
for some small hope
an echo rings back
unanswered
