---
title: "Seizing the Moment"
writer: "Ambermist17"
slug: "seizing-the-moment"
source: "AllPoetry"

A moment in time
suspended
on the fine edge of a razor
teetering, side to side
waiting for it to fall
towards me or no
It could be gone forever
unless I grab it
make the most of it
but wait I'm so scared
what is it I will lose
Is it worth it
How will you ever know
Live your life forever waiting
for that moment to find you
when your fantasy could be reality
You live life only once
Is taking that moment any worse
than a lifetime of not knowing?
