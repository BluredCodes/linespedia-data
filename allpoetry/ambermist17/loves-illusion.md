title: "Love's Illusion"

What is love
nothing but an illusion
what we all search for
but can never find
a mirage on the horizon
always calling to your heart
never to be fulfilled
an empty promise left in the dark
you thought you had it
but you were wrong
fresh perspectives open your eyes
to a world beyond yours
and you realize what you have
is not what you want
and ignorance is bliss
what a shame you can't go back
to that safe warm shelter
the door has been opened
a floodgate has been let loose
and your search starts again
for that which you can't find
