title: "Dark Amber"
writer: "Ambermist17"

Dark amber
wild & sweet
the honey of desire
rich & warm
I swim in it's beauty
so thick I can't escape
drawn to the taste
I long to feel it
surrounding me
drowning me
I want to wrap you
in it's heat
and show you the ecstasy
that lies within it's depths
come,explore with me
the sweet wildness
of dark amber dreams
