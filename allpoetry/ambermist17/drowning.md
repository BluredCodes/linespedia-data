---
title: "Drowning"
writer: "Ambermist17"
slug: "drowning"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/39062-Drowning-by-Ambermist17"
---

Riding the waves
of a hopeless desire
Endlessly beating
on the shores of my soul
Soaring on the crests
of pleasure so sweet
only to be plunged
into valleys that ache
Engulfed in the ocean
of infinite yearning
Drowning in my
wanting of you
