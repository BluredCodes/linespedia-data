title: "Carousel"
writer: "Ambermist17"

A spiral of emotion
circling in an endless rhythym
Cycles of change
follow each other
and chase their dreams
Impossible joy
followed by dark depths of despair
Certain beauty
leading uncertain tears
over and over
every turn different
but so much the same
as the one before
Following the end
or leading the beginning
it all flows together
on this carousel of love
