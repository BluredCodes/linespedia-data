A wall of steelI work on buildingto protect myselffrom your beautiful smilea desperate attemptto shield my heartfrom certain heartbreakback & forthfrom despair to elationbrick by brickI'll hide behindand arm myselfagainst desire's arrowsI'll breathe deepthe breath of solitudeand walk proudlythe path of lonelinessI'll not hang my headdue to your casual indifferenceI'll turn my backagainst your magic gazeand thrust deep the daggerthat spills my soul
