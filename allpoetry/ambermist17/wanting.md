---
title: "Wanting"
writer: "Ambermist17"
slug: "wanting"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/22774-Wanting-by-Ambermist17"
---

I imagine your eyes
darkened by desire
caught in the net
of what I could make you feel
Hearing you moan 
in complete abandon
feeling you ache
for a release only I can give
Teasing you, pleasing you
bringing you to the edge
of something so powerful
it leaves you breathless
on your knees
wanting more
wanting me
