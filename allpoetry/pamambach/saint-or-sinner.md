Saint or Sinner?                               
Pamela Ambach
9/01
Red dress
long dark hair
Not knowing why
she is there
Disappears
gone forever?
No, she's back,
wandering cadaver
To the right
Why I don't know?
filling with fright
Deathly glow.
Flashing movement,
Why is she here?
Heaven sent?
or evil near.
Diminishing image,
Saint or Sinner?
Soul a bridge,
my mind the winner.
