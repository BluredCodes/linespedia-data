Praise to the muses,
For their insight.
Praise to Helios,
For his brilliant light.

Praise to Zeus,
Master of storm.
Praise to Hestia,
Who keeps the hearth warm.

Praise to Demeter,
For he seeds to sew.
Praise to Selene,
Who makes the night glow.

Praise to Athena,
Over wisdom she rules.
Praise to Aphrodite,
Makes all the men drool.

Praise to Eros,
God of love and desire.
Praise to Prometheus,
Who gave us fire.

Praise to Artemis,
Mistress of the wild.
Praise to Hermes,
The mischievous child.

Praise to Hades,
Ruler of the dead.
Praise to Apollo,
Who rides overhead.

Praise to Dionysus,
Giver of wine.
Praise to Hera,
Queen of the Divine.

Praise to Ares,
Bringer of war.
Praise to Poseidon,
Earthshaker at the core.

Praise to Gaia,
Mother of them all.
Praise to Uranus,
Sky-father tall.

Praise to Hephaestus,
Master of the forge and the smith.
Praise to all the gods of Ancient Myth.
