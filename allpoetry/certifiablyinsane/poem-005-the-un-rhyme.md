Her hair tied up in a bun,
She carries away my son!
Just one night of harmless fun,
Life is over before it begun.
My sadness, it weighs a tonne!
My eyes blinded by the sun.
My misdeeds they do shun.
I pull the trigger of the gun.
Won't see when he turns one.
I can't do this, I'm way to young!
Over now, before its begun!
