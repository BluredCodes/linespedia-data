Psst.. Its a secret.
Don't tell anyone!
Don't tell them the answer.
It'll be fun!

No one can see,
Except you and me!

Wait now that you know,
You might let it out!
I'm going to have to kill you,
Sorry, don't try and shout!

I have to keep the secret!
Only I can keep it!
