In the beginning, there was nothing, and it was good!
Then there was matter, and it was good!
Then there was the bang, and it was good!
Then there were the stars, and it was good!
Then there were the planets, and it was good!
Then there was the land, and it was good!
Then there were the seas, and it was good!
Then there was life, and it was good!
Then there was death, and it was bad!
Then there was pain, and it was bad!
Then there was war, and it was bad!
Then there was the atom, and it was bad!
In the end, there was nothing, and it was bad!
Then there was peace, and it was good!
