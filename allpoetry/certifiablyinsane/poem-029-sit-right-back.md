Just sit right back and you'll hear a tale,
A tale of a fateful trip.
That started when I went down stairs,
And I fell and slipped.

My head, it hit the ceiling fan,
It started gushing blood.
Then blacked out when I hit the floor,
The last thing heard was 'THUD'.

I woke up in the hospital,
Many days had passed.
My arms and legs were broken,
A bruise on my ass.

Many injuries many, to list them might take awhile:
Broken Fibia,
My collar too,
My shoulder, and my spine!
A nasty scar. the pressure I cannot stand!
Here I can barely smile!

Rehabilitations hard,
I may never walk.
The thing gets me through the day,
Is the ability to talk.

You listened to my tragic tale,
I thank you much for that!
Just remember when you go downstairs,
Watch out for the cat!
