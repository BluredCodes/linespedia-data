WORDS OF WISDOM:

A wise man once told me that 500 years ago a man found a tortoise!
I hope that helps you more than it helped me!

A wise man once told me that 3 frogs in a blender don't make it rain!
I hope that helps you more than it helped me!

A wise man once told me that there are many paths in life!
I hope that helps you more than it helped me!

A wise man once told me that if you counted all the stars in the sky, you'd get a big number!
I hope that helps you more than it helped me!

A wise man once told me that his boots were made for walking, and that's just what they'll do!
I hope that helps you more than it helped me!

A wise man once told me that some people are born wise, others aren't.
I hope that helps you more than it helped me!

A wise man once told me not to trust wise men!
I hope that helps you more than it helped me!
