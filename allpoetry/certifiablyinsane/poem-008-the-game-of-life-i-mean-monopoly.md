Boardwalk, Parkplace,
Do not Pass Go!

Bill Gates, Mircrosoft!
Rolling in dough!

Oil, Petroleum,
A dollar a litre!

How much money can one have?
Should there be a meter!

Pinky, the Brain,
World rule!

One day, my destiny,
I will not be cruel!

Money, power,
What is it for?

Control of fate,
Too bad for Al Gore!

Life, everlasting,
To not be forsaken!

The world shall be mine,
Mine for the takin'!

3 rolls of doubles!
Off to jail!

I cannot go,
I must not fail!

DO NOT PASS GO... DO NOT COLLECT $200

If life only had a get out of Jail Free Card!
