I felt like writing a poem,
I don't know what for!
Poetry is addictive!
I keep wanting more!

I know I should think,
About what I want to say!
But I find if I let it flow,
It comes out better that way!

This won't be well liked,
That I can't blame!
I just like to write stuff,
And give it my name!

Rhyming is so fun,
I know that's not what its about!
Try to rhyme about,
And surely you will pout!

I wish I actually was a poet,
Then I could write something decent,
I couldn't make a career,
I wouldn't make a cent!

I really am insane,
That you must already know!
I'd do something else,
But I have no where else to go!

I'll just keep writting,
'Till I can't write no more!
Hopefully this poem,
Hasn't become a bore!

I think I'll go to bed,
Get some rest for school!
Or I might end sleeping on my desk,
With my notes covered in drool!

I really should go,
I have a quiz in the morn!
But I don't feel like sleeping!
Boy, am I torn!

I really should go,
Its past midnight!
I'll be so lonely,
Will you tuck me in tight?

This is it,
This is truly the end!
A person who has read this far,
Must really be a friend!
