You've been Hacked!
For no reason, I just felt like screwing around with you computer.

Your codes I Cracked!
There's no defense from me, I am unstoppable my power infinite.

I have no face!
Its not a crime if I don't get caught.

You cannot trace!
I'm in and and out before you can do anything about it.

I sit at home!
This is what I do for fun.

I'm all alone!
I have nothing better to do.

I'm crying out!
I'm angry at the world.

I want to shout!
But who would hear me anyway.

Then a smile!
I got you good.

I deleted your file!
Now what are you going to do?

All for what?
I don't know!

That's all I got!
That's my final blow.
