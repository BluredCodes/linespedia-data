I drank too much coffee this morning and now I can't go to sleep, and my mind is running at a million miles a second, and that doesn't even make any sense, and speaking of sense when I bought my coffee the cashier gave me 2 cents extra, and I didn't say anything because I figured why bother, it was her mistake and I wasn't losing anything, and besides its only 2 cents its not like they're going to miss it, coffee shops must make like atleast thousands of dollars every morning, everytime I go there, there is a huge line and I have to wait, and a couple of times I almost fell asleep in line it was embarrassingm, I was like leaning on the guy in front of me and he was like, Yo... get off, and I was like wooh chill guy, I'm just a little tired, so anyways I'm going to go my phones ringing, maybe I'll have another cup of coffee.
