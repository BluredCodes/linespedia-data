---
title: "Uncaring A$$"
writer: "Night Fighter"

I feel like I put everything to the side for you.If I want something then it takes second place to what you want.I am sick of all this (cruelty)Everytime you walk in the door whatever I'm doing stops , andI have to dedicate the rest of my day to making Youfeel better.What about making me feel better you selfish beast?What about putting aside some time in YOUR day for ME?Not to seem selfish,But everytime you walk by the television, you change the channelI am watching something and mid-sentence the poor actor gets cut off..Due to you...(selfish)You get in a funk and i'm expected to watch over you and tread on eggshells.I get in a funk it either goes unnoticed or... (help me)You walk away, not caring what i want to say.You cant deal with it, you already have enough stress (coward)Well, I want someone to deal with me.I want some one to be with me.Not to seem selfish,But every time you walk by the radio, you change the stationI'm listening to a song and the poor band gets cut off mid-chorus...Due to you...(uncaring)The sad part is that i am completely in love with you...Even though you are an unobservant, uncaring ass.And you say you love me. (hah)I'm so mad!!!!! Oh well, another unspoken argument gets layed by the side of the road to die a lonely death... What do you think?
