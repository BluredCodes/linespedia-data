---
title: "Healing"
writer: "Night Fighter"
slug: "healing"
source: "AllPoetry"

Kiss me.
Kiss me in the morning with the dew beneath our feet,
The start of a new glorious day.
Kiss me.
Kiss me in the evening underneath the stars,
The ending of a magnificent night.
Hold me.
Hold me in the twighlight hours,
When all my fears come to light.
Hold me.
Hold me in those first waking moments,
Letting me revel in the thought that it is you.
Touch me.
Touch me in the bedroom when panting softly,
That you love the way I look tonight.
Touch me.
Touch my heart when you offer nothing but,
The endlessness of yourself.
Trust me.
Trust me to hold your heart in my hands and,
Say the right words.
Trust me.
Trust me to walk into a crowded room,
And know where to find you.
Kiss me, Hold me, Touch me, Trust me
And know that everything will be allright.Maybe there is hope for me yet?
