You picked a fine time to tell me you still love me.
You picked a fine time indeed.
After all the horror you've put me through,
After all the pain you've caused.
You tell me you still love me.

You picked a fine time to tell me you still love me.
You chose a fine time indeed.
After all the warnings and threats,
After all the worrying and weird.
You tell me you still love me.

You picked a fine time to tell me you still love me.
You press at a fine time indeed.
After we have hurt each other so much,
After nothing but rage and hate.
You tell me you still love me.

Well the fact remains that I am no longer in love with you.
The fact remains that you purposefully try to manipulate me.
The fact remains that you are still toying with my emotions.
The fact remains that we aren't together anymore.
THE FACT REMAINS THAT I AM NO LONGER YOUR TOY.

It is probably better that way.
You have learned a valuable lesson...
When you find love, don't run away, don't hide, and don't forget how to
