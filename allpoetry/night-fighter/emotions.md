When I think of all the things that could be...
Between us, for us, around us,
I hope for better things.
I dont want to hear how depressed you are,
Not because I don't care,
But because I care so much.
When I hold you,
When you're hurting
And whenn I'm crying too...
I think of the days to come and am worry free.
You love me more than I have ever been loved.
I love you more than I have ever loved a man.
I want things to grow between us,
Into sparkling young flowers, covered with dew.
I reach towards you in the night when the dreams get to be too much.
You reach for me when your nightmares come alive.
I will be yoour rock.
I will be the one who anchors you and makes you feel alive.
When you want a fight, I'll fight.
When you want to scream, I'll help you.
THe worse thing happening right now is suppression.
Don't supress your emotions let them all out.
Good , bad , frightening , loving , hopefull.
Don't let yourself be done in by emotions that I can help you sort through.
Don't give up on yourself, you are much stronger than you think you are.
For you have been my rock, You keep me alive.
I want to know...
That you won't run...
From...
