I had an experience at the store today.
I walked in, calm as could be and waited in line for it to be my turn.
I peered into the glass cases, knowing what I was going to buy, but still seeking more.
I wanted the blue one.
I waited, and waited, and waited.
Realising that they were now serving the people behind me, I was upset, hurt.
I didn't know what to say.
Do I say excuse me but it was my turn?
Do I leave it alone and wait until the clerk notices me?
Do I throw a fit and call the manager?
I made a split decision and it went something like this.
EXCUSE ME BUT I BELIEVE IT WAS MY TURN.
but they ignored me and served yet another person from behind me.
EXCUSE ME BUT I WOULD LIKE YOU TO SERVE ME OR I WILL TAKE MY BUSINESS ELSEWHERE.
They at last glance at me, a fleeting glance... Little more than a nod...
they serve another person from behind me.
I believe at that point I became one of those rude obnoxious customers who no one wants to deal with.
EXCUSE ME, AS I CAN'T GET ANY SERVICE WITH YOU I WILL TAKE MY BUSINESS TO ANOTHER ESTABLISHMENT. THANK YOU FOR THE WONDERFULL SERVICE AND HAVE A GRREAT DAY!!!
I walked out of there with my head held high, on my way to buy the blue one from some where else.
I felt discouraged and ashamed. I was feeling how they wanted me to feel for being different.
I was letting them hurt me.
I ran out of the complex.
I hit up the Macs Variety and got a blue one.
Realising that they cant hurt me unless I let it happen.
No one should be punished for being different.
Just because I like Blue Popsicles.I thought that this was just a funny little poem/ rant that I would share with you all
