---
title: "Released"
writer: "Night Fighter"

The stars shine bright overhead
I am released.
Everything I've worked for is gone
I am released.
Forever is a long time
I am released.
Ancient in my own mind
I am released.
Old in spirit
I am released.
Young in heart
I am released.
Forgetting the pain
I am released.
Healing once again
I am released.
Moon glow and morning dew
I am released.
Forests of love and wisdom
I am released.
Creativity and naivete
I am released.
Thrusting and invading
I am released.
Interest in the world
I am released.
Never again to cry
I am released.
I am released into a world of doubt and misfortune.
I am released into a world full of hope, wanting not to be crushed.
I am released from the cell, staring at the sun, hoping.
I am released into this world full of wishes and dreams.
I am released into this life with friends and family.
I am released into a place that may yet harm me.
I am released knowing that nothing can harm me.
I am released.hope grows again? maybe...but not today
