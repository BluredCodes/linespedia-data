I once talked to a monkey sitting on my lap.
I once cried for hexagonal skies that were fast disappearing.
I once chased coffee beans around my living room because they were trying
To untie my shoes.

I once laughed at nothing.
I once created great works of art.
I once fed little pink elephants peanuts in my bedroom, to awake
And find only empty peanut shells on my floor.

I once read the stars.
I once climbed a fence to get to the top of a mountain.
I once fell down and couldn't get up because
A giant was standing on my chest.

I once had a ferris wheel spin off into the night.
I once had a tub full of water turn blue.
I once had a friend grow an extra face and
Watched her swallow it.

I once watched a friend learn to fly.
I once saw a friend that had died.
I once refused to repeat mistakes made by others and myself
Concerning LSD.This is a poem about my many years of substance abuse... Which are thankfully mostly over now!!
