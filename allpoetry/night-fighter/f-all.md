I do not want to kill myself.(liar)
I do not want to feel the pain.
I do not want to hurt the only thing i can save.(whore)
My life.
Have you ever tried to rescue yourself?(knight)
Have you ever fought one hand against the other not to pick up the knife?
Have you ever handed the knife to someone else?(coward)
My Life.
I want so much.(greedy)
I expect so little.
I get sweet f-all.(wishing)
My Life.
What is a life worth?(five cents)
What is this life worth?
What is the meaning behind the hidden words?(f-all)
My Life.
Twisted empty soul.(hidden)
Reaching out to humanity.
Finding nothing but a gaping hole.(torn)
My Life.
I am wanting things I haven't wanted in a long time.(death)
I am waiting for something I haven't craved in a long time.
I am sweating for something I shall never attain.(love)
My Life.
I am fighting for My Life.(COWARD)
I am hurting for My LIfe.(HIDING)
I am trying to save MY LIFE.(TROLL)owch...
