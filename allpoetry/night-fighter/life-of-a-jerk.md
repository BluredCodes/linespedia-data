---
title: "Life of a Jerk"
writer: "Night Fighter"

Selfish arrogant fart.
You have gone and broken my heart.
I was a fool,
To get stuck on you.
Now they'll carry me away in a cart.


Retarded pustulant freak.
You made me stand on my feet.
I was a fool 
To get stuck on you.
Now is this what I need to seek?


Stupid little boy
You treated me like I was a toy.
I was a fool
To get stuck on you.
Now is my turn to be coy?

I hate all this rhyming it's driving me mad.
It makes me so angry.
I want to be bad.
I want to sully,
Your thoughts for a while.
And contemplate the life of a jerk.
