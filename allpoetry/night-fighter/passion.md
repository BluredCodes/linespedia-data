Passion
The will 
Or the way?
Have you lost yours?
Where is it?
Without passion, life is empty.
You need to argue about something.
You need to feel the tightening of your stomache,
That is put there by,
Passion
The will
And the way?
Have you found yours?
How is it?
Is it slightly lacking in strength?
Is it feeling sad and low?
Passionate subjects?
Have you found any?
Where?
Are you able to construct a debatable subject.
Do you feel enough about the subject not to sound like you're lying?
Without passion for something, you are nothing.
You can be passionate about pigs?
You can be passionate in love?
You can be poassionate about your side on the debate of the seperation of Canada?
You can be passionate about toads?
You can have feeling about everything. 
All you have to do is try.
Because passion is the health of a persons soul.
Without being passionate about anything,
