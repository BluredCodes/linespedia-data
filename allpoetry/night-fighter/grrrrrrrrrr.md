You.
The one who hides his face while he calls me a whore.
Me. 
The one who denies only the truth.
You.
The one who's been out every night since we've broken up.
Me.
The one who does your dishes without love.
You.
The one who has stayed out all night with girls.
Me. 
The one who comes home.
You.
The one who has slept with two people in the four days.
Me. 
The one who crys and blames herself.
WELL FORGET THAT!
YOU ARE HURTING ME AND I'M NOT GOING TO PUT UP WITH IT!
I WANT YOU TO GO AWAY!
who's the whore now sucka? we broke up four days ago.
I WANT THE SILENT WAR TO END.
I'M GONNA MAKE IT LOUD!
I HAVE NO PROBLEM SAYING WHAT I MEAN.
and let me be brutally blunt.
YOU SUCK!!
FORGET ABOUT THE DISHES, THE MONEY, THE PETTY BULL.
I ONLY ASK FOR A LITTLE CONSIDERATION.
A LITTLE THOUGHTFULNESS.
i guess i expect too much, because...
YOU SUCK!
I HAVE REALISED THAT YOU COULDNT BE A MAN.
MEN DONT TREAT WOMEN LIKE THIS.
YOU ARE  A MOLE ON THE SURFACE OF CIVILIZATION.
UNCIVILISED CURR.
if only....
GRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRRI'm so mad. I'm gonna go cry now...Five in the morning still awake cause I cant get over the rudenes... THis is the second time...we've only been broken up for four days. GRRRRRR..Funny thing to note..we live together. Haven't slept in days. How is this my fault?..Correct class...IT'S NOT!!
