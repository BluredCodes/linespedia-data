title: "Why Are You Yelling?"
writer: "Night Fighter"

Scream in revulsion,
Yell about the grievances of life.
Start a revolution,
If you have nothing to hide.

Roar in desperation,
Wail for all you're worth.
Forget all the temptations,
Live a life of abstinace from all.

Shriek in classifications,
From dumb to idiotic.
For some of you out there,
Yelling is hypnotic.

Silly people,
You don't know what's going on.
You are being played for fools,
Doing exactly what's expected.

Damn it. Forget it,
I'm done explaining others.

Have a good scream...
It feels great,
