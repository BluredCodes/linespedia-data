I look at you across a field, 
splendor of gold and green. 
I watch to see the sign, 
Of what you will do to me. 
I prepare. 
Holding my breath, 
feeling my heart beat. 
I reach for the one, 
power to course through me. 
I am like sung wood. 
You look at me across a feild, 
faeries dancing wildly. 
You watch to see the movement, 
that I have held so tightly. 
You prepare. 
Holding your breath, 
Feeling your heart too. 
You stretch for the one, 
power to course through you. 
You are like obsidian. 
I throw at you a gleaming ray of light. 
Striking your left shoulder with the blight. 
You take that to heart. 
You change me into a gollum. 
Ribbiting and running from. 
I take that to heart. 
I pull the air around you into a storm. 
Whipping your long hair like snakes. 
You take that to heart. 
You pull down fire to burn my skin. 
An inferno of one part. 
I take that to heart. 
I sing a song of ice. 
Freezing you to stone inside a glass cell. 
You take that to heart. 
You sing a song of rupture. 
The ground splits beneath my feet and I fall. 
As I fall I pull a dirty trick. 
I have true weapons. 
I shoot an arrow into the sky. 
You take it in the heart. 
I have won one battle. 
I have been victorious. 
I have cheated.
