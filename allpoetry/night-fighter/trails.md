For each one of us there is a path
Converging but never meeting
Twisting but never returning.

I fell upon the wayside
Twisted and confused.
I fell upon the downside
Ripped and feeling used.

I wanted to feel the touch of your hand.
I wanted to know you were there for me.
I wanted to feel that you were my man.
I wanted to know you loved me.

Instead you ripped the seam of my silver cloud.
Spilling my tears that it contained.
I am no longer very proud,
Of what we then became.

I wanted to see you in a different light.
I wanted to feel your heart beat next to mine.
I wanted to see your eyes smiling bright.
I wanted to feel a physical sign.

Instead you made my world tumble down.
Leaving my soul bare and arms empty.
I am no longer happy when i frown,
And laughingly look at you.
