---
title: "For Your Love - Thank You"
writer: "Night Fighter"
slug: "for-your-love-thank-you"

Every time I open my eyes 
you are there.
To comfort me in my tortured thoughts.
Reassuring me of hope in the future.
A tantalizing breath of fresh air, lightenig my load.
You are the sun,
Rising in my sky to the delight of a new day.
You are my stars,
Brightening my dark self involved path.
You are my strength,
Moving me from day to day,
Helping me become myself.
I love the way at night you wrap your arm around me and whisper in my ear,
I love you.
I love the way you do my over-analysing for me,
Allowing me some peace of mind.
I love the way you smile with all your heart in your eyes,
While looking at me.
Please make this feeling last long into our lives,
When our youth and looks have faded,
And all we're left with are sweet memories and incredible minds.
Thank-you for being there and being 
The friend I always needed,
The lover I had never hoped for,
And the man of my dreams.This is for that special man... You know who you are.
