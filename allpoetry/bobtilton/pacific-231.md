---
title: "Pacific 231"
writer: "Bobtilton"
slug: "pacific-231"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Is your fragility wanting so badly to be crushed,
you'd leave it up to me to do the crushing?
The new sincerity, making a mockery of trust,
but if it's up to me, bring on the new thing.

Pacific 231.

We're not careening off the rails,
just getting up to speed.
I never dreamed I'd learn to love the taste of bitter fictions,
or the ring of contradictions that sing to me.

And it's apparently a faster forward than you want,
But you're still keeping up - Is that devotion?
Unsubtle parody: poor fit and re-conditioned parts.
The very engine is sick from the motion.

And now we're learning about the charity that costs
but we could do with out the education.
