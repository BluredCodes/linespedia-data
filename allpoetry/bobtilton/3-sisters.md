title: "3 Sisters"
writer: "Bobtilton"

Drowning on a perfect day.
A surface I could never break...

... Cold and opaque...

And fear beneath, a current pulling me beyond reach.
River in the coarse daylight, waiting for the sins
that stick too vague to put right.
Wash away belief; eventually they all unravel.
Today I'm going in to stay.

Three sisters and me frozen in the water.
A kiss to send me sinking to the bottom of my right mind;
low lie.

Cameras and crystal sync(sink).
Pictures and the words, never seeming to link.
Try again and fail again,
fail better on the next try.
