---
title: "Sticks and Stones"
writer: "morningstar"

Sticks and stones may break my bones
But the words you say break my heart
The pain they cause was never known
I was never too smart

Pain comes in so many forms
You never know when it will come
And when it rains, it pours in the storm
You will get some

What goes around comes around
It will haunt you till your last day
The pain you've caused is easily found
It'll never go away

Be careful when you speak
You may hurt someone again
Break them down and make them weak
You will know then

Sticks and stones may break my bones
But the words you say break my heart
Be careful where you throw those sticks and stones
And those words you say
