title: "Venom"
writer: "morningstar"

Lost in the lies you tell
I can't find the truth 
Stuck in the webs you've spun 
I can't free myself from you 

I know you're poison
But I can't keep myself from you 
Oh, how I'm attracted to the bad 
Knowing I could be bit from behind
But the adrenaline that pumps 
Keeps me from pulling away

Falling to the ground 
The world turns a purple haze 
Everything I see is deformed
I can feel the burn inside 
As death slowly takes it turn 

You are a lethal dose

Oh girl, your bite's like venom
Slowly killing me from the inside out
And there's no way to stop it
As it runs through my veins
Venom, venom
