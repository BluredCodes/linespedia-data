~Heaven's Jubilee~

Angels celebrated happily on the day;
The Savior, all mankind was to be known.
The world would know by the Divine way,
A beautiful star in Heaven had shown.

A virgin travelled in painful travail,
her husband did the best that he could.
They came to a town for needed avail,
but no one offered to do much good.

Then the keeper of the inn had an idea,
there was a stable waiting in the back.
God made it happen with His great method,
providing needs even when there was lack.

The babe was wrapped in swaddling clothes,
as wise men from the east came and saw.
For salvation this day was disclosed,
those who take the gift, God gave all.

Choruses filled up the evening's air,
singing Glory To God, Peace To All Men.
Shepherds were bless to be shared,
great tidings that were shouted then.

Now heaven's hosts await another day,
to resume their joyful, blissful jubilee.
When thanks to Christ, men know the way,
to partake in the wonderful festivity.

This is the true story about Christmas,
love, peace and happiness to everyone.
Let's share with loved ones his message,
Honor, Praise and Glory, to God's Son.
