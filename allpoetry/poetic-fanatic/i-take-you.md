~I Take You~

I take you by the hand,
because you took me by the heart...
because you captured a fire in my soul.

I take you and love you,
loving every dear, moment together...
loving life no matter what would wither.

I take you, one and only, mine,
my rainbows, my moonlight, my sun,
you're my reason for rising in the dawn.

I take you, hold you through eternity,
holding your heart in my hand tenderly,
you hold the key, breaking my cages...

and unto days, forever plus one...
our love will be defined, for all ages.
