~My Spoiled Baby~

Yes Yes Yes, I want this one!
Please, she says to me.
I replied, but honey, 
it's a lot of money,
but then I said, ok 
if it makes you happy.

So, we'll be cruising in style,
not that anything's 
wrong with my van.
But I love seeing her smile
and so I always do,
whatever I can.

Xena's got a shiny new olds,
and the deal turned 
out pretty fair.
Yet the sacrifice 
has just started
and it'll be hard,
a dime to spare.

I can't help but to laugh,
my spoiled baby
gets her way.
After all the 
car talk quibling,
it sure made her day.

Yet I love her very dearly
and she does
pay of her due.
And so I saw,
it all so clearly,
she deserves something new.

She had sparkles in her eyes,
now the burden
will fall on us.
What can I say,
I love her way,
so I do what I must.

***** Poetic Fanatic *****She works hard for her money and the squeeze
the car puts us in is still worth her getting
something she always wanted.
