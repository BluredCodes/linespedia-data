Here's what I'm about. Tommy ~ PF



I Am What I Am

I am a Christian and proud to be.

I am a poet, writing of sincerity.

I am a man who has many limits.

I am a soul who knows of regrets.

I am a lion, it was done by fate.

I am for peace, to never want hate.

I am a father, hoping for my kids.

I am a husband giving all, there is.

I am a friend, tried and true, test me.

I am a child, My God knows it to be.

I am honest the best that I know how.

I am a worker by sweat off my brow.

I am real, I don't pretend for a show.

I am vulnerable, pain's something I know.

I am devoted to everything I stand for,

I am all of these and very many more,

If you'd like more details about me,

then freely see me in my poetry.
