~Tell 'Em From The Heart~


*Fitted to fit the song by Roxette, "Listen To Your Heart".

You may know a friend or loved one alone,
facing the tough times, all on their own.
What do you do when you hear a heart cry,
Help it to heal or do you just walk on by?

Tell 'em from the heart,
That you really do care.
Tell 'em from the heart,
That you're always there.
Tell 'em from the heart,
that they really must try.

Tell 'em from the heart...
Before... that final... goodbye.

They might only need just your ear to lend
or maybe you can with them, your time spend;
Such simple things, we could all rely-
to remove a tiny tear from a tireless eye.

Tell 'em from the heart,
that they're dear to you.
Tell them from the heart,
what you're willing to do.
Tell 'em from the heart,
to ease their quiet sigh.

Tell 'em from the heart...
Before... that final... goodbye.

This is the start, how a sad story goes,
regrets and broken hearts, no one knows.
You can't turn the tide if a loved one dies,
you'll just pour an ocean with your own eyes.

Tell 'em from the heart,
before it gets too late.
Tell 'em from the heart,
never dare to hesitate.
Tell 'em from the heart,
your love's reasons why.

Tell 'em from the heart...
Before... that final... goodbye.

***** Poetic Fanatic *****
