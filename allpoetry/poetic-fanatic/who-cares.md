Who Cares???

Who cares that you might be feeling down?
Who cares, you're sinking in sad solitude?
Who cares that no one comes to aid your woes?
Who cares that people are treating you rude?
Who cares that your heart's surely in shards?
Who cares when your body feels kind of numb?
Who cares that your life is deathly boring?
Who cares that people think you're dumb?

Who cares when you wallow in your misery?
Who cares if or not you'll be ok tomorrow?
Who cares that your bed is now your refuge?
Who cares that you could die in your sorrow?
Who cares when your spirit is feeling empty?
Who cares that you hate yourself inside?
Who cares when you feeling forsakened?
Well, dear friend here and now I confide-

For it's true this is a world cold like Hell
and only those who know the fire can relate.
There is Someone often whom you can tell,
the big things in your life that you hate.
He has ears for He is the Creator of them,
He holds the tears that you shed in a cup.
He bursted mine throwing them into infinity
and He waits patiently for you to wake up.

Why bear all of your pain and grief alone
During times when the human ear won't hear.
Don't go wrestling of restlessness on your own.
God longs to embrace you for He'll hold you dear'.
And my friend also we who know such things,
desire greatly to help in easing your weary mind.
There are still decent people amongst the weeds.
Seek them out and just behold what you'll find.

Who cares that your life can't get much worse?
Who cares that your load is very hard to bear?
I'd respectfully ask you seek God above first
and friend call on me because I also care.
