` ' " Pieces " ` ' 

' " ' fly all around... ' " '

one here, one there, what's left?

' How do I take a billion shards '

and glue them all back together just like,

my life before it was brokened,

when I was whole, well, sane?

How to find my

pieces?

" ' Pieces ' "

' One by one I ' 

can gather them all up,

place, accordingly together.

Some have brittled, some have softened,

but they are still parts of what I was then.

I'll be brave, my interworkings,

to re-weave, I believe,

I'll restore my

pieces. 



***** Poetic Fanatic *****Written for a friend who if only she knew, she could get
her life on course by the progression of making good choices. 
Tommy
