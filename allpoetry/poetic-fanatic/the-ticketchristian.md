~The Ticket~

How many times had you hoped to win,
only to find
your lot 
once more plotted to lose?

Try if you can, turn the tables again,
yet failure 
seems eminent,
whatever you'd choose.

Venture to gain, straining your brain,
a miracle 
to wipe
away all, your debt;

bringing sunshine through your rain,
here are thoughts, 
I hope
you'll never forget.

To see dreams, we'd do crazy things,
like buying lottery tickets 
wishing, 
all our might;

Yet those odds are impossible it seems,
what would 
it hurt,
an impoverished plight?

Friends, open your eyes, try to be wise,
there is one
vision that,
I and others foresee-

It is guaranteed never to hold lies
God's the only one,
who'll 
make it be.

Seek His ticket and you won't regret,
the peace 
you'll have,
your troubled heart.

For you, yes you are invited to get,
a rich treasure 
if you
make the proper start.

So let life kick you, but be very nice,
to people 
treating you,
so terribly unkind.

Be true to yourself, never think twice,
for something 
better, soon 
you will find.

Your last day, you can climb aboard,
to a journey that will
surpass 
every dream-

Streets, pure gold, presence of the Lord,
what could 
ever kill,
this beautiful thing?

With this plan, no one can take it away,
unless you
 let darkness
gain your control.

It's totally free, there's nothing to pay,
except letting changes,
refresh
your soul.

Why would you wait, holding for life's hate,
when this invitation's
opened, 
one and all?

No chance, luck or unfortunate fate,
hinders reward to those
who answer
this call.
