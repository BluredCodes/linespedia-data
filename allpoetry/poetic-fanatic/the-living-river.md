---
title: "~The Living River~"
writer: "Poetic Fanatic"
slug: "the-living-river"
source: "AllPoetry"

It's a vast mystery as the universe itself,
the very 'key' to the doorway of life,
containing the history of all my being,
who I am, where I'm from and where I'm going.
It nourishes every part of my body and mind,
while cleansing me from the inside and out.
It is a raging river, surging, running inside, 
giving me strength to carry on another day.
Oh, the magnitude that this river holds!
With it, another thing that really amazes me;
The power to purify and renew my very soul;
As it trickeled to the ground from a cursed tree,
He screamed 'My God, Why Hast Thou Forsakened Me'.
And now evermore gratefully, I shall live faithfully.

***** Poetic Fanatic *****
