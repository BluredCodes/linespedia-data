~Funny Thing About My Heart~

My heart is filled, love for my Lord-
My heart is full of love for my mate.
I'm engulfed fully, love for friends.
Yet, I hold not any reserve for hate.

The funny thing about my heart is,
the more it holds, more it can take.
Seems ironic, for what is out there,
we keeping adding to, that won't break-

Even a void doesn't seem to go away easy,
it just gets squeezed in a remote place.
Yet, enough love from others can fill,
everywhere even the void's space.

Look at your heart, it's made like mine,
do you file feelings like on a machine?
To sort your emotions of love and hate,
don't you think that is kind of extreme?

The heart is not created but for love,
anything else is unhealthy and unwise.
Enlighten yourself and go an extra mile,
then the vail will be lifted off your eyes.
