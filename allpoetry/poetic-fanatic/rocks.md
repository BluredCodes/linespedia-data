~Two Rocks~

"There are two rocks in the desert,
both of these are alive and speak.
One helps the other maintain itself
and that way, neither fall too weak.

These rocks both have a common thread,
for they can relate and empathesize
when something comes along with dread,
things work out nicely by surprise."

***** Poetic Fanatic *****

~These Rocks~

I have many rocks I lean upon at times,
my wife, my lovely woman is number one.
With my faith in the great Rock Above,
I still at times feel somewhat alone.

This Rock though gave me an edge,
that at times of despair, I can reach.
For friends are great Rocks on earth
and in this poem, I aim to teach.

There is another rock that helps me,
when the times, I seem to crash.
This rock is also such a beauty,
her heart appears in a flash.

Then there are also other rocks
and these are so important too.
For in a world, storms of tomorrow,
we need them all to get through.

Some advice for soft stones out there,
find these rocks you desperately need
and you can, like I do, find great care,
a friend who shows is a friend indeed!

***** Poetic Fanatic *****For My Best Friend Up There.
For My Best Friend On Earth.
For My Best Friend On Line.
And For Any Friend Of Mine!
