~Sunshine~



I have a sunshine that brightens every day,

no matter what storms that are out and about-

It keeps me happily trodding along my way,

in any situation, that life might spout out.





Whenever the times, I'm riddled in a puzzle,

confusion, making me spurn what I've learned,

a ray of this sunshine reminds me, who I am

and then my spirit, from negativity can turn.





For this sunshine keeps my inner self warm,

my heart and my soul in harmony, are cheered-

It's a special gift to secure from our harm; 

One, which I'll always keep, deeply revered.





My bestest friend out there, many miles away,

the one I met online, February, one year ago.

Though we differed, our respect saved the days

and now it's time to thank YOU, saying so.



You are the sunshine I never would replace.

You have a great way to keep rooting me on-

Your jokes, smiles or just being there to share;

With caring that shows, I'm not walking alone.





We both agreed, by Divine providence, we met.

Because you have helped me, considerably so.

Of all great blessings, you've wished upon me,

one's you and I hope, to your way they'll flow.







Tina, May God bless you abundantly- 

You and Juan and Angel and Ricky





*This is a tribute to a wonderful friend.*

Though I wrote the poem, the words come

from Both me and Sharon (hers too). Tommy
