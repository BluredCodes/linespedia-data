~The Great Physician~

His words remove discourse from a vexed heart,
if you allow him to make, the proper start.
He can open it up and remove all intrusions,
like hate, envy, pride and other confusions.

For He's a two-sided sword with razor's edge
seperating your spirit from the soul's hedge.
So know this, you can fool the whole human race,
but your heart and reckoning, you must face.    

He is a sharp-cutting doubled-edged sword
and His people work with the same accord.
Next time, you'd feel a lump in your throat,
be sure to be right in what you might gloat.

For our guilt is there placed for a purpose,
So we might all discern below our surface,
what hides deeply, embedded in our being,
to make us see the bad others are seeing.
