~Release Your Sweet Peace~

Conflicts, frequently, we all do face-
~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~

Perplexing confusions of judgments tried.
~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~

There's a simple answer to take and replace-
~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~

Misery and agony for the truth on your side.
~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~

For it is certain you can test and you'll know-
~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~

ways to discern the good and evil to part.
~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~ 

The conscience is key to keeping the soul-
~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~

Search daily deeply into thine own heart.
~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~


***** Poetic Fanatic *****
