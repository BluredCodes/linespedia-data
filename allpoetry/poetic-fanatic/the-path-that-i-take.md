The Path that I take,
God leads the way.
Others may forsake,
yet He'll be my stay.

The Path that I take,
has wolves slaying sheep.
'Tis a terrible mistake
surrendering to their keep.

The path that I take,
is not very easy;
My soul might ache,
but it's right for me.

The path that I take,
is truly slow and long,
with many choices to make,
I'll stride with a song.

The path that I take,
is an enduring test.
I may make a mistake,
but I'll score the best.

The path that I take,
is not peaches and cream.
Sometimes I need a break,
when my stress is extreme,

But I will keep on going,
my faith, I can't forsake,
there's light and life glowing,
ending the path that I take.

***** Poetic Fanatic *****
