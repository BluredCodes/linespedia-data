~Counting The Stars~

It's such
a beautiful 
clear skyed, 
cool night
and there's 
not even,
a single 
cloud in 
sight.

You and I, 
here alone
gazing at all, 
the stars.
This peaceful 
experience
is for only, 
being ours.

As we lie 
back calmly
looking way 
up above
pointing 
to them 
randomly,
it makes me 
wonder of

How many, 
are up there
truthfully so, 
God only knows
for we can't fathom
things He's composed.

But one thing I'm certain
as I look into your face
I'll add one more number
that goes in it's place.

For out of the many 
bright bodies
flying, so far 
out there,
your smile 
beats all, 
as being
the most 
radiant 
I know.
That's
what, 
my love...
I just have 
to share.

It's the memories 
like this that I'll
always recall, 
your love and 
tenderness
when you 
give me
your all.

Of every beauty 
on the earth,
I have seen,
pleasures 
to know,
your love, 
your touch 
and kisses
continue to 
freely flow.....

And my 
heart 
melts, 
smiling
at what 
a wonder 
you are-
When the 
true count 
is known,
I'll add just
one more...
