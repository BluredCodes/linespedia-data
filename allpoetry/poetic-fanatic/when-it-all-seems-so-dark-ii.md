A True Account Of My Spiritual Awakening- Christian Poem! Tommy



~When It All Seems So Dark II~



When it all seems so dark,

when the air feels like dust-

When your fire has lost it's spark,

and your world is up in chaos;



When the voices in your head,

scream: Lets us escape.

When people seem so cruel

and tears cover like a drape,



When your body aches for love,

when your soul cries for solace,

there is no refuge you know of 

and gravity compels your face



When it all seems so dark,

hopelessness is all you see

and nothing seems to resolve

your crushing agony.....



There may be a way out,

if in faith you would turn,

to Heaven and cast out,

burdens that make you burn.



I can say for myself, true,

I have known all of these things,

the moment of truth came due

and humility released my chains...



As I cried out to God,

He took it all away...

And you may believe or not,

I was whole the very next day.
