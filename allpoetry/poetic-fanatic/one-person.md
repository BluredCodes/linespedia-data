~One Person~

There is one person I know,
who gives so much around here.
On those times I'm feeling low,
she always can bring some cheer.

She is a big poetry maniac,
who loves the craft to write.
Anytime I might make it back,
she's there to make things bright.

I admire her fire for this place,
she gives equally to us all,
as one the hardest workers here,
keeping us standing strong and tall.

Though poetry may be her passion,
it's evident, we are important too.
She's grown herself to fly happily
and her poetry comes shining through.

Though at times so many folks change,
Nell has always been one to count on.
I've never seen her acting strange,
to leave friends in the cold, alone.

She is such a wonderful inspiration,
Because she stays the trooper here.
She has so much, a giving dedication
anyone around can see it all clear-

Nell, you are the poet for the poets,
not only do you press to be good,
but you have a generous, kind spirit,
to help anyone out if you could.

The one person at this destination,
who will continually be the same,
with the poetry and the people,
is Nell because poetry is no game.

So to you, me dearest appreciation,
I think all those here would agree,
You can't beat Nell over many others,
her love for people and of poetry.
