*For Tina And Juan*

~The Light Will Return~

It may be dark, trouble lurking around.
Skies are grim, silence roars it's awful sound,
making you feel so sad, lonely and worn.
Take heart, hang tight and stay strong,
because the light will return in the morn.

Perplexity of vexations may hurt peace of mind.
Clues are of mysteries as you are groping blind'.
Such things can make us all weary, restless and worn,
but we can have faith, keep straight and meditate,
because the light will return in the morn.

My friend, remember, when life would get you down,
I'm waiting to aid you and I'll always be around.
We all need each other, to prevail of any storm-
You can count on me and I will count on you,
because the light will return in the morn.

Don't forget, there's always one we can turn to,
when it gets too much, feeling as if you're through.
Say a prayer to Him up there, it could be your cure,
A promise to triumph over all the grief, you've born,
because the light will return in the morn.
