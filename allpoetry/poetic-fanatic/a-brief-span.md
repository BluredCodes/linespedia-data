________________________________________________________________
~infinity~













procreation
gestation
you are born
contemplation

contemplation
education
graduation
you are free

relaxation
meditation
contemplation
searching

frustration
aggravation
contemplation
last breaths taken

contemplation
desolation
transformation
destination













~eternity~
