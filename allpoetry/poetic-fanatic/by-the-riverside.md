~By The Riverside~

Laying here by the riverside,
all alone Darling, just you and I-
We watch the big fluffy clouds go by,
a soft breeze blows, sweet serenity flows,
it all just makes me feel so high.

It's such a smoothe transition,
from restlessness to easiness.
With the beauty of my sunshine,
lighting up on my face, yes...
It's you dear that brightens this place.

Look at the sparkling waters traveling,
like a million tiny shards of glass rolling,
reflecting, each one a piece of the sun,
looking like gold, the delight of hearts,
for no color could compare to this one.

Then I ponder which makes my smile more,
the big beautifully blue, October skies,
the color of your loving, bright blue eyes
or the way your lusciously bursting lips, 
like a rose petal, kiss me and make me mellow.

Oh these be such days to lie in the shade,
letting the crazy world of pursuits pass by-
feeling total contentment as cupid waits above.

It will be just in a little bit more time, 
there will be no denying, as the closest of friends,
we will draw over the border, we'll fall in love
and the rest of our days, you will be mine.
