title: "* Diamonds *"
writer: "Poetic Fanatic"

True, diamonds are forever,
the way a marriage is to be;
When you slip the ring on her finger,
it should remain, all eternity.

A diamond is a most precious gem,
wonderful jewel of great value,
to hold, protect and maintain,
makes pure pleasure come true.

A diamond shall not be tainted over-
because it sparkles through the years.
Such a treasure needs much caring for,
lest its gleaming glamour disappears.

There are many things like a diamond-
good friends would come to mind.
Try searching high throughout the land,
but like a diamond, not easy to find.

No one rejects the hand of a friend,
if the gesture's offered in sincerity,
genuine concern and true, deep feeling,
like diamonds shine in crystal clarity.

The good friend always remains the same-
Jesus: yesterday, today and tomorrow.
I desire to be much more like this man,
one step at a time, higher I climb, 
till the world sees it all fit to follow.

***** Poetic Fanatic *****
