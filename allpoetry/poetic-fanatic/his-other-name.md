~His Other Name~

To be Supreme in all of everything,
His glory could not be contained.
So, he spoke to bring life, existing,
His tenderness fell like the rain.

He holds all, in the palm of His hand,
He's the great key to universal harmony.
His nature is against all contention,
He has faith in common humanity-

For we are His sons and daughters,
we should care, each, one for another,
not, pigs of war looking to slaughter,
for He says, every man is your brother.

He is known by several different names,
yet, think upon this one in your heart-
Remember, in His eyes, we're all the same
God is the epitome of Love, every part.

***** Poetic Fanatic *****
