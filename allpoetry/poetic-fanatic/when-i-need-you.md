~To My Darling, Sharon~

When I need you,
you are always there. No one on earth shows
me this kind of care.

I am never alone
because you are in me. When left on my own,
I still have your memory.

Something here going on
that surpasses plain love. As two spirits we're one,
united like angels above.

Through my mind you stream,
running in secret pathways. You fulfill my night's dreams 
and bring life to the days.

Life is never... a bore,
long as you are around. You have a way, I adore,
lifting me high if I'm down.

Together we are a melody,
one heartbeat between two. Our music becomes a symphony,
as the closer I draw to you.

Whenever I've needed you Dear,
you've come through every time. You've taken away my fear
and your touch is so sublime.

How can words tell how I feel?
What shall I measure this to; Something so sacred and real,
Honey, I will always need you.
