~Circles~

we all tred in,

one time or another.

There is a better way to go,

if all people, a clear path would travel-

Viscious cycles could be reversed,

take a new direction,

walk straight, not in

circles.


***** Poetic Fanatic *****



*Rictameter, 
A style, short as a poem, here is criteria for writing one- syllables distributed
in 9 lines as 2, 4, 6, 8, 10, 8, 6, 4 , 2 with same first and last lines.
Sometimes you have to read deeper than just in the words, 
poets know... Try writing one yourself before grading others. PF
