~My Favorite Star~

You can't find it in the sky,
but search it long and hard.
At times you forget it's gleam,
don't be so quick to discard.

*****

It blazes up woeful hearts,
with laugher and loads of smiles.
It's an extremely rare treasure,
you might scan a thousand miles-

*****

We might forget it's great worth,
but it comes and reminds us, true,
there is nothing greater on earth,
of a friend's pure love for you.

*****

I hold mine dearly to my soul-
such a crown shines when I'm down;
Something that can make you whole,
is a good friend, keeping around.

*****

This is my most favorite star-
sparkeling bright forever on end,
the charm that lives in the heart
of a caring and trusting friend. 


***** Poetic Fanatic *****

*This is To Tina*
*This is For Pat*
*This is For Ann*
