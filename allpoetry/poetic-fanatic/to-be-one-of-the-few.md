~To Be One Of The Few~

They may call me a fool
and surely laugh in my face.
They'll try treating me cruel,
like I'm just a living disgrace.

They continue in their pleasures,
evil, selfish deeds within and 'out,
never giving heed to the hurting,
that's not what their life is about.

They'll bow to their mighty dollar,
kissing it's feet until their death.
What will they hope for afterward,
when breathing out the last breath?

The Lord knows my every conflict.
I know He will not let me down,
I'll keep living and contending,
to some day, wear Eternity's crown.

Is it really such a hard burden,
living right between God and men?
Who'd dare think it not a bargain,
after the gift God chose to send?

My whole being, transformed again.
Fool's gold forfeits it's shine.
I've now become a changed man,
walking the way of a solitary line...

Yes Lord, I may stand alone to a world grown cold,
yet with You and the few... I shall surely stand.
