Looks Like I lost My Sauce

Looks like I lost my sauce,
I get visits, but less replies.

Looks like I've taken the toss,
by some who've swallowed lies.

Looks like my spice has left,
fortunately others might replace.

At least knowing some do visit,
keeps a slight smile on my face.

Looks like several want to get even,
by trying to make me come undone.

Looks like my 57 went up to Heaven,
I guess I'll just go with A1.
