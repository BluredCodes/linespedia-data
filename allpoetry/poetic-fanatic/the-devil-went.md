* To the tune of The Devil Went Down To Ga. by Charlie Daniels, 
A great musician and also now, a fellow Christian. 


The Devil Went...

The Devil went on the internet,
He was looking for souls to kill.
In shooting blind, 
He knew he could find
many to seal his deal.

First, to an atheist board
and tensions mounted hot,
The devils junk
was heavily trumped,
so I left that spot.

The Devil chases after me,
to another place I go,
but he sees so cleverly,
capturing many a weary soul.

Everytime I turn around,
that jerk is in my face,
is it anywhere out there,
the Devil has no place?

Hell's on the net,
run folks run,
The Devil's after,
everyone.

He doesn't play favorites,
don't you know-
The Devil savors
your sumptuous soul...

Well, when the Devil loses,
he will crawl back in,
that slippery, slimy, wretched place
he has always been.

For on the net you can bet,
He will not prevale,
but remember clear,
he's there and here
and he knows your weakness well.

Hell's on the net,
run folks run,
The Devil's after,
everyone.

He doesn't play favorites,
don't you know-
The Devil savors
your sumptuous soul...

So, heed this lil' warning,
before he comes your way,
Or you'll be in mourning,
all dogs shall have their day.

For the devil's out there
and you can surely bet,
He wants to quench his anger,
settle a simple debt.

Hell's on the net,
run folks run,
The Devil's after,
everyone.

He doesn't play favorites,
don't you know-
The Devil savors
your sumptuous soul...
