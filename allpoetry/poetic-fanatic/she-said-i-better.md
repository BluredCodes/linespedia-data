She said I Better!

I hear a song on the radio,
it really took me back-

To a moment, long time ago,
when I was thrown off track.

Head over hills, so in love,
just like a puppy child;

Seeking adventure, the world of,
times when I was kind of wild.

I told her that I loved her,
she said to me "you better".

I'd given anything I owned of,
just to try and get her.

Yes her reply made me sigh,
after ways I tried to prove-

I did not know that each time,
closer to her, I'd moved.

As we kissed, saying I love her
she told me again "you better".

Is this a joke she thought of
because the grin never left her.

I knew so clear, even of then
just... something of her way-

testing the depths I would go 
going on only what I might say.

We were then... kids in love, 
yet we've outgrown childish plays.

Still, I glow just thinking of,
those innocently sweetened days.
