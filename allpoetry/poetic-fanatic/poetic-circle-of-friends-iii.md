~Poetic Circle Of Friends Part III~

*Poets That Really Get To Me*



The great poetic circle of friends,

would seem almost, to never end.

it may take many poems to see,

the poetic circle is an infinity.



Let's start fresh with the newbies,

First off an artist named SaNsUi.

Let me say that this guy is super,

for wonderful images, he's a trooper-



A great poet and a very nice friend,

for you, he has many places to send.

My friend and I brought him to see,

the great family, here at allpoetry.



Take a gander at a poet called Hide,

he'll move you many ways inside;

I've enjoyed... and he'll bring you

pleasure to the eyes, a smile or two.



There's one poet, driftway, by name.

to be great, he can make the claim,

for I've enjoyed his work immensely

and scores show he does fine poetry.



Evangelina has a great style to share,

see it and you'll be glad you're there.

I read poems from Birchwood  today,

let me say, it brought pleasure my way.



Summer Dreams has brought me gleams,

I loved basking in her lovely writes,

with Moonmama, Granny Rosie and Del

we'll surely fill our bill of delights.



I think Bonnie Blue is kind of new,

she made me happy and she will you.

Speaking of fun, see Son Of The Sun,

I like the way his poems are done.



Indy Dom can bring 'em all home,

with a sensational flair of style.

I dropped by just to give him a try

and well... he sure made me smile.



Lately here are others I've met,

that have given me pleasure to know-

V O Reason, Big Head Bob, Poetic Pixie,

each of these take from their soul.



It looks, again, I've reached an end,

but don't worry if I'm missing any.

Before I'm done, I'll have everyone,

because of great poets, there are many.
