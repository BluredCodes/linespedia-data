Eagles...
~~~~~~~
marveled beings
~~~~~~~~~~~~
soaring the blue wonder,
~~~~~~~~~~~~~
climbing above all earthly things.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
Would I to fly so high kissed by the sky,
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
free myself, let my spirit rise
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
to brush the tops of trees.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~
To be like these
~~~~~~~~~~~
eagles.
~~~~~~



***** Poetic Fanatic *****


*Rictameter, 
A style, short as a poem, here is criteria for writing one- 

To be centered, of course! The syllables distributed goes as follows: 
in 9 lines down 2, 4, 6, 8, 10, 8, 6, 4 , 2 with same first and last word. 
It can rhyme or not for it's purpose is the power of words  Tommy
