*OK, The gig is up, I am also Penz-o-lot. I wanted a new persona to write my best pieces with and one for all my quick writes. Anyway, this is one that I wrote under this name. Tommy

Shall I pen 
you a dream? 
My lovely lady, 
My earthly angel, 
Dear, sweet love 
Where will you go? 
Where do you desire 
to take our adventure? 
Come, join with me and 
touch the teak of my wings, 
we shall soar into heavens. 
We'll have a cocktail while 
pleasantly resting on a cloud. 
Then when your breath returns, 
we'll top over the mountains, 
flying higher than the eagles, 
and we'll swoop down very low 
skimming the lakes so close, 
you'll feel the cool mist 
tickel you underneath, 
you could reach down 
and grab a fish or 
get a fresh drink. 
I'll lie you down, 
on a bank easily, 
to rest and when, 
you awake, you'll 
swear it was all 
a great dream. 

~Penz-o-lot~
