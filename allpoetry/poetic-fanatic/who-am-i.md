Who Am I?

She joked as I was tapping keys,
saying you are a poetry addict!
That is when I said Honey please,
just call me... poetic fanatic.

That's how this all got started,
she said I am one strange guy,
I laughed so hard that I farted,
one which put a tear in her eye.

Since then, I really got nutty,
hitting the net with a purpose.
I found many a poetic buddy,
deep people beneath the surface.

I got to be a poetry/web maniac
and I was loving every minute,
once we're that, we can't go back,
like I'd really want to end it.

Having such fun with everyone,
soaring deep inside of my soul,
I have hardly yet to be done,
for great poetry is my goal.

So, this fanatic's back at it,
hitting those poems left and right.
That's ok, it's not a bad habit,
just an all consuming delight.
