~My Sweet Rose~

She is... my sweet rose,

pretty flower like no other,

with such a lovely, sweet smell, 

truly I become, all overwhelmed.

Her lips, deep red like the petals.

Her petals are soft and fragile,

I always handle her tenderly.

And she blooms with love...

bursting out with vibrancy

radiance with great power,

as mine own lovely flower.

Sharon, my rose forever.
