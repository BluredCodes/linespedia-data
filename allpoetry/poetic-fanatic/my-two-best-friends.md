~My Two Best Friends~

They are always there, 
my two best friends.
I, only I can tear
unto the bad ends.

They give me courage,
standing so strong.
Whenever I need 'em,
they come on along.

I have their control,
how they will engage.
They have helped often,
when folks rattle my cage.

Each time, I get lessons,
in beating vulverability,
Not, to keep you guessing,
Meet the best friends for me;

One is called "patience"-
to help me wait things out.
Whenever I get frustrated,
patience is good to call about.

The other one is faith,
saying all will be fine.
Asking faith to stay with me,
helps ease my weary mind.

They want to work for you,
as most noble in their cause.
If anyone implements these,
you are safe from all your falls.

***** Poetic Fanatic *****

Thomas F. Crumpler © 2,001 Dec 17
