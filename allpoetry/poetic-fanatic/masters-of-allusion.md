* This is a brain teaser for you wizards out there. lol


They say poets are perceptive. Well, then I tried this before. So many
 people are quick to judge things without getting the full picture. 
Lesson for today, will you be one??? I hope that this practice might do
 good to show when someone tries to fool you, I've had my eyes on things. 

Would anyone really condemn this poem without seeing the key to this rhetorical mystery? PF

Masters Of Allusion, Lead Us To Greater . . . . . . . . . .? 

Poets make poetry full of such mystery 
Everyone thinks the answer's quite clear 
Really though it's done speculatively 
Commit to take the full picture, be near 
Exposing true means, the poet's schemes 
Put focus on what's possible to reveal 
Try closing your eyes, a way it seems 
Invite inner processes, feel the appeal 
Open your mind, messages ringing true 
No? Try again, maybe a different view?
