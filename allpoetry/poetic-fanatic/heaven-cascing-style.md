~Heaven~ - Cascing Style

Ahhh Heaven, what we'd do to get there.
Therein is every single good thought.
Thereafter we can see eternal ecstasy.

Heaven, where awaits Author of endless love,
Lovingly, to kiss His every daughter and son
and the sun will be love's warming glow.

Heaven, where music is universal serenity,
serenading our souls to melt in happiness,
serene sighs will be heard all around.

Heaven, where there will be no sadness,
saddened spirits will be revived in smiles,
smiling for one day that lasts forever.

What would we do to reach such a place?
God awaits to welcome the whole human race.
