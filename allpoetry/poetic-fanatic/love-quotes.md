~Love Quotes~

Love is... 
a heart taking flight in delight
when, giving pleasure to another.

Love is...
long aching in your hungry breast,
to hold her who gives restlessness.

Love is... 
life and limb put on the line,
for someone you feel worthy, at anytime.

Love is...
being completely clumsy in your brain,
when one runs through it like a train.

Love is...
to feel like you are nearly immortal,
when, touched by that significant other.

Love is...
whatever you've got, you would forfeit,
just for the consent to gladly give it. 

Love is...
a beautiful sunrise and there are people
who still insist on sleeping late.

Love is...
A merry-go-round that comes only one time,
you can either jump on or let it pass by.

Love is...
Saying I'll do anything for you, 
long as, you would want me to, 
so we... together, fly freely.
