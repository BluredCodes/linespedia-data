~A Christian's Perception~

~Poetically Speaking~

All were Awe-Struck by his ways;
If only to be there and hear Him!
Though few spoken, His words were
manifestations of ultimate wisdom.
Thousands came to hear Him speak,
as showing salvation and damnation
to each, but depending upon their
perception and reaction, determined
what dominated each heart's fire
thus, how judgment is commenced.
Some people were cut to their core-
as a chill raced through their bones,
His presence, a horrendous nightmare;
they thought... Silence and Destroy!
Other people were elated by His
message of hope, for their hearts 
grew wings, flying straight to heaven.
Who was this man that more than millions
today, know as, "He is called God".
