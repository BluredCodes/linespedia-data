~My Friend~

My friend,
knows me so well-
She has a way to tell
when I am in such, dier need.
The bestest ever of any true friend,
goodness and kindness are her way,
she can brighten my day,
just by being,
my friend.


***** Poetic Fanatic ****



This poem is short, but this is the way the form works. 
Ask yourself, does it cover good ground? If so.... 
I just learned this from Tina Brannon- here is how it goes 
(the rules) 
*Rictameter: syllables distributed in 9 lines as 2, 4, 6, 8, 10, 8, 6, 4 , 2 
The last syllables being the same words you began with*Most already know who this is about.
