~Thank You Darling~

It was years long ago,
that you revived my soul.
My feelings for you flew
as love powerfully grew.

I gave something that day,
to you, my sun-shiny ray-
Now I want to thank you,
in returning to be true-

For it was my whole heart, 
I'd given up for you dear.
I knew it was the start,
one way to be sincere.

So many different ways 
you might have taken it,
by refusing, 
cruel abusing 
or just misusing;

You could have
tattered,
battered
or shattered it...
But you had chosen not.

Like I expected, 
you respected in,
nourishing,
relishing ,
cherishing,
to be flourishing and
I'm thankful such a lot.

Honey, For only you,
was it ever to be given,
no other woman could do,
love is zestfully living.

With our very first time,
I shared with you all of me.
You'll be glad to know now,
I've kept yours so carefully.

Two hearts in exchange,
whoever would have thought,
both of us could keep going,
with the covenant we brought.

I am just for you 
and you are for me,
together we'll always be,
Heavenly happy harmony.

** Poetic Fanatic **
