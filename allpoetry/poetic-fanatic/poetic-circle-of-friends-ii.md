~Poetic Circle Of Friends Part II~

It was such a pleasure, my gift to you all.
In, first of my sequels, I made good ground,
So many great poets knock me off, the wall.
But here are more greats that I have found.

These are new friends that I'm loving reads.
Let me enlighten you to quite a few leads.
First, there's Veelejot who is just so cool,
a writer I've enjoyed, he's quite a jewel.

Then there is Gozling, who's a bit new to me,
I can't believe I've missed such a fine art,
she's been here a while, we've met recently.
not only stylish, but she'll touch your heart.

Want something Lovely, then seek Sunny Dudley-
another imagery writer of fascinating words.
Jeremy, The Black Sheep has his way, surely,
for he can bring out readers by the herds.

Clarity is new and he'll will intrique you,
he's most intelligent, great writer to boot.
Immortal Soul and Zero, are quite the heroes
and Son Of The Sun has me dancing to his flute.

Also we have, Richard J. Sereday, you can know,
one of the elite I've not seen in quite a time,
even his own name shows he has the poetic glow,
a master of images, metaphor, rhythm and rhyme.

One poet I know to mention, I owe him a debt...
loved by many, and he's built up a good name-
Any Mouse In The House will charm you, I'd bet,
shooting arrows of humor and he's a good aim-

1st Poet is another wonderful, must see writer,
shall I say more, see what alias he goes by?
And... Fred is my awe inspirational favorite,
I know he'll take or put a tear... your eye.

Another good rhymer when you look to find her,
is Buzby, why not buzz bye and give her a shot?
If you're looking for activity, see she she,
writes from the heart and she sure has a lot.

If you like perfection, turn Sharon's direction,
such amazing form and with a clean writing style.
Someone new, Kiew (KI-U-) By pronounciation,
will surely have you captivated, for a while.

There is a poet by the name of Amber Dawn,
she will surely bring your big smiles on.
Lisann is new, of life's good, she writes
And the golden pen Mary, is full of delights.

If your world is pouring boulders and hail,
see these fine ones to brighten your heart up-
There's Sandy and hopes for angelic wings,
one or both will surely fill over your cup.

Here are poets and their ratings will show-
they have the secret to getting attention;
Quietly Remembering, Birsky and Rodney know
what it takes to keep you in suspension.

More Great poet names, I've noticed needing,
special honor for the energy they expend,
Connor, Aliana, Infamous, Del And R Merwin, 
all of these have much pleasure to extend.

So many more poetic names are at all poetry,
that deserve our many visits, from everyone.
Please see first poem if yours is missing,
and don't fret, part Three's soon to be done.
