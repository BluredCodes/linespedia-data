~Our Dreams~

with each other...

are visions of heaven.

My lovely, you are, sweetest bliss,

you and I Dear, couldn't ever resist

our heart's yearning, forever burning,

for we are completely one,

smiling, singing...

our dreams.

***** Poetic Fanatic *****


Another rictameter, please consider that if you know what they are. PF
