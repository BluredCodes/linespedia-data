My Prayer

Lord, keep me in humility,
to recall, I am just a man.

Give me the needed energy
to change whatever I can.

Forgive me when I fail Thee.
Help me always stand strong,

Ceasing Never, to love You
all, my life, the years long.

Teach me how to reach the,
ones who know not Your way.

Make me shine by hands of Thine,
Lighting hope for love every day.

Oposers say that I need more humility. I wrote this on July of last year. It is my sincere desire. I'm just sorry that my rivals can't see the real me... Tommy
