Fear Not...

Fear not, my little one,
I will always be with you.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Do not worry, precious child,
I'll let no harm get through. 
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Keep your trust, I am here,
anytime you need my care.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

There's no place of existence,
that my eyes are not aware.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Show me your loyal devotion,
Stand strong in your race-
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

In spite of every commotion,
someday, you'll see my face.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Fear not my weary sheep,
I promise, great happiness.
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

Let not your heart weep,
For you know who this is.
