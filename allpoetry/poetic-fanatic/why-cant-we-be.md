Friends, this is a tribute, my last try for one person, I hope he can see my sincerity. Also, I hope everyone can just get along! It would be nice, right? 

Tommy {a poetic bro... here, I hope}




Why can't we be
a fam-i-ly 
at our home, 
All Poetry?

A sis was sick, 
we showed we cared 
for so much grief, 
we hope, we spared.

She's A OK,
I'm glad for her,
now it's time,
with you, confer.

Things I've seen,
pains I've felt,
bad cards played,
my hand dealt.

I'm for her,
they're for him,
he's for you.
you're for them

Friends and foes,
love and hate,
stop the blows,
before too late.

Poetic kin is
what we are,
so take my hand,
we'll go far.

Great and small
take in stride,
we are all,
the same inside.

Deep or dumb,
no walls build,
rule of thumb-
we'll still yield.

To rate or not,
it's up to you,
but of this thought,
what if "your shoe"?

Return oh fun,
of where you've gone,
so we each sing,
our poetic song.

Why can't we be,
a fam-i-ly
here, our home,
All Poetry?
