~Why I Leave My Name~

*Written for all poetic friends*

I seek a new friend
in every crack or cranny.

There are poets around,
with good hearts, of plenty.

You may think I'm strange,
to leave my name as much-

Truth of this be told,
I like the personal touch.

Don't think evil of my ego,
I personalize my replies.

A big dreamer like I,
can see great stars in my eyes.

I want to grow, learn and fly,
so high by knowing you.

So when you wonder why,
I leave... "Tommy", 
it's an honor, I give due.
