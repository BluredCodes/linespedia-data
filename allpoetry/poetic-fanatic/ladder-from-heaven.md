(*Jacob's Ladder Was Jesus Christ*)

~Ladder From Heaven~

He lowered a great ladder from Heaven,
His own Son, the gift He had brung.
All that He asks for is any effort,
each of us climbing, rung by rung.

##############################

Whenever you reach your hand to start,
God will smile, you'll feel love glow.
He'll transform the reins of your heart,
in maturity of his spirit, you'll grow.

##############################

He lowered his great ladder from Heaven,
piercing big clouds of doubt and despair.
He desires to see what we will give Him-
Shall we go on our way like we don't care?

##############################

No need to worry if you might slip,
He'll catch you by graces of his hand.
Contend towards this one way trip,
more, by and by, you will understand.

##############################

He lowered his great ladder from Heaven.
He's waiting for us to begin our quest.
He knows how simple is of our existence
and He offers what He knows is best.

##############################

When this golden ladder was extended,
God showed how dear we were in his book.
He waits patiently before life has ended,
be wise and try giving, one more look.

##############################

He lowered his great ladder from Heaven.
There is no other way we can reach Him.
This present is truly a gift to treasure,
think about this when life seems grim.

##############################

There are many carnival rides down here,
life has its way, often to be unfair,
but be strong, be noble and never fear,
He has an awesome place for us up there.
