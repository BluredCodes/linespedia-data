~To See the Dancer Dance Again~

To see the dancer, dance again,
in the rain, when storms come in,
to see her awe the lightning show,
lighting her eyes, and her face aglow-

To see her fly through clouds once more,
to see her smile and her poetry soar,
Oh Pat! What will your friends do,
to show how much, we care for you?

We can keep tabs, how your life's going,
lift you up to keep your pen flowing.
But it's more than poetics, for we care,
and I'm saying, call and I'll be there.
