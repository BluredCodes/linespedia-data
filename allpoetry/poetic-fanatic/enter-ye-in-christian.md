* Welcome and Enter Ye In *

This is your brand new home.

Come inside, my dear friend.

Feel free to look and roam.

^~*~^~*~^~*~^~*~^~*~^~*~^~*~^

Your eyes will never tire,

the glorious things to see.

Nothing here can ever expire;

You have an immortal body.

^~*~^~*~^~*~^~*~^~*~^~*~^~*~^

This is the ultimate place.

You are ever yet to be bored.

Forever shining in your face

is the presence of your Lord.

^~*~^~*~^~*~^~*~^~*~^~*~^~*~^

The epitome of blissful beauty,

great treasures, I have for you

for not ceasing to do your duty,

even when it was so tough to do.

^~*~^~*~^~*~^~*~^~*~^~*~^~*~^

You've given up on many things

for your priviledge to do good.

You're beyond realms of dreams;

Welcome to my great brotherhood.

^~*~^~*~^~*~^~*~^~*~^~*~^~*~^

You've kept your faith in my grace

and your heart remained so true.

So now, enjoy, be happy evermore.

This is Heaven, all...  for you.

^~*~^~*~^~*~^~*~^~*~^~*~^~*~^
