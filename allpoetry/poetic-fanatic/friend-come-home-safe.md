~Best Friend, Come Home Safe~



Well, the time has come,

something that you must do-

Breaking from chains of home

and the internet net too.



You will be missed around here,

by me and so many friends.

But the memories will linger

making time move like the winds.



So, have a great time on shore,

don't look back and never fear.

The time will swoop right over,

before you know, you'll be here.



Here's wishing, a big Bon Voyage!

Have a wonderful time, your retreat.

God speed to a very safe return,

when again, here... we will meet.



Ok OK, it's a quickie, but Tina... I wish you the best

time on your get-a-way for you, Juan and everyone. :0)

Take care, we love ya an we'll be here when you get back!
