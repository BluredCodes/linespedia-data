~Poetics Circle Of Friends- Pt I~

I've been here for quite a long time,
with masters of the meaning and rhyme.
Yet, something greater, I have found,
beauty of harmony, floats all around.

Never anywhere I have known to exists,
wonderful journeys, for my poetic bliss.
This place truly has all that I need,
harboring wonderful poets, great indeed.

Without biasm said for their quality,
a tribute to those who have touched me,
For their writings speak of their way,
each master in poetic work and play.

This goes deeper than, any poetic style,
it's thought and soul, making me smile.
For yes, though poetry is a great part,
this is another way they reach my heart.

There's New Xena, Lovely lady of mine,
Sharon is her name and she blows my mind.
She writes to me, maybe a friend or two,
claiming as no poet but love comes through.

She touches deeply, to me, her words,
lovely, sweet and pure, like the songbirds.
From her core she releases of her fire,
she'll always be my greatest, to inspire-

Tinasheart- poetesss, bestest friend to me
and there's such beauty in her pure poetry.
She's also in love and loves telling of,
her husband, friends and our great God above-

AnnD is quite handy, penning, perfecto form,
thoughts of love and life to keep us warm.
Pat's another super, Storm Dancer's her name.   
for friends, it's true, both are great gain- 

Tina Brannon is a fav who knows her stuff,
she used to write how life has been so tough,
but she has such a wonderful way also to write,
of sunsets, star beams and beautiful moonlight-

There is Nell, a growing poet and a friend,
when it comes to learning more, she has no end.
She's a spirit lifter and has a hunger to be,
best poetry writer soon, here at allpoetry-

Sidewinder, is the word grinder you must see,
who uses heart in the art of his poetry.
Highway Mike is liked for his vision clear
and MB Mike, is quite a delight, residing here-

BluPoetess has often impressed, imagery galore
Rubee has such beauty, words true and pure.
WishOnMars shines with the stars, what I see,
Ice Rose, Daddy's girl is growing in her poetry-

Manic Muse is not new news for she is prime,
banging out great creations all of the time.
Sheer poetry is a writer of sweet serenity,
with lovely decorations, she's tops to see-

One guy here, named Nam, an interesting mind,
impresses me with brilliant poetry so fine,
Though some here thinks he might be tough,
his work is beautiful, really great stuff-

Lurie is all well known as another fav.
She sure has flown to get her every rave.
Dreamweaver's a magic believer who can show,
a message of depthness to continually flow-

Webdreamer, story teller, visuals so mellow
as a friend too, he's quite a nice fellow.
Andy Harding has sweet words, beauty from a guy,
with Repomen and BD Grey, they'll make you sigh-

Kathy- Angel Eyes will fill with surprise,
when her works put smiles right in your eyes.
Deb- In Trouble will surely double your fun,
like reading a great rhyme? She's the one-

There's Maureen, very good and poetic friend
and Tigerlilly who has good ideas to send.
Broken worthless surely has her purpose,
of love, life and loss, she sees the surface. 

Dianes is one to take truly from of her soul,
Chasing The Day has a way to move us all.
BM Jen, writes with a grin, of her style,
a poet, we know it, she'll make you smile-

Coyote Moon and CWM, Two poets I like visiting,
Idon'tknowhow and Death Goddess know their pen.
Shelly -Vampire girl, another poet who flies
and Heinzs writes great words to mesmerize-

There's treasie, very nice, I like to read
and Sprite who has poetic ability indeed.
Gunslinger- a talent you should give a look,
along with slekky and a friend, yellowbook-

Chris Kramer, Mysty Saint, Sweet Brother do
great writes that I've enjoyed reading too.
Then Pilo and Embo, are good poets I know,
Nauticalmyst is the finest, name's Michael-

There's Foretold Events who makes sense,
what might be possible, coming days
and Tamara has such a fire, to tell of...
sweet victory in God's saving grace-

Highland Dreams is another poet of high caliber,
with NYJohn loved by many, they'll make you stir.
Cinara is another writer, fine pleasure to see
and wornways really at times just amazed me.

Also we have Lone Poet And desdemona Lee,
two good writers really, treats to see.
There's Dilion, one in a million, to find,
Gecko, Gozling, W. Butterfly and Joy Is Mine. 

Granpappy can make 'em happy, using his way,
he's loved by many and has a lot to say.
Karen- Solitary Tears shares life too clear,
tears of joy and pain, you'll find her here-

Currently off of the top of my tired head, 
here are some more known names that lead-
Ms. Sensual, Singer, Noelani, Dragon Lady,
Big Head Bob and Ghost Writer Ken and Kitty.

Molassis, Melissa writes, great inspiration,
Go_d_d and AntMonkey are a zany vacation.
Kiss the Sky flies so high, any direction
and AA is a wiz nearly with perfection.

As I said before, these are poets I love,
reading and knowing, but there's more of
poetic talent and people to meet here,
At allpoetry, board it's crystal clear...

Allpoetry is a great poetic haven to be,
Thanks Kevin, webmaster and writer of poetry.
