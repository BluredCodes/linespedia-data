~Speaking Entities~

Time...
Use me wisely,
Lest I slip through your hands,
like the tiny grains of the sand.


Faith...
Have hope in me,
let it grow and flow,
then my power, you will know.


Energy...
Use me or lose me,
For many beings can see,
I'll get sucked back into infinity.


Love...
Give respect, not neglect  
or I'll come to a waste
and nothing like me can be replaced.


Life...
Make good choices,
learn to love me,
or sorrow sadly, you'll surely see.

Fate...
don't dare tempt me,
I'll let you go by for a while,
yet you're coming closer, the last mile.
