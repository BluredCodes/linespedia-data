Friends...
We need
all there are
Friends lift your life,
they can be a bucket to hold your tears
and a well weaved net to catch all your falls
sparing much grief
giving you
each day
hope.

Friends...
can light,
those tough times
when swept by gloom
as you feel like dirt, dragged by a old broom,
a good friend will jump to save that bad day
keeping you cheered,
smile a ray,
to give...
peace.

Friends...
make smoothe,
those bumpy
rough highways of life.
A good friend makes a long, dull trip, it's best
especially when the journey's restless.
Such a person is
to treat well,
always,
friends.
