*Something I can ask anyone whoever thought that just 
maybe, There Is A God.

Of course, I and other Christians have no doubt.

Though question marks are the proper punctuation, these
inquiries are rhetorical. PF

What If...

What If God was silent, all of your days,
not once did He ever show to you, Him.

What if evil seemed to win in every place,
our world's state would look terribly grim.

What if every single person you know doubts,
that God has allowed these things as a test.

What if others you know just gave up on,
the fight for living right, would you rest.

What if all people you love, hated your belief,
would you still stand strong, what you know.

What if, no where in sight is there relief,
is it enough to waver convictions in your soul.

What if the choice of men is steal and kill,
would you be tempted to try surviving that way.

What if there is a slim possibility for you,
Heaven is true and God is watching you today.

What if God is holding His hopes, you keep,
in your heart burning hot, your love for Him.

Then friend, Believe in your reward, He speaks, 
prove powers of faith to doubters, all of them.

***** Poetic Fanatic *****
