* * * My Good Friend * * *

I've got this friend who is always there.
One who persists for they continually care.
With deep concern such kindness is shown,
this friend's one of the best, I have known.

~@~@~@~@~@~@~@~@~@~@~@~

I Wonder from whence my good friend came,
though others difer, she stays the same.
Thank you dear friend, being nice and true.
Whatever did I do, getting one like you?

~@~@~@~@~@~@~@~@~@~@~@~

When I get down and feeling kind of blue,
my good friend knows just what to do-
It may be a card sent for smiles my way
or other things that will brighten my day.

~@~@~@~@~@~@~@~@~@~@~@~

I hope through the ages our strength remains,
cheering through the joys, consoling the pains.
Well, my good friend I'm always here for you, 
because you were there helping me, get through.

~@~@~@~@~@~@~@~@~@~@~@~
