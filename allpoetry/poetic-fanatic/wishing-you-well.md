* For Jen, We Wish You Well! :0) Tommy


~Wishing You Well~

She's learn to laugh
through her pain,
making clowns dance,
again and again.

She's cheered up many,
with a joke or two,
but now she depends
on each of you.

She's facing trouble,
but she'll be alright,
let's let her know,
Her smiles still bright.

With love in her heart,
that has touched us all,
she's given us hope,
let every doubt fall.

Good luck to you Jen,
Godspeed to return,
when you come again,
more friends will yearn-

to know you much better,
and see your bright light,
that brings sunny skies,
from the edge of night.

We wish you our best,
go in being bold,
for we can't rest,
your stories untold.

Here's to you Jen,
that best we can do,
come back quickly again,
we're rooting for you! :0)
