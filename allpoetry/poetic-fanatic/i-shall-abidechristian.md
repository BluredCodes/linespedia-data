I Shall Abide- Christian Poem!

*Inspired of Saint Paul~ Ph 4, 13 "I can do all things through Christ, 
who strengthens me."

Life's tornadoes rage, barreling down
Adversaries, many may be lurking around
My state at times, feeling not so sound,
In Thee, I shall abide.


Grief may strike, it's relentless pain
My name and character, slung and slain'
Evil ways seem often to reign,
In Thee, I shall abide.


My heart could be torn and my spirit, low
I'll never know when there comes a blow
But this is sure, we reap what we sow,
In Thee, I shall abide.

Poverty and calamity might swamp me o'er
I may hate things I've never seen before
Death's cold breath could knock at my door,
In Thee, I shall abide.

***** Poetic Fanatic *****
