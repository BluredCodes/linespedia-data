*Another Christian Based Poem. PF

You left your glory encompassed throne,
bringing peaceful love, the sake of man's,
tredding dusty trails, sad and alone,
saving hateful hearts and careless hands;

Letting go, Heaven's light to land of night,
You saw the betterment, a remnant of bands,
knowing many would deny your God given right,
to rescue grateful hearts and feeble hands.

The hope You have, my soul has delight.
Your faith touches and it always stands.
The world will change by your great might,
granting gracious hearts and helping hands.

Oh, how my future now looks so bright!
I'm a debtor as your great love commands.
In departing earth as my spirit takes flight,
I'll look, your loving heart and saving hands.

***** Poetic Fanatic *****
