~You Lord~

The fire for fighting
The flow of loving
the force of righting
the falling of them,
who see not You Lord.

The test of trying
temperance of turmoil
truth over lying
on every soil...
Let us see You Lord.

The love of laboring
the longing for light
the lips savoring
Your great might...
understand You Lord.

These, I pray we all
use with one accord,
the great and the small
Come to find You Lord.
