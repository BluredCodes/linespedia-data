~That's The Way It Is~

So many troubles have come,
and tried to rip us apart.
But still I have your home,
for dwelling only in my heart.

You are all the world to me,
every thrill, desire and need.
What it takes to make you believe,
I'll go any lengths to succeed;

You're my Queen, the song in my soul,
our love-making is most sublime-
When with you I lose all control,
it happens each and every time.

Though our union is always great,
there's much more present with us.
As my best friend and my soul's mate,
you are the one woman, I entrust.

Because simply stated, it is true,
no one else would I think to try,
it's you Dear, just and only you-
that makes my spirit rise so high.

So remember if ever in any doubt,
you are everything sacred to me-
I can not fathom to live without,
holding you by my side endlessly.

***** Poetic Fanatic *****
