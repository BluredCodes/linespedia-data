~Pressing Ahead~

I'm Pressing ahead
There's nothing behind
I have much better
to do with my mind

Resolution
has lifted my heart
For forgiveness
makes me a new start


{I'm Pressing ahead
Pres-sing ahead
No more walking
dark paths of dread
The sun is shining,
behind my every track
I'm pressing ahead
and I'm not looking back}

I'm Pressing ahead
Come whatever may
Yesterday is over
But I've got today
All is refreshed
Completely new
Just like the early
morning sweet dew

{Chorus}

I'm Pressing ahead
No storms are above
The sun is gleaming
God's wonderful love
The birds are singing
Up in every tree
And my soul's ringing,
A sweet harmony.

{Chorus- Repeat}
