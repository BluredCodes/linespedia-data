~Growing~

all of us are.

Strength comes of hills conquered,

continuing to frequently fight,  cover...

sins, scars, stains and stones, we stumble over.

Each time, we're stronger for longer.

Faith keeps showing, flowing,

as we keep on

growing.

***** Poetic Fanatic *****
