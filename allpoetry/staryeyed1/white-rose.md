title: "white rose"

beautiful
intricate rose
when crushed
leaves silky petals
to float to the ground
sweet scents bring memories
of slow grins
and warm companionship
those watching
with brilliant smiles
lay down to rest
and cast their worries aside
before the last petal
stirs the dust
