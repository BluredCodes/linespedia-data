Challenge Description: Write about being trapped in a small, dark enclosed space. I want to feel the desperation and fear! Weather you write about a physical confinement or a mental one is up to you. Try to create the image and emotion I have described, but you DO NOT have to use the exact words. I DO want you to add the word prisoner in either the title and/or the body of your poem. Have fun and good luck! (45 points)
Images: 'Gray haze within the blackness'
Emotions: 'despairing solitude'
---------------------------------------------

awake i've become 
sanity i've been losing 
no matter how many times i try to open my eyes 
i forever see this inky blackness 
its sounds have been assaulting me continually 
louder and louder as never before 
its smell overwhelms my mind preventing recognition 
and i cry out for the light 
the white sweet brightness to shine upon my world 
i scream and claw for the end to this endless night 
they all hear my pain 
every wall has felt my craze 
and yet they refuse to offer help 
i've since lost all track of time 
this voice has been screaming for all of eternity 
my lungs can no longer handle this 
collapsing in, falling down 
suspended in the midst of a starless galaxy 
i've been captured in this shroud of blindness
