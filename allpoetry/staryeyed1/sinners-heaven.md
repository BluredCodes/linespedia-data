---
title: "Sinner's Heaven"
writer: "StaryEyed1"

if touch could only truely convey the passion of the moment,
and lust could only satisfy our unspoken needs,
where would we be now?
oh, we'd be in a sinner's heaven,
just hide ourselves away and continue to indulge,
with no world to seperate us or to provide its own recourse,
gliding in the darkness that erases all consequence,
if only such short term pleasures could ever endure,
i could let myself drown
