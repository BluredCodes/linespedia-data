title: "cold waters with hot steam"

she looks at he
glances away when he looks at she
then we'll play this game over
while i run into a cold shower
and you and i will walk to the harbor
to remenise about the dreams we borrowed
how we could never make them real
just to see if there was something there
a tiny door to success
we just happened to miss
then we'll regret your past together
and together we'll both regret mine
in the ackward silence he looks at she
while she looks at he
as i say i don't want it this way
he'll dive into the cold water
and we'll pretend it didn't happen
you'll say there was never anything there
so waves will continue to break
then the time soon comes to steal us away
to drop you in your empty home
and to leave me in my empty life
