i gave up something
so that i might gain
selfish it seems
was it really worth losing?
i know
i had been falling
apart at the seams
but for all my instincts
who knew
what the future had held
the silence i endure
seems such a heavy price
for an action so necessary
"i regret..."
what a funny little phrase
