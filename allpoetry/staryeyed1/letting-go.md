title: "letting go"
writer: "StaryEyed1"

well i've been trying again
strange you say
i gave up so long ago
but its started over now
been needing to leave
these old thoughts behind
just keep glancing down
at the hole in my chest
ol' heart's still in the trash
though ive come so far
i can't pick it up again
so im starting anew
with emptiness to comfort me
bleeding from these depths
lost and alone
time heals all wounds
some deeper than others
just so tired of being afraid
the pain seems more inviting
than my endless fear
so wisk away
wisk away dear past
release your grasp
then i'll float away
on my cloud of emptiness
but i'll be trying
trying so hard to heal again
