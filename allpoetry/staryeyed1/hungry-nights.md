---
title: "Hungry Nights"
writer: "StaryEyed1"
slug: "hungry-nights"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/29359-Hungry-Nights-by-StaryEyed1"
---

these are the times of loneliness
just the type of moments
when human desires cry hungrily
aching to fill the emptiness
licking your lips with the need
quietly you hunt the city's forests
the want becoming overwhelming
the ache to be satisfied ever growing
n'er do you care for the prey
once you've had a taste
this becomes your ecstasy
voices urging you on become louder
scaling ever higher
as your head ceases its crying
for conscience has quieted to silence
this night is for the darkness
