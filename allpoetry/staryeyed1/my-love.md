what life have I drawn myself into
drowning forever in eyes
that know me far better than I know myself
falling and floating all at once
light as a feather
light as his carress

what madness is this
that compels me to want more
to find myself cherishing every moment
forgetting all else but his voice
the breath he exhales
smells as sweet as the finest perfume

what a life I have been contemplating
everytime he tells me how deeply he cares
the words twisting around my heart
warm as a baby's blanket
melting the ice at the very core

what a love I have fallen intoI love you Kosetsu
