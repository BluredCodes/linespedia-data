---
title: "leave it to the gods"
writer: "StaryEyed1"
slug: "leave-it-to-the-gods"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/29937-leave-it-to-the-gods-by-StaryEyed1"

it will leave you
forlorn and hopeless
when a realization hits
that you try to coax
the impossible dream
from a grain of sand
flowers bloom from seeds
grains of sand can only
fuel sandstorms
such is your faith
the lies of gods
leave us in turmoil
