---
title: "tricks and lies"
writer: "StaryEyed1"
slug: "tricks-and-lies"
source: "AllPoetry"

you've said you care
and left me wondering
if its really true
cause your back is turned
always too soon
to catch my tears
breaking through
this stoic facade
tearing me up inside
though you promised not to
how can i always be here
the strong silent one
stopped at this point
cause im too weak
to just let it go
always disappointing me
the hope i always look for
just dies away
and to what purpose
does it serve?
all i hear
in all you've told
is lie...
because it seems
that it is your talent
to deceive me
forever will be my memory
nightmares of endless trickery
on the dreams
of this innocent little fool
