i've moved on but his face still remains
let go yet the memories still return to taunt
sometimes i can't distinguish between feelings
remembered or present, which is which?
i cared once but he has forgotten
the life we used to share
and the taste of our lips melding into one
i could never return the hurt
of knowing the love i had would never be shared
now its been so long
since he last looked me in the eye
he's found the one i could never hope to bei know the quality of this poem isn't really that great. but its what i was feeling today.
