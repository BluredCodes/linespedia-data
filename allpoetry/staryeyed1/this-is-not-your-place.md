---
title: "this is not your place"
writer: "StaryEyed1"

in the middle of the crowd
still feeling all alone
cry the bloody pain
scream the pulsating cry
squeeze the eyes shut
shake your head side to side
move amongst the madness
pretend you belong
knowing all the time
this just isn't right
then feel the knife
as it presses against your chest
the unexpected rocks
that crash into your head
neither leaves marks you can see
watch always for the life
around the other corner
every time you run into someone new
yet never find anyone to talk to
