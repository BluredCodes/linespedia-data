when you looked again
you thought there was something new
it's got to be something different
hidden in the texture
speckled over in flashy color
crouching slyly in the shadows
but what you see is something true
shining brighter than the spotlights
more precious than diamonds
rising high above it all
and you just thought
hey it must have been hidden from public view
just now fallen out of its caseing
this camoflauged fountain of understanding
but it's lain here unnoticed all along
a sacred trueth gathering dust
you now have what you've been looking for
