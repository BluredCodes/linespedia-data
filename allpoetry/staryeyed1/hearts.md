title: "hearts"

its a frightened heart
that can't see beyond the fear
to meet the eyes that hold it dear


its a cold heart
that purposely walks away
when those eyes shed their tears


its a kind heart
that will always return
to give a comforting shoulder


its a lonely heart
that pines for a friendly voice
to echo through the painful silence


and its my heart
that has run through this cycle
far too often
