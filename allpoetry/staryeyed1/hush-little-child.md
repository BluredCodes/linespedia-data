title: "hush little child"
writer: "StaryEyed1"

here inside the child lies
scribbling with the rainbow
on the walls of her box
to pass the time
outside the world screams by
and her older twin cries
wishing for the times
when she lived inside
in the mind's world
they live on divided
what's this world coming to
when the inner child
lives behind a lock
here we leave our children
in a box of rainbows and dreams
maybe one day we'll join in
