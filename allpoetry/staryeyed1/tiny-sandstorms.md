title: "Tiny Sandstorms"
writer: "StaryEyed1"

traveling through sandstorms
her mind's been wandering
again...
blissful thoughts of him
memories laced with sugar
cracking a giddy grin
eyes shut
arms reaching
waiting for the warmth
of an invisible embrace
her imaginary perfection
in all his blue eyed grace
reflecting the burning
flaming passion in his heart
for this single imperfection
a dream that wouldnt last
her cold reality
tastes of bittersweet tears
a salty desert mixed with mud
the grime of her naked hurt
manifested in shameless flaunts
smears of the innocence
the purity she once held
filled with jealousy
she shatters it again and again
endlessly punishing herself
for her unworthiness of him
