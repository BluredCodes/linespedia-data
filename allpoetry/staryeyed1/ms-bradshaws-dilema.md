goodbye ms. bradshaw
it seems we've found you rather lacking as of late
that waist has become too big
and your hair wont ever stay straight
i'm sorry you'll never fit the description
of all you once were ever again
my appologies, for you're now in breach of contract
we can't allow you to come around here any more
your fans expect a spring in your step
they swarm around for a glint of joy in your eyes
these you just don't provide anymore
do you understand?
we can no longer pay your brother's treatment
no longer pay your appartment rent
nor care for your child support costs
you've become too old for our company
you'll just have to spend what you have left wisely now
good day ms. bradshaw
