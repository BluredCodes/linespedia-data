---
title: "the Ying and the Yang"
writer: "StaryEyed1"
slug: "the-ying-and-the-yang"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/40609-the-Ying-and-the-Yang-by-StaryEyed1"
---

in the wars between light and dark

darkness under the name of desire

always claims the night

twisting light from heavenly stars

into peeping pinpoints on hidden acts

as i [the dark] stalk towards one whom i desire

you, my one and only light

in my vision the stars cling to you [the light]

filling my eyes with such a purity

as i had never hoped to hold

an excitment builds in me the closer i come

to the goal [your light] burning behind those eyes

in the last moment, i [darkness]

reach out to steal your light

but you thwart my dark intentions

with strong arms and loving eyes, i am enraptured

as you cling to me [dark] and i cling to you [light]

i, with nothing to share but darkness

show you how to release

and you, with nothing to share but light

teach me how to love

leaving neither the same as they were before

we become two halves of a whole
