title: "immaculate deceptions"

It’s a dark void, this cold world around us
It’s a black cape glittered with stars, that darkens the moonlight
But it covers us, tangled together as if blanketed in dreams
Nothing else matters, so what do we care?
What more could we ask, but to be left in our own pocket of time
To be left to our own deceptions with out its restrictions
I could pretend to be your world, and you could persuade me that you are mine
For all of time, we could be the best pair of actors the world would never see
But would it suffice?
