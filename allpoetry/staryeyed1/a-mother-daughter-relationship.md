Responsibility links us together
Eternally blood tied
As mother and daughter
Still wanting you to care
When I've shoved you away
Maybe just to try
Don't give up on me!
Tears flood our coexistence
Yours and mine
Do you cry for me?
I cry from you
Is too much being expected
Clueless you've remained
We're really this different?
Maybe I'm not the only one
Who's pushing away
