---
title: "for you"
writer: "StaryEyed1"

she couldn't count the lucky stars
they were blurry to her eyes
she couldn't take a breath
for fear the moment would disappear
she couldn't look away
from the gaze that draws her near
she couldn't forget that laughter
nor your silly dorky grin
she couldn't leave without something
without losing her heart to a final kiss
she couldn't want for more in your arms
listening to your tender heart beat
she couldn't wait for you to return
patience is a hard thing
she couldn't stop thinking of you
just kept wishing you were near
she couldn't ever let you go
never to stop loving you
