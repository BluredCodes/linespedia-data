---
title: "Only of You"
writer: "StaryEyed1"
slug: "only-of-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/27297-Only-of-You-by-StaryEyed1"
---

take a deep breath

then slowly breathe it all out

look down into the unending fog below

it beckons you to take the plunge

blink not once... but twice

what you see isn't all that's there

one... its the ocean

two... its the mountain

weaving all around the illusion

is a sweet breeze

a calm awakening overshadowed

by the forboding clouds of memories

not the kind you'd like

to slip under your pillow at night

look away... at the sky

return your gaze to vastness below

squint all you want

you'll never hope

to catch what's truely there

stop and stare, these eyes see

you, through you

knowing you beyond knowledge

staring back you'll find yourself

falling forwards just to reach those eyes

now drown
