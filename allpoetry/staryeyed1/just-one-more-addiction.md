---
title: "just one more addiction"
writer: "StaryEyed1"
slug: "just-one-more-addiction"
source: "AllPoetry"

just one last pill
just another drink
thats all he craves to have
just one last long night
given up to pleasure
never to be given up

the addiction invoked inside
addicts him to more and more
cravings turning into pain
and he's losing self control
the pleasure and the ecstacy
he can't give it up
he's always wanting more

just one more day
left unremembered
just another person
head bowed from disaster
just another craving
just one more pill
to be added to the growing list

in this neverending cycle
he can only come to pain
the twitching comes to claim him
and the glazed look in his eyes
becomes devoid of life
too stoned to hold on
in his mind he still crys

just one last pill
just another drink
thats all i crave to have
just one last long night
given up to pleasure
i'll give it up someday
