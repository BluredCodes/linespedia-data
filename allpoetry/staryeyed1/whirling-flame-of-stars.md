---
title: "Whirling Flame of Stars"
writer: "StaryEyed1"

silently protesting
he wisks her away
with questionable intentions
to a place of secrecy
deep within her being
calling forth primeval emotions
spiraling her into
the void of her universe
stars of concious thought
cry to flee back to earth
disregarding their wisdom
she clings to him
flowing ever closer to oblivion
her head buried in his shoulder
he increases their flight
gaining on the point of no return
he lets forth a final cosmic burst
of inhuman energy
surrounded by frightened screams
from the impassioned stars here
he stops abruptly
at a last possible moment
gripped in a final embrace
they float back to earth
on a light beam of ecstacy
