title: "Warsaw Uprising"

chanting out in cheerful voices
the children carrying our burdens on their backs
as they run into the fire
perhaps they know their mission better than we
perhaps we should know better than children
what it takes to cleanse our sins
all it leaves us, is lonely death in shame
while they eagerly fight with the courage we lack
we'll avert our eyes, none to break old habits
for its the blood which colors the sheets over our eyes
that keeps us from seeing the other side
from seeing the noose around our necks
they'll leave no home for our children to return to
