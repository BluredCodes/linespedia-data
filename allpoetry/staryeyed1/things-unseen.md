title: "Things Unseen"

fleeting footsteps fade into the silence
but the phantom touches remain to taunt
my wild glances and racing heart
left to the mercy of flickering lights
while my inner senses scream their silent alarms
fearful eyes meet with disembodied ones
as stars and lamps die as one
only the darkness remains to chant
'there'll be no peaceful sleep tonight'
