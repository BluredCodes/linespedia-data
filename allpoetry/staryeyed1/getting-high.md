---
title: "Getting High"
writer: "StaryEyed1"
slug: "getting-high"
source: "AllPoetry"

left in a heap
thrown in a corner
to be brought out again
for later use
its just life
that kicks you down sometimes
when all you want
is to stand back up
learning lessons the hard way
its the only way i know
could be im just not grasping
something i should be seeing
i could throw myself
at the wall again
but somehow i dont think
the bruises would bring
the enlightenment im seeking
could be that im not
being perceptive enough
its so hard to be observant
at a time like this
when all you know
is that the pounding in your head
matches the pounding of your heart
ive come too far along
to let it all go
only to start over again
life wont let you work like that
you can skip the cracks all you want
but the hunters trap is more elusive
everyone falls in at least once
the Dream is so damn beautiful
that everyone wants a piece
something to call their own
happiness is life's ultimate reward
people stretch their hands
toward it daily
only some can never quite stretch
far enough...
ive touched it once
just one of the lucky few
its the most addictive drug
with one glimpse
your always wanting more
reduced to crying in the dark
because its spark wouldn't light
i am crying inside
