title: "broken innocence"
writer: "StaryEyed1"

broken innocence
fallen dreams
the shock and the pain
just overwhelm
im broken
never to be the same
strength is ever waning
don't take me over
don't take me away
destroying the fragility
all thats left of me
shattering in your flame
defenseless and scared
ripe for the hunger
painfully aware
that you revel control
yet lost in illusion
i loose my balance
cruelly taken advantage of
aching to let go
to run...
to hide...
to regain my safety
it may take all of me
but one day
i will tell you 'no'
