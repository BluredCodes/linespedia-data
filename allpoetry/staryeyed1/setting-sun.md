title: "setting sun"

in glory of the sun's passage
rays of brilliant rainbows
shine in their final glory
the warm evening air feels cool
in comparison with my skin
my core colors my aura
with the red of my hot restlessness
unlike the sun, i have no purpose
i am fueled with energy
yet, upon finding no outlet
my energy fuels my emptiness
the true purpose of life
was lost in the passing of time
so confusion is what i look forward to
in every useless travel
but content for the moment
to blind my vision in crimson brilliance
i wallow in the husk that is me
rocking in tune with the universal clock
just ticking my life away
