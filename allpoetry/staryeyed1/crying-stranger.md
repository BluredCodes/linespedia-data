title: "Crying Stranger"

mister stary eyed stranger
tears of shimmering, gleams of light
so far away from my grasp
why cant i reach out to touch you
through the tv screen
my beautiful miserable dreamer
still running away
yet still gripping my picture
fingers around this heart
you've held on for dear life
behind these words i stand
saving pillar from the depths of insanity
guardian angel watching from night
don't cry lovely stranger
just reach out to touch the tv screen
to find me, oh red rose of my heart
