Challenge Description: Write an internal monologue about a major life decision/conflict. No thematic guidelines. (20 points)

----------------------------------------------------------------------------

i know there's a life outside this emptiness 
but compared to this pain everything seems so small 
every moment wasted crying adds another universe between us 
i think i need you and perhaps i don't 
there's more fish in the sea i'm told 
but they don't understand 
in my eyes you were the most precious fish of them all 
and what did it matter to you 
i was walked all over, just another easy thrill 
what makes me want to return 
i deeply cared for you wasn't it enough? 
and you moved on just as quickly 
disregarding my tears 
so what if i loved you 
despite your careless ways i always knew you better 
if i could stop wishing for another kiss 
could only stop longing for another smile 
i've locked every tear away 
that threatens to show whenever you walk by 
but they just keep coming 
you'd think they'd learn when to quit 
i'd never thought living could be so lonely without you 
however you're not mine to hold anymore 
there's a life beyond you and i'll just have to find it
