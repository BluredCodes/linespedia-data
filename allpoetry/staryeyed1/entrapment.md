title: "Entrapment"

watch yon devil
listen for the dangers of the night
you could find yourself caught
hypnotized by the dewy eyes
inviting you to their hidden lair


it could be the sin
the final one you find your grave in
the last one that steals your soul
and feeds upon its light, leading you on
guiding you to eternal night


and yet you still follow the dance
drinking in the lights and the gleam
slowly losing yourself in the blur
you'll soon be swaying your hips
to a very different tune


it could be the sin
the final one you find your grave in
the last one that steals your soul
and feeds upon its light, leading you on
guiding you to eternal night


you throw your hands up for the ride
whipping wind sending shivers to your spine
swinging along the curves, arching up
slowly grinning at the release
the stars are winking back at you
they welcome you to their darkness


you'll find no reverse from the cliff
no turning back once at the end
the evil calls to those who are willing
to wrap their light in the darkness


it could be the sin
the final one you find your grave in
the last one that steals your soul
and feeds upon its light, leading you on
guiding you to eternal night


it could be your end
it could swallow you whole
even as you embrace your sins
you could be clawing to the light
the temptor will be dragging you in
