title: "Stuck in a Tower"
writer: "StaryEyed1"

windows surrounding me
just like the song
i sit in my tower
and spin in my chair
through my telescope
i see you!
happy grin
sorrowing frown
my heart soars when you soar
frowns when you frown
do you see me?
i reach out to you
fingers only fall on cold glass
falling on my knees
screaming for escape
and the glass remains unyielding
do you know i care?
that im here?
aching to be part of the whole
wanting to share my mind
with no other desire
than to be counted
as a friend
is it so wrong?
do you understand?
i'm here
dont walk away
please let me in
