title: "shopping madness"
writer: "StaryEyed1"

smooth, silky, velvety
cream colored dress
tantalizing me devilishly
from your shelf
oh how i covet you
in a hurried rush
racing towards
the dressing room
this is what
you cause me to do
disappointment is all
that can result
from this madness
for when i look
at your promenent tag
both size and price
are too large!
