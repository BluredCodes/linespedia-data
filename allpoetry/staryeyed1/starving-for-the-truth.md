---
title: "starving for the truth"
writer: "StaryEyed1"

here i stand
filled with horror
at the unspeakable things
i stood ready to commit
all for you


i shake my head
at all the anger
that raged through
threating to take control
grown from a lie
you had me firmly convinced


silently contemplating
i watch you
look at the things
you put me through
what possessed you
to break my trust so?


left to wonder
i only wish to know
how much is left
for me to trust
safely in you?
