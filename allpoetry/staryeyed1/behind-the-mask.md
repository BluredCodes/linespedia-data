---
title: "behind the mask"
writer: "StaryEyed1"
slug: "behind-the-mask"
source: "AllPoetry"

i see you walking along
and i turn the other way
time hasn't killed the hurt
it's only dulled the pain
i sat there and cried
then locked it all away
choosing a fancy mask
i grinned and walked on
but today was different
my heart was full
and my fancy mask faded
i saw you walking along
yet couldn't turn away
watching from afar
familiar feelings rising
i found to my surprise
a refusal to be locked away
my suspicions rising
of all i'd held inside
in my head i feared
that my heart couldn't hide
the old tears began to flow
i tried to pour you out
but the crying never ceased
as i realized finally
i'd never be rid of you
