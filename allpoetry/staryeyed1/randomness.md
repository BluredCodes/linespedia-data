lazy boy sundays
she never seems to notice there's something missing
it's like a lark in a tree
only its not singing
you'll want to quell the internal storm i suppose
maybe douse some rage along with it
kill some time with a staple gun
it'll be some wholesome recreational fun
and all the while the world will be spinning
don't you know? you're spinning too
are you dizzy yet?
how about now?
what a silky smooth life
just like her legs
just like his eyes crying inside
the liquid is pouring and the rain is boring
forget the old man, his snoring is annoying
miss the life you wish you had
take the time to kiss the gods of irony
and don't ever forget
to start the coffee tommorrow morningi wasn't trying to create anything, i was just following my train of thought...
