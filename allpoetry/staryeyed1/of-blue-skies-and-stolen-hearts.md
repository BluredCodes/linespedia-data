title: "of blue skies and stolen hearts"

i've never felt a moment so gracefully
nor held a hand so long
seeing the sky so blue
as if smiling upon my good fortune
to be twirling in your clasped arms
blurring my life to this one
moment of pure bliss
i could have been spending the centuries
refracting light from my eyes to yours
just to experience a raised eyebrow
your grin composed with little effort
a melody, a song, a sonnet
this brief moment twisted into
i've never felt happier to be with you
