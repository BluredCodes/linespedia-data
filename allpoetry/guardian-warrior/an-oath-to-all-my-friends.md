If there is ever a dream you awake from scared,
If you ever wonder has there ever been some one who really cared.
If you ever suddenly become confronted by all your fears,
Or need someone to hold you as you shed tears.
If ever you feel like you are all alone with no one around,
If you are in so much pain that you cant hear a sound.
Just close your eyes and I will hold your hand,
I will always be there for you just because I can.
