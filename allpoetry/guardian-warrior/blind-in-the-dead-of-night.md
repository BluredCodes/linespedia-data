I’m walking around in the day like it’s the dead of night
I’m walking around even though I have lost my sight
I have done nothing else but fall into love
With this beautiful angel from up above
But she loves someone else and she cant be with me
And I hurt so much that now I can’t see
Now all I see is the dark even if there’s light
So I walk around the day like it’s the dead of night



I’m walking around in the day like it’s the dead of night
I’m walking around even though I have lost my sight
And I don’t think that the angel will ever be mine
But I beg to the heavens to send me a sign
Telling me that in the end everything will be fine
But they wont even put a glow into time
So I’m on my own and I’m all alone
So in this darkness I will just roamI am not done with this one yet but here is what i have so far.  Tell me what you think of what I have right now.~guardian warrior~
