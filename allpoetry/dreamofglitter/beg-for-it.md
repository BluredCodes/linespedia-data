down deep
you sicken me
to the core of my heart 
you fucking sicken me
in ways that are unimaginable
why you treat yourself this way
when you have something so good
why you crave it all towards you
when you have only one problem
you ADD freak
thats all you want
feed me the attention
I don't care what you have to say
I'm so sexy
I'm so suicidal
I'm so unloved
I'll never be like them
My life's so hard
oh please, cry me an ocean 
and drink it back up
I'm not listening to your shit anymore.
