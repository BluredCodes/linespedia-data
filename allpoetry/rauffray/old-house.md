Old house

Once upon a time

A home 
To hopeful would-be pioneers

Now 

Empty

Abandoned

Grey

Weather-bleached boards

Sagging roof

Is it voices from long ago
I hear
From your dark within?

Or is it the wind
Moaning through your empty window frames
As it passes
On its way
To the desolate prairie? 

Beyond

Memories
