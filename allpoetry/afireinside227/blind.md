Searching for the light that shines 
Faithfulness seems so blind
Can't Believe in anything 
Trust can only go so far
Before the light fades out on every burning star
Shadows lurk from behind the doors
Blured images of your past painted on the floor 
Friends that came, the ones that left
Left you burried, who goes next
Lied to everyday
Wishes always runaway
Belief in friendship, in loyalty
It is all a dream
The demons try to explain, but you learn nothing cause you don't believe
Left out in the cold, in the dark
the night closes in on you, standing there you dont know what to do
If only you believed
If only you could see the real side of things
The light would shine again, and the attention would be on youI wrote this like a month ago it is one of my better songs.
