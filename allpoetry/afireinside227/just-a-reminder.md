The wind blows me so far away
I want to go blind and just run away
Another wasted day
Another wasted minute
It's all the same
My life flashes before me
A ghost with my ability
Tranquilize me, put me to sleep
A crowded room
An empty street
Reminds me of me
Back when the days were short, the nights were long
Closed my eyes, dreamed of this song
An empty ocean
An overflowed sea
Thats how it was back then for me
When things go wrong I'll think of this song and it will all be OKthis is like the 400th song i wrote first one ive actually submitted somewhere though, im sure there will be more to come.
