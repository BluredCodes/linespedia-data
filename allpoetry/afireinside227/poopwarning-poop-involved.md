SHIT ON THE STREET
SHIT ON OYUR FEET
POOP ON THE FLOOR
SQUIRT AT THE DOOR
LIE ON THE FLOOR
AND ILL SHIT ON YOU
THATS WHAT ILL DO
SHIT AND RUN
SHIT AND RUN
SHIT AND RUN
SHIT AND RUN
SHIT AND RUN
RUN FROM THE SHIT MAN
THE SHIT MAN
THE SHIT MAN THE SHIT MAN
HES GONNA GET YOU WITH HIS POOP GUN
HIS POOP GUN
HIS POOP GUN
AND HES GONNA SHOOT YOU
HELL SHOOT YOU
AND YOU ARE GONNA SMELL
