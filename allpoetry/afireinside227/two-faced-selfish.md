title: "Two Faced & Selfish"
writer: "AFIREinside227"

Thoughts erased, friends two faced
acting one way then another
saying we're best friends
but you have plenty others
jealousy is acted out, i dont know what you're about
pretending to shout, i want to hear nothing
silence sounds so good, best when you are not around
fighting me over everything
afraid i'm trying to steal something
your stuff isn't worth anything to me anyways
i dont want to be you
or be like you
no one would
