I AM GOTH
I HATE THE WORLD
I AM ANGRY I WANNA DIE
MAYBE ILL GO EMO AND THEN ILL CRY
BIG OL GOTHIC KIDS IN THE HALLWAY THEY WEAR BLACK EVERYDAY
I AM ANGRY I AM ANGRY I WANNA BE GOTHIC FOR THE REST OF MY LIFE
I HATE THESE PUNK KIDS THEY WEAR COLORS NOT BLACK
BLACK IS BETTER CAUSE YOU CANT SEE IT AT NIGHT
DEVIL IN MY BACKPACK
ON MY CLOTHES 
IN MY HAIR
I SEE THE DEVIL EVERYWHERE
HE LIVES ON MY ROOF PLAYING HIS GUITAR WHILE I LISTEN TO CRADLE OF FILTH ON MY $2000 THOUSAND DOLLAR CD PLAYER
I HAVE NO FRIENDS EXCEPT ME AND ALL THE OTHER GOTHS HANG OUT BUT THEY ARENT FRIENDS WE HATE THE WORLD WE WANT TO DIE I HATE THOSE EMO KIDS THEY MAKE ME CRYAbout gothic people....(im not goth)
