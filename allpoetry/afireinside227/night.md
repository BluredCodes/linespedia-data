---
title: "Night"
writer: "AFIREinside227"

---

the night flies bye
we slowly die
rest in the shadows
lie in the back of your head
wish you were dead
the night swallows us up
consumes us hole
lost in your shadow
never knew where to go
alone all the time
waste away your night
or let the night take you
consume you
intake you
then spit you out
you deserve no better
when you let things like that happen
your only killing yourself
but need no help
offer you nothing
let tomorrow take you away
if you can run from the night
let tomorrow put you in your place
but no matter what the night wont go away
