title: "In my mind"

In my mind im running
forever, out of thought
little less of thinking
something more i forgot
it humors me to lose my mind
running in my head in an alley so dark
so cold, so fucking cold
i might as well collapse
i might as well go back
wasted time, wasted mind
horrible memories
dreadful thoughts
back in reality, im still running
out of ideas
out of my head
out of my body
i wish i was dead
