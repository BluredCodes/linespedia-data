EATING AND SUCKING, LICKING AND FUCKIN, HUMPING AND GRUMPING
IM GONNA KILL YOU IN YOUR SLEEP
IM GONNA LICK YOU WHILE YOU EAT
IM GONNA MURDER YOU OVER AND OVER AND OVER
IM GONNA KILL PEOPLE WHEN I GET OLDER
DONT LOOK OVER YOUR SHOULDER CAUSE I AM BEHIND YOU WITH A BIG SHARP PENIS THAT IM GONNA PUT N YOUR EAR
CAUSE YOUR NEAR
