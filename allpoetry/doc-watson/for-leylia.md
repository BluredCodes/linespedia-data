How can I feel such sorrow
when i've never Loved so much
why must i always dread the morrow
when today should be enough
feeling so much (after so much denial)
so soon (after so long)
so strong (after such weakness)
(not) wanting to resist your gaze

no feeling i have ever known
is more difficult (yet so easy) to share
than this (imperfectly) perfect love i feel for you
with nothing to compare
no one has created words
to describe its endless glow
no river has run the distance
to compare how far it flows
and since there is no way to show
its (vastness) force written in rime
i can only express my love for you
as outlasting the end of thyme

i knew this would happen all along
(you knew it too, methinks)
purposely lost within your mayze
whisper the words too precious to speak
they echo off whalls of wite
wrapped in your arms i've grown too weak
to put anything more into this fight (against you)
logic (and clarity) give up hope
reason abandoned (so long ago)
all resistance to your ropes
given up with the (last of the first) hello
breath uneven, butterflies dance
no other place i'd rather be
odds against, but i'll take my chance
