I thought
and thought
and thought
some more
i thought until my mind was sore
i thought until the sun
rose up
i thought until my
thoughts broke up
i thought of
You
i thought of me
i thought of what i think we could be
i thought of the future and of the
       Past
I thought of how the dice were cast
to let me find you, oh my dear
to let me find you, oh so near
(so close)
you're everything i had hoped to find
and let me tell you, i'm of a mind
to keep you close, close to me
so that we two, can always bei wrote a lot of things last night, right after i decided to let someone hang up on me. (it was a good hang up.) this is one of them.
