---
title: "...If only you would"
writer: "Doc Watson"
slug: "if-only-you-would"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/19675-...If-only-you-would-by-Doc-Watson"
---

you can't scare me off
---Because I am already afraid
you can't set me aflame
---Because I've already been burned
you can't derail me
---Because I'm already a run-away train
you can't fuck me up
---Because I am more twisted than you'll ever know
you can't make me cry
---Because I've already cried an ocean
you can't rape me of my dreams
---Because I have but one to give
you can't stab me in the back
---Because I have too many shanks there already
you can't poison me
---Because I still feel the tingle of the asp's venom on my tongue
you can't delude me
---Because I am already delusional
you can't humiliate me
---Because I've already suffered enough to learn
you can't attack my family
---Because they're fuckin' crazy, they'll kill you
you can't set me up
---Because I've already been exploited
you can't kill me
---Because I'm already dead
you can't destroy what I've worked for
---Because it's already ash and rubble
you can't be apathetic
---Because I could care less
you can't shove me into the abyss
---Because I've already taken too many falls
  
but
   
You can bandage me
---because my cuts are deep
You can wash my face
---because i'm covered in filth
You can cleanse my soul
---because mine is coated in black soot
You can sing to me
---because solitude offers only silence
You can clothe me
---because i am naked in Ignorance
You can kiss me
---because i would relish your lips
You can show me the light
---because i live in darkness
You can fill me
---because i am a void

          You can love me
          because i love you
  
but You won't
