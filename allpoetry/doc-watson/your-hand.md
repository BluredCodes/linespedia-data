When i want to show you thati'm here for You, ihold your Hand.when i can't find words toexpress how much i care, isqueeze your Hand.when i want You tobe happy, istroke your Hand.when i am drunkedly balancing onthe tips of my shoes, ihang on to your (slightly blurred) Hand.i use my Hands to show you mylove for You.please don't take your HandAway.
