---
title: "If this..."
writer: "Doc Watson"
slug: "if-this"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/19681-If-this...-by-Doc-Watson"
---

If my words were fluid
would you bathe in them?
If my lips were wine
would you drink of them?
If my eyes were the light of the sun
would you bask in them?

If my arms were blankets
would you wrap yourself in them?
If my kisses were rouge
would you paint your lips with them?
If my love was a house
would you live in it with me?

If my neck were a mother's nurturing breast
would you suckle of it?
If my touches were joy
would you revel in them?
If my love was death
would you give in to it?

If my feelings were enamel
would you paint yourself in indifference with them?
If my wishes were my ears
would you box them?
If my longings were vermin
would you kill them?

If my convictions were sweet roses
would you trample them?
If my mind was a temple
would you defile it?
If my body was a grave
would you rob it?

If my soul were a church
would you burn it?
If my ideals were a garden
would you tend them?

If I should happen to love you with everything I had

would you possibly give a fuck?
