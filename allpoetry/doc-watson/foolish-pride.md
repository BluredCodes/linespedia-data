---
title: "Foolish Pride"
writer: "Doc Watson"
slug: "foolish-pride"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/41175-Foolish-Pride-by-Doc-Watson"

From your hopes and dreams you hide
Hampered by your foolish Pride
remembering all you tossed aside
with calloused disregard

For what you had and what you'd lose
you would be the one to choose
to be hampered by your Foolish pride

The world is ours and what we make it
it can be yours if you'll only take it
and not be hampered by your Foolish Pride
