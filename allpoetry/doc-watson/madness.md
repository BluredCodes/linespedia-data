Let the Madness sink in
as it talks to me
depressing and keener
than it's ever been.
Laundried thoughts
hanging out to dry
let the Madness sink in
as it starts to cry
'murder, murder,
where is my murder?'
you can see It's blood shine
as It bleeds from inside.
time's passing by
and the Madness still tries
to be what It can
to live whil there's still time
but as It wretches inside of me
It burns me and It blinds me
for the Pain is forgotten
i can't let It win--
fuck the promises that you've made
and let the Madness sink in
