and you asked me what i feel when i kiss you(r skin)
when my lips roll over you(r body) in unspoken sin
as my tongue touches yours, as our mouths collide
one part of it is pure ecstatic delight
the other is that love that i have longed for (so long)
we may go a bit too far at times (??) but it is (never) wrong
for one day you'll love me (like i now love you)
we'll both be a part of everything (we do)
another part still is just you
a masterpiece that Bob so carefully drew
my life so full of joy (and pride)
nobody can ever lower me from the cloud i am onin case the reader was wondering, Bob is a little inside joke for God.  When you have a couple of aetheists, a couple of agnostics, a couple of devout methodists, and a baptist, the reference to any form of supreme being is bound to offend someone.  So we *temporarily*, for the sake of debate, called "God" a force, and named that force Bob.  Long story, quite funny, but for now just take my word for it.
