---
title: "I tried (for you)"
writer: "Doc Watson"
slug: "i-tried-for-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/29659-I-tried--for-you--by-Doc-Watson"

I tried to write a poem last night
to tell you how i feel
but the words scribbled out before me
didn't seem quite real
words like forever, always, true
somehow aren't enough for you
there's little that will rhyme with love
namely things like above and dove
those words don't even begin to start
to tell you what is in my heart
to say each breath i ever take
reminds of the sounds you make
to say that every step i walk
reminds me of our endless talks

(until i fall asleep)

to say to have you in the room
scares away the darkest gloom

(are you real)

to know you make my life complete
filling holes i didn't see
nothing i can ever say
can satisfy my soul
no poem seems to say enough
it leaves me wanting more
just know i tried for you
and with every ounce of me
i love you and i love you twice
for all eternityi hope one day i can write a poem that is worthy of you...of the eight i have attempted, only three are worth anything.  i tried, and i will continue to try
