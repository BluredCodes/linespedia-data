I am choking on epiphany
Horking up wisdom like a Hairball
scratching at my conscience
with the irritating notion
of culpability
i suspect Myself of consequence
of being as good as you, as strong
my needs as important,
my dreams (nightmares) as worthy
laying responsibility for everything i want
and do not have
with authority at my feet
cowering, i gulp grope and swallow revelation
because i am weak
because blaming you

is effortlessi dunno...just kinda spat itself out
