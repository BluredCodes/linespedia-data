---
title: "A challenge lost"
writer: "GoldIWonder"

A caveat greater than all the kings horses
Dare challenge a precious alibi
I kept my self and stayed so pure
You broke down and cried 
Admit you're lost and you are found
Grapple an angel choice unheard
And mock a religion deceitful word 
Dive into a question swim into a thought
Sing a timeless soliloquy
Make it your own
