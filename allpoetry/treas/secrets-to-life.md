---
title: "Secrets to Life"
writer: "Treas"
slug: "secrets-to-life"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

forbidden secrets
haunt my lies
seducing my hate
endulging fate
failing the truth
with so much proof
we skip the conferences
to involve the promises
seperating them from the lies
thats what lies really despise
over ruling the hate
leads to my fate
making the lies and promises to relate
Secertly rebuilding from inside
I'm not able to hide
Telling my secrets
Relieving my unknown pride
Seals me again 
My life to confide.
