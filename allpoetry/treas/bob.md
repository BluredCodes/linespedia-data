Bob has a girlfriend who's as sweet as can beWho deserves a guy like Boband Bob a girl like she.Bob can care forSomeone so much it's unbelievableHe'll stick by their side through anythingAlthough it might not even be reasonableBob makes you happy when your sadHe makes you laugh when your madBob sees the better side of things. .Oh how much happiness he bringsBob's a great guyAnd that is why.Bob's my friendForever and ever With no end.This is about one of my good friends
