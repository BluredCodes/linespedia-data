Months by months
Years by years
Where does time go
It disappears

Day by day
Hour by hour
Wheres my strength
And where's my power

Minute by minute
Second by second
Where's life headed
Where's life end

Person by person
Child by child
Some act as sleeping
Some act so wild

Sea by sea
Drop by drop
Someones watching
Will they stop?

Beam by beam
Ray by ray
Hurrying faster
I'll hide one day.

Strut by strut
Step by step
Talking to myself
My secrets aren't kept

Holy by Holy
Word by Word
My thoughts are out
My thouhts are heard.

Escaping by not knowing
My secrets are showing
No one else can see. .
Yet, knowing, is He.This is about how you can't hide from God. .even though you try. .you can't
