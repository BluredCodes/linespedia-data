title: "Church"
writer: "Treas"

I go to church to learn about God
It makes me wonder. .
How many others actually go for that reason. .
I sit and pay attention
To the words that are spoken
While the others sit there and laugh
And tell jokes and socialize
I sing when it's time to sing
While the others laugh at me
They think I'm dumb for praising the Lord
But how can they call themselves christians
If they don't know anything
They have to laugh and make fun of
The ones who actually care
And go to church. .and who are aware
Aware of God, Aware of life
I'm going to go to Heaven
I know this in my heart
Nothing will tear me and God apart
Not my friends, not the world
Nothing. .ever will destroy me
For who I really am
I am a believer and no one can make me turn back
Because God is my life
I love Him, that's a fact.Really now. .I go to church. .but. .no one else seems to care what we do there. .they just want to hang out. .and it really makes me mad.
