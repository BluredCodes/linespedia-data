Pesimistic View

The sky, filled with blue
On that flight, it’s you.
Off into the farness
Leaving. .with a simple caress

Far across the ocean
Sun going down, silent motion
You’re gone forever now
What’s life without you, survive. .how

The sun goes down
And so do I
My smile beings to frown
My eyes begin to cry

The flight is seen no more
The shadow, my heart has tore
...Come back....please
Travel back . .over the long seas

My heart is on that plane
Never again, will I be the same
A heart broken in two
I can. .never. .ever. .renew

Optimistic View

They sun coming up
Me, drinking coffee from my cup
Awaiting the flight to come in
From distance to distance within

Here it comes, in the midst of the dawn
It’s a new day, nothing will go wrong
Just a bit farther
I can wait, not much harder

Here it comes, abroad and new
My heart. .oh how it grew
My love’s almost here
Oh. .*cries a tear*

Awaiting so long
Patience paid off
It’s a new day
