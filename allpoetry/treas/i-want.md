i want to rip you
i want you not 'fine'
i want to stalk you 
i want you mine
i want to destroy you
i want you to fall
i want to scare you
i want you to crawl
i want to hurt you
i want you to cry
i want to push you
i want you to die.umm. .i guess i have some anger in me somewhere or something hehe
