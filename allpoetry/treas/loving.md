Loving is living
living is dreaming
dreaming is seeing
seeing is wondering
wondering is hoping
hoping is wanting
wanting is needing 
needing is having
having is knowing
knowing is realizing
realizing is beliveing
Believing is seeking
seeking is finding
finding is ending
ending is dying
dying is loving.This is sorta different I suppose. .
