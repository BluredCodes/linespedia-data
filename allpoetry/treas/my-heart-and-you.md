title: "My Heart and You"

My salinity
Is what you destroy
You understand
My heart's not a toy
You look at me
If blinded I see
Shocked, to just a degree
You speak
Words hath not all
You lip-sync
You make me fall
Fall, in lust?
No. .it's more a must. .
Its more a want. .
O, tis not a haunt
Tis for thy shape
Brilliant, you create
They noise, the beat
For only THY to keep
Close to me *pound* *pound*
O, what a lovely joyous sound!
