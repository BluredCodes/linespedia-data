---
title: "Lucky Girl"
writer: "Treas"
slug: "lucky-girl"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/18653-Lucky-Girl-by-Treas"
---

The light leaves my eyes
whenever you come around
my soul deflates, though, not it tries
my heart beats with no sound

my mind says i love him
my emotions do too
I can't help but grin
what else should i do?

You're not a bad girl
I just wish it was me
In his arms..to.. unfurl
That'd make me so happy

But. .I guess I should still smile
since he IS my friend
He makes my life worthwhile
Gives me someone to defend..
He is the greatest guy I have ever met
And to you, his girl.. my hope is sent.
