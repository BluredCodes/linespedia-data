I want to tell you
But I don't know how
When I first saw you
I was like. .WOW
Now I know you better
and I think. .HOW, me
of all people
Did i come upon a guy like you?
It's just like a fairy tale
That has come true

The prince and the princess
met this one lovely day
. .How breathtaking I might say
They fellin so deeply, deeply in love
Deeper than any ocean
And higher than the heavens above
They rode on the wings of a white, magical dove
Out into the sunset they ride
Off to live, Happily ever after
With dignity and pride.

Ok, Ok, so I lied
We haven't gotten to this part
But, I do love you with all my heart. 
And when I see your face
I see a beautiful sunset
No other could replace.

Well, that's the part, where we are
I say we've gotten pretty far
Now,
how do we ride off into the sunset
to live happily ever after
