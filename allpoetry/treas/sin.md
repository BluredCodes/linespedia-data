i like to stalk you
i like to be free
i want to know you
why cant you let me?
how can you deny
that you love who i am
i am the only one
who convinces you, u can
i succeed when you falter
i die when you live
i enlight your spirits
for you everything i give
i want to see you
i want to know
what is it about me
that you i cant show
