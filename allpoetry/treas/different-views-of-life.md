View 1

I hear everyone talk
Bout how bad stuff is
How you gota suit up
Just to go take a walk

I hear them complain
Bout people these days
How they are so lazy
Making them go insane

I always hear them say
"eww look at her
Isn't she gross?"
It's just a regular day

A regular day for a regular teen
So much hate
So much pain, so many lies
Life's not life, is what it seems

View2

I hear the same things
All that bad stuff
But you can't do anything
Becuase. .pain it brings

I hear their lies
They are too lazy themselves
To get up for a while
Too stupid, them I despise

They always talk
But they never do
Why do they complain
If they can't talk the walk?

Why can't they do
Instead of just watch
Sitting there. .gossiping
Saying "eww. .not you too"

This is the world in the view of a
Not-so-regular teen
One who is smart
And respects life, and does his part.ah. .i dont know
