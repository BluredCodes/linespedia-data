title: "A love without you is nothing"

There’s a special someone
Who wipes away my tears
In the dark, while I’m crying
He brings out the sun
And makes me stop dying
While I’m sitting there alone
He always remembers
And calls me on the phone
If I feel like nothing can be okay
He talks to me
And makes everything go my way
If I’m so mad, I can’t take any more
He’ll talk to me
He’ll open my closed door
He is the best guy I’ve ever known
He’s everything I need
All my emotions are somehow shown
My heart plants it’s seed
Into your very own
I’m so happy you’re here
And I am no longer alone
I want you to take this present
That I’m giving to you
It isn’t much, yet it is
Take my heart, Take my life
Because, it needs you to live. .hehe *happy*
