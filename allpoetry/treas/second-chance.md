title: "Second  Chance"
writer: "Treas"

Walls come crashing down
Leaving without a sound.

The lakes, the rivers, and the seas
The red ocean drowns the rest of me

Silently drifting coldly away
Being stabbed, walking to stay.

I seep out the noise with a beat
Escaping the pain in a slow fleet.

Parting the rivers and the roads
Relieving the years, all the loads.

Rebuilding the future, with the past
Making a new home, that will last.

Mending the holes in my heart
Soundly reminiscing every part.

I walk to my grave, and turn around
This beat in my heart, will once again pound.This is about, how it hurts to love someone that doesnt love you, yet you can regain hope and find the perfect someone.
