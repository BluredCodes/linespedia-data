TICK TOCKThe years go byHurrying faster, do they fly?What if I want you what if I don'tWhat if I need you what if I won'tWill my heart still be beatingWith the 'us' never meeting?Is it worth waiting so longOr is this completely wrongI really want youI really doBut what if I wasn't made for youWhat if time will never tellAnd to another my heart will sell?Will it hurt my whole self-life being. .If you and I never hear the alarm clock ringing??
