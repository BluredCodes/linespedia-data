I walked through your life
You said it was really bad
And actually, it is sort of sad
Losing your mom when you were only five
Thinking there was no meaning to be alive
Being hated by your dad
Him abusing you, and always being mad
Your brothers always ignoring you
From this, no acceptance grew. .
Your sister having a baby when it was her freshman year
To my eye, it brought a tear
You having to get out on your own
Wandering through the world, helpless, and alone
Getting a job while you were twelve years old
Everyone yelling, and to you commands were told
Doing that until you graduated school
Kids picking on you, calling you a fool
Living in a broken down house
No kids, nor a spouse
Making progress, in a solitare way
Not for long this would stay.
You found the girl of your dreams
The girl only in fairy tales it seems
You had a family, a loving one indeed
But, too many mouths for you to feed
Your two kids got taken away
Never seeing them, your life would fray
Your wife died of diease when you were twenty three
This isn’t how life is supposed to be.
Just I wish you could have known
That. .you aren’t and never were alone
Life gets better in the end
If it’s not better it’s not the end
And I wanted to let you know
You were always my best friend.I was talking to someone last month, and I got a story from it, and it was pretty sad, it was sorta like what this poem is about.  I just want people to know, that life isn't meant to be horrible, and, in the end everythings better, if it's not, it's not the end.
