title: "Unexistence"
writer: "Treas"

Superficial unexistence
looks down on me
talks about me
loves me.
It stands there,
walking, talking, stalking me
hating, waiting, chasing me
It laughs at me
So loud, that I can't hear.
It's here, but I'm not
It watches me, without looking
It chases me, without moving
It's waiting for a mistake
It kills me, but I don't die
It kills itself,
Trying to exist
