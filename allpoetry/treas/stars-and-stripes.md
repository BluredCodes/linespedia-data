Simple shapes
Tacked upon our flag
Acuainted now we have
Realistic feelings of
Security
~and~
Straight lines
Ttraced in red and white
Rippling in the sky
Indefinately flying
Peace and justice are formed
Entrancing other nations
Stars and Stripes are born.. .at school we had to write a poem about america, or something dealing with it, just thought i'd see what people thought.
