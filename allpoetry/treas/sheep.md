Fluffy sheep trot in my mind.. too
leaving their wool, all dirty, for you!
They mah all night. .until its sunny out
They prance and staggerly skip about
they sharpen their claws
even though they have none
I forgot. .I was thinknig of my inlaws
anyway, hehe,  the sheep are really mean
although, it cant be seen
Because. .the hide themselves w/ their wool
and after they kill you, in they’ll pull
munching on you, for a midnight snack
Niceness is what these sheep lack
