---
title: "Looking for a lover"
writer: "Treas"

Friends are what I don't need
True friends is what makes life succeed
Friends are not what I'm looking for
True friends will come right through the door
Friends just stop and say hey
True friends by your side they will stay
Friends will leave and be no more
True friends you dont have to implore
Friends they say will always seem to depart
True friends, they say, leave footprints in your heart
Friends won't make you feel your best
True friends can you feel like you're blessed
I already have friends
And I have great true ones too
But what should I do
When I'm looking for a lover
and so far, it seems to be you
