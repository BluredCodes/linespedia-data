What makes:
       the sun shine
things mine
       the world go around
voices have sound
       tears form in my eyes
full lakes start to dry
       the wind still blow
trees and plants grow
       a persons day
a person decide to pray
       a rainbow form
 a rainbow come after a storm
       someone really sad
someone mad
       a truth a lie
someone feel like they should die
       important things dead
things go unsaid
       faith into sorrow
us put things off til tomorrow
       things give a sign
things rhyme
       my life worth living
it happy when it's sad
       it better when I'm mad
it a life at all
       For me. .It's you.:)
