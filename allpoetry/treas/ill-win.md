---
title: "I'll Win"
writer: "Treas"
slug: "ill-win"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/41069-Ill-Win-by-Treas"
---

Struggling through life, which is no more
My muscles, my bones, all still sore
Travling the depths, which are endless
My soul finds it as a never ending test
Unseen faces, scream right in mine
Mysertious chills creep up my spine
Hiding in the corner, in the dark
Only light, Is a single spark
Falling faster, I can’t withdrawl
My fast walk, beings to crawl
Stepping up, to reunite
I lose once again, this unending fight
Fight for dignity, fight for pride
But I can’t help it, once I lied. .
I know we all sin
Only once, never again I’ll win
I just want to rid of you
Get my life sorted straight
Get problems solved too
But all these nights I’ve stayed up late
Trying to defeat your games
Losing, but never going insane
I try to block them out
But what are they all about
Nothing really I suppose
Just, winning is what I chose
And winning is what I’ll do
I don’t need my self confidence slaughtered by you.
