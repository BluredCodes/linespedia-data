Mommy was packing her bags one day
She said "boys be good, you have to stay-
I'll be back in a few, I'm going to the store"
That's what she said as she fiercly slammed the door.

I heard daddy yelling in the other room. .
He misses mommy I assume. .

After a while, asleep I fell
I couldn't really sleep that well.

Then I heard a shattering sound
Making me jump, and fall to the ground.

"Johnny?" I whispered and silently said
Walking in the kitchen, there was daddy..lying dead. .
While Johnny had the gun pointed at his head
"No Johnny!" ~bang~ crying, my brother bled.

Holding him close, no help I could do
But, sadly, my brother died too.

Where is my mommy, I wanted to know?
Why does all of this have to be so. .

No mommy, no brother, or my dad
I never cried so much, felt so sad.

Sitting on the floor for a while
A broken heart. .never again will I smile

Some people came, and took me away
The questions, I ask them everyday

"Why did mommy leave, why did they die?"
All these questions, never knowing why.

I'm just glad that I survived
I guess God wants me to be alive.
But. .why. .
