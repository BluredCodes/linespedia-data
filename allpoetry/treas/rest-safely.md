Lightening strikes through my window
Afterwards theres always a rainbow
This isnt the first time. .
It’s sorta weird
First, black dots appear
Then, you start swirling 
Spiarls are twirling
Memories not remembered
Enter your mind
answers to secrets you can find
after a while
You feel some pain
for quite a while, it remains
you feel pressure on your head
you feel like. .you’re dead
you want out
there’s no way. .
You’re in to deep
Next time . .remember
Pray before you go to sleep.
