title: "What If?"
writer: "Treas"

What if I don't love you
What if I never did
What if it was you I never knew
And what if I liked him instead?
What if I never met you
What if I pushed you aside
What if this love never grew
And what if "I love you" lied?
Why am I asking these questions
Why am I asking 'what if'
Why am I so curious if I already have you,
My special gift?
