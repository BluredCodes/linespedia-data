title: "Alive"
writer: "Treas"

Among these walls
It's never heard
The screams of pain
The frightening calls
Torment, horror
Describes it all
Chilling noises continue on
Screeching, howling
Until its dawn
Waking, thanking
Knowing I survived
Makes me glad to know I'm alive.
