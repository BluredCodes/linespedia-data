---
title: "It's Real"
writer: "Treas"
slug: "its-real"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

No need for words
your silence is heard
no need to listen
I don’t charge admission
just hold me tight
don’t let me out of sight
be by my side
be there for me to confide
hold my hand
draw hearts in the sand
show me you love me
act as im blind
Be lost in my heart
So you I can find
Sleep with me at night
Let ‘us’ take flight
Creep up, give me a kiss
Let there be shown bliss
I’ll walk the 2000 miles
Just to see you smile
I’d risk my life on this Earth
To me, That’s how much you’re worth
Actually more. .
But I can’t find a way
To express how I feel
Just know, my love is real.
