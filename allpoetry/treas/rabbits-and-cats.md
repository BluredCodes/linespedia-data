Why do I dream of rabbits. .
I believe it’s just a habbit
but I cant get them out of my mind
they roll around. .scary I find
that these aren’t rabbits
what the heck are they?
Cats you say?
How can I get a cat and a rabbit confused
what. .my thought’s over used?
Hmmm are you sure?
It sure feels like rabbit fur
So soft and snuggly, but. .cats
If it were a cat. .it’d have lots of mats
No?. .no matty cats. .I think you’re wrong
Because this fur isn’t very long
I know it’s rabbit fur, believe me
Can’t you see. .
Rabbit and cat fur is not at all the same
I can get it wet in the rain
and it doesn’t mind
if it were a cat
it’d look like a soaking wet rat
But it had big huge ears
It also, unlike cats, has fears
So. .please tell me again. .how
Is this a cat, not a rabbit, now. .ooooo im bored. .can you tell
