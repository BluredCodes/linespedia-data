---
title: "Be You"
writer: "Treas"
slug: "be-you"
source: "AllPoetry"

What's the point of the ribbon's n' bows
and all the luxury
from your head to your toes
Isn't it the inside that matters
You dont need all that stuff to get someone flattered
Just be the unique individual you are
It won't seem like it
But you'll get far
