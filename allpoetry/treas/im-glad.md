title: "I'm Glad"

I'm glad my world gets crushed down
Because it can be rebuilt
and you'll be the one with all of this guilt
I'm glad I get made fun of
Although it's never fun
But the past is the past, and whats done has been done
I'm glad I get looked down upon
Even though it's wrong
But someday you'll figure out, it doesn't hurt so long
I'm glad I get sad sometimes
Because, later, I feel just fine
The pain will be your's sometime, finally, instead of mine
I'm glad everything in the day 
Never seems to stay
It means, my life moves on, and pain will go away
I'm glad my feelings get hurt
And that I'm treated like dirt
Maybe someday, you'll get this to, and try to change
I'm glad that I'm hated
Even though that's mean
You know what though, you are also rated, and maybe even hated
Although you try to hurt me
I'm so thankful for these things
Why can't you see
This is life, and this is what it brings.I just wrote this on my perspective on life. .any suggestions??
