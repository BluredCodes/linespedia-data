---
title: "Too Deep"
writer: "Treas"
slug: "too-deep"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/26673-Too-Deep-by-Treas"
---

I looked way into the distance
Far into my soul
Laughed at my existence
For what, I'll never know.

I looked into the memories
Painted in my heart
I ended the search,
That I'd never start.

I wandered around motionless
Thinking with no words
Why does life have to be like this
I'm depressed and it hurts.

Scared to death, I screamed
Drifting fast to sleep,
I love you world
But this time, these wounds are too deep.
