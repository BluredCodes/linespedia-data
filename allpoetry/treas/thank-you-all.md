Everyone on fadingflower
you have given the power
to express my self 
and all my feelings
which helps me figure out things
and gives me the wealth
to make life better
in these poem like letters
so people can relate
to how I feel in my state
I want you to know
I’m very thankful
For everyone that reads my poems
You guys are wonderful
I thank you each one of you
God bless you, and all that you do.thanx everyone
