title: "You Could See"
writer: "Treas"

Scared to death I sneak around
Nothing more but to make a sound
I shall do not, what I do to you
For me. .this is what happens too

Lots of things happen now
For reasons, I don’t know how
Careless we never find
Anything that’s meant to remind

Slackingly we feel the pain
It makes us all go insane
We can’t stand it anymore
What is it, what’s it for

Doubtly we know what it’s for
It’s the key that opens the door
The door to love and peace
And that’s just the least

There’s more but you’ll never see
Because you aren’t the holder of a key
Have God in your heart and soul
And the beauty is what you will toll.
