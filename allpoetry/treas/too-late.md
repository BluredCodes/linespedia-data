---
title: "Too Late"
writer: "Treas"
slug: "too-late"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/26968-Too-Late-by-Treas"
---

You screamed at me
i didnt listen
I just walked off
On a different mission
One-thousand miles to my death
Never stopping, my secrets kept
Only lying to themselves
Deep inside my heart all the pain dwells
Walking to the edge
Soaring off the side
I dont want to die!
But it's too late I realized.
