---
title: "Life"
writer: "Treas"
slug: "life"
source: "AllPoetry"

Any living personHas life in a lifeless heart.Whether they agree or notThe truth they can't departWhether they be helplessWhether they be freeThey are always livingBut their life they cannot see.I wrote this because. .I was thinking. .'i sit around all day, and don't do anything, i don't have a life' but . .I know I'm living. .i just don't see that if you are a live you have to have a life. .so I do have a life. .just don't notice it.
