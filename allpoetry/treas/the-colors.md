Looking through the window
While the rain comes pouring in
Smearing all the colors
That were carefully painted on

Touching them, smearing them more
Don’t know what I have in store
Mixing things that don’t belong 
Somehow. .it seems wrong

The rain diluting
Me polluting
Washing it away
Seeing a ray

A ray of sun
The colors are gone
I believe I have won
And that it wasn’t wrong.
