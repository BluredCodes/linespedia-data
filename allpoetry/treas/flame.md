I gaze at the flame
Wondering, to tame
Will it burn. .
The questions I yearn

It's bright shiny glow
In the night it shows
Anywhere to see
The orange to be

Burning my constructions
Rebuilding my destructions
The fire goes furthermore
While lies wash off shore

The lies buried deep below
But the fire burns on
It only to show
The lies left, only to ash

Fire burning still, 
How long will it last
When the lies are gone
Will the fire move on

Is the flame dying slowly
Is the hottness turning coldly
Is it my breath I see in the air
Is it imagination

Nothing, to be aware
I gaze at the flame
Only to stare
But, it's burning me 
The only way to stop

Is for I, to set me free
But it's so amazing
My life is it's changing
Can I look a bit longer
. .Will this flame. .
Make me stronger?
