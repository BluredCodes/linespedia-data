title: "I'll Trust My Heart"

This is what I'm trying to say
For so long, for so many days
I just can't find a  way to explain
That, I love you, my love will remain
I can't think of too many words
That can express exactly how I feel
Becuase, it's greater than words can describe
It's true love. .its undescribable. .it's real
I can't just talk. .But I can't just hide
I don't know what to say becuase, I don't know
I think. . that "I Love You" is the phrase
That I want you to understand
And, it's not a crush, it's not a phase
If it were. .Right now
I wouldn't be crying
I wouldn't of cried so much
I wouldn't feel as if I were dying
I wouldn't feel this and such
I would be over you
So, I'm trusting my heart
That you love me
And you're being faithful and true.
