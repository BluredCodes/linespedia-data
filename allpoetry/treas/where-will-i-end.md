Tonight I learned
Colors are dull
numbers are fake
my blood is a lake
my heart is a creek
that to the lake
its going to leak
it carries the endings
that used to have beginnings
which are leaving
to the lake it’s sending
among my hands
blood drops land
to my toes
the creek still flows
out my physical self
into my soul
to see what journey I will toll
looking again
finding myself
where will it end
. .where will I end
