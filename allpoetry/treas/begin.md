title: "Begin"
writer: "Treas"

Slender shades
stalk the past
reminising can not last
All the memories soon to fade
so smile now
while you can
don't blame me when
you forget how
cry til you shine
open your mind
enter the future
with all your pride
shape the present
with your heart
being living your life
you can't get anywhere
if you don't start
