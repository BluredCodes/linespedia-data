---
title: "I will always love you"
writer: "Treas"

I always love you
no matter where you are
Even if you hurt me
becuase you always are

No matter what makes the sky cry
Or what even makes me die
I will always love you
Because it’s you and I

Tonights the day that we will wake
For ourselves, each others sake
We lack the heart, we lack the soul
No matter what or how we go

You creep around, with no mistake
You fail my honor, my heart you take
You leave me dying, in a tear
Afraid to talk for all my fear

But I always love you
No matter who you are
Because you and I are together
Even if we’re near or far

We can’t describe the love we feel
But we do know. .that it’s real
We like to say that love is true
Even if it’s just for you

We can’t explain the things inside
Becuase what we once had has died
It’s passed away no return
My heart misses it. .it will learn

We won’t ever come to an end
Because you’re our only friend
We can’t get rid of what is here
Becuase there’s just too much fear

But I always love you
No matter where you are
Even if you’re no where
You’re either here or there	

Kuz I’ll always love you
No matter if I can’t
You mean the world to me
Only, if you would stop and care
