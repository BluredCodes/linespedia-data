Gazing out across the ocean
The seagulls cry your name
Ever since you left that day
Nothing's been the same.

I remember your kiss goodbye
Saying you'd be back, yesterday
I am, although you said not to cry
I hope you're safe and everything's ok

I dazed up towards the stars
Their formation was your face
Trembling, reaching up, the sky I embraced
I love you, and I am glad that you're safe.
