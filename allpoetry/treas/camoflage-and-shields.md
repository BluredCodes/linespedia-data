Sometimes you feel as if you can't go on
You go put on your camoflage and invisible shield
After that, you feel rejuvinated and healed
you think no one can see you
. .you fit in with the crowd
Is it me. .or is everyone else like that too
You feel good about yourself
You feel wonderful. .and even proud
. .Because you "look so cool"
While. .you really look like a fool
You think no one can see how you really feel
. ."So I'm not who I am. .no big deal"
No one can see those tears under your skin
Because they're hidden so deep within
Your heart cries out in pain
Your muslces drown in strain
But your camoflage and your shield
. .they blend in too well w/ everyone else's
Everyone trying not to be themselves
With their camo and shields
It seems they can't go much further
Without turning back
Becuase self confidence is what they lack.No clue. but i thought of the world. .and thats what i got
