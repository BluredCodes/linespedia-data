Bob is someone I adore
For so many reasons:

B.rilliant
O.ptimistic
B.eneficial
          T.rying
          C.aring
          L.oving
                G.enerous
                S.ecure
                G.rateful
                        S.incere
                        E.xcellent
                        L.ucky
                E.ducated
                T.errific
                Y.outhful
         D.edicated
         C.reative
         L.eading
D.azzling
E.xotic
G.ifted

He is a special person
There's not enough words to say
But, how can someone smile. .
After they've had their worst day?this is about bob again hehe
