So we were walking by the creek
I heard a noise, decided to peak
It was a beauitful sight
Like no other I've seen before
Sitting there, glistening in the light
I decided to look some more
Water dripping from the skies
Falling so sweetly
So clear, so clean, there, what lies?
I jumped in, and so did you
We splashed around til it got dark
I dried off, you did too
We walked some more. .down the creek
Found a nice little pasture
Private property!. .we can sneak
We layed down. .to watch the stars
Gosh, us together. .under so many of them
Us so close. .Them. .So far
Far away, and far apart
I'm glad that's not us
We're here together in the dark
Under the stars, and the moon
It's wonderful. .It seems like a dream
Will it not end. .will it end soon?
I hate when seeming is real
Because. .this dream. .is a dream it seems
But. .So great. .so wonderful it feels
