---
title: "Missed"
writer: "Treas"
slug: "missed"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/37024-Missed-by-Treas"
---

I didn't know you that well
But from a distance you looked pretty nice
You look like the kind
For the right things
Anything you'd sacrifice
You looked like the type who's always right
Makes good decisions
With cautious precision
From what I hear
You changed peoples lives
lended out your ones and your fives
Helped anyone at anytime
Just to see them smile
You'd lend a hand
To calm someone down
You'd make someone laugh
To remove their frown
You were the friend of anyone
You were your parents nicest son
Too bad this is from a distance
And is just from what I hear
I'm sure if I had that chance
To have you as a friend
You would be my fav.
But now, sadly, I have to come
And  put flowers on your grave
I just wanted you to know
That you are truly missed
I hope your up in Heaven
And in peace I hope you rest.This is sorta for someone I know, but didn't know at all.
