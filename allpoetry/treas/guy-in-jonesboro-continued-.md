Well, as I said
You'd find a girl
One that knows you for you
One who's gona be faithful and true
Even though. .She is your Ex
As long as you love each other
There's no reason to say next
You have got nothing else to lose
From no one else as good as her to choose
What I see from her point of view
Is, an awesome guy, everything I expect to
Everything there is to like in a guy
And even more, if someone disagrees. .WHY
If they do, well, they're wrong
She's a very lucky girl indeed
and you'r a very lucky guy
I'm sure, no matter what, you'll succeed
You'll make life whatever you want it to be
Just know, I'm your friend
and I'll alway sbe
GOod luck, I hope she's the one
I hope you're happy together
I hope you have fun
And I hope she sees
You are for who you are
G'luck
