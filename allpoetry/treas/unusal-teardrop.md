title: "Unusal Teardrop."
writer: "Treas"

This tear is not an ordinary one
It doesn't sparkle in the sun
It's not clear, like the rest
It's red,
Perfectly attest
Why it's red
I do not know
Maybe for all my pain to show
And you know the oddest thing of all,
It's not a sphere or a ball
It's a square
Only one, it's solitare
It doesn't run down my cheek
It just sits there 
And makes me think
Why is this one different from the rest
Is it because I'm crying for you
And our love, of all, was the best?
Suddenly, this tear fell to the ground
Passionatly bursting, with no sound.
I walked away, pain in my eyes,
That's the first tear
I was unable to disguise.
