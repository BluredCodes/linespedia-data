writer: "Treas"

"Smile
 it makes me happy"

~gives a so fake grin~

"Steal
 I love to see you sin"

~grabs while they're away~

"Leave
 I don't like it when you stay"

~walks out and also closes door~

"Silence
 I didn't ask for more!"

~listens to every word~

"Understand
 and do as you have heard"

~works all day and night~

"Bow
 before me, do only as my right"

~bows and kisses the floor~

"Give
 me your money, I like it when you're poor"

~empties pockets, gives every last dime~

"Exhale
 inhale me all the time"

~breathes and trembles deep within~

"Obey
 my orders. . .again"

~is frightened~ "Can. .you let me go?. .and if so. .When?"

"DIE!
 unworthy one, die, you live no life but mine!"

. .what is a life. .what is it to live?

~dies ashamed of questions never known~
~dies guilty of false truths always being shown~ah have no idea. .but it was a good idea if it makes no sense *i think*
