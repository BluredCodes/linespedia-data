---
title: "Ah, life."
writer: "Treas"
slug: "ah-life"
source: "AllPoetry"

I look out the window
All I see is green
I see all the things that are to be seen
I see the leaves blow
Putting on a magnificent show
Dancing in the sunlight
Glimmering so bright
I see brown
swaying in the breeze
Dancing within all of the trees
I see blue
And some white, in the sky
Clouds blowing, seeming to fly
I can feel the wind
Blow in my face
Beautiful does it taste
I can hear the birds
The cars drive by
Ah, lifes wonderful
