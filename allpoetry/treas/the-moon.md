Tonight I looked up at the moon
I thought "I wonder if you're looking at it too."
I starred at it for minutes
In the deepest thought
Maybe for a moment, togetherness was caught.
Of all the times we always say
"We'll meet, there'll be a special day."
It all collides in my mind
Peace and serenity i will find.
Knowing you're out there, thousand miles away
Looking at the same thing, the same thing as I
Makes me happy, it makes me cry
It makes me gain a sense of feeling
Feelings only love can give
Lost in the eyes of the moon
Knowing our destiny will be soon
Maybe it'll be today
Maybe it will be tomorrow
So far away although it's just another day
Why do I feel such sorrow?
Maybe the moon was made for love
Maybe it was made for hope and a start
I just know, the moon has taught me much
And it has really changed the way I feel
It's brought serenity to my heart.
So remember next time you see the moon
Maybe that special someone
