There’s this guy that lives in Jonesboro
Who is often really lonely
I feel kind of bad, and also sad
Because I know he’s not a phony
He’s really nice, and really sweet
He does laundry, and is really neat
He also cooks, which is cool
But he thinks of himself as a fool
He thinks he’s ugly, which he’s not
He thinks he needs more money
He disrespects him self a lot
He has a job, makes good grades
Though it seems his self esteem fades. .
And he loves God, which is great
Meaning, of course, he’ll have good fate
But all he wants, at the moment,  is a girlfriend
A special person, with time to spend
But I know, a girlfriend he will get
I tell him all the time
I guess he’s impatient, and thinks he has to wait in line
All I can say is you’ll meet a girl. .
The girl of your dreams
Although, I know to you, unreal it seems
I can’t say when, and I can’t say how
But you will, and she’ll see the true you
Her only expression for you will be, “Wow!”This is about one of my friends
