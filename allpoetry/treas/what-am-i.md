How'd I get here. .Where am I. .What am I?Gliding through this world of 3 dimensionsMirror reflected images echo everywhereI see myself, but I'm not me?Darkness fills this placeI close my eyes. . .Swirling colors entrance my eyesEnchanting my soul with their colorsBeating so strongly, hardly I can hearSquinting harder I embrace their passionOverdosing, I open my eyesTotal darkness. .i shiverSeeing myself. .no longer I doIn amazement I stare,where am i . .what am i?Ummm. .I'm not sure.
