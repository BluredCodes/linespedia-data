writer: "Treas"

Words spoken by you fools
Makes me feel like
I’m drowning in these pools
these lakes. .these watered bodies
shattered glass. .within the lobbies
strangled ignorance
I can’t take any more
Why are the stupid adored
Do they not have lives
No matter how hard they try
I want them to LEARN
That they need to take part
Their life, they need to start
Stop being dumb
Baby, stop sucking on your thumb
You try to act normal
To life, can you not find a moral?
Don’t you know what you want?
Have you taken the time. .
For anything to decide?
Are you living in a lie
And not know any better?
Well I’m through trying to understand
Find  life with your own kind
I’m sick of you being blind
I’ve helped you, tried to at least
But you’re too entangled with the beast
When you find a life
Please tell me. .and maybe we can
Try to be friends
And with each other
Time we can spend
Til then, leave me alone
Stop calling me on the phone
Away from me, please run.
Or forced, you, I’ll be running from
