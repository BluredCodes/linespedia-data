You walked in my life
Left your soul
Now you're gone
But where'd you go

I want to return this missing piece
Or maybe set it free at least
If I let it go
Will it find it's way
Or on it's journey'
Be led astray. .?

In the paper I'll put an ad
Asking, any guys that have met me that are sad
Call me now, I have your soul
Please hurry before I let it go.Just a little piece I put together
