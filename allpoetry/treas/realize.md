Look at the good points in life
Don't be so negative
Laugh once in a while
Don't frown, just smile
Remember:
Dreams were meant to come true
Words are meant to be spoken
Life was menat to go on
If you ever need a friend,
Talk to someoen that will listen
If you ever have trouble,
Relax and think about it later
Just remember God is with you
Whereever you are and whatever you're doing
He's part of your life, 
The best part ever,
So don't leave him out
Because:
You don't like it when you're left out do you?an old poem i just found
