---
title: "You, Just You."
writer: "Treas"
slug: "you-just-you"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/52919-You--Just-You.-by-Treas"
---

Among your arms of gold
And the things I'm told
And of the things I know
I feel everything you show
Your oceany eyes filled with blue
My heart aches only for you
Your sun-glowing tantalizing hair
Leaves my heart burnt and bare
Your silky fingertips touching me
Leaves a dead trail of tears embedding a sea
The scent of your pine ridden soul
Makes me feel happy, it makes me grow
The presence of your simple form
Enchants me. . leaving the feeling new born
You, just you, revives me how
Just as you seem, amazes me now.:)
