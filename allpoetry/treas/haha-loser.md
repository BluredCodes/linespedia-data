You came up to me the other day
And just started talking away
I was suprised, since that was the first time
You usually ignore me and call me names
But. .for some reason things aren’t the same
You wrote me some notes
And walked me home
You won’t seem to leave me alone
We talked for two hours on the phone
I heard someone talking. .saying some things
I heard my name. .what was it you say?
“Yeah, she slept with him last night”
What??? that’s not right!!!
“I think, that if I’m nice enough, I’ll get some too”
Woah. .what’s up with you?
Your listening to the rumors
And spreading them too?
Gosh, I can’t beileve you!
Why do you listen to them. .
You want to know the truth??
Truth is, I’m not like that, if you knew me, you’d have proof
All you did was walk away
That was a pretty good day. .
Just becuase you listen to what they have to say
Doesn’t mean its true. .
Do you not have a life. .oops I forgot I was talking about YOU
Hehe. .I can’t help myself
Man you’re a loser. .hahahahajust bored
