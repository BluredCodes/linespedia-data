The dying trees
The pain she sees
The life she had
Was once so bad
She hadn't a life
She pulled out a knife
Was going to cut
But she "ran out of luck"
She was unattractive
Beauty she lacked
She didn't care for herself
Just her wealth
She didn't know the Lord
She was always bored
She wasn't an example
Her life always trampled
She's changed now
And I want to know how
Becuase, she is excellent today
And she's going to stay this way
She loves herself
She doesn't care about wealth
She is always in a good mood
She's never rude
She likse to help each other
And they help one another
She's a great girl
Living in a great world
She just has to make the best of things
In the end. .happiness is what it bringshehe about me mostly. .:)
