---
title: "Bones"
writer: "Treas"
slug: "bones"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/36255-Bones-by-Treas"
---

the roof still leaks
milk and stones
dripping over all of those bones
of ones falling short
of everything
who can and watch
evil alarms ring
they faint today
wake tomorrow
they fill the world
with hate and sorrow
we kill the ones
who hate yesterday
those are the ones
whose bones shall lay.
