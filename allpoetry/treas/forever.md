Forever

F-reedom from everything
O-riginally Occuring
R-eal as anything
E-ternity. .always
V-ery long time
E-verlasting time
R-egretless. .maybe

F-orever is a long time
A-nd only part of it is on Earth
D-o you spend time wisely
E-verything you do, is it worth?
S-top and think, are you wrong?

W-rong, yes
I-nitially wrong, no
T-hey think. .ah, forever in
H-eaven will be nice

T-hough. .that’s not where they’ll be
I-ntentionally it’s Hell
M-irroring back on them
E-nding their happines and dreams of forever.

E-nd is what it is
N-ice is what it’s not
D-eath is what it causes
S-anity is not it’s plot

W-illingness to hurt you more
I-npearing nothing for
T-hose who reach out and speak
H-urry to it’s door

P-anically thinking
A-nticipating the best				
I-ndevious decisions made
N–ever again will you rest.
