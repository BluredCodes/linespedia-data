I am so mad
So sick, it makes me sad
I need to know
Why can't I find a good friend?
Sometimes I just wish life would end
I really hate this state
I wish I never moved here
But I guess it's too late
Everyone I know hates each other
I can't hang out with more than one person at a time
Becuase one of them will get mad
And the other one will get glad
I'm just stuck in the middle of everything
I want to punch everyone
Then walk off, and happily sing
That's how I feel
And the feeling is real
People need to figure some stuff out
Call me back when you do
And, again, maybe I'll talk to you.im jsut really mad right now
