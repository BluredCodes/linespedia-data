You say we're too far apart
And so we can't go out,
But you know, I do love you
About it there is no doubt.
I know we can't touch each other
But in our hearts we can be together
I just don't want you to find soem girl
It would destroy me, and my whole world
If your excuse is still the same
. .Which I think is really lame :P
I guess all I can do is pray
That someday,
We'll be together
One day, The greatest of all days.Just my thoughts. .
