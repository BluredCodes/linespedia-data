Tonight I went out to my special spot
  It's beautiful,
     I go there a lot
And too, beautiful it was tonight
  The temperature, it was just right
     Not hot, nor cold, warm
With a light breeze
  Rustling through the bare, winter trees.
     I layed down, on the ground
It was loud, with out a sound.
  Just crickets and birds
     To describe it, there's not enough words
And, amazed I was, by the sky
  It was so enchanting it made me cry.
     Pink, purple, blue, and red
Painted on the sky, 
  All my emotions clearly said
     My eyes glued to it
Questions arose. .all were "why"
  A warm breeze hit my face
     It had a. .warm, bitter taste
Serenity can make you feel so weird
  I sought out things, that I've feared
     Just being in a place like that
You learn a lot about yourself
  Quietly I sat,
     To listen to the things
That I never hear
  Listening to the things I love
     The things I fear
Being so happy,
  I can't help but shed a tear.just got that out of tonight
