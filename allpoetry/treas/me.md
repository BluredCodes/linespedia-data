Hanut me
Taunt me
Keep me down

Hit me
Kick me
Watch me frown

Stab me
Grab me
Build my mound

Burn me
Yearn me
Kill my sound


Shoot me
Boot me
To the ground

Whip me
Hit me
Slap me 'round

Feel me
Heal me
Til I'm bound

Free me
See me
While I'm downi have no idea...
