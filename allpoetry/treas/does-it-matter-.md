---
title: "Does it matter. ."
writer: "Treas"
slug: "does-it-matter-"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

I am fake
Yet so real
Does it matter
No big deal

I am harmless
But cause much pain
Does it matter
No one's the same?

I am fruitful
Not to myself
Does it matter
I'm nothing else

I am great
I'm so small
Does it matter
To us all?

I am living
Inside I'm dead
Does it matter
On the death bed?

I am cowardly
To my kin
Does it matter
That I never win?

I am afraid
Of myself
Does it matter
That I'm someone else

I am afraid
Of this place 
Does it matter
I live a race

Does it matter
Who I am. .
Does it matter
Where I go
Does it matter at all. .
No.no idea. .but. .it's about someone FAKE. .
