you say all you live is a lie
you say you nothing's left but to die
you say your life of all is the worst
you say you always have to be first

You think, you need this chocolate bar
Because, you're starving, and you can't wait an hour to eat
 
Remember. .some little girl in a corner
Not eating for 3 days, sitting there thinking she'd like some meat
 
You want. .this brand new dress
To go with the other 50 you have at home

Remeber. .some kid is dying, in rags
While sitting in the dirt, feeling all alone

You neeeed. .this diamond ring
To match with some clothes you'll wear only one time

Remember. .that kid is sitting there filthy
Wishing they had not a ring. .but a nickle or dime

You have to get this new CD
Because, it is so cool, something everyone's gota hear

Remember. .he's sitting there defending his self
Without a doubt about life, or even one fear

You think. .gosh I wish I had a beautiful house
With 4 stories, 3 bathrooms, maybe even a guest house outside

Remember. .some kid's living in the world
In a shack, cold, starving, living on nothing but pride

You know you have got to get this awesome car
Becuase. .it's so cool, and you'd look so good in that ride

Remember. .that kid sitting out there
Walking place to place, his feet about to die

You have to come home at eleven
Becuase your mom thinks thats an appropraite time

Remeber. .he's out there somewhere
Hoping someday he'll have a house, even to be home by nine

You think you have a horrible life
Because "nothing goes your way"

Well remember there's someone out there
Who has it worse, who died today

Remember the children who starve to death
. .Who don't have homes. .Who don't have moms and dads

You think. .you need everything like "this"
And you know thats really sad

People go through Hell everyday
And yet, things have to be your way

It's an unfair time, in an unfair place
Everybody won't agree, but look at the face
Of a dying boy, who's ribs stick out
Who, is smiling, becuase he had a meal last night
While you're mad, kuz you havent ate in an hour, right?

You are better off than many others
So when you think, Gosh this is BAD
Remember the people, who live in YOUR world everyday
