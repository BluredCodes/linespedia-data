My name es Tureeesuh 
I live in a umm shed 
I came from der Mexico 
Wid no clathes or er bed. . 
I ave a furend 
es name es Joe 
We know each one of us 
For times and times and so 
we go to park 
And eat pork rinds 
in mexico. .me like rat kind 
Here in USA 
what get me 
es you got to pay? 
USA dollars 
No dinero i have 
no can steal pork rinds 
camerus, all kinds 
me see them camerus 
So steal, me cannot 
til the uh next time 
eat the pork rinds es duh plot.No Clue
