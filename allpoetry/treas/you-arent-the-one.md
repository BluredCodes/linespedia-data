title: "You aren't the one"
writer: "Treas"

Crawling under the sheets on the bed
Remember the last words that you said
"Your dignity, your pride, is too much for me
It's not you. .it's me you see?"
When you said that, I began to cry
Because, we had something, you and I
It's been 3 years, and just now you realize
That it was 'your bad', 
The past 2.5 years have all be lies
Do remember anything we've done, and all we had?
This is pathetic, it's really sad
No time to think about anything
Just walk out now. .leave me here
If you can't appreciate me, nor I you dear
I'll search again for my special one
Although I thought it was you . .
But, once I think that, you become untrue
I'll find my guy, at the right time, the right day
Just, you'll be in my memory. .and you'll always stay.
