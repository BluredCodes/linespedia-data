Kangaroos hop through my mind
not the cute, furry ones
but the mean, evil kind
they hop around killing my heart
giving me things from which i cant part
stealing my life, sticking it in their pouch
over there, i hide, behind the couch
chasing me faster, i cannot escape
why cant all these kangaroos be fake!!
devouring me whole, i am no more
just wait til you see, for you
what the kangaroos have in storehaha i dont know
