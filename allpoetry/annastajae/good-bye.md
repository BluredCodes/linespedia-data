---
title: "Good-Bye??"
writer: "annastajae"
slug: "good-bye"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/51814-Good-Bye---by-annastajae"
---

How do I let go I've loved him for so long?
I'd give anymore that I could.
How do you find the words to say good-bye?
When your heart don't have the heart to say good-bye.
           ***good-bye***
Is this the end?
Are you sure?
This will take you somewhere you've never been before.
It's so hard to just let go...
This is the one and only love i've ever known...so..
How do you find the words to say good-bye?
When your heart don't have the heart to say good-bye.this was written for my the love of my life who died two years ago last tuesday (march 12)
