Extrasensory perception a gift or a curse?

If bad is seen, then bad shall occur.
A gift...no it's a curse, a curse of death,
through the years a repetition of death drove me 
to the insanity of my mind.
This prolonged afflicted disorder of my mind 
limits the sanitary thoughts.

Extrasensory perception...how could it be a gift? It's a curse!
