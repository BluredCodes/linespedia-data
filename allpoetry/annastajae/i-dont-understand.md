I DON'T UNDERSTAND!
   ***Why are you so mad?***
The pain that goes through my heart to see you this way is like a thousand knives cutting deep into my body causing the life of me to drain.

I DON'T UNDERSTAND!
   ***What i did do that was so wrong?***
Tell me now what it is i did, so i will never do it again. It's not worthy of losing you.

I DON'T UNDERSTAND!
   ***Why are you so mad, we have any attachments?***
no attachments...enough said.

I DON'T UNDERSTAND!
   ***When will the attachments come?****sigh* no attachments...maybe that should be the name!
