---
title: "You lied..."
writer: "khaisha"

It all started as a friendship: kind and sweet, 
Something special- I never thought it will be. 
I didn't know if it was right... 
And you lied. 

You started to get closer, 
"So long" to being "just friends"; 
Now you were always there... 
And you lied- I could tell. 

We became "the perfect couple" 
Too perfect- I have to say. 
For you: there was never a problem... 
I know you lied everyday. 

Slowly everything changed we drift apart 
Lately you were not there- 
If you didn't call, I didn't care. 
I believe it was wrong right from the start 
'Cause everything seemed perfect- 
Something wasn't quite right. 
Finally, you broke my heart, 
And I just I can't forgive you for that. 
Everything is said and done- 
And I’ve missed you for too long 
Now I must go on- 
Realize that you lied... That everything was a lie... And let go.
