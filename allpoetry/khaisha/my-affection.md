title: "My affection"

MY LIFE HAS CHANGED AGAIN,

CAUSE I'VE FOUND IN YOU MORE THAN A MAN,

MORE THAN A PARTNER-

I'VE FOUND A TRUE FRIEND.



EVERYTHING HAPPENED SO FAST,

BUT I'VE ALWAYS KNOWN WHAT I WANT...

SOMEHOW I KNEW YOU WERE 'THE ONE'-

THE ONLY ONE WHO'LL WIN MY HEART.



DEEP IN MY THOUGHTS YOU WERE,

SINCE THE DAY WE FIRST MET.

AND FROM THAT DAY ON I NEEDED HELP-

CAUSE I KNEW THAT I CARED, BUT I WOULDN'T TELL.



MY FEELING GREW STRONGER,

DIDN'T KNOW WHAT TO DO:

CONFESS, DENY OR TELL THE TRUTH.

OH GOD, IF I ONLY KNEW:

 THAT I CAUGHT MORE THAT YOU ATTENTION;

 THAT YOU'LL BRING TO MY LIFE PERFECTION;

 THAT YOU'LL GIVE TO MY HEART DIRECTION;

 THAT EVERYDAY IS DEEPER OUR CONNECTION,

 AND BY EVERY SECOND GETS MORE OVERWHELMING MY AFFECTION.
