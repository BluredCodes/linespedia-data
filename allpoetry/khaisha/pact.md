title: "Pact"
writer: "khaisha"

TODAY I MADE A PACT,
AND DON'T THINK NOTHING BAD.
I DON'T LIKE THE DEVIL,
I DON'T PLAY NEAR HIS HOUSE.

I MADE A PACT TODAY,
AND IT WAS WITH A FRIEND
I SWORE I WILL TELL
I SWORE I'LL TALK TO THE MAN.

A PACT WAS MADE TODAY,
WE SHOOK HANDS AND SAID:
"IF YOU TELL- I'LL TELL..."
PROMISE WE'LL BE TOGETHER 
WHEN WE DECIDE WHAT WE'LL SAY.

THE PACT: TODAY WAS MADE,
AND WE EVEN HAVE A WITNESS;
SHE DOESN'T NEED NO PROOF
AS LONG AS WE EXPRESS WHAT NEEDS TO SAID.

MADE WAS THE PACT TODAY,
I DON'T KNOW WHAT WAS I THINKING OF,
AFTER ALL THE SORROW, THE HEARTBREAK, THE PAIN-
HOW CAN I LET HIM KNOW HOW I'VE FELT?

TODAY WAS MADE THE PACT,
I THOUGHT YOU SHOULD KNOW YOU WERE INVOLVED;
I NEED... I HAVE TO SAY WAS IN MY HEART TODAY-
AND I HOPE YOU DON'T GET ME WRONG.

YES, TODAY A PACT WAS MADE:
TO REVEALED NOTHING BUT THE TRUTH,
TO TELL YOU WHAT MY MOTHER ALREADY KNEW,
TO LET YOU KNOW THAT FOR A LONG TIME I'VE BEEN BLUE...
TO FINALLY CONFESS THAT I'M IN LOVE WITH YOU...
