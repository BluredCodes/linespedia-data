I HOPE I CAN BRING YOU HAPPINESS WHEN YOUR LIFE GETS LONELY.AND IF BY ANY CHANCE I AM THE CAUSE OF YOUR PAIN,I HOPE THAT THE MEMORY OF ME MAKE YOU SIGH, MAKE YOU SMILE;BUT PLEASE DON'T CRY.AND FINALLY PLEASE UNDERSTAND:THAT WHAT HAPPENED BETWEEN US WAS NOT A DREAM, IT WAS AND WILL ALWAYS BE THE MOST BEAUTIFUL REALITY, ALTHOUGH IT'S OVER...LOVE ALWAYS AND FOR EVER...REAL DREAM
