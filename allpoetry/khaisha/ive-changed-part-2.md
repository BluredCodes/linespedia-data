I'VE REALIZED I'VE CHANGEDCAUSE I DON'T SEE THE SAME PERSON IN THE MIRROR EVERYDAY.I USED TO SEE A GIRL, WHO DIDN'T KNOW WERE SHE WAS HEADING,BUT SHE KNEW SHE WAS GOING SOMEWHERE.I USED TO SEE A GIRL SO OPEN TO LOVE, THAT HER HEART WAS BROKEN SO MANY TIMES SHE COULDN'T COUNT THEM, BUT SHE REMEMBERED EACH AND EVERYONE OF THEM.I USED TO SEE A GIRL SO WEAK SHE HAD NO SELF-CONFIDENCE, NO SELF-ESTEEM.SHE SAW HERSELF AS AVERAGE OR EVEN BELOW- NOT KNOWING SHE WAS ABOVE, NOT KNOWING SHE WAS ON TOP.I KNOW I'VE CHANGED, CAUSE NOW I SEE A CLEAR VIEW OF MYSELF;I KNOW I'M HEADING FOR THE TOP;I'M STILL LOOKING FOR MY ONE TRUE LOVE;I KNOW I'M ABOVE...  CAUSE I'VE ACCEPTED MY CHANGESI NOW ACCEPT MY GROWTH.
