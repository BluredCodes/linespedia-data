title: "I need to tell you"
writer: "khaisha"
slug: "i-need-to-tell-you"

I WANT TO TELL YOU SO MANY THINGS AND I DON'T KNOW HOW TO START.
I DON'T WANT THE HOURS TO PASS BY, WHEN YOU ARE BY MY SIDE.
I WANT TO PLEASE YOU, KISS YOU; FEEL THAT YOU ARE PART OF ME.
HOLD YOU SO CLOSE, THAT I COULD FEEL YOUR HEART BEAT.
I WANT TO ALWAYS HAVE YOU, TO SEE WHO WOULD I MISS.
I, SOMETIMES, DON'T WANT TO SEE YOU, TO SEE IF YOU WILL MISS ME.
I WANT TO CARESS YOU WITH THE TENDERNESS OF THE WIND.
I NEED THE SAME CARESS, I NEED TO SEE YOUR FACE.
I NEED YOUR COMPANY, THAT'S THE ONLY THING THAT LIGHT MY DAYS.
I NEED TO BE IN YOUR ARMS, FEEL YOU TRULY IN LOVE; KNOW THAT YOU'RE ALL MINE.
I NEED YOUR BEAUTIFUL SMILE, THAT GIVES ME COMFORT, THAT GIVES ME LIFE.
AND I NEED SIMPLY WHAT I WANT: YOUR LOVE; 
THINK THAT IT WILL BE FOREVER UNTIL THE END OF THE MOON AND THE SUN.
I NEED TO TELL YOU HOW I FELL, KNOW THAT YOU ARE LOVING ME;
BELIEVE AGAIN IN DREAMS, BELIEVE IN YOU AND ME
AND HEAR FROM YOUR MOUTH THAT OUR LOVE WILL ALWAYS BE.
I NEED TO HEAR, I NEED TO FELL,
I NEED TO TELL YOU SO MANY THINGS...

THIS POEM WAS A GOOD START OR SO I BELIEVE.
