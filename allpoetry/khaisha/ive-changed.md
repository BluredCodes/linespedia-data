---
title: "I’ve changed…"
writer: "khaisha"
slug: "ive-changed"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/18911-I-ve-changed--by-khaisha"
---

HERE I AM, ALL BY MYSELF,LOOKING AT A NIGHT SKY, THAT IS NOT THE SAME;NOT BECAUSE IT CHANGED, BUT BECAUSE I HAVE CHANGED.I'VE LIVED NUMEROUS MOMENTS, OVERCOME OBSTACLES, OVERPOWER CHALLENGES.FOUGHT MANY BATTLES, WON SO FEW WARS.I'VE GAINED KNOWLEDGE AND THEREFORE POWER;I'VE TOUCHED SO MANY PEOPLE- I HOPE THEY'LL BE THOUSANDS.MY LIFE HAS BEEN TOUGH,BUT IT HAS MADE ME GROW.THE SKY, THIS NIGHT, THE PEOPLE, THE WORLD-EVERYTHING IS DEFINITELY NOT THE SAME.EVERYTHING HAS CHANGED, BUT IS BECAUSE I HAVE CHANGED...
