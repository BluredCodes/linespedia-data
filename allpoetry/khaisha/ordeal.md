ANOTHER DAY HAS PASSED BYAND YOU'RE STILL NOT BY MY SIDE.I GUESS YOU MIGHT BE WAITING FOR SOMETHINGI KNOW I CAN HELP YOU FIGURE 'IT' OUT.AND YOU MIGHT BE WONDERING HOW...LONGING TO KNOW RIGHT NOW;WAITING TO HAVE NO MORE DOUBTS;MAYBE EVEN WILLING TO REACH OUT.BUT I GUESS YOUR NEVER KNOW-AND THERFORE I'LL NEVER KNOW.I WON'T TELL YOU, I WON'T CONFESS.I WILL NOT PUT MY HEART TO THAT TEST.AND MY LOVE WILL BE A SECRET- NEVER TO BE REVEALED,SO INTIMATE AND CONFIDENTIAL- NO ONE WOULD KNOW WHAT'S THE DEAL;HERE'S THE TRUTH- HERE'S WHAT'S REALLOVING YOU AND HIDING IT WILL BE: MY BIGGEST ORDEAL.
