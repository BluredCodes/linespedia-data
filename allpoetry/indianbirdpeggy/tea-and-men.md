---
title: "Tea and Men"
writer: "indianbirdpeggy"
slug: "tea-and-men"
source: "AllPoetry"

I like my tea strong and sweet
just like I like my men
His strength unseen
it always seems arises from within

He holds me oh so close
when life is oh so grim.
I like my tea strong and sweet
just like I like my men

Such softness that cushions me
he wills to keep inside
Just with me doth he show
the love he cannot hide.

I like my men strong and sweet
he gives me butterflies.
