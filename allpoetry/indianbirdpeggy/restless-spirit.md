This restless spirit I feel inside
try's to crawl out, like an itch,
I cant relieve.

Something inside just dying to fly
to soar, with the cool night winds,
dying to flee.

I know that I am loved in my life 
yet now and then, from within,
yearn's to be free.

So tell me, Am I normal or wild
Or am I just me, a restless spirit.I WORKED REALLY HARD ON PERFECT RHYME AND METER AND STIILL KEEPING THE MEANING CLEAR... PLEASE LET ME KNOW WHAT YOU THINK? THANKS PEG.
