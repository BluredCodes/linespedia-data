Patiently I sit all dressed in white,in front of the mirror brushing my hair,thinking of things all the while,that we will magically share,for only you my love I care.Shivering with anticipation,making sure everythings just right,readying for such beautiful passion,that I'm hoping we'll share tonight,in these soft glowing lights.Greeting you with such love and fire,and letting you have your way with me,giving in to all our desires,underneath our silken sheets,letting our spirits fly free.Falling down from upon the stars,and all our energy's been spent,we'll lay together arm in arm,undying love we pledgeknowing our love....was Heaven sent.dedicated to my wonderful husband..Clarencie,(tonto)Lewis. :)
