Pain, do you know of this?
it comes to me not with a kiss
Suffering has long been my name,
I'll never know when it shall wane

Every trying day I'll say,
tomorrow will be a better day
Yet this name I seem to possess,
knows not how to give one day's rest

Sometimes even the mind can heal,
with special care and love thats real
Yet this pain that bears down on me,
not even those will bring relief

For the kind of pain that I speak,
shuts me down and makes me weak
So all the doctors have to say,
no walk will I come one day

Pain, I do know of this.I wish I did better on more detail of the pain..
