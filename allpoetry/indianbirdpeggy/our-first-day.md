Oh that very first day
when you were on your way
I couldn't wait to see
your golden little face

We had a rough time you and I
both trying to survive the night  
All I could do during that time
with all my heart I prayed

To see the morning sun shine on us
from the glorious God above
So I could hold you in my arms
through out our life long days

And so when you finally appeared
I knew I had nothing left to fear
You are here with me now and always
my son my life my babe..Dedicated to my son Bradley.. who could never let me down..love you babe... mom
