I've always tried to teach you just right
to give you love and hold you tight,
Through life changes the ups and downs,
I'm holding your hand to guide you.

So remember the times that we've had
the days long past of good and bad,
They'll show you the answers that you seek,
When searching for the real you.

I love you Jason with all my heart
remember this even when we're apart,
I'm here to support you year after year,
I hope you read this from me to you..dedicated to my second son who lives with his dad now after I've had him for 13 years.. I still see him everyday after school though.. he just wanted to spend more time with my ex.I just want him to know how special he is.. thanks for listening all. peg
