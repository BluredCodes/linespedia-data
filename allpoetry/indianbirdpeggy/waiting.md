In the gloom of the night
I sit and wait patiently,

Even though I feel I'll never see the sight
of your face that I've waited for 
forever and eternity,

Minutes have passed one by one
and each one seems 
like an hours just done,

And the days were months
the times without you 
all I did was want,

Your body your voice next to me
for the whole world to see
that the one I loved is Freddie
