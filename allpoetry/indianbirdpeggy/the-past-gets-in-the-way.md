The past gets in the way
for all the words I long to say

Things that happened long ago
instills in me such sorrow

To tell you I love you
and think you are great,
you I would not trade

So thank you Dad 
for all you've done
so now I take the plunge

Things that once were sad
that weren't so kind, 
forgotten in my mind

Good and bad times
through all of these,
we knew you loved us

So now that I have grown
my heart needs to learn,
don't let the past get in the way
of the words I long to say...
I love you Dad!
