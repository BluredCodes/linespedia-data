Precious white amongst the graysagainst the dark blue sky'sA rarity, so few beholdto see with thine own eye'sSpread your wings within the windfor all the world to seeYour beauty once foretold,of peace and love you bringA snow white dove's silent flightfortell's the waiting springI'm revising this one cause I was hoping someone would come just a little closer to what this one meant to me.. thank you very much for your time .. Peg
