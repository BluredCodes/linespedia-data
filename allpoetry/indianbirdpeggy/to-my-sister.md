---
title: "To My Sister"
writer: "indianbirdpeggy"
slug: "to-my-sister"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/15020-To-My-Sister-by-indianbirdpeggy"
---

Even though we have a rocky past,
I remember that little girl I loved
and still do.

My friend you've stood beside me 
when we were young,
Even now, when our lifes almost sung.

I remember all the good times we've had,
I wish I could help you now...
It makes me so sad,
To see the things your going through.

Meter and rhyme I've thrown out the window,
to get this message across, how much your 
loved and apreciated my whole life through.

I want you to know that I think you
are great, your acceptance of me
I do appreciate.

When no one else understood who I am,
with you I never, had to make a false stand.

I want you to know that as our life's
passes us by, I want you to be happy, 
And dont ask no more whys.

The whys will kill you, if you let them rule,
believe me I know this, I've been through this too.

Fate has a way to humble us for God,
it was his intentions, 
not the things that we did.

Forgiveness and love I give to you always,
I love you my sis, and hope you find your way..I know this one is sloppy, and probalby wont mean alot to most people, but I just wanted just one special person to read this.. my sis..
