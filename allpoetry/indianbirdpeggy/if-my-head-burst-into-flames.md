If my head burst into flames,
I don't think It would matter
probably would feel better,
Wouldn't even know the difference...

This consuming pain in my head
sometimes I wish I was dead...
Stealing away my appreciation,
of life's beauty of day to day living.

Taking this pill and that pill,
yet not helping not even a little,
tired of trying and getting nowhere
Knowing they want surgery yet again.

Oh God when will this pain end?
thinking that I'd rather die
cause it hurts to much to even cry,
My head is on fire, I'm just so tired.
