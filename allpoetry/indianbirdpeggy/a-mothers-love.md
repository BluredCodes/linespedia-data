title: "A Mothers Love"

Where were you when I needed this?
Since I was young you've pulled away
from all my love,
from all my kisses.

You've never accepted me
for the me that I am,
You've knocked me down
time and time again.

I wonder why it is
that I still try,
To receive your love
through out my life.

The pain you've caused
stays here in my heart,
Creating deep holes
as black as the night.

Will I ever learn
to stop being that child,
Who sat in the corner 
waiting for your smile.

Of a love your not able to give,
Where were you when I needed this?
Your still not there
to give me your acceptance,
of me, the person that I am..
