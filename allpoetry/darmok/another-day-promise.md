Another Day
by Darmok
2-20-02

~*~*~
As surely as the sun does rise
Into their hearts she gently sets.
And calls the night light softly lets,
The dimming day within her breast.
~*~*~*~
Given promise another day,
The chill of night will briefly lay.
And rise again belong the day,
Her warmth and smile forever say.
~*~*~*~
Belong, 
I to thee every heart,
And share of me of that you see.
The light of love within set free,
Will light the day, what can, 
will be.
~*~
