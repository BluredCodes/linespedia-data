Limb Burger
by Darmok  12/21/01

I knew not what was instore tonight, 
which way my fall would take me.
For out on a limb I stood the day, 
in angst I would await thee. 

Into your heart I fell today, 
beyond the layers of face, 
abide in thee a hidden place, 
away from hells embrace. 

So comfort me assure me of,
the quiet beat therein.
Oh seasons past and winter's last,
the melting packs of sin.

Reach beyond the wired net,
forever reap your words.
Cling unto the quietness,
the strength released... the birds.

-Darmok "Sucatt, their eyes Open"
