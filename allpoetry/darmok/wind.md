Wind..........
by Darmok
2-5-02

ah....there you are....
sweet whispers in my ear........
how lovely is the wind 
that would caress my heart, 
and hold me for all eternity 
in her breath that we 
would never part. 

Though the spirit 
within her has the might 
of many legions, yet
she lays at rest.
She regards this soul 
within the aspect of her eyes 
and bears me within her glint, 
a vision of gentleness.  
He whispers
 'caress, a breeze that rolls
over my flesh.....'

Her kiss is almost there 
but not, ....a phantom 
cannot be touched,
....with hands that is 
or lips that thirst, 
a lover’s breath 
of lust.

~*~*~*~*~*~*~*~*
as allusive as love sometimes is, 
she can be as close as is the wind....
sometimes caught within our sails, 
filling the extremes of our souls.

ahhh...to feel the wind, to feel love
and the freedom to be. 
My sails full again, 
in love and filled 
with thee.
