Anyone been there?

                          "Begone, Be not"

                       by Darmok

                       No, not me ….don’t call me in,
                       To that room where the light is dim.
                       NO, don’t speak…not those words,
                       Nor show me the evil within.

                       Why do you close the door,
                       And quietly sit before.
                       It taken slowly from your drawer,
                       The unimaginable horror instore.

                       I can’t control my breath,
                       As heaps of air choke my throat.
                       Nor rest the racing beats within,
                       As bursting vessels sweat and soak.

                       Should I sit or stand it matters not,
                       The end is near, the time forgot.
                       “You are no longer required”,
                       your time expired. Begone, be not.
