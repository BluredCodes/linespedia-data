Swing Set ~*~Sun Set
by Darmok  3/20/02


Rust covered legs in abandoned yards, 
swings set in concrete
universes hanging idly in want,
echoing children's' voices
and screams... of joy and tears
for bloody knees and broken
spirits wandering empty lots. 
 ~*~
The wind speaks in patient remembrance, 
inspiring swings to utter their
squeaks from a distant day
long forgotten. 
~*~ 
Only the birds come to play, 
to perch and drop 
their layering filth below, 
undisturbed, there on swings 
given to the moon and her tides, 
the sun's scorch and stare.
~*~
A relic of what was, 
fades away in decomposition, 
slowly disappearing
in the silhouette of 
the decaying 
sunset.
