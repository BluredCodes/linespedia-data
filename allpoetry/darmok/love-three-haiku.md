a Haiku 
by Darmok
Love

Smite me with your love,
Embrace me with your passion,
Spare me not your joy.

-----------------------------------

"Deer Winter"
by Darmok

Naked days of Fall,
Winter sighs a chilling breath,
The bark is bitter.


===================================


Days wait for sunshine,          (5)	
Nights surrender to moonlight.   (7)
Dream behind closed eyes.        (5)
