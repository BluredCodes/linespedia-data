The beauty of your execution, 
is a matter of contention
that elaborates the realization 
of what is and what is not.
In so much as I yield unto that 
which is unspoken I am resolve
To speak of it, that which is not said.  

Beyond that which cannot 
be reached by mere reason,
I am famished by my own ignorance 
of what I’m fed.

Believe me yes, believe me not, 
there is but one thing
Left to be said, 
around and around and around I go, 
where I land No one knows.

Except the void that is replacement
for the emptiness of nothing, 
Something that was not, 
but now is dead.
