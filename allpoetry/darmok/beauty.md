'Beauty'

                       by Darmok 6/12/01

                       Beauty, thou hast no name.
                       You are every face...gentle women,
                       every smile so generously served.

                       Blushing cheeks and eyes that drown,
                       away sorrow, away anguish, away fear.

                       Whispers upon your lips.
                       Endless waves adorn.
                       Soft and gentle, your tenderness,
                       caress the wind and soar.

                       There be my heart,
                       rising to your glance.
                       Would that I know beauty,
                       your smile, your touch, your heart,
                       ...I would know love.Written in praise of women, and for men to reflect upon.
