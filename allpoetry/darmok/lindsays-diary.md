Lindsay's Diary, "Did you know"
 by dad
 
 My diary is full of pages,
 of thoughts never shared.
 Days that I wish you'd been,
 there to stroke my golden hair.
 
 Scraped knees, and tiny bandages
 to cover another pair of owies...
 Tooth fairy's dream, days of longing, 
 tears by myself, unseen.
 
 I know you love me and care
 ever so much, it's just too hard
 to let you go. 
 Too hard to watch your car 
 fade away in my heart,
 did you ever know?
 
 So here I have spilled the buckets full, 
 tears rained, poured from my soul.
 Reminding myself what I would have said, 
 if you'd been here... to know.
