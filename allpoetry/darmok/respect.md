Two gentlemen passing 
tip their hats, 
well aware what is written 
in the space between them.

Their silent acknowledgment 
bears witness of the respect 
seen gleaming in their eyes. 

For what has passed beyond these doors, 
lays written forever
in those pages 
that are called 
memories. 




(a little borrowed from La Vita Nova, by Dante Alighieri ) .For Slagman and Darmok, a mutual respect of each others work.'Kewl'
