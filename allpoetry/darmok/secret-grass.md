Secret Grass
	by Darmok
	1/10/02
	On blades of grass our secrets sewn,
	O'er hills of years we've known.
	Come what may the day will pass,
	and lay me down upon the grass.
	Beneath a sky as deep as you,
	Into your heart of what I knew.
	There passes clouds and wishes too,
	Inside your eyes of deep deep blue.
	Surrender all, upon the grass,
	For dreams and visions of longing past.
	Falling ‘ever, the tears that last,
	Upon the blades of secret grass.
