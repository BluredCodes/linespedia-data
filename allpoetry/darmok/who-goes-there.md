"Who goes there"
                    by Darmok
                    10-12-01

                    Who goes there?
                    What flag your allegiance owed,
                    And of the blood that flows,
                    From wounds deeper than your soul.
                    Where I would not look or know,
                    The face of he or she I owe.
                    Gave me my life, my breath, 
                    The future whole.
                    For in my stead there you go,
                    Who goes there, I do not know.

                    Thank you.My future...perhaps the Future whole, I owe to those who 'go',...but not all return.
