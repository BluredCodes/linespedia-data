Night Streams
(rain, trickles...sea)
by Darmok 2/19/02

~*~
Into the rush of gathering streams, 
fed by trickles it grows.   
A rushing river feeds the Sea, 
and earth she drinks of those.
~*~*~
Across the earth in silver streaks
it tickles a barren land.  A quenching
thirst it brings relief, and wets
the throats of sand.
~*~*~
On rocky slopes in sheets it falls,
Glazed sculptures draped in white.
The Titan’s hand directs its course,
What falls away from you
this night
~*~as it rains, it will pour...and so shall you see............more :)
