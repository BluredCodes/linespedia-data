Sandcastles 
                       by Darmok 2/14/99 

                       Tick tock tick tock, 
                       beats the rythm of the clock. 
                       Silent passages of endless time, 
                       inhaled, exhaled oh breath of mine. 

                       Like sandcastles who stood before, 
                       waves dismayed that fell their door. 
                       These grains of sand upon endless shore, 
                       there wet in hands for evermore. 

                       At rest in hidden hollows, 
                       caves filled with ancient swallows. 
                       These winds of eons past, 
                       seek a new life beyond the grasp. 

                       Old, new, what is it? 
                       the moment it is, its not. 
                       Put me then on these shores when I rest, 
                       Let me make sandcastles.
