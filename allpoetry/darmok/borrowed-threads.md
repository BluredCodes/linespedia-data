Borrowed Threads





 by Darmok, 620 96



Borrowed threads for twilling loom,

the cloth is born of love.

Hands that work the tender threads,

have seen and dreamt the pattern of.



The finished cloth of woven words,

embrace the soul they touch.

Blessed with colors dipped in heart,

the cloth reveals so much.
