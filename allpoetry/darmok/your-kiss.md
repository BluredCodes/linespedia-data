Your Kiss
by Darmok
1-3-02

Elusive as slips of wind
    breathing in hollow trunks,
    You pass me in the night
    creating mystery in dreams that fade away.

Just before dawns muted light
    would stream through to wake me,
    in that slow pouring rain of
    timeless clarity...I see you, 
    through eyes nearly open.

As with all apparitions, and
    vague memories from a night's 
    journey to Shangri-La....
You vanish, and the dream almost within
    my grasp, slips off the precipice 
    and into the gray of the passing night.

The clock resumes its clicking
    concealing your face with reality.
Pushing me further away
    From beauty.

A persistent urge to return,
    to behold your face...
    piques my desire,
    and there you are kept
    at the edge of recognition.

So near,
    In the daunting ether, 
    just out of sight... 
Who are you, to whom belongs
    the face of beauty that I have seen
    and touched in my dreams?

Flesh so soft, succulent lips,
    would that I drink from them
    their endless wetness.

A kiss of mixed tongues 
    and Elixirs of love.
I would recognize you
    with my eyes closed,
    simply by your kiss.Written January 3rd, 2002
