Lady of the sea
        by Darmok
          12/28/01

  Lady of the sea, 
  should tempest cast me ashore 
      near some island of your grace.
      I pray thee I saunter not, 
      lest in my folly or whims
      o're day distract, 
                you escape me.

   If you be near this wandering soul, 
   paint for me of your grace. 
      In every raise and every set,
      the marriage of earth and sky.

   Within the sunrise and sleepy moods 
       upon horizons crest, 
       crown thy head with
       clouds of jewels, 
                  should I ever forget,
                               lady of the sea.The picture that I've found on the internet, was done by:

John William Waterhouse
