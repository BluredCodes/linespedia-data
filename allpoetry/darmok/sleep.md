"Sleep" by Darmok 4/26/96

Sleep children sleep- downward fall,
	cool waters of distant years.
Dip your face and quench your thirst,
	abandon sorrows tears.

Fill your hearts with cherished winds,
	all fragrant blossoms dwell.
Light your fires and climb your trees,
	take flight with summer's sails.

Smell the sands of distant shores,
	where life might never speak.
Ride your dreams, give open sail,
	to sands of shores you seek.

Sleep children sleep,
	taunt the night with fire.
Tame your mind and ride the mares,
	to green pastures and hearts desire.
