"Bloody Ground"
                       by Darmok 9/20/96


                       Bloody ground soaked with courage, 
                       how many lives there lost. 
                       For days of war the battle fought, 
                       the thousand tears they've brought.

                       The ground struggles to survive,
                       pulled apart by foes. 
                       The tug-of-war bleeds the sky, 
                       and death the battle sews. 

                       Lives so young so sweet, 
                       we bury the pain the hurt. 
                       Asleep the quiet, beneath the earth,
                       gives rest the sugared dirt.
