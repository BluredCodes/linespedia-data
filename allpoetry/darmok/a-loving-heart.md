A Loving Heart
by Darmok
2/15/02

I desire a loving heart,
one that knows nothing
of selfisheness.
For all that it would pour,
and lose of itself,
I would give back,
then again ...more.
It would never be for want,
except for my taste
there I would leave.
A hunger for my soul,
again and again,
Desire I could believe.
