"Touched"
                       by Darmok 6/14/2000


                       Two strangers passing in the night,
                       Tracks behind them, covered white.

                       Snowflakes forward, left and right,
                       Two strangers passing, in the night.

                       Did you say something, did I hear right?
                       Twas the wind blowing, flowing, might?

                       Nay, but in your eyes, there in this light,
                       Spied I, a glimmer of cool blue moonlight.

                       Then as you passed me by this cold winter night,
                       Your shoulders wrapped warm and tight.

                       Touched me ever so slightly that I should write,
                       Two strangers passing, in the night.

                       Now, warm and wrapped covered in bed,
                       Thoughts of sleep, a book well read.

                       I think of what lay behind those eyes so blue,
                       I’m glad I have been touched by someone, by you.
