The Wall
by Darmok
2/20/02

I yield to thee oh hellish wall,
Rebuilt each day as stones would fall.
My flesh is torn and fingers numb,
For all that I am, I would, have done.

Each stone removed becomes undone,
Two for each in place of one.
I am what I am, cannot beat what is done,
Defeat the wall rebuilt by one.
