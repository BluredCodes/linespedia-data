Silly Love Songs
by Darmok
1/31/02

There is nothing wrong
With another pretty love song.
The ages are filled with odes
To love, and love lost. 
 
Epics have been heralded as
Love personified, perfection, 
But alas, as timeless as is beauty, 
No one face shall ever capture
All that beauty is, can be.
Neither shall one song or
One poem speak all that there
Is of love.  

Dare we say enough,
Enough of silly love songs?
Surely a song is born anew,
In hearts finding love, 
Eyes seeing love,
Ears hearing love,
And poetry,
for the first
Time.
