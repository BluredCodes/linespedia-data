"Grains of Sand"
                       by Darmok

                       You’ve touched the darkest places within my soul,
                       Found a way into my heart.
                       For there amidst my darkest thoughts,
                       Are put away those things that hurt.

                       Buried there yet… not out of sight,
                       I manage to keep them at bay.
                       Aware of them but not with them,
                       Tis where these thoughts should stay.

                       Your words pierce this darkness,
                       They warm the cold and black of night.
                       They walk with me and touch my soul,
                       Release the weight of fear; of fright.

                       Like a common path walked before,
                       You and I have trodden more.
                       And have seen the waves upon each shore,
