Dreams are Nice ....but,
by Darmok
2/22/02

Be happy!
We can't live in our
dream worlds alone...
I've got to practice 
what I preach I know.
Live in the here and now 
with people I can touch, 
find a heart near to me 
and a face that speaks of such.....
Love, 
a hand I can take, 
a soul with whom to wake....
and a smile that lights my life, 
a breath that I might feel, 
upon my lips would softly set......
a person here and now, real. 
Dreams are nice....but
we must strive to make them real
and feel 
life
