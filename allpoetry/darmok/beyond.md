Beyond, 
by Darmok
Credit to Jo-Ellen for her painting “Beyond Echoes”


Over the hill where thermals rise
Toss and tease my cap to fly.
She rises above the golden waves
High into the pastel sky.
	
Oh glorious day 
Where children play.
Daffodils and soft beds of straw we lay,
Butterfly, surely rules the day.

Beyond echoes lies her heart,
Upon the thrusting currents that guide her flight.
High above our straining sight,
Our whispers….. join her lonely flight.Jo-Ellen is an artist and can be 'searched' and 'found' easily on the net :)
