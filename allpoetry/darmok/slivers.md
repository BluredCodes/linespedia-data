Slivers...
by Darmok
2/03/02

Slivers 

The universe that is you, could never 
be fully mapped or explored by my mind. 
I could only believe that my understanding 
is but a drop from tears that hang as stars, 
The sight or touch of just a sliver, could 
overwhelm me.... 

I am but at the foot of the mountain 
that would dare pierce the clouds 
that cover my sight of the stars 
that hold your experience, knowledge, 
beauty, and the ugliness 
you have seen and felt. 

But in all universes 
there is a balance of creation, 
thereto is found destruction 
in black convoluted masses that consume. 
Black holes would pull You to a place 
where neither time, light or sound 
Can escape.... submission lends you no
warmth or hand, only slivers
of temporal existence in 
a light never held, never quite touched.There are so many voices sharing of their hearts within allpoetry.  I could never ever think that I 'know', or see in their darkness all that has been, is.  I might struggle forever 'and a day' :), to catch sight of but a sliver of what weighs so heavily on the souls of children and the shells that protect them now.  The struggle is unimaginable, because my mind has never been rolled flat and twisted, seared
by the ignorance of brutality; how could I imagine, or think that I could see in the dark.
