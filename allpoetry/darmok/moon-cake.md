Moon Cake 
by Darmok 

1/29/02 

The moon mocks me, 
besotting brilliance. 
Disturbing chaos, 
and manic marvels of sublime division, 
I’m tilting. 

~*~

Lunar piercings of eye candy sores, 
too much Light for rooms without doors. 
I lay on the floor. 
Gravity wins. 

~*~

Moon light splinters 
Touching this shattered face. 
A cracked mirror, 
casting rays of silver shards 
and dripping slivers. 

~*~

Icing adorning pretty cakes of 
cold pressed layers 
of ilk and dead flesh. 
I am made. 

~*~

'Happy Birthday darling', 
he 
Mocks... 
the man on the moon 
Feeds my sorrow with 
rusted spoons. 

~*~

Forgotten.
