Burn (love)
by Darmok
2/25/02

Burn oh sons of man,
burn as the sun would
there held in your heart.  
And should you call on  
the daughters of her womb, 
and sing your lustful tune. 
Burn in your souls 
the cleansing fire that strips
flesh from bone, 
and make ready for love
this your heart, 
its sanctuary,
its home.
