Space Above and Beyond Fans might recognize the subject....

                      "Kylen is no More"
                       by Darmok 5/12/96


                       Try as they might to steal you from me,
                       you are hidden in my heart.
                       They cannot find you nor the key,
                       the doorway in the dark.


                       I dare not keep you in my thoughts,
                       they know...the mind...they see.
                       Would rip you from my heart and soul,
                       should my thoughts betray me.


                       Last words spoken will haunt my dreams,
                       as vague and clouded visions.
                       Cold chills will lay with me,
                       empty reaching of heart's decision.


                       Nathan...... I must give you up!
                       trust in you to find me---
                       Reach me...love me...wake my heart,
                       return the light inside me.


                       The woman you'll find, I pray the day,
                       cold hearted and so blind.
                       Warm her with your love so dear,
                       revive the spark you find.


                       Love this woman...Nathan I am there,
                       they will not have taken you.
                       I'm lost and cannot find myself,
                       in search of light that knew.


                       You'll never see these words nor I,
                       unspoken, no page will feel.
                       With sadness and our love denied,
                       I walk into the gray and chill. 

                       Kylen is no more.
