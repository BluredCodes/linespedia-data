Calmate (calm)
By Darmok
1/8/02

Be still my beating heart,
One word 'twas all it was.
Not enough to fill your sails
Or launch a ship built for sea.

A small word,....yet why
Was that word used?
An endearment only
I have ever pondered
Or applied significance too.  
A word that infused
All the joy of my day
And sealed each letter
With my love.

So why, after all these
Years does that word appear, 
So near before my eyes, offering a deep
Departure from my resolve and 
Composure?

Fathoms of angst appear
In that word.
Tempting me deeper 
That I should go...and perhaps 
From this leap I would drown, 
But in what realm or heart, 
I would know.

This word buried in my heart
Where no one has yet found,
Removed nor replaced, 
Is all I have of thee.

Be still my beating heart,
Rest your sails of blue.
Standing still in shallow waters,
'Twas never 'just a word',
              for you.
