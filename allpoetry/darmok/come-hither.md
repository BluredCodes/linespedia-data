Come Hither!
by Darmok   3/8/02

Come hither and set a spell,
welcome to my bower.  
Adorned in glitter and 
feathered blue I welcome you 
come hither.

I assure you no other bower
is built as well, nor would you
ever see one as clever. 
Another bower painted blue, 
from berries ground
and chewed..oh never!

Oh....baby...ahh..
...I like it like that....
yes...
yes...
ahhhhhh....
well...
how time flies,
and now so must you....
now get along
and build our nest.....
for you see,
another mate 
comes hither :)the bower bird is quite the lady's 'man'?  Older males often have potential mates constantly stopping by for a peek. These more experienced suitors may mate with dozens of different females in a single breeding season.
