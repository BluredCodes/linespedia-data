Sing
by Darmok
1/10/02

I am sustained in every note held,
Beyond the capacity 
of my soul you fill.
You sing the very breath of life,
Sweet surrender 
unto your will.
Bali hi, ….
I am lost adrift your sea,
…..come for me….
…. come to me…..
your voice serene, your call,
Sing through me, 
Bali hi, your spell,
the voices there you quell.
