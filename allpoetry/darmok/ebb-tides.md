Ebb Tides
by Darmok
1/14/02

At times a tidal wave of blessings, 
things cast upon the shore...
A cleansing release of things done and past, 
retreats the ebb-tide its endless chore. 

In the midst of starry nights, 
the moons's full lusterous face.
Her lure, pulls with gravametric distortion 
our will in time and space.

We find ourselves in dreamy seas, 
captured in the Picean gardens.
Her beds of pearls, the songs she sings,
Softening hearts forgotten...
hardened.This could be in the 'Sad' category, but it was written for a sense of cleansing and release....'softening the hardened heart'...
