Blue Steel

by Darmok 12/16/01

(for EvolovE)



Work the metal, 

stoke the furnance, 

red hot desire. 

Beat the metal bend its flesh, 

quench the metal's fire. 



Upon the anvil face I my fate, 

to yield beneath the blows, 

harder and harder, 

my soul is tempered, 

never again to know. 



What was I to begin with, 

a small slab of steel, 

bent over, again and again, 

I am no longer real. 



Forged body, and warped for his design, 

I'm put beneath for shoe, 

to be trodded upon and kicked and thrown, 

beyond my body blue.Inspired after reading a verse from EvolovE.  My heart goes out to all our friends, here, victims of abuse, or pretty darn close to someone who has been abused.  Shaka, when the walls fell.
