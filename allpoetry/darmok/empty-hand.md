---
title: "Empty hand"
writer: "Darmok"
slug: "empty-hand"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

Rape me Ripper wield your steel,
slice and dice at my will.
Deny me not what I feel,
display my guts of which I spill.

Taste the bile of my soul,
chunks of heart I vomit whole.
There the lies, a hope that stole,
lay to rest my body cold.

Close these eyes, two pennies put,
so dreams therein forever cut.
From lifeless meal of what I am,
a fool without dreams, an empty hand.
