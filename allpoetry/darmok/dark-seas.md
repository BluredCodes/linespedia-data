"Dark Seas"

                            by Darmok 53096

                           

                       Stolen hours of sleepless thought,

                               tarry the weight of seductions fall.

                       Eyes heavy with crusted tears,

                               my struggle lost as darkness calls.



                       You fail me oh dear friend,

                               I cannot will you to go on.

                       No toxic brew of bean or tea,

                               will save me from the darkening sea.



                       Sleep....you haunt me day and night,

                               seduce my body to betray me.

                       Even under the bathing rays of light,

                               I cannot escape thee.



                       Take me then into your sea,

                               dark fathoms embrace me.

                       I pray you do not keep me as I love the light,

                               my life and waking dreams above your sea.
