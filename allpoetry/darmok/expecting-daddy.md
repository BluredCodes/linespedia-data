Expecting Daddy by Darmok 9/29/01Waxen puddles set below,candles gone, forgotten.Balloons, limp in careless currents,another year forgotten.Held back my tears, o'er the yearsquenched flames dead and rotten.Birthdays missed and promises broken,again not here, forgotten.Another dollar and late regard,surely next year not forgotten.Do you love me Daddy dear,birthdays missed forgotten.No one gift will ever fill,the empty place begotten.Nor lift my heart from depths below,of birthdays past, forgotten.Hi, this is purely a fictional topic, its not me or me ;)by experience that is.
