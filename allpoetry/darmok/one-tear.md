One Tear
by Darmok 3/21/02
~*~*~
Should thou bear me
oh my soul,
a wish granted in requite, 
there upon her face exist,
a single tear regret... resist.
~*~
And offer this in recompense
for suffering here said I,
All would be worth a moment
nay missed...
one tear upon her face 
there exist...
a single drop,
her cry.
~*~*~A tribute verse so to speak, for the song "Cry..by Angie Aparo"
