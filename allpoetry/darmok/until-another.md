Until Another
by Darmok
2/19/02

Now put to rest
my heart will sleep
In  between 
each silent beat 
~*~*~*~ 
A fallen star
from heavens keep
The dreams of love
will ever weep
~*~*~*~ 
Into my day
the tears will seep
And burn their voice
of salty deep
~*~*~*~
Until another
would reach beneath
The icy depths
where heart does 
sleep
