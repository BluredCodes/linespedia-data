Please, Eat the Sheep
by Darmok
one/18/02

The rumbling thunder 
Accompanied by digestive cramps,
Sounds an embarrassment of emptiness
And hollow shell.

Ignored, the feeling grows stronger
Until their eyes you cannot deny
Lay upon you and with raised brow
You submit, yes…. ‘twas me.
(I cannot quell)

Be quiet I demand,
You unruly beast!
So what If I awoke you
Two hours early today.
Go back to sleep
And eat the sheep,
Or that silly cow
Jumping over the
Moon full of cheese.

There is nothing else
This morning for you,
Except more emptiness,
...yes
A diet coke please.

Lunch is two hours away,

...quiet...(shhhhh)
(I think he’s sleeping.)I'm just a bit hungry :)
that feeling  inside,
How wonderful life is,
When lunch has arrived!

(Elton John, said it better:)
