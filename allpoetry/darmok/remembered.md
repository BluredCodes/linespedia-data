Rembered
by Darmok
12/17/01

When I am gone, 
  and there is but one soul left
  that remembers a day.
Filled with song and verse
  and here, a stranger came this way.

 Should one soul remember him,
   from dream, and love 
   shared that day... 
and placed a mark 
   upon this place,
   within you touched your soul,
   and marked  your face.

 There be a smile
    beyond time and space, 
   and somewhere rise
    in heaven's place.

 Sweet memories within 
   he of this place, 
   and of all of you
   who marked his face.I can't help but be inspired to write something every time I read grandpappy.
