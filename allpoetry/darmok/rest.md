"Rest"
by Darmok 1/23/02

I search for rest beyond 
My sin, the ‘things’ held 
Too long, 
within. 

I would share with you 
What sustains these words, 
The pages filled, my quest. 

They are offered here, 
Such as they are, to be held, 
Even for a moment without fear. 

For within your eyes, and beyond 
What lies, the doorways in the dark. 
There a light shines and burns within, 
Loveliness, and compassion, 
Beats your heart! 

And, 

Should these words find their rest, 
The sanctuary of your breast. 
In quiet repose are gathered there, 
These words that give me rest.
