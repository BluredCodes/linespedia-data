Poet...

by Darmok

1/15/2002



There are no words today,

nothing humorous or witty.

Just the same old feelings 

of wondering when the light will return 

to illuminate something useful 

and worthy to be shared.



I'll read from others' works.

Wander through their thoughts

gathering stores of divine

Fruits and wisdom picked

from branches hanging low.



Yet from those branches 

that I would reach 

above these feet grounded in intransience. 

Towards starry skies 

there would I climb,

in search of words complete....

...

serene,

...

devine.



POET...

When I taste of your Golden fruit, 

the mysteries shared of your heart.

Enlighten me and light my way, 

guide me through the Dark.
