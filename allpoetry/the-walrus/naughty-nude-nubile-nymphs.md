title: "Naughty, Nude, Nubile, Nymphs"

Naughty, nude, nubile nymphs,
dancing just for me.
Naughty, nude, nubile nymphs,
for no one else to see.

Naughty, nude, nubile nymphs,
dancing just to please.
Naughty, nude, nubile nymphs,
their dance is just to tease!

Naughty, nude, nubile nymphs,
their dance is like a fire,
chops my breath, thumps my heart,
rises my desire!

Naughty, nude, nubile nymphs,
so beautiful to behold.
Naughty, nude, nubile nymphs,
their touch leaves me cold!

Naughty, nude, nubile nymphs,
they make young men beam!
Naughty, nude, nubile nymphs,
for old men---JUST A DREAM!
