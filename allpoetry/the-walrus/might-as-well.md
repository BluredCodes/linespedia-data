---
title: "Might As Well"
writer: "The Walrus"
slug: "might-as-well"
source: "AllPoetry"

13 and 7---one unlucky the other is great!
Numbers really have nothing to do with our fate.
Somethings JUST happen---others come into play,
by choices and actions we make through each day.
Often we are caught in the spider web we weave,
then blame, fate or luck, ourselves we deceive.
Walking around on our island, puffed up like a king,
be wary of choices and consequences they bring!
When we make poor choices and problems appear,
then we embark upon the flight of fear!
Wise choices, oft times, put us ahead of the game,
so careful consideration determines loss or gain!
Football and Basketball---less Luck than SKILL,
are mostly decided by hustle and sheer will!
"It's the way the ball bounces"---sometime THAT IS TRUE,
but winning or losing, depends most on OUR DO!
So yes, our life can be compared to the game,
where our actions decide the loss or the gain,
but some chioces, the outcome--you never can tell,
so in cases like that----IF YOU WANT------
MIGHT AS WELL!
