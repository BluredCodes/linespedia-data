---
title: "Feels Like a Funeral"
writer: "The Walrus"
slug: "feels-like-a-funeral"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/26320-Feels-Like-a-Funeral-by-The-Walrus"
---

Ankle deep in this ocean of emotion,
check a tear---not here.
Sinking deeper.
Knee deep in this ocean of emotion,
stop a tear---not here!
Sinking deeper.
Waist deep in this ocean of emotion,
hold a tear---not HERE!
Sinking deeper.
Chest deep in this ocean of emotion,
fight a tear---NOT HERE!
Sinking deeper.
Drowning in this ocean of emotion,
Lose a tear---they're here!

Release that I cherish,
in this sea I perish.

Her image burned on the screen of my brow,
while my mortal eyes lose sight through a flood now.

How do I let her go?
How? I do not know.

Mixed emotions,  sadness---PRIDE!
crying I present--My daughter
THE BRIDE!
