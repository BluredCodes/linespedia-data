Undefined friend
I am
Not knowing
Not researching
Not willing to know
If I’m good or mean
If the sources of my
Thoughts
Are reliable
If the future
Waits for me
In triumph or defeat

I just want
To penetrate your present
Your thoughts
Your anxiety
Of vibrating twenty years
Shielded on my caress
And my flights
Into the empty imensitude
Of thou fascinating perplexity
