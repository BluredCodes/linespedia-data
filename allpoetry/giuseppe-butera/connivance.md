---
title: "Connivance"
writer: "Giuseppe Butera"
slug: "connivance"

You don't need to live in my soulto be able to put molding on my thoughts at the mercy of your vibrations You don't need to come down in the dusk of my questions to be able to dominate the crowd of the howling doubts The day-break happens in your diaphanous looks overloaded by fugacious victories and the shadows of your body are dissipated under the recurrent blanket of the slow caressof the old affectionsthat suddenly sprout from a tenuous adolescent connivance
