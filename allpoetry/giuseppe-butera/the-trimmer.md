---
title: "The trimmer"
writer: "Giuseppe Butera"
slug: "the-trimmer"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/9546-The-trimmer-by-Giuseppe-Butera"
---

An enormous spider The thread, so absurd, of his  slaver Has set From Rome (Where I was born In the heart of the old empire) To  Macchu Picchu (Where I wish to die In the womb of the past empire) In the middle hanging Here I am, A pregnant grain Of  meaningless Presence In the space around.
