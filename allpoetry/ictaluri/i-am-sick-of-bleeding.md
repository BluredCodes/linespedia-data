I waited for the 'revelation'
Some kind of ultimate 'understanding'
through all the groans,
and all the bloated mirrors.

I waited for a clue.

Rivers of years have revealed
slyly, slowly, 
all that is cruel
and crept me along to wrinkles and spasms,
my divine purpose being: Woman.

Never owned,
never known,
never loved,
and longing to be wrong.07-01-01,
