Devin will be gone soon,She's been leaving for some time.She's all grown to monsoons,And strange her heart to me,And secret now, her mind.She's long ago a-howled her place,And her baby teeth I find,Abandoned out where spitted she,A-leap, I prowl behind.Devin will be gone soon,I may catch her scent,Trailing me a dark moon,And knowing where she went.07-25-01,
