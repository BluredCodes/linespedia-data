Beloved Stranger

He comes, and he rescues.
He was there for me when Ithought love on earth was through.
He whispers sweetness into my ear.
Only when I'm with him do I truly have no fear
He sings to me a lullaby
He's always there for me when i need to cry.
I know that he never really was there, 
but if he were there he would surely care.
I love this stranger who I don't even know.
The closest thing to our meeting happened at his show.
I need him to keep me going on.
As long as I pretend he's there I can be strong.
My only wish would be, 
For him to come and be with only me.

Kristina Hoeler
Allentown Pa
