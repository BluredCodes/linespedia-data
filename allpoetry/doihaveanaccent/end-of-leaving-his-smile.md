sorry, it didn't let me finish leaving his smile.

here's the real ending

She loved that smile of his, but now he's leaving,  
and she knows that she will have to move on and leave his smile, 
As he leaves he breaks her heart more with every mile, 
Yet she's still leaving his smile.
 
Kristina Hoeller
