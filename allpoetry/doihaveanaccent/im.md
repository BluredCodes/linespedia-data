She sees more Red than blue in their little text box. Is the friendship soon to stop? She gives him her friendly advice.But is it advice to herself? she must think twice.Does she still love him as before?Is he beginning to love her more?The relationship changes its velocity?Is this such proposity?Is he scared to say how he feels?She begs for his love at his feet she kneels.Longing to hold him, she lets him go.She still thinks that he will say no.Does the red and blue show a sign?Was he not meant to be mine?She softly stares at her screen.His eyes slowly turn envy green.She just sits back and thinks.He looks away, to himself he winks.Now I've got her, now it's me she wants.He just has to go and flaunt.He enjoys his ability to make her insecureShe wants it, but thinks that they have no future.This story has no happy end.It's just a see saw of him being more than a friend.She just wants to know the truth from him, Yet he does not share what's within.
