He Never Wrote to Say Goodbye

He never wrote to say goodbye, 
He didn't even have to tell her one lie.
He just never said a thing, 
and he ignored the pain that he would bring.
The poor girl loved him so
and he said nothing, not even no.
So she confronted him dreary day, 
just to tell him the words she had to say.
Then they talked and he tried to be a jerk, 
But he couldn't and his act did not work.
So they fixed the problems in the relationship, 
by deciding to make it right as just a friendship.
In her heart she held him tight,
and used to dream of only him throughout the night.
Now she has to let him go, 
even if he never said no, 
so now she writes this saddened night
to remind herself that he never wrote to say goodbye.
