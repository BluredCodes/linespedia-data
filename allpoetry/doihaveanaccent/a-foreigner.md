A Foreigner

He speaks to me in a foreign tounge,
He says I make him remember the times he had when he was young. 
I spoke to him to learn of a new place, 
but now he's given me a friend with a new face.
He says me not believing in my beauty is just a form of denial. 
I ask him questions every day
He gives me answers and tells me things in aussie slang to say.
He lives in a wonderful world far from here.
My never reaching there is his only fear.
I want to go there, but i don't know why.
The reasons would probably consist of the beauty of the place, and the animals, but sadly, not the guy.
It seems to me that ist a land of oz and fairy tales.
It has all kinds of animals that roam the land, and in the ocean, many kinds of sharks and whales.
And so I end my tale of the one so far out there.
If he's really there, i wonder where.

Kristina Hoeller
Allentown Pa
