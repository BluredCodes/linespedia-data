Journey Through My Heart

If you know the way through the brush and cover stories, 
You rush past the thorns and heartaches of my past and make me happy, let me forget all my night mares.
I'll let you in and take you on a journey through my heart.
I'll let you see every tiny detail, and every painful part. 
I'll let you in my secret garden of love, 
I know it's hard to see through the shyness i once had, and the tales i tell, but give me some time and tell me you care. 
Slowly but surely i will meet you there.
Just be gentle and promise to never go away, 
For i'll promise to always let you stay.
I'll be there whenever you call. 
I promise to take you on that journey through my heart, and finally you will understand every painful part.

Kristina Hoeller
Allentown pa
