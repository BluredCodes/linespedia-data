Losing love again

I'm tired o writing about losing a love.
It seems I fall and then fall down every time.
Will there ever be a love i can say is mine?
I just want to live and love and be loved back. 
Is this too much for one person to ask?
Love is what makes poets write. 
It brings the music that plays throughout the night.
Sometimes i think, do i need to depend on a man?
Or should I depend on me, and bring myself from my knees?
I've been on them praying to god for love and life.
Praying tha one day i'd be loved and be someone's wife.
Let me be loved i say again.
Love is beauty, not a sin.
But boys will be boys, 
and break hearts as though they were toys.
Just unbreak my hart the thousand times.
Take back your broken promises and filthy lies.
Be yourself every whre you go.
Depend on you, and let everyone know. 
You don't have to hide in Roselyn's disguise.
Come on, let me be loved, love me, don't be a typical "guy."

Kristina Hoeller
