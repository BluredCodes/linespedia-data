TreeA tree lives forever, yet is solo.Nurses living thing above, within, and below.Sees life pass by through its leaves, Yet cannot enjoy, cannot be free.Stands strong through the years, Watches many man's hopes and fears.Sees loves found and lost, Wonders if it's worth the costs.Alone is all it knows, never depended, never let down, Never lifted its roots from the ground.Never knew the be, never knew the sorrow, Never had to lend, never had to borrow.A tree's life can be good or bad.At least it never had to be lonely or sad.Kristina Hoeller
