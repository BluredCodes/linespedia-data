Longing Love

There was a girl not long ago. 
Who no one would have known, 
is the girl she is today, 
or how she got to be this way.
She's had her probles, haven't we all?
She would fall in love and take the fall.
Time after tme, she thought she'd grow stronger, 
but to wait for love even a little longer 
Can be so hard, 
with loe, what love? When will it ever stop or start?
When will she be happy? 
When will she know? 
Is this love ever meant to grow?


Kristina Hoeller
