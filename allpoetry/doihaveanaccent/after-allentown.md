After Allentown

After Allentown, she would always say, 
would be when she could finally find her way.
She'd fallen in love and couldn't be convinced 
that he was never there for her, and wasn't heaven sent.
She found many hardships in that place called allentown.
And now she's decided that not until after allentown could she lift her hopes from the ground.
She needs to make a change in her life now.
She needs to get it all out of her mind and find her happiness somehow.
Bigger and better thingsare in store for her.
They"re just out there in another town, another man, out there in that vast world. 
So she'll pretend to smile, ad loose the frown that's been keeping her down, 
and dream of the rest of her life away from here, and after allentown.

Kristina Hoeller
Allentown, Pa
