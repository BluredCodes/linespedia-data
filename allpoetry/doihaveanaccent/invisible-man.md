---
title: "Invisible Man"
writer: "doihaveanaccent"

I draw your visage on every piece of my notebook paper.
Just so i can stare into your eyes and see your face.
I want your smile, I want it to savor. 
These drawings, i will never erase. 
I want to look at you.
I want to talk.
If only these drawings could be you.
If only they could talk and walk. 
Its just you that i'm looking at.
I can't stop looking back. 
I want you to see , that
without you, my life holds a never ceasing lack.
