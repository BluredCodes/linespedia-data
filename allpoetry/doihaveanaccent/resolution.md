ResolutionWe ignore each other, yet leave the word behind. We hide our fears and deny, Pretend not to care, moving coldly, Shouting in fear, yet thinking boldly.Wondering about happiness or heartache future can bring.Wanting to die, yet longing to live, Wanting to lie, yet persisting to give. Hold me now, let me go. Tell me how you feel, but n  scared to knowMissing you while you're still here. Hiding in my solitary fears. Where's the resolution, and the end?When does the friend come from this fiend?Yearning for a love to call my own, Yet wanting to break out all alone. Lonliness is cold, together is painful. Waiting for someone to tell me that i'm beautiful.This life so precious, so heart breaking. Let me be with you, and stop my heart from aching. Don't be afraid of things unfamiliar.In my eyes the night has only one star, which is you, sadly it's true. Now I tell you and our friendhsip is through.Why must i be so sad, when there is so much to make me glad?What did i do right? wrong? and more importantly, why are you mad?Kristina Hoeller
