title: "Sometimes I don’t notice the distance between us."

You brought up a good point today, one I didn’t realize.I guess I didn’t know cuz I see you everyday with my eyes.I see you sitting beside me telling me you love me.You brought up that point today, something I didn’t see.You reminded me of the miles between your feet and mine.I guess I just saw the distance between our visits as only a little bit of time.I guess I just thought of you enough to know that you were here.You reminded me that our feet our drifting, and it’s our hearts for which I should fear.
