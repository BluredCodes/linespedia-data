What to do

Life is full of trials
it goes on mile after mile
what one looses can return
even though all the bridges have been burned
when it seems you want to fly far far away.
It is then that you must decide to stay.
All the world is in your heart and soul.
no one  can tell you this, you just have to know.
all the problems of man 
could be solved if they would learn to understand
every life is a candle in the wind, each just waiting to burn out.
now is your time, your place, make the best of it and have no doubt
do what you love, and love what you do, 
if you do this you'll be no one's fool.

Kristina hoeller
