Lifelong Love

They were 16 when they met at the grocery store. 
He worked hard, but all the while, he was still quite poor.
He invited her out on a date.
And she said yes, and they went out and came home late

A young lady and man
holding each other's hand
As they drove areound the country
they knew that together they would always be.

They werre only 19 when they were wed.
He smiled at her, kneeled and said, 
"would you be mine, 
and stay together for all time?"
she said yes, of course, 
and they bought that house just outside of town complite with their own horse.

A man and his wife 
decided to be together for all their life
went, around the land
just holdin each other's hand

Their children by this time have grown, 
and the couple is now quite old.
They take a ride out in the cold, 
which, in the end would end their life
The man and his little old wife.

An old man and wife
were together for their whole life, 
went round the land 
and left this world together, still holding each other's hand.


Kristina Hoeller
Allentown
Pa
