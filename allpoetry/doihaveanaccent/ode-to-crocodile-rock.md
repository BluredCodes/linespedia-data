Ode to Crocodile Rock

Oh club called crocodile rock,
You're the place that I can go to when I feel down.
When I come here, in my sorrows, i am not alone.
You have introduced me to many bands and to each of them i am now among their greatest fans.
The room is dark cuz the lights are low.
We wait in anxiety and impatience for the upcoming show.
As we wait, we meet new people from different places.
It's always nice to look about and see new faces.
So I give my thanks to the place that is a home for me. 
I arrive in anger, and leave happy, 
thank you crocodile rock for being the place for me.

Krstina Hoeller
allentown pa
