Leaving His Smile

He's leaving her for good this time. 
She still can't get that smile of his out her mind.
She loved him more than any other,
But  there were circumstances that kept them from being together.
She wanted be with him, She loved him so, 
But he caused her all this heartache and didn't even have to tell her no.
She wrote to him to speak of her love.
He didn't push her away not  little innocent shove.
But he ignored her every day and night.
How could she even put up a fight?
She loved him more than anything in this world.
How could he pass her ofher girl?
Does he even know he hurt her?
Why couldn't he just be there for her?
She loved that smile of his, but now he's leaving,  
and she knows that she will have to move on and leave his smile,
