I'm going to miss you.
Lately there's nothing for me to do.
Nothing makes me happy, 
Nothing quite there for me.
Loving you is all I'm capable of.
I dream of you, 
To get to you, 
I would push and shove.
Your hair is so cute tonight.
I wish I may I wish i might.
I dream of kissing you so gently. 
If I  lean my head ever so slightly,
Will you use this precious time?
Will you let yourself be mine?
Let me hug you, no matter how long.
Lean to me, let me know you're strong.
You're sick now, I feel bad I made you walk home.
At least I offered for us to be together; alone.
Now you're there for me, 
Time's only a bit more than a week.
Gosh love songs can be so sappy.
But they are the things that make me happy.
Being with you makes it all worth while.
I just love to hear you laugh.
I love to see you smile.
I WILL truly miss you so, 
Just how much you will never know.
You will be my memory of the summer of 2001.
And truthfully you will be my best one.
I WILL miss you I say.
I wish I might, I wish I may.
Let me be with you.
For our time is nearly through.
I will always remember, 
I'll never forget.
Your love is still, 
the one i have not had yet.

Kristina Hoeller
Allentown Pa
