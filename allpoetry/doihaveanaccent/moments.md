I often look back on moments that we spent together, 
Some can be so happy, and make you so inviting.
Then we get back to the part where we were fighting, 
Then it turns those moments into a bittersweet symphony in my ear.
Just something that I no longer want to hear.
I often remember why we’re no longer together.
