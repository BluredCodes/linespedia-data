A lost friend

Her best friend, one of the close kind
Had a choice and i guess he's made up his mind.
He was the one she shared anything with when she was down.
Now his old friendly spirit is no where to be found.
He was there for her through thick and thin.
Whenever he was around she thought she would always win.
Now as she ponders the musings of life, 
She realizes that she could have been his sweet loving wife.
But now she seems to have lost all hope
She only sits around and mopes  
It seems that he's found a new friend now.
But he's deserted her somehow.
I bet he doesn't even know just how he's hurt her so.

Kristina Hoeller
