Satin MemoryEven in this living HellI can smell youTaste your hairYou skin Like satin memoryLingers there... Like love songs of oldI want to sayThat I careI need a hold on itOn that satin memoryI need it there...Oh the times are toughThe going's roughBut I'm gonna make it throughAll I need with me, with meIs that satin memory...And you...You were my starMy saviorSending my demons awayThat memorySmooth as satinGiving light to my dayBut it wasn't enough for me... was it?I had to have it all.I wanted my fame, my life, my fantasy...And you.Oh the times are toughThe going's roughBut I'm gonna make it throughAll I need with me, with meIs that satin memory...And you...I know I've no rightAsking you to forgive,Like I want you toWho needs a memoryA satin memory...When all I want... is you?
