IgnorantHow can one person be so blind, so blind?How can one person ignore me, all the time?You just don't know, don't knowYou're just gonna go and let me go, go....You're so ignorant, to my love, to my loveYou don't know how much you really need meYou're incomplete, with defeat, you're beatBut you're too ignorant to believe me...You think you're fine without me, without meYou just don't know enough, enough about meDo you really think you can get by, get by?If you do, please just tell me why.......
