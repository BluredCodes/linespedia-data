---
title: "The Rose I Used To Be"
writer: "Jessica Vance"
slug: "the-rose-i-used-to-be"
source: "AllPoetry"
url: "https://allpoetry.com/poem/9973-The-Rose-I-Used-To-Be-by-Jessica-Vance"

Our love was beautifulLike a deep red rose...But roses wither and dieShrivel up dryJust like our loveSoft, healthy petalsBurnt awayBy pain's raysI think of you and herI cry until I too am dryThere's nothing more I can giveNo more tearsNo more painYou've left me dryWithered, shriveledI was like a roseNow you've dried me upLeft me to dieWhy can't I returnTo the rose I used to be?
