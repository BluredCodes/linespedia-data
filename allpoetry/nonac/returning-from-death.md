---
title: "Returning from death"
writer: "nonac"
slug: "returning-from-death"
source: "AllPoetry"

falling from the sky,
escaping my rebirth.
plummeting to my new life,
as a different person on earth.

Spot light eyes searching,
Looking for a body,
falling into the wrong one,
the depressed one that got me.

Starting from scratch,
erasing the chalk board.
recovering from my suicide,
hanging from a guitar string chord.

7 Minutes from my death,
I emerge through the clouds.
falling to another bullshit life,
with the speed of a thousand pounds.

Face first into a new life,
trying to climb back into death.
choking my self all the way in,
Taking away my own breath.
