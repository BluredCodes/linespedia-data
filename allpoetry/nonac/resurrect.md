now that you know me, i can take off my mask,
release my true meaning and reveal my true task,
drinkin gin from a flask yellin all yall kiss my ass,
I told you id be back, and now that im back, armed and dangerous
flauntin the nine millimeter, i married a stripper and murdered flipper,
tortured and killed ginger and the skipper, climbin through the air with a blunt, my eyes glared, strangled a cunt and nobody cared.

I once stood up at a meeting pulled out some hair and started beating the bitches that were keeping me from beeping, kept me from my sleeping and gave me the occasional beating, reminice about those bitches that be creeping, stompin a nigga to death, takin my bling and stealin my breath,
rippin me from the inside till nothin was left, ended up raping and killing my self. but i was resurected backwards, falling into earth, speaking unspeakable words cussin at my rebirth...
