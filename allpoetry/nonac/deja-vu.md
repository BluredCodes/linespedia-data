I have a sense of remember,
maybe it's deja vu,
Call me a psycho,
but i seem to remember you

I remember words,
telling myself im fucked,
remembering my life before death,
and knowing that it sucked.

molding depression into words,
then throwing them at you.
I seem to remeber doing this,
mabye its just deja vu.
