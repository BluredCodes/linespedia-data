Early Morning

I cannot see the birds,
Yet the birds sing.
Their songs short and long,
Opus, just the same.

I cannot see the wind,
Yet the trees sway.
Their leaves soft and green,
Rustle, just the same.

I cannot see my love,
Yet full with song.
My heart warm and free,
Captured, just the same.


Leonard Thomas
