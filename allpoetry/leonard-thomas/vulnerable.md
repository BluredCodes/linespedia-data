Vulnerable

Our early days,
Our early lives.
We live the games,
We live the lies.

They keep us safe,
They keep us sheltered.
From our fears,
From our selves.

We want love,
We want fame.
Give us toys,
Give us dreams. 
 
Past mid-age,
Past pretense.
Our bodies soon sag,
Our souls soon rise.

Thoughts go deep,
Thoughts so creep.
Us revealed,
Us unsealed.

Now cherished,
Now treasured.
With my partner, with my love, 
With you be vulnerable, my love.
 


Leonard Thomas
