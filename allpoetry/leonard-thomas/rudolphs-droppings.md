Rudolph’s Droppings

It moved and it shot, a large Army Tank, 
On four batteries it ran, from Uncle Frank. 
An Erector Set with panels and pillars, 
Next year, in my room, it housed caterpillars. 
A Model Plane, made of plastic and wood, 
For me to build, because I could. 
An arm full of Comics, Super Heroes of mine, 
The Fab Four saves the world, again just in time. 
A Chemistry Set was this young boy’s desire 
The cellar, THE HOUSE, almost lost to the fire.



Leonard Thomas
