Just as i sleep
Everything is calm
Suddenly i feel the pain
Upon my wrists holes apear
So down my arms blood runs
Cant bear this pain
Hoping its a dream
Realizing it's not i scream
I become beaten and bruised
Slits and cuts form on my back
Then it all goes away
