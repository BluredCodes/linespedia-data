---
title: "My dearest of friends"
writer: "Seer"
slug: "my-dearest-of-friends"
source: "AllPoetry"

my dearest of friends
knows me to well
knows my life
knows my hell

i tell her what i feel
through pen and page
my love, my hate,
my pain, my rage

she knows my secrets
and my soul shes seen
she knows about
my vile being

though horrible sights
i still see
she stoped me from believeing
the worst was me

in all the ugliness
filling my sight
she is my savior
she is my light

she shows me
my beauty inside
she makes me think
of myself with pride

my dearest of friends
knows me so well
she helps my life
and calms my hellthank you to my dearest of my friends. you know who you are
