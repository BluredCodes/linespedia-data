I walk the street
Until it splits
And neither way
Is brightly lit

I've seen this fork
And know it well
One leads to heaven
While the other to hell

Only in one ending
Is the taking worth
One path is death
One path is birth

I know my choice
Decides my fate
So for some time
I stand and debate

The thrill of the choice
Comes over me
And i chose
My path randomly

I run down the path
For just a bit
Until I realize I've come
To another split
