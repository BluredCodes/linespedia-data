---
title: "The whiping boy"
writer: "Seer"
slug: "the-whiping-boy"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

My world extends
to the walls of my room
and it wont get any bigger

i have no window
and i rarely see the light
this is the life i live

i am punished for anothers sins
and he i do not know
i am told he is royalty
so i know we'll never meet

i am beat with a whip
amd sometimes a cane

my only wish is for freedom
which i shall never recieve

i cannot escape
for the door is barred
and the walls are made of stone
so here i sit and wait in silence
till the next time they call
for the whiping boy
