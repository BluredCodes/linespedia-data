Now nothing seems to be
I feel like I’ll be lost forever
Emotions cease to war in me
And they all blur together

I feel nothing at all
My world slowly ends
I start to fall
Then reality bends

Nothing in side me
No one to fallow
So why cant you see
That I’m living hollow

I feel the depression settle in
So the end of my life now beginsthis poem is actually about me. it's about the way all my emotions are one never ending dullness.
