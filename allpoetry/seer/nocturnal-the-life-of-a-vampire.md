I live in the shadows
And i feed by night
Ever so deadly
Is my bite
Your as good as dead
Once your in my sight
I feast on your blood
To increase my might

I wonder the shadows
In serch of a meal
And no emotions
Can i feel
In my world
Only death is real
With my fangs
Your fate i seal

I must hide from the sun
Durring the day
So in a coffin
I always lay
Because so deadly
Are its rays
That is will burn
My flesh away

What a creature i am
With a mind so ill
All i know
Is how to kill
I block out the screams
No matter how shrill
Then i leave a body
Lieing dead and still

For all eternity
I roam the earth
With a wonderful power
And a horrible curse
So sweet are the poisons
Of immortalitys birth
That evil consumes
And leaves you emursed

I'll live forever
And I'll never grow old
Which is why
I go hunting so bold
Yet nothing pleases me
Not dimands, not gold
I only want death
Because my soul has been sold
