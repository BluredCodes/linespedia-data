I see my body lay dead
On a hospital bed
And i notice the heart moniter
Is thin as a thread

I see a bright light
And with no fright
A went twards it
So i could see this sight

But as i come near
I feel my fear
I stop listen
And strain to hear

I am twirl around
And i plummet down
into a sea of fire
where i drown

As my soul quickly desends
I hit the ground and the tourture begins
I guess at the end
We are all going to pay for our sins
