---
title: "Beautiful hate"
writer: "Seer"
slug: "beautiful-hate"

i know without hate
there cannot be love
and without either
we confront hells gate

but i resent your anger twards me
and your hate 
even though i know
without it emotions could not be

i never to be liked
i mearly existed
and lived
to where i could get by

i love this hate you see
because its beauty is there
and i know without it
i wouldn't be me
