---
title: "Death"
writer: "Seer"
slug: "death"
source: "AllPoetry"

---

An unwanted end
A horrible loss
The ultimate freedom
At a terrible cost

A peaceful finish
To a painful life
When a tomb is a home
And a worm is a wife

It is the source of our caution
And the cause of our fear
For no matter what
It will always be near

We live in its shadow
And we hide from it's breath
Yet cope we must
Because life is death
