---
title: "I have failed"
writer: "Seer"

Now I look down
At all that was taken
I speak to my children
Yet I am forsaken

I send gifts
But man is filled with greed
They no longer remember
That i give what they need

I send a lamb
And that they slay
First they sin
Then they pray

Because of man
my hands are nailed
So now I see
That I have failedthis was NOT written to anger anyone.
